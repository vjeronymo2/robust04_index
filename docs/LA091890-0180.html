September 18, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

RACING BRIEFS;

SAVING GRACE BIDS FOR 3RD WIN

Suzanne Broughten's Saving Grace takes a drop in class Wednesday night at Los Alamitos as she goes in quest of her third victory of the year in the $5,000 conditioned trot feature.

Seventeen were entered for the feature, necessitating a split into two divisions, which will go as the sixth and eighth races on the 10-race program.

The first section is wide open, with recent winner Kami being given a slight edge over Dear Miss and Devil's Gate. The second division finds Saving Grace dropping in class and into a good spot to get her third win of the year. Her main adversaries will be Moscato Beach and Kimmie's April.

In post position order, the field for the $5,000 Wednesday night main attraction is as follows: Moscato Beach, Nicol Tremblay in the race bike; Supernal, Jim Grundy; Lotto Joe, Jack Parker Jr.; Kimmie's April, Ross Croghan; Oso Speedy, Paul Keays; Le Mans Catch, Steve Hyman; Saving Grace, George Cliff; and Lady Charlee, Rick Plano.

First post time Wednesday night will be the usual 7:30 p.m. with the admission gates opening at 6 p.m.
