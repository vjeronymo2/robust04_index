BFN [Text] Ankara, 21 Jun (PL) -- It was reported today that Turkey and Cuba have assessed the possibility of increasing economic ties, especially in the areas of commerce, tourism, and business. Those possibilities were studied during Cuban Ambassador to Turkey Jorge Castro's visit to the city of Izmir, an important agricultural and industrial center in Turkey, where he met with local authorities and industrial and commercial representatives. The report given by Cuba's diplomatic office added that they had agreed to hold a meeting of Turkish and Cuban businessmen shortly to determine Cuba's participation in the construction of houses in that city. Jorge Castro's two-day visit allowed him to discuss, in a friendly atmosphere, the development of bilateral economic ties and tourism investment and to give Izmir Mayor Burhan Dapatura a message from his Cuban counterpart, Pedro Chavez.
