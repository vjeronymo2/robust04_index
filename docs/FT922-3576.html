920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / International Company News: Airlines match Delta fare cuts UNITED Airlines , American Airlines and Continental said they were matching rival Delta Air Lines' transatlantic fare cuts in markets where they compete, Reuter reports. In Texas, Continental filed a federal court lawsuit charging AMR's American Airlines unit with 'predatory acts' involving recent domestic fare reductions. American, based in Dallas, denied the allegations and asked a court in Chicago to declare its pricing practices legal.
