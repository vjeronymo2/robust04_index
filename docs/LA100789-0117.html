October 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Robert H. Bork, former federal judge and unsuccessful nominee to the U.S. Supreme Court, was elected Friday, along with two others, to the board of Sundstrand Corp., a Rockford, Ill., defense contractor. Bork, 62, currently is with the American Enterprise Institute. Other new Sundstrand directors are David Acheson, 67, a retired partner in the Washington law firm Drinker, Biddle & Reath, and Charles Marshall, 60, recently retired vice chairman of American Telephone & Telegraph Co.
