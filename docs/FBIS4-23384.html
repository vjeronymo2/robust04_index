BFN [Text] A spokesman for the 12 organizations, the Somali Salvation Alliance, today warmly welcomed the UN Security Council's resolution extending UN Operation in Somalia [UNOSOM] operations in Somalia, from 1 June. The spokesman said the resolution would greatly help the efforts being made to bring the Somali people together, for the reconstruction of the country, reconciliation and national unity. Resolution 923 stipulates that UNOSOM would cease its operations in Somalia in March 1995 [as heard]. The spokesman called on all members of Somali society to support UNOSOM operations in the country and participate in the implementation of the UN Security Council resolution, and the peace declarations reached by leaders of Somali organizations regarding a lasting peace and national reconciliation.
