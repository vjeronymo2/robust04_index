940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Commercial rents fall 'has halted' The fall in commercial property rents which started in August 1990 has halted, according to the All Property Index published by Hillier Parker, the chartered surveyors. Shop rents rose 0.1 per cent over the quarter. Although rents in London have continued to fall, rents are starting to rise in the north. Offices rents have remained static outside London and the south-east. In London's West End, rents rose for the second consecutive quarter, while in the City they remained flat. In the industrial sector, London and the south-east rents continued to fall. Elsewhere there were no changes. Hillier Parker Rent Index, 77 Grosvenor Street, London W1A 2BT. Pounds 8.
