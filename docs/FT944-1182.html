941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: Gold supports SA trading South African stocks ended broadly firmer as a strong gold price and a recent improvement in steadiness in world equity markets supported trade. The overall index rose 50.4 to 5,747.3, industrials 25.9 to 6,883.4 and golds 37.1 to 1,900.8. Among the leading stocks, De Beers slipped 25 cents to R93, Dries 75 cents to R57.25 and Anglos R9 to R235. Sasol was 75 cents better at R32.75 and Nedcor up R1 at R42.
