BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] During today's [23 May] session, all the political parties represented in the Chamber of Deputies signed a political statement regarding foreign observers for the upcoming elections. Felipe Munoz, a deputy for the Institutional Revolutionary Party, read the document. [Begin Munoz recording] In light of recent statements by U.S. Congressmen David Bonior and Robert Torricelli, we hereby reject any attempts by any foreign organizations to intervene in our country's electoral process. [end recording] The chairman of the Chamber of Deputies entertained nominations for citizen advisers at the Federal Electoral Institute.
