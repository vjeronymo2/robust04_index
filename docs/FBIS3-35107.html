Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Guarded calm prevails in the liberated areas in the south and western al-Biqa'. However, the massing of Israeli troops in the border strip is continuing. According to information from the occupied border strip, in the past 12 hours Israel brought in about 20 armored vehicles and about 100 troops to the areas of al-Khiyam and al-Ahmadiyah, overlooking the western al-Biqa'.
