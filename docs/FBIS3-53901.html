Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--Guinean Foreign Minister Ibrahima Sylla left Cairo this afternoon after a one-day official visit during which he delivered a message to President Husni Mubarak from Guinean President Lansana Conte. The Guinean minister also held talks with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa on bilateral relations and African issues of common interest.
