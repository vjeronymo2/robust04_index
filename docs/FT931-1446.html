930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Ricardo The impact of a Pounds 650,000 property provision cut profits at Ricardo Group, a provider of engineering and technological services, from Pounds 1.21m to Pounds 875,000 pre-tax for the half year to December 31. Turnover from continuing operations improved to Pounds 31.7m (Pounds 28.6m) and operating profits by 24 per cent to Pounds 1.77m. Pre-exceptional earnings from continuing operations amounted to 3.09p (2.48p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.9p.
