BFN [Text] The prime minister, brother engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, arrived in al-Manamah today for a visit to the State of Bahrain as part of this tour of a number of countries in the region. Reporting this, the Gulf News Agency said brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas had a letter from brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, to his brother Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of the State of Bahrain, on the last developments in the Yemeni arena.
