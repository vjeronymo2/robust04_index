940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / Rolls-Royce Trent engine approved Rolls-Royce Trent yesterday announced it had received airworthiness certification for the Trent 700 version of the engine which will power the wide-bodied Airbus A330 twin-engine airliner. The company is also planning to certify the more powerful Trent 800 version of the engine for Boeing's new 777 twin-engine wide-bodied aircraft at 90,000lb thrust instead of 84,000lb.
