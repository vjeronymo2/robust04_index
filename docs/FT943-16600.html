940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Call to build new nuclear plants The government's power-generation policy runs the risk of exchanging an undue dependence on coal for an undue reliance on gas unless new pressurised-water reactor nuclear plants are built, the cross-party European Policy Forum warns today. It says the government must make up its mind whether the security of supply and environmental benefits make it appropriate for it to provide the support needed to draw in private finance for a new PWR power station, for example by underwriting risk insurance.
