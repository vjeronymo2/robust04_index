BFN [Text] Cape Town May 19 SAPA -- The African National Congress will have 60 senators in the 90-member Senate to be constituted by Chief Justice Michael Corbett on Friday. The National Party [NP] will have 17, the Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] and the Freedom Front [FF] five each and the Democratic Party [DP] three.
