920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Kalon gets go ahead to bid for Manders THE HOSTILE all-share bid by Kalon, the Yorkshire-based paint maker, for Manders (Holdings), the paints, inks and property group, is not to be referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission. This means that day 39 of the progress of the Pounds 89m 8-for-3 bid falls tomorrow. Manders has until then to release new information. The expected document will definitely include its interim results and possibly a full-year forecast.
