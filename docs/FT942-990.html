940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Amoco to spend Pounds 80m on gas site Amoco, the US oil company, plans to spend Pounds 80m to expand its natural gas terminal at Seal Sands on Teesside in a project which will link the facility with British Gas's national transmission system. The Teesside terminal is the destination of the 255-mile Cats pipeline which runs through the central North Sea, and through which gas from the Armada field will be sent.
