941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Strong & Fisher buy Strong & Fisher, the sheep skin and by-products processor, has acquired Belwood Foods from Hillsdown Holdings for about Pounds 2m. Belwood processes frozen offals for the pet food industry. The consideration, in cash, comprises Pounds 1.45m plus the value of Belwood's net assets at December 31 1994. Strong & Fisher also announced the purchase of a freehold property occupied by its Peninsular Proteins arm, for Pounds 10,000 cash. The site, at Torrington, Devon, was owned by a Hillsdown subsidiary.
