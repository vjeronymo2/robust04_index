Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Aden, Yemen, Dec 29 (AFP)--A young member of the Yemeni Socialist Party (YSP) has been murdered in the capital city Sanaa, the party announced here Tuesday. 'Abd-al-Latif Sa'ad al-Ruwayni, "a young student and a dynamic party worker," was killed last Thursday, the 151st member of the YSP to be murdered since North and South united in May 1990, the party added. The YSP blamed "treacherous hands" for the killing but did not elaborate. [background passage omitted]
