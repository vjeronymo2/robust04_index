May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Steve Elkington disqualified himself from a 21st-place finish last weekend in the USF&G golf tournament in New Orleans and forfeited the $10,000 prize.

Elkington said he was unaware, until he saw a newspaper Monday, that he signed an incorrect score card after the final round. Elkington shot an 80, but signed a score card that said he had a 79.
