BFN [Text] Tehran, April 26, IRNA -- Iranian Ambassador to Lebanon, Homayun 'Alizadeh, met Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz in Beirut last night to formally discuss the case of the four Iranian hostages kidnapped by the Christian Phalangists in 1982. The four, Charge D'affaires Mohsen Musavi, diplomat Ahmad Motevasellian, embassy worker Taqi Rastgar Moqaddam and photographer of the Islamic Republic News Agency (IRNA) Kazem Akhawan, who were kidnapped on the Beirut-Tripoli road are the longest held hostages. Sources related to the Zionist regime insisted that the Iranian nationals were killed at Phalangist Militia Chief Samir Ja'ja's headquarters. Ja'ja' said he was not responsible for kidnapping of the Iranian diplomats. Despite Iran's repeated requests the case of the four Iranian has remained unresolved.
