Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. Ambassador Richard Boucher said that the United States will intensify its efforts to help achieve a Cyprus solution this year. Mr. Boucher made this statement upon his arrival, with other members of the diplomatic corps, at the Presidential Palace where they were received by President Glavkos Kliridhis on the occasion of the New Year. Mr. Boucher also said that State Department's Coordinator Robert Lamb will visit Cyprus, but the time has not been set. When asked by correspondents if he had any statement to make, British High Commissioner David Dain confined himself to expressing good wishes for the New Year. Russian Ambassador to Cyprus Boris Zenkov expressed the wish that 1994 will be the last year of Cyprus' division. Asked whether Russia intends to appoint an observer to the Cyprus issue, he said that his country will wait until the talks begin and then consider how it can offer more help.
