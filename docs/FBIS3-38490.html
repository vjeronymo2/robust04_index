Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] A bomb exploded at 0138 today at the German Goethe Institute on 14 Omiros Street in Athens. Approximately one half hour earlier an unidentified person who said he represented ELA [Revolutionary People's Struggle] telephoned ELEVTHEROTIPIA newspaper and warned about the explosion. The police cordoned off the area to prevent casualties. However, the men of the antiterrorism squad did not manage to enter the building before 0420 today because there was nobody in charge to open it. The bomb was planted in the ladies room on the first basement level where it caused considerable material damage.
