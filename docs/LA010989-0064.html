January 9, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Mark McKoy and Courtney Brown, who abandoned the Canadian relay teams at the Seoul Olympics in the wake of the Ben Johnson scandal, were suspended from competition.

McKoy, a hurdler who was supposed to run in the 400 relay, will not be able to compete for national teams for 2 years, said Paul Dupre, president of the Canadian Track and Field Assn. Brown, who was a member of the 400 team, is banned from running for Canada for a year.
