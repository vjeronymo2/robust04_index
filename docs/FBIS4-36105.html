BFN [Text] Muhammad Husni Mubarak, the Egyptian president, last night received Mr. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister. Present at the meeting were Muhsin Muhammad Bin Abu-Bakr Bin-Farid, the deputy prime minister and minister of planning and development; Salih Ubayd Ahmad, deputy prime minister and minister of transport and communications; and 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Majid al-Asnaj, deputy prime minister and foreign minister. Present on the Egyptian side was Dr. Usamah al-Baz, the director of the Egyptian President's Office of Political Affairs.
