940420 FT 20 APR 94 / UK Company News: Brown & Jackson agreement Brown & Jackson, the discount retailer, has reached agreement with Mr Gerald and Mrs Vera Weisfeld on the terms of their proposed capital injection. The Takeover Panel has agreed, subject to the special resolution to be proposed at an extraordinary meeting on May 9 being passed in a poll of independent shareholders, to waive the requirement for the Weisfelds and their charitable trust to make a general offer for the company should they and their trust own or control in total 30 per cent or more of the voting share capital.
