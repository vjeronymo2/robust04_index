BFN [Text] The coalition is recruiting all its members prior to the no-confidence motions that will be heard on Monday [23 May]. The United Torah Jewry [UTJ] has submitted a no-confidence motion over the desecration of the Sabbath and holidays during the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] withdrawal from the Gaza Strip. The Likud submitted a motion of no confidence because because it alleges that the government is prepared to hold negotiations over the future of Jerusalem.
