940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / Foreign investment in Mexico increases by 42.7% Foreign investment in Mexico reached Dollars 6.45bn in the first five months of the year, an increase of 42.7 per cent over the same period a year earlier, according to the Trade Ministry, Reuter reports from Mexico City. The figure took total investment during the administration of President Carlos Salinas de Gortari to Dollars 48.19bn - or twice the target for the six-year term of Dollars 24bn.
