BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar. 31 (A.A.) -- Ankara Chief Prosecutor Nazmi Sarvan said today that his office took under protection ballots which were found in various places and launched a preliminary investigation. As the fundamentalist Prosperity Party emerged as the victor of Sunday's [27 March] local elections, huge amounts of stamped ballots were found in various places, casting a shadow on the election results. Sarvar told the Anatolian Agency that experts were to examine the authenticity of the ballots and will also receive the testimonies of officials who were on duty at the precincts during the election. "The necessary announcement will be made at the end of the investigation," he said.
