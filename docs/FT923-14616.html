920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (12): Hambros Leasing - Company Snapshot THE company was formed in 1983 as a subsidiary of merchant bank, Hambros Bank. Hambros Leasing specialises in medium-sized leasing tickets of Pounds 1m or more, ranging from big computers to oil drilling rigs. The company's 1990-91 income was Pounds 106.8m with pre-tax profit of Pounds 18.6m. The cost of assets currently leased directly, or through operations managed by other companies, is Pounds 1.2bn. The company has 3,000 leases outstanding and 36 staff.
