930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / France denies plans for suspension of Japanese car imports THE French government said yesterday it did not plan to suspend Japanese car imports, writes William Dawkins from Paris. Mr Dominique Strauss-Kahn, industry and trade minister, said France believed in the EC-Japan car import accord signed in July 1991, which had been 'more than respected' by Japanese car exporters to France.
