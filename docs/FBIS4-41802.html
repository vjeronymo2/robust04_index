BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Central Election Commission tonight announced a postponement of the news conference on the preliminary results of the presidential elections earlier scheduled for 1100 on 28 June. The commission told INTERFAX-Ukraine that the large volume of information to be processed and the slow pace of its transmission made the summing up of results in such a short period of time impossible. The commission said that general information on the results of the Ukrainian presidential elections divulged by the media had nothing to do with the official sources.
