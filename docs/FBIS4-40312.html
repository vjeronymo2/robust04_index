BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 16 June -- Today President Akhsarbek Galazov of the Republic of North Osetia met Shmidt Dzoblayev, a member of the public chamber under the president of the Russian Federation and secretary general of the Assembly of Democratic Forces of the North Caucasus, who has decided to act as intermediary in dialogue between the Russian and Chechen leadership. The leader of the regional democratic movement told Akhsarbek Galazov that he had talked to the leadership of the Chechen Republic in Groznyy on 15 June. They discussed the restoration of normal relations with the Russian Federation, the role of Chechnya in settling Ossetian-Ingush relations, and ways to achieve stabilization in the North Caucasus region. Shmidt Dzoblayev told the ITAR-TASS correspondent that Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev is ready to meet Russian President Boris Yeltsin and has said so many times. The Chechen side is considering establishing relations by treaty with Russia and is advocating the creation of guarantees against political repression. Dzhokhar Dudayev expressed readiness to promote the establishment of goodneighborly relations between the peoples of Chechnya, North Ossetia, and Ingushetia.
