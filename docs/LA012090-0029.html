January 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $75 Friday when his $25 win bets on Somebody's Summing in the third, What Has Been in the fifth and I Love Racing in the ninth all failed to win. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,122.

Bob's bet today is a $25 win parlay from Monsieur Maison in the second to Toby Jug in the sixth.

Friday's Loss $75
