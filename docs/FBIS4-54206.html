BFN [Text] Seoul's Tongdaemun Police conducted a seize and search procedure today at the office of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification [Pomminnyon] located on Chongno 6-ka in Seoul. Police disclosed that they conducted a seize and search for relevant documents following the South side headquarters of Pomminnyon's violation of the National Security Law in deciding to hold three-way talks with Pomminnyon's offices in North Korea and Japan over the phone in order to discuss the agenda of the Fifth Pannational Rally, which is slated for 15 August [National Liberation Day].
