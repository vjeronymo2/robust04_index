920407 FT 07 APR 92 / EC Commission president issues an impassioned plea to the Twelve 'The EC Commission president, Mr Jacques Delors, issued an impassioned plea to the Twelve yesterday to accept his plan for a boost in EC spending over the next five years, (AP-DJ) reports from Luxembourg. Mr Delors addressed EC foreign ministers for half an hour, trying to persuade them his package was essential for the goals of political union.
