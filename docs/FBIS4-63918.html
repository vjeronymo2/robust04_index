BFN [By ITAR-TASS freelance correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 25 May -- A Tsiklon-3 booster rocket carrying the satellite Cosmos-2281 was launched by Russia's military space forces from the Plesetsk cosmodrome at 1115 hours today. Major-General Anatoliy Ovchinnikov, commander of the Plesetsk cosmodrome, told ITAR-TASS that the launch (the 110th since 24 June 1977, when the first booster rocket of this type was launched from Plesetsk) was carried out successfully.
