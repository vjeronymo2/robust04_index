BFN [Text] Khartoum, 26 June (SUNA) -- A technical cooperation agreement in the field of transportation has been signed between Sudan and Iraq. The agreement deals with ports, civil aviation, railways, land and sea transport, and the exchange of skills and technical training in these fields. The agreement was signed in Baghdad yesterday by Transportation Minister 'Uthman 'Abd-al-Qadir 'Abd al-Latif, who is visiting Iraq. Dr. Ahmad Murtada Ahmad Khalil, Iraq's minister of transport and communication, signed on behalf of his country. After the signing, 'Uthman 'Abd-al-Qadir 'Abd al-Latif reiterated that Sudan stands alongside Iraq in its confrontation with imperialism and Zionism, which target the Arab nation. He praised the support that Iraq continues to extend to Sudan in different fields, especially in the field of transportation. The Iraqi minister stressed that his country was eager to develop its relations with Sudan, provide it with technical skills, and benefit from Sudanese skills in return. He said all the Iraqi ministry's technical institutions and facilities are fully ready to provide training opportunities to the Sudanese.
