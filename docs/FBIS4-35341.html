BFN [Excerpts] Brother 'Ali Nasir Muhammad, former secretary general of the [Yemeni] Socialist Party [and former South Yemeni President] addressed a cable to the brother Presidential Council chairman. Following is the text: Your Excellency Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, may God keep you. [passage omitted] We strongly believe that there is still motivation for unity, that there will be no future without unity, and that defeat is awaiting all those who antagonize it, represented in the tendencies that are opposed to unity and democracy. We are fully confident that the unified state is the only way to confront every crisis and failure in any field. It is also the sole way to deal with both large and small mistakes. It can contain all negative results of this war and fighting between brothers. The values of unity, democracy, and life will triumph forever. Unity is the cause of life and destiny for our people in Yemen. It is the cause of generations and the future, and it is not linked to individuals, therefore, we are confident that our people will not allow the reversal of unity and the return to the stage before 22 May 1990. [Word indistinct] their strong and unwavering faith in unity as a source of strength and dignity that concerns their future and development. Finally, we assure you and our people of our principled and firm support for Yemeni unity, democracy, and the ambitions of our Yemeni people to realize their happy unionist society. We wish your excellency continued good health and happiness, and accept our sincerest greetings. [Signed] Your brother President 'Ali Nasir Muhammad
