BFN [Text] The National Drug Law Enforcement Agency, NDLEA, has drawn up a master plan to involve all Nigerians in the fight against hard drugs. The chairman of the agency, Brigadier General Musa Bamayi, gave the information today in Lagos when he led members of the task force on hard drugs on a courtesy visit to the Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria, FRCN. Brig Gen. Bamayi stated that the primary focus of the master plan would be on prevention to stop Nigerians from becoming drug addicts. He said that arrangements were being finalized to enable media representatives to monitor the activities of NDLEA as part of efforts to demonstrate that the agency would not operate in secrecy in its assignments. Responding, the executive director of FRCN Lagos national station, Mr. Ikenna Ndaguba, who received the team on behalf of the director general, Alhaji Abdulraman Michika, stressed the importance of the media in prosecuting the drug war. He pledged the continued cooperation of Radio Nigeria in reaching the public effectively with antidrug programs of the agency.
