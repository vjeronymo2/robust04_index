December 10, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

2 MEN ARRESTED IN SHOOTING DEATH

A man was shot in the chest and killed Friday evening in the 5100 block of Imperial Avenue, San Diego police said.

Carlos Morales, believed to be in his late twenties, was pronounced dead at Mercy Hospital at 7:39 p.m.

Ramon Martinez, 30, and Arron Quintero, 24, have been arrested and charged with one count of murder, police said.

The investigation is continuing.
