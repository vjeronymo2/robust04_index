Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 23 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here today Indonesian Ambassador to Vietnam Mr. Juwana who paid him a farewell visit at the end of his term of office in Vietnam. At the reception, Premier Vo Van Kiet and Ambassador Juwana expressed their joy at the encouraging development of the relations between the two countries in recent years in various fields, especially in commerce and investment. Ambassador Juwana said that Indonesia would continue to do their best for further development of the bilateral relations which have been ushered in a new stage following the exchange of the visits of the two countries' leaders. He thanks Vietnamese Government and Premier Kiet for helping Indonesian businessmen in their cooperation with Vietnamese. Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet hailed the Indonesian ambassador's contributions to the consolidation and promotion of the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Indonesia.
