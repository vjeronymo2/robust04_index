Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli diplomatic sources in Cairo have said the Israeli ambassador in Cairo has received threats that he will be killed if he does not hasten to leave Cairo. The sources added that all Israeli Embassy diplomats have received similar recurrent threats over the past few days in protest against the Israeli massacre, which killed and wounded a large number of worshippers at the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. The AL-RA'Y correspondent has learned that the Egyptian Ministry of Foreign Affairs rejected a request by Israeli ambassador to Egypt David Sultan to bring in 40 Israeli security experts to enhance security measures around the Israeli Embassy.
