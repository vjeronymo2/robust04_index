March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

CS NORTHRIDGE SWEEPS BALL ST.

In a meeting of nationally-ranked college volleyball teams, sixth-ranked Cal State Northridge swept ninth-ranked Ball State, 15-6, 15-9, 15-5, at Northridge on Monday.

Raphael Tulino had a match-high 15 kills for Northridge (9-6), which hit .363 as a team in the nonconference match. Coley Kyman and Neil Coffman added 10 and eight kills.
