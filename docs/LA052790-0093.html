May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: THE FUGITIVE BY PRAMOEDYA ANANTA TOER (WILLIAM MORROW: $16.95; 171 PP.)

" 'The Fugitive' is in many ways a road map of the life Toer was to lead for the next 40 years: While his moral compass occasionally sent him down blind alleys, his conscience always destined him for dissidence." Mark I. Pinsky
