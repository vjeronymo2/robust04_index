Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli threats against Lebanon have escalated, accompanied as usual by instigation against resistance and relying on clear, coordinated U.S. support. The U.S. support was evident in U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher's warning message to Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz and in another message from Christopher to Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. MENA today cited a responsible source at the U.S. State Department as saying that the U.S. secretary of state has sent an urgent message to Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul, informing him that Israel may carry out a large attack against south Lebanon.
