930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Minister warns on working hours TOO MANY businesses demand 'outrageous time commitments' from employees, Mr Alistair Burt, junior social security minister, told the Scottish Tory Reform Group at the weekend. In a wide-ranging speech on social policy he said the demands were made 'without thought of the damage to family structure or for the strength employees should get from a sound family life if they are allowed to foster it'.
