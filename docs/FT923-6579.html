920824 FT 24 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: Crumpet revival Britons have eaten a record 860m crumpets in the past 12 months, market researchers Taylor Nelson say. The industry is now worth Pounds 37m a year, an increase of 130 per cent on 1986, partly due to the introduction last year of the finger-shaped crumpet, the first non-circular crumpet since the Middle Ages.
