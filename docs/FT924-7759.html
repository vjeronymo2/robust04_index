921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / Extra funds for British Library The Treasury has made available more than Pounds 60m of additional funds over the next three years for the completion phase of the new British Library building at St Pancras, central London. The library's grant for running costs has been increased by Pounds 3m for 1993-94 and Pounds 9m for 1994-95 to ease the cost of transferring stock.
