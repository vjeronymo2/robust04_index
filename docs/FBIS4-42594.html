BFN [Excerpt] "The situation is worse than was thought. Without a very wide-ranging commitment by a number of governments, European and non-European, the Rwandan crisis will widen." This was said by the Undersecretary for Foreign Affairs, Franco Rocchetta, in Nairobi, where he was brought back by the UN plane which was forced to return to the Kenyan capital from Rwandan shelling in Kigali. Rocchetta added that the Italian Government must remain neutral with regard to both ethnic groups involved in the fighting. [passage omitted]
