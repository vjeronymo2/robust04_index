920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Everards flat but sees improvement Everards, the family-owned independent brewer of Tiger and Old Original beers, blamed difficult trading conditions as interim profits declined by 52 per cent to Pounds 144,000. The outcome for the six months to March 28 compared with Pounds 298,000 at the same stage of the previous year. Turnover improved to Pounds 16.6m (Pounds 15m) and directors of this Leicester-based concern stated that sales had improved further since the period-end. They anticipate profits for the current year to show an improvement on the Pounds 387,000 recorded in the 12 months to September 28 1991. Earnings per share emerged at 6.1p (9.5p). The interim dividend goes up to 6.5p (6.2p).
