BFN [Text] Johannesburg April 24 SAPA -- A blast occurred outside the PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] regional offices of the African National Congress about 10AM Sunday [24 April], the ANC said. The intensity of the blast rocked central Johannesburg and was felt in Hillbrow and heard as far east as Kensington, residents reported. An earlier report indicated the blast went off in Hillbrow. There is still no confirmation from police.
