CSO [Unattributed article in the "Official Chronicle" column] [Text] The visit of Vagan Papazyan, foreign minister of the Republic of Armenia, to Romania began on 19 April. He met with President Ion Iliescu, to whom he will give a message from President of Armenia Levon Ter-Petrosyan. As the Department of the Press and Public Liaison of the Republic of Armenia informs us, V. Papazyan also met with Adrian Nastase, speaker of the upper chamber of the Romanian parliament, Nicolae Vacaroiu, prime minister, and Teodor Melescanu, minister of foreign affairs of that country. A consular convention will be signed by the two countries within the framework of the visit of the Republic of Armenia minister of foreign affairs. Between 21 and 24 April, Mr. Papazyan will take part in the proceedings of an assembly in Krans-Montana.
