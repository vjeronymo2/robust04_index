December 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

CASTING ANNOUNCED FOR MARTHA GRAHAM TROUPE

The Martha Graham Company has announced casting for its Jan. 4-6 engagement at the Orange County Performing Arts Center in Costa Mesa.

Jan. 4, 8 p.m.: "Appalachian Spring" (Copland) -- the Bride: Christine Dakin, the Husbandman: Donlin Foreman, the Pioneering Woman: Maxine Sherman, the Revivalist: Lyndon Branaugh.

"Deep Song" (Cowell): Joyce Herring.

"Temptations of the Moon" (Bartok): Crescent Moon: Thea Nerissa Barnes; Velvet Night: Steve Rooks.

"Maple Leaf Rag" (Joplin): Terese Capucilli, Floyd Flynn, company.

Jan. 5, 8 p.m.: "Diversion of Angels" (Dello Joio): Couple in White: Sherman, Foreman; Couple in Red: Kathy Buccellato, Kenneth Topping; Couple in Yellow: Debra Kantor, Christopher Dolder; Fourth Man: Mark Borneman.

"Steps in the Street" (Wallingford Riegger): Laura Jimenez, company.

"Herodiade" (Hindemith) : A Woman: Dakin, Her Attendant: Denise Vale.

"Acts of Light" (Nielsen): Conversation of Lovers: Sherman, Topping; Lament: Capucilli, company; Ritual to the Sun: Sherman, Topping, company.

Jan. 6, 2 p.m.: "Appalachian Spring": Herring, Flynn, Vale, Dolder.

"Deep Song": Capucilli.

"Temptations of the Moon" and "Maple Leaf Rag": same as on Jan. 4.

Tickets: $13 to $40. (Student rush tickets one hour before each performance, subject to availability: $7.) Information: (714) 556-2787.
