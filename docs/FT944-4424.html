941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: United Drug advances 28% to IPounds 4.4m United Drug, the Dublin-listed pharmaceuticals group, increased pre-tax profits by 28 per cent in the year to September 30. The IPounds 4.41m (Pounds 4.3m) result, up from IPounds 3.44m, was struck on turnover 24 per cent ahead at IPounds 168.8m, compared with IPounds 136.5m. Acquisitions contributed IPounds 112,000 (IPounds 1m) to operating profits. Net interest fell from IPounds 888,000 to IPounds 285,000. Earnings per share, fully diluted, came out at 17.02p (16.54p) with a recommended final dividend of 5.15p, giving a total for the year of 7.5p (7p).
