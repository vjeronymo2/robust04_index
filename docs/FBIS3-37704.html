Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kazakhstan and the United States have signed an agreement on the joint use of the Baykonur cosmodrome. In today's issue, KOMMERSANT-DAILY reports that in this way, Nursultan Nazarbayev's initiative to create an international consortium on the basis of Baykonur is being implemented. The agreement is signed without prior consultations with Moscow, which is given a secondary role in this project.
