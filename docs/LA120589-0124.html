December 5, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Michel Warren Bell -- known as Mic Bell when he sang with the Fifth Dimension -- will sing Joe in Jerome Kern's "Show Boat" for Opera Pacific July 13 through 22 at the Orange County Performing Arts Center, according to a biography of the performer printed in a Los Angeles Philharmonic program booklet last week. Opera Pacific officials did not return calls to confirm or deny the information. Bell was seen as Porgy in a revival of the Houston Grand Opera production of "Porgy and Bess" that Opera Pacific brought to the Center in 1987.

Early 1990 will bring several application deadlines for individual artists grants. For artists-in-residence grants from the California Arts Council: Feb. 9 for Artists in Schools and Feb. 26 for Artists in Communities and Artists Serving Special Constituents (such as prison inmates). Information: (916) 445-1530. For National Endowment for the Arts fellowships: Jan. 25 for photography, Feb. 15 for sculpture, March 15 for crafts. Information: (213) 439-2486. For painting fellowships from the Western States Arts Federation: May 1. Information: (505) 988-1166.
