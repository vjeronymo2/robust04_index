August 22, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SAUGUS WOMAN KILLED WHEN CAR STRIKES TREE

A Saugus woman was killed Monday morning after she lost control of her car on a Santa Clarita Valley street and crashed into a tree, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department said.

Nona McGraw, 34, was killed about 2 a.m. when her southbound car went out of control on Bouquet Canyon Road and hit a tree near Hob Avenue, deputies said.
