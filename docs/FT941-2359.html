940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): RVI - Other companies The trucks and buses arm of Renault, the French state-owned vehicle group, will announce 1993 results tomorrow. The company, like all truck manufacturers, is struggling to emerge from the sharp downturn in European markets. The worst is now over, according to the company, and analysts are expecting a substantial improvement on the losses of FFr1.6bn (Dollars 280m) recorded in 1992.
