BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister brother 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi returned to Sanaa from Doha this evening at the end of a visit that lasted several days during which he held talks with officials in the sisterly State of Qatar dealing with bilateral relations and developments in the Yemeni arena. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the brother deputy prime minister said his visit to Doha came within the framework of continuous contacts and coordination between the Republic of Yemen and the State of Qatar in the interest of the fraternal state and the Arab and Islamic nations. He added that he conveyed the greetings and appreciation of the political leadership and the Yemeni people to the leadership of the State of Qatar and its fraternal people. He hailed the principled and firm Qatari support for unity and legitimacy, and hoped that Qatar will be an example for other Gulf Cooperation Council states in particular, and Arab and world countries in general in its support for right and the Yemeni people's will. Concluding his statement, the brother deputy prime minister stressed that the Yemeni people will not forget the principled support for it during the current critical circumstances.
