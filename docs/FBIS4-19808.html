BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- At the conclusion of her visit to Bonn, Pakistani Prime Minister Mohtarma Benazir Bhutto met with Social Democratic Party Chairman Rudolf Scharping. Afterward, a one-day visit to Berlin was on her schedule. During her almost four days of talks with the Federal Government, Bundestag, and Industry, Benazir Bhutto primarily asked for more intensive economic support. Yesterday President Richard von Weizsaecker and Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel welcomed Pakistan's intention to reach a peaceful solution with India for the conflict over Kashmir.
