BFN [Text] As we have already reported, Foreign Minister Alois Mock had to undergo hospital treatment yesterday. The official reason is a herniation of a vertebral disk. He is in an Innsbruck hospital. One of his closest aides told ORF Morgenjournal this morning that Mock is in good condition, considering the circumstances. Tests are going to be carried out, and the foreign minister will probably have to stay in hospital until next Wednesday [1 June].
