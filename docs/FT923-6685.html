920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Boscombe Property rises to Pounds 351,000 Profits before tax of Boscombe Property rose to Pounds 351,221 for the 12 months ended March 31, a 43 per cent improvement over the previous year's Pounds 245,450. The results were helped by a rise in gross rental income from Pounds 703,980 to Pounds 813,768. Available profits emerged at Pounds 265,748 (Pounds 185,395), equal to earnings per share of 262.25p (181.9p). Dividends totalling 180p (130p) have already been paid.
