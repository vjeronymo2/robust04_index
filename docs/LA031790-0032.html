March 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

READERS TO RAIDERS: HERE'S YOUR HELMET, WHAT'S YOUR HURRY?

(To the tune of "Hello Dolly")

Goodby, Raiders

We won't cry, Raiders

It's so nice you're going back where you belong.

Take your Al Davis,

It will now save us

From his rantings and his raving,

He has earned the gong.

He had this town loco

with that great logo,

but commitment to the best he did not have.

So, take your time, fellas.

Punishment fits the crime, fellas.

We aren't sad you cannot stay

and be a credit to L.A.

Go back to Oakland where you'll play again!

EARL S. DRAMIN

Van Nuys
