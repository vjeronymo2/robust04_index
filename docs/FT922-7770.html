920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / International Company News: Landis & Gyr boosts sales LANDIS & GYR, the Swiss building and energy controls and telecoms equipment group, said half-year sales rose 17 per cent to SFr1.45bn (Dollars 98m). But excluding currency gains, the sales growth was only 11.4 per cent. The order book at end-March was 1.7 per cent lower than a year earlier in nominal terms, 6.4 per cent lower after currency adjustment. The directors still expected full-year net profits would be above last year's SFr62.8m.
