Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 29, BNS -- A Lithuanian-Slovenian agreement on economic cooperation was signed in Ljubljana Tuesday [29 March] by Lithuanian Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius and his Slovenian counterpart Janez Drnovsek. During their meeting, the two premiers discussed several draft agreements brought to Slovenia by the Lithuanian delegation, Lithuanian radio said. On Tuesday, Slezevicius also met with Slovenian Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle. Lithuanian Industry and Trade Minister Kazimieras Klimasauskas met with Slovenian Economy Minister Davorin Kracun. The head of the Lithuanian central bank, Kazimieras Ratkevicius, spent the day talking with Slovenian bankers about ways of improving settlements between the two countries.
