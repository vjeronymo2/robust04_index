BFN [Text] Firmino Lukamba, Voice of Resistance of the Black Cockerel correspondent in N'Dalatando, reports that Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party combat aircraft are currently bombing the civilian population in the area. Lukamba says that the struggle for the control of N'Dalatando involves the use of chemical warfare, leaving behind a carpet of dead and wounded people, and destruction. The Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola Military Command in the area, concerned with such a tremendous violation of the UN charter, today requested that a UN Angola Verification Mission-2 commission be urgently sent to N'Dalatando to investigate the matter. An officer of that command said that a government cannot be allowed to massacre thousands of citizens in the name of democracy.
