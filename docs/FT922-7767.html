920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices SPICES Cloves trading has been more active at present reduced price levels, reports Man Producten. Madagascan cloves traded aslow as USDollars 775 a tonne fob while spot Europe suipplies were down to Dollars 950 ex-warehouse. Commores cloves were offered at FFr7 a lb, cif Rotterdam. Indonesian abcd nutmegs were at Dollars 1,750 a tonne, shrivels at Dollars 1,325 and bwp at Dollars 1,000. Broken mace grade 2 was at Dollars 1,750 a tonne. Seychelles cinnamom bark was offered at Dollars 1,350, cif Europe. Indian cochin ginger was quoted at Dollars 1,100 a tonne,nigerian split selected grade between Dollars 6000 a tonne ex-warehouse, depending on quality.
