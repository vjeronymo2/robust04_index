BFN [Text] Tallinn, Apr 18, BNS -- Ltn. Col. Arvo Sirel took over as acting chief of staff the leadership of the Estonian defense forces' Central Staff from Col. Ants Laaneots today. Laaneots, who entered his resignation on March 24, was listed into reserve as of today. Laaneots' resignation was approved by the country's commander in chief, Maj. Gen. Aleksander Einseln, on March 31. The new acting chief of staff, Sirel, earlier occupied the position of head of the Central Staff's operative department. He was now replaced on the post by Ltn. Col. Jaan Poldma. A spokesman at the Central Staff said Laaneots has not revealed so far what he would do after resignation. According to information obtained by BNS, he has been offered the opportunity to continue his education in Western military academies, as well as a position with the Defense Ministry.
