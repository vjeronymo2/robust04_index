BFN [Text] His Royal Highness Prince Muhammad, the regent, received visiting Qatari Minister of Finance, Economy, and Trade Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Khalifah Al Thani. They discussed bilateral ties and ways to foster cooperation in various fields. Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali also received the Qatari minister who is in Amman taking part in the 17th ministerial session of the United Nations Economic, and Social Commission for Western Asia, ESCWA. The session opened this morning. Dr. al-Majli and the Qatari minister discussed ways of enhancing Jordanian-Qatari cooperation in the fields of economy, industry, and trade.
