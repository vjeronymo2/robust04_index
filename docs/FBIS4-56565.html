BFN [Text] The two main parties that comprise the government coalition in Chile advocate official plans to increase ties of cooperation with Cuba. Gutenberg Martinez, president of the Christian Democratic Party, asserted that his organization has a positive view toward any effort that could signify solidarity with the Cuban people. Socialist Party President Camilo Escalona expressed his belief that the upcoming Ibero-American Summit represents a good opportunity for Chile to substantiate its plan of cooperation with Cuba.
