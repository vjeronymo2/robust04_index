BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 27 (XINHUA) -- Polish Speaker of the Sejm (house of representatives) Jozef Oleksi said here today that his upcoming visit to China will be of great significance to his nation's future strategic interests. Oleksi, who leaves for China on Saturday [28 May], told the Polish Press Agency that during his stay in China, he will hold discussions with Chinese leaders on social, political and economic issues as well as international development. The speaker, who is leading a delegation including a group of entrepreneurs, said that future economic cooperation between the two countries will be among the topics for discussion. He expressed his hope that the visit will help consolidate Polish-Chinese relations.
