BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov] [Text] Simferopol 7 June TASS -- The Crimean police are not transferred in subordination to the Ukrainian Interior Ministry's Main Interior Department for the Crimea as it was claimed by visiting heads of the Ukrainian ministry over the local TV and radio, Crimean Interior Minister Valeriy Kuznetsov has told a parliament meeting. In his words, the Crimean police continue working in a normal regime. Chairman of the republican security service Vladimir Lepikhov told the deputies he has no information on increased tension on the peninsula.
