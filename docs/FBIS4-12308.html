BFN [Text] France has opposed internationalizing the Kashmir issue by Pakistan. The visiting French foreign minister, Mr. Alain Juppe, assured his country's support to India's stand and hoped talks between India and Pakistan will resume soon to sort out the issue. The matter came up for detailed discussions at his meeting with the prime minister, Mr. Narasimha Rao; the external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh; and the home minister, Mr. S.B. Chavan. Mr. Rao briefed Mr. Juppe about Pakistan's support to terrorism on Indian soil and Islamabad's interference to undermine India's territorial integrity. The prime minister said India is open to bilateral discussions with Pakistan.
