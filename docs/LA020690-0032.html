February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Michael B. Lawler has joined Brinderson Real Estate Group, an Irvine commercial and industrial real estate developer, as vice president finance/controller. Lawler was formerly vice president and chief financial officer for Wallace Moir Co. in Beverly Hills. In his new position, he will oversee corporate accounting and finances, in addition to analyzing existing and potential Southern California real estate projects.
