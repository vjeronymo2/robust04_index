BFN [Report by Michel Schnehage] [Text] Durban Apr 20 SAPA -- Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] National Chairman Frank Mdlalose will stand as candidate for the premiership in kwaZulu/Natal, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Wednesday [20 April]. The announcement was made by the IFP leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi at a mass meeting in Ulundi. The party's full list of candidates had to be handed to the Independent Electoral Commission in Johannesburg by Wednesday night. Mr Buthelezi heads the party's national election list.
