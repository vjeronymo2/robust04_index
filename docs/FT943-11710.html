940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / International Company News: Peter Philip elected chief of Bendigo Mining Mr Peter Philip, president and chief operating officer of Newmont Mining, the large US-based goldminer, until his retirement two months ago, is to become chairman of Bendigo Mining, a small Australian exploration company which is looking to rework the historically famous Bendigo goldfield in Victoria, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. Bendigo sprung in prominance last year, when it was revealed that Sir James Goldsmith, the Anglo-French financier, and Mr Kerry Packer, the Australian businessman, had acquired stakes in the company. Bendigo said yesterday their stakes total 15m shares, or 7.5m shares each, equal to two 5.3 per cent stakes.
