930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Partridge Fine Arts halved to Pounds 1.11m Partridge Fine Arts, the antique dealer, reported halved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.11m for the year to October 31 against Pounds 2.16m. Second half profits fell from Pounds 1.43m to Pounds 126,000. Turnover fell to Pounds 8.36m (Pounds 9.64m). Earnings were 3.25p (6.54p). A maintained final of 1.25p is proposed for an unchanged total of 2.25p
