920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Law Debenture net assets up 17.3% Net asset value per ordinary share of the Law Debenture Corporation rose by 17.3 per cent, from 363.4p to 426.3p, over the year to end-December. Net profits emerged at Pounds 4.3m (Pounds 4m), equal to earnings of 18.92p (17.61p). A proposed final dividend of 11.5p makes a 17.5p (16p) total.
