Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ilgonis Upmalis, head of the Latvian bureau for monitoring the Russian troop withdrawal, reported today that the Russian 15th Air-force Army handed over to Latvia the army headquarters on Brivibas Street and the training center on Slokas Street in Riga at the end of December. According to a decision by a government commission, the Rosme state-owned joint stock company took over the headquarters building as its property and the Civil Defense and the Technical University of Riga took over the training center. According to Upmalis, the 15th Air-force Army has been disbanded and the majority of its military facilities have already been handed over to Latvia. The airfields at Lielvarde and Daugavpils, an army command post at Adazi, as well as several facilities in Riga have not yet been handed over.
