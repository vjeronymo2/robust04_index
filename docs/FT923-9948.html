920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Call over costs of research UNIVERSITIES must charge companies, government departments and charities the full costs of the research they sponsor, the government's Advisory Council on Science and Technology said in a report yesterday. Acost also called on the government to increase funding for academic science at more than the rate of inflation, but said universities should be more selective in the range of research they carry out. The Science Base - Research in Universities. HMSO. Pounds 25.
