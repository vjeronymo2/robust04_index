BFN [Text] Baku, April 13 (AZERTAC)--Last night five people died and 2 were injured as a result of the explosion on the Azerbaijan railways passenger train No. 192 from Moscow to Baku. According to the general department of the Republic's transport police and officers belonging to the National Security Service who are investigating the case on the spot, the explosion occurred in the 1st carriage at 1.20 a.m. as the train was at the "Dagestanskiye Oghni" station near Derbent. Three of the dead were identified as residents of Azerbaijan.
