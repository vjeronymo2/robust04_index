941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Frederick Cooper Frederick Cooper, the Worcester-based architectural hardware, coatings and electrical group, is selling its subsidiary Futters (London) for Pounds 3.75m. Futters, which manufactures electrical components for the audio market, has been bought by Roxburgh Holdings. Frederick Cooper said indications were that its results for the year ended July 31 had met directors' expectations. At the midway stage, the company reported what it called its 'best interim trading performance' since 1989. Last year its pre-tax profits were Pounds 4.12m.
