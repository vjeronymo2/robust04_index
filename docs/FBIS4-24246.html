BFN [Text] The head of state, General Sani Abacha, has appointed Mr. (Saleh Abubakar) to replace Alhaji Abubakar Barde from Taraba State as the government's nominated delegate to the Constitutional Conference, which has begun today. The replacement of Alhaji Abubakar Barde followed information that he was one of those already elected to the conference. A statement by the secretary to the Government of the Federation, Alhaji Aminu Saleh, advised the new nominee to proceed to Abuja immediately for the conference as one of the three nominees from Taraba State.
