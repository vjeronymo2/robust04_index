BFN [Report by Sergey Savchenko of CRIME-PRESS-TASS for UKRINFIRM] [Text] Donetsk, 3 May -- Staff of Ukraine's Security Service have carried out in Mariupol a successful operation to seize and unmask a group of foreign citizens who planned to commit an act of international terrorism in Moscow. It has been established that "it was the direct intention of those detained to commit a terrorist act in an embassy located in the Russian capital," Colonel Dmitriy Chernousenko, deputy head of a department of the Ukrainian Security Service for Donetsk Oblast, stated. No specific targets were named, It was pointed out at a news conference in Donetsk, however, that the suggestion that the terrorists had a "Mideast connection" is not without foundation. The special services of interested foreign states have been briefed on the operation in Mariupol.
