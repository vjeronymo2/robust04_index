BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow June 7 TASS -- The 1994 Russian defence budget should be at least 55,000,000,000,000 rubles instead of the 37,000,000,000,000 approved by the State Duma, Chairman of the Federation Council Budget and Finances Committee Petr Shirshov told a news conference here today. In his words, a closed meeting of the parliament upper chamber discussed the draft federal budget approved by the State Duma in the first reading and decided to agree with the committee proposal on the increased defence spending and changes in their structure. Shirshov said that the Russian defence budget is only one tenth of the U.S. planned military spending.
