February 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THEY HAVE POINTS TO MAKE AFTER 102-24 PREP GAME

South Torrance Coach Gil Ramirez lost twice on Wednesday, Feb. 7.

He obviously lost the game, but he also lost his senses.

If the league is too tough for his school, forfeit the games before the season. To deprive Lisa Leslie a chance for a national record was not true sportsmanship.

ANTHONY BURKES

Los Angeles
