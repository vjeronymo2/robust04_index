"The Crimson Pirate" (1952), directed by Robert Siodmak. 104 minutes. No rating.

A spoof of those Errol Flynn pirate movies. The pirates say "Arrrgh!" a lot and get their peg-legs stuck in the grills over the hatches. And this time it's Burt Lancaster who gets to be gallant as hell. M.F.
