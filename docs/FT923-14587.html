920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Index falls in face of bad news Share prices crumbled in London after bad news on the economic and corporate fronts. Consumer credit fell in May, while a rise in new car sales last month failed to rally confidence. At its lowest, the FT-SE index was more than 34 points off, although it closed at 2.469, a loss of 28.1.
