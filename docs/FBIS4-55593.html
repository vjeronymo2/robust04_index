BFN [Text] Two hundred and fify Croats and Muslims from the Serb-held Banja Luka region in northwestern Bosnia entered Croatia at a UN checkpoint near Nova Gradiska today. Twenty-one refugees proceeded to the Gasinici refugee center in eastern Croatia while the others will either leave for West European countries or stay with their relatives in Croatia. The refugees confirmed that the Serb authorities were carrying out a campaign of terror against Croats and Muslims in the Banja Luka area.
