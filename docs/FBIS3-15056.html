Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] In accordance with an edict by the Russian president, Russian Defense Minister Army General Pavel Grachev has signed an order on recruitment for contract military service in 1994. The document envisages an increase in the number of contractors in the armed forces by 150,000 servicemen. For example, it is expected that 90,000 contractors will be recruited by the ground troops, 10,000 each by the navy and the airborne troops and 5,000 each by the airforce and the air defense forces.
