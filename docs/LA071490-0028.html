July 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ETHNIC STEREOTYPES IN KCAL 'DICK TRACY' CARTOON SERIES

I am a Japanese-American and I used to watch the Dick Tracy cartoon series when I was a kid growing up in the '60s.

It did not make me a racist. Richard Katsuda of the National Coalition for Redress and Reparations stated: "Cartoons like this can be destructive at an early age." Is he serious?

My son, 6, and my daughter, 3 1/2, both love the cartoon series! The minority spokespersons do not speak for me and my children; let them dictate their narrow-minded views to their own families.

HENRY SAKAIDA

Temple City

KCAL on July 6 decided to discontinue broadcasting the "Dick Tracy" cartoon.
