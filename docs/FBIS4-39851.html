BFN [Text] An "epidemiological state of emergency" has been declared in Bishkek, Kirghizia's [Kyrgyzstan] capital. According to the Russian News Agency this measure has been taken in connection with the fact that there has been a considerable increase in the incidence of dysentery and typhoid fever in that city. Until further instructions, all markets and bazaars in Bishkek are being closed where public health requirements are not being complied with.
