Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Palestine Liberation Organization has welcomed the noble religious and humanitarian step by King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, the Servant of the Two Holy Places, and his permission to start a popular fund raising campaign at the level of all parts of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to renovate the Islamic sacrosancts in holy Jerusalem, and save them from a deliberate destruction due to the occupation measures that are aimed at wiping out the Islamic and Arab character of Jerusalem. To that end, His Royal Highness Prince Salman Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz has issued an appeal through the various information media to the citizens and residents of the Kingdom, urging them to make effective and generous contributions to save holy Jerusalem.
