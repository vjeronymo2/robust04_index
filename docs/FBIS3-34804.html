Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 14 Feb (INA) -- Iraqi Transport and Communications Minister Dr. Ahmad Murtada Ahmad Khalil, arrived here today within the context of a trade protocol Iraq and Sudan have signed in the field of transportation. In a statement to the INA correspondent in Khartoum, Dr. Ahmad Murtada Ahmad Khalil said his four-day visit to Sudan seeks to promote constant bilateral cooperation in the field of transportation, to discuss the exchange of expertise, and to encourage visits by officials from both countries so they may learn about the potential for bilateral cooperation in this regard. The transport and communications minister expressed Iraq's willingness to cooperate fully and constructively with the fraternal Sudanese, and to provide them with technical expertise in this regard to foster strong and special ties between the two countries -- ties that will realize the aspirations and ambitions of their peoples -- in order to overcome difficult circumstances and break the embargo imposed on Iraq.
