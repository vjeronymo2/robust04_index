940411 FT 11 APR 94 / International Company News: Chairman of Lagardere sees profits increase Lagardere Groupe should see an increase in profits this year compared with 1993, Mr Jean-Luc Lagardere, the chairman said, writes John Ridding in Paris. Addressing a shareholders' meeting called to approve the absorbtion of Matra-Hachette, the missiles to magazines group, into Lagardere, the chairman also indicated his company would play a role in the privatisation of Renault, the state-owned car group. Mr Lagardere confirmed Matra-Hachette achieved net profits of about FFr620m (Dollars 108m) last year, in line with estimates, and confirmed net profits at Lagardere of FFr154m. Lagardere Groupe now has a 93.4 per cent stake in Matra-Hachette, increased from 37.6 per cent.
