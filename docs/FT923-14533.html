920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Tories confident on stamp duty CONSERVATIVE party managers were last night confident the government would survive a threatened revolt today by MPs objecting to the re-introduction of stamp duty on house purchases below Pounds 250,000. Despite public calls from about 50 backbench Tories for an extension beyond next month of the present tax holiday, most have signalled they would not support a Labour amendment to the finance bill.
