920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Benchmark sells banking business Benchmark Group is to sell its banking business, Benchmark Bank, to a related company, Dao Heng Bank of Hong Kong, for Pounds 8.4m. Benchmark and Dao Heng are both controlled by the Quek family of Malaysia, and the sale requires approval from independent shareholders. Benchmark also announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.75m (Pounds 4.95m) in the six months to December 31.
