BFN [Text] As reported earlier, the intra-Tajik talks are continuing in Moscow. According to representatives of the Tajik Government delegation the agenda for the further talks on national reconciliation in Tajikistan was adopted yesterday. The sides agreed that the following issues should be placed on the agenda on the basis of proposals by the Tajik Government delegation and the Tajik opposition: 1. Measures for settling the political situation in Tajikistan by political means, in particular the measures for putting an end to armed clashes and provocations on the Tajik-Afghan border and in other areas of the Republic of Tajikistan as well as the [words indistinct] activity of the mass media in Tajikistan with the purpose of all-round support to the process of national reconciliation. 2. Solving the problems of refugees. 3. Issues concerning the building of constitutional structures and strengthening the state structure of the Republic of Tajikistan. The sides issued a joint statement. The talks are continuing.
