Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The visit which Iraqi Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz was expected to pay to the UN headquarters in New York today has been postponed until 15 March. The purpose of the visit is to hold a new round of talks on removing Iraqi weapons of mass destruction. Lieutenant General 'Amir Rashid, head of Iraq's Military Industrialization Organization; and Riyad al-Qaysi, Iraqi Foreign Ministry under secretary, are scheduled to join these talks. A UN source said the first round of talks which 'Aziz will hold with Ambassador Rolf Ekeus, head of the UN commission in charge of destroying the Iraqi arms arsenal, will be held on Wednesday.
