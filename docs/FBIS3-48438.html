Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 26 (KCNA) -- The opposition Democratic Party of South Korea at a caucus meeting Monday decided to inaugurate a measure committee based on the human rights committee of the party to promote the release of all the prisoners of conscience, a Seoul-based radio reported. The Democratic Party paid high tribute to the reunification will of the late Rev. Mun Ik-hwan and repeatedly urged the authorities to pardon and reinstate him.
