Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin, Feb. 15 (YONHAP) -- North Korea and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) opened a working-level contact on Tuesday on the issue of nuclear inspection of North Korea, sources at IAEA said. The sources said North Korea asked IAEA on Tuesday morning to have working-level contact. It was the first contact between North Korea and IAEA in 20 days after their last contact took place on last Jan. 25. Officials of the North Korean Embassy in Vienna carried instructions from Pyongyang to the talks, the source said. But, no contents of the Pyongyang instructions were immediately known. In this connection, IAEA spokesman Hans Meyer said that at the moment he could not confirm nor deny the resumption of the contact.
