February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BIG WEST

UC Irvine and Fresno State both have 7-6 Big West Conference records and are coming off come-from-behind overtime victories, but there's little doubt about who is favored in tonight's game.

Irvine (10-12 overall) beat Fresno (12-10) a month ago, 64-61, but the Anteaters are 0-9 in Selland Arena. JOHN WEYLER
