May 17, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Chocolate Contest -- A caption appearing in the May 3 Food section incorrectly named the winners of a truffle contest held during the Chocolate Weekend at Le Meridien in Newport Beach. According to Josseline Tamers of the hotel: "The confection picturing Newport Beach Marina is the work of Yvan Valentin of L'Orangerie in Los Angeles. The French chef received the top prize of $300 along with Russell Armstrong of Trees restaurant in Corona del Mar, who also won first place for his truffle presentation."
