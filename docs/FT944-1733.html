941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Business Travel: Chinese road accidents Business visitors to China may ponder its death and accident rates for road travel. More than 6,000 people were killed on the roads in China last month, the National Public Transportation Security Department said. This was a rise of 10.1 per cent on November last year, the department added in a report carried by the Xinhua news agency yesterday. Road accidents rose 3.7 per cent to 23,096; 12,480 people were injured, up 2.8 per cent.
