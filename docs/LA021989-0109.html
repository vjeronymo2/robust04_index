February 19, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GORBACHEV AND TRUST

A front-page article ("Survey Shows Change in Cold War Thinking" on Jan. 24) declared that Gorbachev was rated more favorably than President Bush in a Times Orange County Poll.

I live in Orange County and nobody asked my opinion. The only list that Gorbachev appears on of mine is the one entitled, "People I Least Trust." He's not even No. 1 on that list. He follows a close second -- after the news media!

BRENDA HESTER

Garden Grove
