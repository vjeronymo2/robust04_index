930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Markets: Income trust relaunched - At a glance The Scottish Provident Global Income Trust is being relaunched as the Prolific Global Income unit trust, under the investment management of Prolific. Under its previous management, the fund was bottom of the international balanced sector over the five years to September 1 (source: Hardwick Stafford Wright), although it had an above average performance over the past year.
