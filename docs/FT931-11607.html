930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Rise in Welsh capital spending CAPITAL spending in Wales is to total some Pounds 1.5bn in 1993-94 representing a year-on-year increase of 8.5 per cent, Mr David Hunt, Welsh secretary, announced yesterday. Mr Hunt also used Welsh questions to unveil details of seven new industrial projects involving investment by companies of over Pounds 13m and creating more than 600 jobs. 'The Welsh economy is already in the forefront of the economic upturn,' he said.
