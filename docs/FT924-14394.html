921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Nuclear Electric to seek private cash NUCLEAR ELECTRIC, the state-owned power station operator in England and Wales, intends to be viewed 'as a private sector investment' for its next programme of station construction after 1994, Mr John Collier, chairman, said yesterday. He added: 'We will not be offering returns that are volatile and chancy - they will not be as exciting as returns on shares in speculative and risky businesses.'
