BFN [Text] Skopje, 25 Jun (TANJUG) -- The Democratic Alliance of Serbs in Macedonia [DSSM] today sent a public message to the citizens of Serb nationality instructing them to provide information for the P1 [population census] form only if the form is printed in two languages--Serbian and Macedonian--and to refuse to take part in the census if the form is printed only in Macedonian. A statement released by the DSSM says that this message was sent to the Serbs in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] because some census collectors are trying to register the Serbs on forms printed in Macedonian, with the assistance of an official interpreter, which is in contravention of the Law on the Census applied to citizens belonging to national communities.
