BFN [Text] Amman, 12 Apr (INA) -- King Husayn of Jordan this afternoon received Hamid Yusuf Hammadi, [Iraqi] minister of culture and information and the personal envoy of President Saddam Husayn. Hammadi conveyed to King Husayn a personal message from President Saddam Husayn that deals with the fraternal bilateral relations and the importance of coordinating stands with Arab and friendly countries to support Iraq's position to have the unjust embargo lifted. King Husayn stressed to President Saddam Husayn's personal envoy Jordan's solidarity with Iraq, especially since Iraq has proven its compliance with Security Council resolutions, which means that the Security Council should now implement Paragraph 22 of Security Council Resolution 687 and immediately lift the Iraq embargo. The meeting was attended by Royal Court Chief al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir and the Iraqi ambassador to Jordan.
