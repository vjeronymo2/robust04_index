920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK Company News: BDM to seek full listing British Data Management, the subject of a management buy-in from the Britannia Security Group in 1989, is seeking a full listing for its shares within the next few weeks. The shares are being placed by NM Rothschild and broker to the issue is Smith New Court. Following flotation, the company, a specialist provider of data storage and management services, is expected to have a market capital of Pounds 25m. For the year to end-June 1991 BDM returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.33m on turnover of Pounds 12.68m.
