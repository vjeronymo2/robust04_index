July 11, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SDSU'S HICKS CUT BY CANADIAN TEAM

Former San Diego State defensive back Harold Hicks has been cut by the Hamilton Tiger-Cats of the Canadian Football League.

Hicks joined the Tiger-Cats last season after he was cut by the Washington Redskins in training camp. The Redskins selected Hicks in the seventh round of the 1988 draft.
