920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / UK Company News: William Jackson incurs Pounds 1.01m loss Exceptional charges of Pounds 1.19m left William Jackson & Son with losses of Pounds 1.01m in the year to April 25, against with profits of Pounds 2.98m. Turnover fell from Pounds 207.8m to Pounds 205.8m. Losses per share for this company, which trades as a baker, meat products manufacturer and discount stores operator, came out at 31.4p, against earnings of 77.5p.
