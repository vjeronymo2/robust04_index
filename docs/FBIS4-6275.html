BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- A friendly gathering between the participants in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival and artistes in Pyongyang took place in Pyongyang on the evening of April 18. Present on invitation were foreign artistes and overseas Korean artistes who had participated in the festival. Present were Chang Chol, vice-premier of the Administration Council and minister of culture and art and chairman of the organising committee of the festival, officials concerned and artistes in the city. The participants exchanged impressive and emotional stories during the festival and achievements and experiences they have gained in the work to create popular and revolutionary culture and art and spent the evening with songs and dances.
