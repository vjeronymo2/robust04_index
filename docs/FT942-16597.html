940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Pounds 50m office plan for Nottingham A Pounds 50m office scheme is to be built on the site of the former general hospital in Nottingham, in what is believed to be the city's largest single development for 20 years. Comfort House Investment Properties, a Cardiff-based developer, bought the six-acre city-centre site from the Nottingham Health Authority a month ago. The proposed scheme comprises 12 buildings, which will provide 300,000 sq ft of offices, a hotel and a large public square.
