BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Attempts To Halt Emigration in Kirghizia"] [Text] The Kirghiz Government has formulated a program to reduce the level of emigration. It contains a package of documents aimed at creating the requisite legislative base for emigration based on international legal norms as well as proposals to create joint ventures and production units, interstate investment funds, and a balanced cadre policy. The package also includes documents examining the full-fledged use of Russian within the system of public education, science, technology, and culture. A considerable part of the program is devoted to tightening up administrative and criminal liability for fueling interethnic enmity. What is more, special measures have been formulated to avert a "brain drain."
