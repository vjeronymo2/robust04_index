BFN [Text] Ankara, April 20 (AA) -- Turkey is against any kind of forceful border changing policy, the Foreign Ministry said on Wednesday [20 April]. Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman said at his weekly briefing that a draft was signed by the 11-nation Minsk Group in an effort to find a solution to the problem in Nagorno-Karabakh, during their meeting in Prague on April 11-15. Ataman said it is the second document signed after the Rome Agreement, adding that it is very important that free exchange of ideas can be held between representatives of conflicting sides. He said that Armenia should give up its new offensive against Azerbaijan, which is a violation of UN, CSCE and other international norms. Ataman renewed Turkey's support for Azerbaijan's violated territorial unity.
