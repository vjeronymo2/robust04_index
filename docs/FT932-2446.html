930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Bradstock Bradstock Group, the insurance broker, reported an 8 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.23m to Pounds 4.57m in the six months to March 31. The improvement was led by the direct brokerage activities. Turnover rose from Pounds 12.3m to Pounds 14.4m. Earnings per share were 5.32p (4.65p). The interim dividend is raised to 1.55p (1.45p).
