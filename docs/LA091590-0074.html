September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

NEWHALL PARENTS PRAISED

Hats off to the Newhall School District parents for their willingness to make personal sacrifices in order to provide a new, much needed, school for their children.

If these parents can manage this monumental task, maybe there's a money management lesson to be learned by the state of California. Every community deserves the same caliber school the Newhall School District will have. Wake up, California. Where are your priorities?

SHARON KELLEY

Canyon Country
