920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Ossory Estates Pre-tax profits of Ossory Estates, the property investment and development group, amounted to Pounds 1.52m for the six months to December 31, down from Pounds 4.05m for the corresponding period of 1990. Turnover totalled Pounds 7.36m (Pounds 4.26m). A nominal interim dividend of 0.15p (0.4p) is declared.
