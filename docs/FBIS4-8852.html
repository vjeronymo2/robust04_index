BFN [Excerpts] Cajnice, 19 Apr (TANJUG) -- Srpska republic army positions on the right bank of the Drina River have been under fierce Muslim fire from Gorazde since yesterday, despite the cease-fire agreement signed by Radovan Karadzic and Yasushi Akashi two days ago, the command of the Herzegovina Corps reports. [passage omitted] Serb military sources point out that all residential buildings in Gorazde, including the buildings of international humanitarian organizations, are in fact fortresses from which fire is being directed toward Serb positions.
