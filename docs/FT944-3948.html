941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Stagecoach invests Sistema Metrobus de Bogota, in which Stagecoach Holdings, the UK's largest bus operating group, has a 25 per cent stake, has won a 23 year concession to provide a mass transit system for Bogota in Colombia. Stagecoach's investment is expected to be about Dollars 3m (Pounds 1.8m) in the system, the building of which should start during 1995 and be implemented over the next 2 1/2 years. Stagecoach's partners are Volvo Bus with 25 per cent and local interests which hold the balance.
