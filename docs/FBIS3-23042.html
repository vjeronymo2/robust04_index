Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] France has offered to cooperate with India in the fight against terrorism. The external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh, who met the French interior minister, Mr. Charles Pasqua, in Paris yesterday outlined the problems faced by India due to terrorism. According to the Indian Embassay in Paris, Mr. Pasqua expressed full understanding and stated that France is prepared to cooperate with India in combatting terrorism.
