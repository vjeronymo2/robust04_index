Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow January 14 TASS -- An official dinner in honor of U.S. President Bill Clinton and his wife Hillary was given tonight in candlelight in the Kremlin's Palace of Facets by President Boris Yeltsin and his wife Naina. The presidents and their wives were seated at the central table near U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and his wife. A toast of Boris Yeltsin noted history has given to Russia and the United States a unique chance to make their great possibilities come true. The Russian president raised the toast to welfare and prosperity of the two peoples. He congratulated the U.S. guests with New Year marked today on the Orthodox calendar. In his turn, Bill Clinton wished the Russian people and president health and happiness. The dinner offered champagne, Stolichnaya Vodka, Armenian cognacs, white and red wines, Russian cuisine, including caviar, salmon and shchi, Russian cabbage soup. The official dinner finished with a concert of choreographic and music groups of Russia in Kremlin's St. George Hall.
