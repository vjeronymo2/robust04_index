Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 20 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent a message of greetings to Remmy K.K.G. Mushota on his appointment as foreign minister of the Republic of Zambia. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would develop more favorably, the message wholeheartedly wished him great success in his responsible work.
