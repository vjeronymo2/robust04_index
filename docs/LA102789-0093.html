October 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A mini-brawl broke out during the San Francisco 49ers practice in Santa Clara, Calif.

The team had been in a two-minute drill when a skirmish broke out at the line of scrimmage between offensive players and defensive players.

The initial shoving and punching match involved defensive lineman Kevin Fagan and offensive linemen Steve Wallace and Bubba Paris. Several other players joined in the fray, but there were no apparent injuries.
