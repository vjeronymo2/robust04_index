Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek, 4 Feb -- The Central Electoral Commission of the Republic of Kyrgyzstan today announced the final results of the nationwide referendum on confidence in President Askar Akayev. The referendum was held on 30 January. A total of 2,176,000 people took part in the poll. This is 95.9 percent of all Kyrgyzstanis eligible to vote. A total of 96.3 percent of them expressed confidence in President Askar Akayev.
