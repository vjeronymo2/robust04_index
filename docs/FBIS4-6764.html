BFN ["Message from Khieu Samphan to His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk Varaman"; dated 22 April; place not given -- read by announcer] [Text] Your Majesty: I am pleased to acknowledge receipt of your 22 April 1994 statement. In keeping with the request and with the deepest desire of our entire nation and people, I, on behalf of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea, would like to express total support for the proposal, the product of your profound insight, to organize a round-table meeting for peace and national reconciliation under your chairmanship at the Royal Palace in Phnom Penh from Monday, 2 April 1994, to Saturday, 7 April 1994. Please forgive any inappropriateness. [Signed] Khieu Samphan, chairman of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea. [Dated] 22 April 1994.
