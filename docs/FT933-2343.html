930918 FT 18 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: China hints at Olympics boycott A Chinese Olympic official warned that the country might boycott the 1996 Games in Atlanta unless Beijing was chosen as the site for the games in 2000. His comments were later rejected by Beijing. A decision on the site for the Olympiad will be made by the International Olympic Committee on Thursday.
