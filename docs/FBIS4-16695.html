BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 19 Apr -- State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin today met with PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasir 'Arafat. During the meeting, Yasir 'Arafat expressed confidence that the Russian side will render necessary assistance in implementing the recent agreements between the PLO and Israel, of which Russia and the United States are cosponsors. At the same time, Yasir 'Arafat noted that there have been recent "attempts to depart from the agreement and to push the Russian Federation to the sidelines in this process." For his part, Ivan Rybkin told journalists after the meeting that "the Russian side favors the unconditional implementation of all UN Security Council resolutions concerning a peace settlement in the Middle East." The State Duma chairman said he hopes the agreement between the PLO and Israel "will be implemented without double standards." "I hope the last Israeli soldier will soon leave the occupied territories and free parliamentary elections will take place there to which the Duma will send its observers," Ivan Rybkin said.
