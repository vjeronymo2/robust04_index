Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has ordered Defense Minister Haytham Qasim Tahir to return the southern troops deployed in the Abyan area, east of Aden, to their barracks in Hadhramaut in the east. In a message to the defense minister, President Salih ordered his defense minister to ban armed flights without a prior agreement with the chief of staff and the higher commander of the Yemeni Armed Forces. In his message, the Yemeni president stressed that moving a military unit from one place to another without a regular approval by the higher commander of the Armed Forces constitutes a violation of the Constitution and the document of accord, which was signed in Amman last Sunday to end the political crisis in the country.
