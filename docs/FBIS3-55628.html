Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By TURKMENPRESS correspondent Anna Kurbanova -- TASS] [Text] Ashgabat, 10 Jan -- The Council of CIS Border Guards Commanders met in Ashgabat today under the chairmanship of Andrey Nikolayev, commander of Russia's border troops. The meeting was attended by representatives of eight Commonwealth states. Moldova, Georgia and Tajikistan were not represented. The participants in the meeting discussed the results of work done by the council and its coordination service during 1993, as well as a plan of measures to be carried out during 1994. It was noted that all countries belonging to the Economic Union have an interest in the protection of the external borders of the CIS states. The members of the council prepared for submission to the CIS Secretariat a document dealing with the problem of illegal migration and drug smuggling across the external borders of the Commonwealth countries. Additions and amendments to the regulations on the council's coordinating service were presented. The next meeting of the Council of CIS Border Guards Commanders will be held in Moscow in April.
