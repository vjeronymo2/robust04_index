940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Grafton makes Pounds 2.5m acquisition Grafton Group, the Dublin-based builders merchant, is buying Thrower Brothers Group (Holdings), a plumbing merchant and fasteners distributor, for Pounds 2.5m cash. Thrower reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 209,341 on turnover of Pounds 7.3m for the year to March 31. At the period end net assets were Pounds 1.48m.
