Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin issued by the National Defense Secretariat in Mexico City on 12 January; from the "24 Hours" newscast -- read by announcer] [Text] In compliance with an order from the president of the Republic and supreme armed forces commander, the Mexican Army and Air Force will act as follows: They will not open fire on armed groups unless they attack the Army or Air Force, directly endanger the civilian population, or try to return to towns or positions they initially occupied. Specifically, the Air Force will suspend its fire, except in cases of aggression against Army personnel who require its support in unpopulated areas. They will continue to protect towns that requested their help to prevent possible attacks. They will not permit any movement by these armed people to areas outside the region. They will maintain the free mobilization of their personnel and equipment, including ground patrol and air reconnaissance. They will redouble their contribution to the government effort to distribute food, medicines, and other items which are basic to the affected population.
