BFN [Text] Brussels, April 16 (AFP) -- The pilot of the Sea Harrier jump jet shot down over Gorazde in Bosnia-Hercegovina ejected from the airplane and escaped with only "minor scratches," a NATO spokesman said here Saturday. He said the aircraft had been on a non-offensive operation over the mainly-Moslem UN-protected "safe area," currently under Serb attack, when it was shot down.
