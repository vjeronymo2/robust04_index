Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Knin, 28 Feb (TANJUG)--In connection with the downing of the planes by NATO in the airspace of former Bosnia-Herzegovina, the main headquarters of the Serb army of Krajina announced this evening that no aircraft belonging to that republic had taken off today. "The aircraft of the air force of the republic of Serb Krajina [RSK] made no training or any other flights, and no flights had been scheduled," a short statement from the main headquarters says.
