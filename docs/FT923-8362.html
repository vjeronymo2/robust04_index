920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Trimoco repeats no action call on bid As Hartwell yesterday issued its official offer document, Trimoco, its target, again advised shareholders to take no action. It has promised to write to them with its views on the bid. Hartwell is offering 17 1/2 p per share and Pounds 175 for Pounds 100 nominal of 11.5 per cent convertible unsecured loan stock for Trimoco, the Dunstable-based motor dealer. Trimoco's shares closed unchanged at 19p.
