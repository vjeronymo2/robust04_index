920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Karabakh ceasefire plan Iran has brokered a draft ceasefire agreement between Armenia and Azerbaijan over the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh, the Iranian news agency IRNA said. The plan, signed by Armenian and Azerbaijani deputy foreign ministers, proposes a ceasefire, the exchange of prisoners and efforts to remove regional economic sanctions.
