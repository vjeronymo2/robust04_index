BFN [Text] Today, the administration of Maritime Kray released a statement in which it said that dismantling and scrapping of atomic submarines is to be halted at the Zvezda yard at the settlement of Bolshoy Kamen. Discharge of liquid radioactive waste stored aboard the tanker DMT-5 into the Sea of Japan is also planned. The tanker is now in a dangerous condition. The radioactive waste poses a real threat to Vladivostok and a number of areas of Maritime Kray. That was a report from POSTFACTUM agency.
