930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / Ex-Eagle Trust chief to appeal MR John Ferriday, the former chief executive of Eagle Trust convicted of stealing more than Pounds 12m from the mini-conglomerate, is to appeal against his conviction. He was jailed for six years last month after being convicted of five theft charges. He was said to have siphoned the money from the company to cover losses sustained in the 1987 stock market crash.
