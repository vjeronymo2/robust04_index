BFN [Text] Simferopol [no date as received] -- On 3 May the president of the Republic of Crimea made public a decree on celebrating the constitution adopted in the autonomous republic on 6 May 1992. The decree says in part that 6 May has been declared Constitution Day of the Republic of Crimea and that this day is a day of rest. It will be declared a state holiday. The Crimean Government will take measures to ensure that Constitution Day is celebrated. The decree comes into force on the day of signing. It will be submitted to the Crimean Supreme Council for approval.
