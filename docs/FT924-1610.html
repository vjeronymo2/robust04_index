921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Hoover probes flight offer HOOVER, the electrical goods manufacturer, has launched an investigation into a free flights offer after claims that the rules had been altered. The inquiry concerns an apparent change in the conditions of a recent promotion offering free airline tickets for flights in Europe to purchasers of Hoover goods. Hoover said there had been an error by JSI Travel, the company running the Europe offer.
