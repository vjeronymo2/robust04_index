930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy Cairn Energy, the oil and gas exploration company, has raised Dollars 17.3m (Pounds 11.6m) through the issue of new shares in CEUSA, its US subsidiary. Receipts from the sale, leaving the UK group with a 64.1 per cent stake, will be used by CEUSA to repay debts of Dollars 2.6m to Cairn and Dollars 3.6m in redemption of preferred stock.
