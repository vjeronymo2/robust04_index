BFN [Text] Moscow, 14 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has issued a number of edicts on personnel changes in the leadership of the Russian procurator general's office. According to an announcement from the Russian president's press service, Vildan Uzbekov has been appointed first deputy procurator general. Oleg Gaydanov and Mikhail Slavgorodskiy have been appointed deputy procurators general. Valentin Panichev has been made a Russian deputy procurator general and head of the main administration for supervision over enforcement of the laws in the Armed Forces (main military procurator). President Boris Yeltsin has relieved Eduard Denisov, Ivan Zemlyanushin, and Vladimir Kravtsev of their duties as Russian deputy procurators general in connection with their transfer to other work.
