April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CITY LINES: CRAFTED CABINET

The crafted cabinet of select timber and hand-hammered metal timber, designed by Tony Stuart, opens up to form a cocktail bar. It was displayed to very good advantage in the fifth-floor corridor of the center's Green Building by the firm of Uniquely Australian. Its showroom wasn't quite ready but its products were, the cabinet being one of a wide selection from Down Under. SAM HALL KAPLAN
