BFN [From the "Morning News" program] [Text] Mayor Huang Ju met with Dr. Kissinger, former U.S. Secretary of State and an old friend of the Chinese people, in the evening of 14 May. The two had a warm and friendly conversation. Huang Ju warmly welcomed Kissinger's return to Shanghai and hoped he would maintain his friendship and cooperation with China, particularly his cooperation with Shanghai. Kissinger praised Shanghai as an exciting city. He said: Shanghai's economic progress over the last two or three years is much greater than its progress in the preceding decade and more. Deng Xiaoping discussed some reform measures two years ago, and his views have been affirmed [de dao liao zheng shi] by the changes in last two years. Kissinger arrived in Shanghai on 14 May afternoon. During his stay in Shanghai, he and his party will tour the New Pudong District.
