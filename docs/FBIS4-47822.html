BFN [Text] Messages congratulating Mr. Mandela on his election victory are still streaming in. The German chancellor, Dr. Helmut Kohl, assured Mr. Mandela of practical support and offered development assistance. New Zealand's Prime Minister Jim Bolger praised Mr. de Klerk for his role in dismantling apartheid. Messages of congratulations have also been received from heads of government, among others, from Britain, Australia, the United States, and South Korea. In London the secretary general of the Commonwealth, Chief Emeka Anyaoku, reacted as follows: [Begin Anyaoku recording, in English] I am confident that the start which is being made at the moment, all the statements and indeed the interim constitution of South Africa, that all these point in the right direction. [end recording]
