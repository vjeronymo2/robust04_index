Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- The draft of the law on urban real estate administration was approved in principle today at the 17th executive meeting of the Chinese State Council. The draft law will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for examination. The executive meeting, which was presided over by Premier Li Peng, held that it is necessary to put the urban real estate administration on a legal basis in order to ensure a sound development of the real estate industry.
