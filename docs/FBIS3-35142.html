Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has again called for convening an Arab conference on water immediately within the framework of the Arab League. The conference should include the countries directly concerned with this problem and other concerned countries that want to participate in it so as to define the future strategy of Arab water security within the framework of Pan-Arab security. In a lecture he delivered yesterday evening at the Egyptian University of International Law in Cairo, he said that international developments, major economic challenges, and giant cartels that have begun to form in the world require the Arab countries to take action quickly and to enhance their solidarity and cooperation to be able to deal with these challenges and blocs strongly and effectively.
