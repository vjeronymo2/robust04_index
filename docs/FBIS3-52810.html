Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The meeting of the foreign ministers of the Arab countries that signed the Damascus Declaration opened in Damascus this evening. During the meeting, which will continue until tomorrow, the ministers will focus on evaluating the march of the Damascus Declaration and the difficulties facing it. They will also examine practical steps to activate the principles of the declaration and implement its provisions in order to reinforce cooperation among the Damascus Declaration countries in various fields. Moreover, the ministers will discuss the international and regional situation, especially developments in the peace process in light of the anticipated summit between President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton.
