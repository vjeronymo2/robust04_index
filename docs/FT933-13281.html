930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Yorks Water buys third finance leasing company based water and sewage company, is acquiring its third finance leasing company as part of its ongoing treasury management programme. The company announced yesterday that it had acquired JHSW Leasing (9), a wholly owned subsidiary of J Henry Schroder Wagg. The company, to be renamed Yorkshire Water Third Finance, has a portfolio of finance leases, with lease rentals receivable of Pounds 65.5m. The shares in JHSW have been purchased for Pounds 100 calculated by reference to net asset value, and in addition certain of existing loan facilities totalling some Pounds 30m, previously provided to the company by Schroders, have been refinanced by Yorkshire Water. The purchase price and refinancing came from existing borrowing facilities of Yorkshire Water. Yorkshire said that it expects to earn a good rate of return on this investment.
