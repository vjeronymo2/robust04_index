BFN [Statement from the Office of the President of the Republic of Croatia -- read by announcer] [Text] President Tudjman today received Muhamed Zulic, a deputy of the Croatian Assembly, in the presence of Jure Radic, the HDZ [Croatian Democratic Union] secretary general. With regard to the situation in the assembly, Deputy Zulic said he continues to be an HDZ member, adding that he will explain to the public the misunderstandings in connection with his joining the Croatian Independent Democrats. They also spoke about the implementation of the Washington agreement. In this connection, Mr. Zulic said that both as an HDZ deputy and as a Croat of the Muslim religion, he wishes to take an active part in the implementation of the Washington agreement and to work on building trust between the Croats and Muslims.
