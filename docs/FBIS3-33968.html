Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Cypriot Minister of Communications and Works Adhamos Adhamidhis left Beirut for Larnaca this afternoon after a three-day visit to Lebanon during which he held talks with his Lebanese counterpart Muhammad Ghaziri. A technical cooperation and coordination accord between the two countries was signed. It aims to promote Lebanese-Cypriot relations in posts and communications and the services linked to them. Minister Ghaziri announced the formation of a technical committee comprised of Lebanese and Cypriots to draft an agreement on laying an undersea communications axial fiber cable between the two countries. He added that efforts are under way to extend an undersea cable with a capacity of 8000 axial lines. The first phase will begin with 4000 lines, of which Syria will have 16 percent with the remainder shared equally between Lebanon and Cyprus. [passage omitted] The Cypriot minister, who also called on Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz, said the ongoing negotiations mark the start of important cooperation to develop ties between the two countries. He hoped that the accord will be a prelude to the continuation of good contacts between the two countries.
