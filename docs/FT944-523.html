941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / NHS charges About half of all National Health Service prescription items - 222.8m out of the 445.4m - cost less than the prescription charge, the government acknowledged in a Commons written answer. Government figures also show that NHS spending on health services from private-sector providers has risen 70 per cent in two years - from Pounds 208.5m in 1991-92 to a provisional Pounds 357.4m in 1993-94.
