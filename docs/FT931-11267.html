930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Pelican acquires Fulham Road lease Pelican Group, the restaurateur, has acquired the lease of 855 Fulham Road, London, SW6, currently occupied by the group and trading as a Cafe Rouge restaurant. Consideration for the 25 year lease was 394,737 Pelican ordinary shares which, with the share price closing unchanged at 40p last night values the deal at about Pounds 158,000. The acquisition of the lease will result in a rental saving of about Pounds 27,000 a year.
