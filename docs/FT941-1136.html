940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Inspec offer oversubscribed The intermediaries offer for shares in Inspec, the speciality chemicals company, was over eight times subscribed with applications received for more than Pounds 141m of shares. Intermediaries have been allocated about 12 per cent of the number of shares for which they made applications on a pro rata basis. Monies in respect of excess applications were returned to intermediaries yesterday. Inspec is coming to the market with a valuation of Pounds 136.4m following a Pounds 49.5m share sale. Of these, Pounds 32.3m were placed by stockbrokers Cazenove with its clients. Morgan Grenfell was sponsor to the issue.
