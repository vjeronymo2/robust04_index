920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Bradstock advances 14% to Pounds 4.23m Bradstock Group, the insurance broker, improved pre-tax profits by 14 per cent in the half year to March 31. On turnover up 17 per cent to Pounds 12.3m (Pounds 10.5m) the pre-tax result was Pounds 4.23m against Pounds 3.73m. Mr Oliver Plunkett, chairman, said the direct retail broking businesses had shown remarkable progress with a 23 per cent growth in turnover. Earnings per share worked through at 4.65p (4.31p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 1.45p (1.35p). Mr Plunkett said prospects for the second half remained enigmatic and the uncertainty surrounding the Lloyd's market made 'clairvoyance difficult on the reinsurance side.'
