941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Ransomes sale Sears Property Developments has agreed to pay Ransomes Pounds 9.75m for 12.3 acres of land at The Sandlings site at Ransomes Europark, Ipswich. The sale is subject to a number of possible retentions, of which the cost to Ransomes will be about Pounds 1.2m. The granting of certain planning permissions is a condition of the sale. If Sears cannot exchange contracts for the sale, and for occupational lettings of 84 per cent of the site, by January 12 1995, it will be entitled to terminate the agreement within four days.
