Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 8 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the invitation of the Indian President Sh. D. Sharma, the Government of the Kingdom of Thailand, the Lao President Nouhak Phoumsavan, the chairman of the SRV Le Duc Anh the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat will be on an official visit to India in February 21-25, the Kingdom of Thailand in February 25-28, the LPDR from February 28 up to March 3, the SRV in March 3-6, the Ministry for External Relations of Mongolia informed today. The government and people of Laos will welcome President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat and those accompanying him during his visit to be paid to the Lao People's Democratic Republic considering the current visit as an important historical landmark in the relationship of the two countries i.e. Laos and Mongolia in the period of the transition to market relations. This visit is to promote raising of prestige of Mongolia in the South-Eastern Asia as a whole region being regarded as a region characterized by progressive social and economic development and a political stability as well, underlined in connection with the forthcoming visit of the Mongolian president to this country. As was said by the Ambassador of Laos to Mongolia Mr. Chaleng Varinthrasak, the two countries and peoples from time immemorial have established traditionally good relations. Mongolia and Laos have exchanged visits of their leaders rendering mutual assistance and support both in the cause of defence and strengthening of their countries and on the international scene. The "friendship" hospital and the cattle breeding farm built for residents of the Province of Siengkhuang are symbolizing the friendly relations of the two countries. At present the cooperation in the spheres of economy, trade, and culture should be promoted which is nowadays more needed than whenever taking into account the possibilities of the two sides being based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit, the ambassador noted. In late February of the current year the Mongolian president is likely to pay official visits to such countries as India, Thailand, Laos and Vietnam.
