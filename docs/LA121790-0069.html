December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

BANGLADESH;

INDEPENDENCE HERO ENDS 15-YEAR EXILE

Kader Siddiqi, a hero of Bangladesh's independence struggle, returned home from a 15-year exile to a tumultuous welcome and pledged to help restore democracy to the South Asia nation. Tens of thousands swarmed over Dhaka's airport to greet Siddiqi -- known as "the Tiger" for his guerrilla exploits in the 1971 war against Pakistan -- upon his return from Calcutta. He had been sentenced in absentia to life in prison for killing an army major and several other soldiers in a 1975 revolt.
