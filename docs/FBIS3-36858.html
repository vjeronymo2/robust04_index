Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Feb 09, BNS -- Estonian Defense Minister Indrek Kannik today had a meeting with the U.S Ambassador to Estonia, Robert C. Frasure. Also participating in the meeting were representatives of the "Military to Military Contact" program. The U.S. ambassador spoke about the United States' general cooperation program in the military sector for Central and East Europe, and stopped at some length on further prospects in working closely on embassy and military attache level. The Estonian defense minister was presented with a review of the possibilities offered by the "Military to Military Contact" program.
