BFN [Text] During the course of a clearing up operation, mojahedin and forces of the Islamic army in Samangan Province pushed back the Golboddin-Dostam militia as far as 8 km. One of the country's responsible security sources told the Bakhtar Information Agency that forces of the Islamic army and mojahedin cleared a large area of Samangan Province from the dirt of the militia during the course of a clearing up operation on 27 June. As a result, 10 members of the Golboddin-Dostam militia were killed, and a large amount of their light and heavy weapons have been captured as booty by the mojahedin. The report adds that the militia has completely lost its combat morale, and militia members were obliged to flee upon leaving their positions.
