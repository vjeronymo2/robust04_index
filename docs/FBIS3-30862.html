Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Budapest, 7 February (MTI) -- The U.S. State Department's recent report on the enforcement of human rights in Hungary is unauthentic and biased, Istvan Csurka of the Party of Hungarian Justice and Life told parliament today. "Concerning racial incidents and the freedom of the press, the compilers of the report relied on sources that portrayed the events in a biased and one-sided way," Csurka said. [passage omitted]
