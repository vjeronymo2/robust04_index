Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hangzhou, March 5 (XINHUA) -- A new container cargo route opened a few days ago from Tokyo to Ningbo, a coastal city in East China's Zhejiang Province. It is run by Ningbo Shipping Corporation, which initiated its first shipping route to another Japanese port last year. Ningbo is one of the 14 coastal cities in the country first opened to the outside world.
