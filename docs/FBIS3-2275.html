Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 8 (XINHUA) -- Ren Jianxin, president of the Chinese Supreme People's Court and member of the secretariat of the Communist Party of China Central Committee, met with Nguyen Huan, deputy chief justice of the Supreme People's Court of Vietnam, and his party here this morning. Nguyen, who is also president of the Central Military Court of Vietnam, and other guests arrived here yesterday. In addition to Beijing, they are expected to travel to Shanghai and Guangzhou in southern China.
