931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons Brazil venture Hiram Walker, the spirits and wine sector of Allied-Lyons, is joining forces with Pedro Domecq to form a joint venture sales and marketing company in Brazil. The new company will be based in Rio de Janeiro and the brand portfolio will include Domecq brandy, Ballantine's and Teacher's Scotch whiskies and a range of other premium brands.
