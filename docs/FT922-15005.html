920403 FT 03 APR 92 / International Company News: Heineken to sell Spanish breweries HEINEKEN, Europe's largest brewer, plans a further restructuring of El Aguila, its loss-making Spanish operation, and may take extraordinary charges in 1992 to cover this and other rationalisations, Reuters reports. The company said it would sell two El Aguila breweries in Zaragoza and Cordoba and focus production on Madrid and Valencia.
