Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Feoktistov] [Text] Ankara, 10 Feb -- Turkish President Suleyman Demirel and Azerbaijan President Geydar Aliyev, who is on an official visit here yesterday signed an agreement "On Friendship and All-Around Cooperation" between both countries. The agreement has 10 years to run and can be extended at two yearly intervals afterward. It provides for the "adoption of essential measures by each country to render assistance to the other country that was attacked, in keeping with UN treaties and other international obligations." Azerbaijan and Turkey signed 15 more documents concerning political consultations, stimulating investments, customs benefits, the repeal of dual taxation, interaction in the area of culture, health protection, science, and sport. According to the Azerbaijani president, the agreements with Turkey open up a new period in the relations of friendship and cooperation between both countries.
