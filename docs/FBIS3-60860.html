Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, December 25 (TURAN)--The Azerbaijani Government ordered the Ministry of National Security and Department of Civil Aviation of the Republic to render Russia assistance due to hijacking an airport plane MI-8 in Rostov-on-Don city. As TURAN agency was told, today the helicopter with four terrorists and hostages has left for Baku. Press-service of the Ministry of National Security reports, a special headquarters for cooperation with the headquarters of Russian Government has been created in Baku. Jafar Mamedov [name as received], the head of press-centre told a Turan's reporter that all measures are taken to ensure security of the hostages and to settle the matter by peaceful means. As the "Liberty" radio station reports, two of the terrorists are Armenians, one is of Chechen nationality and the last one is Korean.
