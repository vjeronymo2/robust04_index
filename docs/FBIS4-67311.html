BFN [Text] Cape Town May 17 SAPA -- Police at Beaufort West in the Karoo have confiscated dagga [marijuana] with a street value of R[rand]7,500,000, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Tuesday. The consignment was apparently destined for the Cape Town market. This was the biggest dagga haul this year and the second biggest in the Western Cape in the past two years. A traffic officer stopped an unlicensed lorry and found 7,000 kg of dagga packed into 301 bags. A 32-year-old Cape Town man was arrested and will appear in court soon.
