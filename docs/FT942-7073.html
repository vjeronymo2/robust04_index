940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Clinton aide quits over golf flight A senior White House aide resigned yesterday over the use of a government helicopter on a golf outing, George Graham reports from Washington. Mr David Watkins, head of the White House office of management and administration and an old Arkansas friend of President Bill Clinton, had been photographed by a local newspaper after flying to a Maryland golf course, with another White House aide, on a Marine helicopter. The local congressman, a Republican, wrote to President Bill Clinton demanding an explanation for the use of the government helicopter for what seemed a private golfing trip. Mr Clinton yesterday ordered an immediate investigation, which led to the resignation. 'The taxpayers will be fully reimbursed,' he said.
