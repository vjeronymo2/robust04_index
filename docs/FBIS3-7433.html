Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Italian aircraft maker Aliniar has written to the Commerce Ministry to say that it cannot accept goods in return for six G222 medium transport planes, a source in the ministry said. Aliniar said that though it is not able to take Thai agricultural products in lieu of payment this time because the contract has already been entered into it might be able to do so in the next arms purchase.
