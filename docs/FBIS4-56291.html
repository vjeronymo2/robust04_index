BFN [Text] Dominican President Joaquin Balaguer has reiterated his opposition to reinforcing the embargo against the military regime of Port-au-Prince. The Dominican president stated: The UN countries have demanded reinforcement of those sanctions; I am against those sanctions. Balaguer explained that his regime does not agree with reinforcing the embargo even though it is demanded by the United States. The Dominican Republic is part of the United Nations. Previously, [according to] the texts, or at least the analyses presented by the Dominican newspapers EL DIARIO and LISTIN DIARIO, for example, Dominican President Joaquin Balaguer stated that only humanitarian aid should pass over the border separating the two countries.
