930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Usborne continues its improvement Usborne, the agricultural services, property and motor products group, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 887,000 for the six months to December 31. That brought the figure for the full year to Pounds 1.72m against losses of Pounds 4.12m. However, the company has changed its year-end to June 30. Turnover for the 12 months was unchanged at Pounds 210m. Earnings per share emerged at 1.81p (losses 6.03p). A second interim dividend of 0.2p makes 0.4p to date - a 0.4p total was paid for 1991.
