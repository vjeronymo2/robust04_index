BFN [Unattributed report: "Jordanian Information Minister Tells AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT: No Obstacles to Meetings With Egypt, End of April Date Proposed for Committee Meeting"] [Excerpt] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Jordanian Information Minister Dr. Jawad al-'Anani yesterday denied the existence of any obstacles to the convening of the meetings of the joint Jordanian-Egyptian higher committee. He told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT that the meetings are expected to be held in the "last week of this month," as proposed previously. Nothing has happened to change this date. Al-'Anani added that it is hoped that the meetings will be held in the last week of this month under the two countries' prime ministers. The Jordanian minister denied that there are any problems preventing the convening of the meeting. He said that there is a joint Jordanian-Egyptian desire to hold the meetings to achieve the two countries' interests and work to develop their bilateral relations in all spheres. [passage omitted]
