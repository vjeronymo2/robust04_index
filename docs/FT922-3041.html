920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / Markets: New Britannia account - At a glance The Britannia building society has launched an index-linked savings account which will pay 4.5 per cent gross above the retail price index. The rate is not fixed but Britannia will give 90 days notice of rate changes. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000 and interest is paid quarterly. Penalty-free withdrawals require a 90-day notice period.
