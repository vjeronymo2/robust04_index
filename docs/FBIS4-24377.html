BFN [Text] Bucharest, May 23 (XINHUA) -- Leaders of the Romanian Parliament stressed here today that the further growth of friendly relations between Romania and China will contribute to the interests of both nations and to world stability. At a meeting with visiting members of the Chinese parliament led by Zhou Jue, member of the Chinese National People's Congress, Romanian Senate President Liviu Gherman said that the two nations have a long history of friendly ties and these relations should be further developed. He said that increased contact between the two parliaments will promote economic reforms in both countries. At a separate meeting with the Chinese delegation the same day, Adrian Nastase, president of the Romanian Chamber of Deputies, said that strengthened relations between the two parliaments is in accord with the political and economic interests of the two nations and the demands of the current international situation. The Chinese delegation arrived here on Sunday [22 May] for a four-day goodwill visit to the East European nation. During their stay here they will also hold talks with officials from the Ministries of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Tourism.
