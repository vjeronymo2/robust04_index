June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

JET HITS GLIDER; 1 DEAD

A U.S. Air Force jet fighter collided in the air Tuesday with a glider, and the pilot of the glider was killed, police said.

The F-16 fighter jet returned safely to base, the Air Force said.

The accident occurred near Unkenbach, about 60 miles southwest of Frankfurt, the Air Force said. No further information was available.
