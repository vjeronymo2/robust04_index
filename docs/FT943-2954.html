940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Sending images down the phone A video modem which will allow colleagues to view graphs and charts during business calls has been devised by Sharp, the Japanese corporation. Its TelePort video modem allows colour images to be sent and received during a standard telephone call. The modem, which is connected to Sharp's ViewCam and a telephone line, can be used to transmit live and pre-recorded video stills. The device incorporates a built-in memory capable of storing up to 10 images for transmission at a later date. Sharp Electronics (UK): UK, tel 061 205 2623; fax 061 205 7076
