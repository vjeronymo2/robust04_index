921027 FT 27 OCT 92 / Taiwan job losses Taiwan's state-owned Chinese Petroleum Corp (CPC) plans to cut its workforce by a quarter, or about 5,000 employees, to prepare for competition from the private sector, press reports said, Reuter reports from Taipei. CPC, which will lose its monopoly on oil imports and processing in the mid-1990s, is quoted as saying it hopes to avoid forced lay-offs.
