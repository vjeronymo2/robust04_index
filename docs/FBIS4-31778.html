BFN [Text] Tirana, June 11 (ATA) -- Dr. Fehmi Rexhepi, deputy of the parliament of Kosova, was arrested by Serbian police in a village of Gjilan (city in the centre of the republic of Kosova) on June 10, an ATA correspondent reports from Prishtina. Albanian deputy was sent to the police station of Gjilan. Mr. Rexhepi is also deputy chairman of the Executive Council of the Trade Unions of Education, Science, and Culture of the republic of Kosova.
