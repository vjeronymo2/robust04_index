Rural Electrification Administration Municipal Interest Rates for First Quarter of 1994 AGENCY: Rural Electrification Administration, USDA. ACTION: Notice of interest rates for the first quarter of 1994.

SUMMARY: REA hereby announces the interest rates for municipal rate loans with interest rate terms beginning during the first calendar quarter of 1994. DATES: These interest rates are effective for interest rate terms that commence during the period beginning January 1, 1994, and ending March 31, 1994. Telephone: 202&hyph;720&hyph;0736. FAX: 202&hyph;720&hyph;4120. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: On December 20, 1993, at 58 FR 66260, REA published a rule establishing a program for electric loans at municipal interest rates. Pursuant to 7 CFR 1714.5 of this rule, the interest rates on these municipal rate loans are based on indexes published in the ``Bond Buyer'' for the four weeks prior to the first Friday of the last month before the beginning of the quarter.
