May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

Color photographs of Latin America by Christine Dellosso will be on exhibit from 7 to 10 p.m. Monday and Tuesday at Casablanca restaurant, 220 Lincoln Blvd., Venice. Admission free. For further information call 392-5751.

-- -- --

Mort Loveman, travel photographer and lecturer, will offer a free presentation on Papua New Guinea at 1 p.m. on Wednesday at Roxbury Park Club House, Roxbury Memorial Park, Beverly Hills. For further information, call (213) 550-4761.

-- -- --

A free audio-visual program on Australia and New Zealand has been scheduled by My Travel Agent for 7:30 p.m. on Thursday at the Granada Hills Womens Club, 10666 White Oak Ave. For reservations, call (818) 368-7283.

-- -- --

Elderhosteling in Mexico will be the topic of a travel lecture by Lolita M. Lowell from 1 to 3 p.m., Thursday at Penmar Playground Auditorium, 1341 Lake St., Venice. Admission free. For further information, call (213) 306-8111 after 1 p.m.

-- -- --

An archeological tour of Pueblo cultures in Utah and Colorado will be discussed during a free orientation meeting at 7 p.m. on May 16 in Room 760, UCLA Extension Building, 10995 Le Conte Ave., Los Angeles. For reservations, call (213) 825-2272.

-- -- --

Photographer Dave Wyman will hold a seminar on travel photography at 7:30 p.m. on May 17 in Room 109, YWCA Building, University of Southern California. Admission $15. For further information, call (213) 743-7084.

-- -- --

A 14-day trip exploring the canals of central England by boat will be the topic of a free seminar at 7:30 p.m. on May 22 at Los Alamitos Plaza, 10900 Los Alamitos Blvd., Suite 115, Los Alamitos. For further information, call (213) 598-8890 or (714) 534-8722.

-- -- --

Explorer Shirley Metz will offer a lecture and slide presentation on her ski trip to the South Pole at 7:30 p.m. on May 25 at Charter Oak High School, 1430 E. Covina Blvd., Covina. Admission $3. For more information, call (818) 331-8603 or (714) 624-2611.

Air New Zealand has moved its American/European headquarters to 1960 E. Grand Ave., Suite 900, El Segundo 90245, (213) 648-7122.

Hours: 11 a.m. to 7 p.m. weekdays, 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. Saturday and 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Sunday. Admission $5 for adults, $2 for children 6 to 12, with children under 6 admitted free. For further information, call (714) 673-9360.
