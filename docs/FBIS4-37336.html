BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jun (MENA)--A parliamentary delegation led by Muhammad 'Abdallah, chairman of the People's Assembly Foreign Relations Committee, left Cairo for Moscow this evening on a five-day visit to Russia. The delegation will hold talks with a Russian parliamentary delegation to promote relations between the two countries, particularly in the parliamentary fields. The delegation is comprised of Dr. Sa'd al-Khawaliqah, chairman of the People's Assembly Transport and Communications Committee, and Ahmad Khayri, People's Assembly member and secretary general of the National Democratic Party in the Alexandria Governorate.
