BFN [Text] U Khin Maung Yin, minister of construction, and a five- member Myanmar [Burmese] delegation left Yangon [Rangoon] airport at 1320 for a goodwill visit to the People's Republic of China [PRC] at the invitation of the PRC railways minister. The delegation was seen off at Yangon airport by Brigadier General Maung Maung, minister of Livestock Breeding and Fisheries; U Win Sein, minister of rail transportation; Colonel Aung San, deputy minister of construction; Mr. Liang Feng, ambassador of PRC; responsible personnel from the construction ministry; relatives, and friends.
