Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The 101st session of Arab foreign ministers ends today in Cairo by ratifying various resolutions, following two days of meetings. Before the end, the Algerian delegate announced that the foreign conspiracy against the stability of Algeria was, in fact, directed against all Arab countries. Wahiba Ben Chehab has the details: [Ben Chehab] The Algerian delegate, Foreign Ministry Secretary General Mohamed Hannache, said that despite Algeria's difficult circumstances, it had the means to shoulder its domestic and foreign responsibilities. In that context, Hannache reiterated to the council session yesterday in Cairo Algeria's support for and solidarity with the Palestinian people and the other Arab peoples struggling to restore their territories. The secretary general of the Algerian Foreign Ministry pointed out that the stability of Algeria was targeted by external elements and that the conspiracy was not against Algeria only, but also against all Arab countries.
