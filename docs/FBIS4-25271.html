BFN [Text] Beijing, June 9 (XINHUA) -- China hopes that the concerned parties to the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula should keep calm and restraint and not close the door for negotiations easily. Foreign Ministry spokesman Shen Guofang made the remarks at a press conference held here this afternoon when asked to expound China's views on the latest developments on the issue. China is very much concerned over the latest developments over the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula, Shen said, and hopes that all the directly concerned parties should make further efforts to start talks so as to seek a peaceful resolution of the issue. He stated that the Chinese side advocates two basic targets in solving the issue, that is, to realize final denuclearization of the peninsula and to safeguard peace and stability there.
