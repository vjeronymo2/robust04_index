April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CENTURIES: Still two years out, the Orange County Christopher Columbus Quincentenary Commission plans a daylong seminar Wednesday at the Newport Beach Harbor Club to discuss plans for 1992, including a documentary on local Spanish settlers. . . . Further south, Mission San Juan Capistrano launches its Third Century Stabilization -- billed as the largest and most expensive reconstruction effort of its kind in the country (B1) -- in an effort to save the mission's 214-year-old adobe buildings. Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
