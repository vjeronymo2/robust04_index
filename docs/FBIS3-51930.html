Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Mediation Committee intends to present itself to the public this Wednesday [26 January] during a meeting with the press. The committee includes 11 personalities from various sectors and intends to rally all sectors to the discussion table with the objective of drawing a compromise for a quick solution to the crisis. The coordinator of the movement, Gerard Bailly, told us that the committee was already at work and has already made initial contacts with the Army, the parliament, and the international community. Bailly asserts that the National Committee For Mediation intends to accomplish its tasks objectively in order to prepare the field for a national conference. [passage omitted]
