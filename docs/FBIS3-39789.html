Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] An explosion occurred at the Democracy Party headquarters on Ankara's Necati Bey Street this evening. According to initial reports, one person was killed and 11 were injured in the incident. Two of the injured are reported to be in serious condition. The explosion, which occurred at 1815 on the fifth floor of the headquarters building, damaged the building and shattered the windows of other buildings in the area. Ankara Governor Erdogan Sahinoglu toured the area. He said one person was killed and 11 others were injured in the explosion. Pointing out that work is under way to identify the victims, Sahinoglu said it is believed that a bomb placed in the elevator shaft of the building caused the explosion. It was learned that the name of the dead person is Ekrem Akcakaya, who is not connected with the party, according to witnesses.
