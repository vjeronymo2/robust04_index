BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry gave its first press briefing on the start of Operation Turquoise and on what the French troops discovered when they arrived on the spot. Here is General Germanos being interviewed by Frederique Barrere: [Begin Germanos recording] The mission that we planned, and which consisted of going inside Rwanda without using force, has been conducted in good conditions. The mission allowed us to get in touch with both the people who are governing Rwanda today, and the Tutsi who are being detained in a certain number of camps. At the moment, no French base has been established on Rwandan territory. The aim is to reassure the population without staying in this country. We have to point out, however, that a certain number of [word indistinct] were reported to us and that common graves have been discovered. They are few for the moment. I stress this because we have not yet fully assessed the situation, but we can now say they exist. [end recording]
