921102 FT 02 NOV 92 / London exodus may be reversing THE EXODUS of businesses from London may have been thrown into reverse by the property recession, Personnel Management magazine reports today. Some companies, it says, have begun to move from outer London to more central addresses, and fewer companies are deciding to relocate out of London - 14 last year compared with 36 in 1990. Other organisations have put their relocation plans on hold the magazine says.
