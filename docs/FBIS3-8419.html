Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 3 (ATA) -- The Serbian authorities are presently calling up Albanian young men of the Istog commune born during the 1967-1977 period to serve in the Serbian Army. The young Albanians are ignoring the induction notices because they don't want to serve in the army "of a neighbouring totalitarian state" as they consider Serbia. The aim of these call-ups is to apply pressure on the Albanian young men to force them to abandon Kosova.
