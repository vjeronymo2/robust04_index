940422 FT 22 APR 94 / International Company News: McCain dispute unresolved An arbitrator has failed to resolve the three-year succession dispute between Harrison and Wallace McCain, the brothers who control McCain Foods, the multinational frozen-foods group based in the Canadian province of New Brunswick, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. Though the mediator declared invalid a resolution passed by the McCain board which sought to remove Mr Wallace McCain as joint chief executive, he made no finding on the brothers' disagreement over who should succeed them.
