930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / International Company News: Lufthansa buys 25% of Sky Chefs LUFTHANSA, the German national airline, yesterday agreed to acquire a 25 per cent stake in Sky Chefs, a Texas-based food service business, in a move claimed to create the world's largest airline catering alliance with about Dollars 1.3bn in revenues. LSG Lufthansa Service, the airline's food-service division, is to buy one quarter of Sky Chefs' existing stock for Dollars 75m. Onex, a diversified Canadian group, currently owns 80 per cent of company, with Sky Chefs' management holding the remainder. Onex said the move was part of a global marketing accord between LSG Lufthansa, the largest in-flight food operation in Europe and Asia, and Sky Chefs, the second-biggest airline caterer in the US. Sky Chefs has some Dollars 500m in turnover a year. The alliance will enable the companies to provide food services to airlines on a worldwide basis.
