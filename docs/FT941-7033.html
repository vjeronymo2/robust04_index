940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The markets for both black and white pepper have been steady and very active over the past few days, reports Man Producten. as demand has continued from dealers and grinders trying to cover their short positions. Black MG-1 was quoted at USDollars 1,700 a tonne, cif, for March/April shipment, with spot at Dollars 1,775. Grinding quality pepper, 500 grammes a litre, fetched Dollars 1,475 a tonne on shipment, and Dollars 1,575 on the spot. Black pepper faq was Dollars 1,525 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 1,450, cif for nearby shipments. White muntok faq for March shipment was Dollars 2,825 a tonne and on the spot Dollars 2,950.
