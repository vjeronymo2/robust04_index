940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / International Company News: Dunloe House cuts deficit to IPounds 419,000 Pre-tax losses at Dunloe House Group, the Dublin-based property investor and developer, were cut from IPounds 3.4m to IPounds 418,866 (Pounds 407,536) for 1993. Turnover of IPounds 942,758 compared with IPounds 786,661. Losses per share emerged at 2.35p (19.11p).
