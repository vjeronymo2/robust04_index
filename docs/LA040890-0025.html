April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENTS: A GUIDE TO THE BEST OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA;

FASHION;

WATCH ART

WITH PAINTED FACES depicting such classic works of art as Van Gogh's "Sunflowers" and Toulouse-Lautrec's "El Dorado," the watches of the Masterpiece Collection ($285 each) prove that even at today's hectic pace, there's always time for art.

The Masterpiece Collection watches, by Classic Moments, are available at Circle Gallery in Beverly Hills, Sherman Oaks, Anaheim, Costa Mesa and San Diego, and at Tangent in La Jolla.
