Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Nikolaos Vorvolakos today assumed the leadership of the National Guard stressing that he knows the responsibility he undertakes. Arriving this morning at the National Guard General Staff, Lt. Gen. Vorvolakos was honored by a military unit and the military band. He was welcomed by National Guard Deputy Chief Lieutenant General Ioannis Fotiadhis. In a statement, Vorvolakos said his mission was not difficult, but very responsible. Referring to the Greece-Cyprus unified defense doctrine, Vorvolakos said it is the privilege and responsibility of his political superiors. He said: I just execute the decisions.
