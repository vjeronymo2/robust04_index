BFN [Text] Aden, Yemen, June 27 (AFP) -- At least eight southern Yemeni soldiers were killed and 69 wounded on Monday [27 June] in a battle to repulse a northern advance into Aden's western suburbs, hospital officials said. Southern artillery gunners pounded northern forces in a bid to dislodge them from positions around the power station at Hiswah and Aden Radio station, about 10 kilometers (six miles) west of the city.
