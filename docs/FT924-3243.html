921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / Power station to shed 120 jobs A TOTAL of 120 jobs are to be shed at the Longannet coal-fired power station in Fife, ScottishPower said yesterday. The job losses are expected to be on a voluntary basis and will reduce the workforce by about 20 per cent. ScottishPower said the move was designed to secure a long-term future for the power station and for coal-burning power generation in Scotland.
