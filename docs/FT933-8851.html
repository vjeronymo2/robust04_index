930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / Traces of pesticide found in eels THE Department of Health yesterday issued a warning after pesticide traces were found in eels. It said it was 'prudent to limit consumption' of eels caught in the Humber, Yorkshire Ouse, Thames, the Itchen and Test in Hampshire, the Severn, and Trent. People in the Humber, Yorkshire Ouse and inshore Thames areas were advised to limit consumption to 1lb a month, while eels from the Itchen, Severn, Test and Trent could be consumed at 3lb a month. Friends of the Earth called it a 'serious contamination of the food chain'. Government officials said their advice was 'purely precautionary'.
