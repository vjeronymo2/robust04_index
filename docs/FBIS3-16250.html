Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A well-informed source in Russia's Federal Counterintelligence Service confirmed that the British agent detained in Moscow last January was Russian citizen Vadim Sintsov, who held a high post in the arms industry concern SMM. According to the Federal Counterintelligence Service, he passed over information about Russia's defense potential and test defense projects to British intelligence agencies. The source did not confirm, however, that Sintsov had worked with the British intelligence agencies since 1986. He said that he had been recruited much later, but refused to give the exact time or assess the damage inflicted on Russia.
