DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Forest Service Allegheny Wild and Scenic River Southern and Northern Advisory Councils; Meetings AGENCY: Forest Service, USDA. ACTION: Notice of meetings.

SUMMARY: The Northern Advisory Council for the Allegheny National Wild and Scenic River will meet at 7 pm, Tuesday, March 22, 1994 at the Warren Public Library, Warren, PA. The Southern Advisory Council will meet at 7 pm, Wednesday, March 23, 1994, at the Emlenton Civic Club, Emlenton, PA. Both Councils will discuss (1) work on a newsletter informing the public of their progress to date and (2) also resource opportunities along the river. Meetings are open to the public. A sign language interpreter will be provided if requested by March 14, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Donna McDonald, Allegheny National Forest, 222 Liberty Street, Warren, Pennsylvania 16365, 814/723&hyph;5150 or 814/726&hyph;2710 (TTY). Dated: February 23, 1994.
