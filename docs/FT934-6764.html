931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / International Company News: First Chicago to buy Lake Shore First Chicago, the US bank, is to acquire all outstanding shares of Lake Shore Bancorp for about Dollars 323m. Lake Shore, with Dollars 1.2bn in assets, is the banking holding company for Lake Shore National Bank, Chicago, and Bank of Hinsdale, Hinsdale, Illinois. First Chicago Corp has assets of Dollars 53.2bn. The transaction is expected to close in the second quarter of 1994.
