930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Amber Ind sells subsidiary Amber Industrial Holdings is selling its Causeway Steel Products subsidiary to Sterling Electronics, part of the Sterling Industries group. Causeway and its 85 per cent owned US subsidiary, Causeway Steel Products Inc, make retaining armours and anchorage systems for heat resistant linings, utilised mainly in the protection of refining plant in the petrochemical industry. Consideration is Pounds 1.14m cash, together with Pounds 224,701 for property occupied by Causeway.
