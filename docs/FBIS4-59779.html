BFN [Text] There were demonstrations last night in the wake of the terrorist attack in Hebron and in protest against the government's peace policy. In Jerusalem's Paris Square opposite the prime minister's residence, five demonstrators were arrested by police when they tried to break through barricades. On the Jerusalem-Jericho road, police dispersed demonstrators from the nearby settlement of Kfar Adumim, home of the Hebron attack victims. According to Qol Yisra'el's reporter on the scene, the demonstrators not only blocked the highway, but violently confronted Arab drivers.
