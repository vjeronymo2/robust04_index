February 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

CS LONG BEACH TO PLAY UTAH STATE IN BIG WEST GAME

The Cal State Long Beach basketball team will play Utah State in a battle for fourth place in the Big West Conference tonight at Logan, Utah.

Long Beach is 8-10 overall and Utah State is 7-12, but each is 5-4 in the conference.

The 49ers are led by center John Hatten, who, coming off a career-high 37 points against UC Irvine, has averaged 20 in conference games.

Reid Newey is Utah State's leading scorer, averaging 20.4 points. DICK WAGNER
