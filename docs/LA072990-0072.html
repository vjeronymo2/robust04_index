July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WHISKEY DEBATE

My liquor cabinet contains bottles of Ballantine's Scotch whisky, Haig Scotch whisky and Seagram's (Canadian) whisky; Jameson's (Irish) whiskey and Jim Beam and Jack Daniel's (both American bourbon) whiskey. Please see also the Random House "Dictionary of the English Language."

Perhaps you could be so courteous as to send copies of the Los Angeles Times Stylebook to the several distilleries.

GLENN ALLEN LANE

San Diego
