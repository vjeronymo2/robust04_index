941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Business Travel: Train accidents No passengers or staff were killed in UK train accidents in the 12 months to March 31 - for the first time since 1985. But the number of collisions between passenger trains doubled, according to the Health and Safety Executive. There were also increases in the number of collisions between trains in stations, accidents at level crossings, and vandalism. The number of 'significant train accidents' rose from 133 to 142.
