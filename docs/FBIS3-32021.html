Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The Senate first secretary has called the resigning prime minister to order. In a news release issued 26 January, Senator Ebrane Cadet reminds Robert Malval and the members of the resigning government that they must limit themselves to dealing with day-to-day matters. The legislator, a member of the Alliance for Parliamentary Cohesion, accuses the government of continuing to compromise national sovereignty. Sen. Cadet quotes the letter from Colin Granderson, seeking logistic measures in view of the return of about 30 UN/OAS observers to the country.
