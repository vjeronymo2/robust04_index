BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Jun (MENA)--Egypt has decided to extend material aid worth approximately $20 million to the Palestinian Authority, including food supplies. Part of the assistance will be used to organize training programs for the police. Announcing this today, Dr. Yusuf Butrus Ghali, minister of state in the cabinet for international cooperation affairs, said a number of Arab funds have pledged to extend economic aid to the Palestinian Authority in the coming stages.
