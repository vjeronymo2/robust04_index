930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Kalon Kalon, the paints group, is selling the business and assets of its industrial coatings activities to Croda International for an estimated Pounds 1.8m cash. The assets being sold are the goodwill and intellectual property rights of the business, production plant and machinery and stocks. Their book value is estimated at about Pounds 1.32m. Industrial coatings sales were Pounds 3.8m last year.
