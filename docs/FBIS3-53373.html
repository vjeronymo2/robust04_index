Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Qian Qichen, the Chinese deputy State Council chairman and foreign minister, arrived at Khartoum Airport at 1900. He was received at the airport by Major General al-Zubayr Muhammad Salih, vice president of the Republic, and by a number of ministers and ambassadors accredited to Khartoum, in addition to members of the Chinese Embassy in Khartoum. Mr. Qian Qichen made media statements following his arrival in which he expressed his pleasure at his first visit to Sudan. He said: I am pleased to seize this opportunity to express, on behalf of the Government of China and its people, the greetings and good wishes for the new year to the government and people of Sudan. He said that China and Sudan are bound by the links of a traditional friendship dating back to a remote time. He explained that the 35- year-old relations between Sudan and China had greatly developed. Mr. Qian Qichen said that during his visit he will hold talks with officials centering on relations between the two countries, in addition to international issues of common interest and conditions in the region, affirming that the visit will contribute towards strengthening friendship and understanding between the two peoples and consolidating cooperation between our two countries. Maj. Gen. al-Zubayr Muhammad Salih held a dinner party at the Friendship Hall in honor of the visit by the Chinese deputy chairman of the State Council.
