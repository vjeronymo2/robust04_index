940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / Blair expected to pick Today man Mr Tony Blair, the Labour leader, is expected to announce the appointment today of Mr Alastair Campbell, an assistant editor of Mr Rupert Murdoch's Today newspaper, as his personal press spokesman. Mr Campbell, former political editor of the Daily Mirror, is a friend of Mr Neil Kinnock, the former Labour leader, and a supporter of the party's modernising faction, led by Mr Blair.
