921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Provisions keep Five Oaks Pounds 3.9m in red Further provisions against falling property values kept Five Oaks Investments, the property investment and dealing company, in the red in the year to June 30, albeit at a much reduced level. After an exceptional debit of Pounds 3.54m, pre-tax losses amounted to Pounds 3.9m. In 1990-91 the company carried an exceptional debit of Pounds 9.83m and losses of Pounds 9.37m. Net rental income increased to Pounds 3.19m (Pounds 3.11m) but other income came to just Pounds 20,000 (Pounds 1.83m). As last year the dividend payment is omitted. Losses per share were reduced to 8.32p (20.18p).
