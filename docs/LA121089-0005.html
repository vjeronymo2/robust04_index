December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

The new shows that CBS, NBC and CBS have brought to us are terrible. The sitcoms are not funny and the dramas are boring. The only network that has come up with good new shows this season is Fox. So far it has given us "Booker," "Open House" and "Alien Nation," each of which is entertaining. When you add those to shows such as "America's Most Wanted," "Married . . . With Children," "Tracey Ullman" and "21 Jump Street," you get many hours of entertaining programming.

Tim Polik, Monarch Beach
