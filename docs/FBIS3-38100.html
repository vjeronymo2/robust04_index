Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe February 25 TASS -- The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of Tajikistan issued a decree on Friday on the regulation of television and radio broadcasting in Tajikistan. The two industries are now forbidden to use tv or radio time to propagandize or advertise erotic publications or alcoholic or tobacco products. They also may not broadcast programmes which conflict with the republic's national traditions or human moral norms. In addition, until the adoption of a law on television and radio broadcasting, the draft of which is as yet only supposed to be worked out and discussed by the Supreme Soviet, the all television and radio studios which are not under state control are to be closed down and their wave lengths taken away throughout the entire Republic of Tajikistan. It was emphasized that the decree was adopted in accordance with many requests received from the population of Tajikistan.
