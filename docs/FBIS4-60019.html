BFN [Text] The PLO' chief negotiator, Nabil Sha'th, arrived last night in the Gaza Strip for an unannounced visit. An aide said Sha'th was making a 24-hour inspection trip. He crossed the border from Egypt, and after a brief stop in Khan Yunus, went into conference with Palestinian officials at a Gaza sea front hotel. Sha'th said it was his first visit to Gaza Strip in 30 years. He is to hold a post on the council which is to administer Gaza and Jericho.
