May 11, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SWOON: It's only May, but you can forget about the Angels. . . . At least that's the view of columnist Mike Penner (C1), who ponders the Halos' awful start and says "it's over."

.. . Don't blame $16-million pitcher Mark Langston, says Penner, although his 2-3 record hasn't helped. More at fault: General Manager Mike Port, who "entered the season standing pat and with fingers crossed." . . . Things deteriorated further last night in Seattle, where the Angels lost 5-2. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Sonni Efron
