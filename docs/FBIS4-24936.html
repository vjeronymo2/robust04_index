BFN [Text] Stockholm, 1 Jun (XINHUA) -- Edward Klimosta [name as received], vice president of the Norwegian Parliament, met Deng Pufang, president of the Chinese Federation for the Disabled, in Oslo on the afternoon of 1 June. The latter is on a visit to Norway. Deng Pufang briefed Klimosta on China's development of services for the disabled. Both sides signaled a willingness to increase cooperation between their countries in expanding services for the disabled, and to make joint efforts to draw the international community's attention to issues regarding the disabled. At the invitation of the Norwegian Social Affairs Ministry, the Chinese Federation for the Disabled delegation, led by Deng Pufang, arrived in Oslo on 31 May for a five-day goodwill visit to Norway. The delegation had visited Sweden prior to its arrival.
