931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / Fulham Football Club to continue at Craven Cottage FULHAM Football Club has finally received confirmation that it will continue to play at Craven Cottage in London. Mr John Gummer, the environment secretary, yesterday dismissed, on appeal, an application to develop the site for residential use. Mr Gummer dismissed the appeal because it involved demolishing the Grade 2 listed Stevenage Road grandstand.
