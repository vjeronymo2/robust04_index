January 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

LIBERIA;

CIVILIANS URGED TO HELP FIND REBELS

President Samuel Doe called on civilians to help the army find rebels accused of killing more than 200 unarmed citizens in an attempt to overthrow the Liberian government. "We will carry out a massive search. Furnish us with information if you want to be on the safe side," Doe told thousands of cheering supporters at a rally in Monrovia to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the former sergeant's inauguration.
