921022 FT 22 OCT 92 / Multi-party front formed in Malawi A group of former civil servants and politicians have formed a front to campaign for multi-party democracy in Malawi, according to the official Malawi News Agency (Mana), Reuter reports from Blantyre. The United Democratic Front brings together several former top members of Malawi's sole legal party, the Malawi Congress party, who over the years have fallen foul of self-declared Life President Kamuzu Banda's unchallenged rule.
