BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 May (MENA) -- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa this evening received the credentials of Zuhdi al-Qidrah as ambassador of the state of Palestine to the Arab Republic of Egypt. Mr. al-Qidrah is a Gazan living in Egypt. He has served as Fatah representative in Cairo for the past 20 years, and also held the post of acting Palestinian ambassador to Egypt for the past five years. Al-Qidrah is succeeding Mr. Sa'id Kamal, who was appointed in March as assistant Arab League secretary general for Palestinian affairs.
