940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Ousted Hewson takes shadow post Mr John Hewson, ousted as leader of the opposition Liberal party in Australia this week, stays as industry spokesman in a 32-member shadow cabinet, Nikki Tait reports. Mr Alexander Downer, opposition leader since Monday, announced the new slimmed team yesterday. His deputy, Mr Peter Costello, becomes shadow treasurer. Mr Andrew Peacock and Mr John Howard shadow foreign and industrial relations ministers.
