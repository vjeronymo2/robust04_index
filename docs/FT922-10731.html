920429 FT 29 APR 92 / International Company News: Chevron opens with 45% fall CHEVRON, a leading US energy group, yesterday report-ed a 45 per cent slump in first-quarter net profit to Dollars 304m, writes Alan Friedman. Earnings per share declined to 88 cents from Dollars 1.59 in the first three months of 1992. Total revenues were down by 10.2 per cent, to Dollars 9.7bn. Mr Ken Derr, chairman of Chevron, said 'the first quarter saw no improvement in the depressed state of the US petroleum and chemical industries.'
