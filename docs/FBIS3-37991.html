Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol February 24 TASS -- Yevgeniy Saburov, Russian former deputy prime minister, has arrived in the Crimea to head the peninsula's government. Saburov has adopted an offer by Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov to take the position of the chairman of the Council of Ministers in this semi-autonomous republic within Ukraine. Reportedly, he already receives experts and government staffmen in the premier's office. "I can only promise that together with my future team I will spare no efforts working for the good of the Crimea", Saburov told reporters.
