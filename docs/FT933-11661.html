930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: EFM Java Trust net assets rise EFM Java Trust raised net asset value per share by 20 per cent to 39.3p at the end of June 1993, against 32.88p six months earlier. After-tax losses for the first half of 1993 came out at Pounds 16,000, compared with Pounds 27,000, and deficit per share emerged at 0.054p (0.0908p).
