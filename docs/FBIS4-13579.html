BFN [Text] Amman -- AFP -- Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has canceled Wednesday's [20 April] visit to Bucharest during which he was to attend the Crans Montana Forum which will also be attended by politicians and businessmen from Europe and the Middle East. The cancellation falls within the framework of Jordan's announcement on freezing its participation in the peace process activities until the issue of the siege imposed on the Port of al-'Aqabah is resolved.
