Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent reports from Madrid that Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, who is on an official visit to Spain, today held a number of meetings with Spanish high-ranking officials. He had a conversation with Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez, Commerce and Tourism Minister Gomez-Navarro, and Defense Minister Garcia Vargas. Anatoliy Zlenko informed them about the political and economic situation in Ukraine and spoke of the need to establish trade relations and military cooperation between the two states, and to this end lay the legal foundation required. This afternoon a meeting was held between the Ukrainian foreign affairs minister and Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana. They initialed an agreement on friendship and cooperation between Ukraine and Spain, and signed a memorandum on the freedom movement.
