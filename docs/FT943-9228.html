940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Letters to the Editor: Better effort Sir, Your correspondent, Lucy Kellaway, will be disappointed if I do not write to complain about her very fulsome article, 'Prime of life at Premier Oil' (People, August 8). Frankly, I was less than pleased by her quotation from a discharged employee. But, all in all, it was one of her better columns. However, what really was libellous was the caricature which bore no resemblance whatsoever to . . . yours faithfully, Roland C Shaw, 23 Lower Belgrave Street, London SW1 0NR
