Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [by ITAr-TASS correspondent Vladimir Fedoruk] [Text] Beijing, 5 Jan -- Li Peng, premier of the PRC State Council, today expressed satisfaction at the development of Chinese-Azerbaijani relations. During the course of a meeting with Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, who is here on an official visit, the head of the Chinese Government noted the "solid and healthy relations" between the two states since the establishment of diplomatic relations. The premier stressed that the PRC attaches great significance to the development of bilateral relations with Azerbaijan. China and Azerbaijan are Asian countries, and friendly contacts between their peoples go back 2,000 years, to the time of the "Great Silk Road," he said. Li Peng welcomed the planned visit to China of the Azerbaijani president, noting that an "exchange of visits at high level will undoubtedly foster the development of relations" between the two countries. According to XINHUA, G. Gasanov, stressed Azerbaijan's desire to establish close cooperation with China in various spheres. He also highly appraised the results of the economic reforms now being carried out in China.
