Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Congress-I Seva Dal [volunteers group] workers today held a demonstration near the Pakistan High Commission in the capital protesting against Islamabad's interference in the internal affairs of India. Raising slogans against Pakistan and the United States, they burnt the effigy of Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto. Later, in a memorandum to the Pakistan High Commission, the demonstrators said Pakistan should stop abetting terrorism across the border.
