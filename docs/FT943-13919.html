940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: Pounds 5m Sutton breaks football transfer record Norwich City striker Chris Sutton, 21, ended weeks of speculation when he finally signed for Blackburn Rovers for Pounds 5m - a British football transfer record. The previous highest fee was Pounds 4m, paid last year by Glasgow Rangers to Dundee United for Duncan Ferguson. Confessions of a temporary sports writer, Wkd I; World Cup, Wkd XII
