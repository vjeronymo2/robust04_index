BFN [Text] The University of Illorin has been closed indefinitely. This followed a peaceful demonstration by students of the institution, demanding for the improvement of basic amenities on the campus. A statement by the university authorities today directed the students to vacate their campuses by 4 o'clock in the afternoon [1500 GMT]. Our correspondent, who visited the campuses, reports that the students have started moving with their property out of their hostels, in compliance to the order, while policemen have been drafted to ensure the safety of life and property.
