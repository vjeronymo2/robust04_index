BFN [Vasiliy Ustyuzhanin report: "Where Is Our Share?"] [text] The State Duma yesterday supported an appeal from chief editors of papers and magazines published by the "Pressa" publishing house. What do journalists of KOMSOMOLSKAYA PRAVDA, SELSKAYA ZHIZN, OGONEK, KRESTYANKA, PRAVDA, ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA -- around 20 publications in all -- want? In short -- justice. The publishing house had been expanding for many years, including on the money of newspapers and magazines. Through collective efforts a unique publishing complex was created. But following the well-known August events the publishing house received state-owned status, while the newspapers got the status of juridical entities and...no shares in the property. As a result, every editorial collective was forced to conclude a lease contract separately with the publishing house on -- let us be blunt -- disadvantageous terms. State Duma deputies deemed the demands of the journalists' collectives to be well-founded, and proposed that the government adopt the necessary measures.
