BFN [Text] Johannesburg May 19 SAPA -- Four masked men were on Thursday morning chased from the Berario, Johannesburg home of African National Congress Secretary-General Cyril Ramaphosa, police reported. A security guard became suspicious when the four white men drove past the house in a red 16-valve Golf without registration plates at 11.40AM. He opened fire with a shotgun when two of the men jumped over the back garden wall. It is not known if either of the men was hit. Anyone with information that might assist the police should telephone a Lt Nel at 678-4445.
