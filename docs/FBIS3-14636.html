Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Fatah Movement has announced a general strike in the occupied territories in the aftermath of the martyrdom of eight Palestinians affiliated with the Fatah Hawks in the Jabalyah camp in the occupied Gaza Strip. The Fatah Movement threatened to step up clashes with the Israeli occupation authorities. Israel Radio expressed fears today that the Jabalyah incident could influence the course of the negotiations and the peace process, particularly since brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] has called for an urgent PLO meeting in Tunis to make a decision on this incident.
