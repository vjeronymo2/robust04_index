910427 FT 27 APR 91 / ICI still in recession ICI, THE largest UK man-ufacturing company, is 'still in the grip of recession', Sir Denys Henderson, chairman, said at the annual meeting in London yesterday, writes Clive Cookson. 'We do not yet see signs of the significant improve-ment in demand which is essential for a sustained recovery,' Sir Denys said. He also repeated a warning about job losses, which he first gave at the end of February when ICI an-nounced a corporate restructuring programme. 'There will be plant closures and divestments and withdrawals from some businesses,' he said.
