BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (XINHUA) -- The China Land and Real Estate Market was set up here Wednesday [18 May] with the approval of the State Bureau of Land Administration and the State Commission for Restructuring the Economy. Members of the market will include administrative institutions, commercial organizations and large commercial and development companies dealing in land and real estate at all levels. The market will engage in governmental selling of land-use rights, businessmen's transfer of land use rights and real estate transactions. Its business scope includes agency work, appraisal, advertising, market surveys, estate planning, information, legal consultation and international exchanges. At the same time, China's first fair featuring land and real estate opened here. Attending the fair were representatives from over 60 large real estate companies throughout the country and delegations from northwest China's Shaanxi Province, south China's Haikou city and southwest China's Chongqing city.
