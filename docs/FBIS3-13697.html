Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] United Nations, 17 Mar (INA) -- Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met with a number of ambassadors who are members of the UN Security Council today. He met at his place of residence with Yuliy Vorontsov, Russia's permanent UN Security Council member. He also met separately with Brazilian envoy Ronaldo Mota Sardenberg and New Zealand envoy [name indistinct]. Tariq 'Aziz is scheduled to meet later today with other UN Security Council envoys before he meets with UN Special Commission Chairman Rolf Ekeus at his place of residence today, Thursday.
