Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Jan 25, BNS -- Romualdas Ramoska, Lithuanian ambassador to Ukraine, presented on Monday [24 January] a letter from Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas to Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk. Dainius Trinkunas, the Lithuanian president's foreign policy assistant, said the letter includes proposals on ways of improving economic relations, mutual settlements and exchange of Lithuanian and Ukrainian currencies. To attain these goals, Brazauskas proposed to create a joint Lithuanian-Ukrainian commercial bank, the presidential assistant said. The Lithuanian president expressed hope that Kravchuk's forthcoming visit to Lithuania will stimulate further development of bilateral relations. The Ukrainian president is scheduled to come to Lithuania Feb. 8-9.
