BFN [Text] Baku, June 22 (TURAN) -- Khoshbakht Jusifzade, vice- president of State Oil Company of Azerbaijan Republic (SOCAR) told TURAN, that vessel "Zokhrab Veliyev" will depart at the beginning of July to Iranian for conducting exploration works on Caspian offshore according to bilateral contract, signed on May 7, 1994. According to contract Azeri specialists will conduct exploration, geophysical and drilling works on Iranian offshore. The second stage of works will be conducted on the vessel "Rezonance". The contract costs three million dollars. According to Jusifzade, approximately 100 oilmen will be sent to Iran for implementation of the contract.
