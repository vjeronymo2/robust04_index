Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Spanish Government, through the Diplomatic Information Office, has released a communique in which it expresses its profound consternation at the tragic massacre of Palestinians in the West Bank town of Hebron. The communique adds that the Spanish Government still believes that the peace process and the prompt application of the declaration of principles represent the only possible alternative in the face of the continuation of violence and clashes in the occupied territories. The text also asks for a fresh effort from all the parties to restore a climate of harmony and calm, which will make it possible to achieve peace as soon as possible.
