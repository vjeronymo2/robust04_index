May 10, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: 2 FROM DON BOSCO GET SCHOLARSHIP AWARDS

Two seniors at Don Bosco Technical Institute in Rosemead have been selected for awards in the National Hispanic Scholar Awards Program.

Selected from 3,200 semifinalists, Edward Garcia of Whittier won a $1,500 college scholarship and Frank Castro of Temple City was recognized with a $100 honorable mention award. Both are studying electronics and computer technology.
