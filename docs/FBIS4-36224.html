BFN [Text] Tunis, 7 Jun (MENA) -- Informed Palestinian sources have stated that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat intends to return to Jericho and Gaza in July leading the Palestinian leadership and PLO institutions. The sources stressed that a final and definitive date for 'Arafat's return has not been set despite much talk about unrealistic dates. They say the return is likely to be in July so that it will precede the dates of municipal and legislative elections and they note that the elections, due for October, are inconceivable in the absence of 'Arafat and PLO institutions and agencies connected with the election campaign. The sources said 'Arafat will return to Gaza and Jericho accompanied by 400 high-ranking leaders and agencies associated with the daily routine who have not been separated from 'Arafat for a quarter of a century.
