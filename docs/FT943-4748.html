940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Robinson Brothers Robinson Brothers (Ryders Green), the unquoted West Bromwich-based chemicals group, reported pre-tax profits down by 24 per cent to Pounds 1.45m in the first half of 1994, compared with Pounds 1.9m. Turnover was static at Pounds 14m. The company said that trading continued to be adequate. Earnings per Pounds 1 share were 59p (74p).
