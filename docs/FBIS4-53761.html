BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 20 May (AKP) -- Two personages of the former State of Cambodia regime have been nominated by decrees signed by His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk, king of Cambodia. Mr. Sek Settha, deputy foreign minister of the old regime, has been nominated ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Cambodia to the Russian Federation. Mr. Nhim Vanda has been promoted to four-star general of the Khmer Royal Army [KRA]. He is authorized by the two prime ministers to be in charge of the special commission to provide supplies to military forces on the front. Under the State of Cambodia, Mr. Nhim Vanda was trade minister and deputy defense minister in charge of the Army's General Logistics Department.
