931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Industrial park for Bangalore A Singapore-based consortium led by state-controlled enterprises is joining together with India's Tata group to develop a SDollars 250m (Pounds 106.4m) industrial park in the southern Indian city of Bangalore, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. The Singapore consortium, led by state-controlled Singapore Technologies Industrial Corporation and Technology Parks ,will have a 40 per cent in the project. Tata will have a 40 per cent stake while the Karnataka state government will have the remaining 20 per cent.
