941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Walker jury The jury in the trial of Mr George Walker, former chairman and chief executive of Brent Walker group, and Mr Wilfred Aquilina, its former finance director, is expected to recommence deliberations today after failing to reach a verdict and being given Saturday afternoon off. The two defendants deny fraudulently boosting Brent Walker's profits between 1984 and 1989.
