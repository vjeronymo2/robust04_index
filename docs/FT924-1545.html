921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Bexbuild shows decline to Pounds 66,000 Profits of Bexbuild Developments, the USM-quoted commercial property investor and trader, fell from Pounds 138,000 to Pounds 66,000 pre-tax over the six months ended September 30. The figure included a Pounds 19,000 surplus on the sale of an investment property. Turnover amounted to Pounds 908,000 (Pounds 944,000). The interim dividend is held at 1p and is paid from earnings of 1.1p (2.1p).
