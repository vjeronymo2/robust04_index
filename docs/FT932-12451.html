930428 FT 28 APR 93 / Irish Company News: Tullow's production levels rising Tullow Oil, an Irish oil and gas exploration and development company, reported a pre-tax profit of IPounds 1.63m (Pounds 1.59m) for the year to December 31, against a loss of IPounds 1.24m previously. Turnover grew from IPounds 928,000 to IPounds 1.3m. Earnings worked through at 1.12p (losses of 0.89p).
