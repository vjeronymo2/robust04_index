October 21, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

NORTHRIDGE SOCCER STREAK ENDS AT 4

Cal State Sacramento's soccer team ended visiting Cal State Northridge's four-match winning streak Saturday with a 2-1 victory.

"We just lost a game we shouldn't have lost," CSUN Coach Marwan Ass'ad said.

"The first 20 minutes we were all over them. We beat them down the wings and crossed it over but we could not score. Their goalie (Dave Lamberg) played a great game."

Sacramento (5-10) scored 30 minutes into the match and again three minutes later. The only goal for CSUN (7-6-3) came with 10 minutes left when a shot by Bobby Reyes deflected off a defender and into the net.

In other men's soccer matches:

Occidental 8, Caltech 2 -- Dirk Hogan, Carl Burmeister, Jason Nagler and Scott Sheldon each scored two goals for Occidental in a Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference match at Occidental (5-10-1, 5-2).

Cal Lutheran 4, Whittier 0 -- Dave Rinehart scored twice as Cal Lutheran won a nonconference match at home.

WOMEN'S SOCCER

Cal Lutheran 5, Whittier 0 -- Rachel Wackerman and Vanessa Martin each scored twice to lead Cal Lutheran in a nonconference match at Whittier.

WOMEN'S CROSS-COUNTRY

Cal Lutheran took the top six places and won with 15 points in a four-team Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference meet at Cal Lutheran. Heidi Peterson won the race in 18 minutes 44 seconds.

MEN'S CROSS-COUNTRY

Cal Lutheran and Pomona-Pitzer tied for first in a four-team Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference meet with 33 points, but Cal Lutheran won because of a faster sixth man.

BOYS' WATER POLO

Royal 13, Costa Mesa 9 -- Sophomore Jack Kocur scored a match-high six goals and goalie Paul Krueger had 15 saves for Royal.
