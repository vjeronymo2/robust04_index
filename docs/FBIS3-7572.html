Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] At a news conference on the morning of 29 March, Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa was asked about a news report that an agreement has been reached between Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party] Chief Secretary Ichiro Ozawa, Komeito Secretary General Yuichi Ichikawa, and Hosokawa that they will work to form a new party for which "Hosokawa would serve as representative and Ozawa as secretary general." In reply, Hosokawa denied the report, saying: "It was a complete surprise to me. I am greatly annoyed by that report." When asked about the current situation in which many reports have been circulated on the issue of forming a new party, Hosokawa said, "I cannot answer the question because I am devoted entirely to official business."
