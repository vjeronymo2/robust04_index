940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / British Gas eyes Brazil project British Gas is negotiating to play a leading part in a Pounds 2.8bn project to build a 1,200-mile gas pipeline from Bolivia to Brazil, Bethan Hutton writes. Work on the pipeline is expected to start next year. British Gas has formed a strategic alliance with Tenneco Gas, a US company, for Latin American projects. Last November the two companies won a contract to become technical operators for a Dollars 1.65bn project to build a 750-mile natural-gas pipeline between Argentina and Chile.
