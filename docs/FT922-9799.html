920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / UK Company News: BDA ends year Pounds 18,000 in black With all companies making a positive contribution, BDA Holdings, the property group, stayed in the black through the year to January 31 1992. After Pounds 7,000 in the opening six months, it finished with Pounds 18,000, compared to the loss of Pounds 2.17m previously. Earnings were 0.1p (losses 14.6p).
