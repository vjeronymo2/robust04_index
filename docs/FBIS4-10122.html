BFN [Decree No. 2 issued by the Supreme Electoral Tribunal, TSE, at the TSE headquarters in San Salvador; read by unidentified TSE secretary -- recorded] [Text] Decree No. 2 The Supreme Electoral Tribunal, Whereas, 1. In accordance with the Electoral Code, the elections for president and vice president of the Republic were held throughout the country on 20 March 1994. 2. Since the term specified in Article 324 of the Electoral Code has expired and no writ has been filed to challenge the final results, it is appropriate to declare the results of the aforementioned elections final. Therefore: According to what is stated and according to Article No. 80, No. 6, and Articles No. 261 and 265 of the Electoral Code, Decrees: Article 1: Declare final the results of the elections for president and vice president of the Republic on 20 March 1994, as stated in Final Tally Sheet No. 1 issued at 1400 on 30 March 1994. Article 2: Publish this decree in the official gazette and the newspapers with the widest circulation. Article 3: This decree will be effective on the day of its publication in the official gazette. Issued at the TSE in San Salvador, 6 April 1994.
