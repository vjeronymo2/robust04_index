930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / India to introduce legislation to regulate cable television operators INDIA is to introduce legislation to regulate cable television operators who reach an estimated 5m viewers by distributing overseas satellite broadcasts, Reuter reports from New Delhi. The government said yesterday the cabinet has approved a bill, to be introduced in the next session of parliament, to register cable operators, prohibit them from violating programme and advertisement codes and make it compulsory to distribute at least one channel of state-run television. The operators distribute satellite broadcasts such as Hong Kong-based Star TV and US-based Cable News Network and often an extra channel of Hindi language
