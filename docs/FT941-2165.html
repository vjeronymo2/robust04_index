940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / International Company News: Credito Italiano turns in 36% profits increase Credito Italiano, the newly privatised Italian bank which is the country's seventh largest in terms of assets, yesterday reported a 36 per cent increase in group net profits to L274.9bn (Dollars 164m) in 1993. Interest income was up 16.8 per cent at L2,716.2bn while non-interest income rose 75 per cent to L1,597bn. The results, which follow a steep fall in net profits in 1992, reflect an improved performance by subsidiaries. Gross operating profits were up 87 per cent at L1,770.7bn.
