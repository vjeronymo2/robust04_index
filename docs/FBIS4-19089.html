BFN [Text] Istanbul, April 12 (AA) -- Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres departed Turkey today. Peres was on an official visit to Turkey which began Sunday [10 April], as the guest of his Turkish counterpart Hikmet Cetin. Turkey and Israel signed an agreement on environmental cooperation and protection on Monday. During his visit, Peres also said that his country was willing to buy water from the Manavgat River which flows into the Mediterranean Sea.
