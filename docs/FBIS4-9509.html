BFN [Article by "(R)": "And the Purge Continues..."] [Text] Immediately after he was introduced to his post by Deputy Prime Minister Roman Kovac, newly appointed TA SR Director General Ivan Melichercik recalled TA SR Chief Editor Dusan Kerny and Economic Deputy Rudolf Wurm. Kerny, the first chief editor of an independent Slovak news agency, was recalled 40 minutes after Deputy Prime Minister Roman Kovac, who praised TA SR's reportage and said that it was "done well, to a high professional standard," had left the agency's offices. Kerny managed the editorial office during the transition period following independence from the CTK service and organized the first direct subscription to foreign news agencies, including REUTER, AFP, DPA, and others. He also organized the first Slovak service in English for foreign consumers and established the first international editorial office in Slovakia since 1948.
