910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Barlows slips to Pounds 415,000 From slightly reduced turnover, Barlows, the property investor, saw pre-tax profits fall from Pounds 676,000 to Pounds 415,000 in 1990. Net attributable profit dropped from Pounds 1.17m to Pounds 763,000. Realised capital surplus was up to Pounds 517,000 (Pounds 346,000) but there were no extraordinary credits this time (Pounds 448,000). Earnings per share worked through at 1.23p (1.86p). The dividend is again 2.475p with a final of 1.65p.
