930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Paterson Zochonis Pounds 2.6m purchase Paterson Zochonis, the soaps, toiletries and pharmaceuticals group, has paid Pounds 2.6m for an 80 per cent interest in Pollena, a Polish manufacturer of household detergents and soap. Based at Wroclaw, Pollena had 1992 sales of Pounds 12m and was profitable. The acquisition was carried out via Paterson's Cussons subsidiary. Cussons will spend a further Pounds 4.75m over the next three years on modernising and expanding Pollena's manufacturing facilities.
