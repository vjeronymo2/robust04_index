941108 FT 08 NOV 94 / World Trade News: TransAlta Energy - Contracts and ventures TransAlta Energy, the Alberta electrical power utility, will build a Dollars 120m 250MW coal-fired power plant in Fujian Province, south-east China for Fujian Electric Power Bureau. Construction is due to start next year and the first two generating units will be operating by late 1997. TransAlta will share ownership with the bureau and Great China International.
