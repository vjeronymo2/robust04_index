BFN [Text] Ankara, April 11 (A.A.) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese left for Syria on Monday [11 April] for an official visit as the guest of his Syrian counterpart Muhammad Harbah. He told reporters before his departure at Ankara's Esenboga Airport that during his stay bilateral security issues will be discussed as well as regional and international developments. Mentese said in order to develop present friendly relations and cooperation ties both sides will try to solve any problems that are included within the boundaries of previously signed agreements or that remain outside its scope. He also expressed belief that relations will go further with this visit, adding that "everything" will be discussed during his stay. Mentese and accompanying delegation will return to Turkey on April 13.
