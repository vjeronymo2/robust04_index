940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Nestor Medical in management buy-out Nestor Medical Services, the hospital and nursing home business of Nestor-BNA, has been sold to its management in a Pounds 14m deal structured by 3i, the investment capital group. 3i and CINVen have co-invested about Pounds 7m of equity with senior debt provided by Midland Bank. The company operates four hospitals and two nursing homes.
