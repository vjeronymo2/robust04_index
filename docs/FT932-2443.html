930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Grainger Trust Grainger Trust, the property investor and trader, has reported an improvement in the first half to March 31 with increased pre-tax profits to Pounds 0.94m. This compared with Pounds 0.63m in the previous first half and a Pounds 4.63m deficit in the year to September 30, which carried a exceptional costs of Pounds 6.7m. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.2p on earnings per share of 3.2p (2p). The results and comparatives have been prepared under FRS 3.
