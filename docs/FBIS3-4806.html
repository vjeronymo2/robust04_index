Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 1 KYODO -- Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party] leader Tsutomu Hata said Tuesday that Shinseito will support Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's scheme to reorganize the seven-month-old cabinet. Hata, who serves as foreign minister and deputy prime minister, said he notified Hosokawa of his backing in a meeting at the premier's official residence. "Behind the premier's plan is his desire to run the cabinet more smoothly," Hata told reporters. "We support that kind of notion."
