December 10, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SYLMAR MAN STABBED TO DEATH DURING DISPUTE

A Sylmar man was stabbed to death Saturday during an argument with another man, Los Angeles police said.

The man's body was found by police at 4:30 a.m. in a house in the 13400 block of Dronfield Avenue, authorities said. The victim, who had been stabbed several times, was not identified pending notification of next of kin.

Another man at the location, John Nichols of Sylmar, was arrested on suspicion of murder, officials said. The stabbing occurred during a dispute between the man and Nichols, but police said they did not know the subject of the argument.
