Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 31 (TURAN) -- The Adviser of Azerbaijani President for Foreign Policy, Vafa Gulizade told a TURAN's reporter that the government of the republic knows nothing about Russia's intention to bring its troops in Nagorno-Karabakh. On Saturday [29 January] Russia's INTERFAX news agency reported that Russia prepares to bring its troops to the zone of Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict. "We know nothing about that. I don't know the source of this statement of INTERFAX", stressed State Adviser for Foreign Matters, Vafa Gulizade.
