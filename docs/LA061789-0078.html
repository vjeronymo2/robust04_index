June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HALF OF ALL COUPLES INTO BIRTH CONTROL, REPORT SAYS

About half of all couples where the wife is of reproductive age use some form of contraception, and in developed countries the proportion is about 70%, the United Nations said Friday.

A detailed report by the U.N. Population Division said there was a "rapid increase in contraception use in developing countries," where the average percentage use was 45%, but as high as 74% in China.
