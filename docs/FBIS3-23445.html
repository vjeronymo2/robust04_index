Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon, police will bring to Nicosia District Court four foreign students who were arrested late last night in the yard of Omeriye Mosque in Nicosia. [see item NC1603200294 in previous Terrorism Report] The four are Salah-al-Din Billu from Syria, (Usuani Uushtag) from Pakistan, (Larren Fernandes) from India and Ghassan Muhammad from Lebanon. The four students had entered the yard of Omeriye Mosque in a stolen car, which belongs to a resident of Aradhippou village, with fake license plates from a motorcycle. In their possession police found tools used for burglaries and other items, which police are checking on to see if they are stolen.
