February 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MOTORCYCLE COMMUTERS

Why does this debate about a mandatory helmet law exist? Answer: the insurance industry (who else?). If the insurance people would get their act together and triple the medical premiums for those motorcyclists who choose not to wear a helmet (to reflect the much higher costs of an accident), I'm sure the cyclists would decide having the wind in your hair isn't that important after all.

DAVE DeHERAS

Long Beach
