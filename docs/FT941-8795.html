940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / French media group fined Carat Finance, the French media buying group, has been fined Ffr35m (Pounds 4m) following an inquiry by the Conseil de la Concurrence (the French anti trust body) into the operations of the advertising and media market during the 1980s. It is one of 24 companies to be fined. The Conseil said a number of companies had engaged in practices which were anti-competitive.
