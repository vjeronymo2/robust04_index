BFN [Text] Bratislava April 22 (CTK) -- Former Premier Vladimir Meciar is still the most popular politician in Slovakia with 26 percent of favour among would-be voters, a poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Office in early March revealed. He is followed by President Michal Kovac and current Premier Jozef Moravcik (25 percent each). Moravcik's rating improved 20 percent over the last two weeks, the poll showed. Then follow: Peter Weiss, Chairman of the Democratic Left (SDL) with 13 percent, Parliament Chairman Ivan Gasparovic with 10 percent and Deputy Premier Roman Kovac with seven percent. No politician is trusted by 25 percent of those polled, which is a marked decline in comparison with February when the figure was 41 percent.
