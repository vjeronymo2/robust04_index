December 14, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CHRISTIAN CABLE: Trinity Broadcasting Network, the Tustin-based all-Christian programming service, has a booth not far from the Family Channel, which was started as the Christian Broadcasting Network by Pat Robertson. . . . Several years ago, Robertson and TBN founder Paul Crouch made a straight swap, with TBN carrying Robertson's "700 Club" and CBN carrying Crouch's "Praise the Lord" talk show. . . . The "700 Club" still runs at noon five days a week on Trinity, while Crouch airs on the Family Channel Saturdays and Sundays at 3:30 a.m.
