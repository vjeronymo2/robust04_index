BFN [Text] A correspondent working in the north of Haiti announced on 18 April the release of Le Borgne Mayor Belizaire Fils-Aime, who was arrested in connection with the armed attack against the police station there by armed individuals. The situation is very tense in Le Borgne and the arrival of military reinforcements coming from Cap-Haitien, Fort-Liberte, and even Gonaives was noted. No one can provide explanations of what actually happened because of the fear of the people living in Le Borgne. This area is listed among the main areas of tenseness since the events of 30 September 1991.
