BFN [Text] The High Privy Council held a working meeting at the Royal Palace on the morning of 29 March in the lofty presence of Privy Councillors Samdech Nhoek Chulong, Samdech Heng Samrin, Samdech Son Sann, Samdech Chau Senkosal, His Excellency [H.E.] Say Phuthang, H.E. Chea Soth, and H.E. Mat Ly. H.E. Kong Sam-ol, deputy prime minister of the Royal Palace, was also present. The meeting focussed on the presence of the king of Cambodia, who will return and stay in the Royal Palace in early April. The king will receive the people in audiences to solve their key problems, specifically problems dealing with the farmers' livelihood. The king is the last resort to solving, via the audiences, any problem that the government and National Assembly are not able to smooth out.
