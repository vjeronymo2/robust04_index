920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Holmes Protection makes Dollars 1.47m Holmes Protection, the London-quoted US security company, yesterday reported a return to profits in the first half of the year following its recent refinancing. Pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30 were Dollars 1.47m (Pounds 730,000) on sales of Dollars 28.3m. The company said the figures were not comparable with the same period last year because of changes of accounting policies and adjustments to the previous accounts that followed a change of management. Earnings per share after the capital restructuring were 3.3 cents. As expected, there is no interim dividend.
