June 14, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

NO WINNER; LOTTO AT $20 MILLION

No one won the big jackpot in the latest California Lottery Lotto 6/49 game, bringing Saturday's top prize pool to an estimated $20 million.

Four ticket buyers had five of the six numbers plus the bonus number to share a $1.37-million prize pool for individual payoffs of $343,677.

There were 181 tickets with five of six numbers. They are good for $3,913 each from a prize pool of $708,000.
