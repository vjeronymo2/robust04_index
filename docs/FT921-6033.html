920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Finance & The Family (At A Glance): Smaller companies buoyant Small company shares had a buoyant week. The Hoare Govett Small Companies index (capital gains version) rose 0.7 per cent to 1207.80 over the seven days to February 27; the county Small Companies index rose 0.6 per cent to 957.96 over the same period.
