940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Cadbury in Spain Cadbury Schweppes has acquired Industrias Dulciora a large Spanish sugar confectionary company which employs 400 people. Dulciora recorded sales of Pounds 21.3m and tangible assets of Pounds 8.7m in 1993. Cadbury said the purchase, financed from its own resources, combined with its existing Spanish confectionary business, Chocolates Hueso, would bring its market share in the country to more than 9 per cent by volume.
