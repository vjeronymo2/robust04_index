930315 FT 15 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Opencast mining scheme British Coal has awarded WIMPEY MINING a contract worth nearly Pounds 20m for opencast mining and land reclamation near Durham. Working on the site of a former deep mine at Rye Hill, Wimpey will be recovering 1.2m tonnes of coal over a three year period, some of which will be recovered by washing 3m tonnes of waste material from a disused colliery tip on the site. The coal will be delivered to British Coal's Wardley disposal point.
