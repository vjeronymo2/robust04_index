921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Outspan oranges are the best fruit buy at 12-25p each (12-25p) this week reports the FFVIB. Cox apples at 45-55p a lb (40-50p) and Kiwifruit at 16-20p each (16-20p) are also good buys. There is plenty of English cauliflower in the shops at 35-50p each (35-50p), along with an abundant supply of potatoes at 8-12p a lb (8-12p). There are lots of colourful Autumnal salad vegetables in the shops, choose from beetroot at 15-25p a lb (30-35p), Lollo Rosso and Radicchio lettuce at 60-80p each (60-80p). More traditional are tomatoes at 55-65p a lb (45-55p) and celery at 40-45p a head (40-45p).
