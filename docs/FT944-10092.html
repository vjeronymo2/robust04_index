941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / Five charged over Pounds 5bn forgeries Five businessmen were last night charged with possessing false bills of exchange and foreign government guarantees worth more than Pounds 5bn. They will appear before Bow Street magistrates in London today charged with possessing forged financial documents, many from former Soviet republics, with a total face value of Dollars 7.725bn. Mr Leslie McKay, of Sanquhar, Dumfries and Galloway, was arrested along with the four other businessmen - two from the US, one from Switzerland and one from the Phillipines - at a hotel in Kensington on Wednesday.
