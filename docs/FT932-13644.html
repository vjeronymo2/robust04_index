930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: FKI in Pounds 5.4m disposal FKI has disposed of FKI Communications as part of the programme to concentrate on the development of strong market positions in its core businesses. The sale is via a management buy-out backed by ECI Ventures. Consideration was Pounds 5.4m, made up of Pounds 1.4m in preference shares with the balance in cash.
