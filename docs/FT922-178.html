920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Equipment for Glaxo research centre OTIS, the lifts and escalators manufacturer, has been awarded a contract to supply 30 lifts valued in excess of Pounds 2.5m for the new Glaxo research centre in Stevenage, Hertfordshire. The architects are Ove Arup and Sheppard Robson and the main contractor handling the project is Laing. The passenger lifts will be equipped with microprocessor control and the drive equipment will be a variable voltage variable frequency system.
