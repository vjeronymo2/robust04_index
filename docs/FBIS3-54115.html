Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 Jan (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa left Cairo for Israel today for a brief visit that will last several hours during which he will deliver a message from President Husni Mubarak to Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin. Musa will discuss with Rabin and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres ways of boosting the peace process and overcoming the problems hindering the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles.
