February 13, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Closings: Today marks the observance of Lincoln's Birthday. Feb. 20 is observed as Presidents' Day, marking the birthdays of Presidents Washington and Lincoln. Following is a list of local closings.

GOVERNMENT: State and county offices will be closed today and Feb. 20. Federal offices will be closed Feb. 20. City offices in Westminster will be open both days; municipal offices in Brea, Costa Mesa, Irvine and San Juan Capistrano will be closed both days. All other city offices will be closed only Feb. 20.

MAIL: Mail will be delivered today but not Feb. 20.

TRASH: Trash will not be picked up today or Feb. 20 in Brea, Anaheim, Garden Grove, Placentia and Yorba Linda. Refuse will be picked up today but not Feb. 20 in Buena Park, Cypress and Seal Beach. In all other cities, refuse pickup schedules remain unchanged.

COURTS: County and state courts will be closed today and Feb. 20. Federal courts will be closed only Feb. 20.

BANKS: Banks will be open today, but most will close Feb. 20.

SCHOOLS: In the Capistrano and Los Alamitos unified school districts, schools will be closed Friday and Feb. 20. In the Saddleback Valley Unified School District, schools will be closed Feb. 20-22. All other public schools will be closed today and Feb. 20.
