Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Nikolay Kalintsev and Yuriy Kozlov] [Text] Moscow March 23 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltisn sent a personal message to German Chancellor Helmut Kohl. In the message, the Russian president set out his views on the solution of the problem of mutual return of cultural values transferred in the years in the Second World War and as a result of its consequences. The message was handed over on Wednesday by Russian Minister of Culture Yevgeniy Sidorov to German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel. The Russian and the German ministers head a bilateral commission on mutual restoration of cultural values. The commission began its work in Moscow's President-Hotel on Wednesday morning.
