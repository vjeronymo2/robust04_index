930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: Franc steadies against D-Mark The French franc steadied against the D-Mark, ending a succession of sharp declines, following a show of support from French and German politicians and central bank officials. It closed in London at FFr3.414, almost unchanged from its opening rate. In New York, it closed at FFr3.4148. Embattled franc prepared to fight off attackers, Page 2; Currencies, Page 35
