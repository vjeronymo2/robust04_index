November 30, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

8 NBA PLAYERS FINED FOR FIGHTING

The NBA fined eight players from the Atlanta Hawks and Detroit Pistons a total of $6,750 today for their roles in an altercation during a game last week.

Bill Laimbeer of the Pistons was fined $2,000 for initiating the incident by bumping Dominique Wilkins to the floor with his hip and pushing him twice in last Tuesday night's game.

Wilkins was fined $1,000 for charging Laimbeer. Kevin Willis of the Hawks was fined $1,000, Vinnie Johnson of the Pistons was fined $750 and Doc Rivers of the Hawks was fined $500, each for taking part in the altercation.

Three other Pistons -- William Bedford, James Edwards and John Salley -- were fined $500 each for leaving the bench area during the incident.
