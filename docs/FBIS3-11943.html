Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Villa Clara, Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada, president of the National Assembly of the People's Government and Politburo member, has said that one of the clearest conclusions of the Parliaments in the Workplaces is that workers want to save socialism. Alarcon participated in the conclusion of the parliament assemblies in Villa Clara, which concludes this process at the provincial level. Speaking to deputies and union leaders in Villa Clara, Alarcon de Quesada said that Villa Clara workers agree on the need to preserve the achievements of the Revolution and reject the IMF formula. Let no one think that capitalist formulas will solve the problems of the great majority of the population, he said. Alarcon de Quesada went on to say that after four years of the special period, there are signs of economic recovery.
