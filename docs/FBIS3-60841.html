Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By Vyacheslav Bondarenko, ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Rostov-on-Don, 23 December -- Four armed persons of Caucasian nationality today seized, according to preliminary information, 14 ninth-form schoolchildren from Rostov school number 25, an ITAR-TASS correspondent has just learned from unofficial sources. According to as yet unconfirmed information, the bandits forced the schoolchildren into a bus and subsequently went to the area of the military airport. They then made their demands: make a plane or helicopter available to fly to Tehran. Talks are now underway with the terrorists.
