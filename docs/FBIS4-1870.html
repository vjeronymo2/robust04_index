BFN [Text] Beijing, April 2 (XINHUA) -- Lena H. Sun, a Beijing-based correspondent of the U.S. WASHINGTON POST, was questioned here this morning by Chinese police after taking photographs in a graveyard without having gone through necessary procedures. She was intervened in line with China's relevant laws and regulations and was asked to abide by them. And she then left to resume her Beijing work, according to police sources.
