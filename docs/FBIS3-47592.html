Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- South Korean people express reverence for the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, Seoul- based radio Voice of National Salvation [VNS] said. Choe Kyong-su, a company clerk in Seoul, said when talking with his colleagues about the situation: "General Kim Chong-il was born in Mt. Paektu and cultivated his mind from his childhood. He is an outstanding military strategist and brilliant commander with such a matchless grit and iron will that once he issues an order, any formidable enemy kneels down, [word indistinct] the people and Army of the North have become a most dignified people and an invincible Army in the world because they are guided by the brilliant commander of the century. "No A-bomb and no force can subdue the Army and people of the North who have the brilliant commander, General Kim Chong-il, and are united around him in mind and purpose. "There is nobody but General Kim Chong-il who can command our national Army and take charge of the destiny of the 70 million Koreans. Only when we trust and follow him can the country be reunified and the nation prosper forever."
