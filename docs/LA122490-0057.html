December 24, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Christmas Day. The following will be closed:

MAIL: All post offices will be closed and no mail will be delivered.

BANKS: Most banks and savings and loan institutes will be closed.

COURTS: All courts will be closed.

SCHOOLS: Most public schools in Los Angeles County -- including schools in the Los Angeles Unified School District -- will be closed until Jan. 2.

GOVERNMENT: All federal, state, county and municipal offices -- except those providing emergency services -- will be closed.
