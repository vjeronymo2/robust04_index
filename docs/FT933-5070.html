930906 FT 06 SEP 93 / Employment trends survey says manufacturing still in recession MANUFACTURING remains in recession with falling order books and continuing redundancies, yesterday's employment trends survey by the AEEU engineering and electrical union shows. Just four companies of 100 surveyed had vacancies for skilled or semi-skilled workers. Some 27.5 per cent announced redundancies in the second quarter of the year compared with 20 per cent in the first quarter, and 16 per cent had poor order books compared with 17.5 per cent in the first quarter.
