November 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

THE GROVE RECEIVES A $12,500 DONATION

The Grove Shakespeare Festival announced Monday that it has received $12,500 from the Garden Grove Strawberry Festival to help match a $50,000 grant made earlier this year by the James Irvine Foundation. The latest donation brings the Grove "within $5,000 of reaching that goal," a theater spokeswoman said. JAN HERMAN
