931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / Pounds 200,000 for NHS agency's new look Almost Pounds 200,000 has been spent on creating a new look for NHS Supplies, the agency responsible for providing the health service with drugs, fuel and other supplies, figures given to MPs show. More than Pounds 5,500 went on creating a new logo for the agency. Mr David Blunkett, shadow health secretary, said: 'It is another example of the way consultancies, bureacracy and administration costs are taking precedence over patients' priorities.'
