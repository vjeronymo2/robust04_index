Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 5 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In the course of the counting of the cattle conducted in the country by the end of the last year some 25.5 million head of five kinds cattle were registered to be belonging to private farmers of Mongolia which is 89.6 percent of the total number of livestock of the country. More than two million head of cattle are falling on the share of agricultural companies with state-running ownership. They account for mainly pedigree highly-productive herds and flocks. Since 1992 the number of cattle having been privately owned has been increased by more than four million due to a reprivatization of the cattle belonging to the agricultural organizations.
