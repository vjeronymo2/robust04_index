BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 May (MENA)--Five defendants sentenced to death in connection with the assassination attempt on Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi were executed today. They are: Al-Sayyid Salah al- Sayyid Sulayman, 'Isam Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman Tuni, Nur-al-Din Sulayman Muhammad 'Ali, Amin Isma'il Musaylihi Salim, and Tariq 'Abd-al-Nabi Hasan al-Fahl. A military court on 17 March sentenced nine defendants in the case to death, four of them in absentia. Three other defendants were sentenced to 15 years at hard labor, one defendant to five years at hard labor, one defendant (fugitive) to three years, and one defendant (fugitive) was acquitted.
