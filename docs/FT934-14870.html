931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Anglo St James trims deficit A Pounds 190,000 reduction in interest charges to Pounds 255,000 helped Anglo St James, the commercial property developer, reduce its deficit from Pounds 289,000 to Pounds 25,000 pre-tax for the six months to end-June. Losses per share emerged at 0.2p (1.8p). The directors said market conditions were 'certainly more promising' than 12 months earlier.
