Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The search for peace continues in the Zambian capital. The peace talks are to continue today with a second reading of the general document on national reconciliation that has been presented by the mediators. The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] and government teams held a plenary meeting yesterday, but no one knows what kind of progress may have been made. Meanwhile, UNITA Information Secretary Dr. Jorge Alicerces Valentim yesterday accused the Luanda Government of lacking seriousness in its approach to the national reconciliation dossier. Speaking to a Portuguese radio station, that UNITA official said that the central and provincial government posts Luanda intends to give UNITA amount to nothing more than a joke. UNITA has been asking for greater participation in the country's administrative organs as a [word indistinct] of its commitment to the Lusaka peace talks [words indistinct].
