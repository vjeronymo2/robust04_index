Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] America's intelligence and spying agency, the CIA, in order to encourage the press to increase their propaganda attacks on Islam, has provided $35 million of financial aid to the writers of the secular press in Turkey. (BIKEK) newspaper, published in Istanbul, carries this report in today's edition, adding that, by making use of the CIA's financial assistance and the support of Western countries, Turkey's secular newspapers have started an extensive propaganda campaign among Turkey's secular circles against the Islamic-leaning Rafah [Prosperity] party.
