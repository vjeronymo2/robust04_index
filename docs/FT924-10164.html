921102 FT 02 NOV 92 / Glenys Kinnock seeks Euro seat MRS Glenys Kinnock, wife of Labour's former leader Mr Neil Kinnock, is seeking the party's nomination to contest Wales South-East, the safest seat in the European parliament. Mr Llewellyn Smith, the sitting member, became Westminster MP for Blaenau Gwent this year. He won the Euro-seat with a 108,488 majority over the Tories in a five-cornered contest in 1989.
