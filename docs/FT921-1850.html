920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / Case against woman dropped THE CASE against a woman charged with her ex-husband in connection with a burglary at the solicitors of Mr Paddy Ashdown, Liberal Democrat leader, was dropped yesterday. Ms Maria Watson of Moulsecoomb, East Sussex, had been charged with handling goods stolen from Bates, Wells & Braithwaite in London. At the same court her ex-husband, Mr Simon Berkowitz of Hove, West Sussex, was granted unconditional bail until April 16. He is charged with stealing documents and cash from the solicitors in January this year.
