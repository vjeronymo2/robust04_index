941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / Export confidence at 2 1/2-year high British exporters of manufactured goods are reaping the benefits of faster-than-expected growth in the world economy, with confidence about winning new orders at the highest level for more than two-and-a-half years, according to a survey yesterday. But the survey by Gallup for DHL International, the distribution company, found a growing number of companies concerned about their ability to meet surging demand for their products in the short-term due to a lack of manufacturing capacity.
