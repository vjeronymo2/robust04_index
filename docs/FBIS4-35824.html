BFN [Text] The Islamic Resistance Movement Hamas has stated that it will intensify its anti-Zionist operations. In a statement issued yesterday, Hamas once again condemned the accord between the PLO and the Zionist regime and called on its combatants to intensify their anti-Zionist operations. Hamas also called on the Palestinians in the occupied territories to go on a general strike on Thursday [9 June], the 79th month of the Palestinians' uprising.
