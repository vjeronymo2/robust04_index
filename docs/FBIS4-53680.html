BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 22 KYODO -- A delegation from Japan's No. 2 opposition party, the Social Democratic Party [of Japan] (SDP) [SDPJ], returned home Sunday [22 May] from an eight-day visit to China. During their stay in China starting May 15, the delegation led by SDPJ Secretary General Wataru Kubo met with Chinese officials on Japan's political scene and North Korea's suspected nuclear weapons program.
