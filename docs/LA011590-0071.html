January 15, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Disney Announces Florida Plans: A fourth theme park, a Soviet pavilion and seven new hotels will be among major projects to be built in the '90s at Florida's Disney World, Disney Chairman Michael Eisner announced. The conglomerate will also venture into time-share vacation units, and open 29 new attractions at its three existing Florida parks: the Magic Kingdom, Epcot Center and Disney-MGM Studios. A commercial shopping mall is planned on property that Disney owns in Osceola County, Eisner said.
