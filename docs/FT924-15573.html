921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG saw De Beers marked down on reports of a negative television documentary on the diamond industry due to be screened in the UK on Monday. Its shares fell to a new 12-month low of R50.50 before recovering to R50.85, down R1.90. The overall index fell 24 to 3,180 and industrials were 18 lower at 4,181. The gold index was off 6 at 887.
