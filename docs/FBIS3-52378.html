Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ways of implementing gas and oil agreements between Iran and Turkmenistan were discussed at a meeting between the minister of gas and oil of Turkmenistan and our country's oil minister in Tehran on Monday [3 January] evening. According to a Central News Unit report, under an agreement between the oil ministers of the two countries, Turkmenistan is prepared to provide the gas required by the border areas of the two countries, and to submit the results of technical and economic studies on pipelines for that country's gas exports to Iran. At the meeting, possible ways of routing Turkmenistan's oil and gas pipelines, including routes through the Islamic Republic of Iran, were reviewed.
