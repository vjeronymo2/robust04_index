Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A fragmentation grenade was thrown at an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] unit in the Erez checkpoint north of the Gaza Strip. A Gazan was slightly wounded by shrapnel, but the soldiers did not sustain any casualties. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the IDF closed down the Erez checkpoint and is searching for the terrorist who threw the grenade. A bus which had been hijacked by two youngsters was set ablaze this morning at the Bayt Lahiyah junction. The youngsters threatened the driver with a gun and a knife, and then let him go at the junction.
