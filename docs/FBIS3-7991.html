Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant-General Janos Deak has been appointed commander of the Hungarian Army; at the same time the retiring commander Colonel-General Kalman Loerincz has been relieved of the post. For his excellent work in the maintenance of Hungary's military defense capability, in the establishment of the Hungarian Army's national image, and in the upholding of its military preparedness, the retiring army commander was presented the Hungarian Republic's Medium Cross Order of Merit decorated with a Star, by President of the Republic Arpad Goencz. [Defense Minister] Lajos Fur presented an ornamental saber to Kalman Loerincz. Arpad Goencz stressed in his speech that Hungary's security could be guaranteed by having an army which was not only able to defend the country but able even to deter a possible aggressor.
