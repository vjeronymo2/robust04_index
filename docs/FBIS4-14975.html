BFN [Text] Russia has sent a shipment of fuel for Ukrainian nuclear power plants and thus, started to fulfill part of its commitments. With regard to the U.S. payment of $60 million to Russia, this issue has also been resolved. This was stated today by Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister Borys Tarasyuk. He underlined that the United States has created an impressive legal footing and outlined the directions for assisting nuclear disarmament in Ukraine. According to the deputy foreign minister, the sum of $350 million will be subject to corrections depending on real needs and possibilities. Borys Tarasyuk underlined that an agreement has been signed with Japan on granting Ukraine tens of millions of dollars worth of assistance. Discussions are being held with Italy on aid in the form of new technologies. The deputy foreign minister expressed hope that the new legislature will positively resolve the issue of Ukraine joining the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty.
