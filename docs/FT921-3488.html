920313 FT 13 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Cala interim losses reduced to Pounds 1.57m Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.57m were announced by Cala, the builder and property dealer, for the six months to December 31. The comparable loss was Pounds 3.95m. Turnover was lower at Pounds 27.3m (Pounds 33.8m) but sales continued to be made in all areas although margins were under pressure. Losses per share fell to 2.98p (7.21p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 1.15p.
