BFN [Text] Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir, president of the Republic, has said that the enemies of Islam and the revolution meet abroad and incite the colonialists to invade Sudan. He called on the sons of the homeland to prepare to defend it. During his address yesterday in al-Mujlad town, in Western Kordofan State, Lt. Gen. 'Umar said the banner of Islam has risen in Sudan, pointing out the [word indistinct] of the imam al-Mahdi. He promised the masses to contribute to the success of the missionary activity project and agricultural projects to strengthen development. His excellency also issued a decree granting amnesty to all those who were involved in the incidents of the Misiriyyah and Zuyud [tribal clashes] and he received the reconciliation document concerning this issue.
