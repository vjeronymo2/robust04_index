July 8, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DANNEMEYER'S ATTACK ON MANDELA TERMED SHAMEFUL

The article "Mandela Talk Draws Cheers From Congress" (June 27) by Times staff writer Edwin Chen was indeed inspiring.

It is too bad that Republican Congressman William E. Dannemeyer from Fullerton had to show his narrow-minded attitude when he likened Mr. Mandela to H. Rap Brown and Willie Horton. Perhaps Rep. Dannemeyer has been too long in office and needs a long, worthwhile rest.

JAIME B. VEGA

Santa Ana
