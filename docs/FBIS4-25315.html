BFN [Text] Beijing, June 9 (XINHUA) -- Today's economic news in brief.

Guilin Attracts More Overseas Investment
Guilin, a city known for its superb landscape in Guangxi Zhuang autonomous region, boasts 385 overseas-funded enterprises, 200 of which are industrial projects. The city's overseas investment has broadened from solely service industry to such industrial sectors as electronics, machinery, food processing and handicrafts.
Technological Activities Draw Guangzhou Residents
Residents in Guangzhou, capital city of Guangdong Province, are very active in studying new technology as 610 such organizations have been established in the city, drawing over 80,000 people to learn and exchange skills. This activity has paid off as development results turned out in these organizations produced 150 million yuan in economic returns last year.
Farmers Keen on Ecological Farming
About 20,000 ethnic farmers in Gongcheng Yao Autonomous County have benefited from ecological farming as their per capita grain output and net income have both registered noticeable growth. Farmers here are planting trees and introducing solar energy to neighboring villages to improve the county's ecological structure.
