930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Greyfriars net asset value falls to 26.87p Lower asset value, reduced income and a cut in the interim dividend are reported by Greyfriars Investment Company. At March 31 1993 net assets had fallen from Pounds 3.11m to Pounds 1.88m for a per share value of 26.87p (44.44p). Earnings per share were 0.37p (3.12p) and the interim dividend is 0.5p (2p).
