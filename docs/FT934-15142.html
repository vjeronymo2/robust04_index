931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Frost pays Pounds 4.5m for 17 Texaco sites Frost Group, the independent petrol retailer, is to acquire a further 17 sites from Texaco for Pounds 4.52m cash funded from its own resources. The majority of the sites are located in the north-west of England and the purchase increases Frost's petrol retailing outlets to 168.
