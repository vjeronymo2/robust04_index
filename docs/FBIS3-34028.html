Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has received in his office a senior European Community official. Mr. Eberhardt Rhein, director of the Mediterranean, Near East, and Middle East Division of the Commission of European Communities discussed with the premier means to bolster ties between Jordan and the EC to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in the region. Earlier today, Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Talal al-Hasan held talks with Mr. Rhein on similar issues, particularly that the EC heads the multilateral economic talks for peace in the Middle East.
