BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpts] Efes-94 exercises, one of the planned exercises of the Turkish Armed Forces, have been successfully concluded. The exercises, carried out with the aim of developing the coordination and cooperation among the ground, naval, and air force units, were commanded by Aegean Army Commander General Hikmet Koksal. [passage omitted] The exercises, which were conducted in the Doganbey region of Izmir's Seferihisar District, were observed by seven generals and 14 officers from various countries, including the Macedonian chief of staff.
