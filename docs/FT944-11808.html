941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / Barbican director fights to retain job Baroness Detta O'Cathain, managing director of the Barbican Centre in the City of London, was yesterday battling for her job. After criticism of her management style, the City this week said that it was seeking a deputy managing director who would eventually take control. Fifty of the 223 staff have quit in Lady O'Cathain's time. She said yesterday: 'When I came the place was out of control financially. I have always stayed within budget.'
