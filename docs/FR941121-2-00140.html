Competitive Priority: Within this absolute priority 3, the Secretary, under 34 CFR 75.105(c)(2)(ii), will give preference to applications that meet the following competitive priority. An application that meets this competitive priority would be selected by the Secretary over applications of comparable merit that do not meet the priority: A project that would give a priority to providing support for postsecondary students who are members of groups that have been underrepresented in the field of special education research, such as members of racial or ethnic minority groups (e.g. Black, Hispanic, American Indian, Alaskan Native, Asian, or Pacific Islander), and individuals with disabilities.
