May 31, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: AGENCY BEGINS FUND-RAISER;

SANTA MONICA

Family Service of Santa Monica has launched a campaign to raise the remaining funds for a new building to house the counseling agency. The agency so far has raised $370,000 of the $500,000 needed for construction of its Euclid Street headquarters, but said it will step up its efforts to raise the additional funds.

For more information about the agency's fund-raising campaign, call Karen Howard at (213) 451-9747.
