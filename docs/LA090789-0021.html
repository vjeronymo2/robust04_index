September 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

JURY DETAILS

I read with displeasure The Times' Night Stalker jury story ("Distraught Alternate Named to Stalker Jury," Part I, Aug. 16). I take exception to printing the personal histories and experiences of the remaining jurors on the case.

These people are not politicians who should come under public scrutiny. They are people who have been drafted to serve the county for a pitiful $5 a day and deserve some privacy and respect.

JOSEPH L. SADDLER

Bellflower
