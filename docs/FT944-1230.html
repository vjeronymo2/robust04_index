941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Black & Edgington Black & Edgington Group, the USM-quoted provider of portable buildings and tents for sporting occasions and exhibitions, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.07m for the year to end-July against profits of Pounds 20,000. Turnover improved to Pounds 8.45m (Pounds 7.6m) and there were operating profits on continuing activities of Pounds 340,000 (Pounds 287,000 losses). Losses on discontinued activities, however, were Pounds 967,000, mainly property related, and the pre-tax line was after interest charges of Pounds 440,000 (Pounds 571,000). Losses per share emerged at 0.21p (0.01p earnings).
