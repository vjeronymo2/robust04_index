BFN [Text] On 20 May, Guangdong fulfilled the task of selling 1.2 billion yuan of two-year state treasury bonds ahead of schedule, and greater efforts were made to do a good job in sales of three-year state treasury bonds. By yesterday morning, three-year state treasury bonds amounting to 6.69502 billion yuan had been sold throughout the province. In this way, Guangdong has fulfilled the state-assigned task of selling state treasury bonds ahead of schedule.
