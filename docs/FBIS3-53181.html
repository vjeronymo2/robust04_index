Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a statement issued by the Amal Movement's operations room, the Lebanese resistance group Amal attacked an enemy post near the 'Ali al-Tahir position at 2215 yesterday. The post received direct hits and the troops manning it were killed or wounded. Immediately afterward, the enemy and agents bombarded Kafr Tibnit and al-Nabatiyah with artillery and large-caliber machine guns.
