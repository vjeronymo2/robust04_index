BFN [Text] The Yemeni civil service and administrative reform minister, Yahya Husayn al-Arashi, arrived in Khartoum this evening bearing a written message from the Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to his brother President Lieutenant General 'Umar al-bashir. The Yemeni minister, al-Arashi, was received at Khartoum Airport by Brigadier PSC [past staff college] 'Abd-al-Rahim Muhammad Husayn, the interior minister and secretary general at the Presidency along with the Yemeni charge d'affaires to Khartoum. The Yemeni minister told reporters at the airport that the relations between Sudan and the Republic of Yemen are very special.
