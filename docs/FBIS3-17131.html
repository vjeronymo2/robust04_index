Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] As an Azerbaijani defense ministry spokesman told Interfax, Armenian troops launched an offensive toward the village of Alkhanly, Fizuli region, Azerbaijan at 8 am Tuesday. Many armored vehicles are said to be involved in the offensive. Fighting on this section of the front continues. On Monday, Armenian forces reportedly tried to break through to the strategic Goradiz rail junction near the Azerbaijani-Armenian frontier but were forced back to where they had come from. By reports from Baku, two Karabakh army trucks with servicemen have been destroyed. Meanwhile, Azerbaijani forces have come under mortar fire in the Agdam and Ter-Tersk areas. There has been no reports from the Karabakh army so far.
