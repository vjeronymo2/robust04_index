940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Brent Intl expands Brent International, the specialist chemical manufacturer, has bought ICP West, a California-based supplier of specialist chemicals, for Dollars 3.87m (Pounds 2.65m) cash. Management accounts showed ICP made pre-tax profits of Dollars 600,000 in 1993 on turnover of Dollars 5m. Assets at completion were valued at Dollars 600,000.
