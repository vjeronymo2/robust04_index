February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Dr. Ruth in Love: Dr. Ruth Westheimer will reportedly star in a half-hour ABC comedy series called "Dr. Ruth's House." The sex therapist will play Dr. Ruth Engelhof, who runs an off-campus rooming house near Albert University in Connecticut and who is romantically involved with the dean. Shooting begins in Los Angeles in April, but an ABC spokesman would not confirm when the show will air. BETH KLEID
