Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Business World" column] [Text] (DYN) -- San Carlos de Bariloche Argentina could be building a nuclear reactor for Thailand, officials from INVAP [Applied research Institute], a joint venture company between the Rio Negro government and the Atomic Energy Commission said yesterday. A Thai delegation recently visited INVAP's research center at the invitation of the Argentine government as part of the attempt to take part in Thailand's international tender to build a 100-million dollar reactor. The Thai delegation was also shown round the Sori Moria research centre where they were given details of the SAC-B scientific satellite that is being jointly built by NASA and Argentina's Space Activities Commission.
