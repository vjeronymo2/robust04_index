920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / Iraqi Kurds to stage election IRAQ'S Kurds vote tomorrow for an executive leader and legislature in an election condemned by the regime of President Saddam Hussein and by Turkey and Iran, the two neighbouring states with large Kurdish populations, reports Gareth Smyth in Arbil. The poll has been organised by the Kurdistan Front, a coalition of seven parties which has administered the region following the creation of 'safe havens' by the allies .
