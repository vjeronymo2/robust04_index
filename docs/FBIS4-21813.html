BFN [Excerpts] Bonn -- Security near the Israeli Embassy in Bonn was further increased on Wednesday. In the afternoon, cars using roads near the embassy were checked very thoroughly. It seems that several locations were chosen where a great number of police have been posted. [passage omitted] According to police, stepped-up security is to be in force over the next three to four days. After that, these activities will not be stopped altogether but decreased to a ``normal level.'' An Embassy spokeswoman said that security standards are very high, anyway.
