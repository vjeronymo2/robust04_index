930403 FT 03 APR 93 / International Company News: US carrier seeks Heathrow route AMERICAN Airlines yesterday said it had asked the US Department of Transportation for authority to operate the route between Philadelphia and London's Heathrow airport, writes Nikki Tait in New York. The authority is being sold by USAir as part of its tie-up with British Airways. Earlier in the week, another US carrier, United Airlines, had offered to buy the authority from USAir for Dollars 14.5m.
