Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani president met the deputy foreign minister for Europe and Asia in Baku and congratulated him on the anniversary of the victory of the Islamic revolution, calling for accelerating the implementation of memoranda of understanding between Tehran and Baku. He said that Azerbaijan will do its best toward this end. Aliyev described President Hashemi-Rafsanjani's visit to Baku as a turning point in bilateral relations and expressed the hope that during his visit to Iran, new ground for expanding bilateral relations will be examined. During the meeting, the Azerbaijani president explained the latest developments in the Karabakh conflict and restated his country's wish for a peaceful solution of the conflict, pointing out that the Azerbaijani forces are sufficiently ready to defend the Republic's territorial integrity. During the meeting, Va'ezi stated that one objective of his visit to Azerbaijan is to follow up the process of implementing the bilateral memoranda of understanding.
