920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / Navy increases redundancies THE Royal Navy has increased to 1,150 the number of personnel it plans to make redundant in the second wave of job losses under the Options for Change programme, aimed at cutting personnel by 8,000 from the present level of about 60,000. Mr Archie Hamilton, armed forces minister, said about 450 officers and 700 ratings would lose their jobs next year.
