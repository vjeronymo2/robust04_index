Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] AL-NAHAR has learned that the PLO has decided to transfer the headquarters of popular federations from Tunis to Gaza and Jericho upon taking over power from the Israelis. It is worth noting that Tunis became the headquarters of eight Palestinian federations following the PLO's departure from Beirut in 1983. They are the federations of writers; lawyers and jurists; teachers, women, workers, engineers; doctors, pharmacists, and dentists, and farmers. [passage omitted]
