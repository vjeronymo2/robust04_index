940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Ex-premier to be sentenced Mr Brian Burke, 47, former premier of Western Australia, was found guilty yesterday of four charges of false pretences, and is due to be sentenced today, Mr Burke, who served as Australian ambassador to Ireland between 1988 and 1991, was released on bail of ADollars 25,000 (Pounds 11,904). He had pleaded not guilty to obtaining more than ADollars 17,000 on four separate occasions from a parliamentary account which provides money for politicians to make approved trips out of state. Each charge carries a maximum penalty of three years' jail.
