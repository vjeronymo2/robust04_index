930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / Death sentences for 34 Algerians AN Algiers court condemned 34 Moslem fundamentalists to death at the weekend, the government newspaper El Moudjahid reported yesterday, Reuter reports from Algiers. Five of the condemned were among 22 men in a special court facing charges which included setting up an armed group, conspiracy against the state, murder, kidnapping and sabotage. A further 29 men being hunted were sentenced to death in their absence, the newspaper said.
