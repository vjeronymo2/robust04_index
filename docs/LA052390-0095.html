May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

TWA Announces Summer Fare Sale: Trans World Airlines said it has cut domestic fares to the "lowest, most aggressive level" this year.

The lowest fares for travel Tuesday through Thursday have been reduced to $99 for flights up to 1,500 miles and $149 for flights more than 1,500 miles. Prices are each way and require round-trip purchase. Tickets must be purchased by June 7 and travel completed before December 13. Passengers must book their flights 7 days before departure and purchase their tickets 24 hours after booking a flight. The fares are non-refundable.
