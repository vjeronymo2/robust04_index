Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] After successfully fulfilling all items on the agenda, the Second Session of the Eighth Shandong Provincial People's Congress which focused on the subject of reform, development, and stability, triumphantly ended at Zhengzhuquan People's Assembly Hall on the afternoon of 5 March. During the six-day session, all deputies exercised the sacred authority vested by the Constitution and law with a high sense of responsibility of being masters of their own destiny, conscientiously examined and discussed the work report delivered by Governor Zhao Zhihao and other reports, pooled the wisdom of all, and jointly discussed major plans for making the people rich and Shandong prosperous. The congress session was a meeting to give play to democracy and unity, to do practical things, to boost enthusiasm, and to mobilize the people of various nationalities across the province to strive to successfully realize the objectives and tasks defined by the congress session. Eight hundred and ninety-six deputies attended the closing ceremony on the afternoon session. Li Zhen, executive chairman of congress session and executive member of the congress presidium, presided over the closing ceremony. Present at the session and also seated in the front row of the rostrum were executive chairmen of the congress session and executive members of the congress presidium, including Jiang Chunyun, Ma Zhongcai, Wang Shufang, Miao Fenglin, Guo Songnian, Xu Jianchun, Ma Shizhong, Guo Changcai, Yan Qingqing, Ma Xutao, and Xu Xuemeng. Voting by a show of the hands, the congress session adopted the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on the goverment work report; the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on fulfillment of Shandong Province's 1993 economic and social development plan and on 1994 economic and social development plan; the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on the fulfillment of Shandong Province's 1993 financial account and on the 1994 financial budget; the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on the provincial people's congress standing committee work report; the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on the work report of the provincial Higher People's Court; and the resolution of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress on the work report of the provincial People's Procuratorate. The session was satisfied with the work of the provincial people's government during the past year. The participants at the congress believed 1994 to be crucial to accelerating the building of the socialist market economic structure and maintaining a good economic development trend. During this year, all levels and all trades and professions across the province should act in line with the general demand of the central authorities on seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, expanding opening up, promoting development, and maintaining stability, persist in emancipating the mind, seek truth from facts, comprehensively promote reform, expand opening up, accelerate scientific and technological progress, actively readjust the structure, raise economic efficiency, achieve actual progress in forming the basic pattern of the socialist market economic system, maintain sustained, rapid, and sound development in the national economy, do a good job in building the spiritual civilization, and promote social stability and progress in all fields. The session called on people across the province to rally closely around the party Central Committee with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the nucleus, hold aloft the great banner of building socialism with Chinese characteristics under the leadership of the Shandong Provincial CPC Committee, emancipate the mind, seek truth from facts, work and advance in unity, blaze new trials, comprehensively fulfill all tasks of 1994, and strive to make greater success in reform, opening up, and modernization. All items on the agenda were completed at 1520 in the afternoon. The second session of the Eighth Shandong Provincial People's Congress came to a successful end in the playing of the majestic national anthem.
