BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 10 Jun 94 Evening Edition] [Text] A top official of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MOFA] revealed on 10 June that former U.S. President Jimmy Carter might stop over in Japan on his way home from a visit to the DPRK (North Korea) and South Korea next week. If he does stop over, he is expected to meet with Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa.
