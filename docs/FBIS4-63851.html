BFN [Report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS roundup under the rubric "Yesterday, Today, and Tomorrow": "Design Office Named for Mikoyan Victorious"] [Text] The Mikoyan Design Office has won a contract to modernize more than 100 MiG-21 bis fighter planes in the armory of the Indian Air Force. As the Indian Ministry of Defense reported, the contract is valued at $350 million. The Russians won this right in fierce competition with Western and Israeli companies.
