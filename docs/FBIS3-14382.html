Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (MENA)--The Arab League Council concluded its 101st session tonight following two days of debates that discussed a number of important Arab issues, including continuing the Arab boycott of Israel until the withdrawal of the latter's forces from the occupied Arab territories, support for the Palestinian people and the provision of protection for it, solidarity with the Libyan al-Jamahiriyah in its crisis with the West, and commitment to the Arabism of Jerusalem. MENA learned that the ministerial council stressed in its meetings the need for a comprehensive Arab reconciliation and the importance of Arab water security. The council also decided to lift the Arab boycott of Liberia, which used to cooperate with Israel. Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid will hold a news conference later tonight to explain the outcome of the session.
