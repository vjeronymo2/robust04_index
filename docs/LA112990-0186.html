November 29, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

DECISION EXPECTED FRIDAY ON RIDER'S ELIGIBILITY APPEAL

A decision is expected Friday on basketball standout J. R. Rider's appeal to regain his sophomore year of eligibility at Antelope Valley College.

Rider, a 6-foot-5 transfer from Allen County (Kan.) College, was declared ineligible for this season early this month when it was determined that he did not have the minimum 2.0 grade-point average.

Foothill Conference representatives voted 6 to 1 in Rider's favor and have forwarded Rider's appeal to the Southern California Appeals Board of the Commission on Athletics.

The three-member appeals board will meet today at El Camino College and is scheduled to announce its decision Friday.
