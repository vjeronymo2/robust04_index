Looks `Depressed' PY1606144294 Sao Paulo AGENCIA ESTADO in Portuguese 1152 GMT 16 Jun 94 PY1606144294 Sao Paulo AGENCIA ESTADO Portuguese BFN [Excerpts] Rio de Janeiro, 16 Jun (AE) -- The plane carrying President Itamar Franco and the body of his nephew Ariosto Carlos Borges Franco -- who died of a heart attack early on 15 June -- from Cartagena, Colombia, landed at the Galeao air base at 0045 today. Franco then took part in a motorcade accompanying the fire truck that carried the body of his nephew to the Sao Joao Batista Cemetery, in Botafogo, in the southern part of the city of Rio de Janeiro. [passage omitted] At 0250, looking very depressed, Franco left the cemetery for the Gloria Hotel, where he was going to rest. [passage omitted]
