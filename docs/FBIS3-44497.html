Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, January 4 (XINHUA) -- The Hongqiao International Airport in this China's largest economic center has operated safely for five years running. A survey shows that the airport accommodated 63,000 planes and transported 7.6 million passengers last year without airport-related incidents. An official of the airport attributed the good results to imposing ten measures to ensure safety and on-the-job responsibility. While reinforcing safety checks and inviting specialists to give lectures, airport authorities have increased the number of tours of inspection at the airport. Last year the airport handled 1,310 passengers with incomplete boarding papers and false identity cards as well as 193 persons without tickets. Numerous contraband goods were also confiscated.
