931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / International Company News: Merck drug withdrawn MERCK yesterday withdrew Roxiam, a schizophrenia treatment which was awaiting approval from the US Food and Drug Administration. The move follows the death of a patient in Europe, write Richard Waters and Paul Abrahams in New York. The drug, which has been marketed in Europe by Astra, the Swedish company, for the past two and a half years, was said by analysts to have the potential for peak annual sales of Dollars 100m-Dollars 250m in the US. The withdrawal followed the development of a blood disorder in eight patients taking the drug in Europe.
