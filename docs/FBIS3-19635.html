Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, March 20 (A.A) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said today that Iranian authorities handed 16 terrorists, including two dead, to Turkey. In a written statement, Mentese said that the government had made important gains against terrorists and that 160 separatist terrorists, including 78 dead, were caught by security forces in the last week. Three missile launchers, 20 missile launcher shells, 28 long-range guns, 5 revolvers, 22 hand grenades, and ammunition were seized in the recent searches.
