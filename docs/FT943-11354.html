940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Stock index futures closed above the day's low point after a volatile session dominated by worries over interest rates and weakness in the fixed interest markets, writes Joel Kibazo. The September futures contract on the FT-SE 100 closed at 3,095, at a 13 point premium to cash, but 8 below its previous finish. Volume was a healthy 20,728 lots. It opened at 3,112 and during the session, it touched a high of 3,124 and a low of 3,064. In traded options, stock options made a significant contribution to the day's total of 42,473 lots.
