Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] Manuel Camacho Solis, Mexican foreign secretary, will arrive in Colombia within hours to request emergency towers for an electric interconnection to the electric company in Santa Fe de Bogota. Both Mayor Jaime Castro and the national government have stated their willingness to help Mexico. Colombia will lend the Mexican Government 25 electric towers to cooperate in the crisis in that country, given the partial destruction of its electric infrastructure by Zapatista guerrilla forces. [Begin Energy Minister Guido Nule recording] We began immediately the process yesterday, and it should be complete by now, so we can send them in an airplane that will be directly sent from Mexico by them; in other words, those towers should be in Mexico this very day. [end recording] Mayor Jaime Castro announced that municipal authorities will also join the international cooperation efforts. [Begin Castro recording] It is an act of solidarity from Santa Fe de Bogota to the Mexican people. [end recording] Mexican Foreign Minister Manuel Camacho Solis will arrive in Santa Fe de Bogota within hours to receive the Colombian aid and personally supervise its shipment to Mexico.
