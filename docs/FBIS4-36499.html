BFN [Text] At 1045 this morning his highness the amir received Engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, special envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary-general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, and his accompanying delegation. Mr. al-'Attas briefed his highness, God protect him, on the regrettable development of the Yemeni conflict and the course of the fighting there. The meeting was attended by Shaykh Nasir Muhammad al-Ahmad, minister of the amir's affairs, and Mishari Jasim al-'Anjari, minister of justice and administrative affairs.
