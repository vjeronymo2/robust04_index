BFN [Statement issued by the "Armed Forces Command of the Democratic Republic of Yemen"; place and date not given] [Text] In the context of the escalation in the military situation and in an open challenge leveled against all the Arab and international efforts aimed to bring about an end to the fighting in Yemen, the al-Ahmar gang in Sanaa has launched missile and air strikes against the residential quarters and vital civil installations in Aden. Yesterday at dawn, Aden came under a missile attack aimed at civilian residential quarters. The missiles were engaged by our air defenses, which shot down one of the missiles in flight while another missile fell into the sea. This morning two enemy aircraft tried to bomb the little Aden refinery, one of the vital and important economic installations belonging to our Yemeni people. Our air defenses succeeded in foiling this treacherous attempt, which failed to achieve its objectives. However, one of the houses of the innocent citizens in little Aden was hit, killing a number of women and children. The Command of the Armed Forces in the Democratic Republic of Yemen, while condemning these barbarous acts which violate all the rules of war, reserves the right to respond strongly and decisively to this kind of aggression. Let those who are warned take heed.
