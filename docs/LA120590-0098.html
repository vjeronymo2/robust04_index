December 5, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

More than 300 people attended the "CHOC & Roll Christmas" benefit concert Monday night at the Anaheim Embassy Suites Hotel which, according to event coordinator Kenny Karst, raised about $3,000 for Children's Hospital of Orange County. The event featured Dem Horn Dogs, a '60s-style R&B revue, plus several local performers. "It was an emotional and very rewarding experience for all those who participated," Karst said.
