May 4, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

In keeping with an agreement reached in January, the Dallas Mavericks have placed forward Adrian Dantley on waivers.

On Jan. 20, Dantley signed a contract amendment excusing the Mavericks from their obligation to pay the final year of his guaranteed contract for 1990-91. In exchange, he got the assurance that he would be waived after the season ended.

Dantley, 34, will become an unrestricted free agent upon clearing waivers today.
