BFN [Text] Iraq has informed the Arab League that the Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers are continuing to provide facilities for U.S., British, and French aircraft to violate Iraqi airspace, which seriously threatens Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter he addressed yesterday to the Arab League secretary general, Iraq's permanent representative to the Arab League said that the aircraft violated Iraq's airspace 1-9 May after taking off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. During this period they carried out 355 hostile air sorties. The Iraqi permanent representative attached to his letter a list detailing these violations.
