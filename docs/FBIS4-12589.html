BFN [Text] A senior military source is quoted by the ITIM news agency as confirming that the Israeli Army is preparing for the possibility that the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] organization will follow through on its threat to launch missile attacks against Israeli targets. Hamas has threatened that among the attacks it intends to carry out in advance of Independence Day will be missile launchings against targets that include Israeli settlements in the territories. The military source told ITIM that there is little doubt that Hamas has the ability to use missiles, primarily LAW antitank missiles that are easy to carry and that are widely used by the Israeli Army.
