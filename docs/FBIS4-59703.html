BFN [Text] In the Gaza Strip, Israeli soldiers shot and wounded two Palestinians who opened fire on them from a truck near the settlement Morag. There were no Israeli casualties. Our reporter Alan Ben-'Ami says the gunmen got away and a joint Israeli-Palestinian team arrived on the scene to investigate. Meanwhile, the Israeli pullout from Gaza is nearly complete. The Civil Administration building was turned over to the Palestinians this afternoon at a ceremony attended by Brigadier General David Gazit, Israel's head of the Civil Administration in the strip. Four hundred more Palestinian policemen are due to arrive in the Gaza Strip from Egypt today.
