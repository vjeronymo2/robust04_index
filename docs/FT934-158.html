931230 FT 30 DEC 93 / International Capital Markets: Long-term Bonds in Poland The Polish government was considering issuing up to 45,000bn zlotys (Dollars 2.1bn) worth of long-term bonds to help finance the 1994 budget deficit, Mr Marek Borowski, the finance minister, said, Reuter reports from Warsaw. Mr Borowski said the bonds, to be offered mainly abroad, were not included in the budget plan which was finally accepted by the government last Monday.
