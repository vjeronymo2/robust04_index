May 31, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HYPER: About 80% of hyperactive children are boys, and "first-born males have the highest rate of the disorder," says Dr. Paul Hoernig of Newport Harbor Hospital. Some of those with what is now called attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder, or ADHD, will grow out of it, but there is no way to determine who will, Hoernig said. . . . Newport Harbor is working with UC Irvine in an unusual program for hyperactive kids that links special schooling with medical treatment that many of them need (N1). Bill Billiter and Jan Herman
