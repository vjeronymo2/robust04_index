BFN [Text] Vietnamese and American experts on Wednesday conducted the 31st joint search for MIA's, while there has been a breakthrough to the diplomatic relations between the two countries. The MIA office in Hanoi said about 102 American experts took part in a joint search, which will end by 17 July in 27 provinces. One hundred Vietnamese took part in eight search groups. Five of them will excavate U.S. aircraft downing sites and the rest will interview the local people.
