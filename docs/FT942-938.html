940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Arts: Tanglewood - Openings The summer season at Tanglewood, in the heart of the Massachusetts countryside, begins on Friday with the first of a series of Independence Weekend events featuring Wynton Marsalis, Liza Minelli and Nanci Griffith. Tanglewood's new concert hall will be inaugurated on July 7, when the Boston Symphony Orchestra takes up residence for two months of concerts. Artists appearing with the orchestra this summer include Jessye Norman, Gidon Kremer, Bernard Haitink and Mariss Jansons.
