Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Just in from the news desk: Our correspondent in al-Nabatiyah reported a short time ago that the Islamic Resistance carried out an attack against the 'Alman-al-Shumariyah joint Israeli-South Lebanese Army position with machine guns and rockets shortly after 1330 today. The fighting is still continuing. Our correspondent said that the Israeli forces are retaliating by directing their gunfire against the periphery of Zawtar al-Sharqiyah, Zawtar al-Gharbiyah, and the al-Litani River basin.
