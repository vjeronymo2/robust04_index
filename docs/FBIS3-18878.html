Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar. 8 (A.A.) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said today that all kinds of measures have been taken for the March 27 local elections and any behavior aimed at restricting freedom of choice during the elections, will be neutralized immediately. In a written statement on measures taken by the government to provide security during the polls, Mentese said it was an important duty of the state to enable citizens to vote in a secure atmosphere. He added that any incident occurring in any part of the country will immediately be forwarded to a "crisis desk" to be formed in the ministry, which will take relevant measures. "The security measures will enable the elections to reflect the free will of people," he said.
