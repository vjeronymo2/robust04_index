941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Gains in Toronto for oil and metal stocks helped break the TSE 300 composite index's four-day losing streak with a moderate advance. According to preliminary data, the TSE 300 composite index gained 17.18 points, or 0.40%, to 4,259.95 on volume of 54.845m shares valued at CDollars 708m. Ten of the 14 sub-indexes were up, led by oil and gas, metal and transportation stocks. Gold and real estate stocks posted the biggest declines.
