Language: German Article Type:BFN [APA report: "Protest Against China's Qiao Shi"] [Text] Vienna -- The foreign policy spokeswoman for the Greens, Marijana Grandits, has "sharply protested" against Chancellor Vranitzky's and parliamentary president Heinz Fischer's reception of Chinese parliamentary president Qiao Shi. "As head of the police and the secret services, Qiao Shi is one of the most sinister figures and responsible for all human rights violations in China in the past few years," she stressed.
