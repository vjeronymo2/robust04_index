November 4, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

U.S. VOLLEYBALL TEAM PLAYS JAPAN AT IRVINE

The U.S. men's volleyball team, led by local players Scott Fortune and Mark Arnold, will play Japan at 7 tonight in UC Irvine's Bren Center.

Fortune, a former standout at Laguna Beach High School and Stanford, played on the U.S. gold-medal team at the 1988 Olympics in Seoul. Arnold played at Costa Mesa High School and Pepperdine.

Ticket prices for the match are $25, $10, $7 and $3. Proceeds will go to the UC Irvine men's volleyball program.
