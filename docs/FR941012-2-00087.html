Title: Information Collection Related to Delegation of Authority to States. Abstract: The Secretary of the Interior is authorized to enter into agreements delegating to States the authority and responsibility for conducting royalty inspections, audits, and investigations with respect to Federal and Indian lands within the State. While working under a delegation of authority, the State must submit an annual workplan, quarterly progress reports, and quarterly vouchers claiming 100 percent reimbursement for the cost of eligible activities. Currently, there are 10 States working under delegations of authority. Bureau Form Number: None. Frequency: Annually and quarterly. Description of Respondents: States.
