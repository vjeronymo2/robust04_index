940422 FT 22 APR 94 / International Company News: McDonald's reports 12% advance McDonald's Corporation, the US fast-food group, reported first-quarter income of Dollars 243.4m, or 65 cents a share, in the first quarter, up 12 per cent from Dollars 218.3m, or 57 cents, in the first quarter of 1993, writes Laurie Morse.
