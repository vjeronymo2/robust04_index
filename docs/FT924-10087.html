921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / International Company News: China Light & Power ahead CHINA Light & Power, the monopoly supplier of electricity to Kowloon and the New Territories of Hong Kong, yesterday reported yearly net profits up 11.4 per cent to HKDollars 3.2bn (USDollars 415.6m) from HKDollars 2.8bn, writes Simon Holberton in Hong Kong. Turnover improved 13.5 per cent to HKDollars 13.4bn from HKDollars 11.8bn.
