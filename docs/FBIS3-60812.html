Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 30 Dec (MENA)--The Supreme State Security Court--Emergency today passed sentence in the case of the assassination of writer Dr. Faraj Fudah. It issued the death sentence against 'Ali 'Abd-al-Shafi Ahmad Ramadan and Ashraf al-Sayyid Ibrahim, who was executed earlier for attempting to assassinate Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif. The court sentenced Abu-al-'Ula Muhammad 'Abd-Rabbuh to 15 years with hard labor and fugitives 'Ali Hasan 'Ali and Basim Muhammad Khalil Shahin to 10 years with hard labor and three years imprisonment respectively. The court acquitted eight defendants: Safwat 'Abd-al-Ghani, Mansur Ahmad Mansur, Muhammad Ibrahim 'Abd-al-Hamid, Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Abd-al-Wahid, Ashraf Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman, Jalal Mahmud, Hasan 'Ali Mahmud, and Walid Hasan Kamil.
