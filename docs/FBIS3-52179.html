Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz has stressed that the Israeli military escalation in south Lebanon is aimed at diverting attention from the main objective of the peace negotiations to discussions on security. He said in a press meeting this afternoon that Lebanon must not yield to this or accept the replacement of basic and substantive issues by security proposals. Concerning relations between Israel and the Vatican, Buwayz hoped they would remain within the context of urging and pressuring Israel into implementing international resolutions and complying with the peace principles drafted in Madrid.
