Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bloemfontein Jan 15 SAPA -- A bomb has exploded at the African National Congress [ANC] offices in Harrismith in the Orange Free State, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Saturday. A police spokesman said the ANC office in the Omega Building was the apparent target but no one was injured in the explosion. Extensive damage was caused to the building.
