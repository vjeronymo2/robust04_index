February 15, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

GOLDEN WEST, FULLERTON WOMEN PLAY FOR TITLE

The Orange Empire Conference women's basketball title will be decided tonight when Fullerton College takes a 14-game winning streak into its 7:30 game against visiting Golden West. Fullerton (23-1, 7-0) already has clinched a share of the title.

Golden West (12-15, 6-1), which had to forfeit 11 nonconference games this season for using an ineligible player, has lost to Fullerton twice.
