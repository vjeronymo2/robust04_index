May 25, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

'DAY WITHOUT CARS' IGNORED IN MADRID

Motorists in traffic-congested Madrid ignored calls for a "day without cars" Thursday, snarling the Spanish capital with traffic jams as usual.

Police said traffic decreased by only 3%.

Mayor Agustin Rodriguez Sahagun promised to leave his car at home and use public transit as an example to commuters to keep cars for weekends.
