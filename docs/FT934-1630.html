931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Brunner lifts net asset value by 27% Brunner Investment Trust reported a net asset value, after deducting prior charges at par, of 256.74p per share at November 30. The figure represented a year-on-year advance of 27 per cent. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 3.9m (Pounds 3.83m), for earnings of 6.1p (5.99p). A recommended final dividend of 2.85p lifts the total to 5.25p (5p).
