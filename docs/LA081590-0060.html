August 15, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

WOMAN'S REMAINS FOUND IN FOREST

The partially clothed body of a woman was found Tuesday morning in the unincorporated Acton area of Angeles National Forest, Los Angeles County sheriff's deputies said.

A fire suppression crew from the Little Tujunga Ranger Station found the body six miles south of Soledad Canyon Road in Aliso Canyon, said Deputy George Ducoulombier.

The woman's age, description and cause of death could not be determined. The coroner's office estimated the woman had been dead for about a month.
