BFN [Text] Roads and Communications Minister Andi Meister, Interior Minister Heiki Arike, and Population Minister Peeter Olesk have stated that they will continue work in the government on the basis of the [1992] coalition agreement. These ERSP [Estonian National Independence Party] ministers said in a statement which they sent to the radio that they understand the need to prevent a power vacuum at a critical time for the state of Estonia. They made this decision taking into account the positions expressed at the joint meeting of the ERSP board and its Riigikogu faction.
