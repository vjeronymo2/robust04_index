920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / Polytechnic staff vote for strikes MORE than 11,500 white-collar staff in polytechnics and further-education colleges have voted to strike after rejecting a 4.3 per cent pay offer. Nalgo, the public-sector union, said a one-day strike was planned for August 20 - the date A-level results are announced. Two-day and three-day strikes would follow.
