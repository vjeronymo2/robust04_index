Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] An tremendous explosion took place at a firecracker plant in (Nanwangzhuang) Village of Longyao County on 29 January. The provincial and city departments concerned have attached great importance to this. In the past two days, several units, including the provincial education commission and the provincial public security department, and leaders of Xingtai city, respectively went to the scene of the accident to help deal with the aftermath of the accident. Longyao County has allocated over 80,000 yuan to save the wounded. It is reported that (Wang Zongkun), director of the plant; and (Wang Rongen) and (Zhang Haibing), primary school teachers who had forced pupils to work at the plant, have been arrested for investigations by the security organ. As of 2100 on 31 January when station reporter (Yang Jingzhou) released this report, over 30 pupils were saved from death by all-out rescue efforts, but the majority of them were unable to speak and were still unconsciousness.
