940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / Investor payouts Arbitration awards to investors after poor advice from independent financial advisers amounted to Pounds 1.8m in 1993, some Pounds 500,000 more than in 1992, according to figures produced yesterday by Fimbra, the self-regulating organisation for IFAs. Fimbra has ended just over 1,000 firms' authorisations since 1988.
