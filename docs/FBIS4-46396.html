BFN [Text] With the arrest of a most wanted Kashmiri militant, Mushtaq Ahmad, and his two associates, the Delhi police has foiled their plans to assassinate prominent leaders and to spread terrorism in metropolitan towns of the country. Giving details, the additional commissioner of police, Mr. R.S. Gupta, told newsmen that preliminary interrogations revealed a conspiracy by Hizbul Mojahedin to spread terrorism by using fundamentalist Muslims.
