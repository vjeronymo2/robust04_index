930705 FT 05 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The D-Mark maintained its position in the exchange rate mechanism's grid despite last week's interest rate cuts, which were led by a larger-than-expected easing by the Bundesbank. At the top of the grid, the peseta and the escudo were competing for first place. The remaining rankings were unchanged. Germany faces more pressure to ease rates, Page 4; Currencies, Page 25
