BFN [Text] Colombian Ambassador to Cuba Ricardo Santamaria has said that, over the past few hours, important events have taken place in the development of efforts by Colombia and Cuba to expand their economic and friendly ties. Among these, he included the visit of the Colombian vice minister for economic development with a delegation of businessmen, and the signing of a document by the civil aviation boards of the two countries agreeing to the prompt reestablishment of regular flights between Colombia and Cuba.
