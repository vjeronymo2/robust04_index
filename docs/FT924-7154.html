921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / Essex Water to start metering ESSEX WATER yesterday became the second water company to introduce compulsory metering for households. Essex, one of the 23 privately-owned water-supply companies in England and Wales, will start installing meters in 3,500 homes near Chelmsford in April. Last week Anglian Water announced that it would introduce compulsory metering to all its customers over the next 10 years.
