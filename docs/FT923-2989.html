920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Taking research in your Stride - In A Nutshell Organisations in areas of industrial decline can meet up to half of their research and development costs under an Pounds 11.5m European Community programme known as Science and Technology for Regional Innovation and Development in Europe (Stride). Grants are available for projects which: encourage small and medium-sized businesses to make use of the services and skills of institutions of higher education and other research centres; expand local centres set up to support innovation and technology transfer; promote regional R&D activity; and help education and training activities intended to promote the other three areas. Small firms pursuing their own R&D programmes are not eligible. Contact regional DTI offices and the Welsh and Scottish Offices.
