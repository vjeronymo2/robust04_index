BFN [NEGA report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Nikolay Travkin's New Appointment"] [Text] According to trustworthy information, the question of appointing Nikolay Travkin, leader of the Democratic Party of Russia, as Russian Government representative in the State Duma is now being decided. In this connection Nikolay Travkin obviously would have to retire as chairman of the Democratic party faction in the lower house of the Federal Assembly.
