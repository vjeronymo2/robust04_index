930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / UK Company News: United Drug rights to raise IPounds 4.5m United Drug, the Irish pharmaceutical and consumer products group, is to raise IPounds 4.47m (Pounds 4.4m) net, by way of a 1-for-5 rights issue of 2.73m new ordinary shares at 173p each. The proceeds will partly be used to provide additional working capital and reduce group borrowings.
