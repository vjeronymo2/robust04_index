*** "How to Stop the One You Love from Drinking and Using Drugs"

Paramount. $24.95. Information: (800) 972-5858.

"How to" is a comprehensive and well-organized 55-minute "intervention" guide for family and friends of alcoholics, hosted by Mariette Hartley and based on a book by Mary Ellen Pinkham. Much of the information is presented in dramatized form. JON MATSUMOTO
