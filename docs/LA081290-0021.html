August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GAY-BASHING IN THE RECORD INDUSTRY

Concerning the record industry's simplistic and rather naive blanket defense of some of the crap it releases under "free speech" and "First Amendment" rights: I wish I could make millions at my job and still be able to claim immunity from liability.

Even Kinson now sees his "humor" was dangerous. When will Warners get the message?

JIM ROSS

Los Angeles
