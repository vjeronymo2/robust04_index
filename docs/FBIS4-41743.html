BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on setting up a department for the protection of the Constitutional Court under the Federal Counterintelligence Service Monday. The presidential press office said the decree had been issued in accordance with the Russian law on the protection of top state bodies and officials. The department will coordinate its activities with other federal protection, counterintelligence and interior and state security bodies as well as the administration and leaders of enterprises regardless of their form of property. The decree came into force once signed.
