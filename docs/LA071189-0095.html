July 11, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TWO PALESTINIAN TEENS ARE REPORTED KILLED

Two Palestinian teen-agers were shot and killed by Israeli troops Monday in the occupied West Bank and Gaza Strip, and two others died of wounds suffered in earlier clashes, the army and Arab reports said.

Rami Mustafa Tarisi, 15, was fatally shot in the chest in a stone-throwing clash in the Bureij refugee camp in the Gaza Strip, according to Arab reports. The army said it was checking.

In the West Bank town of Ramallah, 17-year-old Yasser abu Ghosh was fatally shot in the head and back by Israeli security forces.
