Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Preparations are under way to establish a unified national front to confront the Muslim Brotherhood in all trade unions. The front will include all the political forces opposing the Muslim Brotherhood. Following the proclamation of the front, the participants will draw up its charter and by-laws. So far, the national committee and society of the "Young Lawyers," the "Young Doctors Front," "Egypt's Pharmacists Front," and the "Nationalist Engineers" have agreed to participate in the front. The front's provisional general secretariat represented by Dr. Ahmad 'Awf, engineer Raja'i 'Abd-al-Malik, and attorney Ahmad Jum'ah will contact other unions' forces for membership purposes.
