940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / US-N Korean talks to go on The US and North Korea decided yesterday to continue talks on Pyongyang's nuclear programme at expert level, Frances Williams reports from Geneva. The full delegations meet again today or tomorrow. The experts are discussing what to do about the spent fuel rods decaying at the Yongbyon nuclear complex, and North Korea's offer to freeze its graphite-moderated reactor programme in return for light water reactor technology.
