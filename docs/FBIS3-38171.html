Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Jan 30 (A.A) -- An explosion at the Democracy Party office of Mamak District of Ankara Province early Sunday [30 January] caused heavy damage, while no injuries were reported. There were no immediate claims of responsibility for the attack which occurred in the outskirts of the capital. On 20 January another office of the party also in Ankara was bombed. One person was injured in that attack.
