BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister Peter Boross has visited Szekesfehervar. According to our correspondent, the prime minister said NATO AWACS planes have been and do remain in Hungarian airspace; they were here during the NATO air strike on Gorazde, too. However, he said, Hungary had received assurances that the planes are here without target guiding tasks [tuezvezerlesi feladatok] and that they carry out only airspace patrolling duties.
