BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 31, BNS -- The Lithuanian parliament on Thursday [31 March] ratified an agreement between the governments of Lithuania and Latvia on cooperation in the field of social security. The accord was signed in Vilnius last December by Lithuanian Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius and his Latvian counterpart Valdis Birkavs. The agreement will come into effect when the two countries exchange ratification documents. The agreement provides for equal social security rights to citizens of both countries who permanently live or have a labor contract in the other country.
