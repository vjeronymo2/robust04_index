941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Business failures show increase Business failures rose in November for the second month in succession, said the accountancy firm Touche Ross. It said professional administrators were appointed at 185 troubled businesses last month compared with 169 in October and 132 in September. But Mr Ralph Preece, a partner at Touche Ross, said the inclusion of a number of groups of companies in November's figures, each of which was counted separately, boosted the overall tally and gave a 'bleaker impression than is perhaps the case'.
