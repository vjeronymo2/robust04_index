August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COPASETIC

I am an original Copa girl collaborating with Kris B. Milton on a book about the famous Copacabana and early nightclub history.

I would like to hear from and interview any artist who ever appeared at the Copacabana at 10 E. 60th St., New York City, worked at the Copa, or attended the Copa from 1940 to 1972. All clippings and anecdotes will be appreciated.

TONI CARROLL

353 W. 56th St.

New York, N.Y. 10019
