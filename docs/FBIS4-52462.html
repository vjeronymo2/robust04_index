BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 11 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet left Hanoi for Myanmar [Burma] this morning on an official visit to Myanmar as guest of Senior General Than Shwe, Burmese Prime Minister. He is accompanied by Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, Minister of Culture and Information Tran Hoan, Forestry Minister Tran Quang Ha, and other high-ranking government officials. This is the first visit to Myanmar by a Vietnamese prime minister. 110649
