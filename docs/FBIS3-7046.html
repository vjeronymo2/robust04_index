Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, received a gift from the high-level delegation of the Defence Ministry of the Royal Government of Cambodia on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u by Cambodian Co-Ministers of Defence Tie Banh and Tie Chamrat.
