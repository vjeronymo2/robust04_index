920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / Smog shuts Chile factories OFFICIALS in the Chilean capital yesterday ordered five factories to shut down, and two out of every five vehicles off the road for the rest of the week, to fight smog, Reuter reports from Santiago. Levels of pollution which could affect the health of children, old people, the pregnant and the sick were registered, said a pollution commission. It closed three furnaces, a cement works and a metal foundry.
