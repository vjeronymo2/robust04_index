931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports landed good general demand. Useful liquoring Assams met good competition at fully firm rates but poorer sorts were often neglected. Best and brighter medium East Africans were irregularly dearer, others were fully firm. Brighter Ceylons were dearer but plainer sorts were barely steady. Offshore good demand at firm to dearer rates. Quotations: quality 210p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 110p/kg, low medium 98p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 261 for a Rwanda pf. 1.
