941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Richards loss at Pounds 4.9m after provisions The closure of its carpet factory in Northern Ireland and its knitwear activities in Ayrshire resulted in Richards, the Aberdeen-based textile company, reporting annual pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.92m, against Pounds 71,000. The company had warned of a 'significant write-off' when it announced the carpet closure in August. However, the shares still lost 5p yesterday to close at 38p. There were provisions and losses on the closures of Pounds 3.97m. Turnover for the year to September 30 was Pounds 75.1m (Pounds 67.8m) with Pounds 15.7m (Pounds 17.6m) from discontinued activities. Operating losses were Pounds 465,000 (Pounds 147,000) with losses on discontinued activities of Pounds 1.28m (Pounds 1.34m). Continuing operations showed a 54 per cent increase in operating profits. Losses per share were 17.31p (earnings 0.54p) and the proposed final dividend is 0.93p for a total of 2p (3p).
