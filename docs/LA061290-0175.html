June 12, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

TAJ MAHAL FALLS BELOW $1-MILLION-A-DAY GOAL

The Taj Mahal casino in Atlantic City last week failed for the first time to make a projected $1 million a day -- revenues vital to the restructuring of Donald Trump's real estate empire, a published report said today.

Trump's $1-billion Taj took in $6.6 million last week, or $943,000 a day -- well below what analysts predicted during the resort's peak season, the Daily News reported.
