Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] Food imports from the Czech Republic have added at least 1 billion korunas to Slovakia's deficit in the balance of payments with the Czech Republic. According to Milan Filo, director of the Department of Trade at the Slovak Ministry of Agriculture, such imports are the result of the surviving federal structure of agriculture, which concentrates final food production in the Czech Republic. Among the main produce still imported by Slovakia are poultry, dairy and bakery products, margarine, spirits, and tobacco.
