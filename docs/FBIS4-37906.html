BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jun (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has received a message from his Saudi counterpart, Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, concerning current regional affairs. 'Abd-al-Rahman Mansuri, under secretary at the Saudi Foreign Ministry, delivered the message to Minister Musa today. Commenting on Sanaa's announcement of a cease-fire in Yemen, Mansuri said: We hope that the cease-fire in Yemen will hold. Saudi Arabia wants to see the internecine bloodletting end.
