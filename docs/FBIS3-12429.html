Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [ITIM report] [Text] Along with the announcement of PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat, the main Palestinian dailies -- AL-QUDS and AL-NAHAR -- yesterday carried a message of condolences from Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and the Government of Israel. The message, which was published on the front page, said that Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and the cabinet members share in the mourning of the Palestinian people and express their sorrow at the killing of innocent people.
