Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met today with North Korean Education Minister Cho Ae-yong [name and title as heard]. The latter stated that they discussed issues of mutual interest and ways to bolster bilateral relations. He said that the Korean delegation was honored when the president received them. He added that in light of the talks the Korean delegation has held relations between the two countries will progress and develop during the coming stage. The Korean education minister said his country appreciates the efforts Egypt is exerting to resolve the Middle East problem justly and fairly. He declared his country's condemnation of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre.
