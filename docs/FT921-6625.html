920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / More states sign Helsinki accord Five leaders of former Soviet republics signed the Helsinki Final Act yesterday, promising to uphold its human rights and security principles, Reuter reports from Helsinki. Belarus, Moldova, Tajikistan, Ukraine and Uzbekistan signed the accords of the 48-member Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe in the hall, where 35 nations agreed the original document in 1975.
