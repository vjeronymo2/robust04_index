November 1, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

INJURY MAY SIDELINE ARREOLA FOR REGIONAL

Darcy Arreola of Cal State Northridge injured her left foot Monday and is listed as questionable for the National Collegiate Athletic Assn. Division II West regional cross-country championships in McKinleyville, Calif., on Saturday.

"She was just running along and something gave out in her foot," Northridge Coach Don Strametz said.
