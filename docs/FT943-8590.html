940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares in Johannesburg closed just off the day's lows in a session which saw an almost complete lack of foreign interest. De Beers, down R4.25 or 3.7 per cent at R112.25, suffered from profit-taking ahead of today's half-year results. The overall index declined 21 to 5,790, industrials gained 1 at 6,581 and the golds index lost 27 at 2,127.
