BFN [Text] The Party of People's Accord has officially announced that it is going to register its Chairman Henadz Karpenka as a candidate for president. Henadz Karpenka is a deputy to the Supreme Soviet, the mayor of Molodechno (Minsk oblast), and a corresponding member of the Belarusian Academy of Sciences. His electoral team is headed by well-known Belarusian playwright Alyaksey Dudaraw. It is planned to collect no less than 100,000 signatures in support of Karpenka's candidacy.
