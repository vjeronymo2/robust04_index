BFN [INTERFAX report: "Post-Check Privatization Draft Program Will Be Submitted to President Friday"] [Text] A draft state program of post-check [voucher] privatization setting out the rules for denationalization of state property after 1 July will probably be up for President Boris Yeltsin's signature Friday. This has been disclosed by Dmitriy Vasilyev, deputy chairman of the Russian Federation State Committee for the Management of State Property. According to him, an edict setting the state program in motion is supposed to be signed by Boris Yeltsin early next week. Russian Federation Vice Premier Anatoliy Chubays stated earlier that some 20 percent of state property is supposed to be privatized for money in the post-check period. By July 1994 70 percent of Russia's property will be in the private sector; 10 percent of state enterprises are not subject to privatization.
