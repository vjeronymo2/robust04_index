Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Irina Shchogoleva] [Text] Sochi March 7 TASS -- Georgian troops continue to concentrate along the border with Abkhazia that passes down the Inguri river, a well-informed source told ITAR-TASS. According to the source, Georgia is relocating terrorist groups from the Gali District and Kodori Gorge, but the situation on the border is relative stable with the exception of sporadic gunning of Abkhaz positions.
