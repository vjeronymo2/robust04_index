BFN [Unattributed report: "Official"] [Text] By edicts of the Russian Federation president: Vladimir Filippovich Shumeyko has been relieved of his duties as chairman of the Russian organizational committee for preparing and implementing measures in connection with the celebration of memorable dates of the 1941-1945 Great Patriotic War because of his election as chairman of the Federal Assembly Federation Council. Viktor Stepanovich Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government, has been elected chairman of this organizational committee. Ivan Petrovich Sklyarov has been appointed head of the Nizhniy Novgorod city administration for the period through the election of the city's head of local self-management.
