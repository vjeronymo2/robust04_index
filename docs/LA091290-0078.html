September 12, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

MAN GIVES NOTE TO TELLER, ROBS BANK

A man robbed a Simi Valley bank of several thousand dollars Tuesday afternoon using a demand note and the threat of a weapon, authorities said.

Fidelity Federal Bank, 5775 Los Angeles Ave., reported the robbery about 1:45 p.m., Simi Valley Police Lt. Neal Rein said.

Witnesses said the man is about 25 years old, 6 feet, 2 inches tall and has blond hair, said Gary Auer, a special agent with the FBI's Ventura division. KIRSTEN LEE SWARTZ
