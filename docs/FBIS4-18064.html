BFN [Edict No. 836 of the Russian Federation president "On the Introduction of a State of Emergency on Part of the Territory of the Republic of North Ossetia," dated the Kremlin, Moscow, 27 April 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] In accordance with Article 88 of the Russian Federation Constitution, the RSFSR law "On States of Emergency," and the Russian Federation law "On Security," I decree: 1. A state of emergency is to be introduced on the territories of Prigorodnyy Rayon (the population centers of Oktyabrskoye, Kambileyevskoye, and Sunzha) and the city of Vladikavkaz ("Sputnik" military camp) in the Republic of North Ossetia from 1400 hours on 27 April 1994 through 1400 hours on 31 May 1994. 2. Points 3-8 of the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 657 of 4 April 1994 "On the Introduction of a State of Emergency on Part of the Territories of the Republic of North Ossetia and the Ingush Republic" are to be extended to the territory of the population centers and the military camp listed in Point 1 of this edict. 3. This edict comes into force the moment it is signed. In accordance with Article 102 of the Russian Federation Constitution, this edict is to be sent to the Federal Assembly Federation Council for approval. [Signed] B. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation The Kremlin, Moscow, 27 April 1994
