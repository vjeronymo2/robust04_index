940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / Labour MP returns from Somalia Tony Worthington, Labour MP for Clydebank and Milngavie, faces the sack as a foreign affairs spokesman today after an unauthorised trip to Somalia last week. The MP and Mark Robinson, Tory MP for Somerton and Frome, were taken hostage by guerrillas. Both escaped unharmed. Mr Worthington arrived at Heathrow yesterday with aid worker Jeff Chinnock.
