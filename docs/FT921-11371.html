920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Ewart declines to Pounds 104,000 Ewart, the Belfast-based property company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 104,000 for the six months to October 31, compared with Pounds 254,000. Turnover increased slightly from Pounds 1.74m to Pounds 1.79m. Earnings per share were 0.44p (0.92p), after a lower tax charge of Pounds 21,000 (Pounds 84,000). The interim dividend is halved to 0.25p.
