Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Denis Perkin and Vladimir Taranov] [Excerpt] Moscow, 4 Feb -- The Russian Foreign Ministry welcomes the steps taken by the Ukrainian parliament, which ratified two of six points in the START I treaty. An official from the Russian Foreign Ministry stated this in a conversation with an ITAR-TASS correspondent today. In his opinion, this was made possible by an accord reached during the January trilateral summit between delegations from Russia, the United States, and Ukraine in Moscow. A mechanism for implementing Ukraine's decision on the withdrawal of nuclear warheads from its territory was drawn up at the meeting. [passage omitted]
