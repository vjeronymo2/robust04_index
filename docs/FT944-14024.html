941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / International Company News: Environmental Investment Company ENVIRONMENTAL INVESTMENT Company had a net asset value per share of Dollars 10.24 (640p) at June 30 against Dollars 10.65 at December 31. Net losses for the half year were Dollars 1.2m (Dollars 2.21m profits), equivalent to losses per share of 30.12 cents (55.23 cents earnings).
