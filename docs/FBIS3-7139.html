Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Criminal Act Bringing Situation to Brink of War" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists staged a provocative electronic aerial warfare exercise directed against the North of Korea in the air above the Oeyon islet, South Korea, in the daytime of March 23 with the mobilization of more than 200 warplanes of various types, according to military sources. They also staged a war exercise in the air above Kapyong, Uijongbu, Yoju, Kunwi and Tanyang that night till early next morning with sorties of more than 300 fighter planes of various types. On the 23rd, an RC-135 strategic reconnaissance plane flew over the Military Demarcation Line area for the purpose of aerial photographing and electronic reconnaissance on the strategic interior and all other areas of the North. Formations of helicopter gunships belonging to the South Korean puppet army which were sent to support the ground attack operation in Paju County staged a missile strike exercise, while formations of transport helicopters flew over Yonchon County and Chorwon County, South Korean Kangwon Province, for an airlifting operation. Meanwhile, formations of large transport planes carrying many aggression troops and death tools flew to the U.S. Airforce bases in Osan and Kunsan from their overseas bases. The airlifted combat equipment was deployed in operational areas. The war gambles of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets which are getting more reckless with each passing day are driving the situation of the Korean peninsula to the brink of war.
