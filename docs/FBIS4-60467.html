BFN [Text] Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto has left Islamabad for Kathmandu on a three-day official visit to Nepal. She will hold talks with Nepalese leaders on bilateral relations and regional and international issues. The talks will also cover matters relating to the South Asian Association For Regional Cooperation. The prime minister is accompanied by Foreign Minister Asif Ahmad Ali; Water and Power Minister Malik Ghulam Musatafa Khar; Makhdum Shahabuddin, minister of state for finance; Begum Shahnaz Wazir Ali, special assistant to the prime minister; Balochistan Chief Minister Zulfiqar Ali Magsi; and Balochistan Assembly Speaker Abdul Wahid Baloch.
