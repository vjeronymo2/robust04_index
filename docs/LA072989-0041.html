July 29, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CONEJO VALLEY WINS SENIOR DIVISION TITLE

Jeff Naster pitched a complete game and Ryan Kritcher and Jeff Olin collected two hits each Friday as Conejo Valley defeated Northridge National, 11-4, to win the Senior Division Little League Section 1 title.

Conejo Valley (8-1) begins play in the eight-team state championships today in Hacienda Heights. Friday's win over Northridge National was Conejo Valley's second in as many days.
