Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The European Union's [EU] Commissioner for Foreign Affairs, Hans van den Broek, arrived in Cyprus this evening for talks with the government. The meetings will deal with Cyprus' entry into the Union, developments on the Cyprus problem, and the settlement of the fourth Cyprus-EU financial protocol. Van den Broek was accompanied by the EU observer on Cyprus, Serge Abou. The two men will be honored at a dinner given by President Glavkos Kliridhis tonight. Tomorrow, they will meet with the members of the Foreign Ministry and with Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis. They will then have talks with President Kliridhis, Gustave Feissel [the UN secretary general's alternate special representative in Cyprus], and the ambassadors of the 12 EU member states before leaving tomorrow afternoon.
