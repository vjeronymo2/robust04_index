October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: HUNTINGTON BEACH;

CANDIDATES TO SPEAK IN FORUMS AT COLLEGE

Three forums for state Assembly and Board of Supervisors candidates will be held this week at Golden West College.

Democratic state Assembly candidate Luanne Pryor will speak today in the first of the forums being held at the college.

Republican candidate Thomas J. Mays, the mayor of Huntington Beach, will speak on Wednesday.

Pryor and Mays are seeking the Assembly seat in the 58th District, which includes several Orange County coastal cities and part of Long Beach.

A different format is planned for Thursday's forum, which includes 1st District Supervisor Harriett M. Wieder and challenger Joy L. Neugebauer.

Wieder and Neugebauer will engage in a debate to be moderated by Thomas Chambers, a Golden West professor of political science and English. The Wieder-Neugebauer debate will conclude with a question-and-answer session.

The forums begin at 12:30 p.m. at the college's student center.JOHN PENNER
