BFN [By reporterd Li Shengjiang (2621 3932 3068)] [Text] Urumqi, 25 Apr (XINHUA) -- Australian Governor General William Hayden wound up a five-day visit to Xinjiang and left Urumqi for Xiamen by special plane this morning. Hayden told reporters at the airport that the visit to Xinjiang has deepened his impressions of China's remarkable economic achievements. Accompanied by Yu Xiaosong, head of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice minister in charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission, Hayden and his retinue arrived in Urumqi from Beijing on 21 April. While in Xinjiang, the Australian Governor General visited Urumqi, Kashi, and Turpan to obtain firsthand information about Xinjiang's economic development, ethnic habits and customs, and economic, trade, and scientific and technical exchange and cooperation with Australia. The Australian visitors were very much impressed with their visit to Xinjiang. Abulaiti Abudurexiti, chairman of Xinjiang Uigur Autonomous Region, met and feted Hayden and his retinue, and extended a warm welcome to the Australian visitors.
