Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A 40-year-old Jewish man was shot this morning at Damascus Gate in Jerusalem's Old City. He is in critical condition. The man, a guard at an East Jerusalem office, was walking to work when an Arab resident snatched his revolver near Damascus Gate. The Arab then shot him in the head. The wounded man was rushed to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital. Our police affairs correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that large police forces have closed off the area and are searching for the assailant.
