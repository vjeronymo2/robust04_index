Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed RAY: "Claes `Formally Denies' Having Met With Mr. Otetti"; as released by Brussels BELGA database] [Text] Brussels, 28 Jan (BELGA) -- Foreign Minister Claes "formally denies" having met with Agusta Manager Otetti on the second Sunday in January 1989. This is the statement issued to BELGA by a spokesman for the Claes cabinet.
