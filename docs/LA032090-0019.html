March 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Phillips Recovering: Actor Lou Diamond Phillips is in satisfactory condition in an Albuquerque, N.M., hospital, recovering from injuries suffered when his horse spooked and dragged him for approximately 100 yards on the set of "Young Guns II." Phillips was injured Friday and transported by the helicopter from the set near Santa Fe to the hospital. Phillips, 28, who spent about four hours in surgery Friday night, suffered a shattered elbow and a knee injury. SHAUNA SNOW
