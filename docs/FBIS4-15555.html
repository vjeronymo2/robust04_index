BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow April 11 TASS -- Fighters of the Russian airforce forced to land an Israeli "Gulfstream-159" plane flying from Tbilisi, Georgia, to Tel Aviv after it had violated the rules of crossing the Russian border and using the national airspace, spokesman of the Russian Air Defence Leonid Shirobokov told TASS on Monday. The incident occurred on Saturday when the plane entered the Russian airspace to the south-east of the city of Mineralnye Vody without a preliminary request and permission from authorities. The plane ignored the demands of Russian authorities to return back to the airport of departure and continued the flight. Russian airforce fighters then intercepted the plane and forced it to land in Adler, the airport of Sochi.
