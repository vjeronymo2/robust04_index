DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 2088] International Telecommunications Advisory Committee; Telecommunications Standardization Sector (ITAC&hyph;T) Group and Study Group A; Meeting Notice The Department of State announces that the United States International Telecommunications Advisory Committee (ITAC), Telecommunications Standardization Sector (ITAC&hyph;T) Study Group A will meet November 9, 1994, in room 5533, 9:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m., and the Telecommunications Standardization Sector (ITAC&hyph;T) Group, December 8, 1994, in room 5533 from 9:30 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. at the Department of State, 2201 ``C'' Street NW., Washington, DC 20520. The agenda for Study Group A will include a debrief of the September-October ITU&hyph;T Study Group 1 meeting; continue preparations for the Geneva, November 29-December 9, ITU&hyph;T Study Group 2 meeting; continue preparations for the Geneva, December 12&hyph;15, 1994, ITU&hyph;T Study Group 3 meeting; and discuss other issues concerning ITAC&hyph;T Study Group A. The agenda for the December Telecommunications Standardization Sector (ITAC&hyph;T_Group meeting (formerly the CCITT USNC) will include (1) a debrief of the Kyoto Plenipotentiary Conference of September 19-October 14, as it may affect the work of the ITU's Telecommunications Standardization Sector, with particular interest on priorities, and strategic policy and planning issues; (2) discussions covering any contributions for the January 23&hyph;27, 1995, Geneva meeting of the Telecommunications Standardization Advisory Committee (TSAG); and (3) a debrief of the Ottawa CITEL (InterAmerican Telecommunications Commission) meetings, and discussions relating to the CITEL's Permanent Consultative Committee I (PCC&hyph;I) which will be meeting in Honduras in February 1995. Members of the General Public may attend the meetings and join in the discussions, subject to the instructions of the chair. Admittance of public members will be limited to the seating available. In this regard, entrance to the Department of State is controlled. If you are not presently named on the mailing list of the Telecommunications Standardization Sector Group (formerly USNC) or Study Group A, and wish to attend please call 202&hyph;647&hyph;0201, no later than 5 days before the meeting. Enter from the C Street Main Lobby. A picture ID will be required for admittance. Dated: September 22, 1994. Earl S. Barbely, Chairman, U.S. ITAC for ITU-Telecommunication Standardization Sector.
