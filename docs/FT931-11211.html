930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICO extended its run of weakness on higher interest rates, fears of equity oversupply and worries about delays in the approval of the NAFTA free trade agreement. The key IPC index fell another 16.59 points or 1.02 per cent to 1,607.56 by the close following Tuesday's 2.4 per cent decline AT the end of 1992 the IPC stood at 1,759.4.
