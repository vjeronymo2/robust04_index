940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Aegis increases Spanish stake Aegis Group subsidiary Carat Group, is paying Pta750m (Pounds 3.66m) cash to acquire a further 30 per cent in its Spanish offshoot, Publintegral from Propiedades y Filiales. At present, the group's Spanish companies are being reorganised and the Spanish holding company, Carat Espana, is merging into Publintegral.
