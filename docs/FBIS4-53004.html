BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received a gift from Robert G. Mugabe, president of the Republic of Zimbabwe. The gift was handed to Choe Kwang, chief of the general staff of the Korean People's Army, by Dumiso Dabengwa, minister of home affairs of the Republic of Zimbabwe, visiting Korea.
