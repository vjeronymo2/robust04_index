920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / Plan to share technologies AN Pounds 18m technology-transfer scheme was announced by Mr Tim Sainsbury, industry minister. The government expects its Pounds 18m contribution to the five-year Carrier Technology Programme to be matched by industry, making a total of Pounds 36m available. Mr Sainsbury said the UK's 'extensive network of research and technology organisations will act as a bridge to carry technologies from one sector to another'.
