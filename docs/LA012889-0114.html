January 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THEY FULLY ENJOYED SEINFELD SHOW

In response to Lawrence Christon's review of Jerry Seinfeld's performance ("Laughing on Empty," Calendar, Jan. 17), we feel he was very unfair to this talented comedian, not just in the review of the show but also in the attack on Seinfeld's person -- "the New York face, thin as a chisel." (We are not from New York.)

The next time Mr. Seinfeld appears in Orange County, perhaps this critic could take a sense of humor instead of pen and relax and enjoy the show.

Mr. & Mrs. RUSSELL G. GORDON

Huntington Beach
