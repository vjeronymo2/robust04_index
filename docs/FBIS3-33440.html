Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Edgar Gallego Rigo from Medellin; from the "Caracol 0600-0900" newscast] [Text] Medellin authorities are currently investigating the motives and persons responsible for the explosion of 10 kg of dynamite early this morning in the central part of Bello, northern Medellin. The explosion injured two policemen and caused considerable property damage to a local store, the police station, the municipal inspection department, and another municipal building. It also caused damage to three floors of the old municipal palace, 10 local commercial stores, and a fleet of nine official [word indistinct]. The fire department is currently preparing an assessment of the damage caused by this terrorist attack, which has not yet been attributed to any group.
