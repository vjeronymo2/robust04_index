August 11, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

TALLEY TO SIGN BUFFALO CONTRACT

The Buffalo Bills reached a tentative contract agreement with linebacker Darryl Talley, the last of five starters from the 1988 season who were holdouts this year.

Talley was expected to report to camp either today or Saturday and sign a three-year deal that will increase his base salary from $315,000 to $500,000.

Another veteran expected to report today was cornerback Derrick Burroughs, who agreed to a three-year deal worth an average of $400,000 per season.
