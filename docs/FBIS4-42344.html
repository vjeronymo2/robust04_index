BFN [Text] Ankara, June 1 (A.A.) -- Foreign Ministry Spokesman Ferhat Ataman told a press conference today that the Greek ambassador to Turkey has denied claims that Greece has relations with the separatist terrorist organization, PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan], in line with a recent Athens statement. Ataman said that the Greek ambassador met with the ministry's Deputy Under Secretary Unal Unsal on Tuesday [31 May]. During the meeting the Greek ambassador replied to accusations in the same manner as his government. Two weeks ago, the Foreign Ministry summoned the Greek ambassador and asked for information from Athens on confessions made by a number of PKK members. The terrorists, during their interrogation said that they received training in Greece. Athens denied the terrorists' statements.
