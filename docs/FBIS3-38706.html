Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Giovanni Spadolini said: I think that the mission in Somalia must absolutely be brought to an end. Spadolini, who is in Cote d'Ivoire for Houphouet-Boigny's funeral, told journalists that Italy can only try and safeguard its security and interests while supporting a UN solution. He added: We did not particularly want to go there, we made great efforts, and more than 10 of our people were killed.
