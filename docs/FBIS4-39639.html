BFN [Anatoliy Stasovskiy report: "Tajikistan Fights Terror"] [Text] On 7 June Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, commander in chief of Russian Federation Border Troops, received from Tajikistan a telegram from Imamali Rakhmonov, chairman of the republic's National Assembly. In it the head of Tajikistan expressed his sincere condolences in connection with the tragic deaths of Russian border guards -- Lieutenant Colonels Borisenkov and Leontikov. "The criminals," the telegram says, "who have encroached on a most blessed thing -- the lives of Russia's sons who, answering the call of duty and conscience, were protecting the Tajik soil that had become their own -- will not go unpunished. Some of them are already in the hands of the law, the others will be found and punished with all the severity of the law...." Meanwhile, in Tajikistan everything is being done to get control of the acute criminogenic situation. On 7 June 24-hour joint patrols of Tajik and Russian servicemen began. Areas where Russian servicemen and their families live have been put under special guard.
