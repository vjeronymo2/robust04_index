Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4 -- Vietnamese and American delegations held talks in Hanoi from Feb. 28-March 4 on issues relating to asset claim and the establishment of liaison office in each other's capitals. The talks were reported to be useful and constructive. The two sides agreed to continue to discuss the above-said issues. The venue and the time of the next talks will be decided later.
