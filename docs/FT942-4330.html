940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / Touche Ross HQ sold to Germans The UK headquarters of accountants Touche Ross has been sold by PosTel, the UK's largest pension fund, to Despa Fonds, a German fund, for Pounds 65.7m, reflecting an initial yield of 7.8 per cent. The acquisition of the 170,000 sq ft Hill House, in the City of London, is the first overseas purchase by Despa Fonds, which is one of the largest German investment funds.
