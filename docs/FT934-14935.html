931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / People: Other non-executive appointments Sir Michael Bishop, who has been chairman of Airlines of Britain Holdings, owners of British Midland, since 1978, has joined the board of Williams Holdings as a non-executive director. Brian McGowan, currently chief executive, has announced that he is retiring with effect from January 1 1994; Roger Carr, currently managing director, will take over as chief executive.
