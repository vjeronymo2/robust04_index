940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / British soldiers suffer Serbian harassment Bosnian Serbs disarmed and harassed a convoy of British soldiers in eastern Bosnia and forced the commanding officer to sign a confession of alleged wrongdoing, a UN spokesman said yesterday, Reuter reports from Sarajevo. Six British engineers had left Gorazde and were heading back to their base in central Bosnia on Thursday when they were stopped by Bosnian Serb army soldiers near Sokolac, 14 miles east of Sarajevo. The soldiers confiscated their weapons and ammunition along with their personal belongings and threatened them.
