Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Wolfsberg Jan 31 (CTK) -- The Czech Republic is a posttransformation country which "does not need assistance, but business," Premier Vaclav Klaus told the Swiss media today. He noted that the Czech Republic had a normal political system without any ethnic problems. The pace and dynamism of privatisation is more important than the current share -- about 60 percent of privatised companies in the Czech Republic's total production, Klaus said, adding that the share of the private sector was nil in the 1980's.
