920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Property Trust restructures Property Trust, the property investment and development company, is proposing a financial reconstruction and property sale to put the loss-making group on a sound footing. It also announced an interim loss of Pounds 6.32m following provisions on its investments. The company plans to consolidate 200 of its 1p shares into one Pounds 2 share to be reclassified as one 25p share and one deferred share of Pounds 1.75. This will be followed by a reduction in share capital and share premium account enabling dividends to be paid in future. It also plans to move up from the USM to a full listing. The Pounds 8m proceeds from the property sale will be used to repay two loans resulting in an interest saving of about Pounds 1m a year. Following the sale the company said the interest charge would be covered 1.8 times by rental income. In the six months to September 30 there was a pre-tax loss of Pounds 6.32m (Pounds 703,000) on turnover of Pounds 600,000 (Pounds 734,000). The operating loss was Pounds 189,000 plus the carrying values of development properties were written down by Pounds 1.59m and there was loss of Pounds 3.56m arising from the property sale.
