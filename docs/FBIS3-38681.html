Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry announces: On 6 March 1992, the FRG and Turkmenistan established diplomatic relations. On 23 November 1993, the day when the credentials were handed over, the FRG Embassy was opened in the capital city of Ashgabat in temporary quarters. Until further notice, the embassy can be reached at the following address: Head: Peter Schaller, charge d'affaires; Address: Yubileynaya Hotel, rooms 401, 411, 412, Tehran Street 6, 744012 Ashgabat; Mailing address: Posolstvo Federativnoy Respubliki Germaniya, Gostinitsa Yubileynaya, Komnata 412, Ulitsa Tehran, 6, 744012 Ashgabat; Telephone: (0 07 36 34) 51 21 44, 24 49 08, 24 49 11; Telex: (064) 116617 aaash sv [as published]. The embassy is headed by a charge d'affaires (charge d'affaires in line with Article 14, paragraph 1c WUeD), who is directly subordinate to the Foreign Ministry. The embassy's official sphere is Turkmenistan. During the period of establishment, the new embassy is not yet able to operate at full capacity. This applies in particular to issuing visas and other consular tasks.
