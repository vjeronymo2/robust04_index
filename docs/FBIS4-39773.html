BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow June 10 TASS -- The communist faction in the Russian Parliament's lower house has called for the abolition of presidency in Russia and the formation of a government of popular trust. The Communist Party of the Russian Federation and its faction in parliament circulated an official document in the State Duma entitled "on the three-year policy of destruction of the basis of people's life in Russia" which calls on all "truly patriotic and democratic forces to join the lawful struggle for radical changes in the policy of the Russian state and the withdrawal of the ruling regime from the political arena."
