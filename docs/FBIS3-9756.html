Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 25 (ATA) -- The Human Rights and Freedoms Defence Council in Prishtina protested on March 24 against the torturing of three former Albanian officers Ismet Veliqi, Avdi Behrami, and Azem Gasgi who have been imprisoned in Mitrovica. The protest which was signed by the chairman of the council, Adem Demaci, pointed out that according to reports, the prisoners are subjected to barbarous tortures, such as electric batons, chemicals and other means. The Journalists' Association of Kosova protested against the 60 days imprisonment by the Serbian Court in Prishtina of the reporter Shaip Beqiri, who has served sentences by the Serbian prisons several times before. According to the association, the latest imprisonment is made in the course of punishments and persecutions of Albanian reporters and creators in Kosova by Serbian authorities.
