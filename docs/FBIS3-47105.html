Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday [8 January], Samdech Chea Sim, acting head of state and chairman of the National Assembly, visited troops of the Khmer Royal Armed Forces [KRAF] in Division 1 in the Kirirom area and also visited the area's major tourist sites. Accompanying him on this trip were His Excellency General Tie Banh, co-defense minister; princes; excellencies; and many officials from the National Defense Ministry. Speaking on this occasion, Samdech Chea Sim most cordially and warmly enquired about life in the KRAF's Division 1, which is stationed in Kirirom. He further said that there are two tasks for Division 1: first, defend the Kirirom area and protect the local natural resources; and second, build bridges and roads coming into Kirirom. Samdech Chea Sim also called on the officers and men in charge of the entire area to continue further heightening their spirit of responsibility in the protection of the area's natural resources as well as in the speedy and timely construction of roads and bridges leading to the Kirirom area. He also appealed to Division 1 to strengthen and expand national unity with forces from all political parties in order to become the KRAF under the direct leadership of the Royal Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia. Samdech Chea Sim also distributed some gifts in the form of foodstuff to officers and men stationed in the Kirirom area.
