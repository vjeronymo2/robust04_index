Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 15 (KCNA) -- The Korean documentary film studio has released several documentary films recently. The film "Turn" shows imperishable feats performed by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in the ideological field by proclaiming the programme of modelling the whole society on the chuche idea on February 19, 1974, and bringing about a new turn in the party's ideological work. "Embraced in the Bosom of Great Love" shows Yi In-mo living a happy life with great glory, highly praised by people at home and abroad as the incarnation of faith and will, after returning home. Yi In-mo goes round significant places of the socialist homeland which made a signal development, keenly feeling the benevolence of the party and the leader, solicitude of the socialist system and the love of the revolutionary comrades. He says our leader, our party and our country were the best and he is now in an "Earthly Paradise." "Long Live the Great Rural Theses" and "Yongsan-ri Undergoes Changes" represent the looks of the countryside that has changed beyond recognition thanks to the great socialist rural theses and the happiness of the people. "Intimate Feelings of Kinship" vividly depicts the true appearance of Korean-style socialism in which all the people form a big harmonious family and help and lead each other forward. Among the newly released documentary films are also "Self-Reliance Is the Spirit of Korea," "Masters of Forests" and "Respected People" which introduce the noble spiritual world and beautiful traits of honest-minded people who have devoted their all to the country, the people, the society, the collective and the revolutionary comrades, doing what the party wants.
