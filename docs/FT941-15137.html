940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / Observer: Thais of regret Thai bankers have a nice line in cynical humour. Chulakorn Singhakowin, Bank of Asia president, was recently asked how financial institutions could help bridge the chasm between rich and poor in Thailand. 'As a banker basically you have very little opportunity to rip off the poor,' replied Chulakorn, 'You try to rip off the rich. All Thai banks are currently lending to people who don't need the money - classical banking.'
