BFN [Text] Tibor Pongracz, chairman of the board of directors of the State Assets Agency [AVU], has resigned. The state secretary for privatization is the fourth member of the board of directors to step down from his position. The 11-member body has thereby decreased in size to seven. Tibor Pongracz told NEPSZABADSAG that the prime minister accepted his resignation with effect from 1 July. The AVU's board of directors is still capable of making decisions because six members are sufficient for decisionmaking.
