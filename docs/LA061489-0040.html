June 14, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Singer-actress-dancer Mitzi Gaynor will hit the road this fall, starring in a national tour of the Tony Award-winning Lincoln Center production of "Anything Goes." The tour will be launched here Sept. 12 at the Orange County Performing Arts Center, then go on to such cities as Chicago, Dallas, Atlanta, St. Louis, Cleveland, Toronto, Denver, Louisville, Miami, Nashville and Seattle. Zerry Zaks' original direction will be re-created for the Gaynor tour by Philip Cusack, who also directed an international company of the Cole Porter show. Tony Walton's original costumes and scenic design will be featured, with Bob Mackie doing Gaynor's costumes. Choreography will be by Tony-winner Thommie Walsh. The score for "Anything Goes" includes "I Get a Kick Out of You," "Blow, Gabriel, Blow," "All Through the Night" and "You're the Top," in addition to the title tune. ALEENE MacMINN
