920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Committee heads named CHAIRMEN of Commons committees elected yesterday were: Science and technology, Sir Giles Shaw, (Con Pudsey); Defence, Sir Nicholas Bonsor (Con Upminster); Foreign Affairs, Mr David Howell (Con Guildford); Scottish Affairs, Mr William McKelvey (Lab Kilmarnock and Loudoun); Agriculture, Mr Jerry Wiggin (Con Weston-Super-Mare). Environment, Mr Robert Jones (Con Hertfordshire West); Education, Sir Malcolm Thornton (Con Crosby); Transport, Mr Robert Adley (Con Christchurch); Health, Mrs Marion Roe (Con Broxbourne); Home Affairs, Sir Ivan Lawrence (Con Burton); and National Heritage, Mr Gerald Kaufman (Lab Manchester Gorton).
