BFN [Text] The Philippine Government has acted to help eradicate the use of substances harmful to the ozone layer by 1996 and support this through massive reforestation activities. Spearheading these activities are the Departments of Environment and Natural Resources and Finance and the Land Bank of the Philippines. The campaign is in compliance with the Montreal Protocol of 1987, wherein signatory countries -- including the Philippines -- are mandated to do away with harmful substances.
