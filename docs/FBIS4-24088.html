BFN [Text] The secretary general of the Party for Democracy and Progress [PDP], Mr. Boubacar Karamoko Coulibaly, has resigned from the party. He said in a communique that this is a definitive and irrevocable resignation which frees him from his position as party leader and militant. The communique added that the leaders of the PDP did not accept that Boubacar Karamoko Coulibaly decided to remain in the government while the party had decided to withdraw. To remain in the government, Mr. Coulibaly, who said he acted with the best interest of the nation at heart, resigned from the PDP. For more details, Mr. Coulibaly will give a press briefing today as of 1600 at the Hotel de l'Amitie. The national and international media is invited to attend.
