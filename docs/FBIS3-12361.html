Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Algeria we note that the Foreign Affairs Ministry issued a statement in which it strongly condemned the terrible massacre committed against the Palestinian worshipers inside the al-Khalil [Hebron] mosque, and called on the Security Council to adopt measures that secure the protection and safety of the Palestinian people. Before that the head of state, Liamine Zeroual phoned [PLO] Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and conveyed to him the sympathies of the Algerian people following this massacre and Algeria's solidarity with the Palestinian people and its leadership in this tribulation.
