940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Brake Bros in Pounds 1.9m purchase Brake Bros, which supplies frozen food to the catering industry, has bought Jesse Robinson for Pounds 1.9m. The deal, which includes associate John Hill, is to be satisfied by the issue of 145,000 shares with the rest in cash. Robinson is a family business which supplies frozen food and fresh fish to the catering industry in the Nottingham area.
