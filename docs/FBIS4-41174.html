BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] A meeting between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Vyacheslav Kebich, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Belarus, was held in Moscow today. The meeting focused on issues of intensifying and accelerating the processes of Russian and Belarusian economic integration. During a detailed and friendly conversation, the political leaders exchanged views on the democratic reforms in Russia and Belarus, and on the evolution and future of political collaboration within the CIS.
