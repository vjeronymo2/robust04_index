920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / International Company News: Trading starts in Thai Air shares THAI Airways International, the national airline whose shares began trading on the Bangkok Stock Exchange yesterday - and offered to the public at Bt60 a share - traded between Bt61 and Bt64.50, Reuter reports from Bangkok. Brokers and analysts said the price was in line with expectations. Mr Eric Halmon of Premier Finance and Securities, the local brokerage, said: 'The opening is a bit low but not a major surprise.'
