920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Algiers bombing A powerful bomb ripped through a crowded Algiers airport terminal yesterday, killing nine people and injuring more than 100, according to Interior Minister Mohammed Hardi, Reuter reports from Algiers. Mr Hardi promised 'draconian measures' to prevent further attacks. Algeria has been plunged into violence since authorities cancelled general elections in January after the now-banned Islamic Salvation Front (FIS) took a strong lead in the first round of voting.
