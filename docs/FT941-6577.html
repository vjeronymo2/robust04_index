940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Hogg Robinson Hogg Robinson, the business services concern, has acquired Skillbase, a specialist in corporate downsizing, flexible resourcing and management and personal skills training, for Pounds 5.5m in cash. There will be an additional earn-out up to a maximum of Pounds 2m related specifically to profits from corporate downsizing contracts over the next three years. For the year ended June 30 1993 Skillbase achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.56m.
