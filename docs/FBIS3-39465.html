Language: French Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "IPSOS-LE POINT Opinion Poll"] [Text] A national sample of 800 people representing the French population 18 and older were polled by telephone on 8 and 9 February 1994 using the representative quotas method. Following are the results:

Should French UN troops stay in Bosnia or should they be pulled out?

They should stay: 58 percent They should be pulled out: 28 percent Don't know: 14 percent
Should NATO bomb Serbian artillery positions around Sarajevo?
Yes: 54 percent No: 29 percent Don't know: 17 percent
Should French troops take part in the air strikes if NATO
decides to bomb Serbian artillery positions around Sarajevo? Yes: 70 percent No: 21 percent Don't know: 9 percent

Should the French authorities accept risking French military losses in the fighting taking place in the former Yugoslavia?

Yes: 55 percent No: 35 percent Don't know: 10 percent
