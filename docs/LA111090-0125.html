November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: NARCOTICS SUSPECT FLEES SCENE, IS KILLED IN CRASH

A narcotics suspect was killed while trying to elude police Friday when his car crashed into a tree, flipped over and crushed him, police said.

Undercover officers saw the suspect allegedly engaged in a drug transaction near Vermont Avenue and Pico Boulevard near Koreatown about 11:30 a.m.

When the suspect saw police move closer he sped away in his car, police spokesman Don Lawrence said.

The suspect, a juvenile who was not identified, drove about half a mile at speeds described as excessive, and apparently lost control of the car.
