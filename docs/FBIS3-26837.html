Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Article by staff reporter Yang Gongcheng (2799 0501 2052): "Fu Bingyao and Pan Zhaomin Lead Military Region Comfort Group To Visit Autonomous Regional Party Committee, Government, and Production and Construction Corps"] [Text] On the morning of 3 February, Fu Bingyao [0265 4426 5069], deputy commander of the Lanzhou Military Region and concurrently commander of the Xinjiang Military District, and Pan Zhaomin, deputy political commissar of the Lanzhou Military Region and concurrently political commissar of the Xinjiang Military District, led a comfort group from the military region to visit the autonomous regional party committee, the autonomous regional party government, and the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps. During informal discussions with leading comrades from the autonomous regional party committee and government, Pan Zhaomin expressed sincere solicitude for autonomous regional party and government organs and people of all nationalities in Xinjiang, on behalf of party committees and organs in the military region, as well as all officers and men. He also expressed heartfelt thanks for the autonomous region's concern and care for the troops stationed in Xinjiang. He said: People of all nationalities are the backbone of the motherland's border defense. Over the years, party committees and governments at all levels and people of all nationalities have shown concern and support for army building. This has greatly encouraged and inspired officers and men, ensuring the successful completion of tasks in defending the motherland and its border. Abulaidi Amudurexiti, deputy secretary of the autonomous regional party committee and acting chairman of the autonomous regional government, thanked the military region for arranging the visits. He said: Through border defense, troops in the military region have created a border environment in which the autonomous region can safely open up its border areas. Various military units have conducted in-depth education on supporting the government and cherishing the people; actively promoted unity between the Army and the government, between the Army and the people, and among various nationalities; and fostered a peaceful and stable social environment north and south of Tian Shan. The vast numbers of officers and men have fully supported local economic construction, thereby promoting the autonomous region's economic development. Troops in the military region have contributed to defending and building border areas, and people of all nationalities in Xinjiang will never forget this. On behalf of party committees and organs in the military region, Fu Bingyao extended Spring Festival greetings to cadres of all nationalities, workers, and staff members in the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps. He stated: The production and construction corps is an important force in Xinjiang's defense and construction. As always, troops in the military region will support the work carried out by the corps, with whom they will jointly do a good job in Xinjiang's defense and construction. Jin Yunhui, deputy secretary of the autonomous regional party committee and commander of the production and construction corps, noted: In the new year, we will continue to beef up militia reservists for the corps so as to make new contributions to bolstering defense reserve forces and maintaining border security and social stability in the autonomous region. Leading autonomous regional and corps comrades Janabil, Zhang Fusen, Guo Gang, Hailiqiemu Silamu, Aisihaiti Kelimubai, and Mijiti Nasier [4717 0679 2251 4780 2448 1422] held cordial informal discussions and exchanged Spring Festival greetings with the military region's leaders. Yao Tieshan [1202 6993 1472], deputy director of the military region's political department; Lei Yinhai [7191 6892 3189], political commissar of the military region's logistics department; and Zhao Shizhong [6392 1102 1813], director of the military region's equipment and technology department, were among the visitors.
