Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 31 -- Chairman of the Vietnam National Assembly Nong Duc Manh, this afternoon received were the visiting delegation of the European Parliament. [sentence as received] At the reception, Senator Ben Visser, head of the delegation, informed Chairman Nong Duc Manh of the European Parliament's activities in 1993 with a view of promoting the relations between the countries in the European Community and Vietnam. For his part, Charman Nong Duc Manh hailed the positive contributions of the European Parliament, and expressed his thanks to the European Parliament for its wish for the establishment of the relations between the Vietnam National Assembly and the European Parliament and for inviting a delegation of the Vietnamese NA to visit the European Parliament. Mr. Nong Duc Manh and Senator Ben Visser hailed the delegation's visit, as the beginning of a new stage of relations of cooperation, friendship and development between Vietnam and the EC's countries, and between Vietnam and the European Parliament. Early during the day, the delegation held talks with a delegation of Vietnam's National Assembly led by deputy head of the External Relations Commission Phan Quang. At the talks, Senator Ben Visser said that a programme of cooperation with Vietnam was being drafted. He also expressed his hope that a frame-work agreement on trade and economic cooperation between Vietnam and the European Parliament would be signed soon. For his part, Mr Phan Quang spoke about the issues of common concern aimed at further promoting the possibilities of cooperation and mutual assistance between the Vietnam National Assembly and the European Parliament in the spirit of friendship, equality and mutual benifit.
