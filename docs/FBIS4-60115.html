BFN [Remarks by Jibril al-Rujub, official in charge of Palestinian Preventive Security in the West Bank, in Jericho on 22 May -- recorded] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] I dare say that the reception my colleagues and I were accorded at the headquarters of the Preventive Security was a virtual plebiscite on the popularity of Fatah, the PLO, and Yasir 'Arafat and his political program. This is a great thing in which I take pride and which encourages me and my colleagues to stick to our political and organizational program. What has been achieved thus far is just one step on the road to the establishment of our independent Palestinian state.
