910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / UK Company News: London & Overseas Freighters ahead Profits before tax of London & Overseas Freighters, the freight broker and transport agent, improved from Dollars 1.32m to Dollars 2.04m (Pounds 1.19m) for the 12 months to March 31. Directors said the trends that had benefited the tanker market for the last few years had continued in 1990. The group's objective is to acquire modern tankers to meet the future requirements of the oil industry. Earnings per share improved to 8.4p (6.2p). The year's dividend is a same-again 1p.
