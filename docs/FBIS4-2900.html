BFN [Text] Beijing, April 15 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met here this morning with Ella Endicott, widow of Dr. James Endicott, in the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse. Dr. Endicott, a renowned Canadian peace champion, is regarded as a life-long friend of the Chinese people. Dr. Endicott was born into a missionary family in Leshan city, in China's Sichuan Province, in December 1898, and died in Toronto, Canada, in November 1993.
