Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Review of the Latvian Press"] [Text] The newspaper "NEATKARIGA CINA" publishes the data concerning the Latvian Army. At present, the personnel of the Latvian Defence Forces consists of 1,500 soldiers and 150 officers. 600 soldiers and 30 officers serve in the naval forces and 150 soldiers and 30 officers - in the air forces. 3.8 thousand soldiers and 340 officers guard the Latvian frontiers. On the whole, 6 thousand soldiers and 550 officers make up the Latvian Army. 15 ships are at the Latvian Army's disposal. Only 9 of them are cleared of planes: "-2" and two-410". [sentence as received] Only one of them is ready for action. 4 helicopters out of 6 are ready for action. This year, 1.8 per cent from the budget funds are supposed to be allocated for the needs of the Latvian defence forces.
