BFN [Text] Sanaa has rejected the dispatch of Arab forces to stop the war in Yemen, saying that what is happening in Yemen is a domestic affair and could be decided internally, according to sources of the General People's Congress, which is led by President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. Salih dismissed the Yemeni oil minister and the Aden governor, both of whom belong to the Yemeni Socialist Party led by 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Our correspondent Tawfiq Jazulit reported a short time ago that south Yemeni fighter planes staged several raids tonight on Sanaa. Six Scud missiles fell on the city during the day while 2 northern planes raided Aden. The northerners have announced that they are preparing to launch an attack on Aden on several fronts.
