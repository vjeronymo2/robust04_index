BFN [Text] During the second quarter of 1994 Russia will pay 90 million dollars in order to cover a part of its debt to the budgets of the UN system organizations. The appropriate resolution was signed by Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin. During the third and the fourth quarters of 1994 Russia will pay 169 million dollars to the UN budget as membership dues. Russia will also pay 14 million dollars to the UNESCO budget and 11 million dollars to the International Atomic Energy Agency. In accordance with the government resolution, during the third and the fourth quarter of 1994 the Russian Finance Ministry will allot 200 million dollars to pay its membership dues to the international organizations. "The appropriate funds in foreign currency must be allotted from the resources which the federal budget has planned to use for non-trade operations," the resolution underlined.
