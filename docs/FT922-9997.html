920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Council housing Contracts totalling more than Pounds 6m have been awarded to WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION MIDLANDS by Birmingham City Council and Wolverhampton Borough Council. A Pounds 4.1m contract comprises jobbing repairs, cyclical maintenance and minor improvements to the Wolverhampton Borough Council's leisure, social and educational building stock. On the Overpool Estate in Ward End, Birmingham, work is being carried out worth Pounds 800,000 to provide 31 families with totally rewired, replumbed, redecorated and centrally heated accommodation.
