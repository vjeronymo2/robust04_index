921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / Parliament and Politics: Conservative party appoints director-general THE Conservative party has appointed Mr Paul Judge, chairman of Food from Britain, as its director-general - a post created by Sir Norman Fowler, party chairman, as part of his management reforms at Tory central office. Mr Judge, 43, is a former chairman of Premier Brands and former group planning director of Cadbury Schweppes. Food from Britain is the government body responsible for promoting the British food industry. Mr Judge is pictured above (left) with Sir Norman. He said yesterday: 'My aim - in leading the professional team and in strengthening its efficiency and effectiveness - will be to ensure that we play our full part in winning the elections which lie ahead: local elections, European elections and above all our fifth successive general election victory.'
