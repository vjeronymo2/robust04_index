941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / Howard slander action dropped Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, has agreed to the lifting of the injunction he obtained against the Financial Times on October 21. He has further agreed to discontinue his action against the paper. The High Court yesterday made an order by consent accordingly. Mr Richard Lambert, the editor, said: 'Mr Howard accepted that the FT never intended to publish the allegations which were the subject of the order.'
