Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN ["Communique of the General Staff of the Khmer Royal Armed Forces"; dated 19 March -- read by announcer] [Text] To ensure public security and stability throughout the Kingdom of Cambodia and in order to correctly implement the policy of the Royal Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia which does not allow the existence of secessionist areas or division of area under separate administration, the General Staff would like to report that the Khmer Royal Armed Forces [KRAF] have been forced to completely occupy [chol kan kap] the area that is the largest position [ti tang thom bamphot] of the Khmer Rouge rebels at 1800 on 19 March. With the assistance of the KRAF forces stationed there, efforts are being made to normalize the situation. The General Staff will provide detailed information later. [Signed] The General Staff of the KRAF [Dated] 19 March 1994
