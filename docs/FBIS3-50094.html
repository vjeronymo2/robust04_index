Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Aleksander Kwasniewski, leader of the Democratic Left Alliance [SLD], said that the coalition will continue its efforts to obtain deputy minister posts in three of the so-called presidential ministries: the Ministry of National Defense, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. At a meeting with parliamentary reporters, Kwasniewski also said, regarding ratification of the Concordat, that when there are just two options--yes or no--the SLD will vote against it. The SLD leader also let it be known that Social Democracy has given up on the concept of the direct election of village elders and town and city mayors in the forthcoming local government elections.
