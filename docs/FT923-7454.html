920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / International Company News: Kinnevik and Korsnas to merge KINNEVIK, the Swedish telecommunications and media group, intends to merge with Korsnas, the fourth largest pulp and paper company in Sweden to create a new diversified industrial enterprise. Kinnevik owns 51.5 per cent of Korsnas's voting shares and 44.5 per cent of its equity. An extraordinary shareholders' meeting will be held on September 7 to approve the merger. Mr Jan Stenbeck, Kinnevik's chief executive, said the high market interest rates and heavy tax burdens facing Korsnas had led to the decision to merge.
