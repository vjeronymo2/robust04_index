921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / Finance & The Family: Smaller companies slide Smaller companies continued to slide this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) fell by 0.8 per cent to 1059.73 over the seven days to Thursday, October 8 while the County Natwest Index fell by 1 per cent to 820.77 over the eight days to October 8.
