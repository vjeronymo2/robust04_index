Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] In riots in the territories today, three IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers and a border policeman were lightly injured. Another four Israeli civilians were lightly injured by stones. The epicenter of today's riots was in Nabulus, 27 Palestinian residents were wounded, two of them gravely. Riots took place in Tulkarm and Janin as well. The IDF imposed a curfew on Nabulus and Tulkarm. This was reported by our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz.
