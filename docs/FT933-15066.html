930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG gold shares recovered early losses as the gold price found support at Dollars 388 and moved back above Dollars 390. The gold index ended at 1,972, up 1 after an intraday low of 1,903. Industrials slid 32 to 4,659 while the overall index dropped 28 to 4,117.
