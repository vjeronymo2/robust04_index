Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali received at Carthage Palace this morning Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana in the presence of [Tunisian] Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahya. Following the meeting, which was also attended by the Tunisian and Spanish ambassadors in Madrid and Tunisia, Mr. Solana expressed his happiness for the opportunity to hold talks with his excellency the president, pointing out that the meeting dealt with relations between Tunisia and Spain, which he described as being very good.
