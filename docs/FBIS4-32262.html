BFN [Text] Prague, June 21 (CTK) -- In a meeting with Spanish Senate President Juan Jose Laborda, Czech parliament Chairman Milan Uhde today welcomed the possibility that both chambers of the Spanish parliament could ratify the association agreement signed between the Czech Republic and the European Union (EU) this year. A delegation of the Spanish Senate arrived here yesterday for a four-day visit. Uhde said Spanish politicians had supported the Czech Republic's aspirations to become a member of Western European structures. After a meeting with Laborda, Czech Deputy Premier Jan Kalvoda told CTK that Laborda said he was convinced the Spanish parliament would approve the association agreement in autumn without any problems.
