February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

KARPUN GETS 29TH GOAL AS DALLAS BEATS LAZERS

Mark Karpun scored his 29th goal of the season, and Eloy Salgado assisted on Dallas' first three goals as the short-handed Sidekicks beat the Los Angeles Lazers, 4-3, Saturday night at the Forum. The Sidekicks, missing five regulars, won for the fourth time in five games against the Lazers.

The Lazers pulled within 3-2 on Ben Collins' goal at 12:35 of the third period, only to have Jorge Acosta give Dallas another two-goal lead at 1:53 of the fourth.
