930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / Stoy Hayward writ over audit STOY HAYWARD, the accountancy firm, has been served with a writ in connection with its audit of Petrocon, the engineering and surveying group now known as Beverley Group. It is being sued in connection with excessive stock and work-in-progress valuations in a subsidiary allegedly not identified by the firm during 1990. Mr Paul Hipps, senior partner, said: 'We will contest any claim very vigorously.'
