930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / US envoy shot in Georgia A US diplomat has been shot dead in strife-torn Georgia, the first western official to die in the political turmoil in the former Soviet Union, writes Leyla Boulton. Mr Fred Woodruff, a regional affairs officer, was hit by a bullet as he was being driven by the security chief of Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze, outside Tbilisi.
