BFN [Unattributed report: "Libyan Salvation Front Completes Preparations for National Council"] [Text] London, AL-HAYAH -- Ibrahim Sahd, official spokesman of the National Front for the Salvation of Libya [NFSL] (opposition), has stated that "the NFSL's organizational situation is excellent" and that preparations "have been completed for convening the NFSL National Council in the near future. It will be a major demonstration for the Libyan oppositionists to confirm their options and define the new courses of action." He told AL-HAYAH yesterday that 35 representatives of the Central American region met some days ago and elected 10 people to represent them in the National Council along with five observers, and that representataves of Western America had met earlier and elected eight representatives and four observers. He added that this process was carried out in most NFSL offices and branches around the world.
