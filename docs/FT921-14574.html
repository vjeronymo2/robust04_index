920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / World Trade News: ICL wins Pounds 35m German order ICL, the UK-based computer manufacturer in which Fujitsu of Japan has a majority stake, has won a Pounds 35m order for personal computers from the telecommunications arm of Deutsche Bundespost, writes Alan Cane. It is ICL's largest single order for such equipment and goes a long way to establishing the company's credentials as a personal computer manufacturer. ICL is known principally as a manufacturer of mainframe computers and high-performance workstations.
