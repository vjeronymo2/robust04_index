BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS-TURKMEN PRESS correspondent Dzhennet Arrykova] [Text] Ashgabat, 12 May--Bilateral economic cooperation between Argentine and Turkmenistan were today discussed at a meeting between Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella and Turkmenistan Vice President Boris Shikhmuradov. The Argentine minister demonstrated great interest in Turkmen projects in the field of power engineering that, he said, are of world importance. The Argentine guest today arrived in Ashgabat on an official visit.
