910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Tootal stake A further 500,000 ordinary shares in Tootal were bought on Tuesday on behalf of companies controlled by Mr Cha Chi Ming, at an average price of 74.5p per share. Companies controlled by Mr Cha now own 18.22m Tootal ordinary, representing 6.3 per cent of the issued ordinary share capital.
