BFN [Text] The French Foreign Ministry has denied the report in the 17 June issue of the Brussels daily LE SOIR, which hints that two members of the French armed forces were involved in the attack in which the president of Rwanda was killed. The French Foreign Ministry describes this as a ridiculous allegation, recalling that previous unfounded rumors have circulated implicating foreign countries. In Kigali the Rwandan Patriotic Front [RPF], the rebel movement of the minority Tutsi ethnic group, has just seized control of the Sainte Famille Church. It was held by the government forces and Hutu militias until now.
