BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Han Tok-su, chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), on a visit to the socialist homeland. On the occasion Chairman Han Tok-su extended to President Kim Il-song the highest honor and thanks carrying the hearts of loyalty of the entire officials of Chongnyon and 700,000 compatriots in Japan and wished him a long life in good health. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and conversed with him in a cordial atmosphere.
