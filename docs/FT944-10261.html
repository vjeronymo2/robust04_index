941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / Observer: Call me or else Evidently the telephone is an endangered species. Dial-A-Gift of Salt Lake City has just changed its name to 'the Interactive Gift Centres Network'. The New York directory still lists 33 dial-a-somethings, including Date, Mattress and Sewer Man. Given the onward march of political correctness, it can't be long before Interactive Waste Disposal Person makes an appearance.
