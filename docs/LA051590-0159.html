May 15, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

CBS TO PREMIERE ALASKA SERIES

CBS, which like NBC and ABC is making new efforts to keep viewers during the summer with new programs, said it will premiere a one-hour series, "Northern Exposure," this summer.

No date has been yet for the show. It stars Rob Morrow as a young New York physician who, fresh out of medical school, faces culture shock when he becomes the sole doctor in a small Alaskan village.

The series was created by Joshua Brand and John Falsey, who was behind NBC's "St. Elsewhere" and "A Year in the Life." Production of the show has begun in Bellevue, Wash., CBS said.
