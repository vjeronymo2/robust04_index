920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / Observer: Enlightened Meanwhile in America 10-year-old Clint Lenz has earned a place in the Smithsonian Institution by inventing a luminous lavatory seat. Although his father is a plumber, it was his mother who inspired him. 'Mom always said she wanted a heated one, but I said 'glow-in-the-dark',' he explained. Besides taking first place in the household division of the national Invent America contest, the idea won him a Dollars 1,000 savings bond and a computer for his school.
