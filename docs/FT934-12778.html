931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Graystone GRAYSTONE has sold all the issued share capital of GA Stadler, the processor and distributor of natural sausage casings based in Cleveland, to GA Stadler & Co (1993), controlled by Mr Alan Talbot, Mr Michael Talbot and Ms Joan Talbot, all 'related parties'. Consideration will be Pounds 274,000, of which Pounds 160,000 is in cash on completion.
