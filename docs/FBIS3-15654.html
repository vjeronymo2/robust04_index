Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Proposals from a Ukrainian delegation make the idea of forming financial-industrial groups and joint-stock enterprises within the CIS quite achievable -- This is how Volodymyr Melnyk, senior official at the Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers, assessed the preliminary agreements reached among the Commonwealth states at their talks in Moscow. He also pointed out that Kiev's official support of the idea does not contradict Ukraine's admission as merely an associate member of the CIS economic union. This standpoint has been officially proclaimed earlier by the Government of Ukraine.
