Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] Mashhad, IRNA-- In an armed clash between the border law enforcement personnel of Tayebad and armed outlaws and smugglers, 548 kg of opium was seized. The public relations office of Khorasan law enforcement zone announced: In this clash, which lasted four hours in the Ahangaran Heights, the outlaws abandoned their substances and escaped. They are still being pursued. This public relations office also announced: The personnel of the law enforcement of Mashhad, Neyshabur, Torbat-e Heydariyyeh, Torbat-e Jam, Nohbandan, and Dargaz in the past two days during inspections and undercover transactions arrested 18 dealers of death and seized 125 kg of opium.
