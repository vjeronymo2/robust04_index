940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Prudential Prudential Corporation's US subsidiary, Jackson National Life Insurance, saw pre-tax profits fall from Dollars 118.5m to Dollars 71.6m (Pounds 46.1m) in the six months to end-June. There was a further Dollars 4.6m from capital gains against Dollars 55.1m. Premiums dropped from Dollars 1.31bn to Dollars 1.07bn, though investment income rose slightly from Dollars 665m to Dollars 703.9m.
