Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Zhudro] [Excerpt] Tel Aviv, 11 Mar -- [Passage omitted] In a conversation with the ITAR-TASS correspondent, [Russian Foreign Minister] A. Kozyrev expressed his satisfaction with the exchange of opinions he had with Israeli leaders. He also noted that Russia is making unorthodox efforts for the sake of saving the peace process in this region. He said that after the Hebron tragedy, events in the Middle East took a dramatic turn and it was necessary to take extraordinary measures for saving the agreements reached between Israel and PLO earlier. This visit, which is aimed at persuading the Israelis and the Palestinians to return to negotiations table, was one of such measures. Tonight, the Russian foreign minister departed for Tunis where he will have talks with PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat.
