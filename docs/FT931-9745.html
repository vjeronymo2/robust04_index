930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / International Capital Markets: New Citibank loans index CITIBANK has designed a US corporate loan index to provide an effective means of performance measurement, in a market which is becoming more actively traded. In the past few years, banks have become more active in their management of loan assets, sometimes as a result of efforts to offload distressed debt or to control credit risk. The Citibank Loan Index comprises 1,000 floating-rate revolver and term loans to over 600 companies, representing commitments of around Dollars 400bn.
