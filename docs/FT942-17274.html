940406 FT 06 APR 94 / Banker accused of taking Pounds 4m bribes A banker pursued for six years by the Hong Kong authorities allegedly received more than Pounds 4m in bribes, an extradition hearing was told yesterday. Mr Ewan Quayle Launder, former chief executive of Wardley, the investment arm of Hong Kong and Shanghai Bank, was arrested in September. The hearing was adjourned to today.
