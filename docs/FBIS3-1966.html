Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hangzhou, March 3 (XINHUA) -- East China's Zhejiang, one of China's few provinces which abound in fluorite ore, has over the past decade developed the country's top fluorination industrial base. Official sources said that by the end of this century, an estimated output value of more than three billion yuan will be realized by the fluorination industry of the province. At present, the fluorination industrial sector is capable of producing about ten types of products, including basic fluorination materials, elementary fluoride and fluo-medicine, according to official sources. In the past decade, the province has invested a total of some 470 million yuan to introduce advanced technology and equipment such as instruments for the production of anhydrous hydrofluoric acid and methane-chloride, in support of the technological development of its fluorination industry.
