Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, 5 Jan -- The press service of the Crimean Council of Ministers reports that Ukraine and the Crimea have signed an agreement on introducing a national taxation system into the tax relations between the Crimea and Ukraine. In accordance with this, taxes payable by the citizens of the Crimea will go to the budget of the Republic of the Crimea. A sum equal to one-twentieth of the state budget of Ukraine will in its turn be payable as contributions by the republic's centralized budget to Kiev. The relevant document was signed by Acting Ukrainian Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy and the chairman of the Crimean Council of Ministers, Borys Samsonov.
