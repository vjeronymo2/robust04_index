920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / International Company News: French Prop net assets static French Property Trust had a net asset value of 68.21p per share at June 30. The outcome showed little change on the 68.67p for the comparable period - the 12 months to end December. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 132,786 against Pounds 383,608 for earnings of 0.53p (1.53p) per share. The company did not declare an interim dividend.
