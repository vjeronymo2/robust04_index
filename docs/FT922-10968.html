920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: JO Walker losses rise to Pounds 0.47m JO Walker, the Leicester-based timber importer, reported increased losses for the 1991 year of Pounds 467,000, compared with Pounds 36,000. Turnover fell 8 per cent from Pounds 15.1m to Pounds 13.9m. A tax credit of Pounds 165,000 (Pounds 6,000) left losses per share of 37.8p (3.8p). Directors are proposing not to pay a dividend against last year's 2p.
