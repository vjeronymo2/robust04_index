Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 8 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message to Martti Ahtisaari congratulating him upon his election as president of the Republic of Finland. In the message he expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would further develop, and wished the Finnish president success in his work for the prosperity of the country.
