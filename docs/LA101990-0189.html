October 19, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

U.S. HAS 8-STROKE GOLF LEAD

Pat Hurst shot a 2-under-par 71 today and Karen Noble carded a 72 as the United States took an eight-stroke lead over Britain after the second round of the women's world amateur golf championship.

Hurst of San Leandro, Calif., and Noble of Brookside, N.J., combined for a 143 total that gave the U.S. a two-round total of 291.
