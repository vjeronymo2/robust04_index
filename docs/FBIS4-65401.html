BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis called on the UN Security Council to adopt specific alternative ways to implement its resolutions on Cyprus. In an interview with ASSOCIATED PRESS, Kliridhis said the deadlock became intractable because of the Turkish side's continuing intransigence and systematic rejection of UN resolutions. The president added that in light of this, the Security Council must take an alternative approach in seeking a Cyprus solution.
