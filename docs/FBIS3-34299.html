Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus has arrived on an official visit to Israel. An official ceremony was held at Jerusalem's Rose Garden and the visitor was received by Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin. During his visit to Israel, the Czech prime minister will meet Israel's president and the Knesset speaker. He will also hold working meetings with the prime minister, the foreign minister, and other dignitaries. This evening, Mr. Rabin will host Prime Minister Klaus at a festive dinner which will be attended by the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] first pilots and paratroopers who trained in the former Czechoslovakia during the War of Independence.
