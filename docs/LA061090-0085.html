June 10, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

READ THE LABEL

Have Governor Deukmejian and the poison-spraying crews in California read the warnings on the malathion bottles?

They warn against spraying children, animals, fish, and bees.

They warn against inhaling the poison, getting the spray on hands or in eyes.

Which is more important -- subsidizing the pesticide manufacturers and agribusinesses, or protecting American families, their animals, their food?

MARY P. AUSTIN

Pauma Valley
