Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] In a statement that has just been received, Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos says: The Greek Government condemns the recent developments in Sarajevo and expresses its abhorrence of them. The drama of Sarajevo's civilians continues and the latest tragic events make the severity of the problem clear. Searching for those responsible is a duty of all parties involved in the international community. However, what is urgent now is to at last find a peaceful, swift, just, and viable solution to the Yugoslav crisis.
