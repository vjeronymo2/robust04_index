September 20, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Peter Walker will become vice president/station manager at Tribune Broadcasting's KTLA Channel 5 on Oct. 16, the station announced Tuesday. Walker comes from the Tribune's Chicago station, WGN-TV, where he has been general sales manager. He replaces Michael Eigner, who went to New York to run WPIX. ALEENE MacMINN
