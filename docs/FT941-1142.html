940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Premium Trust net asset value at 97.5p Premium Trust, the Lloyd's insurance investment trust which came to the market in December, reported net asset value per share of 97.5p at February 28. Net revenue for the interim period from December 10 to the end of February was Pounds 26,895 for earnings per share of 0.39p. The interim dividend is 0.25p.
