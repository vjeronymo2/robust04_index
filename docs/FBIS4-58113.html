BFN [Text] Our military correspondent reports that the South Lebanese Army [SLA] centers in Kafr Rumman and al-Nabi Tahir were subjected to an abortive terrorist attack at 2130 last night. The terrorists used machine guns and rockets and had the support of long-range artillery. But the two centers' personnel succeeded in resisting the attacks with the use of tanks and artillery. There were no casualties among the SLA troops. The terrorists' losses are not yet known.
