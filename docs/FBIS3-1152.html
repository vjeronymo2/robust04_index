Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos today swore in the new government members appointed by presidential decrees yesterday. Jose Pedro de Morais was sworn in as minister of planning and economic coordination. Joao Baptista Kassuma and Eduardo Severino Morais were sworn in as deputy planning minister and economic coordination deputy minister, respectively. Antonio Gomes Furtado is the new deputy finance minister, while Guilherme do Espirito Santo is deputy minister of sports. The ceremony took place at the Futungo de Belas hall and was attended by government officials. Jose Eduardo dos Santos wished the newly appointed officials success, pointing out that it is the start of a new challenge. We have a new team and we must play to win, he said.
