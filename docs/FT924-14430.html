921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / The Conservative Party at Brighton: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference urged the government to continue to reduce unemployment through 'targeted help for young people and the longer-term unemployed'; asked ministers to take firm action to protect the environment; called for the government to build on the 1980s achievements of 'sustained growth, the expansion of ownership and the rise in real living standards'; welcomed the developments in the national health service; welcomed the creation of a department of national heritage. TODAY: Conference will debate Sunday trading and the Tory party organisation; and will listen to an address from the prime minister.
