December 8, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

COMMON MARKET;

EUROPE GROUP MAY WEIGH NEW ACCORD

Leaders of the 12-nation European Community, meeting in Strasbourg, France, today are expected to discuss a closer relationship with the smaller European Free Trade Assn. Under a proposal by the EC Commission, the six Free Trade countries -- Austria, Iceland, Finland, Norway, Sweden and Switzerland -- would gain freedom of movement for people, goods, services and capital with the Common Market. The New York Times said today that an agreement will probably announced later this month.
