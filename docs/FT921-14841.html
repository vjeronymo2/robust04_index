920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Riksbank signs primary dealers THE Riksbank, Sweden's central bank, has signed primary dealer contracts with four banks and four stockbrokers following a six-month trial period, AP-DJ reports from Stockholm. The central bank has introduced the contracts in a move to create a more efficient secondary market for the Swedish bourse. The dealers granted primary dealerships will be treated by the Riksbank as counterparts in open market operations and other money-market business, the Riksbank said. The Riksbank said that the following eight institutions had been granted primary dealership: JP Bank, Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken, Svenska Handels-banken, Swedbank, Consensus Fondkommission, Transferator Fondkommission, United Securities Fondkommission and E. Ohman Fondkommission.
