February 13, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

FOUNTAIN VALLEY;

SCHOLARSHIP ENDOWMENT CREATED BY CHAMBER

The Fountain Valley Chamber of Commerce has established a $5,000 scholarship endowment to help individuals who live or work in the community to advance their education.

The endowment, to Coastline Community College, will be used to help qualified students and business professionals continue academic or vocational education and training, said Joel Vest, executive vice president of the chamber.

Additional information about chamber scholarships can be obtained by contacting Thomas Snyder, executive director of the Coastline Community College Foundation, at 241-6243.
