BFN [Text] Khartoum, Mar. 31, INA -- Sudanese First Vice-President al-Zubayr Muhammad Salih expressed his people and leadership's appreciation for Iraq's national stance under the leadership of President Saddam Husayn. During a meeting with Iraqi Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research Dr. Humam 'Abd-al-Khaliq, General Salih said the people of Iraq who enjoyed high self-confidence and firm faith in Almighty God managed to reconstruct all that had been destroyed during the 30-state aggression and have confronted the blockade through depending on self-potentials. The Sudanese vice-president stressed his country's adherence to its national and principled stances toward Iraq and the Arab nation. The meeting, attended by Sudanese minister of education and scientific research, and the Iraqi ambassador in Khartoum, discussed means of expanding and developing cooperation and joint action between the two sisterly countries.
