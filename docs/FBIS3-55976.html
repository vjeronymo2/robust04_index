Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Karabakh troops killed 46 Azerbaijani soldiers on Tuesday after it surrounded an Azerbaijani unit in the Mardakert region, northern Karabakh. A spokesman for the Army of the self-proclaimed Nagorno-Karabakh Republic has told Interfax that yet another 270 Azerbaijani servicemen have been put out of action on other fronts. Meanwhile, Karabakh has regained control over a number of strategic heights, earlier captured by Azerbaijan. The Karabakh Army's casualties on Tuesday were estimated at 6 killed and 15 wounded. By latest reports from Stepanakert, Azerbaijani troops mounted an offensive virtually on all fronts on Wednesday. Bitter fighting is under way. Meanwhile, Azerbaijan's Defence Ministry has refuted as false Armenia's reports about Azerbaijan's heavy casualties in the fighting. It contended that last week's Azerbaijani losses in both man power and material were much smaller than Armenia's damage. A ministry spokesman estimated Armenia's losses in the Mardakert region where Azerbaijani troops ringed two Karabakh battalions on Tuesday at 30 men and three tanks. Yet another 35 Armenian soldiers reportedly lost their lives in fighting on the Askeran and Agdam fronts and in the Fizuli region, Azerbaijan. Several tanks and artillery guns were smashed.
