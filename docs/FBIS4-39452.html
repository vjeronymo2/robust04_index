BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Dmitriy Zelenin] [Text] Beirut, 8 Jun -- Lieutenant General Mustafa Talas, the Syrian deputy prime minister and defense minister, held talks with Army General Mikhail Kolesnikov, Russian Armed Forces chief of staff, on 7 June in Damascus. The talks were also attended by Syrian Army General Hikmat Shahabi and Brigadier Generals Ali (?Aslan) and Hasan Turkumani. Urgent problems of Russian-Syrian military cooperation were discussed at the meeting in the context of the bilateral agreement signed in April this year, which envisages the resumption of cooperation of both countries in this area.
