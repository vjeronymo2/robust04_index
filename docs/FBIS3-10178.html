Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Jose Luis Rodriguez Garcia, minister president of the State Committee for Finance, has explained the country's complex economic situation and the efforts being made to bail the country out of the current crisis. Speaking during a national training seminar for higher education teachers, Rodriguez Garcia said the country at this moment has more than 145,000 self-employed workers, and that this figure is increasing. He added that the measures adopted in the wake of dissolution of the socialist countries and the U.S. imperialist blockade have had an economic, political, and social cost. During the seminar, postgraduate education was discussed, during which it was agreed that experts must be integrated to enhance their professional level and the prestige of the university center.
