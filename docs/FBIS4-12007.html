BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Apr (MENA)--Palestinian military sources told MENA that 100 PLO troops left today for the Egyptian border town of Rafah. There the troops will prepare a camp in which Palestinian troops coming from various destinations will assemble before heading to Gaza through the Rafah crossing point. The sources said that the camp will serve as a point of assembly of a Palestiniain police force that will deploy in Gaza as soon as the PLO and Israel sign an agreement to implement the Gaza-Jericho agreement.
