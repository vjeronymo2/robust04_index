Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An-Nawar [Feb] 22, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- The faction of Golboddin Hekmatyar which presently surrounds the Afghanistan capital Kabul has announced that it will not lift its blockade which denies the city any relief supplies. The faction was able to intensify its blockade within the pattern of stuggle for power between what were once called mojahedin and later became political rivals whose conflict is systematically destroying the country.
