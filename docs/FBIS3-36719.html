Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev is continuing his visit to Kazakhstan. [Correspondent Dukesh Baimbetov] Kazakhstan President Nursultan Nazarbayev and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev debated a number of most important documents during a three-hour private conversation. These are a package of humanitarian agreements, issues of settling the migration processes, issues of military and space cooperation, and the problem of Baykonur. Incidentally, Kozyrev will visit the cosmodrome Baykonur tomorrow. [Begin Kozyrev recording] Experts, expert groups, and above all legal specialists rather than demagogues and loud-mouths, in other words, people who know what this is all about will now sit down and make use of international experience and analyze our laws and the needs of specific people and will work out the relevant agreement and I assure you that it will have answers to 90 percent of the questions which specific people are raising. [end recording]
