Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- As RUKH-PRESS reported on 14 March, NRU [People's Movement of Ukraine, Rukh] Chairman Vyacheslav Chornovil declared his readiness to stand for Ukraine's president in the June 1994 election if Rukh approves such a decision. According to him, "the program of Rukh, supported by the people, can become a program of transformations in Ukraine." The final decision on the participation of Vyacheslav Chornovil in the election will be approved at the second stage of the 5th all-Ukrainian Assembly of Rukh due to begin on 16 April.
