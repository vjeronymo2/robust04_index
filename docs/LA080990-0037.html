August 9, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

ZSA ZSA GABOR IN JAIL

What? A 72-year-old woman was jailed for fighting back with a cop who roughed her up when she tried to defend herself against his manhandling. Terrible! What's the U.S. coming to? Oh, it was that loud-mouthed Zsa Zsa Gabor? That's different. Let her eat TV dinners in jail.

JAN KNOWLTON

Anaheim
