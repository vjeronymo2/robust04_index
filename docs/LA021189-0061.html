February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

REBEL ROUSTERS MEET TONIGHT AT IRVINE

Cal State Fullerton and UC Irvine, two teams that have upset Nevada Las Vegas in the past week, meet at 7:30 tonight at Irvine.

Fullerton and Irvine are the only Big West Conference teams that have beaten UNLV this season. Irvine beat UNLV, 99-98, last Saturday night. Fullerton pulled a 93-92 overtime upset Thursday night on Wayne Williams' three-point shot at the buzzer.

Irvine (9-11, 6-5) won the first meeting with Fullerton this season, taking a 77-73 victory Feb. 2, in a game in which center Ricky Butler scored a career-high 23 points. Since then, Butler has scored at least 20 points in two other Irvine victories.

The Anteaters, who have won three games in a row, will be trying to put together a four-game winning streak for the first time since January, 1987.

Fullerton (10-10, 5-6) has won two straight since the loss to Irvine, upsetting UC Santa Barbara and UNLV. The Titans' Cedric Ceballos, a 6-foot-7 forward, is leading the Big West in scoring, averaging 21.5 points a game. ROBYN NORWOOD
