Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The decree on polling citizens issued by the Crimean president on 10 March prompted yesterday's fourth session of the Central Electoral Committee of the Crimea. In the course of the preceding sessions held jointly with presidential representatives, the committee drafted a compromise version of the decree on holding the poll. The Central Electoral Committee resolved to consider it possible to grant organizational support to President Meshkov in holding the poll in such a way as not to contradict laws or endanger the elections.
