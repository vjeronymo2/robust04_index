Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 11 KYODO -- Prosecutors arrested former Construction Minister Kishiro Nakamura Friday [11 March] on suspicion of taking a 10 million yen bribe from leading contractor Kajima Corp. He is the first Diet member to be arrested in a widening scandal involving the construction industry. The arrest of the former senior member of the main opposition Liberal Democratic Party came after the House of Representatives agreed earlier in the day to lift his parliamentary immunity. Legislators cannot be arrested while the Diet is in session unless parliament gives its approval. Nakamura surrendered himself to the Tokyo District Prosecutors office.
