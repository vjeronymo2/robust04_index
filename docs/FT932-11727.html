930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Letter: Cancer not a male preserve You are taking male chauvinism a bit far when you publish an article headlined 'Stacking the odds against cancer' (April 28), which includes three paragraphs on prostate cancer but nothing on breast, ovarian and cervical cancers which affect more than 10 per cent of UK women. Elisabeth Balsom, 16 Coalecroft Road, London SW15 6LP
