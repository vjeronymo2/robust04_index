June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

HOMEMADE GYROCOPTER CRASHES, KILLING 2 PEOPLE

A homemade gyrocopter crashed into a lake at Hueston Woods State Park on Saturday, killing two people, authorities said. Investigators were seeking the cause of the crash.

Gyrocopters differ from helicopters in that a helicopter's engine drives the overhead rotor, while a gyrocopter's engine drives a rear rotor.
