December 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THREE POINTS OF LIGHT FROM JOHN ROBINSON

The snipers will probably be all over Jim Murray regarding his comments on the fake field goal call by John Robinson.

Those will be the people who read Mike Downey. Jim Murray is right, as usual. This would have been a great call in the first game of the season, but in a game like that, you go for the points.

GEORGE FRENCH, Torrance
