930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / International Company News: OGC flotation OGC International, the North Sea construction and project management group, is coming to market on a historic multiple of 11.9 at the issue price of 130p. In its prospectus the Aberdeen-based company, valued at Pounds 78m, indicated a notional gross dividend yield of 4.2 per cent. The offer closes on May 19 and dealings are expected to begin on June 1.
