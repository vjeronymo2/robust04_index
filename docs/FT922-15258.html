920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Ash & Lacy declines to Pounds 4.8m DESPITE A maintained trading margin, Ash & Lacy, the West Midlands-based galvanising and metal pressing group, reported a 5 per cent decline in annual pre-tax profits. The outcome for the 12 months to December 27 - Pounds 4.81m against Pounds 5.05m - came on turnover of Pounds 54.5m (Pounds 56.3m). After tax of Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.55m) and minorities of Pounds 51,000 (Pounds 18,000) earnings emerged at 13.12p (13.53p) per share. The final dividend is held at 3.9p for a maintained total of 6.4p.
