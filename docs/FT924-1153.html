921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value rises at Brunner Brunner Investment Trust had a net asset value of 203.13p at November 30 compared with 174.74p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 3.47m (Pounds 3.5m), equivalent to earnings per share of 5.43p (5.46p). A final dividend of 2.65p (2.55p) is proposed for an increased total of 5p (4.75p).
