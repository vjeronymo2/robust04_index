Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Dirk van Zyl] [Text] Cape Town March 7 SAPA -- The Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] gained its fourteenth MP on Monday when James Kuiler, MP for Manenberg, announced his resignation from the National Party [NP]. Mr Kuiler entered the House of Representatives in 1989 as a Labour Party MP, and in 1991 joined the NP. The IFP now has five members in the House of Assembly, seven in the House of Delegates and two in the House of Representatives.
