921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Heritage ministry staff to relocate THE Department of National Heritage announced plans yesterday to move its headquarters to 2-4 Cockspur Street, close to London's Trafalgar Square. The 66,800 sq ft building will accommodate about 250 people. The department's staff, who now occupy seven different buildings around Whitehall, will move next spring. The rest of the 350 staff will be based at Grove House in nearby Orange Street, which is already used by the department.
