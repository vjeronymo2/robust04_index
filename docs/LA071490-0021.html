July 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

TINKHAM MADE SPECIAL CONTRIBUTION TO LIFE

I had the pleasure of attending Hollywood High School with Harley Tinkham in the late '30s and USC in the early '40s.

"Ace" was an integral part of what was probably the most fanatic quartet of track fans that ever lived -- Tinkham, Dwain Esper, Gordon Candee and Jason Robards Jr.

I feel sure that when the rest of us sports fans check out, Harley will be there at the finish line, stopwatch in hand.

BILL FRANZ

Tarzana
