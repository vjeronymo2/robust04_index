January 13, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRITISH ROYAL BALLET CANCELS TOUR OF U.S. THAT INCLUDED O.C. STOP

The Royal Ballet of Great Britain has dropped plans to tour the United States in July and August, saying its unable to raise enough money, according to a story in the Friday edition of the New York Times.

The tour, announced by the company in August, was to have included the Orange County Performing Arts Center as the last of three stops. The tour would have begun in New York and moved to Washington.

The Royal Ballet last visited Southern California in 1979. CHRIS PASLES
