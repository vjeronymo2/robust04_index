930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Tory ex-minister slams union bill MPs last night backed moves which critics claim will allow employers to pay union members lower wages than non-members, despite a sharp attack by Mr Peter Bottomley, the former Tory employment minister. An amendment to the Trade Union Reform and Employment Rights Bill was approved by 297 votes to 275, with Mr Bottomley voting against.
