940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / International Company News: Genting advances 19% at half-time Genting, the diversified Malaysian gaming, plantations and rubber products group, has reported pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30 of MDollars 564m (Dollars 217m), a rise of 19 per cent on the previous year. Turnover for the group increased 12 per cent to MDollars 1.08bn. Investment and interest income rose 5 per cent to MDollars 56m. The bulk of Genting's profits came once again from its listed Resorts World group, which announced pre-tax profits of MDollars 406m for the period, a rise of 17 per cent. Resorts runs the gaming and leisure side of Genting, centred on the large casino and entertainment complex in the highlands outside Kuala Lumpur. Asiatic Development, the listed company which groups Genting's plantations interests, reported pre-tax profits for the six months of MDollars 15m, an 8 per cent drop on the previous figure.
