Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich has arrived in Moscow Friday to meet unofficially Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chenomyrdin. Reliable sources in the Russian Government have told Interfax that the talk in private took place in a government residence near Moscow. The talks centered on the unification of the two countries' monetary systems. The Belarussian side insisted on finalizing the talks on the subject and on drafting relevant documents and on setting the date. Interfax has no information about the position of the Russian side. Kebich plans to return to Minsk late at night on Friday.
