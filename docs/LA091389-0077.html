September 13, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Marvin Barnes, former National Basketball Assn. player, was ordered to stand trial in San Diego Municipal Court on charges of petty theft and resisting arrest after allegedly stealing videotapes and lingerie from an adult video and bookstore.

Barnes, 37, who played for Detroit, Buffalo, Boston and San Diego in the NBA, allegedly entered the store on several occasions in July, each time taking merchandise he did not pay for.
