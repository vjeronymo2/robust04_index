April 29, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

VACCINATION PRICES

I am responding to a recent letter from a woman who felt that more children would receive their vaccinations if the prices charged by private physicians were more in line with what they used to be.

When I started pediatric practice in Orange County about 17 years ago, vaccines were supplied free by the county health department to physicians who agreed not to charge for them.

Many things economic have changed over time.

Today there is only one source for measles-mumps-rubella vaccine. The price per dose that I must pay is $24.11. Please note that this price includes $4.44 federal excise tax to fund the federal vaccine injury program. The $24.11 cost does not include the cost of a syringe or other office overhead.

DR. MARTIN J. ROSENFELD

Santa Ana
