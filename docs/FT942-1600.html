940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Exceptionals push LPA to Pounds 88,000 loss Exceptional losses on the disposal of its Acrokool drinking water fountain subsidiary pushed LPA Industries, the electrical accessory group, to a pre-tax loss of Pounds 88,000 for the half year to end-March against a profit of Pounds 18,000. The Pounds 142,000 exceptional included a Pounds 39,000 loss on disposal and a Pounds 103,000 write off of goodwill. Turnover was Pounds 2.6m (Pounds 2.35m). Earnings per share from continuing operations came out at 0.45p (0.31p) and there is a reduced interim dividend of 0.8p (1.65p).
