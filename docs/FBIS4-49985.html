BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren met here today with Philip Leigh Tose, chairman of the Hong Kong Peregrine Investments Holdings Limited. Rong said that expansion of China's opening up is an important aspect of its economic development, in the process of which China needs to attract foreign investments as well as upgrade its technology and reform old enterprises, through introduction of international advanced science and technology and management. He expressed hope that Peregrine would play its role in China's economic construction and forge closer ties with Chinese enterprises both in the mainland and in Hong Kong. Tose, who is leading a party here to attend the 1994 China summit meeting scheduled to open tomorrow, said that the future of his firm is closely related to the future of China. "China will play a bigger role in the world in the next century," he noted. "We are full of confidence in the future of Hong Kong."
