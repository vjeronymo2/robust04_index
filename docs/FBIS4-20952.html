BFN [Text] Coca producers from Cochabamba's tropical region have suspended their peasant and self-defense patrols following the signing of an agreement with the government. They will maintain the alert, however, until the government fulfills its promises. The report was provided by coca producers leader Evo Morales to Fides News Agency. Morales said they will fulfill their part of the agreement with the government and will wait a prudent period of time to see the results of the alternative development program.
