Language: Swedish Article Type:BFN [Text] Security police and the customs have increased border control in Norrbotten [northeastern province] to stop uranium smuggling through Sweden. This is taking place after a warning from the Russian police that one kilo of uranium for nuclear submarines has been stolen in Murmansk. Saepo chief (Bo Beckman) warns that the uranium can come into Sweden. The customs is now making random checks of radioactive radiation at the borders in the north.
