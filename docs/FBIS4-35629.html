BFN [Text] In Sanaa, an authoritative source has said that the Yemeni Government will hold an extraordinary meeting today in the wake of the resolution issued by the United Nations Security Council, which calls for a cease-fire in Yemen. The same source added that a scheduled news conference today has been postponed due to the convening of the emergency meeting of the Council of Ministers in which Planning Minister 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani will announce Sanaa's stand on the Security Council resolution.
