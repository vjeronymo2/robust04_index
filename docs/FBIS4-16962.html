BFN [Text] On 20 April, Ukraine's Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko completed his official visit to India. In the afternoon he met India's President Shankar Dayal Sharma. The sides, in particular, expressed their satisfaction with the fact that relations between the two countries are developing actively on the principles of traditional friendly relations and mutual understanding. In their opinion, the agreements on economic and technical cooperation, and on the international commission for trade, economic, scientific, technical, and cultural cooperation between the two countries, signed in Delhi, contributed significantly to their further development.
