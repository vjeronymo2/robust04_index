September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

NEW CANCER SUPPORT CENTER

Emotional and psychological support for cancer patients and their families is being offered free at the Wellness Community Foothills, 100 N. Hill Ave., which opened Aug. 23.

More than 300 clients are expected to be served each month by the nonprofit agency, Executive Director Anne Kennedy said.

The Pasadena clinic is affiliated with eight Wellness Community offices operating in other parts of the country. Services provided include relaxation techniques and group therapy.
