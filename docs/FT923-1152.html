920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / UK Company News: ISA Intl improves 28% to Pounds 1.32m ISA International, the Bradford-based computer consumables supplier, lifted pre-tax profits by 28 per cent - from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 1.32m - in the six months to June 30. Mr John Parkinson, chairman, said the German and French subsidiaries 'performed well in all respects' while Datarekvisita of Norway, acquired for Pounds 1.4m cash in April, was 'progressing well'. UK operations, the end-user division apart, had staged a recovery. Following the Norwegian purchase and Pounds 1.1m of goodwill written off, gearing rose from about 30 per cent to 45 per cent. Turnover advanced 45 per cent to Pounds 52.3m (Pounds 36m). Earnings per share emerged at 2.708p (1.942p) and the interim dividend goes up to 0.482p (0.414p).
