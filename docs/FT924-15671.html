921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (32): Care Business Solutions - Product Profile Supplier: Care Business Solutions was founded in 1986. Its turnover is around Pounds 2.5m. There are 30 employees in the UK. Care Business Solutions specialises in the provision of open, turnkey computer systems for a wide range of computing requirements. It particularly supplies software for associations - providing a range of systems to federations, institutes, unions, charities and registration bodies. Care also has expertise in applications for printing and publishing. Number of product users: 250; number of sites: 50. Hardware and operating systems: Unix, DOS, VMS, Unicos, HP, Wyse. Sales and support: UK only. Other services include: installation, training, consultancy, systems development. User views: 'A tailored solution . . . very close to requirements.' Contact point: Care Business Solutions, Godalming Business Centre, Woolsack Way, Godalming, Surrey; tel. 0483 860001.
