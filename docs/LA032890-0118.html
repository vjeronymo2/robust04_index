March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PINNACLE: During the post-Watergate years, former President Richard M. Nixon writes in this week's Time, golf was his "lifesaver."

.. . But he abruptly quit the game after breaking 80 on a relatively easy San Clemente golf course. "For me," he writes, "it was like climbing Mount Everest. I knew I could never get better." . . . Nixon returns to Orange County on July 19 for the opening ceremonies of his presidential library in Yorba Linda. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
