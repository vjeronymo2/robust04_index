Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks about a free trade agreement will begin today in Brussels between the European Union and Estonia. The Estonian delegation is led by Priit Kolbre, head of the Foreign Ministry's foreign trade policy department. The first round of talks will last one day, during which the delegations will clarify their opinions about the contents of the agreement. It is known already that the European Union will provide Estonia completely free trade in respect of industrial goods, textile products, and agricultural produce.
