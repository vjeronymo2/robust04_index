930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: Headquarters for PowerGen LAING MIDLANDS has won the contract to build new operational headquarters for PowerGen in Coventry. The construction contract is part of a Pounds 16m package which covers the land purchase and shell and core building - which is due to be completed in summer, 1994. The building, at Westwood Business Park on the outskirts of Coventry, will have three floors and cover an area of 12,700 sq metres. There will be an in situ concrete frame with central atrium in steelwork and glazing.
