BFN [Text] India has made contingency plans to airlift its nationals from South Africa if the need arose. The step comes amid the uncertain political situation in South Africa and the recent violence in the black homeland in Bophuthatswana, where the houses of three Indian nationals were razed to the ground. Indian consular spokesman Thettalil Seetharam appealed to Indian nationals residing in South Africa to register with the newly-established consulate. Mr. Seetharam said appropriate steps would be taken if South African Indians were affected by the violence.
