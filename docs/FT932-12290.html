930429 FT 29 APR 93 / BA crews to vote on strike action BRITISH AIRWAYS short-haul cabin crews at London's Gatwick airport who are members of the TGWU general union will vote this week over industrial action against BA's plans to transfer their flight routes to a subsidiary company which the crews say offers inferior conditions of employment. The ballot result will be known on Friday. The union will also ballot all its 18,000 UK ground staff, clerical workers and cabin crew employed by BA over industrial action against contracting-out of airline services and creation of subsidiary companies. The result is expected next month.
