930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Canadian group in telecom link TELUS Corporation, the Alberta telephone company, has formed a partnership with CUC Broadcasting of Edmonton to develop six cable TV franchises presently held by CUC in the UK. The franchises cover 620,000 households in the Thames Valley, Swindon, Coventry, Warwick and Northampton and a number of other areas north-west of London. Telus said that 34 per cent of the households are cable subscribers.
