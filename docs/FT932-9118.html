930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / Norway unveils fiscal measures Norway yesterday unveiled adjustments to its 1993 fiscal budget which pushed the deficit to a record NKr53.9bn (Pounds 5.1bn), up from December's forecast deficit of NKr51.4bn, writes Karen Fossli in Oslo. The minority Labour government also announced plans gradually to reprivatise the commercial banks in 1994, beginning with wholly state-owned Christiania Bank, the country's second biggest bank.
