September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Mason on HA!: "HA! The TV Comedy Network" has signed Jackie Mason to host a one-man talk show to air in December. The Monday-through-Friday half-hour show "Just Jackie" will be taped in front of a studio audience from the Ed Sullivan Theatre in New York, the network announced Wednesday. The comedian-philosopher will hold discussions on current topics and interact with the audience. BETH KLEID
