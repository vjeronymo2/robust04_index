930421 FT 21 APR 93 / UK Company News: Acquisitions help Shani rise 74% to Pounds 1.34m Shani Group, the ladies' and childrens' wear manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits ahead 74 per cent at Pounds 1.34m, against Pounds 770,000, in the six months to January 31. The result reflected a strong performance by coat and suit manufacturing acquisitions. There was some improvement in the core businesses despite competitive pricing required by customers. Turnover for this USM-quoted company more than doubled to Pounds 11.5m. The pre-tax figure included lower interest receivable of Pounds 186,000 (Pounds 271,000). Earnings per share came out at 6.3p (2.6p) and an increased interim dividend of 1.8p (1.6p) is declared. Mr Martin Hollis, chairman, said that the second half had started well and he looked forward with confidence to a successful outcome for the year.
