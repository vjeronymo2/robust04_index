910430 FT 30 APR 91 / International Company News: Polish group still confident UNIVERSAL, a Polish foreign trade company privatised last year, has reported an after-tax profit of 205.9bn zlotys (Dollars 22m) for 1990, of which 58 per cent is to be paid to shareholders as a dividend of 8,000 zlotys per share, writes Christopher Bobinski in Warsaw. When the company was privatised last summer the share price was 20,000 zlotys. The current price is 38,200. The company plans to spend 115bn zlotys on state food processing plants, a metal works and on property and retail trade investments. Mr Dariusz Przywieczerski, managing director, said he was 'confident' about this year's results.
