May 6, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MEDICAL RESEARCH AND ANIMAL RIGHTS

May I quote: "Frankly, I don't shed a tear when I hear a lab has been broken into." Me, too!

Hooray for animal rights groups! They speak up for the billions of animals wasted by the "good old boy" researchers. Methinks the researchers, and their system of grants, are nervous. Reminds me of Congress.

RUTH UNGER

Los Angeles
