Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 21 (CTK) -- Slovak Premier Jozef Moravcik told Czech Premier Vaclav Klaus he would propose postponing the requirement for import certificates for one month at the next meeting with his government, he told journalists today. Moravcik was in Prague for several hours tonight on the occasion of the opening of the European Banking Forum. Moravcik also said he and Klaus agreed that the customs union between the two countries needed to be developed. The two premiers spoke as well about the topic of the countries' borders, and Moravcik noted that his government intended to re-evaluate the previous government's decision in that area.
