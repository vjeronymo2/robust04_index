Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The quaestorship of the Senate of the Republic offers congratulations to staff members and advises that in view of the tense situation developing within the administration since the return from abroad of the members of the office dismissed by resolution dated 4 February, it was decided to ask the staff to remain at home until further notice to allow the senators to settle their dispute. This communique is signed by Dr. Osny Eugene, senator of the Republic and quaestor of the Senate office presided over by Bernard Sansaricq.
