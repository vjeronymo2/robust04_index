940407 FT 07 APR 94 / UK Company News: Slough pays Pounds 15m for industrial estate Slough Estates, the industrial and commercial property developer, has paid Pounds 15.3m for the Railway Triangle, an industrial estate adjacent to the M27 motorway in Portsmouth. The property, a long leasehold from Portsmouth City Council, was acquired from Universities Superannuation Scheme. It comprises 35 buildings with an annual net income of about Pounds 1.31m.
