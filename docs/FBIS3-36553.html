Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report published under the rubric "Moment": "The President -- Candidate"] [Text] The international organization "Chernobyl Association" has expressed its support for the position occupied by Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk in connection with the signing of the Tripartite Statement by the presidents of Ukraine, the United States, and Russia regarding the settlement of the problems of nuclear weapons in Ukraine. The president of the organization, People's Deputy V. Shovkoshytnyy, wrote the following in his letter to the Ukrainian president: "The choice that you have made is correct, and we express the hope that future generations will appreciate it." The letter also states that the "Chernobyl Association" supports the initiative of Ukraine's Green Party to nominate Leonid Kravchuk as a candidate for the Nobel Peace prize.
