Internal Revenue Service OMB Number: 1545&hyph;0122 Form Number: IRS Form 1118, Schedule I and Schedule J Type of Review: Revision Title: Foreign Tax Credit_Corporations Description: Form 1118 and separate Schedules I and J are used by domestic and foreign corporations to claim a credit against tax for taxes paid to foreign countries. The IRS uses Form 1118 and related schedules to determine if the corporation has computed the foreign tax credit correctly.
