940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / Finance and the Family: Debutantes find favour - At a glance As House of Fraser led the latest batch of new issues towards the stock market (see page IV), this week's debutantes won a generally favourable reception. Premiums on the first day's trading ranged from just under 4 per cent for Applied Distribution to more than 40 per cent for Waste Recycling.
