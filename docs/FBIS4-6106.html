BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 16 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Staying here on an official visit at the invitation of the President of Mongolia the Governor General of Australia B. Hayden has met today with newsmen. During the meeting B. Hayden has noted the fruitful cooperation of the two countries and the opportunities available for its furthering as well. He pointed out in his speech that Australia actively takes part in the activities of the countries rendering assistance and support to Mongolia and said that the Government of Australia is interested in the cooperation with Mongolia in the sphere of agriculture, mining industry and use of natural resources of Mongolia. B. Hayden noted that as a country pertaining to the Asian and Pacific region Australia has been paying much attention to the development of its relations with the countries of the region. The honoured guest also gave answers to the questions of interest for newsmen.
