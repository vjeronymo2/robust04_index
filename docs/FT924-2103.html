921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: MP's Bosnia plea A BRITISH initiative to secure action through the United Nations to halt the atrocities and killing being inflicted on Bosnia by the Serbs was demanded by Mr Patrick Cormack (C Staffordshire South) in the Commons last night. He warned that unless he received a firm ministerial assurance that renewed pressure would be brought on the UN before the Christmas recess on Thursday 'the government cannot be sure of my vote on anything'.
