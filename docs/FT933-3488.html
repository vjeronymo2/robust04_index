930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / Collective pay deals still common JUST 13 per cent of companies want to take advantage of recent legislation to offer financial incentives to employees who switch to individual contracts, according to Personnel Today magazine. Of the 275 personnel specialists surveyed, 57 per cent still negotiate collectively with their staff. Of those, 84 per cent have no plans to replace collective agreements with individual contracts over the next two years.
