921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Board changes after Courtyard Leis loss Courtyard Leisure, USM-quoted restaurant and wine bar operator, yesterday announced board changes in the light of annual pre-tax losses of Pounds 288,475. Mr Paul Bowskill has resigned as a director and Mr Ali Safa has been appointed as joint managing director. He holds 705,000 shares in the company and is a director of TW Consultants. Earlier this year bid talks were discontinued with TW, which speaks for 27.78 per cent of Courtyard. Sales for the year to March 31 were up 21 per cent at Pounds 1.9m (Pounds 1.57m). But interest receivable fell to Pounds 15,024 (Pounds 103,242). There is no dividend payment for the year; last year 0.5p was paid.
