BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Wang Bingqian, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met here today with a delegation from the Military Committee of the Thai Senate. The 11-member delegation, headed by Prapan Dhupatemiya [name as received], chairman of the committee, arrived here on May 7 as guest of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the NPC.
