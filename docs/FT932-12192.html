930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Murray Split Capital shows total return of 32% OVER THE SIX months ended February 28 assets of Murray Split Capital Trust increased 28 per cent for a total return, including dividends, of 32 per cent. That compared with a return of 30 per cent from the FT-A All-Share Index. At the end of February net asset value per zero dividend preference share was 118.8p (112.6p), per income share 50.2p (51.2p) and per capital share 172.2p (94.6p). The performance reflected the strength of the high yield shares since interest rates began to fall last autumn. Total revenue was Pounds 580,000 in the six months and earnings worked through at 4.23p. The second quarterly dividend is 2.65p to make 5.3p to date. It is intended to maintain the annualised rate at 10.6p.
