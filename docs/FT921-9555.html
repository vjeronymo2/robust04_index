920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Gartmore American income expands Gartmore American Securities achieved improved total revenue in the nine months to December 31 1991, amounting to Pounds 2.12m, against Pounds 1.92m last time. Earnings per share worked through at 3p (2.8p). The increased interim dividend - 1p, compared with 0.9p - was announced last month. At the end of 1991, net asset value per share was 34.2p, compared with 41.8p at the end of September.
