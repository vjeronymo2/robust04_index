Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Hungarian Foreign Minister] Geza Jeszenszky has said that Hungary sincerely supports the Croatian-Muslim reconciliation because these two nations are dependent on each other more than other countries of the region. The Hungarian foreign minister, who is currently holding talks in Washington, is of the opinion that, if the canton system enabling the coexistence of Croats and Muslims comes into prominence again in the case of Bosnia, the example, in a wider sense, will probably be followed elsewhere as well.
