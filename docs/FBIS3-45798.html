Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, January 19 (XINHUA) -- Some 20 international exhibitions will be held this year in Shanghai, the leading industrial city of China. The exhibitions will cover aviation equipment, medical facilities, building materials, tailoring machinery and commodities for daily use. The participants will be businessmen and enterprises mainly from other economically developed countries. Zhang Renren, general manager of Shanghai International Exhibition Company which will organize all the exhibitions, said that these exhibitions will have great influence on the city's economy and also show that the city is attractive to the world.
