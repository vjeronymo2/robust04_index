931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / (CORRECTED) UK Company News: Automagic Correction (published 10th November 1993) appended to this article. Automagic Holdings, the USM-quoted shoe repairing and key-cutting retail chain, continued its recovery in the second half, ending the year with a pre-tax profit after exceptionals of Pounds 122,000. The outcome for the 12 months to June 26 compared with a loss of Pounds 1.28m last time and with a profit of Pounds 76,000 at the interim stage. It was struck on turnover down slightly at Pounds 11.5m (Pounds 11.8m). Earnings per share came out at 2p (20.1p losses). No dividends have been paid since May 1991. The shares closed up 2p at 15p. CORRECTION Shares in Automagic Holdings rose 14p to 27p on Monday November 8, and not 2p to 15p as reported.
