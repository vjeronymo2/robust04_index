August 28, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FERGUSON'S FOLLY

I cannot believe that Assemblyman Gil Ferguson (R-Newport Beach) refuses to let old wounds heal ("Japanese Internment Argument Is Revived," Aug. 17).

Added to his earlier virulent anti-homosexual statements, we now have his racist stance with regard to Japanese Americans who were interned at various camps in the United States.

Is Ferguson from the Dark Ages? He obviously has no sensitivity. I cannot understand how anyone can reelect him to any office.

CRAIG G. MYERS

Santa Ana
