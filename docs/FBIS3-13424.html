Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Despite the closure on the territories, the number of workers who have received permits to enter Israel has recently been increased. Officials at the Employment Service told our correspondent Ya'el Ayalon that over the weekend, another 5,300 workers were issued permits, bringing the total number of agricultural and industrial workers from the territories with permits to work in Israel to 9,800. From now on, all workers from the territories over the age of 30 will be eligible for a work permit on the condition that they are married.
