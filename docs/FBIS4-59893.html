BFN [Excerpt] The revolutionary council of the Palestinian National Liberation Movement, Fatah, held its 12th ordinary session in Tunis. It was the martyr-leader Abu al-Mundhir and the Khalil al-Rahman martyrs' session held in the period between 16 and 17 May. The council heard the report of the central committee, read out by brother Abu al-Lutf, as well as the reports of the committees related to various activities. Brother President Yasir 'Arafat spoke in detail about what happened from Madrid to Cairo, and what has been realized, the entry of the Palestinian security forces to the homeland and the popular welcome accorded to these forces, as well as about the need for the solidarity of all to care for the newly-born Palestinian Authority which needs the arms of all cadres and leaderships. 'Arafat spoke also about the importance of the historic step achieved as a result of the sacrifice of thousands of martyrs, injured, prisoners, and detainees. [passage omitted]
