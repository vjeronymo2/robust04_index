930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Major cautious on Ulster envoy MR JOHN Major refused yesterday to rule out the possibility of a US 'fact-finding' envoy to Northern Ireland meeting representatives of Sinn Fein, the political wing of the IRA. But Mr Major, who last night met with a number of Ulster MPs in advance of a possible announcement from Washington, urged the Rev Ian Paisley, leader of the Democratic Unionists, to 'wait and see' what announcement was made about a US fact-finder.
