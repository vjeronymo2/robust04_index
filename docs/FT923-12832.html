920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Leslie Wise 12% ahead at Pounds 1.51m In spite of unfavourable trading conditions Leslie Wise, the textiles, women's clothing and knitted fabrics group, lifted profits from Pounds 1.35m to Pounds 1.51m pre-tax for the year to May 31. The near-12 per cent advance was scored from a 25 per cent rise in sales to Pounds 22.8m. Exports also rose by 25 per cent. Margins were maintained leading to a 22 per cent rise in operating profits to Pounds 1.65m. Earnings increased to 2.96p (2.7p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 1.75p.
