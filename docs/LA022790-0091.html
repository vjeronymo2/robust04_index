February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

WEST GERMANY;

30 U.S. YOUTHS HURT AS BUS LEAVES ROAD

A tour bus carrying American youths to a recreation center plunged from the road down a 130-foot embankment near the Rhine River city of Oberwesel, injuring 30 of the passengers, police said. Twenty of the youths were seriously hurt, and 10 others suffered minor injuries, a police spokesman said. He added that authorities had not immediately determined whether the Americans were tourists or relatives of military or diplomatic personnel in West Germany.
