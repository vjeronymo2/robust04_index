BFN [Article signed JCH/ALN: "Full Text of Official Press Release of Limited Belgian Council of Ministers Meeting"] [Text] Brussels, 8 Apr (BELGA) -- "The government met with Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene in the chair, to review the situation in Rwanda. ``The government rendered homage to the courage of the Belgian troops serving in UNAMIR [UN Peacekeeping Force in Rwanda]. It expressed its indignation at the murder of ten Belgian blue helmets who died while peacefully carrying out their mission. He repeated his deep condolences to the victims' families. ``On behalf of the government, the prime minister and the foreign minister have expressed to UN General Secretary Butrus-Ghali, through personal contacts, the imperative need to reinforce the safety of UN forces in Rwanda. The secretary general fully shares this concern. ``The government is moreover extremely concerned about the deterioration of the local situation, and particularly the threats against our compatriots. In consequence, it has decided on the necessary measures to be taken to guarantee the safety of Belgian nationals. A crisis center has been opened at the Foreign Ministry.''
