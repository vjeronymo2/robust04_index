940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Sharpe & Fisher Pounds 2.4m purchases Sharpe & Fisher, the Cheltenham-based building supplies concern, has purchased Gammon & Smith of Petersfield for Pounds 447,000 cash. In a related transaction, also for cash, the company has acquired two freehold and two leasehold properties for Pounds 1.95m. Gammon achieved turnover of Pounds 1.7m for the year ended March 31.
