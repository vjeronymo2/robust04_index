CSO [Report by KRASNAYA ZVEZDA correspondent Vitaliy Denisov under the rubric "Correspondents of KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and Telegraph Agencies Report": "Peacekeepers Are Prepared To Enter Abkhasia"] [Text] Lt-Gen Aleksey Sokolov, deputy commander in chief of Russia's Ground Troops, has arrived at Gudauta in the area of the Georgian-Abkhasian conflict in connection with the imminent deployment of CIS peacekeeping forces there. It is expected that he will meet with Georgia's leaders in Tbilisi on 28 May. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss issues pertaining to the input and deployment of Russian "Blue Helmets" in the area of the conflict.
