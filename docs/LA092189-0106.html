September 21, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

VIDEO TO BE AVAILABLE

Copies of a 17-minute video, "Pasadena: a Special Place," will soon be available from the city's Communications Department for $10.

The Board of Directors on Tuesday approved preparation of copies of a video produced by local public relations people. Calling itself the Pasadena Toward 2000 Committee, the group was formed in December, 1986, and has also published an index of community agencies titled "Focus on Pasadena."

The video is not copyrighted so that businesses can use it in their promotions.
