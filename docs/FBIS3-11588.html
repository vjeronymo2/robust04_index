Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 22 Mar (NA) -- Media Secretary Raul Burzaco has reported that the medical checkup President Carlos Menem underwent this morning "shows his health is normal." Burzaco released a communique, signed by Physicians Alejandro Tfeli, Juan Carlos Parodi, and Cesar Gnocchi, stating that "today, President Carlos Menem underwent a medical checkup. All the tests performed show his health is normal."
