BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, held telephone conversations with Jordanian King Husayn, the servant of the two holy mosques, Saudi Arabian King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, and Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. He briefed them on developments in Yemen, particularly the military clashes. He commended the efforts being exerted by the Arab brothers to contain the war which was sparked off by the al-Ahmar clan against the people and unity.
