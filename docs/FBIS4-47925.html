BFN [Text] (Beira) A reliable UN Operations in Mozambique, Unomoz, source in Sofala Province said that, whatever the real intentions of the Mozambican Government and the Mozambique National Resistance, Renamo, and irrespective of President of the Republic Joaquim Chissano's remarks in Tete Province, the troop demobilization process continued yesterday and is scheduled to continue today, after a break of several days.
