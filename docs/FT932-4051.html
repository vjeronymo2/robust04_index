930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UN soldiers patrol Mogadishu following looting incidents UN soldiers from Pakistan patrol the Somali capital Mogadishu after looters had rampaged through offices of Muslim Relief UK, an aid agency, in the first serious outbreak of looting in the capital since December. Following the killing of 23 Pakistani 'blue helmets' on Saturday, most aid workers have been evacuated or moved into freshly fortified compounds. Meanwhile, four US military warplanes have arrived in neighbouring Djibouti to take part in a possible retaliation for the killings
