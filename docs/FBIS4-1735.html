BFN [Text] Beijing, April 2 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Minister of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation Wu Yi met here this morning with visiting Australian Foreign Minister Gareth Evans and his party, and they exchanged views on bilateral economic and trade relations and other issues of common interest. Wu said that Sino-Australian economic and trade relations have been developing rapidly and well, quoting statistics from China's customs that bilateral trade volume last year reached three billion U.S. dollars, up 29 percent over the record figure of 1992. China had a 800 million U.S. dollars trade deficit with Australia last year with import at 1.9 billion U.S. dollars and export, 1.1 billion U.S. dollars. Wu said that China hopes to have a balanced trade by increasing trade. The Australian foreign minister arrived here yesterday as guest of his Chinese counterpart Qian Qichen.
