Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] inspection team arrived in Pyongyang on 1 March. The seven IAEA inspectors will stay in Yongbyon for two weeks to conduct inspection activities, such as changing and repairing film in monitoring equipment set up in the seven declared facilities and taking samples from some nuclear facilities. These IAEA activities have not taken place since February 1993.
