941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: GRT Pounds 2m purchase GRT Bus Group has announced its second acquisition in just over a month with a Pounds 2.17m recommended offer for Reiver Ventures, which operates services under the Lowland name in the Borders and east Lothian. The consideration will be met as to 50 per cent in cash, with a loan note alternative, and the remainder in shares. In addition Pounds 250,000 will be paid to directors of Reiver in compensation for changes to their service agreements. Directors speaking for 55.5 per cent of Reiver ordinary shares have undertaken to accept the ordinary share offer.
