October 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

WOMEN IN MEN'S LOCKER ROOMS

There appear to be three logical alternative to resolve the current controversy over women reporters conducting their interviews in the men's locker room.

1. Provide an interview room for all reporters; 2. Install a communications system that women can use from outside of the locker room, or 3. Require all reporters to disrobe before entering the locker room.

Hopefully, a compromise will be reached before we find Barbara Walters interviewing Mike Tyson in the men's room at Los Angeles International Airport!

RICK MEASE, Carpinteria
