Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "`Elections of Local Administration Heads Undesirable at This Stage'"] [Text] Nikolay Medvedev, chief of the Russian Federation president's administration for work with the territories, said that during a transition period of two years it is essential to ensure the stability, unity, and governability of the executive authority system both in the center and in the field. Given this, he expressed his opinion that holding elections of local administration chiefs would be "undesirable," because it is on the local chiefs that "Russia's stability and the stability of the very system of state authority" will depend.
