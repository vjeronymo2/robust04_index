940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / New Independent editor sought A replacement is being sought for Mr Andreas Whittam Smith as editor of The Independent, the loss-making national daily newspaper. The search has begun, partly at least, because senior journalists at the paper have been individually approaching the main shareholders warning that editorial renewal will not be possible without changes at the top. In an unprecedented move the journalists on the papers yesterday passed overwhelmingly a motion of no confidence in the two main founders Mr Whittam Smith and Mr Matthew Symonds.
