Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a supreme decree published in the official gazette, the government has officially extended the state of emergency in the provinces of Paruyo, Comayo, Chumbivilcas, Ascona, and Canchis in Cuzco until 19 March. Consequently, the constitutional guarantees related to the inviolability of personal domicile, the right to move freely in the country, and the right to hold peaceful and unarmed meetings remain suspended. The decree dictates that such emergency measures will facilitate the antiterrorist operations in this part of the country. The Armed Forces will ensure an internal order, in accordance with the existing laws, in those provinces.
