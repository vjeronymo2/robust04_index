The CARB submitted a 1990 emissions inventory of ozone precursors as required by section 182(a)(1) on November 12, 1992. On November 18, 1993 the CARB submitted a revised 1990 emissions inventory of ozone precursors which EPA found incomplete on April 13, 1994 because the emissions inventory had not gone through an adequate public notice and hearing process. To correct the deficiency, the BAAQMD held a public hearing on July 29, 1994 (which was the maintenance plan 1990 attainment inventory submitted on November 12, 1993) and will submit the appropriate documentation to EPA. Through this document, EPA also proposes to approve the 1990 emissions inventory as required by section 182(a)(1).
