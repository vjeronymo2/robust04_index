BFN ["Defense Ministry press service report for 21 April" -- read by announcer] [Text] Armenia's Armed Forces tried to launch a new attack in northern Karabakh on the afternoon of 20 April. Having massed a considerable number of troops and hardware in this sector, the enemy launched an attack against the settlements in northern Agdamskiy Rayon. Heavy fighting continued this morning around Guludzha and (?Suma). The situation also remains tense in the southwest part of our Republic. Heavy fighting continued near Fizuli yesterday, last night, and early this morning. Our troops inflicted heavy counterblows on the enemy and forced it to withdraw from a number of heights. The fleeing enemy left behind a considerable amount of arms and military hardware. Our soldiers seized one Armenian tank, one infantry fighting vehicle, two Grad launchers, 11 cannons, five machine guns, seven radio sets, one vehicle, numerous grenade launchers, and automatic weapons.
