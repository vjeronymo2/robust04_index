940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / FT Guide to World Cup Football (13): The route to the final ---------------------------------------------------------------------- FIRST ROUND ---------------------------------------------------------------------- Teams from the 24 qualifying countries are divided into six groups (A-F). Each team plays its three group opponents once; the first two in each group plus the four third-placed teams with the best records proceed to the Second Round. Games played June 17 to June 30 at San Francisco, Los Angeles, Detroit, Chicago, Boston, Dallas, New York, Washington and Orlando. ---------------------------------------------------------------------- SECOND ROUND ---------------------------------------------------------------------- July 2, Chicago Winners Group C vs Third place team July 2, Washington Second Group C vs Second Group A July 3, Los Angeles Winners Group A vs Third place team July 3, Dallas Second Group F vs Second Group B July 4, San Francisco Winners Group B vs Third place team July 4, Orlando Winners Group F vs Second Group E July 5, Boston Winners Group D vs Third place team July 5, New York Winners Group E vs Second Group D ---------------------------------------------------------------------- QUARTER FINALS ---------------------------------------------------------------------- July 9, Boston Winners at Boston vs winners at Washington July 9, Dallas Winners at Orlando vs winners at San Francisco July 10, San Francisco Winners at Los Angeles vs winners at Dallas July 10, New York Winners at New York vs winners at Chicago ---------------------------------------------------------------------- SEMI FINALS ---------------------------------------------------------------------- July 13, Los Angeles Winners at San Francisco vs winners at Dallas July 13, New York Winners at New York vs winners at Boston ---------------------------------------------------------------------- THIRD-PLACE MATCH ---------------------------------------------------------------------- July 16, Los Angeles Losers at Los Angeles vs losers at New York ---------------------------------------------------------------------- THE FINAL ---------------------------------------------------------------------- July 17, Los Angeles Winners at Los Angeles vs winners at New York ----------------------------------------------------------------------
