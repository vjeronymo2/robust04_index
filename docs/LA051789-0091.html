May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

THE NATION

Less than a year out of Stanford, the 22-year-old daughter of new Dallas Cowboys owner Jerry Jones is making $60,000 a year as an aide to a congressman who reports owing at least $100,000 to Jones. Charlotte Jones, who holds three titles on the staff of Rep. Tommy F. Robinson (D-Ark.), said "there is absolutely no connection" between her meteoric rise in position and pay on Robinson's staff and the debt.
