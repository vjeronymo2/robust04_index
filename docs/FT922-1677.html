920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / NSW leader comes under fire Australia's Independent Commission Against Corruption yesterday found the New South Wales premier, Mr Nick Greiner, had engaged in conduct which was reasonable grounds for his dismissal, Emilia Tagaza reports from Canberra. Mr Greiner has refused to resign over accusations of involvement in the appointment of an MP to a public service job, but his minority conservative government next week faces a vote of no confidence in the state parliament.
