Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 2 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In 1992 Mongolia has made the statement on becoming a nuclear free zone. The USA, the PRC, Great Britain, and recently France have announced about their respect for this status of Mongolia, is said in the statement of the representative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation of March 1. I would like to note that position of the Russian side on this issue has been reflected in the clause 4 of the treaty on friendly relations and cooperation, which was signed with Mongolia in January 20, 1993, as follows: "The Russian Federation will have a respect for Mongolia's policy banning the stationing on her territory of foreign troops, nuclear and other weapons of mass annihilation, letting them through her territory", is said in the statement, the Ministry for External Relations of Mongolia informed about this today.
