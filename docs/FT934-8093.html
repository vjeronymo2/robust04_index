931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / Observer: Clearing house Is the National Trust of the Cayman Islands, the well-known international tax haven, experiencing an identity crisis? Its mission - preserving the countryside and historic sites - should be clear enough. But there again, inhabitants of the Caymans are more accustomed to seeing money orders, rather than preservation orders, littering their streets. Presumably that's why the Trust has taken to declaring in its latest radio advertising campaign: 'The National Trust is not a bank.'
