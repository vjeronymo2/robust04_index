BFN ["NG" report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Merger in the Cards Once Again"] [Text] According to information NEZAVISIMAYA GAZETA has received from sufficiently informed sources, there may soon appear a presidential edict on uniting Moscow and Moscow Oblast to form a single "Moscow Region." It is supposed to appoint as its head mayor of the capital Yuriy Luzhkov, who would also receive the rank of vice premier of the Russian Government. A draft of the edict is being prepared in corresponding subdivisions of City Hall, and Boris Yeltsin's agreement to sign it has virtually been secured, according to information from the same sources. Viktor Chernomyrdin, according to confidential information, does not like such plans. The reaction by Moscow Oblast administration head Anatoliy Tyazhlov is yet unknown.
