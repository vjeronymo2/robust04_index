BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a solidarity message from Nina Andreyeva, general secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Bolshevik Communist Party, on the second anniversary of the publication of the Pyongyang declaration. The solidarity message dated April 14 says the declaration is of particular significance in the struggle to defend and advance the cause of socialism and in strengthening the unity and solidarity of the international communist and working-class movements. It further says: The All-Union Bolshevik Communist Party calls for the full achievement of the aim and principles of the Pyongyang declaration and it is conducting activities to check the return to capitalism and reconstruct the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. We will, in the future, too, extend every possible political support to the peoples struggling for defence of their freedom and independence against imperialism and for the victory of socialism in our planet. The All-Union Bolshevik Communist Party highly appreciates the role of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and you, the dear leader, and the Workers' Party of Korea in the publication of the Pyongyang declaration which opened up a new phase for the unity of the world communist movement and revolutionary movement.
