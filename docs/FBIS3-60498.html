Language: Danish Article Type:CSO [Article by ``sc'': ``Donating Hospital to Romania''] [Text] (Hillerod) Frederiksborg County can now present its Romanian sister county, Brasov, an emergency hospital. Forty tons of excess hospital equipment have been waiting a year for the Romanians to pay the shipment expenses. The donation will now be sent, because the [Danish] county has decided to itself pay the 100,000-kroner transport cost.
