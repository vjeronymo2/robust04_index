Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report published under the rubric "Fact"] [Text] The commander of Ukraine's Naval Forces, Vice Admiral Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, has refuted "Ostankino" assertions about alleged plans to conduct a large-scale training exercise of Ukrainian troops in the Crimea that might influence the course of presidential elections in the autonomous republic. As reported by the Press Service of Ukraine's Ministry of Defense, between 24 and 27 January, staff training will be held for all the armed forces without the involvement of troops or technology. The training will be supervised by the supreme commander, L. Kravchuk.
