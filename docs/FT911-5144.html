910416 FT 16 APR 91 / UK Company News: Pounds 211,000 for Venturi in its first year In its first set of annual results, Venturi Investment Trust made Pounds 211,170 after tax in the year to March 31. This represents earnings per share of 3.52p. As forecast in the prospectus of March 1990 the proposed final dividend is 1.7475p to make a total of 3.06p. The net asset value of the capital indexed shares at the year-end was 245.65p, up some 11 per cent; of the income shares 25.46p (25p); and of the ordinary geared shares 9.38p undiluted and 8.93p diluted, 94 and 85 per cent ahead respectively. Mr Simon Dawson Eccles, chairman, said: 'Though never to the exclusion of current income requirements, we strive to get the assets to at least match the market, as cumulative outperformance to date is there to enhance all future years' income flow and growth.'
