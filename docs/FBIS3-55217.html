Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 6 TASS -- The Russian Central Electoral Commission announced on Thursday that new elections to the Russian parliament will be held on March 13, 1994 in the Republic of Tatarstan where less than 50 per cent of the electorate came to the polls on December 12. In the self-styled republic of Chechnya, where elections were not held at all, the new polls are also scheduled for March 13. In other Russian constituencies where December elections failed new polls will be held either on March 6 or 26.
