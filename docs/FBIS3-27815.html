Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received from party leaders of different countries messages supporting his New Year address. General Secretary Renan Raffo Munoz and International Secretary Guillermo Herrera Montecinos of the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party in their message said that the Political Committee of the Central Committee of their party adopted a decision fully supporting the New Year address. They said 1993 was a historic year in which the Democratic People's Republic of Korea won victory in the political and military confrontation with the enemy, pushed ahead with socialist economic construction, and made strenuous efforts for the reunification of the country on the principle of independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity, thus proving the validity of the revolutionary line of the Workers' Party of Korea. Madhav Kumar Nepal, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Nepal Communist Party (United Marxism-Leninism), in his message said that his party and all the progressive people of Nepal fully support the Korean people's just cause of the reunification of divided Korea and the solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula. "We hold that the United Nations should adopt an impartial and correct policy for peace and security on the Korean peninsula," he urged. National General Secretary of the Socialist Political Action Party of Peru Gustavo Mohme Ryona, President of the National Executive Committee of the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru Angel Castro Lavarello and General Secretary of the People's Unity Party of Tunisia Mohamed Bel Hadj Amor in their messages expressed full support to the principled stand for the independent and peaceful reunification of Korea and the stand for a durable peace on the Korean peninsula which was put forward by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address.
