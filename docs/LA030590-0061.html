March 5, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

BRIEFLY

Tissue To Be Made from Recycled Paper: Ft. Howard Corp. has announced that it will introduce Green Forest, the first U.S. line of tissue and paper towels to be touted as made from 100% recycled paper. A few other tissue manufacturers have long used undisclosed amounts of recycled fiber, but haven't promoted the fact, fearing that consumers could consider them unhygienic.
