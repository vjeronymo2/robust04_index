BFN [Excerpts] Strikes in the Silesia region are spreading. The Solidarity Slasko-Dabrowski regional chairman said today that railway workers have joined the strike. Strikes began this morning at Bytom, Radzionkow, Brynow, Lisow, Kochanowice, Herby Stare, and Krupski Mlyn stations. Railway workers from Lubliniec joined the strike at 0700. The strikers have stopped servicing cargo trains and train engines, a communique says. The length of the strike depends on a decision of the Solidarity national strike headquarters. Railway workers have presented a list of demands that are similar to the trade union's national demands. [passage omitted] Miners are also on strike. According to trade unionists, the strike continues in 22 hard-coal mines. The State Hard Coal Agency in Katowice confirmed that coal is not being mined in 20 collieries. Miners from three zinc and lead mines are also on strike.
