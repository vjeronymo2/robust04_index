BFN [Text] We have just received a report saying the following: An agreement has been reached between Karadzic's Serbs and Bosniaks on the cessation of fighting in the Bihac region, UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] has reported. The agreement envisages the separation of forces, buffer zones, withdrawal of heavy weapons, the possibility of supervision by the UN forces, freedom of movement for international humanitarian organizations, and foundation of a joint commission.
