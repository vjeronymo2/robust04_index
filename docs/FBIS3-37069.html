Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev--A UNIAN correspondent learned from sources close to Ukraine's Defense Ministry that Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy, Ukraine's defense minister, issued an order approving a new deputies staff. As before, Staff Colonel General Anatoliy Lopata is first deputy defense minister. Colonel General Ivan Bizhan, the deputy defense minister also remains in office. Colonel General Hryhoriy Mykhalchenko, the deputy defense minister for rear services, and Ivan Oliynyk, the deputy defense minister for armaments, are joined by the head of the Main Directorate for Land Forces who was promoted to their position. His previous post remains vacant.
