BFN [Unattributed report under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] Izhevsk -- Hidden unemployment at Russia's defense complex enterprises now tops 10 percent while it is 6 percent on average around the country, Russian Federation Labor Minister Gennadiy Melikyan told a RIA correspondent. He pointed out that military-industrial complex enterprises cut back the output of military products by 65 percent over the recent year. In the minister's estimate, 1.4 million of the 70.5 million able-bodied citizens of Russia have been registered to date as officially unemployed with 4.45 million categorized among those who need to be set up in employment.
