BFN [Text] Beijing, June 2 (XINHUA) -- Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met with a delegation from Japan's Nagano Prefecture here today. The 26-member delegation, headed by Kushihara Yoshinao, former chairman of the Japan-China special committee of Social Democratic Party of Japan, arrived here yesterday as guests of the Chinese Association for International Understanding.
