July 20, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Gillett Sells Baltimore TV Station: Gillett Group signed a letter of intent to sell NBC affiliate WMAR-TV in Baltimore for $154.7 million to Scripps Howard Broadcasting Co., a subsidiary of E. W. Scripps Co., the Cincinnati-based newspaper publisher and broadcaster. Gillett Group, controlled by Denver businessman George Gillett, has been under pressure to sell some of its TV station holdings to reduce debt. The transaction is also one of the few sales of a major-market TV station in the past year, after a slowdown in debt-driven, leveraged media deals.
