BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Thai Foreign Minister Prasong Sunsiri, who arrived in Ankara on an official visit yesterday, has begun his contacts. He met with Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin this morning. Later, the official Turkish-Thai talks were held. In a statement at the meeting, Cetin said that geographic distances no longer affect relations between nations. Pointing out that Turkey and Thailand are two countries with significant economic power in their respective regions, Cetin added: We will discuss ways in which we can cooperate in the economic field and the two countries can combine their respective influences in their regions. There might be significant developments in the field of tourism. We can also develop our cultural and trade relations. Recalling that Sunsiri arrived in Turkey after attending the Nonaligned conference in Egypt, Cetin added that Sunsiri will brief him on that conference and that various regional problems will be discussed. President Suleyman Demirel also received Sunsiri at the Cankaya Mansion today.
