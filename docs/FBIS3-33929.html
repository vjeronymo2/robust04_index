Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian sources in Amman have stated that two newspapers will be launched shortly in the West Bank. The first one, to be published in English, will be called JERUSALEM TIMES. It will be edited by Hanna Sinyurah, former editor of AL-FAJR, and will be partially financed by 'Afif Safiyah, the PLO's ambassador in London. The second newspaper will be called FAJR AL-SHA'B [The People's Dawn] and will replace the newspapers AL-FAJR and AL-SHA'B. Some Saudi businessmen have expressed their desire to finance this paper.
