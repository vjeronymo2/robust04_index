Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 Jan--Wlodzimierz Cimoszewicz, the deputy prime minister and justice minister, this afternoon received World Bank representative Kemal Dervis. During the meeting, officials from the Ministries of Health, Education, and Labor briefed the guest on how the credits were used and on the possible future needs of the ministries. This morning the head of the World Bank mission met Marek Borowski, the deputy prime minister and finance minister, and Hanna Gronkiewicz-Waltz, the president of the Polish National Bank. During the meeting with the deputy prime minister, Dervis announced that a World Bank mission will come to Poland in March to review the existing loans and to discuss loans under negotiations.
