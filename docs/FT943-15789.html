940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: FR Group in Pounds 7m purchase FR Group, the manufacturer of in-flight refuelling equipment, yesterday announced it had sold its FR Electronics division to Silicon Power Corporation, a Californian company, for an expected consideration of Pounds 1.2m cash. SPC is taking over the FRE business as a going concern and will continue for a limited period to trade under the FR Electronics name. FR also announced it had entered into a conditional agreement to purchase the business and assets and certain liabilities of SF, a California-based company for Dollars 10.5m (Pounds 7m) cash. SF is involved in the manufacture of external fuel tanks, air-to-air refuelling systems and special purpose aircraft pods. The acquisition is conditional upon the due process of the US Bankruptcy Court. SF filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the US Bankruptcy Code on June 29 in order to facilitate its prompt reorganisation funded by the sale of its business to FR.
