August 28, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

What a Boss: Bruce Springsteen has given $10,000 to singer Mary Wells, who is suffering from throat cancer and is broke.

The money was donated in care of the Rhythm and Blues Foundation in Washington, a non-profit group that comes to the aid of blues singers. Singer Bonnie Raitt has also pitched in to help Wells, the former Motown star known for the 1964 song "My Guy." Wells, 47, is receiving outpatient radiation treatments at County-USC Medical Center, has no health insurance and has been evicted from her Los Angeles apartment. BETH KLEID
