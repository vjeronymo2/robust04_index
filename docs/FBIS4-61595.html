BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 7 May -- Azerbaijani troops this morning attacked Karabakh positions in the north of Kelbadzharskiy rayon in the Omarskiy Pass. This was reported by the press service of the Karabakh Army. According to its information, Karabakh elements controlling all of the dominating heights in the sector had repulsed the enemy attack. Information on the number of losses suffered is being established, it was stressed in Stepanakert. At the same time, the press service of the Karabakh Army reported that sustained fighting had continued late on Friday evening in the north-eastern and eastern sectors of the front in Nagornyo-Karabakh. As in preceding days, the Karabakh side repulsed the offensive in several directions and had to some extent forced the Azerbaijani elements back to the very rear of their positions at the villages of Leninavan and Osipavan in Mardakertskiy rayon of the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh, official sources noted in the capital of Nagorno- Karabakh.
