Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 12) TURAN -- President Geydar Aliyev returned to Baku after having completed his five-day visit to China. During his visit, President Aliyev met the leader of the Peoples' Republic of China Jiang Zemin, and Head of State Council, Li Peng, and with other officials of the republic. The visit resulted in the signing of a number of the documents such as an agreement on friendly relations between the two countries, the beginning of flights, the development of scientific, technical, cultural, and relations, and the promotion of investment. During the talks, Geydar Aliyev raised the issue of obtaining credit to Azerbaijan. According to official sources this question was resolved during talks between President Geydar Aliyev and Chairman Li Peng. In addition, Li Peng said that China, as the permanent member of the UN Security Council, acknowledges the territorial integrity of Azerbaijan and supports it.
