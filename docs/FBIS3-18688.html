Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The German Government has welcomed the agreement in the engineering industry wage dispute. Government spokesman Dieter Vogel told DDP/ADN news agency today in Bonn that it had been possible in this way to avert a strike in the German engineering industry. Vogel did not wish to comment on the details of the compromise between IG Metall and the employers' association, Gesamtmetall.
