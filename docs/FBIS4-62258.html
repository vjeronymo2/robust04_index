BFN [Unattributed reports under the "Provincial Chronicle" rubric] [Text] Ufa -- A republican government delegation led by Prime Minister Anatoliy Kopsov has left for Moscow to agree on 12 draft agreements to form the basis for a treaty between the Russian Federation and Bashkiria [Bashkortostan]. The treaty is to be signed 20 May. Vladikavkaz -- North Ossetia President Akhsarbek Galazov and Supreme Soviet Chairman Yuriy Biragov have turned to the Russian Federation's Federation Council with a request for assistance to prepare the republic's draft constitution whose provisions should fully conform with those of the Russian Federation Constitution.
