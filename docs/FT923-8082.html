920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / Australia gives interest pledge The Australian government yesterday gave assurances there would be no further cuts in interest rates and that the 1992-93 budget deficit would be less than ADollars 16bn (Pounds 6bn) in an attempt to stem the slide of the Australian dollar, writes Emilia Tagaza from Canberra. Speculations about a ADollars 16bn deficit and of a cut in interest rates sparked heavy sales of the currency on Tuesday.
