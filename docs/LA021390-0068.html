February 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

HONDURAS TO LET PUBLIC USE U.S.-BUILT AIRPORT

The Honduran government will open the U.S.-built Palmerola Air Base for public use, Honduran Vice President Roberto Martinez said Monday.

A U.S. Embassy spokesman said that the decision to convert the airport, currently used only by the Honduran and U.S. military, should be left to Honduras.
