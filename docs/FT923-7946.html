920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Sharp fall at North Mid Construction North Midland Construction, the civil engineer, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 452,000 to Pounds 84,000 for the half year ended June 30. Turnover declined 24 per cent to Pounds 9.87m. The company does not expect any improvement in the second six months. The interim dividend is cut to 0.3p (0.5p) from earnings of 0.56p (3.01p).
