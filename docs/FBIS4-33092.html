BFN [Text] The government of provisional president attorney Emile Jonassaint has officially banned the members of the resigning government of Mr. Robert Malval from leaving the country. In a communique issued on the night of 30 May, the Ministry of the Interior asked the state employees and government members not to leave the country before providing explanations about their management of the public coffers. A formal order has already been applied in that regard at the border posts in order to block the travel of the people affected by those measures. No reactions to the measures have been recorded from the standpoint of the resigning government of Robert Malval. The resigning prime minister was supposed to leave the country at the beginning of this month in order to participate at the OAS General Assembly on 6 June in Belem, Brazil. We have learned today from the cabinet of Mr. Malval that he had decided not to go to Brazil after all, feeling that after 11 May 1994 his place is in Haiti along with the Haitian people and standing before the usurpers of the legal power -- unquote [no opening quote as heard].
