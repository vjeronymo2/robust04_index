December 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Antidote to Bowling: Bowled over by the unabashed commercialism of such New Year's Day staples as the Mobil Cotton Bowl and the Federal Express Orange Bowl? Well, KCOP Channel 13 will provide an attractive and uncommercial alternative to football when it airs 10 classic movies without commercials beginning at the stroke of midnight New Year's Eve. All the films included in this 22-hour movie blitz will be broadcast unedited in their original form. They include: "From Here to Eternity," "The Good Earth," "Camelot," "McCabe and Mrs. Miller" and "Chinatown." CLAUDIA PUIG
