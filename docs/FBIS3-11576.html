Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Managua, 22 Mar (ACAN-EFE) -- Unidentified gunmen today murdered Cesar Barahona, the Sandinist National Liberation Front's [FSLN] political secretary in Wiwili Municipality, near the border with Honduras. FSLN sources report that the body of Barahona, who was kidnapped yesterday by a group of rebels of unknown political affiliation, was found by Army troops in Aguas Rojas, some 7 km from Wiwili. Barahona was killed while carrying a list of regional delegates who are to participate in the FSLN's special congress in May. According to political sources, the activist could have been kidnapped and murdered for "being a Sandinist." Police have not released any official explanation.
