BFN [Text] The five-member presidium of the Akali Dal-Badal in Punjab has announced suspension of the party's political activities for an indefinite period. Addressing a news conference at Jullundur today, the members of the presidium said that under the existing circumstances, it is not possible to carry on the political activities of the party. As already reported, the party president, Mr. Prakash Singh Badal, resigned yesterday and set up the five-member presidium to run the party's affairs.
