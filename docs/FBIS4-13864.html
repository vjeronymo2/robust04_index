BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs, met with Dr. Nabil Sha'th, adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and head of the Palestinian delegation to the negotiations with Israel. They discussed the negotiations and the extent of progress made. The Palestinian-Israeli talks will resume in Cairo tomorrow morning. Following the meeting, Dr. al-Baz expressed hope that the Palestinians and Israelis would do their best to complete discussion of the pending issues during the round of talks that will begin Sunday so that the implementation of the Declaration of Principles can start and the Palestinian people can feel the big change that will take place when a new phase of good relations between the Palestinian and Israeli peoples begins. He said the positions of the two sides are quite close, which makes it possible for the talks to have a "positive and happy ending."
