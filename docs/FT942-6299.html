940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / International Company News: Orix Corporation Orix Corporation, Japan's largest leasing company, reported a 7 per cent drop in consolidated pre-tax profits to Y34.23bn for the year to the end of March. Turnover fell by 13.5 per cent to Y325.4bn. Orix said it had minimised losses by focusing strongly on profitable operations and disposing of bad loans quickly. For the current year it expected to see consolidated pre-tax profit rise to Y36bn on sales higher by 2 per cent at Y331bn.
