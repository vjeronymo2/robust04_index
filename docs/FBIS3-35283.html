Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Damascus -- A prominent Syrian figure yesterday said in an official, open, and clear manner that Dr. Bashshar al-Asad, President Hafiz al-Asad's second eldest son, will assume the duties which used to be carried out by his brother Staff Major Basil al-Asad, who was killed in a car crash last month. This announcement came in a lecture delivered by Dr. Bahjat Sulayman, a senior and influential political figure very close to President Hafiz al-Asad. He said: The masses in Syria see in Dr. Bashshar al-Asad a guarantee and a symbol needed for the continuation and stability of Hafiz al-Asad's line. [passage omitted] There have been numerous indications since Basil al-Asad's death that Dr. Bashshar al-Asad is going to carry the leadership banner and continue the big aspirations launched by his brother, Basil. Dr. Bashshar al-Asad is an ophthalmologist who was preparing to specialize in a European state when he heard the news of his brother Basil's death.
