Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- The East Asia Construction Industry Corporation Ltd. from the Republic of Korea (ROK) will offer loans to build two thermal power plants in China. The total installed capacity of the two power plants will reach more than 300,000 kw. The corporation signed a cooperation agreement with China and the U.S. here recently. The corporation is a leading power plant construction engineering corporation in the Republic of Korea.
