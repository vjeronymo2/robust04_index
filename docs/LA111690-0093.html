November 16, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

WORK WILL CLOSE PART OF VENTURA FREEWAY

Construction on the Ventura Freeway will continue this weekend, closing several lanes and an on-ramp.

Up to three eastbound and westbound lanes between Wilbur and White Oak avenues will be closed from 10 tonight to 8 a.m. Saturday, Caltrans reported.

Three westbound lanes between the Hollywood Freeway Interchange and Laurel Canyon Boulevard, and the westbound Laurel Canyon Boulevard off-ramp will be closed from 10 p.m. Saturday to 11 a.m. Sunday.
