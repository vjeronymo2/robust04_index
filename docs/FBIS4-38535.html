BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow, 31 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The Russian Federation president's edict "On the introduction of a state of emergency on part of the territory of the Republic of North Ossetia and the Ingush Republic" was endorsed by the Federation Council today. The state of emergency in the conflict zone is being extended to 31 July. Deputies decided to set up a monitoring commission which will verify that the stipulations of the presidential edict are implemented.
