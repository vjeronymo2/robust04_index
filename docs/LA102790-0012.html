October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NOVAK ON AMERICANS MOVING UP

Perhaps Novak is right. It may be time for a flat tax for everybody.

Individuals, companies and corporation, with no tax exemptions or tax credits for anyone. I can hear the screaming already.

No more abusing business-related expenses, because there will be no more business-related exemptions. I am sure it will be easier for the IRS auditors. No need for tax experts, lawyers, etc.

ROBERT C. NELSON

Los Angeles
