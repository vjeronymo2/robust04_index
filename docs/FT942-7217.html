940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / UK Company News: New London Capital declares dividend New London Capital, one of the recently formed Lloyd's investment trusts, reported its results for the period from incorporation to March 31 1994. Net assets per share of the trust at end-March stood at 91.2p. Attributable revenue for the period amounted to Pounds 539,592. Earnings per share emerged at 0.9p and an interim dividend of 0.5p is declared.
