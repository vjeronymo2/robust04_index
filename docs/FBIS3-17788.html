Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A reorganization of Turkmenistan's Armed Forces is soon to start in the Republic. The basic principles of the reorganization were set out at an expanded session of the country's Defense Council by President Saparmurad Niyazov. It provides for the establishment of the Defense Council and a National Security Council, a General Staff and Headquarters of the Armed Forces and rear services. The numerical strength of the country's Armed Forces is to be cut in line with the principle of essential defense sufficiency. Niyazov stressed the particular role of Russian officers in forming the Turkmen Army and noted the coincidence of the strategic interests of Turkmenistan and Russia.
