940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / Canada Gold shares lost ground and held back a fledgling recovery in Toronto, the TSE 300 index closing just 4.6 points better at 4,052.2. The slight increase in the index followed six straight days of losses. Overall declines still held an advantage over advances, by 421 to 354. Volume contracted to 54.1m shares from Tuesday's 64.9m, and trading value amounted to CDollars 663.4m. Turnover was boosted by heavy trading in the shares of Falconbridge, which today completed its initial public offering for proceeds of about CDollars 750m. Falconbridge shares lost CDollars 1/2 at CDollars 17 1/2 . Ten of the 14 stock groups rose, led by real estate and forestry, which gained 2.22 and 2.09 per cent respectively. Carena Developments rose 26 cents to CDollars 1.75 on reporting higher six-month earnings.
