910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: JO Walker tumbles Pounds 36,000 into the red JO Walker, the Leicester-based timber importer and building materials group, fell Pounds 36,342 into the red at the pre-tax level in 1990 compared with profits of Pounds 56,298 in 1989. The deficit at half year was Pounds 22,000. Losses per share amounted to 3.8p (earnings 5p) after a tax credit of Pounds 6,081 (Pounds 16,260 charge). However, an unchanged final dividend of 2p is proposed for a 2p (4p) total. Turnover was slightly lower at Pounds 15.09m (Pounds 15.69m).
