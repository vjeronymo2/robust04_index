CLOTHES AND CASH

Costa Mesa is king when it comes to money and clothes in Orange County. In the first quarter of 1989 (the most recent period from which information is available), Costa Mesa had the highest taxable sales of clothing and shoes, the largest per capita and per store sales and the biggest percentage of all sales in the county. Its first quarter, 1989, sales increase over first quarter, 1988, was topped only by the 185% increase in La Habra.
