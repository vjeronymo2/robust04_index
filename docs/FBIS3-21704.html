Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] La Paz, 4 Mar (EFE) -- Radio Panamericana reported here that Susana Seleme, former President Jaime Paz Zamora's private secretary, was arrested today in the city of Santa Cruz on charges of alleged involvement in the drug trade. Seleme's arrest was neither confirmed nor denied by Interior Minister German Quiroga. Seleme is considered to have been one of Paz Zamora's closest aides both during and after his presidential tenure. Quiroga stated that he could not talk about arrests or arrest warrants because this is a confidential matter. He did not rule out, however, the arrest of other politicians linked to Paz Zamora's administration (1989-93).
