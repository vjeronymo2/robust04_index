940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / International Company News: Case Equipment prices offering The initial public offering of 17.5m common shares of Case Equipment, the US farm and construction equipment machinery group which is a subsidiary of Tenneco, was priced at Dollars 19 a share through underwriters led by Morgan Stanley and Lazard Freres, AP-DJ reports from New York. The company had expected the shares to be offered at between Dollars 19 and Dollars 22. Earlier this month, the size of the offering was reduced from the planned 24.5m shares.
