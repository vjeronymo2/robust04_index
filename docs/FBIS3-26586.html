Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] As the Spring Festival is approaching, the provincial party committee and government sent a letter to convey regards to retired cadres and elderly people throughout the province. The letter says: Last year the province made marked achievements in reform, opening up, and economic construction. These achievements were the result of the common efforts of cadres and masses throughout the province, and cannot be separated from your enthusiastic concern and support. The letter points out: This year is a crucial one for the province to fulfill the Eighth Five-Year Plan one year ahead of schedule and to quadruple the GNP three years ahead of schedule. The fulfillment of this objective requires the efforts of cadres and masses throughout the province, including the concern and support of retired cadres and elderly people of all nationalities. They should do their best to display their professional skills, experience, and exemplary role, and should make new contributions to Gansu's reform and opening up, to the establishment of the socialist market economy structure, to economic construction, and to spiritual civilization. The letter further points out: In this new year, we should make a success of the work concerning retired cadres and elderly people, should propagate their historic exploits and present devotion so as to create a fine social mood of respecting and loving the elderly, should show concern and care for them in politics and life, and should create conditions for their good health and longevity and for them to devote their remaining efforts, so that they will spend their remaining years happily. The letter sincerely wishes retired cadres and elderly people throughout the province a happy Lunar New Year and a harmonious family.
