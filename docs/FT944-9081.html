941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / World Trade News: US keeps trade and rights link The US yesterday said it would continue to press for a link between fundamental worker rights and trade, despite strong opposition from many developing countries and lukewarm support at best from other western nations. Mr Jack Otero, US deputy undersecretary of labour, said Washington hoped to convince trading partners that all nations, whatever their level of development, should respect some core principles of 'basic human dignity'. He denied charges that the US stance was a pretext for trade protectionism.
