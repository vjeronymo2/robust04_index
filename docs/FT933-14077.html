930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / Bundesbank key interest rates unchanged The Bundesbank left its key interest rates unchanged yesterday as its policy-making council met for the first time since announcing cuts in official interest rates on July 1, writes David Waller in Frankfurt. The markets had not been expecting reductions so soon after the Bundesbank's last moves. The discount and Lombard rates remain at 6.75 and 8.25 per cent respectively.
