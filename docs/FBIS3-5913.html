Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Italy, Indonesia Seek Industrial Cooperation"] [Excerpt] Jakarta, 4 Mar (ANSA) -- Privatizing Italian companies in the IRI [Institute for Industrial Reconstruction] group and their counterparts in Indonesia's state industrial sector are looking at possible avenues of cooperation, said IRI's international affairs director Alessandro Ovi, in Jakarta for a meeting with the Indonesian science and technology minister Habibie in the company of Italy's ambassador Mario Brando Penso. The meeting, focusing on IRI's international objectives and on the industrial holding's interest in greater Italian-Indonesian cooperation, was followed by another with the Minister for Industry and Trade Hartarto, during a seminar organized by the Indonesian Agency for Strategic Industries (BPIS), attended by representatives of Finmeccanica [Mechanical Engineering Finance Corporation], Ansalfo, Alenia, Italtel and Elsag Bailey, all of the IRI group, as well as by representatives of BPIS companies and the relevant government ministries.
