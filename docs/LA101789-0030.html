October 17, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Fun and Games: Dick Clark, who spent 15 years hosting "Pyramid," will be executive producer and host of "The Challengers," a new game show announced Monday by Buena Vista Television. Ron Greenberg is co-executive producer of the show that would ask questions about current events. It's planned for fall syndication . . . . KMPC personality Jim Lange, who formerly hosted "The Dating Game," hosted a run-through the other day of "Top Dog," a new game show from BMA Productions in development with CBS. ALEENE MacMINN
