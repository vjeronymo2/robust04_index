Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In 1993, Henan's total telecommunication volume registered a 72.8 percent over 1992 with its total number of telephone exchangers reaching 0.77 million. Except for Quxian, Huaibin, and Shangcheng counties, all cities and counties in the province installed national direct-dial long-distance telephones. Seventeen prefectures and cities and some counties in the province installed international direct-dial long-distance telephones. Last year, the province also succeeded in rapidly developing various paging services with the result that 17 prefectures and cities and 35 counties and districts in the province installed 900 mobile paging antennas.
