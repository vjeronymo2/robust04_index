930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / International Company News in Brief: Fiat Fiat said its automotive division Fiat Auto had taken 100 per cent control of troubled luxury carmaker Maserati, Reuter reports. Fiat, which previously owned 49 per cent of Maserati's share capital, took control of the rest of the company 'on the request of the De Tomaso family,' a Fiat statement said, referring to Maserati's founders.
