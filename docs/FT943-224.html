940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / Bucks voters back unitary option Buckinghamshire residents have opted for unitary authorities, according to the results of an advisory referendum published yesterday by the Local Government Commission. It found that creating four all-purpose unitary authorities was favoured by 45 per cent of respondents, compared with 27 per cent for the commission's favourite option of three, and 19 per cent for giving unitary status to Milton Keynes and keep the two-tier system of county and districts for the rest of Buckinghamshire.
