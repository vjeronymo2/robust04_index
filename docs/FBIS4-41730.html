BFN [Text] A permanent Latvian delegation today left for the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe [PACE] to familiarize representatives of PACE with problems related to the process of passing the citizenship bill. A member of the delegation, Aleksandrs Kirsteins, the chairman of the parliamentary group Latvian National Independence Movement, told journalists that these issues would be discussed in political groups of the Council of Europe and PACE commissions, as well as during the course of unofficial meetings with representatives of the EC leadership.
