BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 13 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held Sunday to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the on-the-spot guidance of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to the Korean Central News Agency. Comrade Kim Chong-il guided the KCNA on the spot on June 12, 1964. He had a historical talk entitled "The Basic Tasks Facing the Korean Central News Agency" with KCNA officials, clearly indicating the road ahead of it. Comrade Kim Chong-il gave flawless answers to all the theoretical and practical questions for the development of the work of the news agency such as its basic tasks, functions and role, the principles it must maintain to become a mighty ideological weapon of the party and ways for their application. After his on-the-spot guidance, KCNA has been able to serve better for the cause of modelling of the whole society on the chuche idea as an ideological weapon of the Workers' Party of Korea and defend and exalt the revolutionary tradition of the chuche-based press. KCNA has grown to be a collective unfailingly loyal to the party and the leader over the past 30 years. Tasks were set forth at the meeting to enhance its functions and role in every way as a mouthpiece of the party and the government.
