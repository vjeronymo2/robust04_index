940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / National Savings net receipts fall National Savings' contribution to government funding fell again last month to Pounds 424m including Pounds 158m from accrued interest. This compares with Pounds 554m in April. Net receipts in May were Pounds 266m with gross sales of Pounds 985m being offset by repayments of Pounds 719m. The highest-selling product was the Pensioners Bonds at Pounds 248m, although Pounds 65m of this was transfers from income bonds. Premium bonds were the next best-selling product at Pounds 153m. The total amount invested in National Savings at the end of May was Pounds 49.4bn.
