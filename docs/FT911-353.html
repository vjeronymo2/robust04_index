910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / Postal order charges rise POSTAL order charges increase today for the first time in 12 months. Charges on orders up to and including Pounds 1 remain at 25p. Charges for orders valued from Pounds 2 to Pounds 10 rise 5p to 49p and the charge for Pounds 20 orders rises 7p to 75p. The Post Office said the new prices were to ensure that the postal order service remained viable.
