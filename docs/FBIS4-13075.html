BFN [Text] Fifty Dostum militia surrendered to forces of the Islamic army in Fariab Province. Reports received by BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY from Fariab Province say the men, headed by Habibollah, realizing the inhuman and un-Islamic character of Dostum's militia, joined the forces of Jehadi Division 24 of the province in Qoshqar area in Fariab Province along with five rocket launchers, eight PK machine guns, and two ZK1 machine guns. The report adds that the surrendered people expressed their hatred of the crimes and atrocities of Dostumites and promised that, with all their will, they are ready to fight against Dostum's militia.
