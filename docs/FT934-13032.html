931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Lon & Strathclyde London & Strathclyde Trust reported net asset value per share of 258.5p at August 31, against 191.7p a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months to end-August was Pounds 816,000 (Pounds 840,000) for earnings of 5.58p (5.74p). The final dividend is maintained at 4.25p for a total of 5.75p.
