BFN [Text] In the view of Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel, Turkey must be openly reminded to keep human rights -- "especially because we have had friendly relations with Turkey for decades, because it is our partner in NATO and has an important bridging function." In an interview with BILD AM SONNTAG, Kinkel said: "Yes, there are human rights violations in Turkey." Kinkel once again said that delinquent Kurds should only be deported in absolutely exceptional cases. Anyone who commits a crime belongs before a court and in prison. The government is currently examining whether it is safeguarded that those that are deported will not face torture, death penalties, or anti-constitutional treatment.
