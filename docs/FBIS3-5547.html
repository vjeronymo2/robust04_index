Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] After attending a training course for the district chapter chairmen of the Democratic Liberal Party, Vice Foreign Minister Hong Sun-yong said today that the inspection of North Korea by the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] had not reached a point that would be acceptable to us, and that the North-South dialogue had not proceeded smoothly. It is therefore not clear whether the third round of U.S.-North Korean high-level talks will be held on 21 March as scheduled. Vice Foreign Minister Hong said: If the talks are not held as scheduled, the North Korean nuclear issue cannot but return to the starting point. Considering this, the government has continued to discuss the presentation of the nuclear issue to the UN Security Council with friendly countries, such as the United States.
