January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PAIN-COMPLIANCE TECHNIQUES

Whatever are we to do with these anti-abortionist gadflies? They trample the rights of most to intercede where they are wanted the least. Then they whimper that their right to violate the law is frustrated by police who, they say, have no business shoveling them from the public thoroughfare, like so much refuse, given their determination to behave irresponsibly.

Is there, perhaps, some address to which we can send donations in order to buy the LAPD more nunchakus?

STEPHEN C. LEE

La Habra
