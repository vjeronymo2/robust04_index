940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Bletchley Motor Group BLETCHLEY MOTOR Group has acquired the Godfrey Davis site at Brick Knoll Park, Ashley Road, St Albans, for Pounds 1.7m cash. BMG intends to convert the site at a cost of about Pounds 600,000 into a main dealer outlet to be known as BMG St Albans.
