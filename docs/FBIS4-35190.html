BFN [Text] The PLO has handed out 14 of 22 portfolios in the Palestinian self-rule administration in the Gaza Strip and Jericho area. Mr. Ahmad Quray', a key player in the secret peace deal with Israel, was named to be in charge of economy and trade. Mr. Samir Ghawshah, who was given the labor and social affairs portfolio, said other ministers in the Palestinian National Authority included chief self-rule negotiator Nabil Sha'th at planning and economic cooperation, and PLO Executive Committee member Muhammad Zuhdi al- Nashashibi at finance. Mr. Ghawshah said the portfolios were handed out at a meeting in Tunis last night and consultations were continuing on those still to be handed out. He listed the 14 so far distributed as: 1. Economy and Trade, Ahmad Quray', alias Abu-'Ala' 2. Finances, Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi 3. Planning and Economic Cooperation, Nabil Sha'th 4. Housing, Zakariya al-Agha 5. Communications and Post, 'Abd-al-Hafiz al-Ashhab 6. Education and Teaching, Yasir 'Amr 7. Culture and Arts, Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh 8. Justice, Furayh Abu-Middayn 9. Labor and Workers, Samir Ghawshah 10. Social Affairs, Intisar al-Wazir, alias Umm-Jihad 11. Local Government, Sa'ib 'Urayqat 12. Youth and Sports, 'Azmi al-Shu'aybi 13. Tourism and Monuments, Ilyas Furayj 14. Health, Riyad al-Za'nun The meetings are due to resume later today.
