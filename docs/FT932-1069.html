930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Disability move for Wales CAMPAIGNERS for the disabled yesterday stepped up the fight to outlaw discrimination by proposing new legislation for Wales. Former shadow Welsh secretary Mr Barry Jones (Lab Alyn and Deeside) gained an unopposed formal first reading for his Civil Rights (Disabled Persons) (Wales) Bill. It seeks to introduce in the principality the provisions of earlier measures intended to cover the whole UK, which were piloted by Mr Alf Morris (Lab Manchester Wythenshawe), a former minister for the disabled, but blocked by Tories.
