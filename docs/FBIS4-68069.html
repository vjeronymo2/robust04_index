BFN [Text] As INTERFAX has learned from a source close to Tajikistan's Security Minister, unidentified gunmen tried to kill in Dushanbe Thursday night Nurzullo Dustov, a deputy transportation and road construction minister and a member of parliament. An ex vice-president, Dustov, and his bodyguards, were machine-gunned from a passing Zhiguli car when they were getting out of Dustov's office limousine near his home. The attackers escaped. An investigation is underway.
