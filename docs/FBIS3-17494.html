Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 26 Mar -- The press service of the Russian president has reported that in keeping with part two of Article 85 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, Russian President Boris Yeltsin today issued an edict suspending the 22 February 1994 resolution issued by the Krasnoyarsk Kray administration "On the Referendum on the Draft Law of Krasnoyarsk Kray `On the Fundamentals of Direct Democracy and of the Structure of State Power in Krasnoyarsk Kray.'" The edict notes that the inclusion of the draft law, which determines the kray's status, in the kray referendum violates Article 10, part two of Article 66 and part one of Article 77 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation.
