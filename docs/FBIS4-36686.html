BFN [Text] At 1245 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Saudi Foreign Minister Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal conveyed a message to President al-Asad from Saudi King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the two holy mosques, dealing with the situation in the region. Talks dealt with the situation in the area, Arab problems, and ways of tackling them on the basis of general Arab interests.
