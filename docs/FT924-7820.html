921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / The Autumn Statement - Details: Drug industry reacts angrily THE UK drug industry reacted angrily last night to the government's extension of the so-called 'limited list' of drugs which cannot be prescribed under the NHS. Mrs Virginia Bottomley, the health secretary, said steps had to be taken to limit growth of the NHS drug bill, which is set to rise by 11.5 per cent this year. The list is being extended from seven categories to 17, including diarrhoea, allergies and appetite suppressants.
