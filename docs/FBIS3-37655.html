Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian president's press secretary, Vyacheslav Kostikov, has said in an interview with INTERFAX that Boris Yeltsin's Bosnia initiative has lifted the danger from a crisis threatening to acquire worldwide dimensions and laid the foundations for a definitive settlement. Russia, the press secretary believes, has in fact won a vital battle for global status. This is important for the domestic political scene, in that it strengthens Boris Yeltsin's position.
