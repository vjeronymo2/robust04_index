920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / International Company News: Ban extended on Upjohn's Halcion UPJOHN, the US pharmaceuticals group, has been told by the UK's Committee on Safety of Medicines that it has extended its ban on Halcion, the world's best-selling sleeping tablet, for a further three months. The committee said it was taking steps to make sure that a final decision on whether the product should be relicenced would be taken in the near future.
