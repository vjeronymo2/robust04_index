941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Polls show Tories inching ahead but still languishing nearly 29 points behind Labour. The Gallup poll in the Daily Telegraph puts the Tories on 25.5 per cent, Labour on 54 per cent and the Liberal Democrats on 16.5 per cent. The figures contrast with early September's 56.5 per cent for Labour, 22 per cent for the Tories and 17 per cent for the Liberal Democrats.
