Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Kuchina] [Text] Riga, 1 Mar -- Restoration work has started today on the site of the explosion which took place yesterday five km from the Russian radar station in the Latvian village of Skrunda. One of the masts of the electric power line supplying electricity to the station was damaged here. A commission consisting of representatives of the station, Latvenergo [Latvian power supply company], and the criminal police have officially established that this was an act of sabotage. In connection with this, the press center of the North-Western Group of Forces reports, the Russian embassy is preparing a note of protest to the Latvian Foreign Ministry. Leonid Mayorov, commander of the group of forces, sent a request to the procurator general of Latvia.
