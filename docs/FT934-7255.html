931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Scot National net asset value at 94.9p Net asset value per capital share of the Scottish National Trust recovered to 94.9p at September 30, against 2.8p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to the end of September was Pounds 14.6m (Pounds 16m) for earnings per share of 7.83p (8.74p). A final dividend of 2.6p has been proposed plus a special payment of 0.25p making a total for the year of 7.75p (8.85p). Net asset value per the stepped preference shares improved to 134p (127.6p) while the figure for the zero preference shares rose to 190.1p (170.8p).
