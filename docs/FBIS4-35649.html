BFN [Text] In his office at noon today, Dr. 'Abd-al-Razzq al-Nusur, minister of public works and housing, received Dr. Zakariya al-Agha, member of the Palestinian National Authority [PNA] for housing. The ministry's director general and the director general of the Housing and Urban Development Corporation also attended the meeting. Dr. al-Nusur said after the session that talks covered the question of housing in the self-rule areas. He indicated that the two sides will exchange views and technical expertise in the best interests of the two brotherly peoples. The Ministry of Public Works and Housing, al-Nusur noted, is placing all its technical expertise in institution building at the disposal of the Palestinians. The sides agreed that Jordanian contractors will receive preferential treatment in the self-rule areas.
