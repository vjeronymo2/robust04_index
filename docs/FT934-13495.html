931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Wensum recovery The recent improvement in fortunes at Wensum continued in the six months to July 31. The USM-quoted men's wear and corporate clothing company reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 56,000 (losses of Pounds 46,000). Turnover improved from Pounds 2.83m to Pounds 4.12m. Earnings per share came out at 0.56p (losses 0.43p) and the interim dividend is raised from 0.575p to 0.625p. The company said that a large part of its new clothing project should completed in the second half.
