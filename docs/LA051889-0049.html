May 18, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY SPOTLIGHT: SAN DIEGO DANCE IN BRIEF

Betzi Roe, founding member of Three's Company and an accomplished choreographer, has undertaken the collecting and showcasing of dance works by California women. Roe will present a program of these contemporary pieces at 7:30 p.m. next Thursday at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Valley Center. She will discuss the challenge of life as a woman artist and offer reflections on her experiences in the Soviet Union.
