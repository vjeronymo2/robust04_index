Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 12 (KCNA) -- The Seoul-based radio Voice of National Salvation published a communique on the external activities of the South Korean National Democratic Front [SKNDF] (Hanminjon). According to the communique, a delegation of Hanminjon led by vice-chairman of its Central Committee Hong Chi-yong visited Belgium in late November last year and held two rounds of talks with a delegation of the Belgian Labour Party led by chairman of its Central Committee Ludo Martens. At the talks, Hanminjon expressed support and solidarity for the Belgian party's struggle for the interests of the working class and other working masses, social progress and world peace and noted with appreciation that the Belgian party was striving to implement the Pyongyang declaration after signing it. The Belgian Labour Party recognised Hanminjon guided by the chuche idea as the organisation representing the desire and will of the South Korean people and the patriotic vanguard of the movement for a change in South Korea and highly estimated the South Korean people's struggle for independence, democracy and reunification. It also urged the South Korean authorities to stop their acts of dependence, fascism, division and war. The Belgian Labour Party recognised "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" put forward by the great leader President Kim Il-song as the best way of removing the danger of catastrophic war and tragic division and achieving peace and reunification on the Korean peninsula, and expressed support and solidarity for the struggle of Hanminjon and the South Korean people to realise the 10-point programme. Hanminjon and the Belgian Labour Party published a joint statement expressing their will to support and closely cooperate with each other on the principles of independence, mutual respect and non-interference in other's internal affairs and further develop inter-party relations through expanded mutual contacts. The Hanminjon delegation also visited Switzerland where it held talks with a delegation of ohe Workers' Party of Switzerland led by President Josef Zisyadis and with former mayor of Geneva Roger Dafflon who is Politburo member of the Workers' Party of Switzerland, president Edith Ballantyne and the general secretary of the International League of Women for Peace and Freedom, and others.
