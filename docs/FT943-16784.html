940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Jones & Shipman Jones & Shipman, the machine tool manufacturer, reported pre-tax losses increased from Pounds 1.54m to Pounds 2.82m for the year to end-March. The result was struck on turnover down from Pounds 13.4m to Pounds 10.9m and after a loss of Pounds 41,000 (Pounds 20,000 profit) from an associate and reduced interest charges of Pounds 141,000 (Pounds 332,000). Losses per share came out at 14.4p (11.7p).
