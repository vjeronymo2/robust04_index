Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 15 SAPA -- The first official trade delegation from Brazil met Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Renier Schoeman in Johannesburg on Tuesday [15 March]. The Brazilians were led by the Minister of External Relations Mr Celso Marcos Vierra de Souza who said the visit heralded "the expansion and strengthening of trade between South Africa and Brazil". The delegation is due in Cape Town on Wednesday, from where it will travel to Maputo for a brief visit.
