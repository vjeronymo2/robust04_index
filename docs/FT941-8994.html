940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / World Trade News: Russia, US sign accord on drugs The US and Russia have signed an agreement to give US pharmaceutical companies speedier and greater access to the Russian market, the White House said yesterday, Reuter reports from Washington. It said that Russia's Ministry of Health and Medical Industry would allow the import of US-approved and US-made drugs without additional clinical trials and would limit its review period to 90 days. The White House said that in effect medications meeting US standards would get nearly automatic entry into Russia. It said the agreement was signed in Washington last month and in Moscow on Tuesday.
