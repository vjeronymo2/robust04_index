941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Siemens and Ansaldo SpA win Italian contract Siemens of Germany and Ansaldo SpA have won a contract to supply electrical equipment for 30 high-speed locomotives in Italy. The contract, awarded by the Italian state railway, is worth a total of DM220m (Dollars 142.8m) with Siemens' part worth some DM50m.
