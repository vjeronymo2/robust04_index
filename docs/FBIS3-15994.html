Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" feature No. 47 (537) compiled by Nikolay Zherebtsov, Andrey Petrovskiy, edited by Vladimir Shishlin] [Text] The press service of the Ukrainian president and Government circulated a statement refuting the existence of an agreement between Kiev and Simferopol concerning the Crimea's possible joining of the ruble zone. "Such statements are no more than political speculation," the statement underlines. The statement also says that the issues on "the state financial policy, including introduction of whatever currency regime, is within the exclusive authority of the Ukrainian state bodies rather than that of particular administrative territorial units."
