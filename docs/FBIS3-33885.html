Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation in the curfew-bound areas of Kanpur in Uttar Pradesh is under control. The state inspector general of police told our Kanpur correspondent that there has been no report of any untoward incident since last evening. The Anwarganj police station in charge has been suspended. CRPF [Central Reserve Police Force] and Rapid Action Force jawans [soldiers] have been deployed in the curfew-bound areas. No arrest has so far been made in connection with yesterday's violent incidents. As reported, eight people were injured in these incidents in Anwarganj area and curfew was imposed in the entire area between Mulganj crossing to Bansmandi.
