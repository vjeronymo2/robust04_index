940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / International Company News: Strong gains at Newbridge Networks Newbridge Networks, a fast-growing Ottawa digital transmission equipment maker, more than doubled third-quarter profit and said demand continued to be strong. Third-quarter net profit, which was better than expected, rose to CDollars 41.4m (USDollars 31.4m), or 52 cents a share, up from CDollars 17.7m, or 24 cents, a year earlier, on sales of CDollars 145m, up 73 per cent. Nine-month profit advanced to CDollars 110.5m, or CDollars 1.39, from CDollars 34.5m, or 48 cents, on revenues of CDollars 388m, a leap of 88 per cent. Teleglobe, Canada's overseas telecommunications utility, earned CDollars 75.5m, or CDollars 1.20 a share, in 1993, against a loss of CDollars 50.6m, or CDollars 1.19, after special charges in 1992. Revenues were CDollars 1.4bn, up 18 per cent. International telephone traffic gained 12.5 per cent in 1993.
