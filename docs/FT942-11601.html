940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / Civil servants to re-run ballot The Civil and Public Services Association, Britain's largest civil service union, is to hold its election for president again after irregularities in the nominating procedure for one of the candidates were discovered. The union said yesterday that although the ballots for the executive committee and its two vice-presidents were unaffected, their results would not be announced until after the re-run in June.
