BFN [Text] Baghdad, 13 Jun (INA)--President Saddam Husayn today chaired a Cabinet meeting to discuss the general economic policy and the specific trade and economic issues that were discussed in the meeting which the president had this morning with a number of ministers and specialists in trade and industry affairs. Following a lengthy discussion, the Cabinet took the necessary measures in terms of legislation and execution. Leader President Saddam Husayn ordered an increase in the purchase price of wheat and barley for the current season, retroactively. The price for one tonne of first-class wheat will be increased from 15,000 dinars per tonne to 35,000 dinars, and the price for barley will be increased from 7,500 dinars per tonne to 20,000 dinars. Discussion of other subjects will continue in future meetings.
