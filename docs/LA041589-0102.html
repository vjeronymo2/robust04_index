April 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WEEKEND HIGHLIGHTS FOR L.A. INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL

The American Film Institute Los Angeles International Film Festival continues at the Cineplex Odeon Century Plaza Cinemas this weekend. Highlights from today's and Sunday's program follow.

TODAY

'Letters From the Park'

Cuba, 1988, 87 minutes

Noon

Cuban director Tomas Gutierrez Alea has shimmering period decor and a lovely Gabriel Garcia Marquez script, as a sad, voyeuristic professional letter-writer composes the love-epistles for both members of an impassioned couple, even as he falls helplessly in love with the woman. Of all AFI/LA FilmFest's Marquez films, this is the simplest and gentlest: a touching idea, exquisitely realized. RECOMMENDED. MICHAEL WILMINGTON
