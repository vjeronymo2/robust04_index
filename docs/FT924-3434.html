921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / Observer: Dirty weekend There must be easier ways of winning a weekend for two in Paris. Adams-Hydraulics, whose sewage-handling equipment is fitted to the QEII, has launched a competition to find the oldest Adams automatic sewage ejector still operational in the UK. Date of installation and serial numbers, please, to PO Box 15, York YO1 1XA.
