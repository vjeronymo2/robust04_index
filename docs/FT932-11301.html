930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / People: Non-executive directors Sir Harry Solomon, the 56-year-old who has recently retired as chairman of Hillsdown, the international food group of which he was a co-founder, has joined the board of FROGMORE ESTATES, a property company. Sir Harry's connection with the company is through Dennis Cope, who is both chairman of Fairview, a housing subsidiary of Hillsdown, and chairman of Frogmore.
