920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: London Atlantic asset value down Over the six months to March 31 net asset value per share of London Atlantic Investment Trust fell from 84.6p to 80.3p. A year earlier it stood at 79.7p. For the 1991-92 year gross revenue was Pounds 1.87m (Pounds 1.85m) and net income worked through at Pounds 1.39m (Pounds 1.32m) for earnings per share of 3.06p (2.9p). The final dividend is 2.2p for a total of 2.95p (2.8p).
