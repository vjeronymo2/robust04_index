BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, yesterday received a letter from his brother, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the 1 September revolution in Libya. The letter deals with the current developments in our country and Libya's firm, constant position that strongly supports Yemeni unity and rejects secession under whatever name, since Yemeni unity is a pan-Arab gain and an advance step along the path to embody the pan-Arab objective of comprehensive Arab unity. The letter expressed the fraternal Libyan people's support for their brothers in the Yemeni Republic and their backing under the umbrella of unity and democracy. It also dealt with issues of interest to both fraternal countries. The letter was delivered by brother Sayfawi Yusuf, the charge d'affaires of the Libyan Brotherhood Bureau in Sanaa who met with the president yesterday. The brother president praised the principled pan-Arab stand of the brothers in Libya, led by brother Col. Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, who has devoted all of his life to the cause of Arab unity and who played a key role in supporting all steps and efforts that led to the Yemeni unity and the establishment of the Yemeni Republic and supporting the march of unity since it was launched on 22 May 1990. The brother president asserted that Yemeni unity is here to stay. It will be safeguarded by the Yemeni people and their Armed Forces.
