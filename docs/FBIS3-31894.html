Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Federation of Mining Trade Unions of Romania affiliated to the Alfa Cartel has faxed us a communique in which it announces that the trade union leaders from all mining basins, except for the Jiu Valley, who held a meeting in Caciulata from 24-25 February, decided to organize a one-day general strike on 1 March, starting right at the beginning of the first shift. In this way, the trade unions in the mining and geological industry support the demands made by the Mining Trade Union Federation and the Alfa Cartel, the communique signed by the president of the federation specifies. The Autonomous Civil and Municipal Construction Trade Union Federation has also informed us in a communique that it will participate in the one-day strike on 28 February, together with the CNSLR [National Confederation of Romania's Free Trade Unions] Fratia Confederation, of which it is a member.
