October 8, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Muir 48, Servite 20 -- Servite running back Kent Weiss scored two touchdowns and a two-point conversion, but it wasn't enough at Glover Stadium. Servite fell to 1-4, while Muir is 4-0-1. Weiss scored touchdowns on a one-yard run and a seven-yard pass from Rob Walker.
