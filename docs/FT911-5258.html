910415 FT 15 APR 91 / Construction Contracts: Offices plan in Salford MANCHESTER-based Co-operative Bank has awarded WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION NORTH WEST a Pounds 1.2m contract to extend its facilities at Montford Street, in the heart of the Salford enterprise zone. Over seven months, Wimpey will design and build 12,000 sq ft of additional office space for the administration centre, which serves the bank's north-west operation. Architect Austin Strzala Turner, in conjunction with White Young Consulting Engineers, has produced a design for the two-storey extension to complement the original building, put up by Wimpey three years ago. The Co-operative Property Group is acting as employer's agent for the scheme. MOWLEM EUROPE, in joint venture with Conran Roche, is to undertake an economic and infrastructure study for the Schwedt/Angermunde region of eastern Germany. Work will last throughout this year.
