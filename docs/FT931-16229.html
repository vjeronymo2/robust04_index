930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / Hindu-Moslem violence flares Hindu-Moslem violence flared in parts of Bombay yesterday as rival groups battled with guns and swords, taking the death toll in two days of clashes to nine with more than 50 injured, Reuter reports from Bombay. Indian President Shankar Dayal Sharma meanwhile issued a law acquiring the site of the mosque destroyed last month by militant Hindus in the northern town of Ayodhya. Indian police admitted that paramilitary security forces killed 53 people and torched scores of buildings in the Kashmir town of Sopore on Wednesday in revenge for an attack by anti-Indian militants.
