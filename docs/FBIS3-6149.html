Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] At 0830 GMT today, Roberto Robaina Gonzalez, Politburo member of the Communist Party of Cuba [CPC] Central Committee and foreign relations minister of the Republic of Cuba, and his delegation paid a courtesy call on Nouhak Phoumsavan, Political Bureau member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee and president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], at the Hokham Presidential Palace. President Nouhak Phoumsavan hailed and warmly welcomed the delegation at the reception held in an atmosphere of warm friendship and goodwill. He noted that the visit to Laos by the Cuban Foreign Relations Ministry delegation would further strengthen the existing bilateral relations of friendship and cooperation. At the same time, the Cuban foreign relations minister also handed our president a message from Fidel Castro, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and conveyed the greetings of Cuban leaders to Lao leaders and people. Roberto Robaina Gonzalez expressed thanks to the Lao leadership for according him and his delegation a warm welcome, saying that even though their visit was short, it served to effectively increase and strengthen the Lao-Cuban relations of friendship and cooperation. The meeting between the host and guest proceeded in a warm and intimate atmosphere. At 0900 on the same day, the Cuban foreign relations minister and party also paid separate courtesy calls on Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the LPRP Central Committee and LPDR prime minister, at the Prime Minister's Office; Saman Vi-gnaket, Political Bureau member of the LPRP Central Committee and chairman of the National Assembly at the National Assembly Hall; and many other important figures of Laos. At noon today, Roberto Robaina Gonzalez and delegation left for home after successfully concluding the friendship visit made at the invitation of the LPDR foreign affairs minister. Seeing the delegation off at Wattai airport were Foreign Affairs Minister Somsavat Lengsavat and many high-ranking officials of the Foreign Affairs Ministry.
