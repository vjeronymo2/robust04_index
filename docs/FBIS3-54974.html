Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers of Uzbekistan has adopted a resolution regulating the distribution of hard currency earnings of enterprises. According to the resolution, enterprises, organizations, and associations of all forms of ownership are obliged to pay a 15 percent tax on foreign currency earnings to the hard currency fund, and another 15 percent must be sold to the republic's Central Bank. On the conclusion of barter export-import operations, the amount of earnings will be calculated from the value of the contract. Work on special measures to monitor the movement of money in foreign currency, in order to exclude the possibility of the unauthorized holding of money in foreign banks, is also envisaged.
