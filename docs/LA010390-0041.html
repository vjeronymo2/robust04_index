January 3, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Dole Food Co., a division of Los Angeles-based Castle & Cooke Inc., has appointed Timothy R. Barron president of Dole Dried Fruit & Nut division, which is based in Fresno. He succeeds Jack Feder, who has been named a vice president of Dole Food. Most recently, Barron was president of the dried fruit segment of the fruit and nut division.
