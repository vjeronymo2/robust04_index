Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al-Annar [January] 3 (JAMAHIRIYA NEWS AGENCY) -- The masses of the Basic Peoples Congresses in Rajban rallied in a demonstration of protest and anger for the tightening of unfair sanctions imposed on the Great Jamahiriya. The masses announced their solid rejection for the blockade and for any language of threats or warnings applied by the western states against the Great Jamahiriya. A statement was issued to express rejection, defiance and the readiness to defend the Great al-Fatah revolution and the Arab nation against the lurking attacks of the vicious neo-crusaders.
