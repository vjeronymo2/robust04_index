BFN [Text] In view of the prevalence of security and stability throughout the governorates of the republic, and in light of the brother citizens' great and positive cooperation with the curfew instructions, the Ministry of Interior announces to all brother citizens throughout the governorates of the republic that it has been decided to reduce the curfew hours. As of today, Thursday, curfew hours will be from 2200-0500. Thus, all businesses and activities in all cities can proceed in accordance with this new schedule, which must be complied with. The ministry wishes to explain that any party, institution, or individual whose jobs necessitate moving from one place to another during the curfew hours must obtain a special license from the Ministry of Interior. Motorists should always carry with them the documents that prove their ownership of the vehicles that they drive. The ministry takes this opportunity to express thanks and appreciation to all citizens for cooperating with the security agencies.
