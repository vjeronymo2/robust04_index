921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / Unions win Pounds 1/4 bn in legal awards UNIONS won more than Pounds 1/4 bn in legal awards for their members during 1991, after spending Pounds 20m on legal services. Most of the 150,000 legal cases a year pursued by unions are actions for damages following personal injuries at work. The survey by the Trades Union Congress covered 28 unions representing 89 per cent of members in unions affiliated to the TUC.
