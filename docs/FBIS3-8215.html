Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Serb aggressors have continued an offensive on the Usora area (northern Bosnia), the local command of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO] reported on Sunday. The Serb forces fired three howitzer shells at civilian targets in the village of Jelah destroying several residential buildings on Sunday morning, the same source said. The HVO units managed to repel a severe artillery-supported infantry attack of the Serb aggressors in the area of Glavasi-Kriz-Komin on the western part of the Usora battlefield last night, whereas on the eastern part of the crisis area the Serb forces failed to carry out an infantry breakthrough in the region of Cerici-Razbjenjak-Makljanovac at the same time. The enemy forces opened antiaircraft weaponry and mortar fire all the night on the Usora battlefield, said the same source. Thus far one HVO soldier has been reported to be gravely wounded. The HVO reconnaissance troops noticed last night a concentration of the Serb aggressors' forces and artillery on the western part of the Usora battlefield. Another offensive of the enemy formations is being expected last few days.
