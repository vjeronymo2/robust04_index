BFN ["Special Dispatch": "Tian Jiyun Inspects Shenzhen, Urges Efforts To Properly Administer the Special Economic Zone"] [Text] Shenzhen, 15 May (TA KUNG PAO) -- Tian Jiyun, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice chairman of the National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee, recently conducted an inspection tour of Shenzhen. On the afternoon of 15 May, Tian Jiyun listened to a report on the zone's work by Li Youwei, Shenzhen party secretary and mayor. Tian fully affirmed the achievements made by the Shenzhen special economic zone [SEZ] in economic and social development, reform, and opening up over the past decade or so and called for further efforts to properly administer the SEZ and give full play to its role as a window. Following his inspection tour of Australia and New Zealand, Tian Jiyun arrived in Shenzhen on 12 May.
