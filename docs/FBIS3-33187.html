Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] During a visit to an exhibit of photographs and crafts of the PRC in Ciego de Avila, PRC Ambassador Xu Yicong pointed out that the relations with Cuba are increasing in the economic sector. The diplomat added that ties in education, sports, and technical consultation have also increased significantly and are a sign of the friendship between the peoples. Xu Yicong described the recently inaugurated exhibit as modest but representative of the feelings and warmth of the Chinese people. Politburo member Alfredo Hondal, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Ciego de Avila Province, and other regional leaders and Chinese diplomats attended the exhibit.
