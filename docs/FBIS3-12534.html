Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A Border Police patrol captured four armed terrorists in Khan Yunus in the southern part of the Gaza Strip this afternoon. Our army affairs correspondent Alon Ben-David reports that the terrorists were carrying two submachine guns and three revolvers. Rioting continued throughout the Gaza Strip today, especially in the southern part of the Strip. Youths clashed with IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers in Khan Yunus and Rafah. Nine residents were lightly injured. Eighteen firebombs were hurled at IDF troops in the Gaza Strip today. There were no casualties. Ten explosive charges were also discovered and safely dismantled. Gaza City and the nearby refugee camps remain under curfew. The IDF has up till now refrained from clamping a curfew on the southern part of the Gaza Strip despite the disturbances which have continued since last Friday [25 February].
