941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Warnford slips to Pounds 2.22m Profits of Warnford Invest-ments slipped from Pounds 2.31m to Pounds 2.22m after tax in the six months to June 24 and directors of the property investment concern said the second half figure would be about the same as for the first. Gross rents and service charges totalled Pounds 5.53m (Pounds 5.79m) and earnings per share came to 5.78p (6.01p). The dividend is held at 2.75p. Mr George Ross Goobey, chairman, is retiring on December 25 and will be replaced by Mr Michael Ross.
