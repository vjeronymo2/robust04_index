920519 FT 19 MAY 92 / European Monitors to invest in Prestwick plant A GERMAN-backed company is investing Pounds 2.92m in a plant to manufacture computer monitors for sale in continental Europe at Prestwick, Ayrshire, Scotland, James Buxton writes. The plant, which is starting with 63 employees and hopes to reach 500 in three years time, is located in Prestwick's Freeport, a zone that offers exporters exemptions from value added tax. The company, European Monitors, is a joint venture between Escom Computer of Germany and Data Technology Holdings of the UK. Escom, a privately owned company based at Heppenheim, near Frankfurt, is one of the biggest distributors and retailers in the German computer market as well as being a manufacturer of personal computers.
