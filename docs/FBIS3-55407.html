Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- A UNIAN correspondent learned from reliable sources on 6 January that an agreement determining Ukraine's debt to Kazakhstan and transferring this debt to the state account was made ready for Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev's arrival in Ukraine, which according to preliminary information is planned for mid-January. Ukraine will have to have 1.2 million U.S. dollars repaid by 1999. The Ukrainian side has not yet defined its position on determining either the debt of Kazakhstan to Ukraine or those of Russia and other CIS countries, although there have already been cases of debts made over to be repaid by the state, in particular in mutual relations with Russia.
