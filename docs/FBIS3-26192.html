Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi, February 2 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese experts team comprising diplomatic and military personnel arrived here early this morning for border talks with India. The Chinese delegtion, which is led by deputy director in the Asia department of the Foreign Ministry Fu Xuehang, will dicuss further confidence-building measures with their Indian counterparts led by joint Secetary of External Affairs Ministry Shiv Shankar Menon [name as received]. The creation of such an experts team by each side was decided during Indian Prime Minister Narasimha Rao's visit to China in September last year.
