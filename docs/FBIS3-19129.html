Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- This morning the Free Democratic Party [FDP] Presidium described its election defeat in Lower Saxony as a warning not to be ignored. Before the FDP Executive meeting which followed, party chairman Klaus Kinkel said it is now time to "roll up our sleeves." Kinkel regards the FDP's difficulty as walking a tightrope between loyalty to the coalition and taking a position of its own: "The FDP has the problem that it is a coalition partner in Bonn and, as far as actual issues and style are concerned, it wants to bring this coalition to a respectable end and it will do so. On the other hand, we have to show independence. So we are caught in a bind."
