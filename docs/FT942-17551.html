940402 FT 02 APR 94 / Markets: Speculators' silver lining - At a glance The silver price has been heading for the skies as speculators pile into the market. This week the bullion price topped Dollars 560 an ounce, compared with a low of Dollars 490 earlier in the year. Some traders say that the big speculators are hoping to push the price to Dollars 8 an ounce.
