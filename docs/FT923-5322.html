920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Review of fines for late VAT CUSTOMS and Excise has launched a review of its default surcharge system for companies that do not pay value added tax contributions on time, after criticisms of its operation. The review comes as part of a wider examination of the VAT penalties system announced earlier this year. It will examine the effectiveness of the system of progressive penalties - designed to encourage timely compliance. The system has been criticised as indiscriminate, providing insufficient warning before fines are incurred, and for being particularly hard on small businesses. VAT Default Surcharge Consultation Paper. HM Customs and Excise, VAT Enforcement Division, St John's House, Merton Road, Bootle, L20 3NN.
