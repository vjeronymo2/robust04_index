BFN [Text] Doha, 18 May (WAKH) -- Qatari Amir His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani has expressed his country's extreme regret over the human and material losses resulting from the current situation in Yemen. He said that considering Yemeni events an internal affair, the State of Qatar hopes that the current situation will end as soon as possible in a manner that preserves Yemen's unity and historic achievements and restores security and stability. The amir made these remarks when he received Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salih Basindwah, who conveyed to him an oral message from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The Yemeni foreign minister left Doha this afternoon after a two-day visit to Qatar.
