Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said that the government has received clarifications from the UN envoys to all questions raised on the ideas for implementation of the confidence-building measures. He also said that the government will announce its position on the substance of the measures next week. The answers were given during this evening's meeting between President Glavkos Kliridhis and the UN envoys headed by Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's deputy special representative in Cyprus. Further talks with the two sides will take place Friday [18 March]. The spokesman said that the government now has a complete understanding of the draft ideas and is satisfied with the clarifications given by the UN envoys. [passage omitted]
