940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Gas meter plant to shed up to 500 Up to 500 jobs are to be axed at the Schlumberger Industries gas meter factory in Stretford, Manchester, in the next year. The plant employs more than 600 people. The company blamed the job losses on a fall in orders from British Gas, the company's main customer. 'Revised purchasing requirements during 1994 have recently been confirmed by British Gas,' it said.
