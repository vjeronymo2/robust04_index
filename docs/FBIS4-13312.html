BFN [Text] A special discussion was held at the prime minister's bureau this morning on the dispute with the PLO over who will have jurisdiction over Israelis coming to Gaza and Jericho. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that Major General Amnon Shahaq and the Foreign Ministry's legal adviser Yo'el Singer, who participated in the deliberations, will return to Cairo at noon. The water discussions apparently concluded successfully. It was decided that Israel will continue supplying water to the settlements in the Gaza Strip.
