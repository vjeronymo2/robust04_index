BFN [Text] Beijing, May 20 (XINHUA) -- The office of the spokesman of the Chinese Ministry of Public Security today expressed indignation over a report by the UNITED PRESS INTERNATIONAL (UPI) which says that a ministry of public security spokesman confirmed the Chinese Government decision to start the indictment process with Wei Jingsheng. The spokesman said that some news media just fabricated news without interview to fool readers. He expressed indignation over fabrication of news, saying that this is against the ethics of journalism and reflects ulterior motives.
