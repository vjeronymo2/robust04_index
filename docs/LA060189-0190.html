ANNUAL PROFESSIONAL BUSINESS LICENSE FEES

City: Anaheim

Fees: $150 per professional; $10 per employee; $17 processing fee

City: Brea

Fees: $50 for the first $50,000 of gross receipts; adjusted upward thereafter

City: Buena Park

Fees: $60 plus $4 per employee

City: Costa Mesa

Fees: $60 for first $200,000 of gross receipts; adjusted upward thereafter

City: Cypress

Fees: $25 first year; renewal based on $0.52 per thousand dollars of gross receipts or flat rate per professional based on Consumer Price Index.

City: Dana Point

Fees: No fee

City: Fountain Valley

Fees: $40 plus $3.50 per employee

City: Fullerton

Fees: $50 per professional; $10 per employee up to 10; $2 surcharge per employee

City: Garden Grove

Fees: $30 per professional plus $3 per employee

City: Huntington Beach

Fees: $45 plus $3.75 per employee

City: Irvine

Fees: $50 for up to 4 employees plus $3 per employee for more than 4

City: Laguna Beach

Fees: Based on estimated or gross receipts

City: La Habra

Fees: $35 per professional plus $5 per employee

City: La Palma

Fees: $66 per professional

City: Los Alamitos

Fees: $75 per professional plus $6 per employee

City: Mission Viejo

Fees: No fee

City: Newport Beach

Fees: $45 plus 30 cents for every thousand dollars over $150,000 in gross receipts, or $75 flat fee

City: Orange

Fees: $35 base fee; $37.50 one-time processing fee or $200 flat fee if gross receipts are more than $275,000 plus $37.50 processing fee

City: Placentia

Fees: $55 per professional plus $11 per employee

City: San Clemente

Fees: $20 for first $10,000 in gross receipts; adjusted upward thereafter

City: Santa Ana

City: Seal Beach

City: Stanton

City: Villa Park

City: Yorba Linda
