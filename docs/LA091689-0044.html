September 16, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Beverly Hills 25, Harvard 6-Beverly Hills (1-1) caught Harvard in the wrong defensive coverage and used its speed advantage to score two first-quarter touchdowns at Beverly Hills.

The Normans took an 18-0 halftime lead when linebacker Terry Fahn picked off a pass by Harvard quarterback Scott Collins and ran 37 yards with one second remaining.

Harvard (0-2) avoided a shutout when running back Kent Dahn scored from the one with 4:43 left. Sophomore Jeremiah Aguolu had 148 yards in 17 carries. SEAN WATERS
