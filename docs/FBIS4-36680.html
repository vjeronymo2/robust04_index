BFN [Excerpt] Lakhdar Brahimi, the envoy of the UN secretary general, arrived in al-Mukalla at 1800 today on his mission to establish the facts surrounding the war being fought in Yemen and with the objective of bringing about implementation of the UN Security Council cease-fire resolution. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, Aden, he said his presence here was to continue talks with the brother officials on the same issues as discussed in Sanaa. He went on to say: I believe that the sides concerned are those in the north and south of Yemen. What we have heard is that they all want a cease-fire, which is what we want and want to implement. Brahimi stressed that first and foremost was the substantiation of the cease-fire. We shall be talking with the brothers about this matter; everything has to be talked about. He expressed regret over the violation of the cease-fire, saying he hoped it would finally be implemented. [passage omitted]
