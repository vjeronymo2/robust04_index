930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Lex joint venture expands HARVEY PLANT, the fork lift truck hire joint venture between Lex Service and Lombard North Central, has acquired Clarion, which services and hires fork lift trucks, for a total of Pounds 3m cash. The consideration will be met from the joint venturers cash resources. The purchase increases Harvey's fleet to more than 6,500 units with 12,000 on servicing arrangements. Phillips & Drew Fund Management has acquired a further 460,000 shares in Lex Service taking its holding to 14.4m shares or 15.4 per cent.
