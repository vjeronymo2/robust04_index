January 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HIS RECORD IS GREAT, EXCEPT FOR JANUARY

Before Trojan fans start placing "Bury Larry" bumper stickers on their cars, they should look at the overall picture:

-- Larry Smith is the only active Pac-10 coach to have an undefeated conference record.

-- He is the only active Pac-10 coach to have won 10 regular-season games.

-- He is the only active Pac-10 coach to guide his team to consecutive Rose Bowls in his first 2 seasons. (And before anyone moans about his Rose Bowl record, remember that Terry Donahue was 0-2-1 in bowl games before his current winning streak began.)

-- Smith may have lost his last 2 games of the season, but wasn't there a coach named Lou Holtz who had the same experience last season?

DAVID PAGE

Ventura

Editor's note: Holtz actually lost his last 3, to Penn State, Miami and Texas A&M in the Cotton Bowl.
