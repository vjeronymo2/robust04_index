Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 21 (YONHAP) -- The floor leaders of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party [DLP] and opposition Democratic Party [DP] on Monday put off indefinitely their planned visit to Europe due to strained situation at home over the breakup of the South-North talks. Reps. Yi Han-tong of the DLP and Kim Tae-sik of the DP earlier planned to visit eight European countries for two weeks beginning March 22. They were to be accompanied by two other solons. Yi told reporters that he met the opposition floor leader earlier in the day and agreed to put off their visit until a later date due to the tense situation. "If and when the situation calms down, we will try to make the visit for about ten days," Yi added.
