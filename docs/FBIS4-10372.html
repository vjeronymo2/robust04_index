BFN [From the "TV Today" newscast] [Text] Tonight, Andres Pastrana sent a letter to 17 other presidential candidates, asking them to demand clarification from the U.S. State Department regarding recent statements in connection with the use of drug money in the current election campaign. In his letter, Pastrana says these statements are very serious, and, therefore, all candidates are obliged to request that specific charges be made, including details on which campaigns allegedly received such money and the origin of any such funds.
