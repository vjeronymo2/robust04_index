920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Wellman makes Pounds 0.7m purchase Wellman, the Midlands-based specialist engineer, has acquired Midcast Engineering, a manufacturer of aerospace components for Boeing, LTV, Textron and British Aerospace. To finance the Pounds 726,000 purchase Wellman has raised Pounds 1.07m via the issue of 2.9m new shares which have been conditionally placed with institutional investors.
