930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Building activity still depressed CONSTRUCTION activity remains depressed in spite of renewed optimism this autumn in the housing market, the Department of the Environment said yesterday. Provisional data shows that total construction industry output was Pounds 8.62bn in the second quarter of the year, down 3 per cent on the second quarter of 1992. Employment in the industry was 4 per cent lower in July, compared with April. The figures are the first of a revised series intended to give a more reliable indication of activity in the industry.
