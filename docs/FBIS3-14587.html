Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has called for an urgent meeting of the PLO leadership in Tunis today to discuss the peace process and the developments in the occupied Arab territories after eight Palestinians were killed distributing leaflets in Gaza and 47 others were wounded by Israeli occupation gunfire. 'Arafat described the incident as a new massacre. He said: We are facing a secret organization from inside the Israeli Army and the settlers, which seeks to destroy the peaceful solution. The Palestinian president said he informed Washington and Moscow, in their capacity as the sponsors of the peace negotiations, about the incident and said the PLO will present a memorandum to the Security Council in this regard. 'Arafat stressed that the Israeli Army and Government are blamed for such attacks against the Palestinian people. [passage omitted]
