Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] An agreement, which has been signed by the leadership of the Kyrgyzfarmacia [pharmacy] company and the Elkashan Private Ltd. company from Karachi foresees supplies of medicine on consignment terms from Pakistan to Kyrgyzstan. Representatives of this company have recently visited Bishkek and discussed not only volumes of supplies, but also prospects for further cooperation. Experts from Pakistan intend to assist us in the production of medication as well as training experts and providing us with sophisticated equipment. The project is financed by a credit, which has been allocated by the government of this Asiatic country.
