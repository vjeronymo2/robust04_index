Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The party school of the Tianjin Municipal CPC Committee held a ceremony for the beginning of the 1994 spring semester on the morning of 1 March. Gao Dezhan, secretary of the municipal party committee and president of the party school, attended and addressed the ceremony. Fang Fengyou and Zheng Zhiying, standing committee members of the municipal party committee, attended the ceremony. Gao Dezhan pointed out in his speech: The municipal party committee has attached extreme importance to the cadres' training and study work. The party committees at various levels across the municipality have paid high attention to the study of the Third Volume of the "Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping." The broad masses of cadres are enthusiastically engaged in the study. An upsurge of studying theories has started in the municipality and a gratifying situation has emerged. Gao Dezhan stressed: We should firmly grasp the fundamental principle of linking theory with practice, pay attention to the results of study, and promote the study work. Linking theory with practice is a fine style of study that our party has consistently encouraged. In the course of study, we should proceed from the actual conditions of Tianjin, keep close contact with reality, use the vivid facts that the people have personally experienced to deepen the (?understanding) about the party's basic theories and basic line, and strengthen the awareness of firm implementation. The most important factor for studying Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory is to master the ability in consciously applying this powerful ideological weapon to solve the realistic problems cropping up in the course of reform, opening up, and modernization; and to upgrade our ideology, understanding, and leadership ability. Only by so doing will we able to [words indistinct] and to make real results.
