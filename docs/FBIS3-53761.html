Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Seven Arabs were wounded during disturbances in the al-Shaykh Radwan neighborhood in Gaza today. Our correspondent Shlomi Eldar reports that soldiers shot and wounded the youths after the latter stoned them. Terry Grill, a senior UNRWA [United Nations Relief and Works Agency] official who drove into the area was also wounded. Grill is the UNRWA official in charge of refugee affairs in Gaza. A general strike was observed in Gaza today following the killing of five Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] members in Gaza and Hebron yesterday.
