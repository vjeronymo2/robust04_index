May 5, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HISTORY OF LITHUANIAN NATIONALISM

The article by Frankel and Kux is very much like the articles which were printed by the Nazis in the 1930s.

Then the mud was slung at the Jewish people. In this article it is slung at Lithuanians. Note the derogatory adjectives, the hateful tone, the distortion of facts and one-sided, broad generalizations which are used to arouse contempt. Hitler built the ghettos based on contempt.

It is disgusting to think that a "historical writer" (Brian D. Kux) is capable of such bigotry.

Mr. Frankel and Mr. Kux, your writing is more suited to the 1930s.

MELANIE MASKOLIUNAS

Costa Mesa
