BFN [Text] Al-Sulaymaniyah, northern Iraq, 9 Jun (INA)--Pitched fighting has continued in various areas in the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate between Jalal Talabani's troops on the one hand, and the forces of the so-called Islamic Movement and the gunmen of the Kurdistan Democratic Party allied with it on the other. Today's edition of the newspaper AL-'IRAQ published here today quoted news reports originating in the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate as saying that the most violent battles witnessed there were those waged in Qal'at Dizah, Banjwin, and Halabjah. The reports added that the gunmen of Mas'ud Barzani managed to kick Jalal Talabani's loyalists out of Qal'at Dizah and completely occupy the entire city after killing several of these loyalists. Jalal Talabani's loyalists fled the city, leaving behind them the bodies of their dead and wounded. Moreover, hundreds of citizens fled the city to neighboring villages and mountain caves, seeking shelter and safety. The news reports also confirmed that battles continued in the areas surrounding the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate. The elements of the so-called Islamic Movement, which supports Mas'ud's group, are attempting to control some areas inside the city of al-Sulaymaniyah, the reports added. Tension and fear spread to other areas and places in the Autonomous Region of Kurdistan because of the mistrust between the two groups of Mas'ud and Jalal.
