940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / International Company News: Rubbermaid in venture with Japanese group Rubbermaid, the Ohio-based plastic and rubber group, yesterday signed a joint venture agreement with Richell, a privately-owned Toyama-based company and one of Japan's leading manufacturers of household products. Richell will put its home and leisure products division, with a Dollars 60m turnover, into the venture. Rubbermaid will hold 40 per cent, with the option of lifting its stake to 51 per cent by the year-end.
