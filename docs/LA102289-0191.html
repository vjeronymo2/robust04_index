October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD: A LOOK AT UPCOMING EVENTS IN LOS ANGELES COUNTY

STRIKE FORUM: The Beverly Hills PTA Council, a neutral party in the strike at the city's public schools, will hold an open forum for parents and community members Monday at 7:30 p.m. at Beverly Hills High School. Administrators and teachers' union leaders will answer questions.

A DREAM COMES TRUE: The Pasadena Tournament of Roses Assn. will announce its choice for queen of the 101st Tournament of Roses on Tuesday.

JUANITA ST. JOHN: The head of the city's Africa trade task force faces a pretrial hearing Tuesday for a misdemeanor charge that she failed to obey a city subpoena for records showing how she spent $180,000. A trail date will be set at this hearing.

WATERFRONT: The Los Angeles Board of Harbor Commissioners will hold a public hearing Wednesday to consider restoration of waterfront property at the Port of Los Angeles.
