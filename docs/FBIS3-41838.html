Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In the past 6 years, 128 people infected with the AIDS virus have been registered in Moscow -- 15 of them have AIDS. According to a spokesman for the local epidemics control commission, from 1987 through 1993 15 AIDS victims died; 4 of them committed suicide. The average age of those infected is 28. The ratio of men to women among them is 8:1. Two people with the AIDS virus were revealed among blood donors.
