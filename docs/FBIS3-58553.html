Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Gursel Balci video report from Agri Mountain region] [Text] The operations launched on Agri Mountain are continuing despite the heavy fog and snow fall. Sikorsky and Cobra helicopters are participating in the operations when the weather permits. The bodies of 28 terrorists were found during searches conducted following the clashes between the security forces and the terrorists in the Korugan-Kislagi region on Thursday [13 January]. Furthermore, it has been determined that at least 20 of the some 100 terrorists who were under fire yesterday night have been killed, and many have been injured. Based on the blood and other evidence, it has also been determined that many injured were taken to other regions of the mountain. A terrorist shelter was discovered during an intense operation in the northern peaks of Agri Mountain. Various equipment, a generator, a television, a video machine, a camera, lighting and medical equipment, and tonnes of foodstuffs were found in the shelter, which has two separate entrances. It was, however, impossible to film due to the heavy fog around the mountain. Operations are continuing in the region.
