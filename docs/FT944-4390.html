941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Archimedes Inv Tst Net asset value per capital share of Archimedes Investment Trust stood at 656.39p at the October 31 year-end, against 704.07p six months earlier and 676.54p at the previous year end. Net revenue for the 12 months amounted to Pounds 352,523 (Pounds 310,345) for earnings per income share of 28.77p (25.33p). A second interim dividend of 17.5p (17p) is declared in lieu of a final, making 26.5p (26p).
