BFN [Text] Moscow, 24 May -- Russian President Yeltsin has signed a decree "on the military uniform and badges of military rank." With this document, in accordance with Article 38 of the Russian Federation law "On Military Duty and Military Service," Yeltsin has confirmed the uniform and badges of rank of servicemen of the armed forces of the Russian Federation and other troops, organs of external intelligence, counterintelligence, the chief directorate for protection and the security service of the president. ITAR-TASS has been told by the president's press service that the Russian Government has also been instructed to confirm within three months regulations governing the provision of soldier's supplies and standard quantities for the supply of items. Yeltsin decreed that the corresponding decrees of the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet, concerning servicemen's badges of rank, be considered inoperative.
