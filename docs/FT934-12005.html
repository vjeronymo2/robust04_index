931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Sony moves into video games SONY, the Japanese consumer electronics company, said it will enter the video game machine market next month, establishing a new company, Sony Computer Entertainment, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The new firm, which is to be set up jointly with Sony Music Entertainment (Japan), will unveil the next-generation game machine at the end of 1994 in Japan, Sony said. The new machine will offer three-dimensional computer graphics and the initial year's sales target has been set at 1m. Sony's entry into the video game market is expected to increase the already intense competition in the new generation of video games.
