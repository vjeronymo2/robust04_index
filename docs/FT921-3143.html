920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Election 92: Thatcher's expression - Ballot Box While Margaret Thatcher will be abroad for the last few days of the campaign, her views will appear in the Sunday Express, apparently until the final Sunday before polling. She will also be travelling the country for the next two weeks, still capable of arousing what the tabloids call Maggiemania. Surely, however, the loyal Tory Express would not allow any indiscretions?
