BFN [Text] The water resources working group within the multilateral talks stemming from the Middle East peace conference continued its meetings at the Muscat Intercontinental Hotel this morning. The sultanate's delegation to this meeting was led by His Excellency Muhammad Bin-'Alawi al-Khusaybi, the Omani ambassador to Japan. A source close to the meeting said the group will discuss the same issues on its agenda again today, including regional cooperation, administering water resources, and collecting data on water and water supplies. The conferees have initially agreed to set up an international research center on sea water desalination in the sultanate. The center will try to coordinate research efforts in order to reduce the costs of sea water desalination technology, in order to enable the countries of the region to get fresh water at low cost, and to reduce dependence on declining quantities of surface and ground waters.
