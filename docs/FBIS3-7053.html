Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- Figures of Mozambique and Zimbabwe praised the advantages of the Korean-style socialism. The general secretary of the Mozambican Parliament said on March 11: Socialism of Korea is the most advantageous one in the world. It is a socialism that enables all the people to be well off evenly. In Korea all people become true masters of the country and are firmly united behind the party and the leader. The Korean-style socialism under the guidance of the great President Kim Il-song and dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is a model and centre of world socialism. Joseph Macheka, a member of Parliament of the Republic of Zimbabwe, in a statement March 6 said now the Korean people, united in one mind around the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, are successfully building the Korean-style socialism, smashing the heinous moves of the imperialists to isolate and stifle the DPRK. He stressed that the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il has developed the Workers' Party of Korea into the most authoritative and dignified party, the most experienced and tested party in the world and strengthened the unity and cohesion of the party behind the leader and made it invincible. The dear leader has thoroughly implemented the line of building an independent national economy set forth by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and thus made the Republic a socialist power not affected by a world-wide economic fluctuation, and built the Korean People's Army into invincible Armed Forces capable of coping with a modern warfare by training the whole Army into an army of cadres and modernizing it with accelerated pace, he said. He said the present of socialist Korea making a long drive with every passing day and a brighter [word indistinct] the Republic are ascribable to the wise guidance of Comrade Kim Chong-il.
