(C) *␣*␣* ( 3 ) A manufacturer may elect to include any or all of its liquified petroleum gas-fueled Otto-cycle heavy-duty engine families in any or all of the NO X averaging, trading or banking programs for heavy-duty engines, within the restrictions described in §86.094&hyph;15.
