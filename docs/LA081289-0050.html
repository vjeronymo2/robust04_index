August 12, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DISCIPLES OF CHRIST WILL BUILD NEW 7-STORY HEADQUARTERS IN INDIANAPOLIS

The Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) has decided to build a new seven-story, limestone and glass headquarters building in downtown Indianapolis at a cost of $15 million to $18 million.

Facing the need for major renovations in current offices, now housed in three separate facilities here, the 1.1 million-member denomination chose to erect the new headquarters.

About 5,000 participants in the denomination's general assembly last week joined in a celebration, "blessing" the new building site.

The headquarters staff numbers about 250. Construction of the building is expected to begin in late 1990 with completion in the fall of 1992. The Lilly Endowment, a funder of religious organizations, has made grants totaling $2 million to assist in planning and relocation.
