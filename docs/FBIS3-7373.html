Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Singapore and Australia have signed a letter of intent promoting closer ties between the armed forces of the two countries. It was signed by the defense minister, Dr. Yeo Ning Hong, and his Australian counterpart Senator Robert Ray in Canberra. Mindef [Ministry of Defense] says the two armed forces will explore ways of extending and enhancing reciprocal activities, exchanges, and cooperation for mutual benefit. Dr. Yeo is in Australia on a four-day official visit at the invitation of Senator Ray. He held discussions with Senator Ray and the minister for trade and acting foreign minister, Senator Bob McMullan. Dr. Yeo also called on the Australian prime minister, Mr. Paul Keating. He is accompanied on the visit by the minister of state for finance and communications, Commodore Teo Chee Hean.
