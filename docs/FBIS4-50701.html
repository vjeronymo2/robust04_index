BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (CNS)--According to a report from the State Statistical Bureau on operation of the national economy in China, the economic development in the country last month was stable and the macro economic situation was basically normal. The price growth, however, was still high, difficulties facing state-owned enterprises still existed and macro control and adjustment still needed to continue. Last month saw an increase of investment in fixed assets and the investment structure was improved with fixed assets investment in last month increased by 44.2 percent over the same period last year and by 8 percentage points over the first three months this year. Investment in transportation continued to grow and that in machinery, light industry and construction of vocational villages and luxurious villas was curbed. The overall investment structure in the country turned for better than the same period last year. The social retail value of consumer goods in last month increased by 24.3 percent over the same period last year. Last month saw a steady increase of financial revenue. In the first four months of this year, the growth of financial revenue was slightly bigger than that of financial expenditure, indicating a normal operation of reform for the financial and tax system in the country. The added-value earned from industry in the country last month increased by 17.9 percent over the same period last year with growth being 2 percentage points higher than the average growth in the first quarter this year. Growth of export was faster than that of import for the first time since September 1992. The state's surplus of foreign exchange continued to grow and the financial situation in the country was on the whole good. Despite large scale of purchasing treasure bonds in the country, residential savings deposits in last month increased by RMB 15.9 billion [renminbi], loans for agricultural and industrial purposes also increased and currency withdrawal from circulation continued. Last month saw a 21.7 percent rise of prices of consumer goods in the country over the same period last year with the prices for grain, catering trade, fruits, clothing and service items increasing by a comparatively large scale. Prices for vegetables, however, declined. In the first four months, residential living costs increased by 22.2 percent over the same period of last year and commodity retail prices by 20 percent. In this period, profits earned and tax paid by state-owned enterprises grew at a relatively large scale but at the same time, loss made by loss-making enterprises also increased by 62.6 percent and capital occupation rate by products by 13.8 percent. [sentence as received] It is predicted that the national economic operation in this month in the country will remain in the "yellow light" zone with various kinds of economic indices to be nearly the same last month.
