Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Ahsen Zahidov, chief inspector of the press service of the Azerbaijani Interior Ministry, said that during an authorized search the city police found three machine guns, one mine, one double-barrelled BM (Zenit) launcher, two AK automatic guns, 91 bullets, 1078 cartridges, feeders for automatic weapons and machine guns, military maps, and sanitary equipment at the Azerbaijan People's Front city headquarters in Nakhichevan. An investigation has been launched into the case.
