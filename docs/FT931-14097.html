930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Dares extends banking facility Dares Estates, the property investment and development group, has reached agreement with its principal bankers for an additional working capital facility of Pounds 1m and a formal standstill arrangement until April 15. During this period, it is anticipated that the ongoing negotiations for the reorganisation of Dares' financial arrangements and capital structure will be finalised.
