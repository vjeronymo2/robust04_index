November 15, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

USC;

SIX TO PLAY IN JANUARY ALL-STAR GAMES

Six USC seniors will play in postseason all-star games.

Flanker Gary Wellman and offensive guard Mark Tucker will play in the Senior Bowl on Jan. 19 at Mobile, Ala.

The same day, linebacker Scott Ross and offensive tackle Pat Harlow will play in the Hula Bowl at Honolulu.

Ross and tailback Ricky Ervins will play in the East-West Shrine Game Jan. 26 at Stanford. Harlow and defensive guard Don Gibson will play in the Japan Bowl Jan. 13 at Tokyo. JERRY CROWE
