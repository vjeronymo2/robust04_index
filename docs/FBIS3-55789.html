Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Zhilyakov] [Text] Krasnodar January 11 TASS -- A group of members of the Small Council of the Kuban intends to seek support from the State Duma and also to institute legal proceedings against governor of the territory, Nikolay Yegorov. Yegorov had signed a decree terminating as of this Tuesday the powers of The Territorial Council of People's Deputies. The apparatus of the council is dissolved. Elections to the Krasnodar territory are to be held on April 3. Until the beginning of the work of the future parliament, matters in the competence of the Territorial Council are to be decided by the head of the territorial administration.
