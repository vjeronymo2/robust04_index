January 19, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

FOCUS: MOLA CENTRE

Mola Centre, located on 40 acres in Irvine, is a mixed-use project that is designed as an urban village and features classic European styling. Grading has begun on the project, and five sculptures of white Carrera marble, created by artist Paolo Borghi of Italy, were unveiled as the centerpiece of the project last month.

Phase I of the center is planned to include an office tower, a 164-unit residential condominium tower, other condominiums and apartment units, a 2,100-seat theater complex, a health club, a nightclub and a day-care center, as well as retail and restaurant space. Phase II will add a 450-room hotel.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: Jamboree Boulevard and Campus Drive, Irvine

Developer: Mola Development Corp., Newport Beach

Architect: McLarand, Vasquez & Partners Inc., Costa Mesa

Square feet: 486,000 in the office tower
