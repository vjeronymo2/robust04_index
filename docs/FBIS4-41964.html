BFN [Text] At an expanded session of the Foreign Ministry collegium, issues concerning the practical implementation of President Nazarbayev's concept on the creation of a Eurasian Union, as well as the participation of the ministry and its foreign missions in tackling the problems resulting from the president's speech to the Supreme Kenges. In a report presented by Foreign Minister Saudabayev he stressed the importance of Nazarbayev's idea on forming a Eurasian Union in the context of strengthening integration tendencies in the world, first of all in [word indistinct] space, and in the context of strengthening the stability and security of states which may become subjects of the Eurasian Union. The need for more active participation by the central apparatus of the Foreign Ministry and by its foreign missions in ensuring wide international promotion of the reforms carried out in the Republic was stressed at the meeting. The collegium endorsed the planned measures, foremost among which is elaboration of our sovereign state's foreign policy strategy. The consultative council's meeting on foreign policy was held at the Foreign Ministry.
