Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, promulgated a decree of the Central People's Committee dated January 12 on the dismissal and appointment of the minister of forestry of the Administration Council. According to the decree, Kim Chae-yul was relieved of his post and Yi Chun-sok was appointed to it.
