921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / Oil clean-up A 23 sq-mile oil slick from the wrecked Greek tanker, Aegean Sea, which spread along Spain's north-western coast, was under control yesterday, local authorities said, Reuter reports from La Coruna. Fierce storms had earlier hampered attempts to contain the spillage from the ship which ran aground at the entrance to La Coruna harbour last Thursday.
