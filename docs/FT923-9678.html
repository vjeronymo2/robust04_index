920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Cornwell Parker Pounds 2.75m acquisition Cornwell Parker, the furniture and fabrics group, is to pay Osborne & Little Pounds 2.75m for Fardis, the French furnishing fabrics company. The cash amount paid will be reduced by a sum, to be determined on completion, of approximately Pounds 1.7m in respect of bank debts assumed by Cornwell Parker. The move is part of Cornwell Parker's strategy of developing a stronger presence for its fabric brands in mainland Europe.
