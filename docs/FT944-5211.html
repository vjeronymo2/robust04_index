941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Arts (Openings): Milan Riccardo Muti adds 'Die Walkure' to his Wagner repertory on Wednesday, the opening night of the season at La Scala. The staging is by Andre Engel, with designs by Nicky Rieti. The cast includes Jane Eaglen, Waltraud Meier, Placido Domingo and Robert Hale.
