BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 20 -- President Le Duc Anh received here this afternoon Foreign Minister of Republic of Korea (ROK) Han Sung-chu now on an official visit to Vietnam. Foreign Minister Han conveyed to President Le Duc Anh best regards from President of ROK Kim Yong-sam. Mr. Han said he was glad to have seen the achievements the Vietnamese people recorded in their renovation process as well as the fine development of the economic cooperation between the two countries. He stressed that the ROK was ready to share experience with Vietnam and cooperate with it in national construction for mutual benefit, and for peace, stability, and development in the region. For his part, President Le Duc Anh hailed Foreign Minister Han's visit as manifestation of the enhancement of the friendly and cooperative ties between Vietnam and ROK. He said he was confident that with good-will from both sides cooperation in economy, trade, education, science and technology, and the implementation of projects on infrastructural construction and technology transfer would gain good results, thus making contributions to the consolidation of peace, stability, and development in the region. On this occasion, President Le Duc Anh asked Foreign Minister Han to convey his invitation to President Kim Yong-sam visit to Vietnam.
