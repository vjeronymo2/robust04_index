920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / Malaysia seizes damaged tanker MALAYSIA has detained a 96,000 tonne oil tanker involved in a collision in the Strait of Malacca which has drifted into Malaysian waters, Kieran Cooke writes from Kuala Lumpur. Nagasaki Spirit, Liberian registered and reportedly owned by a Vancouver company, was carrying 57,000 tonnes of crude oil when it collided with Ocean Blessing, a Panamanian registered container ship, nine days ago. The charred bodies of 12 seamen have been found on the Panamanian vessel; 29 crew from both ships are missing.
