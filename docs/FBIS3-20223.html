Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today described statements made by the European Union [EU] foreign ministers in Ioannina encouraging Skopje's [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] provocativeness as strange. Venizelos called upon the EU foreign ministers to be more careful when making statements so that [FYROM President Kiro] Gligorov does not see them as an encouragement toward his positions. The spokesman added: The Greek positions on Skopje are well known, specific, firm, and clear. Referring to the recent violations of national airspace by Turkish aircraft, Venizelos said that the neighboring country has circles of the political and military leadership who cultivate a sense of tension.
