941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / International Company News: Salomon's forex chief joins Meriwether firm Salomon Brothers is losing another top executive to Long Term Capital Management, the specialist money-management company set up by the Wall Street securities firm's former vice-chairman, Mr John Meriwether, writes Patrick Harverson. Yesterday, Mr Hans Hufschmid, head of Salomon's worldwide foreign exchange operations, announced his resignation from the firm. He is expected to join LTCM shortly, where he will become the latest in a line of top personnel to have left Salomon for Mr Meriwether's operation, which is based in the New York suburb of Greenwich, Connecticut.
