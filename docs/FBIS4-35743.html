BFN [Text] Asad Mubarak, the general prosecutor of the local judicial system in Judaea and Samaria, has said that ever since the autonomy was established in Jericho, there has been no contact between his judicial system and that of Jericho. He noted that the Palestinian Authority had appointed a new judicial system in Jericho consisting of a prosecutor, a judge, and administrative staffers. Our correspondent Mira Amsalem reports that Justice Minister David Liba'i heard Mubarak's remarks during a tour of the court system in Ramallah. In the course of the visit, Minister Liba'i rejected 'Arafat's demand to terminate the application of Israeli military laws in the territories. The justice minister said that the Palestinian Authority has jurisdiction in Gaza and Jericho only. He pointed out that according to the Israeli-PLO agreement, the Palestinian Authority does not have to extradite murderers and terrorists to Israel.
