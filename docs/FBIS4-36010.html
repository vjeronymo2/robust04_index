BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers held an extraordinary meeting this morning under the chairmanship of Acting Prime Minister Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar to discuss Security Council Resolution 924 adopted in its session No. 3,386, which was held on 1 June. After discussing the resolution, the council expressed its appreciation for the resolution based on the provisions of the UN Charter, particularly the Republic of Yemen's sovereignty, independence, and territorial integrity. The council will resume its meeting this evening.
