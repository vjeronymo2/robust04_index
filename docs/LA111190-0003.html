November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

With interest in ETs and UFOs soaring, it is time to bring back "The Invaders," the well-executed series from 1967 that focused on a young architect's quest to thwart an alien takeover of terra firma. Roy Thinnes, the star of that series, could take up where he left off, combatting the invasion.

Franklin R. Ruehl, Glendale
