Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (INA)--[Iraqi] Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met on the sidelines of the Arab League Council meetings at noon here today with Sudanese Foreign Minister Dr. Hasan Abu-Salih. The two ministers discussed a number of Arab issues, particularly the threats to Sudan by external forces, the agenda of the Arab League Council meeting, and bilateral relations. Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf also met with Comoran Foreign Minister Said Mohamed Said Ahmed, who is attending an Arab League meeting for the first time. The two ministers discussed bilateral relations and ways to develop these relations in the interest of the two countries. They also discussed a number of Arab issues and the agenda of the Arab League meeting. The two meetings were attended by Sa'd 'Abd-al-Majid al-Faysal, under secretary of the Foreign Ministry, and Dr. Nabil Najm, Iraq's permanent representative at the Arab League.
