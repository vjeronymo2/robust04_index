Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] There was a confrontation in Guerrero State between Party of the Democratic Revolution [PRD] members and state public security agents in Cutzamala de Pinzon, in the area of Tierra Caliente. The confrontation occurred when PRD members staged a demonstration to demand the dismissal of Mayor (Ramperi Suarez Berrun). Policemen prevented the PRD members from seizing the municipal building, triggering the confrontation and some shooting. State authorities said at first two policemen died in the confrontation while another six were injured. The PRD state leadership revealed while two members received bullet wounds and many others suffered bruises, no one died. In the meantime, Cutzamala de Pinzon is living in a climate of tension.
