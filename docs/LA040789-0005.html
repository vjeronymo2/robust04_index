April 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"Most doctors would say, get some bed rest. But of course, these people don't have beds, or if they do, they usually have to give them up during the day."

-- Fran Seizer, executive director of the Venice Family Clinic, commenting on the homeless people afflicted with sunburn, dehydration and hypertension aggravated by the heat wave.
