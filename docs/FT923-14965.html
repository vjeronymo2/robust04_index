920704 FT 04 JUL 92 / Finance & The Family: House prices fall 0.5 per cent House prices fell 0.5 per cent last month after rising in April and May, according to Nationwide building society. An average house cost Pounds 55,671 in June, down Pounds 253 from the previous month, according to the Nationwide house price index. The society said many people were still delaying moves because of fears over job prospects and financial security.
