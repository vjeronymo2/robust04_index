930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / People: Macmillan Macmillan, the UK publisher, has appointed a new group managing director to take responsibility for book publishing operations in the UK. Iain Burns, who has been a non-executive director there for the past three years, has been involved in publishing for nearly 20 years having joined Collins in 1973. He has also worked for International Thomson and Octopus. After Reed International's takeover of Octopus in 1987, Burns became chief executive of Abaco Investments. Nicholas Byam Shaw remains executive chairman of Macmillan. ***** Derek Hunt, the director of finance at Thames Television since 1985, has decided to leave the company in June 'to devote more time to his other interests'. He will be succeeded by Nick Humby, the present financial controller. Humby, 35, who qualified as a chartered accountant with Price Waterhouse, has been appointed deputy director of finance and joins the board as director of finance on July 1. ***** Bruce Hewett, formerly group director of current affairs at Southern Water, has been appointed director of technical affairs at SOUTH WEST WATER; he succeeds Bill Dickens who retired last autumn. ***** John Hogan, chief operating officer of LASMO, has been appointed to the board.
