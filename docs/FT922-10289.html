920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / Pay deal for some NHS staff agreed NATIONAL Health Service administrative and clerical workers yesterday voted 'reluctantly' to accept a pay offer of Pounds 400 a year or 4.75 per cent, whichever is the greater. The offer will add 4.93 per cent to the pay bill and covers 140,000 workers. More than 60,000 will receive the flat rate.
