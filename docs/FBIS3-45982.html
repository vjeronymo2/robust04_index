Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Lilian Wu] [Text] Taipei, Jan. 21 (CNA) -- Taiwan's trade deficit with Japan registered a record US$14.22 billion in 1993, up 10.5 percent from the previous year, the Ministry of Economic Affairs said Friday [21 January]. The ministry noted that Taiwan exported US$8.97 billion worth of goods and services to Japan in 1993, up 0.9 percent from the previous year, while imports increased 6.5 percent to US$23.19 billion. The ministry said imports from Japan accounted for 30.1 percent of Taiwan's total imports last year, the same ratio as in 1992. Parts, components and machinery made up 90 percent of the imports.
