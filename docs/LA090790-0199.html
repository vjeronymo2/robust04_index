SPOTLIGHT

JIM EVERETT

Ram quarterback Coach Dick Coury on Jim Everett: "Probably next to Joe Montana, he's as good as any quarterback in the NFL. How's that for a summary? Because of his knowledge of the game and his tremendous ability to lead a team. He's always been an excellent leader since the day he got here. He's probably one of our best-conditioned players, both physically and mentally. He's just an outstanding guy."
