931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: New evidence in Bridgewater case Evidence of alleged discrepancies has been found in documents concerning the trial of four men convicted of the murder of newspaper boy Carl Bridgewater in 1978. Jim Nichol, solicitor for the three surviving prisoners, said: 'This is truly fresh evidence. The home secretary will be compelled to send it to the Court of Appeal.'
