931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Ferguson International Holdings FERGUSON International Holdings, the labels, hangers, printing and publishing group, is paying up to Dollars 11.5m (Pounds 7.5m) for Red Wing Products and the affiliated Commander Industries. The deal was effected through Ferguson Investments, the group's US holding company. Red Wing, based in Long Island, New York, is a maker and distributor of garment hangers and related products.
