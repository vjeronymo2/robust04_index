940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican stocks ended sharply firmer at a new closing high for the second straight session, driven by hopes for a peaceful ending to the Chiapas conflict and expectations of favourable fourth quarter earnings. The IPC index of 37 leading shares finished the day with a gain of 30.29 points, or 1.12 per cent, to 2,743, led by a resilient rally by Telmex stocks. In Brazil the Bovespa index gained 0.7 per cent to 68,921.
