Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- In the view of the Federal Economics Ministry, the west German economy will have overcome the recession by the end of March. In the first quarter of 1994, the real gross domestic product of the old laender will increase again over the same period of the previous year for the first time since the end of 1992, the ministry stated Tuesday [15 March] in its monthly economic report. Despite the 0.5-percent decline from the third to the fourth quarter of 1993, economic activities have overcome the lowest point. The last time the west German GDP achieved a real plus (0.9 percent) compared to the same three-months period of the previous year was in the fourth quarter of 1992. In 1993, the decline in the first quarter was 3.4 percent but diminished in the course of the year to 1.9, 1.4, and finally 0.9 percent in the last quarter.
