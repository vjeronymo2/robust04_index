Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Police have warned the members of the public to stay away from Louis Botha Avenue as thousands of Zulus are returning to Alexandria via this route. Members of the public have been asked to avoid Wolhuter and Koch Streets. At least 2,000 Zulus are currently gathering at the George Goch Station where they are being addressed by Zulu leaders. Another large group of marchers are waiting for trains at the Jeppe Station. People believed to have been injured during the march are streaming into Johannesburg and Hillbrow Hospitals, and the situation in both hospitals is said to be chaotic. A spokesman for the Johannesburg Hospital said the victims were suffering from gunshot and sjambok wounds, and multiple injuries. Altogether 42 people were admitted by the casualty section of Baragwanath Hospital in Soweto following violence in the area. A spokesperson said 30 of those admitted had bullet wounds and three people were in a critical condition, the other 12 were stabbed, beaten or had been struck by motor vehicles.
