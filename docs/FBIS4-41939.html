BFN [Report by Omer Kebapcioglu in Baku] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] President Geydar Aliyev was pleased with the Turkish prime minister's parliamentary motion requesting authority to participate in the multinational force in Azerbaijan and to set up installations in Turkey to extend support to this force. Replying to our questions, Aliyev said that a multinational force including Turkey would prevent efforts to impose solutions on the problem. Turkey is the greatest guarantee for Azerbaijan, he said, adding that the latest move by the Turkish Government proves this once again. Aliyev expressed the belief that Turkey's participation in the multinational force will help solve the Nagorno-Karabakh problem quickly and justly.
