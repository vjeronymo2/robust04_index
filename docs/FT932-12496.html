930428 FT 28 APR 93 / Observer: Down the hatch Perhaps the City of London is not so unflappable in the face of IRA terrorism after all. An urgent message arrived yesterday from a PR firm hastily rearranging a press conference on May 4. 'Due to bomb damage at the City of London Club over the weekend, we are advising all invitees of a change of venue to a more secure location,' it said. The new place? The Gun Room, HMS Belfast.
