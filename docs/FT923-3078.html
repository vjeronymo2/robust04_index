920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: J Billam advances to Pounds 239,000 Maintained margins and a reduced interest charge helped J Billam, the Sheffield-based precision engineer, report increased interim profits. On turnover virtually unchanged at Pounds 3.08m, the pre-tax line for the six months to June 30 amounted to Pounds 239,000 which compared with Pounds 109,000 last time, struck after an exceptional debit of Pounds 107,000. Gearing at the period-end was 36 per cent, against 47 per cent in December. Interest payable took Pounds 34,000 (Pounds 51,000). Earnings per share jumped from 4.5p to 9.9p and the interim dividend is raised to 1.9p (1.64p).
