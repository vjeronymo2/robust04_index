July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

REPRINTS OF TIMES' SURVEY OF HOME BUILDERS AVAILABLE

Reprints of The Times' 18th annual Survey of Residential Building in Southern California are now available.

To order copies of the survey, priced at $4 each, please send a check or money order, specifying the number of reprints wanted, to 18th Builders Survey, Marketing Research Department, Los Angeles Times, P.O. Box 60195, Los Angeles, Calif. 90060.

Please allow two to three weeks for delivery. Builders participating in the survey will be mailed five complimentary copies.
