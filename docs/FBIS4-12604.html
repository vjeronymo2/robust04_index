BFN [Text] Industry and Trade Minister Mikha Harish left for Morocco this afternoon to represent Israel in the final round of GATT talks. This agreement is supposed to lift trade restrictions and expand it by $300 billion per year. Israel can also benefit from this agreement. The conference will open in Marrakech tomorrow. It will be attended by representatives of 114 countries. Minister Harish told our correspondent Arye Schorr he hopes to use the conference for holding contacts with representatives of Arab countries that do not have relations with Israel.
