940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / International Company News: Siecor makes Dollars 135m purchase Siecor, a joint venture between Germany's Siemens and Corning of the US, has agreed to pay Dollars 135m cash for various telecommunications hardware and components businesses presently owned by Northern Telecom, the Canadian telephone equipment maker, writes Bernard Simon. The deal will expand both the size and product range of Siecor's components business, adding plants in Mexico, Puerto Rico and Canada.
