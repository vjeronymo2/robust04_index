Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today at the Belarusian State Committee for External Economic Relations, a meeting was held between Orjan Berner, Sweden's ambassador extraordinaire and plenipotentiary to Russia and charge d'affairs to Belarus, and Uladzimir [name indistinct], chairman of the committee. In the course of the meeting, the officials exchanged opinions on prospects for economic cooperation between the two countries.
