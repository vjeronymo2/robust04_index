BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- A national seminar on the chuche idea was held in Helsinki on the occasion of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Pekka Rantala, chairman of the Finnish National Committee for the Study of the Chuche Idea, made a report, followed by speeches. They noted that President Kim Il-song authored the chuche idea during the anti-Japanese revolutionary struggle and has applied it in all fields of the revolution and construction and built Korea to be a model country of socialism as today. They said Korea is making a great progress in the revolution and construction under the banner of socialism, not wavering in face of the pressure and provocations of the imperialists, because it is guided by the chuche idea authored by President Kim Il-song. The chuche idea now serves as the undisputed guiding idea in the building of socialism and communism and the most universal guiding idea from which the world people learn, they added. A congratulatory message to President Kim Il-song was adopted at the seminar. DPRK book, photo and handicraft exhibitions and film shows for April 15 took place in Montevideo, Uruguay, Almada City, Setubal Province, Portugal, and Islamabad, Pakistan, and at the Madagascan metropolitan special police headquarters, the Lagos Liaison Office of the General Film Bureau of Nigeria and the sky line company of Thailand.
