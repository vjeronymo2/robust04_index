BFN [Editorial Report] Managua Radio Nicaragua Network in Spanish at 1200 GMT on 13 June reports a government delegation will arrive in Paris this week to meet with members of the advisory board of the World Bank to analyze the impact of international cooperation on Nicaragua's process of economic stabilization. Jose Evenor Taboada Arana, president of the Nicaraguan Central Bank, has said the meeting will be held in Paris on 16 and 17 June to confirm a $50 million loan promised to Nicaragua over the next three years. Nicaragua will also seek a $200 million loan to accelerate the peace process in Nicaragua, Taboada added.
