June 17, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

PARKER AND POLL BATTLE AT FORUM

Edward (Pee Wee) Parker of Houston faces Jesus Poll of Los Angeles for the vacant International Boxing Council 126-pound junior championship Monday night at the Forum.

Parker brings a 20-4-2 record with eight knockouts into the scheduled 12-round fight. Poll is 21-3-3 with four KOs. Both fighters are coming off losses.

On the undercard, Rafael Ruelas fights Abe Gomez in a scheduled 10-round featherweight bout. Ruelas is 17-0 with 14 KOs. Gomez is 14-4-1 with seven KOs. Preliminaries will open the program at 7 p.m.
