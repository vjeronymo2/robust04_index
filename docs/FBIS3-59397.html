Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] In 1994 Sichuan Province plans to attain a gross value of agricultural output of 85.6 billion yuan, a 4 percent increase; gross output of grain 43 million tons; cotton 130,000 tons; oil crops 1,480,000 tons, an increase of 141,000 tons; sugarcane 2 million tons, an increase of 193,000 tons; and to remove 68 million hogs from stock, an increase of 620,000 hogs. The gross output value of town and township enterprises should reach 221 billion yuan, a 30 percent increase.
