The heading and paragraph (c) of section 75.13 are revised to read as follows: §75.13 Review by the Director. * * * * * (c) Upon determining that the State's plan of self-insurance equals or exceeds the standards set forth in §75.11 of this subpart, the Director shall certify that the State is exempt from the requirement for the purchase of flood insurance for State-owned structures and their contents located or to be located in areas identified by the Director as A, AO, AH, A1&hyph;30, AE, AR, AR/A1&hyph;30, AR/AE, AR/AO, AR/AH, AR/A, A99, M, V, VO, V1&hyph;30, VE, and E Zones. The Director shall review the plan for continued compliance with the criteria set forth in this part and may request updated documentation for the purpose of such review. If the plan is found to be inadequate and is not corrected within ninety days from the date that such inadequacies were identified, the Director may revoke his certification. Dated: October 17, 1994.
