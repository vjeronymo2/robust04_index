September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD CLOSURES

Caltrans plans the following closures on area freeways this week:

One lane of the northbound Pasadena (110) Freeway will be closed Tuesday in South Pasadena from Fremont Avenue to Glenarm Street from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m.

One lane of the westbound San Bernardino (10) Freeway will be closed Wednesday in El Monte from West Covina Parkway to the San Gabriel River (605) Freeway from 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.

One lane of the eastbound Pomona (60) Freeway will be closed Friday in Monterey Park from Atlantic Boulevard to Markland Boulevard from 9 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.
