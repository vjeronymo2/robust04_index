BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Mainayev] [Text] Bishkek 18 May TASS -- Commander of CIS coalition peacemaking forces in Tajikistan, Colonel-General Valeriy Patrikeyev paid a short visit to Bishkek, the capital of Kyrgyzia, on Wednesday [18 May]. The press centre of the Kyrghyz Defence Ministry told ITAR-TASS that during his few hours' stay in Bishkek, the commander met with Kyrgyz Defence Minister Myrzakan Subanov and discussed with him the possibility of strengthening the Tajik-Afghan border. The Kyrgyz Battalion staff was recently fortified with military personnel from the Murgab frontier guard detachment, but the situation remains tense.
