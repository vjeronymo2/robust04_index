930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Treasury admits company profit fall PRE-TAX profits of the 100 largest British companies have fallen by more than 40 per cent at constant prices in the three years since 1989, the Treasury said yesterday. Mr Stephen Dorrell, financial secretary to the Treasury, said profits totalled Pounds 22bn last year at constant 1987 prices, down from Pounds 37bn in 1989.
