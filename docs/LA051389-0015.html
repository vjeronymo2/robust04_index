May 13, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

IN 'FIELD OF DREAMS,' NOT EVERYTHING IS RIGHT

In the current movie, "Field of Dreams," Shoeless Joe Jackson bats right-handed and throws left-handed.

In the 1,344 games that Shoeless Joe played in the American League, he batted left-handed and threw right-handed.

Picky picky.

DUDLEY ABOURISK

Granada Hills
