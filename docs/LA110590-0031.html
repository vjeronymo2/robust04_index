November 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Tuning In WKRP Again: Hugh Wilson, the creator and executive producer of the situation comedy "WKRP in Cincinnati," will supervise the creation and oversee the casting, writing and production of the new version of "WKRP" set to begin production this spring.

The series, which will air next fall, will be produced by MTM Enterprises, which produced the first series. Also, Wilson will direct the first episode. The new show will feature several of the cast members from the first show, including Richard Sanders, Frank Bonner and Gordon Jump. CLAUDIA PUIG
