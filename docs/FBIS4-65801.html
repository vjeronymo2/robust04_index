BFN [Text] Berlin (DDP/ADN) -- According to Chancellor Helmut Kohl, with the election of Roman Herzog, Germany has chosen a "good federal president." Following Herzog's election, Kohl said on ZDF [Second German Television] that Herzog, as a man who believes in a state based on the rule of law, will be able to fulfill his duties well. Kohl stressed he was completely convinced that the Free Democratic Party [FDP] would also vote for Herzog in the end. The FDP made a decision which is also important for the future. He saw the election of Herzog as a "good omen" for the outcome of the Bundestag elections.
