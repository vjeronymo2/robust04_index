940314 FT 14 MAR 94 / Company News (This week): Hoogovens - Other companies The Dutch steel and aluminium company will publish annual figures on Thursday which are expected to show an improved trend in steel but continued difficulties in aluminium. The company, which raised Fl 362m (Dollars 189m) through a rights issue in late 1993, is struggling to contain costs but it is also at the mercy of factors beyond its control, such as the flood of cheap aluminium from the former Soviet Union.
