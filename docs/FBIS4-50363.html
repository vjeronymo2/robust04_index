BFN [Text] At the invitation of Shandong Province, the economic observation group of South Kyongsang Province of ROK headed by Yi Tok-yong, director of the regional economic bureau of ROK provincial government, came to the Shandong to pay a friendly visit. Governor Zhai Zhihao cordially received the ROK guests, including Yi Tok- yong, at the Qilu Guest House of Jinan on the evening of 13 May. Shandong established friendly relations with South Kyongsang Province last year. The visit paid by the ROK economic group is aimed at carrying out overall observation of Shandong's investment environment so as to encourage more ROK enterprises to invest their money in Shandong or to open plants. During the reception, Zhao Zhihao briefed the guests on the province's situation in reform, opening up, and economic development. He also urged both sides to further enhance contact and cooperation and to promote friendship and economic development between Shandong and South Kyongsang Province.
