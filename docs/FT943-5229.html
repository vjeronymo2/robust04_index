940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Bangladesh Mr Shamsul Islam Khan, the Bangladesh Commerce Minister, asked parliament this week to approve a new companies act seeking to safeguard the interests of shareholders and investors. The bill seeks to make all company information and statistics transparent, remove disparity between public and private sector companies, and relax government control on management of companies.
