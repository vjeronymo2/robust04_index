921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / Parliament and Politics: Clarke pledge on war refugees NO REFUGEES from the former states of Yugoslavia refused political asylum in Britain would be returned to war zones, Mr Kenneth Clarke, the home secretary, assured the Commons last night. He said the government was considering what assistance it could provide to local authorities which include points of entry whose resources were under strain through having to accommodate large numbers who had fled from Bosnia and other areas. Mr Clarke faced a barrage of protests from Labour MPs when he sought to justify the new powers being sought by the government to secure the speedier removal from Britain of bogus applicants for political asylum. The bill was given a second reading by a government majority of 45 (321-276).
