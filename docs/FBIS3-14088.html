Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In al-Shaykh Radwan in Gaza City security forces last night arrested 30 residents during an operation searching for members of the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] military wing 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam. Twenty-four of the detainees were released after a short period of time, while the remaining six are being investigated for aiding members of 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam. In disturbances in the territories yesterday, 16 Arab residents were wounded by Israeli Army fire.
