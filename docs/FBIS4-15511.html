BFN [Text] The Chief of the National Security Agency under the Belarussian [Belarusian] government Major-General Valeriy Pavlov told INTERFAX Minsk had not yet voiced its view of the Russian plans to build bases in Commonwealth republics. But, Pavlov said the deployment of strategic units of a foreign army in Belarus was out of question as it would contradict the Belarussian decision to be a neutral and nuclear-free country. In Pavlov's view, the deployment in Belarus of Russian air bases or antiaircraft units would keep Belarussian armymen in good shape. The Belarussians would familiarize with advanced know-how and be able to resolve their social problems. According to Pavlov, Minsk would study in detail every scenario for the creation of foreign army bases in Belarus.
