BFN [TA SR report: "For the Nuclear Plant's Safety"] [Text] Bratislava--"An agreement has been signed between the Jaslovske Bohunice nuclear power plant and the Siemens Aktiengesellschaft, Berlin und Munich company on the gradual reconstruction of the Bohunice nuclear power plant," said J. Sidor, press secretary of the Slovak Energy Enterprise, Bratislava. He added that, on the basis of this agreement, the plant's safety systems will be improved stage by stage, and, thus, its equipment will correspond to the normal international standard for water-pressure reactors. In this context, the older reactors will also be supplied with the necessary equipment.
