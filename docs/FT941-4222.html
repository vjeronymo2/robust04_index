940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / International Company News: Italian dairy group up 22% Parmalat Finanziaria, the Italian dairy products group, yesterday said consolidated turnover rose 74 per cent to L2,845bn (Dollars 1.7bn) in 1993 on the back of acquisitions, writes John Simkins from Rome. Operating profits rose 22 per cent to L251bn, and net borrowings from L500bn to L822bn after expenditure of L386bn on acquisitions. On the basis of provisional results for the first quarter, the group expects sales to rise by 34 per cent to L3,820bn in 1994 and operating profit by 37 per cent to L344bn.
