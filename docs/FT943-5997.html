940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / Unison expected to recommend rejection of pay offers The health group executive of Unison, the public-service union, is expected tomorrow to recommend rejection of pay offers averaging 2.4 per cent to 400,000 NHS workers not covered by pay review bodies If the recommendation is accepted Unison will ballot the members - including ambulance, ancillary, administrative, clerical and technical staff - on a programme of industrial action.
