Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] According to a Libyan diplomat, Libya has at its disposal $18 billion in cash, which it has withdrawn from banks. Therefore, the diplomat says, Libya can withstand international sanctions for another seven years, even if it were to face an oil embargo. The diplomat notes that there are 4 million people living in Libya whose basic--subsidized--needs of such goods as sugar, oil, and tomato puree amount to about $1.5 billion annually.
