April 15, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST : SPRING VALLEY

A 12-year-old boy was struck by a car Friday in Spring Valley and taken to Children's Hospital, where he was listed in fair condition Saturday, a hospital spokeswoman said.

James Rainbolt, a Spring Valley resident, suffered head injuries after he was hit, said spokeswoman Jeannie Berghorn. The boy was transported by Life Flight helicopter to the hospital at 6:36 p.m.

Officials have not yet released details on the cause of the incident.
