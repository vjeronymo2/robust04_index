910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Fractional rise at Clayton Son & Co Clayton Son & Co, which makes bulk storage, pressure vessels, fabrications, pipes, presses and machinery, reported a fractional increase in pre-tax profits for 1990 - from Pounds 540,000 to Pounds 543,000. Turnover rose to Pounds 17.41m (Pounds 15.98m) but net interest payable worked out at Pounds 42,000 (Pounds 11,000 receivable), though the share of losses from associated undertakings was down at Pounds 57,000 (Pounds 119,000). After tax of Pounds 194,000 (Pounds 221,000), earnings emerged at 13.8p (12.6p) per share. The final dividend is unchanged at 9.3p for an increased total of 11.8p (11.5p).
