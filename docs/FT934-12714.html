931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Gainsborough fetches Pounds 3.5m A Gainsborough landscape owned by Royal Holloway College, part of London University, fetched Pounds 3.5m, a record for the artist. Auctioneer Christie's said the painting was sold in a private deal to an anonymous British collector who planned to lend it to the college from time to time.
