BFN [Text] Russian oil and gas will be supplied to Belarus duty free. This is the essence of a resolution, which has been adopted by the Government of the Russian Federation. The document, signed by Viktor Chernomyrdin, determines the mechanism for facilitating oil supplies, including condensed gas [hazavy kandensat] and products of oil refining, to Belarus. An assent was granted to the proposal of our Republic to extend Russian pricing policies to cover energy resources and raw materials to Belarusian economic bodies beginning from the moment the two countries' monetary systems are unified. Appropriate ministries and committees of the Russian Federation have been instructed to safeguard organizational measures for implementing this resolution, which is one of the first concrete steps toward the accomplishment of the agreement -- signed by Vyachaslaw Kebich and Viktor Chernomyrdin -- on the unification of the Belarusian and Russian monetary systems.
