BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 21 -- A delegation of senior official of some provincial and municipal party committees of Vietnam led by Nguyen Tan Dung, member of the party Central Committee, secretary of the provincial party committee of the southern province of Kien Giang, made a working visit to China from April 9 to 19. The 10-day visit was made at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC). While in China, the delegation had working sessions with a delegation of the Chinese Communist Party led by Zhu Shanqing, deputy head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, the State Commission for Restructuring the Economic System. It also met with the leadership of the two provinces of Guangdong and Hainan. The two sides exchanged views on the issues relating to the socio economic development in the present situation of each country. The Vietnamese delegation also studies the renovation process in agriculture and industry in China.
