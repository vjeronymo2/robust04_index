920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Appleby Westward declines 20% Profits of Appleby Westward Group, the USM-quoted Spar and VG wholesaler, fell from Pounds 2.06m to Pounds 1.64m pre-tax for the 53 weeks ended February 28. The 20 per cent downturn was scored from higher sales of Pounds 72.9m (Pounds 69.1m). The directors said trading in the second half had shown an improvement and that trend had continued into the current year. Earnings emerged at 20.7p (25.8p). In view of the improving trend the dividend for the year is being lifted to 8.8p (8.5p) via an increased final of 5.8p.
