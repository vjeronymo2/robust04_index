940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Electric & General Electric & General Investment had a net asset value of 212.3p at May 31, a 14 per cent improvement on the 185.8p of a year earlier. Earnings per share for the year were 3.3p (3.21p). A final dividend of 1.65p (1.6p) makes a total of 3.2p (3.1p).
