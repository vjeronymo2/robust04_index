May 16, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

DODGER GAME POSTPONED BY RAIN

Tuesday night's game between the New York Mets and the Dodgers was postponed by rain and will be played Thursday night at 7:35 p.m., the Mets announced today. Thursday had been scheduled as an off day for the Dodgers, who are to travel to Montreal for a weekend series with the Expos.
