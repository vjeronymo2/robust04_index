BFN [By reporter Wu Ping (0702 1627)] [Text] Stockholm, 8 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, this afternoon went to the Chinese Embassy in Sweden to call on embassy staff and meet with Chinese nationals and Chinese student representatives in Sweden. Li Ruihuan briefed the embassy staff on the domestic situation. He encouraged them to work hard and make even greater contributions to further strengthening Sino-Swedish friendship. During his meeting with Chinese nationals and student representatives, Li Ruihuan expressed the hope that they would promote the development of Sino-Swedish relations in all areas by making use of their intimate knowledge of both Sweden and China. He also expressed the hope that Chinese students would acquire Sweden's advanced knowledge and use it to serve their motherland. Li Ruihuan also had a photo session with the embassy staff, local Chinese nationals, and Chinese student representatives.
