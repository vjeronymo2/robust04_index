BFN [Text] An ISPR [Inter-Service Public Relations] press release says the Indian soldiers resorted to unprovoked and indiscriminate firing with automatic weapons on the civilian population on village (Viladadoot) in the Nokial subsector as a result of which a six-year old child was killed. In another incident of firing by Indian troops from the across the Line of Control on village Karothi, a young man was critically wounded.
