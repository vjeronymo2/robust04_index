921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Dundee and London net assets lower Net asset value for Dundee and London Investment Trust fell from 277p to 221p over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue for the year to the end of October was lower at Pounds 1.84m (Pounds 1.92m) for earnings per share of 10.84p (11.32p). An unchanged final dividend of 8.2p is recommended for a maintained total of 12p.
