931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Euro-sceptic may resign next week SPECULATION was mounting last night at Westminster that Sir Richard Body, the Tory MP for Holland with Boston, might resign his seat. Sir Richard, a strong Euro-sceptic, said in September he was considering leaving politics for a career writing books on agriculture. His office said yesterday he would not make a statement until next week. Loss of the seat would cut the Tory majority to 15.
