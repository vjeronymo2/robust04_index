March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NATURALLY, READERS TAKE RIPS AT GARVEY

Steve Garvey, who has recently impregnated two women while marrying a third, stated that, "I'll live up to my moral obligations, which I feel strongly about because I'm a Christian."

Apparently he feels that meeting his financial obligation to the children is tantamount to meeting his moral obligation to them.

He struck out on this one. But perhaps some good will come of this. Maybe Californians will finally see Garvey for the sanctimonious phony he always has been.

BOB LENTZ

Sylmar
