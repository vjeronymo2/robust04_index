930410 FT 10 APR 93 / World News in Brief: Iraqis fire on US warplanes in no-fly zone Four US warplanes were attacked by ground artillery while patrolling the no-fly zone in northern Iraq. The US administration said the aircraft responded to the fire by dropping four cluster bombs before returning safely to their base. Iraq said a soldier was wounded when an aircraft dropped cluster bombs on a military position guarding a dam.
