930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Rentokil offer unconditional Rentokil, the environmental and property services group, has declared its Pounds 75.7m offer for Securiguard unconditional as to acceptances. By noon yesterday, acceptances had been received for shares representing 40.35 per cent of the security and cleaning company's equity. Rentokil had already bought 29.7 per cent of Securiguard when it lifted its bid to 345p per share, so now speaks for 70 per cent of the equity.
