BFN [From the "JB Report" column by Teodomiro Braga: "War in the North"] [Text] On 13 May, the Venezuelan National Guard again invaded Brazilian territory in Roraima, arresting gold prospectors at the Dicao airstrip. Jose Altino Machado, leader of the prospectors, criticized: "They invade our territory and arrest Brazilians, while the Brazilian Army applauds and the Foreign Ministry hides."
