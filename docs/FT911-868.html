910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Parliament and Politics: Tax relief for training welcomed LABOUR yesterday welcomed the government's move to extend tax relief to people who pay for their own vocational training, but said delaying its introduction until next year showed that training was not a government priority. During the debate on the Finance Bill, Mr Nicholas Brown, a Labour treasury spokesman, said the issue dividing the parties remained the priority given to training in the current economic climate.
