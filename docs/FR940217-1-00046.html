DATES: Comments must be filed on or before April 4, 1994, and reply comments on or before April 19, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 94&hyph;6, RM&hyph;8410] Radio Broadcasting Services; The Dalles, Oregon AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission requests comments on a petition filed by Larson-Wynn, Inc., seeking the allotment of Channel 224C3 to The Dalles, Oregon, as the community's third local FM broadcast service. Channel 224C3 can be allotted to The Dalles in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements without the imposition of a site restriction, at coordinates North Latitude 45&hyph;35&hyph;42 and West Longitude 121&hyph;10&hyph;24. DATES: Comments must be filed on or before April 4, 1994, and reply comments on or before April 19, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Federal Communications Commission, Washington, DC 20554. In addition to filing comments with the FCC, interested parties should serve the petitioner, or its counsel or consultant, as follows: Elwyn T. Wynn, President, Larson-Wynn, Inc., Radio Station KODL&hyph;AM, P.O. Box 741, The Dalles, Oregon 97058 (Petitioner).
