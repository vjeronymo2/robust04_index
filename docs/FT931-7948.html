930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Central ordnance depot at Donnington TRAFALGAR HOUSE CONSTRUCTION companies have won Pounds 28m worth of new work in two construction management contracts in the UK and the US. Trafalgar House Construction Management has won a three-year contract managing an estimated Pounds 15m of works services at the Army's central ordnance depot at Donnington, near Telford. Work at the base will involve controlling and managing the electricity supply, water supply and disposal, boilers, roadways, landscaping and maintaining the fabric of the buildings. Trafalgar House Construction's US subsidiary, Federal Construction, has won a Pounds 13.5m management contract for the runway extension project at southwest Florida regional airport in Fort Myers. The 1,090 metre extension of runway 6-24 will increase its overall length to 3,636 metres.
