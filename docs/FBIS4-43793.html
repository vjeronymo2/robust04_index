BFN [Unattributed report: "Adi Pinter New Chairman of United Greens"] [Text] Adi Pinter, a former soccer coach, was elected chairman of the United Greens by the national assembly of the United Greens on Sunday [19 June]. He received 70 percent of the votes, thus defeating the Vienna biologist Gerhard Pretzmann. Pinter, who is succeeding Josef Buchner after internal differences, has spoken against the ratification of the European Union treaty in parliament.
