Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sergey Khetagurov, the head of the North Ossetian Government, announced his resignation at today's meeting of the Council of Ministers. He did so after the results of the election to choose the republic's first president were announced, RIA notes. Over 60 percent of the electorate cast their votes for his election rival, Akhsarbek Galazov, chairman of the North Ossetian Supreme Soviet. In accordance with the law on the president, Galazov is also head of the executive branch. He will shortly submit a new cabinet list to parliament.
