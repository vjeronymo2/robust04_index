931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Water companies make progress Two water companies controlled by Lyonnaise des Dumez returned higher profits for the half year ended September 30. Suffolk Water's rose from Pounds 2.4m to Pounds 2.9m pre-tax on turnover of Pounds 9.9m (Pounds 8.2m). Earnings per share emerged at 56p (43p) but the interim dividend is 23.3p (23.7p). Essex Water returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 9.9m against Pounds 9.4m on turnover of Pounds 35.4m (Pounds 33m). Earnings per share were 90p (113p). The interim dividend on the voting A shares and non-voting B shares is 39.1p (36.8p).
