941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / Finance and the Family: Increased dividend flow - At a glance Water company shares were marked up this week after Thames Water announced a higher than expected interim dividend, up 11 per cent to 8.2p. The market had expected an 8 per cent increase. Thames was the first of the privatised water companies to bring out interim results, and its increased payout made analysts optimistic about the prospects for the rest of the sector. However, Thames chairman Sir Robert Clarke, warned investors not to expect such a generous dividend under the new pricing regime. 'Life is going to be tougher,' he said.
