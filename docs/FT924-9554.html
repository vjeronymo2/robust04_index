921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Impshire reduces loss to IPounds 116,000 Impshire Thoroughbreds, the Dublin-based bloodstock group, reduced pre-tax losses to IPounds 116,000 (Pounds 126,000), for the six months to June 30. The deficit last time was IPounds 159,000. Operating losses fell to IPounds 48,000 (IPounds 68,000) and there was a lower IPounds 68,000 (IPounds 91,000) deficit reflecting the change in value of bloodstock. The company continued to reduce its bloodstock holdings because of the continuing weak market. Losses per share were 1.8p against 2.4p.
