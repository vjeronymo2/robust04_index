BFN [Text] Bratislava, May 6 (CTK) -- The Slovak Foreign Ministry has welcomed the self-rule accord concluded between Israel and Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO), the ministerial press office told CTK today. The accord is an important moment in fulfilling the principles of the Israeli-Palestine agreement which were signed last September in Washington and an important watershed on the way to the definitive settlement of the Arab-Israeli conflict, the ministry stated.
