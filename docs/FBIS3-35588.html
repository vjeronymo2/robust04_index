Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lev Konorev] [Text] Belgorod (Russia), 28 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- A meeting of leaders of the border oblasts of Russia and Ukraine ended in Belgorod today with the adoption of a package of documents setting out the principles of economic and scientific-technical cooperation between the 10 border oblasts of Russia and Ukraine. Participants unanimously spoke in favor of creating an economic cooperation zone, granting their territories special status, and instituting direct financial accounting between enterprises in the border oblasts of Russia and Ukraine. The adoption of the statutes of the council of the leaders of border oblasts of Russia and Ukraine was a concrete expression of the coordinated approach by the Russian and Ukrainian sides. A permanent working body -- the council's executive committee -- was set up to coordinate cooperation between the regions. Participants appealed to the presidents of Russia and Ukraine to conclude an intergovernmental agreement between the two countries and to adopt the necessary legislative acts in the shortest possible time.
