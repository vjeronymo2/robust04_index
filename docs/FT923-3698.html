920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Dixons buys rest of Codic Dixons Group, the retailer, is to acquire the outstanding 10 per cent in Codic, its Belgium-based property company, from Mr Jacques Lemal, the company's founder and managing director. Mr Lemal will retire from the company and its subsidiaries at the end of this month. The move is in accordance with the agreement reached when the majority of Codic was acquired in 1985. No price was disclosed.
