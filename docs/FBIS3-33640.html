Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Shim'on Peres and Yasir 'Arafat announced that progress has been made in the Davos talks. Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa told our correspondent Gid'on Kouts that the agreement will be signed in Cairo, apparently within two weeks. Our correspondent reports that differences of opinion remain only on the Jericho area issue. On the issue of the border crossings it was agreed that a Palestinian attorney will be permitted to attend the interrogation of suspects detained at the crossings. The Davos negotiations will continue this evening at a junior level. Peres and 'Arafat will intervene if called to do so. Minister Peres will leave Davos tomorrow morning.
