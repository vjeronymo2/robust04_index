Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By Ivan Novikov, ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 13 Jan -- This afternoon the State Duma deputies began putting forward candidacies to the post of chairman of the State Duma. Nine candidacies have been put forward -- Aleksandr Braginskiy, Yuriy Vlasov, Sergey Shakhray, Valentin Kovalev, Sergey Kovalev, Vladimir Lukin, Anatoliy Lukyanov, Vladimir Medvedev and Ivan Rybkin. The Liberal Democratic Party leader, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, was the 10th, but he has just withdrawn his candidacy, referring to his unwillingness to "act in the role of an overseer."
