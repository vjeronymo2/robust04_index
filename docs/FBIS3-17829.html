Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Mikhail Stetsyuk for TASS] [Text] Sevastopol 29 Mar, TASS -- The command of the Crimea-based Black Sea Fleet has demanded that the Ukrainian Army pay its debts for electric power supplies and construction works provided by the fleet. A message sent Tuesday to Ukrainian Defence Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy mentioned that the Ukrainian Army debts to the fleet amounted to 20 billion karbovanets (about 0.6 million dollars). In case the debts are not settled before 10 April, the command will consider abandoning all construction works at the Ukrainian military sites and cutting off power supplies for the units of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, the message said.
