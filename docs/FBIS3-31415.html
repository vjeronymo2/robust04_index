Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prishtina, February 15 (ATA) -- A staged process against Mr. Rustem Demaj, from Vrelle of Istog in Kosova, concluded in the Serbian Court of the region of Peje. Mr. Demaj, a captain in rest, was sentenced with five years in prison accused for jeopardizing of the territorial integrity. The defendant didn't accepted the accuse on the whole. The Serbian Court of Peje began on February 14, the judgment of ten Albanians being under the charge of lesion of the territorial integrity of the so-called Yugoslavia. Three of the defendants, Mr. Myrteza Nura, writer, and Mr. Naser Zeka and Mazllum Kumanova, who were investigated, refuted the accuse on the whole. At the same time, the Serbian Court of Prishtina went on with the judgment of nineteen Albanians, which has began since a year ago.
