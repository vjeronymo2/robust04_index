Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe January 12 TASS -- Missile fire was again opened from the territory of Afghanistan Tuesday on the 12th post of the Moskovskiy detachment, guarding the Tajik frontier. ITAR-TASS has learned from military sources in Dushanbe that there were no casualties among Russian servicemen. According to the same sources, the number of armed provocations on some sectors of the Tajik-Afghan border has increased sharply since early January. For instance, fire was opened on the deployment area of the 13th post of the Moskovskiy border guard detachment on Sunday. Tajik mujaheds fired eight shells at the post from Afghan territory. Borderguards of the Pyanj detachment have cut short three attempts of small groups of Tajik opposition fighters to break into Tajikistan from Afghan territory in the past five days.
