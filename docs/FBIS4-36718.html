BFN [Text] Quoting a security source, a Bakhtar Information Agency correspondent reports that Commander Mohammad Zaman of the 195th Division of Dostam's militia and a number of his men joined the Mojahedin of the Islamic Army in Durah-e Hayratan, Mazar-e Sharif Province yesterday. The men who joined the Islamic Army pledged to carry out self-sacrifices for the defense of the territory of the Islamic State alongside Mojahedin and forces of the Islamic Army.
