910425 FT 25 APR 91 / Trading decline sees Dencora fall to Pounds 2m With the management preferring to retain commercial properties rather than sell them at the depressed prices of 1990, Dencora, the East Anglian property group, saw profits fall from Pounds 7.06m to Pounds 2.21m pre-tax in the 12 months to end-December. Net asset value was down 17 per cent at 280p (336p). Turnover dropped to Pounds 34.34m (Pounds 41.22m), with a contraction in trading to Pounds 27.31m (Pounds 36.1m) and an expansion in rental income to Pounds 7.04m (Pounds 5.13m). Net interest payable was Pounds 405,000 higher at Pounds 5.35m. Fully diluted earnings tumbled to 7.7p (21.6p) but the single dividend is maintained at 5.5p. Bank borrowings at the year-end were up at Pounds 71m (Pounds 59m).
