920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Redland gets just 1.7% of Steetley The hostile Pounds 630m offer by Redland for Steetley, its rival building materials group, had, by 3pm on Monday, been accepted in respect of 2.65m ordinary shares, representing about 1.7 per cent of Steetley's equity. In addition, valid acceptances had been received in respect of 18,677 Steetley 3.15 per cent cumulative preference shares - about 4.8 per cent of the issued preference capital. The offers and partial cash alternative have been extended until 3pm on February 24.
