November 26, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PASTOR AND MISSION FUNDS

I take exception to the implication The Times made in its Nov. 20 edition in which a picture of Pastor Chuck Swindoll was shown with a recap of the funds his mission was capable of generating in 1988.

With all the "witch hunting" taking place in our society today regarding televangelism, I think it only fair that you write an article on the great things that "Insights for Living" has done for people throughout the world.

If you're not willing to that, how about publishing the profits of the Los Angeles Times?

RON KEE

Garden Grove
