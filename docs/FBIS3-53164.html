Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Presidential Council vice chairman, received U.S. Ambassador Arthur Hughes in Aden today. They discussed ties of cooperation and means to improve them. They also exchanged views on current developments and the efforts to end the crisis and find practical solutions to the issues and problems facing the march of development to protect unity and strengthen the democratic march in our country. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Presidential Council vice chairman, voiced his appreciation for the United States' interest in helping our country to overcome the crisis and find solutions to developmental issues and problems. He praised the bilateral ties and said he hoped for further progress and improvement. Brother Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas today received U.S. Ambassador Arthur Hughes in Aden, the country's trade and economic capital. They discussed ties of cooperation between the two countries and prospects for their development. They also discussed current developments in the Yemeni political crisis.
