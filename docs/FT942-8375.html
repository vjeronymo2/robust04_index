940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Fine Decor shows 44% advance to Pounds 3m Fine Decor, the printed wallcoverings maker and fabrics designer which came to the market last June, lifted pre-tax profits by 44 per cent from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 3.03m in the year to January 31. Turnover rose to Pounds 37.6m (Pounds 33.2m) with exports contributing Pounds 16m (Pounds 13.4m). Earnings per share advanced to 17.16p (13.73p) and a proposed final dividend of 3.7p makes a total of 4.7p. On current trading Mr Roger Regan, chairman, said that first quarter sales had been in line with the corresponding period last year, but sales forecasts showed a stronger second half performance.
