930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / International Company News: Matif accepts IBJ, Midland MATIF, the French futures exchange, has accepted IBJ (France) and Midland Bank as members of the exchange. The Industrial Bank of Japan unit said the move would allow the it to give clients access to the exchange part of its clearing service in the world's major futures markets. Midland Bank, part of the HSBC group, said it wanted to strengthen its presence in markets. Matif's 39 foreign members make up 40 per cent of membership.
