920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / Environment: Bush will go to Earth Summit PRESIDENT George Bush confirmed yesterday he would go to the Earth Summit in Brazil next month, and rejected criticism that his demands have blocked chances for a breakthrough there, Reuter reports from Washington. White House officials have been divided over whether he should attend the conference, but concessions by other nations on a global warming treaty tipped the scales in favour of his going. In London, Mr John Major, UK prime minister, also to attend the meeting, sought to play down expectations of what might be achieved, saying there was 'bound to be a limited amount of success' at the Earth Summit.
