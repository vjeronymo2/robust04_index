Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 12) TURAN -- A meeting between the Armenian and Azerbaijani speakers of parliament, Babken Ararktsyan and Rasul Guliyev, respectively, begins today. The meeting is to take place at a point along the border between the Azerbaijani district of Kazakh (Azerbaijan) and the district of Idzhevan (Armenia). According to preliminary information available, the sides will discuss settlement options for revolving the conflict peacefully. It was announced that the meeting, which began at around two o'clock in the afternoon, will also include participants from the Georgian parliament.
