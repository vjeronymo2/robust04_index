940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Servicemaster in Pounds 6.7m agreed bid Servicemaster Limited Partnership, through TMX-Europe, its newly formed Dutch holding company, has made a recommended Pounds 6.7m offer for Peter Cox. Cox was formed in 1989 via a management buy-out from John Mowlem. It is engaged in timber treatment and damp-proofing, building restoration, pest control and hygiene services, and the manufacture of an electric fly killer. TMX is offering 227p cash for each Cox ordinary share. Shareholders can elect to receive unsecured loan notes guaranteed by Servicemaster instead of cash.
