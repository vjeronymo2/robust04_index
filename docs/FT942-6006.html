940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / Euronews financial relaunch The 15 public television companies which own Euronews said they had decided on a financial relaunch of the satellite news channel, and if this failed to materialise by October they would reconsider their investment in it. Lyons-based Euronews said shareholders wanted to prevent last year's start-up loss of FFr50m (Pounds 5.8m) continuing.
