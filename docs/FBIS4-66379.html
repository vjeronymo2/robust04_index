CSO [Text] On 15 March, the "Xizang Yangbajain Geothermal Deep Well" project passed appraisal by the China Energy Expert Committee in Qiaoxia County. Xizang is rich in geothermal resources and data support State energy development criteria for the creation of 10,000-kilowatt-class geothermal electric power plants. The Yangbajain deep well project would make use of a single-unit 12,000-kilowatt geothermal power plant that draws on deep-well high-temperature, high-energy underground heat sources. The project could be completed and in operation in 1995.
