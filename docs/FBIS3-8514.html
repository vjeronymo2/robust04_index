Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Representatives of the Serbian Consultative Council, the Council of the Congress of Bosnian-Muslim Intellectuals, and the Croatian Coordinating Committee held a joint meeting today at which they examined the general agreement on the establishment of a federation and the general agreement on confederation with Croatia. The joint stance of the Serbian Consultative Council, the Council of the Congress of the Bosnian-Muslim Intellectuals, and the Croatian Coordinating Committee, is that the sovereignty of Bosnia- Herzegovina as a state over its entire territory, peace, and human rights for all its citizens and peoples must be secured in the future documents, and this appears in a statement from today's meeting. At the meeting they expressed their full support for the initiative to call an assembly of the citizens of Serbian nationality and Serbian ethnic origin, as was initiated by the Serbian Consultative Council, the statement reads.
