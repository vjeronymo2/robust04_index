BFN [Unattributed report: "Ambassador on Call"] [Text] French Ambassador Francois Scheer, 60, has overreached himself with his criticism of [German Foreign Minister Klaus] Kinkel, which he launched via the German press. "One more blunder and he will be gone," it was said in the French Foreign Ministry last week. At home Scheer has been known for some time for his willfulness. However, so far French journalists have deliberately kept silent about his excesses. "Something like the incident in Bonn is simply inconceivable among friends," it is being said at the Quai d'Orsay. As an excuse, it was stated that Scheer, who comes from Strasbourg, formulated his criticism more strongly than intended because of his inadequate command of German.
