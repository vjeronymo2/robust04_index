Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Mar (MENA)--Dennis Ross, the U.S. coordinator for the Middle East peace negotiations, and his delegation arrived in Cairo from Tunis this afternoon on a working visit of several hours. Ross will hold talks with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa on developments in the region, efforts to remove obstacles to the peace negotiations, and the results of his talks with the Palestinian leadership in Tunis.
