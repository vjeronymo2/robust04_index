BFN [Unattributed report: "Patrol Clashes With Gunmen in Amman"] [Excerpt] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Well-informed security sources in Amman said yesterday that a clash had taken place between a Jordanian security patrol and two gunmen belonging to the "Afghans" organization. It resulted in their arrest and the confiscation of quantities of arms. The sources told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT that the clash took place a few days ago and that a member of the patrol was wounded. It was discovered that the two detained men belong to the group of 10 arrested last week on charges of forming a banned organization and planning to carry out terrorist actions inside Jordan. The sources said that the clash took place in a suburb west of the Jordanian capital, Amman, in Mahalah called Wadi al-Sir. [passage omitted]
