920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Newey advances to Pounds 968,000 at halfway Newey Group reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 493,000 to Pounds 968,000 in the half year to June 30. Turnover amounted to Pounds 12.2m, compared with Pounds 14m. Earnings per share emerged at 26.2p (11.6p). The company is wholly owned by William PrymWerke of Germany.
