BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to President Nouhak Phoumsavan of the Lao People's Democratic Republic on his 80th birthday. President Kim Il-song in the message noted that President Nouhak Phoumsavan joined in the revolutionary movement in his early years and has worked devotedly at responsible posts of the party and the state for the consolidation of national independence and the happiness of the people and for the peace and prosperity of the country. He wished the Laotian president, a close friend and brother, longevity in health and greater success in fulfilling his heavy duty as president.
