BFN [Text] OAU leaders and member governments yesterday unanimously called for the lifting of the unjust measures imposed on the Great Jamahiriyah. Moreover, they expressed their appreciation for the positive initiatives presented by the Jamahiriyah with a view to solving its crisis with western states. At the end of OAU's 30th summit held in Tunis, African leaders adopted a resolution, issued by the Arab League, which called for the two suspects in the Lockerbie affair to be put on trial at the headquarters of the International Court of Justice. The African leaders stressed that this proposal was the suitable solution to this crisis and appealed in their resolution to the concerned western states to respond to initiatives calling for dialogue and negotiations to reach a settlement of the crisis, according to the sixth chapter of the UN charter which calls for the conflicts to be solved via negotiations and legal settlement.
