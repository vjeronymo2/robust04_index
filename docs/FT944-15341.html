941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / Complaints about British Gas up 6% Complaints about British Gas rose 6 per cent in the first nine months of this year, the Gas Consumers Council reported, after a 14 per cent fall last year. The main areas of concern are problems with service and repair work, disputes over gas bills and arguments over the contract conditions of the Three Star Service Scheme, where complaints have risen 33 per cent.
