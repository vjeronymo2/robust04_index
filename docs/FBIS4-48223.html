BFN [Text] The Private Newspaper Publishers' Association, PRINPAG, condemned the attack on the offices of the Free Press in Accra, in which unknown assailants smeared the place with night [as heard] soil. A statement issued by the association described the attack as part of a long chain of acts of commissions and omissions, which have been calculated to intimidate the independent press into abandoning its responsibility of contributing to the building of a decent society in which the principles of probity and accountability are respected. The private media representative on the National Media Commission, Mr. Adu Kwabena Asem has, in a similar statement condemned the soiling of the premises of the free press.
