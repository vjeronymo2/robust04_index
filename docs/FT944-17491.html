941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / International Company News: Goldsmith lifts share in Bendigo Mining Sir James Goldsmith, the Anglo-French investor, has raised his beneficial interest in Bendigo Mining, a small listed company looking at reworking the Bendigo gold field in Victoria, writes Nikki Tait. He has purchased a further 6m shares, to take his total holding to 13.5m. Historically, Bendigo has been Australia's second most productive field after Kalgoorlie's 'golden mile', although mining halted in the 1950s.
