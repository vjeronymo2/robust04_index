December 10, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PLAN AHEAD: Speaking of arts grants (above), Security Pacific Bank paid $25,000 this year to be honorary producer of South Coast Rep's "A Christmas Carol," but when the programs came out, oops, the usual credit was missing. . . . Smoothing this over fell to SCR receptionist Holly Van Holt and her volunteers, who are applying 10,000 gold stickers to programs to credit the bank. "I'm only on the thousandth sticker," she said. "I'm not even close."
