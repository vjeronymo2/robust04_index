BFN [Text] The British aircraft carrier Ark Royal, which was anchored in Piraievs last weekend, was to be the third target of the 17 November terrorist organziation, which claimed responsibility for the attacks against the offices of two insurance companies on Kifissia Avenue last night. In a telephone call to a private radio station, 17 November stated that it was also going to strike at the aircraft carrier. At 1230 today, a passerby accidentally found two 2.3-inch rockets in a metal pipe and a firing mechanism on a building site along the Miaoulis coast in Piraievs. The rockets were defused by explosives experts.
