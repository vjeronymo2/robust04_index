940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks displayed an easier tendency in continued light trading, although the TSE 300 composite index lost just 3.7 points at 4,206.8. Declines outscored advances by 412 to 288 after volume of 48m shares valued at CDollars 534.8m. Eleven of the 14 stock groups ended lower, with real estate falling 1.94 per cent. Alberta Energy, up CDollars 1/2 at CDollars 21 3/8 , said it will restructure its forest products assets into a new unit and proceed with an initial public offering of the unit's shares when market conditions warrant. It will retain at least a 50 per cent stake in the subsidiary. The company also said it expects record earnings and cash flow in 1994.
