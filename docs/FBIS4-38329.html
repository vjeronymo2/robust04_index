BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin signed a decree today extending the state of emergency in the Ingush-Ossetian conflict zone to the Malgobekskiy and Sunzhenskiy Rayons of Ingushetia. This decree has to be approved by the Federation Council. It is worth mentioning that Boris Yeltsin's actions will provoke a strongly negative reaction in Groznyy. Here these rayons are considered to be historically Chechen lands. This is what (Mavlen Salimov), an aide to the president of Chechnya, said to Radio Ekho Moskvy about this: [Begin Salimov recording] We consider that these actions are unlawful, and consider them as aggression, as an attempted act of aggression. [end recording] [Announcer] Also today, a high-ranking official from the Chechen state security department told INTERFAX that Russian troops are being redeployed today on the border of the republic. Khamid Zhemoldayev, deputy head of the department, says that special units of the Russian Ministry of Internal Affairs, equal to an army division, have arrived in Prigorodnyy Rayon of North Ossetia. Also today, Chechen President Dudayev held an emergency meeting with power ministers.
