Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Sarajevo, Jan 6 (TANJUG) -- One woman was killed and six civilians were injured in fierce Muslim attacks on the Serb part of New Sarajevo, Serb military sources reported Thursday. The Muslim attacks started [word indistinct] 22:00 hours (local time) Wednesday but stopped after about two hours when the Serb side returned fire. They were resumed early in the morning Thursday. The Serb side lodged a complaint with the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] Command asking for the attacks to be halted fault of which it would answer back with all means of arms at its disposal. Muslim artillery and infantry attacks were reported in north Bosnia in the Maglaj region and an attempt of a Muslim sabotage group to infiltrate into Serb territory was thwarted in the Doboj front. Serb military sources report that in the region of Srbobran (formerly Donji Vakuf), central Bosnia, Muslims are attacking Serb positions and concentrating their forces which could mean that they are getting ready for more heavy fighting. In (?the night) of Wednesday/Thursday the Muslims also mounted attacks from the Gorazde enclave, eastern Bosnia, on Serb defence positions around Foca and Cajnice. Along the Sava river (north Bosnia) the Croat forces renewed artillery attacks last night firing 30 grenades on Serb positions. The attack stopped when the Serb fighters returned fire. In the Mostar valley, south-west Bosnia, fierce fighting continues between Croats and Muslims, erstwhile allies against the Serbs. Both sides use all means of weapons at their disposal in the combats in Mostar and its surroundings, including artillery and tanks, acording to Serb military sources.
