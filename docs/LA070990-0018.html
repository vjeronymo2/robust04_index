July 9, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Ex-Abba Members File Suit: Two members of the disbanded Swedish pop group Abba are suing their former record company for alleged underpayment of millions of dollars in royalties. Agnetha Faltskog and Benny Andersson, two members of the foursome whose hits in the mid-1970s included "Dancing Queen" and "Fernando," say that Polar Music International has not carried out an agreement to increase their royalties to 9% from 3% since January, 1983 -- which could amount to $27 million. GREG BRAXTON
