930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Observer: Writer's cramp London's literary world was buzzing yesterday. 'Wanted - billionaire art-lover' read the classified ad tucked away in the FT's London edition. Who was the 'distinguished playwright/author' planning to leave England, 'due to health', offering to sell 51 per cent of all 'past, present, future' for ' 1/2 m'? All Observer could discover was that John Osborne, Harold Pinter et al are staying put.
