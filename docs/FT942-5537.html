940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto ended modestly lower in moderate trading, after Moody's Investors Service downgraded Canada's foreign-currency debt. The TSE 300 composite index lost 9.1 at 4,280.6 and falls outscored rises by 385 to 321 after volume of 54.2m shares valued at CDollars 568.1m. The composite index opened higher and stayed in positive territory throughout the morning. But after the Moody's announcement at midday, the market declined and remained mildly lower for the rest of the session. Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce dipped CDollars 3/8 to CDollars 29 1/2 . Its second-quarter earnings increased to CDollars 217m, or 86 cents a share, from CDollars 180m, or 75 cents a share, a year earlier. Seagram rose CDollars 1 to CDollars 42 7/8 following its annual meeting.
