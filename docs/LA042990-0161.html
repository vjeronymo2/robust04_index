April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

REAGAN'S FALL

I have heard from a couple of people who at one time thought highly of Reagan that his $2-million fee was a most demeaning act. They are sorry now; I've been sorry for the last 10 years.

Frankly, I do not know what that man has to fill up a library with, since he can't remember anything.

RUTH H. RALPH

North Hollywood
