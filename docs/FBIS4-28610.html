BFN [YONHAP report from Tokyo] [Text] Quoting a North Korean source, Japan's JIJI News Agency reported that North Korea decided to withdraw from the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA]. JIJI News Agency reported that through its Central News Agency, North Korea issued a statement by the spokesman of the North Korean Foreign Ministry on the evening of 13 June and said that North Korea decided to withdraw from the IAEA. JIJI News Agency also reported that in the statement North Korea emphasized that if international sanctions are carried out regarding suspicions of its nuclear development, it means an immediate war, and that the war will be merciless [mujabihangot].
