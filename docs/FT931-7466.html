930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: East German Inv Net asset value per share at the East German Investment Trust improved from DM2.71 to DM2.8 (114p) over the year to December 31. Net revenue fell from DM2.53m to DM701,000 for earnings per share of 1.4 pfennigs (6.3 pfennigs). The dividend is 0.5 pfennigs (5 pfennigs).
