Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] ITAR-TASS reports that the Russian Government has approved a draft agreement with the Latvian Government on reciprocal visits by citizens. The document regulates the procedure for entry, exit, transit, and for the citizens of one state staying on the territory of the other using visas and documents recognized by both states as valid for foreign trips. The head of the Russian delegation at the talks with Latvia, which, as is known, began in Riga on Tuesday, is instructed to sign the agreement on behalf of the Russian Government.
