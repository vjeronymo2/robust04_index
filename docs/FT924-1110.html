921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / The Lex Column: Hong Kong Jardine Matheson doubtless deplores being caught up in the cross fire between Beijing and Mr Chris Patten. But one cannot resist a sneaking admiration for China's adept manipulation of the Hong Kong stock market. Although it leaves the Hang Seng temporarily vulnerable, it shows China has a much more sophisticated grasp of capitalist ways than even the most ardent Russian reformer. That may be to Hong Kong's advantage after 1997.
