April 9, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

CITY TO BUY PROPERTY FOR SENIORS COMPLEX

The City Council has agreed unanimously to buy property on East Amerige Avenue for an apartment building for low-income senior citizens.

The owner, First Lutheran Church of Fullerton, has offered to sell the property for $750,000.

The council, acting as the Redevelopment Agency, agreed to search for a developer willing to build up to 50 studio or one-bedroom apartments and keep the rents affordable for low- and moderate-income seniors for the life of the building.

The city would sell the land at a discount and might agree to pay for other improvements at the site to attract a builder willing to keep rents low, redevelopment manager Terry M. Galvin said.
