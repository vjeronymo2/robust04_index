Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cape Town 31 Dec SAPA -- In a telephone call to SAPA on Friday afternoon, a Mr Zwaye Mfeya said he was the commander of the APLA [Azanian People's Liberation Army] unit which attacked the bar just before midnight on Thursday, killing four people and wounding five. Mr Mfeya said he was phoning from central Cape Town. "The Afrikaner regime should expect more attacks. APLA confirms these operations will continue as long as our people don't have the vote...Ours is the vote for majority rule. APLA operations will continue as long as our people are being maimed and killed by the SA Defence Force and police." SAPA was unable to immediately verify the authenticity of the claim. Mr Mfeya would give no more details of the attack other than those reported.
