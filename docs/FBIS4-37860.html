BFN [Text] Delegations from the Alliance of Palestinian Forces and other Palestinian popular organizations staged a sit-in at the headquarters of the International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC] in Damascus today to show solidarity with the Palestinian prisoners and detainees held in Israeli occupation jails. The delegations presented memorandums to the ICRC representative in Damascus addressed to the ICRC in Geneva and the UN secretary general asking them to intervene for the release of Palestinian prisoners and detainees and to ensure their right to express their views on all that concerns their cause, people, and homeland.
