CSO ["Election Results in Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast"] [Text] On 10 April, in Kosiv e.d. No. 200 of Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast, Economy Minister Roman Shpek defeated (with 50.1 percent of the vote) Vasyl Hrechanyuk, an economist; in Nadvirna e.d. No. 201, Mykhaylo Kostytskyy, dean of the law faculty at Lviv University, defeated People's Deputy of Ukraine Dmytro Zakharuk; in Rozhnyativ e.d. No. 203, Borys Kozhyn, former commander of the Ukrainian Navy (supported by Rukh), defeated Orest Dychkovskyy, the KUN candidate; in Snyatyn e.d. No. 204, Lidiya Hryhorovych, a physician, defeated Ivan Hnidan, also a physician; in [NB TO EDITOR: the name and number of electoral district is missing in text, but probably Tlumach e.d., since that is the district that Osadchuk represented in the former Supreme Council], Petro Osadchuk, a poet and a deputy to the old Supreme Council of Ukraine, defeated Roman Luchytskyy, the Rukh candidate.
