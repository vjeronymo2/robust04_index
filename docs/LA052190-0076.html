May 21, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

2 MEN SHOT TO DEATH IN LYNWOOD OUTSIDE FAST-FOOD SHOP

Two men in front of a fast-food restaurant in Lynwood were shot to death early Sunday, deputies said.

Deputies identified the victims as Robert Collins, 41, of Compton, and Fenton Anderson, 28, of Los Angeles.

The incident occurred in front of Fast Food Deli Burgers in the 12600 block of Long Beach Boulevard, Deputy Rafael Estrada said. "The employees reported hearing shots, but they didn't see anybody, so we don't have (an explanation for) why the crime occurred," Estrada said.

The men were dead when deputies arrived.
