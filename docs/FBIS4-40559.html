BFN [Text] Moscow June 17 ITAR-TASs -- Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet left Moscow for home today. He was seen off by Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation Viktor Chernomyrdin at Vnukovo-2 Airport. The head of the Vietnamese Government completed his official visit to Moscow, during which he held talks with the Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin and signed the treaty of friendly relations between Russia and Vietnam.
