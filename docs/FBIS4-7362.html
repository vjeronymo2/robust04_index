BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Iranian Embassy in Korea Saeed Lashgari and embassy officials visited Mangyongdae Wednesday. The guests, being briefed on the glorious and brilliant revolutionary history of the great leader President Kim Il-song and his revolutionary family, saw the historic relics preserved with much care at the old home in Mangyongdae and went round the Mangyongdae Revolution Museum. After the visit, Saeed Lashgari said that his excellency the great leader President Kim Il-song, born into a poor family, achieved the liberation of the country with his energetic activities and has glorified Korea.
