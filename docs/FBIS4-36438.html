BFN [Excerpts] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat arrived in our country this afternoon on a visit to Morocco. [passage omitted] 'Arafat is accompanied by a delegation made up essentially of Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, the culture minister, and Marwan Kanafani and Khalid Salam, advisers to the Palestinian president. At the airport lounge 'Arafat made the following statement: [Begin 'Arafat recording] First, you all know the deep-rooted, strong relationship which exists between us and his majesty the king, his rightly guided government, and his fraternal people. As is also known, we have for his majesty the king all the affection, all the fraternity, and all the strong brotherly feelings of which we are always proud. We are about to leave for Palestine, God willing. We have to come and see the king in order to listen to his valuable advice and to his great wisdom which we, our people, and our nationals are in need of at this historic, critical, and important moment. God willing, we will pray together in the first of the two Qiblahs [Jerusalem], the third holy place, the place from where Prophet Muhammad, God's prayers and peace upon him, made his midnight journey to the seven heavens, and the birthplace of Jesus, God's peace be upon him. [end recording]
