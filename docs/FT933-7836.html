930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / People: Gresham Telecomputing Hamish Donaldson, the former Hill Samuel chief executive who quit two years ago after the bank made heavy losses, has been appointed non-executive chairman of GRESHAM TELECOMPUTING, the USM-quoted software and computer broking group, having joined the board only last month. He takes over from Sid Green who is splitting his role and remains chief executive.
