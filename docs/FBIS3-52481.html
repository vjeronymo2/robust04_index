Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] State Prosecutor General Musavi-Tabrizi took part in a news conference today. [passage omitted] Making a positive evaluation of the situation of prisoners in the country's jails, the prosecutor-general said that at present, 70 percent of prisoners are narcotics' smugglers and addicts. The remaining 30 percent are convicted of general offenses such as murder, robbery, and social corruption, and a few are minigroup members. Considering the population in Iran, the number of prisoners are less than in other countries. [passage omitted] The prosecutor general added: The trial of a foreign spy has ended and he has been sentenced to death.
