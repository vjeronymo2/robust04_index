BFN [Text] According to statistics released recently by the provincial statistics bureau, the living expenditure income of the province's urban population ranked first among the nine coastal provinces and cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, and Tianjin. In terms of [words indistinct], although city dwellers in the province had an average per capita net income of 4,240 yuan or so this year, ranking first among the nine provinces and cities, they spent a very large portion of their income, with average per capita spending 71 percent higher than the average level among the nine provinces and cities. Overall, the living expenditure level of the province's urban population belongs to an intermediate one in the nine provinces and cities. The rumor that city dwellers in Guangdong have the highest consumption level in the country is not a fact. It is said that Guangdong's relatively high price levels have to do with this phenomenon.
