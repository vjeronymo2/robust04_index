Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hamburg (DPA)--German Defense Minister Volker Ruehe has defended the Bundeswehr soldiers involved in the fatal shooting in Somalia. Ruehe told BILD newspaper (Saturday [22 January] edition): "This is a tragic incident, but I cannot see that the German soldiers in Somalia acted incorrectly." With regard to the forthcoming German withdrawal from Somalia, Ruehe said: "I hope and wish that the good relations between our soldiers and the Somali population will continue." [passage omitted]
