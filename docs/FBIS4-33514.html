BFN [Report by Luiz Augusto Mendonca from Belem -- live] [Text] President Itamar Franco arrived in Belem [Para State] on the night of 5 June. This morning at 1000 [1400 GMT], he will officially open the 24th OAS General Assembly. The president of the Republic was welcomed at the Belem Air Base by Para Governor Carlos Santos on the night of 5 June and immediately left for the Hilton Hotel, where he is staying. After opening the OAS meeting, President Franco will visit the (Emilio Goeldi) Museum in Belem where he will plant a tree to celebrate the International Environment Day. Franco will then hear speeches by the ministers of the environment and Legal Amazon Region, and science and technology. Franco will issue a stamp celebrating International Environment Day. In the afternoon, President Itamar Franco will have a private meeting with ousted Haitian President Jean-Bertrand Aristide. At night, the president of the Republic will be decorated with the Grao Para medal, the most important Para State decoration. He will later host a dinner for the foreign delegations.
