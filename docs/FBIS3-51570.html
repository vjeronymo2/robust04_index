Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Caracas, 19 Jan (AFP) -- Reliable sources report that violent riots and looting continued to shake Venezuela today, leaving at least five people injured. The eastern tourist resort of Puerto La Cruz has been enduring riots since last week. Today rioting spread into Caracas. There also were violent demonstrations near the Central University of Venezuela and two secondary schools. The riots in Puerto La Cruz, where for the second day in a row businesses were looted, began because of a fare increase in public transportation. Yesterday, bus drivers refused to drive their routes because of the increase. [passage omitted]
