March 6, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

'BAD SCENE IN SOUTH TEXAS'

"Bad scene in South Texas" has the INS and the refugees from Nicaragua between a "rock and a hard place." Members of Congress, above all, must be held responsible for their procrastination and ineptitude in developing a logical immigration policy. Their lack of prescience in the "on again, off again" penurious funding of the Contras would have made a difference in the lives of their citizens had they funded the Contras properly.

Not unlike a plague, the Nicaraguans, now considered refugees, are joined by the people of El Salvador and Guatemala inundating our ports of entry on our southern border.

Until the political, socioeconomic conditions improve they shall continue to deluge our borders.

"We inherit the wind!"

SAMUEL S. SHERWIN

Los Angeles
