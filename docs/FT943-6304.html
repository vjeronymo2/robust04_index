940830 FT 30 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Claverhouse net assets edge ahead Fleming Claverhouse Investment Trust had a net asset value of 195.9p per share at June 30, a modest improvement on 193p a year earlier. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-June amounted to Pounds 1.5m, up from Pounds 1.12m, for earnings of 2.71p (2.55p). A second interim dividend of 1.25p maintains the total to date at 2.5p.
