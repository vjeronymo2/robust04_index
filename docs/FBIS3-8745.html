Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Summary] Rudolf Krc, chairman of the Statistical Office of the Slovak Republic, briefed the media today about the development of the Slovak economy in January 1994. For example, the consumption of the population, assessed on the basis of retail trade takings, was just below 16 billion korunas. Compared to December 1993, this is a drop of 21 percent. Krc said that, compared to December 1993, industrial production dropped by 1.2 percent in January.
