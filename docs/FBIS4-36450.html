BFN [Text] New York, June 4 (INA)--Iraq has protested to the United Nations over continued breachings of its airspace by the American warplanes with purpose of provocation and surveillance and also dropping the flares. In a letter to U.N. Secretary General, Dr. Butrus Ghali, Iraq's U.N. ambassador said that these warplanes had carried out a total of 681 sorties on May 20-31 over vast areas in the north and the south of the country and also violated the sound barrier and dropped flares that damaged the crops.
