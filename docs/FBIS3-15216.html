Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with a presidential decree signed by Igor Smirnov, the state of emergency has been prolonged on the territory of the self-proclaimed Transdniestrian republic till March 31. As it was reported earlier, the state of emergency was introduced in Transdniester for one month late January. This was officially explained by the need to stabilize the internal political situation, exchange bank notes, and to improve the economic situation. Independent political observers assess this move as an attempt to prevent the Transdniestrian population from participation in the elections to Moldova's new parliament scheduled for March 27. The decree on prolonging the state of emergency points out that this measure is aimed at further stabilization of the situation in this region.
