Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Chief of Staff Ehud Baraq said that once the agreements with the PLO are concluded, the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] will be capable of completing a speedy withdrawal from the Gaza Strip and Jericho without holding up the implementation of the agreements. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that Lieutenant General Baraq told the Knesset Foreign Affairs and Defense Committee that if the wave of terrorist attacks continued, the defense establishment would be forced to consider closing the territories. Our correspondent notes that several Knesset members expressed concern over possible future developments in the Gaza Strip after the IDF's withdrawal.
