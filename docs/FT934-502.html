931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Ramus rights to raise Pounds 3m Ramus Holdings, the USM-quoted building materials supplier, is calling for Pounds 3m net via a rights issue of 12.7m new ordinary shares on 9-for-8 basis at 25p per share. Depending on the take-up, the holdings of HLI-Hume and Postel Investment Management could rise to 73.5 per cent and 7.5 per cent respectively of the group's enlarged share capital. Ramus has also sold its freehold property at Bounds Green, north London, for Pounds 3.1m, first outlined in September.
