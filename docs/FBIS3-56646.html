Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow January 15 TASS -- Chairmen of six committees of the Federation Council, the upper chamber of the Russian Parliament, were elected here today. Isak Kostoyev was elected chairman of the Committee for Constitutional Legislation and Legal Issues, Vitaliy Zelenkin was elected chairman of the Committee for Economic Reform, Vadim Gustov was elected chairman of the Committee for International Affairs, Yevgeniy Stroyev was elected chairman of the Committee for Science and Culture and Galina Karelova was elected chairman of the Committee for Social Policy. Heads of the rest four committees will be elected at the next Federation Council meeting. The Federation Council session goes on. It is likely to end today.
