930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Courtaulds sells Wrightcel stake Courtaulds has sold its 40 per cent interest in Wrightcel to Gadsden Rheem, the Melbourne-based packaging subsidiary of SA Brewing Holdings. Wrightcel was set up in December 1990 as a result of a management buy-out of Courtaulds Packaging Australia. Former CPA management holds 20 per cent and the remaining 40 per cent is held by AIDC. Wrightcel's turnover in the year ended March 31 1992 was ADollars 55.4m (Pounds 27.1m).
