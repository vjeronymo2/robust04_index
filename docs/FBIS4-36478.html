BFN [Text] Philippine Foreign Minister Roberto Romulo says he will positively recommend to his president Israel Aircraft Industries products, particularly Kfir planes. At the end of his visit he called on Israeli high-tech firms to establish production and marketing systems in the Philippines that could serve as a basis for all Southeast Asia. Romulo also said his country would be glad to help Israel in promoting relations with Muslim states in the area, whom he believed would establish full ties with Israel as the peace process advanced.
