931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / Report on taxman sent to CPS FRAUD squad detectives investigating the affairs of a tax official linked to Mr Asil Nadir, the fugitive tycoon, have sent a preliminary report to the Crown Prosecution Service, it emerged last night. Lawyers acting for the official, Mr Michael Allcock, said yesterday he had been rebailed until January 21 pending a decision by the CPS on whether to proceed with the case against him. Mr Allcock investigated the tax affairs of Mr Nadir while serving as head of the Inland Revenue's London-based investigative unit.
