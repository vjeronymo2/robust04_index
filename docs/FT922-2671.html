920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / UK Company News: British Land pays Pounds 22m for retail park British Land, the property group, has bought the Teesside Retail Park for Pounds 22m from the Teesside Development Corporation. The park comprises 330,000 sq ft with 22 retail units, including Do It All, Texas and Comet. Three units are unlet. British Land also announced yesterday that it is entering into negotiations with SmithKline Beecham about a joint venture to develop its office site alongside the M4 at Brentford, in west London. SB owns 14 acres of land on two connected sites either side of the M4. It anticipates that it will occupy the first phase of the redevelopment. The Richard Rogers Partnership, architects, is advising on the scheme.
