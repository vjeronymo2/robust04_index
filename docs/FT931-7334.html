930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Ladbroke plans 2,000 new jobs LADBROKE'S, the UK's largest betting shop chain, hopes to create up to 2,000 part-time jobs across the country as a result of the Home Office ruling allowing evening opening of betting offices from April 1. Ladbroke's has nearly 2,000 betting offices which will be allowed to extend their opening from 6.30pm to 10pm to take advantage of evening racing fixtures.
