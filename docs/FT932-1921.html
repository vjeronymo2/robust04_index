930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Car components plant planned JOHNSON Controls, the US car components supplier, is setting up its second factory in the UK with a Pounds 10m investment in a new plant to make seat foam pads at Wednesbury, west Midlands. The new plant, opening in July next year, will employ 130 initially and 160 after a year. Johnson will be the first company at a car components park planned for the site of the old Patent Shaft steelworks by Black Country Development Corporation.
