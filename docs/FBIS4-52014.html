BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- The statement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MOFA] of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea dated April 28 proposing to the United States to hold negotiation for the establishment of a new peace arrangement that could guarantee peace and security on the Korean peninsula on a practical basis was distributed as official document S/1994/513 of the United Nations Security Council on April 28.
