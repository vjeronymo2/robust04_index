Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Presidential Council Chairman Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih returned to the homeland at 0130 today following a short working visit to fraternal Egypt. During the visit, he met with his brother Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. The two presidents held talks on ways to further the close relations between the two fraternal countries, on the regional, Arab, and international developments, and on issues of interest to the Arab nation, particularly the two countries' efforts to restore Arab solidarity. The Presidential Council chairman also briefed President Mubarak on developments in our country, the Pledge and Accord Document which was signed in Amman, and the efforts being exerted to implement it on the ground in a way to safeguard unity and democracy in our country.
