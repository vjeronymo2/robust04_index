920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / International Company News: Aegon buys into Hungarian group AEGON, the second largest Dutch insurance group, is to take a 75 per cent stake in Allami Biztosito (AB), the state-owned Hungarian insurer, for a reported Dollars 50m, writes Nicholas Denton in Budapest. Aegon said the Hungarian acquisition was a logical step in its strategy of expanding from a north-west European and US base into southern and eastern Europe. Aegon has said that it is in talks with Tirrena, the Italian insurer.
