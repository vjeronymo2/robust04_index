BFN [Excerpts] Six were killed and about 25 wounded, mostly lightly, in an explosion inside a bus during a boarding interval in the Hadera central bus station. The No. 820 bus was on its way from 'Afula to Tel Aviv. It is believed that Arabs, who boarded the bus at one of the stops, left the bomb on the bus. Another theory says the perpetrator was a suicide terrorist. [passage omitted] Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv adds that there has been a second explosion on the bus, this time apparently a planned blast activated by bomb disposal experts, who found another bomb. The Hadera central bus station has been closed off, and local inhabitants are staging a demonstration nearby, calling out anti-Arab slogans. [passage omitted]
