February 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GARDEN GROVE WOMAN DIES AFTER HITTING TREE

A 32-year-old Garden Grove woman died after she lost control of her car in Westminster and crashed into a tree, police said.

The victim was identified as Evalan M. Latour. Sgt. Larry Russell said Latour was driving north on Edwards Street at 11:12 p.m. Friday when her 1982 Datsun 300-ZX crossed over into the southbound lanes, hit the curb and crashed into a tree.

Russell said it appeared Latour was speeding when she crashed. He said she died shortly before arriving at Fountain Valley Regional Hospital and Medical Center.
