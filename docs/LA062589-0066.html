June 25, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

IN A MUSICAL FRENZY OVER ART

Suzanne Muchnic's June 11 article on the art-buying frenzy prompted me to write the following -- to be sung to the tune of "Love and Marriage":

Art and money / Art and money / Go together like the bees and honey / Like the Dow-Jones brothers / You can't have one / You can't have one / You can't have one / Without the othhhhhhher.

ED KYSAR

Reseda
