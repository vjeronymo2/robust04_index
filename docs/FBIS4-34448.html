BFN [Text] On 16 June, employees of the U.S. Embassy in Port-au-Prince began leaving Haiti for the United States. A preliminary group of seven people departed on a commercial American Airlines flight. Ambassador Swing took leave of this group at the airport in the presence of American journalists currently in Port-au-Prince. It should be noted that about 70 members of the families of the U.S. diplomatic staff accredited in Haiti, including the spouse of Ambassador Swing, are due to leave Haiti before the suspension of commercial flights between Haiti and the United States on 25 June, and according to information provided by AFP, 75 diplomats, including Ambassador Swing, will remain in Port-au- Prince. It should also be pointed out that U.S. citizens residing in Haiti have begun to leave the country under their government's recommendation.
