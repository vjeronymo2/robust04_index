941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Pearson disposal Pearson, the media and entertainment group which owns the Financial Times, is to receive at least Pounds 106m net from the sale of shares in Camco International, the Houston-based oil services company. Pearson has sold 8.15m shares through an underwritten public offer at Dollars 19 per share. In addition, 1m shares are being bought by Camco when the offer closes, at the same net price, leaving Pearson with an estimated Pounds 106m.
