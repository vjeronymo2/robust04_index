BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Demidenko and ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vitaliy Makarchev] [Text] Kiev, 24 May -- Douglas Hurd, Britain's secretary of state for foreign and commonwealth affairs, arrived in the Ukrainian capital today for a one-day official visit. He is expected to be received by Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and to have meetings with Supreme Council Chairman Aleksandr Moroz, Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy, and with other leaders of the Republic.
