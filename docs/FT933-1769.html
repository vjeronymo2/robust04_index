930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Mayborn rises 14% MAYBORN GROUP, the fabric dyes, baby products and florists' sundries concern, made further steady progress in the first half of 1993 and helped by a sharp fall in interest charges, pre-tax profits rose by 14 per cent to Pounds 1.9m, against Pounds 1.66m. Turnover of the USM-quoted group edged up 3 per cent to Pounds 17.5m. At the operating level, profits grew 4 per cent to Pounds 1.93m. before interest charges of Pounds 34,000 (Pounds 195,000). Earnings per share rose to 6.4p (5.8p) and an increased interim dividend of 1.8p (1.6p) has been declared. Mr Ian Peacock, chairman, said trading in the second half was expected to show more satisfactory growth. Net borrowings at the half-year were Pounds 841,000 (Pounds 2.84m).
