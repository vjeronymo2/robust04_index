Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 22 Mar (NA) -- Domingo Liotta, an outstanding heart surgeon and General Juan Domingo Peron's personal doctor, will replace the late Raul Matera as secretary for technical and scientific affairs. In talks with journalists accredited to Government House, Liotta announced that this afternoon he accepted President Carlos Menem's proposal to become secretary for technical and scientific affairs. According to government sources, Liotta will meet Menem at Government House at 0800 on 23 March. Liotta reportedly will be sworn in at 1100 on 24 March in the White Hall.
