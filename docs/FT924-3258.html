921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Malaysian power Tenaga Nasional, the partially privatised Malaysian electricity utility, has signed a MDollars 272m (Dollars 109m) contract with Marubeni of Japan to build a gas turbine power plant, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. Construction will start immediately at Malacca on peninsula Malaysia's west coast, and is due to be completed by the beginning of 1994.
