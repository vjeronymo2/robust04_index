BFN [Text] On 11 June the people and the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK] swept the invading forces of the two-headed government from Toek Chum commune to Spean Treng and from Prasat commune to Phnum Liep commune in Preah Net Preah District, Battambang Province. We completely liberated and and now control this area including the following nine villages: Rouch, Spean, Toek Chum, Cha, Kouk Siem, Toek Haoch, Aumal, Anlung Cha, and Bat Trang. We killed or wounded a number of enemy soldiers and seized 15 assorted firearms and a quantity of ammunition and war materiel. The people also joined hands in destroying the Phnum Liep bridge to deprive the communist Vietnamese puppets and two-headed government of its use for transporting troops and ammunition in their operations to persecute, kill, and rob the local people.
