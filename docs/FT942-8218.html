940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Cleveland pays more than forecast In its first results since obtaining a listing in November last year, Cleveland Trust, the London-based property company, reported a rise in profits from Pounds 41,000 to Pounds 687,000 pre-tax for the year to end-March. Turnover totalled Pounds 2.06m (Pounds 896,000). Earnings per share emerged at 8.9p (5.2p). The dividend for the period of 2.3p compares with a promised 2.2p.
