BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Staff Lieutenant General 'Abd-al-Hafiz al-Ka'abinah, Jordanian chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, who is currently visiting Turkey as the guest of Gen. Dogan Gures, chief of the General Staff, is meeting with officials in Ankara. Al-Ka'abinah first called on Gures today. He was received with a military ceremony upon his arrival at the Chief of the General Staff's Office. The two chiefs of staff conferred for some time. The Jordanian chief of staff later visited National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan. During the meeting, Golhan recalled that Jordan and Turkey are situated in the vicinity of war, adding that the two countries can contribute to world peace through joint efforts. Al-Ka'abinah, in turn, noted that the existing process of friendship between the two countries will be further strengthened in the future. The Jordanian chief of staff was then presented with an order of merit for his contributions to Jordanian-Turkish military cooperation. Addressing the ceremony on the occasion, Gures remarked that the Jordanian chief of staff's efforts for the development of bilateral relations will never be forgotten. Golhan also attended the ceremony.
