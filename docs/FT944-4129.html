941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Titon at Pounds 2m Titon Holdings, the USM-quoted building products company, reported annual pre-tax profits slightly ahead at Pounds 2.02m, against Pounds 2.01m. Turnover rose 7 per cent from Pounds 11.3m to Pounds 12.1m. Mr John Anderson, chairman, said it had been a difficult trading year. The company had continued to invest, with capital expenditure up 67 per cent to Pounds 973,000 and research and development spending up from Pounds 426,000 to Pounds 517,000. Earnings per share for the 12 months to September 30 were 12.41p (12.07p) and the proposed final dividend is 3.1p (2.9p) for a total of 4.5p (4.2p).
