940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons Hiram Walker, the spirits and wine division of Allied-Lyons, has acquired a significant interest in the Swiss distribution activity of Bols-Cynar, a subsidiary of Bols Wessanen, the Dutch food and beverages group. The joint venture will be operative from July 1. Bols-Cynar has been distributing Ballantine's, the leading whisky in Switzerland for over 60 years, and its name will be changed to Bols-Cynar Ballantine's in recognition of the new venture.
