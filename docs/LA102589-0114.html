October 25, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

RAMS, BILLS TOP TV SPORTS RATINGS

The game between the Rams and Buffalo Bills Oct. 16 on ABC's "Monday Night Football" was last week's highest rated sports program, according to Arbitron ratings of 14 markets. It received a 17.9 rating and a 31 share. Saturday's USC-Notre Dame game on CBS received a 10.6 rating and a 25 share.
