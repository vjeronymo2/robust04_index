941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: HK Bank Canada Hongkong Bank of Canada, the HSBC Holdings subsidiary, reported net income for the year to October 31 of CDollars 85.9m (Pounds 38.5m), against CDollars 63.2m, despite a slowing in the fourth quarter. Total assets rose 18.5 per cent over the year to CDollars 16bn. Risk asset ratio was 8.71 per cent with a tier 1 capital ratio of 5.38 per cent.
