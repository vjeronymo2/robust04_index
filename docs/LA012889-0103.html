January 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

COMMUNITY COLLEGE WOMEN'S BASKETBALL;

JENKINS AND JACKSON LEAD GOLDEN WEST

Allison Jenkins and Claudette Jackson scored 17 points each to lead Golden West College to a 97-72 victory over Riverside in an Orange Empire Conference women's basketball game Friday at Riverside.

DeeDee Stigar scored 13, Kim Wilson 12 and Stacy Johnson 10 for Golden West (10-5, 4-1).

Fullerton 82, Rancho Santiago 55 -- Debbie Wagner scored 19 points to lead visiting Fullerton (21-5, 5-0). Vanessa Early scored 26 points and grabbed 12 rebounds for Rancho Santiago (7-16, 1-4).
