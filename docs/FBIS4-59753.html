BFN [Communique "No. 21" issued by the Ministry of Defense on 17 May; place not given] [Text] 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih yesterday sent large numbers of barbaric forces with their vehicles into a deadly battle in a desperate attempt to break through the fortified defense of the Armed Forces. These forces, together with the masses of our people, are defending justice, freedom, and honor. This was preceded by false statements, such as those of al-Ansi and al-Iryani, that the barbaric forces advanced behind a human shield [words indistinct] into a deadly battle. Our armed forces repulsed these forces by striking heavy blows on them on the fronts of al-Karish, Abyan, Qa'tabah, and Bayhan during which many of them surrendered, while the remnants (?fled) toward Qa'tabah, al-Rahidah, and al-Bayda with our forces still behind them. Our forces captured many arms and vehicles abandoned by the oppressors.
