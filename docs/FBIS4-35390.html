BFN [Text] Manama, 31 May (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, received here today Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, who conveyed a message to his highness from al-Bid dealing with latest developments in Yemen. Al-'Attas briefed his highness the amir of Bahrain on the latest developments in Yemen and the efforts aimed to stop fighting between brothers in Yemen. Shaykh 'Isa regretted [words indistinct] fighting between brothers in Yemen and said he hoped this bloody fighting would stop and that differences would be resolved through negotiations. His highness said the regrettable and bloody events in Yemen will have repercussions on the Arab and Islamic [words indistinct]. The amir of Bahrain called on both sides in Yemen to resort to reason and logic in order to preserve Yemen and its brotherly people, and expressed hope that the Arab [word indistinct] efforts would succeed in halting fighting in Yemen and restoring peace, security, and stability to this sisterly country. The meeting was attended by Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Khalifah Al Khalifah, interior minister; Shaykh Khalid Bin-'Abdallah Al Khalifah, housing minister; Yusuf Irhamah al-Dawsari, head of the Amiri Court; and Nabil Ibrahim Qambar, director general of the Foreign Ministry's Protocol Department.
