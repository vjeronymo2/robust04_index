BFN [Excerpts] Army Commander General Augusto Pinochet yesterday completed the Army high command with the appointment of General Jaime Concha, current Advisory Committee chief, as Army inspector general. Gen. Concha replaces General Jorge Ballerino, who resigned on 31 January. [passage omitted] Thus, the Army command reads: Army Commander in Chief: General Augusto Pinochet. Deputy Commander in Chief: Lieutenant Colonel Jorge Lucar. Major General Guillermo Garin remains in his position as commander of the 1st Army Corps; Major General Richard Quass is the Army chief of staff; Major General Ramon Castro Ivanovic is the personnel director; Major General Eterio Pavez is the commander of the Southern Military Region; Major General Jaime Concha is the Army inspector general; Major General Guido Riquelme is the commander general of the Metropolitan Region Army Garrison; Major General Ricardo Gaete Villasenor is the deputy Army chief of staff; Major General Hernan Abad Cid is the commander of the Engineers Command; Brigadier General Hernan Ramirez Rurange is the commander of the 2d Army Division; Brigadier General Javier Salazar is the director of the Strategic and Political Studies Academy (Anepe); and Brigadier General Carlos Krumm is the director of the Army Logistic Division. [passage omitted]
