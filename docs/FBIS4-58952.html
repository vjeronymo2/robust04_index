BFN [Message from commanders of the 14th Infantry Brigade to President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih; place and date not given -- read by announcer] [Text] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Esteemed brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman and supreme commander of the Armed Forces; esteemed brother Staff Brigadier General 'Abd-al-Malik al-Sayyani, chief of staff of the Yemeni Armed Forces. On behalf of the commanders, officers, noncommissioned officers, and soldiers of the 14th Infantry Brigade, we send to you our warmest greetings, wishing you success in extricating the homeland from the ordeal imposed on it. We stress to you our adherence to Yemeni unity as an absolutely irrevocable decision and our adherence to our legitimate leadership. We consider the 14th Brigade a part of the Armed Forces defending unity, legitimacy, and democracy. May you be preserved as an asset for the revolution and the homeland. [Signed] Your brothers: Staff Colonel 'Abdallah al-Khadr Sa'id; commander of the brigade; Colonel 'Abd-al-Hakim 'Abdallah Ahmad, deputy commander for supply affairs; Lieutenant 'Abdal-al-Karim Miqbil, political staff officer
