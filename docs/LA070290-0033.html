July 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Art Conference: For the first time in its 25-year history, the International Committee for Modern Art Museums will hold its annual meeting in Los Angeles. The conference, Sept. 8-15, is expected to draw a distinguished group of directors and chief curators of modern and contemporary art museums throughout the world. Members represent 33 countries including the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Spain, Japan, Israel and Australia. GREG BRAXTON
