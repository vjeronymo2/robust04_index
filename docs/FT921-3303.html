920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / Scottish union official demoted MR DAVID SHOAT, Scottish regional secretary of the TGWU general union, has been removed from his post by the union's general executive committee. Mr Shoat was among several of the union's 11 regional secretaries who was appointed during the brief period in the mid 1980s when the general executive committee was dominated by the union's rightwing grouping. The committee is now dominated by the left.
