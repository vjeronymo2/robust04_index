BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow June 11 TASS -- The speakers of the Russian Parliament upper chamber and the Armenian Parliament specified some items for their cooperation at their meeting at the upper chamber on Saturday. Telling this to journalists, Russian Speaker Vladimir Shumeyko noted that a cooperation agreement between the two chambers of the Russian Parliament and the Armenian Parliament was signed on Friday. The two speakers agreed that it is necessary to establish inter- committee parliamentary relations and develop their contacts while drafting legislative acts in order to boost ties. Armenian Speaker Babken Ararktsyan stressed: "We highly assess relations in the framework of the inter-parliamentary assembly and claim at the same time that bilateral parliamentary contacts should be also developed". The sides discussed problems connected with Nagorno-Karabakh at the meeting. Noting that the Bishkek protocol is still in force, Shumeyko stressed that the meetings of parliament speakers in St. Petersburg and Moscow "plotted a good road for the negotiating process to settle the Armenian-Azeri conflict with the participation of the third party -- the Nagorno-Karabakh parliament". The Armenian speaker invited Shumeyko to visit his country.
