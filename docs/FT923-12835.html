920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Smiths Inds buys HG Wallace Smiths Industries is buying the medical devices manufacturer HG Wallace from the present family owners. Wallace makes disposable plastic products for infusion, dialysis, incontinence and wound drainage applications. In its last financial year it made pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.3m from sales of Pounds 6.3m. Net assets, including cash, were Pounds 3.5m.
