920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / Mexico retires over Dollars 7bn debt THE Mexican government has cancelled Dollars 7.17bn (Pounds 4bn) of its foreign debt thanks to successful operations in the secondary debt market in the 2 1/2 years since it reached a debt agreement with its creditors in 1990, said Mr Pedro Aspe, finance minister, writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. Debt has fallen from Dollars 80bn on March 31 this year to about Dollars 73bn, saving some Dollars 400m a year in interest payments.
