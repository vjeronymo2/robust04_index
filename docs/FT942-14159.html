940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: TR Property pays Pounds 3m for portfolio TR Property Investment Trust, through its Trust Union Properties subsidiary, has acquired four industrial properties in the north of England for Pounds 3m from Liverpool Victoria Friendly Society. The price reflected a capital value of Pounds 20 per sq ft and showed an initial yield of 12.8 per cent. The sites, developed during the 1970s in Bolton, Osset, York and Manchester, currently produce a rental income of Pounds 396,000 a year.
