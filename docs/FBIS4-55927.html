BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 20 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Friday accepted the resignation of Government Commissioner for Public Administration Reform Michal Kulesza, the commissioner's bureau reported. Kulesza was appointed for the post in autumn 1992 by Hanna Suchocka who was Poland's prime minister at the time. He submitted his resignation at the beginning of this week, blaming the government with stopping systemic reforms in Poland .
