940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / International Company News: US custodian in Czech move Morgan Stanley Trust, the US-based global custodian, has launched custody service for Czech securities transactions through the Prague branch of Dutch-based ING Bank, a Morgan Stanley official said, Reuter reports. Morgan Stanley, which started similar services in Poland and Hungary last year, said it decided to launch its Czech operation because of increased client interest and confidence in the Prague Stock Exchange trading system.
