921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / International Company News: Prague airline closes debt offer CZECHOSLOVAKIAN Airlines has completed the final stage in a Dollars 141m debt offering to pay for five Boeing 737 aircraft, writes Daniel Green. The Dollars 113m, 12-year issue was set at a fixed rate of 7.02 per cent and is backed by the Export-Import Bank of the US. It is the first time aircraft acquisitions have been financed through a fixed rate Exim-backed public offering.
