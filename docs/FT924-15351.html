921005 FT 05 OCT 92 / Mr Chris Watney: Correction A REPORT concerning West Midlands Regional Health Authority (Financial Times, September 11) wrongly stated that the consultancy which carried out a cost-cutting study was run by Mr Chris Watney. It should have said that the contract for the study was awarded to United Research Group by West Midlands Regional Health Authority when Mr Watney was a director of the authority's supplies division.
