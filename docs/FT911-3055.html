910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: Steady growth at Sara Lee SARA LEE, the Illinois-based food group, saw an 11 per cent increase in net profits during its third quarter, to end-March. It made Dollars 120.8m after tax, compared with Dollars 108.9m in the same period a year ago, leaving it with profits of Dollars 393.9m after the first nine months, against Dollars 353m last time. Sales during the latest three months rose by a more modest 4 per cent, to Dollars 2.91bn, with the company reporting an 8.2 per cent improvement in operating income, at Dollars 114m. Interest charges in the quarter fell from Dollars 36.9m to Dollars 24m.
