October 4, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Kevin Mack, the Cleveland Browns running back, was sentenced to six months in prison after pleading guilty to using cocaine.

Judge Richard McMonagle of Cuyahoga County Common Pleas Court in Ohio sentenced Mack to a reformatory for first-time offenders younger than 30 years old.

Mack, 26, must spend a minimum of 30 days in jail before he can apply for probation, said Frank Gasper, assistant prosecutor.
