BFN [Text] Khartoum, 5 Apr (KNA/PANA) -- The Sudanese Government controls 90 percent of the territory in the south where a civil war has raged for some years, Mahdi Ibrahim, the director of political affairs in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, has said. Ibrahim disclosed this when he addressed a political forum in Omdurman Province. He accused a rebel leader, John Garang, of fearing peace in the territory because he did not have any plans for its development.
