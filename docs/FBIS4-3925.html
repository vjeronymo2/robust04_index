BFN ["Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress on the Work Report of the Anhui Provincial Higher People's Court (Adopted on 12 April 1994 at the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress)"] [Text] The Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress heard, examined, and deliberated over the work report delivered by President Han Yunping [7281 0061 5493] on behalf of the provincial higher people's court. The session decided to approve the report. The session urged courts at all levels throughout the province to follow the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics as well as the guidelines of the 14th national party congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee; to uphold the guiding thought of subordinating judicial work to reform, development, and stability; to further strengthen judicial work; to continue to take stern measures against serious criminal offenses and economic crimes in accordance with the law; to actively participate in improving all facets of social order; to wage an in-depth anticorruption drive; to maintain social stability; and to protect the lawful rights and interests of citizens and legal persons. We should earnestly consolidate the ranks of people's courts, strictly enforce laws, and improve judicial standards so as to provide effective legal protection for the socialist market economic system and for economic construction.
