Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Rauf Denktas has announced that the Turkish Cypriot side has agreed to discuss the confidence-building measures without making any concessions on the principles it has defended so far. The Council of Ministers met under President Denktas at 0900 today. Upon emerging from the meeting at 1130, the president said the Council of Ministers discussed the confidence-building measures and reached a decision which will be conveyed to the UN secretary general. Denktas pointed out that after the decision is conveyed to Secretary General Butrus-Ghali, the necessary announcement will be made to the public. In turn, Prime Minister Hakki Atun said the experts will continue to study the confidence-building measures. He added that work on these measures will be conducted in cooperation with President Denktas and the Turkish Republic. The Republican Assembly and the other parties will be briefed on the issue, he said, adding: The issue will be conducted in unity, taking into consideration the interests of the community and the state. The Council of Ministers meeting continued under Prime Minister Atun after Denktas left it. The meeting is still in session.
