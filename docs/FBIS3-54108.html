Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Jan (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Dr. Usamah al-Baz, Foreign Ministry first under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs, met today with a Palestinian delegation including Dr. Nabil Sha'th, chairman of the Palestinian delegation to the Gaza-Jericho talks; Ahmad Quray', alias Abu-'Ala', head of the PLO's Economic Department; Palestinian Ambassador in Cairo Sa'id Kamal; and Zuhdi al-Qidrah, Fatah representative in Cairo. Ambassador Kamal stated that 'Arafat has left Cairo for Damascus to offer his condolences to Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad on the death of his son. 'Arafat will return to Cairo this evening to leave later for Jeddah.
