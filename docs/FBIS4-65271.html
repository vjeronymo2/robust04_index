BFN [Text] Feliks Bogdanov and Pyotr Dobroserdov, Russian Foreign Ministry officials, left Cyprus this evening. In a statement made at Larnaca Airport, Pyotr Dobroserdov said that their contacts had given the two officials a better understanding of the situation in Cyprus. Dobroserdov added that the meetings they held were very constructive, because they discussed the entire spectrum of Cypriot- Russian relations and their further development. Mr. Dobroserdov noted that both sides desire not merely to develop their relations but to implement a plan for their consolidation.
