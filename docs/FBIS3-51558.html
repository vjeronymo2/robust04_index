Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Statement issued by the Secretariat of the Sandinist National Liberation Front National Directorate in Managua on 21 January 1994] [Text] During its regular meeting on 21 January, the National Directorate of the Sandinist National Liberation Front [FSLN] analyzed the letter on reforms to the Constitution signed by 13 National Assembly deputies. The National Directorate hereby ratifies its total backing for the Document on Agreements, a document that was signed on 29 November by the Constitutional Commission of the National Opposition Union [UNO] and by the FSLN commission. The National Directorate also states its disposition to meet with the 13-deputy commission integrated by Dr. Miriam Arguello, who heads it, and Adolfo Jarquin, Dr. Edmundo Castillo, and Zacarias Hernandez, to discuss the reforms to the Constitution issue. During the session, the FSLN National Directorate condemned the assassination of Nicaraguan citizen Juan Pablo Cruz Laguna at the hands of members of the Honduran Armed Forces. The National Directorate demands of the Honduran Government that it clear the crime and punish the guilty persons; and of the Nicaraguan Government, the National Directorate demands that it carry out the necessary work so that the guilty parties are punished and Nicaraguan citizens in transit through Honduras are offered security. The National Directorate expresses its condolences and solidarity to the relatives of Juan Pablo Cruz Laguna. [Issued] Managua, 21 January 1994 Free Fatherland or death! [Signed] Secretariat of the National Directorate, Sandinist National Liberation Front
