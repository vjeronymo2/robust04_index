August 19, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

STILL THINKING OF R.M. NIXON

Stanley Kutler's article (Op-Ed, Aug. 8) on Richard Nixon's resignation is the same old rehash on the subject that we are always subjected to at this time of year. One certainly gets the feeling that Mr. Kutler is in a vain campaign against Richard Nixon!

I really feel sorry for Mr. Kutler's students, as they will surely be required to read his negatively biased view of history. His students could use their time much more wisely by reading Mr. Nixon's book, "1999: Victory Without War." This is a book every college student should be required to read.

CHRIS CRAIN

Mission Viejo
