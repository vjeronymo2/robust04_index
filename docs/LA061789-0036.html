June 17, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: CHARGERS SIGN ONE ROOKIE, RELEASE ANOTHER

The Chargers signed Greg Lewis, a rookie free agent wide receiver, and released rookie free agent linebacker Mike Matthews on Friday.

Lewis is a 5-foot-9, 157-pounder who was a member of Texas A&M's 1987 All-American sprint relay team. He didn't play football his senior year and saw limited playing time on special teams and at receiver as a junior.

Matthews attended Oregon State.

COLLEGE BASKETBALL

U.S. International University has named Jack Murray an assistant basketball coach. He was a part-time assistant a year ago.

Murray will replace Steve Kinder, who left USIU two weeks ago for the head coaching position at Oceanside High School.
