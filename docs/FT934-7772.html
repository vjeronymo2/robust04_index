931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / International Company News: Holden marks resurgence HOLDEN, the Australian subsidiary of General Motors of the US, said its net profit jumped to ADollars 61.8m (Dollars 40.30m) in the year to June 30 from ADollars 36.9m in 1991-92, Reuter reports from Melbourne. Holden said the 67 per cent rise in net followed a 10 per cent rise in revenue to ADollars 1.95bn. It said a resurgence in the six-cylinder car market and a continuing focus on efficiency and productivity targets helped the company to boost profits.
