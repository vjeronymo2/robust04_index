BFN [Text] Paris, April 8 (AFP) -- French firm Dassault Automatismes et Telecommunications (of the Dassault Electronique Group) on Friday [8 April] announced signature of a contract for supplying a complete wireless digital telephony system for the city of Chongqing, in Sichuan Province, southwestern China. It did not indicate the value of the contract. The announcement indicated that the network for Chongqing will be the equivalent of the French "Bi-Bop" cordless telephone, used by some pedestrians in Paris and other areas to make calls from wherever they happen to be.
