930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Offshore fund launch - At a Glance Morgan Grenfell is launching a Dublin-based offshore fund investing in UK equities. The Selector UK fund is designed to complement Morgan Grenfell's existing tracker and income funds. The new fund will have an initial charge of 5.5 per cent and an annual charge of 1.5 per cent; the yield will be 2.5 per cent.
