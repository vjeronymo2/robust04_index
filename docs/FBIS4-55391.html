BFN [Text] Prishtina, May 12 (ATA) -- The republic of Kosova president, Dr. Ibrahim Rugova, received yesterday the chairman Mark Krasniqi, and Simon Agustinin, the secretary of Albanian Christian Democratic Party [CDP] of Kosova, informs from Kosova, Belul Jashari, journalist to ATA. They informed the president on the party's meeting a few days ago and on its results for the consolidation of the party. In the meeting was pointed out that the Albanian CDP of Kosova supports the peaceful policy for self-administration and the results of the referendum for an independent and neutral Kosova. President Rugova, hailed the holding of CDP meeting of Kosova and estimated its contribution to the political life of Kosova and its commitments for the solution of Kosova issue as well as the Albanian one.
