BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- Senior party and government officials, People's Army [KPA] servicemen and working people laid baskets of flowers at the monument to the victorious fatherland liberation war today on the 62d anniversary of the foundation of the heroic Korean People's Army. Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-nam were among those senior party and government officials. Laid there were floral baskets from the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central People's Committee and the Administration Council of the DPRK. Floral baskets were also laid in the name of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces, the Ministry of Public Security, ministries of the Administration Council, working people's organizations, KPA units, and Pyongyang municipal party and power organs and administrative and economic bodies. The military attaches corps in Pyongyang laid a floral basket at the monument today.
