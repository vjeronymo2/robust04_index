Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS freelance correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 5 Feb -- At 1146 Moscow time today, Russia's military space forces launched a Raduga-1 communications satellite from Baykonur cosmodrome, using a Proton heavy three-stage carrier rocket with a DM 2 [?dvigatel marshevyy, sustainer engine] unit. At the military space forces press center the ITAR-TASS correspondent learned that the launch was successful. The satellite is being guided by the main center for testing and guidance of spacecraft (Golitsino-2), which is a part of the military space force structure.
