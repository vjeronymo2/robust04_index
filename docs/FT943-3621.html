940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Sharpe & Fisher advance to Pounds 2.04m Recovery in the housing and home improvement markets helped Sharpe & Fisher, the building supplies company, to increase first half pre-tax profits from Pounds 658,000 to Pounds 2.04m, helped by a Pounds 559,000 contribution from the sale of investment property. Turnover for the six months to June 30 was 27 per cent ahead at Pounds 31.5m (Pounds 24.9m), with acquisitions contributing Pounds 32,000 (nil) and rental income Pounds 345,000 (Pounds 325,000). Earnings per share came out ahead at 6.6p (2.1p) and there is an increased interim dividend of 1.7p (1.5p).
