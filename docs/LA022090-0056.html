February 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Picked-On Men Pick Barr: The National Organization for Men on Monday gave Roseanne Barr its Wimps, Grumblers and Grousers Award for her jokes about males. The award is given "to recognize deliberate efforts to drive a wedge between men and women," said Sidney Siller, founder of the 10,000-member group. Shauna Snow
