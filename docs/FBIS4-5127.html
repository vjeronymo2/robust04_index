BFN [Text] Netherlands Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers cut short his visit and left Jakarta for home this evening. The state guest earlier paid a farewell call on President and Mrs. Suharto before returning home. Minister and State Secretary Murdiono said Prime Minister Lubbers was originally scheduled to have a four-day stay in Indonesia. Lubbers had earlier been scheduled to meet tomorrow morning with Hartarto, coordinating minister for industry and trade; Saleh Afiff, coordinating minister for economy, finance, and development supervision; and Wardiman Joyonegoro, minister of education and culture.
