940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / International Company News: Fleet Financial expansion Fleet Financial, New England's biggest banking group, is set to strengthen its position further in Massachusetts through a Dollars 420m takeover of the state's biggest savings bank, writes Richard Waters in New York. Fleet has agreed to pay a mix of cash and shares for NBB, parent of the the savings bank, New Bedford Institution for Savings, which has Dollars 2.5bn in assets. In addition to the Dollars 420m, Fleet will issue 2.5m warrants to NBB shareholders giving them the right to buy Fleet shares at Dollars 43 7/8 over the next six years. The bank also announced it would buy back 6m of its own shares to satisfy the stock portion of the offer.
