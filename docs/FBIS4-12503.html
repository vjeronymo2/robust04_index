BFN [Text] Nayif Hawatimah, secretary general of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP], has said that the growing popular Palestinian resistance will wreck the Oslo and Cairo agreements. He stressed that it is impossible to coexist with the settlements and that there is no alternative to the removal of the occupation. Nayif Hawatimah renewed his criticism of the Palestinian leadership, which, he said, has ceded all the Palestinian people's demands after the Hebron massacre. Meanwhile, the unified central leadership of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] and the DFLP demanded PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and his team resign. It condemned the occupation authorities' decision to close the occupied Palestinian territories, deeming it a collective detention of the Palestinian people.
