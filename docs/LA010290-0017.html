January 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: WEDDING BELLS

Sajak Buys W-E-D: The host of "Wheel of Fortune" and CBS's late-night talk show has a new role: husband.

Pat Sajak, 42, married Lesly Brown, 24, in a private service late on New Year's Eve in Annapolis, Md. The Baltimore Sun reports the newlyweds ducked in a side door of St. Mary's Catholic Church to avoid about two dozen onlookers, and -- after a service featuring a 25-piece orchestra -- left with similar stealth. Sajak fans waiting in the rain outside the church were mollified, however, by a glimpse of Vanna White, Sajak's "Wheel of Fortune" co-star. Claudia Puig
