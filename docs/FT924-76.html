921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / Sheffield cuts jobs SHEFFIELD City Council is seeking a further 1,120 voluntary redundancies by the end of January, Mr Mike Bower, the council leader, said yesterday. The new cuts are needed to head off a projected overspend of Pounds 37m in the next financial year, and are in addition to 1,300 early retirements this year from the Labour-controlled council's workforce of 25,000.
