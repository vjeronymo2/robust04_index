Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A 12-year-old boy, 'Ayman al-Suri, was killed this afternoon in the Jabalyah refugee camp when IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers fired at stone throwers. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that this morning, two firebombs were hurled at IDF troops in Jabalyah and in Gaza city. There were no casualties and no damage was caused. A general strike was observed for the third consecutive day in the Gaza Strip today, in mourning for the death of the leader of the Fatah Hawks cell in Rafah, who was killed in a shootout with IDF soldiers. Khayr Yusuf 'Abdallah, from the village of Bal'a in Samaria, was killed this morning by IDF gunfire when he attempted to flee his village, which was under curfew. The curfew was imposed in an attempt to locate stolen cars. A youth was injured by soldiers' gunfire near al-Duhayshah refugee camp after breaking through an Army roadblock in his car.
