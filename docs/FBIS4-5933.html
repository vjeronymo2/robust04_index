BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- The Ministry of People's Armed Forces Thursday hosted a cocktail party for the military attaches of foreign embassies in Pyongyang on the occasion of the 15th of April. It was attended by Vice Marshal Kim Kwang-chin, Col. Gen. Pak Chae-kyong, Lieut. Gen. Pak Song-won, other generals and officers of the Korean People's Army. Speeches were made at the cocktail party.
