Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] An investigatory body of representatives of the prosecutor's office of the Republic of Georgia is continuing its work in Zugdidi and Tsalenjikha rayons checking the authenticity of reports on former president Zviad Gamsakhurdia's death. The investigatory body does not yet possess any data confirming these reports. The investigatory group will arrive in the villages of Nakipu and Jagira in Tsalenjikha rayon today because, according to rumors, Zviad Gamsakhurdia committed suicide and was buried in one of them.
