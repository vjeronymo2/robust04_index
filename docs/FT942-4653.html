940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / UK Company News: FT-SE Actuaries Share Indices The FT-SE Actuaries Industry Classification Committee has decided on the following changes to classification of companies, to take effect on July 1: Caverdale Group from Distributors of Industrial Components & Equipment (Sub-sector 412) to Vehicle Distributors (413); James Crean from Food Manufacturers (330) to Diversified Industrials (240); Halkin Holdings from Breweries (310) to Distributors, Other (414); Huntleigh Technology from Instruments, Tools & Mechanical Handling Equipment (269) to Health Care (360); Process Systems from Instruments, Tools & Mechanical Handling Equipment (269) to Electronic Equipment (253); Martin Shelton from Other Businesses (516) to Printing (284); Spandex from Building & Construction (210) to Distributors of Industrial Components & Equipment (412); Sutcliffe Speakman from Pollution Control (512) to Chemicals, Speciality (234); Western Mining from Diversified Industrials (240) to Other Mineral Extractors & Mines (125). London Share Service: Sungard Systems will be moved from Electronic & Electrical Equipment to Support Services with effect from July 1.
