921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: BSR makes Dollars 8m disposal ASTEC, a member of the BSR group, announced that its subsidiary, Beckman Industrial, had disposed of its instrumentation products division, which manufactures and markets test instruments, to Wavetek Corporation of San Diego, California. Total consideration was Dollars 7.97m (Pounds 4.71m) and in addition, the purchaser assumed bank borrowings of Dollars 1.21m. Some Dollars 3.5m of the price was received in cash with the balance payable in ten instalments over five years. Net book value of the assets disposed of amounted to Dollars 6.67bn at December 31 1991.
