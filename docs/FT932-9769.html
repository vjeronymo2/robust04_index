930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Micro Focus and IBM relationship Micro Focus shares yesterday advanced to Pounds 24.28p before settling back to close 58p higher on the day at Pounds 24.18p, on the news that the company and IBM Programming Systems of California had expanded their long-term relationship in mainframe operating software. Micro Focus is licensing technology from IBM and will market it with a new version of one of its existing products in the MVS (IBM's flagship operating system) area, expected to be ready in September.
