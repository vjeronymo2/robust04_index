BFN [Text] Paris, June 26, INA--French Foreign Ministry said an official Iraqi delegation is currently in France for talks with various French companies. A Ministry spokesman admitted that this visit by the Iraqi delegation, which is composed of petroleum experts, was not the first to be made by an Iraqi group and noted that all nations had legitimate right to do so. The spokesman added that the delegation's visit came only one day after the meeting between Rolf Ekeus, head of the UN Special Commission, and Foreign Minister Alain Juppe. Ekeus' current visit to Paris falls within a tour of many European capitals prior to the July Security Council discussions of the UN resolutions on Iraq.
