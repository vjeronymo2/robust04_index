BFN [Text] The Government of Japan has asserted its support for unity and democracy in the Yemeni Republic, regretting the exceptional situation our country is experiencing. A press statement issued by the Press Office of the Japanese Foreign Ministry -- a copy of which was given to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', by the Japanese Embassy -- said the Government of Japan welcomes the announcement of the government of the Yemeni Republic of its acceptance of Security Council Resolution 924, issued on 1 June, and its willingness to implement it. The Government of Japan expressed its hope that all the parties concerned will respond to the Security Council resolution.
