Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Presidential Council Vice Chairman brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid received brother Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, adviser to His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, in Aden today. The Jordanian official relayed to brother al-Bid a message from His Majesty King Husayn on bilateral ties and the efforts made by his majesty the king and fraternal Jordanians to help our country cope with the crisis and address the difficulties and challenges posed to the efforts to build a modern Yemeni state. The message said that His Majesty King Husayn welcomes and blesses the document of accord agreed to by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee. In his message, King Husayn also expressed a willingness to host a meeting of all concerned parties to sign the document of accord, saying that he is willing to make the necessary arrangements for such an event. Presidential Council Vice Chairman brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid expressed his appreciation for the efforts made by His Majesty King Husayn and fraternal Jordanians in this regard. He welcomed His Majesty King Husayn's invitation to all concerned parties for a meeting to endorse the document of accord. However, he said that the issue must be decided by the committee formed by dialogue committee, given that it is the party charged with setting a date and a venue for this event. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid asked his brother Dr. Khalid al-Karaki to relay his greetings to His Majesty King Husayn, as well as his wishes of continued good health and happiness to him, and of further progress and prosperity to the fraternal Jordanian people. The meeting was attended by Aden Governor Salih Munassar al- Siyali and Jordanian Ambassador to Yemen Dr. Fayiz al-Rabi'.
