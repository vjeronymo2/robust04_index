September 19, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PHILHARMONIC SOCIETY RECEIVES TWO GRANTS

The Orange County Philharmonic Society has received two grants for education and community outreach programs.

The Times Orange County Edition has contributed $25,000 plus services to help support the society's "Cruise of Lights," an annual series of holiday boat cruises of decorated homes and waterways in Huntington Harbour, scheduled Dec. 13 through 21.

Toshiba America, which will not disclose the amount of its gift, will help underwrite the society's fifth-grade youth concerts. ZAN DUBIN
