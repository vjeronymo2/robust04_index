BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 22 TASS -- A Progress M-23 cargo spacecraft, launched from Baykonur Cosmodrome at seven hours 30 minutes 02 seconds, Moscow daylight saving time, on Sunday, was put into orbit with the following parameters: -- Maximum distance from the Earth surface -- 247 km, -- minimum distance from the Earth surface -- 192 km, -- orbital period -- 88.6 minutes, -- orbital inclination -- 51.6 degrees. The onboard systems of the spacecraft are functioning properly. The spacecraft is to deliver 2,207 kilogrammes of various cargoes to the orbital station Mir. The two spacecrafts are to dock on May 24.
