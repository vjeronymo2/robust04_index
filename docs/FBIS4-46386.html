BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Jun (MENA)--A responsible security official has denied a report by a news agency on a clash between worshipers at al-Sahabah Mosque in the town of Akhmim in the Governorate of Suhaj and a number of extremist elements recently. The source said that this report is totally false. There is no truth in it since no confrontation or clashes of any kind took place at all, as the agency claimed. A news agency had reported that six people, including two extremists, were injured in a confrontation between worshipers and elements which it described as terrorist in al-Sahabah Mosque in Akhmim town, according to its report.
