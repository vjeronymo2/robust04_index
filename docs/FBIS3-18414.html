Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report by Peter Gnam: "Majority Says `Yes' to European Union!"] [Excerpt] Vienna -- After the agreement in Brussels, the Austrians seem to have been seized with euphoria for Europe: A poll that was exclusively carried out by the OGM [Austrian Marketing Company] on behalf of NEUE KRONEN-ZEITUNG shows that the majority now says `yes' to entry into the European Union [EU]. However, this only shows the mood at the moment, which might be different in a few days. This is the result of a flash poll that was carried out by the OGM on Wednesday evening (500 telephone contacts that are representative of the whole of Austria): Extrapolating from these results, 42 percent of the population would vote for Austria's entry into the EU in the June referendum, 25 percent against, and 32 percent undecided.
