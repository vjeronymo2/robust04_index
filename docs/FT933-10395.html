930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Unigroup gets Pounds 15m for Pharos stake Unigroup, the timber and building materials group, will receive Pounds 14.8m from Antah Holdings, one of its shareholders, for its outstanding 60 per cent stake in Golden Pharos. Unigroup announced earlier in the year it was floating 35 per cent of Pharos on the Kuala Lumpur stock exchange. Dealings of 17.5m shares will begin on September 13 at a price of MDollars 1.9m each.
