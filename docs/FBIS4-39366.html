BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 8 June TASS -- Russian border troops suppressed at least four attempts at illegal crossing of the border between Tajikistan and Afghanistan on Tuesday [7 June] night. Artillery fire was opened to stop a group of transgressors at a stretch guarded by the Pyanj unit, and more fire had to be used to stop the violation of the border near Sarigora and Shuroabad where the Moscow border guard unit is in charge. Up to thirty Tajik armed opposition members were repelled when trying to cross the border from Afghanistan into Tajikistan in the Pamirs mountains. None of the Russian border troops was injured.
