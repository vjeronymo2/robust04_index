940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / European Elections: Party chairman says Labour the 'only truly national party' 'Labour is the only truly national party now,' Mr David Blunkett, Labour party chairman, said yesterday. 'These superb results confirm that Labour is not only on course to defeat the Conservatives in the general election, but has finally laid to rest the Liberal Democrat myth that Labour cannot win in the south of England.' As Labour and the Liberal Democrats blamed each other for limiting Tory losses, Mr Blunkett said: 'If the Tories had not been rescued by the Liberal Democrats in Thames Valley, Sussex South and Crawley and the Cotswolds, they would have suffered an even worse humiliation than they did in the end.'
