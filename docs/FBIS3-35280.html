Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, Feb 22 (AFP)--Northern Yemeni troops on Tuesday ambushed and killed 15 soldiers from the rival south, a Yemeni official said here. The ambush was sprung by a northern brigade at al-'Amaliqah, based in the Abyan region of southern Yemen, the south Yemeni official said in Jordan where the rival leaders signed a reconciliation accord on Sunday. There was no confirmation from the north.
