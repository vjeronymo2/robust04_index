§22.563 Provision of rural radiotelephone service upon request. Channels in the frequency ranges 152.03&hyph;152.81, 157.77&hyph;158.67, 454.025&hyph;454.650 and 459.025&hyph;459.650 MHz, inclusive, are also allocated for assignment in the Rural Radiotelephone Service. Stations in the Paging and Radiotelephone Service that provide two-way public mobile service on these channels must also provide rural radiotelephone service upon request from a subscriber. §22.565 Transmitting power limits. The transmitting power of base, mobile and fixed transmitters operating on the channels listed in §22.561 must not exceed the limits in this section.
