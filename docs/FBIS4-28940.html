BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 10 (KCNA) -- Koreans' organisations and Koreans in Americas and Europe are briskly conducting activities to make this year a year of the great unity of the nation. The American Committee on the Korean Question repeatedly published informations supporting the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation in December last year and early this year. The informations said the 10-point programme is the most fair and aboveboard proposal of reunification acceptable to anyone irrespective of differences in ideology and idea, party affiliation and religious belief. The One Korea Movement in a statement said the 10-point programme indicates the way of ending the national division, promoting the national interests and achieving security and prosperity of the Korean nation. Son Hak-won, chairman of the Society for the Study of the Chuche Idea of Koreans in the United States, in his address to a meeting held in Los Angeles in April to celebrate the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song, called on overseas compatriots to build an independent, democratic, neutral and denuclearized country by following the spirit of the great national unity of President Kim Il-song. A meeting for achieving Korea's reunification under the banner of the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation also was held in Washington in April, and the 3rd meeting of the Society for Peaceful Reunification of Korea took place in Berkeley, the United States. Organisations of Koreans overseas are briskly conducting activities to explain and disseminate the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation. Organizations of Koreans in Germany published and distributed to compatriots the "10-Point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" and books explaining the programme, including "Great Charter of National Unity" and "New Evolution of Principles of National Reunification."
