930416 FT 16 APR 93 / People: Bodies politic Colin Ford has been appointed director of the National Museum of Wales. Currently head of the National Museum of Photography, Film and Television in Bradford, Ford will take over in Cardiff in October when a Pounds 26m building programme has been completed. James Roe, chairman of Equity Consort Investment Trust and of CST Emerging Asia Trust, and a director of Rothschild Trust Corporation, has been appointed a reserve member of the MONOPOLIES AND MERGERS COMMISSION.
