April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GOOD TIME TO PLANT JAPANESE MAPLE TREE

Now is a good time to plant a Japanese maple tree -- it fits into any garden. It grows as a small tree or shrub that offers spring growth in a red color, soft green summer leaves and fall foliage of scarlet, orange or yellow. As a bonus, it provides a graceful silhouette of delicate green or red branches in the winter.
