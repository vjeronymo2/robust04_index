January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ROCKIN' AND RACKIN' THE CRITIC

Will someone please inform Robert Hilburn that his feeble attempt at being the weatherman of rock 'n' roll is not amusing ("Today's Stars: Tomorrow's Legends," Jan. 15).

According to Hilburn, at the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame there is a 60% chance of scattered Los Lobos, followed by a 35% chance of Billy Idol. The high will be 99% Michael Jackson and the low will be 1% Tiffany.

Tell us, Mr. Hilburn, should we take our umbrella?

RICK STEPHENSON

West Covina
