January 3, 1989, Tuesday, Late Final Edition

PEACE ACTIVISTS DAMAGE 2 DUTCH AIR FORCE JETS

Two peace activists attacked two Dutch air force jets with a sledgehammer and an ax, damaging the planes, a Defense Ministry spokesman confirmed Monday.

The activists, one a former army chaplain, remained in military police custody on this southern Dutch air force base where the two NF-5 fighter-bombers were waiting to be flown to Turkey, the planes' new owner. The suspects were not identified.
