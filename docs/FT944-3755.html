941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Main post offices face strike today Counter staff plan to strike in more than 300 main post offices today in protest at the partial privatisation of services. The action will involve post offices in London, the Midlands, Wales and Scotland, in addition to Merseyside, east Cornwall, Hertfordshire and Cambridgeshire. The one-day strike is over the Post Office's plans to transfer individual post offices from the public to the private sector.
