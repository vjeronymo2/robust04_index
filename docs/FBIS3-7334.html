Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 26 -- Vietnamese Ambassador to Sweden Pham Ngoc, on behalf of the Vietnamese Government, and Vice Minister of Finance Dick Kling, on behalf on the Swedish Government, signed in Stockholm on March 24 an agreement on avoidance of double taxation between the two countries. Together with the agreement on investment promotion and guarantee signed in September 1993, the new agreement will contribute to promoting their economic, commercial, scientific-technical and investment cooperation on the basis of mutual benefit in conformity with the traditional friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
