921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Majedie asset value declines to 338p Over the 12 months ended September 30 net asset value of Majedie Investments fell from 348p to 338p. However, on the revenue side earnings improved. Directors said their generally cautious approach proved to be advantageous in a period of volatility in the currency and securities markets. Investment income came to Pounds 4.56m (Pounds 4.53m) and earnings per share worked through at 10.07p (9.81p). The final dividend is 7p, for a total of 9.5p (9p) and a 1-for-1 scrip issue is proposed.
