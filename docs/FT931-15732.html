930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / People: Insurance moves Following completion of the Pounds 33m management buy-out from Brown Shipley, HOLMWOODS Insurance Group, the specialist schools insurance broker, has announced two new appointments. David Godfray, 51, who has been with the group since 1960, has been appointed managing director. Carl Openshaw, 54, a former executive director of the Guthrie Corporation, the trading and agribusiness group, takes over as finance and development director. Openshaw, like chairman Colin Ingleby-Mackenzie, is a keen cricketer, and has captained a highly successful Kent over - 50s side in recent years. ***** Tony Hart is appointed md, Maggie Belson director of information, and Eamon Bardon accounts director at MERRETT MANAGEMENT SERVICES, part of Merrett Holdings. ***** Kent Urness has been appointed md of St Paul (UK) and will become ceo on April 5 when the present chairman and ceo Vartkis Boghos continues solely as chairman until he retires a year later. ***** Rick May, deputy chief executive of CLERICAL MEDICAL INTERNATIONAL, has been appointed a director of Coryo-CM Life Insurance Corporation, its joint venture with the republic of Korea. John Ferguson, formerly executive vice-president of Coryo-CM Life Insurance, has returned to the UK to become development and insurance marketing director of CMI. ***** Christopher McKnight has been appointed a director of ATTWOOD & HART. ***** Richard Coleman and Steve Taylor have been appointed directors of H CLARKSON AND COMPANY. ***** Terry Marris, formerly administration director of Black Horse Financial Services, has been appointed operations director of HAMBRO GUARDIAN ASSURANCE. ***** Ted Constable, a former md at Frizzell Life & Financial Planning, has been appointed a director of HOLMWOODS LIFE & PENSIONS.
