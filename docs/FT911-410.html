910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / UK Company News: P & 0 chief repeats warning on results At the annual meeting of Peninsular & Oriental Steam Navigation, Lord Sterling, the chairman, repeated his warning that the 1991 results could not be 'anything but disappointing, especially in the first half.' While the company's weekly indicators were not showing a worsening position, there was little more than a suspicion of a recovery.
