940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Daejan rises to Pounds 8.4m Daejan Holdings, the property company, yesterday announced pre-tax profits up from Pounds 7.64m to Pounds 8.39m for the half year ended September 30. The company said that full-year profits were likely to be similar to those reported last year which amounted to some Pounds 14.2m, excluding the surplus arising on disposal of a subsidiary company. The interim dividend is maintained at 12p from earnings per share of 34.85p (30.84p). Net rental income rose to Pounds 8.36m (Pounds 7.91m) and there was a Pounds 4.09m (Pounds 4.16m) surplus on sale of trading properties. Operating profits on continuing activities were virtually flat at Pounds 10.1m and there was a Pounds 482,000 (Pounds 354,000) surplus on the sale of investment properties.
