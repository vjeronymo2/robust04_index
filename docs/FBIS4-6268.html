BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Foreign party delegations staying in Korea saw a Taekwon-do demonstration at the Taekwon-do Hall on April 17. Invited to see the demonstration were the delegation of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana headed by its General Secretary Huudu Yahaya, the delegation of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan headed by its co-chairman P.V. Svoik, the delegation of the Workers' Party of Ireland headed by its General Secretary Pat Quearney, General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Turkey Yalsin Buyukdagli, and other party delegations. Seeing the demonstration together with the foreign guests were Kim Chung-nin, secretary, and Kim Yang-kon, vice director of a department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials concerned. The demonstration was acclaimed by the spectators for its high technique and might.
