Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] Two PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] rival groups that are disputing municipal headship in San Mateo, Atengo, Mexico State, clashed on the morning of 3 February, causing severe damage to municipal headquarters and injury to several individuals. One municipal police patrol vehicle and a private vehicle were torched during the clashes. Both sides are fighting over the San Mateo Municipality, Atengo, one in favor of Santiago Cepeda and the other in favor of (Gustavo Sejuro).
