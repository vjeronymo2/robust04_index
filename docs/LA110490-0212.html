November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SO LITTLE RESPECT RECENTLY FOR S. PASADENA POLICE DEPT.

Sunday, Oct. 28, you printed a letter to the editor titled "Party-Goer's Side of the Story."

It's signed John Bernardi Jr. In my opinion, John Jr. is a big crybaby. In the article you printed (Times, Oct. 4), it said when the police tried to do their job he said wait till I tell my dad.

I think it really stinks that the South Pasadena police can't do their job without thinking twice!

TIM TANAKA

South Pasadena
