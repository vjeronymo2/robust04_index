940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Metro Scottish Metropolitan Property has acquired eight retail investment properties within the Carlton Highland Hotel, Edinburgh, for Pounds 3.75m, at an initial yield of about 8 per cent. The company has also let Kirk House, West Drayton, Middlesex, for 15 years at an annual rental of Pounds 160,000 to the Secretary of State for Health.
