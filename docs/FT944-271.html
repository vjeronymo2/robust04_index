941230 FT 30 DEC 94 / Talks on Polly Peck assets Negotiations are taking place between the administrators of Polly Peck International, part of the collapsed business empire of fugitive Asil Nadir, and two sets of prospective buyers interested in the assets of the Polly Peck group in northern Cyprus. Coopers & Lybrand, the London-based administrators, last night confirmed that talks were taking place over the purchase of the assets, which have an estimated avalue of Pounds 50m. It is understood that one of the buyers is a prominent northern Cypriot politician. The assets include two operating hotels in Cyprus, a newly completed hotel, a fruit packaging company and a cardboard box maker.
