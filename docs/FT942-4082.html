940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Laird in US acrylics deal with ICI Laird Group, via its US holding company Laird Inc, is buying the Compton, California, facility of ICI Acrylics. The plant makes acrylic pellets and sheet and will employ 50 people. The value of the deal is less than 2 per cent of Laird's net assets and will be paid in cash.
