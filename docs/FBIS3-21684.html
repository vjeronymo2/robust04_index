Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sofia, February 21 (BTA) -- Today the government discussed regulations concerning the National Council for Control of Drug Abuse and Arms Trafficking. It was established by a government decree last August and is chaired by the minister of health. It is to develop the legal framework of drug control and will be in charge of the prevention of drug abuse and the treatment and rehabilitation of drug addicts. It will coordinate the actions of all state institutions and will collect and analyze data on the matter. The council's financing will be provided by the Ministry of Health. [passage omitted]
