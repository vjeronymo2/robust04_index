BFN [Text] Majles Deputy Speaker Hasan Rowhani and Shaykh Sa'id Sha'ban, the leader of Lebanon's Islamic Tawhid Movement, held talks in Damascus last night. According to the Central News Unit, in this meeting issues related to the world of Islam, in particular Palestine, were discussed. Shaykh Sa'id Sha'ban said: Under the wise leadership of His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the Islamic Republic of Iran stands as an obstacle to world arrogance. Witness to this fact are the various plots against the Islamic revolution. The Majles deputy speaker responded by stressing the need for greater coordination between combatant Palestinian and Lebanese groups. According to this report, a number of other Lebanese group leaders also met with Mr. Rowani yesterday and last night.
