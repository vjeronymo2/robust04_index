941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / Letters to the Editor: Drastic consequence of biscuit cut-back Sir, Your article, 'City thefts take the biscuit' (December 20) spells out in detail the pounds (Pounds ) saved by solicitors in reducing the consumption of biscuits. Surely of much greater importance would have been to have noted the savings in pounds (lbs). Geraldine Kave, GAAPS, Grafton House, 2/3 Golden Square, London W1R 3AD
