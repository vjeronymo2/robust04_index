940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / International Company News: Glencar losses deepen to IPounds 0.49m Increased pre-tax losses of IPounds 490,502 (Pounds 482,000) were announced by Glencar Explorations, the Dublin-based mineral exploration company, for the year to December 31. In 1992 losses were IPounds 271,801. The loss was after exceptional charges of IPounds 419,871 (IPounds 212,474) comprising a IPounds 309,000 write-down of the investment in Andaman Resources and a IPounds 100,000 provision relating to the Hungarian uranium mine. The balance relates to exploration expenditures written off.
