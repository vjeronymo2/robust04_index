October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

VOTE ON BUDGET ACCORD

Here is how members of the California delegation voted on the broad outlines of the bipartisan budget accord:

Democrats for -- Anderson, Beilenson, Bosco, Fazio, Lantos, Matsui, Mineta, Panetta, Torres.

Republicans for -- Lewis, Lowery, Shumway.

Democrats against -- Bates, Berman, Boxer, Brown, Condit, Dellums, Dixon, Dymally, Edwards, Hawkins, Lehman, Levine, Martinez, Miller, Pelosi, Roybal, Stark, Waxman.

Republicans against -- Campbell, Cox, Dannemeyer, Dornan, Dreier, Gallegly, Herger, Hunter, Lagomarsino, McCandless, Moorhead, Packard, Pashayan, Rohrabacher, Thomas.
