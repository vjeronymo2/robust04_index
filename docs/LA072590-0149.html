July 25, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

3 CUBANS VOLUNTARILY LEAVE SWISS EMBASSY

Three Cubans who had sought refuge in the Swiss Embassy in Havana have voluntarily left, Swiss officials said today.

The asylum-seekers, who managed to penetrate tight Cuban security, had entered the Swiss compound on Sunday. They told diplomats that they wanted to leave the country because of restrictions on free speech.
