Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 10 (XINHUA) -- A diplomatic function was held here today to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the Republic of Tunisia. Chinese Assistant Foreign Minister Wang Changyi and Tunisian Ambassador to China Mohamed Habib Kaabachi attended the function given by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries (CPAFFC). In his speech, CPAFFC President Han Xu noted that in the past 30 years the two countries have supported each other politically and cooperated with each other economically and have made big progress in their relations of friendship and cooperation in all domains on the basis of the five principles of peaceful co-existence. He said that his institute is ready to do its part to promote further development of Sino-Tunisian ties. Tunisian Ambassador Kaabachi said "though far away from each other, Tunisia and China are linked together by friendship." The Tunisian Government pays great attention to its ties with China and hopes that these ties would be further developed, he added.
