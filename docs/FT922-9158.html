920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / International Company News: Kazakhstan in joint venture with Chevron CHEVRON, the fourth largest US oil and gas company, yesterday said it has agreed to form a 50-50 joint venture with the Republic of Kazakhstan to invest Dollars 1.5bn in oil exploration, writes Alan Friedman in New York. Mr Ken Derr, Chevron chairman, said the joint venture would explore 4,000 square kilometres in the Tengiz area. Chevron said the initial investment would be Dollars 1.5bn over the first three years, but the company refused to say what proportion of the funds it would provide.
