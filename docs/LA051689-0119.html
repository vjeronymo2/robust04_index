May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: TERMINAL DATA DROPS PLAN TO BUY PHOTOMATRIX CORP.

Terminal Data in Moorpark said its plans to buy Photomatrix Corp. in Culver City have been scrapped.

The companies gave no reason why the deal fell through. In February, the companies announced that an agreement had been reached for Terminal Data to buy Photomatrix for an undisclosed price.

Terminal Data makes equipment that converts documents into images for storage on disks and microfilm. Photomatrix, a private company, makes microfiche duplicating equipment and scanning devices.
