931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / Tecs' budgets win approval TRAINING and Enterprise Councils have made substantial improvements in the financial control of publicly funded training programmes. As a result the National Audit Office has approved the accounts of the Department of Employment for the first time in four years. Even so, the Tecs made incorrect or unsubstantiated claims for training totalling Pounds 16.1m compared with Pounds 40m in 1991-92 and Pounds 53m in 1990-91.
