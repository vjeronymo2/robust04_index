BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Nekrasova] [Text] Lvov 24 June TASS -- The Ukrainian National Assembly (UNA), which has a combat paramilitary organisation UNSO (People's Self-Defence), has become the initiator of the formation of an anti-fascist committee and appointed Oleg Vitovich, a nationalist from Lvov and people's deputy to the Ukrainian parliament, as committee chairman. UNA leader Anatoliy Lupynis, in an interview with the UNIAN news agency, said the democrats and liberals were the locomotives of political fascistisation in Ukraine. It is noteworthy that it is precisely the selfsame democrats and liberals who refer to the UNA-UNSO organisation as a modern neonazi one, because its militants participated in the recent military conflicts in the Dniester region, Georgia, interconfessional strife inside the state, and now leave for the Crimea for summer "holidays."
