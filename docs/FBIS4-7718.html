BFN [Text] Bosnian Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic supports the plans of the International Committee of the Red Cross to evacuate non-Serb residents of Prijedor in order to protect them from violence. Human lives are most important, even if that helps the Serbs in their ethnic cleansing, Silajdzic told journalists. Around 9,000 Muslims and Croats and 55,000 Serbs live in Prijedor at the moment.
