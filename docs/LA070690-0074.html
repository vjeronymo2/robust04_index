July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Western Digital Sued for Patent Infringement: Cadtrak Corp., a small computer graphics licenser in San Mateo, Calif., said it filed a lawsuit against Western Digital and one of its subsidiaries for allegedly infringing two Cadtrak patents that cover several fundamental functions in most computer graphics devices. Cadtrak's suit, filed in U.S. District Court in San Francisco, did not specify damages. Western Digital said in a statement that Cadtrak's claim lacks merit and that it will file a countersuit asking for treble damages against Cadtrak.
