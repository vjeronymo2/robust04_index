BFN [Text] Yerevan, 4 May (SNARK) -- Artavazd Gevorkyan, Armenian General Prosecutor, reported today to the Armenian temporary Parliamentary commission that presently the Prosecutor's Office is investigating seven criminal cases connected with the abuse of power by high rank officials. The temporary Parliamentary commission is engaged in control of the corresponding inquest group of the Armenian Prosecutor's Office. He said the inquest group has revealed "a system of theft and misappropriation of big money sums, coming to a person through a dummy." The General Prosecutor declared that some persons, engaged in this case, had left Armenia, and it hampers the effective investigation. He also reported that the Interior Ministry is intensively searching these persons. The General Prosecutor added that over 1,000 people in Armenia are in a search on suspicion of committing various crimes. Mr. Gevorkyan said nobody has exerted any pressure on him connected with the cases of power abuse.
