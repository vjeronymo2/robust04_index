Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] In an interview given to Prishtina's weekly ZERI, commenting on his latest tour of several European countries, Ibrahim Rugova, president of the republic of Kosova, states that he encountered understanding for the right to independence and neutrality of the people of Kosova in all of his meetings. The proposal to place Kosova under international protection is simply a political means toward Kosova's independence. The UN should decide on this with the mediation of the European Union and the United States, said Mr. Rugova, among other things, to Prishtina's ZERI.
