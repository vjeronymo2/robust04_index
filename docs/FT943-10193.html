940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Possible backers for mines buy-out bid The Northern Coal Company, set up by Mr Alan Houghton to bid for the Central North Regional Coal Company being offered under proposals for the privatisation of British Coal, has eight financial institutions interested in providing funds. KPMG Corporate Finance, which is advising the management and employee buy-out team, said representatives from Schroder Ventures, Apax Partners, Phildrew Ventures, Barclays Bank, Midland Bank, NatWest Ventures, Royal Bank of Scotland and 3i had travelled to the Selby complex in Yorkshire to visit Mr Houghton, group director of British Coal Corporation Northern Group.
