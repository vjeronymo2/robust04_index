940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Briefcase, Q&A: Income tax query I wonder if you would be good enough to set out for me, step by step, the income tax calculation for 1993/94 assuming one has a salary of Pounds 20,000, investment (equities gross) of Pounds 10,000, a gross income of Pounds 30,000, and one is under 65 and married. It is the treatment of the married couples allowance at 20 per cent which has perplexed me. I might add that I phoned the Revenue Enquiry Room for an explanation but they 'regretted that they had no instructions yet'. The restriction of relief on the married couple's allowance to 20 per cent does not come into effect until 1994-95; it will be restricted to 15 per cent for 1995-96.
