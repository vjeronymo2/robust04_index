920428 FT 28 APR 92 / International Company News: Hawaiian trust puts Dollars 250m into Goldman Sachs A Dollars 1.1bn HAWAIIAN educational trust, Kamehameha Schools/Bishop Estate, has invested Dollars 250m of long-term equity in Goldman Sachs, the US investment bank and broking house. It is the latest move by Goldman to bolster its capital base, which currently stands at Dollars 3bn. In December Goldman sold Dollars 275m of limited partnership interests to 22 US insurance companies. Soon after, Sumitomo Bank agreed to extend its Dollars 500m investment in Goldman for another 20 years.
