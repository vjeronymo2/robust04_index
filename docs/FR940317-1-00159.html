ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) requests comments from the public on proposed changes to rules concerning grants for State and community programs on aging. These changes are required to clarify and implement Older Americans Act (Act) Sections 305(a)(2)(C), 305(a)(2)(D), 307(b)(1), 307(c), 307(d), and 307(e) as amended by the 1992 amendments to the Act. The Act now requires the Commissioner on Aging to review and approve each State's intrastate formula for the distribution of funds under Title III of the Act, and requires the State to specify the bases and impact of its intrastate funding formula on each Planning and Service Area (PSA) in its State plan. DATES: In order to be considered, comments on this proposed rule must be received on or before May 16, 1994.
