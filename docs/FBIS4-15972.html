BFN [Text] RIA reports: The State Duma has agreed with a Federation Council proposal to create a conciliation commission to overcome any disagreements which may arise. Disagreements arose after the Federation Council rejected a law, approved by the Duma, on the procedure for publishing federal constitutional laws, federal laws, and acts of the chambers of the Russian Federal Assembly and for their coming into force. From the State Duma, Vladimir Bessarabov, Sergey Zenkin, and Anatoliy Lukyanov, members of the Committee on Legislation and Judicial and Legal Reform, will be on the conciliation commission.
