Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Consultative Council for Romania's Euro-Atlantic Integration held a meeting today at the Senate headquarters in Bucharest. [passage omitted] At the end of the meeting, the participants adopted a declaration on the results of the NATO summit meeting in Brussels. In this declaration, the Consultative Council for Romania's Euro-Atlantic Integration recommends that all political parties and institutions in Romania should openly support the effort of the country's integration in NATO, which is an essential goal of a policy devoted to guaranteeing our national security, as well as the European stability. In another declaration, the members of the Consultative Council pledge to support, through relevant actions and means, the process of Romania's integration in the European Union.
