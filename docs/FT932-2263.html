930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Northern Investors assets increase The year ended March 31 1993 was a mixed one for Northern Investors, a venture capital fund. On the capital side, net asset value rose 2.1 per cent, from 289.4p to 295.6p per share. But earnings per share fell from 9.3p to 5.6p, and the final dividend is 3.5p for a total of 5.5p, compared with 8.5p. Investment income fell to Pounds 705,000 (Pounds 867,000) reflecting lower cash balances as well as the fall in interest rates. Dividend income, however, was up 12 per cent to Pounds 361,000. Net revenue attributable dropped to Pounds 207,000 (Pounds 348,000).
