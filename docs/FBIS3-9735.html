Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 28 Mar (MTI) -- Hungarian President Arpad Goncz met Polish Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Monday, the last meeting on the first day of his official visit to Poland. The Polish prime minister met Hungarian journalists afterwards. Talks are in progress on creating CEFTA, the Central European Free Trade zone comprising the Visegrad countries (Hungary, Poland, the Czech Republic, and Slovakia).
