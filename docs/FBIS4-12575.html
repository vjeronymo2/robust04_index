BFN [Text] Iraq has informed the Arab League that the Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers continue to grant facilities to the U.S., British, and French aircraft for violating Iraqi airspace and threatening Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter he addressed to the Arab League secretary general yesterday, Iraq's Arab League permanent representative said that these aircraft continued to violate Iraqi airspace 25-30 April from their bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, and carried out 233 hostile air sorties. A list appended to the letter explains these violations in detail.
