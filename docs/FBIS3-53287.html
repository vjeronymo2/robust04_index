Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 12 Jan (INA)--Staff General Husayn Kamil Hasan, minister of industry and minerals, has received Jordanian Energy and Mineral Resources Minister Walid Mithqal 'Asfur. During the meeting, they discussed industrial and economic cooperation between the two fraternal countries, as well as the means to promote the exchange of expertise and joint action between them.
