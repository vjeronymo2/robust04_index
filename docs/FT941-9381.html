940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Second Alliance Second Alliance Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 18.95p per stock unit at January 31, up from Pounds 16.40p at its July year-end and Pounds 15.70p at end-January 1993. After the preference dividend, available profits for the six month period improved from Pounds 3.74m to Pounds 4.01m, equivalent to earnings of 20.9p (19.49p) per stock unit. The interim dividend goes up 0.5p to 13p.
