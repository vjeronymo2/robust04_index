Columbia proposes to replace approximately 1.3 miles of deteriorating 18-inch pipeline located in Greene and Madison Counties, Ohio and designated as Columbia's Line A, with approximately 1.3 miles of 20-inch pipeline. Columbia states that the sections proposed here for replacement were constructed in 1944 and 1948, using coupled pipeline and are the last coupled sections of Line A between Howell and Mt. Columbia reports that recent examinations of Line A have confirmed general corrosion to the extent that replacement is required in order to maintain service to Columbia's existing customers.
