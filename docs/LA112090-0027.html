November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

ENVIRONMENTAL POLITICS

Congratulations to the voters of California who voted no on the professional/radical environmentalist Props. 128 and 130.

You showed that just tacking the word "environment" on to bad law won't get it passed.

Now that we've cleaned up the trash, maybe we rational environmentalists can get to work on the many real environmental problems we have here in California.

E.F. (GENE) LUCAS, Fall River Mills, Calif.
