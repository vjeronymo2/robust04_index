Language: English Article Type:BFN [Beijing CEI Database on 7 January transmits a "correction" to the following item, which states: "The information contained in the CEIS story 0106029, which was issued 6 January under the title `HK Dollar To Circulate in Shenzhen, Zhuhai,' was later found to be erroneous. We regret any inconvenience caused."] [Text] Beijing (CEIS)--The people's bank of China, the country's central bank, has agreed to the circulation of the Hong Kong dollar in Shenzhen and Zhuhai, two special economic zones in Guangdong province. Experts and officials of the central bank said in line with internationalization, special economic zones need a monetary system different from that in inland cities. They suggest that the Hong Kong dollar, while circulating with renminbi at the same time, should become the main currency in these two zones.
