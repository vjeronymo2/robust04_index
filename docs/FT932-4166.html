930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Banks extend BM facilities BM Group, the engineering concern, said that its principal bankers had extended its banking facilities for a further period to August 6 while discussions continued regarding suitable longer-term finance. The group has also completed the sale of its Robinson Milling Systems group of companies to the Tate & Lyle subsidiary, United Molasses, for Pounds 3.5m including acquired debt. Robinson's is one of Europe's largest feed milling engineering businesses. As a result of the disposal BM's borrowings will be reduced by Pounds 2.9m. It has also sold its shareholding in Blackwood Hodge Zambia for Pounds 200,000 cash.
