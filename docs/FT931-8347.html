930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Shield reaches new agreement on banking Shield Group, the estate agency and property company, has reached agreement with one of its bankers. The bank has agreed to assign to Shield liabilities comprising loans, overdrafts and accrued interest totalling Pounds 448,000, owed by certain dormant subsidiaries, in return for Pounds 50,000. The effect is that group pre-tax profits for the year ended March 31 1993 and net assets at that date will be increased by Pounds 398,000. Mr Nicholas Warriner Brown has acquired 300,000 ordinary shares (3.11 per cent) in the company.
