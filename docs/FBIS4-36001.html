BFN [Text] Jeddah, May 30, SPA -- Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud has sent a reply cable to information minister 'Ali al-Sha'ir expressing thanks to Information Ministry officials for their sincere efforts in covering the hajj season this year. King Fahd said he read the cable sent to him by al-Sha'ir on the occasion of the end of the hajj season and the services and care extended by the kingdom to pilgrims, and the information minister's explanation of accomplishments achieved by the ministry through television, radio, the Saudi Press Agency, and the departments of external information, internal information, engineering affairs, administrative affairs in addition to local newspapers and magazines.
