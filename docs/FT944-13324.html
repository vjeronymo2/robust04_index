941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / Euro-MPs back bigger budget Bringing Austria, Finland, Sweden and Norway into the European Union will mean the EU budget needs to be expanded by more than Dollars 1bn next year, the European Parliament said yesterday. The parliament proposed the draft EU budget for 1995 be increased by Ecu800m (Pounds 629m) to reflect the imminent enlargment of the bloc. Austria and Finland are due to join the EU on January 1 following successful referendums. Sweden and Norway will vote on membership on November 13 and 28 respectively. The figure was included in the parliament's first reading of the draft budget for 1995, in which it proposed an overall budget of Ecu80.9bn.
