BFN [Text] Bangkok, April 25 KYODO -- Thailand on Monday [25 April] welcomed the election of Tsutomu Hata as Japan's prime minister and expressed confidence in continuing close relations centering on regional economic cooperation. "Under Hata's leadership, we believe that Thailand and Japan will continue to enjoy their close and cordial relations, particularly on economic cooperation on both the bilateral and regional level," said Government spokesman Abhisit Vejjajiva [name as received].
