Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A few minutes ago, as soon as NATO's demand that Bosnian Serbs lift the siege of Sarajevo was made public, Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana said in a statement to Radio Nacional that contrary to what one might think, warning of air strikes is a move toward peace. [Begin Solana recording] It is a decision that is, or should, be understood in the context of a peace process. The logic that all of us are still backing is the logic of peace, not the logic of war. Unfortunately, what is happening is that sometimes one has to threaten with a specific military action, with a concrete one, one that has a specific timeframe and in a specific area to reach one's aim, peace, so that it happens more rapidly. [end recording]
