November 6, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: PROBERT WILL JOIN BAKKER IN MINNESOTA PRISON

Bob Probert of the Detroit Red Wings will join former television evangelist Jim Bakker at a federal prison in Minnesota this week, Probert's agent said Sunday.

Probert, 24, who pleaded guilty to importing cocaine into the United States, will begin serving a 90-day term Tuesday at the Federal Medical Center in Rochester, Minn., said Patrick Ducharme.

Bakker, 49, a native of Muskegon, Minn., and founder of the PTL television ministry, arrived Friday at the Minnesota facility to begin serving a 45-year prison term.
