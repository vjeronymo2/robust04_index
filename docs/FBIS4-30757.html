BFN [Unattributed report: "Philippines Wants to Trade With the Czech Republic"] [Text] Manila -- The Czech-Philippine trade agreement that is to replace the no-longer-satisfactory treaty of 1977 was signed in Manila yesterday by Industry and Trade Minister Dlouhy and his Philippine counterpart Rizalino Navarro. Minister Dlouhy was also received together with other members of the Czech delegation by Fidel Ramos, president of the Republic of the Philippines, who expressed great interest of his country in the expansion of the relations with the Czech Republic, particularly in the sphere of trade.
