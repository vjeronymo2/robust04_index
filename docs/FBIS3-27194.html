Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 21 (XINHUA) -- A Sino-Slovak economic and trade seminar was held this morning. Visiting Slovak Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar, and the chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zheng Hongye, spoke at the closing ceremony at noon. Zheng said that, economic and trade relations between China and Slovakia had witnessed rapid development. He said that the seminar would no doubt contribute to the development of such relations. He expressed the hope that business people of the two countries will strengthen exchanges and cooperation, so as to push forward the development of bilateral economic and trade relations. Meciar talked about Slovak preferential policies for foreign investment and development of its economic reform, as well as his ideas about Sino-Slovak economic cooperation. On the basis of equality and mutual benefit, Slovakia is ready to enhance economic and trade cooperative relations with China, he said. He hoped that more Chinese businessmen will go to Slovakia. Some 30 representatives from China's large corporations and nearly 20 Slovak entrepreneurs attended the seminar and conducted business talks.
