November 4, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

* Timothy McLoughlin has been named vice president of marketing for Pacific Inland Bank, Anaheim.

McLoughlin will assist with marketing programs directed at the title and escrow industry. Before joining the bank, he was vice president and sales manager at North American Title Co. Prior to that, he was senior vice president and marketing director at Fidelity National Title Insurance Co.
