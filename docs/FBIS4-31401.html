BFN [Excerpts] Sarajevo, 9 Jun (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces violated the cease-fire on the Sarajevo front last night by opening sniper fire on Serbian defense lines, wounding one civilian and one Serbian soldier, the information center of the Sarajevo-Romanija corps announced this morning. [passage omitted] Last night Serbian reconaissance units sighted a column of vehicles moving along the Mt. Igman-Hrasnica road. It is said that the vehicles were transporting military equipment for the Muslim army, with the tacit approval of the UN Protection Force.
