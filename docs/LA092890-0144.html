September 28, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP

St Bonaventure 13, Calabasas 6 -- St. Bonaventure quarterback Peter Houston connected with Rudy Camacho on a 35-yard scoring pass and Jose Payan scored on a one-yard run in a nonleague game at Calabasas (1-3).

St. Bonaventure (2-2) added a touchdown in the third quarter to take a 13-0 advantage. Calabasas' only score came in the fourth quarter when Damon Riggins, who rushed for 52 yards in 17 carries, recovered a fumble and ran 48 yards for a score. J. SCOTT KLEIN
