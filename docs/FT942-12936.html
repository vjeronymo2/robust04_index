940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wensum makes strong recovery Wensum, the USM-quoted men's wear and corporate clothing group, made substantial progress in the second half to January 29 with pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 56,077 to Pounds 261,680. This contributed to a full year surplus of Pounds 317,757, compared with a Pounds 43,807 loss previously, on turnover up from Pounds 6.57m to Pounds 8.37m. The corporate clothing side performed well and lifted profits from Pounds 38,000 to Pounds 344,000. Earnings per share were 2.87p (0.33p losses) while a final dividend of 1.375p makes a total of 2p (1.825p).
