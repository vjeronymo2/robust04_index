930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Bosnian Moslems set to reject plan Bosnia's Moslems looked set yesterday to reject a plan for a three-way partition of the republic despite warnings from UN envoy Thorvald Stoltenberg that this could threaten peacekeeping efforts there, Reuter reports from Sarajevo. The bulk of Bosnia's collective 10-member presidency met to consider a report recommending formation of a federal state or protectorate under international control. Both ideas would appear to be at odds with a joint Serb-Croat proposal for a division into Serb, Croat and Moslem states.
