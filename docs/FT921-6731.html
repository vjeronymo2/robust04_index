920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / Racing venture to develop 'superbet' BASS LEISURE, the bookmaking division of the brewing and hotels group, and the Horse-race Totalisator Board yesterday announced a joint venture to promote off-course pool betting on horseracing. One of the main aims of the joint venture, Tote Direct, is to develop a new 'superbet' - a national betting pool on a number of televised horseraces designed to produce a winning payout of Pounds 250,000 or more for small stakes.
