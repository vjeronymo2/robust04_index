BFN [Text] Tyre, Lebanon, April 18 (AFP) -- A leading anti-Israeli fighter in south Lebanon was killed as his booby-trapped car exploded on Monday, a spokesman for his Shiite Muslim group Amal said. Ni'mah Ahmad Haydar, 32, was killed as he started up the car in his home village of Qa'qa'iyat al-Jisr. The spokesman blamed Israeli agents for the murder of Haydar, who had led several raids against Israel's self-declared "security zone" in southern Lebanon.
