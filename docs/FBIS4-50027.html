BFN [Text] Beijing, 13 May (XINHUA) -- Presidential decree No.24 of the People's Republic of China: "Decision of the National People's Congress Standing Committee on amending `Regulations Governing Offenses Against Public Order of The People's Republic of China,'" adopted by the Seventh Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee of the People's Republic of China on 12 May 1994, is hereby promulgated and put in force upon promulgation. [signed] Jiang Zemin, president of the People's Republic of China [dated] 12 May 1994
