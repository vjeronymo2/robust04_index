June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WOULD NEVER RETURN

Jerry Hulse's article on Raiatea (May 21) was delightful, as was his piece on Bora Bora. We have just returned from our seventh visit to the islands.

I'm sorry to disagree with his observation of the Relais Mahana. I'm certain that because of his position and the fact that he was with travel agent Ted Cook, he no doubt had royal treatment. I do not want royal treatment, but the charming French family was anything but. They were rude, unhelpful and discourteous. The rooms were small and had an extremely obnoxious odor -- we could not use the pillows (because) they smelled so bad. Other American visitors were treated poorly there also, we were told. The French guests were treated very well. The beach is beautiful, but I would never return.

ALLE SCHUMACHER

Hermosa Beach

Editor's note: All of Jerry Hulse's expenses were paid for by the Los Angeles Times. Ted Cook, of Islands In The Sun, was traveling independently, not with Hulse.

6/18/89
