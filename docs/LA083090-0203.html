August 30, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

DEBATE OVER BUSH'S ACTIONS IN PERSIAN GULF

Comparing Saddam Hussein to Hitler is like trying to compare my cooking to Julia Child's.

I was a witness to Hitler's invasion of Poland. Hitler was a master, in charge of a slaughter of millions.

Saddam, judging by his beginning, is a good student, but let's not give him a chance of catching up with the master.

SOPHIE BINGHAM

Laguna Hills
