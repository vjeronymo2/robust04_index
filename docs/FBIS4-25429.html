BFN [Text] Havana, June 8 (XINHUA) -- Cuban President Fidel Castro Wednesday [8 June] met with a delegation of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Wu Bangguo, the party secretary of Shanghai. Castro said he was happy about the development of friendship between Cuba and China, and highly evaluated the achievements made by the people of China in reform, opening to the outside world and economic construction. Wu said China admired Cuba for its adherence to the road of socialism in difficult circumstances.
