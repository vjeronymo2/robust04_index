November 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DOUGLAS, RAMS TAKE MORE PUNISHMENT, THIS TIME FROM FANS

Once my anger subsided after watching the latest heavyweight championship debacle, it became clear to me what the problem is and what the solution is.

Give the challenger a guaranteed $1 million. Give the champion a guaranteed $2 million. Give the winner the rest of the purse, be it $30 million, $50 million or $100 million. Then you'll see how fast Buster or any other fighter will get off the canvas after a knockdown.

SCOTT MAURER

West Los Angeles
