BFN [Text] Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating and his wife visited Ho Chi Minh City on 12 April. Truong Tan Sang, the people's committee chairman, held a meeting with the Australian prime minister and his entourage that same day. Prime Minister Paul Keating expressed his pleasure in visiting Ho Chi Minh City, a beautiful and lively city of Vietnam. He said that Australia and Vietnam shared similar features in various aspects and that the Australian Government wishes to expand cooperation with Vietnam in various domains. Prime Minister Paul Keating visited the Australian invested BHT Bien Hoa Steel Company and the Vietnam-Australia joint venture VIDEMIX Prefabricated Cement Block Factory in Thu Duc District. He also met with the Australian community in Ho Chi Minh City. Prime Minister Paul Keating and his wife left Ho Chi Minh City this morning, 13 April, concluding his official visit to Vietnam with splendid success.
