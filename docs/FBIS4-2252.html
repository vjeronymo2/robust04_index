BFN [Text] Beijing, April 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zem in today urged Chinese officials at all levels to better arm themselves with knowledge of modern science and technology. The president's call was contained in the foreword to a textbook entitled "ABC of Modern Science and Technology", which was published here today. The book, produced by the State Science and Technology Commission upon the instruction of President Jiang, is intended for government officials at county level and above. Jiang, who is also general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, said in the foreword that the period between now and the middle of the next century is vital for the prosperity of the Chinese nation. He said that the current world situation characterized by peace and stability and the development of new science and technology have provided a precious opportunity for China to advance its economic development. Jiang called on the whole nation to grasp the historical opportunity to keep track of the development of world science and technology and strive to transform the traditional industries and develop new ones. He said that China must give full scope to science and technology in promoting economic growth so as to ensure a sustainable, fast and healthy development. He urged officials at all levels to renew their knowledge in science and technology and keep abreast with modern development. "Only by unswervingly keeping on learning and continuously renewing, can we improve our abilities of making policy decisions and the art of leadership," he said.
