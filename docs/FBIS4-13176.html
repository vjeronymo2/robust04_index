BFN [Text] The Congress-I has welcomed the signing of the new GATT accord by India at Marrakech and charged the opposition with indulging in a disinformation campaign on the issue. Speaking to reporters in New Delhi today, the party spokesman, Mr. V.N. Gadgil, said the agreement will boost India's agricultural and textile exports. He said the government will now have to translate the agreement into various laws and these have to come before parliament. The minister of state for commerce, Mr. Kamaluddin Ahmad, said that the new agreement is on the whole beneficial to India. Speaking to a group of foreign journalists in New Delhi today, he reaffirmed India's faith in a rule-based transparent multilateral trading system. The BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] president, Mr. L.K. Advani, has criticized the signing of the Marrakech Declaration by India. He said in New Delhi that the treaty has been conceived by developed countries to promote their trade interests.
