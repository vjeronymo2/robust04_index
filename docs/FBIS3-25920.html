Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, January 31 (XINHUA) -- China's first educational institute for training brokers engaged in cultural undertakings was set up today in this China's largest metropolis. The training center will aim to ensure that brokers are qualified for cultural undertakings concerning literature, art, publishing, relics, tourism, the transfer of intellectual rights, video and audio products and fashion shows. In its first term, the center will open two classes on training brokers dealing with transactions of cultural relics and arts and crafts. The center is sponsored by the Shanghai Jiaotong University, the Shanghai Cultural Bureau and the Shanghai Administration for Industry and Commerce.
