May 4, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FEUD: The dispute between the airport construction firm and the county over delays in completing the new terminal got so bitter late last year that Britain's Lord Taylor of Hadfield, owner of the firm, and county Supervisor Thomas F. Riley tried to personally negotiate a settlement. . . . Newly released letters show that secret correspondence and even a face-to-face meeting in Riley's office failed to fix blame for the delays, and the dispute remains as hot as ever. . . . At stake: Millions of dollars in withheld county payments and possible penalties. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Dennis McLellan
