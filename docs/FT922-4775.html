920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Gascoigne fit for Pounds 5.5m soccer deal: Paul Gascoigne (left), the British football player sold to Italian club Lazio for a transfer fee of Pounds 5.5m (Dollars 10m), celebrates his return to fitness after an injury that kept him out of the game for the whole of last season. Gascoigne, famed in the UK for his antics on and off the field, had to prove his fitness before the transfer from Tottenham Hotspur could go ahead.
