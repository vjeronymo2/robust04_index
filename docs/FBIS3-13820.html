Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Knesset will meet in special session on Wednesday [23 March] to discuss issues raised by the opposition Likud faction. Yesterday the Likud demanded an urgent Knesset debate in the recess to discuss what it called the government's far-reaching concessions. A Qol Yisra'el reporter says it is possible that there will be discussions on unrelated issues brought up by other parties.
