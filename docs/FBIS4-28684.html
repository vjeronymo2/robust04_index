BFN [Text] The DPRK delegation led by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam, that participated in the foreign ministers' meeting of nonaligned countries held in Egypt, flew back home on 11 June. Greeting the delegation at the airport were First Vice Foreign Minister Kang Sok-chu and other functionaries of relevant sectors and 'Ali Hijazi, ambassador of the Arab Republic of Egypt to our country. The foreign minister and his entourage departed Kiev on 9 June winding up their visit to Ukraine. Seeing off the delegation at the airport were Makarenko, vice foreign minister of Ukraine, functionaries of relevant sectors, and the DPRK ambassador to Ukraine and embassy personnel.
