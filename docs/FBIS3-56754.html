Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- A meeting of the Russian Security Council was held in the Kremlin today under the chairmanship of President Boris Yeltsin. In light of the "Basic Principles of the Military Doctrine of the Russian Federation", the council discussed programmes for developing and producing perspective types of armaments and military hardware. The Security Council mapped out the key directions and principles on which this work is to be organised. The implementation of these programmes will allow Russia to keep the combat readiness of its Armed Forces at the necessary and sufficient level, to guarantee Russia's dependable security by rationally using the country's scientific, technological, defence, and industrial potential with the optimal expenditure of forces and resources. The president of the Russian Federation issued concrete instructions to implement the decisions adopted by the Security Council, the press service of the Russian president reported today.
