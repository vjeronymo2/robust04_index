Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] While peace is being negotiated in Lusaka, Zambia, Eduardo dos Santos' men continue to decimate innocent civilians in the Huila and Benguela Provinces. According to correspondent Silva Aleluia, terrible crimes are being committed by the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party [MPLA-PT], particularly in the Chongoroi and Caimbambo Districts. The People's Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FAPLA] have been carying out acts of murder, violating [words indistinct], and stealing people's goods. At least 223 people have been massacred in these areas since December last year. Silva Aleluia learned this from Colonel Catu, from the UNITA Armed Forces Command in the Southeastern Region.
