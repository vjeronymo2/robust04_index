940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / Collision closes straits to ships A fire boat battles to control the blaze on board an oil tanker after the Greek-Cypriot registered vessel collided with a coaster in the Bosporus. At least 15 seamen were killed and up to 16 are missing. The accident, which closed one of the world's busiest shipping lanes, has intensified Turkish concern over the number of tankers passing through the straits. Report, Page 2
