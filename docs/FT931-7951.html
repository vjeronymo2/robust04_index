930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Motorway work KIER CONSTRUCTION has acquired the rights to the M74 contract in Strathclyde following the receivership of LLC Realisations (formerly Lilley Construction). Kier, on behalf of Strathclyde Regional Council, will complete work on the Pounds 17m contract of which about Pounds 9.7m is outstanding. The project is funded by the Scottish Office.
