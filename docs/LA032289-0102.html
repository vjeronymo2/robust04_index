March 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Neighbor Buys Mining Firm: Louisiana Mining Corp. is being sold to Gordon Lee of Beverly Hills, the principal in two other companies involved in gold mining in Nevada, its board said. His companies own the claims adjoining a Louisiana Mining project. L. Cade Havard, chairman and chief executive of Louisiana Mining, agreed to sell his controlling block consisting of about 24% of the issued and outstanding shares of the company. The value of the transaction was not disclosed.
