930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Trio purchase beats forecast Martin Bierbaum, the international money broker, which was acquired for Pounds 25.5m in January by Trio Investment Trust, recorded a pre-tax profit ahead of forecasts at Pounds 7.04m in the year to September 30. Over the same period the trust, now re-named Trio Holdings, posted a Pounds 68,000 pre-tax loss compared to a Pounds 73,000 profit in the previous year.
