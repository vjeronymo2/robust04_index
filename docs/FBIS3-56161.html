Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 13 Jan -- An Iberia correspondent learned that the all-Georgia Merab Kostava Society has obtained a sensational document which proves that on 14 April 1992 -- that is three months before the war broke out in Abkhazia -- Russia's President Boris Yeltsin gave an order to the heads of Krasnodarskiy and Stavropolskiy kray administrations to speed up establishing direct economic links with Abkhazia, avoiding Georgia.
