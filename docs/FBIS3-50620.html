Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Defense Minister Hugo Estigarribia stated: "If abusing the right to veto means resorting to this institutional tool as many times as is deemed convenient, then I think the executive branch is going to abuse it, but only to the extent that Congress, too, abuses its powers." The secretary of state also noted that the military law is flagrantly unconstitutional, as is the Council of Magistrates Law and radio broadcasting law, and added that the veto is democracy's natural mechanism to submit a difference of criteria or legal interpretation to a third state power.
