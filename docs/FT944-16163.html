941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: NatWest Bancorp NatWest Bancorp, the US offshoot of National Westminster Bank, reported net income up 6.7 per cent at Dollars 76.3m (Pounds 48.2m) for the third quarter to September 30, compared with Dollars 71.5m. For the nine month period net income amounted to Dollars 215m (Dollars 202m). Provisions for loan losses were reduced to Dollars 20m for the quarter and Dollars 61.5m for the nine months, against Dollars 40.2m and Dollars 106.9m respectively.
