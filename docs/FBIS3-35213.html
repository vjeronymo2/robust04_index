Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih has announced the postponement of the meeting between President Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir and Kenyan President Daniel arap Moi, at the request of the Kenyan side. He told SUNA [as received] that an envoy of Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir would leave for Nairobi to look into the reasons for the postponement and to confirm Sudan's firm stance towards the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development [IGADD] initiative, as characterized by Sudan's unreserved backing for the initiative. The envoy would explain that Sudan regarded IGADD as a neighborly group, close to itself, aware of conditions in Sudan and which enjoys life [as heard] for the sake of a peaceful solution. He added that his visit had also been postponed at the request of the Kenyan Foreign Ministry. His excellency added that the Kenyan ambassador to Sudan had left Sudan for home yesterday after meetings with Sudanese officials in Sudan. He had taken with him the Sudanese Government's request that the IGADD states persevere in their efforts.
