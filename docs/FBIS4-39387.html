BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 6/6/1994 -- The 9th round of Russian-Moldovan negotiations on the status and terms of 14th Army's withdrawal will begin tomorrow in Moscow. BASA learned from the Defense Ministry Press Secretariat that Nicolae Turtureanu, head of the military experts' group of the Moldovan delegation, will not take part in the negotiations due to an illness. According to some sources from the Defense Ministry, a new head of the military experts' group has not been appointed yet. Moldovan delegation is headed by Nicolae Osmochescu, prime vice minister of foreign affairs. A Dniester delegation is taking part in the negotiations. A representative of the Moldovan Government stated to BASA that Tiraspol representatives by no means can be regarded as a third side in the negotiations. "According to an agreement between the Moldovan and Russian delegations, Tiraspol was allowed only to be present at the negotiations without a right to make statements," said the quoted source.
