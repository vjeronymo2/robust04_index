940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / Gas fields contracts awarded Amoco UK has awarded contracts worth more than Pounds 46m in the development of the Davy and Bessemer natural gas fields, which lie about 60 miles off the East Anglian coast. The contracts include Pounds 14m to Brown and Root Highland Fabricators for the construction of two single-leg platforms, the first time such designs have been used in the North Sea. Total development costs of the two fields are expected to be about Pounds 160m.
