Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cheju, South Korea, Jan. 14 (YONHAP) -- The island province of Cheju will exchange a memorandum of understanding on a meeting to establish friendly relations with the Chinese province of Hainan, officials said Friday. A delegation of nine officials and 13 businessmen from the Chinese province arrived in Cheju on Friday afternoon to discuss the establishment of sisterhood relations. Governor Sin Ku-pum and Vice Governor Wang Sufeng, head of the delegation, discussed ways to form sisterhood relations between the provincial governments on Friday afternoon. Wang plans to visit the provincial government office on Saturday to meet with Vice Governor Yi Man-ni to sign and exchange the memorandum. The memorandum says that the two provincial governments agree that maintaining friendly and cooperative relations between the governments and populations and promoting mutual relations based on the principle of equality and reciprocity will be profitable for both governments. The two sides decided to form sisterhood relations at an early date after recognizing the necessity of such relations.
