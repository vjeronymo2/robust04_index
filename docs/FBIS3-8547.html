Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 9 (CTK) -- A Czech Army mechanized company will soon be transferred from Croatia to Bosnia-Herzegovina to reinforce UNPROFOR [United Nations Protection Force] troops in a trouble spot north of Sarajevo, Premier Vaclav Klaus announced today. The Czech government approved the move based on a proposal by Defense Minister Antonin Baudys, who was responding to a request by UNPROFOR leaders.
