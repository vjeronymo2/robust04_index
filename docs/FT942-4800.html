940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / People: British Venture Capital Association Brian Larcombe, finance director of 3i, has been elected chairman of the British Venture Capital Association, replacing Ron Hollidge, managing director of Lloyds Development Capital. The position rotates annually. The 40-year-old Larcombe joined 3i in Manchester in 1974 and spent 18 years on the investment side of the business before being appointed finance director in 1992.
