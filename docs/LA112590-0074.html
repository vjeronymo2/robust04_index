November 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ALLA BREVE

DUETS WITH SPANISH GUITAR. Laurindo Almeida, guitar; Salli Terri, mezzo; Martin Ruderman, flute.

EMI CDM-7 63256 2 (compact disc). This elegant, re-released collection of 20 miniatures mixes predictable selections from Ibert, Villa-Lobos, Ravel and Falla with lesser-known gems, particularly from the Brazilian composers Jayme Ovalle, Valdemar Henrique, Ernani Braga and Francisco Mignone. Easy-listening in the best sense, but the remastered sonics sound even more glossily reverberant than on the original vinyl. JOHN HENKEN
