BFN [Text] An amphibious maneuver codenamed Nasr-2 began yesterday in the northern waters of the Persian Gulf with the participation of various units of Imam Hoseyn Third Naval Region. The aims of the maneuver are maintaining combat readiness, gaining further experiences, assessing the capability of the participating forces in carrying out amphibious operations, as well as maintaining a continuous and widespread presence in the border waters of the Islamic homeland. The Nasr-2 maneuver will continue for four days.
