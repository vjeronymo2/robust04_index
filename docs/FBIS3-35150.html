Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Esteemed Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, has sent a message of gratitude to Riyadh in response to a message of congratulation from King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques and King of Saudi Arabia. Text is as follows: Esteemed brother Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, peace and the blessings of God on you: With great pleasure I received your excellency's message of congratulation on the arrival of the blessed month of Ramadan. Thereby on behalf of myself, the Islamic State and the Muslim people of Afghanistan I express congratulations to you dear brother and to the state and Muslim people of Saudi Arabia. I am fully confident that the blessed month of Ramadan will be days of happiness for the Muslim fraternal people of Saudi Arabia and for all Muslim people of the world. Taking the opportunity once again I wish your excellency and the people of Saudi Arabia health, prosperity, and tranquility.
