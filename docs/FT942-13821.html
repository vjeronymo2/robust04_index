940422 FT 22 APR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Base metals under pressure - Market Report Base metals ended lower as market indicators continued to point downwards. COPPER broke through the bottom of its recent Dollars 1,880-Dollars 1,900 a tonne range following commission house selling and long liquidation. But solid buying and short-covering aided a recovery. Commission house and trade selling kept ALUMINIUM on the defensive. Compiled from Reuter
