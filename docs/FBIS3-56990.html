Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Liberal Democratic Party, Agrarian Party and Communist Party groups in the lower house of the Russian parliament made public Friday [21 January] their draft statement on the situation in former Yugoslavia. The State Duma would denounce the use of any kind of force, in particular imposition of no-fly zones, against former Yugoslav republics. The draft also calls for withdrawal of all foreign military contingents from former Yugoslavia and for appropriate steps to be taken by the Russian authorities. At their Wednesday meeting the Duma speaker Ivan Rybkin and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev had agreed that the ministry [as received] or his deputy Vitaliy Churkin would attend the discussion on former Yugoslavia during the regular Friday "government hour."
