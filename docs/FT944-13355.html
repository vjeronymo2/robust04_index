941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: American Cyanamid reports Dollars 25.3m charge American Cyanamid, the US healthcare group which is being taken over by American Home Products, recorded a Dollars 25.3m after-tax charge for the merger, writes Richard Waters. It also said it would pay a further Dollars 300m before tax to settle employee stock options and what it called 'contingent investment advisory fees'. The first part of the merger costs reduced operating income to Dollars 22.1m in the latest quarter, compared with Dollars 49.6m a year ago, while sales rose 15 per cent to just over Dollars 1bn.
