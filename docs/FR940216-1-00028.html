It is prohibited to operate any vehicle off Forest development, State or County roads: (a) Without a valid operator's license or permit in possession if required by State law. Add a new §261.22 to read as follows: §261.22 Buildings used in furtherance of the administration of the National Forest System or Forest Service Programs. The following are prohibited in buildings owned or leased by the Forest Service: (a) Engaging in conduct which impedes or disrupts the performance of official duty or the safety of Government employees. (b) Engaging in conduct which prevents the general public from obtaining the services provided by the Government or its agents or contractors on the property. (c) Failing to submit packages, briefcases, or other containers for inspection, when required, prior to entrance. (d) Carrying, possessing, depositing, or placing firearms, other dangerous or deadly weapons, explosives, or items intended to be used to fabricate an explosive or incendiary device, unless authorized by special use authorization, contract, approved plan of operations, or Federal law or regulation.
