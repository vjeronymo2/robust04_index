September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: GLENDORA;

WATER-SAVING KITS OFFERED

To help residents conserve water, the city will distribute 5,000 kits containing devices that restrict water flow in faucets, toilets and shower heads.

The kits are available for free at City Hall, 116 E. Foothill Blvd.

Since May, when the City Council approved a resolution asking residents to reduce water usage by 10%, there has been a reduction of more than 7%.
