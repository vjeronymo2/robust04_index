Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 2 (XINHUA) -- China's frontier police handled 827 smuggling cases in the coastal regions and border areas in the past year. These included 201 cases involving more than 1 millon yuan in value each. According to sources at the Ministry of Public Security, the total value of all the smuggled goods reached .5 [as received] billion yuan. The smuggled goods included automobiles, electronic appliances and cigarettes. The smuggling ativities were most widespread in the coastal regions. The public security department took effective measures to enhance control of vessels at sea. Last year 706 vessels engaged in smuggling were seized by the public security organizations in coastal regions and 273 vessels which had no numbers, no names and no registration documents were caught along the coastlines of Guandong, Fujian and Zhejiang Provinces.
