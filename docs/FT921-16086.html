920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / UN investigator to vist Iraq A UN investigator is visiting Iraq to press President Saddam Hussein's government on accusations of executions, mass arrests and disappearances. 'There are quite a number of allegations of violations of human rights .. we will be trying to clarify these matters,' Mr Max van der Stoel, appointed by the Human Rights Commission as special rapporteur for Iraq, said yesterday. A report, to be presented to UN secretary general Boutros Boutros Ghali in February, is expected to coincide with a debate on extending Security Council sanctions against Baghdad.
