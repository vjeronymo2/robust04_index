921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: First Ireland net asset value falls Net revenue of the First Ireland Investment Company advanced from Pounds 240,000 to Pounds 333,000 for the six months to September 30, for earnings per share of 1.11p against 0.8p. The net asset value per share of the trust fell by 11.4 per cent, although in sterling terms the decline was just 0.3 per cent, reflecting the appreciation of the Irish pound against sterling.
