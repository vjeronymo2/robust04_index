January 8, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

'AUNT BEE' LEAVES ESTATE TO HOSPITAL, PUBLIC TELEVISION

The woman who portrayed the kindly Aunt Bee on television's "The Andy Griffith Show" left her house to the town hospital and its contents to a center for public television, an attorney said.

"Anybody who reads the will can see that she remembered those who remembered her at the last moments," attorney Sam Williams said about how Frances Bavier divided her $700,000 estate.

The huge brick-and-stone house, where Bavier died in her sleep at 86 on Dec. 6, was bequeathed to Moore Memorial Hospital in Pinehurst, where the actress was treated several times.

The contents of the house were donated to the University of North Carolina Center for Public Television, he said.
