Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (January 3) TURAN--As TURAN agency was told by the military sources, fightings in the north of Karabakh continue. Armenian Army is supplied with military hardware and artillery. Last night Armenian Army tried to take back top-hills and settlements of the region, which they have lost the day before. At the same time, Armenian units tried to search the region round Omar crossing (the border between Kelbadzharskiy and Khanlarskiy regions of Azerbaijan), however, they have retreated having ten dead and wounded soldiers.
