920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Clinton to deny allegation Governor Bill Clinton of Arkansas, front-runner for the Democratic presidential nomination, will today try to fend off a second wave of accusations of marital infidelity which have enveloped his campaign, writes Lionel Barber. In a joint CNN television interview planned with his wife, Hillary, he intends to deny a report in a tabloid newspaper that he conducted a 12-year affair with a singer and former TV reporter in Little Rock, Arkansas.
