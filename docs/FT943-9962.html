940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Acatos agrees for supplier to subscribe Acatos & Hutcheson, whose shares climbed almost 12 per cent from 283p on Wednesday to 316p on Friday, said it had reached agreement with 'a major international supplier' to subscribe for about 22.5 per cent of A&H's share capital, as enlarged by the share subscription, based on Thursday's close of 288p. The edible oils and fats manufacturing group said it would make a further announcement 'as early as possible.'
