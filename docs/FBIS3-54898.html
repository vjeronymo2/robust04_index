Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Accords reached with Russia and Turkmenistan regarding oil and gas deliveries to Ukraine at prices considerably lower than they were in December 1993 make it certain that we will be able to make sure of stable work within industry. Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, acting prime minister of Ukraine, stated this in an interview with the press. He noted that the government is taking under its control the prices of oil products, and at what price they will be sold both to state and private firms.
