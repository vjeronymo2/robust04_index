Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 12 (SNARK) -- No success met Azeris this morning undertaking a current attack in the direction of the Novruzlu settlement at the eastern part of the Karabakh front. Having losses the attackers had to retreat, press-service of the Karabakh Defense Army told SNARK. No success met Azeris yesterday evening in the Agdam direction. Losses aren't being reported. Eastern two-day offensive Azeris undertake is connected with the considerable failure they met at the south-eastern parts of the front, according to the military experts' opinion.
