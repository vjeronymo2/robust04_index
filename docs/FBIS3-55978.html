Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 12 Jan -- From the territory of Armenia the settlements of Kazakhskiy, Kedabekskiy, and Tauzskiy Rayons, situated in the northwest of Azerbaijan are being subjected to intensive missile and artillery fire. Considerable damage has been caused, there are fatalities, the daily report of the Azeri Defense Ministry says. According to military department data, today's military operations by the sides did not on the whole change the situation on the front. Considerable losses in manpower and equipment by the Armenian side are also reported.
