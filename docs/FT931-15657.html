930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / Newcastle council to cut 480 jobs NEWCASTLE upon Tyne Council's controlling Labour group announced yesterday that the council will have to shed 400 full and part-time jobs as part of a Pounds 7.9m cuts package for 1993-94. The group expects to make cuts totalling Pounds 20m in the coming three financial years to keep within government spending targets, which have pegged its 1993-94 budget at Pounds 222.5m.
