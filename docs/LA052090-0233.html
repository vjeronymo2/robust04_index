ELDER CARE: CARING FOR CALIFORNIA'S AGING POPULATION;

RESOURCE GUIDE: CARING FOR THE ELDERLY;

CENTERS

Centers operated by the city and county of Los Angeles offer referrals, in-home services for the homebound, nutrition and transportation. Most are open 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Mondays through Fridays. Hours vary on weekends. Calling ahead is suggested.

Services are also available for elderly persons with legal problems.
