No official of this institution has stated that DEA agents engaged in the alleged kidnapping of Lujan Valladares in Mexican territory, thereby violating national sovereignty. 2. It has been learned that Modesto Lujan Valladares was arrested on 21 April in Marfa, Texas, near the town of Presidio, which borders Ojinaga, Chihuahua. Lujan Valladares was found in a vehicle with Victor Valle Lujan, Isidro Armendariz Morales, and Samuel Garcia Wilfar, ready to sell 125 pounds of marijuana to an undercover agent. 3. Nevertheless, the PGR has initiated an investigation into the affair, instructing its representatives in the United States to obtain detailed information on the case to totally clear up the facts for the public.
