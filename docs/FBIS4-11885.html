BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 3 Apr (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], received in Abu Dhabi today 'Ali Nasir Muhammad, former South Yemeni president, as part of the good offices the UAE president is extending to help end the Yemeni political crisis, safeguard Yemeni national unity, and maintain the security and stability of Yemen and its people. During the meeting, they reviewed the efforts being made to contain the Yemeni problems and achieve reconciliation among the Yemeni brothers.
