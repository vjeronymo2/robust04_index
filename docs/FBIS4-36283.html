BFN [Text] An official source in the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] affirmed that the Sanaa ruling regime and its head, 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, did not abide by the cease-fire as announced by it last night. The source indicated at 1130 last night information was picked up confirming that military reinforcements had come from Ta'izz to his forces and at 1216 minutes [as heard] a call was picked up while ordering the Sanaa regime's forces in their first axis to advance on Aden. The source affirmed that there is a recording of the voice of 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih who was ordering his defense minister to continue the war. The order was carried out, but our forces repelled them and dealt killing blows to them.
