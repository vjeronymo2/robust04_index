BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song Monday received again a visiting Chinese woman related to the anti-Japanese revolutionary struggle, Hu Zhenyi, widow of Chai Shirong, a Chinese anti-Japanese revolutionary martyr, and her family members. President Kim Il-song had a cordial conversation with the guests looking back with deep emotions on unforgettable days of the anti- Japanese armed struggle. He gave gifts to the guests and hosted a luncheon for them. Present were Kang Sok-song, director of the party history institute of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials concerned.
