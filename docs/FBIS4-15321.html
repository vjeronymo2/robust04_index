BFN [Text] A mission of UN military observers in Abkhazia was shelled on April 6 by artillery of Georgian units stationed in the upper reaches of the Kodor River, a spokesman for the Abkhazian Defense Ministry said Thursday. The mission was shelled when it visited the villages of Lata in the Kodori canyon. No casualties have been reported. Abkhazian Defense Minister Sultan Sosnaliyev described the incident as the flagrant violation by the Georgian side of the documents for a settlement in Abkhazia signed in Moscow recently. There are wounded among the servicemen of the Abkhazian army and the civilian population.
