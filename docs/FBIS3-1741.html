Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 1 (XINHUA) -- A two-day symposium on rural women in development ended here today. More than 100 specialists and scholars in sociology and women attended the symposium, which focused on the role of women in the socio-economic progress of underdeveloped areas and mutual aid among women in both economically developed and underdeveloped areas. It also centered around topics such as education, technological extension and medical care for rural women in underdeveloped areas and effective means to finance the development of rural women in underdeveloped areas. The symposium, the first of its kind held in China, was sponsored by the China features of the XINHUA NEWS AGENCY and the Rural Women's Development Research Center. Chen Muhua, president of the All-China Women's Federation, and officials from some offices of U.N. organizations in China also attended today's meeting.
