Language: English Article Type:BFN ["MINJU CHOSON on U.S. Radio `Free Asia' Project" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, February 3 (KCNA) -- MINJU CHOSON in a commentary today brands the U.S. scheme to start the broadcasting of radio "Free Asia" as a shameless interference in the internal affairs intended to instill "the view of the value of American-style democracy" into the DPRK and other socialist countries and independent countries in Asia and thus stifle socialism and undermine the independent regimes in this region. It fully reveals the wild ambition of the U.S. to expand its sphere of domination in the Asia-Pacific region, says the paper. The Korean people bitterly denounce the U.S. scheme to start the broadcasting and demand its immediate cancellation, the news analyst says, and goes on: The United States is making a gross mistake. It seems to have profited by its false propaganda offensive in disintegrating the socialist countries in East Europe and bringing them back to capitalism. But such propaganda cannot work on Korean-style socialism in which the leader, the party and the masses are united in one mind. The Korean people who are closely united behind the illustrious leader are not shaken by any war provocation manoeuvres, threat, blackmail and pressure nor by any yellow wind. The United States must stop its foolish acts to corrupt and degenerate the Korean people and other people of Asia by base false propaganda and act with discretion.
