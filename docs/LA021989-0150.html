February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COUNTY WORKERS WIN AWARDS FROM ENERGY ENGINEERS

Four staff members of the county Facilities Management Department have won awards in energy conservation from the Southern California chapter of the Assn. of Energy Engineers.

The winners are Kenneth Hammer, Norm Hermreck, Thomas Schriber and Frank Work.

Gregory Aden of Cushman & Wakefield won the Energy Engineer of the Year award and Ted Bischak of the Irvine Office Co. won the Energy Manager of the Year award.
