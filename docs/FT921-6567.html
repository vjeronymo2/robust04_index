920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Lord Donoughue Lord Donoughue wishes us to point out that he is not a former director of Bishopsgate Investment Management as stated in evidence given before a hearing of the House of Commons Select Committee on Social Security and reported in yesterday's Financial Times. In fact, Lord Donoughue is a former director of another Maxwell-owned company, London and Bishopsgate International Investment Management.
