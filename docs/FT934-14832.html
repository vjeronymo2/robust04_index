931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / International Company News: SAB subsidiary in Dollars 20m brewery deal SOUTH African Breweries said that its Dutch-based subsidiary Indol International had reached agreement in principle with the Tanzanian government to buy 50 per cent of its Tanzania Breweries, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. A spokesman for SAB said that the Dollars 20.5m deal hinged on the company satisfying certain regulations in South Africa.
