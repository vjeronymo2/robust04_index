930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Yorkshire Cable Yesterday it was reported that Generale des Eaux, Singapore Telecom and Yorkshire Water were to invest Pounds 200m in Yorkshire Cable. The companies wish to make clear that their total investment over the next 10 years will be Pounds 500m. In addition to Pounds 200m of equity, they plan to invest Pounds 300m raised through borrowing and cash flow.
