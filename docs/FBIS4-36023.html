BFN [Text] The Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Beirut has issued a statement in which it strongly condemns yesterday's aggression by the Zionist regime against Lebanon. The Central News Unit reports that the statement, which was published yesterday, describes the Zionist regime's attacks as a sign of its terrorist nature and calls on the world's Muslim and freedom-seeking nations to support the Lebanese nation vis-a-vis the Zionist regime's aggressions.
