August 31, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The U.S. women's volleyball team advanced to the semifinals of the World Championships at Beijing with a 15-3, 15-5, 11-15, 15-13 victory over Japan.

The U.S. plays China in one semifinal today and Cuba will face the Soviet Union in the other.

The United States would qualify for the 1992 Olympics by winning the title or placing second behind the Soviet Union.
