June 3, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ON THE FACE OF IT, FAN HAS A SOUND ARGUMENT

As a longtime Dodger fan, I've seen some changes in the game that have been hard to swallow -- AstroTurf, the DH, Steve Sax in pinstripes. . . . And now, the Padres printed in boldface in The Los Angeles Times baseball standings.

What a sellout.

ANTHONY MORETTI

Lomita

Editor's note: Not anymore. The use of boldface type for team names in the standings has been discontinued.
