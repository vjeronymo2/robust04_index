October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

RAIDERS;

BELL NOT TALKING MUCH ABOUT BILLS

Tailback Greg Bell, a former Bill, declined to give his old team any bulletin board material for the Raiders' Sunday night game in Buffalo. While playing for the Rams last season, Bell criticized the Bills, who then defeated the Rams, 23-20, in a Monday night game.

But the normally outspoken Bell is keeping quiet this week. "It's just another trip," he said. "It's just another away game. It's been too many years now to worry about it. It's a completely different team with a whole new attitude. I'm still friends with a lot of those guys back there and I don't have any feelings. It's just going to be a hard-fought game."

Injury report: Raider defensive tackle Mike Wise, who sustained a sprained ankle in Sunday's 24-10 victory over the Chicago Bears, is questionable because of a sprained ankle, as is safety Mike Harden, who sat out the Bear game because of a neck injury. CHRIS BAKER
