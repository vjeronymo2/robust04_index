BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow June 7 TASS -- A Russian state delegation headed by Ambassador at Large Yuriy Dubinin will fly to Kiev on Wednesday to continue the negotiations on the Black Sea Fleet. The delegation includes Supreme Commander of the Russian Navy Admiral Feliks Gromov, Russian Deputy Minister for Cooperation with Member-Countries of the CIS, German Kuznetsov, and other officials. Grigoriy Karasin, spokesman for the Russian Foreign Ministry told this a briefing on Tuesday. "The main purpose of the Russian delegation is to try to ensure that questions standing on the road to working out an agreement on the initial stage of the settlement of the problem of the Black Sea Fleet are resolved at the meeting in Kiev on a basis of principles and directives worked out and coordinated at the summit level", the spokesman said.
