BFN [Text] Jeddah, 15 Jun (KUNA) -- It has been reported here that Abu Bakr al-'Attas, prime minister of the declared state in the southern part of Yemen, arrived today in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for talks with Saudi officials on the Yemeni crisis. Al-'Attas is accompanied on this visit by 'Abdallah al-Asnaj, his foreign affairs minister. Al-Asnaj who is visiting Saudi Arabia for the first time since his appointment in the southern government, which has been recently announced. Al-'Attas's visit is considered the second in the current month [item incomplete as received]
