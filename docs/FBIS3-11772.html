Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of the state security fighters have been promoted to the immediate upper rank at a ceremony held in Santiago de Cuba Province on the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the foundation of that organization, which is subordinate to the Interior Ministry. Reporter Carlos Sanabria Marrero will give us further details from that province: [Begin recording] [Sanabria] The activities carried out at the Interior Ministry building were summed up by Esteban Lazo Hernandez, Politburo member and first secretary of the Cuban Communist Party in that province. [Lazo] I want to wholeheartedly congratulate you for this 35th anniversary, and also for what you are doing, for the work you are doing for security, and for the awareness of working efficiently, and for the things that are still to be done, because I know that you will continue working with that same awareness to overcome problems and to be increasingly better every day. I hope to celebrate many anniversaries here with you along with the history of this glorious and everlasting Cuban revolution, placing symbollically on the hearts of each of you that medal which symbolizes the heroes of the difficult war, the heroes of the war that decides the fate of a nation. Fatherland or death! We will win! [end recording]
