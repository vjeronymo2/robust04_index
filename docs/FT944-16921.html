941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Sales rise helps Elys to Pounds 107,000 A modest improvement in sales and maintained cost control helped Elys (Wimbledon) lift profits before tax to Pounds 107,000 for the six months to July 30, against Pounds 74,000 last time. Turnover at the department store group amounted to Pounds 4.54m (Pounds 4.35m) reflecting growth in linens, soft furnishings and electricals. Earnings per share improved from 4.1p to 6p and the interim dividend is raised to 2p (1.5p).
