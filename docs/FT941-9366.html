940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Bristol Scotts BRISTOL SCOTTS is selling the freehold of a completed office building comprising 10,000 sq ft to the TSB Staff Pension Fund for Pounds 1.48m cash. The realised profit of Pounds 360,000 over book value, subject to disposal costs, will be included in the 1994 results. Bristol Scotts has also sold its Sheekeys restaurant business for an initial Pounds 35,000. A further payment of up to Pounds 10,000 may become due.
