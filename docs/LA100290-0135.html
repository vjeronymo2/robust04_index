October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FIRE DESTROYS HISTORIC HOTEL IN SANTA CRUZ

An old hotel that became the center of a preservation battle after it was closed after last year's earthquake has been gutted by fire.

Firefighters were investigating the cause of the blaze Sunday at the 95-year-old St. George Hotel.

Gas and electricity had been turned off since the hotel was boarded up after the Oct. 17 earthquake.
