BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 1 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Lao Federation of Trade Unions led by its Chairman Khampane Philavong arrived here Tuesday. It was met at the airport by Chu Song-il, chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea. The Central Committee of the GFTUK gave a banquet for the delegation in the evening.
