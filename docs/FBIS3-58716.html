Language: Dutch Article Type:BFN [Article signed BVP, PDJ: "Eddy Baldewijns New President Flemish Council;" as released by Brussels BELGA Database] [Text] Brussels, 3 Jan (BELGA) -- The Flemish Parliament on Thursday [13 January] appointed socialist Eddy Baldwijns from Limburg as its new president. He replaces fellow party member Louis Vanvelthoven, who resigned because of links to a case of fraudulent examinations in the assembly. In his first speech as president, Baldewijns pleaded that a code of ethics be established for all elected representatives. His opponent was Vlaams Blok Representative Marijke Dillen. Baldewijns received 121 of the 142 votes. Although the Vlaams Blok group has only 17 members, 19 members of parliament voted for Dillen.
