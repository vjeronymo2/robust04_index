930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Fresh US-Japan chip link-up Mitsubishi Electric of Japan and Digital Equipment, the second largest computer manufacturer in the US, are poised to agree to jointly develop and manufacture semiconductors, writes Michiyo Nakamoto in Tokyo. This move promises to accelerate the cross-border integration of the world semiconductor industry. The deal will focus initially on the Alpha chip microprocessor developed by DEC, which Mitsubishi will manufacture mainly in Japan. In future, the two companies could jointly develop and manufacture new generations of semiconductors.
