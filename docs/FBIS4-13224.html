BFN [Unattributed report: "Two Diplomats Admit Assassinating Iraqi Oppositionist"] [Excerpt] Beirut, Paris, London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- An authoritative Lebanese source has said that the two Iraqi attaches detained on charges of assassinating Iraqi oppositionist Shaykh Talib Suhayl al-Tamimi in Beirut yesterday admitted killing the person in question on orders from Iraqi authorities. They also stressed during their interrogation in a police center in Beirut that they received the order to kill Shaykh Talib during their meeting with the Iraqi charge d'affaires in Beirut. According to well-informed sources, the two Iraqi diplomats will stand trial in Beirut if Iraq agrees to the Lebanese request to lift their immunity in accordance with the Geneva convention signed in 1961.
