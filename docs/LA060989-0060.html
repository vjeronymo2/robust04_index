June 9, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN MARCOS

A Valley Center man was killed Wednesday when he lost control of the truck he was driving and was thrown from the vehicle when it overturned, Deputy Coroner Max Murphy said Thursday.

Terry Lynn Leopard, 36, of Vesper Road in Valley Center, was believed to have been speeding on Elfin Forest Road near Questhaven Road in San Marcos when he lost control and overturned about 6:30 p.m., Murphy said.
