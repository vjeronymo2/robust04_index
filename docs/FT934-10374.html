931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: P&O enhanced scrip alternative is 565p Peninsular and Oriental Steam Navigation Company has announced that the reference price in respect of the enhanced scrip dividend alternative is 565p. The directors pointed out that this would result in the allotment and issue of some Pounds 33.2m nominal of new deferred stock, representing an increase of 5.9 per cent in the company's existing issued deferred stock.
