BFN ["Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers Decree No. 427 on Creating the Secretariat of Ukraine's Prime Minister" issued on 16 June; place not given] [Text] Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers decrees as follows: 1. To create within the apparatus of Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers the Secretariat of Ukraine's Prime Minister, having abolished Ukraine's Prime Minister's Office. 2. To appoint Kasyan, Vasyl Vasylyovych as head of the Secretariat of Ukraine's Prime Minister and confer upon him category three of state official. [Signed] Ukraine's Prime Minister V. Masol [Signed] Minister of Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers I. Dotsenko
