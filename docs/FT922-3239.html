920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / UK Company News: ICD progresses to Pounds 1.13m International Communications & Data reported continuing progress in its core activity in the year to February 29, which helped raise pre-tax profits by 37 per cent from Pounds 830,081 to Pounds 1.13m. ICD's main business provides information on British consumers, principally to the marketing industry. It has extended its services into the provision of mainframe computing via the acquisition of Lindor. And following the separate purchase of Compass a company has been created to specialise in information technology. Turnover rose 16 per cent to Pounds 9.59m (Pounds 8.23m). Earnings on capital enlarged by acquisitions came to 2.12p (2.28p). Directors said that provided the company continued to perform well they intended to begin to pay dividends in 1992-93.
