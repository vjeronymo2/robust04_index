BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 1 (KPL) -- A delegation of the Lao National Assembly Commission for Ethnics led by Mr. Vongphet Saikeuyachongtoua, vice president of the National Assembly and head of the comnission left here yesterday for the Socialist Republic of Vietnam on an official visit at the invitation of Mr. Yngong Nie Kdam, president of the Commission of Ethnics of the National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Mr. Khambou Sounisai, vice president of the National Assembly, Mr. Bui Van Thanh, Vietnamese ambassador to Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] and other high-ranking officials.
