BFN [Text] Tunis, 16 May (MENA) -- 'Abbas Zaki, Fatah Central Committee member, stated that the committee met with PLO Fatah leader and PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat last night. The committee discussed completing the formation of the Palestinian National Authority Council and building the authority's institutions in Gaza and Jericho. In a statement to MENA in Tunis, Zaki said the committee also discussed Fatah's future strategy, because Fatah's role has not yet ended and because it is responsible for transforming the new Palestinian entity in Gaza and Jericho into a Palestinian state. In his statement, the Palestinian official said that the committee assessed Fatah's future political and practical policies and the transfer of 1,200 PLO cadres from Tunis to Gaza and Jericho during the next stage. Reacting to reports about his appointment as the Palestine ambassador to Oman, Zaki said that as a member of the Fatah Movement he is committed to the instructions of the Fatah leadership, whether inside or outside the territories.
