Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Colonel Aleksandr Oliynik: "Five Years After the War"] [Text] A ceremonial gathering to mark the fifth anniversary of the Soviet troop withdrawal from Afghanistan was held 14 February in the Russian Army Central House. The gathering was addressed by Army General Pavel Grachev, Russian defense minister, and internationalist soldiers. The day before, Hero of the Soviet Union Colonel General Boris Gromov, deputy defense minister, who commanded the 40th Army five years ago, held a press conference at the Defense Ministry.
