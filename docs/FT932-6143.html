930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG's gold share rally was trimmed back as bullion failed to hold above Dollars 380. Off the day's highs, the gold index closed up 13 at 1,847. Industrials gained 14 to 4,535 and the overall 8 to 3,992. Anglos gained R2 to R139 ahead of results next week.
