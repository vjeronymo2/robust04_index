921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / GMB worker gets compensation A LIVERPOOL Labour councillor sacked by the GMB general union after voting in favour of redundancies among GMB-member council workers was awarded Pounds 7,850 yesterday by an industrial tribunal, which also ordered her reinstatement. Earlier this year, an industrial tribunal ruled that Mrs Frances Kidd, a book-keeper, had been the victim of unfair dismissal by the union. Yesterday's award was compensation for lost earnings.
