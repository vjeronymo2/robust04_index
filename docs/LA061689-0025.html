June 16, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

A THRILLING MOMENT WINS AT HOLLYPARK

A Thrilling Moment took the lead around the far turn and drew off to a 2 3/4-length victory over favored Paper Princess in the $31,000 allowance feature for 3-year-old fillies Thursday at Hollywood Park.

Ridden by Alex Solis, A Thrilling Moment covered the 1 1/16 miles on the turf in 1:41 3/5 for her third consecutive victory and her fourth in five career starts.

A Thrilling Moment returned $14.40, $5 and $3.20.
