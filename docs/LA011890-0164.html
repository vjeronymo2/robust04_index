January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: POMONA;

TOP DEPUTIES AT COURTHOUSE

New head deputies have moved into the top jobs in the district attorney's office and the public defender's office at the Pomona courthouse.

Gerald G. Haney, formerly head deputy district attorney at the Norwalk courthouse, now heads the district attorney's office in Pomona. He replaces Gail H. Huttenbrauck, acting head deputy since April, who has been transferred to the Compton courthouse.

Ronald Atsushi Yorizane, a public defender at the Los Angeles courthouse, has been named head deputy public defender in Pomona. He replaces Jon C. Chambers, head deputy for just a month, who has been transferred to night court in Los Angeles.
