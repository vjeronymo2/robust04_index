Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Turkish Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin today met with Chinese Assistant Foreign Minister Wang Changyi, who arrived in Ankara to prepare for the upcoming visit of President Suleyman Demirel to China. "There is no problem between Turkey and China," said Turkish Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. He expressed that Turkey has no intention to establish political relationship with Taiwan, which has set up an economic and culture office in Ankara. Wang Changyi and his Turkish counterpart Bilgin Unan later held talks "on all aspects of bilateral ties with special emphasis on improvement of economic relation," according to sources who attended the meeting. Both countries are eager to increase trade links with each other. An agreement on avoidance of double-taxation is expected to be signed during President Demirel's visit to China.
