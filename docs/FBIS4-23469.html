BFN [Text] In Bengo Province UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] soldiers tried to seize a number of government positions, paying dearly for their adventure. In three attacks and in one clash with the FAA [Angolan Armed Forces] between 13 and 30 May, UNITA abandoned 71 killed on the ground, and four of its men were captured. The FAA captured 60 AKM's, four PKM's, two 60-mm mortars, one RPG-7, one antitank missile, 82,000 rounds of ammunition of various caliber, and other military equipment. Seven UNITA soldiers turned themselves over to government forces during the same period. They revealed that right now UNITA intends to recapture the locations of Ucua and Gombe and to destroy the bridge over the [name indistinct] river. The FAA has already taken measures to deal with the situation.
