920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Spania strawberries are this weeks best buy reports the FFVIB, prices range from 55-85p a 1/2 lb. Other good buys include Cape black seeded grapes 75-85p a lb, Jaffa oranges 12-30p each, bananas 48-60p a lb, Cape Golden Delicious apples 65-75p, Kiwifruit 12-20p each, and Jaffa and Cyprus white fleshed grapefruit at 20-25 each. New season English cauliflowers are this week's best vegetable buy at 55-60p a head. Egyptian potatoes are 20-35p a lb, and English carrots at 15-20p a lb. Large Spanish Iceberg lettuce are this weeks best salad buy at 55-70p each, other good buys include English radishes at 30-45p a bunch.
