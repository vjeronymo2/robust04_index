Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cameroon Foreign Minister Ferdinand Oyono, currently in Addis Ababa for the OAU talks, has said that war between Cameroon and Nigeria can break out at any time over the Bakassi peninsula. He said he was not satisfied with the OAU recommendations which, despite urging troop withdrawal from the peninsula, have refrained from condemning the Abuja regime as Yaounde expected. According to Oyono, the Cameroon and Nigerian armies are now facing each other and are only 300 meters apart.
