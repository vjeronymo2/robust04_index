August 8, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

CZECHOSLOVAK CHEMICAL BLAST KILLS 1, HURTS 12

An explosion and fire at a chemical plant killed one person and seriously burned 12 others, a newspaper reported Tuesday.

The explosion and fire Monday at the plant in Zilina, about 100 miles northeast of Bratislava, caused no significant leakage of toxic gas, the daily Prace reported.
