930104 FT 04 JAN 93 / Letter: Russia in the pink Sir, I was recently on a flight to Barbados reading an FT purchased in San Juan when I was accosted by a charming lady speaking a foreign language. Luckily she switched to English. 'You read Russian?' 'No.' 'But your paper is Russian.' 'No.' I think you have a new subscriber to Financial Izvestia] J G Bennett, senior Caribbean director, Barclays Bank, Caribbean Head Office, Rendezvous, Christ Church, Barbados, West Indies
