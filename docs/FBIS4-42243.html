BFN [Text] In comments for our station, Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas said that the blue helmets' humanitarian aid for the Bosnian conflict cannot go on forever if the parties in conflict do not reach a peace agreement. Julian Garcia Vargas. [Begin Garcia Vargas recording] We have to establish a principle: We will set a reasonable period of presence and if this period does not lead to anything, if the negotiations do not make any progress, we will have to tell the parties that we do not want to stay there forever. In two months, [corrects himself] in five months, we will have been there for two years, and I think that it is time to speak to our European partners and to discuss with them the fact that this cannot go on forever. [end recording]
