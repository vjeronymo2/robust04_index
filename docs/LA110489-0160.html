November 4, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Trident Investment Management: Philip Bourdillon has been named president and chief executive of Trident Investment Management, a subsidiary of Anaheim-based Pacific Inland Bancorp. Previously he was vice president and a shareholder of Clifford Associates, a Pasadena investment banking firm, and before that directed portfolio consulting operations for the Los Angeles office of Kidder Peabody.
