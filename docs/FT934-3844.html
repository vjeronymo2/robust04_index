931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: de Morgan back in the black Helped by a 22 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 1.21m, de Morgan Group, the commercial property and financial advisory concern, moved from losses of Pounds 131,000 to profits of Pounds 24,000 pre-tax for the half year to October 31. Earnings per share emerged at 0.05p (losses 0.27p). Directors said operating costs and cash resources remained under firm control.
