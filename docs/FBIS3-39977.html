Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Kurdish conference being organized in Moscow is causing a crisis in the ties between Turkey and Russia. Turkey is not satisfied with Russia's explanations on the issue and is asking for additional clarification. Russian Ambassador to Turkey Albert Chernyshev indicated that he has little information on the conference in which organizations affiliated with the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] are participating and added that Russia has not changed its views on Turkey's unity and territorial integrity. In a statement to a Russian paper, President Suleyman Demirel said Russia has shown sensitivity toward Turkey's difficulties as regards terrorism. But, Demirel added, our people are extremely sensitive. He stated the need for additional explanations so that the people don't get the impression that Russia is supporting terrorism.
