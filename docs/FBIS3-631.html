Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A helicopter belonging to UNAMIR's Belgian contingent crashed into a high-voltage cable before crashing to the ground at about 1040 today in [words indistinct]. According to first reports from UNAMIR, the two persons aboard the helicopter suffered superficial cuts. Meanwhile, Belgian Defense Minister Leo Delcroix was expected in Kigali early this evening. Indeed, Minister Delcroix has just landed with a delegation of over 10 parliamentarians and about 20 journalists. He will hold talks tomorrow morning with the UN secretary general's special representative to Rwanda, and at 1100 Mr. Booh-Booh will give a news conference at UNAMIR headquarters for the Belgian journalists and the national press.
