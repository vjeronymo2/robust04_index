April 1, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

MAN KILLED WHEN CAR SLAMS INTO TREE

A motorist was killed early Saturday when his speeding car failed to negotiate a bend in Jesmond Dene Road north of Escondido and slammed broadside into an oak tree, a California Highway Patrol spokeswoman said.

Dead at the scene of the accident was Terry L. Johnson, 38, of Escondido. Johnson was alone in his l983 Mercedes-Benz driving south on Jesmond Dene Road at a high speed when his car failed to ride out the turn, the spokeswoman said.

The car crossed the northbound lanes, struck the tree and overturned.
