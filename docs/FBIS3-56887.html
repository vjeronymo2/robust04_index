Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 18 TASS -- Tests of an automatic cargo spaceship "Progress M-17" continue. On August 11, 1993, the cargo spaceship was disconnected from the orbital complex "Mir" and since then it had been performing an independent space flight. The parameters of the cargo spaceship, reported by the Space mission Control Centre, are as follows: -- the maximum distance from the earth - 333 kilometers; -- the minimum distance from the earth - 231 kilometers; -- the period of revolution - 90 minutes; -- inclination - 51.6 degrees All systems on board are operating normally. Information transmitted to the earth is being processed and analysed. According to the ballistic provision department of the Space Mission Control Centre, the cargo spaceship is expected to enter thick layers of the atmosphere at the end of February - beginning of March, 1994.
