BFN [Text] National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih today met with Muhammad Gulcegun, member of the Turkish parliament and deputy chairman of the Turkish Social Democratic Populist Party [title as heard], and his accompanying delegation. The National Assembly speaker reviewed the impact of the unjust blockade against the Iraqi people and their suffering as a result of the severe shortage of food and medicine. He said Iraq has fulfilled all its obligations toward the Security Council resolutions and that the continuation of the blockade blatantly violates international and humanitarian laws and norms. Sa'di Mahdi Salih underscored the importance of developing Iraqi-Turkish relations. He said: We should provide the conditions that enhance cooperation between our two friendly countries in all fields in the interest of our two peoples. Gulcegun expressed the Turkish party's solidarity with the Iraqi people in their fight against the blockade.
