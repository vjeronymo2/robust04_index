July 5, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

INDY WINNER LUYENDYK ENTERS TORONTO MOLSON

Arie Luyendyk, winner of this year's Indianapolis 500, will race in the Molson Indy July 20-22 at Exhibition Place.

Luyendyk, whose best finish at Toronto was seventh in 1987, currently sits sixth in the CART-PPG Indy Car World Series going into the seventh round at Cleveland this weekend. The Holland native has 48 points.
