February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

George DeHuff and Roland Marcello have been named division presidents for EECO Inc., as part of the company's plan to establish its product divisions as subsidiary companies.

DeHuff will head the switch division, and Marcello becomes president of the maxi-switch computer keyboard division. Both had previously been general managers. Santa Ana-based EECO manufactures computer keyboards, electronic keypads and hotel computer systems.
