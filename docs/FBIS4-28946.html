BFN ["NODONG SINMUN Calls For Reviving Fighting Spirit of June Resistants" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN in a commentary today says the desire of the fighters of the anti-U.S., anti-fascist resistance in June 1987 for independence, democracy and reunification has yet not been realized in South Korea though seven years have passed since the resistance of the South Korean people. This is because of the U.S. policy of occupying South Korea and maintaining it as a colonial dependency, it adds. The analyst says: As long as South Korea remains a colony of the United States and fascist traitors stay in power, neither the desire of the people for a social change nor the reunification of the country can be achieved. This is the precious lesson gained by the June resistants and the South Korean people and the sum total of the struggle. The South Korean students and people of all segments should wage a more valiant anti-U.S., anti-war, anti-nuke struggle and build up fierce flames of massive resistance in all areas to overthrow the Kim Yong-sam puppet regime and establish an independent and democratic government. The South Korean people of all strata and Koreans overseas must wage a persistent nationwide struggle to reunify the country by the confederacy formula and, for the present, vigorously turn up in a joint struggle for the convocation of a national conference.
