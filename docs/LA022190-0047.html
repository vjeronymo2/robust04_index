February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Berlin Winners: "Music Box" by Greek director Costa-Gavras and "Skylarks on the String" by Czechoslovakia's Jiri Menzel shared the top Golden Bear prize at the 40th Berlin Film Festival on Tuesday. The unusual joint award reflected the festival's dedication to blooming East-West rapprochement. "Coming Out," an East German film about a young man's repressed homosexuality, won the festival's overall Silver Bear. BETH KLEID
