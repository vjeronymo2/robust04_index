October 11, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: NAMES

Actor Randy Quaid and businesswoman Evi Motolanez were married at a private evening ceremony at a San Ysidro Ranch near Santa Barbara, it was announced Tuesday. Family and close friends attending Thursday's wedding included the bridegroom's brother, actor Dennis Quaid, actress Meg Ryan and Amanda Quaid, Randy's 6-year-old daughter from a previous marriage. ALEENE MacMINN
