March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

JACKSON ASKS PROBE OF VIDEOTAPED ARREST

The Rev. Jesse Jackson said Wednesday he has asked Atty. Gen. Dick Thornburgh to look into the Long Beach incident in which a white police officer was secretly videotaped as he appeared to push the head of black activist Don Jackson through a plate-glass window during his arrest for an alleged traffic violation in January.

As a result of the phone conversation Tuesday with Jesse Jackson, Thornburgh has asked for a status report on the FBI investigation into the incident, a spokesman for the attorney general said.
