Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "News at Ten" program] [Text] Jordan and the United States today signed an agreement under which the United States will finance a Jordanian purchase of 100,000 tonnes of U.S. wheat at a cost of $15 million. The agreement was signed by Finance Minister Sami Qammuh and U.S. Charge d'Affaires Douglas Keene. Mr. Qammuh said the loan will carry 2 percent annual interest for the first seven years and 3 percent thereafter. He said the loan was repayable in 24 installments beginning in the seventh year. Mr. Qammuh said 60 percent of the value of the loan was a grant from the United States to shore up Jordan's budget. The U.S. charge d'affaires said the loan was one aspect of Jordanian-U.S. cooperation.
