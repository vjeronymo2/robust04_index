Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The military ruler of Venda, Brigadier Gabriel Ramushwana, has been appointed by the Transitional Executive Council [TEC] as commander of the national peace-keeping force. With his appointment he renounces the Venda leadership and ceases to be a candidate on the ANC [African National Congress] election list. Members of the peace force will be deployed within the next five weeks following the completion of their training near Bloemfontein.
