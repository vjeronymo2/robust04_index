920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / International Company News: S Korean groups hit by slowdown AN ECONOMIC slowdown and high financing costs took their toll on first-half profits posted by listed South Korean companies, Reuter reports from Seoul. A report on 487 listed companies said combined taxed profits totalled Won1,830bn (Dollars 2.3bn) compared with Won1,750bn in the first six months of 1991.
