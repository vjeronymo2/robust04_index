Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers adopted a resolution on the 1994 plan of Ukraine's social and economic development, the presidential cabinet press service reported. The plan specifies, in particular, the state contracts and orders amounts, including those of basic agricultural products and foods. It also specifies the amounts of centralized capital investments and new production facilities to be commissioned. The plans reportedly contains provisions for enhancing state discipline by subjects of economic activities involving, in particular, pricing discipline in fuel supplies to Ukraine under intergovernmental agreements with exporter nations. As the presidential cabinet press service disclosed, President Kravchuk provided a formal additional submission to the parliament of Ukraine's accedence to the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty.
