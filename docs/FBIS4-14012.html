BFN [Excerpts] Brother Abu-Mazin [Mahmud 'Abbas], member of the PLO Executive committee, stressed that the Palestinian leadership will in no way accept that Jerusalem is not considered an integral part of the future Palestinian land or state. Brother Abu-Mazin, who is currently on a visit to the United Arab Emirates, said that the implementation of the Israeli-Palestinian Declaration of Principles would take place next May. [passage omitted] In a statement to the United Arab Emirates News Agency, brother Abu-Mazin said that the next round of talks, expected to start in Cairo the day after tomorrow, would be more intensive than previous ones in order to speed up an agreement.
