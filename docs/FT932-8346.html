930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: 17% asset rise at Baring Stratton At March 31 1993 net asset value of Baring Stratton Investment Trust stood at 215p, a 17 per cent rise over the 183p of 12 months earlier. Revenue totalled Pounds 960,000 for the year, against Pounds 792,000. There was a special dividend of Pounds 120,000 from Tennants Consolidated, an unlisted investment. Net revenue was Pounds 427,000 (Pounds 288,000) for earnings per share of 3.39p (2.28p). The dividend is raised to 2.29p (1.7p).
