930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / Drugs body backs price scheme THE Association of British Pharmaceutical Industries has endorsed the new five-year Pharmaceutical Price Regulation Scheme, which includes a 2.5 per cent cut in prices for three years. Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary, said: 'The need to reduce public spending is widely recognised, and it is right that reducing the rapid growth in the NHS drugs bill should play a part.'
