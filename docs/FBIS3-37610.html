Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Kazan -- Speaking on republican TV, the Tatarstan president said that "having resolved the questions of political interrelations, we shall consistently promote decentralization of the Russian economy.... I have told this to Yeltsin and Chernomyrdin, and it was met with understanding." According to him, Tatarstan should play a leading role in pursuing this policy. In the near future a Tatarstan permanent mission will be opened in Washington, in the Russian Federation trade representation, to promote contacts between the republic's firms and enterprises and U.S. partners and to exchange information. It will enjoy diplomatic immunity.
