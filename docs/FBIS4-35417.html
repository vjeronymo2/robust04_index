BFN [Text] Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz left Amman this morning at the end of a four-day visit to Jordan during which he participated in the meetings of the 17th session of the UN Economic and Social Commission for Western Asia, ESCWA, which concluded its meetings in Amman yesterday. Buwayz met with Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali and discussed with him ways of fostering fraternal bilateral relations in various fields, especially the economic, commercial, and customs fields. They also reviewed the latest developments of the peace process. He was seen off at Queen Alia International Airport by Dr. Fawwz Abu-al-Ghanam, acting minister of state for foreign affairs, and the Lebanese ambassador in Amman.
