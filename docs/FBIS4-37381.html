BFN [Text] The brother leader of the revolution [Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi] met yesterday evening with Malian President Alpha Oumar Konare, who is currently visiting the Great Jamahiriyah. The meeting was attended by: the assistant secretary for women's affairs at the secretariat of the General People's Congress; Mrs. Sy Khadiatou Sow, secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation; the Malian minister of foreign affairs, Malians abroad, and African integration; the minister for women's affairs; and Moussa Coulibaly, political adviser to the presidency. During this meeting, the two sides discussed bilateral relations and ways to strengthen and develop relations in all fields, and also the latest developments in African and international issues of mutual interest.
