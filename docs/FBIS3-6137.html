Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC], at 1200 today met with officers of the People's Police Force [PPF] at the Ministry of Home Affairs located at the corner of Sethmu-1 Road and Saya San Road. He discussed PPF work and delivered an address. Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt explained that the major responsibility and objectives of the PPF are crime prevention to ensure law and order, local peace, and tranquillity; to expose and investigate crimes, arrests, and collect evidence against offenders; to take legal action; and to provide local security. He said a review of the current state of the PPF shows that the time has come to change and reform the law, methods of operation, and practices. Therefore, he said, the SLORC has formed the Committee To Reform the Administrative System of the PPF with ranking officials with a view to reforming the spirit, discipline, and methods of operation based on the administrative and training system of the entire PPF. The committee will review and scrutinize the current system of PPF administration, intelligence, legal action procedure, and the PPF training system. It will also attempt to promote public trust in the PPF, eradicate graft and corruption, prevent the deliberate use of power to create hardships for the people, and to eradicate undesirable practices inherited from the colonial era. He said the committee was empowered to lay down the necessary rules, regulations, and procedures to reform the administrative and training system of the PPF, amend the existing laws, and enact new laws. [passage omitted] In conclusion, he called on the members of the PPF to use their mental and physical powers to help reform the PPF administrative system. He called on them to get eliminate undesirable habits and practices left over from the colonial era and to strive to become a PPF well loved by the people. [passage omitted]
