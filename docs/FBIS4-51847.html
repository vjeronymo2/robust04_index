BFN [Text] A ceremony was held at Lan Xang Hotel on 3 May to sign an agreement on 1993 financial cooperation for implementation in 1994 between the Government of the Lao People's Democratic Republic and the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany. Mrs. Khampheng Phonsena, vice chairman of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation, represented the Lao Government at the signing ceremony, while the German side was represented by Wighard Hardte, state secretary and deputy minister for economic cooperation and development. Witnessing the signing ceremony were Souban Salitthilat, deputy foreign affairs minister; Claus Soenksen, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Germany to Laos; and a number of officials concerned from both sides. The agreement stipulates that the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany will grant financial aid of 23 (?million) deutsche marks to Laos for spending in the second stage of the rural telecommunications development project, including telephone links among provinces and major urban areas throughout the country, in addition to the first phase of the project which has already been completed. The German economic cooperation and development delegation concluded its three-day visit to Laos and left for home at noon today.
