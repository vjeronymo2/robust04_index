Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Over $2 Million Aid"] [Text] Turkey approved of an irretrievable aid for Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] of 10,000 tons of corn. This month, Turkey will also pay the promised $1 million on the account of the fund for settling the Macedonian debt toward the World Bank. The Macedonian Government delegation, during its yesterday visit to Turkey, reached an agreement for purchasing 30,000 tons of wheat and 20,000 tons of corn. They also agreed to speed up the procedure of getting a $1.5 million credit from the Eksim Bank.
