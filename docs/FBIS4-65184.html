BFN [Unattributed article: "CARIPLO, June 6 Capital Hike Before Market Launch"] [Text] Rome 6 May (ANSA) -- The Milan-based commercial bank CARIPLO [Lombard Provinces' Savings Bank] plans a capital hike from 3.5 trillion to 4.5 trillion lire to be voted at a shareholders meeting on June 6, the agenda published today indicated. The capital increase is planned in two tranches, the second of which to be a warrant bond issue. The assembly is expected to vote the stock market launch of CARIPLO shares at that meeting, according to the agenda outlined by the bank's new president Sandro Molinari.
