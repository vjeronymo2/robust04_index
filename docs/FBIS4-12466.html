BFN [Text] Yemeni Minister of Education Dr. Abu-Bakr al-Qirbi has arrived in Amman at the head of a delegation on an official visit to Jordan. During its three-day stay, the delegation will discuss with Jordanian officials cooperation in all areas. In a statement to the Jordan News Agency, the Yemeni minister said that he was carrying a message to Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali from the Yemeni prime minister. He said the letter related to fields of cooperation between the two countries, as well as latest political developments
