941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Craig & Rose Craig & Rose, the Edinburgh-based paint and varnish maker, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 135,000 to Pounds 115,000 for the six months to June 30 on lower turnover of Pounds 2.37m, against Pounds 2.64m. Mr John Wightman, chairman, said that in addition to the decline in sales which was in line with the industry generally, the directors had also decided to withdraw from certain specific markets which were unprofitable. Losses per stock unit worked through at 29.25p (32.25p).
