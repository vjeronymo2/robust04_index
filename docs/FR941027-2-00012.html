COMMISSION OF FINE ARTS Notice of Meeting The Commission of Fine Arts' next meeting is scheduled for 17 November 1994 at 10:00 a.m. in the Commission's offices in the Pension Building, Suite 312, Judiciary Square, 441 F Street, NW., Washington, DC 20001 to discuss various projects affecting the appearance of Washington, DC, including buildings, memorials, parks, etc.; also matters of design referred by other agencies of the government. Inquiries regarding the agenda and requests to submit written or oral statements should be addressed to Charles H. Atherton, Secretary, Commission of Fine Arts, at the above address or call 202&hyph;504&hyph;2200. Dated in Washington, DC, October 21, 1994. Charles H. Atherton, Secretary.
