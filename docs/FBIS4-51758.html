BFN [AFP/YONHAP from Vienna] [Text] The International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] announced today that it will not send its inspection team to observe the changing of nuclear fuel rods at North Korea's nuclear reactor in Yonbyon for the time being since North Korea has rejected conditions set to inspect its nuclear facilities. IAEA spokesman Hans Meyer disclosed that the IAEA sent a notice on 1 May that the IAEA will not be sending its inspection team due to North Korea's unsatisfactory responses.
