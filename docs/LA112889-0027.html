November 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

UCLA, PEPPERDINE TO MEET IN NCAA WOMEN'S VOLLEYBALL

Top-ranked UCLA will play host to unranked Pepperdine Friday in the opening round of the NCAA women's volleyball tournament.

A victory for the Bruins (27-2), would probably make them the host team for the West Regional Dec. 8-9. The site of the West Regional will be announced after the first round.

The 32-team tournament will begin Thursday with fourth-ranked Cal State Long Beach playing host to San Diego State.
