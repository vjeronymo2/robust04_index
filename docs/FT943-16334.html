940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Observer: Snakes 'n' ladders KPMG Career Consultancy Services is holding a career management day today, where some of its neat little gadgets will be on display. When the firm goes along to see prospective clients these days, it whips out its collection of brain teasers, or mind twisters as they are known in the US. The puzzles include a 'three-card trick'; 'clip-the-queen'; the 'stubborn pencil'; and a miniature treasure chest from which you must extract a playing card without breaking the seal. 'It's a marketing tool with a psychological edge, which is designed to show the creativity of the KPMG approach,' says a staffer. Given some of the twisted minds populating the marketing world, mind twisters seem an entirely appropriate way of getting a foot in the door.
