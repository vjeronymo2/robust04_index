BFN ["Anti-U.S. Struggle of South Korean Youth" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- South Korean youth and students waged anti-U.S. struggles in front of the "American Cultural Centres" in Taegu and Pusan, Seoul-based radio reports said. Young people in Taegu Tuesday scattered copies of literature denouncing the United States for screwing up the situation of the Korean peninsula with nuclear pressure on the North, shouting anti- American slogans and pouring paint on the building of the "American cultural centre" in the city and a police post. They accused the United States of driving South Korean agriculture into bankruptcy with pressure to open agricultural product market. On the 15th, students under the Pusan-South Kyongsang Provincial Federation of General Student Councils staged a surprise demonstration in front of the "American Cultural Centre" in Pusan. They occupied a road and condemned the United States for creating a war crisis, shouting "objection to the U.S. heightening tensions on the Korean peninsula" and other slogans. In a statement they charged that the U.S. harms the peaceful atmosphere of the Korean peninsula, clamouring for 'sanctions' against the North with the approach of the 3rd round of North Korea- U.S. talks.
