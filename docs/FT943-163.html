940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Garton slips midway Pre-tax profits at Garton Engineering, the components and special fasteners maker, slipped from Pounds 197,000 to Pounds 170,000 in the first half of 1994. The result included the gain on land surplus to requirements following the fire in December 1990. Turnover improved from Pounds 10.2m to Pounds 12.2m. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.25p (1.125p), payable from earnings of 2.99p (3.41p) per share.
