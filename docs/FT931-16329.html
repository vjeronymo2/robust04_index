930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Intl High earnings improve Net asset value per ordinary share of the Fleming International High Income Investment Trust rose from 33.7p to 39.8p over the 12 months to November 30. The figure for the zero dividend shares improved from 65.2p to 73.6p over the same period. Available revenue for the half year to end-November totalled Pounds 2.36m (Pounds 1.78m), equal to earnings of 2.11p (1.59p) per share. An unchanged 1p interim dividend is declared and a same-again 3.5675p total is forecast.
