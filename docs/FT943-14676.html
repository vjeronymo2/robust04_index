940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Dwyer makes Pounds 4.8m property sale Dwyer Estates, the Irish property investment and trading group, has sold Porterbrook House in Sheffield to Great Portland Estates for Pounds 4.8m. The 41,000 sq ft office building, which is let to the Department of the Environment for 25 years from 1989, has a rental income of Pounds 430,000 a year for an initial yield of about 8.7 per cent. The sale by Dwyer, which returned to the black in the six months to March 31 with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 378,000, follows two recent property acquisitions. It is continuing to seek further acquisitions.
