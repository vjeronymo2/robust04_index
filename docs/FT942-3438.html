940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / Libya 'absolved' over Lockerbie Libya said yesterday a claim by a follower of guerrilla leader Abu Nidal that he blew up a US airliner over Lockerbie in 1988 absolved it of responsibility, Reuter reports from Tunis. In Beirut, however, where Youssef Shabaan is on trial on other charges, prosecutors denied he had made such a confession.
