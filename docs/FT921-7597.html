920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa PRESIDENT FW de Klerk's announcement an hour before close of trade that he planned to hold a referendum on his reform programme shook Johannesburg. The overall index, which had been 30 points higher before the news, closed up 3 at 3,541, while the industrial index shed a 35-point gain to close 3 down at 4,325. The all-gold index gained 5 to 1,237.
