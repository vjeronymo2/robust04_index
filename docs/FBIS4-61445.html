BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol 5 May TASS--No mine has been found in the building of the Crimean local government. By 0200 on Thursday [5 May] afternoon, field engineers of the Black Sea Fleet's 126th Coastal Guard Division completed inspection of the building but failed to detect the reported explosive device. The inspection was prompted by a telephone call this morning. An unidentified person said a mine had been planted in the government building, after which a government session was broken off and everybody was evacuated from the building.
