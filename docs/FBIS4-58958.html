BFN [Text] Brother Muhammad Bin-Muhammad Shakir, the acting director general of the port of Aden, has stated that operations at the port have been uninterrupted and are proceeding normally. In a statement to the Yemeni news agency, SABA', he referred to reports put out by a number of news agencies, to the effect that the port had been closed. He said that the port's operations had been proceeding normally without interruption, noting that the port was open to international navigation and was providing full services to all ships reaching it from other ports. Brother Muhammad Bin-Muhammad Shakir went on to say that a number of ships carrying various types of cargo were outside the port, and were awaiting their turn to enter the port when other ships had completed discharging their cargoes.
