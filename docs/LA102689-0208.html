October 26, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

MORNING REPORT: BENEFACTORS

The Red Cross San Francisco Earthquake Relief fund will benefit from a special performance of "Durante," the musical based on the life of Jimmy Durante, Sunday at 7 p.m. the Shubert Theatre in Century City. Tickets are $25. "Durante" was playing in San Francisco when the quake struck last week. The show's regular run opens Tuesday and plays through Nov. 19. ALEENE MacMINN
