930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Banesto arm launches Pta12bn bid LA CORPORACION Banesto, the Spanish industrial holding company, is bidding for the 64 per cent of Spanish property group Inmobiliaria Urbis it does not already own, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. La Corporacion, owned by Banco Espanol de Credito, the Spanish bank, is offering Pta600 a share, plus Pta50 per subscription right to a capital increase, valuing the stake at more than Pta12bn.
