BFN [Text] Israeli military aircraft raided Tallat Mlita in Iqlim al-Tuffah twice at 1420 today. The Lebanese Army antiaircraft guns confronted the Israeli planes. After carrying out this act of aggression, the Israeli military aircraft continued to fly over the region. The Israeli positions in Sujud, al-Rayhan, and Tallat al-Ghuzlan exchanged artillery duels with the resistance positions facing them. A number of shells landed in al-'Ayshiyah.
