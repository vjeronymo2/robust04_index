940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Eidos launches rights issue Eidos, the USM-quoted dev-eloper of video compression and decompression software, is launching a rights issue to raise Pounds 582,000 before costs. The issue is of 242,500 new shares on a 1-for-10 basis at 240p apiece. It is fully underwritten by Charles Street International, a Geneva-based investment company. Funds raised will be used to strengthen the balance sheet and accelerate R&D and marketing plans.
