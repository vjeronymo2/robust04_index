920627 FT 27 JUN 92 / International Company News: Hellenic Bottling lifts earnings 45% HELLENIC Bottling Company, the Cypriot-controlled soft drinks group which holds the Coca-Cola franchises for Greece, Northern Ireland and Bulgaria, lifted 1991 pre-tax profits by 45 per cent to Dr10.3bn (Dollars 54.2m), writes Kerin Hope from Athens.
