940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / Competitiveness - The White Paper: Digital broadcasting delayed The government yesterday had to back down on its pledge of 'moves towards early introduction of digital broadcasting'. Mr Peter Brooke, national heritage secretary, said studies were continuing on how to launch an analogue Channel 5 without prejudicing development of digital terrestrial television. In a clear hint that Channel 5 is increasingly likely to get the go-ahead, Mr Brooke said the sentence in the white paper had been overtaken by his answer.
