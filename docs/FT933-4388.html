930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / Ex-Peugeot Talbot executive charged A FORMER executive at Peugeot Talbot, the subsidiary of the French carmaker, has been charged with corruption and conspiracy to corrupt. Mr Philip Scaysbrook, former head of corporate identity at Peugeot Talbot, faces committal proceedings on October 25. Five other people have been charged in the case.
