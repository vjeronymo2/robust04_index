BFN [Text] Having received the blessing of senior government elements, Knesset House Committee Chairman Yehoshu'a Matza has submitted a bill prohibiting the existence of Palestinian institutions and societies in East Jerusalem in violation of the relevant basic law. The new bill says that Palestinian institutions inherently reject the existence of the State of Israel. Knesset Member Matza told our correspondent Hayim Hecht that the officials with whom he coordinated his bill were frustrated by the fact that a similar bill initiated by the prime minister had fallen through.
