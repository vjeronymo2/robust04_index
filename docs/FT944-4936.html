941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Hunters Armley buy Hunters Armley Group, the commercial printing company, is to pay Pounds 4.4m for the Bristol-based printer Harlequin Colourprint. The initial consideration will be satisfied by Pounds 1.81m in cash and 57,470 new ordinary shares, equivalent to Pounds 100,000, which the vendors have agreed not to dispose of before December 1995. The remainder, to a maximum of Pounds 2.53m, will be paid in cash or Hunters Armley ordinary shares in three annual instalments beginning in 1996. Harlequin had pre-tax profits of Pounds 258,000 for 1993 on turnover of Pounds 2.58m. Net assets stood at Pounds 850,000.
