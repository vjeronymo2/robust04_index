BFN [Text] The Kurdistan Bureau of the Supreme Assembly of the Islamic Revolution in Iraq stated that on 28 April the Iraqi opposition forces pounded the governorate building, the intelligence directorate headquarters, and the Ba'th Party building in al-Nasiriyah Governorate with mortars, killing or injuring dozens of Iraqi henchmen. A statement released by the bureau said that the revolutionaries launched an assault on 27 April at an inspection checkpoint belonging to the al-Qurnah security directorate, killing or injuring 25 agents and destroying two armored vehicles. On the same day, the revolutionaries attacked a large force composed of 45 armored vehicles and a large number of infantry men, who were preparing to launch an offensive on [name indistinct] in al-Nasiriyah marshland. They inflicted casualties on the force and prevented it from launching the assault. The statement said that the Iraqi regime formed two military divisions under Qusayy, the dictator's son, and Muhammad, son of Barzan al-Tikriti. The two divisions' task is to quell any possible popular uprising. On the Iraqi intelligence acts of terrorism in neighboring countries, the statement said that the regime's intelligence began some time ago to train a group of agents to send them to Iran to carry out acts of terror there.
