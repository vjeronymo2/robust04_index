Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking to newsmen this morning at Government House on the strong reply of the Thai armed forces to Cambodian allegations on Thai support for the Khmer Rouge, Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri said the reply will not cause Thai-Cambodian relations to deteriorate. This is because the clarification by the armed forces was straightforward for the purpose of achieving nutual understanding. The two countries will have to work together to solve their border problems. Regarding the criticism by the Cambodian finance minister that Thai businessmen are not honest, the foreign minister said some businessmen, regardless of their nationalities, are not good. Thai businessmen were the first lot to risk their investment in Cambodia. Therefore, they should be viewed in a broad-minded manner.
