Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Republic of Singapore Navy is to acquire 12 new patrol craft to replace 12 coastal patrol craft that have served the Navy since 1980's. The second minister for defense, Dr. Lee Boon Yang, said the new patrol craft will be equiped with state-of-the-art command, control, and communications system. They will further enhance the Navy's fighting and civil defense capabilities. Dr. Lee said for the first time in Singapore Navy's shipbuilding program, all the phases of design, construction, and system integration are carried out locally. The patrol vessels will be designed and built by the Singapore Shipbuilding and Engineering, a subsidiary of Singapore Technology. Dr. Lee was speaking at the launch of the Navy's second mine counter-measure vessel, RSS [Republic of Singapore Ship] (Kalau) at the Singapore Shipbuilding and Engineering yard at Tuas. The vessel is one of the four mine counter-measure vessels Singapore has ordered from Sweden.
