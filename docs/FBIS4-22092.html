BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Apr (MENA) -- AL-JUMHURIYAH reports in its edition tomorrow, Thursday [21 April], that Colonel Mustafa al- Kharrubi, member of the Libyan Revolution Command Council, arrived in Beijing from North Korea today, met with the Egyptian ambassador, and asked him to convey a message to President Husni Mubarak from Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. The paper adds that the message deals with the president's discussion of the Lockerbie issue with the Chinese leaders.
