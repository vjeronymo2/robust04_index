930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Assisted areas map ready for EC THE GOVERNMENT is expected this week to submit to the European Commission its redrawn assisted areas map, the subject of intensive lobbying by the regions. The map will specify areas in which companies can qualify for UK government and EC regional aid. It is expected to provoke fierce controversy by including areas of inner London and south-east England, at the expense of parts of Wales, the Midlands and the north of England.
