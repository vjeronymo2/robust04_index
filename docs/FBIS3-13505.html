Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] One terrorist was shot dead and another seriously wounded in Bayt Lahiyah in the Gaza Strip during a firefight between terrorists and the security forces. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that shots were fired at a security forces vehicle in the Bayt Lahiyah area a short time ago. The soldiers returned the fire, killing the terrorist and seriously wounded his comrade. Our correspondent adds that the security forces captured two Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] fugitives last night during an initiated operation in Gaza's Rimal and al-Shaykh Radwan neighborhoods.
