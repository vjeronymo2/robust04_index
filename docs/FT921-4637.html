920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / Finance & the Family: Bonus cuts by Equity & Law Equity & Law has reduced terminal bonus rates on life policies and shorter term pension policies. Reversionary bonuses, and terminal bonuses on long-term pensions, remain unchanged. Assuming policies were taken out by a 29-year-old man paying Pounds 30 per month, pay-outs on 25-year endowments have dropped to Pounds 62,426 from Pounds 63,451, while on ten-year policies the fall is from Pounds 7,767 to Pounds 7,263. Equity & Law also announced that its funds under management rose from Pounds 4.7bn to Pounds 5.6bn.
