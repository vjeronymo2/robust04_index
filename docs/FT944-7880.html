941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / World Trade News: John Brown - Contracts & Ventures John Brown, part of the Trafalgar House engineering division, is to build two multi-million dollar projects in Chile and New Zealand for Methanex of Canada, the world's largest producer of methanol. Both projects involve expansion of existing Methanex plants. Terms were not disclosed but the proposed Chile contract, for which John Brown has signed a letter of intent, comprises most of the Dollars 275m which Methanex is spending on the project.
