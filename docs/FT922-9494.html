920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / The Queen's Speech: Opt-out schools a priority - Main Points The government intends to put into legislation its blueprint for state schools in the next century. A white paper will be published before the summer, and legislation will follow soon afterwards. It will focus on the regime for schools that have opted out of local education authority control. The government intends a substantial increase in the number of state schools funded directly by Whitehall.
