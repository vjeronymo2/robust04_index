March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

ETHIOPIA;

MARXIST CHIEF NOW CALLS FOR CAPITALISM

The Marxist economic system imposed after the late Emperor Haile Selassie was overthrown in 1974 is a failure, President Mengistu Haile Mariam said in a speech to the Central Committee of the ruling Marxist Workers' Party.

Mengistu said he wants a return to capitalism. The Central Committee must approve Mengistu's recommendations for a drastic overhaul of the shattered economy. In recent months, Mengistu has taken many seemingly small steps to attract foreign investment and encourage domestic production.
