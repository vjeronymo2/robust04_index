921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Lon & St Lawrence net assets fall Net asset value of London and St Lawrence Investment fell 11 per cent from 140.2p to 124.82p per share over the year to August 31 last. Again in the light of the high level of income received - gross income rose from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 1.23m - it had resolved to pay a special dividend of 0.48p, although this compares with 0.6p for the previous year, in addition to a single payment of 3.36p (3.06p) for the year. Pre-tax revenue amounted to Pounds 1.2m (Pounds 1.1m) and net attributable to Pounds 827,000 (Pounds 814,000). Earnings were shown as 4.34p (4.27p).
