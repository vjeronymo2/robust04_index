March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

I watch old reruns of "I Love Lucy," "Sanford and Son" and "The Honeymooners" and they still make me laugh. The other night I watched "Roseanne" for the first time and I didn't laugh once during the whole show. Have I lost my sense of humor? Should I go see a psychiatrist?

Frederick D. Mullen, Upland
