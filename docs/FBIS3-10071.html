Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Chilean Foreign Ministry has reported that a ceremony was held at the Chilean mission before the United Nations on 16 February, during which Chile and Cambodia resumed diplomatic relations at ambassadorial level after a 20-year severance. The agreement was signed by Juan Somavia, the Chilean permanent representative before the United Nations, and Prince Sisowath Sirirath [name and title as published], his Cambodian counterpart. [passage omitted] Both countries severed diplomatic relations 20 years ago. A communique released by the Chilean Foreign Ministry states that notwithstanding this, "Chile condemned the invasion of Cambodia by Vietnam and supported all initiatives to disarm the feuding factions and re-establish a peaceful coexistence."
