BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- An agreement on cooperation in information services between the Information Committee of the DPRK and the Ministry of Information of the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia was signed in Phnom Penh on April 4. It was signed by vice-chairman of the DPRK Information Committee Yi Pong-hui and Minister of Information of Cambodia Ieng Mouly.
