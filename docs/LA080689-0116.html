August 6, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE: COLUMBIA CENTER BUILDING SOLD TO RPM INVESTMENTS

Columbia Savings Realty Group, a subsidiary of Columbia Savings & Loan, has sold its Columbia Center office building to RPM Investments of Los Angeles.

The 26,000-square-foot building, located at 72990 El Paseo Road in Palm Desert, was purchased for $4.5 million, according to Jeffrey E. Palmer, chief operating officer for the seller.
