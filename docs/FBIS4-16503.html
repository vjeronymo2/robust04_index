BFN [Text] The Moldovan leadership intends to examine the memorandum on border cooperation between Russia, Ukraine and Belarus signed in Moscow on April 15, Moldovan President Mircea Snegur told Interfax before leaving Moscow. "I am not familiar with this memorandum. It was not discussed at the CIS summit. However, we must study this issue. It would be quite useful for us," Snegur pointed out. Snegur welcomed Ukraine joining the CIS economic union as an associated member. "This would enhance economic cooperation between Ukraine and Moldova," he stressed. However, Snegur underlined that economic relations between Ukraine and Moldova were based primarily on a bilateral treaty.
