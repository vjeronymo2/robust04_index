930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Faber and Faber Profits of Faber and Faber, the independent publisher, rose from Pounds 267,000 to Pounds 930,000 pre-tax for the year to end-March. The growth was achieved despite first half trading conditions which many in the book trade saw as the worst in living memory. The second half showed a marked improvement.
