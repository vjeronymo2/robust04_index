Enterprise HK2804102994 Changsha Hunan People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 20 Apr 94 HK2804102994 Changsha Hunan People's Radio Network Mandarin BFN [Excerpt] Our province's first share-holding science and technology enterprise--Hunan Haili Chemical Industrial Company Limited--was officially founded in Changsha today. Gu Xiulian, member of the CPC Central Committee and minister of chemical industry; He Guoqiang, vice minister of chemical industry; Wang Maolin, secretary of the Hunan Provincial CPC Committee; Governor Chen Bangzhu, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee; Liu Fusheng, chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Liu Zheng, chairman of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and other provincial party and government leaders; gladly wrote inscriptions to express their congratulations on the founding of the company. Starting reform 10 years ago, the Hunan Chemical Industry Research Institute has been following the road of integrating science, industry, and trade. The institute has managed to increase its economic returns at an annual growth rate of 35 percent. Its output value last year exceeded 100 million yuan. In order to advance on the overseas market, to apply the institute's scientific and technological research achievements to industrial production, and to present them to the overseas market as commodities, the Hunan Haili Chemical Industrial Company Limited--the province's first share-holding science and technology enterprise--was established, with the approval of the provincial commission for restructuring economic systems.
