Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Report by Beijing-based correspondent Mun Il-hyon] [Text] Jiang Zemin, Chinese president and CPC general secretary, is expected to make an official visit to the ROK during the second half of this year. A senior ROK Government official on 18 March said: Chinese President Jiang Zemin will make an official visit to the ROK this year to reciprocate President Kim Yong-sam's visit to China. The specific time of President Jiang's visit will be decided in the upcoming summit talks between President Kim and President Jiang.
