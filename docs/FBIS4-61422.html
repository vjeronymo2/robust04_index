BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Nazran -- Isa Kostoyev, chairman of the Russian Federation Council's Constitutional Legislation Committee, stated that together with Ingushetia's justice minister he has prepared for President Yeltsin proposals on settling the Ossetian-Ingush conflict. In particular it is proposed -- under the supervision of an authorized representative of the Russian Federation president -- before 20 May to return all refugees and forced resettlers to six residential areas in Prigorodnyy Rayon and by 20 September to return them to all the rayon's villages. "The provisional administration can do a lot if it wants to, but its leadership has no such wish," Mr. Kostoyev said. "Vladimir Lozovoy should be replaced and the provisional administration moved to neutral territory."
