920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / UK Company News: French Connection apologises Mr Michael Shen has won Pounds 20,000 damages and an apology from French Connection Group for wrongful dismissal and its allegations of his 'gross misconduct'. The clothing manufacturer and retailer will also repay a Pounds 1m loan made to it by Mr Shen, who was dismissed as chief executive last September. Mr Stephen Malme, a former finance director, has also received an apology and compensation.
