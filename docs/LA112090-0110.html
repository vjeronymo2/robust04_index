November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

WHAT WORLD LEADERS SAID

'The Continent, frozen in hostility for so long, has become a Continent of revolutionary change.'

GEORGE BUSH

U.S. President

'Our meeting takes place at the turning point of two eras. It constitutes a major event. Its consequences will be felt for centuries.'

MIKHAIL S. GORBACHEV

Soviet president

'There would be no summit today . . . had it not been for the . . . conviction of President Gorbachev that the desire for peace lives in the hearts and minds of all men and all women.'

BRIAN MULRONEY

Canadian prime minister
