930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Bass plans Pounds 35m pub expansion BASS, the UK's leading brewer, is to spend Pounds 35m in 1993-94 on building and acquiring new pubs, writes Philip Rawstorne. The company has set up a special team of surveyors, based in London, Leeds and Birmingham, and has begun a nationwide search for suitable sites. Mr Bob Holtham, director of acquisitions for Bass Taverns, said: 'We are already well on the way towards our target of 35 new pubs this year and we aim to open one a week during 1994.
