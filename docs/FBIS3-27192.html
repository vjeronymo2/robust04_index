Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 21 (XINHUA) -- Italian-Chinese cooperation, especially in the fields of economy and trade, is "satisfactory", said visiting Italian Senate President Giovanni Spatolini. At a press conference held here today at the Italian Embassy in Beijing, he noted that Italy and China share many common views in their foreign policies, such as settling Middle East and Bosnian conflicts by peaceful means. He said there have been a lot of achievements in the two countries' economic and trade cooperation, and Italian entrepreneurs have shown great interest in investing in China. Spatolini pointed out that the political changes in Italy will not affect Italian-Chinese relations. He also stressed the importance of cultural exchanges between the two countries. This afternoon Spatolini and his party are scheduled to head for Shanghai.
