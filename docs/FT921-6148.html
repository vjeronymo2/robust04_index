920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: County Smaller County Smaller Companies Investment Trust, which began trading last July with a net asset value of 96p, reported one of 94.4p at December 31. Net revenue for the period, July 25 to December 31, amounted to Pounds 236,086 for earnings per share of 1.44p. An interim dividend of 1.125p is declared.
