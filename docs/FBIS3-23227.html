Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Israel Air Force fighter planes raided targets belonging to the Hizballah terror organization in the region of Jabal Safi in south Lebanon this afternoon. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman reports that all the planes returned safely to base and that the pilots reported direct hits. The targets attacked served as a training site and launching pad for Hizballah terror attacks. This was reported by our correspondent Yariv Ben-Yehuda.
