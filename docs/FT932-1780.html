930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / International Company News: Amdahl joins EDS to sell software products AMDAHL, the US computer maker, and Electronic Data Systems, a unit of General Motors, has formed a company to market products that offer a business applications software environment available across multiple computing platforms, AP-DJ reports from Dallas. Amdahl's contribution to the joint venture, to be named Antares Alliance Group, is its Huron applications development, production and maintenance system. EDS is providing the InCASE data modelling and application building system, and its COBOL conversion and business modeling tools. Antares will market its products through original equipment manufacturers, value-added resellers, and other distributors. They will also be available through Amdahl's direct sales force and will be used by EDS to standardise the development environment for its strategic business units and their customers.
