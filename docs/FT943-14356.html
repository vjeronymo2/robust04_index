940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Delays at Athens airport Greek Transport Minister Theodoros Pangalos warned summer travellers yesterday that long delays at Athens airport would persist because of the big seasonal increase in flights. The delays, often of two to four hours, are due to air traffic controllers' refusal to work overtime, an inadequate radar system and a 30 per cent increase in flights during the summer. Mr Pangalos wants a new radar system operational as soon as possible but ruled out a wage increase. The Board of Airlines Representatives said: 'The situation is a nightmare for tourists and threatens Greece's commercial interests.'
