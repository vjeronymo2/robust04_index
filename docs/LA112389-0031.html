November 23, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST : EL CAJON

With a simple 3, 8, 20, 25, 40, 48, James P. Carroll of El Cajon won more than $5 million in Saturday's California Lottery game, officials announced Wednesday.

Specifically, Carroll won $5,211,954 of a $10.4-million jackpot. He purchased his winning ticket at the 7-Eleven store at 1296 N. 2nd St., said Bruce Cherubin, district manager for the State Lottery.

Carroll will receive annually $260,597 minus 20% in federal taxes for the next 20 years, Cherubin said. Carroll, shunning all publicity of his big win, will get his first check through the mail within the next couple of weeks instead of at the usual check presentation ceremony, Cherubin said.
