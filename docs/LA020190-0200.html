February 1, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ANGELUS LEAGUE ROUNDUP

In another Angelus League game:

Mater Dei 85, Bishop Amat 35 -- David Boyle had 20 points, Andy Karich 11 points and eight rebounds and Jason Quinn 11 points and nine assists to lead visiting Mater Dei (22-1, 6-0). Bishop Amat fell to 15-8, 1-5.
