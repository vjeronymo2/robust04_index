931206 FT 06 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Premium Trust allocation The Premium Trust offer for subscription for 3.3m units, which was increased from 3m units, has been fully subscribed with no scaling down required. Accordingly, the number of issued ordinary shares of Premium Trust and Premium Underwriting will be 19.8m and 13.2m respectively. Total raised under the offer is Pounds 33m.
