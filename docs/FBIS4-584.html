BFN [Text] The honorable minister of foreign affairs, Mr. Molapo Qhobela, left Maseru today on an official visit to the Peoples' Republic of China at the invitation of his Chinese counterpart. The minister is accompanied by the principal secretary for foreign affairs, Mr. Nthabise Manoko, and the acting director of political affairs in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Victor Mthetho. A spokesperson for the Foreign Ministry said that the purpose of the visit is to strengthen links between Lesotho and China following the decision to reestablish relations between the two countries. Plans were underway to establish a Lesotho diplomatic mission in Beijing.
