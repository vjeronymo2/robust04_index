July 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

In an unusual marriage between pop and animation, the Jets pop band will make a music video to the theme of "Chip 'n' Dale's Rescue Rangers," the new animated series from Walt Disney Television that will premiere this fall on stations around the country. The video will be used in promoting the series that will be seen here on KHJ-TV Channel 9. ALEENE MacMINN
