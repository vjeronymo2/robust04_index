920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Strawberries from Spain and France are excellent this week at Pounds 1.10-1.70 a lb (Pounds 1.30-1.80) reports the FFVIB. Other good buys include varieties of apples such as Cape Granny Smith at 55-60p a lb (45-55p) and New Zealand Royal Gala at 60-70p a lb (60-70p) pineapples remain a good buy at Pounds 1.00-1.50 each (Pounds 1.00-1.50). Jersey Royal potatoes are currently in peak supply and are down in price to 18-25p a lb (45-55p). Flat mushrooms at 60-70p a 1/2 lb (60-70p) and English spinich at 50-70p a lb (60-75p) round up the best vegetable buys. British, Dutch, Guernsey & Canary Island tomatoes at 80-90p a lb are excellent value this week.
