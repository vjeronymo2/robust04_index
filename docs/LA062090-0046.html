June 20, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: CHULA VISTA

A committee of the California Energy Commission will hold a public hearing Thursday to allow testimony on SDG&E's proposal to build an additional power plant next to its existing plant in Chula Vista.

SDG&E says the proposed $600-million plant would serve about 331,000 homes.

The plant site is one of five in Southern California proposed by the utility for a combined-cycle project, in which the output of the five plants would be combined to distribute power as needed.

The hearing begins at 10 a.m. at the City Council chambers.
