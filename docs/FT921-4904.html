920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Order against Ramsden A BANKRUPTCY ORDER was made yesterday against Mr Terry Ramsden, the former chairman of Glen International, an investment firm that collapsed in 1987 with losses of Pounds 142m. The order was made in the London Bankruptcy Court on a petition by the Inland Revenue, which claimed Mr Ramsden owed it about Pounds 21m. Mr Ramsden, who did not oppose the petition, was recently granted legal aid for his defence to criminal charges.
