941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Highland hopes for Christmas cheer - Other Companies The maker of Famous Grouse, the second largest Scotch whisky brand in the UK, is today expected to report a rise in pre-tax profits to about Pounds 42m for the year to August 31 from Pounds 38.8m in 1992-93. The full-year dividend is likely to be about 7.3p against 6.6p. Ahead of the Christmas period, investors will be keen to hear Highland's view of the intensely competitive market.
