941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Wyevale Garden Centres WYEVALE GARDEN Centres has acquired the freehold of Peter Barratt's Garden Centre in Washington, Tyne and Wear, for Pounds 825,000 plus stock at valuation. The purchase brings the the number of garden centres operated by Wyevale to 44. The Barratt partnership owns two other centres.
