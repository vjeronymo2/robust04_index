Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Aleksandr Kopnov and Anatoliy Krasnov] [Text] Moscow March 21 TASS--Russia and Japan agreed in principle on Monday to hold exchanges in the field of defence and security, Japanese Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata told a press conference. He explained that in order to enhance trust between the two sides it had been agreed to develop contacts between people responsible for drafting the policy in the spheres, particularly between representatives of defence ministries.
