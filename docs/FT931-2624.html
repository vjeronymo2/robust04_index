930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Holders Technology improves to Pounds 0.39m Holders Technology, the USM-quoted precision tools and specialised materials business, saw pre-tax profits advance from Pounds 380,000 to Pounds 394,000 in the 12 months to November 30. Turnover was Pounds 3.48m, against Pounds 2.98m. Despite the increasing confidence expressed at the interim stage the company said the second half was below best expectations. Earnings per share were 9.07p (8.56p). An unchanged final dividend of 4p is proposed for a maintained total of 6p.
