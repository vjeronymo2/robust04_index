920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Sycamore rises sharply to Pounds 0.3m Sycamore Holdings, a maker of kitchen furniture, foundry and leisure products, returned profits of Pounds 314,000 pre-tax for the half year to end-March, a substantial improvement over last time's Pounds 113,000. Turnover totalled Pounds 9.69m (Pounds 3.95m) with the second quarter contribution up by 45 per cent over the first quarter's and profitability returning to a 'level more in line with target'. The second half is traditionally better and the directors said a continuation of the second quarter improvement in turnover was reflected in the forward order book which currently stood at Pounds 4.4m compared with Pounds 2.6m at the September year-end. Earnings per share were 0.29p (0.24p).
