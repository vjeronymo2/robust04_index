BFN [Text] A Tajik Government delegation headed by Kozidavlat Koimdodov, deputy chairman of the republic's National Assembly, continues its work in Moscow. The delegation held talks with Sergey Dubinin, acting Russian finance minister. A wide range of issues, in particular implementation of the agreement on allocation of the loan to our country by Russia, were discussed. Methods of solving economic problems during Tajikistan's entry to the ruble zone were thoroughly discussed. Kayum Kavmiddinov, the chairman of the National Bank of the republic, Rustam Mirzoyev, the minister of economy and forecasting, and Sirojiddin Nasriddinov, an employee of the Tajik embassy in the Russian Federation, attended the meeting. The meeting was held in an atmosphere of mutual understanding. The Tajik news agency KHOVAR received this report from our country's embassy in Moscow.
