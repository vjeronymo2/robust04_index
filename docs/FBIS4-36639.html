BFN [Text] Honorable listeners: A responsible source at the Democratic Republic of Yemen Defense Ministry has stated that the popular resistance, in the center of Shu'ayb in al-Dali' Province, with rare heroism under the leadership of fighters from the war of liberation, yesterday repulsed attacks by the invaders of the bloody al-Ahmar gang, who were targeting citizens' villages and property. The source said, in a statement for the YEMENI NEWS AGENCY, Aden, that the popular resistance succeeded in repulsing enemy attacks, on whom they inflicted huge losses, including the destruction of a military crew [taqm]. They killed Staff Lieutenant Colonel Salih 'Ali Salih Zahir al-Sanhani, in whose possession they found a quantity of drugs and important documents. They also killed a number of enemy officers and men, and captured a sophisticated communications system.
