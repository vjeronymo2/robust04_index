Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Our soldiers of the People's Army actively wage activities to create literary and art works praising the greatness of our Supreme Commander General Kim Chong-il. This reflects the soldiers' limitless trust in and boundless loyalty to our Supreme Commander General Kim Chong-il. The soldiers, who created and announced more than 700 pieces of literary and art works during last year, continue to create many literary, art works this year. The works emphatically sing of the great honor and pride of the soldiers of the People's Army who are advancing along an ever-victorious road under the leadership of the Comrade Kim Chong-il, an iron-willed brilliant commander possessed of extraordinary military intelligence and great commandership. The works also displays well their single-hearted unity, and steadfast faith and fiery pledge to remain unfailingly loyal to the supreme comrade leader by wholly devoting their destiny and future to him.
