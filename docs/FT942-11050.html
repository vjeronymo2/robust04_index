940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Lawyer to probe union ballot The CPSA civil servants' union is to appoint an independent lawyer to investigate balloting irregularities that have forced a Pounds 100,000 repeat of its presidential election. A re-run of the vote among the union's 131,000 members was ordered by the High Court when Mr Alan England, a defence ministry branch official in Harrogate, challenged his omission from the list of candidates in the ballot.
