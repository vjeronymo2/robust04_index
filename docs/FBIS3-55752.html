Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 11 Jan -- Serhiy Plachynda, the chairman of the Peasant Democratic Party of Ukraine [SelDPU] has stated that U.S. President Bill Clinton's declaration that a trilateral agreement on nuclear weapons is to be signed in Moscow, is none other than a diktat and that it grossly interferes in Ukraine's internal affairs. "Clinton ought to think about the fact that Ukrainian nuclear missiles are a guarantor of peace worldwide, rather than just for Ukraine, as well as for Europe and America itself," said the SelDPU chairman. "If Zhirinovskiy does come to power, he will not be fastidious in either utterances or actions, which is why there must be a deterrent, which is what Ukraine's nuclear weapons are. After a considerable section of Russians have followed Zhirinovskiy, having opted for fascism, to make such statements as that by Clinton is quite outrageous," SelDPU Chairman Plachynda pointed out.
