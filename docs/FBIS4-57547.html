BFN [Text] President Juan Carlos Wasmosy yesterday received Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu. Later the president said that Paraguay will place a list of national products at the disposal of Romanian authorities in order to begin trading with that European country. Vacaroiu has begun a four-day official visit to the country that will end at 1240 on 21 May. "This is the first time a Romanian prime minister has visited us. Borders are opening, the world is getting smaller, the economy is becoming global, and it is important for these people to get acquainted with our country," President Wasmosy said at Silvio Pettirossi International Airport. Vacaroiu arrived at 1450 yesterday. Wasmosy said the government had prepared a draft of agreements to be analyzed by the two presidents' ministers and advisers. Among the documents that are scheduled to be signed at 1030 on 21 May are agreements on cultural exchange, the elimination of visas for diplomatic and official personnel, documents on cooperation between the two countries' foreign ministries, and a trade agreement. Wasmosy said the purpose of the trade agreement is to start trade relations with Romania, because we want Romanians to know what the country can produce. The president added that it is also important to know what products Romania can sell to our country.
