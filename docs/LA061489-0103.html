June 14, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP WEDNESDAY;

1988-89 YEAR IN SPORTS;

PREP BY THE NUMBERS

Notable numbers in the 1988-89 high school year:

2,301 -- Number of yards Servite's Derek Brown rushed for in his senior season, setting an Orange County record.

2,301 -- Number of interviews El Dorado swimmer Janet Evans granted in the week after returning from the Seoul Olympics.

93 -- Number of consecutive bases stolen by Liberty Christian's Bryan James over four seasons.

84 -- Number of consecutive victories by Mission Viejo girls' soccer team, a national record, before losing to El Toro Jan. 20.

42 -- Number of consecutive golf matches Valencia won.

25 -- Number of points Trabuco Hills center Rick Swanwick scored in the second half of state championship game against Central Valley in Oakland in March.

25 -- Number of points Trabuco Hills scored in the second half of the state championship game against Central Valley in Oakland in March.

39-11 1/2 -- Number of feet and inches Lisa Fager of Mission Viejo triple jumped to set the only Orange County track and field record this season.

13 -- Number of former and current Mission Viejo athletes who competed at the 1988 Olympics in Seoul.

12 (and counting) -- Number of meetings to decide which league Santa Margarita will be placed in.

8 -- Number of people watching Keri Phebus of Corona del Mar win the Southern Section girls' tennis singles title.

7 -- Number of Southern Section and state championships won by Corona del Mar and its athletes this season.

4 -- Number of Orange County wrestlers who won titles at the state wrestling championships in Stockton in March, the county's best performance.

2 -- Number of consecutive state titles won by the Irvine girls' volleyball team.
