930420 FT 20 APR 93 / UK Company News: London & Associated rises 14% LONDON & Associated Investment Trust, the property investment group, reported a 14 per cent rise from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 1.17m in pre-tax profits for the year to the end of December on gross income which had improved 16 per cent from Pounds 3.61m to Pounds 4.19m. Net assets at the year end showed a gain of 5 per cent to 39.34p on a year earlier. An independent valuation of the group's portfolio showed a 'creditable' surplus. The acquisition of the Brunel Centre, Bletchley, since the year end brings the total number of shops to more than 550 and the annual rental income to over Pounds 5m. Property operating profits were higher at Pounds 1.96m (Pounds 1.87m) with other investments contributing a static Pounds 200,000 (Pounds 213,000). Share of associates was Pounds 94,000 (Pounds 42,000). Earnings were down slightly from 1.14p to 0.99p, but the dividend goes up from 0.53p to 0.58p with a proposed final of 0.53p. Bisichi Mining, in which London & Associated has a 42 per cent interest, showed a 57 per cent increased in pre-tax profits from Pounds 106,000 to Pounds 166,000 in the 1992 calendar year. Earnings per share jumped from 0.75p to 1.34p and the proposed dividend goes up to 0.63p (0.6p)
