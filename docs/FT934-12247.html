931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE September/October c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 390, BWC Dollars 410, BTD Dollars 355, BWD Dollars 375; C and F Antwerp BTC Dollars 365, BWC Dollars 365, BTD Dollars 345, BWD Dollars 345. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 40 tonnes for the week ended 22 October, against none in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles,notably in the Syrian range.
