Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES 15/2/1994 -- The Brussels-based NATO headquarters has agreed to the setting up in Petrosani an information centre of the treaty for the zone of Transylvania, central and north-western Romania. For the time being, the respective town will host the information office and adequate headquarters will be found in future fit for the equipment with a computer network to be connected to the NATO centres in Europe. A NATO delegation from Brussels is expected to come to Petrosani to inspect technical conditions on the spot.
