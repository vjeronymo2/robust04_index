Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Hizballah has staged a rally in Beirut's Southern Suburb to mark the International Jerusalem Day. Speaking at the rally, Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah stressed the importance of the struggle to liberate Jerusalem and the need to confront the Zionist enemy's attempt to Judaize the holy places. He also demanded Lebanon's withdrawal from the peace negotiations. Mass marches were held in the suburb's streets, carrying banners denouncing Israel and its aggressive practices.
