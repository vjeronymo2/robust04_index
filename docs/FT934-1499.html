931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Burndene static at Pounds 5.8m A second half slowdown left Burndene Investments static for the year to October 2 with profits of Pounds 5.8m pre-tax, against Pounds 5.78m. Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 59m (Pounds 57.1m), of which the UK contribution was unchanged at Pounds 46.3m, while western Europe grew from Pounds 10.8m to Pounds 12.8m. Results from the group's business areas were mixed with profits from caravan and mobile homes rising to Pounds 4.16m (Pounds 3.64m) and property to Pounds 199,209 (Pounds 192,290); the hosiery side, however, dipped to Pounds 597,319 (Pounds 760,802). Finance and administration costs were down from Pounds 1.19m to Pounds 838,474. Earnings per share were 3.73p (3.59p) and the recommended final dividend of 1.25p makes a total for the year in effect 48 per cent higher at 1.75p (1.182p adjusted for scrip).
