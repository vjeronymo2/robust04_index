930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Finance and the Family: New trust from Axa - At a glance Axa Equity & Law has launched a new Balanced unit trust, which will invest in its range of funds. The initial charge is 6 per cent and the annual charge is 1.5 per cent - this allows for the charges on the underlying funds. The trust will qualify for Personal Equity Plan status, with no additional charges. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000, or Pounds 60 per month. Axa Equity & Law is offering a 1 per cent discount on lump sum investments made before June 30.
