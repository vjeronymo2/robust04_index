BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to war veterans in Yongsong District, Pyongyang municipality, who had given energetic assistance in the construction of grand monumental edifices. About 300 veterans of the fatherland liberation war in the district had rendered active assistance in the construction of the monument to the victorious fatherland liberation war and other grand monumental edifices. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to Yi Pong-suk, an agitator of the February 8 vinalon complex, and Mun Chae-kyong, an agitator of the Ponghwa cooperative farm in Kangdong County, Pyongyang municipality, who had set examples in propaganda and agitation. Yi Pong-suk had contributed to the education of working people, using over 14,000 items of agitation data made by herself in the agitation activity. In the case of Mun Chae-kyong, not only he but also all his family had become agitators and roused co-op farmers to implement the socialist rural theses.
