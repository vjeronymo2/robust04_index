940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / Observer: Doppelganger What do Sir Leon Brittan, Peter Sutherland and Ruud Lubbers have in common - other than the prospect of losing out to Jean-Luc Dehaene? There is an uncanny physical resemblance linking them: all three possess a fine pair of bushy eyebrows and each has a thick thatch of hair swept low across his forehead. By contrast Dehaene slicks his hair back to reveal some inches of temple, wears glasses and has no eyebrows to speak of. And who should he resemble in those three ways? The outgoing president, Jacques Delors.
