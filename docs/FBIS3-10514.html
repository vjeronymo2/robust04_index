Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A shipment of pencils and notebooks donated by Vietnam has arrived in Cuba. This donation, consisting of 500,000 notebooks and the same number of pencils, arrived in the port of Nicaro, in the Mayari Municipality, Holguin Province. This donation was sent to Cuba aboard the ship (Rose Island) by the Vietnamese Youth League. This gift from the young Vietnamese people will be distributed among Education Ministry schools in the provinces of Santiago de Cuba, Las Tunas, Granma, Guantanamo, and Holguin. This is the first shipment of a plan for 10 million of these school items. The unloading at the Nicaro pier was carried out by (Rene Ramos Latur Nickel Mining Company) workers, with the support of coast guard members and students from the Mayari community. Cuban Government officials and local Cuban Communist Party members from Mayari expressed thanks for the donation from the Vietnamese Youth League, and stressed the significance of this donation during the country's current period.
