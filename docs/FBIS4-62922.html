BFN [Text] Moscow May 19 TASS -- A contingent of Ukrainian home guards and special forces numbering 1,000 was dispatched to the Crimea over the past 24 hours, ITAR-TASS learnt on Thursday from sources close to the headquarters of the Black Sea Fleet. According to the same sources, over 300 armed Ukrainian home guards as well as a group of saboteur swimmers from a unit stationed on Pervomaisky Island were airlifted via the Belbek military airfield situated close to Sevastopol, the main base of the Black Sea Fleet. The contingent is mostly massed in the area of Sevastopol as well as at the airfields in Khersones, Belbek, Yukharina Balka and in the village of Tylovo. Sources close to the Black Sea Fleet also told ITAR-TASS that a group of commanding officers from the Ukrainian security service led by first deputy head of the service arrived in Sevastopol.
