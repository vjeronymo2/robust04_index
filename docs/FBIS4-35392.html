BFN [Excerpts] We have received the following from the Royal Court: The custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, yesterday evening, 30 May, underwent a medical examination at the King Fahd Hospital for the Armed Forces in Jeddah. During the examination, a stone was discovered in the passage leading to the gall bladder. The stone was successfully removed with a surgical probe. [passage omitted] The king left the hospital this afternoon for al-Salam Palace in good health. [passage omitted]
