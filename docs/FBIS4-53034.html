BFN [Text] A delegation led by General Bipin Chandra Joshi, chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee of the Republic of India and chief of staff of the Armed Forces, and his wife left Yangon [Rangoon] International Airport in a special plane at 1100 today at the end of their visit to Myanmar [Burma]. Gen. Bipin Chandra Joshi, his wife, and delegation were seen off at the airport by General Maung Aye, deputy commander in chief of the Defense Services and commander in chief of the Army, and his wife; Lt. Gen. Tin U, commander of the No. 1 Special Tactical Command and chief of staff of Army, and his wife; Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt, commander of the Yangon Command, and his wife; Brigadier General Than U, judge advocate general; senior military officials from the Ministry of Defense; Mr. Gopalaswani Parthasarathy; Colonel (P. C. Braradras), military attache; and responsible officials.
