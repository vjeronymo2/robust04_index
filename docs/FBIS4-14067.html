BFN [Text] Mohammad Nawaz Sharif, opposition leader in the National Assembly, has said that all National Assembly members belonging to the Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz Group and the Awami National Party have tendered their resignations to their respective leaderships to protest what he called the government's unconstitutional attitude. Addressing a news conference at Parliament House in Islamabad today, he said these resignations will be submitted to the speaker at an appropriate time. He alleged that the government has utilized Mehran Bank funds for political purposes.
