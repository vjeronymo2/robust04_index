931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: F&C Smaller net asset value ahead A strong showing by share prices of smaller companies, particularly in the UK and the US, helped Foreign & Colonial Smaller Companies report a net asset value of 169.1p per share as at October 31. The figure compared with 136.1p at the trust's April 30 year-end and 109.2p at end-October 1992. Attributable revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 1.58m (Pounds 1.5m) for earnings of 1.74p (1.66p) per share. The interim dividend is lifted to 0.7p (0.68p).
