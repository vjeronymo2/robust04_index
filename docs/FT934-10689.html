931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / Plymouth docks facelift launched THE REGENERATION of Plymouth's historic Royal William Yard was launched yesterday with a conference aimed at attracting private-sector investment. Plymouth Development Corporation has a Pounds 45m budget over five years to regenerate the yard and two other waterfront sites. The project is one of 14 announced this week by the Department of the Environment under its initiative to encourage private-sector involvement in regeneration.
