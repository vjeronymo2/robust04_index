BFN [Text] Sanaa, 27 Apr (MENA)--A Yemeni military source in Sanaa today accused the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] of causing the military situation in Yemen. In a statement this evening, the source said that at 1330 today while the military committee, accompanied by the U.S. and French military attaches, was visiting the 1st Armored Brigade and the 3d Armored Brigade in the 'Imran Camp in Sanaa Governorate, armed groups led by YSP member Yahya Dahish 'Alyan, an "old officer expelled from the Armed Forces," stormed the camp and began firing various small-, medium-, and large-caliber weapons as well as tank, artillery, and rocket fire. The source pointed out that the bombardment targeted the headquarters of the 1st Armored Brigade, the soldiers barracks, the central security camp nearby, and residential areas in the city of 'Imran. The source said that the intensive bombardment caused heavy losses in lives and property. Dozens of people were martyred and others were injured, but the casualty toll has not been determined. The Yemeni military source expressed his regret for this incident. He said that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih followed up on the situation from the very beginning and immediately issued instructions to the defense minister and the general chief of staff to quickly contain the situation and prevent any military deterioration.
