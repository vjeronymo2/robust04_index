April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The San Diego Yacht Club will probably appeal a court order to forfeit the America's Cup to New Zealand, the president of the America's Cup Organizing Committee said.

Malin Burnham, president of the ACOC, said the yacht club board will decide by Monday if it wants to proceed with the appeal of New York Supreme Court Justice Carmen Ciparick's March 28 ruling awarding the trophy to the Mercury Bay Boating Club.

The board of the America's Cup Organizing Committee voted unanimously to recommend that the SDYC appeal the court decision.
