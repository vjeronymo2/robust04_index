February 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BANNING GAY SPEAKERS AT CORONA DEL MAR HIGH SCHOOL

Corona del Mar senior Doug Shulein's petition to overturn Supt. John Nicoll's prohibition of homosexual speakers in a supervised high school classroom psychology class comes as a breath of fresh air to a stagnant atmosphere that has developed over the past few weeks.

Shulein (and other responsible, well-informed students and parents alike) have learned more about the workings of a free democratic society than some of our less-enlightened colleagues. This latter well-intentioned group would continue to protect us all from social and political evils -- the "we-know-what's-best-for-you" approach that is all too common amongst fearful persons.

Obviously, I agree with Doug Shulein. The best method of dealing with a complex issue -- be it homosexuality, drugs, firearms or other social dilemmas that permeate our society -- is through trying to understand all component parts. Our generation has not done well. Our children should be allowed the opportunity to do better.

ELLIOTT C. MERCER

Corona del Mar
