920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / China denies power grab Premier Li Peng, seeking to calm fears among China's neighbours in south-east Asia, told Malaysia's visiting defence minister yesterday that Beijing was not making a grab for power in the region, Reuter reports from Beijing. His remarks to were clearly aimed at soothing anxieties sparked by Chinese moves on the potentially oil-rich Spratly islands in the South China Sea.
