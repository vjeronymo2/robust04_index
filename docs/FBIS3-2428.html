Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporters Yu Yiguo (0358 1355 0948) and Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Tokyo, 9 Mar (XINHUA) -- Xiao Rong, the author of My Father Deng Xiaoping, said here on 8 March: Although Deng Xiaoping will be 90-years-old, he continues his workout, walking three km daily. Xiao Rong made the above remark at a press conference at Tokyo Akasaka Prince Hotel. She arrived in Tokyo 7 March at the invitation of the Japanese bookstore Tokuma to launch the publication of her book in Japan. At the 8 March news conference, Xiao Rong discussed the process of her writing the book and fielded questions from reporters. On her father's view regarding China's present and future, she said: Deng Xiaoping believes China's decade-long policy of reform and opening up has laid the foundation. China is facing a new opportunity now, and China should seize it. He is fully confident about the future of China. She said: "My father has a strong desire to go to Hong Kong after it returns to China in 1997. He said: 'In 1997, I will be 93 years old and probably unable to walk. But I will go to Hong Kong even if I am in a wheelchair. I will be very happy even if I stay in Hong Kong for only a minute'." In response to a Japanese reporter's question about Deng Xiaoping's everyday life, she said: "The secret of my father's longevity is living a regular life." She said Deng Xiaoping likes reading and subscribing to a dozen newspapers which he finishes everyday.
