Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] After observing and monitoring the movements of Saddam's security forces in al-Basrah Governorate, the Islamic resistance forces last week set up ambushes on the road between the district of (al-Mdaynah) and the area of (al-Nasri) which belong to the Governorate of al-Basrah. Our sources affirmed that the resistance ambushes opened fire when the tyrant's security forces passed the above-mentioned road. They said that a fierce battle ensued between the resistance groups and the regime's security forces. A number of soldiers and officers from the tyrant's security forces were killed and the rest withdrew, dragging their tails behind them. The Islamic resistance forces returned to their bases safely.
