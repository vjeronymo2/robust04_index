September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Members of the New York City Ballet will be guests Sept. 30 at a "Stars and Stripes" dinner-dance and fund-raiser for the Center Dance Alliance, an Orange County Performing Arts Center support group. The gala will follow the troupe's final matinee performance at the Center. Information: (714) 556-2122, Ext. 220 or 222.
