Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 18 TASS -- President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree on a citizenship department at the Russian president's office, reported the Russian president's press service on Friday. The decree sets up the citizenship department on the basis of the former citizenship division at the president's office. The president appointed Abdulakh Kasbulatovich Mikitayev chief of the department. He will concurrently head the commission on citizenship at the Russian president. The department's main task is to ensure the commission's operations in conducting a united state policy in the sphere of citizenship.
