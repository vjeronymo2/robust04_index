June 29, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

MIAMI WILL PAY $1,000 TO OWNER OF DOG SHOT BY OFFICER

The city announced Thursday that it will pay about $1,000 for the veterinarian bills and cremation of a golden Labrador retriever named "Bear" that was shot by a policeman.

A police officer shot the dog June 4 when he was investigating two men suspected of being intoxicated. The barking animal ran toward the officer, who responded by shooting the dog, police said.
