May 20, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SANTA PAULA;

HEARING FOCUSES ON LEVY TO SAVE OPEN SPACE

The Santa Paula City Council will hold a public hearing Monday on an assessment district to maintain open space in the new Hillsborough tract.

The tract of upscale housing in north Santa Paula, where houses cost at least $395,000, is in the first phase of construction.

The open space, including a flood-control basin and a grove of trees, must be maintained under an agreement made between the developers and the city when the 69-house project was approved in 1988.

The cost of maintaining the basin and landscaping is to be borne by homeowners in the tract. An assessment of about $100 annually would appear on the property tax bill.

Wilkinson emphasized that surrounding homeowners would not pay for the proposed assessment district. JANET BERGAMO
