March 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Bank of New England Corp., Boston, has named Lawrence K. Fish chairman and chief executive, as expected.

Last month, the bank announced that its losses for 1989 totaled $1.11 billion. Fish, 45, has been president and chief operating officer of Columbia Savings & Loan Assn. in Beverly Hills. He also had been an executive vice president at Bank of Boston.
