BFN [Text] Arak (Central Province), May 25, IRNA -- IRNA Director Fereydoun Verdinejad said here today that the main task of the Iranian journalists is to introduce the real nature of the Islamic Republic system. Speaking at the inaugural ceremony of the IRNA satellite system, and the new building of IRNA bureau here, he said that the enemies of Islam were attempting to misportray the Islamic Republic in the eyes of the world people. "The arrogance media circulate only a selection of the Iranian news in line with their political objectives," he said. Meanwhile, Mohammad Reza Sadeq, in charge of the expansion of the IRNA local centers, said that the new 350-sq.-meter building had been constructed on 1,118 sq. meters of land at a cost of Rls. 125 million.
