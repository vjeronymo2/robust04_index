Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] On the occasion of 45th founding anniversary of the Lao People's Army [LPA] (20 January 1949-94), General Doan Khue, minister of National Defense, sent a greeting message to Senior Lieutenant General Choummali Sai-ngason, defense minister of the Lao People's Democratic Republic. The message reads: Dear comrade minister; on the occasion of the 45th founding anniversary of the LPA, on behalf of all cadres and combatants of the Vietnam People's Army and on my behalf, I would like to convey to you, and through you to all the cadres and combatants of the fraternal LPA, my most sincere greetings of solidarity. Throughout the last 45 years, under the leadership of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP], the LPA and the multiethnic Lao people have bravely advanced through all difficulties and obstacles, continously defeated all the invading imperialists as well as the domestic reactionaries, achieved glorious victories, and restored independence and freedom to the fatherland. After the complete liberation of the country, the Lao armed forces still went on to crush all the dark schemes and sabotage activities of the enemies. In the past 20 year, the Lao People's Democratic Republic have recorded important achievements in all areas of national security, politics, and external relations, and in the socioeconomic domain. Domestic stability is constantly improving, the country's status on the international stage is being heightened, thus contributing to the protection of peace and stability in Southeast Asia as well as in the world. We are happy for your achievements, and strongly trust that the Lao people and the LPA will continue to record ever greater achievements, successfully implement the resolution of the Fifth Congress of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party, and build a peaceful, independent, unified, and prosperous Laos. Also on this occasion, on behalf of the Vietnam People's Army, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the LPRP, the government, the people, and the LPA, for the fine sentiments and valuable assistance they had given to the Vietnamese people and the people's army during the past revolution as well as in the present rennovation undertaking. May the special friendly solidarity and the comprehensive cooperation between the two peoples and the two armies of Vietnam and Laos be evermore solidified and developed. I wish you, comrade minister, good health and happiness in your life, and more achievements in fulfilling your noble duties. My solemn salute to you, dear comrade.
