Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] A consultative meeting of the Presidency and the government was held in the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency building today, with only one item on the agenda -- scrutiny of the constitutional solution for the Federal Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the signing of the agreement with the Republic of Croatia. The meeting was chaired by Bosnia-Herzegovina Deputy Prime Minister Edib Bukvic. Several suggestions were put forward in the debate that will be conveyed to our negotiators. It is expected that the Washington agreement will be signed by Presidents Izetbegovic and Tudjman, to be subsequently ratified by the deputies of our assembly. The Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly session is expected to take place at the end of this month.
