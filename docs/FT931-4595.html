930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News: N Midland decline continues PROFITS continued to decline at North Midland Construction last year. Following the halving of profits to Pounds 555,000 in 1991, the result came out at Pounds 159,000. Turnover fell 25 per cent to Pounds 18.2m (Pounds 24.1m). Earnings per share were 1.3p (3.8p). A final dividend of 0.3p is recommended for a total of 0.6p (1p).
