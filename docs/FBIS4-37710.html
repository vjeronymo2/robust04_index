BFN [Text] Aden, 22 June, KUNA -- At noon today, southern antiaircraft defenses shot down a northern warplane that was raiding Aden. A southern military source has said that the aircraft was trying to raid the television station, which is situated in the mountainous al-Tawahi region. He added that the northern warplanes conducted more raids on Aden a few hours after the airport was the target of a Katyusha barrage from a northern military launcher. He pointed out that the shells did not hit the airport, but a number of ordinary houses were damaged instead. The source did not give any details on the damage of the missile barrage. KUNA's correspondent, however, saw a number of fire engines heading to the areas close to the airport, which is situated in the Khur Maksar district in the heart of the city of Aden.
