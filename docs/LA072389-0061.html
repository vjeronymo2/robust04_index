July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE TONGUE MUST TRAVEL TO UNRAVEL WHAT THE MIND TRUSTS TO THE LUNGS.;

BREATHLESS

by Douglas Messerli

Through is always made

when up & down

delay admit

before behind

comes between

complete. Hesitation

is a distance

from the lean to

bar of belly.

That is why

the globe is covered.

From "Maxims From My Mother's Milk/Hymns to Him: A Dialogue" (Sun & Moon Press: $12.95; 69 pp.; 1-55713-031-0). Messerli is the author of "River to Rivet: A Poetic Trilogy" and the editor of " 'Language' Poetries," published by New Directions. He is also the director of Sun & Moon Press. Douglas Messerli.
