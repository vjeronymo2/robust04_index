930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / International Company News: BDA takes in Pounds 1.2m exceptional charges After an exceptional Pounds 1.2m against the carrying values of its stock and work, BDA Holdings incurred a loss of Pounds 1.16m in the year ended January 31. In the previous year this group of quantity surveyors, architects and construction management consultants, was just in the black at Pounds 18,000. Mr Brian Duker, chairman, said in the review of properties the value of the head office in Chingford was cut by Pounds 300,000. This was offset against the revaluation reserve. Turnover was Pounds 1.33m (Pounds 1.89m) and losses per share worked through at 6.4p (earnings 0.1p).
