BFN [Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma decree No. 99-1, signed by I.P. Rybkin, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma, and dated 22 April 1994: "On Elections to the St. Petersburg City Assembly"] [Text] Expressing concern over the results of the elections to the St. Petersburg City Assembly, which did not lead to the election of the aforesaid organ of representative power, and also over the violations of citizens' electoral rights, and taking into consideration the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Address on Ensuring the Electoral Rights of Citizens During Elections to the Legislative (Representative) Organs of State Power of Components of the Russian Federation and Organs of Local Self-Government dated 25 February 1994, the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma decrees: 1. To instruct the Russian Federation Prosecutor's Office and the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission to examine the question of compliance with legislation and implementation of court decisions by the mayor of the city of St. Petersburg during the elections to the St. Petersburg City Assembly in March 1994. 2. On the basis of the results of the examination of the aforesaid question, to instruct the Russian Federation president to adopt the necessary measures in respect of officials of the city of St. Petersburg. [signed] I.P. Rybkin, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma [dated] Moscow, 22 April 1994 No. 99-1
