Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nairobi, March 8 (AFP) -- A Saudi Arabian Airlines plane was hijacked early Tuesday in flight between Addis Ababa and Jeddah and made an emergency landing here, the airlines office in the Kenyan capital said. All 159 passengers were allowed to leave the plane after landing and only two crew members remained on board, the spokesman said. There were thought to be three or four hijackers, though there were no immediate details on their demands, according to the source.
