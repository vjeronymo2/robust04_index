920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / PLO wins West Bank 'election' The Palestine Liberation Organisation yesterday won a clear victory over Islamic fundamentalists in a rare election in the West Bank, writes Hugh Carnegy in Jerusalem. PLO-backed candidates nine of the 12 posts in the Nablus chamber of commerce against three for Islamic candidates. Elections to chambers of commerce have been almost the only test of political opinion since Israel scrapped municipal elections.
