BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 6 (KCNA) -- Vols. 1 and 2 of Part 1 "The Anti-Japanese Revolution" of the great leader President Kim Il-song's reminiscences "With the Century" were published in one book by the social science publishing house of China in April. A picture of President Kim Il-song standing on a lakeside is printed on the front cover of the book. Carried on the front and back covers of the book are the propositions of President Kim Il-song "Revolutionaries, believe in the people and rely on them at all times and you shall always emerge victorious; if you are forsaken by them, you will always fail. Let this be your maxim in your life and struggle" and "The destiny of a nation can be saved only through the unity and struggle of all the forces that love their country and treasure their nation."
