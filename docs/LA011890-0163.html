January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: COVINA;

PLANNING, ZONING FEES SET

The City Council approved five new fees for city planning and zoning services Monday, including a landscape inspection fee that will cost developers $650 or more per project.

The city will now charge $1,100 for zoning ordinance amendments, $450 for minor amendments to subdivision maps, $350 for changes to planned community development proposals and $65 for some transfers of alcoholic beverage licenses.

Previously, there had been no charge for the services.
