Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Veronika Romanenkova] [Text] Moscow, 8 Jan -- The Soyuz TM-18 spacecraft with three cosmonauts on board has just been launched from the Baykonur cosmodrome by the Russian military-space forces. This marks the start of the 15th main expedition to the Mir station with Viktor Afanasyev (ship's commander), Yuriy Usachev (flight engineer), and Valeriy Polyakov (cosmonaut-researcher, doctor).
