921124 FT 24 NOV 92 / UK Company News: James Cropper falls James Cropper, the Cumbria-based paper and packaging group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 595,000 for the six months to September 26. The outcome, from turnover little changed at Pounds 20.3m, compared with profits of Pounds 621,000 last time. Mr James Cropper, chairman, said trading remained difficult. The interim dividend is held at 0.975p, payable from earnings of 4.8p (5.3p) per share.
