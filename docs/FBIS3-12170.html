Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rabin made a comment about the massacre a short time ago in an address to the International Jewish Media Conference in Jerusalem. [Begin Rabin recording] So far, we know that he did it on his own but I am also ashamed about others that came out publicly and supported his act. He tried not just to kill innocent Palestinians while they were praying in a holy place. He joined with the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] and the Islamic Jihad in the purpose to kill the peace negotiations. [end recording]
