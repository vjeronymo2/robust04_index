BFN [Text] A responsible military source at the Command of the Armed Forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has said that the ruling Sanaa gang, at the same time that it claims it wishes to reach a cease-fire, is continuing its practice of misleading Arab and international public opinion. Yesterday evening it fired another ground-to-ground missile toward the capital, Aden. The source confirmed to the Yemeni news agency, SABA', that our vigilant ground defenses destroyed it in the air. Its parts fell in one of the farms in the Lahij Governorate, causing no deaths or material damage.
