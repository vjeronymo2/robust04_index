930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Le Pen trip sparks inquiry The European Parliament will carry out a travel funding inquiry after protests at plans by French extreme-right leader Mr Jean-Marie Le Pen to visit Edinburgh and other cities in Europe, Reuter reports from Strasbourg. The inquiry could lead to all political groups in the EC's 518-strong assembly being deprived of funds for meetings in any town or city besides Strasbourg, Brussels and Luxembourg, their traditional places of work.
