September 6, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

The Sunrise, 701 N. El Camino Real, San Clemente. Open daily from 6 a.m., Mondays and Tuesdays till 7 p.m., Wednesdays through Saturdays till 8 p.m., Sundays till 4:30 p.m. (714) 498-6379. Huge helpings, continual coffee refills, prompt and friendly service and a menu that features, but certainly isn't limited to, 20 different omelet combos help make this the brunch house of choice in South County. LAURA MICHAELIS
