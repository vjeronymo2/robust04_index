Lynda C. Thompson; Change in Bank Control Notices; Acquisitions of Shares of Banks or Bank Holding Companies; Correction This notice corrects a notice (FR Doc. 94-21210) published on page 44426 of the issue for Monday, August 29, 1994. Under the Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta heading, the entry for Lynda C. Thompson is revised to read as follows: 1. Lynda C. Thompson , Duluth, Georgia; to retain personally 9.8 percent and as trustee for the American Bancshares, Inc., ESOP, Marietta, Georgia, 2.4 percent of the voting shares of American Bancshares, Inc., Marietta, Georgia, and thereby indirectly control Cobb American Bank & Trust Company, Marietta, Georgia. Notificant also proposes to acquire an additional 1.4 percent in her personal capacity and 3.0 as trustee, of American Bancshares, Inc., for a total of 16.6 percent. Comments on this application must be received by September 12, 1994. Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, September 7, 1994. Jennifer J. Johnson, Deputy Secretary of the Board.
