930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / People: Finance moves - Indosuez Gartmore Asset Management (Japan) Peter Whelpton, formerly head of the Far East desk of Prudential Portfolio Managers, has been appointed president and investment director of INDOSUEZ GARTMORE ASSET MANAGEMENT (JAPAN); he replaces Andrew Fleming, who is appointed head of the Pacific Rim desk in London.
