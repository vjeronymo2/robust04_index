930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Corporate Services Corporate Services returned to profit in the six months to June 30 with Pounds 32,000 before tax, which the USM-traded company attributed to its efforts to integrate individual companies into a cohesive employment services group. The previous first half bore a loss of Pounds 466,000 and that year recorded a Pounds 1.55m deficit. Turnover from continuing operations rose 23 per cent to Pounds 16.1m (Pounds 13.1m) and operating profit of Pounds 162,000 compared with a Pounds 185,000 deficit.
