940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / OAS steps up pressure on Haiti The Organisation of American States yesterday stepped up pressure to restore democracy in Haiti by calling on members to reinforce an economic embargo, Reuter reports from Belem, Brazil. It urged suspension of commercial flights, a freeze on the military rulers' assets and a ban on financial transactions. Ms Harriet Babbitt, US ambassador to the OAS, said her government would implement the proposals.
