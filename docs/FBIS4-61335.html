BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz May 4 TASS -- "The provisions of the social concord agreement, signed in the Kremlin on April 28, must serve as guidelines for talks between the presidents and official delegations of North Ossetia and Ingushetia," North Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov said here today, addressing leaders of North Ossetian art unions, rectors of higher educational establishments, and representatives of the republic's national cultural centres. The North Ossetian leader went on record for the continuation of the dialogue with the Ingush side, but bluntly rejected the idea of a moratorium on border changes. In his opinion, it is necessary to completely delete from the agenda the question of recarving the borders of the North Caucasian area, because this may provoke a new tragedy in the region. The North Ossetian president dwelt at length on the forthcoming celebration of the 220th anniversary of Ossetia's accession to Russia and the 210th anniversary of the foundation of the city of Vladikavkaz. The jubilee celebrations, devoted to these dates, will be held from October 7 to October 15. Galazov said that these dates "have not lost their importance at all, but are assuming a new historical meaning in the light of modern political problems".
