Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, March 10 (MENA)--Investigations conducted by the Supreme State Security Prosecution with terrorists accused in placing explosives at premises of banks in Cairo and al-Jizah revealed that the accused belong to Islamist group involved in the attack on a movie theatre in Hulwan. The accused have confessed placing explosives at bank premises. They said they belonged to the group of a terrorist named Tharwat Hajjaj who was killed recently when he tried to open fire on policemen during his arrest.
