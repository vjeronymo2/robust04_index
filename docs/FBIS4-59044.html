BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 May (MENA)--The Arab League Secretariat General has gained Saudi Arabia's approval to receive the Arab League delegation that will travel to Saudi territory and from there to Yemen overland on a new mediation mission to stop the fighting in Yemen. The delegation, led by Major General Bayraqdar, assistant Arab League secretary general for military affairs, will leave Cairo Airport for Jeddah and from there travel overland to Sanaa across the Saudi border. Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has asked the delegation to travel to Yemen and meet Yemeni leaders in an effort to stop the fighting, resolve the differences, and replace the guns with dialogue. In a telephone contact with Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid, Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah agreed to guarantee the delegation's arrival in Sanaa.
