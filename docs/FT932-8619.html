930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / International Company News: IPounds 1m loss at Ryan Hotels RYAN HOTELS results for the 15 months to January 31 revealed that the group incurred pre-tax losses of IPounds 1.1m (Pounds 1.09m) against a profit of IPounds 975,085 (Pounds 959,000) for the year to October 31 1991. Turnover for the 15 months amounted to IPounds 25.2m (IPounds 19.4m for year). Mr Conor McCarthy, chairman, said that as the additional three months, November to January, is traditionally the most difficult trading quarter, the loss before interest for that period of IPounds 18,786 was encouraging Interest charges for the 15 months were IPounds 3.35m (IPounds 1.72m). Losses per share amounted to 2p (1.11p) and a final dividend of 0.5p makes a total of 1p (same for year).
