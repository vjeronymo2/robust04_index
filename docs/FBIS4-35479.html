BFN [Excerpts] Head of State Liamine Zeroual received at the presidency this afternoon the European Union Troika delegation which arrived in Algeria this morning. The delegation consists of Mr. Willy Claes, Belgian deputy prime minister and foreign minister; Mr. George Papandreou, Greek minister of state for foreign affairs; and Mr. Dieter Kastrup, state secretary in the German Ministry for Foreign Affairs. [passage omitted] After the meeting, Mr. George Papandreou made the following statement to Cherif Mamri: [Begin Papandreou recording, in English with superimposed Arabic translation] We spoke frankly and clearly with the president and we exchanged views. We also expressed our support for the economic reform program which Algeria has begun, and for the political national dialogue. We hope that in the future there will be more cooperation between Algeria and the European Union. Our visit today to Algeria is merely an encouraging start. [end recording]
