940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Sears - Other Companies Exceptional restructuring costs obscured last year's results from the retail group. But, on a directly comparable basis, it is forecast tomorrow to report an increase in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 39.5m to about Pounds 46m. Interest charges are likely to be down and both British Shoe Corporation and Selfridges department store are expected to show strong performances, but the picture from the clothing multiples is likely to be mixed.
