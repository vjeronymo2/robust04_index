Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Oleg Stepanenko report: "A `Tainted' Greeting from the U.S. President"] [Text] The number four children's hospital in Minsk, which received a visit from Mrs. Hilary Clinton, rejoiced at a gift of drugs -- prostafilin -- that it received from her, but the celebrations were short-lived. It seems that the these antibiotics had passed their use-by date back in 1992. The doctors were afraid to give children medicine that was not absolutely fresh. They decided to "pass up" the gift from the U.S. President's wife and send it to a hospital for adults instead.
