BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali has announced that the Deutsche Welle employees left behind in the Rwandan capital of Kigali are to be evacuated by UN troops. His information was that the situation in the vicinity of the Deutsche Welle relay station is still quiet at present, Butrus-Ghali said in Bonn today. It is planned to evacuate the Germans with the aid of helicopters. German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party) expressly thanked the UN secretary general for the UN support in the evacuation of German citizens from Rwanda.
