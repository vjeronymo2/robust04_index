March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WEEK'S FILM PICKS BY THE CRITICS: STAND BY ME

This is Rob Reiner's compassionate, perfectly performed look at the real heart of youth, a treasure told in the form of a memoir, as established writer Richard Dreyfuss remembers back to the pivotal summer of 1959. Adapted from a Stephen King story and starring River Phoenix, Wil Wheaton, Corey Feldman and Jerry O'Connell.
