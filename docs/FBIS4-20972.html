Colombian Drug Trafficker PA2603023194 Panama City Circuito RPC Television in Spanish 2300 GMT 25 Mar 94 PA2603023194 Panama City Circuito RPC Television Spanish BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] The Supreme Court of Justice, in a plenary session, has studied the case of drug trafficker (Jacobo Lerner Zimmerman) in detail. Chief Magistrate Arturo Hoyos believes that keeping him in jail is the right decision. [Begin Hoyos recording] The habeas corpus we reviewed refers to crimes (Mr. Lerner Zimmerman) is accused of committing in the Republic of Panama. Regarding said accusations, the Supreme Court finds that there is sufficient evidence to hold this person in connection with these drug related crimes against the public. [end recording] It is noteworthy that (Jacobo Lerner Zimmerman), a Colombian citizen involved in drug trafficking activities, is wanted by the United States for the same crime. The Supreme Court, however, has already denied an extradition request. [Begin Hoyos recording] There is a ruling by the Criminal Court that denies the extradition request, and that ruling must be executed. [end recording] In view of this Supreme Court decision, (Jacobo Lerner Zimmerman) will have to spend a long time in jail unless he escapes, which is not uncommon in the country's jails among people connected with drug trafficking.
