Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The proceedings of the extraordinary congress of the National Liberal Party [PNL], which was convened by the initiative committee for organizing the congress, began at the National History Museum Hall this morning. The congress is being attended by 475 delegates representing 34 counties, all the organizations of the Bucharest Municipality and Ilfov agricultural sector, by representatives of certain political formations, cultural and socioeconomic figures not involved in political activity, and journalists. The congress proceedings, presided over by Petre Dragomir, member of the initiative committee, began with the presentation of the political report, which specifies that the aim of this congress is to relaunch a political project of regenerating national liberalism and to elect a leadership made up of competent people characterized by genuine political legitimacy. Greeting messages were presented at the congress by representatives of the Liberal Party '93, the Ecological Movement of Romania, the Romanian Humanist Party, and the Republican Party. At this time, the congress proceedings continue with discussions on the political report.
