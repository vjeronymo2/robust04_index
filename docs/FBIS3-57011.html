Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 22 (SNARK)--Today the Azerbaijani troops have resumed their attacks after a days respite towards the Armenian positions of Madaghis and Akop Kamari [names as received] villages. By 10 o'clock violent fights went on there. The Karabakh Defence Army Headquarters told SNARK the Karabakh forces reject the enemy's attacks. The positional fights take place at the other parts of the front.
