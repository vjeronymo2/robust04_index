Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (INA)--[Iraqi] Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met at the Arab League headquarters in Cairo today with Mauritanian Foreign Minister Mohamed Salem Ould Lekhal on the sidelines of the 101st Session of the Arab League Council. The talks dealt with bilateral relations, strengthening joint cooperation, a number of Arab issues, and the agenda of the Arab League Council. Al-Sahhaf informed his Mauritanian counterpart of Iraq's efforts to lift the embargo on its people and the Security Council's stand toward these efforts. Al-Sahhaf also informed the Mauritanian counterpart of the positive move by a number of the permanent members of the Security Council in Iraq's favor and their understanding of its viewpoint.
