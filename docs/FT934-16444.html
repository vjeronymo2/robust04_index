931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / UK Company News: FKI in Pounds 900,000 buy from John Wood The engineering offshoot of FKI, the West Yorkshire-based electrical engineering and electronic components company, has paid Pounds 900,000 cash for the business and certain assets of Wood Group Industrial Controls from John Wood Group, as a going concern. Aberdeen-based Wood will trade as FKI Industrial Controls.
