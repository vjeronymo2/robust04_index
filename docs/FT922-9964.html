920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Dana Exploration Dana Exploration, the Dublin-based onshore mineral exploration company quoted on the USM, incurred an increased net loss of IPounds 82,664 (Pounds 75,000) for the six months to July 31, compared with IPounds 42,694. The loss comprised an exceptional IPounds 43,338 (IPounds 21,044) incurred on licences and administration costs of IPounds 39,334 (IPounds 21,690).
