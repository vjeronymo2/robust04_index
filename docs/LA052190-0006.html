May 21, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MILLS COLLEGE

Dichotomy: As a former student politician, I find some satisfaction that the students of Mills were successful in directing their institution to remain all female (Part A, May 19).

As a human, however, I am appalled at the utter hypocrisy of their feminism. OK, girls, you've got it. Now just leave my men's club alone!

CARL G. HOKANSON

Encino
