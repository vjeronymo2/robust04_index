BFN [Text] The Abu Sayyaf extremist group has claimed responsibility for the Sunday bombing at the commercial center in Zamboanga City in southern Philippines. Xeroxed letters reportedly from a certain Abu Jihad were delivered by an unidentified youth yesterday afternoon. Thirty-six people were wounded in the bomb blast. The explosive was believed planted inside or beneath a car parked beside Shopper Central. The car was a total wreck. The explosion was so powerful, it was heard by residents within a five kilometer radius.
