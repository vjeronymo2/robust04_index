Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Banja Luka, 23 Mar (TANJUG) -- A UN convoy bound for Maglaj was stopped today near the village of Crna Rijeka west of Novi Seher because the road was mined, we have learned from Srpska republic army sources. The convoy was stopped and all personnel were warned that the road was mined. The drivers and crew left the vehicles, and the Srpska republic army provided safety for the convoy to avoid the possibility of theft and plunder. According to Serb military sources, claims published by foreign media that the Srpska army plundered the convoy were incorrect. This is yet another attempt at vilifying and accusing the Serb side of a lack of cooperation, the Srpska republic army sources say.
