930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Cable networks continue to grow THE NUMBER of homes that could be served by cable television networks increased by 100,000 in May to 2,484,255. The actual number subscribing to cable services rose by 6,587 to 464,997, figures from the Cable Television Association show. The number of telephone subscribers on cable rose 13,488 over the month to 170,178.
