940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: Second round contenders emerge With two days' first-round play to go (Groups F and D), the line-up for the second round, starting on Saturday, is emerging. So far, 12 teams have booked second-round spots: Romania, Switzerland, the US, Brazil, Sweden, Germany, Spain, Argentina, Mexico, Ireland, Italy and Belgium, with four more to be confirmed. From the second round, matches are decided by knock-out. The top two teams in each group, plus the next four best performers, play in the second round: 16 in all. Second round: July 2: Switzerland vs Spain (Washington, 9.30pm; all times British Summer Time). July 2: Germany vs a 3rd-place finisher (Chicago, 6pm). July 3: Romania vs a 3rd-place finisher (Los Angeles, 9.30pm). July 3: Group F runners-up vs Sweden (Dallas, 6pm). July 4: Brazil vs a 3rd-place finisher (San Francisco, 8.30pm). July 4: Group F winners vs Ireland (Orlando, 5pm). July 5: Group D winners vs a 3rd-place finisher (Boston, 6pm). July 5: Mexico vs Group D runners-up (New Jersey, 9.30pm. Quarter-finals: weekend of July 9-10, in Boston, Dallas, New Jersey and San Francisco; semi-finals: Wednesday, July 13 (New Jersey and Los Angeles); final: Sunday, July 17 (Los Angeles).
