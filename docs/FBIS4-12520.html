BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has strongly criticized Israeli maneuvers in the peace talks and its disregard for the timetables which had been agreed in accordance with the declaration of principles signed between the two sides on 13 September last. He warned of the dangerous results which could ensue from these maneuvers. The Palestinian president expressed this stance when he received in Tunis the delegation of the enquiry commission representing journalists accredited to the United Nations from various countries, which is touring the Middle East.
