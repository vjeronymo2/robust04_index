940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Spain mobile phone contest The Spanish government has prepared the way for competition in mobile telephones, approving a set of rules for digital cellular networks, writes David White in Madrid. Spain is due to invite bids later this month for a licence to compete with the national operator Telefonica. Five consortia of Spanish and foreign companies are set to enter the contest, which is due to be decided by the end of November. The winner will have to pay an entry fee estimated at up to Pta100bn (Pounds 487.8m).
