BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has said: Our aim is to preserve the country. What we are seeking through our stance is the best interests of the people. Addressing large public delegations that flocked to his residence from various areas in Lebanon today, he said: Our assets are the good people of this country. The country has had enough of 17 years of war. The country has been hijacked for 17 years, and now is the time for our good people to govern, and you represent these good people. There are some who have paid the war bill and we will not allow anyone to make you pay the peace bill. Al-Hariri made these remarks when he received public figures and delegations that visited Quraytim Palace to show support and solidarity with the prime minister. At 1900 today al-Hariri received Ministers Bisharah Mirhij, 'Umar Misqawi, Elie Hubayqah, Hagop Demerdjian, 'Adil Qirtas, Muhammad Ghaziri, and As'ad Rizq and Deputies Wajih al-Ba'arini, Zuhayr al-'Ubaydi, As'ad Harmush, and Farid Mkari.
