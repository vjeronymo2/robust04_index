930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Johnston Press Johnston Press, the Edinburgh-based newspaper publishing group, increased 1992 pre-tax profits by 32 per cent to Pounds 9.76m, with just over half the increase coming from acquisitions. Turnover rose 16 per cent to Pounds 71.7m, of which all but 1.6 per cent was due to acquisitions. A final dividend of 4p makes a 6.25p (5.5p) total. Earnings per share were 22.7p (17.9p).
