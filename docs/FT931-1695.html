930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Derwent Valley Derwent Valley Holdings, the property group, reported a 9 per cent gain from Pounds 821,000 to Pounds 896,000 in pre-tax profits for the 1992 year. The profits were achieved despite a write down of the trading stock amounting to Pounds 406,000, which compared with a net loss for the trading activity of Pounds 20,000 in the preceding year. Earnings per share increased 67 per cent from 6.1p to 10.2p but the total dividend is held at 8.6p with a proposed unchanged final of 5.7p.
