Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Chairman of the North Ossetian Council of Ministers Sergey Khetagurov intends to ask the republic's Interior Ministry to clarify circumstances behind the murder in the summer of 1993 of Viktor Polyanichko, head of the temporary authority on the territory of North Ossetia and Ingushetia. Khetagurov made the statement Sunday addressing a news conference in Vladikavkaz. The statement was caused by the article in the MEGAPOLIS-EXPRESS weekly, which says about the the North Ossetian prime minister's complicity to Polyanichko's assassination. Khetagurov characterized the publication as an outrageous statement which deliberately appeared during the election campaign in North Ossetia and Khetagurov stands for presidency. The statement by the MEGAPOLIS-EXPRESS weekly was denied at the news conference in Vladikavkaz by a senior official of the criminal department at the Russian Interior Ministry, Lieutenant General Vladimir Kolesnichenko.
