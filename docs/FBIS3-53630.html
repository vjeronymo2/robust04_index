Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Radio News will begin work soon, Mr. [Mohammad] Hashemi, head of the Islamic Republic of Iran's Voice and Vision [radio and television], announced this morning in a gathering of personnel from the Voice and Vision's public relations office. He added: Radio News will begin work on the occasion of the blessed 10 Days of Dawn [anniversary of the revolution during the first ten days of February]. Mr. Hashemi said with regards to the need for the creation of Radio News: Because news tends to be generated constantly and because current bulletins are broadcast at specific times, Radio News was felt to be needed, and work on the planning and implementation was set into motion. As a result, this radio will begin work soon. According to this report, Radio News will broadcast three to five minutes of political, economic, sports, scientific, and cultural news every 15 minutes. Radio News will broadcast between 0630 in the morning and 2100. The Voice and Vision chief added: At the beginning, Radio News will be received within the boundaries of greater Tehran; efforts will be made to increase its range in the future. Our aim is that much of the news we cannot use in current bulletins because of time limitations will be broadcast on this radio station for the information of our compatriots.
