Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 21 Mar--Issues of European security and the international situation were the main topics of talks held today by the foreign ministers of Poland and Ukraine, Andrzej Olechowski and Anatoliy Zlenko. The talks, scheduled for half an hour, lasted twice that long. Plenary talks between both delegations began after the ministers' meeting. These talks focus on bilateral political and economic relations. At the outset of the talks, Minister Zlenko said that Poland and Ukraine are two great neighbors who might play an important role in the region.
