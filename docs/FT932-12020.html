930430 FT 30 APR 93 / Accountancy firms merge COOPER LANCASTER, and Brewers, two medium-sized accountancy firms, are to combine into a single partnership from tomorrow in the latest in a series of recent mergers in the profession. The new firm, to be called Cooper Lancaster Brewers, will have 24 partners, 118 professional staff and 37 support staff. It is projecting fee income for 1993-94 of Pounds 6.4m.
