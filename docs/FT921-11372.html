920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Warner Estate falls to Pounds 7.6m A shortfall in second-half profits from Pounds 4.28m to Pounds 3.71m left Warner Estate Holdings, the real estate owner and dealer, with pre-tax figures 5 per cent lower at Pounds 7.64m for the year ended September 30 1991, against Pounds 8.01m previously. Turnover slipped from Pounds 10.4m to Pounds 10.2m. Earnings per share were marginally ahead at 10.8p (10.73p), while the dividend is raised to 10p (9p) with a recommended final of 6.75p.
