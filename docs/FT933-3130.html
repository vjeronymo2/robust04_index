930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: EBC Profits of EBC, the Exeter-based construction and development group, improved from Pounds 481,000 to Pounds 562,000 pre-tax for the half year ended June 30. External turnover of Pounds 28.4m compared with Pounds 25.3m. Trading conditions did not improve and competition for available work remained fierce. The interim dividend is being maintained at 1.75p from earnings of 3.03p (2.9p).
