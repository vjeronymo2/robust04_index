August 2, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"I thought he was just lovesick, which I think he was. He was terribly insistent on being let in. 'Rebecca Schaeffer' was every other word.'

-- Jack Egger, chief of security for The Burbank Studio, recalling that Robert John Bardo, accused of killing actress Rebecca Schaeffer, visited the studio where she was taping a television show two years ago and begged to see her.
