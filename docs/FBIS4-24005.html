BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers has approved the subsidies for members of the National Elections Commission [CNE], which range from 250 and 3,000 contos. At the district level, CNE members will receive 250 contos, the CNE deputy chairman 600 contos, and the CNE chairman 800 contos. At the provincial level, CNE members will receive 500 contos, the deputy chairman 1,000 contos, and the chairman 1,500 contos. The CNE chairman will receive 3,000 contos, the CNE deputy chairman 2,600, and CNE members 1,100 contos. Apart from the subsidies, the CNE members will continue to receive their salaries at their workplaces. The subsidies are in accordance with the government's original proposal.
