940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Dares Estates on the recovery road Dares Estates, the property investment and development group, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 369,000 for the 1993 year, against losses of Pounds 22.4m in the previous 12 months. The accounts, again prepared on a going concern basis, showed operating profits of Pounds 5.1m (losses of Pounds 9.21m) from turnover of Pounds 6.84m (Pounds 5.53m). Profits on the termination of operations contributed Pounds 3.3m, against losses of Pounds 3.18m. Interest payable amounted to Pounds 8.76m (Pounds 10.43m). Losses per share were reduced from 66.54p to 0.69p.
