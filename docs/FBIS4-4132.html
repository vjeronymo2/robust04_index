BFN [Excerpts] The provincial education department and cultural department jointly held the premiere of the province-wide patriotic movies month for primary and secondary schools at the Qinghai Opera House yesterday. Present at the premiere were the individuals in charge of the provincial leading group for three educations [education in patriotism, collectivism, and communism], the provincial radio and television broadcasting department, the provincial cultural department, the provincial education department, the provincial working committee, and the Xining city education committee. During patriotic movies month, which will last for a full month, 100 fine patriotic movies will be shown. [passage omitted] Jiang Huandong, deputy director of the provincial education department, gave an opening speech on behalf of the sponsoring organizations. He expressed the hope that the education and cultural departments at all levels would clearly define their duties and responsibilities, work closely together, and thus ensure the smooth progress of the patriotic movies month.
