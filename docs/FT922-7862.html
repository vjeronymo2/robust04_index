920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / Observer: Spoken for The Iron Lady is still stealing the show over Europe, as witness her performance at the International Securities Market Association's conference in Munich. Appearing alongside German finance minister Theo Waigel, Mrs Thatcher advanced the thesis that all EC convergence means is other countries getting bigger subsidies from Germany and Britain. Isn't that right, she asked, turning to Waigel for support - only to find he had slipped away. 'Well,' she said, unperturbed. 'If he were still here, he'd say yes.'
