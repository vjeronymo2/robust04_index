Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Li Peng Arrives at Shekou by Boat, To Visit Shenzhen for Three Days"] [Text] Chinese State Council Premier Li Peng arrived at Shekou, Shenzhen by boat from Zhuhai this morning. It has been reported that during his three-day inspection tour of Shenzhen, Li Peng and his party will attend a ceremony to mark the operation of the No. 1 generating unit of the Daya Nuclear Power Station the day after tomorrow (6 February). Previously, Li Peng and his party conducted inspection activities in the Zhuhai Special Economic Zone for five days.
