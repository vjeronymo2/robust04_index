BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 17 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on June 16 sent a message of greetings to France Albert Rene, president of the Republic of Seychelles, on the occasion of the national day of the republic. Saying that the Seychellois people are actively striving to achieve national unity and the prosperity of the country, President Kim Il-song in the message expressed the belief that the excellent relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries would grow stronger and develop.
