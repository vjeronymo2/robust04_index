BFN [Excerpt] Joint attacks launched by Karadzic's fascists and Abdic's phalanges of traitors [Muslim forces of self-proclaimed autonomous province of western Bosnia founded by Fikret Abdic] were intensified today. Chetniks repeated their artillery attacks on the central part of the Bosanska Krupa area this afternoon [words indistinct]. Since last night members of rebel-traitor units [Muslims loyal to Abdic] have been launching artillery attacks on the Liskovac, Todorovo, and Varoska Rijeka areas. On several occasions they were backed by chetniks from the so-called Serb Krajina in shelling the areas mentioned. The focus of today's attacks was the Medik medical equipment factory in Skokovi. Several attempts at an infantry breakthrough were made during the day and this afternoon a sabotage group from Trzac [town held by forces loyal to Abdic] was destroyed. Many people were wounded today and one person was killed. [passage omitted]
