Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Morozov] [Text] Bucharest, 15 Mar TASS -- Ukraine plans to have friendly relations with Romania and settle all problems in a political way, Ukrainian Defence Minister Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy said today upon his arrival in Bucharest with a Ukrainian military delegation at the invitation of the Romanian defence minister. The visit to last till 17 March includes negotiations with Romanian Defence Minister Gheorghe Tinca, head of the Romanian Armed Forces General Staff Lieutenant-General Dumitru Cioflina and meetings with President Ion Iliescu, Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu and Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu. The sides are expected to sign a military cooperation agreement. "We had long planned to sign a bilateral accord on military cooperation, especially as we have a border running over 600 kilometers (375 miles) with Romania," said the Ukrainian official. "The agreement will help military interaction, including joint training of personnel."
