940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / Observer: Waterproof Britain's John Major and his Malaysian counterpart, Mahathir Mohamad, may not get on but they share one feeling - that they are being hounded unfairly by the British press. Indeed, John Major used the 50th anniversary of Canning House in London the other day to trot out a hardy favourite of persecuted politicians: what would the headlines say, asked the PM, if he rowed into the middle of the Serpentine, found himself marooned and walked back over the water to dry land? Answer: 'John Major Can't Swim.'
