BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 22 Apr (INA)--Nabil Sha'th, the Palestinian president's adviser, has criticized the evasion by the U.S. Administration and other Western countries of their pledges to extend needed financial aid to the Palestinian self-rule area in Gaza and Jericho. Sha'th told the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY correspondent in Cairo yesterday that the United States and other Western countries halted the extension of the pledged financial aid to the Palestinian side. Expressing regret over this deceitful retreat, he said that reality has shown that these pledges were merely promises. The Palestinian official asserted that, for its part, the Zionist entity has also tried to evade its pledges in the Washington agreement on Palestinian self-rule in Jericho and Gaza. [passage omitted]
