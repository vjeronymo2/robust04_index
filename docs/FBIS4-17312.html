BFN [Text] According to BELAPAN, Colonel General Pavel Kazlowski, Belarusian defense minister, signed an order activating on 25 April the statute defining conditions for contract service by privates, sergeants, sailors, and master sergeants in the Belarusian Armed Forces. Thus, all Belarusian citizens under 35 years of age who have already been on active military service and are still subject to call-up, can sign a contract with the Defense Ministry for two years and serve in the Army according to their military or civilian profession. The same applies to privates and sergeants who are currently awaiting transfer to the reserves. Experts estimate that the monthly salary of a contract serviceman this April will be about 390,000 rubles, while a sergeant will make around 509,000 Belarusian hares.
