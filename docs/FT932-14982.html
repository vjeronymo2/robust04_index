930416 FT 16 APR 93 / World News in Brief: Srebrenica bombarded Serb artillery and infantry pounded the besieged town of Srebrenica and radio hams reported that Moslem defenders had been driven back to within 1 1/4 miles of the town. Earlier a UN convoy left with only five elderly women on board after local leaders blocked a mass refugee evacuation.
