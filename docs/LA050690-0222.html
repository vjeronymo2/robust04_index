May 6, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SUNSET LEAGUE BASEBALL;

HUNTINGTON BEACH BEATS OCEAN VIEW

Mike Chavira's bunt single scored pinch-runner Harold Zamora in the bottom of the 10th inning to give Huntington Beach High School a 7-6 victory over sixth-ranked Ocean View in a Sunset League game Saturday at Golden West College.

Josh Faur pitched five scoreless innings of relief for the victory. J.P. Rizzo singled in a run in the seventh to tie the score, and Jesus Espinoza had three RBIs for Huntington Beach.

Scott Caringella homered for Ocean View.

Huntington Beach is 6-7 and Ocean View is 11-2 in league play.

In another league game:

Edison 7, Marina 4 -- Jeff Daniel had a two-run single in the third inning and picked up another RBI in the seventh on a squeeze bunt for visiting Edison (17-6, 8-5). Phil Jensen pitched the final three innings, allowing no hits and striking out three, for his third save.
