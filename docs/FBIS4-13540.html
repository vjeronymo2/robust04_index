BFN [Text] The foreign affairs ministers of the Islamic Republic of Iran and Ireland have expressed concern over the continued killing of the inhabitants of the city of Gorazde. Mr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati, our country's foreign affairs minister, today spoke on the telephone with Mr. Dick Spring and expressed concern over the continuation of the Serb crimes against the defenseless city of Gorazde and called for practical action to stop the killing of the city's inhabitants. Dick Spring, deputy prime minister and Irish foreign minister, also expressed regret at the Gorazde tragedy and announced the readiness of his country to help establish a cease-fire in that city.
