Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Polish Embassy in Lithuania has sent a note of protest to the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry regarding the statements made by the chairman of the Parliamentary Regional Problems Commission, Romualdas Ozolas. Commenting on the recently-reached breakthrough in Lithuanian-Polish talks over the good neighborhood treaty MP Romualdas Ozolas had stated in the LIETUVOS RYTAS daily that -- quote -- Poland will be glad because of its own freewill Lithuania will apparently accept a large package of Polish claims to Lithuanian sovereignty in its south-east and southern territory -- unquote.
