Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 6 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In the years from 1994-1997 with the participation of the Asian Development Bank there would be developed a number of new projects directed at solution of burning questions of the Mongolian economy. The major part of the credits and assistance of this weighty international organization will be used for a development of the agriculture and providing technological renovation for thermoelectric powers of Mongolia. In the new year for the purpose of providing the technical reconstruction of the thermoelectric power station the bank will grant to Mongolia some 35 million USD [U.S. dollars]. The Asian Development Bank will also finance the projects in the sphere of telecommunication, ecology construction of motorways and health protection of the population.
