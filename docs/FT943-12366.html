940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Ford's exports from US rise 75% Ford said it shipped 57,215 cars and trucks from North America to overseas markets in the first six months of 1994, up 75 per cent from a year earlier, Reuter reports from Dearborn. Ford said it also shipped 9,676 component kits of vehicles for assembly at overseas locations in the same period. While shipments were up in virtually all regions, Ford said the single largest increase was to Mexico. It shipped 8,470 cars and trucks to Mexico, up from 698 a year ago. Exports to Europe and the Asia-Pacific region each were up about 85 per cent, the Middle East was up 37 per cent, and Caribbean and Latin America shipments rose 28 per cent, Ford said.
