BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 4 (KCNA) -- The birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song was celebrated in different countries. Cultural evenings were held in Dar es Salaam of Tanzania and by the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru. Speaking at the cultural evening, Angel Castro Lavarello, president of the National Executive Committee of the union, said: "The great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song is the sun of the Korean nation with a 5,000-year history. "He is the great leader who founded the chuche idea and has devoted his whole life to the national liberation and human emancipation. "He is the ever-victorious, iron-willed brilliant commander, standard-bearer of the world revolution and veteran of revolution and construction who defeated two imperialisms in one generation and brought a new era of independence against imperialism." DPRK film shows and photo exhibitions, lecture meetings, book exhibitions and other functions to celebrate April 15 were organised in Denmark, Malta, Mozambique and by the maritime regional Russia- Korea Friendship Association, the Zimbabwean National Liberation War Veterans Committee, the Belgian International Solidarity Centre, the guard unit of the Angolan president, the Senegalese Socialist Party, the Zimbabwe-Korea Solidarity and Friendship Association, the National Committee of the National Movement of the Genuine Lumumbist Combatants of Zaire, the Cairo film centre of Egypt, the Yara cinema house in Havana, Cuba, the Sandinist Centre of workers of Nicaragua, the Imazic company of Algeria, in Zashikan County of Ghana, at the Chollima Institute of Agricultural Science of Tanzania and in Sancti Spiritus Province of Cuba.
