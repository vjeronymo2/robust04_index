940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Investment trust funds rise - At a glance Savings made through investment trusts savings schemes rose by 41 per cent in the first quarter of this year to Pounds 67.9m, compared with Pounds 48.1m in the first quarter of 1993. In the financial year 1993-94 as a whole, they rose 91 per cent to Pounds 259.4m. This was in addition to the Pounds 293.7m invested through personal equity plans in 1993-94.
