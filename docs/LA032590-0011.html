March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MALCOLM X

I am white. For a long time my color has hampered me from voicing my opinion of Malcolm X.

I have never been against his teachings.

On the contrary, I believe that his views of black suppression and how to remedy the situation are just.

Thank you, Charisse Jones, for the clear and precise article "The Rebirth of Malcolm X." I hope that my white contemporaries have taken the time to read it in full, instead of banishing it to the wastepaper basket -- as has been done many times before with anything that has contradicted popular white beliefs.

MONICA BLUMENTHAL

Los Angeles
