941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / International Company News: Walt Disney completes Euro Disney share sale Walt Disney has completed the sale of 74.6m shares in Euro Disney, the operator of the theme park near Paris, to Saudi Prince Al-waleed bin Talal, Reuter reports from Burbank, California. As a result of the deal, which was part of a restructuring unveiled on June 1, Disney's stake in Euro Disney has fallen to 40 per cent from 49 per cent. The stake held by Prince Al-waleed and United Saudi Commercial Bank, of which he is chairman, rises to 24.6 per cent, Disney said.
