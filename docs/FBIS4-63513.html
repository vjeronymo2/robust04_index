BFN [Text] In connection with reports by Russian mass media about the supposed redeployment of Ukrainian military units and formations in the direction of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, the press service of Ukrzaliznytsya [Ukrainian Railways] made a statement on 22 May. In particular, it says that over the last 10 days Ukrzaliznytsya has not received or examined any requests from the Defense Ministry or the National Guard of Ukraine to move military cargoes, personnel, or military equipment in the direction of the Crimea.
