920424 FT 24 APR 92 / UK Company News: VTR improves to Pounds 276,000 VTR, the video editing group, made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 276,000 in the six months to February 29. That was down on the Pounds 432,000 of the comparable period, but improved over the Pounds 169,000 of last year's second half. Turnover came to Pounds 3.53m (Pounds 3.18m) and operating profit to Pounds 413,000 (Pounds 556,000). Earnings were 2.4p (3.7p) and the interim dividend is held at 1.2p.
