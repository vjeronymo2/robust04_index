920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / UK Company News: NHL merges finance and treasury sides National Home Loans, the mortgage lender and consumer finance group, is to merge its treasury and finance operations under the control of Mr Nigel Terrington. The merger reflects the reduced size of the company. Mr Terrington's appointment follows the resignation of Mr Tony Moir, a leading figure in the company since its establishment in 1986, as finance director. In July NHL reported losses of Pounds 85.9m after making bad debt provisions of Pounds 79.4m, and passed its dividend. Mr Jonathan Perry, chairman, denied that Mr Moir's departure was linked to a boardroom row. 'It is arguable that there is not the necessity for both posts,' he said.
