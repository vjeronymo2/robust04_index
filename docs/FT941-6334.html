940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / Observer: Frozen out A bored Eurocrat was seen to be penning a little ditty during the now extended talks on extending the European Union. It's called The Nordic Bride, and sums up the state of play. 'When first we kissed, you said, my dear, that you were well endowed; Your GDP, it was so clear, made you extremely proud. But years have passed, and now you say that you are rich no more; Our union may incur delay if you appear so poor]'
