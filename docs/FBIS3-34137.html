Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres confirmed today that he would meet PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat 7 February in Cairo. The meeting between the two men was supposed to take place Sunday but was postponed. In their meeting, Peres and 'Arafat will try to overcome the last obstacles to the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] withdrawal from the Gaza Strip and Jericho. Peres added that he expected that the agreement signed with the PLO in September would be implemented in full by the end of 1994.
