920411 FT 11 APR 92 / Sri Lanka car bomb kills 10 A bomb exploded in the suburbs of the Sri Lankan capital Colombo last night, killing at least 10 people and injuring 35, Reuter reports from Colombo. Police said they suspected the separatist Tamil Tigers placed the bomb in a parked car. Earlier at least 45 people were killed by a bomb on a bus and in two ambushes in eastern Sri Lanka yesterday. The authorities again blamed Tamil separatists.
