940819 FT 19 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Cheshire Building Society at Pounds 7.89m Pre-tax profits were little changed at Cheshire Building Society in the six months to June 30. On total income of Pounds 17.3m (Pounds 17.6m) and after provisions of Pounds 2m (Pounds 2.25m) for loan losses the pre-tax outcome was Pounds 7.89m (Pounds 8.1m). Total assets at the period end amounted to Pounds 1.38m (Pounds 1.31m).
