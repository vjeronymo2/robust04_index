January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Technology Marketing Inc.: The Irvine-based technology company lost $644,041 for the third quarter of fiscal 1990, contrasted with a year-earlier profit of $269,330.

Revenue for the quarter ended Nov. 30 declined 44%, to $1.5 million from $2.6 million. For the nine-month period, TMI recorded a net loss of $379,900, contrasted with net income of $894,675 for the like period last year. Revenue decreased 30%, to $6.1 million from $8.7 million.
