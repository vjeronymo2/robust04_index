Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the recent bomb attacks at the law court and the Northern League office, a bomb exploded last night at the provincial office of the National Agency for Social Security on Piazza Insurrezione [in Padua, northern Italy]. A rudimentary device exploded at around 0300 shattering the two large windows of the office, damaging the ceiling, and one of the main doors. Police went quickly to the scene after being alerted by local residents who were woken up by the blast.
