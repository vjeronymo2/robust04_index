940314 FT 14 MAR 94 / UK Company News: DCS rights to fund purchases DCS, the computer software supplier, is calling for Pounds 1.05m net via a rights issue of 2.33m new ordinary shares on a 1-for-3 basis at 55p a share, to fund two acquisitions. These are Motis, and the DMS division of GSI UK - suppliers of computer systems and services to the automotive industry. Motis will receive Pounds 300,000 in cash plus the allotment to certain vendors of 126,866 new ordinary shares worth Pounds 85,000. The maximum consideration for DMS is Pounds 400,000.
