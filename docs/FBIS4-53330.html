BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 18 KYODO -- Finance Minister Hirohisa Fujii said Wednesday [18 May] his ministry's goal of holding bond revenues to less than 5 percent of the government's general account budget in fiscal 1999 may be difficult to achieve. Fujii, answering questions before the Budget Committee of the House of Representatives, said the goal is a "target to strive for." The ministry published the goal in a medium-term fiscal outlook it submitted to the committee Tuesday.
