BFN [Excerpt] On the instructions of President Boris Yeltsin the official text of the draft agreement on civic peace in Russia he signed was sent to the media on Tuesday evening. According to a well-informed source in the presidential administration, the document was simultaneously submitted to 25 potential sides to the agreement -- political parties and public movements. The same source said a working group which drafted the agreement is expected to hold a session on April 18-20 chaired by Yeltsin. It will discuss proposals and amendments suggested by political groups and individuals to work out the final version. Earlier the president named April 28 as the possible date for signing the agreement. [passage omitted]
