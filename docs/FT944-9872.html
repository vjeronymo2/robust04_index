941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Sane to build Pounds 6m research centre Sane, the mental health charity, is to build a Pounds 6m centre in Oxford for research into psychiatric illness. The Prince of Wales International Centre for Research into Schizophrenia and Depression will be funded mainly by overseas donors, including King Fahd of Saudi Arabia and Mrs Stamatia Xylas, the Greek shipowner, who have each given Pounds 1.75m. Its planned research programme includes the genetics of schizophrenia, signs in childhood of future psychotic illness and the relationship between mental illness and asymmetry in the brain. 'We've learned more about the working of the brain in the last 10 years than in the entire history of science,' said Prof Colin Blakemore, a scientific adviser at the centre. 'Now we are on the brink of extending our knowledge towards the conquest of brain disease.'
