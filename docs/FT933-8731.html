930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Spania Santa Rosa plums are an excellent buy this week at 35-50p a lb (35-50p) reports the FFVIB. South African avocados at 50-70p each (60-70p), French Guyot pears at 40-50p a lb (45-55p) and Cyprus Perlette white seedless grapes at 70p-Pounds 1.00 a lb (99p-Pounds 1.70) are also good value. English, Jersey and French runner beans are this week's best vegetable buy at 60-90p a lb (90p-Pounds 1.40). Cauliflowers are still excellent quality at 35-50p a lb (35-55p). English summer cabbage is also still plentiful and is priced at 25-30p a lb (25-30p). English and Dutch tomatoes are the best salad buy this week at 405-55p a lb (45-65p).
