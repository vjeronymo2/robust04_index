930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Pegasus sells rest of Stockforms PEGASUS Group, the USM-quoted software and stationery group, is selling the remaining 75 per cent of Stockforms to Deluxe Corporation's UK offshoot for not less than Pounds 6m. Deluxe UK acquired 25 per cent of Stockforms for Pounds 1.71m in September last year and was granted an option to acquire the balance at any time between July 31 1993 and December 31 1994.
