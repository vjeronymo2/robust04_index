920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Violence in the US: Verdict that caused outrage A week before the King trial decision, another verdict that caused outrage and protests among members of the city's black community was upheld by an appeals court, Louise Kehoe reports from Los Angeles. The convicted killer of a young black woman was sentenced to probation instead of jail. A 15-year-old black girl argued with a Korean grocer over a bottle of orange juice. As the girl left the store the grocer shot her in the back of the head.
