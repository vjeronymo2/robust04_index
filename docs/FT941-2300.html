940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / Peer group gathers to mark anniversaries This month sees the 15th anniversary of the fall of James Callaghan's Labour government. This year is also the anniversary of the longest-serving Labour peer, Lord Listowel, who served in the wartime coalition government in 1944. Lord Graham of Edmonton, chief whip in the Lords, invited Labour peers who had served the Attlee (1945-51), Wilson (1964-70, 1974-76) and Callaghan (1976-79) governments to assemble for the above photograph.
