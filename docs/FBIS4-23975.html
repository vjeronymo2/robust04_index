BFN [Text] Kinshasa, 17 Jun (AFP) -- Marshal Mobutu Sese Seko today signed the decree appointing the new prime minister, Kengo Wa Dondo, who was elected on 14 June by the High Council of the Republic-Transition Parliament, an official source disclosed. The ordinance was signed in line with the transitional constitution that will regulate the functioning of the Zairian institutions up to the adoption of the new constitution which will mark the advent of the Third Republic. Mr. Kengo Wa Dondo's election has already been rejected by hardliners of the Sacred Union of the Radical Opposition [USOR] which supports Etienne Tshisekedi whom they consider the sole "legal" prime minister of Zaire. Mr. Kengo Wa Dondo belongs to the moderate group of the USOR led by the Union for the Republic and Democracy.
