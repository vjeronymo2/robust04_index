930626 FT 26 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Shield near Pounds 0.3m in black The Shield Group, which has estate agency and other property related interests, turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 282,000 in the year to March 31, compared with losses of Pounds 1.37m. The company said it was taking steps to wind up certain non-trading subsidiaries in order to rationalise group operations. It should not have any adverse effect on the overall profit or asset position. Turnover fell to Pounds 2.07m (Pounds 10.6m). Losses per share came through at 3.7p (20.8p) and there is no dividend on either the ordinary or preferred shares.
