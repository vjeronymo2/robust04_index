Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Lou Zhenxu (1236 3791 7312): "The Provincial State Security Department Is Formally Established"] [Text] The state security department of Hebei Province was formally established and began handling official business on 7 December. Attending the inaugural ceremony were Provincial Leaders Cheng Weigao, Ye Liansong, Lu Chuanzan, Li Wenshan, Chen Yujie, Chen Liyou, Xu Yongyue, and Zhao Jinduo. Responsible persons of the Ministry of State Security also attended the ceremony.
