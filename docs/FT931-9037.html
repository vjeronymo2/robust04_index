930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG eased as institutional interest remained muted after last week's strong gains, particularly in the gold sector. The stock market auth-orities said yesterday that turnover during the course of last week of some R763m, against R619m in the previous week, was the highest since August 1990. The overall index lost 18 to 3,474 and industrials shed 22 to 4,550. The golds index eased 3 to 961.
