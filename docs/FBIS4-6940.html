BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 25 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Continuing political consultations of the leaderships of the Mongolian National Democratic Party, Mongolian Social Democratic Party, Mongolian Democratic Association, Union of Mongolian Students and Union for New Progress with the participants of the hunger strike lasting for 15 days already who are demanding the resignation of the government have so far resulted in nothing. The disagreement existing between the participating sides is apparently telling on in reference to the issue of the further ways and perspectives for continuing the political struggle. The press conference scheduled to be held today at the headquarters of the political strike has not been held and the reasons for its postponing to tomorrow have not been announced.
