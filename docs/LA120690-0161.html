December 6, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLORADO STATE LANDS FULLERTON'S GRENIER

Jeff Grenier, a fullback at Fullerton High School, orally committed to play football at Colorado State next season, Fullerton Coach Pat Ward said Wednesday.

Grenier, 6 feet 2 and 225 pounds, rushed for 628 yards in 106 carries this season. Grenier, who transferred to Fullerton from Redwood High School in Visalia, also was recruited by San Diego State and Oregon.
