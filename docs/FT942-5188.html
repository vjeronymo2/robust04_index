940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Northern Foods - Other Companies The former stock market favourite is expected to show a modest increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 153m to between Pounds 155m and Pounds 160m when it announces its full-year figures on Wednesday. The results will reflect difficulties in the meat business and the decline of doorstep milk deliveries. But a good performance is expected in chilled foods.
