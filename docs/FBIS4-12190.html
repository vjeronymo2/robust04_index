BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, this evening telephoned his brother, His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the sisterly Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. They discussed issues of mutual interest. The brother chairman reassured his brother, His Majesty King Husayn, about the situation in our country. He also briefed him on the latest efforts to contain the political crisis in our country through the reactivation of constitutional agencies and implementation of the accord document. The brother chairman expressed his profound thanks and appreciation for his brother, His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, for the continued efforts made by his majesty and the brothers in Jordan to enable our country to overcome the crisis and safeguard Yemeni unity and stability. The brother chairman also expressed thanks to all brothers and well-meaning people in the Arab nation and the region for their concern about the developments in our country, as well as their concern for the security and stability of Yemen and support for Yemeni unity, since this issue, although a Yemeni affair, is of interest to all the sons of the Arab nation and the region. The brother chairman wished the fraternal Jordanian people further progress and prosperity.
