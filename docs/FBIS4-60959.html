BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 30 Apr -- A clash between Russian border guards and an armed group of Tajik fighters trying to make their way into Tajikistan from Afghanistan occurred last night in the Shuroabad sector of the Moskovskiy border detachment. According to infomation from military sources in Dushanbe, the Russian border guards used heavy weapons to repulse the breakthrough. The number of dead among the intruders is not being given, while there were no casualties among the border guards. Also last night, an attempt by a large party of armed intruders from Afghanistan to cross into Tajikistan was thwarted by Russian border guards in a sector of the border near the village of Porshnev in the Pamir mountains.
