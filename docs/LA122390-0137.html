December 23, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

ORGANIZATION OFFERS GIFT-WRAP SERVICE

The Manhattan Beach Neptunian Woman's Club is providing holiday gift-wrapping at Manhattan Village Shopping Center through Monday.

The gift-wrap area is next to Lady Footlocker in the Bullock's wing. All proceeds will assist the club in its philanthropic endeavors. The club's activities help support scholarships and various community organizations.

Manhattan Village is at at Sepulveda Boulevard and Rosecrans Avenue. Holiday hours are 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. today and 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. Monday. For more information about the gift-wrap, call 546-5555.
