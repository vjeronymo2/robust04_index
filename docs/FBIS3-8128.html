Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 1 (CTK) -- A coordinated approach to community elections will be taken by the Christian Democratic Party (KDS) and the Christian Democratic Union-Czechoslovak People's Party (KDU-CSL), according to a joint communique issued after talks between the two parties. KDS Chairman Ivan Pilip and KDU-CSL Chairman Josef Lux said in the communique that discussion of the electoral laws should be a priority in negotiations between the four governing coalition parties. Both the KDS and KDU-CSL favor the creation of constituencies with more than one mandate, the communique says.
