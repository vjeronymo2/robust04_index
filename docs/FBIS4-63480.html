BFN [Text] The press center of our state's Foreign Affairs Ministry reported today the deep concern of the world community in connection with the Crimean events, directed at changing the legal status of the autonomous republic as a constituent part of Ukraine. Their disquietude is also evoked by tendencies, which lead to tension in interethnic relations on the peninsula and threaten peace and calm in the region. The report by the press center of Ukraine's Foreign Affairs Ministry notes that the European and world community, in the person of official factors and the mass media, is expressing its support for the principles of Ukraine's territorial integrity and the inviolability of its borders. They are also calling on political forces, which are destabilizing the situation in this region, to a balanced approach and the understanding of their responsibility to mankind.
