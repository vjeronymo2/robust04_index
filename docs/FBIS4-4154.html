BFN [Text] Beijing, April 27 (XINHUA) -- Lei Jieqiong, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with a delegation from the U.S. based Haggai Institute here this afternoon. The delegation, headed by John Haggai, is here at the invitation of the China Association for International Friendly Contacts. The Haggai Institute is a famous Christian educational organization in the southern part of the United States. It is engaged in training leaders of various circles in Third World countries.
