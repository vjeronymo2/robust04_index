June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SAFE AND SOUND NO MORE

It is difficult to describe the intense emotions I felt when I read John Doyle's "Safe and Sound No More." It was the first time that someone has dared to adequately describe the same painful feelings and emotions that I have experienced. Doyle's story is so similar to mine that I could have written it. I applaud his courage for sharing this important topic.

NAME WITHHELD

Newport Beach
