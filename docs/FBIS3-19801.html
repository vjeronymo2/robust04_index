Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn -- The special representative for rationalization measures within the Bundeswehr, General Lorenz Huber, has asked for his dismissal. The Defense Ministry confirmed the step of the 57-year-old general today. As DPA learned, he cited lacking support for his proposed austerity measures by senior generals as the reason for his departure. The Defense Ministry announced that no decision has yet been made regarding Huber's request. The defense expert of the Christian Democratic Union, Paul Breuer, demanded the "unconditional examination of all of Huber's proposals and a clarification of the circumstances that prompted Huber to take this personal step." Juergen Koppelin from the Free Democratic Party of Germany spoke of a "serious warning" for Defense Minister Volker Ruehe (Christian Democratic Union). [passage omitted]
