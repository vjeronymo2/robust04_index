BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Athens, 25 Jun -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin left Athens today for Russia. On 24 June, on the Greek island of Corfu, he signed an agreement on partnership and cooperation between the Russian Federation and the European Union [EU]. Yeltsin was seen off at Athens Airport by Greek National Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis, Russian Federation Ambassador to Athens Valeriy Nikolayenko, and other officials. The farewell ceremony was simplified as top Greek leaders are staying in Corfu, where the EU summit ended earlier today. The Russian president travelled from Corfu to the port of Piraievs in the yacht Alexandros, which had served as his residence during the visit.
