Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] The longest strike in the post-war history of Poland ended last night. After 19 days, 78 miners from the Pomorzany mine [belonging to the Boleslaw Mining and Metallurgical Plant] returned to the surface. A signed agreement guarantees the miners a pay rise of 1.5 million zlotys a month and a quarterly bonus based on the enterprise's profits. According to the director of the Boleslaw mining and metallurgical plant this is all the enterprise can manage at the moment.
