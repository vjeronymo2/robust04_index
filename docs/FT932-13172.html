930424 FT 24 APR 93 / UK Company News: Jones Group slips to IPounds 3.06m Jones Group, the Dublin-based metal engineering and shipping group, reported a fall in pre-tax profits from IPounds 3.45m to IPounds 3.06m in 1992. Turnover improved slightly, from IPounds 97.5m to IPounds 101.3m. There was an extraordinary charge of IPounds 7.52m. Earnings slipped to 18.9p (20p) and the dividend is being held at 12.5p.
