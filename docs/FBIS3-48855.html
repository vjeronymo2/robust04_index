Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 31 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the working people who have done good things for society and collective. 37 family members of the officials of the cooperative farm management committee of Kowon County, South Hamgyong Province, formed a work team last year for an active assistance to the socialist countryside. They have increased the yield of maize per hectare by doing well farming as demanded by the chuche farming method in 10 hectares of maize fields of a cooperative farm. Choe Kum-sik, curator of the library of the Kang Pan-sok University for the bereaved children of revolutionaries, has contributed her all to rearing children as the reliable successors of the revolution, the national cadres, over the past forty years. Yun Myong-suk and Cha Kyong-hui who are cleaners of Pyongyang-Nampo highway showed the fine spiritual and moral traits of the Korean youths by giving back the things dropped by foreigners. At the meetings conveying the thanks, speakers said the thanks contained warm love and trust of the dear leader who even values and estimates humble things done by revolutionary soldiers.
