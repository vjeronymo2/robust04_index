BFN [Text] By now, the situation in the Black Sea Fleet's conservation division has been stabilized, and is under full control. This information was disclosed in an interview to the NOVYNY Broadcasting Agency by Volodymyr Orlov, Odessa senior naval chief [starshyy morskyy nachalnik]. He emphasized that the majority of division personnel will continue their service in the Ukrainian Navy. Material for initiating criminal suits against Oleh Feokistov, commander of the division, Oleksandr Zelenko, chief of the division staff, and Dmytro Starikov, commander of the coastal base, is being prepared. Volodymyr Orlov also emphasized that Russian media reports do not correspond to reality -- there were neither wounded children nor wounded servicemen.
