930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / Observer: No comment A 70-year-old hobo died the other day in Palermo, Sicily. When the authorities went through his few belongings, they found Dollars 300,000 in stock certificates in a plastic carrier bag. Colleagues said they had suspected their late buddy had independent means because when he dossed down on the park bench at night he always covered himself with the Financial Times. . . .
