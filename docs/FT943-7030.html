940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Net assets slip to 227.24p at Jos Jos Holdings, a split capital investment trust, reported a net asset value per capital share of 227.24p at the July 31 year end, against 277.9p six months earlier and 217.29p at end-July 1993. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 748,142 (Pounds 751,853), equivalent to earnings of 11.58p (11.63p) per income share, and a final dividend of 3.625 makes a total of 12.25p (11.65p) for the year.
