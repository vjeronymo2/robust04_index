BFN [Text] Ankara, May 14 (A.A) -- Turkey's exports for the Jan-March 1994 period reached three billion 826 million 367 thousand dollars while its imports totalled 5 billion 902 million 128 thousand dollars for the same period, the State Statistics Institute (DIE) said on Saturday [14 May]. According to DIE's written statement in March only, Turkey's exports were 1 billion 320 million 83 thousand dollars, while its imports were 1 billion 996 million 532 thousand dollars. Exports in March increased by 7.2 percent compared to last year's 1 billion 231 million 149 thousand dollars for the same period. As for imports, it decreased by 5.4 percent when compared to last years's 2 billion 110 million 911 thousand dollars. During the Jan-March period exports increased by 4.2 percent compared to the same period last year, while imports decreased by 1.0 per thousand for the same period. In the same period in 1993 exports were 3 billion 673 million 268 thousand dollars while for imports the amount was 5 billion 908 million 27 thousand dollars.
