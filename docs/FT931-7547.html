930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / Levitt denies all charges MR ROGER Levitt, chairman of the Levitt Group, yesterday denied at the Old Bailey 21 charges related to his collapsed financial services company. The charges include fraudulent trading, false accounting and deception. Three other former Levitt Group executives, Mr Mark Reed, Mr Alan McNamara and Mr Robert Price, also pleaded not guilty to a number of similar charges. All four defendants are on bail. The trial is expected to start in September.
