931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / Viag and Bayernwerk form telecoms subsidiary Viag, the energy-based conglomerate, and Bayernwerk, Germany's third largest utilities group, yesterday formed a telecoms subsidiary, writes Ariane Genillard. The company, called Telekommunikations-gesellschaft fur Betrieb und Dienstleistungen, will manage the telecoms network linking Bayernwerk and Viag to their domestic and international subsidiaries. It will offer voice telephony, data transmission and value-added services to the two companies' subsidiaries.
