930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO was 4.1 per cent higher at midsession, helped by an announcement clearing the way for the privatisation auction of Acominas, the steel group. The Bovespa index was up 327 at 12,151. Acominas will be the last state steel manufacturer to be sold under the government's privatisation programme. News was also awaited of a meeting of the Democratic Movement party, when members are expected to decide whether to continue supporting President Itamar Franco in Congress.
