910427 FT 27 APR 91 / German rail car maker seeks buyer THE Deutsche Waggonbau, the world's largest builder of railway carriages, has been placed on the market by the Treuhand, the east German privatisation agency, David Goodhart writes from Bonn. The Treuhand said yesterday it would seek buyers in the US, Europe, Japan and South Korea, as well as Germany. The company has been established from the ruins of a giant railway equipment Kombinat but has been given about DM200m (Pounds 66.8m) for new investment this year.
