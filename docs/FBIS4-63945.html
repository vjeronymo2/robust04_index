BFN [Text] The Saeima faction Harmony for Latvia [Saskana Latvijai] will in the future be known as the Economic-Political Union Faction [Tautsaimnieku Politiskas Apvienibas Frakcija]. This was decided today at a session of the faction. The faction was formed to unite deputies who were elected to the Saeima from a list of 19 candidates and the Economic-Political Union. The faction resolved to support the policy consultation formed at the initiative of the Harmony for Latvia party from center-oriented parties of the right and left, represented and not represented in the Saeima. Mentioned as possible participants in the consultation are such organizations as the Latvian Way, the Democratic Party, the People's Harmony party, the Landowner [Saimnieks], the Latvian Social Democratic Party of Labor, and Harmony for Latvia.
