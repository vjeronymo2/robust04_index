BFN [Text] Beijing, May 31 (XINHUA) -- Lojze Peterle, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs of the Republic of Slovenia, arrived here by air this evening. Lojze Peterle has come for a six-day official visit to China as guest of his Chinese counterpart Qian Qichen . Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Dai Bingguo and Slovenian Ambassador to China Ivan Senicar [name as received] were among those present at the airport to greet the Slovenian guests.
