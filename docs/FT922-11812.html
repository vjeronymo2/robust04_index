920423 FT 23 APR 92 / London lighthouse up for sale City lights: Trinity Buoy Wharf lighthouse, a 19th-century landmark in the shadow of Canary Wharf, is up for sale. Marking the mouth of the River Lea on the Thames, it is more than 40 miles from the sea. Although familiar to mariners and bargees, its main use was as a Trinity House training centre for lighthouse-keepers. The 70ft building, listed as of architectural and historic interest, is being sold by the London Docklands Development Corporation
