930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / UK Company News: First Spanish net asset value falls First Spanish Investment Trust reported net asset value of 71.5p at May 31, compared with 78.6p a year before. Net revenue for the year to the end of May was ahead at Pounds 559,000 (Pounds 421,000) for earnings per share of 7.9p (5.95p). The final proposed dividend is 7.8p, compared with 5.75p, restated for the share consolidation.
