920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Cyprus sultana, Israeli and Greek Thompson seedless grapes are this weeks best fruit buy at 45-60p a lb,and 68-90p a lb. reports the FFVIB. Victoria plums are plentiful at 35-65p a lb. A good choice of melons is available; galia 70-95p each from Spania, Israel and France,honeydew 75p-Pounds 1.00 each and Spania watermelon at Pounds 1.50-Pounds 2.00 each depending on size. Good quality English and Jersey courgettes are the best vegetable buy this week at 40-50p a lb. A variety of English lettuce is available: Iceberg 50-75p a head, Webbs 30-40p each and Lollo Rosso 45-60p each. New in are Dutch salad onions 40-50p.
