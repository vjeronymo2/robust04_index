September 12, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

World champion and Tour de France winner Greg LeMond will be taken to court by his current team, ADR, for alleged breach of contract, ADR owner Francois Lambert said.

LeMond is expected to announce Wednesday in Paris which team he will ride for next year. But he will definitely leave ADR, and is widely expected to sign a three-year contract worth $5.7 million with the French team Z.
