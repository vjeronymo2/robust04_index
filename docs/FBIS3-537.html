Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Angolan authorities say they have lost contact with a ship apprehended by UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] forces in northern Angola, near the border with Zaire. Officials say the ship was transporting an unknown number of civilians, who had been prevented from disembarking. It is now docked at the (Cuanda) oil installations, near the city of Soyo, which is under UNITA's control.
