Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] St. Petersburg January 27 TASS -- President Boris Yeltsin had to cut his visit to St. Petersburg to one day because of important developments awaiting him in Moscow. "Tomorrow will be a very tense day" and it is necessary for the president to be in Moscow, Yeltsin told reporters upon arrival to St. Petersburg on Thursday. He did not elaborate. Initially the president planned to stay two days in St. Petersburg and to visit the fortress of Kronshtadt where an anti-Bolshevik uprising took place in 1921 and was drowned in blood. Yeltsin has recently rehabilitated the victims of the uprising.
