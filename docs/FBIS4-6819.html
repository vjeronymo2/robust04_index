BFN [Text] A Public Relations Department correspondent reported from Nakhon Si Thammarat Province that a bomb exploded at the railway station in Nakhon Si Thammarat at about noon. The motive of the explosion is unknown. Six persons were killed instantly and several were injured. Second Lieutenant Thanaphong Chakkaphak, Nakhon Si Thammarat deputy governor, and police officials went to the scene to investigate the incident. Those injured were rushed to the Maharat Hospital of Nakhon Si Thammarat for treatment.
