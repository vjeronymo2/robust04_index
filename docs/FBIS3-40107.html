Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] According to a survey of 29 counties and cities in Jiangxi Province, the per capita net income of farmers in 1993 was 870 yuan, an increase of 102 yuan, or 13.3 percent over 1992. Deducting price increases, the actual increase was 4.6 percent. The major reason for the increase was a reduction in peasant burdens. In March, Jiangxi redeemed over 300 million yuan in IOUs from farmers. In addition, cotton, oil crop, silkworm, tobacco, and fruit output increased 5.1 percent, 4.2 percent, 10.4 percent, 29 percent, and 20 pecent respectively over 1992. Town and township enterprises also quickly developed. In 1993 the gross output value of rural enterprises was 57.9 billion yuan, a 92.2 percent increase over 1992.
