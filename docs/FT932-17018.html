930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: AAF makes Pounds 7.65m cash call AAF INDUSTRIES, the modular building and alloy wheels group, has launched a Pounds 7.65m rights issue of 4.6m new ordinary shares on a 1-for-4 basis, priced at 175p each. The group also announced a 20 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.97m to Pounds 4.76m for 1992, on turnover 12 per cent ahead at Pounds 72m. An increase in the proposed final dividend to 6p makes an unchanged total for the year of 11p gross. Earnings per share improved to 25.2p (21.4p). Proceeds of the issue, which is fully underwritten by Hoare Govett, will be used mainly to finance capital expenditure and for working capital. Following completion of the rights, W&A Group, a South African consumer and industrial group, will control about 43.1 per cent of AAF compared with 53.8 per cent at present.
