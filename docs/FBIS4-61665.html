BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 5/5/1994 -- The rearrangement in the Ministry of Justice will be performed according to judiciary reform just when it comes into effect, declared to BASA Vasile Sturza, Moldovan minister of justice. According to judiciary reform, changes in both the Ministry of Justice and other justice organs will take place, he specified. The number of ministry departments and the objectives of their activities will not be considerably altered; only some slight modifications have been planned, said Sturza. No personnel cuts will be enacted in the Ministry of Justice, excluding the vice-minister and five employees from auxiliary personnel. At the same time, an expansion of the Department of Legislation is planned, for its employees are overtaxed by the number of legislative acts to be designed and transferred to the Parliament for adoption [words indistinct].
