October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

BUSH ATTENDS FUND-RAISER FOR 2 ALASKA CANDIDATES

President Bush attended a $2,000-a-plate breakfast fund-raiser Tuesday for two Alaska Republicans that pulled in at least $150,000, a spokesman said. Arliss Sturgulewski, the GOP candidate for governor, and Rep. Don Young (R-Alaska) were the beneficiaries of the event, which was also attended by Sen. Ted Stevens (R-Alaska).
