BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Apr (MENA)--A dangerous terrorist was killed this afternoon and another terrorist was arrested during a chase in the al-Munib area of al-Jizah on the Cairo-Upper Egypt agricultural road. The two were riding in a small car. Acting on information that terrorist elements were in the area, a force from the State Security Intelligence Department headed there and arrested a terrorist. Another terrorist was killed during the exchange of fire. According to initial reports, there were no casualties among the security forces or citizens in the area during the exchange of fire.
