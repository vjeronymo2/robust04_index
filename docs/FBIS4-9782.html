BFN [Text] On 2 April, Zambian Foreign Minister Remmy Kaindu Gilly Mushota leaves for Jamaica to continue a tour of the Caribbean. During his busy one-week official visit, which strengthened cooperation between Zambia and Cuba, the Zambian official discussed details of Zambian President Frederick Chiluba's forthcoming trip to Cuba. Mushota met with high-level Cuban officials and visited the Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology Center, the Finlay Institute, and Santiago de Cuba Province. He also met with Raul Trujillo, first vice minister of the Ministry of the Sugar Industry, a meeting which paved the way for a technical assistance agreement. Mushota also held a news conference during which he stated that his visit to Cuba has been extremely positive and that he is very pleased with the agreements concerning health, sports, and sugarcane agriculture that have been signed.
