Language: English Article Type:BFN [From RAHVA HAAL, PAEVALEHT, POSTIMEES, February 21, pp 1,3; "News Bulletin" No. 35] [Text] Joint exercise of the Estonian, Latvian and Lithuanian defence forces began in Vdru yesterday. C Company of the Kuperjanov Battalion (129 men), 1 Company of the Latvian Aluksne Infantry Battalion (150 men) and 1 Company of the Lithuanian "Iron Wolf" Brigade Panevezys Battalion (96 men) take part in the exercise. Unlike the first joint exercise held last spring, which had a military-sporting nature, the present exercise is strictly military, Lieutenant Meelis Sare of the Kuperjanov Battalion said. The exercise includes meeting an enemy attack, retreat to the second line of defence and counter-offensive. One hundred and forty men from the non-commissioned officers combat school perform as the enemy. The exercise is also meant as a part of forming the Baltic joint peacekeeping battalion, Major Mart Tiru of the Defence Forces GHQ [general headquarters] explained. The battalion is to include 70 officers, 120 non-commissioned officers and approximately 500 soldiers. The exercise is to last until Tuesday [22 February].
