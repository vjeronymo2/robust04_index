931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Genesis Chile net assets at Dollars 29.24 Genesis Chile Fund net asset value was Dollars 29.24 at September 30, against Dollars 24.83 a year earlier. Net revenue was Dollars 5.27m (Pounds 3.53m), against Dollars 4.89m, for earnings per share of 64 cents, compared with 60 cents. The dividend is raised to 60 cents (56 cents).
