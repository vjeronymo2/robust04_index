New Mall Intercept Survey File No. 270&hyph;393 Notice is hereby given that pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq ), the Securities and Exchange Commission (``Commission'') has submitted for OMB approval a request to conduct a mall intercept survey to obtain information on what format and presentation of information about mutual funds will make it easier for investors to make inferences and make correct comparisons of fees, expenses and risks for different funds. The results will enable the Commission to better understand the level of investor comprehension of mutual fund prospectuses. The mall intercept survey is estimated to require a total of 33.33 burden hours. The burden hour per participant will be .33 hours or 20 minutes.
