January 9, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

ALARM ROUTS BROOKE SHIELDS

When a fire alarm went off at one of Charlotte's fanciest restaurants, actress Brooke Shields joined other patrons outside in the rain until it proved to be a false alarm.

Once it had been determined the alarm had been set off by a coffee maker, Shields returned in bright spirits.

"I love trauma," she said. "I feel closer to you all. We've bonded."

Shields was in town to promote a line of curling irons, hair dryers and other products bearing her name.
