Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Trite Stance of Confrontation" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 9 (KCNA) -- At a time when the working contact for the exchange of special envoys between the North and the South is under way, the Kim Yong-sam group started on March 7 military exercises directed against the North in Inchon and Kyonggi Province which will last till the 14th. Commenting on this, papers here today say this clearly indicates that the Kim Yong-sam group has no willingness to resolve the questions of the peace and reunification of the country and is going to invariably seek confrontation and war this year, too. A NODONG SINMUN analyst says: If the South Korean rulers are willing to solve the pending issues between the North and the South through dialogue and negotiation by realizing the exchange of special envoys, they must not do acts getting on the other's nerve. Recently, the traitor Kim Yong-sam impudently cried that they "could not be optimistic" about North-South relations and "the United States would not hold the third round of the DPRK-U.S. talks before the exchange of special envoys." His remarks fully revealed his sinister intention to balk the DPRK-U.S. talks, obstruct the solution to the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula, persistently seek North-South confrontation and thus slay the fellow countrymen in the North with outside forces. The Kim Yong-sam group decided to introduce into South Korea U.S. Patriot missiles which had been used in the Persian Gulf war and is now welcoming the deployment of two groups of Apache, a main attack helicopter belonging to the U.S. Army, in South Korea. And it does not bother to conceal its plan to stage a large-scale nuclear war exercise with outside forces. With the Kim Yong-sam group, "civilian" warmaniacs desperately trying to plunge the nation into a nuclear holocaust, left alone, is it impossible to solve the questions of peace and reunification through dialogue and negotiation or save our fellow countrymen from another war disaster. A MINJU CHOSON analyst says that the Kim Yong-sam group is attempting to frighten the North and ensure the "security of power" with reckless war gambles, but all in vain.
