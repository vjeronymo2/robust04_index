930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / International Company News: Cooper Clarke hit by bad debts For the year 1992 Cooper Clarke, builders' merchant and distributor of specialised products, made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 203,000 from Pounds 23.6m turnover. The 1991 period was for eight months only, and that produced a profit of Pounds 126,000 from sales of Pounds 13.2m. Mr Peter Clarke, chairman, said the company had been hit by bad debts of Pounds 316,000 in the year despite close control of credit management. Intense competition again squeezed margins. After a substantially higher tax charge earnings per share for the year came to 2.6p (4.1p for the period).
