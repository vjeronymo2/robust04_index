941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / World Trade News: San Miguel and Yamura to build can plant Philippine food and beverage giant San Miguel plans to build an aluminium can plant and is negotiating with Japan's Yamamura Glass to take part in the project. The plant, which SMC hopes to start building next January in General Trias, Cavite province, will have a capacity of 420m cans a year and is expected to be on stream by 1996. About 50 per cent of committed capacity would be exported to Hong Kong, China, Vietnam and Japan.
