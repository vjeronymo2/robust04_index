941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Tokyo Electric Power - Other companies The world's largest electric power company is expected to announce a small fall in recurring profits on Tuesday, from Y58.5bn to Y58bn, despite the summer heatwave. Kansai Electric Power, the world's second-largest power group, will report better results on the same day. Analysts expect Kansai's interim profits to rise from Y49bn to Y65bn.
