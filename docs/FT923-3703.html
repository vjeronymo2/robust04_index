920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / BCCI creditors approve scheme Large creditors of Bank of Credit and Commerce Hong Kong Ltd (in liquidation) have endorsed a repayment scheme, the bank's special managers said, Reuter reports. Creditors owed more than HKDollars 100,000 (Pounds 6,500) had voted 83 per cent by value in favour. Under the scheme, creditors owed HKDollars 100,000 or less will be paid back in full. Large creditors will receive a first dividend cheque estimated at 40 per cent of their claims. BCCHK, local arm of Bank of Credit and Commerce International, was closed by the Hong Kong government in July last year after scandal engulfed the parent bank. Large creditors are owed a total of HKDollars 4.3bn (Pounds 279m).
