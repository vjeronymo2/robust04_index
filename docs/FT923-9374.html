920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort High assets top 74p At June 30 1992, Kleinwort High Income Trust had produced a net asset value of 110.72p per zero dividend preference and 74.2p for each ordinary share. The trust, which started trading on July 8 1991, saw gross revenue reach Pounds 3.57m by June 30 1992, and earnings come out at 8.64p. The fourth quarterly dividend is 1.875p for a total of 7.5p, in line with the forecast.
