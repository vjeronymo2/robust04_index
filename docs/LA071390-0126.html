July 13, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BUSH TREE? Will the President take time to plant a tree for the local environment? . . . Maybe, says Tom Larson, founder of the local Tree Society.

Bush's advance planners "tell me he wants to do it, but the location is up in the air." . . . It won't be in Little Saigon. The White House considered a side trip but says there's no time. Larson recommends the Cal State Fullerton arboretum and says it ought to be a California live oak. "It's stately, and it's California history." Dave Lesher and Mary Helen Berg
