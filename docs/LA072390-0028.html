July 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Geraldo 'Vice' Suit Settled: Attorneys say they have settled out of court a $30-million lawsuit filed against Geraldo Rivera by a woman who was arrested during a drug raid carried live on national television. Terry Rouse, 32, of Houston, Tex., filed the libel suit against Rivera in 1987 stemming from the Dec. 2, 1986, telecast of "American Vice: The Doping of a Nation." The settlement, involving an undisclosed amount of money, came after Rivera was questioned for six hours while giving a deposition Thursday. GREG BRAXTON
