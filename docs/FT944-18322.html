941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Herring Baker In another difficult six months Herring Baker Harris Group, the chartered surveyor, cut its interim pre-tax loss from Pounds 1.08m to Pounds 306,000. However the dividend is being passed. Turnover for the half year to July 31 was lower at Pounds 7.48m, against Pounds 8.48m which included Pounds 618,000 from discontinued activities. Losses per share fell to 2.47p (8.95p). There was an interim payment of 0.5p last time.
