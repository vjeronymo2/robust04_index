Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A five-member delegation of the Chinese Communist Party held talks with the foreign minister, Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali, in Islamabad today. The member of CPC [Communist Party of China] Central Committee, Mr. Hou Zongbin, led the delegation. Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali briefed the members of the delegation on the gross human rights violation being committed by Indian forces in Indian-held Kashmir. He emphasized the need for China to use its influence on India to give up her inflexible attitude and resolve the Kashmir issue in accordance with UN resolutions. Both sides expressed satisfaction over the current status of bilateral relation and stressed the need to further improve mutual cooperation in trade and economic fields.
