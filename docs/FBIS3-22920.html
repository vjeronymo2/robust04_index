Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 16 Feb (TANJUG) -- Sinisa Vucinic, chairman of the Serb Royalist Movement [Srpski Rojalisticki Pokret, SRP], said in Belgrade today that if NATO launches air strikes on Bosnian Serb positions on 20 February, "a special unit of the SRP would attack UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] members in Belgrade." He said that "the West should take this threat seriously" and that the SRP would carry out its threats regardless of the Serbian Interior Ministry's reaction. Vucinic stressed that SRP members in Frankfurt were ready to "launch terrorist attacks on the German people" if air strikes were launched against Bosnian Serb positions.
