940312 FT 12 MAR 94 / International Company News: Minmet back in the black Minmet, the Dublin-based extractor of minerals, precious stones and strategic metals, swung from losses of IPounds 371,052 to profits of IPounds 32,897 (Pounds 31,600) pre-tax for 1993. Turnover totalled IPounds 301,963 (IPounds 42,978). At year-end net assets were IPounds 1.58m (IPounds 159,743).
