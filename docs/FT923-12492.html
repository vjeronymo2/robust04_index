920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Baring Tribune Net asset value per share at Baring Tribune Investment showed a marginal increase from 273.9p to 274.7p over the six months ended June 30 1992. But it was down on the 279.2p of a year earlier. Gross revenue for the half year was up to Pounds 3.73m (Pounds 3.34m), with earnings per share rising from 3.09p to 3.62p. The interim dividend is again 1.7p.
