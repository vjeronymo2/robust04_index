920104 FT 04 JAN 92 / Discussion on Gatt response Trade and agriculture ministers of the European Community are to meet in Brussels on January 11 to decide on a common response to the proposals for a world trade liberalisation agreement submitted by Mr Arthur Dunkel, the director-general of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (Gatt) on December 20, our Foreign Staff writes. This meeting comes two days before the deadline for country responses to the Dunkel text, which is intended to bring to a conclusion more than five years of trade negotiations in the Uruguay Round held under the aegis of Gatt.
