930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: Aviva Petroleum loss deepens Aviva Petroleum, the Texas-based oil and gas company with a London quote, announced increased net losses of Dollars 9.83m (Pounds 6.92m) for the year to December 31. In 1991 there were losses of Dollars 8.81m. The company said the figures reflected a change in accounting for oil and gas assets from the 'successful efforts' to the 'full cost' method for better comparability with similar sized oil and gas companies. Sales improved to Dollars 10.4m (Dollars 9.32m) primarily as a result of production from the Colombia fields. Losses per share were 119 cents (169 cents).
