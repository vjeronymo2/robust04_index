July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BEST BETS

No plastic pails and rusty trowels here, please. Rising well above the high tide line at Will Rogers State Beach on Saturday will be sandcastles and sand sculptures worthy of a Main Street gallery.

This, the 9th Annual Sandcastle Competition, is being held to benefit the William Z. Landworth Memorial Scholarship Fund. The competition is divided into three categories: sandcastle building, sand sculpture and "anything goes," the latter for children under 10 years. It will be staged from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. where Pacific Coast Highway and Temescal Canyon Road meet on the beach. The public is invited to participate as well as view the sculptures. Entry fee is $15. Phone: (213) 380-4595.

It's Sunday morning and you don't have any plans for the afternoon. Dig out a pair of walking shoes for an adventure through the streets of West Hollywood. Fantastica '90 is a self-guided stroll through West Hollywood's art galleries and designer showrooms from noon to 5 p.m. Dancers, poets and clowns will provide entertainment, and goodies from top Los Angeles restaurants will be served along the way. The afternoon activities will culminate with a juried silent auction. Tickets are $25 per person and can be purchased at the plaza of the Pacific Design Center, at 8687 Melrose Ave. Phone: (213) 653-0440 for information. Proceeds benefit the Los Angeles Free Clinic.
