BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi June 15 TASS -- Four U.N. military observers arrived in Sukhumi on Wednesday to monitor a CIS peace-keeping operation in Abkhazia, a spokesman in the Abkhazian Ministry of Information and the Press, told ITAR-TASS. According to the same source, a total of 88 U.N. observers are to be posted in the area of the deployment of a CIS peace-keeping force along Inguri river. General supervision will be exercised by Colonel-General Georgiy Kondratyev, Russian deputy defence minister, from the Russian peace- keeping force headquarters in Sukhumi.
