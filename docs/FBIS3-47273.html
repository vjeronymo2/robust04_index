Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 11 (KCNA) -- Foreign mass media reported the great leader President Kim Il-song's New Year address. The journal DE ANGOLA carried the part on national reunification and the part on foreign policy from the New Year address. National Radio of Guyana broadcast the New Year address three times, saying that President Kim Il-song in his New Year address described the victory of the Korean people last year as a victory of the revolutionary line of the Workers' Party of Korea, a great victory of the singlehearted unity of the leader, the party and the masses. The Mozambican paper NOTICIAS quoted President Kim Il-song as saying that the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula must be settled through DPRK-U.S. talks and that pressure and threats will have no effect on the Korean people and may invite catastrophe, far from achieving a solution to the problem. Newspapers, news agencies and radios of Italy, Egypt, Ethiopia, China, Zaire, Peru, Poland, Nigeria, Austria, Laos and Yugoslavia reported the gist of the New Year address.
