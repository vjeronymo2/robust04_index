941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Southern Water - Other Companies The utility is expected on Wednesday to report interim profits of about Pounds 68.5m, a 6 per cent increase on last year. The dividend could rise by almost 10 per cent to 8.45p. Southern is one of the few water companies to enjoy earnings enhancement from its non-regulated businesses. This is likely to show further improvement.
