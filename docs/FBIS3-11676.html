Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Official Results of the Chamber of Representatives election.

Liberals Maintain Majority.
In certain regions of the country seats were decided by two to five votes. The ruling party defeated the Conservative Party by about 1.2 million votes nationwide. There was scarce voting for candidates from Indian communities. The participation of Christians increased.
