930427 FT 27 APR 93 / US company opens in Hanoi An American consulting firm opened for business in Hanoi yesterday, the first US company to operate officially in Vietnam since the end of the war in 1975, AP reports from Hanoi. Vietnam America Trade and Investment Consulting Co will advise other companies interested in penetrating Vietnam's market of 70m people. It says clients include Chrysler. At least two other US companies - Bank of America and a consultancy called Ashta International - have also obtained licences to open representative offices in Vietnam.
