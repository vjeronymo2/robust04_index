December 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THREE POINTS OF LIGHT FROM JOHN ROBINSON

A few Christmas wishes from me for myself and other frustrated Ram fans: I wish Georgia Frontiere would sell this team. I wish John Robinson would throw away his USC playbook. I wish the Rams would stop passing when they should run and stop running when they should pass.

But most of all, I wish that from now on, when the Rams have them down, they kick 'em, not fake 'em!

DWAINE E. MACKEY, Sunland
