BFN [Text] Havana, 28 Mar (PL) -- Today, Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina wished newly elected OAS Secretary General Cesar Gaviria success in leading that organization. Our hope and wish is that he will be successful in his new role, Robaina said in a statement to PRENSA LATINA when asked about the nomination [as received] of the current Colombian president to that important regional post. Regarding the Colombian daily EL TIEMPO's retraction of previous reports on an alleged guerrilla summit in Havana -- another current topic relating to that South American country -- Robaina simply said: "EL TIEMPO admitted we were right." Robaina made that statement upon welcoming Zambian Foreign Minister Remmy Kaindu Gilly Mushota [title as received], who arrived in Havana today on an official five-day visit.
