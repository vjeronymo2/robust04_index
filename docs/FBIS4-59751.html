BFN [Text] The custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin'-'Abd-al-'Aziz, received a letter from Dr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council. The letter was handed over by Mr. Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, the minister of oil and mineral wealth, while being received by King Fahd in his office yesterday at (?al-Yamamah) palace. The letter contained an explanation of the latest developments in Yemen and the initiative presented by the Yemeni Socialist Party to stop the infighting.
