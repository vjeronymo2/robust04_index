BFN [Text] The Americans believe that there is a possibe compromise formula on access to the fenced area of Famagusta that will be handed over to the United Nations. The CyBC [Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation] correspondent in New York reports that the Americans are promoting a plan to provide safe access to the fenced area of Famagusta in exchange for the removal of the area north of Dhimokratias Avenue from the package of confidence-building measures or an amendment to the timeframe for return of the area. The CyBC correspondent also reports that it cannot be ruled out that the UN secretary general and the Security Council's five permanent members will invite President Glavkos Kliridhis and [Turkish Cypriot Leader] Rauf Denktas to New York. It is also not ruled out that President Kliridhis will go to the United States by the end of this week for consultations with the leadership of the U.S. State Department and the Security Council permanent members.
