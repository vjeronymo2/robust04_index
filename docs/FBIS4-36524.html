BFN [Text] As the Palestinian issue develops, and in preparation for the move to the self-rule area in Jericho, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat ordered the closure of the PLO Central Committee's offices in Tunisia next week. The source who announced the news said orders have been issued to more than 600 Palestinian administrative and security officials at the beginning of sthe week, pointing out that President 'Arafat will temporarily keep his offices and place of residence in the Tunisian capital. The transfer process will not include the PLO Political Department, which is being kept in Tunisia to coordinate between the Palestinian national authority and the international community and to look after the affairs of the Palestinians living abroad. The source neither specified the time of the move nor the number of officials who will accompany President 'Arafat to Jericho.
