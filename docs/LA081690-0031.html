August 16, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

LETTERS

* We have followed with great interest your "reporting" of the recently completed Kentucky Fried Chicken on Western Avenue.

First your readers were enlightened about its aesthetic value in the View section (nice picture). Then they were informed regarding its bill of fare in the Food section (better pictures). They marveled again at this unique building in your L.A. Times Magazine (best pictures yet!). But before you "cover" it in your Real Estate or Business sections, you may choose to "report" who built it.

We did.

-- PERRY A. STONE

2M Construction Inc.

Glendale
