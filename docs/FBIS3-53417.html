Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 13, IRNA -- Following the blast at Ferdowsi Square at 20:30 hours here Thursday, reports said two bombs had been placed in a corner street garbage bin. Police is investigating into the incident. A police officer said he could not comment until the end of investigations. He observed that the bombs were very small and were of the sound type. The windowpanes of a sandwitch shop and a nearby bookshop were shattered as a result of the blast. The IRNA reporter saw an ambulance taking three injured persons to a nearby hospital. All the three were innocent passers-by. The emergency hospital of Tehran said one of the victims is criticially injured. The area is one of the busiest in central Tehran but fortunately shops were closed at the time of the blast and pedestrians were few.
