Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] In a communique issued today the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs states that it has noted with satisfaction the fact that -- with the considerable contribution of the U.S. Administration -- an accord was concluded on the establishment of a Croatian-Muslim federation in Bosnia-Herzegovina and a confederation between the latter and Croatia. Romania's Ministry of Foreign Affairs considers that the implementation of the stipulations of the aforementioned accord represents a substantial step toward the political settlement of the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina, parallel to efforts made to find solutions for the rest of the package of issues that is being negotiated. Romania's Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses the hope that this favorable moment will be turned to good account to the benefit of all parties and stability and security in the region, the communique concludes.
