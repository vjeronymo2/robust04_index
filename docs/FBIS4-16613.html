BFN [Report from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS reports: "Unsettled on Border Again"] [Text] Attempts by armed groups to penetrate Tajikistan's territory are not ceasing. Over the past 24 hours on the sector of the Pyandzh detachment's 10th border post a large bandit formation tried to break through from Afghanistan onto the Republic's territory but when it met with resistance was obliged to retreat. Fighting also broke out on the sector of the Moskovskiy border guards detachment's 12th border post where heavy weapons had to be used. There were no casualties on the border guards' side.
