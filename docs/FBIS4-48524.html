BFN [Text] Senior police officers have committed themselves to upholding the constitution and fighting against crime and violence. The commitments were made this morning during discussions between the minister of safety and security, Mr. Sydney Mufamadi, and his deputy, Mr. Joe Matthews. The police said in a statement that members would work together in a professional, unbiased, and apolitical way in the service of the community. The senior officers assured all policemen that they would be handled in a reasonable and fair way at all times.
