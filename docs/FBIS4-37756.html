BFN [Text] Yesterday evening Mas'ud Barzani, leader of the Kurdistan Democratic Party, attended the joint meeting held by the information offices of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan and the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP]. The meeting was held in the KDP Political Bureau Headquarters in Salah al-Din summer resort in the Irbil Governorate. During the meeting, the conferees discussed joint information issues and made the necessary decisions. The meeting lasted over one hour.
