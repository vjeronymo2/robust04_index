930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Brown calls for 'new standards' in industry MR GORDON BROWN, the shadow chancellor, yesterday called for 'new standards' in industry after compiling a survey showing boardroom executives taking pay rises of up to 212 per cent, with golden handshakes and share option awards. 'Banks, building societies, insurance companies and companies close to the Tory party are all responsible for pay awards that cannot be justified on economic grounds and in some cases where profits are falling,' he said. 'Continued silence from government ministers is not acceptable - it is time for ministers to call for new standards.'
