BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has declared that during operations conducted against the terrorist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization since the beginning of 1994, 1,037 terrorists were killed, 871 of whom were killed in the state of emergency region. During the same period, a total of 1,976 terrorists were captured. Of these 865 terrorists were caught in the state of emergency region. Mentese added that 88 terrorists surrendered to the security forces in the same period. In a statement he issued today, the interior minister reported that a group of militants -- comprised of approximately 4,500 persons -- was neutralized as a result of intelligence work and weakening within the organization. Mentese declared that as a result of coordinated operations conducted by the Turkish Armed Forces and the security forces, the terrorist organization sustained significant loss of logistic support and militants. In northern Iraq, Mentese reported, 79 terrorists were killed and two were captured. Pointing out that nothing can be achieved through terrorism, Mentese reiterated his call on the youth, who are deceived by the PKK, to surrender as soon as possible and benefit from the repentance law.
