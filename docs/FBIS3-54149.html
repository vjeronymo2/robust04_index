Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Hizballah has claimed responsibility for detonating an explosive charge near the Hula position against a South Lebanese Army [SLA] foot patrol. A Hizballah statement said the explosion killed three SLA personnel, but REUTER cited security sources as saying that only one was killed by the explosion. It added that the SLA and Israeli soldiers carried out a retaliatory attack and shelled areas north of the occupied security strip.
