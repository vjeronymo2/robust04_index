940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): SBC weighs up its rocket scientists - Other Companies Investors will be hoping that the directors of Swiss Bank Corporation will explain at their annual press conference on Wednesday the 44 per cent jump in provisions last year to SFr2.8bn (Dollars 1.9bn). Of special interest is the portion paid to the former O'Connor 'rocket scientists' - a team of derivatives specialists brought in from a Chicago partnership - as it will indicate how profitable SBC's highly respected derivatives business has become. SBC has already announced 1993 net income of SFr1.37bn, up 36 per cent.
