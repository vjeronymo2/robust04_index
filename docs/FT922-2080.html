920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Saatchi to reduce debt via US sale SAATCHI & Saatchi has sold its wholly-owned subsidiary, Yankelovich Skelly White/Clancy Shulman of the US, to an investor group led by Wand Partners. Proceeds will be used to reduce group debt. Consideration consists of an immediate cash payment of Dollars 4.6m (Pounds 2.52m) and a Dollars 4.5m interest-bearing note repayable in tranches up to the end of 1997. Saatchi will also receive non-compete payments totalling Dollars 1.5m. Yankelovich is a public opinion and market research company. In 1991 it made pre-tax profits of Dollars 988,000 on revenues of Dollars 11.1m.
