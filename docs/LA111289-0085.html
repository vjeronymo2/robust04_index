November 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

POLLING PLACE GUARDS

I believe that the district attorney's office made a mistake in investigating the 20 security guards involved at the Santa Ana polling stations. It was a waste of time because the guards were hired and were following orders from the Orange County Republican officials, headed by Tom Fuentes. These are the people to be investigated.

What an excuse by the Republican Party. It's outrageous. This means that the party can take the law and play judge, jury and executioner. Hasn't Tom Fuentes heard that we have police and sheriff's departments to handle such supposed incidents?

Orange County Republican officials made a grave and, I am sure, an intentional mistake. They should pay for it.

VIC PENA

Santa Ana
