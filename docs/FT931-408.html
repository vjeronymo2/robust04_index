930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Caverdale loss rises to Pounds 0.95m Caverdale Group, the motor and engineering trade consumables group, fell further into the red in 1992 with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 950,000, against Pounds 244,000. Mr Arild Nerdrum, chairman, said the results had been affected by charging Pounds 470,000 in goodwill which had been previously written off against reserves. Also, the incorporation of Caverdale Motor Holdings and the acquisition of the loss-making Dunham & Haines showed a combined pre-tax loss of Pounds 220,000. Turnover was Pounds 12.9m (Pounds 10.3m) with acquisitions contributing Pounds 4.36m. Losses per share were 0.94p (1p).
