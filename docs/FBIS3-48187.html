Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 21 (YONHAP) -- A joint South Korean, Japanese and Russian investigation into possible contamination of the East Sea by Russian dumping of nuclear waste is to be carried out in late February, informed sources said Thursday. The three countries had planned to hold the investigation in mid- January, but had to postpone the plan because of differences of opinion over cost sharing, they said. The problems were resolved recently. The area for investigation will be the East Sea off Hokkaido, where the Russian Far-East fleet said it dumped nuclear waste in October last year.
