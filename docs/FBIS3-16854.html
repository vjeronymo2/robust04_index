Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Moscow, March 21 KYODO -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev urged North Korea on Monday [21 March] to abide by controls on nuclear facilities as imposed under the international nuclear safeguard agreement, Japanese officials said. During a meeting with Japanese Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata, Kozyrev argued that all controls should be abided by through inspections by experts from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), the officials said.
