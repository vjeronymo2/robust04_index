Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] There are various rumors circulating in the republic alleging that the opposition forces would start riots in Dushanbe and other towns and rayons of the republic on 13, 15, or 21 March, and the armed groups of the opposition would organize armed incursion on the Tajik-Afghan border. The Tajik Security Ministry, Internal Affairs Ministry, and Defense Ministry state with full responsibility that these rumors are groundless and the republic's social and political situation is under full control. The situation on the Tajik-Afghan border is under control as well. The border is safely controlled by Russian border troops in Tajikistan. The border units of the Tajik Army, Russian servicemen of the 201st motorized rifle division, as well as the units of the CIS coalition peacekeeping forces render all-round assistance to the Russian border troops. There is no doubt that Tajikistan's border and territory will be protected from any kind of invasions. The ministries firmly state that in the republic and on its borders there are no forces able to violate the peace and tranquillity of Tajik citizens.
