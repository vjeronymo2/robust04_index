930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / International Company News: IRI to sell Italgel stake to Nestle ITALY'S financially troubled state holding company IRI has agreed to sell its 62 per cent stake in its frozen foods and sweets manufacturer Italgel to the Swiss food group Nestle for L1,550 a share, or a total of L437bn (Dollars 272m), AP-DJ reports from Rome. The Italgel sale is IRI's first large privatisation since the government said last year it would embark on a major asset sales programme. But IRI's decision was only half a success as it failed to conclude the simultaneously planned sale of its canned food unit Cirio-Bertolli-De Rica. IRI rejected all bids received for Cirio as 'incomplete, conditional and insufficient' and said it would now seek a buyer through private negotiations. It had arranged an international competitive auction for the two food companies, previously owned by its food and retail holding company Societa Meridionale Finanziaria (SME).
