BFN [Text] Dushanbe, May 31, IRNA -- A senior Tajik official here Tuesday expressed satisfaction with Tehran's hosting of second round of talks between the Tajik Government and opposition forces. Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Olimov, speaking to visiting head of CIS and Caucasus Affairs Department of the Iranian Foreign Ministry Mehdi Mohtashami, called for further expansion of Tehran-Dushanbe relations and direct air link between the two countries. Mohtashami, on behalf of the Islamic Republic, formally invited the Tajik foreign minister to take part at the second round of talks to be held in Tehran late in June. The Iranian envoy has also invited Russian officials to participate in the negotiations.
