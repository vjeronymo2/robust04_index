940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equities Futures and Options Trading A steadier performance in UK gilts encouraged a recovery in stock index futures. A particular feature was the first day of dealings in the FT-SE Mid-250 futures contract launched by Liffe. The March contract on the FT-SE 100 closed at 3,268, a discount to cash Volume was 19,322. Dealers said the first day's turnover of 617 in the FT-SE Mid-250 futures contract was disappointing. It closed at 3,927. In traded options, Volume fell back to 47,819 of which 20,033 was dealt in the FT-SE 100 option
