BFN [Text] Osaka, April 1 KYODO -- The Kansai Economic Federation will appoint Tetsuro Kawakami, chairman of Sumitomo Electric Industries Ltd., as its next chairman, federation officials said Friday [1 April]. The Federation, known as Kankeiren, will officially approve the appointment at a regular general board meeting to be held May 22. Kankeiren will also newly appoint nine vice chairmen. Among them, Hiroshi Ohba, president of Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd., and Kenji Kawakatsu, chairman of Sanwa Bank, will continue in their current vice presidential positions. Kyocera Corp. Chairman Kazuo Inamori and Sekisui House Ltd. President Isao Okui will be newly appointed as vice chairmen of the business leaders' organization based in Osaka. The average age of the chairman and vice chairmen will be 64, younger than 73-year average of the current executive lineup, they said.
