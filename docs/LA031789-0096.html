March 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MARINARO GETS HAT TRICK IN LAZERS' 3-2 WIN

Hector Marinaro scored a hat trick to lead the Lazers over the Major Indoor Soccer League-leading Baltimore Blast, 3-2, Thursday night at the Forum. The Lazers (17-19) are now 4-3 against the Blast (24-12).

Marinaro's hat trick was his sixth of the season. The Blast, trailing 3-1, pulled goalie Scott Manning with seven minutes left and got a goal from Domenic Mobilio.
