BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Raul Tukhvatullin] [Text] Ufa May 14 TASS -- "I have not seen a presidential decree on my dismissal, so I cannot comment on reports about my dismissal," Russian Vice-prime Minister Sergey Shakhray said. The announcement followed mass media reports about Shakhray's dismissal from the post of nationalities minister. In an interview with ITAR-TASS on Saturday, Shakhray said that even if these reports turn out to be true, he will continue as vice-prime minister. Shakhray is in Ufa to attend a conference of the regional section of his party of Russian Unity and Accord. Shakhray met with Bashkir President Murtaza Rakhimov today to discuss economic problems and ways to resolve the economic crisis in Bashkiria, a Russian republic in the Volga region.
