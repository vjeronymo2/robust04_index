BFN [Text] Sergey Stepashin, director of the Russian Federation Counterintelligence Service, today visited the 14th Russian Army stationed in the Dniester area. He was accompanied by the Moldavian [Moldovan] Minister for National Security, Vasile Calmoi. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned that Sergey Stepashin praised the work of military counterintelligence in his conversation with the commander of the 14th Army. They also exchanged views on the agreement on cooperation signed between Russian and Moldavian special services.
