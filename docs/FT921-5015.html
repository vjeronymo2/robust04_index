920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Observer: Expert view The rehabilitation of ex-Guinness boss Ernest Saunders continues apace. The British Institute of Management is the latest to give a helping hand. Along with 3i chief executive Ewen Macpherson and Barclays Bank deputy chair- man Humphrey Norrington, Saunders is billed as among 30 'top managers' due at a BIM seminar on Monday. Its topic is business ethics, and he is apparently going as a guest of the Legal Protection Group.
