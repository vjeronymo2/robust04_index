930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / International Company News: Investors rush for Woolworths THE ADollars 2.45bn (USDollars 1.6bn) flotation of Woolworths, the Australian retailer, due to be listed on Monday, has been heavily oversubscribed, perhaps by a factor of four or five. The rush of investor interest is likely to present the vendors, Industrial Equity (IEL), with problems in allocating shares between public and institutional investors. Analysts said IEL may reduce allocations.
