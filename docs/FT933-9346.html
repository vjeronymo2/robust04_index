930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Receivers called in at Dunkeld Dunkeld, the shirt and swimwear manufacturer, went into receivership yesterday. The group's banks were not prepared to refinance the company which was facing a liquidity crisis. Its shares had been suspended at 28p on Friday. In February, the group returned annual pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.45m, against a loss of Pounds 17.5m. Dunkeld has debt of about Pounds 7m, the legacy of rapid expansion in the 1980s.
