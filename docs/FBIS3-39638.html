Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis said the Greek Cypriot side will attend tomorrow's proximity talks with clear positions, goodwill, and honesty. Speaking this afternoon in Nicosia, Mikhailidhis added that if the other side shows the same goodwill we can move forward and reach an agreement on the confidence-building measures, which would be the beginning of a solution to the Cyprus problem. Mikhailidhis called on Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas to abandon his demands for separate sovereign rights. He added that European Union observer Serge Abou is expected in Cyprus next Monday [21 February].
