940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Spargo Consulting beats float forecast Spargo Consulting, the computer services company, reported pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 272,000 to Pounds 606,000 in the six months to June 30. This compares with the Pounds 600,000 forecast at the time of the listing in June. Turnover increased from Pounds 2.31m to Pounds 2.85m. Earnings per share came out at 3.2p (1.52p).
