BFN [Unattributed report: "Belarusian Security Council Held Session"] [Text] A session of the Belarusian Security Council was held on 11 April. Conditions for Belarus possibly joining Bill Clinton's "Partnership for Peace" program were discussed. BELAPAN has learned that a final decision on the issue was not adopted. According to competent circles, there is a decision to suspend the resolution of this issue until Belarusian presidential elections are held. Participants in the session also approved a government proposal concerning urgent measures to combat corruption. An interdepartmental commission, headed by First Deputy Supreme Soviet Chairman Vyacheslav Kuznetsov, is authorized to implement this program addressing the coordination of activities between law enforcement agencies.
