Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Eduard Nalbandyan, the Armenian charge d'affaires ad interim in Egypt, and (Bogos Akobov), a Moscow diplomat and ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary, submitted a message from Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan to Amir of Kuwait Jabir al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah during their visit to Kuwait on 5 February. The Armenian Foreign Ministry press and public relations service reported that numerous matters relating to the establishment and development of ties between Armenia and Kuwait were discussed in a friendly and warm atmosphere. Eduard Nalbandyan and (Bogos Akobov) were received the same day by the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah. Matters pertaining to cooperation between the two countries were raised during the meeting.
