Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with a pre-term dissolution of the Supreme Kenges, which was charged with the responsibility of arranging the functioning of courts, and in conformity with the law of the Republic of Kazakhstan on the temporary delegation of additional powers to the president and heads of local administrations, [President] Nursultan Nazarbayev issued the following resolution on organizational issues of the work of the Supreme Court of the Republic of Kazakhstan: To state that the number of members of the Supreme Court of the Republic of Kazakhstan is determined by the president of the Republic of Kazakhstan; and the structure and number of personnel of the Supreme Court of the Republic of Kazakhstan is determined by the president of the Republic of Kazakhstan after they have been submitted by the Supreme Court chairman of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
