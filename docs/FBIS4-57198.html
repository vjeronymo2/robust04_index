BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 12 May: According to the latest data disclosed by the National Institute of Statistics and Census cited by Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo, the unemployment rate has fallen from 9.9 to 9.3 percent. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 1644 GMT 6 May 94) The National Accounting System has reported that the GDP grew by 6 percent in 1993 compared with the previous year. Investments were up 13.7 percent in general, though they declined 15.7 percent on tractors and 12.7 percent on agricultural machinery and inputs. The items that registered the highest growth were public utilities, with 11 percent; construction, with 10.9 percent; and financial houses, insurance, and property, with 10 percent. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 2250 GMT 10 May 94)
