CSO [Text] By order of Hojjat ol-Eslam Rahimian, the vice regent's representative and chief of the Islamic Revolution Martyr Foundation, Javad Moqadam was appointed general manager of computer services and data processing at this foundation. By order of Ayatollah Yazdi, chief of the judicial power, Hadi Haydarzadeh, chief of Branch 15 of the Administrative Justice Court, retained his position as deputy minister of justice and was appointed as chief of the Administrative Justice Court. By order of Dr. Vahid Dastjerdi, chief of the Red Crescent Society of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Mohammad Hoseyn Dajmar was appointed general secretary of the Red Crescent Society.
