BFN [Text] Plock, June 10 -- Asked by journalists whether President Lech Walesa's positive assessment of the "Strategy for Poland" programme means that the conflict between the government and the president came to an end, Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak said that never there was such a conflict but there were only differences of opinion. The prime minister told journalists during his visit to the Plock petrochemical combine SA that the "Strategy for Poland" document was an example of agreement.
