Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Italian] Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri said at a summit of five NATO defense ministers held in Aviano that "the decision of the international community to bring the Yugoslav crisis under control is now irreversible." As for the ultimatum, Fabbri said that "a full enforcement of the conditions set by the NATO order is required. There can be no bargains and no gifts. However, the news we received during our discussions is encouraging."
