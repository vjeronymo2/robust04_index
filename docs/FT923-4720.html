920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Armour Trust declines to Pounds 1.46m Armour Trust, confectionery maker and supplier of car accessories, announced pre-tax profits slightly down from Pounds 1.51m to Pounds 1.46m for the year to April 30. The fall reflected a 6 per cent decline in the contribution from the automotive division. Confectionery reported record operating profits, the company said. Earnings per share were 3.9p (4.1p) and the proposed final dividend is 1.1735p (1.1175p) for a 1.4885p (1.4175p) total. The shares are traded on the over-the-counter market.
