Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Police Minister Moshe Shahal recommends continuing the closure on the territories. Police Commissioner Rafi Peled added that the closure will be lifted gradually beginning after the Muslim holidays, but did not mention any date. Commissioner Peled told our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky that the police have a high deployment in sensitive areas where Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] might operate, but stressed that a police presence cannot be assured everywhere.
