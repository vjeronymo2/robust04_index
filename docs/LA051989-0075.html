May 19, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

Advanced Marketing Services: The San Diego-based supplier of books to discount warehouse chains, including Price Club, reported fourth-quarter net income of $565,000 on sales of $26.3 million, up from a profit of $551,000 on sales of $24.1 million over the same quarter the previous year.

For the fiscal year ended March 31, the company reported a profit of $5.1 million on sales of $155.6 million, up from a profit of $3.6 million on sales of $110 million for the previous fiscal year.
