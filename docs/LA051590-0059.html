May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MALATHION SPRAYING

The state Department of Food and Agriculture will conduct aerial spraying of malathion tonight beginning at 9 p.m., weather permitting.

Area: Nine square miles in Compton will be sprayed for an infestation of the Mexican fruit fly. For Mediterranean fruit fly infestation spraying will cover 48 square miles encompassing Bell, Cudahy, Huntington Park, Lynwood and parts of Athens, Bell Gardens, Commerce, Compton, East Los Angeles, South Gate, Vernon, Walnut Park and Watts.

Precautions: Stay indoors; keep animals indoors; wash animal dishes and toys left outside; cover cars; keep doors and windows closed.

Information: Toll-free numbers for the Agricultural Commissioner: (800) 356-2894; (800) 225-1346.
