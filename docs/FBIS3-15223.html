Language: English Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Viktor Demidenko for TASS] [Text] Kiev March 3 TASS -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk flew to the U.S. for an official visit today. A programme of the visit includes his meetings with U.S. President Bill Clinton and Vice President Albert Gore. Kravchuk will also meet senior officials of the House of Representatives and the Senate, representatives of the Administration and the U.S. Congress, heads of the corporation for foreign private investments and the Chamber of Commerce. Ukrainian-American talks will also be held in the Pentagon. A number of important Ukrainian-American documents are expected to be signed during the visit, primarily a treaty on the encouragement and mutual protection of investments and a convention between the governments of the two countries on avoiding double taxation and preventing the dodging of the income tax and the capital levy tax, the press service of the Ukrainian president reported. Documents to be signed also include an agreement between the Ukrainian and U.S. Governments on cooperation in the sphere of science and technology, and agreement between the Ukrainian Defence Ministry and the U.S. Department of Defence on rendering additional assistance in the elimination of strategic nuclear weapons and on preventing the proliferation of mass destruction weapons. Kravchuk will attend in New York the ceremony of the opening of the Ukrainian general consulate and will meet leaders of Ukrainian and Jewish public organisations, as well as representatives of the biggest American corporations. He will be received by U.N. Secretary-General Butrus-Ghali.
