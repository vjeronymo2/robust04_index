February 8, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

LOS ANGELES CAMPAIGN '89

Mayor's Race

Mayor Tom Bradley meets with Police Chief Daryl Gates today at 10:30 a.m. to announce a new city program to evict drug dealers from apartments within the city.

Councilman Nate Holden and former Los Angeles County Supervisor Baxter Ward debate at 11 a.m. Thursday at the Nickerson Gardens housing project auditorium, 1513 East 103rd St.

City Council Races

Councilman Zev Yaroslavsky seeks the endorsement of Sierra Club officials at a City Hall meeting today.

Yaroslavsky appears Thursday at 11 a.m. on Century Cable television to discuss issues in the 5th City Council District.

Campaign Quip

"I consider my running for office to be part of my ministry as well. It's all about feeding the hungry, sheltering the homeless and setting the captives free."

-- The Rev. Jules S. Bagneris III, an African Methodist Episcopal minister opposing 7th district Councilman Ernani Bernardi.
