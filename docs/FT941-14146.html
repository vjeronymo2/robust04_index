940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Golden Vale raises Danish stake to 66% Golden Vale, the Irish dairy group, has exercised an option to acquire a further 33 per cent of Vejle Margarinefabrik, the Danish margarine company, bringing its holding to 66 per cent. Golden Vale paid a further DKr25m (Pounds 2.47m) on December 31 to increase its original stake purchased in May. Its investment now amounts to DKr50m.
