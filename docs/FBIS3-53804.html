Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak resumed his talks with Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat during an expanded round at the Presidential Palace attended by Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs. The talks were attended on the Palestinian side by Yasir 'Abd- Rabbuh, head of the PLO's Information Department; Akram Haniyah, 'Arafat's adviser on occupied territories affairs; Nabil Sha'th, president 'Arafat's political adviser; Sa'id Kamal, Palestine's ambassador in Cairo; and Zuhdi al-Qidrah, Fatah's representative in Cairo.
