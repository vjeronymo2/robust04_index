Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The secretaries general of the Trade and Food Industry Workers Union [Sindicato de Comercio y Gastronomia] from Cuba's 14 provinces and the special municipality are meeting today at the CTC [Cuban Workers Federation] to evaluate the process of holding Parliament in the Workplace assemblies at work centers. In the presence of Osmany Cienfuegos, vice president of the Council of State, these worker leaders will assess how the subject of tourism was reflected during the meetings at which this subject was discussed by the various unions. One of the demands of the Trade and Food Industry Workers Union's membership is the need to designate a more flexible manner in which each work group donates a portion of its tips to indirect workers and public health. During the afternoon, Sonia Rodriguez, [minister] president of the State Committee for Material and Technical Supply, will be participating in the Parliament in the Workplace process of the Trade and Food Industry Workers Union at the CTC.
