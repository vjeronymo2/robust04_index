Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Invincible Is Cause of Korean-Style Socialism Led by Great Strategist"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, February 1 (KCNA) -- The cause of socialism continues advancing triumphantly in Korea, unshaken by any challenge and manoeuvres of the imperialists. The key to this advance is the guidance of this cause by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, a great strategist of revolution. NODONG SINMUN says this in a by-lined article today. The article says Comrade Kim Chong-il is leading the revolution and construction of our age to victory with rare wisdom and ever-victorious, chuche-based strategy and tactics. Noting that the Korean people are able to successfully accomplish the revolutionary cause of chuche as they have the dear leader at the head of the party and the revolution in the historical period in which the cause of socialism is being carried to completion, the article says this is a great fortune and happiness for the Korean people. The article quotes the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song as saying: "Comrade Kim Chong-il is a true leader of the people, possessed of literary and military accomplishments and loyalty and filial piety. He is an outstanding thinker and theoretician, politician and military strategist and the pattern of those infinitely loyal and devoted to the country and the people." The article says: The history of the dear leader's leadership of the revolution and construction is recorded with brilliant leadership of a great strategist of the revolution who has led the socialist cause of our age along a straight path to victory with ever-victorious strategy and tactics. His traits as a great strategist of the revolution find graphic manifestation in that he, with a scientific insight into the requirements of the time and revolution, clearly indicates all the strategic and tactical questions arising in accomplishing the cause of socialism. His greatness as a strategist of the revolution is well illustrated by the fact that, after the collapse of socialism in some countries, he has given a clear exposition of strategic and tactical questions for rebuilding the socialist movement on a new foundation and leading it to a constant upswing. The traits of the dear leader as a great strategist of the revolution find expression also in that he has led socialist construction to a success, overcoming difficulties with tactical resources, and has effected a steady upsurge in revolution and construction with bold and ambitious operations. He employs mysterious tactics of turning adversity into favorable circumstances and turning a misfortune into a blessing in face of any formidable enemy and vast tasks of the revolution. It is the source of invincible strength that makes it possible to actively overcome the difficulties in the revolution and constantly make an impressive advance in the revolution and construction. All the operations shaped by him are far-sighted ones foreseeing the distant future of the revolution. They are characterized by unexcelled grit, skilful organizational ability and revolutionary sweep which guarantee greatest successes in the shortest period through the mobilization of all forces. The article further says: The great strategy and tactics of the dear leader who plans all the work in a bold and ambitious way are most revolutionary ones based on unquestioned trust in the popular masses. It is because he is possessed of matchless intelligence and scientific insight that he has been able to map out great strategy and tactics one after another to bring the socialist cause of our age to accomplishment. The unexcelled grit and will unshaken under any stress and storm are the secret of his advancing great strategy and tactics for leading the revolution always to victory under any circumstances. Comrade Kim Chong-il is, indeed, an outstanding leader of the socialist cause of our age, who is possessed of the qualities and traits as a great strategist of the revolution on the highest level.
