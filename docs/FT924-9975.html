921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Wm Sindall incurs loss of Pounds 417,000 AFTER redundancy costs of Pounds 138,000, William Sindall incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 417,000 in the opening half of 1992. That compared with a profit of Pounds 432,000, and with a loss of Pounds 4.19m for the full year after exceptional charges of Pounds 5m. Turnover of the group, which is engaged in building and civil engineering, declined from Pounds 33.9m to Pounds 22m. A month ago judgment was given in favour of the group in an action for rescission of a contract made in 1988 under which land was purchased for Pounds 5.08m from the Cambridgeshire County Council. Since then, the council had achieved a mandatory stay of execution by paying into court Pounds 8m. The accounts take in nothing from the judgment and the costs of the action have been fully provided. Losses per share were 7.55p (earnings 2.65p). There is no interim dividend (1.5p).
