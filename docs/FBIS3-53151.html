Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A mission of the International Monetary Fund has held its first meeting with the Sudanese side headed by Dr. Muhammad Khyar al-Zubayr, minister of state at the Ministry of Economic Planning and Investment. The meeting reviewed economic performance and the national and financial product, rates of growth, and the performance of the general budget. Muhammad Khayr al-Zubayr explained that the mission will continue its meetings with the finance minister and the governor of the Bank of Sudan.
