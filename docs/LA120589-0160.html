December 5, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

TOLLIVER TO START FOR CHARGERS

Rookie quarterback Billy Joe Tolliver will get another start for the San Diego Chargers next weekend when they play the Washington Redskins, Coach Dan Henning said.

Henning also said Monday that he has not considered whether Tolliver will start San Diego's final two games. The Chargers play at Kansas City on Dec. 17 and end their season at home Dec. 24 against Denver.

"The main idea is to win football games and at the same time find out as much about players as you possibly can," Henning said. "In both games that Billy's been in, he's done some things you would expect from a rookie. He's also made some plays. The results haven't been a lot different from some of our other games."

Tolliver made his second start of the season Sunday in the Chargers' 20-17 loss to the New York Jets.
