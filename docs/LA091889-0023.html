September 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

Members of the Los Angeles-based Jazz Tap Ensemble will be featured tonight in what is reportedly the first full evening of tap ever held at Carnegie Hall in New York City. . Jazz Tap artistic director Lynn Dally and music director Eric Von Essen will join other guests (Bunny Briggs, Brenda Bufalino, Steve Condos, Arthur Duncan, Jimmy Slyde and Savion Glover) in a program headlined by dancer/actor Gregory Hines. ALEENE MacMINN
