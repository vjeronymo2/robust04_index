921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Letter: Imaginative quality Sir, Your leader, 'Predators come out to play' (October 6), contrasts the doubtful quality of Trafalgar House's assets and earnings with RHM. But the largest single asset on RHM's balance sheet - brands - just appeared out of the blue in 1988, valued on a basis one can only describe as imaginative. D R Myddelton, professor of finance and accounting, Cranfield School of Management, Cranfield, Bedford MK43 0AL
