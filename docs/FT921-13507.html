920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / Malaysian support for Vietnam Malaysia said yesterday it would support an attempt by socialist Vietnam to sign a treaty and join the Association of South East Asian Nations (Asean), set up as an anti-communist bulwark in the Vietnam War, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Mr Mahathir Mohammed, the Malaysian prime minister, said he would raise the issue of Vietnam's membership at the Asean summit meeting in Singapore on January 27 and 28. Mr Vo Van Kiet, the Vietnamese prime minister, is in Malaysia on a four-day visit.
