Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Irina Bolshova and Vladimir Kikilo] [Text] Moscow March 30 TASS -- "We want real cooperation, not an ostentatious one," President Boris Yeltsin said on Wednesday when receiving a U.S. business delegation led by Secretary of Commerce Ronald Brown, Russian Minister for Foreign Economic Relations Oleg Davydov told ITAR-TASS. Davydov is attending the current session of the American-Russian Committee for the Development of Business Cooperation at President-Hotel here. Four Russian-U.S. trade documents are expected to be signed at the session, Davydov said. These are: -- regulations for a committee on the development of Russian-U.S. business cooperation, -- regulations for the establishment of American business centres, to be funded by the U.S., -- a joint statement on Russian commodities' access to the American market and of American ones to the Russian market, on the training of personnel and standardisation, -- an agreement on terms for the supply of American medicines to the Russian market.
