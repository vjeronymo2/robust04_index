October 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FEINSTEIN, WILSON CAMPAIGNS FOR GOVERNORSHIP

Wilson's rhetoric is very familiar. Like George Bush, when running for President, Wilson pledges to be the "education governor."

However, he is part of the same class of politicians who have seen constant cuts in education -- Ronald Reagan, George Deukmejian and Bush. We cannot afford another clone of Reaganomics for our state.

GWEN KOZMAN

Irvine
