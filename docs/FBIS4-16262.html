BFN [Excerpts] Emomali Rakhmonov, head of state and chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan, has left for Moscow to take part in the meeting of CIS heads of states and governments. He is accompanied by Kazidavlat Koimdodov, deputy chairman of the Tajik Supreme Soviet, some members of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers and a number of experts. Emomali Rakhmanov and his delegation arrived in Moscow the same day. [passage omitted] The same day, Rakhmonov met with Ambassador [Ramiro] Piriz-Ballon, the UN secretary general's envoy to Tajikistan. Piriz-Ballon informed the Tajik head of state about the intra-Tajik talks. He highly assessed the Tajik leadership's (?efforts) to start the talks and its measures to bring peace and calm to the country. Emomali Rakhmonov appraised as positive the role of the United Nations in stabilizing the situation, not only in Tajikistan but throughout the Central Asian region. Emomali Rakhmonov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Soviet, spoke to journalists at Moscow airport. In a brief statement, he said he will meet with Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. In his meetings with Russian Federation leaders he will discuss the issue of Tajikistan's entry to the ruble zone.
