BFN [Text] Guangzhou, April 21 (CNS) -- A Deng Xiaoping medal officially produced and put on sale for the fist time has been well received with its sale in the city exceeding 2,000 pieces in just one month. The obverse side of the copper medal portrays Deng's head and the characters "Our General Architect" while the converse side shows a map of China and with the characters "Construct socialism with Chinese characteristics" both in Chinese and in English. The medal selling at RMB 46 is produced by institutions in Beijing to meet the demands of the public there and also as a gift item on the occasion of the 90th birthday of Deng Xiaoping on August 22 this year. An observer here believed that production of the badge was of great significance not only for its intrinsic but also its monetary value.
