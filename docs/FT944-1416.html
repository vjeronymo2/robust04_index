941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Blackwood Hodge A rival bid is expected today for the preference shares in Blackwood Hodge, the engineering company owned by BM Group. Mr Stephen Cockburn, acting in a personal capacity, said that he would launch a bid for two of the three classes of the company's preference shares. The board of BM announced an offer for the preference shares in October which closes on December 22. BM then plans to put Blackwood Hodge into liquidation.
