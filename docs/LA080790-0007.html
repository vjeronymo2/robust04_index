August 7, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PROPOSALS ON BALANCING FEDERAL DEFICIT

There they go again! Robin Hood rides in reverse again!

While the congressional Budget Reduction Committee is planning to raise taxes and cut benefits for the lower- and middle- income citizens, Congress is also working on approval of a $55-billion subsidy over five years for the very wealthiest farmers and huge agricultural landowners (Part A, July 26). There is an election this November.

TERRY L. MALONE

Orange
