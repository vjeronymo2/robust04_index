January 24, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

TIA JUANITA WINS

Tia Juanita beat B. J. Brenner by a head Tuesday in the $32,000 Nepal Purse for fillies and mares on the grass at Gulfstream Park. Ridden by Jean Cruguet, Tia Juanita was timed at 1:41 2/5 in the one-mile feature, and paid $18.20, $9.40 and $5.60. B. J. Brenner paid $16 and $5.20. Host of Angels paid $4.20 to show.
