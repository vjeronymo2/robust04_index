BFN [Report by Marcela Gutierrez from the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas met with members of his cabinet on 17 June. It has been reported that the central subject of the meeting was Chiapas. Shortly thereafter, the president of the republic announced the appointment of Hector Hernandez Llamas as executive spokesman for the peace process in Chiapas. Hernandez will work to fulfill the social and economic pledges made during the San Cristobal de las Casas talks. There was much activity today at Los Pinos. Governor of Chiapas Javier Lopez Moreno arrived at 1400 local time. Agrarian Reform Secretary Victor Cervera Pacheco, Agriculture Secretary Carlos Hank Gonzalez, Communications and Transportation Secretary Emilio Gamboa Patron, and Chamber of Deputies leader Maria de los Angeles Moreno also attended the meeting. At 1600 local time today, the Presidency's social Communications Directorate reported that Hernandez Llamas will act as intermediary between the federal government and the State of Chiapas. The office also said that Chiapas Governor Moreno will be in charge of peace process activities. Hernandez Llamas will work closely with Moreno. Moreno has held several public posts in the government.
