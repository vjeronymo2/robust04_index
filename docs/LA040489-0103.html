April 4, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: FIRST CITIZENS BANK EARNS $130,470 IN 1ST QUARTER

First Citizens Bank, a fast-growing bank in Sherman Oaks, said it earned $130,470 in the first quarter ended March 31, up 189% from a year earlier.

Total assets on March 31 were $28.8 million, up 22% from a year earlier.
