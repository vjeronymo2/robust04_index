Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At the bilateral talks in Washington, Israel has rejected Jordan's request to discuss the border between the two countries. The Israeli delegation was instructed by the government to turn down the request. According to Israel, the matter should only be discussed when the two sides come up with a draft for a peace agreement between Israel and Jordan. Talks on the Israel-Jordan track will resume Tuesday [22 February] and will focus on security arrangements.
