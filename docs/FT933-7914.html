930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Miner dies and two are trapped in colliery cave-in A colliery under-manager was found dead last night by rescuers digging with their hands to reach colleagues trapped underground after 50 yards of tunnel roof caved in at Bilsthorpe colliery, Nottinghamshire. Two men were later released, but two more remained trapped. Earlier a sixth man, rescued after he crawled clear on hands and knees, had been taken to hospital with back injuries and shock.
