Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] The cabinet is in session in Prague today. Jitka Kucerova phoned in this report. [Kucerova] The cabinet has decided on the participation of the Czech Republic in the Partnership for Peace program. The so-called presentation document will be submitted by 31 March. The individual program of the Czech Republic in this project will be worked out on its basis. The government also discussed the situation of national minorities. Vaclav Klaus said the following on this question: Our point of departure is no discrimination against minorities. The government, however, does not intend to set up any special institution to discuss these problems. A draft amendment to the law on land communications was also adopted. In practice, this will mean the introduction of the long-discussed motorway tax. The preliminary day of introduction is 1 January 1995.
