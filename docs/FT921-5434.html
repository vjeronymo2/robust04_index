920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: LVMH places FFr1.5bn of notes LVMH, the French luxury goods group, has placed privately FFr1.5bn of repackaged subordinated notes - Titres Subordonnes a Duree Indeterminee, or TSDIs, Reuter reports from Paris LVMH said the operation, yielding net available cash of FFr1.1bn, will refinance part of its long-term debt as it reaches maturity as well as short-term borrowings used to fund the group's 1991 acquisitions. The issue was placed with leading European and Japanese banks.
