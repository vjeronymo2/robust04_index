May 25, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Keating Sues Government: Beleaguered Arizona businessman Charles H. Keating Jr. and top executives of his bankrupt American Continental Corp. sued the federal government for $300 million, claiming that regulators wrongly took the company's property -- its Lincoln Savings & Loan subsidiary -- and destroyed it. The suit, filed in U.S. Claims Court in Washington, is similar to one American Continental filed a year ago after regulators seized the Irvine-based thrift.
