930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Helene/Gabicci bid goes unconditional Helene, the clothing distributor, has won control of Gabicci, the USM-quoted casual clothing company, with shareholders representing 76.19 per cent of the shares accepting the 2.043-for-1 share exchange bid. The offer was declared unconditional and re-mains open until further notice. The announcement follows the withdrawal of Apax Partners, a venture capital company which was considering a rival bid. The offer yesterday valued Gabicci shares at about 48p each. The shares were unchanged at 46p.
