June 23, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

FULLERTON LOSES, 3-1, IN METRO LEAGUE GAME

The Fullerton Blues lost to the Pomona Colts, 3-1, in a Metro League baseball game at Cal Poly Pomona Thursday.

The Blues scored in the second inning when Jeff Manship singled, advanced to second on Kevin Humphery's single and scored on Chris Sheffer's single.
