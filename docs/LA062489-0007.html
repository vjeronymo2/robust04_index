June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ZSA ZSA'S ENCOUNTER WITH POLICE

Ms. Gabor admits her vehicle registration and driver's license were expired. She also admits having an open container of liquor in her car when stopped. She slapped the policeman who stopped her, and drove away because she didn't know what the "F" word meant.

She then calls a news conference to announce she is "standing up for the battered women in America." It would appear she was really just standing up for all the cameras and as a result dealt a damaging blow to the issue of battered women.

MICHAEL KENYON

Fountain Valley
