941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices White pepper prices have tended firmer in recent weeks, reports Man Producten, the main reason being the growing uncertainty about the size next year's crops. The long drought in Indonesia will no doubt affect 1995 production, and supplies from China and Sarawak are gradually drying up. Muntok white spot was priced at at USDollars 3,700 a tonne and for November/December shipment at Dollars 3,650, cif. Black pepper prices tended somewhat easier, mainly due to slow demand in recent weeks. Prices seem to have stabilised at about Dollars 2,650 a tonne for spot black pepper faq and at Dollars 2,575, cif, for November/December shipment. While supplies from India and Indonesia were very limited there were more offers from Brazil.
