Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Information Department of the Tajikistan's Foreign Ministry reported to INTERFAX that on Monday a frontier post deployed on the section of the 12th frontier post of the Moscow frontier department on the Tajik-Afghan border was fired at by four jet shells. The adversary's firing hardware was damaged by counterfire. The same day a frontier detachment stationed on the section of the Khorog frontier came under small arms fire from Afghanistan territory. No casualties are reported. On the whole, Tajikistan's territory and Russian frontier detachments have been subjected to shelling from the Afghan territory 18 times over the month and a half of this year, said leader of the Information Department of the Tajik Foreign Ministry Zafar Saidov. In his words, this creates conditions for further destabilization of the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border.
