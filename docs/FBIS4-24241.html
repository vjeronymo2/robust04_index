BFN [Text] According to reports reaching KBC newsroom on the Mathare constituency by-election, the late Dr. Fredrick Shikuku Masinde of FORD [Forum for the Restoration of Democracy]-Kenya won the seat. Dr. Masinde won a total of 6,609 against the close FORD-Asili candidate, Mr. Geoffrey Macharia, who garnered 5,984 votes while Mr. Andrew Kimani Ngumba who is contesting on a KANU [Kenyan African National Union] ticket got 3,533 votes.
