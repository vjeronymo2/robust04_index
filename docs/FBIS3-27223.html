Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 22 (XINHUA) -- The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue a new set of stamps to honor four noted Chinese non-communist patriots February 25. It will be the second set of stamps commemorating the contributions by non-communist patriots for China's liberation and socialist construction. The four patriots are Chen Qiyou, former chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party (Public Interest Party), Chen Shutong, former chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Ma Xulun, former chairman of the Central Committee of the China Association for the Promotion of Democracy and former vice-chairman of the China Democratic League, and Xu Deheng, former chairman of the Jiu San Society's Central Committee. The face values of the stamps will be 0.2 yuan, 0.2 yuan, 0.5 yuan and 0.5 yuan. The stamps were designed by Zhu Zhengeng, associate professor of the Department of Fine Arts of the Central China Teachers University.
