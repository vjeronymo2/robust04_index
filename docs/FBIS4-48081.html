BFN [Text] Pretoria May 10 SAPA -- Former arch-enemies Cuban leader Fidel Castro and South Africa's now Deputy President F W de Klerk and the chief of the defence force, General Georg Meiring, chatted amiably at lunch on Tuesday [10 May]. "Welcome to South Africa," Mr de Klerk told the Cuban leader on being introduced by President Nelson Mandela at his inauguration lunch at the Presidensie. Mr Castro replied, through an interpreter, "I hope you are successful". Mr de Klerk then introduced Mr Castro to Gen Meiring. The two uniformed men both shook each other's hands warmly.
