BFN [Text] Reports indicate that preparations are in full swing for the inauguration of the new state president on 9 May and that invitations are to be sent to heads of state all over the world. Among the guests of honor are leaders of terrorist countries and sworn enemies of South Africa and the West, such as Fidel Castro, Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, and Saddam Husayn. Western leaders mentioned include President Bill Clinton, Mr. John Major, and Dr. Helmut Kohl. Political observers believe this could be a protocol and diplomatic hot potato that might leave organizers of the event with red faces, as Western leaders distance themselves from the leaders of the terrorist countries.
