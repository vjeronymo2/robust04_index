March 11, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A motorist on the San Diego-Coronado Bay Bridge stopped his car in mid-span about 5 p.m. Saturday and jumped to his death, San Diego police said.

The man was identified as William K. Klein, 40, of Philadelphia, by San Diego County Coroner Chuck Kelly.

San Diego police said that the apparent suicide victim was driving east on the bridge when he stopped and jumped over the side. A San Diego Harbor Police patrol boat, which was nearby, recovered the body within a few minutes.
