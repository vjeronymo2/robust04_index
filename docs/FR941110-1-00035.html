DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration 50 CFR Part 641 [I.D. 110294C] Reef Fish Fishery of the Gulf of Mexico; Public Hearings AGENCY: National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Commerce. ACTION: Public hearings; request for comments.

SUMMARY: The Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council will convene nine public hearings on Draft Amendment 8 to the Fishery Management Plan for the Reef Fish Resources of the Gulf of Mexico. Draft Amendment 8 contains alternatives for limited access systems for the Gulf commercial red snapper fishery, including license limitation and individual transferable quota systems. DATES: Written comments on the proposed amendment will be accepted until January 4, 1995. Public hearings will be held in December. See SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION for specific dates and times of the hearings.
