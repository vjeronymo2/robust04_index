Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- The high-level delegation of the Defence Ministry of the Royal Government of Cambodia headed by Co-Ministers of Defence Tie Banh and Tie Chamrat left here today after a 5-day visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u, generals and officers of the Korean People's Army and Cambodian Ambassador to Korea Oum Mannorine. A farewell ceremony took place at the airport. The delegation visited Mangyongdae and inspected the Kang Kon Military Academy and the Mangyongdae School children's palace while staying in Korea. It laid a wreath in the revolutionary martyrs cemetery on Mt. Taesong and saw a circus performance.
