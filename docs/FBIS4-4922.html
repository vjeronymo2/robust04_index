BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 1 (KCNA) -- Classical works of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were reported in different countries. The Guinean paper HOROYA printed a portrait of Comrade Kim Chong-il and carried the gist of his work "On the Fundamentals of Revolutionary Party Building". The Nepalese paper NEPAL SAMACHAR, the Cameroonian paper LA VISION and a Radio of the Congo gave the gist of his work "Abuses of Socialism are Intolerable". The first issue of DIPLOMATISCHER PRESSEDIENST, a magazine published in Vienna, Austria, carried the gist of his work "The Historical Lesson in Building Socialism and the General Line of Our Party".
