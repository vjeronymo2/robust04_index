Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 22 KYODO -- Japan welcomed Tuesday [22 March] the peaceful general elections held in El Salvador on Sunday, saying it was a major turning point for the peace process of the Latin American country. Foreign Ministry spokesman Terusuke Terada said Japan welcomes the elections held "without turmoil or political terrorism" and that it hopes the elections will serve to advance the peace process there. Fifteen Japanese election observers took part in monitoring the voting and ballot counting. They are scheduled to return to Japan on Friday.
