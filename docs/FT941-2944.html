940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / People: Non-executive directors Shiraz Malik-Noor, the chairman and chief executive of the USM-quoted Royal Jelly company Regina, has parted company with the other members of the board over 'a difference in management style'. Despite announcing increased turnover for the opening six months last month, Malik-Noor said then that it was not possible to sustain the increase. The company aims to appoint a non-executive chairman and a further non-executive director very soon.
