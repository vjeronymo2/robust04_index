BFN [Text] Stuttgart (DDP/ADN) -- PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat has arrived for a one-day visit to Stuttgart. This morning he will meet the leadership of the Daimler-Benz group, and a meeting between 'Arafat and chief executive Edzard Reuter is also planned. According to Daimler-Benz AG, 'Arafat's visit is "an important element in the group's strategy of actively supporting the peace process in the entire Middle East region." The PLO chairman is accompanied by his closest economic and political advisors. During his visit to Daimler-Benz, 'Arafat will be shown products and research projects. The talks will focus on extending telecommunication systems and solving transport problems in the Middle East. This evening 'Arafat is to meet Baden-Wuerttemberg's Prime Minister Erwin Teufel. The ministry of state announced that their talks will focus on the Middle East peace process and the role of the PLO. Afterward, Teufel will host a reception and dinner in honor of his guest at the Neues Schloss [New Palace] in Stuttgart.
