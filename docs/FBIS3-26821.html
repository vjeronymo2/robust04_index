Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] In Chengdu yesterday morning [14 February], the provincial party committee and government held a meeting on banking, financial, and taxation matters. Zhu Rongji, member of the Political Bureau's Standing Committee and vice premier of the State Council, attended and addressed the meeting. He said: The central authorities' basic policy for this year is to seize the opportunity to deepen reform, to widen the scope of opening up, to promote development, and to maintain stability [zhuazhu jiyu, shenhue gaige, guoda kaifang, cujin fazhan, baochi wending]. There is a need to do a better job in reform and opening up. On this year's development in the financial situation, Zhu Rongji pointed out the following three tasks: 1) Under all circumstances, we must control the scale of fixed-asset investment, and the scale of capital construction should correspond with national economic development; 2) Professional banks should be turned into commercial banks; 3) People's banks should make earnest efforts to transform their functions and to supervise the work of nonprofessional banks. Among those present at the meeting were Xie Shijie [provincial party secretary], Xiao Yang [governor], Song Baorui, Gu Haiqing, and Yao Jinxiang, as well as people in charge of banks, financial and taxation departments, and insurance companies at the provincial and Chengdu city levels. As has been reported, Comrade Zhu Rongji left Chengdu for Beijing yesterday afternoon.
