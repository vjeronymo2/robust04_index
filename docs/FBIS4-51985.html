BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 30 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], received a floral basket presented in joint name by Tie Banh and Tie Chamrat, co-defence ministers of Cambodia, on the 62nd KPA founding anniversary. The floral basket was conveyed to the Korean Embassy in Phnom Penh on April 25.
