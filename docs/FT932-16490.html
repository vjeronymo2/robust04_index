930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Strike halts Zaire capital A one-day protest strike called by opponents of Zaire's president Mobutu Sese Seko paralysed the capital yesterday but there were no reports of unrest. Reuter reports from Kinshasa. Armed troops and paramilitary gendarmes patrolled Kinshasa's deserted streets where army jeeps were almost the only traffic.
