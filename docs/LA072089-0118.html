July 20, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Soybean Trading Data Demanded: Lee Adams, president of the American Soybean Assn., asked the Chicago Board of Trade to release trading positions of "all market participants that held (soybean) contracts for more than 1 million bushels in July soybean futures during May through July. If we do not get a quick and satisfactory response, we are prepared to take appropriate legal action," Adams said in a statement. The association represents soybean growers. The commodities exchange last week ordered holders of large July soybean positions to sell all holdings of more than 1 million bushels by today.
