Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Report by Ivan Sergeyev: "The GKChS Has Been Upgraded to a Ministry"] [Text] Under an ukase signed by the President of Russia, "On the Structure of Federal Executive Agencies," the State Committee of the Russian Federation for Civil Defense, Emergencies and Disaster Amelioration has been transformed into a ministry. At the signing of the ukase the new ministry took over the functions of the abolished State Committee of the Russian Federation for the Social Protection of Citizens and the Rehabilitation of Areas Which Suffered From the Chernobyl and other Radiation Disasters and the Committee for Special-Purpose Underwater Work. The Russian Federal Air and Space Search and Rescue Service is also transferred to the jurisdiction of the new ministry with the status of a state establishment.
