Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India today warned Pakistan that the process of bilateral dialogue will suffer a serious setback if Islamabad moves its resolution on Kashmir at the UN Human Rights Commission in Geneva. An External Affairs Ministry spokesman said in New Delhi today that the whole world knows that Pakistan's only intention is to get political gains out of it. He said India is committed to resolve all the outstanding issues,including Kashmir, through mutual consultations with Pakistan. The spokesman said New Delhi is in touch with the permanent members of the UN Security Council on the development.
