BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 27 (XINHUA) -- Ford Motor Company of the United States signed agreements with two Chinese enterprises here today to establish two joint ventures producing auto components. These are the first Ford-launched manufacturing companies in China. According to the agreements signed today, Ford and the Yanfeng division of the Shanghai Automotive Industry Corporation will set up a company to manufacture plastic automotive components, including interior trim, seats, instrument panels and other plastic parts for the Chinese auto industry. The joint venture between Ford and Shanghai Yaohua Glass Works will produce auto safety glass and architectural glass. "For Ford Motor Company, these new partnerships represent the beginning of our manufacturing presence in China, this important new market, and the chance to show our strength and commitment," said James Paulsen, president of Ford China Operations. Ford will invest more than 50 million U.S. dollars in all in these two projects.
