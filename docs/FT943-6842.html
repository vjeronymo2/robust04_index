940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Select Industries loss widens to Pounds 2.32m Select Industries, which joined the USM in September last year, reported losses of Pounds 2.32m pre-tax for the year to June 30, against Pounds 889,000 for the previous six months. The company, which has changed its year end from December to June, makes the AirBoss wheel system. Its ultimate holding company is AirBoss of Australia. Turnover was Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 311,000). Mr Simon Raynaud, chairman, said sales had exceeded Pounds 1m for the first time and had doubled compared with the previous 12 months. He added that the loss was in line with management projections. The company intends applying for a full listing next year.
