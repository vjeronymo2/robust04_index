BFN [From the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] At approximately 0530, a group of unidentified individuals opened fire on a military checkpoint located on the highway from Tuxtla Gutierrez to Chicoasen Dam in Chiapas State. The attack lasted approximately 50 minutes. As a result, one Mexican Army communications sergeant was wounded and died a few hours later at a hospital. This is all that happened in that isolated incident. Miguel Angel Godinez Bravo, Seventh Military Region commander, explained on behalf of the National Defense Secretariat that there is no relation between this incident and the Zapatist National Liberation Army [EZLN]. We must also point out that at 0600, two Bell combat helicopters and a Pilatus Air Force airplane overflew La Chacona Mountain, strafing different areas in an attempt to locate the perpetrators of the attack. Chiapas Attorney General Arturo Becerra Martinez ruled out any connection between this morning's clash in Tuxtla Gutierrez and the EZLN. [Begin Becerra recording] So far, we do not know the motive or name of the assailants. Until now nothing indicates the EZLN is linked to the attack. Should we obtain further information, the Attorney General's Office will pass it along to you later. [end recording]
