Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The rumors circulating about Russians leaving Moldova are without foundation, President Mircea Snegur has said. He described the rumors as tendentious. At a meeting today with leaders of Tarakliyskiy Rayon, which has a large population of Bulgars, Mircea Snegur also mentioned the examination of the population's knowledge of the state language planned for 2 April. In his opinion, the timing should be considered for a last time in the new Parliament.
