September 8, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Royal 31, Santa Paula 3 -- Ryan Fein completed 13 of 15 passes, including two long touchdown tosses, to lead Royal past host Santa Paula.

Fein, who passed for 231 yards, teamed with Jeff Creed on touchdown throws of 71 and 77 yards. Creed finished with three catches for 164 yards. LANCE O'FALLON
