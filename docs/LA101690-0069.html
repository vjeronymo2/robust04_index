October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Journal Reacts to Singapore Press Restrictions: The owners of the Wall Street Journal announced that they will halt the circulation of the Asian Wall Street Journal in Singapore, following the publication of new restrictions on the foreign press there. "What the government of Singapore wants is for the foreign press to practice self-censorship," Dow Jones & Co., the majority owner of the Asian Wall Street Journal, said in an editorial.
