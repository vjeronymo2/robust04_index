920224 FT 24 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Taveners recommends Toms' offer Taveners, the manufacturer of sugar confectionery, has recommended to shareholders a 165p per share cash offer - which values it at about Pounds 4.8m - from Toms Fabrikker, a Danish maker of luxury chocolate and confectionery. Toms has received irrevocable undertakings to accept in respect of 1.56m shares (nearly 54 per cent). In the same announcement Taveners unveiled a surge in pre-tax profits to Pounds 400,000 for 1991 (Pounds 81,000) in spite of a Pounds 325,000 decline in sales to Pounds 12.4m. A special interim dividend of 6p has been declared.
