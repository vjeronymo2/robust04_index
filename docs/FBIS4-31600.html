BFN [Text] Warsaw, 11 Jun--The Democratic Left Alliance [SLD] parliamentary floor group today submitted a motion to the Sejm Presidium for "postponing the first reading of the bill on the ratification of the concordat between the Holy See and the Polish Republic until after a new constitution of the Polish Republic comes into force," group Chairman Aleksander Kwasniewski told journalists. Earlier it was said at the Sejm that the debate on the concordat would be held on 2 July. Kwasniewski said that this step was not directed against the church or the believers, and it was not aiming at stirring up a war over outlooks on life. He added that it intended to lead to the situation when the concordat "will be freed from public doubts, also expressed during the campaign before the 19 September general election."
