November 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

AND NOW, A ROUND OF APPLAUSE FOR THE NEW NC-17 MOVIE RATING

The protests against the much-needed NC-17 rating are naive.

Luckily, for some time film distributors had been able to easily overcome the "no X-rated-film-advertising/censorship" problem: Simply release a film with no rating at all.

Hence there is absolutely no difference between the NC-17 rated "Henry & June" and numerous unrated films over the years.

JIM and PATTY DEE

San Luis Obispo
