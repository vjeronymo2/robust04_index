Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Arne Lonbe, the second highest official and the consul of the Norwegian Embassy in Tehran, has been expelled due to acts incompatible with diplomatic norms. The Foreign Affairs Ministry announced: For this reason, the ambassador of Norway to Tehran was summoned to our country's Foreign Affairs Ministry before noon today. He was informed of the news, and it was decided that he should leave the Islamic Republic of Iran's territory by 4 April 1994.
