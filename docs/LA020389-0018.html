February 3, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

FAURIA PLANS TO ATTEND NORTHERN ARIZONA

Quinn Fauria of Crespi High, a Times' All-Valley defensive lineman, said Thursday that he has verbally committed to attend Northern Arizona, a Division I-AA school in the Big Sky Conference.

Fauria also had been considering Cal State Northridge, Santa Clara and the Air Force Academy. He will play fullback at NAU. Fauria can sign a national letter of intent Wednesday.
