930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: SWP An increase in market share within the timber frame industry helped SWP Group record an increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 30,000 to Pounds 170,000 in the year to June 30. Turnover for this USM-quoted supplier of components to the building industry rose 4 per cent to Pounds 8.05m (Pounds 7.74m). Earnings per share came out at 0.5p (0.1p) and the the company proposes a return to the dividend list with a final of 0.2p.
