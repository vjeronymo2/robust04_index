920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Little Brown agrees to buy Macdonald Little Brown, the Boston-based publisher owned by Time-Warner, the media concern, yesterday agreed to buy Macdonald, part of Maxwell Communication Corporation. Macdonald was placed in administration last month amid fears that creditors would seek petitions to wind up the loss-making company. Mr Tony Lomas of Price Waterhouse, the administrator, said yesterday that the offer was the best available for Macdonald creditors. The purchase price was not revealed.
