Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN ["Text" of statement issued by the Jordanian Government on "the savage aggression against innocent Muslims in Sarajevo;" date not given -- read by announcer] [Text] The Jordanian Government has followed with deep concern the reports on the painful and bloody massacre to which Sarajevo, the capital of Bosnia-Herzegovina, was exposed and in which a large number of defenseless civilian children, women, and elderly people were killed. As the Jordanian Government denounces the brutal aggression against innocent Muslims in Sarajevo and strongly condemns this fierce barbarism, which ignores all values, ideals, and human rights, it calls on the international community to move immediately to shoulder its responsibility to save the innocent Muslims in Bosnia-Herzegovina from the continuing aggression and to work toward ending the siege on the Bosnian capital. The Jordanian Government appeals to the UN Security Council, the United Nations, and human rights organization to work earnestly to stop the bloodshed and achieve security, tranquility, and peace for the people of Bosnia-Herzegovina who are being exposed, day and night, to human rights violations before the eyes of the world.
