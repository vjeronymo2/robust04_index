921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Lendu buys another farm in Australia Lendu is buying another property in southern Queensland, Australia, through the Gubbagunyah Partnership. Consideration is ADollars 3.25m cash (Pounds 1.45m) for the 9,029 hectares farm property, of which Lendu's share will be ADollars 2.63m.
