April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DEEJAYS IN TV, FILMS

Thank you, Calendar, for adding one more reason to my list of why I haven't been able to get my own television show. I already knew it's because I'm a straight, talented WASP who is not a stand-up comic, not an ex-professional athlete, not the son of a celebrity. Now I know it's because I'm also not a disc jockey.

ROBERT BRISCOE EVANS

Los Angeles
