940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Reciprocal deal for Nissan Nissan will export cars made in Mexico to the US next year, while shipping models made in the US to Mexico, agencies report from Tokyo. The US model to be shipped to Mexico will be the Altima sedan, one of two models built at Nissan's factory in Smyrna, Tennessee, while the Mexican model will be the Sentra sedan, which is built at a plant in Mexico City.
