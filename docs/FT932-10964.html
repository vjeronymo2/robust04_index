930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Asil Nadir Flees to Cyprus: Asil Nadir In Cyprus - The route to escape THE SERIOUS FRAUD OFFICE: Police attached to the SFO arrest Asil Nadir at Heathrow airport as he arrives on a flight from Turkey in December 1990. He is charged with theft and false accounting. THE COURTS: Bow St magistrates court. Concerned Mr Nadir will flee the country, the SFO opposes bail and asks for him to be kept in custody. The magistate refuses, but imposes record bail of Pounds 3.5m. THE POLICE: Saville Row police station. Mr Nadir reports weekly to police according to his bail conditions. He makes he last visit onMonday this week. THE FLIGHT: The airport. At an unknown airport, the Polly Peck chairman boards a private jet. Within hours he is in Northern Cyprus, beyond the reach of extradition. --------------------------------------------------------- WHAT HE HAS LEFT BEHIND --------------------------------------------------------- ASSETS: realisations to date Under Pounds 100,000 --------------------------------------------------------- Amounts held in companies and trusts and various investment unknown Three oil paintings 0.9m --------------------------------------------------------- LIABILITIES: --------------------------------------------------------- Writ from the administrators of Polly Peck Pounds 370m Bank debts Pounds 100m ---------------------------------------------------------
