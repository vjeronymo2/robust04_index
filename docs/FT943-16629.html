940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Carolina air crash kills 37 The crash of a USAir DC-9 jet near Charlotte's International Airport on Saturday night killed 37 passengers, the airline said last night, Reuter reports from North Carolina. Twenty people survived the disaster, including all five crew members. The cause of the crash, during a thunderstorm, is unknown. The jet's voice recorder and black box have been recovered.
