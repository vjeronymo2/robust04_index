Language: English Article Type:BFN [By KHOVAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Zhukov] [Text] Dushanbe January 4 TASS -- The second anniversary of diplomatic relations between Tajikitan and China is being marked here today. "This is a major event in the lives of the two nations," Chinese Ambassador Xi Zhaoming told the KHOVAR news service. "Relations between our two countries are being promoted on a sound and perfectly normal basis. Our stands largely coincide in foreign politics." The Chinese diplomat said that there were great potential possibilities for promoting Sino-Tajik cooperation and "this process must be accelerated."
