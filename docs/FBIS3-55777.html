Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By unidentified BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk, 12 Jan -- The signing of a statement by the Governments of the Russian Federation and Belarus on the forthcoming unification of their monetary systems should not have any effect on the results of cooperation between the republics and international financial organizations, a BELINFORM correspondent was told by Yevgeniy Bogomazov, head of the sector of the Belarusian Council of Ministers for coordinating the work of republican bodies with international economic and financial credit organizations. He said that plenipotentiary representatives of the two republics had signed a message in Moscow to the IMF, the EBRD [European Bank for Reconstruction and Development] and other international organizations, in which they gave an assurance that the unification of the two monetary systems would not have an effect on the prospects for cooperation between the two republics and these organizations and would not affect the independent status of each of the republics.
