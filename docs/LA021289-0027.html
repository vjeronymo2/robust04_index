February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE COLOR OF WAR

Re director Oliver Stone's glorifying of white males in the Vietnam War ("New Film Brings the Old War Home," Jan. 22): Many thousands of Vietnam casualties were African Americans -- heroic, dedicated and patriotic soldiers. Stone has portrayed African American military men as lazy, inferior and cowardly. The world knows better.

Please! Please! Spare us from another "White Platoon"!

JOHN JOHNSON

Venice Beach
