Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Article by Dan Maracineanu: "Major General Gheorghe Diaconescu, the Head of the Counterespionage Division of the SRI, Has Been Deposed and Is Being Investigated by a Special Commission"] [Text] On Monday, 7 March 1994, Major General Gheorghe Diaconescu, chief of the Counterespionage Division of the Romanian Intelligence Service [SRI], was removed from office. Virgil Magureanu, director of SRI, has ordered a full investigation of the activity of Major General Gheorghe Diaconescu. Sources from the SRI leadership have told us that the cause that led to the demotion of the counterespionage division head is "a complicated one." Despite the fact that one of the rumors that are circulating claimed that Major General Diaconescu had been placed under arrest, our own investigations have proved that this was a false report.
