940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: LME copper prices finish near day's lows - Market report Late liquidation in the London Metal Exchange COPPER market set off some stop-loss selling in the final minutes of after hours 'kerb' trading, ensuring that the market finished near its lows. The three months price finished the kerb session at Dollars 2,237 a tonne, down Dollars 38. NICKEL prices fluctuated in a lower range as the market continued to re-trace from recent highs. Compiled from Reuter
