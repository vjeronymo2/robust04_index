Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Frankfurt/Main (DPA) -- The key German interest rates remain unchanged. The Central Bank Council of the German Bundesbank decided today in Frankfurt, at its first meeting in the new year, that the third key interest rate -- the securities repurchase rate -- will be left at its present level of 6.0 percent for a further two weeks. The discount rate has thus remained unchanged at 5.75 percent since 21 October 1993. The Lombard rate remains 6.75 percent.
