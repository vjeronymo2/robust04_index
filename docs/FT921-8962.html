920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables FRUIT & VEGETABLES Top quality Jaffa Shamouti oranges at 12-30p a lb are outstanding this week, along with grapefruit at 30-45p each, reports the FFVIB. Cape Thompson grapes are at 90p-Pounds 1.20 a lb with Cape Festival at Pounds 1.00-1.20 a lb both are also good fruit buys. English Comice pears at 60-70p a lb and English Conference at 40-60p a lb are excellent, together with plums at 85p-Pounds 1.20 a lb. Notable vegetable buys are carrots at 55-60p each, broccoli at 55-60p a lb and Egyptian new potatoes at 20-30p a lb. Supplies of tomatoes are plentiful and are priced at 45-75p a lb. Celery at 50-60p a head and spring onions at 45-55p a bunch.
