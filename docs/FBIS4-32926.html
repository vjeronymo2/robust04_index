BFN [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 29 May (AFP) -- After counting 8 percent of the voting tables, the first, preliminary, official returns of the Colombian presidential elections held today show Liberal presidential candidate Ernesto Samper as having a slight lead over his Conservative opponent Andres Pastrana. After tallying 3,916 polling tables of the 47,000 throughout the country, Samper garnered 201,530 votes (47 percent) while Pastrana obtained 183,846 (42.8 percent).
