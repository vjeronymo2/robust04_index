BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Prosperity Party [PP] leader Necmettin Erbakan has said that the Poised Hammer mandate has been extended, despite the fact that a majority of the people are against it. Addressing the PP assembly group, Erbakan said the Poised Hammer forces are extending aid to the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terror organization and to Armenia. He noted that these forces are stationed in Turkey not to defend northern Iraqis but to watch over U.S. interests in the Middle East. [Begin Erbakan recording] Some 70 to 80 percent of our citizens want these forces to leave Turkey. The administration is fulfilling the wishes of foreign forces, despite the will of the people and despite claims that there is democracy in Turkey. [end recording] Commenting on the constitutional amendments, Erbakan said his party wants a laicism similar to that in Europe to be implemented in Turkey. Noting that constitutional amendments have been presented with the aim of covering up the deterioration in the economy, Erbakan added that the people want, above all, to be fed.
