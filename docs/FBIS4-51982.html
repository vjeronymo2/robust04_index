BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song's famous works "Answers to the Questions Raised by the U.S. WASHINGTON TIMES News Coverage Group," "Answers to the Questions Raised by the U.S. CNN News Coverage Group" and "Answers to the Questions Raised by the Japanese NHK News Coverage Group" were reported by foreign news media. The works were reported by the Yugoslav paper POLITIKA with a portrait of President Kim Il-song under the title "President Kim Il- song: We Have No Nuclear Weapons" and by the Vietnamese TV with a picture of President Kim Il-song receiving foreign delegations. The Danish papers BERLINGSKE TIDENDE and JYLLANDS-POSTEN reported that President Kim Il-song said the Korean people love peace and set great store by the edifices they have built by their devoted efforts. The works were also reported under the titles "President Kim Il-song Declares Peace Is the Basic Policy and Fundamental Idea of the Workers' Party and Government of Korea," "President Kim Il-song Declares the DPRK Has No Nuclear Weapons", etc. By the Danish paper POLITIKEN, the Hungarian papers MAGYAR HIRLAP and NEPSZABADSAG, the Malaysian paper SIN CHEW JIT POH, the Egyptian papers EGYPTIAN GAZETTE, AL AKHBAR and AL AHRAM, the Pakistani paper MUSLIM, the Tunisian paper LE RENOUVEAU, the Swiss paper RENOUVEAU COTIDIEN, the Chinese paper HEILONGJIANG DAILY, the Swedish papers DAGENS NYHETER and SYDSVENSKA DAGBLADET, the Jordanian papers AL RAI and AL DUSTOUR, the Kazakh paper VESNIK KAZAKHSTANA, Hungarian Radio and TV, Egyptian Central TV and Radio Jordan.
