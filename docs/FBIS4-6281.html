BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Pyongyang artistes gave a music and dance performance at the February 8 House of Culture today to celebrate the 15th of April. Invited to see the performance were the visiting General Secretary of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana Huudu Yahaya, the Co-chairman of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan P.V. Svoik, the General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Ireland Pat Quearney, the General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Turkey Yalsin Buyukdagli and other foreign party leaders, high-level delegations and delegates and the members of the Pyongyang-visiting group of former heads of state and government and politicians of different countries including ex-president of Costa Rica Rodrigo Carazo Odio, former Governor-General of Canada Edward Schreyer and former Prime Minister of Egypt Aziz Sedky. Kang Song-san, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and premier of the Administration Council; Yi Chong-ok and Pak Song-chol, Politburo members of the C.C., the WPK and vice-presidents; vice-President Kim Pyong-sik, chairman of the C.C., the Korean Social Democratic Party; and other senior party and government officials were present. The performance included the orchestra and chorus "Long Live Generalissimo Kim Il-song," the dance "The Snow Is Falling," the female folk quintet "The Day When He Came," the female chorus "We Will Live Always With the Leader" and other colorful numbers. The performers vividly depicted the immortal revolutionary exploits of the great leader President Kim Il-song, the great honor and happiness of the Korean people blessed with the leaders and their firm faith and will to carry the cause of socialism to accomplishment under the guidance of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il.
