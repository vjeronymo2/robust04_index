Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At a just-concluded provincial meeting on foreign economic and trade relations, Vice Governor Wang Shuangxi asked all foreign economic and trade departments in the province to speed up reform; transform their operational mechanism; establish a new system; and promote the sustained, speedy, and healthy [chi xu, kuai su, jian kang] development of the province's foreign economic and trade relations. In his speech, Wang Shuangxi confirmed the province's achievements in foreign economic and trade relations over the last few years. On foreign economic and trade structural reform, Wang Shuangxi stressed: First, we should seize the opportunity to promote the province's foreign economic and trade relations to a new height. Second, we should rationalize relations, delegate powers to the lower levels, and exercise graded management. Third, we should speed up foreign economic and trade structural reform in cities and prefectures; city and prefectural foreign trade organizations should be put under city and prefectural management and their revenues should be separated from the provincial-level foreign trade revenue. Fourth, great efforts should be made to transform the foreign trade operational mechanism and gradually to form a modern enterprise system. Wang Shuangxi also set demands for strengthening leadership and transforming functions, saying: The foreign trade tasks set by the provincial government are to ensure $1.05 billion in foreign exchange earnings through exports this year and to strive for $1.1 billion; to increase the number of three-capital enterprises to 2,000 by the end of this year; to top $400 million in foreign capital actually used; to ensure $96 million in three-capital enterprises' foreign exchange earnings throughout exports; to ensure $40 million in the amount of contracts for overseas projects and labor services; to obtain three or four international aid projects; and to ensure $20 million in the amount of contracts for technology exports. Governor Bai Qingcai and Vice Governor Xu Shanlin also attended and addressed the meeting. The director of the provincial foreign economic relations and trade department made a work report at the meeting. Governor Bai Qingcai; Vice Governor Wang Shuangxi; Ren Guoyi, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; and Liang Qi, vice chairman of the provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; attended the closing ceremony on 21 February and presented prizes to units that had won the glorious title of best enterprise in creating foreign exchange earnings in 1993.
