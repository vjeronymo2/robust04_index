January 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

PEREZ TO DEFEND TITLE TONIGHT AT FORUM

Raul Perez makes the fourth defense of his World Boxing Council bantamweight championship at the Forum tonight against ninth-ranked Gaby Canizales, a former champion.

Perez, 5 feet 11 inches, from Tijuana, is one of the tallest bantamweight champions and is a textbook boxer. He will have a major height advantage over the 5-5 1/2 Canizales, an aggressive fighter from Laredo, Tex. This is the first of a planned 12 world title fights at the Forum this year.

Perez, who earned the bantamweight title by beating Miguel Lora in Las Vegas in 1988, is 44-1. Canizales, briefly the World Boxing Assn. champion in 1986, is 45-6.
