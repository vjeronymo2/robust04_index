BFN [Text] The security services have announced that Omar Khennouche, 43, a party activist in the HAMAS [Islamic Society Movement] movement and a member of the Reform and Guidance Association, was assassinated on 7 April in Khemis El Khechna in Boumerdes Province. Omar Khennouche is the second figure affiliated to Hamas and the Reform and Guidance Association to be assassinated after Mohamed Bouslimani two months ago.
