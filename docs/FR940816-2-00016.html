CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY COMMISSION [CPSC Docket No. 94&hyph;C0013] The Toro Company, Inc., a Corporation; Provisional Acceptance of a Settlement Agreement and Order AGENCY: Consumer Product Safety Commission. ACTION: Provisional Acceptance of a Settlement Agreement under the Consumer Product Safety Act.

SUMMARY: It is the policy of the Commission to publish settlements which it provisionally accepts under the Consumer Product Safety Act in the Federal Register in accordance with the terms of 16 CFR Part 1118.20(e)&hyph;(h). Published below is a provisionally-accepted Settlement Agreement with The Toro Company, Inc., a corporation.
