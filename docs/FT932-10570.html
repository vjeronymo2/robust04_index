930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Peers warn on World Service PEERS warned the government yesterday against cuts in the budget of the BBC World Service. Lord Henley, for the government, said no decisions had been taken but the Service would have to meet its share of any Foreign Office 'budgetary restraint' after next March. Labour's Lord Ashley said severe cuts had been proposed for the service, many people's 'only lifeline to the truth'.
