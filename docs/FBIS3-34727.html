Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of Afghanistan, has conditionally accepted the proposal of Islamic Conference Organization [ICO] Secretary General Hamid al-Ghabid to establish a cease-fire. During a two-day visit to Pakistan, Algabid meet with met representatives of the warring sides in Afghanistan and proposed that a cease-fire be observed during the blessed month of Ramadan. Borhanoddin Rabbani has said that he will accept a cease-fire with Hekmatyar's Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party] on the condition that the Hezb-e Eslami pledges to observe it as well. In the meantime, reports from Afghanistan suggest that the two sides' planes subjected one another's positions to aerial bombardment today. On the basis of these reports, heavy weapons fire was exchanged between the warring sides today in several parts of Kabul.
