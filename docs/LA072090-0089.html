July 20, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HALL OF SHAME? U.S. Senate staffers consider Sen. Alan Cranston "headed for the hall of shame," according to a Washingtonian magazine poll. . . . Cranston awaits possible discipline for allegedly interfering with regulators investigating Lincoln Savings & Loan of Irvine. . . . A spokeswoman says he's still running for reelection and "doesn't take the poll seriously. The Washingtonian is the slick equivalent of a supermarket tabloid." Vince Sweeney and Janet Eastman
