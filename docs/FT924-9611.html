921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / International Company News: Reliance improves Meanwhile, Mr Saul Steinberg's Reliance Group Holdings, the investment and insurance company, reported a Dollars 1.3m profit after tax in the third quarter of 1992. It said results had been depressed by Dollars 41.7m of pre-tax losses relating to Hurricane Andrew, which cut net income by Dollars 27.5m. However, in Reliance's case, the tiny profit marked an improvement on the Dollars 126.3m loss seen in the same period of 1991.
