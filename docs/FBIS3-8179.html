Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 5 Mar--The Sejm has adopted the 1994 budget law with 289 votes for, 115 against, and 27 abstentions. In a vote on the assumptions of the monetary policy for the year 1994, 286 deputies voted for, 97 were against, and 44 deputies abstained. The Sejm has also adopted the law to increase the national income. There were 332 deputies for and 59 against; 33 abstained.
