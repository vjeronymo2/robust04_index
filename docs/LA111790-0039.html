November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT'S SO MARVELOUS ABOUT BERATING FANS?

Beware, USC fans: You have raised the ire of Mr. Marvin Marinovich, a.k.a. "Robo Daddy," when you booed Todd.

If this action continues, "Robo Daddy" will make you walk home as he once did "Robo Boy" when he had less than a perfect day.

But don't worry, the USC band could accompany you and play the two songs that they know, alternating them every other block.

GRANT R. IRVIN

Ontario
