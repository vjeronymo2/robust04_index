920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Nigerian elected governors sworn in To shouts of 'long live Nigeria', elected state governors took over from military officers yesterday at the start of a crucial period of power-sharing in Africa's most populous country, Reuter reports from Lagos. The governors were elected in all 30 states last month after bitter primaries involving two military-created parties. This is the spearhead of a full return to civilian rule by the end of 1992 - after national assembly and presidential elections.
