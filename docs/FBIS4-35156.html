BFN [Report by Annerys Gonzalez; from "The News" newscast] [Text] Regarding the suspension of constitutional guarantees, Interior Minister Ramon Escovar Salom explained that the right to stage peaceful demonstrations has not been suspended. [Begin Escovar recording] I have told people that the fact that the guarantees have been suspended does not mean that the government is going to become a dictatorship, nor does it mean that the government is going to suppress the rights of citizens. I have asked them to expect a prudent attitude, a discreet, but at the same time stern attitude on the part of the government. [end recording] The interior minister will meet in his office today with the governor of the federal district and the chiefs of the various security corps of the state to discuss the security situation in the country. The objective of the meeting is to define the security strategies to be followed during the time that certain constitutional guarantees are suspended.
