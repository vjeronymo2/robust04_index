Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoria] [Text] Tbilisi February 2 TASS -- Deputy Speaker of the Georgian Parliament Vakhtang Rcheulishvili said that Georgia "expects much from Boris Yeltsin's visit". "We hope that the Russian president's visit will set the beginning to the new stage in relations between the two countries", he said in an interview with the newspaper "SVOBODNAYA GRUZIA", published on Wednesday. "The period of confrontation, covert and overt, is over", he said. "Economic, cultural and other ties must be restored. We expect that specific steps will be taken to ensure Georgia's territorial integrity and state interests".
