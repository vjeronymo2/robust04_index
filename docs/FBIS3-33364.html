Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Carlos Alvarado from the Government Palace's Gold Hall in Lima; from the "Newspaper of the Airwaves" newscast--live] [Excerpt] The ceremony to swear in the ministers who were still pending got underway a few minutes ago at the Government Palace's Gold Hall. Although they had been confirmed in their posts, the protocol ceremony had not been held yet. Here are Economy and Finance Minister Jorge Camet, Justice Minister Fernando Vega Santagadea, Fisheries Minister Jaime Sobero Taira, and Industry Minister Absalon Vasquez Villanueva. As we said, the reading of the ministers' respective letters of resignation began a few minutes ago; immediately after, the resolutions confirming their posts will be read. [passage omitted]
