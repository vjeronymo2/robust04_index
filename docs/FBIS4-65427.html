BFN [Text] Ankara, May 16 (A.A.) -- Turkey obtained important financial gains from the F-16 production for Egypt, the Defense Ministry said on Monday [16 May]. A ministry secretary told the ANATOLIA Agency that according to the agreement signed previously between Egypt and the United States, two F-16 planes were manufactured and delivered to Egypt by Turkey on April 4. According to the agreement, the planes were inspected by Tugas aviation and space industries. The official said that there are no off-set agreements signed between Egypt and Turkey but that there is a memorandum of understanding on cooperation in defense industries between the two countries. Turkey will deliver 44 more F-16's to Egypt on June 31, in line with the agreement.
