Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Delhi police have arrested six persons and foiled a well-planned conspiracy for creating widespread disturbances in India by Pakistan-trained militants. Briefing newsmen in New Delhi today, the city police commissioner, Mr. N.B. Kaushal, disclosed that they had planned to create disturbances, attack religious places, and use poison. He said it is for the first time that Pakistan used its own nationals for such operations who are trained and experienced in guerrilla warfare and insurgency. Sixteen kg of explosives, detonators, hand grenades, and Pakistani passports were seized from the arrested persons who belong to the Islamic Inqilabi Mahaz [Revolutionary Front]. Two sets of floppies were also seized from the terrorists, who were planning to prepare a computer. Cases against them have been registered, and they were remanded to police custody.
