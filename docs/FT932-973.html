930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Amber Industrial Amber Industrial Holdings, which manufactures and distributes speciality chemicals, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.55m to Pounds 3.07m in the year ended March 31. Turnover increased to Pounds 21m (Pounds 16.4m). Earnings rose from 34p to 43.7p. The final dividend is lifted to 13p, making an increased total of 18.5p (17p).
