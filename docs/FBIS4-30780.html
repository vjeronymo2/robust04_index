BFN [Text] The Press Center of the BSP [Bulgarian Socialist Party] has informed BTA that BSP leader Zhan Videnov and Professor Krustyu Petkov, president of the KNSB [Confederation of Independent Trade Unions in Bulgaria], met today to discuss their attitudes to the government's intentions, the possibilities for early parliamentary elections, and obtaining the compensations for price increases desired by the KNSB. The two agreed to establish a degree of coordination in the actions taken by the BSP and KNSB to defend the population's most important social and material interests during the forthcoming period.
