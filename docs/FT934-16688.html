931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: BLP shows advance to Pounds 194,000 BLP Group, the USM-quoted wood laminate and veneer company, reported profits before tax increased from Pounds 11,000 to Pounds 194,000 for the six months to June 30 as difficult trading in the UK was offset by a buoyant performance by its Woodtape business in the US. Turnover amounted to Pounds 13.5m (Pounds 11.8m).
