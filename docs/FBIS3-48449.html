Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Indian President Shankar Dayal Sharma on the occasion of the day of the Republic of India. Noting that since the founding of the Republic the Indian people have made great progress in their efforts to promote national unity and achieve independent development of the country, President Kim Il-song wished the president and people of India success in the work to build a new independent and prosperous India. He stressed that the traditional relations of friendship and cooperation between Korea and India would continue to develop in strength in future in accordance with the ideals of independence, friendship and non-alignment.
