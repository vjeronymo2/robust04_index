940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / International Company News: Inishtech up 11% to IPounds 8m A strong second half helped Inishtech, the Dublin-based maker of disposable hygiene and protective packaging and self adhesive labels, lift pre-tax profits 11 per cent to IPounds 8.1m (Pounds 7.8m) in 1993. Profits in 1992 were IPounds 7.28m. Sales improved from IPounds 52.7m to IPounds 60.3m. After tax of IPounds 1.59m (IPounds 1.2m) and a IPounds 661,000 (IPounds 713,000) charge for goodwill amortisation, earnings per share amounted to 35.6p (32.8p). Adjusted earnings were 39.6p (37.1p). At the interim stage the directors indicated they would recommend a final dividend of 4.75p. However, in light of the improved trading conditions they are now recommending a final of 5.5p, making a total of 8p (6.05p).
