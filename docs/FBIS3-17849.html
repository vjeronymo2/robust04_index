Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 28) TURAN -- The Azeri President Geydar Aliyev accepted the Apostolic Nuncio of the Vatican to Azerbaijan, Jean Paul Gobel, who presented his credentials to the President. Aliyev noted that diplomatic establishment between Vatican and Azerbaijan, as well as the Vatican ambassador's appointment to Azerbaijan "witnesses the attention paid by His Majesty Pope John Paul II to our republic". Aliyev said that relations between Azerbaijan and the Vatican are a positive development. Nuncio Gobel in his turn noted that he was very glad to carry out his functions in Azerbaijan. He also expressed his belief that soon he would looks forward to working in Azerbaijan under peaceful conditions.
