930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Yates Brothers uncorks 12% rise Yates Brothers Wine Lodges, the independent drinks group, lifted profits before tax by 12 per cent over the year to March 28. The increase - from Pounds 2.49m to Pounds 2.78m - came on turnover ahead 24 per cent to Pounds 35.3m (Pounds 28.5m). Profits at the operating level advanced 22 per cent to Pounds 3.42m (Pounds 2.81m); interest charges amounted to Pounds 440,000 (Pounds 320,000). Earnings per share emerged at 9.4p (7.8p). The dividend goes up 35 per cent to 2.46p.
