BFN [Text] Kiev, 24 May (AFP) -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk will travel to Luxembourg next month to sign a partnership and cooperation agreement with the European Union, deputy foreign minister Alexander Makarenko said Tuesday [24 May]. Makarenko told AFP that Kravchuk would visit Luxembourg on 12-14 June. The Foreign Ministry official said that British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd, currently visiting Kiev, told him that Ukraine's closer ties with the EU would "create a different political climate." Hurd said that member-states of the EU would guarantee the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Ukraine, Makarenko said.
