930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Shevardnadze named PM Georgia's parliament yesterday named head of state Eduard Shevardnadze temporary prime minister after the government of Prime Minister Tengiz Sigua resigned, Reuter reports. The legislature gave Mr Shevardnadze, a former Soviet foreign minister, two weeks to put forward a new cabinet to grapple with the economic disintegration and military upheavals facing the country.
