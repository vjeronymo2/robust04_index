920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Tunstall moves 20% ahead to Pounds 2.55m Tunstall Group, which makes, installs and services security and emergency communications equipment, lifted pre-tax profits by 20 per cent in the six months to March 31. On turnover just ahead to Pounds 19.6m (Pounds 19m) the pre-tax result came out at Pounds 2.55m against Pounds 2.13m and included net interest received of Pounds 89,000 against Pounds 214,000 charged. The interim dividend is stepped up to 2.25p (2.15p), payable from earnings per share of 10.2p (8.4p).
