920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Abaca in discussions with possible buyer Abaca Group, the USM-quoted motor trader and property concern formerly known as Zurich Group, is in discussions which might lead to an offer being made for its share capital. It said that any offer would value its shares at or about the current level of net assets per share which are substantially below the current share price. Abaca's shares fell 1p to 1 1/2 p yesterday.
