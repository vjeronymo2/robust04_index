930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Gamsakhurdia returns Ousted Georgian president Zviad Gamsakhurdia returned suddenly from exile yesterday and urged President Eduard Shevardnadze to resign, Reuter reports from Tbilisi. Mr Shevardnadze was leading resistance to rebels in the Black Sea city of Sukhumi when Mr Gamsakhurdia arrived in Zugdidi, west Georgia.
