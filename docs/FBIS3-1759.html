Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The reception room of Nanhu Guesthouse was permeated with a lively and vigorous atmosphere on the afternoon of 22 February. Provincial leaders He Zhukang, Gao Yan, Liu Yunzhao, Zhang Yueqi, Liu Xilin, Fang Jianyu, Zhang Tienan, and Hu Houjun came there to meet with the members from Hong Kong to attend the second session of the seventh provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC]. He Zhukang, secretary of the provincial party committee, first extended a warm welcome to the members. He said: Jilin Province is a member of the big family of the motherland. Hong Kong compatriots are concerned about Jilin, and we are also concerned about Hong Kong. He urged the members to actively participate in and discuss the administration of state and government affairs, help in Jilin's opening to the outside world, and offer more ideas, suggestions, and views. The provincial leaders and the members exchanged name cards and greeted each other. They held cordial discussions in a cheerful atmosphere. Governor Gao Yan and Vice Governor Liu Xilin introduced the reform in the finance, tax, and banking systems which the members were concerned about. Liu Yunzhao, chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee, said that the suggestions and views offered by the members during the discussions at the session were delivered to provincial leaders in a written form. The members were pleased to hear this. They also offered the opinions and suggestions on the matters they respectively are concerned about. In conclusion, the leaders and the members had a valuable group picture taken.
