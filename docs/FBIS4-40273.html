BFN [Text] Son Song-pil, DPRK ambassador in Moscow, has said that the Korean [choson] Government withdrew from the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] because this agency's Board of Governors had recently adopted a resolution on the North Korean issue. During a news conference, Ambassador Son Song-pil stressed that the IAEA Board of Governors' adoption of the resolution meant, in reality, the start of sanctions against the DPRK. Ambassador Son Song-pil also declared that imposing sanctions against Pyongyang and convening an international conference on the denuclearization of the Korean peninsula were in no way compatible.
