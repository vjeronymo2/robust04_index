Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 23 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met and exchanged views here this evening with David Graaff, vice-minister of trade and industry of South Africa, and his party. The South African guests discussed bilateral economic and trade relations with Wu Yi, Chinese minister of foreign trade and economic cooperation this afternoon. Graaff and his party arrived here on March 20 at the invitation of China's Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation.
