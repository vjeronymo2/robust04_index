940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / North Sea gas project approved The government yesterday approved one of the biggest offshore gas developments since 1990. The Department of Trade and Industry approved British Gas's Pounds 600m plan to develop the Armada complex, which includes the Drake, Fleming and Hawkins fields in the central North Sea. The fields, which will come on stream in late 1997, will produce 350m cu ft of gas a day, about 4 per cent of the UK's requirements.
