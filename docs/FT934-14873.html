931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Receivers fixed for Ossory Administrative receivers have been appointed to two wholly owned subsidiaries of Ossory Estates - Hulton Estate and Ossory Road (Skelmersdale). The two companies own interests in properties in Kensington and Skelmersdale respectively. The receivers were appointed by Bayerische Vereinsbank and Sumitomo Trust and Banking. The appointments will not affect the group's position, or proposals for reconstruction of group debt.
