December 21, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

OPRAH GIVES $50,000 TO AID POOR

Oprah Winfrey is giving $50,000 to Glide Memorial United Methodist Church, which appealed for help in its holiday grocery giveaway to the poor, the church said.

"Because she is a very caring person who reaches out to the poor, she immediately responded," the Rev. Cecil Williams, the church's pastor, said Thursday.

Glide Memorial has distributed thousands of bags of groceries to the poor since 1979. But Williams reported earlier this week that holiday contributions were down 37% from 1989 and asked the public for donations.

Winfrey learned of the appeal through poet Maya Angelou, a member of Glide Memorial, and called Williams to say she will be sending a check, the church said.

The church planned to start its holiday giveaway of more than 10,000 bags of groceries today. The church said that while donations have declined, the need for the groceries has risen.
