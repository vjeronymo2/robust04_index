June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RESULTS OF PRIMARY ELECTION

When I was a child and went to the polls with my mother she always had to stand in line to wait her turn to vote.

Now that I'm an adult things have certainly changed. I sail right into the voting booth, I never have to wait.

Has some efficiency expert improved the voting process or is it that no one shows up? I believe it's the latter.

JENNIFER BEMOWSKI

Redondo Beach
