Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- The launch of the first "Gals" tv relay satellite of a new generation scheduled for 12.53 hours Moscow time on Wednesday was postponed for one day because of technical reasons, TASS was told at the press centre of Russian space forces which gave no further details. The satellite was to be launched from the Baykonur cosmodrome in Kazakhstan.
