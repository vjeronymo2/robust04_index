September 28, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION ROUNDUP

Marshall Fundamental 34, Grant 12 -- Wide receiver Rashard Mosely scored three touchdowns and caught six passes for 102 yards to lead the Eagles in an intersectional game at Grant.

The Eagles scored two touchdowns each in the second and fourth quarters, and amassed 257 yards in offense. Grant had allowed just 198 yards in two previous games against City Section opponents. MIKE GLAZE
