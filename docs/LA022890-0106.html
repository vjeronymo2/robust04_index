February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ON THE STREET: Should the homeless participate in the census? Homeless advocate Mitch Snyder doesn't think so and is urging a boycott (A3). . . . Orange County has an estimated 5,000 to 10,000 homeless, and the Orange County Homeless Issues Task Force, which surveyed about 2,000 of them, said most cited job loss and eviction as the major causes of their situation. Kristina Lindgren and Mark I. Pinsky
