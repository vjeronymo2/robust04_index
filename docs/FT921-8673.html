920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / Blue Arrow Verdicts: SFO claim 'a very good result' AN OFFICER in the SFO said yesterday that the Blue Arrow result was being viewed as a success. The officer said: 'It's a very good result and I think it justifies what we did in a case in which we attracted a lot of criticism. It's quite an important result, not just for us at this critical time, but as far as the perception of what is tolerable and not tolerable in the City.'
