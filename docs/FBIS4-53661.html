BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- Supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA] Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the officers and men of the Pak Yong-ho unit of the KPA. The soldiers made a great contribution to building, preserving and managing the revolutionary site which is associated with the glorious and brilliant revolutionary history and the immortal revolutionary feats of the party and the leader. Comrade Kim Chong-il also extended thanks to the men of merits in Hoeryong city of North Hamgyong Province who had helped toward the management of the revolutionary site.
