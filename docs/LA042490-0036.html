April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

OptimumCare Corp.: The Laguna Niguel-based psychiatric and chemical dependency treatment program company lost $391,157 for 1989, contrasted with a loss of $760,940 for 1988. Revenue more than tripled to $1.3 million from $384,400. The company attributed the revenue gain to an increased number of patients in its programs.
