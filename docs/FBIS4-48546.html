BFN [Text] A plane belonging to the Tanzania Peoples' Defense Forces, TPDF, crashed shortly before landing at the Bukoba airport. The Tanzania news agency, SHIHATA, said several people are feared dead in the accident which occurred about 1300 hours today. The Buffalo plane, registration number JWY 902, was on a scheduled Dar es Salaam-Mwanza-Tabora flight. SHIHATA quoted eyewitnesses as saying that one of the wings of the aircraft hit a tree as it was approaching the airport before it crashed into a house bursting into flames. The house used as a clinic also caught fire. Survivors say that the plane was also carrying two coffins scheduled for burial at Bukoba and Tabora respectively.
