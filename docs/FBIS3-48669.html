Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Unha Trading Combined General Bureau, upholding the great leader's New Year's speech, is implementing the trade-first policy and vigorously accelerating production. It has set a high target to increase its exports fourfold this year. The Combined General Bureau is increasing the processing volume of clothes for export while improving the product quality and is establishing active measures to continuously increase export volume. Workers and technicians of many clothes plants of the Combined General Bureau, including Tongdaewon Cloth Plant, Songyo Cloth Plant, and Hadang Cloth Plant, have advanced in the implementation of the trade-first policy, and have achieved innovations in production day after day and have overfulfilled their daily targets every day.
