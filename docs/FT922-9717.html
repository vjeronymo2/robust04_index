920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Boutros Ghali warns on troop deployment delays in Cambodia Delays in the UN's troop deployment in Cambodia could have a negative effect on its peace-keeping operation unless they were soon remedied, Mr Boutros Boutros Ghali, secretary-general, warned last night, writes Michael Littlejohns. In a report to the Security Council, he mentioned the slow pace of recruiting and deploying civilian administrators, which could impair the UN's ability to maintan supervision and control.
