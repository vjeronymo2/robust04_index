920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Sharp fall at Stag Furniture PROFITS FELL by 66 per cent at Stag Furniture Holdings in the six months to June 30. The cabinet furniture maker reported a pre-tax figure of Pounds 317,000, against Pounds 932,000. Sales were Pounds 2m lower at Pounds 12.5m. The company said margins were under pressure. Costs were carried from promotional activities and introduction of new ranges. Earnings were reduced to 0.9p (6.2p) per share and the directors have decided to lop 1p off the interim dividend at 1.5p.
