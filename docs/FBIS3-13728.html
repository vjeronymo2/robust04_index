Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad today phoned President Ilyas al-Hirawi and briefed him on the telephone call he had received from U.S. President Bill Clinton on the peace process and the need to continue negotiations. Presidents al-Asad and al-Hirawi exchanged views on developments in the region and the UN Security Council's discussions. They agreed to meet shortly to formulate a joint position.
