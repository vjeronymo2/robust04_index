921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / International Company News: Rights issue by St George Bank 93% subscribed ST GEORGE Bank, formerly Australia's biggest building society, yesterday said its ADollars 102m one-for-five renounceable rights issue had closed 93.3 per cent subscribed, Reuter reports. The issue of 22.2m new ordinary shares offered at ADollars 4.60 (Dollars 3.28) each, fully underwritten by BT Securities, increases shareholders' funds to more than ADollars 564m.
