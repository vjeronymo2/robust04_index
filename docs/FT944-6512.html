941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Drayton Blue Chip Drayton Blue Chip Trust suffered a fall in net assets per share to 63.4p at September 30, against 80.7p six months earlier. Net revenue for the six months however, improved from Pounds 503,000 to Pounds 551,000 and earnings per share were 4.359p (3.981p). The interim dividend is maintained at 2.905p.
