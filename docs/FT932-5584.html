930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / PowerGen denies safety breach POWERGEN, the electricity generator, yesterday denied at Leicester Crown Court breaching health and safety law with working practices which led to a workman's death. The prosecution alleges that PowerGen and contractor Credfeld Camtorc of Portsmouth failed to ensure the safety of people not directly in their employment. Both companies pleaded not guilty. The case follows an investigation into a fire in the oil-fuel pump room at Ratcliffe on Soar power station, Nottinghamshire, last year.
