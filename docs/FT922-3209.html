920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / International Capital Markets: HK Macau in hotel deal HONGKONG Macau (Holdings), a mainland-controlled property investment group, has announced plans to acquire an 81 per cent stake in the Hyatt Xian Hotel for USDollars 42.8m, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. The purchase from Landtrade Engineering & Development, a closely-held Hong Kong group, will be partly financed through a share issue. Hongkong Macau said it would issue 16m shares, representing 10.4 per cent of its existing capital, at HKDollars 14.30 each, an 8.3 per cent premium to their previous close.
