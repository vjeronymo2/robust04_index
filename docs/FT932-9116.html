930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / Kenyan economic liberalisation Kenya reintroduced key economic liberalisation measures yesterday in a bid to win back western aid suspended 19 months ago, Reuter reports from Nairobi. Government statements said it was reintroducing 'retention accounts', which allow exporters to keep hard currency instead of remitting it to the central bank were being reintroduced with immediate effect.
