Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Georgian police have detained two close aides of ex-President Zviad Gamsakhurdia in Zugdidi, a region in the west of that republic. The detainees are Valter Shurgai, the organizer of a rebellion by zviadists in Tbilisi in June of 1992, and Zaur Kobalia, the brother of Loti Kobalia, the commander of troops loyal to Gamsakhurdia. According to a Georgian Interior Ministry spokesman, the arrested men surrendered arms and forged documents. Gamsakhurdia committed suicide in a west Georgian village on December 31 of last year.
