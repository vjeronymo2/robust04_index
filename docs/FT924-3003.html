921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Argentine debt aid Japan's Export-Import Bank is to lend Argentina Dollars 800m to help it restructure its debt, Argentine Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo said yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Argentina has to buy about Dollars 3.2bn of 30-year zero-coupon US Treasury bonds to guarantee an agreement it signed last Sunday with its bankers on a plan to restructure commercial debt. Mr Cavallo said the IMF would provide about Dollars 1bn towards the purchase, the World Bank Dollars 750m and the InterAmerican Development Bank Dollars 700m.
