BFN [Excerpt] Cochabamba, 1 June (ANF) -- Colonel Hugo Guarachi, Special Antinarcotics Force (FELCN) department commander, has rejected the possible extradition to the United States of Jose Luis Seleme, the owner of several night clubs and an alleged drug trafficker. Recent reports in Cochabamba have stated that Seleme's working capital totals at least $7 million. FELCN personnel arrested Seleme at dawn on 27 May for his ties with international drug trafficking rings. [passage omitted]
