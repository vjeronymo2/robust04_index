940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury Pounds 29m debenture issue Shaftesbury, the property concern, yesterday launched an issue of Pounds 29m First Debenture Stock 2024, the net proceeds of which will be used to repay bank loans and assist the company in taking advantage of future opportunities. The stock is secured by fixed charges on properties in London's Chinatown and Eastern Avenue Trading Estate, in Gloucester.
