931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Carlisle halves deficit to Pounds 344,000 Carlisle Group, the property services company, reported pre-tax losses less then half the previous year's figure at Pounds 344,000 for the six months to June 30. The outcome was struck on turnover up by Pounds 705,000 to Pounds 2.69m and compared with a deficit of Pounds 707,000 last time. Mr Jonathan Harris, chairman, said that in spite of the useful fillip given to certain sectors of the property market by the fall in interest rates, the business of property services, the core activity, remained difficult.
