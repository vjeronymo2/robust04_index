April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AND NOW, THE GOOD WORD ABOUT MADONNA

Really! All this fuss over Madonna. I find her no more objectionable than any of the other airheaded, pretentious pop stars of today (Michael Jackson, George Michael, etc.).

I don't know what the woman is like personally, but I do know that when I'm in a disco and a Madonna tune comes on, I want to dance. After all, isn't that the point?

FREDERIC COOPER

Torrance
