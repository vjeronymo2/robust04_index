February 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

TIME TO APPRECIATE LEGENDS WHO ARE LEFT

How dare there be disappointment that Bill Shoemaker didn't win his final race.

How dare there be catcalls for winning jockey Eddie Delahoussaye.

Shoemaker is enough of a man to have been outraged and embarrassed if the other riders had let him win, and Delahoussaye was only one of three who beat the Shoe. It doesn't lessen Shoemaker's accomplishments at all.

PATTI GARRITY, Manhattan Beach
