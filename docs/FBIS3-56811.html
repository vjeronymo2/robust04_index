Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] President of Uzbekistan Islam Karimov has carried out a reshuffle in the republic's government. Erkin Bakibayev has been released from his post as minister of finance. Bakhtiyar Khamidov has been appointed deputy prime minister and minister of finance. It was he who directed all financial issues and conducted negotiations with the government of Russia on the entry of the republic into the ruble zone. By the same decree, Khamidov is released from his post of prime minister -- chairman of the state committee for statistical forecasting of Uzbekistan.
