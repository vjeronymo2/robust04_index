921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Bulgaria pays Dollars 10m interest Bulgaria made its first Dollars 10m current interest payment on its Dollars 10bn commercial bank debt since its former Communist rulers stopped paying interest and principal in 1990, officials said yesterday, Reuter reports from Sofia. 'Our first payment of some Dollars 10m covers about one quarter of the current interest for September,' Mr Svetoslav Gavriiski, chief of Bulgaria's foreign debt commission, said. The next current interest payment for the coming three months would be made by the end of this year, Mr Gavriiski said.
