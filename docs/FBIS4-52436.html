BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 10 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, Monday sent a wreath to the bier of Yi Tong-chun, member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and deputy to the Supreme People's Assembly of the DPRK, expressing deep condolences over his death.
