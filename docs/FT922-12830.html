920415 FT 15 APR 92 / Ukraine to resume nuclear transfers UKRAINE is to resume transfers of tactical nuclear weapons to Russia for destruction, Mr Anatoly Zlenko, the country's foreign minister, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Kiev. Mr Zlenko said the transfers would resume in 'the coming days,' but gave no date. The suspension had widened divisions with Russia over military policy and brought a sharp response from the West. The cessation had threatened the Start treaty between the Soviet Union and the US to slash nuclear arsenals and heightened concern inabout the former Soviet nuclear arsenal slipping out of control.
