Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- Korean fishermen are reporting remarkable achievements in fishing. According to data available at the State Fisheries Commission, the January plan for aquatic products was fulfilled at 115 percent, of which fishes at 178 percent, on the 23rd of January. In this period, the South Hamgyong Provincial General Bureau of Fisheries topped its fishing plan for the first half of the year and the General Bureau of Pelagic Fishing its first quarterly target. The fisheries stations caught 40 to 50 tons of fish, 70 tons at maximum, per net by nimbly occupying the central fishing ground and applying advanced fishing methods.
