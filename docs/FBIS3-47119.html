Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] General Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Chinese Central Military Commission, paid a courtesy call on Supreme Commander Air Chief Marshal [ACM] Woranat Aphichari at the Supreme Command Headquarters yesterday. The two sides also held cordial talks. After the meeting, ACM Woranat briefed correspondents on the results of his talks with Liu Huaqing. He said that Liu Huaqing had told him that China is willing to sell weapons to Thailand. On this issue, ACM Woranat told the Chinese side that the Thai military units have to stick to their plans when it comes to procuring any weapons and cannot buy weapons at will. Warships ordered by the Navy are now under construction in China. ACM Woranat also said that Thailand has no policy of setting up refugee holding centers along the border. Any rumors regarding such a matter are untrue.
