930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: McLeod Russel McLeod Russel has announced that its paints division, which comprises Granyte Surface Coatings and Joseph Mason Paints, has acquired the technical knowhow, order book and goodwill of the plastic wood and drum coatings businesses of ICI Paints. Consideration was Pounds 2.3m cash of which Pounds 1m will be payable in instalments over the next two years. In 1992 the businesses had a combined turnover of Pounds 3.8m.
