BFN [Text] Sources from the Cuban Attorney General's Office have announced that conditions have been created to implement Decree-Law 149 nationwide by means of which legal action will be taken in cases of illegal accumulation of wealth. Representatives from the Office of the Attorney General and the public administration in every province will move against hoarders by seizing their ill-gotten goods while confiscation, which will be determined by the Finance and Prices Ministry, is in process. This launches a unique and vigorous endeavor that gives continuity to the collective efforts of the revolutionary process to preserve social discipline. Official sources have announced that the population will be informed in a timely manner of the fulfillment of this measure.
