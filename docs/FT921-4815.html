920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / Turkish trade gap narrows Turkey's trade deficit narrowed by 20 per cent to Dollars 7.4bn (Pounds 4.2bn) last year, thanks mainly to oil aid from Saudi Arabia, the State Institute of Statistics (SIS) said yesterday, Reuter reports from Ankara. Exports rose by 5 per cent to Dollars 13.6bn and imports fell by 5.7 per cent to Dollars 21bn.
