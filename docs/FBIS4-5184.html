BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 8 Apr (AKP) -- Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut, minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation, returned to Phnom Penh on Wednesday, 6 April, after a four-day official visit to Malaysia. The aim of the visit is to improve cooperation between Cambodia and Malaysia, said the foreign minister, adding: "Within the framework of economic cooperation and development, Malaysia has the possibility of restoring Cambodia. It is necessary for Cambodia to establish its embassy in Kuala Lumpur as this is very important for ties between the two countries." Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut also announced the forthcoming visit to Cambodia of the Malaysian prime minister, who would be guest at a reception to be given by the king of Cambodia at the Royal Palace.
