Rural Electrification Administration Municipal Interest Rates for the Second Quarter of 1994: Correction AGENCY: Rural Electrification Administration, USDA. ACTION: Correction.

SUMMARY: There is a typographical error in the notice document published on April 1, 1994, at 59 FR 15371, setting out the interest rates on advances from municipal rate loans for interest rate terms that begin during the second quarter of 1994. The correct interest rate for terms that end in 1997 is 4.000 percent.
