Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a report by a staff reporter of the Guangdong television station, from 28 January to 8 February, State Council Prime Minister Li Peng made an inspection tour of Guangdong. Those who accompanied Premier Li Peng on his inspection tour included Ding Guangen, member of the CPC Committee Political Bureau; Xie Fei, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Guangdong provincial party committee; Guangdong Governor Zhu Senlin; and responsible comrades of the relevant departments under the State Council. In Guangdong, Li Peng successively inspected projects for basic facilities, as well as some hi-tech and new projects in Foshan, Zhongshan, Zhuhai, Shenzhen, Huizhou, and other places. He also had discussions with local party and government leading comrades. Premier Li Peng and other leading comrades also visited holiday markets, and conveyed their spring festival greetings to Guangdong people. Comrade Li Peng said: [Begin recording] Guangdong is a forward position of reform and opening up. It has done very well in various aspects. I wish Guangdong people a still greater success in the new year under the guidance of the spirit of the 14th CPC National Congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee. I wish them good health, and hope that everything will turn out as they wish. [end recording]
