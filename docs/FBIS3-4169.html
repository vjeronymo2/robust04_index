Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, March 27 (XINHUA) -- Visiting President Kim Yong-sam of the Republic of Korea (ROK) said here today that economic and trade cooperation between ROK and China will be beneficial to the development of both countries. He pointed out that his current visit to the Pudong New Area in the eastern part of Shanghai will help enhance understanding and promote participation by ROK businessmen in the area's development. The ROK president made the remarks after hearing an introduction on the development of the Pudong New Area by Vice-Mayor Zhao Qizheng of Shanghai. This morning President Kim Yong-sam and his party toured the Jinqiao export processing zone, which now houses 2,000 projects spreading on 6 square kilometers. The Jinqiao zone has now attracted 13 million U.S. dollars of foreign investment. The ROK president wrote an inscription for the zone: "The development of Pudong promotes prosperity of China as a whole". At noon Kim had a luncheon with representatives from Shanghai-based ROK businesses. It is reported that there are now about 50 ROK businesses in Shanghai.
