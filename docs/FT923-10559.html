920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / Scottish milk merger probed THE government yesterday referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission the proposed acquisition by the Scottish Milk Marketing Board (SMMB) of the Scottish milk business of the Co-operative Wholesale Society. This is the first time the MMC has been asked to report on a milk industry merger. Its investigation follows the government's announcement in April that it plans legislation to transform the Milk Marketing Boards from statutory monopolies into voluntary co-operatives.
