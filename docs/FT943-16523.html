940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Dixons - Other Companies Exceptional charges of Pounds 214m connected with the sale of its Silo chain in the US will obscure a small fall in underlying profits at the UK's largest electrical retailer on Wednesday. Before the charges - which include trading and disposal losses - pre-tax profits are forecast at about Pounds 75m. Last year's figure, after provisions for losses at Silo and other charges, was Pounds 33.5m, but before exceptionals was Pounds 76.7m.
