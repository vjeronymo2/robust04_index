920219 FT 19 FEB 92 / Polystyrene recycling plant LINPAC Plastics International, the food packaging group, has opened the first polystyrene recycling plant in Europe with a capacity to process 2,500 tonnes of waste. The 21,000 sq ft plant based near Macclesfield, Cheshire, will collect polystyrene packaging waste such as vending cups from fast-food restaurants and local authorities and use it for multi-layer polystyrene-foam egg cartons.
