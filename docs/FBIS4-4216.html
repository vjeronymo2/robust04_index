BFN [By reporter Lin Meifang (2651 5019 5364)] [Text] Buenos Aires, 22 Apr (XINHUA) -- Hu Jintao, Standing Committee member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the CPC Central Committee Secretariat, who was paying a visit to Argentina, called on the Chinese Embassy staff and personnel from Chinese organs in Argentina and had a group picture taken with them on the evening of 22 April. Hu Jintao extended warm regards from the CPC Central Committee and the State Council to all personnel stationed in Argentina, a country far away from the motherland. He also briefed them on the detailed political and economic conditions at home and encouraged them to work hard.
