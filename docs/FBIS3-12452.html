Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli President Ezer Weizman telephoned President Husni Mubarak this afternoon to express his country's denunciation of the horrible crime that took place at the Hebron Mosque. He also extended condolences to President Mubarak regarding the Palestinian victims. He informed President Mubarak that he had visited Hebron this morning to meet with its mayor, the city mufti, and senior officials to extend condolences for the victims of the abominable crime.
