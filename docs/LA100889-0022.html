October 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MICHAEL CHANG

Inasmuch as we never fail to blast your socks off when you report erroneously regarding spiritual matters, it is only fair to compliment you when you publish a balanced piece that does not misstate the facts.

With rare exception, secular media misses the correct interpretation of things spiritual. We found that your lead article on Mike Chang did not God-and-Christian bash. And for that we want to thank you.

MICHAEL LOFTIN BETHANY, ESTELLE BETHANY BOLTON, LOIS BETHANY JOLLEY, Los Angeles
