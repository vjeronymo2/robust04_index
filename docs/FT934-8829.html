931113 FT 13 NOV 93 / National Savings bring in Pounds 435m NATIONAL SAVINGS brought in a net Pounds 435m in October, up from Pounds 275m in September and Pounds 227m in August. Gross product sales were Pounds 879m and accrued interest was Pounds 182m. Repayments were Pounds 626m. As in September, the highest contribution came from income bonds. National Savings introduced tiered rates for larger investments in August and gross sales of income bonds have now reached their highest level for 3 1/2 years. The total amount invested in National Savings at the end of October was Pounds 45.9bn.
