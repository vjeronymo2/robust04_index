Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek March 3 TASS -- Russian and Kyrgyz Defence Ministry officials are discussing problems relating to their nationals serving with each other's armed forces. Technical and consumer goods supplies, and accommodation to Russian troops stationed in Kyrgyzstan and military transport across the territories of the two countries are also on the agenda of the negotiations in which the Russian delegation led by Colonel-General Stanislav Petrov, Kyrgyz Defence Minister Lieutenant-General Myrkazan Subanov and Russian Ambassador to Kyrgyzstan Mikhail Romanov are participating. The programme of the Russian delegation visit which is due to run through March 5 includes meetings with the republic's prime minister and high-ranking presidential staff officials.
