Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 13 Feb (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has received a cable of congratulation from King Husayn Bin-Talal of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan on the arrival of the blessed month of Ramadan. In his cable King Husayn expressed, in his name and on behalf of the fraternal Jordanian people and government, his true feelings of brotherhood, asking almighty God for many happy returns. His majesty also wished good health and happiness for the president, continued progress and prosperity for the Iraqi people, and elevation and strength for all Muslims.
