BFN [Text] A delegation from the Iranian Majles arrived in Damascus this evening. The delegation is led by Dr. Hasan Ruhani, deputy speaker of the Majles and chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee. The delegation will hold talks with the People's Assembly officials. The delegation was received at Damascus International Airport by 'Adil Jamus, the deputy speaker of the People's Assembly; Shakir Is'ayyid, the head of the Arab and Foreign Affairs Committee; and the Iranian ambassador in Damascus. Upon arrival, Dr. Ruhani said he is carrying two messages for President Hafiz al-Asad from Islamic Revolution Leader Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i, and Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. Dr. Ruhani expressed the Iranian people's appreciation of the wise political leadership in Syria, and hailed bilateral relations in various fields.
