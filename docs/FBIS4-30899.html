BFN [Text] Prague May 30 (CTK) -- Indignation at the arrival of former Chilean President and current Ground Forces Chief Commander Augusto Pinochet's visit was expressed by representatives of several civic initiatives today. They established the Czech Republic's committee of the European Campaign Against Racism, Nationalism, Xenophobia and Intolerance, the committee's press release said. Pinochet is responsible for "thousands of ruined lives," said the press release, signed by representatives of the Movement for Civic Freedoms and Tolerance, European Human Rights Foundation and other human rights organisations.
