930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Improvement at Martin Currie Net asset value per share of the Martin Currie European Investment Trust stood at 92.5p at the April 30 year end, against 82.6p six months earlier and 85.5p a year ago. Net revenue for the 12 months grew from Pounds 121,000 to Pounds 152,000. Earnings per share improved to 0.66p (0.53p) and a final dividend of 0.29p (0.2p) is recommended.
