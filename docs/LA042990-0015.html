April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RECORD RACK: IN BRIEF:;

*** SALT-N-PEPA. "BLACK'S MAGIC." NEXT PLATEAU.: ALBUMS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE STARS (A CLASSIC.)

Few other women in rap have a firmer grip on the in-your-face concept of machisma than this spicy three-female unit.

They seldom deal in heavyweight social matters but when it comes to voicing a swaggering sense of selfhood, Salt-N-Pepa delivers. "Negro Wit' An Ego" has strong comedic energy. "Expression," written/produced by Cheryl (Salt) James with a power-to-the-individual emphasis, is one of the wittiest rap tracks to emerge this year. CONNIE JOHNSON
