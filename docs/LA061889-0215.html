June 18, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

FRANKE FINISHES THIRD IN PREP DISCUS EVENT

Canyon High School's Allison Franke, The Times' female track athlete of the year in Orange County, finished third in the discus at the Keebler International Prep Invitational track and field meet Saturday in Elmhurst, Ill. Franke had a throw of 151-feet-3. Dory Reeves of Spokane was first with a throw of 157-8.
