Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow February 21 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin is satisfied with the results of the Russian initiative in Bosnia which followed the NATO ultimatum about possible air strikes on Bosnian Serbs, TASS was told by head of the presidential staff Sergey Filatov on Monday [21 February]. Filatov qualified the Russian initiative as "a major victory of the Russian diplomacy". He added that the latest developments in Bosnia clearly show that "the adoption of major decisions without Russia on the security of the world community does not work".
