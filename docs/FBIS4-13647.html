BFN [Text] In Damascus, the Hamas Movement has proposed a truce with Israel to find a way out of the Palestinian crisis. In a statement issued in Damascus, Hamas Political Bureau chief Musa Abu-Marzuq said his proposal is based on the following principles: Comprehensive Israeli withdrawal from the West Bank, the Gaza Strip, and occupied Jerusalem, and the dismantling of all settlements in the occupied territories and the evacuation of the settlers. Abu-Marzuq emphasized the need to hold free and fair general elections among the Palestinian people in order to elect their leadership and representatives. He promised the Hamas Movement will respect the views of the majority.
