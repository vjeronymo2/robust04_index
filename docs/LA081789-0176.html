August 17, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: COUNTYWIDE;

DAN QUAYLE TO APPEAR AT 2 GOP FUND-RAISERS

Vice President Dan Quayle will help raise money for the Republican Party of Orange County and the state Republican Party at a reception and dinner next Thursday.

Quayle will first attend a $100-per-person reception at 5 p.m. at the home of Buck and Colleen Johns in Santa Ana Heights. Guests are asked to wear red, white and blue attire.

More information about the reception may be obtained by phoning (714) 750-3335.

After the reception, a private $500-per-plate dinner at another location in Orange County is planned.

Proceeds will be divided between the state and county GOP, officials said.
