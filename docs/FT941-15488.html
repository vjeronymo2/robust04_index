940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Castle Cairn lifts net asset value 44% Net asset value of Castle Cairn Investment Trust jumped 44 per cent, from 49.78p to 71.67p per share, over the 1993 calendar year. Net revenue improved marginally to Pounds 106,000 (Pounds 102,000) for earnings of 0.88p (0.85p) per share. The single distribution goes up from 0.8p to 0.825p. Directors said they expected to publish in February proposals for the enlargement of the company and a change of investment policy.
