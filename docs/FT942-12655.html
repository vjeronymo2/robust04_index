940428 FT 28 APR 94 / UK Company News: Mithras Investment net assets climb Mithras Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 54.2p at February 20, the day before it came to the market, compared with 36.2p at December 31 1992. Net revenue for the 60 weeks to February 20 was Pounds 304,000, compared with Pounds 324,000 for 1992. Earnings per share were 1p (1.1p).
