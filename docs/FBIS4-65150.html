BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- According to President Boris Yeltsin, Russia wants to become a "member with equal rights" of all European institutions. Immediately before the start of his talks with Chancellor Helmut Kohl, Yeltsin told journalists that he hoped to get the chancellor's support for this. Yeltsin announced that a new era of reconciliation will begin between the two nations after the Russian troops depart. Kohl welcomed Yeltsin as a "friend of our country and our people." With reference to the dramatic changes in the world, he said it is now important for Russia and Germany to make the correct decisions with prudence and far-sightedness. He also expressed the hope that the reforms in Russia will be successful under Yeltsin's leadership. Kohl emphasized that there have been terrible but also great chapters in the history of the two states. They must learn from both the bad and the good things.
