Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, Dec 30 (TANJUG) -- Inflation in Yugoslavia in December alone will stand at one million percent, the Federal Bureau of Statistics said on Thursday [30 December]. Retail prices will be 1,790 times higher than in November, and living expenses will increase 2,839 times. The draconic sanctions which the U.N. Security Council imposed on Yugoslavia in late May of 1992 have resulted in severe shortages of goods, a monetary chaos and general impoverishment of Yugoslavs the majority of whom now live on a subsistence level.
