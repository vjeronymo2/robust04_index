BFN [Text] Ankara, April 14 (A.A.) -- The Turkish Armed Forces struck terrorist bases in northern Iraq's Mezi region and have killed at least 80 PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terrorists, it was reported on Thursday [14 April]. The Anatolian Agency learned that it was determined that a group of 500 to 600 terrorists were preparing to cross the border from northern Iraq on April 12. Acting upon this information, the Turkish Armed Forces started operations in the region with Air Force cover. Chief of Staff Gen. Dogan Gures, accompanied by Air Force Commander Gen. Halis Burhan and Gendarmerie Force Commander Gen. Aydin Ilter went to Diyarbakir earlier in the day. Gures will be briefed on the operations by security forces in Mezi region as well as in the rural regions of Tendurek, Gabar [place-name as received], Diyarbakir and Bingol. Operations are still continuing, officials said.
