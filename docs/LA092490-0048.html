September 24, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ON PARADE: Campaigners are marching through the county this week as the Nov. 6 election nears. . . . Today: Kathleen Brown, Democrat candidate for treasurer, and state Sen. Marian Bergeson (R-Newport Beach), candidate for lieutenant governor, jointly attend a forum in Orange. . . . Tuesday: Vice President Dan Quayle arrives to raise money. . . . Friday: White House drug czar William Bennett raises more money. . . . And Saturday: Iran-Contra's Oliver L. North attends a big-bucks, black-tie fund-raiser for Assemblyman Gil Ferguson (R-Newport Beach). . . . Tentative for October: President Bush for more fund raising. Kristina Lindgren and Dave Lesher
