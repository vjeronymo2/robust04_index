941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Toad Pounds 3m placing Toad Innovations, the car security company, has raised Pounds 3m following a private placing of 3.55m shares that will be traded under the Stock Exchange's Rule 4.2 facility. The issue, which closed on December 12, significantly exceeded expectations. The issue is sponsored by Henry Cooke, an active promoter of issues under Rule 4.2, formerly known as Rule 535.2. Dealings are expected to begin on December 22.
