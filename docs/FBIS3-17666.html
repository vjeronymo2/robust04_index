Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] There are no problems between Belarus and Germany that would divide our countries, Bundestag State Secretary Berndt Wiltz told a BELINFORM correspondent. He visited our Republic as head of a German Defense Ministry delegation. The main goal of this visit was to improve military cooperation between the two states. As a result of this visit, a joint communique was signed on the continued development of military cooperation. The communique point outs that intensive contacts aimed at improving security and military cooperation are regarded by the two sides as a constructive contribution to international stability and peace according to the UN statute, the Helsinki agreement, and the Paris Charter for New Europe.
