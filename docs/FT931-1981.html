930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Arcolectric falls to Pounds 105,000 Arcolectric (Holdings), a maker of switches, neon signal lamps and other electrical and electronic components, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 318,299 to Pounds 104,944 for 1992. The figures took account of reorganisation charges of Pounds 315,961 (Pounds 120,527). Sales were virtually static at Pounds 10.8m (Pounds 10.9m). A final dividend of 0.555p holds the total at 1.11p. Earnings fell to 2.13p (3.8p) per share.
