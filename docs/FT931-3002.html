930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / The Budget (Government Statements): Fall in Gift Aid minimum limit THE MINIMUM limit on Gift Aid donations is to fall Pounds 400 to Pounds 250 from yesterday and the annual limit on charitable gifts qualifying for income tax relief under the payroll giving scheme rises from Pounds 600 to Pounds 900 from April 6. The government said it would legislate to give tax relief on an employer's voluntary contributions towards a charitable agency's costs in running a payroll giving scheme.
