920605 FT 05 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Young Elvis earns stamp of approval A picture of the young Elvis Presley (left) is to appear on special US postage stamps from next January. The US Postal Service held a ballot to decide if Presley should be depicted at the start or end of his career. More than 1m people voted, with a 3-1 ratio in favour of the young Presley.
