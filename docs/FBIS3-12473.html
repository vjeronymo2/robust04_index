Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother President Yasir 'Arafat received the U.S. ambassador to Tunisia, John McCarthy, yesterday evening. The recent political developments and the dimensions and effects of the holy Ibrahim mosque massacre--which was committed by the Zionist criminals--on the peace process and on the talks currently in progress were discussed during the meeting. The UN Security Council's deliberations on reaching a decision on the massacre committed by the Zionist settlers, the need to provide international protection to the Palestinian people, and disarming the Zionist settlers were also reviewed.
