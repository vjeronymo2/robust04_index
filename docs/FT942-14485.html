940420 FT 20 APR 94 / Observer: Anglo's Politico The tentacles of Anglo American, the powerful conglomerate, have always extended beyond the boardrooms of South Africa. No surprise then that even in the new South Africa there was an Anglo representative on duty when yesterday's historic deal which brought Chief Buthelezi into next week's elections was signed. Standing discreetly in the background at Union Buildings, the seat of government in Pretoria, was none other than Michael Spicer, an Anglo director possessed of one of the keenest political brains in the country, who was taking a close interest in the proceedings that led to the breakthrough.
