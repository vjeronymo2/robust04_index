Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 4 Feb -- The State Security Court will tomorrow resume hearings in the case of the terrorist network which was intending to assassinate former US President George Bush during his visit to Kuwait last year. The plot was instigated and planned by the Iraqi intelligence services. On 25 December last year, the court postponed issuing sentences in this case in which 11 Iraqi and three defendants are implicated and decided to listen again to the main witness.
