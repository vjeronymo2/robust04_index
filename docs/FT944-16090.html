941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa moves higher Gold shares were under pressure from a falling bullion price but other issues posted strong gains in late afternoon trading, taking their lead from Wall Street's strong performance. The overall index collected 53 to 5,612, industrials rose 62 to 6,324 and golds finished 19 ahead at 2,301, off a high of 2,309. Dealers noted that the slightly softer financial rand had given shares leeway to make ground in rand terms after recent pressure. De Beers rose R2 to R101.75, Anglos rose R5 to R236 and Gencor was steady at R14.30. Remgro managed a strong 50 cent gain to R25.50, Malbak made 35 cents to R16.35 and Sasol rose 60 cents to R36.35. Sappi bounced as buying returned after its purchase for Dollars 1.6bn earlier in the week of a 70 per cent stake in SD Warren. Sappi rose R5 or 8.8 per cent to R62.
