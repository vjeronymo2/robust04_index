February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE AIR FARE CONTROVERSY

Simross wrote about shopping for emergency air fares where there is a death in the family. Recently I researched this problem for myself and found that Delta Airlines has an exceptional program. They offer a 35% discount for all family members traveling to a funeral from within the continental United States. This would include whether or not you are traveling with the remains as required by some of the airlines.

It is also a specific policy as opposed to whatever relief may be gained from a supervisor. The phone number is (800) DL CARES.

ROBERT L. KAILES

Playa del Rey

The Delta Airlines DL CARES program is unusual in the airline business because the carrier has an 800 number for consumers to dial in case of a death in the family. The requirement for the 35% discount on a regular coach fare is only that Delta must be shipping the remains of the deceased. Family members do not have to fly on the same flight with the body. Family members may get the discounted funeral fare from Delta up to one day prior to the shipping of the body and up to 21 days after.
