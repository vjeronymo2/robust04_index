April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"It's hard to believe that 40 pounds of muchacho could cause $275,000 of damage, but there it is."

-- Los Angeles Police Detective Ben Lovato, after two boys -- one 9 years old, the other 8 -- were arrested on suspicion of starting a fire that caused an estimated $275,000 damage to their Boyle Heights school, forcing officials to cram six classes into the auditorium and other temporary quarters.
