930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: James Bulger's funeral The funeral of two-year-old James Bulger, who disappeared while shopping with his mother in Bootle, Merseyside, and whose body was found 15 days ago on a railway line, took place in nearby Kirkby. Two 10-year-old boys have been accused of abducting and murdering James and attempting to abduct another two-year-old boy.
