940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: TBI pays Pounds 9.7m for mixed portfolio TBI, the property investment and development company, is paying Pounds 9.68m in cash and shares to acquire a mixed portfolio of 21 properties from Legal & General Assurance. The portfolio consists of 14 retail units, five industrial units and two office buildings. The majority of the properties are in south-east England with three in the south-west and TBI intends to hold the portfolio for future development and resale. Current total net rental income is Pounds 1.03m. After the transaction, L&G will have about a 5 per cent stake in TBI.
