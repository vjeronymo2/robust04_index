BFN [Unattributed report: "Right-Wing Parties Are Creating a New Bloc"] [Text] Zagreb -- As stated by the Croatian National Democratic League, right-wing Croatian parties held a meeting in Zagreb on Thursday [28 April]. These were the Croatian State-Creating Movement (HDP), the Croatian Democratic Party (HDS), the Croatian Democratic Party of Rights (HDSP), the Croatian Liberation Movement (HOP), the Croatian Republican Community (HRZ), the Croatian Party (HS), the Croatian Party of Rights (HSP), the Party of Croatian State Rights (SHDP), and the Croatian National Democratic League (HNDL). The parties agreed in principle on the creation of a bloc of Croatian national, rights-oriented, state-creating, and right-wing parties so that they could jointly appear and act in public in the future, proceeding from Croatia's state and national interests. According to the statement, they discussed the political situation in detail.
