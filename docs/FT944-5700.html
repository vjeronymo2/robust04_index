941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / Medical records network planned General practitioners will soon be able to use a national computer network to call up patients' medical records with graphics, details of operations and HIV status, Dr Kenneth Calman, the government's chief medical officer, announced yesterday. But the British Medical Association expressed fears about patients' confidentiality. Dr Fleur Fisher, head of the BMA's ethics and science committee, called for an independent report on the network before it comes into operation.
