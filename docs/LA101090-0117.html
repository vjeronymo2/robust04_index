October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

FISHING REPORT: PLENTY OF BASS AWAIT PUDDINGSTONE ANGLERS

Southland bass fishermen have been finding their quarry to be more than cooperative at Puddingstone Reservoir.

Butch Ham of Chino spent one day catching -- and releasing -- 52 bass weighing up to six pounds.

"And I got a call yesterday from a guy who caught 45," lake spokesman Ron Lindbloom said.
