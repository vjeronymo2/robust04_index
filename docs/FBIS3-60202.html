Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Effort To Smuggle Huge Amount of Narcotics From Kohat Foiled"] [Text] Kohat (MASHRIQ Correspondent) -- The Kohat customs staff has foiled an effort to smuggle opium worth hundreds of thousands of rupees to Punjab and arrested nomad female smugglers. According to the details available, the state collector of customs at Kohat received confidential information that some smugglers were going to smuggle a huge amount of narcotics soon. A raid party under the leadership of the deputy superintendent of narcotics began to check all vehicles. When they were searching through a GTS bus, tag number PRP-1793, to Multan, they recovered 54 kilogram of heroin from six nomad woman named Guddi, Shehla, Masrat, and Begi of Muzaffar Garh; and Allah Ditti and Surayya of Rahim Yar.
