941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Emerging Kleinwort Emerging Markets Trust, which aims for capital growth, had a fully diluted net asset value per share of 134.8p at the end of the six months to September 30. This compared with 103.9p a year ago. The undiluted figure of 141.7p represented a 3.8 per cent rise since the trust's year-end. Losses per share were 0.3p (0.07p earnings).
