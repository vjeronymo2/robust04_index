Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Tallinn, March 15 -- Minister of Justice Kaido Kama told a news conference that Estonian Government discussed, at its Tuesday [15 March] meeting, the order of issuing temporary travel documents to foreigners living in Estonia. He said that temporary travel documents are issued to persons that do not need a foreigners passport indispensably but who need to travel abroad for a shorter or a longer period. He added that Foreign Ministry was holding talks, at present, with other states on which form of the temporary travel documents would be acceptable and how visas could be stamped in them. Kama said that the final decision on temporary travel documents will be made by the government when solution to the problems of issuing foreigners passports is found.
