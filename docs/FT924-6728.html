921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / Sweden ratifies EEA treaty Sweden's parliament yesterday voted decisively in favour of a treaty creating the European Economic Area, Reuter reports from Stockholm. The EEA will comprise the 12 EC countries plus members of the European Free Trade Association: Austria, Finland, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Sweden and Switzerland. If ratified by all, the treaty will permit the free flow of people, goods, services and capital in an area from the Arctic to the Mediterranean from January 1.
