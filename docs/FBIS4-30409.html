BFN [Text] At a news conference after a 24 June cabinet meeting, Chief Cabinet Secretary Hiroshi Kumagai commented on the yen's rebound on New York Foreign Exchange Market, saying: "Finance Minister Hirohisa Fujii has also repeatedly said the government will deal with currency movements with a resolute attitude. We will maintain this attitude while considering a concerted intervention with other countries." He thus indicated a strong possibility of a concerted dollar-buying intervention in currency markets with the United States and European countries.
