Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 10 Feb -- The coordinating committee of the Central Committee of the trade unions of communication workers is suspending cuts in radio and television broadcasting. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told today at the Central Committee of the industry's trade unions that this decision was made in connection with the appeal made by Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin to the managers and workforces of radio and television relay centers to continue work in accordance with radio and television broadcasting schedules, and the decision to begin, from 10 February, paying the debts for work carried out in the fourth quarter of 1993. According to the same source, a meeting between Viktor Chernomyrdin and the leaders of the trade unions to examine the demands put forward will take place in the next few days.
