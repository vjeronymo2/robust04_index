Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad and His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain, this evening held a meeting at the People's Palace. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Bahraini Foreign Minister Muhammad Bin-Mubarak Al Khalifah. His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad awarded His Highness 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain, The National Umayyah Medal--Sash of the First Order. Shaykh 'Isa also awarded the president the Gulf Pendant--the highest honor in Bahrain. The president and His Highness Shaykh 'Isa exchanged words on this occasion. The president expressed his happiness to award his highness the amir the National Umayyah Medal which symbolizes the Arab nation's glory and illustrious history, as well as the pan-Arab ties among all the sons of the Arab nation. His Highness Shaykh 'Isa expressed his thanks for the hospitality and generous reception which he and the accompanying delegation received in Syria. He said: We feel that we are in our country. We consider Syria and Bahrain as one country. We are also proud of you as a president and a leader and hope that Syria will triumph over its enemies. The exchange of medals ceremony was attended by the official Bahraini delegation accompanying his highness the amir of Bahrain. On the Syrian side, the ceremony was attended by Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Dr. Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and ministers Faruq al-Shar', Dr. Muhammad Harbah, Dr. Muhammad Salman, Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, Wahib Fadil, As'ad Mustafa, and Radwan Martini. President Hafiz al-Asad this evening held a dinner banquet in honor of Syria's guest His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain. Also attending the banquet were the delegation accompanying his highness [the amir], Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Dr. Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, the deputy prime ministers, the ministers, secretary of the Presidential Office, Damascus governor, Damascus rural area governor, assistant ministers, Foreign Ministry departments heads, and heads of media organs.
