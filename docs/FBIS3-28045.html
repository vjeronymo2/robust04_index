Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 3 (KCNA) -- Letters to the great leader President Kim Il-song were adopted at a meeting and joint seminars on his New Year address held in Georgetown, Guyana, Harare, Zimbabwe and Lima, Peru. The letters say that the proposal to realise the reunification of the country in the form of confederation based on one nation, one state, two systems and two governments put forward by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address is the most realistic and fair one suitable to the conditions of Korea. They express firm solidarity with the Korean people in their just struggle to resolve the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula through dialogue and negotiation. They promise strengthened support and solidarity for the struggle of the Korean people for national reunification this year and wish President Kim Il-song good health and long life for the victory of the socialist cause and a world-wide victory of the cause of independence.
