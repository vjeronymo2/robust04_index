920424 FT 24 APR 92 / Observer: Dirty work So what are Britain's customs and excise officials now spending inordinate numbers of work-hours doing? Poring over imported pornographic books, pictures and such, says the National Audit Office's report, adding that every item suspected of obscenity has to be examined in detail. 'This can take a great deal of time. It is also a stressful and extremely unpleasant task. . . and staff have to be rested from the work at regular intervals.'
