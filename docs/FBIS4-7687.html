BFN [Text] The Slovak National Party [SNS] did not send its deputy chairman, Juraj Molnar, to the fifth Congress of the Liberal Democratic Party in Moscow. This was stated by SNS Chairman Jan Slota in an interview with TASR today. He said that Molnar had left as an observer for the world congress of Slav Orthodox and Christian nations with the consent of the SNS executive but without authorization to deliver any kind of message. According to Jan Slota, the invitation to the congress of Slavs did not mention the Fifth Congress of the Russian Liberal Democrats. The SNS chairman stressed that the SNS has not made any contacts with Zhirinovskiy's party nor has it the intention to do so in the future.
