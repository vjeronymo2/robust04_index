Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Bonn (DDP/ADN)--At the start of a series of talks in which the possibilities for a cease-fire in central Bosnia are to be sounded out, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel met Bosnian Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic in Bonn this afternoon. Just before the start of the talks, Kinkel told reporters he was glad for the opportunity to discuss with Silajdzic the difficult issues with regard to ending the conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina. [passage omitted] On Saturday [8 January], Franjo Tudjman and Alija Izetbegovic, presidents of Croatia and Bosnia, are to hold talks with the Yugoslavia mediators at the Petersberg [government guest house] near Bonn. Kinkel will then meet Tudjman and Izetbegovic on Saturday afternoon.
