930826 FT 26 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: New highs in London and New York The FT-SE 100 Index rose nearly 30 points to close at a new peak of 3,079.2, as London stocks followed the lead from across the Atlantic, encouraged also by optimism in Germany ahead of today's Bundesbank meeting.
