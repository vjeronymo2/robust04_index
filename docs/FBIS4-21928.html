BFN [Text] Barukh Marzel, who was sought by the police for three weeks, was arrested a short while ago in the settlement of Bney Sever, south of Qiryat Arba'. Our police affairs correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that detectives from the National Unit for Serious Crimes Investigations, policemen from the Judaea Region, and a Border Police contingent captured Barukh Marzel at his hideout. He is currentlty being interrogated at the Petah Tiqva offices of the Natioanl Unit for Serious Crimes Investigations.
