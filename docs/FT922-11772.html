920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Kingston Oil & Gas doubles to Pounds 1.19m Net profits of Kingston Oil & Gas, the oil and gas producer, more than doubled from Pounds 501,399 to Pounds 1.19m in the year to December 31. Earnings per share amounted to 9.01p (4.86p) and a recommended final dividend of 1.5p lifts the total to 2.5p (1.56p).
