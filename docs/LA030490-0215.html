March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MONTOYA DEAL WORTH $1.45 MILLION

Did someone say Max Montoya's reported $650,000-a-year contract makes him the highest-paid Raider offensive lineman ever?

Actually, he'll make more than that.

A source familiar with the details of the contract said it's a two-year, $1.45-million deal, composed of:

-- A $50,000 "relocation" bonus.

-- A $550,000 salary plus a $100,000 reporting bonus this season.

-- A $650,000 salary plus a $100,000 reporting bonus in 1991.

On an annual basis, Montoya's contract beats the three-year, $1.8-million package the 49ers gave Dave Waymer, the biggest Plan B total contract of this off-season. MARK HEISLER
