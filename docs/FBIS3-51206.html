Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "National" newscast] [Text] The Attorney General's Office decided to intervene in the case of the gringo soldiers who are now in Bahia Malaga. A group of Armed Forces lawyers will travel to the area, where they will interview residents, visit the military installations, and inspect aircraft and military vehicles to determine how they are being used. The Attorney General's Office will also determine the purpose of the construction projects in Juanchaco, as well as the size and type of terrain where a school is supposedly being built.
