920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: ICI in Pounds 45m Diprivan expansion Imperial Chemical Industries is proceeding with a Pounds 45m expansion of its production site near Milan. The move reflects the success of Diprivan, its injectable anaesthetic. The investment project at ICI-Pharma at Caponago will approximately double capacity and increase flexibility in the manufacture of Diprivan. The project, due for completion in late 1994, involves the purchase of 13,000 sq m of land.
