Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Estonia's former Foreign Minister Trivimi Velliste who will this summer become the country's ambassador to the United Nations said NATO programme "Partnership for Peace" was very favourable to Estonia as there were no alternatives. "If the programme will be developed in a way that Estonia has enough time to join in and this way develop its own political and national defence structures, practical cooperation with NATO and bringing Estonia's national defence in line with NATO standards will become possible in near future," Velliste said.
