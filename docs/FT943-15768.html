940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / International Company News: Motorola to buy IBM data stake Motorola, the US electronics group, has signed an agreement to acquire International Business Machines' 50 per cent interest in the Ardis wireless data network, Reuter reports from Schaumburg, Illinois. Terms were not disclosed, but as a result of the agreement, Motorola will own all of the four-year-old venture, the companies said. Ardis provides radio data communications to more than 400 metropolitan areas, the companies said.
