Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Xiao Gong (2556 1362]) [Text] Beijing, 25 Dec 93 (XINHUA) -- MARKET LEGAL SYSTEM HERALD (Shi Chang Fa Zhi Dao Kan 1579 1034 3127 0455 1418 0436), a general interest news monthly covering the socialist market's legal system, will be inaugurated next January [1994]. The magazine allots some 20 columns to themes related to the market and the legal system.
