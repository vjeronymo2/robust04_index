930607 FT 07 JUN 93 / UN flies civilian staff from Somalia to Kenya The UN has begun flying civilian staff from Somalia to Kenya for safety, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. A UN spokesman said three flights would be used to evacuate some civilian staff to Nairobi. Officials were also considering concentrating staff from isolated posts in Mogadishu at safer centres. Some UN officials spent Saturday night in their offices, trapped by street fighting. Foreign aid workers said fresh attacks on UN forces were feared.
