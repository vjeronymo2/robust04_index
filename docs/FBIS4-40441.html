BFN [Unattributed report published under the rubric "Fact"] [Text] In the opinion of Ukrainian presidential candidate Valeriy Babych, it is inexpedient to build the oil terminal in Odessa, as, having spent billions of dollars, Ukraine may find itself totally dependent upon oil-producing countries. He believes that it would be more expedient to lease from Russia or to redeem some of the oil field in the Tyumen Region, where Ukraine took part in oil exploration.
