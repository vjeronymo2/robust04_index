October 15, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP WATER POLO;

CORONA DEL MAR MOVES TO 16-0

Corona del Mar defeated El Toro, 6-4, to win the North Orange County Water Polo Tournament at Independence Park in Fullerton Saturday.

Conrad Macedo and Ryan Weir each had two goals to lead Corona del Mar, which went 5-0 in the tournament to improve to 16-0 on the season. It was the third meeting of the season between the two teams.

Robbie Pike had eight saves for Corona del Mar.

In its semifinal match, Corona del Mar defeated Sunny Hills, 10-4. Weir had four goals and Pike had 12 saves to lead Corona del Mar.
