BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, clashes between the freedom fighters and Indian troops continue in various parts of the valley in which 18 more people have been killed. The clashes occurred in Pulwama, Punch, Baramula, (Dandarbal), Srinagar, and Ghazipura- Hindwara. A total strike was observed in Pulwama and adjoining rural areas as a protest against the killing of five people by Indian troops in (Guddu) village. The people in Nagabal also observed a strike against Indian atrocities. Meanwhile, Jammu and Kashmir Trade Union Front has called a strike on 7 and 9 May as a protest against the violation of human rights by the occupation forces.
