BFN [Text] Cairo -- President Hafiz al-Asad and President Muhammad Husni Mubarak resumed talks in a closed meeting held at the permanent headquarters of the Egyptian Presidency in Cairo at 1115 Damascus time [0815 GMT] today. Talks dealt with the Arab situation, inter-Arab relations in light of the current developments and circumstances, and the importance of working to improve inter-Arab relations and for an Arab solidarity that serves both the Arab nation's pan-Arab interest and the pan-Arab issues.
