BFN [Report by political correspondent Shim'on Schiffer] [Excerpt] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and King Husayn may hold a public meeting at the White House next month. Top-ranking political sources in Jerusalem said last night that contacts toward the meeting are in high gear. The intention is for Rabin and Husayn to hold their first public meeting in Washington, under the patronage of President Clinton. [passage omitted]
