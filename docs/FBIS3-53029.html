Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Informed sources have stated that the U.S. Embassy is encouraging U.S. firms and businessmen to buy land in the "occupied strip" [Israel's self-declared security zone in south Lebanon]. This information coincides with reports from Marj 'Uyun that a U.S. company has bought vast tracts of lands in Sarda and al-'Umrah near the al-Wazzani water spring.
