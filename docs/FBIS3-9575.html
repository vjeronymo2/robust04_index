Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "No Longer Relevant...."] [Text] In a public opinion poll conducted by the Statistical Office of the Slovak Republic in March, 1,273 citizens were asked to reply to the question: "Which politician active in the Slovak Republic enjoys your trust at present?" The replies acquired do not yet reflect the situation that arose after the 27th meeting of the National Council of the Slovak Republic with its consequences [passage of the vote of no-confidence in Vladimir Meciar's government and the appointment of a new government].
