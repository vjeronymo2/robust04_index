Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The second session of the 12th Tianjin Municipal people's congress ceremoniously opened at Tianjin auditorium on the morning of 2 March. Shouldered with the heavy trust of Tianjin's 9 million residents, 686 municipal deputies will conscientiously discuss and decide on Tianjin's various tasks for 1994 and strive to turn the congress session into a meeting characterized by democracy, unity, the work style of seeking truth from facts, and a practical and pioneering spirit. The Tianjin auditorium was solemnly and grandly decorated on 2 March. In the middle part of the rostrum hung the national emblem flanked by 10 red flags. Fresh flowers were placed on the rostrum, and the horizontal streamer hanging in the middle of the meeting place carried the words of seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, expanding opening up, promoting development, and maintaining stability. Sitting on the front row of the rostrum were executive chairmen of the congress session, including Gao Dezhan, Li Jianguo, Nie Bichu, Lu Xuezheng, Zhu Wenju, Wang Chenghuai, Qian Qiao, Yu Fujing, Chen Rongdi, Liu Wenfan, Zhang Yuhuan, Su Baocong, and Liu Huigen. At 9 AM, Executive Chairman Nie Bichu declared the congress session open. [passage omitted] Mayor Zhang Lichang delivered a government work report, entitled: Seizing the opportunity, advancing in unity, and creating a new situation in reform, opening up, and development. [passage omitted] His government work report is composed of five parts: 1. review of last year's work; 2. work principles and major tasks of 1994; 3. maintenance of sustained, rapid, and sound development in the national economy; 4. promotion of the development of social undertakings; 5. strengthening government building. [passage omitted] Attending the session on the morning of 2 March were municipal leaders, including [name indistinct], Liu Jinfeng, Yang Zhihua, Liu Fengyan, Fang Fengyou, Li Huifen, Song Pingshun, Lu Jiyun, and Zheng Zhiying. Deputies of the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] in Tianjin, and all members attending the second session of the ninth municipal CPPCC Committee attended the session as observers. Responsible comrades of relevant government departments were also invited to the congress session.
