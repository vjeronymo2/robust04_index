940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: M&G Income Investment Trust Asset value of capital shares rose from 59.52p to 93.45p in the year ended January 31 1994. Asset value of zero rated preference shares was 43.68p (39.22p). Revenue after tax was Pounds 11.2m (Pounds 15.1m) and earnings per income share 4.54p (6.13p). Final dividend 1.9125p making 4.9125p (4.9125p).
