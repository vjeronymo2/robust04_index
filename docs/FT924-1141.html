921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Abtrust Preferred asset value declines Abtrust Preferred Income Investment Trust had a net asset value of 71.55p per income share at November 30 compared with 78.86p a year earlier and 82.38p at the May 31 year end. Net revenue for the half year was Pounds 683,000 (Pounds 817,000) for earnings of 7.59p (9.08p) per share. An unchanged second interim dividend of 2.90625p is declared.
