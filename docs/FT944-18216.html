941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Vodafone to sponsor Derby Mobile communications company Vodafone is to sponsor the Derby in a deal worth Pounds 3.5m over three years. The Epsom horse race is to be run on a Saturday instead of the traditional first Wednesday in June, in a move to reverse falling attendances. Observer, Page 23; Record growth in mobile phones, Page 11
