940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: Greek fans uncompromising Greece has been stunned by its team's second consecutive World Cup loss, suffered when Bulgaria trounced the Greek side 4-0. 'Greek national team shame on you,' the Athens afternoon daily Ta Nea said in a banner headline, accompanied by a half-page picture showing fans at an Athens coffee shop making obscene gestures at a TV set. Other papers, television and radio were much more harsh in their criticism of the team and its coach, Alketas Panagoulias. 'The people are enraged,' the daily Eleftheros Typos said.
