BFN [Text] An Armenian parliamentary delegation headed by Deputy Speaker Aram Saakyan arrived in Tbilisi today. The delegation had a meeting with Eduard Shevardnadze, the chairman of the Georgian Supreme Council and head of state. The conversation dealt with fruitfully developing relations between Armenia and Georgia. Problems common for the two countries have been considered. In particular, the Armenian representatives pointed out that the return of refugees to Abkhazia is very important for them, too, because a lot of ethnic Armenians, who have also been affected by the conflict, live in Abkhazia. Shevardnadze has said that the Georgian side will appreciate the involvement of the Republic of Armenia in the settlement of the issue. The conversation also touched upon the work of the two parliaments. The sides have shared their experience. The meeting was attended by Vakhtang Rcheulishvili, the deputy speaker of the Georgian Supreme Council.
