940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Rothmans Industries Rothmans Industries' subsidiary, Rothmans of Pall Mall (Singapore), has sold buildings for MDollars 185.3m (Pounds 47.5m) to Winwill Investment and Welwyn Investment, conditional on shareholders' approval. The buyers will grant a tenancy of the buildings to RPMS until no later than February 1998. The sale will result in an expected gain of MDollars 163.4m.
