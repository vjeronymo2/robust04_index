BFN [Text] The protest action initiated by the Alfa Cartel Trade Union Confederation, which was joined by the CNSLR-Fratia [National Confederation of Free Unions in Romania] and the National Trade Union Bloc [BNS], is still continuing in Victoriei Square. Being present among the protesters, Mr. Victor Ciorbea, the chairman of the CNSLR-Fratia, stated that, quote, further efforts are being made to organize a second round of negotiations with the government. Until now those efforts have failed, so what is left is the meeting scheduled to take place tomorrow at 1000 [0700 GMT]. However, we still hope we could meet with the government today, unquote. At the same time, rallies and marches have been organized in several towns of the country by the teaching staff of schools. Also today, in Mures County, all employees of the Mures Wood Processing Company have gone on strike.
