BFN [Text] President Husni Mubarak has declared that the PLO and Israel have agreed on 4 May as the target date for the final signing of the agreement on Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. He noted that he proposed signing the agreement in Cairo. This statement was made after the talks President Mubarak held with Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. President Mubarak said that a meeting will be held between Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin on 3 May. He said that he asked U.S. Secretary of State Christopher to extend his mission in the region to attend the signing of the Palestinian-Israeli agreement. It is noteworthy that both sides signed an agreement in principle in Washington on 13 September. President Mubarak received Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat in the Presidential Palace in Heliopolis this afternoon. They reviewed the latest developments in the Middle East issue and the efforts designed to propel the peace efforts in the region. President Mubarak then received U.S. Secretary of State Christopher. He then held a meeting with Yasir 'Arafat, Secretary Christopher, and Israeli Foreign Minister Peres. After the meeting, the four leaders held a news conference at the Presidential Palace in Heliopolis.
