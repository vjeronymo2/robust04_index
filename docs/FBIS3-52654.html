Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd promised during a tour of the Gaza Strip today that the British would assist in the establishment of the autonomy in the West Bank and Gaza Strip and would immediately grant 20 million pounds sterling to the Palestinians and another 70 million pounds sterling in another two years. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that during the tour, Hurd conferred with Zakariya al-Agha, Fatah leader of the Gaza Strip, and Mansur al-Shawwa, a leading Palestinian in the strip.
