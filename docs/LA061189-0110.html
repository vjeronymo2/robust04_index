June 11, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

OCEANSIDE WOMAN FOUND SLAIN IN HOME

Oceanside police were investigating the killing of an elderly woman who was found dead by her husband when he returned to the couple's home in the 800 block of Rio Viento on Saturday morning.

Investigators released few details about the case, saying only that the woman, identified only as being about 65 years old, had been stabbed several times and was dead by the time they arrived at the scene
