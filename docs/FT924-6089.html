921123 FT 23 NOV 92 / Israel adamant on peace talks Israeli cabinet ministers yesterday said that Middle East peace talks would continue despite Arab warnings that the talks face collapse, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. Mr Shimon Peres, foreign minister, said: 'There is no deadlock because there will be a continuation of the negotiations.' Police Minister Moshe Shahal denied a report that Palestinians at the talks in Washington had threatened if no progress was achieved soon.
