931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / Hain attacks rise in mail charges Mr PETER HAIN, Labour MP for Neath, has written to Mr Bill Cockburn, chief executive of the Post Office, complaining at steep increases in charges for redirecting mail. He said prices for the service had been 'arbitrarily increased' by up to 71 per cent. The Post Office said it had invested Pounds 10m in upgrading the service to provide 'a much higher degree of efficiency'.
