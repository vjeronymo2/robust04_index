MCC believes the proposed rule change is consistent with Section 17A of the Act in that it promotes the prompt and accurate clearance and settlement of securities transactions. MCC's procedures setting forth the shortened time period for transferring accounts are appropriate because they properly reflect the changes set forth by the NYSE and NSCC in their efforts to enhance automation of the transfer process.
