921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / UK Company News: TDS Circuits lifts exports by 89% TDS Circuits, the printed circuits board manufacturer, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 595,000 to Pounds 488,000 for the six months ended August 31. Turnover of the Lancashire-based company increased 18 per cent to Pounds 3.46m (Pounds 2.92m), while exports jumped 89 per cent to Pounds 2.24m (Pounds 1.18m). Losses per share were 7.08p (8.28p).
