940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Delaney back in the black Delaney, the shopfitter and joiner, returned to the black in 1993 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 210,000, compared with restated losses of Pounds 5.23m last time. Turnover from continuing operations increased by 15.8 per cent to Pounds 15.7m (Pounds 13.6m). The turnround followed a Pounds 187,000 loss in the first half. Earnings per share at the year end were 0.5p (9.9p losses). There is no dividend.
