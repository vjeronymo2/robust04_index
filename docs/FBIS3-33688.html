Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] We have received confirmed news from Baghdad saying that on the evening of Wednesday, 26 January 1994, a number of jumbo [preceding word in English] cars, affiliated with the Iraqi regime's intelligence service, were driven at high speed into a square near the Baghdad International Fair, which has become a market for dollar dealers. The Iraqi regime's intelligence forces asked the dealers to be quiet and to remain in their places. However, the dealers, who were aware of the intentions of the intelligence men, tried to escape. The regime's men fired at those who tried to escape, killing and wounding many innocent people. After gaining control over the area, the regime men arrested many people and left the bodies of the slain in the streets. Such criminal acts have exacerbated the miserable situation in Baghdad and increased the citizens' worries.
