Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Canada and Mexico have agreed to work together to push for an end to the U.S.-imposed trade embargo against Cuba. Canada's foreign minister, Andre Ouellet, and his Mexican counterpart, Manuel Tello, met in Mexico City yesterday, and they said it is time to convince the U.S. to end the isolation of Cuba. Neither Canada nor Mexico observe the U.S.-led embargo. The embargo has been in force since the 1960's.
