September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

In Black's Shoes: Clint Black, the 1989 Country Music Assn.'s Horizon Award winner, will debut his upcoming single, "Put Yourself in My Shoes," on the 24th annual "CMA Awards Show" Oct. 8. The single, scheduled to be released to radio Oct. 9, is on Black's next album, due out in November. Black is nominated for four CMA Awards this year, including entertainer of the year. Reba McEntire and Randy Travis will host the awards, to be broadcast live from the Grand Ole Opry House on CBS at 9 p.m. BETH KLEID
