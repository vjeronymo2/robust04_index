BFN [Text] Israeli security forces have uncovered a Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] cell believed to be responsible for the bus attack in Hadera, in which a suicide bomber killed five Israelis. The Army said 27 suspects were arrested in the operation in the Samaria villages of Ya'bad and Barta'ah. It said the group leader, Sa'id Muhammad Bardanah, aged 24, is suspected of making the two bombs that exploded in the bus in Hadera on 13 April. Ahuva Kohen-Unaylah, wounded in the 'Afula bomb attack on 6 April, has died at the Haifa hospital. She was married to an Arab citizen from the village of Yafa in Galilee and converted to Islam a month ago. Her death brings to eight the number of casualties in the Hamas attack in 'Afula.
