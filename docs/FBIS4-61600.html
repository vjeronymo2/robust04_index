BFN [Text] Armenia's new military detachments, which were transferred to the hostilities zone in Nagorno-Karabakh resumed the offensive on the Fizuli section of the front, Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry reported on Sunday [8 May]. As a result of fierce fighting, the Azerbaijani Army destroyed one tank and one missile launcher Grad by yesterday's evening. The Defense Ministry also reports that a car Niva with officers of the Armenian Army was destroyed as a result of hostilities. The Azerbaijani units forced the adversary to move back to the starting positions having killed about 100 Armenian soldiers. Simultaneously the Armenian forces launched an offensive in the Kelbadjar region in the direction of the mountain pass Omar. As the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reports, at present Armenians continue to methodically subject Azerbaijani positions (located in the Agdam, Terter, and Geranboi directions) to rocket-artillery shelling.
