BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] With regard to Palestinian diplomacy, the Palestinian president received today the U.S. ambassador in Tunis, who delivered to him a message from the U.S. Administration in connection with the recently signed agreement between the Palestinians and the Israelis on security in Hebron. In his reply to this message, Mr. 'Arafat asked the U.S. Administration to speed up the implementation of the pledges of the international banks and the countries that expressed readiness to offer financial and economic aid to the Palestinians in order to begin executing the essential services in the territories that would come under Palestinian self-rule.
