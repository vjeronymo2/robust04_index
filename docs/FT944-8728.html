941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / Observer: Greener grass Rather than political power, it seems German-born Henry Kissinger really hankered after fame as a sports commentator: 'I always wanted to be a sportscaster, but I just couldn't lose this New York accent,' he says in a new advertisement for the New York Times. Perennial presidential candidate Jesse Jackson says he takes the paper for its real estate; he's hunting for 'a big white house with pillars' on Washington's Pennsylvania Avenue. And Bill Clinton? No news as yet. But maybe he takes it for its job vacancies.
