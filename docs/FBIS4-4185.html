BFN [Text] Beijing, April 19 (XINHUA) -- A reception was held here today to mark the 20th anniversary of the establishment of China-Gabon diplomatic relations. The reception was sponsored by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries (CPAFC) and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association. Wang Jincheng, secretary general of the CPAFC, Wang Changyi, assistant foreign minister, and Benjamin Legnongo-Ndumba, ambassador of Gabon to China, attended the reception.
