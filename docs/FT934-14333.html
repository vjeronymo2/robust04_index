931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / Cardenas to stand again MR Cuauhtemoc Cardenas, a defector from Mexico's ruling party who almost beat President Carlos Salinas in 1988 elections, was named by his left-leaning Democratic Revolutionary party yesterday to seek the presidency once again in 1994, Reuter reports from Mexico City. Mr Cardenas, selected unopposed, described the August 1994 presidential elections as 'the decisive battle for democracy' in Mexico.
