921116 FT 16 NOV 92 / Graduate job chances decline JOB opportunities for next year's graduates are set to contract by as much as 7.3 per cent, according to a survey of more than 150 employers by the IRS independent research group. It found that starting salaries are unlikely to keep pace with inflation. Employers said they would cut graduate recruitment by between 5.9 per cent and 7.3 per cent. Employee Development Bulletin No. 35, November 1992, Industrial Relations Services, 18-20 Highbury Place, London N5 1QP. Pounds 17.50.
