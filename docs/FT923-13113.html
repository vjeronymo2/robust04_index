920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / French open Vietnam bank France's Banque Indosuez yesterday became the first foreign bank to open a branch in Vietnam, Reuter reports from Hanoi. Banque Indosuez, one of five French banks with representative offices in Vietnam, opened in the nation's commercial hub, Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon). Representative offices have not been allowed in the past to carry out full banking business.
