Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jan (MENA)--New Argentine Ambassador in Cairo Albino Gomez has voiced his country's support of Egypt's effort to boost the Middle East peace process and find a comprehensive settlement. In a statement today, the ambassador said his country has always backed efforts to find a peaceful solution to this crisis. Gomez praised Egyptian-Argentine relations, adding that his country is eager to consolidate and develop these relations. The ambassador was speaking after a meeting with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, who received a copy of the ambassador's credentials.
