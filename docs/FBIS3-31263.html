Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "(per)": "There Will Be Joint Exercises and Also Units With the Bundeswehr"] [Text] Prague--Not only should joint exercises be held in the future between the Czech Army and the Bundeswehr, but also joint units should be formed within the framework of the Partnership for Peace project. This statement was made to reporters on Tuesday [8 February] by General J. Nekvasil, chief of the Czech Army General Staff. He added that "this will not be this year, but next year." The first joint exercises with a Western army will take place from 15-25 March. Forty Dutch marines will conduct exercises in the Czech Republic with the Czech Rapid Deployment Battalion. Further joint exercises will be held with the French in the May-June period.
