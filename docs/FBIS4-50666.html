BFN [By reporter Xu Furui (6079 4395 3843)] [Text] Copenhagen, 17 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee, wound up his four-day official goodwill visit to Denmark and left here for Belgium by special plane this afternoon. He was sent off at the airport by Danish Foreign Ministry officials and Chinese Ambassador to Denmark Zheng Yaowen. During his visit, Li Ruihuan held talks with Danish Government and parliament leaders, visited universities and industrial enterprises, met with entrepreneurs, and stayed at a farmhouse. The visit was a success. It is understood that while departing Denmark, he cabled a telegram from the airplane to Danish Foreign Minister Petersen, thanking the Danish Government for the warm reception granted to him. He said: "I am convinced the strengthening of Sino-Danish friendship and cooperation accords with the interests of the two peoples."
