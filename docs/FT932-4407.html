930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: CML gains ground in second half CML Microsystems made up lost ground in the second half of 1992-93, producing pre-tax profits virtually maintained at Pounds 4.3m for the year to March 31. The result for this USM-quoted specialised electronic products group followed a decline of 9 per cent at the halfway stage and was aided by a strong performance by the US semiconductor and UK traffic operations. The proposed dividend is lifted 27 per cent to 4.7p, payable from earnings per share of 15.15p (16.24p). The shares rose 7p to 258p. Operating profits showed a 2.5 per cent gain on turnover ahead 9.7 per cent at Pounds 15.7m.
