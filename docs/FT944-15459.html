941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / International Company News: HK property disposal behind sharp increase at San Miguel San Miguel, the Philippines' largest food and beverage conglomerate, posted a sharply-improved net profit, of 9.6bn pesos (Dollars 375.7m), in the first nine months of the year. It attributed the rise to an extraordinary gain from a Hong Kong property sale, Reuter reports from Manila. Even without the 6.46bn pesos gain, San Miguel would have posted a net profit of 3.14bn pesos in the January to September period, 40 per cent up on the 2.24bn in the same period last year. San Miguel, through its San Miguel Brewery Hong Kong unit, sold a prime property in the colony for HKDollars 3.5bn (Dollars 452.9m). The company's consolidated sales in the first nine months amounted to 48.9bn pesos, 12 per cent better than the same period last year.
