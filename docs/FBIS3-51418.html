Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Panorama" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has postponed for an indefinite period his state visit to Spain due to the conflict in Chiapas State. The presidential trip was scheduled for the end of the month and was in response to the visit that King Juan Carlos paid to Mexico in January 1990. Spanish Government spokesmen indicated that the governments of the two countries are going to decide on a new date for the visit.
