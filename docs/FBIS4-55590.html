BFN [Excerpt] Zvornik, Bratunac, 17 May (TANJUG) -- Resuming their offensive against the republic of Srpska, Muslim units are still using chemical weapons in the Majevica area where they have enjoyed UNPROFOR's military backing again, TANJUG learned today from republic of Srpska army sources. According to these sources, strong Muslim attacks in cooperation with UNPROFOR's Nordic unit continued against most Serbian defense positions on Mount Majevica, particularly against Serbian villages in the Stolice area. Muslim units again targeted Serbs with large caliber chlorine-charged shells in addition to artillery and infantry weapons. Serbian military sources pointed out that for the second consecutive day a part of UNPROFOR's Nordic unit continued to provide military back-up for the Muslim side. The same unit targeted the Serbs with tank fire yesterday, also using armored personnel carriers. [passage omitted]
