931029 FT 29 OCT 93 / War bunker costs reach Pounds 126m THE GOVERNMENT has so far spent Pounds 126m on building and equipping a wartime command centre underneath the Ministry of Defence in Whitehall. The figure emerged last night in a parliamentary written answer by Mr Jeremy Hanley, armed forces minister, in response to a question by Mr John Hutton, Labour MP for Barrow and Furness.
