930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands makes Pounds 6.6m property buy Ex-Lands, the property management group, has acquired Centre Heights, Finchley Road, London, for Pounds 6.6m, of which Pounds 6.4m is cash and Pounds 201,400 by the allotment of ordinary 10p shares. The property has retail, office and residential accommodation.
