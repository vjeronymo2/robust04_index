August 4, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

PACIFIC CHORALE EXPANDS EXECUTIVE ROLES

The Pacific Chorale announced Thursday that Bonnie McClain has been appointed executive director in addition to her current position as director of development. Also, Richard Messenger, assistant conductor, has been named director of operations. Leila Asato will be administrative assistant.

Rita Major, formerly manager of the Pacific Chorale, has become assistant ticket manager for the Pacific Symphony, orchestra officials confirmed. CHRIS PASLES
