930705 FT 05 JUL 93 / Sir Patrick Mayhew condemns IRA attacks on emergency crews in Belfast SIR Patrick Mayhew, Northern Ireland secretary, yesterday condemned 'cowardly attacks' on police and fire crews in Belfast. Police said the unrest, which flared after the funeral on Friday of a member of the extremist Protestant Ulster Volunteer Force, was the province's worst for years. They said several people were injured during seven hours of 'serious public disorder' on Saturday night.
