Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 31 Jan (INA)--The Arab League has expressed its willingness to host a reconciliation summit between Yemeni President Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. It invited them and the other parties concerned [to Cairo] to endorse the document of accord recently agreed upon by Yemeni parties to defuse the crisis. The invitation came in a telephone call to Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, who is in Aden, from Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid. It was reported that the Arab League secretary general informed al-Bid of the Arab League's willingness to host, at the Arab League Headquarters, the reconciliation meeting of the parties concerned so that they may sign the accord document. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid also welcomed the participation in such a meeting of all the Arab and friendly sides that might want to attend the document signing ceremony--the parties whose attendance at such a ceremony is deemed necessary.
