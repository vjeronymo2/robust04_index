930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Mercedes-Benz revives Philippine interest MERCEDES-BENZ, the automotive division of Daimler-Benz, is interested in restarting assembly of its cars in the Philippines following changes to import regulations, Reuter reports from Stuttgart. Mercedes said it was still uncertain whether a new plant would be built and when assembly could begin. Talks are under way with the government.
