Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Dispatch from Urumqi by correspondent Xin Wen (6580 5113): "Eight More Papers in the Region Start `Weekend Edition,' `End-of-Month Edition'"] [Text] With the approval of the Autonomous Regional Press and Publications Administration, eight more newspapers in Xinjiang have started publishing "weekend editions" and "end-of-month editions" since the beginning of 1994. They are YILI RIBAO, GONGREN SHIBAO [WORKERS' TIMES], XINJIANG SHIYOU BAO [XINJIANG PETROLEUM NEWS], JUNGGAR SHIBAO [JUNGGAR TIMES], XINJIANG TIEDAO BAO [XINJIANG RAILWAY NEWS], XIBU JIANSHE BAO [WESTERN REGION CONSTRUCTION NEWS], URUMQI GONGAN BAO [URUMQI PUBLIC SECURITY NEWS], and XINJIANG JIAOYU BAO [XINJIANG EDUCATION NEWS]. With the same approval given to XINJIANG RIBAO and XINJIANG JUNKEN BAO [XINJIANG ARMY FARM NEWS] earlier, there are now ten Xinjiang newspapers publishing "weekend editions" and "end-of-month editions." Since the beginning of this year, XINJIANG RIBAO has published eight pages on Wednesdays and Saturdays. The four additional pages, including the "weekend edition," deals mainly with matters of everyday life.
