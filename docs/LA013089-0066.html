January 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Trans World Airlines Inc. is liable for civil damages to 3,000 former flight attendants who were replaced when they went on strike in 1986, a federal judge has ruled. U.S. District Judge Ilana Rovner set the stage for a hearing on damages with her ruling last week. No date for the hearing was set, but lawyers for both sides are scheduled to meet with Rovner this Wednesday and decide how to proceed.
