October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CLIPPERS PLAY IN NEBRASKA

The Clippers, opening a stretch of three games in three nights, will begin their exhibition schedule tonight against the Detroit Pistons at Lincoln, Neb.

The teams will also play Saturday night in St. Louis. The Clippers travel to Richmond, Va., the next day to meet the Washington Bullets.

Sixteen players will travel for the Clippers. Danny Manning is expected to see only limited action because of a sprained left ankle.
