BFN [Excerpt] The Coalition Council met in Bratislava today under the chairmanship of Jozef Moravcik, the Slovak prime minister and chairman of the Democratic Union of Slovakia. It discussed the course to be taken by the Slovak Republic in the drafting of documents on the security initiative named as the pact of stability and other topical issues. Regarding Slovakia's participation in meetings of the stability pact, the Coalition Council agreed that Slovakia would be represented by a governmental delegation. This also applies to the opening meeting of the stability pact planned to take place next Thursday and Friday. The Coalition Council regard the solution of the problem of minorities as an internal matter of the state, although representatives of the Hungarian political parties voiced their demand to be allowed to participate in the meetings of the stability pact. However, it will be up to the government to decide who should form Slovakia's Government delegation. [passage omitted]
