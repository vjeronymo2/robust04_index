941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Cattle's (Holdings) CATTLE'S (Holdings) is selling its five travel agencies, which trade as Travelplan, to Going Places, part of Airtours. The consideration is Pounds 150,000 cash. There will be an exceptional charge of about Pounds 120,000, due to reinstatement of goodwill, in the group's results for 1994.
