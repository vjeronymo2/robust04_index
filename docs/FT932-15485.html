930413 FT 13 APR 93 / Observer: Value for money A Barclays banker writes. 'We enclose your cheque for Dollars 8.15. We feel that it is not in your best interest to negotiate this cheque for you as its sterling value totals Pounds 5.43 and our charge for negotiating the cheque is Pounds 8.' So who gets to keep the Dollars 8.15?
