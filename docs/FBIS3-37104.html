Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of Tajik opposition guerrillas on Friday [11 February] evening assaulted a detail of Russian guards on the section of the Tajik- Afghan border controlled by Post No. 3 of the Pyandzh Border Guard Detachment. A source in the press bureau of the Russian Border Guard told INTERFAX the detail and a reinforcement that had arrived from the post beat off the assault, one Russian serviceman being wounded. The same day, another clash occurred on the same section of the border, the Tajik militants retreating and dispersing in rush thickets on the bank of the Pyandzh River. The press bureau reported that Tajik guerrillas on Friday launched four attacks, using small arms, rocket missiles and grenade projectors, on Russian squads guarding the strip of the border controlled by post No. 12 of the Moskovskiy Border Guard Detachment. The Russians suppressed all the firing points of the guerrillas, the bureau said. It said none of the Russian servicemen were hurt, the guerrillas' losses being so far unknown.
