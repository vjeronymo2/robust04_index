Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 19 KYODO -- Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, accompanied by his wife Kayoko, left Tokyo Saturday [19 March] morning for Beijing for three days [as received] of his first official visit to China. On Sunday, Hosokawa will meet Chinese leaders, including President Jiang Zemin, Premier Li Peng and Vice Premier Zhu Rongji, to discuss bilateral and international affairs. The topics discussed will include North Korea's suspected nuclear weapons program, human rights in China, increases in China's defense budget and bilateral economic cooperation. On Sunday, he will visit Shanghai before returning to Japan the same day.
