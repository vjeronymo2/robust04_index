April 20, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

NATION;

PUPPY'S 911 GROWL JUST IN FUN

A dispatcher taking an emergency 911 call was puzzled when the caller said nothing, just growled.

Dispatchers traced the call to a house, and when police arrived, they didn't find Spuds McKenzie, only a snarling 2-year-old pit bull named Sheba and two puppies, Lt. Lyle Roberts said. One of the puppies had the receiver of the push-button telephone in its mouth.

"There were no humans in the house," Roberts said.
