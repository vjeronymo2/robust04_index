930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Nesco lifts first half profit Nesco Investments, the computer software group which also generates and supplies electricity in Nigeria, reported pre - tax profits up from Pounds 28,000 to Pounds 169,000 in the six months to December 31. Turnover and profits of the computer systems side were both well up on last year and profits in Nigeria were ahead of budget, directors stated. Earnings per share were 1.4p.
