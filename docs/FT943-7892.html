940820 FT 20 AUG 94 / Tour operator ceases trading Solflights, a holiday tour operator based in Beckenham, Kent, ceased trading yesterday. The company specialised in flights and holidays to Spain. The Civil Aviation Authority's Air Travel Organisers' Licensing (Atol) section has called in the company's Pounds 33,000 bond. About 250 travellers abroad will be able to continue their holidays and return as planned. Atol will arrange refunds for customers unable to travel.
