941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bridgend ahead Bridgend Group, the wholesale distribution and hotel company, announced a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 25,000 to Pounds 68,000 for the six months to June 30. The outcome, achieved on turnover ahead from Pounds 7.66m to Pounds 9.17m, was struck despite interest charges of Pounds 212,000 (Pounds 122,000). The interim dividend is held at 0.1p, payable from earnings of 0.11p (0.06p).
