May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

Stolen Violins Recovered: San Francisco police recovered a Stradivarius and another violin, with a total value of more than $2 million, that were snatched in a daring burglary at the home of former Soviet ballerina Natalia Makarova.

Her husband, Edward Karkar, said the thief "obviously" was not aware of the violins' value. Police arrested Robert Fisher, 27, of San Francisco, in connection with the robbery. Makarova recently retired from the American Ballet Theatre. GREG BRAXTON
