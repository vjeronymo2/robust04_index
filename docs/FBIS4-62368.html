BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 14 -- The Russian family, its needs and interests are priorities in the social and economic policy of the Russian state, according to President Boris Yeltsin. He stated this in a message to the nation on the occasion Of International Family day which is marked on Sunday. The president called on all bodies of state authority, public movements and religious confessions of the country to protect the family from armed conflicts, ecological and technological disasters. "The family is the mainstay of public stability, peace and progress. In it future citizens of the state are born and brought up," the message says.
