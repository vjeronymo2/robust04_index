BFN [Text] Yerevan, 18 May (SNARK)--Levon Zurabyan, acting press-secretary of the Armenian president, declared today that the sides of the Karabakh conflict have never been so close to peace as they are now. "The road to the armistice is difficult, but the negotiations are going on and Armenia hopes that the Azerbaijani leadership will display common sense and sign the agreement," said Mr. Zurabyan at a briefing in Yerevan. He also declared that Armenia welcomes and highly estimates the readiness of Russia "to undertake the burden of peace-keeping forces' deployment in the region."
