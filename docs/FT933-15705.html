930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: German prosecutor to be sacked in row over shooting German federal prosecutor Alexander von Stahl is to be sacked for his role in a dispute over the shooting of a suspected urban guerrilla, the justice ministry said. The authorities have given contradictory accounts of the shooting, in the northern town of Bad Kleinen, which forced the resignation on Sunday of interior minister Rudolf Seiters.
