September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENTS: A GUIDE TO THE BEST OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA;

FOR KIDS;

A CHILD'S WORKSHOP

NOT ONLY IS Ages + Stages in Beverly Hills a user-friendly toy store, it is a creativity center for children as well.

The clang of cymbals or the laughter of toddlers occasionally drifts out of a cozy workshop room in the rear of the store, where classes and events, such as music and movement for children 3 months to 3 years old, multimedia art and fairy-tale theater are held. Advance sign-up ($10 a class) is required for these and other classes.

Ages + Stages, 8950 W. Olympic Blvd., Beverly Hills; (213) 274-4301. KAY DIEHL
