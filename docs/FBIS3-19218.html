Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin (DPA) -- With a call for more self-confidence, the congress of the Party of Democratic Socialism [PDS] was concluded in Berlin on Sunday evening [13 March]. "We are the fresh wind from the left in this country," PDS Chairman Lothar Bisky said at the conclusion of the party congress with more than 430 delegates present. He called on the party members to support the PDS candidates for the Bundestag and European elections. Before that, the program for the Bundestag and European elections had been adopted by a vast majority of the delegates with few votes against. The PDS placed Honorary Chairman Hans Modrow first on their federal list for the European elections. A program for the European elections with a call for a change of the course of European unification that has been taken so far was also adopted.
