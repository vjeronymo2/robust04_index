Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lhasa, March 7 (XINHUA) -- A forum on medical education sponsored by the China Medical Board of New York City in the United States opened Saturday [5 March] in Lhasa. More than 70 medical workers from all over the Tibet Autonomous Region will be briefed on the modern medical examination system during the five-day forum. Dr. Sawyer, chairman of the board, attended Saturday's opening ceremony. He is on his fifth trip to Lhasa. The chairman said his board would help Tibet improve its medical education and examination system and train more medical professionals. The board last year reached an agreement with authorities in Tibet to expand the regional medical school in Lhasa to a medical college. It had already sponsored two medical forums in Lhasa.
