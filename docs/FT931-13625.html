930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / International Company News: Time Warner elects Levin as chairman TIME Warner's board yesterday elected Mr Gerald Levin as chairman of the group. The 53-year-old Mr Levin was named sole chief executive last month, following the death of Mr Steve Ross, the chairman and co-chief executive. Mr Levin, who joined Time Inc in 1972 was named president and co-chief executive of Time Warner in February 1992, replacing Mr Nick Nicholas.
