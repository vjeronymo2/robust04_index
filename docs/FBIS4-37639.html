BFN [Text] Our intelligence minister ['Ali Fallahian] has sent a telegram to His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the leader of the Islamic revolution, expressing condolences for the tragedy of the desecration and bomb explosion at Imam Reza's shrine. He declared the intention of the intelligence, military, and law enforcement forces to identify and arrest the black-hearted perpetrators as soon as possible. The telegram reads: So far, definite information has been obtained toward the apprehension of the perpetrators of the crime and is being seriously followed up. It is hoped that the mercenary criminals will be arrested and punished for their shameful deeds as soon as possible.
