940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Glaxo opens office in Rangoon for research projects Glaxo, Europe's biggest drug company, has opened an office in Rangoon to help Burmese hospitals conduct research projects. Glaxo has branches in 70 countries and is marketing its products in 150 countries with total sales amounting to Dollars 5.6bn in 1993-94.
