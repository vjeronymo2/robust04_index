BFN [Excerpt] The Central Committee of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] and its Political Bureau announce the death of the brother struggler Qa'id 'Ali Muhammad Salah, Central Committee member, first secretary of the party's organization in Lahij Governorate, who was martyred in front of his home in Dar Sa'd on Tuesday [7 June] night as a result of the indiscriminate shelling which the al-Amar gangs are launching on the innocent citizens and the safe residential areas. [passage omitted] A few days before the death of the martyr Qa'id, his friend and comrade, who has the same role and characteristics, the brave struggler Naji Muhsin, Central Committee member, first secretary of the party's organization in the Ibb Governorate, Eastern Region, fell on the same road. At an earlier time and as a result of a treacherous act which does not befit men, but befits the mercenaries of al-Ahmar gang and their agents, the struggler Hanash Thabit Sufyan, member of the party's Central Committee member, was martyred.
