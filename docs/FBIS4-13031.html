BFN [Text] Baghdad, 17 Apr (INA) -- The political editor of the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY has denied in part and parcel the allegations published by the Zionist paper THE JERUSALEM POST of 3 April in which it claimed that contacts, which it described as "secret and important," are being held between Iraq and "Israel" through Palestinian and European parties in order to expand what it called the scope of the Middle East peace process. The INA political editor commented: The objective of propagating such lies is to tarnish the reputation of Iraq and its clear, faithful pan-Arab policy, which is not built on the basis of rotten corridor deals and narrow interests. The political editor added: Such things are useless attempts to peddle exposed lies which no longer fool anybody.
