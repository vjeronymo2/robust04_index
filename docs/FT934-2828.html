931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: TR Technology Net asset value per share of TR Technology rose by 55 per cent to 266.38p at end-October 1993, against 172.28p six months earlier. Group net assets expanded by 19 per cent to Pounds 263.3m. After-tax revenue for the six months was up 18 per cent to Pounds 1.94m, against Pounds 1.64m, giving earnings per share of 2.15p (1.47p).
