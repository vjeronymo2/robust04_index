BFN [Text] Warsaw, 20 Apr--The Program Council of Polish Television S.A. [joint-stock company] was formed on 20 April. Jozef Zych, deputy Sejm speaker and Polish Peasant Party [PSL] deputy, was elected chairman of the council. Zbigniew Niezgoda, head of the Office of the Polish Television Board S.A., said that film director Krzysztof Zanussi and defense lawyer and scriptwriter Krzysztof Piesiewicz were elected deputy chairmen. Other members of the program council are Maria Braun-Galkowska, Lublin Catholic University professor; Maciej Englert, film director; Ryszard Jarzembowski, the Democratic Left Alliance [SLD] senator; Krzysztof Krol, the Confederation for an Independent Poland deputy; Jacek Kurczewski, Warsaw University professor; Zdobyslaw Milewski, Democratic Union deputy; Jan Sek, PSL senator; Adam Struzik, PSL senator and senate speaker; Ryszard Ulicki, SLD deputy; Andrzej Urbanczyk, SLD deputy; Marek Wielgus, the Non-Party Bloc in Support of Reforms deputy; and Krzysztof Wolicki, journalist.
