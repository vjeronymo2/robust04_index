BFN [Text] On Monday [23 May] the Ukrainian parliamentary delegation to the talks with the Crimean parliament held a session in private in Kiev. The head of the delegation Boris Oleynik refused to disclose the approach of Kiev to the negotiations. "I have gone through 25 different conflicts and know what these comments lead to," he told INTERFAX-Ukraine. Oleynik, a prominent poet, before the collapse of the Soviet Union was deputy chairman of the House of Nationalities of the USSR Supreme Soviet. He said he also banned commentaries on the situation by all members of his delegation. So far it is only known that the final list of delegates will be announced late on Monday evening. The Crimean delegation is expected to arrive in Kiev at about 1100 on Tuesday. The same day it intends to return to Simferopol.
