920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Control Techniques disposal CONTROL Techniques, the electronic drives and controls group, yesterday announced the sale of its loss-making process control division for Pounds 350,000 to Coreci of France. This would allow it to concentrate on its automation and variable speed drive business. As part of the sale, Coreci will also take on Pounds 1.2m of third party debt. In the year to September 30 1991 the division incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 480,000; its net assets were Pounds 992,000. Control Techniques also announced its results for the half year to March 31 1992, showing pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 1.1m to Pounds 1.57m. The group, 29.9 per cent owned by Emerson of the US, increased turnover to Pounds 43.2m (Pounds 29.9m). Earnings per share were unchanged at 2.3p and the interim dividend stays at 2.15p.
