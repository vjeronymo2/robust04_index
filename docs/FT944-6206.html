941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their budget): The headteacher David Levin, 45: Headmaster, the Royal Grammar School, High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire. Politics: Would rather not say. Wanted: Spending be held at forecast levels so that interest rates remain stable and long-term industrial investment rises. Said yesterday: He was pleased the education budget was not cut, but disappointed new funds are not to be earmarked specifically for nursery education, 'which ought to be a critical growth area'. Also worried at how little was being done to tackle youth unemployment. 'We have to do more to tackle the alarming problems that school and college leavers are having getting worthwhile work.'
