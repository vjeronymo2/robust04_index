June 23, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

250 ARRESTED AT ABORTION CLINIC

The Los Angeles Police Department really showed its stuff in putting down the anti-abortion protesters on June 10. Yes, the police really showed that they can hand it out to those men, women and children by breaking an arm here and stepping on a head there. I'm not sure that it demonstrated the "right stuff," but it proved that LAPD can make the streets of Los Angeles safe from those who pose such a threat to society by peacefully protesting abortion. Too bad LAPD can't make our streets safe from drug dealers and gangs.

ROBERT WALTON

Sylmar
