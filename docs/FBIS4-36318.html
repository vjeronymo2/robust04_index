BFN [Text] At 1215 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Kamaluddin Ahmed, India's minister of state for trade, and his delegation. Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahim al-Subay'i, minister of state for planning affairs, and the Indian ambassador in Damascus attended. The Indian minister conveyed to the president a message from Indian Prime Minister Narasimha Rao and greetings from Indian President Shankar Dayal Sharma. The president asked the Indian minister to convey greetings to the Indian president and prime minister and to the Indian people. Discussion during the meeting revolved around bilateral ties and the means to improve them. Discussions also touched on issues related to the situation in the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and the Nonaligned Movement.
