April 9, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

PADRE HOME GUIDE

Home park: San Diego Jack Murphy Stadium

Location: 9449 Friars Road, San Diego, 92108.

Phone numbers: 619-283-7294 or 619-283-4494 (weekends and game nights).

Seating capacity: 59,022

Year completed: 1967

1989 attendance: 2,009,032

Number of home dates: 80

Average: 25,112

Largest crowd, 1989: 52,883 (July 15 vs. Chicago)

Record crowd, regular season: 54,732 (June 7, 1986 vs. Atlanta)

Home run fences: Left and right fields 330 feet; left- and right-center 378; center 408.

Ticket office hours: Gate C -- Monday through Friday, 9 a.m.-5 p.m.; 9 a.m. until start of game when team is in town. Advance ticket window: Gate C -- Monday through Saturday, 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.; 9 a.m. until after game when the team is in town. Tickets are also available at Teleseat locations. There are 12 of them around San Diego County, and there is a service charge of $1.75 per ticket.

Telephone orders: Call Teleseat at (619) 283-SEAT. Tickets can be charged on Visa or MasterCard. Tickets ordered seven days or more before the game will be mailed. Tickets ordered after that time will be available on game day at the Will Call window at Gate E two hours before game time. Teleseat phone lines are open 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. Monday through Friday and from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday.

Mail orders: Orders must be received at least before the game. Include $3 per order for postage and handling. Mail orders to San Diego Padres, P.O. Box 2000, San Diego, 92120. Gift certificates also are available by mail and at the ticket office.

Ticket prices:

Field seating $9.50

Plaza seating $9.50

Press seating $9.50

Loge seating $8.00

Upper reserved seating $8.00

General admission* $4.00

* General admission tickets are sold only on the day of the game, beginning at 9 a.m. at Window C or at Gate B two hours before game time.

Parking: $3
