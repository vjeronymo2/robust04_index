940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / People: Finance moves David Godfrey, whose job as head of merchant banking at Swiss Bank Corporation disappeared during a sweeping reorganisation of SBC's corporate finance business, has not taken long to find another plum job. Having left SBC at the end of June, he resurfaced at JP Morgan this week as a managing director with responsibility for global credit business in the Middle East, India, Africa, eastern Europe and the former Soviet Union. Godfrey, 43, will focus on risk management and marketing of JP Morgan's emerging market credit business. He will report to Adam Wethered, JP Morgan's European head of global credit.
