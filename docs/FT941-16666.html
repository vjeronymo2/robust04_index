940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmeg and mace prices were firmer, reflecting new advisory prices issued by AEPA, the Indonesian exporters association, reports Man Producten. Spot europe bwps were quoted at USDollars 1,450 a tonne, shrivels at Dollars 1,700, abcds at Dollars 1,800 and mace at Dollars 3,000. Shipments were Dollars 100/150 higher. Grenadan nutmeg prices were unchanged. Indonesian cassia values were easier with ka/va sticks at Dollars 2,300 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 2,150, shipment, and kb br/cl Dollars 1,600, spot, and Dollars 1,475 shipment. There was discounting on later shipments. Madagascan cinnamon was on offer at FFr5.70 a kilogram and Seychelles at Dollars 1,400, cif. Pimento prices were weaker. Mexican spot was at Dollars 2,000 a tonne and shipment between Dollars 1,700 and Dollars 1,750, fob Mexico. Jamaican pimento was at Dollars 2,300 a rtonne, spot, and Dollars 2,150, cif, shipment.
