BFN [Excerpts] Cajnice, 25 Apr (TANJUG) -- The cease-fire on the Gorazde front is today being violated only by Muslim snipers firing from the town center in a vain attempt to provoke the Serbian army, it has been announced in the command of the Herzegovina Corps [of the Bosnian Serb forces]. The command says that, in line with the Karadzic-Akashi agreement, the Serbian forces are today continuing to withdraw to 3 km from the center of Gorazde, while heavy weapons have been withdrawn to the agreed distance of 20 km. [passage omitted] Serbian military sources say that scattered Muslim forces are withdrawing from the center of Gorazde and regrouping in Orahovica, northwest of the town, probably planning to link up with their forces on Mount Igman near Sarajevo.
