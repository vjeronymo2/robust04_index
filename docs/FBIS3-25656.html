Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] The Organization of African Unity [OAU] will not send troops to monitor the situation in South Africa. This was said by the secretary general of the OAU, Dr. Salim Ahmed Salim, after a meeting with Foreign Affairs Minister Pik Botha in Pretoria. Mr. Salim said that peace could be brought about only by South Africans. He said that South Africa was part of the African family of nations, and that it would be welcomed as a member of the OAU.
