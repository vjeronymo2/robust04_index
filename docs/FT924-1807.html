921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Midlands Radio lower after exceptional cost After exceptional restructuring costs of Pounds 277,000 compared with Pounds 35,000, Midlands Radio's pre-tax profits showed a marginal decline in the year to September 30. On turnover 3 per cent up at Pounds 10.4m, operating profits, after regulatory costs, advanced 24 per cent to Pounds 1.28m. Pre-tax profits, however, fell by just Pounds 8,000 to Pounds 679,000. Earnings per share improved slightly, from 3.3p to 3.35p, and the proposed final dividend is maintained at 1p for an unchanged total of 2.5p. Mr A Parkinson, chairman, reiterated that an approach had been made to the board which might or might not result in an offer for the company. He said that discussions were continuing.
