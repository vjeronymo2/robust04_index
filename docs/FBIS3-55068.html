Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] SNARK reported that the deputy prime ministers of Armenia, Georgia, and Azerbaijan, Vigen Chitechyan, Avtandil Margiani, and Abbas Abbasov, are meeting today at the Red Bridge on the Georgian-Azerbaijani border. Gagik Guliashvilli, the head of the Georgian [word indistinct] Department, told SNARK's reporter that the leaders of Georgia's Gardabanskiy and Marneulskiy Rayons and Azerbaijan's Kazakhskiy Rayon will also take part in the meeting. It is assumed that the subject of the talks is protecting the gas pipeline entering Armenia. The Armenian Government Press Service reported that no gas has entered Armenia since the last days of December.
