BFN [Text] Certain media have recently been disseminating reports that a significant increase in retail prices and tariffs for consumer goods and services is planned for May and June of this year. Additionally, it has been insinuated that the Economy Ministry has already drafted a resolution, the adoption of which will lead to a new wave of inflation and further deterioration of the people's living standards. The press service of the Economy Ministry states that the ministry is not working on any plans to increase retail prices or tariffs.
