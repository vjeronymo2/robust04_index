July 6, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

LA MESA TICKET WINS $10.5 MILLION

One lucky player picked all six numbers in the midweek Lotto 6-49 drawing to win the $10.5-million jackpot, state lottery officials said today.

The winning ticket was purchased in La Mesa in San Diego County.

In the five-of-six plus bonus category, seven players each won $213,686.
