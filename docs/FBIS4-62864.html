BFN [Text] Baku, 18 May (AZERTAC)--A comparative lull is maintained in the zone of hostilities, the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry reported today. However, the Azeri Army's positions have come under the mortar and tank fire in some sectors of the front. Thus the village of Sadarak in the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic was shelled last night. One soldier was injured. According to the Defence Ministry, the Azerbaijani Army has not yet opened retaliatory fire, thus proposing the Armenians to observe the truce.
