BFN [Text] Ankara, March 31 (AFP)--Four Turkish ministers symbolically handed their resignation to Prime Minister Tansu Ciller late Wednesday [31 March] after failing to win election as mayors in municipal elections Sunday, a government source said. In a statement, Ciller thanked the four, all members of her True Path Party, for their "gesture" but said she had no intention of reshuffling the cabinet at the moment.
