BFN [Text] Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department's special coordinator on the Cyprus issue, said the United States will continue to work hard toward both sides in Cyprus accepting the confidence-building measures. In a statement made this morning before leaving for Athens and Ankara, Lamb said significant progress has been made. He added that just a few problems remain, but he believes they can be resolved. Asked whether responsibilities will be attributed for the deadlock, Lamb said the problem is not responsibility or which side will receive more advantages over the other. He said: The issue is to break the deadlock and find a solution.
