920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / Mortgage rescue scheme launched NORTHERN ROCK Building Society yesterday launched a Pounds 5m mortgage rescue scheme with Paddington Churches Housing Association, David Barchard writes. The scheme is the first phase of a Pounds 20m mortgage rescue package aimed at helping up to 100 customers in the south-east of England who are in serious arrears. Northern Rock will buy the property from the borrower, who will become its tenant, and the housing association will manage the tenancy.
