940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Sherwood Computer denies possible offer Sherwood Computer Services, the USM-quoted financial software company, said it had noted the recent rise in the share price following the announcement of its 1993 results last week. The directors said that, contrary to certain recent press speculation, they were not engaged in any discussions concerning a possible offer for Sherwood. The company incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.67m, after exceptional charges of Pounds 1.86m, last year. Yesterday the shares closed down 5p at 110p.
