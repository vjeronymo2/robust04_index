920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / Election 92: Brown to stand against Labour MR Ron Brown, deselected by the Labour party in his Scottish constituency, is to stand against the official party candidate in the general election. Mr Brown, who represents Leith, said he had 'tried by all reasonable means including legal action to have this undemocratic decision reversed, but to no avail'. At the 1987 general election he had a majority of more than 11,000.
