June 19, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: ANAHEIM;

DRAIN, RECREATION PLANS FOR DEER CANYON STUDIED

Representatives from the city engineer's office will attend a special meeting to discuss proposed improvements to Deer Canyon on June 28, from 7 to 10 p.m. at Canyon Hills Library, 400 Scout Trail.

Topics will include information on a proposed storm drain and recreational improvements to Deer Canyon.

The meeting is sponsored by the city Parks, Recreation and Community Services Department. For information, contact Chris Jarvi, the department's director, at 999-5160.
