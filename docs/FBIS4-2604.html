BFN [Text] Beijing, April 11 (XINHUA) -- General Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), held talks with General M.P. Kolesnikov, chief of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Russia, here this afternoon. During the talks, they exchanged views on relations between the armed forces of China and Russia. Kolesnikov and his party arrived here this morning as Zhang's guests. They are scheduled to visit Shanghai tomorrow.
