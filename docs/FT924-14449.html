921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Chepstow R'course advances 27% Chepstow Racecourse raised turnover and profits by 27 per cent in the six months to June 30. The pre-tax result advanced from Pounds 32,694 to Pounds 41,599 on sales of Pounds 917,595. Earnings per share came through at 6.3p (4.9p).
