920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Fledgeling assets edge ahead The Fleming Fledgeling Investment Trust, which invests in smaller companies, primarily in the UK and US, for capital growth, reported a net asset value of 260.4p per share as at June 30, against 254.7p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to end-June amounted to Pounds 274,000 (Pounds 152,000) for earnings of 2.18p (1.21p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
