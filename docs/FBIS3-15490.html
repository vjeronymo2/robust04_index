Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Startsev] [Text] Trieste (Friuli-Venezia Giulia region) March 5 Tass -- Thorvald Stoltenberg, coordination committee co-chairman of the international conference on former Yugoslavia, called efforts by Russia, the United States and the European Union to settle the Bosnian crisis "a real chance to reach lasting peace". He stressed in an exclusive interview with ITAR-TASS on Saturday [5 March] that the initiatives by Moscow and Washington have become "an important step on the road to peace" in the region. Attempts to resolve the Bosnian crisis were based on theoretical congectures until recently, Stoltenberg stated. The peaceful process has switched to the sphere of practical solutions. "I'm satisfied that Russia and the United States has started to move in the correct direction," he noted.
