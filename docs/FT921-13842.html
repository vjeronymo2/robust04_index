920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / US Governor Clinton denies accusations of extramarital affairs Governor Bill Clinton of Arkansas, the early front-runner for the Democratic presidential nomination, yesterday angrily denied charges that he had extramarital affairs with several women, including a former Miss America, writes Lionel Barber from Boston. Charges of infidelity surfaced in a US tabloid magazine this week. The charges stem from a 1990 lawsuit brought by a former aide to the governor. Mr Clinton said: 'I got into this race in the aftermath of those things, knowing that they would probably be resurfaced, but they've been exposed for the trash they are. The American people have an innate sense of fairness and I feel good about it.'
