931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Hamlet rises 32% to Pounds 2m Profits of Hamlet Group, the clothes importer which obtained a stock market listing in October, rose from Pounds 1.52m to Pounds 2m pre-tax for the half year to September 30. The 32 per cent improvement was achieved on the back of a 16 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 30.5m. Interest charges were cut to Pounds 258,000 (Pounds 324,000) but tax took Pounds 177,000 more at Pounds 682,000. Earnings per share emerged at 4.41p (3.51p). There is no interim dividend but, as referred to in the flotation prospectus, a final will be payable in September 1994. Mr John Lusher, an ex-director of Marks and Spencer, has joined the board as a non-executive director.
