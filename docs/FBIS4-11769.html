BFN [Text] This afternoon, the brother president, leader Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], received his excellency the ambassador of the People's Republic of China. The brother president Abu-'Ammar briefed him on the current events inside the occupied Palestinian territories, particularly after the Gaza massacre and the effect of these criminal acts on the peace process. Brother Abu-'Ammar also briefed him on the Israeli replies to the Palestinian demands and charged him with carrying a message to the Chinese leadership in which he asks for China's support and efforts to implement the Security Council's Resolution 904, which stipulates the provision of international protection to our people in the occupied territories.
