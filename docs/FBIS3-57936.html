Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis described the statement by [EC Commissioner] Jacques Delors as encouraging and positive for Cyprus regarding both the Cyprus issue and the application for Cyprus' accession to the European Union. In a statement to CyBC [the Cyprus Brodcasting Corporation], Mikhailidhis said he hoped Delors would also actively support his positions, especially now, when Cyprus will seek to insure a date for opening negotiations on its accession to the Community.
