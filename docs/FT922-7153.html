920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / International Company News: Bull approves IBM alliance BULL, the French computer maker, yesterday approved details of a wide-ranging accord with IBM, which will pay FFr540m (Dollars 100.9m) for 5.68 per cent of the state-controlled company, writes William Dawkins in Paris. The groups will put into effect their technology, research and commercial alliance from next month.
