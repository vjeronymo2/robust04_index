July 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

KKGO FORMAT REVISION

The conversion of KKGO to a classical format will have no impact on the jazz listener; KKGO ceased to be a jazz station some time ago.

One waits in vain for Charlie Parker, Art Tatum, Lester Young, etc., only to have to wade through endless recordings of synthesizers and of saxophone players all playing the same solos.

KLON is the only jazz radio station in the L.A. area, and it's a blessing for lovers of the art.

DAVID M. SHERR

Santa Monica
