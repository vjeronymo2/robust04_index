BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 20 Apr -- Last night a group of Tajik opposition militants attempted to break through from Afghanistan to Tajikistan in one of the sectors covered by the Pyandzh border guards. In the course of a short armed clash between the militants and the Russian border guards, one of the transgressors was killed and the rest returned to the Afghan bank of the Pyandzh. All was quiet last night on all the other areas of the Tajik-Afghan border. This information was given to an ITAR-TASS correspondent at the headquarters of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan.
