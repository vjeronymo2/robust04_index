BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 6 -- Vietnamese Ambassador to Thailand Le Cong Phung on Wednesday reiterated Vietnam's support for the ASEAN's initiative to held the ASEAN Regional Forum (ARF) to create a favourable atmosphere for regional development and cooperation. After stresssing on the timeliness and vitality of the initiative the ambassdor said that Vietnam over the past years constantly pursued its comprehensive renovation policy, a priority of which was given to expanding international cooperation, and that Vietnam always wished that foreign investors and entrepreneurs would further promote their investment and business in Vietnam. The forum is expected to be held in Bangkok in July.
