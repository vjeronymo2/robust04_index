August 19, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PURCHASING RAILROAD RIGHTS OF WAY FOR TRANSIT

Hooray for The Times! I left Los Angeles last year because of the intolerable traffic and smog. Thanks to the Santa Fe and Southern Pacific, in five years Los Angeles can once again have a fast and affordable alternative to the region's hopelessly congested streets and freeways. For those Angelenos who doubt the efficacy of light rail, come to San Diego and ride our fast and quiet trolleys. Don't miss this chance, L.A. -- it may be your last.

VICTOR NEIBURG

Vista
