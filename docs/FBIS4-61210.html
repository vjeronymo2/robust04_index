BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 4 TASS -- The second stage of the signing of the Social Concord Agreement is to take place in the Kremlin on Wednesday. This time signatures will be attached to the document by representatives from industrial, banking, and business associations. ITAR-TASS was told by Sergey Filatov, head of the presidential administration, that more than 200 public organisations have expressed their readiness to sign the document. The Social Concord Agreement stipulates basic guarantees for social stability, the use of conciliation procedures for amending the Constitution, a commitment not to initiate political campaigns calling for preterm elections, a ban on the solution of conflicts by forcible methods. The first stage of the signing procedure took place on April 28. Signatures were then attached to the document by leaders of the executive and legislative branches of power, subjects of the Federation, representatives of political, public and religious organisations. According to Sergey Filatov, the Social Concord Agreement "has positively influenced the general climate in our society, and has clearly revealed the urge of the Russian people for peace and tranquility". Filatov is convinced that the Social Concord Agreement will allow the nation to avoid political upheavals and to create normal conditions for the implementation of the economic reforms.
