BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias raised the Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] issue last night at the working dinner of the European Union [EU] foreign ministers, which was held at the same time as the dinner of the European Council. The issue was raised within the framework of discussions of joint foreign policy, particularly during the discussion of the Yugoslav issue, which was also attended by UN mediators Lord Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg. Mr. Papoulias referred to the open Vance initiative, the Greek positions, and to the Commission's court appeal against Greece. The Foreign Ministers Council will continue its work today and will discuss the prospects of Cyprus' accession to the EU. Another issue to be discussed is cooperation among the Mediterranean, Central European, and East European countries.
