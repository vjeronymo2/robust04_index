930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury back into the black SHAFTESBURY, the property group, returned to profits in the six months to March 31 with Pounds 344,000 pre-tax, against Pounds 1.27m losses. Mr Peter Levy, chairman, said the improvement resulted from the completion of the disposal of the development portfolio. The company also announced that the pre-tax loss for the year to September 30 had been restated to take account of a Pounds 616,000 increase in provisions against the cost of development properties, leaving the figure at Pounds 5.3m. Net revenue from properties was Pounds 2.7m (Pounds 2.53m) plus surplus on disposals of Pounds 79,000 (Pounds 403,000 deficit) and other operating income of Pounds 272,000 (Pounds 46,000). Losses were 1.2p (9.1p).
