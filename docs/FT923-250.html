920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Supplying switchgear equipment NEI REYROLLE, of Hebburn, part of the Rolls-Royce industrial power group, has won an order worth Pounds 5m from the National Grid Company. The contract is for four circuits of 300kV switchgear to connect NGC's sub station at Barking in east London with a 1,000MW gas-fired power station being built nearby. This order follows a contract placed with Reyrolle earlier this year for 145kV switchgear for Barking. Equipment for the latest contract is due to be installed and commissioned by August 31 1994.
