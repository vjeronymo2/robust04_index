Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Congressional Investigating Committee on Illegal Activities (CBII), in an unexpected action, yesterday raided the Paraguayan Cotton Chamber (Cadelpa) and seized a great quantity of documents (acts of the last few years, and 15 recorded hours of sessions) that bring to light the oligopolistic procedures used by that organization to impose a national cotton price on the country's peasants, as well as control the administration of all stages of the production and marketing of "white gold."
