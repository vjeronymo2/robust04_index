Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Senegalese Prime Minister Habib Thiam received a DPRK Foreign Ministry delegation led by Vice Minister Kim Yong-il on 24 March. During the meeting, the prime minister said he hopes the DPRK will continue to prosper under the wise leadership of the great leader His Excellency President Kim Il-song. He said he hopes Korea's reunification will be realized in a peaceful way through the strength of the Korean people themselves, and that he supports the method of Korea's reunification through the confederal system. He pointed out that the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula cannot be resolved by applying pressure or threats, and that he hopes this issue can be resolved through DPRK-U.S. dialogue. He expressed satisfaction over the fact that the friendly relations between the two countries are developing well. The Senegalese African economic integration minister, other functionaries concerned, and DPRK ambassador to Senegal were on hand.
