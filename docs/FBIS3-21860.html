Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Car Seized at Gulbai Chaurangi; Forty Kilogram Heroin Recovered"] [Text] Karachi (Staff Reporter) -- Mohammed Umar Moryo, regional director of the Pakistan Narcotics Control Board, sent a team composed of Inspectors Jahangir Khan and Ghulam Mohammed Sheikh to seize a car after receiving some information. This team stopped a car, tag number W-1807, near Ghulbai Chaurangi on Maripur Road and searched it. They recovered 40 kilograms of high grade heroin and arrested Khalid Khan Afredi who was riding in the car. The accused is a resident of Khyber Agency and is reportedly a member of an international drug smuggling gang. Further investigations are going on.
