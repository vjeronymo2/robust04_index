Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Dec. 30 (MAP) -- An authorized source at the elections and census department said the Ministry of Interior was not notified as to any decision to cancel the result of elections in any constituency and was not informed by any side of the content of a ruling made by the constitutional chamber. The press service at the elections and census department was reacting to some news reports that parliamentary by-elections should be held in some constituencies of which the judiciary authorities annulled the results. In this regard, the source underlines the government's commitment to organize in due time by-elections as soon as it receives judiciary decisions from the Supreme Court's constitutional chamber.
