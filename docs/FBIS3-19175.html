Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Sixteen members of a separatist terror organization, who escaped into Iran during an operation launched by the security forces on Agri Mountain, were returned to Turkey by Iran yesterday. Iranian officials handed over 15 separatist bandits -- five of them injured, and the body of one who was killed -- to Turkish officials at the Gurbulak border crossing yesterday. The Igdir governor said that talks with Iranian officials are continuing and that more might be returned. A group of separatist terrorists escaped into Iranian territory during an operation conducted by the security forces on Agri Mountain.
