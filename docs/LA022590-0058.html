February 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: PASADENA OUTDOOR ART CLASS TO PAINT BARRICADE

Pasadena City College will offer an outdoor art course, "Art 130 -- Art in Public Places," directed by outdoor muralist Kent Twitchell.

Students will meet one day a week to paint 3,000 square feet of a construction barricade along Colorado Boulevard in Old Pasadena surrounding the One Colorado project. Information on the course is available at (818) 578-7238.
