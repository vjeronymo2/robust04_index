940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / Ukrainian farm credits approved Ukraine's parliament approved a new credits to the state farm sector which are expected to add 20-25 percentage points to inflation, and could bring a renewed cycle of hyperinflation by summer's end, according to reform economist Mr Victor Pynzenyk's. The government called the support, 'the only hope for rescuing agriculture,' which employs a third of the nation's labour force.
