BFN [By reporter Li Xiongxiong; from the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Text] A national forum on cracking down on counterfeit goods opened in Hangzhou today. According to information revealed at the meeting, the State Economic and Trade Commission, the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, the State Bureau of Technological Supervision, as well as public security, supervision, and judicial organs are currently making arrangements for a new offensive against counterfeiting. Three major campaigns will be unfolded across the nation in June, July, and August to smash counterfeiting dens, investigate major cases, and rectify the market. The national office for cracking down on counterfeiting has listed 10 major cases to be intensively investigated and dealt with in 1994. Counterfeiting dens that need to be smashed as well as counterfeit and shoddy goods markets to be rectified and banned have been determined as well. At the national forum, delegates from a dozen or so provinces and municipalities will hold discussions on the specifics in conducting the three major campaigns against counterfeiting.
