USE ONLY 944Q0170A Moscow DIPLOMATICHESKIY VESTNIK Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed report under the rubric: "Diplomacy, Science, Public: Meeting of the Foreign Policy Council under the Russian Federation Ministry of Foreign Affairs: The New Situation in East Europe and Russia's Foreign Policy"] [Text] This pro blem was the topic of discussion at the scheduled meeting of the Foreign Policy Council under the Russian Federation MFA [Ministry of Foreign Affairs], which took place on 3 November. Minister of Foreign Affairs A.V. Kozyrev chaired the meeting, which was conducted by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs S.B. Krylov. Among the participants in the council's work were noted scholars on international affairs, political scientists, representatives from business circles and the Russian Orthodox Church, journalists, and diplomats. The meeting participants analyzed from a conceptual and practical angle the fundamental approaches in Russia's foreign policy toward countries of the East European region. The tone of the meeting was set by A.V. Kozyrev's speech, which we publish below.
