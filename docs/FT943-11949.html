940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / Bombing incidents ---------------------------------------------------------------------- 26.07.94. Car bomb explodes behind consular section of Israeli embassy in Kensington High St., London, injuring 13, one seriously. 19.07.94. Twenty-one killed, mostly Jewish-Panamanian business leaders, when a commuter plane on a short flight from Colon to Panama City exploded and crashed. The President-elect of Panama said later a bomb was responsible. 18.07.94. Bomb in van outside main Jewish centre in Buenos Aires leaves 96 dead and others missing. 17.03.94. Thai police discover a huge bomb in an abandoned truck in what is believed to be a bungled attack on the Israeli embassy in Bangkok. ----------------------------------------------------------------------
