Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 2 (KPL) -- The labour and social welfare service of Vientiane Prefecture, on February 28, received from Thai Nongkhai authority 92 Lao families who formerly lived in Thai refugee camps. The handing-over ceremony of 307 refugees, the first group in 1994, at Hatsaifong District administrative office was attended by all sides concerned including a representative of the UNHCR here, Mr. Hiromi Suzuki. There are still about 20,000 Lao refugees living in Thai camps who will return home within this programme.
