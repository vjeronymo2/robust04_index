Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Arab Maghreb Union [AMU] Secretariat General has issued the following communique: The council of foreign ministers of the member-states of AMU at its 14th session, held in Tunis on 3-4 February 1994, looked into the request of some member-states for the postponement of the convening of the sixth session of the presidential council of the union, which was scheduled for this week. Agreement was reached among the member-states of the union that the sixth session of the presidential council will be held in Tunis at a future date to be fixed through consultation between His Excellency President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and his brothers the leaders of the member-states of the union.
