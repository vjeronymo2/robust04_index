930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: Fidelity adds seven currencies - At a glance FIDELITY has added seven currencies to its Bermuda-based range of currency funds. Now available are: Belgian francs, Danish kroner, Finnish marks, Norwegian kroner, Portuguese escudos, Singapore dollars and Swedish kroner. The funds have no initial charge and no minimum investment; the annual charge is 1 per cent.
