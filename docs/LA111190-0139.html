November 11, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Ventura 29, L.A. Harbor 26 -- Aaron Clark kicked a 31-yard field goal with six seconds remaining for Ventura's third WSC win in a row after losing its first six games.

L. A. Harbor had tied the score, 26-26, with 13:11 remaining on a 25-yard pass from Bill Inman to Vinny Bajazevich. Tim Albrent completed 15 of 23 for 124 yards to lead the Pirates. Bill Harris paced the Seahawks with 142 yards in 27 carries. LES HERNDON
