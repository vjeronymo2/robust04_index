BFN [Text] Stepanakert, 26 April (SNARK)--Trying to extend the combat line, the Azerbaijani side undertook an offensive today in the south-eastern direction. As SNARK was told in the press bureau of Karabakh Defense Army, nearly at 0600 26 April, the Azeri troops supported by armoured vehicles attacked the positions of Karabakh forces in the south of Martuninskiy region, as well as in the Fizulinskiy section.
