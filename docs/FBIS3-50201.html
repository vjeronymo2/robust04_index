Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Prague, Jan 23 (CTK) -- Czech President Vaclav Havel said today he would like to see the issue of property restitution resolved as soon as possible, adding he was slightly nervous about the protracted negotiations on it. In his regular radio program "Talks From Lany," he said all the discussions about the return of property to the Jewish communities were sometimes "humiliating." He added he was convinced that the nearest parliament session would pass a reasonable law providing for the rectification of the wrongs done to "our Jewish fellow-citizens." The issue of restitutions concerns churches, Jewish communities and most lately also the gentry. [passage omitted]
