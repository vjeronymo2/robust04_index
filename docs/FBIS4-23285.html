BFN [Text] Guidelines for a new training strategy for South Africa have been published by the Department of Manpower. Earlier, a task group comprising representatives of the National Training Board, employers, employee organizations, education and training institutions, and the state was appointed by the Minister of Manpower to investigate the issue. The questions investigated includes the training of educators, basic adult education, and legislation. The report is available from the state printer and reaction to it must be submitted to the secretary of the National Training Board at Private Bag X117, Pretoria, before the 20th of this month.
