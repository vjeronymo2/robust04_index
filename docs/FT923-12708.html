920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Bananas are this weeks best fruit buy at 45-55p a lb (40-60p), reports the FFVIB. Peachs at 20-25p each (15-20p) , Nectarines at 18-28p each (25-35p), and kiwifruit at 16-20p each (16-20p), are also good fruit buys. This weekend will see the first flush of Runner beans at 99p-Pounds 1.20 a lb (99p-Pounds 1.20), while English broccoli is superb at 70-80p a lb (80-80p), along with English Hispi cabbage at 25-30p a lb (25-30p). The best salad buy this week are tomatoes at 35-50p a lb (40-50p). Other good salad buys are English Iceburg at 45-60p each (50-65p) and English and Dutch cucumbers at 30-50p each (40-50p).
