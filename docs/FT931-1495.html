930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Airbuses for Uzbekistan Uzbekistan Airways ordered two Airbus Industrie A310-300s valued at an estimated Dollars 156m (Pounds 110m) and will use the long-range wide-body aircraft on foreign routes, Airbus said, Reuter reports from Paris. The airline, which acquired most of its fleet from former Soviet carrier Aeroflot, will use the A310s in a three-class, 192-seat layout on routes from Tashkent to London, Frankfurt, Beijing, Kuala Lumpur, Tel Aviv, Jeddah and Istanbul.
