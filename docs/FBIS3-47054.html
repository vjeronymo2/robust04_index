Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 6 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The information released by the press office of the Mongolian Government, concerning the first sitting of the cabinet of ministers in the new year provides with only a list of the issues considered. Proceeding from this information, the government has discussed the draft laws on making changes and amendments to the package of laws on social insurance, the progress of the governmental projects for 1993-1996, report by the minister of food and agriculture on a purveyance of meat the state of providing the population with meat stuffs in the last year, totally 10 issues [sentence as received]. However the press office of the government has provided any concrete facts and data on none of these issues. By the end of the last year the government decided not to allow journalists to observe the government's meetings and the function on covering these meetings to shift on to its press office.
