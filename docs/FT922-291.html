920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Fizz goes out of bubbly UK champagne prices are at their lowest in years as sales fall and producers try to offload stockpiles. The Champagne Bureau, the industry's UK office, said just over 3m bottles were shipped to the UK by the end of May, about 20 per cent below 'dismal' figures for the same period last year.
