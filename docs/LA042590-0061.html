April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Keith McCants underwent arthroscopic knee surgery, and Tampa Bay Coach Ray Perkins said the procedure reassured him it was not a risk to take the linebacker with the fourth pick in the NFL draft.

Perkins said Dr. William Carson, the Buccaneer orthopedic consultant, removed "a couple of bone chips" from McCants' right knee but found no cartilage or ligament damage.
