February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Ford May Build Trucks With Mazda: Ford Motor Co. said it is in talks with Mazda Motor Corp. to jointly build a new compact pickup truck. A Ford spokesman declined to give a specific production time for the vehicle, other than to say it would be in the 1990s but added that Ford favored a U.S. production site. The trade publication Automotive News, citing Ford planning documents, said the new vehicle will debut in the 1995 model year to replace the Ford Ranger.
