BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Saturday sent a message of greetings to president of the Republic of Senegal Abdou Diouf on the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the independence of Senegal. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the president and the people of Senegal great success in their work for the stability and prosperity of the country and expressed the conviction that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would continue to develop on good terms.
