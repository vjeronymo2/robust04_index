BFN [Text] Belgrade, May 31 (TANJUG) -- Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic on Tuesday conferred with Russian presidential envoy Vitaliy Churkin about the latest aspects of settling the Bosnian conflict and about the republic of Serb Krajina-Croatia relations. In a brief statement to the press, Churkin said that the first step in resolving the Bosnian conflict was an overall cessation of hostilities, which, he stressed, enjoyed strong Belgrade's support.
