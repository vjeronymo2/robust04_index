June 24, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

IMMIGRATION BATTLE

Instead of expanding the checkpoints at San Onofre, which will only further compound the problem, there is a simple, foolproof system. Relocate the Camp Pendleton Marine Base on the stretch of land that separates Mexico from the United States.

Any illegal immigrants or drugs that slip past that checkpoint deserve to be admitted.

KEN ANDERSON

Pine Valley
