930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Hillsborough victim allowed to die Hillsborough victim Tony Bland, aged 21, is likely to die from infection within two weeks, his doctors said after the House of Lords ruled that his artificial feeding can be stopped. He has been in a persistent vegetative state in Airedale General Hospital, West Yorkshire, since he was crushed at the FA Cup semi-final in April 1989.
