Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 20 Jan (AFP) -- Emisoras Caracol Network reported today, citing sources close to the Colombian Supreme Court of Justice, that this court has authorized the extradition to the United States of an Ecuadoran arrested last year in Colombia. The man under arrest is Raul Eduardo Reyes Torres, one of Ecuador's most wanted criminals. The Ecuadoran authorities accuse him of being part of the Reyes Magos cocaine clan, the radio network reported. Reyes was captured in June 1993 in the city of Cali in possession of false documents that say he is a Colombian citizen. According to the report, Reyes Torres is wanted by a Denver federal court on drug-trafficking charges.
