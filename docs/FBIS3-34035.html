Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 2 Feb (INA)--Official talks between Iraq and the UN Special Commission began here this evening. The Iraqi delegation to the talks is led by Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf. The Special Commission's delegation is led by its chairman, Rolf Ekeus. On the Iraqi side are Lieutenant General 'Amir Muhammad Rashid, director of the Military Industrialization Organization; Dr. Riyad al-Qaysi, Foreign Ministry under secretary; and a number of officials. On the Special Commission's delegation are Maurizio Zifferero, deputy chairman of the International Atomic Energy Agency, Tim Terevan [name as received], the committee spokesman; and John Scott, the committee's legal adviser. Ekeus and the accompanying delegation arrived in Baghdad at noon today on a visit of several days to Iraq.
