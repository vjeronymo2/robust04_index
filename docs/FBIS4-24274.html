BFN [Excerpts] On the occasion of the celebration of the 19th aniversary of national independence, President of the Republic Joaquim Chissano said that the issue of an eventual government of national unity has not been definitely put aside. [passage omitted] The president of the Republic also said he was not worried about his fate or that of his party in the forthcoming elections. Chissano said: I would only be very worried if there was another war in Mozambique. I am not worried about whether I will continue as the country's president. The president added that he would be worried if we failed to create a better future, which is our challenge for the future. He said the forthcoming elections should bring the results wished for by the Mozambican people.
