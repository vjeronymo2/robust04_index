BFN [Text] Baghdad, 7 Jun (INA)--Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met this evening with the visiting German parliamentary delegation, led by Dr. Hans Sterchen, chairman of the Bundestag Foreign Policy Committee. The minister discussed with the guest delegation the negative impact of the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq. The blockade, he said, continues although Iraq met the obligations stated in UN resolutions. The two sides exchanged views on several issues of mutual interest. Sterchen stressed eagerness to strengthen contacts between Iraq and Germany.
