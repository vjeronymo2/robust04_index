941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / International Company News: CSA deficit is cut to Kcs281m CSA, the Czech national airline, cut its loss to Kcs281m (Dollars 24.4m) in the first seven months of this year from a Kcs708m deficit in the same period of 1993, Reuter reports from Prague. In August, CSA reported a first-half loss of Kcs273m, of the year, against a Kcs620m loss for the same period in 1993. The Czech government is talking with several airlines about selling a 19.1 per cent share of CSA originally held by Air France and sold back to the government in March.
