April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RONALD COLMAN

I am researching a biography of Ronald Colman for Greenwood Press and I am trying to locate prints of TV shows he did for "Four Star Playhouse," "The Halls of Ivy," "G.E. Theater," "Studio 57" and "The Jack Benny Show." I am also trying to locate copies of his first and last radio shows, both for NBC: "A Tale of Two Cities" (1935) and "A Christmas Carol" (1956), plus his appearance on the NBC game show "Double or Nothing" in 1951.

If you know the whereabouts of any of these radio and TV shows, please write me.

SAM FRANK

P.O.B. 417

Los Angeles, CA 90078
