Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 28 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today that China appreciates a reconciliation accord recently signed by the leaders of Yemen. The spokesman said this when commenting on the accord that was reportedly signed. "The Chinese Government appreciates the signing of a reconciliation accord by the leaders of Yemen," the spokesman said, adding that China sincerely hopes that Yemen will enjoy stability and economic development and that its people lead a happy life.
