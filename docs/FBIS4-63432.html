BFN ["Karabakh Is Placid"--SNARK headline] [Text] Stepanakert, 21 May (SNARK) -- Military situation has not changed essentially in the Karabakh confrontation areas. As SNARK newsman was told in the NKR [Nagorno-Karabakh Republic] Information and Press Department, the relative calmness that has settled there is preserved. Confrontation parties abstain from any military activity.
