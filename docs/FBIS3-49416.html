Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Sergej Kozlik received Rem Ivanovich Vyasirev, president of the Russian company Gazprom, in Bratislava today. Mr. Vyasirev briefed the deputy prime minister on the deliveries of Russian gas for Slovakia. The two officials agreed to increase shipments of natural gas to Slovakia to 5.5 billion cubic meters this year. They have also assessed the possibilities of a long-term contract for the year 2005, which should represent the delivery of 10-11 billion cubic meters of natural gas for Slovakia.
