November 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ALSO WORTH WATCHING:

Veteran blues pianist and singer Eubie Blake teams up with hoofer Gregory Hines on The Best of Saturday Night Live (Monday at 10 p.m. on Nickelodeon).

The Back of Patty Duke's Head (Tuesday from 8-10 p.m. on Nickelodeon) features four vintage episodes of "The Patty Duke Show." Interspersed will be interviews with the woman who doubled for Duke and whom audiences only saw from the back of her head.
