May 9, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

L.A.-DUSSELDORF FLIGHTS DUE JUNE 13

Lufthansa German Airlines announced today that it will begin service between Los Angeles and Dusseldorf, West Germany, on June 13. The non-stop services will operate twice weekly and continue on to Munich -- offering Lufthansa's first Los Angeles-Munich flights.

An excursion fare of $699 will be offered between June 13 and July 13 as an introduction to the service, Lufthansa said.
