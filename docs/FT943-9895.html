940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / World Trade News: Alcatel ALCATEL, the French telecommunications group, yesterday announced an expansion of its presence in the South African market with the conclusion of a FFr210m (Dollars 39m) contract to supply a telephone transmission network to Eksom, the South African electricity board, writes John Ridding in Paris. The five-year contract, which was won by Alcatel Italia, the company's Italian subsidiary, includes the supply of medium and high capacity digital radio links and a network management centre.
