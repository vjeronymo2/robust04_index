931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / International Company News: SAB buys 80% of Hungary's largest brewer SOUTH African Breweries, the country's largest consumer goods company, yesterday announced that it was paying Dollars 50m for an 80 per cent stake in Kobanyai Sorgyar, Hungary's largest brewer, writes Nicholas Denton in Budapest. SAB is also committed to investments of Dollars 40m over four to five years at the state-owned Kobanyai Sorgyar. Kobanyai's capacity will be increased to 2.5m to 3m hectolitres of beer per year, with particular emphasis on the brewery's Dreher brand. The State Property Agency, the privatisation authority, named SAB as preferred bidder in June this year.
