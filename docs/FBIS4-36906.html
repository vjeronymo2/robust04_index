BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran has vehemently denied a report published in the French newspaper LE POINT saying that Iran has supplied our country with arms. A statement distributed by the Iranian Embassy in Sanaa asserted that Iran's principled position on the current Yemeni developments is clear and unambiguous. This position cannot be distorted by these suspect media tricks. The statement pointed out that Iran supports Yemeni unity and hopes that the existing problems will be resolved as part of the unified Yemen.
