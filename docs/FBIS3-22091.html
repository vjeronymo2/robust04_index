Language: Danish Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: `More Drug Users in Ringkobing County'] [Text] New statistics for the counties show that the number of drug users in Ringkobing County has quadrupled since 1988. Currently the county has contact with 271 needle users and users with serious mixed-use problems. In contrast, the number of drug users in Viborg County has remained comparatively stable, the radio station for the midlands and western region reported.
