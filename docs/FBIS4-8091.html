BFN [Text] Brcko, 11 Apr (TANJUG) -- Early this morning, Muslim forces launched a heavy combined mortar and infantry attack from the direction of Pejici and Pejina, seven kilometers south of Brcko. They attacked Serb positions in the areas of Ulice, Lukici, and Lipovac, Serb military sources have announced. More than 20 60-mm and 82-mm mortar shells were fired on the Serb defense lines, followed by fierce fire from infantry weapons. Members of the Serb republic army responded to the attack, after which there has been a lull in this section of the Posavlje [River Sava valley] battlefield. Last night and this morning was mainly quiet on the Gradacac and Orasje battlefronts [northern Bosnia].
