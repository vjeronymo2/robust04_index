930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / International Company News: Mellon Bank eyes disposals MELLON Bank of the US is exploring the sale of three of its information services businesses, AP-DJ reports. The operations provide data processing and management information services for more than 200 banks, trust companies, thrift institutions and mortgage companies in the US. Together, these businesses generated 1992 revenues of about Dollars 94m and employ about 940 people.
