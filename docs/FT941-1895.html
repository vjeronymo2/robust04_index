940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Trace Computers Falling interest rates, reduced borrowings and an improving market helped Trace Computers, the software specialist, increase interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 105,000 to Pounds 216,000. Turnover for the six months to November 30 was also ahead, at Pounds 9.24m (Pounds 8.84m). Earnings per share were higher at 1.22p (0.59p) and the interim dividend is again 0.55p.
