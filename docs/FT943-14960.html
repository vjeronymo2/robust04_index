940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / UK Company News in Brief: First Philippine Investment Trust FIRST PHILIPPINE Investment Trust: net asset value per share at the six months ended April 30 was 106.4p, against 65.2p a year earlier. Net loss for the period was Pounds 61,606 (Pounds 67,517) giving losses per share of 0.12p (0.14p earnings).
