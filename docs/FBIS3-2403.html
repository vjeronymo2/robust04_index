Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Video report by reporter Li Yuangao (2621 6678 7559) and correspondent Yan Xinggao (2518 5281 7559) -- from the "Regional News Hookup" program] [Text] After having satisfactorily fulfilled various tasks, a regional conference on judicial, procuratorial, and public security work ended on the afternoon of 26 February. Deputies attending the meeting held group discussions on how to improve judicial, procuratorial, and public security work to maintain social stability in Tibet under the new situation. Their dicussions revolved around Comrade Zi Cheng's report. They made valuable suggestions in light of the actual conditions of judicial, procuratorial, and public security work in their respective localities, departments, and units and in light of certain important issues raised by Comrade Zi Cheng in his report. Gyaincain Norbu, Danzim, Zi Cheng, Pudoje, Namgyai, Gyamco, Zhou Qishun, (Bai Zhao), and Zhang Zhu, leaders of the autonomous region, attended the closing ceremony. Gyamco, executive vice chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Regional People's Government and deputy secretary of the Tibet Regional Commission on Politics and Law, delivered a concluding speech. He said: The year 1994 is an extremely important one for China to deepen reform. The number of destablizing factors will continue to increase due to the readjustment of the relationship of interests and due to various reasons existing at home and abroad. We must truly carry out judicial, procuratorial, and public security work under the new situation of our effort to meet the needs of reform and opening up; we must spend time and effort on implementation; we must persist in and carry forward the fine tradition of integrating special organs with the mass line; we must catch up with the pace of our times by reforming ourselves. Chairman Gyaincain Norbu of the Tibet Autonomous Regional People's Government delivered a speech at the closing ceremony. He called on those who are engaged in judicial, procuratorial, and public security work to have a clear understanding of the situation; to seek unity of thinking; to persist in the principle of attaching equal importance to two types of work [promote reform and opening up and, at the same time, crack down on various criminal activities], and to persist in taking a tough stand on both so as to provide a stable environment for reform, opening up, and economic construction; to vigorously wage struggle against splittism in a bid to ensure stability in the whole region; to persist in the principle of dealing a harsh blow to criminals and to step up the crackdown; to further mobilize the forces from all walks of life to vigorously improve comprehensive management of public security; to successfully carry out self-construction by the contingents of personnel for judicial, procuratorial, and public security work in a down-to-earth manner; and to strengthen the party's leadership over judicial, procuratorial, and public security work.
