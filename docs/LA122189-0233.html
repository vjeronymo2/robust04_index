December 21, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

BEL RAY WINS N.J. FUTURITY BY A NECK

Bel Ray swung four-wide in the stretch and took the lead in the final stride to capture the filly division of the New Jersey Futurity on Wednesday night at the Meadowlands. The John DeStefano Jr. trainee covered the six furlongs in 1:14 on a frigid night and posted a neck victory over Jersey Fresh.
