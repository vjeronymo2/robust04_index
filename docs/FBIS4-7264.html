BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 24 (KCNA) -- The text of the brief biography of the great leader President Kim Il-song was carried by the Nepalese paper KATHMANDU POSO, the Russian paper KRASNAYA ZNAMIYA and the Indian papers WORUM, PROLETARIAN ERA, DELHI BLITZ, HIND, CULCUTTA SEKIGRAM RURAL and DELHI CONGRESS on his birthday. The Pakistani papers NIDAZ WAQT and WAFAQ carried a brief history of the revolutionary activities of President Kim Il-song.
