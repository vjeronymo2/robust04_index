July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Cruise Races to Radio: Tom Cruise has gone from the racetrack to the radio to hype his "Days of Thunder" film, which is doing less-than-zooming business at the box office.

Cruise visited KIIS-AM (1150) and KIIS-FM (102.7) morning personality Rick Dees for an hour Thursday in what was apparently his only local radio interview. The actor told Dees that he liked Maria McKee, Melissa Etheridge, the Rolling Stones, AC/DC and Technotronic's "Pump Up the Volume." Cruise also said he considers "Days of Thunder" a huge hit already. GREG BRAXTON
