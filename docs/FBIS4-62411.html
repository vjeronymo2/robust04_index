BFN [Text] Listen to the Georgian head of state's edict. An explosion organized by criminals today, 15 May, inside the Tbilisi Armenian Drama Theater -- known for its deep-rooted cultural traditions -- causing injuries of various degrees to 16 people and inflicting significant material damages, shall be regarded as a provocative terrorist act carried out by enemies of the state. 1. Georgian Prosecutor-General [Jamlet] Babilashvili, Chairman of the Georgian Security Service [Igor] Giorgadze and Georgian Minister of Interior [Shota] Kviraia shall be instructed to secure the investigation of the above-mentioned terrorist act and the revealment of the criminals. The progress of the investigation shall be reported to me systematically. 2. The Georgian Republic Cabinet of Ministers and Tbilisi Mayor's office shall set up a special commission in order to secure a prompt assessment of the damages caused by the explosion and valuation of restoration works and shall take urgent measures to provide the theater with other necessary help. [Signed] Mr Eduard Shevardnadze.
