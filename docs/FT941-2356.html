940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): Skanska - Other companies Scandinavia's biggest construction group should show a strong rebound from its disastrous 1992 results when it unveils 1993 figures on Thursday. The group will be relying on capital gains on disposals to compensate for continued recession in the Swedish construction market and big property write-downs. At the half-way stage, the group predicted a SKr2.2bn (Dollars 280m) full-year profit.
