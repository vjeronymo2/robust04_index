Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Rome, March 17 (AFP) -- Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin flew in here Thursday [17 March] from the United States for talks with Pope John Paul II on bilateral relations and the Middle East peace process. It is Rabin's first visit to the Vatican since the signing of the December 30 accord establishing diplomatic ties between the two states. The Israeli prime minister was also to confer with his Italian counterpart Carlo Azeglio Ciampi before returning to Israel Thursday in the evening. [passage omitted]
