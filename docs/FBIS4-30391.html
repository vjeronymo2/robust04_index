BFN ["Information on Expected Third Round of DPRK-USA Talks"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 27 (KCNA) -- A spokesman of the Foreign Ministry of the DPRK issued the following information today on the expected third round of DPRK-USA talks. A series of working contacts have of late taken place between the DPRK and the USA for the opening of the third round of talks. The sides agreed to begin the third round of talks on July 8, 1994, in Geneva. The talks will be held alternately at the DPRK Mission and the U.S. Mission in Geneva, the first-day session taking place at the DPRK Mission. The talks will be attended by a delegation of the DPRK led by Kang Sok-chu, first vice-minister of foreign affairs, and a delegation of the USA led by Robert L. Gallucci, assistant secretary of state in charge of political and military affairs. The talks are expected to discuss matters for a fundamental solution to the nuclear issue in a package deal and other outstanding issues between the DPRK and the USA.
