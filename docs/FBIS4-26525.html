BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 20 (XINHUA) -- Shanghai will build the country's largest container-shipping base in co-operation with a Hong Kong company. Shanghai is China's largest port and its container shipping operations have boomed in recent years. The port handled a record 937,000 standard containers last year, and the number of containers it handled in the first five months of this year was up 40 percent over the same period of last year. To meet the needs of the rapid development of container transport, the Shanghai Port Comprehensive Container Development Corporation is co-operating with Orient Overseas (Holdings) Ltd of Hong Kong to develop a 260,000-sq-m area into a container shipping base on the outskirts of the city.
