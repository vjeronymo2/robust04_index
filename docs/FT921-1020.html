920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / International Company News: BNP roars ahead to FFr2.94 bn BANQUE Nationale de Paris (BNP), the largest state-owned French bank, yesterday announced an 82 per cent increase in net profits to FFr2.94bn (Dollars 521m) in 1992 from FFr1.62bn in 1990, writes Alice Rawsthorn in Paris.
