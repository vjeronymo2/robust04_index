Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1630 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Choe Ki-chong, chairman of the DPRK Education Committee, and his accompanying delegation. The meeting was attended by Education Minister Muhammad Ghassan al-Halabi and the DPRK ambassador in Damascus. Choe Ki-chong conveyed to the president a message from DPRK President Kim Il-song.
