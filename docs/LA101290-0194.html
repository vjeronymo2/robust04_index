October 12, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

LEPTON WILL TRY FOR HER 19TH WIN

Lepton will be gunning for her 19th career victory when she takes on a field of four in the $39,862 Electioneer Futurity for sophomore pacing fillies tonight at Los Alamitos.

Also on the 12-event program is a $20,000 California Sires Stakes for 2-year-old pacing fillies.

In post position order, the field for tonight's $39,862 main attraction is as follows: Red Pepper, Joe Anderson in the race bike; Black On Black, Robert Rosen; Preciosa, Gene Vallandingham; Denali's Ginger, Rick Plano; and Lepton, Ross Croghan.
