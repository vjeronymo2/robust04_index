930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Asset value improves at Drayton Far East Drayton Far Eastern Trust had a net asset value of 107.3p per share at December 31 1992 against 96.3p a year earlier. Earnings emerged at 0.94p (0.92p) per share. An unchanged final dividend of 0.5p is proposed to maintain the total at 0.625p.
