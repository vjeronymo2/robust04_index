BFN [Text] Price hikes in the city of Guangzhou are slowing down somewhat. According to statistics, in April this year, the city's general level of retail prices and general level of wholesale prices were 17.5 and 21 percent higher respectively than the same period last year. However, compared with March this year, the margins of increase were 3.7 and 4.6 percent lower respectively. The main reason why price hikes in April slowed somewhat is that the government has adopted relevant measures to curb price increases.
