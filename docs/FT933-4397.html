930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / RUC chief rejects terror criticism SIR HUGH ANNESLEY, chief constable of the Royal Ulster Constabulary, yesterday rejected suggestions that police were not making a big enough effort to combat loyalist terrorism. His remarks followed complaints from Dr Joe Hendron, Social Democratic and Labour party MP for West Belfast, that there was not the same level of patrolling in loyalist areas of Belfast as in nationalist parts of the city.
