Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our sources have reported that more than 2,000 people staged a demonstration in Madinat al-Thawrah in Baghdad on Wednesday morning, 2 February. These sources said that the Iraqi regime's security service, intelligence units, special security units, Republican Guard, and Army personnel confronted the demonstration in the al-Jamaliyah quarter. The demonstrators wanted to march to Baghdad. These sources said that the regime's personnel used batons to stop and disperse the demonstration, killing or injuring dozens of innocent civilians.
