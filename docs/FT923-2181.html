920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Ernest Green declines to Pounds 0.46m In a year of rationalisation, Ernest Green and Partners Holdings experienced a sharp fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.15m to Pounds 458,000 on sales down from Pounds 11.7m to Pounds 7.74m. The USM-quoted structural and civil engineering consultancy took exceptional costs of Pounds 304,000 (Pounds 152,000) representing redundancy payments. The dividend for the year is unchanged at 7p, including a maintained final of 4.25p. Earnings per share at 4.3p (18.1p) exclude an extraordinary profit of Pounds 133,000 on the sale of the company's freehold premises at Sydenham, south London.
