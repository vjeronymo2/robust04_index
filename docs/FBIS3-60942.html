Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Badr al-Alfi] [Excerpt] During the extensive police hunt for outlaws, especially those involved in black terrorism, the Cairo Intelligence Directorate discovered two factories that produce various types of firearms in al-Sharabiyah, one of the hotbeds of crime in the capital. Interior Minister Major General Hasan al-Alfi authorized generous rewards for the officers involved and the citizens who gave information about the two factories. Twenty rifles and revolvers, as well as weapons parts, and assembling machines were found. The owners confessed to Major General Mansur 'Isawi, assistant interior minister for Cairo security, and Major General Mahmud Wajdi, director of the General Intelligence Directorate, that they sold weapons to outlaws at very high prices. The prosecution ordered that they be remanded and the two factories closed. [passage omitted]
