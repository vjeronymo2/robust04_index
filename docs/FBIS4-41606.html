BFN [Text] Decree by the head of state of Georgia: On measures for preparing a Russian-Georgian conference on the issues of economic cooperation and investment, and with the aim of accomplishing economic agreements signed between Russia and Georgia, a Russian-Georgian conference will be held on 21-24 July of the current year in Tbilisi. The following should be done to this end: 1. The management of ministries and departments of the Republic of Georgia, with the help of enterprises subordinate to them, are to guarantee, by 30 June, the preparation of data of those draft investments which are of mutual interest for Georgian-Russian cooperation. They are also to present suggestions as to which representatives of Russian enterprises will be invited to the conference. 2. Carrying out of the decree is to be monitored by Georgian Deputy Prime Minister Zurab Kervalishvili.
