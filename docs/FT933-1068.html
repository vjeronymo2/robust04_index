930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper prices moved lower this week, reports Man Producten. Indonesia reduced the price for Lampong Asta black pepper to a level that could compete in New York with Indian MG1. Meanwhile Malabar reinstalled the export tax of 2 per cent as from October 1. White pepper prices stabilised at lower levels in a very thin market. White: Muntok/Sarawak faq for September/October delivery was quoted at USDollars 3,200 a tonne and spot at Dollars 3,300. Black: Sarawak black label spot was Dollars 1,800 a tonne and September/October Dollars 1700. Yellow label spot was Dollars 1,875 and September/October Dollars 1,775. Brazilian grade 1 spot was Dollars 1,775.
