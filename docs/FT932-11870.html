930430 FT 30 APR 93 / International Company News: AIG rises 12.8% in first period AMERICAN International Group, the US insurer, reported first-quarter net earnings up 12.8 per cent to Dollars 495.9m, or Dollars 2.34 a share, compared with Dollars 439.6m, or Dollars 2.06, a year earlier, writes Karen Zagor. Excluding accounting changes and realised capital gains, income rose 15.8 per cent to Dollars 452.7m, or Dollars 2.13, from Dollars 390.9m, or Dollars 1.83, last year. Revenues advanced 6.4 per cent to Dollars 4.64bn, from Dollars 4.36bn. AIG's general insurance operations in the US saw reasonable results in spite of several catastrophes.
