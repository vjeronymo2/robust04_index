930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies gain - At a Glance Smaller company shares continued to gain this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 0.7 per cent from 1466.13 to 1476.36 in the week to July 1. The index has now climbed for 10 consecutive months for the first time since the crash of 1987.
