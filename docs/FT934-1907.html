931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Haemocell loss rises to Pounds 2.13m Losses at Haemocell, the USM-traded medical equipment maker, rose from Pounds 1.69m to Pounds 2.13m pre-tax for the year to end-August after taking account of an exceptional provision of Pounds 413,000. Before the provision, which related to the termination of the distribution agreement with Stryker Corporation of the US, the deficit was in line with the directors' forecast in August. Turnover expanded from Pounds 676,000 to Pounds 1.12m. Losses widened to 10.1p (9.3p) per share.
