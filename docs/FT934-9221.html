931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Foster Wheeler and John Wood venture John Wood Group and Foster Wheeler have acquired from the JP Kenny group its worldwide engineering companies other than those in the former Soviet Union. The deal is worth about Pounds 5m, including a capital injection by the purchasers. A new company, JP Kenny Engineering, has been formed to take over all the specialist pipeline and undersea engineering services. It has an estimated turnover of Pounds 14m for the first nine months of this year, and about 320 full-time employees worldwide. John Wood, based in Aberdeen, is the UK's largest indigenous oil services company, and Foster Wheeler is the UK subsidiary of Foster Wheeler Corporation, the US engineering company. The two companies, which already have joint ventures together, said that JP Kenny's expertise would expand their services to the oil and gas industries.
