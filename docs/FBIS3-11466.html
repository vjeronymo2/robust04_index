Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 18 Mar (NA) -- The Foreign Ministry announced tonight that Jorge Campbell will be sworn in on 20 March as the new secretary for international economic relations. He replaces resigning International Economic Relations Secretary Hector Gambarotta. Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella accepted Gambarotta's resignation and promptly appointed engineer Jorge Campbell to this post. Campbell has headed twice the Foreign Trade Undersecretariat, and in 1991 he was appointed head of the Integration Undersecretariat in the president's office.
