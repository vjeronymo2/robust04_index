Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In another development, security services announced today that three members of armed groups were killed during the last 24 hours in Constantine, Boumerdes, and Ain Defla. Security services also said that it arrested six other members of the armed groups during the same period in Bouira, Tipaza and Boumerdes. In the capital, security services have discovered an arms and medicine cache in Birkhadem. The National Social Democratic Party has condemned the assassinations which have recently targeted all groups and sections of the society as well as the operations of sabotage which have hit public establishments and property. In its communique issued today, the Party called on the People's National Army to bear its responsibilities.
