930605 FT 05 JUN 93 / Loan approved The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development has agreed a Dollars 40m loan for the Chernogorskoye West Siberian oil joint venture between Russia's Chernogorneft and Anderman/Smith overseas, a US based oil company, writes Anthony Robinson. Itochu, the Japanese trading company, has just acquired a 20 per cent equity stake in the joint venture. The US Export-Import bank will finance 85 per cent of US-sourced equipment costs in a related deal worth Dollars 44m. Morgan Grenfell acted as financial adviser to the project financing deal for both the EBRD and Exim bank.
