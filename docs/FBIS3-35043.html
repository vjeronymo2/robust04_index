Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] To be rightly guided by the Holy Koran as the law of the new Jamahiri society, the General People's Congress has [in its January session in Surt] formulated seven laws that the Basic People's Congresses decided in regard to the application of Islamic law [shari'ah]: 1. The law on purification [al-tatthir] 2. The law on retribution and blood money. 3. The law on rules of the will. 4. The law on prohibition of alcohol. 5. The law amending the law on the punishment of theft and [word indistinct] 6. The law on protecting society from things prohibited by the Holy Koran. 7. The law amending provisions of the law of marriage and divorce and their consequences.
