940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / World Trade News: Rivals link up to win ship order Mitsubishi Heavy Industries of Japan and Samsung Heavy Industries of South Korea, in a rare show of co-operation for the shipbuilding industry, have received a joint order for six large container ships from Hong Kong's Orient Overseas Container Line. According to Samsung, it is the first time rival shipbuilders from Japan and South Korea have formed a consortium to bid for an order. Mitsubishi will construct four of the ships and Samsung two. The vessels are for delivery in late 1995 and early 1996.
