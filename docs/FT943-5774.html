940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / Daylight change campaign starts A campaign in support of a bill to bring Britain's clocks into line with those on the Continent, extending daylight hours in the evening, was launched yesterday. The Daylight Extra pressure group is lobbying MPs, hoping to have a supporter high enough in the annual ballot for a Private Member's Bill to be submitted to the Commons in November, and it is seeking legislation by 1996.
