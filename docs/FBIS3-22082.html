Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Brno, South Moravia, March 24 (CTK) -- Two Brno men who last week attempted to sell 1.024 kilogram of heroin to a Dutch citizen have been charged with the unlawful production and possession of opiates and psychotropic substances, the district investigation office told CTK today. The two men, aged 43 and 55, are now in police custody. If found guilty they face eight to fifteen years' imprisonment. The price of the heroin was 60,000 German marks. The Dutch citizen is probably a Czech emigre who regularly visits the Czech Republic, the investigator said.
