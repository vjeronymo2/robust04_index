Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian Defense Ministry has just received an ITAR-TASS report that allegedly a program for military cooperation between NATO and the Russian military department was signed on 28 January. However, the Russian Defense Ministry knows nothing about this. One thing is known for sure, a group of NATO representatives really did work in Moscow for two days and discussed cooperation prospects with the Russian military. Mutual understanding was reached on many problems. But no official documents were signed.
