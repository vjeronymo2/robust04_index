Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Mar (MENA)--Counternarcotics squads today seized 1 kg of heroin having an estimated street value of 1.5 million pounds. Information reached Maj. Gen. Mahmud Wajdi, director general of the Criminal Investigation Department, that Musa Kayrub Musa, a motor spare-parts dealer in Sulayman al-Halabi Street in the center of Cairo has brought 1 kg of heroin with the assistance of others through the Red Sea with the intention of selling it during the 'Id al-Fitr holiday. The defendant, who is 60 years old and is considered very rich, confessed to possessing the heroin. He said two of his colleagues from Qina Governorate in Upper Egypt brought him the heroin. The public prosecutor began interrogating the defendant.
