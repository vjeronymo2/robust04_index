Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The three-day seminar on the strategy for building Harbin into an important international economic and trade city of Northeast Asia ended on the afternoon of 8 January. Attending the seminar were Li Ruihuan, standing committee member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Li Peiyao, vice chairman of the National People's Congress; and Sun Weiben, Shao Qihui, and Zhou Wenhua, leaders of Heilongjiang Province. The specialists and scholars attending this seminar maintained: Harbin is in an advantageous geological position, has an age-old tradition of economic, trade, and cultural flows with foreign countries, solid scientific and technological strength, extensive international contacts, convenient communications and transportation facilities, and a flourishing and brisk market. In particular, the economic foundation based on relatively strong comprehensive functions and the favorable domestic and international economic environment make it completely possible for Harbin to become an important international economic and trade city. At the closing ceremony held on the afternoon of 8 January, Suo Changyou, mayor of Harbin city, expressed in his speech: We should translate the achievements of this seminar to the development plans of Harbin city as quickly as possible, should strive to enhance Harbin people's understanding of the significance in building the important international economic and trade city of Northeast Asia, and should make concerted efforts to march toward the goal of building an important international economic and trade city of Northeast Asia.
