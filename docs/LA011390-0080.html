January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRONKO NAGURSKI A REAL ALL-TIMER

Anyone lucky enough to watch Bronko Nagurski play football got their money's worth. He played hard and clean for 60 minutes against top and tough competition that gave no quarter. Comparisons are academic, but should there be a Super Bowl in heaven, Nagurski will be the starting fullback.

BILL RETCHIN, La Quinta
