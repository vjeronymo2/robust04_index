941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / Unit trust sales rise to net Pounds 419m Sales of unit trusts rose last month to a net Pounds 419m, up from Pounds 302m in October but down on November 1993 sales of Pounds 426m. The figures from the Association of Unit Trust and Investment Funds show that private investors accounted for 70 per cent of the total. The value of unit trust Peps in November came to Pounds 11.8bn out of a total value of Pounds 91.8bn. Total gross sales were Pounds 1.26bn in November with repurchases of Pounds 836m during the month. The number of unit-holder accounts rose to 6.08m from 4.94m a year ago.
