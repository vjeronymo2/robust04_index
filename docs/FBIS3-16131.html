Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 11 TASS -- The recent arrest of a Russian citizen on charges of spying for Germany was a key issue of a telephone conversation between Sergey Stepashin, head of the Russian Federal Counter-Intelligence service (FCS), and German State Minister Bernd Schmidbauer on Friday [11 March], a spokesman for the Russian Counter-Intelligence Service told ITAR-TASS. Aleksandr Mikhailov, the FCS spokesman, said that "the conversation was of a professional character". The arrest was earlier officially confirmed by the agency. According to Mikhailov, "the arrest has no political bearing". The detainment of the German agent was the result of counter-intelligence activities aimed at securing Russia's state interests", he said.
