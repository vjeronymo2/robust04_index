BFN [Text] The antiterrorism unit believes that the explosion of a powerful home-made time bomb that ignited at 2330 [2030 GMT] last night under a car was the work of 17 November and ELA [Revolutionary People's Struggle], or of ELA. The car, which was parked at the junction of Rigas Fereos and Mesoyion Streets in the vicinity of the Pentagon [Greek National Defense General Staff headquarters], belongs to the Miele company. The explosion caused serious material damage to shops and buildings in the area.
