BFN [Text] Hong Kong, April 26 KYODO -- Taiwan's President Lee Teng-hui expressed "deep concern" Tuesday [26 April] night about the crash-landing of a China Airlines jetliner at Nagoya Airport and pledged to take immediate measures to help the victims and bereaved families, Taiwanese officials said. Nearly 220 people of a total of 272 passengers and crew, most of them Japanese and Taiwanese nationals, died trapped in the burning aircraft.
