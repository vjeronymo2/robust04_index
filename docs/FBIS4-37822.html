BFN [Summary from poor reception] Acting Prime Minister Brother Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar has visited al-Hudaydah Governorate and inspected storehouses of basic food and became acquainted with the food situation there. Al-'Attar emphasized that measures should be taken to ensure that food regularly reaches the governorate and that monopoly of goods should be fought. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', brother al-'Attar stressed that the quantities of food available in various storehouses are sufficient and reassuring, and there is no reason at all for concern. He said the Council of Ministers will resume its meeting next Sunday [26 June] to discuss supply issues.
