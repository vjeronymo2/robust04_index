July 1, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

WE CALLED IT PUPPY LOVE, BUT FANS SAY WE'LL NEVER KNOW . . .

Regarding Rachelle Moore's June 11 letter saying New Kids on the Block is "really stupid and immature": I'd like to say that at least they don't cavort around with sleazy blondes singing unmentionable things while trying to get them up to their bedrooms. And this is only in the videos!

KIMBERLY SMITH

Northridge
