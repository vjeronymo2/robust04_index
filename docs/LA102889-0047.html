October 28, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

PACIFIC LEAGUE

Glendale 6, Crescenta Valley 3 -- Pathon Rucker scored with 7:45 remaining and the Dynamiters (4-4, 1-2) stopped Crescenta Valley (6-2, 2-1) on a fourth-and-goal play from the five-yard line with 28 seconds remaining at Glendale.

Rucker finished with 106 yards in 30 carries, including 100 yards in 20 first-half carries. Glendale receiver John Martell caught seven passes for 116 yards. MIKE GELLER
