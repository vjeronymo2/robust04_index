921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Kevin Maxwell court plea fails MR KEVIN MAXWELL yesterday failed in his second High Court attempt to win a judicial review of the Serious Fraud Office's refusal to disclose documentary evidence of the case against him. Mr Maxwell, who faces charges of theft and conspiracy to defraud, had claimed the SFO was acting unlawfully and unfairly in not disclosing such evidence before questioning him under powers which remove the right to silence.
