BFN [Text] Warsaw, ROMPRES, 23/6/1994 -- Poland's Minister of Foreign Affairs Andrzej Olechowski, and Romania's Minister of Foreign Affairs Teodor Melescanu signed Thursday, 23 June, in Warsaw several documents regarding the cooperation between the two countries. They are two economic agreements: for the avoidance of double taxation and prevention of fiscal evasion, and, respectively, the mutual promotion and protection of investments. The sides also signed a cultural cooperation agreement and an accord of collaboration in foreign policy matters. The two ministers shared the opinion that the documents signed today are apt to relaunch the bilateral relations, stagnant for some time.
