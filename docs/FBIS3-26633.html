Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Haikou, February 10 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, today visited the construction site of the international airport at Sanya city, Hainan Province, south China. Qiao, also member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, expressed festival greetings to the workers and technological staff at the site. He said that this year China is expected to make still greater progress in various sectors under the guidance of Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics. Sanya Phoenix International Airport is one of the major infrastructure projects in Hainan Province. Work started in May 1990. The airport is expected to open on July 1 this year. Qiao Shi inspected the main runway and the passenger lounges and parking apron which are under construction. He also had a talk with French specialists who are involved in the installation of navigational facilities. From February 4 to 10, Qiao Shi, accompanied by Hainan Governor Ruan Chongwu, inspected Haikou, Qiongshan and Sanya cities, including some major projects and nature reserves.
