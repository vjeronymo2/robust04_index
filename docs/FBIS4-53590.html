BFN [Text] The Vietnam Oil and Natural Gas General Corporation's Vietsovpetro joint enterprise is striving to exploit various new oil wells and develop old ones to increase crude oil production. As of 15 May, the enterprise had produced more than 2.5 million tonnes of crude oil. It has also exploited the acid substances produced at the bottom of oil wells and has signed contracts to improve conditions at various oil wells to increase the production output by 8,000 tonnes per day. While making great efforts to improve conditions at new and old oil wells, the enterprise is renovating the oil tanker Ba Vi to assist in the exploitation of oil fields in the third quarter this year.
