930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / World Bank funds for Kiev The World Bank said yesterday it was extending its first credit to Ukraine - Dollars 46m (Pounds 29.8m) to support market-oriented reforms in the former Soviet republic, Reuter reports from Kiev. Mr Daniel Kaufmann, the bank's representative in Kiev, said the bank was considering extending a further Dollars 400m to Ukraine to support market reform. New credits, he said, depended on the Kiev government adhering to strict financial policies and structural reforms.
