Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 22 (SNARK) -- Active combats have resumed today in a number of places on the Karabakh-Azerbaijan confrontation line. As told to SNARK by the Karabakh Defence Army press-service, Azerbaijani troops undertook since morning Feb. 22 an offensive in Martuniskiy and Fizoulinskiy directions, at the Eastern and South-Eastern battle areas. By the noon data, here combats were started in process of which the Karabakh forces were able to neutralize the enemy's efforts. As yet there is no information of losses. At the same time, artillery shelling of Karabakh Army positions is made in suburbs of regional center Mardakert. Karabakh side undertook counter actions.
