930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Bramley apples are this weeks best fruit buy at 28-35p a lb (30-35p), along with other apple varieties such as English Russet and British Columbia Red Delicious, both at 45-50p a lb (45-50p), reports FFVIB. Navel oranges at 10-30p each (10-30p) and white-fleshed grapefruit at 12-25p each (12-25p) round off this weeks best fruit buys. Savoy Cabbages at 25-35p a lb (25-35p), broccoli at 45-60p per 8oz pre-pack (45-60p) and carrots at 18-20p a lb (18-20p) are all excellent vegetable choices. Round lettuce at 25-30p each (30-35p) and Spania celery at 50-65p a head (50-65p) are good buys.
