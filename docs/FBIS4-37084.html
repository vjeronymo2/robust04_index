BFN [Text] Kuwait City, 14 Jun (KUNA) -- The car of Ryan Crocker, U.S. ambassador to Kuwait, was involved in a road accident today. Two persons sustained slight injuries as a result of this accident. A statement released by the U.S. Embassy, a copy of which was received by KUNA, said that "the ambassador was not injured in this accident; while two other persons, one of whom was the ambassador's driver, sustained slight injuries." The statement indicated that the accident happened at 1000 local time at Roundabout No. 2.
