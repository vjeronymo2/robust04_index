BFN [Text] Mr. Velayati, the foreign minister, arrived in Kampala, the capital of Uganda, on Saturday evening in a continuation of his tour of several African countries. Our country's foreign minister and his accompanying delegation were welcomed at Kampala Airport by the foreign minister, deputy prime minister, and the head of the Shiite Association of Uganda. According to the Central News Unit, during his visit to Uganda, Mr. Velayati will not only hold talks with that country's senior officials, but will also attend and speak at the seventh assembly of the Pan African Congress at the invitation of that congress.
