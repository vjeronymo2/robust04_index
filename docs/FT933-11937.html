930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Campbell Armstrong Campbell & Armstrong, the shop and office fitting group, incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 3.94m for the year to March 31 - in line with last month's warning. The outcome, prepared under FRS 3, compared with a restated deficit of Pounds 822,932 and was struck after bad debts, redundancy costs and losses from closed businesses totalling Pounds 900,000 (Pounds 500,000). The group also took exceptional charges of Pounds 1.4m relating to a property write-down and a loss on disposal of businesses. Turnover was Pounds 50.5m (Pounds 61.3m). Adjusted losses per share were 14.5p (4.1p).
