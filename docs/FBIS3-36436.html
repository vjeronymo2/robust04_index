Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 4 (SNARK)--Today 500-people meeting was held at the Liberty Square in Yerevan. The meeting's participants expressed their discontent with the policy of the current Armenian authorities and proposed changing the leadership of the Republic, that, in their opinions, doesn't have trust and authority among people. The meetings' participants suggested to the leading opposition party - the Armenian Revolutionary Federation "Dashnaktsutyun" (that published today its programme on Armenia's quitting a crises) to take initiative on the building the National Agreement's government. In the appeal to this party's leadership, unanimously accepted, Yerevan inhabitants stand for the party "Dashnaktsutyn" presents the National Agreement government's staff at the meeting held by initiative of several social and political organizations.
