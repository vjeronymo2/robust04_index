931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bass in Indonesian joint venture Holiday Inn Worldwide, the hotels division of Bass, the brewing and leisure group, has signed a joint venture with PT Hotel Dirgahayu Indah Raya, part of the Susanti Group, to develop 30 hotels in Indonesia over the next seven years. Bass and Susanti will each invest Dollars 30m (Pounds 20m) progressively in the venture. Additional debt financing will be sought as hotels are developed.
