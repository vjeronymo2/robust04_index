BFN [Text] Beijing, May 18 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today that the report that Wei Jingsheng is being prosecuted on charges of high treason is a "sheer fabrication." The spokesman made the statement when asked to comment on the report that the Chinese Judicial Department is prosecuting Wei on charges of high treason and he is likely to receive a sentence of 10 to 15 years imprisonment. "Our information from the departments concerned shows that this report is a sheer fabrication," he noted.
