Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 17 Jan (ARMENPRES)--By a decree of the Republic's president, the operative services of the State Directorate for the National Security of the Republic of Armenia have been dissolved and the activities of their employees have been suspended, with the exception of the Main Directorate for the Defense of the State Border and administrative services. The objective is to restructure the inner parts of the State Directorate for National Security in light of problems facing the State Directorate for National Security under conditions of independent statehood. With the same decree, Davit Shahnazaryan--the Armenian president's ambassador on special missions--has been appointed acting head of the State Directorate for National Security. Within three months he must conduct an inspection of the quality of the services of the Directorate and draw up and submit for the government's approval the bylaws, structure, and list of permanent staff.
