Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- The birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was celebrated in different countries. A celebration meeting was held at the building of the Nicaraguan Association of Democratic Lawyers. Chairman of the Nicaragua-Korea Friendship and Solidarity Association Albaro Ramirez Gonsalez in his speech stressed that the Nicaraguan people were significantly celebrating the birthday of Comrade Kim Chong-il together with the Korean people. He said: "Dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is an illustrious leader who is constantly exalting socialism with his outstanding idea and theory and his energetic leadership. The Korean people are holding him in high esteem and creditably building Korean-style socialism under his wise leadership." Meanwhile, a seminar on the chuche idea was held at the independence study centre of Bangladesh and a seminar on Nepal-Korea friendly relations at the National Press Club of Nepal. DPRK book, photo and handiwork exhibitions, film shows, photo illustrations, lectures and other functions were arranged in Islamabad, Pakistan, the House of the Nepal Academy of Sciences, a City House of Culture of Poland, Khodenka Exhibition-Gallery in northwest administrative district of Moscow, the Banat Museum in Timisoara of Romania, a company of Thailand, Abidja state of Nigeria, Helsinki, the National Youth Union of Algeria, the Cultural Palace of Workers in Yanji, Jilin Province, China, and the group for the study of the chuche idea of the Pugura Club of the Malta Labour Party. A performance of "Art Evening of February" was given in Tokyo and a "national table tennis competition for February 16 cup" was sponsored by the Mongolia-Korea Friendship Association and the Mongolian Table Tennis Federation at the gymnasium of the sports college of Mongolia.
