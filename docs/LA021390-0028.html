February 13, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Sing Along with "Miss Saigon": The original London-cast album of "Miss Saigon" will be released today by Geffen (two albums, CDs or tapes.) The raved-about musical from Cameron Mackintosh of "Les Miserables" fame is expected to come to Broadway some time in 1991. BETH KLEID
