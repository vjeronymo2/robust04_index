BFN [Text] The minister of safety and security, Mr. Sydney Mufamadi, and his deputy, Mr. Joe Matthews, say stability in the country is among their priorities as social development will depend on personal security and law and order to attract foreign investment. The two men were addressing a news conference in Pretoria after talks with the commissioner of Police, General Johan van der Merwe. Mr. Mufamadi and Mr. Matthews are to hold talks later this week with all police generals and former homeland police commanders.
