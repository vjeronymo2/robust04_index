May 14, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Video works by artist Ilene Segalove, now featured in her first career survey at the Laguna Art Museum, will be shown June 1 on KCET Channel 28 in conjunction with the exhibit. The 30-minute program, titled "The Eleventh Hour," is sub-titled "Why I Got Into TV and Other Stories." Segalove is well-known for humorous videos and radio works about television and other aspects of popular culture.
