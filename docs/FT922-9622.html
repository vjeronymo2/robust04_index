920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Queen's Speech: Missing agency - Environment A CONSPICUOUS absentee from the Queen's Speech was any commitment to set up an Environment Agency, as promised in the Tory election manifesto. This is a thought to result from a disagreement between the Department of the Environment and the Ministry of Agriculture over who should be responsible for water pollution controls. Mr Bryan Gould, the shadow environment secretary, said the omission showed 'just how shallow is the Conservative commitment to the green agenda'.
