August 25, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Denny Neagle became the first 20-game winner in the minor leagues since 1986 when he won his 12th game for the Orlando (Fla.)

SunRays in the Southern League. Neagle had an 8-0 record at Visalia in the California League before joining Orlando.

Neagle, a 6-foot-4, 200-pound left-hander from Gambrills, Md., is 12-3 for the Minnesota Twins' farm team.
