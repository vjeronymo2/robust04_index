BFN [Text] Yerevan, 2 May (SNARK)--Tomorrow, following the invitation of the Iran Government, the Armenian delegation headed by the Vice-President of Armenia Gagik Arutyunyan leaves for Teheran. Press service of the Armenian President told SNARK the problems connected with the Armenian delegation's visit were discussed at the meeting of Gagik Arutyunyan and Ahmad Sobkhami, Iran charge d'affaires in Yerevan.
