BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow June 27 TASS -- The consultative Foreign Investment Council attached to the Russian Government is entrusted with working out the strategy for investing foreign capital to Russia, Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said in a conversation with Western businessmen who have arrived in Moscow to participate in the council meeting which opens today. Judging by all appearances, the consultative council will mark another phase in rapprochement between the Russian and world economies hot on the heels of the Russian president's recent successful trip to Corfu and of the prime minister's visit to the United States. The council meeting today is to be addressed by Prime Minister Chernomyrdin, First Vice-Premier Oleg Soskovets, Central Bank Governor Viktor Gershchenko and Acting Finance Minister Sergey Dubinin. The leaders of Russian key ministries and the departments are taking part in the council meeting. Representatives of transnational corporations of North America, Europe and Asia are taking part in the meeting. Western businessmen said in the conversation with the Russian prime minister that they would like to see a better organisation of the consultative council's work. They called for setting up panels and deciding on the specific directions of work.
