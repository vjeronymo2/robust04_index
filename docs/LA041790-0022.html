April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Seeing Double Oprah: Daytime talk-show host Oprah Winfrey will be prime-timing it next month when "Brewster Place," a dramatic series based on last season's miniseries "The Women of Brewster Place," debuts on ABC. The show will have special premieres May 1 and May 8 before moving to its regular time on Wednesdays at 9:30. Winfrey will reprise her role as the strong-willed Mattie Michael in the series, which centers around an inner-city community in 1967. GREG BRAXTON
