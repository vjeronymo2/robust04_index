940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Pilkington chief makes Pounds 60,000 in option deal Sir Antony Pilkington, chairman of the Pilkington glass group, has exercised options and sold the shares, taking a profit of nearly Pounds 60,000 before expenses. Sir Antony bought 128,500 shares last Friday by exercising options at 139 2/3 p. He sold the same number of shares the same day at 185 1/2 p, giving a gross profit of Pounds 58,900. The deal leaves his holding of shares held beneficially at a little less than 1m, representing 0.1 per cent of the total shares in issue. He is also listed as a non-beneficial owner of shares held in trusts.
