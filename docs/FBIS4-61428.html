BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Kazan -- President Shaymiyev has stated that a high- level bilateral commission will soon be set up to examine the implementation of treaty-based obligations between the Russian Federation and the Republic of Tatarstan. He stressed that without assistance from Russia the economic situation in Tataria would have been substantially worse. "At present we are receiving much more from the federal budget for current expenses than we remit. To conduct spring agricultural work alone the republic has received an easy credit of 65 billion rubles, which exceeds our contribution to the Russian budget by almost 10 times."
