BFN [Text] Mexico City, 11 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- The Mexican Secretariat of National Defense reported that various national institutions are conducting counternarcotics operations this rainy season, a time suitable for the planting of drug crops. Mexican troops have conducted operations in Mexico, Sinaloa, Durango, Chihuahua, Nayarit, Jalisco, Colima, Michoacan, Oaxaca, Nuevo Leon, Tamaulipas, Veracruz, Hidalgo, Guerrero, Zacatecas, Aguascalientes, San Luis de Potosi, Guanajuato, Queretaro, Puebla, and Morelos States, according to a communique issued by the Secretariat this Friday. In the communique, the Secretariat of Defense indicated that these operations were not carried out solely in Guerrero State, where local media reported a "massive troop presence." The communique indicated that operations staged in 21 states on 1 June resulted in the arrest of 10 local drug traffickers, the destruction of 2,242 drug crops, four drug traffickers' camps, and a marijuana drier. Officials also incinerated 323 kg of marijuana seeds, 30 kg of poppy seeds, and two kg of cocaine and confiscated 233 firearms and two vehicles. The institution said that in addition to the counternarcotics operations, authorities will also apply the "Federal Law on Weapons and Control of Explosives." The Secretariat added that following the "Regional Operation 1-94," conducted from 10 March to 5 April, 22,418 poppy and marijuana fields, 6,000 kg of marijuana, and 431 kg of poppy and marijuana seeds were destroyed. In addition, 256 firearms and 28 vehicles were seized. The Secretariat of National Defense stated that it is continuing its counternarcotics operations.
