Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Terrorists fired at a minibus near Ekinozu District in Kahramanmaras, killing two village guards and wounding seven persons, including another village guard. According to information received from Governor Attila Osmancelebioglu, a group of terrorists fired at the minibus near Ciftlikkale village. Village guards Ahmet Polat and Mevlut Yildiz, who were in the minibus, died on the spot. The seven wounded were taken to hospitals in Kayseri, Malatya, and Elbistan. Security forces are conducting search operations in the area.
