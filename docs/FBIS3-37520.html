Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN ["Ukrainian Presidential Edict on Provisions for the Accommodation in Ukraine of Official Observers From Foreign States and International Organizations During the 1994 Elections" issued in Kiev on 10 February] [Text] In order to organize the accommodation in Ukraine of official observers from foreign states and international organizations during the preparation for and holding of the elections of people's deputies on 27 March 1994 and of the Ukrainian president, chairmen of local councils, and deputies of local councils on 26 June 1994, I decree as follows: 1. To support the proposals by Ukraine's Supreme Council Presidium and the Central Electoral Commission on the Elections of Ukraine's People's Deputies and to create a Working Group for Accommodating in Ukraine of Official Observers From Foreign States and International Organizations at the 1994 Elections (hereafter -- Working Group). 2. To approve the composition of the Working Group. 3. To approve the Plan for Organizational Measures on the Accommodation in Ukraine of Official Observers From Foreign States and International Organizations at the 1994 Elections. 4. Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers must resolve, within 10 days, questions of material, technological, financial, and other types of provision for the accommodation in Ukraine of official observers from foreign states and international organizations. 5. The Council of Ministers of the Crimean Republic, local councils of people's deputies, and local state administrations must ensure the organization of work and services for official observers from foreign states and international organizations. 6. This edict becomes effective on the day of its signing. [Signed] Ukrainian President L. Kravchuk [Dated] Kiev, 10 February 1994
