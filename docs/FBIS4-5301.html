BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 8 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The press office of the Mongolian Government has released officially regarding the public accusations addressing a number of high ranking officials including the prime minister, as involved in the abuse of official position and bribery. [sentence as received] The statement said that the Prime Minister P. Jasray considers such allegations as being designed to undermine the prestige of and confidence in the government and applied to the court in this reference. P. Jasray emphasized that as notes the governmental press office, the government would be steadily struggle with any cases of abuse of official position and bribery. [sentence as received] Availing this opportunity the press office stated also that the prime minister is not going to take part in the today's meeting, announced by the Mongolian Democratic Association, as was informed earlier by a member of the Great State Hural T. Elbegdorj which was not agreed upon with himself.
