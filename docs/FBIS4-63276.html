CSO ["Announcement of Press Center of Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Azerbaijan Republic"] [Text] Certain press agencies, referring to the Press Center of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, are maintaining that on 13 May 1994 at the presidency of the Azerbaijan Republic there was a secret meeting at which opinions on the commitment of Russian troops to Azerbaijan were exchanged. It is maintained also that certain leading persons who took part in this meeting spoke for and against this matter. The Press Center of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Azerbaijan Republic deems it important to announce the following. A meeting was held at the presidency of the Azerbaijan Republic on 13 May 1994. The military-political situation that has taken shape in the republic and ways out of it were discussed. The question of the commitment to Azerbaijan of troops of Russia or any other state was not discussed.
