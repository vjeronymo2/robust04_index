BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- Views were exchanged between Kang Sok-chu, first vice-minister of foreign affairs, and Jimmy Carter, former U.S. President, at the People's Palace of Culture here today. Present there were Choe U-chin, vice-minister of foreign affairs, and other officials concerned and Carter's entourage. They compared notes on a series of matters of common concern including the nuclear issue.
