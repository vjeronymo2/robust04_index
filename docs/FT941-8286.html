940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / Lib Dems forecast to win Eastleigh A Liberal Democrat victory at the coming Eastleigh by-election is forecast by an opinion poll for The Independent newspaper today. The NOP poll gives the Liberal Democrats 43 per cent of the vote, 11 points ahead of the Tories, with Labour on 25 per cent. Stephen Milligan, whose death caused the by-election, had a 17,702 majority at the last general election.
