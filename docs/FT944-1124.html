941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / World Trade News: SNC-Lavalin win Kenyan contract SNC-Lavalin, Canada's biggest engineering consultants and construction managers, has won a CDollars 115m (Dollars 83m) turnkey contract from the Kenyan government to build an international airport at Eldoret, 240 miles from Nairobi, with a 11,500ft main runway.
