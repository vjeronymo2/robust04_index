August 17, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

SUNNY HILLS NAMES WATER POLO COACH

Keith Nighswonger has been hired as water polo coach at Sunny Hills High School, Ralph Trigsted, the school's athletic director, said Wednesday.

Nighswonger has coached at Montebello for the past five years. He replaces Jim Sprague, who resigned last spring to become an assistant coach at USC.
