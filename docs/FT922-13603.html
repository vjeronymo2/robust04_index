920410 FT 10 APR 92 / Briton wins Nato job BRITAIN'S chief of defence staff was chosen for Nato's most senior military job yesterday, beating off a challenge from his Belgian counterpart, Reuter reports from Brussels. Field Marshal Sir Richard Vincent, 60, was elected by Nato chiefs of staff as chairman of the military committee. The committee is Nato's highest military authority and helps co-ordinate defence planning. It is now chaired by Norwegian General Vigleik Eide, whose term has nearly ended.
