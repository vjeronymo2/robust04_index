940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: ICI makes Spanish disposal Imperial Chemical Industries has sold its Nurel Spanish fibres units to its management for an undisclosed amount. Nurel, based in Zaragoza, makes Nylon 6, polyester staple fibre and polyester filament yarn. It has annual sales of about Pounds 50m. The transaction represented less than 1 per cent of ICI's net assets.
