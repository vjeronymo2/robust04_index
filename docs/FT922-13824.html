920409 FT 09 APR 92 / Rise in graduate unemployment UNEMPLOYMENT among graduates has nearly doubled in the past two years, from 5.8 per cent in 1989 to 10.1 per cent last year, in 1991, the highest level since 1983, according to figures released yesterday by the Labour party. Only 40 per cent of graduates had found full-time employment six months after graduating, down from 46 per cent last year, according to a survey for the Department of Employment and released early by Labour.
