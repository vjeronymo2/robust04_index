BFN [Text] Quito, 5 May (EFE) -- Today, Ecuadoran Defense Minister General Jose Gallardo announced the resignation of Ecuadoran Army Commander General Edmundo Luna, who will be replaced by Gen. Victor Bayas, current Army chief of staff. Gallardo explained the change is part of a process of normal renewal in the Armed Forcues and denied that there is a dispute within that institution. "Gen. Edmundo Luna has asked for a discharge and will be replaced in the next few days through the normal Armed Forces promotion process," Gallardo added. The defense minister concluded that Gen. Alfonso Alarcon, chief of the Armed Forces Joint Command, will also be replaced in the next few days. His successor will be appointed by President Sixto Duran-Ballen.
