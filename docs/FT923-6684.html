920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Baillie Gifford Shin Nippon assets lower Baillie Gifford Shin Nippon had a net asset value per share of 85.6p diluted at July 31, against 145.5p a year earlier and 124.4p at its January 31 1992 year end. The net loss for the half year was Pounds 53,843 (Pounds 79,499). losses per share came out at 0.34p (0.5p).
