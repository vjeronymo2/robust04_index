Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 11 (SNARK)--Today Levon Ter-Petrosyan, the President of Armenia, met with the editors of the newspapers issuing in Armenia. SNARK was told by the press-secretary of the president that during the meeting they discussed, mainly, the questions concerning Karabakh conflict. According to the press-secretary the president presented the view point of Armenia's authorities relating to the problem of Karabakh. Zurabian refused to inform about the details of the meeting.
