Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB Proposal: Real Estate Mortgage Investment Conduit (REMIC) Notice of GNMA. Office: Government National Mortgage Association (GNMA). Description of the Need for the Information and its Proposed Use: The purpose of this information collection is to allow GNMA's REMIC Program to raise revenue for the U.S. Government through receipt of guarantee fees and to benefit borrowers using Federally insured or guaranteed mortgages by lowering financing costs for these mortgages.
