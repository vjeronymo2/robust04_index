920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / International Company News: Bankers Trust abandons purchase BANKERS Trust, the New York investment bank, has abandoned plans to buy Integrated Resources, a New York finance group which is in Chapter 11 bankruptcy, in a complex Dollars 565m deal, writes Martin Dickson. Bankers Trust, confirming the move yesterday, regretted it had been unable to reach a definitive agreement with committees of the company's creditors. An agreement was meant to have been reached by mid-January.
