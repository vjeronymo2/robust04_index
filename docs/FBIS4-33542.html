BFN [Text] Peruvian Congressman Carlos Torres continues his program of activities in Cuba. Torres has been in Cuba since 3 June, honoring an invitation by the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP]. Today, Torres, who is the first vice president of the Peruvian Congress, will meet with the Foreign Ministry and the ANPP Commission on Constitutional Affairs. Torres is also scheduled to visit the University of Havana where he will speak with the dean and students of the Law Department. Torres has already met with ANPP President Ricardo Alarcon and was briefed about the family doctor and nurse program.
