BFN [Excerpts] As is well-known, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat began a visit to Morocco today. At the Rabat-Salle VIP lounge he made a statement in which he praised the stances of His Majesty King Hassan II toward the Palestinian issue, in particular, and Arab issues, in general. [passage omitted] On the meeting of the countries offering aid to the self-rule areas that will begin in Paris tomorrow, the Palestinian president said: [Begin 'Arafat recording] I received an important message yesterday from Mr. Warren Christopher on the occasion of the donor countries' conference, which--God willing--will begin its meetings in Paris tomorrow. This message demonstrates a clear-cut U.S. stance that is supportive of this process and the Palestinian authority. It urges all donor countries to follow the example of the U.S. Administration in offering urgent and immediate aid to the national authority to enable it to perform its duties, particularly because we need great potential in the first days to be able to carry this heavy burden. [end recording] [passage omitted]
