Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 1500 GMT on 21 March reports "Choe Kil-yong, chairman of the Educational Committee, arrived in Cairo on 20 March by plane for a visit to Egypt as the great leader Comrade Kim Il- song's special envoy." Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 2300 GMT on 23 March reports that "Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak met with Choe Kil-yong, chairman of the Educational Committee, who is the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song's envoy, on 21 March," adding that the envoy conveyed the greetings of Kim Il- song and Kim chong-il to the Egyptian president. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 1300 GMT on 23 March reports that "a delegation of the Korean Social Democratic Party led by Kim Kyong-sik visited Uruguay from 4 to 10 March" and met "Gonzalo Aguirre Ramirez, vice president, president of the Senate, and acting president," and other political leaders. The report says that "the delegation conveyed the greetings of Kim Il-song and Kim Chong-il to the Uruguayan president and heads of political parties." The report adds that the delegation visited Paraguay from 10 to 18 March.
