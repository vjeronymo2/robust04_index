930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Continued progress for Metsec THE RECOVERY seen in the second half of the previous year continued in the first six months of 1993 at Metsec and a loss of Pounds 868,000 was converted into a profit of Pounds 505,000 pre-tax. The USM-quoted company, which has interests in building products, electronics and engineering, said profits were generated in all divisions. The most marked improvement was in the overseas activities of pneumatic tube systems Turnover was Pounds 25.9m (Pounds 31.1m). The result was helped by a fall of Pounds 100,000 in interest charged. Earnings were 2.48p (losses 5.75p).
