BFN [Text] By 1 May, the city of Zhengzhou had sold 380 million yuan worth of treasury bonds, including 60 million yuan worth of two-year bonds and 320 million yuan worth of three-year bonds. Zhengzhou party and government leaders have attached importance to promoting the sales of treasury bonds this year. On 30 March, the city government held a special mobilization meeting to promote treasury bond sales. Quotas were assigned and the responsibilities of relevant units were defined. Various relevant units responsible for selling treasury bonds have taken strengthening service and providing convenience for buyers as their guideline to make early and full preparations for promotion work. Various specialized banks also have strengthened internal management to assign tasks to various levels, based on the responsibility system. They have extended business hours, so that buyers can buy treasury bonds at any sales point at any time they like. By 1 May, some sales points already had fulfilled their tasks.
