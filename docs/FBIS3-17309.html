Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow March 22 TASS -- Russian and Ukrainian border guards gathered on Monday in a working meeting in the central russian town of Voronezh. The meeting was chaired by Russian Border Troop Commander-in- Chief Andrey Nikolayev. It was also attended by officials of the Russian and Ukrainian Foreign and Interior Ministries, customs and migration services, Russian Border Troop chief spokesman Aleksandr Suvorov reported. Participants in the meeting passed a resolution on joint protection of the 2,000-kilometre-long border between the two states, including by joint Russian-Ukrainian border details, whose main task will be to thwart smuggling attempts. As a result of the meeting, proposals were elaborated which are to be submitted for the consideration of the Russian and Ukrainian presidents. According to the spokesman, the Russian Border Troop commander-in-chief plans to visit Kaliningrad in western Russia on March 25-26, and then to take a short-term working trip to Tajikistan.
