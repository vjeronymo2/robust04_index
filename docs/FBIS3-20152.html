Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar. 27 (A.A.) -- Voting has ended in 31 eastern and southeastern provinces and the counting of votes has begun. Polling stations were closed at 4 p.m. In Adiyaman, Agri, Artvin, Bingol, Bitlis, Diyarbakir, Elazig, Erzincan, Erzurum, Gaziantep, Giresun, Gumushane, Hakkari, Kars, Malatya, Kahramanmaras, Mardin, Mus, Ordu, Rize, Siirt, Sivas, Trabzon, Tunceli, Sanliurfa, Van, Bayburt, Batman, Sirnak, Ardahan and Igdir. Regional Emergency Governor Unal Erkan said during the voting that no important election-jeopardizing incident has occured in the region. The Office of the Governorate organized a helicopter-tour in the region's villages for members of the foreign press who came to the site to cover the polls. Erkan told the Anatolian Agency that 96 foreign journalists were in Diyarbakir. "The majority of foreigners were conditioned to witness great unrest in the region," Erkan said. "They expected to see freedoms curbed, clashes erupting and security forces having difficulty in providing security." Erkan said that the voter turnout was high.
