BFN [Text] Simferopol, 24 June -- Presidential Candidate Leonid Kuchma visited the Republic of Crimea. In Kerch, he met the director corps and employees of the Kerch Metallurgical Combine lease association and the "Zatoka" ship-building works (an enterprise in the military-industrial complex). His meeting with the Crimean Republic's agrarians took place in Simferopolskiy rayon. Professor Serhiy Nikolskyy, Crimea's minister of agriculture and food, took part in the meeting. At a meeting with voters Leonid Kuchma said that if he becomes president of Ukraine he will resolve the problem of the Black Sea Fleet by leasing it out on condition of receiving free energy carriers from Russia, and blamed the previous government and parliament for the ratification of the START-1 Treaty, in accordance with which Ukraine lost nuclear fuel, which according to Leonid Kuchma would have been sufficient for six or seven years.
