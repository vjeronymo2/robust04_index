940428 FT 28 APR 94 / Final honour for disgraced president who won back respect Five presidents pay homage to Richard Nixon at his funeral yesterday in his home town of Yorba Linda, California. Bill Clinton stands to attention with George Bush, Ronald Reagan, Jimmy Carter and Gerald Ford and their wives. Clinton paid tribute to Nixon, who died on Saturday, as a patriot, a keen intellect and a fighter who made mistakes but whose life must be judged in the totality of his record. 'He made mistakes and they, like his accomplishments, are part of his life and record.'
