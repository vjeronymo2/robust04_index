931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / Audio-visual industry and Uruguay Round agreement President Clinton yesterday told representatives of the US film industry that open markets for audio-visual industries must be included in any final Uruguay Round agreement. Mr Jack Valenti, head of the US Motion Picture Association, said the White House was preparing to issue a statement that any deal must be 'fair and just to American audio-visual'. Mr Valenti was upset about French taxes on films which are used to subsidise the French film industry. 'About 60 per cent of the subsidy money is taken from theatres showing American films. We deserve a share of that.'
