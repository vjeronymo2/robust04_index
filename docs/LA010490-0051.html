January 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

COOK IT LIGHT: REDUCING FAT IN 'DELICIOUS' BREAD

DEAR JEANNE: This recipe looks delicious, but I want to try to decrease the fat.

-- MARY, Fort Worth, Tex.

DOM DELUISE'S CHEESE AND ONION BREAD

1 1/2 cups chopped onions

1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons butter

1 egg

1/2 cup sour cream

Pepper

2 cups flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

2/3 cup milk

3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Saute onions in 2 tablespoons butter until tender. Combine onions with egg, sour cream and pepper to taste in bowl, mixing well. Set aside.

Place flour and baking powder in another bowl. Cut in remaining 1/4 cup butter until crumbly and fine. Stir in 1/2 cup cheese. Add milk to make soft dough.

Pat into buttered 9-inch square pan. Spread sour cream mixture on top. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese and parsley. Bake at 425 degrees 25 minutes. Cut into squares and serve warm. Makes 8 servings.

DEAR MARY: In spite of the changes I have made and the huge reduction in calories, cholesterol, fat and sodium, most people would not be able to tell the difference between the original and my revision. I took advantage of the reduced-fat products now in the markets to lower the calories and fat from the sour cream and the cheese. Using egg whites instead of a whole egg eliminates the cholesterol from the yolk, and nonfat milk instead of whole milk also worked beautifully.

-- JEANNE

JEANNE'S CHEESE AND ONION BREAD

2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon corn-oil margarine

1 1/2 cups chopped onions

2 egg whites

1/2 cup light sour cream

1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

2 cups unbleached flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

1 cup shredded 20% fat-reduced Cheddar cheese

2/3 cup nonfat milk

3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Melt 1 teaspoon margarine in skillet over medium heat. Saute onions until soft, then cool slightly. Add egg whites, sour cream and pepper. Mix well and set aside.

Combine flour and baking powder in mixing bowl. Cut in remaining 2 tablespoons margarine with pastry blender or fork until crumbly and fine. Stir in 1/2 cup shredded cheese. Add milk and stir to make soft dough.

Pat dough into 9-inch square pan sprayed with nonstick vegetable coating. Spread sour cream mixture on top. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese and parsley. Bake at 425 degrees 25 minutes. Cut into squares and serve immediately. Makes 8 servings.

Each serving contains approximately:

Original Recipe:

375 calories

92 mg cholesterol

24 gm fat

561 mg sodium

Revised Recipe:

200 calories

18 mg cholesterol

6 gm fat

338 mg sodium
