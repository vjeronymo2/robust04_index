921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / International Company News: Rockwell hit by fall in sales ROCKWELL International's operating net income for the fiscal fourth quarter and year to end-September fell due to declining sales in several of its main business segments, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Sales declined on the year for its electronics, aerospace, and graphics segments, and fell for the quarter in aerospace and graphics, leaving only automotive to post increased sales. Rockwell reported net income of Dollars 138.1m, or Dollars 0.62 per share for the fourth quarter against net income of Dollars 152.4m, or Dollars 0.66 per share, after a gain in the previous-year period. It reported a fiscal year net loss of Dollars 1.04bn, or Dollars 4.62 per share, after a charge of Dollars 6.78 per share on adoption of new accounting rules for post-retirement benefits.
