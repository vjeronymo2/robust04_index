931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Chepstow R'course rises to Pounds 140,500 Chepstow Racecourse raised pre-tax profits by 45 per cent, from Pounds 96,565 to Pounds 140,522, in the first half of 1993. Turnover grew from Pounds 917,595 to Pounds 929,240. The increase was largely attributable to additional income from Satellite Information Services. Earnings per share increased from a restated 17.4p to 24p.
