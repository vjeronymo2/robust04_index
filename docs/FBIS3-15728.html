Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The states that are Russia's creditors, united within the framework of the Paris Club, have agreed to prolong by four months the period of repayment of part of the Russian foreign debt amounting to $15 billion. The postponement applies both to the repayment of part of the credits and to the interest on them. All in all, Russia must repay $2-3 billion during the first four months of this year. However, the Paris Club has not calculated the exact amount yet.
