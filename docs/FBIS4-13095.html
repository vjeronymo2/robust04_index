BFN [Text] The executive council of the All Parties Hurriyat Kashmir Conference at its meeting in Muzaffarabad today reviewed the latest political situation prevailing in the occupied Jammu and Kashmir. The council strongly rebutted Indian claims that the leadership of the conference was engaged in secret negotiations with the Indian authorities. The council said this is a technique used to mislead the Kashmiri people, which are engaged in relentless struggle against repressive measures of Indian forces in occupied territory. The council said that the Kashmiri people will not be affected through such unfounded and fabricated media tricks aimed at spreading disinformation. The executive council of All Parties Hurriyat Conference called upon the Kashmiri people to step up their struggle against Indian subjugation till their rights are achieved.
