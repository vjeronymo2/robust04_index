920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / EC forces Italy to think anew A SPECIAL cabinet meeting of the outgoing Italian government has been called tomorrow to examine the economy following the EC's blunt warning that the country risked being unable to meet the requirements for economic and monetary union if it did not put its finances in order. The meeting will be little more than a symbolic act as the outgoing Christian Democrat coalition has been operating since last month's general election in a caretaker capacity.
