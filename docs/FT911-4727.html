910418 FT 18 APR 91 / The Lex Column: PSBR Yesterday's UK public sector borrowing requirement figures for March are a little worrying given that the recession looks likely to haunt the Treasury for some time yet. But bearing in mind that the Pounds 8bn deficit projected on Budget day looked out of date even then, the implications for government funding should not be exaggerated. With the British Telecom stake flotation up its sleeve, the government has plenty of room for manoeuvre. Its biggest concern might be the pressure placed on institutional liquidity by the competing demands of gilts, rights issues and give-away privatisation shares.
