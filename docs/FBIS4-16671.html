BFN [Decree No. 318 of the Russian Federation Government signed by Russian Government Chairman V. Chernomyrdin and dated Moscow, 14 April 1994: "Questions of the Customs Tariff Regulation of Foreign Economic Relations"] [Text] The Government of the Russian Federation resolves: 1. In partial amendment of Russian Federation Government Decree No. 196 of 10 March 1994 "On the Approval of Rates of Customs Import Duties" (SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, 1994, No. 11, p. 863), subparagraph one of paragraph one of the aforementioned decree shall read as follows: "1. To approve the rates of customs duties with regard to goods imported into the customs territory of the Russian Federation (hereinafter known as rates of customs import duties) according to the appendix and to enforce them: "for goods with regard to which rates of customs import duties are not to be increased -- from 15 March 1994; "for other goods -- from 1 July 1994." 2. The Russian Federation Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations, the Russian Federation Ministry of Finance, the Russian Federation Ministry of the Economy, the Russian Federation State Customs Committee, and the Russian Federation State Committee for Antimonopoly Policy and Support for New Economic Structures are to elaborate and present to the Government of the Russian Federation no later than the third quarter of 1994 proposals on stepping up interdepartmental coordination in the preparation of materials on the establishment and amendment of rates of customs import duties and also on a plan for their phased reduction as the position of Russian commodity producers strengthen when judged by internationally accepted practice. To envisage the priority reduction before the end of 1994 of groups of commodities [tovarnaya pozitsiya] for which the established rates of customs import duties exceed 30 percent of customs value. [signed] V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government.
