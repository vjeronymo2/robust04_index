BFN [Text] Sanandaj, Kurdistan Prov., June 18, IRNA -- The Islamic Movement Party (IMP) and Kurdistan Democratic Party (KDP) of Iraq repelled an attack by Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK) forces on their joint base in Asous region Friday, an IMP spokesman said here Saturday. According to the spokesman after being repelled, the PUK forces had reported to Iraq's National Congress troops stationed in the buffer zone between the belligerent parties, that the IMP-KDP forces had intended to attack them. Since the establishment of a ceasefire and deployment of Iraq's National Congress troops, the PUK forces have repeatedly violated the ceasefire under various pretexts, the spokesman added. PUK holds the view that Badenban region in the north and Souran in the south of Kurdistan should be partitioned and Kurdistan be administered as favored by the PUK.
