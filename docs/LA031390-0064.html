March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Transamerica Insurance Co. announced that is has signed an agreement to acquire the Completion Bond Co. from its owner, Bette Smith, for an undisclosed price. CBC is a provider of film and television production completion guarantees . . . The Commerce Department reported that the number of newly finished homes rose in January by 9.2% from December levels to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 1.42 million units . . . The Federal Reserve reported that the average yield for one-year T-bills, the most popular index for maing changes in adjustable rate mortgages, rose to 8.34% last week, up from 8.14% the week before.
