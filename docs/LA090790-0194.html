September 7, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

SAFETY KITS GIVEN TO 1,300 FIRST-GRADERS

First-graders in the Newport-Mesa Unified School District will begin the school year with a song in their hearts and a lesson on safety.

As part of an annual program, the Newport-Mesa Assn. of Realtors has distributed safety kits to the nearly 1,300 first-graders who attend school in the Newport-Mesa district. The kits include a coloring book and cassette tape with musical lessons on everything from staying away from the pool to never getting in a car with strangers.

"Our objective is for them to take this home and work with their parents and talk about the various situations," explained Rose Carey, a spokeswoman for the association. LAURA MICHAELIS
