940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: PHIT net assets rise to 54.91p Pacific Horizon Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 54.91p at the six month period ended January 31, up from 41.75p six months earlier and 36.44p at the comparable date last year. After exercise of warrants the figure was 54.09p. The trust, which is managed by Baillie Gifford, reported losses after tax of Pounds 6,723, down from Pounds 63,590. Losses per share came out at 0.02p (0.16p).
