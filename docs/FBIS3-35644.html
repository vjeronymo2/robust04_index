Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 29 (TURAN) -- Fighting along all front-lines continued yesterday, Azeri Defense Ministry reports. Last morning, Armenian units launched an offensive along the sou?hern sector of the front towards the town of Goradiz, but having suffered losses retreated. In the north, fighting in the vicinity of the city of Agdere (Mardakert), where the Armenian units try to beat Azeris out of their fortifications around the city, is also taking place. Towards the Agdam direction (south-west) Armenian units attempted to advance towards the city of Beylagan, but were stopped by th? artillery shelling. Two Armenian tanks and one armoured personnel carrier were destroyed, and twenty soldiers were killed.
