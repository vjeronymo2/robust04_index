Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 9 KYODO -- Honda Motor Co. said Wednesday [9 March] it will open a showroom in Beijing on April 6 to promote automobile sales in China. The showroom, the first such facility to be opened in Beijing by a Japanese automaker, will exhibit Legend and Accord models, Honda said. At the same time, Honda's Hong Kong-based sales subsidiary, Honda Motor (China) Co., will launch active automobile-marketing efforts in China, company officials said. The wholly owned subsidiary was established in February 1993 to market and service Honda motorcycles, for which Chinese demand is increasing, the officials said. Honda so far has sold automobiles in China through trading companies. The officials said Honda decided to have the subsidiary promote direct automobile marketing in China after Honda's auto exports to the country hit a record 12,200 vehicles last year.
