931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Miramar turns in 34% advance Miramar Hotel and Investment, 34.8 per cent owned by the Hong Kong Henderson Investment group, reported interim earnings jumped 50 per cent to HKDollars 40.3m (USDollars 5.2m) in the six months to September 30 compared with the year-ago period, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. Turnover rose 26 per cent to HKDollars 521.7m from HKDollars 412.7m. The interim dividend rises to 6 cents a share from 5 cents.
