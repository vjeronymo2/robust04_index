940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Eaglet Inv Trust Eaglet Investment Trust, which was floated last June, is raising about Pounds 8m net in a 1-for-3 rights issue at 113 1/2 p per share. Proceeds of the issue, which is fully underwritten by Greig Middleton, will be used to invest in the smaller companies sector.
