BFN [Text] Madrid, May 31 (XINHUA) -- Spanish Trade and Tourism Minister Javier Navarro said here today that his government regards China as one of the most favorite nations with which to develop economic and trade relations. Addressing a seminar on the potential of the Chinese market, Navarro urged Spanish entrepreneurs to strive to increase their investment in China and to promote bilateral trade. He pointed out that China is a developing nation with a population of over 1.1 billion, and that the Spanish government will assist businesses to take advantage of the opportunities available. Song Guoqing, the Chinese ambassador to Spain, and more than 200 Spanish businessmen attended the seminar.
