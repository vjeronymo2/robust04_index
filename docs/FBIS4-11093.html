BFN [Text] Louis Vianet, secretary general of the French General Confederation of Labor, arrived this afternoon in Cuba at the head of a high-ranking delegation. Upon his arrival at the Jose Marti International Airport, where he was met by Pedro Ross Leal, secretary general of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC] and other officials of the Cuban labor union movement, Vianet stated: I bring the solidarity of the French workers to the Cuban labor union movement. The members of this high-level delegation from France will also participate in various ceremonies in Havana City to salute 1 May, and will visit centers of economic and social interest on the island. It was also announced that so far, 389 delegates from 27 countries have confirmed their participation in Cuba's central activities marking International Workers Day. These guests belong to 59 labor union organizations from different parts of the world.
