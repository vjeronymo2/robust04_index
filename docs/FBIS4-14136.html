BFN [Text] The Central Bureau of Investigation [CBI] has found evidence that arms training was imparted in a neighboring country to some of the accused in last year's Bombay blasts. A CBI spokesman said that two people have been arrested in this connection. The bureau is said to have gathered more material against the prime accused in this case. It is alleged that the two arrested were sent abroad for training in handling explosives by another accused who is absconding. The CBI is reported to have made the breakthrough while checking the records of the regional passport office.
