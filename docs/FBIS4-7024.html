BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- "Our motherland under the wise guidance of the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il is making a long drive as a powerful country," said Chu Yong-il, vice-chairman of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification. He told KCNA: "I deem it a great honor and pleasure to have had an opportunity to visit the homeland and celebrate the birthday of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song. Seeing the motherland prospering, I feel my heart swell with pride in being a member of the Korean nation. "Thanks to our great leader and dear leader, my motherland has greeted prosperity without precedent in its history of five thousand years and become the most dignified country in the world. "There is no such person as the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song who has devoted everything to the people all his life, regarding `believing in the people as in heaven' as his motto. He has led our people to be the happiest and most proud and intelligent people. Our motherland owes its singlehearted unity to this care shown by him. "The building of an independent national economy, the embodiment of the chuche idea in the economy, has brought about a dazzling development of our motherland today and it has served as an unshakable foundation which is not affected by any worldwide economic fluctuation and threat from the imperialists."
