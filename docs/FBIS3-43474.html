Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Twenty-four hours after the commando attack on the Togolese Inter-Arm Regiment camp, calm broken by sporadic bursts of automatic gunfire now prevails in the capital, Lome. Streets and main highways of the city were deserted by its inhabitants who respected the government instructions. The curfew decreed yesterday evening is still in force from 1800 to 0600 and the land borders remain closed. The operation carried out by unidentified elements was preceded by an attempted attack on Head of State General Gnassingbe Eyadema.
