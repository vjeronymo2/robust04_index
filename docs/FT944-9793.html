941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Company News This Week: Unigate - Other Companies Flat interim profits of around Pounds 50m are expected from the food and distribution group on Wednesday, and an unchanged interim dividend of 6p after tough trading in the grocery sector and the deregulation of the milk market. This month the group, which delivers milk to 1.6m households in south and south-west England, raised its prices by 2p a pint to 40p.
