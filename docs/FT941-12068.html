940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Birkdale Difficult trading conditions coupled with 'unacceptably' high costs left Birkdale Group Pounds 1.47m in the red at the pre-tax level for the six months to end-September. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 494,000. Turnover of continuing activities rose to Pounds 10.4m (Pounds 8.16m) - the group runs marketing, advertising and public relations agencies. Net operating expenses rose to Pounds 6.68m (Pounds 4.76m). There was a Pounds 62,000 profit on the sale of discontinued business and an exceptional provision of Pounds 150,000, being a loss on a property sale. Losses per share emerged at 4.7p (2.2p).
