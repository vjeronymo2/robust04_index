May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: CAMARILLO;

TRAILER DUMPS SOD, CLOSING HIGHWAY

California 34 was closed Monday night after a tractor-trailer accidentally dumped its load of sod on the two-lane roadway east of Las Posas Road, the California Highway Patrol reported.

A CHP dispatcher said the driver lost control of his vehicle while heading toward Somis, and the truck ended up in a ditch alongside the highway. The dispatcher said she did not know if the driver was injured or if other vehicles were involved.

The road was closed for several hours Monday night because it required "extensive cleanup," the dispatcher said.
