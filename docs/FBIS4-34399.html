BFN [Interview with Colombian Defense Minister Rafael Pardo Rueda by unidentified reporter in Santa Fe de Bogota on 19 June; from the "NTC News" newscast -- live or recorded] [Text] [Reporter] There is relative normality on this election day. What is the final report you have? [Pardo] There have been some problems with regard to the destruction of voting material in District 1 in Cauca and another in northern Santander. There have been problems in approximately six to seven districts upon the arrival of Civil Registry delegates or those from the electoral organization. In the Sucre municipality, Sucre Department, a confrontation also took place between a group of rebels and electoral officials. All these sites are preparing to replace the material, while delegates have been appointed by the electoral organization to replace those who did not show up.
