940406 FT 06 APR 94 / Auditor warns on cashflow Two in five businesses in north-west England are risking receivership by failing to manage cashflow properly, says the latest quarterly survey by Latham Crossley & Davis, the Lancashire-based independent accountancy firm. All but 11 of 110 businesses in the survey sample used sophisticated computer software for their accounts, but 40 per cent did not use their computers to plan their cashflow. The survey showed 77 per cent of companies produce regular management accounts, 57 per cent produce profit-and-loss statements, 54 per cent regular balance sheets and 60 per cent cashflow reports.
