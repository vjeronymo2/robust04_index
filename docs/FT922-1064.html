920624 FT 24 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Sterling Industries falls to Pounds 3.5m IN LINE with comments issued in last year's statement, pre-tax profits at Sterling Industries, the engineering group, fell from Pounds 4.07m to Pounds 3.5m in the year to March 31. The downturn was blamed on the general economic climate, with the hydraulics division suffering the brunt of the slow-down and incurring an operating loss of Pounds 29,000 against a Pounds 755,000 profit last time. Combustion engineering was less affected, with a Pounds 1.83m (Pounds 2.43m) operating figure. Group turnover was down at Pounds 34.5m (Pounds 39.5m). Earnings came out at 7.45p (9.02p) and an unchanged final of 4.1p maintains the total at 5.6p.
