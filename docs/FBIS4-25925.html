BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 16 (XINHUA) -- Estonian President Lennart Meri ended his two-day visit to Shanghai and left here for Shenzhen by air this afternoon. Huang Ju, shanghai's mayor, met with Meri and his party and hosted a banquet in their honor at noon today. Meri said that Estonia hopes to strengthen its economic relations with Shanghai and cooperate with it in the fields of paper-making materials and harbors, among others. Meri and his party visited a wrist-watch factory and the China Foreign Exchange Center in the municipality this morning.
