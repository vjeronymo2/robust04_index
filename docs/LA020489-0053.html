February 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JERRY RICE KNOWS NOTHING ABOUT REAL EMPTINESS

So Jerry Rice feels an emptiness because he's not getting enough ink in the newspapers. And I always thought he was being paid handsomely for performing with his God-given talents.

Poor baby, maybe he should check out the local homeless. Then maybe he'll see what emptiness is all about.

FRANK CASILLAS

Mar Vista
