930612 FT 12 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The Pepper market was steady in active trading, reports Man Producten, while Far Eastern exporters, who are expecting smaller crops, were reluctant sellers. Muntok white pepper, spot, was available at Dollars 1,900 a tonne, with June/July at Dollars 1,800 and July/August at Dollars 1,825. White supplies from Sarawak, Thailand, China and Brazil were off the market. Among the black peppers Sarawak black label, spot, was on offer at Dollars 1,200 a tonne and June/July at Dollars 1,100. Sarawak yellow label, spot, was Dollars 1,250 a tonne and June/July Dollars 1,150. Brazilian grade 1, June/July, was Dollars 1,085 a tonne. Indian MG1, spot, was Dollars 1,225 a tonne and June, Dollars 1,100.
