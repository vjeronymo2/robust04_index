CSO [Text] Police antinarcotics officials seized 605 kg of opium in Shiraz, Darab, Lamard, and Nayriz in Fars Province. The Fars Police Precinct public relations official said on Saturday: This opium was seized during the last few days from passing vehicles, and the distribution agents were identified. According to him, several of the primary defendants involved in distributing the opium were arrested and turned over to judicial officials.
