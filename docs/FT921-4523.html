920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / UK News (Employment): 'Peace dividend' projects begin BUILDING work by English Estates, the government property developer, has begun on three factories intended to help offset the effect of 'peace dividend' job cuts on Barrow-in-Furness. The Cumbrian town needs to diversify its economy to cope with the loss of up to 7,000 jobs between 1990 and 1995 from its main employer VSEL, builder of the Trident submarine.
