940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / Pounds 1.2bn planned for cable schemes The UK cable television industry plans to spend Pounds 1.2bn this year in building the networks that carry both television channels and telecommunications services. A survey by the Cable Television Association found that members plan to build cable networks past 1.6m homes this year and 2m homes next year, giving a total of 6.6m homes with access to cable. The extra construction will increase the number of people in the industry by the end of the year to 16,000, compared with 10,100 at present.
