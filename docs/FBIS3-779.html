Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Afrikaner Weerstandsbeweging [Resistance Movement -- AWB] said in a statement this morning that it was disappointed by General Viljoen's decision to participate in the election. The statement, which was issued before the news of Gen. Viljoen's resignation, said that the AWB was forced to concede that those who had called Gen. Viljoen a political Judas -- I suppose sent by the ANC [African National Congress] and the South African Communist Party alliance -- had been correct.
