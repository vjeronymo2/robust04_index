BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk told Friday Ukrainian journalists that from the end of May US ballistic missiles would not be targeted at the republic. He said he obtained the information from U.S. officials. Kravchuk said that for its part, Ukraine would also observe the three-partite statement by the presidents of Ukraine, the United States and Russia and continue deactivation of the strategic missiles situated on its territory. Kravchuk said that all 46 SS-24 strategic missiles have been deactivated in Ukraine. All in all, there are 176 strategic missiles on the territory of the republic. Kravchuk said that earlier Ukrainian warheads "were targeted at the United States, but we did not control them". According to statements by the republic's leaders, Ukraine is unable technically to change the flight tasks for the missiles being deployed by Russia. But they could be launched from the territory of Ukraine only with consent of the republic's president. According to US Ambassador in Kiev, William Green Miller, Ukrainian officials confirmed to him that all strategic missiles on the republic's territory are not targeted at the United States.
