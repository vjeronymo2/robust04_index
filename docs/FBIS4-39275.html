BFN [Excerpt] Kiev, 8 Jun -- [passage omitted] DPRK Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam is in Kiev on an official visit. He told reporters at Borispol airport that "No sanctions, no pressure put on us will help a fair and unbiased solution to our nuclear problems. We are against nuclear arms. If the United States sincerely intends to solve the so-called nuclear problem, they should get down to the third round of Korean-American negotiations." "We welcome Ukraine's intention to become a nuclear-free state because we, too, are making every effort to make the Korean peninsula nuclear-free." [passage omitted]
