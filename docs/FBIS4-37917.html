BFN [Text] According to yesterday's issue of the newspaper AL-MU'TAMAR [Congress], three explosions have occurred in Baghdad, burning a poster showing Saddam Husayn, causing severe damage to a ruling party office, and damaging an office belonging to the Iraqi National Assembly in the city's al-Thawrah suburb. Iraqi opposition sources told AL-MU'TAMAR that the bombings were carried out by opposition fighters. The sources added that the regime's security forces were unable to harm these fighters. The security situation in Baghdad and its suburbs is disorganized to an unprecedented degree.
