BFN [By Business-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 15 TASS -- The first issue of a new economic weekly entitled "CIS: COMMON MARKET" was released on Friday timed to coincide with another CIS summit which opened in Moscow today. The weekly was prepared with the assistance of the ITAR-TASS news agency. The aim of the new newspaper is to organise common information space in the economic sphere. A decision to publish the weekly was adopted by heads of government of the 12 Commonwealth States in Ashgabat last December.
