920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Lotus sheds 70 more jobs LOTUS CARS, General Motors' troubled UK sports car subsidiary, is to shed a further 70 jobs, bringing the total lost since the middle of last year to 370, or more than 40 per cent of the work force. Lotus said Lotus Engineering and Millbrook Proving ground, its research and development and consultancy engineering subsidiaries, were unaffected. They also escaped the earlier cuts.
