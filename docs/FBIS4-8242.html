BFN [Text] Tirana, April 7 (ATA) -- "Albania is grateful to the Council of Europe [CE], its member states, and the European institutions for the assistance it has received from them," President Berisha said in a meeting he had yesterday at the presidency with the Council of Europe delegation headed by Lord Finsberg. President Berisha said that as it has been asserted "the reforms in Albania have been advancing quicker than in other countries in the past two years," adding that Albania fulfills all the conditions for becoming a member of the Council of Europe: free elections, observation of the human rights and the rights of national minorities. "Albania is determined in further cooperation with the Council of Europe and its integration into Europe," President Berisha emphasized. Lord Finsberg appreciated the profound changes made in the course of reforms and said he would like to see Albania be as soon as possible a full member of the Council of Europe.
