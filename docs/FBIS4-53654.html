BFN ["National Day of Cameroon" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- Pyongyang-based papers today observe the national day of Cameroon. The Cameroonian people who have registered successes in the endeavours to consolidate the national independence in the past days are today making energetic efforts to promote the national unity and the stability of the country against the interference of outside forces and develop the economy, MINJU CHOSON in a signed article says, and goes on: Pursuing the non-aligned policy externally, the Cameroonian Government is strengthening the friendly and cooperative relations with many countries and striving for peace in Africa. Though Korea and Cameroon are far away from each other geographically, their peoples have long since forged and developed the friendly and cooperative relations. Believing that these relations between Korea and Cameroon will further develop in the future, the Korean people wish the Cameroonian people greater success in the work for national unity and economic development.
