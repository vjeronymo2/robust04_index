November 30, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

MISSOURI KANSAS CITY GETS HOT IN SECOND HALF TO ROLL PAST SCC

Missouri Kansas City rallied in the second half for an 88-69 victory over Southern California College in a nonconference men's basketball game Thursday at Kansas City.

Southern California (3-2) led at the half, 35-34, but Missouri Kansas City shot 62% in the second half to pull away.

Ronnie Schmitz scored 18 points for Missouri Kansas City (3-2).

Kent Denmon scored 15 points and Anthony Davis added 12 points and a game-high 14 rebounds for Missouri Kansas City. Terry Dickerson and David Robinson added 10 points each for the Kangaroos, who have a nine-game home winning streak.

Jeff Bickmore scored 23 and Mike Henjum added 16 for SCC.

In the Azusa Pacific tournament:

Colorado Christian 97, Pacific Christian 76 -- Phil Garcia scored 21 points for Pacific Christian, which lost in the first round.

Kenny Crawford added 20 and Kenny Kao 11 for Pacific Christian.
