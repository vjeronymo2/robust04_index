931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Ruberoid offer oversubscribed The share offer for Ruberoid, the roofing subsidiary Tarmac is spinning off to the market, has been oversubscribed. A further announcement as to the level of subscription and the basis of allocation of shares is expected to be made today. More than 30m shares - 65 per cent of the issue - are being placed firm with institutions. The balance of 16.5m shares is being placed under the offer. At the flotation price of 150p, Ruberoid was valued at about Pounds 72m. The issue is expected to raise Pounds 68.8m for existing shareholders, principally Tarmac subsidiaries, and Pounds 2m for the company.
