930826 FT 26 AUG 93 / French undecided about Balladur The French are in two minds about their prime minister, Mr Edouard Balladur, Reuter reports from Paris. A poll in the the weekly l'Express shows his support up 10 points to 64 per cent. Another poll in the weekly l'Evenement du Jeudi showed a nine-point fall to 40 per cent in approval of his economic policies.
