July 24, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

The life of movie legend Charles Chaplin is chronicled in a free photographic exhibit opening today at the Samuel Goldwyn Theatre in Beverly Hills. The exhibit, under the auspices of the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, is in recognition of the 100th anniversary of Chaplin's birth. It will be on view weekdays through Sept. 1. Claudia Puig
