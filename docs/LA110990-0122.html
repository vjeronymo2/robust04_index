November 9, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Ex-Merrill Lynch Official Admits Payoffs: Albert Young, 61, of New York and Hawley, Pa., a former Merrill vice president, pleaded guilty to mail fraud, bribery and tax evasion. Young said he received about $127,500 in payoffs from contractors who built the brokerage's headquarters in Manhattan's World Financial Center. He faces up to 18 years in prison and a fine of $1 million.
