920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / Indonesia opposition in show of strength INDONESIA'S populist opposition party, campaigning against government corruption and press censorship, claimed a street turnout of 3m supporters yesterday - the last campaign day in the capital before the June 9 election, Reuter reports from Jakarta. This is the biggest street demonstration since general election campaigning started three weeks ago. 'The biggest enemy is corruption not inflation,' leaders of the Indonesian Democratic party (PDI) told a big crowd in Jakarta. They said police reported that 3m PDI supporters had gone into the streets, dwarfing Saturday's estimated 1m people campaigning for the ruling Golkar party.
