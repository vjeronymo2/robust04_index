May 20, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: BUCKMAN SPRINGS

A 75-year-old Mount Laguna man died Saturday afternoon in an accident on Old Highway 80 near Buckman Springs, the California Highway Patrol reported.

The man, whose identity was not released, was driving east when his car drifted off the road and overturned. He died at the scene.
