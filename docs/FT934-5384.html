931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Tops Ests falls to Pounds 0.83m Tops Estates, the shop and office property company, yesterday announced a 30 per cent rise in rental income from Pounds 5.25m to Pounds 6.84m for the six months to September 30, but saw pre-tax profits fall from Pounds 918,000 to Pounds 825,000, writes David Blackwell. The group, which is lifting the interim dividend by 5 per cent from 0.5775p to 0.6064p, lifted earnings per share from 1.33p to 1.41p. However, the earnings increase reflected a lower tax charge of Pounds 175,000, down from a previous Pounds 305,000. Mr Everard Goodman, chairman, said the six months had been 'a progressive period' for the group, which would continue to grow. He described the consistent rise in consumer spending as encouraging. Net interest charges rose from Pounds 3.55m to Pounds 5.16m. Mr Goodman said high interest borrowings of Pounds 16m would be repaid next May 'removing this short-term burden.'
