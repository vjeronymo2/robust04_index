BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 27 June TASS -- An unspecified number of Tajik opposition militants were killed over the weekend when Russian border guards in Tajikistan shelled the frontier with Afghanistan cutting short three armed attempts to break into the Central Asian republic. The Russian frontier troops in Tajikistan said on Monday the strikes were delivered on river crossing areas by border guards of the Pyandzh and Moskovskiy Districts. None of the Russian soldiers were wounded.
