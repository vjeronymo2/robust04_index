920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Estates & General Shares of Estates & General yesterday fell 16p to 29p after the property investor and developer announced that it did not expect to make a pre-tax profit in the half year to June 30 and was therefore unlikely to declare an interim dividend. It made Pounds 1.48m pre-tax in the comparable half and paid an interim of 1.225p.
