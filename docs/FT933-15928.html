930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / Iran seeks Dollars 670m for metro project IRAN is seeking Dollars 670m of investment by Chinese, Korean and European companies to complete the two main lines of Tehran's underground railway, the head of the Tehran Metro authority said yesterday, Reuter reports from Nicosia. Iran's IRNA news agency quoted the official, Mr Asghar Ebrahimi, as saying Chinese companies would invest Dollars 300m of the total. He was speaking while accompanying China's vice-premier Li Lanqing on a tour of Metro construction sites. Mr Ebrahimi also said that China would provide Dollars 120m in credits to build subway carriages. Other agreements with Chinese enterprises covered the building of a 145MW gas-fired power plant and electricity distribution networks, he said.
