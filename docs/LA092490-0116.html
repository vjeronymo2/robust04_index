September 24, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

CANDIDATE CALLS FOR VIGILANTE CORPS TO BATTLE N.Y. CRIME

Maverick GOP gubernatorial candidate Pierre Rinfret today called for armed vigilantes to battle crime in New York City.

"What I really want is a vigilante system with people carrying guns, unknown to anybody, so anybody who comes along and mugs somebody may have, standing next to him, a man who's been authorized," Rinfret said.

Aides later said the Manhattan-based economist envisioned a volunteer corps of up to 100,000 police-trained vigilantes.

A spokesman for Gov. Mario M. Cuomo, Rinfret's opponent, dismissed the proposal as dangerous.
