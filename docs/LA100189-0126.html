October 1, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

LINDBERGH HORRORS

The brains have fallen out of Mr. Roberts' head. They must have landed next to Mr. Filner's on the floor of a Boeing 747 on a collision course with the Laurel Travel Center.

Their developer-buddy's multiairport proposal calling for four airports to be maxed-out to capacity to handle our forecast air traffic needs (Lindbergh, Brown, Palomar and Montgomery) is ludicrous. This is not a solution. It's another way of keeping special-interest groups happy.

How stupid do they think we are?

Our two councilmen haven't even finished the year-old airport course at Sandag, yet they've already flunked out.

JO ANN ZOLEZZI

San Diego
