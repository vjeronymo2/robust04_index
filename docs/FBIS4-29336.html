BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 17 KYODO -- North Korea is ready to propose a compromise package to the United States to solve the standoff over its nuclear program if bilateral talks are held, a Japanese politician Friday [17 June] quoted a senior North Korean official as saying. Kim Yong-sun, (North) Korean Workers' Party Secretary in charge of International Affairs, made the remarks in a meeting late Thursday with Taku Yamasaki, former director general of the Defense Agency and a Liberal Democratic Party (LDP) Diet member, Yamasaki told reporters.
