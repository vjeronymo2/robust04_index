December 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ANGELOVIC AMONG SIX OCC PLAYERS TO CHOOSE SCHOOLS

Six members of the Orange Coast College football team agreed to attend four-year colleges, OCC announced.

Quarterback Greg Angelovic and linebacker Ernie Goddard have signed with Utah State, defensive back Dwayne Armstrong will attend Iowa State, offensive lineman Scott Craig and linebacker John Kelley are going to Northern Arizona, and wide receiver Patrick Reilly will play for UC Davis.

Angelovic passed for 1,398 yards and nine touchdowns in leading OCC to an 8-3 record and its first bowl game appearance since 1975.

Goddard led OCC in tackles with 102. Armstrong had 90 tackles from his strong safety position and led the team with four fumble recoveries. Craig is 6 feet 4 and 290 pounds.

Kelly, a two-year starter, was fourth on the team in tackles with 79. Reilly had 17 catches, one for a touchdown.
