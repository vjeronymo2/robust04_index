BFN [Text] Beijing, June 14 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, state councillor and secretary general of the State Council, left here today on a visit to Cyprus, Tunisia and Libya at the invitation of the governments of the three countries. During his visit, Luo will discuss with leaders of the three countries on issues of bilateral relations as well as regional and international affairs of common concern, so as to further promote the friendly cooperative relations between China and the three countries.
