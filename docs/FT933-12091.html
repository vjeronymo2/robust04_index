930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / The Week Ahead: Diary Dates PARLIAMENTARY DIARY TODAY Commons: Questions to Transport ministers, Public Accounts Commission, Commons Commission and Commons Leader. Summer adjournment debate. Consolidated Fund (Appropriation) Bill, all stages, followed by an all-night session of backbench debates. Lords: Statute Law Repeals Bill, third reading. Pension Schemes Bill, third reading. Pension Schemes (Northern Ireland) Bill, third reading. Highland Regional Council (Wester Bridge) Order Confirmation Bill, third reading. Education Bill, Commons amendments. Criminal Justice Bill, Commons amendments. Agriculture Bill, Commons amendments. Select committee: Employment, subject - the management of redundancies. witness: Mr Neil Clarke, chairman, British Coal (5pm, room 15). TOMORROW Commons: Short backbench debates. Lords: Finance Bill, all stages. Criminal Justice Bill, Commons amendments. Debate on call for aid to Refugee Council. WEDNESDAY Commons: Not sitting. Lords: Noise and Statutory Nuisance Bill, committee. Defence estimates debate. Select committee: Health, subject - public expenditure on health and social services. Witness: Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary (4.15pm, room 8). The Commons rises for the summer break tomorrow and the Lords on Wednesday. Peers return on October 11 and MPs on October 18.
