Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow, 20 January -- Viktor Chernomyrdin has read out a decree by the President of Russia on immediate changes in the composition of the government which he received just before the end of today's hour-long press conference. The decree says: To appoint on the proposal of the Chairman of the government: Oleg Soskovets as first deputy chairman of the government; Aleksandr Zaveryukha -- deputy chairman of the government; Anatoliy Chubays -- deputy chairman of the government; Yuriy Yarov -- deputy chairman of the government; Eduard Nechayev -- minister of health and the medical industry; Viktor Khlystun -- minister of agriculture and food; Sergey Shakhray -- minister for nationalities affairs and regional policy; Sergey Shoygu -- minister for civil defense, emergencies and natural disasters; Aleksandr Shokhin -- minister of economics. Vladimir Shumeyko is to be released from the post of first deputy chairman of the government at his request in connection with his election to the post of chairman of the Federation Council. To release Yegor Gaydar from the post of first deputy chairman of the government at his own request; To release Boris Fedorov, Sergey Shakhray, and Aleksandr Shokhin from the posts of deputy chairman of the government in connection with the alteration in the structure of the government; In connection with the alteration in the structure of the federal bodies of executive power, to release Vasiliy Voznyak, Vladimir Kuramin, Vladimir Mashchits, and Ivan Chernoyvanov from the posts of chairmen of state committees of the Russian Federation.
