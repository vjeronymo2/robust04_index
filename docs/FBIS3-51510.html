Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hour" newscast] [Excerpts] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has appointed Beatriz Paredes Rangel president of the National Commission for the Overall Development of and Social Justice for Indian Peoples [Comision Nacional de Desarrollo Integral y Justicia Social para los Pueblos Indigenas], which was created by Presidential Agreement on Wednesday [19 January] as published in the official gazette. At 2000 President Salinas held a 30-minute meeting with Mrs. Paredes and representatives of several churches, indigenous community leaders, prominent intellectual figures, authors, actors, and other Mexican citizens to install the advisory commission for the National Commission for Overall Development and Social Justice for Indigenous Peoples. The meeting took place in the Vicente Guerrero Room at Los Pinos, the official presidential residence. The advisory commission held its first meeting at Los Pinos, which lasted two hours, prior to meeting with President Salinas. [passage omitted] President Salinas is scheduled to meet with 42 representatives of more than 200 indigenous groups this Thursday. The official list of members of the National Commission for Overall Development and Social Justice for Indigenous Peoples will be completed in the next few days.
