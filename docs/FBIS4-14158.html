BFN [Letter from the Kurdistan Democratic Party's Central Public Relations Office to the London-based Arabic daily AL-HAYAH; date not given] [Text] To the esteemed AL-HAYAH newspaper: Subject: Explanation Greetings: In its issue No. 11,386 dated 20 April 1994, your newspaper published an article by Kamiran Qurrah Daghi under the headline "Barzani Sets Three Conditions for Acceptance of Damascus Invitation." In order to shed light and explain the truth about what was published in the article, we ask that the following explanation be published: 1. Our party's relations with Syria are strong. They are based on mutual respect and understanding. We attach great importance to these relations. 2. His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad has kindly received Mr. Barzani during all his visits to Syria. Mr. Barzani has strong and close relations with President Hafiz al-Asad. 3. We would like to explain that Barzani would not set conditions for visiting Syria. He regards Syria as his own country. The doors of Damascus have always been and still are open and there is no need for friends to set conditions among themselves. Please accept our regards. Signed: The Central Public Relations Office
