BFN [Text] Lebanese Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul has said that the bomb that exploded in the Sayyidat al-Najat Church and killed 11 people last month was made at the headquarters of the banned Lebanese Forces militia in the presence of Fu'ad Malik, senior aide to Samir Ja'ja'. In an interview with MBC, Dallul said that he believes that the objective behind the explosion was to create sedition in the country. He added: [Begin Dallul recording] The measures taken by the government were correct. From the security aspect, there was a coup attempt in every sense of the word, an attempt that served the Israeli enemy. There were attempts to undermine the homeland. What happened was very serious and it is up to the judiciary to decide on it. [end recording]
