920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Petbow management in Pounds 13m buy-out Management of the Petbow group, a manufacturer of diesel generator sets, has concluded a buy-out from FLS Industries, its Danish parent. Financing for the deal - a total of Pounds 13m - has been led by 3i, the investment capital group. Barclays Development Capital, BZW Buy-Out Trust II and Bank of Scotland have also participated. The new group will be renamed Power Group International.
