BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 21/4/1994 -- After his arrival Wednesday evening, 20 April, for an official visit in Romania, on which occasion he will attend the Bucharest conference of the Crans Montana Forum, Israel's Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres was received Thursday, 22 April, by the president of Romania, Ion Iliescu, at the Cotroceni Palace. The beginning of the dialogue between the two high dignitaries confirmed the news that last night Shim'on Peres met in Bucharest with Palestinian Leader Yasir 'Arafat, the talks between the two lasting till late in the night. Their dialogue will continue, as a matter of fact, during the day, as the chief of the Israeli diplomacy himself confirmed. Present at the meeting at Cotroceni Palace were Romanian Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu and Traian Chebeleu, counselor to the president of Romania.
