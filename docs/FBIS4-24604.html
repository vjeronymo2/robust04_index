BFN [Text] Yaounde, 25 May (XINHUA) -- Bipoun Woum, Cameroonian cultural minister, and Franses Enkuayin, External Ministry Foreign Envoys Department director [name as received] today met separately with a Chinese Government cultural delegation, led by Vice Culture Minister Chen Changben. At the meetings, the two sides spoke highly of the friendly cooperation in the cultural field between the two countries, and expressed the desire for further development of such cooperation. The Chinese delegation arrived here this morning, and will visit cultural and educational facilities in the country.
