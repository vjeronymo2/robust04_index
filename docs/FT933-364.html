930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Amberley makes Pounds 6m purchase AMBERLEY Group, the USM-quoted building services concern, has made the first move in its intended growth by acquisition with the purchase of Lawrence Industries. It is also raising Pounds 6.7m net through a placing and open offer. It is paying a maximum of Pounds 6.27m, of which Pounds 200,000 is dependent on net asset value and Pounds 700,000 profit-related. The initial payment of Pounds 5.27m will be satisfied by Pounds 4.19m cash and the balance in the shares. Lawrence, an importer of processed mineral and fibre products, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 291,000 on turnover of Pounds 952,000 in the four months to July 31 having reported Pounds 697,000 profits on Pounds 2.16m turnover in the previous nine months. The placing is of 17.6m shares at 38p, of which 6.44m is being offered to shareholders on a 1-for-1 basis. The proceeds will be used for the initial cash payment, with the rest used for expenses of the purchase and reducing borrowings.
