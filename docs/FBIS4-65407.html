BFN [Text] Today Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said the Greek position on Skopje's [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] name has been stated and the Greek Government is not under any pressure. This was in reference to today's press reports and to UN mediator Cyrus Vance's meeting with Ambassador Khristos Zakharakis, the Foreign Ministry secretary general. He said the government is talking with everyone who has undertaken any initiative to resolve the issue and is seeking solutions. Venizelos reiterated that Greece remains firm in its positions, and discussed the obvious U.S. interest toward a Skopje solution.
