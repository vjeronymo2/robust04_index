December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $60 when the front horse in his $10 triple bets, Open Hand, ran third. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical bankroll of $1,000, is now down to $897.30. This total reflects a $20 adjustment for Sunday's bet when he selected a losing $10 exacta box on Nijinsky's Lover and Lucky Kate. However, There was no exacta betting on the race.

Tor today, Bob's bet bet is a $20 parlay from Flippable in the fifth to Patchy Groundfog in the eighth.

Wednesday's Bankroll: $957.30

Wednesday's loss: $60.00
