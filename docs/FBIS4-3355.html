BFN [Text] Qingdao, April 2 (XINHUA) -- The Sixth China International Co-Publication Fair is to be held from April 20 to 25 in Qingdao, a coastal city in east China's Shandong Province. More than 120 overseas publishers from Britain, the United States, Japan, Singapore, the Republic of Korea, Malaysia, Taiwan and Hong Kong and 180 Chinese mainland publishers will take part in the fair. They will exhibit their new books and hold talks on cooperative publishing, copyright transfer, book trading and printing. China has successfully held five such fairs since 1989 and signed more than a thousand contracts with foreign publishers.
