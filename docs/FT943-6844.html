940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: London City has 85% of Towles London City Equities now controls 85 per cent of the voting rights of all shares in Towles. All offers have been declared unconditional. London City now speaks for 83.08 per cent of the ordinary, 82.21 per cent of the A ordinary, 89.48 per cent of the A preference and 87.8 per cent of the B preference.
