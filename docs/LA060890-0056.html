June 8, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

HARASSMENT AT NAVAL ACADEMY

The command structure at Annapolis that countenanced the climate that led to the outrageous attack on Dreyer, then winked at the perpetrators, should be immediately reassigned.

As for those "good-natured" midshipmen, participants and the gawkers who could have stopped the stupidity, let them see what it's like to be ordinary seamen for a year or two. Maybe by then they will have a better understanding of the damage they have done to Ms. Dreyer and be more worthy of becoming officers.

FRED BENES

Cambria
