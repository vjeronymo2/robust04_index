BFN [Text] A DPRK military delegation held talks with a high-ranking military delegation of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam [SRV] in Pyongyang today. Attending the talks from our side were Comrade O Chin-u, member of the Political Bureau Presidium of the Workers' Party of Korea Central Committee and minister of the People's Armed Forces; Vice Marshal Kim Kwang-chin; and generals, including Colonel General Pak Chae-kyong, Lieutenant Gen. Pak Sung-won; and officers of the Korean People's Army. Attending from the other side were Gen. Doan Khue, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam and ministry of defense, who is the delegation head; and delegation members such as Lt. Gen. Phan Thu, vice minister of defense; Lt. Gen. Le Hai, deputy director of the General Political Bureau of the Vietnamese People's Army; Lt. Gen. Tran Hanh, deputy chief of the General Staff; other members; and Duong Chinh Thuc, SRV's ambassador to our country. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
