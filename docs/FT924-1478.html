921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Kvaerner deal Kvaerner, Norwegian engineering, shipping and shipbuilding group, has won a contract worth NKr400m (Pounds 39m) for fabricating the drilling module for the Heidrun production platform in the North Sea, writes Robert Taylor in Stockholm. The order from Conoco Norway, the field's development operator, will be carried out in Kvaerner's yard at Egersund south of Stavanger by July 1994.
