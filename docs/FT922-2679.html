920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / UK Company News: BBA expands in US airport facilities BBA Group, the automotive and engineering and aviation services group, is buying Butler Aviation International. The purchase is being made through Page Avjet Airport Services, its US subsidiary. Butler, based in Dallas, Texas, operates corporate aircraft terminals and airline support facilities. It will lift Page Avjet's facilities in the US from 20 operations at 18 airports to 42 operations at 38 airports. On a pro-forma basis, the enlarged group would have achieved sales of some Dollars 260m (Pounds 142m) for the 1991 year.
