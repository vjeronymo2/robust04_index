920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Helene shows 15% rise to Pounds 812,000 A 15 per cent expansion in first half profits was yesterday reported by Helene, the clothing distributor. On turnover ahead to Pounds 36.7m (Pounds 33.2m), the pre-tax line for the six months to June 30 amounted to Pounds 812,000 (Pounds 706,000). Earnings per share improved to 0.71p (0.56p); the interim dividend is held at 0.65p.
