BFN [YEDI'OT AHARONOT poll conducted by the Dahaf Institute headed by Dr. Mena Tzemah] [Text] Should or should not the settlers be evacuated from the town of Hebron? Should be evacuated -- 35 percent; should not be evacuated -- 60 percent; did not answer -- 5 percent. Should or should not the government agree to the stationing of an armed international force in the territories? Should agree -- 35 percent; should not agree -- 60 percent; did not answer -- 5 percent. The poll which was conducted on Tuesday and Wednesday this past week encompassed 503 interviewees constituting a representative sample of the entire adult population in Israel. Maximal poll error is 4 percent.
