Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 20 (XINHUA) -- Slovak Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar arrived here by plane today on a five-day official visit to China, at the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Peng. This is the first visit by a Slovak Government leader since the country gained independence. Meciar's entourage, numbering nearly 60 members, includes Slovak Deputy Prime Minister Sergei Kozlik, Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Julius Toth, the minister of the economy, the minister of culture and some businessmen. Chairman of the Chinese Government reception committee and Vice-Minister of the Electronics Industry Lu Xinkui and Vice-Foreign Minister Dai Bingguo greeted the Slovak prime minister at the airport.
