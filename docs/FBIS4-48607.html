BFN [Text] Mmabatho May 21 SAPA -- North West Executive Commitee member Rocky Malebana Metsing said on Saturday his administration would not hesitate to confiscate land, without compensation, from farmers who allowed AWB [Afrikaner Weerstandsbeweging; Afrikaner Resistance Movement] activities on their farms, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. Addressing a meeting at Bapong near Brits on Saturday Mr Metsing, whose portfolio is agriculture, said the government would not allow farm land to be used for storing weapons or for training members of the Afrikaner Weerstandsbeweging's military wing. Referring to people who were forceably removed from their land under apartheid laws, he said the state would do all in its power to ensure they got their land back.
