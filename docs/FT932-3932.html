930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Alphameric cuts loss to Pounds 321,000 Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 321,000 were announced by Alphameric, the information technology group, for the year to March 31, with losses down to Pounds 90,000 in the second half. Losses for the 1991-92 year were Pounds 2.08m. Mr Alan Benjamin, chairman, said the group was now trading profitably and the higher level of orders and business experienced in the latter part of last year had been sustained. Sales were ahead 33 per cent at Pounds 6.83m (Pounds 5.14m) with acquisitions accounting for Pounds 1.16m. Losses per share came to 1.4p (28.8p).
