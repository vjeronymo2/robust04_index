BFN [Text] Asyut, 7 Apr (MENA)--Asyut Governor Samih al-Sa'id has praised local leaders who helped in the surrender of a number of extremist leaders to security organs. He said that these extremists have repented and returned to the right path in society. His remarks came after six extremist terrorists voluntarily surrendered themselves to the security organs in Asyut Governorate after becoming convinced that the wrong ideas they believed in were corrupt. The six repentant terrorists are: Rushdi Sayyid Muhammad, Hasan 'Abd-al-Rasul, Hasan Ibrahim Mahmud, 'Abd-al-Badi' Rashwan, 'Abd-al-Halim 'Ali 'Abd-al-Halim, and al-Qadhdhafi 'Abd-al-Raziq 'Ali. The Asyut governor was present at a religious celebration attended by more than 10,000 people from the governorate's various towns and villages. They strongly denounced extremism and violence and declared solidarity with the security organs in the confrontation with this phenomenon that is alien to Egyptian society.
