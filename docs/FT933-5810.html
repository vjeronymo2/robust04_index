930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / International Company News: Tubemakers surges TUBEMAKERS, the Australian manufacturer, has raised its annual dividend to 10 cents a share from 7 cents after a strong earnings performance in the year to June, achieved despite modest sales growth, writes Bruce Jacques. The company, 49 per cent controlled by BHP, yesterday reported a 66 per cent net increase in earnings to ADollars 43.3m (USDollars 28.9m) from ADollars 26.2m on a 3 per cent sales rise to ADollars 1.16bn from ADollars 1.13bn. The result followed tax provisions of ADollars 23.3m, against ADollars 13.7m, and depreciation of ADollars 22.2m, against ADollars 31.2m. Interest expenses fell marginally to ADollars 14.8m from ADollars 15.6m.
