930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Best liquoring East Africans remained fully firm reports the Tea Broker's Association, while brighter mediums attracted improved competition at dearer rates. Plainer descriptions were barely steady. Prices for Ceylon teas showed little change. Offshore only fair demand and mostly at lower levels except the brighter Zimbabwe offerings which proved appreciably dearer. Quotations: quality 155p/kg, nom good medium 132p/kg, medium 105p/kg, low medium 85p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 165p for a Rwanda pd.
