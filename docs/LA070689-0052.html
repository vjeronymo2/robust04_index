July 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST: CUDAHY;

FUNDS FROM CITY, COUNTY USED TO BUILD SWIM POOL

Cudahy received $50,000 from the county to help construct an outdoor swimming pool in Lugo Park, 7810 Otis Ave., Supervisor Ed Edelman announced. "This money, together with $170,000 Cudahy officials are using, will pay for construction of the aquatic complex," Edelman said.
