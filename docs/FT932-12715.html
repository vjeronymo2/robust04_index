930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Lowland Investment net asset value rises Net asset value at Lowland Investment Company increased from 201.2p to 252.3p over the 12 months to March 31. At September 30 1992 it stood at 200.8p. Net revenue for the six months to the end of March was lower at Pounds 899,000, compared with Pounds 983,000. Earnings per share came out at 3.82p (4.19p), but the interim dividend is raised from 3p to 3.2p. Directors said the final should be at least maintained at 5.5p.
