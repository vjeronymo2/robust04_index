931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Net asset value up at Scottish Asian The Scottish Asian Investment Company saw net asset value rise 65.7 per cent in the year to July 31, from 137p to 227p, adjusted for the 4-for-1 scrip issue made earlier in the year. The attributable loss rose from Pounds 42,000 to Pounds 92,000. Losses per share came out at 0.53p (0.24p).
