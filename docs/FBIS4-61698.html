BFN [Text] The collection of signatures among the Ukrainian citizens in support of the contenders to the post of the Ukrainian president was concluded on 6 May. Reliable sources told the agency INTERFAX-Ukraine that according to preliminary data, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk, President of the Industrialists and Entrepreneurs Union and former premier Leonid Kuchma, the parliament ex-speaker Ivan Plyushch, Education Minister Petro Talanchuk, leader of the socialist party Aleksandr Moroz have collected the necessary number of signatures (100,000 in all 27 electoral districts on the presidential elections, including not less than 1500 in 18 districts). There is no information about other contenders. As many as nine contenders were collecting signatures of the Ukrainian citizens. Within five days the district electoral commissions are to verify the correctness of the signatures' collection and sum up the final results. In accordance with the decree On Elections of Ukrainian President, after that district commissions are to forward their protocols to Ukraine's central election commission which is to adopt its decision on registration of the candidates within a week. Elections of the Ukrainian president are scheduled for 26 June.
