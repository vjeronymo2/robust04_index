BFN [Text] As of 20 June, of the 7,300,000 eligible Belarusian voters, 41,000 have already cast their ballot for Belarusian president. Based on results from the six days of early voting, Central Electoral Commission Chairman Abramovich told a news conference that election turnout will not be high. On election day [23 June], updates on the voting will arrive at the commission every four hours. The final results will be made public within ten days of the election.
