BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Konstantin Zhukovskiy] [Text] Moscow, 16 May -- Guido Di Tella, minister of foreign relations and worship [title as receieved] of Argentina, arrived in Moscow today from St. Petersburg. Today, he is to have talks with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. It is expected that the meeting will result in the ratification of an agreement on the fundamentals of bilateral relations and that an agreement on the reciprocal abolition of the visa practice of both countries will be signed. A group of Argentinian businessmen are accompanying the minister on his trip around Russia.
