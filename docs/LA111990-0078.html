November 19, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

SURELOCK lost $120 Sunday when he came up with only one of the correct horses in each of his two exacta boxes.

SURELOCK's Sun. Bankroll: $2,108

Sunday's Loss: $120

Current Bankroll: $1,988

Bob Mieszerski lost $72 when his $9 triples got off to a bad start in the fourth race.

Bob's Sun. Bankroll: $1,677.20

Sunday's Loss: $72

Current Bankroll: $1,605.20

Both started with $2,000 bankrolls.
