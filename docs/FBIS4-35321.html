BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 28 May (WAKH)--Engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al- 'Attas, envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, arrived in Abu Dhabi tonight on a visit to the United Arab Emirates as part of a tour that has thus far included Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia, and Oman.
