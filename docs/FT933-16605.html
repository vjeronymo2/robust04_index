930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Umeco Umeco, the USM-quoted maker of aircraft refuellers and aerospace components distributor, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 211,000 for the year to March 27, against Pounds 312,000. Turnover was Pounds 11m (Pounds 9.95m) with earnings per share of 2.27p (3.3p). A proposed unchanged final dividend of 1p makes a maintained total of 1.75p.
