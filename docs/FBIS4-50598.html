BFN [From the "National Hookup" program] [Text] The Urumqi Railway Intermediate People's Court recently announced judgment on a serious case of theft committed by a unit's own staff members. The chief culprit (Ou Qilin) was sentenced to death in accordance with the law. From the end of 1992 to the end of 1993, (Ou Qilin), a former commodity inspector of Sulehe station, Liuyuan Locomotive Section, took advantage of his position to repeatedly steal more than 60 kinds of railway transportation materials in collaboration with other people, with a total value of 70,000 yuan. He himself took goods worth more than 30,000 yuan and was sentenced to death. From March to August last year, (Bao Yunhai), a cleaner of Qiaowan station, Liuyuan Locomotive Section, repeatedly stole 14 kinds of transportation materials in collaboration with other people, with a total value 43,000 yuan. He himself took goods worth some 20,000 yuan and was sentenced to life imprisonment. (Hu Wenjun), (Liu Xinting), (Hao Shaowen), (Chen Yuanwen), and (Gao Shangyou) who also took part in the activities, were sentenced to imprisonment.
