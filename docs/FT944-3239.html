941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: First Technology jumps to Pounds 2.8m Shares in First Technology rose 34p to 364p yesterday as the company, which supplies sensors and crash test dummies to the car industry, announced a 60 per cent rise in pre-tax profits and a sharper rise in the interim dividend from 1p to 2.8p. Profits advanced to Pounds 2.82m (Pounds 1.76m) on turnover which was flat at Pounds 16.4m (Pounds 16.1m) as a four-year contract to supply Cadillac with accelerometers came to an expected end. That business is to be replaced as work for Fiat and PSA Group comes on stream. Earnings per share emerged at 11.77p (7.36p).
