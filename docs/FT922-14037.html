920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Tops Estate Tops Estate, the property investment group, has placed a further tranche of Pounds 25m nominal of 10.25 per cent first mortgage debenture stock 2011-16. Following this fourth tranche there will be Pounds 75m in issue. The gross redemption yield at issue will be at a margin of 1.95 per cent over yesterday's level at 3pm of 9 per cent Treasury stock 2008. Dealings in the further stock are expected to start today.
