940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Castle Mill Castle Mill International, the clothing and textiles company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 882,000 from turnover of Pounds 3.48m for the year to end-December 1993. The outcome compared with a loss of Pounds 383,000 from turnover of Pounds 9.31m. The profit was after taking into account a surplus arising from the waiver of bank indebtedness of Pounds 1.12m resulting from last September's financial restructuring. The effect of the restructuring also impacted on net interest payable, which fell from Pounds 594,000 to Pounds 259,000. Earnings per share emerged at 5.75p (3.21p losses).
