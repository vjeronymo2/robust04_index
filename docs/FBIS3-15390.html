Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] As the government information service announced, a general agreement for 1994 has been signed between the Belarus Council of Ministers, the Federation of Trade Unions, and the Association of Employers. Taking into account an assessment of the socioeconomic situation and prospects for the Republic's emergence from the crisis, the document fixes the following major indicators for improving the operation of the national economic complex and improving monetary and financial policy: not to allow a reduction this year in the gross national product of more than 3.2 percent, in industrial production --4.7 percent; to increase output of consumer goods by 1.6 percent; to achieve a reduction in the average monthly rates of inflation of 10-12 percent. The document, signed by the leaders of the interested parties, also sets out the rights and social guarantees for workers. The full text of the general agreement will be published in the press.
