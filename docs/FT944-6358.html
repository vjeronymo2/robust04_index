941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Investment in unit trusts falls Investment in unit trusts last month fell for the second consecutive month this year to a net Pounds 302.2m, almost 36 per cent lower than September's figure of Pounds 470.7m and the lowest monthly investment figure this year. Institutions were net sellers of unit trusts last month but retail investors bought a net Pounds 330.9m, of which almost 75 per cent - Pounds 245.6m - was in the form of tax-free personal equity plans.
