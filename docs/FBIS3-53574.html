Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton began their meeting in the conference hall at the Geneva Intercontinental Hotel at 1000 [as heard] Geneva time today. Attending on the Syrian side are Dr. 'Abd-al-Ra'uf al-Kasm, head of the National Security Office, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Faruq al-Shar'. For the U.S. side, the meeting is attended by Secretary of State Warren Christopher, National Security Adviser Anthony Lake, and Middle East Peace Coordinator Dennis Ross. Shortly before their meeting, Presidents Hafiz al-Asad and Bill Clinton met outside the conference hall and shook hands in their first direct introduction to each other. They then exchanged cordialities. Reporters asked President Hafiz al-Asad if he was pleased about this meeting with President Clinton. The president said: I am glad to meet with President Clinton and for us to be here in Geneva to work for a just and comprehensive peace in our region.
