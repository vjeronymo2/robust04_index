921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Lecturers accept 4.3% pay offer LECTURERS in further and adult education yesterday accepted by a two-thirds majority a 4.3 per cent pay offer from local authority employers. They are members of the 60,000-strong National Association of Teachers in Further and Higher Education. Some members in higher education are still in dispute with the Polytechnics and Colleges Employers Forum over a basic pay offer of 3.9 per cent.
