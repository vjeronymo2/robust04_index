October 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Elvis in London: A British car registration plate spelling ELVIS is expected to fetch a price equivalent to that of a London family house at auction. Christie's auctioneers say the plate ELV 1S is likely to break the previous record of almost $350,000 paid for the plate 1A when it goes under the hammer in December. CLAUDIA PUIG
