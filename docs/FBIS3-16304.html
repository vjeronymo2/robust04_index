Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The fourth round of negotiations between Ukraine and the European Union [EU] on concluding a treaty on partnership and cooperation began yesterday at the EU headquarters. Assessing the first day of the talks, Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister Oleksandr Makarenko, head of the Ukrainian delegation, noted that they were held in an atmosphere of constructiveness and mutual understanding. If the negotiations conclude with the results expected by both sides, Ukraine will be the first CIS state to conclude such a treaty with the EU.
