EFFECTIVE DATE: November 10, 1994 DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES Food and Drug Administration 21 CFR Parts 520 and 522 Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products; Change of Sponsor AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect a change of sponsor for eight new animal drug applications (NADA's) from Hoffmann-La Roche, Inc., to SmithKline Beecham Animal Health.
