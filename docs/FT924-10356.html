921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value falls at Contra-Cyclical Net asset value per capital share for Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust fell to 26.2p at September 30, against 53.3p a year earlier. However the figures per income share improved from 1p to 3.7p and for the zero dividend preferred shares from 52.9p to 59p. The trust, chaired by Sir Alan Walters, is paying an unchanged second interim dividend of 2.25p making a maintained total for the year so far of 4.5p. Net revenue for the six months to the end of September was Pounds 451,000, against Pounds 681,000 for the period from February 26 to September 30 1991. Earnings per income share were 5.64p (8.51p).
