920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Pakistan triumph Pakistan's captain Imran Khan celebrates the fall of the final England wicket in the cricket World Cup final in Melbourne. Pakistan won the trophy for the first time by making 249 for 6 in 50 overs and then bowling England out for 227 in their final over. Imran's 72 was the highest score of the match. England's top scorer was Neil Fairbrother, with 62.
