BFN [Text] Tashkent, May 6, RIA -- The Parliament of Uzbekistan, a former Soviet republic in Central Asia, has passed a law guaranteeing the security of foreign investments and foreign property and granting foreign investors a number of benefits. One of the clauses of the new legislation says that should any subsequent law deteriorate investment conditions, the legislation that was in force when the contract was signed would be applied to foreigners for 10 years. Uzbekistan also plans to set up a state insurance company to protect its clients from political risks. The company's assets will be deposited in foreign banks.
