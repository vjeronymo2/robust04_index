BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] The operations launched by the security forces south of Hakkari's Cukurca District on 12 April against terrorists trying to infiltrate into Turkey are continuing. So far 57 terrorists have been killed in these operations. The operations being conducted by the Hakkari mountain commando units are, at times, being supported by aircraft. According to information obtained from Turkish Radio and Television correspondent Bahadir Serif Onari, nine mortars, three machine guns, 16 Kalashnikovs, 152,000 rounds of ammunition, mortar shells, rockets, 98 hand grenades, 48 mines, radios and electronic equipment have been seized. Eight tonnes of foodstuff found in the caves belonging to the terrorists have been destroyed.
