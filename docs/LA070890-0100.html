Danton Bleakly beautiful and richly detailed, Andrzej Wajda's 1982 film is an electrifying depiction of a society hurtling toward chaos. Gerard Depardieu is superb in the title role as the boisterous and earthy revolutionary. (3 hours) Edited. (A&E early Sunday at 1 a.m.) Kevin Thomas
