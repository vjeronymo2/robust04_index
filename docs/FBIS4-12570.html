BFN [Excerpt] Two officers of Saddam's intelligence service have been killed in central Baghdad. Sources opposed to the Ba'thist regime said unknown persons assassinated the two officers late last week and that security forces rushed to the scene, cut off all roads leading to it, and launched a search of the public in shops and other places nearby. The sources added that Saddam's security agencies arrested several citizens and took them to unknown locations. [passage omitted]
