931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / Waterborne freight falls 5% Total domestic waterborne freight movements last year fell 5 per cent to 54.9bn tonne-kilometres, while the amount of freight lifted was 140m tonnes, a drop of 3 per cent, the Department of Transport announced yesterday. The reduction in freight movements was due mainly to a fall in the transport by sea of bulk liquids - mainly oil.
