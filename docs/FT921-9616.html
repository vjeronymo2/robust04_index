920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / Sultan Of Brunei may buy 10 percent of PAL The Sultan of Brunei is negotiating to buy 10 per cent of the equity of Philippine Airlines (PAL), Reuter reports from Manila. The Philippine government said the Sultan had reached agreement with the committee on privatisation last week. A 67 per cent stake in PAL was awarded to a consortium led by AB Capital and Investment Corporation at a January 30 auction.
