930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: OFT looking at NatEx buy The Office of Fair Trading is currently examining the proposed takeover of Citylink Coaches by National Express, Mr Neil Hamilton, minister of state at the Department of Trade & Industry, told parliament yesterday. National Express, the coach service operator which was floated on the Stock Exchange in December, announced on May 5 the acquisition of Citylink, the main subsidiary of Saltire Holdings, for Pounds 5.11m in shares, loan notes and cash.
