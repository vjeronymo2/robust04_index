941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Sedgwick disposal Sedgwick Group has sold its 75 per cent stake in CLM Advisers to CLM Insurance Fund for an initial Pounds 1.7m. Sedgwick will receive an initial Pounds 1.3m and a further amount may be payable in respect of underwriting profits for the 1994 to 1998 Lloyd's years of account. Mr Michael Wade, chief executive and a 25 per cent shareholder in CLM Advisers, has agreed to receive 400,000 preference shares instead of cash.
