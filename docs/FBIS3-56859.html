Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] To start this bulletin here is some information from the Russian prime minister's press service. After two days' lack of clarity the new skeleton of the government was announced today. So, Oleg Soskovets is first deputy prime minister and Aleksandr Zaveryukha, Anatoliy Chubays, and Yuriy Yarov are deputy prime ministers. Boris Fedorov has been confirmed as finance minister.
