931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmegs prices were firm this week, reports Man Producten. Indonesian bwps ranged from USDollars 800 to Dollars 1,000 a tonne, with shrivels at Dollars 1,000 to Dollars 1,300 and abcds Dollars 1,150 to Dollars 1,400. Indonesian mace was priced from Dollars 1,350 to Dollars 2,000 a tonne. Grenada defective nutmegs were fetching Dollars 1,100 a tonne and suns Dollars 1,700, while Grenadian mace was available at Dollars 1,875 a tonne, cif. Madagascan cinnamom was quoted at FFr5.90 a lb and Seychelles at Dollars 1,300 a tonne. Mexico spot pimento was offered at Dollars 2,000 a tone, with shipment at Dollars 1,975. Jamaica spot was Dollars 2,300 a tonne and shipment Dollars 2,175.
