930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Reinsurance company formed MARSH McLennan, the world's largest insurance broker, yesterday announced that it has fully capitalised a new Bermuda-based company set up to provide reinsurance for syndicates managed by the Merrett Group at Lloyd's of London. JP Morgan, the US bank, is also backing the Dollars 70m (Pounds 47m) venture. Both Marsh and Morgan will invest Dollars 7m with 30 other investors taking stakes.
