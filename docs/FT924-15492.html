921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / International Company News: K mart buys 21 book superstores K MART, the US retail group which already takes in the Waldenbooks chain, is acquiring Borders, operator of 21 book superstores, in a share exchange deal. The retailer said it planned to issue an unquantified amount of convertible preferred stock to the sellers. Borders, based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, runs the bookstore outlets in the midwest and northeast and is a privately-owned company. K mart said that Borders' annual turnover was around Dollars 150m.
