Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN in Japanese 20 January 94 Morning Edition] [Text] The government's Tax System Research Council (an advisory body for the prime minister, chaired by Kan Kato) decided on 19 January to postpone a 24 January general meeting to discuss specific ways to decrease the income and resident taxes and raise the consumption tax. The general meeting will be held after 25 January. The council decided to put off full-fledged discussion of the tax issue out of consideration for parliamentary deliberations on the political reform bills which are at a crucial stage.
