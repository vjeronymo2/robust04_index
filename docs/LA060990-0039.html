June 9, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AGASSI SHOULD WORK ON SUBSTANCE OVER STYLE

I have never been more provoked to write a letter in response to an article than after I read about Andre (Endorsements Have Gone to My Head) Agassi.

It is a tragic scenario to see a great talent like Agassi striving for recognition as a flash rather than striving for tennis excellence at Centre Court at Wimbledon.

Grow up, kid, and have your agent call mine to talk about designing you some new outfits with an emphasis on white.

LONNIE BERGER, Van Nuys
