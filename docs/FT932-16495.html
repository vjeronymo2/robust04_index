930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Pay increases for Japanese workers lowest since 1987 Pay increases for Japanese office workers this year were the lowest since 1987, a private survey revealed yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The rises for the fiscal year starting April 1 averaged 3.83 per cent, said the Japan Federation of Employers' Associations survey. It said the average monthly salary increase of Y10,767 (Pounds 62.96) was Y2,562 less than in the previous year.
