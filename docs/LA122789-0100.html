December 27, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE/TECHNOLOGY

Contract Award: Racal Recorders Inc., an Irvine electronics company, has won a three-year contract with a potential value of $6 million to supply instrument recorders to Edwards Air Force Base. Under the contract, Racal, a unit of New York-based Racal Corp., will supply up to 40 recorders to be used for collecting and analyzing large amounts of flight data. Compiled by David Olmos, Times staff writer
