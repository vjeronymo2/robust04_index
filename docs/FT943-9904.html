940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / Goodwill games hit for six Parachutists with bad timing knocked down a group of youths marching with banners over the weekend in a fittingly clumsy conclusion to St Petersburg's Goodwill Games. As an effort to reclaim its historic place as Russia's 'window on the west', the international sporting event has sorely backfired for St Petersburg. The swimming was postponed for a day because of pool water so filthy that western teams decanted it into plastic bottles to show friends back home. Skaters fared no better: the ice was so thin and so soft that their events were delayed as well. CNN owner Mr Ted Turner, who organised the games, put a brave face on the sparsely attended event, describing it as 'a huge success'.
