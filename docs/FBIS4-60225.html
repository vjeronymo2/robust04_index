BFN [Text] Nairobi, 20 May (SUNA) -- Deputy speaker of the Transitional National Assembly (TNA) Angelo Beda, Bahr al-Jabal State's Wali [Governor] Mrs. Agnes Lokudu and director of the peace department at the Foreign Ministry Mathiang Malwal have held here on Friday [20 May] a news conference on the resolutions of Juba political forum for peace forces. Beda said that over 350 of the southern leaderships, besides representatives of international press and some ambassadors accredited to Khartoum, had participated in the forum, describing the forum as a "real gathering" to hear the voice of southern citizens, who confirmed their insistence on the unity of Sudanese soil and called for ending the war. He added that the existing government is the only one that sat to negotiate seriously with the rebels' movement to end the southern Sudan war by presenting the Federal Government system as a comprehensive solution. Mrs. Agnes Lokudu said that the forum asserted that the southern leaderships are aware of the Sudanese people's interests and not toys in the hands of government, as termed by the rebels' government and some hostile (?mass) media.
