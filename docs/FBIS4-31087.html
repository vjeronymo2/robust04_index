BFN [CTK report: "Counterintelligence Has a New Director"] [Text] Prague--Defense Minister Antonin Baudys yesterday appointed Jiri Ruzek (1952) to the post of director of the Military Defense Intelligence Main Office. Until now, he had been deputy director of the Interior Ministry's Office for Foreign Relations and Information, which replaced our intelligence service abroad. However, according to Interior Ministry representatives, it does not conduct traditional espionage, and its staff apparently is working officially at our embassies.
