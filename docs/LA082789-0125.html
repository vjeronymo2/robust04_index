August 27, 1989, Sunday, Southland Edition

PLO WILL SEEK MEMBERSHIP IN WORLD TOURISM ORGANIZATION

The Palestine Liberation Organization, campaigning for international recognition, will try this week to become a member of the world body that promotes international travel and tourism, diplomatic and other sources said Friday.

The World Tourism Organization, which has 107 member states including Israel, has a six-day meeting starting in Paris on Monday. At least 50 tourism ministers are due to attend.
