BFN [Article by correspondent Muhammad al-Talibi] [Text] Babil -- The largest wheat and barley harvest campaign to be witnessed by Babil Governorate this year has begun with the participation of thousands of peasants and farmers. Sa'id Salman al-Fadili, director of Babil Agriculture Department, said some 14 committee have been formed to supervise harvest operations and to provide the necessary means of transport to ensure the success of the campaign, which covers 293,000 donums and in which 105 harvesters will be used. Fuel and spare parts have also been ensured for the harvesters. He added that trucks have been provided to transport grains directly to silos and government receiving centers, and the Agriculture Department's teams have been instructed to join the campaign to follow its progress. Al-Fadili said a mobile repair workshop with mechanics will accompany the harvesters, which can be transferred from one administrative unit to another if necessary to achieve a balance and to complete the harvest in all areas at once.
