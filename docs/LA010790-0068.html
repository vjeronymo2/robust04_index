January 7, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

FOR PRESERVING PIERCE'S FARM

The Calabasas Historical Society strongly urges the preservation of the Pierce College farm as a most valuable and significant historical landmark.

Creative approaches which can be implemented immediately will result in increasing prosperity for the college. The preservation of the farm will benefit the entire community. Its citizens do feel they have a vested interest in seeing that the farm is maintained and improved as a living green area. Oxygen and food are essential for human life. Therefore, the Pierce College farm should be preserved for posterity, the heritage of the future, providing a variety of educational experiences for students of all ages.

ALICE STELLE

Calabasas
