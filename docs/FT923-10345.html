920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / Offshore oil licensing begins THE Government yesterday launched the second stage of the 14th UK oil offshore licensing round - with the largest number of blocks available for some years. More than 170 blocks are available for standard licences in the northern, central and southern North Sea, the Moray Firth, the English Channel, and the Irish Sea. A further 123 blocks are available either singly or in groups in more speculative areas.
