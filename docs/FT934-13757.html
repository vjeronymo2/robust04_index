931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bulgin recovers to Pounds 0.28m AF BULGIN, the Essex-based electronics components and power supplies group, reported pre-tax profits, under FRS 3, of Pounds 280,000, against Pounds 32,000, for the six months to July 31. Turnover amounted to Pounds 7.84m (Pounds 6.58m), including a contribution of Pounds 589,000 from acquisitions. Earnings per share improved to 1p (0.11p).
