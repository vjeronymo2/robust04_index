September 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BOOK COLLECTING

As a lifelong bibliophile, I'm delighted to see that news about books is making the front page ("The Binding Attraction of Books" by Terry Pristin, Aug. 30).

It is impressive to read all about the prominent people who have taken up book collecting as a hobby but there is one element about books that seems to be overlooked: It's fun. You don't have to be rich and famous to enjoy books. And now that it has become a status symbol to be surrounded by books, I've been appalled to see expensive homes decorated with wallpaper with books sketched in, instead of real shelves overflowing with books. That's where the fun is!

SHERRY TERZIAN

Los Angeles
