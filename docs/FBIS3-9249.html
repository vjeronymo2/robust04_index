Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] A meeting of the Defense Ministry leadership reportedly took place on 18 March. It was chaired by Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov and attended by deputy ministers of defense, the chief of the General Staff and his deputies, and the commanders of the Armed Forces branches. The elimination of duplicating units in the Ministry of Defense system was one of the items on the agenda discussed at the meeting. In this connection the minister of defense ordered that his personal chancery and the general chancery of the Ministry of Defense should be closed down. A chancery of the Ministry of Defense was established instead. Colonel Engineer Konstantin Seizov was appointed chief of the new chancery. He is the former chief of the general chancery.
