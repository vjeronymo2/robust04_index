June 3, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PEOPLE IN THE NEWS: NURSING DIRECTOR WINS AWARD

* Marcia Grant, director of nursing research and education and an associate research scientist at City of Hope National Medical Center in Duarte, has received the ONS/Roche Distinguished Service Award from the Oncology Nursing Society.

The $4,000 award acknowledges a member of the society who fosters high professional standards that improve the image and practice of oncology nursing. Grant's research focuses on nutrition and cancer.
