BFN [Text] Baku, June 28 (TURAN) -- Baku is deeply concerned about the statements repeatedly distributed by Armenians officials in Yerevan and Khankendi (Stepanakert), which report that "Azerbaijan prepares an offensive towards Nagorno-Karabakh", an employee of Azerbaijan's Presidential staff told TURAN. "Such irresponsible statements do not allow to create favourable conditions for settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict. Both parties to the conflict have to be responsible for their activities", reports the same source. The Republic of Azerbaijan calls upon the Armenian authorities and the heads of Armenian community of Nagorno-Karabakh not to break the peace settlement process and not to destabilize the situation. On June 25 ITAR-TASS referring to military leaders of Nagorno-Karabakh distributed a statement about Azerbaijan's preparations to a large-scale offensive.
