940406 FT 06 APR 94 / UK Company News: Clarke Nickolls Following the approval by shareholders late last year for a restructuring and the acquisition of some income-producing properties, Clarke, Nickolls & Coombs ended the year to December 31 with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 99,000. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 711,000 and came from turnover up from Pounds 3m to Pounds 3.78m. Earnings emerged at 0.48p (2.5p losses). There is no dividend. The last pay-out was in 1991.
