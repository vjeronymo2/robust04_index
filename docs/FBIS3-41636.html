Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] Approximately 700 tonnes of toxic waste consisting of first-category chemical substances that could poison all living organisms have already been brought to Ukraine through all sorts of illegal channels, says Yuriy Kostenko, minister of environmental protection. In his opinion, the main reason for the illegal transportation of the deadly cargo is the absence of law that would regulate the procedure for bringing toxic substances to our territory.
