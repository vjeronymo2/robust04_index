Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lebanese judiciary is eager to handle the murder of the Jordanian diplomat Na'ib 'Umran al-Ma'ayitah with extreme delicacy and secure the defense for those charged with the murder, who belong to the Abu-Nidal-led Fatah Movement--the Revolutionary Council [FRC]. The defendants are interrogated in the presence of Bisharah Abu-Sa'd, a lawyer who is a partner and the brother-in-law of Deputy Najah Wakim. AL-SHIRA' previously reported that the Abu-Nidal group undertakes the personal protection of Deputy Wakim by providing him with a permanent escort of armed men. Hence, Wakim adheres to the FRC line and adopts it in his political positions.
