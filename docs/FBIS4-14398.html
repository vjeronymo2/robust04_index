BFN [Excerpt] President Hafiz al-Asad returned to Damascus this afternoon at the end of a visit to Amman during which he offered his sincere condolences to His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, monarch of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, and His Highness Hasan Bin-Talal, the crown prince, and the royal family on the death of Queen Zayn al-Sharaf, the queen mother. At the end of the visit, His Majesty King Husayn, and His Highness Prince Hasan accompanied the president from the royal palace to Amman's airport, where they led those who saw him off.
