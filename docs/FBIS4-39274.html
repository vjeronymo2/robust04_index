BFN [Text] Kiev [no dateline as received] DPRK Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam, currently in Ukraine on an official visit, met Ukraine Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko 6 June. The talks can be seen as introductory and consultative in nature. A wide range of issues were discussed in their talks. The key issue was focused on the development of bilateral relations, the possibility of economic cooperation, and the establishment of a legal and contractual basis for that. It was noted that Ukraine and the DPRK had signed only two agreements to date. In addition, the two officials voiced their countries' positions on nuclear issues. Kim Yong-nam declared that the DPRK intended firmly to become a nuclear-free state. The DPRK's position on the matter coincides with Ukraine's, he stressed. The sides plan to sign a number of bilateral agreements at the end of the visit 9 June.
