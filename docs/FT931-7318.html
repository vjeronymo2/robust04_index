930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Rise in executive job offers THE PERCENTAGE of redundant executives receiving job offers is rising, particularly in the south-east, Drake Beam Morrin, outplacement consultants, says. A total of 16 per cent of executives it counselled received offers in the first four weeks of the year - up from 10 per cent last year. In the south-east the proportion rose from 9 per cent to 18 per cent and in the Midlands from 10 per cent to 17.6 per cent.
