BFN [Text] Santa Fe, 30 May (NA) -- Raul Alfonsin, leader of the Radical [Radical Civic Union -- UCR] bloc, and Carlos Chacho Alvarez, head of the Grand Front bloc, have agreed that the political forces they head will reject any government project to include a clause that allows the re-election of governors in the new constitution. Alfonsin and Alvarez met at the seat of the Litoral University, where the Constituent Assembly will formally begin its sessions at 1700.
