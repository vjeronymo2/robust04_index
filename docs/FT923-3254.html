920914 FT 14 SEP 92 / US company to handle BA bags BRITISH AIRWAYS has contracted out its baggage security operations to Argenbright, an Atlanta-based company. Argenbright will take responsibility for the security of BA baggage worldwide under a contract that will run until 1995, when it will be reviewed. BA said yesterday that 400 employees of Aviation Defence Limited (ADL), its baggage security arm, will be transfered to Aviation Defence International (ADI), a subsidiary of Argenbright.
