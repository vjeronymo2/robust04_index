BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin left a short time ago saying that Russia's agreement for cooperation with the European Union [EU] is historical. Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis accompanied Yeltsin to the airport. Correspondent Alkis Mallidhis reports: [Begin recording] Just before leaving for Moscow, Russian President Boris Yeltsin expressed his gratitude for the Greek presidency's and Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou's substantive contribution in the achievement of an agreement between the EU and Russia. The Russian president stressed that with this fact, Europe becomes the only continent to cancel all economic distinctions. He added that the agreement has mutual benefits for Russia and the European states that now gain accession to a large and rich market. He concluded that the Corfu summit is an important event for Russia's history. [end recording]
