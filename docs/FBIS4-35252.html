BFN [Text] Shaykh Baqir Fasihi, a member of the Supervisory High Council of Hezb-e Wadaht-e Eslami [Islamic Unity Party], has been martyred in internecine clashes. As a result of these clashes, Sayed Qasem Nateqi, another member of the Supervisory High Council, along with a number of his guards was injured. According to reports, clashes among the supporters of Abdol Ali Mazari, secretary general of Hezb-e Wahdat-e Eslami, and supporters of Akbari have taken place after several days of peaceful demonstrations against each other. The report adds that disputes have arisen following the arrival of members of the Central Committee of Hezb-e Wahdat-e Eslami in Kabul in order to elect the future leader of the party. It is noted that 'Abdol 'Ali Mazari is not ready under the present conditions to make a fresh decision on the leadership issue. He has said that members of the Central Committee of Hezb-e Wahdat should be elected first by the party members and than they can decide on the future leadership of the party.
