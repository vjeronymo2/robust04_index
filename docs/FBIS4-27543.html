BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 2 KYODO -- Vice Finance Minister Jiro Saito said Thursday [2 June] he hopes to see "some progress" in the ongoing insurance talks under the resumed U.S.-Japan "framework" trade talks. But Saito, speaking at a regular press meeting, declined to elaborate, saying negotiations are still under way and he has not yet heard full details. Japanese and U.S. negotiators are meeting in Tokyo for the second day of the two-day talks that are expected to end later this evening. Insurance business is one of the three priority areas along with automobiles and auto parts, and government procurement of telecommunications and medical equipment.
