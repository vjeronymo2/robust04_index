940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / French media aid package The French government has announced an aid package for the print media in response to a crisis caused by the recession and outdated publishing practices, Reuter reports from Paris. Mr Nicolas Sarkozy, the government spokesman, gave no overall figure for the aid, which includes a previously announced subsidy of FFr200m (Dollars 34m) to dailies with poor advertising revenue. But he said it would involve guaranteeing loans.
