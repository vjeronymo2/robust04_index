920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Dunedin Worldwide Net asset value per 25p share of Dunedin Worldwide Investment Trust stood at 569.7p on April 30. That compared with 595.3p six months earlier and with 578.9p at end-April 1991. After-tax revenue for the half year to April 30 declined from Pounds 1.53m to Pounds 1.23m, equal to earnings of 3.61p (4.5p). The interim dividend is again 2.4p and the directors anticipate a maintained final of 7.1p.
