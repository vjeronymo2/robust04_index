BFN [Text] An agreement on trade and economic cooperation has been signed in Almaty by the trade and commerce chambers of Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. It is well known that the trade and commerce chamber of Kyrgyzstan includes entrepreneurs, industrialists, representatives of cooperative movements, and private farms. The signing of this agreement is expected to be followed by the opening of joint ventures and the organization of fairs and meetings at a state level between businessmen of both countries. The decrees recently adopted by the Supreme Council will help encourage partnership and economic cooperation and create favorable conditions for mutually beneficial cooperation.
