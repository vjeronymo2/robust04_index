7. Under the undesignated center heading ``Federal Participation,'' a new §447.85 is added to read as follows: §447.85 FFP for ambulatory prenatal care. If a State plan provides for coverage of ambulatory prenatal care, as defined in §440.172 of this subchapter, to pregnant women during a presumptive eligibility period, FFP is available for payments made on the woman's behalf for services covered under the plan that are furnished during that period, regardless of whether the pregnant woman is determined to be eligible for Medicaid after the presumptive eligibility period ends.
