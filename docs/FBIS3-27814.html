Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- Political figures of Kazakhstan elaborated on the singlehearted unity of Korea at a lecture on the subject "paternal love and solicitude shown by dear Comrade Kim Chong-il for the Korean young people" and a Korean film show which were held in Kazakhstan on January 14 and 15. Antonov, secretary of the Political Executive Committee of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan, said the secret to the invariable defence and advance of the idea of socialism in Korea under the complicated international situation lies in the singlehearted unity of the entire people under the wise guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and dear Comrade Kim Chong-il. V.I. Ran, secretary of the Political Executive Committee of the Aktyubinsk Regional Committee of the Party, noted that support and trust of the Korean people for their leaders is immense, indeed. "No one can break the crystal- like singlehearted unity of Korea" he said, adding: "In Korea the question of carrying forward the revolutionary cause of the leader has been successfully solved and education of the young people has reached the highest level beyond imagination". Co-chairman of the party A. Alimjanov said: "Only the rich monopolize arts and scenic spots in many countries. But in Korea all people take part in the creation of arts and enjoy holidays at scenic spots. This is the reality which can be seen only in Korea where the people revere their leaders as fathers."
