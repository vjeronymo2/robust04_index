Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrat, BASAPRESS 27/2/1994 -- Some 50 percent of 40,000 eligible voters in Comrat came to vote this morning at the 33 polling centres in this town. Piotr Ivanov, acting secretary of the district Executive committee, told BASAPRESS. Despite forecasts, there was a high turn-out at all polling stations. Ivanov pointed out that the voters did not follow the political games of certain local leaders who called on people not to participate in the elections. Ender Arat, general consul of Turkey in Moldova, arrived in Comrat a day before election day. He called on all political forces and movements during a meeting with local leaders to be reasonable. Also, Arat emphasized the importance of Gagauzians in creating the new Moldovan Parliament.
