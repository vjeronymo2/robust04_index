931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Linread gives full year warning Linread, the components and fasteners group, warned yesterday that its pre-tax profits for 1993 would be lower than market forecasts, but ahead of last year's Pounds 810,000. Profits would be held back by redundancy charges roughly double the Pounds 268,000 for the first half. The shares closed down 8p at 98p.
