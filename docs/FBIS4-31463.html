BFN [Text] Prishtina, June 8 (ATA) -- In Prishtina was founded the Coordinative Council of the branches of the Albanian political parties, which conduct their activities in the Kosova's capital. The council expressed in the meeting its unanimous support to the diplomatic activity of President Rugova. They also condemned the Serb's violence upon the Kosova state institutions, the imprisonments, the oppression and terror against the Albanians in general in the region of Kosova.
