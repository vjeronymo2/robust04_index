May 16, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

12 S.D. COMPANIES FOCUS OF SEMINAR

"Investing in San Diego 1989," a conference designed to acquaint investors with 12 of San Diego's public companies, will be held May 24 from 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. at the U. S. Grant Hotel. The seminar is sponsored by the Financial Analysts Society of San Diego.

Presentations will be made by Great American Savings, Burnham Pacific Properties, Mail Boxes Etc., Sym-Tek Systems, Imperial Corp. of America, Home Federal Savings, Precision Aerotech, Beeba's Creations, Molecular Biosystems, Cipher Data Products and Synbiotics.

A Southern California Edison representative will discuss the utility's proposed merger with San Diego Gas & Electric.

Luncheon speaker will be Greta E. Marshall, who until last year was investment manager of the California Public Employees Retirement System, the largest public pension fund in the country with about $45 billion in assets.

For information, call Linda Parks Gibson, 454-3188, or Irving Katz, 233-4721.
