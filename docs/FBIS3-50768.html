Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Ambassador to Mexico Jose Fernandez de Cossio Rodriguez has said that a strong, stable, and prosperous Mexico is important for the Cuban people. Fernandez de Cossio made this statement to the Mexican press when asked about the situation in Chiapas State. PRENSA LATINA reported that the Cuban diplomat in Mexico said that the Chiapas State incident is strictly a domestic matter and that the Cuban Government supports all efforts being made to find a peaceful solution to the problem. Regarding specific charges aimed at linking Cuba with the so-called Zapatist Army of National Liberation [EZLN], Fernandez de Cossio said that they are groundless. The ambassador said that those making these charges only want to cast a shadow on the relations between Mexico and Cuba, adding that, for the longest time, these relations have been based on the principle of non-intervention.
