BFN [Excerpt] Buenos Aires, 17 Jun (NA) -- Government House sources reported that early today President Carlos Menem underwent a medical checkup to renew his pilot's license and played a soccer game with former professional soccer players after successfully passing it. The spokesmen told NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS that Menem showed up at the metropolitan airport early this morning to undergo a medical checkup in order to fulfill aeronautical regulations. Immediately afterward the president went to the "La Celia" ranch in Buenos Aires' Ezeiza district owned by Deputy Dulce Visconti and entrepreneur Alejandro Granados, where he played a soccer game.
