921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / Paris hearing clears Bluebeard Some 552 years after he was hanged and burned in France for 150 murders, the man who served as model for the literary Bluebeard got his day in court again yesterday - and was cleared, Reuter reports from Paris. A self-styled court, including magistrates and a former justice minister, found that Gilles de Rais, a top battlefield companion of Joan of Arc, had been wrongly convicted.
