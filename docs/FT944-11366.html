941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Investment trust popularity grows Investment trusts popularity is still growing among small private investors, figures from the industry show. The Association of Investment Trust Companies says that contributions to savings schemes grew 23 per cent to Pounds 66.7m in the third quarter of this year. The amount invested in the first three quarters of the year was Pounds 189m compared with Pounds 166m in the same period last year.
