Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Beijing, 25 Mar (XINHUA) -- During the Second Session of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC], Li Ruihuan, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, met and had a cordial conversation with [Yaoxi Sulangzhuoma], a CPPCC member who was attending the session in Beijing and the mother of the Tenth Panchen Lama. Li Ruihuan said: I am very pleased to meet you. The Panchen Lama and I were good friends. His death has deprived our country of a very prestigious religious leader, and our party has lost a good friend. The Panchen made outstanding contributions to Tibet's development and stability and to China's nationality and religious work. I hope there will be more nationality and religious figures like the Panchen, who was dedicated to safeguarding the unification of the motherland and to promoting national unity and economic development. This is the basic guarantee for stability, prosperity, and development in the nationality areas. [passage omitted] [Yaoxi Sulangzhuoma] said gratefully: I am 78 this year and very healthy. I am grateful to the party Central Committee, the CPC Central Committee United Front Work Department, and the Tibetan and Qinghai authorities for their concern for me. To express her gratefulness, she presented a piece of scarf to Li Ruihuan. Present at meeting were Wang Zhaoguo, vice chairman of the CPPCC National Committee and director of the CPC Central Committee United Front Work Department, and deputy head of the department Li Dezhu [Li Dek Su].
