920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / World Stock Markets: Brazil BRAZILIAN equities have retrieved some ground after Monday's 14.6 per cent drop, writes Bill Hinchberger from Sao Paulo. The Bovespa index rallied 6.2 per cent on Tuesday and a further 0.77 per cent yesterday, to close at 20,223. The calm followed President Fernando Collor de Mello's national broadcast on Tuesday night, pledging to finish his term and defending himself against allegations of corruption. A parliamentary commission is investigating influence peddling and other charges against Mr Collor's former campaign treasurer.
