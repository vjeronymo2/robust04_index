BFN [Text] In Qalqilyah in western Samaria security forces have captured an Islamic Jihad terror band. The five members are suspected of being involved in the murder of an alleged collaborator with Israel in the center of Qalqilyah several days ago. Security forces also seized three weapons, including the gun which belonged to the murdered man. One suspected member of the band is believed to have fled and taken refuge in Jericho.
