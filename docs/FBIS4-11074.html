BFN [Text] There was a brief conversation on the morning of 19 April between the U.S. ambassador to Port-au-Prince and three officials of extreme-right organizations of lesser importance, including the Convergence of Mass Organizations, the Haitian Front for Democratic and Nationalist Organizations, and the Haitian People's Alliance. Those officials asked the Americans on 18 April to defend the honor of Haiti in view of France's arrogance. We are but a few minutes from the biggest power. We cannot tolerate our humiliation by France, they said during a sit-in. They were not admitted to the office of the ambassador on the morning of 19 April.
