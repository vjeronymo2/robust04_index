BFN [Text] Khartoum, 5 June (SUNA) -- The president of Yemen, General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, received Saturday [4 June] a message from his brother General 'Umar al-Bashir the president of the Republic, which was handed to him by the minister of culture and information 'Abd-al-Basit Sabdarat. Sabdarat who returned here from Sanaa on Saturday stated to SUNA that the message was in reply of a message sent to al-Bashir by Salih, which was handed to him by the Yemeni president's envoy Yahya Husayn al-Arashi, who visited Khartoum recently. He also said the message affirmed Sudan's stance toward Yemen and its concern with Yemen unity, besides ceasefire in Yemen to stop the bloodshed there.
