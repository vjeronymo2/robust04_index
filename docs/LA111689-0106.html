November 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

COMMUNITY DIGEST: PICO RIVERA;

$2.9-MILLION SENIOR CENTER WITH 13 ROOMS OPENED

Pico Rivera has opened its new Senior Center at 9200 E. Mines Ave. to provide a central gathering place for the community's more than 10,000 senior citizens.

The 16,500-square-foot, 13-room center was built at a cost of $2.9 million, City Manager Dennis Courtemarche said.

The Senior Center is open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday except holidays.
