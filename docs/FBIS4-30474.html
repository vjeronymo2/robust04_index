BFN [Text] Kozo Watanabe, acting secretary general of Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party], held a news conference on the afternoon of 25 June and disclosed that the party plans to promptly wrap up policy consultations between the ruling coalition parties and the Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ]. He indicated the party seeks to form a new coalition government that excludes the Liberal Democratic Party before the Naples summit which is to start on 8 July. Regarding the framework of the new government, Watanabe said: "We would like to treat with respect the feelings of the large majority of upper and lower house members who voted for `Tsutomu Hata' [as prime minister]." He thus expressed strong hopes for the SDPJ and the Sakigake [Harbinger Party] to return to the coalition.
