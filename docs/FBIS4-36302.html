BFN [Text] Baghdad, 7 Jun (INA) -- President Saddam Husayn chaired a meeting of the Council of Ministers today. During the meeting, his excellency stressed the need for the members of the Council of Ministers to directly follow up decisions and measures, to honor those who excel in applying them, to punish those who fail to apply them, and not to tolerate any poor performance or weakness in state-run institutions. His excellency said: Ours is a state of the Ba'th Party and it should continue to enjoy the strength, enthusiasm, and vigor of youths. His excellency also stressed the need to improve the standard of living of government employees, as well as the need to work out appropriate ways of transporting them to and from their departments and work sites and regulating office hours. The Council also discussed a number of economic and organizational issues of interest to the citizens, and his excellency ordered that fruit and vegetable prices be watched carefully to guarantee balanced prices between producers and consumers. His excellency called for developing and purifying seeds to plant them in a scientific way and to launch media campaigns to explain ways of preserving animal wealth.
