December 18, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LAGUNA BEACH;

AIDS GROUP STARTS SERVICE FOR WOMEN

A new support group for women who are infected with the AIDS virus has been formed by the AIDS Services Foundation.

On Mondays from 10:30 a.m. to noon, foundation staffer Dr. Valerie Gold-Neil will head the free counseling service at 1205 N. Coast Highway.

The group is open to women who have tested positive for the AIDS virus, or who have been diagnosed with AIDS or AIDS-related conditions. Call (714) 494-7547 for more information.
