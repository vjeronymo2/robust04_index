May 5, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MANY LAKER FANS SAY THERE'S NOTHING FINE ABOUT NBA'S DECISION

Mike Downey, do you know pro basketball is a team game? Yes, if I paid 500 bucks to see Mike Tyson's rematch with Buster Douglas and one did not fight, I'd want my money back. Same as if I bought Prince or Madonna or Sinatra tickets, or Michael Crawford in "Phantom of the Opera."

But the people in Portland got what they paid for -- same as I got in reading your article.

RIC MANDELL, Torrance
