Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "News" program] [Excerpts] Tibet's deputies to the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] and members of the Second Session of the Eighth Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] National Committee arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of 7 March. At Beijing's Xijiao Airport, Gyaincain Norbu, NPC deputy and chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Regional People's Government, told reporters: We feel very honored to represent the over 2 million people of various nationalities in Tibet in attending the Second Session of the Eighth NPC. We also feel the heavy responsibility. Under the current good situation of deepening reform, accelerating development, and maintaining stability, I feel cheerful at attending the session. [passage omitted] Chen Kuiyuan, NPC deputy and secretary of the Tibet Autonomous Regional CPC Committee; Raidi, executive deputy secretary of the Tibet Autonomous Regional CPC Committee; Sengqen Lozong Gyaincain, vice chairman of Tibet Autonomous Regional People's Congress, who were among the first group to arrive in Beijing, greeted these deputies and members at the guesthouse.
