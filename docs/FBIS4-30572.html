BFN [Text] Panmunjom, June 28 (YONHAP) -- Inter-Korean summit will open for the first time in over four decades July 25-27 in the North Korean capital of Pyongyang. The historic agreement came after hours of working-level negotiations at the truce village of Panmunjom. Talks to settle protocol details will open in the Northern side of the border on July 1. Date and site of the second summit will be decided at next month's first summit.
