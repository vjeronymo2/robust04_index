940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / Big increase in drug seizures Customs officers seized a record amount of amphetamines last year, the National Criminal Intelligence Service said yesterday. About 550kg was seized as it was being smuggled into the UK, a 342 per cent rise on 1992, and 450kg was found by police. Crack cocaine seizures rose by 90 per cent and cannabis by about 20 per cent.
