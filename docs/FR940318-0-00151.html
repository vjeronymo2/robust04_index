High to low pressure stepdown process is acceptable as an alternative technology to cold storage warehouses using CFC&hyph;12 or R&hyph;502. This process takes advantage of the work potential of pressurized natural gas. As its pressure is reduced from transmission pipes to the distribution system, the gas cools. This refrigeration is then used to cool a transfer medium such as water, which then cools the refrigerated space. It uses very little energy and produces no global warming emissions, since the gas is not burned. (k) Stirling cycle.
