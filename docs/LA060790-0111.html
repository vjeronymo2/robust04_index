June 7, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PICK OF THE WEEK

Antique and rare photography artifacts will be featured at the Western Photographic Collectors Show at the Pasadena Center, 300 E. Green St., Saturday from 10:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. and Sunday from 10:30 a.m. to 3 p.m. Tickets are $4.

Among the items are a rare poster, above, advertising the first Brownie camera, introduced by Kodak in 1900 and sold for $1; the original Kodak camera produced in 1888; an 1890 series of box cameras made of polished wood and meant for use by children, and a folding camera introduced in 1890.
