930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / International Company News: Laurentian Bank improves 2% to CDollars 10.4m LAURENTIAN BANK reported third-quarter net profits of CDollars 10.4m (USDollars 7.8m), up 2 per cent from a year earlier, and for the nine months CDollars 26.1m, down 9 per cent, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Earnings per share in the latest quarter were 48 cents against 53 cents, and in the nine months CDollars 1.22 against Dollars 1.53. The average number of shares outstanding was 18m against 15.5m, due to expansion and acquisitions. The bank is the fastest-growing affiliate of the Laurentian Group, now being merged with the big Desjardins financial services group. Control of the bank will be held through a central Desjardins-Laurentian holding company.
