940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / London Transport advert arm sold The government yesterday approved the sale for an undisclosed sum of London Transport Advertising, the advertising arm of London Transport, to a consortium led by the largest transport advertising company in the US, Transportation Displays Incorporated, and Hambro Group Investments. LTA, which generates about Pounds 23m a year, is responsible for selling advertising space on London Underground and most of London's buses.
