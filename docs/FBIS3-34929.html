Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tyre, Lebanon, Feb 15 (AFP) -- The Israeli navy abducted at dawn Tuesday five fishermen in Lebanese territorial waters and towed their boats towards Israel, fishermen here said. The five were on board two boats just south of the port of Tyre when an Israeli gunboat intercepted them, a fisherman who was in the vicinity said. He charged that members of Israel's proxy militia manning a position overlooking Tyre opened fire at the fishermen at the same time but there were no casualties. The Israeli navy patrols the waters of south Lebanon, intercepting boats and seizing travelers or fishermen for questioning as part of operations to check for anti-Israeli guerrillas.
