Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received a telephone call from His Excellency Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriyah, this evening. During the call, the Libyan leader congratulated His Majesty King Husayn on the signing of the Yemeni accord in Amman. He also expressed his appreciation for his majesty's great role and his efforts to secure the signing of the document, beseeching Almighty God to preserve his majesty as an asset and hope for the nation.
