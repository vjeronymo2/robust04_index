BFN [Text] State Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin fully shares President Yeltsin's view that international sanctions against North Korea should be coordinated with UN members and imposed gradually. As the chairman of the State Duma Defense Committee, Sergey Yushenkov, told Interfax, Rybkin expressed this agreement with Yeltsin's view on Tuesday, when receiving a parliamentary delegation from Britain. Led by the chairman of the Standing Commission for Defense of the House of Commons, Nicholas Bonsor, the delegation took part in Tuesday's round-table conference on collective security in Europe held at the lower chamber of Russia's parliament. Interfax's correspondent reports that on Wednesday British MPs intend to visit Russia's Defense Ministry, Institute for Strategic Studies and a military unit. Thursday evening they will leave for Kiev.
