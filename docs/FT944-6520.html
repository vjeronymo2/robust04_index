941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: OEM losses OEM, the property company, incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 232,000 in the year to June 30, compared with profits of Pounds 263,000, which included Pounds 679,000 from the office equipment business sold last year. Turnover was Pounds 744,000, against Pounds 3.27m, which included Pounds 1.69m from the discontinued activities. Losses per share were 3.8p (1.5p).
