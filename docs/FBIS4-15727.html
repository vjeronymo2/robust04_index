BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 12 Apr (TASS) -- Russia will never be reconciled to the role of "a younger partner", who is informed after the event about such vital decisions as the use of armed force. Grigoriy Karasin, the director of the Information and Press Department of the RF Ministry of Foreign Affairs, made this statement today at a briefing for Russian and foreign journalists. "We are opposed to the practice of Russia being consulted only in order to get an affirmative `yes' from us," the diplomat said. "We have naturally reached the appropriate conclusions from what occurred on 10th and 11th April in the region of Gorazde."
