October 22, 1989, Sunday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Favorite Steinlen, in his final prep for the Breeders' Cup Mile, held off longshot Crystal Moment, by a head to win the $187,850 Keeneland Breeders' Cup in Lexington, Ky.

Steinlen, ridden by Jose Santos, covered the 1 1/8 -- mile course in 1:52 2 -- 5 and paid $2.60, $2.60 and $2.20. Crystal Moment returned $13.80 and $5.60, and Posen paid $3.
