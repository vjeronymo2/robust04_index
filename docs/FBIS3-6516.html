Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 17 -- Vice Premier Phan Van Khai received in Hanoi yesterday afternoon Mr. Toru Toyoshima, president of the Japan External Trade Organization (JETRO), who is here for a visit and for the opening of the first Japanese Industry Exhibition-94 in Hanoi. General Secretary of the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry Pham Chi Lan and Japanese Ambassador to Vietnam H. Yushita were present at the reception. Mr. T. Toyoshima expressed his thank to Vietnamese Government and offices for helping JETRO and Japanese companies in organizing the above-said exhibition. He said that Japan highly appreciated the achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in their renovation, and the prospects of the Vietnamese-Japanese economic relations. He noted that JETRO will encourage Japanese companies to invest in Vietnam and support Vietnam to expand its export to Japan. For his part, Vice Premier Phan Van Khai hailed JETRO's contributions to the promotion of economic and trade cooperation between Vietnamese and Japanese businessmen. He expressed his pleasure at the rapid development of commercial relation and economic cooperation between the two countries in recent years, and his hope that economic exchanges between the two countries will further develop.
