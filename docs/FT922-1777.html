920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / UK Company News: BA Holidays 29% ahead at Pounds 2.7m British Airways Holidays, a subsidiary of British Airways, returned profits of Pounds 2.7m pre-tax for the year to end-March, an improvement of 29 per cent over 1990-91's Pounds 2.1m. The directors yesterday forecast a rise in profits to about Pounds 3m for the current year.
