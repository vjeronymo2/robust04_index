940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / Two found guilty of wine theft plot Mr Raymond Burraway and Mr Joseph Mankowitz, former directors of Green's, the wine merchant, were yesterday found guilty at Inner London Crown Court of conspiracy to steal Pounds 2m worth of vintage stock from clients. They will be sentenced on March 10 along with Mr Timothy Soames, who admitted the charge. The jury cleared Mr Anthony Boylan of taking part in the conspiracy.
