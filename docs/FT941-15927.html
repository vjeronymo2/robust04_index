940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa A fall in Anglos of R14.50, or nearly 7 per cent, to R105.50 led golds lower, the index losing 72 to 2,138. De Beers hit an intraday high of R113 before closing R1.25 up at R110.25. Industrials fell 65 to 5,677 and the overall index 83 to 4,961.
