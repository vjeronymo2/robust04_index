September 8, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Arroyo Grande 49, Hueneme 6 -- Paced by Clemente Sainten's record-breaking performance, Arroyo Grande handed Hueneme its 14th consecutive loss.

Sainten, a junior transfer from Fontana High, scored five touchdowns to set school records for most points and touchdowns in a game. Sainten rushed for four touchdowns on runs of three, seven, 15, and 20 yards. He also scored on an 18-yard pass from Clayton Millis. BRYAN RODGERS
