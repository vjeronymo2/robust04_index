BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 22 (KCNA) -- A protocol on cooperation between the DPRK Information Committee and the Ministry of Communications, Post and Telecommunications of the Congo was signed in Brazzaville on May 16. The protocol was signed by Maganga Boumba, secretary general of the Congolese Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation, and by Cha Chong-ung, Korean ambassador to the Congo.
