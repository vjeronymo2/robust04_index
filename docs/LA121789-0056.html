December 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PLEASANT DISCOVERY

My wife and I are fortunate in being able to travel frequently, both in the United States and internationally. Recently we had the pleasure of flying on Cathay Pacific Airways. I cannot praise Cathay Pacific too highly for the outstanding in-flight service and the fine food that we encountered on all of our trips. This was particularly noteworthy since we were traveling during a heavy traffic period and the cabin was fully occupied. The purpose of this letter is to share our discovery with your readers. I wish someone had told me about Cathay Pacific long before I discovered it for myself.

ROGER H. SCHNAPP

Newport Beach
