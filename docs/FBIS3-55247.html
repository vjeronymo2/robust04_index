Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] President Askar Akayev has received a letter from citizens of Toktogul district. It says that the people of Toktogul district, having familiarized themselves with the presidential program for social and economic development of Kyrgyzstan, fully support this important document aimed at lifting the country's economy out of crisis. Citizens of the district assure the president that they will do everything to implement it successfully. The letter also spoke about a forthcoming referendum. On 30 January citizens of the region intend to confirm at the referendum that the president, who was elected by the nation on 12 December 1991 for a period of five years, is the president of Kyrgyzstan until the end of his term with all the powers ascribed to the post. The letter has been signed by 16 people on behalf of labor veterans and workers of Toktogul district.
