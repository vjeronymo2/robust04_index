940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / Justin Long of the Financial Times Justin Long, who was parliamentary correspondent of the Financial Times for more than 20 years, died on Friday aged 82. A highly respected figure at Westminster, he worked for a number of provincial newspapers before joining the FT in the early 1950s. He covered Budgets introduced by every chancellor from RA Butler to Reginald Maudling for the Conservatives, and from Lord Callaghan to Lord Healey for Labour.
