920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: Wall Street WALL STREET was closed for President's Day. Toronto finished slightly softer, the US holiday having kept trading light. The TSE 300 eased 3.1 to 3,570.8. Declines led advances by 275 to 236 after a volume of 15.3m shares valued at CDollars 149.9m. Cascades, down CDollars 3/8 at CDollars 7, reported lower operating income in the fourth quarter.
