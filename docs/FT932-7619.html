930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Far Eastern asset value ahead Fleming Far Eastern Investment Trust lifted net asset value from 228.96p to 294.82p over the year to March 31. Net revenue fell to Pounds 1.89m (Pounds 2.42m), or 1.18p (1.53p) per share. The dividend is cut from 1.5p to 1.1p.
