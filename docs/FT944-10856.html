941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / Manila steps in over peso The Philippine central bank is not trying to reverse the rise of the peso but is using 'limited interventions' to smooth volatility, Mr Edgardo Zialcita, acting governor, said. The bank's intervention in the market yesterday was designed to 'stop a sharper decline in the dollar value'. The central bank bought Dollars 40m as the peso surged 68 centavos to hit a 29-month high of 23.50 pesos to the dollar before easing.
