Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Potsdam (DPA) -- Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) expects the government in Oslo to come to [applaud] the positive decision about Norway's planned accession to the European Union (EU) by next Monday [7 March]. After the meeting of the liberal group in the European Parliament (LDR) in Potsdam, he said on Wednesday that a compromise regarding the problems of fishing quotas is foreseeable. Furthermore, Kinkel stressed that Germany will advocate more rights for the European Parliament. The LDR group has 45 deputies from 10 countries and is thus the third largest group in the European Parliament. It holds a meeting in Potsdam from Tuesday to Thursday.
