There are 540 registered closed-end investment companies, all of which are eligible to use the rule. Estimated annual burden is .250 hours per response. Form F&hyph;6 is used by depository banks to register American Depository Receipts of foreign companies pursuant to the Securities Act of 1933. All filings on Form F&hyph;6 are prepared and filed by a limited number of banks that operate as depositories. An estimated 339 filings on Form F&hyph;6 are made annually at an estimated burden on one hour per response.
