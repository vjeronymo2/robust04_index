April 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

PAGAN DAY

If the members of the Truth Missions really knew the "truth" (Only in L.A., April 20), they would lighten up. That Christmas was originally a pagan festival is beyond all doubt, the tree being equally common in pagan Rome and pagan Egypt. Take a trip to the library of your choice and relax; "only 249 shopping days until Pagan Day," aka Christmas.

LISA VONN

Montecito
