BFN [Report by Dmitriy Gutenev: "Cossacks in Operation: An Army Being Formed in Omsk"] [Text] The spring draft to the ranks of the Russian Army is approaching. The creation of a number of military units manned by Cossacks, which will be located in Omsk, will be a novelty. According to military commissariats' explanations, members of public organizations of Cossacks will be drafted under their banners, and if there are none of these in the areas, citizens who are their direct descendants will be drafted. Cossacks will also be drafted on contract as officers, warrant officers, sergeants, and soldiers.
