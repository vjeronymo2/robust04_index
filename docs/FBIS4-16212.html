BFN [Text] The Government of Kyrgyzstan has adopted a decree on the state and preservation of state property by Kyrgyzstan's industrial works, enterprises, agriculture, and transport. It says: Under the conditions of introducing market reforms and enterprises gaining their economic independence financial discipline has fallen down in the Republic and the number of economic crimes has increased. Ministries and departments are not exercising control over the preservation of state property. Embezzlement, misappropriation, and shortages are [word indistinct] widespread. This is the result of the shortcomings in the work of the city administrations. With the aim of eliminating these shortcomings, the State Financial Inspectorate under the Government of Kyrgyzstan has been invested with powers to carry out regular checks of the interdepartmental financial control bodies, and the results of the checks are to be submitted for consideration by the government of the Republic.
