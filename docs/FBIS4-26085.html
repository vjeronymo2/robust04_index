BFN [Text] Beijing, June 16 (XINHUA) -- The Preparatory Committee of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Preliminary Working Committee [PWC] Special Cultural Group held its fourth meeting in Beijing 15-16 June. The meeting discussed an "outline of the plan to step up efforts to popularize the Basic Law in Hong Kong," "report on the textbook question," and reports to be delivered by the group to the third PWC plenary session. While discussing the "report on the textbook question," group members said the existing textbooks may in general continue to be used, with the exception of contents that contravene the Basic Law, which should be amended.
