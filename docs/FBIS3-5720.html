Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 9 (KCNA) -- Timber production is on an increase in Korea. According to data available at the Ministry of Forestry, over 23,000 more cubic metres of timber has been produced in the last one month than in the like period last year. The Yanggang and Chagang provincial combined general bureau of forestry set themselves the a goal of carrying out the yearly timber production quotas at more than 75 percent in wintertime. Ty fulfilled the February production plans respectively at 107 and 106.7 percent and are expanding the success this month. The Sijung, Yonam and many other forestry stations are these days overfulfilling their daily production assignments by more than 30 percent through an effective control of machinery coupled with meticulous organisation of economic affairs.
