940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Bridgend sells health club for Pounds 1.36m Bridgend Group is to sell the health and fitness club Stocks at Birmingham to Living Well Health & Leisure for Pounds 1.36m, payable in stages. The book value of the assets is Pounds 1.7m and the club made a loss of Pounds 23,000 in the year to December 31 1992.
