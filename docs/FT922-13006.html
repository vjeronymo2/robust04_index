920414 FT 14 APR 92 / UK Company News: Allied London tops Pounds 2.6m A 19 per cent increase in rental income from Pounds 7.28m to Pounds 8.65m helped Allied London Properties, developer and housebuilder, turn in a Pounds 2.62m pre-tax profit for the half year ended December 31. This compared to a Pounds 5.35m loss last time, resulting from an Pounds 8m exceptional provision for the reduction in value of housebuilding land and work in progress. The group has now further reduced its housebuilding operations and has no property developments in progress. Earnings per share came out at 1.7p against losses of 9.4p last time, and the interim dividend is maintained at 1.075p.
