BFN [Text] The issue of the reduction of Armed Forces human and financial resources continues in the news. In a speech, President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada has denied it. [Begin Sanchez de Lozada recording] I want to tell you that there never were, and never will be any plans of the sort reported in an opposition newspaper article--which, unfortunately, acquired some notoriety--because this institution safeguards all Bolivians, our sovereignty, our democracy, and our development. We undoubtedly need to adopt many measures to improve the institution. That is only natural. These measures seek to make the Armed Forces a stronger institution, allowing them to better fulfill their institutional duties and to better represent and defend our fatherland. [end recording]
