930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Molyneux Estates Molyneux Estates, the USM-quoted property investment group, reported increased losses before tax of Pounds 245,000 for the six months to December 24. The outcome was struck after taking account of a Pounds 303,000 deficit from its Overgate Centre associate and compared with a deficit on an FRS 3 basis of Pounds 222,000. Turnover increased from Pounds 1.02m to Pounds 1.22m. Losses per share came out at 1.35p (1.22p). Net indebtedness was broadly unchanged and currently stands at Pounds 16.5m for the parent company and Pounds 5.2m representing Overgate's borrowings.
