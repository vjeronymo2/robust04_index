Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Wahn (DPA) -- The final 200 German blue helmet soldiers arrived home on Wednesday [23 March] from their humanitarian mission in the East African country of Somalia. They landed at the military section of the Cologne/Bonn Airport aboard two German Air Force aircraft. They will be officially welcomed this afternoon by Defense Minister Volker Ruehe, of the Christian Democratic Union. The minister will then put the "German Somalia Support Union" out of service after a 10-month mission.
