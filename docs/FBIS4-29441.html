BFN [Text] According to a report, 1,200 Burmese Government soldiers attacked the Khun Sa military stronghold in Chot Town on 17 June. They advanced to the center of the town and encountered strong opposition by Khun Sa soldiers. At 1000 on 18 June, Burmese helicopters fired rockets and heavy machineguns at the Khun Sa force northeast of Chot Town. There were no reports of the battle or casualties.
