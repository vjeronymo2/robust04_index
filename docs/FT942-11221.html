940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Castle Comms Castle Communications, through its Castle Copyrights subsidiary, has agreed to buy the rights to the Solar catalogue of master recordings, excluding the US, Canada and Africa, for Dollars 2m (Pounds 1.3m) cash. The Solar catalogue comprises some 400 soul, dance and disco tracks performed mainly by black American west coast artists, and is owned by Sound of Los Angeles Records.
