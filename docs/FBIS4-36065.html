BFN [Excerpts] The Council of Ministers met today, Wednesday, 1 June 1994, under the chairmanship of His Eminence Mr. Maaouiya Ould Sid' Ahmed Taya, the president of the Republic. The council studied and approved the following draft laws: 1. A draft law that allows the ratification of the loan agreement signed on 26 April 1994 between the Islamic Republic of Mauritania and the international development association regarding the financing of the agricultural services project. [passage omitted] 2. A draft law that includes the amendment of Law No. 91-24, issued on 25 June 1991, regarding political parties. This draft law aims to simplify and extend the criteria used for the distribution of financial aid put aside by the government for national political parties. [passage omitted]
