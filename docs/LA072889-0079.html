July 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

A scheduling conflict has forced actress Margaret O'Brien to bow out of the Long Beach Civic Light Opera production of "Cinderella." Marilyn Child will replace her as the Queen, joining a cast that includes Alan Young, Pat Carroll and Greg Louganis. The Rodgers and Hammerstein musical plays Aug. 4-20 at the Terrace Theater in Long Beach. ALEENE MacMINN
