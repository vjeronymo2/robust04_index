Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The sessions of the Tieling City People's Congress and the city Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] committee recently elected the new leading bodies of the city. At the first session of the third city CPPCC committee, the participating members elected (Tan Tianmin) chairman of the city CPPCC committee. At the first session of the third city people's congress, the participating deputies elected (Yan Qize) chairman of the city people's congress standing committee and Li Shiwen mayor of the city.
