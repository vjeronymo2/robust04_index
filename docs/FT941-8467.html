940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Amicable Smaller asset value rises Net asset value per share of Amicable Smaller Enterprises Trust rose from 96.66p to 142.86p over the year to December 31. Net revenue fell from Pounds 798,000 to Pounds 722,000 for earnings of 3.46p (3.83p) per share. Fully diluted earnings were 3.21p. The proposed final dividend is held at 1.7p for an unchanged total of 3.4p.
