930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Manchester chess hopes boosted MANCHESTER'S hopes of staging the World Chess Championship between Garry Kasparov, the champion, and Nigel Short received a boost yesterday. Kasparov said the match would definitely take place in Britain and Manchester had not been ruled out. Fide, the world chess federation, last month accepted a Pounds 1.2m bid from Manchester to stage the championship, but Short and Kasparov rejected the offer.
