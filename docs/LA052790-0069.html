May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THANKS FOR TIP

I have been meaning to write and thank Jerry Hulse for a squib in his Travel Tips column last year on CampAlaska Tours, P.O. Box 872247, Wasilla, Alaska 99687, phone (907) 376-9438.

I went with them for two weeks and had about the best time I have ever had on a vacation. We must have covered 2,000 miles and saw just about everything. It was a most reasonably priced trip, and the people in our group, from all over the world, were bright, fun and good sports. I highly recommend this company for a great way to really experience Alaska.

VAUGHN HART

Beverly Hills
