941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Minister pledges help for Tyneside Extra measures to help rejuvenate Tyneside in the wake of a wave of recent heavy job losses were pledged yesterday by Mr Ian Taylor, trade and technology minister. He said during a visit to Newcastle upon Tyne that the government had given the Tyne and Wear Development Corporation approval to spend Pounds 8.7m on redeveloping part of South Shields' riverside. The Northern Development Company would be given an additional Pounds 50,000 to promote inward investment opportunities.
