Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 17, BNS -- The political treaty between Lithuania and Poland is not a responsibility of a single party, even the party in power, but a concern of the entire Lithuanian nation, the Board of the Lithuanian Union of Nationalists (LUN) said Thursday [17 March]. In an address to President Algirdas Brazauskas, the LUN suggested to discuss the basic principles of the treaty with all political parties represented in the parliament. The union asks the president to "take the initiative and to achieve a general agreement on this vitally important" problem.
