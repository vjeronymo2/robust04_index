February 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

LONG BEACH WOMEN GET PAST FRESNO STATE, 104-98

Penny Moore scored 25 points and Trise Jackson added 22 as Cal State Long Beach outlasted Fresno State, 104-98, Thursday night in a Big West Conference women's basketball game.

Simone Srubek scored 31 points in defeat for the Bulldogs (16-9, 9-6 in the Big West). Angelique Lee added 19 points and Vjera Kaludjerovich had 17 for the 49ers (21-5, 13-2).
