BFN [Presidential Decree No. 23, issued by the Presidency in Sanaa on 13 May] [Text] The Presidential Council chairman, taking cognizance of the Constitution of the Republic of Yemen and Law No. 52 of 1991 concerning the Local Administration Law, and at the recommendation of the acting prime minister, and following the approval of the Council of Ministers and the Presidential Council, decides the following: Article 1: Brother 'Ali Shaykh 'Umar shall be appointed governor of Abyan Governorate. Article 2: This decree shall go into effect as of the day of its issuance. It shall be published in the official gazette. Issued at the Presidency in Sanaa on 3 Dhu al-Hijjah 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 13 May 1994. [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman; Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister
