910424 FT 24 APR 91 / UK Company News: Conrad Continental losses rise to Pounds 0.68m Conrad Continental, which runs leather clothing, fashion accessories and the Bobby Charlton football coaching operations, increased losses from Pounds 283,000 to Pounds 680,000 in 1990. Turnover fell to Pounds 12.06m (Pounds 13.12m) and the operating profit was only Pounds 9,000 (Pounds 302,000). The situation was exacerbated by higher interest charges of Pounds 481,000 (Pounds 278,000), but offset to some extent by a reduction in exceptional charges to Pounds 208,000 (Pounds 307,000). Losses per share were 3.51p (1.2p). There is no dividend (interim 1p).
