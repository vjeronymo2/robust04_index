Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Ambassador Traian Chebeleu, spokesman for Romania's Presidency, held his weekly news conference today at Cotroceni Palace. [passage omitted] Commenting on the upcoming visit to Romania of the two cochairmen of the Geneva peace conference, David Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg, Chebeleu said that Romania intends to support and encourage the two cochairmen and reasserted that our country opposes a military settlement of the conflict and that a viable solution should come solely from within former Yugoslavia.
