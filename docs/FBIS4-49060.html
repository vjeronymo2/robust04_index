BFN [Text] Beijing, April 24 (XINHUA) -- General Fu Quanyou, director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), met with a delegation from the General Logistics Department of Hungary here this afternoon. The delegation headed by Hollosi Nandor, deputy commander of the National Defence Forces, arrived here on April 16 as Fu's guest. The visitors toured Guangzhou and Shenzhen before they returned to Beijing. The meeting was followed by a dinner hosted by Fu in honor of the delegation.
