January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I can't believe people in charge of "Julie & Carol: Together Again" think that a pie/cake in the face routine is funny today.

It isn't. It is incredible that two such wonderful performers who have such combustion together would agree to such stupid direction. I was looking forward to their special and it was a pleasure to see both Julie Andrews and Carol Burnett but certainly not with the routines they were expected to perform. The entire show was boring, ridiculous and devoid of humor.

Renee Jones, Granada Hills
