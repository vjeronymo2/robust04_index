March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

SCHABARUM WON'T RUN

I have known Sarah Flores for the last 10 years and have been quite impressed with the devotion and loyalty she had for Schabarum.

It is quite all right for Schabarum to endorse whomever he wants to succeed him. But it was so sickening of him to be so unkind to Flores who has been such a loyal and trusted secretary and deputy for 18 years ("Schabarum Snubs Aide and Backs Judge as His Successor," Part B, March 16).

It will be interesting to note how Schabarum could attract loyal friends and associates in his future endeavors in Southern California.

VICTOR S. SY

Los Angeles
