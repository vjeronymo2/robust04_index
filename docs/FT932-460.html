930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Plea to save ITV's News at Ten MR Peter Mandelson, Labour MP for Hartlepool, has written to Mr Peter Brooke, the heritage secretary, calling on him to intervene to save ITN's News at Ten bulletin on ITV and to undertake a 'searching inquiry' into the future of the 1990 Broadcasting Act. He has also put down an early day motion expressing concern at ITV plans to move the bulletin, possibly to as early as 6.30pm.
