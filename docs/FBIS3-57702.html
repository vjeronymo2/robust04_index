Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The radio and television debates between Nikolay Bagrov and Yuriy Meshkov, the candidates for the post of president of Crimea, planned for today and tomorrow will not now take place. In an official statement on his refusal, Yuriy Meshkov accused his opponent of using state media funds to discredit other candidates for the post of president. In Meshkov's opinion, this propaganda machine is now aimed at him. In addition there have been threats against him and his family. In such conditions, Yuriy Meshkov believes, it is impossible to take part in a discussion at the same table as Nikolay Bagrov.
