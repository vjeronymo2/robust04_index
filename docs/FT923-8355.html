920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Increased deficit at Howard Holdings An increased loss of Pounds 813,635 pre-tax was reported by Howard Holdings, the property development and plant hire group, for the 12 months to April 30. The decline, from Pounds 494,795, came on turnover of Pounds 5.83m (Pounds 7.04m) and was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 113,110 (Pounds 949,408). Losses per share worked through at 2.08p (1.19p) but the single dividend for the year is maintained at 0.5p.
