BFN [Text] Staff General 'Abd-al-Hafiz Mar'i al-Ka'abinah, chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff today received Vice Admiral Douglas Katz, commander of the U.S. Naval Forces Central Command. They discussed the situation in the region, joint coordination and cooperation, and issues of joint interest. Attending the meeting were the chief of staff of the Jordanian Ground Forces, the assistant to the chairman of the chiefs of staff for intelligence, the Royal Naval Force commander, and the U.S. military attache in Amman.
