940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Secure Retirement Pounds 0.3m in the black Secure Retirement, the developer of sheltered housing which gained a listing in April, returned to profit in the year to March 31 with Pounds 341,000 pre-tax compared with losses of Pounds 2.1m. Turnover rose from Pounds 1.67m to Pounds 2.7m reflecting an increase from 20 to 34 in units sold. Earnings per share were 3.09p (20.92p losses).
