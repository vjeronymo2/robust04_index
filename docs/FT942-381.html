940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Vodafone chief enjoys rise to Pounds 1m Mr Gerry Whent, chief executive of Vodafone, the mobile communications company, last year more than doubled his pay to Pounds 1.04m. His basic pay rose from Pounds 515,144 to Pounds 523,284, with the remainder of the total Pounds 520,840 increase coming from pension contributions dating back to 1990. Vodafone - which boasts the 'most profitable contiguous mobile telephone service in the world' - saw a 13 per cent increase in pre-tax profit for the year to March 31, up from Pounds 322.5m to Pounds 363.3m on turnover 28 per cent ahead at Pounds 850.5m.
