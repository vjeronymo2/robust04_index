BFN [Text] Palestinian leader Faysal al-Husayni says preparations are underway for the elections in the territories. He told reporter Jack Katzenell the proportional system will be used to give the opposition parties fair representation. [Begin recording] [Al-Husayni] I believe that elections will be on 15 October. [Katzenell] Do you know if the Palestinian opposition parties have agreed to take part in the elections? [Al-Husayni] We hope that they will, especially if they will have a list, and we hope that everyone will be there, even if they will be with a small percentage or a bigger percentage, he will be represented there. This is what we are aiming at. [end recording]
