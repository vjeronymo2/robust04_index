930430 FT 30 APR 93 / International Company News: Turnround at Nobel Industries NOBEL Industries, the Swedish chemicals group, yesterday reported a SKr50m (Dollars 6.9m) profit for the first quarter. The result turns round a SKr24m loss struck in the same period in 1992, but does not include an estimated SKr77m payout on preference capital. Sales rose 5.4 per cent to SKr5.50bn from SKr5.22bn, although adjusted for comparable units, the increase was 11 per cent.
