Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Indian reply to Pakistan's non-papers for a plebiscite in Jammu and Kashmir and improvement of human rights situation is not positive, as New Delhi seems to be intent on skirting around on the core issue of Jammu and Kashmir. Briefing newsmen in Islamabad today, a Foreign Office spokesman recalled that Pakistan had given two non-papers to India on the 18th of January for a peaceful solution of Jammu and Kashmir issue and other problems. He said it appears from reply to these papers that India is going back from a meaningful and purposeful discussion on the Jammu and Kashmir issue as agreed upon between the two countries. The spokesman said the Indian reply appears to be a propaganda exercise camouflaged in diplomatic language and the Indians have merely reiterated their old inflexible position on Jammu and Kashmir.
