BFN [Excerpt] Istanbul, April 15 (AFP) - Kurdish rebel leader Abdullah Ocalan, who heads the Kurdistan Workers Party (PKK) fighting the Turkish government, said Friday he was ready to end a 10-year war against Ankara if favourable conditions for a peaceful solution were set up. He made the offer in an interview in the pro-Kurdish newspaper OZGUR GUNDEM, which sent three journalists to interview him at Barr Ilyas in Lebanon. [passage omitted]
