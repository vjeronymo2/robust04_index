930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Sumit net asset value falls to 90p Sumit, an investment trust, reported basic net asset value per share of 90p at December 31, compared with 108p 12 months earlier. On a fully diluted basis the values were 95p (109p). Net profit came out at Pounds 160,681 (Pounds 97,027), and earnings per share were 2.2p (1.4p). The single dividend is increased by 12 per cent from 1.25p to 1.4p.
