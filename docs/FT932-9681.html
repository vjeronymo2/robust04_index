930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / ITV attacked over UK Gold COX ENTERPRISES, the US investor in UK Gold, the satellite television channel, yesterday attacked ITV for suggesting it had been subsidised to enter the UK market. The ITV companies criticised the BBC in a submission to the government last week for getting involved in risk ventures such as UK Gold. ITV said the only obvious beneficiary of the BBC's involvement in UK Gold had been Cox, which owns US cable companies and papers.
