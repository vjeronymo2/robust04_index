BFN [Text] The Libyan foreign minister, Mr. 'Umar Mustafa at-Muntasir, arrived in Islamabad this morning for talks with Pakistani leaders on bilateral matters. On arrival at the airport, he was received by the foreign minister, Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali. Later, the two foreign ministers held talks on ways and means to promote bilateral relations. They also exchanged views on the situation in the region, particularly the Indian atrocities against innocent civilians in occupied Kashmir. During his two-day visit, the Libyan foreign minister is expected to call on the president, Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, and the prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto.
