920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Warnford Invests advances to Pounds 8.2m Warnford Investments, the property investment group, raised pre-tax profit by 23 per cent, from Pounds 6.84m to Pounds 8.17m, in the year to December 25 1991. The final dividend is 4.5p to make 7.25p (7p). Directors limited the increase to 3.57 per cent in view of the uncertainties surrounding the commercial property market. Earnings per share came to 14.51p (10.85p).
