May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: FRONTIER BANK ACQUIRES WESTCO SAVINGS BANK

Frontier Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary of Ventura County National Bancorp in Oxnard, said it has acquired for $750,000 the deposits and some assets of Westco Savings Bank from the federal government's Resolution Trust Corp.

Frontier said it acquired Westco's $116 million in deposits and $35 million of Westco's loans.

Westco has offices in Culver City, La Palma, Wilmington and Harbor City. Orange County-based Frontier has its only office in La Palma.

Ventura County National Bancorp, the parent of Frontier and Ventura County National Bank, had assets of $314 million on Dec. 31, 1989.
