February 16, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

VALLEY COLLEGE FORFEITS MEN'S BASKETBALL GAME

Valley College, reduced to four players in uniform, forfeited a Western State Conference men's basketball game to Glendale on Wednesday at Valley.

"It was a lousy way to win or lose," Glendale assistant Jack Giles said.

Glendale improved to 17-11, 8-5 in conference play with two games remaining. Valley dropped to 17-13, 7-6.

Valley Coach Jim Stephens was unavailable for comment.
