August 20, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BIRD PICTURE

Regarding your article on Cecil Hicks, Orange County district attorney (Aug. 13), I take exception to your picturing former California Chief Justice Rose Elizabeth Bird along with known convicts. We may not all agree with her legal and political opinions, but she doesn't rank with these offenders. You owe her an apology.

E. T. STEARNS

Tustin
