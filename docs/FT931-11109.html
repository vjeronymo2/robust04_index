930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Restrictions on legal aid President of the Law Society, Mark Sheldon, with John Rowe QC, chairman of the Bar, in a lobby of parliament by the Save Legal Aid campaign. Last night Lord Taylor, the Lord Chief Justice, joined with other peers in strongly criticising the proposed restrictions on eligibility for legal aid in the House of Lords. Lord Mackay of Clashfern, the Lord Chancellor, promised to consider alternative proposals from professional bodies but held out little hope they would be acceptable to the Treasury
