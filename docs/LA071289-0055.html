July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"He just ate and ate and ate, and when he was done he said, 'You wanna go to bed? You wanna go to bed?' He was so tired. I'm very happy now."

-- Magda Wilson, 80, speaking about the return of Baby, the parrot she had for nearly 40 years before it was apparently stolen last week.
