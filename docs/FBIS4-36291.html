BFN [Excerpt] Brother Muhsin Bin-Muhammad Bin-Farid, deputy prime minister and minister of planning and development, has said that defending the homeland is a life and death issue. He said that preparations were actively being made for a counterattack on the various fronts. In an interview for the Kuwaiti AL-SIYASAH, the minister stressed that the sons of the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] were prepared and fully determined to regain all their territory and to drive out the aggressive invaders from the gang of the al-Ahmar clan. He said there were thousands of the sons of the Shabwah and Hadramawt Governorates who were going to the fronts and performing battle feats as part of their sacred duty to defend the homeland. They were fighting with high morale because they had a just cause. He noted that the citizens had [word indistinct] well and had expressed their joy at the declaration of the establishment of the DRY as constituting a fundamental change in their political, economic, and social life and a strong factor for changing the political and military balance in south Yemen in general.
