941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cosalt purchase Cosalt is expanding its workwear division with the purchase, for about Pounds 1.2m cash, of certain assets and the associated workwear manufacturing business of the PUC Group, a subsidiary of Davis Service Group. Cosalt has also entered into a three year trading agreement with Sunlight Service Group, PUC's sister company, for the supply of workwear garments.
