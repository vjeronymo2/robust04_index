May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MAYOR BRADLEY'S PROPOSAL FOR MANDATORY WATER RATIONING

If Mayor Bradley wants to begin water rationing, he ought to begin in his own back yard. For the last two months, everyday in the late afternoon as I stroll by City Hall East at Main and 1st streets, the automatic sprinkler system comes on with one particular sprinkler head missing, hence the water just pours out from the shrubbery bed onto Main Street with not one city worker in the least bit attentive to this small hypocrisy.

ANDY AVALOS

Los Angeles
