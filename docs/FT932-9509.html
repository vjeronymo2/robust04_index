930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Scottish Value Trust net assets up 25% Net asset value of Scottish Value Trust rose to 75.12p at March 31, an improvement of 25 per cent over the 59.22p standing a year earlier. Net revenue for the half year to end-March rose from Pounds 191,000 to Pounds 316,000. Earnings emerged at 0.66p (0.82p). The interim dividend is lifted to 0.85p (0.8p).
