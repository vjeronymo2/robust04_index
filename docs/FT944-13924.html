941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / 2% fall seen in electricity prices Electricity prices for the domestic customer have fallen 2 per cent in real terms since the privatisation of the electricity industry five years ago, says the Centre for the Study of Regulated Industries. In its annual report on charges, the centre says typical bills have risen by 10 per cent since privatisation, excluding the effects of the introduction of value added tax, but in that period inflation was 12 per cent.
