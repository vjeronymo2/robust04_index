Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Higher Judicial Council held a meeting under Judge Philip Khayrallah in al-Yarzah today [Defense Ministry's premises] to evaluate the findings of the security departments concerning the church massacre. Judge Nasri Lahhud, government-appointee to the military court; Judge Tarabay Rahmah, first attorney general for Mount Lebanon; and Riyad Tal'i, first military investigation judge; attended a part of the meeting. After deliberations into the Sayyidat al-Najat Church massacre and reviewing new information, which the conferees unanimously decided to keep secret, the Higher Judicial Council approved a proposal by Justice Minister Bahij Tabbarah to appoint former Prosecutor General Judge Joseph Furayhah as investigating judge into the explosion.
