940105 FT 05 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Woodcote acquisition Woodcote Industries, the privately-owned West Midlands forging company, is buying Burton's Scaffolding (International) from Burton Delingpole Holdings. The merger of Burton's with Woodcote's Edge Scaffolding subsidiary will create the biggest manufacturer of scaffolding fittings in Europe. The two units have a combined turnover of Pounds 3.5m, down from Pounds 12m in 1990. Woodcote would not disclose its buying price.
