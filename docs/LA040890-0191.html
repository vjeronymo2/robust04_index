April 8, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

THREAT FROM DROUGHT

It has occurred to me that the water shortage is not the fault of the public's using too much water. I agree we should not use water unwisely during these times.

My question to the city councils and the Board of Supervisors: Why do you let this happen?

As elected officials, your job is not to cave in to builders, city or county staff, and investment groups that say in their EIRs that there is plenty of water for now and all times. From now on, the EIR should include, "What effect will the added use of water have on the residents of our area?" It should have statements from the water providers saying that there will be enough water.

I call on all the people to call on their councilmen, local boards, planning commissioners and ask them why they allowed this to happen.

LEO E. NICHOLS

Port Hueneme
