920409 FT 09 APR 92 / Nato head forced to cancel trip The Nato secretary-general, Mr Manfred Worner, is to undergo surgery and has cancelled a trip to the United States due to start today, a Nato spokesman said, Reuter reports from Brussels. The spokesman did not give details of what was wrong with Mr Worner, 57. Nato sources said the former West German defence minister had told few people the reason for the operation, scheduled for next week. Mr Worner is due to step down next year.
