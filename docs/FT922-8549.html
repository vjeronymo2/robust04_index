920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / Fire destroys Cook Island buildings Fire has destroyed a landmark complex of Cook Islands government buildings and severed nearly all communications with the South Pacific country, AP reports from Avarua. The 60-year-old wooden complex of five buildings housed the prime minister's department, the justice and internal affairs departments, the courthouse and the telephone exchange. It was located in the capital Avarua on the country's largest island, Rarotonga.
