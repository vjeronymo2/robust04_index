Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] Almaty -- Speaking before journalists yesterday, Tuligan Zhukeyev, state counselor of the Republic of Kazakhstan, declared that he regarded as premature the remarks by Yuriy Koptev, general director of the Russian Aerospace Agency, about Russia's getting a 99-year lease on the Baykonur space launch facility. According to Zhukeyev, an agreement in principle has been reached on "Russia's presence at the space launch facility" and on terms of its "paid use."
