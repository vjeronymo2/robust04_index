Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Mar (MENA)--MENA has learned that the Arab League Council decided at its closed session this afternoon to postpone its debate of the proposed amendment of the Arab League Charter and the formation of an Arab court of justice until the first session of the Arab foreign ministers, to be held following the next Arab summit meeting. The Arab League's General Secretariat had prepared a proposed amended Arab League Charter, by which resolutions would be passed by a majority of two-thirds of member countries instead of the current requirement for unanimity.
