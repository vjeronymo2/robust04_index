January 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

TRANSMITTING AIDS

Question: Is it possible for one woman to give the virus that causes AIDS to another woman while having sex?

Answer: Transmission of the AIDS virus from one woman to another is very rare, but it has been reported. Transmission may occur as a result of the exchange of blood either during menses or as a result of trauma.
