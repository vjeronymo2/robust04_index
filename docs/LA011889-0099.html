January 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

James A. (Skip) Lusk has been appointed senior vice president-post production at Universal Studios, Universal City. Lusk previously was vice president-post production at 20th Century Fox. Michele Reese has been named executive vice president-marketing at Universal Studios. Reese previously was senior vice president-marketing at Weintraub Entertainment Group.
