940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: F&C Enterprise Net asset value per share of Foreign & Colonial Enterprise Trust stood at 72.9p at end-December, a 35 per cent improvement over the 53.8p standing 12 months earlier. Available revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 841,000 (Pounds 426,000), for earnings of 0.89p (0.45p) per share. A single dividend of 0.6p (0.44p) is proposed.
