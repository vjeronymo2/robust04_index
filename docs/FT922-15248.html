920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: African Lakes turns in static Pounds 1.55m Following the failure of the hoped-for recovery in the UK motor industry African Lakes Corporation turned in a static pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.55m for the year ended September 30 1991, on turnover down from Pounds 49.4m to Pounds 45.2m. Other sectors of the group, which has forestry and wholesaling as main interests, fared better. Its activities in Malawi turned in the most notable performance and contributed an extraordinary Pounds 188,000 from the sale of property. Earnings per share came out at 6.48p (7.77p) and the dividend is maintained at 2p.
