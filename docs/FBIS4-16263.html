BFN [Text] The president of Kazakhstan has issued a decree on reorganizing the staff of the Republic's president and council of ministers. The decree says, in part: The existing staff of the president and the cabinet of ministers should be reorganized by being divided into the staff of the president and the administration of the council of ministers of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Those made redundant from the staff of the president and the council of ministers will be given social guarantees and compensation in conformity with the Labor Code.
