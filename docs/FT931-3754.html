930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies on the rise - At a Glance In a week when the FT-SE 100 Index reached more all-time highs, small company shares joined in the fun. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 1.2 per cent from 1366.23 to 1382.97 over the week to March 11; the County Smaller Companies Index rose 1 per cent from 1080.21 to 1090.85 over the six days to March 10.
