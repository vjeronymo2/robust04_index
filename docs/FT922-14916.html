920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Election 1992: Computer data - Ballot Box This is the first general election in which the parties are making widespread use of computers, as the article on today's Technology page (page 12) shows. But corporate computer systems can also have political uses. The UK managing director of a well-known international computer company has sent staff a spreadsheet and asked them to fill in their salaries. The system then tells them how much worse off they would be under Labour.
