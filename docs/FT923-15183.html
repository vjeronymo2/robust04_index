920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Partridge Fine Arts shows 34% growth Although trading 'remained difficult' for Partridge Fine Arts, the New Bond Street-based antique furniture and objets d'art dealer, pre-tax profits for the six months to April 30 rose by 34 per cent from Pounds 738,000 to Pounds 987,000. Mr John Partridge, chairman, said that prices of the finest furniture and other items had remained strong. Turnover advanced to Pounds 4.88m (Pounds 4.41m) and operating profits to Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 685,000). The interim dividend is maintained at 1p, payable from earnings per share of 2.94p (2.16p).
