Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Belskiy] [Text] Moscow January 28 TASS -- The Russian Supreme Court of the Russian Federation finished questioning of defendant Dmitriy Yazov, former defense minister of the Soviet Union, concerning his part in the case of the State Emergency Committee, a spokesman for the press service of the military collegium of the Supreme Court told ITAR-TASS. Today's hearings were held behind closed doors as the court session considered issues representing state secrets. The next, open, court session is scheduled for Tuesday, February 1. Oleg Shenin, former secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, will give his testimony.
