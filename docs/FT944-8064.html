941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Fuel distribution contracts won British Fuels, a British Coal subsidiary, and Anglo Coal, a subsidiary of Anglo United, have won the competitive tender to supply and distribute concessionary fuel to former British Coal employees and their dependents. The contracts are worth Pounds 100m. The contract for the biggest concessionary fuel region, centred on Yorkshire, will be awarded to Anglo. British Fuels will have the contracts for the other nine regions.
