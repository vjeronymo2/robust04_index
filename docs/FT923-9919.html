920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Olympic News In Brief: Allan on target for medal Alister Allan, the 48-year-old Scot who has already won two Olympic medals, made a sound start in the three positional free rifle competition. Allan scored 399 points out of a possible 400 in the prone position to lie second equal with three other competitors behind Nils Petter Hakedal of Norway who shot 400.
