940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / London Stock Exchange: BAT recommended The insurance and tobacco conglomerate, BAT Industries, held its own in a badly shaken sector as SG Warburg recommended the stock. International stocks fell sharply in late trading onnews that the US Federal reserve was tightening monetary policy. However BAT, which carries out a large proportion of its business in the US, was unchanged at 510 1/2 p with Mr Mark Duffy of Warburg arguing that the yield on the stock was at a 40 per cent premium to the market yield and concerns over group's tobacco interests were already in the share price. He also felt rumours of BAT buying Frenchinsurer Group Victoire were completely overdone. Pearson fell 13 to 712p, Reuters Holdings shed 30 at 1935p and RMC, tumbled 19 to 967p.
