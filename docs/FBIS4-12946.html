BFN [Text] Brother president Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] this afternoon received the three ambassadors of the the European Union -- the troika -- led by the Greek ambassador, Konstandinos Theodhorakis. During the meeting, they discussed the situation resulting from the arrival of 13 April without the implementation of the Declaration of Principles agreement. The Israeli withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho that was expected to take place was delayed due to Israeli obstructions and procrastination. Brother President Abu-'Ammar explained the importance of the implementation of Resolution 904 to provide international protection for our Palestinian people starting with Hebron. He said: We insist on the presence of the European Union within international protection, especially since we are dealing with it on the economic front as the European Union, in addition to the participation of the two peace conference co-sponsors, the Scandinavian countries and Egypt. Brother president Abu-'Ammar thanked friendly Greece for the financial aid it had presented to the Palestinian police force. Furthermore, the ambassadors of the European Union stressed the importance of the European Union's presence as a union among the international [protection team], and welcomed the participation of some of the member-countries of the union as a first step in the international presence. The meeting also reviewed matters of common concern with the European Union.
