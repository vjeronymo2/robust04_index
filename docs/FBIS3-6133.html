Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 14 (KCNA) -- An upswing in production is effecting at coal mines of Korea. The Sunchon district coal mining complex is now overfulfilling its plan of capital tunnelling by 3 percent, preparatory tunnelling by 5 percent, sloping pit tunnelling by 4 percent and coal production by 1.5 percent on a daily average compared with the previous month. The Myongchon district coal mining complex is fulfilling its coal production at 110 percent on a daily average in March by substantially organizing and commanding production and actively tapping potential reserves. The Anju, Tokchon and Pukchang district coal mining complexes are increasing coal production by managing equipment well and introducing new blasting methods and coal cutting methods. The February 8 Jiktong Youth, Sinri, Hwasong, Thongchon and other coal mines are increasing daily coal production 1.2 times as against the same period last year by keeping equipment in full capacity operation.
