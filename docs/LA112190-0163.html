November 21, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

HARDY OF RANGERS SUSPENDED

New York Rangers defenseman Mark Hardy has been suspended for five games for a stick-swinging incident last Friday against the Winnipeg Jets, the league said today.

Hardy received a five-minute match penalty for belting Doug Evans with his stick early in the second period of a 6-4 Rangers victory in Winnipeg.
