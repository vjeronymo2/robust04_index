930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Richards declines to Pounds 53,000 PRE-TAX profits of Richards Group, the specialist engineer, fell by Pounds 969,000 to Pounds 53,000 in the year to December 31. Turnover dropped from Pounds 13.1m to Pounds 11.3m. The company said the recession had continued to place a heavy burden on the group's performance and in the second half of the year trading losses could not be avoided. However, notwithstanding the profits fall, the directors were confident that prospects for 1993 were brighter and were recommending a same again final dividend of 2.75p, maintaining the total at 4.4p. The dividend is uncovered by earnings per share of 1.03p (9.18p). The shares put on 8p to 68p.
