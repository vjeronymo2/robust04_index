930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: William Ransom William Ransom, the pharmaceutical products maker, lifted pre-tax profits by 21 per cent from Pounds 677,000 to Pounds 817,000 in the 12 months to March 31. Turnover improved to Pounds 7.2m (Pounds 6.91m). After tax of Pounds 274,000 (Pounds 208,000) earnings per share came through at 3.49p (3.04p). The proposed final dividend of 1.378p (1.245p) lifts the total from 1.77p to 1.903p.
