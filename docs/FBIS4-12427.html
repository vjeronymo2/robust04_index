BFN [Excerpt] Pending the signing of the one-year stand-by agreement with the IMF, the financial and monetary authorities have announced that the value of the national currency, the dinar, will be devalued as from today by 40.17 percent. Accordingly, $1 will be equivalent to 36 dinars, compared to 25 dinars in the past. The loan and monetary council has also announced a tangible increase in interests on loans. These corrective measures come to confront the current economic stagnation and to ensure boosting the economic mechanism in the future. [passage omitted]
