BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku 11 May TASS--The parliament and government of the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic of Azerbaijan issued an ultimatum to the local section of the Popular Front on Wednesday [11 May], demanding it to stop unlawful actions aimed at destabilising the situation in the Ordubad District of the Republic. The Nakhichevan authorities also said that they will appeal to Azerbaijani President Heydar Aliyev to impose a state of emergency in the region if the situation is not normalised within three days. The situation in the district, home base of ex-President Abulfaz Elchibey, worsened two days ago, following the appointment of a new head of the administration. Several local leaders and deputies refused to obey the new head and called on their supporters to follow their suit. A group of front activists tried to seize the building of the local executive council last night. As a result of a clash with the police, several people were injured.
