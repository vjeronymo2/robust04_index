Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to constitutional stipulations, the Romanian president has signed a decree on the basis of which the penalties applied to the following convicts are pardoned: Gheorghe Dorus, Ion Manole, and Gabriela Cristina Muresan. Pardoned from the execution of the rest of their penalties are the convicts Stefan Andrei, Silviu Curticeanu, Suzanica Gadea, Mihai Gere, Dan Ludlin, Ana Muresan, Eva Eugenia Nicolau, Titu Popescu, Constantin Radu, Ion Stoian, and Ion Toma. One-third of the penalties applied to the following convicts are pardoned: (Veronica Gansmer) and Ioan Popa. Two-thirds of the penalties applied to the following convicts are pardoned: Pavel Ambrus and Istvan Nagy. Three-quarters of the penalties applied to the following convicts are pardoned: Mihaly Balint, Ferenc Boldizsar, Istvan Illyes, Laszlo Karsai, Imre Nagy, and Elod Vas Kiss. The decree is dated 24 March 1994.
