BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 May (MENA)--Habib Ben Yahya, Tunisian minister of foreign affairs and personal envoy of Tunisian President Zine El-Abidine Ben Ali, arrived here this evening on a few days' visit to Egypt. He stated upon his arrival that he is carrying a written message from President Ben Ali to President Husni Mubarak as part of the high-level and constant consultations between the two countries.
