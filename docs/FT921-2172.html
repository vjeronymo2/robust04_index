920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Ferromet statement on US subsidiary Ferromet Group, which supplies raw materials to the stainless steel industry, stated that an involuntary petition under Chapter 11 of the US Bankruptcy Code, was filed in Texas on Tuesday against Ferromet Resources, the group's principal operating subsidiary in the US, by certain of its bankers. This followed an announcement on March 6 that the offshoot was expecting to report substantial losses.
