930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Symonds slides into the red Symonds Engineering, the Hertfordshire-based engineer, fell into the red in the six months to September 30. Pre-tax losses of Pounds 155,755 (profits Pounds 123,523) were struck on sales of Pounds 2.31m (Pounds 2.67m). Losses per share were not supplied: last time earnings were 0.892p. The interim dividend is halved to 0.15p.
