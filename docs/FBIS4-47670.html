BFN [Text] At least (?53) government soldiers lost their lives in a clash with UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] armed forces in Cuanza Sul Province on 29 April. It was a violent clash that unfolded southeast of Uaku Kungo. Our correspondent says People's Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FAPLA] suffered more than 47 wounded. UNITA forces seized large quantities of war materiel, including 25 AK-47's, four light machine guns, four RPG-7's, 12 TNT blocks, and thousands of rounds of ammunition [words indistinct].
