Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Jan 5 (CTK) -- The Czech Government today granted almost 20 million crowns (680,000 USD) to support minority newspapers and magazines, a government spokesman announced. A similar grant in 1993 amounted to 18.5 million crowns. The Polish, Ukrainian, German, Hungarian, Romany and Slovak minority press will benefit from the grant, Premier Vaclav Klaus said. The government also approved setting up 150 individual grants, to be created by 1996, for third world students wishing to take up university-level study, the government spokesman said.
