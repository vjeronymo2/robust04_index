941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Siemens - Contracts and ventures Siemens, the German electrical and electronics group, has set up a joint venture in Zagreb with the Croatian industrial company Koncar Holding to make power transformers and components. Siemens will be the majority shareholder in the new company, Koncar Power Transformers, and will take a 1 per cent stake in Koncar Holding. Initially, the venture is expected to have sales of DM25m (Dollars 16.5m) a year, mainly in Croatia and Slovenia.
