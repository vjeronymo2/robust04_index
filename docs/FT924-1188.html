921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / Workers occupy Kirkby factory WORKERS have occupied the former Lyons Maid ice cream factory in Kirkby, Liverpool, in an attempt to persuade the new owners, Nestle UK, to keep the plant open. The workers at Kirkby and at another former Lyons Maid plant at Greenford in London lost their jobs in October after Clarke's Foods, which had bought the plants, put the company into receivership.
