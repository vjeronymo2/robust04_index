BFN [Report by station correspondent on 15 Jun proceedings of the current Ninth National Assembly's fifth session in Hanoi] [Summary] "Dear friends: the Ninth National Assembly's fifth session entered its 18th work day today. The National Assembly held a plenary session at the conference hall under the direction of National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh to hear ministers answer questions put forth by various deputies." In the morning session, the National Assembly heard Tran Dinh Hoan, minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Welfare answer questions related to pensions for retired cadres, allowances for wounded soldiers, and wage scales for cadres of education and medical agencies at the grass-roots level. They also heard Construction Minister Ngo Xuan Loc answer questions concerning the prices of houses in Ho Chi Minh City and other provinces, and Trade Minister Le Van Triet answer questions related to the import of papers, motorcycles, and cars. "In the afternoon session, the National Assembly heard Dau Ngoc Xuan, minister and chairman of the State Commission for Cooperation and Investment, answer questions on granting licenses to foreign investors and the results of joint ventures during the past period. They also heard Phan Ngoc Tuong, minister and chairman of the Government's Cadre Organization Committee, answer questions on issues related to the implementation of government decree no. 46 on the policy toward village cadres at grassroots units during the past year, the assignment of cadres to villages and wards, and issues related to the illegal import of cars in Song Be, Tay Ninh, and Dong Nai provinces. "At the same session, Tran Hoan, minister of Culture and Information answered questions on the current cultural activities and management of cultural publications and artistic shows." Tomorrow, 16 June, the National Assembly will continue its work at the conference hall.
