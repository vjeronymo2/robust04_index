931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Iran curbs civil servants Iran's parliament has passed a law banning government employees from a wide range of activities, including unauthorised contact with foreigners and membership in freemasons' organisations, according to Iranian Radio, Reuter reports from Nicosia. The radio, monitored by the BBC, said the new law detailed a wide range of administrative offences and provided guidelines for professional conduct by government employees.
