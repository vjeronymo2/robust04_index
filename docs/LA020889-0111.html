February 8, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP BASKETBALL;

BOYS' ROUNDUP

In other Sea View League games:

Estancia 68, Corona del Mar 52 -- After leading at halftime, 28-26, Estancia went on an 18-11 run in the third quarter at Corona del Mar. Mike Curtis scored 21 points for Estancia, while Brian Spratt led Corona del Mar (14-9, 4-5) with 18. Estancia is 5-4 in league play.

University 53, Newport Harbor 44 -- Erik Glassen scored 17 points and Pat Ferrell 11 for University (10-14, 4-5). Newport Harbor is 2-7 in league play.

In the Olympic League:

Orange Lutheran 75, Capistrano Valley Christian 55 -- Jason Neben scored a game-high 27 points to lead host Orange Lutheran (20-3, 13-0). Orange Lutheran has won 13 games in a row, the second longest streak in Orange County to Saddleback's 18-game streak.

Whittier Christian 65, Brethren 35 -- Scott Ferguson scored 19 points and Paul McKinley 14 for visiting Whittier Christian (17-6, 10-3). Rob Diaz added nine rebounds for Whittier Christian.

Santa Margarita 74, Whitney 59 -- Dan Bathey scored 22 points and grabbed eight rebounds to lead Santa Margarita (8-14, 3-10).

In the Academy League:

Newport Christian 77, Heritage 68 -- Jon Bahnsen scored 20 points and Wayne Everett had 19 for visiting Newport Christian.
