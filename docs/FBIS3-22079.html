Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Interior Ministry has issued a statement on the execution of Muhammad Sulayman Kul Hasan, a Pakistani national, who had been found guilty of smuggling a quantity of heroin into the Kingdom. [passage omitted] Thanks to God, Muhammad Sulayman Kul Hasan, a Pakistani national, was arrested while he was smuggling a quantity of heroin into the Kingdom. The investigation found him guilty of the charges against him. The court then sentenced him to be beheaded with a sword. The sentence was approved by the permanent bodies of the Higher Judicial Council. Royal decree No 4/1263/M issued on 11 Sha'ban 1414 AH authorized the execution of the sentence. Muhammad Sulayman Kul Hasan was beheaded in the city of Jeddah, region of Mecca, on Friday 13 Sha'ban 1414 AH [25 March 1994]. [passage omitted]
