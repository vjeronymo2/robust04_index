August 30, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

HOW TO CONTACT PEDIATRIC AIDS UNIT

A story about Elizabeth and Paul Michael Glaser in last Friday's View section, detailing their family's battle against AIDS, touched readers' hearts. Hundreds of readers have called wanting to know how to contact Elizabeth Glaser's Pediatric AIDS Foundation, which raises funds to fight the disease.

Here is the address: 2407 Wilshire Blvd., Suite 613, Santa Monica 90403. The phone number is (213) 395-9051.
