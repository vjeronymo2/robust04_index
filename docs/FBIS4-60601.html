BFN [Text] In continuation of the Ra'd-7 [Thunder-7] military exercise in the Darenjan region of Shiraz, the operations entered its fourth phase. During this exercise infantry units, an airborne division, and tank units crossed minefields and barbed wire with the support of the ground forces artillery and helicopters and the air force of the Islamic Republic of Iran. They captured various obstacles and dug canals. It is worth mentioning that after completion of this phase, the participating units were deployed in their defense positions.
