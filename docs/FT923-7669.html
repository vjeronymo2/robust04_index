920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / UK Company News: ECC shares surge 24p on disposal Shares of English China Clays rose 24p to 481p yesterday following news of the disposal of its Haul Waste subsidiary, which operates in the south west of England. The offshoot has a turnover of Pounds 15m with profits of between Pounds 1m and Pounds 1.5m. The disposal is consistent with ECC's policy of concentrating on its core businesses.
