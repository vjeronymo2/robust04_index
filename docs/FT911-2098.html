910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / International Company News: Suez decline beats forecasts COMPAGNIE de Suez, the French investment and banking conglomerate, has announced final net attributable profits of FFr3.71bn (Dollars 620m) for last year, slightly higher than its earlier estimates, writes George Graham in Paris. Suez said the result represented a 9 per cent fall from 1989's FFr4.06bn, but only a 4 per cent decline if the FFr189m one-off negative effect of a change in accounting policy on swaps and deferred taxation were stripped out.
