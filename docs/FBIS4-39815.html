BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow, 10 Jun -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin returned to Moscow today from Germany. Let us recall that since 5 June he had been undergoing a medical examination and a course of treatment there. Regarding the rumors that had appeared about his possible resignation from the post of Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin stated: "In society there are still forces putting forth similar rumors." "They are trying thereby to keep the whole country in a state of tension," he emphasized. The prime minister will spend the remainder of his holiday until 15 June in Moscow.
