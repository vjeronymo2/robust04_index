930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / UK Company News: African Lakes African Lakes, the motor trading, mining and agriculture group, recorded a deficit of Pounds 181,184 pre-tax for the six months to March 31. The outcome, which came on turnover ahead to Pounds 25m (Pounds 22.7m), compared with profits last time of Pounds 485,330. The figures were compiled under FRS 3. Losses per share emerged at 4.51p, against earnings of 0.69p, or after adjustment, losses of 5.01p.
