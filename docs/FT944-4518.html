941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / Diva to auction costumes at Sotheby's Dame Joan Sutherland is selling her collection of costumes at Sotheby's on February 8 next year for an expected Pounds 250,000. The highest price, up to Pounds 5,000, is expected for the one she wore as Donna Anna in the New York Met's 1978 production of Don Giovanni. Dame Joan is pictured with Luciano Pavarotti at her farewell performance at London's Royal Opera House, Covent Garden
