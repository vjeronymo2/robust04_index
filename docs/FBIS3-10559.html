Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] The Democratic Revolutionary Party [PRD] has formally requested that the Justice and Peace Commission investigate the participation of PRD party members in violent actions last weekend, as charged by the Arnulfist Party and the Christian Democratic Party. A PRD communique stressed it will respect and accept the commission's decision in this respect. The PRD document added that its Honor and Discipline Commission will be instructed to impose the corresponding penalties if any PRD party member is found to be responsible for the reported events. The PRD called on all its members to maintain their composure and act strictly in accordance with noble goals and Santa Maria La Antigua's ethical electoral commitments.
