931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / People: Cooper moves to Vestey Robert Cooper, 47, is joining the Vestey group as finance director. Since 1988, he has been a director of Robert Fleming, and head of corporate finance worldwide since 1990. At the Vestey group he will be working closely with Sir John Collins, appointed chief executive in September, with effect from January 1994; together they will have responsibility for overseeing the future strategy of the group through the mechanism of a new holding company yet to be formed, and to be called Vestey Group Holdings. Currently the group is divided into two main operating entities, Union International and Frederick Leyland. After qualifying as an accountant with Peat Marwick Mitchell, Cooper spent a short time with first BAT and then Rank Xerox in 1971-72, before leaving to join Robert Fleming in December 1972, where he first became a director in 1977. In 1984 he joined Kleinwort Benson as a director in the corporate finance department. In 1986 he joined the clothes retailer Next as group finance director, returning to Robert Fleming in 1987 as a director of corporate finance, becoming head of UK corporate finance in 1988 and head of corporate finance worldwide in 1990. Robert Fleming said yesterday that no replacement has yet been chosen.
