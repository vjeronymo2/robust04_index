Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on a statement by Turkish Republican Party Chairman Deniz Baykal in Xanthi, government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said that before making a statement the Turkish official should have known the Lausanne Treaty as well as international and Greek law. Venizelos added: Baykal and the Turkish politicians who visit Greece discover a just state that respects human rights. Just like every Greek, the Muslims in our country exercise all of their constitutional rights. The spokesman condemned the incidents against the Turkish politician, saying that the government condemns any form of violence no matter where it is coming from or where is directed.
