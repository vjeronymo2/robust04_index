Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, February 14 (TURAN) -- During the last day attempts by the Armenians to attack Kelbadzharskiy, Terterskiy and Fizulinskiy failed, says the press-centre of the Azerbaijan Defense Ministry. The same source reports that all of Armenia's attempts of advance forward have been stopped. Sustaining tremendous losses, the Armenian units were forced to retreat to their previous positions. The Press-centre of the Defense Ministry denied information from Armenian sources saying that in the northern Kelbadzharskiy region 300 Azeri soldiers were surrounded. According to the Azeri Defense Ministry, the Armenian Army only placed some orderlies on a predominant hill and shelled the road connecting the Northern section of the Kelbadzharskiy region (which is controlled by the Azeri Army) with the remaining regions of the republic.
