December 27, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

COLLEGE BASKETBALL;

ARKANSAS' CREDIT IS GOOD IN 117-75 WIN

Mario Credit scored 23 points in 24 minutes and 11th-ranked Arkansas held Delaware State to two field goals in the first eight minutes of a 117-75 victory Tuesday night at Little Rock, Ark.

Lee Mayberry's basket gave Arkansas (8-1) a 28-10 lead with 12:33 left in the first half. At that point, the Hornets (2-4) had just two three-pointers by Tim Anderson and four free throws by Tom Davis.

Todd Day added 19 points for Arkansas. Davis and Roy Williams had 21 each for the Hornets.

Arkansas plays 12th-ranked Nevada Las Vegas Saturday.
