930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Mermaid Inn to be sold THE MERMAID, one of England's oldest inns, has joined the list of casualties in the depressed UK hotel market. The 15th-century hotel in Rye, East Sussex -once a favourite haunt for smugglers such as the Hawkhurst desperadoes - has been in receivership since March and has now been put up for sale by receivers Arthur Andersen. They are inviting offers in excess of Pounds 1m.
