920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / International Company News: Eon lifts annual net profits to MDollars 103.99m EDARAN Otomobil Nasional (Eon), the Malaysian distributor of the Proton car, which last year took a 15.1 per cent holding in Cycle Carriage (CC), a publicly-listed car assembler operating in Singapore and Malaysia, yesterday reported a rise in net profits to MDollars 103.99m (USDollars 39.96m) for 1991 from MDollars 68.98m a year earlier, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Turnover advanced to MDollars 2.57bn from MDollars 2.08bn and pre-tax profits moved ahead to MDollars 174.16m from MDollars 112.84m. A maintained final dividend of 10 cents a share has been recommended, making a total of 20 cents for the year compared with 10 cents for 1990.
