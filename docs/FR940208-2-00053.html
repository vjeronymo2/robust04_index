Bureau of Land Management [AZ&hyph;040&hyph;4333&hyph;03&hyph;04] Road Closure; Arizona AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management, Interior. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The road generally known as the Black Hills Back Country Byway will be closed on March 19, 1994 between the hours of 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. The road is within Graham and Greenlee Counties located in Southeast Arizona. Personnel will be on site to ensure the closure. This closure is authorized as per title 43 CFR §8365.1&hyph;6; Supplementary Rules. The legal description is within the following townships: Gila and Salt River Meridian, Arizona T. 5 S., R. 29 E. T. 5 S., R. 30 E. T. 6 S., R. 29 E. T. 7 S., R. 29 E. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: For further information, contact Matt Wohlberg, BLM Ranger, Safford District, 711 14th Avenue, Safford, Arizona 85546, telephone number (602) 428&hyph;4040.
