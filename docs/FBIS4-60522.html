BFN [Text] Khartoum, 25 May (MENA) -- Sudanese security authorities tonight seized the residential quarters of the Egyptian Community School teachers along with the administrative headquarters of the Egyptian education mission in Khartoum. The Sudanese authorities expelled the teachers from their quarters and assembled them in one location. It is not clear how many facilities the Sudanese authorities seized. The move is part of a campaign launched recently by the Sudanese authorities to confiscate Egyptian facilities in Sudan. Earlier tonight, the same authorities confiscated a house belonging to the Egyptian Irrigation Ministry.
