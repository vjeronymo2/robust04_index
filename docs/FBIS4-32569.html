BFN [Text] Warsaw, 27 Jun--The Ministry of Defense press and information office said today that, as part of the "Partnership for Peace" program, the first land military exercises in a country that is not a NATO member will take place from 9 to 17 September on training grounds in Zagan. The exercises are planned with a view to preparations for UN peace missions, and troops from some of the states from the Partnership of Peace program will take part. Apart from Poles, U.S. soldiers will also participate. Poland now awaits confirmation from other invited states.
