BFN [Text] Market nervousness at the possibility of a two-thirds majority, once final election results are known, has contributed to the commercial rand losing more than five cents against the dollar. The rand has slipped to nearly 3.605 rands against the dollar, a currency which is in short supply on the local market. The rand has also lost five cents against sterling to 5.42 rands. The financial rand has weakened by 11 cents to trade at 4.71 rands to the dollar.
