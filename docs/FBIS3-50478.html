Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Mexico City, 3 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- [passage omitted] A group identified with the Zapata National Liberation Army [EZLN] kidnapped former Chiapas State Governor Absalon Castellanos Dominguez in Comitan near the Guatemalan border, according to a report received on Sunday night [3 Jan]. Some 40 armed men captured the former state governor along with his brother and his wife, said a niece of Castellanos Dominguez, a retired major general who governed Chiapas State from 1982 to 1988. Castellanos Dominguez also served as regional commander of the Mexican Army and director of the Military Academy in the Mexican capital.
