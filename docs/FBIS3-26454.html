Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Xichang satellite launch center has smoothly completed its large-scale technical transformation. A comprehensive appraisal by scientific and technological personnel indicates that following this technical transformation, the launch facilities can launch many types of carrier rockets. China's comprehensive satellite launching ability has improved. In 1993, the Xichang satellite launch center carried out a large-scale technical transformation, with the support of dozens of scientific research and production units throughout the country. Technological personnel completed the transformation of more than 100 important projects -- including the launch pad system, the booster acceleration system, and the testing system for rockets and satellites -- thus improving the ability of China's space flight industry to join international competition.
