Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, Jan. 6 (INA)--Iraq's Justice Minister Shabib al-Maliki has affirmed that Iraq under leadership of President Saddam Husayn is ready to render all forms of support for the joint Arab action with the aim of countering enemies of the Arab nation and defending the destiny national causes. Addressing the Arab Interior Ministers Council last night, Mr. al-Maliki said that the blockade imposed on Iraq since August 1990 did not prevent Iraq from performing its national role and adopt best means of cooperation with the brethren in different fields, chief among which the fields pertaining to consolidating stability and the pan-Arab security. The minister also called for significance of the joint Arab action for the Arab human being and the Arab destiny and deepening the inter-Arab cooperation to confront with all forms of intervention and foreign ambitions.
