BFN [Text] London, 14 Jun (TANJUG)--A delegation of the Republic of Montenegro headed by Prime Minister Milo Djukanovic arrived in London today. British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd will receive the delegation this afternoon. Tomorrow at the British parliament, the Montenegrin prime minister will meet the members of the British-Yugoslav parliamentary group who arranged the invitation to the Montenegrin delegation. Deputy Prime Krunoslav Vukcevic and Foreign Minister Miodrag Lekic also arrived in London.
