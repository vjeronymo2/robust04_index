BFN [Text] According to the Shenzhen Commission for Restructuring Economy, the city soon will conduct comprehensive experiments in 20 selected enterprises on the establishment of a modern enterprise system. It will open up a new path by summing up the experience gained in these experiments and lay a foundation for the city to be the first in the country to establish a market economy structure. The main contents of these experiments include the establishment of the enterprise legal-person system, the limited liability system, and the scientific management system; the effective solution of such crucial problems as property, personnel, and distribution rights; and the adoption of appropriate supporting measures. While carrying out these experiments, the city will continue to do a good job in the work in three ways: 1. The city will reappraise the stocks and assets of enterprises, register their property rights, harmonize property rights relationships, and institute and perfect an enterprise legal-person system. 2. It will reform and improve the enterprise internal leadership system, and will set up boards of shareholders and boards of directors, as well as a scientific leadership structure of checks and balances -- under which functions and powers are clearly defined -- between [words indistinct]. 3. It will improve the enterprise distribution system and set up a rational encouragement mechanism.
