BFN [Text] Lisbon, April 29 (XINHUA) -- A Sino-Portuguese committee on science and technology met here today and signed an agreement to implement the cooperation accord reached between the two countries last year. According to the summary of the meeting, China and Portugal will launch exchanges and cooperation on 20 projects in such fields as biological technology, material science, agriculture, and environmental protection. During the meeting, each side briefed the other on the policy of their country regarding the development of science and technology and expressed a desire for further cooperation.
