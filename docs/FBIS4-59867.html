BFN [Text] Today Prime Minister 'Atif Sidqi issued statements to reporters announcing that there are comprehensive plans to develop, economically and socially, the governorates of Upper Egypt and to build a number of industrial zones in the new cities of these governorates. Dr. 'Atif Sidqi also announced that the Investment Authority has begun to act on President Husni Mubarak's directives to immediately launch the projects that investors will be carrying out without any hindrances. These investments amount to 10 million Egyptian pounds. The objective of these projects is to encourage industrial and agricultural development, to create new job opportunities, and to increase exports. Dr. Sidqi concluded his statements by saying that this was one of the major points in President Mubarak's Labor Day speech.
