BFN [Text] Russian and Ukrainian experts discussed in Sevastopol behind closed doors on Thursday ways of dividing naval vessels and infrastructure between Ukraine and Russia, a Sevastopol-based source has reported to Interfax. The results of these negotiations are to be announced later. Russia was represented at the talks by the Naval Chief of Staff Feliks Gromov, the First Deputy Commander of the Black Sea Fleet Vitaliy Larionov, and the Head of the Routine Management Department Aleksandr Grinko. Negotiating on Ukraine's behalf were the Naval Chief of Staff Vladimir Bezkorovayniy, the Head of the Routine Management Department Viktor Fomin, and the Naval Air Force Commander Nikolay Volodin.
