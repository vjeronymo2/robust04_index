Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Walbrzych, Feb 3 -- President Lech Walesa, who is relaxing with his family in Duszniki Zdroj, Walbrzych Province, stated in an interview granted to the local "Facts" TV programme that Walbrzych Province has great possibilities that are not exploited. Investments that could serve Europe should be made here. It is a beautiful region and such great possibilities should be used, the president said. Walesa stressed that despite the private character of his visit, he is still in contact with the central authorities. President denied allegations about the building of a chapel for his needs in the "Jodla" army resort where he is staying with his family.
