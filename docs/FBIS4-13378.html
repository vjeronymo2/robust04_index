BFN [Text] The government has decided to import edible oil, sugar, and cotton under the Open General License. The decision was taken at a high-level meeting in New Delhi chaired by the prime minister, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao. The three commodities will now be permitted to be imported at almost zero level duty to ensure availability and to check the rising trend in prices. The government has already made arrangements for import of 1 lakh tons of sugar. Of this 40,000 tons is expected to reach the country by this month-end. Import of 50,000 tons of edible oil has already been cleared. The meeting was attended by the Finance Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh; the Commerce Minister Mr. Pranab Mukherjee; the Agriculture Minister Mr. Mr. Balram Jakhar; the Civil Supplies Minister Mr. A.K. Antony; and the minister of state for food, Mr. Kamaluddin Ahmad.
