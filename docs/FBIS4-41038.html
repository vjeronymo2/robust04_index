BFN [Text] Kiev, 20 Jun -- As Ukrainian TV has reported, newly elected Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol met Oleksandr Stoyan, chairman of the Ukraine's Federation of Trade Unions, and other trade union leaders. They discussed the need to adopt urgent measures in order to increase production, which would remove tension in most of the sectors of the national economy, and promote a strengthening of workers' social protection. They also discussed problems of social insurance and ways of resolving conflicts between the government and trade union concerning the implementation and setting up of a tariff agreement.
