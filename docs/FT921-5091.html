920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Further advance by Metal Bulletin Metal Bulletin, the publisher of trade journals, surveys, directories and information systems, increased pre-tax profit from Pounds 1.64m to Pounds 1.7m in the 1991 year on turnover up from Pounds 10.8m to Pounds 11.5m. The dividend is increased from 6.9p to 7.4p via a proposed final of 5p, out of earnings of 12.5p (12.2p). See Observer
