940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Pacer Systems Pacer Systems, the US-based electronics group traded on the USM, increased turnover from Dollars 27.6m to Dollars 30.2m (Pounds 20.6m) for 1993 but suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Dollars 531,000 to Dollars 357,000 (Pounds 244,520). Earnings per share were 4 cents compared with 6 cents while the dividend is unchanged at 6.5 cents with a same-again final of 3.5 cents.
