930420 FT 20 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Downing St sifts honours nominees MORE than 1,600 names, including about 12 self-nominations, have so far been been put forward under the new honours system by which the public can propose their heroes for awards, the prime minister said last night in a Commons written reply. Mr Major announced six weeks ago that he was reforming the system, seeking to scrap class distinctions and inviting the public to make their own nominations, particularly for voluntary work.
