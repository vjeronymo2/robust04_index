BFN [Text] Beijing, April 18 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng left here this morning on official visits to four Central Asian countries and Mongolia. He was invited by Uzbekistan President Islam Karimov, Turkmenistan President Saparmurad Niyazov, Kyrgyzstan President Askar Akayev, Kazakhstan President Askar Akayev [as received] and Mongolian Premier Puntsagiyn Jasray. Zhu Rongji will be acting premier during his absence, Li announced at a press conference before his departure at the Great Hall of the People.
