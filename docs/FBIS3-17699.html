Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN ["Information from the Government House"] [Text] By a decree of the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers: The Agreement on International Air Communications concluded between the Government of the Republic of Georgia and the Government of the Russian Federation on 8 October 1993 has been ratified. The proposal of the Republic of Georgia Ministry of Foreign Affairs on ratification of the intergovernmental agreement between the Republic of Georgia and Turkmenistan concluded on 17 August 1993 "on international air communications between the Government of the Republic of Georgia and the Government of Turkmenistan" has been adopted. The proposal of the Republic of Georgia Ministry of Foreign Affairs on ratification of the intergovernmental agreement between the Republic of Georgia and Ukraine concluded on 13 April 1993 "on international air communications between the Government of the Republic of Georgia and the Government of Ukraine" has been adopted. The Agreement on International Air Communications concluded between the Government of the Republic of Georgia and the Government of the Republic of Azerbaijan concluded on 3 February 1993 in Baku has been ratified. The Agreement on International Air Communications between the Republic of Georgia and the Republic of Armenia concluded on 19 May 1993 in Yerevan has been ratified.
