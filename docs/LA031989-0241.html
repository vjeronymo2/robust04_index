March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TURPIN'S COLUMNS

Heartfelt congratulations to retiring editor Dick Turpin and his successor, Dick Barnes. Mr. Turpin's retirement plans, which include travel and the "enjoyment of time," refer to a possible book based on his rough-and-ready experiences, which I surely hope will materialize.

I'm much indebted to him for opening up a whole new world for me -- i.e. real estate about which I knew nothing until I started to read his section regularly.

SHERRY TERZIAN

Los Angeles
