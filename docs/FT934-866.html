931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: I&S Optimum net assets at 114.46p Net asset value at I&S Optimum Income Trust stood at 114.46p per 10p share at the end of the six months to November 30, an increase of 20.5 per cent against 95.02p at the comparable date last year. Earnings per share rose from 3.27p to 4.06p. The second quarterly dividend is maintained at 1.85p.
