Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President Itamar Franco this morning concluded his visit to Uruguay where he met with the other presidents of the Mercosur [Common Market of the South]. He left Montevideo at 0900 and should be arriving in Brasilia at 1400. Argentine President Carlos Menem also cut short his visit, leaving for Buenos Aires yesterday afternoon. He feared an attack and left under an unprecedented security blanket at a meeting of South America presidents.
