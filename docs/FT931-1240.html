930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: QSP placed at 380p per share Quality Software Products came to market yesterday through a placing of 2.85m shares priced at 380p to value the accounting software group at Pounds 29.6m. The floatation involved the placing of 1.46m shares sold on behalf of the company to raise about Pounds 5m net of expenses and 1.39m shares sold on behalf of existing shareholders which together represent about 36.6 per cent of the enlarged share capital following the placement. Dealings in the stock are due to begin on March 30.
