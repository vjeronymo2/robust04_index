930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Dual Throgmorton Dual Trust reported a 24 per cent advance - from 581.3p to 718.3p in net assets per capital share over the 12 months to July 31. Net revenue, however, fell to Pounds 1.57m (Pounds 1.61m) for earnings of 6.82p (7.02p) per income share and the trust had to dip into reserves to pay a proposed final dividend of 1.85p and a total of 7.1p (7p).
