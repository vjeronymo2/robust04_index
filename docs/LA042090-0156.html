April 20, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: COUNTYWIDE;

ARTISTS GIVE WORKS TO CHURCH FESTIVAL

Two Los Angeles artists who are widely known for religious art have donated three of their works to Catholic Charities of Ventura County, which will sell them during its annual Gloria Dei Art Festival this weekend in Camarillo.

Artists Isabel and Edith Piczek, renowned throughout the country for their stained glass, mosaics and murals in churches, will be present to discuss their work during the third annual festival at St. John's Seminary, 5012 East Seminary Road.

Also on display will be colorful banners that the Piczeks created for an ecumenical meeting between Pope John Paul II and religious leaders in Los Angeles during the pontiff's 1987 visit.

The festival will begin tonight with a buffet from 6 to 10 p.m. Liturgical dancers from California Lutheran University and a dramatic reading by actor David Soul will highlight the presentation of the first Gloria Dei Art Award. The exhibit will continue from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday.

For further information, call 379-4721 or 495-1269.
