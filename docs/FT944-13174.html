941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Fleming European Fleming European Fledgeling Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 102.2p per share at September 30, up from 88.3p a year earlier. Attributable revenue for the six month period improved to Pounds 373,000 (Pounds 240,000) for earnings of 0.93p (0.6p) per share.
