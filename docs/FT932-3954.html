930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Observer: Well staged Dramatic emphasis was given to the much improved results presented by Chubb Security's Sir Ernest Harrison, whose policy of demerging Vodafone and Chubb from Racal has staved off hostile bids, and seen the value of all three companies rise. He gave his presentation at London's Waldorf hotel. Playing at the theatre next door . . . The Importance of Being Earnest.
