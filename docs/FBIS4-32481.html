BFN [Text] We just received the following announcement from the Press Center of the Ministry of Defense: On 23 June at 1040 [0740 GMT] the Air Force guard duty units noted a violation of Bulgaria's airspace committed by 21 light transportation aircraft flying from near the village of Radovets in Turkey, in two directions: Burgas and Sofia. The Air Force guard duty aircraft were immediately placed in a state of readiness, which included the takeoff of fighters. The aircraft that violated Bulgarian airspace were forced to land at the airports of Saratovo and Sofia. A commission appointed by the Air Force commander is investigating the reasons the flight regulations were violated.
