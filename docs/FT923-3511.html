920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Lower interest costs push up Porvair Reduced interest charges following February's rights issue were behind a 13 per cent pre-tax profits rise to Pounds 758,000 at Porvair in the six months to May 31. The improvement, from Pounds 670,000, was scored from sales little changed at Pounds 7.86m. Interest costs accounted for Pounds 143,000 (Pounds 225,000). The interim dividend is lifted to 1.2p (1.1p) on unchanged earnings per share of 4p. The company, which manufactures microporous plastics for clothing and industrial applications, moved from the USM to the main market in February.
