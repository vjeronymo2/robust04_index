September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RECORD RACK: IN BRIEF: ;

** 1/2 JANE WIEDLIN, "TANGLED," EMI. ALBUMS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE (A CLASSIC).

You wouldn't think the comely, unclad figure on the cover needs a diet, but Wiedlin has to get off the sugar.

Half of "Tangled" is slick and over-sweet. The former Go-Go does much better when setting her fragile, Cyndi Lauper sound-alike voice in more intimate ballad surroundings. Wiedlin also excels in two anthems that engage broad themes of war and freedom with emotional acuity. Why not make every song matter? MIKE BOEHM
