940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican equities went into 1994 with a sharp retreat as investors reacted to the uprising by armed peasants in Chiapas over the weekend by taking profits on the strong run-up of last year. After breaking through 2,600 with a new high last Thursday, the IPC index fell 100.40, or 3.86 per cent, to 2,502.23. It was the biggest one-day percentage drop since August 24, 1992.
