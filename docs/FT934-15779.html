931009 FT 09 OCT 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil BRAZILIAN equities advanced 3.5 per cent in listless trading on the Sao Paulo bourse. The increase in the Bovespa index followed a 5.8 per cent gain on Thursday. At yesterday's close, the index was up 594 at 17,291. Some commentators said the rally had been triggered ahead of constitutional reform talks scheduled to begin next week.
