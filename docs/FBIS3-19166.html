Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin has stated that the resolution adopted by the European Parliament [EP] regarding the lifting of certain deputies' immunities is unacceptable adding that the reason for it is lack of information. Answering reporters' questions in Eskisehir, Cetin pointed out that there is a wish to politicize the issue and take it outside its limits. Indicating that similar decisions have been taken in numerous countries, Cetin gave the lifting of four German deputies' immunities for holding an illegal demonstration as an example. In answer to a question, Cetin stated that Turkey does not make concessions, the government being a government which cannot be coerced.
