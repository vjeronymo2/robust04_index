Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ramiro de Leon Carpio has condemned yesterday's terrorist act that destroyed the bridge over the San Juan River. De Leon Carpio said he did not want to think that the URNG [Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity] staged the attack, because if they did, no one could rely on their political willingness to achieve peace in Guatemala. The president confirmed that the damage has been estimated at 1 million quetzals, including the demolition of the structure, installation of a Bailey bridge today, and construction of a new bridge.
