930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / Japanese Elections: Kiichi Miyazawa inspects damage on Okushiri island Kiichi Miyazawa, Japan's prime minister, wearing a security outfit, inspects damage on Okushiri island. Mr Miyazawa said temporary housing was urgently needed for evacuees after Monday's earthquake off Hokkaido. The ministry of home affairs said it would provide relief grants.
