Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Nikolay Semena: "Crimea: `Rossiya' Party Bloc Wins Elections"] [Text] Simferopol -- According to preliminary information, the greatest number of deputies' seats at the elections to the Crimean Supreme Council held 27 March were won by the "Rossiya" bloc of parties. The Communist Party of Crimea was in second place, and the Party for the Economic Revival of Crimea came third. Around 59 percent of the 1.8 million voters took part in the elections.
