930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Jacques Vert chiefs sell share parcels Mr Jack Cynamon and Mr Alan Green, joint chairmen of Jacques Vert, the fashion company, have each reduced their stake to 26.3 per cent of the capital. According to Ms Denise Williams, company secretary, the sales were made to 'increase marketability'. She believed there may have been an approach from one of the institutions to buy the shares. Each sold 250,000 shares - a 2.6 per cent stake - at 109 1/2 p to raise Pounds 250,000. In the market yesterday the shares rose 1p to 114p.
