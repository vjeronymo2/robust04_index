September 28, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

FULLERTON MEN LOSE 2-0 IN SOCCER

The Fullerton men's soccer team lost to San Diego Mesa, 2-0, Wednesday at Fullerton.

John Tuttle and Enrique Melcer scored in the first half for Mesa. Fullerton's record dropped to 6-3-2.

In another nonconference soccer game:

Cuyamaca 3, Cypress 1 -- Cypress' (1-5-1) lone goal was by Marius Maihele , who scored at the 34:00 mark. Cuyamaca's record is 5-2.

In nonconference water polo:

USC JV 15, Cypress 11 -- Craig Garcia had three goals for host Cypress (8-3). Andy Hoover scored seven goals for the Trojans (9-1).

In nonconference women's volleyball:

Long Beach 3, Cypress 0 -- Long Beach swept, 15-4, 15-10, 17-15, and Cypress' record dropped to 3-2.

El Camino 3, Rancho Santiago 0 -- Visiting El Camino swept, 15-8, 15-7, 15-4.
