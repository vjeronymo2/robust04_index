930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Venezuelan oil deal for BP BRITISH PETROLEUM has agreed with Venezuela's state oil company PDVSA to bring the Pedernales oilfield in the Orinoco delta back into production. The field has been shut since 1985. BP believes it can bring it back into production soon with output rising to 20,000 b/d by 1997. The agreement marks the first upstream involvement of a foreign oil company in Venezuela since 1976.
