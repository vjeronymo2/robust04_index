Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin, Jan. 24 (YONHAP) -- North Korea and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) held a working-level contact in Vienna Monday to discuss problems related to the IAEA's inspection of North Korean nuclear facilities, IAEA spokesman Hans Meyer said. At the two-hour meeting, the two sides agreed to meet again Tuesday, Meyer said. He declined to disclose details of Monday's talks. Informed sources said North Korea conveyed its position on the IAEA demand on the scope of its inspection. It was not known, however, whether the two sides made any progress in Monday's talks held at the IAEA headquarters in Vienna.
