November 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Art by Night: Tonight, the Los Angeles County Museum of Art continues its extended Friday evening hours for "The Fauve Landscape." The special exhibition, plus the Museum Shop and Museum Cafe, will be open Fridays from 6-9 p.m. through December. Free parking is available on Fridays from 5-9:30 p.m. in the museum parking lot on the southeast corner of Wilshire Boulevard and Spaulding Avenue. CLAUDIA PUIG
