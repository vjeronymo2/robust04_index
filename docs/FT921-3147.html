920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Election 92: Can Mr Major stand up? - Ballot Box A KEY question in the election campaign is whether John Major will crack. He does not always seem particularly relaxed when under pressure. There is a strain in his voice that sometimes comes close to stridency. Mr Major came through his interview on The World This Weekend on BBC Radio 4 yesterday well enough, though the questions were relatively soft. On TV there is just a trace of the problem of the young Richard Nixon and the five o'clock shadow: the prime minister sometimes looks as if he is trying to grow a moustache, even quite early in the morning. Such details can matter.
