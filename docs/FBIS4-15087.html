BFN [Text] Yerevan-Stepanakert, 5 APRIL (SNARK)--Ellen Djubua [name as received], French representative in the CSCE Minsk group arrived today in Stepanakert -- capital of the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh. She met with Karen Baburyan, acting Chairman of the Karabakh Parliament, and Arkadiy Gukasyan, Karabakh Foreign Minister. They discussed questions connected with the peaceful settlement of the Karabakh problem. French embassy in Yerevan told SNARK Ellen Djubua is also going to meet with Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan and other Armenian officials.
