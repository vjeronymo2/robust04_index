BFN [Text] According to Minister of National Defense Piotr Kolodziejczyk, military cooperation between Poland, Germany, and other Baltic states is very good. Kolodziejczyk is paying a visit to Germany at the invitation of German Minister of Defense Volker Ruehe. The talks are also being attended by Danish Defense Minister Hans Haekkerup. The Polish defense minister announced that it was agreed to include Finland in cooperation, which has joined Partnership for Peace, and also Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. He said that the first step would be a military exercise of sapper units, planned for October in Jutland and to be attended, among others, by the Lithuanians. At Denmark's request, the Poles will provide them with logistics.
