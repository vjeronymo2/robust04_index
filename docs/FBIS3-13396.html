Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Palestinians have welcomed the cabinet's decision on Kakh and Kahana Hay, but stressed that its test will lie in its enforcement on the ground. According to Faysal al-Husayni, one of the PLO leaders in the territories, the decision is not enough to return the Palestinians to the negotiations table. [Begin al-Husayni recording, in English] If it will be followed by all of the steps that we were talking about and asking for, like evacuating the settlers from the heart of al-Khalil [Hebron], from Gaza, I believe this will help starting fruitful negotiations. [end recording]
