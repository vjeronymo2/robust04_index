BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Kholodyuk] [Text] Tiraspol 1 April, ITAR-TASS -- The state of emergency has been extended by another two months - from 1 April to 31 May 1994 by decree of the president of the Moldovan Transdnestrian Republic. The decree says the state of emergency is to be extended "for purposes of strengthening the civil rights of the inhabitants of the Republic, stepping up the struggle against organised crime in the cities and rural areas of the republic and upgrading the protection of the state system of the Transdnestrian Moldovan Republic."
