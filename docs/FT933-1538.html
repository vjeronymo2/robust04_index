930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ennex loss reduced to Dollars 82,000 Ennex International, the Dublin-based mineral exploration company, reported a reduced pre-tax loss of Dollars 82,000 (Pounds 53,000) for the first half of 1993, against Dollars 262,000. Losses per share came out at 0.1 cents (0.31 cents). The company said that the strengthening of the dollar against UK and Irish currencies resulted in a significant increase in the sterling value of its cash deposits which stood at Dollars 4.2m at the end of the period.
