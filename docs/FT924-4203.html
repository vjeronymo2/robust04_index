921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / UK Company News: 4% advance lifts Alba to Pounds 1.2m DESPITE the recession Alba produced satisfactory results in the home market and, overall, lifted interim pre-tax profit by nearly 4 per cent. For the six months ended September 30 1992 the profit came to Pounds 1.2m (Pounds 1.16m). Turnover fell almost 9 per cent to Pounds 45.1m (Pounds 49.5m). Earnings per share were again 1.82p and the interim dividend is held at 1p. The group makes audio, television and domestic appliances under the Alba, Bush and Hinari brand names, and they were achieving substantial market share, said Mr John Harris, the chairman.
