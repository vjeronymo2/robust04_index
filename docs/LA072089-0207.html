July 20, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

2 SPLIT $11-MILLION LOTTERY JACKPOT

Two lucky players picked all six numbers in the California Lottery's "Lotto 6/49" game held Wednesday night and will split an $11.03 million jackpot, state lottery officials said today. The two winners will receive $5,515,173 each.

Seven players picked five of six numbers plus the bonus number and won $233,275 each, lottery officials said. A total of 369 people guessed five of six numbers and will take home $2,280 each.
