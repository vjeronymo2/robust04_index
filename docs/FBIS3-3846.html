Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Summary] The cable television station of the Jinan Railway Bureau officially began broadcasting on 22 March. This television station was built in 1993 with the approval of the Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television. It adopts an optical fiber cable transmission system, which is currently the most advanced transmission system. In the second half of this year, people will be able to receive 30 programs, which will be transmitted mainly to the Jinan area.
