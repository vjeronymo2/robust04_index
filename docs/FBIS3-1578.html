Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 28 (XINHUA) -- Aisin Giorro Pu Jie, brother of the last Chinese Emperor Aisin Giorro Puyi, died of illness at 7:55 am [2355 GMT 27 February] today, at the age of 87. Pu Jie was a member of the Standing Committee of the Eighth National People's Congress (NPC), vice-chairman of the NPC Nationalities Committee, and a commissioner of the Subcommittee of Cultural and Historical Data of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC).
