940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Raglan Property Pounds 1.6m in black Following a return to profit in the first half with Pounds 89,000, Raglan Property Trust, the property dealing, development and investment concern, finished the year to end-March with Pounds 1.58m pre-tax, compared with Pounds 2.44m losses. Raglan has undergone a 'complete financial and management reorganisation' during the year which produced 'good results', the directors stated. Earnings per share were 2.73p, against losses of Pounds 29.15p, while the directors said that again there is no dividend.
