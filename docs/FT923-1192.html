920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / GE wins Derby plant order GENERAL ELECTRIC of the US, the world's largest power plant supplier, has won a Dollars 77.5m (Pounds 44.2m) order to supply equipment for the 210MW cogeneration plant - producing electricity and steam - planned by the Derwent Cogeneration consortium for Derby. GE will supply a combined-cycle power island including four gas turbine generators whose primary fuel will be natural gas, with distillate oil as a backup. The order was placed by Ebasco UK, the project's prime contractor.
