Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ramos said today that there is no need to send Philippine combat troops to Korea in view of the threat of war there. According to the president, there is no need to panic, and he gave assurances that the Department of Foreign Affairs and the Philippine Embassy in Seoul have already prepared contingency plans for Filipino workers there. Ramos emphasized that the Philippines supports all of the peace efforts to resolve the Korean crisis.
