BFN [Text] On May 11 at about 5:00 in the morning, after artillery preparations the Azerbaijani troops backed by armored vehicles started an attack in the Mardakert Region of Nagorno-Karabakh towards the populated center of Seisulan. The Nagorno-Karabakh defense army's press service said the attack had been resisted and the Azerbaijani troops had retreated to their earlier positions. The Karabakh defense positions on the eastern section of the front were subjected to artillery fire. Hostilities continue in the region. However, the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reported on continuing military actions in the northern and northeastern zone of the Karabakh conflict (the Azerbaijani regions of Agdam and Geranboi). Baku said the Armenian formations kept trying to break through the Azerbaijani positions.
