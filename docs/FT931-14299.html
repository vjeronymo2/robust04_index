930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: SA's maize crop facing failure SOUTH AFRICA'S 1992-93 maize crop is at a critical stage and widespread rains are needed in the next few days to prevent a crop failure for the second year running, industry officials said, reports Reuter from Johannesburg. 'The crop is at a critical point. We need good, widespread and soaking rains in the next few days,' Mr Peter Cownie, head of the Maize Board, said yesterday.
