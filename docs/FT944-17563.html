941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Observer: Forte measure There was an inevitability about Business Traveller magazine's annual awards, dished out at the Savoy Hotel yesterday. Best airline - British Airways; favourite business city worldwide - London, for the 11th year; and so on. But Lord Forte's special 'lifetime achievement award' - marking six decades in business - was at least timely, coming just after the company he founded tightened its grip over the Savoy. The George V in Paris (a jewel in the Forte crown) beat the Savoy as best European business hotel into the bargain, and the 85-year-old hotelier was to be seen reaching up to a waiter's tray to augment slightly mean portions of bubbly.
