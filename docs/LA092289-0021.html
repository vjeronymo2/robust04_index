September 22, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

DISAPPOINTED AT CSUN CUTBACKS

I was very disappointed to learn that Cal State Northridge is cutting back on the number of applicants accepted to their business program for the school year of 1989-90.

While this action may eventually ease the problem of over-enrollment, it does not help students acquire the impacted classes needed for graduation. CSUN should be offering more classes, especially those currently impacted.

I hope CSUN finds a solution to solve their problem of impacted classes, for it is dearly needed, particularly by those students transferring from two-year colleges such as Moorpark College.

LAURENCE BENNETT

Thousand Oaks
