Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] San Miguel de Tucuman LA GAZETA in Spanish on page 12 of its 11 December 1993 issue reports that Colonel Carlos Roberto Matalon was installed as 5th Mechanized Brigade commander, replacing General Rafael Alberto Cazaux. The ceremony was held at the Gregorio Araoz de la Madrid Military School. Col. Matalon was installed by 3d Corps Commander General Carlos Alberto Quevedo.
