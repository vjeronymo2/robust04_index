Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Jean-Pierre Cot Welcomes Clinton's `Important Political Message'"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] In response to the speech by U.S. President Bill Clinton on relations between Europe and the United States, the Socialist leader in the European Parliament, Jean-Pierre Cot, welcomed the "important political message" of the speech. Concerning the opening of the Community market to products from East Europe, however, he stressed that this must be a gradual process as "we cannot afford to get into a major economic and social crisis by opening up completely our trade to East European countries just as the United States cannot afford to do so." Mr. Cot feels that Europe has already "done more than its share and the American side could do a bit more."
