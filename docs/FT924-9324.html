921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Bruntland calls for meeting of EC and Efta ministers Norway's prime minister, Mr Gro Harlem Brundtland, yesterday called for a meeting of EC and Efta ministers in 1993 to fight unemployment, revive economic growth and cut interest rates, Reuter reports from Oslo. 'We propose strengthening co-operation in order to enhance the possibilities of growth and increased employment in Europe in the years ahead,' Mrs Brundtland said in a speech opening the Labour party annual conference in Oslo. Mrs Brundtland, whose government holds the rotating presidency of Efta, said she had written to all EC and Efta heads of government and to the European Commission to propose a meeting of finance and economy ministers in the spring of 1993.
