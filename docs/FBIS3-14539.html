Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our sources in the southern marshlands have confirmed that two of tyrant Saddam's senior officers have been killed in recent battles between tyrant Saddam's troops and the Islamic Resistance forces. They are the commander of the 51st Armored Division and Staff Colonel [name indistinct], commander of the 47th Brigade. According to the sources, the division commander was killed in an attack launched by the Islamic Resistance last week against a military convoy in the area of al-Rumaylah, in al-Basrah governorate. The sources added that the men of the resistance inflicted heavy losses on the regime troops and forced them to retreat to their back positions. Our sources in al-Basrah marshlands have said that tyrant Saddam's 47th Brigade has sustained heavy human and material losses due to the courage and steadfastness of the Islamic Resistance troops. The sources affirmed that dozens of bodies were scattered over the battlefield and that the body of the brigade commander was seen among those of a number of officers of different ranks. Our sources in the southern marshlands report the continued massing of regime troops around the marshlands to prepare for new attacks against displaced civilians.
