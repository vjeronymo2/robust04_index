BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 28 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets staged a joint air war exercise to examine the capacity of a surprise strike on the North in the skies above Pyongchang, Hoengsong and Asan bay, South Korea, on May 26, military sources said. It involved many overseas-based U.S. warplanes and fighter-bombers, assault planes, observation planes, reconnaissance planes and early warning planes belonging to the U.S. Airforce in South Korea and the puppet Airforce. Meanwhile, 105-mm artillery groups and bandits of the puppet army took combat positions in Paju and Yonchon Counties, Kyonggi Province, and Chorwon County, South Korean Kangwon Province, and fired more than 300 shells and bullets of large-caliber machineguns at spots adjacent to the Demilitarized Zone along the Military Demarcation Line.
