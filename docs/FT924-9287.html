921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / British Coal wins Scottish tender BRITISH COAL is to increase its supplies of coal to ScottishPower, the electricity company privatised last year, after a successful tender. It is to supply an extra 250,000 tonnes to ScottishPower in addition to the 2.5m tonnes it supplies annually under contracts running to 1995. Adding in other coal which it sells to ScottishPower, British Coal will provide almost 3m of the utility's total consumption this year of 4.8m tonnes. ScottishPower is also buying 100,000 tonnes from Scottish private mines, 360,000 tonnes from Australia, 250,000 tonnes from Poland, 170,000 tonnes from the former Soviet Union and 60,000 tonnes from the US.
