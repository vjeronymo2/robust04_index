930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Dencora incurs Pounds 404,000 loss Provisions against land values resulted in Dencora, the property and housebuilding group, swinging from profits of Pounds 404,000 to losses of Pounds 936,000 pre-tax for the 1992 year. The dividend is cut from 5.5p to 3p. Fully diluted losses emerged at 3.1p (earnings 4.7p) per share. Trading turnover fell to Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 27.4m) and left total turnover some Pounds 16m lower at Pounds 20.6m. The value of the investment portfolio was written down by Pounds 8.6m to Pounds 72.4m - an average reduction of 10.5 per cent on the value of properties held throughout the year. Net asset value per share fell from 228p to 182p.
