931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / International Company News: More calls to China lift KDD KDD, Japan's international telecommunications company, posted a rise in six-month sales and profits due to an increase in international calls to China and other Asian countries, writes Emiko Terazono. Unconsolidated pre-tax profits for the first half of 1993-94 rose by 21.3 per cent to Y16bn (Dollars 149m) on a 3.5 per cent advance in sales to Y124.4bn. After-tax profits increased by 52.4 per cent to Y10.5bn. For the full year to March, KDD expects pre-tax profits to rise by 12.4 per cent to Y30bn on a 3.3 per cent increase in sales to Y248bn.
