940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / Observer: Double dealing Barclays Stockbrokers is clearly mindful of Samuel Johnson's adage about patriotism being the last refuge of the scoundrel. Unveiling its new Barclayshare Overseas Service, it proudly announces that facilities for dealing on the London Stock Exchange will be available to overseas nationals as well as to 'expatriots'. So what form of proof does Barclays require from potential customers that they have abandoned their wicked ways?
