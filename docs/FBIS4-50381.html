BFN [Text] Yangon [Rangoon], May 17 (XINHUA) -- Myanmar [Burma] leader Senior General Than Shwe has stated that the economic relations between his country and China's Yunnan Province would be promoted further through strengthening the trade and economic cooperation between the two sides. Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of State Law and Order Restoration Council (SLORC), made the remarks here today when he received Governor of Yunnan Province of China He Zhiqiang. Present on the occasion were SLORC Vice-Chairman General Maung Aye [name and title as received], SLORC Secretary-1 Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, SLORC Secretary-2 Lieutenant General Tin Oo, Minister for National Planning and Economic Development Brig. General Abel, Foreign Minister Ohn Gyaw and Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar Liang Feng. He Zhiqiang told the Myanmar leader that the relations on trade and economic cooperation between Yunnan and Myanmar are developing and friendship between China and Myanmar are also deepened further. At the invitation of Minister for National Planning and Economic Development Brig. General Abel, an eight-member Yunnan delegation led by He Zhiqiang and accompanied by a 18-member economic cooperation mission of Yunnan Province arrived here on Monday to begin a 10-day visit to Myanmar.
