BFN [Text] Cairo, 14 May (MENA) -- The Arab Bar Association has expressed its concern about the continuation of the sanctions imposed on Iraq despite the fact that their harmful effects infringe on the Iraqi citizens' basic rights to food and medicine. In a statement issued today, the association said that Arab lawyers, appalled by the continuation of these conditions imposed on the Iraqi people, appeal to the UN Security Council members and president, the UN secretary general, and international humanitarian organizations to immediately lift sanctions imposed on Iraq. The statement was issued on the occasion of the debate that the UN Security Council will hold tomorrow to discuss continuing the sanctions on Iraq.
