BFN [Text] Russia plans to complete its program for the privatization of military enterprises by the end of 1994, said the State Committee for the Defense Industry. According to a report prepared by the committee for parliamentary debates, more than 700 enterprises have already been privatized of a total of 1,500 subject to denationalization. "The pace of privatization in the defense industry is constantly increasing," the report says. While before 1993 it was decided to privatize 131 enterprises, by the beginning of this year the number trebled. Today another 640 are undergoing privatization, the report says. It says privatization has been particularly intensive in the electronics, aircraft and ship-building industries. The report also says the State Committee for the Defense Industry is making serious efforts to attract capital to military enterprises. Thus, investment projects involving more than 170 enterprises are due to be carried out this year, the report says.
