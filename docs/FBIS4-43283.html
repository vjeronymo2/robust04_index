BFN [Text] Ankara, June 14 (A.A.) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese on Tuesday [14 June] said that Turkey will not allow any activities against Iran within its territories. Iranian Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammad Besharati-Jahromi, who is currently in Ankara on an official visit, said that Iran's borders are closed to the enemies of Turkey. Besharati-Jahromi, who is continuing his contacts, in the afternoon visited Mentese at his office. Mentese told reporters that Besharati on Monday met with President Suleyman Demirel, Prime Minister Tansu Ciller and Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin and the meetings were held in a positive atmosphere. As for Besharati-Jahromi, he said that relations with Turkey are developing day by day, especially in cultural and security fields. He said that Iran was helpful to Turkey in combatting drug trafficking, especially those smuggled through its borders, adding that Iran's borders are closed to the enemies of Turkey. `I can assure you that there are no PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] members in Iran,' Besharati-Jahromi said.
