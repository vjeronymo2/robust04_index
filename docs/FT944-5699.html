941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / Contracts won for bus advert sales LTA, formerly London Transport Advertising, which was privatised in August, has won four contracts to sell advert ising on buses outside the capital. The company, which is now owned by TDI, the largest transport advertising operator in the US, will sell national advertising on buses owned by British Bus, and on buses in Cambridge, Maidstone and the West Country.
