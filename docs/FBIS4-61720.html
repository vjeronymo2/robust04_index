BFN [Text] Candidate of Economic Sciences Mikalay Pankrutski has been appointed economic adviser to the Belarusian Supreme Soviet chairman. Mikalay Pankrutski, 34, graduated from the Belarusian Agricultural Academy; worked at the Western Region Department of the All-Union Academy of Agricultural Sciences and at the International Agricultural Administration of the Belarusian Cabinet of Ministers. He also completed a training course at the World Bank on analysis of economic projects.
