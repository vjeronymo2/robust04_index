BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 25/5/1994 -- The government of the self-proclaimed Dniester republic today requested President Boris Yeltsin and the Federal Assembly to facilitate the shipment of Dniester rubles to Tiraspol. Boris Akulov, deputy of the Dniester supreme soviet, told BASA that the banknotes are currently stored at the Gosznak plant in Moscow. Previously, some Russian organs blocked their shipment to Tiraspol. This decision was later annulled by the Russian Arbitration Court at the Dniester official's request. According to Akulov, there is the shipment of banknotes to Tiraspol. A delegation comprising Dniester deputies and public organization representatives has been organized and will leave tomorrow for Moscow to conduct negotiations on shipping the banknotes as soon as possible.
