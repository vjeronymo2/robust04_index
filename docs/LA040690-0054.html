April 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Coe Leaves Philharmonic: NancyBell Coe, general manager of the Los Angeles Philharmonic, will take over as manager of the Cleveland Orchestra beginning in June. Coe said she decided to leave the Philharmonic for "personal reasons." Jim Donahue, Coe's executive assistant, was named as her replacement. BETH KLEID
