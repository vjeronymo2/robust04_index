BFN [Text] There was a barrage of explosions last night in the center of Athens. Specifically, within 15 minutes at around midnight homemade explosive devices went off in a school bus from the Kostas Yitonas educational institute at the intersection of Skiros and Evelpidhes Streets in Kipseli [Athens suburb], at a refreshment shop on Andrianos Street in Plaka [Athens district], and outside the Thivai textile mill on Kapnitareas Street. A very strong explosion also took place at 0230 today at the intersection of Vasilissis Sofias and (Iridhanos) Streets, destroying a small, white truck with the registration No. YZ8660. The Antiauthority Battle [Andiexousiastiki Pali] organization made a telephone call to a private radio station claiming responsibility only for the first explosion.
