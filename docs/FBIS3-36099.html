Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the beginning of today's Supreme Soviet session, Chairman Myechyslaw Hryb congratulated People's Deputy Leonid Dzeyka on the occasion of his birthday. Next, the lawmakers began discussing miscellaneous issues. People's Deputies Shushkevich, Henadz Karpenko, Syarhey Antonchik, and others delivered speeches. Then, the chairman proposed to discuss several key issues. The deputies adopted the Supreme Soviet decree: "On Implementation of the Law of the Republic of Belarus on Joint Stock Companies, Companies With Limited and Additional Liability." The deputies introduced several changes and supplements to current legislation and adopted the Law on Vouchers. In addition, the deputies had the second reading of the law on state tax inspection in the Republic of Belarus. Two hundred and six deputies voted for the adoption of this law. A corresponding Supreme Soviet decree was also adopted. Later, the deputies began to dispute the law on pensions. They introduced some changes and amendments to this law. The deputies are currently on a break. The evening session will begin at 1600 [1400 GMT].
