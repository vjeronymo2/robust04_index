Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Mar. 9 (A.A.) -- Police seized 12 kgs. (27 lbs.) of heroin and 164 kgs. (361 lbs.) of hashish in various districts of Istanbul on Wednesday and 11 people were taken into custody. Officials said security forces acting on a tip captured Kerem Tekin, the purchaser, in the district of Kocamustafapasa. The hashish was seized in Zeytinburnu, Tuzla, Gungoren and Tozkoparan Districts.
