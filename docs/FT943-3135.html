940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Era loss at Pounds 2.26m Era Group, the specialist retailer and distributor, reported slightly reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.26m against Pounds 2.59m for the six months to June 30. Turnover was flat at Pounds 29.1m (Pounds 29.2m). Losses per share came through at 0.72p (2.91p restated).
