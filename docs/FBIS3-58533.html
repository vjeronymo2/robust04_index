Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Germany's Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has urgently appealed to Iran to pardon the German Helmut Szimkus, sentenced to death for espionage. The German Government regards the death sentence recently confirmed by Iran's supreme court as an "extremely serious affair," Kinkel said in Bonn today during talks with Mohamad Larijani, secretary of the International Relations Committee of Iran's Supreme Council for National Security. Kinkel welcomed the intention voiced by Larijani to press for a resolution of the case, the Foreign Ministry added.
