Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn -- FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) gave assurances at a news conference that the foreign ministers of the European Union [EU] will meet again next Tuesday [15 March], because "we do not give up so quickly." Kinkel regretted that the breakthrough "in the vexatious fishing issue" has failed because of Spain. It is not justified to speak of a crisis, even in view of the differences of opinion with Great Britain concerning the majorities in a 16-member EU. Kinkel appealed to the European Parliament in Strasbourg not to let the procedure for membership fail because of the pressure of time before the new elections. He is convinced that the negotiations with Norway will be concluded successfully.
