May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

LOVINGIER'S NO-HITTER PACES LAGUNA HILLS

Kevin Lovingier pitched his second no-hitter of the season in Laguna Hills' 4-0 victory over Trabuco Hills in a Pacific Coast League baseball game Tuesday at Laguna Hills.

Lovingier (11-1), a left-handed senior, struck out 14 and walked three and Laguna Hills (16-7, 11-1) clinched at least a tie for the league championship.
