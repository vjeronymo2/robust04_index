Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Feb. 14--Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak appointed Secretary of State and Deputy Finance Minister Henryk Chmielak as head of the Finance Ministry, the government press office reported on Monday. Henryk Chmielak has got a PhD in economics. In the years 1971-1987 he worked at the Higher School of Social Sciences and the Main School of Planning and Statistics (SGPIS.) From 1987 till 1991 he worked at the Central Office of Planning (CUP.)
