BFN ["NODONG SINMUN Supports Struggle of South Korean Students Against Rice Market Opening" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 4 (KCNA) -- The struggle of the South Korean students against the "parliamentary endorsement" of "the Uruguay-Round accord enforcement plan" including rice market opening is a most righteous patriotic action, stresses NODONG SINMUN in a commentary today. It says: Handing over the whole rice market of South Korea to outside forces by the traitor Kim Yong-sam is a treacherous act to prolong his dirty remaining days as a colonial lackey by selling off the interests of the country and the nation. It is the unshakable will of the South Korean students and people to overthrow the treacherous group, not yielding to any suppression by the "civilian" dictators. Their fighting spirit cannot be broken by any fascist bayonet.
