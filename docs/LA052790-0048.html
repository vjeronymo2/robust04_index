May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

UES MOVE CALLED AN EXCELLENT IDEA

In my opinion, the move of University Elementary School to Ocean Park is an excellent idea.

Our family has researched UES for our children to attend. The possibility of attending UES at its present location is slim to none, and the proposed move would bring UES to our neighborhood as a public school. This quality school could benefit many families like ours without the worry of financial burden.

The proposed site is a superior location for the students, as well as the surrounding community. The present public school (John Muir) is located on a very busy intersection, and UES needs to be relocated from its current privileged isolated location into a neighborhood setting.

BOB MOORE

Santa Monica
