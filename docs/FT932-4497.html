930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: 'Purdah' to begin at conference THE traditional pre-Budget 'purdah,' when Treasury ministers refrain from public comment, is expected to start immediately after Mr Kenneth Clarke, the Chancellor, addresses the Tory party conference in October. Treasury officials are drawing up a timetable for the run up to the first unified Budget, in November, that envisages a shorter than usual 'purdah' but does not abolish the convention completely.
