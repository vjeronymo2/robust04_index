921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute Spot and shipment sales for the week ended 11 December amounted to 228 tonnes against 116 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in Central Asian, African, and Russian descriptions.
