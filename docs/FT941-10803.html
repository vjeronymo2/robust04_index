940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / Temporary closure for City station Fenchurch Street Station, in the City of London, is to be closed for seven weeks from July as part of an Pounds 83m resignalling project. The station handles thousands of commuters daily on lines from Southend, Essex. Travellers face up to an extra hour's journey to London. Network SouthEast said: 'We realise this closure will cause inconvenience to many of our passengers. We hope they will understand the reason for the service alteration.'
