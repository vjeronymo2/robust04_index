Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 11 KYODO -- Japan will extend a total of 1.57 billion yen to Cambodia to help with projects to improve water supplies and sewage systems in Phnom Penh and to repair a road construction center, the Foreign Ministry said Tuesday [11 January]. The latest offering brings total Japanese aid to Cambodia for this fiscal year to 7.9 billion yen, the ministry said. The assistance includes 98 million yen to repair and expand a water purification plant and construct conduit pipes, it said. It also includes up to 59 million yen to purchase equipment to repair the road construction center, it said.
