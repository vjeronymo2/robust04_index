940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / UK Company News: BIT net assets show 18% increase Net asset value per share of the British Investment Trust showed a 17.7 per cent in-crease over the year to March 31 1994, rising from 198p to 233p. Ordinary shareholders' funds grew from Pounds 617m to Pounds 727m. Net revenue for the year increased to Pounds 20.4m (Pounds 14.5m), and after minorities of Pounds 3.26m (Pounds 1.11m) and preference dividend payments totalling Pounds 77,000 (same), attributable profits came out at Pounds 17m (Pounds 13.4m). Earnings per share emerged at 4.97p (4.48p) and a proposed final dividend of 2.85p raises the total to 4.85p (4.5p).
