931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Aborted acquisition costs slow H Young H Young Holdings, the marketing and distribution group, reported profits of Pounds 787,000 pre-tax for the 12 months to September 30 on turnover of Pounds 32.7m. The outcome, struck after aborted acquisition costs of Pounds 231,000 and losses on discontinued operations of Pounds 263,000, compared with pre-tax losses, restated for FRS 3, of Pounds 1.48m. Turnover last time amounted to Pounds 31.1m, including Pounds 1.13m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share under the new accounting standard emerged at 3p (losses of 8.3p); a proposed final dividend of 1.3p brings the total to 3.3p (3p).
