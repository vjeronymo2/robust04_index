940131 FT 31 JAN 94 / Observer: Mudslinger Field Walton, the former Leicester prop-forward and Cazenove stockbroker who took over as chairman of Manchester brokers Henry Cooke Lumsden a few months ago, seems to be getting the right sort feedback from the local business community. 'Dealing with auditors,' a local merchant tells him, 'is like wrestling in mud with a pig. You think it's all pretty ghastly - then you find the pig's enjoying it.'
