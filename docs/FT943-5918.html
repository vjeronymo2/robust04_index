940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Pacific Horizon Net asset value at Pacific Horizon Investment Trust at July 31 was 49.65p per share, a rise of 19 per cent on the 41.74p of a year earlier. Available revenue was almost trebled at Pounds 107,001, against Pounds 37,520. Earnings per share worked through at 0.26p (0.09p) and a final dividend of 0.11p (nil) is recommended.
