941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cranswick surges to Pounds 1.31m A more stable trading environment helped Cranswick, the supplier of grain, feed, livestock and meat products, hoist pre-tax profits by 75 per cent from Pounds 745,000 to Pounds 1.31m in the six months to September 24. Mr Jim Bloom, chairman, said that despite low pig prices, there had not been the same degree of volatility as last year. Turnover grew by Pounds 2m to Pounds 56.2m. Earnings per share climbed from 4p to 6.1p and the interim dividend is stepped up to 2.45p (2.4p).
