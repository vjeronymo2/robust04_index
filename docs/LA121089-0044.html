December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

VICTOR SALMONES; FAMED MEXICAN SCULPTOR

Victor Salmones, 54, a Mexican-born sculptor whose bronze figures are in public buildings throughout the world. Locally his "Force" is at the UCLA Sculpture Garden while "Aquarius" and "Water Gods" are in place at Metromedia Square in Hollywood and on Sunset Boulevard in Beverly Hills respectively. Others are in Acapulco, the Mayo Clinic and in the hands of private collectors and corporations in 30 countries. In Los Angeles on Tuesday of cancer.
