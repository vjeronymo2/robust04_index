BFN [Text] Geneva, 7 May (INA)--Iraq has urged the WHO to intercede with the UN Security Council to secure the release of frozen Iraqi assets, so that it can purchase medicines and other basic human necessities and medical supplies. In an address to the 47th WHO session currently convening in Geneva, Health Minister Umid Midhat Mubarak urged the international community to speak out against the continued blockade imposed on Iraq since Iraq has honored its commitments in accordance with the pertinent UN Security Council resolutions. The health minister reviewed the health situation in Iraq in light of the unjust economic blockade imposed on the Iraqi people.
