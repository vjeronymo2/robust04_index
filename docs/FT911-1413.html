910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Finance & The Family: Smaller company investment trusts Two smaller company investment trusts were launched this week - from Aberforth and Moorgate - as managers attempted to call the tune in the sector. There was little movement in small company share prices, however. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (Capital Gains Version) rose by 0.1 per cent to 1234.57 and the County Nat West small companies index fell 0.1 per cent to 1008.2.
