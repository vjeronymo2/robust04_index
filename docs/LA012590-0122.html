January 25, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COLLEGE BASKETBALL

TODAY'S HIGHLIGHTS

WESTERN ATHLETIC CONFERENCE

SAN DIEGO STATE (11-8, 3-3) at HAWAII (15-4, 4-2), 9:30 p.m. (PST)

TV -- None. Radio -- KFMB (760).

The first round of WAC play comes to a close, and the Aztecs are still trying to establish some consistency. Case in point: Last Saturday in San Diego, the Aztecs led Texas El Paso at halftime, 44-38, but shot a frigid 24% in the second half and lost, 71-63. Michael Best made just two of 11 shots and scored five points in 33 minutes after scoring 24 points (10 of 13 shooting) Thursday against New Mexico. Center Marty Dow has been SDSU's most consistent force in WAC play, averaging 15 points and 6.3 rebounds. He will be key against Hawaii's strong rebounding team (second in WAC rebounding advantage at an average of 4.9 more than opponents per game). Terry Houston leads Hawaii with 6.7 rebounds a game. Chris Gaines, who missed second half of last season because he was academically ineligible, is Hawaii's leading scorer (19.3 points).
