SUMMARY: In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (title 44, U.S. Code, chapter 35), this notice announces a request for reclearance of an information collection. OPM Form 2809&hyph;EZ22, Open Season Health Benefits Enrollment Change Form, is used by annuitants only at Open Season to elect a change in health benefits coverage. Approximately 36,559 OPM Forms 2809&hyph;EZ2 are completed annually. Each form takes approximately 30 minutes to complete. The annual burden is 18,280 hours. For copies of this proposal, contact C.
