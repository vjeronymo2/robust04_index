Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A demonstration by the Coalition for the Defense of the Republic [CDR] is being held in Kigali. At 1600, members of the Coalition for the Defense of the Republic sealed off the roundabout outside the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and hoisted their party's flag in front of the ministry. The CDR party wants to claim the seat reserved for it in the future National Assembly, as stipulated in the Arusha Peace Accord. As I speak to you, the demonstration is continuing and the personnel from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation, men and women, are being held hostage by the demonstrators.
