930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / Conoco base move may cost jobs CONOCO, the oil company, yesterday announced plans to move its entire British exploration and production work to Aberdeen. The company, which has offices in London and Warwick, implied the restructuring would involve job losses in London where it employs 450, but gave no figures. It employs 400 in Aberdeen on production operations, 250 in Warwick and 400 offshore.
