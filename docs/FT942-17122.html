940406 FT 06 APR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves Housebuilding and construction group George Wimpey jumped 11 to 198p after taking full control of its West of England and Welsh quarrying subsidiary Wimpey Hobbs. International Business Communications rose 21 to 207p on a sharp upturn in 1993 profits. USM-quoted Proteus International, which announced a one-for-seven cash call last week, slipped below the rights issue price to close 7 off at 178p.
