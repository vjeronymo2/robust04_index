920429 FT 29 APR 92 / UK Company News: NI merger creates First Trust Bank The merged AIB and TSB banks in Northern Ireland will be known as the First Trust Bank, it was announced yesterday. The merger will bring a combined workforce of 1,500 and more than 100 branches to the province. The First Trust Bank identity will be introduced to TSB branches in the coming months and extended to all AIB group offices in Northern Ireland during 1993.
