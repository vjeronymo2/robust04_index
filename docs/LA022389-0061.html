February 23, 1989, Thursday, South Bay Edition

TOROS CAGERS BATTLE FOR HOST HONORS IN CCAA

The Cal State Dominguez Hills men's basketball team can secure its playoff future with a pair of victories this week.

Wins over visiting Cal State Bakersfield tonight at 7:30 and Cal Poly San Luis Obispo on Saturday night at 8:05 will assure that the Toros will play host to the California Collegiate Athletic Assn. postseason tournament March 4-5

Dominguez Hills (18-7) is tied with UC Riverside (21-4) for the CCAA lead at 10-2. If the conference race finishes in a tie, the tiebreaker would come down to each team's record against Bakersfield, which is in third place. Riverside lost at Bakersfield last week, so a pair of Toro wins over Bakersfield would put the tournament in Carson. PAUL McLEOD
