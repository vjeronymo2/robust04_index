940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Bearing Power at Pounds 404,000 Despite continuing pressure on margins, Bearing Power International, which distributes precision bearings and power transmission components, raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 257,000 to Pounds 404,000 in the half year to March 31. Acquisitions helped turnover jump 58 per cent to Pounds 17.9m. After a tax charge of Pounds 120,000 (Pounds 26,000 credit), earnings per share emerged lower at 0.84p (0.91p). There was again no interim dividend - last year's single distribution was 0.75p.
