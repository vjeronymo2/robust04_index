Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Hamburg (DDP/ADN) -- The death sentence against the German engineer Helmut Szimkus, who is in prison in Iran, is to be commuted into a prison sentence, according to BILD AM SONNTAG. The paper reports that a delegation from the Tehran Foreign Ministry had given Bernd Schmidbauer, the state secretary at the Federal Chancellery, a promise to that effect last week. Szimkus was given a death sentence for allegedly spying.
