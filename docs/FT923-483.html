920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / Fraud sours Nigeria polls Boycotts and fraud charges soured the final round of Nigeria's presidential primaries, Reuter reports from Lagos. Police arrested at least 50 people on electoral malpractice charges during Saturday's polls in 10 states, boycotted by nine of the 12 Social Democratic Party (SDP) candidates. Elsewhere, polling was marked by low turnout and general apathy.
