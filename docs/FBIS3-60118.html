Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Sergy Krayukhin report: "Drugs Business Incurs Losses"] [Text] St. Petersburg -- St. Petersburg City Internal Affairs Administration [MVD] personnel in conjunction with Moskovskiy and Frunzenskiy Rayon task forces have detained four drug dealers doing a brisk trade in a prohibited substance. Thanks to a skilfully planned operation, the underground dealers were arrested with incontrovertible evidence--while they were trying to sell over 20 kg of poppy haulms [makovaya solomka]. A resident of Pskov Oblast, a native of Chechnya, was also caught on Kolomenskaya Street. Some 17 kg of plant raw materials for the production of drugs were confiscated from this drug baron. Criminal proceedings have been instituted.
