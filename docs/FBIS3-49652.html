Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Generals Ante Roso and Rasim Delic, commanders of the Croat and Muslim forces in Bosnia-Herzegovina, have agreed that they will determine a way to implement a cease-fire that is due to come into force on 15 January, the UN Protection Force in Sarajevo announced today. The cease-fire should be proclaimed in accordance with an agreement concluded in Bonn between Croatian President Franjo Tudjman and Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic. Commanders Delic and Roso also agreed that they will begin negotiations on the exchange of POW's and the return of two Muslim army helicopters that have been held in Medjugorje for the past two months.
