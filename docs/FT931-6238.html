930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Cosa Nostra suspect denies Mafia links Salvatore 'Toto' Riina, (left) accused by the Italian authorities of heading Cosa Nostra, the umbrella organisation of the Sicilian Mafia, denied he had been a Mafia 'superboss'. 'I don't know what Cosa Nostra is,' he said in a Palermo court, in his first public appearance since his arrest in January after being on the run for 24 years. Riina testified for an hour at a trial for the Mafia killings of three Sicilian politicians which began before his arrest.
