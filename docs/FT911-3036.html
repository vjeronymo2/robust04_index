910426 FT 26 APR 91 / Technology (Worth Watching): Shorter road to the Soviet Union WESTERN companies looking for partners in the Soviet Union often face difficulties in finding the appropriate company or agency with which to join forces. To help electronics companies in this plight, trade consultancy Asumer, of Espoo, Finland, has compiled a directory of 700 organisations in some 140 Soviet cities. The directory - USSR Electronics Industry Manufacturing Organisations and Design Centres - contains details of companies such as design bureaux, microelectronics and components companies and industrial automation manufacturers. A paper copy of the manual plus a disc costs Dollars 995 (Pounds 600). Contacts: Asumer: Finland, 0 465 557.
