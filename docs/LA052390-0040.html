May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Record Relief: HBO's Comic Relief '90 has generated a record $8.6 million, Comic Relief President Bob Zmuda announced. As of May 21, pledges for the fourth all-star event to benefit America's homeless were running $2 million higher than they were at the same point for Comic Relief III. Pledges are still being accepted at Comic Relief's 24-hour toll-free number, (800) 528-1000. GREG BRAXTON
