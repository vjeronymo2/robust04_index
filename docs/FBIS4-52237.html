BFN [Text] Five top leaders of CPP-NPA-NDF [Communist Party of the Philippines-New People's Army-National Democratic Front] fell into the hands of the military authorities of Bulacan PNP [Philippine National Police] Command, Recom [Regional Command] III Intelligence and PNP Intelligence Command, after they were arrested on their way to a meeting in Barangay [village] Santiago, Malolos, Bulacan. Those arrested were identified as Wilma Austria-Tiamzon alias Comrade Rhea, Eugenia Magbantay-Kabaso and her sister Caridad Magbantay-Pascual alias Caring, Luisito de la Cruz alias Ted or Janus and Gerry Rato: all ranking members of executive committee or the Politburo of Central Luzon Regional Party Committee of CPP-NPA-NDF. The five are presently under the custody of Bulacan PNP headquarters and are scheduled to be brought to Camp Crame at 3 o'clock this afternoon.
