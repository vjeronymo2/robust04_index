930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Boost to currency and gold reserves BRITAIN'S official gold and foreign currency reserves rose by Dollars 760m (Pounds 493.5m) last month, bringing reserves at the end of the month to Dollars 41.65bn compared with Dollars 40.89bn at the end of March. Economists suggested that the government had taken advantage of the stronger pound to replenish its reserves of foreign currency.
