October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WILSON, FEINSTEIN ON TERM LIMITS

Feinstein is perplexing. She's running against Wilson, a man of strong 19th-Century views, who, for example, has voted again and again to finance the murderous regime in El Salvador.

Yet, Feinstein chides him for absenting himself from votes in Washington -- a cause, rather, for rejoicing. Dianne, get with it.

ANTHONY SAIDY

Los Angeles
