Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yegor Gaydar's possible resignation from the post of first vice premier "would seriously weaken the president's reformatory flank", the president's Press Secretary Vyacheslav Kostikov told Interfax special correspondent Vyacheslav Terekhov in an exclusive interview on Sunday [16 January]. Kostikov expressed hope that Boris Yeltsin would not accept Gaydar's resignation while the reasons for Gaydar's decisions "were not strategic" and that a compromise was possible.
