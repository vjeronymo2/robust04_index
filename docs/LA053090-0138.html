May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS;

DISTRIBUTED LOGIC CORP. OF ANAHEIM PICKS 3 VPS

Distributed Logic Corp., an Anaheim-based computer products company, has promoted three senior managers to new posts as vice presidents.

William E. Walker and Paul Cummings have been named vice presidents in the sales division, supervising sales to original equipment manufacturers and to end-users, respectively.

Lewis Wagner is the new vice president of operations.

Walker joined Dilog in 1985 as western region sales manager and was named director of international sales in 1986. Before that, he was a founder and president of Xebex Data Systems.

Cummings joined the company in 1988 as eastern regional sales manager after working as eastern regional manager of Cydrome, a computer manufacturer.

Wagner, who had been director of manufacturing for Dilog before his promotion, was previously director of manufacturing for Digitran in Pasadena and spent 25 years with Beckman Instruments.
