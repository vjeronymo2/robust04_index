Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, the commander of the Northern Command, said that terrorist activity in south Lebanon had risen recently. Maj. Gen. Mordekhay noted that terrorist activity had returned to its level prior to the meeting between Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton in Geneva. Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports that the commander of the Northern Command and his officers today attended a memorial day ceremony in south Lebanon for fallen SLA [South Lebanese Army] soldiers.
