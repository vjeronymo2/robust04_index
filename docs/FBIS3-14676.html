Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In his Friday sermon at Imam al-Rida Mosque in Beirut's Southern Suburb today, 'Allamah Muhammad Husayn Fadlallah strongly criticized the U.S. arrogant policy against the Islamic peoples. He also criticized the Zionist entity's brutal attacks in occupied Palestine and Lebanon. Fadlallah called on the world's Muslims to confront the expansionist ambitions of the Zionist entity and global arrogance. He also criticized the U.S. stand at the UN Security Council and U.S. President Bill Clinton's remarks that Jerusalem is the capital of the Zionist entity. Fadlallah said U.S. policy is based on hostility to Muslims. Referring to the recent developments in Lebanon, Fadlallah urged the Lebanese people to unite and to be vigilant against the enemies' plots.
