920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / International Company News: Hoesch approves Krupp deal THE SUPERVISORY board of Hoesch,the German steelmaker, has formally approved its merger with steel and engineering group Fried. Krupp, AP-DJ reports from Dortmund. Krupp last year acquired a controlling stake of more than 50 per cent of Hoesch. A meeting for both companies will be held on July 27 to approve terms of the share swap to complete the merger.
