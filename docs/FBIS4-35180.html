BFN [Text] Algiers Special Judicial Council today issued 5 life imprisonment sentences and 13 death sentences in absentia against the group of El Askri Jamal. The life imprisonment sentences include El Askri Jamal and Charratti Yakhlef, a prominent member of the dissolved Islamic Salvation Front. The same council issued death sentences in absentia against Chaboutti Abdelkader, Makhloufi Said, and Baa Ezzeddine. These are associated with the group which carried out the bombing of Houari Boumediene Airport in August 1992.
