January 4, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFLY

American Restaurants Corp. said it has purchased the stock of privately held International Eateries Corp. and of Ultimate Eateries of America. American is the franchisee for and operator of 11 Wendy's hamburger restaurants in Ventura County. Terms were not disclosed. In addition, Charles L. Boppell, formerly president of Taco Bell and chairman of Godfathers Pizza, was elected president of American.
