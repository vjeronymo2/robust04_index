BFN [Text] Tehran, May 10, IRNA -- Visiting Deputy Prime Minister of Belarus Nikolay N. Kostikov here Tuesday called for further expansion of ties between Tehran and Minsk in political, economic and commercial areas. Kostikov, talking to Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, expressed hope that his country could export goods to the Persian Gulf states via Iran. He also told Velayati that he had reached some economic agreements with Iranian offcials. Velayati announced Iran's readiness to allow commodities made by Belarus to be transited to the Persian Gulf. Kostikov arrived here on Monday at the head of a high ranking politico-economic delegation at the invitation of Commerce Minister Yahya ol-Eshaq.
