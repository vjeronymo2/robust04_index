921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / International Company News: Amax issue raises Dollars 340m AMAX, the US metals and mining company, has sold 7m shares of series A convertible preferred stock in a private offering, raising about Dollars 340m. Proceeds will be used to reduce debt. The company has Dollars 2.2bn in long-term debt and other obligations. After 60 days, the preferred stock will be convertible into Amax common stock at a rate of 2.5197 shares of common for each share of preferred. The shares have an annual dividend rate of Dollars 4.
