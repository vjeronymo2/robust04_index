Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] [Video shows Public Security Minister Tao Siju talking to a group of officers in front of the Beijing railway station and beside a row of fire engines; some unidentified officers visiting people in their homes] Public Security Minister Tao Siju and other comrades today separately called on public security police officers and cadres as well as armed police force fire fighters on duty at the Beijing railway station, Qianmen, Daxing County, and Beijing municipal fire bureau. Tao Siji asked the vast number of public security police officers as well as armed police officers and men to stand firm at their posts during Spring Festival to safeguard social security and prevent fires, so that the people nationwide could spend a stable and peaceful festival. In the past few days, relevant leaders of the Ministry of Public Security dispatched over a dozen groups of officers to various localities nationwide to convey greetings to heroic models and family members of public security police who sacrificed their lives on duty, and to help them solve their problems.
