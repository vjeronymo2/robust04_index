920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Dewhurst up 54% despite recession Dewhurst, the electrical control equipment manufacturer, reported a 54 per cent profits increase for the six months to March 29. The advance, from Pounds 201,330 to Pounds 309,874 was achieved on turnover up by some Pounds 300,000 to Pounds 4.21m. Directors said the results showed an encouraging improvement in a period of persistent recession. Earnings per share improved to 1.85p (1.22p) and the interim dividend is raised to 0.63p (0.6p).
