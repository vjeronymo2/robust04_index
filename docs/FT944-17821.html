941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / Observer: Mum's the word Close-knit family, the Kennedys, even when divorcing. Joan Kennedy has just postponed - until after elections on November 8 - a court action aimed at reopening her 1982 divorce settlement with Senator Edward Kennedy. She hasn't said why, and nor will her lawyer, Monroe Inker, say if the two have met recently. 'I have been sworn to silence by my client,' says Inker. Can it be that Kennedy, who has been a Democrat senator for 32 years, is facing an unusually tough fight from Republican Mitt Romney, a venture capitalist and son of former Michigan governor, George Romney?
