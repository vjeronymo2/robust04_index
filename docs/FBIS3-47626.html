Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Prosecutor-General's Office will set up a new department to deal mainly with international criminal affairs, a spokesman said yesterday. The prosecution will also appoint a prosecutor at each district prosecution office who will exclusively handle crimes by foreigners, international disputes over intellectual property rights, and criminal affairs related to tariff and foreign currencies, he said. Meanwhile, the Justice Ministry yesterday said it plans to dispatch prosecution officials to the Korean Embassies at Geneva and the United Nations who will provide legal advice.
