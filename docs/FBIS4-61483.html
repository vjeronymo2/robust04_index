BFN [Text] The Russian Foreign Affairs Ministry has not yet had any consultations with the U.S. State Department about the possibility of swapping Aldrich Ames for one of the American spies detained in the territory of the Russian Federation. This was said today by Grigoriy Karasin, the director of the Russian Foreign Ministry's Press and Information Department. According to him this issue should, first and foremost, be settled among themselves by the leaders of the two states' special services.
