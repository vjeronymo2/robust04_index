August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I enjoyed last June's cover story on the differences between dads in real life and on TV.

Yes, there did appear to be a difference, especially in TV's early series -- and all to the good, it seems to me.

Your story didn't mention the father of them all -- Charlie Ruggles, who starred in "The Ruggles." It was a comedy series that aired for three years on ABC from 1949 to 1952, but that probably was before writer Joe Rhodes was born. Too bad -- he missed some good shows.

June D. Raisbeck, Glendale
