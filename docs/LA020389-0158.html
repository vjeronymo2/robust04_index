THE ALCOHOL TOLL

Through the first 10 months of 1988, alcohol-related traffic accidents killed more people on county streets and roads than was the case for any of the nine preceding full years. Between January and October, there were 151 fatal accidents (also more than any of the nine years before) that killed 168 people.
