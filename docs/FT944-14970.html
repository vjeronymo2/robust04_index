941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy Cairn Energy, the Edinburgh-based oil and gas explorer, has completed the sale of another tranche of shares in Cairn Energy USA. This transaction brings the total raised to about Pounds 17.3m after allowing for expenses and tax and leaves Cairn Energy with a holding of 16.5 per cent. Petrie Parkman have placed 1.93m shares in CEUSA at Dollars 7 each and has also exercised its option to acquire a further 250,000 shares in addition to the 1.68m originally agreed.
