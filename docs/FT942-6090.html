940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Cardiff lifts offer for First Choice Cardiff Property has increased its offer for First Choice, the residential property developer. The new offer, which is recommended, values First Choice at Pounds 2.59m and will be satisfied by the issue of 796,588 new Cardiff ordinary shares at 325p apiece. The revised offer comprises 53 new Cardiff ordinary for every 500 First Choice shares and 22 new ordinary for every 15 A ordinary Pounds 1 shares. Some 200,000 new Cardiff ordinary have already been placed firm at 315p, raising Pounds 630,000. Assuming full acceptance of the offer, 996,588 new shares, representing 26.6 per cent of the enlarged share capital, will be issued following the offer and placing.
