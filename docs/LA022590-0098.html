February 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

VISALIA;

TODDLER SAVED FROM FIRE; 2 PEOPLE DIE

Two people were killed when fire destroyed a one-story home in Visalia, but a 3-year-old girl was saved when a family friend broke a bedroom window and snatched her from the smoke-filled room, firefighters said. "When I heard the children were in there, I wasn't afraid," said Troy Green Jr., 25, of Visalia.
