Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 23 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The monthly of credits interests of commercial banks directed to development of the sector of small and medium enterprises constitute for the present 8-15 percent, which is by almost 10 percent lower than in the last year. 15 commercial banks of Mongolia by the beginning of March have granted to economic units credits worth 39 billion tugriks, which is by 2.4 percent higher than in February. The credits to economic units and enterprises of the state sector constituted 15 billion tugriks, or by 12 percent lower then in the last months. The credits to the private sector have increased and constituted 23 billion tugriks.
