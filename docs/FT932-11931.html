930430 FT 30 APR 93 / Observer: No escape Whether the Financial Times readers who've asked are relieved, alarmed or merely joking, Observer can confirm that it is not about to be sold to the Guardian. Nor has it ever been owned by Lonrho, let alone Tiny Rowland. Although a slice of the transfer fee would be welcome, the object of the transaction must be some lesser font of wisdom graced by the same handle.
