BFN [Text] The Bosnia-Herzegovina Government at today's session, which was chaired by Deputy Prime Minister Edib Bukvic, passed the [word indistinct] Law on the Government of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, the Law on Ministries and Other Offices of the State Administration, the Law on the Government of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation, and the Law on Ministries and State Administration Offices of the Federation. Until a final peace agreement for Bosnia-Herzegovina is reached, the Bosnia-Herzegovina Government will perform the role of the government of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation. The government will consist of a prime minister, a deputy prime minister, 11 ministers to oversee ministries, and four ministers without portfolio. The deputy prime minister will also act as the defense minister. The ministers in charge of ministries must ensure that their deputies do not come from the same constituent ethnic group as them. The Ministry for Special Purposes Production, the Ministry of Economy, the Ministry of Reconstruction and Development, the Ministry of Environment, the Ministry of Transport and Communication, the Ministry of Trade, and the Ministry of Information will cease to exist. Based on the Bosnia-Herzegovina Constitution, the internal structure of the territory with a majority Bosniak and Croatian population in the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina has been transformed and a segment of the executive functions of the federation allocated to the government of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation. The ministries of the federation are: the Defense Ministry; the Ministry of Internal Affairs; the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; the Ministry of Finance; the Ministry of Justice; the Ministry of Energy and Industry; the Ministry of Transport and Communications; the Ministry of Urbanism, Resources, and Protection of Environment; the Ministry of Trade; the Ministry of Refugees and Social Policy; the Ministry of Health; and the Ministry of Education, Science and Culture. The Bosnia-Herzegovina Government also passed a draft law on the Official Gazette of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation.
