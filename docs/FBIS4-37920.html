BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has affirmed that liberating our country from the Israeli occupation continues to be our firm objective. It is also one of the first objectives of the Arab movements. He said: We cling to liberation, which also means that we cling to the ancient principles of our nation and the goals for which we have struggled. Prime Minister al-Hariri noted that the recent government action regarding the nationality law constitutes a pillar for national accord, justice, and the restoral of the government to its citizens. He affirmed that the crucial stage we are in requires us to rise to the level of national duty. Prime Minister al-Hariri made these statements during a dinner party held this evening at the Summerland Hotel at the beginning of celebrations for the fiftieth anniversary of the Arab Cultural Club. A number of ministers, deputies, and political and cultural figures attended the celebration.
