940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / Nissan talks over stadium plan fail A final attempt to resolve the disagreement between Nissan, the carmaker, and Sunderland football club over the latter's plans for a Pounds 75m stadium and leisure complex on green-belt land beside Nissan's Wearside plant foundered yesterday. The club said it had ended talks with Nissan, which opposes the club's proposal. A public inquiry into the club's plans is to be held.
