931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Kelsey shares fall despite turnround Shares in Kelsey Industries fell 25p to 230p yesterday despite the company returning to the black in the year to September. Pre-tax profits were Pounds 212,000 (losses Pounds 307,000) on turnover of Pounds 49.7m (Pounds 41.4m). Losses per share were 12.2p (16.2p). A same-again final dividend of 5p gives an 8p (13p) total.
