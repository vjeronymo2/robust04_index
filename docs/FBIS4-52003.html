BFN [Summary] On 24 March [date as heard], the government issued Decree No. 29 on establishing Dac Ha District of Kon Tum Province. The district was established on the basis of incorporating two villages of Dac To and Dac Lay with four villages of Kon Tum city. Dac Ha has almost 75,000 hectares of natural land and a population of more than 29,800. It has seven administrative units.
