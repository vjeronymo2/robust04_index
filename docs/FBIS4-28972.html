BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Japan Social-Democratic Party headed by director of the general affairs department of the Central Executive Committee member of the house of representatives Kazu Watanabe and a friendly visiting group of the Communist Party-KPML (R) in Sweden headed by Politburo member of the Central Committee Roger Gross arrived here today. A delegation of the Jilin Yuwen Middle School, China, led by Zhao Zhongxin, secretary of the party committee of the school, came here Friday.
