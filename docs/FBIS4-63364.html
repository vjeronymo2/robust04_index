BFN [Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Appeal "in Connection with the Situation on the Territory of the Former Yugoslavia"] [Text] Moscow, 13 May 1994 -- The Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma believes that in the situation which has taken shape a real opportunity has appeared for establishing peace on the territory of the former Yugoslavia. In this connection the State Duma appeals to all sides taking part in the conflict to cease all hostilities immediately and to embark on talks to establish peace. For its part the State Duma is prepared to render all-around assistance to set up dialogue between the sides involved. The State Duma appeals to all parliaments of the world to prevent decisions which could destabilize the situation in the former Yugoslavia and primarily those leading to the violation of the embargo on arms supplies to any of the warring sides. The State Duma suggests that the parliaments of states adjacent to the territory of the former Yugoslavia hold an international conference with the participation of UN and CSCE representatives on ways of overcoming the crisis in the Balkans.
