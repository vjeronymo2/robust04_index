Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 10 Mar (MENA)--Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, has welcomed the new Russian initiative, which calls for a new international conference along the lines of the Madrid conference to assess the current status of the peace process. In a statement today, Qaddumi said this initiative is worthy of consideration and the Palestinian leadership must give it the attention it deserves.
