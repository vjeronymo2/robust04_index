May 15, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

TOXIC WASTE

After several telephone calls to the appropriate city offices, and speaking to very nice personnel, it became quite apparent that toxic waste collection points are sadly missing. There are sporadic Saturday, 9-3, collection spots around the city. What is one to do with oil-base paint cans, car batteries, etc.?

The environment and toxic waste are in the forefront in thought with only actual remedies (accessible collection points) lacking. Also, what about the collection of food cans, household plastic bottles other than soft drink bottles?

JANET GORDON KAPLAN

Van Nuys
