BFN [Text] Azerbaijan's military forces fired on the Armenian defense positions in Mardakertskiy Rayon on the night of 15 May. The incident took place after a two-day lull in the fighting. As reported earlier, the Armenian side refrained from returning fire. Although an agreement has been reached in Moscow to end the fighting, the situation in the confrontation areas between the Nagrono-Karabakh Republic [NKR] and Azerbaijan continues to be unstable. Azerbaijan National Army subunits have violated the cease-fire agreement from time to time. The NKR Defense Army Headquarters Press Service has disclosed that Azerbaijan's military units fired on the Armenian positions in the northern part of Mardakertskiy Rayon and the central areas in Agdamskiy Rayon last night and this morning. One NKR military man was wounded. However, the defense units continued to heed the NKR Defense Army Headquarters' order not to return the enemy fire. The same source also disclosed that the situation on the other parts of the NKR front line has been relatively peaceful. The situation in the border region between Armenia and Azerbaijan was peaceful last night and this morning, that is, as of noon today. Peace and security must prevail on the border between the two republics. Regardless of the tension between the two sides, the relations between the two republics must be restored. Our people lived as neighbors in the past; they will continue to live as neighbors in the future. It is common knowledge that close neighbors are [words indistinct] than distant countries. Therefore, it is time to end the hostile activities between the two sides.
