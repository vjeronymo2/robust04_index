BFN [Text] Thailand has reaffirmed its readiness to fully cooperate with Brunei Darussalam in various areas both under bilateral relation and within the ASEAN framework. Bruneian Foreign Minister Prince Mohamed Bolkiah held talks with Prime Minister Chuan Likphai on Friday on bilateral cooperation. He concluded his three-day visit to Thailand yesterday. Prime Minister Chuan said he hoped that Brunei would increase its investment in the region. He also expressed his appreciation to the Bruneian Government for taking good care of Thai workers in that country. The Bruneian foreign minister praised Thai workers for their efficiency, saying that Brunei is still in need of foreign labor as the country is accelerating the construction of infrastructure. He said Brunei would learn from successes in national development in various countries, Thailand included. The country will host the next ASEAN ministerial meeting after Thailand. Both sides will work out the program for the visit to Thailand by Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei as guest of His Majesty the King of Thailand.
