Antidrug Actions PA1106200994 Panama City Circuito RPC Television in Spanish 2300 GMT 10 Jun 94 PA1106200994 Panama City Circuito RPC Television Spanish BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] Foreign Minister Jose Raul Mulino has stated that drugs and illegal drug trafficking are matters that concern both the United States and Panama. He added that it is a priority to hold a high-level meeting to coordinate joint actions. [Begin recording] Mulino: If the U.S. Government confirms it, we will hold a meeting at the highest level in Panama during the first week of July. I will preside over this meeting, and it is to be attended by very important U.S. State and Justice Department, perhaps even U.S. Treasury, officials to discuss all the drug trafficking and money laundering problems. As you will recall, on 13 April I sent a letter to U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher telling him that, instead of talking and arguing whether we have cooperated or not, I invited him to a high-level meeting in Panama to discuss this topic. The invitation has been accepted and, hopefully, will be held during the first week of July. [end recording]
