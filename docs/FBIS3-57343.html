Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" feature] [Text] President Geydar Aliyev says the Azeri army is setting an example of heroism reviving the nation's confidence, respect and love for its defenders. Speaking during a visit to an army unit on Monday, he said there was a revival of patriotic feelings in the nation and that latest developments indicated the ability by the Azeris and their army to defend their own lands." Aliyev spoke of military successes notched up by the army on the frontlines of Fizuli, Kelbajar, Agdam and Agderin (Mardakert) recently and the nation's pride and joy over those achievements. At the same time, the President said that Baku was opposed to war and still committed to a peaceful solution to the conflict. However, the Armenian army continues aggression against Azerbaijan by launching new offensives, he said. Aliyev pledged commitment to further efforts for a negotiated settlement to the conflict saying appropriate contacts were under way to this end including Armenia. But he warned against further Armenian aggression saying his country would have no other option but to recover lost territory by force if that was not stopped. The President called on Yerevan to relinquish the occupied territories of its own accord, withdraw its troops and respect the sovereignty and integrity of Azerbaijan. The war will end if the Armenian government fulfils those conditions, Aliyev said.
