BFN [Text] Harare April 6 SAPA -- Home Affairs Minister Dumiso Dabengwa has ruled out door-to-door registering of Zimbabwean voters, saying the suggestion was not feasible, ZIANA national news agency reports. Several members of Parliament, including a senior Zimbabwe Government official, have suggested that the country should consider door-to-door registering so as to ensure that all eligible voters register and participate in Zimbabwe's forthcoming general elections. However, Mr Dabengwa shut the door on the suggestion saying the exercise would be far too costly.
