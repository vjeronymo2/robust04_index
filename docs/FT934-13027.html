931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Gresham House Gresham House, the investment trust, reported net revenue for the first half of 1993 of Pounds 1.17m, against losses of Pounds 1.5m. The result was helped by the issue of Pounds 7.42m secured loan stock by its Gresham House Finance subsidiary. That allowed the release of provisions for interest and guarantees which, with the release of other provisions, resulted in an exceptional credit of Pounds 1.16m (Pounds 982,000 charge). Earnings per share came out at 27.6p, against losses of 35.3p.
