BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 11 (XINHUA) -- Shanghai Mayor Huang Ju met with President Alberto Fujimori of the Republic of Peru here this evening. They had a cordial and friendly talk and both expressed the hope for stronger economic links. President Fujimori arrived here this morning from Beijing. This afternoon, Fujimori attended the opening ceremony of a Peruvian photo show. Then, he toured the Pudong New Area.
