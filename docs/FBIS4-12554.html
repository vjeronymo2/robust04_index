BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad and Austrian President Thomas Klestil resumed talks in a meeting held at the People's Palace at 1030 today. Later, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Austrian Foreign Minister Alois Mock joined the meeting. Talks dealt with international issues, the Middle East situation and developments, and issues pertaining to the regional peace process. The two sides also discussed bilateral issues. Both presidents expressed satisfaction with bilateral relations. President Klestil extended an invitation to President al-Asad to visit Austria. President al-Asad thankfully accepted the invitation and promised to respond to it at an appropriate time.
