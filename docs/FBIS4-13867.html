BFN [Text] Asyut, Egypt, April 24 (AFP)--Egyptian authorities seized a quantity of explosives here Saturday that Islamic fundamentalists planned to use in attacks, a source close to the security forces said. The haul followed information given by three "dangerous" fundamentalists arrested at Tahta in Suhaj Province south of Asyut, the source said. Comprising 16 detonators and 10 kilos (22 pounds) of TNT, it was found in a cemetery at al-Badari, 410 kilometres (250 miles) south of Cairo.
