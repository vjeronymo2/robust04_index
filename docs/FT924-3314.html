921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / Jensen Car goes to mystery group JENSEN Car Company, the former builder of hand-assembled luxury sports cars, is passing out of the hands of Grant Thornton, the receivers, to a consortium of English businessmen for an undisclosed sum, it was announced yesterday. The consortium, none of whom was named, plans to resurrect the Jensen Interceptor. A new version of the car had been announced early this year - when it found just one customer.
