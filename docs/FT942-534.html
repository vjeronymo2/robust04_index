940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / Economic watch Producer prices in Sweden rose 0.4 per cent in May from April (see chart), and by 3.2 per cent in the year to May. German engineering orders rose 18 per cent in real terms in May compared with the same month last year, while March-May orders were up 9 per cent from the same 1993 period. German orders from other EU countries rose a real 34 per cent in May from May 1993 and March-May EU orders rose 21 per cent from the year earlier. Italian unemployment rose to 2.61m in April from 2.38m in April last year and from 2.52m in January. Belgian inflation rose to a provisional 2.76 per cent this month from 2.56 per cent in May. Italy's GDP will grow by a real 1.3-1.5 per cent this year, and by 2.3-2.5 per cent next, the Treasury said. Finland's trade surplus fell to Markka 2.4bn (Pounds 290m) in May, from Markka 2.9bn in April and Markka 3.6bn in May last year.
