920130 FT 30 JAN 92 / International Company News: Bang & Olufsen stems losses BANG & Olufsen, the Danish television and audio equipment maker, staunched its losses in the first half to November 30. The group reported a first half profit of DKr2m (Dollars 322,000) compared with a loss of DKr45m in the first half of last year and a full-year loss of DKr145m. Sales were up 10 per cent to DKr1.16bn from DKr1.05bn. The result after depreciation was turned from a DKr6m loss to a profit of DKr24m and net financial costs were cut from DKr42m to DKr22m. There was a management reorganisation last year, when Mr Anders Knutsen took over as managing director.
