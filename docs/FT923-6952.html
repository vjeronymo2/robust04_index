920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 5.4m exceptionals pull down Bredero Exceptional charges in the half year to June 30 increased losses at Bredero Properties from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 4.43m. Turnover rose to Pounds 40.5m (Pounds 24.1m). There was a profit of Pounds 991,000 before exceptionals of Pounds 5.42m, comprising adjustment on the sale of the interest in Bon Accord Centre, Aberdeen, and the expected loss on the disposal of a development property which, because of local planning changes, will now be sold. Losses per share reached 13.4p (3.9p).
