940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Business Travel: In the bag Britain lost out to Spain last week in what it prides itself on doing best - making a cup of tea. Spain's national airline Iberia won the 1994 In-Flight Best Cup of Tea Award from Britain's National Tea Council, beating British Airways and British Midland at making the national drink. 'We were absolutely delighted,' said an Iberia spokeswoman. To make the award a team of tea tasters travelled 672,430 air miles on 239 flights with 30 different airlines. The defeated Britons were characteristically tight-lipped.
