Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The laboratory of atmospheric processes of the Kyrgyzstan Academy of Sciences' Physics Institute has been tasked with monitoring the effects of activity at the Chinese Lob Nor nuclear testing range on the neighboring territories in Kyrgyzstan. A RIA correspondent has learned at the government press service that an agreement to this effect was signed today between the Academy of Sciences and the republican government's State Commission for Emergency Situations and Civil Defense.
