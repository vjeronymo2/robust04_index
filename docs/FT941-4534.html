940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves Precision engineering group James Dickie recorded the day's best performance, the shares leaping 47 to 150p after the company said it anticipated 'significantly improved interim figures' next month. Specialist paper making group Portals jumped 29 to 608p after announcing a 22 per cent improvement in annual profits to Pounds 34.08m and a 1.5p rise in the dividend to 16p. Holliday Chemicals improved 5 to 214p on well received results. Storage, distribution and hire company Transport Development fell 24 to 284p on reporting a 20 per cent drop in profits to Pounds 6.9m.
