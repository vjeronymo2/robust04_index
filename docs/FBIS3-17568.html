Language: English Article Type:BFN [By GIA-TASS] [Text] Tbilisi March 27 TASS -- Abkhazian armed formations continue attacks on Georgian villages located on the Abkhazian administrative territory, sources from the Georgian Defence Ministry told the Georgian Information Agency (GIA) on Sunday. Reports say that apart from heavy guns and artillery, on Sunday, Abkhazian armed formations used helicopters. Georgian military sources say that settlements in the Lata Gorge have been shelled by artillery and heavy guns. The villages of Khutia, Henchvishi, Sakeni, Myasnikovka and others came under fire on Sunday. Casualties among civilians have been reported. Fighting has been going on for three days in the mountainous region of Svanteni on the Abkhazian territory, GIA learned from the Georgian leader's press office. According to preliminary reports, 10 people were killed and 22 injured. Georgian sources said that the settlements were shelled by the Abkhazian side from "Grad" rocket launchers and howitzers. Georgian Prime Minister Otar Patsatsiya and chairman of a temporary committee for the state of emergency, Dzhaba Ioseliani, have arrived in the area of the conflict.
