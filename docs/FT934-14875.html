931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Moody's cuts Lasmo ratings Moody's has downgraded Lasmo's debt and preference shares because it believes the independent oil company's cash flows will decline and debt remain high. Moreover, 'management appears willing to accept a higher, permanent level of financial risk', Moody's said. It cut the rating on senior debt from Baa1 to Baa3 and on preference shares from Baa2 to Ba1.
