Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly was in session in Sarajevo today. [passage omitted] The assembly endorsed an agreement on future military and economic cooperation between the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the Republic of Croatia, with 83 votes for. Continuing, the assembly passed a law on the adoption of the draft constitution of the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and a decision on the adoption of the draft constitution of the republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina. A decision on the future work of the Assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina and municipal assemblies was also adopted. The assembly is to resume its session at 1000 tomorrow.
