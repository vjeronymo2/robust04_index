In §110.43, paragraph (a) is revised to read as follows: §110.43 Physical security standards. (a) Physical security measures in recipient countries must provide protection at least comparable to the recommendations in the current version of IAEA publication INFCIRC/225/Rev.2, December 1989, ``The Physical Protection of Nuclear Material,'' and is incorporated by reference in this part. This incorporation by reference was approved by the Director of the Federal Register in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51. Notice of any changes made to the material incorporated by reference will be published in the Federal Register . Copies of INFCIRC/225/Rev.2 may be obtained from the Director for Nonproliferation, Exports, and Multilateral Relations, Office of International Programs, U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission, Washington, DC 20555, and are available for inspection at the NRC library, 11545 Rockville Pike, Rockville, Maryland 20852&hyph;2738.
