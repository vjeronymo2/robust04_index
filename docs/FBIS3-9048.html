Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 16 (ATA) -- The first congress of the Trade Union of the Army and Police Civilians was held in Tirana on March 16. Participants in the congress pointed out in their addresses that this trade union is fighting for the rights of its members in different army sectors. Taking the floor in the congress, Defence Minister Safet Zhulali emphasized that it is necessary to fight manifestations of corruption and misappropriation. "We always should be strict who may be involved in such acts and the trade union has an undisputable role in keeping them away from abuse," Mr. Zhulali said. The Trade Union of Army and Police Civilians was so far part of the Trade Union of Albania.
