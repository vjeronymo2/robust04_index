BFN [Text] According to Article 86 of the Labor Code, the Ministry of Labor and Social Security has decided to transfer the 1 May holiday, which falls on a Sunday, to Monday, 2 May. The suspension of labor activities on Monday will be in effect except for those that relate to harvest and other urgent agricultural and livestock production activities, continuous-production industries, and urgencies in the the port-transport-internal economy network. Likewise not included in this labor holiday are services related to transport, hospitals, funerals, pharmacies, on-duty gasoline stations, gardens, recreation centers, radio and television broadcasting, tourist attractions, and collection and distribution of milk. The Ministry of Domestic Trade will establish the opening and closing hours for the domestic trade system.
