October 6, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

RAMS-49ERS GAME TOP DRAW IN TV RATINGS

The Rams' game at San Francisco Sunday was the nation's highest-rated sports program of the week, drawing an 18.6 rating. In Los Angeles, the Rams-49ers drew a 16.9 rating, compared to 6.5 for USC's nail-biter with Washington State Saturday.
