BFN [Text] Yerevan, 24 May (SNARK)--Vahan Papazyan, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia, will participate in the meeting of Foreign Affairs Ministers of the NATO members. The meeting will take place in Istanbul, in the first 10-day period of June 1994. Speaking to SNARK Aram Safaryan, press secretary of the Armenian Foreign Affairs Department, doesn't exclude the possibility of Vahan Papazyan's meeting with the Turkish Foreign Affairs Minister Hikmet Cetin.
