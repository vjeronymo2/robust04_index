BFN [Text] The naval exercises Breeze-94 in the Black Sea due between 25 and 29 July were in the center of attention of the Wednesday meeting in Odessa between the chief of staff of the Bulgarian Navy Admiral Ilia Popov and the Deputy Commander of the Ukrainian Navy Rear-Admiral Nikolay Kostrov. According to INTERFAX-Ukraine, the details of interaction of the two navies were discussed. The exercises are going to involve two Ukrainian, one Russian and one Turkish ships, two missile cruisers from Greece and Rumania each as well as two ships of the U.S. sixth fleet. Two Bulgarian naval ships, a frigate and a demagnetizing vessel, are now visiting Odessa. On Saturday they are going to leave for Novorossiisk.
