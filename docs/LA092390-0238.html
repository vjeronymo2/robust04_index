September 23, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

In other nonleague games:

Birmingham 21, University 14 -- Tailback Vince Lampkin rushed for 121 yards in 17 carries to lead host Birmingham (1-0).

Cornerback Mike Moguel returned an interception 70 yards for a touchdown after only 1 minute, 43 seconds had elapsed in the first quarter. Quincy Hill caught a seven-yard touchdown pass from Noah Kirshbaum early in the fourth quarter to give the Braves a 21-0 lead. Lampkin scored on a three-yard run in the third quarter. J. SCOTT KLEIN
