BFN [Text] Tbilisi 9 April -- An Iberia news agency correspondent reports that representatives of the Round Table political bloc [supporters of former President Zviad Gamsakhurdia] gathered near the Philharmonic Concert Hall today at 1300 hours and attempted to hold an unauthorized rally. The police dispersed the gathered. Several people were arrested. Some of the ralliers marched peacefully towards Rustaveli Prospekt to lay flowers at the memorial to those killed on 9 April [day in 1989 Soviet troops dispersed a peaceful rally killing 20 people]. By 1400 hours about 2,000 supporters of the former Georgian President Zviad Gamsakhurdia approached the House of Government with the intention of holding a rally there. The area in front of the Government House was guarded by some 500 policemen led by Gela Lanchava. The ralliers then went to the Mtatsminda Pantheon and paid homage to Merab Kostava.
