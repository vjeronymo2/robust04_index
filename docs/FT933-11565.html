930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Nigeria parties bid to end deadlock Nigeria's two parties yesterday presented a plan for an interim government to the electoral commission in a bid to end the country's political crisis, Reuter reports from Lagos. The resolution, signed by the national chairmen of both parties, was submitted to the electoral commission at a meeting with the two parties in the capital, Abuja. All three sides said in a joint communique that the plan, details of which were unavailable, would be passed to the government for consideration.
