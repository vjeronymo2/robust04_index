Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 09, BNS -- Latvia's National Armed Forces hold routine exercise this week to drill the tasks and actions of the defense formations in extraordinary situations. The exercise is designed for the Defense Force and Homeguard staff units and the Interior Ministry's mobile regiment. The National Defense Academy's representatives participate in the status of observers. According to the exercise plan, the participating units were put on alarm early Tuesday [8 March] morning. Two hours later, 85 percent of Homeguard Staff members and 62 percent of Defense Forces Staff members had arrived in the place of service. Defense Force Cmdr. Dainis Turlais and Homeguard Staff Cmdr. Juris Eihmanis reported on the results of the training alarm to President Guntis Ulmanis, who leads the exercise. The meeting also heard a resolution by the National Defense Council to set up a National Armed Forces Staff from the Defense Forces Staff and the Homeguard Staff in the conditions of exercise and extraordinary situations.
