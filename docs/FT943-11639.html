940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / Crown Estate shows Pounds 486m rise The value of the Crown Estate, which manages property on behalf of the sovereign and is one of Britain's biggest landowners, increased Pounds 486m in real terms between 1985-94, the National Audit Office reported yesterday. Capital values, in constant 1993-94 prices, rose from Pounds 1.55bn to Pounds 2.03bn. Net revenue paid to the exchequer, on the same basis, rose from Pounds 39m in 1985-86 to Pounds 79m in 1993-94.
