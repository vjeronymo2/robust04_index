921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / Gorbachev allowed to travel The Russian government yesterday ended a foreign travel ban on former Soviet President Mikhail Gorbachev, Reuter reports from Moscow. It had refused to let Mr Gorbachev, 61, visit Italy last month because of his refusal to testify at Constitutional Court hearings into the banned Communist party. Mr Gorbachev said before leaving for Berlin he hoped this was the end of the matter.
