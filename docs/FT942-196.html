940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / Power pay boost Mr John Baker, chief executive of National Power, received an 11 per cent increase in total remuneration in the year to March 27, taking the total to Pounds 437,000. His salary rose about 4 per cent to Pounds 281,000 but performance-related bonuses rose from Pounds 64,000 to Pounds 79,000 and taxable benefits from Pounds 8,000 to Pounds 14,500.
