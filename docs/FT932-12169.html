930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Haemocell loss deepens to Pounds 0.87m Expenditure related to international product approval and launch programmes resulted in increased pre-tax losses at Haemocell in the first six months, but the USM-quoted company said 'meaningful progress' was expected in the second half. On sales of Pounds 542,000 (Pounds 305,000) the pre-tax deficit rose 11 per cent to Pounds 868,000 (Pounds 783,000). Losses per share were unchanged at 4.3p. At the end of last year a Pounds 6.1m rights issue was launched following US approval of the S350 autotransfusion system.
