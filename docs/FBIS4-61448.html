BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 5 May TASS--"Relations between Ukraine and Crimea should be built on an equal and mutually beneficial basis. This will be the main topic of my meeting with President Yuriy Meshkov, which is due to be held in Simferopol on 7 May," Ukrainian Acting Prime Minister Yefim Zvyahilskyy told TASS on Thursday [5 May]. The proposed meeting is prompted by yesterday's closed-door talks between Zvyahilskyy and Crimea's Deputy Prime Minister Yevgeniy Saburov. The Ukrainian acting prime minister was pleased that when asked with whom the peninsula was siding, Saburov answered definitely: "with Ukraine." "Only after this, I made up my mind to meet the Crimean president," Zvyahilskyy stressed.
