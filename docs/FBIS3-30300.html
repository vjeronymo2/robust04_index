Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, January 27 (BTA) -- National Assembly Chairman Aleksandur Yordanov assessed as extremely successful the visit to Kuwait by a Bulgarian parliamentary delegation headed by him. On his return today, Mr. Yordanov said the delegation had been received by top state officials, Kuwait's Amir Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah, Crown Prince and Prime Minister Shaykh Sa'ad al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah, and the speaker of the National Assembly. He said the Bulgarian MP's had been warmly greeted at the Kuwaiti Parliament. They all stressed the significant role played by Bulgaria and the democratic forces in particular in support of Kuwait's cause during the Iraqi aggression, Mr. Yordanov said. Asked by BTA whether Kuwaiti businessmen were ready to invest in Bulgaria, Mr. Yordanov stressed they were showing interest but Bulgaria had to remove certain obstacles. "We are the ones who lack a successful policy of attracting foreign investment," he said. Mr. Yordanov pointed out the sides had reached full understanding over the entry into Kuwait of more Bulgarian engineering and construction firms, as well as of food makers.
