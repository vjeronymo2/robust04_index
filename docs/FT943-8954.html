940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Win for Schumacher Germany's Michael Schumacher cemented his position as Formula One championship leader when he won the Hungarian grand prix in a Benetton. Britain's Damon Hill, driving a Williams Renault, was second and Schumacher's teammate, Jos Verstappen of the Netherlands, third. Black flag at Monza, Page 2
