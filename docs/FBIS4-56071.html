BFN [Report by Mirza Sadikovic from Bihac] [Text] After yesterday and last night's bestial shelling of Bihac and the outskirts of Cazin, the joint aggressors today continued their attacks on the Bihac Commune. The chetniks are using mortars and howitzers of all calibers to shell the forward defense lines on the Grmusko-Srbljanski plateau and Grabez, while on Hasin Vrh and Vrsak they continue attempts at infantry breakthroughs. The 5th Corps members are energetically resisting, and the defense lines remain stable. On the other parts of the front, they continued using antiaircraft weapons, while sniper fire and small arms' fire has intensified. The members of Abdic's paramilitary army are also not quiet. They are increasingly collaborating with their allies. In the wider area of Trsak, Viskovac, and Todorovo, they haven been concentrating fresh forces and hardware since yesterday, and are preparing fresh attacks in collaboration with the chetniks. In the course of the day, they have been intensifying activity on all demarcation lines, using small arms, antiaircraft arms, and armor-piercing weapons.
