Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenian units launched a large scale attack in the north of Karabakh on the town of Ter-Ter Thursday evening, a spokesman for the Azeri Defense Ministry told Interfax. The attacking force of about 2000 troops and nearly ten tanks continued the attack on Friday morning. According to the ministry, the Karabakh units succeeded in advancing several kilometers towards Ter-Ter but the offensive was fended off in the early hours of January 21. In some areas the enemy had to retreat beyond the line of departure. According to Baku reports, numerous villages in the Kazakh and Tauz districts of Azerbaijan were shelled from Armenia. There were civilian casualties. The Karabakh army communique has not been available to Interfax.
