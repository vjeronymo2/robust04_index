Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] SLA [South Lebanese Army] soldiers captured a lot of equipment in a pre-dawn clash with Popular Front terrorists, in which two were killed and two captured. The commander of the area said the weapons included grenades and explosive charges. They also had leaflets saying they had come to avenge the Hebron massacre. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1600 GMT on 12 March adds that "the leaflets also specified terms of negotiations, in which the terrorists had planned to involve the French ambassador in Israel."] The commander added that there had been an increase of terrorist activity in south Lebanon, and the wave of terror had reached its peak. The gang was on its way to an attack on one of the northern border settlements. SLA soldiers noticed the band near Har Dov. In an initial clash, one terrorist was wounded and captured and three others escaped. Later, one of them surrendered to UNIFIL [UN Interim Force in Lebanon]. The other two were killed after a chase.
