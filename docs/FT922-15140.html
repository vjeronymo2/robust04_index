920402 FT 02 APR 92 / Laker wins approval SIR FREDDIE LAKER, the pioneer of cut-price transatlantic air travel in the 1970s, yesterday received approval for his new charter airline Laker Airways (Bahamas). Ten years after the collapse of Laker Airways, Sir Freddie, aged 70, has two Boeing 727 aircraft to link eight US cities, including Chicago and Atlanta, to the Bahamas Princess Resort and Casino on Grand Bahama Island. Sir Freddie is a consultant to Princess Vacations, whose parent company owns the resort.
