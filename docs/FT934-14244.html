931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / Observer: Clerical error Britain's two main opposition parties, Labour and the Liberal Democrats, eagerly rushed out statements congratulating Nelson Mandela and FW de Klerk on their joint winning of the Nobel peace prize on Friday. But more haste, less speed; both spellt the South African premier's name incorrectly. John Smith, the Labour leader, plumped for 'de Clerk', while Sir David Steel, Liberal Democrat foreign affairs spokesman, settled on 'DeClerk'.
