BFN [Text] Malaysia is satisfied with the final act embodying the results of the Uruguay Round multinational trade negotiations which is to be signed in Marrakesh, Morocco, in the middle of this month. Minister of International Trade and Industry Datuk Sri Rafidah Aziz says based on what Malaysia has been offered under the negotiations, it is set to undertake all commitments in implementing the results. A ministerial meeting to be held in Marrakesh for four days beginning on the 12th of this month will formally conclude the Uruguay Round multilateral trade negotiations. The minister believes participating countries will formally accept results of the negotiations by signing the final act at the end of the meeting, which is on the 15th of this month. The final act contains legal texts of agreement negotiated in the Uruguay Round. The results will be accepted as an all-around single package effective on the first of January next year.
