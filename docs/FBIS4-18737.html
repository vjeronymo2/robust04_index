BFN [Excerpt] Vienna, April 9 (AFP) -- Austria has asked France, Belgium and Germany to evacuate if necessary 15 Austrians living in the strife-torn Rwandan capital, Kigali, the Foreign Ministry said Saturday [9 April]. The Austrian civilians would be flown out of the country, where savage ethnic fighting has left at least 1,000 dead in the last three days, along with citizens of the three other countries, under the protection of French and Belgian troops. Austria also has some 40 nationals serving with United Nations peacekeeping troops deployed in Rwanda as part of a transition scheme ahead of elections planned for next year. [passage omitted]
