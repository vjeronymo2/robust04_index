Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "`Battle' in Crimea, Everything Up in Smoke"] [Text] The increased tension in Crimea's sociopolitical life caused by Kiev's dissatisfaction with the results of the first round of the presidential election in Simferopol cannot, it appears, reduce the fighting spirit of the Ukrainian Armed Forces. From 24 through 28 January large-scale command-staff exercises are being conducted by Armed Forces units and commands on the territory of Odessa, Kherson, and Nikolayev Oblasts and in Crimea. No doubt in this connection, in an interview with KIYEVSKIYE VEDOMOSTI Ukrainian Defense Minister General Radetskyy made the statement that "I would not like to have to liberate Crimea as the Americans liberated Kuwait." In response to this, Admiral Eduard Baltin, commander of the Black Sea Fleet, expressed doubt that the young, respected, and promising Ukrainian defense minister could become a General Schwarzkopf....
