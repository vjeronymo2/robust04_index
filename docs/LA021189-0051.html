February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JORDAN HAS STATISTICS TO RANK WITH THE BEST

In reference to a Feb. 4 letter about Michael Jordan's selection to Scott Ostler's all-time NBA team: Who in their right mind would not name him to their all-time team?

Last season alone, Jordan was league MVP, defensive player of the year, MVP of the All-Star game, slam dunk champion and steals leader, and he also earned his second consecutive scoring title. He is also the only player to score 50-plus points in three consecutive playoff games.

But you're right, he's just a flash in the pan.

JEFF GREEN

Encino
