930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Difficult times for BG Shin Nippon Baillie Gifford Shin Nippon had a diluted net asset per share of 111.1p at the year ended January 31, against 85.6p at the halfway stage and 124.4p 12 months earlier. The net loss for the year was Pounds 134,831 against Pounds 106,562. Losses per share came out at 0.84p (0.66p). The directors said it had been another difficult year in the Japanese stock market with the Tokyo second section index falling 29.4 per cent in yen terms and by 14.5 per cent in sterling terms.
