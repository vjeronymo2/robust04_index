941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met good general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Assams came to a firm market apart from plainest sorts. Brightest Kenya and Burundi teas sold well at fully firm to dearer rates, particularly pd's. Below best sorts and coloury mediums were irregular and barely steady. Ceylons met improved demand with brighter bop's selling well at dearer rates. Other sorts were irregularly easier. Offshore: good demand with brighter teas fully firm. Plain Ugandas were unwanted. Quotations: best available 200p/kg, good 140p/kg, good medium 120p/kg, medium 110p/kg, low medium 88p/kg. The highest price realised this week was a 205p/kg for an Assam pf.
