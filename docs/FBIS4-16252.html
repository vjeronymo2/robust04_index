BFN [Text] A group of Supreme Soviet deputies (Viktar Alampiyew, Aleh Trusaw, Lyavon Dzeyka, and three more deputies) has submitted to the Supreme Soviet Presidium an appeal with three demands: To entrust Prosecutor General Vasil Shaladonaw with the task of immediately investigating and, if necessary, of initiating criminal action against the participants in the preparation and signing of the monetary merger agreement between Belarus and Russia; to put the issue of the dismissal of the prime minister, other government members and officials for their anticonstitutional activities on the agenda of an extraordinary Supreme Soviet session; and to conduct a political assessment of these events. The authors of the appeal maintain that the Moscow agreement violates Articles 8, 145, and others of the Belarusian Constitution.
