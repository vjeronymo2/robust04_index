930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Siam Selective net asset value up 33% Siam Selective Growth Trust had a net asset value of 119.2p per share at September 30 - up 33 per cent on the 89.6p of 12 months earlier. The trust reported net profits of Pounds 21,975 (Pounds 19,256) for the six months to end-September. Earnings per share improved from 0.13p to 0.15p.
