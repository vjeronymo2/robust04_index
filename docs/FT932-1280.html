930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Observer: Hyperactive The Welsh Development Agency, having waited so long for the appointment of its new chairman, seemed completely unprepared for the great event. Apart from hardly anyone having heard of the new man, finding a photograph to accompany the announcement seemed even more difficult. Another sign that Cabinet new boy Welsh Secretary John Redwood, who has been churning out announcement after announcement about new jobs in the past few days, is overdoing it.
