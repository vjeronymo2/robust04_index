940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Nu-Swift sells Pounds 22m property Nu-Swift, the USM-traded fire protection equipment and property investment group, is to sell its leasehold interest in International House in the City of London for Pounds 22m. The deal is being done through its Nu-Swift (Creechurch) subsidiary. Proceeds will be used to repay debt currently secured on the property and to provide additional working capital.
