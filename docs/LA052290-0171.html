May 22, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

COMIC RELIEF TALLY: $8.6 MILLION

The May 12 presentation of Comic Relief '90 on HBO has now generated a record $8.6 million in funds, it was announced today by Bob Zmuda, president of the Comic Relief organization. As of Monday, pledges for the fourth all-star event to benefit America's homeless were running $2 million higher than at the same point last year.

The $8.6-million tally includes: $6.9 million in pledges; $700,000 in ticket sales for the live presentation at Radio City Music Hall; $500,000 from corporate sponsor Canada Dry; $400,000 raised at local events across the country, and $100,000 taken in from merchandising and special events conducted in New York City the week before the live show. A total of 175,000 people have phoned in pledges, averaging $45 per pledge.
