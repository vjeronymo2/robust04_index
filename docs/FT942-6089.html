940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 9.7m purchase for UniChem UniChem, the pharmaceutical wholesaler and chemist retailer, has paid about Pounds 9.7m cash for Hospital Management & Supplies, a hospital supply company. In 1993 HM&S handled some Pounds 30m of supplies through warehouses in Northampton and Glasgow. The vendor was Baxter Healthcare.
