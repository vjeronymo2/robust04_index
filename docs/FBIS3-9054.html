Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ivaylovgrad, March 19 (BTA) -- Late last night the police in Ivaylovgrad (in southern Bulgaria, near the border with Greece), detained two diplomats from the French Embassy in Bulgaria. Ivaylovgrad falls in the border zone and the two diplomats' presence there without the permission of Bulgaria's Foreign Ministry is a gross violation of border regulations. The police do not know how the two diplomats reached Ivaylovgrad unimpeded. A protocol was drawn up and they left for Sofia.
