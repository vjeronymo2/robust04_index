August 16, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: BREA;

BREA BLVD. RENOVATION TO BE CELEBRATED TODAY

Festivities to celebrate the renovation of Brea Boulevard south of Imperial Highway will begin today at 5 p.m. with a party and ribbon-cutting ceremony at 633 S. Brea Blvd.

The celebration will continue on Saturday from noon to 6:30 p.m. with a $10,000 raffle and a chance to meet California Angels pitcher Bert Blyleven and former UCLA basketball star Ann Meyers.
