January 21, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

CROSSKEY, FULLERTON DEFEAT GOLDEN WEST

Lisa Crosskey scored 26 points to lead Fullerton College to a 92-75 victory over Golden West in an Orange Empire Conference women's basketball game Friday at Golden West.

Crosskey was 9 of 14 from the field and 7 of 8 from the free-throw line. Heidi Shikuma added 20 points. Kelli Johnson had 12 for Fullerton (19-1 overall, 4-0 in conference play).

DeeDee Stigar scored 14 points for Golden West (9-15, 3-1), which committed 30 turnovers. Stigar scored all her points in the final 5 minutes of the game.
