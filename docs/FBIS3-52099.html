Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jan (MENA)--The Palestinians and Israelis are making intensive contacts with each other, both directly and via intermediaries, notably Egypt, to eliminate the misunderstanding between their leaders which have delayed the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement. Muhammad Subayh, secretary of the Palestine National Council, told MENA that the misunderstanding resulted from the fact that the contents of several documents that had been submitted were inaccurately conveyed to the leaders. Subayh explained that the points raised by President Yasir 'Arafat in connection with the Gaza-Jericho agreement are part and parcel of the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles signed in Washington 13 September. Subayh stressed that the Palestinians have a definite wish to implement the Gaza-Jericho agreement and they believe that the Israelis have the same wish.
