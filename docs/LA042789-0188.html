April 27, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: MALIBU;

HEARING ON PHONE ANTENNA

The Santa Monica-Malibu Board of Education has scheduled a public hearing May 8 on a request by PacTel Cellular to build a cellular telephone antenna at Malibu Park Middle School. PacTel Cellular is seeking to lease the site for 25 years. The district would be paid $500 a month for 15 years, and the rent for the remaining 10 years would be negotiated later.
