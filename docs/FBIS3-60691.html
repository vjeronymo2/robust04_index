Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the ``Radar'' column by Ancelmo Gois] [Text] Since it began to operate in March 1982, the Angra dos Reis nuclear plant has been idle for a total of 1,937 days. This is probably a world record. The Angra dos Reis nuclear plant generated its last kilowatt on 5 March. Furnas [Furnas Electric Power Plants, Inc.] hopes that its nuclear plant will return to life during the second part of January.
