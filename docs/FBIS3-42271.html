Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Da'ud Kuttab] [Excerpt] Jerusalem -- Jibril al-Rajub, adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, has protested the killing of Salim Muwafi, the Fatah Hawks leader in Gaza, by the Israeli forces. He considered this act as an attempt to kill the peace process. In an exclusive interview with AL-QUDS, al-Rajub said that the Palestinian leadership regards the Fatah Hawks as part of the forces that have to protect the national authority. Consequently, we have to provide security for them. [passage omitted]
