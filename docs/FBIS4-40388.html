BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Mainaev] [Text] Bishkek June 16 TASS -- The heads of the national Chambers of Commerce and Industry of the Commonwealth of Independent States opened a conference on Thursday in the Kyrgyz capital of Bishkek. The main items on their agenda are ways to implement earlier-signed and soon-to-be signed economic agreements between the CIS countries, and meetings with influential Kyrgyz businessmen. The two-day conference is being attended by 14 representatives from the CIS and one of the heads of the Estonian Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
