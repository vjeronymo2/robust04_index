920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Ferry Pickering falls to Pounds 577,000 Profits of Ferry Pickering, the packaging, printing and publishing group, fell from Pounds 1.2m to Pounds 577,000 at the pre-tax level for the six months to February 28. Turnover declined to Pounds 13.99m (Pounds 15.12m). Earnings amounted to 3.37p (6.1p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 2.1p. The year-end is to be changed to December and the directors anticipate paying a second interim dividend, covering the period to August 31, in January 1993. The shares slipped 5p to 115p.
