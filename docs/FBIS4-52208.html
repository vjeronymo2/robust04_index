BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- A high-level delegation of the Ministry of National Defence of Laos headed by Choummali Sai-ngason, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and minister of National Defence, arrived here today. It was met at the airport by Choe Kwang, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army [KPA], Vice-Marshal Kim Kwang-chin and other generals and officers of the KPA. A ceremony for welcoming the delegation took place there.
