BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- Several scientific films have been released of late by the Korean scientific and educational film studio. The scientific film "Lactic Acid Drink Made of Water Strained Off Coagulating Bean Curd" introduces an easy method of making the drink. The water in which bean is swollen and the water strained off coagulating bean curd contain different kinds of nutritious ingredients. When they are fermented by putting lactobacilli into them, they are turned into a good lactic acid drink. It is good for daily drinking and is highly effective in promoting the digestive functions and preventing and curing colitis and other diseases. The scientific film "Magnet and Fur" shows the mechanism of the device of cleaning fur from boilers, telling it will pay off profusely if it is installed at all places where fur forms. Besides, the studio has made scientific films of different themes such as "Production of Methane in Winter", "Calcining Egg Coal", "King Tongmyong and His Tomb" and "Taekwon-do and Health".
