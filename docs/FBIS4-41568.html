BFN [Text] Stepanakert, JUNE 27 (SNARK)--Today, the acting Chairman of the Karabakh Supreme Soviet Karen Baburyan left for Strasbourg on the invitation of the European Parliament. Vasiliy Atajanyan, the Chairman's Counsellor on Foreign Policy, told SNARK a meeting with the Azerbaijan Parliamentary delegation on the problem of the Karabakh conflict's settlement will take place there on June 29.
