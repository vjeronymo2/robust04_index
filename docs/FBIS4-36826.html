BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi received Yemeni Justice Minister 'Abdallah Ahmad Ghanim at the B'abda Palace this evening. Yemeni Ambassador to Lebanon Ahmad Muhammad al-Mutawakkil also attended. After the meeting, the Yemeni minister said that he delivered a verbal message to President al-Hirawi from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih regarding the latest developments of the war in Yemen. He said the war was ignited by the separatists to break up Yemen and to move the country backward. He added: I was pleased by his excellency the president's response and eagerness for Yemen's stability, security, and continued unity. The Yemeni minister arrived in Beirut earlier today.
