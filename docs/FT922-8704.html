920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Downturn at First Ireland After-tax revenue of the First Ireland Investment Company fell from Pounds 718,000 to Pounds 589,000 over the 12 months to March 31, equal to earnings of 1.1p against 2p. The dividend is lifted by 0.18p to 1.18p. Dealings in the company's shares commenced on March 15 1990 and since then net asset value per share has fallen by 19.9 per cent in sterling terms and by 16.8 per cent in Irish currency terms.
