940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: Mortgage arrears fall - At a glance Fewer homebuyers were in arrears with their mortgages at the end of June than six months earlier: 142,230 were more than 12 months behind with payments, compared with 151,810 at the end of 1993. Shorter-term arrears also showed an improvement. But lenders warned that rumoured government plans to cut back income support payments to unemployed homeowners could lead to a resurgence of payment problems.
