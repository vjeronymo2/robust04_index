Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] The 10th CPC Congress of Weihai City was held in Weihai from 20 to 23 December. At the first session of the 10th city party committee, a new leading body was elected. Comrade Zang Haiqiang was elected secretary of the city party committee; and Wu Longjiang, Sun Shoupu, Cui Yuechen (1508 2574 5256), and Lian Chengmin (6647 2110 2404) were elected deputy secretaries of the committee. At the first meeting of the discipline inspection commission under the Weihai city party committee, held at the same time, Comrade Xue Hongzhen (5641 3163 2182) was elected secretary of the discipline inspection commission.
