BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Kishinev, 5 May TASS -- The Moldavian [Moldovan] Government and a visiting delegation from the Council of Europe are to discuss the programme of mutual cooperation. The delegation, which includes experts from the European Parliament, arrived here on Wednesday [4 May] night. Moldavia, which now enjoys the status of special invitee, may be admitted to the Council of Europe during the organisation's autumn meeting, Dmitriy Dyakov, chairman of the Moldavian Parliament Commission for Foreign Policy, told journalists. During the two-day visit, the delegation is scheduled to meet parliament head Petr Luchinskiy, Prime Minister Andrey Sangeli and Foreign Minister Mikhail Popov.
