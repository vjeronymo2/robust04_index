940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Electrocomponents expands in Denmark Electrocomponents, the electrical and mechanical distribution group, yesterday announced the acquisition, by its RS International subsidiary, of Radio-Parts of Denmark for DKr28m (Pounds 2.8m). Radio-Parts, the leading high service level distributor in Denmark, achieved operating profits of DKr3.8m on turnover of DKr61.7m in the 12 months to June 30. Net assets amounted to DKr21.2m.
