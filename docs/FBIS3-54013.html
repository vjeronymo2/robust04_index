Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak and Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat held a bilateral meeting at the presidential headquarters this morning. They reviewed the latest developments in the Middle East peace process in light of the talks recently held on the Palestinian track. After 30 minutes of private talks, members of the Egyptian and Palestinian delegations joined the meeting. The Egyptian delegation included Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa; Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif; Dr. Zakariya 'Azmi, head of the Presidential Office; and Dr. Usamah al-Baz, Foreign Ministry first under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs. The Palestinian delegation included Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, head of the PLO Information Department; Ahmad Quray', head of the PLO Economic Department; Dr. Nabil Sha'th, president 'Arafat's political adviser and head of the Palestinian delegation to the Gaza-Jericho talks; Sa'id Kamal, ambassador of Palestine in Cairo; and Zuhdi al-Qidrah, representative of the Fatah Movement in Cairo.
