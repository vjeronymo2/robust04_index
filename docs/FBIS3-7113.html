Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 24 -- Dr. Sueo Machi, deputy director general of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) concluded today his five-day working visit to Vietnam. While here Dr. Machi and Prof. Nguyen Dinm Tu, director of the National Institute of Nuclear Energy (NINE) exchanged views on activities of IAEA and NINE and the application of nuclear technology in agriculture, industry, health care and environmental protection. Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, received Dr Machi on March 20. During the reception Dr Machi highlighted the close cooperation of the Vietnamese Government in implementing projects funded by IAEA. He also expressed his hope that in the future, nuclear technology will be widely applied in various economic branches in Vietnam, especially in agriculture. For his part, Minister Nguyen Manh Cam thanked IAEA for its aid to Vietnam through technological assistance projects and expressed Vietnam's concern about the promotion of cooperation between IAEA and Vietnam in the use of nuclear energy for peace purpose. Dr Machi visited sub-institutes of the National Institute of Nuclear Energy in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City and Da Lat.
