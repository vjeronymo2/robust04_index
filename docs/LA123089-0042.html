December 30, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Two pro basketball fans were shot after they refused to surrender their wallets to gun-wielding robbers in Dallas after the Dallas Mavericks-Atlanta Hawks game.

According to Dallas Police who investigated the Wednesday night incident, Troy Bennett, 20, of Dallas, and Christopher Martin, 20, of Dallas, were both shot in the arm during the robbery attempt. They were treated at Charlton Methodist Hospital and later released.
