940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Panasonic's portable PC Panasonic, the electronics company, has designed what it believes to be the first genuinely portable, multimedia PC. The Panasonic CF-41 is a battery-operated, notebook PC with a CD-ROM (read only computer memory stored on a compact disc) drive which weighs less than 8lbs. The notebook will be launched at the end of October costing between Pounds 2,700 and Pounds 4,200. Panasonic UK: UK, tel 0344 853598; fax 0344 853847
