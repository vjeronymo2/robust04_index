July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

UPLAND GROUP BUYS 13-ACRE SITE IN NORCO

A 13.55-acre site on River Road in Norco in Riverside County has been sold for $3.25 million.

Misretta/Downey Roach, an Upland partnership, purchased the land from Sam Spizman of Palm Springs. The Hanes Co. handled the deal.
