921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Gas supply joint venture for London LONDON Electricity has formed a joint venture with Total Gas Marketing, part of the French international oil group, to supply gas to to the capital. London Total Gas will use British Gas's existing pipeline system to sell gas to commerce and industry, and plans to sell gas to household customers when that becomes possible. London Electricity is the 11th of the 12 regional electricity companies in England and Wales to enter the gas supply market.
