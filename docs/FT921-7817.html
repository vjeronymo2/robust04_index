920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / Few vote in Punjab election Only 30 per cent of Punjabi voters went to the polls yesterday in the state's first elections in nearly five years after Sikh militants urged a boycott, reports K K Sharma from New Delhi. The election is for 13 members of parliament and 117 members of the state legislature. Fear of the militants was blamed for the low turnout, particularly in the rural areas.
