930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / Syria 'to blame for attacks' ISRAEL yesterday blamed Syria for the upsurge in attacks by Arab guerrillas in southern Lebanon which last week left five Israeli soldiers dead, and said it was considering how best to retaliate, writes Julian Ozanne from Jerusalem. Syria, which has up to 40,000 troops in Lebanon, responded by saying Israel's self-declared security zone in southern Lebanon was an 'explosive trap'.
