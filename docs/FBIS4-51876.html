BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 30 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to many units and working people who had actively helped in the construction of grand monumental edifices including the monument to the victory in the fatherland liberation war. They include the North Pyongan Provincial People's Committee, the Pyongchon District, Pyongyang Municipality, Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central Court, the Kangwon provincial bureau of public security, the Kumsong general tractor works, veterans of the fatherland liberation war in different places of the country and Chon Pil-yol residing in Sinhung-tong, Hyesan City, Yanggang Province. Upon receiving thanks, they vowed, filled with revolutionary enthusiasm, to creditably discharge their duties with loyalty to the party and the leader, to the homeland and the people and with the spirit of devoted service and thus firmly defend and further glorify the Korean-style socialist system centred on the masses.
