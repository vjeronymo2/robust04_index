Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4 -- General Secretary Do Muoi of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee received here this afternoon Mongolian President P. Ochirbat and his entourage, now on an official friendship visit to Vietnam. In a cordial and sincere atmosphere, the two leaders informed each other of the situation in each country and exchanged views on orientations and steps to enhance the traditional and friendly relations, and expand the existing cooperation between the two countries. Mr Do Muoi praised President Ochirbat's Vietnam visit as an important landmark which ushers a new chapter in the relations between the two countries in line with the common tendency of our times. He expressed his deep gratitude to the Mongolian fraternal people for their wholehearted and valuable support and assistance to Vietnam's national defence and construction in the past years. He affirmed that Vietnam wishes and is striving to create the friendship and strengthen the solidarity and expand the multi-faceted cooperation with Mongolia. For his part, President Ochirbat thanked the Vietnamese party, state and people for extending him and the Mongolian delegation a warm welcome. He expressed his satisfaction at the fine results of his visit, which he said would contribute to the further promotion of ties between the two countries. Also in this afternoon, Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet paid a visit to Mongolian President Ochirbat, who arrived here yesterday. President Ochirbat and Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet talked cordially about the friendly and cooperative links between the two countries. The Mongolian president briefed Mr Vo Van Kiet of the results of the talks held between him and President Le Duc Anh. He said that agreements reached during that talks were the guidelines to further strengthen the traditional friendship and cooperation between the two fraternal peoples. He expressed his hope that these relations would enter into a new stage of development with cooperation agreements on economic, scientific and technical, and trade fields between the two countries to be signed soon. At the meeting Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet said that though each country had their own advantage and difficulty, Mongolia and Vietnam could promote the mutually-benefit cooperation for the interest of the people of each country and of the region. The Vietnamese Government and people always treasure the traditional friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Mongolia. Mr. Kiet affirmed that the Vietnamese Government would implement the agreements on the bilateral cooperation development agreed upon by the Vietnamese and Mongolian presidents during President Ochirbat's visit to Vietnam. Earlier in the second day of his official visit, Mongolian President P.Ochirbat and his entourage paid a floral tribute to late President Ho Chi Minh, visited his home and office, then the Ho Chi Minh Museum. Speaking at today's meeting with a delegation of the Vietnam-Mongolia Friendship Association led by its President Hoang Minh Thang, President Ochirbat expressed his pleasure at the continued development of the traditional friendship between the two countries and the association's activities contributing to the strengthening of the relations and the mutual understanding between the two nations and between the Vietnamese and Mongolian people. President Ochirbat, his wife and his delegation also called at the historical museum and a number of economic and cultural establishments in the capital.
