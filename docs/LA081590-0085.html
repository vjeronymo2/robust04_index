August 15, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

QUOTABLE

"I had this perception in the air around me that there was something wrong with me, and I knew that what was wrong with me wasn't wrong with anyone else.

And I saw that if you talk in a loud voice people will think you have something to say, so I cultivated a loud voice. And I learned to say all my vowels and consonants. I developed grandeur at a very early age. Say, 4."

-- Actress Linda Hunt, in the New Yorker
