BFN [Text] Istanbul June 9 TASS -- Speaking at a foreign minister level sessopn of the NATO Council here today, French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe said that he is for an all-embracing partnership with Russia in political, economic and military spheres. The head of the French Foreign Ministry expounded this stand at a closed-door meeting, the AFP news agency reported, quoting informed sources. According to Juppe, Western countries must not only involve Russia in resolving questions of security in Europe, but also propose to Russia to conclude a treaty on partnership with the European Unon, induct her into the Council of Europe and give her an opportunity to participate in work of the G-7's political sphere, the next regular session of which is scheduled to be held in Naples in July.
