[Dkt. 9250] Stouffer Foods Corporation; Prohibited Trade Practices and Affirmative Corrective Actions AGENCY: Federal Trade Commission. ACTION: Final Order.

SUMMARY: This final order prohibits Stouffer Foods Corporation, the manufacturer and advertiser for Lean Cuisine frozen entrees, from misrepresenting, in any manner, the existence or amount of sodium or any other nutrient or ingredient in any of its frozen-food products.
