Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres met his Egyptian counterpart 'Amr Musa in Davos this morning in anticipation of his meeting with Yasir 'Arafat this evening. The PLO chairman told a British diplomat the gap between the sides is wide and outside intervention is required to bridge it. The Egyptian foreign minister will meet 'Arafat after the latter arrives in Davos this afternoon, and later he will hold another meeting with Peres. Mr. Peres expects a tough meeting with 'Arafat, perhaps the toughest so far at this stage of the talks. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in English at 1300 GMT on 29 January reports that "Musa told Israel radio that he intends to mediate between Israel and the Palestinians prior to the Peres-'Arafat meeting tonight. The 'Arafat-Peres meeting is due to begin at 2100, at first alone and then later with the heads of the two delegations.]
