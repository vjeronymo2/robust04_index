Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 2 -- A delegation from Cambodia's National Assembly [NA] led by its chairman, Samdech Chea Sim, arrived here this morning as guest of National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh. The delegation was welcomed at an official ceremony held at the presidential palace by Chairman Nong Duc Manh, Deputy Prime Minister Tran Duc Luong, National Assembly Vice Chairman Dang Quan Thuy, Chairman of the NA Council of Nationalities Y Ngong Niek Dam, head of the NA Commission for External Relations Hoang Bich Son, and Deputy Foreign Minister Trang Quang Co. Cambodian Charge d'Affaires Chhong Toeun was also on hand. Speaking at the ceremony, Chairman Nong Duc Manh said that the visit would contribute to the promotion of the two countries' in the coming period. For his part, Samdech Chea Sim stressed that the first visit to foreign countries of a delegation of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia is to Vietnam, expressing the great significance in the two countries' relationship. He said he hoped that after the visit, the relations between the two legislative bodies would have more chances for further cooperation.
