Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A conference on supporting the Palestinian police, under the auspices of Norway and with the participation of 25 states, in addition to the World Bank and the UN, opened in Cairo today. Prior to the conference, a meeting was held among Dr. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation; Israeli Foreign Ministry Director General Uri Savir; Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs; Russian Middle East envoy Viktor Posuvalyuk; and Norwegian Foreign Ministry adviser Larsen.
