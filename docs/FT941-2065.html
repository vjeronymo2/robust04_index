940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / World Trade News: Gas contracts agreed Five contracts have been awarded for conceptual engineering on the proposed UK-Continent Gas Interconnector, a Pounds 300m natural gas pipeline which will provide a direct link between the UK and European gas grids. Conceptual work on the Belgian terminal at Zeebrugge will be done by Tractabel, with British Gas designing the UK terminal at Bacton. A consortium headed by EMC will work on the 215km-long, offshore pipeline with Geoteam conducting the offshore survey. Haecon was awarded a contract for conceptual engineering on the onshore section in Belgium. The Interconnector group, which includes British Gas, British petroleum, Conoco, Distrigaz, Elf, Norsk Hydro and Statoil, announced that they were contemplating enlarging the diameter of the pipeline.
