BFN [Text] Amman -- An informed Palestinian source has said that al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim, state of Palestine ambassador to Jordan and Fatah Central Committee member, was appointed secretary general of the Palestinian Authority council at the rank of minister. The source said that al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim will leave Amman for the Palestinian self-rule area to start his new job in Gaza and Jericho.
