October 7, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION ROUNDUP;

NORTHERN CONFERENCE

Verdugo Hills 18, Hollywood 13 -- Quarterback Lance Novak completed nine of 17 passes for 139 yards and three touchdowns at Hollywood, and the Dons limited the Sheiks (0-3, 0-1) to 131 total yards.

Verdugo Hills (1-2, 1-0) built an 18-6 lead on scoring passes by Novak to Joe Lecher of 28 and 25 yards, and of 10 yards to Robert Jamison. Hollywood's Moriba Camara cut the lead to five on a 50-yard punt return. SCOT BUTWELL
