940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Suter lifts J Wilkes stake to 29.53% Suter, the industrial conglomerate, has bought a further 1.53m shares (8.7 per cent) in James Wilkes, the specialist engineer, bringing its holding to 5.21m shares (29.53 per cent). Suter said the shares were for investment purposes and it had made no decision about its next course of action.
