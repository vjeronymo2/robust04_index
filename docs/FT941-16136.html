940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / Quebec gets new cabinet Mr Daniel Johnson, 49, the new Liberal premier of Quebec, yesterday announced a cabinet of 20, down from the 25 of his predecessor Mr Robert Bourassa, who is retiring. The new finance minister is Mr Andre Bourbeau, who has previously held several ministerial posts. Mr John Ciaccia retains the international affairs portfolio. Mr Johnson, a federalist and scion of a leading Quebec political family, entered politics in 1981 after eight years in business. He was educated at universities in Montreal and London and at Harvard.
