BFN [Text] Aden, 27 Apr (MENA)--An official Yemeni Defense Ministry spokesman in Aden has said that a large-scale military campaign is moving toward 'Imran Camp (which witnessed clashes between northern and southern units today) to besiege the 3d Armored Brigade (southern) despite telephone appeals to the chairman of the Presidential Council and the chief of staff for a cease-fire. The spokesman said in a statement tonight that the campaign does not only target the "steadfast" brigade but seeks to push Yemen into a devastating war. The spokesman warned "those who play with fire" against such acts and demanded an immediate halt to the fighting against the 3d Armored Brigade. The spokesman appealed to the joint military committee to investigate the incidents and condemn those responsible for them.
