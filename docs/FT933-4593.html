930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Queens Moat sells stake QUEENS Moat Houses, the hotel group trying to restructure its Pounds 1bn debt, has sold for Pounds 4m its stake in the S&S Group of companies involved in residential property development. The buyer is Genesis Holdings, in which Mr John Williams, a former Queens Moat director, has a stake. The board of Queens Moat resigned after its serious financial condition was revealed and its shares were suspended.
