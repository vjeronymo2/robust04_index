920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / UK Company News: TransAtlantic merger approved The recommended merger between TransAtlantic Holdings, the Luxembourg-listed life assurance and property group, and Capital & Counties, the UK property group in which Transatlantic owns a majority stake, was approved yesterday in court meetings and at extraordinary general meetings of the two companies. The deal is expected to be sanctioned by the High Court on July 27 and to be made effective on July 30.
