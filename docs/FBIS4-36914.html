BFN [Unattributed report: "Russian Experts To Prepare Northern Air Force"] [Text] London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Yemeni military sources have said that Russian aviation engineers have arrived in Sanaa recently to supervise the preparation of a squadron of Sukhoy fighter aircraft. The information says that the five engineers who were granted entry visas to Yemen at Sanaa airport are: Alivov Aliksuvich, passport No. 1402266-41; Otsirnov Alikov Bigrovich, passport No. 1402267-41; Blukhin Tsikhal Ikhliuvich, passport No. 1402265-41; Bgribineskiy Ginya Livovich, passport No. 1402264-41; Yamorin Vladimir Ivanovich, passport No. 1279750-431 [all names as transliterated]. Aden had accused states it did not name of financing a huge arms deal which includes Sukhoy aircraft for Sanaa, saying that these arms are supplied from the former Soviet Union's arsenal in the Republic of Uzbekistan. Information obtained by AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT says that Colonel Muhammad Salih al-Ahmar, half brother of the Yemeni president and commander of the Air Force and Air Defense, was behind the official invitation sent to the five Russian engineers, as mentioned in the original version of the invitation document sent by the General Staff in Sanaa. Sources say that Yemeni Embassy officials in Moscow pointed out to the Russian experts that there was no means of transport to Sanaa as a result of the fighting, but the experts surprised Yemeni diplomats by saying they would go to Sanaa by a special plane -- which confirms the existence of direct contacts between Sanaa and the Russian experts without the Yemeni Embassy's knowledge. The sources add that northern military [officials] visit Tashkent to purchase various types of weapons, especially fighter aircraft.
