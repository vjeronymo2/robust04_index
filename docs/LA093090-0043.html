September 30, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROARING RIDES IN GRAND CANYON

Grand Canyon North Rim Trail Rides offers full-day trips to Roaring Springs for $52, including a picnic lunch.

Riders must be at least 12 years old, weigh less than 200 pounds and understand English. Riding experience is not required, but being in good physical condition is important. Drinking water is supplied. Riders may carry only one camera or one pair of binoculars and a jacket. Half-day and one- and two-hour rides are available. Some excursions accept children as young as 6.

An information and reservations desk is at the Grand Canyon Lodge. Call (602) 638-2292. During the off-season, contact Grand Canyon North Rim Trail Rides, P.0., Box 58, Tropic, Utah 84776, (801) 679-8665. JEAN CRAMER
