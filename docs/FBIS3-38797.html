Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Greece's Coast Guard yesterday made the largest ever cocaine seizure in Greek history when it discovered 60 kgs of the drug hidden aboard a ship, the Ministry of Merchant Marine said last night. The cocaine, in paste form, was found in the engine exhausts of the 10,736 ton Panamanian-flagged Kinaros V. after it went into a dry dock near Athens for repairs. The vessel, a refrigerator ship, had been sitting in port for nearly eight months after returning from a trip to Colombia.
