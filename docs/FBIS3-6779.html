Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense and Security Minister Edi Sudrajat and Singapore's defense minister, Yeo Ning Hong, held talks on the technological development of defense and security at the Defense and Security Department this morning in Jakarta. They particularly discussed the aerospace field as well as socioeconomic issues. Accompanying the Singapore defense minister were Lim Siong Guan, permanent secretary to the Ministry of Defense; Brigadier-General By Soo Khiang, the air chief of staff; and Commodore Kwek Siew Jin, the Navy chief of staff. Singapore Defense Minister Yeo Ning Hong and Defense and Security Minister Edi Sudrajat will attend the commissioning of the Air Combat Manuevering Range in Pekanbaru, Riau tomorrow.
