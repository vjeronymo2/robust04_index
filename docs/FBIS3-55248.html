Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] Rustembekov, the state secretary of [word indistinct] of the Kyrgyz Republic held an expanded sitting with the participation of leaders of the State Committee for National Security, the Interior Ministry, the procurator's office, and a number of ministries, departments, and banks. The participants familiarized themselves with the president's latest instructions, which he had signed on the eve of the meeting. These documents concern strict observance of the government's resolution on the urgency of specifically targeted social protection of the population. Urgent measures were discussed in order to swiftly tackle the emerging problems. The head of state set up working commissions that will control the accuracy and timeliness of paying wages, pensions, and stipends. These commissions, which consist of the president's apparatus' personnel, will be visiting all regions of the republic in accordance with a relevant schedule. They must submit the results of their audit by 15 January. The state secretary under the president will coordinate this work. The working groups are led by: Bakayev, chairman of the State Security Committee; Interior Minister Sutalinov; Procurator General Sharshenaliyev; Justice Minister Cholponbayev; Finance Minister Shakirov; Khakimov, minister of labor and social protection; and Kunakov, director of the State Taxation Inspectorate. Senior officials of the government's apparatus will cooperate with them. Members of the working commission have left for the regions.
