August 25, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SHOW'S ON WAY OUT BECAUSE OF HIMSELF

The change in colors of the Padre uniforms is long overdue. The ugly mud and yellow has been an eyesore for many years. The change to blue and orange will be most welcome by all San Diego Padre fans, and I commend the new owners, one and all.

BOB FARRAR

La Jolla
