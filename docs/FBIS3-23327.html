Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Mar. 8 (A.A.) -- Two university students were shot to death near Aziziye, Diyarbakir Tuesday. The Anatolian News Agency was told that the incident happened when unidentified assailants stopped a public bus coming from Dicle University to the provincial center and opened fire on three university students. Another, who was wounded during the attack, was rushed to the hospital. The assailants fled after the attack.
