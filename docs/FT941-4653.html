940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Observer: Tebbit's twist Sighs of relief in Downing Street. Lord Tebbit, the Tory party's arch-Eurosceptic, has told John Major he intends spending the whole of the campaign for June's European elections in the US. Thus no one will be able to blame him when the campaign exposes the fault-lines in the party's attitude to Europe. But Tebbit may have the last laugh after the expected electoral catastrophe. He plans to return from the US with a bumper sticker reading: 'Don't blame me - I was out of the country.'
