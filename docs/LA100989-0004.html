October 9, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BEACH TRASH

Recently, volunteers gave up their valuable time to clean up all the trash on the local beaches. My point is, why should they do it?

The real culprits are the slobs who leave their trash behind, without even so much as a second glance at the mess. I cannot understand why there isn't a stiff fine, or even a jail sentence for these people. They should be made to take all their own rubbish away, and if a trash container isn't handy, take it home and dispose of it on trash day.

The beaches should be policed regularly, and if these people are guilty, ticket them, just like illegal parking or not using a seat belt.

ELIZABETH C. MOSS

Los Angeles
