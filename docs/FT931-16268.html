930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / (CORRECTED) UK Company News: Sidney Banks moves ahead to Pounds 996,000 Correction (published 8th January 1993) appended to this article. Sidney C Banks, the grain and agricultural specialist, reported a 23 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Pounds 996,000 for the half year ended October 31. The shares responded with a 10p rise to 172p. Turnover amounted to Pounds 119.2m (Pounds 114.7m). Earnings increased from 11p to 12.4p per share and the interim dividend is stepped up from 2.75p to 3p. CORRECTION Pre-tax profits of Sidney C Banks, the grain and agricultural specialist, increased from Pounds 1.25m to Pounds 1.53m in the six months ended October 31 1992. In yesterday's report the net taxed profits given were incorrectly described as pre-tax.
