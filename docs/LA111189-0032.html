November 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEW ADDITIONS MEET WITH THEIR APPROVAL

As a once-upon-a-time journalism student and a lifelong reader of both The Times and Herald Examine, I am among the many thousands who were sad to see the Herald go.

But I am also among many more who I'm sure are as tickled as I am that The Times hired Allan Malamud and Bob Mieszerski.

For my dough, Mieszerski is right up there with Andrew Beyer of the Washington Post and Steven Crist of the New York Times.

Just wanted to say thanks.

LEE HUTSON, Malibu
