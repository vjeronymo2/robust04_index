930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / Northern business confidence seen A MARKED difference in confidence about recovery has emerged between professional property advisers and companies with head offices in northern England, a survey by Coopers & Lybrand, the accountancy firm, shows. Nearly 70 per cent of 154 firms or businesses surveyed expect an upturn in northern property markets in the second half of this year. But occupiers of buildings are more cautious than senior professionals in surveying, engineering and construction.
