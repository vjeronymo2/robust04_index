BFN [Unattributed report: "Belarus Is Expanding Relations in Military Area"] [Text] As the Defense Ministry Legal Administration has reported, Belarus has already signed bilateral interstate agreements on military cooperation with Russia, the United States, Poland, Ukraine, India, and Moldova. It is planning to conclude similar treaties with Uzbekistan, Lithuania, Bulgaria, China, and Romania in the immediate future. Currently, Belarus and its partners are working on different issues linked with these documents.
