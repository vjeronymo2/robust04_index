Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Pshenichnikov] [Text] Oslo, 1 Mar -- Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, who has been in Norway on a working visit, today visited Haakonsvern naval base, located near the town of Bergen, in the west of the country. The Russian guest was given an opportunity to go aboard one of the Norwegian Navy's submarines. During the visit to Haakonsvern, the Russian minister was accompanied by head of Norway's war department, Joergen Kosmo. Today the working visit was completed. Pavel Grachev and the Defense Ministry delegation accompanying him left Bergen for Moscow.
