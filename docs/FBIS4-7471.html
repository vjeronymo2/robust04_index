BFN [Text] During the period of 25-31 March Prime Minister Berov had working meetings with Ahmed Dogan, chairman of the parliamentary faction of the Movement for Rights and Freedoms [DPS]; Nora Ananieva, chairman of the Parliamentary Alliance for Social Democracy; and Zhan Videnov, chairman of the Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] Supreme Council; as well as with Stefan Bozhilov and Georgi Kostov, cochairmen of the parliamentary faction of the New Alliance for Democracy. The prime minister insisted on speeding up the adoption of the amendments to the Law on Reorganizing and Privatizing the State and Municipal Enterprises, and assured the representatives of the National Assembly that the government is prepared to implement the mass privatization program. Certain weaknesses in the work of certain governmental structures also were discussed. Prof. Berov proposed structural changes in the cabinet that will be discussed by the parliamentary factions. The prime minister outlined his views on overcoming the crisis situation in the foreign currency market and stressed the government's determination to successfully complete negotiations on the country's foreign debt.
