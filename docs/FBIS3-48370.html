Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] (Roberto Cabrulli), general secretary of the Italian Movement for Peace and Socialism, received a delegation of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] led by Pak Kyong-son, director of a department of the WPK Central Committee on 18 January. At the meeting, the head of the delegation conveyed greetings from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to General Secretary (Roberto Cabrulli). Expressing deep gratitude, the general secretary asked the head of the delegation to convey his warmhearted greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. He said that the DPRK's success in socialist construction gives great encouragement to his movement. He pointed out that he is pleased at the Korean people's great advance in struggle for national reunification under the wise leadership of the WPK.
