BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoria] [Text] Zugdidi (western Georgia) June 24 TASS -- Two battalions of Russian peackeepers with about a hundred motor vehicles and armoured personnel carriers arrived in the afternoon on Friday in the Zugdidi area. Peacekeepers who came from the Russian base in the Georgian city of Batumi, were met by friendly local population and their drivers saluted back blowing their horns.
