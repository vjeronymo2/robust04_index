940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Cookson sells business for Pounds 2.3m Cookson Group has sold its UK colour compounding and masterbatch business, Colourfast Plastics, to Americhem Holdings International for Pounds 2.3m cash. The disposal is the last stage in the group's exit from this activity. In 1993 Colourfast incurred a small operating loss on turnover of Pounds 2.6m. Its net assets at disposal were about Pounds 2.3m.
