940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / Khmer Rouge releases hostages Ms Melissa Himes, an American aid worker, and two Cambodian colleagues, held hostage for 42 days by Khmer Rouge guerrillas were released unharmed yesterday, aid officials said, Reuter reports from Phnom Penh. 'They are now free - that is the news we got this afternoon,' said an official from the relief agency, Food for the Hungry International. Ms Himes, 24, an FHI development worker, was reported missing on March 31 after she went to retrieve a vehicle in southern Kampot province.
