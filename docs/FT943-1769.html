940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Henderson EuroTst shows improvement Henderson EuroTrust, a split level investment trust, raised net asset value by 10.6 per cent from 126.98p to 140.44p in the year ended July 31. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 679,000 (Pounds 841,000 for 14 months) and earnings per share came to 3.86p (4.84p). The final dividend is 1.85p, making a total of 3.35p (3.2p).
