November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

JAMES BAKER'S STATEMENT ON JOBS AND IRAQ

I am totally supportive of the column by Christopher Martin ("We Who Object Must Be Heard," Commentary, Nov. 16). I couldn't have expressed my feelings any better being a member of that "insignificant" peace movement.

Yes, a peace movement exists! Are you listening, President Bush?

GRETCHEN WALLS, Wilmington
