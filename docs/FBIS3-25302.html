Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has called on the United Nations to stop funding the ANC [African National Congress] and PAC [Pan-Africanist Congress]. In a letter to UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali, Foreign Affairs Minister Pik Botha drew his attention to a UN resolution in terms of which funds were to be given to the two organizations until their positions in South Africa had been normalized. Mr. Botha said that in the light of political developments in the country, and the organizations' registration as political parties, the requirements of the resolution had been met.
