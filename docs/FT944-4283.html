941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / Wellington offers NZDollars 1bn for Maori land The New Zealand government yesterday offered the Maori people NZDollars 1bn (Pounds 405m) in permanent settlement of all land claims dating back to 1840, but Maori leaders said it was not enough. One, Sir Tipene O'Regan, said the present generation had no right to make final settlements that took away the rights of future generations.
