Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Nekrasova] [Text] Lvov March 28 TASS -- Elections may be regarded as valid in all the 450 one-seat constituencies of Ukraine, chairman of the Central Electoral Commission Ivan Yemets announced today in the morning. The last reports came on Sunday night from the Republic of the Crimea, specifically, Sevastopol and Yalta, where the voter turnout was 50.9 and 51.8 percent respectively. A total of 74.7 percent of electors took part in the parliamentary elections in Ukraine, which makes some 28 million people. Final results will be made public on March 29 at a news conference of the Central Electoral Commission.
