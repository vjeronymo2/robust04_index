April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

"The Wonder Years" is a wonderful program. I hope ABC does not discontinue it. When the network advertised the premiere of "Have Faith," I was afraid it was scheduled to replace "The Wonder Years." Fortunately, it did not. That was when I decided to write. I don't want to feel guilty for not telling someone that the high quality of "The Wonder Years" is appreciated.

Raymond M. Ingels, Monterey Park
