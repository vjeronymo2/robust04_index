April 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

FALCON PROTECTOR

Milt Swimmer, partner with J.H. Snyder Co., is to be commended for his attitude towards the pair of peregrine falcons mating on his building ("Love on a Ledge," Metro, April 26). By treating the birds as his "first tenants," Swimmer breaks important ground in the battle of developers vs. nature. I hope his placing the needs of two raptors before those of efficient construction sends a signal to other developers to examine their own priorities.

DOUGLAS C. GOULD

Marina del Rey
