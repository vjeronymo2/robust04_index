BFN [Excerpt] A Russian border guard died on 2 May when the (Navranga) border post in the sector under the 12th Moscow border detachment on the Tajik-Afghan border was fired on. According to a report from the press office of Russia's borderguards, the serviceman received fatal wounds and died on the way to hospital. It is thought that a sniper was firing on the borderguards. However, the precise location of his position was not found. [passage omitted]
