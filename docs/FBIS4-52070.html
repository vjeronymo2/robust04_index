BFN ["Flash news"] [Text] According to a source in the General Staff of the Khmer Royal Armed Forces [KRAF], at 1000 on 3 May KRAF units assigned to ensure security for the populace along Route 10 in Battambang Province launched a counterattack against Khmer Rouge [KR] forces who were plundering and torching people's houses in villages and communes on Route 10. According to a preliminary report on the clash with the Khmer Rouge [KR], five Khmer Rouge soldiers were killed on the spot, and many others were wounded and were taken away by their comrades. The KRAF seized a T-58 tank, a car, a 76.2-mm cannon, a 37-mm gun, a 23-mm gun, and a large number of handguns and machineguns. The KRAF General Staff will supply additional details about the situation on the battlefield later.
