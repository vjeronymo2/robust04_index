940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / Poll puts SNP vote down to 27% Support for the Scottish National party, which in late June reached its highest level since 1988, has fallen back, says a System Three opinion poll in today's Herald newspaper puts support for the party at 27 per cent compared with 30 per cent in the previous poll. Labour is on 51 per cent (46 per cent), the Conservatives unchanged on 12 per cent and the Liberal Democrats on 9 per cent (11 per cent). The poll was conducted among 1,027 people between July 28 and August 2.
