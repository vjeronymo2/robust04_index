940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Branson drops lottery demands Mr Richard Branson last night dropped demands for the national lottery franchise to be subject to judicial review, after a meeting with Mr Peter Davis, director general of Oflot, the lottery controlling body. Mr Branson had called for the review after his UK Lottery Foundation failed to win the franchise in spite of pledging that every penny raised from the event would be used for good causes with no profits for himself or his partners.
