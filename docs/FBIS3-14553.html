Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shots were fired last night at the Nabulus home of Sa'id Kan'an in what appears to be an internal Fatah dispute. No one was injured and no damage was caused. In Janin, two petrol bombs were thrown at the Civil Administration building. Again, no damage was caused. In the Qatif Bloc junction in the Gaza Strip, a bomb exploded close to an Army jeep, which was damaged, but none of the soldiers was injured.
