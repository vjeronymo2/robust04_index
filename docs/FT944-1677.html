941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Electra Inv Trust Fully diluted net asset value per share at Electra Investment Trust stood at 380.24p at end-September, against 341.3p a year earlier. This represented a rise of 11.4 per cent, while the FT-SE-A All-Share Index rose by 0.3 per cent in the period. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 14.7m (Pounds 14.1m), with earnings of 7.5p (7.54p). A proposed final dividend of 3.7p (3.55p) gives a total of 7.25p (7p).
