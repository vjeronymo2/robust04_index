April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

SURINAME POLICE BURN 1,000 KILOS OF COCAINE

Surinamese police Monday burned 1,000 kilograms of cocaine handed over to them by guerrillas who said they seized it from Colombian drug smugglers.

The cocaine, reportedly taken from a Colombian plane that had landed in the guerrillas' stronghold, was worth an estimated $40 million.
