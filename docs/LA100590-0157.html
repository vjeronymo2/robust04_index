October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

'GOOD MORNING AMERICA' HOLDS ITS LEAD

ABC's "Good Morning America" continued its lead in the morning ratings race during the past three months, figures from the A.C. Nielsen Co. showed Thursday.

During July, August and September, "Good Morning America" had an average audience of about 3.9 million homes each weekday morning. NBC's "Today" show averaged about 2.9 million, and CBS' "This Morning" averaged about 2.1 million.
