Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Buenos Aires LA NACION in Spanish on 16 January in Section 1, page 16, publishes an unattributed 250-word article that says police Antidrug Squad agents arrested Luis Alberto Cayo Velazco with more than 6 kg of cocaine hydrochloride during an operation at the corner of Humahuaca and Gascon Streets in Almagro. Velazco was arrested while waiting to pass the cocaine to a contact who never appeared. Agents had been watching the house where the delivery was supposed to have taken place for two months.
