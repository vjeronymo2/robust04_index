BFN [Text] Within the framework of contacts being made to contain the situation set off by influential [mutanaffidah] military forces in the General People's Congress on 27 April in the area of 'Imran, brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, deputy chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] Central Committee, continued contacts with a number of his brother kings and presidents of Arab states. This evening he spoke on the telephone with the custodian of the two holy places, His Majesty King Fahd Bin 'Abd-al-'Aziz al Sa'ud, the king of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, His Excellency President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, the president of the Arab Republic of Egypt, and His Excellency Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, the leader of the 1st of September Revolution. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid briefed the kings and presidents on the latest developments in the Yemeni arena, in light of the massing of troops and reinforcements by these forces, and the pursuit of soldiers of the 3d Armored Brigade in the mountains and areas close to 'Imran, as well as the treatment of the brigade's wounded as POW's in their own homeland. He clarified that these forces are pushing the country into the inferno of a destructive civil war. He asked his brothers the kings and presidents to attempt to prevent this from happening, and to lessen the impact of the pschological and physical pressures from which the wounded are suffering.
