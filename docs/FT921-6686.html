920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: B'ham Midshires falls 40% to Pounds 9.5m Pre-tax profits at Birmingham Midshires, the building society, dropped by 40 per cent last year after loan loss provisions of Pounds 36.6m. Profits for 1991 were down from Pounds 15.9m to Pounds 9.5m. Bad debt provisions in 1990 were Pounds 13.5m. During the year the society repossessed 1,542 homes, proportionately double the average for the industry size. Total assets rose from Pounds 3.65bn to Pounds 3.74bn and reserves by Pounds 6.1m to Pounds 143.1m.
