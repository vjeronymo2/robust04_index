BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, at 1215 today. The meeting was attended by Rashid Akhtarini, deputy prime minister for services affairs; Faruq al-Shar', the foreign minister; and the Yemeni charge d'affaires in Damascus. 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani left Damascus following a two-day visit to Syria. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Rashid Akhtarini, the deputy prime minister for services affairs; and the Yemeni charge d'affaires in Damascus.
