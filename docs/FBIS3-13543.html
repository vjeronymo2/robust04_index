Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The government representative at the military court has instructed all security services to look for those who paraded with weapons in Ba'labakk on 11 March immediately. The security services have been instructed to arrest the demonstrators and refer them to the military court. The government representative added: If this is not possible, the security services should prepare a statement on the identity and place of residence of the demonstrators and another statement that could assist the investigations.
