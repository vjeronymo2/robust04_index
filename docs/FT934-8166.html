931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / Call for open lottery bids THE Lottery Promotion Company, a non-profitmaking body which has been campaigning for charitable status for the National Lottery, said yesterday that the bidding process to determine the lottery's running should be open. Mr Peter Davis, director-general of the National Lottery, is insisting on confidentiality - no details of applications will be published other than the names and addresses of applicants. The office of the National Lottery has stated that it will award a single operating licence. However, Mr Denis Vaughan, director of the Lottery Promotion Company, added yesterday: 'Unless open competitive tendering is used and the bids made public we have no guarantee that the government is creating the right monopoly.'
