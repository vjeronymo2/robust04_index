930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: GPA Mr Tony Ryan, chairman and founder of GPA Group, has agreed in principle with Merrill Lynch, the US investment bank, to restructure his Dollars 35m (Pounds 24.6m) loan facility. It is understood that the loan, which could have been called within 60 days, has been extended to 1996. Mr Ryan borrowed the money against the collateral of his 9.3m shares. GPA's shares have fallen in value from Dollars 30 to just Dollars 1 in the space of a year.
