June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HE'S ALL BROKEN UP OVER RAIDERS' NON-DEPARTURE

Just as we had wound down our party in celebration of the Raiders' departure, we get the bad news they're staying.

Please don't let it be. We don't care where they go; the farther away the better. Take your logo and your Commitment to Greed with you.

FERNANDO A. TRUJILLO

Pomona
