BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Eight members of two separate drug networks have been captured in Istanbul. The 13 kg heroin and 18 kg of hashish in their possession have been seized. According to information obtained from the Istanbul Directorate General, five members of a network preparing to smuggle drugs abroad were captured while in possession of 3 kg of heroin in Istanbul's Atakoy quarter. Meanwhile, three persons trying to sell drugs to the police acting as buyers have been captured in Ferikoy quarter. The 8 kg heroin and 17.5 kg hashish in their possession have been seized. An additional 2 kg of heroin and .5 kg of hashish were seized in searches conducted in hideouts shown by the smugglers. [Ankara TRT Television Network in Turkish at 1000 GMT on 22 Apr reports: "Six kg of pure heroin were seized near Sevindik village in Gurpinar District in Van. One person was arrested. The heroin was hidden in the secret compartments of a car."]
