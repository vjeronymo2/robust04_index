910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Rathbone Brothers advances to Pounds 2.55m Rathbone Brothers, the USM-quoted private banking, investment and financial services group, raised pre-tax profits by 11 per cent, from Pounds 2.3m to Pounds 2.55m, in 1990. Turnover expanded 19 per cent to Pounds 7.6m, against Pounds 6.4m. Tax took Pounds 908,000 (Pounds 837,000) and earnings per share came to 22.11p (20.75p). The final dividend is 6p, for a total up from 6.6p to 8p. Mr Oliver Stanley, chairman, said 1991 had begun well and he was confident that the company could meet growth objectives. Already this year, Rathbone had expanded its investment business by the recruitment of Mr Ian Tod and the purchase of his investment business in Liverpool. The company had restructured its services to help corporate clients with their financial problems, while the tax return service in Liverpool was attracting many new customers.
