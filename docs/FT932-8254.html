930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News In Brief: Bridport-Gundry BRIDPORT-GUNDRY, the medical, aviation and defence products company, is selling certain assets and trade of J&W Stuart to its management for Pounds 300,000 cash. Further consideration up to Pounds 150,000 relates to debtors and stock. Stuart, which makes and sells fishing trawls and supplies fishing gear, made pre-tax profits of Pounds 50,000 in the year to July 31 1992.
