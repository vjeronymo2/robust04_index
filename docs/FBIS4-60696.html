BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 May (MENA) -- Petroleum Minister Hamdi al-Banbi and a technical delegation left Cairo for Damascus today on a three-day official visit to Syria. Al-Banbi said before leaving that he would discuss with his Syrian counterpart, Nadir Nabulsi, ways to boost technical cooperation in the field of petroleum industries and a project for a joint Egyptian-Syrian company for digging and maintaining oil wells. He said Egyptian petrochemical industries will be exported to Syria.
