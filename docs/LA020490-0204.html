February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LEANER, MEANER

After reading Richard Reeves' column (Op-Ed Page, Jan. 24) on the Bush Administration's misuse of the Social Security tax, I finally understand what conservatives are talking about when they say that they want a "meaner, leaner" government: They want the rich to get meaner so the poor can get leaner.

LUCIA K.B. HALL

San Diego
