May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

I'M SKY HIGH WINS AT BELMONT

I'm Sky High closed from near the back of the field and scored a two-length victory in the $50,000 Determined Handicap for 3-year-olds and up Wednesday at Belmont.

Ridden by Mike Smith and carrying 113 pounds, I'm Sky High covered the 1 1/16 miles in 1:41 1/5 and paid $9.20. Doc's Leader was second and Joker's Farce finished third.
