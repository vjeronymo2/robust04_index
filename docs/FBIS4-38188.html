BFN [Text] Baghdad, 24 Jun (INA) -- Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz received here today Ozdem Sanberk, under secretary at the Turkish Foreign Ministry, and his delegation. They reviewed the progress made in draining the Iraqi oil pipeline. They also discussed ways to develop bilateral ties in the best interests of the two friendly peoples and neighboring states. Dr. Riyad al-Qaysi, under secretary at the Iraqi Foreign Ministry, attended the meeting.
