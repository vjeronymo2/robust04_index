July 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WATCH'N WIN TAKES HOLLYPARK FEATURE

Watch'N Win wore down Bag of Magic in the stretch and scored a 1 1/4-length victory in the $31,000 feature Friday at Hollywood Park.

Watch'N Win, ridden by Laffit Pincay, covered the six furlongs in 1:09 2/5 and paid $5.40, $3.40 and $2.40. Bag of Magic, with Corey Black up, paid $4.80 and $2.60. Waterscape, with Eddie Delahoussaye aboard, was another 3 1/2 lengths back and paid $2.20.
