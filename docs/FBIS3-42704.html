Language: Russian Article Type:CSO ["Edict of the President of the Russian Federation `On Making Changes and Additions to the Statute on the Procedure for Consideration of Questions of Citizenship in the Russian Federation'"] [Text] In connection with the adoption of the Law of the Russian Federation "On Making Changes and Additions to the Law of the RSFSR `On RSFSR Citizenship,'" I decree: 1. Changes and additions shall be made to the Statute on the Procedure for Consideration of Questions of Citizenship in the Russian Federation, approved by the Edict of the President of the Russian Federation of 10 April 1992 No. 386 (VEDOMOSTI SYEZDA NARODNYKH DEPUTATOV I VERKHOVNOGO SOVETA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, 1992, No. 17, p. 952), set forth with new wording (appended). 2. The present edict shall take effect on the day of its publication. [Signed] President of the Russian Federation B. Yeltsin the Kremlin, Moscow 27 December 1993 No. 2299
