BFN [Text] The visit of Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic to Moscow will be of non-official nature. He will come in order to receive the Sholokhov literature award, Russian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Grigoriy Karasin told Interfax on Thursday. Karasin said that Karadzic had informed the Russian Foreign Ministry on his visit and had asked to arrange for him several meetings with Russian officials. "We will see how it will be. The issue has not been solved yet since we do not know the schedule of the officials who Karadzic wants to meet," Karasin emphasized. Karasin did not rule out meetings at the official level.
