BFN ["Edict of the Georgian Head of State on the Appointment of D. Asatiani as Deputy Chairman of the Republic of Georgia Chamber of Control" issued in Tbilisi on 11 May] [Text] To appoint Davit Asatiani, head of the Republic of Georgia Chamber of Control Internal Departmental Control and Special Assignments Office, also as deputy chairman of the Republic of Georgia Chamber of Control. [Signed] E. Shevardnadze. Tbilisi, 11 May 1994.
