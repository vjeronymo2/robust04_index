940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: GrandMet share price blow - At a glance Grand Metropolitan, the international drinks and food company, has seen an improvement in its share price relative to the FT-SE 100 index since the end of last year after three years of outperformance. However, it expects falling demand in the US drinks market to cost it Pounds 40m this year and the share price fell 26p to 457p on Thursday when it announced its interim results.
