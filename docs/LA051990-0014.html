May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FRAUD AND DEFENSE CONTRACTORS

Congratulations, Times, and congratulations, Ralph Vartabedian, on a very fine and necessary job well-done!

Finally, the truth comes out! It's what activists have been saying all along, but no one wanted to listen to them. It was first-rate coverage of the scope that this scandal is taking, the tip of the iceberg, as we're just beginning to realize but always feared. By all means, stay with it.

CASEY KASEM

Los Angeles
