Administration on Aging White House Conference on Aging; Recognition of Activities; Extension of Recognition Period AGENCY: White House Conference on Aging, AoA, HHS. ACTION: Notice; extension of recognition period.

SUMMARY: The 1995 White House Conference on Aging (WHCOA) published a document in the Federal Register on March 17, 1994, page 12609, announcing its intention to recognize local and regional activities, events and programs developed by outside organizations during 1994. The WHCOA is extending the period for recognition to April 30, 1995.
