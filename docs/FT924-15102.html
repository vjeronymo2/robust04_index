921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / International Company News: Citibank reshapes German interests CITIBANK, the leading US bank, said it would reorganise its German banking interests into one new bank holding company in preparation for stricter capital adequacy rules that take effect next year. Until now the interests in Citibank's three German units have been split between holding companies in the US and Germany. The new holding company - Citibank Beteiligungen - will include the former Citibank AG banking business in Frankfurt, Citibank Privatkunden, the private banking division in Dusseldorf, and Citicorp Kartenservice, the credit card business in Frankfurt.
