Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report under "Yesterday" rubric: "Kazakhstan Will Fulfill Its Peacemaking Mission to the End"] [Text] As Tulegen Zhukeyev, state counselor of the Republic of Kazakhstan, told journalists, the Kazakh battalion presently stationed in Khorog, the administrative center of the Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Oblast, which is working with Russian border guards to protect the Tajik-Afghan border, will not be withdrawn from the Repulbic of Tajikistan. All that is happening is that servicemen whose contracts have expired are being replaced. Zhukeyev stressed that Kazakhstan is consistently fulfilling all the documents signed by it regarding participation in the peacemaking mission in Tajikistan.
