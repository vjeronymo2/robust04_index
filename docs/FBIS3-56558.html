Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk January 15 TASS -- U.S. President Bill Clinton, currently here on an official stay, visited Kuropaty, the burial place of Stalin repressions' victims, this afternoon. The president lit a candle and inclined his head in a minute of silence near the memorable cross. Bill Clinton left a symbolic memorial sign in Kuropaty to read in Belarussian and English "from the people of the United States of America to the people of Belarus. January 15, 1994". The President was accompanied by Belarussian leader Stanislav Shushkevich. He left Kuropaty for the airport to depart for Geneva after meeting staffers of the U.S. Embassy.
