930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Ivory Coast destroys first 60 tonnes of confiscated cocoa The Ivory Coast yesterday destroyed the first 60 tonnes of cocoa out of 1,000 tonnes confiscated for failing to meet export quality standards and said it would destroy all substandard beans whenever they were discovered, reports Reuter from Abidjan. The 60 tonnes was only a test batch to find the best way of burning the beans and more would be burnt tomorrow, officials said.
