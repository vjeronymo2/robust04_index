Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih today received Veselin Koshev, leader of the Bulgarian Liberal Democratic Party, and his delegation. The National Assembly speaker reviewed the dimensions of the U.S. plot aimed at harming Iraq's sovereignty and national unity. He also reviewed the Iraqi people's extensive efforts to rebuild what the evil ones have destroyed and to continue the march of progress and reconstruction under the leadership of struggler President Saddam Husayn. He emphasized Iraq's desire to restore relations with Bulgaria to the state that prevailed before the spiteful 30-state aggression in the service of the two friendly peoples' interests. The leader of the Bulgarian Liberal Democratic Party praised the brave stand of the Iraqi leadership and people against a fierce, unprecedented aggression and blockade. He expressed his party's readiness to do all it can to break the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq and to promote the relations of friendship and cooperation between Iraq and Bulgaria. The meeting was attended by Safa' al-'Umar, head of the National Assembly's Arab and International Relations Committee.
