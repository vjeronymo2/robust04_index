930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Castle Mill negotiates debt restructuring Castle Mill International, the lossmaking clothing and household textiles company, has successfully negotiated a financial restructuring package. The proposals principally entail the satisfaction of Pounds 1.5m of bank debt via the issue of 6.31m ordinary shares, the restructuring of the repayment timetable for the balance of the bank debt and the settlement of three items of litigation in which Castle is involved by cash repayments. Last week the group reported a widening of losses from Pounds 182,000 to Pounds 256,000 pre-tax for the half year to end-June on turnover of Pounds 1m (Pounds 3.75m). Losses per share were 1.84p (1.36p). The company's shares shed 1p to 6 3/4 p yesterday.
