August 24, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

SURELOCK turned in his biggest winning total Thursday, collecting a $1,011 profit from his $10 exacta bet when Justoneofthegirls won the eighth race and paid $71 and Cee's Tizzy finished second for a $214.20 payoff. The major haul pushed his money total past the $3,000 mark -- $3,335 to be exact -- and a $1,734.25 lead over his opponent

SURELOCK's wager today will be $10 exacta box on Medical Marvel and Fatease in the fifth race.

SURELOCK's Wed. Bankroll: $2,324

Wednesday's Profit: $1,011

Current Bankroll: $3,335

Janice Goldinger lost $45 dollars when Vault finished out of the money in the fifth race.

For today, Janice will wager a $10 late double on Fashionable Flair in the eighth to Very Personably and $40 to win on Very Personably.

Janice's Wed. Bankroll: $1,645.75
