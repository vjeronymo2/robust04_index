October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

GRETZKY COVERAGE DRAWS DIFFERENT POINTS OF VIEW

Gretzky! Gretzky the Great One! Gretzky the Greatest One!

Enough already! This isn't even a hockey town.

I don't recall Kareem Abdul-Jabbar receiving this kind of coverage when he became the all-time scoring leader in the NBA. I can appreciate what Wayne Gretzky has done for the sport, but, Gretzky Watch, give me a break.

JUANITA JORDAN, Los Angeles
