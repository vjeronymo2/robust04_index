BFN [Excerpt] Aden, Yemen, June 10 (AFP) -- Northern heavy artillery gunners shelled the southern stronghold of Aden on Friday, a day after the collapse of the latest ceasefire in Yemen's civil war. Shells crashed into the Khormaksar district, near the airport used by southern warplanes, an AFP correspondent said. Al-Jumhuriyah hospital said at least two civilians were killed and 15 wounded in shelling of Aden and nearby villages since Thursday night. [passage omitted]
