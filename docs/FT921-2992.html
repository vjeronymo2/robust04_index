920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Further progress as Mayborn rises 75% Taxable profits at Mayborn Group, the USM-quoted florists' sundries, fabric dyes and baby products company, jumped 75 per cent from Pounds 1.56m to Pounds 2.73m in the year to December 31. The outcome followed trebled profits at the interim stage and a recovery from Pounds 200,000 to Pounds 1.56m in 1990. There was a modest advance in turnover from Pounds 32.7m to Pounds 33m but operating profits improved 55 per cent to Pounds 3.43m (Pounds 2.2m). However, the figures for last year included a loss of Pounds 478,000 from discontinued operations. Earnings emerged at 10.1p (7.4p) per share and the final dividend is lifted to a proposed 2.8p for a total of 4.2p (3.8p).
