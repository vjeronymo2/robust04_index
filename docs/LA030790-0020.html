March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

HOMELESS HELP

Your story of the end of Drexel Burnham Lambert's distribution of leftover food from its daily luncheon buffet to homeless people (Feb. 16), puts the lie to George Bush's "thousand points of light" theory. When the lights go out, shouldn't the government be there to make sure people aren't left in the dark?

PETER M. SEARS

Long Beach
