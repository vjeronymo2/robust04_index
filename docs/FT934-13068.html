931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Threat to disrupt Booker ceremony NEXT WEEK'S Booker Prize, the most prestigious event of the literary calendar, faces disruption by several dozen mushroom pickers dismissed by a Booker subsidiary after a new compensation offer from the company was rejected. Many of the 89 part-time women workers who were dismissed by Middlebrook Mushrooms for taking industrial action over a reduction in overtime pay are expected to form a picket line outside London's Guildhall and hand out copies of their book about the dispute.
