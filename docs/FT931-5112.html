930308 FT 08 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Upgrading housing in Essex UNITED HOUSE in Swanley has been awarded a Pounds 6m design and build housebuilding and improvement contract jointly from Basildon District Council and the New Islington and Hackney Housing Association - to completely modernise, upgrade and extend the Crudens Estate at Pitsea in Essex. The work includes the design and construction of 64 houses, bungalows and flats (Pounds 2.3m), the refurbishment of 351 houses and flats including roofs, external walls and landscaping (Pounds 2.5m) and new infrastructure (Pounds 1.2m).
