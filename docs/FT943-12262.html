940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 227,000 loss for Clinical Computing The costs of moving to new premises in the UK, setting up an office in the US and investing in product development, together with delays in customers placing orders, pushed Clinical Computing, the medical software company, into a Pounds 227,000 loss for the six months to June 30. The share price fell 10p to 90p on the news, against a flotation price of 124p and a high of 173p on the first day of trading when the company came to market in a Pounds 3.1m flotation in February. Turnover was down from Pounds 1.18m to Pounds 735,000 and the first-half loss compares with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 446,000 for the same period last year. Losses per share came out at 1.4p against earnings of 3.7p.
