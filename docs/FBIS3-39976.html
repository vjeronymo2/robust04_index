Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara (A.A) -- Foreign Ministry Deputy Spokesman Ferhat Ataman has announced that work is under way regarding the delivery of urgent essentials to Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. Ataman stated that the Greek Government's 16 February decision imposing an economic embargo on Macedonia exacerbated the already dire economic conditions in that country. Ataman said that work is under way to supply Macedonia with petrol and fuel oil at convenient prices adding Turkey will open its ports to transportation destined for Macedonia. It is expected, Ataman further stated, that the Macedonian Deputy Prime Minister Jovan Andonov will visit Ankara.
