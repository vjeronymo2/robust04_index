November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Legal Eagles: So what's the deal with "L.A. Law's" Arnie Becker and Roxanne? Will the sleazy-but-lovable divorce attorney and his long-suffering secretary on the NBC series ever get together? Susan Ruttan, who plays Roxanne, says she and co-star Corbin Bernsen wonder, too. "We fantasize that in the very last episode, in the very last year, he'll look at Roxanne and say, 'Oh my gawd, it's been you all along!' " Ruttan said this week on her own honeymoon in Honolulu. "And she'll go, 'Bleah!' " Ruttan is in Hawaii with her husband of three weeks, sound technician and artist Randy McDonald. BETH KLEID
