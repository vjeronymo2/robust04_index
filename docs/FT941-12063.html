940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Healthcare Hldgs Healthcare Holdings has completed the sale of two hospitals to General de Sante International for Pounds 6.5m cash. Proceeds will be used to repay bank borrowings and other unsecured creditors. Directors confirmed that the group had entered into non-binding heads of agreement with a third party with a view to a recommended offer being made for Healthcare. The shares remain suspended.
