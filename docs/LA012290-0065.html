January 22, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LANE CHANGE: Even as officials take up the touchy issue of how to go back to the voters with a new transportation sales tax (B1), some vocal foes of last fall's failed tax measure are mulling their own proposal. . . . A change of heart? Not really. The group, Drivers for Highway Safety, fought the 1989 tax because some of the money would go to car-pool lanes. . . . Any proposal coming from the group would preclude funds for car pools and rail transit, sources said. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
