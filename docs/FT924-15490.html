921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Demand for spot and nearby deliveries of Indonesian cassia as well as for substitutes from Madagascar, Seychelles and China exploded, reports Man-producten. Grinders have not been able to keep up with seasonal spot orders. Traders have been cautious about building stocks. Prices will remain high until mid-November when demand is expected to retreat. Va/ka sticks, spot USDollars 2,600 a tonne, shipment Oct/Nov Dollars 1,975; kbbc spot Dollars 1,700, shipment Oct/Nov Dollars 1,350, Dec/Jan Dollars 1,250; Seychelles spot Dollars 1,500, shipment Dollars 1,300; Madagascar spot unavailable, shipment Dollars 1,200. Cloves unchanged at Dollars 1,000 spot, Dollars 770 shipment.
