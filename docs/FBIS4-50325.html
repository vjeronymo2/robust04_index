BFN [Text] Beijing, May 14 (XINHUA) -- Four prominent Hong Kong businessmen have donated a total of 400 million Hong Kong dollars to set up a foundation to reward Chinese scientists who have made outstanding achievements. The Ho Leung Ho Lee Foundation was founded by Ho Sin-hang, honorary chairman of the Hang Seng Bank Ltd, directors of the bank Leung Kau-kui and Ho Tim, and chairman of the board of directors Lee Quo-wei. The foundation will offer ten prizes a year for achievements in mathematics, physics, chemistry, astronomy, meteorology, geology, biology, physiology, medical science and other scientific fields.
