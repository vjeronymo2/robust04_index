930423 FT 23 APR 93 / UK Company News: Net assets jump at Govett Strategic Govett Strategic Investment Trust had a net asset value, after taking prior changes at par, of 270.8p per share at March 31, an advance of 23 per cent on the 219.3p at the same stage of 1991. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-March amounted to Pounds 3.54m, down from Pounds 4.21m last time. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.65p, payable from earnings of 3.59p (4.28p) per share.
