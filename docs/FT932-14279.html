930420 FT 20 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Timex strikers may be expelled from Union STRIKERS at the Timex company's plant in Dundee have been warned by their trade union that it may expel them if they break the law in the course of the dispute. In a letter sent to each of the 340 strikers Mr Gavin Laird, general secretary of the Amalgamated Engineering and Electrical Union, said: 'Failure to obey the instructions of the union's executive council will render you liable to expulsion from the union.' Today the Scottish TUC is debating the Timex strike , while tomorrow the AEEU's national committee will discuss the Timex dispute at its annual conference. Next week, representatives of both the company and the union will present their cases before the House of Commons employment committee.
