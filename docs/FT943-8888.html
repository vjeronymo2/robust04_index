940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Business Travel: Third time unlucky An Air India flight attendant has been sacked for giving birth to a third child in contravention of the national carrier's rules, the airline said yesterday. Audrey Coutinho, 34, said she would file a suit against the airline challenging the dismissal. A flight attendant who gives birth to a third child must resign under the airline's rules. This is thought to be the first time the regulation has been implemented.
