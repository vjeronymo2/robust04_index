Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "Hookup" program] [Text] Yesterday, Nintendo Corporation of America and some 60 video game cartridge companies formally called on U.S. Trade Representative Office to keep Taiwan included on the special 301 priority watchlist. (Vansaw), legal adviser to Nintendo, pointed out that as Taiwan is still allowing the export of fake Nintendo video game products all over the world, Taiwan still remains the world's major source of fake products. Yesterday, the Board of Foreign Trade under the Ministry of Economic Affairs lodged a strong protest over Nintendo Corporation of America's request of the U.S. Trade Representative Office to blacklist Taiwan on the special 301 priority watchlist and over the release of a newsletter in which Nintendo accused Taiwan's manufacturers of heavy involvement in the production of counterfeit products. (Cai Nian-sen), head of the Third Division of the Board of Foreign Trade, pointed out that as Nintendo Corporation of America's charge against Taiwan is baseless and as Nintendo showed no sincerity in cooperating with Taiwan's practicing an export inspection system involving Taiwan's computer products, it has to bear the responsibility for the serious counterfeiting of its products.
