BFN [Text] The renowned Jirki, Gevdan, Mamguran, Giravi, and Goran tribes in Sirnak and Hakkari Provinces have joined their forces against the Kurdish Workers Party [PKK]. The tribes, which have thousands of armed men, assembled in Beytussebap District to demonstrate against the PKK at 1030 yesterday. Some 5,000 demonstrators marched and chanted: "The banner will not be lowered; the cause cannot be abandoned; the Turkish, Kurdish, Laz [Black Sea people], and Circassian peoples are brothers; this country will be the PKK's grave; the PKK will drown in the blood it sheds."
