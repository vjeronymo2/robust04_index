Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine has approved a resolution on procedures for conducting state registration in the field of atmospheric air protection. The state's executive authorities, enterprises, departments, and organizations, regardless of their ownership status, are obliged to comply with the resolution. The document emphasizes that the main goals of state registration in the field of atmospheric air protection are collecting data on facilities that contribute or may contribute to atmospheric pollution, making assessments on the volume of harmful substances emitted into the atmosphere, and evaluating the influence of different physical and biological factors on air pollution.
