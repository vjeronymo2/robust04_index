BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Apr (MENA)--A hand grenade placed by unidentified people outside a shop on al-'Ishrin Street in the 'Ayn Shams neighborhood of Cairo went off this morning. According to initial reports, the explosion left two people slightly wounded. They were taken to the hospital for treatment. The explosion also caused damage to the front of the house and shop. Security commanders and various investigation organs proceeded to the site of the explosion.
