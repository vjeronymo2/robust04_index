930423 FT 23 APR 93 / International Company News: Cigarette price war hurts profits AMERICAN Brands, the Connecticut-based consumer products company with large tobacco interests, yesterday warned of lower first-half profits due to the cigarette price war begun this month by its rival Philip Morris, writes Nikki Tait in New York. First-quarter profits, before accounting charges, edged up to Dollars 247.1m, from Dollars 245.2m a year earlier, on revenues of Dollars 3.74bn down from Dollars 3.83bn.
