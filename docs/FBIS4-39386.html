BFN [Text] According to a poll conducted by Doctor of Sociological Sciences Kotlyarow, director of the Center for Political and Socioeconomic Analysis, 75 percent of Belarusian citizens consider price hikes the most important problem facing Belarus. More than half of society -- 54.3 percent -- consider the crime rate the most important problem. As RID has learned, corruption within state authorities comes in third. It worries 38.8 percent of Belarusian citizens.
