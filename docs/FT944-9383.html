941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / Fall in water disconnections Fewer households are being disconnected for failing to pay their water bills, Ofwat, the industry's watchdog, said yesterday. There were 5,532 domestic disconnections between April and September - a 7 per cent drop on the 5,924 in the same period last year. The number of non-domestic disconnections fell by 3 per cent to 1,437.
