BFN [Text] 'Abd-al-Rahman Sulayman was killed at his home in al- 'Amiriyah, south of Tyre, at about 0215 today. Unidentified people planted an explosive device near his house. The explosion killed Sulayman immediately and wounded his brother. The Amal Movement has issued a statement on Sulayman's murder, pledging to press ahead on the path of resistance until the land is liberated.
