930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / International Company News: Keppel lifts pre-tax profits 11% midway KEPPEL, the diversified conglomerate controlled by the Singapore government, lifted pre-tax profits to SDollars 145.8m (USDollars 91m) for the six months to June 30 1993. The result was an an 11 per cent rise compared with the year-ago figure. Turnover was down 2.4 per cent to SDollars 728.7m from SDollars 746.6m. Keppel's core shipbuilding and repair business in Singapore suffered from a generally weak global market. Improved revenues have come from non-shipping activities and the group's rapidly-expanding overseas interests. Offshore ship and rig building activities improved.
