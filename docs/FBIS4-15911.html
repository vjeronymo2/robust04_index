BFN [Text] Kiev, 12 April (AFP) -- Ukraine has placed the Black Sea Fleet based at Odessa under the authority of the Ukrainian Army, Deputy Defence Minister Ivan Bijan was quoted by the INTERFAX-UKRAINE news agency as saying on Tuesday [12 April]. Talks to end a row between Russia and Ukraine over the fate of the fleet began earlier Tuesday in Kiev between a Russian delegation headed by Admiral Felix Gromiv and Ukrainian Armed Forces chief of staff General Anatoly Lopata.
