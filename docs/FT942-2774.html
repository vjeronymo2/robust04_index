940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Thomas Locker A Pounds 2.6m exceptional charge this time left Thomas Locker (Holdings), the general engineering group, with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 3.43m for the year ended March 31. This compared with a Pounds 421,000 profit previously. The exceptional comprised a Pounds 2.6m provision for losses on disposal of Associated Perforators and Weavers and APW Two, and a Pounds 596,000 full provision against the investment in and amounts due from Locker Asia and also for a guarantee in place to a Thailand Bank. Turnover came to Pounds 34.42m (Pounds 37.62m) and losses per share were 8.89p (0.42p earnings). A recommended final dividend of 0.2p (0.65p) makes a total for the year of 0.4p (1p).
