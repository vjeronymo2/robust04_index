940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Cullen's swings into deficit of Pounds 674,000 Cullen's Holdings, the grocery retailer, suffered pre-tax losses of Pounds 674,000 for the year to February 27 compared with profits of Pounds 401,000. The losses were mainly associated with the operations of the Reds Chicken and Ribs division, Mr Robert Rayne, chairman, said, while the Neighbourhood Food Stores and Patisserie divisions had performed satisfactorily. Total turnover improved to Pounds 29.7m (Pounds 27.4m) with some Pounds 24.4m (Pounds 23m) coming from the Cullen's estate. Mr Rayne added that in the current year sales of the Neighbourhood Food Stores were 5.6 per cent up on a like-for-like basis and profits were in line with budget. Losses per share amounted to 2.8p (0.8p earnings) and there is no dividend (0.5p).
