BFN [By ITAR-TASS freelance corrrespondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 28 Apr -- Today at 2014 the Russian military space forces launched a space vehicle, Cosmos-2280, from launch pad number 31 at Baykonur space center. The satellite was launched using a three-stage Soyuz-V launching vehicle. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned from the military space forces press center that the launch, which was carried out in the interests of the Russian Defense Ministry, took place successfully.
