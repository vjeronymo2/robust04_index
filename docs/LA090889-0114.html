September 8, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

ZIONIST GROUP HAS SPEAKERS AVAILABLE

NA'AMAT USA now has available a speakers' bureau to address such issues as women's and children's rights in the United States and Israel.

The women's Zionist group funds services such as day care and adolescent counseling in Israel and, at home, advocates legislation including reproductive choice and civil rights.

Speakers' bureau information: (213) 876-1705.
