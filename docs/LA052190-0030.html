May 21, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

World Savings & Loan, Oakland, said it acquired the $725 million of deposits of American Savings of Colorado Springs from the Resolution Trust Corp. in a supervisory transaction . . . Hewlett-Packard won a license to export drug-testing equipment to Cuba for three months coinciding with next year's Pan-American Games . . . Todd Shipyards Corp. has approved a plan to close its Galveston, Tex., operation, a move that would leave the once-large company with just its Seattle shipyard.
