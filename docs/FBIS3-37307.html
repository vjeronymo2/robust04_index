Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (February 15) TURAN--Yesterday Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandr Chikvaidze and accompanied him persons arrived in Baku. The meeting of Aleksandr Chikvaidze and Azerbaijani Foreign Minister, Gasan Gasanov is taking place at present. President's Staff of the Republic reports that today President Aliyev is due to receive Georgian Foreign Minister. This evening Chikvaidze will leave for Iran. According to Georgian sources, Chikvaidze will discuss the matters concerning delivery of Iranian gas to Georgia through the territory of Azerbaijan. Some Western sources report that both parties will discuss military-political situation connected with return of Russian troops in Georgia.
