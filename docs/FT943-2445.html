940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Taipei Foreign institutional investors remitted about Dollars 44m into Taiwan for stock investment in the week ending last Wednesday, AP-DJ reports. The Securities and Exchange Commission said that with the latest inflow of foreign capital, foreign investors had a total of Dollars 4.64bn in the country since Taiwan opened its stock market to foreign institutions for direct investment in early 1991. This compared with the ceiling of Dollars 7.5bn set by the government.
