921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Observer: Outgoing Incoming Philips finance director Dudley Eustace told the Reuters newswire yesterday that he wants to improve the company's communications with the outside world. After its clumsy handling of its profits-warning in June, Philips needs to improve relations with the investment community. But Eustace did not exactly prove a dab hand in that respect while at British Aerospace's financial controls. The market was certainly ill-prepared for the appalling background to BAe's rights issue flop last year.
