930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Policy Port joins market in placing Policy Portfolio, a market-maker in second-hand endowment policies, is joining the main market via a placing of 3.6m shares at 130p per share. The company will have a market capitalisation of Pounds 10.4m at the placing price. Exactly 2.5m new shares are being issued, raising Pounds 2.9m for the company after expenses, with 1.1m shares being sold by existing shareholders. Following the placing, the Rubin family and associates will control 54 per cent and have undertaken not to sell further shares until January 1 1995. Pre-tax profits have increased from Pounds 245,000 in the year ended March 30 1991, to Pounds 817,000 in the year to end March 1993. At the placing price, the shares are on a p/e ratio of 13.3. The directors expect to recommend a dividend of 4.5p in the year to end March 1994, putting the shares on a notional gross dividend yield of 5.8 per cent.
