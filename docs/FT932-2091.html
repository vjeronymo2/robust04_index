930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / New building for Lloyd's Register LLOYD'S REGISTER, the ship classification society, has applied for planning permission for a 35,000 sq m development in Liphook, Hampshire, which will be designed by the Richard Rogers Partnership. The new building will house 1,350 staff from Lloyd's offices in the City's Fenchurch Street and Croydon, Surrey. The company will retain its listed building at 71 Fenchurch Street.
