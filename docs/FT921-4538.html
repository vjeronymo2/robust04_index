920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / Price cuts 'fail clothes shops' THE recent unprecedented level of price-cutting among mass clothing retailers has largely failed to stimulate enough additional sales to prevent a severe erosion of margins, according to a report from Verdict, the retail consultants. Verdict estimates that between November and January clothing retailers on average sacrificed 20 per cent of their gross margin by marking down prices. Clothing retailers typically achieve gross margins of 50 per cent.
