BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali al-Bujayri, our country's ambassador in Moscow, has met with (Joseph Vladimir), first deputy chief of the Near Eastern and North African Countries Administration at the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. They discussed developments in our country, and the issues that are of interest to the friendly bilateral ties. During the meeting, the Russian official reiterated his country's position, which supports Yemeni unity, democracy, and dialogue among brothers in unified Yemen. He also spoke of his country's support for UN Security Council Resolution 924 on the situation in Yemen.
