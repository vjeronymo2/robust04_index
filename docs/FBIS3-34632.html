Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Hizballah Secretary General Shaykh Hasan Nasrallah has called for the destruction of Palestinian leader Yasir 'Arafat and the Palestinian-Israeli agreement, which he described as an accord of humiliation. Speaking during a Hizballah rally held on the second anniversary of the assassination of former Hizballah leader Shaykh 'Abbas al-Musawi, Nasrallah said that armed struggle is the sole way to achieve real peace. He pledged to continue attacks on the Israeli forces' positions in Lebanon.
