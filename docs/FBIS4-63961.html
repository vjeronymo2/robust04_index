BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, 25 May -- The all-hands assembly of fleet commanders in Sevastopol, which, according to some Ukrainian mass media, provoked a negative response in Kiev, is not linked to the recent events in Crimea. According to the press center of the Black Sea Fleet, the commanders of formations, groupings, and units gathered today at the headquarters of the Black Sea Fleet to sum up the results of winter training. It was noted at a sitting of the military council that the fleet had successfully carried out its tasks in the past six months and was prepared to implement the programs of summer training. The sitting of the military council devoted particular attention to the problem of injuries of servicemen during military training and operating the equipment.
