October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LA HABRA;

10 LA HABRA TEACHERS TO GET MENTOR PAY

Ten La Habra City School District teachers will receive an additional $4,000 apiece next year for serving as mentor teachers under a state-funded program.

The school board voted to continue participation in the program, in which a committee selects veteran teachers to serve as mentors, training other teachers.

The mentors also are expected to work two weeks during the summer on special projects.
