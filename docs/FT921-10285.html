920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / World Trade News: US cigarette plant for Poland RJR Reynolds Tobacco International, part of the RJR Nabisco food and tobacco group, has announced plans to build a cigarette factory in Poland, writes Nikki Tait in New York. The plant, expected to cost around Dollars 33m (Pounds 18.2m), will cover 132,000 sq ft and should see production begin in mid-1993. It will initially produce RJR's 'Camel' cigarette brand, but eventually extend to other brands. The eventual plant capacity is put at 8bn cigarettes.
