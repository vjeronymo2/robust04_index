940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Middlesex well ahead of forecast Shares in Middlesex Holdings edged ahead 1/4 p to 4p after Mr Phil Edmonds, the chairman, said that the Dollars 1m (Pounds 600,000) gross trading profits warranted to be provided by Mr Masoud Alikhani, the chief executive within a 12 month period, had been exceeded well ahead of forecast. Mr Edmonds, the former Middlesex and England cricketer, said the company, which is engaged in metals and oil trading, continued to earn profits at a level the directors regarded as 'very satisfactory'.
