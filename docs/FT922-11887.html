920423 FT 23 APR 92 / International Company News: Union Pacific improves 9% UNION PACIFIC, one of the largest US transportation companies, yesterday reported a 9 per cent improvement in first-quarter profits, at Dollars 145m after tax. The core railroad operations saw earnings advance by 6 per cent to Dollars 136m, while operating profits were up by 11 per cent. The company said lower fuel prices, productivity gains and stronger traffic volumes contributed to the improvement. Union Pacific's operating revenues overall were unchanged at Dollars 1.74bn. Earnings per share were 71 cents, against 66 cents a year ago.
