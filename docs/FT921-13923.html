920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / Finance and the Family: Fillip for smaller companies Small company shares showed promising signs of recovery this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 0.9 per cent to 1183.14 over the seven days to January 16, while the County Small Companies Index increased 0.7 per cent to 946.72 over the same period.
