BFN [Text] Chief of Staff Ehud Baraq says the PLO and Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] represent two aspects of a national movement possessing rather similar long-term aims vis-a-vis Israel. Lieutenant General Baraq added, however, that the two organizations have different methods for arriving at these goals. Our correspondent Yo'el Nir reports Lt. Gen. Baraq today briefed the Knesset Foreign Affairs and Defense Committee about reports on agreements between the PLO and Hamas. Baraq said that he will not be surprised if Hamas people join social networks in Gaza and Jericho, such as schools and welfare services. The chief of staff also said he will not be surprised if one faction of Hamas will pursue the path of terrorism. The chief of staff believes that the PLO will on the whole be interested in proving that terrorism is on the wane in the areas under their jurisdiction.
