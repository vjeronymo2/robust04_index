Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani this evening received the Saudi Arabian education minister, Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Khuwaytir, who is carrying a written message from King Fahd for him. In the meeting, the president referred to the need for the further strengthening of unity and cooperation among Islamic countries to help resolve problems of the world of Islam and said: Regional problems and falling oil price throughout world are among the most important issues that require cooperation and exchange of views among Islamic countries, particularly Iran and Saudi Arabia. Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani pointed out the need for the exchange of political and economic delegations between the two countries to bring the views of Iran and Saudi Arabia closer together. He welcomed the deepening of bilateral relations in the issues of mutual interest. He said: I will, very soon, send a reply to King Fahd's message via a special envoy for holding discussions on the issues of interest to the two countries. During the meeting, the Saudi Arabian education minister submitted the written message of King Fahd and expressed the hope for the expansion of the two countries' relations and cooperation in the future.
