941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / Observer: Bow tight Once upon a time, possession of a dinner jacket meant certain death in Russia, and the demand for 'smokings' has never really recovered. So the invitation to political bigwigs to join the Queen at a reception at the British Embassy on Monday caused a spot of embarrassment, seeing that the dress code specified dinner jackets. Hasty calls to the foreign ministry which keeps a stock of the things for its diplomats' use. These proving insufficent in number however, a harrassed official was forced to sound out the Moscow Light Opera Theatre, whose performances regularly feature actors kitted out as waiters. Its collection proved numerically suitable - only problem being that the apparel is cut for svelte actors, not for the ample girths and spreading derrieres of some of the top political class.
