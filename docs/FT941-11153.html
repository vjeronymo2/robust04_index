940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / Markets: Latest house prices figures highlight market fragility - At a glance The fragility of the housing market was emphasised this week by Halifax, the largest lender, which found that house prices fell 0.4 per cent last month compared with December, although they are up in the year to January. However, Nationwide, the second largest building society, produced figures showing that prices rose on average by 0.4 per cent in January. The standardised average house price has risen from Pounds 60,471 in January 1993 to Pounds 61,133 last month, according to Halifax. A better model, Page V.
