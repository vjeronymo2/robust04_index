April 30, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

DAMAGE IN GARAGE FIRE SET AT $60,000

A garage fire caused an estimated $60,000 in damage in Simi Valley, authorities said.

The fire started in the garage at 2772 Harrington Road at 7:10 p.m. Saturday and was under control at 7:39, said Vicki Crabtree, a Ventura County Fire Department dispatcher.

Cause of the fire is being investigated, Crabtree said. Damage was estimated at $30,000 to the structure and $30,000 to its contents, including a Volkswagen van and a tractor, she said.

No injuries were reported.
