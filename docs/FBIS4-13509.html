BFN [Text] Agreements were reached in Cairo this morning on water, finance, electricity, employment, and aviation. The drafting committee if now working on an agreement on civilian issues on which deliberations have been concluded. Muhammad Dahlan, a member of the Palestinian delegation in the committee discussing the release of prisoners, told our Cairo correspondent Moti Amir that these negotiations continue and that two committees were set up, one dealing with the timetable for freeing 5,000 Palestinians soon, and the other, with the release of the remaining Palestinians after the agreement is signed.
