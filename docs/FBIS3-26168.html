Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On the morning of 1 February at the theater of the provincial exhibition center, entrusted by the provincial party committee and the provincial government, the organizational department and the veteran cadres bureau under the provincial party committee and the provincial personnel affairs department held a soiree attended by retired cadres of the provincial-level organs to welcome the arrival of the Spring Festival. Provincial Leaders Sun Weiben, Zhou Wenhua, Ma Guoliang, Wang Xianmin, Qi Guiyuan, and Sun Kuiwen happily gathered together with veteran comrades to extend regards and greetings to one another and to cheerfully welcome the arrival of the Spring Festival. Ma Guoliang, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, made an ebullient speech at the soiree. He said: Last year in 1993, the province ensured a stable and coordinate development of the economy and social undertakings. These achievements were brought about from the concerted efforts of the people of various nationalities in the province and were also cemented with the vast number of retired cadres' sweat and blood. Many veteran comrades have experienced the great causes of reform, opening up, and four modernizations; positively suggested ways and means for reform and economic construction; continued to maintain and carry forward the fine traditions and work style of the party; constantly worked to serve the society, the masses, and the grass roots; and made great contributions to building the spiritual and material civilizations of the province. On behalf of the provincial party committee and the provincial government, he sincerely expressed thanks to the retired comrades in the province. [passage indistinct] Wang Xianmin, Standing Committee member and secretary general of the provincial party committee; and Zhao Dezun, Chen Lei, Li Jianbai, Wang Zhao, and Chen Jianfei, veteran comrades retired from the provincial-level organs, were present at the soiree. The provincial sports team performed brilliant programs at the soiree.
