Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 12 (SNARK) -- Today the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan has returned to Yerevan from London. During the four-day official visit of the Armenian delegation to Great Britain the agreements of friendship and collaboration, of cooperation in the field of air communications, science, education and culture were signed. They also achieved the agreement with the European Bank of Reconstruction and Development about assistance in the restoration the work of the Armenian Nuclear Power Station and reconstruction of Yerevan airport "Zvartnots."
