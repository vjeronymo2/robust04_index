FEDERAL RETIREMENT THRIFT INVESTMENT BOARD Employee Thrift Advisory Council; Open Meeting In accordance with section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463), a notice is hereby given of the following committee meeting: Name: Employee Thrift Advisory Council. Time: 10 a.m. Date: June 14, 1994.
