BFN [Text] President Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO's Executive Committee, this afternoon received Russian envoy Sergey Virshinin, who delivered an urgent message from the Russian leadership in reply to the letter President 'Arafat sent to President Boris Yeltsin on recent developments and the contacts under way to ensure an accurate implementation of the agreements in connection with the peace process.
