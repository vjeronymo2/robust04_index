Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Niculae Spiroiu, Romanian national defense minister, in a statement to our reporters today, has denied the report published by the AR-PRESS news agency according to which General Paul Cheler had been replaced as commander of the 4th [Transylvanian] Army. Spiroiu said that General Cheler is still commander of that army and there are no problems whatsoever. This report is false, the defense minister asserted, after which he assessed that such misleading information is also intended to create an unfavorable atmosphere around the leadership of the ministry.
