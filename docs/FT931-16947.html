930104 FT 04 JAN 93 / Letter: Boardroom fun Sir, The contributors to 'Boardroom bestsellers' (December 30) missed a good book in Nicholas von Hoffman's Capitalist Fools: Tales of American Business, from Carnegie to Forbes to the Milken Gang (Doubleday, 1992). Among other things, the book is full of wonderful anecdotes, such as Mellon exclaiming something like 'you just can't run a coal mine without machine guns'. Or, it was said that J P Morgan collected old masters and old mistresses. Business people can be interesting] Linda Hesselman, Unilever, Blackfriars, London EC4P 4BQ
