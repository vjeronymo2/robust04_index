BFN [Text] Beijing, May 28 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren met with foreign participants of the symposium on China's economic reforms at the Great Hall of the People here this morning. The symposium opened here yesterday. Its sponsors included the International Academy of Management (IAM), China International Economic Consultants, Inc. (CIEC), and the China Europe Management Institute. Rong said that he was pleased with the symposium and expressed congratulations to its success. Briefing guests on China's economic growth, Rong said that in building a socialist market economy, China attaches much importance to the absorbing of foreign management expertise. He asked foreign management experts to give their valuable advice as often as possible. IAM President Pedro Nueno said that the symposium helped him and his colleagues acquire more knowledge about China's economic growth. China promises a very bright future, he said. Founded in 1985, IAM boasts of over 200 members from 34 countries. CIEC President Jing Shuping took part in the meeting.
