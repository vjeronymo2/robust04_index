L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting. Name and Committee Code: Special Emphasis Panel in Chemistry ( # 1191). Date and Time: October 27&hyph;28, 1994. Place: Room 1060, NSF, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, Virginia. Type of Meeting: Closed. Contact Person: Drs. Karolyn Eisenstein and George M.
