Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Valeriy Kovalev under "In Brief" rubric: "Belorusian `Slavyanka' Goes on Air"] [Text] Minsk -- A television and radio broadcasting studio has been set up under the Ministry of Defense of Belorusia [Belarus]. It is staffed by 28 creative and technical workers who will prepare television and radio broadcasts on military subjects. The Defense Ministry hopes that the military television and radio studio will make it possible to provide more prompt and, most importantly, more objective coverage of the life, combat training, and service of servicemen and will help to enhance their authority in the eyes of their fellow citizens. Topics prepared by military television journalists are already used in national television news programs.
