May 16, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

STATE OFFICIALS CONTINUE AERIAL SPRAYING AGAINST PESKY MEDFLY

Officials of Los Angeles are constantly telling us to conserve water and cut down on usage. Seems to me they have overlooked the quickest solution: Stop the malathion spraying so we don't have tens of thousands of people all over the Los Angeles Basin forced into washing their cars to avoid paint damage. The officials can't have it both ways, folks.

PAT MASON

Covina
