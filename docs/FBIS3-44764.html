Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporters Liu Siyang (0491 1835 2254) and Zhang Yinshu (1728 6892 2562)] [Text] Beijing, 30 Dec (XINHUA) -- The Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee, which ended yesterday, has adopted a "decision of the NPC Standing Committee on amendment of 'Accounting Law of the People's Republic of China.'" According to the decision, the Accounting Law which has been implemented for eight years will be partially amended. President Jiang Zemin yesterday signed presidential decree No. 17 promulgating the "decision." According to it, the law will contain 30 articles in six chapters after the amendment. Some experts concerned have pointed out that the amendment of the Accounting Law will play an important role in perfecting the accounting system, maintaining market economic order, and improving China's investment environment.
