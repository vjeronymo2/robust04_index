BFN [Text] Hamburg (DPA) -- Violent riots took place during a demonstration against Serbian attacks on the Muslim enclave of Gorazde in eastern Bosnia on Thursday evening [21 April] in Hamburg. Five policemen were injured by thrown stones. According to the police, about 500 people participated in a demonstration under the slogan "Gorazde Must Live" in front of the FRY Consulate General. Police had cordoned off the building. When stones were thrown from the crowd, police moved forward and arrested four persons.
