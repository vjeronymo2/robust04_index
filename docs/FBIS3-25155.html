Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] has acknowledged that certain teething problems are being experienced with the National Peacekeeping Force. Following media criticism of the force, the TEC Subcouncil on Defense said it had held a meeting today with the commander of the force, Major General Gabriel Ramushwana, and the acting commander, Colonel Fred Burger. The subcouncil and the force's command council would visit the training base at De Brug near Bloemfontein, and everything was being done to ensure that the peacekeeping force was a success. The subcouncil said a media day would be held at the base on Thursday [10 February].
