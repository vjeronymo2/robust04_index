BFN [Excerpts] In Vilnius today Wieslaw Walendziak, the president of Polish Television, and Laimonas Tapinas, the director general of Lithuanian Radio and Television, extended for one year the agreement on cooperation between the two television networks. This agreement was signed two years ago. [Correspondent Aleksandra Akinszon] [passage omitted] After the agreement was signed, Lithuanian journalists returned in their questions to the incident of a week ago, when the Lithuanian side refused to sign an agreement on the retransmission of Polonia TV in the Vilnius region. President Wieslaw Walendziak stressed that it had indeed been a regrettable incident, but it had been resolved successfully, and he proposed that these matters now be left alone. This is Aleksandra Akinszon reporting from Vilnius.
