March 1, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

CHARGERS SIGN RODENHAUSER

Mark Rodenhauser, a punt snapper for the Minnesota Vikings last season, was signed today by the San Diego Chargers as a Plan B free agent.

Rodenhauser appeared in 16 games last year, registering five tackles and four assists. He joined Minnesota as a free agent after one season with the Chicago Bears, who waived him before the 1988 season. In 1987, he played in 10 games for the Bears, including starts in all three strike games.
