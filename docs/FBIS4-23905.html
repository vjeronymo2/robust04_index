BFN [Text] Justice Minister Dullah Omar says plans for a Truth and Reconciliation Commission will go ahead despite opposition expressed by the commissioner of police. Police Commissioner General Johan van der Merwe has submitted a document to the safety and security minister in which he warns that the commission could bring about the downfall of top ANC members. Johannesburg's BUSINESS DAY newspaper quoted Mr. Omar as saying that the government was not worried about this possibility and that it was no reason to suppress the truth. He said the ANC accepted that its members might have to appear before the commission.
