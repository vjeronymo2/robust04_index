930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Estates & General loses Pounds 25m ESTATES and General, the property group, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 25.4m in the year to end-December, after making provisions totalling Pounds 20.6m against trading stock, joint venture schemes and the Castle Mall at Norwich. That compared with a profit of Pounds 2.3m for the previous 12 months. No dividend is declared; last year saw a 3.75p total with a final of 2.525p. The net asset value of the company had been reduced by Pounds 42.9m to Pounds 17.3m.
