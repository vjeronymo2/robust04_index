BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sergey Borovkov, Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Petropavlovsk-Kamchatskiy June 26 TASS -- Head of the State Duma Ivan Rybkin, who is leading a delegation of the lower chamber of the Federal Assembly now touring Siberia and the Far East, had a meeting here today with representatives of the command of the Russian Pacific Fleet. They discussed at the meeting problems of financing and supply of the fleet, Aleksandr Piskunov, deputy chairman of the State Duma committee on defence, told ITAR-TASS. The conversation also touched upon the problem of the utilisation of the used nuclear fuel from reactors of nuclear-powered submarines and ammunition. Rybkin told naval officers about the efforts taken by the State Duma for finding additional sources of financing for the Armed Forces. They discussed tasks facing the Russian Navy in connection with the bringing of the strength of the Armed Forces in conformity with the mobilising and financial resources of the country.
