BFN [Text] Gyula Horn, chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Party [MSZP], replied with a warm message to the congratulatory letter sent to him by Bela Marko, executive chairman of the Democratic Union of Hungarians in Romania [RMDSZ -- UDMR in Romanian], MAGYAR HIRLAP was informed by the office of the RMDSZ chairman. Expressing thanks for the good wishes expressed on behalf of the RMDSZ, Horn pointed out in his letter that his party has always followed with great attention and sympathy the "heroic struggle of the RMDSZ for the survival of the ethnic Hungarians living in Romania and the full assertion of their individual and community rights," and that the MSZP has endeavored to support all this with their available means. Gyula Horn assured Bela Marko and the RMDSZ of the fact that "supporting the vital autonomy efforts of the Hungarians living beyond the borders" is one of the outstanding goals of the Socialists' government program. The MSZP is determined to do everything in its power to successfully assert this in the increased government responsibility of the party. Gyula Horn expressed his hope that he would be soon able to discuss the issue of "effective RMDSZ-MSZP cooperation" personally with Bela Marko.
