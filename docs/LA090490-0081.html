September 4, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN CLEMENTE;

GENERAL PLAN PANEL TO BE APPOINTED

About 35 people have shown an interest in helping to revise the city's General Plan.

The residents have taken out applications forms to join a 25-member citizens advisory committee that will review issues ranging from disaster preparedness to zoning laws.

The deadline for submitting an application to City Hall is Wednesday at 5 p.m.
