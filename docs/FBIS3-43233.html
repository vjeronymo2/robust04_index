Language: Somali Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the Somali National Alliance [SNA], who is also the chairman of the United Somali Congress, Mr. Mohamed Farah Aidid, has today sent his best wishes and a congratulatory New Year 1994 message to the Somali people in general, and in particular the supporters of the SNA. The chairman said in his congratulatory message: We have passed through many difficulties and beseech God the almighty to help the Somali people out of the current problems and wish the Somali people victory, unity, and progress and that they reach next year [1995] having made progress and in prosperity, God willing. The chairman of the SNA, Mr. Mohamed Farah Aidid, sent a special message to SNA supporters, congratulating them on the way they respected the cease-fire and the general peace.
