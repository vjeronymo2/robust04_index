June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BOXED-IN

Re: Claire Simpson's letter in Calendar (June 3) regarding the automatic renewal of Hollywood Bowl boxes in which she argued that this practice should be abandoned and boxes assigned by lottery:

This is no different from season tickets at any theater.

(I'd like to sit in the first five rows of the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion, Ahmanson, Pantages, etc., too.)

For 10 years we enjoyed the Playboy Jazz Festival and looked forward each year to our box for this concert. Then someone got the brainy idea to offer the boxes in a lottery. Since then, we have not attended a Playboy concert.

S. NORMAN

Encino
