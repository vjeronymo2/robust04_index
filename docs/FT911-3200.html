910425 FT 25 APR 91 / International Company News: Slump at Eaton Eaton Corporation, the Cleveland-based engineering group, yesterday reported a net loss of Dollars 12m for the first three months of 1991, down from a Dollars 54m profit in the period a year earlier, writes Nikki Tait. First-quarter sales slumped to Dollars 818m from Dollars 942m. There was a Dollars 39m restructuring charge; without this, Eaton would have made a Dollars 13m profit.
