BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 15 TASS--A decree of Russian President Boris Yeltsin on construction of the Ingush capital was signed here today. Follows the text of the decree: "On Construction of the Capital of the Republic of Ingushetia. "In order to assist the Ingush people in construction of the capital of the Ingush republic as a major attribute of its forming statehood and in accordance with the Russian Constitution and the Russian law `On Formation of the Ingush Republic Within the Russian Federation' I resolute: "1. To support the initiative of republics, territories and regions of the Russian Federation on joint construction of the capital of the Ingush republic. "2. To instruct the Russian Government to elaborate no later than June 1994 a set of arrangements to design and construct the capital of the Ingush Republic. "3. This decree shall enter into force since the moment of its signing".
