930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables French cherries are this week's best fruit buy at 25-30p a 1/4 lb, along with Spania strawberries at 45-55p per 8oz punnet (50-60p) reports the FFVIB. Packham pears at 40-55p a lb (45-60p), pineapples at 90p-Pounds 1.30 each (90p-Pounds 1.30) and Galia melons at 90-Pounds 1.25 each (90p-Pounds 1.50) round off this week's best fruit buys. New season summer cabbage at 30-35p a lb or 40-44p each is an excellent vegetable choice this week. Among the best salad buys are cucumber at 50-70p each (50-70p), round lettuce at 25-30p each (25-35p), English tomatoes at 55-65p a lb (60-65p) and Spring onions at 35-40p a bunch (35-40p).
