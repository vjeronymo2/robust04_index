Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Dr. Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih received in succession this morning at his office at the ministry Arab and African ambassadors accredited to Khartoum. The meeting discussed numerous Arab and African issues of mutual interest. Dr. Abu-Salih gave the ambassadors a detailed explanation on the background of the recent developments of Sudanese-British relations in addition to the reasons that resulted in expelling the British ambassador from Khartoum. Abu-Salih revealed the improper conduct of the British ambassador, which obstructed the development of relations between the two countries. He pointed out that the decision to expel the ambassador was aimed at putting relations between the two countries in their correct framework.
