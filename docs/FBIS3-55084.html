Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 5 (AZERTAC)--The Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev has accepted an invitation sent by Great Britain Prime-Minister, Mr. John Major for an official visit to this European country, according to the press-service of the Azerbaijani leader. The trip is likely to take place by the end of January. It is supposed that the Azerbaijani Embassy in London will be opened at the same time. According to the Foreign Ministry of Azerbaijan, Mr. Geydar Aliyev in his turn has invited the head of the British Government to pay a visit to the Republic at any convenient time. Officials in Baku stress the great importance of the development and intensification of relations with Great Britain, which was among the first states that established diplomatic relations with independent Azerbaijan. The two countries have already signed a protocol on economic cooperation and ratified documents on cooperation on Foreign Ministry level, as well as a memorandum on unlimited freedom of movement for British citizens in Azerbaijan. At present, Great Britain has taken a leading position in the external economic relations of Azerbaijan coming third after Iran and Turkey. A number of leading British companies such as British Petroleum, JPT communication, Worldwide, Kaizer Engineering have already installed themselves. The first of these is going to take part in joint exploration and exploitation of off-shore oil fields and according to experts, has got excellent opportunities.
