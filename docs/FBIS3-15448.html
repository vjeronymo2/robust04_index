Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] After two days of fierce fighting, Armenian forces in Nagorno-Karabakh have beaten off the Azeri advance on Fizuli dealing tangible losses on the adversary, a military spokesman for Karabakh defence forces told Interfax. The situation has somewhat evened on Sunday morning. There were positional battles there throughout Saturday with no substantial gains made by either side. At the same time, the spokesman denied the Azeri defence ministry's report saying that up to 100 Armenian soldiers were killed following an abortive Armenian advance. Three Armenian tanks were also destroyed. Reports from Stepanakert say that 120 Azeri soldiers have been killed in two days of fighting. Two Azeri tanks and one APC were hit. Nine Armenian soldiers were also killed and 35 others injured.
