BFN [Excerpt] Cajnice, 5 Apr (TANJUG) -- The army of the Srpska republic today halted the eight-day offensive by Muslim forces toward Cajnice, Foca, and Visegrad, and undertook a counteroffensive, the headquarters of the Herzegovina Corps announced this evening. The same source says Muslim soldiers are withdrawing toward the Drina, where strong police forces, which are not allowing them to cross over to the left bank of this river, are waiting for them on all bridges. The defeat of the Muslim forces was also admitted today by Ferid Buljubasic, commander of the eastern Bosnia corps, and he sought help from the international community. At 0900 this morning, the headquarters of the Serb forces says, two NATO aircraft violated the air space of the Srpska republic throughout the whole area of the upper Drina valley at the impermissible altitude of 600 meters. If this happens again, the headquarters of the Serb army says, such aircraft will be shot down. [passage omitted]
