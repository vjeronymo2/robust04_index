930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / International Company News: Speer resigns as HSN chief MR ROY SPEER, founder of the US Home Shopping Network and head of the company since its inception in the 1980s, has resigned as chairman and as a director, writes Nikki Tait. HSN, one of the two big US TV shopping networks, last month received a merger approach from its rival, QVC. HSN and Mr Speer have been subject to scrutiny by regulators, centring on alleged questionable business practices. Last month, when HSN shook up its board, it said Mr Speer had not been actively involved in management since mid-February.
