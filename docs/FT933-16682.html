930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / People: Bullock goes Nuclear John Bullock, joint senior partner of Cooper and Lybrand UK until his retirement last year, has become a non-executive director of Nuclear Electric. Bullock is currently a non-executive director of Kingfisher and is on the board of the UK Atomic Energy Authority. He fills the vacancy left by Fred Bonner, who retired from Nuclear Electric at the end of March.
