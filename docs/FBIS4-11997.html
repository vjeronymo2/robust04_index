BFN [Text] The security servives in the province of Tamanrasset today announced that they have arrested more than 300 people of foreign nationality during the combing operations which the security forces recently carried out in a number of quarters of the capital of Hoggar. The security services added that those persons were residing illegally in Algeria. The services pointed out that most of those arrested are perverts who carry out unlawful practices, including trading in drugs and armed robbery, as well as money dealings, as they have not presented any identity documents. During this day-long combing operation, in which joint forces of the army, police, gendarmerie, and customs participated, more than 1100 tins of Lahda [powdered] milk and baby milk, and more than 2000 packs of cigarettes - all of which were going to be smuggled to neighboring states - were seized.
