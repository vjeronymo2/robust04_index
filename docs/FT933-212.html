930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / National lottery adviser appointed MR PETER DAVIS is to be the regulatory adviser to the National Lottery, Mr Peter Brooke, national heritage secretary, announced yesterday. Mr Davis, a former executive deputy chairman of Harris Queensway, the furniture and carpet group, and recently deputy chairman and finance director of Sturge Holdings, the Lloyd's underwriters, is expected to become director-general of the lottery.
