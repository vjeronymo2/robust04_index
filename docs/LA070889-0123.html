July 8, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DANA HILLS EDGES WESTERN IN BASKETBALL

Dana Hills High School defeated Western, 49-46, in a Capistrano Beach basketball tournament game Friday. Keith Barnett's three-pointer gave Dana Hills the lead and J.B. Taylor added two free throws for the final margin.

Other second-round scores: Anaheim defeated Don Lugo, 60-38; Antioch defeated San Clemente, 59-49; Capistrano Valley defeated Tracy, 69-40; Irvine beat Savanna, 47-41; Clovis West defeated Mater Dei, 39-37; St. Francis defeated Swink, 67-50.

In third-round games, Antioch defeated Anaheim, 71-53, and San Clemente defeated Don Lugo, 69-47.
