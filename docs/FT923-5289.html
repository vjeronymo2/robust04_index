920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Accountancy firms are disciplined THREE FIRMS of chartered accountants have been disciplined in the latest rulings from the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales for breaches of investment business regulations. Winfield Hart of Shipley, West Yorkshire, was reprimanded, fined Pounds 3,000 and ordered to pay Pounds 500 costs for a series of transgressions including failing to reasonably establish whether a client could have obtained more suitable policies. Entwistle of Stockbridge, Hampshire, was reprimanded and charged Pounds 1,000 towards costs for failing to pay its authorisation fee and for not co-operating with the investigation committee. CA Rackstraw of High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, was reprimanded, fined Pounds 1,000 and ordered to pay Pounds 250 costs for breaches including failing to notify clients in writing of commissions it received.
