BFN [Excerpts] An agreement on the government coalition has been drawn up. [passage omitted] The agreement will only become final if the Hungarian Socialist Party [MSZP] congress and the Alliance of Free Democrats [SZDSZ] convention adopt it on Sunday. [MSZP Chairman] Gyula Horn spoke highly of the agreement: [Begin Horn recording] The MSZP and the SZDSZ are committed to an effective market economy supported by social guarantees. They take a stand for the guaranteeing of human and political rights, for parliamentary democracy and constitutional statehood. The joint goals of the MSZP and the SZDSZ are the guaranteeing of the independence and security of the Hungarian Republic and our country's joining the democratic community of the European peoples, in cooperation with neighbouring countries, and supporting the Hungarians living in neighboring countries. The MSZP and the SZDSZ wish to lead the country out of the serious crisis with a sensible and resolute economy policy and a sensitive social policy. The two parties are well aware of the fact that the preconditions of this are the maintenance of social peace and the achievement and maintenance of a general agreement with the various groups and [word indistinct] of society. [end recording]
