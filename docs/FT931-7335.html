930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Rise in executive job offers THE PERCENTAGE of redundant executives receiving job offers is rising, particularly in the south-east, Drake Beam Morrin, outplacement consultants says. A total of 16 per cent of out-of-work executives it counselled received job offers in the first four weeks of the year - up from 10 per cent last year. In the south-east the proportion doubled, from 9 per cent to 18 per cent while in the Midlands it rose from 10 per to 17.6 per cent and in Scotland and the north from 10 per cent to 16.2 per cent. The figures covered executives earning from Pounds 25,000 to Pounds 60,000.
