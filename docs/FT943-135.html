940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Geest sells two ships Geest, the fresh and chilled foods group, has sold its two 'Bay' class ships, for their book value of Pounds 9.1m and will use the proceeds to cut borrowings. The two ships - the 'Geestbay' and the 'Geestport' - are more than 12 years old and will be replaced on the Windward Island route by more modern charter vessels, while options for more permanent replacements are reviewed.
