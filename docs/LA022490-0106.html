February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Standard Logic Inc.: The Anaheim-based electronics manufacturer said it lost $99,485 for the first quarter of fiscal 1990, contrasted with the net loss of $474,428 reported for the like period the previous year. Revenue for the quarter ended Jan. 27 declined 19%, to $187,850 from $232,595. The company said the first-quarter loss is a result of lower bookings and that it expects to report a profit for the third quarter.
