940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / Managers reopen Hatfield colliery Hatfield colliery, one of the pits closed by British Coal last year, was reopened by a management buy-out team yesterday. Mr Jeff Ainley, former colliery manager, and his partners expect to employ about 200 men when the colliery near Doncaster, South Yorkshire, reaches full production of 400,000 tonnes a year for the household and industrial markets. Last December the pit employed 400.
