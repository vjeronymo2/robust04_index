Extension of Comment Period for Draft Environmental Impact Statement, and Section 4(f) Evaluation_The Port Authority of New York and New Jersey's Airport Access Program, LaGuardia_John F. Kennedy International Airports (DEIS) AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration, DOT. ACTION: Extension of comment period.

SUMMARY: The FAA, acting as ``Lead Agency'' and the New York State Department of Transportation (NYSDOT), acting as ``Joint Lead Agency'' prepared and distributed as Draft Environmental Impact Statement on Section 4(f) Evaluation for the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey's Airport Access Program, LaGuardia_John F. Kennedy International Airports. The availability of the DEIS was previously announced by the Environmental Protection Agency in the June 24, 1994 Federal Register. The comment period was to have closed on August 8, 1994 and was later extended to September 30, 1994 and extended again to October 17, 1994. By this Notice, the comment period is further extended to December 16, 1994. In addition to allowing additional time for commentors on this extensive DEIS, this time extension provides the opportunity, to those who desire to do so, to consult with FAA prior to submitting the comments. This may be especially valuable for those who intend to submit extensive and/or detailed technical comments.
