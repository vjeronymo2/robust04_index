BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon the Thai ambassador in Tehran was summoned to the Foreign ministry and handed a note of protest by the deputy foreign minister and head of the Department for Southern and Eastern Affairs. The Central News Unit reports this note protested the arrest of several Iranians on the trumped-up charges of their attempting to blow up the Zionist regime's embassy in Bangkok and also to related interviews by Thai officials. In this note of protest, the Islamic Republic of Iran has demanded an immediate response from the Thai Government and has asked for documented proof of the charges brought against the Iranian nationals. It has also asked the Thai Government to arrange a meeting between officials of the Islamic Republic's consulate in Bangkok and the accused.
