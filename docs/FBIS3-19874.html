Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Suleyman Demirel has left on an official visit for Romania. In a predeparture statement at Ankara's Esenboga Airport, Demirel remarked that mutual high-level visits will contribute to the further development of Turkish-Romanian relations. Pointing out that Turkey is following with great satisfaction the progress achieved by Romania in the field of democracy and a free market economy, Demirel declared: Turkey wants and is determined to further strengthen its relations with Romania in every field. Demirel was seen off at Esenboga Airport by Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly.
