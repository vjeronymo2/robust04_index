Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- Russia's Finance Minister Boris Fedorov said he understood First Vice-Premier Yegor Gaydar's decision to resign from the government, announced on Sunday [16 January]. The government's last decisions indicate the "beginning of changes", Fedorov pointed out in an interview with the "Echo of Moscow" radio station. "I did not have any advance notice of Yegor Gaydar's intention", Fedorov said. He said he had not yet got an invitation to join the reshuffled government. If such a proposal is made, he will make a decision on Monday or Thursday, Fedorov added.
