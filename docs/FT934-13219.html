931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Net assets improve at London Atlantic The net asset value of London Atlantic Investment Trust was 99p at September 30, an advance of 34 per cent on the 74p of a year earlier. Net asset value at the March year-end was 87p. Net revenue for the six months to end-September edged ahead to Pounds 711,000 (Pounds 701,000) for earnings of 1.56p (1.54p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.78p.
