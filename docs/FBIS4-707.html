BFN [Excerpt] The head of state, Colonel Idriss Deby, returned to Ndjamena this afternoon from Kuwait where he paid a 48-hour visit. The head of state was welcomed upon his arrival at the airport by transitional Prime Minister Nouradeen Delwa Kassire Koumakoye accompanied by cabinet ministers. Also present were (Gol Mohammed Shuwa), chairman of the Higher Transitional Council, and some members of the diplomatic corps. [passage omitted]
