BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 18 KYODO -- The House of Representatives Steering Committee decided Monday to postpone to 1 P.M. [0400 GMT] Tuesday a decision on the timing of a Diet plenary session to choose Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's successor. Committee members decided on the postponement in order to monitor the views of the coalition parties and the leading opposition Liberal Democratic Party. The postponement means a vote on a successor to Hosokawa will not take place until on or after Wednesday, parliamentary sources said. Hosokawa announced on April 8 that he would resign as prime minister.
