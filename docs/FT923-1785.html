920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / Unions fight BR sale THE biggest union campaign against rail privatisation will be launched On Wednesday with warnings that loss-making routes may be cut. The rail unions will claim that half Britain's rail lines are at risk under the government's 'ill considered' proposals to sell off BR. Mr Jimmy Knapp, general secretary of the Rail, Maritime and Transport Workers Union, said the government would continue to make reassuring statements about the future of BR routes before the network is sold off. 'If you believe them, you will be in for a terrible shock,' he said.
