BFN [Text] Tripoli, 5 Jun -- A friendship, goodneighborliness, and cooperation agreement between the Great Jamahiriyah and Chad was signed yesterday. The brother leader of the revolution and Chadian President Idriss Deby signed this historic agreement in the process of consolidating cooperative relations and economic and social integration between the two fraternal peoples who belong to a single African, Arab civilized character, with all the basic factors of unity of blood and history and a single destiny.
