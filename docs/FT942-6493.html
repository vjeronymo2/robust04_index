940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Granada - Other companies The City is expecting considerable growth from the leisure, television and computer services group, which reports interims on Wednesday. Last time, before the takeover of London Weekend Television, Granada had pre-tax profits of Pounds 68m. This time analysts are expecting Pounds 90m-Pounds 100m, even though little more than a month's profits will be included from LWT.
