BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 21 (KPL) -- The `Intersputnik' Satellite Station has been transferred from the Ministry of Communication, Transport, Post and Construction (MCTPC) to the Ministry of Information and Culture for use in expanding radio and television system throughout the country. The handing over of the satellite station was held yesterday between Bouathong Vongnokham, minister of the MCTPC and Osakan Thammatheva, minister of information and culture. Officials concerned said that to implement a plan for the expansion of radio and television network throughout the country, the two ministries have agreed that this satellite station come under the management of the Ministry of Information and Culture. Located at km 52 on road 13 north, the Intersputnik Satellite Station was built for Laos in 1980 by the former Soviet Union and used for communication purpose in 1983 under the MCTPC.
