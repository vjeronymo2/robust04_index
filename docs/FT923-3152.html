920914 FT 14 SEP 92 / Teachers seek 16.5% pay rise THE NUT teachers' union is seeking a 16.5 per cent salary increase for its members next year, plus limits on class sizes and teachers' working time. Its submission to the teachers' pay review body, due to report at the start of next year, calls for a Pounds 14,000 starting salary for teachers, and the abolition of performance pay and all pay flexibilities.
