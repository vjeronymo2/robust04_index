940127 FT 27 JAN 94 / Observer: Simply Simpkin 'So, do you think you're getting the hang of this derivatives business, Simpkin?' the bailiffs enquire with ill-concealed glee as they bundle the contents of Simpkin's office into their van. Roger Beale's cartoon in Wednesday's FT, illustrating an article on those damnably tricky financial instruments called derivatives, did not escape the notice of one Guy Simpkin, head of quantitative research at BZW Asset Management. Luckily he seems to have a sense of humour.
