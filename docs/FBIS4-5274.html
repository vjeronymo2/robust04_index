BFN ["Text" of Okinawan Governor Masahide Ota's statement on 6 April the crash of a USMC helicopter at Futenma Air Station] [Text] The statement of Governor Masahide Ota is as follows: I have not yet fully been informed of the details of the accident, but I am greatly frustrated with repeated accidents by U.S. Forces. My impression is that the increase in accidents is part of a sequence caused by U.S. defense budget cuts. If so, I cannot help feeling great fear about the recurrence of such accidents. The U.S. Forces say their training must continue to fulfill their duties defined in the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty. I am wondering if we cannot be free from the danger and problems until the security treaty is scrapped.
