BFN [Text] Volodymyr Butkevych, leader of the Ukrainian parliamentary group, who arrived in Crimea to solve the acute issues in relations between the Ukrainian state and its autonomy, and Ukrainian People's Deputy Yuriy Karmazin spoke at a plenary session of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Crimea on 2 June. They informed the Crimean Supreme Council deputies about the delegation's objective, called on them for cooperation, and answered their questions. Regret was expressed in connection with the fact that the Crimean delegation was absent from the Ukrainian Supreme Council session on 1 June, at which the problems of the peninsula were considered. It is indicated that the speeches by the guests from Kiev were applauded by the Crimean deputies.
