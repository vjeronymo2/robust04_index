940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Compensation hope Customers of three financial services firms declared 'in default' may be eligible for compensation, the Investors Compensation Scheme has said. The three, former members of Fimbra, the self-regulator for financial advisers, are: Brokeraid, trading as Morgan Associates of Worthing; Highcliffe Financial Services of Lymington; and Siltala Fund Managers of Bishop's Stortford.
