BFN [Text] The Turkish National Assembly's decision [as heard] to turn Ayia Sofia into a mosque represents another provocation by Turkey. Deputy Foreign Minister Yeoryios Papandreou said that this development causes concern. Public Order Minister Stilianos Papathemelis described the Turkish decision as an advanced form of provocation. New Democracy issued a statement asking for the World Council of Churches to convene and for mobilization of all Greeks throughout the world to prevent the desecration of Ayia Sofia. Commenting on the proposal to turn Ayia Sofia into a mosque, Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said that, given its everyday practices, Turkey needs to prove that it has some kind of relation with what is called European civilization. Turkey, the spokesman said, has the duty to prove that. Archbishop Serafim of Athens issued a letter to the United Nations and all other international and church organizations as well as to the leaders of Christian churches regarding the threats against the Ecumenical Patriarchate by Islamists.
