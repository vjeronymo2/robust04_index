BFN [From the "National Radio News"] [Text] On 28 June, Ukraine and Cuba held talks in Kiev to strengthen bilateral ties. A positive step was the signing of an accord to create a technical economic commission. The accord was signed by Lionel Soto, vice president of the Council of Ministers, and Vice Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov. Soto is visiting several CIS nations, having already been to Russia, Kazakhstan, Belarus, and Azerbaijan.
