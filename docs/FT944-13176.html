941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Inchcape Estates Inchcape Estates, formerly Tozer Kemsley & Millbourn (Estates), the property arm of Tozer Kemsley & Millbourn (Holdings), the motor distributor acquired by Inchcape at end-1991, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 6.26m for the first half of 1994 against profits of Pounds 1.84m. The result, on turnover of Pounds 2.78m (Pounds 3.39m), was after a loss of Pounds 354,000 (Pounds 25,000) on the sale of properties and an exceptional Pounds 6.4m provision against a property sold after the period-end. Losses per share were 78.1p (10.8p earnings).
