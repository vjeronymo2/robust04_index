May 31, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GIRTH CONTROL: Just what is Orange County's leading gourmand, Tommy Lasorda of Fullerton, trying to tell us? . . . Last year he dropped 40 pounds and went on TV to endorse a weight-reducing milkshake. But this year, his food company in Irvine is going national to tout his pasta sauce from Maine to Malibu (D7). . . . No conflict, says a Lasorda spokesman. Tommy has the milkshake for breakfast and lunch and pasta for dinner. Bill Billiter and Jan Herman
