August 18, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

CONTROVERSY IS ONE THING, BUT GIVE FANS SOME GOLF

In the Viewpoint letters Aug. 11, Sandy Cohen stated that the media had blown the Shoal Creek situation out of proportion and that a more urgent need was to " . . . educate young blacks so that they can get better jobs and economically have their own private clubs."

Perhaps even more urgent is the need to educate whites, both young and old, so that there will be no more private clubs like Shoal Creek.

WILLIAM M. HOMANN

San Marcos
