931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / Head teachers warning to Major The National Association of Head Teachers has warned Mr John Major of the problems of enforcing discipline in schools against a background of declining standards of behaviour and increasing levels of crime in society. It has told the prime minister: 'Not nearly enough has been said about the responsibilities of parents for the behaviour of their children.' Mr David Hart, general secretary, said teachers had less and less time to devote to personal or social education.
