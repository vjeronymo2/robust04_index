BFN [Excerpt] The government troops' assembly area in Boane, Maputo Province, was closed down today. The Boane Assembly Area is the first to be closed down within the framework of the implementation of the General Peace Accord. Present at the closing ceremony were officials from the government, the Mozambique National Resistance, and the UN Operations in Mozambique. The assembly area was opened last February and it had 1,500 troops. [passage omitted]
