940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Net assets up at Value & Income The fully-diluted net asset value per share at the Value & Income Trust stood at 111.7p at the year ended March 31, against 101.1p six months earlier and 92.5p at the previous year-end. Net revenue for the 12 months fell slightly, from Pounds 1.64m to Pounds 1.44m, giving earnings per share of 3.39p (3.97p). A final dividend of 2p makes a 4p (3.6p) total. The directors said they expected to increase the total pay-out in the current year to 4.2p.
