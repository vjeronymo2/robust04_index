931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / President Yeltsin creates new banking mechanism President Boris Yeltsin has set up a new bankruptcy mechanism, clearing the way for the mandatory sale of insolvent state-run enterprises into private hands, his office announced yesterday, AP reports from Moscow. His decree assigns the Federal Department for Insolvency Affairs, a unit of the State Property Committee, to identify failing enterprises and decide their future. Businesses found to be insolvent can be liquidated, restructured or transferred to other owners, including the private sector, the decree said.
