930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Cape William Bon Chretien pears at 45-55p a lb (50-65p) are this week's best fruit buy reports the FFVIB. White seedless grapes at Pounds 1.00-1.20 a lb (Pounds 1.50-1.60) and early forced rhubarb at 55-65p a lb (60-80p) are all plentiful. Green veg such as broccoli at 40-45p per 8oz prepack (40-45p) and English Savoy cabbage at 25-35p a lb (25-35p) are great quality this week. Onions at 18-20p a lb (18-20p) remain a good vegetable buy. Best salad buys include watercress at 40-45p a bunch (35-45P), tomatoes at 55-60p a lb (55-65p) and celery at 50-60p a head (45-60p).
