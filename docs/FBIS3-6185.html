Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, the government held a strategic meeting participated by unification-related ministries, and chaired by Yi Yong-tok, vice prime minister and national unification board minister, to discuss follow-up measures for the seventh North-South working-level contact for the exchange of special envoys scheduled to be held on 16 March. At today's meeting, the government confirmed the position that the exchange of special envoys must be carried out before the third round of U.S.-North Korean high-level talks is held. It has decided to call on the North side to make efforts to expedite the process of initiating exchange of special envoys during tomorrow's contact in Panmunjom. During tomorrow's contact, if there is no final agreement between the North and South, and see only a negative attitude of the North side, the government will officially ask the United States to postpone the third round of U.S.-North Korean high-level talks. The government has decided that if the International Atomic Energy Agency's inspection in North Korea which ended on 15 March, does not produce sufficient resuslt, the government is considering to resume the Team Spirit exercise.
