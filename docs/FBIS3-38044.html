Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Bratislava February 24 TASS -- Russian Foreign Intelligence Chief Yevgeniy Primakov ended his visit to Slovakia on Thursday [24 February]. He expressed satisfaction with the results of his talks in Bratislava, adding that both sides reiterated their willingness to develop mutual respectful relations between Russia and Slovakia. During the visit, Primakov met with the leader of the Slovak Information Service, Vladimir Mitro, other officials to discuss ways to combat organised crime, international terrorism and illegal drug trafficking, as well as the situation in trouble spots. Asked to comment on the spy scandal involving a senior CIA official, Aldrich Ames, and his wife arrested on charges of spying for the Soviet Union and then Russia for nearly a decade, Primakov replied "no comments," adding "but when I return to Moscow..."
