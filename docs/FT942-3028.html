940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / International Company News: UAP sells stake in Nordstern Union des Assurances de Paris, the French insurer, has sold a 37 per cent stake in Nordstern Allgemeine Versicherungs to Colonia Konzern, Reuter reports from Cologne. Mr Klaus Dieter Laessker, Nordstern chairman, told the company's annual general meeting that Colonia had acquired the Nordstern shares several days ago. He did not say how much UAP received. Mr Laessker said that Nordstern would offer its shareholders a cash payment of DM2,630 per ordinary share as a result of the recent controlling agreement with Colonia.
