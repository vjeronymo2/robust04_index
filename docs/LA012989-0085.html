January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FIRM PLEDGES FUNDS FOR LAB

George E. Brandow, Gregg Brandow and Roy Johnston, principals in the Los Angeles-based structural engineering firm of Brandow and Johnston, have pledged $300,000 to help fund a structural components laboratory at USC.

The gift contributes toward The Campaign for USC, the university's program to raise $557 million by 1990, and in recognition of this contribution, the new facility will be named the Brandow and Johnston Structural Components Laboratory.
