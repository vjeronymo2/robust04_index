Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Triq 'Aziz met with British Ambassador Bavid Hannay at noon today, Wednesday [New York time]. Tariq 'Aziz briefed the British ambassador on Iraq's clear position on applying relevant Security Council resolutions and on the need for the Security Council to meet its obligations toward Iraq as stipulated in these resolutions. The deputy prime minister will also meet with the ambassadors of Morocco, Djibouti, and Oman separately. The latter two are Security Council member states.
