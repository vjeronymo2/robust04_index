BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Argentina monitored through 2 June. Media Secretary Raul Burzaco reported that Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo today informed the cabinet that the total provincial deficit stands at $1.3 billion, of which $500 million involves the northwestern provinces. (Buenos Aires Radio Nacional Network in Spanish 1600 GMT 27 May 94) It was reported that the unemployment rate hit 12 percent at the end of April 1994, or an increase of 2.7 percent in the past six months. In October 1993 the unemployment rate stood at 9.3 percent. Industrial circles blamed the increase on foreign competition, increasing internal costs, and the incorporation of new machinery and technologies that replace labor. (Buenos Aires LA PRENSA in Spanish 1 Jun 94 Economic Section p 5)
