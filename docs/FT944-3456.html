941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: ED&F Man close to acquiring US financial services group ED&F Man Group, the international trading and financial services company, yesterday confirmed that it was in 'the final stages of negotiations' to buy Gelderman, a US financial services group, based in Chicago. An announcement is expected shortly. A price of about Pounds 20m is thought to have been agreed with Gelderman's owner, the US food and agricultural giant, ConAgra. Gelderman has assets of Dollars 30m (Pounds 18m).
