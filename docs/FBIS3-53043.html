Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Shim'on Sheves, director general of the Prime Minister's Office, said today that Hizballah's announcement that it will agree to provide details about Israeli MIA's only to international organizations is good news. Our correspondent Nati Zilberman reports that during a tour of northern Israel with the committee of ministerial directors, Sheves asserted that the U.S. congressional delegation currently in Syria should be allowed to look into the affair unimpeded.
