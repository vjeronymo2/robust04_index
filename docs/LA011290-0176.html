January 12, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

STATE;

BLAST JOLTS S.F. STATE BUILDING

An explosive device, apparently a simulated military grenade, detonated outside a state government building this morning, a day after incendiary devices were found in San Francisco and Palo Alto Macy's and Sak's Fifth Avenue department stores, police said.

State Police Lt. Randy Greer said the explosion at 7:30 a.m. in a driveway of the state building on Larkin Street caused no damage or injuries.
