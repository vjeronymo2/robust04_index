940105 FT 05 JAN 94 / Observer: Cold turkey Who said Chinese walls never work? Just as N M Rothschild, the UK merchant bank, was poised to win a contract to advise Turkey on the privatisation of its state-owned petroleum industry, the boys at Smith New Court, Rothschild's stockbroking affiliate, decided to put out an analysis of the Turkish economy, under the gloomy title: 'A plateful in 1994 - are the festivities over?' The document was illustrated with some rather unflattering cartoons of Tansu Ciller, Turkey's prime minister. Lucky old Chase Manhattan has got the job.
