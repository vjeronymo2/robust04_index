940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / International Company News: ANI ahead at nine months ANI, the Sydney-based heavy engineering group which owns Aurora in the UK, has reported a 13.6 per cent rise in after-tax profits for the nine months to end-March to ADollars 43.8m. Revenues were up by 25.8 per cent to ADollars 1.1bn, writes Nikki Tait. Operating profit after tax rose by 19.3 per cent in the third quarter to ADollars 14.1m and ANI said the trend should continue for the rest of the year.
