May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

COMMENCEMENT 1989

Hebrew Union College -- Jewish Institute of Religion held its 31st commencement Monday. Advanced degrees were conferred on 25 students. Professor Gerald B. Bubis, founding director of the college's School of Jewish Communal Service, delivered the commencement address and received an honorary degree for his contributions to Jewish communal life.

Honorary Degrees:

Bubis.

Rabbi Jerry Fisher, serving the Jewish community in Irvine.

Saul Friedlander, author and professor of history at UCLA.

Rabbi Richard N. Levy, executive director of the Los Angeles Hillel Council.

Rocco C. Siciliano, attorney and founding member of the California Business Roundtable.

Rabbi Martin Weiner, Congregation Sherith Israel in San Francisco.

Rabbi Raymond Zwerin, Temple Sinai in Denver.
