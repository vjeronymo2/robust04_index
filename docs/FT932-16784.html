930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Fixed rate Tessa offer - At a Glance A fixed rate Tessa has been launched by Birmingham Midshires, the 13th largest building society. The Quantum Fixed Tessa will pay a fixed rate of 7.25 per cent annually throughout its five year term. An extra 2 per cent bonus based on the balance at the end of the fourth year will be added to the final amount. However, you need Pounds 8,400 to open the account. Three thousand pounds is immediately put in the Tessa and the rest remains in a feeder account, also earning a fixed 7.25 per cent gross. There is a penalty of five months' loss of interest if the Tessa is closed early.
