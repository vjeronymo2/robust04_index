920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Platignum returns to the black Tight cost controls helped push Platignum, the pen maker and office equipment manufacturer, back into profit with Pounds 192,000 pre-tax in the six months to September 30. This compared with a Pounds 331,000 loss last time and was achieved despite sales falling from Pounds 10m to Pounds 8.89m. Earnings per share came out at 0.09p (losses 0.16p).
