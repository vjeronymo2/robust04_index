BFN ["Report by NAEWOE News Agency"] [Text] North Korea stressed that in preparation for economic sanctions against the North as a result of its refusal of nuclear inspections, it will strengthen independence in its economy so that its economy can be self-reliant in the future under any situation. In yesterday's special article [nonsol], North Korean Central Broadcast Radio [chungang pangsong] reported that North Korea will win a brilliant victory in implementing new economic strategies under the leadership of Kim Chong-il, and that the economic tasks promoted by North Korea can be carried out as long as there is a revolutionary spirit of self-reliance.
