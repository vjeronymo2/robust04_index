920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / World News In Brief: Cocaine haul Cocaine worth an estimated Pounds 6m was found by customs officers at Sheerness, Kent, hidden in a trailer carrying a car owned by a top motor racing team, Vic Lee Motorsport of Northfleet, Kent - holder of the British Touring Car Championship title. Lee and three others were charged with the illegal importation of drugs. Four other people were released on bail.
