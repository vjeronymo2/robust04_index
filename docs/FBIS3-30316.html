Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 31/1/1994 -- The national federation of the transport unions, Elcatel, called Monday, 31 January, a protest rally in front of the transport ministry building to express their dissatisfaction with the attitude taken by the Romanian National Railway Corporation (SNCFR) and the relevant ministry to the "serious problems" railway employees have to endure and with the hazards arising from such an attitude. Among the reasons of discontent are the delay in the debate and passing of the railway transportation bill, the persisting economic blockage, and the compensation by the government of railway tariffs. Should their claims be disregarded, Elcatel promised concerted strikes on 15 February, to be then extended to a general strike on 17 February.
