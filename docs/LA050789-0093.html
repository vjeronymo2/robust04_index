May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

On The Line, the odds-on favorite ridden by Gary Stevens, edged True and Blue by a neck to win the $234,800 Carter Handicap at Aqueduct in New York.

On The Line, carrying high weight of 125 pounds and trained by Wayne Lukas, covered seven furlongs in 1:21 2/5 for his third victory in four starts this year and earned $140,880.
