940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Carson rides fourth Derby winner Willie Carson steered 7-2 favourite Erhaab (left) to win a sprint finish to the Epsom Derby. It was the fourth Derby winner for Carson, 51, one of three grand- fathers in the race. Erhaab won Pounds 470,000 for owner Hamdan Al-Maktoum, finishing one-and-a-quarter lengths clear of King's Theatre (14-1), with Colonel Collins (right) (10-1) a further one-and-a-half lengths behind.
