BFN [Editorial Report] Managua Radio Nicaragua Network in Spanish 1200 GMT 28 April 94 reports that Evenor Taboada, president of the Nicaraguan Central Bank, has said regulating interest rates through a law is dangerous, even though he admitted banking interests "are extremely exaggerated." Taboada made the remark when asked about a draft law that aimed at revaluing bonds owned by individuals whose property the previous government expropriated and confiscated.
