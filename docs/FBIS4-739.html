BFN [By Jonathan Rees] [Text] Maseru April 15 SAPA -- Several bursts of what is believed to be automatic rifle fire were heard early Friday [15 April] morning in Lesotho's capital, Maseru, where troops on Thursday shot dead Deputy Prime Minister Selometsi Baholo and took four cabinet ministers hostage for a day. It was not clear who was firing or at whom and there was no indication of any formal demands being released by the soldiers.
