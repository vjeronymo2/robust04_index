BFN [Own correspondent Vladimir Gundarov report from Severodvinsk under the "In Brief" rubric: "At Present, Ships Are Also Born in Pain"] [Text] The creation of the "Severodvinsk" nuclear-powered submarine -- KRASNAYA ZVEZDA reported the laying of its keel in late December -- has entailed painstaking efforts from the first days of building work. The shipbuilders are not satisfied with the quality of design documents, which, according to specialists, can bear no criticism. This is not the sole reason for wrecking the timetable of work on the building stocks, however. According to M. Zhukov, who is responsible for commissioning the ship, the main reason are undersupplies of metal and steel work because "Sevmashpredpriyatiye" [Severodvinsk Engineering Enterprise] does not have money to pay to supplier plants in advance.
