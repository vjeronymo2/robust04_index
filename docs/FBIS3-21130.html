Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Text] Mitsubishi Motors Corp. began selling its Libero EV electric vehicle on 25 January. Selling at the high price of ¥11.23 million, the company plans for sales to government agencies and large corporations. Three vehicles are already said to have been ordered by the [Tokyo] Minato District office. Mitsubishi Motors is aiming for annual sales of 50 vehicles. The Libero EV is based on the ``Libero Cargo 1500G.'' Mitsubishi Motors and Tokyo Electric Power Co. converted it into an electric vehicle. Mitsubishi Motors is the first company to sell the bonnet-wagon type of electric vehicle. On a single charge, the vehicle is capable of travelling 165 kilometers at a constant speed of 40 kilometers per hour.
