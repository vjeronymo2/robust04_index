September 28, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NON-RESIDENTIAL BUILDING PERMIT VALUATIONS

The value of non-residential building permits issued in the county during the first six months of the year was $733.5 million, a small 3% increase over the comparable period of last year. The same figure for Los Angeles County was down 10% while the three other contiguous counties made larger gains.

Orange County permit valuations compared to 1988 were higher in two categories, office buildings (up 2%) and alterations/additions (25%), but declined in three others -- industrial buildings (down 11%), stores (26%) and hotels and motels (22%).
