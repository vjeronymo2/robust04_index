940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: BTR spins off Dartington Crystal gazing - Mr Eric Dancer (left), managing director of Dartington Crystal, and Mr Russ Cummings of 3i, reflect on the future of the giftware and tableware producer which has been purchased by its management from BTR in a Pounds 10m deal, writes Peter Franklin. Under the terms of the transaction, which is backed by 3i, the venture capital group, BTR's Rockware Group received Pounds 4.71m for the sale of its 95 per cent holding as well as repayment of Pounds 3.25m in loans. The Dartington Hall Trust, which founded the company in 1967 to create employment in north Devon, has also sold its 5 per cent holding. In addition, Dartington Crystal will receive Pounds 2m from the deal to fund future growth plans. Mr Cummings said that his company had led, structured and underwritten the transaction, while Bank of Scotland was providing debt and working capital.
