Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Vienna/Moscow (DPA) -- In an interview with the Austrian magazine NEWS, Vladimir Zhirinovksiy, the right-wing extremist leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia (LDPR), has apologized for the insults and threats he uttered in Sofia against Germany. "If what I said was taken as insulting, then I am sorry," Zhirinovskiy said in the interview, excerpts of which were prereleased to DPA. He had been "misunderstood." Shortly before Zhirinovkiy made the remarks to which he refers, the German Government had refused him an entry visa. [passage omitted]
