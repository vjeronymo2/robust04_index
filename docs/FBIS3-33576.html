Article Type:BFN [Text] It was reported a short while ago from Caulatu, Quilali, that an agreement has been reached between the government and the rebels. As a result of this agreement, Esteban, the brother of Jose Angel Talavera, a.k.a. El Chacal, head of the 3-80 Northern Front, demobilized. Another important result of this agreement is that 100 recontras will surrender on 2 March, and the last man will surrender his arms no later than 8 April. The government has agreed to create a provisional demilitarized zone in Caulatu and El Zungano, Esteli, and provide food, clothing, and medicines to the rebels while they wait for the demobilization's deadline.
