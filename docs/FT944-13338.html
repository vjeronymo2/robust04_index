941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Three Graces to stay in Britain Antonio Canova's The Three Graces will stay in the UK after the Court of Appeal rejected a challenge by the Getty Museum of California, which paid Pounds 7.6m for the statue, against a government decision to delay the granting of an export licence. The move allowed UK galleries time to raise a similar amount to keep the work in Britain.
