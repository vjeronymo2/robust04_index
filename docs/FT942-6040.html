940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto also ended on a mixed note following moderate trading. The TSE 300 index gained 6.1 points at 4,326.8 but falls narrowly led rises by 409 to 401. Volume was 48.1m shares. Eight of the 14 sub-indices were lower, led by transportation, off 1.6 per cent. The gold group rose 1.6 per cent as the New York bullion price put on USDollars 2.40 to USDollars 387.15 an ounce.
