920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Tactel boosts ICI sales Imperial Chemical Industries has captured 10 per cent of the European market for hosiery fibres with a new product launched 18 months ago. The use of Tactel nylon microfibre in 350m items of hosiery this year means sales of about Pounds 20m to ICI. Tactel is a nylon microfibre and buyers include all the big manufacturers of hosiery in Europe.
