October 28, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

NORTHWEST VALLEY CONFERENCE

Canoga Park 33, Chatsworth 13 -- Quarterback David Erhardt rushed for two touchdowns, passed for two scores and accumulated 218 yards in total offense to lead the host Hunters (1-5, 1-3) to their first win. Erhardt completed 10 of 11 passes for 157 yards and rushed for 51 yards in 15 carries. Teammate Scott Byrd rushed for 106 yards in 12 carries and caught five passes for 109 yards and one touchdown.

Canoga Park outgained Chatsworth (2-3-1, 1-3), 347 yards to 161. TIM MARTINEZ
