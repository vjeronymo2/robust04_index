Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Robert Pelletreau, U.S. assistant secretary of state for Near East affairs. The meeting was attended by Martin Indyk, U.S. presidential assistant for Middle East affairs; Dan Kurtzer, U.S. deputy assistant secretary of state; and Christopher Ross, the U.S. ambassador in Damascus. The meeting was also attended by Raslan 'Allush, the assistant foreign minister; Dr. Najdi al-Jazzar, head of the Foreign Ministry's U.S. Desk, and Walid al-Mu'allim, the Syrian ambassador in Washington. Discussion focused on bilateral relations and means to improve them in light of the agreement President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton reached during the Geneva summit.
