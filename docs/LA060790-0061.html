June 7, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: CAMARILLO;

LUTHERAN CHURCH BURGLARIZED AGAIN

For the second time in less than two months, burglars broke into First Lutheran Church of Camarillo and stole electronic equipment.

The latest burglary was discovered about 6:30 a.m. Wednesday, according to a spokesman for the Ventura County Sheriff's Department. The thieves smashed a window to enter the church at 380 Arneill Road during the night. Electronic equipment worth about $200 was missing.

About 1 1/2 months ago, a videocassette recorder was taken, a church employee said.
