Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense and Security Minister Edi Sudrajat and Religious Affairs Minister Tarmizi Taher say religious believers are are currently mature enough to face a leadership succession. Speaking during a news conference yesterday, the defense and security minister said national resilience, particularly in the face of the leadership succession, is in good condition and there is no situation that may lead to an upheaval. According to Minister Edi Sudrajat, national resilience must be consistently enhanced. Thus, the defense and security minister called on all parties to continue to enhance national resilience. Meanwhile, Religious Affairs Minister Tarmizi Taher said the fact that Muslim scholars had directly asked the head of state about the leadership succession is proof of the high degree of their concern. In addition, the Muslim scholars hope that everything will proceed constitutionally for the sake of all Muslims.
