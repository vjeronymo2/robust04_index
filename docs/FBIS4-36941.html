BFN [Text] A delegation from the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], led by PUK Secretary General brother Jalal Talabani, met with a delegation from the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP], led by KDP Leader Mas'ud Barzani, in the city of Silopi in Turkey at 1030 yesterday. A Turkish Government delegation attended the meeting, which was held to discuss the means and possibilities of normalizing the situation in Kurdistan, speeding up the agreements already approved by the two parties, and consolidating security and stability in the Kurdistan region.
