EFFECTIVE DATE: September 6, 1994. ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENCY 40 CFR Part 52 [MO&hyph;13&hyph;1&hyph;6558; FRL&hyph;5059&hyph;6] Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Missouri AGENCY: Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). ACTION: Final rule; correction.

SUMMARY: On August 30, 1993 (58 FR 45451), EPA approved revisions to Missouri's rules 10 CSR 10&hyph;2.290 and 10 CSR 10&hyph;5.340, which apply to rotogravure and flexographic printing facilities in Kansas City, Missouri, and St. Louis, Missouri. Part 52 was amended to incorporate by reference the changes to those rules; however section (2), Definitions, from both rules was not included. This document is to correct the deficiency. EFFECTIVE DATE: September 6, 1994.
