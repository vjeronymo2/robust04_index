920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Early forced rhubarb at 45-60p a lb (40-65p) is superb this week, reports FFVIB. Grapes remain a superb choice with Cape seedless at 90p-Pounds 1.20 a lb (90p-Pounds 1.20). Jaffa oranges at 12-30p each (12-30p) and Spania strawberries at 65-80p a 1/2 lb (85p-Pounds 1.00) are also good value for money. An excellent vegetable buy is French cauliflower at 50-75p each (60-80p), also good is broccoli at 40-48p a 1/2 lb (35-45p), Spring cabbage at 25-35p a lb (25-35p) and parsnips at 30-40p a lb (30-40p). The new crop of English watercress is now available at 35-45p a bunch (35-45p), together with radishes at 30-45p a bunch (30-45p).
