Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 14 (YONHAP) -- The 21st Century Committee, created by South Korea and the United States, will hold its first meeting next month in Washington, the Foreign Ministry said Friday. For three days, Feb. 17-19, prominent politicians, economists, scholars and businessmen, including U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, will discuss how to expand cooperation and will present reports and recommendations on improving relations. Sakong Il, a former finance minister, and Fred Bergsten, a former deputy Treasury secretary, led preparations to form the committee.
