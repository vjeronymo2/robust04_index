May 15, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SOVIET TEAM TO HIT GRIDIRON IN U.S.

Former Denver Broncos Coach John Ralston will coach a Soviet team that will play five American-style football games in the United States in July.

The tour was announced by the International Sports Connection, which represents the USSR-American Football Union, a league made up of 24 teams in the Soviet Union. The Moscow Bears will play Minor League Football System teams in Tacoma, Wash.; Fresno, Calif.; Oklahoma City; Macon, Ga., and Charlotte, N.C.
