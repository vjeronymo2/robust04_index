931102 FT 02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Britannia Group BRITANNIA GROUP: Rights issue taken up as to 8.84m shares (77.6 per cent). Figure includes acceptances in respect of 7.39m new ordinary shares, being the renounced entitlements of certain directors and major shareholders (64.8 per cent), which were placed with institutions and two other directors. The balance will be taken up by sub-underwriters.
