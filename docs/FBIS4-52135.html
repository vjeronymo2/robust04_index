BFN [Text] Visiting Thai Education Minister Samphan Thongsamak and his delegation called on Lieutenant General Aung Ye Kyaw, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of culture, at the latter's office at the Department of Fine Art in Kanbawza Yeiktha at 1400 this afternoon. They held cordial, frank, and detailed discussions on cultural exchange matters: archaeological research, museums, culture, song, dance, music, cultural education, and the improvement of mutual bilateral efforts in matters concerning libraries.
