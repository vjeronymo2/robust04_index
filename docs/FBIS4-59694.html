BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met at the United Nations today with the ambassadors of the UN Security Council nonaligned member states. The deputy prime minister briefed the Nonaligned Movement's ambassadors on Iraq's stand on the implementation of pertinent UN Security Council resolutions. In a brief press statement, Tariq 'Aziz expressed satisfaction with the results of this meeting. He told journalists that the meeting was "characterized by a spirit of friendship." Taking part in the meeting were the ambassadors of Oman, Djibouti, Nigeria, Rwanda, and Pakistan--nonpermanent members of the UN Security Council.
