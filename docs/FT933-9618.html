930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Improving access to the M11 The first contract to construct the eastern end of the Hackney Wick to M11 link road at Wanstead - known as contract 4 - has been awarded to NORWEST HOLST by the Department of Transport. Contract 4 runs from Selsdon Road through to the River Roding covering a distance of 1.54 kilometres. Work on the Pounds 31.5m contract is expected to start in September and will take four years.
