941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Five Oaks' Pounds 9m buy Five Oaks Investments has bought three retail properties for a total of Pounds 9.23m in cash. These comprise a parade of 10 shops in Rotherham's Cascades shopping centre and two warehouses, one in Bridgwater let to B&Q and the other in Portsmouth leased by WH Smith as a Do It All store. Combined annual rental income is in excess of Pounds 756,000.
