940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / International Company News: Spanish clothier to raise Pta17bn Cortefiel, a leading family- owned Spanish clothing group with more than 300 retail outlets, is to make an international public offer early next month which is expected to raise some Pta17bn (Dollars 120m) writes Tom Burns in Madrid. Goldman Sachs, the US investment bank and global co-ordinator for the group's stock market debut, said up to 30 per cent of Cortefiel would be offered in two tranches.
