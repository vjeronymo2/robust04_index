September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Alemany 42, Mary Star of the Sea 27 -- The trio of wide receiver Richard Dice, tailback Terry Barnum and quarterback Adam Romandi powered Alemany to a nonleague victory at home.

The Indians' ball-control offense was led by Barnum's 200 yards in 25 carries and Dice's 198 receiving yards. Each scored three touchdowns.

Romandi completed nine of 13 passes, all to Dice. GARY HYNES
