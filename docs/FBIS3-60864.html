Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Rostov-on-Don, Russia, Dec 26 (AFP) -- Gunmen freed Sunday three of the five hostages they were holding at the southern Russia airport of Mineralnyye Vody but took off with the two pilots of their commandeered helicopter, officials in Rostov-on-Don said. The four gunmen freed the last two schoolboys and a busdriver they were holding, at around 7:00 p.m. (1600 GMT). The helicopter then took off for Makhachkala, capital of the Russian republic of Dagestan on the Caspian Sea, a two-hour flight. The officials said the information came from the crisis center in Mineralnyye Vody. They did not say if concessions had been made to win these releases. [passage omitted]
