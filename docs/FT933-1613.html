930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Gain in readers held by Times THE TIMES is continuing to hold on to a circulation increase of more than 30 per cent nearly three weeks after the newspaper cut its weekday cover price by 15p to 30p. The ABC sales figure for the first week of the price reduction shows an average sale of 461,000. The average figure for August was 354,280. The Daily Telegraph, with a cover price of 48p, may have lost up to 4 per cent. Sales of The Independent, at 45p, were less than 1 per cent lower. The ABC figures for The Sun, another News International paper, indicated that its 5p price reduction to 20p increased the sales of the popular tabloid by 234,341 to 3.832m in August, an increase of nearly 7 per cent.
