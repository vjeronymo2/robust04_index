BFN [Text] A 23-member Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by Major General Ket Sein, commander of the Southeast Military Command, and including Maj. Gen. Saw Tun, commander of the Eastern Military Command, left for Thailand this morning on a special military aircraft to attend the ninth meeting of the Myanmar-Thai Regional Border Committee in Thailand. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Lieutenant General Tin U, secretary-2 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council; Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw; Rear Admiral Tin Aye, Navy chief of staff; Maj. Gen. Tin Ngwe, Air Force chief of staff; and responsible personnel from the Foreign and Defense Ministries.
