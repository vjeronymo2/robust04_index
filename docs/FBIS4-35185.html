BFN [Excerpt] Tunis, 27 May (MENA) -- At its meeting today under Yasir 'Arafat's chairmanship, the Palestinian National Authority Council decided to have its members take the constitutional oath before 'Arafat on the homeland's soil. The Council also decided to have 'Arafat take the constitutional oath in the homeland before Salim al-Za'nun, alias Abu-al-Adib, acting chairman of the Palestine National Council, the Palestinian parliament. This was stated by council member Dr. Samir Ghawshah following today's meeting. Ghawshah said that 'Arafat has decided to appoint Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, member of the PLO Executive Committee and chief of its economic department, as a member to the council. He is the 20th member. Ghawshah said that 'Arafat will continue his consultations with the various Palestinian powers to appoint five more members of the 25-member council. [passage omitted]
