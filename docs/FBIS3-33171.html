Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Front for Change and Democracy, FNCD, is going to propose a political compromise to reconcile the interests of all parties involved in the [Haitian] crisis. According to party leaders, the FNCD plan, to be submitted in Washington, draws its inspiration from the Governors Island agreement and the New York pact. The FNCD proposal reportedly includes a modification of certain sequences of the plan presently being discussed in Washington. An FNCD delegation has gone to Washington for the purpose of supporting this new proposal, the details of which were not made public.
