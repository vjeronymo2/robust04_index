BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentin Pavlov] [Text] Barnaul April 24 TASS -- Elections to the local legislative assembly were rerun on Sunday in nine constituencies of the Altay territory in southern Siberia. As of 18:00 local time, turnout in the town of Rubtsovsk made up 34 per cent, in the regional capital Barnaul -- 28 per cent and in the city of Biysk -- 27 per cent. Thus, all 50 deputies have been already elected to the local legislature, and today's polls can be declared valid.
