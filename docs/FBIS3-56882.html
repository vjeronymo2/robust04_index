Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 15 TASS -- Vasiliy Tsibliyev has been awarded the honorary title of pilot-cosmonaut of the Russian Federation for the successful accomplishment of a space flight on board the orbital research complex Mir and for a high professionalism displayed in performance of his duties, according to a decree issued by President Boris Yeltsin. Tsibliyev has also been awarded the title of hero of the Russian Federation for courage and heroism displayed during the flight, the decree said.
