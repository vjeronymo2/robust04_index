BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 12 May -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has congratulated the workforce of the All Russian Television and Radio Company on the occasion of its third anniversary, which is marked today. "I well remember the first broadcasts of Russian radio and Russian television," the Russian leader commented. "These events were a true festival of democracy and glasnost, the enthusiasm of journalists, and the sincere desire to tell and show the truth. "Not everything worked out or is working out. There are not a few problems, faced with which, I know, one sometimes loses heart. But stand firm and I am sure that you will succeed honorably in travelling the difficult path of our Russian reforms. "I will never forget that many-hours-long broadcast of that awful October night [Russian TV's special all-night program during the conflict with the Supreme Soviet in October 1993], which has become a masterpiece of television. I bow before your courage and loyalty to your professional duty. I believe in the enormous creative potential of your workforce. I wish you new successes," the president of Russia stressed.
