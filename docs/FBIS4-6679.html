BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 19 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The number of those participating in the hunger strike reached 39 due to joining of activists of the regional branches of the MDA [Mongolian Democratic Association] and MDSP [Mongolian Social Democratic Party]. The president of the country continued to consult the leaders of the parliamentary parties prime minister and the leader of the MDA. Tonight he appealed to the public over the national television and radio. In his speech the president expressed his commitment to solve the problem in line with the parliamentary activities frameworks. Then he received the participants of the hunger strike trying to persuade them to renounce the extremities in carrying out political struggle. As was said the president would submit for consideration by the parliament the demands of hunger strikers to discuss and settling down sooner this hard situation. The former vice president of the country, member of the parliament and leader of the MSDP R. Gonchigdorj declared his intention to join in the participants of the hunger strike. Parallel with this two residents of the Dzabhan Aymag in the northwestern part of Mongolia have gone on a hunger strike but for another reason. They are demanding to stop the hunger strike by the Democrats. The two of hunger strikers were also received by the president who also asked them to give up their action in order not to aggravate the situation.
