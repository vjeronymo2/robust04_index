930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Scottish work WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION SCOTLAND has picked up seven contracts totalling more than Pounds 13m for clients in the Clydebank and Glasgow area. The largest is a Pounds 5.5m development to build a 9,500 sq metre retail park in Coatbridge for food retailers, William Low. The scheme includes a 5,200 sq metre store and a 3,750 sq metre retail unit and garden centre for Texas Homecare.
