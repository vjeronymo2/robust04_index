February 26, 1990, Monday, Valley Edition

HIT-AND-RUN DRIVER KILLS 2 PEDESTRIANS

Two pedestrians were killed by a hit-and-run driver as they crossed Burbank Boulevard in Tarzana Sunday afternoon, police said.

Arnold Feldman, 61, and a 37-year-old Tarzana woman whose name was not released pending notification of relatives, were struck by a white car as they crossed the street in the 18600 block of Burbank Boulevard, said Los Angeles Police Sgt. Joe Cross.

Cross said the two people were crossing mid-block when the car struck them. Both were taken to Northridge Hospital Medical Center, where they were pronounced dead.

One witness said the car, which has not been identified, did not pause after the accident and headed west on Burbank Boulevard, Cross said.
