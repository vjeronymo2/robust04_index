Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, January 4 (XINHUA) -- The Telecommunications Department of Guangzhou, capital of south China's Guangdong Province, is offering a dozen new services to customers. The city has opened China's largest video telecommunications system, telephone post boxes and videophone service. Recently the city's "108" station began to handle international collect calls. People can use an ordinary telephone to dial 1-0-8 and the number of the expected country and region, and the operator will switch to the receiver, who pays the toll. At present, the service covers the United States, Japan, Singapore, Italy and the Republic of Korea. In addition, Guangzhou residents can also make long-distance calls on any phones just by dialing their own account number and cipher, and the charge will be assessed automatically by the computer system.
