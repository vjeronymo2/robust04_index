Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Ivan Timchenko, the head of the Ukrainian president's legal service says Yevgeniy Saburov cannot become prime minister of Crimea. He said that Ukrainian law does not ban foreign citizens from occupying such a post, but that, as a citizen of a foreign state, Saburov cannot have access to information that constitutes Ukraine's state secrets and that, without this, he cannot really carry out the duties of Crimean prime minister, Timchenko said.
