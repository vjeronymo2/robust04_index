930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Chieftain forecasts small loss quoted supplier of specialist insulation ands fire proofing services, yesterday forecast a small loss for 1993, as a result of the receivership of Swan Hunter Shipbuilders. The shares fell 10p to 59p. The directors said that the company was 'in negotiation with various parties in an attempt to improve upon this position'. They said that the company was continuing to trade profitably and it expected to report a profit in 1994. In 1992 pre-tax profits fell sharply from Pounds 1.06m to Pounds 613,000, reflecting pressure on margins and reduced demand. There were also delays in contract completions.
