940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Leeds & Holbeck declines 13% Leeds and Holbeck Building Society saw pre-tax profits decline 13 per cent - from Pounds 8.84m to Pounds 7.72m - over the 12 months to December 31. The outcome, which masked a sharp improvement in the society's second half, was struck after a marginally reduced loan loss provision of Pounds 19.4m (Pounds 19.8m). Total assets improved to Pounds 2.45bn (Pounds 2.43bn). The cost:income ratio rose from 39.9 per cent to 41.5 per cent.
