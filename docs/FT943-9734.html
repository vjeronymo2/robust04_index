940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: S African industrials improve Industrials extended Friday's good gains, while gold shares climbed off earlier lows as hopes remained that the bullion price would maintain its current firmness. The overall index advanced 30 to 5,758, the industrials index added 46 at 6,757 and golds lost 8 on balance at 2,103 after touching 2,095. Traders said that selected stocks had led the market higher, with Sasol building on Friday's R1.90 gain with a rise of 75 cents to R32, a high for the year. Among the mining sector, De Beers appreciated 25 cents to R117.75, Anglos moved ahead R2 to R252 and Vaal Reefs relinquished R8 to R399.
