Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Last night and this morning Muslim forces resumed attacks on Serb defense lines. Two Serb soldiers were wounded in attacks on the Sarajevo and Majevica fronts. Muslim work on extending fortifications and trenches has been registered from the direction of Sedam Suma, Hladivode, and Sedrenik [Muslim-held parts of eastern Sarajevo] towards Donji Biosak, Faletici, and Mrkovici [Serb-held suburbs in eastern part of the city].
