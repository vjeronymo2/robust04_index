940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / International Company News: MHS expands aerospace side Malaysian Helicopter Services (MHS) has bought 66.7 per cent of Aerospace Industries Malaysia for MDollars 9.62m cash (USDollars 3.8m), Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. MHS said the AIM shares were bought from the national carrier, Malaysian Airline System and UMW, a unit of UMW Holdings. AIM owns 51 per cent of Airod Sdn Bhd which overhauls military aircraft.
