BFN [By Mun Chae-cho from Washington] [Text] It has been learned that in a meeting of the U.S. National Security Council held early this morning at the White House, the United States has decided to hold a third round of high- level talks with North Korea as soon as possible. In today's meeting, the United States has reportedly discussed the resumption of the third round of U.S.-North Korean high-level talks in Geneva next month, at the earliest, as a result of the International Atomic Energy Agency's report that North Korea is cooperating on the nuclear inspections.
