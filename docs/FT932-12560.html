930428 FT 28 APR 93 / London to lobby for EC funds LONDON business and local government leaders will today lobby the European Commission for aid from the European Regional Development Fund. The delegation includes Labour and Conservative council leaders as well as the London Chamber of Commerce and Industry and London Training and Enterprise Councils. According to a report from the Henley Centre for Forecasting, commissioned by the Association of London Authorities, London could qualify for Objective Two status under special case provisions. The association says that this could bring Pounds 100m of EC aid for infrastructure.
