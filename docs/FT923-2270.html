920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Sharp recovery at Frank Usher Despite a reduction of gross margins in the second half, pre-tax profits of Frank Usher Holdings, the USM-traded women's clothing group, jumped some 38 per cent from a depressed Pounds 775,000 to Pounds 1.07m for the 12 months to May 31. Turnover rose marginally to Pounds 15.6m. The proposed final dividend is 3p for a total of 5p (4p), payable on earnings of 10.2p (7.2p).
