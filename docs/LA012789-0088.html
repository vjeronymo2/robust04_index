January 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

UAL Corp.'s 1988 earnings were $1.12 billion, up from $335 million in 1987. Revenue rose to $8.98 billion, up 8%. The Chicago-based parent of United Airlines credited the results to discontinued operations and a $524-million after-tax gain on the sale of Westin Hotels & Resorts.
