BFN [Text] The third conference on Ahl al-Bayt -- peace be upon them -- was held today at the esteemed leader's headquarters in holy Mecca. Our correspondent in Mecca sent the following report: [Unidentified correspondent] Dear listeners of the midday news bulletin of the Voice of the Islamic Republic of Iran [IRI], I am speaking to you from the esteemed leader's headquarters in holy Mecca. The third Ahl al-Bayt conference was held here today, attended by a group of cultural, scientific, and religious personalities from Iran and other Islamic countries. Various speakers from the IRI, Iraq, Afghanistan, Nigeria, Turkey, Azerbaijan and the United States addressed the audience on the social circumstances of the Ahl al-Bayt followers throughout the world, and their struggle against tyranny and dictatorship. Despite the restrictions imposed by the Saudi police, the esteemed leader's headquarters is witnessing a constant flow of Iranian and non-Iranian pilgrims, and the ceremonies held inside the headquarters are warmly welcomed.
