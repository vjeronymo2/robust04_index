940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / Lock & Co wins NatWest Export Award for small businesses Michael Sydney, export director of Lock & Co, a hat shop in central London which was founded in 1676. The company was yesterday presented with the NatWest Export Award for small businesses, given to companies with an annual turnover of less than Pounds 1m. Mr Sydney said turnover had increased by about 350 per cent - to Pounds 500,000 - over the past two years. He said the best-selling lines were tweed caps, which are popular in Italy, France, Germany and Belgium, and felt trilbies, which Italians are particularly keen on. Americans and the Japanese sometimes buy bowlers, Mr Sydney said, although he added: 'Don't ask me what they use them for.'
