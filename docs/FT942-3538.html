940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: BA chief pay rise In his first full year as chairman of British Airways, Sir Colin Marshall received a 19 per cent increase in his total pay, from Pounds 663,830 to Pounds 788,019. During the 12 months to March 31 BA's pre-tax profits rose 63 per cent to Pounds 301m, against Pounds 185m. Sir Colin, the highest paid director, received an increase to Pounds 492,662 (Pounds 429,949) in his basic salary with a further performance related Pounds 61,750 this time. Of the comparative figure Pounds 86,691 related to his period from being appointed chairman in February 1993.
