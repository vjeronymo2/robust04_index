BFN [Text] Washington, 13 May. An agreement in the sphere of military conversion was signed during the visit by Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov to the United States. US Defense Secretary William Perry described the document, which is first in the history of Kiev-Washington relations, as a historic one. The agreement foresees a joint venture between the American company Westinghouse and Khartron production association in Kharkov. The Ukrainian-American enterprise will develop and produce high technology control systems for all types of electric power stations.
