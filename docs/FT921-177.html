920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Chambers forge links with Romania - In a Nutshell The Association of British Chambers of Commerce (ABCC)is sending a delegation to Bucharest to help develop the Romanian chamber system. The delegation, including representatives from Birmingham, London and Sheffield, will establish links with individual Romanian chambers, help with training, and provide advice on how to establish income-generating activities.
