May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BEST MEDICINE ANYONE WOULD WANT

Re Diane Haithman's "The Industry's Debt to Lucy," April 30:

Proverbs 15:30 says, "A cheerful look brings joy to the heart, and good news gives health to the bones." Proverbs 17:22 says, "A cheerful heart is good medicine. . . ."

Assuredly, Lucille Ball brought joy to hearts, health to bones and the best medicine anyone would want -- down-to-earth comedy.

A. MANRIQUEZ

La Mirada
