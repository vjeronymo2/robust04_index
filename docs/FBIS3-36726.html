Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 8 (SNARK) -- The financial policy of the Armenian Government inspires an optimism because it is aimed to a decrease of the budget deficit. Mohammed Shadman, the head of the IMF's delegation, said it during the meeting with Grant Bagratyan, the prime-minister of Armenia. Press-Center of the Armenian Government informs SNARK the representatives of IMF assured that IMF will support the financial policy of Armenia. Meanwhile, recently, Armen Egiazaryan, the minister of economy of Armenia, said to SNARK's reporter that till IMF abstains from monetary relief for Armenia, fearing that this relief will go for military purposes.
