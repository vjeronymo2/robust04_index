BFN [Report by J.J. Pinilla from the "National Newscast"] [Text] A new narcocassette was just disclosed, and those who sent it assured have us that it contains new evidence on the involvement of drug funds in the political campaigns. Here is a report by J.J. Pinilla: [Begin Pinilla recording] Attention. A communique and a cassette have just been dropped off at the "National Newscast" reception desk. In this cassette, a group called Honorable Colombians reveals two messages: The first message includes statements by journalist Alberto Giraldo and Gilberto Rodriguez Orejuela; it deals with a meeting between Giraldo and Santiago Medina, Samper's campaign treasurer. In this meeting Gilberto Rodriguez Orejuela confirms that the Cali Cartel funded Samper's campaign. The second message contains statements by Alberto Giraldo and Miguel Rodriguez Orejuela. The topic revolves around the, quote, honorable, unquote, General Miguel Maza Marquez's receiving money from the Cali Cartel. The communique was also sent to the Supreme Court of Justice, the Prosecutor General's Office, the Attorney General's Office, all the media, and the French and U.S. Embassies. The "National Newscast" has not yet disclosed the contents of the cassette because the voices of the people in it have not been identified. [end recording]
