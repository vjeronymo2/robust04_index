Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Tucuman -- While the provincial Congress held an emergency session to condemn the attack against it by a group of workers of La Florida Sugar Mill, Judge Alfonso Zottoli ordered the arrest of Miguel Angel Brito, one of the Tucuman CGT [General Confederation of Labor] secretary generals; Jorge Gonzalez, secretary general of La Florida Mill labor union, and Enrique Romero, local Justicialist Party leader who hopes to occupy a seat in the Tucuman Congress. These three are considered the masterminds of the incidents which took place on 17 March. [passage omitted]
