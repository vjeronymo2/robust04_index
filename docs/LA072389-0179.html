July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ARCHITECTS SEEK ENTRIES FOR DESIGN COMPETITION

The Los Angeles chapter of the American Institute of Architects is calling for entries for its 1989 Design Awards competition, established by the chapter in 1975 to honor excellence in design.

Submissions for the annual competition may include buildings, additions, remodels/renovations, historic preservation, urban design or planning and interior architectural projects designed by AIA/LA members or Los Angeles projects by architects from other AIA chapters.

Deadline for entry application is Aug. 4; submission packages must be in the chapter office by 2 p.m., Sept. 3.

Entry forms and information on the competition are available through AIA/LA at (213) 380-4595.
