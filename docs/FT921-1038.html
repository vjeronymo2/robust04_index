920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Chelsea and Cabra agree on deferment Cabra Estates, the heavily indebted property company, and Chelsea Football Club have agreed to defer the completion date for the latter's Pounds 22.85m purchase of its Stamford Bridge ground. Following Chelsea's appeal to the High Court, the original date - March 26 - has been deferred to a date seven days after publication of the Court's decision.
