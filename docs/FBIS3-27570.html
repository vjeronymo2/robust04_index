Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 21 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li lanqing met here today with a delegation from the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Senate of Paraguay headed by Committee Chairman Carlos Alberto Gonzalez. The visitors arrived here last Friday [18 February] as guests of the Foreign Affairs Committee of China's National People's Congress. They are also scheduled to visit the cities of Xian, Shenzhen and Shanghai.
