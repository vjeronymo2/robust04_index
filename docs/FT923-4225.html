920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Sharp decline to Pounds 0.35m at WSP WSP Holdings, the consulting engineer, saw profits fall to Pounds 352,000 pre-tax in the six months to June 30. The decline from the comparable Pounds 613,000 came on turnover of Pounds 5.01m (Pounds 5.27m). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.1p, payable from earnings of 2.4p (4p) per share.
