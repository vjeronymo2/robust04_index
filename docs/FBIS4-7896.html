BFN [Text] Litomysl, East Bohemia, April 5 (CTK) -- President Vaclav Havel is staying at his private residence outside Prague to recover from a virus infection complicated by pneumonia. Presidential spokesman Ladislav Spacek said the president was trying to get better in time for the meeting of Central European presidents taking place here April 15-16. Spacek said the president was recovering as expected and had canceled all his plans in order to ensure he recovered completely. His participation in the presidential summit, however, depends on several medical tests which are still being run.
