Language: Spanish Article Type:CSO [Article by Ignacio Gutierrez A.] [Excerpts] Yesterday, the Office of the Attorney General of the Republic [PGR] dealt the international drug traffic a severe blow by seizing 1.5 tons of cocaine, 8.7 tons of packed marijuana, 3.5 kg of heroin, and 1.2 kg of opium gum, with a combined worth of approximately 5 million new pesos. During the operations conducted by Federal Judicial Police [PJF] forces in several of the Federation's states, 20 persons presumed guilty of crimes against health were arrested. Eight vehicles used to commit these crimes were seized, including a speedboat employed to transport drugs. The confiscated drugs would have put 100 million doses into circulation. [passage omitted] The PGR branch office in Sonora announced that, at the highway checkpoint (Precos) set up on the outskirts of the town of Hermosillo, Arturo Huerta Castaneda and Leopoldo Guzman Ortega were arrested. They were carrying 22 kg and 500 grams of cocaine on a Mexican Interstate Line passenger bus. The drugs were bound for the border at Tijuana, Baja California. [passage omitted] As for marijuana, the largest seizure was made in the Sonora municipality of Benjamin Hill, at a highway checkpoint, where the PJF located a shipment of 5,595 kg of the grass. It had been packed and concealed in freight car 104533, owned by Ferronales, which was making the run between Guadalajara and Mexicali, Baja California. The PGR's state branch office revealed that, as a result of an anonymous phone call reporting the transport of said shipment, the cannabis contained in 897 packages was confiscated. Furthermore, federal agents arrested Jose Francisco Arevalo Alvarado, who was driving a Federal Public Service Torton truck bearing the firm name "Autotransportes Valladolid," with domicile in Morelia, Michoacan. The vehicle was stopped when its load was noted to be overweight. The driver claimed to be carrying earthenware to the town of Reynosa, Tamaulipas. However, during the inspection 78 packages of cannabis were found, with a gross weight of two tons and 727 grams. [passage omitted]
