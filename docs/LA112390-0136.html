November 23, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

TITANS PLAY NORTHRIDGE

The Cal State Fullerton women's basketball team opens the season at 6 tonight against Cal State Northridge in the first round of the Titan Gatorade Classic in Titan Gym.

Southwest Texas State plays Oregon State at 8 p.m., and the winners will meet in Saturday's 8 p.m. championship game. The losers will play at 6 p.m. Saturday.
