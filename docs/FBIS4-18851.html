BFN [Article signed LVE (CET): "Crisis -- Emergency Aid for Rwanda"] [Excerpt] Brussels, 11 Apr (BELGA) -- "The European Commission (EC) unblocked emergency aid worth ECU500,000 (20 million Belgian francs) to help the victims of the fresh wave of interethnic violence that is sweeping through the country," announced a EC spokesman on Monday [11 April]. This aid would go toward the dispatching of medical and surgical equipment to be placed at the disposal of teams of Belgian "Medecins Sans Frontieres" [Doctors Wtihout Borders] who are already there on the ground. "This equipment was already sent on its way Saturday from Rotterdam airport," specified the spokesman. "In fact, it would be the first of several consignments of emergency aid which would follow in the days or weeks to come," he added. [passage omitted]
