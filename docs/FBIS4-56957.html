BFN [Text] Paulo Sampaio, vice president of the Brasilia Federal Policemen Union, fears the Federal Police strike may get out of control unless the government meets the policemen demands. The institution has been on strike for 50 days. Federal policemen countrywide want salaries equal to those of the Brasilia civil police. The difference between the salaries of the two institutions surpasses 200 percent. Federal Police Director Wilson Romao has asserted that the government will provide the union leaders with a proposal today.
