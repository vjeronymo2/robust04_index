BFN [Text] It became known that on 11 and 12 April, Abkhazian separatists and mercenaries looted and burned down about 25 dwelling houses in the village of Pichora in Gali Rayon. Several defenseless elderly people were brutally killed. The unrestrained actions of Abkhazian militants has reached its peak while negotiations on a peaceful settlement of the conflict are under way. The Gudauta grouping has no power to control criminal activities of hired murderers and looters.
