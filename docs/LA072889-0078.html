July 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Audrey Hepburn will be back on the big screen in Steven Speilberg's upcoming film "Always." The Amblin Entertainment production for Universal Pictures/United Artists stars Richard Dreyfuss, Holly Hunter, John Goodman and Brad Johnson. A contemporary love story set against the background of fighting forest fires, the film is scheduled for a December release. Hepburn in recent years has served as a special good-will ambassador for UNICEF. She won a best-actress Oscar in 1953 for "Roman Holiday." ALEENE MacMINN
