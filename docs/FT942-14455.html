940420 FT 20 APR 94 / UK Company News: River & Merc Extra River & Mercantile Extra Income Trust had a net asset value of 125.75p per ordinary share at end-March 1994 against 104.74p a year earlier. The value per zero dividend preference share was 107.1p. Net available revenue for the half year improved to Pounds 1.71m (Pounds 1.35m) for earnings of 4.6p (3.65p) per share. An unchanged second interim dividend of 1.96875p is declared, making 3.9375p (same) so far.
