930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Capital Industries Pounds 1.9m acquisition Capital Industries is acquiring Arthur Wollacott, a printer and converter of packaging materials, for Pounds 1.9m cash. The acquisition represents a further step in the development of Capital Industries' industrial activities. AWC operates in a related area to that of Samuel Jones, which makes and converts laminated paper products. For the year 1992 AWC increased turnover from Pounds 2.82m to Pounds 3.1m and pre-tax profits from Pounds 299,000 to Pounds 362,000.
