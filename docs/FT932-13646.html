930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Bourne End cuts losses to Pounds 1.15m Bourne End Properties, which owns and manages investment properties, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.42m to Pounds 1.15m in 1992. Net rental income totalled Pounds 3.47m (Pounds 3.38m) but there were no profits this time from property sales (Pounds 647,000). Losses per share amounted to 11.2p (13.7p) and an unchanged dividend of 1p is recommended. At December 31 the property portfolio was professionally revalued and net asset value per share was 73p (103p). The ultimate holding company is Vasella Holdings.
