Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services today announced that two citizens were assassinated yesterday at the hands of armed individuals. The first was called Nadhir Ammar, 62 years mudjahid [liberation war veteran] who lived in the municipality of Boukaid, province of Tissemsilt. As for the second, he was called Aouni Nasser and was 54 years old and lived in Benkhedda, province of Tiaret. On the other hand, the security services announced that they had killed five members of the armed groups yesterday in Bouira and Oran. In parallel to the escalation of the assassination operations aimed at various sections of society, the French language HORIZON newspaper today launched an urgent appeal to Cheikh Mohamed Sahnoun, chairman of the Islamic Call League, urging him to abandon his current silence and to work for an end to bloodshed in Algeria.
