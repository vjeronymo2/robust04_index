Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The investigation into the criminal case instituted with regard to the organization of mass unrest on 3-4 October 1993 in the capital is continuing. This is set out in the statement from the information and public relations center of Russia's General Procuracy. It is noted in the report that the investigation has not yet been completed of the circumstances regarding the infliction of material damage, which, according to the legislation, is subject to compensation by those who are guilty of it. The investigation also needs to be completed regarding individuals who took advantage of the situation that had taken shape in Moscow from 21 September to 4 October 1933 and committed thefts, acts of violence, deliberate hooliganism, and other breaches of the law. The general procuracy stressed that if those amnestied take the path of unlawful action again, it will use all the powers granted to it by the law that has come into force where there are conditions of unavoidable liability.
