930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, landed selective demand at easier rates. Prices for better liquoring East African teas declined by upto 10p with quality. Mediums opened 4p to 6p lower but weakened further towards the close. Plainer descriptions also showed an easier tendency. Offshore only fair demand at irregularly lower levels. The highest price realised this week was 177p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 150p/kg, nom good medium 138p/kg, medium 132p/kg, low medium 102p/kg.
