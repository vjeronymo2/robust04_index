BFN [Text] British Prime Minister John Major has conveyed his answer to President Glavkos Kliridhis' letter about negotiations at the UN Security Council regarding Butrus-Ghali's report. According to a reliable source, Major's letter is positive and ends the president's fears and concerns. The president sent personal letters to U.S. President Bill Clinton and Major expressing his intention to resign and declare early elections if the Security Council decided to continue talks on the confidence-building measures.
