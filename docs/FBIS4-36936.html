BFN [Text] The president of the Republic, Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir, met today in his office at the Republican Palace with the ambassador of the Republic of Iran to Khartoum, Majid Kamal. The Iranian ambassador said the meeting was held on the occasion of the conclusion of his mission in Khartoum while Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir assured him of the strong bilateral relations between Sudan and the Republic of Iran. The Iranian ambassador added that they touched, during his meeting, his stay in Sudan and the serious efforts exerted by the two sides for strengthening and progressing relations between the two countries. Ustadh Majid Kamal concluded that he hoped Sudan-Iranian bilateral relations to witness further progresses for the benefit of the peoples of the two countries.
