920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Powell Duffryn pays Dollars 8m for US group Powell Duffryn has acquired Peabody Engineering Corporation of the US from an affiliate of the Pullman Company for Dollars 8m (Pounds 4.5m) cash. Peabody, which becomes part of the Hamworthy Engineering group, is an established supplier of combustion equipment and related systems in North American and selected international markets. It has annual sales of Dollars 15m.
