920302 FT 02 MAR 92 / International Company News: American Express and Banamex deal AMERICAN Express and Banamex, Mexico's largest bank, have formed a joint venture, known as Banamsa, to provide financial services to the fast-growing Mexican market, writes Damian Fraser. The new company will start by offering in Mexico an American Express gold card, co-branded with the Banamex name. Mr Roberto Hernandez, the president of Banamex, hoped 500,000 customers would subscribe to the card within three years. Banamex will market the new card in its 725 branches.
