BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 May (MENA)--Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and head of the Palestinian side to the talks with Israel, has stated that the negotiations between the Palestinian and Israeli sides on the size of the Jericho area will continue. 'Arafat today expressed some reservations when he signed the annexes pertaining to the Jericho area.
