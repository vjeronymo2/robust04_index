Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN ["Statement" issued by the Provisional Committee for the Emergency Situation in West Georgia on 28 March] [Text] Despite numerous warnings from the local bodies of the Provisional Committee for the Emergency Situation in West Georgia, some groups and individuals from the population forcibly displaced last November and December made willful decisions to return to their homes, which ended tragically. Abkhaz separatists killed, insulted, and robbed thousands of people. According to the recent information, certain people are still calling on the refugees from the Gali Rayon and other parts of Abkhazia to return to their homes in Abkhazia. The provisional committee is making this authoritative statement urging people not to be guided by such provocations. The committee warns categorically all forcibly displaced persons to stop entering the territory occupied by the separatists until the government adopts a relevant resolution.
