BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] There are many rumors about Russia's position in the Yugoslav conflict. Specifically, today the Foreign Ministry denied a report on a possible severance of diplomatic relations between Moscow and Belgrade. Yevgeniy Podkolzin, commander of Russian airborne landing troops, described as speculations reports on a withdrawal of the Russian battalion of Blue Helmets from Sarajevo. He said that this issue has not even been discussed. Also, a group of Russian servicemen was dispatched there yesterday, and today it was followed by another one. Russian Blue Berets have been serving with UN peace-keeping forces in Yugoslavia for over two years. Two Russian battalions are stationed in the eastern sector and in Sarajevo area. Now their personnel is being relieved, which is a normal rotation. Two hundred paras are replacing their comrades in the hottest spot in the world. They have left for Yugoslavia from Ryazan.
