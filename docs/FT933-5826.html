930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: London Finance net assets increase Net asset value per share of London Finance & Investment Group, the investment finance and management concern, expanded to 31.93p as at June 30, compared with 19.77p a year earlier and 20p at December 31 1992. After tax of Pounds 40,000 against Pounds 58,000, available profit amounted to Pounds 79,000 for the six months to end-June, compared with losses of Pounds 98,000. Earnings per share were 0.32p (0.4p losses). As at August 28 the market value of fixed and current asset listed investments was Pounds 7.97m.
