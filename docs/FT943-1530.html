940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Highcroft slightly lower at Pounds 597,000 Highcroft Investment Trust, a financial trust, reported slightly lower pre-tax profits of Pounds 597,000 for the six months to June 30, against Pounds 611,000 last time. Earnings per share were 7.4p (7.8p) including gains on assets disposals or 6.9p (same) excluding these. The interim dividend has been stepped up to 2p (1.9p).
