BFN [Text] Beijing, April 11 (XINHUA) -- Chen Xitong, member of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC) Central Committee and secretary of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee, met with a delegation from the Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV) here today. The delegation, headed by Nguyen Tan Dung, member of the CPV Central Committee and secretary of the CPV Kien Giang Provincial Committee, arrived here April 9 as guests of the CPC. The delegation is also scheduled to visit South China's Hainan and Guangdong Provinces.
