March 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

'Alright' for Janet: Janet Jackson's new single "Alright" is available in stores this week, and the "Alright" video premieres on MTV.

Jackson pays homage to the Broadway musicals of the '40s and '50s in the video. Cab Calloway and Cyd Charisse make cameo performances. "Alright" is the fourth single from the quadruple platinum LP "Rhythm Nation 1814." BETH KLEID
