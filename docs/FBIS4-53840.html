BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 20 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- O Hyong-chin, vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) and chairman of its Osaka prefectural headquarters, and other delegates of Chongnyon in Kinki District called at the central office of the Japan Social-Democratic Party on May 20 as regards the unjustifiable suppression of the Osaka prefectural organisation of Chongnyon by the Japanese police and handed a letter of request addressed to the party to the chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the party, Tomiichi Murayama. Chairman Murayama said the search of the Osaka prefectural organisation of Chongnyon must not be repeated and his party will in the future examine a countermeasure and make active efforts for a stop to the suppression of Chongnyon at the Diet and at various opportunities. Delegates of Chongnyon in the Kinki District met in Tokyo on May 19 and 20 Japanese Dietmen from the district and Koreans under the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon staged a protest against the police on May 16 and 17, strongly demanding an immediate stop to the unjust political suppression of Chongnyon and an apology for it and an immediate return of the things confiscated in the search.
