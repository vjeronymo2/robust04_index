BFN [Excerpts] Mr. Michael Heseltine, president of the Board of Trade, was received this afternoon in Jeddah by the minister of post, telephone, and telegraph, Dr. Alawi Darwish Kayyal. The talks covered topics related to communications and telecommunications. [passage omitted] The minister of commerce, Dr. Sulayman Sulaym, at his office this morning conferred with the British president of the Board of Trade. During the meeting issues of mutual interest and means of promoting the balanced trade relations between the two countries were discussed. [passage omitted] Mr. Heseltine was also received by the minister of industry and electricity, Engineer 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Zamil, at his office this morning. During the meeting facets of cooperation between the Kingdom and Britain were discussed as well as means of reinforcing industrial cooperation through the establishment of more joint industrial ventures between the two countries. [passage omitted] His Excellency the guest and his accompanying delegation took a tour in the permanent exhibition at the headquarters of the Ministry of Industry to acquaint themselves on samples of products that reflect the level reached by Saudi industry.
