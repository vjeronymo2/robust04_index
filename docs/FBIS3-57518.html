Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk has received Michel-Yves Peissik, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the French Republic to Ukraine. Issues on the further development of Ukrainian-French relations and the participation of the two states in the world processes were discussed during the conversation, which was held in an atmosphere of an open exchange of opinions and mutual understanding. The ambassador said that, in France, they highly assess the trilateral statement by the presidents of Ukraine, Russia, and the United States concerning nuclear disarmament. France is ready to grant security guarantees to Ukraine.
