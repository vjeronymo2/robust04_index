Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut Governor Muhammad Samih al-Sa'id has asserted that there is no militia in the governorate and that no weapons are being distributed to the citizens. In a letter to ROSE AL-YUSUF, al-Sa'id said that the governorate's security forces are combating terrorism with a high degree of efficiency, backed by various executive, political, and popular organizations as well as citizens.
