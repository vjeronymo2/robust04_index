BFN [From the "Leaks and Tips" column] [Text] In violation of electoral provisions, some PRD [Democratic Revolutionary Party] candidates in various electoral districts offered to buy votes with payments ranging from $20-$30 per vote. This was also an old practice of ousted General Manuel Antonio Noriega's associates. The Office of the Electoral Prosecutor should immediately conduct a detailed investigation of this crime. The purchase of votes occurred in Cabuya, Anton... Parque Lefevre... Rio Abajo, as well as in David and Puerto Armuelles. [ellipses as published] At the Salinera voting table in Nata, even presidential ballots were stolen. These ballots, according to what was learned, were removed by PRD activists. Also, in Chitre, Ocu, and Parita, PRD activists protested at several voting stations with other parties. The PRD activists arrived at the time of closing of the voting tables to cast their votes, which is illegal.
