September 19, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: TRANSIENT STABBED OUTSIDE TUJUNGA HOUSE

A 44-year-old man was fatally stabbed shortly after walking out of a friend's house in Tujunga, Los Angeles police said Tuesday.

The victim, a transient, was stabbed at 9:30 p.m. in front of a house in the 10300 block of Fernglen Avenue, Detective Al Ferrand said. His name was not released because detectives had not yet notified his family.

"He had apparently just walked out to the front of the house and shortly thereafter came back in with a stab wound," Ferrand said.

The victim collapsed inside the house and died. Ferrand said detectives had not determined the motive for the slaying or located any witnesses who saw the attack or a suspect.
