BFN [Text] Moscow April 5 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree appointing Colonel-General Anatoliy Yefimovich Safonov first deputy director of the Federal Service of Counterintelligence of the Russian Federation. The same decree relieves Anatoliy Safonov from the post of the deputy director of the Federal Service of Counterintelligence. This was reported by the press service of the president of the Russian Federation.
