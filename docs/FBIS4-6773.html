BFN [Text] According to the Agriculture Ministry, more than 138,100 hectares of dry-season rice, or over 86 percent of the planned target, has been planted nationwide by 4 April. Outstanding provinces and cities in the planting effort included Takeo, Kandal, Prey Veng, and Kompong Cham Provinces. The report also said that during this 1993-1994 dry season, our peasants planted 7,900 hectares of rice fewer than in the previous year. Many factors, such as [words indistinct] and late and prolonged harvest of rainy-season rice, delayed this year's dry-season rice planting. During the same period, over 50,000 hectares of dry-season rice were harvested nationwide and the average yield was about two metric tons per hectare.
