May 18, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

DEFENSE SPENDING HELPS FEW AREAS

Nearly three out of four Americans live in communities that pay more in taxes to support the Pentagon than they get back in defense spending, a study released today said.

The study, "Bankrupting America," found that 321 of the nation's 435 congressional districts are net-loss areas in the trade-off between defense spending and military taxes.
