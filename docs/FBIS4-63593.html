BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigoriy Arslanov and Vyacheslav Tomilin] [Text] Beijing May 23 TASS -- A second Russo-Chinese summit has been scheduled to take place in Moscow early in Autumn. Chinese Chairman Jiang Zemin is expected to arrive in Russia at the invitation of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, the Russian Ambassador to China Igor Rogachev told ITAR-TASS on Monday. Deputy Premier of the Chinese State Council and Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen is expected to arrive in Moscow at the end of June to discuss practical matters connected with preparations for the Russo-Chinese summit.
