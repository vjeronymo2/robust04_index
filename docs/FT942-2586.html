940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Faupel Trading The lack of an exceptional charge resulted in Faupel Trading, the USM-quoted textiles importer, reporting pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.02m for the year to March 31, against losses of Pounds 59,000 restated for FRS 3. Turnover was Pounds 27.8m, against Pounds 31.4m including Pounds 2.63m from discontinued activities. Operating profits were little changed at Pounds 1.54m (Pounds 1.59m) but last year there was an exceptional charge of Pounds 884,000. Earnings per share were 6.68p (losses 4.73p). The dividend is held at 4.9p with an unchanged 3.05p final proposed.
