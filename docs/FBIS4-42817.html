BFN [Text] Geneva, June 8 (AFP) -- Both parties to the Bosnian conflict, the Bosnian Serbs and the Moslem-Croat side, Wednesday [8 June] agreed to observe a month-long truce, officials said. Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic told reporters his side would observe the accord, which would be overseen by the United Nations Protection Force. Diplomatic sources said the Moslem-Croat federation had also signed the accord.
