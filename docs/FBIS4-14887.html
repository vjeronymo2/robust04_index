BFN [Text] Moscow, 5 Apr (INA) -- 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, met here today with Mr. Boris Kolokolov, deputy foreign minister of the Russian Federation. They discussed Iraq's fulfillment of all the obligations specified by UN Security Council resolutions and the need for the council to fulfill its obligations as stipulated by Paragraph 22 of Resolution 687 and to lift the economic sanctions. 'Abd-al-Ghafur noted the positive development in the attitudes of some permanent members of the Security Council, including Russia. He called for the Russian stand to be developed to a more effective footing so as to end the blockade and the hardships endured by the Iraqi people. The Russian minister told 'Abd-al-Ghafur his country was eager to develop its ties with Iraq in the best interests of the two friendly nations. The session was attended by Mr. 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Hashimi, director of the Ba'th Party's Foreign Relations Bureau, and Russian Foreign Ministry officials.
