930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Vinten makes US acquisition Vinten Group, the Suffolk-based camera mountings and systems, surveillance and electro-optics company, has acquired Total Spectrum Manufacturing, a US maker and supplier of remote controlled camera systems, for Dollars 9.3m (Pounds 6.2m). The consideration of 1.76m new ordinary shares are being placed with institutional investors at 353p per share. TSM, a New York State private concern, made pre-tax profits of Dollars 24,705 on turnover of Dollars 5.4m in the 1991/92 year.
