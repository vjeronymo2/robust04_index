Remaining sections are unchanged. Changes to 46 CFR Subpart 25.25 Life Preservers and Other Lifesaving Equipment. Section 25.25&hyph;5␣Life Preservers and Other Lifesaving Equipment Required (f)(1) The text previously published as §25.25&hyph;5(f)(2) has been moved to this paragraph to make it clear only Type V commercial hybrid PFD's will be required to be worn as stated in the revised §25.25&hyph;5(f)(3). (f)(2) The text previously published as §25.25&hyph;5(f)(3) has been moved to this paragraph to make it clear only Type V commercial hybrid PFD's will be required to be worn as stated in the revised §25.25&hyph;5(f)(3). (f)(3) The Coast Guard proposes to revise the Scope in 46 CFR subpart 160.077&hyph;1(b) to indicate that hybrid PFD's approved as Type I devices do not have the restriction REQUIRED TO BE WORN. In keeping with the changes to the scope in 46 CFR subpart 160.077&hyph;1(b), the text (originally published as §25.25&hyph;5(f)(1)) will be moved to paragraph (f)(3) and will be changed to show that Type I commercial hybrid PFD's do not have to be worn to meet carriage requirements.
