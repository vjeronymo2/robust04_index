October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ELDERLY CALIFORNIA COUPLE KILLED IN FALL FROM TRAIN

A California couple were killed in a fall from a moving Amtrak train early Saturday, authorities said.

Investigators were trying to determine the events that led to the deaths of Arthur Kohfeld, 87, and his wife, Dorthea, 76, of Reedley, Calif.

The train was traveling at 70 m.p.h. when the incident occurred near Middle Point in northwest Ohio, the Van Wert County Sheriff's Department said.
