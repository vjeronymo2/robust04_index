August 24, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MADAGASCAR SWAP

I am almost certain to be the only one from Madagascar to live in Southern California. So when I saw the article Aug. 4 about the $1 million debt-for-nature swap, I decided to write.

Thank you America.

I know there must have been official thanks, but this is from a simple citizen who often feels sorry for his country. The idea is good. I thought of other ones: how about debt-for industrialization, debt-for education, debt-for transportation, debt-for-hygiene?

Madagascar is a precious country, and not enough people know that. Madagascar needs help. That's why I came to study here. Thanks again.

DOMINIQUE

ANDRIAMANANTOA

Huntington Beach
