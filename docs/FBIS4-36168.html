BFN [Text] This morning, Acting Prime Minister brother Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar received a delegation from the International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC] led by (Michelle Canio), the general ICRC delegate for Middle Eastern and North African affairs, and (Arthold Leotold), the regional ICRC commissioner for the Arabian Peninsula. During the meeting, the delegation explained the ICRC's readiness to assist the Yemeni Red Cross and the Yemeni Government by dispatching medicines and medical supplies to face the extraordinary circumstances. The brother acting prime minister thanked the delegation for this aid and promised to facilitate its mission.
