910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Br Assets Trust net asset value rises British Assets Trust, investment trust, had a net asset value of 92.2p per share at March 31 1991 compared with 88.6p a year earlier. Total revenue for the six months to end-March was slightly down at Pounds 16.06m (Pounds 16.72m) and net revenue came through at Pounds 8.05m against Pounds 8.17m for earnings per share of 2.07p (2.1p). A second quarterly dividend of 1p (0.95p) is declared to make 2p (1.875p) for the half.
