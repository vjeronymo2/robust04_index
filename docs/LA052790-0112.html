May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THIS WEEK

NINE CEREMONIES: More than 6,400 students will receive degrees during nine ceremonies over three days at the 41st commencement exercises at Cal State Long Beach.

There will be three ceremonies a day beginning at 9 a.m. Wednesday. The final ceremony starts at 5 p.m. Friday. All are in the central quad of the campus at 1250 Bellflower Blvd., Long Beach.

GOVERNOR TO SPEAK: Gov. George Deukmejian will give the commencement address as 935 students graduate from Long Beach City College at 9:30 a.m. Thursday at the Liberal Arts Campus, 4901 E. Carson St., Long Beach.
