Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking to newsmen on 9 March after a meeting with Cuban Ambasssador Richardo Danza Sigas, Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri said they discussed Thai-Cuban relations and ways to expand the currently low volume of bilateral trade. On U.S. ecomomic sanctions against Cuba, the Cuban envoy said his country hopes that the United States will lift the sanctions as it has done with Vietnam. The foreign minister said these issues will be discussed with the Cuban foreign minister during his visit to Thailand on 11 March.
