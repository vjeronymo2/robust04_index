February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX

Compared to the '80s, when, desperate for innovation, we flogged a moribund avant-garde for signs of life, the '90s sound like an adventure in a new cultural wonderland.

I have been among The Times' sharpest critics, and I am glad the paper has made strides toward intelligent cultural journalism.

LINDA FRYE BURHAM

Santa Monica
