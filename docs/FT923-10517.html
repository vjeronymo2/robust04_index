920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / UK Company News: S Korea halves bidders for telephone franchise SOUTH Korea's Ministry of Communications yesterday eliminated three of the six consortia competing for the country's second mobile telephone franchise. The winner is expected to be selected next month. The ministry's selection board said Sunkyong, the country's seventh largest business group, was the best qualified bidder on technical and financial grounds, followed by Kolon and Pohang Iron and Steel Corporation (Posco). The consortium led by Sunkyong includes the mobile telephone operators Vodafone of the UK, GTE of the US and Hutchison Telecom of Hong Kong. Kolon, a textile group, is being backed by British Telecom and Nynex of the US. Posco, the world's third largest steelmaker is being supported by Pacific Telesis of the US and Mannesmann of Germany.
