July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

PET VACCINATIONS OFFERED

Dogs will be vaccinated against rabies for $3 cash per animal at two city parks during the city's annual low-cost anti-rabies clinic tonight.

Dog licenses will also be sold for $8.41 for spayed or neutered animals and for $16.81 for all other pets. The clinics will be held from 7 to 8:30 p.m. at Victory Park, 2575 Paloma St., and at Brookside Park, 1333 Rosemont Ave.

The clinics are sponsored by the California Veterinary Medical Assn., the Pasadena Public Health Department and the Pasadena Humane Society.
