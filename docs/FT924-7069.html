921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Alexandra Towing Alexandra Towing, Britain's biggest tugboat company, has conditionally agreed to a Pounds 52m takeover by Howard Smith, an Australian shipping group quoted on the Sydney stock exchange. Privately-owned Alexandra has 46 tugs operating towage services in UK ports and Gibraltar. In the year to December 1991 it made pre-tax profits of Pounds 5.1m on turnover of Pounds 40.4m. The recommended cash offer, which carries a loan note alternative, has been accepted by shareholders speaking for 29 per cent of the capital. It is conditional on acceptances reaching 90 per cent.
