940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Sheffield Insulations Pounds 1.2m purchase Sheffield Insulations is to buy Turton Ventures, a distributor of safety and workwear clothing products, for Pounds 1.2m. The consideration will be satisfied by a payment of Pounds 642,500 cash on completion, and Pounds 557,500 by the issue of 202,727 ordinary shares. Turton imports many of its products from east Asia. Wong Shi, Sheffield's Taiwan-based subsidiary, will act as the procurement agent for the enlarged Turton business.
