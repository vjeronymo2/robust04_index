930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / UK Company News: S&F nets Pounds 31m from ACT stake Singer & Friedlander has netted Pounds 30.8m from the sale of its entire shareholding in ACT Group, which is primarily engaged in the supply of computer software and services and the design and development of business computer applications. The deal involved the placing by Credit Lyonnais Laing with a number of UK institutions of 19.1m shares, nearly 10.4 per cent of the capital, at 161 1/2 p. In the market yesterday the shares slipped by 1p to 175p. For the year to September 30 the investment in ACT contributed some Pounds 2.7m to Singer & Friedlander pre-tax profits. Book value of the investment at that date was Pounds 18.9m.
