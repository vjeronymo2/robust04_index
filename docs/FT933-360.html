930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: William Jacks shows improvement A significant reduction in interest charges, partly offset by restructuring costs, enabled William Jacks to report pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1,000 to Pounds 65,000 in the six months to July 31. Turnover of the motor dealer - the ultimate owner of which is Johan Holdings, a Malaysian incorporated company - rose from Pounds 21.2m to Pounds 22.6m. Interest payable fell from Pounds 442,000 to Pounds 284,000, but exceptional items totalled Pounds 90,000 (nil), comprising Pounds 64,000 of redundancy costs and Pounds 26,000 of closure costs at Caxton. Losses per share were reduced to 0.22p (0.96p).
