BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 21 April, a delegation of the parliamentarians elected on 18 January 1993, accompanied by Senator Bernard Sansaricq, met with Haitian military authorities at the Army's General Headquarters. The senators in this delegation were: Osny Eugene, Romel Manigat, Margareth Martin, Amos Andre, and Gabriel Ancion. The issues discussed during this meeting are so far unknown.
