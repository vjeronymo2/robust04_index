ACTION: Notice of final actions.

SUMMARY: The purpose of this notice is to announce that between February 1, 1994 and April 30, 1994, the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region II Office, issued 3 final determinations and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection and Energy issued 1 final determination pursuant to the Prevention of Significant Deterioration of Air Quality (PSD) regulations codified at 40 CFR 52.21. In addition, EPA Region II and the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation (NYSDEC) issued 2 final determinations between October 1, 1993 and January 31, 1994. These 2 determinations were inadvertently omitted from the last publication.
