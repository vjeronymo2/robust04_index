930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Rolls-Royce car sales down a fifth WORLDWIDE sales of Rolls-Royce and Bentley cars fell by a fifth last year from 1,723 to 1,378 - nearly 60 per cent lower than the record 3,300 sales reached in 1990. But their manufacturer, Crewe-based Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, said yesterday sales had begun to show signs of an upturn in the last quarter. 'Most markets are now showing signs of limited recovery after one of the most difficult years in our company's history,' said Mr Peter Ward, chairman and chief executive of the Vickers subsidiary.
