Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the opposition in the Lok Sabha, Mr. Atal Behari Vajpayee, has said that the government should demonstrate its clarity of purpose and foil the evil designs of Pakistan on Jammu and Kashmir. In a statement in New Delhi today, the BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] leader said Mrs. Bhutto's call for a Pakistan bundh [strike] on the fifth of next month cannot cover Kashmir as it is an integral part of India.
