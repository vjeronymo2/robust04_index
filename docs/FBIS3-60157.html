Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The following is a report from FIDES news agency. The Senate Drug Trafficking Committee will carry out an inspection in Santa Cruz with Interior Ministry officials to verify the existence of an alleged DEA intelligence center in that capital. A report from a Santa Cruz newspaper says that on Kilometer 5 of the road to the Cotoca sanctuary there is a farm rented by the DEA from where it taps telephones.
