BFN [Text] Prishtina, May 18 (ATA) -- A considerable number of Serb soldiers are deployed recently in the border area between Albania and Kosova so banning the Albanian farmers from using the pasture land. They have declared it "forbidden area," because it is near the border zone. The army has blocked the roads that lead to the pasture land and conducts a severe search, prohibiting so the free pass of people. Against such measures, have reacted the Albanian political subjects of Decan commune and has appealed to the farmers to be cautious not to fall into the trap of the Serb militaries.
