Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Feb 17 (AFP)--Egyptian security forces rounded up more than 900 suspected Islamic militants in a countrywide sweep this week, the government daily AL-JUMHURIYAH reported Thursday. The 942 people arrested included 38 known militants. The raids, part of a campaign against Islamic fundamentalists battling the government, also netted 270 kilograms (595 pounds) of explosives and 920 weapons, the daily said.
