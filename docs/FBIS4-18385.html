BFN [Text] Here in the Netherlands an aircraft belonging to the Dutch airline KLM has crashed while landing at Amsterdam's Schiphol airport. At least three of the 24 people on board have been killed; 13 others were seriously injured and eight people received slight injuries. The aircraft, a Swedish-made Saab 340, had taken off from Schiphol bound for Cardiff in Wales. Shortly after departure, however, the pilot reported engine trouble and returned to the airport. While landing, the plane overshot the runway, crashed into a nearby field, and broke in two. Eyewitnesses report seeing an explosion but no fire. Air traffic to and from Schiphol was temporarily stopped after the accident.
