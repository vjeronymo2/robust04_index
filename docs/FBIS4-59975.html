BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin says he does not accept the clarifications of PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat that he was referring to a peaceful jihad during his controversial address at a mosque in Johannesburg earlier this month. Rabin says 'Arafat's explanation is far-fetched. Rabin says the episode is making the process with the Palestinians more difficult and will continue to do so in the future.
