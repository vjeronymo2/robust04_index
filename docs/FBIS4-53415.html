BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 19 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a message of greetings to Cameroonian President Paul Biya on May 18 on the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Cameroon. President Kim Il-song wished the president and people of Cameroon success in the work for national unity and the prosperity of the country and expressed the belief that friendly relations between the two countries would further develop.
