BFN [Text] Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] sources announced today that Ahmad Khalid Sayf, the secretary of the YSP branch in al-Najdah region in the Ta'izz Governorate was killed there, where the Yemeni Government is scheduled to convene today. The deceased's body, according to the sources, was found last Monday [4 April] evening close to the general reserve camp. Eyewitnesses report that Sayf was shot by unknown gunmen, who fled the scene in a getaway car.
