BFN [Text] Moscow April 7 TASS -- The Russian Government has approved a draft agreement between the Russian and Latvian Governments on the social security of officers of the Russian Armed Forces, the Russian frontier guard troops and their families, staying temporarily on the territory of the Latvian Republic, until their complete withdrawal. The draft agreement was submitted by the Russian Foreign Ministry after being coordinated with the Russian Defence Ministry, the Federal frontier guard service and other ministries and institutions concerned, and was analysed in a preliminary way jointly with the Latvian side. The Russian Foreign Ministry was assigned to sign the draft agreement on behalf of the Russian Government at the talks with Latvia after the two sides reach accord, and was allowed to make changes and amendments in it, which are not of principled importance.
