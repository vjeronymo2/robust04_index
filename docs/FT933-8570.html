930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / Finance and The Family: Tokyo market welcomes cabinet - At a Glance A new Japanese cabinet was announced this week by Prime Minister Hosokawa. Share prices rose steadily over the week with the Nikkei 225 ending Friday's trading at 20,745, compared with 20,357 at Monday's opening. Meanwhile, the yen continued its seemingly inexorable rise, reaching a post-war high of Y103/Dollars on Thursday and had risen further to Y102.5/Dollars by Friday lunchtime.
