941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Hadleigh Pounds 324,000 in the red Pre-tax losses of Pounds 324,000 were announced by Hadleigh Industries Group, the USM-quoted storage tank and trailer manufacturer, for the half year to September 30. Last time there were profits of Pounds 332,000. The result included a loss of Pounds 490,000 on the disposal of Lynton Commercial Units in September and an operating loss from that company of Pounds 325,000. Turnover improved to Pounds 15.7m (Pounds 14.7m), with Pounds 2m (Pounds 2.66m) from discontinued operations. Losses per share emerged at 3p (3.1p earnings). The interim dividend is doubled to 1p.
