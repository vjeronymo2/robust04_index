Cocaine PY1805030494 Lima Global de Television Network in Spanish 0130 GMT 18 May 94 PY1805030494 Lima Global de Television Network Spanish BFN [Text] An Army patrol captured a light Colombian airplane after a clash with its crew members. The airplane crashed in the crew's effort to escape. According to a Huallaga Front Political Command communique, the clash occurred at an illegal airfield in the San Pablo district, San Martin Department. The airplane crew members escaped but the patrol found 124 kg of cocaine base inside the airplane.
