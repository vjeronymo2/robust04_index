940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / IMF aid for Sierra Leone The International Monetary Fund has approved loans totalling Dollars 163m for Sierra Leone after lifting its six-year ban on lending to the country, Reuter reports from Washington. The loans follow payment of outstanding obligations to the lending agency facilitated by bridge financing by France, Norway and the US. In a separate transaction, the IMF said it had approved a credit of Dollars 23m to support economic reform policies in the Central African Republic over the next year.
