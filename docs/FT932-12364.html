930429 FT 29 APR 93 / Bankrupt Walker has Pounds 180m debt George Walker (left), former chairman and chief executive of the Brent Walker group, was declared bankrupt yesterday with debts of Pounds 180m. He is pictured leaving the High Court in London with his solicitor, Michael Coleman, after the decision. The judge accused Mr Walker of taking a 'cavalier' attitude towards his creditors. Report, Page 8.
