Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Talking to newsmen in Hyderabad yesterday evening, Narasimha Rao said a polarization of political parties is taking place on the basis of secular and nonsecular policies in the country. He said that is a reason why many parties are being attracted to the Congress-I. Regarding India-Pakistan ties, the prime minister reiterated that India is persuading Pakistan to give up its support to terrorists. Pakistan is not responding so far, but we will continue with our efforts. Replying to a question, Mr. Rao said that his government is committed to build a temple and the mosque at Ayodhya.
