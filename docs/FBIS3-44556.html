Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, January 4 (XINHUA) -- The Baoshan Iron and Steel Plant in Shanghai, the most modern steel producer in China, has beaten its 1993 production targets, according to latest statistics. In 1993, Baoshan produced 7.22 million tons of steel, an increase of 7.55 percent over the previous year, and 6.57 million tons of iron, an increase of 5.47 percent over the previous year. It reported a sales volume of 17.5 billion yuan and its profits and taxes grew by 110 percent. According to its officials, the plant plans to produce 7.5 million tons of steel in 1994 and products of high added value will account for one-third of the total output.
