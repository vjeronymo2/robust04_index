931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / International Company News: Fike to retire as president of Ford of Europe Ford of Europe's president, Mr William Fike, is to retire at the end of the year, when the post of president will effectively be abolished. Mr Fike's responsibilities in the areas of manufacturing, sales and product development are to be redistributed among Mr Jacques Nasser, who took over as Ford of Europe's chairman at the start of the year, and members of the business management committee, which reports to Mr Nasser. The committee includes vice-presidents such as manufacturing director Mr Albert Caspers, who is also chairman of the German company, Ford-Werke.
