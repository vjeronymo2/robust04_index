940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Chartwell cuts loss Chartwell Group, the carpet tile and cubicle manufacturer, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 51,000 for the six months to September 30. The outcome, struck on turnover of Pounds 2.83m (Pounds 3.11m), compared with Pounds 137,000 last time. Losses per share were cut from 1.5p to 0.6p.
