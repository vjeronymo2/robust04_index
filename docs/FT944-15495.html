941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Richards Group jumps to Pounds 0.12m Richards Group, the engineering concern, doubled pre-tax profits from Pounds 62,000 to Pounds 120,000 in the six months to June 30. Turnover moved ahead from Pounds 6.38m to Pounds 8.23m. Mr John Crabtree, chairman, said Steel Supports was making inroads into the European and overseas power generation markets, while Richards Engineering had secured its first substantial order in the expanding Chinese market. The interim dividend is held at 1p, payable from earnings of 1.07p (0.55p) per share.
