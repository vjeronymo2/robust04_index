921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / Third night of clashes in Lyons Six people were arrested and a dozen cars burned when mostly-Arab youths clashed with riot police for the third night running in Vaulx-en-Velin, the troubled immigrant suburb of Lyons, Reuter reports. Youths threw stones at police firing tear gas grenades after a local resident shot dead a Moroccan-born 18-year-old.
