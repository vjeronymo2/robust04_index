BFN [Text] Moscow, 31 May -- Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin has just returned to Moscow from Kazan. He spent two days on a working visit to Tatarstan. Druing the trip, he visited Naberezhnyye Chelny, where he familiarized himself with the problems of the KAMAZ motor vehicle works. The main point of today's meeting with the leadership of the republic in the capital of Tatarstan was to work out the machinery for the implementation of Russia's treaty with Tatarstan. The president of Russia and his counterpart Mintimer Shaymiyev confirmed this to journalists at the end of their meeting in the Kazan Kremlin.
