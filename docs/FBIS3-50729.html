Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Alfredo Jordan, who is a Politburo member and also minister of agriculture, said in Las Tunas that the main challenge before the more than 600,000 sector workers in Cuba is to increase production this year and progressively reduce the state subsidy to agriculture. Julian Rizo Alvarez, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Camaguey Province, at a meeting with rural leaders expressed the opinion that every Basic Cooperative Production Unit in cane agriculture should have a tractor and a plow with which to prepare the land. He explained that he defends this idea because plowing must be done rapidly for cane recovery.
