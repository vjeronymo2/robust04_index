930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / Surveyors' income shows 6% fall THE income of chartered surveyors and commercial estate agents fell by 6 per cent in the past year, says a survey published by Economic Development Briefing, a research group. The survey found that the UK's largest 3,000 property clients cut their spending on property services in the past 12 months from Pounds 903m to Pounds 843m. The fee income for agency and financial services work fell by about a quarter.
