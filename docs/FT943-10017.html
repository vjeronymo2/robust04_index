940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / Business Travel (Update): Strike averted Cathay Pacific Airways said at the weekend nearly all its rebellious pilots had accepted company contracts, averting the prospect of a strike. The Hong Kong Aircrew Officers' Association said a special meeting had decided not to vote on taking industrial action. But future action was not ruled out. Talks between the union and the airline to fix an increase in flying hours for the pilots broke down in July.
