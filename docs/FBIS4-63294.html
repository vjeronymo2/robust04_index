BFN [Text] Vilnius, May 19, BNS -- Lithuania welcomes Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha af Ugglas' position that the number of Russian troops in the Kaliningrad region must be reduced, the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry said in a press release Thursday [19 May]. "The Kaliningrad region is an anomaly in the Baltic Sea region because of the huge army and navy forces concentrated in this area," af Ugglas was quoted as saying by the Lithuanian ministry. The Swedish minister, who visited the region Thursday, reportedly expressed hope that Russia will demonstrate its preparedness to reduce its military potential in Kaliningrad. The Lithuanian ministry said efforts to raise the issue of Kaliningrad's demilitarization on an international level and to integrate the region into international cooperation processes would significantly contribute to enhancing security in the Baltic Sea region.
