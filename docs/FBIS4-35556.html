BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA) -- 'Umar al-Muntasir, Libyan secretary of the General People's Committee, met with Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati today on the sidelines of the Non-Aligned Movement Conference currently being held in Cairo. During the meeting, they discussed bilateral relations, coordinating their countries' stances at the conference, developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina, and restructuring the United Nations.
