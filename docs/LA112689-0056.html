November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BROKERS' BUFFET TO BENEFIT HOMELESS

A fund-raiser for St. Joseph's Center for the Homeless in Venice will be held by Beverly Hills real estate brokers Wednesday from 6 to 9 p.m. at Jimmy's Restaurant, 201 S. Moreno Drive, Beverly Hills.

The $125-a-person benefit, including drinks and a buffet, will be underwritten by Cecelia Waeschle, a broker with Rodeo Realty, and her husband, TWA pilot Clifford Waeschle.
