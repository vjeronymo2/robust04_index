Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Jan. 26 KYODO -- Eighteen people were sentenced to death for crimes ranging from murder to robbery and rape, as violent crimes in China's capital continue to increase as the economy develops, Chinese state press reported Tuesday [25 January]. According to the BEIJING DAILY [BEIJING RIBAO], the 18 were sentenced by the Beijing Intermediate Court on Monday in what is believed to be one of the largest instances in Beijing of a group of convicted criminals receiving death sentences in recent memory. Twelve of those sentenced will appeal while the other six were executed immediately, the paper said. Many of those sentenced were unemployed or outsiders who had migrated to the capital, while five of the convicted were part of a group which committed several armed robberies in 1991-92, the paper said.
