Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Mozambican Government team to the Cease-Fire Commission [CCF] has asked the CCF chairman to ensure that the date for the beginning of the demobilization process be set as soon as possible. In a note it sent to the Radio Mozambique news desk, the government team to the CSC has said that soldiers confined to the assembly areas that opened in December have already been there for more than eight weeks, adding that that is much too long.
