920313 FT 13 MAR 92 / Observer: Rough A golfer, teeing off, is boasting to his partner about his marvellous Japanese-made golf ball. 'Hit it into the water, and it floats. It glows in the dark so you can see it, and it emits an audible whine so that it's easy to detect in the long rough.' 'Where did you get it?', asks his partner, visibly impressed. 'I found it.'
