BFN [Text] Regarding reports on terrorism, the Algerian national Abdelkrim Boutrif appeared before a French magistrate today and was remanded in custody. Boutrif was arrested on the French-German border in possession of a quantity of weapons and ammunition. In the trunk of his car police discovered over 129 sticks of dynamite, about 100 detonators, some 3,000 bullets, three automatic pistols, night vision binoculars, and 14 two-way radios. Abdelkrim Boutrif is 34 and an activist in the banned Islamic Salvation Front. His mission was to drive the car and its contents all the way to Spain, from where this arsenal would have been taken to Algeria.
