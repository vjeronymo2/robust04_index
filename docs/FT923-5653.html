920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / A Russian Ilyushin IL-76 burns outside Kabul A Russian Ilyushin IL-76 military transport aircraft burns outside Kabul yesterday, after rebels ignored a temporary ceasefire and fired a salvo of rockets into the airport. The aircraft had been sent to evacuate Russian diplomats and their families from the besieged capital. Four Russian commandos who were aboard the aircraft were injured escaping from the blaze.
