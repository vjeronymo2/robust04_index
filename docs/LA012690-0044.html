January 26, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Donahue's Moore Interview Draws Crowds: More than 1,000 people lined up Wednesday morning in Flint, Mich., for tickets to Phil Donahue's Monday show, in which he will interview Michael Moore, maker of the General Motors Corp. documentary "Roger & Me." The satirical documentary shows Moore trying unsuccessfully to interview GM Chairman Roger B. Smith on plant closings and layoffs in Flint. Police helped disperse a crowd of people who waited all night in hopes of seeing Donahue and Moore but were turned away when the 1,266 free tickets ran out. SHAUNA SNOW
