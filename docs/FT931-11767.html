930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Fish diseases TAYLOR WOODROW CON STRUCTION SOUTHERN has won a Pounds 7.4m contract to build a new fish disease laboratory in Weymouth for the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food. The new laboratory, one of three outstations of MAFF's directorate of fisheries research centre, will be the most advanced of its type in the world. Completion is scheduled for early next year.
