BFN [Text] Tiraspol, BASAPRESS, 1/4/1994 -- Informed sources from the Russian Defense Ministry told BASAPRESS that is has been recently decided to create a military militia battalion comprising several hundreds people in the 14th Army. The former Tiraspol militiamen are now joining the battalion. According to local observers, the force used by the Dniester ministries of interior and security as well as the difference in salaries made many people quit the Ministry of Interior and join this battalion.
