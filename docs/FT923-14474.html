920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / International Company News: Expansion sells La Tribune EXPANSION, the French publishing group, has sold La Tribune de L'Expansion, its economic daily newspaper, to Desfosses International, another publisher, writes Alice Rawsthorn. Desfosses will in September merge La Tribune with its own paper, La Cote. La Tribune has for some time been struggling for circulation and advertising revenue in the competitive French market.
