Whiteman, Secretary, Kansas Department of Social and Rehabilitation Services, 6th Floor, North Wing, 915 S.W. Harrison St., Topeka, KS 66612 Dear Ms. Whiteman: I am responding to your request for reconsideration of the decision to disapprove Kansas State Plan Amendment (SPA) 93&hyph;25. Kansas submitted SPA 93&hyph;25 which would increase the copayment amount for the general hospital inpatient services and inpatient free standing psychiatric facility services from $25 to $325 per admission. The issues are whether Kansas SPA 93&hyph;25 adheres to the Federal law at section 1902(a)(14) of the Social Security Act (the Act) (referencing section 1916 of the Act), as implemented in the regulations at 42 C.F.R. section 447.54(c), and section 1902(a)(19) of the Act. I am scheduling a hearing on your request for reconsideration to be held on November 30, 1994, in Room 111, New Federal Office Building, 601 East 12th Street, Kansas City, Missouri, 64106&hyph;2808. If this date is not acceptable, we would be glad to set another date that is mutually agreeable to the parties. The hearing will be governed by the procedures prescribed at 42 CFR, Part 430. I am designating Mr. Stanley Katz as the presiding officer. If these arrangements present any problems, please contact the presiding officer. In order to facilitate any communication which may be necessary between the parties to the hearing, please notify the presiding officer to indicate acceptability of the hearing date that has been scheduled and provide names of the individuals who will represent the State at the hearing. The presiding officer may be reached at (410) 597&hyph;3013. ␣␣Sincerely, Bruce C. Vladeck, Administrator.
