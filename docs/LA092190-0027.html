September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

COLORADO;

MAN CHARGED WITH POSING AS STUDENT

A 26-year-old female impersonator posed as an 11th-grade student at Coronado High School in Colorado Springs, Colo., and even became a cheerleader, at which students said he did a good job.

Student reaction ranged from amused to angry or scared. Parents were just plain angry, school officials said. Charles Janloyames Daugherty, a 5-foot-9 164-pound man, was arrested at the school on suspicion of third-degree forgery and criminal impersonation. Daugherty was only in school eight days. School officials said they discovered the masquerade within two or three days.
