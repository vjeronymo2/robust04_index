Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Justice and Defense Minister Kobie Coetsee met his Namibian counterpart in Walvis Bay yesterday in an effort to promote cooperation in the administration of justice between the two countries. This follows a meeting between the ministers of justice of South Africa and Zimbabwe on Monday [7 February], where a similar agreement was reached. Mr. Coetsee and Mr. Peter Mueshihange, the Namibian minister of defense, agreed on the phasing in of a Namibian military presence in Walvis Bay which will fall under Namibian rule in less than three weeks. They also discussed the final withdrawal of the SADF [South African Defense Force] from the enclave. Walvis Bay and the Guano Islands off the Namibian coast will be transferred to Namibia at midnight on February the 28th.
