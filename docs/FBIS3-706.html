Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Monrovia, 12 Mar (AFP) -- General Roosevelt Johnson, head of the military high command of the United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia, ULIMO, announced today in Monrovia that he is "reconciled" with the president of the organization, Alhaji Koromah. In a statement broadcast on national radio, Gen. Johnson called on all members of the movement to unite, and confirmed that all fighters should comply with the disarmament process, which started early this week throughout the country.
