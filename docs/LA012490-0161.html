January 24, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

CHILDHOOD CRUSH TO BE FULFILLED

Martha Quinn had a childhood crush on Bobby Brady of the old "Brady Bunch" TV series.

Now she gets to marry him. Quinn, one of MTV's original veejays, joins the cast of "The Bradys," a series revival now in production for a spring debut on CBS, as Bobby (Mike Lookinland) Brady's fiance. "I grew up watching 'The Brady Bunch' and my favorite character was always Bobby," she gushes. "I had a big crush on him and now we're getting married. It's like a dream."
