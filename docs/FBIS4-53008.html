BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 15 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received congratulatory messages from the participants in celebration meetings which were held in Tulubolo and Western Shoa region, Ethiopia, in Damascus, Syria and by the Chittagong Branch of the Bangladesh-Korea Friendship Association, on his birthday. The messages say President Kim Il-song enjoys deep respect from the world's peaceloving and democratic forces for having devoted his all to the happiness of the people and victory of the Korean revolution. Noting that the chuche idea founded by President Kim Il-song is the one and only just ideological weapon for the working peoples of the world to free themselves from exploitation and oppression by imperialism, they express the resolution to deepen the study of it. They also express firm solidarity with the Korean people in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of the country and building of socialism, saying "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" worked out by President Kim Il-song is the most constructive programme which helps realise the reunification of Korea in a peaceful way. The messages wish President Kim Il-song a long life in good health. Meanwhile, letters to President Kim Il-song were adopted at the Guinean national seminar on his famous work "For the ultimate solution of the rural question under the banner of the socialist rural theses" and at friendly gatherings with Korean visitors in Katmandu, Nepal.
