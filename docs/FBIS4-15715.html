BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] [Announcer Aleksandr Sapozhnikov] Latvian President Ulmanis, speaking on national radio, said that under the present circumstances, he was not ready to sign the Latvian-Russian agreements on conditions for the pullout of [Russian] troops. He added that he did not consider it possible to sign those documents on the agreed date of 20 April.
