930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / UK Company News: West Trust West Trust accompanied its interim figures, which showed a pre-tax profit of Pounds 25,000, with news of expansion. It will pay Pounds 750,000 for Veeraswamy's (Food Products). The consideration will be met by the issue of 25m shares. of which the vendors will retain 6.7m. The balance will be offered to shareholders on a 2-for-9 basis at 3p. To cover the costs and fund further development of the food side, West proposes to raise nearly Pounds 400,000 through a rights issue of 13.3m shares on the same terms. In the six months to March 31 turnover at West came to Pounds 3.76m (Pounds 7.74m) and profit to Pounds 25,000 (Pounds 36,000). Earnings per share were 0.02p (0.06p). The shares are to be consolidated from 2.5p to 25p.
