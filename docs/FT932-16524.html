930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Russian venue for EBRD The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) said its board of directors recommended that the 1994 annual meeting of the board of governors be held in St Petersburg on April 18 and 19, Reuter reports. The board has also recommended that the 1995 and 1996 meetings be held in London and Sofia, Bulgaria respectively. This year's meeting is being held in London on April 26 and 27, and includes seminars at the EBRD's headquarters from April 23.
