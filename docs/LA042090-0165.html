April 20, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

HOSTAGE RELEASE 'CERTAIN' DESPITE U.S. 'ARROGANCE'

The reputed mentor of kidnapers holding three American educators said today that the release of a hostage is certain despite complications caused by the United States' "arrogant . . . cowboy" behavior.

"The delay is certain, the complications are certain, but the release is also certain," said Hussein Musawi.

Musawi, a Shiite Muslim, heads Islamic Amal, a faction within the pro-Iranian Hezbollah that advocates strong links with Syria.

Asked when he expects the release to take place, Musawi said, "That depends on how farsighted the Americans might become. So far, their behavior is arrogant and one of cowboy mentality."
