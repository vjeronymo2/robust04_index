BFN [Text] Tianjin, June 25 (XINHUA) -- Hu Jintao, a senior Chinese Communist Party leader, today ended a six-day tour of the north China port city of Tianjin and its environs. Hu is a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. Accompanied by Gao Dezhan, secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Zhang Lichang, mayor of Tianjin, Hu Jintao inspected a number of villages and rural industrial enterprises. He discussed with officials at county, township and village levels how to further strengthen building of the CPC grassroots organizations in the rural areas. He also visited some farm produce markets, grain shops and large and medium-sized enterprises and talked with workers and local residents, inquiring about the situation of their work and daily life and gathering their opinions and suggestions. During his tour of inspection, he stressed the need to further strengthen the building up of the party. In the countryside, he said, it was necessary to make great efforts to establish strong village party branches, promote to the position of the party branch secretary those party members who are of good political quality, willing to serve the people, good at business and management, and able to lead the local people to develop the economy.
