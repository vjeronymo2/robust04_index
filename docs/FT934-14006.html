931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Hepworth pays Pounds 4m for dolomite quarry Hepworth, the building materials and boiler group, has, through its Hepworth Minerals and Chemicals offshoot, acquired the operating assets and mineral reserves of Pilkington Quarries' dolomite quarry for Pounds 4m cash. Dolomite from the quarry, situated near Doncaster, is used in the manufacture of flat glass, container glass and fibreglass. Pilkington Glass (UK) will continue to be a sizeable customer.
