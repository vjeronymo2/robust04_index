BFN [Text] Malcolm Rifkind, the defense secretary of the United Kingdom, today visited the Joint Staff of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the Croatian Defense Council [HVO]. After these talks, Brigadier General Fikret Muslimovic explained the goal of this visit in a statement for the Radio and Television of Bosnia-Herzegovina: [Begin recording] I got the feeling that the English defense minister has come to Bosnia-Herzegovina, that is, the Joint Staff of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the Croatian Defense Council, in order to acquaint himself with the role and problems the Joint Staff is encountering in realizing tasks (?in the process) of establishing peace between the Croats and Bosniaks in the present situation. I think that we presented him with the problems we are confronted with and that he left with more optimism about the fact that peace will really be established between our two [word indistinct]. [end recording]
