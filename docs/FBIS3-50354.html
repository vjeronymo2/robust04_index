Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the Russian nationalist Liberal Democratic Party, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, arrived to Brnik airport after a one-hour delay this afternoon. The mere announcement of his arrival received considerable attention in the domestic and international media. The controversial Russian politician is paying a private visit to our country. Immediately upon his arrival he left for Bled. He will be staying in Portoroz tomorrow and Sunday, and on Monday he is supposed to visit Serbia.
