March 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The hearing for track and field coach Chuck DeBus set for today has been rescheduled, according to The Athletics Congress, the national governing body of track and field. TAC announced that the hearing, which has been rescheduled several times, would be continued at the request of DeBus' attorney. DeBus, formerly a coach at UCLA and Cal State Northridge, has been under investigation by TAC for allegedly providing banned substances to athletes.
