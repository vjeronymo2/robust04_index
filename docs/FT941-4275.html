940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / International Company News: James Hardie to float subsidiary James Hardie, the Australian building materials group, is to float its Hardie Technologies subsidiary, which manufactures printed circuit board assemblies and distributes electronic components, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. The Melbourne-based company is expected to have revenues of around Adollars 70m (USdollars 33m) in the year to end-March. Flotation details are being finalised, but Hardie said that the disposal was part of its strategy of shedding non-core investments.
