BFN [Text] A Turkish military delegation led by Deputy Defense Minister General Toman Erkan arrived in Albania today. General Erkan was received by Defense Minister Safet Zhulali, who welcomed him and wished him a pleasant stay in Albania. Then a meeting was held, in which Minister Zhulali appraised the relations and cooperation between the two countries in the field of defense and paid tribute to Turkey's contribution to the education and training of personnel, as well as its assistance in various fields. Mr. Zhulali went on to stress that, in view of its modernization and Partnership for Peace, our army requires more help and stronger support in the fields of communication, food, medicine, the military industry, etc. For his part, after conveying greetings from the Turkish defense minister to Zhulali, General Erkan confirmed that Turkey would help the Albanian Army in accordance with the bilateral agreement. The cooperation and support that we render to the Albanian Army, he said, is a question of mutual assistance. Metin Ornekol, the Turkish ambassador to Tirana, attended the meeting, which took place in a warm atmosphere. In addition to other activities, the Turkish military delegation held working meetings with Lieutenant General Ilia Vasho, chief of the Army General Staff, Deputy Defense Minister Bashkim Shehu, and other top officials to pinpoint possible avenues for greater assistance to the Albanian Army.
