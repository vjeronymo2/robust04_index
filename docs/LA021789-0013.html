February 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

One of two shows originally listed as "to be announced" on the La Jolla Playhouse summer season will be "Tango/Orfeo," to be offered in a three-way production involving the Spoleto Festival USA, the American Music Theatre Festival in Philadelphia and the La Jolla Playhouse. It plays La Jolla's Mandell Weiss Theatre July 2-Aug. 6. SHAUNA SNOW
