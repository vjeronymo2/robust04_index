BFN [Text] One Armenian tank was destroyed outside the Azeri administrative center Ter-Ter on Saturday [14 May] as fighting continues unabated there for the second day in a row, a source close to the Azeri Defence Ministry told INTERFAX. Azeri Army positions outside Ter-Ter were shelled again by Armenian forces on Sunday morning. In the meantime, the press service of defence forces of Nagorno-Karabakh reports that Karabakh forces came under attack outside Seisulan village of the Mardakert region late on Saturday and overnight. There was relative calm on other parts of the front.
