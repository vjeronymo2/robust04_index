Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Approximately 200 delegates from 17 countries will participate in the conference entitled "the Nation and Emigration" scheduled for the last 15 days of April in Havana. This has been announced by Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina, who also announced that 14 Nobel Prize winners will be invited soon, as will a group of U.S. Congressmen, so that they can learn about the national reality firsthand. Robaina also confirmed that he will be leaving for Asia tomorrow [4 March] on a tour of several countries. In connection with the meeting called "the Nation and Emigration," the foreign minister observed that it will allow everyone to talk in a respectful and civilized way about subjects of mutual interest. Robaina spoke at the 17th seminar improvement seminar for national higher education leaders that was held in the capital.
