940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Scottish National Net asset value per capital share of the Scottish National Trust improved from 94.9p at end-September 1993 to 145.7p at December 31. After-tax revenue for the three months improved to Pounds 2.5m (Pounds 2.29m), equal to earnings of 1.55p (1.42p) per income share. A same-again first interim dividend of 1.55p was announced last Tuesday.
