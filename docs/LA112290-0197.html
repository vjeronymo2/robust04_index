November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NATION;

OREGON STATE COACH WON'T BE BACK

Dave Kragthorpe won't be back next year as Oregon State's coach, it was announced Wednesday.

"After a lengthy and considerable discussion this morning, Coach Kragthorpe and I have mutually agreed to make a change at the head coaching position," Oregon State Athletic Director Dutch Baughman said.

The Beavers were 1-10 in 1990 and had a record of 17-48-2 in six seasons under Kragthorpe.

The University of Alabama faculty senate, citing the failure of Arizona voters to approve a holiday honoring Martin Luther King Jr., urged the school's football team not to play in the Fiesta Bowl if asked.

The resolution was passed, 19-10, and will be forwarded to Roger Sayer, university president, and Pat Green, senate president.
