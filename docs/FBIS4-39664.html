BFN [Text] A session of the Supreme Soviet Presidium, headed by Vyachaslaw Kuznyatsow, first deputy to the Supreme Soviet chairman, was held to discuss a range of issues: The establishment of diplomatic relations between Belarus and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia; some legislative acts of the Republic, regulating the mechanism of mutual accounts in a range of national sectors; adoption of a decision to award honorary titles and diplomas and Belarusian Supreme Soviet diplomas. A report by the Central Election Committee on the implementation of measures for holding elections for Belarusian president was heard.
