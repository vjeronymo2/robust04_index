Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mate Granic, deputy prime minister and foreign minister of the Republic of Croatia, today received Margaretha af Ugglas, foreign minister of the Kingdom of Sweden. Foreign Minister Granic briefed the Swedish foreign minister on the negotiations which resulted in the signing of the Croat-Muslim agreements in Washington, on the contents of the signed documents, and on the work of the joint committee in Vienna, which is drafting several agreements envisaged by the Washington agreement. Foreign Minister Granic thanked the Kingdom of Sweden for its substantial humanitarian aid, for accepting large numbers of refugees, and for the participation of Swedish units in the UN peacekeeping operation in the occupied parts of the Republic of Croatia. Foreign Minister Margaretha af Ugglas expressed the Swedish Government's satisfaction with the Washington agreements and pledged support for the peace process and aid for the reconstruction of the Republic of Croatia. She invited Granic to visit Sweden. It was agreed that Granic will visit Sweden in the second half of March.
