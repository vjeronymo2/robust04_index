940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Venezuelan buy for Unilever Unilever, the Anglo-Dutch food and consumer products group, is extending its position as the world's leading ice-cream producer with the acquisition of Helados Tio Rico in Venezuela from the privately owned Cisneros Group. The consideration is undisclosed. Helados Tio Rico, based in Caracas, employs 780 people and has annual turnover of Dollars 28m (Pounds 18.6m). The Tio Rico brand is market leader in the impulse ice-cream sector.
