930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Huntingdon up in first quarter HUNTINGDON International Holdings, which is engaged in life sciences, biological safety testing and engineering and environmental services, reported pre-tax profit of Pounds 3.73m for its first quarter to December 31. The outcome marked a modest advance on the comparable Pounds 3.58m and came on sales ahead 39 per cent at Pounds 40.1m (Pounds 28.9m), net of subcontract costs. Mr Bennie Wooley, chairman, said the group entered 1993 with signs of better prospects for both the US and UK economies later in the year. He believed the long term outlook for the company remained good. Earning per share were unchanged at 2.9p.
