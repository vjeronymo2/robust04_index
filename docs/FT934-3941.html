931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / Multi-million bonuses condemned Bifu, the banking and finance union, has condemned the multi-million pound bonuses being given to City 'high-flyers' at a time when most staff are being asked to accept lower pay deals. Richard Lynch, Bifu's senior London organiser, said: 'The City high-flyers getting those huge bonuses are the people who tell the rest of us we must tighten our belts to help economic recovery.'
