October 7, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

LOS ALAMITOS BLASTS LAKEWOOD TO IMPROVE TO 4-1

Quarterback Todd Gragnano completed 10 of 21 pass attempts for 298 yards and three touchdowns as Los Alamitos High School rolled to a 35-7 victory Friday night over host Lakewood.

Running back Erik Mitchell rushed for 65 yards and two touchdowns and had 115 yards and another touchdown in pass receptions as Los Alamitos improved to 4-1. Lakewood is 1-4.
