940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Brent Walker makes Pounds 2.75m disposal Brent Walker has completed the sale to Stakis of the Bath Hotel and Thimble Mill restaurant at Widcombe Basin, Bath, Avon, for Pounds 2.75m cash. During 1993, the hotel incurred an operating loss. Proceeds will be used to reduce group borrowings.
