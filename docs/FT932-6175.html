930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / UK Company News: RPC shares up 34p on first trading day The first day of trading of RPC, the rigid plastic packaging company, resulted in its shares rising by 34p to 159p. When the offer closed on Tuesday the issue has been subscribed more than 11 times. On May 18 Cazenove placed 18.2m shares with institutions, representing 65 per cent of the issue.
