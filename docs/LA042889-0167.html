April 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NUCLEAR PLANT RESTARTED AFTER 2-YEAR SHUTDOWN

Peach Bottom nuclear plant was restarted Thursday after a two-year shutdown ordered by regulators after they found operators sleeping on the job.

The federal Nuclear Regulatory Commission granted final approval for a restart of Unit 2 Wednesday night and the chain reaction began about 5:40 a.m., said Bill Jones, a spokesman for plant operator and part-owner Philadelphia Electric Co.
