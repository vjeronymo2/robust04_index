Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] The infamous militia of Golboddin-Dostam last night mounted a failed offensive on the positions of the Islamic army's forces in the easterly direction. A security source told the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY [BIA] military correspondent that the offensive of the militia was aborted in the very first hours of the attack, and damage was inflicted on rebellious militia, which suffered a great loss of life. Following their defeat last night, the militia subjected residential areas of city of Kabul to rocket attacks last night and this morning. It caused no loss of life, but some damage was inflicted on the residents of Kabul. According to another report, yesterday afternoon a heavy clash took place between the militias of Golboddin and Dostam in the area of Brishnakot in Kabul. The conflagration continued until 2200 last night, and much loss of life was suffered by the warring militias. The security source added differences between the commanders of Golboddin's party and commanders of Dostam were increasing. Since the start of the unholy alliance between Golboddin and Dostam, this was the umpteenth clash between these two militia groups.
