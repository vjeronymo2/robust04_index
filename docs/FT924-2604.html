921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Phoenix Timber Slimmed-down Phoenix Timber reduced losses further in the first-half to September 30. On sales of Pounds 10.5m (Pounds 10.3m) from continuing businesses, the pre-tax deficit was halved to Pounds 533,000 (Pounds 1.13m), and after an extraordinary charge of Pounds 50,000 (Pounds 2.65m) relating to the discontinuance of the timber trading business, the attributable loss declined to Pounds 583,000 (Pounds 3.78m) or 3.7p (7.8p) per share.
