BFN [Text] At the invitation of Swiss-China Friendship Association, a six-member economic delegation of Shandong Province headed by Wang Shufang, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, left Jinan for Beijing on the evening of 22 April. The delegation is going to pay an eight-day visit to Switzerland. During the visit, the provincial delegation will conduct extensive contacts and exchanges with the personages in the fields of enterprises, the economy, and trade in Switzerland. It will also hold talks with the Swiss authorities on establishing friendly ties between the province and Switzerland.
