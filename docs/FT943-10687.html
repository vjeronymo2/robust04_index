940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa golds give up gains Gold shares gave up the day's strong gains after the bullion price unexpectedly tumbled to Dollars 379 an ounce in late trade and buyers turned sellers. The overall index ended 32 higher at 5,684, industrials put on 35 at 6,437 and golds finished 1 ahead at 2,143. Strong speculative interest by local and foreign buyers continued for steelmaker Iscor, which added 8 cents at R4.18 but was off the day's high. Highveld Steel, up R3.25 at R31.75, held on to its gain after bouncing on good results and improved sector sentiment. De Beers rose R1.75 to R113 and Anglos R3 to R253.
