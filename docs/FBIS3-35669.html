Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Jan 29, BNS -- Russian Ambassador to Estonia Alexandr Trofimov yesterday delivered to President Lennart Meri Russian President Boris Yeltsin's reply to Meri's letter of Jan. 5. The president's press service informs that Yeltsin's letter touches on problems which so far haven't found a solution during the Estonian-Russian negotiations, and expresses hope that a mutually acceptable solution will be arrived at in the near future. This puts back on the agenda an Estonian-Russian summit preparations for which were made last autumn, the president's press service told BNS.
