Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has left Amman after he took part in signing the Yemeni reconciliation and reform accord. His majesty the king and His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan led a farewell ceremony to President Salih at the airport. The prime minister, the speakers of Upper and Lower Houses of Parliament, and his majesty the king's advisers also took part in the farewell ceremony. In a departure statement, President Salih paid tribute to efforts exerted by his majesty in ending the rift in Yemen. He expressed his thanks to those who worked and planned for the signing ceremony. President Salih said he discussed during his stay bilateral issues of concern and other pan-Arab and Muslim issues. The Yemeni president said more challenges are facing Arabs that need firmness and faithfulness. He said this would be achieved by reconciliation among Arabs and putting pan-Arab interests before regional ones. Mr. Salih said that he is looking for bilateral Arab meetings as a first step toward gatherings that would be of greater benefit for all Arabs.
