BFN [Text] The Russian State Duma has adopted at its first reading the draft Federal Constitutional bill on the Russian Constitutional Court. The issue of judges' terms of office provoked a lot of controversy during the debate. The term of 12 years has been defined. The right granted to the president to suggest candidates for judges' positions in the Constitutional court was another important norm which also provoked a controversial reaction among the deputies. However, consensus was also achieved on this matter.
