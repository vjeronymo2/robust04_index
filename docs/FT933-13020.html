930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Dwyer's Pounds 12m sale cuts into borrowings Dwyer, the property investment and trading group, has contracted to sell a portfolio of seven freehold properties to Frogmore Estates for Pounds 12.3m cash. This will reduce borrowings by Pounds 10.8m and, with the other sales effected in the year, will cut gearing to 48 per cent. The portfolio comprised three retail, three office, and one mixed use investment. It had a book value of Pounds 13.9m and produced gross rents of Pounds 1.28m. Net rental income from the remaining portfolio will be Pounds 2.2m a year.
