BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow April 22 TASS -- The Russian State Duma decided on Friday to hold parliamentary hearings on lifting sanctions against Yugoslavia. The proposal was made by Aleksandr Vengerovskiy, deputy chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party of Vladimir Zhirinovskiy. He also proposed to invite to the hearings representatives of all conflicting sides in former Yugoslavia. Vengerovskiy said that, according to his information, representatives of Yugoslavia, Bosnian Serbs, Krajina Serbs, and Croatia "had already expressed the wish to participate in such hearings".
