930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: J&J Dyson A fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 1.05m was announced by J&J Dyson for the year to March 31 after exceptional rationalisation costs of Pounds 140,808, against Pounds 186,541. Turnover for the Sheffield-based group was reduced to Pounds 42.6m (Pounds 49.5m). Losses of Pounds 10,643 were incurred in the vehicles and trailers division, against profits of Pounds 93,237, while profits from refractories fell to Pounds 837,783 (Pounds 913,138). However, an improved contribution of Pounds 183,677 (Pounds 126,646) was made by builders' merchanting. The final dividend is held at 3p for an unchanged total of 5p. Earnings were 5.76p (6.54p).
