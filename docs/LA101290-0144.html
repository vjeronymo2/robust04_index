October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

UCLA;

BRUINS PASS WELL, DEFEND PASS WELL

UCLA has the distinction of leading the Pacific 10 Conference in passing offense and pass-efficiency defense with a 94.3 rating.

The Bruins are averaging 265.8 yards a game passing, while allowing an average of 148.4 through five games.

Moreover, only two touchdown passes have been thrown against the Bruins. MAL FLORENCE
