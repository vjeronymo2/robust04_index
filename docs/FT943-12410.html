940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Du Pont and Dow Chemical - Other companies The two chemical giants are expected to report advances in second-quarter profits this week, reflecting higher sales volumes in an improving economy. Last week, Monsanto and Arco Chemical topped most earnings predictions, thanks to the strength of demand from US car and housing markets. Du Pont is expected to report a rise in earnings per share from 79 cents to between Dollars 1.00-Dollars 1.10, while Dow Chemical should see a more modest rebound.
