BFN [Text] This morning, a delegation of our Lao Foreign Affairs Ministry led by Deputy Foreign Affairs Minister Phongsavat Bouppha held, in Vientiane, talks with a delegation of the Foreign Affairs Ministry of the Union of Myanmar [Burma] led by Deputy Foreign Affairs Minister U Nyunt Swe. The Myanmar Foreign Affairs Ministry delegation is currently on a visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR]. The visit is scheduled for 24 to 27 April. In the talks, the two sides discussed the bilateral relations and cooperation in addition to exchanging views on various issues of common interest in the region and the world. They particularly discussed means to raise to a new, higher level the promotion and expansion of cooperation between the two countries in the economic, technical, scientific, and other fields to be worthy of being neighboring countries with mutual benefit. U Maung Maung Lay, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Union of Myanmar to the LPDR, also attended the meeting on this occasion.
