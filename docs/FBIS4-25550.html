BFN [By reporter Zhang Yijun (1728 4135 0193)] [Text] Beijing, 13 Jun (XINHUA) -- Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission, met with a Korean military delegation led by Choe Kwang, chief of the Korean People's Army General Staff, in the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this morning. Liu said during the meeting that China hopes that all the parties concerned will try every means to resort to dialogue to settle the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula. Liu Huaqing said: As for the nuclear issue on the Korea peninsula, China always upholds the objective of promoting the denuclearization on the peninsula and safeguarding peace and stability on it. He said: There is a profound friendship between the peoples and between the armies of China and the DPRK. Such a friendship is very precious and should be passed on from generation to generation. He expressed the belief that the visit to China by Choe Kwang, chief of the General Staff, will certainly be conducive to promoting mutual understanding, friendship, and the development of bilateral relations. Liu Huaqing visited the DPRK and met with Choe Kwang five years ago. Choe Kwang said that he was pleased to have the opportunity to meet with Liu Huaqing again and that he appreciated the efforts made by China to develop friendship between the DPRK and China. During the meeting, both military leaders expressed their willingness to increase exchanges between the two armies so as to promote the development of relations between the two armies and the two countries. The delegation arrived in China on 6 June on a visit at the invitation of Zhang Wannian, chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Staff. In addition to Beijing, it has also visited Shanghai and Hangzhou.
