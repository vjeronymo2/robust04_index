February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NONSMOKERS SPEAK OUT

I was distressed and frustrated to read Dirlam's article. My husband and I are nonsmokers and travel a fair amount of miles. Due to my husband's illness (post open-heart surgery), we seek nonsmoking areas everywhere. It is not just a matter of preference, but of life and death! Why wasn't the airline named? Why should they be protected if the story was true? Please do not protect companies that have no regard for their customers. And do not publish such articles again unless companies are named.

PAT SAX

El Toro
