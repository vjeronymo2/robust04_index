BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 20 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the invitation of the Prime Minister of Mongolia P. Jasray the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Li Peng will arrive in Mongolia on an official visit on 28-29 April of the current year. During the visit P. Jasray and Li Peng are to carry out negotiations. The Chinese premier will call on the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat and the Chairman of the Great State Hural of Mongolia Bagabandi. In the course of the visit it is expected that the Mongolian-Chinese Treaty on Friendly Relations and Cooperation be signed as well as the intergovernmental agreements on cooperation in the spheres of culture and veterinary service and the protection and use of border rivers waters, informed the press office of the Ministry of External Relations of Mongolia.
