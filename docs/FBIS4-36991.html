BFN [Text] The union cabinet has cleared a proposal for restructuring the subordinate engineering service of the All India Radio and Doordarshan [Television]. Our correspondent reports two new grades have been created for the subordinate personnel. The number of posts at the higher level have been increased accordingly. The cabinet has also approved India's accession to the international convention against taking of hostages. At a meeting yesterday, it approved a proposal to amend the Indian Penal Code to give effect to the provisions of the convention.
