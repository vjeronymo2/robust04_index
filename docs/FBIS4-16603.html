BFN [Text] Riga, Apr 15, BNS -- Latvian Defense Minister Valdis Pavlovskis appointed Captain Janis Adamsons as commander of the country's Frontier Guard Brigade, the Defense Ministry reports. Adamsons has been deputy commander of Latvia's Naval Forces since June 15, 1992. Adamsons was born in 1956 at Livani, graduated from Kiev (Ukraine) naval school in 1979, served in various units of the Soviet Naval Border Guards. As reported, the former commander of border guards, Ivars Redisons, resigned in the end of March. His duties were performed by acting commander, former deputy commander Viktors Sviklis, until the appointment of Adamsons.
