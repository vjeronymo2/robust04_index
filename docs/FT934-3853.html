931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Ugland in loss Closure of its dry docks meant that Ugland International, the shipping company formerly known as Bristol Channel Ship Repairers, turned in a loss of Pounds 780,000 on turnover of Pounds 4,500 for the six months to September 24. That compared with a loss of Pounds 236,000 on turnover of Pounds 34,000 last time. Losses per share were 0.98p (0.3p).
