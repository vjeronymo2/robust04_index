BFN [Tex] His Royal Highness Prince Muhammad, the regent, received South Korean presidential envoy Pak Su-chol. The envoy conveyed to the regent a message from the South Korean president addressed to His Majesty King Husayn on bilateral ties. Mr. Pak Su-chol said South Korea highly appreciated Jordan's role in keeping the stability and security of the Middle East, and praised the kingdom's endeavor to achieve just and comprehensive peace. His royal highness reiterated Jordan's position towards the peace process and the need to attain just and permanent peace in line with UN resolutions.
