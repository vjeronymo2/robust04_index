BFN [Report by Dmitriy Orlov: "Nail Aminov Ready To Cooperate with All Constructive Politicians"] [Text] In accordance with the treaty between Russia and Tatarstan by an edict of President Mintimer Shaymiyev Nail Galeyevich Aminov has been appointed Tatarstan's fully empowered representative in the Russian Federation. Nail Aminov is a Tatar by nationality. He was born in 1945 in Kazan and then studied in the Tatar capital. He worked in the Bashkir Government. In 1990 he was elected a Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic people's deputy and worked as a committee deputy chairman, subcommittee chairman, and parliament department chief. Last year he was appointed deputy chairman of a Russian state committee. The Tatarstan representative stated in a talk with your ROSSIYSKIYE VESTI correspondent that the main parting message given to him by President Shaymiyev was "to serve loyally." In Aminov's opinion it is essential that the treaty between Russia and Tatarstan should be implemented in all spheres, for which the relevant agreements should be concluded. Nail Aminov noted that he regards collaboration with the recently created Russian-Tatar commission under the leadership of Oleg Soskovets as one of the most important tasks facing him and expressed the certainty that Vice Premier Sergey Shakhray and minister Nikolay Yegorov will take an active part in the commission's work. Aminov stated his readiness to cooperate with all Russia's political forces "of a constructive bent."
