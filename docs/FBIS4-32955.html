BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Text] The Basic Industry Ministry's Electric Union has reported that power outages should be reduced in the capital within the next few hours, once repairs to one of the boiler pipes at the power plant in Matanzas are completed. One of the units of the Mariel power plant is also in the final stages of its maintenance program. Meanwhile, work should begin on the power plant in Cienfuegos Province.
