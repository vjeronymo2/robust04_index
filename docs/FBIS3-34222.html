Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] In Algeria, the authorities have confirmed that there have been contacts with leading representatives from the Islamic Salvation Front [FIS]. The two Islamist leaders, Abassi Madani and Ali Belhadj are reported to have approved these high-level contacts. Nevertheless, the violence continues in Algeria. The subprefect of Ouled Mimoun, south of Sidi Bel Abbes, was murdered on Wednesday [2 February]. He was murdered by unknown assailants as he went to work.
