Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 9 Mar (XINHUA) -- Agenda for the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] Adopted at the preparatory meeting for the Second Session of the Eighth NPC on 9 March 1994 1. Hearing and deliberating a report by Li Peng, premier of the State Council, concerning the government's work; 2. Hearing and deliberating a report by Chen Jinhua, minister in charge of the State Planning Commission, on the implementation of the 1993 national economic and social development plan and the 1994 draft plan for national economic and social development; Examining and approving the report on the implementation of the 1993 national economic and social development plan and the 1994 draft plan for national economic and social development; 3. Hearing and deliberating Finance Minister Liu Zhongli's report on the implementation of the 1993 state budget and the draft state budget for 1994; Examining and approving the report on the implementation of the 1993 state budget and the state budget for 1994; 4. Deliberating the "Budget Law (Draft) of the People's Republic of China"; 5. Deliberating the "Decision (Draft) of the National People's Congress on Empowering the Xiamen City People's Congress, Its Standing Committee, and the Xiamen City People's Government To Formulate Laws and Regulations for Implementation in the Xiamen Special Economic Zone"; 6. Hearing and deliberating a report by Tian Jiyun, vice chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, on the work of the NPC Standing Committee; 7. Hearing and deliberating a report by Ren Jianxin, president of the Supreme People's Court, on the work of the Supreme People's Court; 8. Hearing and deliberating Zhang Siqing, procurator general of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, on the work of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; and 9. Others.
