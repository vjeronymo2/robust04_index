940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: C&W Russian purchase Cable and Wireless, the telecommunications group, has spent Dollars 39.9m (Pounds 27.3m) on a 25.1 per cent stake in Petersburg Long Distance, a private telecommunications concern, which is building a network in Russia's second city. PLD has a 59 per cent stake in a joint venture with St Petersburg's state-owned telecommunications operator. The company is building a network to help plug unmet demand for local, national and international telecoms services in the city, the directors stated. PLD also has a 50 per cent stake in a cellular mobile communications venture in Kazakhstan.
