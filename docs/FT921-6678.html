920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Fisons accounting policies cleared Fisons has been cleared of questions regarding its 1990 accounts. The Financial Reporting Review Panel had been examining criticisms that the accounts did not disclose the basis of consolidation of the group accounts. Fisons argued that consolidation requirements were defined in company legislation and it did not need a separate statement of policy. The panel stressed last month that this did not remove the requirement to separately specify the accounting policies used.
