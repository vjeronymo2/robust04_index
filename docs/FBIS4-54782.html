BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 4 -- President Lech Walesa attended a part of the Wednesdays Cabinet meeting devoted to state security. Discussed were proposals for legally regulating the Army command and issues related to the modification of Army structures, the presidential press bureau said Wednesday. The president announced that positions agreed during the meeting would be presented to the Sejm, the communique says. Walesa said that the strikes of the strategic branches of the economy had a negative impact on state security, but he warned against forcing a temporary solutions on trade unions.
