BFN [Text] Tirana, May 17 (ATA) -- President Sali Berisha met on May 16 with president of Kosova Ibrahim Rugova. They discussed the conditions in which the Albanian nation is at present, the grave situation in Kosova and the development of democratic reforms in Albania, the President's Press and Information Office reports. Both presidents were of the opinion that the establishment of dialogue between Prishtina and Belgrade, in the presence of a third party is imperative for the measures to normalize the life and for a political solution of the Kosova issue. They pointed out that the stability and the independence of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] are important for the peace and stability of Albanians and other nationalities of the Balkans. They encourage the Albanian political forces to cooperate with each other for the consolidation of the stability of the Macedonian Republic as well as for a guarantee of the human rights and freedoms of the Albanians as a condition for the stability of this republic. The meeting was conducted in a spirit of full understanding.
