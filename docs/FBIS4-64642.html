BFN [Report by Gerard Nirascou: "Balladur in Nice"] [Excerpt] Nice -- The prime minister went to Nice yesterday to attend the opening ceremony of the 27th General Assembly of the Asian Development Bank. Edouard Balladur took advantage of the opportunity to forcefully reaffirm France's interest in that continent and the desire of our country and Europe to have a greater presence there. "The stakes are considerable," the prime minister explained, "in the political sphere, the European countries and their Asian partners have prime responsibility for the security and stability of these continents...and in the economic sphere, Europe is the world's leading trading partner and is one of the most prosperous markets."
