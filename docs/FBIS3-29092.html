Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 15 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Monday sent a message of greetings to Antonio Manuel Monteiro Mascarenhas, president of the Republic of Cape Verde, on his 50th birthday. President Kim Il-song in the message wished him great success in his future work for the prosperity of the country and health and happiness.
