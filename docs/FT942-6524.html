940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Business Travel (Update): Romanian passports Romania last week started to issue what it claims are the most high-tech passports in Europe, with sophisticated features to combat forgery. The new passports should be issued nationwide within about three months. 'They are the very latest technology, more modern than the passports of other European states,' a spokesman for the Ministry of the Interior said. Passport forgery reached epidemic proportions after the fall of the communist regime in 1989.
