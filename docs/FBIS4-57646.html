BFN [Text] Dr. Carlos Uribe, who successfully treated [soccer player] Pibe Valderrama's knee injury, operated on President Cesar Gaviria yesterday morning. The Presidency Information and Press Secretariat reported Gaviria "is recovering from the surgery he underwent." According to the medical report, "the surgery lasted one hour and 50 minutes, and it freed the posterior tibial nerve, which was restrained at the tarsus tunnel of his right foot. This is why the operation could be satisfactorily performed." Presidency sources also reported the chief of state was hospitalized at 0630. One hour later, he was transferred to the operating room, where he underwent surgery until 0930. Gaviria remained unconscious until noon due to the anesthesia. Dr. Uribe said the president remained under medical supervision all day yesterday.
