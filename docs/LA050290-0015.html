May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

TAX REVOLT TIME

If the Boston Tea Party and the American Revolution were the shots heard 'round the world, then this Poll Tax Revolt in Great Britain should trigger a "read my lips" revolt against all the nickel-and-dime taxes in the U.S. But it won't because Americans have been so lulled that they will endure all kinds of political abuse.

We must stop all this foolishness. A progressive income tax properly administered should cover all costs of providing services our citizens want and need. We must stop special taxes on gasoline, tobacco, alcohol and use taxes for purposes not relating to these taxes.

Americans have just paid their various income taxes. Wasn't that enough?

HARRY WALD

Sherman Oaks
