930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: TJ Hughes TJ Hughes, the multiple discount department store operator, reported a 33 per cent surge from Pounds 1.11m to Pounds 1.47m in pre-tax profits for the 53 weeks to January 30. Turnover, at Pounds 40.2m, was ahead 42 per cent of the previous Pounds 28.4m. Like-for-like sales increased 10 per cent. A proposed final dividend of 1.55p makes a total for the year of 2.3p, payable from earnings of 5.2p per share. There are no comparisons since the company came to the USM in May last year. It intends to seek a full listing as soon as possible.
