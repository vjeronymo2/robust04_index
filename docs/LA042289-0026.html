April 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WELCOME TO HOCKEY WORLD, WHERE KINGS RULE

As your team starts a second-round playoff game, it is appropriate for a coach to show support for his team by wearing:

A -- The team colors.

B -- Anything but the opposing team's colors.

C -- Nothing.

D -- The opposing team's colors.

Well, if you're Robbie Ftorek of the L.A. Kings, the answer is D. But of course, don't we all remember Tommy Lasorda wearing a Mets' hat?

JONATHAN E. MILLER

Reseda
