931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: J Smart shows 38% decline to Pounds 2m With a second half not dissimilar to the first six months, profits of J Smart & Co (Contractors) for the year to July 31 worked through at Pounds 2.04m pre-tax. That was a 38 per cent shortfall on last time's Pounds 3.29m and was scored from a turnover some Pounds 1.2m lower at Pounds 12.8m. Earnings emerged at 13.26p (22.08p) and a proposed final dividend of 6.2p makes an unchanged 8.5p total.
