930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Bad weather forces closure of the Ninian platform Bad weather has forced the closure of the Ninian platform, one of the largest in the UK sector of the North Sea, producing 70,000 barrels a day. Other fields affected include Beryl/Ness, Magnus and North Alwyn. North Sea oil prices rose, with the February contract for Brent crude gaining 15 cents to Dollars 17.
