BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 5 May, the delegation of the National Defense Ministry headed by Lieutenant General Choummali Sai-gnason, national defense minister, left for a friendship visit to the PRC at the invitation of Gen. Chi Haotian, state councilor and national defense minister of the PRC, from 5 to 8 May, During its stay in the PRC, the delegation of the National Defense Ministry of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] will exchange views and experience with the delegation of the PRC National Defense Ministry on the basis of strengthening the long-standing traditional relations of friendship between the Lao and Chinese peoples and Armed Forces. At the same time, the delegation will also visit a number of Army- and civilian-operated modern industrial establishments in the PRC. After concluding its visit to the PRC, the delegation of the LPDR National Defense Ministry will pay a friendship visit to the DPRK from 9 to 14 May at the invitation of the DPRK national defense minister. A sending-off ceremony for the delegation was held at the National Defense Ministry in Vientiane. Attending the ceremony were Lt. Gen. Siphon Phalikhan, deputy minister of national defense and chief of the Army General Political Department; Lt. Gen. Ai Souli-gnaseng, deputy minister of national defense and chief of the Army General Staff Department; Li Jiazhong, PRC ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Laos; Chang Yong-chun, DPRK ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Laos; and a number of senior military officers.
