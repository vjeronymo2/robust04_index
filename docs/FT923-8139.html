920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Guinness to buy Spanish minority Guinness has offered about Pounds 8m to acquire the outstanding shares in Union Cervecera, the Spanish brewer which it took over last year. The drinks group, which already holds more than 90 per cent of Union Cervecera, has offered Pta250 (138p) a share - the same as last year's bid price. It has applied to the Madrid stock exchange for the shares' delisting. Union's operations have now been integrated with those of Cruzcampo, which Guinness also bought last year.
