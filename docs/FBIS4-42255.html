BFN [Text] The Greek Cypriots are continuing their border provocations and shooting incidents. The Greek Cypriot National Guardsmen fired 12 shots, in twos or threes, towards the Turkish Cypriot side in the Lefke [Lefka] [word indistinct] region at 2045 today. No one was killed or injured by the shots, which were probably fired from automatic weapons. Soldiers attached to the Security Forces Command acted with a cool head and did not respond to the provocations, preventing the incident from escalating any further. Turkish Cypriot authorities lodged a protest about the incident with the UN Peace Forces. The Greek Cypriots fired shots on the Turkish Cypriot borders on three different occasions in the past week.
