930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Billam rises 57% to Pounds 479,000 J BILLAM, the specialist engineer, returned profits of Pounds 479,000 pre-tax for the year to end-December, an improvement of 57 per cent over the previous Pounds 306,000. Turnover was static at Pounds 5.99m (Pounds 5.85m) although second half sales showed a 5 per cent rise over those for the corresponding period. Earnings rose to 20.1p (13.3p) and a final dividend of 3.1p makes a 5p (4.28p) total.
