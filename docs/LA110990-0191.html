November 9, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

HOMELESS TELL DUCHESS THANKS

A group of street children gave the Duchess of York flowers and a scroll of thanks Thursday for her help with a foundation to end homelessness among Australian youths.

The former Sarah Ferguson spoke at a fund-raising luncheon to initiate a foundation called Caring for Young Australians.

The former homeless youths joined about 350 community and corporate figures at the event.
