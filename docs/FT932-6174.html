930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / UK Company News: MMI shares suspended at 25p Shares in MMI, the local authority-owned insurer, were suspended at 25p yesterday evening. The company said it had reached 'an advanced stage in discussions which are expected to lead to a substantial acquisition' and it would send details to shareholders as soon as possible. In March MMI sold its local authority and some other insurance business to Zurich Insurance for about Pounds 70m.
