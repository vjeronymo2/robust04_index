940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Denmans Electrical Denmans Electrical, the USM-traded wholesaler, importer and manufacturer of electrical goods and fittings, yesterday reported a 33 per cent advance in interim profits. On turnover ahead 22 per cent to Pounds 24.1m, helped by the acquisition last November of Palmer Riley, pre-tax profits for the six months to March 31 improved from Pounds 1.08m to Pounds 1.44m. The interim dividend goes up from 1.9p to 2.1p, payable from earnings per share up 30 per cent to 21.48p (16.53p). The shares fell 33p to 625p.
