March 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CULVER CITY;

CENTER PRESENTS TEEN VIDEO

Cope III Video Productions, a program of the Didi Hirsch Community Mental Health Center, will show, "Help, I Need Somebody," a video about teen-age problems and solutions.

The video was written, performed and produced by Culver City High School students involved in a three-month substance abuse prevention program, sponsored by the Los Angeles County Office of Alcohol Programs.

The premiere showing will be April 6 from 7 to 9 p.m. at the center, 4760 S. Sepulveda Blvd. Admission is free. The audience will have a chance to meet the students involved in producing the video.

For more information, call (213) 390-6612, Ext. 438.
