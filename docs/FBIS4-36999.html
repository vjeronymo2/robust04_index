BFN [Excerpts] Jeddah, 13 Jun (SPA) -- The Council of Ministers held its regular weekly meeting today at al-Salam Palace under the chairmanship of His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, second deputy prime minister, minister of defense and aviation and inspector general of the army. [passage omitted] Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz talked about the continuous efforts made by the government of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, under the leadership of the custonian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, who is continuing his consultations and contacts with his Arab brethren kings and heads of state, as well as his meetings with envoys sent to the kingdom with a view to finding rapid solutions to the deteriorating situation in the region, particularly the current situation in sister Yemen. The information minister said: "In this respect, his royal highness the second deputy prime minister listened to the views and questions of some ministers and he discussed all the opinions expressed and clarified the position of the kingdom about them. He also lauded the role played by the UN envoy, Lakhdar Brahimi, and wished him success in his crucial mission. [passage omitted]
