Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by Yosi Torpstein] [Editorial Report] Tel Aviv MA'ARIV in Hebrew on 3 March, in a page 12 report by Hanan Shlein and Eli Buhadana, adds: "The 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam squad of Hamas yesterday issued a leaflet demanding that Israeli Arabs avenge the Hebron massacre by attacking Israeli targets. The leaflet announces that the squad will escalate its activities on the 25th of each month. On 9 March, the leaflet announces, `we will burn all Israeli cars, industrial plants, and farmland.' "A new Fatah body calling itself the Western Sector Eagles have disseminated a leaflet in the territories. The leaflet calls on the Palestinians to take up arms and fight the army and settlers. The Western Sector is the name of the Fatah's military apparatus formerly headed by Abu-Jihad [Khalil al-Wazir]."
