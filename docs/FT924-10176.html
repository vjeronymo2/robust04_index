921102 FT 02 NOV 92 / Premier Hariri announces new Lebenese cabinet Mr Rafik Hariri, the new Lebanese prime minister, announced at the weekend a 30-strong, half-Christian, half-Moslem cabinet of which 12 members are technocrats or business associates of his, writes Lara Marlowe in Beirut. The prime minister, who has taken the finance portfolio, has appointed Mr Fouad Siniora, who headed Mr Hariri's banking group in Lebanon, as minister without portfolio.
