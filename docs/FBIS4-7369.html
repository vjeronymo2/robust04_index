BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received messages of greetings from foreign state and party leaders on the occasion of his birthday. They are from President of the Republic of the Niger Mahamane Ousmane, President of the Republic of the Cote d'Ivoire Henri Konan Bedie, general secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Bangladesh National Socialist Party Hasanul Huq Inu, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers and Peasants Socialist Party of Bangladesh Nir Mal Sen, convenor of the Central Committee of the Socialist Party of Bangladesh Khalequzzaman Bhuiyan, convenor of the Central Committee of the Socialist Party of Bangladesh A.F.M. Mahbubul Huq, chairman of the National Executive Committee of the Polish League of Communists "Proletariat" Zbigniew Wictor, chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party-KPML(R) [expansion unknown] of Sweden Frank Baude, chairman of the National Direction Committee of the Democratic Intervention of Portugal Blasco Hugo Fernandes, chairman of the Central Committee of the Czechy-Morava Communist Party Miroslav Grevenicek, general secretary of the New Yugoslav Communist Movement Branko Kitanovic, secretary of the Lebanese Regional Committee of the Arab Socialist Baath Party Abdallah al Chahal, chairman of the Council of the Federation of Communist Party-the Communist Party of the Soviet Union O. Shenin, and general secretary of the Central Committee of the Jordanian Communist Party Yacob Zayadine. The messages most warmly congratulate President Kim Il-song and wish him good health and a long life. Expressing firm support and solidarity for the struggle of the Korean people to achieve national reunification in an independent and peaceful way with the great unity of the whole nation, they evince the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the Korean people and the peoples of the afore-said countries and between the Workers' Party of Korea and their parties would grow stronger and develop.
