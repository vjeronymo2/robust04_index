941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / City jobs scheme short of targets Only 16 local people were offered jobs in the City of London during the first year of Local Recruitment Brokerage, a non-profit making company backed by the Bank of England which aims to recruit from neighbouring areas with high unemployment. The target figure for job offers had been 200. 'We were over-ambitious,' said Ms Sharon Collins, the company's managing director, yesterday. But Ms Collins said the placement level had improved over the past six months.
