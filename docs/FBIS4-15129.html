CSO [Unattributed report: "Six for One"] [Text] A prisoner exchange was organized several days ago in a region of Nagornyy Karabakh by the Karabakh and Azerbaijan State Commissions for Matters Dealing with Hostages, Missing Persons, and Prisoners of War. According to data from the Noyan Tapan agency, one prisoner of war of Armenian nationality was exchanged for six Azeri hostages.
