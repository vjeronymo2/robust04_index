July 5, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

PRINCESS DI HONORS TENOR

Princess Diana gave Spanish tenor Jose Carreras an honorary membership in the Royal Academy of Music on Wednesday.

Carreras, who resumed his singing career two years ago after bone marrow surgery for leukemia, traveled here for the school's graduation ceremony.

"It is a great honor because this is one of the most prestigious institutions for music in the world and it is a very special moment for me," the 44-year-old star said.
