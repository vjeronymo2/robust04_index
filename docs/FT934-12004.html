931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Tata Engineering profit up 49% TATA Engineering & Locomotive, part of Tata grouping, India's largest commercial grouping, announced a 49 per cent jump in net profit to Rs180.5m (Dollars 5.94m) for the six months ended September 30, Our Financial Staff writes. The announcement provided the springboard for a rise in Tata Engineering's share price, which yesterday languished Rs2.50 down at Rs270 until a late rally to Rs275.
