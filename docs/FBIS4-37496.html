BFN [Text] Itamar Rabinovich, Israel's ambassador in the United States, said that a date and level has so far not been set for meetings between Israeli and Jordanian representatives. Responding to King Husayn's remarks that there would soon be overt meetings between the two countries, Ambassador Rabinovich said that although important progress has been made in the negotiations with Jordan, issues have to be defined precisely prior to overt meetings. The ambassador added that U.S. Secretary of State Christopher is planning to visit the region in July. Ambassador Rabinovich said the focus in the Israeli-Syrian negotiations must be a direct dialogue between the sides. The ambassador remarks to our correspondent Ya'el Eylon at a meeting of Jewish leaders at the president's residence in Jerusalem.
