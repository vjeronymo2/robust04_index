BFN [Text] Canberra, 8 Apr (XINHUA) -- According to a report from Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea Governor General Korowi indicated on 7 April: The Papua New Guinea Government attaches importance to developing economic and trade cooperation with China and hopes to import China's technology and personnel to help develop its medium and small enterprises. Korowi made the above statement when receiving credentials from the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Papua New Guinea Yuan Zude. During the conversation, Korowi highly commended China's accomplishments in its reform and opening up and its important roles in international affairs. He was satisfied that Papua New Guinea- Chinese relations have been progressing smoothly and that both countries have been mutually supporting, and actively cooperating with, each other on international issues. He said: He expects China to expand its investments in Papua New Guinea and to develop Papua New Guinea's resources to enhance bilateral cooperation and economic and trade exchanges that are mutually beneficial.
