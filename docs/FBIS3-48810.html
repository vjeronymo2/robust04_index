Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today at the Ministry for External Relations of Mongolia signed was the inter-government agreement on definition of the junction points of the borders of Mongolia, the Russian Federation and the People's Republic of China by the First Vice-Minister of External Relations of Mongolia J. Choinhor [name as received], the Ambassador of Russia to Mongolia S.S. Razov, the Ambassador of China to Mongolia Pei Jiayi. J. Choinhor characterized the signing of the inter-government agreement as a manifestation of the respect for sovereignty of Mongolia on the part of China and Russia. Tne Russian Ambassador S.S. Razov highly valued the work of the experts of the three sides and noted that the signing of the inter-government agreement is not just an event of a technical character, but is of great importance [for] relations, not only in trilateral but in development of constructive, positive changes in mutual relations in this region. The Ambassador of China to Mongolia Pei Jiayi noted that the signing of the agreement is a contribution to the development of friendly relations and cooperation between the countries and a guarantee for stability on borders of Mongolia, China, Russia.
