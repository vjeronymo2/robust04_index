940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / International Company News: Rhone-Poulenc sells unit to NZ group Salmond Smith Biolab, of New Zealand, said it had agreed with Rhone-Poulenc Australia, a unit of the French Rhone-Poulenc chemicals and pharmaceuticals group, to buy the Australian and New Zealand business of Rhone Poulenc Laboratory Products Australia for an undisclosed sum, Reuter reports from Wellington.
