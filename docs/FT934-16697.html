931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: TDG makes Pounds 2.1m acquisition Transport Development Group has bought Young's (Stokesley), an established dry bulk tank operator and warehousing company, for Pounds 2.1m. The business, which operates a fleet of over 100 tanks and containers from two locations on Teesside, will be integrated with Nexus Logistics, TDG's specialist business focused on the movement of dry bulk chemicals and foodstuffs.
