941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / Christie's overtakes rival Sotheby's Christie's, the fine art auction house, has edged ahead of rival Sotheby's for the first time in nearly 40 years. In the autumn season Christie's recorded a 10 per cent rise in turnover from Pounds 357m to Pounds 393m (Dollars 625m). Sotheby's sales fell from Pounds 428m a year ago to Pounds 370m. Over the year as a whole Sotheby's is slightly ahead, with a turnover of Pounds 862m compared with Pounds 818m at Christie's.
