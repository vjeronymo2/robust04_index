920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pimento Jamaica lowered its official prices for pimento to usd 1900 per mt, fob Kingston reports Man Producten. Decreasing almost usd 400 per tonne hoping to be more competitive to Mexican, Honduran and Guatemalan pimento. Quotes here in the market usd 2325-2300 for spot Jamaica and shipment usd 2235 cif terms. Mexico is offered between usd 1550 and 1500 cif terms, spot nothing available. Madagascan cinnamom quotes between FFr5,50 and FFr5,75 cif terms. Cloves and nutmegs/mace maintain weak undertone. Indian cochin ginger firm on shipment. Nigerian available at usd 850 spot Rotterdam.
