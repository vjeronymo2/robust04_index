Poly (96.5/3.5 ethylene/cyclohexylenedimethylene terephthalate) HTS number: 3907.60.00.90 CAS number: 26100&hyph;86&hyph;7 Poly (96.5/3.5 ethylene/cyclohexylenedimethylene terephthalate) is derived from the taxable chemicals ethylene and xylene. Poly (96.5/3.5 ethylene/cyclohexylenedimethylene terephthalate), a solid, is produced predominantly by reacting dimethyl terephthalate with ethylene glycol and cyclohexanedimethanol. Dimethyl terephthalate is produced by the air oxidation of p-xylene to yield terephthalic acid, and the acid is subsequently esterified to the dimethyl ester with methanol. Ethylene glycol is produced via reaction of ethylene with oxygen and water, and cyclohexanedimethanol is produced via the hydrogenation of dimethyl terephthalate.
