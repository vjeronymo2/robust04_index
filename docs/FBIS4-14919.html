CSO ["Election Results in Zaporizhzhya Oblast"] [Text] Elections to the Supreme Council of Ukraine were held in Zaporizhzhya Oblast. Vyacheslav Byelskyy (CPU), collective farm chairman, won in Vasylivka e.d. No. 189. Those who will stand for election in the second round are: in Zhovtnevyy e.d. No. 177 -- Yuriy Syzenko, first secretary of the oblast committee of the CPU, and Tamara Kudlay (independent), doctor of juridical sciences; in Zavodskyy e.d. No. 178 -- Leonid Anisimov (CPU) and Serhiy Kazanov, an independent candidate; in Komunarskyy e.d. No. 179 -- Serhiy Kuzmenko, candidate of the AvtoZAZ labor collective, and Oleksandr Yatsenko ("Ukrayina" bloc); in Levanevskyy e.d. No. 180 -- Mykola Taran (CPU) and Pavlo Fedotyuk, chief of SIZO [investigative isolation facilities]; in Leninskyy e.d. No. 181 -- Oleksandra Kuzhel ("My" [We] bloc, which unites PDVU members) and Mykhaylo Kalynyak ("Ukrayina" bloc); in Ordzhonikidzevskyy e.d. No. 181 -- Viktor Ponedilko (CPU) and Yevhen Kostenko ("My" bloc); in Khortytskyy e.d. No. 183 -- People's Deputy of Ukraine Serhiy Sobolyev ("My" bloc) and Yuriy Yerokhov, no party affiliation; in Shevchenkivskyy e.d. No. 184 -- Fedir Sunitsyn ("My" bloc) and People's Deputy of Ukraine Valeriy Izmalkov. In Berdyansk e.d. No. 185 -- Yevhen Todorov, first secretary of the city committee of the CPU, and Serhiy Kharchenko, no party affiliation; in Melitopol city e.d. No. 186 -- Hryhoriy Oharkov and Vasyl Didur, both with no party affiliation; in Tokmak e.d. No. 187 -- Viktor Yurchenko SelPU) and Oleksandr Moroz, first secretary of the city committee of the CPU; in Berdyansk rayon e.d. No. 188 -- Industry Minister Oleksandr Holubchenko and Volodymyr Chichkov, collective farm chairman; in Zaporizhzhya rayon e.d. No. 190 -- Borys Oliynyk (CPU) and Oleksiy Spaskyy (SelPU); in Kamyanka-Dniprova e.d. No. 191 -- Hryhoriy Lunyov (SelPU) and Volodymyr Murashov (candidate of the Zaporizhzhya AES labor collective); in Melitopol rayon e.d. No. 192 -- Stepan Tkachenko, editor of the Prosvita newspaper, and Volodymyr Hrytchyn, collective farm chairman; in Polohy e.d. No. 183 -- Oleksandr Yermak, deputy director of counterintelligence of the Security Service of Ukraine, and Volodymyr Bruy; in Pryazovske e.d. No. 194 -- collective farm chairmen Oleksandr Bryt and Oleksandr Lysenko.
