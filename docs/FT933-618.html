930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Greenacre jumps 31% on expansion Greenacre Group, the nursing home operator, benefited from continuing expansion to produce a 31 per cent rise in interim profits, from Pounds 605,000 to Pounds 794,000 pre-tax. The outcome was achieved despite a turnround from net interest receivable of Pounds 152,000 to payable of Pounds 45,000. Over the six months to July 31 the USM-traded group lifted the number of beds under operation and/or development from 384 to 575, located in 12 homes across the UK. Turnover rose to Pounds 3.32m (Pounds 2.08m) including a Pounds 194,000 contribution from acquisitions. Profits at the operating level leapt 85 per cent to Pounds 839,000 (Pounds 453,000) with acquisitions accounting for Pounds 60,000. The interim dividend is raised to 0.15p (0.13p), payable from fully diluted earnings of 0.32p (0.25p) per share.
