Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Lieutenant General Nikolaos Vorvolakos, the new National Guard chief, was received this morning by House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos. Defense Minister Konstandinos Iliadhis attended the meeting. In a statement, Galanos said the new National Guard chief is assuming his duties at a very critical period. He added that he is convinced that Vorvolakos and the defense minister will proceed to implement the defense doctrine declared between the Greek and Cypriot Governments. [passage omitted]
