920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / Observer: Extra special Are any companies retaining PR consultancies finding that the London Hilton on Park Lane is increasingly being recommended as a venue for product-launches, press conferences and the like? If so, and they're wondering why, the answer may lie in a 'very special offer' by the hotel's general manager Michael Schuetzendorf to 'all bona fide public relations professionals' who book the hotel for such a function between now and the end of the year. '. . . while you will be billed for the total amount spent with us,' his letter tells them, 'you will enjoy the benefit of a direct 8 per cent rebate to your company.'
