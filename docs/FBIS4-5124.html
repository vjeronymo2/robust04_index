BFN [Text] A regiment of the invading troops of the communist Vietnamese puppets and the two-headed government -- backed by five tanks, artillery, and multiple rocket batteries -- launched an attack on 5 April to break through our forces surrounding them in Sala Krau. The enemy was completely routed. Here is the preliminary result: We killed 15 soldiers and wounded a large number.
