931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Immunity vote goes against Tapie Businessman and former French urban affairs minister Bernard Tapie (left) was brought a step nearer investigation for alleged fraud after a National Assembly committee recommended the lifting of his parliamentary immunity. The vote follows a request by a state prosecutor investigating alleged embezzlement at Testut, a maker of scales and part of the holding company Bernard Tapie Finance. The full assembly will decide on the recommendation.
