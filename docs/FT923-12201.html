920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Nacer resigns as governor of central bank THE governor of the central bank, Mr Abderrahmane Hadj Nacer, resigned yesterday and was replaced by an Algerian diplomat, Mr Abelwahab Keramane, whose brother Abdenour holds the industry portfolio, writes Francis Ghiles. No official reason was given for the departure of the former governor, who argued against rescheduling Algeria's Dollars 25bn (Pounds 13bn) foreign debt in favour of refinancing it. Mr Nacer also backed convertibility of the dinar and allowing more freedom to the private sector.
