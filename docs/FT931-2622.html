930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Edmond Holdings Pounds 1.15m in the red Edmond Holdings, the housebuilder operating in Humberside, East Anglia and South Wales, fell Pounds 1.15m into loss at the pre-tax level in 1992. The previous year saw profits of Pounds 1.73m. Turnover amounted to Pounds 12.6m (Pounds 17.3m) and the pre-tax loss was after exceptional redundancy costs of Pounds 253,000 and provisions on land and debtors of Pounds 420,000. House completions totalled 232 at an average price of Pounds 54,000 compared with 329 at an average price of Pounds 52,500. Losses per share came through at 1.52p (2.41p earnings) and the proposed final dividend is cut to 0.15p (1.2p) for a total of 0.5p (1.85p).
