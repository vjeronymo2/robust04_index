BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 7 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received congratulatory messages and letters from the participants in celebration meetings held in Corriverton, Guyana, Kalofer, Bulgaria, Weneba, Ghana, Antananarivo, Madagascar, and Lima, Peru, on the occasion of his birthday. They say that President Kim Il-song has built independent socialism, brought great socio-economic changes and opened a new era of prosperity for the Korean nation by wisely leading the Korean revolution. They note that thanks to the wise leadership and tireless activities of President Kim Il-song, the Korean people are leading a happy and creative life under the Korean-style socialist system centred on the popular masses and Korea has been turned into a powerful sovereign and independent state with the might of singlehearted unity which the progressive people of the world admire. The messages and letters express full support and solidarity for the struggle of the Korean people to defend the sovereignty of the country from the aggression moves of the imperialists and achieve the cause of national reunification according to the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation advanced by President Kim Il-song. Letters to him were adopted at a symposium on President Kim Il-song and Korean-style socialism held in Amadora, Portugal, and a seminar on his work held by the Nepal Institute for Chuche Studies. The messages and letters wish President Kim Il-song good health and a long life.
