921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Bula to raise IPounds 10m via offer Bula Resources, the oil and gas exploration group, is to raise up to IPounds 10.7m (Pounds 11.55m) via an open offer of up to 1.08bn new shares at 1p each on a 2-for-1 basis. The target minimum amount to be raised is IPounds 800,000. Qualifying shareholders may apply for any number of open offer shares. Proposals to sub-divide and convert the ordinary 2.5p shares into 1p ordinary and 1.5p deferred shares will be put to shareholders at an extraordinary meeting. The interim results for the six months to June 30 showed there was a net profit of IPounds 31,000, compared with IPounds 34,000 last time. Turnover was down from IPounds 1.23m to IPounds 913,000. Earnings were little changed at 0.006p (0.007p).
