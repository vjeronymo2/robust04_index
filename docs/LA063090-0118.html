June 30, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Constitution Savings Closed: Federal regulators shut down Constitution Federal Savings & Loan in Monterey Park.

Depositors, who have $61.9 million in 5,200 accounts, will be paid off starting Monday, said the Resolution Trust Corp., the federal agency managing seized thrifts. There are few uninsured deposits -- amounts over $100,000. Holders of those amounts will be paid proportionately with other general creditors from proceeds of the liquidation of Constitution's $65.6 million in assets.
