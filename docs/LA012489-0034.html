January 24, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

CS NORTHRIDGE OPENS WIVA SEASON ON ROAD

Cal State Northridge, coming off an impressive nonconference victory over Chapman, begins Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn. play tonight at Pepperdine.

Northridge (1-0) lost to Pepperdine two weeks ago at the 24-team Santa Barbara tournament in a match that does not count on either team's overall record. Pepperdine, ranked fifth in the nation, finished third at the Santa Barbara tournament.

Northridge, which finished 7-11 in the WIVA last season, is led by outside hitter Bob Samuelson, who had 7 kills in the Matadors' sweep against Chapman on Thursday.
