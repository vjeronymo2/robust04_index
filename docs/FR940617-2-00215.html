How Must the Pre-Application Be Submitted? The applicant must submit one original and two copies of the preapplication (a signed SF 424, an SF 424A, and a work plan). The pre-application must be signed by a person authorized to receive funds. Please sign the original pre-application in blue ink to help EPA distinguish which document is the signed original and which documents are copies. Pre-applications must be reproducible. They should be stapled once in the upper left hand corner, on white paper, and with page numbers in the upper right hand corner.
