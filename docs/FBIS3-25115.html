Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Feb 6 SAPA--Yeoville MP Douglas Gibson was named Sunday [6 February] as the Democratic Party's [DP] PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] provincial chairman at the Johannesburg launch of the DP's election campaign. Hillbrow MP Lester Fuchs, Benoni-based senior court interpreter William Mnisi and Mr Robert Harpur, a human resources director from Pretoria, were named as his three vice-chairmen.
