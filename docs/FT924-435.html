921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / Toxic dam is shored up Efforts to strengthen the unstable Mojkovac dam in the Yugoslav republic of Montenegro are progressing well, Mr Hans Zimmermann, co-ordinator for the emergency repair work for the UN disaster relief agency in Geneva said yesterday, writes Frances Williams. Floods in October damaged the dam, threatening an ecologicial disaster if 7m tonnes of toxic waste pour into the Danube.
