December 16, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

FANS CAN'T TAKE TOO MUCH MAS OF THIS

It is widely accepted that former NBA center George Mikan is credited with the widening of the "key" area on a basketball court.

Well, after viewing the "no mas" boxing farce between Roberto Duran and Sugar Ray Leonard, Sugar Ray could very well be the boxer responsible for the shortening of the dimensions of a boxing ring. If I want to watch people dance, I'll rent "Saturday Night Fever." Give me Golden Gloves or Olympic boxing any time.

ANDY S. GUYER, San Diego
