Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Information Minister Dr. Jawad al-'Anani has denied that there was any intention to reshuffle Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali's cabinet now or even after 'Id al-Fitr [13 March]. In a statement to AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT, he said that he knew nothing about the reports about a possible imminent reshuffle of the Jordanian cabinet. He said that the cabinet as a whole still has not completed implementation of the program passed by parliament around the end of last year.
