Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Mar (MENA)--Dr. Nabil Najm, Iraqi permanent delegate to the Arab League, has stated that yesterday's renewal of sanctions on Iraq will have a tremendous effect on the Iraqi people and will cause more deaths among the Iraqi women, children, and patients. In a statement here today, Dr. Najm described this step as one that "will not help stability in the region" and denounced what he called "the double standards adopted by the Security Council on Arab issues." The Iraqi delegate denied that Iraq had proposed to swap some Kuwaiti detainees with some Iraqi defendants detained in Kuwaiti prisons on charges of attempting to assassinate former U.S. President George Bush. He added that this is completely untrue because Iraq has no Kuwaiti detainees.
