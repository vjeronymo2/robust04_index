BFN [Text] (Joe Jacob), vice president of the Irish House of Representatives, has arrived in Havana at the invitation of the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP]. (Jacob) is heading a delegation of three deputies, two senators, and a secretary. They were received at the Havana airport by Anicio Leon, president of the Cuban-Irish friendship group. In his welcoming words, Leon explained to the Irish congressmen the Cuban situation, the role of ANPP, and other aspects of our current situation. The NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY reported that the Irish deputies and senators expressed great interest in the development of Cuban public health, agriculture, and especially sports, in which area they aspire to expand the cooperation that began a few years ago.
