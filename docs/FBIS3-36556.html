Language: English Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev February 7 TASS -- Despite the critical situation in Yugoslavia brought to a head by the bomb explosion in downtown Sarajevo on Saturday that killed at least 68 people and wounded over 200, Ukraine has decided to go ahead with the planned replacement of its peace-keeping troops in that country, the press service of the Ukrainian Defence Ministry said. Ukraine dispatched the second planeload of peace-keepers to the Sarajevo sector on Monday, the press service said.
