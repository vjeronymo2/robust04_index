BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (XINHUA) -- Ren Jianxin, the president of China's Supreme People's Court, hosted a banquet here this evening in honor of a judicial delegation from Colombia. The 16-member delegation, headed by Hernando Herrera Vergas, president of the Colombian Constitutional Court, arrived here on Sunday at the invitation of China's Supreme People's Court. They are also scheduled to visit Xian and Shanghai.
