920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Paribas French net asset value rises Paribas French Investment Trust had a net asset value of 91.15p per share at December 31 against 80.98p a year earlier. Total income for the year was lower at Pounds 964,593 (Pounds 991,467) and net income fell from Pounds 459,028 to Pounds 405,259 after tax of Pounds 187,238 (Pounds 211,791). Earnings per share amounted to 1.35p (1.85p) and the proposed dividend is an unchanged 1.2p.
