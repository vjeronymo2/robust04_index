BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Apr (MENA)--Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will visit Egypt on 12 April. Sources from the Israeli delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations made this statement to MENA today. These sources added that Rabin is expected to meet during the visit with Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat. Informed sources in Washington had also spoken about the travel of U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher to Cairo on the same date to attend the ceremony in which Rabin and 'Arafat will sign the agreement on implementing autonomy in Gaza and Jericho which will take place under the patronage of President Husni Mubarak.
