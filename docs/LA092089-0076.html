September 20, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: NAMES

Stevie Wonder will donate royalties from his new song "Parents of the World" to UNICEF, the singer announced Tuesday at a Beverly Hills press conference. He will premiere the song Oct. 27 at the World Popular Song Festival in Tokyo. Proceeds from that event will also go to UNICEF. Wonder also said that he will perform at the NAACP 80th anniversary celebration in New York on Dec. 15. Wonder has long been an active supporter of the civil rights organization. ALEENE MacMINN
