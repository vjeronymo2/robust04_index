BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow May 20 TASS -- The recognition by the NATO leadership of Russia's special status as a great power may come either in a protocol within the frame of the Partnership for Peace program or in a separate bilateral document signed between the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation (NATO) and the Russian Federation, President Yeltsin told ITAR-TASS in the Kremlin today. According to President Boris Yeltsin, Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev will leave for Brussels soon to solve the problem through talks with the NATO leaders.
