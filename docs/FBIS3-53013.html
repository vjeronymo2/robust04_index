Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Major al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi has ended a visit to Tunisia during which he met President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali, [Prime Minister] brother Hamed Karoui, and a number of senior Tunisian officials. In a statement to JANA after his return from Tunisia, Maj. al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi praised the results of this visit and said that he was very grateful for the visit and the brotherly reception given him by the brothers in Tunisia headed by President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali. He added that President Ben Ali had asked him to convey brotherly greetings to the leader of the revolution [al-Qadhdhafi] and the Libyan Arab people and had reaffirmed Tunisia's firm solidarity with the Great Jamahiriyah.
