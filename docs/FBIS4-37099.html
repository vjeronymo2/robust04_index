BFN [Text] A $110-million loan agreement between the World Bank and Algeria was signed in Washington by Algerian Ambassador [to the United States] Noreddine Zerhouni and Daniel Richie, director of the Maghreb Department at the World Bank. This agreement, which was approved by the World Bank board, will finance in hard currency the acquisition of technical equipment to modernize the drinking water systems in the following towns: Oran, Annaba, Ain Timouchent, Bejaia, Jijel, Mascra, Relizane, Setif, Sidi Bel Abbes, and Tlemcen.
