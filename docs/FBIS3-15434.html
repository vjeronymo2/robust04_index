Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 5 [date as received] -- The Georgian Defense Ministry Press Center supplied the IBERIA news agency with the following statement: "On 4 March the `Alioni' program [Georgian TV] broadcast a report made public by the ITAR-TASS news agency on the beginning of the reorganization process in the Georgian Defense Ministry. It was mentioned that the reduction of Georgian Armed Forces would begin soon and certain structures would be abolished, and all this would be carried out under the control of the command of a group of Russian troops in Transcaucasia. The Georgian Defense Ministry Press Center is authorized to state that the Defense Ministry has long started the process of reorganization and all military activities are controlled by the Georgian Defense Ministry leadership. As for relations with the Russian Armed Forces, all packages of documents pertaining to this issue were signed on 3 February, and the contents of the documents are well-known. The process of settling the relations is still under way. A Georgian delegation, including the Georgian prime minister and the Defense Ministry leadership, will hold talks in Moscow soon. The results of the talks will be known after the delegation returns to Tbilisi.
