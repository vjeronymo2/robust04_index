Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian Finance Ministry intends to continue the struggle against inflation using a stricter financial policy combined with re-targeting the investments on specific sectors of the economy, according to an official statement by the Finance Ministry. The ministry will accomplish the preparation of budget proposals for 1994 within the next few days to submit them for consideration by the government and, later, by the new Russian parliament. The draft budget will strictly conform to the strategy of tackling inflation and restructuring the economy pursued by the president and the government, the Finance Ministry pointed out. The this year's budget expenditures will be severely restricted due to limited revenues. At the same time, the Finance Ministry believes the draft budget is quite realistic and feasible. If approved by the State Duma, it will be a good basis for a further financial stabilization and the necessary adjustment of the social and economic policies. The Finance Ministry points out that the central financial problem of the first quarter of 1994 is the last year's debts. The ministry said it was taking all possible measures to find additional sources of incomes for their reimbursement.
