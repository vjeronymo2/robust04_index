July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD NOTES

Caltrans plans the following closures on area freeways this week:

* One lane in the eastbound Foothill Freeway (210) will be closed Monday from Magnolia Ave. to California Ave. in Monrovia from 9 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.

* One lane in the westbound Foothill Freeway (210) will be closed Tuesday from Amelia Ave. to Lonehill Ave. in Glendora from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

* Two lanes in the westbound Foothill Freeway (210) will be closed Wednesday at Huntington Drive in Pomona from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.
