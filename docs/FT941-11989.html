940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / International Company News: Knight-Ridder optimistic Knight-Ridder, the US media group, expects its best year of earnings per share growth since 1989, Reuter reports. Fourth-quarter 1993 earnings per share were 93 cents, a gain of 17.7 per cent over the 79 cents in the fourth quarter of 1992. Operating income rose 9.7 per cent to Dollars 96.6m. For all of 1993, the company earned Dollars 2.68 a share, a gain of 1.1 per cent on 1992 before a cumulative charge of Dollars 1.91 a share for accounting changes.
