940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Quadramatic Quadramatic, the coin handling and optical group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.72m from a turnover of Pounds 17.6m for the six months ended March 31. A maiden interim dividend of 2.5p is being paid from earnings of 6.7p. Quadramatic also announced the acquisition of certain assets of KM Comark, a temperature instrumentation business for Pounds 3.85m to be satisfied by a vendor placing of 2.26m shares at 170p.
