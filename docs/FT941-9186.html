940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / People: Non-executive directors George Wright, regional secretary in Wales of the Transport and General Workers Union, is joining the board of the Welsh Development Agency. Wright, who is 66, is the WDA's third non-executive director to be appointed by John Redwood, the Welsh secretary, since the start of the year. But the agency, in the throes of a management restructuring after being criticised by the Commons public accounts committee, still awaits a chief executive to replace Philip Head, who is resigning.
