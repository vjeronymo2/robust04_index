920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / Ramsden back MR TERRY RAMSDEN, former chairman of Glen International, will appear today at City of London magistrates' court charged with 22 offences involving fraudulent trading, obtaining property by deception, dishonestly inducing creditors to wait for payment, false accounting, making deceptive statements to investors and making false statements to government-appointed inspectors. The Serious Fraud Office said last night that Mr Ramsden had returned to the UK yesterday after waiving his right to contest extradition proceedings from the US.
