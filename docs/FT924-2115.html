921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / SFA puts ban on futures firm AN INVESTMENT firm dealing in the futures markets has been banned from dealing with personal clients after a number of complaints to the Securities and Futures Authority, the self-regulatory body. David Coakley, based in the West End of London, had 'repeatedly failed' to ensure the appropriateness of investments undertaken for clients, and had put its own interest above those of its clients, the SFA said.
