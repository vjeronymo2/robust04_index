930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / International Company News: Sears, Roebuck sells division SEARS, Roebuck, the large retail and financial services group, is selling its business centres division to InaCom, a Nebraska-based company which markets computer systems and services, for an undisclosed sum. Sears started the Sears Business Centers operation in 1981. The division sells advanced computing equipment and networking products aimed at business users through free-standing branch offices.
