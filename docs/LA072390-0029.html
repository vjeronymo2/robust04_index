July 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Tuesday: Many of the nation's leading filmmakers are expected to deliver a petition to the Motion Picture Assn. of America asking for a change to the movie rating system.

Wednesday: Will Rosanne Barr get a hit or a strike out when she sings the national anthem at San Diego's Jack Murphy Stadium before the second game of a double-header between the San Diego Padres and the Cincinnati Reds?

Friday: Protesters may be present when David Wojnarowicz' "Tongues of Flame," the traveling show attacked by Rev. Donald Wildmon, who is now being sued by Wojnarowicz for defamation and copyright infringement, opens at the Santa Monica Museum of Art. GREG BRAXTON
