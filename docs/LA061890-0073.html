June 18, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Cineplex Odeon to Sell More Theaters: The movie theater chain said it is selling 24 movie theaters in the Carolinas and Tennessee to Carmike Cinemas Inc. of Columbus, Ga., for $11 million. Toronto-based Cineplex Odeon announced last week that it was selling 38 theaters in five Midwest and Western states to Plitt Amusement Co. Inc. of Los Angeles for $31.7 million. Cineplex, which has been under financial pressure, will use proceeds from the sales to pay down its long-term debt.
