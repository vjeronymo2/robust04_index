940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Period extended for Granada's LWT bid Another 15-day extension for consideration of the Pounds 716m hostile by Granada Group for LWT (Holdings), its fellow ITV franchise holder, has been given by the Office of Fair Trading. It has been granted to give Mr Michael Heseltine, secretary of state for trade and industry, more time to rule on the office's recommendations.
