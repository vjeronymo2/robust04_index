Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] [Werner] Girke, the former administrator of the foreign assets of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany [SED], has denied having forwarded funds to Russian nationalist Zhirinovskiy. In an interview with STERN, Girke stated that he met incidentally with Zhirinovskiy in Austria shortly before Christmas. He also rejected the accusation concerning embezzled SED assets. In fact, he said, over 53 million German marks [DM] have been transferred to accounts held on trust. Another DM6 million will follow shortly.
