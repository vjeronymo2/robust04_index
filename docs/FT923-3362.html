920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest net asset value falls Berry Starquest, the investment trust, saw its net asset value fall from 181.2p to 157.1p over the six months to July 31. The company blamed the recession and investors' attitudes to smaller companies. A year earlier the net asset value had been 177.5p. Net profit for the six months was lower at Pounds 40,000 (Pounds 119,000) for earnings per share of 0.8p (2.3p).
