941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Castings advances to Pounds 3.1m Castings, the maker of iron and precision castings, said demand had remained high in the six months to September 30 and sales rose 20 per cent from Pounds 18.3m to Pounds 22.1m. This worked through into pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.1m, up 55 per cent from Pounds 1.97m. The interim dividend has been raised to 1p (0.775p restated) on earnings per share of 4.81p (3.125p).
