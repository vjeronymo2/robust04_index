September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

HEY, SOME OF THESE PEOPLE ARE FAMOUS FOR REAL REASONS TOO

Your in-depth report (on "Famous for Being Famous") failed to mention Angelique (I think that's her name) -- the Hollywood poster bimbo.

I remember reading or hearing somewhere that she has a manager to make her famous. She's not an actress, not a major league ball player and doesn't endorse a product to my knowledge. But she's aspiring to be famous.

What a country!

MICHAEL B. BRISON, San Diego
