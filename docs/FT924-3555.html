921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Burtonwood in Pounds 5m expansion Burtonwood Brewery has agreed to buy 34 public houses from Tetley Walker for a cash consideration of Pounds 5m. Cheshire-based Burtonwood was confident that the contribution from about 9,000 extra barrels would improve the utilisation of brewery capacity and thus increase its profitability.
