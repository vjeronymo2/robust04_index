Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 3 (KCNA) -- Premier of the Administration Council Kang Song-san sent a message of greetings to Sylvestre Siale Bileka upon his appointment as prime minister of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations existing between the two countries would continue to develop and wished the prime minister great success in his responsible work.
