Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 10 Feb (INA) -- Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf this evening received Asif Zardari, Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto's personal envoy to President Saddam Husayn, and his accompanying delegation. The two sides reviewed ways to enhance bilateral relations. They also exchanged views on several issues of mutual interest. The meeting was attended by 'Abd-al-Jabbar al-Duri, the Foreign Ministry under secretary, and Pakistan's ambassador in Baghdad.
