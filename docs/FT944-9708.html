941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Copper surges Copper reached a four-year high on the London Metal Exchange after it rebounded from a morning bout of selling. The three months delivery contract reached Dollars 2,751 a tonne at the close, an increase of Dollars 68.50. Other base metals followed copper's lead and all ended the day with substantial gains.
