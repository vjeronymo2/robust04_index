BFN [ADS report under the "Regional News" rubric: "Government Is Allocating 85 Billion Rubles to South Kurils" -- from the FINANSOVYYE IZVESTIYA section] [Text] Sakhalin Oblast Administration has received notification from the Economy Ministry regarding the allocation of 85 billion rubles [R] in 1994 to the federal program titled "The Social and Economic Development of the Kuril Islands for 1994, 1995, and Through 2000." Sakhalin Oblast Vice Governor Viktor Serenko announced that R26.6 billion of this sum would be channeled into modernizing or constructing airports and seaports on Iturup and Kunashir, and also developing the power industry and air transportation and communications. In addition, R24.89 billion will be allocated in the form of investment loans to enterprises that take part in implementing these projects. The remaining R34.45 billion are earmarked for current expenditure (transportation costs and freight and food deliveries).
