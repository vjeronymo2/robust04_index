May 18, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SERVICE PLANNED FOR DANCER SCARBOROUGH

A memorial service for Dr. Danny Scarborough, a prominent dance maker and unofficial caretaker for black cultural expressions in San Diego, will be held at 4 p.m. Friday.

Scarborough, who was 41, died May 11 after a two-year battle with AIDS.

The service to honor the former associate professor of dance and literature at San Diego State University will be in the university's Dramatic Arts Building.
