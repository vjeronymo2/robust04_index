August 29, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

'CAN WE GO HOME AGAIN?'

Dennis McDougal's story ("Lynwood: Downhill on the Freeway," Part I, Aug. 20) was timely, interesting and certainly pointed out the major changes occurring in our region.

Although he focused on Lynwood, the former All-American city, other areas have also undergone major changes. Overbuilding and clogged streets have all contributed to an erosion of the quality of life.

I hope we will see more stories from The Times on changes in our cities and areas.

Can we go home again?

CLIFF DEKTAR

North Hollywood
