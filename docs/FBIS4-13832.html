BFN [Text] Mashhad (Khorasan), April 22, IRNA -- Doctors at the Qaem hospital here have described the condition of Friday prayer leader Mohammad Abaei Khorasani, as "satisfactory". Hojjat ol-eslam Abaei who substituted as the Friday prayer leader today, was shot at by a lone gunman just as he had finished the sermon. "Luckily the bullets hit the back of his shoulder and did not cause him any serious injury," said Dr. Fattahi the Mashhad representative to the National Parliament (Majlis) who happens to be a specialist surgeon by profession. The terrorist tried to shoot himself and was injured badly when faced with the fury of the worshippers. He was immediately whisked away by law officials.
