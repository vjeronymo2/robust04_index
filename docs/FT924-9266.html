921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Ever Ready to cut 175 jobs EVER READY, the battery manufacturer, is to cut 175 jobs at its Newburn plant, Newcastle upon Tyne, as part of a review of its European operations by Ralston Purina, its US parent. The cuts follow a decision to end production of alkaline batteries at Newburn. A staff of 30 will continue producing zinc-carbon batteries. Newburn will become a satellite of the company's other plant at Tanfield Lea, County Durham.
