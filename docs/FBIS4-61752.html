BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Grachev] [Text] Vladivostok May 10 TASS -- Another Russian Pacific Fleet officer was murdered by several gunshots fired into his back near the entrance to his house. According to the Pacific Fleet Navy press service reports, the victim was returning home after patrolling city streets on Monday night, when he was suddenly attacked by two unidentified persons. The officer had his service revolver on his person, but he could notv use it. The bandits took away his pistol and documents and disappeared. Lately there have been quite frequent attacks on army officers in the maritime territory, mainly with the aim of robbing them of their service arms. Major Tedeyev, who was on guard duty in his unit, was attacked by two unidentified persons two weeks ago. After murdering him, the bandits ran away with two pistols and cartridges. The Vladivostok police department and the military prosecutor's office are investigating the case.
