February 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

U2 Singer Advocates Human Rights: Bono Hewson, lead singer of U2, makes a plea to promote human rights on a public service announcement airing at the end of Fox's "21 Jump Street" tonight at 9 p.m. The announcement will encourage viewers to call Amnesty International for more information on human rights activities. The U2 hit "With or Without You" will be part of the spot. The "21 Jump Street" episode deals with the civil war in El Salvador. BETH KLEID
