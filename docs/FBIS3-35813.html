Language: English Article Type:BFN [By CRIME-PRESS correspondent Yuriy Shcherban] [Text] Moscow January 31 TASS -- The trial of organisers of the 1991 abortive Soviet coup adjourned indefinitely again because of an illness of one of the accused, CRIME PRESS learned from the court on Monday. This time it is Former KGB Chairman Vladimir Kryuchkov who is ill. The court did not say what the disease was. The trial has already adjourned several times because of bad health of several defendants. On Tuesday the court planned to begin interrogating Oleg Sheynin, former secretary of the Soviet Communist Party.
