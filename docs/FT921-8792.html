920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG drifted lower in quiet trading. The JSE share index fell 25 to 3,672, while the industrial index shed 51 to 4,462. The all-gold index weakened 18 to 1,308. Vaal Reefs fell R1.50 to R228.50 as the gold price slipped to Dollars 353 an ounce.
