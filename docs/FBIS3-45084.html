Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] A provincial feedback meeting about curbing smuggling ended in Fuzhou yesterday. It was learned from this meeting that since last year, the province handled 2,669 smuggling cases involving cars, motorcycles, color television sets, video recorders, air conditioners, cigarettes, gold, and 49,406 grams of drugs, the total value being 187 million yuan. At the meeting, Vice Governor Tong Wanheng pointed out: In this new year, there is a need to continue to implement the spirit of the central meeting on curbing smuggling activities, to profoundly carry out a combined operation, and to increase the momentum of crackdown to ensure phased achievements in the period up to the Spring Festival.
