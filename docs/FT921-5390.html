920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / Armenia accuses Azerbaijan of shooting down helicopter Armenia yesterday accused Azerbaijan of shooting down a low-flying military helicopter carrying 43 people, mainly women and children, the independent Interfax news agency reported. All survived. Interfax quoted Armenia's interior ministry as saying the helicopter was flying from Stepanakert, chief town of Nagorno-Karabakh, to Armenia. It said the aircraft came down near the Azeri town of Kelbadzhar. 'According to the ministry's information, the helicopter, carrying mainly women and children, was hit by fire from Azeri armed units,' it said. Earlier, armed groups and civilian protesters halted the withdrawal of Red Army troops from their base in Stepanakert. Armenian residents say only the army can prevent an all-out Azerbaijani attack.
