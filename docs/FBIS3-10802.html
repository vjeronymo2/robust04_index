Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [passage omitted] Gabriel Valdes was elected today as Senate president, and Socialist Senator Ricardo Nunez as Senate vice president, thus concluding one of the most anecdotal chapters of Chilean policy. According to a political agreement reached at the last minute, National Renewal Party, RN, Senator Sergio Diez will assume the position as Senate president in 1996, and Independent Democratic Union leader Eugenio Cantuarias will become the Senate vice president. One year later, RN Senator Sergio Romero will become the Senate president, while Cantuarias will continue in his position as Senate vice president. [passage omitted] Party for Democracy Deputy Jorge Schaulsohn was elected Chamber of Deputies chairman with 65 votes against 51 votes received by rightist Independent Democratic Union candidate Andres Chadwick. Christian Democratic Party leader Juan Carlos Latorre was elected first vice chairman with 68 votes against 51 votes obtained by RN leader Marina Prochelle. RN Deputy Alberto Espina was elected second vice chairman with 95 votes. [passage omitted]
