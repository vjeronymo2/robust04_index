Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Haitian Armed Forces Headquarters announced that the forces of order conducted simultaneous operations in the regions of Malick, Petionville, Thomassin 23, and Carrefour-Marin, Croix-des-Bouquets, on 5 March. During these operations, a stockpile of weapons, ammunition, and explosives were seized, and some people were arrested. The preliminary elements of the investigation, according to the press release, confirm that this group of terrorists intended that same day to attack military posts in the capital and in the Central Department, to perpetrate attacks against civilian leaders, and against members of the Haitian Armed Forces and their relatives. The Army Headquarters, in this press release, asks all Haitians for actions favoring the spirit of dialogue, consultation, and reconciliation in view of finding a Haitian solution to our country's problems, for too long wounded by the division among its children.
