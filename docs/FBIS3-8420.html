Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 8 (ATA) -- Two drunk Serbian policemen killed Faik Adem Maloku and wounded Xhevat Bajzaku on March 6 in the "Lokomotiva" Cafe on the main road of Fushe-Kosova, during the control they made among the people in the cafe. Maloku did not had his identity card with him and was explaining the police that he was a local inhabitant known by the people there, but one of the Serbian policeman shot Maloku dead with his pistol. The other Albanian, Xhevat Bajzaku, tried to avoid the same tragedy on him, but he got seriously wounded by the drunk policemen.
