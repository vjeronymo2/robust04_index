BFN [Text] Today marks the 30th anniversary of the death of Comrade Jiao Yulu. The "Memorial Hall of Comrade Jiao Yulu" and the "Bronze Statue of Jiao Yulu" were solemnly and simultaneously inaugurated at a ceremony held in Lankao County today. Jiang Zemin, CPC Central Committee general secretary, had inscribed "Memorial Hall of Comrade Jiao Yulu" for the ceremony. Those attending the ceremony included: Hu Jintao, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee member and Secretariat member; Lu Feng, CPC Central Committee Organization Department director; Zeng Qinghong, CPC Central Committee General Office director; Zheng Keyang, CPC Central Committee Central Policy Research Center director; other CPC Central Committee department leaders and state ministry leaders; Henan Provincial CPC Committee and People's Government leaders, such as Li Changchun, Ren Keli, Song Zhaosu, Wu Guangxian [provincial military district political commissar], Song Guochen, Ma Jianzhang, Zhang Wenbin, and Zhang Shiying; and Kaifeng City and Lankao County leaders. Today's ceremony was presided over by Song Zhaosu, Henan Provincial CPC Committee deputy secretary. Hu Jintao; Li Changchun, Henan Provincial CPC Committee secretary; and Wang Rixin, Kaifeng City CPC Committee secretary, cut the ribbon at the inauguration ceremony for the "Memorial Hall of Comrade Jiao Yulu." Hu Dawei, Lankao County CPC Committee secretary, delivered a speech at the ceremony in which he cherished dearly the memory of the late Comrade Jiao Yulu and urged all comrades to learn from him; carry forward the Jiao Yulu spirit; and strive to make a success of Lankao's work. Then the CPC department and state ministry leaders; the provincial, prefectural, and county leaders; and the late Comrade Jiao Yulu's family members, including sons and daughters, entered the memorial hall, where they stood in silent mourning in front of the bronze statue. After Hu Jintao and Li Changchun had unveiled the statue, all the comrades present made three deep bows to it. When the ceremonies were over, all the comrades were shown around the memorial hall. On behalf of the CPC Central Committee, Hu Jintao laid a wreath at the tomb of Comrade Jiao Yulu. Also laid at the tomb were wreaths from: The CPC Central Committee General Office; the CPC Central Committee Organization Department; the CPC Central Committee Propaganda Department; and the Henan Provincial CPC Committee. In front of the tomb, Hu Jintao cordially met with the late Jiao Yulu's wife, sons, and daughters and had a group photo taken with them.
