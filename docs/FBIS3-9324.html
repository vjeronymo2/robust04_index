Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, March 18 (BTA) -- Prime Minister Lyuben Berov is in good condition a week after major by-pass surgery, the Council of Ministers said. His cardio-vascular system, as well as his other organs and systems, are functioning normally. Berov's recovery is following the regular course, aided by postoperative treatment and exercise. In a conversation today with Yunal Lyutfi of the ethnic Turks' Movement for Rights and Freedoms the prime minister said he would be back to work soon. Lyutfi quoted Berov's words in an informal conversation in parliament after they had talked at the hospital of the Military Medical Academy. Berov takes eight steps every day. Twenty days after the operation he will move to Bankya near Sofia for further treatment.
