930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / Funds for Barrow FURNESS Challenge, a Pounds 400,000 loan fund with a fixed interest rate of 5 per cent for 10 years and discretionary two-year capital repayment holidays, has been launched by National Westminster Bank, VSEL and economic development bodies. The fund aims to promote small and medium businesses in the Barrow in Furness area.
