Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of fileworthy reports on antinarcotics operations in countries covered by FBIS London Bureau from 5 to 10 January. Sources are given at the end of each report.

Iran
Antinarcotics agents of the Qazvin Security Force arrested members of a narcotics gang engaged in the production and distribution of narcotics. The agents impounded 49 kg of opium. (Tehran IRIB Television First Program Network in Persian 1230 GMT 6 Jan 94)
Italy
At the end of investigations which began in March, the carabinieri of the Frascati group have broken up a gang of drug traffickers, arresting 11 people and seizing 15 kg of pure cocaine and several thousand ecstasy pills. The organization operated between South America, Spain, and Holland, with Rome as its terminal. (Rome RAI Televideo Teletext in Italian 0652 GMT 7 Jan 94)
Ukraine
According to the Ukrainian border guard's press service, during 1993 border guard units confiscated 7,209 kg of drugs. Over 48 billion karbovanets in of smuggled merchandise was confiscated, plus $29,000 and 52 million Russian rubles in cash. (Kiev Radio Ukraine World Service in English 0100 GMT 5 Jan 94)
