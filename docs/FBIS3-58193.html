Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Finland is reportedly prepared to give the western defense alliance, NATO, a positive response to its Partnership for Peace initiative. It is expected that on Monday [10 January] a NATO summit in Brussels will present a proposal on the issue to all the countries involved in the CSCE process, including Finland. According to a review prepared at the Foreign Ministry in Helsinki, the NATO initiative is in harmony with Finland's own foreign policy and security policy aims. The ministry is stressing, however, that Finland is not seeking new defense or security arrangements. [passage omitted]
