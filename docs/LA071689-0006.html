July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: WEIRD AL'S OSCAR

Is parodist Weird Al Yankovic in hot water with the Academy of Motion Picture Arts & Sciences, which routinely takes action to prevent unauthorized use of Oscar's image?

In his offbeat comedy, "UHF," due out Friday from Orion Pictures, Weird Al does an Indiana Jones "Raiders" send-up in one sequence -- scheming to steal a prop Oscar.

Academy exec administrator Bruce Davis said the organization would wait until the film's release before considering legal action.

Orion declined to comment, as did reps for Weird Al. David Pecchia
