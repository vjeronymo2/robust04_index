October 8, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

COVINA FIRM'S BID WINS STREET CONTRACT

The Fullerton City Council has hired a Covina-based construction company to finish the Euclid Street improvement project.

The council accepted the bid of $1.7 million by Aman Brothers Inc., which was 16% higher than the city originally had estimated, but the lowest bid received.

The $2-million project, between Malvern Avenue and Bastanchury Road, will add additional sidewalks and install left-turn lanes. The project is expected to be completed by summer of next year.
