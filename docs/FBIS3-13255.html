Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, Israel and France signed an agreement on cooperation in research and development of military technologies. The agreement was signed by Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and French Defense Minister Francois Leotard. The defense establishment received Mr. Leotard with a full-fledged military ceremony. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the prime minister reviewed the difficulties in the political negotiations in the wake of the massacre in Hebron. The visiting minister is due to confer with President 'Ezer Weizman, tour industrial plants, and meet with a group of Palestinians headed by Faysal al-Husayni.
