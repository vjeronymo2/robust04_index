Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Yesterday provincial party Secretary Li Changchun, Governor Ma Zhongchen, provincial party Deputy Secretary Song Zhaosu, and Zhang Wenbin, chief of the Propaganda Department of the provincial party committee, together with central press units stationed in Henan and provincial press units, gathered together to usher in the Spring Festival and talk about seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, promote development, maintaining stability, and invigorating Henan. Li Changchun, Song Zhaosu, and Zhang Wenbin made speeches. Li Changchun first expressed his Lunar New Year greetings to central press units stationed in Henan and provincial press units and thanked them for their hard work over the past year. In his speech, Li Changchun said: In the last few years, the provincial party committee and government have paid attention to emancipating the mind and changing concepts; they have pointed out the need to break away from both leftist and rightist restrictions; and they have paid attention to changing cadre work styles. He hoped that journalists would use various methods to provide more publicity to Henan's longstanding history and culture and Henan people's devotion to reform. [passage omitted] In their speeches, Song Zhaosu and Zhang Wenbin pointed out: This year is an important and crucial year for reform and development. Journalists and propagandists undertake glorious and arduous tasks. Press and propaganda work must cater to and serve the overall situation of seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, widening the scope of opening up, promoting development, and maintaining stability.
