BFN [Russian Federation President Directive no. 306-rp] [Text] With a view to implementing the Russian Federation and Republic of Tatarstan treaty "on the delimitation of areas of authority and the reciprocal delegation of powers between the Russian Federation organs of state power and the Republic of Tatarstan organs of state power" and the agreements between the Russian Federation Government and the Republic of Tatarstan Government and with view to monitoring their observance: 1. A commission consisting of representatives of federal organs of executive power and Republic of Tatarstan organs of executive power shall be created on an equitable basis. 2. O.N. Soskovets, first deputy chairman of the Russian Federation Government, shall be appointed leader of the commission. The commission leader in coordination with the Republic of Tatarstan organs of state power shall approve the commission's composition. [signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin, 18 June 1994.
