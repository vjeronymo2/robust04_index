941216 FT 16 DEC 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Video connects Internet novices Cyberia, the London cafe where customers get hitched up to the Internet, has produced a video to smooth the learning curve for novices of the net. Internet: The Cyberian Connection, which was produced with Purple Training, is a step-by-step guide to Internet services and how to get connected.
