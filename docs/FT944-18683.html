941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / IMF/World Bank in Madrid: Loan planned for Algeria - Madrid Conferences Digest The World Bank is preparing a loan of between Dollars 100m and Dollars 200m for Algeria to help it with an ambitious structural reform programme. Mr Caio Koch-Weser, World Bank president for the Middle East and North Africa, said he hoped to put the proposed 'emergency rehabilitation loan' before the Bank board before the end of this year. In addition, the Bank was providing an emergency Dollars 50m loan in the wake of an earthquake disaster.
