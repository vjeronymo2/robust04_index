BFN [Text] Warsaw, June 23 -- Romanian Minister of Foreign Affairs Teodor Melescanu arrived for an official visit to Poland on Thursday. Melescanu started his visit with talks with his Polish counterpart Andrzej Olechowski. Following the talks, the two are expected to sign an agreement on avoiding double taxation and mutual support and protection of investments. Melescanu is to be received by President Lech Walesa and by Prime Minister Pawlak later in the day.
