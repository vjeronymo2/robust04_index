Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The elections to the Supreme Soviet of the Crimea are valid. Taking part in them were 60.24 percent of the electors. Elections to the Crimean Supreme Soviet were held according to party lists. According to figures from the Central Electoral Committee, quoted by news agencies, the Rossiya bloc received an overwhelming majority of votes compared to other parties. Meanwhile, the president of the Crimea, commenting in an interview with INTERFAX on the results of the 27 March poll of inhabitants of the Crimea, said they were confirmation of the correctness of the course chosen by the leadership of the Crimea. According to INTERFAX figures, more than 70 percent of Crimeans answered positively to all three questions on the poll sheet: That relations between the Crimea and the Ukraine should be built on a contractual basis, on dual citizenship, and on granting the force of law to the decrees of the Crimean president on questions of temporarily unregulated Crimean legislation.
