BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 7 TASS -- Finnish representatives have held an inspection in the Leningrad military District in accordance with the 1992 Vienna document of negotiations on confidence-building measures and security. The Finnish Government requested the Russian Government on May 2 to hold the inspection in the area of Alakurtti, Polyarnye Zori, Primorskiy and Selkyalya in the Leningrad Military District the Russian Defence Ministry's Information and Press Department reported today that the Finnish request has been satisfied. The four-member Finnish inspection group arrived in the aforesaid area on May 4 and held a 48-hour inspection.
