Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Manama, 8 Jan (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain; His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, prime minister; and His Highness Shaykh Hamad Bin-'Isa Al Khalifah, heir apparent and commander in chief of the Bahrain Defense Force, received Malcolm Rifkind, British secretary of state for defense, and the delegation accompanying him, in Manama today. During the meeting, the amir of the State of Bahrain welcomed the British guest and his delegation, noting the close relations of friendship and cooperation between the two friendly countries and people. His highness voiced the State of Bahrain's eagerness to consolidate the cooperation existing between the two countries in all fields of service of the two people's interests. [passage omitted]
