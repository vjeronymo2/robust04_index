BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers of Belarus has approved and submitted for confirmation to the Supreme Soviet a report on fulfillment of the republican budget for 1993. According to the report, the revenue was 2,106,904,000,000 rubles and the expenditure was 2,500,282,000,000 roubles. Therefore the expenditure exceeded revenue last year by 393,377,000,000 rubles.
