920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Forminster declines to Pounds 2.11m Forminster, the clothing manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 2.24m to Pounds 2.11m in the year to April 30. Turnover was Pounds 22m, against Pounds 22.2m. The company said that sales had been satisfactory in view of the second half having started poorly. Earnings per share were 46.7p (48.16p). A proposed final dividend of 8.75p makes a raised total of 12.53p (11.75p).
