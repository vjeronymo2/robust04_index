BFN [Text] Baghdad, 8 May (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has received a thank-you cable from his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in reply to a cable his excellency had sent to his majesty on the anniversary of his assumption of his Constitutional authorities. The cable says: We received with appreciation your brotherly congratulations on the anniversary of our assumption of Constitutional authorities. As we send your excellency our sincere thanks, we beseech almighty God to preserve you and grant you success in serving your fraternal country. Please accept our heartfelt wishes of good health, happiness, and success.
