930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Greenwich Comms cuts loss to Pounds 23,000 Greenwich Communications, the USM-quoted supplier of satellite receiving equipment, cut its pre-tax loss from Pounds 92,000 to Pounds 23,000 in the six months to February 28. Turnover was Pounds 162,000 (Pounds 171,000). Losses per share came out at 0.31p against 1.33p.
