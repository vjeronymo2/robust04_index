941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Ivory & Sime rises Ivory & Sime, the Edinburgh-based fund management group, yesterday announced pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 2.77m to Pounds 3.1m for the six months to October 31, reflecting success in reducing the company's cost base. The improvement was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 8.07m to Pounds 8.23m and despite a Pounds 374,000 reduction in interest and investment income to Pounds 135,000. Earnings emerged at 6.52p (5.91p) per share and the interim dividend is unchanged at 2.25p. Funds under management were static at Pounds 3.7bn.
