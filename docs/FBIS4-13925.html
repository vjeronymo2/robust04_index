BFN [Text] A large roadside charge exploded near an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] force moving in the eastern sector of the security zone at midday. There were no casualties and no damage was caused. Our correspondent Nati Zilberman reports that the IDF and SLA [South Lebanese Army] have been sporadically exchanging fire with Hizballah in recent hours. This morning, terrorists fired several mortar shells at SLA positions and at an IDF patrol near al-Rayhan. Our correspondent notes that today's exchanges of fire occurred after two days of quiet in the security zone.
