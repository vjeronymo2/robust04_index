BFN [Text] President Husni Mubarak, Mrs. Mubarak, and the delegation that accompanied the president during his visit to Tunis to attend the OAU summit and to Geneva to receive the UN population award for 1994 returned safely to the homeland this afternoon. President Mubarak has decided to donate the money award he received from the UN to the Ministry of Local Government. The money will be used to set up a number of family planning centers in the Upper Egyptian villages.
