BFN (Text] Sofia, April 5 (A.A) -- Parliament speaker Husamettin Cindoruk who is currently in Bulgaria held official contacts with senior Bulgarian officials on Tuesday. Bulgaria called for cooperation on struggling with international drug traffic and on terrorism. Bulgarian acting prime minister and labor and social minister Evgeni Matinchev said Turkish-Bulgarian political relations are improving day by day. Cindoruk said Turkey and Bulgaria should improve relations and cooperation in every field being a good neighbor adding that Turkey could help Bulgaria on the construction of highways in Bulgaria. Matinchev called on Turkish initiators to invest in Bulgaria and wanted them to purchase touristic facilities in Bulgaria. Cindoruk during the meeting conveyed President Suleyman Demirel's invitation for Bulgarian President Zhelyu Zhelev to come to Turkey. Cindoruk and a delegation accompanying him will return to Turkey on Wednesday.
