940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Record awards at industrial tribunal A Birmingham industrial tribunal yesterday awarded what are believed to be the highest compensation awards in Britain to workers sacked because of their age. Mr Ray Walker, Mr Larry Nolan, and Mr Peter Kiddy have been awarded, respectively, Pounds 12,348, Pounds 16,047 and Pounds 12,767, following their dismissal by Carbodies in Coventry, makers of the black cab. The three workers were dismissed in September 1992 when the company decreed that all workers over the age of 60 had to go.
