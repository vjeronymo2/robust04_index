920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Opren claimants lose court battle MOST OF the remaining alleged victims of the now banned arthritis drug Opren who are claiming compensation had their actions dismissed in the Court of Appeal yesterday. In a test-case ruling, three judges decided that 13 of 16 representative claimants were barred from seeking damages against Eli Lilly, the pharmaceuticals company, because their actions started too late.
