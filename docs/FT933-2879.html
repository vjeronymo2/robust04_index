930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Allied abandons Aitken proposals Allied Group, which holds about 30 per cent of Aitken Hume International, the banking group, has decided not to pursue its proposals for Aitken outlined in July at the time of its preliminary results. The proposals - among other possibilities - involved investment by the group in the Asia Pacific region. The decision followed discussions between the Aitken board and Allied. The Aitken board said yesterday that it continued to explore means by which shareholders could realise the underlying value of their investment.
