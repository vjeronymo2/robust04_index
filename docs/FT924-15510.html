921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Bank breached guidelines Bank of America has admitted it contravened the Indian central bank's guidelines and diverted large sums of the deposits of public undertakings to securities transactions, writes Shiraz Sidhva in New Delhi. The American bank also admitted to the joint parliamentary committee investigating the Rs35bn (Pounds 717m) Bombay stock market scandal that it had offered Mr Amitabh Ghosh, a former deputy governor of the Reserve Bank of India, the chairmanship of the bank's advisory board.
