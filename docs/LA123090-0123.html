December 30, 1990, Sunday, Southland Edition

CHAS'WHIM'S VICTORY AT LAUREL IS OUT OF SIGHT TO HIS BACKERS

In fog so thick racegoers couldn't see the horses they had bet on, Chas'Whim captured the $100,000 Congressional Handicap at the Laurel Race Course on Saturday.

Chas'Whim, who carried top weight of 119 pounds in the field of seven, completed 1 1/4 miles in 2:01 4-5 over a muddy track.

Jet Stream finished a 1 3/4 lengths back in second place and Reputed Testamony was another three-quarters of a length behind in third.
