920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / International Company News: Alcan halves its dividend ALCAN Aluminium is cutting its quarterly dividend to 7.5 cents US per share from 15 cents US with the September 21 payment to conserve cash in the face of 'difficult business conditions and an uncertain outlook'. This is the second reduction during the present industry down cycle. Alcan had maintained 15 cents US for the past four quarters. On July 13 Alcan reported a loss of USDollars 40m or 22 cents a share on revenues of USDollars 3.83bn, against a loss equal to 7 cents a share in the 1991 period.
