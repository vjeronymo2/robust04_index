Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 8 Mar (NA) -- Personnel of the Argentine Federal Police Interpol Department today arrested a notorious international drug trafficker who was on the Spanish drug enforcement authorities' most wanted list. The criminal is 28-year-old Tomas Moran who, after escaping from Spain, clandestinely entered Argentine territory in December 1993 to seek sanctuary in our country. After a thorough investigation, Interpol Buenos Aires located Moran at a rehabilitation center for drug addicts in Buenos Aires Province. Moran carried out his unlawful activities in the fashionable resorts of Ibiza and Formentera on the Spanish Mediterranean coast. Moran is being held in custody at Interpol quarters awaiting a decision by the Argentine courts about his extradition to Spain.
