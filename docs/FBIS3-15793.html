Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] A Reform Bloc for the People [Blok Reform Dlya Narodu] has been set up in Ukraine. Leading specialists from various sectors of the economy and specialist practitioners from 12 of the main regions of our country are in the bloc. The purpose of the newly created bloc is to stabilize the economy by conducting real market reforms, to increase the income of the populace by reforming the financial and currency system, and also by reducing nonproductive expenditure in the budget, to campaign against crime and corruption, and to protect the rights, property, and dignity of each individual by conducting constitutional, judicial, and law enforcement reforms. Volodymyr Lanovyy, a well-known economist in Ukraine and president of the center for market reforms, heads the bloc.
