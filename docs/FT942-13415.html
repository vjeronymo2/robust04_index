940425 FT 25 APR 94 / International Company News: HK utility surges China Light and Power, the monopoly supplier of electricity to Kowloon and the New Territories of Hong Kong, announced interim profits of HKDollars 2.08bn (USDollars 270m) for the six months to March 31, up 20 per cent from HKDollars 1.7bn last year, writes Lousie Lucas in Hong Kong. Earnings per share climbed to HKDollars 1.04.
