CSO [Unattributed article: "Unemployment in March at February Level"] [Text] According to figures from the Government Office of Statistics, the number of unemployed in March remained at the level for February, but rose by more than 60,000 compared with March last year. The figure for seasonably adjusted unemployment also stabilized at the February level. The jobless rate rose from March of last year to March of this year by 2.5 percent, or to 19.8 percent. The rate for males was a full 22 percent, while that for females was around 17 percent. There were around 100,000 unemployed youths. The rate for youth unemployment was 41 percent. In the public sector and private service sector there were 96,000 individuals without jobs, in industry 85,000, and in construction 74,000. The highest rate of unemployment was found in home construction, with nearly half of this sector's labor force not working.
