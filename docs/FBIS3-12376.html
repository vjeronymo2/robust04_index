Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Reports by 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Haydari and Faysal Karam in Sanaa and Iqbal 'Ali 'Abdallah in Aden] [Excerpts] The situation remained tense yesterday in Yemen's Abyan Governorate as the General People's Congress [GPC] and the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] exchanged accusations about provocative military movements. [passage omitted] Military sources in Sanaa have revealed that a number of soldiers from the northern al-'Amaliqah Brigade were ambushed yesterday morning in the town of Zinjibar, capital of the Abyan Governorate, by soldiers from the al-Wahdah Brigade and some YSP militia. They said that "a limited number of al-'Amaliqah Brigade soldiers were on their way to al-Razi Hospital in the town of Ja'ar to collect the body of a colleague of theirs treacherously killed by soldiers from the Madram Brigade camp, when they were intercepted and fired on by an armed group in front of the hospital. Brigade member Hazzam 'Ali Musayli was wounded." According to other reports, tanks and howitzer artillery units from the YSP's al-'Anad camp have moved in the direction of the areas of al-Milah, al-Hubaylayn, and al-Dali' in the province of Lahij "to occupy the old partition positions and prepare to besiege the forces of the (northern) 2nd armored brigade stationed in the area of al-Milah in Lahij." "Informed sources" in Aden have been quoted as saying that at dawn yesterday 12 tanks tightened their siege of the central security battalion in the Radfan camp in al-Sawlaban (Aden). They added that "the supply of water and electricity to the battlion were still cut off on the direct orders of Aden Governor Brigadier General Salih Munsir al-Suyayli who ordered the expulsion of that battalion from Aden to the remote area of Dar Sa'd." [passage omitted] According to reports received in Aden from the Abyan Governorate, forces of the southern al-Wahdah Brigade "continue to hunt down members of the Islamic Jihad Organization concentrated in the al-Maraqishah mountains in the directorate of Mudiyah." The same reports revealed "the arrest of 85 Islamic Jihad members of various Arab nationalities who participated in the armed clashes in the directorate" since last Monday between forces of the northern al-'Amaliqah Brigade and forces of the southern al-Wahdah Brigade which came from Hadramaut (some 920 km east of Aden). [passage omitted] AL-HAYAH learned from YSP sources yesterday that the YSP's Political Bureau has been in "continuous session" since 'Ali Salim al-Bid's return to Aden the day before yesterday after brief visits to Saudi Arabia, the Sultanate of Oman, and Eritrea after signing the Pledge and Accord Document in the Jordanian capital last Sunday. The sources said that "al-Bid returned secretly to Aden after the discovery that there was going to be yet another attempt by the air defenses of the al-'Amaliqah Brigade to target his plane as it crossed the Abyan air space." They also said that shots were fired by al-'Amaliqah forces on an Ethiopian Airlines plane over Abyan as it was on a regular weekly flight to Aden International Airport on the morning of the day before yesterday but it was not hit.
