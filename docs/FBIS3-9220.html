Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 19 March (MTI) -- The National Board of the opposition Federation of Young Democrats [Fidesz] adopted its national list for the May parliamentary elections with an overwhelming majority, Board Chairman Jozsef Szajer told the press after the meeting in Sopron, western Hungary, on Saturday [19 March]. The list of 86 is led by Party Chairman Viktor Orban, with Szajer in second place, followed by Parliamentary faction leader Laszlo Kover, acting Deputy Chairman Janos Ader, and Tamas Deutsch, deputy chairman in charge of information. Szajer announced that Orban is the Fidesz's prime minister candidate for the event the party is elected to a government position. The board authorized the party chairman to make minor changes in the list if necessary.
