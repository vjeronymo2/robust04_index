921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Scapa in German textiles purchase A subsidiary of Scapa Group, the Blackburn-based company which supplies technical products and services to the paper industry, has agreed to buy part of the technical textile business of Technische Textilien L'rrach, a German company. The Scapa subsidiary, P&S Textiles of Bury, Lancashire, will pay a maximum Pounds 1m. As part of the deal, the German company will become an agent for P&S, representing it exclusively in Germany, Austria and Switzerland, and non-exclusively in France. Last week the UK group said it was in talks with the receivers of a German company, F Oberdorfer, which might lead to Scapa acquiring the business and assets of its German, Austrian and US operations.
