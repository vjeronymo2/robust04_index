Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] I have just received an INTERFAX report in the studio. It says that Boris Yeltsin has accepted the resignation of Procurator General Aleksey Kazannikov. This is followed by a report from the presidential press service which says that the edict also decrees that Aleksey Nikolayevich Ilyushenko be presented to the Federation Council as a candidate for nomination to the post of procurator general of the Russian Federation. Until the procurator general is appointed, Ilyushenko is entrusted with carrying out his duties. Under another edict, Ilyushenko is released from the post of head of the control directorate of the president's administration.
