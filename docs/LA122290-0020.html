December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DODGERS HAVE ALREADY TAKEN STEPS TO KEEP TEAM FREE OF INFECTION

So Hubie Brooks is upset by the trade sending him from 1991's top contending team back to the New York Mess. His warning to the Dodgers that they have 1% that could ruin a very good team comes from a player unable to remember how many outs there were, allowing Brett Butler to score the winning run in a game at Candlestick Park last season crucial to the Dodgers' comeback hopes. Come on, Hubie, leave with the dignity everyone expects from such a nice guy.

ALAN AMITIN

Montrose
