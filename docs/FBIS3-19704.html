Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] France believes that the UN resolution condemning the Hebron massacre "should now be translated into effective measures." This declaration came in a statement made on Saturday [19 March] by French Foreign Ministry spokesman Richard Duque. In the statement, France "welcomes" the adoption of the resolution "to which it actively contributed," and "gives its fullest backing" to a resumption of dialogue between Israelis and Palestinians.
