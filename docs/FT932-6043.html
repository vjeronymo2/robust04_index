930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Nadir backs Mates as row grows Mr Asil Nadir, the fugitive businessman, yesterday fuelled the political row over his connections with Mr Michael Mates, the Northern Ireland minister. In a statement from northern Cyprus, he defended the minister's conduct and said his help had been much appreciated. Earlier, Mr Mates insisted he had done nothing wrong in taking up the case of the former chairman of Polly Peck. Report, Page 8
