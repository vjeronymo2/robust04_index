940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Company News This Week: London Electricity - Other Companies The utility is expected on Wednesday to disclose pre-tax profits of about Pounds 185m for 1993-4. The previous year's total was Pounds 145.5m. A rise of around 14 per cent from last year's 19.5p is on the cards for the dividend. The following day South Wales Electricity is expected to announce Pounds 100m pre-tax, against Pounds 87m. The City expects a dividend of about 25.3p, against 22.3p.
