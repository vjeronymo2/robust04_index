Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Leaders of the Arab public in Israel were invited for an urgent meeting with the prime minister. Our correspondent Yo'el Dar reports that the meeting was initiated by the prime minister and is slated to take place in Tel Aviv at the end of the Sabbath [26 February]. Arab Knesset members, as well as the members of the Supreme Arab Follow-Up Committee, including committee head, Ibrahim Nimr Husayn, are among those who were invited to the meeting. A strike is slated to take place in the Arab sector in Israel tomorrow, and no school classes will be held. [passage omitted]
