930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / The Independent to pay libel damages THE Independent newspaper is to pay 'substantial' libel damages to Mr Conrad Black, chairman of The Telegraph, and its solicitor and secretary Mr Anthony Rentoul, it was announced in the High Court yesterday. The undisclosed settlement follows the newspaper's story about the approval by Telegraph shareholders of the company's purchase of a Pounds 70m stake in the Canadian publisher Southam Inc. The Independent also made a public High Court apology and apologised to the two executives. Telegraph results, Page 10
