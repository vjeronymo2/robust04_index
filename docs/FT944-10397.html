941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: FA launches probe into football bribery allegations The Football Association began a 'thorough and urgent investigation' into allegations of bribery and match fixing centred on Southampton and former Liverpool goalkeeper Bruce Grobbelaar, who has denied the claims. The FA said The Sun newspaper, which made the allegations, had agreed to hand over information, including video tapes, on which its claims are based.
