BFN [Text] The foreign ministers of Pakistan and Germany held detailed discussion in Bonn today on a number of issues of mutual concern and of regional and international importance. Later, Pakistan Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali told Radio Pakistan's special representative that he had informed Mr. Kinkel of Pakistan's sincere desire to amicably resolve the Kashmir problem and normalize its relations with India. But, he said, India has not responded positively to various proposals presented by Pakistan to realize this objective. Mr. Kinkel said Germany was fully aware of the situation in Indian-held Kashmir and the importance of the Kashmir dispute in Pakistan-India relations. He assured that Germany would make efforts to persuade India to find a peaceful solution of the Kashmir problem. Mr. Kinkel was also briefed on Pakistan's efforts to bring about peace in Afghanistan, Tajikistan, and Somalia.
