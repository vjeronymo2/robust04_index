BFN [Excerpt] The provincial meeting on the work of opening to the outside world was held in Jinan on 25 April. Under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the guidelines of the 14th party congress and of the third plenary session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, participants in this meeting conscientiously summarized the province's opening up work since the commencement of reform and opening up since 1992. In particular, they learned from and used for reference the successful experiences of fraternal provinces and municipalities, researched and drew up the province's general pattern of opening up in the foreseeable future, and defined the province's overall goal and policy on developing the economy oriented to opening up in the next step. Attending the meeting were provincial leading comrades Jiang Chunyun, Zhao Zhihao, Li Chunting, Li Wenquan, Li Zhen, and Lu Maozeng. Li Chunting presided over the meeting. He said: This meeting, held by the provincial party committee and the provincial government, is an important one related to the province's overall economic situation and is a major move adopted by the province to accelerate the development of the economy oriented to opening up since the issuance of Deng Xiaoping's speeches made during his south China trip in spring of 1992 and since the convocation of the provincial meeting on opening up. The purpose of this meeting is to again mobilize the whole province to deepen reform and open even wider to the outside world and to further emancipate the mind and seek unity in understanding and action in order to push the province's opening up towards the orientation of high level, high sphere, and in-depth development.
