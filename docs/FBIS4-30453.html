BFN [Text] Visiting Malaysian Primary Industries Minister Datuk Sri Dr. Lim Keng Yaik and delegation called on Lieutenant General Kyaw Min, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] and minister of mines, at the latter's office at 1400 this afternoon. At the meeting, the visiting Malaysian minister inquired about bilateral cooperation matters in the mining sector and explanations were given by the Burmese mines minister. The visiting Malaysian delegation accompanied by U Hlaing Win, deputy minister of mines, visited the Myanmar [Burma] Gems Emporium Hall at 66 Kabaaye Pagoda Road.
