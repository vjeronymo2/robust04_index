May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

CBS to Lose Faith Daniels?: NBC News is negotiating with Faith Daniels to be the news reader on "Today," according to sources at the network, and is expected to sign her today. Daniels has been co-anchor on CBS News' "Morning News." NBC denied rumors last week that "Today" co-anchor Deborah Norville, who previously had been the show's news reader, was in danger of being replaced due to sagging ratings. GREG BRAXTON
