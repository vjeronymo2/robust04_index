940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / Markets: FT Quarterly Review - At a glance To most people, the benefits of investing offshore are far from clear. But Kenneth Clarke's Budget did make one tax change that could give offshore funds an advantage. The winter issue of the FT Quarterly Review of Personal Finance, to be published with next Friday's paper and repeated with Saturday's, considers the pros and cons of five types of offshore investment. The survey section of the review focuses on domestic finance matters, including school fees, the cost of going to university and paying for a wedding.
