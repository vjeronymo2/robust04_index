930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: Stratagem raises Harrison holding Stratagem, the investment group, has acquired a further 1.19m shares in Harrison Industries, the doors and castings supplier, at 4.1p per share from Mr Ken Harrison, former chairman, and his family. Stratagem has also been granted an option to acquire a further 28.81m shares from the family at the same price, exercisable on April 6. If this option is exercised, the total consideration payable to the family would amount to Pounds 1.23m. Stratagem already owns 1.18m Harrison shares and following completion of the latest transactions would own an aggregate 31.18m shares, representing some 25.1 per cent of the issued capital.
