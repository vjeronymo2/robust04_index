January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BEST-AND-WORST LIST GETS A FEW ADDITIONS

An addendum to Larry Stewart's best-and-worst list for 1989.

Best sports interviewer: Roy Firestone.

Best sports reporter/radio: Lee Hamilton.

Finally, with regard to KABC's radio "personality," the worst crime is that station management apparently underestimates the intelligence of its audience. I'm amazed that KABC continues to air (Ed Bieler's) mediocrity.

SHEL KAPLAN, Rolling Hills Estates
