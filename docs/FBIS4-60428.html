BFN [Text of cable from Saudi King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the two holy mosques, to Presidential Council Chairman 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih -- date not given] [Text] His Excellency brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, Sanaa: I received your congratulations and those of your brothers in the Presidential Council of the fraternal Yemeni people on the occasion of the blessed 'Id al-Adha. I congratulate you on this blessed occasion and ask God Almighty that we all celebrate this occasion next year while enjoying continued happiness and the fraternal Yemeni people and the Islamic nation prosperity and success. Many happy returns. [Signed] Your brother, Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques and king of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
