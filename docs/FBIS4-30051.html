BFN [Text] Seoul, June 23 (YONHAP) -- Thai Prime Minister Chuan Likphai arrived in Seoul Thursday afternoon aboard a special Thai Air Force plane for a four-day official visit at the invitation of President Kim Yong-sam. The Thai premier was greeted by Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu on arrival at Seoul airport. On Friday morning, Prime Minister Chuan and President Kim will have a summit meeting at Chongwadae [presidential offices] to exchange opinions on East Asia situations centered on the nuclear question. Later in the day, Chuan will meet with Prime Minister Yi Yong-tok and National Assembly Speaker Yi Man-sop. In the evening, he will attend a dinner to be hosted by President and Mrs. Kim. Chuan is the first Thai prime minister to visit Seoul since 1981.
