920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: New Zealand Trust suffers downturn Net asset value per share of the New Zealand Investment Trust stood at 89.3p at January 31. That compared with 68.5p a year earlier and with 95.8p at the October 31 year-end. Net revenue for the three months to January 31 amounted to Pounds 62,141 (Pounds 93,766), for earnings of 0.62p (0.94p) per share. The first interim dividend is reduced from 0.75p to 0.5p.
