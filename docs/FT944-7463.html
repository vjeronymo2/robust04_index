941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / UK Company News: F&C Income Foreign & Colonial Income Growth Investment Trust reported net revenue of Pounds 795,000 for the period from incorporation on February 3 to September 30. Earnings per share came to 1.85p and there is a maiden interim dividend of 1.4p. Net asset value per share at the end of September was 92.6p against 96p six months earlier.
