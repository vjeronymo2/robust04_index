930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / Paraguay to resume sell-offs Paraguay's new government plans a rapid resumption of its privatisation programme, officials said yesterday, George Graham writes from Washington. Mr Enzo Debernardi, principal counsellor to president-elect Juan Carlos Wasmosy, said in Washington the new government aimed to sell Paraguay's state-owned airline, railways, merchant river fleet, rum distillery, cement factories and steel mill. It may also privatise the telephone company.
