920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / Two jailed for Pounds 30m conspiracy TWO businessmen were yesterday jailed for attempting a Pounds 30m fraud involving forged bank drafts. Mr Gurvinder Chopra, a Birmingham property dealer, was sentenced to two years after admitting conspiring to use a false instrument, forgery and deception. Mr Jabinda Singh Badesha, who was convicted of the conspiracy charge, was given a four-year sentence. Southwark Crown Court in London was told that Mr Chopra erased details on two genuine Pounds 10 and Pounds 15 bank drafts, inflated their respective values to Pounds 15m and inserted new payees.
