BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 4 May -- The Sakartvelo group of Russian border troops has started recruiting citizens of the Republic of Georgia. Colonel Vladimir Berezin, commander of the Sakartvelo group, said that 350 Georgian citizens are to be recruited during the first stage, which will make up 10 percent of the group's entire personnel. In the future, a Georgian national unit will guard the Georgian-Turkish border.
