940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / Gunmen attack bus in S Egypt The Moslem group el-Gama'a el-Islamiya claimed responsibility for a gun attack on a bus carrying Romanian engineers in Assiut, southern Egypt, yesterday, Reuter reports from Cairo. 'The attack resulted in the smashing of the bus and the injury of several,' the group said. But security officials said no one had been hurt. The attack, by two gunmen, was part of Gama'a's warning to investors and tourists to leave Egypt, the group's statement added.
