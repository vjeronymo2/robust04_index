931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / People: Nomura International - Finance moves David Porter has been appointed deputy managing director of NOMURA International, in charge of UK, Scandinavian and Dutch investment banking. The position was created as part of a reorganisation of the investment banking operation. He joins Nomura from the Municipal Insurance Group where he was group treasury director. Michael Williams, formerly md Europe for Barclays global services, has been appointed general manager in charge of the banking division at Nomura Bank International.
