Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 17 March, Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation, received a delegation from the Organization for Consolidating International Ethics led by Mrs. (Verney Pan), chairman of the Organization for Consolidating International Ethics in Cambodia. On this occasion, the prince kromkhun welcomed the delegation and talked about the elections in Cambodia two years ago, which were successful because of the correct implementation of the Paris agreement, particularly under the leadership of His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk. It was also due to the participation of the Cambodian people nationwide. Answering his guests' questions on the Khmer Rouge and investment in Cambodia, the prince kromkhun said that the Khmer Rouge issue is of no importance to the Royal Government as long as this group refuses to abide by the national reconciliation principles. On the investment issue, the United States will soon grant Cambodia favorable trading status. Cambodia will always welcome foreign investors who want to do business in the Kingdom of Cambodia.
