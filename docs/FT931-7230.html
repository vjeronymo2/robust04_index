930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Jos Holdings show improvement Jos Holdings, which last year became a split capital investment trust after a reorganisation, raised net profits from Pounds 186,000 to Pounds 287,000 in the six months to January 31 1993. Earnings per income (ordinary) share were 4.43p (2.01p). A second quarterly dividend of 2.875p makes 5.75p to date - a total of 11.5p has been forecast for the current year. Total assets rose by 19.4 per cent, from Pounds 15.1m to Pounds 18.1m. Net asset values were 196.2p per capital share, 82.3p per zero dividend preference share, and 1p per income share. At July 31 1992, net assets per ordinary share stood at 163.6p.
