June 8, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

REPAVING TO CLOSE SIMI FREEWAY LANES

Two lanes of the westbound Simi Valley Freeway between Topanga Canyon Boulevard and Rocky Peak Road will be closed tonight and Saturday for paving, the California Department of Transportation reported.

The lanes are scheduled to close at 8 p.m. tonight and reopen about 6 p.m. Saturday. The Topanga Canyon Boulevard on-ramp to the highway will be closed most of that time, Caltrans officials said.

Caltrans advised motorists to use the De Soto Avenue on-ramp instead.
