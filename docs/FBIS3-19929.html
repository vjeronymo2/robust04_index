Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos has described European Union [EU] Commission President Jacques Delors letter to Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou as positive. In his letter, Delors asks the Greek Presidency to raise the issue of the Greek measures against The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] at the informal EU Council of Foreign Ministers meeting to be held in Ioannina this weekend. The spokesman said that in his letter, Delors accepts that Greece's decision is based on political principles and asks that the foreign ministers investigate the political reasons for this.
