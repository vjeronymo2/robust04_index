910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / US shuffle for European team THE US will soon reshuffle its senior State Department officials dealing with Europe, writes Peter Riddell Mr Thomas Niles, US ambassador to the European Community, is expected to be nominated shortly as the new Assistant Secretary for European and Canadian affairs, taking over from Mr Raymond Seitz, who has just become US ambassador to Britain. Mr Niles is to be succeeded in Brussels by Mr James Dobbins, now principal deputy assistant secretary in the Bureau of European and Canadian Affairs.
