March 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

CAMBODIA;

VIETNAMESE STILL FIGHTING, TWO SAY

Two Vietnamese soldiers who defected to Cambodian guerrilla forces said at least 300 Vietnamese troops are fighting alongside Cambodian government forces in the northwest of the country. Hanoi, which installed the Cambodian government in 1979, claims it pulled out its last troops last September.
