Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Well-informed Israeli sources attribute great importance to a statement made by a Radio Damascus commentator that in exchange for a withdrawal from all the occupied territories, Israel will reap the fruits of peace on all fronts, without exception. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that according to these sources, this is the first statement of its kind. They believe it was made in anticipation of the Clinton-al-Asad summit.
