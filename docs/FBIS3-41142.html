Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Diego Fajardo from the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] Italian drug trafficker Roberto Panunzzi is currently in a DIJIN [Judicial Police and Investigations Directorate] cell in Santa Fe de Bogota. He was captured in Medellin and will be extradited to his country in a few hours. Panunzzi faces two charges in Colombia: one for drug trafficking and another for forgery, because he was carrying a personal identification card, a driver's license, and a safe-conduct issued in the name of Francisco Oliva Gomez, a native of Florida, Valle Department. [Begin Colonel Teodoro Campos recording] We have not caught everyone, but we have the most important ones in Colombian, English, and Italian jails. [end recording] Roberto Panunzzi is the sixth person captured in Colombia as part of Operation Angelo II. Italy has offered Colombia 18 billion pesos to assist in the war on drugs.
