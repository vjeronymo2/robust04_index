Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The cabinet list formed by the Democratic Party [DP] and the Republican Turkish Party [RTP] was submitted to President Rauf Denktas today. Denktas approved of the new cabinet list which means that the new government has officially started to function. The following names appear on the cabinet list: Hakki Atun, prime minister; RTP leader Ozker Ozgur, state minister and deputy prime minister; Atay Ahmet Rasit, foreign affairs and defense minister; Taner Etkin, interior minister, Onur Borman, economy and finance minister; Mehmet Ali Talat, national education and culture minister; Ferdi Sabit Soyer, agriculture, national resource, and energy minister; Ahmet Kasif, public works and transportation minister; Ergin Abdullah Iltac, health minister; Ozkan Murat, labor and social security minister; Serdar Denktas, youth, sports, and environment minister. The coalition government formed by the DP and the RTP will be submitted to the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus Republican Assembly tomorrow. The government program, in turn, will be submitted to the Assembly within 10 days.
