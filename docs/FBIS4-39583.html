BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow June 9 TASS -- "The negotiating process with Chechnya will have to be conducted," Federation Council Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko told journalists today. Shumeyko expressed regret in connection with the act of terror against Dzhokhar Dudayev and surmised that "forces which do not want stabilisation of relations" are seeking to impede the process. He noted that the attitude to the act of terror has changed in Chechnya, "which is evidenced by the stripping of some individuals of the Chechen citizenship".
