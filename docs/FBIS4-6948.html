BFN [Text] At 0300 on 24 April, routed troops fleeing toward Treng quarreled and fired at one another, killing or wounding hundreds. Those who fled toward Battambang did so chaotically to save their lives. On the battlefield east of Pailin: On 24 April we swept from O Kchheay to Chisang and completely controlled the area. We seized a T-54 tank and a tractor. Special report: Preliminary results say that at 0020 on 23 April, we launched a special attack against the regimental position of the communist Vietnamese puppets and the two-headed government in Sdau on Route 10, killing 19 on the spot, including a major. A number of soldiers were wounded and dragged back by their colleagues. We seized 10 assorted firearms. At 0300, their commanders ordered tanks to retake this position. However, so far no tanks have dared to show up.
