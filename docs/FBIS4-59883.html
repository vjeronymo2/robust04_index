BFN [Text] In their meeting last night in Oslo, Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres and PLO Chairman 'Arafat agreed on principles and an agenda to begin negotiations on widening the authority of Palestinian self-rule to other areas of Judaea and Samaria, aside from Jericho. The negotiations are to take place in Cairo, but no date was set. Peres told reporters the withdrawal from Jericho and the Gaza Strip has been completed properly.
