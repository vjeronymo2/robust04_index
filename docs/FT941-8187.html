940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): American International Group - Other companies The bellwether among US property/casualty insurers, is expected to report further strong premium gains for the last quarter of 1993. With growth in its overseas base and a recovery in some insurance lines in the US, premium growth could top 10 per cent, as in the previous two quarters. The market is looking for earnings per share of around Dollars 1.50 for the period, up from Dollars 1.40 the year before, making Dollars 5.75 for the year as a whole (1992: Dollars 4.90.)
