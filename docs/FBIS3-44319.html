Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hefei, December 30 (XINHUA) -- Program-controlled and mobile telephone networks now cover every city and county in east China's Anhui Province. The province has invested 1.3 billion yuan to build program- controlled telephone switches, optical cables and microwave relay stations this year. It has developed several telecommunications networks, of both cable type and wireless type. Now the province has 660,000 automatic telephone terminals which are connected to international networks, a digital info-transmission network and a 900 kilohertz mobile telephone network which enables people to call from anywhere in the province with a wireless telephone.
