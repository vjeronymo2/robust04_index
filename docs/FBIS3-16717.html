Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva, Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 18 Mar -- After the short period of calm that followed the meeting of the heads of parliament of Azerbaijan and Armenia, the Armenian side has again started military operations. According to the reports from Azerbaijan's Ministry of Defense that have come in today, they resumed on Thursday after the shelling of Azerbaijan Army positions in the direction of Agdam from a "Grad" missile installation, which did not stop for 24 hours. The same source informs of a concentration of forces and equipment by the enemy on other sections of the front.
