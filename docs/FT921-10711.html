920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / Parliament and Politics: News in Brief WALES: Health authorities in Wales will receive a Pounds 1.42bn cash allocation in the coming year, a Pounds 117m increase, Mr David Hunt, Welsh Secretary, announced in a Commons written reply last night. TRAVELLERS: The Caravan Sites (Amendment) Bill, introduced by Sir Michael McNair-Wilson (C Newbury), gained an unopposed formal first reading but stands no chance of becoming law due to a lack of parliamentary time. Sir Michael called for a 'new balance' between the right of local residents and the rights of nomadic travellers.
