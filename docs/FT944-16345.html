941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico The bolsa closed sharply higher on investor optimism about third-quarter results and bottom-fishing for stocks whose prices have fallen in recent weeks. The IPC index moved ahead 75.23, or 2.82 per cent, to 2,740.82 in strong volume of 105.6m shares. Analysts said investors believe Mexican companies will report healthy third-quarter earnings and that the market's decline following the September 28 assassination of a top ruling party official had made a number of stocks very attractive.
