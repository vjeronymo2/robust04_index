940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Enviromed achieves Pounds 126,000 Enviromed, the biotechnology and healthcare group which joined the market last June, achieved a Pounds 126,000 pre-tax profit for the six months to March 31. The result, on sales of Pounds 2.85m, compared with a Pounds 76,000 deficit previously on sales of Pounds 2.11m. The directors declared an interim dividend of 0.2p, payable from earnings per share of 0.87p (1.73p losses).
