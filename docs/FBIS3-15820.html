Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Official Visit"] [Text] Defense Minister Army General Pavel Grachev has arrived in Germany on an official visit. In reply to a question by an ITAR-TASS correspondent on what concrete results he hopes to achieve in the course of his visit to the FRG, the Russian defense minister emphasized at the airport: They will primarily be in questions of expanding bilateral cooperation between our countries within the framework of the agreements signed in April 1993. Particular attention will be paid to the education of our servicemen in the FRG and of German servicemen in Russian higher military educational establishments. A number of questions will be discussed concerning German-Russian military-technical cooperation, since part of the military hardware in the Bundeswehr's arsenal was produced here. There is special interest in questions of its modernization and recycling. Much attention will be paid to the problems of withdrawing the Western Group of Forces from German territory, the Russian military department chief noted in particular.
