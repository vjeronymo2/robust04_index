941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / Ex-accountant of singer faces trial The former accountant of pop star Sting was yesterday committed for trial accused of stealing about Pounds 3m from the artist and one of his overseas companies. Mr Keith John Moore, a chartered accountant of Fulham, west London, is accused of 14 specimen charges relating to dates between August 1988 and July 1992, involving Sting - whose real name is Gordon Matthew Sumner - and Steerpike Overseas. Mr Moore was bailed by Bow Street magistrates to face trial on a date to be fixed at Southwark Crown Court.
