BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Mineyev] [Excerpts] Brussels, 31 May -- [passage omitted] Having arrived in Brussels on Tuesday morning [Kyrgyz President] Akayev visited the European Commission twice, where he had talks with its heads -- Hans van der Broek and Leon Brittan. At the end of these meetings a partnership and cooperation agreement between the European Union [EU] and Kyrgyzstan was initialled. The economic theme was also of paramount importance during bilateral talks held in the Egmont palace where the high guests of the Belgian Government are usually received. A. Akayev had talks with Robert Urbain, minister for foreign trade and European affairs. At the end of this meeting a joint declaration in which the sides stated their decision to intensify cooperation in political and economic spheres was initialled. On Wednesday the Kyrgyz president will have meetings in the NATO headquarters, where he will sign the framework document of the Partnership for Peace program.
