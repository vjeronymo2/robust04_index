September 15, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

WINGING AWAY FOR THANKSGIVING DAY WEEKEND

People who plan to fly away for Thanksgiving Day weekend would do well to shop for airlines and airports. The thrifty traveler can save, depending on the destination, airport and whether the flight has any restrictions.

The following chart illustrates round-trip air fares to Chicago, Honolulu, Miami, Phoenix and San Francisco from Orange County, Los Angeles, Long Beach, San Diego and Ontario airports. The fares are for departure on Wednesday, Nov. 22, and return Sunday, Nov. 26.

Two quotes are listed for each destination. The first quote is unrestricted coach. The second quote is the lowest fare with various restrictions. See notes below. Reservations are booking up very quickly, so call as soon as possible.
