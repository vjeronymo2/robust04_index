BFN [Text] Vice-Premier, Minister for Nationalities and Regional Policy of the Russian Federation Sergey Shakhray expressed the opinion that he will be in charge of national and regional policy in the government, as well as relations with the Federal Assembly and the media. He gave an interview to INTERFAX after the president issued a decree nominating Shakhray the deputy prime minister. Shakhray noted that a final decision on the range of his duties will be taken at a meeting of the government presidium. Shakhray said that after the new appointment he has no intention of losing his deputy mandate. He pointed out that he is not working in the ruling structures in the State Duma. "My job in the lower chamber of the parliament boils down to coordination of activities of the Russian Unity and Accord Party's faction," he noted.
