BFN [Unattributed report under the "Provincial Chronicle" rubric] [Text] Nazran -- The Ingushetia People's Assembly has recalled Speaker Murat Keligov and accepted the resignation of his deputy Azamat Nalgiyev. Unanimously elected two months ago, Murat Keligov has stated: "The question of confidence in myself has been raised by the president, and I believe the will of the president should be fulfilled." The man elected to the position of speaker is Visangirey Gagiyev, former first deputy parliament chairman, chairman of the committee for the affairs of refugees and forced resettlers. The question of deputy chairman has been postponed till the next session.
