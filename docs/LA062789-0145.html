June 27, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

PARILLI NAMED DYNAMITE COACH

Babe Parilli, who played quarterback in the NFL and old AFL and has coached in the pro ranks as well, has been named head coach of the Denver Dynamite of the Arena Football League. Parilli, a resident of Denver since he was the quarterback coach for the Broncos from 1977 to 1979, said Monday he is optimistic about the Dynamite, the 1987 champion and a non-contender last season. Parilli coached the New England entry in the Arena League last season.
