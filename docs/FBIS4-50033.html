BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Five offenders were recently released from education-through-labor institutions before their terms expire, according to a department concerned here. Chen Zhuman, Yan Peizhi, Xu Zhihe, Cui Tai and Zhang Li were ordered to be re-educated through labor respectively between December 1991 and September 1993 because of disturbing social order and endangering public security by making use of religion. Decisions to release them were made by the eduction-through-labor committees of Quanzhou City in Fujian Province, Weifang City in Shandong Province and Zhangjiakou City in Hebei Province. The department concerned said that they were released ahead of time because they had been amenable to discipline and behaved themselves during correctional period.
