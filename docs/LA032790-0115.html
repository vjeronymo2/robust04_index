March 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BOFFO: Disney's new policy barring on-screen advertising isn't a problem at the Newport Beach-based Edwards Theatres, which has 100 screens in 20 Orange County theaters. "I don't like running commercials," said James Edwards, the chain's chairman and founder, adding that he never has run paid commercials. "I hope that I'm in the entertainment business." Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
