Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 Mar (MENA)--Hundreds of university students assembled in al-Tahrir Square this afternoon carrying placards condemning the ugly massacre inside the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron last Friday [25 February]. The students denounced this painful incident and demanded a halt to the peace negotiations with Israel. After one hour of slogan shouting, when the square became overcrowded with other citizens, the police dispersed the demonstrators in order to prevent other elements infiltrating them and to keep traffic moving as it had begun to be disrupted. The police used tear gas and some warning shots to disperse the demonstrators.
