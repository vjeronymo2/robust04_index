BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Kanat Saudabayev received today Boris Krasnikov, the Russian ambassador to Kazakhstan, at his request. Under examination during the meeting were a wide range of topical problems. They discussed issues of diverse Kazakhstan-Russian cooperation. Boris Krasnikov emphasized that the first official visit by President Nazarbayev to the Russian Federation is an important factor contributing to further development of bilateral relations. He highly evaluated the idea on creating the Euro-Asian Union that has been put forward by head of our state. He described it as an interesting and promising proposal. On his behalf, Kanat Saudabayev emphasized that Russia will remain a priority in Kazakhstan's foreign policy. The Foreign Ministries of both countries can play a great role in this.
