940917 FT 17 SEP 94 / Major to address SA assembly The prime minister is to address South Africa's national assembly on his historic visit next week. Mr John Major will make a keynote speech to an informal gathering of both houses of the South African parliament. The session in Cape Town will follow talks with President Nelson Mandela. Mr Major will tell the politicians that Britain is keen to re-establish a close relationship with its former Commonwealth partner. Invitations to speal to the parliament are usually accorded only to visiting heads of state.
