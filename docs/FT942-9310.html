940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / New town offers Pounds 25m assets sale The Irvine Development Corporation yesterday announced that it was putting its industrial assets up for sale in September at a price of Pounds 20m-Pounds 25m. The sale of the 1m sq ft of industrial buildings and development land will be a step towards the winding-up of the Ayrshire new town, which is planned for the end of 1996. The corporation's shops and office property portfolio will be put on the market in the autumn of 1995.
