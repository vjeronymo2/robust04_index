BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Taganrog Metallurgical Plant Halted"] [Text] The Taganrog Metallurgical Plant, the largest enterprise in southern Russia, closed on Tuesday. It specialized in the production of pipes, mainly for the oil industry. The main reason for the closure is that consumers owe the plant more than 20 billion rubles for output. The Taganrog plant does not have the means to pay for fuel for the open-hearth furnaces.
