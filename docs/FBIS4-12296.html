BFN [Text] At 1300 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Cypriot Health Minister Manolis Khristofidhis and his accompanying delegation. The meeting was attended by Health Minister Dr. Muhammad Iyad al-Shatti and the Cypriot ambassador in Damascus. Khristofidhis conveyed to the president the greetings of Cypriot President Glavkos Kliridhis. The president thankfully accepted the greetings and asked Khristofidhis to convey his greetings and best wishes to President Kliridhis. Discussion during the meeting dealt with the situation in the region, the peace process, and bilateral relations.
