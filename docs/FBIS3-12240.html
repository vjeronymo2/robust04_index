Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] Francois Mitterrand has written to Yitzhaq Rabin and Yasir 'Arafat. Bill Clinton has invited them to come to Washington again, but is not under any illusion for the time being. Yasir 'Arafat prefers to wait and see, as he told our correspondent in Tunis, Michel Montpontet: [Begin recording 'Arafat in English with superimposed French translation; processed from the English] We are in need now of an international protection for our people. This is very important and the peace accord we have signed in Washington last September mentions an international presence, and the Israelis accepted that. Armed settlers are a real time bomb. We are asking the Israeli government to take measures, steps, and to take their responsibility, and to respect what they have signed. It is true that President Clinton offered us an invitation, but before that we would want to know under what umbrella. [end recording]
