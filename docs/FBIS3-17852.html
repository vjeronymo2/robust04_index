Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, March 29 (SNARK) -- Tension still exists at the Kodory ravine (Abkhazian Svanetia). Using helicopters Abkhazian side goes on with additional man power and military hardware transfer. Press-Service of the Georgian Defense Department told SNARK hard clashes are going on at the Kvemo-Lata village. Using artillery launchers, military hardware and howitzers Abkhazian side continues Georgian villages shelling. Georgian side manages to repel the enemy. The Georgian volunteer forces lost 5 soldiers, 4 soldiers were wounded. Abkhazian side lost 60 soldiers. As for the Zugdidi region, the situation is stable. No serious clashes took place.
