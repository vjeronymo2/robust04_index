March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AIDS OUTREACH TEAM ADDED TO DRUG ABUSE CENTER

An AIDS outreach team has been added to the counseling services offered by Los Angeles Centers for Alcohol and Drug Abuse in this community.

The team's goal is to provide AIDS education and prevention services to the center's clients. The AIDS service complements existing services at the center such as outpatient counseling, drug-diversion sessions, drunk-driving classes and youth services.

Los Angeles Centers for Alcohol and Drug Abuse is a cooperative effort between the Rio Hondo Community Action Network and Open Door Clinics, two nonprofit agencies that have provided treatment for substance abuse to many communities for 18 years.

The Rio Hondo Community Action Network is at 11769 Telegraph Road. Programs and service information can be obtained by calling 949-2381.
