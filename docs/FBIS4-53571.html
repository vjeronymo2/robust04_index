BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 20 KYODO -- Japan's previous vice finance minister, Mamoru Ozaki, was appointed Friday [20 May] by Finance Minister Hirohisa Fujii to head People's Finance Corp., replacing Sadaaki Hirasawa. Hirasawa, also a former vice finance minister, is widely expected to be become president of the Bank of Yokohama in June. People's Finance Corp. is a government-run firm that provides low-interest loans to small businesses. A rash of such high-profile appointments recently has raised anew the issue of "amakudari," the "descent from heaven" of ranking bureaucrats to influential private-sector positions.
