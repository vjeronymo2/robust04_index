910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / Police battle with extremists in the Croatian port of Split Police battle with extremists in the Croatian port of Split yesterday after clashes on Monday in which a soldier was killed and others injured. Yugoslav leaders held crisis talks after General Veljko Kadijevic, the defence minister, said civil war had begun and that troops, on full alert, would open fire if attacked. The Tanjug news agency reported that the army had seized control of three bridges linking Croatia and the Serbian province of Vojvodina. Schools and some factories in eastern Croatia were also closed.
