BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received 'Abd-al-Basit Salih Sabdarat, culture and information minister of fraternal Sudan, who delivered a message to the brother president from his brother Sudanese President Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan al-Bashir dealing with bilateral relations, developments in our country, and issues of mutual interest. The message stresses Sudan's firm and principled support for Yemen's unity, as well as Sudan's concern about the march of stability and peace in Yemen. During the meeting, they reviewed aspects of bilateral relations and ways of enhancing them to serve joint interests. The brother president praised the brothers in Sudan, led by brother Lt. Gen. 'Umar Hasan al-Bashir, for their support for our people and Yemen's unity, calling it a national and pan-Arab gain for all the sons of the Arab nation. The brother president referred to current developments in our country and the challenges traitor agent al-Bid has imposed on the homeland and its unity by triggering sedition and infighting among the sons of the one homeland. He stressed that our Yemeni people will stick to their will and unity. The president asked the Sudanese culture and information minister to convey his greetings to his brother Lit. Gen. 'Umar Hasan al- Bashir, as well as his wishes for progress and prosperity for the Sudanese people. The meeting was attended by Information Minister Hasan al-Lawzi.
