BFN [Text] Thousands of cassette tapes were destroyed earlier today at the Board of Investments' parking lot in Makati. The pirated music tapes were confiscated from various establishments these past three months. Trade Secretary Rizalino Navarro, General Javier Carbonell of the Videogram Regulatory Board, and representatives from the music industry witnessed the destruction of the tapes. Millions of pesos are lost from piracy and counterfeiting of articles with intellectual property rights including movies and videos tapes, medicine, garments, books, and other publications.
