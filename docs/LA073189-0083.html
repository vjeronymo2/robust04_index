July 31, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

LAGUNA HILLS' KORTMAN PICKS SAN LUIS OBISPO

Ron Kortman of Laguna Hills High School has signed a letter of intent to play baseball at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.

Kortman was 11-2 last season for the Hawks, who reached the Southern Section 3-A quarterfinals. He had 53 strikeouts and a 0.89 earned-run average during the regular season.
