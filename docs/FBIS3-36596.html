Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, February 7, (SNARK) -- Confidential sources in Baku told SNARK that the youth patriotic organization entering the Azerbaijani People's Front has appealed Geydar Aliyev to resign as a president. Otherwise, the organization threatens to effect terrorist and sabotage acts on Azerbaijan territory. In this connection, the official circles in Azerbaijan do not rule out that the recent explosion in the Baku-Kislovodsk passenger train was undertaken by fighters of this organization.
