930430 FT 30 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Nelson warns on regulation THE PACE of change in financial markets should not be allowed to get ahead of the ability of managers and regulators to deal with it, Mr Anthony Nelson, financial secretary to the Treasury, said yesterday. Mr Nelson said at a conference organised by the Financial Times and the Centre for the Study of Financial Innovation, a London-based group: 'I share some of the CSFI's concern that at times the understanding of what is happening in the financial community is in danger of lagging behind the dynamics of the markets.' The development of new financial products and the application of new technology to the financial markets were strengths of London's international financial centre, he said. But he added: 'We should think very carefully about introducing new methods just because the technology is available.'
