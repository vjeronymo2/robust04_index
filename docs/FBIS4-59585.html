BFN [Text] New York, 14 May (INA)--At his place of residence here last night, Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met with Russian Ambassador to the UN Yuliy Vorontsov. The deputy prime minister also met with Henrick Fally [name as transliterated], Brazilian deputy permanent representative, whose country is a nonpermanent member of the UN Security Council. Tariq 'Aziz also met yesterday with British Ambassador David Hannay, Djbouti Ambassador Roble Olhay, and Ambassador Rolf Ekeus, chairman of the UN Security Council Special Commission. Following his one-hour meeting with Tariq 'Aziz, Amb. Ekeus told reporters that the meeting was fruitful and that there was a common understanding between the two sides. He explained that the two sides discussed developments in all fields, as well as monitoring and future verification.
