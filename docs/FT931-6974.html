930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: British Vita in two N American deals British Vita, the polymer, fibre and foam company, is expanding its foam conversion activities in North America through two deals worth about Pounds 4.5m. In the US, the Vitafoam subsidiary has acquired Tennessee-based Nabors Manufacturing Company, which has turnover of some Dollars 8m (Pounds 5.6m). In Canada, Vitafoam Products Canada has bought Pre Fab Cushioning Products, a Toronto-based foam conversion business with CDollars 10m (Pounds 5.6m) turnover.
