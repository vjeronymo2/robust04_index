940413 FT 13 APR 94 / Ryanair to start Prestwick service Ryanair, the independent Irish airline, is to operate flights between Dublin and Glasgow using Prestwick, the privately owned airport in Ayrshire which has had no scheduled flights since 1991. The twice daily flights in each direction will be priced at much less than the Aer Lingus services between Dublin and Glasgow's Abbotsinch airport.
