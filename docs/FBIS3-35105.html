Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Acute tension and caution prevail in the south and western al-Biqa' amid continuing enemy reinforcements and threats. Israeli radio said intermittent firing took place between the Resistance men and the occupation forces and agents. The militia [South Lebanese Army, SLA] radio said a Katyusha rocket fell at the al-Bayyadah post. The Agent militia continue to force residents of the Strip to enlist in their ranks. Reports indicate that the militia personnel raided Kafr Kila, al-Tayyibah, and al-Qantarah and arrested 80 young men aged from 18 to 35 and herded them to a training camp in al-Majidiyah. The agent militia commander, Antoine Lahd, has been absent recently and his deputy Karamallah Sa'id represents him at various meetings and activities. Elsewhere, two officers of the UN Interim Force in Lebanon [UNIFIL] were wounded this morning in a landmine explosion while on a field tour north of al-Naqurah. They were taken to the UNIFIL hospital in al-Naqurah. One of the officers was said to be seriously injured and the other slightly.
