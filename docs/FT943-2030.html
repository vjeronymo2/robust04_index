940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Parambe tumbles to Pounds 17,000 Pre-tax profits at Parambe, an investment company, fell by 58 per cent from Pounds 40,238 to Pounds 17,002 in the six months to June 30. Turnover was down at Pounds 90,357 (Pounds 106,792). The company said that while some of the larger holdings in its investment portfolio performed strongly, security dealing income was well down at Pounds 26,130, against Pounds 47,240. Gross investment income edged up to Pounds 42,609 (Pounds 41,289), and art dealing income produced Pounds 21,618 (Pounds 18,263). Earnings per share however, fell from 0.49p to 0.21p. The company has stopped paying interim dividends (0.55p in 1993).
