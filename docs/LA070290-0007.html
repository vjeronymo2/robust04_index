July 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

U.S. SUPREME COURT OVERTURNS 'OPINION DEFENSE' IN LIBEL CASES

1984 is here, six years late. Conrad's cartoon makes the point that the libel ruling will stifle opinion, and thus I say, stifle dissent.

Now our government has apparently decided on "thought control" beyond its attacks on free speech and a free press.

Can't we see where this Republican-appointed Supreme Court is taking us?

JIM MOORE

North Hollywood
