930206 FT 06 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Tesco's Catteau purchase cleared The EC Commission has cleared Tesco's FFr1.47bn (Pounds 180m) acquisition of Catteau, the food retailer which operates 90 stores in northern France. The purchase, announced in December, is the biggest move into continental Europe by a UK retailer. It was suggested that other retailers would have to follow with similar diversification.
