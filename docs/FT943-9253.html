940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Main points of Offer's review Distribution prices to fall by 11 per cent to 17 per cent next year, and 2 percentage points below inflation for the four following years Average household bill to fall Pounds12 - Pounds16 in first year with a saving of Pounds70 - Pounds90 over the five years. Total saving for all consumers of Pounds2.5bn Controls allow for 'substantially higher investment' but 10 per cent less than electricity companies were planning Companies have until September 30 to accept new controls or appeal to MMC Prices to be based on previous year's inflation rate rather than forecast of coming year's Cost of capital assumed to be 7 per cent before tax New measures to encourage energy efficiency and cut power losses Standards of service on supply restoration and meter reading to be raised Controls assume cuts in wage bills, though chairmen's salaries are 'a matter for the companies not the regulator'
