930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG gold shares finished off their lows but remained sharply down - 157, or 8.5 per cent lower at 1,699 - after the bullion price failed to bounce from its overnight slide and anxious players marked down prices in anticipation of heavy selling. Industrials were 14 off at 4,509 and the overall index was 79, or 2 per cent, lower at 3,902.
