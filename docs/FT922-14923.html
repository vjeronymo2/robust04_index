920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Election 1992: FT Election Share Index SHARES that might benefit from a Tory victory have dropped close to 10 per cent over the election campaign. Possible 'Labour gainers' are 3 per cent down. The big losers among Conservative stocks yesterday were SG Warburg, the investment bank, down 8p to 463p; and Whitbread, the brewer, down 8p to 386p. The big loser on the Labour side was ICI, down 15p to 1,224p.
