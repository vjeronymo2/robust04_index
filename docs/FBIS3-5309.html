Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4 -- President Le Duc Anh received here today Chairman of the Cambodian National Assembly Samdech Chea Sim, who is now on a visit to Vietnam. Mr Samdech Chea Sim expressed his pleasure at the multi-sided achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in the renovation process. He informed President Le Duc Anh of the result of the talks between the two legislative delegations and expressed his belief that the agreements reached during this visit would promote the cooperations between the two legislative bodies, thus contributing to the consolidation and strengthening of the friendly relations between Cambodia and Vietnam. President Le Duc Anh welcomed Mr Samdech Chea Sim's visit describing it a significant contribution to the building of the good neighbourliness and multi-faceted cooperation between the two countries in the interest of the two people and for peace, stability, friendship, development, and cooperation in the region as well as in the world. He highlighted Vietnam's foreign policy to be friends with all countries stressing that it had done and was doing its best to strengthen its friendly relations with neighbouring Cambodia, and to support the Cambodian people's efforts to build their country into an independent, peaceful, neutral and non-aligned Cambodia which maintains friendly ties with all countries, especially those in the region. Also present at the reception were head of the National Assembly Commission for External Relations Hoang Bich Son, and Deputy Head of the Presidential Office Hoang Tuan.
