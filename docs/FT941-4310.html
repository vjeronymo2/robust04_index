940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / UK Company News: M&G Recovery net assets improve The net asset value per capital share of the M&G Recovery Investment Trust stood at 81.25p at December 31, against a value of 46.43p 12 months earlier. The values for the zero dividend preference shares stood at 55.55p, against 49.25p. Net revenue for the nine months to the end of December amounted to Pounds 3.39m, against Pounds 4.3m for the 11 months to end-1992. The third interim dividend on the income shares is being maintained at 1p, payable from earnings per share down from 3.31p to 2.55p.
