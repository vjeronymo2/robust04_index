BFN [Text] New York, 8 May (INA)--Iraq has protested to the United Nations against U.S. aircraft continuing to violate Iraq's airspace for purposes of surveillance and provocation. In a letter he sent to UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali yesterday, Iraq's permanent UN representative, said these aircraft carried out 323 hostile sorties over large areas of northern and southern Iraq on 21, 22, 23, 28, 29, and 30 April. The memorandum also said that a drone fell on the village of Berminkah east of Mosul on 28 April and that hostile enemy dropped hostile publications over al-Ta'mim Governorate on the same day.
