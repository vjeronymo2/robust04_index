BFN [Text] Sofia, April 14 (BTA) -- The Bulgarian Government reiterates Bulgaria's hope to be granted full membership in the European Union [EU]. Bulgaria is ready to make the required representations as soon as the prerequisites for that have been created, says a declaration of the Council of Ministers adopted today. The document states the resolution of the Bulgarian Government to do its best and accelerate this process, proceed with the reforms and create the necessary economic, legislative and other conditions for Bulgaria's full integration into the EU. The government is ready to continue its political dialogue with the EU on foreign policy and security issues on both bilateral and multilateral bases. The Council of Ministers suggests that the National Assembly consider the effective acts of the EU in the course of its legislative work in order to bring Bulgarian legislation in line with their standards. In their cooperation with the institutions of the EU member countries, the Bulgarian ministries and departments will be guided by the stipulations of this declaration. The government will rely on the cooperation and support of the European Council, the European Commission and the EU countries in the implementation of its declaration, the document says in conclusion.
