940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: RJB Mining RJB MINING said that of the 4.11m new ordinary shares available in its open offer, 2.09m were placed firm at 340p, and at the closing date for applications 4.09m shares (99.4 per cent of the offer) had either been placed firm or taken up by shareholders. Dealings are expected to commence on March 3.
