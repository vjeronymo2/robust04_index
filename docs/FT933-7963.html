930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / US coal groups in merger talks ASHLAND Coal, the West Virginia coal company 41 per cent-owned by Ashland Oil, and Arch Minerals are discussing a merger which could give the combined company North American recoverable coal reserves in excess of 2.5bn tons, writes Laurie Morse. Arch Minerals is a St Louis-based company with six independent divisions. The majority of Ashland Coal is publicly held.
