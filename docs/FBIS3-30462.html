Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 1 Feb -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak has accepted the resignation of the Bielsko-Biala provincial governor and dismissed the Kielce provincial governor. This was reported on 1 February to journalists by Michal Strak, the head of the Council of Ministers Office. Miroslaw Styczen, the former Bielsko-Biala provincial governor, is a member of the Conservative Party, while Kielce provincial governor Jozef Plaskonka is a member of Solidarity. They were both appointed to their offices in April 1990 by Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki.
