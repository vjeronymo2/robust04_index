940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / KPMG settles KPMG Peat Marwick has agreed to pay Dollars 186.5m (Pounds 121m) to settle a string of government charges that the US accountancy firm failed properly to audit several banks and savings and loan institutions which collapsed in the 1980s, reports Patrick Harverson in New York. The settlement, which Peat Marwick made without admitting the charges, resolves six cases brought against the firm by federal agencies.
