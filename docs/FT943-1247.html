940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Adams begins two-week tour of US: Sinn Fein leader Gerry Adams began a tour of the US aimed at building support for a peace settlement in Northern Ireland. Speaking in Boston, Mr Adams said Sinn Fein, the IRA's political wing, would consider a coalition government with Britain. His two-week tour will also take him to Hartford, Detroit, Cleveland, New York, Philadelphia, Washington, San Francisco and Los Angeles.
