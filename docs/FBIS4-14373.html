BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, has asked Palestinian negotiators to open the file of the Palestinian martyrs who fell during confrontations with the Israeli occupation troops during the period of armed fedayeen action that was launched from Jordanian, Syrian, and Lebanese territories, in addition to the occupied territories. Palestinian sources said that the Israeli authorities have been holding the bodies of more than 100 martyrs in morgues for over two decades, and that they refuse to hand them to their families. The sources explained that this request was passed on during the meetings that were held in Tunis over the past two days.
