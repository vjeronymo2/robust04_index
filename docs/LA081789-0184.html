August 17, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

TouchStone Software Corp. posted net earnings of $10,078 for the second quarter, a 49% increase compared to the year-earlier $6,752. Revenue rose 38% to $351,780 from $254,017. TouchStone earned $39,650 for the six-month period, 48% above the $26,758 for the first half of 1988. Revenue increased 22% to $703,348 from $575,661.
