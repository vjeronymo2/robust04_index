BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] The Constitutional Court has ruled to stop the implementation of the law allowing the government to issue law-decrees to make changes in the state organization and personnel regulations. The Motherland Party applied to the Constitutional Court asking it to stop the implementation of the law until the case for its cancellation is concluded. The Constitutional Court discussed the application today and with a majority of votes, ruled to stop the implementation of the law to prevent situations and losses that might be impossible or difficult to recover.
