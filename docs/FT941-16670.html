940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / International Company News: Skandia takes Spanish insurer Skandia, the Swedish insurance group, is to buy the outstanding 50 per cent of Spanish pension insurer Intercaser de Seguros y Reaseguros, from Caja de Seguros Reunidos (CASER), the Spanish insurance company, Reuter reports from Stockholm. Intercaser was established through a joint-venture between Skandia and CASER in 1980. Skandia said the transaction was in line with its operating strategy which was oriented toward increasing emphasis on long-term insurance savings.
