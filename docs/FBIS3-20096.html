Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The prototype of the Eurofighter 2000 plane successfully made its first test flight. The one-seat combat plane, the follow-up model of the disputed Fighter 90, is extremely maneuverable and can reach almost twice the speed of sound. According to Deutsche Aerospace, the unit price is to be no more than 100 million German marks. In addition to Deutsche Aerospace, British, Italian, and Spanish companies are also involved in the development of Eurofighter 2000.
