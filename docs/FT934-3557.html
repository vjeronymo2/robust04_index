931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Perkins Foods warns profits will fall short Perkins Foods warned yesterday that its profits would fall 'somewhat short' of market expectations for the year ending December 31. The shares fell 8p to close at 74p. The company, which expects second half pre-tax profits to be similar to the first half's Pounds 10.1m, also said it would be making exceptional provisions of Pounds 4m for the closure of some loss-making operations. However, it expects an improvement next year, and intends to maintain the final dividend at 2.7p a share, which would give a total for the year of 4.45p (4.4p). The company said that since the interim statement 'trading conditions have deteriorated with poorer than anticipated sales and operating margins in September and October'. While the group was experiencing a seasonal improvement in demand, 'operating margins are expected tor remain under pressure.' Last year pre-tax profits were 8.7 per cent down.
