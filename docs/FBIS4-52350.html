BFN [Text] Tehran, May 6, IRNA -- Iranian Ambassador to Australia [Mohammad] Ruhisefat in a meeting with Australian Foreign Minister Gareth Evans in Canberra today exchanged views on regional and international issues as well as on human rights. Evans referred to Iran's importance in the history of world civilization and said the Islamic Republic should have a greater role in international issues. He added that his country was independent and adopted decisions based on its own national interests.
