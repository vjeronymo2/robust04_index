BFN [Text] Eduard Shevardnadze, the Georgian head of state, has said that President Boris Yeltsin has confirmed Russia's stance regarding the introduction and deployment of CIS peacekeeping forces in Abkhazia. According to the Georgian leader, Boris Yeltsin said in a telephone conversation with him that there were plans to begin the introduction of peacekeepers in the very near future. We remind you that two days ago the Federation Council voted against the participation of Russians in peacekeeping operations in Abkhazia.
