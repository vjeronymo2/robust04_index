940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / Maxwell pension ruling reserved The Court of Appeal yesterday reserved judgment on the action between the liquidators of Bishopsgate Investment Management, the former manager of Maxwell pension funds, and the administrators of Maxwell Communication Corporation. The liquidators want a declaration that the pensioners are entitled to mount an action to claim the first Pounds 235m of the Pounds 680m-plus raised by sales of former MCC businesses.
