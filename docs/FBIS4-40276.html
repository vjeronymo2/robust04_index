BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Text] Moscow, 15 Jun -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev today met Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam in a Foreign Ministry mansion. Nguyen Manh Cam arrived in Moscow together with Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. "We have many interests in common with Vietnam.... Our Vietnamese friends are our wealth in Southeast Asia," the head of the Russian foreign political department said at the beginning of his conversation with his Vietnamese counterpart. It is not Nguyen Manh Cam's first visit to Moscow as Vietnamese foreign minister. Earlier he worked here as his country's ambassador for some years.
