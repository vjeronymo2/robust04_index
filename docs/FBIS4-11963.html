BFN [Text] Two IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers were lightly injured in stone-throwing incidents in the territories today. Eight Arabs were wounded, three of them moderately: five in Hebron, and the other three in Qalqilyah, Ramallah, and Yattah. Our correspondent points out that several locations are under curfew, including Ramallah, 'Anabta, and several refugee camps in the Ramallah and Hebron area.
