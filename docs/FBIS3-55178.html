Language: English Article Type:BFN [By CRIME-PRESS correspondent Yuriy Shcherban] [Text] Moscow, January 5 (TASS) -- The trial of 1991 Soviet coup organisers adjourned on Wednesday [5 January] until January 10 because of the illness of two accused. Former soviet Prime Minister Valentin Pavlov has a high blood pressure, while former Soviet parliament speaker Anatoliy Lukyanov is suspected to suffer from pneumonia.
