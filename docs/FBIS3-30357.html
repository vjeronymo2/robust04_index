Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 1 Feb--Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy received Belarusian Ambassador Vladimir Senko on 1 February. The Sejm Chancellery has reported that the ambassador described the internal situation in Belarus in connection with the recent dismissal of Stanislav Shushkievich, chairman of the Belarusian parliament. They discussed Belarusian-Russian relations in the areas of the unification of the monetary systems of both countries and closer economic cooperation. Much attention was devoted to the Partnership for Peace program, particularly the procedure of joining the program. Both men expressed deep satisfaction with the state of bilateral relations but pointed out that the level of parliamentary contacts was insufficient. At the end of the meeting, Ambassador Senko issued an official invitation to the Sejm speaker to visit Belarus, the Sejm Chancellery statement says.
