940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Observer: Gift of the gab The government's increasingly risible attempts to redefine its 'back to basics' campaign bring to mind the Law of Irrelevance as promulgated in Yes Minister and helpfully reprinted in week one of the BBC TV series' 1994 desk calendar. 'The less you intend to do about something, the more you have to keep talking about it.'
