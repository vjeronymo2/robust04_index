931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / MoD unions seek Devonport talks AN URGENT meeting with ministers is being sought by Ministry of Defence unions following Friday's announcement of 500 redundancies at Devonport naval dockyard in Plymouth, Devon. Mr Jack Dromey, chairman of the group of MoD industrial unions, said yesterday that when the government awarded the Trident submarine refitting contract to Devonport in June, ministers had promised job losses of no more than 350.
