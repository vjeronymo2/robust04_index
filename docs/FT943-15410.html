940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: M&W From turnover ahead 21 per cent to Pounds 39.3m, pre-tax profits of M&W, the Southampton-based convenience store operator, rose by 13 per cent from Pounds 927,000 to Pounds 1.05m for the 26 weeks to April 3. Earnings per share were 4.3p (3.81p) while the interim dividend is lifted to 1.25p (1.1p). Seven stores were opened during the period - bringing the total to 118 - and directors expected to open 'at least a further 25' in the second half.
