BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Comrade Choe Kwang, chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army [KPA], met and had a friendly conversation with Colonel Mustafa al Haroubi, inspector-general of the Libyan Armed Forces who is a special envoy of the leader of the great September First revolution of the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, and his entourage, when they paid a courtesy call on him at the Mansudae Assembly Hall on April 17. Present were Chang Song-kil, vice-minister of Foreign Affairs, and Ramadan Muhammad El Laubi, acting secretary of the People's Committee of the Libyan People's Bureau in Korea.
