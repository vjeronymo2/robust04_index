930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: John Maunders With turnover down 6 per cent at Pounds 25.2m, pre-tax profits of John Maunders Group, the housebuilder, fell sharply from Pounds 2.19m to Pounds 1.46m in the six months to December 31. Earnings per share were 3.98p (5.97p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 2.3p. Gearing at the half year stood at 38.7 per cent (22.2 per cent).
