Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, January 24 (BTA) -- Factor-R, a Bulgarian preparation stimulating the immune system, will be tested on HIV- positive Bulgarians. The National Institute of Medical Preparations endorsed the clinical testing today. It is up to HIV-carriers and persons who have developed the full symptoms of AIDS to decide whether to accept the proposal for taking part in the clinical tests of Factor-R which will be carried out in Bulgaria for the first time. Factor-R has been developed by a medical team headed by Prof. Bogdan Petrunov, director of the National Centre of Infectious and Parasitic Diseases. The team has been working jointly with Life Choice International and the Applied Research Combine of Pharmaceutics. The tests of the preparation began in October last year. It is tested on a voluntary basis in the United States, being administered to subjects from Austin, Texas. The blood tests of the patient who was the first to start taking Factor-R show a definite improvement, BTA was told by Life Choice International.
