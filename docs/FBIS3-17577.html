Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by special ITAR-TASS correspondents Vasiliy Belousov and Dmitriy Gorokhov] [Text] Murmansk, 26 Mar -- During his two-week vacation, the head of the Russian state worked intensely and gave us so many instructions that it will take at least an entire month to carry them out. This capacity for work shows that Boris Yeltsin is in good health and in good shape, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev stated here today. He commented on the president's state of health at the request of journalists, after having completed his talks with [Foreign Minister] Bjorn Tore Godal, the head of the Norwegian foreign policy department.
