Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 5 Jan (WAKH)--It was announced here today that Rashid Bin-'Abdallah, foreign minister of the United Arab Emirates, will lead the Emirian delegation to the two-day meeting of the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration states to be held in Damascus on Sunday. During the meeting, the ministers will discuss developments in the Gulf, the Middle East peace process, and the situation in Somalia and Bosnia-Herzegovina. The ministers will also discuss ways of reinforcing cooperation among the signatories of the Damascus Declaration that was signed in March 1991.
