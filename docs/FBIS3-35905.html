Language: English Article Type:BFN [From 31 January RAHVA HAAL, PUHAPAEVALEHT, pp 1,6] [Text] Monday, January 31 -- President Lennart Meri met Russian Ambassador Alexander Trofimov at the latter's request on Friday [28 January]. Ambassador Trofimov handed over an answer to the letter sent to Russian President Boris Yeltsin on January 5. Yeltsin discussed the problems the negotiations have failed to solve so far and expressed his hope that a mutually acceptable solution would be found. The Estonian delegation is to leave for Moscow today for another round of negotiations. Head of the delegation Ambassador Vaino Reinart said that all four groups of issues -- military, border, humanitarian and economic -- will be discussed. He hoped that the basic agreement on the troop withdrawal may be initialled this time. But he admitted the existence of disagreements on the compensation of the environmental damage and the armament confiscated in 1940, as well as the ownership of military establishments and the jurisdiction of the Russian military units. Head of the Russian delegation Vasili Svirin announced that the Russian delegation will hand over the accurate troops withdrawal timetable at the February 1-2 round of negotiations. He added that the Estonian side is attempting to replace the basic agreement with a brief protocol on the troops withdrawal.
