920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / Debt concerns top CAB queries CITIZENS' ADVICE bureaux are dealing with nearly 30,000 inquiries every working day because of the recession, with consumer and debt inquiries top of the list of concerns for the first time for a decade. The National Association of Citizens' Advice Bureaux says there were just over 7.6m inquiries last year - 7.7 per cent up on the previous year.
