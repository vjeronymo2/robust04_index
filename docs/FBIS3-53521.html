Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Jan (INA)--Iraq has again informed the Arab League that the Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers have continued to provide facilities to U.S., British, and French aircraft to violate the Iraqi airspace, noting that this seriously jeopardizes Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter addressed to the Arab League secretary general yesterday, the Iraqi permanent representative to the Arab League said that the aforementioned aircraft continued on 10-13 January to violate the Iraqi airspace, flying from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. They carried out 176 hostile air sorties on the aforementioned dates. A list attached to the letter detailed these violations.
