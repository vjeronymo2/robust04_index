BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs has disclosed that on Wednesday he had a telephone conversation with Swedish Prime Minister Carl Bildt, in which the Swedish leader was cautious about the order from Russian President Boris Yeltsin [on establishing military bases], and expressed the supposition that Russia could revoke the document, as has repeatedly happened with Russian statements. At a news conference Birkavs said that recently Russia has adopted resolutions testifying to its unwillingness to normalize relations with Latvia: For example, it has decreed very unfavorable customs tariffs for mutual relations and high levies for the use of roads.
