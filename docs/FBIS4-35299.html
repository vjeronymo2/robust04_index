BFN [Text] Brother Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister, today received brother 'Awni al-'Ani, resident representative of the UN Development Program [UNDP] in Sanaa. They discussed cooperation between our country and the UNDP and the urgent aid -- including food, clothes, medicine, medical equipment, and electricity generators -- which the UNDP is expected to provide to deal with the circumstances facing our country as a result of the crisis fabricated by the secessionist rebels in the Socialist Party leadership. The UNDP resident representative expressed readiness to provide aid and help in the food and health fields and to revitalize the institutions that sustained damage.
