Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Quito, 25 Mar (EFE) -- The Government of Ecuador announced today that it will vote for Colombian President Cesar Gaviria for OAS secretary general, and not for Costa Rican Foreign Minister Bernd Niehaus, whom it had promised earlier to support. The announcement of the vote change at the OAS is included in a brief, two-paragraph communique issued this morning by the Foreign Ministry.
