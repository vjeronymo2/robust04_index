BFN [Text] The Brazilian Government has donated 15 Brazilian-made primary training airplanes to the Bolivian Air Force. The Brazilian Aeronautics Ministry considers the refurbishing cost of these airplanes, which are poorly maintained, very high. "They are as worn out as a driving school Volkswagen Beetle," an Aeronautics officer said. These airplanes, also called [Aerotec T-23] Uirapuru, were used to train Aeronautics cadets in their first year of pilots school.
