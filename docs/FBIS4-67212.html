BFN [Text] Ruse, May 12 (BTA) -- More than 120 kg of heroin--refined, according to police sources, and unrefined, according to the prosecuting magistracy--have been seized at the Danube Bridge checkpoint in Ruse. The narcotic was distributed in about 250 packets and concealed under the bed on top the driver's cabin of a Fiat caravan which was leaving Bulgaria. The boy and the girl--both from Germany--travelling in the Fiat were arrested. This is the largest amount of heroin discovered at the Danube Bridge checkpoint so far.
