BFN [Text] Istanbul, May 18 (A.A.) -- Security forces caught five members of the separatist terrorist organization PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan], together with two Kalashnikov rifles, revolvers and rounds. A statement by the anti-terror department of the Istanbul Security Directorate said today that, security forces, acting on a tip, conducted operations in a number of districts. Members of the terror gang, Ali Kanat, Hiyasettin Altun, Vahit Sevim, Mehmet Celik Altindere and Cumhur Altun were caught. Interrogations showed that Altun had received accelerated bombing training in Greece. An investigation into the group is underway.
