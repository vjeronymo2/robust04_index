940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: M&G Income net asset value at 73.3p The split capital M&G Income Investment Trust had a net asset value of 73.3p per capital share at July 31, down from 93.45p at end-January, but representing a modest increase on a year-on-year basis. Net revenue for the six months improved to Pounds 6.72m (Pounds 6.06m) for earnings of 2.72p (2.46p) per income share. A second interim dividend of 1p makes 2p to date. The smaller M&G Recovery Investment Trust reported net asset value per capital share of 70.04p as at June 30, up from 67.59p a year earlier. Attributable revenue for the three months to end-June amounted to Pounds 1.41m (Pounds 1.31m), equivalent to earnings of 1.05p (0.99p) per income share. An unchanged first interim distribution of 0.85p is declared.
