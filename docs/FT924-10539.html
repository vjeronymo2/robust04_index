921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Davenport Knitwear advances 75% Davenport Knitwear, the Birmingham-based knitted fabrics and garments manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits ahead 75 per cent to Pounds 374,000, against Pounds 214,000, for the six months to June 30. Turnover increased to Pounds 3.54m (Pounds 3.33m). Earnings per share emerged at 14.46p (8.18p).
