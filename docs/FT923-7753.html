920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / International Company News: Worms acquires option for Agache stake WORMS, the French industrial holding company, has acquired an option to buy a minority stake in Financiere Agache, the luxury goods company associated with Mr Bernard Arnault, the ambitious French businessman. Worms, which has interests in luxury goods including a holding in Agache through its Financiere Truffaut subsidiary, has acquired an option to buy up to 10.6 per cent of Agache's equity over the next two years. Agache has a significant holding in LVMH, the prestigious products group of which Mr Arnault is chairman. It also holds investments in Christian Dior and Christian Lacroix, the Paris fashion houses, and the Celine luggage company.
