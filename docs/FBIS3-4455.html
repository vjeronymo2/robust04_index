Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 27 (XINHUA) -- Air China today opened a new international air route from Shanghai, China's biggest industrial city, to Frankfurt, Germany. The non-stop 13-hour flight by Boeing 747-400 is scheduled to fly every Sunday, Tuesday and Thursday. In Beijing, the China Xinhua Airlines (CXA) today announced new scheduled flights to provincial capital cities including Xian, Haikou, Jinan and Kunmin, expanding Xinhua's total of air routes to 14. Founded in August, 1992, the CXA now has four Boeing 737-300 planes, running nine of the domestic air routes from Tianjin to Shenzhen, Shanghai, Guangzhou and Harbin.
