CRITICS' CHOICE: RICHARD MILHOUS NIXON: THE RISE OF AN AMERICAN POLITICIAN BY ROGER MORRIS (HENRY HOLT: $29.95; 944 PP.)

"The most detailed and authoritative reconstruction of Nixon's career we have ever had . . . Morris shows a keen understanding of Nixon's strengths and importance as a representative American."Robert Dallek
