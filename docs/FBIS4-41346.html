BFN [From the "Pacific Ocean" Program] [Text] A regional privatization voucher outlet opened in Maritime Kray center. Its main goal is extending post privatization assistance to Russian companies in Siberia and the Far East. It is largely a commercial structure, and was founded by the European Bank of Reconstruction and Development and the Japanese Foreign Ministry. Its capital of $30 million will be used to finance the vitality of investment projects. Moreover, the Japanese Government will extend assistance to the Fund for Technical Aid to the tune of $20 million. Speaking at opening of the new investment structure, Nobuachi Ito, representative of the Japanese Foreign Ministry, announced that the Land of the Rising Sun had high hopes for cooperation with enterprises of this region of Russia because the privatization program has been implemented here with the greatest degree of success. The Japanese expect a positive response from local industrialists, and hope for long-term and fruitful cooperation.
