Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] More Finnish peacekeepers left Finland for the Balkans this morning. At the request of the UN, a troop of 39 guards is being sent to Croatia. In this UN operation in Yugoslavia, Finland already has 230 men in The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia. The Finnish guards will take up their duties tomorrow. The troops' task is to guard the service base and airfield at Pleso near Zagreb.
