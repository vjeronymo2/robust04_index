920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / Fujimori cracks down on guerrillas PRESIDENT Alberto Fujimori of Peru has announced a series of tough measures aimed at cracking down on a wave of guerrilla violence, including a 10 pm-5am restriction on the circulation of private vehicles, Reuter reports from Lima. 'It is a prohibition of circulation of vehicles, not people. Factories will not be affected,' Mr Fujimori said.
