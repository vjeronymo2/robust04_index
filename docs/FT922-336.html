920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / Building Contracts: Pounds 11m workload awarded to John Lelliott JOHN LELLIOTT CONST RUCTION GROUP has been awarded a total of Pounds 11m worth of contracts, including the Pounds 7m fitting out of the new headquarters building in Grosvenor Place, London SW1 for oil company Amerada Hess. The fast-track 17-week project is due to start in August following the completion of the reconstruction of the seven - storey office building behind the existing facade being carried out by developer Land Securities. Work involves the fitting out of 123,000 sq ft of office space with the installation of partitioning, M&E services including air conditioning, and high quality finishes. A computer suite and fitness area will also be fitted out, while a 200-seater restaurant and conference suite will be finished to shell and core standard. John Lelliott Construction Group has also won the Pounds 2.4m contract to build an extension, petrol filling station and car park at the Safeway store in Station Street, Ross-on-Wye. Other contracts include Pounds 600,000 conversion of terraces into seating for 7,000 spectators for Tottenham Hotspur at White Hart Lane, London N17; Pounds 485,000 window renewals and repairs to 71 dwellings at Newlands Estate, London SW18 for Wandsworth Borough Council; Pounds 330,000 strip out of Goodenough House, 33 Old Broad Street, EC3 for Limebank Property Co; Pounds 200,000 refurbishment of Tl/T2 landside corridor at Queens Building, Heathrow Airport; Pounds 160,000 of work for Mercury/Unitel comprising 11 seperate cell installations; and Pounds 150,000 external redecoration of Albert Court, Prince Consort Road, London SW1.
