Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Uzbekistan president, Mr. Islam Karimov, has said that his country will work to prevent conflicts in Central Asia and for the expansion and development of regional and international cooperation. He was speaking at the banquet hosted by the president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, in New Delhi yesterday. Mr. Karimov said his country looked forward to friendly relations and establishment of cooperation with India. In his address, the president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, called for common efforts to fight the threat of terrorism and extremism which subvert the economic development and progress of nations. He expressed the hope that the emergence of independent nations in Central Asia will lead to another cultural renaissance and interaction among the nations of Asia.
