940721 FT 21 JUL 94 / Manila agrees Dollars 469m deal The Philippines has concluded an agreement for the rescheduling of Dollars 469m (Pounds 308m) worth of debts from its Paris Club of official creditors in a meeting in Paris, the government announced yesterday. The rescheduling follows the approval on June 24 by the IMF of a Dollars 685m credit package to support the country's growth-oriented economic programme up to June 1997. It precedes a meeting today of the group that co-ordinates a World Bank-led multilateral assistance programme. Pledges of Dollars 2bn concessional loans and grants are expected.
