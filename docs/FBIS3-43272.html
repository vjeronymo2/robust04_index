Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president, Flight Lieutenant J.J. Rawlings, today received in Accra a Libyan delegation led by (Musa Kosa), deputy minister of foreign affairs. The delegation delivered a special message from the Libyan leader, brother Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, in connection with a number of issues discussed with President Rawlings during his recent visit to Libya. The delegation took the opportunity to convey to President Rawlings and the people of Ghana a message of solidarity and goodwill on the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the 31 December Revolution.
