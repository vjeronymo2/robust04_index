BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 28 -- A sea vessel named Truong Sa-10 has been launched recently by the Ha Long shipyard. The 70.75-metre-long and 11.8-metre-wide ship has a design capacity of 1,000 tonnes and is provided with modern equipment. This transport ship was specially built in service of the Truong Sa archipelago [Spratlys]. In the first half of this year, the Truong Sa Marine Product Company maintained the operation of all its ships. The company has well combined its tasks of economic production, scientific surveys and national defence. It discovered and prevented dozens of foreign vessels from illegally operating in the country's territorial waters and continental shelf.
