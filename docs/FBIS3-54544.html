Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tripoli, Ay Annar [January] 26, JAMAHIRIYA NEWS AGENCY -- In his response to the matter of the basic peoples congresses, 'Umar al-Muntasir, the secretary of the General Peoples Committee for Foreign Liaison and Interational Cooperation spoke on the subject of loans and trusts given to friendly countries saying that they were 2,127 million and 842 thousand dollars, and 22 million french francs of which 947 million and 728 thousand dollars were payed back. He said that the total amount of loans due were a billion, 352 million and 58 thousand dollars as well as 22 million French francs. He added that some countries had payed back their loans while several of these countries did not comply with their payment deadlines.
