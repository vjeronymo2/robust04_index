Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Mar (MENA)-- A responsible security source has denied that any security man has fired a single bullet at the students who staged demonstrations in universities in protest over the Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. In a statement today, he said that the security men's task was to prevent the students from leaving the university campus to preserve security and stability. He stressed that reports that certain security men fired bullets at students are totally baseless and false. He asserted that the task of the security men is primarily to keep law and order and maintain stability, noting that students have the right to express their feelings by staging a peaceful demonstration inside the campus without causing riots and instability.
