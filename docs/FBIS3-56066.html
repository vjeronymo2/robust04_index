Language: English Article Type:BFN [by ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Spirin] [Text] Beijing January 12 TASS -- The Chinese Armed Forces will never act as an aggressor. But they will not tolerate any aggression against China either, Deputy Chairman of the Chinese Central Military Council, Zhang Zhen, said. The statement was made at Zhen's [as received] meeting with visiting Kazakh Defence Minister Sagadat Nurmagambetov. Zhen said the Chinese Army is based on the concept of active defence and bears full responsibility for the preservation of the socialist system in China. Nurmagambetov praised the achievements of economic reform in China. The XINHUA news agency quoted him as saying that "we should learn Chinese experience." The minister expressed satisfaction with the results of his visit the aim of which he formulated before his departure to Beijing as follows: "The establishment of personal contacts with Chinese colleagues and development of friendly relations between the armies of the two countries."
