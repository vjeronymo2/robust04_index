BFN [Text] The security services announced today the killing of several members of armed groups during the last three days, more details with our correspondent: [Unidentified correspondent] In a communique issued today, the security services said that 23 members of armed groups were killed during the last three day in Tlemcen, Boumerdes, Tizi Ouzou, and Blida. The same source said that during operations carried out by security forces considerable amounts of arms were seized. These included shotguns, Kalashnikov assault rifles, machine-guns, ammunition, and other equipment. The communique said that during some of those operations five members of the security forces were slightly injured in the cross- fire with members of the armed groups in Bouguerra in Blida Province. Another communique said that three secondary school girls, who were working as contact agents for an armed group, were arrested in the (Wannouaa) region in M'sila Province on Wednesday [25 May]. The same source said that a machine-gun, subversive documents, and a list containing security vehicles' license numbers were recovered following a search in one of the girls' abode. Investigation revealed that the three pupils played the role of informer under the influence of their mathematics teacher. The communique said that search was under way for this teacher who is still on the run. The three girls appeared before a court on Thursday.
