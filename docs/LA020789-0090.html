February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Radio audiences will be able to order entertainment services advertised on the air by telephone under an agreement between Westwood One, a broadcaster and programmer, and 900 Services, a private telemarketing firm. The deal calls for Westwood One to provide the content for the audiotex offerings, while its partner provides the interactive telecommunications gear needed to enable listeners to participate. No specific products were disclosed.
