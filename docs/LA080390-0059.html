August 3, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Walters Selling Newport Beach Home: Bill L. Walters -- the Denver developer and former business associate of Neil Bush who defaulted on $96 million in thrift loans and told Congress he was broke -- may be moving out of a $1.9-million home in Newport Beach.

Real estate agents say the home -- owned by a trust for Walters' wife -- is now up for sale. It is listed with Prudential California Realty in Newport Beach. The house was the site of a citizens' demonstration Thursday night to protest the nation's S&L crisis.
