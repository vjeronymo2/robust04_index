December 29, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COUNTYWIDE;

ORANGE COUNTY 1990-1991 RESOLUTIONS

"My New Year's resolution is a reaffirmation of my commitment to provide responsive, professional and caring law enforcement service to the citizens of Orange County, with a continued emphasis on drug enforcement. During 1991, the Orange County Sheriff's Department will respond to calls for service promptly, protect lives and property to the best of its ability, initiate and maintain crime-prevention programs to help citizens protect themselves against crime, and apprehend criminal offenders."
