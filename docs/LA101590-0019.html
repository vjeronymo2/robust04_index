October 15, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Dick Tracy's Ultimate Caper: "Dick Tracy," the much-hyped film, will soon become the much-hyped video -- and just in time for Christmas. The Warren Beatty-Madonna blockbuster is to be released Dec. 19. "Dick Tracy," 10 years in the making, has grossed $107 million at the box office. CLAUDIA PUIG
