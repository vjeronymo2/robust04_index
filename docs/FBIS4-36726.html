BFN [Text] Tehran, June 10, IRNA -- Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati will leave here for Germany on Monday [13 June] to follow up the issues raised in the previous telephone conversations between President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani and German Chancellor Helmut Kohl, announced a Foreign Ministry spokesman here today. The spokesman, Mahmud Mohammadi, said during the visit Velayati would also hand over a message from President Hashemi-Rafsanjani to Chancellor Kohl. While in Bonn, Velayati is also to discuss expansion of bilateral ties as well as Iran's relations with the European Union (EU) with other German authorities. Political observers attach importance to the Velayati's visit to Germany in the wake of the Germany's EU presidency in July.
