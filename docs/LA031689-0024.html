March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PAY RAISES FOR COUNTY EXECS

Hooray for our Los Angeles County supervisors!

On one side of the front page of the Metro Section (March 1) is a story about the havoc wrought in county mental health centers by budget cuts and proposed closings.

On the other side of the page is a story about the 3% to 12.9% pay hikes the supervisors (excepting Pete Schabarum) voted for their cronies in county government.

The bureaucrats usually get their way. The poor and sick very seldom do.

EMORY SMITH

Rancho Palos Verdes
