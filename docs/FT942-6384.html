940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / People: Liffe members Andrew Barrett, head of European equity trading at Salomon Brothers International, and Jan Waye of Cargill Investor Services have been elected to the board of directors of London International Financial Futures and Options Exchange (Liffe). The following were re-elected to the Liffe board of directors at the annual general meeting held on May 26: Mark Eynon, SG Warburg Futures & Options; George Moller, MeesPierson ICS; Michael Stiller, Caranco Futures; Evelyn Wright, GNI and John Young, Dean Witter International.
