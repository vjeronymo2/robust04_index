Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 28 TASS -- Commander of air-borne forces Yevgeniy Podkolzin refuted a report by the MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS newspaper that representatives from an air-borne division commander participated in a secret meeting held by Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the ultra-nationalist Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, with the military late in December, reports the same newspaper on Friday. It quotes Podkolzin's words that "not a single air-borne division or brigade commander sent his officers to this or other meetings". Referring to an unidentified Russian intelligence officer, MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS wrote in the report published on Thursday that the LDPR leader held the meeting at a secret country house of the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service. The report noted that the meeting was spearheaded "against the president and that Zhirinovskiy called on army officers to cooperate and pointed to the need for rapprochement between some parliamentary groups and commanders of combat units".
