BFN [Text] Moscow, June 16 (XINHUA) -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and his visiting Vietnamese counterpart Vo Van Kiet failed to sign the treaty on friendly relations on Thursday [18 June] because of contradictions over several issues, the ITAR- TASS NEWS AGENCY reported. It said the disputed issues include prospecting and production of oil and natural gas by joint "Vietsovpetro" venture, the problems of the Vietnamese community in Russia and the Russian naval base in Vietnam's Cam Ranh. The Vietnamese premier, who arrived in Moscow on Wednesday for a three-day official visit, held talks with Chernomyrdin on Thursday morning. He is also expected to meet with Russian President Boris Yeltsin. The sides were planned to sign a package of documents including, originally, the treaty. [sentence as received]
