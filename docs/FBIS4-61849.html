BFN [from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Our correspondent Yevgeniy Kuznetsov has reported from Syktyvkar that today the federal government of Russia set 20 billion rubles aside for the Republic of Komi to enable essential freight to reach the north of the republic. What was also achieved is that the money, bypassing the banks, will be paid directly into the accounts of administrative bodies of the towns and villages concerned.
