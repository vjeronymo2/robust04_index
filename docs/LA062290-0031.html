June 22, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

HOW CALIFORNIA DELEGATION VOTED

Here is how the California delegation voted on the proposed constitutional amendment to prohibit physical desecration of the flag:

Democrats for -- Condit, Martinez.

Republicans for -- Campbell, Cox, Dannemeyer, Dornan, Dreier, Gallegly, Herger, Hunter, Lagomarsino, Lewis, Lowery, McCandless, Moorhead, Packard, Pashayan, Rohrabacher, Shumway, Thomas.

Democrats against -- Anderson, Bates, Beilenson, Berman, Bosco, Boxer, Brown, Dellums, Dixon, Dymally, Edwards, Fazio, Hawkins, Lantos, Lehman, Levine, Matsui, Miller, Mineta, Panetta, Pelosi, Roybal, Stark, Torres, Waxman.

Republicans against -- None.
