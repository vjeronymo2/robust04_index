940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Rise in nickel exchange stocks -Market Report NICKEL was under pressure on the London Metal Exchange yesterday after a sizeable - 1,518-tonnes - rise in exchange stocks stimulated general liquidation. Nickel closed at Dollars 5,855 a tonne, down Dollars 237.50, and only slightly above its three-month low of Dollars 5,850. Dealers said ALUMINIUM could be poised for an attack on overhead resistance at Dollars 1,485 after recent improving fundamentals.
