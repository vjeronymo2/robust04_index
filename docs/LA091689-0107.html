September 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PULSAR DATA SYSTEMS NAMED AN IBM DEALER

Pulsar Data Systems has been selected as the first minority company to become an IBM advanced product dealer, the company has announced.

Under the agreement, IBM will provide Pulsar with the complete advanced product line networking products.

Pulsar, a 7-year-old systems integration firm based in Landover with major facilities in Newark, Del., will focus its marketing efforts on federal, state and municipal government agencies as well as the prime contractor community.
