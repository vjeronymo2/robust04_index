BFN [Text] Dushanbe 14 April, TASS -- Russian border guards detained two Tajik opposition militants who tried to cross into Tajikistan from neighbouring Afghanistan on Wednesday, military sources in Dushanbe said today. Two days earlier Afghanistan-based militants opened gunfire on one of the border villages. No casualties among villagers or border guards were reported. On 11 April opposition fighters attacked an ambulance belonging to the local hospital. A doctor who was travelling in it was wounded. The Tajik Government is preparing a note of protest to Afghan authorities over these incidents. At the same time, independent observers report that over the last six months three such notes were sent to the Afghan Government, but none of them has been answered yet.
