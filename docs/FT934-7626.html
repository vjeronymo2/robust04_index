931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / Cragnotti may surrender soon Mr Sergio Cragnotti, the former Ferruzzi group executive against whom Milan magistrates issued an arrest warrant this week, may give himself up to police shortly, according to his defence lawyer, writes Haig Simonian from Milan. The warrant against Mr Cragnotti, who left Ferruzzi in 1990 to set up his own merchant banking group, is part of a new sweep against suspects, including Mr Enrico Braggiotti, the former chairman of Italy's prestigious Banca Commerciale Italiana. Both are being sought in investigations into the affairs of the troubled Ferruzzi group. Mr Cragnotti was a close aide to Mr Raul Gardini, the former Ferruzzi boss who shot himself last July.
