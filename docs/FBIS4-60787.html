BFN [Text] The edict of the presidium of the National Assembly of the Republic of Tajikistan on celebration of Victory Day: (1) To permit holding festivities dedicated to Victory Day from 2 to 9 May 1994, as an exception in conditions of the state of emergency in Dushanbe city and other areas of the republic where the state of emergency is declared. (2) The Ministries of Internal Affairs, Security, and Defense of the Republic of Tajikistan are to strictly ensure the public order and security and safety of citizens in the festive areas. (3) The edict comes into force from the day of its issue. [Signed] Emomali Rakhmonov, the Chairman of the National Assembly of the Republic of Tajikistan.
