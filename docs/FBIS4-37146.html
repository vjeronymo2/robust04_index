BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Jun (MENA)--The Central Cairo Prosecution today released 14 people, including 10 lawyers, and decided to continue the detention of 19 others, including 17 lawyers, for another 15 days. Their detention was extended to continue their questioning regarding the incidents that took place at the lawyers syndicate on 17 May. The prosecution had leveled against all of them charges of assembly, demonstration, shouting antiregime slogans, and attacking some policemen.
