940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Late selling hits copper market - Market Report A late fall in COPPER prices at the London Metal Exchange erased earlier gains but several other metals found underlying support and ended steady. Three months delivery copper fell to Dollars 1,893 a tonne under a late wave of liquidation orders before ending after hours trading at Dollars 1,895, down Dollars 1 from Monday.
