BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 18/6/1994 -- A Romanian Governmental delegation, led by the head of the Direction for Relations with Moldova of the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and secretary of the Romanian Inter Ministry Committee, Gheorghe Savuica, visited Chisinau this week. The visit was made according to the agreement signed at the reunion of the Romanian and Moldova Inter Ministry committees held 15 February in Bucharest. Dorin Cimpoiesu, first secretary of the Romanian Embassy to Chisinau, stated to BASA that the purpose of this visit was to analyze progress regarding economic integration and consolidation of the common spiritual area and to prepare new projects for future joint reunions to be held in Chisinau. The Romanian delegation met with Governmental representatives. Cimpoiesu pointed out that the secretaries of the two Inter Ministry Committees met with the management of the sanitation equipment plant in Falesti and TEPRO plant in Iasi to discuss the common project of constructing a pipeline.
