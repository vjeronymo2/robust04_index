940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / BR sells charter Pullman unit British Rail is to sell its charter and special train unit, which hires Pullman-style carriages pulled by steam and diesel locomotives, to Flying Scotsman Railways. BR will hand over the six locomotives and 200 passenger and sleeping coaches in April when it has completed its present charter programme. This will give Flying Scotsman, owned by record producer Pete Waterman, time to acquire the licences it will need to operate the rolling stock. Mr Waterman, a former locomotive boilerman, helped singers such as Kylie Minogue and Rick Astley to international stardom.
