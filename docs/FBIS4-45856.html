BFN [Text] The Hue Central Hospital recently detected three AIDS cases in a family of three -- a husband and wife and their four-year-old child. The husband, a 33-year-old drug addict, went to the Hue hospital for treatment of a fever. He was found to be infected with HIV. Since May 1993, Thua Thien Hue Province has discovered 12 cases of HIV infection, also known as AIDS.
