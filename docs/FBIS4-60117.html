BFN [Text] Joint Palestinian-Israeli patrols began operating today in the areas of Khan Yunus and Gaza city. According to the agreement, the patrols will travel on three routes along the Netzarim, Morag, and Qatif Bloc roads. Each patrol will include two jeeps. A Palestinian officer and three policemen will ride in one vehicle, whereas the other jeep will carry an Israeli Border Police officer and three soldiers. Our Army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the length of each route five to seven kilometers and the units patrolling it will be responsible for maintaining security in the area. There will also be a joint Palestinian-Israeli foot patrol in the vicinity of the Erez checkpoint and at the entrance to the Gaza Strip.
