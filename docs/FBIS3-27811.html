Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 30 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam exchanged messages of greetings with 'Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir, secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation, on the occasion of the 20th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya. In the message Kim Yong-nam said that after the establishment of diplomatic relations the two countries have supported and cooperated with each other in struggle for independence against imperialism over the last 20 years. The message expressed belief that the bonds of friendship and solidarity between the two countries would grow stronger and develop. In the message the Libyan secretary said with pleasure that the bilateral relations between the two countries have steadily strengthened and developed in the deep interests of the leaders of the two countries over the last 20 years.
