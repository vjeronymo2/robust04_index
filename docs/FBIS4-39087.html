BFN [Report by Fedor Ignatov, ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Alma-ata [Almaty], 6 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- In accordance with intergovernment agreements, Kazakhstan is to supply over 5 million [metric] tonnes of grain to other countries this year. Three million tonnes is to go to Uzbekistan, while Belarus, Kirghizia [Kyrgyzstan], and Iran are to get 500,000 tonnes each. Grain from Kazakhstan will also go to Tajikistan, Moldavia [Moldova], and Turkmenia [Turkmenistan]. Individual producers will contest the right to export grain in auctions to be staged by the Kazakhstan International Exchange and the Republic's commodity exchanges. A resolution adopted on this subject by the Cabinet of Ministers notes that exported grain will be exempt from customs duty.
