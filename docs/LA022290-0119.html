February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Irvine Buildings Sold: In a deal estimated to be worth more than $40 million, Newport Beach developer Hillman Properties sold two buildings in Irvine near John Wayne Airport to a partnership including billionaire Robert M. Bass.

The partnership is leasing most of the space in the buildings -- about 234,000 square feet -- to Bass' American Savings Bank. The lease is the third largest ever signed in Orange County. American, one of the nation's biggest thrifts with $16.3 billion in assets, finished moving 500 people into the buildings last week from offices in Whittier, Sherman Oaks, Fullerton and elsewhere in Irvine.
