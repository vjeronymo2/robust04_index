BFN [Text] Sarajevo, May 6 -- The United Nations Protection Force (UNPROFOR) will stop two Serb tanks from crossing the military exclusion zone around Sarajevo Friday, UNPROFOR announced. The transit of the tanks -- the last of a total seven scheduled to cross the zone between 2:00 p.m. and 3:00 p.m. (1200 and 1300 GMT) -- had been announced earlier Friday by a UNPROFOR spokesman. But a military UNPROFOR spokesman who refused to be identified said orders were given by the Bosnia UNPROFOR command to block the tanks.
