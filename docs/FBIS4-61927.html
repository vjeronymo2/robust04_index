BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunayskiy] [Text] Moscow May 11 TASS -- 100 foreigners, including 19 Russians, will leave Yemen aboard a Russian plane which left for Djibouti on Wednesday. The foreigners were evacuated from Yemen and are now awaiting the plane aboard two Russian vessels docked in Djibouti -- the "Nikolay Tikhonov" and the "Khudozhnik Kustodiyev". TASS was told by the Russian Ministry for Emergency Situations that the evacuation of Russians from Yemen is expected to be completed by the end of the week.
