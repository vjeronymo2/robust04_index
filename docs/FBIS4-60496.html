BFN [Text] Iraq's senior diplomat in Washington has been expelled for alleged violations of the agreement that allowed Iraq to maintain a diplomatic presence in the United States. THE WASHINGTON POST said that the diplomat had tried to lobby members of Congress and sent out news releases calling for an end to the United Nations economic embargo against his country. It said that he had also hired and fired staff without notifying the State Department or actions counter to the agreement. The paper said that the State Department did not announce his expulsion, but confirmed it when queried.
