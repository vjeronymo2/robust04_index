920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / Russia begins reshuffle A promised government mini-reshuffle has begun in Russia with the appointment of Mr Georgy Khizha, formerly head of a St Petersburg's entrepreneurs association, as deputy prime minister responsible for industry, Leyla Boulton writes from Moscow. His appointment is designed to appease critics of the fall in Russia's industrial output. Mr Alexander Shokhin, previously vice-premier for social policy, has been made vice-premier for foreign economic relations.
