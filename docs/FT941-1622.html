940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Whitecroft WHITECROFT has sold two properties in the south east of England for Pounds 1.72m cash. The value of property awaiting disposal in the balance sheet at March 31 was Pounds 14.4m and this figure has now fallen to Pounds 6.5m. The transactions - further steps towards complete withdrawal from property development - have reduced borrowings and showed a small surplus over book value.
