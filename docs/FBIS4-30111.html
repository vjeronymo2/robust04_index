BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 18 -- A delegation of the Vietnam War Veterans's Association led by Sr. Lt. Gen. Tran Van Quang president of the association, paid a working visit to the United States and France from mid-May to early June. The visit came at the invitation of the president of the International Committee for Fund Raising for the Construction of `Vietnam's Van Canh Friendship Village', the mayor and the Santa Cruz City Council, California, the US, and the French War Veterans, and Invalids Association Arac-France and the Marseille chapter of the Vietnamese's Association in France. While in the US, the Vietnamese delegation attended a conference of the fund-raising committee held in Santa Cruz, California, with the participation of representatives from Britain, Germany, the US, Japan, and France aimed at stepping up preparation for the construction of the village. It also took part in a round-table meeting on veterans' reconciliation in the presence of US veterans from different localities. In France, the Vietnamese delegation attended a ceremony held in Marseille to mark President Ho Chi Minh's birthday. In Paris, it took part in an international conference of the veterans' organizations of Britain, Bulgaria, Germany, Hungary, Russia, France, Slovakia and Vietnam. It also attended a meeting in support of the construction of the Van Canh Friendship Village.
