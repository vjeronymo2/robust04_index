BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 7 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received messages of greetings from party leaders of different countries on his birthday. The messages were sent by Krishna Prasad Battaray, president of the Central Committee of the Nepali Congress Party; Pyotr Simonenko, first secretary of the Central Committee of the Ukrainian Communist Party; Manuel Vargas Loureiro, chairman of the Democratic Renovation Party of Portugal; Leonel Mario Da Alva, chairman of the Democratic Convergence Party and Reflexion Group and president of the National People's Assembly of Sao Tome and Principe; Youssef Amin Wali, general secretary of the Egyptian National Democratic Party; Talal Hafun Ala Mai, general secretary of the People's Unity Party (Unionist) of Jordan; Kaled Bensmain, general secretary of the Movement for Democracy in Algeria; Hanial Beins, national leader of the Communist Party (Marxism-Leninism) of Canada; Virgilio Godoy, secretary general of the Independence Liberal Party of Nicaragua; Denzil Daglas, leader of the St. Kitts-Nevis Labour Party; and Miguel Mejia, secretary general of the United Left Movement of Dominica. The messages sent warmest congratulations to President Kim Il-song and wished him good health and a long life. The messages expressed firm support and solidarity for the Workers' Party and people of Korea in their struggle for the peaceful reunification of Korea.
