Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 4 Jan--Only an interim version of standing orders will be ready for the first session of the Lower Chamber of the Federal Assembly, Grigoriy Bondarev, coordinator of the commission for standing orders and for the status of deputies of the State Duma, told ITAR-TASS today. Bondarev said that "a certain temporary document" would be worked out by 11 January that will allow the State Duma to commence work. Later, according to Bondarev, the State Duma will form its own commission for standing orders, which will "prepare a document for all occasions".
