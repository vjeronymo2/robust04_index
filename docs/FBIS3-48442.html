Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Workers' Party of Korea Central Committee, and his entourage visited the Beijing Plaza and a newly built housing complex at the Fengtai District on 15 January. Hwang Chang-yop and his entourage also visited the Guangzhou Labor Movement Training School's Memorial Hall in Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province, which was set up by President Mao Zedong; the (Tueshu) Park; the Agricultural Science Institute and various plants and enterprises in Sunde City and (Puer) City, from 17 to 20 January. The group arrived in Shenyang City on 21 January. It visited various organizations and plants, including the Liaoning Exhibition Hall in Shenyang City on 22 January. During its stay, the group was invited to banquets hosted by Comrade Ding Guangen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and the Guangdong Province Committee and Liaoning Province Committee of the CPC. Participants of the banquet toasted to the long life of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, and the Chinese leaders, including the respected Comrade Jiang Zemin.
