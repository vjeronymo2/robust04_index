941230 FT 30 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Profit-taking and an easier gold price bumped South African shares off their earlier highs in quiet afternoon trade. The overall index gained 17.1 to 5,857.2, industrials were 14.2 firmer at 6,977.2 and golds picked up 38.5 to 2,014.6. Vaal Reefs put on R8.25 to R370.25 in active trade and Kloof gained R1.40 to R60.75.
