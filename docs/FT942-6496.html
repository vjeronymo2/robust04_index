940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): East European help for bank note maker - Other companies De La Rue, the bank note printer which ended bid talks with Portals on Friday, is tomorrow expected to report strong profits progress. Full-year pre-tax profits of around Pounds 121m are forecast, up from Pounds 104.7m. The bank note division has benefited from strong demand, particularly from the emerging nations of eastern Europe.
