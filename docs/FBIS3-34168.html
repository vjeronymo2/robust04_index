Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A contract was signed today between the Development and Reconstruction Council and the Deloitte and Touche company under which the company will prepare a study on reorganizing the Telecommunications Ministry to establish a more productive and effective telecommunications sector. The study also aims to provide the necessary conditions for attracting local and foreign capital. The Development and Reconstruction Council has received an urgent loan from the World Bank to rehabilitate the various sectors and improve the level of services.
