July 22, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

VH-1 MAKES HIM M-A-D

I have a lot of respect for the cable music channel VH-1. So I hope the channel stops its disreputable practice of continuously plastering VH-1 over all the videos. This is equivalent to a radio deejay whispering the call letters of his or her station over and over during the songs.

Michael Storm, Santa Barbara
