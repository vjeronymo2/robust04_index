930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Suspension of EIT shares at 9 1/2p Dealings in the shares of EIT, the USM-quoted information technology group, were suspended yesterday at 9 1/2 p pending clarification of the company's financial position. The group, formerly known as Maxiprint, reduced its pre-tax losses from Pounds 246,000 to Pounds 190,000 in the year to March 31 1992.
