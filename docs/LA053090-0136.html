May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Larry London has been promoted to vice president of Geneva Corp., an Irvine merger and acquisition company. Before joining Geneva in 1985 as a seminar leader, London was regional vice president of Control Data Corp. In addition, Patti Pepper was named vice president of Geneva Business Research Corp. Before joining the company in 1985 as a market research analyst, she was an advertising intern for Ervin Advertising and Design Inc. in Seal Beach.
