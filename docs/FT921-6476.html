920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / Arco in Pounds 500m gas supply deal ARCO BRITISH, a subsidiary of the US oil company Atlantic Richfield, has agreed a Pounds 500m deal to supply gas to National Power, the UK electricity generating company. The agreement covers the entire Orwell field, which is estimated to contain 250bn cu ft of gas, close to the border between the UK and Dutch sectors of the North Sea. The field will start producing gas at a rate of 120m cu ft a day in October next year and has a 10-year life. Arco has kept a one-year option in the contract to pull out 45m cu ft a day which it could sell directly to the gas market if prices were favourable.
