920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Shortlist to build Olympic stadium FOUR companies have been shortlisted out of 30 applications to design, build and operate the Pounds 170m stadium complex for Manchester's bid for the 2000 Olympic games. Amec, Balfour Beatty and Trafalgar House of the UK and Societe Auxiliaire d'Entreprises of France have until mid November to produce detailed plans. Manchester hopes to have work under way when the International Olympic Committee awards the 2000 games in September next year. Editorial comment, Page 12
