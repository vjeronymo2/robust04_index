February 2, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

COSTA MESA'S TEAM TO POLICE DRIVERS

Today I had the opportunity to read the article by Patrick Mott about the Costa Mesa Police Department's driving under the influence team that was printed in the Dec. 16 edition of the Los Angeles Times.

Mr. Mott's article was informative, very well researched and portrayed an honest example of the Police Department's efforts to reduce the trauma caused by "drunk drivers."

Please thank Mr. Mott for taking the extra time and effort to write the story.

David L. Snowden,

Chief of Police

Costa Mesa
