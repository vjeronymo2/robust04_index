Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid yesterday evening signed a document on setting up the military committee in charge of withdrawing military units and returning them to their barracks and following up the implementation of the provisions of the Pledge and Agreement document. In another development, the European Union ambassadors in Sanaa appealed to all the parties to the recent violent clashes to refrain from making any military moves that could adversely affect the implementation of the Yemeni reconciliation agreement.
