BFN [Text] Halle (DPA) -- Social Democratic Party of Germany [SPD] Chairman Rudolf Scharping is the official SPD candidate for the chancellor's office. At the special congress in Halle, 479 of 502 -- that is, an approval of 93 percent -- delegates voted for him on Wednesday afternoon [22 June]. The 46-year-old minister president of Rhineland-Palatinate did not have an opponent in the secret vote.
