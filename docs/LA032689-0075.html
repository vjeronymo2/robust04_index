March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DELICIOUS APPLE FARM

We stayed at the Apple Farm Inn & Restaurant, 2015 Monterey St., San Luis Obispo 93401, phone (805) 255-2040, as a result of Jerry Hulse's recommendation in "The Heart's Hideaways" (Feb. 12) and it was everything Hulse said it was . . . and more! Very charming, spotless, friendly and beautifully decorated. And delicious! It was our favorite spot for a three-day getaway. I'm keeping the whole page of "The Heart's Hideaways." Thank you so much for this story.

MR. and MRS. DON SHEARING

Rolling Hills Estates
