July 12, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

U Got the Look: Prince, who has been known to appear on stage in little more than a G-string and a guitar, is coming out with his own fashion line. The Purple One plans to open a shop in the Minneapolis warehouse district in September, and a line of official Prince merchandise -- T-shirts, sweat shirts, jewelry, buttons, calendars and posters -- also will be sold through other retail outlets. The apparel will be designed by his wardrobe department. GREG BRAXTON
