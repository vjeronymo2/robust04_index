BFN [Text] Durres, June 15 (ATA) -- Some 1,562 families from other districts, consisting of 9,419 members, have been settled in Durres district during the past three months, according to a report by the Ministry of Agriculture and Food. The great "rush" of people, some 465 families, have been settled in the Jub Sukth commune. Most of the families come from the northern east of Albania but there are also families from southern east, central and western Albania. They are mainly from Pogradec, Korce, and Tirana. The families from northeastern Albania, which fled from the terrain dangers, have been given 176 hectares of land according to a decision by the government.
