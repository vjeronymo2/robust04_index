BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow May 12 TASS -- A regular meeting of the Russian Government's Presidium, currently under way here, began with the examination of the bill "on civil defence". The meeting is chaired by Prime Minister Viktor Chenomyrdin. After that the ministers will discuss the draft land code of Russia. Moreover, the Cabinet will hear reports on the drafts of two federal programmes -- to modernise the single system of air traffic in the Russian Federation for the period of up to 2005 and on a target programme for 1994-1997 to protect Russian territories from the penetration and spreading of infectious diseases, which are particularly dangerous for human beings, animals and plants, and also of toxic substances.
