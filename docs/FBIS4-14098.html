BFN [Text] Tehran, April 27, IRNA -- The terrorist Mujahideen Khalq Organization (MKO) has backed the aborted attempt on the life of the substitute Friday prayer leader of Mashhad Hojjat ol-Eslam Aba'i Khorasani, on April 22. In a radio broadcast from Iraq, the terrorist grouplet described the failed assassination attempt as a popular move without mentioning its links with the assassin. An armed assailant, identified by the Ministry of Information as 'Ali Huri, shot and wounded Hojjat ol-Eslam Khorasani on Friday. He was later arrested by worshippers and handed over to police. Huri has confessed to his link with the Iraq-based terrorist grouplet and the MKO has not denied the link.
