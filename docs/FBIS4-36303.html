BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God protect him, has ordered increases in the prices of sugar beets from 1,000 dinars per tonne as follows, depending on the percentage of sugar available in the sugar beet: Sugar beets that are 15 percent sugar shall be purchased for 1,600 dinars per tonne. Sugar beets that are 14 percent sugar shall be purchased for 1,500 dinars per tonne. Sugar beets that are 13 percent sugar shall be purchased for 1,400 dinars per tonne. Sugar beets that are 12 percent sugar shall be purchased for 1,400 dinars per tonne. Sugar beets that 11 percent sugar shall be purchased for 1,100 dinars per tonne. Sugar beets that are 10 percent of sugar and below shall be purchased for 1,000 dinars per tonne.
