August 14, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

TENNESSEE RECRUIT FLUNKS SAT

Tennessee's top basketball recruit did not meet requirements on his Scholastic Aptitude Test and will be ineligible as a freshman, according to published reports.

The Knoxville Journal reported today that Norman Marbury, a 6-foot-3 guard from New York City, did not qualify for freshman eligibility under NCAA standards.
