920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / International Company News: Cartel office to approve AGF's stake in AMB THE German cartel office is to approve a holding of 25 per cent plus one share by French insurer Assurance Generales de France (AGF) in German financial holding company Aachener und Munchener Beteiligungs (AMB), AP-DJ reports from Berlin. AGF this week filed suit against AMB's management, which it accuses of blocking AGF from exercising full voting rights in the German company. State-controlled AGF built up the share in AMB without AMB's consent.
