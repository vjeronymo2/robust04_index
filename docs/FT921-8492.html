920217 FT 17 FEB 92 / More Japanese banks in London THE NUMBER of Japanese banks in London has overtaken the total of US banks, according to a survey of foreign bank offices. Noel Alexander Associates, the banking consultancy, says that at the end of last year there were 49 US banks in London compared with 54 Japanese banks. The number of US banks in London has been declining steadily since 1982, when there were 77. In all, 31 foreign banks set up offices in London last year, while 15 left. This contrasts with a net loss of four foreign banks in 1990. New Foreign Banks in London. Noel Alexander Associates, 6 Broad Street Place, Blomfield Street, EC2M 7JU. Pounds 45.
