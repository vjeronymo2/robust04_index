BFN [Text] In accordance with the coordinated agreement reached between the two countries on the repatriation of those who had illegally left Myanmar [Burma] for Bangladesh, a delegation led by Camp Supervisor Mr. Rashidul Islam from the country on the other side [Bangladesh], transferred 61 returnees from 12 households to our Kanyinchaung reception center on the morning of 30 March. The returnees were brought in by two schooners. Since the repatriation of those who left the country illegally began on 22 February 1992 [date as heard], a total of 53,409 persons from 11,990 households have been accepted through 30 March 1994.
