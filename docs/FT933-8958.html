930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / International Company News: HK Electric climbs 13% HONGKONG Electric, the monopoly supplier of electricity to Hong Kong island, announced profit attributable to shareholders of HKDollars 1.3bn (USDollars 167m) for the six months to June 1993, up 13 per cent from the 1992 figure, writes Simon Davies in Hong Kong. The company, 35 per cent owned by Mr Li Ka-shing's Hutchison Whampoa, received a HKDollars 112m pre-tax contribution from sales of property in its South Horizons development. Turnover increased by 11 per cent to HKDollars 2.6bn at the interim stage. cent
