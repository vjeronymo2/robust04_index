October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

READERS HIT BACK WITH COMPLIMENTS

Unlike the tone of the letters toward Larry Stewart in Saturday's Letters (Sept. 29), I happen to think he is great.

I enjoy his column every week. It's the first thing I turn to on Friday. I think he has a perfect feel for what works and what doesn't in radio and television.

LIZ ANDERSON, Alhambra
