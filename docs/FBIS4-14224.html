BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 27 Apr (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates, has received two telephone calls, one from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, and another from Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. The conversations dealt with the acts of violence which took place between Yemeni forces in northern Sanaa today. His Highness Shaykh Zayid called on the Yemeni president and vice president to exert every possible effort to contain the armed confrontation between the sons of the one people, to exercise self-restraint and patience, and to prevent the use of violence to solve the Yemeni crisis. He also called for adopting the principle of dialogue and reconciliation as the only means to end the Yemeni crisis and to stabilize the situation there.
