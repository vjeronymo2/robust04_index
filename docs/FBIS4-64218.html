BFN [Text] Stuttgart -- PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat has arrived in Stuttgart this morning. This was announced by the press office of the airport. The chairman of the PLO Executive Committee is honoring an invitation by Daimler-Benz chief Edzard Reuter. During his one-day visit to the Swabian capital, 'Arafat, who is being accompanied by a delegation of close economic and political advisers, will meet with Reuter and the board of directors of the concern. In addition, the company plans a product and research presentation for the Arab host. Baden-Wuerttemberg's Minister President Erwin Teufel (Christian Democratic Union) will receive 'Arafat for an exchange of views on the peace process in the Middle East. Subsequently, Teufel will host a dinner for his visitor.
