January 22, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING

Two firefighters who were part of a recent earthquake relief effort in Soviet Armenia will present slides and discuss their observations today at Armenian Apostolic Church of Orange County-Forty Martyrs in Santa Ana. Battalion Chief Mike McGroarty of the La Habra Fire Department and Division Chief Larry Greene of Fullerton will be joined by the guest speaker, Prof. Hovhannes Agajanian, chairman of the urology department at the Post-Graduate Institute of Yerevan, Armenia.
