BFN [Text] Montevideo, April 19 (XINHUA) -- Uruguayan Vice President Gonzalo Aguirre Ramirez met with the visiting Chinese Communist Party delegation at the Parliament building here on Monday [18 April]. The delegation, headed by Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the party's politburo, arrived here Monday at the invitation of Uruguay's ruling Blanco Party. Aguirre, who is also president of the National Congress, extended his warm welcome to the delegation, saying that bilateral ties have seen smooth development since the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries. Hu, the highest-ranking Chinese Communist Party official ever visited a Latin American nation, said the purpose of his visit is to promote friendship, cooperation, peace and development. At the meeting, the Chinese official also hoped to further strengthen the friendly relations between the Chinese Communist Party and Uruguayan political parties and between the parliaments of the two countries. Meanwhile, Hu had a meeting with Uruguay's former president Julio Maria Sanguinetti Monday and they exchanged views over both national and international issues of mutual concern. Uruguay is the first leg of Hu's three-nation tour, which will also take him to Argentina and Brazil.
