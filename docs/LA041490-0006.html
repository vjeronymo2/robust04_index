April 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SENATE CONFIRMATION OF THRIFT REGULATOR DESPITE PAST DRUG USE

I wonder if the Grand Old Party would have demonstrated the same compassion toward Michael Dukakis or any of the other presidential candidates in the last election if they found out that any of them had experimented with drugs at some time in their life.

According to Sen. John Warner (R-Va.), "Ryan's generation of Americans is now emerging into top leadership positions in the government." If you follow that train of thought, then Timothy Leary should be President of the U.S.

RICK ZABEL

West Los Angeles
