BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Jun (MENA)--A guard was killed today and two others were wounded in Asyut when unidentified people opened fire at them at a checkpoint in Manfalut district. A responsible security source in the Interior Ministry explained that at about 1100 today, unidentified people opened fire at guardsmen Rayyan Ahmad Muhammad, Salihin 'Abd-al-Hamid Jum'ah, and 'Abd-al-Nazir 'Abd-al-Hamid Hasanayn, who work at the Umm al-Qusur police station in Manfalut district. The source said that the first guardsman was killed in the incident and the two others were wounded and taken to the hospital in Manfalut for treatment. Commanders of the Asyut security directorate, criminal investigation personnel, and criminal laboratory experts went to the scene of the incident, and the prosecution was informed to begin an investigation.
