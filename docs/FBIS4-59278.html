BFN [Text] Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah hes arrived in Amman from Cairo on an official visit to Jordan. Basindwah is carrying a message from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to his brother His Majesty King Husayn that deals with recent developments in fraternal Yemen. He was received on arrival by Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; Nayif al-Hadid, the Foreign Ministry's secretary general; the Yemeni ambassador in Amman, and the Yemeni Embassy's staff.
