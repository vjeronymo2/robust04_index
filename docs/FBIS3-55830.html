Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the meeting between Russian President Boris Nikolayevich Yeltsin and Stanislav Shushkevich, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Belarus, in Moscow today, a detailed analysis was made of the interstate relations which have taken shape. In the center of attention were questions of economic cooperation. There was also discussion of other questions of bilateral relations. The sides are convinced of the need to draft a new interstate Russian-Belarusian treaty of friendship and cooperation that highlights the desire of Russia and Belarus to build democratic states with a socially-oriented market economy and that also highlights the special level of economic, political, and human relations whicha are historical between the two countries.
