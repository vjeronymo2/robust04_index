920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / International Company News: Fruit of the Loom marches ahead FRUIT of the Loom, a leading US underwear maker, lifted second-quarter net earnings 57.5 per cent to Dollars 57m or 75 cents a share from Dollars 36.2m or 52 cents a year earlier, writes Barbara Durr. Sales were Dollars 534m, up 14.1 per cent from Dollars 468m.
