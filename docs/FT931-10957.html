930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / Save-as-you-earn rates come down INTEREST paid on save-as-you-earn share option schemes is about to be lowered. From April 1 the rate of interest on five-year schemes will be lowered from 7.5 per cent to 5.53 per cent per year. On seven-year schemes it will drop from 7.83 per cent to 5.87 per cent per annum.
