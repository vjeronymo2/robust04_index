April 19, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Nichols Institute: The San Juan Capistrano manufacturer of medical testing and diagnostic kits posted a first-quarter net income of $1.7 million, an increase of 77% over the $960,000 reported for the 1989 first quarter. Revenue rose 36% to $40.9 million from $30.2 million.
