Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 26 (ATA) -- The Albanian Academy of Sciences condemned today, through a statement, the illegal and anti-scientific act of the Serbian invading authorities in Kosova who confiscated on February 23 the building of the Kosova Academy of Sciences and Arts in Prishtina. The Academy of Sciences of Albania regards the Serbian act as part of a short-sighted policy inspired by a medieval mentality. The Albania's Science Academy appeals to all the world academies and the progressive international community to protest against those illegal acts in order that the Albanian scientists in the Republic of Kosova may freely exercise their peaceful activity serving the science and prosperity.
