BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Excerpt] Moscow, 16 Jun -- A visit to Brussels by Army General Pavel Grachev, the Russian defense minister, is not planned for the immediate future, Yelena Aganova, the Russian defense minister's public relations and press aide, told the ITAR-TASS correspondent today. According to her, the reports by some mass media that the minister will leave for Brussels in the immediate future do not correspond to reality. [passage omitted]
