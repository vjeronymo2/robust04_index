Language: English Article Type:BFN ["High-ranking Cambodian Defence Delegation Feted" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- The Ministry of People's Armed Forces arranged a banquet at the Mansudae Assembly Hall on the evening of March 21 for the high-ranking delegation of the Ministry of Defence of the Royal Government of Cambodia headed by Co-Ministers of Defence General Tia Banh and General Tia Chambrat on a visit to Korea. In his speech, Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u said that the friendly relations between Korea and the Kingdom of Cambodia based on the particular and deep intimacy between the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and esteemed His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk are gaining greater scope and developing day by day. He said that the royal government, the people and the officers and men of the Cambodian Army are registering remarkable achievements in the work to attain national reconciliation and unity and accelerate the reconstruction of the country, upholding the noble intention of esteemed His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk. "Our people and the officers and men of the People's Army will as ever extend full support and firm solidarity to the fraternal people and officers and men of the army of Cambodia in their efforts for the independence and peace of the country, national unity and territorial integrity," declared O Chin-u. Co-Minister of Defence Tea Chambrat spoke next. He recalled that the friendship between the two countries based on the excellent relations of intimacy between His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk and respected His Excellency Generalissimo Kim Il-song has a long history. "The current visit of our high-ranking military delegation is aimed at demonstrating that we are always with Korea," he said. He expressed the hope that the Korean people would enjoy a greater happiness.
