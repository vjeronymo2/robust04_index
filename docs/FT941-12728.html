940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Partridge recovers to Pounds 1.77m Partridge Fine Arts, the antique dealer, reported pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 1.11m to Pounds 1.77m in the year to October 31. Turnover advanced to Pounds 13.7m, against Pounds 8.36m, the highest since the 1989-90 year. Mr John Partridge, chairman, spoke of a return of confidence, with recent auctions showing considerable demand for the finest pieces with strong prices. However, there was some way to go before there was a lasting effect on the market. Earnings per share came out at 5.54p (3.25p). A proposed final dividend of 1.5p makes a total of 2.7p (2.25p).
