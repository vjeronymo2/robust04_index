BFN [Excerpts] Jeddah, 20 Jun -- The Council of Ministers held its weekly meeting today in al-Salam Palace in Jeddah under the chairmanship of His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, second deputy prime minister, minister of defense and aviation, and inspector-general. After the meeting, the Information Minister 'Ali al-Sha'ir made a statement to SPA in which he said: After a quick review of a report on the most significant political and military events in the Arab region, the council heard a comprehensive explanation by his royal highness the second deputy prime minister on some topics in the report, primary among which are events on the Yemeni scene. In this context, the information minister said: The council followed with great interest the continuous efforts made by the Security Council through UN Envoy Lakhdar Brahimi, who still continues his attempts to secure an implementation of Resolution No. 924 calling for a cease-fire throughout all Yemeni territory in order to end the bloodshed and save the lives of the sons of fraternal Yemen. [passage omitted] The information minister concluded his statement by saying that the cabinet discussed the items on the agenda and issued the following resolution: Approving negotiations with the Russian Federation with a view to reaching an agreement on joint cooperation between the two countries in various fields.
