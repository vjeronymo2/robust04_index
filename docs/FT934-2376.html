931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Flats purchase for Frogmore Frogmore Estates, together with British Ensign Estates, has acquired a portfolio of 15 blocks of flats from Liverpool Victoria Friendly Society for Pounds 23.7m cash. The portfolio comprises 650 rented flats. Following completion of the purchase, Frogmore will own 11 of the blocks, consisting of 497 flats acquired for Pounds 18.9m and producing annual rental income of Pounds 1.3m.
