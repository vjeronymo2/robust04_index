Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Decree No. 32 of the Russian Federation Government dated Moscow, 24 January 1994 and signed by Russian Federation Government Chairman V. Chernomyrdin: "On Resource Provision for Military Courts and Organs of Military Justice"] [Text] For the purposes of ensuring the necessary operating conditions for military courts and organs of military justice, the Russian Federation Government decrees: 1. Until a legislative act in respect of military courts has been brought into force, the Russian Federation Ministry of Defense is to be entrusted with the funding, the supplying, and the material, technical, and other backup of military courts, the Military Collegium of the Russian Federation Supreme Court, and the Military Courts Administration of the Russian Federation Ministry of Justice. 2. The Russian Federation Ministry of Finance is to ensure the allocation of the necessary appropriations to the Russian Federation Ministry of Defense for the said purposes. 3. The Russian Federation Ministry of Justice, together with the Russian Federation Ministry of Defense and the Russian Federation Ministry of Finance, is to prepare within one month proposals for regularizing the pay of servicemen who are military court judges with due regard for Russian Federation legislation. [Signed] V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government [Dated] 24 January 1994
