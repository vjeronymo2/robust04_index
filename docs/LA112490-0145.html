November 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Mortgage Brokers: With more and more people crossing banks and S&Ls off their lists when they go shopping for mortgage loans, the state's mortgage brokers have come into their own.

The brokers generally don't provide funding; they put borrowers and willing lenders together. And mortgage brokers now are responsible for a majority of the residential loan originations in the state.

So now they've formed an association -- the California Assn. of Mortgage Brokers. One of the founding chapters is in Orange County.

The statewide group is headquartered in San Diego.
