BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Apr (MENA)--Sources close to the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations expect Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres to visit Cairo on 24 April to supervise the Palestinian-Israeli talks so as to reach agreement on the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho First agreement. The sources said that next week might mark the final round of these negotiations, as Peres and PLO Executive Committee member Mahmud 'Abbas, alias Abu-Mazin, will try to give impetus to the talks to conclude them. They added that the agreement could possibly be signed during the first days of May.
