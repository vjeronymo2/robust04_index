December 6, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

BOSTON SYMPHONY TO GO ON TOUR

The Boston Symphony Orchestra will tour the United States next spring and perform in Europe late in the summer, conductor Seiji Ozawa said Wednesday.

The U.S. tour, scheduled for April 22-May 3, will be the orchestra's first transcontinental tour since 1981.

The U.S. tour will include performances in Pittsburgh, Toronto, Chicago, New York, Cleveland, Los Angeles, San Francisco and Tempe, Ariz.

The European tour, running from Aug. 24 to Sept. 8, includes stops in Athens; Salzburg, Austria; Munich, Stuttgart and Frankfurt, Germany; Paris and London.
