Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 23 Mar (PL) -- The Attorney General's Office [PGR] today reported that Mario Aburto Martinez, alleged to be the gunman who murdered Revolutionary Institutional Party [PRI] presidential candidate Luis Donaldo Colosio, has said he "will not speak." In a news release, the PGR said Aburto Martinez was born in Michoacan State, where he lived until he was 15. He said he is a mechanic and has four brothers who live in the United States. The alleged murderer of Colosio lives in the northern city of Tijuana, the scene of the attack that cost Colosio his life. Aburto Martinez described himself as pacifist and the author of several books on the subject. The news release says the Harrison test applied on him was positive and that the attack was carried out with a .38 caliber pistol. According to the PGR, Aburto Martinez said: I will not speak, even if I am tortured.
