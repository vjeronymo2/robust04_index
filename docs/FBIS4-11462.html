BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 26 Apr (NA) -- The Senate this afternoon reelected Justicialist Party [PJ] Senator Eduardo Menem for the sixth consecutive time as provisional Senate president, and confirmed PJ Senator Oraldo Britos as Senate vice president. The Senate unanimously elected Rio Negro UCR [Radical Civic Union] Senator Faustino Mazzucco as Senate first vice president, and Neuquen Popular Movement (MPN) Senator Jorge Solana as Senate second vice president. The elections involving the four posts took barely 20 minutes during a session to which Presidential Secretary General Eduardo Bauza, former Interior Minister Julio Mera Figueroa, and Institutional Affairs Secretary Liliana Gurdulich de Correa had been specially invited.
