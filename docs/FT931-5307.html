930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Lloyds Smaller Cos earnings at 4.21p Lloyds Smaller Companies Investment Trust, a split level trust, reported a net asset value of 97.5p per capital share at January 31 1993, or 99.2p per package unit. After-tax revenue for the period from February 13 to January 31 came to Pounds 1.03m and earnings per share were 4.21p. A final dividend of 1.85p is recommended for a total of 3.55p. Total revenue was Pounds 1.58m.
