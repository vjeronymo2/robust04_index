Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Panamanian economic developments monitored through 17 January; source follows in parentheses after the item. Panama National Bank (BNP) President Luis Moreno has said his bank has shown a "satisfactory" recovery from January 1990 to December 1993. He has said BNP's liquidity rose from 9.7 percent in 1989 to 58.9 percent in 1993. (Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish 14 Jan 94 p 11a)
