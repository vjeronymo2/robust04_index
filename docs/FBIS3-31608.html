Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Greek Consul to Macedonia [Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Elias Eliadis today officially left Macedonia. The complete furnishings from the Skopje-based consulate were also moved to Greece. Border crossings on the Greek-Macedonian border will be closed until further notice, not only for Macedonian citizens and vehicles, but also for citizens from European countries, with the exception of the citizens of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. As Macedonian radio has reported, long lines of heavy vehicles from Italy, the Netherlands, Denmark, Germany, Macedonia, and other countries are piling up on both sides of the border. Rail traffic was also interrupted in recent days.
