BFN [Text] PRC Ambassador Xu Yicong has participated in the presentation of the 35th Anniversary of the Agrarian Reform medal to national vanguard workers of the National Association of Small Farmers. The ceremony was held at the Calixto Sardui Agricultural-Livestock Cooperative in Las Tunas Province. Ambassador Xu addressed the vanguard workers and stated: [Begin Xu recording] First I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the first secretary, the president, and the other comrades who have received vanguard status. I believe this is an example, not only for the farmers but also for the entire Cuban people. [end recording]
