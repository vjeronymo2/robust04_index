931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / Letters to the Editor: Historic right Sir, Women priests have a long and distinguished history in Britain except for a brief period of less than two millenia when the wishes of the Christian god held sway. Are they not, therefore, entitled to be addressed correctly as priestesses? Jane de Belvoir, 4 Feurise Lodge, Swindon, Wiltshire SN15 9YZ
