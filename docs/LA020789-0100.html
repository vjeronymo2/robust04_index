February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

LAS VEGAS FIRM TO DO ROAD REPAIRS NEAR HOOVER DAM

The U.S. Bureau of Reclamation has awarded a $4.7-million contract for road realignment at Hoover Dam to Frehner Construction Co. of Las Vegas, government officials said. The project will include straightening a portion of U.S. Highway 93 adjacent to Hoover Dam in Nevada.

When complete, the highway will be safer and provided more direct access to the future Hoover Dam visitor's center.
