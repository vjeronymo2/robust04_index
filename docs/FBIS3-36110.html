Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Nursultan Nazarbayev has issued a decree on setting up a Finance Ministry Treasury with the aim of effective control over revenues and expenditures in administering budget, raising the efficiency of financing state programs, and strengthening control over the proceeds, targeted and other kinds of use of state funds. It will consist of the main board, the ministry and its regional territorial missions, the towns Almaty and Leninsk, and districts in the cities and towns. The treasury houses are charged with the task of supervising cash performance and republican budget's cash controlling, accounting of cash movement, servicing of external and internal debt of the republic, and regulating financial relations between the republican budget and the state non-budgetary funds. The National Bank was authorized to supervise the cash performance of the republican budget in accordance with the existing law until the treasury bodies are fully formed. The treasury houses have the right to examine papers at the ministries, departments, enterprises, and organizations related to crediting, transferring, and using of sums of the republican budget. They also have the right to recover money of enterprises, works, and organizations, which were allocated from the republican budget and not being used by them properly. They can also impose sanctions on banks or other financial and credit enterprises for tardy crediting of funds entering the republican budget revenue, or funds that are credited from it to the recipients accounts at the relevant banks or financial and credit enterprises. The Cabinet of Ministers was charged with the task of issuing a statute on formation and activities of the treasury houses, as well as to submit a proposal to the Supreme Soviet on amending the existing legislature related to assigning the Treasury bodies of rights to cash execution of the republican budget and granting it with other rights that will be stipulated by the statute.
