910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Hillsdown places Wickes stake Hillsdown Holdings, the food group, yesterday placed its 19 per cent stake in Wickes, the DIY retailer and timber group. The sale of 50.5m shares raised Pounds 27.4m for Hillsdown, writes Maggie Urry . Wickes shares were unchanged yesterday at 60p as were Hillsdown's at 261p. The shares were sold at 54 1/4 p apiece to SG Warburg Securities which placed them with UK institutions.
