940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Purchases push Lynx to Pounds 460,000 Lynx Holdings, the acquisitive computer services and leisure products company, reported pre-tax profits more than quadrupled from Pounds 105,000 to Pounds 460,000 for the six months to March 31. Mr Roger Pinnington, chairman of this USM-quoted company, said that acquisitions contributed Pounds 312,000 to profits, including a strong performance from Financial Systems, acquired in December 1993. Turnover improved to Pounds 7.26m (Pounds 4.72m). Overall operating margins were 8.8 per cent, compared with 5.5 per cent last time. Earnings per share were 1.15p (0.83p) reflecting the increase in shares issued for the purchases. The interim dividend is raised to 0.36p (0.3p). Mr Pinnington said the balance sheet had been strengthened since the year end and net assets at March 31 stood at Pounds 2.1m, including net cash of Pounds 1.4m.
