BFN [Text] Tirana, April 11 (ATA) -- Great Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] police forces refused entry on April 10 in the Palace of Culture in Tetove where the congress of the branch of the Democratic Prosperity Party [PDP] in this city would be held, Albanian television reported on April 10. Despite police threats, participants of the congress, led by the DPP chairman, Arber Xhaferri and Deputy Chairman Mendu Thaci, held the congress in the residence of this party in Tetove. Referring to reports from Tetove, Albanian television said that participants in the congress declared that they will not allow any compromise detrimental to the just resolution of the question of the Albanians in the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia.
