930102 FT 02 JAN 93 / Finance and the Family: Discount offer on investments - At a glance Mercury Asset Management is offering a 1 per cent discount on investments in two of its unit trusts - Income and British Blue Chip - which it has selected as core Personal Equity Plans for the 1992-93 tax year. Those who invest the full Pounds 6,000 in one of the Peps will receive 600 free Air Miles. Initial charge on the Peps is 4 per cent and the annual charge is 1.5 per cent.
