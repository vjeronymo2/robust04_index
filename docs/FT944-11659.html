941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / International Company News: Peugeot sales up 14.2% Peugeot Citroen, the French carmaker, reported group turnover up 14.2 per cent to FFr121.7bn (Dollars 23.8bn) in the nine months to September 30, compared with the same period last year, reports Andrew Jack in Paris. The increase came in spite of a slower rise in demand for cars in western Europe during the third quarter - up 3.3 per cent compared with 5.7 per cent in the first quarter.
