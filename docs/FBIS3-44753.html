Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 7 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese armed services have exchanged more visits with their counterparts in other countries, according to an official of the Ministry of National Defense. The official said that last year the armed services received 120 visiting groups from more than 40 countries and regions, and sent some 60 groups abroad. During the past 15 years, the Chinese military have received tens of thousands of foreign military and political leaders. Some of the well-known Chinese military institutes, such as the Academy of Military Science of the Chinese PLA [People's Liberation Army] and the National Defense University, have established broad contacts with relevant foreign organizations for academic exchanges.
