930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: IoM Steam down 24% to Pounds 3.29m With the recession affecting summer holiday carryings, pre-tax profits of the Isle of Man Steam Packet Company fell 24 per cent, from Pounds 4.05m to Pounds 3.29m, for 1992 on turnover virtually unchanged at Pounds 24.5m, against Pounds 24.1m. Profits were struck after an exceptional debit of Pounds 158,000. Earnings per share were 19.5p (23.6p) while the dividend is maintained at 10p with a same-again final of 7p.
