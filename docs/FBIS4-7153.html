BFN [Text] The Ministry of People's Armed Forces arranged a banquet on the evening of 23 April on the 62d anniversary of the founding of the heroic Korean People's Army [KPA]. Invited to the banquet were the military attaches from the embassies of many countries in our country. Present at the banquet were generals and officers, including KPA Vice Marshal Kim Kwang-chin, Colonel General Pak Chae-kyong, and Lieutenant General An Pi-tok. Speeches were made at the banquet. The participants in the banquet toasted to the long life and good health of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, to the long life and good health of the dear leader and KPA Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il, and to the long life and good health of the heads of state of the friendly countries represented by the military attaches. In addition, the KPA delegation of the Military Armistice Commission gave a reception on the evening of 23 April.
