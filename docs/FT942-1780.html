940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Supply fears keep oil price rally going - Market report Continued concerns about supply tightness in the US Midwest kept the OIL price rally going yesterday, defying forecasts of a 'correction' following the Dollars 2 - a - barrel gain over the past week. COFFEE futures showed resilience, meanwhile, with Monday's shake-out being recovered with interest. At the London Metal Exchange COPPER prices tended slightly lower as the market tried to consolidate recent gains and build a base for a further advance. Compiled from Reuter
