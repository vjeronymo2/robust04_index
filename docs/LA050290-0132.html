May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

TACKLE TODD DROPPED FROM ALL-STAR ROSTER

Dan Todd, a 6-foot-4, 260-pound offensive tackle from Foothill High School, has asked to be dropped from the South team that will compete in the 31st Orange County All-Star football game July 6 at Orange Coast College's LeBard Stadium.

South Coach Dave White of Edison has replaced Todd with Capistrano Valley guard Chris Tarnowski, a 6-2, 204-pound senior.
