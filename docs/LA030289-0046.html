March 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

The eighth annual home video ViRas Awards, a creation of Video Review magazine, will be handed out this evening at the Ma Maison Sofitel in West Hollywood. The 23 awards, covering movies, documentaries, children's video, music videos and health and fitness, are voted on by critics who review videocassettes for the magazine. ALEENE MacMINN
