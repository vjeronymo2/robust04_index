Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] At the invitation of the Government of the Republic of Kazahkstan, Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam paid an official visit from 30 January to 1 February. During his vivit, the foreign minister was warmly received by Vice President Asanbayev and Prime Minister Tereshchenko. He also met with Foreign Minister Suleymenov and held working sessions with officials of the Ministries of Economy and Foreign Economic Relations. On behalf of the two governments, the foreign ministers signed an economic and trade cooperation agreement. The two sides also agreed to accelerate agreements on investment encouragement and protection, double taxation avoidance, and other agreements in order to promote development of long-term and fruitful relations. Vietnamese Ambassador Nguyen Van Dac also participated in activities of the visiting delegation.
