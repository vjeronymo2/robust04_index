BFN [Text] A large arms and ammunition depot of Golboddin's militia in Riskhor area caught fire last night. A large quantity of arms and ammunition, including heavy weapons and various kinds of rockets, were stored in the depot to be used to kill our innocent people. Reports say the fire and explosions started in the depot at 2200 [1730 GMT] and continued until 0300 in the night. As a result of the fire, which was accompanied by terrifying explosions, a large quantity of Golboddin-Dostam's arms and ammunition and various kinds of stored rockets have been destroyed. No report has been received on the cause of the fire or loss of life.
