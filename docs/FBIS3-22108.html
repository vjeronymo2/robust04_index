Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today here signed was the agreement on cooperation between Mongolia and the Russian Federation in the sphere of nature and environment protection. In the agreement reflected is the number of concrete issues of cooperation, including an issue about foundation of an international park for rare animals by efforts of the two neighboring countries. The agreement was signed by the Minister of Nature and Environment Protection of Mongolia G. Batjargal, the Minister of Protection of Environment and Natural Resources of the Russian Federation V.I. Danilov-Danilyan. The member of the Russian Government, member of the Duma, minister of the Russian Federation was received today by the Prime Minister of Mongolia P. Jasray, who expressed satisfaction apropos of the meeting with the first representative of the government of the Russian Federation after it was recently formed. The sides exchanged information about situation in their countries, measures being take by the governments of the two countries the deputy-premier of Mongolia, chairman of the Mongolian part of the Inter-Government Mongolian-Russian Commission C. Purebdorj, and other officials participated in the meeting.
