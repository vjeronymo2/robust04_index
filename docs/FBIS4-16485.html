BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz April 15 TASS--The two-day second round of North Ossetian-Ingush summit negotiations ended in Nalchik today after discussing a draft agreement on principles, measures and time to implement the Russian president's decree "On Procedure of Return of Refugees and Forced Migrants to Places of Their Permanent Residence in Northern Ossetia and Ingushetia". The sides have discussed the document's preamble and the first article. The Ossetian side suggested to have the following wording of the first article "The parties to the agreement confirm the status of the Prigorodny region as a territory of the North Ossetian Republic in accordance with a decree of the Russian president of December 13, 1993, and voice their refusal from any territorial claims on each other and a third party". In the opinion of the Ingush side, the Prigorodny region belongs to Ingushetia as ethnic Ingushes populated the area before deportation. A break in negotiations has been announced. The date of the next meeting in Nalchik will be announced later. The North Ossetian and Ingush working groups and experts in Vladikavkaz and Nazran will continue to elaborate articles of the draft agreement.
