BFN [Text] The time is approaching for the complete withdrawal of Russian forces from Latvia. A decision was made at a government session which has just taken place, on permission for Russian military personnel and members of their families to stay until 31 August. We asked Ilgonis Upmalis, head of the government commission for monitoring the withdrawal of the Russian Army, to comment. [Begin Upmalis recording] So far, permits of stay have been valid until 30 June. As the stipulated period is expiring and the deadline for the withdrawal set in interstate agreements is 31 August, amendments were made today to the previous decision, and it was stipulated that permits to stay have been extended until 31 August 1994. The procedure is that, Army units have to submit lists of military personnel, as well as lists of members of families, who have these permits to stay. [end recording]
