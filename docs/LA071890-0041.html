July 18, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: COUNCIL OKS $185,000 TO CONTINUE MEDFLY SUIT

The Los Angeles City Council on Tuesday unanimously approved spending another $185,000 to continue its lawsuit against the state to stop malathion spraying over urban areas to eradicate the Mediterranean fruit fly. The city already has spent $40,000 in various legal challenges that so far have been unsuccessful.
