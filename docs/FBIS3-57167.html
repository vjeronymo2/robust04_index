Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BUSINESS-TASS] [Text] Moscow January 21 TASS -- The output of Russian defence plants dropped by 19.6 per cent in 1993 as compared to 1992, including the production of civil goods by 14.1 per cent. The production of consumer goods by enterprises of the State Committee for Defence Industries was cut by almost five times, sources at the committee told BUSINESS-TASS here today. The rates of production recession gradually increased from September 1993; the December drop amounted to 28.2 per cent as against the annual drop of 19.6 per cent. According to committee specialists, the overall defence production recession amounted to 42.3 per cent from December 1991 through December 1993 (a period of reforms), which may result in the soonest halt of some branches.
