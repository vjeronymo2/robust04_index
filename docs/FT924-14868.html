921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / International Capital Markets: US card group in Hungary deal AMERICAN Express said that Hungarians with the necessary hard currency would now be able to acquire its payments cards, the first such move in the former Soviet bloc, Reuter reports from Vienna. Hungarians applying for the card will need an annual salary of at least Dollars 25,000 and a bank deposit or guarantee of Dollars 5,000 in hard currency. American Express will work with two Hungarian banks, General Banking and Trust and Commercial and Credit Bank. The group has 16 offices spread across eastern Europe.
