BFN [Text] On 9 June, the CPC delegation headed by Politburo member Wu Bangguo, secretary of the CPC in Shanghai, visited the Hermanos Ameijeiras Clinical Surgical Hospital in Havana. Public Health Minister Dr. Julio Teja and hospital director Dr. Raul Cabrera briefed the visitors on all aspects of the Cuban medical system. Previously, the CPC delegation had seen a video covering the work done at the hospital. The hospital has 52 consulting rooms for diverse medical specialities to care for the population, mainly from Centro Habana and Habana Vieja. Later on, the CPC delegation visited various areas of the hospital and expressed interest in other aspects of Cuban medicine.
