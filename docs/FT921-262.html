920330 FT 30 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Hard going - Ballot Box How long would someone have to work at Labour's proposed minimum hourly rate of Pounds 3.40 to earn an annual salary of Pounds 21,060, the present ceiling on National Insurance contributions? A reader who is a retired accountant was struck by the disparity between these two hotly debated figures and worked out the answer - almost 17 hours a day, 365 days of the year.
