Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report: "Hu Ping Inspects Shenzhen"] [Text] According to news from Shenzhen, Hu Ping, director of the State Council's Special Economic Zones [SEZ] Office, inspected Shenzhen from 3 to 6 February. He pointed out: Shenzhen has scored achievements through solid work and it should still rely on solid work to have a more leading edge. Travelling with a minimum of pomp, Hu Ping and his five-member party, inspected the city's goods and material corporation, urban construction group, petrochemical group, Yantian port, Shatoujiao, and Futian Bonded Area; heard work reports by Shenzhen Mayor Li Youwei and Vice Mayor Wang Zhongfu; and inquired in detail about the measures adopted by Shenzhen in opening wider to the outside world and deepening reform. Hu Ping and his party left Shenzhen today.
