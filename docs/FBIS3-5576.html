Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN ["Message from His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk Varaman of the Kingdom of Cambodia in reply to the 5 March telegram of His Excellency Khieu Samphan, head of the party of Democratic Kampuchea"; dated 8 March -- read by announcer] [Text] His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of the Kingdom of Cambodia, graciously sent a message on 8 March in reply to the telegram dated 5 March from His Excellency [H.E.] Khieu Samphan, head of the party of Democratic Kampuchea [PDK]. The text of the royal message is as follows: To H.E. Khieu Samphan, head of the PDK, through the Royal Office in Phnom Penh. Esteemed and beloved Excellency: I have already received the statement you made on behalf of the PDK concerning the current situation in our Cambodian motherland and the attitude of your excellency's party on this matter. With many thanks to you and your party for the noble and lofty sentiment and confidence toward myself, I would like you and your party to accept in return my best wishes with the fervent hope that peace will be restored unfailingly on the Cambodian land and national reconciliation and concord among all our compatriots of all tendencies and political beliefs will finally be achieved according to our wishes. Otherwise, it will certainly be difficult for our motherland to avoid a danger that could affect even its survival. Please, your excellency and the PDK, accept the profoundest and warmest regards from myself and the royal consort. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk
