Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], watched the overall song and dance performance by the artists of the KPA entertainment group. Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the DPRK National Defense Commission and supreme commander of the KPA, watched the overall song and dance performance by the artists of the KPA entertainment group on 5 March. Comrade Kye Ung-tae, member of the Political Bureau of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK] Central Committee and secretary of the party Central Committee; Comrades Kim Chung-nin, Kim Ki-nam, Kim Kuk-tae, and Kim Yong-sun, secretaries of the party Central Committee; and responsible functionaries of the party Central Committee and the field of press and publication were on hand. The performance began with the orchestral music and chorus of "The Sound of Horses Hooves at Mt. Paektu." Various performances were presented such as the dance, "The Palanquin Carriage Is Running," and "The Final Showdown;" a mixed vocal duet, "Love of Officers and Men Which Is Blossoming Along the Marching Road;" a men's chorus, "We Will Deeply Bear in Our Hearts the Red Certification of the Party;" and a solo and kayagum [a Korean instrument with 12 strings] ensemble, "It Is a Paradise of Hope at Which the Whole World Is Looking." In May 1992, the creators and artists of the KPA entertainment group highly upheld the militant task presented by the supreme comrade commander and brought about a new turning point in the artistic creation work. Therefore, they were able to present on stage magnificent artistic works that greatly contribute to indoctrinating the people and soldiers of the People's Army in a revolutionary way. After watching the performance, Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il expressed great satisfaction over the fact that the creators and artists of the entertainment group were able to create and perform revolutionary and militant art works which are of high ideological and artistic spirit. He highly assessed their results, and gave them a thank-you message of the party Central Military Commission and the KPA supreme commander. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said that all performances that were put on stage are very good works that are in accordance with the character of the military and in conformity with the demand of the era. He pointed out that the performance by the entertainment group is giving great faith and courage to our people and soldiers of the People's Army due to its militant spirit and strong appeal. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said all art works including songs and dances well reflected the courage, wisdom, and revolutionary spirit of our People's Army, which is the party's army, a revolutionary army armored to uphold the party and the leader, defend socialism, and complete the socialist cause. He showed his satisfaction that the People's Army entertainment group has created many revolutionary and combat-oriented art works and successfully fulfilled its fundamental duty of indoctrinating the soldiers of the People's Army to devotedly serve for the party and the leader, and for the fatherland and the people with the self-consciousness of defenders of the party and revolution. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said the KPA entertainment group, a combat art unit of our revolutionary armed forces, should continue its effort in artistic activities for the revolutionary and combat indoctrination for the soldiers and concretely set forth creative directions to serve as the guidelines for artistic creative works. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said it is important that the People's Army entertainment group well reflects in the art works the great political and ideological aspects and invincible might of the soldiers of the People's Army, who are strongly guaranteeing our party's revolutionary cause with armed forces firmly united around the party and the leader. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said to vividly show the soldiers' life, as did in the show, is a very important question in creative works and pointed out that the entertainment group should well describe the lofty spiritual world of the soldiers of the People's Army and how they struggle, including their loyalty toward the party and the leader, their spirit of devotion to serve the fatherland and the people, indomitable revolutionary spirit, and revolutionary comradely love. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il pointed out that the People's Army entertainment group should keep its identity of being socialist, revolutionary, winning, grand, and magnificent not only in the contents but also in the format in conformity with its mission and duty as an art group which indoctrinates and serves the soldiers of the People's Army. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il pointed out that as all art works created by the People's Army entertainment group should be perfect ideologically and artistically as they contribute to the indoctrination of the invincible revolutionary rank, a-match-for-a-hundred revolutionary fighters, and added that new orchestral music should be created in chuche-oriented and national format in conformity with the characteristics of the Army units. The Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il said he firmly believes that the creators and artists of the People's Army entertainment group will actively contribute to our party's revolutionary cause by vigorously carrying out creative artistic activities that contribute to the indoctrination of the soldiers.
