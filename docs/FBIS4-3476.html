BFN [Text] Shenzhen, April 21 (CNS) -- The Shenzhen municipal public security authorities have ordered 37 people of U.S., Canadian, Singaporean, Sri Lankan, Nigerian and Pakistani nationalities to leave China within three days. The 37 persons are so-called "three-no" people, namely without legal certificate, a proper job and lawful residence, being found in lent accommodation and an "underground guest house" in the Luohu District of the city yesterday when the police conducted a sudden raid in the area. These 37 people had entered the city as tourists and had not returned to their own countries when their tourist documents expired. They were instead illegally living, working, doing business and taking part in activities in the city, posing troubles to social order and the management of foreign affairs there. The municipal public security department imposed a fine on them according to foreign affairs management regulations and ordered them to leave the country within three days.
