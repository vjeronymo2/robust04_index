January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

REEL LIFE: Marc Christian, above, was not among those tuning to ABC last night for "Rock Hudson," the two-hour TV movie featuring an account of Christian's ill-fated relationship with the late screen idol. . . . The Villa Park High graduate called the movie "truncated" and "a rush job" but said, "They got the gist of it pretty accurate." . . . Christian turned down a $30,000 offer to serve as a consultant on the film because "I didn't want to cash in on the TV thing." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
