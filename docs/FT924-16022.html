921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Wool The Market in Australia is keeping firm in the face of continuing currency volatility. The US and Australian dollars have been taking a downward course against Europe and Japan. In every wool-using country currency has become far more important in affecting prices from day to day than the course of the wool market itself. International relationships among companies and with suppliers and customers confuse the issue, and trading increasingly cautious. The Australian Wool Corporation's market indicator on September 30 was 533c a kg., just one cent higher than a week before.
