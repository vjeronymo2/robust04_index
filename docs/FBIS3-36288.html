Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow February 3 TASS -- A terrorist seized on Thursday two kids, their teacher and the driver of a van on Thursday [as received] in the Ukrainian city of Sukhodolsk in Lugansk region and is demanding 500,000 US dollars ransom, a pistol and a plane. The kidnapper, who was identified as Nikolay Skvortsov a resident of Sukhodolsk, is holding a grenade with a pulled-out pin in his hand [as received] and threatens to explode it if his demands are not met, TASS was told at the Russian Ministry for Emergency Situations. Currently Skvortsov with hostages is going on the van to the Russian city of Rostov-on-Don where a plane is being prepared for him and anti-terrorist groups are ready. The pistol which he received in Ukraine was out of order and he threw it away.
