BFN [Text] Mr. 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Ali al-Jifri, the vice chairman of the Presidential Council, accompanied by Mr. Sulayman Nasir Mas'ud, the member of the Presidential Council, today fulfilled the Friday prayers in al-Aydrus Mosque in crater. After the prayers, the vice chairman delivered a speech to the worshippers. He said: We had tried before entering the authority to rectify the matter, and the Arabs and Muslims had also tried their best, but 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih refused. We had only to fight that group in order to defend our homeland. The vice chairman indicated to the strange decision made by Dr. 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Daylami last Monday [6 June] over Sanaa radio, in which he allowed the killing of all of us with our women and children -- not only the rulers. He said that his decision included the weak people, and we have tape recordings to prove the issue of this decision over Sanaa radio. Mr. al-Jifri told the audiences that we have nothing remaining except to fight on all battlefronts and here in the capital, Aden, in order to refute the false rumors. He said: You must know that our sons are fighting on all battlefronts, and we must know about their fate.
