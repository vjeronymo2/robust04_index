December 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Teen newscast -- A photo caption in the Nov. 29 edition of The Times incorrectly stated the number of schools that receive a teen-oriented television newscast called Channel One. Two public schools and more than 60 parochial schools in California subscribe to the newscast, according to officials of Whittle Communications, the Knoxville, Tenn., company that produces the satellite newscast.
