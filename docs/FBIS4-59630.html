BFN [Text] Tunis--Informed Palestinian sources say that the Fatah Revolutionary Council led by Yasir 'Arafat yesterday heard a report by Faruq Qaddumi, the Fatah secretary general, in which he attacked the recent Cairo agreement between Israel and the PLO. The sources say that Qaddumi's attack was particularly directed at 'Arafat's agreement to concede the right of representation for the national authority abroad. The sources said that a number of the Fatah Movement's Central Committee members are unwilling to leave for Jericho and assume their duties within the so-called national authority. The source said that Faruq Qaddumi, Hani al-Hasan, Khalid al-Hasan, Muhammad Jihad, and Muhammad Ghunaym have refused to go to Jericho, especially since there are differences on coordinating work between the Fatah commands at home and abroad.
