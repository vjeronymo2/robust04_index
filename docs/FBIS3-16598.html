Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, March 17 (TURAN) -- The session of Milli Majlis which [was] scheduled for March 17, at 12.00 a.m. has been postponed, reports national TV. The agenda included discussion of the results of the Armenian-Azerbaijani talks on March 12, and the intent by the Popular Front to carry out a coup d'etat on March 5. Yesterday, the deputies of the PFA [People's Front of Azerbaijan] and the leader of the National Independence Party, Etibar Mamedov, said that they will raise the issue of the harassment and persecution of the independent press and the concluding truce with Armenia which is disadvantageous for Azerbaijan.
