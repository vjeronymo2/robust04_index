November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AN ACTIVE ALLIANCE OF CHEFS

The Nov. 5 cover story presents a distorted impression of the Women's Culinary Alliance.

Support organizations such as the alliance, founded by chef Elka Gilmore and several other high-profile chefs, provide opportunities for members to learn from each other's trials and successes. It is unfortunate that in many such groups those who have become the most successful no longer take the time to participate.

Happily, the Women's Culinary Alliance counts among its members many hard-working chefs -- some high-profile and some working for their stars and toques -- as well as a wide variety of food industry professionals.

DEBBIE SLUTSKY

President

The Women's Culinary Alliance

Los Angeles
