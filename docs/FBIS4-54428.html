BFN [Text] Warsaw, April 29 -- President Lech Walesa appointed Professor Grzegorz Kolodko to the post of deputy prime minister and finance minister on Friday. During the ceremony of handing a nomination act to Kolodko, President Walesa expressed his satisfaction at the fact that the long-vacant post has been filled. Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak, who attended the ceremony, stressed that the appointment proved that the consistent search for a compromise helped overcome all the difficulies. Kolodko assured that he would do all he can to serve Poland well.
