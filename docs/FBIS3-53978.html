Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Eighty reform rabbis from the United States concluded a visit to Jordan this evening. Rabbi (Amiel Hersh), head of the delegation, said that there were no meeting between the rabbis and Jordan's Prince Hasan, the regent. He indicated that a meeting had been scheduled. A spokesman for Prince Hasan said that the Jordanian Embassy in Washington informed the rabbis delegation of the meeting without obtaining prior clarification from the Jordanian royal palace. Meanwhile, the Muslim Brotherhood in Jordan issued a statement today denouncing the delegation's visit to Jordan.
