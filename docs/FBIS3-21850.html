Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] About 400 antidrug officials are gathering in Bangkok for two-day workshop aimed at combating the drug problems in the country. Deputy Permanent Secretary for Interior Chaloem Phromloet said the workshop will take place from March 7th until the 8th at the Asia Hotel, with Deputy Interior Minister Mr. Den Tomina presiding over the opening of the meeting. The participants are from Bangkok and 23 provinces where the drug problems have become more serious than others. The Ministry of Interior has received a budget of 30.3 million baht to its project on narcotics prevention and suppression in 23 provinces. The four-year project, which started last year, comprises six [as heard] work plans; namely, narcotics crop control, narcotics suppression, narcotics prevention, treatment of drug addicts and solving of drug abuse problems, and personnel administration and development. The workshop of the antidrug officials is expected to help improve the operations of the work plans in a systematic manner.
