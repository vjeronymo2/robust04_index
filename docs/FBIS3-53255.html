Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transitional National Assembly [TNA] today held a session under the chairmanship of Mr. Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah, speaker of the assembly. The membership affairs committee issued a decision revoking the membership of the one called Aldo Ajo Deng [deputy speaker of the TNA] from the assembly because of his recent conduct. The decision was put before the assembly. It was debated in all its dimensions. After the debate, the assembly unanimously approved the decision to revoke the membership of the one called Aldo Ajo Deng. The assembly will submit a recommendation in this respect to the president of the Republic. On the other hand, Mr. Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah, speaker of the Transitional National Assembly, affirmed that the motives that led to the one called Aldo Ajo Deng joining the opposition ranks were not religious motives as he alleged, nor were they related to discrimination between the north and the south, which Aldo Ajo Deng alleged.
