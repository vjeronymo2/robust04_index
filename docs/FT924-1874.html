921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Algiers curfew worsens violence The curfew imposed on Algiers and surrounding provinces 12 days ago has led to an escalation of violence, with five members of the security forces and six Islamic militants losing their lives since the beginning of this week, writes Francis Ghiles. The militants were killed early yesterday in a house where they had found refuge near the Kouba mosque on the eastern side of Algiers.
