940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Burton Burton Group, the clothing retailer and property developer, has completed the sale of Terra Firma, Redhill, Surrey, to CIN Properties for Pounds 13.5m cash. CIN is the property investment manager of British Coal pension funds. The property was let earlier this year for Pounds 919,000 a year, equating to a yield of 6.5 per cent.
