BFN [Text] This morning, the brother President leader Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received the U.S. ambassador in Tunis. During the meeting, the brother President leader Abu-'Ammar charged his excellency the U.S. ambassador with conveying a message to the U.S. Administration in connection with the Palestinian stance toward the Israeli replies to the Palestinian demands on providing international protection to our Palestinian people and implementing Security Council Resolution 904. The recent developments in connection with the Palestinian issue and the events currently taking place in the occupied Palestinian territories were also discussed during the meeting.
