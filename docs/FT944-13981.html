941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / International Company News: Host Marriott spends Dollars 149m on seven hotels Host Marriott has agreed to buy seven hotels in the US from the Equitable Life Assurance Society of the US, for a total of Dollars 149m, Reuter reports. With these hotels, Host Marriott said it will have acquired 16 properties in 1994 for about Dollars 472m. CDL Hotels, a subsidiary of Singapore's listed City Developments group, is paying USDollars 96m for the 639-room Hotel Macklowe in Times Square, New York, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. The purchase is the latest in a series of hotel acquisitions by CDL, a company which is ultimately controlled by the Singapore branch of the Hong Leong conglomerate.
