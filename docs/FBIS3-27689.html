Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Washington, February 25 (XINHUA) -- U.S. Assistant Secretary of State John Shattuck left here today for a week-long visit to China. The purpose of Shattuck's trip is to pave the way for a visit to Beijing in mid-March by Secretary of State Warren Christopher, said State Department spokesman Mike McCurry during a regular news briefing. McCurry said that Shattuck will also visit Shanghai.
