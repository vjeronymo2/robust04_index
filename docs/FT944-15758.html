941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Observer: Sold short No doubt the Italian stock exchange authorities, always eager to enhance the bourse's standing within the international financial community, thought long and hard before christening a new futures contract - based on the index of leading Italian shares - the FIB 30. If the product takes off, trading on the Italian futures market can then be termed fibbing, with specialist Italian dealers affectionately known as fibbers.
