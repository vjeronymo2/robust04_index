920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Daejan rises 6% Daejan Holdings, the property group, raised pre-tax profits by 6 per cent to Pounds 8.77m in the half year to September 30, but warned that because of the slackness in residential sales, the full year figure may be lower than the previous Pounds 16.4m. Gross rental income edged ahead 4 per cent to Pounds 6.94m, while earnings per share came to 34.96p (32.24p). The interim dividend is raised to 12p (8p) to reduce disparity.
