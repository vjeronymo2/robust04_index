940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / UK Company News: St James Beach Pounds 1m placing St James Beach Hotels, the operator of hotels in Barbados which came to the market in April, yesterday announced a share placing to raise Pounds 1.1m. Some 900,000 shares have been placed with various institutions at 120p apiece; the shares were unchanged at 122p on Friday. As foreshadowed in the annual results, proceeds will be used for the purchase and redevelopment of a 14,000 sq ft office and warehouse building to provide a new headquarters and a central storage and distribution facility.
