Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Experts from the Rio Grande do Sul, RS, State Foundation for Environmental Protection seized 32 containers with clandestine radioactive waste. The cargo was transferred to warehouse A5 in Rio Grande Port, which was designed to store dangerous material. On 27 December, the experts will test the contents with radiation-measuring equipment and investigate the rest of the cargo being carried by the ship Belatrix, where the containers were found.
