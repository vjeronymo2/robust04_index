Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A reliable security source has denied a report carried by some news media on an attempt to assassinate the president of the republic a month ago. This report is completely untrue, the source said, and called on all mass media not to publish such biased reports until they are verified by the competent security agencies. He also cautioned the citizens against believing such rumors, which seek to cast doubts on and shake confidence in the march to security and stability, which has gained strength over the past three years.
