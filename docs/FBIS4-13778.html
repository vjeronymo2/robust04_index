BFN [Text] Iraqi National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mhadi Salih, and his delegation called on Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali in Islamabad yesterday. The Iraqi speaker briefed the foreign minister on the difficulties being faced by the Iraqi people due to a serious scarcity of essential commodities. The foreign minister said we are fully aware of the current problems of the Iraqi brethren and Pakistan will do everything possible within the jurisdiction of the international law to mitigate their hardship.
