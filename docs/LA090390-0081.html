September 3, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Chrysler Announces Incentives on '91 Models: Chrysler Corp. said it would continue incentive programs on a wide variety of its cars and light trucks. Cash rebates, ranging from $500 to $1,000, are available on all domestically produced vehicles except minivans, convertibles and standard-size vans and wagons -- plus some import models. As an alternative to rebates, a consumer may choose low-interest financing plans with interest rates keyed to the term of the loan.
