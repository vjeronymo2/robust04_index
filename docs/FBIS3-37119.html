Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] This is the text of the edict of the president of the Kyrgyz republic: On the Membership of the Security Council Attached to the President of the Kyrgyz Republic. In conformity with the edict of the president of the Kyrgyz Republic of 2 February 1994 on the Security Council under the president of the Kyrgyz Republic, I decree: 1. To confirm the membership of the Security Council attached to the president of the Kyrgyz Republic as follows: Prime Minister Dzhumagulov; Rustembekov, state secretary under the president and head of the president's administration; Fisher, deputy prime minister and chairman of the State Commission of the Kyrgyz Republic on Emergency Situations and Civil Defense; Internal Affairs Minister Sutalinov; Defense Minister Subanov; Bakayev, chairman of the State Committee of the Kyrgyz Republic for National Security; and National Guard Commander Chotbayev. 2. To hold as no longer valid the decree of the president of the Kyrgyz Republic of 5 August 1993 on the formation of the headquarters under the commander in chief of the Armed Forces of the Kyrgyz Republic. [Signed] Askar Akayev, president of the Kyrgyz Republic
