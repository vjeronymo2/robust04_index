Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, 27 January (TANJUG) -- Hungarian Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky arrived in Belgrade this afternoon on board a special flight from Budapest. He is paying a two-day official visit to the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia at the invitation of Yugoslav Foreign Minister Vladislav Jovanovic who welcomed him at Surcin airport. Immediately upon Jeszenszky's arrival, the two foreign ministers began official talks on important issues of mutual interest in the Yugoslav Ministry of Foreign Affairs. [passage omitted] The Hungarian foreign minister is expected to be received by Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic today. Talks with FRY President Zoran Lilic, Yugoslav Prime Minister Radoje Kontic, and Vladislav Jovanovic are scheduled to take place tomorrow.
