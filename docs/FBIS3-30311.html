Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan 29 -- A visit to Poland by leader of Russian ultra-nationalist Vladimir Zhirinovskiy sheduled for Sunday was cancelled due to his illness, Bogdan Poreba of the National Front "Samoobrona" (Self-Defence), the organisation that invited Zhirinovskiy to Poland, said on Saturday evening. According to Poreba, Zhirinovskiy invited leader of the "Samoobrona" Front Janusz Bryczkowski to pay a visit to Moscow on Feb. 2 so that they could fix a new date of Zhirinovskiy's visit to Poland.
