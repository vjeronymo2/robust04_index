BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 19 May (EFE) -- Dominican President Joaquin Balaguer has 29,368 more votes than Jose Francisco Pena Gomez, a 1 percent advantage, according to the vote count in Monday's elections, the Central Electoral Board (JCE) announced today. JCE Bulletin No. 21 reported that Balaguer and his allies received 1,236,075 votes while the coalition backing Pena Gomez has received 1,205,707, a difference of 29,368 votes [as received]. These figures correspond to 97.47 percent of the electoral boards. Only 241 electoral boards remain to be tallied. According to the JCE bulletin, Balaguer has garnered 42.39 percent of the valid votes while his opponent, Pena Gomez, has 41.39 percent.
