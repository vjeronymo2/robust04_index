BFN [Excerpts] Kuwait, 18 Jun (KUNA) -- Admiral Jacques Lanxade, chief of the general staff of the French Armed Forces, and an accompanying delegation met here today with Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, amir of the State of Kuwait. [passage omitted] Lanxade had arrived in Kuwait yesterday on an official visit that is to last a few days. [Kuwait KUNA in Arabic at 1003 GMT on 18 June reports that Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah, heir apparent and Kuwaiti prime minister, also received Admiral Lanxade and the accompanying delegation.]
