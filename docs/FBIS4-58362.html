BFN [Text] We have just received a report saying that a military source in Sanaa has stated that northern Yemeni forces deployed in the southern parts of the country have downed a southern military aircraft. The source told REUTER that the MiG-21 was downed by ground fire last night when a number of southern aircraft tried to fly provocative sorties over the northerners' 2d Armored Brigade in Lahij in south Yemen.
