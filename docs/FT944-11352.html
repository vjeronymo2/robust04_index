941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Business Travel: Give and take Even the bedside Gideon Bible is not safe from the clutches of business executives staying in hotels, says a survey by Britain's Reed Travel Group. Coat-hangers, cutlery and TV remote-controls are other items they take, while nightclothes, underwear and ties top the list of things they leave. Younger business travellers are the most light-fingered. British executives visit the US on business more than their European counterparts. One in five business travellers takes exercise while away. The survey covered 1,270 business travellers.
