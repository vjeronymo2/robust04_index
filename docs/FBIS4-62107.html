BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 11 May--Georgian Deputy Prime Minister Zurab Kervalishvili received Fereidn Haqbin, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Islamic Republic of Iran to Georgia. Members of the commission for the development of the Europe-Caucasus-Asia highway corridor; Valeriy Gegidze, head of the sea and trade port; and others were present at the meeting. The meeting discussed the specific results of the implementation of the 17 agreements signed by Iran and Georgia, as well as Iran's role in reconstructing the Port of Poti.
