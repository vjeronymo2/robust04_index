920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / World Trade News: Polish airline sells aircraft to Ukraine LOT, Poland's state-owned airline, has sold its seven long-range IL-62s along with 10 short-range AN-24 aircraft to Avialinie, the Ukraine's newly-established airline, Christopher Bobinski reports from Warsaw. Delivery is to be completed by February. LOT has been seeking a buyer for its IL-62s since last autumn. The Polish airline has two Boeing 767s for its transatlantic route, another is soon to be delivered, and the purchase of two or three more is being considered. LOT yesterday refused to confirm that the Ukraine had agreed to pay Dollars 15m (Pounds 8.2m) for the aircraft. Last year, it signed a Dollars 300m contract to buy nine Boeing 737s for use on its European routes. The mid-1990s should see it with 20 aircraft, all made in the west.
