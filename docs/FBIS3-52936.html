Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Muscat, 7 Jan (WAKH)--Malcolm Rifkind, British secretary of state for defense, has asserted that his country attaches great importance to its relations with the Gulf states. The British secretary of state, currently on a visit to Muscat, added that his visit to Oman aims to bolster Britain's relations with Oman and other regional states. In a statement to the Omani News Agency, he said his talks with the Omani officials focused on security in the Gulf region and issues of common interest.
