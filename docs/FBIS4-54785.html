BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 5 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak, on the motion of the interior minister, appointed Zbigniew Sobotka undersecretary of state at the Interior Ministry as of May 4, the government press bureau has announced. Zbigniew Sobotka, 42, is a metallurgist. He was a Sejm deputy of the first and second Sejm tenure and a member of the Sejm administration and internal affairs committee and the political advisory committee attached to the interior minister. In the years 1986-1990 he was a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party (PZPR) and between 1989 and 1990 he was a deputy member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of PZPR. He participated in the round table talks in 1989. Zbigniew Sobotka is a member of the main council of the Social Democracy of the Republic of Poland (SDRP). He is married, with three children.
