940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / International Company News: New chief for Alusuisse-Lonza Mrs Dominique Damon, head of the packaging division of Alusuisse-Lonza, has been appointed chief operating officer of the Swiss aluminium, chemicals and packaging group and the designated successor to Mr Theodor Tschopp as chief executive. Mrs Damon, who is 47, joined Alusuisse in 1988 and has built up the Swiss group's packaging division to be one of the leading participants in that sector.
