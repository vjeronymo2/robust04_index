September 15, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE;

IRVINE PARTNERSHIP PURCHASES 7.5-ACRE ARBOR VILLAGE CENTER FOR $13.6 MILLION

An Irvine partnership bought Arbor Village Center, a complex of offices, stores and restaurants in Irvine, for $13.6 million.

Selling to Globe Partnership was Rancon Realty Fund of Temecula. The 7.5-acre complex is a block west of the Santa Ana Freeway at Walnut Avenue.

The 9-year-old complex is almost fully leased. It has eight buildings with 77,000 square feet of space on 7.5 acres.

Burke Commercial Real Estate's Irvine office represented Globe.
