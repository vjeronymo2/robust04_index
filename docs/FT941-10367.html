940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Latvia seeks UK backing Mr Anatolijs Gorbunovs, speaker of the Latvian parliament, said the future of the powerful, Soviet-era early warning radar station at Skrunda and the prospects for withdrawal of the estimated 13,000 Russian troops still in Latvia were high on his agenda of talks with the British government this week, writes Anthony Robinson. Mr Gorbunovs said the Baltic state is seeking support for its plan to put the former Soviet base under international supervision until it can be dismantled.
