Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 13, IRNA -- Afghan Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar appealed to Iran to take an active role for restoration of peace in Afghanistan. In a telephone conversation with Iran's Minister of Foreign Affairs, 'Ali Akbar Velayati, he appreciated Iran's former peace efforts in his country. Hekmatyar congratulated Velayati on the anniversary of the triumph of the Islamic Revolution and on the beginning of the holy month of Ramadan. Velayati in response expressed concern over the current fighting in Afghanistan and voiced Iran's readiness to resolve the conflict. Since the outbreak of the civil war in Afghanistan, the Islamic Republic of Iran has repeatedly called on the belligerent parties to end the conflict and has launched mediation efforts to this end.
