940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / International Company News: Pirelli Cables Australia listed Shares in Pirelli Cables Australia, which is being spun off by its Italian parent, were listed on the Australian Stock Exchange at a 15 per cent discount to the ADollars 1.45 issue price yesterday morning, and were still trading at the ADollars 1.30 level before the close, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. The Italian parent company raised ADollars 61m (USDollars 43.8m) through the flotation of a 49 per cent stake in its Australian subsidiary, offering 42.1m shares for sale.
