Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 1 SAPA -- Members of the National Peace Keeping Force [NPKF] are refusing to continue training until a dispute over a disparity in salaries has been resolved, the NPKF's Command Council said on Tuesday [1 March]. A NPKF statement said both the Command Council and the NPKF's general officer commanding, Maj-Gen Gabriel Ramushwana, took the matter "very seriously". "It is believed the necessary steps will be taken quickly to resolve this issue," said the statement. The NPKF could not be reached for further information.
