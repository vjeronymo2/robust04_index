941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / 'Delicate matter of definitions' Mr Ryutaro Hashimoto, international trade and industry minister, had not deviated from the cabinet's stance when he stated on Monday that the question of whether Japan committed aggression against Asian neighbours was 'a delicate matter of definitions,' Mr Kozo Igarashi, Japanese government spokesman, said yesterday. Mr Hashimoto claimed Japan's fight was not with Asian nations but with the US and European powers.
