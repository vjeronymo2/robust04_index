BFN [Text] An Evrazia [Eurasia] news agency correspondent reports that last night and this morning, exchanges of artillery fire were continuing near the Lata tunnels in the Kodori gorge. The opposing force did not attempt to launch an attack. The Georgian home guard retains its positions. Let me remind you that for several days heavy fighting has been going on near the Lata tunnels. Our correspondents report from [word indistinct] that Georgian home guards have managed to improve the situation and take the initiative. Another round of quadripartite talks on a settlement of the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict is to open in Sochi tomorrow, 30 May. The talks will discuss the return of refugees to their homes, initially in Gali rayon.
