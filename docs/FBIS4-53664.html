BFN [Text] Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA] Comrade Kim Chong-il, the dear leader of our party and people, has sent thanks of the Party Central Military Committee and the supreme commander to the officers and men of the KPA unit to which Comrade Pak Yong-ho belongs for making a contribution to building, preserving, and managing the revolutionary site. He has also extended thanks to the men of merits in Hoeryong city. Taking the work of building, preserving, and managing the revolutionary site associated with the glorious and brilliant revolutionary history and the immortal revolutionary feats of the party and the leader as their due responsibility as the leader's army and the party's army, the officers and men of the KPA unit to which Comrade Pak Yong-ho belongs participated in this work as a group. They have intensified the indoctrination work on the revolutionary tradition through their visit to the site. In addition, using their leisure time between trainings, they made a great contribution to preserving the revolutionary site to its original state handing it down for ages to come. Taking the pride and dignity in living in a place with a historic background, some 80 men of merits, including Yu Tae-kyu, in Hoeryong city resolved to take part in managing the revolutionary site in the city in February 1991. Since then, they have carried out the management befitting masters. The have not only transplanted some 800 rare trees and flowers and set up new audio system around the site, but also prepared numerous materials and equipment necessary for the construction of the site and took part in the work of introducing the revolutionary site. Meetings to deliver KPA Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il's thanks were held at relevant sectors. Functionaries concerned, the soldiers of the People's Army, and the men of merits were present in the meetings. In the meetings, the comrade supreme commander's thanks were delivered amid the participants' fervent applause. Discussions for resolution were held at the meetings. A letter of pledge and a resolution to KPA Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted in the meetings.
