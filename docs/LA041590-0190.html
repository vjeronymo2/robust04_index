April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $18 Saturday when Dearly Loved finished third in the eighth race to ruin his $9 triple bet after Fashionable Flair and Big Bass won the sixth and seventh races. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is at $807.40.

Bob's bet today is a $25 win parlay from Inquisitorial in the third to Great Relations in the fifth.

Saturday's Bankroll: $825.40

Saturday's Loss: $18
