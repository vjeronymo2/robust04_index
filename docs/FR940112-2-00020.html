[Docket No. JD94&hyph;01918T Oklahoma&hyph;65] State of Oklahoma; NGPA Notice of Determination by Jurisdictional Agency Designating Tight Formation January 6, 1994. Take notice that on December 29, 1993, the Corporation Commission of the State of Oklahoma (Oklahoma) submitted the above-referenced notice of determination pursuant to §271.703(c)(3) of the Commission's regulations, that the Douglas (Tonkawa) Formation, underlying portions of Roger Mills and Ellis Counties, Oklahoma, qualifies as a tight formation under section 107(b) of the Natural Gas Policy Act of 1978. The recommended area is described as all of Sections 9, 10, 15&hyph;22, and 28&hyph;30, in Township 17 North, Range 24 West, and Sections 24 and 25, in Township 17 North, Range 25 West. The notice of determination also contains Oklahoma's findings that the referenced formation meets the requirements of the Commission's regulations set forth in 18 CFR Part 271. The application for determination is available for inspection, except for material which is confidential under 18 CFR 275.206, at the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426. Persons objecting to the determination may file a protest, in accordance with 18 CFR 275.203 and 275.204, within 20 days after the date this notice is issued by the Commission. Lois D.
