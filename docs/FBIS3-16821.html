Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Swedish state nuclear energy inspection has acquired funds that will be destined to ensure the safety of the Ignalina nuclear power station. The Swedish Government yesterday allocated for this purpose 17 million kroner from the budget of the foreign affairs department. Another 10 million kroner has been allocated by the nature protection department. A control service taking care of the safety of the nuclear power station is to be formed in Lithuania. A part of the Swedish fund will be used for this service's needs.
