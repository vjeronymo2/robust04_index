Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister Jose Naranjo, chief of the Council of State's Coordination and Support Staff, has stressed the confidence the Cuban political leadership has that Granma's sugar industry workers and entire people will carry out a sound productive response in the current harvest. Naranjo pointed out that this province has traditionally marked the pace for the sugar industry. Jose Naranjo and Alfredo Enrique Pina, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Granma Province, chaired an assessment meeting, which discussed the development of the sugarcane harvest and its recovery program. The overall good performance of the Roberto Ramirez and Bartolome Maso Mills, as well as the significant sugarcane planting efforts by the Union of Young Communists in the province, were underscored at the meeting.
