BFN [Text] An official source in the Ministry of Electricity and Water told the Yemeni news agency, Aden, that in continuation of the all-out war launched by the Sanaa military rulers against the people of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, particularly the brutal shelling of the economic and social establishments, the al-Ahmar gang's warplanes last Saturday [4th June] shelled the (?Aden- Lahij) water electricity generator and stopped pumping of water to the residential quarters in the capital, Aden. The Public Corporation for Water was able to repair the generator and pump the water. The enemy warplanes once again shelled the same generator yesterday by personal instructions of the head of the Sanaa ruling regime. The source confirmed that the Public Water Corporation is currently repairing the generator.
