BFN [Text] All refugees from Bosnia who are fit to carry arms and who are staying in Sweden should travel home and take part in the defense of the country, says the Tuzla Mayor Selim (?Bezladcic), who is currently visiting Goteborg. According to the mayor, within one month the refugees will receive a summons to travel home, and that summons will come from the Bosnian Government. If they do not heed the call, they may encounter problems when they want to return to Bosnia. New passports may then be denied them, says the Tuzla mayor, currently visiting Goteborg.
