BFN [Text] "The matter is closed", said Claudio Dematte, the chairman of the RAI [Italian Broadcasting Corporation], who was referring to the storm surrounding the recent remarks of Silvio Berlusconi. The prime minister "told me that his plan is very clear: The RAI cannot be progovernment or antigovernment," said Dematte, who along with RAI Managing Director Gianni Locatelli, met Berlusconi at the Saxa Rubra television center. "We agreed to see each other in order to begin work immediately on the regulations governing the RAI and its reorganization plan. That's all."
