921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / World Trade News: Bouygues Singapore deal BOUYGUES, the leading French construction group, yesterday reported a FFr1.6bn (Pounds 190m) contract to build a combined hotel, shopping and office centre in Singapore. The contract was signed between Bouygues' subsidiary Dragages et Travaux Publics, and a joint venture of Pontiac Land, one of Singapore's largest property developers, with Kajima Overseas Asia, a Japanese construction company. Building will start next year and take about 30 months.
