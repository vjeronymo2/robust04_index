940402 FT 02 APR 94 / Coal-fired power stations to close National Power is to close up to five coal-fired power stations in the next 12 months with the loss of 270 to 410 jobs. It will mothball three oil-fired generating units immediately. It is closing Rugeley A in Staffordshire, Skelton Grange in Leeds, and Willington A near Derby from September 30. It expects to close Aberthaw A, South Glamorgan, and Uskmouth, Gwent, in a year. It has mothballed one oil-fired unit at each of Fawley, Southampton, Littlebrook, Kent, and Pembroke, Dyfed.
