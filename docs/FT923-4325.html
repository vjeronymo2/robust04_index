920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / US tries to explain to China Mr William Clark, the US assistant secretary of state, arrived in Beijing yesterday, beginning a one-day mission to try to explain to China's leaders why Washington has decided to sell F-16 jets to Taiwan, Reuter reports. A US Embassy spokesman said Mr Clark had arrived on a military aircraft from Tokyo, where he had been waiting since last Friday for Beijing to approve his visit.
