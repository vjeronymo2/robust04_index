Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Exclusive] [Text] In a story totally different from the official story announced earlier by Damascus about the death of Basil al-Asad, eldest son of Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad, highly reliable sources have emphasized that Basil was the target of an armed attack on Wednesday, 19 January 1994, during a visit to Beirut. Following the attack, Basil was moved to a Syrian Hospital in a complete coma. He died on Friday morning after a two-day coma. The sources emphasized that the killers succeeded in escaping. A joint committee of the Syrian and Lebanese intelligence services is investigating the incident. The preliminary conclusions of the committee indicate that foreign quarters are behind the sinful assassination.
