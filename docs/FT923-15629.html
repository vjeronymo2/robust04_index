920701 FT 01 JUL 92 / Thatcher sworn in at Lords DEMURE and self-effacing on what she conceded was 'a very nervy occasion', former prime minister Mrs Margaret Thatcher kept her cool as she was sworn in as Baroness Thatcher of Kesteven in the House of Lords yesterday. The Lords' scarlet benches were as packed as for the Queen's formal opening of parliament.
