930320 FT 20 MAR 93 / Motoring: Helping hand after an accident AN ACCIDENT is like a family bereavement; a time of stress, form filling, visiting offices and answering mail. You are feeling upset and nothing would be nicer than someone to take care of the details. Which is where Accidentcare comes in. This service arranges car recovery, estimates and alternative transport, monitors repairs and handles claims, even filling in the insurance form over the phone and sending it to you for signature. The cost is Pounds 19.95 a year per car, which can be driven by anyone covered by the insurance policy. For details, call 081-202-2020.
