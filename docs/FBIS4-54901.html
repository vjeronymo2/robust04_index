BFN [TA SR report: "Netherlands Embassy"] [Text] A reception on the occasion of the Netherlands national day, the Day of the Queen, hosted by Hans J. Heinemann, ambassador of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to the Slovak Republic resident in Prague, marked the official opening of an independent representative office of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in Bratislava. The embassy's task is to intensify contacts between the two countries. Initially, the Kingdom of the Netherlands will be represented in Slovakia by Charge D'Affaires Hugo Gajus Scheltema.
