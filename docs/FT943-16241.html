940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Casket issues trading warning Casket, the UK's second largest bicycle manufacturer which also distributes clothing and hosiery, has warned that 'the promising start to the new financial year has not been sustained and in both our businesses . . . we have been experiencing very competitive trading conditions. 'As a result, prompt action has been initiated by the board to reduce costs to reflect the current levels of demand.'
