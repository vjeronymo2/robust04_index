940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Church & Co A stronger performance from its A Jones & Sons retailing side enabled Church & Co, the footwear group, to lift pre-tax profits by 53 per cent from Pounds 565,000 to Pounds 864,000, on turnover 5 per cent higher at Pounds 34.7m. Earnings per share were 5p (2.8p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 3p.
