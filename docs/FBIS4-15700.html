BFN [Text] An extended meeting of the Kyrgyz Government took place in Bishkek yesterday. It was chaired by Prime Minister Dzhumagulov, and was attended by leaders of ministries, departments, and enterprises. The meeting discussed how to raise industry out of crisis. The situation is very serious. In March, 65 enterprises ground to a halt. The production capacities of the remaining ones are half used. Warehouses have a surplus of goods in stock. Light industry alone is overstocked with goods to the value of 63.6 million soms and the level of indebtedness exceeds 700 million soms. A resolution was adopted on this issue and steps have been specified aimed at lifting the country's industry out of crisis.
