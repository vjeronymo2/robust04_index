941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: Apple in Banai deal Apple Computer has announced an agreement with Banai, the Japanese toy manufacturer, to develop multimedia CD-ROM players based on technology called Pippin, derived from Apple's Macintosh personal computers, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. Banai said that it plans to launch the 'Power Player' in late 1995. The machine, which will be designed to plug into a TV set, will play games and entertainment CD-ROMs and is expected to sell for about Dollars 500.
