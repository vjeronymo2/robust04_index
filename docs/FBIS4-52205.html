BFN ["Just Efforts of Arab People for Peaceful Solution of Mideast Problem"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, May 8 (KCNA) -- A spokesman of the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today answered the question raised by KCNA in connection with the recent signing of an accord on autonomy by Palestinians in Gaza and Jericho. He said: "Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organisation Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin met in Cairo on May 4 and signed an accord on enforcing autonomy by Palestinians in Gaza and Jericho. "The accord signed this time, a detailed working agreement to implement the declaration of principle on Palestine autonomy which was signed in September last year, is a result of the patient efforts of the Palestinian people to retake the lost land and restore their legitimate national rights. "The master of the Palestinian problem is the Palestinian people. "We respect the will and choice of the Palestinian people in settling the Palestinian issue and consider signing of the accord by the Palestine Liberation Organisation to be their right. "We hope the accord will be a basis of founding an independent state of Palestine and the first step for a fair and comprehensive solution to the Mideast problem. "The Korean people will as ever actively support the just efforts of the Palestinian and Arab people for a peaceful solution of the Mideast problem including the Palestinian issue, standing on their side".
