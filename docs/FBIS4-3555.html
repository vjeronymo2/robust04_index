BFN [Text] Seoul, April 22 (XINHUA) -- South Korean Prime Minister Yi Hoe-chang handed in his resignation to President Kim Yong-sam here today, YONHAP NEWS AGENCY quoted Yi's spokesman as saying. The exact reason for Yi to resign was not immediately known. Yi, 59, former chairman of the Board of Audit and Inspection (BAI), was nominated as prime minister by President Kim to replace Hwang In-sung on December 16 of last year. Meanwhile, President Kim named Deputy Prime Minister Yi Yong-tok as his new premier today, a presidential spokesman said.
