920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Ferrum declines sharply to Pounds 424,000 As forecast at the December year end, pre-tax profits of Ferrum Holdings, the engineering group, showed a marked decline, from Pounds 1.42m to Pounds 424,000, in the six months to June 30. Turnover fell from Pounds 24m to Pounds 19.2m. In spite of the fall an unchanged interim dividend of 1.1p is declared. The company said that although the dividend was uncovered by earnings of 0.97p (3.82p) per share, it was well covered by cash flow.
