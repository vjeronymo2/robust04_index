Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] La Paz, 26 Mar (EFE) -- Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada has changed five of his 10 cabinet ministers and created a new minister. This is Sanchez de Lozada's first ministerial change since taking office on 6 August 1993. The ministries in which the Bolivian president made changes are: finance, human development, defense, justice, and social communication. He created a minister without portfolio in charge of capitalizing public enterprises.
