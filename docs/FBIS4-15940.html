BFN ["Federal Law No. 1-FL on Financing Expenditure from the Federal Budget in the Second Quarter of 1994"] [Text] Article 1. The Russian Federation Government is to finance expenditure from the federal budget in the first half of 1994 on the basis of the federal budget projections for 1994 approved by the Russian Federation Government, in accordance with the taxes and payments that the federal budget actually receives. Article 2. The Russian Federation Central Bank is to provide the Russian Federation Government in the second quarter of 1994 with credit totaling 10.6 trillion rubles [R] to wipe out the federal budget deficit. It is 10-year credit, to be repaid in equal installments, beginning in 1998, at 10 percent interest per annum. Article 3. In order to curb expenditure on the upkeep of federal organs of state power, maximum amounts of finance for current expenditure are to be set in the second quarter of 1994: For the Russian Federation President's Administration of Affairs (in millions of rubles): Total: 82,000, including: Russian Federation President's Administration: 27,754 Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council: 11,303 Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma: 27,754 Russian Federation Government (aside from Russian Federation ministries and departments): 15,189 Article 4. This Federal Law enters into effect the moment it is published. Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin. The Kremlin, Moscow, 10 April 1994.
