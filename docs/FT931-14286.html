930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO'S Bovespa index rallied from an early afternoon low, but was still off 1.3 per cent, or 1,164 points, at 87,833 at the close. At worst it was down some 4 per cent. Brokers said that investors were anxious over the Brazilian senate's vote today on a long-delayed port bill, which will propose the privatisation of services and deregulation of the labour market.
