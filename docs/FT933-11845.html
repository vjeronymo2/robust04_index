930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / International Company News: Credit Agricole and Banque Sofinco agree collaboration on consumer credit Credit Agricole and Banque Sofinco, a subsidiary of Suez, have agreed to collaborate in the area of consumer credit, Reuter reports from Paris. Credit Agricole plans to launch a revolving credit product attached to a bank card and Sofinco will provide the expertise to manage the business. The deal is initially to run for five years, after which Credit Agricole may take a stake in Sofinco. Suez will retain majority control of Sofinco.
