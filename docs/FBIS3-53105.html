Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Negotiations between Israel and the Palestinians in Washington will not be resumed together with other bilateral talks due to open in about 10 days. Officials in Jerusalem told Qol Yisra'el they wanted the Israel-PLO talks in Taba to conclude before a decision was made on reopening the negotiations in Washington. Israel will, however, send delegations to the American capital for the 12th round of talks with Syria, Lebanon, and Jordan.
