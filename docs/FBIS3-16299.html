Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan March 15 TASS -- "The presence of the Russian border troops in the Transcaucasus on the basis of an agreement with Georgia and Armenia points to the recognition of Russia's strategic interests in this region," the Commander in Chief of the Russian Border Troops General Andrey Nikolayev said here today. On behalf of the Russian president he signed an agreement on the enlistment and service of Armenian citizens in Russia's border troops stationed on the territory of this republic. Russia and Armenia also signed bilateral protocol on the text of the oath to be taken when joining the Russian border troops. Both the agreement and the protocol are of extremely great importance, General Nikolayev told ITAR-TASS. They practically complete a series of documents relating to the presence of Russian border troops on Armenian territory and on cooperation between the two countries in protecting the state border. We have completed all steps connected with the preparation of the necessary legal basis, from the status of presence to the system of manning borderguard units and funding provisions for them, the general said. General Nikolayev stressed the importance of this accomplishment for Russian border guards and Armenian citizens.
