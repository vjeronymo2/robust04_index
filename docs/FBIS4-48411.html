BFN [Text] Northwestern Province Deputy Minister Reverend Amos Chisipawa has directed police in Mililunga to close two shops opened on the Angolan border by a foreign investor. Addressing public workers at Mililunga Secondary School, Rev. Chipawa said that police in the area should not allow foreign investors to establish shops in the border area. He emphasized that investors interested in retail should establish shops at the Boma [traditional authority] and not border areas because the peace of the nation could be compromised. The minister called on Zambians to be security conscious and report suspicions characters to authorities.
