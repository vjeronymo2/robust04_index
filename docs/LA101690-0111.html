October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

RUTH ANN HODGES; FORMER GROCERY STORE OWNER

Ruth Ann Hodges, who once owned a neighborhood market in North Hollywood, has died in a Glendale retirement home. She was 89.

Mrs. Hodges died Friday of heart failure, said her son-in-law, Mark Forrest.

Born Ruth Ann Reed on Sept. 4, 1901, in Dayton, Ohio, she came to California as a young woman and later settled in Sepulveda. From about 1970 to 1980, she and her husband owned and operated the Community Market in North Hollywood.

She is survived by a grandson, William Huber of Encino; a granddaughter, Christine Conover of Glendale; and a great-granddaughter. Her husband, Frank Hodges, died in 1984.

A memorial service is scheduled for 4 p.m. today at Pierce Brothers Valhalla Mortuary, 10621 Victory Blvd. in North Hollywood, which is handling arrangements.
