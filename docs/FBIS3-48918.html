Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] After a complex and comprehensive inspection, the State Receiving Committee signed Act 16 with which the 1000-megawatt sixth reactor of the Kozloduy Nuclear Power Plant has been accepted as suitable for regular functioning. The sixth reactor is now functioning at full capacity. The other two reactors -- the second and the fourth -- are also functioning at full capacity. The capacity of the 440-megawatt first reactor is constantly being increased on the basis of tests that are being constantly conducted. At the beginning of the week, that reactor was connected to the country's power grid. The 1000-megawatt fifth reactor has been switched off for repairs and refueling. It is expected that this reactor will be connected to the power grid by the end of January 1994.
