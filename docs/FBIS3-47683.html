Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Convention Plenary Session was held at 1000 today in the Central Meeting Hall at the President's House compound on Ahlone Road, Yangon [Rangoon]. It was attended by Lieutenant General Myo Nyunt, chairman of the National Convention Convening Commission [NCCC]; Lt. Gen. Maung Thint and Brigadier General Myo Thant, NCCC vice chairmen, and NCCC members; U Aung Toe, chairman of the National Convention Convening Work Committee [NCCWC], U Tha Tun NCCWC vice chairman, and NCCWC members; Brig. Gen. Tin Aye, chairman of the National Convention Convening Management Committee [NCCMC], and NCCMC members; national convention delegates including two new delegates from Southern Shan State Special Region No. 6 and one new delegate from Northern Shan State Special Region No. 3, who were invited to participate in the special invitees delegate group; and local and foreign journalists. NCCC Chairman Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt acted as chairman and NCCC Secretary Brig. Gen. Aung Thein acted as secretary of the meeting. First, the secretary of the meeting announced the validity of the meeting as 668 of the 696 national convention delegates attended the plenary session. Next, NCCC Chairman Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt delivered an address. Continuing, NCCWC Chairman U Aung Toe presented explanations. [passage omitted]
