Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Excerpts] Kitakyushu, 2 Mar (XINHUA) -- Koichi Sueyoshi, mayor of Japan's Kitakyushu city, and persons in charge of local economic circles today hosted a dinner at the Kitakyushu Royal Hotel to welcome Zhu Rongji and his party. [passage omitted] At the dinner, Zhu Rongji expressed the hope that cooperation between Kitakyushu city and China would continue. This afternoon, Zhu Rongji and his party visited Yasugawa Electric Machinery Company, Japan's largest manufacturing plant for industrial-use robots. In the morning, Zhu Rongji visited Japan's wellknown Toto Electric Company's First Sanitary Porcelain Plant in Kokura, and heard a briefing by company President Yoshine Koga on its cooperation with China. Zhu Rongji expressed the hope that Toto Electric Company would further its cooperation with and transfer its technology to China. Zhu Rongji said: With an urban population of 300 million, China has a very large market and the prospects for cooperation between the two sides are very bright. [passage omitted]
