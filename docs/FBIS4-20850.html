BFN [Text] The withdrawal of Russia's strategic missile forces deployed in Belarus has already begun. This relates to the Mazyr and Lida missile divisions composed of eight missile regiments equipped with 72 launching facilities. The divisions are armed with the portable ground-based missile systems RS-12M and Topal. In 1993, one missile regiment was withdrawn from Pastawy, Vitsebsk Oblast, to Russia. In 1994, another four regiments with 36 launching facilities will be withdrawn from Belarus. The last regiments will be pulled out in 1995. In 1996, the Mazyr and Lida divisions will cease to exist.
