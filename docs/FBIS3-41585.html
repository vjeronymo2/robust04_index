Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Nov 15 -- Minister of Environmental Protection, Natural Resources and Forestry Stanislaw Zelichowski on Monday announced that Poland will ask the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Ukraine and Belarus to sign a convention in which the four countries will state they will not implement investment projects which are unfriendly to the natural environment of their neighbours. Speaking at a press conference, the minister also said that ``ecology is without frontiers.'' ``The issues of the environmental protection are common in our part of Europe and therefore, establishing closer cooperation ties with our neighbours will be one of the ministry's policies,'' he added.
