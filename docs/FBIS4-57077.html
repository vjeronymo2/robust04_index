BFN [From the "JB Report" column by Teodomiro Braga: "Nuclear NGO"] [Text] Sidney de Miguel, PV [Green Party] deputy for Rio Grande do Sul, has discovered that the National Nuclear Energy Commission [CNEN] is registered as a nongovernmental organization in the National Registry of Environmental Organizations. As an NGO, the CNEN, which is responsible for the country's nuclear policy, might have access to environment preservation funds.
