920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / Fighting flares in Kabul Scores of people were killed or wounded in rocket and artillery duels between government forces and dissident mujahideen guerrillas in the Afghan capital Kabul, Reuter reports from Kabul. Each side blamed the other for the fighting, which lasted for several hours until early yesterday. Doctors reported at least 70 casualties, including nine killed. Government sources said nearly 40 rockets fell in the area of Kabul airport, which was closed on Thursday after a mujahideen leader threatened to shoot aircraft using it.
