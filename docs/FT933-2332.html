930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / Lib Dems boost A confidential Conservative poll suggests that if this year's local election results were replicated in the European parliament vote next summer Liberal Democrats would win eight of the 32 seats currently held by the Conservatives. The study will provide a further boost for Liberal Democrats at the formal opening this morning of their annual conference in Torquay. Tories fear big loss of Euro seats; Lib Dems conference, Page 11
