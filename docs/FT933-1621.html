930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Support found for dedicated taxes MORE THAN 75 per cent of people think that earmarking taxes for specific spending could be a boost for public services, an NOP poll for New Century, a left-leaning political magazine, found. Six out of 10 would be more likely to vote for a party which proposed to put 1p on income tax with all the money going to the National Health Service.
