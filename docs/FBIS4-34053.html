BFN [Text] Brasilia, 12 Jun (AE) -- At 1900 today the Superior Electoral Court (TSE) closed registration for presidential candidates. Only one party registered candidates over the weekend: the Brazilian Party for the Defense of Women's Rights (PBDDM) [Partido Brasileiro de Defesa dos Direitos da Mulher]. The party's presidential candidate is Aldenora de Sa Porto and the vice presidential candidate is Nina Maria Alexim. The PBDDM has been provisionally registered, and the registration of its candidates is subject to approval by the TSE. Aldenora de Sa Porto is the 11th registered candidate. The others are: Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva (PT) [Workers Party], Fernando Henrique Cardoso (PSDB) [Brazilian Social Democracy Party], Orestes Quercia (PMDB) [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party], Leonel Brizola (PDT) [Democratic Labor Party], Esperidiao Amin (PPR) [Progressive Renewal Party], Flavio Rocha (PL) [Liberal Party], Eneas Carneiro (Prona) [Party for the Redefinition of the National Order], Hernani Goulart Fortuna (PSC) [Social Christian Party], Walter Queiroz (PRN) [National Reconstruction Party], and Caetano Matano (PT do B) [Brazilian Workers Party].
