Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] Last year party development work was carried out fairly well nationwide. According to incomplete reports, party organizations admitted nearly 50,000 new members, 30 percent more than the 1992 figure. Among the departments under the Central Committee, the Army party organizations had the most new members admitted. The number of young people joining the party who had been members of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Federation was also significantly large, comprising about 50 percent of the total. The number of new members with high levels of education was also large, and the numbers of women and ethnic minorities also were higher than before.
