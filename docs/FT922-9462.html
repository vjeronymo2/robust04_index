920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Sega in Pounds 2m TV sponsorship deal SEGA Europe, the video games manufacturer, is to pay Pounds 2m to Independent Television for sponsorship rights of the European football championship, to be played in Sweden between June 10 and 26. Sega will use the deal to market its products in a variety of ways, including television commercials, a national consumer competition, point-of-sale promotions and dealership marketing drives.
