Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 23 (KCNA) -- The Central People's Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea recently awarded the title of Hero of the Republic to Kim Chol-chin (23), a non-commissioned officer of the Korean People's Security Forces (KPSF), and the "Kim Il-song Youth Honor Prize" to Kim Kyong-chol (20), a KPSF soldier, who have come back home in triumph by fighting it out with a rock-firm faith and principles as People's Army soldiers, not yielding to the enemy when they were taken [word indistinct] South Korea under unavoidable circumstances. At the end of January, they were arrested by the South Korean authorities in distress in the West Sea while on military duty. The enemy employed all conceivable moves to convert the two soldiers. The enemy put them each in a solitary cell, allured them with money and goods, sent in turncoats to win them over and threatened them, showing them bloody torture rooms. The enemy, however, could not break the revolutionary constancy and will of the KPSF soldiers who have had iron faith. Although they were detained by the enemy, they resolutely fought the enemy with a dignified attitude as soldiers of Comrade Kim Chong-il, the supreme commander, believing him only and sent tremor through the enemy, before coming back to the bosom of the socialist homeland, the bosom of General Kim Chong-il.
