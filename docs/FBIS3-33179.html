Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon in Kingston, Jamaica and Cuba signed a maritime border delimitation treaty establishing national waters and continental shelf borders of both countries on the basis of equidistant delimitation lines. Jamaican Foreign Minister Paul Robertson and Cuban Ambassador Dario Urra headed the two delegations and signed the new bilateral accord. Robertson stressed that the expediency and ease with which the accord was completed reflects the level of existing relations between neighboring peoples who share many common traits as developing Caribbean states.
