930821 FT 21 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: Savings guide for the elderly - At a glance The charity Help the Aged has produced a free booklet which gives a clear guide to the mysteries of savings and investment. The booklet, Managing a Lump Sum, is available from Help the Aged shops or by sending a 9 in by 5 in SAE to: MALS, The Information Department, Help the Aged, St James's Walk, London EC1R 0BE.
