BFN [Text] Beijing, April 4 (XINHUA) -- Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee, met here today with Jacques Attali, former special advisor to French President Francois Mitterrand. Attali, arriving here yesterday at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, came to acquaint himself with China's reform and open-up and economic development.
