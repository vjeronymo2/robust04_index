Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- Paek Chong-su, secretary general of the National Association of Bereaved Families for Democracy and Reunification, accused the traitor Kim Yong-sam of protecting former dictators. In an article contributed to HANGYORE SINMUN Jan. 16, he branded Chon Tu-hwan and No Tae-u as the main culprits in illegally seizing "power" through the "December 12 coup". Noting that the traitor Kim Yong-sam "invited" those guys to "Chongwadae" [presidential office] allegedly for "reconciliation", Paek Chong-su said such act could not be overlooked. There can be no "reconciliation" with Chon-No group, he stated.
