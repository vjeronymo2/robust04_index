BFN [Text] Russia's air and space forces (ASP) launched a Foton satellite with the Soyuz carrier rocket from the Plesetsk space site at 20:00 Moscow time. The Foton satellite which weighs 6,200 kg will carry out a series of material processing experiments. The program will last 18 days plus one reserve day. The satellite carries 700 kg of research equipment. Experiments were developed at the experimental engineering office in Moscow. The launch was attended by members of the former Russian royal family, designers of space technologies and representatives of the European space agency. The program envisages the launch of another Foton satellite. The first Foton satellite was launched on April 16 1985. Eight Fotons have so far been launched. The last launch took place on October 8 1992.
