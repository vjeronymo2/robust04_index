March 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GRAHAM QUITS TROY JOB TO COACH AT DON LUGO

Ed Graham, varsity basketball coach at Troy High School, has resigned his position effective April 7 to become an assistant basketball coach at Don Lugo High in Chino Hills.

Graham, 38, compiled a 34-33 record at Troy in three seasons, leading the Warriors to two Southern Section playoff berths. He also was an assistant track coach and taught English.

Before coming to Troy, Graham was basketball coach at Orange High School.

"I moved to Chino Hills five months ago and was spending too much time on the freeway and not enough time with my family," Graham said. "I thought the commute would be no problem, but I was wrong."
