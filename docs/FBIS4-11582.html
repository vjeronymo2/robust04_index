BFN [Text] Guatemala City, 27 Apr (ACAN-EFE) -- The Constitutionality Court (CC) reported today that the country's new Congress will be elected on 14 August, just as had been established by the Supreme Electoral Tribunal (TSE). The CC turned down the temporary amparo granted by the Supreme Court of Justice (CSJ) to Deputy Carlos Arroyave against the TSE, whom he accused of violating his rights by calling parliamentary elections for 14 August. According to the resolution issued today, the CC turned down the amparo granted by the CSJ on 22 April because it considers the situation presented by Solidarity Action Movement Deputy Arroyave as "nonexistent."
