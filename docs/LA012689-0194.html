January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Services for Notre Dame football player Bobby Satterfield, who died last Thursday in Niles, Mich., will be held at 11 a.m. today at Founders Church of Religious Sciences, 3281 West 6th St., Los Angeles. Burial will follow at Forest Lawn, Hollywood Hills. Mark Green, Notre Dame football captain, will lead a delegation of students.
