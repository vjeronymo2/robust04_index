BFN [APA report: "Verdict for Kuessel Overruled"] [Text] Vienna -- The Supreme Court of Justice partly set aside the verdict on the neo-Nazi leader Gottfried Kuessel yesterday. The founder of the "Extraparliamentary Opposition Loyal to the People" was sentenced to 10 years imprisonment for illegal National Socialist activities. The judges ruled that the directions to the jury regarding individual charges were insufficient. A new trial is now necessary, and a new verdict must be passed.
