BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridnyova] [Text] Dushanbe May 29 TASS -- A Russian border guard was killed in the Tajik town of Khorog, administrative centre of the Gorno-Badakhshan region of Tajikistan, on Saturday. Deputy commander of Russian border troops in Tajikistan Major- General Aleksandr Savchenko told ITAR-TASS that the soldier was killed by an unidentified terrorist who fired two shots at him at 14:20 local time. An investigation has been launched. A total of 32 Russian border guards have been killed in Tajikistan since 1993.
