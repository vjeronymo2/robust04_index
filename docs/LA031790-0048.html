March 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Bergen Brunswig Corp.: The Orange-based distributor of prescription drugs, health care products and prerecorded videocassettes reported that net earnings for its second fiscal quarter reached a record $15.4 million, 34% higher than the $11.4 million posted for the comparable 1989 period. Revenue for the three months ended Feb. 28 increased 12% to $1.1 billion from $970.4 million.

For the first six months, net earnings rose 29% to $30.3 million from $23.4 million for the comparable year-earlier period. Revenue increased 12% to $2.2 billion from $1.9 billion. Emil P. Martini Jr., chairman and chief executive, said the prescription drug unit showed strong sales and earnings growth, while Commtron, the company's 80%-owned home entertainment distribution subsidiary, reported lower sales and operating earnings.
