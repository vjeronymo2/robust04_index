BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 5 -- The Trans-Vietnam 500-kv [kilovolt] power transmission line began test operation this morning, announced the Ministry of Energy. In anticipation of the commission of the north-south line, the eighth and also the last turbine generator of the Hoa Binh Hydro-Electric Power Plant was officially hooked to the national grid yesterday after seven days' test run. Since the first generator unit of the Hoa Binh Hydro-Electric Power Plant was operational on Dec. 31, 1988, the plant has supplied 17.108 billion kwh [kilowatt hours].
