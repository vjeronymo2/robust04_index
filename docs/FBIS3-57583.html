Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Jan 26, BNS -- The reaction of Belarusian authorities to the arrest of two Lithuanian communist leaders in Minsk will "undoubtedly cast a chill over our relations" with Belarus, Lithuanian Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius said on Lithuanian radio Wednesday. The premier said he regrets such "energetic" reaction, which shows that Lithuania's neighbors "have not yet completely shaken off old principles and formulas." In Slezevicius' opinion, the problem also resulted from political tensions in Belarus. The Lithuanian premier said the arrest operation in Minsk was indeed very successful and fully coordinated with Belarusian authorities. Slezevicius said he will seek to further expand the earlier positive developments in Lithuanian-Belarussian relations.
