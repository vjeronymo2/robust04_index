November 9, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Meanwhile, Eddy Raven has replaced Earl Thomas Conley on the country music bill Saturday night at the Celebrity. Conley cancelled because of illness, according to a theater spokeswoman. The Forester Sisters will perform as scheduled. Ticket holders who want a refund can get it at the place of purchase. Also, Kenny Rogers' upcoming shows at the Celebrity have been rescheduled from Jan. 27-28 to Feb. 3-4.
