BFN [Text] After concluding his visit to New Delhi, Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin said that there is no reason for Israel not to sell arms to India. Beilin stressed, however, that he has no knowledge of any joint military plans between the two countries. Beilin asserted that the potential for cooperation between India and Israel is unlimited. The deputy foreign minister expressed the hope that India will become more involved in the peace process.
