December 19, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ANCHORS AWEIGH: One problem for Jennings, Brokaw and Rather (F1) is that there are more cable and local options for viewers. . . . In Orange County, that means you can watch Merker and Matthews. . . . That would be Michelle Merker and Pat Matthews, anchors of the county's only regular newscast. For the first time, the KDOC-Channel 56 news show has showed up on the Arbitron ratings service, watched in at least 5,000 non-cable households. . . . Starting Dec. 27, the half-hour nightly broadcast moves from its 8:30 slot to 8. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
