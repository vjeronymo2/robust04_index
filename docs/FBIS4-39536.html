BFN [Text] At its morning plenary session today, the parliament approved the agenda for two days. The law on entering amendments and additions in the code of administrative breaches was adopted on an article-by-article basis, in view of the adoption of the law on architecture and urban construction in the Republic of Kazakhstan. The Supreme Council ratified an agreement on friendship and cooperation between Kazakhstan and Mongolia; an agreement between the Governments of Kazakhstan and China on the encouragement and mutual protection of investments; and the UN convention on children's rights. The parliament also ratified an agreement on the pensions and state insurance of the staff of the CIS Ministries of the Interior. Altynshash Zhaganova, chairwoman of the committee on culture, press, the media, and public associations, spoke on coverage of the parliament's work in the media.
