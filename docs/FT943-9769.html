940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Pillar public offer 5 times subscribed The public offer of shares in Pillar Property Investments was subscribed five times, Barclays de Zoete Wedd said yesterday. A placing of 60m shares at 150p apiece to institutional and other shareholders, valuing the group at about Pounds 170m, was announced last month; 15m shares were subject to clawback under the terms of the public offer. In the event, applications were received for 74.9m shares and allocations have been scaled down. Investors applying for between 200 and 400 shares will receive 200 shares; between 600 and 1,000, 300 shares; for 1,500, 375 shares; between 2,000 and 15,000, 20 per cent; between 20,000 and 30,000, 17.5 per cent and for more than 30,000, 15 per cent. Dealings will commence on August 15. See Lex
