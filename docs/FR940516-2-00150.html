E. Scoring and Grant Application Selection Criteria Upon receipt of an application for an off-reservation grant under 25 CFR 23.33 which requires the approval of the Area Director, the Area Director shall: (1) Establish and convene an area review committee, chaired by a person qualified by knowledge, training and experience in the delivery of Indian child and family services. (2) Review the area office certification form required in 25 CFR 23.33(a). (3) Review the application in accordance with the competitive review procedures prescribed in 25 CFR section 23.33.
