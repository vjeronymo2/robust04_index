931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / Banda 'making good progress' MALAWIAN President Kamuzu Banda was making good progress in a Johannesburg hospital yesterday after an operation to remove excess fluid from the brain, a hospital spokesman said, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Dr Banda, believed by diplomats to be in his 90s, was flown to Johannesburg from Malawi on Saturday and underwent surgery on Sunday. 'The operation was performed in order to remove excess fluid from the brain. His physical condition is satisfactory and a good prognosis is expected,' the spokesman said.
