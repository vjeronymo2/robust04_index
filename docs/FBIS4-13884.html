BFN [Text] Baghdad, 23 Apr (INA)--Vice President Taha Yasin Ramadan met this evening with Colonel Mustafa al-Kharrubi, personal envoy of Libyan President Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi to his brother President Saddam Husayn, at his residence. The two officials reviewed forms of U.S.-Zionist plots concocted against Iraq and Libya. They also discussed ways of cooperation and coordination between the two sides to confront the challenges imposed on the two countries, particularly the continuation of the unjust blockade on the two fraternal people. Col. Mustafa al-Kharrubi arrived in Baghdad today on a visit of several days. He is accompanied by Ahmad 'Abd-al-Salam Bin-Khayyal, official at the General People's Committee for External Liaison, and Husayn Salih, director of the Arab League Affairs at the General People's Committee for Unity. INA learned that Col. Mustafa al-Kharrubi is bringing a personal message to President Saddam Husayn from his brother Libyan President Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi.
