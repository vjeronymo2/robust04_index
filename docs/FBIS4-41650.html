BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kulbitskiy] [Text] Bonn June 24 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and German Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl have managed to agree on details of the seeing-off ceremony for Russian troops withdrawn from Germany during their meeting in Kerkira (Corfu), official representative of the German Government Dieter Vogel told German news agencies today. Under the agreement, Boris Yeltsin and Helmut Kohl will take the parade of the Russian troops on August 31, 1994. Vogel made no further comments on the issue. He said that the Kerkira summit is not the best place to do it. All the details will be made public early next week upon Vogel's return to Bonn.
