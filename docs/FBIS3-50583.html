Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The News" newscast] [Text] In order to facilitate the pardoning of soldiers who participated in the 4 February and 27 November coup attempts, President Ramon J. Velasquez urged them to abide by the law so that decisions in that regard can be made within the legal framework. President Velasquez is studying the possibility of issuing a new list of soldiers to be pardoned, including officers who sought asylum in Peru. He invited them to return to the country, guaranteeing their safety. He stressed that Venezuela is ruled by laws based on the Constitution, which is a guarantee for all Venezuelans.
