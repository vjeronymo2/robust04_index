941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / People: Non-executive directors Dominic Cadbury (above), 54, chairman of Cadbury Schweppes who joined the Guinness board in 1991, has been appointed joint deputy chairman and chairman of the non-executive committee of GUINNESS. He takes over at the end of the year when Sir David Plaistow, 62, retires; Sir David has been chairman of Inchcape since 1992.
