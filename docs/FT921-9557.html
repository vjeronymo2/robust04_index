920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Asset dip at English & Caledonian Inv Over the six months to December 31 1991, net asset value of English & Caledonian Investment fell further, from 193.4p to 188.9p. At the end of 1990 it had stood at 210p. In the latest half-year, revenue totalled Pounds 342,000 (Pounds 271,000). Earnings advanced to 3.3p (1.39p) and the interim dividend is raised to 1.25p (1p).
