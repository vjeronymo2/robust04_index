BFN [Text] The second use trial of the short-range surface-to-surface missile Prithvi was successfully conducted at the interim test range in Orissa today. The Defense Ministry sources in New Delhi said that the launch was to the maximum range of the missile and all the mission requirements were achieved. Today's test flight of the sleek missile is the 14th in the series, and second from the indigenously built [word indistinct] mobile launcher. The prime minister, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao, has complimented the Army, the scientists, and staff of the concerned organizations for the accomplishment.
