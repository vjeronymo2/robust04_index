920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / World Trade News: Disk-drive plant goes to Ireland TWO leading disk-drive makers, Matsushita-Kotobuki (MKE) of Japan and Quantum Corporation of the US, are to open a factory in Ireland as their European market base, Tim Coone reports from Dublin. MKE makes 40MB to 240MB hard disk drives in Japan, while Quantum markets them to personal computer makers. European sales top IPounds 250m (Pounds 227m) a year (35 per cent of Quantum's world market). The plant should be on stream by autumn.
