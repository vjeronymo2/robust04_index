Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pristina, March 24 (ATA) -- Serbian army and police are training their units for four days now in Landovica, the Prizren district, near the border with Albania. On March 23, military trucks and cannons of big calibers headed towards Djakova on the border with Albania. On March 22, Serbian army forces stationed on the road that links Prizren with Kukes, city in the territory of Albania. Concentration and training of Serbian army and police forces have been noticed in other areas of Kosova as well.
