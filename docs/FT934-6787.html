931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Worthington Profits of Worthington Group, the textiles accessories manufacturer, rose from Pounds 305,000 to Pounds 508,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. The 67 per cent improvement was achieved from turnover little changed at Pounds 7.49m (Pounds 7.3m). The interim dividend is lifted to 0.6p (0.5p) from earnings of 2.01p (1.25p) per share.
