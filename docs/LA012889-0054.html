January 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PULLING THE PLUG

In reference to Diane Haithman's Jan. 9 article, "NBC's Tartikoff Takes Heat for Rivera's Satanism Show": I am leading a grass-roots movement for an annual national TV blackout -- a blackout for one day to send a signal to the networks that we demand better programming.

I consider watching TV an unhealthy habit, not unlike smoking. There is an annual "smoke out" to help smokers -- why not an annual blackout to help TV addicts?

Stay tuned for the "National Unplug Your Set Day."

RICHARD V. STEWART

La Crescenta
