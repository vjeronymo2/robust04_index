December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: WEST COVINA;

BACKTRACKING ON ADDITIONS

A month after liberalizing a temporary limit on the size of additions to single-family homes, the City Council went back to the old, stricter guidelines.

On Monday, the council voted 5 to 0 to limit room additions to 50% of existing floor area. The strict rules, originally adopted in October, were eased Nov. 12 to allow additions on a sliding scale ranging from 100% to 200% of the original floor area, depending on lot size.

The new ordinance will be in effect until September. Permanent guidelines are being prepared.
