930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / Observer: Over and over As the English cricket team makes a final attempt at the Oval today to recover some credibility after its dismal test series, some of Australia's more abrasive supporters are flaunting their latest fashion accessory - a T-shirt. Referring to the second test in a more select part of London, the front of the shirt asks: 'Who took four wickets at Lords?' On the back - 'England'.
