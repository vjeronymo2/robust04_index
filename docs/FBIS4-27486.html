Detonators for Mortars 94P30060A 94P30060A CSO [Editorial Report] The 12 April QUAN DOI NHAN DAN reported that the Military Region 3 Political-Military School recently produced a test run of 35 detonating fuses for 82mm mortar rounds. This technical improvement was the idea of Major Nguyen Manh Chinh, a cadre at the school. When tested, 100 percent of the rounds exploded successfully. The new rounds will be an economically effective aid in weapons training.
