Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Boris Yeltsin signed an edict on the Scientific Council attached to the Russian Federation's Security Council. The Scientific Council's functions include defining priority areas of scientific research in the security sphere for subsequent examination by the Security Council, the organization of scientific expertise for draft federal programs on security, the evaluation of how effectively these programs are being implemented, involvement in supervising the protection of intellectual property, and a number of other functions. The chairman of the Scientific Council is appointed by the president and is not a member of the Security Council's staff. Purimov, vice president of the Russian Academy of Natural Sciences, has been appointed chairman of the Scientific Council attached to the Security Council.
