BFN [By reporter Chen Maodi (7115 3029 1717)] [Text] Shanghai, 10 May (XINHUA) -- During his 8-10 May inspection tour of Shanghai, Liu Huaqing, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice chairman of the Central Military Commission [CMC], said emphatically: The entire party and Army, as well as people across the country, should subordinate themslves to the overall task of economic construction, further strengthen the Army's unity with the people and the government, and make concerted efforts to contribute more to the socialist modernization drive. While he was in Shanghai, Comrade Liu Huaqing heard work-related briefings by Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai municipal party committee, and by Mayor Huang Ju. He also toured the New Pudong District. He fully affirmed Shanghai's achievements in economic construction. He said: The Shanghai municipal party committee and the municipal government have earnestly implemented Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, and they have carried out their work vigorously. Shanghai has undergone phenomenal changes, and its situation is very good. On the Yanpu bridge, CMC Vice Chairman Liu Huaqing joyously told Zhu Zhihao, who was in charge of directing the bridge's construction: The working class in Shanghai has rendered outstanding services and boosted the Chinese people's morale. During his stay in Shanghai, CMC Vice Chairman Liu Huaqing also inspected troops on naval warships stationed in Shanghai, kindly encouraging naval officers and men to devote serious attention to acquiring modern military scientific know-how, undergo rigorous training, strive to improve their fighting capability, and further consolidate and expand their achievements in carrying out "double-support" work and in undertaking joint army-civilian efforts to promote spiritual civilization.
