BFN [Unattributed report: "Jordanian Information Minister Stresses Amman's Neutrality in Yemeni Crisis"] [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Jordanian Information Minister Jawad al-'Anani has said that Jordan's neutral position on the Yemeni crisis has not changed since the crisis began, contrary to what some media services say. In a statement to AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT he added that "Jordan was aware early on of the dangers of an armed conflict in Yemen. It made great efforts with the two parties to the Yemeni crisis until we held a conference to sign the Pledge and Accord Document, which was tantamount to a defusing of the fighting and a step toward dialogue and a peaceful understanding. It was in response to a written request from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his deputy 'Ali Salim al-Bid."
