920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / Terry Ramsden case moves court THE PROSECUTION of Mr Terry Ramsden by the Serious Fraud Office has been transferred from City of London Magistrates Court to the Central Criminal Court. Mr Ramsden - former chairman of Glen International, an investment firm that collapsed in 1987 with losses of Pounds 142m - faces 22 charges. The charges include fraudulent trading, obtaining property by deception, false accounting and making false statements to Department of Trade and Industry inspectors. He has been granted legal aid with a contribution of Pounds 25,000 which, magistrates directed, will not become payable until the end of the criminal proceedings. Mr Ramsden is on Pounds 150,000 bail.
