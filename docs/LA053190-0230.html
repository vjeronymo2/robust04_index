May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

5 PICKS, BONUS WIN $1.14 MILLION

No one had all six winning numbers needed to claim the $4.19-million jackpot in the latest California Lottery "Lotto 6-49" drawing, lottery officials said today.

The unclaimed top prize will roll over to an estimated $10 million for Saturday night's drawing.

One lucky ticket had five of six winning numbers plus the bonus number. It is worth $1.14 million. There were 150 tickets with five of six winning numbers. Those tickets are worth $3,916 each and share a prize pool of $587,000.
