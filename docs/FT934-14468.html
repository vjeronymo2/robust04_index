931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Reduced loss at WB Industries WB Industries, the spring manufacturer, incurred losses of Pounds 185,000 before and after tax in the first half of 1993. The figure, which compared with losses of Pounds 657,000 last time, was struck after a reduced interest charge of Pounds 77,000 (Pounds 185,000) and an exceptional Pounds 74,000 for redundancy and severance costs. Turnover from continuing activities - West Bromwich Spring - amounted to Pounds 1.34m. The comparable figure of Pounds 3.94m included Pounds 2.79m from Elson & Robbins, sold in October 1992. Losses per share were cut from 10.24p to 2.88p.
