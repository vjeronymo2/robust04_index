Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Leonid Vinogradov] [Text] Nakhodka March 3 TASS -- The investing agencies of the Maritime territory have started the investigation of the death in Nakhodka of an officer of the counterintelligence service and a militia officer. According to the ITAR-TASS information, Yevgeniy Vyskubov, an officer of the regional branch of the Federal Counterintelligence Service, and 1st Lieutenant Mikhail Rusin, senior official of the Department for Combatting Organised Crime at the city branch of the Interior Ministry, were shot dead from revolvers and a submachine-gun late at night on Wednesday. Another militiaman was wounded. According to preliminary information, the tragedy was the result of the careless handling of guns.
