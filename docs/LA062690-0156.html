June 26, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

BULLS GORE RUNNER, 38, TO DEATH

A 38-year-old man was gored to death today when he tripped and fell during the annual running of the bulls in this southwestern town.

The running of the bulls in this town of 12,000 people 149 miles west of Madrid is staged as part of the annual fiesta dedicated to St. John.

In the ritual, the bulls are turned loose before dawn in the town's sealed-off streets, and young men race ahead of them. The bulls are killed in bullfights later in the day.

Many small towns and cities across Spain maintain the tradition. But in recent years, there has been growing opposition from Spanish and foreign groups to bullfights and the running of the bulls on the grounds that the practices constitute cruelty to animals.
