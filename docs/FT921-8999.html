920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value up 12.8% at Yeoman Yeoman Investment Trust lifted net asset value from 134.7p to 152p per capital share over the 1991 year - an advance of 12.8 per cent. Net revenue of this split-capital trust amounted to Pounds 3.17m (Pounds 3.22m) for earnings of 12.87p (13.06p) per income share. The recommended final dividend is again 1.5p for a maintained total of 13.6p.
