BFN [Text] The station correspondent reported from the Royal Thai Air Force Headquarters airport that Prime Minister Chuan Likphai and his delegation left Bangkok this morning for an official visit to the Republic of Korea from 23-26 June at the invitation of President Kim Yong-sam of the Republic of Korea. High-level dignitaries in the entourage include Deputy Prime Minister Amnuai Wirawan, Deputy Prime Minister Suphachai Phanitchaphak, Foreign Affairs Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri, Defense Minister General Wichit Sukmak, and Deputy Commerce Minister Chaiyot Sasomsap, as well as a group of 14 businessmen. The main aim of the visit is to strengthen and further develop the existing relations and cooperation between Thailand and the Republic of Korea, especially in the economic and political fields, as well as cooperation in this region.
