BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 27 June (KUNA) -- Michael Heseltine, British president of the Board of Trade, said here today that he did not discuss "any new trade deals" for British companies in Kuwait as part of a privatization drive. Heseltine told a news conference here today that his visit was confined to strengthening bilateral ties between the two countries and to present Britain's privatization experience and its success so that Kuwait could benefit from it. He expressed his hope that British companies would be given more investment opportunities in Kuwait, stressing that the final decision belonged to the Kuwaiti Government. [passage omitted]
