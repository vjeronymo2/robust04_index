941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Matthew Clark Matthew Clark, the indepen-dent drinks wholesaler which acquired Gaymer, the fortified wine and cider company last month for Pounds 109m, has spent Pounds 5.1m to buy a further 12.55 per cent of Freetraders Group, a drinks wholesale business. The purchase, made under the terms of the Freetraders shareholders' agreement struck at the time of the initial acquisition of 74.9 per cent, brings Clark's holding to 87.45 per cent. Clark has the option to acquire the outstanding 12.55 per cent in October 1995.
