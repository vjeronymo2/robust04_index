940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / International Company News: Japanese insurer in UK venture Sanshin, a Japanese non-life insurance company affiliated with Sanwa Bank, a leading commercial bank, is to set up a joint reinsurance venture in the UK with Robert Fleming Insurance Brokers, part of the UK merchant bank group. Sanshin Fleming Reinsurance Brokers will be established in London this month, with Sanshin and Robert Fleming Insurance each holding 42.5 per cent stakes, and Japan England Insurance Brokers the remaining 15 per cent. The company will be the first Japanese insurance company to be involved in the reinsurance business.
