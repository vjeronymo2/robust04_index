Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan. 6 -- The Polish trade deficit increased to 2,036 m [million] dollars after 11 months of 1993, according to a high ranking official of the National Bank of Poland (NBP). After 10 months the deficit amounted to only 1,949 m dollars. The same official told reporters here Thursday [6 January] that foreign exchange reserves of the bank at the end of November amounted to 3,840 m dollars.
