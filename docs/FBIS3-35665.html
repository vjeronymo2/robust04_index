Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Georgian Security Minister told Interfax on Friday that the murder of a Mkhedrioni leader Temur Talakhadze was "a deliberate act". Talakhadze was killed on January 28 afternoon in the city of Rustavi by a bomb placed under an armchair in a barber's shop. According to sources in the Georgian Security Ministry, the bomb killed another 3 people and wounded several visitors nearby. According to the ministry, that was not the first attempt to assassinate Talakhadze. Terrorist acts have previously been committed against him, however unsuccessfully.
