Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source in the city of al-Ramadi told us that while the Jordanian ambassador was travelling in his private car to Baghdad in mid-December 1993, he was stopped by a number of unknown gunmen on the highway between Baghdad and al-Ramadi. He was robbed of all his personal belongings and money, down to his watch. The source also said that the unknown gunmen cursed the Jordanian ambassador and the Iraqi regime.
