940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: Former E German leader Honecker dies Former East German leader Erich Honecker (left) died in the Chilean capital of Santiago, aged 81. He was charged with manslaughter over the deaths of people trying to escape to the west over the Berlin Wall, but his trial in Berlin was abandoned in January last year because he was suffering from liver cancer. He left Berlin to live with his wife in Chile, where his daughter had married. Obituary, Page 3
