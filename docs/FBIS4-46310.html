BFN [Announcer-read report] [Excerpt] The state has filed charges against 35 members of terror organization known to the public as the Hezbollah. According to information received from Bekir Selcuk, public prosecutor of the Diyarbakir State Security Court, the indictment demands the death penalty for 15 of the suspects, 15-year prison terms for three of the suspects, and prison terms ranging from 10 to 15 years for the remaining 17 suspects. The 36-page indictment notes that in accordance with Article 125 of the Turkish Penal Code, 15 of the suspects are accused of engaging in activities aimed at establishing a Kurdish state based on Islamic principles in southeastern Anatolia.
