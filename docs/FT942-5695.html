940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / Engineering wage pressure still low There is no sign of upward pressure on pay settlements in the engineering industry, in spite of the upward drift in national average earnings over the past few months, the Engineering Employers Federation said yesterday. In the three months from February to April the average settlement level was 2.27 per cent, only slightly up on the 2.14 per cent recorded from December to February.
