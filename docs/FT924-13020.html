921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Radiotrust net asset value declines Radiotrust had a net asset value per share of 49.89p at July 31 compared with 56.72p a year earlier and 50.21p at the trust's January year-end. Net losses for the half year amounted to Pounds 21,416 (profits of Pounds 33,363), equivalent to losses per share of 0.25p (0.39p earnings).
