Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Tzomet leader Refa'el Eytan announced the renewal of the alliance between Tzomet and the National Religious Party [NRP] and Agudat Yisra'el in order to set up an alternative bloc to Meretz. The block consists of 15 Knesset members. Eytan told our party affairs correspondent Shmu'el Tal that if the prime minister does not pick up the gauntlet and include it in the coalition, this bloc will act against the government. He added that as part of the renewal of the alliance, the parties decided on joint activities.
