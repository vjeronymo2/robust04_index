BFN [Text] Foreign Ministry Secretary General Zafir al-Hasan today summoned the ambassadors of the United States, Russia, France, Britain, and China for consultations on Lebanon's call for a UN Security Council [UNSC] meeting to discuss Israel's kidnapping of al-Hajj Mustafa al-Dirani. Al-Hasan said Lebanon's decision to request a Security Council meeting will not await a response by the five big powers. He explained that Lebanon's Permanent Representative to the UN Khalil Makkawi has been instructed to call for a meeting without specifying a date for it. After the meeting, Russian Ambassador Gennadiy Ilichev said he will convey Lebanon's request to his government for a decision. U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley denied that he knows anything about U.S efforts to discourage Lebanon from calling for a UN Security Council meeting.
