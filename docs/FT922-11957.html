920422 FT 22 APR 92 / Islamic leader stays in jail Algerian judges yesterday threw out a charge that Islamic Salvation Front provisional leader Abdelkader Hachani urged troops to disobey orders, but left him in prison on a lesser charge, his lawyer said, Reuter reports from Algiers. The three-member court also rejected prosecution appeals against a lower court which quashed charges that Hachani distributed harmful tracts and attacked the honour of public bodies, the lawyer added.
