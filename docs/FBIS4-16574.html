CSO ["Election Results in Kiev"] [Text] The winners in the three electoral districts of Kiev where people's deputies of Ukraine were elected on 10 April are: in Radyanskyy E.D. No. 16 -- Myroslav Horbatyuk, the Rukh candidate; in Svyatoshynskyy E.D. No. 18 -- Ivan Zayets (Rukh), a deputy to the old Supreme Council of Ukraine (he defeated a CPU member); in Chervonoarmiyskyy E.D. No. 23 -- Environmental Protection Minister Yuriy Kostenko, a Rukh member (he defeated Semen Yufa, chairman of the Merkuriy Cooperative Society). No deputies were elected in five other districts [because neither candidate obtained the qualifying number of votes], and the elections in the remaining districts were invalid [because voter turnout was lower than required by law].
