BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, May 3 (AFP) -- [passage omitted] UNPROFOR spokesman French Major Eric Chaperon said a Serb T-34 battle tank was intercepted overnight by French troops near the Lukavica barracks -- right in the heart of the NATO exclusion zone on heavy weapons around Sarajevo ordered in February. Both here and in the eastern town of Gorazde, heavy weapons were building up again in the exclusion zones "drop by drop," the spokesman said. Informed military sources also reported increased Bosnian Government army activity in the Mount Igman region overlooking Sarajevo, with units being relayed by fresh troops. The Serb tank was heading that way. [passage omitted]
