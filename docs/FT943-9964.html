940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / UK Company News: ICS expands with Pounds 1.47m purchase Industrial Control Services, which provides electronic control systems, has bought the business and certain trading assets of Transmitton for up to Pounds 1.47m cash. Transmitton, a division of Balfour Beatty, designs and makes computer-based control and monitoring equipment for the mining, oil and gas, transport, electricity and water industries. The price consists of an initial Pounds 922,500 and deferred payments of Pounds 410,000 and Pounds 140,000. In addition, there will be an adjustment, up or down, and up to Pounds 600,000 to reflect the value of assets acquired at completion.
