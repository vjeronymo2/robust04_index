BFN [Text] President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani received Bosnian Foreign Minister Irfan Ljubljankic today. The president praised the brave holy war of the Muslims of Bosnia-Herzegovina against the Serb aggressors, and said that the Islamic Republic of Iran will continue to stand alongside the Muslim people of Bosnia. The president also stressed the need for comprehensive international support in realizing the rights of the Bosnian Muslims. Emphasizing the determination of the Muslims of Bosnia-Herzegovina in defending their beliefs and existence, and speaking on behalf of the president, the government and the people of his country, the Bosnian foreign minister thanked the revolutionary people and government of Iran for their humanitarian aid.
