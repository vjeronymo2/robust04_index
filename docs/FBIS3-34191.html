Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Greek foreign minister has described the Islamic Republic of Iran as a very important regional power. Welcoming our country's foreign minister at Athens airport, Karolos Papoulias described Velayati's visit to Greece as important. He said that issues relating to the Middle East, the Mediterranean, Central Asia, and the Balkans will be discussed at their talks. Velayati left Bucharest for Athens last night, at the end of his two-day visit to Romania. Before leaving Bucharest he separately met and conferred with the Romanian president and parliament speakers. During the meetings President Ion Iliescu and the speakers of the Romanian parliament expressed gladness about the failure of the attempt on President Hashemi-Rafsanjani's life. They stressed the importance of the expansion of Tehran-Bucharest ties. According to an IRNA report Ion Iliescu reiterated the grounds for cooperation between the two countries and emphasized Iran's role in Central Asia and the Persian Gulf region. He invited Hashemi-Rafsanjani to pay a visit to Romania. Also, at a news conference, Velayati expressed gladness about the visit. He said that during his meetings with Iliescu he had submitted a verbal message from Hashemi-Rafsanjani to him.
