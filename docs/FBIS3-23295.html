Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] According to our correspondent in Tyre, clashes involving the use of machine guns and hand grenades occurred in al- Rashidiyah refugee camp at dawn today between personnel belonging to the pro-'Arafat Fatah and others from the pro-Abu-Nidal Fatah-the Revolutionary Council. Four pro-'Arafat personnel were injured in the clashes, including Major Abu-'Ali, deputy to the official in charge of military affairs in the camp, and his wife. The casualties were taken to a hospital in the camp. Meanwhile, an explosion was heard in the Khirbat Silm village last night when unidentified men hurled a bundle of dynamite sticks in front of a small mosque. Tension prevailed in the village in the past two days in the wake of clashes between Amal Movement members and Hizballah militants after two cars and a home belonging to Amal members were bombed.
