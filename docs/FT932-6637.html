930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Dencora placing to raise Pounds 1m Dencora, the property and housebuilding group, has placed 840,000 ordinary 25p shares for cash with institutional investors at 122p each. Last month, the company announced a swing from pre-tax profits of Pounds 404,000 to losses of Pounds 936,000 in 1992.
