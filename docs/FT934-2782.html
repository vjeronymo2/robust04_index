931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / Markets: Healthy rally for smaller companies - At a Glance Smaller companies have enjoyed a healthy rally over the last fortnight. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose from 1576.43 on November 25 to 1604.65 on December 2 and 1609.28 on December 9, for an overall gain of 2.1 per cent.
