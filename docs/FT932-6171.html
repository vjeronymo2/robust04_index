930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Turnround for Kelsey Industries In the half year to March 31 Kelsey Industries achieved a turnround from pre-tax losses of Pounds 119,000 to profits of Pounds 32,000. For its last full year to September 1992 losses were Pounds 307,000. Turnover for the group, which makes solder and audio and video accessories and has interests in industrial roofing and insulation contracting, grew from Pounds 19.6m to Pounds 22.8m. Most of the solder companies had performed better, Mr Philip Arbib, the chairman, said. Kelsey Roofing had a much improved order book and he expected its losses to be eradicated by the year end. Losses per share amounted to 2.7p (5.6p) and the interim dividend is cut to 3p (8p).
