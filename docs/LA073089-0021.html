July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

'THE GREENING OF THE SUMMIT'

The front-page articles of July 17, "Summit Stresses Ecological Threat" and, July 18, "Lujan to Defend Offshore Oil Drilling as Vital" make one wonder if members of the Administration are in touch with one another, let alone the rest of the world and its needs.

C.L. PEDDIE

Laguna Niguel
