CSO [Text] (STA) Podgorica -- The accusation leveled by the Belgrade Center for Antiwar Activities against the Montenegrin state leadership for war crimes committed against civilians, specifically refugees, no doubt seriously endangers the security and stability of that state. The accusation is plausible, based on documents signed by Nikola Pejakovic, Montenegrin minister of interior affairs. The documents state that scores of Muslim refugees arrested by the Montenegrin police last May were handed over to the Serbian police, after which they vanished without a trace.
