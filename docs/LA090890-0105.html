September 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PETER SELLARS' REMARK

I was appalled when I read "The Artist at the Helm" (Aug. 31), which was about Los Angeles Festival Director Peter Sellars.

That a public figure would say "it doesn't make you look like a low-IQ retard" is almost unbelievable. And that his cast giggled in response says a lot about their IQs.

Sellars, Times staff writer Greg Braxton and The Times editing staff all owe apologies for having said, written and published this remark.

JOANNE TURNER

Pasadena

Sellars made the comment in directing the "Nixon in China" cast to actually converse rather than to mime conversation.
