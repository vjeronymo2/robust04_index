931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Optometrics improves to Dollars 98,000 Optometrics, the USM-quoted optical systems group, reported increased pre-tax profits of Dollars 98,000 (Pounds 65,770) for the six months to the end of September, compared with Dollars 29,000. Mr Frank Denton, chairman, said shipments were above the levels of last year and the order book remained more than 30 per cent ahead. Turnover was Dollars 1.88m (Dollars 1.76m). Earnings per share came out at 0.7 cents (0.05 cents). In October the company signed an agreement with Integrate Russia, which represents several large Russian optical institutes, for worldwide distribution rights of their products. The shares rose 2p to 23p.
