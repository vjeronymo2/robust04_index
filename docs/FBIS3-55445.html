Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Another missile strike has hit the positions of the 13th Moscow border guard contingent in Tajikistan. On Sunday, at 10:35am, local time, seven missiles were launched against the border guard station from the Tajik territory, INTERFAX was told by a spokesman for the headquarters of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan. On Saturday, at 3pm, local time, five missiles were launched against the 13th border station of the Moscow border guard contingent. On Saturday, at 6pm, local time, Russian border guards of the 12th Moscow contingent curbed the attempt by seven gunmen to penetrate inside the territory of Tajikistan. During the clash three attackers were killed. There were no casualties among the Russian border guards. The deputy commander of the Russian border guard troops, General Anatoliy Parakhin has told INTERFAX that, as Russian border guards forecast earlier, during the winter period more small groups will cross the border to get ready for the spring offensive on the territory of Tajikistan.
