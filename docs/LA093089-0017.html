September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A LOGICAL EXPLANATION OF WHY A PITCHER LOSES

That Orel Hershiser is a great pitcher is a given, but when Hershiser loses a game he, the press, the Dodger announcers and Tom Lasorda blame Hershiser's teammates for not scoring enough runs for him to win -- isn't that why any pitcher loses?

BILL STEIN, Cambria
