940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / UK Company News: George Simpson prepares to take up his post as the new Lucas chief Mr George Simpson, who played a leading role in the sale of Rover Group to BMW, will next month begin his new job as chief executive of Lucas Industries, the automotive and aerospace components group. He will leave his current post as deputy chief executive of British Aerospace at the end of March - a month earlier than previously announced. He was also chairman of Rover Group, BAe's vehicles arm. Mr Simpson, 51, was elected to the board at Lucas last Friday - the day on which Rover, the last British-owned volume car producer, officially passed into the ownership of BMW, the German carmaker. Lucas sought for months to secure the services of Mr Simpson.
