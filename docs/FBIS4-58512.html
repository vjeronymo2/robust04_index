BFN [Text] Two soldiers were injured in stone-throwing incidents in the territories yesterday. Near Nabulus, a soldier suffered medium injuries when a military vehicle in which he was traveling came under a hail of stones. He was taken to Beilinson Hospital for treatment. The village of Hawwarah, where the incident took place, is under curfew. In Jericho, a soldier suffered light injuries when local residents threw stones and bottles at the police station at the center of town. During the night, dozens of right-wing activists and settlers continued their efforts to try to reach the ancient Jewish synagogue in Jericho. Part of the town has been declared a closed military area. A Qol Yisra'el reporter says soldiers were busy chasing those trying to infiltrate. Several arrests were made.
