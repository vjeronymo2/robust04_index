Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, January 12 (KNS-KCNA) -- Chairman of the Association of Koreans in Japan for Peaceful Reunification Kim Chong-su published a statement on January 11 supporting the policy of national reunification reclarified by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his New Year address. "The policy of achieving the country's reunification through confederacy on the three principles of independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity, clarified once again by the great leader in his New Year address, fully conforms to the will and interests of the whole nation and serves as a highly important guideline in accomplishing the cause of national reunification by the efforts of the nation itself," he said. "The 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation is a great charter for national reunification reflecting the ardent desire of all the fellow countrymen to achieve unity and national reunification transcending differences in ideology, ideal and system," he said, and stressed: "The Kim Yong-sam `regime' must refrain from obstructing a fair solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula through DPRK- USA talks and take the road of national reconciliation and unity in response to the 10-point programme. "The Association of Koreans in Japan for Peaceful Reunification will wage a more vigorous struggle to make 1994 a historic year in which a new phase of national reunification shall be opened for founding a pan-national unified state of confederacy form in the 90s," he said.
