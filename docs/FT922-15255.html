920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Capital and Regional ahead Capital and Regional Properties, a USM-quoted property investment company, reported a slight increase from Pounds 351,507 to Pounds 367,378 in pre-tax profits for the year to December 25, after exceptional costs of Pounds 84,791. Operating losses amounted to Pounds 214,241 (Pounds 351,507 profit) after increased net interest payable of Pounds 3.09m (Pounds 2.89m). The pre-tax result was after a Pounds 666,410 profit on the sale of investment properties. Earnings per share emerged at 1.26p (0.91p) and the proposed dividend is raised from 0.9p to 1p through a proposed final of 0.7p. There was an extraordinary Pounds 1.19m charge mainly relating to the discontinued partnership in 545 North Michigan Avenue, US. Full provision was made in the 1990 balance sheet in respect of that partnership. Net asset value per share improved to 153.5p (148.1p).
