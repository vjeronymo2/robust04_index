December 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

"What She Found There" by John Glore, the literary manager of South Coast Repertory, has been declared co-winner of the 1990 National Ten-Minute Play Contest at the Actors Theatre of Louisville with Neal Bell's "Out the Window." Glore and Bell will share a $1,000 award, and their mini-plays will be staged in Louisville as part of the annual Humana Festival of New American Plays on the weekend of April 5-7.
