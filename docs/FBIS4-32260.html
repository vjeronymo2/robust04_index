BFN [Text] Tirana, June 21 (ATA) -- In the yesterday's meeting with the delegation of the European Democratic Union, President Berisha made a panorama of the continuation of reforms in Albania and expressed the Albania's determination for protecting and further developing the human rights, establishing a state of the law and market economy. He also expressed his commitment for a policy of cooperation and understanding with the countries of the region and broader in favour of stability and peace. President Berisha emphasized that the Albania's integration in Europe is one of priorities of the Albania's policy.
