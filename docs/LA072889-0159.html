July 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Goodyear Tire & Rubber of Akron, Ohio, reported a second-quarter profit of $27.1 million, compared to a $134.9-million profit in the same period a year ago. The company said its profit was affected by the sale of its South African subsidiary, which resulted in an after-tax charge of $52 million. Proceeds from this sale are expected to total $41 million when the sale is completed in the third quarter.

Some figures for these companies were misstated in Thursday's earnings roundup.
