920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / International Company News: Kyocera Canada KYOCERA, the Japanese maker of ceramic integrated circuit packaging, has restructured its a sales unit in North America, Reuter reports. It has closed Kyocera Canada in Ontario, a Canadian sales company for optical products, and set up a Canadian branch of Yashica, taking over the business of the former Kyocera Canada, it said.
