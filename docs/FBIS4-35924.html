BFN [Text] The popular resistance in the al-Shu'ayb district succeeded in resisting an aggressive assault mounted by the enemy forces last night and today, and inflicted heavy losses on them. This happened when the enemy forces sent a company [sariyyah] from the Hamzah Brigade forward, with support from all forms of heavy weapons, including Katyushas. They attempted to occupy a number of villages in al-Shu'ayb. They carried out random shelling against the villages and their peaceful inhabitants, which led to the wounding of four citizens. Popular resistance forces engaged the attackers, led by the fighters of the liberation war, from last night until this afternoon. The enemy suffered heavy losses, as 24 men were killed or wounded. Our forces also destroyed three military vehicles, along with their crews. An enemy reconnaissance officer was captured. The rest of the enemy forces were compelled to withdraw and flee in the direction of Qa'tabah.
