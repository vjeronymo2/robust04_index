April 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LIMBAUGH'S HECKLERS

Your silence is deafening. . . .

KFI-AM's infamous Rush Limbaugh appeared on "The Pat Sajak Show" March 30.

As I write this, it is four days later. This is The Times' town. Has the newspaper lost its voice?

Yes, Rick Du Brow had a short item April 3 in which he used the rather benign term "hecklers." But it doesn't take much imagination to visualize The Times' outrage had the victim been a liberal and the hecklers what the media usually calls "dangerous and fascistic right-wing extremist censors who are destroying free speech."

ANN NORMAN, Newport Beach
