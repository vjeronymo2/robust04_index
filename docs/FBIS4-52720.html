BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 13 (KCNA) -- More than 500 students affiliated with the Seoul Eastern District Federation of General Student Councils of South Korea (Tongchongnyon) held a rally at Sejong University in Seoul on May 12, at which they demanded a probe into the truth behind the death of a student of Sejong University Chang Tok-su who was hit by a live bullet in the reserve force training on May 3 and the punishment of those responsible. The students took to the street and held a demonstration, fighting the riot police.
