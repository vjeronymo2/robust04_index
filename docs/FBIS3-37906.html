Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow February 23 TASS -- "It is only the court which can find the Lefortovo prisoners guilty or non-guilty", Federation Council Speaker Vladimir Shumeyko told reporters on Wednesday referring to October revolt organisers who were pardoned by the lower chamber of the parliament on Wednesday. He stressed that the organisers are not political, but common criminals. "There are no political crimes or political prisoners in Russia", Shumeyko said. The revolt organisers face criminal charges and it is only the court which can make the final verdict, according to Shumeyko who said that his upper chamber does not plan to discuss the amnesty.
