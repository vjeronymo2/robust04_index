BFN [Text] A man who claimed to be from Sharpeville, Vereeniging, in South Africa alleged in Maseru yesterday that he was in Lesotho on a recruiting campaign to sign on people into the military wing of the African National Congress, Umkhonto we Sizwe [Spear of the Nation -- MK]. The man, who also claimed to be born at Kolonyama in the Leribe District, was wearing military camouflage uniform with a firearm strapped to his waist. He said he operated his recruiting campaign from the village of Ha Matala on the outskirts of Maseru. A large number of Maseru residents walked behind the man as he was escorted by police and Army officers. He has been detained by police in Maseru.
