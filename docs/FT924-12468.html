921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / Russian parliament restates claim to ownership of Izvestia Russia's conservative parliament renewed its challenge to the authority of President Yeltsin yesterday, restating a disputed claim to ownership of the influential newspaper, Izvestia, Reuter reports from Moscow. Mr Yeltsin issued a decree in September establishing Izvestia as a separate legal entity under state control to keep it out of the hands of parliament, where opponents of radical market reforms are in the majority.
