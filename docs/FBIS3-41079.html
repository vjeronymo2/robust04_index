Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Havana, 21 Jan (PL) -- The Cuban Justice Ministry said today a delegation of the Argentine Secretariat for the Prevention of Drug Addiction and the Struggle Against Drug Trafficking is negotiating a cooperation agreement in that sphere with the Cuban National Drug Commission. The Argentine delegation is led by Eduardo Luis Gonzalez, the director of international affairs of the Argentine organization, which is aimed at coordinating actions against drug trafficking. The Argentine and Cuban organizations have held meetings for the future signing of a cooperation agreement to fight drug trafficking and psychotropic substances. The Argentine delegation met also with Cuban Justice Minister Carlos Amat and will visit various health centers and places of scientific interest in Havana until 26 January, when it concludes its 10-day visit to Cuba.
