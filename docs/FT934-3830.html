931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / International Company News: Carrefour Carrefour, the French retailing group, is to sell its 30 per cent stake in But, the furniture retailer, to Kleinwort Benson for FFr536m (Dollars 90m). Carrefour said yesterday that it had accepted an offer for its stake from the UK merchant bank, equivalent to FFr937 per share, writes John Ridding from Paris. Kleinwort Benson said it had placed the shares with investors in France, the UK and the US. The placement is subject to a right to match the offer during the next 15 days from Venturini But, a family company which holds just over 50 per cent of But.
