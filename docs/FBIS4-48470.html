BFN [Text] Mrs. Musuleng-Cooper is to be inducted today as foreign minister. Mrs. Cooper was yesterday confirmed by the Transitional Legislative Assembly following confirmation hearings at the Capitol. Speaking during the hearing, Mrs. Cooper expressed her desire to work closely with all friendly nations for the speedy restoration of general peace to this country. She said a lot is required from the outside world for the furtherance of peace and reconciliation. She stressed the need for proper coordination and consultation between the transitional government and the international community and build a vibrant nation through free and fair democratic elections. Mrs. Cooper pledged not to disrupt the existing operations of the Foreign Ministry but to improve working conditions of the staff so that we can effectively strengthen the relationship between Liberia and the world community.
