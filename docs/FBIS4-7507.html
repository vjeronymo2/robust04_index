BFN [Text] The signing of the agreement and the promulgation of the Constitution of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina are already yielding good results in the Zepce Commune. After the cooperation in the field between the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the Croatian Defense Council [HVO] was established and (?two-day) mutual visits of civilian population made possible, the HVO concentration camp in Zepce was closed yesterday. Under the auspices of the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] Sector Command in the region, the HVO Command released all the remaining prisoners, members of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina, who then freely decided to travel to free territories or to remain in Zepce with their families.
