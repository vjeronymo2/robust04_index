931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Elton John wins Pounds 350,000 damages Rock musician Elton John was awarded Pounds 350,000 damages in the High Court against the Sunday Mirror, which claimed he had a form of the slimmers' disease bulimia. The jurors said that Pounds 275,000 of the award was to punish the newspaper. Mirror Group Newspapers said the punitive damages were 'grossly excessive' and the concept was 'contrary to the basic right of freedom of speech'. It was granted a stay on their payment pending an appeal.
