Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Vladimir Skosyrev report under the "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" rubric: "Pakistan Will Help Settle Conflict on Afghan-Tajik Border"] [Text] Delhi -- Pakistan is ready to help settle the conflict on the border between Afghanistan and Tajikistan. Head of the Pakistani Government Benazir Bhutto explained at a press conference on her visit to China that during a recent meeting with Afghan President B. Rabbani she had supported his policy to improve relations with Tajikistan and other CIS states. At the same time the prime minister let it be understood that Pakistan does not wish to interfere in the disputes between the various armed groupings in Afghanistan, on which the situation on the border with Tajikistan depends.
