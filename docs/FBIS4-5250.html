BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 7 -- Deputy Foreign Minister Vu Khoan is leading a Vietnamese delegation to the annual meeting of the Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (ESCAP) being held in New Delhi India. In this 50th annual meeting, the Vietnamese lead delegate was elected vice chairman of the meeting at its opening session on April 5. Addressing the meeting in the afternoon the same day, Mr Vu Khoan highlighted the major achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in implementing the policy of renovation of economic development and external affair activities in the past years. He called on all countries and international organizations over the world to assist and cooperate with Vietnam in the construction of its infrastructure in service of economic production and people's daily life. He highly appreciated ESCAP's role in promoting regional cooperation in all fields, especially in economy. He also appreciated ESCAP's effective cooperation and active assistance to Vietnam.
