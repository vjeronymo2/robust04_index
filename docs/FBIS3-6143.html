Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN ["Announcement"] [Text] By virtue of the remarks made by Prince Krompreah, first prime minister, concerning his initiative to raise living standards, rehabilitate and develop the whole nation, and promote and quickly improve education and sports, and also by virtue of instruction from the royal government, the Ministry of Economy and Finance would like to inform the general public that the importation of the following goods: A. is subject to a total tax exemption: -- farming supplies and equipment, such as fertilizer, insecticides, power tillers, water pumps, spades, hoes, animal breeds, seeds, plows, rakes, and so on. -- school equipment, such as textbooks, pencils, ball-point pens, blackboards, erasers, and so on. -- sports equipment, such as athletic gear. -- medicines that have been inspected and approved for import by the Ministry of Public Health. -- kerosene for lighting. B. The following consumer goods and utensils are subject to a duty reduction from 35 percent to 7 percent: -- instant noodles, canned fish, cakes, sweets, candies, condensed milk, fresh milk, bowls, needles, thread, cotton thread, all kinds of buttons, elastic bands, and so on. [Signed] Sam Rangsi, state minister and minister of economy and finance
