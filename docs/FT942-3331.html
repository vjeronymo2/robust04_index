940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / Observer: US bonding The word from Wall Street is that the Clinton administration has announced plans for a new, three-tranche US government bond issue. The first tranche is the 'Gore' - No interest. The second is the 'Stephanopoulos' - No maturity. The third is the 'Billary' - No principal. A rumoured fourth tranche, to be used only in emergencies, is the 'Paula' - No date.
