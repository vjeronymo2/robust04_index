941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / People: Whatman Jeremy Leigh Pemberton is to retire as deputy chairman of Whatman, the manufacturer of filtration and purification products, on December 31. Leigh Pemberton, brother of the former Bank of England governor Robin Leigh-Pemberton (now Lord Kingsdown), was chief executive of Whatman until 1990. David Smith and Edward Libbey, who have both joined the company from BP this year, are appointed executive directors from January 1.
