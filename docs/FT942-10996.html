940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Lack of buying interest caused a weaker trend resulting in lower prices of white pepper, reports Man Producten. Spot white was offered at USDollars 2,600 a tonne and July/October shipment, ex-new crop, at Dollars 2,550, cif. Black pepper prices could not be maintained either. Faq, spot, was at Dollars 1,550 a tonne and Grade 1 at about Dollars 1,650. Prices on shipment were about the same. Negative reports about the crops of countries like Sarawak and Brazil have failed stimulate buyers so far.
