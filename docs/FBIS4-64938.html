BFN [Text] Algerian Foreign Minister Mohamed Salah Dembri today met Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou and conveyed a message from the Algerian president stressing his respect for Greece and Papandreou in his struggle for democracy. The Algerian foreign minister also met his Greek counterpart, Karolos Papoulias, with whom he discussed bilateral issues as well as Algeria's relations with the European Union. Papoulias later said that the Algerian foreign minister assured him of his country's support for Greece on the Cyprus issue and the issue of Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, FYROM]. The Algerian foreign minister invited Papoulias to pay an official visit to Algeria. The Greek minister accepted.
