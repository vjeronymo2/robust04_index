Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Dear listeners, we bring the following information to your attention: The duration of programs of Azerbaijani National Television is being reduced because of the energy shortage in our Republic. For that reason, National Television has been signing on at 1000, instead of 0900, since 14 March. We will suspend the broadcast of the Azeri program by Tehran Television on 16 March. Further reductions in our programs are expected in the next few days.
