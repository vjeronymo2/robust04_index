930421 FT 21 APR 93 / International Company News: Martin Marietta earnings up MARTIN Marietta, the US defence contractor, reported first-quarter earnings of Dollars 76.4m, or Dollars 1.61 a share, before accounting changes, compared with Dollars 74.6m, or Dollars 1.51, a year ago, writes Martin Dickson. However, the company reported a net loss of Dollars 335.6m in the latest quarter after taking a Dollars 412m charge for a change in methods of accounting for retirees' health benefits. Sales totalled Dollars 1.17bn, down from Dollars 1.37bn, and operating margins grew more than 20 per cent to 10.7 per cent.
