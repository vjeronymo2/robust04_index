930206 FT 06 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Tops Estates' Pounds 20.3m purchase Tops Estates, the shops and office property company, is paying Pounds 20.3m cash to George Wimpey for the long leasehold Clayton Square shopping centre in Liverpool. Gross rents receivable amount to more than Pounds 2.3m before the letting of vacant units and is Tops' first purchase in almost four years.
