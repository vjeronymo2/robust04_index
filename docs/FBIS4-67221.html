Protocol With France, Spain BR1605130394 Rome ANSAMAIL Database in English 1915 GMT 13 May 94 BR1605130394 Rome ANSAMAIL Database English BFN [Unattributed article: "France/Spain/Italy: Police Cooperation Protocol Signed"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, May 13 -- The commander general of Italy's Carabinieri force, General Luigi Federici, with a mandate from the ministry of the interior, Thursday [12 May] signed a protocol agreement with his counterparts from France and Spain so that the police departments from the three countries can work more closely together, an official communique from the Carabinieri's general command reported here this evening. Patrice Maynial signed for the French national gendarmerie, while Ferran Cardenal De Alemany signed for the Spanish Civil Guard. The three police forces agreed to step up their ties within the framework of international accords and national norms, with regard to training, recruitment, the organization of services, the exchange of information and experience, scientific data and techniques, and the acquisition of new technologies. The battle against drugs will figure highly in their cooperative efforts, plus, in addition to their crime-fighting activities, the three institutions also will promote protection of the environment and of the artistic heritage of their countries.
