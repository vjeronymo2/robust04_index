December 26, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Indianapolis Colt rookie wide receiver Andre Rison was on his way home to Flint, Mich., for Christmas when he was charged with reckless driving in Ft. Wayne, Ind.

Rison was driving his 1989 Mercedes when he was stopped about 5:30 a.m. near Ft. Wayne. Police said he was driving 128 m.p.h. on Interstate 69. The speed limit is 55 m.p.h. on the interstate near the city.

He was taken to the Allen County Lockup where he posted $500 bond and was released.
