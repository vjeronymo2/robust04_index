BFN [Text] U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev had a telephone conversation on Tuesday. They discussed the North Korean problem and in particular, the status of North Korea following its official announcement that it resigns from the IAEA. Both sides agreed that the announcement had the most serious consequences and that the United States and Russia would continue to hold close consultations through their representatives in the United Nations. They also discussed the question of Russia joining the Partnership for Peace program.
