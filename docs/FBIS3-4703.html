Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 1 (YONHAP) -- The government will postpone the announcement of suspending this year's "Team Spirit" military exercise with the United States to Thursday, as North Korea counterproposed to hold on that day a working-level inter-Korean meeting on special envoy exchange. The decision is in accordance with the policy that government will make the Team Spirit suspension announcement on condition that the inter-Korean dialogue is resumed and that an International Atomic Energy Agency inspection team arrives in Pyongyang. Proposing to North Korea on Monday that the working-level meeting be held on Tuesday, the government decided to make the Team Spirit suspension announcement at the Defense Ministry on Tuesday.
