920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / Appointments: Lease-back One might have thought that the former staff of British & Commonwealth Merchant Bank, which was dragged down by problems at Atlantic Computers, the computer- leasing operation that also belonged to the B & C group, would wish to have nothing further to do with the subject of leasing. However, David Milne, who was director of capital markets at the merchant bank until its demise last August, has moved to Baltic, the Mayfair-based leasing and finance house. Milne, who is 39, says Baltic is a 'conservatively-run leasing company that is at the same time very entrepreneurial'. Sounding predictably jaundiced about the Square Mile - 'a very inward-looking place these days' - he says he has been hired by Baltic to build up its structured finance side. He had been with B & C MB from its inception, 'a great opportunity that foundered due to a sick parent'. Not afflicted with false modesty, he adds: 'The fact that 80 per cent of the former employees of (B&C merchant bank) have now got good jobs is a reflection of the calibre of those people.' ***** Hugh Dumas and Chris Mason are directors of CREDIT LYONNAIS ROUSE. ***** David Brennan, Richard Chenevix-Trench and Kevin Lee are directors of BARING ASSET MANAGEMENT.
