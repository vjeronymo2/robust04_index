940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Edmond returns to the black Edmond Holdings, the housebuilder, returned to profit in the six months to June 30. Pre-tax profits were Pounds 130,000, compared with losses of Pounds 187,000, on turnover up 10 per cent from Pounds 5.7m to Pounds 6.28m. The turnover was achieved on 116 completed sales at an average price of Pounds 54,100 (102 at an average Pounds 55,800). Earnings per share were 0.26p (0.26p losses) and the interim dividend remains at 0.15p.
