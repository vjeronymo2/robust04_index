Bureau of the Public Debt OMB Number: 1535&hyph;0089. Form Number: None. Type of Review: Extension. Title: Implementing Regulations: Government Securities Act of 1986. Description: The regulations require government securities brokers and dealers to make and keep records concerning their business activities and their holdings of securities, to submit financial reports, and to make certain disclosures to investors. The regulations require depository institutions to keep records concerning non-fiduciary holdings of government securities. The goal is investor protection.
