940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / Revenue union set for recognition The Inland Revenue Staff Federation yesterday said it was hopeful of achieving recognition by EDS-Scicon, the General Motors information technology subsidiary, which is preparing to take over the Revenue's computer services. The union is negotiating terms and conditions for the 2,000 staff at the Revenue's Information Technology Office. EDS said union recognition would transfer to the company under the Transfer of Undertakings, Employment Protection legislation.
