941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Finnish PM forecasts referendum Yes vote The prime minister, Mr Esko Aho, says he expects Finns to vote for European Union membership in their referendum on Sunday, Recent opinion polls have shown Finns leaning strongly towards joining the EU. Mr Aho, chairman of the agrarian-based Centre party, said there would be some disadvantages to membership, like the impact on agriculture which must adopt to much lower producer prices. 'And it is quite clear that sometimes it is going to be difficult also to accept decisions not made at home,' Mr Aho said in an interview. 'So it means that in certain way our independence is going to be decreased.' Mr Aho, whose country shares a 1,270km border with Russia, said he hoped Moscow would continue with reforms. Asked if Finland would feel greater security in EU membership, he said: 'It is very important to notice that we have no reason to see Russia as a military threat.'
