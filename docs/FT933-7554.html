930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / UK Company News: North Midland Cons increases 13% North Midland Construction increased pre-tax profits by 13 per cent from Pounds 84,000 to Pounds 95,000 in the six months to June 30 on sales down 11 per cent at Pounds 8.75m. The interim dividend is 0.4p (0.3p), payable from earnings per share of 0.71p (0.56p).
