BFN [Text] A number of foreign correspondents yesterday visited al-'Anad Base and viewed the fortifications and military equipment and vehicles captured by the forces of Constitutional legitimacy when they took control of the base the day before yesterday. BBC radio correspondent (Simon Angerma), who visited the base, said in a report aired by the radio today that he saw some tanks of the forces of legitimacy deployed within the perimeter of the base. He also noted that the antiaircraft guns repulsed several air raids launched by aircraft of the forces of apostasy and secession over the area and forced them to flee. The BBC correspondent added that a large number of the rebel troops withdrew from the base. He also said he saw the wreckage of a helicopter downed near the base entrance and the charred corpses of the two pilots.
