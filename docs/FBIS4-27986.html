BFN [Text] Narita, Chiba Pref., June 5 KYODO -- Peruvian President Alberto Fujimori arrived in Narita Sunday [5 June] afternoon for a four-day visit to Japan on his way to China. Fujimori is scheduled to hold talks with Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata on Monday night, government officials said. It was the fifth time for Fujimori to visit Japan, including the 1990 visit just before he became president. He will make an official visit to China from June 8-12.
