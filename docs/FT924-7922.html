921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Losses deepen at Christie Group The deficit before tax at Christie Group, the specialist business agency, increased by Pounds 26,000 to Pounds 507,000 in the six months to September 30, reflecting 'continuing problems' in the company's markets. Turnover fell from Pounds 9.3m to Pounds 6.98m. Losses per share worked through at 2.8p (1.81p).
