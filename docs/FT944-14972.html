941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Avis Europe Avis Europe achieved revenues of Pounds 202m in the half year to August 31 and earnings before interest and tax were Pounds 33m, Cilva Holdings, its parent company said. Cilva also announced that it had reached agreement over future payments due in respect of the sale of Avis Lease in 1992, so that the amounts no longer depended on the performance of Avis Lease.
