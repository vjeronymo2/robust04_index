Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 29, BNS -- Practically all offensive weapons have been removed from the Tallinn-based Russian 144th motorized rifle division, a high-ranking official from the Estonian Defense Ministry told BNS on condition of anonymity. The Defense Ministry has information that a troop train with the last remaining armored equipment of the 144th division leaves Estonia at the beginning of April. The number of means of transport and men in the division is not known. The Russian division has to date turned over to Estonia the practice range at Aegviidu and the car repair shop in Tallinn. The facilities in Klooga, Keila and Tallinn are due to be handed over to the Defense Ministry later this spring.
