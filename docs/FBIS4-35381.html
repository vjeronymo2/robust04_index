BFN [Text] Cairo, 31 May (PETRA) -- Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, today met with 'Amr Musa, Egyptian foreign minister and chairman of the Nonaligned foreign ministers' conference. The talks dealt with the topics listed on the agenda of the conference, bilateral relations, issues related to the Middle East peace process, and some important Arab issues. The talks also dealt with a number of Arab and African issues. Al-Hasan also met with Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah. They reviewed developments in the Yemeni arena. He also met with the Tunisian foreign minister and reviewed with him the Arab situation and bilateral relations. The minister of state for foreign affairs also met with some of the foreign ministers of the states participating in the nonaligned foreign ministers' conference which began its work today.
