930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Tilbury Douglas disposal Tilbury Douglas has sold Douglas Concrete & Aggregates for a total of Pounds 14.7m to its managers led by Mr B Morgan, the offshoot's managing director. The shares rose 11p to 543p yesterday. Some Pounds 5.5m of the consideration was paid as a dividend, the remainder was paid in cash on completion. After Tilbury repaid certain inter-company balances the proceeds of the deal were reduced to about Pounds 11.1m.
