September 14, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

JESSE JACKSON'S ROLE IN RELEASE OF HOSTAGES

I feel it is time for the U.S. government to impose some restrictions on private citizens such as Jackson from pursuing their own personal foreign policy. Jackson doesn't seem to realize that if a player continues to carry the ball by himself to exploit his own ambitions, it eventually reduces the effectiveness of the team as a whole and causes a strong resentment among other players.

JOHN ERICKSON

Ontario
