930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Joseph Holt Joseph Holt, the Manchester-based brewer renowned among real ale aficionados for producing some of the cheapest beer in the UK, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.82m for the half year to June 30 - up 5 per cent on the comparable Pounds 3.63m. Turnover improved to Pounds 13.6m (Pounds 12.4m). The interim dividend is increased by 1p to 11p, payable from earnings of 84.58p (80.56p) per share.
