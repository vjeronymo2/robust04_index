Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 27 Jan (ARMENPRES)--The relative calm prevailing on Armenia's borders for the last few days was again disrupted today. The Azerbaijani Army formations shelled Sotk (former Zod) in Vardenisskiy Rayon with Grad rockets at 1730 today. At the same time, two SU-25 warplanes belonging to the Azerbaijani Air Force violated our Republic's airspace and dropped two bombs around Sotk. Our border guards took countermeasures and silenced the enemy, shooting down one of the two SU-25 planes. There are no casualties but there is destruction on our side.
