Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Midyat (AA) -- Eight people were injured when a bomb placed in front of a workplace exploded in Mardin's Midyat District today. According to information that has been received, the bomb that was placed in front of a supermarket in the Orta Carsi neighborhood exploded around 1200 seriously injuring Hamit Akan, Huseyin Alpsoy, M. Ali Akyuz, Murat Orek, Ferman Akyuz, Hasan Topuz, M. Emin Alpsoy and Bilal Turkcan. The injured are being treated at the Midyat and Mardin state hospitals and an investigation into the incident is underway.
