930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Vosper Thornycroft buys water jet maker Vosper Thornycroft, the shipbuilding and engineering group, has bought RG Parker (Engineering), which manufactures water jets used in high speed craft. No price was disclosed. Parker is based in Bury St Edmunds, Suffolk, and trades as PP Jet. It had sales of Pounds 1.1m for the year ended December 31, of which 80 per cent were exports.
