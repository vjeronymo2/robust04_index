930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / International Company News: Kelsey-Hayes joins forces with Japanese group KELSEY-HAYES, a unit of Varity, the automotive parts, farm equipment and diesel engine maker, and Topy Industries, the Japanese engineering group, are to set up a joint venture to develop, sell and distribute four-wheel anti-lock brake systems in Japan and other Asian markets, Reuter reports from Buffalo. The joint-venture company will be named Topy/Kelsey-Hayes and each company will hold a 50 per cent stake. The venture is exclusively licensed in Japan and certain other countries to sell Buffalo-based Kelsey-Hayes' latest EBC 10 family of anti-lock brake systems for use on vehicles which are manufactured in those countries.
