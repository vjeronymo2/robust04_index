930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / International Company News: Ahold raises retail holdings in Portugal AHOLD, the Dutch retail and supermarket group, said yesterday its Portuguese joint venture, Jeronimo Martins Retail (JMR), planned to take full control of a large Portuguese supermarket chain, Ino Supermercados, writes David Brown in Amsterdam. The deal will add 54 supermarkets and annual sales of some Dollars 200m to the JMR joint venture, which presently consists of 40 Pingo Doce chain stores with annual sales of over Dollars 300m. JMR now enjoys a 10 per cent market share in Portuguese food retailing. An Ahold spokesman said the terms of the deal would be finalised and disclosed 'within several weeks'.
