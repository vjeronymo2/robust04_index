BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 14 Jun -- The latest meeting of delegations from Georgia, South and North Ossetia, and representatives of the CSCE, aimed at continuing the search for peace and reconciliation, which began after the dialogue on 12 May, took place in Vladikavkaz today on the initiative of the CSCE mission in Georgia. The parties agreed in particular on the need to carry out joint work to stabilize the situation in the conflict zone, to fight organized crime, to restore road and rail traffic between Tskhinvali and other towns, to begin cooperating on economic revival, and to restore housing and the appropriate infrastructure. The meeting participants agreed to help keep peace and law and order in the conflict zone by means of observation by the CSCE mission. There are proposals for the practical and legal basis to be created jointly for the speedy return of refugees and for an appeal to be made to the peacekeeping forces for the preparation of the necessary conditions to guarantee the refugees safety and also for enlisting the support of the UN High Commissioner for Refugee Affairs.
