910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Appointments: Board moves at Aegis Group AEGIS GROUP has made the following appointments: Mr Charles Hochman, a director, becomes an executive deputy chairman; Mr Frank Law, a non-executive director of Aegis, and chairman of The Varta Group, becomes a non-executive deputy chairman; Mr John L. Vogelstein, vice chairman of EM Warburg, Pincus & Co Inc, a major shareholder in Aegis, replaces on the board the current Warburg Pincus representative Miss Barbara Manfrey. ***** Mr Henry Reece has been appointed managing director of Pitman Publishing, part of LONGMAN GROUP. He will succeed Mr Anthony Forbes Watson in July when Mr Forbes Watson move to Ladybird Books as managing director on the retirement of Mr Malcolm Kelley. Mr Reece moved from Allyn & Bacon, where he was responsible for all business publishing, to Pitman Publishing last February to develop the new Financial Times business books series.
