BFN [Text] Eyewitnesses have confirmed that the head of the ruling regime in Sanaa, 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, has personally executed a number of his military commanders and officers whom he had sent to the battlefronts, particularly those whose forces were routed under the blows of the forces of the young Democratic Republic of Yemen. The eyewitnesses, who arrived in Aden from Sanaa, said that the criminal, 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, had executed a number of commanders of the fronts in Kharaz, Karish, Tawr al-Bahah and Hadramawt. The eyewitnesses also confirmed that the fundamentalist, Colonel 'Ali Muhsin al-Ahmar, and the criminal Muhammad Abdallah Salih, 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih's brother, have executed many of the commanders and officers of the units providing supplies, provisions and communications after their failure to provide their units with the necessary supplies for the fighting, which was thought to last less than 24 hours.
