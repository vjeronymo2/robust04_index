Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman (J.T.) -- The Islamic Action Front's (IAF) executive office Thursday [6 January] selected Ishaq al-Farhan as its secretary-general, and Dr. 'Abd-al-Latif 'Arabiyat as deputy secretary-general. The office also selected Muhammad 'Uwaydah and Ahmad Tanash, as administrative and financial assistants respectively. The executive office also accepted the resignation of 'Abd-al-Rahim al-'Ukur, who was elected in absentia as member of the executive office. The office named Ziyad Khalifah, the first alternate member, as Mr. al-'Ukur's replacement.
