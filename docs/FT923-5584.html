920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Smaller companies index falls The fall in smaller company shares accelerated this week. The County smaller Companies Index fell 2.1 per cent to 798.71 over the seven days to August 27; the Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) also fell 2.1 per cent to 1004.73 over the same period. The indices have fallen by around 23 per cent over the last three months.
