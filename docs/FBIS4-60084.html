BFN [Excerpt] On the occasion of the auspicious anniversary of the 'Id al-Adha and 'Id al-Ghadir, His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the eminent leader of the Islamic revolution and the guardian of the cause of Muslims, pardoned or reduced the sentences of 2,289 persons convicted by Islamic revolution, general, and military courts throughout the country. Ayatollah Yazdi, head of the Judicial Power, requested a pardon or reduction in the sentences of the aforementioned convicted persons and the eminent leader agreed with his request. [passage omitted]
