BFN [Unattributed report: "The Crimea: New Replacements"] [Text] On the eve of the last session of the Crimean Supreme Council of old convocation that was held on 12 April under N. Bagrov's chairmanship, Ukrainian Radio programs were interrupted and the speech by Vyacheslav Lebedev, press secretary of the Crimean president, was broadcast live. He said that, by edict of [Crimean president] Yu. Meshkov, Minister of Internal Affairs of the Crimean Republic Oleksandr Plyuta had been dismissed from his post and replaced by Major General Valeriy Kuznetsov. Chairman of Ukraine's Security Service for the Crimean Republic Ivan Kolomytsev was also dismissed and replaced by Vladimir Lepikhov. Ministerial appointments were also made: Lyudmila Zaytseva became minister of finance and Andrey Chernyavskiy of economy. The Presidium of the Crimean Supreme Council adopted a decision to hold a session of the newly elected parliament on 10 May 1994. The commission for the election of the Crimean people's deputies was authorized to take upon itself the duties of the district electoral commission for Ukrainian presidential elections. N. Bagrov pointed out that the appointment of Crimean power ministers, without a coordination with corresponding Ukrainian departments, was illegal.
