920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / International Company News: Drugs group forecast ROUSSEL Uclaf, the French drugs group, said it expects 1992 net attributable profit to be about FFr660m (Dollars 124.2m), up from FFr598m in 1991, Reuter reports. Turnover should reach FFr15.55bn, from FFr14.35bn. The profit rise between the two years should be 30 per cent the company said.
