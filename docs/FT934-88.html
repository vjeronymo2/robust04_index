931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / Number of new businesses rises The number of new businesses has risen for the first time since 1989, figures from Barclays Bank show. Based on data from its branchs, Barclays believes that 409,000 enterprises started in 1993, an increase of 13 per cent on last year. The figures show 2.37m UK businesses are now operating. The figures, which cover many small enterprises not accounted for by other surveys, show that a total of 515,000 businesses closed this year - 6 per cent down on last year.
