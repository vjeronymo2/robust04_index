941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Albion falls Annual profits at Albion, the Belfast-based men's wear company, fell from Pounds 1.09m to Pounds 620,420 pre-tax. The company blamed tighter margins and exceptional costs of Pounds 273,970 to cover closures and the write-down of its Hungarian investment. Turnover for the year to September 30 rose to Pounds 25.6m (Pounds 21.6m). Earnings were 13.6p (20.2p) and a reduced final dividend of 1.6p is proposed for a total of 3.2p (4p).
