920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Avonmore Avonmore Foods has established a joint venture in Hungary which has paid IPounds 504,000 (Pounds 474,000) to purchase the assets of a dairy business north-east of Budapest. Avonmore has taken 75 per cent with the remainder held by Nograd Tej Kft. Significant development of the liquid milk business was planned.
