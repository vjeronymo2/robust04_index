930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Tribune Group cuts deficit TRIBUNE GROUP, the Dublin-based newspaper publisher, reported a pre-tax loss of IPounds 328,000 (Pounds 328,400) in the six months to December 31, against IPounds 739,000 in the half year to the end of September. Turnover was IPounds 3.91m with losses per share of 21.84p. In the comparative period turnover was IPounds 3.63m with losses of 28.4p. Interest payable was IPounds 318,000 (IPounds 152,000) because of high interest rates and the level of debt. Mr Gordon Colleary, chairman, said the company was seeking new partners to cut debt and allow the Sunday Tribune to develop.
