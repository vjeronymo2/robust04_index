941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Bombardier to provide light transit system for Kuala Lumpur Bombardier, the Canadian aerospace and transport equipment group, and SNC-Lavalin, Canada's biggest engineering group, will provide a CDollars 961m (Dollars 717m) 30km, 25-station light transit system for Kuala Lumpur, for completion in 1998. Bombardier-SNC will build 70 vehicles and be responsible for system controls and rails. Renong of Malaysia will build the glideway and stations. Bombardier-SNC's share of the total contract is almost CDollars 600m. The transport system will be based on Vancouver's Skytrain.
