BFN [Text] Beijing, June 12 KYODO -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa arrived in Beijing from Seoul Sunday to seek cooperation from China on the North Korean nuclear issue. Kakizawa is scheduled to hold talks with his Chinese counterpart Qian Qichen. In Seoul, Kakizawa and his South Korean counterpart Han Sung-chu confirmed that planned U.N. Security Council sanctions against North Korea over its refusal to allow full inspection of its nuclear facilities are aimed at encouraging Pyongyang to hold a dialogue for a negotiated solution, Japanese officials said.
