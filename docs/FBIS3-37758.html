Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Belarus Is Planning To Trade Weapons"] [Text] According to governmental circles, it is not excluded that Belarus will trade weapons in the nearest future. In any case, during the negotiations, Belarusian and Latvian Prime Ministers Vyacheslav Kebich and Valdis Birkavs discussed and agreed on the transit of Belarusian weapons through Latvian territory to its seaports.
