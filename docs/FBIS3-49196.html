Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Sarajevo, fierce fighting continued this morning in the Grbanica district. Heavy fighting is reported round the old Jewish cemetery to the south of the city center. Mortar explosions, heavy artillery shells and tracer fire pounded the area on the Serb-Muslim frontline. According to Sarajevo hospitals, 27 persons were killed and 87 wounded in the fighting in the last few days.
