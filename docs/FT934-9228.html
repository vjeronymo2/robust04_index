931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: TR Property Pounds 10m debenture TR Property Investment Trust is to issue, via its subsidiary Trust Union Finance (1991), Pounds 10m of debenture stock 2008 by way of a placing, payable in full on November 17 1993. The issue has been fully underwritten by NatWest Capital Markets, acting as agent for National Westminster Bank, which is also acting as sole placing agent. The stock will be guaranteed by TR Property Investment Trust and the directors said it was intended that the proceeds of the placing would be used to repay existing unsecured debt of the group and its subsidiaries.
