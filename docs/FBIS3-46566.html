Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [New Year message from 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, president of the Islamic Republic of Iran, to DPRK President Kim Il-song--read by announcer] [Text] Pyongyang Your Excellency DPRK President Kim Il-song: I extend my New Year's greetings to Your Excellency and the DPRK people. I wish that all countries actively cooperate in the struggle to dispel oppression, achieve justice, and salvage mankind. I pray for Your Excellency's happiness and success in the New Year. [Signed] 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, president of the Islamic Republic of Iran [Dated] 28 December 1993, Tehran
