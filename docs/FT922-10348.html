920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Kiwifruit is this week's best fruit buy at 12-18p each reports the FFVIB. Spania strawberries are at 55-65p a Dollars lb, bananas at 49-58p a lb and Cape grapes at 90p-Pounds 1.00 a lb are also good buys. There are many different varieties of apples on the market including Cape Golden Delicious at 60-70p a lb and Chilean Granny Smith at 50-60p a lb. Dutch white cabbage at 20-35p a lb is excellent this week, along with English spinach at 40-60p a lb. Spring greens at 30-35p a lb with Dutch baby carrots at 35-40p a lb pre-pack. British cucumbers are at 40-55p each, tomatoes at 50-60p a lb and Iceberg lettuce at 55-65p each.
