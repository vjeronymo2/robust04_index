Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] At Saint-Ouen, site since last Tuesday [18 January] of the 28th Congress of the French Communist Party, a new generation has taken over. The National Committee has made its choice, and as Alain Bocquet, MP [member of parliament] for the Nord [department] has just announced, it chose Robert Hue. Many people think that there will be no change. Robert Hue is known as a man of the Party apparatus, whose rise is relatively recent. [passage omitted] Robert Hue had been a member of the political bureau since the last Party congress in 1990. He is; nevertheless, an old communist, having joined the Party at 18, in 1964. [passage omitted] He is mayor of Montigny-les-Cormeilles [in the Val d'Oise department].
