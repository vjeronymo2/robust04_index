930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Property Part'ships advances 17.5% Property Partnerships, which develops commercial properties for investment and operates hotels, lifted pre-tax profits by 17.5 per cent, from Pounds 1.76m to Pounds 2.07m, in the year to March 31. The result was achieved on turnover slightly ahead at Pounds 6.37m (Pounds 6.24m), of which Pounds 2.09m (Pounds 1.91m) was net rental income and Pounds 4.28m (Pounds 4.33m) from hotels. A final dividend of 4.7p lifts the total to 7.25p (7p), covered twice by earnings of 14.63p (13p) per share.
