BFN [Text] Today, Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov issued edicts on appointing the republic's plenipotentiary representatives to Moscow and Russia and on opening Crimea's permanent mission in Krasnodar Kray. According to ITAR-TASS information, Meshkov also signed an edict on setting up a space information agency in Crimea. The leadership of the republic's parliament has adopted a decision on setting up a supreme economic council attached to the presidium of the Supreme Soviet.
