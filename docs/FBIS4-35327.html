BFN [Text] Aden radio broadcast a statement this morning by a responsible source in the Democratic Republic of Yemen -- which has been declared in the south -- denying Sanaa's assertion that officials in the Yemeni Socialist Party have proposed an unconditional cease-fire in Yemen. The source declared that Aden insists on two basic conditions for a cease-fire. These are the withdrawal of all northern forces to positions behind the previous borders between the two parts of Yemen, and the holding of direct talks with the Aden government.
