PHYSICIAN PAYMENT REVIEW COMMISSION Commission Meeting AGENCY: Physician Payment Review Commission. ACTION: Notice of meeting.

SUMMARY: The Commission will hold its next public meeting on Thursday, September 22, 1994, and Friday, September 23, 1994 at the Embassy Suites Downtown Hotel, 1250 22d Street NW., Washington, DC, in the Consulate Room. The meetings are tentatively scheduled to begin at 9 a.m. each day. Among the topics to be discussed are the changing structure of the health care market, Medicaid managed care, adjustments to the AAPCC, antitrust issues related to joint physician behavior and laws affecting relationships between physicians and managed care organizations, risk adjustment, the effect of Medicare relative price changes on site of service, and an overview of the Commission's 1994&hyph;95 workplan. Several other topics may be added to the final agenda, which will be available on September 16, 1994.
ADDRESS: Please note that the Commission has a new address: 2120 L Street, NW./Suite 200/Washington, DC 20037. The telephone number is the same: 202/653&hyph;7220.
FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Lauren LeRoy, Deputy Director, or Annette Hennessey, Executive Assistant, at 202/653&hyph;7220. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Agendas for the meeting will be available on Friday, September 16, 1994 and will be mailed out at that time. To receive an agenda, please direct all requests to the receptionist at 202/653&hyph;7220. Paul B. Ginsburg, Executive Director.
