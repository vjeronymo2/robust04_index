940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Avon managers more confident Companies in Avon are more confident about economic prospects, says a survey of 150 managing directors in the county. Three out of five of the companies surveyed have launched new products or services in the past three months or plan to do so this year, and 54 per cent said they intended to expand into new markets. But the survey, by the law firm Burges Salmon on behalf of Bristol Chamber of Commerce and Initiative, found nearly a third of directors believed the economy was still in recession or that it was too soon to tell whether it was emerging from recession.
