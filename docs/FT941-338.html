940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Persona valued at Pounds 19.3 for placing Persona Group, a distributor of personal computer network and communications products, is coming to the market through a placing at 160p a share, valuing the company at Pounds 19.3m. The placing is sponsored by Beeson Gregory. Some 8.4m shares are being placed, 6.8m on behalf of existing shareholders and 1.6m on behalf of the company. Some Pounds 1.98m is being raised for the company. At the placing price the price earnings multiple is 14.4; the notional net annual dividend is 3.84p and the notional gross dividend yield at the placing price is three per cent.
