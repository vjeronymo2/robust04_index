Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 14 (KCNA) -- Jose Ramon Rodriguez Varona, new Cuban Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea, today presented his credentials to the great leader President Kim Il-song. Present at the presentation ceremony were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and officials of the Cuban Embassy in Pyongyang. President Kim Il-song received credentials before conversing with the ambassador.
