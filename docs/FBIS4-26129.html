BFN [Text] Beijing, June 18 (XINHUA) -- Estonian President Lennart Meri and his wife left here tonight for home, winding up his seven-day state visit to China. Among those who saw them off at the airport was Liu Songjin, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice-minister of communications. Meri returned to Beijing this afternoon from south China's Guangzhou, the capital city of Guangdong Province. During his one-day stay in Guangzhou, Meri met with Zhong Qiquan, vice-governor of Guangdong Province, and visited state and rural collectively-owned enterprises.
