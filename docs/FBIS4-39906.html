BFN [Text] The committee for international affairs of the Russian State Duma has expressed concern over the adoption by the House of Representatives of the U.S. Congress of a resolution in favor of the unilateral lifting of embargo on arms supplies to Bosnian Muslims. The statement adopted by the lower chamber of the Russian parliament notes that such a measure would lead to an escalation of conflict in southern Yugoslavia [as heard] and undermine the current peace process. In this situation the committee sees it as its obligation to reaffirm a recommendation to the president and the government of the Russian Federation to study the question of taking practical measures to lift unjustified sanctions imposed on the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia.
