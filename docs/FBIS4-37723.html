BFN [Text] Cairo, 22 Jun (MENA) -- President Husni Mubarak this morning received Slovak Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan, who is currently visiting Cairo. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa; Ambassador Mushirah Khattab, Egypt's ambassador to Slovakia; Ambassador Jan Borieh [name as transliterated], Slovakia's ambassador in Cairo; and Mr. Ivan Borkosh, director of the Middle East desk at the Slovak Foreign Ministry [name and title as received]. Slovak Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan stated after the meeting that he is very happy that President Mubarak received him today at the beginning of his visit to Egypt. He said he held fruitful discussions with the president regarding the possibilities and means of bolstering cooperation between Egypt and Slovakia. He added that we will exploit the good political intentions of both sides to strengthen and bolster this cooperation. He said it was a pleasure to convey to President Mubarak the invitation from the Slovak president to officially visit our country. The president has accepted the invitation and the date will be fixed at the appropriate time. The Slovak foreign minister concluded his statement by expressing his hopes that the discussions he held with President Mubarak today would yield some positive results in future relations between Egypt and Slovakia. He noted that his discussions with the president today on this topic were very encouraging. The Slovak foreign minister arrived in Cairo yesterday evening on a visit to last several days.
