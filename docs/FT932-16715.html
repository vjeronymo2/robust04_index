930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Former Nalgo chief dies MR Geoffrey Drain, general secretary of the National and Local Government Officers' Association from 1973-83, was found dead yesterday at his home in London. Mr Drain was a member of the TUC general council from 1973-83, a director of the Bank of England from 1978-86, and a Freeman of the City of London. He was made a CBE in 1981.
