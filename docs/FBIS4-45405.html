BFN [Unattributed report under the "Crime" rubric: "Colonel Sold Drugs"] [Text] A colonel of a Moscow military unit has been arrested by militiamen at the Podmoskovye Hotel, Podolsk, during the sale of a large batch of drugs. The officer was attempting to sell 52 ampoules and nine tablets of promidol, eight ampoules of butrenorphine, and 88 ampoules of morphine hydrochloride. During a search of his personal belongings $100, 110 German marks, and over one million rubles were discovered.
