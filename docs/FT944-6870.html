941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Telstra announces corporate service Telstra, the telecommunications group owned by the Australian government, said yesterday that it plans to launch an international telephone service for UK-based corporate customers. Telstra acquired a licence to operate in the domestic UK market in April. At the time it said it would offer a range of domestic voice and data services, and resale services to Australia. However it now plans a broader international call service aimed at corporations and financial institutions.
