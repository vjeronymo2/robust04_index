December 15, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

ALL-ROUND EFFORT BY IWAMASA LEADS EDISON PAST LOARA

Melanie Iwamasa scored 22 points and had 14 rebounds and eight assists as Edison High School defeated Loara, 66-55, Friday in the fifth-place game of Savanna girls' basketball tournament.

Erika Miller scored 19 points and Laura Vinch 10 for Edison (8-2).

Tracy Titus had 28 points and 14 rebounds for Loara (4-4).

In other Savanna tournament games:

Ocean View 67, Torrey Pines 50 -- Kristen McClustey scored 16 points for Ocean View.

Mission Viejo 47, Trabuco Hills 35 -- Kelly McGlothlin scored 17 points, and Kendra Okura added 15 for Mission Viejo (4-3).

In El Dorado tournament:

Canyon Country Canyon 57, El Dorado 49 -- Canyon outscored El Dorado, 26-11, in the fourth quarter as Cyna Schuster scored 10 of her 21 points, and Katrina Musingo scored nine of her 15 points in the quarter in a semifinal game.

Briana Yoshina had 17 points, and Erin Vessey had 11 for El Dorado (2-2).

In the Santa Ana Valley tournament:

University 47, Inglewood 28 -- Tara Wolff scored 18 points for University (3-4) in a consolation bracket game.
