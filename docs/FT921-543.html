920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / Finance & The Family (At A Glance): Smaller companies fall again Small company shares fell again this week in the midst of pre-election gloom for stock markets. The Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version) dropped 1.4 per cent from 1179.63 on March 19 to 1163.53 on March 26 and the County Small Companies Index fell 1.3 per cent from 937.3 to 925.28 over the same period.
