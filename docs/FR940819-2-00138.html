SUMMARY: In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (title 44, U.S. Code, chapter 35), this notice announces a request for reclearance of an information collection. Form RI 92&hyph;22, Annuity Supplement Earnings Report, is used to annually obtain the amount of personal earnings from annuity supplement recipients to determine if there should be a reduction in benefits paid to the annuitant. Approximately 1,500 RI 92&hyph;22 forms are completed annually. The form requires approximately 15 minutes to complete. The annual burden is 375 hours. For copies of this proposal, contact C. Ronald Trueworthy on (703) 908&hyph;8550. DATES: Comments on this proposal should be received on or before September 19, 1994.
