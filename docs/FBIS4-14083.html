BFN [Text] The former president of Afghanistan, Professor Sebghatollah Mojaddedi, called on the president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, in Islamabad today. During the meeting, he held wide-ranging discussions with the president. Speaking of prospects of peace in Afghanistan, President Leghari emphasized that this will have to be an indigenous process, as anything imposed from outside would never last. The president also expressed the hope that developed countries would not wait for complete peace to begin reconstruction work in Afghanistan. Prof. Mojaddedi thanked the president for his noble sentiments and affirmed that he would make every effort to bring to an end the fratricidal struggle in his country.
