BFN [Text] Paris, June 22 (AFP) -- Singapore Airlines ordered 10 more A340-300 long-range Airbus planes and took out options for another 20 on Wednesday [22 June], the European consortium Airbus Industrie announced. The total order for the 30 planes would be worth 5.4 billion dollars. The airline, which already ordered seven A340's in December 1992, will thus become Airbus Industrie's biggest customer with 37 of this very long-range model. The planes ordered Wednesday will be delivered starting in spring 1996.
