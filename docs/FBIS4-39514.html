BFN [Text] Ukraine will support the UN decision on sanctions against North Korea due to its refusal to allow IAEA experts to its nuclear sites. If the decision is taken, we shall support it, the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry spokesman Yuriy Sergeyev told a briefing in Kiev Tuesday [7 June]. He said that as a UN member, Ukraine is obliged to implement and is implementing all decisions of this international organization. He cited Ukraine's position concerning UN sanctions against Yugoslavia.
