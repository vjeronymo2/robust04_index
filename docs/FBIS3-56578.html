Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [All Serbian names and titles as published] [Text] Yerevan, 18 Jan (ARMENPRES)--Arkadiy Gukasyan, the foreign minister of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR], returned today from a visit to the conflict zone in the former Yugoslavia. The purpose of the visit was to gather first-hand information on the UN peacekeeping force's operations in the region. The NKR Foreign Ministry reported that Gukasyan met with First Deputy Prime Minister of Serbia Danilo Markovic in Belgrade and with Serbian President Radovan Karadzic and Foreign Minister Aleksa Bukha in Pale. They exchanged views on ways to achieve a peaceful settlement similar to the ones existing in the territory of the former Yugoslavia and Nagorno-Karabakh.
