September 7, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

THE TIMES EXPANDS BUSINESS COVERAGE IN SAN DIEGO COUNTY

Beginning today, The Times expands its San Diego business coverage by adding a second day of special pages for San Diego County each Thursday. New, regular features will focus on products developed by San Diego firms, personality profiles, and updates on key issues affecting the local business community. The San Diego County business pages also are published each Tuesday.
