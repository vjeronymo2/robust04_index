November 13, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LOCAL BOY: Kevin Costner attended Villa Park High School and Cal State Fullerton, then left for Hollywood to become a star ("Bull Durham," "Field of Dreams"). Now he's back as a first-time director, and Orange County is giving him his biggest crowds. . . . Orion Pictures reports that Costner's "Dances With Wolves," which opened in 14 theaters nationwide on Friday, grossed $79,001 in three days at the Newport Cinema, the biggest turnout nationally (F1). Steve Emmons
