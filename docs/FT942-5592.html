940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Kalamazoo shows surge to Pounds 6.36m Kalamazoo, the computer services and printed systems group, achieved a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 940,000 to Pounds 6.36m for the year to end-March. Sales rose by Pounds 4.4m to Pounds 60.9m. Earnings were 10.2p (losses 0.6p) and a proposed final dividend of 2.45p makes a 3.3p (2.1p) total.
