Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] On Sunday night [as heard], a pylon of a 110 kv high-tension power line between Broceni and Skrunda, 3 km from the Skrunda radar station, was blown up. An investigation into the incident is currently under way. The explosives used have not been identified yet. The Skrunda radar station is still receiving power via another power line from Tukums. Valdis Lidumnieks, head of the Broceni substation group of the high-tension power network, reports that the power supply via the damaged line will be resumed today. The press center of the Northwestern Group of [Russian] Forces circulated a statement today saying that a joint commission of representatives of Latvenergo, the criminal police, and the Skrunda radar station assessed the explosion as an act of sabotage. A rapid reaction group from the Ministry of Internal Affairs, headed by Gunars Liepins, the senior investigator of the Latvian prosecutor's office for particularly serious (?cases), has left for the scene of the incident.
