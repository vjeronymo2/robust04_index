BFN [Article by JCH/RHR: "Two Bomb Attacks Against Banks in Luxembourg"] [Text] Luxembourg, 12 Apr (BELGA) -- During the night from Monday to Tuesday, two banks in the Grand-Duchy of Luxembourg were damaged by explosions, the legal authorities revealed Tuesday. Nobody was injured. At approximately 0040 GMT on Tuesday morning, an explosive charge went off in front of the entrance to the Caisse Rurale in Bous, which is situated approximately 12 km from the capital, near the German-Luxembourg border. One hour later, at approximately 0200 GMT, a second explosive charge went off outside the entrance to a branch of the Caisse d'Epargne de l'Etat in Bridel, in the center of the country, about 10 km away from Luxembourg. The door and a few windows were blown to pieces. Nobody was injured. No witnesses noticed whether there had been one or more terrorists at the site of the explosions. The explosives used were stolen from a quarry one week ago. Nobody has claimed responsibility for the attacks. The special branch of the gendarmerie and the crime squad are conducting an inquiry.
