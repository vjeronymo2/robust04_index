940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / People: CS First Boston James Leigh-Pemberton, the investment banker son of the former governor of the Bank of England, has unexpectedly quit SG Warburg after 17 years to join CS First Boston as a managing director. He will head the European equity syndicate desk. The news came as a surprise to colleagues who believed that Leigh-Pemberton, 37, who joined the firm as a graduate trainee, was being groomed for the top. 'It's a bad loss,' says a Warburg insider.
