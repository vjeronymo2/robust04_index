BFN [Text] Maputo, April 15 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese Red Cross Committee today granted 20,000 U.S. dollars to the Mozambican National Committee of disaster prevention and resistance. Chinese Ambassador to Mozambique Mi Shiheng, on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross, handed over the cheque to deputy director of the Mozambican committee Oldemiro Baloe. Mi said at the hand-over ceremony that the grant is a response to Mozambique's appeal for emergent aid for Nampula Province, northern Mozambique, where people have suffered great losses from tornado and storms. Meanwhile, Baloi expressed thanks for the Chinese grant.
