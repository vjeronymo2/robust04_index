Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian president's special representative Igor Ivanov has had a meeting in Tunis with the Palestinian leader Yasir 'Arafat. He handed him a personal message from Boris Yeltsin. The message expresses Russia's firm intention to further the achievement of peace in the Middle East. After the talks were concluded, the Russian diplomat set off for Israel. Igor Ivanov spoke of Russia's support for the Palestinian proposal to ensure an international presence in the territories occupied by Israel.
