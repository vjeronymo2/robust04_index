921124 FT 24 NOV 92 / Equality body to petition Lords THE Equal Opportunities Commission is to petition the House of Lords for leave to appeal against the Court of Appeal's ruling that it may not pursue judicial review proceedings against the employment secretary. It will also seek leave to appeal against the Appeal Court's judgment that the government is not discriminating against part-time workers by requiring them to work three years longer than full-timers to qualify for redundancy and unfair dismissal rights, or by excluding them if they work fewer than eight hours a week.
