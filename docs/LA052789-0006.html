May 27, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ERROR JORDAN: REASON FOR THE SUCCESS OF BULLS DESERVED AN MVP TIE

In reference to Magic Johnson winning the MVP over Michael Jordan: Give me a break.

Jordan was ahead of Tragic in scoring, field-goal percentage, steals and blocked shots.

Johnson had Jordan beat in free-throw percentage, assists and turnovers.

And where would the Bulls be without Jordan? No better than the Clippers.

Where would the Lakers be without Tragic? Still in the playoffs.

The real MVP was Jordan. Ask the players.

JEFF FEUER

Reseda
