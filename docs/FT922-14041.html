920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Bourne End Bourne End Properties reported losses sharply reduced, from Pounds 2.21m to Pounds 1.42m pre-tax, over the 1991 year. Directors have decreased the book value of the portfolio leading to an overall reduction in net asset value from 159p to 103p per share. Losses per share fell from 26.4p to 13.7p. The interim dividend was omitted but a proposed final of 1p compares with the previous year's total of 2p.
