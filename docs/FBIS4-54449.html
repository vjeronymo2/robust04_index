BFN [Text] Prishtina, April 27 (ATA) -- The Serbian military forces increased recently and the situation is became tense in the Presheva commune after the Serbian Army killed Naim Bajrami, 26, in the Kosova-Macedonia border. For this reason the Albanian Party for Democratic Activity, based in Presheva, appeals to all the citizens, especially the inhabitants of the bordering villages, to be careful and avoid the provocations which might be escalated in a violence of wide dimensions. The doctor of the Council for Human and Freedom Rights has ascertained that the victim Bajrami has been killed from behind and has been tortured.
