930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / Khmer Rouge dilemma The Khmer Rouge would give up the 20 per cent of Cambodia it controls in return for a role in the newly-elected government, Prince Norodom Sihanouk said yesterday, AP reports from Phnom Penh. But, said Prince Sihanouk, the head of state, such a move would lead to Cambodia being denied foreign aid. He noted that the US had said it would not provide assistance to Cambodia if the Khmer Rouge was given a role in the administration.
