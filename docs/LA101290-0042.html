October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Cary Gott was named executive vice president, wineries and vineyards, of Seagram Classics Wine Co., San Mateo.

Gott, formerly president of Seagram's Monterey Vineyard, will also be responsible for Sterling Vineyards and Mumm Napa Valley. Winemakers Bill Dyer of Sterling and Greg Fowler of Mumm were named vice presidents. Seagram Classics will move its headquarters to Sterling Vineyards in the Napa Valley.
