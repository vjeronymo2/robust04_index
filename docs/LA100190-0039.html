October 1, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

REUNIFICATION: Germans and German-Americans will be doing a lot of celebrating this week, observing the reunification Wednesday of East and West Germany. . . . On that day, some members of the Phoenix Club in Anaheim will gather for a festive luncheon, while others will join a larger group at the Los Angeles Music Center. "It's a fantastic day for us," says club President Hans Klein. On Oct. 6, German-American Day, Mayor Walter Momper of West Berlin will address the club in Anaheim. Mark I. Pinsky and Sonni Efron
