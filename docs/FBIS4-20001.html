BFN [Text] Stuttgart (DPA) -- PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat will visit Stuttgart on 2 May (Monday) at the invitation of Edzard Reuter, chairman of the board of directors of Daimler-Benz AG. According to an announcement made by the corporation on Friday [22 April], talks with the board of directors are scheduled. The Baden-Wuerttemberg land government confirmed that Minister President Erwin Teufel (Christian Democratic Union) plans to receive the PLO chairman for an exchange of views on the peace process in the Middle East. Since Reuter's visit to Israel in September 1992, Daimler-Benz has been intensively working on expanding economic relations with the entire Middle East region. At the beginning of the year Daimler opened a representation in Tel Aviv and a liaison office in the Egyptian capital of Cairo.
