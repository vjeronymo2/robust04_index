Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The Russian Procuracy General has deemed as not corresponding to reality the information on a coup d'etat allegedly in preparation, which was published in the OBSHCHAYA GAZETA and disturbed public peace. Taking into account the fact that the actions of the unidentified persons, who had sent the above information to the OBSHCHAYA GAZETA editorial office, can be characterized as crime carrying liability for slander accompanied by accusation of a state crime, the Procuracy General instituted criminal proceedings on the basis of this fact today.
