Finalization 94P60158A Beijing ZHONGGUO DIANZI BAO [CHINA ELECTRONICS NEWS] in Chinese 7 Feb 94 p 3 94P60158A Beijing ZHONGGUO DIANZI BAO [CHINA ELECTRONICS NEWS] Chinese CSO [Article by Zheng Zhaosen [6774 0340 2773]: ``808-nm-Wavelength, 980-nm-Wavelength Quantum Well Laser Diodes''] [Summary] The 808-nm-wavelength high-power AlGaAs quantum well laser diode and 980-nm-wavelength InGaAs/AlGaAs strained-layer quantum well laser diode developed by MEI's Institute 13 as pump sources for solid state lasers recently passed design finalization. The former laser diode is a key device used in all-solid-state YAG lasers, while the latter is a key device for erbium-doped fiber optical amplifiers. Both achievements were listed as Eighth FYP Key State Projects and 863 Plan key projects.
