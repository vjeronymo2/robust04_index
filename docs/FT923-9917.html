920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Olympic News In Brief: Angola basketball win Angola earned its first victory in men's Olympic basketball with an 83-63 win over Spain. The African champions had played well in all their games in their first Olympic appearance except for an opening-game 68-point loss to the US. The win over the host country kept alive quarter-final hopes for both teams as well as Brazil, with one of the three moving on from the six-team pool play.
