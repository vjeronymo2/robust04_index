931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / Construction Contracts: Upgrading printing facility A consortium of SULZER INFRA (UK) and two TRAFALGAR HOUSE CONSTRUCTION companies, Trollope & Colls and Rashleigh Phipps, has been awarded a contract worth about Pounds 25m to refurbish the Bank of England's printing works at Loughton, Essex. The three companies will carry out an extensive modernisation of the buildings, services and finishes at the 1950s complex. The two-year project will also include some reorganisation of the buildings to reflect modern production technology.
