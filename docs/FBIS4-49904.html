BFN [Text] Vienna, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Austrian Chancellor Franz Vranitzky today said he will actively support the further development of the relations between Austria and China. Vranitzky made the remarks at a meeting with a municipal delegation from the Chinese capital city Beijing led by its Mayor Li Qiyan. Vranitzky said his successful visit to Beijing last year deeply impressed him, adding that the development of good relationship and economic cooperation between the two countries have been greatly boosted by the exchange of visits between the two countries' leaders. The Austrian chancellor also expressed his support for further cooperation between the two countries' capital cities. The Beijing delegation arrived here on May 8 at the invitation of Vienna Mayor Helmut Zilk. The two sides today signed two documents to increase bilateral exchange of information in the health and cultural fields.
