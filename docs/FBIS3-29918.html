Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- South Koreans held protests last Saturday against projected deployment of Patriot missiles in South Korea, a foreign news report from Seoul said. The demonstrators watched two actors perform a parody of the U.S.-South Korean "alliance," wearing cartoon masks of Uncle Sam and the traitor Kim Yong-sam. They attacked the "alliance" as an obstacle to Korean reunification, sang "Yankee Go Home" and chanted "Don't import weapons" and "No to Team Spirit."
