BFN [Excerpt] Mr. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-Attas, the prime minister, had a meeting yesterday [7 June] evening with His Excellency Shaykh Zayid Bin Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the head of state of the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. During the meeting, the prime minsiter acquainted His Excellency Shaykh Sayid Bin Sultan on the latest developments of the situation in Yemen. He also explained to him the aims of his current tour of a number of Arab countries concerned with solving the Yemeni crisis. During the meeting, His Excellency Shaykh Zayid Bin Sultan Al Nuhayyan expressed his sorrow for the continuation of the fighting and the danger of the escalation of the tragic situation in Yemen. He also expressed his hope that the Sanaa rulers will respond to the Security Council resolution which stipulates an immediate cease-fire and puts an end to the bloodshed in Yemen. [passage omitted]
