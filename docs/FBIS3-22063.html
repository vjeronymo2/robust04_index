Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hanoi Youth Union implemented anti-drug addiction work outlined in the Planning No. 39 of the youth union Central Committee with some initial results. It coordinated with the Women's Union and the War Veteran Association to encourage 84 people to stop using drugs either at home, in drug centers, or in groups in the three pilot subwards of Tinh Quang and Cat Linh of Dong Da Precinct and Thanh Duong of Hai Ba Trung Precinct. Apart from material assistance, the youth union also contacted economic production units to provide jobs for the former drug users.
