BFN [Text] The mayor of the Hungarian capital has stated that friendly ties of cooperation between Havana and Budapest, which many thought long lost in the wake of changes in Eastern Europe, can be revitalized and even expanded in the future. This statement was made during a meeting with Cuban Ambassador to Hungary Carlos Trejo Sosa. The Hungarian mayor added that a visit of his Cuban counterpart to Budapest would greatly contribute to establishing real possibilities for cooperation between the two cities in the areas of environmental protection, transport, and others.
