940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / International Company News: Hakuhodo ends US joint venture Hakuhodo, Japan's second largest advertising agency, yesterday dissolved a 34-year-old Tokyo-based joint venture with McCann-Erickson of the US, writes Paul Abrahams in Tokyo. The Japanese group said the joint venture, the country's ninth biggest agency, had started to compete with its parent, and the company has handed over its 49 per cent stake in the venture to McCann-Erickson.
