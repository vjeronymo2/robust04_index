940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: RMC Dollars 33m buy RMC, the world's largest concrete producer, has paid Dollars 33.1m (Pounds 21.7m) to acquire the plant which supplied cement for the construction of the Panama Canal and for the rebuilding of San Francisco following the 1906 earthquake. The cement works at Santa Cruz, California, was previously owned by Lonestar Industries of the US and leased to RMC Lonestar, in which RMC owns 50 per cent.
