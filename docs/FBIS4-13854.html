BFN [Text] Tehran, April 23, IRNA -- Minister of Economy and Financial Affairs Mohammad Khan arrived in Washington Friday to take part in the annual session of the World Bank (WB) and the International Monetary Fund (IMF). Mohammad Khan will also take part in several other meetings and sub-commissions on the sidelines of the IMF-WB joint meeting. Governor of the Central Bank of Iran Mohammad Huseyn Adeli and a group of experts of Economy Ministry are accompanying the minister in his trip.
