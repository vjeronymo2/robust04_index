930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / International Company News: Unit Trust of India UNIT TRUST of India, India's largest mutual fund group, is to launch its India Liberalisation Fund in the US, writes R. C. Murthy in Bombay. This will be the third country fund, slated for launch in August-September, after a gap of more than four years. The first, the sterling-denominated India Fund, was floated from Guernsey for Pounds 75m (Dollars 115.5m). This is to be converted into an open-ended fund from July 1. India Growth Fund is dollar denominated and listed on the NYSE.
