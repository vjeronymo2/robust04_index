BFN [Text] On 13 April 1994 Khristodhoulos Tsalikis, Greek ambassador in Tirana, was summoned to the Foreign Ministry and was handed a note saying that owing to the lack of an agreement on the necessary personnel of the diplomatic staff of the Republic of Albania in Athens and the Republic of Greece in Tirana, the Albanian side considers that these staffs should each be reduced to six diplomats. The Embassy of the Greek Republic in Tirana should take the necessary measures to send home, within 10 days, all diplomatic staff in excess of this number.
