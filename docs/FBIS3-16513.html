Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 16 (SNARK) -- Shevardnadze is doing his best to use the UN peace-making forces to abolish the legislative authorities of Abkhazia, the Abkhazian [Supreme Soviet] Chairman Vladislav Ardzinba told the journalists at his press-conference March 15 in Moscow. According to Mr. Chairman, the Abkhazian side is against the UN forces introduction into the territory of Abkhazia and offers to distribute the troops at the Georgian-Abkhazian border -- along the Ingury River.
