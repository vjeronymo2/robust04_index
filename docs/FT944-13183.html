941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Japanese Net asset value at Fleming Japanese Investment Trust rose to 260.7p fully diluted at the end of the year to September 30, against 245.1p. Mr Patrick Gifford, chairman, said the trust had achieved 'a significant outperformance' of the Japanese stock market. Net revenue fell to Pounds 53,000 (Pounds 544,000) and earnings per share were 0.02p (0.46p). No dividend is declared (0,45p).
