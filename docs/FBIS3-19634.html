Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] A bomb placed in the Republican People's Party [RPP] election headquarters in Agri's Patnos District was discovered in time and rendered harmless. According to information obtained by a Turkish Radio and Television correspondent, one person was seen entering the crowded election headquarters and placing the bomb. A member of the RPP picked up the bomb and threw it to an empty area. Because of the explosion, several of the surrounding houses' windows were smashed. Nobody was injured in the incident.
