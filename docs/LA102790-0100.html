October 27, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

MISSION LEAGUE

Notre Dame 28, St. Bernard 14 -- Senior running back Setefano Malieitulua rushed for a career-high 280 yards, and added three touchdowns and a 42-yard scoring pass to Vinnie Orlando, leading the Knights (5-2-1, 3-0-1) at St. Bernard (2-6, 1-4).

It was Malieitulua's first game since spraining his right knee two weeks ago.

Malieitulua scored the Knights' first touchdown on a 23-yard run late in the first quarter. In the second quarter, he scored from seven yards after his 64-yard run five plays earlier put the Knights in position for the score. ADAM BENSON
