BFN [Text] Baghdad, 23 Apr (INA) -- Tariq 'Aziz, official in charge of the Foreign Relations Bureau of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, today received here a Jordanian Islamic Action Front delegation. The delegation, which is currently visiting the country, is led by 'Abdallah al-'Akayilah. Tariq 'Aziz stressed that thanks to the wisdom of President Saddam Husayn and the resolve of the Iraqi people, Iraq has managed to rebuild what the evil and arrogant forces have destroyed, despite the continuation of the unjust blockade against it. He said that when people have sincere and faithful leaders with clear objectives, they manage to hold fast and attain their objectives. Such is the case in Iraq, he noted. He added: Despite all difficulties, we in Iraq are optimistic about the future. Al-'Akayilah praised the Iraqi people's steadfastness and courage in confronting the aggressors and the iniquitous blockade. He said that Iraq has initiated the phenomenon of defying the world's arrogant powers, and that Iraq represents the Arab nation's ammunition for confronting the serious plot targeting Arabism and Islam. The two sides stressed the importance of cooperation and coordination between pan-Arabist and Islamic forces and movements in the joint struggle against the imperialist and Zionist plots and threats.
