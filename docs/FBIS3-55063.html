Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday in Dushanbe there was a joint session of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. It discussed the financial position of the republic. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan, Abdujalil Samadov, flies out to Moscow today. It is expected that he will meet with the prime minister of the Russian Federation, Viktor Charnomyrdin, on questions of interest to both sides.
