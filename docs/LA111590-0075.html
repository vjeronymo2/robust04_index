November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

Jade Restaurant, 3013 Harbor Blvd. (across from Fedco), Costa Mesa. Open daily, 11 a.m. to 10 p.m. (714) 966-2988. Appetizer plates of shredded jellyfish and five-flavor beef, no fewer than 13 soups, more than four dozen entrees and a dozen special luncheon plates make this anything but your run-of-the-mill Chinese diner. JOHN O'DELL
