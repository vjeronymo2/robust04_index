940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Manakin Manakin Holdings, the former venture capital investment group which has been in member's voluntary liquidation since 1991, had a net asset value of 85p per share at April 15, up from 82p at the company's October year-end. A further capital distribution of 30p per share to shareholders - the seventh so far - will be paid later this month, bringing the total returned to date to Pounds 77.9m, or 380p per share. Total income for the six months to April 15 amounted to Pounds 117,915 (Pounds 137,802) but the pre-tax loss narrowed to Pounds 99,293 (Pounds 191,904). Losses per share were 0.51 (0.92p).
