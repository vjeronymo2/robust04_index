October 22, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

PRINCESS BANNED FROM DRIVING

A court today banned Princess Anne from driving for one month and fined her nearly $300 because she was caught speeding twice on the same road in one week.

The 40-year-old daughter of Queen Elizabeth II admitted driving 77 m.p.h. on Aug. 2 and 90 m.p.h. on Aug. 9 on Fosse Way, near Stow, northwest of London. The speed limit is 60.

Her attorney, Michael Sullivan, said she wished to "express her regret for both offenses." The princess was not in court.
