BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Russia and the United States have shared their experience in training officers for ground troops, the ground forces Press center reports. A U.S. military delegation led by General John (Fraser), deputy head of the ground troops training center, today completed its work in the Russian Armed Forces. The delegation had been staying in Moscow since 22 May in accordance with a treaty on contacts between Russian and U.S. armed forces. In the course of the visit, the Americans visited the Vystrel advanced training courses for officers and the Taman motorized rifle division. In the training classes and at the test grounds they familiarized themselves with the training of Russian officers and shared their experience in officer training.
