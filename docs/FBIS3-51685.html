Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] The Colombian and Venezuelan defense ministers concluded today a two-day meeting during which they agreed to increase joint efforts to fight drug trafficking and subversion. Janet Zepeda filed this report: [Begin recording] [Zepeda] It became clear to the Venezuelan military that operations to fight drug trafficking and subversion must be executed independently [as heard] in each country, because it is worthless for Venezuelans to execute them in Colombia and vice versa. [Vice Admiral Radames Munoz, Venezuelan defense minister] If Venezuelan authorities have committed abuses, I am willing to apologize personally to the Colombian Armed Forces and to the Colombian people, whom I admire, respect, and hold in esteem. [end recording]
