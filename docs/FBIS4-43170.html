BFN [Text] Madrid, June 13, IRNA -- The Iranian Embassy here today rejected as "baseless and utterly false" a report in the latest issue of the weekly TIEMPO alleging secret links between Iranians and the Basque separatist group ETA (Euzkadi Ta Azcatasuma [Basque Fatherland and Liberty]). The statement said "Iranian officials believe that the objective behind the groundless article is to create tension in the friendly relations between Islamic Iran and Spain." ETA which means `Basque Homeland and Liberty' was founded in 1959 and is regarded as an `illegal terrorist' organization by the Spanish Government. The Basques are an ethnic minority inhabiting the western Pyrenees mountains in northern Spain on the Bay of Biscay.
