930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Second Alliance net assets up 28% The Second Alliance Trust turned in a 28 per cent increase in net asset value for the year ended July 31, up from Pounds 12.79p to Pounds 16.4p. The improvement largely occurred in the earlier part of the financial year and was triggered by the effect of the withdrawal of sterling from the ERM on the trust's overseas portfolio. Net revenue improved to Pounds 7.66m (Pounds 7.17m) and earnings per share came out at 39.74p (37.22p). A final dividend of 26.5p (24.5p) makes a total for the year of 39p (36.5p).
