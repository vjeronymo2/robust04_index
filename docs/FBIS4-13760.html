BFN [Text] Woreshmin, a commander attached to Golboddin, together with his 24 men were killed in the vicinity of Bala Hesar during the recent operation carried out by Karmal-Golboddin-Dostam militia. The BIA military correspondent reports that during a counterattack, the military forces of the Islamic State of Afghanistan forced the Karmal-Golboddin-Dostam militia to retreat and imposed heavy losses on the aggressors. The report adds that Woreshmin was a general attached to Toran Amanollah. He had intended to appoint Woreshmin as chief of staff of his forces.
