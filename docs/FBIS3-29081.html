Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 15 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received the visiting delegation of the Communist Party of Uruguay led by Executive Secretary General of the Central Committee of the Party Pedro Balbi. Present there were Secretary Kim Chung-nin and Vice-Director of a department Chi Chae-yong of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. The head of the delegation extended warm congratulations to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il on his birthday and wished President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il good health and long life. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and conversed with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. He arranged a luncheon for the guests.
