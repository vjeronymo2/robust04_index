Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Georgian-Abkhaz talks on settlement of the Abkhaz conflict which started at the UN headquarters on 7 March ended without success, said Alvaro de Soto, the chief political adviser to the UN secretary general. He said that on Wednesday evening, at the closed consultations of the Security Council, the UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali told the members of the Security Council that the Georgian-Abkhaz negotiations which had been under way at the UN headquarters since 7 March ended due to unavailability of good prospects for reaching an agreement. There is not a single agreement signing which could be possible, said de Soto. According to him, Georgian-Abkhaz negotiations would probably resume in Geneva next month.
