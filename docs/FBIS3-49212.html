Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, December 31 (ATA) -- At the end of the penal case of the 10 former communist blockmen of the Party of Labour of Albania, accused of violation of the equality of citizens through the creation of privileges, the Tirana Court decided to arrest in the court hall the defendants Llambi Gegprifti, Lenka Cuko, and Hajredin Celiku. While seven other defendants, Pali Miska, Muho Asllani, Besnik Bekteshi, Qirjako Mihali, Vangjel Cerrava, Prokop Murra, and Foto Cami were punished with imprisonments terms respectively seven years and payment of 263,733 leks; six years and payment of 359,110 leks; eight years and payment of 402,753 leks; five years and payment of 193,340 leks; five years and payment of 474,906 leks; six years and payment of 313,118 leks. The jury also decided that all titles of honour and medals be from now on considered null and void for the defendants.
