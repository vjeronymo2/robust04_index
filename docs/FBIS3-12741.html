Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Bezeq Company employees in the Haifa district and the north returned to work last night but telephone disruptions elsewhere in the country continue. The Bezeq Company did not order employees to return to work last night and conducted negotiations with them to secure their agreement to end the disruptions. Our correspondent Meron Tzur reports that the employees have not said whether they will end disruptions before the District Labor Court issues a judgment on Sunday.
