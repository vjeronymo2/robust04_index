March 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

IDENTICAL TWINS PULL SWITCH AT JAILHOUSE

Teen-ager Terry Litton paid a visit to his identical twin Tony in a South Wales prison, swapped clothes with him and then stayed behind while his brother walked out of the jail.

Police on Wednesday were still hunting for Tony, who had been serving a two-year sentence for burglary. They have charged Terry with helping him escape from Cardiff Prison on Tuesday.
