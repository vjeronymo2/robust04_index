920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Caldwell raising Pounds 395,000 via rights Caldwell Investments, the USM-quoted textile company, has announced a 1-for-4 rights issue of 1.69m new ordinary shares at 27p each to raise about Pounds 395,000 net of expenses. Mr Stanley Wootliff, chairman, said the company had taken the decision to fi-nance its proposed expansion by way of a rights issue to maintain prudent levels of borrowing. The issue is being underwritten by Harris Allday Lea & Brooks and it is expected that dealings in the new shares, nil paid, will commence on March 9. Caldwell directors intend to subscribe for their full entitlement.
