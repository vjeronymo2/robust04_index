April 20, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: TEMPLE CITY;

COUNCIL NAMES NEW MAYOR

Mayor Pro Tem Patrick Froehle was named mayor by council members at the City Council's annual reorganization meeting Tuesday. Councilman Thomas Breazeal was selected as mayor pro tem.

Both are former planning commissioners. Froehle has been on the five-member council since 1985, and Breazeal has been on the council since 1988. Their terms expire in 1992.
