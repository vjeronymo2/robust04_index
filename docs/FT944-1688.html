941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week and Next): NFC - Other companies Sir Christopher Bland, who officially takes up his new position today as chairman of the transport and logistics group, has a tough job on his hands. A former chairman of LWT, Sir Christopher has the task of restoring the City's confidence in the group. But before that he must solve the more pressing problem of finding a new chief executive. The group has said an announcement will be made in the next few weeks. NFC's problems are not limited to personnel. Operating profits fell last year to Pounds 114.2m from Pounds 116.7m and the shares have dropped from 243p at the beginning of the year to 162p on Friday.
