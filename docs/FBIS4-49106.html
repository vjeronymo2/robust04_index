BFN [Text] Hefei, April 30 (XINHUA) -- Anhui Province in East China has expanded its export market to the Middle East, South America and Africa while consolidating its traditional markets in Hong Kong, Macao, Japan, the United States and Europe. Of its total export volume, some 33 percent, or over one billion U.S. dollars worth, goes to the Middle East, South America and Africa. Meanwhile, Anhui Province has approved 2,000 foreign investment projects from 28 countries and regions in Asia, Europe, Africa, North America and Oceania. Such enterprises involving in agricultural production, construction, communications, telecommunications and the service trade, are active in selling their products in international markets. The total export volume of these enterprises has already accounted for 10 percent of Anhui's total.
