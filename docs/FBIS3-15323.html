Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 3. (SNARK) -- Today Jan Eliasson, CSCE Minsk Group chairman has arrived to Stepanakert. He is expected to discuss with Nagorno-Karabakh leaders the peaceful settlement of the Karabakh problem. This news was obtained by SNARK from a responsible official in Nagorno-Karabakh Foreign Ministry.
