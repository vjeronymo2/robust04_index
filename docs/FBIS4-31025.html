BFN [Text] Pristina, June 1 (ATA) -- The Serb Court in Peje sentenced two Albanian youths, age 17, from the Caralluke village of the Kline commune, with one and a half year in jail under the charge of "military training" in Albania. Recently, there have been frequent punishments of Albanian youths under this false charge. Serb mass media, reporting on this punishment, did not make known the names of the accused, but only the initials: K. Sh. and K. V.
