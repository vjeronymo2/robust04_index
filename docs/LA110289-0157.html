November 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

ANGELS ASK WAIVERS ON GLENN HOFFMAN

The Angels asked waivers on infielder Glenn Hoffman Wednesday for the purpose of giving him his release.

Hoffman, 31, joined the Angels as a free agent last January. During the 1989 season, he appeared in 48 games and had 22 hits in 104 at-bats for a .212 average. He had one home run and drove in three runs.

Hoffman appeared at all four infield positions and made his first career starts as a first baseman and as a designated hitter.
