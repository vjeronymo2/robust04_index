940426 FT 26 APR 94 / International Company News: Jones recovers to IPounds 3.3m A lack of exceptional losses enabled Jones Group, the Dublin-based shipping, manufacturing and oil distribution company, to report pre-tax profits of IPounds 3.3m (Pounds 3.2m) for the year to December 31, against losses of IPounds 4.46m. The comparable figure was restated for FRS 3 and after losses of IPounds 7.5m relating to the sale of the environmental and engineering divisions. Operating profits were IPounds 3.49m, against IPounds 3.17m including losses of IPounds 714,000 from discontinued activities. Turnover was IPounds 68.1m, compared with IPounds 101.3m which included IPounds 35.1m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share were 23.1p (40.4p losses). A final dividend of 8.5p is proposed for an unchanged total distribution of 12.5p.
