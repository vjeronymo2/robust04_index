941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Slingsby dips 33% HC Slingsby, the trucks and ladders company, reported a 33 per cent drop in pre-tax profits from Pounds 302,169 to Pounds 203,633 for the six months to June 30. Turnover edged ahead 11 per cent from Pounds 5.9m to Pounds 6.55m. The company said trading conditions continued to be variable. Earnings per share fell to 14.1p (20.9p). An interim dividend of 3p is maintained.
