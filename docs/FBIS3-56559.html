Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk January 15 TASS -- U.S. President Bill Clinton completed his official Belarusian visit tonight and left for Geneva. At the Minsk-2 airport he was seen off by Belarusian leader Stanislav Shushkevich. Right upon his arrival from the airport Stanislav Shushkevich gave a news conference for Belarusian and foreign correspondents who covered the visit of Bill Clinton.
