BFN [Untitled Directive No. 189-rp of the Russian Federation President, dated 15 April 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] Based on the need for the political regulation of relations between federal organs of state power and Republic of Chechnya organs of state power: 1. The Russian Federation Government is to hold consultations with representatives of organs of state power and all Chechen Republic political movements with the aim of getting out of the sociopolitical situation that has developed, and on their basis is to prepare a draft treaty on delimiting areas of control between federal organs of state power and Chechen Republic organs of state power and on the reciprocal delegation of powers. 2. The Russian Federation President's Administration and leaders of federal organs of state power are to give the necessary assistance in holding the consultations referred to in paragraph 1 of this directive. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 15 April 1994
