Cherokee Valley Federal Savings Association, Cleveland, Ohio; Notice of Replacement of Conservation With a Receiver Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to the authority contained in Subdivision (C) of §5(d)(2) of the Home Owners' Loan Act, the Office of Thrift Supervision has duly replaced the Resolution Trust Corporation as Conservator Cherokee Valley Federal Savings Association, Cleveland, Ohio (``Association''), with the Resolution Trust Corporation as sole Receiver for the Association on September 23, 1994.
