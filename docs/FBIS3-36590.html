Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Turkmenistan President Saparmurad Niyazov had surgery in Houston, Texas, U.S.A., Saturday [5 February], for phlebitis of his left leg. He flew to the U.S. for an examination on February 1 following the end of the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland. Sources close to the Turkmenistan Government told Interfax that Niyazov was in good condition after the surgery. He is expected in Turkmenistan next Wednesday or Thursday.
