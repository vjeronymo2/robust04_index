BFN [Text] Beijing, April 23 (XINHUA) -- Egyptian President Husni Mubarak left here this morning, ending his three-day state visit to China. Mubarak arrived here on April 21 for his sixth trip to China at the invitation of Chinese President Jiang Zemin. Among those seeing President Mubarak and his party off at the airport were Xiao Yang, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of justice, Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Yang Fuchang and Assistant Foreign Minister Wang Changyi. Egyptian Ambassador to China Samir Burhan and diplomatic envoys from other Arab and African countries also went to the airport to bid farewell to Mubarak and his party.
