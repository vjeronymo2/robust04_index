Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of the Republic of Latvia, led by State President Guntis Ulmanis, is continuing its visit to Italy. At 1100 today the Latvian president met Pope John Paul II. Also this morning the delegation, led by Foreign Minister Georgs Andrejevs, met the Vatican secretary of state, Cardinal Angelo Sodano. At present, the Latvian president is holding a news conference at the Grand Hotel, and later there will be a dinner given by Italian industrialists.
