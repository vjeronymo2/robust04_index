December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HIGH TECH HIGH: Century High School, Santa Ana's new state-of-the-art campus, was praised lavishly in USA Today "as a school of the future." . . . In a series devoted to high-tech schools, the paper Wednesday called Century "the most technologically advanced school in California, and one of the most advanced in the USA." Despite the computer-assisted curricula, visitors from abroad go away impressed most with the students and staff, the paper reported. Jon Nalick and Mark Landsbaum
