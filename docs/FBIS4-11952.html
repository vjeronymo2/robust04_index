BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres hopes the Gaza-Jericho agreement will be signed and finalized in letter and spirit in April. He is convinced that a far-reaching agreeement will be attained on the release of Palestinian prisoners. Minister Peres refused to voice his position on the evacuation of the Jewish settlement from Hebron. He was speaking with Shim'on Aran, Qol Yisra'el's Arabic program correspondent, during the Mimuna celebrations in Yaqum Park in the Sharon area.
