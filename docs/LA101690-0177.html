October 16, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

LAKERS SIGN UP IRVING THOMAS

Forward Irving Thomas, a rookie free agent from Florida State University, was added to the Lakers' preseason roster today.

The 6-foot-8 Thomas was a member of the Lakers summer league squad. He averaged 17.1 points and 8.8 rebounds during the team's 10-game schedule.

As a senior at Florida State, Thomas averaged 16.7 points and 7.6 rebounds per game.
