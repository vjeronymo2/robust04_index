March 9, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

MOSBACHER OPTIMISTIC

Commerce Secretary Robert A. Mosbacher said today he believes the chance for progress toward opening Japanese markets is greater than ever and he hopes to impart a sense of urgency to Japanese leaders in Tokyo next week.

Mosbacher is leading a delegation of top-level Commerce Department trade experts to a series of meetings with Japanese government and business leaders Tuesday through next Friday.
