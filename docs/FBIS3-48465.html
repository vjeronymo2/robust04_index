Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- Working people across Korea are increasing production to register proud achievements this year, the first year of the period of adjustment. The Kaechon coal mine is topping its daily quotas by 40 to 50 percent, concentrating efforts on tunneling to create an additional production capacity of two million tons by the end of April. The coal miners there have created several new cutting faces and are boosting production. The pipeline transport station of the Kim Chaek iron and steel complex has lifted the daily transport of iron headings through the pipeline extending 100 kilometres from Musan and Chongjin by 50 percent entering this year over the daily average towards the end of last year. The Pukchang thermal power complex increased electricity output by 30 percent as of January 24 as against that in the same period of the previous month. Industrial establishments under the Ministry of Chemical Industry are registering signal achievements in the efforts to boost fertilizer production this year in which falls the 30th anniversary of the publication of the great rural theses. Many units including the State Fisheries Commission and the North Phyongan Provincial Management Office of minor power stations beat their January targets ahead of schedule.
