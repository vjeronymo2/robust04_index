BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 May -- Polish Republic President Lech Walesa had lunch at his residence with Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy, the president's press office reported today. They discussed the country's defense and security matters, the role of the Armed Forces supreme commander, and the need for a parliamentary debate on the issues with the participation of the president. These matters should be discussed at the coming meeting of the National Defense Committee. It was also considered important that mechanisms be defined for political consultations on the security of the state. The speaker also briefed the president on the parliament's agenda in the immediate future, before the summer recess. Within the framework of the coordination of Polish foreign policy between the highest state authorities, the speaker discussed his planned visit to China, the communique says.
