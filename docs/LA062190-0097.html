June 21, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: THE REGION;

MEETING ON POLLUTION LAWS

Water quality issues facing San Gabriel Valley business and property owners will be the focus of a meeting on Wednesday.

Members of the South El Monte Property Owners Assn. have been upset by the way regulatory agencies have handled water contamination problems. The group will host the talks, in which regional water quality officials will discuss how pollution laws affect small businesses.

There is no charge for the meeting at Coast Machinery Movers, 2431 Chico Ave., South El Monte. Those who plan to attend may make reservations by calling (818) 579-4510.
