BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 19 (KCNA) -- The Korean Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries and the Korea-Iran Friendship Association arranged a friendly gathering here Wednesday on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of Korean visit of leader of the Iranian Islamic Revolution Seyed Ali Khamenei. Saeed Lashgari, charge d'affaires ad interim of the Iranian Embassy in Korea, and officials of the embassy were invited to the gathering. Present at the gathering were Chong Ha-chol, chairman of the DPRK Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee and chairman of the Korea-Iran Friendship Association, Chon Yon-ok, vice-chairwoman of the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, and other officials concerned. The participants saw an art performance of school children and had a friendly conversation.
