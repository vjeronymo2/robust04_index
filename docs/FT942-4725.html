940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / UK Company News: CML moves ahead to Pounds 4.55m Sales of CML Microsystems, the specialised electronic product manufacturer, expanded 20 per cent from Pounds 15.7m to Pounds 18.8m while pre-tax profits for the year ended March 31 moved ahead 5.6 per cent to Pounds 4.55m, compared with Pounds 4.31m. Earnings per share were 15.58p (15.15p), and the dividend is lifted from 4.7p to 6p.
