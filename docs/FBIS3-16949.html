Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From "In Brief" roundup of ITAR-TASS/EAN reports] [Text] The Khabarovsk Kray Administration has adopted emergency measures to complete preparations for the spring field work. The local authorities have found an opportunity to allocate fuels and lubricants and spare parts for agricultural machinery to sovkhozes and associations of peasant farms without payment up front. Credits have been offered to farms for the purchase of seeds and fertilizers which are in short supply.
