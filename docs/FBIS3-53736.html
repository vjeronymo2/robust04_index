Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Israeli occupation forces shelled the township of Shaqra with 120-mm mortars from their positions in al-Qantarah at noon today. Citizen Muhammad 'Ali Dib suffered grave head injuries and was taken to Tibnin's government hospital. The shelling extended as far as Wadi al-Saluqi, Qabrikha, and Majdal Silm. Earlier today, the occupation forces raked the periphery of al-Ghanduriyah, Frun, and the al-Litani River basin with heavy machine-gun fire.
