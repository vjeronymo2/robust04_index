February 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

SATELLITE TV

The most recent honeymoon of the Europeans with satellite television (Part I, Feb.8) includes an experience that escaped your London correspondent: the first 24-hour-a-day satellite channel in Spanish in Europe known as Galavision. It is a mixture of news and information services called ECO. It is actually a new opportunity for the dial of the Spanish-speaking people in Western Europe.

FELIX CORTES

Executive Vice President

ECO

Los Angeles
