Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 3, IRNA -- Deputy president of Turkmenistan in charge of communications Sarjayev [name and title as received] Monday [3 January] met with Iran's Minister of Roads and Transportation Akbar Torkan and reviewed outlooks for surface, air and sea transport between Iran and Turkmenistan. The two officials were hopeful of setting up a shipping link between Iranian and Turkmen ports on the Caspian Sea. Meanwhile, the ministers of culture, press, and physical education of Turkmenistan along with the managing director of the television station of that country on Monday visited Niavaran Culture House of Tehran. Turkmen Minister of Culture and Tourism, Geldymurad Nurmukhamedov, called for cultural cooperation especially exchange of books and scientific information between the two countries. Iran can help Turkmenistan set up libraries and cultural centres in his country, he said. Turkmen Minister of Press and Publications, Divan Qoliev, [name and title as received] who is a leading writer himself, suggested media cooperation and exchange of periodicals and related matter between Iran and Turkmenistan. Also a group of religious figures of that republic on Monday met with the officials of the Islamic Propagation Organization of Iran here Monday. President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan arrived here on Sunday heading a high-ranking delegation.
