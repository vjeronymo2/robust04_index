Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] says India should formally register a protest with the United States against its attitude toward Kashmir. Addressing a news conference in New Delhi today, the party president, Mr. L.K. Advani, took exception to a letter reportedly written by President Clinton to a Washington-based supporter of militancy in Kashmir. Replying to questions, Mr. Advani said that Pakistan should be told that any further talks on Kashmir will depend on Islamabad ending its proxy war and the inclusion of the future of Pakistan-occupied Kashmir in the agenda.
