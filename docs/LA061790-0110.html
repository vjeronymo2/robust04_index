June 17, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD NOTES

Caltrans plans the following closures on San Gabriel Valley freeways this week:

One lane on the westbound San Bernardino Freeway (10) in Pomona from before the Dudley Street exit to the southbound San Gabriel Freeway (605) Freeway will be closed Tuesday from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.

The Walnut Grove on- and off-ramps on the eastbound San Bernardino Freeway (10) in Rosemead will be closed Wednesday from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.

The San Gabriel Boulevard off-ramp on the westbound San Bernardino Freeway (10) in San Gabriel will be closed Thursday from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.
