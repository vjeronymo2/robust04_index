920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / International Company News: Blick pays pounds 3.8m for contracts from GPT Blick, which sells, rents and maintains communications equipment, has purchased a portfolio of contracts for rental and maintenance from GPT, a company jointly owned by GEC and Siemens, for Pounds 3.8m cash. Of the consideration Pounds 2.5m was immediate and the balance is payable as to Pounds 650,000 on November 4 1992 and Pounds 650,000 on April 20 1993.
