February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Gateway Communications Inc.: The Irvine-based computer products company reported that for 1989, net income rose 84% and revenue rose 53%.

Net income for 1989 was $1 million, up from $544,000 for 1988. Revenue was $25.7 million, contrasted with the $16.8 million reported for 1988. Gateway supplies network and communications products for IBM personal computers.
