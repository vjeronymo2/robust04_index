BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondenbt Igor Belskiy] [Excerpt] Moscow, 22 Apr -- The Working Moscow organization has declared its intention to "continue lawful counteraction" against the Russian authorities at a rally held on the 124th anniversary of Vladimir Ilyich Lenin's birthday. A statement by the organization's Political Council read out on Moscow's Teatralnaya Square today says that it is ready to join the agreement on public accord only "on condition that the country's present leaders withdraw from the political scene." The organizers of today's mass action are Working Moscow and the Russian Communist Workers' Party. Several thousand people attended the rally. The slogans and appeals that have become traditional at all opposition rallies alternated today with attempts to interpret Lenin's activities. [passage omitted]
