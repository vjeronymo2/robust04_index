BFN [Text] The Office for Refugees of the Republic of Croatia has decided to strip all the citizens of Bosnia-Herzegovina who fled to Croatia from the Mostar area of their refugee status, B-H Press [Bosnia-Herzegovina state agency] reports. Taking into account the fact that the roads are still closed and that parts of the Mostar region are still under occupation, a number of refugees from Mostar will have to go to a third country now instead of returning to their homes.
