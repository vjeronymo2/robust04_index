December 8, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

CATALOGUING THE WATER LILY

The water lily season is nearing its end, but there is no reason to wait for spring. Just as there is the Burpee's seed catalogue for the home gardener, there are water garden catalogues for the water lily enthusiast to ponder over during the winter months.

Here are several water gardens that offer mail-order service to California:

Lilypons Water Gardens, 1626 Lilypons Road, Brookshire, Tex. 77423-0188.

Paradise Water Gardens, 14 May St., Whitman, Mass. 02382.

Van Ness Water Gardens, 2460 N. Euclid Ave., Upland, Calif. 91786.

Waterford Gardens, 74 E. Allendale Road, Saddle River, N.J. 07458.

William Tricker Inc., 7125 Tanglewood Drive, Independence, Ohio 44131.

If you are interested in further information on water lilies and related activities, contact the International Water Lily Society, c/o School of Science, Southwest Texas State University, San Marcos, Tex. 78666-4616. Marilyn Pitts
