BFN [Text] The president, Flight Lieutenant Jerry John Rawlings, has rejected the resignation from government of the minister of education, Mr. Harry Sawyerr. This is in response to the minister's letter of resignation, tendered on Friday, June 3, over the strike action by teachers. The president's reaction is contained in a statement issued in Accra today, and signed by the deputy minister of information, Mr. Kojo Yankah. The statement said the president affirmed his confidence in Mr. Sawyerr's ability to handle the issues confronting his ministry, and that this is not the time to pull out. It quoted the president has saying: We need to collectively continue to meet the challenges of the times.
