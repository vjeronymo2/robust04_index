941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Superscape VR Higher than budgeted sales of software enabled Superscape VR, the virtual reality software company, to report lower than forecast losses of Pounds 238,024 for the year to July 31. At the time of the placing in April the group foresaw losses of up to Pounds 450,000. Turnover was Pounds 859,192, against Pounds 642,018 when the pre-tax loss was Pounds 21,450. Losses per share were 6.1p (0.5p). The figures were produced on a pro forma basis.
