Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 6 Jan (XINHUA) -- This morning, General Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission [CMC], left Beijing on a special plane for official goodwill visits to Thailand and Indonesia at the invitation of General Woranat Aphichari, supreme commander of the Thai Armed Forces, and General Feisal Tanjung, commander-in-chief of the Indonesian Armed Forces. He was seen off at the airport by General Chi Haotian, state councillor and defense minister; general Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army [PLA]; Hu Guangbao, deputy director of the General Office of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee; Tang Jiaxuan, vice foreign minister; Cheng Jianning, director of the General Office of CMC, as well as embassy officials of the relevant countries. Vice Chairman Liu Huaqing's party includes his wife Xu Hongxia; Lieutenant General Xu Huizi, deputy chief of the general staff of the PLA; Lieutenant General Tao Bojun, deputy commander of the Guangzhou Military Region; and Major General Fu Jiaping, director of Foreign Affairs Bureau under the Ministry of National Defense.
