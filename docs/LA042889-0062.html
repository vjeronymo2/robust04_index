April 28, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

MEMORIAL SERVICES SET FOR FORMER GYMNAST

A memorial service will be held Sunday for Javier Salazar, a former member of the Mexican national gymnastics team who was killed Monday in a car accident in Northridge.

Salazar, 30, who competed in the 1979 World Gymnastics Championships, was a resident of Arleta and a gymnastics instructor at the Junior Elite Training Center in Canoga Park.

Services will be held at 6 p.m. at the Noble Chapel, 1231 First St., in San Fernando.
