For the reasons set forth in the preamble, the Coast Guard amends 46 CFR part 16 as follows: PART 16_CHEMICAL TESTING 1. In §16.207, paragraph (b) is revised to read as follows: §16.207 Conflict with foreign laws. * * * * * (b) This part is not effective until January 2, 1996, with respect to any person onboard U.S. vessels in waters that are subject to the jurisdiction of a foreign government. On or before December 1, 1995, the Commandant shall issue any necessary amendment resolving the applicability of this part to such person on and after January 2, 1996. Dated: December 2, 1994.
