CRITICS' CHOICE: LIAR'S POKER: RISING THROUGH THE WRECKAGE ON WALL STREET BY MICHAEL M. LEWIS (W. W. NORTON: $15.95; 265 PP.)

"Despite its iconoclastic tone, the book is an informative insider's description of the peculiar macho culture of investment bankers. . . a sort of pilgrim's progress through the land of avarice." Scot J. Paltrow
