Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow February 16 TASS -- Viktor Kobelev and Aleksandr Pronin, members of the State Duma (lower house of the Russian parliament), left Vladimir Zhirinovskiy's parliamentary faction. They said about their move at the Duma's parliamentary session on Wednesday, requesting to circulate their statement on reasons for the withdrawal. In turn, faction leader Zhirinovskiy said that legislators Kovelev and Pronin were expelled from the Liberal Democratic Party and the federal list of lawmakers from the LDP. He added that criminal cases will be instituted against the two legislators in question. However, Zhirinovskiy did not specify their supposed crimes.
