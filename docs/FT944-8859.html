941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Taking extremely good care of the bears Luke Jeffrey Green, aged 3, cuddles a pair of antique teddy bears outside the London auction rooms of Sotheby's. Emil, the bear on the left, and Kristopher, on the right, date from the early part of the century. Both were produced by the famous German maker Stieff and are expected to fetch more than Pounds 50,000 each when they come under the hammer on Thursday.
