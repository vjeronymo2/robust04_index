BFN [Text] The ROK Government has decided on the members of our side's delegation to a preliminary contact for North-South summit talks to be held on 28 June in Panmunjom. The delegation will be led by Yi Hong-ku, deputy prime minister and minister of the National Unification Board. The government notified the North Korean side of the list of delegates and accompanying officials just a few moments ago at 1400 [0500 GMT]. Hong Chong-chin reports from the National Unification Board: [Hong Chong-chin] Our side's delegation is composed of senior delegate Yi Hong-ku, deputy prime minister and minister of the National Unification Board; Chong Chong-uk, senior secretary for foreign and national security affairs; and (Yun Yo-chun), special adviser to the prime minister. The government sent a telephone message to the North Korean side to notify it of this list just a few moments ago at 1400.
