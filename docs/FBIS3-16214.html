Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Galina Gridneva and Aleksandr Kharchenko] [Text] Dushanbe, 15 Mar -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev arrived in Dushanbe today to take part in the conference of heads of foreign policy departments of the Central Asian Republics and Kazakhstan and the Russian Federation. Consultative meetings of the foreign ministers of Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and Tajikistan also began this morning. The consultative meetings are being held behind closed doors, but it is known that according to the preliminary agenda the participants in the conference are to discuss the political situation in Tajikistan and around it and also prospects for getting a dialogue under way. After the conference its participants will be received by Tajik Supreme Soviet Chairman Emomali Rakhmonov. This morning it is also envisaged that Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev will make a trip to one of the sectors of the Tajik-Afghan border. After the trip he will have a conversation face-to-face with head of Tajikistan Emomali Rakhmonov.
