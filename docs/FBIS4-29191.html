BFN [Announcer-read report; from "NHK News" program] [Text] Li Daoyu, Chinese ambassador to the United Nations, in a 15 June interview with NHK, declined to directly comment on the draft sanctions resolution that the United States has prepared to submit to the UN Security Council [UNSC], merely saying: China's position remains unchanged. The PRC has maintained a cautious stance on imposing sanctions against the DPRK. As a result, it is expected that for the time being, behind-the-scenes maneuverings -- including possible revisions to the draft resolution -- will continue among UNSC member nations, with attention focused on how China will react to the U.S. proposal.
