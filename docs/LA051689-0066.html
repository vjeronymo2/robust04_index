May 16, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

Homicide detectives Monday were investigating a drive-by shooting in Southeast San Diego that left a 24-year-old pregnant woman dead. Police are searching for the occupants of two vehicles involved in the attack.

A police officer heard a number of gunshots about 12:30 a.m. Sunday in the 2600 block of L Street shortly before he found the body of Sheri A. Gibson slumped behind the wheel of her 1979 Cadillac.

A coroner's spokesman said Gibson, of the 2000 block of Franklin Avenue in Logan Heights, died of a shotgun blast to her head. Her car crashed into a fence in the 2500 block of L Street after two vehicles pulled up alongside it and opened fire, police said.
