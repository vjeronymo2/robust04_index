BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 23 (KCNA) -- More than 3,000 villagers of Yangsan, South Kyongsang Province, held the sixth rally on May 20 against the construction of a nuclear waste dump by the puppet authorities, according to a radio report from Seoul. At the rally the promotion committee of the struggle against the construction of the Yangsan nuclear waste dump declared that the boycott of schools by students in Yangsan and Ulsan would be expanded, if the puppet authorities refused to give up the project. The villagers demanded that the puppet authorities release those taken into custody while participating in the struggle against the project and stop police search for those on the want list.
