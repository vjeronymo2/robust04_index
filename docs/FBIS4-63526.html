BFN [Text] Ukrainian Vice Premier on Foreign Economic Activities Valentin Landik told INTERFAX-Ukraine that he had applied to Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk for resignation today in the morning. Landik stressed that he had made this decision in order to conform with the law, which does not permit Supreme Soviet deputies to work in a government department. Commenting on the results of his activities in the government, Landik stated that Ukraine's foreign trade balance had been significantly improved and its export had increased several times. "We must stimulate export and limit import by all means. We can do without Snickers. We need import for our industry," he said.
