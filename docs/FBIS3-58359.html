Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Democratic Party-Republican Turkish Party coalition government has received a vote of confidence. Of the 48 deputies who participated in the vote this morning, 28 voted in favor of the government and 20 against. Two deputies did not participate in the Assembly session because they are on leave. In a speech after the vote, Prime Minister Hakki Atun said that the aim of his government is to run a parliamentarian and democratic system through the Assembly in the most effective and fruitful way. Our aim is to run the state in the best possible way, he added, to strengthen it, and to make our people prosperous and happy.
