Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Thursday [17 March] Speaker of the Russian State Duma Ivan Rybkin met with U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry who is on an official visit to Moscow. Chairman of the State Duma's Committee for International Affairs Vladimir Lukin, who attended the meeting, told Interfax that the meeting focused on some aspects of "pragmatic partnership" in the defense sphere between the two countries. "It is not a tragedy that our interests in this sphere do not always coincide. These are normal relations between two great powers," Lukin said.
