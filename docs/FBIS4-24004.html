BFN [Text] The Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] has allowed the reopening of two electoral registration centers in areas under its control in Nampula Province's Malema District -- namely, (Nicuassula) and (Leia). Benedito Vasco Verde, chairman of Nampula's Provincial Electoral Commission, says the delay in the reopening of the centers was due to a lack of transport. With the reopening of the two centers all Renamo-controlled areas in Nampula Province can now be registered.
