BFN [Text] Here is a short profile of the new interior minister appointed by presidential decree. Ramil Idris ogly Usubov was born in Khodzhaly in 1948. Since the 1970's he has been working in the police organs. He was the interior minister of Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic [NAR] in the years 1987-89. He served as the head of the passports and visa department and then as the head of a desk in the personnel department at the Interior Ministry until June 1993. He has been working again as the interior minister of NAR since June 1993. He is a lawyer by profession, and a graduate of the police academy. He is married and has three children.
