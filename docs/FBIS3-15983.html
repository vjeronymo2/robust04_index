Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today in Riga, Girts Kristovskis, Latvian minister of internal affairs, and Reinhard Teiling, a spokesman for the German Ministry of Internal Affairs, signed a protocol to the treaty concluded in 1992 between the two Ministries of Internal Affairs on technical assistance to the latvian Ministry of Internal Affairs. A news conference was held after the signing of the protocol. Linards Mucins, parliamentary secretary at the Latvian Ministry of Internal Affairs, said that this treaty provides for German assistance to a value of 3 million marks to the internal affairs system of Latvia. Most of this sum is due to be used for acquiring communications equipment. At present a new cooperation treaty with the German Ministry of Internal Affairs is in preparation.
