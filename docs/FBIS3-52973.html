Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Taba, 10 Jan (MENA)--The Palestinian and Israeli delegations concerned with implementing the Gaza-Jericho agreement concluded their evening working session after meetings that lasted approximately seven hours under Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, and General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff. Dr. Sha'th told reporters that today marks the beginning of a return to negotiations here in Taba on the basis of the principles included in the agreement and the concepts worked out in Cairo. He noted that this meeting, which has brought us again to Taba, will lead us in the shortest possible time to formulate a full agreement on withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho. He described today's negotiations in Taba as successful and useful, noting that all issues were reviewed in preparation for forming subcommittees tomorrow to formulate clauses relating to security, the transfer of civil powers, and the general agreement. He voiced his hope that this work will be completed as quickly as possible. [passage omitted]
