941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Coats Viyella COATS VIYELLA, Britain's largest clothing and textiles company, claimed that its Indian subsidiary had enjoyed a 29 per cent increase in first half profits. The Bombay-based group - Coats Viyella India - reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 5.8m on sales ahead 17 per cent at Pounds 75m in the six months to September 30.
