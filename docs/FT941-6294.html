940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: Bula Resources Russian moves Bula Resources, the Ireland-based oil and gas exploration company, is expected to announce an acquisition in the next two weeks which could result in a share issue and the nomination of board members from the former Soviet Union. The company has sought to quell speculation that it was withdrawing from negotiations in Russia by announcing that it was in advanced discussions with a number of parties. Bula raised IPounds 2.3m (Pounds 2.2m) through a placing earlier this year.
