BFN [Text] The members of the regional committees of the Party of Democratic Action [SDA] for central Bosnia, deputies in the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly, and the members of the executive and main committee of the SDA [words indistinct] at a meeting held in Travnik on 8 April have extended unequivocal support to the proposal that Mr. Alija Izetbegovic should be a candidate for the presidency of the future federal community. The said proposal and the proposal that Mr. Alija Izetbegovic -- for moral and political reasons that are clear to all the members and supporters of our party -- should be given the first presidential mandate in the future federation, are fully supported by the party members who hold functions in the municipal and regional bodies of legal authority in the region of Zenica and Travnik.
