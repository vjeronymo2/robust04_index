Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Sa'ud, the custodian of the two holy mosques, may God protect him, today received in his office in al-Yamama Palace His Excellency President Yasir 'Arafat, the president of the State of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, and the delegation accompanying him. The meeting was attended by His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin- 'Abd-al-'Aziz, the second deputy prime minister, defense minister, and aviation and general inspector, His Royal Highness Prince Salman Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, emir of the Riyadh region, His Excellency, the special adviser to the custodian of the two holy mosques, Mr. Ibrahim al-'Angari, and Mr. Mustafa Hashim, the charge d'Affaires of the embassy of the State of Palestine to the kingdom.
