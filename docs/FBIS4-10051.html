BFN [Text] Guadalajara (Mexico), 6 Apr (EFE) -- Nicaraguan President Violeta Barrios de Chamorro was transferred to a hospital in Guadalajara after fainting today in this Mexican city. Chamorro arrived today in Guadalajara, Mexico's second largest city, to attend a forum entitled "Women of the Americas: Participation and Development." According to the private television station Televisa, Chamorro was taken to the El Carmen Hospital in Guadalajara for medical attention. No information is available about what caused her to faint.
