931122 FT 22 NOV 93 / International Company News: Pelican flies into the US Pelican Group, the restaurant concern, is to acquire a US corporation to be named Pelican USA, the assets of which comprise a 50 per cent interest in the Cafe Tu Tu Tango restaurant business, together with Dollars 1m cash, for a consideration of Dollars 2.08m (Pounds 1.39m). Following completion of the deal Mr Robert Earl, who is behind the Planet Hollywood chain and is currently a non-executive director of Pelican, will become an executive director of Pelican USA.
