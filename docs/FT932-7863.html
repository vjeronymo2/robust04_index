930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / People: Hardy Oil & Gas John Wilson, previously general manager of exploration, has been appointed to the board of HARDY OIL & GAS. From 1960 to 1972 Wilson was head of the British Geological Mission in Peru and worked for BP until 1989 culminating in the position of business development manager responsible for Australasia and Latin America. In 1990 he joined the energy unit of International Finance Corporation, a World Bank affiliate.
