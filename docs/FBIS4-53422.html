BFN [Text] Thailand has extended textile agreements with the United States, Norway, and Canada. The extension of the agreements was acknowledged by the Cabinet as proposed by the Ministry of Commerce. With the United States Thailand signed a memorandum of understanding on 1 April 1994 extending their bilateral textile agreement for another year. The agreement would automatically be extended for another year if the GATT textile agreement does not come into effect. With Norway Thailand signed agreed minutes extending their textile agreement for another two years from 1 January 1994 to 31 December 1995. Thailand and Canada signed an agreed record of discussion extending their textile accord for another two years from 1 January 1994 to 31 December 1995.
