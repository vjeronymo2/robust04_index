Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Unrest in Bophuthatswana has claimed the life of a policeman. The policeman was on duty in Mabopane when he was shot by members of the public. In other incidents yesterday 13 people were wounded in Mafikeng, and a policeman's house was burned down in Mmabatho. [passage omitted] Meanwhile President Lucas Mangope of Bophuthatswana has said he is prepared to resist incorporation into South Africa even if he is left with only 50 soldiers. Addressing senior officers of the Bophuthatswana Defense Force in Mmabatho, Mr. Mangope said he had been promised military assistance from outside the state which he had turned down. According to a senior officer who attended the meeting, members of the defense force defied the president by refusing to sing the national anthem.
