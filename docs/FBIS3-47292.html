Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 10 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a message of greetings to Lansana Conte on his election as president of the Republic of Guinea on January 8. In the message President Kim Il-song said Lansana Conte's reassumption of important office as president is an expression of deep respect and trust of the Guinean people in him. He expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would consolidate and develop still further in the future, and wished the president greater success in the work for the prosperity of the country.
