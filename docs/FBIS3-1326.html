Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The government and the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] had demobilized 4,490 men by 23 March. This is revealed in a daily report of the UN Operations in Mozambique [Unomoz]. According to Unomoz, the government and Renamo have already sent more than 1,800 men to join the future Mozambique Defense Armed Forces.
