BFN [Article by "rum": "The Annual Level of Inflation Continues To Decline"] [Text] Overall consumer prices, expressing the inflationary development, rose by 0.4 percent in April in comparison with the preceding month. Czech Statistical Office Spokesman J. Lehnerova announced this yesterday. Prices of services, food, and nonfood products each increased by the same 0.4 percent. Public catering prices recorded the greatest increase in April--0.6 percent. Among food products, chocolate and sweets showed the largest increase--2.8 to 5.5 percent. On the other hand, the price of eggs fell by 5.3 percent. Among the services, cinema and theater seats and municipal transport fares rose by more than 2 percent. Total inflation over the four months of this year reached 2.8 percent and thus corresponds to government expectations and the Czech National Bank's estimates. The annual level of inflation is declining as the year progresses. It was 9.2 percent in April, down 0.2 percent from March.
