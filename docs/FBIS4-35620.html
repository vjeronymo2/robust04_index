BFN [Excerpts] Kuwait, 28 May (KUNA) -- Visiting Chinese Deputy Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei met here today with Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad, Kuwaiti first deputy prime minister and foreign minister. An official source at the Foreign Ministry told the Kuwaiti News Agency that Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad has praised China's principled stance regarding the treacherous Iraqi aggression against Kuwait in August 1990. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad has also praised China's understanding of the need for the Iraqi regime's commitment to the implementation of legitimate international resolutions. The source said that Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad has noted Kuwait's stance regarding the need to compel Iraq to implement all the international resolutions pertaining to the war of liberation against Kuwait, stressing that the UN sanctions imposed on the Iraqi regime were the only guarantee to achieve this goal. [passage omitted] The Chinese deputy foreign minister is visiting Kuwait at the start of a tour of a number of Arab countries. He is due to meet the amir tomorrow and deliver a message from Chinese President Jiang Zemin.
