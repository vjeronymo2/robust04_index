940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / Lady Archer silent on row Lady Archer, pictured at the family home at Grantchester, Cambridgeshire, declined to comment yesterday on the Department of Trade and Industry's decision not to reopen the case of alleged insider dealing in Anglia shares involving her husband, Lord Archer. Mr Robin Cook, the shadow trade secretary, had argued that the Crown Prosecution Service should review the DTI's decision not to prosecute. Report, Page 9
