March 16, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

EARNINGS

Toys R Us Inc: The Paramus, N.J.- based toy store chain said fiscal 1989 net earnings increased 31.4% to a record $268 million from a year ago. Sales were up 27.5% to $4 billion.The company said it opened 45 toy stores in the United States and 15 internationally during the year. Also, it opened 38 Kids R Us stores, including stores in Southern California.
