930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / Tory split on tax set to widen FURTHER EVIDENCE of Tory disarray over taxation is set to emerge when Mr Norman Lamont, the former chancellor, argues against further tax increases in an article which is due to be published in a right-wing journal on the eve of the Conservative party conference. His article for Conservative Way Forward, a Thatcherite pressure group, is said to call for deeper spending cuts instead of tax rises in the unified Budget statement in November.
