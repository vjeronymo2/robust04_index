BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniy Sabetskiy] [Text] Kyzyl, 16 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- President Boris Yeltsin's working tour of the country's eastern regions has ended. Having assessed its results as rather important at the conference with leaders of the Republic of Tuva, he left Kyzyl for Novosibirsk. Boris Yeltsin is returning to Moscow today.
