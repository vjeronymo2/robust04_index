BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 24 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] hosted a reception at the Mansudae Assembly Hall Saturday for a delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam on a visit to Korea. Invited there were the members of the delegation headed by Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party Hong Ha and Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc. Speaking at the reception, Secretary of the C.C., the WPK Hwang Chang-yop said that mutual visits between the two parties are a clear token of the common will of the two parties to support and cooperate closely with each other in accomplishing the cause of socialism. He said that the Korean people rejoice as over their own over the achievements made by the fraternal Vietnamese people and sincerely wish them new successes in their future efforts to implement the resolution of the conference of the party. "The friendship between the two parties and two peoples is noble friendship nursed by the respected leader of our people President Kim Il-song and the respected leader of the Vietnamese people Comrade Ho Chi Minh in the anti-imperialist common struggle long ago. Our party and people will support and closely cooperate with the fraternal Vietnamese party and people in the cause of socialism in the future too," he stated. Speaking next, head of the delegation Hong Ha said: "The Korean people have registered great achievements in the cause of socialist construction under the leadership of the Workers' Party of Korea headed by respected President Kim Il-song and the sagacious guidance of Comrade Kim Chong-il." Saying that the Vietnamese people always rejoice as over their own over the achievements made by the Korean people in socialist construction and have actively supported the independent and peaceful reunification of Korea, he declared his party would make beneficial efforts to further develop the friendly relations between the two parties and two peoples.
