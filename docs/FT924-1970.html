921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Supplying equipment to power stations NEI INTERNATIONAL COMBUSTION has won orders for power station combustion equipment worth Pounds 25m in the UK and US. In Britain, the company has won an order worth Pounds 18m to provide the waste heat recovery steam generators for National Power at the company's gas-fired combined cycle power station at Deeside. In the US, the group has signed a further contract for the supply of combustion equipment to Georgia Power. The contract is part of a four unit order worth more than Pounds 7m. The Deeside commission was placed by NEI ABB Gas Turbines, which won the order to construct the 500MW plant last year.
