910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Construction Contracts: Hospital project Beatty Building Northern has secured three contracts. Work will start shortly on the construction of a Pounds 4.5m single-storey hospital at Campbeltown for the Argyll & Clyde Health Board. The single-storey structure will comprise a concrete semi-raft slab foundation, walls of block cavity construction, timber roof trusses and a tiled finish. The works also include all services, associated drainage and external works. Completion is scheduled for March 1993. The second project is also within the health sector. The contract involves the Pounds 136,000 substructure and retaining walls package works for a nursing home in Mount Vernon, Glasgow. The third project is the third award for the same client at the Kvaerner Govan shipyard and involves reinstating and improving the apron area and is valued in excess of Pounds 350,000.
