930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / World Trade News: S Korea secures oil contract Hyundai of Korea has won a CDollars 100m (Pounds 55m) contract to build two super modules for the Hibernia offshore oil platform, Robert Gibbens writes from Montreal. One will house production equipment and the other crew quarters. Contracts for two other modules will be awarded later this year. The 110,000 barrels a day Hibernia project off Newfoundland is due on-stream in 1997. Webb Zerafa Menkes Housden, one of Canada's best known firms of architects, will design Shanghai's CDollars 100m 40-storey stock exchange building.
