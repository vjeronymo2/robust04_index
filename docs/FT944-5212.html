941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Arts (Openings): Munich An exhibition opening on Wednesday at the Lenbachhaus aims to speed up the rehabilitation of Helmut Kolle (1899-1931), the German figurative artist who flourished in Paris in the 1920s. His early death, together with the theft and destruction of many of his paintings during the Nazi era, led to his neglect after the war.
