December 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JOCKEYS SEEM TO GET BETTER WITH AGE

The national top-10 jockey standings contain some interesting information. All of the top six riders -- except (Eddie) Delahoussaye, and he has a national riding title -- are past Eclipse Award winners. All of the top six -- except (Jose) Santos -- are over 30 years old, and two of them -- (Angel) Cordero and (Laffit) Pincay -- are over 40. This seems to be one sport where the cream comes to the top -- and stays there.

DON LOVE, Palm Springs
