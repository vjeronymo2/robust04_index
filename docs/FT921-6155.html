920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Green Property Green Property, the Dublin-based real estate group, lifted pre-tax profit from IPounds 1.47m to IPounds 1.7m (Pounds 1.57m) for 1991. Earnings per share worked through at 8.42p (6.01p) and the final dividend is 2.6p for a total of 3.8p (3.5p). Net rents rose to IPounds 4.56m (IPounds 3.8m) and other operating income to IPounds 173,000 (IPounds 150,000). Debt/equity ratio with convertible loan stock as debt stood at 101 (98.1) per cent at the end of 1991; with loan stock fully converted it was 56.7 (56.5) per cent.
