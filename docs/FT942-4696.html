940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / International Company News: Brierley sells building arm to HK group Brierley Investments, the New Zealand hotels and investments group, is selling Downer, its construction arm, to Paul Y-ITC Construction of Hong Kong, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. The deal will make Brierley a 17 per cent shareholder in Paul Y, which will issue 98m shares at HKDollars 2.50 each to pay for Downer. It will also place a further 52m of its shares, at the same price, with Brierley. Paul Y is a Hong-based construction concern. The merged group will have activities in Hong Kong, China, south-east Asia and Papua New Guinea.
