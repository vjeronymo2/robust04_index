930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / Japan appoints first Welsh consul JAPAN is to appoint Mr Robin Geldard, a commercial lawyer based in Cardiff, as its first honorary consul in Wales. The appointment was announced by Mr David Hunt, Welsh secretary. He also said that last year was a record for inward investment in Wales. A total of 206 projects involved capital expenditure of more than Pounds 1bn and the potential creation of 14,000 jobs.
