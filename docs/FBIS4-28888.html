BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 12 (KCNA) -- A seminar on the work of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il "Our Socialism Centred on the Masses Shall Not Perish" was held by the Dar-es-Salaam, Tanzania, association for the study of the chuche idea. The speakers said that the advantages and vitality of Korean-style peculiar socialism centred on the popular masses have been fully displayed in all fields and that the socialist system of Korea is a genuine popular one which provides the masses of the people with an independent and creative life. A reading session of this work was held by the Zairean Society for the Study of Kim Chong-il's Works. The speaker said Korean-style socialism is a socialism centred on the popular masses where they are the masters of everything and everything serves them. They noted that since the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has always adhered to the independent stand, it has been able to defend the national dignity and successfully carry on the cause of socialism in any storm and stress. A seminar on the work "Abuses of Socialism Are Intolerable" took place at the department of the state in charge of irrigation of the Ministry of Agriculture of Mozambique. The speakers said the work is a great textbook of revolution that gives a scientific exposition of the unavoidableness of the downfall of imperialism and the inevitability of the triumph of socialism. Noting that though the imperialists are trying to destroy Korean socialism, it is a daydream, they stressed that nobody can match the Korean people united closely around their leader and their party.
