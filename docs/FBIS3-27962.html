Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 1 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on January 29 sent a message to Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organisation, in reply to his message of solidarity on the New Year 1994. In the reply message, President Kim Il-song said that the full solidarity of the Palestinians with the DPRK's efforts to denuclearise the Korean peninsula and reunify the country in the form of confederation is a great encouragement to the Korean people. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two peoples would grow stronger and develop, President Kim Il-song heartily wished the Palestinian president and people greater success in their struggle to restore their legitimate national rights and completely solve the Palestinian issue.
