BFN [Text] Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, the autonomy's finance minister, crossed the Allenby Bridge and arrived in Jericho a short time ago. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that al-Nashashibi, a member of the Palestinian Authority and the PLO Executive Committee, will be the minister in charge of economic issues in the autonomous areas. Upon arriving in Jericho, he stated that he came to organize the economy of the population of the territories.
