BFN [Text] Two more top communist leaders in Mindanao were expelled from the underground movement for their refusal to recognize Jose Maria Sison as the chair of the Communist Party of the Philippines (CPP), reports reaching Camp Aguinaldo said yesterday. Col. Angelo Reyes, commanding officer of the Civil Relations Service (CRS) of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, said the CPP "faces more serious crises" due to the worsening rift within the movement." Reyes identified those expelled as Timoteo Pupa, Central Mindanao Regional Committee (CMRC) secretary and Ike de los Reyes, Pupa's deputy.
