Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Vladimir Belyakov report: "All Eggs in One Basket"] [Text] Cairo, 10 Jan -- Evaluating the recently ended 10-day joint Kuwaiti-Russian naval maneuvers held within the framework of the agreement on military cooperation between the two countries signed in Moscow at the end of November, a spokesman for the Kuwaiti Ministry of Defense described them as "excellent." It would be possible to welcome Russia's contribution to ensuring Kuwait's security. But there is the troubling fact that Russian-Kuwaiti military cooperation has begun before the normalization of relations between Kuwait and Iraq and so inevitably acquires an anti-Iraqi complexion. The door to cooperation with Iraq -- a rich and influential state with which we used to be linked by very close ties -- has thereby essentially been slammed shut. In such a situation it is hardly rational, as our new friends, the Americans, say, to put all our eggs in one basket.
