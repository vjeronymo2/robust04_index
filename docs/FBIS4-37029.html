BFN [Text] Trade Minister Dr. Muhammad Mahdi Salih has told the Iraqi News Agency that the assistance provided by the state in accordance with the directives of leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve and protect him, in order to ensure food for the citizens at symbolic prices amounts to 99.7 percent of the cost of the foodstuffs that Iraqi families receive under the ration card. The minister added: The official price of the foodstuffs that are given to every individual under the ration card is 3.085 dinars, while their market price is 1,015 dinars. Thus the state pays the difference of 1,012 dinars for every individual. The official price of milk and the other materials such as soaps and washing powders given to children below one year is 3.400 dinars, while the market price is 700 dinars. Thus, the state pays a difference in price of 697 dinars.
