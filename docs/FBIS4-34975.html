BFN [From the "Evening Information Review" newscast] [Text] Fernando Remirez de Estenoz, Cuban ambassador to the United Nations, has said his country applauds the Group of 77's resolve to continue contributing to strengthening the role of the highest international organization in promoting economic growth and development. Speaking to the ministerial level meeting on the 30th anniversary of the aforementioned group, the Cuban diplomat said the premises and principles that brought about the creation of that movement continue to be as valid and necessary as before.
