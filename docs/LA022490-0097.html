February 24, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

TROY LINEBACKER SIGNS TO PLAY AT NORTHRIDGE

Jason Gudzanas, a linebacker from Troy High in Fullerton, signed a letter of intent Friday to play football at Cal State Northridge. He is the first high school player to be signed by CSUN this season.

Gudzanas, 6-foot-3, 200 pounds, was a first-team All-Freeway League selection in 1989.
