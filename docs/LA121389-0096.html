December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

ICN Biomedicals: Jean-Francois Kurz has been elected to the board of directors of ICN Biomedicals Inc., Costa Mesa. Kurz is a member of the board of directors and the executive committee of DG Bank Switzerland Ltd. He previously served as a general manager of TDB American Express Bank of Geneva, and, before that, was chief executive of Banque Gutzwiller, Kurz, Bungener S.A. in Geneva. ICN Biomedicals develops medical test kits and instrumentation.
