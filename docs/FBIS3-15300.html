Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A high-ranking Russian military man has reported in an interview with INTERFAX that the withdrawal to Russia of the first part of the strategic missiles located on the territory of Ukraine is expected in the first ten days of March. According to him, this batch will consist of sixty military units. The person interviewed by the correspondent of the INTERFAX agency expressed satisfaction with the fact that Moscow and Kiev have found a political way to resolve this problem. He stressed that the delay in withdrawing the missiles is explained by the fact that the necessary documents for the withdrawal of this specific cargo had not been drawn up.
