930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / International Company News: Cockerill sees steel side loss THE steel-making activities of Cockerill Sambre, the Belgian steel-based group, are likely to make a loss in 1993, said Mr Jean Gandois, chairman, writes Andrew Hill. Mr Gandois said the recession had deepened since October, but the company's cash reserves meant it was well-placed to resist the downturn.
