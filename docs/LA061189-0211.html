June 11, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

2 COUNTY RESIDENTS WIN IN 'BIG SPIN'

Two Orange County residents were among the 14 winners of the state lottery's "Big Spin" contest held Saturday.

Rhonda Johns, 21, a Buena Park nursing assistant, won $50,000 and Darryl Chamberlain, 40, a La Habra data processor, won $30,000.

The two biggest winners Saturday were Stephen Jacobson, 26, of La Mesa, and Kenneth Hildreth, 47, of Yuba City. Each won $1 million.
