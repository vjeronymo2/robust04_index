BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- A seminar of party cadres training centres was held here Tuesday on the lapse of 30 years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il began to work at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. Speeches were made there on the subject "Holding in High Esteem the Dear Leader Comrade Kim Chong-il at the Head of Our Party is a Great Historical Event Which Brought About a New Upswing in the Development of the Party and the Revolution" and other subjects. Comrade Kim Chong-il has comprehensively completed and brilliantly applied the revolutionary theory and method including the chuche-based theory of the party building, thus victoriously leading the whole party and the entire people to the accomplishment of the socialist cause and performing the immortal feats for the party and revolution, the era and the history, the speakers said. They noted that the feats performed by him in the work of training party cadres are that he has brought up cadres to be competent true leading men who have been tested politically and ideologically in a revolutionary way and prepared theoretically and practically and thus firmly built core elements to firmly defend and glorify the party, the revolution and the socialist system. Today the socialism of our style is winning victory one after another and our party is displaying its invincible might, not wavering in any vicious anti-DPRK moves of the imperialists, the speakers pointed out, saying this is a clear demonstration of the greatness of the idea and the leadership of the dear leader. Brilliant today and bright future of the party, the country and the people lie in holding him in high esteem at the head of the party and the revolution forever with loyalty and filial piety, the speakers stressed.
