940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: Fall in US sales expected to cost GrandMet Pounds 40m Shares in the international drinks and food giant Grand Metropolitan fell 26p to 457p as the group said falling demand in the US drinks market was likely to cost it Pounds 40m this year.
