930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / Newspapers fear VAT may lead to closures A FIFTH of Britain's regional newspaper titles might be forced to close with a total loss of nearly 2,500 jobs if the government imposed value added tax on their cover prices, a report commissioned by the Newspaper Society said yesterday. The impact of a 17.5 per cent tax on newspapers was described as 'catastrophic' by consultants Price Waterhouse in the report for the society, which represents most regional and local newspapers.
