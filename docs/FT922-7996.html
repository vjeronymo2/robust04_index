920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Lombard North Central bounces to Pounds 44.6m The selection of better business enabled Lombard North Central, National Westminster Bank's credit finance and leasing offshoot, to record pre-tax profits of Pounds 44.6m in the six months to March 31, against losses last time of Pounds 1.4m. The charge for bad and doubtful debts was slightly lower at Pounds 79.3m (Pounds 81.6m). Earnings per share were 16.9p, against 6p.
