930430 FT 30 APR 93 / Pay freezes for Sheffield staff MORE THAN half of pay deals reported this year by large engineering companies in the Sheffield area have involved a wage freeze. In addition, only one fifth of agreements involve increases above 3 per cent. The figures, from the Sheffield's Engineering Employers Federation, cover 50 deals. One company negotiated a 10 per cent pay cut, 27 agreed a standstill and three settled for under 2 per cent.
