940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: USDC Inv Trust The net asset value per share of USDC Investment Trust fell 2.5 per cent from 281.5p to 274.4p in the six months to June 30. This compares with a fall of 13 per cent in the FT-SE-A All-Share Index. Net revenue for the half year to the end of June was Pounds 931,000 (Pounds 685,000) for earnings per share of 2.54p (1.87p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.25p.
