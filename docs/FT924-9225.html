921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Gartmore Emerging net assets increase Gartmore Emerging Pacific Investment Trust reported an upturn in net asset value to 62.9p per share as at September 30, against 51.1p a year earlier. However, net revenue for the first nine months of 1992 dropped to Pounds 111,000, against Pounds 277,000 for the same period last year. Earnings per share came out at 0.18p (0.44p).
