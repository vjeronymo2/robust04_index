BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday evening in Tripoli, Armed Forces Inspector General Colonel Mustafa al-Kharrubi met Arab League Secretary General Dr. Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, who is currently on a visit to the Great Jamahiriyah. During the meeting, efforts made by the Arab League to find a just and peaceful solution to the so-called Lockerbie affair were reviewed and several other Arab and international issues discussed.
