January 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PASADENA TOURNAMENT OF ROSES MARKS ITS CENTENNIAL FESTIVAL

There is no other way to describe this year's Rose Parade, than to say that it was a most outstanding, superb, fantastic, and smashing show on earth. Definitely the best.

Above it, it couldn't have happened without all those talented people, who put this wonderful event together. They were blessed by God with special gifts.

ROSE ORENSTEIN

Los Angeles
