February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BIG WEST

Cal State Fullerton, which has won three of its past four games, plays San Jose State in a Big West Conference game today at 5 p.m. at San Jose Civic Auditorium.

San Jose State has lost 12 games in a row and is 0-7 since 10 players left the team in protest of Coach Bill Berry in January.

Fullerton (11-11, 6-7) ended a run that included victories over UC Santa Barbara and Nevada Las Vegas with a loss to Utah State Thursday. ROBYN NORWOOD
