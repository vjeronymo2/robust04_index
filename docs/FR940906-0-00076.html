This assessment is based on the fact that the rule will impose little or no costs on aircraft operators or aircraft manufacturers (U.S. or foreign). Paperwork Reduction Act In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (Pub L. 96&hyph;511), there are no requirements for information collection associated with this rule.
