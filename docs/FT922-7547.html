920518 FT 18 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Loss at Automagic in opening half Automagic Holdings, which operates shops taking in shoe repairs and dry cleaning, stayed in the red in the half year ended January 11 1992. The loss for 28 weeks came to Pounds 436,000. That compared with a profit of Pounds 43,000 for the comparable 37 weeks, but that was turned into a deficit of Pounds 96,000 for the 61 weeks ended June 29 1991. Turnover in the period was Pounds 6.47m (Pounds 8.73m) which produced an operating loss of Pounds 228,000 (profit Pounds 339,000). There were exceptional charges of Pounds 55,000, of which Pounds 42,000 accounted for director's compensation and loss on sale of properties. Losses per share reached 7.03p (earnings 0.49p).
