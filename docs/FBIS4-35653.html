Process JN3005163494 Amman Radio Jordan Network in Arabic 1100 GMT 30 May 94 JN3005163494 Amman Radio Jordan Network Arabic BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at his office today Pak Su-chol, special envoy of the [ROK] president. They discussed relations between the two friendly countries, particularly in economic, technical, and technological areas, as well as ways of promoting bilateral cooperation. During the meeting, Pak praised the democratic experiment in Jordan, as well as Jordan's effective role in supporting the peace process. The meeting was attended by Dr. Fawwaz Abu-al-Ghanam, acting minister of state for foreign affairs, and the ROK ambassador in Amman.
