940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / US company sues metals giant Metallgesellschaft, the heavily indebted Frankfurt-based metals, mining and industrial group, together with its BUS environmental technology subsidiary, are being sued in New York by Horsehead Resource Development Company. Horsehead, a US concern 45 per cent owned by BUS, is seeking at least Dollars 500m in damages. It alleges that BUS and Metallgesellschaft fraudulently induced Horsehead to enter a joint venture in 1990. Horsehead claims the German companies misrepresented the quality of their technologies, failed to market Horsehead's technology in Europe amd fraudulently concealed the existence of new technology developed by a BUS affiliate in Europe.
