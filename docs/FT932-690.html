930626 FT 26 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Syltone down Difficult trading conditions in the second half affected the full year's performance at Syltone, the transport engineering group. The pre-tax profit worked through at Pounds 909,000 for a total of Pounds 2.13m for the year ended March 31 1993, compared with Pounds 2.87m. Mr Tony Clegg, chairman, said the first quarter of the current year had been 'more encouraging'. He saw positive signs of an upturn in the US, while at home the 'worst is behind us'. Continental Europe, however, remained difficult. Turnover rose to Pounds 36.7m (Pounds 35m) with overseas accounting for Pounds 20.2m (Pounds 18.3m). Earnings per share came to 15.03p (22.22p) and the dividend is held at 9.45p with a final of 6.3p. Also proposed is a 1-for-1 scrip issue.
