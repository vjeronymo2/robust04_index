BFN [Article by "joe": "Gross Domestic Product Declined 0.3 Percent in Real Terms Last Year"] [Text] Prague--The Czech Republic's gross domestic product [GDP] declined by 0.3 percent in 1993, compared with 1992, in real terms. The Czech Statistical Office [CSU] estimates the total volume of GDP in current prices at 923.1 billion Czech korunas. As CSU Press Spokeswoman J. Lehnerova told our paper yesterday, the proportion of the nonstate sector in GDP reached more than 45 percent last year. The above data is only preliminary, Lehnerova said, because the information available at the time the estimate was made was not complete.
