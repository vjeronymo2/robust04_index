940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Parkside opens at 14p premium Shares in Parkside International, the Yorkshire-based packaging group, opened yesterday at a 14p premium to the 110p flotation price. The company, with a market capitalisation of Pounds 32.8m, said it expected to raise Pounds 12m through the sale of 11.04m shares. Trading volume yesterday was put at 3.6m shares, although the closing price was unchanged on the day.
