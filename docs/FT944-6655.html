941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / Call for millennium festival proposals Britain will celebrate the millennium with a year-long festival primed with National Lottery money, Mr Stephen Dorrell, the heritage secretary, said yesterday. He invited applications for big and small projects to be built with an estimated Pounds 1.7bn Millennium Fund money as permanent monuments to the year 2000. Ideas already floated include a national sports stadium for Manchester, a waterside opera house in Cardiff, a Gaelic and Nordic heritage centre on Skye, and a nationwide network of paths and cycleways.
