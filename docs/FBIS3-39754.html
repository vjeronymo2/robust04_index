Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin is in Spain on a two-day official visit. In his first meeting with Prime Minister Gonzalez at the Moncloa, only questions relating to the Middle East peace process were discussed. Present at the meeting were Foreign Minister Javier Solana and the ambassadors of the two countries. Solana stressed that Spain will try to play an active role in achieving peace between Arabs and Israelis. Rabin will be meeting a delegation of Spanish businessmen this afternoon.
