Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers, which met from 1100 to 1500 today under the chairmanship of President Ilyas al-Hirawi and attended by Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri and the ministers, has decided to reinstate the law providing for the death penalty for those convicted of premeditated murder, and to extend this punishment to those who kill for political motives. The Council referred to the Chamber of Deputies a host of draft laws designed to facilitate judicial work. These include an amendment of legal procedures with a view to shortening trial periods and speeding up interrogations in murder cases. [passage omitted]
