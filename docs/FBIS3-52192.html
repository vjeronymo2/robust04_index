Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Dec. 30, IRNA -- The National Iranian Gas Company (NIGC) on Thursday [30 December] denied a report by Azerbaijan's unofficial news agency that Iran is planning to send natural gas to Armenia through building a gas pipeline. The news agency on Tuesday, quoting Armenian sources, claimed that recently a delegation from NIGC travelled to Armenia and held talks with the Armenian vice-president on building the pipeline. The agency further claimed that the preliminaries for signing an agreement over the issue has been concluded and that it will be signed by the two sides by next April. The NIGC added that it had discussed exporting Iranian natural gas with some of the newly-independent Central Asian republics including Azerbaijan and Armenia, but at at present it has no intention of exporting gas to Armenia.
