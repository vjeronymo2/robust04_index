921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Plans to develop North Sea field KERR-McGEE oil, operator of the UK North Sea Gryphon field, has agreed to purchase a floating production vessel,the Tentech 850C. It hopes to begin production by the end of 1993. Government approval for the 100m-barrel field,which will be one of the fastest developments in the North Sea, is expected by the end of the year. The purchase price of the Tentech was not disclosed but analysts estimate Gryphon's development costs will be close to Pounds 300m.
