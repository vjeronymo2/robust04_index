November 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: REGION;

COASTAL PANEL RELOCATES

The California Coastal Commission on Tuesday moved its San Francisco headquarters to larger offices.

The state panel's new address is 45 Fremont Street, Suite 2000, San Francisco, CA 94105-2219. The agency's new phone number for general information is (415) 904-5200.

A spokesman for the commission, which oversees development all along the California coast and up to five miles inland, said the move was essential to provide more space for the agency's administrative staff. The panel is meeting in Marina del Rey this week.
