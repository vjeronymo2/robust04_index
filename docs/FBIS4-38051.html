BFN [Text] India has called upon the developing nations for joint action to combat new forms of protectionism. The minister of state for external affairs, Mr. Salman Khurshid, said developing countries should remain especially vigilant against those measures that are sought to be promoted in the guise of social and environmental concerns. Mr. Khurshid was addressing the special meeting of G-77 [Group of 77] developing nations in New York yesterday. He said it is important for the developing countries to ensure that their major trading partners in the industrial world observe the rule of law and of free trade and competition.
