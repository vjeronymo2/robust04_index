Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN ["Declaration No.1/94 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council," issued on 30 Jan 94] [Text] Appointment of Cabinet Minister. The State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] has appointed Lieutenant General Maung Thint, minister of development of border areas and national races, as minister of progress of border areas and national races and development affairs. By the authority vested in me, Signed; Khin Nyunt, Lieutenant General, secretary-1 of the SLORC.
