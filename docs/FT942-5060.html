940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / Shortlist for fingerprint deal The Home Office has shortlisted three computer companies to implement Britain's first comprehensive national automated fingerprint recognition system. They are International Business Machines, which will be supplied with automated fingerprint recognition technology by Morpho and Martin Marietta, TRW, bidding with Cogent as its partner, and Digital, in partnership with Printrak. A decision will be made in October. The system, which will cost Pounds 45m at present prices, will be used by police forces in England and Wales.
