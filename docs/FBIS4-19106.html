BFN [Text] Acting government spokesman Spiros Arotis said the government is satisfied with the United Nations Security Council letter to the UN secretary general. He stressed that the Cypriot Government believes the Turkish Cypriot side must lift its objections before an agreement on implementing the confidence-building measures [CBM's] is reached. The letter notes that President Glavkos Kliridhis accepted the 21 March document on implementing the CBM's on condition that Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas do the same, although he did raise a series of protests.
