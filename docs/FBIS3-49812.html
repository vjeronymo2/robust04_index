Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, the Serbian aggressor again started shelling Srebrenica. The regions of (?Lihtari) and Vranik were shelled from the direction of Bratunac, while the residential areas of the town were shelled from Krvavac. One civilian was killed and 11 seriously wounded. This was announced by the War Presidency of the Srebrenica commune. At about 1230, the aggressor shelled the Suceska area from the direction of Milici. After the shelling of all (?lines), the aggressor fired with anti-aircraft machine guns, guns, and other infantry weapons. Members of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] in our town can confirm this attack. It has been proven once again that Srebrenica is not protected according to the provisions of the Security Council Resolutions 819 and 824. This was stated in a communique issued by the Srebrenica War Presidency. Because of this, the Srebrenica War Presidency appeals once again to the Presidency and the Government of Bosnia-Herzegovina, as well as the General Staff of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina and UNPROFOR to require the aggressor strictly to observe the provisions of the agreement on the demilitarization of Srebrenica.
