Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 17 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received messages of greetings from party leaders of different countries on the new year 1994. The messages were sent by chairman of the African Socialist Movement of the Congo Gerard Francois Yandza, chairman of the Congolese Movement for the Construction of New Society Antonie Malonga, secretary general of the C.C. [Central Committee], Algerian National Liberation Front Abdel Hamid Mehri, President Andriantiana Rakutuvau and General Secretary Gisele Rabesahala of the Party of the Congress for the Independence of Madagascar, president of the National Executive Committee of the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru Angel Castro Lavarello and chairman of the Romanian Party for New Society Victor Voichita. They in the messages unanimously said that they sincerely wish the Korean people greater success in the socialist construction and the accomplishment of the cause of national reunification. They wholeheartedly wished Comrade Kim Chong-il a long life in good health.
