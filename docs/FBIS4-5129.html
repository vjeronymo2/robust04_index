BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Ali Alatas and his Netherlands counterpart held talks on the human rights issue during the latter's visit to Indonesia. The Netherlands foreign minister, Kooijmans, was accompanying Premier Ruud Lubbers on a state visit to Indonesia. Ali Alatas told newsmen in Surabaya, East Java, yesterday that he explained to Kooijmans about various problems in the country, including the human rights and East Timor issues and efforts to resolve them. On bilateral cooperation, Ali Alatas reiterated that economic ties between Indonesia and the Netherlands have proceeded smoothly, while the private sector from the two countries is involved in matters pertaining to the transfer of technology.
