BFN [Text] A communique has been issued by the security services, which says said that yesterday, 11 people, including eight policemen and three Russian nationals, were killed after an attack against a bus which was carrying Russians near the town of Ziama Mansouria in Jijel Province. The same source adds that the group which carried out the attack was liquidated after the security forces sealed off the area. The authorities immediately informed the Russian Embassy of the incident and took all the measures required by such circumstances.
