Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Feb 4 (CTK) -- To discuss the Czech Republic's relationship towards the North Atlantic Alliance in the framework of the U.S. Partnership for Peace project is the goal of a NATO delegation which arrived in Prague today, Director of the Foreign Ministry Press Department Marek Skolil told CTK today. The delegation, led by NATO Deputy Secretary General Gerhart von Moltke, is scheduled to meet with Deputy Foreign Minister Alexandr Vondra and with members of the parliamentary Security and Defence Committee and Foreign Affairs Committee. It is also to meet with Defence Minister Antonin Baudys. The NATO representatives came to Prague from Budapest where they discussed specific steps towards implementing the Partnership for Peace plan with high-ranking officials of the Foreign and Defence Ministries. On Wednesday [2 February] the delegation visited Albania, Bulgaria and Romania. The delegation is one of the three NATO missions which are currently travelling the Central and Eastern European countries to discuss details concerning Partnership.
