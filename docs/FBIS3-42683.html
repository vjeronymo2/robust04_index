Language: Russian Article Type:CSO ["Edict of the President of the Russian Federation: On the Federal State Service"] [Text] For purposes of providing for the function of the Federal State Service I decree 1. The Statute on the Federal State Service (appended) shall be approved. 2. It shall be established that, from the moment the present Edict goes into effect, all individuals holding state positions in the Administration of the President of the Russian Federation, the apparatus of the Government of the Russian Federation, the apparatus of the Security Council of the Russian Federation, the apparatuses of the chambers of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation, the Constitutional Court of the Russian Federation, the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation, the High Arbitration Court of the Russian Federation, and in federal organs of state power shall be considered to be in the Federal State Service. Work in staff positions in other federal state organizations and institutions shall be included in the Federal State Service by decisions of the president of the Russian Federation at the suggestion of the Personnel Policy Council under the President of the Russian Federation. 3. The statute on the Federal State Service shall not apply to individuals for whom questions of working in the state service and holding state positions are regulated by laws of the Russian Federation adopted before the present Edict takes effect. The present statute shall not regulate issues of positions of members of the Government of the Russian Federation, the head of the Administration of the President of the Russian Federation, and the secretary of the Security Council of the Russian Federation and also positions filled by appointment in order to provide for the political activity of individuals holding the aforementioned positions. In the future up until the adoption of a special legislative act regulating questions of the aforementioned positions, social guarantees established by the Statute on Federal State Service shall apply to individuals holding these positions in organs of executive power specified by the Constitution of the Russian Federation. 4. Regulations on discipline established previously in state organs by legislation of the Russian Federation and also ranks and titles for particular kinds of state service shall be retained. 5. The Government of the Russian Federation shall develop and submit drafts of acts of the president of the Russian Federation, the need for whose adoption ensues from the Statute on Federal State Service, and also on the basis of an analysis of the application of the aforementioned Statute--a draft law of the Russian Federation on the Federal State Service. 6. The present Edict shall be presented for the consideration of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation. 7. The present Edict shall take effect the day of its publication. [Signed] President of the Russian Federation B. Yeltsin Moscow, The Kremlin 22 December 1993 No. 2267
