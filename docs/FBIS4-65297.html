BFN [Text] "If there are any attempts to `take away' confidence in the government during the Senate vote, then the Northern League will ask for immediate elections." The statement was made by Umberto Bossi, the leader of the Northern League, who added that the "alliance currently in office was the only one capable of providing a democratic government." Bossi said that the opposition would have "to bear the responsibility of provoking fresh elections, which would not only impose on the country an irresolvable and dangerous crisis, but would also go against the will of the the Italian people who expressed their will at the ballot box."
