BFN [Text] At his office at 2000 today, Lieutenant General Mustafa Talas, deputy commander in chief of the Army and Armed Forces, deputy prime minister, and defense minister, received Canadian Defense Minister David Collenette and his accompanying delegation. The meeting was attended by Major General 'Awwad Bagh, deputy defense minister, and Canada's ambassador in Damascus. The conferees discussed the cordial relations between the armed forces of the two countries and ways of enhancing them. The guest minister arrived in Damascus this afternoon on a cordial visit, during which he will inspect Canadian troops operating within the disengagement troops on the Golan.
