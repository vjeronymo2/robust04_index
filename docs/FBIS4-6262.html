BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- A banquet was given in Pyongyang on the evening of April 18 for the Pyongyang-visiting group of former heads of state and government and politicians of different countries on a visit to Korea. Invited there were head of the group Rodrigo Carazo Odio, ex-president of Costa Rica, and members of the group, former Governor-General of Canada Edward Schreyer, former Prime Minister of Egypt Aziz Sedky, former member of the Parliament of France and member of the European Parliament Pierro Ceyrac, senior vice-president of the Center for Strategic and International Studies of the United States William Taylor, senior vice-president of the CNN International Eason Jordan, executive director of the Summit Council for World Peace Antonio Betancourt, director of research of the Summit Council for World Peace Mark P. Barry, former Senior Advisor to the Assistant Secretary of State for Human Rights of the United States James Zumwalt, Managing Editor of the WASHINGTON TIMES Josette Shiner, Executive Director of the WASHINGTON TIMES Photo Agency Victoria Yokota, President Hirogatsu Ota and Vice-President Togashi Takayoshi of the Summit Council for World Strategy of Japan, and other members and journalists accompanying them. Kim Yong-sun, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and head of the group Rodrigo Carazo Odio made speeches at the banquet.
