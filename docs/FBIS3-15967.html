Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- The Russian counterintelligence service press centre refused to comment on Thursday on media reports about the arrest of a German spy in Moscow. Russian Intelligence Service spokesman Aleksandr Mikhailov told ITAR-TASS that these reports need to be checked and verified. A German press and information department official told ITAR-TASS in Bonn that he did not receive any such information and therefore could not confirm or comment on the fact of the arrest of the German intelligence agent in Moscow who was said to have worked for the Bundesnachrichtendienst, Germany's intelligence service.
