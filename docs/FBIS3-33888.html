Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The most modern antitank missile, Nag [Cobra], has been successfully flight tested. Developed by the Defense Research and Development Organization, DRDO, it is a third-generation missile with a range of 4 km and is capable of destroying armor. A Defense Ministry release in New Delhi says four test-flights of Nag were successfully conducted for two days on Saturday and Sunday. A missile carrier truck vehicle was used for carrying out these tests. India is one of the three countries which are developing this class of advanced missiles.
