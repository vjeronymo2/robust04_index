920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Greenacre spreads to over Pounds 600,000 Greenacre Group, which operates two nursing homes in Scotland and four in the west country, expanded pre-tax profits from Pounds 377,000 to Pounds 605,000 in the half year to July 31. A further strong performance was expected in the second half, said Mr Tony Acton, chairman, both from existing homes and from newly acquired and developed beds coming on stream. Turnover rose from Pounds 1.26m to Pounds 2.08m, and the profit contribution from nursing homes was Pounds 597,000 (Pounds 330,000). Earnings per share were 0.45p (0.27p) and the interim dividend is 0.13p (0.125p).
