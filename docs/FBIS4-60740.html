BFN [Text] At 1000 today in Latakia, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Mr. Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, who is also head of the Chinese Federation of Trade Unions, and his delegation. The meeting was attended by comrade 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, assistant secretary general of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Barty, and Mr. Li Qingyu, the PRC's ambassador in Damascus. Mr. Wei conveyed greetings from PRC President Jiang Zemin and Prime Minister Li Ping to his excellency the president. Acknowledging the greetings, his excellency asked that they communicate his greetings and good wishes to the Chinese leadership and people. The Chinese delegation told the president they were satisfied with their talks in Damascus and that they would help boost ties between the two countries. The Chinese delegation said they were impressed by the advancment made by Syria, led by his excellency the president. The sides discussed the Middle East, with the Chinese delegation applauding Syria's position on the peace process. They said China's Middle East policies were in line with Syria's position as far as the peace process goes. A standing invitation for his excellency the presdient to visit China was renewed. The president promised to make the visit at an appropriate date.
