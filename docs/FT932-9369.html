930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / Bangkok fire offer Kader Industrial, the Hong Kong company with a 40 per cent stake in the Thai toy factory where at least 209 workers died in a fire this week, said yesterday the plant would compensate victims' families, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. It will pay about six months' wages to relatives of the deceased and two months' to the injured. Workers at the toy factory said they made between Dollars 120 and Dollars 160 a month.
