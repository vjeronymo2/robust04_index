October 19, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

The fifth annual Women in Film Festival will wrap up Sunday night at the Directors Guild with a presentation of the Lillian Gish Awards for excellence in film making, and the Los Angeles premiere of Sandy Wilcox's "American Boyfriends." Among the celebrities scheduled to present the Gish Awards are Jane Alexander, Dixie Carter, Hal Holbrook, Joan Van Ark, Jill Eikenberry, Michael Tucker, Helen Reddy and Henry Winkler. KABC-TV's Tawny Little will host the 8 p.m. event. ALEENE MacMINN
