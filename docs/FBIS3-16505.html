Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Tleuzhan Esilbayev: "Directors Will Become Property Owners by Edict"] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty] -- Kazakhstan President Nursultan Nazarbayev has signed an edict "On the Transfer of Part of Sovkhoz Property to the Ownership of the Directors." On the basis of the document in question, a director, having worked for 20 years in this position, will be allocated 10 percent of the redeemable part [vykupnaya chast] of property belonging to a state agricultural enterprise on a noncompensatory basis. Moreover, he will be given a further 10 percent (the state share) of the redeemable part of the property for temporary usage for a period of no more than five years.
