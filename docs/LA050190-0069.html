May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OAK VIEW;

CITY WILL CHOOSE 1ST FATHER OF YEAR

Oak View has decided to take notice of fathers.

Until now, the Oak View Civic Council had only honored a mother of the year and an outstanding citizen.

Now the council will choose its first father of the year at the annual awards dinner June 1.

Nominations will be taken for all three awards through May 22. THIA BELL
