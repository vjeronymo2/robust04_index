920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / Sixth Kenyan minister resigns Mr Njoroge Mungai, Kenya's environment minister, announced his resignation at the weekend, the sixth minister or assistant minister to quit in the last two weeks, Reuter reports from Nairobi. Mr Mungai said he would join the new opposition group, the Forum for the Restoration of Democracy (FORD), set up after Kenya ended its one-party system in December. He also blamed corruption within the government, election rigging and ethnic conflicts for his decision. Another leading political figure, Mr Maina Wanjigi, a former minister, has also announced his defection from the ruling Kenya African National Union (Kanu), saying he would join FORD.
