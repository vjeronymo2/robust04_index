BFN [Text] Kuwait, 30 May -- Haydar Abu Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, left Kuwait today. During his one-day visit, al-Attas conveyed a message from al-Bid to the amir of Kuwait. The content of the message was not disclosed. Al-Attas, who is touring a number of Arab countries, said that his visit to Kuwait was aimed at briefing Kuwaiti officials about the latest developments of the war raging in Yemen.
