January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

WASHINGTON D.C.;

BAKER, SOVIETS TO HOLD ARMS TALKS

Secretary of State James A. Baker III will go to Moscow on Feb. 6-7 for talks to help reduce obstacles to a new U.S.-Soviet strategic arms treaty, Administration officials said. The trip to Moscow is one of three sessions Baker intends to hold with Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard A. Shevardnadze before a U.S.-Soviet summit in Washington in June. Baker also may visit Prague to demonstrate U.S. support for the peaceful revolution in Czechoslovakia, officials said.
