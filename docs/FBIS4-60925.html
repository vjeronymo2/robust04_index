BFN [Text] Ukraine's president has issued a decree on improving the preparation of draft laws and conducting legal reform. In accordance with the decree, Ukraine's Ministry of Justice is additionally charged with organizing work on elaborating the concepts of draft legislative laws on the most important issues of the development of society and the state, their scientific argumentation, and the coordination of the activity of the central bodies of state executive power, scientific organizations, and higher education establishments of Ukraine in the area of preparing draft laws. Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers has been instructed to organize the work of the legal reform center and draft legislative work at Ukraine's Ministry of Justice within a period of two months.
