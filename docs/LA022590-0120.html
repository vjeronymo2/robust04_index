February 25, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

NOISE POLLUTION

Three cheers for your editorial Feb. 4, "Off-Roaders Don't Belong in Anza-Borrego Desert."

You failed to mention, however, one more good reason why off-roaders should be kept out of the state park: noise pollution.

I travel the back roads of Anza-Borrego by mountain bike, and I wander in the back country on foot. One of my primary rewards is the peace and quiet and solitude which the desert offers, and which is easily destroyed by the whine of an all-terrain vehicle. Let the stimulation junkies get their adrenaline highs on their noisy vehicles, but please, not in Anza-Borrego Desert State Park. That is not why the park is there.

PHILIPPE VERMEYEN

Vista
