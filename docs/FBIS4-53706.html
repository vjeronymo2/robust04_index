BFN [Text] Seoul, May 23 (YONHAP) -- South Korea and the United States are expected to have working-level talks in Washington very shortly to discuss matters related to third-round U.S.-North Korea high-level talks. Deputy Prime Minister Yi Hong-ku said at a National Assembly Foreign Affairs and Unification Committee session Monday that the third-round U.S.-North Korea meeting could be agreed on before long at the U.S.-North working-level contacts. A South Korea-U.S. working-level meeting is set to be held in Washington soon in connection with the U.S.-North Korea high-level talks likely to take place shortly in pace with a progress in the IAEA (International Atomic Energy Agency) inspection of North Korean nuclear facilities, Yi said.
