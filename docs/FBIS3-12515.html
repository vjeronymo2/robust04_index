Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A huge explosion shook the al-Waziriyah District in the center of the capital, Baghdad, yesterday, four days after three big explosions occurred in Karkuk. Iraqi opposition sources said the explosion yesterday took place outside the entrance of al-Mustansariyah University. They said the explosion targeted a big mural, which had a picture of tyrant Saddam Husayn, mounted outside the entrance to the university. Security forces rushed to the scene of the crime and the main entrance to the university was closed off.
