January 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

The competition for Los Angeles' morning radio audience could soon turn into an all-out war with the move here from New York of Scott Shannon, creator of the "Morning Zoo" concept of slap-happy hip talk on morning rock radio. He'll be taking over the morning show on KIQQ-FM. That will put him head to head in the mornings against KIIS-FM's long-time ratings champ Rick Dees and his close runner-up, KPWR-FM's Jay Thomas. In addition, Shannon has been named vice president for programming of Westwood One Radio Station Group. ALEENE MacMINN
