930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / Finance and the Family: Going Dutch on shares - At a Glance Robeco, the Dutch investment company, is celebrating its 60th anniversary and is offering investors the chance to buy shares free of commission and brokerage charges through its Swiss bank subsidiary. The minimum investment is for Pounds 5,000, or Dollars 10,000, and involves opening an account at the bank; the offer lasts until June 30. Robeco is an open-ended investment company which owns shares in around 400 companies in 20 countries.
