930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Increase in car credit purchases THE NUMBER of new cars purchased on credit in the second quarter of this year reached 221,532, a 12.6 per cent rise on the same period last year, says HPI, the vehicle credit finance information organisation. It was the third quarterly rise in succession. Credit sales of used cars were 9.2 per cent higher, at 247,529 - the fourth quarterly rise in succession for the used car sector.
