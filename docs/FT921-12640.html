920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Charles Baynes Pounds 1.1m acquisition Charles Baynes, the specialist engineer in the aerospace and construction sectors, has paid Pounds 1.1m cash for Frank Ford, a maker of components for the civil aircraft market. For the year ended July 31 1991 Ford returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 74,000 from a turnover of Pounds 3m. The net asset value at the year-end amounted to Pounds 580,000.
