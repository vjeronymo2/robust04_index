920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / Bosnian breakthrough claimed Mr Cyrus Vance, UN mediator, yesterday claimed a breakthrough in the independence dispute between Bosnia's rival ethnic communities and said they had pledged to seek a settlement, Reuter reports from Belgrade. Mr Vance, who met Moslem, Serb and Croat leaders in the Bosnian capital of Sarajevo, said they would attend EC-sponsored peace talks in Brussels this weekend.
