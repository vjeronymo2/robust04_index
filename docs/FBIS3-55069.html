Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] SNARK reported that the Georgian and Armenian Deputy Prime Ministers Avtandil Margiani and Vigen Chitechyan met in Tbilisi yesterday with the commander of the Russian troops in Transcaucasia, Lieutenant-General Fedor Reut, his deputy, and the chief of Staff [of the Russian troops in Transcaucasia], Major General Yuri (Bavoyevskiy). SNARK's reporter received this information from reliable sources close to the headquarters of the Russian troops. According to our information, the meeting discussed issues concerning the security of transport routes and energy lines entering Armenia.
