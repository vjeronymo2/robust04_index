February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Barr Sings the King: Two of the tabloids' favorite subjects -- Roseanne Barr and Elvis -- will be on display for a three-night stand in San Carlos, Calif., starting today.

It will be Barr's first stand-up show in three years and she plans to sing an Elvis tribute as part of her act, which also will include new husband Tom Arnold as an Elvis impersonator. "I'm singing," Barr said. "I know my fans have waited a long time to hear that." BETH KLEID
