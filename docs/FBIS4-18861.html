BFN [Article signed MAR/GGD/SVD: "Rwanda: Eight C-130's, Including One From the Netherlands, Have Left Melsbroek"] [Text] Brussels, 9 Apr (BELGA) -- All available C-130 Hercules aircraft of the Belgian Air Force, together with a Netherlands plane of the same type, were seen leaving Melsbroek [miliary] airport one after the other on Saturday morning between 0715 and 1100. The eight aircraft appeared to have been transporting essentially equipment and jeeps. During the afternoon (between 1400 and 1500), men from the Flawinne barracks are expected to set off, according to a reliable source. Part of these troops can be expected to be transported by the National Defense Force's two Boeing 727's. In addition, the U.S. Air Force (USAF) is expected to assist the Belgian Army by making available additional air transport. According to reliable sources, a giant C-5 Galaxy transport plane is expected at Melsbroek in the afternoon.
