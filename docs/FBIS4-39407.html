BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow June 7 TASS -- "Law is on Chubays' side". Sergey Filatov, chief of the Russian president's administration, said this in answer to a question from ITAR-TASS about his stand on a conflict regarding privatisation between Russian Deputy Premier, Chief of the State Committee on Property Anatoliy Chubays and Moscow Mayor Yuriy Luzhkov. Filatov believes that to change the rules of privatisation of state property 20 day before the validity of vouchers expires means to undermine the confidence of citizens in the authorities.
