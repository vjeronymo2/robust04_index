BFN [Text] Twenty-two large industrial and production companies under the supervision of the Organization for the Development and Modernization of Iranian Industries [OEMII] will be transferred to the private sector. In a statement published this afternoon, OEMII, an affiliation of the Ministry of Heavy Industries, said: In accordance with government economic policy, shares in the 22 companies will be transferred through Tehran's stock exchange or auction. Companies whose shares are totally ready for transfer to the private sector are active in the production of machines, light and heavy vehicles, engines, as well as engineering and road-construction machinery.
