Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Konstantin Parishkura: "Donetsk To Conduct Regional Poll"] [Text] A session of the Donetsk Oblast Soviet has rejected the protest of Ukraine's general prosecutor regarding the cancellation of the poll, scheduled simultaneously with the elections for the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet, on state language status for Russian, and a federal system for Ukraine and its joining the CIS. The poll will be held all the same.
