920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Poland and IMF in loan talks Poland and the IMF yesterday began talks to renew Warsaw's access to a Dollars 2.5bn (Pounds 1.41bn) loan suspended by the IMF last September when the previous government failed to curb the booming budget deficit, Reuter reports from Warsaw. Mr Stefan Kawalec, deputy finance minister, said an agreement would include guidelines on the size of the budget deficit.
