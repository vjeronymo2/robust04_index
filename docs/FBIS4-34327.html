BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] On 17 June, PRC Ambassador to Cuba Xu Yicong and several Embassy staff members paid an official visit to Guantanamo Province. Upon his arrival in this city, Xu Yicong was extensively briefed by Juan Carlos Robinson, member of the central committee of the Communist Party of Cuba and first secretary of the party in the province, on social and economic developments in the province. Robinson informed Xu Yicong of the efforts that the population is making to eliminate, as soon as possible, the consequences of the May rains, which caused damages estimated at 90 million pesos. Xu Yicong expressed satisfaction with his visit to Guantanamo and highlighted the similarity of ideas, principles, and paths chosen by the peoples of Cuba and of China to build socialism, aside from the different characteristics of the two countries.
