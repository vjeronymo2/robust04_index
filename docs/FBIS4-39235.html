BFN [Text] Moscow, June 6, IRNA -- Russian authorities have banned Moscow-based weekly AL-QUDS for alleged violation of Russian laws and printing of anti-Zionist articles. Officials cite anti-Jewish propaganda and anti-government stance of the weekly as reasons for its ban. The weekly was viewed by political parties and people as a prestigious publication which criticized the government, revealed plots of Zionism and imperialism and published documents relating to the last year's government troop attack on parliament.
