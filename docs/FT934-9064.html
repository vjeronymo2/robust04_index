931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / Computer services group bought EDS-SCICON, the information technology subsidiary of General Motors of the US, is to buy DVOIT, the organisation that provides computer services to the Department of Transport and its executive agencies. DVOIT, based in Swansea, was formerly the drivers, vehicle and operators information technology arm of the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency. It became a separate executive agency just over a year ago, with an annual turnover of about Pounds 26m and about 480 employees, and is the first agency to be privatised.
