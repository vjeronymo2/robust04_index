April 28, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

RESIDENTIAL BUILDING VALUES

The value of home building permits issued in the county was up 5% over 1989's level.

During the First two months of this year (the most recent information available), permits for $248.9 million were let. That's also the first year-to-year two-month increase since 1987. The January-February totals for 1988 and 1989 were both 4% lower than the year before. Here's how the last five years compare:

1986: $234,166

1987: $259,336

1988: $249,074

1989: $238,090

1990: $248,955

Note: Amounts shown in thousands of dollars, not adjusted for inflation

Source: Construction Industry Research Board
