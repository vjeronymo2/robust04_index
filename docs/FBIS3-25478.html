Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The negotiating council has decided to send a written invitation to the Freedom Alliance inviting them to join the council. Negotiating council Chairman Pravin Gordhan said he had received feedback that members of the alliance would appreciate an invitation requesting some kind of a response to the constitutional proposals. Mr. Gordhan said the council expected the alliance to respond to the invitation by early evening. The council is processing a package of constitutional concessions and amendments to secure the Freedom Alliance's participation in the election. The government and the ANC [African National Congress] earlier submitted their proposals to the negotiating council at Kempton Park. The Freedom Alliance did not attend the meeting at the World Trade Center.
