940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / UK Company News: SR Gent pulls out of retailing SR Gent is to withdraw from retailing and concentrate on its core manufacturing business. The fashion garments group, which operates a chain of 28 women's fashion stores under the Susan Woolf name, said the start-up costs and protracted recession in mainland Europe had resulted in bigger trading losses than planned. A Pounds 7m provision would be made in the year to end-June 1994.
