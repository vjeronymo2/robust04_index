920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Spirits sales fall is reported SALES of spirits may have fallen by up to 15m bottles last year, according to the Wine and Spirit Association. Trading reports suggested that sales during the Christmas period might have been 5 per cent lower than the previous year, said Mr David Jenkins, the association's chairman. Figures covering the first nine months of the year showed a decline in sales of 4.2 per cent from 563m hectolitres of alcohol to 539m hectolitres.
