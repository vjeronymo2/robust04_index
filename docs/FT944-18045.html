941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: UDO in Pounds 3.4m deal UDO Holdings, the supplier of drawing office materials, has bought LDO and Geoprint for a total of Pounds 3.38m cash. LDO, another drawing office supplies company, reported pre-tax profits for the year to May 31 1994 of Pounds 366,000 and net assets at the period end of Pounds 1.21m. Geoprint, a reprographic company, had pre-tax profits of Pounds 106,000 for the year to March 31 1993 and year end net tangible assets of Pounds 900,000.
