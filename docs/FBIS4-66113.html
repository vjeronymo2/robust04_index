BFN [Unattributed article: "Rwanda: Italy Wants Clear Signal From UN"] [Text] (ANSA) Brussels, May 24 -- Italy remains ready to participate in an eventual international effort in favor of Rwanda but first wants to see a clear decision by the United Nations, Italian Defense Minister Cesare Previti said here on Tuesday [24 May]. Speaking on the sidelines of the ministerial NATO planning committee, the defense minister added, "We expect to see some clear ideas. Foreign Minister (Antonio) Martino is sounding out available energies in the United Nations" for a humanitarian effort. Italy, Previti said, stands ready to do its part as it has in the past.
