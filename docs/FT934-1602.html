931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / International Company News: Metro Holdings sells its interest in toys Metro Holdings, the Singaporean retailing and construction conglomerate said it would sell its entire 50.1 per cent interest in Toys 'R' Us Metro to TRU for USDollars 2.5m cash, Reuter reports from Singapore. It is also finalising the sale of its 60 per cent sake in Toys 'R' Us (Malaysia) to be announced later, the company said. Metro said Toys 'R' Us Inc had expressed a desire to own and operate TRUS and TRUM. The relationship between Toys R Us and TRU Inc was not specified.
