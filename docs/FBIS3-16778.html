Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Monday Prime Minister Victor Chernomyrdin submitted to the Russian State Budget [as received] a draft law on federal budget spending in the second quarter of 1994. Under the draft in the second quarter the Russian Central Bank is to lend the government 10.6 trillion rubles for covering the federal budget deficit. The loan will be given for 10 years at 10 268460096nterest with repayment in equal installments beginning in 1998. In his message to the Duma the prime-minister said that before the draft law is approved the government intends to earmark means proceeding from budget plans and considering the actual spending in the first quarter. According to preliminary reports, in the first quarter federal budget spending has been as follows: on the economy -908 Bn rbls, government investments 969 Bn, on social and cultural programs 1109 Bn, on defence 3627 Bn, on law enforcement bodies 1077 Bn, to local budgets under mutual settlements, including subsidies and subventions 2080 Bn. The budget message also said that taxes and payments to the budget in the first two months amount to 4.7 trillion. The figure predicted by the government for March should not exceed 3.3 trillion. The prime-minister pointed to the reduction of the share of taxes and payments in the federal budget. Last January the debt of Bashkiria to the federal budget was 52 Bn rubles, Tatarstan 37 Bn and Yakutia 32 Bn.
