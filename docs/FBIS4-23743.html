BFN [Text] The Rwandan Patriotic Front [RPF] is conducting elections of local leaders in Prefecture Byumba. The exercise started on 12 June 1994 in Mukarange and Muranda sectors, in Prefecture Byumba. The elections were conducted democratically and (?peacefully). After the elections, the RPF officials explained the RPF political approach to Rwanda's problems. The delay in the implementation of the Arusha peace agreements and why the RPF has been forced to resume fighting were also explained to the gathering. The officials of the RPF repeated the call on the people who have fled their homes to return and settle down, for the RPF has re-established security for all persons in the areas under their control. The RPF chairman, Colonel Alexis Kanyarengwe, has said that even the Republican National Movement for Democracy and Development militias, who will lay down their arms and stop committing atrocities, will be free to go home and settle down.
