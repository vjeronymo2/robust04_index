940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / Chambers merger to go ahead The merger of north-east England's three chambers of commerce to create a single regional body is to go ahead, after members of the Tyne and Wear and Teesside chambers voted in favour at meetings yesterday. The membership of the third chamber, Tynedale, in Northumberland, had already backed the merger. The North East Chamber, due to start operating next January, will have nearly 3,000 member companies.
