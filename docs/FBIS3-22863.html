Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Official data on chemical weapons in Russia is very conservative, thinks Aleksey Yablokov, chairman of the Russian Security Council's Interdepartental Commission for Ecological Safety. According to Interfax, he declared this at a news conference today. He said that in Russia there are not 40,000 tonnes of toxic substances, as officially stated, but possibly a whole order of magnitude more. Apart from Aleksey Yablokov, the news conference was also attended by Lev Fedorov, a doctor of chemical sciences and co-author of the scientist Vil Mirzoyanov, who published the well-known article Poisoned Politics [Otravlennaya politika].
