920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Quadrant chief quits over vessel payments Mr Robert Brothers yesterday resigned as chairman of Quadrant Group, which said that Beckworth Enterprises, owned by a trust of which Mr Brothers was a beneficiary, had defaulted on an agreement to underwrite earnings from two vessels Quadrant bought from Beckworth in 1991. Lord Rees, an existing director, has become chairman.
