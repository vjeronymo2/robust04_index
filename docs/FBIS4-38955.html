BFN [Text] Today the Crimean parliament excluded from its agenda the issue on giving the force of law to the presidential decrees on the issues which were not as yet regulated by legislation. A total of 51 deputies voted for this decision (the total number of deputies in the Crimean parliament is 98). Earlier, Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov had proposed that the Crimean parliament give him additional powers. Meshkov said his proposals concerning force of law to be given to the presidential decrees was of great importance for Crimea which would have more possibilities for quick reaction to the changing situation in Crimea and around it.
