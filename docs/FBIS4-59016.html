BFN [Text] Most Knesset factions voted last night in favor of a resolution stating that Jerusalem will forever remain a united city under Israeli sovereignty. The Knesset asserts that Israel will prevent any attempt to impair the city's status and territorial integrity. Jerusalem and its environs are not a political or a security issue but the very soul of the Jewish people, the Knesset resolution affirms. The resolution was initiated by Likud Chairman Binyamin Netanyahu, and Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin endorsed it.
