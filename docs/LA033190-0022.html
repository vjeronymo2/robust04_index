March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CRISIS OVER LITHUANIAN INDEPENDENCE

Soviet tanks and helicopters in Vilnius. KGB troops closing the Lithuanian border.

Soviets expelling foreign diplomats and reporters. All of this in a country that the United States has recognized as independent for more than 50 years. So I ask: What "peace dividend"? What "new" Soviet Union? What "new" world order? It seems obvious to anyone who will face the facts that the Soviet Union still only understands force, repression and submission.

NEIL SIEGEL

Redondo Beach
