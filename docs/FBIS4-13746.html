BFN [Text] The National Assembly was informed today that the government has again raised with the French Government the issue of the supply of the nuclear power plant to Pakistan. Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali, in a written reply to a question, described as false the rumors that the United States has sought Pakistan's permission for establishing military bases. He said there is no question whatsoever of any commitment from Pakistan's side to the United States to set up military bases here. In reply to a question by Mian Abdul Waheed, the foreign minister said spares of the defense equipment, including the F-16's, are being purchased from the United States on commercial basis due to the Pressler Amendment.
