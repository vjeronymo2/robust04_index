930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Acquisitions boost Norbain Norbain Electronics, USM quoted distributor of closed circuit television and access control equipment for security and surveillance, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 33,000 to Pounds 224,000 in the half year ended October 31 1992, aided by acquisitions. Turnover was Pounds 7.62m (Pounds 5.75m). Earnings per share were 2.16p (0.33p). Mr John Nicol, chairman, said that sales to Continental Europe were expanding. After the end of the period the company acquired Baxall and Peca Electronics for Pounds 414,000 cash. Peca was subsequently sold for Pounds 49,000, the price at which it was bought.
