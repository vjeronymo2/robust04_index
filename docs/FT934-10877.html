931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Unichem expands Moss Chemists UniChem has added a further nine shops to its Moss Chemists chain at a cost of Pounds 3.42m. The company now operates 253 shops through E Moss. The nine outlets have a combined turnover of Pounds 5.76m. UniChem intends paying with Pounds 2.61m in cash and the balance via the issue of 309,482 ordinary shares. Included are six pharmacies run by the north-east chain Sarah Kirkup.
