August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

POPE NAMES ROMANIA ENVOY, 1ST IN 40 YEARS

Pope John Paul II on Saturday appointed the Vatican's first envoy to Romania in 40 years.

Father John Bukovsky was named apostolic nuncio in Bucharest, a move made possible after diplomatic relations were re-established between the Holy See and Romania three months ago.

The Pope elevated Bukovsky, 66, to the rank of archbishop.
