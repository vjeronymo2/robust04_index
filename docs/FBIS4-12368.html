BFN [Text] Asyut, Upper Egypt, 4 Apr (MENA)--Three terrorists were killed today in a clash with security forces and citizens in Asyut. The clash took place on the al-Ma'abidah Island in Markaz Manfalut, Asyut Governorate. Several citizens clashed with three people, strangers to the island, who had weapons hidden under their clothes. When the three noticed that the citizens were in pursuit, they opened fire on them. A crowd gathered and chased them, preventing their escape. One of the three terrorists was wounded [as received] in an exchange of fire with citizens carrying licensed weapons. The citizens apprehended the other two terrorists to hand them over to the security forces who arrived on the island. When the two realized the security forces were approaching, they tried to escape, prompting the policemen to shoot them. They died instantly. The terrorists were found in possession of an automatic rifle and a pistol. The pistol belonged to a noncommissioned police officer who was assassinated last year. The Manfalut prosecutor started an investigation into the incident.
