940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / International Company News: Caisse de Depot investments Quebec's public sector pension fund manager, the Caisse de Depot, has invested CDollars 108m (USDollars 82m) in a limited partnership managed by New York's Blackstone Group and in the British Schroder Buy-out Fund III, managed by Schroder Venture, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Blackstone invests in US corporate buy-outs, reorganisations and recapitalisations.
