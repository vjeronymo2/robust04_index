BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry is waiting for facts from the Cambodian prime ministers concerning the accusation by the Cambodian foreign minister about Thailand supporting the Khmer Rouge [KR] . Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri was asked by news reporters about Cambodian Foreign Minister Prince Norodom Sirivut's charge, which he made when he was in Malaysia that Thailand supported the Khmer Rouge and that there are pictures substantiating the charge. Prasong told reporters that he had instructed the Thai ambassador in Phnom Penh to seek a meeting with the Cambodian prime ministers in order to get facts from them so the Thai Government could take a proper course of action. Prasong said he did not understand why Prince Norodom Sirivut did not tell Thailand directly when he visited Thailand last time. [Begin recording] [Prasong, in progress] ... this is not good for our relations -- existing and future relations. I will wait for information from the ambassador. [Unidentified reporter] Prince Sirivut said he brought up this matter when he was in Malaysia. He said he wanted Malaysia to convey his concern to Thailand as Malaysia is an ASEAN friend of Thailand. [Prasong] Why didn't he discuss it with us when he was in Thailand? We would like to ask him this question? [end recording]
