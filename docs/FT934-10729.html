931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / World Trade News: Lucas technology for China Lucas Industries, the UK automotive to aerospace group, has signed its first technology transfer deal for the production of motor components in China. Its first stage envisages the production of 200,000 vehicle brake systems a year, John Griffiths writes from London. The agreement covers the provision of product and manufacturing technology to a new joint venture company, Hwa Heng, set up between Lucas's licensee in Taiwan and the Wuhu Auto Parts factory in Anhui province. Lucas has no stake in the venture.
