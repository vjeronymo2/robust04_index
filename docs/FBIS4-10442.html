BFN [Text] On 12 April, Sugar Minister Nelson Torres stated that the remaining part of April is decisive for the sugarcane harvest and that we have to do everything possible, and more, to meet the production plan. He made this statement during a harvest emulation assessment over the last 10 days in the eastern provinces. Torres, who is also a member of the Politburo, added that the weather is helping; we therefore have to take advantage of it during the last 20 days of April and make the best effort in cutting and grinding with great efficiency.
