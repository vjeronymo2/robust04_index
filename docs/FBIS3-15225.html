Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 1/3/1994 -- Reliable sources told BASA that a group of persons involved in the armed rebellion in September-October 1993 in Moscow, released by the decision of Russian State Duma, arrived in Tiraspol. According to some statements Nikolay Terekhov chairman of the Russian Union of Officers, released on the same basis, also came to Tiraspol. In September 1993, he organized in Moscow the armed attack on the residence of CIS Armed Forces.
