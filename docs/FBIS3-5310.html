Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 5. -- The national daily NHAN DAN today runs an editorial hailing the result of the Cambodian National Assembly chairman's visit to Vietnam as good sign of strengthening of the friendship and multi-faceted cooperation between Vietnam and Cambodia. It says that the fine result of Samdech Chea Sim's official friendship visit is vivid manifestation of important significance of the consolidation and strengthening of the friendhip and multi-faceted cooperation between the people of the two countries. The paper expresses the Vietnamese people's pleasures at the important achievements recorded by the Cambodian people in implementing their target of building an independent, peaceful, and non-aligned country, friendly to all other countries.
