March 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DEATH OF WRITER EDWARD ABBEY

It was with great sadness that I learned of the death of author Edward Abbey. I will not be able to think of the arid beauty of northern Arizona or Utah's Canyonlands without his writings coming to mind.

I hope that his publishers will release the draft of his last novel. A sequel to "The Monkey Wrench Gang" will be welcomed with open arms.

Thank you, Mr. Abbey, for your thought-provoking plots and rich characters that will continue to defend the red rock country from the fences and pavement of land developers, and rest assured that Hayduke lives!

BRUCE NOLTE

Pasadena
