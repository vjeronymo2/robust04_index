920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / International Company News: Power Financial sells HD Intl POWER Financial, the financial services arm of the Desmarais family's Power Corp of Canada, is selling its 60 per cent interest in HD International, a London investment management company, to Credit Suisse Asset Management for an undisclosed sum, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. PFC said it was withdrawing from European financial services operations, where it does not have a dominant market share.
