931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / The Old and New Lloyd's (circa 1989) The Old Lloyd's (circa 1989) Capital provided by 30,000-plus Names with unlimited liability Organised into more than 400 syndicates by More than 200 agents Lloyd's Corporation administers and regulates market but stands back from its commercial operations THE NEW LLOYD'S (circa 1994) Capital provided by 17,000 individual Names with unlimited liability, many spreading their risks across the market through so-called MAPA arrangements; institutional and retail investors backing investment trusts and companies, some of them listed on Stock Exchange Number of syndicates cut to 150 Fewer than 100 agents Lloyd's Corporation plays more active role in enforcing professional standards in market, developing strategy and controlling costs
