930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Chinese acetic acid plant JOHN Brown, part of the Trafalgar House construction, property, hotels and shipping group, has won a Dollars 60m (Pounds 42m) contract to build an acetic acid production plant at Wujing near Shanghai. The contract has been awarded by China National Technical Import and Export Corporation for Shanghai Chemical Industries. Acetic acid is an intermediate chemical in the production of a range of items, including paints and plastics. The process equipment for the plant will be bought in Britain, says John Brown, responsible for engineering and procurement for the plant, which is expected to start production in 1996.
