Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Two North Korean solders, who were found adrift on the West Sea near Paengnyong Island, were rescued by our Navy on 27 January. These two North Korean solders were repatriated to North Korea through Panmunjom at 1500 [0600 GMT] on 1 February. The two North Korean soldiers are Private First Class Kim Chol-chin and Private Kim Kyong-chol of North Korea's People's Security Forces. They were being treated at the capital military hospital. The issue of their repatriation was carried out from a humanitarian point of view, following their desire to return to the North. The National Defense Ministry had discussed this matter with the North Korean side through the Military Armistice Commission on 29 January. This is the first instance of repatriation of North Korean soldiers who are on active duty.
