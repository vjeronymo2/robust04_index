930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Moorgate Smaller net assets improve Moorgate Smaller Companies Income Trust lifted net asset value from 112.01p to 125.29p per share over the 12 months to April 30. The trust, which in February raised a net Pounds 24.3m from a placing and offer of new shares, reported attributable revenue of Pounds 2.15m, down from Pounds 2.33m, for earnings of 4.41p (4.81p) per share. A proposed final of 2.48p brings the total for the year to 4.28p (4.125p).
