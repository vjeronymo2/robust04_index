BFN [Text] A U.S. economic delegation arrived in Kiev today. The delegation includes representatives of the U.S. State Department, corporate circles, banking institutions, and legal experts. The visitors wish to acquaint themselves with the progress of market reforms in Ukraine, which will help them pinpoint the main directions of U.S. aid to our country. The visit's agenda includes meetings with the Ukrainian president's administration and other high-ranking officials. Also planned are negotiations with the leadership of the National Bank and the Association of Ukrainian Commercial Banks. On 5 May, the delegation members are going to organize a round-table meeting with prominent Ukrainian economists.
