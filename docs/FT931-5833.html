930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Fall in number of business failures THERE were 404 receiverships or administrative receivership appointments last month compared with 539 in February last year and 561 in February 1991, figures compiled by accountants Touche Ross show. Receiverships and administrations are running at 90 a week compared with 100 a week last year but Touche Ross warned that businesses need additional liquidity. It said: 'The majority of additional funding, at least in the short term, has to come from bank borrowing.'
