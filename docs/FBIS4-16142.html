BFN [Excerpt] The issue of the Black Sea Fleet is to be resolved in the spirit of the Massandra protocol. A provisional understanding to this effect has been reached in Kiev at the meeting of the Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk with a Russian delegation which included, among other participants, Yuriy Dubinin, a special envoy of the Russian Foreign Ministry, and the commander in chief of the Russian Navy, Admiral Feliks Gromov. I would like to remind you that under the Massandra document the Ukrainian part of the Black Sea Fleet is to be handed over to Russia as a part payment towards Ukraine's debt for the Russian energy resources.
