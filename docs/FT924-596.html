921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / Air crash in Libya leaves 157 dead A LIBYAN Boeing 727 crashed yesterday, killing all 157 people aboard, official sources said, Reuter reports from Tunis. An official at Tripoli airport said the aircraft was on a domestic flight to Tripoli from the eastern city of Benghazi. Libyan Arab Airlines flight 1103 crashed at 10.07am local time, the official news agency Jana said. Foreigners were among the dead, the agency added. Neither Jana nor the airport official could say how many.
