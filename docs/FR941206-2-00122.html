DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 2125] Delegation of Authority 215; Designation and Delegation of Authority Concerning the Department's Ethics Program Pursuant to the regulations of the Office of Government Ethics, 5 C.F.R. 2638.202, and under the authority delegated to me under Delegation of Authority No. 198 dated September 16, 1992, I hereby designate William M. McQuade, senior Attorney Adviser responsible for ethics, as the Alternate Designated Agency Ethics Official for the Department of State. I delegate to him the duties referred to in Section 2638.203, to be carried out in conjunction with the Designated Agency Ethics Official of the Department. Dated: November 9, 1994. Richard M.
