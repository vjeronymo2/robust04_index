910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Vivat returns to the black Vivat Holdings returned to profits and the dividend list in 1990. The casualwear retailer reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.65m against losses of Pounds 1.15m. Earnings were 8.3p (losses 2.8p) and a 0.5p dividend is proposed. Turnover fell to Pounds 103.71m (Pounds 108.51m). Litigation with Chelsea Man continues and Vivat has made a Pounds 1.8m extraordinary provision for legal costs.
