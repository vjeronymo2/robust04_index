930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Health link with work claimed A STRONG link continues to exist between where people live and the likelihood of their becoming permanently unwell, according to figures published by the GMB general union. The 20 areas in which the highest proportion of the working population were permanently sick were locations, or former locations, of heavy industry. The union said this suggested that both environmental factors and the work itself were significant causes of ill-health.
