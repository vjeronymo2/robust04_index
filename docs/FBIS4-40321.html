BFN [INTERFAX report: "Minsk Asks Moscow to Revise Prices of Energy Resources"] [Text] Belorussia [Belarus] has requested the Russian Federation Government to revise the foreign trade prices of Russian energy resources. A ranking source close to the Belarussian Government has stated that "the lowering of prices of energy resources for Belorussian consumers would help a merger of the monetary systems of Russian and Belorussia."
