940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / Card sales steady Spending with credit and debit cards was fairly flat in August for most high street retailers, the Credit Card Research Group said yester-day. Total spending on credit and debit cards was Pounds 5.017bn in August, up by 5.2 per cent from July and 19 per cent higher than in August last year. The year-on-year figure was the same in July. Credit-card spending was Pounds 3.119bn in August, up 4.6 per cent from July, while debit-card spending was Pounds 1.898bn in August, an increase of 6 percent from the previous month.
