BFN [Text] There was a wave of arrests in East Timor on the eve of the meeting between [Portuguese Foreign Minister] Durao Barroso and [Indonesian Foreign Minister] Ali Alatas. Ten East Timorese were arrested early this month by the Indonesian Army, and were accused of attempting to organize a demonstration for 6 May, the date of the meeting between the foreign ministers. A report by the Fretilin says that some of those arrested are being tortured and beaten up.
