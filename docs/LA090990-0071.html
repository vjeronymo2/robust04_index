September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LOOKING AT WHAT MAKES LEWIS WORK

We send a round of applause to Champlin for his balanced and critically accurate article on Lewis.

Jerry's fans get a little tired of the constant Jerry-bashing, an activity that seems to have become a trendy cottage industry practiced by several uninformed journalists and critics. It was, therefore, refreshing to read about his accomplishments for a change.

. . . And we still say that Jerry has the best legs of any male star in Hollywood.

FAWNIE WEISSMAN

Canyon Country
