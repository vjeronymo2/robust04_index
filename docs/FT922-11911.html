920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: First quarter rise at AIB's US offshoot First Maryland Bancorp, Allied Irish Banks' wholly-owned US subsidiary, lifted pre-tax income in the first quarter to Dollars 30.6m (Pounds 17.2m) against Dollars 14.7m. After tax of Dollars 10.6m (Dollars 4.29m) net income for the three months to March 31 rose by 93 per cent to Dollars 20m (Dollars 10.4m). The directors said that increases in fee income, lower provisions for possible credit losses and continued attention to cost control all contributed to the favourable performance.
