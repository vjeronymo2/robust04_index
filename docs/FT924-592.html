921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / Collor defence The head of the impeachment proceedings against Brazil's suspended president Fernando Collor de Mello named a former attorney general yesterday to defend him after Mr Collor forced a week's delay in his Senate trial, Reuter reports from Brazilia. Supreme Court President Sydney Sanches, who is overseeing the impeachment, named Mr Inocencio Martires Coelho as Collor's new defence attorney and put off his trial for a week.
