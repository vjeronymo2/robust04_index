Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [CTK report: "A Contract With India"] [Text] Kosice--The director of the East Slovak Engineering Works [VSS], I. Dufinec, has signed a memorandum with the Indian BEML company on cooperation in commencing special production for the requirements of the Indian Army and for joint commercial purposes in specific territories. This involves the type-AV 15 military tow trucks that the VSS is assembling on Tatra undercarriages. A similar contract with a partner in the United Arab Emirates should be signed this year, and negotiations are under way with Syria, among other countries.
