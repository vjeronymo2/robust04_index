May 26, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CELEBRITY DEATHS: DAVIS, HENSON AND JILL IRELAND

It's hard to believe that the "Candy Man" and the "Muppet Man" are no longer with us.

So much talent. So much creativity. So much energy. So much love of life. Sammy Davis Jr. and Jim Henson will indeed be missed.

Sammy and Miss Piggy and Kermit the Frog are as much American institutions as baseball and cherry pie. Sammy's broad smile and dynamic style and Kermit's soft-spoken charm have made a lasting impression on me.

KENNETH L. ZIMMERMAN

Cypress
