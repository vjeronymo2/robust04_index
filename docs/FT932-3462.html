930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: Vtech shows sharp decline to Dollars 8.5m Vtech Holdings, the Hong Kong-based electronics, computers and telecommunications group, announced reduced pre-tax profits of Dollars 8.5m (Pounds 5.5m) for the year to March 31. Profits last time amounted to Dollars 24.6m. Turnover edged ahead to Dollars 565m (Dollars 561m) but the pre-tax result was affected by lower profits of Dollars 2.2m (Dollars 16.8m) on the sale of investments. Earnings per share fell to 2 cents (15.9 cents) and the final dividend is omitted, making 1 cent (7 cents) for the year.
