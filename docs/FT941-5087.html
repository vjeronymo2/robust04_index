940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / International Company News: NZ Trust Bank plans to float 22% of share capital Trust Bank, New Zealand's fifth-largest retail bank, is to raise NZDollars 200m (USDollars 114m) by floating 22 per cent of the company at NZDollars 2.10 a share, it said yesterday. It is expected that the 98m shares will be split in approximately equal three blocks for institutional investors, clients of selected brokers, and bank customers and staff. A further 2.3m shares are being reserved for two of the nine regional trusts which control the bank.
