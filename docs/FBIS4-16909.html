BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau] 19 April TASS -- A statement of the Romanian Parliament's Chamber of Deputies to denounce Moldovan joining of the Commonwealth of Independent States and the CIS economic union was estimated by Kishinev as an interference in internal affairs of independent Moldova. Deputy Moldovan parliament speaker and leader of the ruling Agrarian-Democratic Party Dumitru Motspan told ITAR-TASS that the leadership of independent Moldova "does not intend to ask for permission to conclude beneficial treaties and will not tolerate interference in its internal affairs."
