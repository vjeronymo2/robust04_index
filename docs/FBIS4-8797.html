BFN [Text] Three persons have been killed and 10 injured in Serb attacks on Brcko. Over 3,000 shells have landed on the defense positions of the Brcko municipality, the political department of the 108th Croat Defense Council [HVO] Infantry Brigade has reported. The Serbs have been using howitzers, tanks, multiple rocket-launchers, and mortars, while a number of commando groups have attempted to break through. These attempts have been successfully foiled. An HVO advance party has reported that the Serb formations are again concentrating in several locations, while intelligence officers have said that a group of the so-called Bijeljina Panthers, a criminal formation that has taken part in some dozen massacres in northeast Bosnia, has arrived in Brcko.
