921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Keystone Investment net assets fall 11% Keystone Investment saw its net asset value fall 11 per cent, from 489.13p to 434.87p per share, over the 12 months to September 30. The investment trust reported net revenue marginally ahead at Pounds 2.16m (Pounds 2.1m) for earnings of 15.14p (14.74p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 10p brings the total for the year to 15p (14p).
