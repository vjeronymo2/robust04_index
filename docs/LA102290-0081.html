October 22, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NET LOSS? During Charles Keating's 33-day stay in Los Angeles County Jail, he lost 20% of his net worth. . . . Along with the shirt off his back and the rest of the clothes he came in with -- which the Sheriff's Department misplaced -- the former head of Irvine's Lincoln Savings & Loan had $25 in his wallet when he arrived -- but only $20 when he left. . . . No word from Keating on where the money went. Dan Weikel and Kris Lindgren
