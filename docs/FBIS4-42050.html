BFN [Text] Stepanakert, June 29. (SNARK)--The Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh is anxious with the appeals of the Azerbaijani side to enlarge the range of active intermediaries in the process of the Karabakh-Azerbaijani conflict regulation owing to reinforcement of the Turkey's role. The Karabakh Foreign Affairs Department told SNARK Turkey's attempts show the official Baku's intention to blockade the Russia's peace-keeping attempts to reach a deadlock in the peace-making process. The Karabakh side for numerous times declared the unacceptability of the Turkey's intermediary instigating role which is of the pro-Azerbaijani character cannot be considered neutral.
