BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held at the People's Palace of Culture Monday to mark the tenth anniversary of the publication of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's famous work "On Further Improving the Work of the Trade Unions" (May 3, 1984). In the work, Comrade Kim Chong-il said the trade unions are mass political organisations of the working class and dependable assistants and defenders of the party. And he gave scientific and perfect answers to all theoretical and practical questions in the building and activity of trade unions ranging from the character and mission of the trade unions to their fighting goal and tasks, principles and ways of activity. In his report at the meeting, Chu Song-il, chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, praised Comrade Kim Chong-il's work as a unique and revolutionary, great charter of the trade union movement in that it consummated the theory of building the trade unions and their activities in the present time and developed them to completion. "In the period following the publication of the work", he said, "The work for imbuing the trade unions with the great chuche idea has been invigorated under the wise guidance of the party with the result that the unions have successfully fulfilled their duties and tasks as organisations of ideological education and active assistants of the Workers' Party of Korea." "We are faced with the honorable task to defend socialism from all challenges and obstructive moves of the imperialists and reactionaries, exalt it and accomplish the cause of national reunification at an early date. The unionists must devotedly work to defend and exalt our socialism, that is, our life and soul," stressed the reporter.
