October 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT WILL DAVIS SAY TO HIS NEW COACH, 'JUST DO OK, BABY'?

Mike Shanahan wasn't responsible for the Raiders' poor record. He had one back, an erratic quarterback, a porous defensive line, linebackers who couldn't tackle and an ineffective secondary.

He needed some quality players.

DON RADEMACHER, Los Angeles
