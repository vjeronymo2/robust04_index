930215 FT 15 FEB 93 / Observer: Royal interest Notwithstanding last week's unprecedented disclosures about the state of Britain's royal finances, one small question continues to niggle. If the Queen is as relatively poor as her Lord Chamberlain insists, then either she inherited far less money than was thought or her traditional financial advisers have not been doing much of a job. Time, for a change of investment advisers, perhaps?
