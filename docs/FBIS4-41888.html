BFN [Unattributed report under the "Talks" rubric: "Discussion on the Two Countries' Security"] [Text] J. Enhsaihan, secretary of the Mongolian National Security Council, visited Moscow last week. He was received by Oleg Lobov, secretary of the Russian Federation Security Council. The conversation was constructive and friendly in character and was held in an atmosphere of complete mutual understanding. An accord was reached that in the near future the secretary of the Russian Federation Security Council will pay a return visit to Mongolia during which a memorandum on cooperation between the Russian Federation and Mongolia in the national security sphere will be signed. The sides listed the following priority areas for cooperation: regional and international security, the strengthening of Mongolia's nonnuclear status, economic security, and the fight against crime, international terrorism, and illegal trafficking in weapons and narcotics.
