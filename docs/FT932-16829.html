930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: Shorco falls 30% to Pounds 206,000 Shares of Shorco Group Holdings fell 12p to 83p yesterday on news of a 30 per cent decline in 1992 pre-tax profits to Pounds 206,000. Turnover of the USM company, which has interests in trench support systems and allied equipment, edged ahead to Pounds 6.86m (Pounds 6.76m). Profit margins fell from 4.3 per cent to 3 per cent. Basic earnings emerged at 4.2p (7.2p). A final dividend of 2.8p holds the total at 5.2p.
