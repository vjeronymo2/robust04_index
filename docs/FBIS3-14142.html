Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent in the south reports that a South Lebanese Army [SLA] unit raked the al-Hasbani-Zummarayya axis at 0730 today. In the one-hour operation, the SLA unit fired bursts from machine guns at 'Ayn Bu Siwar and the Mimis-Marj al-Zuhur Highway. The resistance posts in 'Ayn al-Tinah shelled the border area and Hasbayya with rockets all night last night and until dawn today. The Israeli forces retaliated by shelling Qilya, al-Dallafah, Abu Rashid, and Birkat Jabbur areas in Western al-Biqa'.
