940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Andaman Resources ANDAMAN RESOURCES shareholders have subscribed for 3.08m new ordinary shares at 10p each, representing 66 per cent of the open offer. The balance of 1.57m shares has been placed by brokers. FRENCH CONNECTION is acquiring the leasehold interests of 249 Regent Street, London, and the basement, ground and fourth floors of 251 Regent Street, for Pounds 100,000 from Touche Ross.
