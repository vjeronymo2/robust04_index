December 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SOCCER TEAM TAKES A LOSING ATTITUDE

So the U.S. soccer team is 1,500 to 1, huh?

And we couldn't win even if we had Diego Maradona, according to Times writer Randy Harvey?

And worst of all, the U.S. coach said, "We are dead ducks against Italy."?

Well, I say that people like the coach and reporter are the heart and soul reason that soccer is where it is in the United States today. That kind of negative thinking is about losing.

DAVE GONZALEZ, Bellflower
