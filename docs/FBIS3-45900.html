Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 23 (XINHUA) -- Wang Guangying, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with a delegation from the Belgium-China group of the Parliament of Belgium here this afternoon. The delegation, headed by Eric Vankeirsbilck, second vice-president of Chamber of Representatives of Belgium, arrived here this morning as guest of the NPC's China- Belgium Friendship Group. In addition to Beijing, they are scheduled to visit the cities of Xian and Guangzhou.
