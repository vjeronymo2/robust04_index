January 19, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

RETIRED POLICEMAN JOINS PLANNING PANEL

Retired Fullerton police officer Wade G. Richmond was appointed to an open Planning Commission seat Tuesday.

The City Council appointed Richmond, 57, after commission member Jack Snyder chose not to seek another term.

Richmond, a 31-year Fullerton resident, retired two years ago after 28 years as a policeman. He serves on the Fullerton Municipal Airport's noise and safety committee, an advisory board to the City Council.

His term on the commission expires in December, 1993.
