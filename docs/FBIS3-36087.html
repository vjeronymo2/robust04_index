Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS report from "Facts and Assessments" column] [Text] Ukraine has suggested to the Persian Gulf countries that they buy "a substantial part of its share of the former Soviet navy," the Saudi newspaper AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT reported Tuesday. Missile and torpedo boats, destroyers, and minelayers are scheduled for sale, the newspaper writes, citing "sources in Washington." Ukraine has suggested that Iran also buy a submarine from it.
