931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / International Company News: Qantas names head of finance Qantas, the government-controlled Australian airline in which British Airways holds a minority stake, yesterday announced that Mr Gary Toomey had been appointed chief financial officer with effect from December 1, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. He will also be appointed a director, filling one of two vacant positions on the board, where British Airways holds three seats. Mr Toomey's appointment follows the departure of Mr Graham Jones, former finance director, who resigned in September after only 11 months with Qantas.
