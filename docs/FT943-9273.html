940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / The main points of C&G's plan C&G's plan will mean that: Borrowers will get no cash payments. Investors - savers who are members of the society - who opened accounts after December 31 1992 will get no cash payments. Investors of more than two years' standing at the end of this year will get about 13 per cent of the balance of each investment account up to a maximum of Pounds 100,000, provided the account is still open on completion day. The sums will be on the basis of the lower balance on either completion day or April 20 this year. Those with more than Pounds 100 at the end of 1994 get an extra Pounds 500. Holders of deposit accounts who maintain an account open at the end of March until completion day will get about 13 per cent of the lower balance of each account on March 31 this year or the completion day. Those who had more than Pounds 100 at the end of March will get an additional Pounds 500. Holders of Pibs - permanent interest bearing shares - and C&G Channel Islands accounts will qualify for the flat payment of Pounds 500.
