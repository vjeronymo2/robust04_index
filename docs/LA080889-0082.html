August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BUSH SIGNS $70-BILLION DEBT CEILING INCREASE

President Bush signed on Monday a $70-billion increase in the federal debt limit, increasing the borrowing ceiling to $2.87 trillion from $2.80 trillion.

The new debt limit will expire on Oct. 31. The debt ceiling is increased once or twice a year as the government amasses annual budget deficits.
