940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / International Capital Markets: SHL Systemhouse in CDollars 109m share issue SHL Systemhouse, a large Ottawa-based international computer service outsourcer, is raising CDollars 109m (USDollars 80.7m) with an issue of 14.4m new shares at CDollars 7.85 a share. About 10 per cent of the issue is being taken up by Warburg Pincus Investors, the New York investment group. Proceeds are being used to repay debt and bolster working capital. SHL recently won several large capital-intensive outsourcing projects. Hydro-Quebec, one of Canada's largest power utilities, plans to begin a USDollars 1bn Euro medium-term note programme with maturities of one month or longer. The notes are expected to be listed on the London Stock Exchange. Notes denominated in French francs are also expected to be listed on the Paris bourse. Citybank is the issuing and principal paying agent. The programme has an A1 Moody's rating and an A-plus from Standard & Poor's.
