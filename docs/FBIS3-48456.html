Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Monday sent a message of greetings to the 28th Congress of the French Communist Party. The message says over the past 70 years the French Communist Party has been engaged in a vigorous struggle against war and fascism and for defence of political right of broad segments of the French working masses and their freedom, democracy, the sovereignty of the nation, and the socialist cause and expresses full support and firm solidarity for the struggle of the French Communist Party for peace and socialism. The message expresses the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties will grow stronger and develop in the future and wishes the French Communist Party new success in the congress.
