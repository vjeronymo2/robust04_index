930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Sales rise for unit trust Peps - At a glance Sales of unit trust personal equity plans achieved their strongest quarter ever. Net investment in the first quarter of the year at Pounds 846m was almost four times the first quarterly inflow of any year. It easily beat the previous record quarter of Pounds 336.4m, in the second quarter of 1992. The value of unit trust Pep funds stood at Pounds 4.4bn at the end of the quarter compared to Pounds 3.3bn at the end of last year. Page IV
