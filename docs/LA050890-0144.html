May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DRUG BABIES: Several county health organizations, including the UCI Medical Center, are planning a week of confidential testing of all new mothers here in order to track the growing number of births to drug-abusers (B10). . . ."We need to know, but we have no reliable figures," said Dottie Andrews, March of Dimes executive director. A routine blood test taken by pregnant women can also be used to monitor for drugs. Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
