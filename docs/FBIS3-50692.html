Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Global TV News" newscast] [Text] Today General Guido Guevara Guerra became the chairman of the Supreme Council of Military Justice. During a ceremony at council headquarters on Arequipa Avenue, General Guevara took his oath as head of military justice. Addressing the president of the Supreme Court of Justice and other guest authorities, Guevara said the reason for the council's existence was to safeguard Armed Forces and police discipline. Guevara Guerra replaces Admiral Roberto Duvoc.
