940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / Nadir associate questioned by SFO Elizabeth Forsyth, an associate of Asil Nadir, the fugitive former head of Polly Peck International, arrived at the Serious Fraud Office yesterday to answer questions. She worked for South Audley Management, a company with close ties to Mr Nadir, and went to northern Cyprus before Mr Nadir fled there after breaking his bail conditions in May last year.
