931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Depressed sales shake Honeywell HONEYWELL, the US controls group, yesterday reported net income of Dollars 80.9m, or 60 cents a share, for the third quarter, compared with Dollars 81m, or 59 cents, last year, excluding special charges in both periods, writes Martin Dickson. Sales totalled Dollars 1.45bn, down from Dollars 1.55bn. Operating profits, excluding special charges, came through at Dollars 151.7m, down from Dollars 164.2m, and were adversely affected by European currency fluctuations and a strong dollar. Mr Michael Bonsignore, chairman, noted that 25 per cent of the company's business came from Europe, which was in recession.
