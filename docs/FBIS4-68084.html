BFN [Text] Ankara--Twenty-one suspected supporters of the Palestinian Hamas organization have been arrested, an announcement from the anti-terrorism branch of the Istanbul Security Directorate said on Wednesday. The statement said police acting on a tip also seized 14 Kalashikov rifles and 14 pistols of various trademarks and caliber. It said that the people placed under arrest were trying to establish a radio station and that they had participated in the recent Bosnia-Herzegovina rally in Istanbul's Taksim Square. According to the announcement, the suspects participated in demonstrations following noon prayers at some mosques and carried out political activities by setting up educational, financial and logistic units at universities. The suspects also published a magazine called the MUSLIM YOUTH. It said they had received armed training in Istanbul, Canakkale and its environs. It noted that they set up regions nationwide and carried out activities particularly in Istanbul, Izmit, Konya, Adana, Mersin, Kahramanmaras, Isparta, Erzurum, Trabzon, Van, Eskisehir, Kutahya and Bilecik.
