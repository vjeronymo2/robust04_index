Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] During a news conference held today, Mircea Geoana, the spokesman of Romania's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, saluted the proposal for a Partnership for Peace that has been accepted by Romania's president as a precursory step of our country's joining NATO as a full member. The declaration presented by Geoana says that the Foreign Affairs Ministry will forward proposals to the government recommending that necessary official steps should be urgently taken in view of launching the Partnership for Peace and to sign the proposed document.
