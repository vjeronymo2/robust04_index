BFN [From the "Brothers" page] [Text] Informed sources in the Sudanese capital have affirmed that during the first week of March the Sudanese Interior Ministry granted Sudanese nationality and Sudanese passports to more than 120 Arab refugees from Egypt, Algeria, Tunis, Jordan, Libya and Palestine, in implementation of the recommendations of the Arab Islamic Popular Conference, which is chaired by National Islamic Front leader Dr. Hasan al-Turabi. The Islamic Movements's department at the Revolution's Security Agency will keep files on these terrorist elements and will follow up on the settlement, accommodation, and guarding of these elements, who are in camps throughout Sudan.
