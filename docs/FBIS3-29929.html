Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The delegation of the Canadian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) headed by its national leader Hardial Bains left here for home on February 22 after successfully concluding its 11-day visit to Korea. It was seen off at the airport by Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. During its stay in Korea, the delegation visited Mangyongdae, the party founding museum, the West Sea Barrage and other places in Pyongyang and local areas.
