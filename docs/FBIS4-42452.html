BFN [Text] President Konstandinos Karamanlis today said that what happens in relations among states can not be explained logically, as these relations are ruled by absurdity and anarchy. The president said an example of the above is the Greek-Albanian relations which are ruled by absurdity. Furthermore, the president refused to comment on the elections for the European Parliament, citing his office, but he indirectly advised fewer statements. He said that he prefers to remain quiet when everybody else is talking.
