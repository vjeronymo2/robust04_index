941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg overlooked softer world markets and a weak gold price to post steady gains as local interest propped up prices in mostly lacklustre trade. The overall index improved 30 to 5,738, industrials were 27 better at 6,605 and the golds index rose 33 to 2,241. Anglos put on 75 cents at R237.75 in R25m turnover.
