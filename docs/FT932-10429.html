930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Smaller companies creep ahead - At a Glance Smaller company shares edged higher, despite the bearish tone of much of the stock market. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 0.2 per cent from 1405.03 to 1407.68 over the period April 28 to May 6.
