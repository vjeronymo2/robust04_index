BFN [Text] Latvian Defense Minister Valdis Pavlovskis and Ilgonis Upmalis, head of the Latvian bureau for monitoring the withdrawal of the Russian Army, today met Leonid Mayorov, supreme commander of the Northwestern Group of Russian forces, to discuss problems related to the withdrawal. Mayorov told the Latvian officials that the withdrawal of troops and Army property was proceeding according to schedule, coordinating it with the progress of housing construction in Russia.
