920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / International Company News: Public lifts pre-tax profit 28% to MDollars 164m PUBLIC, Malaysia's fourth largest domestic bank, has reported a 28 per cent rise in pre-tax profit to MDollars 164m (USDollars 63m) last year from MDollars 128m in 1990. The group, which has interests in stockbroking and finance, has been active abroad for the past two years, with acquisitions and investments in a finance company in Hong Kong, 40 per cent of Bancorp in New Zealand and ventures in Sri Lanka and Vietnam. It marked its 25th anniversary with a 36 per cent improvement in turnover to MDollars 1.03bn from MDollars 753m. Attributable profit was 33 per cent higher at MDollars 100m, giving a 12 per cent return on equity capital and earnings per share of 9.1 cents, compared with 7.4 cents. Public raised its dividend payout 7 per cent to MDollars 30.6m, or 4.25 cents a share. Group assets were 39 per cent higher at MDollars 13.8bn, while deposits rose by 40 per cent to MDollars 12.1bn and by 28 per cent in loans to MDollars 6.2bn. Shareholders' equity stands at MDollars 838m.
