Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The La Paz district attorney's office has been damaged by a terrorist attack. An explosion shook the La Paz district attorney's office at noon today. The explosion occurred in a restricted area very close to Prosecutor Javier Dibs's office. The damage was small and no one was injured. [passage omitted] The bomb was placed in a corridor through which Dibs enters and leaves his office. [passage omitted] Two weeks ago the Ministry of Government withdrew the police who were guarding the office. It is expected that the new order assigning officers to different posts will resolve the problem.
