BFN [Text] Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan held a consultative meeting today with the leadership of Nagorno-Karabakh. Chairman of the State Defense Committee Robert Kocharyan, Commander of the Defense Army Major-General Samvel Babayan, and Foreign Minister Arkadiy Gukasyan represented Nagorno-Karabakh Republic during the meeting. The meeting was devoted to assessing the current phase of the settlement of the Azerbaijani-Karabakh conflict, and to discussing future (?options). The presidential press service reported that State Minister Vazgen Sargisyan, Defense Minister Serzh Sargisyan, Interior Minister Vano Siradegyan, Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan, Acting Head of the State Directorate for National Security and Ambassador for Special Missions Davit Shakhnazaryan, and First Deputy Foreign Minister Zhirair Liparityan also took part in the meeting.
