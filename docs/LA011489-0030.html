January 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HIS RECORD IS GREAT, EXCEPT FOR JANUARY

In rebuttal to Mr. Cowdery's letter (Jan. 7), saying the championship of L.A. should not be decided until after the bowl games:

The last 2 years, USC has defeated UCLA with the Rose Bowl on the line for both teams. Since UCLA lost those games they had to settle for a lesser bowl, and thus, a lesser opponent.

Let UCLA brag about beating up on the BYUs and Floridas in other bowl games. For Pac-10 teams, the Rose Bowl is the big show, and you can't win it unless you get there.

JOHN RIS

Mission Viejo
