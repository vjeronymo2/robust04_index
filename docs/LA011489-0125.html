January 14, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST : LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

SAN JUAN CAPISTRANO;

GOP BALL TO CELEBRATE BUSH'S INAUGURATION

An "inaugural ball west" is planned for Friday by the San Juan Capistrano Republican Women's Club, Federated.

The event, which will be keyed to President-elect George Bush's inaugural activities in Washington, will be at 6:30 p.m. at Michael's Supper Club, 24599 Dana Drive, Dana Point. Tickets are $30. There will be a live band.

Tickets may be obtained by calling (714) 496-4595.
