December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

GUNMAN FLEES WITH RESTAURANT'S RECEIPTS

A gunman held up three restaurant employees early Monday morning and stole several thousands dollars before fleeing, police said.

The employees, a busboy, manager and bartender, were unharmed, police said.

The robbery occurred shortly after midnight at El Torito Grill, 951 Newport Center Drive. The gunman entered the restaurant as the employees were preparing to close for the evening, Police Sgt. Andy Gonis said.

No patrons were in the restaurant at the time, he said.

The gunman ordered the employees to the back of the restaurant and made them tie each other up, Gonis said.

The robber then fled with the day's receipts, estimated at several thousand dollars, Gonis said.
