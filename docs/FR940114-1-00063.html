FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS COMMISSION 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 93&hyph;318; RM&hyph;8364] Radio Broadcasting Services; Clinton, KY AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission requests comments on a petition filed by Thunderbolt Broadcasting Company, Inc., proposing the allotment of Channel 271C3 to Clinton, Kentucky, as that community's first local aural transmission service. Channel 271C3 can be allotted to Clinton in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements with a site restriction of 11.2 kilometers (7.0 miles) northeast to avoid short-spacings to Station WCMT&hyph;FM, Channel 269A, Martin, Tennessee, Station KJBR, Channel 270C, Jonesboro, Arkansas, and Station KDEX&hyph;FM, Channel 272A, Dexter, Missouri. The coordinates for Channel 271C3 at Clinton are North Latitude 36&hyph;44&hyph;30 and West Longitude 88&hyph;54&hyph;30. DATES: Comments must be filed on or before February 22, 1994, and reply comments on or before March 9, 1994.
