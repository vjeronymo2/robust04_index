921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Nth Brit Canadian assets decline Net asset value of North British Canadian Investment Trust declined to 95.9p per share at end August compared with 103.4p a year ago and 112.3p at the February 1992 year end. Total revenue rose from Pounds 749,000 to Pounds 794,000. Profits after tax rose from Pounds 462,000 to Pounds 505,000 leaving earnings per share at 1.87p against 1.71p per share. The interim dividend is raised from 0.9p to 0.94p.
