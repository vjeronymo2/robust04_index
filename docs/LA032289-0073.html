March 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

LOS ANGELES CAMPAIGN '89;

MAYORAL RACE

* Elizabeth Munoz, a bilingual school teacher, will hold a press conference at 10 a.m. today on the south steps of City Hall. Munoz and grass-roots leaders of the black, Latino and gay communities will discuss the AIDS crisis in South Central and East Los Angeles.

CITY COUNCIL RACES

* Councilman Michael Woo of the 13th District has received the endorsement of the United Firefighters of Los Angeles.

* Based on information supplied by Councilman Zev Yaroslavsky's campaign, The Times erroneously reported that the Coalition For Clean Air had endorsed Yaroslavsky. The endorsement is from coalition Vice President Mark Abramowitz and not from the coalition itself.
