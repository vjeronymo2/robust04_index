BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Transportation Ministry has stated that the Transportation Ministry wishes to draw the attention of all airlines and maritime shipping companies to the fact that the airspace over and the waters around the airports and seaports in the following areas: Aden, Hadhramaut, and al-Mahrah, are military zones. Consequently, flying, sailing, landing at airports, or docking at seaports in these areas may expose their aircraft and vessels to danger. The Government of the Republic of Yemen will not be responsible in any way for any damage or losses any company may incur as a result of violating these regulations. Issued by the Transportation Ministry.
