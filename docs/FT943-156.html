940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Speciality Shops Speciality Shops, the shopping centre management company which came to the market in May, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 602,000 for the six months to June 30, against losses of Pounds 71,000. The result was after Pounds 916,000 of provisions against properties which have been written back. Turnover was Pounds 2.04m (Pounds 4.34m) reflecting lower property sales of Pounds 100,000 (Pounds 2.4m). Earnings per share were 5.58p (0.11p losses).
