Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A cessation of activities is imminent at Radio Lumiere. The very high cost of oil products has prompted the officials of Radio Lumiere to consider ceasing all activities within two weeks. On 22 February, officials of the Protestant radio station commemorated its 35th anniversary by calling on evangelical Christians to make financial contributions so the radio station can continue its broadcasts. Raymond Exume, general manager of the satellite stations of Radio Lumiere, says he considers a shutdown of Radio Lumiere a failure that will have consequences for the entire Haitian evangelical community.
