September 19, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Madonna's Money: Apparently, the Material Girl isn't all that concerned with money.

Madonna was listed in a recent New York newspaper ad trying to notify people who have $50 or more in accounts that have been dormant for five years at Chemical Bank. The notice listed her as Madonna Ciccone of 1775 Broadway and she has until Oct. 20 to claim the money. After that, the bank turns the money over to the state. Earlier this week, Forbes magazine included Madonna on its list of the top 40 wealthiest entertainers, estimating her fortune at $62 million. BETH KLEID
