921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / International Company News: Svedala Industri acquires US pump group SVEDALA Industri, the Swedish mining equipment group, has acquired Denver Technologies of the US in a deal which will make the Swedish company a world leader in the supply of pumps and process equipment to the mining industry. The announcement did not supply financial detail about the deal. The US company which is strong in the North American market has annual sales of Dollars 75m and a workforce of 700 people. By contrast Svedala has annual sales totalling more than SKr8bn (Dollars 1.3bn) and employs 10,000 people with 80 per cent of its operations conducted outside Sweden. 'The companies complement each other both in terms of geographic markets and product range,' said Mr Kjell Akesson, business manager of Svedala's business area for pumps and process equipment. 'This acquisition is part of the company's ongoing strategy to strengthen our position as a leading supplier to the mineral processing industry. Finanzauto, the Spanish earthmoving equipment supplier which is part of the South African Barlow Rand group, has increased its stake in its Portuguese subsidiary Sociedade Tecnica de Equipamentos e Tractores (Stet) to 96 per cent from 59 per cent, Reuters reports from Madrid. Finanzauto, which is the sole Caterpillar dealer in Spain, spent Pta620m (Dollars 5.45m) lifting its stake.
