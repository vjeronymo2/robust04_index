921214 FT 14 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Supplying mechanical services HOW ENGINEERING SERVICES has been awarded three mechanical, electrical and plumbing services contracts totalling some Pounds 16m. A Pounds 5.7m order for Glaxo research campus at Stevenage, Hertfordshire, has been won for the microbiology laboratory and biology node. The second contract is for the BAT project test Phase I at Southampton which is worth Pounds 6.9m. The third contract, worth Pounds 3.2m, is at Heathrow Airport for services to the base building for the new combined operations centre.
