941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: S African golds strengthen The market extended morning gains, with gold stocks particularly strong. The overall index improved 29.5 to 5,696.9, industrials 27.4 to 6,857.5 and golds 30.5 to 1,863.7. Among golds, Vaal Reefs went against the trend to close R2 off at R341, Southvaal gained R4 at R112 and Dries was up R1.50 at R56.50. Elsewhere, Anglos fell R1 to R226, De Beers put on 75 cents at R93.25 and SAB climbed R3 to R96.50. Remgro was up 50 cents at R28.25 and Anglovaal R2 stronger at R137.
