Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 10 SAPA -- Freedom Alliance leaders have decided to terminate all negotiations and submission of candidates for the election, Afrikaner Volksfront [Afrikaner National Front] leader Constand Viljoen said on Thursday [10 March]. This stance would be maintained until the African National Congress stopped its action in Bophuthatswana, "so as to allow President Lucas Mangope to make the decisions to give up or maintain the sovereignity of the territory without being intimidated", Gen Viljoen said in a statement in Pretoria Bophuthatswana is a Freedom Alliance member.
