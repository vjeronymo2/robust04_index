April 4, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

PICUS, 5 CHALLENGERS TO SPEAK AT FORUM

Los Angeles City Councilwoman Joy Picus will meet the five challengers for her 3rd District seat, representing the west San Fernando Valley, for the first -- and probably only -- time before the April 11 election at a candidate forum Wednesday night.

The forum will be held at 7:30 p.m. at El Camino Real High School, 5440 Valley Circle Blvd., Woodland Hills.

It is sponsored by the League of Women Voters, the West Hills Community Organization, the Woodland Hills Homeowners Organization and the Valley Industry and Commerce Assn.
