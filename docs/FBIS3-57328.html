Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Ivan Ivanov, Yuriy Likhopek, and Serafim Bykhun] [Text] Orel January 25 TASS -- A four-hour conference of leaders of federal bodies and heads of 17 Russian regions ended in Orel on Tuesday. The conference decided that "the Federal Programme of Development of Central Regions of Russia until the Year 2000" be submitted for endorsement by the president and government of the Russian Federation early in March this year. This programme needs 40.3 trillion roubles to implement. After the conference Russian Prime-Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, told ITAR-TASS the government would make maximum efforts to revive regions in Central Russia so that they "could feed the whole country". "Before we only pumped resources from Central Russia giving nothing in return. This practice should be changed radically", the prime-minister said.
