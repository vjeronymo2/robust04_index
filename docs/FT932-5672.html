930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / Talks on E Europe economic reform Members of parliament from 39 countries and financial experts are to attend a three-day conference in Helsinki from today on economic reform in eastern Europe, Reuter reports from Helsinki. The conference is being organised by the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe. It will 'analyse the specific problems facing the economies of those countries and will propose policies designed to facilitate the reform process and strengthen economic co-operation,' according to the organisers.
