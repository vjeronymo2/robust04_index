941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Intel on course for 3Q earnings rise - Other companies Intel: The world's largest computer chip maker will report its third-quarter results after the close of trading in New York today. Analysts are forecasting earnings of about Dollars 1.51 per share, compared with Dollars 1.33 in the same period last year. Intel has just begun an Dollars 80m advertising campaign to boost sales of PCs containing Pentium, its highest performance microprocessor, during the busy Christmas selling season. The company continues to battle imitators in the microprocessor market. Last week, Intel sought a temporary restraining order to force Advanced Micro Devices to halt sales of microprocessor clones.
