941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / Economic Watch: Retail sales rise in Switzerland Swiss retail sales turned up in August, recording a 2.3 per cent value growth year-on-year after a 2 per cent decline in July. Private consumption up to now has been the weakest component in the recovery that has been under way in the Swiss economy since the fourth quarter of last year, in spite of indications of improving consumer sentiment. Sales of food and luxury items rose 3.5 per cent in August while clothing and textile sales declined by 3.4 per cent, the federal statistics office said. On a volume basis, August sales were up by 2 per cent.
