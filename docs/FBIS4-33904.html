BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Text] Politburo member Jose Ramon Machado Ventura has participated in the District 27 progress report assembly in Guantanamo City. Machado witnessed an extensive debate with considerable participation by the people, who calmly suggested many ideas aimed at resolving shortages through local alternatives. Machado, who is Guantanamo Municipality's deputy to the National Assembly of the People's Government, was briefed on the efforts the population is making to recover from the recent rains, efforts not only in each neighborhood but also on the farms of the Segundo Frente Frank Pais contingent, which Machado also visited and praised: [Begin Machado recording] This is heartwarming. Here, you can see the disposition and drive with which the people renew their work. [end recording] Machado's tour of Guantanamo included an evaluation of a great number of Basic Cooperative Production Units, where the need to stabilize the work force was emphasized. The suggested solutions included improving living conditions, guaranteeing cost reductions, and increasing production.
