Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Nigeria and Cuba, today in Lagos, signed the protocol for the implementation of the cultural and educational agreement between the two countries. Kobalakin Pong reports that the accord was signed on behalf of Nigeria by the minister of information and culture, Professor Jerry Gana, while the Cuban ambassador to Nigeria, Mr. Giraldo Mazola, initialed the document for his country. [Begin Pong recording] The agreement on cultural and educational cooperation between Nigeria and Cuba was signed in 1981. The accord, according to Prof. Gana, had brought more awareness of the benefits to be derived from closer economic, cultural, and educational cooperation between the two countries.
