941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Business Travel (Update): Typhoon hits Taiwan Typhoon Seth, with winds of 107mph, struck Taiwan yesterday, leaving one person dead. Four domestic airports in eastern Taiwan were closed but international airports stayed open. A highway in eastern Taiwan was closed following landslides. Officials were considering whether to cancel today's National Day celebrations. Seth is the sixth typhoon to hit Taiwan since early July. Storms have killed 30 people and caused extensive damage.
