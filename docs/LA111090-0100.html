November 10, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION VOLLEYBALL PLAYOFFS;

JORDAN RALLIES TO OVERCOME BURROUGHS

Ramona Jessop had 18 kills and Jennifer Salanoa added 12 to lead Long Beach Jordan High past Burroughs in the second round of the Southern Section 4-A Division volleyball playoffs at Jordan, 9-15, 15-9, 15-8, 15-5.

Jordan (15-1) could not contain senior middle blocker Jennifer Wagner, however, who finished with 18 kills.

Eight of those kills came in the first game, which Burroughs dominated.

But Wagner was not enough to carry Burroughs (20-2).

Jordan trailed, 6-1, in the second game but rallied behind the serving of DeAnn Machlan, who went to the service line for eight consecutive points.

Setter Naomi Shimada had 48 assists.

In other second-round 4-A matches:

Long Beach Wilson d. Notre Dame, 11-15, 15-2, 15-12, 16-14 -- Lauren Agajanian had nine kills to lead host Wilson.

Glendale d. Beverly Hills, 15-2, 15-8, 15-6 -- The Dynamiters advanced to the next round with a sweep at Glendale.

DIVISION 1-A

Village Christian d. Whittier Christian, 17-15, 13-15, 15-13, 16-14 -- Visiting Village Christian needed nearly two hours to pull out the match.

Jennifer Kossuth led fifth-seeded Village Christian with 10 kills, seven aces and five blocks. Christina Gumpert added seven kills and five blocks.

L.A. Baptist d. South Hills, 15-12, 15-5, 15-1 -- Lucretia Haynes led a balanced L. A. Baptist attack with eight kills.

Monica Davis and Sarah Rameson each had seven kills for host L. A. Baptist (17-1).
