April 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV/VIDEO

Sullivan on 'Murphy Brown': Former "CBS This Morning" co-anchor Kathleen Sullivan is in Los Angeles taping the season finale of CBS' "Murphy Brown."

Sullivan plays herself in the episode, which has Frank (Joe Regalbuto) asking Murphy (Candice Bergen) to get him a date for Corky's (Faith Ford's) wedding. The episode will air during the May ratings sweeps. Sullivan's contract with CBS doesn't end until November and, with the sudden firing of Brent Musburger last weekend, there are rumors that she just might find another role at the network with CBS Sports before spring is out.
