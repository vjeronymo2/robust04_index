Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Macedonian President Kiro Gligorov has sent a letter to European Union [EU] President Jacques Delors warning that during recent weeks Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] has been left to face the Greek economic embargo and the hermetically closed borders with Greece alone. Gligorov stresses that this could result in a dramatic economic destabilization of Macedonia, and consequently of the overall situation in the region. The Macedonian president goes on to say that the EU must find a solution to this problem, and notes the EU's slowness, lack of determination and unequal treatment of the two sides. At the end of his letter to Jacques Delors, Gligorov stresses that if the problem with Athens is not solved, Macedonia will have to initiate a discussion of the Greek embargo in the Security Council.
