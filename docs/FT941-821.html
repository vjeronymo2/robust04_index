940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): Metallgesellschaft - Other Companies Plenty of noise can also be expected at today's annual meeting of the metals and engineering group recently dragged from the brink by a banking consortium. While the near-catastrophic events of the recent past will exercise some, most shareholders - seated in the arguably appropriate surroundings of Frankfurt Zoo - are likely to want to hear more on restructuring plans.
