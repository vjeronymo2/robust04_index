931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: SCT disposes of 0.69m Oak shares Second Consolidated Trust is selling 40.4 per cent of its 10 per cent holding in Oak Industries, a Boston-based electronics group. The 693,306 shares, of which 266,827 were acquired through the exercise of warrants, are being sold at Dollars 14.50 (970p) each and net proceeds will be about Dollars 9.3m before allowing for the cost of exercising the warrants. Following the sale SCT will hold 1.02m Oak shares. Also, SCT will receive Dollars 6.5m from the repayment of a loan to Invesco, the fund management company, secured on Invesco's shares in Oak. Invesco announced on Wednesday it was selling its 4.6 per cent stake in Oak.
