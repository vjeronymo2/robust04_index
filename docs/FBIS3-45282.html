Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] After successfully fulfilling all items on the agenda, the fifth standing committee meeting of the eighth provincial people's congress ended in Jinan on the morning of 17 January. The meeting decided to hold the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress in Jinan on 28 February. Ma Zhongcai, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, presided over the plenary meeting held in the morning. The meeting adopted by vote the decision of the provincial people's congress standing committee on holding the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress, the appointments and removals of personnel and the by-election of provincial deputies to the eighth National People's Congress. It decided to appoint (Sun Guangyuan) chairman of the Shandong Provincial Economic Commission and removed Han Yuqun from his post as chairman of the Shandong Provincial Economic Commission and Li Shuxiang from his post as chairman of the Shandong Provincial Nationalities Affairs Committee. The meeting also adopted by vote the report of the provincial Financial and Economic Commission on the result of handling the No. 7 motion submitted for discussion by the presidium of the first session of the eighth provincial people's congress; the report of the provincial rural economic committee of the provincial people's congress on the result of handling the No. 21 motion submitted for discussion by the presidium of the first session of the eighth provincial people's congress; decided by vote the implementation method of Shandong Province of the PRC's law on protecting the rights and interests of women, the regulation of Shandong Province on managing the economic and technological development zone and the regulation of Shandong Province on managing Qingdao bonded zone, the regulation of Shandong Province on preventing and improving water pollution in the drainage areas of the southern four lakes; listened to an explanation given by Feng Lizu, vice chairman of the provincial Urban and Rural Construction and Environmental Protection Committee of the provincial people's congress, on the revised draft regulation of Shandong Province on managing the invitation and submission of tenders for construction projects and adopted this regulation; adopted by vote the decision of the Shandong Provincial people's congress standing committee on approving Jinan city's method on managing private houses in the urban areas, the decision of the Shandong Provincial people's congress standing committee on approving Qingdao city's method on managing the family planning of transients and the decision of the Shandong Provincial people's congress standing committee on approving Qingdao city's stipulation on labor management of foreign-funded enterprises and the decision of the Shandong Provincial people's congress standing committee on approving Zibo city's regulation on scientific and technological progress. Through discussions, the meeting maintained that the Shandong provincial draft regulation on agricultural environmental protection, the Shandong provincial draft regulation on environmental protection of petroleum prospecting and development, and the Shandong provincial draft regulation on land management in the development zone, which were submitted for examination and discussion, will be further revised and will be submitted to the standing committee for continued discussion. Li Zhen, chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Wang Shufang, Miao Fenglin, Guo Songnian, Xu Jianchun, Ma Shizhong, Guo Changcai, Yan Qingqing and Xu Xuemeng, vice chairmen of the provincial people's congress standing committee; and Zhao Linshan, secretary general of the provincial people's congress, attended the meeting. Vice Governor Zhang Ruifeng; Wang Zunong, vice chairman of provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and responsible comrades of the provincial higher people's court and the provincial people's procuratorate attended the meeting as observers.
