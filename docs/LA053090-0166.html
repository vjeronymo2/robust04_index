May 30, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

U.S. BEATS LIECHTENSTEIN, 4-1

The U.S. national soccer team beat Liechtenstein, 4-1, today in a World Cup warm-up game before a crowd of 2,400.

The teams battled to a 1-1 tie in the first half.

Peter Vermes opened the scoring for the United States with a goal in the 10th minute. Also scoring for the U.S. team were Marcelo Balboa, Eric Wynalda and Chris Henderson.

Hans Marxer scored for Liechtenstein.
