Language: German Article Type:BFN ["voel"-attributed report: "Letter Bombs: Detained Suspect Allegedly Contact Man"] [Text] Vienna--On Wednesday [16 February] the Interior Ministry denied the allegation leveled by Joerg Haider, chairman of the Freedom Party of Austria, that the prisoner Peter Binder, who is suspected of having been involved in the letter bomb attacks and who is waiting trial, was a contact man of the state police. However, the Ministry had to admit that the state police had contacts with Binder before the attacks. The 26-year-old man provided information on the desecration of the Jewish cemetery in Eisenstadt that led to the arrest of a suspect, who has again been released.
