920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Observer: Down and out However the mighty may have fallen, few can have taken such a dive as deposed Australian premier Bob Hawke. In his heyday, a pub was named after him in his constituency, the working- class Wills district of Melbourne. Now the R J Hawke Hotel has not only had its name changed to the Paris Tavern, but has been turned into a pastel-shaded, neon-lit gay bar.
