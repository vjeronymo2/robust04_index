941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / Ariane launches resume soon Arianespace, makers of western Europe's Ariane rocket, said yesterday that flights would resume in February 1995 after the December 1 failure of an Ariane rocket which crashed into the Atlantic Ocean. Mr Charles Bigot, Arianespace chairman, told a news conference: 'We hope to limit the delay in our programmes to two months . . . to resume launches before the end of February.'
