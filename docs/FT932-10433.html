930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Investment trusts tempt savers - At a Glance Money flowed into investment trust savings schemes in the first quarter of 1993, with Pounds 48.14m invested, the highest total for a single quarter. The inflow came after investment into such schemes dipped slightly last year. Lump sum investments were mainly responsible, totalling Pounds 35.13m compared with Pounds 21.72m in the fourth quarter of 1992. The number of regular savings accounts also increased, from 55,972 to 57,641, with the average investor contributing Pounds 71 per month.
