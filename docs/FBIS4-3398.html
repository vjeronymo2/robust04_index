BFN [Text] Vice Governor Jiang Xinzhen met and feted Kurt Waldheim, former UN general secretary, former Austrian president, and president of the UN Association of Austria and his party in Xian yesterday evening. Jiang briefed the Austrian guests on Shaanxi Province's history and the achievements it has made since the drive of reform and opening up to the outside world was launched and the latter expressed their thanks for the warm hospitality accorded to them by the province.
