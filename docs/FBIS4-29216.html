BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN in Japanese 15 Jun 94 Morning Edition] [Text] A top official of the Ministry of International Trade and Industry disclosed on 14 June that working-level negotiations on plate glass, which the United States included in the area of priority negotiations at the Japan-U.S. comprehensive economic talks, will be resumed in Tokyo on 16 June. Negotiations on plate glass have been suspended since last December.
