930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Record price for Hilliard miniature A PORTRAIT miniature painted in 1588 by Nicholas Hilliard, A Man Clasping a Hand From a Cloud, sold for Pounds 177,500 to an anonymous buyer at Christie's yesterday. The price was a record for Hilliard, the most accomplished miniaturist of the Elizabethan and Jacobean era. The pre-sale estimate was around Pounds 50,000.
