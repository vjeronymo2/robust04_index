Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow March 15 TASS -- Russian and Polish prime ministers met in the Russian Government house today to consider closer interaction in the economic field. They are working towards the signing of an intergovernmental agreement on the principles of Russian citizens' labour activity on Polish territory and Polish citizens on Russian territory, as well as an inter-governmental agreement on cooperation in the anti-monopoly activity. The two premiers first met for a one-to-one talk and are due to continue negotiations in a broader format within the frame of the Polish Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak's official visit to the Russian capital.
