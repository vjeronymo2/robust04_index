Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Jan (MENA)--A committee on disarmament stemming from the multilateral talks for peace in the Middle East opened meetings on the expert level in Cairo this afternoon. Ambassador Nabil Fahmi, political adviser to the foreign minister, is leading Egypt's team to the talks. The committee will discuss various topics concerning the limitation of nuclear, chemical, and biological weapons in the Middle East. The committee, which will continue meetings for six days, will also discuss the possibility of establishing a demilitarized zone in the Middle East as well as look into aspects of regional cooperation.
