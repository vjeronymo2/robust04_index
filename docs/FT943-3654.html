940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / Observer: Harbouring doubts Next month's Conservative conference contains a poignant side amid all the more traditional fare of Tory conference agendas - hanging, flogging, tax cuts, withdrawal from Europe and so on. There will be a heartfelt plea from the local party in Market Harborough in Leicestershire, which will urge 'Her Majesty's government to be more mindful of single people who feel discriminated against with regard to single supplements for hotel accommodation'.
