931106 FT 06 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Anglo St James' Pounds 6.33m disposal Anglo St James, the commercial property developer, has exchanged contracts for the sale of its freehold interests at 63/65/65A Oxford Street and 14 Soho Street, London, to the Scottish Life Assurance Company for Pounds 6.33m cash before costs. The property was part of the group's dealing property portfolio and the net profit arising on the sale will be accounted for as a revenue item through the profit and loss account.
