May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NUDE NEWS: Orange County's latest artistic encounter with the undraped human form came off without a hitch over the weekend, when the Momix dance troupe performed partially nude at Saddleback College. . . . As is customary when the college knows about nudity, notices were included in the series brochure and mailed with tickets. . . . The performances were well received and there were no complaints or ticket returns. Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
