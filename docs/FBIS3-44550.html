Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yichang, January 4 (XINHUA) -- China plans to increase the investment in the preparatory work of the Three Gorges project in 1994, an official said here today. He Gong, deputy general manager of the Three Gorges Project Development Corporation, told XINHUA that 1994 will be the first year to see large-scale digging and other preparatory construction work for the project. The manager did not release the exact amount to be increased. Currently, he said, all the preparatory work, including earth and rock work on the southern bank of the river, a specially-built highway and a highway bridge across the Chang Jiang river has started. Also, included are construction of on-site transportation work, power supply, heating, telecommunications and accommodation for the workers. He said that his corporation will also invite public bidding nationwide for this year's preparatory work and will select high efficient construction companies for the work.
