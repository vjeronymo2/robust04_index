940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Appetites whetted by Sandoz acquisition - Other Companies Sandoz, the Swiss pharmaceutical and chemicals group, has already revealed that its first half sales were up only 2 per cent to SFr8.22bn (Dollars 6.2bn). A more complete interim statement is expected late this week from the group. Following the completion last week of its SFr3.7bn acquisition of the US baby food maker Gerber Products, some indication of its impact on full year results would be welcomed.
