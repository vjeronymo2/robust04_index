920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / UK Company News: TR Pacific Trust asset value rises Net asset value per share of the TR Pacific Investment Trust rose from 92.65p to 95.3p over the 12 months to June 30. Available revenue for the half year to June 30, however, fell from Pounds 444,000 to Pounds 328,000, equal to earnings per share of 0.49p (0.663p).
