Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] After the conclusion of the military parade marking the anniversary of Greek Independence Day, Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou was asked to comment on the dispatch of Turkish troops to Bosnia-Herzegovina. He said: I am amazed that an army that invaded and occupied the northern part of Cyprus may be used for peacekeeping forces. It is amazing. Asked about today's parade, Papandreou said: It was a perfect parade. Our Armed Forces are a strong force for safeguarding our country's integrity and defending peace.
