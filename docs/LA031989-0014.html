March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

Correction Appended

RICHARD ARMOUR

Regarding the recent passing of whimsical poet Richard Armour (Los Angeles Times, Part 1, March 1):

Poet Richard Armour (sigh!)

Wrote doggerel's magnum opus,

Now that he's left for places high,

I hope-us we can cope-us.

ED HYSAR

RESEDA
