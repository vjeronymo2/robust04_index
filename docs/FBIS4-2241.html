BFN [Text] Cairo, April 8 (XINHUA) -- Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat met with Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Yang Fuchang Thursday [7 April]. 'Arafat reviewed the latest developments of the Middle East peace process, particularly the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations on implementing the September 13 accord on Palestinian self-rule in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank area of Jericho. 'Arafat hoped that the negotiations will make headway as soon as possible and the accord on Palestinian self-rule will be put into effect in time. The Chinese ambassador told 'Arafat that China supports the persistent efforts made by the Palestine Liberation Organization and the parties concerned to push forward the Middle East peace negotiations. Yang said he hoped that a just and comprehensive peace will be brought about in the Middle East as early as possible through negotiations. 'Arafat arrived here Wednesday for consultations with President Husni Mubarak of Egypt on all peace-related issues, especially the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations.
