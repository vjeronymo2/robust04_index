BFN [Sulayman Nimr report: "Saudi Arabia Refuses To Host Multilateral Negotiations Committees"] [Excerpts] Doha -- The meeting of the Arms Limitation and Regional Security Committee arising from the multilateral negotiations concluded in Doha yesterday. [passage omitted] Prince Turki Bin-Muhammad Bin-Sa'ud, head of the Saudi delegation, has told AL-HAYAH that Saudi Arabia refuses to host any meeting of the committees arising from the multilateral negotiations or to attend any of their meetings in Israel.
