BFN [Text] South Korea has delivered 1.5 tonnes of humanitarian aid, including blankets and medicines, to the Ministry of Health of Kyrgyzstan. The consignment was brought by the Korean ambassador to Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. This is his second visit to our republic, the first one being three months ago when he presented his credentials. The ambassador has met with representatives of the Korean diaspora. More than 18,000 Koreans are living in Kyrgyzstan, more than 10,000 of them in the capital. A number of joint ventures have been set up with them.
