940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Country Casuals sells print works Country Casuals Holdings, the women's wear group, has sold its Dutch textile printing operations for Fl 3m (Pounds 1m), less Fl 500,000 for staff reorganisation costs. The printing works are being sold to Lekal Pensioen, a consortium led by Mr HJ Koelers. Net asset value of the Dutch works was Fl 522,000 and it incurred losses of Fl 279,000 in 1992.
