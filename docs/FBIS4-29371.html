BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 17 (KCNA) -- The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, today received Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter and his entourage on a visit to Korea. First Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Kang Sok-chu was present on the occasion. President Kim Il-song had a sincere talk with Jimmy Carter on matters of mutual concern. He gave a luncheon for the guest.
