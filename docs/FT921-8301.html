920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Determined route to success - In A Nutshell Determination rather than knowledge, intelligence or luck is the main factor in career success, according to a survey of managers by recruitment consultants Robert Half. Fifty-six per cent of respondents listed determination, followed by 'hard work', mentioned by 14 per cent. Nine per cent said good contacts were the key to success, followed by knowledge, mentioned by 7 per cent, and luck, cited by 5 per cent. Tel. 071 836 3545.
