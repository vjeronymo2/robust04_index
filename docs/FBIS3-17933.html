Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow March 30 TASS -- "We have to meet, discuss and solve all the problems which can accumulate in bilateral relations", President Boris Yeltsin said on Wednesday before meeting US Secretary of Commerce Ronald Brown. The Russian leader noted that he was aware of the essence of the talks which Brown had held with Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. The meeting is attended by Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, presidential aide Dmitriy Ryurikov and US Ambassador in Moscow Thomas Pickering.
