March 7, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

IRA BARRAGE KILLS 3 IN ULSTER GARAGE

IRA gunmen shot and killed three men at a Northern Ireland parking garage today, police said.

The Irish Republican Army said it attacked the garage in the County Tyrone village of Coagh because it is a local stronghold for the Ulster Volunteer Force, an outlawed Protestant paramilitary group. The Rev. William McCrea, the local unionist member of Parliament, said, "The gunmen opened fire from a passing car. After doing their job, they cheered as they drove away."
