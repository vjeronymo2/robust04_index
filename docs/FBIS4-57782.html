BFN [Text] As a reminder, next we reiterate the dates on which the new prices and fees on selected products and services established by accord of the Council of Ministers' executive committee will go into effect. New prices for controlled-distribution cigarettes, rolled tobacco, beer, rum, brandy, and fuel for private vehicles will go into effect after the next sale cycle, which begins on 1 June. New fees for electric service for homes will become effective on the basis of September consumption, which will be payable in October. In the case of air and maritime transportation to and from the Isle of Youth, new prices will go into effect beginning 1 June. Likewise, new prices for interprovincial bus and railroad transportation will go into efffect beginning 1 September, as will the new national and international postal and telegraph prices. Water service fees for homes will increase on October 1, while retail food prices at workers' cafeterias will change beginning 1 July. We also want to remind you that the accord of the Council of Ministers Executive Committee includes provisional increases in coastal transportation fees where required. These prices will be set definitely on 1 January 1995.
