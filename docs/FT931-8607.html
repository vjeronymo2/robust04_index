930218 FT 18 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Hammerson property disposal HAMMERSON Property Investment & Development has sold Lonsdale Chambers, Chancery Lane, London, to overseas investors for Pounds 21m. The property was refurbished in 1984 and provides about 60,000 sq ft of office accommodation and 10,000 sq ft of retail space. Top rents in the building, which is let to 15 tenants, are Pounds 40 per sq ft.
