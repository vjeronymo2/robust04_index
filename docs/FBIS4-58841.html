BFN [Text] Aden, 7 May (WAKH) -- The southern leadership in Aden has asked the United Nations to intervene to stop the fighting in Yemen. Radio Aden reported this evening that Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas last night telephoned UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali and informed him of the bloody incidents in Yemen. The radio said Dr. Butrus-Ghali stressed UN keenness to help Yemen to stop the fighting and overcome the crisis. The radio pointed out that al-'Attas asked Yemen's permanent UN representative to hold consultations with the president and members of the Security Council to coordinate international efforts to support the efforts of the Arab League to end the war in Yemen.
