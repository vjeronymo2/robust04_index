Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 24 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met and had a cordial talk with John Patten, visiting president of the U.S. Business Week Corporation, and his party here this afternoon. The U.S. Business Week Corporation and China Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Publishing House have jointly published BUSINESS WEEK in Chinese since 1986, which has served the exchange of the economic and trade informations between China and the United States.
