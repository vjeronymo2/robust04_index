910415 FT 15 APR 91 / UK Company News: Tyndall approach Jupiter Tarbutt Merlin, the fund management group, is understood to have made a bid approach to Tyndall, the quoted financial services company, after picking up a 2.9 per cent stake. Tyndall announced it had received an approach on Friday after its share price had risen sharply in Thursday's trading. Its shares moved ahead 10p to 60p on Friday. Jupiter Tarbutt Merlin recently joined the stock market via the reverse takeover of Vantage, an investment trust. Tyndall also has an investment management arm, but recently wrote off its investment in its Australian subsidiary.
