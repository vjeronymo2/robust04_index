BFN [Text] The new Directorate for Affairs of Cubans Residing Abroad [Direccion para los Asuntos de los Cubanos Residentes en el Exterior] has been created at the Foreign Ministry. The creation of the directorate was announced on 24 May by Foreign Ministry spokesman Miguel Alfonso, who pointed out that the decision of the Cuban Government to create this directorate, directly subordinate to the minister's office, is part of the accords made in the recent conference, The Nation and the Emigre Community: [Begin Alfonso recording] The characteristics of this new directorate are: First, it is directly subordinate to the foreign minister. As its name -- Directorate for Affairs of Cubans Residing Abroad -- indicates, the goal is to harmonize and coordinate an entire series of problems facing the emigre community and the solutions to those problems. [end recording] The Foreign Ministry spokesman also announced that Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina will travel to Cairo to participate in the ministerial conference of the Nonaligned Movement from 31 May to 3 June. Later, Robaina will make an official trip to Qatar to inaugurate the Cuban Embassy. Alfonso added that in coming months, Cuba will participate in the Fourth Ibero-American Summit in Cartagena de Indias on 12-15 June, and the assembly that will establish the Association of Caribbean States in Barbados.
