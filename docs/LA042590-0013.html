April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: DRIVE-BY GANG SHOOTING VICTIM DIES OF WOUNDS

A man hit by gang gunfire in a drive-by shooting in the Willowbrook area last week died Monday of his wounds, sheriff's deputies said.

The victim, Javier Casteneda, 26, was not a gang member, but gang members are believed to have fired the shots that killed him, Deputy Pat Hunter said.

Casteneda was standing with a group of friends about 8:45 p.m. Friday in front of his home when a gunman in a passing car fired several shots at them with a small-caliber handgun, striking Casteneda in the head.
