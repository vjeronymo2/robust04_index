BFN [Text] Today, 240 soldiers belonging to the Cordoba Group are traveling to Croatia in a plane flying the UN flag. From there, from Croatia, some of them will go to the Bosnian towns of Dracevo, Mostar, and Medjugorje, and the logistics group will go on to the Croatian town of Divulje. This is the fourth and most numerous group of blue helmets belonging to the Cordoba Group to leave for Bosnia-Herzegovina to relieve the members of the Madrid Group [of Spanish troops].
