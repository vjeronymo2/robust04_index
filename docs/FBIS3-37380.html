Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, February 15 (TASS) -- Chief of the General Staff of the Russian Armed Forces Colonel-General Mikhail Kolesnikov described as falsehoods reports of a number of the media that he had sent in a resignation to the Russian president. Kolesnikov said these abominable publications are aimed at splitting the leadership of the Defence Ministry and thus weakening one of the most stable institutions of the state -- the armed forces. In his statement for the press circulated by the Information and Press Agency of the Defence Ministry, the chief of the General Staff said no secret is made of the work of the Defence Ministry. Its work can always be discussed in an open and constructive way. Representatives of the media are informed of every important function of the Defence Ministry or of the General Staff. Those who are really interested in getting reliable information about the armed forces have every opportunity to receive this information, the statement says.
