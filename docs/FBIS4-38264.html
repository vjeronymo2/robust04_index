BFN [Text] President 'Arafat, chairman of the PLO executive committee and chairman of the Palestinian National Authority, today decided that a provisional committee be set up to manage the municipality of Nablus, whose task will be to manage the administration of the municipality in accordance with the provisions of municipality laws and regulations, and to make preparations for municipal elections in the town. The work of the committee will last one year during which the elections will be held. Here are the names of the brother members of the provisional committees: lawyer Ghassan Walid al-Shak'a, Dr. Hatim Jamil Kamal, engineer Hani Tawfiq 'Arafat, engineer Adnan, Umar al-Dirhalli, Azzam Muhammad Sa'id al-Alul, Samir Sadiq Qadri, engineer Abbas Muhammad Hasan Jabir, agricultural engineer 'Amid Wahid al-Masri, Ibrahim Sa'id Shikha, Khaldun Bakr 'Abd al-Haq, Dr. Amin Haddad, Muhammad Yusif Shahin, engineer Mufid Adil Nabulsi, Asim Ghalib Salim, Salih Rushdi 'Abd al-Hadi, and brother Saliq Nasuh Kan'an.
