Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN ["M.S."-initialled report: "15,000 Mercenaries Arriving"] [Text] The Indonesian committee for helping Bosnian Muslims has announced that at least 15,000 young men had reported to leave for Bosnia. Ahmed Sumarugono [name as published], deputy chairman of this committee, said, according to a report in the Istanbul newspaper GUNAYDIN, that about 50,000 voluntaries will be registered by the end of the week, after which the Indonesian Army will be asked to train all those people who have registered to leave for Bosnia in handling arms and in other military skills as soon as possible.
