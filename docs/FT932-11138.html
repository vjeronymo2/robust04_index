930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Change in UK Perrier distribution COCA-COLA & Schweppes Beverages, the soft drinks joint venture between Coca-Cola and Cadbury Schweppes, is to take over UKdistribution of mineral water brands belonging to Perrier of France and its owner, Nestle of Switzerland. The new agreement, which takes effect in early 1994, covers the Perrier, Buxton, Vittel and Ashbourne brands. With Cadbury Schweppes' Malvern Water, the brands had UK sales last year of almost Pounds 30m. The Perrier brands were previously distributed by HP Bulmer, the cider maker. The new arrangements follow Nestle's acquisition of Perrier last year.
