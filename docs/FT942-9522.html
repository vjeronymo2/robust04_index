940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices White pepper - fewer sellers and some more buying interest for the new crop positions. Prices remained unchanged. July/Oct shipment is offered at USDollars 2,575 cif. Black pepper - prices stable. Buying interest still low. Faq spot at USDollars 1,575 and Grade 1 at about USDollars 1,650. Indonesia reports that the Lampong crop will be delayed. Cassia and cinnamon -spot positions tight. Indonesian export capacity is booked until June/July. Prices moving up further. Nutmegs - Prices in the Far East are increasing but the European market is not yet following because of the unsold stocks there. This situation is expected to change as soon as these stocks have disappeared. Pimento, cloves unchanged.
