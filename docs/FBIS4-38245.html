BFN [Excerpt] Aden, 29 Jun -- A military communique issued by the forces of the Democratic Yemeni government that has been declared in the southern part of Yemen said that Sanaa's forces have launched an intensive attack this morning on Aden from the north and the west. The communique added that battles are continuing between the attacking northern forces and the southern forces defending Aden. The communique indicated that rockets and missiles being fired at random by the northern forces have hit residential areas and led to more civilians being killed and wounded. The communique did not give the number of those killed and wounded as a result of the resumption of fighting today because final statistics are not available. [passage omitted]
