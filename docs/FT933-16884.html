930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Dwyer There was little change in half year results at Dwyer, the Irish-based commercial property investor, as losses on sales masked a substantial improvement in underlying performance. In the six months to March 31 net rents fell to Pounds 2.33m (Pounds 2.59m) and that was exacerbated by losses on disposals of Pounds 537,000. However, there were substantial reductions in administration costs and interest payable to leave the pre-tax loss at Pounds 729,000 (Pounds 731,000). Losses per share came to 2.84p (2.85p).
