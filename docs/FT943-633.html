940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Wensum well ahead Higher sales and improved productivity helped the Wensum Company, clothing manufacturer, turn in a substantial increase in pre-tax profits for the half year to July 30, up from Pounds 56,000 to Pounds 509,000. Turnover was up by 35 per cent to Pounds 5.59m (Pounds 4.12m). Earnings per share were 4.56p (0.56p) and there is an increased interim of 1.5p (0.625p).
