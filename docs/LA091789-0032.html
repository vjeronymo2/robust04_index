September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A SOBERING THOUGHT

Gregory Harrison, who "Bares Soul to Help Drug Abuse Victims" ("From Addict to Role Model," by Lynn Simross, Sept. 5) also bares a considerable ego. As a two-year member of Alcoholics Anonymous and Cocaine Anonymous, he must surely have heard the 11th tradition of the fellowship, which is read at every meeting: ". . . we need always to maintain personal anonymity at the level of press, radio, and films."

MICHAEL, Los Angeles
