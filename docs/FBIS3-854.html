Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Minister Dr. Eneias Comiche has admitted that taxes in force until now have been quite high and he added that they were to be reduced for that very reason. Speaking at a meeting with Mozambican and foreign businessmen in the Mozambican capital, Finance Minister Comiche said such reductions had been introduced in the wake of a profound study on the prevailing fiscal taxes. Those reduction affect primarily the main taxes, notably the Industrial Contribution Tax, the Complementary Tax, and the Income Tax.
