April 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DUNNING HOUSE

Please encourage anyone who is involved with the Dunning house to let it remain. Bend requirements, if necessary, to have it declared a "historic" house. So what, if it isn't really.

There is so little of the past that remains in the disposable society of today. I love to drive by older, well-kept houses. Not everything in Los Angeles needs to be new and palatial.

BARBARA S. WINDT

Los Angeles
