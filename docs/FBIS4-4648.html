BFN [By reporter Yi Yong-yon] [Text] In connection with the plan to deploy U.S. Patriot missiles to the Korean peninsula, about 500 university students affiliated to the Korean Federation of University Student Councils [Hanchongnyon] held a sit-in demonstration for about an hour at 1730 on 2 April in front of Gate 21 of the Eighth U.S. Army in Namyong-tong, Yongsan-ku, Seoul, shouting the slogan "The United States must withdraw its plan to deploy war weapons." The police took about 450 university students to 15 police stations for questioning.
