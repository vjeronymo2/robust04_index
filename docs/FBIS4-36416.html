BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Ali al-Jifri, deputy chairman of the Presidential Council, accompanied by brothers Sulayman Nasir Mas'ud, member of the Presidential Council, and 'Abd-al-Qawi Muhammad Rashad, member of the Presidium of the National Assembly, this morning made an inspection tour of several residential quarters in Crater, al-Mansurah, Shaykh 'Uthman, and Dar Sa'd in Aden Governorate. He also visited a number of police stations and acquainted himself with how daily life was proceeding and with the concerns and problems affecting the inhabitants as the result of their water supplies being cut. He also acquainted himself with the state of homes that had been damaged as the result of the random shelling by the [word indistinct] of the ruling military regime in Sanaa, to which a number of peaceful and innocent citizens had fallen victim. The deputy chairman of the Presidential Council and those accompanying him also visited the Abyan front and met the commanders of the axis. They acquainted themselves with the progress of operations on the front, expressing the appreciation of the political leadership with the heroic steadfastness of those defending the territory of the Democratic Republic of Yemen and in defense of its brave people.
