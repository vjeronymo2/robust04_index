941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Wiggins in black with Pounds 172,000 Wiggins Group, the commercial and residential property development concern, reported a return to profits for the six months to the end of September. On turnover of Pounds 1.84m (Pounds 12,000) pre-tax profits were Pounds 172,000, compared with losses of Pounds 84,000. For the year to March 31 losses were Pounds 1.58m, after taking into account exceptional losses of Pounds 1.6m. During the period it sold a site at the Kent International Business Park for Pounds 1m and said that it had received a large number of enquiries for more than 1m sq ft of factory and distribution units. It is also proceeding with the acquisition of a 26.08 per cent stake in Tomorrows Leisure with which it is to carry out joint ventures. Earnings per share were 0.035p (losses 0.5p).
