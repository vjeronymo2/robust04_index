Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow, 16 Feb -- The third session of the Federation Council is to open on 25 February and not the 18th as was announced earlier, the ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the chamber's office. This is due to the postponement of the parliamentary address of the president of the Russian Federation. We remind you, that the majority of the deputies to the upper chamber of the Federal Assembly, working on a non-permanent basis, are now in the regions where they are carrying out their permanent duties. They will gather in Moscow on 24 February, to hear Boris Yeltsin's message together with the deputies of the State Duma.
