Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Datuk Sri Anwar Ibrahim has announced that the cabinet has decided not to entertain participation by British companies in new contracts, especially those funded by the government. He said this in Kuala Lumpur today following British press reports of the alleged tactics used by the British Government and British companies in winning Malaysian contracts. The alleged arms-for-aid deal has prompted the House of Commons Select Committee on Foreign Affairs to launch an investigation into the Pergau Dam affair.
