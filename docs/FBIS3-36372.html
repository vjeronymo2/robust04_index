Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Efim Shvartsman] [Text] Simferopol February 4 TASS -- Yuriy Meshkov, who was elected the first president of the Crimea, was inaugurated on Friday at a session of the parliament of the peninsula. The 49-year old lawyer put his hand on the constitution of the Crimea and the human rights declaration and read out the oath of the president. He said his actions would be based on people's will and law.
