Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The ANC [African National Congress] says there is little likelihood that Parliament will be moved from Cape Town in the foreseeable future. The head of the department of information and publicity in the Western Cape, Mr. Bulelani Ngcuka, and parliamentary candidate Jay Naidoo told a news conference that building another parliament would be a waste of resources. They said the ANC's priorities were jobs, housing, electricity, and health services, not putting up new public buildings.
