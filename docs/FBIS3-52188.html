Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 2, IRNA -- Visiting Turkmen vice-president, Boris Shikhmuradov, today met Iran's Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati here and discussed avenues to boost Tehran-Ashgabat ties. The officials of the two countries exchanged views on such issues as raliways, oil and gas, border issues, consulate affairs, customs and industrial issues as well as transmission of elecricity from Turkmenistan to other countries via Iran. Shikhmuradov briefed Velayati on the recent meeting of the heads of Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) in Ashgabat. "The Turkmen nation attaches great importance to relations with Iran," Shikhmuradov said. Meanwhile, the visiting Turkmen minister of culture and tourism, Geldymurad Nurmukhamedov, in a meeting with Iran's culture and Islamic guidance minister, 'Ali Larijani, today expressed hope for fruitful cooperation with Iran in the fields of culture and art including press, tourism, sports as well as radio and television. Larijani in return expressed readiness to speed up bilateral negotiations in order to sign a protocol for establishment of four commissions for expanding cooperation in the fields of religion, culture, art, sports and tourism. He also expressed Iran's willingness to sign a cultural protocol with Turkmenistan. Meanwhile Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov heading a delegation arrived in Tehran today for a five-day visit and was welcomed by Iran's President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, Iranian ministers of foreign affairs, finance and economic affairs, road and transport, the interior, agriculture, and culture and Islamic guidance.
