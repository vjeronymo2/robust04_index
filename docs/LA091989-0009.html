September 19, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FIGHTING THE WAR ON DRUGS

The Times provided its readership with an apt illustration of why we're losing the war on drugs when, on the day after the President's drug policy speech, it featured on its Op-Ed Page two "just say yes to drugs" items: the article by Prof. Nadelmann advocating legalization of drugs and the Morin cartoon excusing drug dealing in the ghetto because of allegedly "no jobs, no opportunity, no education."

HERBERT L. ROTH

Beverly Hills
