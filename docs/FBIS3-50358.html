Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 28 Jan--President Lech Walesa today received Israel's Knesset Speaker Shevah Weiss, who has been on a parliamentary visit to Poland. Weiss handed the president a letter from Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin, in which he recalls the importance of the visit to Israel by Lech Walesa and underscores that Poland occupies an important place in Europe. Prime Minister Rabin writes that everything connected with the known Israeli-Palestinian declaration is most important in Israeli policy. During talks in the Belweder Palace, reference was also made to history and the contemporary issues of both states and the world. President Walesa said that Poland is presented so far as a cemetery of our (Polish and Jewish) nations. He called for looking into the future and focusing on the youth and their upbringing. He also underscored that Poland is a victim of the betrayal by "the great of this world."
