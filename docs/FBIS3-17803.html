Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Armenian Defense Ministry press service informed us that they have no new information to add to the report issued this morning. According to this morning's report, the Azerbaijani Army fiercely bombarded Choratan, Artsvaberd, Movses, and Aygepar in Tavushskiy Rayon and the defensive positions around these villages with Grad rockets, tanks, and howitzers between 1650-1720 yesterday. The shelling caused destruction, deaths, and injuries. The situation remained tense on the borders of Idzhevanskiy and Noyemberyanskiy rayons between 1830-2315. The Azerbaijani border troops fired towards Diran, Voskepar, and (?Sosi) using tanks, artillery, and infantry fighting vehicles. The shelling caused some injuries in Noyemberyanskiy. This periodic aggression proves that, as a rule, Azerbaijan uses the relative calm on the Armenian borders to prepare for new border violations. The Armenian Army's fighters had no choice but to retaliate in kind in all directions yesterday evening to force the Azerbaijanis stop the shelling. The situation was calm on Armenia's borders during the night and this morning. [passage omitted] Situation in Karabakh: After having suffered a major defeat in the direction of Agdam on eastern front yesterday, the Azerbaijani Army carried out a massive rocket and artillery bombardment of Karabakh positions in that sector this morning. The artillery shelling was still continuing at 0900. Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army headquarters press service reported that the Karabakh formations are taking countermeasures. The situation was relatively calm on the remainder of the front.
