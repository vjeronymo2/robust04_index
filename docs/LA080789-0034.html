August 7, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

FOUR KILLED, 24 HURT AS CAR HITS TOUR BUS HEAD-ON

Four young people were killed and 24 other people were injured when a car slammed head-on into a tour bus carrying senior citizens, police said Sunday.

The accident occurred Saturday when the car crossed the center line and hit the bus, state police said. All four people in the car were killed when it was hit by another vehicle, officials said.
