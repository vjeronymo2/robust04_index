940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: S Korean shipyard set for stoppage Thousands of workers at the world's largest shipyard, in South Korea, plan to go on 'temporary strike' during today's Group C match between South Korea and Bolivia in Boston. The labour union at Hyundai Heavy Industries in Ulsan, south-east of Seoul, said it plans to call a three-hour stoppage to enable its 25,000 members to watch the match. South Korea need to beat the Bolivians if they are to reach the second round for the first time.
