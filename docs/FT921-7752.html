920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Leslie Wise falls 43% to Pounds 2.86m Leslie Wise, the textiles, womenswear and knitted fabrics group, suffered a 43 per cent fall in profits to Pounds 2.86m pre-tax for the year to end-November. However, with the core companies reporting 'an improved outlook' and 'in the light of the sound financial strength of the group' the dividend for the year is maintained at 4p via a proposed same-again final of 2.25p. The shares rose 7p to 65p. Turnover fell by 7 per cent to Pounds 40.5m. A 93 per cent increase in exports to Pounds 5.31m partially offset a 14 per cent reduction in UK turnover. Tax was reduced from Pounds 1.7m to Pounds 895,000 leaving earnings per share at 5.8p (9.96p).
