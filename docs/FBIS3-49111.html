Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES 4/1/1994 -- The unemployment rate was of 10.2 on January 4, with the number of unemployed standing at 1,164,705, i.e. 8,000 more than on December 27, 1993. 2,000 people were made redundant in the capital over December 27, 1993-January 4, 1994, bringing the total number of Bucharest unemployed at 142,602. Unemployed women outnumbered men. 1,065, 844 people were registered for unemployment benefits or subsistence allowances, of whom 422,673 are men and 623,171 women. 911,191 of the lay-offs are workers, 136,751 graduated high schools and 17,174 higher schools. 428,636 people made redundant no longer received allowances as they no longer met the requirements of the law. 7,972 work places were available in the country and 615 in the capital.
