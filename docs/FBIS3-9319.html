Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 22 (ATA) -- The trial of the KOHA JONE journalists, Martin Leka and Aleksander Frangaj, requested by the prosecutor of the case, Teodor Mosko, was held at the Tirana Court of Appeals on March 21. The court did not change the first sentence given to Martin Leka, but it sentenced with 5 months imprisonment the KOHA JONE editor-in- chief, Aleksander Frangaj, who at the trial of the first degree on February 28, was released. The sentence was requested by Prosecutor Mosko. Meanwhile, Aleksander Frangaj is abroad.
