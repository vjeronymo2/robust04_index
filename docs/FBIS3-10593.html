Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The purpose of the working visit to Venezuela of Cuban Deputy Minister [title as heard] Jorge Aspiolea is to seek cooperation mechanisms between Caracas and Havana, aimed at exploiting water resources. Aspiolea, vice president of the Cuban Institute for Water Resources, has told the press that both parties have set the groundwork for cooperation, service imports, and technical assistance. He added that they have concluded the final details for the creation of a joint Venezuelan-Cuban company for the exploration of water resources in the desert region of Los Medanos in the northwestern state of Falcon. He added that during the talks they discussed a project to study the lease of a dredge, a highly developed technique with broad experience in our country. [sentence as heard]
