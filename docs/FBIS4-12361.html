BFN [Text] Cairo, April 4 (MENA)--Majdi Ahmad Husayn, chief editor of AL-SHA'B newspaper, organ of the opposition Labour Party, and 'Abd-al-Sattar Abu-Husayn, an editor in the same paper, were released by the prosecution after being interrogated in connection with a complaint filed against them by the head of the Heliopolis Company for Chemicals. The paper had accused the company head of allowing relatives to submit tenders and using the company's potentials in the service of his family members in violation of the law. The two journalists asserted that they have documents proving that and pledged to put them forward on Saturday.
