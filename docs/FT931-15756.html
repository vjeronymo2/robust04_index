930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / International Company News: Increased deficit at Torex Hire Losses at Torex Hire, the USM-quoted group with interests in the hire of tools, small plant and catering equipment, rose from Pounds 216,000 to Pounds 335,000 for the year to end-October. The second half deficit of Pounds 45,000 (profits Pounds 12,000) was struck after taking account of redundancy costs of Pounds 47,000. Full year turnover was static at Pounds 4.21m (Pounds 4.42m). The dividend for the year is maintained at 0.4p. Losses per share emerged at 2.09p (1.16p).
