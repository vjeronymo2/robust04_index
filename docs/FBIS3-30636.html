Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 4 (ATA) -- In a meeting held on February 3 by a group of representatives from the existing businessmen associations in Albania, the creation of the Albanian Businessmen Association, KOBIZ, was proclaimed. The association will be independent from the state, political parties and religious communities. Its program stipulates that the confederation appears in the economic and social life of the country as an organization which will represent and support the interests of the industrialists, constructors, merchants, traders and all businessmen who will be organized in their federations. It will contribute to the strengthening of the democratic state and democracy in general as an worthy partner in the triangle state- trade-union-employer. This confederation will be an active organization which will protect the financial position the Albanian businessmen in front of foreign capital competition. The confederation will have relations with its analoguous organizations in other countries.
