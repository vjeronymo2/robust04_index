August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS: GOODBODY PROMOTED TO EXECUTIVE V.P. FOR B OF A REGIONAL UNIT

Erich Goodbody, regional director of the Orange County/San Diego region of Bank of America's real estate industries division, has been promoted to executive vice president.

Goodbody joined the bank in 1986 as division president of BankAmerica Mortgage and International Realty Corp., a subsidiary, and in August, 1986, was promoted to senior vice president.

Previously, he was vice president and manager of Wells Fargo Bank's real estate industries group in the Orange County office.
