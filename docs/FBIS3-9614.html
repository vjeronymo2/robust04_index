Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Banja Luka, 26 Mar (TANJUG) -- Two unidentified aircraft bombed positions of the army of the Srpska republic today, near Velika Suljaga, 15 km northeast of Kupres, Serb military sources report. After the air attack, members of the so-called army of Bosnia-Herzegovina carried out -- from the direction of Bugojno, or rather Mala Suljaga and Glavica -- a powerful artillery attack. In the Prusac region, from where artillery has been in action daily against Kupres and Srbobran, an intensive regrouping of units of the so-called army of Bosnia-Herzegovina has been observed.
