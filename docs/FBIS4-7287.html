BFN ["New Step in Friendly Relations" -- MONTSAME headline] [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 28 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Under such heading the newspaper "ARDYN ERH" publishes today an interview of the deputy head of the National Development Department L. Demberel who is taking part in the forthcoming III [as received] meeting of the inter-governmental Mongolian-Chinese Commission on Economic, Trade, Scientific and Technical Cooperation as a deputy head of the Mongolian delegation. The trade and economic relations between Mongolia and China, he said, have been increasingly intensifying over a few last years. Just for the period from 1991 to 1993 the trade turnover between the two countries has increased nearly three fold. The government of the People's Republic of China (PRC) has granted to Mongolia during the years of 1991, 1992 disinterested credits worth 80 million yuan as well as commodities and gratis financial assistance. In the course of the visit of the premier of the State Council of the PRC, he noted also, it is expected to sign a treaty on friendly relations and cooperation between Mongolia and the PRC, agreements on economic and technical cooperation, on granting by the Chinese Government gratis aid to Mongolia, cooperation on examining the quality of importing and exporting commodities mutually being supplied between the two countries.
