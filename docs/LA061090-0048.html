June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OH, YOU KIDS

I hate Chris Willman! How could he write such a bad review of New Kids on the Block's album "Step by Step" (June 3)? I've heard three of the songs on the album and it already sounds like a hit to me! New Kids must be "all right" if they are No. 20 on the pop chart already!

New Kids make music that teen-agers can relate to! That's not all! My mom happens to love them.

Why don't you go to a concert and see how many adults go with their kids. My mom would never be caught dead at one of my brother's Metallica concerts!

Next time you write a review on them, remember, New Kids rule! And people will always stick up for them!

KELLY SMITH

Los Angeles
