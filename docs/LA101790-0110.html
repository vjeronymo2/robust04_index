October 17, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPOTLIGHT: POP MUSIC

LINER NOTES: Various winners of the ninth annual Entertainer Music Awards will be performing tonight at Club Diego's in Pacific Beach. Like the awards ceremony, which was held last month at the La Paloma Theater in Encinitas, the "Musicians Care for Kids" concert is a benefit for the Mary Lou Clack Center for Handicapped Children. . . .

Best concert bets for the coming week: Ex-Grand Funk singer Mark Farner, tonight at the Bacchanal; Lou Rawls, Friday at Humphrey's on Shelter Island; Bad Manners with the Skatalites, Friday at the Price Center Ballroom; Los Lobos, Saturday at the Starlight Bowl in Balboa Park, and Sam Hinton, Saturday at Words & Music in Hillcrest.
