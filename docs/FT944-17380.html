941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / UK Company News: T&S Stores chooses chief executive Mr Jim McCarthy has been appointed chief executive of T&S Stores, the newsagent and convenience store retailer. Mr McCarthy was previously joint managing director with Mr Stephen Boddice, 42, who retires from business life on December 31. Mr McCarthy joined the T&S board as group retail director from Dillons newsagents, where he had been managing director since 1985. T&S acquired the Dillons and Alfred Preedy chains of confectionery, tobacco and newspaper stores from Next for Pounds 53.9m in 1989. Mr Boddice leaves T&S after 17 years at the Walsall-based company, which has grown from owning three kiosks to more than 700 stores in less than 20 years. Mr David Crellin has been appointed group financial director, after working as operating financial director. He joined T&S in 1990 after directorships at Evode and Scotcade.
