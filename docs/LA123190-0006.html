December 31, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LIFE ON EARTH

In response to Lee Dye's article "Galileo Views Earth With an Alien's Eye" (front page, Dec. 20):

In light of all the endless conflicts between men across our planet, Galileo, the spacecraft, seems to have discerned the root of the problem -- "it detected no clear sign of intelligent life" on planet Earth!

MARILYN E. WHITAKER, Glendale
