930402 FT 02 APR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Imported avocados from Carmel are excellent this week at 50-60p each (60-65p), reports FFVIB. English Bramley apples remain good at 35-45p a lb (35-45p), along with white-fleshedgrapefruit at 15-25p each (15-25p) and Jaffa Shamouti oranges at 10-25p each (10-25p). English leeks continue to be good quality at 50-60p a lb (55-70p). Onions at 18-20p a lb (18-20p), swede at 15-20p a lb (15-20p) and parsnips at 20-30p a lb (20-30p) remain stable. New season Spring onions are 30-35p a bunch (30-35p). Iceberg lettuce at 50-90p each (Pounds 1.00-1.25) and celery at 45-50p a head (45-50p) complete this week's salad prices.
