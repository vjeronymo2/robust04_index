941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / Observer: Quavering What was that faint tremolo perceptible during the Vienna Philharmonic's otherwise exemplary performance at London's Royal Festival Hall on Wednesday evening? With not a single Frau to be seen among the players, could it be that even this notorious bastion of male chauvinism is quaking at the prospect of Austria joining the European Union band and hence being forced to play in tune with Brussels' equal opportunities legislation?
