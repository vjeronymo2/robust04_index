BFN [Report by Ghunaym Muhammad al-Mutayri: "Kuwaiti Cabinet Reshuffle To Include Oil and Interior Ministries"] [Text] Kuwait -- There was an atmosphere of anticipation and expectation in Kuwait yesterday as the countdown began for the announcement of the names to be included in the new cabinet. There is all kinds of speculation in various circles with everyone expressing his own views as to who will be appointed and who will be dismissed, but the final decision rests with Prime Minister and Heir Apparent Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah who is still holding consultations on this matter. AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT has learned from sources close to Shaykh Sa'd that the expected cabinet reshuffle will be ready within the next few days and may be announced Saturday. The sources added that the cabinet reshuffle will cover the ministries of oil, interior, electricity, works, communications, and housing but will not affect the other main ministries such as the foreign, defense, and information ministries. The sources also said that some new names have been tipped for important ministries. They include Shaykh Jabir 'Abdallah al-Jabir al-Sabah, the former cabinet affairs minister who held the post at the time of the occupation and liberation and who also held other posts such as governor of al-Ahmadi and governor of Hawalli. Other prominent names include al-Ahmadi Governor Shaykh Muhammad al-Khalid al-Sabah and former Defense Minister Shaykh Nawwaf al-Ahmad al-Sabah.
