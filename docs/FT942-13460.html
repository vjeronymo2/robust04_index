940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Observer in South Africa: Lunch in sights The Ritz restaurant in a northern Johannesburg enclave of the white and well-off offers more than fine food. The gentleman at the entrance, with a distinctly authoritative air, is not the maitre d'. He packs either an Uzi machine pistol or a sawn-off shotgun; both tried and tested methods of ensuring hold-up artists get their deserts.
