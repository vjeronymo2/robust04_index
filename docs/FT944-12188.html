941102 FT 02 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada The worrisome economic news out of the US took Canadian stocks lower in moderate trading. The TSE 300 composite index finished 28.13 off at 4,263.52, while declines outscored rises by 448 to 308 after volume of 58.3m shares. Losses were broadly based, with all 14 sub-indices weaker, led by forestry, transportation, consumer products and metals. The golds group dipped 0.4 per cent in spite of a rise in the New York spot price for gold of 80 cents to USDollars 384.55 an ounce.
