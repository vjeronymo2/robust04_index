940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Comedy songwriter Donald Swann dies Comedy songwriter and performer Donald Swann (left) died aged 70. He and partner Michael Flanders produced distinctively British musical humour including 'The Hippopotamus Song'. Their revue At The Drop Of A Hat ran for more than 1,700 performances with a transfer from London's West End to Broadway. Flanders, who was wheelchair-bound after polio, died in 1975.
