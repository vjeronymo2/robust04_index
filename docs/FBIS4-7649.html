BFN [Text] Gjirokaster, April 2 (ATA) -- Some 800 Albanians have been expelled from Greece over the Kakavije border crossing point during the past three days. The average number of expelles reached 230 a day. Some of the Albanians have been working in Greece for years with regular visas. They were abused, their money was confiscated, and their passports were torn to pieces.
