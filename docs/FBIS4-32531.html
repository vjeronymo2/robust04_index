BFN [Text] Belgrade, 24 Jun (TANJUG) -- The Yugoslav-Korean Friendship Society was founded in Belgrade today, the society's newly formed main committee announced. The constituent assembly, attended by more than 200 participants, adopted the society's code of practice and elected the 15-member main committee. Milorad Zecevic, director of Coca-Cola [as received], was elected as the society's president, and author Djurica Labovic as secretary. Yu Se-ung, ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Yugoslavia, addressed the participants in the constituent assembly, and thanked the founding committee for its efforts in forming the society.
