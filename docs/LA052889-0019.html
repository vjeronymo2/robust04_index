May 28, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ARSENIO HALL

Patti LaBelle gave Arsenio Hall the best advice he'll probably ever hear. By Hall's being himself we are rewarded with a host who is sincere, interested in his guests, fun and, best of all, funny. Where I live we receive one channel. I'm grateful it happens to be the one that carries "The Arsenio Hall Show."

JILL BROUILLARD

Santa Barbara
