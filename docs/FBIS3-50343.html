Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 26 Jan (TANJUG) -- After a three-hour session this evening, deputies of the republican parliament failed to agree on a joint solution and decided to hold another discussion on Tuesday [1 February] on candidatures for president [speaker] of a new Serbian Assembly. The session was broken off after heads of the deputies' groups of five parliamentary parties -- in the absence of radicals -- agreed to resume the discussion on Tuesday in order to reach a compromise concerning the president of the Serbian Assembly.
