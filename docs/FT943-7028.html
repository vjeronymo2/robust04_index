940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: P&P rights issue 93.9% subscribed A Pounds 8.6m rights issue by P&P, the computer services company, was 93.9 per cent taken up. Acceptances were received for 14.1m new shares at 60p apiece. The proceeds will be used to help fund the Pounds 18m purchase of QA Training, a computer training concern.
