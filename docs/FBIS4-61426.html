BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Chuvashiya President Nikolay Fedorov has issued an edict terminating the duties of the deputies of the soviets of all levels in the territory of this republic. Their mandates have now run out. This is connected with the elections to the new bodies of power. In those places where new bodies of authority have been only partially elected, consultative meetings of the new deputies will operate. In other places, heads of administrations will temporarily carry out the duties of the new authorities. Run-off elections in Chuvashiya have been planned for the end of May.
