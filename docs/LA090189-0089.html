September 1, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE

Multimillionaire publisher Malcolm S. Forbes has a problem: how to celebrate his 71st birthday next year. "We're busy recovering from the last one," Forbes said of the extravagant 70th birthday bash staged earlier this month in Tangier, Morocco, with 700 of his rich and famous friends. Forbes arrived in Maine this week on his 151-foot yacht, Highlander. "We always wander the Maine coast before Labor Day," Forbes said. "It's a state of mind, the state of Maine. Everybody likes to come here." ALEENE MacMINN
