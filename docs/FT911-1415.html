910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Finance & The Family: Britannia launches instant account Britannia Building Society has launched an instant access current account, which will use the 'Link' cash machines. Initial net compound annual interest rates will be 7.85 per cent (10.47 per cent gross). Britannia will phase in the account, offering it in the West Midlands in early May, and then in other regions.
