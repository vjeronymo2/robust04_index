920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Construction Contracts: Supermarket scheme in Essex The JOHN LELLIOTT CONSTRUCTION GROUP has been awarded a total of Pounds 20m worth of contracts, including the Pounds 6.7m management contract to construct and fit out a new supermarket with petrol filling station for Safeway at Chafford Hundred, Grays, Essex. A Pounds 2m contract has also been won to design and construct a supermarket shell for Waitrose in Chantry Fields, Gillingham, Dorset. Another retail project is the Pounds 3m fitting out of the first London store for Sogo, a Japanese department store chain. Work on the two-floor 22,000 sq ft store in the Criterion Building, Piccadilly, includes installation of glazed shopfronts, floor, wall and ceiling finishes, mechanical and electrical services and a lift and staircase. Also secured are the Pounds 2m refurbishment of the White Elephant Club in Curzon Street, W1; a Pounds 1.2m fitting out of seven-storey offices at 21 Southampton Row, W1 for Mishcon de Reya; a Pounds 1.2m residential renovation including a swimming pool at 13 Melbury Road, Holland Park, W14 and a Pounds 1.1m refurbishment of bedrooms at Forte Crest Hotel in St James, SW1. Other contracts include the Pounds 900,000 fitting out of Balliol House in Banbury Business Park for CC Projects; Pounds 900,000 formation of three offices for Abbey National Building Society in Palmers Green, Walthamstow and Epping; Pounds 600,000 psychiatric ward in the Royal Free Hospital, Hampstead, NW3; Pounds 500,000 building of extension to Sandown Bay leisure centre, Isle of Wight; and Pounds 350,000 provision of a new reception area and other works at Banqueting House, Whitehall, SWl for Historic Royal Palaces.
