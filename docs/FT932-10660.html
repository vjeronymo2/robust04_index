930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Capital gains boost Highcroft Trust Pre-tax profits of Highcroft Investment Trust rose from Pounds 1.06m to Pounds 1.26m over 1992. The year included capital profits and the comparison has been adjusted. Dividends and interest came to Pounds 223,000 (Pounds 233,000) and net rental income was Pounds 838,000 (Pounds 808,000). Net taxed capital profits of Pounds 276,000 (Pounds 184,000) have been taken to reserve. Earnings per share, including capital profits, rose to 16.9p (14.3p). The final dividend is 3.1p to make 4.9p (4.5p). Properties were revalued at December 31 at Pounds 10.8m, and the deficit of Pounds 1.35m went to revaluation reserve.
