BFN [Text] The majority of the peoples in the European Union [EU] support a quick accession of Cyprus to the Union. A poll conducted for the EU last February and made public today shows that 53 percent of the Europeans in the poll support the quick accession of Cyprus, 35 percent were against, and 12 percent did not express an opinion. Regarding Turkey, the poll had a negative result: 43 percent of those asked were in favor of Turkey's accession and 48 percent were against. Of those in favor of Cyprus' accession, first were the Greeks, then the Irish, followed by the Spaniards and British.
