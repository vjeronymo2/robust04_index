930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / AT&T bid payment A former employee of American Telephone & Telegraph, accused by the US Securities and Exchange Commission of insider trading during AT&T's 1990 bid for NCR, is to pay Dollars 262,314 in penalties and Dollars 306,768 in profits and interest from the dealings, reports Martin Dickson in New York. The man, Mr Robert Dudgeon, worked in AT&T's corporate strategy department at the time of the bid.
