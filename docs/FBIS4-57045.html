BFN [Text] Cuban President Fidel Castro is having dinner with the current Bahia governor and with former Governor Antonio Carlos Magalhaes. Fidel Castro was returning to Cuba after attending Nelson Mandela's installation in South Africa, and decided to make a stopover in Salvador. Since very early, a strict security regime had been organized at the Salvador Air Force base to welcome Cuban President Fidel Castro. Members of the Air Force and of the Federal Police cordoned off the landing strip and allowed only members of the welcoming delegation to enter. President Fidel Castro arrived at the Air Force base in the middle of the night on board a Cuban Airline plane that was returning from South Africa, where he attended Nelson Mandela's installation. Fidel Castro was welcomed by Bahia Governor Antonio Imbassahy and Salvador Mayor Lidice da Matta. It still has not been reported how long President Castro will stay in Salvador, or his agenda for the visit.
