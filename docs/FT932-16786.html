930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Guide to stockbrokers - At a Glance A new directory of private client stockbrokers has been issued by Apcims, the Association of Private Client Investment Managers and Stockbrokers. Professional and Impartial Investment Advice contains an introduction to stock market investment and a comprehensive directory of Apcims members throughout the UK, detailing their services. It is available free by writing to Apcims at 20 Dysart Street, London EC2A 2BX or by calling 0891-335521.
