BFN [Text] Beijing, 26 Mar (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE) -- Li Peng, premier of the State Council, and Qiao Shi, chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, wrote inscriptions a few days ago to greet the 10th anniversary of the national law science theoretical magazine ZHONGGUO FAXUE [CHINA LAW SCIENCE], which is published by the Law Society. Li Peng's inscription reads: "Govern the country with law and carry out administration according to the law." Qiao Shi's inscription reads: "Strengthen studies in the science of law, serve the establishment of a legal system for the socialist market economy." Wang Hanbin, Ren Jianxin, Lei Jieqiong, and Peng Chong also wrote inscriptions of greeting to mark the 10th anniversary of ZHONGGUO FAXUE. ZHONGGUO FAXUE is a law science theoretical magazine of the law science circles of China. It has a relatively high theoretical level and more pages than other law publications. In the past 10 years, the magazine has closely linked reform and opening up with the building of democracy and the legal system, and has published nearly 1,000 theses totaling 10 million characters. The magazine has played a positive role in promoting the studies and research of law science circles and the legal circles of China in the Marxist science of law and Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, as well as in providing theoretical bases, policy-making reference, and public opinion support for building democracy and the legal system in China. The magazine is highly spoken of by the law science circles and legal circles of China and has a considerable influence in foreign countries.
