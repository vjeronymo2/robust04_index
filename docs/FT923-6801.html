920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Changes planned at Embassy Embassy Property, the USM-quoted property developer, housebuilder and construction group, is proposing a share placing, a rights issue and a restructuring of group borrowings. The placing is of 56m new ordinary shares at 5p to raise Pounds 2.5m net which will result in a change of control of the company. The 3-for-4 rights issue of 5.73m shares at 5p will raise some Pounds 280,000. The directors are of the opinion that the proposals will place Embassy in a position to take new opportunities which will arise in the commercial property market over the next two to three years.
