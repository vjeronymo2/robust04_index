December 21, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

FNN TO BUY STOCK DATA SERVICE

Financial News Network said today it has agreed to acquire Lotus Information Network Corp., a stock market information service for private investors, from Lotus Development Corp.

Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.

FNN, a cable television network, said it intends to combine LINC with its Data Broadcasting Corp. subsidiary, which uses a similar technology to feed market quotes and financial news into personal computer systems.

Infotechnology Inc., an information and communications company that owns United Press International, owns 45% of FNN.

"The acquisition of LINC positions FNN as the dominant player in the distribution of real-time market data to the private investor," said John F. Berentson, FNN executive vice president.
