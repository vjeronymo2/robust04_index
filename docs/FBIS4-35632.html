BFN [Text] The Higher Council for Oil, Economic, and Investment Affairs held a meeting today under Acting Prime Minister Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar. It discussed a number of topics on its agenda and approved a decision to begin the best and quickest exploitation of the Hulaywah oil field in the Jannah sector. The Council instructed the Ministry of Oil and Mineral Resources to take the necessary measures to guarantee the best revenues to serve the national economy. The Council also discussed a number of reports submitted by the Finance Ministry, the Central Bank, and the Ministry of Oil and Mineral Resources, and took the necessary measures on them.
