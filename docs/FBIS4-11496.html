BFN [Text] An official communique issued by the Ucayali Front Political-Military Command reports that five Shining Path [SL] terrorists, among them a woman known as Comrade Elisa, who was logistical commander of the SL unit Atreverse, were killed during a clash on 21 April in Lower Chambira and Nuevo Progreso in Ucayali Department. The communique adds that guns and abundant subversive propaganda were seized on the occasion.
