930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: First half sales rise 18% at Irish Life For the first six months of 1993 sales at Irish Life rose 18 per cent to IPounds 165.4m (Pounds 154.9m). Single premium sales in Ireland were down slightly at IPounds 60.7m while recurring premium sales fell by 8 per cent to IPounds 26m. In the UK, single premium sales were ahead by 22 per cent to IPounds 13.7m but recurring premium sales were down 12 per cent to IPounds 7.3m.
