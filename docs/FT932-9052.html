930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Muntok white pepper prices were unchanged, with spot at Dollars 1,650 a tonne, April/May delivery ay Dollars 1,550, cif, and July/August at Dollars 1,580, cif, reports Man Producten. Indian MG-1 spot was quoted at Dollars 1,200 a tonne and May/June delivery at Dollars 1,125, cif. Cloves prices were also unchanged. Madagascan spot was at Dollars 850 a tonne and shipment at Dollars 750. Brazil cloves were offered at Dollars 900 fob and Zanzibar spot at Dollars 825. Nutmegs and mace were cheaper, with BWP spot at Dollars 700 a tonne and shipment at Dollars 675, shrivels at Dollars 1,200, spot, and Dollars 950, shipment, ABCD spot at Dollars 1,300 and shipment at Dollars 1,150.
