BFN [Excerpts] Burg (DDP/ADN) -- Today the 10th Russian Guards Tank Division left Altengrabow Garrison in Burg district. This means that the last major combat unit of the former Soviet forces in Saxony-Anhalt is now on its way home. Wolfgang Rauls, deputy prime minister and land environment minister, stressed that there has been extensive personal contacts between local people and the members of the division, despite the odd problem with living side by side over the past few years. [passage omitted] Before the withdrawal agreement there were around 100,000 Russian soldiers as well as 40,000 civilian employees and family members stationed in Saxony-Anhalt. They were based at a total of 276 locations.
