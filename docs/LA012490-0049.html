January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

In other boys' games:

Loyola 86, Crespi 64 -- Joe Burghardt had 19 points to lead Loyola (12-3, 5-0) in a Del Rey League game at at Loyola.

Rasaan Hall had 17 points to lead Crespi (9-10, 3-2), which shot 31% from the field in the first half.

Pasadena 72, Glendale 60 -- Jason Harper scored 15 points and grabbed 13 rebounds in host Glendale's Pacific League loss.

Jerry Armen and Brian Porter each had 10 points for Glendale (7-13, 1-5).

Muir 67, Crescenta Valley 65 (OT) -- LaDay Smith's tip-in from four feet in the final second of overtime gave Muir a win in a Pacific League game at Muir.

Paul Matijasevic scored a team-high 16 points and added 14 rebounds for Crescenta Valley (12-7, 4-2), which defeated Muir at home in overtime earlier this season.

Arcadia 72, Hoover 65 -- Despite 21 points from John Hillman and 15 points and 11 rebounds from John Worden, Hoover (4-12, 1-5) fell in the Pacific League game at Hoover.

Harvard 97, St. Genevieve 49 -- Rick Osterloh (19), Mike Wolf (16), Matt Clark (12), Spencer Miller (11) and Andres Carlo (10) all scored in double figures as Harvard routed the Valiants in a San Fernando Valley League game at St. Genevieve.

L.A. Baptist 50, Village Christian 35 -- Reid Chobanian scored a game-high 17 points and teammate Glen Dodson added nine points and 10 assists to lead host L. A. Baptist in an Alpha League game.

The win was the fourth in a row for the Knights (7-10, 4-3) after they opened league play with three consecutive losses.

Burroughs 93, Schurr 59 -- Marnie Calderon scored 23 points to lead four Indians in double figures in a Foothill league game at Burroughs (11-6, 5-0). S. J. Boldvich scored 20 points, Ray Velasquez had 16, and Ryan Goeringer added 14 for Burroughs.

Chaminade 72, La Salle 64 -- Colin Havert scored a season-high 28 points and had six steals as Chaminade (9-8, 3-2) used a 23-12 fourth-quarter advantage to overcome a 52-49 deficit.

Faith Baptist 87, Bel-Air Prep 72 -- Darren Wyre scored 23 points to lead Faith Baptist (10-8, 4-1) to a Delphic League victory over Bel-Air Prep (2-3 in league play) at home.

GIRLS' BASKETBALL

Thousand Oaks 78, Camarillo 58 -- Michelle Palmisano scored 30 of her school-record 42 points in the second half of a Marmonte League game at Thousand Oaks (11-9, 4-3). Palmisano, a 5-9 sophomore guard, hit 14 field goals -- including three three-point shots -- and was 11 of 13 from the free-throw line.

Palmisano and Shani Smyth shared the previous record of 39, which Palmisano tied earlier this season.

Hart 58, San Gabriel 40 -- Anjanette Dionne scored 27 points and had six assists and six steals to lead Hart to a Foothill League victory over San Gabriel at home. Sara Wilson added 18 points and 18 rebounds.

L.A. Baptist 40, Village Christian 30 -- Kristin Herrema and Karleen Cox each had 11 points to lead L. A. Baptist (8-4, 5-2) to an Alpha League victory over host Village Christian.

Fillmore 55, Oak Park 38 -- Tammy Hill scored 25 points to lead Fillmore (5-3) to a Tri-Valley League victory over Oak Park at home.

Kelli Romney scored 21 points and had 24 rebounds and six steals to lead Oak Park (3-12, 3-5).
