920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / Finance and the Family: Abbey to close accounts - At A Glance Abbey National is winding down its Higher Interest and Seven Day accounts and is writing to customers to advise them to switch to the Investment or Instant Saver accounts. Depositors can switch without notice or penalty and should lose no time in doing so; the old accounts will start to pay just 1 per cent gross interest from August 31.
