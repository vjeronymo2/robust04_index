931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / International Company News: Spain launches options contract Spain's MEFF fixed income exchange said it would start trading an options contract based on notional 10-year Treasury bond from January 11 1994, with monthly maturities, Reuter reports from Barcelona. The contract will give investors and fund managers a new instrument to cover interest rate risk. MEFF already trades an options contract on 10-year notional bond, but with quarterly maturities. In September volume rose to 1.78 contracts from 894,469 in August.
