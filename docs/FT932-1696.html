930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Danes join with Nasa in project Denmark is to take part in a DKr120m (Dollars 18.5m) scientific satellite project in co-operation with the US space agency, Nasa, in 1995, the Industry Ministry announced yesterday, Reuter reports from Copenhagen. The project involves the launch of a small Danish-built 50 kg satellite into orbit at a height of 800km to study the Earth's magnetic field.
