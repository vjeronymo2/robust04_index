930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / International Company News: Motorola advances on record sales MOTOROLA'S share price rose sharply yesterday on record fourth-quarter sales and earnings, driven by strong growth in the US electronics manufacturer's communications and semiconductor businesses, writes Louise Kehoe. The share price rose Dollars 10 7/8 to Dollars 123 1/4 , a 52-week high, at the close in New York. Net earnings for the quarter were Dollars 181m, or Dollars 1.35 a share, against Dollars 126m, or 96 cents, a year ago. Revenues were Dollars 3.71bn, up 22 per cent from Dollars 3.04bn in the corresponding period a year ago. Net earnings for the year were Dollars 453m, or Dollars 3.39, after a Dollars 123m charge for accounting changes related to retirement benefits, against Dollars 454m, or Dollars 3.44, last time. Sales rose 17 per cent to Dollars 13.3bn from Dollars 11.3bn in 1991.
