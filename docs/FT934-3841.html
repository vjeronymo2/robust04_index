931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Yates Brothers advances 13% Yates Brothers Wine Lodges, the independent drinks group, lifted pre-tax profits by 13 per cent, from Pounds 1.25m to Pounds 1.41m, in the six months to September 26. Turnover rose by 34 per cent to Pounds 22m, with sales buoyant across retail and wholesale activities. The interim dividend is increased from 0.78p to 1p, payable from earnings per share of 4.5p (3.8p).
