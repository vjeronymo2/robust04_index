BFN [Excerpt] Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov arrived in Jakarta this evening. The Turkmen president was welcomed by President Suharto in a state ceremony highlighted by a 21-gun salute at Merdeka Palace. After accompanying the state guest to the state guesthouse, Minister and State Secretary Murdiono told reporters that President Saparmurad Niyazov praised President Suharto for Indonesia's successful development. According to Minister Murdiono, the Turkmen president also praised Indonesia's chairmanship of the Nonaligned Movement. [passage omitted]
