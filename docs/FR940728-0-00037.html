EFFECTIVE DATE: July 28, 1994. Department of the Air Force 32 CFR Part 855 Use of United States Air Force Installations by Other Than United States Department of Defense Aircraft AGENCY: Department of the Air Force, DOD. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Department of the Air Force has added provisions for alternate use of Air Force airfields by commercial air carriers that have contracted to participate in the Civil Reserve Air Fleet (CRAF) program. It is necessary to identify the CRAF Alternate as a purpose for which the Air Force will grant civil aircraft landing permits. The availability of Air Force airfields for use as alternates by CRAF carriers is intended to encourage greater participation in the CRAF program, which is structured to meet military airlift requirements during military emergencies that involve US forces.
