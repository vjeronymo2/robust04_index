940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Veba - Other companies The energy-based conglomerate reports half-way figures on Thursday. Much favoured among investors, especially after a 35 per cent earnings increase in the first quarter, its stock has recently been towed upwards by rising interest in the German chemicals sector. Although far smaller than the national giants, its chemicals division has been radically restructured and is performing strongly.
