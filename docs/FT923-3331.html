920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / Finance & the Family: New account from A&L Alliance & Leicester building society is introducing a 90 day notice account called Bonus 90. The top rate will be 9.5 per cent for sums over Pounds 50,000; balances from Pounds 2,500 (the minimum) to Pounds 9,999 will earn 7.25 per cent.
