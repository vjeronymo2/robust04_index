BFN [Text] Vilnius, May 20, BNS -- The Lithuanian parliament on Thursday [19 May] passed the law on government which provides for 19 ministries instead of the present 16. The parliament voted 49-43 and 3 lawmakers abstained. Lithuania will have a new Administration Reforms and Municipal Affairs Ministry and an Environment Protection Ministry. The presently existing Ministry of Culture and Education will be divided into two separate institutions. The new law also provides for ministers without portfolio whose functions and authorizations will be defined by the prime minister.
