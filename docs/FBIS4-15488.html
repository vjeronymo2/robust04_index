BFN [Text] Moscow, April 5, RIA -- Speaking at the opening session of the talks between the official Tajik leaders and the opposition on the internal settlement, Russia's Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said Russia regards its assistance in promoting the settlement of the political crisis in Tajikistan both as moral duty and the effort which corresponds to Russia's national interests. In the opinion of the top Russian diplomat, the talks will gradually gain significance, and called on the negotiators to prevent an outbreak of a new conflict in Tajikistan. He noted that Russia will shape its relations with respect to both parties proceeding from the progress of the talks.
