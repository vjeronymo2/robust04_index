BFN [Excerpt] Beirut--A Lebanese military source announced that Israel has committed a new act of piracy against Lebanon. The source said an Israeli commando force, using two helicopters, kidnapped Hajj Mustafa al-Dirani, a Lebanese national resistance official, from his house in Qasr Naba at 0200 today after this force infiltrated from the area between Jabal Kasrawan and al-Biqa'.
