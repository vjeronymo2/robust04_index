Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn--German Chancellor Helmut Kohl last night received Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', who has begun an official visit to Germany. Al-Shar' relayed the greetings of His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad to the German chancellor, who, for his part, asked that his greetings and best wishes be communicated to his excellency the president. During the meeting, the talks dealt with means of promoting and expanding the ties of cooperation between Syria and Germany so that they may cover all possible areas. The German chancellor emphasized the German Government's desire to promote ties with Syria. He proposed setting a timetable to discuss all issues that could further promote bilateral ties in all fields. For his part, al-Shar' stressed the need for Germany to honor its commitments to the efforts to achieve just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East, and to support, within the context of the European Union, the implementation of the relevant UN resolutions. The German chancellor also underlined the basic role of Syria and President al-Asad in the region. He commended Syria's positive stands, emphasizing the need for Germany and the European Union to exercise their influence to advance the peace process in order to achieve comprehensive and durable peace in the region. The meeting was attended by (Schmidt Bau), minister of state at the German Chancellor's Office [as heard], and Sulayman Haddad, the Syrian ambassador in Bonn. Al-Shar' arrived in Bonn from Algiers yesterday afternoon.
