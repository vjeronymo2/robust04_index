930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ropner down to Pounds 1.6m but confident PRE-TAX profits for Ropner, the Darlington-based engineering, shipping and property group, fell from Pounds 1.72m to Pounds 1.63m in the first half of 1993. However that hid an improvement in all divisions which produced increased operating profits of Pounds 1.55m (Pounds 982,000). Investment income, less interest payable, fell from Pounds 733,000 to Pounds 82,000. Mr Jeremy Ropner, chairman, expected profits in the second half to be above those for the first. Turnover rose to Pounds 9.01m (Pounds 8.26m). Earnings per share were 4.2p (4.4p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 3.5p.
