Language: English Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Viktor Demidenko for TASS] [Text] Kiev February 25 TASS -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk conditioned his participation in presidential elections and said it is first necessary to elect a new parliament and determine the powers of the future president. "My decision on participation in early presidential elections depends on many factors", he told reporters on Friday. "First and foremost we have to elect a new parliament and appoint a new Cabinet of Ministers", he stressed and added that any candidate for the presidency "should know his future powers" before joining the election campaign. "At present they (the powers) have not been determined. If the new president is elected according to the acting constitution, the new parliament is sure to raise the question about the powers of the president, the Supreme Soviet and the government. The powers of local heads of administrations have not been determined either", he said.
