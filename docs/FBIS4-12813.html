BFN [Text] Islamabad, April 12, IRNA -- The visiting speaker of Iran's Majlis (parliament) 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri here Monday called for closer ties between the Iranian nation and people of other countries especially the Muslims. Addressing a group of Iranians residing here, the speaker warned against elements attempting to sow discord among Muslims and reminded them of their grave responsibility in foiling such conspiracies. Hojjat ol-Eslam Nateq-Nuri touched on the friendly Tehran-Islamabad ties and called on the Iranians residing in Pakistan to make efforts to further strengthen the ties. The Majlis speaker arrived here Sunday on a five-day official visit on the first leg of his three-nation tour which will also take him to Sri Lanka and Malaysia.
