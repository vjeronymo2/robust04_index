BFN [Unattributed article: "Comit Privatization, Final Act"] [Text] (ANSA) Milan, April 26 -- Leonello Adler, 73, was named the new chairman of Banca Commerciale Italiana [Comit] today as the curtain raised on the final act of the privatization of the big bank put on the block at the end of February. The new deputy chairman and managing director is Luigi Fausti, and Enrico Beneduce was picked as managing director at a meeting of the Banca Commerciale board. Earlier, former managing director Pietro Grandjacquet left his post and resigned [from] the boards of Banca Commerciale, Mediobanca and Banca Internazionale Lombarda. Banca Commerciale, known as Comit, went private February 28 when the government sold off its stake of 54 percent.
