941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / National Lottery could turn Post Office into art gallery Glasgow's main Post Office building could become a new national art gallery for Scotland. The Pounds 25m cost could be paid for out of National Lottery cash, and the gallery could open in 1999, the year of Glasgow's reign as the UK City of Architecture and Design. The possibility emerged when the National Galleries of Scotland yesterday set out options for a new gallery in Glasgow
