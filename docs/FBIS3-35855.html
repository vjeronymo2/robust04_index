Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Simferopol, 31 Jan -- The news conference by Yuriy Meshkov, elected on Sunday as first president of the Republic of Crimea, started with the reading of the Central Electoral Committee resolution on the election of the president of the Crimea. Yuriy Meshkov read out his own appeal to the Crimeans, in which he assured them that he was ready for creative work and cooperation with all political groups and representatives of all ethnic groups. The Crimea could and should become a bridge of friendship between Russia and the Ukraine, he stressed. Replying to journalists' questions, Yuriy Meshkov announced that with his election the question of Crimean statehood was decided once and for all, but the Crimea will not become a "hot spot" like Karabakh or the Dniester Region. As for the Black Sea Fleet, it should remain united and Russian.
