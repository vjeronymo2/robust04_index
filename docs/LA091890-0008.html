September 18, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A portion of Ocean Beach, closed along with Mission Beach by a sewage spill Friday, could open again by the middle of the week after more tests to measure the water's bacteria level.

Mission Beach has reopened, but Ocean Beach is still closed from the Mission Bay Channel to just south of the Ocean Beach Pier, said Sgt. Steve de Bry of the San Diego city lifeguard service.

The spill occurred when a pipe carrying sewage from the Point Loma treatment plant to Fiesta Island burst.
