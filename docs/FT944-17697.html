941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Berlusconi to press case against public prosecutor The Italian government decided to press a formal complaint against Saverio Borrelli, Milan's chief public prosecutor, over a newspaper interview on Wednesday that contained damaging allegations about prime minister Silvio Berlusconi and justice minister Alfredo Biondi. The move is a victory for the 'hawks' in the cabinet, who argue that the government must take tough action to curb the increasing political interference of the anti-corruption magistrates in Milan.
