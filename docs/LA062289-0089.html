June 22, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST: NORWALK;

$5,000 REWARD OFFERED IN DRIVE-BY KILLING OF YOUTH

The Norwalk City Council on Tuesday voted to offer a $5,000 reward for information leading to the arrest and conviction of the person who killed John Glenn High School football star Juan Enriquez in a drive-by shooting last month.

A Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department spokesman said Wednesday that no one was in custody in connection with the shooting.

Deputies suspect that gang members shot Enriquez, who was not a gang member, as he walked near his Norwalk home on May 9.
