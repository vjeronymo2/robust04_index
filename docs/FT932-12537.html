930428 FT 28 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: PM and Reynolds to meet for talks MR John Major and Mr Albert Reynolds, the Irish prime minister, are expected to meet in June to discuss political talks on Northern Ireland. The two are due to meet during the European Council meeting in Copenhagen. Separately, the Ulster Unionist party yesterday launched a manifesto for the May 19 local elections in the province that called for greater powers for local councillors.
