940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / BT database to be offered British Telecommunications' database of telephone directory information is likely to be made available to other telephone operators - at a price - as a result of an inquiry by Oftel, the telecommunications regulator. At present BT owns the computerised database of names and telephone numbers which is the basis of all directory inquiry services. With the growth in the number of telephone operators, Oftel has been concerned to establish a code of practice on the provision of directory information services.
