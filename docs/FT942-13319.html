940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Gateau maker halts production Receivers Arthur Andersen yesterday halted production at gateau maker Livera Foods in Stockton-on-Tees, Cleveland, with the loss of 100 jobs. This brings to almost 300 the number of jobs lost since the receivership in February. Dillons, the bookshop company, yesterday announced 185 job losses.
