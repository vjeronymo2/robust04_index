Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Donetsk Television Network in Russian at 1630 GMT on 9 December carries its regular "Novosti Donetchiny" program which contains an announcement about delegation of powers for management of state property to four oblast administrations starting 1 January 1994. The anchorwoman says that Presidential Representative Yu. Smirnov has signed an instruction to organize the implementation of President Kravchuk's decree of 26 November on delegation of powers to Dnepropetrovsk, Donetsk, Zaporozhye and Lugansk Oblast Administrations for management of state property. As was explained to executive committees, city Soviets of People's Deputies, district state administrations and oblast directorates and departments, this measure is aimed at the stabilization of the economy, introduction of market relations, further development of these regions and enhancement of efficiency of management of state property. During the period from January 1, 1994 to December 31, 1995, as an experiment, Donetsk State Administration will perform the functions of management of property of state enterprises, institutions and organizations.
