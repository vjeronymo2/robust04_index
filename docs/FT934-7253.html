931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Chester Water rises to over Pounds 1m mark For the six months ended September 30, Chester Waterworks, the water supplier, has moved ahead both turnover and profits, indicating that the company was 'continuing to make progress on its high level of performance to date.' Turnover amounted to Pounds 2.99m (Pounds 2.86m) while the pre-tax surplus was Pounds 1.07m against Pounds 1.04m. And the interim dividend is lifted from 75p to 85p from earnings per share of 316.9p (283p).
