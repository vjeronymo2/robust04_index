940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Quicks advances to Pounds 1.94m Quicks Group, the Manchester-based motor distributor, reported interim pre-tax profits ahead by 41 per cent at Pounds 1.94m, against Pounds 1.38m. Turnover for the six months to end-June improved 52 per cent from Pounds 98.5m to Pounds 150.3m, helped by the acquisition of Laidlaw in July last year. The result was struck after interest charges of Pounds 905,000 (Pounds 644,000). Earnings per share came out at 5.3p (5.2p), taking account of the Pounds 8.78m placing and open offer to fund the Laidlaw purchase. The interim dividend is raised from 2p to 2.25p.
