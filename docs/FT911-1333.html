910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / International Company News: Towles into the red Towles, the manufacturer of knitwear, hosiery and underwear, fell into the red in the year to February 28. From taxable profits of Pounds 97,000, the company declined to losses of Pounds 56,000 on turnover up at Pounds 15m (Pounds 14.72m). Losses per share were 1.53p against earnings of 3.28p and the dividend is halved to 0.25p.
