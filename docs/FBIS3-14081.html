Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An Israeli delegation -- headed by Major General Amnon Shahaq, deputy chief of staff, and Uri Savir, Foreign Ministry director general -- left for Cairo an hour ago for meetings with PLO officials. On the agenda will be security arrangements for the Palestinians in Hebron, and the resumption of the negotiations on Gaza and Jericho. This was reported by our correspondent Yo'el Nir. The delegation is due back in Israel tomorrow.
