Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Luxembourg/Dresden (ADN-lsc) [DDP/ADN's Saxony regional service] -- The European Investment Bank (EIB) in Luxembourg has made 80 million German marks available for the modernization and development of the gas supply network in eastern Saxony. The loan is to go to the Gasversorgung Sachsen-Ost GmbH (Gaso) [gas supply company] and is to help finance conversion from town gas to natural gas in the Dresden area, it was announced in Luxembourg 11 March by the EIB, which is responsible for long-term EU financial projects. Work is to include laying over 100 km of pipes. Of these, half will involve the replacement of old pipes. The other half will be for laying pipes connecting new domestic and industrial consumers.
