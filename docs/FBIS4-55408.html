BFN [Text] In keeping with the guidelines issued by Army General Rasim Delic, chief of the General Staff of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, and Major General Milivoj Petkovic, the commander of the Main Headquarters of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO], the joint chiefs of staff in Sarajevo, headed by Corps General Ante Roso and Brigadier [words indistinct] officially started working today, [word indistinct] the press center of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army. During the transitional period, until the federation is established, the joint chiefs of staff will have the task of setting up a unified armed force of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The work on setting up joint armed forces has been progressing in line with the existing military agreements and the political concept outlined by the Washington Agreement, as well as by the federal agreement signed in Vienna recently.
