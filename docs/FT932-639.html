930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Pay inflation MANUAL workers, white-collar staff and employees in the retail industry have won pay rises of three times the rate of inflation, Incomes Date Services, the pay analyst, has found. Two thirds of recent pay rises have been between 2 per cent and 3.9 per cent compared with 1.3 per cent inflation. One in seven rises has been more than 4 per cent.
