BFN [Text] Commander of the Chinese Air Force Cao Shuangming, who arrived in Russia for an official visit, flew out to Sochi on Monday for a two-day stay in the military sanatorium of Chemitokvadzhe, located in the village of Lazarevskoye. According to military sources, the Chinese delegation,led by Cao Shuangming, showed great interest in MIG-29 fighters. At the end of last week, the Chinese representatives could see Russian MIG and SU-27 fighters and SU-25 assault planes in the Kubinka air field not far from Moscow. Upon his return from Sochi on June 8, the Chinese military official is planning to visit the Sukhoi design bureau, the Gagarin military aviation academy and the military aviation school in Volgograd. A visit to St.Petersburg is also on the program of his 10-day visit. Cao Shuangming arrived in Moscow on June 2 on the invitation of the Russian air force command.
