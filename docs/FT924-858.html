921222 FT 22 DEC 92 / Paris acts on jobless pay The French government yesterday agreed to help bail out the Unedic unemployment pay system, run jointly by employers and unions, from its impending bankruptcy, writes David Buchan in Paris. After employers and unions accused the state of shirking its responsibilities, the government agreed to shoulder more of the cost of early retirements and of dole for people coming off temporary state-run employment schemes, and to subsidise the interest rate on Unedic's borrowings.
