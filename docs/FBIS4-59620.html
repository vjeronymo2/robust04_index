BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 16 May (MENA) -- MENA has learned that the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations scheduled to be held in Cairo tomorrow, Tuesday, have been postponed until after the 'Id al-Adha holiday. Diplomatic sources in Cairo said the talks were postponed because of the Muslims' celebration of 'Id al-Adha next week, which precludes continuing the talks. [passage omitted]
