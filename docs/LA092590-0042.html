September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A former Tiger Stadium locker-room attendant struck out with a scheme to sell Dave Winfield's bats, Lance Parrish's jerseys and other baseball items as souvenirs.

Charles Cox, 17, was arraigned in 36th District Court in Detroit on a larceny charge after being apprehended.

The scheme came to light when Parrish asked for his backup jersey Aug. 22 during a game between the Tigers and the Angels. When the Angels' equipment manager went to the locker room to get it, he found that Parrish's and Winfield's equipment bags had been raided, court documents said.
