Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Jan (MENA)-- A noncommissioned officer, Muhammad Darwish, of al-Minya State Security Detective Bureau sustained bullet wounds to the chest, thigh, and the right foot when unidentified gunmen fired at him today as he was on his way to work on al-Ashraf Street in Mallawi. This was stated by an official source in the Interior Ministry, who said that Darwish was taken to hospital for treatment. Key figures in the al-Minya Security Directorate and the Public and State Security departments and experts from the various detective departments and the criminology laboratory headed for the scene of the incident to examine it. Meanwhile, the police are making intense efforts to apprehend the culprits and the incident has been reported to the prosecutor's office to start investigations.
