BFN [Text] Baghdad, 24 Apr (INA) -- The National Assembly held a session today under the chairmanship of Deputy Speaker Ghanim 'Aziz Khadduri. At the beginning of the session, the people's representatives discussed a report, which the Assembly presented to the annual conference of the Arab-European parliamentary dialogue held in Paris from 14 to 17 April. Sixteen Arab parliaments and 13 European parliaments, as well as representatives of the European Parliament, the Arab League, and the European Union also attended. Assembly members Bashir Hammud al-Ghazali and 'Abd-al-Karim Jihad who participated in the conference said that participants stressed the need to implement Security Council resolutions calling for the achievement of a comprehensive and just peace in the region. Regarding the imperialist-Zionist conspiracy that is targeting Iraq, they said that participants also acknowledged the suffering of the Iraqi people as a result of the continued unjust blockade. They also expressed their hope that the implementation of Security Council resolutions would lead to the lifting of the blockade. The assembly then elected a permanent committee for environment. 'Abd-al-Karim Jihad was elected as chairman of the committee, Raja' al-Shawi as deputy chairman, and 'Abd-al-Majid Khunayjir as rapporteur.
