Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 11 Feb -- The head of the Russian Government has issued an instruction that the state's debts to the coalminers should be settled immediately. Viktor Chernomyrdin issued this directive to the Russian Federation Finance Ministry after this morning's meeting with coal-industry managers and trade-union leaders. Viktor Chernomyrdin also proposed that a study should be made of the idea of gradually paying off the debts to the coalminers by means of bills of exchange that would be freely accepted for encashment by the Russian Central Bank.
