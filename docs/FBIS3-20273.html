Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] According to Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana, Spain supports Malta's EU [European Union] membership bid and urges ending the dialogue phase in order to start the formal negotiations. Javier Solana made a short visit to Malta on Monday [28 March] where he met President Vincent Censu, the prime minister, and the foreign minister. Solana believes that one should work in the areas of politics, economics, and the institutions to find ways that will allow this country to join the EU. According to Solana, the discussions of these three points essential for a rapid accession are progressing well, and the dialogue that started over the last few years is about to come to a conclusion. Malta applied for EU membership in July 1990.
