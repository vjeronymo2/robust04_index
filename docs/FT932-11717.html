930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / UK Company News: PCT shows 20% increase to Pounds 1.4m Pre-tax profits of PCT Group showed a 20 per cent improvement, from Pounds 1.17m to Pounds 1.4m, in the year to end-December. Turnover grew from Pounds 17.9m to Pounds 20.5m. The USM-quoted group, which has interests in the marketing, hire and development of power tools and lifting and welding equipment, said the advance had been achieved against a background of continuing economic uncertainty. A final dividend of 4.75p (4.5p) makes a 7.25p (7p) total.
