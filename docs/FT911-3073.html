910426 FT 26 APR 91 / UK Company News: Net asset value up to 461p at Keystone The net asset value per share of Keystone Investment Company stood at 461p at the half-year ended March 31 1991. This is compared with 450p a year earlier and 382.01p at the September 30 year-end. Earnings per share came out at 6.12p (7.15p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 4p.
