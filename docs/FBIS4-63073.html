BFN [Report by BELINFORM reporter Viktar Kotaw: "Russian Pilots Remain in Belarus"] [Text] The fate of the Russian Air Force regiment, stationed in Zyabrawtsy near Homel, has been determined. Pilots from the neighboring state will remain in Belarus until the year 2000. At this point, the arrangement is said to satisfy both sides because following the withdrawal of some of the Russian troops from this oblast, local authorities found themselves faced with the problem of efficient use and maintenance of military settlements.
