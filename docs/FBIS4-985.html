BFN [Text] Four accords concerning the creation of the new Mozambique Defense Armed Forces, FADM, were signed in Maputo yesterday by General Tobias Dai, on behalf of the government, and General Edmundo de Morais, of the Mozambique National Resistance. Those documents dealt with hierarchical structure, general organization, and the organization and training of the battalion of special forces and of air force personnel. Aldo Ajello, head of the UN Operations in Mozambique, said that the signing of those documents is a step forward toward pacifying the country.
