BFN [Text] The talks held in Oman between Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid have failed to resolve the differences between them, Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs, stated today at the end of the second round of talks between the two leaders. Oman has hosted the talks between Salih and al-Bid as part of the Arab efforts to resolve the Yemeni crisis.
