920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Craton Lodge Craton Lodge & Knight Group incurred a loss of Pounds 431,000 in the year ended September 30 1991, against Pounds 235,000 previously. The screen printing business sold recently contributed Pounds 547,000 of loss, while the remaining businesses achieved a profit before interest and central costs of Pounds 301,000. The USM-quoted group, which is changing its name to Princedale Group, now comprises product development consultancies, a design agency and sales promotion consultancy. Turnover rose to Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 3.65m). Losses were 0.11p (0.2p). There was an extraordinary deficit of Pounds 1.05m on disposal of the print business.
