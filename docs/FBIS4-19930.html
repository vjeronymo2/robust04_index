BFN [Text] Dortmund (DPA) -- FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) hopes that the NATO ultimatum to the Bosnian Serbs will relieve Gorazde and the other Muslim enclaves in Bosnia of the "murderous stranglehold." "The community of states had to act," said Kinkel, referring to the NATO threat of air strikes against Serbian positions at the FDP land party congress in Dortmund on Saturday [23 April]. The FRG Government supports NATO's decisions, he said.
