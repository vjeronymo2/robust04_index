August 19, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

San Diego Police homicide detectives are investigating the death of an unidentified man found in La Jolla about 7 a.m. Saturday morning.

A jogger running north on Via Capri found the body in a dirt lot at the intersection of Via Siena, said Lt. Dan Berglund.

The Caucasian male is estimated to be about 35, has reddish-brown hair, a brown mustache and false teeth. He was wearing a white T-shirt, aqua sweat pants and white high-top shoes, police said.

There were no obvious signs of trauma on the body, which appeared to have been dumped there, Berglund said.

Cause of death is unknown, and an autopsy is pending.
