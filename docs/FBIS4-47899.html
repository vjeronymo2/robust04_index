BFN [Excerpts] In Burundi Leonard Nyangoma, the minister of the interior and security, has been fired [declare demissionaire]. [passage omitted] After the prime minister announced the dismissal of Mr. Nyangoma, the Front for Democracy in Burundi [Frodebu] recalled that its view is that, by virtue of the constitution, the head of government is no longer in office. Frodebu is therefore saying that the decisions of the prime minister are null and void.
