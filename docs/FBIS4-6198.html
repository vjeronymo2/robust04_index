BFN [Report by Yi Tong-sik from Beijing] [Text] A Beijing-based source said that North Korea had sent a special task force to Beijing during President Kim Yong-sam's visit to China. The task force was made up of responsible North Korean high-level working officials from various sectors -- such as politics, international affairs, and economics -- and arrived in Beijing two days before President Kim Yong-sam's visit and collected information regarding the contents of the ROK-PRC talks, including the summit talks between Presidents Kim Yong-sam and Jiang Zemin, and the future development of their talks. The source stated that through the task force, in particular, North Korea prudently focused on China's position regarding the nuclear issue and observed how the ROK-PRC talks could affect the United States or the United Nations.
