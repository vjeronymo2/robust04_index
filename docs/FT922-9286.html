920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / International Company News: Chiron wins FDA approval for cancer drug CHIRON, the US biotechnology company, has received approval from the US Food and Drug Administration to market Interleukin-2, a long-awaited treatment for aggressive kidney cancer, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. The approval marks the end of an eight-year struggle to bring to the US market what had widely been regarded as one of the most promising products of the US biotechnology industry. Interleukin-2 will be sold under the brand name Proleukin.
