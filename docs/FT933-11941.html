930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Specialeyes losses cut to Pounds 333,000 SPECIALEYES, the USM-quoted retail optician, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 333,000 for the 24 weeks to May 15. That compared with a loss of Pounds 696,000 for the 28 weeks to May 30 1992 and a forecast of Pounds 350,000 in May's placing of unsecured loan stock. Turnover was Pounds 9.49m (Pounds 9.09m). Losses per share were 2.18p (3.51p). Mr James Power, chairman, said that the Pounds 1.5m loan stock issue had been completed providing the resources to continue the rationalisation begun at the start of this year.
