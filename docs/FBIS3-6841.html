Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 22 KYODO -- The government agreed Tuesday [22 March] to draw up a provisional draft budget for the first 50 days of the new fiscal year starting April 1, government sources said. The agreement was reached in a high-level meeting of coalition parties and the government following a proposal by Finance Minister Hirohisa Fujii. The provisional budget, covering the period to May 20, will amount to 11 trillion yen, the biggest ever, covering personnel expenditure and public works programs, the sources said.
