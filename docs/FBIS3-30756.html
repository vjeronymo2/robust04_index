Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Slovak Republic today issued a statement on the criminal attack on the Sarajevo market. In its statement, the ministry strongly denounces such deliberate attacks, whichever of the fighting sides is their originator. At the same time, the Foreign Ministry appeals to the UN secretary general and the UN Security Council to adopt more radical measures to speed up the solution to the situation on the territory of Bosnia-Herzegovina, which is becoming unbearable both politically and from the humanitarian point of view. The Slovak Republic will continue to assist in the search for a peaceful solution to the war conflict on the entire territory of the former Yugoslavia in accordance with the extent of its possibilities, says the statement issued by the Foreign Ministry.
