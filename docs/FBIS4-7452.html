BFN [Text] General Shalikashvili, chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff of the U.S. Armed Forces, arrived this evening in Skopje for a one-day visit to The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM]. Gen. Shalikashvili, who arrived here from Sarajevo, is to be received tomorrow by Macedonian President Kiro Gligorov and by Stevo Crvenkovski and Vlado Popovski, ministers for foreign affairs and defense, respectively. During the course of his visit, Gen. Shalikashvili is to pay a visit to the U.S. Blue Helmets and is to have talks with Norwegian Gen. Tringve Telefsen, UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] commander for Macedonia.
