941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / Confidence dips over economy Consumer confidence about the economy dipped slightly in October, says a Gallup survey conducted on behalf of the European Commission. Thirty-two per cent of those polled expect the economic situation to deteriorate over the next 12 months, compared with 25 per cent who expect it to improve. The negative balance of 7 percentage points compares with a negative balance of 3 in September.
