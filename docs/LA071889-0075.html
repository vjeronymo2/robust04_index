July 18, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

GALLERY PARTY TO BENEFIT PACIFIC THEATRE ENSEMBLE

A benefit "Hats Off to PTE" party to raise funds for Pacific Theatre Ensemble's summer production of Shakespeare's "The Merry Wives of Windsor" will be held at the Saxon-Lee Gallery on Saturday at 8 p.m. The Santa Monica Arts Commission has awarded the Ensemble $15,000 toward the production, which will be presented on the Santa Monica Pier from Aug. 18 to Sept. 10.

The party includes live entertainment and refreshments. Tickets are $25 per person; $45 per couple.

Information: (818) 761-5368.
