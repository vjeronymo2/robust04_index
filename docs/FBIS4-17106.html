BFN [Text] Russia will put forward a new proposal on division of the Black Sea Fleet in accordance with which the strategic ships (anti-submarine ships and warships) will belong to Russia and the tactical ships (the patrol ones and minesweepers) to Ukraine. This was announced to Interfax by reliable sources in the Black Sea Fleet headquarters. The same sources said the talks between Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev and Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy in Sevastopol planned for Thursday would focus on division of the Black Sea Fleet.
