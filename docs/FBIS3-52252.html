Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Reports from Irbil say that six clergymen have been killed or executed by the gang of the agent Jalal Talabani and that many mosques have been severely damaged as a result of artillery shelling of al-Sulaymaniyah and Irbil cities, and the Chamchamal, Darbandikhan, Kallar, and Raniyah districts. It is reported that more than 200 casualties fell in Kallar. Reports earlier said that the security situation was still tense in Irbil and al-Sulaymaniyah and that most shops are closed and life paralyzed. Sound of explosions and guns are heard on the outskirts of Irbil and al-Sulaymaniyah. All roads connecting towns, districts, and townships have been closed by warring gangs. Hospitals are full of dead and wounded with no medical attention. People do not have the means to obtain food. Reports from al-Sulamaniyah say that the gang of agent Mas'ud Barzani and the gang of agent Jalal Talabani are massing their saboteurs in and around al-Sulaymaniyah to begin a new round of fighting. This time the two sides will fight directly to heighten the tragedy which they created in the region.
