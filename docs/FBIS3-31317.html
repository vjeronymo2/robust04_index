Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Protocols on an agreement concerning the completion of the construction of the mining and processing combine enterprise at Dolinskaya in Krivoy Rog was signed in Poprad today by Jan Ducky, Slovak minister of economy, and Hrihoriy Ousalenko, deputy Ukrainian minister of industry. The documents update previous agreements and protocols signed by the Governments of the former USSR and the former CSSR. The East Slovak Ironworks has undertaken to complete the combine enterprise. About 1,500 Slovak workers will take part in the construction and assembly work at the Dolinskaya enterprise.
