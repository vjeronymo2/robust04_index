BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon, Ruan Chongwu, provincial party committee secretary and governor, met in China Hainan Reform and Development Research Institute with a 12-member Hong Kong Trade Development Council [TDC] delegation headed by Francis Lo, Hong Kong TDC executive director, and Mary Wong, Hong Kong TDC assistant executive director. During the meeting, Ruan Chongwu briefed the Hong Kong guests on Hainan's investment environment, preferential taxation policies, and convenient and efficient enterprise registration process. The Hong Kong TDC delegation expressed profound interest in Hainan's development prospects. Over the next few days, the Hong Kong TDC delegation will hold separate talks with Hainan's economic cooperation, commercial and trade, and financial and banking departments on a number of cooperation projects. Persons in charge of the provincial economic cooperation department, commercial and trade department, foreign affairs office, and other departments concerned were also present during the meeting.
