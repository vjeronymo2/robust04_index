BFN [Excerpt] Willy Claes, Belgian deputy prime minister and foreign minister, arrived this afternoon in Kuwait on a two-day official visit. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir, first deputy prime minister and foreign minister, a number of Foreign Ministry officials, and the Belgian ambassador to Kuwait met the visiting minister. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir held talks this afternoon with Willy Claes on bilateral ties and the latest developments in the international and Arab spheres. [passage omitted]
