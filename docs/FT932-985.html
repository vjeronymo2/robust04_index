930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Faupel Trading Pounds 2m placing Faupel Trading Group, the USM-quoted textile importer, is raising Pounds 2.05m in a placing and open offer. The group also announced a fall from Pounds 1.21m to Pounds 825,000 in pre-tax profits for the year to March 31. Turnover moved ahead 19 per cent, however, to Pounds 31.4m. Earnings per share fell to 6.42p (10.22p) and a final dividend of 3.05p makes a maintained total of 4.9p. The placing is of 3.55m new ordinary 5p shares at 64p each.
