BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 23 -- The first section of the trans- Vietnam 500 kv [kilovolt] power transmission line was put into official operation on May 21. The section covers a distance of 350 kilometers linking the Hoa Binh Hydro-Electric Power Plant in the north to Ha Tinh Province in central Vietnam. Specialists said that the voltage measured at the Ha Tinh Station was 400 kv, and all equipment and line of the first section met all required technical specifications. The Ministry of Energy and the agencies concerned continued conducting tests and adjusting equipment in order to extent the operational line to Danang and Pleiku (central highlands) soon.
