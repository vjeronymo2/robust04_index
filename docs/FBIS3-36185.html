Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Excerpt] Moscow, 3 February -- President of the Russian Federation Boris Yeltsin has left Moscow on an official visit to Georgia early this morning. The program of his one-day visit is going to be very busy. Immediately after his arrival in the Georgian capital and the greeting ceremony at the airport Yeltsin will have one-to-one talks with Eduard Shevardnadze. After that, the Russian-Georgian talks will continue with an extended number of participants. [passage omitted]
