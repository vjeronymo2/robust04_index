940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Far Eastern net assets ahead Fleming Far Eastern Investment Trust reported net assets per share up 35 per cent, from 291.7p to 393p, at end-March. Net revenue for the year increased from Pounds 1.77m to Pounds 2.32m, equivalent to earnings per share of 1.53p (1.17p). The dividend is stepped up from 1.1p to 1.5p.
