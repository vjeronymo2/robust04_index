930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Plantsbrook placing and acquisitions Plantsbrook Group, the funeral services provider, has bought two businesses for Pounds 990,000 cash and is at an advanced stage of negotiation to acquire a third. The completed purchases are both funeral businesses - one in north-west London, the other in Lancashire. The company also announced that it was raising some Pounds 2m via the placing of 2,99m new ordinary 10p shares at 67p each.
