BFN [Text] Beijing, May 12 (XINHUA) -- The remark made by the U.S. Assistant Secretary Winston Lord at the Senate Subcommittee of Foreign Relations is totally groundless, said a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman here today. At a weekly press conference here this afternoon, Spokesman Wu Jianmin made the statement when asked to comment on a report that on May 4, Lord indicated at the Senate Subcommittee of Foreign Relations that China in the past possibly aided the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's ballistic missile program, and surely helped its nuclear program. Wu said "such a remark is totally groundless and entirely irresponsible".
