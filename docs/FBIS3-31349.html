Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, Feb 17 (AFP) -- Russian special envoy Vitaliy Churkin is to have talks with Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic on Thursday, the Bosnian Serb news agency SRNA announced. The meeting has been scheduled for after noon (1100 GMT) at the Bosnian Serb stronghold of Pale, east of Sarajevo. Churkin has been on a special mission for Russian President Boris Yeltsin since Saturday to mediate in the Bosnian conflict. He met twice with Serbia's ultranationalist President Slobodan Milosevic. A third meeting was scheduled for later Thursday.
