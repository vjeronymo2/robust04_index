920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Inv Trust asset value declines Net asset value per share of the Scottish Investment Trust stood at 190.6p at the end of the nine months to July 31. This represented an 8.5 per cent fall from the 208.3p reported at the interim stage and a 6.5 per cent decline from the 203.8p of a year earlier.
