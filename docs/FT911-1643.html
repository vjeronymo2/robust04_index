910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / Relief operation begins for Bangladesh BANGLADESH was pledged millions of dollars in aid from foreign governments and relief organisations yesterday to help victims of the cyclone which struck the country on Monday. The resort district of Cox's Bazar (pictured) is just one of the areas in ruins and still under flood waters. The death-toll has risen to 37,000 and could climb to more than 100,000, relief officials in Dhaka said. At least 20,000 people are still missing and more than 10m people have lost their homes. Britain has pledged immediate aid of Pounds 2.5m and the European Community will give Ecu 10m (Pounds 7m). Millions of dollars of aid pledged Page 4
