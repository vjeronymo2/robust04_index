BFN [Excerpts] Wuensdorf (DDP/ADN) -- The supreme command of the Western Group of Russian Troops (WGT) in Wuensdorf, Brandenburg, officially bade farewell to Germany with a troop parade today. After the parade, which included military hardware and around 1,000 soldiers, WGT Commander in Chief Colonel General Matvey Burlakov and Prime Minister of Brandenburg Manfred Stolpe spoke in favor of completing the final phase of troop withdrawal in a dignified way and of cultivating friendly contacts in future. Stolpe assured the returnees further support in providing housing in Russia and training for civilian jobs. He pointed out that the Western Group had so far carried out the return of troops successfully and without major incidents. [passage omitted] The official date for the final withdrawal of the Russian troops from Germany is 31 August.
