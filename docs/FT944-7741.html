941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cosalt maintains recovery An improvement from its four main divisions enabled Cosalt to maintain its recovery with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.58m for the year ended August 28, against a Pounds 232,000 loss previously. Profits from continuing activities nearly doubled to Pounds 3.59m (Pounds 1.86m) and included fibres Pounds 845,000 (Pounds 426,000); safety and protection Pounds 769,000 (Pounds 563,000); holiday homes Pounds 1.64m (Pounds 649,000) and workwear Pounds 684,000 (Pounds 295,000). Discontinued activities incurred losses of Pounds 10,000 (Pounds 673,000) on turnover of Pounds 590,000 (Pounds 8.27m) included in a total figure of Pounds 68.4m (Pounds 69.2m). Earnings per share were 14.17p (2.72p losses) and the final dividend is 5p for a total of 8p (5.375p). The shares rose 19p to 154p yesterday.
