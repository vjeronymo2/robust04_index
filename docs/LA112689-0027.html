November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A CHINA WATCHER

So Orville Schell now realizes that he has been somewhat mistaken about the government of China ("The Re-Education of a China Watcher," by Jacques Leslie, Oct. 15). Hasn't he ever heard of the large-scale executions that took place there in the 1950s? What about the ongoing genocide against the Tibetans that has recently intensified?

May we be protected from these naive, starry-eyed "China experts." Their misinformation is partly responsible for the continual misery of the Chinese people and our own befuddlement and inaction over events in China.

RICK GARCIA

El Monte
