BFN [Unattributed untitled report] [Text] According to the investigation's theory, the attempt on the life of Moscow City Duma Deputy Nikolay Moskovchenko was made by persons from the Tajik antigovernment opposition, informed sources in the Moscow prosecutor's office have disclosed. According to a source, the suspects were in Moscow as part of a criminal gang connected with the drugs and arms trade. Composite pictures of some of the gang members suspected of the crime have been compiled, and a Russia-wide hunt for them is under way. "We know who we are looking for, but it will not be easy to catch them because they are migrant elements, and by now they may already be in Afghanistan," the source pointed out.
