931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: NatWest Bancorp net income ahead NatWest Bancorp, the US retail banking subsidiary of National Westminster Bank, reported net income up 82 per cent to Dollars 71.5m (Pounds 47.3m) for the third quarter to September 30. The outcome increased net income for the first nine months of the year from Dollars 105.1m to Dollars 202m. Provisions for loan losses were Dollars 20m (Dollars 30.5m) for the quarter.
