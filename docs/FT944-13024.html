941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Overseas Inv Trust Overseas Investment Trust, managed by Morgan Grenfell to seek longer term capital growth, reported a net asset value as at September 30 of 417.5p per share, a gain of 23 per cent over the year. Net revenue dipped to Pounds 1.37m (Pounds 1.54m) reflecting higher administrative expenses and a changed investment mix. Earnings per share were 3.59p, down from 4.04p, but the proposed final dividend is raised to 2.45p lifting the total to 3.3p, an increase of 4.8 per cent.
