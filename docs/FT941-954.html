940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Airline to serve new destinations American Airlines, the US carrier, is to operate daily non-stop flights from Gatwick to Nashville, Tennessee, and to Raleigh/Durham in North Carolina from May 27 for the first time. It is also starting daily non-stop services from Heathrow to Philadelphia from May 27, and from tomorrow will operate six flights a day from Heathrow to New York's John F Kennedy airport. Its 10am flight to New York will be non-smoking from May 2.
