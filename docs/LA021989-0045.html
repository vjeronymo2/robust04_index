February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE AIR FARE CONTROVERSY

Regarding "Shopping for Emergency Air Fares" (by Lynn Simross, Jan. 26):

I am writing to thank Simross on behalf of my sister and brother-in-law. Last Wednesday, my brother-in-law, Mike, was notified of the death of his father in Michigan. On Thursday morning Simross' article was in the View section.

After trying two airlines with no success, he called American Airlines and the agent agreed to give them the discount air fare -- saving them approximately $1,000.

To have to face this unexpected and unplanned-for expense when you are so upset just adds to your distress. So your information helped to ease the burden a little by saving them a considerable amount of money.

SANDRA KLINE

Lancaster
