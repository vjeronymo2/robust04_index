941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Cox Insurance Cox, a Lloyd's agency, and Raphael Zorn Hemsley, the stockbrokers, have raised Pounds 12m for a new company to invest in the insurance market next year. The launch of Cox Insurance Holdings means the total extra corporate investment in Lloyd's this year may now exceed Pounds 350m. This is in addition to the Pounds 900m invested last year. The new company hopes to be listed on the stock exchange next year.
