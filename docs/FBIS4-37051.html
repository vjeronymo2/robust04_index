BFN [Text] It has been officially announced in Rabat that Morocco has again rejected any OAU participation in carrying out the self-determination referendum in the Western Sahara, which the United Nations has set for the end of 1994. This confirmation came after the Moroccan Government received a message from the UN secretary general in which he informed Morocco of the appointment of two OAU representatives charged with the task of monitoring the work of the voter identification commission for the people invited to participate in the elections.
