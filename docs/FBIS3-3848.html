Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, March 23 (XINHUA) -- This, China's largest economic center is employing advanced auditing methods to supervise its rapidly growing economy. According to Deputy Mayor Meng Jianzhu, 84.2 percent of the government's financial activities came under the supervision of auditing departments last year, in comparison with 71.4 percent in 1992. He praised the auditing departments for playing an important role in economizing on capital and improving work efficiency in urban construction last year. Shanghai is China's leading industrial center with many key state enterprises. To help these enterprises to adopt a modern enterprise system as quickly as possible, the auditing departments audited ten joint- stock companies, 82 partnership enterprises and three nationwide exchanges of goods and materials last year. The auditing departments also completed checks on 22 items of loans granted by international banking organizations and put forward proposals on making efficient use of the loans to the municipal government last year.
