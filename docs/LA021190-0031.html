February 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE TO LATINOS AT MT. SAC

Latino college students with at least 45 units and a grade-point average of 2.5 or better can apply at Mt.

San Antonio College for the 1990 Assn. of Hispanic Professionals for Education Allstate scholarship.

The deadline is Friday. For information, call the Mt. SAC transfer center director at (714) 594-5611.
