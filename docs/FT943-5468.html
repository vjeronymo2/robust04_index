940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Golds led Johannesburg higher in spite of a late dip in the bullion price, the sector index rising 71, or 3 percent to 2,402 on speculative buying ahead of any easing in foreign exchange controls. Industrials rose 39 to 6,586 and the overall index by 47 to 5,916.
