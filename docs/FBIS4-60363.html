BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says Yasir 'Arafat must reaffirm his commitment to all the components and appendixes of the Israeli-PLO Declaration of Principles. In addition, 'Arafat must reiterate his opposition to the use of force or violence in the relations with Israel. Thirdly, 'Arafat must reiterate his pledge to resolve conflicts peaceably. Shim'on Peres says 'Arafat made a declaration in this spirit during their meeting in Oslo some 10 days ago, in the wake of the public disclosure of the first passage of the Johannesburg tape. Shim'on Peres says 'Arafat must make all these pronouncements for all the world to hear, and not only in a talk between politicians. Mr. Peres made these remarks after his meeting with the UN secretary general in New York.
