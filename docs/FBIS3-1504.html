Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 29 SAPA -- At least 53 people died as a result of violence that flared in Johannesburg on Monday [28 March], police said on Tuesday. A police statement said an investigation into the killings had begun under the leadership of Brig Louis van der Westhuizen. The police said permission for the Zulu meeting had been applied for and granted; the meeting had been peaceful until shooting started; chaos and violence followed when "groups of persons" at the meeting proceeded into different parts of the city; and one of these groups proceeded to African National Congress headquarters at Shell House. "Shooting with automatic weapons from within Shell House left eight persons dead and 20 persons injured," the statement said. "No automatic firearms were found among the dead or injured outside Shell House. Two handguns, one of which was homemade, were found among these persons."
