920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Jos net asset value shows improvement Net asset value per share of Jos Holdings, an investment trust, improved to 177.6p over the half-year to end-January against 150.9p a year earlier, but was lower than the 180.0p at the July 1991 year-end. Investment income for the six months was down slightly from Pounds 273,666 to Pounds 268,221 but other income fell to Pounds 45,134 (Pounds 93,132). Net income emerged at Pounds 185,812 (Pounds 224,793). Earnings were 2.01p against 2.43p but the interim dividend is lifted to 1.75p (1.125p).
