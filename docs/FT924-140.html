921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Usher-Walker acceptances The recommended offer by Sun Chemical Corporation for Usher-Walker has been accepted in respect of 7m ordinary shares, representing nearly 80 per cent of the capital. The offer for the preference shares has been taken up as to 99 per cent of the capital. All the offers have been extended until January 13. Payment of the preference dividend of 1.75p due on December 31 is deferred until March 1.
