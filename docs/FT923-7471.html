920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: US Smaller Cos income boosted At the end of June 1992, net asset value of US Smaller Companies Investment Trust was 93.9p. For its initial 11 months trading the final dividend is 0.1p which makes a total of 0.3p. Net revenue for the period came to Pounds 114,000 for earnings per share of 0.54p. The level of income was greater than would be expected to be distributable in the future, because of the high amount earned on cash held prior to investment in US equities.
