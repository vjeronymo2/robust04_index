August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BREAKING UP THE KINGS NOT A SOUND DECISION

I can't believe the Kings are breaking up the best announcing team in Los Angeles. By demoting Nick Nickson to radio only and replacing him with former player and rookie broadcaster Jim Fox, King fans come up big losers. With Bob Miller and Nick you were always sure to get an accurate and honest call of the game, with no cheerleading.

BILL NOYES

Diamond Bar
