920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Pears are this week's best fruit buy with English Conference at 35-50p a lb and English Comice at 45-60p a lb, reports the FFVIB. Oranges remain a good buy at 12-35p each as are kiwifruit at 15-20p each. Cape Thompson and sultana grapes at Pounds 1.00-1.40 are plentiful. Egyptian new potatoes at 25-30p a lb are a good veg buy as are English and Dutch onions at 18-20 a lb. Various mushrooms are plentiful with cups 60-90p a 1/2 lb and buttons 75p-Pounds 1.00 a 1/2 lb. Tomatoes are reasonably priced this week at 50-80p a lb, along with celery at 55-65p a head and Iceberg lettuce at 50-80p a head.
