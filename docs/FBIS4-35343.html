BFN [Text] The sons of our Yemeni people continue to reject the destructive war which the Sanaa regime insists on continuing to drive (?large numbers) of youth people into the holocaust of death and destruction. Reports confirm that many citizens have begun to refuse to send off their sons to the battlefronts to fight their brothers in the Democratic Republic of Yemen. The regime's organs in Sanaa have resorted to brutal and repressive methods such as those used by the military police, which subjects those refusing their orders to threats and [words indistinct]. In this fashion, the authorities killed the sons of a shaykh in al-Hudaydah Governorate as a way of terrorizing the citizens and forcing them to obey the orders of the repressive authorities in Sanaa.
