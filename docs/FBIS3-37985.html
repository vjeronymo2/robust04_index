Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Fedoruk] [Text] Beijing February 22 TASS -- China's intention to base relations of friendship and goodneighbourliness with Turkmenistan on the five principles of peaceful co-existence was expressed by Qiao Shi, chairman of the standing committee of China's National People's Congress, during a meeting with Turkmen Mejlis Chief Sakhat Muradov, who is currently on an official visit here. The Chinese parliament chief emphasised the need to broaden friendly cooperation between the two countries in politics, economics, science, technology, and culture. Qiao pointed out a considerable strengtheing of contacts with foreign countries over the past 15 years, RENMIN RIBAO reported on Tuesday. This, he said, was resultant of an active realisation of reforms and the open-door policy. The Chinese parliament chief said China cannot do without drawing on the experience of foreign countries in the development of democracy and economics. "However, one should not copy foreign models. One should act in line with the country's current realities," Qiao said. Muradov said Turkmenistan attaches much importance to the development of contacts with China, whose experience may prove useful for the development of the Turkmen economy, RENMIN RIBAO reported.
