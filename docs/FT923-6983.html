920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / Bank to fund wind farm BARCLAYS Bank will fund 80 per cent of an Pounds 11.5m wind farm at Coal Clough, near Burnley in Lancashire, with a six-year loan. The rest will come from South Western Electricity, Manweb and Renewable Energy Systems. The farm will be capable of supplying 7,500 homes with electricity.
