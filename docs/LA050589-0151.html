May 5, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: CENTERPOINTE LA PALMA

Centerpointe La Palma is a 42-acre, $100-million, mixed-use office park at Orangethorpe Avenue and Valley View Street in La Palma. When the third phase is completed at the end of the year, the complex will include 11 office buildings, ranging from single-story to seven stories, a 162-room Day's Inn hotel, four restaurants, 34,000 square feet of retail space and a three-level parking garage.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: 1 Centerpointe Drive, La Palma

Developer: Birtcher

Architect: Ware & Malcomb Architects, Irvine

Primary Contractor: Birtcher Construction

Financing: Joint venture of the Mutual Life Insurance Co. of New York Real Estate Investments and Birtcher

Square feet: 375,000 office; 256,000 R&D

Use: Office, R&D, retail, restaurant, hotel

Scheduled completion date: December, 1989

Percentage of building leased: 90% (Phases 1 & 2)

Parking capacity: 4 per 1,000 square feet

Projected rental cost per sq. ft.: $1.05 for R&D; $1.80 for office
