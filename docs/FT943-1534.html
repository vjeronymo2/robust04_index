940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Ernest Green falls to Pounds 419,000 Ernest Green and Partners Holdings, the USM-quoted consulting engineer, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 773,000 to Pounds 419,000 in the year to June 30. Mr David Legg, chairman, said it had been another tough year for the construction industry. Turnover was lower at Pounds 7.37m (Pounds 7.66m). Earnings per share came out at 3.6p (6.6p) and a final dividend of 3.25p is proposed, cutting the total from 7p to 6p.
