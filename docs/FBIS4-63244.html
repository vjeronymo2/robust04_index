BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The FRANCE PRESSE AGENCY has reported, quoting sources in the Ukrainian president's entourage, that Leonid Kravchuk is planning to introduce direct presidential rule in the Crimea. However, the final decision will depend on the consultations currently held between Russia, Ukraine, the United States, and Crimean authorities. The consultations began after more than 1,000 Ukrainian servicemen were sent to the peninsula last night. The Ukrainian Interior Ministry today denied the report that the troops had been brought in but confirmed the fact that the Crimean Interior Ministry had been transformed into the Crimean Directorate for Internal Affairs of Ukraine, and that Colonel Vladimir Chernyshov had been appointed its head.
