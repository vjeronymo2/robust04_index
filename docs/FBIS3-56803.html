Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 19 January, Rustam Akhmedov, the defense minister of the Republic of Uzbekistan, arrived in Kiev on an official visit. On 20 January he will meet his Ukrainian counterpart Vitaliy Radetskyy. They will sign an agreement on cooperation of the two states in the military sphere. It is also planned that the Uzbek delegation will visit the aviation scientific and technical complex named after Oleh Antonov.
