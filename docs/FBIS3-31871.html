Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Croatia's Foreign Minister Mate Granic has called for continuing dialogue in order to find a lasting solution to the armed conflict in the former Yugoslavia. Granic told the United Nations Human Rights Commission in Geneva that negotiations were the only way to end the war and find a political settlement to the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina. However Granic, who is also deputy prime minister, made no reference to Wednesday's statement by ethnic Croatians in Bosnia that they were willing to consider a union with Bosnia's Muslims. Croatians and Muslims in Bosnia have agreed a cease-fire, which comes into effect at midday on Friday.
