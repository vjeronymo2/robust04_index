940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: British women extradited to US Former cult members Susan Hagan, 47, of Bedmond, Herts, and Sally Croft, 44, of Totnes, Devon, were flown to the US after losing a lengthy battle to avoid extradition. They face trial on charges of conspiring to kill Oregon state attorney Charles Turner in 1985.
