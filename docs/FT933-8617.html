930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Sea Containers' second half slip Second quarter net income of Sea Containers, the Bermuda-based cargo equipment and ferry group, fell from Dollars 13.3m to Dollars 10.4m and left the figure for the half year to end-June at Dollars 17.9m (Pounds 12m) compared with Dollars 13.6m. Earnings per common stock for the half year emerged at 97 cents (55 cents).
