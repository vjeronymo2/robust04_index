941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Blagden disposal As part of the ongoing reorganisation of its industrial packaging business, Blagden, the steel drum maker, has sold its shares in Aug. Schmalenbach, a German manufacturer, to Sulo Eisenwerk Streuber & Lohmann. Blagden sold its main plastics packaging operations for Pounds 6.9m in July.
