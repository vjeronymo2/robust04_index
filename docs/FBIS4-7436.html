BFN [Text] VNA April 28 -- President Le Duc Anh left Jakarta this morning for Bandung and Bali cities, continuing his official friendship visit to Indonesia. Before leaving the Indonesian capital, President Le Duc Anh expressed his thanks to President Suharto and his wife, the Indonesian Government and people for their hospitality and expresed his confidence that the friendship and cooperation between the two countries would continue to be consolidated and developed. For his part, President Suharto expressed his pleasure at the fine results of the Indonesia visit by President Le Duc Anh and his wife. He believed this important event would help to further promote the traditional relations and all-sided cooperation between the two countries. President Le Duc Anh has invited President Suharto and his wife to pay an official friendship visit to Vietnam. His invitation was accepted with pleasure. President Le Duc Anh and his party spent the following day in Bandung and Bali, visiting the places of historical and cultural interests in those cities in the company of their high-ranking officials as the last leg of the president's official friendship visit to Indonesia.
