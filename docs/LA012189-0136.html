January 21, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

ROUNDUP;

JALEWALIA SCORES 52 TO BREAK HER RECORD

Amy Jalewalia scored 52 points, breaking her school record, to lead La Quinta High School to a 85-12 victory over Pacifica in a Garden Grove League girls' basketball game Friday at Pacifica.

Jalewalia, a junior forward, scored 48 points against El Modena in December. She also had 12 rebounds Friday.

Carrie Egan of Brea-Olinda set the Orange County single-game girls' record with 56 points in 1987.

Tanya Krill had 16 points and 7 rebounds and Heather Brannan had 8 points and 10 rebounds for the Aztecs (14-3, 7-0).
