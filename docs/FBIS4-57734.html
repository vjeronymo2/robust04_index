BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 23 May (NA) -- Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo believes the recent cabinet crisis "has not weakened the economic plan," and he indicated that it would be better not to create the Security Secretariat "until the public has been given an appropriate explanation about what is planned." Cavallo referred to the controversial topic of the so-called super secretariat at the end of a ceremony in which agreements were signed for supplying drinking water to several provincial localities with financing from international organizations. "President Carlos Menem has given a lot of importance to the topic and it is he who will finally decide it," the minister said.
