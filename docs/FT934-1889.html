931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Group Development Group Development Capital Trust had a net asset value of 54.3p at September 30 compared with 41.1p a year earlier. After-tax revenue for the 12 months to September fell from Pounds 109,662 to Pounds 38,074 for earnings per share of 0.16p (0.46p after the section 242 tax claim). The proposed final dividend is lifted to 0.33p (0.3p).
