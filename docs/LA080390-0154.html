August 3, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

JAPANESE FUND PH.D. PROGRAM IN PITTSBURGH

Mitsubishi has given $500,000 to the University of Pittsburgh's Joseph Katz Graduate School of Business to establish the Mitsubishi Corp. Doctoral Fellowship Program in International Management.

Mitsubishi took a majority equity position in Aristech Chemical Corp. of Pittsburgh this year as a way of helping Aristech managers buy the company.
