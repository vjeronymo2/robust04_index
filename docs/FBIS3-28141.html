Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign minister, Gareth Evans, is urging the United States not to downgrade its commitment and contribution to the United Nations. Senator Evans says a bill before the American Congress seeks to reduce its support for UN peacekeeking operations and cut funding to the organization. He says as the UN's 50th anniversary approaches, Australia must make every effort to pressure America to maintain its support: [Begin Evans recording] But after the lessons of Somalia and the lessons we are still seeing working themselves out in the former Yugoslavia, I believe very strongly that the United States should join the international authority as a whole in making a thorough review of how the UN decides and manages resources for its peace operations, rather than being tempted to step away from the organization. [end recording]
