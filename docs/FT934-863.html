931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Fife Indmar director cashes loan notes Mr Charles McDonald, a disgruntled non-executive director at Fife Indmar, is cashing in Pounds 300,000 of loan notes from the Scottish engineering and distribution company following the failure last week of a rebel bid to oust the chairman. Mr McDonald, a leader of the rebel group, said he would not have demanded payment had the dissidents succeeded in winning control. Mr Gavin Hepburn, chairman, said the group had already budgeted for Mr McDonald's demand and it would not affect 'future progress'.
