BFN [Text] Quoting (Anatoliy Feofilaktov), a deputy in the Crimean Supreme Soviet, INTERFAX reports that Ukrainian national guards who have arrived in Crimea are being armed. The deputy claims that 2,600 assault rifles, light machine guns, grenade launchers, and other weapons have been brought from Kiev. In addition, armored personnel carriers have been removed from storage in the settlement of Perevalnoye near Yalta. (Feofilaktov) also said that all of the national guardsmen who had arrived were wearing civilian clothing. (Aleksandr Kostyukov), brigade commander in the Ukrainian National Guard in the Crimea, categorically denied this.
