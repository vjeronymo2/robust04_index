National Workplace Literacy Program AGENCY: Department of Education. ACTION: Withdrawal of notice of proposed priority.

SUMMARY: The Secretary withdraws the notice of proposed priority for the National Workplace Literacy Program: Workplace literacy services for empowerment zones or enterprise communities published on June 16, 1994 (59 FR 31032). The Secretary withdraws this proposed priority because no funds are available for fiscal year 1995 to support awards under this priority.
