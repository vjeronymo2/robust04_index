BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 23 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Permanent representative of the United Nations Development Program to Mongolia Jan. V. Switering and the chairman of the National Development Department of Mongolia Ch. Ulaan signed today the projects concept on development of management and management system, reforming the state governing and state service, to a value of 2.7 million U.S. dollars. With signing of these documents realization of the government programme of development of management meant for 5 years and directed to reforming the state governing and management will start in cooperation with the UNO, donor countries, international organizations. Four other projects of the given programme on decentralization and consolidation of local administrations, development of the private sector of economy, structural reconstruction of privatized enterprises, strengthening of the system of training of officials are also planned to be realized with technical assistance of Sweden, Japan, Denmark, Holland, Germany, Australia and other countries in the course of the coming five years.
