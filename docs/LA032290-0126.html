March 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: TRAFFIC SIGN INSTALLED AT BLAIR HIGH SCHOOL

A traffic sign to help mobility-impaired students at Blair High School has been installed at Marengo Avenue.

Because campus facilities are located on both sides of the street, students must cross frequently.

The flashing yellow warning sign was installed by the city of Pasadena for $1,700. An underground pedestrian tunnel is available to students, but it cannot accommodate wheelchairs.
