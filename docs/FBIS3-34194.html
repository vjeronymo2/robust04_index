Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] During their talks Thursday night [3 February], the president of the Islamic Republic of Iran and the Sultan of Brunei discussed and exchanged views on the latest world developments, regional issues, and bilateral cooperation between Iran and Brunei. During the talks, Hashemi-Rafsanjani thanked Hassanal Bolkiah for visiting Iran and expressed the hope that mutual cooperation would further deepen in all cultural, political, economic and technical fields. The president added: Our foreign policy gives a particular priority to cooperation with Islamic countries -- your country is one of them -- and that is a very good factor for the further expansion of cooperation between us. Hashemi-Rafsanjani added: We are aware of your satisfactory stances vis-a-vis the problem of Bosnia-Herzegovina at the Islamic Conference Organization [ICO]. We hope to be able to increase the ICO activities. Our country's president pointed out the existence of the Association of South East Asian Nations and the Economic Cooperation Organization in the two mainly Muslim regions, and described cooperation between Iran and Brunei as an effective factor that can contribute toward further cooperation between the two regions. Hashemi-Rafsanjani also stressed the need for the two countries' cooperation in the oil and gas industries and announced Iran's readiness to help Brunei with its technical and scientific needs in those industries. The president also welcomed the two countries' cooperation in cultural and artistic fields. Then the Sultan of Brunei Hassanal Bolkiah, thanked our country's president for his kind remarks and praised the certain and valuable status of the Islamic Republic of Iran at global levels and said: Brunei has always supported Iran's principled and progressive stance in international circles and believes itself in such stances. He added: We cooperate very well within the ICO and the Non-Aligned Movement. We have always condemned the aggression against the Bosnian Muslims and are ready to help them. We are ready to cooperate in the oil and gas industries and hope to be able to witness the expansion of relations between the two countries in all fields, particularly cultural fields.
