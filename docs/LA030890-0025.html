March 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

ARMENIAN GENOCIDE

Daniels apparently feels that America should not trifle with Turkey's sensibilities by recognizing the Armenian genocide. This attitude of willful neglect of moral responsibility for short-term political gain sells America's soul for immediate foreign policy gratification. Daniels should not forget that Turkey could easily go the way of Iran as a strategic ally while the Armenian genocide will remain a historical fact forever.

SHANT AGAJANIAN

Costa Mesa
