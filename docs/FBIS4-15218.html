BFN [Text] Moscow, 6 Apr (INA)--'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, met here today with Valentin Kuznetsov, first deputy leader of the Russian Communist Party [name and title as received]. During the meeting, 'Abd-al-Ghafur gave a detailed explanation on the stable situation in Iraq as well as the Iraqi people's support for President Saddam Husayn's leadership and their insistence on breaking the economic blockade through patience, jihad, and continued progress and construction. He also pointed to the positive development in Russia's position at the Security Council, and stressed the need for Russia to adopt more effective positions, and for the Russian parties work through the Parliament, the government, and popular bodies to help lift the economic blockade on Iraq and implement Paragraph 22 of Resolution 687. Kuznetsov reiterated the Russian Communist Party's position, which supports the Iraqi leadership and people. He lauded the Iraqi people's achievements under President Saddam Husayn, and voiced his party's readiness to do further work on the parliamentary and popular levels in order to lift the economic blockade on Iraq.
