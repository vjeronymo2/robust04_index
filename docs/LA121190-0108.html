December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: DATRON SYSTEMS RECEIVES $14 MILLION IN NEW ORDERS

Datron Systems Inc., a Simi Valley communications and defense electronics concern, said it received more than $14 million in new orders in October and November.

The amount includes $2.7 million for radios and a $2.9-million order for its Landsat land-based satellite system.
