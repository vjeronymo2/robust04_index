BFN [Text] The UN forces this afternoon handed over to the Lebanese Army four Iraqis who had been taken prisoner by the Israeli occupation troops in Jordan [as heard]. At 1600 today, the Israeli occupation troops released four Iraqis, who were taken prisoners in the Jordan Valley four months ago, at Jisr al-Hamra' in Tyre. The Israeli soldiers untied the prisoners and ordered them to walk toward the liberated area. The released Iraqis sought the help of the Finnish contingents in al-Hamra'. Following contacts the Finnish contingents made with the Lebanese authorities, the Iraqis were handed over to the Lebanese Army.
