Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Feb. 25 (YONHAP) -- South Korea has decided to provide the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) with 12 million dollars in economic cooperation funds in the next five years, the Foreign Ministry reported on Friday. The amount will comprise 2 million dollars in grant and 10 million dollars in economic development cooperation fund (EDCF). The ministry said that this year, 260,000 dollars will be disbursed for use in financing the training of 10 Palestine technical students in Korea, dispatch of two Korean specialists to Palestine and the supply of various equipment and materials, the ministry report said. A ministry official said the cooperation fund is part of international efforts to support the autonomy of Palestine. "PLO's aid recipient office has not been singled out yet, but we are studying a plan to furnish the cooperation fund through the Palestine economic development and reconstruction committee headed by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat," he said.
