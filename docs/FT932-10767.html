930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / Landlords drop legal challenge A GROUP of landlords has decided not to take action against Northern Rock building society. They were seeking to test the law on societies being required to discharge the liabilities of their subsidiaries. They claimed that Northern Rock should pay them rents owed following the liquidation of a company to which it had sold 51 estate agency offices. Mr Peter Hughes, a partner in Corbould Rigby & Co, acting for two of the landlords and co-ordinating investigations for a further 10, said yesterday the group had been deterred by the likely cost of High Court action against Northern Rock, the 11th largest building society.
