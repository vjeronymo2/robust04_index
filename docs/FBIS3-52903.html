Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 8, IRNA -- Russia's Special Envoy for Karabakh Affairs, Vladimir Kazimirov, here today met with a senior Iranian to discuss current regional condition and the ways and means to end Karabakh crisis. Kazimirov, who arrived here Thursday [6 January], briefed Director General of Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) and the Caucasus Affairs Department at the Foreign Ministry, Morteza Bank, on the measures taken by his country to end Karabakh crisis. He underscored the role the regional countries and Minsk Group as well as the European Cooperation and Security Conference can play in ending the Karabakh issue. Bank said in return that Islamic Republic of Iran is ready to intensify its efforts to put an end to war in Karabakh enclave.
