August 16, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: EL MONTE;

PARKING TIME LIMIT LIFTED

Shoppers can take their time and not worry about parking tickets since the City Council voted Tuesday to remove the two-hour time limit on city lots on the north and south sides of the Valley Mall between Granada Avenue, Ramona and Valley boulevards.

The change was made after a request from the Downtown El Monte Business Assn. The two-hour limit will still apply to street parking on Valley Mall Boulevard. The ban on parking between 3 and 5 a.m. will continue.
