BFN [Text] Israeli President Ezer Weizman telephoned President Suleyman Demirel today and briefed him on the agreement signed in Cairo regarding the transfer of Gaza and Jericho to Palestinian autonomy. A statement issued by the President's Press Center on the subject noted: President Demirel declared that the agreement turns a new page in the Middle East peace process and congratulated the Israeli and Palestinian leaders, who enabled the process to reach this stage through their self-sacrificing efforts. Demirel also informed the Israeli president that Turkey is determined to continue its contribution to the peace process.
