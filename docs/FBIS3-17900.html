Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Decree No. 76-1 GD, signed by State Duma Chairman Rybkin and dated 25 March 1994: "On Certain Questions Relating to the Application of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Decrees `On the Declaration of an Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution' and `On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty'"] [Text] In connection with questions that have arisen in the practice of applying the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Decrees "On the Declaration of an Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution" and "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty," the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma decrees that: 1. The criminal cases against persons who, during the events referred to in Paragraph 1 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty," committed criminal acts with no direct relation to these events shall not be closed. 2. Persons convicted of the crimes stipulated in Paragraph 3 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty" shall be exempted from terms of imprisonment, irrespective of the term handed down and the amount of time already served. 3. Under Paragraph 4 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty" the amnesty shall be applied to persons who committed crimes up to and including 31 December 1991. 4. Criminal cases brought under Article 92 Part 3, Article 93 Parts 2 and 3, and Article 93-1 of the RSFSR Criminal Code and referred to in Paragraph 4 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty" shall be deemed closed on the basis of that paragraph. 5. The restriction stipulated in Paragraph 4 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty" on applying the amnesty to persons convicted of the embezzlement of state and public property through theft, larceny, robbery, or fraud shall apply solely to those convicted under Article 93-1 of the RSFSR Criminal Code. 6. Execution of Paragraph 4 of the decree "On the Declaration of a Political and Economic Amnesty" shall be the responsibility of the courts in the cases stipulated by Paragraph 1 Subparagraph B of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Decree "On the Procedure for Applying the Decree `On the Declaration of an Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution.'" 7. Paragraph 1 Subparagraph B of the decree "On the Declaration of an Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution" shall apply to persons born before 26 February 1934. 8. Paragraph 3 of the decree "On the Declaration of an Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution" shall apply to male juveniles born not before 26 February 1976. [Signed] I.P. Rybkin, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma [Dated] Moscow, 25 March 1994 No. 76-1 GD
