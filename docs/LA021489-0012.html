February 14, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Weyerhaeuser Co. is closing its milk carton plant in La Puente, resulting in the loss of 140 jobs during the next two to four months . . . A $23.3-million stock offering was launched to finance the purchase of the Mustang Ranch, east of Reno, America's largest and probably best-known legal brothel . . . Ashton-Tate Corp., Torrance, said its directors have adopted a shareholder rights plan to thwart any potential hostile takeover.
