June 26, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: YORBA LINDA;

SEMINAR TO EXAMINE ROLE OF CHOLESTEROL

A three-hour seminar on fats and cholesterol will be held tonight at the Yorba Linda Adult Education Center.

The seminar will discuss the relationship of fats and cholesterol with various diseases, and how much fat and cholesterol is necessary to maintain good health.

The seminar begins at 7 p.m. at the Education Center, 4175 Fairmont Blvd. Registration is $15. For information, call (714) 635-8560.
