920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / EC ministers agree food aid EC aid ministers yesterday approved the dispatch of 800,000 tonnes of food to Africa and other famine-stricken parts of the world, David Buchan reports from Brussels. Most of the aid, worth a total Ecu220m (Pounds 154m), represents an increase in the EC's 1992 aid budget, requiring approval of the European parliament which is expected today.
