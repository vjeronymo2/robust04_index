940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / Arts: New boost for Arts Council The director Trevor Nunn and architect Sir Richard Rogers are to take up key roles in the Arts Council under new chairman Lord Gowrie. The appointments, announced yesterday by the Heritage Secretary Peter Brooke, are seen as part of a move to restore the council's damaged reputation. Following abortive attempts to restructure London's orchestras last year, there were widespread calls for the abolition of the Arts Council.
