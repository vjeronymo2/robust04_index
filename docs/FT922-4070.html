920608 FT 08 JUN 92 / Observer: Wigging It seems that new Lord Chief Justice Lord Taylor has not yet convinced everyone that judges are user-friendly and in tune with the times. A piece of graffiti at Knightsbridge Crown Court reads: 'The Ice Age wiped out the dinosaurs, but how did the judges survive?' Of course, carbon-dating of the inscription may show it was written during the tenure of Taylor's predecessor, Lord Lane.
