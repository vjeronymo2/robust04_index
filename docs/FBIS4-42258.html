BFN [Text] National Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis today is visiting the town of Kastoria and the Dhipotamia guard post on the Greek-Albanian border. Shortly before his departure for the Greek-Albanian border region, Mr. Arsenis said that this visit had been schedule long ago and that his intention is to visit not only the Greek-Albanian borders but also other border areas. Mr. Arsenis said: Of course, this visit is taking place at a time of tension in Greek-Albanian relations, but my task is to ascertain that the Armed Forces will do their duty by being vigilant and guarding the area.
