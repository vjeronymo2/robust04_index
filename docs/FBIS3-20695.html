Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Text] (HINA) Split -- On Monday [7 February], Dr. Goran Dodig, a member of the Board of Directors of the SLOBODNA DALMACIJA Newspaper, Inc., handed his irrevocable resignation to Miroslav Kutle, president of the Shareholders' Assembly, and director Ante Busic, stating: "I would like to inform you that I have decided to terminate my participation in the work of the Board beginning 6 February 1994. I wish you much success in your future endeavors." Metod Jurisic, president of the Board of Directors, had resigned before Dodig, citing disagreements with the editorial policy of the editor in chief. Dodig and Jurisic had been members of the Board of Directors since the transformation and privatization of SLOBODNA DALMACIJA.
