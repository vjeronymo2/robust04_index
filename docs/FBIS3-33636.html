Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The meeting of Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres and PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat in Davos -- also attended by Minister Yosi Sarid and Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa -- has ended. After the meeting 'Arafat said that not all the problems were solved, but he expects an agreement soon. Yosi Sarid said there are ups and downs; agreement was achieved on some issues and now we are closer to an agreement. Arab sources told our correspondent Gid'on Kouts that for all intents and purposes an agreement has been achieved, except for some minor issues. Israeli members in the formulations committee said that understandings were reached on all issues; the two sides made concessions and there is a chance for reaching an agreement. Foreign Minister Peres will stay in Davos until tomorrow morning. The contacts will be carried on tonight.
