930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / International Company News: Circle K move THE Official Bondholders Committee at Circle K, which is presenting one of the rival reorganisation schemes for the bankrupt US convenience store chain, yesterday filed a motion with the Arizona bankruptcy courts, seeking information about Sheik Ali Khalifa al-Sabah, writes Nikki Tait. Sheik Khalifa is a backer of CK Acquisition Corporation, which is putting forward a rival reorganisation proposal for Circle K.
