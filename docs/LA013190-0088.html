January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The Limited Inc. announced that it is moving the headquarters of its 720-store Lane Bryant division to a Columbus, Ohio, suburb from New York . . . Utilitech Inc. said it has signed a definitive agreement with United Merchants & Manufacturers Inc. to acquire its Decora division, the original manufacturer of ConTactR brand self-adhesive products. Utilitech will pay $23 million in cash for Decora . . . H.F. Ahmanson & Co. set March 13 as the record date for the 1990 annual meeting of stockholders, to be held on May 8 in Los Angeles . . . Shareholders of Combustion Engineering Inc. approved a previously reported $1.6-billion merger with Asea Brown Boveri of Zurich, the Stamford, Conn.-based company announced . . . San Jose-based Altos Computer Systems plans to repurchase up to 2 million shares of its 10.8 million common shares outstanding.
