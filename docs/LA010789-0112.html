January 7, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

OCEAN VIEW AND ST. PAUL MEET IN REMATCH

Ocean View High School takes a break from Sunset League action and will play host to St. Paul in a nonleague game at 4 p.m. today in Huntington Beach.

Ocean View (11-3) is led by center Todd Norman and guards Mike Frohn and Greg Evans. St. Paul (11-5) has scored 90 or more points in 9 games and is led by forward Greg Willig.

The game is a rematch of last year's 5-A division first-round playoff game in which Ocean View defeated St. Paul, 91-88.
