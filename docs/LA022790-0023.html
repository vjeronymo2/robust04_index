February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Philip Jacobs has been promoted from vice president to senior vice president-sales in the boys and preschool toys division of Mattel Inc., Hawthorne.

Ronald Kesselman has been promoted to senior vice president-marketing and product development, preschool toys. Bob Rao has been promoted to senior vice president-marketing and product development, boys toys. In the girls and activity toys division, Joseph A. Cristina has been named vice president-finance and administration. Ramon Fuentevilla has been named senior vice president-sales.
