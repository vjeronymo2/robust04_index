December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

URGENT CARE CENTER OFFERS FREE FLU SHOTS

Free flu shots are being offered this month by the new East Canyon Urgent Care Center, which is affiliated with Anaheim Memorial Hospital.

The shots are offered daily between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. at the center, 8201 Santa Ana Canyon Road, Anaheim Hills. For information, call (714) 921-1833.
