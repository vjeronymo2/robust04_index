920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / International Company News: Olipar talks with investors OLIPAR, the French property company headed by Mr Christian Pellerin, the developer charged with financial irregularities, has confirmed it is in talks with potential investors. This follows days of volatile trading in which Olipar's shares have fluctuated on the Paris stock market. The company said it was holding talks with its shareholders and partners about Lucia, a subsidiary which specialises in property sales. Like other French property companies, it has been hit by the depressed state of the French real estate market where sale prices and rentals have fallen sharply in recent years. This year, Mr Pellerin was charged with aiding and abetting the abuse of corporate property and falsification of documents, which he denies.
