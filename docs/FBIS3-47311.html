Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, January 11 (KPL) -- A joint venture agreement of the Company for Expert Services was signed on January 8 between the Lao Government and Lio Yao Petro-chemical Company of China. The joint venture was aimed at updating the management and business operation of the existing buildings. These buildings in the past have been used for business operation by the state-run company for expert services. They are located on Luang Prabang road, Sithan Neua, Sikhottabong District, Vientiane Municipality. This joint business was to serve both local and foreign investors. The sides have also agreed to make these buildings as a facility for training Lao nationals in housing services. The term of the joint venture is 30 years. The Lao side holds a share of 30 per cent. Upon termination of the term, all assets worth U.S. [$]1,350,000 under the agreement will go to the Lao Government.
