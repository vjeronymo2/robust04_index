Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ivan Korotchenya, CIS executive secretary, reported to Interfax that the next summit of CIS heads of states had been postponed from the end of March to April 15. According to Korotchenya, Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev asked Boris Yeltsin about this during their telephone talk. "There are no written orders or instructions about the reasons of the summit's postponement in the executive secretariat," noted Korotchenya.
