August 15, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

ISLANDERS GET DON MALONEY

Left wing Don Maloney signed a one-year contract with the New York Islanders today.

Maloney, 31, made the Islanders as a free agent last season and finished with 16 goals and 27 assists for 43 points in 79 games. He was seventh on the team in scoring and sixth in plus-minus ratings at plus-6.
