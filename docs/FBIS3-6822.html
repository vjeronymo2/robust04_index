Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Ba Dinh Conference Hall in Hanoi on 19 March, Comrade Nong Duc Manh, member of the Political Bureau and chairman of the National Assembly, received a delegation of cadres from Lai Chau Party College. Also present was Comrade Cu Hoa Van, deputy chairman of the Nationalities Council. The delegation is composed of 108 comrades of 10 ethnic minority groups who are leading cadres of villages in nine mountain districts of Lai Chau Province. After hearing the delegation head's briefing on the activities of the college, Comrades Nong Duc Manh and Cu Hoa Van inquired about the socioeconomic situation of Lai Chau Province under the renovation process. Chairman Nong Duc Manh also urged the college to help its students to grasp firmly party and state lines and policies with a view to building the local economy and improving the provincial socioeconomic situation.
