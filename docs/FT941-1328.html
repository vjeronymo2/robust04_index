940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Enlarged TLS Pounds 209,000 in profit TLS Range, the USM-traded vehicle rental group, continued to progress through the second half and for the full 1993 year swung from losses of Pounds 190,000 to profits of Pounds 209,000 pre-tax. Turnover improved from Pounds 9.79m to Pounds 11.63m with acquisitions during the year lifting the hire fleet by 1,318 vehicles to 3,056. Earnings per share worked through at 0.9p (losses 0.98p) and the recommended single dividend is being doubled to 0.5p. Mr Peter Busby is joining the group as finance director effective from April 1. He was formerly with the Scapa Group.
