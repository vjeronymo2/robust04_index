BFN [Text] China says India and Pakistan should find a solution to the Kashmir issue bilaterally. The Chinese ambassador in India, Mr. Cheng Ruisheng, told a press conference in Calcutta this afternoon that China does not want to be a mediator on the issue. On nonproliferation, Mr. Ruisheng said no country should pressurize India to sign the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty. Replying to a question on India's new economic policy, he said the time has come to integrate developing economies with the world economy to expedite progress.
