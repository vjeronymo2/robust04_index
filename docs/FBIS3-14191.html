Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 23 Mar (INA)--The second two-day engineering conference of the Military Engineering Academy convened today under the motto "Nothing Can Prevent Us from Achieving Scientific Advancement." The conference, which was attended by Defense Minister Staff General 'Ali Hasan al-Majid and several other ministers, began with a Koranic recitation. Then, the opening chapter of the Koran was read to invoke God's mercy on the souls of those who are nobler than all of us, the martyrs of Iraq and the glorious Arab nation. The conferees will discuss ways of mobilizing Iraqi scientific expertise to break the embargo, and endorse reasonable criteria for the assessment of scientific research. They will also debate 89 research papers in various areas and engineering specialties. The conference is attended by 96 researchers, 95 associate professors, and 25 university professors working at all Iraqi military and civilian universities, as well as specialized agencies and research institutes and centers affiliated with the Military Industrialization Organization, the National Construction Center, the Ministry of Housing and Reconstruction, the Ministry of Oil, the Ministry of Transport and Communications, the Ministry of Irrigation, and the Ministry of Agriculture.
