BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Chile monitored through 31 March. The National Petroleum Enterprise decided to increase oil and kerosene prices. As of 26 March, 81-93 octane gasoline increases 4 pesos per liter and kerosene drops 2 pesos per liter. (Santiago Radio Cooperativa Network in Spanish 2300 GMT 25 Mar 94) CCU, United Breweries Company, purchased 15.24 percent of the Croatian company Karlovacka Pivovara. CCU's partner, Southern Breweries Establishments, purchased 15.24 percent of the Croatian company. CCU and Southern Breweries Establishments purchased 151,126 common registered shares of Karlovacka Pivovara for 10.7 million German marks, equivalent to $6,342,620. (Santiago ESTRATEGIA in Spanish 18 Mar 94 p 13)
