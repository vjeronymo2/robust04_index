930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Sea Containers Sea Containers, the Bermuda-based cargo equipment and ferry group, beat its earnings per share forecast with a 4 cent rise to Dollars 2.35 (165p) for 1992. For the final quarter the earnings figure of 28 cents compared with previous losses of 4 cents. Total revenue for the year grew to Dollars 421m (Dollars 385m). Net income of Dollars 39.8m (Dollars 32.7m) was aided by an increased contribution of Dollars 33.3m (Dollars 25.5m) from marine container leasing. Ferry and port income fell Dollars 4m to Dollars 16.2m. Leisure industries incurred a loss, but this was offset by profits from foreign exchange contracts and left profit from other activities of Dollars 400,000 (Dollars 4.9m). The company said the results were about Dollars 1m greater than predicted due to year-end accounting adjustments.
