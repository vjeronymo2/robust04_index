BFN [Text] President Ion Iliescu today sent separate telegrams to Yasir 'Arafat, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee and president of the State of Palestine, Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin, and Israeli Foreign Affairs Minister Shim'on Peres, in which he congratulated them on the occasion of signing the accord on autonomy in Gaza and Jericho, an act to whose finalization, the cable reads, the recent talks held in Bucharest between the Palestinian leader and the Israeli foreign minister also made a contribution. President Iliescu reasserts Romania's wish to continue to contribute to the achievement of favorable conditions for a just and durable peace in the Middle East. In another telegram sent to Husni Mubarak, president of the Arab Republic of Egypt, President Iliescu voices his appreciation for the steady actions made by Egypt and its president to achieve peace in the Middle East region. President Iliescu also expresses the belief that Romanian-Egyptian relations will continue to develop to the mutual benefit of both countries.
