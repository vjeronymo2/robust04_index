June 1, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Musical Politics: Crosby, Stills & Nash, Gladys Knight and comedian Dana Carvey are scheduled to perform at "Democrats: The Party of the Decade" June 20th at the Kennedy Center in Washington. The fund-raiser will benefit the Democratic campaigns of 1990. Tickets range from $30 to $1,000. GREG BRAXTON
