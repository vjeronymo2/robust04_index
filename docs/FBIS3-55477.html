Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer Sergey Vozyanov] Colonel General Fedor Reut, the commander of the Group of Russian Forces in the Transcaucasus, is continuing his visit to Azerbaijan. He has had a meeting with Geydar Aliyev, the president of Azerbaijan. Aliyev noted that active fighting is in progress at the moment, but it was not initiated by the Azerbaijani side. For a month now, he said, plans are being hatched for the annexation of further Azerbaijani land. Addressing Fedor Reut, the president called on the Russian side to promote a peaceful settlement of the question of Azerbaijan's territorial integrity. Aliyev also asked for help in defending the 600,000-strong Azerbaijani community in Georgia, which is being subjected to attack by criminal elements. That was a report from the ANI news agency.
