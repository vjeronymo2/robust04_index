BFN [Text] Riyadh, 7 Apr -- In response to claims by the Yemeni paper AL-HIWAR, which stated that Saudi forces had penetrated five kilometers inside Yemeni territory, an official source at the Ministry of Defense, Aviation and the Inspectorate General said: The entire report of this paper is fabricated and has no foundation of truth. Yemeni officials themselves are aware of the extent of the falsity of the content of the newspaper's report. The source added: As regard the other topics created by the said paper, they are, in our view, unworthy of any mention or consideration. At the end of the statement the source said: While we are clarifying the truth to refute these claims, we underline the strength of the relations that link the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Yemeni Republic.
