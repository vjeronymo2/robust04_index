930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Harrison disposes of French division Harrison Industries has agreed to sell its French industrial doors division for FFr9m (Pounds 1.07m) in cash to La Societe Fontenay Industries. At March 31 1993 estimated net assets of the two French subsidiaries were FFr2.1m, sales for 1992-93 were FFr35.7m and pre-tax profits FFr1.2m. The companies are Bostwick France and SCI Harrison. Bostwick will pay Harrison a dividend of FFr6m while Harrison will repay its indebtedness to Bostwick of FFr8.7m. Net sale proceeds and the dividend will be used to reduce Harrison's borrowing.
