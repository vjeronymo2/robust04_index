BFN [Text] Today's session of the Crimean parliament adopted a law restoring the Constitution of 6 May 1992, INTERFAX reports. This Constitution envisions that the legal basis of relations between Ukraine and Crimea will be a treaty. It also stipulates that inhabitants of the peninsula are entitled to dual citizenship. As has already been reported, Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk earlier warned that if the Crimean Supreme Soviet restores the Constitution, he will immediately ask the Ukrainian parliament to suspend the decision.
