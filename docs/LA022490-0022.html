February 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PICKING WINNERS BETTER THAN A WINNING PICKER

Your improved horse racing coverage is good, and I like Bob Mieszerski, but the "Betting With Bob" feature has got to go.

As any handicapper can tell you, a $30 win parlay on two odds-on horses is not artful betting.

I'd sooner invest in a money-market CD. I'd have profits, lower risk, and probably even more excitement.

BOB HERR, North Hollywood
