941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Chinese power output to rise China's electric power production is expected to rise by 10 per cent from 1993 to exceed 910bn kilowatt hours this year. But this will not be enough to prevent power shortages in the winter months, the Xinhua news agency said. In the first three quarters of the year power output was 664.9bn kilowatt hours, the Ministry of Power Production said. It forecast a sharp rise In demand from businesses over the coming months because of rapid economic growth and said power cuts were inevitable toward the end of the year.
