940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Cardiff Property Cardiff Property reported a strong advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 35,861 to Pounds 98,450 in the six months to March 31. The figures were helped by acquisitions and rent reviews. Gross rental income improved to Pounds 368,479 (Pounds 342,534). Earnings per share were 3.6p (1.19p) but the interim dividend is maintained at 0.85p.
