940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: British Fittings up British Fittings, the pipeline equipment, water products and metals distribution group, lifted pre-tax profits by 65 per cent, from Pounds 672,000 to Pounds 1.11m, in the half year to June 30. Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 36.7m (Pounds 36.4m), including Pounds 1.37m from acquisitions. The interim dividend is lifted to 1p (0.75p), payable from earnings of 2.73p (1.71p) per share.
