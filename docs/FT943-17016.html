940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / New Jubilee line stop gets go-ahead Construction of a station at North Greenwich on London Underground's Jubilee Line extension is to go ahead after British Gas, which owns the site, agreed to contribute to building costs. The British Gas contribution is understood to amount to about Pounds 25m of the total Pounds 70m cost of the station and the line between Canary Wharf and Canning Town stations.
