920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Observer: Playtime Puzzling to see the three Shakespeare plays which education secretary John Patten says must be covered in English tests for 14-year-olds: Julius Caesar, A Midsummer Night's Dream, and Romeo and Juliet. But he's no doubt right in saying their 'enduring themes . . . will capture the imaginations of pupils'. What better to suit young teenage tastes than themes like cowardice, lust, betrayal, murder and war?
