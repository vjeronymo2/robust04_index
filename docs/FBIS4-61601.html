BFN [Text] Sunday morning the Azeri troops resumed their offensive in the direction of the villages of Chailu and Leninavan in the north of Mardakert Region of Nagorny Karabakh [Nagorno-Karabakh]. They are supported by artillery and armored vehicles. Military actions are continuing all along the front line, said the press service of Nagorny Karabakh's defence army. According to reports issued at 1200 on Sunday, the Karabakh forces have repulsed all attacks. Their enemy sustained losses in personnel and military equipment. Concrete losses are not known yet. At the other sectors of the front routine exchanges of fire went on last night and Sunday morning.
