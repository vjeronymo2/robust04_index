940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / International Company News: Three S Korean IPOs planned South Korea's Securities Supervisory Board will allow three local companies to go public late next month, AP-DJ reports from Seoul. It said Kyoungnam Energy, a gas supplier, Shinsung Industrial, a printing circuit board maker, and Chonggu Corporation, a general housing constructor, are all to propose initial public offerings towards the end of June this year. Chonggu hopes to raise 37.06bn won (Dollars 45m), Shinsung 4.79bn won and Kyoungnam Energy 7.65bn won.
