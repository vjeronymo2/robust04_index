Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] His Majesty King Husayn today received Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabbur Al Thani at his place of residence in Washington. During the meeting, his majesty stressed to the minister the need for Arab coordination, as well as the need to revive solidarity and unify the stands and views of the Arab brothers, particularly at this critical phase in the life of the Arab nation. He said the challenges facing the nation dictate that efforts and energies should be pooled together to ensure the minimum level of Arab solidarity and coordination that would benefit all peoples of the nation, strengthen the ties existing among them, and protect the interests of the nation and its future generations.
