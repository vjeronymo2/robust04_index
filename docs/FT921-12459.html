920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / Probe of Gulf troop charters under way AN INVESTIGATION is under way into suspected irregularities in the shipping of British troops and equipment to Saudi Arabia during the Gulf campaign, David White writes. The Ministry of Defence said at the weekend that documents were seized last month from premises in London and the south-east by MoD police. The documents related to the chartering of ships for Operation Granby - the British contribution to forces in the Gulf.
