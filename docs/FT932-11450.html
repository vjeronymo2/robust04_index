930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Office & Electronic Office and Electronic Machines, which has changed its year end to June, announ-ced a profit of Pounds 94,000 pre-tax for the six months to December 31 against losses of Pounds 71,000 in the comparable period of 1991. Turnover, which included Pounds 721,000 from discontinued activities, amounted to Pounds 2.3m. That compared with Pounds 734,000 from discontinued businesses. Exceptional credits amoun-ted to Pounds 265,000 (Pounds 385,000).
