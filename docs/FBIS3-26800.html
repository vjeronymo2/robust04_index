Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "Hookup" program] [Text] After hearing a brief report yesterday [8 February] by (Hsu I-ming), chairman of the Atomic Energy Council under the Executive Yuan, on procedures for the handling of buildings affected by radioactive steel, Premier Lien Chan instructed the council to work in coordination with other departments and commissions to submit the draft procedures to the Executive Yuan for examination and approval as quickly as possible. The draft was supposed to be submitted yesterday. Many relevant commissions and departments are opposed to the draft, and no consensus was reached after consultations. Suggestions have been made for amending the crucial articles of the procedures. [words indistinct] (Hsu I-ming) indicated his council will act in accordance with the instructions of the Executive Yuan and submit the draft procedures for examination and approval after the Spring Festival holidays.
