921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Grenade attack in Jerusalem An attacker tossed a grenade into a crowded market in old Jerusalem yesterday, killing one Arab and wounding 12, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. Police said they did not know who threw the grenade from a rooftop in the walled Old City's Moslem quarter but they sent reinforcements to the area to prevent reprisals. Jerusalem police chief Haim Albaldes said police were investigating several possibilities, including an attack by Jews. Police detained 12 Arabs after the blast.
