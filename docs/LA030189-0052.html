March 1, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

LOS ANGELES CAMPAIGN '89

MAYORAL RACE

Mayor Tom Bradley spoke to a Westside group of young professionals at a luncheon on Tuesday about Los Angeles in the coming decade.

CITY COUNCIL RACES

Jeanne Nemo, a candidate in West San Fernando Valley's 3rd City Council District, opened her campaign headquarters on Monday, at 7607 Fallbrook Ave. Canoga Park.

QUOTABLE

"Well, I just hope these weapons don't explode in his face."

-- -- Mayor Bradley responding to a question regarding an offer by one of his opponents, Councilman Nate Holden, to pay $300 to anyone turning an assault rifle in to police.
