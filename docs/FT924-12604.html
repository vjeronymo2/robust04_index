921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Lowland Investment net assets decline Net asset value per share of the Lowland Investment Company declined by 5 per cent, from 211.2p to 200.8p, over the year to end-September . Earnings per share were 8.75p (8.73p). A final dividend of 5.5p makes an 8.5p (8.1p) total.
