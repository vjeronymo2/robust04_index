921222 FT 22 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Moody's cuts debt rating for GPA Group GPA Group, the Irish aircraft leasing company struggling to restructure its finances, suffered a further blow to confidence when Moody's rating agency cut its debt rating from Ba2 to B1. Moody's said the cut reflected uncertainty over the outcome of restructuring and the depressed condition of the airline industry. The Dollars 1.8bn in debt affected had already fallen below investment grade.
