BFN [Text] Tunis, 18 May (MENA)--MENA has learned that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat has appointed al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim, the current Palestinian ambassador to Jordan, as secretary general of the Palestinian national authority with the rank of minister. The agency also learned that 'Arafat has decided to appoint Hakam Bal'awi, the Palestinian ambassador to Tunisia, as director general of the Palestinian general intelligence. 'Abd-al-Rahim and Bal'awi are both members of the central committee of the 'Arafat-led Fatah Movement, the major PLO faction.
