941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / Locals denounce free trade Latin American indigenous leaders have denounced free trade as a scourge for their land and people, as government officials from the 34 countries scheduled to attend next week's Summit of the Americas negotiate last-minute agreements on trade issues. Signed by representatives of more than 8m indigenous people in South and Central America, the letter said free trade would benefit multinational corporations at their expense and 'reinforce the economic dominion' of the US.
