BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- Formations of fighter bombers, assault planes, observation planes, reconnaissance planes and electronic warfare helicopters of the U.S. Air Force stationed in South Korea and the South Korean puppet air force Thursday staged an air war exercise against the North in the sky above Yoju, Tanyang and Oeyon Islet of South Korea with the support of a refuelling tanker to test the capacity of successive strikes at long-range targets, military sources said. Meanwhile, a tank group of the puppet army deployed in Paju County, Kyonggi Province, fired scores of shells into the opposite side of River Yimjin and the area of Hapo-ri and puppet army soldiers who took position near the demilitarized zone in Yinje County, South Korean Kangwon Province, blindly fired hundreds of large-calibre machine gun bullets.
