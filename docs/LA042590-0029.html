April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CONTRACT AWARDED FOR BALBOA PARK CENTER

The Los Angeles City Recreation and Parks Commission awarded a $2-million contract on Tuesday that will allow construction of a community activities center that will also serve as a meeting hall for Balboa Park, which is part of the Sepulveda Basin.

Plans for the recreational facility, a 15,000-square-foot building at 17015 Burbank Blvd., will include a gymnasium and an auditorium.

Also included will be a stage, three meeting rooms, a kitchen, offices and restrooms.

Construction is expected to begin this summer. Completion of the building is expected by summer, 1991.
