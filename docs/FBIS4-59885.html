BFN [Text] Political sources in Jerusalem have said that in evaluating the proposals relayed by Syria to Israel through U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, one can deduce that Damascus is ready to discuss the time factor for a settlement on the Golan provided Israel announces its agreement to withdraw completely from the Golan. Our political affairs correspondent Shlomo Ganor has learned that Syria proposed that the withdrawal be carried out within a period not to exceed four years on condition that it includes security arrangements on both sides of the border.
