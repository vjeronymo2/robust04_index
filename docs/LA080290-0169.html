August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FHP FOUNDATION GIVES $265,000 IN AID

The FHP Foundation awarded more than $265,000 in grants at its quarterly board of directors meeting.

California Pools for the Handicapped, operating in Long Beach, received $25,000 in support of warm-water hydrotherapy programs for senior citizens.

Other local recipients included Young Horizons of Lakewood and the Los Angeles Poison Control Center. The Los Angeles Free Clinic accepted $15,000 for its medication information service, a caller-directed program that answers consumer questions about drug interactions and side effects.

The FHP Foundation supports education, research and development of community activities related to health care.
