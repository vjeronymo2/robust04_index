940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / Accused agent tells of invite A trainee patent agent accused of suggestively touching an office worker and making unwanted suggestions told an industrial tribunal yesterday that she invited him into her flat when she was not fully dressed. Dr Ian Coates, of patents agent William Jones in Norwich, denied the allegations and said at the hearing in Leeds that he turned down an offer made my Miss Carol Leather, an office worker at the company, to share her flat.
