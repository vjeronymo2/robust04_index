Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister [Nicola] Mancino will not be investigated [as part of the Intelligence and Democratic Security Service -- Sisde -- slush fund inquiry]. The news was disclosed by Judge Ettore Torri at the end of the meeting which took place today in the Rome prosecutor's office. Announcing the shelving of the case, Torri also said the files concerning statements made by former Sisde directors [Maurizio] Broccoletti and [Riccardo] Malpica will be sent to the parliamentary immunity committee. The prosecutor's office also announced that he has no immediate plans to question Chief of Police [Vincenzo] Parisi.
