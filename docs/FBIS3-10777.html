Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Next May, the Mexican organization "For Cuba" [Va por Cuba] will send a third shipment that includes over 80 tons of lubricating oil, in defiance of the U.S. embargo [embargo] of our Caribbean island. The "Oil for Cuba" campaign is in its final phase, we already have a figure similar to last year's, and the donations continue -- said (Rodolfo Echevarria), representative of [words indistinct], who arrived in Havana along with over (?250) members of the third Pastors for Peace caravan. In a wheelchair because of his progressive neurocerebral affliction, Echevarria reported that in the past, the "For Cuba" group has sent to the island shipments of lubricants totalling 175 tons. The organization this time contributed six tons of medicines, school supplies, powdered milk, and other articles to the Pastors for Peace campaign.
