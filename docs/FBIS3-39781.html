Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Ankara, Feb 21 (AA) -- Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin on Monday [21 February] met with Armenian Assistant Foreign Minister Zhirair Liparityan, currently in Ankara for official talks. During the meeting, Assistant Foreign Ministry Undersecretary Ambassador Bilgin Unan, Caucasian Affairs Department Assistant Director General Candan Azer and a number of other Foreign Ministry officials were also present. Political consultations were held on the latest developments in the Caucasus, it was reported. [passage omitted]
