940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Blenheim trustees to act over marquess The trustees of Blenheim Palace won permission in the High Court to pursue an action to stop the Marquess of Blandford, 38, (left) from managing the Pounds 100m Oxfordshire estate on the death of his father, the Duke of Marlborough. Lord Blandford is contesting the application.
