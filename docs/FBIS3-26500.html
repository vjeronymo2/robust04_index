Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, February 9 (XINHUA) -- The maintenance of Macao's stable development and smooth transition conforms to the interests of China, Portugal and local people, Guo Dongpo said in his spring festival message here today. Guo, director of the XINHUA NEWS AGENCY Macao branch, said that in the coming new year, we hope to continue friendly cooperation with the Portugal Government in implementing the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration in convergence with the Basic Law of Macao Special Administrative Region (SAR), in a bid to properly deal with a series of important issues in the transitional period. He pointed out, these issues include the three major questions, namely localization of public servants and law and officialization of Chinese language. In handling some key issues, Guo noted, we hope to, together with the Portugal side, seek proper ways to work out solutions on the basis of mutual respect and mutual understanding. He added that this would also be done in line with the spirit of friendly relationship and cooperation between the two Governments of China and Portugal, and in the view of Macao's long-term interest. He commented that as a result of friendly cooperation between the two Governments of China and Portugal, all matters concerning the transition in Macao have developed smoothly. He hoped that Macao compatriots and people from all walks of life continue to actively participate in various matters in the transitional period and contribute more to the prosperity and bright future of Macao.
