BFN [Text] Bandar Anzali (Gilan), April 24, (IRNA) -- The Irish minister of defence and marine affairs, David Andrews, and his delegation visited the fishery facilities at this Iranian port on the Caspian Sea. The visit by the Irish official has been made upon the invitation of the Ministry of Construction Jihad. The Iranian and Irish delegations exchanged views on fishing and related activities and cooperation. After meeting with governor general of Gilan, Ali Akbar Tahaei, the Irish official visited Martyr Beheshti fish culturing workshops, Langar Dam, and Zibakenar which is a spot for fishing caviar.
