Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Nigeria Labor Congress, NLC, has called on workers to participate fully and be prepared to make necessary sacrifices this year to save the country's democratic aspiration and socioeconomic well-being. In his new year message, the NLC president, Mr. Pascal Bafyau, said that 1994 holds another promise for the working class and labor unions in the nation's drive to democracy and struggle for economic survival. He said despite the experiences of last year, the working class and Nigerians in general should show a renewed sense of optimism, commitment, and confidence in the ability of the nation to transform its [words indistinct] in the future. Mr. Bafyau said that the government had taken the initiative to ensure that workers were in the mainstream of national output. He also enjoined the business community, especially transporters, landlords, traders, and petty businessmen to be humane in fixing (?tags) and prices for their goods and services to make life more bearable for the public.
