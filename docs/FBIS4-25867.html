BFN [From the "Xinjiang News" program] [Text] Estonian President Lennart Meri, who is coming to pay a state visit to China at the invitation of President Jiang Zemin, made a brief stopover at the Urumqi Airport yesterday afternoon (12 June) on his way to Beijing. Entrusted by President Jiang Zemin, Xinjiang Regional Chairman Abulaidi Amudurexiti greeted the Estonian president at the airport. The host and guest had a cordial and friendly conversation in the airport's VIP lounge.
