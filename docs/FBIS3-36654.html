Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander of the anti-Dudayev armed formations General Ibragim Suleymenov stated that reconciliation with the Chechen regime was only possible on the following terms: immediate and unconditional resignation of Dzhokhar Dudayev, the return of the capital from foreign banks (about $1 Bn) and proportional distribution of this capital among the population, and putting Dudayev on Shariat and secular trial. On Monday, Suleymenov made a corresponding statement which was circulated in Moscow on Monday by the armed opposition's spokesman Zanbek Aliyev. He said that the Suleymenov-controlled units had started military maneuvers in Chechnya last Friday to improve interaction between units and rehearse bomb attacks on land targets. Suleymenov was a deputy in the Chechen parliament disbanded by Dudayev in June 1993.
