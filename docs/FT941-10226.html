940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / International Company News: Tullow Oil in IPounds 6m placing and offer Tullow Oil, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration and development company, is raising up to IPounds 6m (Pounds 5.8m) through a placing and open offer of up to 27.3m new ordinary shares at 22p each. Some 18.1m new shares are being conditionally placed with institutional and private investors. The placing has been fully underwritten. The offer to existing shareholders is of up to 9.25m shares on a 1-for-16 basis. Tullow also announced operating profits of IPounds 1.66m for 1993 (IPounds 112,591 losses). Pre and post-tax profits were IPounds 1.08m against IPounds 1.63m which included a IPounds 2.31m profit on the sale of licence interests. Turnover rose to IPounds 3.42m (IPounds 1.3m). Earnings declined to 0.73p (1.12p). The company, currently traded on the USM, intends to apply for admission to the Official List.
