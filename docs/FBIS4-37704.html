BFN [Text] Brother Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development, left Sanaa for the United States today on a working visit of several days. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the minister noted that, during his New York visit, he will meet with Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali, secretary general of the United Nations, and brother Lakhdar Brahimi, envoy of the UN secretary general, to follow up the situation in the Republic of Yemen. Al-Iryani added he will then head for Washington to hold meetings and talks with officials at the U.S. Department of State to discuss bilateral relations. The minister of planning and development said he will discuss, during the visit, ways to implement Security Council Resolution 924 under Yemeni unity and constitutional legitimacy. He will also explain to UN and U.S. officials the violations committed by rebels against international legitimacy represented by Security Council Resolution 924 in an attempt to circumvent the content of the resolution to achieve illegitimate objectives.
