930118 FT 18 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Runway lighting THORN LIGHTING has won a Pounds 1m plus order for a lighting project at Kansai Airport in Japan. The contract covers the supply of over 2,200 inset taxiway lights at the airport, which is located at Osaka and designed to meet passenger growth into the next century. Electrical installation will be undertaken by Chudenko.
