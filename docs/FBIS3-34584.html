Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 9 Feb (INA)--'Ali Salim al-Bid, Yemeni vice president and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, has announced he will expeditiously and finally sign the document of pledge and accord signed by the Yemeni parties recently. In press statements published in Aden, al-Bid said that by signing the document his party supports all the options of the Yemeni people. He called for Yemeni coherence for the sake of Yemen's unity and awakening. Al-Bid accused some parties of trying to create an atmosphere that would prevent him from signing, adding that the signing will take place as soon as possible. He said what counts is not the signing of the document, but its implementation.
