BFN [From the "National Radio News"] [Text] Since 21 June, Vice Foreign Minister Isabel Allende has been in Austria, where she has met with several politicians and members of parliament. Cuban diplomatic sources in Vienna advised that Allende has met with the Austrian interim foreign minister, and with political, economic, and European integration officials. Allende also met with members of parliament and relayed to Heinz Fischer, president of the Nationalrat, Ricardo Alarcon's invitation to visit Cuba. Allende also held an affable meeting with members of the Cuban-Austrian Friendship Association.
