931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Deficit at Bullers reduced to Pounds 1.42m Bullers, the giftware, fine art and decorative products group, incurred losses of Pounds 1.42m over the 12 months to June 30. The outcome - which came on turnover of Pounds 2.43m - compared with a deficit of Pounds 8.22m for the previous 18 month period, restated for FRS 3, some Pounds 6.2m of which related to discontinued operations. Sales last time amounted to Pounds 10.8m, including Pounds 5.68m from discontinued activities. Losses per share were reduced to 1.22p (12.49p). Lord Eden is standing down as chairman of the group in favour of Mr David Cunningham, chairman of Waterglade International.
