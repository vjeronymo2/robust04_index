Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Mar (MENA)--An official Palestinian source said tonight that the Fatah delegation that visited the Israeli Knesset today consisted of Arabs from inside the occupied territories and was led by lawyer Ziyyad Abu-Zayyad. The source said the delegation had a dialogue with an Israeli Labor Party delegation led by Ya'el Dayan, the party's official spokesman, with a view to ending the current crisis faced by the peace negotiations. The source said that members of the Palestinian delegation stressed that peace and settlements do not go together, and Israel must realize that the PLO postponed the peace talks because of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. The Israeli Labor Party, led by Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin, had invited the Palestinian delegation to visit the Knesset.
