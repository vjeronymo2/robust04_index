December 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Coopers & Lybrand Expands: Coopers & Lybrand, the international accounting and consulting firm, and Gelfand, Rennert & Feldman, a business management firm for the entertainment industry, announced that they have merged. Gelfand, Rennert & Feldman will operate as a division of Coopers & Lybrand and will continue to have headquarters in Los Angeles.
