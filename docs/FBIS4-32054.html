BFN [Text] A sit-in began this morning at the board offices of the Lucchini Steelworks in Warsaw. Jacek Gasiorowski, cochairman of the Solidarity strike committee, said this form of protest will last until the board begins negotiations with the strikers. The dispute at the Lucchini-Warszawa Steelworks began on 9 June. The steelworkers are demanding a pay raise of 30 percent and that modernization of the steelworks be undertaken by the Italian owner. The strike has been organized jointly by Solidarity and the National Trade Union Alliance [OPZZ].
