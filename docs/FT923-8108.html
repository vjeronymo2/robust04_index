920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG remained under pressure as De Beers fell R5.90 or 8.6 per cent to R62.60 following Tuesday's announcement that it may cut its final dividend. The overall index fell 101 to 3,208 while industrials lost 110 to 4,049 and the gold index shed 15 to 979.
