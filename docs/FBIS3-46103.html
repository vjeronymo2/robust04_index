Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 19 (XINHUA) -- A financial group consisting of several major specialized banks in China will provide a 40 million U.S.-dollar unsecured loan for the basic construction of the capital city, according to a contract signed here today. The five-year loan will be jointly provided by the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, the Bank of China, the Everbright Bank of China, the Citic Industrial Bank, the Beijing branch of the Bank of Communications, the Everbright International Trust and Investment Corporation and Huaxia Bank. The loan will be entrusted to the Investment Corporation for Economic Development of Beijing, which was established for funding the basic construction of Beijing.
