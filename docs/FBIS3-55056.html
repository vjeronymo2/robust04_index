Palchevskiy: "One More Step Toward Unification of Left Forces"] [Text] One more step toward the unification of left forces has been made in Grodno, where a unification plenum of the oblast branches of the Communist Party, the People's Movement of Belarus, the Movement for Democracy, Social Progress, and Justice [DSPS], was held. Following speeches on the socioeconomic situation in the Republic delivered by N. Prisada, K. Lisitsin, and S. Posokhov, secretaries of the Communist Party Oblast Committee, the plenum adopted a resolution on the collective joining of the National Movement of Belarus by the oblast branches of the Communist Party and DSPS.
