940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Smaller Kleinwort Smaller Companies Investment Trust reported net assets down 13 per cent from 168.1p to 145.7p in the six months to July 31. Net revenue dropped from a restated Pounds 231,000 to Pounds 149,000 giving earnings per share of 1.13p (1.75p). The interim dividend has been cut to 1.1p (1.5p).
