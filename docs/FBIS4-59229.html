BFN [Text] The security forces have announced that they managed to kill eight members of armed groups and arrest 30 others between 6-9 May in Algiers, Blida, Batna, Boumerdes, Ain Defla, Sidi Bel Abbes, Mascara, Skikda, and Bordj Bou Arreridj. The same source added that during these operations the security forces recovered a quantity of weapons.
