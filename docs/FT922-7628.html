920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / Channel ferry route to reopen THE Newhaven-Dieppe cross-Channel car ferry route, closed by its French operator at the end of last month, is to be reopened by Sealink Stena Line of the UK. The two French vessels that served the route have been chartered by Sealink and will be put back into service - one of them next Thursday and the second a week later.
