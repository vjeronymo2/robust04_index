BFN [Text] Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih this evening phoned His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad to congratulate him on the blessed 'Id al-Adha. They also discussed Yemeni developments. His excellency the president said he hoped fighting among the Yemeni brothers would be brought to an end and that the country would emerge from this sorry state as soon as possible.
