Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Munition depots of the Tajikistan Defense Ministry in the settlement of Aini, 6 km outside Dushanbe, came under fire last night and in early hours of Monday. A source close to military circles told INTERFAX that the depots stored weapons, fuels and lubricants as well as munition. There were no casualties or damage. Appropriate agencies started an investigation. The area of the 12th and 13th posts of the Moskovskiy border guard unit were fired at with heavy machine guns and rocket shells from inside Afghanistan at 16:30 on March 20. The border guards returned fire and wiped out the enemy firing positions. There were no casualties among the Russian border guards. Armed groups and hardware of Tajik opposition were seen to move actively in the Afghan area opposite the 1st post of the Pyanj border guard unit.
