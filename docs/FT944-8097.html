941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Bombardier - Contracts & ventures Bombardier, the Canadian aerospace and transit equipment group, will supply 66 rail cars worth CDollars 180m (Dollars 133.3m) for a new 170km intercity connection in Guanajuato state, Mexico. A Mexican private sector group will finance, build and operate the CDollars 840m system.
