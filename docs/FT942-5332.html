940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / Wellcome finance director to go Wellcome, the pharmaceuticals group, yesterday announced that its finance director was leaving 'to pursue other opportunities'. In a statement the company said that Mr John Precious would continue in his present capacity while a successor was sought. Mr Precious is also non-executive chairman of Celsis International, a biotechnology company with whom Wellcome recently agreed a collaborative deal to look at the potential of using Celsis's technology in Wellcome's research. Wellcome's share price dropped 13 to 543p on the news.
