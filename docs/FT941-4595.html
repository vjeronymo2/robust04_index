940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: WMI sells 40% of Spanish arm Waste Management International, the UK quoted arm of the US group WMX Technologies, is selling 40 per cent of its Spanish subsidiary to one of the country's leading financial institutions for Pta800m (Pounds 3.85m). The company said the cash would be used to expand Inusa, the subsidiary, in advance of the long-awaited increase in investment in environmental infrastructure. The buyer of the 40 per cent holding is Argentaria Corporacion Bancaria de Espana, the former state-owned institution which was partially privatised last year.
