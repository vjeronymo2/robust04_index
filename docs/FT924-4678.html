921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Regina back into profitable trading A fall in sales and increased exceptional charges lifted the pre-tax loss of Regina, the international brand leader in Royal Jelly products, to Pounds 1.23m in the year ended August 31. Turnover came to Pounds 1.91m, compared to Pounds 3.21m in the previous 14 months. The previous loss was Pounds 505,000. However, the group had traded profitably in September and October. Sales in November were ahead of budget and a profit was forecast for the month. Exceptional charges came to Pounds 490,000 (Pounds 252,000). Losses were 0.91p (0.29p).
