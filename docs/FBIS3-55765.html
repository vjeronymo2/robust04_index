Language: English Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Kiev January 11 TASS -- Ukraine's central electoral commission examined applications and other documents submitted by political parties seeking registration for the upcoming parliamentary elections in the republic. The commission passed a decision to register the Constitutional Democratic Party, the Organization of Ukrainian Nationalists in Ukraine and the Ukrainian Peasants' Democratic Party, which intend to nominate their candidates for the elections of Ukrainian People's Deputies scheduled for March 27, 1994.
