BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received gifts from the National Executive Committee of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Turkey, the Maritime Territorial Committee, the Khavarovsk Territorial Committee and the Amur Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation. The gifts were handed to officials concerned.
