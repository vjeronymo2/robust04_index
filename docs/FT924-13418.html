921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / International Company News: News Corp priced NEWS CORP, the media group, yesterday said its 40m share issue would be priced at ADollars 24.10 a share, of which 18m will be American depository shares (ADSs), Reuter reports. The ADS issue will be priced at USDollars 34.83. The issue will be in four tranches worldwide and handled by Merrill Lynch, the US brokerage.
