Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The IPTN [Nusantara Aviation Industries] will begin production this year of the N-250 aircraft, powered by turbo-prop engines. The aircraft, suitable for short flights, was designed by Indonesian technicians. Research and Technology Minister B.J. Habibie disclosed this in Jakarta yesterday when he met with the Indonesian ambassadors-designate and consuls general-designate to several countries. The minister said IPTN is planning to produce one N-250 plane every week, adding that IPTN will produce wide-bodied jets by the year 2006. [passage omitted]
