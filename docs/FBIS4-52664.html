BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 12 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, today sent a message of greetings to Henri Konan Bedie upon his election as president of the Central Committee of the Democratic Party of Cote d'Ivoire. Comrade Kim Il-song wished him greater success in his responsible work for the strengthening and development of the party and for the prosperity of the Cote d'Ivoire people.
