BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- A concert of Koreans at home and abroad for great national unity was held at the Yun I-sang Concert Hall in Pyongyang on April 21. Korean artistes from Japan and China, artistes of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification and artistes in Pyongyang put on stage a colorful program including mixed choruses "Song of 10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation" and "We Sing of Our Happiness in Having the Great Leader". The performers emotionally sang of the unanimous desire of the Korean nation to live happily in one territory after achieving the reunification of the country by concerted efforts of the nation.
