920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / Observer: Rare party Worldwide recession has taken its toll of the IMF annual meeting. Veterans of the event complain that the parties are far less ritzy than they used to be - and there are far fewer of them. Hence it's good to see that American Express can still be relied on in time of cocktail crisis. The queue for its thrash on Monday evening stretched a quarter of a mile. Indeed, canapes had to be handed out on the streets of Washington to sustain the hungry bankers during their long wait.
