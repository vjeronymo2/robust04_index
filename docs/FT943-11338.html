940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / Craxi sentenced to 8 1/2 years for bank fraud The former Socialist premier Bettino Craxi was sentenced to 8 1/2 years in jail for fraudulent bankruptcy in the 1982 collapse of the Banco Ambrosiano. A similar sentence was handed out to Claudio Martelli, former Socialist justice minister and one-time heir to Mr Craxi. Since February Mr Craxi has been living at his seaside villa in Tunisia. He still has the right to appeal.
