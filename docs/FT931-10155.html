930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Waste-dumping tax proposed A NEW levy on dumping waste in landfills would encourage more incineration and recycling of rubbish, according to a report commissioned by the government. The report, by Coopers & Lybrand for the Department of the Environment, says a levy on landfills - licensed pits for dumping rubbish - would be feasible. Its main effect would be to increase the amount of waste sent to incinerators, but in the long term it would make recycling more commercially attractive. Landfill Pricing: Correcting Possible Market Distortions HMSO, Pounds 12.
