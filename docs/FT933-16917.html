930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Observer: Out of order Britain's coppers might see BAT chairman Sir Patrick Sheehy as taking a liberty in proposing an end to the tradition of jobs for life for the men and women in blue. Although other ranks in BAT tend to retire at 60, the 62 3/4 -year-old Sheehy has already twice delayed his departure after 43 years with the company and means to hang on for two or three more.
