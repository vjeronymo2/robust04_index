Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 4 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Zhu Rongji, concluding his official good-will visit to Japan, returned here by special plane this afternoon. Greeting him at the airport were Chinese State Councillor and Secretary-General of the State Council Luo Gan, minister in charge of the state planning commission Chen Jinhua, Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu and Japanese Ambassador to China Michihiko Kunihiro.
