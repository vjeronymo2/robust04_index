BFN [Text] Tunis, 28 Jun (MENA) -- Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has issued a decree forming a committee chaired by Yahya Khalaf, head of the PLO's Cultural Department, to travel to the Palestinian autonomy region to learn about the situation there at first hand and prepare a report to be presented to the Palestinian leadership. Yahya Khalaf stated that the Palestinian leadership will study this report in light of which it will make its final decision on who should return to Gaza and Jericho immediately and who should wait until special arrangements to return are made. He said the committee, which includes Jamil Shihadah, secretary general of the Palestinian Teachers Union, and Rawhi Futuhi, secretary of the Palestinian popular organizations, will travel to Amman later today en route to Jericho and then the Gaza Strip to prepare the report and present it to the Palestinian leadership within three days.
