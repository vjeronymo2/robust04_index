Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting of the UN General Assembly working group, Ukrainian Envoy to UN Oleksandr Mosych addressed the issue of just representation in the UN Security Council, and expanding its membership. He emphasized that the current composition of the UN Security Council, as well as its operation under the current multitude of work, requires that its number be increased, and that new regional circumstances and new participants in international relations be considered. The implementation of this proposal would significantly improve the productivity level of the council that is a body acting in the name of all UN members, according to its statute responsibilities. At the same time, it is necessary to preserve all [words indistinct], to ensure that it immediately reacts to situations that require intervention, examining them, and adopting appropriate decisions. It is important to find compromises to resolve questions of membership in the Security Council, which would be acceptable to all countries, including the council's permanent members.
