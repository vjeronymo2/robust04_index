940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Smaller bounce at Abbey National - Other Companies On Tuesday, Abbey National is likely to show a steadier increase in 1993 profits than the UK's other banks because it does not have the same exposure to past bad debts from corporate lending. With the pre-tax figure forecast to rise to about Pounds 700m, from Pounds 564m, most attention will focus on the dividend increase, which some analysts are expecting to be generous.
