September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LIFE WITHOUT KFAC: WHEN ROCK ROLLS OVER BACH

KFAC was my favorite radio station for 38 years, until Heifetz bought it in 1986 and the programming became worse and worse. Never had I heard so many dull flute compositions.

Given the fact that she is the daughter-in-law of violinist Jascha Heifetz, I do not understand how she could preside over the decline of good music.

ROBERT C. SNYDER

West Covina
