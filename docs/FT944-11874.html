941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Fed intervenes for a second day to prop up dollar The US Federal Reserve stepped into the money markets for the second day running yesterday to buy dollars and support the flagging US currency. Analysts said the Fed's intervention of about Dollars 400m (Pounds 244m) had bought short-term relief for the dollar, but today's publication of the monthly employment report presents an important test for the currency.
