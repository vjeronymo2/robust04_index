920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / BCCI deal moves closer TOUCHE Ross, the provisional liquidators of Bank of Credit and Commerce International, has moved closer to gaining complete approval for the Dollars 1.7bn compensation package negotiated with the Abu Dhabi majority shareholders. A court in Grand Cayman which has jurisdiction over BCCI Overseas, one of BCCI's two banking arms, approved the package on Thursday, endorsing an earlier English High Court decision.
