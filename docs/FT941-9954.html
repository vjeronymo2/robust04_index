940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Jersey Phoenix earnings rise For the half year ended December 31 available revenue of Jersey Phoenix Trust totalled Pounds 472,975 for earnings per share of 3.05p. That compared with Pounds 400,356 and 2.63p respectively for the corresponding 1992 period. A same-again second interim dividend of 1.25p makes 2.5p (same) to date.
