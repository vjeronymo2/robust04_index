August 15, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Charlotte Witkind, a New York Yankees limited partner, said that George Steinbrenner's son, Hank, may not want to succeed his father.

The 18 partners are scheduled to meet today and are expected to vote for a new general partner.

Since reports began circulating that Hank Steinbrenner doesn't want the job, there has been speculation that Marvin Goldklang, a limited partner who also owns minor league teams, might take over. Goldklang declined comment.
