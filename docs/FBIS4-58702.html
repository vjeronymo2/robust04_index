BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 May (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has issued an order for the Palestinian national police and security forces to prepare to move immediately to take up their positions in Gaza and Jericho. This was stated to MENA by Zuhdi al-Qidrah, acting Palestinian ambassador in Cairo and Fatah's representative. He said that 'Arafat issued this order following the signing of the agreement on the implementation of Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho in Cairo yesterday.
