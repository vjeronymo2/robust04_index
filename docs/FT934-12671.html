931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / World Trade News: Siemens expands in China SIEMENS, the German electrical and electronics group, is expanding in the Chinese power plant market through a joint venture to make instrumentation and control equipment there. The venture, Siemens Power Plant Automation, will be 60 per cent-owned by Siemens, with the balance held by Nanjing Electric Power Automation Equipment General Factory, controlled by the Chinese ministry for the electric power industry. The venture will be based in in Nanjing, 150 miles from Shanghai, and will employ about 100 people.
