BFN [Text] The security forces are continuing the campaign of detention against Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activists in the Janin area in the wake of the terrorist acts in 'Afula and Hadera, which resulted in the death and injury of some Israeli civilians. Our correspondent Yoni Ben-Menahem reports that so far the security forces have detained more than 150 persons in the Janin area on suspicion of being Hamas activists.
