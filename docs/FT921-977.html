920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / Gorbachev in cash probe row Russian prosecutors have reportedly asked Mr Mikhail Gorbachev to remain in Moscow next month for questioning about the finances of the suspended Communist party, although a spokesman for the former Soviet president denied the report, Reuter reports from Moscow. Many members of the party's former politburo have already been questioned, but a spokesman for Mr Gorbachev said: 'There is no question of any measures of a restrictive nature against Gorbachev.'
