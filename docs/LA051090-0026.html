May 10, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

'Nightmare' Suit Thrown Out: The producer of the "Nightmare on Elm Street" movies won a court order dismissing a $5-million lawsuit by an investor for a share of profits from the films. The lawsuit had been filed by Smart Egg Productions, an investor and joint-venture partner in the successful horror series. Los Angeles Superior Court Judge Miriam Vogel agreed with New Line Cinema Corp. that the suit should have been filed in New York. GREG BRAXTON
