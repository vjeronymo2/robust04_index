Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 8 (XINHUA) -- More than 300 overseas reporters attended a reception held here this evening by the Press Center for the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress (NPC) and the Second Session of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). By this afternoon, over 1,400 reporters have checked in to cover the two events, setting a record. Among them, over 160 have come from Taiwan, Hong Kong and Macao and more than 200 are foreign reporters. The Second Session of the CPPCC Eighth National Committee opened here today and the NPC Session is due to open on Thursday [10 March]. Zhou Jue, spokesman for NPC, pledged to provide convenient conditions for reporters to cover the meetings, including interviews with deputies to NPC and members of the CPPCC National Committee.
