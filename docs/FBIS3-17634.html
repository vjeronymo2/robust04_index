Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 465] [Text] Vilnius, March 25, ELTA -- Lithuanian Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys received a telegram from U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher in which congratulations were expressed for Lithuanian and Polish foreign ministers on signing the good neighbourhood treaty. The telegram says that hopefully the treaty will become the background of cooperation between Lithuania and Poland and will help to achieve stability and harmony in the region. This document confirms that the relations will be developed between two equal partners. This sets a good example for the whole world, the countries of the European Security and Cooperation Conference in particular. Despite the complexity of the issues considered, Lithuanian determination to achieve the pragmatic compromises determined the success of the negotiations, the telegram says.
