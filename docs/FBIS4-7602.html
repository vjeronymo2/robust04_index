BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 1 (AFP) -- Seventeen Moslems and two Croats have been murdered in the Serb-controlled western Bosnian town of Prijedor in the past two days, Kris Janowski, spokesman for the UN High Commissioner for Refugees, said Friday [1 April]. Janowski said he had received the figures from the Moslem humanitarian organisation Merhamet, but that a UNHCR official who had visited the town Friday said the figures were "probably right."
