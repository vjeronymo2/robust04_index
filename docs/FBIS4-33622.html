BFN [Excerpt] The official bloc of the CCD [Democratic Constituent Congress] on 27 May approved the bill that includes special, agricultural, and hydroelectric projects in the privatization process, disregarding the opposition's warning. Therefore, large hydroelectric projects such as Olmos, Chavimochic, Majes, Tinajones, and Jequetepeque-Zana; the San Lorenzo irrigation project; and the Huinco and Marcapomacocha Machu Pichu hydroelectric plants, among others, can be sold. The organizations, production units, and other projects under the state's responsibility also can be sold. The new law that changes Legislative Decree No. 674 for Promoting Private Investments in State Enterprises also grants full powers to the Privatization Commission (Copri) to carry out the auctions.
