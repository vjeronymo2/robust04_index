940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The currencies of the ERM grid converged during the week, with attention focused outside the system on the dollar, sterling and the yen. The D-Mark held its position as the third strongest currency after the Bundesbank decided against a cut in interest rates while the Belgian franc showed little reaction to the resignation of three socialist politicians from the ruling coalition.
