931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: CIA and CDP media services CIA Group, the UK's largest independent buyer of advertising media, and CDP Europe have formed a 50/50 joint venture to operate as a full media services company. CDP, whose UK operations centre on the London-based advertising agency Collett Dickenson Pearce & Partners, has Dentsu of Japan as its financial and business partner. The new company will be called the cdp media company. Mr Mike Elms, CIA's UK chief executive, will become chairman. Mr John Wilson will be managing director. Media billing for the company is in excess of Pounds 50m.
