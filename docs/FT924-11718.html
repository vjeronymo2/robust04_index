921024 FT 24 OCT 92 / Tram venture A PRIVATE-sector consortium has joined London Transport and Croydon borough council in their plans to build a Pounds 140m tram link between Croydon and Wimbledon in south London. Tarmac Construction of the UK, AEG Rail Systems of Germany and Transdev of France will do detailed work on the scheme in the hope that the government will help to finance it.
