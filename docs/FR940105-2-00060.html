National Institutes of Health National Cancer Institute; Notice of Meeting of the Subcommittee To Evaluate the National Cancer Program, National Cancer Advisory Board Pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given of the meeting of the Subcommittee to Evaluate the National Cancer Program, National Cancer Advisory Board, National Cancer Institute, National Institutes of Health on January 20&hyph;22, 1994 at the San Diego Hilton, 1175 East Mission Bay Drive, San Diego, California 92109. The meeting will be open to the public from 3 p.m. to recess on January 20; from 9 a.m. to recess January 21, and from 9 a.m. until adjournment on January 22.
