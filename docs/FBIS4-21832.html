BFN [Text] Molotov cocktails were hurled at the True Path Party [TPP] and the Nationalist Action Party [NAP] election offices situated in two different quarters of Adana this evening. One person was killed in the incidents. According to information obtained by a Turkish Radio and Television correspondent, the Molotov cocktail hurled at the TPP election offices in Adana's Dumlupinar quarter killed Serdar Aydemir, a 12-year-old passerby. It has been reported that damage was caused in both the TPP and NAP offices. Meanwhile, a bomb hurled at the NAP election communications office in Istanbul's [name indistinct] quarter this evening injured five persons. The injured are being treated at a private hospital and at the Istanbul University Medical Department Hospital. The police have begun an investigation into the incident.
