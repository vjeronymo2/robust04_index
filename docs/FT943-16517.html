940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Kleeneze jumps 40% to Pounds 0.85m Strong growth in its core home shopping businesses enabled Kleeneze to report a 40 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 606,000 to Pounds 849,000 for the six months to February 28, despite restructuring costs of Pounds 404,000. The costs related to the retail business which also incurred operating losses of Pounds 324,000. Group operating profits were Pounds 1.32m (Pounds 621,000). Turnover was Pounds 40.3m, against Pounds 29.2m, which included Pounds 2.87m from discontinued activities. Earnings per share came out at 4.71p (3.17p). No interim dividend is declared but the company intends increasing its final dividend from last year's 1p.
