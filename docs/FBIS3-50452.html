Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Chile monitored through 30 December. The profits of the Copper Corporation's Chuquicamata Division will amount to $385 million this year, a drop of 43 percent compared with 1992. Production this year will total 615,000 metric tons, which means that the company fulfilled its production plan by 102.3 percent. Investments were $207 million compared with 1992 when they were $206 million. (Santiago EL MERCURIO in Spanish 17 Dec 93 pp B1-B20) The Institute for Export Promotion has reported that from January to October Chilean exports to Europe totaled $2.179 billion and those to the Middle East totalled $70.8 million, a drop of 19 percent and 3.2 percent respectively compared with the same period last year. Exports to Latin America totaled $1.560 billion, an increase of 12 percent during the same period. (Santiago EL MERCURIO in Spanish 14 Dec 93 Economic and Business Section p B2)
