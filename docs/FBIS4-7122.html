BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received letters of thanks from the senior vice-president of the U.S. CNN and the Japanese NHK news coverage group that had visited Korea with former heads of state and government and politicians of different countries. The letters dated April 19 expressed sincere thanks to Comrade Kim Chong-il for inviting them to visit and report on the DPRK. Senior Vice-President of the U.S. CNN Eason Jordan in his letter of thanks said; "Our visit was extremely successful, especially our meeting and coverage of President Kim Il-song. We owe a great debt of gratitude to you". He hoped that through coverage and report the CNN has made some contribution to international understanding of Korea and to the cause of world peace and laid the groundwork for a long-term and positive relationship with Korea. The letter expressed once again thanks to consideration and support of Comrade Kim Chong-il. The Japanese NHK news coverage group in the letter of thanks said that the invitation of the NHK coverage group representing Japan and CNN coverage group representing the United States under the present rigorous situation surrounding the Korean peninsula today was made by the wise decision of Comrade Kim Chong-il. The letter continues: As the old saying goes, "seeing is believing." The true looks of the DPRK which no one can know without visiting it and meeting people have been conveyed to the world, I believe. NHK will in the future, too, make efforts for the improvement of the DPRK-Japan relations and for peace and prosperity of Northeast Asia on the basis of the deep cooperative relations with the DPRK hitherto. The letter hoped that Comrade Kim Chong-il would understand such a stance of report of NHK and give guidance for the establishment of eternal relations between NHK and the DPRK. It sincerely wished President Kim Il-song and Secretary Kim Chong-il good health and a long life.
