BFN [Unattributed report in the "By Telex" column] [Text] Deputy Z. Vlcek has become a new member of the Left Bloc deputies' club and the Left Bloc Party [LBS]. LBS Deputy Chairman J. Ortman announced this at a news conference on Thursday [12 May]. Vlcek stood for election for the Association for the Republic-Republican Party of Czechoslovakia and transferred to the Movement for Self-Governing Democracy-Society of Moravia and Silesia [HSDMS] but, since the HSDMS was established, had never joined the club.
