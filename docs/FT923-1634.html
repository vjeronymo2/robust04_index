920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / World News In Brief: British woman 'stabbed to death' One of two bodies found in an Australian forest has been identified as Joanne Walters, 22, of Maesteg, Glamorgan, who died of stab wounds. The other, which had suffered gunshot wounds, was thought to be that of her friend Caroline Clarke, from Slaley, Northumberland.
