BFN [Text] Police Commissioner Asaf Hefetz today disclosed plans to set up a guard unit to secure the passage from Jericho to Gaza. The unit, called Safe Passage, will daily guard one of the four roads on which Palestinians will drive from Gaza to Jericho. The unit consists of 400 border policemen: two companies of policemen will drive along the road, and two other companies will serve as reserves and will be based in Qiryat Gat. Our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin ordered the establishment of this unit to thwart terror attacks, arms smuggling, and provocations by Jews against Arabs.
