Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report by Norbert Rief: "Austria To Train Inspectors for Chemical Weapons"] [Excerpt] Vienna -- Austria will now once more participate in an international peacekeeping program. In the Seibersdorf research center, in Lower Austria, UN personnel of the Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW) will be trained this year; the objective is that they can control civilian chemical plants. The OPCW inspectors should be able to find out whether chemical weapons are being or can be produced in chemical plants. [passage omitted]
