Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] As Interfax learned at the press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh Defense Army, a relative calm on the Karabakh front established in recent days was violated on Saturday morning. According to the press service, concentration of Azeri troops on the southeastern part of the front undertook an offensive in the direction of the regional center Fizuli. However, by the afternoon Nagorno-Karabakh defense units managed to repel all attacks of the Azeri Army inflicting it considerable losses. As of 15:00 fighting on that part of the front continued. There have been no major changes in other parts of the front. In the meantime, the Azeri Defense Ministry reports that yesterday Armenian artillery shelled Azeri positions in the Fizuli and Agdam parts of the front. There have been no casualties. Damage is being estimated.
