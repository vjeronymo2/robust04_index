Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The decision on the political amnesty of the SCSE [State Committee for the State of Emergency] members and the participants in the October events is completely in keeping with the Constitution from a judicial point of view. This was stated by Aleksandr Yakovlev, the official representative of the Russian president at the Federal Assembly today. However, he noted, the political and social aspects in this case are more important than the judicial one. He thinks that an act on amnesty may create a false illusion of hopes for civic peace.
