930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Westminster Health Care allocations The public element of the flotation of Westminster Health Care, the nursing home group, was oversubscribed 4.8 times. The float involved the sale of 25.6m shares at 260p - 51 per cent of the total. All were placed by BZW with institutions, but 6.4m shares were available to the public. After allocating 223,000 shares to preferential applicants, the balance was scaled down. Those applying for 200-800 shares get 200; 1,000 get 225; 1,500 get 250; 2,000 get 275; 2,500 get 300; 3,000 get 325; and 3,500 and over get 10 per cent of the application. Dealings are expected to start on April 15.
