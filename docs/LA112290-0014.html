BOOKS FOR THE BORROWING ACROSS THE NORTH COUNTY

NORTH COUNTY LIBRARIES

Three cities in North County maintain their own libraries. In addition, there are San Diego County Library branches in 14 area communities. All are members of the Serra Cooperative Library System.

Each library maintains separate operating hours, but all are closed on Sundays and major holidays.
