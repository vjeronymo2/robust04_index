BFN [Text] We present an order by the Georgian interior minister. In accordance with the decision made at the joint meeting of the collegiums of the Georgian Interior Minister, the Security Service, the Prosecutor's Office, and the Tbilisi Mayor's office I order: 1. Major General Davit Zeikidze, the deputy interior minister and the head of the Main Directorate of Tbilisi Police, be relieved of his post for organizing a raid on the Georgian Supreme Council on 31 March 1994. 2. It will be acknowledged that a legal proceedings have been instituted and the investigation has been started by the Georgian Prosecutor's Office in connection with the above-mentioned event. 3. Major General Demur Mickadze will be appointed head of the Principal Police Department of Tbilisi. 4. Major General Roman Gventsadze will be appointed Georgian deputy interior minister. [Signed] Shota Kviraia, Interior Minister of the Republic of Georgia.
