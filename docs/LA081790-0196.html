August 17, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

STUDENTS IN INDIA BLOCK OIL PUMPING

Student protesters have widened a campaign to obstruct oil production in the Indian state of Assam after New Delhi threatened tough action against them, an official said Thursday.

Assam's Home Minister Bhrigu Kumar Phukan said members of the All Assam Students Union had formed a human chain around all six crude-pumping stations in the northeastern state, preventing employees from working.

The students want the government to implement a 1985 accord promising deportation of illegal Bangladeshi immigrants and industrial development of resource-rich Assam.
