BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song's famous work "Enhancing the Role of the Popular Masses is the Guarantee for Victory in the Cause of Independence" was published in booklet by the Grafica Europa publishing house of Portugal, on the occasion of his birthday. The preface of the booklet says: President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is recognised by the world people as the leader who has adorned the current century with unheard of great traces which will remain in the history of mankind. President Kim Il-song is respected by the entire Korean people as well as hundreds of millions of people all over the world as he has devoted his 80 odd years to the people as their son.
