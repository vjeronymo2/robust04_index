Language: Spanish Article Type:CSO [Text] Tegucigalpa--The Liberal Party (PL) will be entitled to 71 deputy seats, the National Party (PN) 55 seats, and the Innovation and Unity Party (PINU) two seats, according to the calculations of Radio America, whose modern methods have proven to be the most efficient. The parties' relative positions in Congress were reversed, since the National Party has 71 seats in the current session, the Liberals 55, and the PINU two, while the Christian Democrats still have no seats at all.
