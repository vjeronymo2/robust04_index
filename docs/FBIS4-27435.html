BFN [Text] Masayoshi Takemura, head of Sakigake [Harbinger], held a news conference in Matsue City on 29 May, in which he commented on the possibility of the cabinet resigning en masse to pave the way for the Social Democratic Party of Japan's [SDPJ] return to the coalition government. He said that his response to SDPJ Secretary General Wataru Kubo's remark seeking the voluntary resignation of the cabinet was, "The SDPJ's return to the coalition by resigning en masse is improbable." This is an attempt to restrain ongoing moves in the SDPJ.
