BFN [Resignation statement of Prime Minister Redha Malek, read by presidency spokesman Mihoub Mihoubi] [Text] This morning State President Liamine Zeroual appointed Mokhdad Sefi as prime minister, replacing Redha Malek who earlier today presented his resignaton and that of his government to the state president. The resignation statement was read this afternoon before the national press by Mihoub Mihoubi, the official spokesman for the presidency. [Begin Mihoubi recording] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. This morning President Liamine Zeroual met with Redha Malek, who turned in the resignation of his government. The president accepted this resignation and took the opportunity to praise the efforts made by Redha Malek and the government members which he led with all dedication and self-denial. Thank you. [end recording] A short while after this the official spokesman for the presidency presented a statement on the appointment of Mokhdad Sefi as prime minister with a biography of the latter. [Resume Mihoubi recording] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. President Liamine Zeroual today received Mokhdad Sefi whom he appointed as prime minister in replacement of Redha Malek. Mokhdad Sefi was born on 21 April 1940. He is married and has four children. He is a former student in the Shakyh Larbi Tebessi school and Saint-Augustin secondary school in Annaba. He has a degree in physics from Algiers University and has made advanced studies in physical chemistry in the universities of Algiers and Paris. He obtained a diploma of a mechanical and electrical engineer in Paris, and has occupied several functions including: engineer of gas works in SONALGAZ, the national company for electricity and gas; director of industrial projects at the Ministry of Industry; director general of coordinaton and control at the Ministry of Trade; secretary general of the Ministry of Public Works; secretary general of the Ministry of Finance; head of a mission at the premiership; head of the prime minister's office; and finally minister of equipment. The new prime minister is due to meet the national press this afternoon, and afterward there will be a handover of functions ceremony between former Prime Minister Redha Malek and his successor Mokhdad Sefi. [end recording] [passage omitted]
