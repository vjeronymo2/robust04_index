920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: Sanyo to reduce workforce by 2,000 people SANYO Electric plans to reduce its workforce by about 2,000 people by March 1996, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The company said it would not lay off any of its 30,000 workers, but would allow numbers to fall by not hiring employees to replace those who retired. It plans to cut mainly at production units.
