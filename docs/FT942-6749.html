940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Millwall Holdings Millwall Holdings, the USM-quoted football company which moved to a new multi-purpose stadium last August, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.37m for the six months to November 30 1993, up from Pounds 383,000. Total turnover was Pounds 2.2m (Pounds 1m). Transfer fees in the period were Pounds 1.98m (Pounds 1.38m). Earnings per share were 0.49p (0.34p).
