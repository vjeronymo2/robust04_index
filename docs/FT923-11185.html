920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Hatton denies eight charges MR Derek Hatton, former deputy leader of Liverpool City Council, yesterday pleaded not guilty to eight charges of conspiracy to defraud the council. He appeared at Stafford Crown Court with six others, all facing various charges of conspiracy to defraud the council. The case was adjourned to Liverpool Crown Court on October 14 for further legal discussions.
