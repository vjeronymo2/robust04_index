Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, December 23 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In accordance with the outcome of the 11th session of the Mongolian-Korean Intergovernmental Commission on Economic Cooperation held in Seoul there has been reached an agreement on a creation of a number of joint ventures in Mongolia including taxi parks, restaurants, shops and enterprises for producing glass and processing leather. In the composition of the Mongolian delegation besides the government officials there were participating also over 20 representatives of private sector who have established business contacts with branches of the "Daewu" corporation and other known companies, banking and trade organizations of Korea.
