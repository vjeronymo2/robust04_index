920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / International Company News: Banks to form finance joint venture CONTINENTAL BANK of Chicago and Credit Lyonnais, the French state bank, yesterday said they had established a joint venture aimed at providing finance to US companies with at least Dollars 125m of receivables, writes Alan Friedman. The new venture, to be known as Financial Asset Securitisation Corporation (FAST), is a special purpose vehicle that expects to provide up to Dollars 2bn of total financing for corporate customers over the next two years.
