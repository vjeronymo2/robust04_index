931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: London & Assoc improves to Pounds 713,000 London & Associated Investment Trust, the property investment company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 713,000 for the first half of 1993, against Pounds 540,000 restated for FRS 3. Gross income came to Pounds 2.2m (Pounds 1.97m). The property portfolio, which is spread throughout the UK, was expanded by the Pounds 4.1m purchase of the Brunel Centre in Bletchley. The net interest charge rose to Pounds 641,000 (Pounds 552,000). Earnings per share were 0.64p (0.5p) and the interim dividend is a maintained 0.05p. Bisichi Mining, its 42 per cent-owned property and mining finance associate, reported static pre-tax profits of Pounds 102,000 (Pounds 106,000) for the same period. Earnings per share were 0.74p (0.81p).
