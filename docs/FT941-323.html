940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / International Company News: GM to take Dollars 758m charge General Motors said it would take an after-tax charge to consolidated net income of Dollars 758m, or Dollars 1.05 a share, due to its adoption of Financial Accounting Standard 112 relating to employee severence payments, AP-DJ reports. The company said it was adopting the accounting standard with effect from January 1 1994, but the was not expected to be material.
