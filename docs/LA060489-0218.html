June 4, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THESE 3 TV HOMES HELD MOST APPEALING

What do Hope and Mike of "Thirtysomething," Clair and the Huxtables of "The Cosby Show" and Pee Wee Herman have in common ?

According to a recent survey of interior designers and architects by Metropolis magazine, they have the most appealing television homes, based on comfort.

Based on questions to 300 design professionals, the TV home of "Thirtysomething" appeared most comfortable, followed by the Huxtables' and Pee Wee's playhouse.
