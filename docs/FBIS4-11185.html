BFN [Text] Mexico City, 23 Apr (NOTIMEX) -- In a message of condolence addressed to Richard Nixon's family, Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari today lamented the former U.S. President's death. Salinas expressed his condolences to Nixon's daughters Patricia Cox and Julie Eisenhower, the president's office reported in a press communique. Nixon, 81, died in a New York hospital on Friday night. On Monday, he suffered a cerebral hemorrhage, which kept him in a coma until his death. He had to step down in 1974 as the result of the Watergate scandal. Nixon's funeral will take place at Yorba Linda, his birthplace in California, next Wednesday.
