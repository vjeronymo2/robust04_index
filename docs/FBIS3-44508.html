Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 5 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Wenlong, who hijacked a plane from the Chinese mainland to Taiwan on June 4 last year, was sentenced to nine years' imprisonment by a local Taiwan court Tuesday [4 January]. According to Taiwan newspapers, the Taiwan procurators in Taoyuan also instituted proceedings yesterday against Zhang Hai, Han Shuxue and Li Xiangyu, who also hijacked mainland planes to Taiwan last year, and demanded imprisonment of over 12 years for them. Previously, some Taiwan media and public figures had urged that the hijackers should all be sent back to the mainland, where they should be sentenced to death or life-long prison terms without probation, according to China's laws.
