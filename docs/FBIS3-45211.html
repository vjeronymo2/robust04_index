Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, December 13 (XINHUA) -- Former U.S. President George Bush arrived here this evening to start a five-day goodwill visit to China. Bush's entourage includes General Brent Scowcroft, former assistant to the president for national security affairs. Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu and J. Stapleton Roy, U.S. Ambassador to China, greeted Bush and his party at the airport.
