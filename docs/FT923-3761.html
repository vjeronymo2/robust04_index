920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Dividend cut likely at Kleinwort Smaller Kleinwort Smaller Companies Investment Trust had a net asset value of 100.8p per share at July 31. The figure compared with 108.9p 12 months earlier and 113.7p at the trust's January year-end. Net revenue for the six months to end-July amounted to Pounds 305,000 (Pounds 333,000), equivalent to earnings of 2.31p (2.52p) per share. The interim dividend is held at 2p but directors said they did not expect to maintain last year's total of 4.6p. They hope to pay a final of at least 2p making 4p for the year. installation and a reduction in subscriber cancellation rates.
