Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Newspaper of the Airwaves" newscast] [Text] President Alberto Fujimori has just sworn in the members of the cabinet presided over by Foreign Relations Minister Efrain Goldenberg Schreiber, new president of the Council of Ministers, here at the El Dorado Room in the government palace. The new ministers are: Dr. Jorge Trelles, minister of education; Dr. Liliana Canales, minister of industry, tourism, integration and international trade negotiations minister; and Engineer Raul Vittor Alfaro, minister of the presidency. Other ministers who were ratified also took the oath of office. They are: Major General Victor Malca Villanueva, minister of defense; Maj. Gen. Juan Briones Davila, minister of the interior; Eng. Daniel Hokama, minister of energy and mines; Dr. Augusto Antonioli Vasquez, minister of labor and social promotion; Dr. Dante Cordova Blanco, minister of transportation, communications, housing, and construction; Dr. Jaimne Freundt-Thurne Oyanguren, minister of public health. The other ministers will take the oath of office at a later date because they are currently out of the country. They are: Eng. Jorge Camet, minister of economy and finance; Dr. Fernando Vega Santagadea, minister of justice; and Mr. Jaime Sobero Taira, minister of fisheries. Eng. Absalon Vasquez Villanueva, minister of agriculture, will also be sworn in at a later date because he is on a working tour of the interior.
