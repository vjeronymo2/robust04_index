940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Genesis Emerging net assets improve Genesis Emerging Markets Fund reported a net asset value per participating share of Dollars 31.62 (Pounds 21) at end-December, against Dollars 19.05 a year ago. The fund reported net deficit of Dollars 914,414 for the half year to December 31 compared with net income of Dollars 198,031. Losses per share were 12 cents, against earnings of 3.7 cents.
