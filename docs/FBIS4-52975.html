BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 16 (KCNA) -- Kim Yong-sun, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, on May 16 met and had a conversation with a delegation of the American Committee on Korea led by its executive director John M. Swomley. The head of the delegation said he was deeply moved by achievements made in Korea in political, economic, cultural and other domains. Pointing to distorted propaganda continued in the United States on the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, he said he could witness with his own eyes the Korean People's Army participating in the construction with shovels and helping farmers in their work in countryside, not preparing a war. He said he saw peaceful and happy Korean people's looks through Korean visit.
