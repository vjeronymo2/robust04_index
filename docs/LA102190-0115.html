October 21, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

In another nonleague game:

Fillmore 35, Bishop Diego 28 -- Tory Cabral's fifth touchdown run with 3:18 remaining was the difference as Fillmore rallied from a 28-7 deficit at San Marcos High.

Cabral, a senior running back, scored on runs of 16, four, 58, four and one yard and scored 33 points.

He ran for a game-high 221 yards in 29 carries.

Cabral also intercepted a Bishop Diego pass at the Fillmore 30 with 1:37 remaining to clinch the win for the Flashes, ranked No. 1 in the Southern Section Division VIII poll. Fillmore outscored Bishop Diego, 21-0, in the fourth quarter.

Fillmore quarterback Art Sosa completed five of 12 passes for 164 yards and a touchdown. Quarterback Richie Donati completed 15 of 38 for 279 yards for Bishop Diego (2-5). TIM PERERA
