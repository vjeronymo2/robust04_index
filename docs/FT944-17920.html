941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Pakistan in Dollars 7.5bn power deal Hopewell Holdings International, the Hong Kong-based group run by Mr Gordon Wu, is today due to sign deals worth approximately Dollars 7.5bn in Pakistan to build power plants. Mr Wu's Consolidated Electrical Power Asia may also secure contracts for transmission lines and oil supply facilities by the end of the year, officials say. Hopewell is to develop coalfields at Thar, in the southern province of Sind.
