920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / London house prices fall 5% HOUSE PRICES in London were 5 per cent lower at the end of last year than at the end of 1990, according to Nationwide Building Society's house price index. In the final quarter of last year the only area in which prices rose was the northern region, centred on Newcastle upon Tyne, where they were 1.5 per cent higher.
