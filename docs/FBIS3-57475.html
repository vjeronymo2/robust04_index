Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigoriy Arslanov, Vyacheslav Tomilin, Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Beijing, 27 Jan -- A ceremony of signing Russian-Chinese documents took place immediately after the talks between the Russian and Chinese foreign ministers at the Diaoyutai residence in Beijing had ended. In the (Fangfeiyuan) hall Andrey Kozyrev and Qian Qichen signed an intergovernmental agreement on crossing points on the Russian-Chinese border and a protocol on consultations between the Russian and Chinese foreign ministries.
