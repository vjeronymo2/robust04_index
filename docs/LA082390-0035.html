August 23, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: CORONADO

A 24-year-old man was in critical condition Wednesday night at UC San Diego Medical Center after his motorcycle hit the rear of a truck on the Coronado Bridge.

Noel Howard of Imperial Beach was riding west on the bridge about 8:10 a.m. Wednesday and apparently did not notice a Caltrans sign truck ahead of him, said a spokeswoman for the California Highway Patrol.

Howard drove into the truck, flew off his bike and slammed into the rear of the truck, according to a CHP report.
