BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 13 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The United Party for Mongolian Traditions has issued a statement addressing the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party (MPRP), the National Democratic Party and the Social Democratic Party. The statement draws their attention to the necessity for extracting lessons from the past reminding that frequently the struggle between the state officials for power and privileges was serving to the interests of alien conquerors. On behalf of its membership the United Party for Supporting the Mongolian Traditions has called upon the members of the Great State Hural to cease the political argues and disputes and unify their efforts in order to justify their voters' confidence in the common interests of Mongolia.
