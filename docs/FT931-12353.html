930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Observer: Far stretch Now here's a statistic to boggle the mind. Japan's Toilet Association says that the paper the Japanese flush down their lavatories daily would wrap 10 times round the equator. At that rate, it would take them only one year, one week and one and a half days to bridge the gap to the rising sun - given adequate fire-proofing, of course.
