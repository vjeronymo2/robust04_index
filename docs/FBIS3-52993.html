Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] National Economy Minister Sadok Rabah stressed during his meeting today with Mr. Davies, the director general of the Confederation of British Industries [CBI], Tunisia's adherence to the necessity of strengthening cooperation with Britain. This will be in the form of economic partnerships which will help entrench the continuous and fruitful cooperation between Tunisian and British economic operators. The minister added that the opening up of the Tunisian economy to the world economy was bolstered by a firm will aimed at extending and varying cooperation with European partners. The head of the Confederation of British Industries expressed his admiration for the promising and positive talks between Tunisia and the European Community. He also expressed the hope that these consultations will contribute to establishing solid foundations for continuous and fruitful exchanges between Tunisia and the European Community.
