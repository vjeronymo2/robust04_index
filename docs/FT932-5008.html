930605 FT 05 JUN 93 / Ecology switch The US, reversing Bush administration policy, yesterday signed a treaty, designed to protect plants and animals, that was a centrepiece of the Earth Summit in Rio de Janeiro last June, Reuter reports. US ambassador Madeleine Albright signed the convention on biological diversity in a ceremony at the UN in New York. More than 150 countries signed the convention at the Rio summit, but President George Bush declined to do so.
