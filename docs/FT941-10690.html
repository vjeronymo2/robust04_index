940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Low & Bonar makes CDollars 6.3m acquisition The Canadian subsidiary of Low & Bonar, the packaging and materials group, is buying the multiwall paper and industrial plastic bag business of Twinpak for CDollars 6.3m (Pounds 3.2m) cash. It will pay CDollars 4.6m on completion and the balance a year later. It will also transfer certain coating and laminating assets with a book value of CDollars 4.7m.
