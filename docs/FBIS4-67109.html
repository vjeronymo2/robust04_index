BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem New Channel 2 Television Network in Hebrew at 1700 GMT on 27 April reports that "280 units of heroin were discovered yesterday in Beersheba prison. The authorities also know with certainty about another 50 incidents in which drugs were smuggled into the prison over the past five months." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in English at 1000 GMT on 18 April reports: "Two Egyptian infiltrators were last night caught south of Mitzpe Ramon after a long chase. A kilogram of heroin was found in their possession. They also had binoculars, clothing, and provisions for a long stay under field conditions."
