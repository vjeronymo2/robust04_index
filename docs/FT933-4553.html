930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Burmah Castrol Burmah Castrol, the international manufacturer and marketer of specialised oil and chemical products, has set up a new trading company in Thailand in partnership with Loxley, the partner of sister company Castrol in Thailand. The joint venture, to be known as Foseco (Thailand), will be 74 per cent-owned by Burmah Castrol. It will source and supply metallurgical chemicals from the area.
