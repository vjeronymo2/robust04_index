Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, January 26 (SNARK)--Abkhazia shelled the Georgian village Lata, located in the Abkhazian Svanetia. SNARK was informed of the fact at the Press-Center Georgian Defence Ministry. The representatives of the press-center familiarized journalists with a statement of the village's inhabitants, who demand the arrival of international observers.
