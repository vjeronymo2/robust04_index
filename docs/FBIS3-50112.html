Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The information service of the Srpska army has informed the public that, quote, the Muslim forces are preparing an attack on the Srpska republic from the direction of Srebrenica, Zepa, Gorazde, Sarajevo, Tuzla, and Bihac. This has been reported by the SRNA news agency. It is also pointed out that the UN Protection Force is obliged to prevent this and other combat activities by the Muslim units from the above-mentioned enclaves. Nevertheless, if the Muslims do carry out an attack, the Srpska army informs the domestic and international public that it will attack the Muslim enclaves from which combat activities will be exhibited, is written in a statement carried by the SRNA news agency.
