Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES 1/2/1994 -- The number of jobless grew over January 24-31, 1994 by another 20,000. The unemployment rate that is forecast at 12-14 percent this year, reached 10.8 percent at the end of January. The total number of unemployed is 1,230,433, including 137,822 in the records of the Bucharest labour offices. Bucharest too, knows a rise in the jobless number by almost 2,000 in the above-mentioned period. Unemployment keeps affecting especially women (there are now 526,926 jobless men and 703,507 jobless women). Most unemployed -- 970,363 -- are blue-collar workers and only 141,122 are people with medium education and 18,211 with academic education. Consequent to the expiry of the length of time for which jobless are entitled to unemployment benefit or subsistence allowance, 100,734 jobless were stricken out of the labour offices' records. Of them, 21,308 are Bucharesters. As regards vacancies on January 31, of the total 4,076 vacancies, Bucharest unemployed may apply for 557. Labour market demands for the respective week favoured mostly the tailors (165).
