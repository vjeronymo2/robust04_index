BFN [Text] The material damage caused by the explosions at the Pacific Fleet's aviation depot 14-15 May near the village of Novonezhino has reached approximately 31 billion rubles. This sum does not include losses incurred by the military, stated (Svetlana Parinova), chairwoman of the economy and planning committee of the Maritime kray administration, speaking at a conference in Vladivostok devoted to measures to clear up the aftermath of the blast. Many of the Maritime kray's commercial structures have expressed their willingness to provide those affected with material help, INTERFAX notes.
