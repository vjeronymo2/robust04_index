931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / EU invites Yeltsin to Brussels The European Union has invited the Russian president, Mr Boris Yeltsin, to Brussels on December 9 to sign a declaration of intent on the Treaty of Partnership planned between the EU and Russia, writes John Lloyd. Mr Yeltsin's visit coincides with a summit of the EU on December 10 and 11. The Russian election is on December 12.
