920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Pegasus to form joint venture Pegasus Group, the USM-quoted computer software supplier, yesterday announced plans to form a joint venture with the Deluxe Corporation of Minnesota. Under the terms of the deal Deluxe UK, a subsidiary of Deluxe Corporation, will acquire 25 per cent of Stockforms, the computer forms and ancillary products business which is a wholly owned subsidiary of Pegasus. Deluxe will pay Pounds 1.71m cash for the stake, with an option to acquire the remainder. In the event that Deluxe exercises its option, Stockforms will pay commissions to Pegasus for five years.
