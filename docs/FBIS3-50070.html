Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 21 Jan (TANJUG) -- Tomislav Nikolic, deputy chairman of the Serbian Radical Party [SRS], announced today that he refused to attend a meeting with Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic. A statement dispatched to TANJUG says that along with some other leaders of the opposition, Nikolic was invited to meet the president of the republic today, and that the meeting was scheduled to take place at 1600. "I refused to go to his office because I have nothing to discuss with Slobodan Milosevic as long as state television imposes a blockade on the SRS," the statement by the SRS deputy chairman says.
