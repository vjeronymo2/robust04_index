Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow January 20 TASS -- Russia will ship to North Korea several decommissioned submarines intended for scrap, Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing on Thursday. These are not military equipment deliveries, but a commercial deal, he said and explained that North Korea had bought through a Japanese company several decommissioned submarines from Russia which had been completely dismantled and cannot be restored as means of warfare. "We view it as an ordinary trade operation. Russia concludes similar commercial deals with other countries as well", Karasin said.
