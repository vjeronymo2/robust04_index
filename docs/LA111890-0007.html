November 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

O, YOSEMITE!

I am in love with Yosemite Valley and have been since my parents introduced me to it in the mid-1930s. I care very much about the preservation of our great natural areas such as Yosemite, and I want for them the kind of management that will maintain them for future generations -- but offer them for the first-hand enjoyment of these future generations. It takes a Curry Co. to do that.

ROBERT M. CUTSHALL

Pasadena
