930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Merlin Properties cuts loss MERLIN International Properties, reported a lower pre-tax loss of Pounds 3.96m in the year to end-June, against Pounds 21.3m. Net exceptional costsfell from Pounds 13.9m to Pounds 2.43m. As there has been no improvement in market conditions the company is seeking a financial reconstruction with a share placing and open offer and at present is relying on the support of its bankers. The auditors say that the accounts have been prepared on a 'going concern' basis. The company said that a request to the Stock Exchange to have the shares re-listed, in view of the progress in cutting losses, was turned down. Losses per share were 23.8p (84.4p).
