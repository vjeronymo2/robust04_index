Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 Jan--Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak will pay a working visit to Brussels on 2-3 February. During the visit, the government press office reports, he will meet NATO General Secretary Manfred Woerner; Jacques Delors, president of the European Commission; Egon Klepsch, speaker of the European Parliament; W. van Eekelen, secretary general of the Western European Union; and Jean-Luc Dehaene, prime minister of Belgium. The chairman of the Council of Ministers will also take part in a conference organized under the auspices of the Center of International Strategic Studies devoted to the future cooperation between Poland and the European Communities and NATO. A meeting with Poles living abroad is also expected.
