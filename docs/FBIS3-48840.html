Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 31 (KCNA) -- A spokesman for the Foreign Ministry of China said that China does not approve of any actions detrimental to peace and stability on the Korean peninsula. The spokesman made the remark at a weekly press conference in Beijing on January 27 when asked to comment on a report that the United States is planning to deploy "Patriot" missiles in South Korea, XINHUA reported.
