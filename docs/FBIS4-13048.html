BFN [Text] Palestinian and Israeli negotiators will today continue the talks they began in Cairo yesterday. The talks aim to resolve some unsettled issues concerning the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation, said the current talks focus on the release of detained Palestinians, crossing points between both Gaza and Jericho and Israel, and joint Palestinian-Israeli patrols. Sha'th added that any delay in the implementation of the Declaration of Principles will increase the suffering of the Palestinian people in the occupied territories. He expressed hopes of progress in the current round of talks, which will last until the end of this week.
