940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Bowater shares up on firm start to year Shares of Bowater rose 14p to 456p yesterday after Mr Michael Woodhouse, chairman of the packaging, printing and coated products group, told the annual meeting that overall operating profits and earnings were currently better than expectations. After the first four months of 1994, the performance of the US and Australian businesses was ahead of the same period last year. The European operations however, were lagging but still in line with plans.
