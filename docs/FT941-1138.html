940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Sphere Investment net assets ahead Sphere Investment Trust raised net assets in 1993 from 75.57p to 85.58p per zero dividend share, and from 24.41p to 54.35p per income and residual capital share. Net revenue increased from Pounds 4.79m to Pounds 5.29m for the year and earnings per income and capital share came to 4.33p (3.92p). A fourth interim dividend of 1.15p (0.85p) makes a total for the year of 3.4p (3.1p). Sphere said it was considering proposals to be put to shareholders for the continuation of the trust after October 1995. Options included an extension of the trust in its present form, and the establishment of a newly created vehicle with similar objectives to the present trust.
