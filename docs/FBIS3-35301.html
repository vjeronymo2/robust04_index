Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- 'Udayy Saddam Husayn assumed the task of running the affairs of the Iraqi Industry Ministry 10 days ago, namely when Minister Husayn Kamil al-Majid's condition began to deteriorate and as a result he was moved to Amman to undergo surgery to remove a brain tumor. Sources said that the Iraqi president has authorized his eldest son to temporarily assume the task of running the establishments which used to be run by al-Majid, Saddam's son-in-law.
