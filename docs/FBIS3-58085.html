Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The talks between Cyprus and Slovakia in Nicosia aimed at developing bilateral relations, particularly cooperation in the area of trade, conclude today. Cyprus and Slovakia are also interested in developing cooperation through European international organizations on security issues. Slovak Foreign Minister Jozef Moravcik and Economic Minister Jan Ducky met this morning with President Glavkos Kliridhis. Later they had separate meetings with Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis and Finance Minister Faidhros Oikonomidhis. The Slovak ministers, who are leading a large delegation, will depart this afternoon.
