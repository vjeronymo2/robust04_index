931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / Canary Wharf ruling expected THE High Court hearing to allow Canary Wharf to come out of administration and resume normal trading is expected to end today. Mr Christopher Carr QC, for Ogilvy & Mather, the advertising agency opposing the discharging of the administrators, told the court that too little information had been supplied for the rescue plan to be assessed properly. Mr Michael Crystal QC, for the administrators Ernst & Young, said the restructuring plan was the only means of securing some financial return for creditors. If the restructuring plan is approved tomorrow, more legal formalities will have to be completed.
