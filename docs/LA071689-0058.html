July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

GROUND BROKEN FOR NEW KAISER OUTPATIENT CLINIC

Ground has broken for the new 34,000-square-foot Whittier Medical Office Building designed by Rosetti Associates for Kaiser Permanente.

The $5-million facility to be located at the southwest corner of Whittier Boulevard at Putnam Street and Pacific Place, will provide outpatient care for Kaiser Permanente members in the areas of pediatrics and adult primary care.
