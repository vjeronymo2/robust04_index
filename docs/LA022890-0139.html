February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Brokaw Comes to Tinseltown: NBC News announced that Tom Brokaw will anchor a primetime documentary at 10 p.m. on March 21, called "The New Hollywood." It will probe the new trends in movie making, ingredients for success at the box office and the changing business of film production. Among those lined up: Robert Redford, Robert De Niro, Sally Field, Spike Lee, Sidney Pollack and Oliver Stone. Beth Kleid
