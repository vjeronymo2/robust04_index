BFN ["mh"-signed report: "According to Prime Minister Moravcik, the Abolition of the Screening Law Is Out of the Question"] [Excerpt] Bratislava -- [passage omitted] Asked by SME about his course of action regarding the request to have members of the coalition government screened, Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik replied that at present he cannot ask the Slovak Information Service [SIS] to issue screening certificates for government members because it would constitute an illegal act. Even though the prime minister had requested screenings, he did so on the basis of material law [hmotny zakon]. "After we realized that the procedural conditions had not been met, we agreed that the screening certificates would not be issued," Jozef Moravcik said.
