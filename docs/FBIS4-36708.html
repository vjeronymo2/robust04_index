BFN [Text] In the Kashmir valley, the two British kidnapped by militants continue to be under their captivity despite the promises made by the abductors that they will be released at any time. The father of the kidnapped 16-year old boy, Mr. David Housego, told our Srinagar correspondent that his anxiety is increasing because of the attitude of the militants. Relatives of the kidnapped persons had even gone to Pahalgam in the hope of securing their release, but returned to Srinagar last evening.
