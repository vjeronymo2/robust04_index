921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Rowlinson edges ahead to Pounds 515,000 Rowlinson Securities, the property group, showed a small improvement in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 503,000 to Pounds 515,000, in the six months to end-September. Gross rental income was Pounds 1.68m (Pounds 1.62m) and sales by the development and contracting divisions jumped to Pounds 2.76m (Pounds 1.41m), but operating profit fell to Pounds 1.4m (Pounds 1.47m). Finance charges, however, were reduced to Pounds 882,000 (Pounds 971,000). Earnings per share were 2.76p (2.7p) and the interim dividend is again 0.24p.
