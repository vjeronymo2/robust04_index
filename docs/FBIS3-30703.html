Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava Feb 3 (CTK) -- Premier Vladimir Meciar will sign a treaty on Slovakia's joining the Partnership for Peace initiative in the Brussels NATO headquarters on February 9, the Press Office of the Slovak Government told CTK today. Estonia, Romania, Lithuania and Poland have officially confirmed their participation in the Partnership. It is generally assumed that the project is a preliminary stage to admission to NATO for some East European countries. Full-fledged NATO membership is one of the aims of the Slovak foreign policy.
