August 7, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Freuhauf Changes Name: Freuhauf Corp., based in Romulus, Mich., has changed its name to K-H Corp. as part of the automotive parts manufacturer's sale of its truck trailer and maritime businesses to Terex Corp. On May 5, Toronto-based Varity Corp. agreed to buy Freuhauf for $436 million and 55 million shares of Varity stock. The name change won't affect that takeover.
