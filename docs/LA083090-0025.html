August 30, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: FESTIVAL NEWS

Festival Club Moves: The Festival Club at Bruno's Ristorante will move to Cafe Largo and Canter's Delicatessen because recent surgery has left Bruno's owner unable to oversee operations. Cafe Largo and Canter's -- opposite each other at 432 N. Fairfax and 419 N. Fairfax, respectively -- have agreed to serve as informal gathering places for Open Festival attendees and festival artists during the event. The buses for the Open Festival's "99 Hours of Art" tours are now tentatively slated to leave from Farmers Market. BETH KLEID
