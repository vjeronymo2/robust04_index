940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / International Capital Markets: Vietnam opens up share sale Vietnam is to allow foreigners to buy bonds and shares issued by state-owned companies in what one economist said was a form of privatisation, Reuter reports from Hanoi. The scheme will be open to foreign companies and residents, overseas Vietnamese and Vietnamese enterprises. A government economist said the share issue would amount to issuing new capital that would be privately held, diluting state ownership. New regulations say the purchase and sale of bonds and shares should be in Vietnamese dong.
