Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 10 February (MTI) -- Police found 36.5 kilograms of heroin in an apartment in one of Budapest's outer districts, in the course of a house search on February 7, the spokesman of the National Police Headquarters, Laszlo Garamvolgyi, told MTI on Thursday [10 February]. He said officers of the anti-organized crime service took into custody a Hungarian citizen living in the apartment, whose confession in turn led to the apprehension of a 33-year-old Turkish citizen in Budapest on February 8. He is accused of handing over the heroin to the Hungarian citizen for safekeeping. Investigations are continuing into the case, in cooperation with foreign police authorities.
