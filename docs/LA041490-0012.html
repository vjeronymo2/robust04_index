April 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

RESIDENTS GRAPPLE WITH THE DROUGHT

When is a crisis -- a crisis -- a crisis?

When there's no more water at all!

The Los Angeles Board of Supervisors and Mayor Tom Bradley should issue an immediate order for a moratorium to become effective not later than May 15 on all building and construction that will have water hook-ups of any type.

Exceptions would be those that are within 90% of completion by May 15 and have an OK for water and sewage hook-ups.

If the rainfall exceeds normal levels in our vital areas of water supply -- namely Mono Basin and Shasta Lake regions -- then and only then should the moratorium be lifted in 1991.

THOMAS E. GRAHAM

Santa Monica
