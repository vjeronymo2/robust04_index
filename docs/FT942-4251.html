940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Melville Street net asset value ahead Melville Street Investments, which specialises in the provision of development capital to small and growing companies, saw a rise in its net asset value from 140p to 187p per share over the year to April 30. Net revenue for the 12 months improved from Pounds 800,758 to Pounds 950,775 for earnings per share of 5.1p (4.3p). A final dividend of 2.7p is proposed for a 4.2p (4p) total.
