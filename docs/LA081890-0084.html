August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

7 KILLED IN GUN BATTLE WITH PHILIPPINE REBELS

Communist rebels ambushed a police jeep in the central Philippines on Friday, killing a 13-year-old boy. Police fought back and killed six guerrillas, police officials said.

Four police officers and two rebels were wounded in the 30-minute gun battle near Tacloban, 350 miles southeast of Manila.
