September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LONG BEACH OFFICE TOPS IN CENTURY 21 REGION

Century 21 A Marketplace, 1650 Ximeno Ave., Long Beach, was named top producing office in the Los Angeles district of Century 21 of the Pacific. In July, the office closed more than $12.3 million in real estate sales.

David Carden received the coveted President's pin for outstanding sales production and Carl Proctor, also of Century 21 A Marketplace, was cited among the top agents for the Los Angeles/South Bay area.
