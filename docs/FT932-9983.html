930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / International Company News: Sumitomo MI pays tax penalty SUMITOMO Metal Industries said yesterday it had paid more than Y600m (Dollars 5.4m) in penalty taxes to the Osaka regional tax bureau after failing to report Y101bn in taxable income between 1990 and 1992, writes Charles Leadbeater in Tokyo. The money involved was spent on community projects in localities around the company's plants. Sumitomo said it represented tax-free social spending.
