Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Foreign Minister Luis Maria Ramirez Boettner will travel tonight to Washington to participate in the OAS assembly on 27 March. During the course of the assembly a new secretary general will be elected to replace Brazilian Joao Clemente Baena Soares. Foreign Ministry sources have reported that the foreign minister will, in the name of the government, vote for Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo. [passage omitted]
