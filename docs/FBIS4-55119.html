BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 6 -- President Lech Walesa received on Friday British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd, who is on a working visit to Poland. President said that he is satisfied with Polish-British relations and stressed that Poland attaches great importance to bilateral relations. He also expressed the hope that the Polish point of view on the development of situation in eastern Europe would be better understood by Great Britain. Douglas Hurd expressed his satisfaction at the meeting emphasising that Poland made huge progress on the path of transformations under Walesa's leadership. Changes taking place in Europe, however, require cooperation, especially in political, economical and military areas. Our common aim, said the British politician, should be unification of Europe, whose Poland is a very important member.
