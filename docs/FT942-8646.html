940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Bryant acquires rest of Vigobridge Bryant Group, the Midlands-based housebuilder, has acquired all of Vigobridge, a joint venture between Bryant and Allied Irish Banks, for Pounds 500. Vigobridge's main asset is the Maidenbower development at Crawley, West Sussex, where land for 500 units remains to be developed. At acquisition it had about Pounds 14m of land, Pounds 5m of work in progress, Pounds 13m bank borrowings and Pounds 6m of shareholder loans.
