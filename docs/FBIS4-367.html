BFN [Excerpt] The Ministry of Defense has denied reports that two Ghanaian soldiers serving with the UN Assistance Mission in Rwanda are missing. The reports say two Ghanaians and two Belgian observers could not be found. A statement issued in Accra today and signed by the director of the Public Relations Directorate of the Ghana Armed Forces, Colonel Akowia, said the commander of the Ghanaian contingent in the mission area has confirmed that no Ghanaian officer or man is missing. He added that they are all safe and sound. [passage omitted]
