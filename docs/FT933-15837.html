930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / People: Boots Healthcare International Boots Healthcare International has appointed Peter Milburn as director of marketing. He succeeds Phillip Davey who has become md of Crookes Healthcare, Boots' over-the-counter medicines subsidiary in the UK. Alan Horgan has been promoted from general manager to be md of Boots' UK pharmaceuticals subsidiary. He joined Boots from Bristol-Myers Squibb in 1991 as head of UK sales and marketing.
