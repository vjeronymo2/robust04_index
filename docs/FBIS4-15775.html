BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 11 April, TASS -- On Monday morning Russian frontierguards stopped three attempts at illegal crossing from Afghanistan to Tajikistan of militants of the Tajik opposition on the Pamirs stretch of the border. At around 1030 local time a small groups of commandos tried to cross to Tajikistan at Ishkashim stretch. There was a skirmish. One border violator was detained. Two fled back. Two more attempts to cross into Tajikistan were made in the section of the Khorog frontierguards unit around 1000 and at noon. Russian frontierguards opened preventive fire, and violators returned to Afghanistan. The situation on other sections of the border was difficult, but it was kept under control, ITAR-TASS learned in the headquarters of Russian Frontier Troops in Tajikistan.
