Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 28 (SNARK) -- Three armoured units and dozens of soldiers killed -- these are the losses the Azerbaijani side suffered as a result of yesterday's offensive in the Gadrut direction of Karabakh front. As SNARK was informed in the press-center of Karabakh Defence Army, by the evening of February 27 the defenders of Karabakh had managed to repel the enemy's attack and made him retreat to the initial positions. On Karabakh side there are 8 soldiers killed and one wounded. Today at the greater part of the front line there is relative peace, occasionally violated by short exchange of fire. Meanwhile, according to Karabakh Defence Army data, the accumulation of Azerbaijani man power and military hardware is observed at eastern part of the front.
