SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Coast Guard published an interim final rule promulgating hybrid inflatable PFD requirements in the Federal Register on August 22, 1985 (50 FR 33923). The SNPRM published in the Federal Register on January 18, 1994 (59 FR 2575) proposes changes to the requirements for approving hybrid PFD's and for the carriage of hybrid PFD's on commercial vessels. Correction of Publication The publication on January 18, 1994, of the Supplemental Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (CGD 78&hyph;174), which was the subject of FR Doc. 94&hyph;1135, is corrected as follows: 1. On page 2578, first column, under section 160.077&hyph;31 Size Ranges, paragraph designation (1) should be a lower case (l). §160.077&hyph;23 [Corrected] 3. On page 2585, first column, in §160.077&hyph;23(b)(1)(i), ``§160.077&hyph;3(d)(5)'' should read ``§160.077&hyph;23(d)(5)''. §160.077&hyph;23 [Corrected] 4.
