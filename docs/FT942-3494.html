940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Equities in Sao Paulo dropped from earlier highs to end a net 2.4 per cent down in light trading dominated by a struggle between longs and shorts prior to this week's futures index settlement. The market was 2 per cent up at midday. The Bovespa index lost 735 points at 29,469, while turnover totalled Cr343bn (Dollars 159m). The futures index settlement is scheduled for tomorrow, while options settlement takes place on June 20.
