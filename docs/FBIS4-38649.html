BFN [Text] A total of five high-ranking officials of the Georgian power ministries were detained in May. They are suspected of participating in the assassination of defense minister Nikoloz Kekelidze and deputy defense minister and chief of transport police Giya Gulua. Reliable sources told Interfax that all the detained are colonels. Kekelidze was killed as a result of an explosion on February 3 morning in his house in Tbilisi. Gulua had been killed near his house in Tbilisi on April 20 morning. During the recent six months about ten terrorist acts against high-ranking officers of the Defense Ministry, the Interior Ministry and the Mkhedrioni armed group have been carried out in Georgia. Chief of the Mkhedrioni Rustav regional department Temur Talakhadze was killed in January, a Security Ministry officer Vasil Koberidze in February. None of the murderers has been found.
