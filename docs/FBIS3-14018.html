Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The minister of state for external affairs, Mr. R.L. Bhatia, said over 150 foreign journalists visited Jammu and Kashmir last year. In a written reply in the Rajya Sabha today, he said Pakistan has been indulging in a disinformation campaign alleging that members of international media cannot visit Jammu and Kashmir. He said the government is taking all steps to inform the international community that there is no restriction on visits of foreign journalists to the state.
