940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Honduras murder plot The Honduran army says it is investigating a foiled plot to assassinate President Carlos Roberto Reina and kidnap a businessman, Reuter reports from Tegucigalpa. Lt Col Napoleon Santos, an army spokesman, said one of four men implicated in the conspiracy had told the Interior Ministry that a Honduran citizen with links to the drug trade had offered the group Dollars 400,000 to kill the president and more than Dollars 800,000 to kidnap the businessman. President Reina won the election in November and took office on January 27, pledging to carry out a 'moral revolution' in Honduras, weed out corruption and curb the army's power.
