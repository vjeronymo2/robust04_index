BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents] [Text] Bonn May 13 TASS -- "All issues which were planned for discussion have been discussed. And we did not lose in any of them", Russian President Boris Yeltsin told TASS on Friday in his residence near Bonn where he had a lunch with German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel. After the meeting with Kinkel the Russian president left for Stuttgart where he is to visit an enterprise of the Alkatel-Sel concern and meet local leaders and businessmen.
