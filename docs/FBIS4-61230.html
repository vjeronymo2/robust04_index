BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Makhachkala May 4 TASS -- A group of armed extremists attacked a training centre of the Russian border troops in Makhachkala, capital of Daghestan [Dagestan], last night. The attackers armed with firearms penetrated the territory of the centre, but were seized by its guards. They were then handed over to the local law enforcement bodies. The press centre of the Russian border troops told ITAR-TASS that this is not the first such case in Daghestan where the Russian border is now being established.
