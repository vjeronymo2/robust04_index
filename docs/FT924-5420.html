921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: Nomura offers to buy back bonds Nomura Securities will offer to buy back US property-backed bonds it sold to investors without sufficient explanation of the risk attached to them, the brokerage house said this morning. It said the move would cost it up to Y23bn (Pounds 122m). It will sell fixed assets to cover the losses and will not alter its forecast for profit for the fiscal year.
