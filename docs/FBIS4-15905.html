BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Kochetkov] [Text] Tbilisi April 12 TASS -- Georgian Cabinet of Ministers has declared a spring draft in the Armed Forces of the country. It will be held from April 20 to July 1 and citizens from 18 to 25 years of age, who do not have a right to delay their recruitment, will be recruited. According to an inter-governmental agreement, about 600 draftees will serve together with Russian border guards on the Turkish border. They join on a voluntary basis the "Georgia" armed group of the Caucasian special district of Russian border-guard troops on the Turkish border. The special district was created in March, this year, following a decree by Russian President Boris Yeltsin. It includes the relevant armed groups in the Transcaucasian region: "Georgia", "Azerbaijan" and "Armenia". As against Russian conscripts, Georgian draftees will be administered not to an oath, but to a solemn obligation.
