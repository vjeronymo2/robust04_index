941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Equity Markets: Zurich - Other markets Today brings Holderbank's autumn news conference. Analysts and the cement company itself have anticipated a happy event; on November 4, Holderbank justified analysts' earnings upgrades by forecasting that consolidated net income for 1994 would rise by 37 per cent, sharply above even the best expectations. Also today, Electrowatt hosts its news conference on business this year; last June the electric power generation and engineering group forecast that last year's record net profits should be 'almost equalled'.
