Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 25 Feb (MENA)--The Palestinian news agency WAFA, citing its own sources in Hebron, reports that the number of victims in the massacre at the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron has risen to over 38 Palestinians. Over 300 Hebron citizens were injured. The news agency added that the Palestinian settlers surprised the worshippers as they prayed, firing on them in one of the ugliest massacres since the West Bank and Gaza Strip were occupied. Initial reports provide the following information: 18 martyrs in al-Ahli Hospital, 11 in 'Aliyah Hospital, three in al-Muhtasib Hospital, and five martyrs in al-Maqasid Hospital [figures as received].
