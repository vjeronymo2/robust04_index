September 20, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SPANISH CROWN PRINCE LEAVES FOR U.S., CANADA

Spain's Crown Prince Felipe left for Canada and the United States today on his first official visit abroad, planned to include a meeting with President Bush, a royal palace spokesman said. Prince Felipe de Borbon, 21-year-old son of King Juan Carlos I and Queen Sofia, will visit Montreal and Toronto before traveling to Santa Fe, N.M., and Houston andWashington.
