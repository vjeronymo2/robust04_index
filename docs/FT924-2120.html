921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Sheikh Khalid: Correction In Saturday's FT, it was reported that a law suit issued against Sheikh Khalid bin Mahfouz, former head of the National Commercial Bank of Saudi Arabia and the bank, sought to freeze the assets of Sheikh Khalid and the bank. The suit, issued by liquidators of the Bank of Credit and Commerce International, seeks to freeze the assets of Sheikh Khalid and not the bank itself.
