BFN [Resolution by the Lvov Oblast Council of People's Deputies "On Suspending the Activities of the Commissions for Oblast Council Elections"] [Text] Proceeding from the constitutional provision to the effect that the people are the superior and sole representatives of power; taking into account that as of today: - The provisions of Article 1 of the Law of Ukraine "On the Forming of Local Bodies of Power and Self-Government" have not been implemented; - Society's stance with regard to the state structure has not been determined, and normative acts about the structure and powers of the administration have not been adopted; - Unwilling to deceive the electors and thus plant disbelief and cause total degradation of the administration's prestige; - Realizing its responsibility for the fate of the state; - The Lvov Oblast Council of People's Deputies has resolved: 1. To suspend the activities of the territorial and 75 district commissions for the elections of the chairman and deputies of the oblast council until a law is adopted on the structure and powers of the administration. 2. The resolution becomes valid on the day of adoption.
