June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PARTNERS: Primary hostilities in the 58th Assembly District (B1) went to the sidelines Tuesday when two Republican opponents played as a team in a set of tennis. . . . Sy Alban, an orthopedic surgeon from Long Beach, joined foe Peter von Elten, an Orange County developer, in a doubles match. They beat Scott Stadler, Alban's campaign manager, and Al Pross of the California Medical Political Action Committee, 6-4. Said Alban: "He wasn't my opponent this afternoon, he was my partner." Tony Marcano and Shannon Sands
