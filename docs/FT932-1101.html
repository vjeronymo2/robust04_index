930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / World Trade News: TV compression technology sold NCL, the privatised engineering and transmitter arm of the former UK Independent Broadcasting Authority, has sold its digital compression technology to FilmNet, the European satellite television operator, writes Raymond Snoddy. It will be the first example of its use on a regular basis in Europe for broadcast quality television. Digital compression squeezes several channels into the space occupied by one by removing unnecessary information in the picture.
