BFN [Text] Shenzhen, June 18 (XINHUA) -- The seventh national prize-awarding ceremony to honor the top ten [figure as received] foreign-funded firms was held in Shenzhen, Guangdong Province, today. Vice-Premier Li Lanqing sent a message of congratulations to the ceremony. Li said that China will be more active to introduce overseas funds and technology, personnel and advanced management experiences. He assured that China will further improve the investment environment and provide more convenient conditions and legal guarantee for overseas investors. The best foreign-funded enterprises awarded today include: Xian-Janssen Pharmaceutical Co. Ltd., Shanghai Bell Telephone Equipment Manufacturing Co, Ltd., Shanghai Volkswagen Automobile Corporation Ltd., Shenzhen Kongka Electronic Group Co. Ltd., Shenzhen China Bicycles Company (Holdings) Limited, Shanghai Ek Choi Motorcycle Co., Ltd., China Nafang Glass Products Company (Holding) Ltd., China-Schindler Elevator Co., Ltd., Jiangsu Chunlan Refrigeration Equipment Co., Ltd., Guangzhou Metal Container Packaging Company Ltd. and Shanghai Dajiang Co., Ltd.
