940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / Clinton pledges support to crime bill President Bill Clinton promised yesterday to 'fight and fight and fight' to push his crime bill into law after it suffered a devastating defeat in the House of Representatives. Mr Clinton, pictured trying to rally support at a meeting of the National Association of Police Organisations in Minneapolis, must now negotiate changes to the bill, which includes measures such as gun control.
