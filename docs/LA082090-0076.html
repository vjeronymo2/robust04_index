August 20, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS

Sally Claybourn, controller at the Institute for Biological Research and Development Inc., has been promoted to vice president. Before joining the Irvine-based pharmaceutical development company in 1988, she was a senior field analyst for Taco Bell Corp. Claybourn has also held financial analysis and accounting positions with MAI Basic Four Inc., Pertec Computer Corp. and Vought Corp.
