BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko, Mikhail Melnik and UKRINFORM correspondent Vladimir Novak] [Text] Odessa 16 June TASS -- Russo-Ukrainian relations are normal and from now on will be a top priority, they should be developed and reinforced in every way, President Leonid Kravchuk of Ukraine told a meeting of industrialists, agricultural workers, businessmen, students and representatives of various political parties and movements at the polytechnic university on Thursday. As president, I will do my best to restore ties with the Russian Federation, to make them work both for Russia and Ukraine. "Ukrainian president should protect interests of his country, and (president) of the Russian Federation -- those of his country. This is normal," Kravchuk emphasized. Touching upon his relations with the newly elected Ukrainian Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol, Kravchuk described him as an educated and experienced person who has changed his views in many respects since his resignation. Judging by his programme presented to Ukrainian parliament, Masol has changed his approach to settling economic problems and sees clearly how to lead the country out of crisis. It is important now that Masol select a strong team and that nobody interfere with his work, Kravchuk said.
