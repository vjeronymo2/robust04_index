931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / UK Company News: IMC declines to Pounds 377,000 Pre-tax profits at IMC Industries, the USM-quoted company with interests in soft drinks, in-flight entertainment systems and blank and pre-recorded video tapes, slipped by Pounds 1,000 to Pounds 377,000 in the year to April 30. However, turnover almost doubled to Pounds 6.13m (Pounds 3.22m). Earnings eased to 0.17p (0.18p). The company said the year had been one of consolidation.
