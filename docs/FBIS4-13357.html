BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Apr (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid met today with Ambassador Nabil Najm, Iraq's permanent envoy to the Arab League. Following the meeting, Ambassador Najm said that he briefed the Arab League secretary general on the statement made by Mahdi Sa'di Salih, the Iraqi National Assembly speaker, in which he invited a Kuwaiti parliamentary delegation to visit Iraq to discuss all pending issues between the two countries in order to serve pan-Arab causes, fulfill the two sides' interests, and end the state of conflict and instability in the region. The Iraqi envoy regretted the decision made by the Lebanese Government to sever diplomatic relations with Baghdad. He said that this decision came at a time when Iraq was eager to normalize its Arab ties.
