940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Global ahead Global Group, the USM-quoted meat trading and shipping services company, reported pre-tax profits ahead at Pounds 715,000 for the six months to June 30, against Pounds 678,000. Turnover was up from Pounds 40.6m to Pounds 41m. Earnings per share were static at 0.38p (0.37p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.2p.
