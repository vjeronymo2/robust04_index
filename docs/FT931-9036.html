930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: America WALL STREET was closed for President's Day. Toronto went into limbo for the US holiday, share prices tending to ease slightly in moderate trading. The TSE 300 index slipped 3.4 points to finish at 3,442.2. Declines outnumbered rises by 305 to 277 and volume fell to 30.1m shares from Friday's 49.1m. The market's dull tone was not felt in the real estate group, which rose 2.4 per cent.
