BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Osipov] [Text] Abidjan May 19 TASS -- The Foreign Ministries of Russia and Mali signed a protocol on consultations in Bamako on Wednesday, a spokesman for the Russian Embassy in Mali told ITAR-TASS by telephone. "The parties believe that the signing of the important document will strengthen and put onto a legal basis the constructive dialogue between Russia and Mali in the foreign policy sphere", the protocol states. The two ministries pledge to hold regular consultations in turn in each country on a wide range of bilateral and international issues. The parties also plan familiarising trips by the ministries' officials to exchange experience and to settle current issues of bilateral relations.
