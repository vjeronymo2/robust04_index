930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / France and Germany agree to maintain EC monetary union schedule France and Germany agreed yesterday to maintain the present timetable for EC economic and monetary union, Quentin Peel writes from Dresden. Mr Klaus Kinkel, the German foreign minister, and Mr Alain Juppe, his French counterpart, said at a meeting in Dresden they would push for greater economic convergence 'to revive the credibility of the aims of economic and monetary union'.
