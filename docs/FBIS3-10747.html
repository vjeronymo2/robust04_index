Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Maceio -- The newspaper CORREIO DE ALAGOAS begins to circulate here today. It will be published by the staff of journalists and with the equipment of the Arnon de Mello organization -- which includes companies owned by former President Fernando Collor but managed by his brother, Pedro Collor. Officially, the newspaper does not belong to either Fernando or Pedro. According to journalist Pedro de Oliveira, the newspaper's owner, a lease contract has been signed.
