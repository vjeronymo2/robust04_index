920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (25): Lloyds Bank - Company Snapshot Lloyds is one of the top four UK banks. Its income last year was Pounds 3.98bn and profit after tax was Pounds 449m. The bank has more than 71,700 staff, of whom 62,000 are in the UK. The Corporate Banking and Treasury Unit includes banking services for companies, leasing, and trade finance. It also includes Europe's biggest share registration operation. The unit made an Pounds 11m pre-tax loss last year. It has 2,550 staff in the UK, New York and Tokyo.
