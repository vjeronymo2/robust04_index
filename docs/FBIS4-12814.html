BFN [Text] Dear listeners, as you heard earlier, Mr. Nateq-Nuri, the Majles speaker, visited the Iran-Pakistan-Persian Research Center in Islamabad today and took part in a news conference in which he answered reporters' questions. Our correspondent reports: [Correspondent] Mr. Nateq-Nuri described the results of his trip to this country as positive and said: Our discussions revealed that the two countries share the same viewpoints with respect to the issues of the world of Islam. In response to a question, the Majles speaker, who was speaking to correspondents, denied having any discussions with Pakistani authorities about cooperation on the manufacture and use of nuclear weapons. He said: We support the peaceful use of nuclear technology by Pakistan. On the Kashmir issue, he said: The Islamic Republic of Iran can be a good mediator toward the resolution of this problem. He added: On the initiative of the Islamic Republic of Iran, it has been agreed that a delegation consisting of representatives of Islamic countries will be sent to study the situation in the part of Kashmir under the control of India and the members of this delegation have been selected. Mr. Nateq-Nuri described Iran and Pakistan's capabilities for supplying one another's requirements as plentiful, saying: The Islamic Republic of Iran has agreed to increase the volume of gas exports to this country from 25,000 tonnes to 50,000 tonnes. Elsewhere in the news conference, the Majles speaker explained that, with regard to Iran's three Persian Gulf islands [Abu Musa and the Lesser and Greater Tunbs], we are prepared to continue the contacts toward the resolution of the difficulties. Mr. Nateq-Nuri also visited the Iran-Pakistan-Persian Research Center in Islamabad today. During the visit and in a gathering of dozens of Persian language lecturers in Pakistan, the Majles speaker called for the greatest possible efforts to be made to revive this language in Pakistan, and he described such efforts as very effective toward the protection of that country's cultural heritage.
