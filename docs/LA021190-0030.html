February 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: NURSING SCHOLARSHIPS GO TO 9 AT AZUSA PACIFIC

Nine Azusa Pacific University nursing students have been awarded $1,000 scholarships by the Kaiser Permanente Scholarship Foundation.

They are Elizabeth Argandona of Diamond Bar; Jaqueline Ismail of Beaver Falls, Pa.; Terry Labansat of Sunland; Lisa Lee of Hacienda Heights; Ellen Mandani of La Verne; Tina Morrison of West Covina; Bernard Prouty of Seal Beach; Shelly Salse of Azusa, and Arlene Vander Dussen of Chino.
