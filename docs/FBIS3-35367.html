Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A border police undercover unit this evening captured an Islamic Jihad fugitive in Gaza city. The man was captured when he refused soldiers' orders to stop and tried to run over one of the soldiers with his car. In Rafah, a Palestinian was shot by troops and moderately wounded. He was part of a group that ran away after refusing soldiers' orders to stop. A border police unit in Bethlehem this afternoon discovered a bomb in Manger Square. The device was safely defused by a Border Police sapper. A Tel Aviv Magistrates Court has extended the remand in custody by a week of a Ramla resident suspected of handing over an Uzi submachine gun to a resident of Rafah. The accused, who was also suspected of hiding other weapons near his home, denies the allegations. A police officer has been shot near Dimona. He is now hospitalized at Soroka Hospital in Beersheba. His condition is described as light to moderate. The officer told investigators that while he was waiting at a bus stop, a car stopped alongside him and three Palestinians attempted to force him into the vehicle. He succeeded in fighting them off, but a third man shot him in the leg. Security forces are investigating.
