Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By special AL-QUDS correspondent] [Text] Gaza -- The Islamic Jihad Movement in Palestine issued a statement yesterday in which it called on all political, Islamic, and national factions to stop competing strikes, which have surpassed the limits of logic and wisdom. The movement said: We appeal to all factions to ease rather than make the masses burdens heavier by sudden strikes, which are called upon the martyrdom of young men from all factions.
