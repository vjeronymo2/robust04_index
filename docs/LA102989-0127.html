October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SAVAGING REV. MARTIN LUTHER KING JR.'S REPUTATION

It is interesting to note that Ralph David Abernathy is vice-president of the board of directors of the American Freedom Coalition, one of the most ferociously right-wing, allegedly Moonie-backed groups that currently exists in the United States.

Is his biography of the Rev. Martin Luther King Jr. truly a quest for truth, or is it one more attempt to discredit those men and women who fight for the civil liberties of the disenfranchised?

VIRGINIA URIBE, Los Angeles
