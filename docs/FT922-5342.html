920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Barratt busy in Scotland the Scottish-based construction arm of Barratt Developments - has been awarded new contracts worth in excess of Pounds 5m. In Edinburgh, work will begin shortly on a Pounds 2m contract at the former Scottish & Newcastle Maltings on Slateford Road for its housebuilding sister company, Barratt Scotland. In addition to building a six-storey block of 36 apartments, Barratt Construction will carry out the demolition and facade retention of the two Victorian B-listed maltings buildings, where a further 114 new flats are planned. Work has already begun on a Pounds 1.6m design and build contract in Dundee for rented housing specialist Quality Street, constructing a range of 42 apartments in Abercorn Street. In the North East, the company has secured new business worth more than Pounds 1.5m, including a development of 27 flats for Langstane Housing Association at Great Northern Road, Aberdeen. Additional projects scheduled to begin shortly include the fitting out of offices in Albyn Place for Coopers & Lybrand Deloitte, and a new residence for private clients at Birchbank, near Kemnay.
