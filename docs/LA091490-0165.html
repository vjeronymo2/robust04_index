September 14, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Hughes Suit -- A Superior Court jury awarded Bobby Coffey of Woodland Hills $1.95 million after finding that his former employer, Hughes Aircraft Co., was guilty of breach of contract and breach of public policy in firing him in January, 1986. In a United Press International article carried in Wednesday's editions of The Times, the jury's finding and the time of Coffey's dismissal were reported incorrectly.
