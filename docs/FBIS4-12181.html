BFN [Text] At a meeting in Damascus today with the leaders of the 10 Palestinian groups, Deputy Majles Speaker Hasan Ruhani stressed: The massacre of the Muslims at al-Khalil Mosque shows that the Zionist regime will never shed its savage streak. The secretary of our country's Supreme National Security Council added: The demonstrations on International Jerusalem Day show that not only did the Zionist regime not achieve its cursed aims in the al-Khalil incident but that the affection of the world Muslims for the Palestinians has increased. Sayyid Hasan Nasrallah, the secretary-general of the Lebanese revolutionary movement Hizballah, at a meeting with the deputy Majles speaker this morning, thanked the esteemed leader [Khamene'i], the president, the Majles speaker, and the people of Iran for their attention to the justice-seeking struggles of the Muslim people of Lebanon.
