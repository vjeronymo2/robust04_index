BFN [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers decided to establish an economic committee to decide on the economic measures to be taken and whether it is possible to index foreign currency. Taner Etkin, government spokesman and interior minister, in a statement he issued following the meeting, said that it was decided to establish an economic committee that will decide on the economic measures to be taken and ways to implement foreign currency indexation and submit its report as soon as possible to the Council of Ministers. Explaining that the members of the economic committee will be determined by the deputy prime minister, Etkin added that Turkish experts may also participate in the committee. Etkin further added that the government will also conduct a dialogue with various organizations and institutions in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus regarding the issues under discussion.
