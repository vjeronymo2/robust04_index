920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Commercial Bank of London falls 20% Commercial Bank of London reported a 20 per cent fall in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 389,246 to Pounds 310,813, in the six months to June 30. The result was struck on gross income down by Pounds 228,000 to Pounds 1.87m. Tax took Pounds 102,000 (Pounds 136,000) leaving attributable profits of Pounds 208,813 (Pounds 253,246). Earnings came out at 20.8p (25.3p).
