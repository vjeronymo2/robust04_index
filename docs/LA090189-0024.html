September 1, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

An all-woman American crew in the 33,000-mile Round-the-World yacht race starting Saturday from Southampton, England, may have to withdraw because of lack of sponsorship.

The 10 women, headed by Nance Frank, 40, must raise $150,000 before the owner of an unidentified 57-foot sailboat will let the crew get underway.
