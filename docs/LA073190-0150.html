July 31, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Los Angeles artist Charles Ray will discuss his quirkily provocative sculpture -- on view through Sept. 23 at Newport Harbor Art Museum -- on Wednesday at 7:30 p.m. at the museum.

A reception will follow. Tickets: $5, general; $3, students and seniors. Reservations are suggested; call the museum education department: (714) 759-1122. The museum is at 850 San Clemente Drive, Newport Beach.
