920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Oliver Resources losses reduced Oliver Resources, the Dublin-based oil and gas company which has a USM quote, reported reduced pre-tax losses of IPounds 100,000 (Pounds 94,000) for the six months to April 30 compared with a deficit of IPounds 272,000 previously. Included in the loss were interest payments of IPounds 19,000 (nil) and a IPounds 37,000 (IPounds 47,000) share of losses of associated companies. Losses per share were cut to 0.1p (0.3p).
