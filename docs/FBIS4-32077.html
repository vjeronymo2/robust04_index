BFN [Unattributed report: "Identical Attitudes With Serbian Socialists"] [Text] The press conference of the Democratic Christian Party in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], held in Belgrade, focused on the Orthodox religion and Macedonia. Representatives of this party have been guests of the ruling Socialist Party in Serbia [SPS] for already two days. We are discussing possibilities of opening a process of unity of all orthodox and Christian peoples in the Balkans, as a part of the all-European integration process, the party leader Vasil Ristevski said. He stressed his party has identical points of view with the Serbian Socialists on all main issues concerning the Balkan political crises. He also pointed out he had been assured Macedonia is not faced with any danger coming from its northern brotherly neighbor. Representatives of the party were also received by the Serbian Government and the Holy Synod of the Serbian Orthodox Church.
