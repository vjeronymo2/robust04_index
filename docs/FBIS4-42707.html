BFN [Text] Hamburg (DPA) -- Hamburg lawyer Mike Bashford, 36, has become the new federal chairman of the Instead Party. Bashford was elected chief of the Instead Party at a federal delegates meeting in Hamburg on Saturday [4 June]. He received 70 of 88 votes cast. Twelve delegates voted no, and four abstained from voting. The previous chairman, Munich law professor Bernd Schuenemann, had been voted out of office before by a constructive vote of no confidence.
