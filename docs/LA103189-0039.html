October 31, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: SOUTH KOREA AWARDS $500,000 CONTRACT TO DASIBI

Dasibi Environmental Corp. said it won a contract worth about $500,000 to install nine government air-pollution monitoring stations in South Korea.

Glendale-based Dasibi, which makes devices that measure air pollution and monitor smokestack emissions, said it was the fourth contract it had won from the South Korean government.

Separately, Dasibi said a subsidiary, Dasibi Europa, has received orders from several customers totaling $300,000.
