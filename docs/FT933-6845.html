930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Bass poised for Indian expansion BASS has signed a joint venture agreement with INN Realty Hotel Ventures of India to develop 70 franchised Holiday Inn hotels throughout India over the next 10 years, writes Philip Rawstorne. The joint venture will focus on secondary cities, catering to a domestic market of an estimated 30m households. Bass has only four Holiday Inn franchises in the subcontinent, at Bombay, Bangalore, Hyderabad and New Delhi. It said yesterday that it had identified a growing demand for top-class hotel accommodation throughout the country.
