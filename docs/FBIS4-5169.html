BFN ["7th Session of 9th SPA of DPRK Closes" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- The Seventh Session of the Ninth Supreme People's Assembly [SPA] of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea which opened here April 6 closed today after successfully discussing the items on the agenda. The session adopted with the unanimous approval of the deputies the decision of the SPA "on the review of the performance of the state budget of the DPRK for 1993" and the ordinance of the SPA "on the state budget of the DPRK for 1994."
