BFN [Text] Today, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk received Sir Leon Brittan, vice president of the European Union Commission and commissar for external economic affairs of the European Commissions, who is on a visit to our country at the invitation of the foreign affairs minister to familiarize himself with the situation in Ukraine and to find out about the course of reforms. A conversation took place in the course of which a wide variety of issues relating to cooperation between Ukraine and the EEC was raised. Ukraine attaches significant importance to the development of cooperation with the European Union, said President Kravchuk. We seek to have good relations with every country on the continent. I am happy that work on preparation for the signing of an agreement between Ukraine and the EEC on partnership and cooperation is over. This cooperation will be on the basis of temporary accords. President Kravchuk expressed the wish that the agreement should be signed as early as the beginning of June. This, he said, is necessitated by our economic and political problems.
