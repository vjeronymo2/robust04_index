921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / International Company News: Ahold plans retail expansion AHOLD, the Dutch retail and supermarket group, plans to acquire Jamin, a chain of Dutch confectionery shops, before the end of March, writes Ronald van de Krol in Amsterdam. Ahold did not disclose how much it would pay Jamin's owner, Goudsmit, a Dutch holding company with varied interests in the retail sector, for the chain of 261 shops. Jamin had sales of Fl 150m (Dollars 82m) in 1991.
