Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ejup Ganic, member of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency, has sent a protest note to UN secretary general's special envoy Yasushi Akashi, [who is currently] in Zagreb, saying that the Serb forces have been attacking Bihac and the surrounding areas. Human and material damage is vast. This region was declared a zone protected by the UN Protection Forces. Even after several appeals your troops have so far only watched the aggressor's barbaric actions. We demand action from you, Ejup Ganic's letter to Jasushi Akashi says.
