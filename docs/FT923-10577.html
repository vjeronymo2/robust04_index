920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / Australian inflation at 1.2% Australian inflation fell to an annual rate of 1.2 per cent in the three months to June, compared with 1.7 per cent in the previous quarter, the government said yesterday, Kevin Brown reports from Sydney. Officials said inflation was at its lowest since March 1964. The announcement also gives Australia one of the lowest rates of inflation in the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). Analysts said the unexpectedly sharp reduction in the annual rate would allow the Reserve Bank of Australia to consider further cuts in interest rates.
