BFN [Text] The multinational observers will apparently begin deploying in Hebron next week, Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres determined today after meeting for the first time with representatives of the countries participating in the force. The foreign minister added that the 160 observers from Italy, Denmark, and Norway will mainly engage in administrative duties: 60 of them will report on the situation in the field directly to the Israeli security forces, and the other 100 will advise the Israeli Government and Hebron Municipality in a bid to attain a normalization of the situation in the city.
