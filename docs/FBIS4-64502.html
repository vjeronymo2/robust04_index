BFN [Text] Rome, May 4 KYODO -- Japanese Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata left Rome Wednesday [4 May] morning for France as part of his four-nation European tour, which will also take him to Germany and Belgium. Hata told Italian Prime Minister-Designate Silvio Berlusconi Tuesday night that Japan will further open up its market. He also said Japan wants to broaden the scope of relations with Italy, Japanese officials said.
