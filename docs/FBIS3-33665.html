Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the appointment of Mr. Liamine Zeroual, a number of parties, associations, and national organizations have expressed their satisfaction at and support for such an appointment; they have considered as a suitable choice at this difficult period. Among those parties and associations we can cite the Social and Liberal Party, the Arab Islamic Rally, Ettahadi, the National Organization of the Sons of War Veterans, the National Coordination Bureau of the Sons of Martyrs, the National League of the Algerian Students, the Algerian Society of Information and Communications, the National Union of the Algerian Students, the Algerian Women Organization for Solidarity with the Rural Family, the National Society for the Economic and Social development, the National Confederation of the Algerian Employers, and the FLN Youth Association. The above listed parties and organizations believe Liamine Zeroual is the suitable person for the current period and a factor for bolstering national concord and surmounting the crisis facing the country.
