Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Vladimir Beskromnyy and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow, 9 Mar -- A wide range of issues relating to ways to settle the Bosnian crisis are being discussed by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and Haris Silajdzic, prime minister of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Their talks have just started in Moscow. The head of the Bosnian Government arrived here during the night from France, where he has been on a working visit. Mate Granic, Croatian deputy head of government and foreign minister, is expected in Moscow later today. Kozyrev is to give a news conference in the afternoon following his meetings with the Bosnian and Croatian representatives.
