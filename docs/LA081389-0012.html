August 13, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRACING THE UP AND DOWNS OF JOHN TRAVOLTA'S CAREER

Where's Travolta? Where's Kim Novak? Where's Toppo Giggio?

There's something in Broeske's article that seems to expect a reply from Hollywood for its missing persons (as if we're serving up an answerable question to a logical deity).

We twist off the old heads and screw new heads onto the same bodies because it's Tuesday and we wouldn't have it any other way. What's the use of watching the tide rise and fall in a cup?

GREGORY BURNS

Glendale
