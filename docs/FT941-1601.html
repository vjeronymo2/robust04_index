940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Brown & Jackson in rescue talks with discount retailer Brown & Jackson, the UK's largest discount retailer which faces possible collapse if it cannot secure further working capital, yesterday confirmed talking about a rescue with a South African company. In a letter to shareholders the company said it was talking to Pepkor, a South African-based discount retailer, about a possible investment in Brown & Jackson. 'The discussions are at a preliminary stage and a further announcement will be made as appropriate,' the group said.
