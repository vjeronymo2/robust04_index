Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] The Armed Police Yunnan border guard division has produced good results in the effort against narcotics and weapons trafficking. According to incomplete statistics, between November 1992 and August 1993, this division solved over 600 cases involving smuggling, gun-running, and narcotics trafficking. It seized over 350 kg of heroin, 499 kg of opium, 385 guns of all types (including military guns), 49,467 rounds of ammunition, 397 hand grenades, 116 land mines, and 12 40-mm rocket launchers. It also seized 2,244 g of gold, 500 g of silver, automobiles, motorcycles, and other smuggled goods. The total value of seized items has exceeded 4 million yuan, and 984 criminals have been caught.
