931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Budget 93: Their Budget - The retailer Richard Woodall, 28. Married with a one-year old child and another on the way. Has small mortgage on three-bedroomed house in Wangford, Suffolk Job: general manager of Asda superstore in Great Yarmouth, Norfolk. The store occupies 46,000 sq ft and, with petrol, turns over pounds 500,000 a week Politics: last election voted Conservative and is a keen Tory supporter Verdict: 'From a business point of view, it is good for us. There have been no increases in VAT on books, foodstuffs, magazines or the like. Yes, there has been an increase in petrol, but we are bery competitive in this area. Personally, I am pleased there has been no increase in duties on beer and spirits. I drive a company car, so I am not affected there. I am particularly pleased there have been compensations for the VAT increases on fuel. Overall, I am pleased and a little surprised.'
