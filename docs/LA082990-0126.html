August 29, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

ARTS ON THE GREEN ACCEPTING ENTRIES

Orange County artists can submit a maximum of two entries for Arts on the Green, an annual festival of visual and performing art in Costa Mesa.

Orange Coast College art professors will judge entries for the show, to be held Sept. 22-23 at Town Center Park. Each artist may submit two entries at a cost of $5 per entry.

Art must be two-dimensional, be able to hang from a wall, be original and created within the last three years. Information: (714) 432-5629.
