May 14, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MOBILE FREE CLINIC, HEALTH CARE FUNDS

Once again we read (May 3) the news of another part of our health care system that is suffering from a lack of funds. This time it is the community development council's mobile free clinic.

America, America, where are our values? We hear and read that health care -- something that is so necessary to the whole community -- must pay for itself in order to survive. I ask this question: why do the Stealth and B-1 bombers not have to pay for themselves? Why doesn't the cruise missile pay for itself?

Something is terribly wrong here.

BERNICE RANFORD

Garden Grove
