Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In a statement issued today, the Red Star Battalions, affiliated with the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP], claimed responsibility for the armed operation that targeted a collaborator with the Zionist enemy, named 'Ayn Mim from the Ra's 'Atiyah village. The statement said the Martyr 'Umar al-Qasim Group opened fire on him in al-Sabi' Street in Qalqiliyah the day before yesterday, injuring him in the head. The enemy forces transported him to hospital.
