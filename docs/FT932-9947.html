930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Unigroup offshoot valued at Pounds 24m Unigroup, the timber and building materials group, announced that the subscription price for flotation of its Malaysian subsidiary has been fixed at Dollars 1.90 to value it at Dollars 98m (Pounds 24m). The subsidiary, Golden Pharos, will be offering 35 per cent of its capital to Malaysian institutional and private investors. Unigroup will retain 60 per cent, worth some Pounds 14m at the flotation price. Mr Jerry Owen, chief executive of Unigroup, said the flotation provided both companies with valuable cash injections which will substantially reduce borrowings and strengthen the underlying value of the group.
