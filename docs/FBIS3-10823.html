Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [from the "NTC Newscast"] [Text] President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo was the first citizen to cast his vote. With his wife Ana Milena Munoz present, he produced a copy of his identification card and cast his vote for the Senate, Chamber of Representatives, and Liberal Party presidential candidate elections. The president ruled out a break in relations with Cuba in the wake of an alleged meeting of Colombian guerrillas on the Caribbean island: [Begin Gaviria recording] It is obvious in international relations we cannot take action until facts are verified. [end recording] The president termed as positive the surrender to justice of alleged drug trafficker Julio Fabio Urdinola. Gaviria said he hopes the Prosecutor General's Office and the Justice Ministry will smooth out their differences. [Begin Gaviria recording, in progress] ...that we will be able to reach an agreement with the Prosecutor General's Office. [end recording] President Gaviria will remain at Narino Palace with his minister to monitor the elections.
