940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / World Trade News: US sees air travel up 5% in 1993 The US Transportation Department says 1993 was the busiest year ever for air travel between the US and other countries, with 92.5m passengers, up 5 per cent from 1992, AP reports from Washington. US airlines carried 54 per cent of that traffic, an increase of 1 percentage point from 1992, the department said. It was also a record year for international air freight, totaling nearly 4.7m tons, a 10 per cent increase over the previous year. US airlines carried 41 per cent of the freight traffic, also a one-point increase over 1992.
