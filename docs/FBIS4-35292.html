BFN [Text] Minister of Development and Planning 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani has threatened that Sanaa will close the door to dialogue with moderate and unionist leaders of the Yemeni Socialist Party if the UN Security Council [UNSC] intervenes in the Yemeni crisis. He said Sanaa is awaiting a political message from Aden but did not disclose its contents. Al-Iryani said one the reasons that military communiques have not been released since Tuesday is because the situation on the front is calm.
