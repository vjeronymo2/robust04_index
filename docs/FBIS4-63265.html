BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers has adopted a resolution regarding the border zone. The zone is established along Ukraine's state land border, as well as along banks of border rivers and water reservoirs, within territories of settlements and village councils of people's deputies, which are adjacent to the border. The resolution also approved general border regulations. In particular, they stipulate that citizens entering or staying within the border zone should have documents confirming their identity and need to enter the zone or remain there. Within the border zone, auto travel is limited, and a veterinary-sanitary zone is established, in which the breeding and grazing of livestock is prohibited. [passage indistinct]
