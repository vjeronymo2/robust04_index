Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] At about noon today, Manuel Camacho Solis, special commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, delivered a very brief message over the local radio. The message read as follows: This is Manuel Camacho Solis and I am the commissioner for peace. President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has approved an amnesty law. We are now waiting for its approval by the National Congress. This law will help us attain peace. The message adds: It is necessary to beg the Indians'and the communities' forgiveness for all the suffering they have endured. Now is the time to stop bothering one another. Now is the time to look after our families. Let everyone work in his own home and let everyone have tranquility in his community. The message goes on to say: There is a new governor. He is going to send his representatives to help you and your communities. Now is the time to work again in the [word indistinct], to take care of your families, and improve Chiapas. Chiapas is everything. Let us all make an effort to build peace in Chiapas.
