BFN [Text] Cairo, 14 May (MENA) -- Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid received a letter from Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev in which he expressed his country's readiness to cooperate with the Arab League in its efforts to contain the Yemeni crisis. The letter was delivered by the counselor at the Russian Embassy in Cairo when Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid received him today. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid welcomed this Russian initiative.
