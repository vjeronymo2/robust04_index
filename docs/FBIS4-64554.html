BFN [Text] Vienna, May 3 (AFP) -- The International Press Institute [IPI], made up of journalists from 85 countries fighting for freedom of the press, Tuesday [3 May] set up an IPI Russian Committee, a statement from the organisation said. The IPI said Russian media faced serious economic and administrative problems as well as a poor distribution system. The new IPI cell in Russia will report back to the central secretariat on the free flow of information and on general developments in the media.
