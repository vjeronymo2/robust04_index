BFN [Text] Salah Djebaili, director of Bab Ezzouar university who was assassinated 31 May, is to be buried today in his home town of Khenchela. Teachers and students had a last look at the deceased whose coffin was displayed yesterday evening in the university's main hall. The National Association of Professors and Lecturers called on all teachers and employees in all universities to stop work yesterday and today. University employees and teachers were also called on to meet at Bab Ezzouar university at 1000 next Saturday to organize a march at the university campus.
