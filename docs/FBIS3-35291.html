Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Amin al-Hafiz, former prime minister and chairman of the Chamber of Deputies' Foreign Affairs Committee, has stated that the message delivered by U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley to Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz contains an Israeli warning over what is happening in southern Lebanon. Al-Hafiz stressed that if the peace process gives Israel everything it wants, then it is not a peace process but a surrender process.
