Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 8 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Ulaanbaatar city authorities have complied with the request of the group of Mongolian and American citizens about foundation of a Baptist church in the Mongolian capital. In the rules of the Baptist church, which has been presented to the Presidium of the City Hural of representatives of citizens, it is pointed out that the Baptists of Mongolia would carry out their activity, being supported by the International Baptist Organization, carry out humane and charity actions, organize a work on teaching English language, contribute to development of health protection, education, agriculture, sports, radio and television broadcasting in the country. Thus, the first non-Buddhist, non-Moslem church in Mongolia will be founded.
