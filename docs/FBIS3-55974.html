Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Joint Statement of the Ministry of Finance and Central Bank of Armenia"] [Text] Yerevan, January 11 (SNARK) -- The juridical and natural persons on the territory of Armenia, who use foreign currency, including Russian rubles, for paying operations and haven't the license of the Central Bank of Armenia, will be prosecuted and may condemn to three-year imprisonment. Press-Center of the Central Bank informs SNARK about it. Simultaneously it is said in the statement on the territory of Armenia the legal means for paying are the notes of the State Bank of the USSR of 1961-1992 with value till 500 rubles and Armenian drams of 1993 with value 10, 25, 50, 100, 200, 500 drams.
