Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to reports from Russian and western news agencies, Yegor Gaydar told a briefing in Moscow today that he has not accepted the offer of a job as first deputy head of government. According to AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSE, he showed journalists a letter addressed to the president asking to resign. Yegor Gaydar explains that he is taking this action because he finds it difficult to accept responsibility for decisions has has not personally made. This refers to disagreements with other members of the government over implementation of the reforms.
