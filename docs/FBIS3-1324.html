Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 25 SAPA -- Three employees of the South African-based Executive Outcomes CC are believed to have been kidnapped by UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] rebels in Angola, a statement by the company's lawyers in Pretoria said on Friday [25 March]. The statement by Wilsenach, Van Wyk, Goosen and Bekker said the employees were believed to have been kidnapped by UNITA on Wednesday while investigating "criminal activities" near the company's training area in Angola. "The next of kin of the three members have been notified. Currently a large-scale operation is being conducted by the company and assistance is being rendered by the Angolan armed forces," the statement added. More information would be released later. Executive Outcomes provides security services in Angola.
