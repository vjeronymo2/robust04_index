May 14, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

LOMBARDI'S 8 RBIS PACE TIDEWATER

Phil Lombardi of Granada Hills drove in eight runs Saturday night as the Tidewater Tides defeated the Toledo Mud Hens, 10-3, in an International League baseball game in Toledo, Ohio.

Lombardi, a graduate of Kennedy High, had a two-run double in the first, a three-run double in the second, a two-run double in the third and a run-scoring single in the fifth. Tidewater is the triple-A affiliate of the New York Mets.

Lombardi, a first baseman, came within one RBI of tying the single-game league record, last achieved in 1963.
