Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India has called for renewed search for peace and stability in Afghanistan. Expressing deep concern at the recent events there, an External Affairs Ministry spokesman said in New Delhi this has resulted in heavy loss of life and destruction. He called upon the international community and the countries of the region in particular to urgently address the political and security situation in Afghanistan. Reiterating New Delhi's strong support to the unity, integrity, and independence of Afghanistan, he said India is willing to contribute to any such initiative. Meanwhile, 13 Indian diplomats and staff members in the war-torn Afghan capital, Kabul, arrived in New Delhi today by air from Lahore.
