October 17, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY BRIEFLY

Advanced Logic Research Revenue Up: Irvine-based Advanced Logic Research Inc., maker of personal computers, said Tuesday that it expects to report revenue for its fiscal year ended Sept. 30 in the range of $170 million to $172 million, up from $73 million a year ago.

For the fourth quarter, the company expects to report revenue of $48 million to $50 million, up from $26 million a year earlier. About $18 million of the fourth-quarter revenue was related to sales of the company's computers based on a new architecture.
