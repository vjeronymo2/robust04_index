April 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $36 Sunday when his two horses in the sixth race in his triple, Proud Irish and T.V. Screen, failed to win. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $775.40.

Bob's bet today is a $50 parlay from Latest Release in the fifth to Sports View in the sixth.

Sunday's Loss $36
