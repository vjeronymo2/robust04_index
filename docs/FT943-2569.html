940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Employment prospects rise Employment opportunities for the last quarter of this year are the best of any last quarter for five years, says a survey of employment prospects by Manpower, the employment services company. The survey - based on responses from 2,100 employers - shows 27 per cent of employers forecasting job increases and 13 per cent forecasting losses. The net balance of 14 per cent is double the 7 per cent for the last quarter of last year, although below the 15 per cent of the third quarter of this year.
