940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Instem advances in first half Instem raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 410,000 to Pounds 505,000 in the six months to June 30, alth-ough the electronics and information services group has continued to experience underlying pressure on margins. Turnover was lower at Pounds 8.04m (Pounds 9.5m) reflecting the nature of the USM company's project business, although order intake in both systems and electronics manufacturing businesses had increased. Earnings per share rose from 6p to 7.4p and the interim dividend has been stepped up to 1.4p (1.3p).
