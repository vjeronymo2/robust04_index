Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe February 21 TASS -- Russian and Tajik border guards together with the units of the 201st Russian division deployed in Tajikistan are engaged Monday [21 February] in a heavy combat with a group of Afghan and Tajik gunmen who had penetrated from Afghanistan. According to a source in the Tajik Foreign Ministry, the gunmen belong to supporters of Afghan Prime-Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar and Tajik rebel Islamist movement led by Mullo Adburahim [name as received]. In the morning, several frontier Tajik settlements were shelled with missiles launched from the Afghanistan territory. No casualties or damage have been reported so far.
