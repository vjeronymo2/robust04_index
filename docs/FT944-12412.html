941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / International Company News: Stadshypotek success The international equity portion of the privatisation of Stadshypotek, the Swedish mortgage lender, was five times subscribed, writes Martin Brice. A total of 15.4m shares at a price of SKr92 each was sold to international investors, giving that tranche a value of SKr1.4bn (Dollars 200m). The total raised in the offering, handled by Kleinwort Benson of the UK, was SKr3.4bn.
