940509 FT 09 MAY 94 / EU backs Gulf Arab states to unify tariffs The European Union backs plans by Gulf Arab states gradually to unify tariffs, paving the way for a free-trade accord, the Greek foreign minister, Mr Karolos Papoulias, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Riyadh. Speaking at the opening session of talks in Riyadh between the six-nation Gulf Co-operation Council (GCC) and the EU, Mr Papoulias also urged the oil-rich states to support environmental protection.
