Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev--The press service of Ukraine's border troops reported on 29 March that a trilateral meeting of the heads of the border troops of Ukraine, Hungary, and Romania in the Hungarian town of Nyiregyhaza had ended. In accordance with a proposal by Army General Valeriy Hubenko, head of the Ukrainian delegation, a decision was made at the meeting to set up a joint coordinative group of the three countries for the cooperation of border troops in the campaign against illegal migration and organized crime.
