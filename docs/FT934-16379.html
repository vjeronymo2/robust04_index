931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / London Stock Exchange: BAe easier Shares in British Aerospace (BAe) fell sharply as doubts about its proposed regional jets joint venture with Taiwan resurfaced following a renewed attack on the deal by the country's opposition political party. Opposition deputies were reported to have suggested that the Taiwan Government may drop its support for the proposed Pounds 250m venture. The reports once again led to jitters on the London market which saw the shares tumble 13 to 407p at the day's worst, before they steadied, assisted by the strong market trend, to close 9 off at 411p, in thin trading of 1.6m. One analyst said: 'There is nothing concrete or new in these doubts but it brings to the surface worries that the deal may yet fail.'
