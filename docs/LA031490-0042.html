March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

The Los Angeles office of the Houston law firm Fulbright & Jaworski (formerly Fulbright Jaworski & Reavis McGrath) has named six partners. Harry L. Hathaway, Tim C. Bruinsma and David A. Ebershoff join from Hill, Farrer & Burrill. Jesse D. Miller, Peter H. Mason and Douglas W. Stern join from Pepper, Hamilton & Scheetz.
