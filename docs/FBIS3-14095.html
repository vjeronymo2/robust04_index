Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Doha, 23 Mar (WAKH)--King Husayn of Jordan arrived in Doha from Muscat this morning on a two-day visit to the State of Qatar. His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of the State of Qatar, led those who received the Jordanian monarch. He was also received by His Highness Shaykh Hamad Bin-Khalifah Al Thani, heir apparent and defense minister, ministers, and ranking officials.
