May 20, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

EVERYBODY'S A CRITIC

Based upon the absolutely glowing review by Michael Wilmington (Movies of the Week, April 15), we decided to view "That's Life!" rather than Vaclav Havel's "Audience" on PBS or "20/20" on ABC. We saw a tedious, unfunny, unwitty, pop psychology movie and now understand why we never heard of it before. I taped the Havel play (fortunately) but regret missing "20/20."

Alan Rosin, Camarillo
