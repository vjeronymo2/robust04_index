930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Teredo recommends Cairn offer Teredo Petroleum is recommending acceptance of the Pounds 1.2m offer from Cairn Energy and the Cairn board has agreed to extend the offer to May 12. Since the Cairn offer was announced on April 7 Teredo has examined a number of proposals, none of which reached the stage at which it could be put to shareholders.
