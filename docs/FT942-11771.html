940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Compass turns to France for growth Compass Group, the catering and healthcare company that last week announced plans to buy Canteen Corporation, the third largest US caterer for Dollars 450m (Pounds 308m), yesterday switched its attention to France. It is acquiring, through Scandinavian Service Partner, its European airport catering arm, the airport restaurant division of Saresco for Pounds 10.7m cash. The business's main contract is with Charles de Gaulle Airport in Paris, although it also operates at Strasbourg and Toulon. It has 28 restaurants and bars and made pre-tax profits last year of FFr3m (Pounds 350,000) on turnover of FFr127m. Assets were about Pounds 4.5m at the year end, including net cash of Pounds 2m. Mr Fritz Ternofsky, chief executive of Compass's European catering operations, described the acquisition as an 'ideal first step for the group in France'. Compass surprised the City with its US acquisition plans last week. Mr Francis Mackay, chief executive, said the group would continue to expand organically in Europe.
