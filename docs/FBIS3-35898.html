Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 1 (SNARK)--Karen Baburyan, acting as a chairman of the Karabakh Parliament, told Yerevan TV "The war should be finished and the problem could be solved only by negotiations". According to Baburyan, Nagorno-Karabakh and Armenia for many times showed their readiness to start the negotiations without any preliminary conditions, including the negotiations under the aegis of the intermediary missions. Unfortunately, the official Baku took the opposite position. Saying "no" to the latest schedule of the necessary measures suggested by the Minsk group of the CSCE and the Russian peace- making initiatives, Azerbaijan follows the only way to solve the conflict -- by clashes. Baku also ignores all the attempts of the international community to achieve peace in the region -- Azerbaijan didn't send his delegation to the session of the European Council Parliamentary Assembly, Baburyan mentioned.
