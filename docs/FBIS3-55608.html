Language: English Article Type:BFN [by UKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev January 10 TASS -- The press service of the Ukrainian president confirmed that a Ukrainian-Russian-US meeting will be held in Moscow on Friday "with the aim to conclude trilateral talks". In Brussels US President Bill Clinton announceed that on Friday the United States, Russia and Ukraine will sign an agreement to eliminate all nuclear weapons from Ukrainian soil. "This trilateral agreement we will sign will lead to the complete removal of nuclear weapons from Ukraine", Clinton said, adding that this includes 176 intercontinental ballistic missiles and some 1,500 warheads targeted on the USA. The press service of the Ukrainian president also confirmed that a consultative meeting between Presidents Leonid Kravchuk and Bill Clinton will be held in Kiev on Wednesday.
