Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] One person has been wounded in a shooting incident outside the City Hall in Durban. Several shots were fired into the air by people in the crowd of Zulu supporters gathered outside to wait for the outcome of talks between King Goodwill Zwelithini and State President F.W. de Klerk. The Zulu King was rushed outside along with Chief Minister Mangosuthu Buthelezi to placate the crowd. They told the gathering to stop shooting, and calm has now been restored.
