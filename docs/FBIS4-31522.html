BFN [Text] A one-month cease-fire that was agreed in Geneva is coming into effect in Bosnia-Herzegovina at this very moment. However, reports coming from the front lines do not speak of peace. As regards the Brcko area, Serb artillery attacks have been in progress since last night against civilian targets in Boderiste and deep into the free territory. Reconnaissance units of the 108th Infantry Brigade of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO] of Brcko have reported that Serb tank reinforcements were being brought into the southeastern part of the Brcko area. As regards the Orasje-Samac area, the Serb aggressor engaged in provocations from small infantry weapons, the Orasje operative zone of the HVO reported. They said that the Serb Army was continuing to bring in reinforcements south of the so-called corridor around Krepsic and Pelagicevo.
