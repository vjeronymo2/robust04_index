920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / British Gas second in N Sea British Gas owns the second most valuable portfolio of North Sea oil and gas assets, according to the annual league table published by County NatWest WoodMac, the firm of stockbrokers. Although BP is still top of the league with Pounds 7.5bn worth of assets, British Gas has overtaken Royal Dutch Shell and Exxon to advance from fourth to second place, with a portfolio of Pounds 4.3bn.
