BFN [Text] Sarajevo, May 17 (AFP) -- Bosnian president Alija Izetbegovic led 360 Moslems from Sarajevo Tuesday [17 May] on a Saudi-financed pilgrimage to the Islamic holy city of Mecca. The pilgrims left Sarajevo for Zagreb on two separate flights aboard a Ukrainian UN plane. They were to be joined in the Croatian capital by 120 more Moslems for the flight to Mecca on planes provided by the Saudi Government.
