July 28, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

HARKEY WILL MISS REST OF SEASON AFTER SURGERY

Mike Harkey underwent arthroscopic surgery on his right knee Thursday and the Chicago Cubs said the former Cal State Fullerton pitcher will be sidelined the rest of the season.

Harkey, who was 2-7 at Triple-A Iowa with a 4.43 earned-run average in 12 starts, will begin pitching again in October. Harkey was scheduled to be in the Cubs' starting rotation last spring but failed to make the team in spring training.
