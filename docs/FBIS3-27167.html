Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 22 (XINHUA) -- The 12th round of talks between the working group of the Chinese Government delegation on border issues and the working group of the joint delegation on border issues of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, the Russian Federation and Tajikistan was held here from February 15 to 22. In a serious and realistic atmosphere, they discussed and made some progress on the border issues between China and the above-mentioned four countries. The two sides decided that the next round of talks will be held in Bishkek. During the talks, Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Dai Bingguo met with the working group of the joint delegation headed by Afanasyev.
