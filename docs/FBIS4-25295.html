BFN [Text] Beijing, June 8 (XINHUA) -- Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, met here here this afternoon with Chu Song-il, president of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, and his party. During the meeting, Hu recalled his visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) last July, saying that China and the DPRK are close, friendly neighbors, and that the traditional friendship between the two parties and countries has taken root among their peoples. The Chinese party and government cherish the friendly cooperative relations between China and the DPRK, which has withstood the tests of time, Hu noted. Under the current world situation, Hu said, consolidating and strengthening bilateral cooperation and friendship not only accords with the fundamental interests of two countries and the wills of the two peoples, but also is conducive to stability and peace in Asia and in the world at large. Hu also briefed the DPRK visitors on China's reform, opening and modernization drive as well as Communist Party construction. Ju expressed congratulations on China's achievements in its modernization drive. He said that strengthening exchanges and cooperation between his organization and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) will promote the friendly relations between the DPRK and China and that such relations should be passed on from generation to generation. The DPRK visitors arrived here yesterday as ACFTU guests.
