BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said that the Israeli Embassy in Rome was checking the positions of the neofascist ministers in the new Italian Government on questions related to Jews and anti-Semitism. Peres told the Knesset that the policy of the new government should be examined before reaching any decisions on the future of relations with Italy. Peres added that the deep friendship between the two countries must also be taken into account. Foreign Minister Peres will meet his Italian counterpart next week to discuss bilateral relations.
