BFN [Text] Shanghai, May 11 (XINHUA) -- Shanghai's exports are soaring this year. According to the latest statistics, Shanghai, China's largest economic center, exported 2.464 billion U.S. dollars-worth of goods in the first four months of this year, an increase of 26.64 percent over the same period of last year. A municipal foreign trade official estimated that the city's exports will climb to 8.5 billion U.S. dollars this year, up 15.4 percent over 1993 and exceeding the city's annual gross domestic product. Shanghai's key export products include ships, complete sets of equipment, machine tools, sewing machines, containers, garments, toys and shoes. In addition, the city has set up 420 trading and non-trading enterprises in more than 60 countries and regions to promote sales of Shanghai-made goods.
