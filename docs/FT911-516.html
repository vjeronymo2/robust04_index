910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Markets (At a glance): Guaranteed income bond 'best buys' Savers who want to buy guaranteed income bonds may be interested in the following 'best buys' compiled by Baronworth Investment Services. For one-year bonds, ACUMA is paying 9.27 per cent on sums of Pounds 1,000-Pounds 4,999, and 9.57 per cent on sums of Pounds 5,000-Pounds 19,999. Among the three-year bonds, Premium Life is paying 8.6 per cent on Pounds 1,000-Pounds 1,999 while Prosperity Financial Services is offering 9.4 per cent on sums of Pounds 2,000-Pounds 24,999. For five-year bonds, Cornhill Life is paying 9 per cent on sums of Pounds 1,000-Pounds 1,999, while Consolidated Life is paying 9.2 per cent on Pounds 2,000-Pounds 4,999.
