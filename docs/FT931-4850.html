930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Japanese acquire Pounds 0.7m stake in Arm A Japanese venture capital company is spending Pounds 650,000 to take a stake in Advanced Risc Machines, the semiconductor company owned by Acorn Computers of the UK and Apple Computer and VLSI Technology of the US. Nippon Investment & Finance, part of Daiwa Securities, is taking the stake in the company because of its technical expertise in designing high performance, low cost microprocessors.
