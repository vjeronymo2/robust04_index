931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / World Trade News: ABB buys Czech wire company Asea Brown Boveri, the international electrical engineering group, has bought Elektro-Praga, a leading Czech manufacturer of wiring accessories, Patrick Blum reports from Vienna. The price for the company to be renamed ABB Busch-Jaeger Elektro-Praga was not disclosed. A spokesman said the company would work closely with ABB's Busch-Jaeger German subsidiary, and help to introduce ABB products on to the Czech market and in central and eastern Europe.
