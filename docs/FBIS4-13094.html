BFN [Text] Denmark's defense forces chief, General (Jorgen Maine), who visited (Pando) Post along the Line of Control [LOC] in Azad Kashmir, was briefed on the situation in occupied Kashmir and the increasing violations of the LOC by Indian forces. He was accompanied by General Shamim Alam Khan, chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee. The Danish general was also apprised of the large-scale violation of human rights at the hands of the Indian forces in occupied Kashmir. He was told that infamous laws have been enforced in occupied Kashmir, under which the Kashmiris have been deprived of their basic rights. The Indian judiciary is not allowed to hear any case relating to any crime committed by the Indian forces against the Kashmiris. The Danish general was also informed that India has deployed a large number of troops along the LOC to prevent the helpless refugees from coming into Pakistan from India. He was also told about the unprovoked violation of the cease-fire, as a result of which civilians living along the LOC are being killed. The Danish general also attended a briefing at the field station of the UN observers in Muzaffarabad.
