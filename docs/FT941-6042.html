940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Biotrace deal with Ecolab Biotrace, the microbiological testing company that floated in November, has signed a North American marketing agreement with Ecolab, a Minnesota-based manufacturer of sanitising equipment with annual sales of over Dollars 1bn (Pounds 600,000). Previous US distributor ar-rangements have been ended. Ecolab's Klenzade division has been using Biotrace's hygiene testing systems for some time to demonstrate the effectiveness of their own cleaning products and services. Biotrace shares rose 4p to 155p.
