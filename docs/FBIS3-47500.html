Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] Nouhak Phoumsavan, Political Bureau member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee and president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], received Vu Oanh, Politburo member, who is in charge of guiding mass organizations, of the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] Central Committee, and the CPV delegation at the Presidential Palace in Vientiane yesterday afternoon. The Vu Oanh-led high-level CPV delegation is here to attend the funeral of Phoumi Vongvichit, adviser to the LPRP Central Committee, former Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee, former acting president of the LPDR, former chairman of the Lao Front for National Construction [LFNC], and former deputy prime minister and minister of education, sports, and religion, who passed away at 0822 on 7 January. Nouhak Phoumsavan expressed appreciations to the high-level CPV delegation led by Vu Oanh for making a trip to Laos to attend the funeral of Phoumi Vongvichit. This, the Lao president remarked, would contribute to the endlessly furthur improving and expanding of the long-standing special relations of friendship and all-around cooperation between the two parties, states, and peoples of Laos and Vietnam. On the same occasion, Vu Oanh extended regards and deep regrets from the Vietnamese party, state, and people to Lao counterparts for the loss of Phoumi Vongvichit, a much-beloved leader of the party, state, and people of Laos and a good friend of Vietnamese people. In addition, the Lao president and the guests also exchanged views on the issues of relations and cooperation between the parties, states, and peoples of Laos and Vietnam for the immediate future and in the long run to enhance the further strengthening of the time-honored fine traditions to flourish ever more.
