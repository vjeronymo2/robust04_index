Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS Parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow March 18 TASS -- The Russian State Duma refused on Friday to discuss the cancellation of the agreement instituting the Commonwealth of Independent States which was signed by the leaders of Russia, Ukraine and Belarus in 1991 and which actually signalled the collapse of the former Soviet Union. The proposal was submitted by Anatoliy Lukyanov, former speaker of the Soviet parliament, who faced trial as an organiser of the 1991 coup which resulted in the Soviet collapse, but was amnestied by the Duma. Lukyanov is now a member of the "Russian Way" group in the Duma.
