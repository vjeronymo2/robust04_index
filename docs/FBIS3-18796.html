Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The Social Democratic Party [SPD] calls the failure of the membership negotiations with Norway a "disgrace" for the governments of the European Union. The inflexible attitude by Spain and Britain toward Norway calls the already concluded agreements with Austria, Finland, and Sweden into question again, Deputy SPD Chairwoman Heidi Wieczorek-Zeul said in Bonn on Wednesday. The negotiations in Brussels have shown how necessary a reform of the Europan institutions is "at the head and at the limbs," if an expanded European Union wants to remain capable of acting.
