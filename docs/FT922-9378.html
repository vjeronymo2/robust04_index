920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Bass sells 16 pubs to JT Davies Bass has sold 16 pubs in London and the south-east to JT Davies & Sons, a Surrey-based wine merchant and pub operator. The deal, estimated to be worth between Pounds 2.5m and Pounds 3m, is part of a Pounds 4m expansion programme announced by JT Davies last year. The company, which runs the Davisons chain of 78 wine stores, now has an estate of 35 pubs.
