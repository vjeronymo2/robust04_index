Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk today held a conference in the Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers to discuss a number of topical economic issues such as, in particular, the need to create favorable conditions for the Ukrainian national economy to function this year. The meeting also looked at the state of affairs in implementing the Ukrainian Supreme Council's resolution on measures to stabilize the socioeconomic situation in Ukraine. Acting Ukrainian Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, Vice Premier Vasyl Yevtukhov, National Bank of Ukraine Board Chairman Viktor Yushchenko, State Property Fund Head Volodymyr Pryadko, and representatives of a number of ministries and departments took part in the conference.
