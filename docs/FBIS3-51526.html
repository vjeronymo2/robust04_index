Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Vice Foreign Minister Isabel Allende has concluded a working visit to Las Palmas, Canary Islands, where she met with political and government officials. The vice foreign minister met with Manuel Hermozo, president of the government of the Canary Islands, and with Jesus Gomez, vice president of the Canary Islands council. The island authorities confirmed a coming visit to Cuba by representatives of the government of the Canary Islands to sign an agreement for the renovation of the building that will house the headquarters of the Cuban-Canary Islands Association. Allende's working visit to Spain began on 11 January in Madrid where she met with high-ranking Spanish Foreign Ministry officials to analyze relations between the two countries and to prepare for Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina's scheduled visit to Spain.
