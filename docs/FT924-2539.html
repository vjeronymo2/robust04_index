921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / International Company News: Maybelline lifts share offer price THE flotation of Maybelline, the US cosmetics company which is owned by interests associated with Wasserstein Perella, the US investment banking boutique, went ahead yesterday. The 4.65m shares being sold to investors were priced at Dollars 23.50 each - higher than the original estimates of Dollars 20 to Dollars 22 a share.
