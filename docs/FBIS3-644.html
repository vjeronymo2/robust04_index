Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 10 SAPA -- The South African Defence Force [SADF] has not been deployed in Bophuthatswana, South African Defence Ministry Communications Manager Dr DAS Herbst said in Johannesburg on Thursday night. He said the SADF was not on a basis of alertness specifically with regard to Bophuthatswana. Dr Herbst was speaking against the background of ongoing speculation that the SADF had moved into the riotous homeland. "Any decision about Bophuthatswana will be made by the South African Government and in which the Transitional Executive Council will be involved," Dr Herbst said. He pointed out however that the SADF had, on an ongoing basis, contingency plans for all events.
