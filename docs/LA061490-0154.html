June 14, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

EAST VS. WEST: Are Florida beaches more uptight than California's? . . . This week Florida authorities banned from state beaches bathing suits that fail to cover the buttocks entirely. . . . Such suits are common here. One local company reports string bikini sales have jumped from 10% to 25% of the total since last year. . . . One concession: Laguna Beach's Margie Webb Swimwear makes suits that come with two bottoms -- one string and one three inches wide. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
