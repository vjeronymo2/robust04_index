930417 FT 17 APR 93 / UK Company News: Elys suffers 43% decline to Pounds 502,000 Pre-tax profits of Elys (Wimbledon) showed a 43 per cent decline, from Pounds 879,000 to Pounds 502,000, in the year to January 30. Turnover for the period fell by Pounds 472,000 to Pounds 12.11m. A same-again final dividend of 14.5p is proposed to maintain the total for the year at 16p. The dividend is covered 1.8 times by earnings per share of 29p (49.3p).
