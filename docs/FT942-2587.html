940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Bankers Inv Trust Bankers Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 185.7p per share as at April 30, a rise of just 1.2 per cent on the trust's year-end value of 183.5p. The increase on a year-on-year basis was 21 per cent. Net revenue for the six months to end-April dipped to Pounds 2.79m (Pounds 3.01m); earnings per share emerged at 1.78p (1.93p). A second interim dividend of 0.97p (0.9p) brings the total to date to 1.94p.
