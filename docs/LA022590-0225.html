February 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DON'T-MISS FILMS WITH MINIREVIEWS FROM TIMES CRITICS;

CAL

(1984) Bravo, Sunday at 5 p.m. and 11:30 p.m. A grave, mournful movie set in a dreary Northern Ireland in which a reluctant young Irish Republican Army recruit (John Lynch) is caught up in a nightmarish merry-go-round of violence but seeks love and redemption with one of its victims, a young widow (Helen Mirren). PATRICK GOLDSTEIN
