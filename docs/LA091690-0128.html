September 16, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

Miraleste 26, L.A. Baptist 14 -- Miraleste took a lead it would not relinquish when Danny Sarner scored the first of his four touchdowns on a seven-yard run on the first drive of the nonleague game at Monroe High.

Sarner finished with 218 yards in 28 carries for Miraleste (1-1).

Dane Brown completed 14 of 30 for 161 yards and one touchdown for L. A. Baptist (0-2). Colyn Van Buren carried seven times for 33 yards. J. SCOTT KLEIN
