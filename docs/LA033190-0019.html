March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT BUSH BANS BROCCOLI

Well, well! At last we know that our chief executive can take a definite stand on something instead of moving those skinny hands of his in that phony "meaningful" way and saying nothing about every really important question that has come up since he came into office.

At least we know he hates broccoli -- who says he's a wimp? Hooray!

ROSLYN SMITH

North Hollywood
