Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Assam, the officer in charge of the Gopur Police Station of Sunitpur District was killed in a bomb explosion last night. Another person received serious injuries. Police sources at Guwahati said that the suspected ULFA [United Liberation Front of Assam] militants planted the bomb under the bed of Mr. T. Dadahara in his residential quarter, inside the police station complex. Reports say police have recovered an unexploded bomb this morning inside the campus of the station.
