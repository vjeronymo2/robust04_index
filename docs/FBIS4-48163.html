BFN [Text] The minister of foreign affairs, Alhaji Babagana Kingibe, said Nigeria and Algeria have lessons to share in the recent international developments. He made the remark while receiving his Algerian counterpart, Mohamed Salah Dembri, in Abuja. The minister noted that both countries needed to exchange views on the task of establishing a durable democracy in their societies. The Algerian foreign minister is at the head of a six-man delegation with a message from President Liamine Zeroual to General Sani Abacha. The delegation is expected to hold talks on the forthcoming OAU summit with the Nigerian authorities.
