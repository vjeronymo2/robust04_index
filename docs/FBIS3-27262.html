Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] To strengthen ties with Hong Kong and Macao press circles, the Guangzhou City Press Office held a forum in the China Hotel yesterday afternoon for high-level leaders of CHENG PAO, TA KUNG PAO, WEN WEI PO, HSIN WAN PAO, and AOMEN JIH PAO, as well as of Hong Kong and Macao radio and television stations. Guangzhou city party Secretary Gao Siren and Mayor Li Ziliu briefed them on the situation. Among those present at the forum were the principal leaders of NANFANG RIBAO, YANGCHENG WANBAO, and GUANGZHOU RIBAO, as well as provincial and Guangzhou city radio and television stations.
