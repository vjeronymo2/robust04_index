920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / World Trade News: ABB's Dollars 255m cable contract Asea Brown Boveri, the Swiss-Swedish engineering group, is to supply Dollars 255m (Pounds 140.8m) of equipment for a planned high-voltage link of direct current between southern Sweden and northern Germany. writes Andrew Baxter. ABB has reached agreement with Baltic Cable, jointly owned by Sydkraft and Preussen Elektra, two Swedish and German utilities, to supply two converter stations and a 250km submarine cable.
