BFN [Text] On 29 April, the Executive Council of the Confederation of Independent Trade Unions in Bulgaria [KNSB] adopted a package of urgent measures aimed at overcoming the destabilization of the financial system and the crisis in income and production. An appendix to the package was adopted which demands the government inform the parliament and the trade union about commitments to international institutions, which affect production in Bulgaria. The Executive Council will convene the KNSB Coordinating Council on 9 May. The goal is to report the result of the warning strike and national protest rallies, conducted by 6 May, to update the assessment of the sociopolitical situation in the country, and determine the KNSB attitude toward the government.
