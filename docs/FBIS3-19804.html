Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Koenigswinter (DPA) -- Rainer Eppelmann, east German Bundestag deputy and last GDR defense minister, is the new chairman of the Christian Democratic Union Social Committees (CDA). The 51-year-old priest was elected to this post at the CDA Federal Executive Committee meeting in Koenigswinter on Saturday [19 March]. He received 77 of 82 votes cast. There were three votes against him and two abstentions. [passage omitted]
