931124 FT 24 NOV 93 / Abel Matutes to lead Partido Popular in European elections Spain's conservative opposition party, the Partido Popular, said yesterday that Mr Abel Matutes, the EU energy commissioner, is to leave Brussels early next year to lead the party in the European elections next June, writes Tom Burns in Madrid. Mr Matutes has served on the Commission for the past seven years. A fellow PP politician, Mr Marcelino Oreja, will take the position, one of two Spanish commissioners.
