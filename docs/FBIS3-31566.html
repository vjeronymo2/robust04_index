Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Zvornik, 22 Feb (TANJUG) -- The Muslim side again violated the cease-fire last night in the Sarajevo area, TANJUG learned from Serb military sources in the Srpska republic. According to the same sources, UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] members identified the culprits and the location from which they opened fire, and "a resolute effort" was made to stop the shooting from Muslim positions. According to Serb military sources, members of Muslim units have been using infantry weapons to target Serb areas from several directions. The Muslim side violated the cease-fire in the Lukavica, Lemezovic Kuca, Kosevo, and Grbavica areas of Sarajevo. [passage omitted]
