Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The second round of formal peace talks between the government and the Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF] will be held in Jakarta, Indonesia. The talks beginning 13 to 22 April this year will focus on the substantive portions of the Tripoli Agreement of 1976. They will also take up the reports by the joint working groups, joint cease-fire committee, and the mixed committee set up during the first formal talks last November, also in Indonesia. MNLF Chairman Nur Misuari said he remains optimistic about the outcome of the peace talks. Misuari was interviewed by reporters in Sulu, where he delivered a message of peace at a mosque in Jolo.
