Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Jakarta, Jan 24 (ANTARA) -- Foreign Affairs Minister Ali Alatas is on an official visit to Yemen from January 22 to 27, 1994 at the invitation of his Yemeni counterpart. His agenda in Yemen include meetings with the Yemeni Foreign Affairs Minister, Trade Minister, Minister of Development Planning Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani and Industry Minister Mohammad Sa'id al- 'Attar, according to the minister's programme received from the Yemeni embassy here Monday. Minister Alatas is also scheduled to pay courtesy calls on President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Prime Minister Haydar al-'Attas and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. A bilateral cooperation agreement between Indonesia and Yemen is also expected to be signed during his stay in Yemen. Alatas' visit is the first visit ever made by an Indonesian official to Sana'a since the reunification of north and south Yemen in May 1990. Indonesia opened an embassy in Sana'a in 1992 and Yemen has had an embassy in Jakarta since 1990.
