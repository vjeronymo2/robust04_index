February 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WHODAM WINS CZARAVICH HANDICAP

Whodam took the lead at the head of the stretch and went on to a two-length victory in the $50,000 Czaravich Handicap Wednesday at Aqueduct.

The winner, carrying 117 pounds and ridden by Angel Cordero Jr., covered the 1 1/16 miles in 1:43 3/5 and paid $4.20. Wind Chill finished second in the seven-horse field, 2 1/4 lengths ahead of Dr. Carrington.
