Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] In Islamabad today, the Senate unanimously adopted a resolution strongly condemning the incident of firing at Ibrahim Mosque in al-Khalil City. Scores of people were martyred or injured in the firing by a Zionist with the help of Israeli authorities. In the resolution presented by Justice Minister Iqbal Haider and Hafiz Hussain Ahmad, the house described the incident as the century's worst act of terrorism. The house also decided to dispatch copies of the resolution to various international fora. In her brief speech on the occasion, Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, on behalf of the government and the people of Pakistan, expressed deep sympathy with the bereaved families. Expressing complete solidarity of the people of Pakistan with the Palestinian people, she said Pakistan will continue to extend full support to the cause of the Palestinians forever.
