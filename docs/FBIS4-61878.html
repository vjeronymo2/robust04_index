BFN [Text] Kyrgyz President Askar Akayev discussed in Bishkek Tuesday the program of his visit to Paris with the French Ambassador Pierre Morel. During the visit planned for 1-3 June, Akayev is expected to meet French President Francois Mitterrand, as well as to sign agreements. Prepared for signing are agreements on trade and economic cooperation as well as on cooperation in the sphere of science, culture and education. It is expected that Kyrgyzstan will sign the Paris Human Rights Charter adopted in 1990. During the talk Akayev said that he attaches great importance to his visit to France since it will be his first visit to Europe. The president also said he was interested in the intensification of cooperation between the two countries. During the visit it was stressed that Kyrgyzstan was interested in attracting French private capital to the Republic.
