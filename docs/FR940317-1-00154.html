Payment of solatia in accordance with local custom as an expression of sympathy toward a victim or his or her family is common in overseas commands (see DA Pam 27&hyph;162, chap 8). Such payments are not to be made from the claims CEA. These payments are made from local operation and maintenance funds pursuant to directives established by the appropriate commander for the country concerned. This applies even where a command claims service is directed to administer the command's solatia program.
