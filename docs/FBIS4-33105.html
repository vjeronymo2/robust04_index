BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres spent a lightning, four-hour visit in Buenos Aires last night. The foreign minister conferred with Argentine President Carlos Menem and briefed him about the autonomy agreement with the Palestinians. After the meeting, Minister Peres told reporters that Israel has evidence that a foreign country was involved in the explosion at the Israeli Embassy in Buenos Aires two years ago. He added that Israel will publicize the details after it completes its investigation into the incident. Twenty-two people were killed and many wounded in the explosion. Minister Peres also denounced the continued presence of Nazi criminals in Argentina.
