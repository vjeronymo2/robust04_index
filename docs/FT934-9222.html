931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Wyevale Garden calls for Pounds 10.9m Wyevale Garden Centres is raising about Pounds 10.9m through a rights issue of 7.54m ordinary shares at 152p each. The issue is on the basis of 2-for-9 ordinary shares and 2-for-10.12601 preference. The shares fell 4p to close at 181p. The company, which currently trades on the USM, has applied for admission to the Official List and dealings in its shares are expected to start today. It also announced the acquisition of Challis Garden Centre near York for Pounds 1.22m in cash. Wyevale said it was also considering other acquisition opportunities and the rights proceeds would enable it to respond quickly should they materialise. The issue is fully underwritten by Morgan Grenfell and brokers are Credit Lyonnais Laing.
