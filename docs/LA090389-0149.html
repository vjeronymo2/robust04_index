September 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Nebraska Coach Tom Osborne said that "Big Red Confidential: Inside Nebraska Football" -- a book that reviews the problems of the Cornhusker football program over the past eight years -- is not all bad.

Osborne said that author Armen Keteyian and a 1986 National Collegiate Athletic Assn. investigation have failed to implicate the school's coaching staff or administration in any wrongdoing.
