940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Lofs London & Overseas Freighters, the Bermuda-based shipping company, returned pre-tax profits of Dollars 4.17m (Pounds 2.78m) for the year to end-March. That compared with Dollars 1.09m last time. The fourth quarter contribution improved from Dollars 722,000 to Dollars 1.8m. Earnings per share for the 12 months emerged at 10.2 cents (4.3 cents).
