920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG was lifted by a number of factors including a higher bullion price and stronger overseas markets. The overall index gained 87 to 3,151 while the industrial index was 84 higher at 4,104. The gold index rose 48 to 905. Anglos put on R6.20, or 7.2 per cent to R92.25.
