930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / Tajik-Afghan summit agreed TAJIKISTAN and Afghanistan agreed yesterday their leaders would hold a summit meeting to resolve a conflict over their border but talks with Tajik rebels failed to free five hostages, Reuter reports from Dushanbe. The commander of Russian troops guarding the border said talks to free four Russian and a Kazakh soldier, taken into Afghanistan on Tuesday, had so far failed.
