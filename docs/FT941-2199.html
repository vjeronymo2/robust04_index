940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Trafford Park rises to Pounds 2.44m Turnover of Trafford Park Estates, the property investment, management and development concern, edged ahead from Pounds 4.87m to Pounds 4.98m and pre-tax profits expanded to Pounds 2.44m for the six months ended December 31 1993, compared with Pounds 1.74m. Earnings per share were 2.413p, against 1.72p, while the interim dividend is lifted to 0.95p (0.85p) - last year's final payment was 1.95p from pre-tax profits of Pounds 4.12m. The directors stated that the general outlook for the full year, and long-term prospects for growth, were promising with further increases in rental income anticipated.
