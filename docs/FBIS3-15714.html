Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Pacific Ocean" Program] [Text] Even if the Defense Ministry does not have any money, other latter-day structures have. The first issue of VOYENNYY PARAD [MILITARY PARADE], the new illustrated analytical news magazine, saw the light of day in Moscow. The magazine will be published once every two months and will analyze the problems of Russian military and strategic policies. Among its publishers are the Association of Investors in the Conversion of the Defense Complex of the Russian Federation and the U.S. Zigzag Venture Group company.
