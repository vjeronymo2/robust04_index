Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Voting on Pakistan's resolution on Kashmir at the UN Human Rights Commission session at Geneva today has been postponed until 1930 this evening to allow time for further consultations. The postponement was sought by Pakistan. Reports say that Islamabad is under pressure from some Islamic Conference Organization -- ICO -- countries, notably Iran, to withdraw the resolution. As the commission took up voting on Pakistan's resolution early during the day, the chairman informed the house that Pakistani and Iranian delegations have sought postponement of the vote.
