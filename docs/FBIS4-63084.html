BFN [Text] Over the course of five days, Russian border guards carried out an operation to destroy gunmen in the rear sectors of the 11th and 12th posts of the Moskovskiy border guard detachment in Tajikistan. Since the beginning of May, gunmen have fired almost daily on details and military cantonments of the Russian border troops. According to preliminary information, which INTERFAX obtained from the press office of the Russian border troops, more than 20 gunmen of the Tajik opposition were killed and a large quantity of weapons and ammunition was seized during the operation.
