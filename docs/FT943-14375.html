940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / German armed forces return to the Champs Elysees German armoured cars yesterday rolled down the Champs Elysees in Paris for the first time since the second world war as part of the new Eurocorps' involvement in France's Bastille Day parade. President Francois Mitterrand, who invited the Germans, said he felt 'emotion and joy' at helping to heal the wounds of the last war. The Eurocorps comprises troops from France, Germany, Belgium, Spain and Luxembourg.
