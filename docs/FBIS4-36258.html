BFN [Text] Rabbi Shlomo Goren [former chief army chaplain] has once again published a halakhic edict pertaining to political affairs; this time with regard to PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat. In his halakhic edict, Rabbi Goren contends that Yasir 'Arafat deserves to die according to both Israeli and international law, because he has declared a jihad. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that the halakhic ruling is included in a communique published by the Committee of Jewish rabbis in Judaea, Samaria, and the Gaza Strip.
