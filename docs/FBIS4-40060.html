BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat 14 June TASS -- "We cannot conceive a future for our selves without close cooperation with the Russian Federation, without mutual fraternal support in all aspects of policy and economy. Friendship with Russia is for us the basic foreign political factor, proceeding from which we determine our positions in international relations," President Saparmurat Niyazov said in a message of greetings to President Boris Yeltsin on Russia's Independence Day. The message says that having embarked on a path of deep-going reforms the renewed democratic Russia has made it clear to the world that it is a great power which will not succumb to temporary difficulties. "I am confident that the time is not far away when Russia shows the world its own "economic miracle" and regain its might and glory," President Niyazov said. The peoples of great Russia and Turkmenistan have been bound together by their common historical fate and deep inter-penetration of cultures, as well as by the common tasks facing them now. "We know that Russia's well-being is a major component of our own independence which we value highly and of our own well-being both at present and in the future," Turkmenistan's president said. Niyazov also sent a message of greetings to Viktor Chernomyrdin expressing satisfaction with the level of relations between the two countries, as well as gratitude for support which the Russian premier extends to Turkmenistan.
