July 12, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'Dick Tracy' Not a Video Star Yet: Disputing a report that "Dick Tracy" may appear in video stores as early as November, the Walt Disney Co. said Wednesday that the film's video debut has not been set.

The Wall Street Journal, citing industry sources, said in its Wednesday edition that "Dick Tracy" could arrive in video stores for the Christmas season. But officials for Disney's home video arm said that while the company has discussed "Dick Tracy's" video release with retailers, no firm decision has been made. Disney also has not decided the suggested retail price for the video. GREG BRAXTON
