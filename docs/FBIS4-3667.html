BFN [Text] Beijing, April 25 (XINHUA) -- China today expressed "heartfelt congratulations" to Tsutomo Hata on his being elected prime minister of the new Japanese Government. In response to a question from correspondents, a Foreign Ministry spokesman said, "We would like to express our heartfelt congratulations to Mr. Tsutomo Hata on his election to prime minister of the new government at both House of Representatives and House of Councillors of the Diet this afternoon." "Mr. Hata is a statesman well known to the Chinese people. We appreciate the zeal and vision he has shown for closer Sino-Japanese friendship and cooperation and the tremendous useful efforts he has made toward this end. We are confident that during his tenure of office, Sino-Japanese relations will surely develop further on the present basis," the spokesman said.
