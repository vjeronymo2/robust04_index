BFN [Text] According to SAKINPORM, Georgia's customs department has received a letter from the Customs Committee of the Russian Federation signed by Kruglyakov, first deputy chairman of the committee. The letter says: Because the Republic of Georgia has been a member of the CIS since 1 March 1994, in accordance with the edict issued by the Russian Federation Customs Committee, the heads of its regional departments and customs houses have been officially instructed not to levy a tax in the form of VAT, special duties, and excise duties on goods produced in Georgia and carried from the Georgian territory retroactive to 1 March 1994. Money already paid for the above-mentioned taxes should be refunded from the customs house account or it can be applied to future payments.
