BFN [Text] Moscow, 12 May -- Representatives of the Abkhazian side at the Georgian-Abkhazian talks in Moscow have said that they are against the deployment of CIS or Russian troops on the entire territory of the Abkhazian region. They justifyg their stance through the fact that the United Nations has not yet adopted a resolution on the deployment of peacekeeping forces in the Abkhazian region. According to observers, the Abkhazian side's stance is just an excuse in order not to compromise.
