BFN [Text] Pakistan has condemned the assassination of a prominent Kashmiri leader and scholar, Mirwaiz Dr. Qazi Nissar Ahmed, in Anantnag-Islamabad. Briefing newsmen in Islamabad today, a Foreign Office spokesman described the murder of Qazi Nissar Ahmed as an act of terrorism which, he said, is the handiwork of Indian agencies. The spokesman said the Indian intelligence agencies have chalked out a sinister plan to eliminate religious and political leadership in occupied Kashmir in order to fragment the Kashmir struggle.
