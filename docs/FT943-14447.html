940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Increased losses for Waterglade Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.37m against Pounds 954,000 were announced by Waterglade International Holdings, the property investment and development group, for the half year to November 30 1993. The result last time, however, included significant profits from property disposals, the company said. Turnover fell to Pounds 172,000 (Pounds 1.03m) but administrative expenses were cut from Pounds 1.04m to Pounds 961,000 including Pounds 130,000 relating to the costs of the capital reconstruction in June 1993. Losses per share were 0.24p (0.08p).
