April 26, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

PACOIMA MAN FOUND FATALLY STABBED IN CAR

A 47-year-old Pacoima man was found stabbed to death in his car early Wednesday.

A passing motorist saw the victim in his car parked on Weidner Street near San Fernando Road and called police about 7:30 a.m., Lt. Bernard D. Conine said.

The victim, whose name was not released pending notification of his family, had apparently been dead for several hours, Conine said. There were no suspects, and police did not know why the man had been parked there.
