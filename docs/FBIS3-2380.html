Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At the 4 March provincial conference of party secretaries of the schools of higher learning, Jiang Chunyun, secretary of the provincial party committee, pointed out the need to further strengthen and improve the ideological and political work of the schools of higher learning, promote their reform, development, and stability, and strive to build well the socialist universities with Chinese characteristics. On behalf of the provincial party committee, Jiang Chunyun set four expectations and demands on the work of the schools of higher learning. First, it is imperative to thoroughly study Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, adhere to the socialist orientation for running schools, build well the socialist universities with Chinese characteristics, and strive to bring up the builders and successors to carry on the socialist cause who are morally, intellectually, and physically developed. Second, firmly focusing on the general objective of establishing the socialist market economy system, it is imperative to comprehensively promote the reform of the schools of higher learning. Jiang Chunyun pointed out: Educational reform should be focused on the general objective of establishing the socialist market economy system, and a new mechanism for education compatible with the development of the socialist market economy should be established as soon as possible. Third, it is imperative to greatly strengthen and improve ideological and political work, train the new people who have lofty ideals and moral characters and are better educated and well-disciplined, and urge party members, teachers, students, and administrative personnel to embrace correct ideals, faith, and concepts on life and value. Fourth, it is imperative to further strengthen party leadership over the schools of higher learning, improve their leading bodies, attach importance to upgrading their teachers and cadres in charge of political work, and increase investment in higher education. Jiang Chunyun urged the schools of higher learning to uplift the spirit, unite as one, work in a down-to-earth manner, and strive to build socialist universities with Chinese characteristics. Han Xikai, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, presided over the conference. Attending were Dong Fengji, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and director of its propaganda department; Cui Weilin, vice chairman of the provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and secretary of the provincial higher education work committee; and pertinent comrades of schools of higher learning and relevant provincial departments.
