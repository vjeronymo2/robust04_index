941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / Royal Mint workers to strike Workers at the Royal Mint are to hold a 24-hour strike next month over a performance-related pay scheme. Members of three civil service unions at the mint in Mid-Glamorgan, voted by 170 to 64 to strike on December 8. The cleaners, messengers, scientists, supervisors, engravers and administrative staff rejected an offer of 1.8 per cent.
