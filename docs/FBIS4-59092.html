BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers held an extraordinary meeting today under Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, deputy prime minister industry minister, and acting prime minister. The Council of Ministers reviewed developments in the general situation in the country since the rebelling secessionist gang, led by the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], exploded the situation militarily with the objective of returning the country to pre-22 May 1990 status. During this meeting, the Council of Ministers praised the heroism of the Armed Forces soldiers, noncommissioned officers, and officers, the forces of unity and Constitutional legitimacy. The Council also praised the people's rallying around these forces. The Council of Ministers commended all the decisions and legitimate measures adopted by the Presidential Council and the House of Representatives. The Council of Ministers discussed a number of important issues, particularly issues related to supply, trade, electricity, and oil, and took the necessary measures on them in order to reinforce the course of normal life in all governorates.
