940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Minister quits in Zambia A Zambian cabinet minister and the deputy speaker of the National Assembly resigned yesterday, saying they wanted to give the government time to investigate allegations against them of drug dealing, Reuter reports from Lusaka. Community development and social affairs minister Princess Nakatindi Wina and her husband Sikota Wina said other members of President Frederick Chiluba's cabinet were involved in corrupt practices and urged them to resign.
