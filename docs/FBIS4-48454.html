BFN [Text] Pretoria May 18 SAPA -- Almost 7,000 former Umkhonto we Sizwe (MK) [Spear of the Nation; ANC military wing] troops are being processed into the SA [South African] National Defence Force [SANDF] at Wallmansthal outside Pretoria, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Wednesday. A spokesman at the base said the group would be trained by MK until the end of next month. If they passed a selection process after their initial training they would be allowed to choose a career in one of the four arms of the SANDF.
