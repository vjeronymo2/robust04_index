BFN [Text] The Latvian Government must still adopt a resolution on participation in the Cocom [Coordinating Committee for Multilateral Export Controls] organization, the multilateral strategic export control coordination committee (?to) monitor the export of weapons, strategic resources and technology to former countries of the Warsaw Pact and other communist lands. It would be in Latvia's interests to accept this system, because it would stimulate foreign trade and access to investment, chiefly in such fields as electronics, computer systems, and high technology, says a press statement from the Latvian Foreign Ministry. The member states of the Cocom have changed their strategy of activity, to promote the recovery of the market of the former communist countries, at the same time maintaining control over the investigation...
