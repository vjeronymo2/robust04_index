BFN [Text] On separate occasions yesterday [19 April], Governor Ruan Chongwu and provincial people's congress Chairman Du Qinglin met a friendly visiting 6-member delegation of the Commercial and Industrial Resources Committee of the ROK National Assembly, led by committee Chairman An Tong Saeng. The delegation was visiting our province at the invitation of the provincial people's congress. Ruan Chongwu and Du Qinglin separately briefed the guests on our province's investment environment and on other provinces' investments in the island, and hoped that both sides would conduct more friendly exchanges and develop cooperative projects. An Tong Saeing said that Hainan was a place full of vitality, and expressed his hope to develop undertakings in Hainan. In attendance at the meetings were Wei Zefang, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, and Mao Ping, secretary general of the provincial people's congress, as well as those in charge of relevant departments.
