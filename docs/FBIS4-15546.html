BFN [Text] The leadership of the Russian Defense Ministry did not consider the question of suspending the airlift to the former Yugoslavia of an additional contingent of Russian peace-keepers due to the NATO air strike against Serb positions in the area of Gorazde, INTERFAX was told by the spokesman for the Defense Ministry Vladimir Kosarev. He said that a Ukrainian infantry company of the peace-keeping contingent is stationed in the vicinity of Gorazde where earlier NATO forces bombed the Bosnian Serb positions. The spokesman said that there are no casualties among the Ukrainian servicemen. Earlier the Federation Council decided to send to the former Yugoslavia an additional 300 Russian servicemen to raise the number of the Russian contingent to 1500 people.
