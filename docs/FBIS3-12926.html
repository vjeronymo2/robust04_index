Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Press statement issued by the Russian press office in Tunis on 1 March] [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin decided to send his personal envoy to carry out consultations with the leadership of the PLO within the existing framework of Russian-Palestinian cooperation to resolve issues involving a Middle East settlement. President Yasir 'Arafat received a personal letter from President Boris Yeltsin in which he expressed Russia's strong intentions to help on all fronts to reach comprehensive peace and security in the Middle East. Yasir 'Arafat expressed deep appreciation for the support that the Russian president was giving Palestinian people during these difficult times. He pointed out Russia's important role in the Middle East. The meeting with the Palestinian leader focused on steps aimed at preserving the peace process in the light of the current tension in the occupied territories after the Hebron tragedy. The Russian side expressed support for the Palestinian position on the necessity of securing an international presence in the occupied territories that would become an additional guarantee against the possible recurrence of such provocative acts. President 'Arafat received with appreciation the Russian decision to provide practical aid to establish Palestinian self-rule. With the importance of Russian-Palestinian cooperation in the peace process in mind, an agreement was reached to continue close contacts.
