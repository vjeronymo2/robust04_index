Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Tunis, the Palestinian Popular Struggle Front [PPSF], a PLO-affiliated group led by Samir Ghawshah, has criticized the agreement signed in Cairo by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres. The PPSF said that it considers the agreement incomplete, stressing that it cannot help complete talks on the other issues to reach a comprehensive agreement.
