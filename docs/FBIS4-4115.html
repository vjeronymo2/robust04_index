BFN [Text] Seoul, April 27 KYODO -- China's paramount leader Deng Xiaoping is in intensive care at a military hospital following a sudden deterioration of health, a Seoul daily reported Wednesday [27 April]. The SEGYE TIMES [SEGYE ILBO] in a dispatch from Beijing quoted Western sources as saying that Deng, who will turn 90 in August, has been hospitalized and is in critical condition. The newspaper said in a front-page story that rumors are circulating in Beijing about Deng's worsening health, and possible death. An official at the Foreign Ministry's [country not further identified] Northeast Asia Bureau reached by phone dismissed the report as totally groundless.
