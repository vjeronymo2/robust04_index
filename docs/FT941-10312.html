940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Decentralised pay deals may pick up The trend towards decentralised bargaining in the public sector is expected to pick up speed again this year after the brake imposed by last year's uniform 1.5 per cent settlement, according to pay analysts Incomes Data Services. An IDS report says the government's policy of applying a pay bill freeze throws into doubt the future of the pay review bodies which recommend pay levels for about one-third of public-sector workers.
