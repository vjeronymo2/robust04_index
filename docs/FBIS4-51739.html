BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 2 (KCNA) -- Foreign mass media gave special write-ups on the occasion of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. The Pakistan paper NIDAE WAQT April 15 gave special write-ups with a portrait of President Kim Il-song. The paper edited articles titled "Kim Il-song, the Great Sun" and "Warm Congratulations to His Excellency President Kim Il-song, the Great Leader of the Korean People, on His 82nd Birthday." It wrote in the preface: Today the Korean people and the world people are grandly celebrating the birthday of President Kim Il-song as a common auspicious fete of mankind. President Kim Il-song has turned Korea into a powerful socialist country independent in politics, self- sustaining in the economy and self- reliant in national defence in a short historical period, and a people's paradise. He enjoys boundless respect and reverence of the Korean people and the world revolutionary people as the most outstanding leader of the working class for his immortal feats and contributions to the Korean revolution and the world revolution. The Tanzanian paper MFANYA KAZI, the Yemeni paper AS-RAI AL-AAM, the Nepalese paper MAJADUR KISSAN, the Guyanese paper NEW NATION and the Ugandan paper THE NEW VISION gave special write-ups. The Exito Radio of Peru, a radio of Equatorial Guinea, the Kilikya TV of Lebanon organized congratulatory programs on his birthday. The French Committee to Support the Founding of the Democratic Confederal Republic of Koryo, the Algeria-Korea Friendship Club, the Guyana-Korea Friendship Association, the Zimbabwe-Korea Solidarity and Friendship Association and the Burundi-Korea Friendship Association issued bulletins on the occasion of the April 15 fete.
