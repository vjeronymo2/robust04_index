921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 0.77m for enlarged Southern Radio Southern Radio, which broadcasts in Kent, Sussex, Hampshire and the Isle of Wight, achieved a pre-tax profit of Pounds 774,000 for the year ended September 30 1992. The results are the first since the merger between Southern Radio and Radio South, and include the latter from December 6 1991. In the previous year there was a loss of Pounds 363,000 after exceptional charges of Pounds 382,000. In the current year, revenue had been encouraging, although trading in the south-east continued to be short-term and unpredictable. Earnings per share in the year came to 2.02p (losses 3.51p). The dividend is 0.794p.
