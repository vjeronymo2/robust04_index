BFN [Text] Indonesia appreciates the Philippine Government's handling of the Manila conference on East Timor. The conference is taking place after the Philippine Supreme Court overruled a lower court's ban on the conference. Only Filipinos are attending the conference, however, after the Philippine Government exerted various effective efforts to bar foreigners from attending the conference. According to a press release from the Directorate for Foreign Information of the Department of Foreign Affairs received by RRI [Radio Republik Indonesia] this evening, the Philippine Government has also given assurances that the conference is not being used as a platform to launch a campaign of lies or an international disinformation campaign against Indonesia. In this connection, Indonesia respects the sovereignty of other countries and the right of their citizens to hold debates.
