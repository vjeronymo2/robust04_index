BFN [Text] Doha, Apr. 25, INA--Qatari Information Minister Dr. Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Kawari received today Iraq's Information Under Secretary and Head of the Iraqi Delegation, Naji al-Hadithi. During the meeting, the Qatari minister welcomed the Iraqi cultural delegation, saying the Arab culture is part and parcel, and its most prominent sources are the Iraqi culture and arts. The Iraqi cultural week will certainly contribute to strengthening cultural ties between Iraq and Qatar, confirming his country's keenness to help succeed this cultural demonstration, Dr al-Kawari said. For his part, the Iraqi official affirmed that this week was a message of love and solidarity from Iraq's men of letter to Qatar and an expression of solidarity and cooperation and good ties between the two sisterly countries. He lauded efforts by the State of Qatar exerted to help succeed activities of the Iraqi cultural week.
