920519 FT 19 MAY 92 / S African murder probe ordered South Africa's chief detective has been told to investigate evidence that a cabinet-level committee ordered the 1985 murder of black activists, Reuter reports from Cape Town. The Law and Order Ministry said Col Carel Brits, national head of the murder and robbery unit, would go to Port Elizabeth to lead the investigation into the unsolved murder of Mr Matthew Goniwe.
