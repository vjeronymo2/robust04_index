930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / OFT to probe bus company's routes THE OFFICE of Fair Trading is to investigate allegations of anti-competitive behaviour by Fife Scottish Omnibuses, part of Stagecoach, the Perth-based bus company. The investigation follows a complaint from Moffat & Williamson that Fife Scottish has been running uneconomic services on 11 routes operated by Moffat & Williamson in Fife.
