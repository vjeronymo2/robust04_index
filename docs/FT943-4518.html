940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cakebread Robey Cakebread Robey & Co, the Enfield-based building materials concern, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 106,000 to Pounds 425,000 in the first half of 1994, helped by a Pounds 213,000 surplus this time on the sale of properties. Following the closure of some branches, turnover edged up to Pounds 8.96m (Pounds 8.87m). Interest was unchanged at Pounds 49,000 and earnings per share came to 7.1p (1.8p).
