Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Three political formations, i.e. the National Party for Solidarity and Development [al-Hizb al-Watani lil-Tadamun wa al- Tanmiyah], the Boumedienist Islamic National Front [al-Jabhah al-- Wataniyah al-Islamiyah al-Boumedianiyah] and the Front of the Generations of Independence [Jabhatu Ajyal al-Istiqlal] announced, in a statement released today, the formation of the Constitutional National Movement [al-Harakah al-Wataniyah al-Dusturiyah]. According to the statement, the formation of the movement comes after the dissolution of the Constitutional National Rally which included 12 political formations.
