940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / International Company News: Brascan settles dispute with banks Brascan, a key holding company in the Edper-Hees group, has settled a dispute with an international banking consortium, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. It will repay immediately USDollars 62.5m or 12.5 per cent of Dollars 500m owed to 10 foreign banks led by Chemical Bank of the US. A lawsuit begun in the UK by the banks has been dropped and Brascan will consult the banks before making any further significant asset sales. Another Dollars 12.5m will be paid to Germany's Commerzbank, which is owed another Dollars 100m.
