930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Sun Hung Kai Properties Sun Hung Kai Properties, the leading Hong Kong property development company controlled by the Kwok family, has announced a 21 per cent rise in net profits for the first half to December to HKDollars 3.24bn (USDollars 420m), up from HKDollars 2.67bn a year earlier, writes Simon Davies in Hong Kong. The interim dividend is set at 45 cents a share, up from 39 cents. In spite of uncertainty on the Sino-British dispute over political reform, demand for its residential projects had been strong so far this year.
