920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value falls at Contra-Cyclical Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust had a net asset value of 35.8p per capital share at June 30 against 51.6p a year earlier. For the three months to end-June net revenue amounted to Pounds 269,269 (Pounds 360,370) for earnings per share of 3.4p (4.5p). The interim dividend is a same-again 2.25p. The trust was incorporated in February 1991 and started trading on March 28 1991.
