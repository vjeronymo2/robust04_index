940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / UK Company News: CrestaCare to buy ScotCare for Pounds 12.5m CrestaCare, the third largest private nursing home operator in the UK, is to expand into Scotland with the proposed Pounds 12.5m acquisition of ScotCare, the nursing home group. The consideration is to be satisfied in part by an issue of 20.8m new shares at 30 1/2 p, providing Pounds 6.35m. The remainder will be payable in cash, with Pounds 4.36m due on completion and Pounds 1.8m in September 1995.
