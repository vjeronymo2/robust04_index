October 1, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

PARENTING SKILLS FOCUS OF CLASS SERIES

Classes on basic parenting skills such as family management and communicating with children will be offered by the Learning Center, a storefront school at Anaheim Plaza.

Conducted in both English and Spanish, the free classes will held Wednesday for three weeks, beginning this month. For information, call (714) 999-3791.
