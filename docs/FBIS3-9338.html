Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 23 March (MTI) -- Following a government decision approved by parliament, Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky is to present the official membership application of the Republic of Hungary to Greek European Affairs Minister Theodoros Pangalos, whose country currently holds the EU presidency, Foreign Ministry spokesman Janos Herman told MTI today. The minister is to submit the application on April 1, 1994, the spokesman said.
