930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Higgs & Hill makes Pounds 720,000 purchase Higgs & Hill has acquired Carter Commercial Developments for Pounds 720,000 plus a profit-related payment from the receiver of Flintclose, a subsidiary of Speyhawk. Carter specialises in the development of out-of-town retail sites. Mr John Theakston, chief executive of Higgs, said the purchase was an important part of the company's strategy as it came out of recession.
