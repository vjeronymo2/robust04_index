Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Despite the agreement on a ceasefire in Karabakh from 1 March, hostilities in the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict area are continuing. According to reports from Stepanakert, during the past 24 hours the Azerbaijani side has repeatedly shelled the positions of Karabakh forces in the north of Mardakertskiy rayon. For its part, the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reports artillery bombardments of inhabited localities in a number of rayons in Azerbaijan. According to information from Baku, battles from entrenched positions took place from first thing this morning on all sectors of the front.
