BFN [Text] Sanaa, 3 May (INA) -- A Yemeni Interior Ministry source today revealed the parties behind the criminal assassination attempt on the life of Hasan Muhammad Makki, the Yemeni first deputy prime minister, last Thursday. In a statement tonight, the source said that preliminary investigations conducted by the competent authorities have proven the involvement of Shaykh Naji 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Shayif, who is member of the General Committee, which is the highest leadership at the General People's Congress [GPC], in the assassination attempt in which eight of his loyalists took part. The source pointed out that the Yemeni authorities have failed to arrest Shaykh Naji al-Shayif because he and his loyalists fled the Yemeni capital, Sanaa, on Monday.
