Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO ["SSH Public Statement"] [Text] The SSH [Socialist Party of Croatia] leadership distributed the following public statement at a news conference: 1) The Republic of Croatia must immediately and clearly, without any reservations, declare its support for a unified Bosnia-Herzegovina, which should not be divided on an ethnic basis. 2) The Republic of Croatia must make a clear and unambiguous statement that it will strive to protect, with all means except military ones, those elements of the Croatian nation who live outside the republic's borders. 3) The Croatian people -- a substantial part of whom gave their votes of confidence to the HDZ [Croatian Democratic Community] at the last elections -- did not vote for a policy of war and military engagement in other countries. They must be informed of all the relevant factors that have led to the growing international isolation of Croatia and the threat of international sanctions. 4) The introduction of sanctions against Croatia would be counterproductive, as confirmed by the case of Serbia, and they must be avoided in the interest of the majority of the population which would be hardest hit.
