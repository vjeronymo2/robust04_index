July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA;

WOMAN HIT BY CAR, KILLED AT INTERSECTION

An elderly Santa Ana woman was struck and killed when she ran into the intersection of Harbor Boulevard and 17th Street in front of oncoming traffic, police said.

Ignoring a red light and "don't walk" sign, Guu Teh Pak, 69, ran into a crosswalk about 8:46 p.m. Monday and was struck by a car driven by a 17-year-old male, who was not held or charged, according to Santa Ana police spokeswoman Maureen Thomas.

Pak was taken to UCI Medical Center, where she was pronounced dead at 11:17 p.m., Thomas said.

Police did not identify the driver because of his age.
