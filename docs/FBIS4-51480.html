BFN [Text] Beijing, May 28 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Russian Prime Minister V.S. Chernomyrdin and his party left here today for a visit to China's largest industrial city of Shanghai. He Guangyuan, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of machine-building industry, accompanied Chernomyrdin on the trip. The Russian prime minister is scheduled to visit the Pudong New Area while in Shanghai.
