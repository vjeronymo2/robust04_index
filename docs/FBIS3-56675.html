Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The Russia's Choice faction in the State Duma intends to submit to deputies the following amendment to the provisional standing orders: A deputy who undermines the dignity of the State Duma through his speeches or actions will -- by the decision of the chamber -- be banned from participating in 10 of its sittings. The faction has made this decision because on 13 January Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party, used his speech from the podium to insult and directly threaten deputies. The Russia's Choice amendment also states that if a deputy banned from participating in the chamber's sittings does not obey the decision, the chairman will be able to summon help from the security services.
