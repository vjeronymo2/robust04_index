Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow, 10 Mar -- The partnership which was difficult to achieve must not be allowed to weaken; its content must be specified on the basis of a complete equality of rights and obligations and the partnership must acquire a mature and strategic outlook. It is in this tone that Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev is preparing to hold an open dialogue with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher in Vladivostok. The Russian minister stated this today while opening a session of the Foreign Policy Council in Moscow, devoted, in particular, to the problems of European security, questions of relations with NATO, and other international structures.
