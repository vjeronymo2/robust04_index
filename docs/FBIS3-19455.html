Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Vienna today, 'Abd-al-Jabbar 'Umar al-Duri, under secretary of the [Iraqi] Foreign Ministry met separately with Dr. Shinon Berg, secretary general of the Austrian Foreign Ministry, and Dr. (Hovel Miller), head of the Foreign Ministry's Political Department. During the two meetings, bilateral relations and ways of promoting them were discussed. 'Abd-al-Jabbar 'Umar al-Duri briefed the Austrian officials on the Iraqi people's suffering as result of the unfair blockade imposed on it despite Iraq's fulfillment of its commitments toward the UN Security Council resolutions. The Austrian officials expressed their hope for lifting the blockade imposed on Iraq as soon as possible so that it could take take its human and civilized role in the international community. 'Abd-al-Jabbar 'Umar al-Duri arrived in Austrian capital yesterday on an official two-day visit.
