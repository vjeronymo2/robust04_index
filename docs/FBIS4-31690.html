BFN [Text] Dr. Franjo Tudjman, president of the Republic of Croatia, and his aides have arrived on the first official visit to Bosnia-Herzegovina and Sarajevo, as was announced for some time. President Tudjman was met and welcomed by Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina, Kresimir Zubak and Ejup Ganic, president of vice president of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina respectively, Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic, Sarajevo Mayor Tarif Kupusovic, and other prominent politicians and public dignitaries. The official Izetbegovic-Tudjman talks and the talks at the level of the delegations of the governments of the Republics of Bosnia-Herzegovina and Croatia opened in the building of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina immediately after the festive welcome with the highest state honors. After these talks, Dr. Franjo Tudjman, president of the Republic of Croatia, will visit the Embassy of the Republic of Croatia. Dr. Tudjman's visit to Bosnia-Herzegovina and Sarajevo will end early this afternoon with a luncheon that will be hosted in honor of the prominent guest from the Republic of Croatia by Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina.
