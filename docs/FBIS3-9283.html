Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The extreme sections of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO] are continuing to violate the cease-fire agreement. Provocative fire was again opened with infantry arms. One member of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina was wounded near Plocari in the Fojnica commune. In the Bakovicaka Ciptonja region, the fighters of the HVO used incendiary ammunition and set ablaze the forest close to our defense lines. Members of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] can confirm what the situation on the ground is. As the Information Service of the 1st Army Corps reports, our units are observing the cease-fire agreement.
