Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 24 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU), met with a group from the Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions and hosted a dinner in its honor. The two-member group, headed by President of the Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions Fadhil Mahmoud Gharib, arrived here on March 23 at the invitation of ACFTU.
