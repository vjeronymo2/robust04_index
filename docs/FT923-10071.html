920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Brammer in Pounds 7m French purchase Brammer, the industrial services company, has conditionally agreed to acquire Roulement Service, a Strasbourg-based bearing distribution business, for FFr70m (Pounds 7.3m). Of the consideration, FFr60m is payable in cash on completion with the balance in three equal instalments after three years.
