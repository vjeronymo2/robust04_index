Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Turkish map with statistical figures] [Text] The inflation rate in 1993 was 71.1 percent. During December, prices increased by 3.6 percent. According to a statement issued by the State Statistics Institute, the consumer price index increased by 71.1 percent and the wholesale price index by 60.3 percent in the past year. In December, consumer prices rose by 3.6 percent, while wholesale prices registered an increase of 2.9 percent.
