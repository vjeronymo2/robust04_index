Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister of Transport Adib Halasah has denied reports by the Israeli media on statements attributed to him on a plan to establish cooperation between Jordan and Israel in the field of transportation, especially in the Gulf of Aqaba area. Halasah told the Jordanian News Agency that reports on this issue are baseless. He said that the Jordanian delegation's stand during the meetings of the second European conference on transportation recently held on the Greek Island of Crete was not to contact the Israeli delegation taking part in the conference.
