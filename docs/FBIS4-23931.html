BFN [Text] Alioune Blondin Beye, UN special representative in Angola, has returned to Lusaka after visiting Huambo and Luanda for consultations with UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] and government leaders at the weekend. In Huambo, Beye briefed Savimbi and his closest aides on the positive steps that have been taken at the talks. He guaranteed that some 90 percent of the hurdles have already been overcome. He conveyed similar views to Angolan President of the Republic Jose Eduardo dos Santos. UN sources in Luanda noted that Beye received a positive response from UNITA and government.
