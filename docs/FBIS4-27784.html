BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 6 June Morning Edition] [Text] Yohei Kono, president of the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP], gave a news conference in Fukuoka City on 5 June, in which he commented on how to deal with the DPRK's (North Korea) suspected development of nuclear arms. He said: "It is necessary to exert efforts to make North Korea understand correctly the appeals and demands of the international community." He stressed that priority should be given to continuing the dialogue with the DPRK.
