941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / Rebels in Cabinda claim capture of military base A separatist movement in Angola's oil-rich enclave of Cabinda said yesterday it had captured a military base, killing 25 government soldiers, Reuter reports from Kinshasa. FLEC-Renovada (Liberation Front for the Cabinda Enclave - Renewed) said in a statement released in Kinshasa its forces had captured the base at Tando-Zinze early Tuesday. Cabinda is separated from the rest of Angola by a 25km strip of Zairean territory.
