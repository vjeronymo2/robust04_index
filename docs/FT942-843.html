940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Adam Opel - Other companies Adam Opel: General Motors' German subsidiary, will tomorrow hold its annual press conference in Frankfurt. High on the agenda is likely to be the feud with rival Volkswagen over Mr Jose Ignacio Lopez de Arriortua, the former GM director who defected to VW in March last year. Opel will also spell out its 1993 results - recent speculation suggests that it lost DM500m (Dollars 306.7m) last year - and comment on trading in the first half of this year.
