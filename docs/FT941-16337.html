940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / Management (The Growing Business): Information for small businesses - In a Nutshell A new series of basic information fact sheets for small businesses has been welcomed by leading academics. Business Hotline Publications has drawn on an impressive number of contributors from companies, business institutions and trade associations to produce 36 fact sheets covering subjects from the writing of business plans and forming businesses, to caring for customers, choosing accountants, computers and premises and coming to grips with employment law. James Curran, Professor at the Small Business research Centre, Kingston University, said: 'I see many business publications but know of none that crams so much practical information into so few pages.' Business Hotline Publications - 081 640 7806 - Pounds 72 till end February.
