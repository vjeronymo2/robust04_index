Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 29 (YONHAP) -- The Foreign Ministry designated 15 overseas missions as diplomatic hubs for the country's efforts for internationalization, a senior ministry official said Saturday. The fifteen are embassies in the United States, Japan, Germany, France, Britain, Italy, Canada, Australia, Switzerland, Austria, Belgium, Netherlands, Sweden, Hong Kong and Singapore. The missions will take on the job of gathering information on high technology and high quality products and deliver them to Seoul, he said. They will also collect, investigate and analyze information, laws and practices on environment protection and transportation and will report the results to Seoul. The Foreign Ministry also is studying plans to form a large-scale inspection team with officials of related ministries and send it to foreign countries. Meanwhile, the ministry will issue daily reports on international situation with assistance of the Institute of Foreign Affairs and National Security and will distribute them to related ministries.
