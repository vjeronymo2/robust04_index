BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and President Francois Mitterrand of the friendly French Republic, spoke on the telephone last night. The two leaders discussed relations between the two friendly countries and ways of promoting them to serve common interests. They discussed developments in Yemen after some secessionist, rebellious elements within the Socialist Party ignited a war as a means to achieve their objectives. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council briefed the French president about the reality of what is happening in our country and the brave confrontation of the secessionist rebels to champion unity, democracy, and constitutional legitimacy. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council expressed his extreme appreciation for the position of the French president and France in support of unity, democracy, and party pluralism in our country. The French president reiterated his country's support for Yemeni unity, democratic approach, and development drive.
