920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / Ramos ahead in Philippine poll Mr Fidel Ramos, the former Philippines defence secretary, has widened his lead in the presidential election, with 54 per cent of the vote counted, writes Jose Galang in Manila. Mr Ramos, has recorded 861,900 more votes than Mr Eduardo Cojuangco, the business tycoon and 'crony' of former president Ferdinand Marcos. In third place is Mrs Miriam Defensor Santiago.
