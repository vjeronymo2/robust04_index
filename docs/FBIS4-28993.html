BFN [Text] Australia and Vietnam have signed an agreement on cooperation in education and training. The Vietnamese minister of education and training, Tran Hong Quan, and Australia's minister for employment and training, Simon Crean, signed the agreement in Hanoi. The agreement reinforces Australia's commitment to building closer trade and investment links with Vietnam. Mr. Crean is in Vietnam as part of the seven-day program promoting Australia's education and training expertise in Vietnam, Indonesia, and Malaysia.
