930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Scottish Eastern Strength in overseas markets and currencies lifted the net asset value of Scottish Eastern Investment Trust by 14 per cent from 69.5p to 79.2p in the year to January 31. After-tax revenue rose from Pounds 9.02m to Pounds 10.17m, while earnings per share came out at 1.59p (1.41p). The recommended final dividend is 1.02p making a total of 1.52p (1.42p).
