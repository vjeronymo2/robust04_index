October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

"A Cry for Help: The Tracey Thurman Story" was a realistic and disturbing story of spousal abuse. Dale Midkiff and Nancy McKeon were riveting in their portrayals of abuser and victim. Such productions are few in number yet valuable and substantial in their impact on public awareness. Midkiff and McKeon are to be lauded for their contribution.

Jeannette R. Nemon, Woodland Hills
