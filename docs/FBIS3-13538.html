Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Mar (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that the Libyan request to send Egyptian forces to the Aouzou Strip to supervise the Libyan withdrawal from the region and their handing it over to the Chadian authorities is currently under consideration. Commenting on this issue today, he said that a decision will be made proceeding from Egypt's chairmanship of the OAU.
