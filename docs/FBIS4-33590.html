BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Ecuadoran economic developments monitored through 7 June. Juan Jose Illingworth, director of the National Institute of Statistics and Census, reported on 30 May that the monthly consumer price index for Ecuador's urban area dropped to 1.38 percent in May compared to 3 percent in April. He added that the accumulated index for the past 12 months stands at 26.9 percent. (Paris AFP in Spanish 0148 GMT 31 May 94) Guillermo Ortega, director of the National Banana Program, has confirmed that the restrictions imposed by the EU on Latin American banana exports have not yet affected Ecuador, because such measures will first be fully implemented on 1 July. In fact, Ecuador increased its volume of banana exports by 25.8 percent and revenues by 24 percent during the first trimester of 1994 compared to the same period in 1993. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 1607 GMT 6 Jun 94)
