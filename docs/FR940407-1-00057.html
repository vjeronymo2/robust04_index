Commenters in Docket No. 91&hyph;51 suggested several alternative allocation methods, including a method based on cargo carried on the outbound portion of the voyage or based on revenue generated by Commission and non-Commission regulated cargo. These proposals stemmed from the bifurcation of regulatory authority in the domestic offshore trades between the Commission and the Interstate Commerce Commission. However, that split in jurisdiction has no direct connection with the costs a carrier incurs in providing service. The Commission shall not attempt to contrive an allocation methodology as a solution to an issue that can best be remedied by legislative action.
