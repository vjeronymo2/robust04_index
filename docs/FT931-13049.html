930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / Observer: Overdue With Virgin Atlantic and British Airways bosses puffing on the peace pipe last night at Richard Branson's grand Holland Park home, the guillotine baskets remain woefully empty. Brian Basham, the public relations consultant temporarily hired by BA and quickly embroiled in Virgin's 'dirty tricks' allegations, so far is the only persona non grata at Speedbird House. His role, however, is likely to be immortalised in the latest catchphrase in the rough, tough airline world: 'If you can't beat 'em, Basham'.
