930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / UK Company News: de Morgan cuts loss to Pounds 258,000 Pre-tax losses at de Morgan Group were reduced from Pounds 1.71m to Pounds 258,000 in the year ended April 30. Mr Patrick Cooper, chairman, said turnover in both core activities of property investment and finance advice had improved significantly. Turnover came to Pounds 1.91m (Pounds 1.38m) producing an operating loss of Pounds 293,000 (Pounds 1.73m). Net borrowings fell from Pounds 70,000 to Pounds 24,000. The chairman said that in recent months the number and value of property transactions had shown a marked increase. Losses per share came to 0.54p (3.06p).
