BFN [Text] In a marathon session, parliament gave the go-ahead for the referendum on Austria's accession to the European Union [EU] on 12 June. The vote was 140 in favor, 35 against the EU accession act. In the vote by name, all deputies of the Social Democratic Party, the Austrian People's Party, and of the Liberal Forum, as well as the Environment spokeswoman of the Green Party, Monika Langthaler, voted in favor of EU membership.
