920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Finlan Gp losses well down at Pounds 2m Finlan Group, the contracting and construction combine, reduced its loss from Pounds 5.95m to Pounds 2.12m, in the half year ended September 30 1991. This time there were no exceptional charges (Pounds 2.5m) and interest payable was cut to Pounds 683,000 (Pounds 3.14m). Loss per share came to 0.6p (7.2p). The directors pointed out that the period ended prior to the reorganisation and did not take account of the resultant significant reduction in overheads and borrowings.
