940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Sharpe & Fisher rises 61% A 'steady improvement' in trading conditions in its core business of building supplies helped Sharpe & Fisher increase pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.45m to Pounds 2.35m for the year to end-December. The 61 per cent improvement was achieved on a Pounds 7m sales rise to Pounds 52.3m, assisted by continued tight control of costs. Operating profits grew by Pounds 1m to Pounds 2.89m. In October it acquired the seven building supplies depots of Phillips & Son (Alton), bringing the total number of depots to 22. Acquisitions added Pounds 1.34m to sales and Pounds 22,000 to operating profits. The property side produced profits of Pounds 1.43m (Pounds 1.3m). Interest charges fell to Pounds 541,000 (Pounds 688,000) but tax took Pounds 448,000 (added Pounds 159,000). Earnings came out at 9.2p (7.8p) and a final dividend of 2.7p makes a 4.2p (4p) total.
