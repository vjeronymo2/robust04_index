BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 2 (KCNA) -- Talks were held here Sunday between secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop and secretary of the national executive committee of the American Popular Revolutionary Alliance of Peru Javier Pedro. Present at the talks were officials concerned. At the talks the sides informed each other of their party activities and exchanged views on further developing relations between the two parties and a series of issues of common concern. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
