Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 0930 today, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi received the All-Polish Trade Unions Agreement delegation led by Mrs. Ewa Spychalska, chairwoman of the All-Polish Trade Unions Agreement. Attending the meeting were comrade 'Izz-al-Din Nasir, Arab Socialist Ba'th Party Regional Command member and head of the General Federation of Trade Unions [GFTU], and comrade Mustafa 'Isa, member of the GFTU Executive Bureau. During the meeting, the talks dealt with relations of cooperation between the two friendly countries and peoples, means of promoting them in all fields, and issues of mutual interest. The chairwoman of the All-Polish Trade Unions Agreement expressed her appreciation for the Syrian achievements under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad.
