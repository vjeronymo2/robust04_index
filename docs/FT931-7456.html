930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Shirescot Shires High-Yielding Smaller Companies Trust reported a net asset value of 111.5p per share as at December 31; fully diluted the figure was 109.58p. The trust, launched on August 28, achieved net revenue of Pounds 334,000 for the period to end-December, equivalent to fully diluted earnings of 2.62p per share. A dividend of 2.2p is proposed for the period; directors intend to pay a total distribution of not less than 5p for 1993, in line with the prospectus forecast.
