BFN [Text] In Punjab, dreaded militant Avtar Singh, the deputy chief of the Babbar Khalsa, was among four militants killed in an encounter in Punjab. A large quantity of arms and ammunition was recovered from them. Police officials say the security forces cordoned off an area near Sangrur on a tip-off, leading to the encounter. Avtar Singh carried an award of 10 lakh rupees. Police say the gang led by Avtar Singh was responsible for over 100 killings in Punjab. Avtar Singh visited Pakistan on numerous occasions and brought back consignments of weapons to Punjab.
