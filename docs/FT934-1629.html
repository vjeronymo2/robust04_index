931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: MS recovery continues MS International, the specialist engineering products manufacturer, continued along its recovery path with profits of Pounds 462,000 before tax for the six months to October 30, against Pounds 250,000. Turnover was ahead slightly from Pounds 13.8m to Pounds 13.9m, including Pounds 214,000 from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 1.1p, compared with 0.6p, while the interim dividend is unchanged at 1p. Mr Michael Bell, chairman, said there was a considerable level of improvement in the performance of a number of the individual businesses. Some other operations, however, still had some way to go to achieve what could be deemed as 'acceptable levels of trading consistency.'
