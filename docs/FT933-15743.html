930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / International Company News: Cogeco Cogeco, a Canadian cable operator and broadcaster and newspaper publisher, earned CDollars 5.5m, or 38 cents a share, in the nine months, up from CDollars 1.3m a year earlier when after preferred dividends nothing was available for the common stock. Sales were CDollars 144m, up 9 per cent. The company is raising CDollars 65m of new equity.
