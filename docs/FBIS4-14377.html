BFN [By 'Abd-al-Ra'uf Arna'ut] [Excerpt] Jerusalem -- AL-NAHAR has learned that an internal dialogue among Palestinian factions that support and oppose the Palestinian-Israeli agreement is currently taking place inside and outside the occupied territories. The aim of this dialogue is to reach a draft joint agreement on unity in view of the conditions that will prevail in the occupied territories during the coming stage. Fatah has invited all Palestinian political and militant forces to a comprehensive national dialogue. The invitation, extended by Ahmad Ghunaym, head of Fatah's office in Jerusalem, during a commemorative ceremony for martyr Khalil al-Wazir, said: We invite our brothers in Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement], the Islamic Jihad, the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, the People's Party, FIDA [Palestinian Democratic Union], and all our national movements to a comprehensive dialogue in order to affirm that this homeland does not belong to one organization but to the entire people.
