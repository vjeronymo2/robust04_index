Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman today decided to appoint Professor Djordje Pribicevic as special adviser to the president of the republic. President Tudjman has also relieved Major General Slobodan Praljak, the head of the military office of the president of the republic, of his duties, because he is taking up the duties of head of the Croatian Information and Culture Center. The president has appointed Maj. Gen. Zvonimir Cervenko, the hitherto deputy chief of general staff for the Home Guard, as head of the military office. President Tudjman has decided to relieve Brigadier Drago Krpina, head of the political department of the Ministry for Defense of his duties and demote him to reserve service. Brigadier Ivan Tolj has been appointed to this post.
