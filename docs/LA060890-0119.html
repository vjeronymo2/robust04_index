June 8, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Rockwell Reorganizes Unit: Rockwell International Corp. said it has reorganized its defense electronics operation again in a move aimed at further streamlining the business and boosting weapons sales in the U.S. and abroad.

The move marks the third time since July that Rockwell has announced a reshuffling of its Anaheim-based Defense Electronics unit, which is headed by John A. McLuckey, group president. The changes will not result in any layoffs or relocations. The new organization allows the defense electronics group to more efficiently handle several major contract programs the company has won in the past several years.
