BFN [Text] Today the presentation of the European Union's program on the development of power engineering and energy saving was held in Tashkent. This program consists of four large projects which covers all the areas in industry. The implementation of these projects will make it possible to save about 250 million kilowatt hours of energy annually. The European Union has allocated $7 million for this project. The program is already operating and in the first quarter of this year 218,000 tonnes of fuel units have been saved.
