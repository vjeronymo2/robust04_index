940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / Customs initiated third of failures Customs and Excise were responsible for more than a third of all company winding-up orders in England and Wales in the last financial year - up from just over 17 per cent in 1987-88. Mr Neil Hamilton, trade and industry minister, said in a written answer that 3,434 company winding-up orders had been made following petitions by Customs and Excise. This was out of a total of 9,542 orders. In 1987-88 just 690 of the 3,952 winding-up orders were made following Customs and Excise petitions.
