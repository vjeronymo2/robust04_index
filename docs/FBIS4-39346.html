BFN [Text] Moscow June 7 ITAR-TASS -- A meeting of Russian President Boris Yeltsin and State Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin was held in Kremlin today to discuss interaction of the president and the State Duma in the economy and economic legislation, as well as the adoption of the budget, the presidential press service reports. The sides also discussed information provision of deputies and Duma factions. The president hopes the deputies will abide by the law on mass media in this sphere. Bearing in mind numerous foreign trips of State Duma delegations and deputies, the president expressed concern over the Duma spending and budget. The president and the speaker coordinated a number of international issues.
