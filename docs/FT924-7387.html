921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Rogue directors in the firing line - In a Nutshell Action to prevent rogue directors winding up one business to escape their creditors and then immediately setting up in business again emerged as the top priority for small businesses in a survey by the Forum of Private Business. Seventy-five per cent of respondents named rogue directors as their top concern followed by late payment legislation, mentioned by 69 per cent, the simplification of regulations (67 per cent), compensation for complying with government red tape (62 per cent) and signed contracts with banks (62 per cent).
