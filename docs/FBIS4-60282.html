BFN [Text] Palestinian policemen arrested two settlers from Na'ama in Jericho and confiscated their handguns. Sa'adi Naji, the commander of the Jericho Police, apologized for the incident and explained that his men had acted wrongly because they had not understood their orders. The two settlers and their weapons were transferred to the regional coordination office. This was reported by our correspondents Gil Litman and Moti Amir.
