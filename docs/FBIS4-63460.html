BFN [Text] Sevastopol, [No dateline as received] -- On 21 May a UNIAN correspondent learned from reliable sources that in the afternoon the Black Sea Fleet forces are planning to redeploy 51 armored personnel carriers [APC's] from somewhere near Yevpatoriya to the vicinity of Sevastopol. These APC's from the composition of the coastal units will reinforce the Black Sea Fleet Marine Infantry. Relevant headquarters of a reinforced tank battalion and an antiaircraft missile battalion have already been formed.
