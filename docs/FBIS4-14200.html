BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Mustafa Talas, deputy commander in chief of the Army and the Armed Forces, deputy prime minister, and defense minister, today received Oleg Soskovets, first deputy prime minister of the Russian Federation, and the delegation accompanying him. Lieutenant General Hikmat al-Shihabi, chief of staff of the Army and the Armed Forces, and the Russian ambassador in Damascus, attended the meeting. The talks dealt with the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two friendly Syrian and Russian armies, and the means of promoting them in the interest of the two friendly countries' interests.
