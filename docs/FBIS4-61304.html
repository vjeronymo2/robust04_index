BFN [Report by UNKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev 4 May, TASS -- Ukrainian Vice-Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov met U.S. National Security Council Senior Director Nicholas Burns in Kiev on Wednesday [4 May] to discuss the implementation of the agreement signed by Russia, Ukraine and the United States to scrap Ukrainian strategic nuclear missiles. Shmarov stressed that Ukraine has been observing its commitments under the agreement, adding that disarmament requires proper financial support. Speaking about the safety of Ukrainian nuclear power plants, Shmarov said that Ukrainian experts are preparing proposals to this effect and will submit them to the upcoming G-7 summit meeting in Naples. The Ukrainian vice-premier also informed the U.S. guest about conversion and space exploration programmes which offer good opportunities for cooperation with American businesses. The meeting was attended by U.S. Ambassador to Ukraine William Green Miller.
