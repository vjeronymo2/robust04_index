September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ALLA BREVE:: SAINT-SAENS: "SAMSON ET DALILA." CHRISTA LUDWIG, JAMES KING, BERND WEIKL; CHORUS OF THE BAVARIAN RADIO AND MUNICH RADIO ORCHESTRA CONDUCTED BY GIUSEPPE PATANE. EURODISC 7793-2-RG (TWO COMPACT DISCS).

In Patane's lackluster approach, Saint-Saens' most familiar opera still sounds like an oratorio with moving parts. Neither sufficiently suave nor enjoyably vulgar, the conductor offers little assistance to a mismatched, unidiomatic international cast. Even in 1973, when the recording was made, Ludwig sounded hollow at the bottom, and a voice never noted for pure sensuality makes rough going of Delilah's lush musical line. King's reliable, stolid Samson misses Old Testament fervor and even a dram of poetry. Originally issued on RCA, the compact disc remastering is excessively reverberant. Allan Ulrich
