September 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COVERAGE OF ZSA ZSA'S DAY IN COURT

Is Zsa Zsa Gabor trying to prove how far a "has-been" or a "never-was" will go to gain publicity? She must live a very boring life if she needs excitement this much. Mostly I resent that her nonsense is taking up valuable court time and the time of 12 jury members and alternates.

Obviously, she was in the wrong if she had no driver's license with her so why the big court deal? But maybe 60 days in jail with the "common folk" would wake her up. Let's hope she doesn't make a mockery of our court system.

JANET GARRETT

Alhambra
