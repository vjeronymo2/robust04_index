Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 22 Feb (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak this morning received Ibrahim al-Bishari, Libyan permanent delegate to the Arab League, who conveyed a message from Colonel Mu'ammr al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the Libyan revolution. The message deals with bilateral relations and the regional situation in general. The message also touches on President Mubarak's efforts to resolve the region's problems and achieve stability, to which everyone aspires.
