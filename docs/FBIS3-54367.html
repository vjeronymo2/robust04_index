Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Mahdi 'Abd-al-Hadi, head of the Palestinian Institute for International Affairs, disclosed today that preparations are currently under way on the local level to set up a Palestinian council, headed by Faysal al-Husayni, to run the affairs of the Arab residents in East Jerusalem. Our correspondent Yoni Ben-Menahem reports that this council will form a link between the self-rule's Palestinian National Authority and East Jerusalem's inhabitants.
