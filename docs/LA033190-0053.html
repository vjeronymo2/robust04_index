March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGING OSCAR: TELECAST VIEWERS HAVE THEIR SAY

The results of the 62nd Academy Awards have convinced me that the academy places more value on harmless, sticky-sweet "fluff" movies ("Driving Miss Daisy") than hard-hitting, thought-provoking works of art ("Born on the Fourth of July").

Beware! The vast wasteland may soon envelop the silver screen.

RON KOBAYASHI, Tustin
