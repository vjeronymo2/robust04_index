June 28, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Susan Sullivan, who has starred as Maggie Channing on CBS' "Falcon Crest" since 1981, will leave the nest sometime after the series begins its ninth season this fall, Lorimar Television President David E. Salzman announced Tuesday. "I've been with the show since the beginning and I feel the character has run its course," Sullivan said in a news release. "It feels like leaving home." ALEENE MacMINN
