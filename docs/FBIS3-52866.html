Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The U.S. team investigating the fate of Israeli soldiers missing in Lebanon is expected to arrive in Beirut today. The team held talks in Damascus yesterday with Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. Meanwhile, Hizballah Deputy Secretary General Shaykh Na'im Qasim has denied that Hizballah officials met with the U.S. Congressional team in Damascus to discuss the issue of the Israeli soldiers. Shaykh Qasim said that Hizballah is against meeting with any U.S. team because the Americans are a party to the problem, like Israel, and therefore are not qualified [to mediate].
