CSO ["Election Results in Poltava Oblast"] [Text] On 10 April, in Poltava's Kyyivskyy e.d. No. 317, V. Bilous, a member of the Union of Officers of Ukraine, defeated S. Osyka, deputy minister of external economic relations; in Leninskyy e.d. No. 318, V. Holovko, a member of the Union of Officers of Ukraine, defeated M. Kulchytskyy, the chairman of the local Prosvita [Enlightenment] Society; in Oktyabrskyy e.d [NB TO EDITOR: thus in the text, but should be Zhovtnevyy e.d.] No. 319, People's Deputy of Ukraine Vladyslav Nosov, a democrat, defeated a Communist; in Kremenchuk's Avtozavodskyy e.d. No. 321, Viktor Teren, a URP member, defeated a CPU member; in Hadyach e.d. No. 325, Mykola Kovalko, head of the State Oil and Gas Committee, defeated an official of the rayon state administration; in Karlivka e.d. No. 327, V. Kapustyan, a teacher representing the URP, defeated a collective farm chairman; in Lokhvytsya e.d. No. 329, O. Panchenko, a lawyer, defeated a lecturer; in Orzhytsya e.d. No. 330, O. Masenko, a collective farm chairman, defeated another collective farm chairman; in Poltava e.d. No. 331, People's Deputy of Ukraine Vasyl Stepenko, a collective farm chairman, defeated an agrofirm chairman. No deputy was elected in Khorol e.d. No. 33 because neither candidate received the qualifying number of votes. In the remaining electoral districts (five), elections were invalid because voter turnout was lower than required by law.
