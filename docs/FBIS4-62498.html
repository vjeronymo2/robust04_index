BFN [Unattributed POSTFACTUM report: "Kirgizia Holds Military Games"] [Text] According to the Kirgizia [Kyrgyzstan] Defense Ministry Press Center, on 11 May large-scale command and staff exercises that are without precedent in the country's history began in the republic's south. In addition to army units, they involve personnel of the Internal Affairs Ministry, the State National Security Committee, the National Guard, and the State Defense Committee. The aim of the maneuvers is to improve interaction of all power structures in peacetime.
