March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . For its Wednesday airing, CBS' "The Young and the Restless" will feature a takeoff of the sizzling piano seduction scene from "The Fabulous Baker Boys" -- with actress Barbara Crampton doing the Michelle Pfeiffer bit atop the piano, and Eric Braeden reprising the Jeff Bridges part.
