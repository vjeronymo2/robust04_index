Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro has signed the decree dissolving parliament. Prime Minister Carlo Azeglio Ciampi had arrived at the Quirinale [president's office] shortly before 1100 [1000 GMT]. The head of state also rejected Ciampi's resignation. The cabinet will meet at 1700 [1600 GMT] today to decide on the date of the elections.
