940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: London Industrial downturn London Industrial, the property investment group which came to the market in December, reported a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 363,200 to Pounds 182,200 for the quarter to December 31, bringing the total for the nine months to Pounds 537,200 against Pounds 799,700. Turnover on continuing operations improved to Pounds 1.71m (Pounds 1.52m) for the three months. Earnings per share were reduced to 2.1p (6.3p). In addition to five acquisitions during the period, an industrial estate was bought for Pounds 164,000 from the London Borough of Southwark.
