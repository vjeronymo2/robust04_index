930705 FT 05 JUL 93 / Renewed office building urged THE SUPPLY of empty office space in some of the UK's largest cities is low enough to justify a return to office building, says a report by Chesterton, property consultants. Edinburgh, Glasgow and Manchester have less than a year's supply of new office space, and with Birmingham and Leeds are likely to see rental growth before 1995, the report says. City Centre Office Markets: The National Picture. Chesterton Research, 54 Brook Street, London W1A 2BU. Pounds 55.
