BFN [Text] Chairing the first session of the cabinet, His Excellency President Saddam Husayn has ordered that the fees of monitors at general examination centers be raised to 50 dinars per day. This was disclosed by Minister of Education Hikmat 'Abdallah al-Bazzaz to the Iraqi News Agency. This generous act on the part of his excellency the leader president, al-Bazzaz said, is another addition to his numerous such favors in support of a superior educational process. The move doubles the educationists' responsibility to carry out their monitoring duties in the test centers so as to raise a generation armed with science and faith. In the name of the teaching profession, the minister promised His Excellency leader, mujahid President Saddam Husayn that efforts will be maximized to enhance the scientific and educational standard and quicken the pace of the march of advancement Iraq is experiencing in all domains.
