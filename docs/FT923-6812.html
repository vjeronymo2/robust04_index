920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: EIT cuts deficit to Pounds 190,000 EIT, the USM-quoted information technology group formerly known as Maxiprint, cut its losses from Pounds 246,000 to Pounds 190,000 pre-tax for the year to March 31. The results were the first to be announced by EIT since it was created in its current form via three acquisitions during the 1991-92 year. Turnover totalled Pounds 7.03m (Pounds 485,000) and losses per share emerged at 1.3p (4.15p).
