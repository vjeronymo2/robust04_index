November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NOT THAT POWERFUL?

The Joe Eszterhas/Michael Ovitz flap ("How One Letter Has Divided Hollywood," Oct. 30) certainly has divided my feelings. I can't decide who is more ridiculous: Ovitz, whom the film writer accuses of saying, "You'll never work in this town again" . . . or Eszterhas for believing him.

GENE FARMER, Northridge
