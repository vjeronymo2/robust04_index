Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The central leadership of the Ukrainian People's Movement for Perestroyka [Rukh] has described the latest government moves -- the cancellation of credit arrangements, immediate repayment to the National Bank of loans, withdrawal of state subsidies for food, another round of administrative price rises, and procrastination in index-linking wages and delay in paying them -- as none other than the continuation of the war waged by nomenklatura in power against the Ukrainian people. Rukh demands the removal of these faults and warns that the continuation by state power of anti-popular measures will trigger spontaneous actions of protest which for their part will violate civic peace and impede a possible change in political tack by way of free elections.
