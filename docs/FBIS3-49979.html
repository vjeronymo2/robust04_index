Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hungarian Foreign Ministry welcomes the agreement between Ukraine, Russia, and the United States on dismantling the nuclear weapons in the territory of Ukraine, on the implementation of the START I treaty. The Hungarian Foreign Ministry also welcomes Ukraine's determination to join the nuclear nonproliferation treaty as soon as possible. The step reduces the danger of a nuclear war, eases the tension prevailing in our region, and increases the chance for peace, the communique of the Hungarian Foreign Ministry reads.
