Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 3 -- The National Assembly delegation led by Chairman Nong Duc Manh during its China visit from Feb. 21 to March 1 called at Shandong, Jiangsu, and Guangdong Provinces. It was accompanied by Mr Liu Suinian member of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, and other senior Chinese officials. It had working sessions with delegations of provincial people's congress organizations in Shandong, Jiangsu, and Guangdong. In these meetings, the hosts highly appreciated achievements recorded by Vietnam during its renovation process, and expressed their wishes to strengthen the multisided cooperation with Vietnamese provinces, especially in the economic and trade fields. While there, the delegation toured various industrial, agricultural, cultural, and educational establishments, and economic development zones. Chairman Nong Duc Manh and his entourage hailed the Chinese people in general and people in Shandong, Jiangsu, Guangdong in particular for their persistence in building socialism, overcoming many difficulties to implement the open-door policy, and achieving important goals. On behalf of the delegation, Chairman Nong Duc Manh extended his thanks to Chinese leaders and people for their hospitality. He also expressed gratitude for the Chinese people's effective support and assistance to the Vietnamese people in their struggle for national independence and national construction, as well as his wish for the further development of the long lasting, traditional friendship between the two countries.
