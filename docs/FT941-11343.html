940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / UK Company News: TR City net asset value advances The first half to December 31 at TR City of London Trust ended with net asset value per share up 27 per cent from 125.69p to 159.39p. Retained revenue came to Pounds 103,000, compared with a Pounds 705,000 deficit previously. As already announced. the second interim payment of 1.23p brings the dividend for the year so far to 2.46p (2.38p). Earnings per share amounted to 2.51p (2.01p).
