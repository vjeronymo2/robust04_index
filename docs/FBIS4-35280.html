BFN [Text] His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id Al Sa'id, the sultan of the Sultanate of Oman, received late this afternoon Brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the chairman of the Council of Ministers. The OMANI NEWS AGENCY, which reported this, said that the talks concentrated on the current situation in the Yemeni arena, and the means by which a solution could be found to save the Yemeni people from the woes of the ongoing war and achieve peace and stability for the Yemeni people. During the meeting Sultan Qabus expressed his interest in an end to the fighting, so that the shedding of the blood of the brothers in Yemen could stop and the stability of Yemen and its security could be maintained and preserved. He stressed the necessity of the disputing parties returning to dialogue, instead of fighting among themselves, to achieve the aspirations of the Yemeni people for stability and development. Brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas arrived in Muscat this afternoon for a visit to the Sultanate of Oman. During this visit he will be holding talks with Omani officials about the ongoing situation in Yemen, within the framework of the efforts and concern of the fraternal Sultanate of Oman, to stop the war and stop the bloodletting of the Yemeni people. On arrival at the Sib airport he was received by al-Sayyid Bin-Hamad Bin-Su'ud, minister of the sultan's palace, and Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, the Omani minister of state for foreign affairs.
