BFN [Text] INTERFAX has reported today that fighting between President Dzhokhar Dudayev's supporters and the opposition is continuing for the second day in Groznyy, the capital of Chechnya. Troops loyal to the president are fighting an opposition armed formation under the command of Ruslan Labazanov. The INTERFAX correspondent reports that armor is being used in the armed clashes. INTERFAX has been told at opposition headquarters that there are many civilian casualties. They also clarified that the armed clashes are talking place in one of the areas of Groznyy. They were preceded by a rally staged by Labazanov in a square in Groznyy on Saturday [11 June], where he demanded the resignation of President Dudayev.
