Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 9 (KCNA) -- Feelings of deep reverence for the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il are growing among South Korean puppet Army soldiers, the Seoul-based radio Voice of National Salvation (VNS) reported. A Captain Kim of the 21st Division of the puppet Army, chatting with his pals over the situation recently, said the People's Army of the North under the command of General Kim Chong-il, the most famous general in the world, is an unrivalled strong Army. He said: "General Kim Chong-il, possessed of outstanding military strategy as his born gift, has built the People's Army into a well-disciplined, unrivalled strong Army. Unity between officers and men, Army-civilian unity, the arming of all the people and the fortification of the whole country are true looks of the mighty North." Listening to him, a second lieutenant surnamed Choe said: "The way of averting the danger of war, loving peace and achieving reunification, the ardent aspiration of the nation, is to hold in high esteem and uphold General Kim Chong-il, the most famous general in the world produced by our nation, as commander-in-chief of the national Army. Herein lies the way of warding off national disaster, the way for us to live, for the nation to live and the way of achieving reunification."
