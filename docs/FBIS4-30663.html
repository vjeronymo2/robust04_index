BFN [Text] Another power project will be constructed in the Philippines. President Ramos, on Monday, led the groundbreaking rites for the Masinloc coal-fired thermal power plant in Zambales in northern Luzon. The $525.6 million project will add 600 megawatts of electricity to the Luzon grid by 1998. In his speech, the president told the residents of Masinloc to discard their fears of environmental pollution and hazards that the project may bring. The president assured them that this will not happen due to the rigid standards adopted by those involved in the planning and construction of the new power plant.
