930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Hemingway Props Hemingway Properties is selling Dorset House, which is occupied by the Department of the Environment on a 25 year lease, for Pounds 21.8m cash to a foreign investor. The book value is Pounds 21.2m and the money will be used to reduce debt. It is estimated that pro-forma net debt will be Pounds 28.8m after the already announced issue of Pounds 12.1m of convertible loan stock and the Pounds 30.2m acquisition of a portfolio of properties.
