Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 12 (KPL) -- Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina Gonzalez, who is also Politburo member of the Communist Party of Cuba Central Committee, yesterday evening arrived here on a two-day official visit to Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic] at the invitation of his Lao counterpart, Somsavat Lengsavat. The Cuban foreign minister with his delegation was warmly greeted at the Wattai airport by Lao Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat and senior officials of the Foreign Ministry. Cuban Ambassador to Laos Ricardo Danza Sigas was also present at the welcoming ceremony. The two foreign ministers together with their delegations held talks at the Lan Xang hotel on the same evening. At the talks, the sides informed each other of the developments in their respective countries and the further strengthening of their bilateral relations and cooperation. They also agreed to renew their cooperation on a mutual benefit basis. The Cuban side showed interest in small hydro-power generation, large scale cattle breeding for export, cooperation in public health, sugar cane plantation and sugar production in Laos in the future.
