BFN [Text] The governors of Mount Lebanon, Northern Lebanon, and Southern Lebanon today chaired meetings of the local security councils to discuss the measures adopted by the Central Security Council to confront any disturbance of security following the General Labor Federation's call for demonstrations on Tuesday [26 April] despite the government's ban on demonstrations. Meanwhile, the National Defense Ministry today reminded the citizens of the decision issued by National Defense Minister Muhsin Dalull yesterday suspending arms licenses throughout Lebanon for 38 hours from Monday [25 April] evening to Wednesday [27 April] morning. The army command was ordered to implement this decision.
