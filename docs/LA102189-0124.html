October 21, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

NONLEAGUE

St. Bernard 25, Bell-Jeff 22 -- Two Bell-Jeff fumbles and a blocked punt helped host St. Bernard build a 25-7 lead.

Trailing, 25-7, after three quarters, Bell-Jeff (1-5-1) drew close on Sean Casagrande's second one-yard touchdown run and Alfred Silva's 10-yard scoring burst. BRYAN RODGERS
