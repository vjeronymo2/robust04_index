Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, Feb 3 (AFP) -- Palestine Liberation Organisation (PLO) leader Yasir 'Arafat sent a message to the Russian government Wednesday [2 February] to brief them on last weekend's peace talks with Israel in the Swiss resort of Davos, the Palestinian news agency WAFA reported. 'Arafat sent the message through Russia's ambassador to Tunis Boris Shchiborin after a meeting Wednesday at PLO headquarters here. 'Arafat is to meet Israel's chief negotiator Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres in Cairo next Monday in a bid to nail down a long overdue accord on withdrawing Israeli troops from the Gaza Strip and Jericho, territories earmarked for Palestinian autonomy under an historic accord signed on September 13. The troops were supposed to have started pulling out on December 13, and their withdrawal is scheduled to be complete by April 13.
