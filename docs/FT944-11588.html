941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / Tour company ceases trading Meridian Tours, a holiday tour operator specialising in flights to Europe, has ceased trading, the Civil Aviation Authority said yesterday. The CAA has called in the company's bond of Pounds 350,000. About 150 passengers are abroad with the company and they will be able to return as planned. Customers due to travel will be advised whether they can go ahead with their trips or sent refund forms. The company traded as Meridian Holidays and Meridian Travel. Meridian Morocco, a separate company, continues to trade.
