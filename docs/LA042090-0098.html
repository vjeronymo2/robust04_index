April 20, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: MEDFLIES FOUND IN 2 AREAS NOT BEING SPRAYED

Agricultural officials announced Thursday the discovery of two more Mediterranean fruit flies, this time in Azusa and Pomona.

A single immature female Medfly was found near Azusa Tuesday in a previously uninfested area off Chadley Street, said Larry Cooper, an eradication project spokesman. The Pomona Medfly was found Wednesday in a loquat tree off Dudley Street, also in an uninfested area. No decision has been made on whether the areas will be treated with aerial malathion spraying.
