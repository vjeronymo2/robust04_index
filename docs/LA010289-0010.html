January 2, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

ARKANSAS BEATS SOUTH ALABAMA

Larry Marks scored 21 points, including 9 straight during a 16-point Arkansas run, and pulled down 12 rebounds to lead the Razorbacks to a 103-71 victory over South Alabama Sunday night.

Arkansas (6-2) struggled in the first 20 minutes, trailing by as many as 7 points before rallying to lead the Jaguars (5-4) at halftime, 34-33.

A 24-14 surge early in the second half gave the Razorbacks an 11-point lead. Then 16 unanswered points over the next 2 minutes put Arkansas up 76-49.
