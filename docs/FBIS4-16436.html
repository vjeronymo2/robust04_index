BFN [Text] ITAR-TASS has learned that Russia's procurator general has reopened the so-called Rutskoy trust case, in connection with the incompleteness of the investigation conducted earlier. Among other things, Rutskoy, the notary who verified the documents, and other persons were not questioned on all the circumstances of the trust agreement. Furthermore, a number of expert investigations need to be carried out, including one into the trust agreement itself.
