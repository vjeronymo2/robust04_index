July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

5 KILLED IN FIERY PILEUP ON PENNSYLVANIA TURNPIKE

A tractor-trailer truck smashed into a line of cars and trucks stalled by an accident on the Pennsylvania Turnpike, killing five people in the fiery pileup Saturday. The truck driver and a family of four were killed, police said. Some drivers apparently were spared because they had stepped out of their cars and were standing near the road.
