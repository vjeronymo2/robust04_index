BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 6 (KCNA) -- Three environmental organisations of South Korea held a press conference in Seoul on May 3 and denounced the puppet Home Ministry for destroying environment, according to a radio report from Seoul. They in a statement expressed concern over the possible serious environmental destruction to be entailed by the large mountain villa which the puppet Home Ministry is going to build in Mt. Sorak by abusing power and demanded an immediate stop to the plan.
