Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 2/2/1994 -- Romania's Senate passed on February 2 an amendment to the bill of changing and extending the Criminal Code and the Penal Procedure Code, according to which the amended criminal code stipulates that the public defamation by any means of the country or of the Romanian nation shall be punished with 1-5 years of jail. The amendment, passed with 75 votes for, 25 against and 5 abstentions, was promoted by Senator Valer Suian of the Romanian National Unity Party (PUNR).
