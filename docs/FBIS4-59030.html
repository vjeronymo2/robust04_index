BFN [Text] Armenian Vice President Gagik Arutyunyan met Foreign Minister Dr. Velayati and expressed the deep sorrow of his Government and the Armenian people on the Iranian plane accident in Nagorno-Karabakh in which some of our compatriots lost their lives. He said: The Armenian nation and Government share the sorrow and sadness of the people of Iran, especially of the bereaved families. In this meeting, the Armenian vice president expressed the hope that peace would return to the Nagorno-Karabakh region as soon as possible. Arutyunyan left Tehran last night. Earlier he described the outcome of his talks with our country's officials as positive.
