BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 21 (KPL) -- The construction of the 156 km Vientiane-Vangviang section of road 13 north undertaken by the Road No.10 Construction Company is now completed. The director of the road construction company, Mr. Savat Nokeo said that the construction project of the road section cost 15 million U.S. dollars, which was a loan from the World Bank. He said his company conducted the construction for three years. He also said that his company was continuing with the construction of the 60 km Vangvieng-Kasi section of the same road. In addition, he said his company had received a contract from Tianjin Company of China, the contractor of road 13 south, for work on a long section of the road from Pak Kading to Savannakhet.
