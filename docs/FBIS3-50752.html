Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] Early this morning President-elect Eduardo Frei arrived at Las Condes Hospital, where his wife, Marta Larraechea, was released after having undergone surgery on 2 January. Frei then went to his office on Hindenburg Street where, while he held a series of protocol meetings with various sports, artistic, and labor union organizations, a communique was issued disclosing the latest appointments designed to complete Frei's first cabinet. Ernesto Schiefelbein, independent, was appointed education minister; Emiliano Ortega, of the Christian Democratic Party, agriculture minister; and Alejandro Jadresic, also independent, head of the National Energy Commission. The first meeting of the new cabinet will be held tomorrow at 0945 at the Andres Bello Diplomatic Academy building. [passage omitted]
