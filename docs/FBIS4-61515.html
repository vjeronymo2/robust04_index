BFN [Text] Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Pastukhov arrived in Sukhumi on 5 May for talks with the Abkhazian leadership, INTERFAX learned in the press service of the Abkhazian Embassy in Moscow. The visit is aimed at preparing for the resumption of the Georgian-Abkhazian talks on Abkhazia's status in Moscow on 10 May, a press service spokesman said. Pastukhov is head of the Russian delegation, which is the mediator in the settlement of the Georgian-Abkhazian conflict.
