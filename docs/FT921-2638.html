920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / Election '92 (Ballot Box): Smart money Highly paid City dealers are protecting themselves against a Labour victory. Many have piled in to place straight bets on Labour. City bookmaker IG Index said that yesterday was its busiest day yet, with 70 per cent of bets coming from City institutions. The weight of money is for a hung parliament with Labour the biggest party. But some City brains are more sophisticated. 'Bet on the Tories to win and buy puts on the Footsie index and sterling,' is the smart approach.
