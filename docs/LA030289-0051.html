March 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BALALAIKA ORCHESTRA TOUR OF WEST COAST CANCELED

The West Coast portion of the tour by the Osipov Balalaika Orchestra, including performances at the Celebrity Theatre in Anaheim March 3 and at Ambassador Auditorium in Pasadena March 4, has been canceled. The producers, Classical Artists International, say that the company will be back in 1990, with a restructured tour of three- or four-performance engagements, rather than the single night stands of the tour now ending after the East Coast leg.
