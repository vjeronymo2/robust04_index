920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / Firms appointed for BR sell-off THE Department of Transport has appointed two City firms to join Samuel Montagu, the merchant bank, in advising it on the privatisation of British Rail. KPMG Peat Marwick, the accountants, will advise on accounting and taxation, and Linklaters & Paines, the lawyers, will advise on commercial, company, employment, pensions and taxation law.
