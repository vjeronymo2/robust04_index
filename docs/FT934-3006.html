931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: BBA shares jump 19p after December sales Shares in The British Bloodstock Agency rose 19p to 102p yesterday, after its December sales provided evidence of the bloodstock market's strong recovery and of the group's dominant market position within it. The BBA, the world's largest bloodstock agency, doubled its total December sales spend to 4.74m guineas on 178 horses, against 2.35m guineas on 203 horses last time. The average price was 2.3 times higher at 26,640 guineas (11,597 guineas). The BBA said its outlay was more than six times that of its nearest rival.
