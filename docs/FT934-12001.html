931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Newmont Mining tops expectations NEWMONT Mining, the Denver-based gold company, exceeded market expectations yesterday with third-quarter earnings boosted by the higher gold price and an increase in production. Newmont Gold, the company's 90 per cent-owned north American mining subsidiary, reported net income of Dollars 41.7m, or 40 cents a share, up from Dollars 23.7m or 23 cents the year before. Sales rose from Dollars 143.5m to Dollars 170m as production at Newmont Gold jumped by 10 per cent to 453,200 ounces. The results were also bouyed by the gold price, which averaged Dollars 375 an ounce, up from Dollars 348. Total sales were up by 15 per cent, to Dollars 178m. The parent company reported net income of Dollars 25.7m or 32 cents, up from Dollars 18m or 27 cents.
