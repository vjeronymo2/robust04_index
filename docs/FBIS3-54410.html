Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, people observed Black Day today on the occasion of the Republic Day of India. The Indian authorities took strict security measures and curfew was imposed in and around capital Srinagar. Black flags fluttered everywhere in response to call made by All Parties Hurriyyat Conference. At various places, people burned copies of the Indian Constitution. Mojahedin attacked Indian military targets with rockets in Sopor, Baramula, Anantnag-Islamabad, Kupwara, Pulwama, (Charar Sharif), Bandipura, Badgam, and Uri areas. Mojahedin also attacked the Indian Brigade Headquarters in Punch Cantonment, inflicting heavy loss of life and property to the Indian troops. At least five people were martyred and more than 70 injured during clashes at various places in the held valley.
