BFN [Text] The Agrarian Bloc -- which includes the United Agrarian Democratic Party of Belarus, the Agrarian Union [Sayuz Ahrarnikaw], the Council of Collective Farms [Savet Kalhasaw], and the Republican Committee of the Trade Union of Agro-Industrial Workers -- has resolved to put forward Alyaksandr Dubko, chairman of the Agrarian Union and the Council of Collective Farms, as a candidate for president. A. Dubko is also manager of the agricultural enterprise "Prahres" in Grodno Oblast.
