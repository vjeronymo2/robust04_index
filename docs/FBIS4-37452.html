BFN ["Text" of Revolution Command Council, RCC, Decree No. 67 issued on 13 June 1994] [Text] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. The Republic of Iraq The Revolution Command Council [RCC] Decree No. 67 Dated 4 Muharram 1415 Hegira, corresponding to 13 June 1994 Decree: In accordance with the provisions enshrined in Paragraph A of Article 42 of the Constitution, the RCC has decided the following: 1. Former ministers and former officials enjoying the rank of a minister shall be banned from performing the following activities until further notice: A. The launching of mixed, joint-stock, or private firms. B. Engaging in business and trade. C. The launching of all kinds of consulting bureaus. D. Working as chairmen or members of the boards of directors of the aforementioned firms. E. Working as authorized directors of the aforementioned firms. 2. Former officials enjoying special grades, former directors general, as well as former Armed Forces and Internal Security officers shall be banned from performing the activities enshrined in this decree for five years as of the date they abandon their official posts. 3. This decree shall be published in the official gazette. The concerned agencies shall see to its implementation. [Signed] Saddam Husayn, RCC chairman
