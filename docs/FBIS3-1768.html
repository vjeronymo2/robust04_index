Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shenyang, February 26 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Rongmao, a senior engineer, was elected mayor of Shenyang city, capital of Liaoning Province, at the second session of the city's eleventh people's congress, which closed today. The former mayor, Wu Disheng, died in a helicopter crash in Israel last November. Zhang, 56 years old, is from Taoyuan County in Hunan Province. He graduated from the graduate school of Dalian Institute of Industry in 1956 and he was among the first group of senior engineers in the environment protection field. Before today's election, he served as vice-mayor of Shenyang city and deputy governor of Liaoning Province. Since January he has served as acting mayor of Shenyang city.
