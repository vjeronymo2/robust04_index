Language: Lao Article Type:BFN ["Resolution" of the Political Bureau of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee on the appointment of the Committee in Charge of the Funeral for "Comrade" Phoumi Vongvichit, adviser to the party Central Committee -- dated 7 January] [Text] To express gratitude for Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit and to organize the funeral for him in an honorable and orderly manner, the Political Bureau of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee has agreed to: Article 1: Appoint the Committee in Charge of the Organizing of the Funeral for Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit comprising the following Comrades: 1. Comrade Saman Vi-gnaket, Political Bureau member of the LPRP Central Committee, and chairman of the National Assembly, as chairman; 2. Comrade Khamphoui Keoboualapha, Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee and deputy prime minister, as deputy chairman; 3. Comrade Oudom Khatthi-gna, Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee in charge of mass organization affairs, as deputy chairman; 4. Comrade Maichantan Sengmani, Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee and chairman of the Central Party and State Control Commission, as member; 5. Comrade Choummali Sai-gnason, Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee and minister of national defense, as member; 6. Comrade Thongsing Thammavong, Political Bureau member of the Party Central Committee and head of the Organizating Committee of the Party Central Committee, as member; 7. Comrade Thongsavat Khaikhamphithoun, member of the Party Central Committee and head of the Office of the Party Central Committee, as member; 8. Comrade Phao Bounnaphon, member of the Party Central Committee and minister attached to the Prime Minister's Office, as member; 9. Comrade Maisouk Saisompheng, member of the Party Central Committee and chairman of the Lao Front for National Construction, as member; 10. Comrade Somsavat Lengsavat, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of foreign affairs, as member; 11. Comrade A-sang Laoli, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of interior, as member; 12. Comrade Osakan Thammatheva, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of information and culture, as member; 13. Comrade Khamsai Souphanouvong, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of finance, as member; 14. Comrade Boun-gnang Volachit, member of the Party Central Committee and lord mayor of Vientiane Municipality, as member; 15. Comrade Vongphet Saikeu-yachongtoua, member of the Party Central Committee and deputy chairman of the Lao Front for National Construction, as member; 16. Comrade Thongloun Sisoulit, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of labor and social welfare, as member; 17. Comrade Khampan Philavong, member of the Party Central Committee and chairman of the Federation of Lao Trade Unions, as member; 18. Comrade Mrs. Onchan Thammavong, member of the Party Central Committee and chairwoman of the Federation of Lao Women's Unions, as member; 19. Comrade Khamman Sounvileut, member of the Party Central Committee and second secretary of the Lao People's Revolutionary Youth Union Central Committee, as member; 20. Comrade Phoumi Thipphavon, member of the Party Central Committee and governor of Xieng Khouang Province, as member; 21. Comrade Phimmason, member of the Party Central Committee and minister of education, as member. Article 2: The Committee is authorized to hold discussion and clearly assign responsibilities for each comrade. It is also authorized to guide various branches and sectors concerned to effectively perform duties as entrusted to them. Article 3: This resolution is effective as of the date it is signed. [Signed] Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the LPRP Central Committee acting on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Party Central Committee [Dated] Vientiane, 7 January 1994
