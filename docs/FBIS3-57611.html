Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigoriy Arslanov, Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Beijing, 28 Jan -- This morning Li Peng, prime minister of the PRC State Council, received Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who is here on an official visit, at the Zhongnanhai party and government residence. Greeting the Russian minister, Li Peng stressed that the visit of Russian President Boris Yeltsin to Beijing at the end of 1992 was an exceptionally successful one, that fruitful and business-like conversations on all aspects of Chinese-Russian relations took place. Ties between the PRC and Russia, Li Peng noted, are developing successfully on the basis of friendship, good-neighborliness and mutual advantage, in spite of the changes in the world and in our countries. Andrey Kozyrev, in his turn, said that the purpose of his visit is not only to maintain the relations that currently exist between the two countries, but also to further expand them, to come out on a new, higher level of development in relations. In this respect, he noted, we intend to discuss the forthcoming visit to Moscow by PRC President Jiang Zemin and other Chinese leaders. The Russian minister conveyed very best wishes from the Russian president and his words that Russia does not intend to back down even a single step on reform, on foreign policy, a priority trend in which is the development of goodneighborliness and partnership with China.
