930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Businesses total falls 200,000 THE number of businesses fell by nearly 200,000 in the two years to 1991, a review of small business trends by National Westminster Bank found. The report, published by the Small Business Research Trust, said there had been a fall from 2.9m enterprises to 2.7m. The largest decline was in the small-business sector with a fall of 184,000 in the number of businesses with a turnover of less than Pounds 1m.
