Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A team of 17 envoys from Botswana, South Africa, and Zimbabwe arrived in Maseru this morning at the beginning of a three-day fact finding mission in Lesotho regarding the tense security situation in the country. During their stay the team will meet with the government, leaders of political parties, and other leaders. At the end of their stay in Lesotho, they will report their findings to their countries who will consider how they can assist Lesotho in finding a peaceful solution to its present tense security situation.
