BFN [Text] Mr. Vo Van Kiet, prime minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam [SRV], his wife, and entourage, who were paying a goodwill visit since 11 May at the invitation of Senior General Than Shwe, prime minister of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], left Yangon [Rangoon] by a special airplane this afternoon. Prime Minister Mr. Vo Van Kiet and entourage were seen off at Yangon airport by Prime Minister Sr. Gen. Than Shwe and his wife; General Maung Aye, vice chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC], and his wife; Lieutenant General Tin Tun, deputy prime minister, and his wife; Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the SLORC; Lt. Gen. Tin U, secretary-2 of the SLORC, and his wife; Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt, minister of religious affairs; Brigadier General Myo Thant, minister of information; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs, and his wife; U Nyunt Swe, deputy minister of foreign affairs, and his wife; U Aye, Myanmar ambassador to the SRV, and his wife; Mr. Tran Viet Tan, ambassador of the SRV to the Union of Myanmar; high-ranking diplomats from the foreign missions; and members of the SRV Embassy. The visiting Vietnamese Prime Minister Mr. Vo Van Kiet, his wife, and entourage visited the oil refinery in Syraim and Dagon University this morning. The visiting prime minister and entourage, accompanied by Deputy Foreign Minister U Nyunt Swe and party, arrived at the guest house of the Syriam Oil Refinery at 0820. They were welcomed by U Khin Maung Thein, minister of energy; U Tin Tun, deputy minister of energy; and responsible personnel. The visiting prime minister, his wife, and entourage then looked around the oil refinery and left the refinery at 0930. Then they proceeded to the Dagon University in North Dagon Myothit. After visiting the campus, they left the university at 1130.
