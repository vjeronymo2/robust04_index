Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federal Government has no intention to withdraw the troops deployed to the Nigerian Bakassi Peninsula as a condition for peaceful resolution of the conflict with Cameroon. The minister of state for foreign affairs, Chief Anthoni Ani, stated this in an interview in Harare, Zimbabwe. He said Cameroon was insisting Nigeria withdraw from the peninsula and revert to its position as at last December, before any dialogue. Chief Anthony Ani, who is on a tour of eastern and southern Africa, said that Nigeria could not withdraw from her own territory. The minister has already met the presidents of Zimbabwe, Kenya, Uganda (?on the matter).
