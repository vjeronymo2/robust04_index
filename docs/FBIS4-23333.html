BFN [Text] National Party Deputy Secretary General Mulondwe Muzungu says democracy cannot be sustained in Zambia if the ruling MMD [Movement for Multiparty Democracy] continues to dominate Parliament and the local government. Speaking in Lusaka yesterday, Mr. Muzungu called for a stronger opposition in the country, adding that the MMD hold in decision-making bodies must be diluted so that democracy is upheld. He was speaking at the National Party Secretariat when three members of the Democratic National Party joined the National Party.
