BFN [Text] The Armenian and Azerbaijani formations operating in the zone of the Karabakh conflict have violated the cease-fire agreement on several occasions, although the general situation is relatively calm. According to a well-informed source in the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry, Karabakh soldiers fired at the Azerbaijani positions in the Fizuli, Geranboy and Agdam regions. The same source reports that a number of villages in Azerbaijan's Tauz and Kedabek border regions were shelled from Armenia and that personnel and equipment were transferred from Armenia to Nagorno-Karabakh. Meanwhile, the Karabakh Defense Ministry's press service reports that Azerbaijani units shelled Karabakh positions in the Agdam direction last night. No return fire was opened.
