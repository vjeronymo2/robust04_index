BFN [Text] Beijing, April 8 (AFP)--Chinese dissident Xu Wenli was detained by police Friday, his wife said, in the third such action against pro-democracy activists in a week. Xu was picked up by police on Thursday then returned home around midnight, but was detained again on Friday, the wife, Kang Tong, said. Xu, 49, is a former activist in the 1978-79 pro-democracy movement who was freed last year after 12 years in jail. In the past week, China's most famous dissident, Wei Jingsheng, and his secretary, Tong Yi, have also been detained.
