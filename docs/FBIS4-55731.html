BFN [Unattributed report: "Crvenkovski Refutes"] [Text] In regard to the reports for interruption in the [Greek-Macedonian] negotiations, Macedonian Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski stated for the Macedonian Radio "nothing was offered in New York and the talks focused only on continuation of the negotiations. I refuse to comment Greek media speculations." Citing the Athens news agency, the Macedonian Radio and Television informs of a statement of Crvenkovski on chances for a way out of the dead end. But, he said, Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] remains firmly on its positions as regarding he name issue. The Greek govt representative Venizelos stated his country will also keep its point of view on the name of its northern neighbour.
