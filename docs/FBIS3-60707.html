Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Vladimir Zaynetdinov report: ```Robbery of the Century.' Almost''] [Text] The ``robbery of the century'' could have occurred in Moscow, had help not arrived. Around 0500 seven criminals attacked a training battalion at the Russian Federation Defense Ministry's Chemical Defense Academy. Colonel Yuriy Reshetnik, a staffer at the Russian Federation MVD [Ministry of Internal Affairs] Public Relations Center, gave the details. The attackers beat up the duty officer and a soldier. The bandits then took their keys to the ``armory'' [oruzheyka]. But no weapons were stolen--the military arrived in time.
