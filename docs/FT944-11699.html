941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Saracen Value Trust Saracen Value Trust reported net asset value per share of 92.6p at September 30 or 93.8p fully diluted. For the period from incorporation on November 1, 1993 to the end of September net profits were Pounds 344,000 for earnings per share of 0.93p. An interim dividend of 0.6p is declared.
