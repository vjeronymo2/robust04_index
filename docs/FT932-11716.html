930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Clayton Son losses accelerate Shares of Clayton Son & Co (Holdings), the Leeds-based engineer, fell 13p to 70p yesterday on news that losses had accelerated from Pounds 490,626 to Pounds 1.11m pre-tax for the year to end-December. The interim dividend was omitted and the final is also being passed - shareholders received a single 3p payment for 1991. The deficit took account of a rise in interest payable to Pounds 432,478 (Pounds 296,713) and exceptional costs of Pounds 411,180 (Pounds 49,629). Turnover fell to Pounds 16.62m (Pounds 19.68m). Losses per share emerged at 38.64p (8.89p).
