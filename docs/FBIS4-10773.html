BFN [Text] With 297 votes in favor and 116 against, the Chamber of Deputies on 13 April stripped Deputy Carlos Benevides (PMDB-CE) [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party-Ceara] of his political rights. In the morning, with 346 votes in favor and 42 votes against, alternate Deputy Feres Nader (PTB-RJ) [Brazilian Labor Party-Rio de Janeiro] also was stripped of his political rights. He cannot become a deputy nor run in the next elections. Benevides and Nader were both involved in the budget scandal. President Itamar Franco on 13 April sanctioned a law approved by the Senate that increases from three to eight years the term during which members of Congress are deprived of their political rights when found guilty of a lack of decorum.
