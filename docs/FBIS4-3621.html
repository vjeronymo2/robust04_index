BFN [Text] Beijing, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak flew in here at noon today, starting his three-day state visit to China. Mubarak arrived for his sixth trip to China at the invitation of Chinese President Jiang Zemin. Mubarak's entourage include Information Minister Muhammad Safwat al-Sharif, Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Zakaria 'Azmi, secretary general of the presidency. Among those greeting Mubarak at the airport were Xiao Yang, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of justice, Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Yang Fuchang and Assistant Foreign Minister Wang Changyi. Egyptian Ambassador to China Samir Burhan and diplomatic envoys from other Arab and African countries also went to the airport to greet Mubarak and his party. The Chinese president is expected to host a welcoming ceremony for President Mubarak and hold talks with him this afternoon.
