930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Dolphin Packaging jumps 43% DOLPHIN Packaging, which supplies packaging mainly to the food industry, reported pre-tax profits up 43 per cent to Pounds 1.13m in the six months to June 30, against Pounds 789,000. Mr Moger Woolley, chairman, said that the rise had been achieved despite pressure on prices which remained in all markets. Turnover rose 2 per cent to Pounds 12.6m (Pounds 12.3m), increased volumes offsetting price falls. Earnings per share increased to 3.42p (2.39p) and the interim dividend is held at 1.7p.
