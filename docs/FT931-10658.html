930206 FT 06 FEB 93 / Chorley borough council asks Mitsubishi to use Leyland plant as European base CHORLEY Borough Council in Lancashire, based a few miles from the threatened truck plant at Leyland, has asked Mitsubishi, the Japanese vehicle and engineering group, to use the plant as its European base. In a letter to Mr Hirokazu Nakamura, the company's president, it said: 'We think that the present situation presents a unique opportunity for an ambitious player in the world commercial vehicle market to make a significant intervention.'
