Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers captured a person who crossed the border from Jordan in the Jordan Rift Valley, east of the city of Bet She'an. IDF troops arrived last night at the site of the infiltration and began combing the area. They apprehended the infiltrator this morning and brought him in for questioning. Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports that Major General Nehemya Tamari was in the area of the infiltration last night. The helicopter accident in which he was killed took place while he was returning from the area.
