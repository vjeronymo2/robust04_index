October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Reebok International Ltd.: The footwear and apparel designer and marketer reported third-quarter earnings of $41 million, down 18% from a year ago. Sales totaled $589 million, up 12%. The Stoughton, Mass., firm attributed the results to lower U.S. sales of its Reebok brand footwear and higher advertising and promotional expenses.
