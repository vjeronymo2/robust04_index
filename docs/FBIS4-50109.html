BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren said here today that China attaches importance to its friendly relations and cooperation with the Council for the Arab States of the Gulf and its member nations. During a meeting with the council's secretary-general Shaykh Fahim Bin-Sultan al-Qasimi and his party here this morning, Rong said that since reform and opening was initiated over a decade ago, China's economy has developed fastly and vigorously. He said that China has a vast market potential and good conditions for investment, so economic and trade cooperation between China and the council have broad prospects. Rong noted that China welcomes business people from the Gulf countries, hoping that they come either for visits or for on-the- spot investigations. Rong said that China is willing to further cooperate with the council and its member nations on the basis of mutual benefit and respect so as to learn from each other and progress together. Al-Qasimi said that he and his delegation are satisfied with the results of their visit, adding that they, as developing countries, also feel proud of China's economic development. He said that they believe bilateral cooperation has a broad prospect.
