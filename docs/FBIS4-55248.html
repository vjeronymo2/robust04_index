BFN [Text] Belgrade, 11 May (TANJUG) -- On the basis of the decision by Sadako Ogata, UN high commissioner for refugees [UNHCR], employees of this organization will not be returning to the Srpska republic [RS], Koning Abuzaid, head of the UNHCR mission for former Bosnia-Herzegovina, said after today's visit to Brcko, the SRNA agency reports. During a meeting today with Nikola Koljevic, RS vice president, and RS Health Minister Dragan Kalinic, Mrs. Abuzaid said this move was made because of a security threat to UNHCR personnel following the UN decision that Serb targets can be bombed without previous announcement. Humanitarian aid to the people in the Srpska republic will from now on be delivered via Belgrade, which is seen as technically more complicated.
