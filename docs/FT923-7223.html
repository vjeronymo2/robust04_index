920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / UK Company News: GT Chile Fund assets growth Over the six months to June 30 net asset value of the GT Chile Growth Fund increased 32 per cent to Dollars 30.79 (Pounds 16.10). A year earlier it stood at Dollars 19.12. The half year produced gross income of Dollars 7.97m (Dollars 8.3m) and earnings per share of 44 cents (60 cents).
