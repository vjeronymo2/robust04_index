931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Cairn makes New Zealand disposals Cairn Energy, the independent oil and gas exploration and production company, is to dispose of certain New Zealand exploration and production interests including the Ngatoro oilfield, for Pounds 1.7m cash. Cairn's 13.35 per cent interest in licence PPL38706 and its 5.34 per cent in licence PPL38707 will be jointly acquired by Petrocorp Exploration and Southern Petroleum (Ohanga). Cairn retains a 26.7 per cent stake in licence PPL38702 onshore Taranaki Basin.
