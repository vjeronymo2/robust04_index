910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Odyssey accident inquiry ends THE fatal accident inquiry into the death of a radio operator on board the North Sea oil rig Ocean Odyssey, started a year ago, ended in Aberdeen yesterday. Mr Timothy Williams, 25, of Greenhithe, Kent, died on September 22 1988, when an attempt to control a rogue high pressure well 130 miles east of Aberdeen went wrong.
