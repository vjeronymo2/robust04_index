Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday evening Brother Abu-'Ammar, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, received Viktor Vasilyev, special envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin for the Middle East peace process. During the meeting, which came as a continuation of Palestinian-Russian contacts, a discussion took place on the current developments in the situation in the region, in light of the massacre carried out by Zionist settlers in the holy Ibrahim mosque against the sons of the Palestinian people. The Russian envoy informed President 'Arafat of the viewpoints of the Russian leadership on the latest developments. Brother President Abu-'Ammar told the Russian envoy of all the developments which have taken place at the UN Security Council concerning the importance of international protection, the Jerusalem issue, and the problem of settlements and armed settlers, particularly the extremists among them, inside the Palestinian cities.
