Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Assam, suspected ULFA [United Liberation Front of Assam] militants shot dead the general manager of a tea company early this morning at (Talab) Tea Estate of Tinsukhia District in upper Assam. Police sources at Guwahati said the miscreants stole his vehicle, which was recovered later from a nearby village. A vigorous manhunt is on to nab the culprits.
