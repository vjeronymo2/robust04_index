BFN [Text] Kharkov, [no date as received] -- As a UNIAN correspondent learned, on 16 June an attempt was made on the life of Oleksiy Stelmakh, age 28, a people's deputy candidate of the city council, Constituency No. 70, and editor of the independent city newspaper V KAZHDYY DOM [TO EVERY HOUSE] with the largest circulation in the city. A stranger accosted him at the entrance to the editorial office and hit him several times on the head with a heavy metal object, thus inflicting grave bodily injuries. The stranger disappeared. In an interview with a UNIAN correspondent Oleksiy Stelmakh said he believed this was a political attempt, because his chances of winning in the constituency were extremely high. Criminal proceedings have been instituted in connection with this attack.
