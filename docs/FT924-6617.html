921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / Walt Disney improves by 29% WALT DISNEY yesterday posted a 29 per cent improvement in fourth-quarter net income to Dollars 223.7m (Pounds 150m), on revenues which rose 21 per cent to Dollars 2.08bn, writes Karen Zagor in New York. A year earlier, Disney had net earnings of Dollars 174.1m. For the full year, Disney had net income of Dollars 816.7m, up from Dollars 636.6m. In contrast to Euro Disney, operating income from Disney's domestic theme parks and resorts soared 44 per cent in the quarter to Dollars 213.9m on revenues which rose 26 per cent to Dollars 996.2m. For the year, operating income and revenues rose 18 per cent, to Dollars 644m and Dollars 3.31bn respectively.
