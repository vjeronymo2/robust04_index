930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Stakis STAKIS is raising Pounds 9.6m by a placing of 18m shares at 53 1/4 p. Of the proceeds, Pounds 5.85m will be used to buy a 4-star 128-room hotel in York, which at present is managed by Holiday Inns (UK). The balance will be used for other acquisitions.
