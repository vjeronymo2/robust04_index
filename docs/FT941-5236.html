940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / Deaths of valuable working dogs Nicholas Soames, junior agriculture minister, yesterday said sheep dogs were the victims of illegal, indiscriminate poisoning by some farmers wanting to kill predators. Mr Soames, pictured in London with a border collie, said: 'One irresponsible act can result in the agonising death of a valuable working dog.' Last year more than 70 dogs were poisoned
