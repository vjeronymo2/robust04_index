921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Thorp inquiry completion date A GOVERNMENT inquiry into the future of British Nuclear Fuel's Thorp reprocessing plant at Sellafield in Cumbria may be finished by early January. Discussions chaired by the Cabinet Office between the Department of the Environment, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Treasury and the Foreign Office, will produce a paper assessing the economic and environmental case for starting the Pounds 2.8bn plant. BNF estimates the eventual decommissioning costs of the plant at more than Pounds 1bn if it starts and becomes radioactive.
