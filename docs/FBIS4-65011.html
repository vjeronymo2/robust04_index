BFN [Text] Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's alternate special representative in Cyprus, left this afternoon for Vienna. Tomorrow he will meet Turkish Deputy Foreign Minister Tugay Ulucevik and Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department's special coordinator on the Cyprus issue. Feissel noted in a statement that two Turkish Cypriots, advisers to Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas, will also be in Vienna. He added that during tomorrow's meeting the situation will be evaluated since the deadlock on the talks on implementing the confidence-building measures. The Vienna talks are expected to be the last before the UN secretary general's report is circulated by the end of next week. Asked about the Lamb-Feissel-Ulucevik meeting, acting Government Spokesman Spiros Arotis referred to the statement by President Glavkos Kliridhis that the government does not expect anything from this meeting.
