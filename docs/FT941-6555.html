940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Prudential Corp Prudential Corporation's US subsidiary Jackson National Life Insurance saw a decrease in pre-tax earnings for 1993 from Dollars 339.1m (Pounds 232m) to Dollars 239.5m. Premiums were down at Dollars 2.06bn (Dollars 2.97bn). Assets increased to Dollars 16.9bn (Dollars 14.8bn).
