BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received messages of solidarity from Heinz Stehr and Rolf Trimer, co-chairmen of the Communist Party of Germany; Narayan Man Bijukchhe, chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party; and Man Mohan Adhikari, president of the Central Committee of the Nepal Communist Party (United Marxism-Leninism), supporting the struggle of the Korean people to defend the dignity of the nation and the sovereignty of the country. The messages denounced the policy of confrontation pursued by the United States and the South Korean authorities, escalating tensions on the Korean peninsula, and expressed full support to the just and invariable stand of the DPRK to defend its sovereignty and dignity. The messages declared firm solidarity for the just struggle of the Korean people led by President Kim Il-song against the war moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean authorities and the struggle of the progressive forces in South Korea.
