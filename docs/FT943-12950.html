940722 FT 22 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Daewoo wins Dollars 208m water supply order in Libya Daewoo of South Korea yesterday said it had won a Dollars 208m (Pounds 134m) contract from the city of Benghazi in Libya to undertake water supply, sewage and drainage works and lay pipes to absorb rain in the city's Slawi area, AP-DJ reports from Seoul. Separately, Daewoo Corporation, the trading arm of the conglomerate, said it had won a Dollars 30m order from Malaysia's real estate developer, Selangor Dredging group, to build a 27-storey building complex.
