940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / International Company News: First Ireland Investment FIRST IRELAND Investment increased net revenue from Pounds 505,000 to Pounds 606,000 for the year to March 31. Earnings per share on a net basis were 2p (1.7p) and 1.7p (1.4p) fully diluted. The single dividend is raised to 1.52p (1.44p).
