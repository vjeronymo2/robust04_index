941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Flogas doubled to IPounds 1.1m Growth in its liquefied petroleum and natural gas markets helped Flogas, the Irish gas supplier, to more than double its first half pre-tax profits to IPounds 1.06m (Pounds 1.05m). The figure for period to end-September compared with IPounds 414,000, which was after exceptional costs of IPounds 320,000. Turnover was up from IPounds 19.7m to IPounds 21.5m. Earnings per share were 3.41p (1.31p). The interim dividend is 3.57p (3.24p).
