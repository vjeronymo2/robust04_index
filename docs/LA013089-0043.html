January 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Mike Holland won the U.S. national ski jumping championship, and his brother, Joe, won the Nordic combined national title at Lake Placid, N.Y.

Mike Holland, 27, took the 70-meter jumping title 1 day after winning the 90-meters. He had jumps of 86.5 and 84 meters for 219.2 points. Tad Langlois of Newport, N.H., was runner-up both days.

Joe Holland won his second consecutive Nordic combined title. He was the leader Saturday in 70-meter jumping and, was second in Sunday's 15-kilometer cross-country race. Holland had 432.23 points. Runner-up Dan O'Meara of Steamboat Springs, Colo., had 405.32 points for finishing fourth in the 15-kilometer race and second in jumping.
