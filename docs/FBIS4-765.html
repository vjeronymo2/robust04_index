BFN [Text] The African National Congress says it would like officials from the Persian Gulf to attend Mr. Nelson Mandela's presidential inauguration in the event of an ANC victory in this month's elections. During a brief visit to Kuwait, ANC National Chairman Thabo Mbeki said that he has extended provisional invitations on visits to Qatar, Kuwait, and the United Arab Emirates this week. Mr. Mbeki met Kuwait's Foreign Ministry Under Secretary Sulayman al-Shahin [words in distinct].
