BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received a gift from the friendship visiting group of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army Choe Kwang by the head of the group, General Wang Ke, commander of the Shenyang military area of the CPLA.
