Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Aswan, Upper Egypt, 28 Feb (MENA)--Security agencies in the Governorate of Aswan, Upper Egypt, have exterminated three fugitive terrorist elements in a pursuit in the Idfu plantations area. One of the three killed last night was fugitive terrorist Mahmud Abu-al-Majd, a resident of Draw. He was wanted in several cases in Aswan. The competent authorities are examining the two other bodies to determine their identity. A conscript was slightly wounded during the chase. He was immediately given first aid treatment. The three terrorists killed conscript 'Ali 'Abd-al-Fattah Abu-Hilb in an attack in front of the Idfu traffic department last night.
