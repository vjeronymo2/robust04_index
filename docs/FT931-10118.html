930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Observer: Boot in Keen to impress, a much landed Swiss businessman proudly walked a visiting Texan customer around his wide acres. 'Fine trek,' the Texan remarked afterwards. 'When I look over my spread back home, d'you know, I have to go in my car and it takes me a whole day to cross it.' 'I had a car like that once, too,' the Swiss said.
