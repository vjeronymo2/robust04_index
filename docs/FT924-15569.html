921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / World Stock Markets: Brazil The Sao Paulo Stock Exchange (Bovespa) index ended down 3,711 points (7.95%) at 42,917 after the announcement that Mr Gustavo Krause had been appointed as finance minister, writes Bill Hinchberger in Sao Paulo. Trading was higher than normal over recent months, with Dollars 85.8m changing hands. The Bovespa rose by 4.7 per cent on Thursday.
