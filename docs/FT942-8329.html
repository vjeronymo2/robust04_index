940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto finished mixed after moderate trading. The TSE-300 composite index gained 7.6 at 4,285.7, but declines outscored advances by 382 to 356. Volume of 60.3m shares was below Wednesday's 66.4m, and trading value came to CDollars 649m. The 14 stock groups were evenly divided between winners and losers. The golds sector registered the day's biggest change, a rise of 1.83 per cent. OCS Technologies jumped 55 cents to CDollars 3.05. The company said it will provide computer information systems solutions to the public safety marketplace under an alliance with Motorola.
