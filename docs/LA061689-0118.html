June 16, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: ANAHEIM;

ARSON SUSPECT HELD AFTER $6,000 BLAZE

A 42-year-old man was arrested Thursday on suspicion of arson in a fire that destroyed a storage shed and a fence in a residential neighborhood.

Anaheim fire officials said there were no injuries in the blaze, which caused an estimated $6,000 in damage to the structure and its contents.

Firefighters responded to the call at 4:43 a.m. Thursday in the 300 block of South Helena Street. Investigators believe the cause was arson, fire officials said.

The name of the suspect was not released pending further interrogation, a spokesman for the Anaheim Fire Department said.
