Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Numerous national newspapers, which disappeared from the newsstands due to the strike at the central Algiers printing company, will start reappearing beginning this evening because this strike has come to an end. [passage omitted] The employees of the Central Algiers Printing Company have been on an open strike since yesterday. This was due to a disagreement with management regarding the review of the salary grid and the implementation of the collective agreement. This strike had a quick effect, which resulted in the dailies EL MASA and LE SOIR D'ALGERIE not being published since yesterday evening and the absence of about seven national dailies from the newsstands today.
