Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] At the residence of Panamenist Party Vice President Alfredo Arias in Altos del Golf, where negotiations to establish the progovernment presidential ticket were conducted, it has been disclosed that the Democratic Alliance has been formed. The Alliance's presidential ticket is as follows: Presidential candidate, Mireya Moscoso; first vice presidential candidate, Dr. Arnulfo Escalona; and second vice presidential candidate, Dr. Jose Teran Sitton of the Liberal Party.
