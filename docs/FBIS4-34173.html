BFN [Text] During an evening debate with the Jewish community on 13 June, PT [Workers Party] presidential candidate Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva said he, like the Jews, is a "survivor." He said: "I survived the starvation war in the northeastern region." He included something new in his government proposal, saying: "I will negotiate the foreign debt directly with the presidents." He added: "I will contact U.S. President Bill Clinton because I know he is more sensitive to the social situation resulting from our debt." He criticized the current Brazilian foreign policy, saying: "We are important enough to play the international power game, yet all we do is run after the ball." Lula and Jose Dirceu, the PT candidate for the governorship of Sao Paulo State, kicked off a series of candidate debates sponsored by the Sao Paulo Israeli Federation at the Hebrew Club. Progressive Renewal Party presidential candidate Esperidiao Amin and Luiz Antonio de Medeiros, the Popular Party candidate for state governor, will participate on 27 June. Brazilian Social Democracy Party candidates Fernando Henrique Cardoso and Mario Covas are scheduled for 8 August, and Orestes Quercia and Barros Munhoz for 22 August.
