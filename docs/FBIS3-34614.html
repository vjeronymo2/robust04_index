Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Israel Television Channel 1 in Hebrew at 1800 GMT on 9 February carries a live report from the Prime Minister's Office in Tel Aviv by political correspondent Gadi Sukenik, who states that he has "just learned that Shim'on Sheves, director general of the Prime Minister's Office, and Jacques Neriya, the prime minister's political adviser, have left secretly" "for some European country." Sukenik adds that the country may be Spain, where Rabin is slated to pay an official visit in two weeks. He emphasizes that "their departure has not been announced."
