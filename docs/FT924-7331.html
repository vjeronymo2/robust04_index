921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Black Arrow trebles BLACK ARROW Group, the office furniture and leasing finance group, saw pre-tax profits treble in the half year to September 30. However, the company warned that the outlook for the rest of the year was bleak. On turnover up 41 per cent at Pounds 11.7m (Pounds 8.35m) profits rose from Pounds 538,000 to Pounds 1.55m. including a Pounds 668,000 contribution from a property disposal. But Mr Arnold Edward, chairman, said trading conditions had deteriorated in recent months and were not expected to improve for some time. The interim dividend, doubled at 1p, 'but does not necessarily imply an increase in the final dividend', he said.
