BFN ["st."-signed report: "Marijuana in Ketchup"] [Text] Vienna/Schwechat -- A suitcase with stinking fish, a putrid steppe animal, and African fruits from Lagos: At Vienna-Schwechat Airport, the drug squad headed by Colonel Alfred Rupf checked the strange luggage, where the four large ketchup cans were probably supposed to attract the least amount of attention. In fact, however, 19 kg of marijuana packed in plastic bags were found in the sauce. A suspect, who wanted to pick up the luggage, is under arrest.
