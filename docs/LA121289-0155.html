December 12, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

MCCALLUM TO DEFEND WBA TITLE

Jamaican Mike McCallum will defend his World Boxing Assn. middleweight title against Ireland's Steve Collins on Feb. 3, promoters said today.

Collins, the WBA's fifth-ranked middleweight, will fight the champion at the 4,000-seat Point Depot, a new arena along the waterfront in Dublin.

McCallum was scheduled to defend his crown against Briton Michael Watson last month in London, but the top-ranked challenger broke his nose during training and had to pull out of the fight.
