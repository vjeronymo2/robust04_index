BFN [Text] Havana, 23 Jun (EFE) -- Cuban State security has summoned several human rights activists for questioning on their alleged cooperation with those individuals inside the residence of the Belgian ambassador in Havana, a dissident source said today. Among those summoned to "Villa Maristas," which is the general headquarters of Cuban State security in Havana, are Elizardo Sanchez and Vladimiro Roca, both members of the "Democratic Socialist Current," and Jesus Yanes Pelletier of the "Cuban Human Rights Committee." The dissident source explained they had been summoned by authorities for questioning on their alleged connection with the Cubans occupying the Belgian diplomatic mission. The source, however, affirmed they do not know those who are inside the Belgian diplomatic mission, adding that they could be independent human rights activists.
