BFN [Text] Lunan Cement Plant, China's first modernized large cement enterprise to use domestically produced equipment designed in China, and to be furnished with two new production lines, formally passed the state acceptance test on 30 March. This is a breakthrough in China's large cement industry as well as a milestone in the history of developments in the building materials industry. This has also put an end to China's long history of importing modernized cement production lines from abroad. The construction of the Lunan Cement Plant is a key project of the state during the Seventh Five-Year Plan period. All the major equipment of this plant was produced by China itself. The two new production lines with a daily production capacity of 2,000 tonnes of clinker [words indistinct] are designed to produce 1.3 million tonnes of cement annually. Zou Jiahua, vice premier of the State Council; and Wang Guangying, Lei Jieqiong, and Lu Jiaxi, vice chairmen of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, wrote inscriptions for and congratulated Lunan Cement Plant on its receipt of state approval. Jiang Chunyun, secretary of the provincial party committee, sent a congratulatory message to the plant. (Zhang Renwei), director of the State Building Materials Bureau, and Li Wenquan, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, attended the completion ceremony.
