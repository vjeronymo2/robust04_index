BFN [From the "Danuza" column by Danuza Leao] [Text] Foreign Minister Celso Amorim will leave Brazil on 3 May on a 10-day world tour. Amorim will visit France, where he will meet with the French defense minister and the UNESCO director general. Later he will represent President Itamar Franco at the inauguration of Nelson Mandela. Amorim also will visit Mozambique and Angola.
