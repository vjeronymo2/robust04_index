BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- Overseas Koreans on a visit to the socialist homeland paid a congratulatory call on the great leader President Kim Il-song today. The visitors included the congratulatory group of Koreans in Japan for celebrating April 15 led by Yi Chin-kyu, first vice- chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), the delegation of old cadres of Chongnyon led by Paek Chong-won, chairman of the Sports Federation of Koreans in Japan, and other delegations of Chongnyon, the congratulatory group of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification for celebrating April 15 led by its vice-chairman Chu Yong-il, the congratulatory group of Koreans in China under the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification (Pomminnyon) for celebrating April 15 led by Nam Su-nam, first vice- chairman of the Pomminnyon headquarters of Koreans in China, the congratulatory group of the Organisation of Korean Citizens in Russia for celebrating April 15 led by Chae Chong-man, and Choe Hong-hui, president of the International Taekwon-do Federation, Yi Yong-pin, chairman of the Association of Korean Christians for National Reunification in Germany, and his party and Rev. Cho Tok- chon, director of the Korea Institute of William Carey University, the U.S. Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Kim Yong-sun and officials concerned were present. The congratulatory groups, delegations and overseas Koreans presented floral baskets to President Kim Il-song on his birthday and wished him good health and a long life. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and had a talk with them in a cordial atmosphere overflowing with compatriotic feelings.
