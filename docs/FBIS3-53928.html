Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve him, has ordered an increase in the rice, vegetable oils, tea, washing detergents, and soap rations. This has been stated to the Iraqi News Agency by Trade Minister Dr. Muhammad Mahdi Salih. He explained that the increase in these rations, as ordered by the leader president, will become effective in February. They are as follows: --The monthly rice ration per individual shall be increased by 250 grams, up to 2.5 kg from 2.25 kg. --The monthly vegetable oils ration per individual shall be increased by 125 grams, up to 625 grams from 500 grams. --The monthly tea ration per individual shall be increased by 25 grams, up to 100 grams from 75 grams. --The monthly washing detergents ration per individual shall be increased by 50 grams, up to 250 grams from 200 grams. --The monthly soap ration per individual shall be increased by 25 grams, up to 150 grams from 125 grams. The trade minister explained that the new increases of the supplies included in the ration card range from 20-33 percent per month.
