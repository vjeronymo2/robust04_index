Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with the North Korean Foreign Ministry spokesman's statement on the warning of the withdrawal from the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty, a government official today issued a commentary revealing that the government expects the North Korean side to respond to the government's efforts to resolve nuclear issues through dialogue under close cooperation with the relevant countries, including the United States. In particular, in connection with the issue concerning Patriot missiles which was mentioned in the North Korean Foreign Ministry spokesman's statement, the government revealed that because Patriot missiles are purely defensive weapons, the government's review [komto] of deploying Patriot missiles is only one of the efforts to improve the ROK-U.S. combined defensive posture and thus has nothing do with nuclear issues.
