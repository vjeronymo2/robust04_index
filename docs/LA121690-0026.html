December 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PRIME-TIME FLICKS

Scrooge (Channel 56 Friday at 9 p.m.) is the delightful 1970 musical version of Charles Dickens' "A Christmas Carol" that captures the snow-bright Victorian Yuletide celebration of roast goose, mulled wine and urchins caroling. Under Ronald Neame's direction Albert Finney brings an agreeable depth to the title role.
