941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Simons & Co Simons & Co, a subsidiary of Geest Industries, the food group, has not traded in the half year to July 2. Interest of Pounds 12,005 was received on intergroup indebtedness, from which a dividend was paid to preference shareholders for the six months to June 30. Last year's interest received amounted to Pounds 12,041. No dividend is payable to ordinary shareholders.
