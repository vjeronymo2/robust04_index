Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Iran Opposition Leader Reported Attacked in Florence"] [Text] Nicosia, 27 Feb (ANSA) -- The Iranian National Resistance Council said that one of its members in Florence escaped an assassination attempt yesterday. In a statement to ANSA's Nicosia bureau, the group said that "two terrorists sent by the Khomeyni regime" chased one of their members in a car. The two, arrested by Tuscan police, were identified by the opposition group as Ayoub Fayoui and Iraj Oaderi [names as received]. Last March, an opposition leader Mohammed Hossein Naghdidel [name as received] was assassinated in Rome.
