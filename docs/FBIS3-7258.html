Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] In order to speed development of the Myanmar [Burma] hotel and tourism industry, to enhance the economy through the hotels and tourism industry, and to welcome tourists to Myanmar, which is rich in tradition, cultural heritage, and nature, the Ministry of Hotels and Tourism has been finding ways and means to develop its activities quickly. Thus, with the view to attract more tourists, the duration of entry visas for tourists has been increased from two weeks to four weeks and the foreign exchange requirement has been increased to US$300 from US$200. These changes have been effective since 14 March.
