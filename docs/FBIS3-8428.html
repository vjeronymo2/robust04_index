Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The regular meeting of the cabinet today lasted less than an hour. The cabinet adopted the regulations for the activity of the Interdepartmental Committee on Priority Environmental Projects and the Development of the Republic of Bulgaria. The draft agreements for trade-economic cooperation with Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Uzbekistan, and an agreement on the mutual protection of investments with Poland, also were adopted. The cabinet decided to propose to President Zhelyu Zhelev to appoint Bulgarian Ambassador to Spain, Mikhail Petkov also to the post of Ambassador to Andora. The other items on the agenda, including the draft bill on the diplomatic service, were postponed.
