930421 FT 21 APR 93 / International Company News: Reversal for Polaroid POLAROID, the US photography group, reported a first quarter net loss of Dollars 122.8m, or Dollars 2.63 a share, including an after-tax charge of Dollars 125.7m for accounting changes, writes Karen Zagor. A year earlier, the company earned Dollars 6.2m, or 13 cents. Sales rose to Dollars 468.4m in the latest quarter from Dollars 236m. Estimated net income, excluding special items, was Dollars 2.9m, or 6 cents, in the 1993 quarter. Polaroid expects start-up costs for new product to affect its operating results this year.
