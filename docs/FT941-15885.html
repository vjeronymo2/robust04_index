940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Repeat threatened of previous violent protests Private schools get 13 per cent or FFr34.5bn (Pounds 3.96bn) of national education budget which totals FFr250bn for 1994 Private schools educate 2.07m pupils, or 17 per cent of total of 12.2m French schoolchildren Private schooling is more important at the secondary level (20.7 per cent of total) than at the primary level (13.8 per cent of total)
