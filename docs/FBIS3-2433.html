Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 9 Mar (XINHUA) -- The Presidium of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] held its first meeting in the Great Hall of the People this morning. Chairman Qiao Shi presided over the meeting. The 137 presidium members present at the meeting first elected the chairman, vice chairmen, and secretary-general of the NPC Standing Committee as executive chairmen of the Presidium. The meeting adopted the schedule of the NPC session and the namelist of the executive chairman groups. It decided on the choice of persons for deputy secretaries-general and on a voting method for bills. It also adopted a draft election method for the NPC session and a draft decision on approving the request of the NPC Standing Committee for accepting the resignation of Yang Yanyin from membership of the NPC Standing Committee. The two drafts will be printed and transmitted to all delegations for deliberation before it is submitted to the plenary session for a vote. The meeting set 6:00 PM [1000 GMT] of 16 March as the deadline for bill submission by deputies. According to the schedule, the NPC session will last 13 days.
