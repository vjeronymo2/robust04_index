920430 FT 30 APR 92 / AT&T offers a telephone number for life AMERICANS are to be offered a personal telephone number for life, the perfect accessory for a nation whose citizens move house an average of 11 times. In June, AT&T, the US telecommunications group, will introduce a personal telephone number that will allow subscribers to be reached at any telephone in the continental US. AT&T's Easy Reach 700 service provides subscribers with a personal identification number - their own access number to the service. The number need not change if the subscriber moves house within the US. Although call-forwarding is already available, Easy Reach subscribers not only have a number for life but can also select which calls they want forwarded to them at any location. At Dollars 7 (Pounds 3.90) a month the service will not be cheap for consumers used to receiving telephone numbers free. Calls within the US will cost 25 cents a minute between 8am and 5pm and 15 cents a minute at other times.
