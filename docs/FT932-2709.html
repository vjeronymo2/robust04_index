930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Clyde Blowers Despite recording a first-half loss before tax of Pounds 17,632, against a Pounds 33,723 profit, Clyde Blowers hoisted its interim dividend from 0.83p to 4p. Mr JA McColl, chairman, said the decision was in recognition of progress now being made and would reflect 'the pattern of profit recognition that we have the potential to achieve'. Turnover for the half year to February 28 was Pounds 2.27m (Pounds 2.01m) and losses per share came to 1.76p (2.52p earnings).
