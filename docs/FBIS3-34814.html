Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Alain Juppe, who is currently visiting Israel, repeated that the NATO ultimatum was not a bluff: [Begin Juppe recording] The NATO decision is not bluff. It is a real ultimatum, and it is up to the UN Protection Force command to tell us when the ultimatum expires, whether the alliance demands -- which were drawn up in narrow consultation with the UN secretary- general -- are met or not. If they are not met, then we will have to draw the conclusions. In other words, we will have to destroy the artillery positions that have not been pulled back. If the demands are met, then all the best. You know, I have kept repeating -- when I was in Zagreb, in Sarajevo -- that there is no lasting solution other than a diplomatic and political one. [end recording]
