BFN [Text] Archangelsk Oblast has formed its own government. Pavel Balakshin, head of the oblast administration, appointed Valentin Vlasov, 48, to be its chairman. Up to now, Vlasov has been mayor of Archangelsk. Valentin Vlasov considers the establishment of a government is not merely another change of labels, but an attempt to set up an effective executive body which matches up to contemporary requirements.
