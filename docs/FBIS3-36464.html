Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 7 TASS--A communications satellite, Raduga-1, was launched by a proton booster from the Baykonur cosmodrome on February 5, 1994. The satellite carries multichannel relay equipment to boost telephone and telegraph radio communications over the territory of the Russian Federation. The satellite was put into a near-stationary orbit with the following parameters: distance from the Earth's surface -- 36,513 kilometers, revolution period -- 24 hours 33 minutes, orbital inclination -- 1.4 degrees. Installed equipment operates smoothly. The command and measurement complex exercises contol over the satellite. Work with the satellite will be conducted in compliance with a set programme.
