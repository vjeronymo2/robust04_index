Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ARMENPRES correspondent Garri Galustyan for TASS] [Text] Yerevan February 15 TASS -- Fighting goes on in the Karabakh front's northern section, sources at the Karabakh Defense Army's headquarters told ARMENPRES. The main developments are reported in direction of the strategic Omar mountain pass in the north of the Kelbajar region. Karabakh units are fighting for full control in the front's important area. The Azeri offensive towards the Tonashen settlement of the Mardakert region resumed today to continue the two-day fighting. Karabakh units are repulsing the attacks. There is no information about casualties. It was relatively calm in all other areas of confrontation this morning.
