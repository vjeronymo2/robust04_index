Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning the provincial party committee office, government office, and the propaganda department of the provincial party committee jointly held a meeting in Nanchang for journalists to celebrate the Spring Festival. Provincial leaders Wu Guanzheng, Zhu Zhihong, Lu Xiuzhen, Shu Shengyou, Zhong Qihuang, Chen Guizun, and Wu Yongle, reporters of central press units stationed in Jiangxi, provincial and city journalists and publishers, as well as some retired comrades from the press and publishing front gathered together to usher in the Spring Festival. The meeting was presided over by Zhong Qihuang. Wu Guanzhen and Lu Xiuzhen spoke at the meeting. In his speech, Governor Wu Guanzhen fully confirmed the province's achievements in last year's press and publicity work. He thanked central press unit reporters stationed in Jiangxi and Jiangxi reporters for their hard work and expressed his Lunar New Year greetings to them. He stressed: In this year's economic work, the province should pay attention to three main points: The first is strengthening agricultural and rural work to ensure an increase in peasant income. The second is strengthening basic facilities construction to improve Jiangxi's investment environment in three to five years. The third is concentrating efforts on reform, opening up, introducting foreign capital, and expanding exports. He hoped that central press unit reporters stationed in Jiangxi as well as provincial and city reporters would make further efforts to give wide publicity to Jiangxi's development. In his speech, Lu Xiuzhen requested Jiangxi reporters to continue to give publicity to Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, to uphold the policy of unity, stability, boosting the morale, and positive dissemination, to strengthen guidance over publicity, to properly organize reports on reform, to propagate Jiangxi's achievements overseas, and to improve the ranks of journalists.
