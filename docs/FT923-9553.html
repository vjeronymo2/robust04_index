920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / International Company News: Banks in talks with Torras REPRESENTATIVES of about 60 banks will meet officials of the Kuwait Investment Office's (KIO) fully-owned subsidiary, Grupo Torras today to discuss debt servicing within the group, Reuter reports from Madrid. The meeting will focus on Prima Inmobiliaria, a real est-ate company in which Torras holds 27 per cent, but may also review other companies in the group, including Ercros, which filed for a partial suspension of payments on July 8.
