Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On the morning of 3 February, Jinan's Nanjiao Guest House was filled with thoughts of love and an enthusiastic atmosphere as the Shandong Provincial CPC Committee and the provincial people's government jointly sponsored the Spring Festival tea party for personages from various social circles. Participants gathered together happily for 1994's Spring Festival. Jiang Chunyun, secretary of the provincial party committee, presided over the tea party and Zhao Zhihao, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee and governor of the province, delivered an ebullient speech at the party. In his speech on behalf of the provincial party committee and the provincial people's government, Zhao Zhihao first extended festive greetings and best wishes to the participating personnel; the democratic parties and federations of industry and commerce across the province; various mass organizations; nonparty patriotic personages; representative personages of national and religious circles; nonparty intellectuals; representative personages from non-publicly-owned enterprises; compatriots from Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao and overseas Chinese, who are currently in the province; as well as friends of various circles, who have shown concern and support, and provided help in the province's program of modernization. [passage omitted] Also attending the tea party were responsible comrades from the provincial party committee, the provincial people's congress standing committee, the provincial people's government, and the provincial Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference committee, the provincial discipline inspection commission, and Jinan city, including Li Chunting, Li Wenquan, Han Xikai, Li Zhen, Lu Maozeng, Ma Zhongcai, Dong Fengji, Han Yuquan, Yan Qingqing, Zhai Yongbo, Tian Jian, Kong Lingren, Hu Yuliang, Cui Weilin, Wu Fuheng, Wu Minggang, Wang Zunong, Su Yingheng, Miao Yongming, and Li Gongjiu; as well as veteran comrades from the provincial level organs, including Liang Buting, Li Zichao, Zhou Xingfu, Xu Wenyuan, Jin Guomin, and Jin Baozhen.
