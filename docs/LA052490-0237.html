May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: BISHOP AMAT STUDENT TO TAKE PART IN SEMINAR

Donna Varner, a sophomore at Bishop Amat Memorial High School in La Puente, will represent her school at the annual Hugh O'Brien Youth Foundation Leadership Seminar June 1-3 at Pepperdine University.

The seminar, attended by 200 sophomores from Southern California, will deal with essay writing, entrepreneurship and student debates.
