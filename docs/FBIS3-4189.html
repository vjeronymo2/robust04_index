Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hainan delegation to the second session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] returned to Haikou by special plane at 1600 today. Receiving them at the Haikou airport were leading members of the provincial party committee, people's congress, government, and committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference including Wang Xiaofeng, Chen Yuyi, Xiao Xuchu, Wang Houhong, Dong Fanyuan, Liu Qibin, Hui Hefang, Xin Yejiang, Liu Mingqi, Lin Mingyu, Liu Jiangqiao, and Li Qingyun, Haikou provincial people' congress chairman. Head of the delegation Ruan Chongwu, Hainan Provincial Party Committee secretary, concurrently governor and its deputy head, Du Qinglin, provincial party committee deputy secretary, concurrently provincial people's congress chairman, accepted interviews by reporters. They briefed the reporters on the grand occasion of the second session of the Eighth National People's Congress, indicated that it was a session characterized by solidarity and seeking truth from facts, and would eventually play a role in promoting the deepening of reform, expanding opening up, and accelerating economic development in Hainan.
