Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 16 Jan--An ITAR-TASS correspondent at the headquarters of the command of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan was informed today that one of the border posts of the Gandyshskiy border detachment came under intensive mortar fire yesterday evening. More than 20 mortars were launched along the stretch of the border with Afghanistan, some of which exploded right in the outskirts of the Ayvadzh border settlement. Russian border guards neutralized the enemy's weapon emplacements with powerful artillery fire. There were no casualties among border guards and local civilians. On the same day, Tajik opposition fighters fired from the territory of Afghanistan at a sector controlled by border guards from the Moskovskiy border unit. The Russian border guards neutralized the firing points of the mojahedin with answering fire.
