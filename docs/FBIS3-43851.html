Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] In a communique received at our news desk this afternoon, through our correspondent Morais Abel, the military command of the Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FALA] in the Cuanza Norte Military Region has reported the presence in Cambambe and Dondo of a large number of well-equipped Cuban and Katanguese mercenaries. According to the communique, these forces, acting jointly with the People's Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola and the riot police, have the mission of occupying all areas under control of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] in Cuanza Norte, particularly the city of Ndalatando, the provincial capital. The FALA military command said the mercenaries [words indistinct] will not find a room for maneuvers and will be repulsed. The military command has warned the international community of the danger that this Eduardo dos Santos' adventure represents for the Angolan people and the ongoing peace talks in Lusaka.
