February 8, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

NO LOTTERY JACKPOT WINNER

No one picked all six winning numbers in the California Lottery's Wednesday night "Lotto 6-49" game, leaving a $4.8-million jackpot untouched, Lottery officials said today.

The $4.8-million jackpot will roll over to help build the Saturday night jackpot to an estimated $10 million, officials said.

There was one ticket with five numbers plus the bonus number worth $1.3 million. Another 173 tickets with five of six will share a $679,000 prize pool, each receiving $3,929.
