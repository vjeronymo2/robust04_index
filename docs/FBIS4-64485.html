BFN [Excerpts] The Rwandan Patriotic Front is still refusing to negotiate with the Rwandan transitional government. [passage omitted] France's Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin yesterday explained Paris' position in a speech in the National Assembly: [Begin Roussin recording] Our ambassador in Rwanda has been entrusted with an assessment mission and has been asked to establish contacts with a view to obtaining a cease-fire and the resumption of dialogue between the Rwandans. Our ambassador is meeting with all sides: the transitional government and the leaders of the Rwandan Patriotic Front. France is maintaining close contact with the UN secretary general in order to assess the contributions of the international community toward the normalization of the situation. Finally, as long as conflicts continue, it is our duty to continue to carry out humanitarian operations. French NGOs [nongovernmental organizations] especially are bringing support to the martyred people, and I would like to hail their action. However we must do more. [passage omitted] [end recording]
