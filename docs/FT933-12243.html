930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Spanish Finance Minister warns that recession shows no signs of ending Spain's new finance minister, Pedro Solbes, has warned that the country's recession shows no sign of ending and that a sharp reduction of labour costs is required to stimulate the troubled economy. He indicated that there was little chance over the next two years of reducing Spain's budget deficit and said he would amend a package of ambitious targets set by the government for economic and monetary union convergence.
