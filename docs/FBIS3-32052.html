Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Jorge Celis] [Text] The National Police public relations spokesperson reports on last night's bomb scares. One of the bombs was defused, and the other exploded at 12th Street and 19th Avenue in Zone One. [Begin unidentified spokesperson recording] Two bomb scares were being handled. One was defused at 40th Street and 3d Avenue in Zone Three, behind the quarters of the National Police 1st Brigade [words indistinct]. The other, a fragmentation grenade, was thrown against a house. We cannot assume the last one was part of the terrorist actions that have been taking place in Guatemala City. We believe it could have been hurled at a private home, but we do not know which one, perhaps by someone who was inebriated. [end recording]
