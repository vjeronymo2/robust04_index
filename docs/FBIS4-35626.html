BFN [Text] Presidential Council Member 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani met with UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus-Ghali in New York last night. He briefed him on the situation in our country from all its aspects. Attending the meeting were 'Abdallah Ghanim, minister of justice, and 'Abdallah al-Ashtal, our country's permanent ambassador to the United Nations.
