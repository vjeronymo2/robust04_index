October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS CHOICE: PHILADELPHIA FIRE BY JOHN EDGAR WIDEMAN (HENRY HOLT: $19.95; 199 PP.)

"It is a swirl of stories, images and voices. Some burn indelibly. Wideman has made fire his own, and there are fire figures everywhere, illuminating us and driving us back with heat and smoky confusion."Richard Eder
