930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Renault continues Daf project RENAULT, the French vehicle maker, confirmed yesterday that it would use Leyland Daf, the collapsed commercial vehicle maker, as a sub-contractor in developing a new heavy van range. Renault and Daf started on the joint development of a new 2.5 tonnes to 4.2 tonnes van range, code-named Excel, in 1990. Renault has now acquired all the industrial property rights for the range. The development of the van will continue in Birmingham with the existing team of 50 Renault engineering staff and will use 190 Leyland Daf engineering staff on a sub-contract.
