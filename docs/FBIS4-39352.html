BFN [Excerpt] The State Duma Budget Committee on Budget, Taxes, Banks, and Finances' fears about the dynamics of production this year have proved to be right, Committee Chairman Mikhail Zadornov told the media today. The results of the first four months of the year are depressing. The fall in production is still 25 percent. Only 9 percent of taxes were collected instead of 11 percent. As a result, revenues covered only about half of budget spending, namely 49 per cent. [passage omitted]
