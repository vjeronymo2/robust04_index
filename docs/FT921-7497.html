920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / International Company News: Phillips to sell 51% of gas unit GPM Gas, a subsidiary of Phillips Petroleum of the US, is to make an initial public offering of up to 18.36m shares, Reuter reports from Washington. The offering constitutes 51 per cent of Phillips' stake, leaving it with a 49 per cent stake in the offshoot. Proceeds will be used by GPM to repay about Dollars 200m of Dollars 600m debt owed to Phillips.
