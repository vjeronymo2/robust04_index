920803 FT 03 AUG 92 / Sniper's victims A child cries behind the bullet-holed windscreen of a bus in which two children were killed by a sniper in Sarajevo. The bus was evacuating orphans from the besieged Bosnian capital to go to a refuge in Germany when it came under fire as Moslem forces battled to break the Serb hold on the city.
