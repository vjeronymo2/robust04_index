June 14, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OJAI SAILOR'S BODY IS FOUND ON BEACH

The body of an Ojai man missing at sea for 17 days was found washed ashore on an Oxnard beach by a jogger Wednesday, officials said.

Heinz Gunter Kluckow, 49, fell into the ocean Memorial Day while trying to recover a dinghy from his boat sailing two miles off Mandalay State Beach.

A co-worker and friend identified Kluckow on Wednesday by distinctive tattoo marks on his upper forearms, Deputy Coroner Mitch Breese said.

Richard Walczak of Springfield, Va., found Kluckow about 6 a.m. on the beach near Ocean Drive and Las Palmas Street, said Lt. Gary Backman of the Ventura County Sheriff's Department. KIRSTEN LEE SWARTZ
