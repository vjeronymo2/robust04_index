BFN [Text] Rafah, 14 May (MENA) -- The Palestinian Liaison Committee has resumed its work in the Gaza Strip to take over the rest of sites allocated for the Palestinian national security forces and to take the final measures for the police forces' deployment. Liaison officer Brigadier General Ghazi al-Jabali said the handover of police and civilian authority sites in the Jericho area has been completed and that the Palestinian authorities have begun work. He added that police forces are being deployed in the northern part of Gaza in preparation for its handover to the civilian authorities. According to the agreement, he said, the civilian authority should remain in place for a three-year transitional period under the PLO leadership. Coordination official Colonel Zakariya Ba'lushah said that obstacles impeding the entry of some personnel of the Palestinian national security forces have been removed and the civilian police will assume their duties in providing security as soon as they enter. Major General 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Majayirah said the forces coming from Libya, Sudan, and Algeria continue to assemble inside Rafah on the international border with Egypt and enter the Gaza Strip in stages according to a timetable being drawn up in coordination with the Israeli side. He added that the Palestinian national security forces are made up of several groups, such as the civilian police, security forces, and intelligence, in addition to civil defense and other police services, who will perform their roles in maintaining internal security and order; guarding military posts, border crossings, and public figures; and maintaining the security of the country, its citizens, and the self-rule areas. Elsewhere, Major General Nasr Yusuf, commander in chief of the forces, and members of the civilian authority continue to follow up on the withdrawal from various sites, the Israeli withdrawal from the few remaining places, and the deployment of Palestinian personnel in them. This procedure is scheduled to be complete by Wednesday, when the self-rule authorities will assume their role inside the Gaza Strip and Jericho area.
