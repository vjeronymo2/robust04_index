Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (MENA)--At his place of residence in the al-Andalus Palace in Heliopolis today, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat received members of the Israeli delegation to the negotiations for transferring self-rule powers to the Palestinians. Among others, the Israeli delegation includes Uri Savir, the Foreign Ministry director general; Uzi Dayan, political planning director at the Foreign Ministry; and Yo'el Singer, legal adviser at the Foreign Ministry. Attending the meeting on the Palestinian side were Dr. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation in the Security Committee; Ahmad Quray', alias Abu-'Ala', chief of the PLO economic department; Sa'id Kamal, Palestinian ambassador in Cairo; Zuhdi al-Qidrah, Fatah representative in Cairo; and a number of 'Arafat's aides. This is the first time 'Arafat has received an Israeli delegation at this level in Cairo. This meeting is intended to help reach a solution for implementing the Gaza-Jericho agreement on self-rule, which was scheduled to be implemented on 13 December. The Israeli delegation arrived in Cairo yesterday.
