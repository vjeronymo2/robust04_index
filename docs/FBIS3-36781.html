Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (February 7) TURAN--Local fighting continued along the front during yesterday, according to a source at the Azeri Defense Ministry. According to the information available, Armenian commanders carries out the regrouping of its forces at the northern (Agdere) and southern (Fizuli) sectors of the front. Extra units and armored equipment is being transported there. As soldiers of the Azeri army report, the most of the destroyed Armenian tanks and arms were made in Russia in 1993. The seized arms testify that Armenians receive arms from Russia and other countries. As reported earlier, Russian aircraft transporting hardware made tens of trips from Sochi to Gumri. According to the reports of the Western sources from Armenia, these aircraft transported arms and fuel to Armenia.
