December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ALL-ACADEMIC TEAM NAMED

The Rams and GTE will honor its first all-Orange County Academic High School Football team Sunday before the Rams' game with the New Orleans Saints at Anaheim Stadium.

The minimum criteria was achieving a grade-point average of at least 3.0 and earning at least second-team all-league honors.

Offense

Line: Jeff Dorsz (Troy), Steve Miller (Orange), Karl Shroller (Fountain Valley), Paul Richard (Valencia) and Ben Seguia (Brea-Olinda); QB: Rob Johnson (El Toro). Back: Visko Ancich (Tustin) and Mike Sullivan (Sunny Hills); TE: Reid Pullen (Esperanza); Rec: David Shearer (Santa Margarita) and Tyler Cashman (Los Alamitos); K: Doug Roberson (Mission Viejo).

Defense

Line: Jeremy Hogue (El Toro), Damon Becknel (Villa Park), Brian Pearsall (Villa Park) and Ismael Lopez (Anaheim); LB: David Phillips (Costa Mesa), Mike Silvey (Mater Dei) and Greg Livermore (La Quinta); Back: Bill Denny (Mission Viejo), Mike Ryan (Brea-Olinda), Eric Davis (Laguna Hills) and Eric Moore (Rancho Alamitos); P: Trevor Yankoff (Santa Margarita).
