BFN [Text] On the 19th anniversary of the liberation of South Vietnam, on the afternoon of 25 April, Comrade Vo Tran Chi, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Ho Chi Minh City party committee, cordially met with ethnic Chinese intellectuals in Ho Chi Minh City. The intellectuals are young people who have grown up in the new regime and developed political awareness early. They have actively contributed to the city building cause and been successful in their life advancement. The Chinese intellectuals honestly recalled their feelings and life experiences. They also expressed their enthusiasm and belief in the national renovation effort. Comrade Vo Tran Chi urged the intellectuals to continue building their beliefs and study continuously to enhance their contributions to the city and country.
