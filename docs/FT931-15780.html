930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / Observer: Inner spirit Anyone doubting that talent for big-business leadership can show very early may care to consider the following teatime conversation between a four-year-old and his mother, quoted in Margaret Donaldson's book, Human Minds, published by Penguin. He: Is God everywhere? She: Yes dear. He: In this room? She: Yes. He: Is he in my mug? She (growing uneasy): Yes. He (clapping his hand over the mug): Got him]
