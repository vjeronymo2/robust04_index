BFN [Report by D. Issacson] [Text] Johannesburg April 1 SAPA -- As much as 38 per cent of the country's electorate could stay away from this month's poll, the Human Sciences Research Council said. SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio reported on Friday [1 April] that contributing factors could include the high level of violence, uncertainty of confidentiality in voting booths, and a low literacy rate.
