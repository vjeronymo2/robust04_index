BFN [Text] The Yemeni News Agency correspondent in the al-Dali' district has reported the return of normal life to the city of al-Dali' and neighboring areas after the forces of constitutional legitimacy purged them of the forces misled by the secessionist elements in the Socialist Party. The correspondent said citizens resumed work and shops opened normally. In another development, the [Yemeni Television] First Channel team shot pictures of the 'Abbud Camp area and other areas which were brought under the control of the constitutional legitimacy forces, including arms depots, tanks, and vehicles left behind by the fleeing forces misled by the elements of apostasy, rebellion, and secession. The news team interviewed citizens and fighters who joined the legitimacy forces. They expressed their pleasure that the forces of unity and legitimacy had managed to rid the area and its citizens of the hegemony of the forces of apostasy, rebellion, and secession and their divisive practices. The brother citizens in the city of al-Dali' and neighboring areas appreciated the good treatment extended to them by the forces of legitimacy and unity, contrary to the falsehoods propagated by the secessionist elements in the Socialist Party. This good treatment proved that words are coupled by deeds by the legitimate leadership under brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council and supreme commander of the Armed Forces, who instructed the Armed Forces to extend all possible assistance to the citizens in the region and protect their property in confirmation of the responsibility shouldered by the state of unity under his leadership for the sake of protecting the citizens throughout Yemen.
