BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Tigran Liloyan and Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Yerevan, 1 Apr -- Levon Ter-Petrosyan, the president of Armenia, reaffirmed his country's desire to assist in a constructive solution to the Karabakh conflict at a meeting today with a delegation from the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly. He described the Commonwealth parliamentarians' efforts as serious, responsible, and also noble. "In Armenia you will only encounter the desire to make your work easier", L. Ter-Petrosyan stressed.
