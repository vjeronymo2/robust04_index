Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Fellow listeners, carrying the joy of spring and magnificent expectations, more than 600 CPPCC members from across the province with the purpose of attending the second session of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee happily met at Shenyang Bayi Theater on 27 February. With the spirit of showing utter devotion and sharing honor or disgrace, these members suggested ways and means and commonly discuss the major policy of deepening reform and accelerating development. At 0900 on the morning of 27 February, the session opened in Shenyang amid the majestic sound of national anthem. Executive chairmen of today' session included Sun Qi, Lin Sheng, Liu Mingjiu, Yue Weichun, Wang Shuzhi, Gao Qingzhou, Zhang Lingyun, and Zhang Chenglun. Among the leaders of the provincial party committee, the provincial people's congress, the provincial government, and the provincial military district who were invited to the session were Gu Jinchi, Quan Shuren, Yue Qifeng, Wang Huaiyuan, Li Guozhong, Xu Wencai, Wen Shizhen, Yu Junbo, Liu Zhenhua, Xiao Zuofu, Li Jun, Wang Rongmao, Cong Zhenglong, Guo Tingbiao, Zhang Rongming, and Xiang Jingyuan. Also attending the session were veteran leaders Guo Feng, Li Tao, Song Li, Xu Shaofu, Yang Guangzhong, Wei Zhi, (Wu Jianen), (Zhang Yuan), (Yu Jingqing), Shen Xianhui, Chen Yuanzhi, Li Qisheng, and Li Ming. Officials of the various consulates stationed in Shenyang were also invited to today's opening ceremony. Yue Weichun presided over the session. Sun Qi delivered an opening speech. [passage omitted] Sun Qi said: The CPPCC is an important place where various democratic parties, various people's groups, and the people of various nationalities on various circles can participate in and discuss political affairs in unity and cooperation. We hope that fellow CPPCC members will exercise their democratic rights to participate in and discuss political affairs with a high degree of the senses of responsibility and mission. Lin Sheng, vice chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee delivered a work report. In his report, Lin Sheng looked back on the work done by the seventh provincial CPPCC committee in 1993 and offered suggestions for this year's work. He said: From now on, the provincial CPPCC committee should continue to profoundly study Volume Three of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping, conscientiously carry out the guidelines of the 14th party congress and the third plenary session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, better perform the functions of political consultation and democratic supervision, help accelerate the building of socialist market economic structure, positively press forward the building of socialist democratic politics and the socialist spiritual civilization, continue to help stabilize the society, and utilize the advantages of the CPPCC to continuously expand the friendship at home and abroad in order to facilitate the reunification of the motherland and the expansion of the opening up. He also demanded that the provincial CPPCC committee should establish even closer ties with CPPCC members and democratic parties, strengthen leadership over the work of CPPCC organizations in various cities, counties, and districts, and further strengthen the self-construction of the CPPCC in line with demands of the new situation.
