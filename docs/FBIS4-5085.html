BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 2 (KNS-KCNA) -- A meeting of the Committee for Defending Human Rights of Koreans in Japan was held at the Korean House in Tokyo on March 30. The meeting stressed the need to radically develop the work for defending rights of the compatriots, enhance the role of the special measure committee and briskly conduct activities to defend rights of the compatriots in face of the anti-DPRK, anti-Chongnyon (the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan) moves of the international reactionaries.
