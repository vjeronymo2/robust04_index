BFN [Text] Srbobran, 8 May (TANJUG) -- Serbian military sources in Srbobran (former Donji Vakuf) said today that a Catholic church in the nearby Croatian village of Drvetine near [Muslim-held] Bugojno was set on fire by Muslims last night. The church in the village, currently held by the Muslims, was burned down. The radio of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna has reported that there are still no reports of the whereabouts of 26 Croatian intellectuals arrested and taken away from their homes by the Muslim authorities over nine months ago.
