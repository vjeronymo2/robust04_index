Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 18 KYODO -- Japan's industrial production remains sluggish so far in January, except in limited sectors including the semiconductor industry, the Economic Planning Agency (EPA) said in a survey published Tuesday [18 January]. It also said efforts to reduce inventories were making little progress. Domestic shipments of videocassette recorders, refrigerators and washing machines in January recorded year-on-year increases, however. In the nonmanufacturing sector, the value of lease contracts continues to decrease and domestic freight transport business, other than home delivery of parcels, remains slow, the EPA said.
