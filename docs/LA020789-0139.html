February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

SADDLEBACK STILL ATOP PREP POLL

Saddleback High School, riding a 17-game winning streak, is the top-ranked team in the Orange County Sportswriters' Assn. prep basketball poll for the fifth consecutive week.

Saddleback has the longest winning streak in the county, followed by eighth-ranked El Dorado (12) and 10th-ranked Trabuco Hills (11). Trabuco Hills makes its first appearance in the poll this week.

In the featured game of the week, third-ranked Capistrano Valley plays at sixth-ranked Irvine on Wednesday in a South Coast League game.
