March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: AWARDS

Laughing All the Way to the Awards: Meg Ryan and Billy Crystal gave "When Harry Met Sally . . ." a sweep Saturday night at the fourth American Comedy Awards at the Shrine Auditorium. Ryan was named 1989's funniest film actress and Crystal funniest film actor for their lead roles in the movie.

Julie Kavner was the only double winner during the ceremony. She was named funniest supporting film actress for "New York Stories" and funniest supporting actress in a TV series for "The Tracey Ullman Show." Tracey Ullman upset Roseanne Barr and Candice Bergen to win funniest female performer in a TV series. John Goodman of "Roseanne" won for funniest male in a series. The show was taped for an ABC telecast next Monday. BETH KLEID
