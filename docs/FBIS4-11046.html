BFN [Text] Cordoba, 19 Apr (NA) -- A low-intensity explosive device exploded at the home of an Army official who, according to neighbors, had been commissioned to Artillery Group No. 161 in Zapala, Neuquen Province. Even though the reports that First Lieutenant Daniel Vega, a physician, had been commissioned to that garrison were not officially confirmed, they were not denied by military authorities either. According to what was made public by Cordoba Province Police, the explosive device went off at around 0200 local time and caused only minor damage and no injuries. The explosion only caused some damage to the door of the house located at 112 Jacinto Rios Street in the General Paz district, where Vega and his wife live.
