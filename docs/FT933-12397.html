930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Third of women 'harassed at work' but only 14 per cent of those who have suffered have told their managers, according to a survey for the BBC, conducted by MORI, the market research group, Diane Summers writes. The survey of 700 women found that less than half (49 per cent) of those experiencing harassment had discussed it with others. Jokes and lewd comments were said to be the most common forms of harassment.
