BFN [Report by Aleksa Crnjakovic: "The Opposition Will Not Give Up Mesic"] [Text] Zagreb -- "Owing to the need for consultations within our parties, we agreed to conclude them by Friday and to agree on the continuation of the negotiations then," Jure Radic stated briefly on Wednesday, after the meeting between the representatives of the HDZ [Croatian Democratic Community] and parliamentary opposition parties that lasted for an hour and a half. The representatives of the Croatian opposition have briefly confirmed Radic's statement and wanted to make no more comments about the negotiations. The HDZ representatives in the negotiations are: Jure Radic, Mate Granic, Adam Mestrovic, Ivan Valent, and Ivic Pasalic. The representatives of the opposition in the negotiations are: Savka Dabcevic-Kucar and Slavko Mestrovic (Croatian National Party), Drazen Budisa (Croatian Social Liberal Party), Stjepan Radic (Croatian Peasants Party), Ivica Racan (Party of Democratic Changes), Ivan Jakovcic (Istrian Democratic Congress), and Slavko Degoricija (Croatian Independent Democrats).
