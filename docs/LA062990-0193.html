June 29, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

RIGAMAJIG CAPTURES $33,000 TERPSICHORIST

Rigamajig slipped through between horses to take the lead in the deep stretch and then held on to win a neck victory in the $33,000 Terpsichorist purse for fillies and mares 3-years-and-up at Belmont Park.

Noble Links was runner-up in the eight-horse field another neck ahead of Le Famo.

The win was the third in seven starts this year for Rigamajig who covered one mile and a quarter on the firm Inner Turf Course in 1:59:3 to earn $19,800 and return $19.00, $8.40 and $7.00. Noble Links paid $10.00 and $5.00 and Le Famo was $4.20.
