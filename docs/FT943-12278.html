940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / Observer: Blindman's buff To the back of the class. The government, struggling to secure a chief executive for the new Teacher Training Agency, has had to readvertise the job, increasing the pay by a third on the way. So if you are an excellent communicator, have experience of start-ups, and fancy managing a Pounds 180m budget, you can now reap Pounds 80,000 a year for running a quango. And ignorance of the subject is really no handicap. Knowledge of the English education system is merely 'desirable', the ad suggests.
