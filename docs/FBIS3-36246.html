Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ARMENPRESS correspondent Garri Kyufaryan for TASS] [Text] Yerevan, 3 Feb -- "Our location on the territory of Armenia has nothing to do with events in Nagorno-Karabakh," the commander of the group of Russian forces told an ARMENPRESS-TASS correspondent in Yerevan today. "We are carrying out tasks stipulated by the interstate treaty of the Russian Federation. Any rumors and speculation about additional supplies of weapons being sent from Russia to Armenia are unfounded, whatever source they may be coming from."
