BFN [Text] Prishtina, June 14 (ATA) -- During the last years 48,000 pupils and 1,200 teachers have been forced to abandon their schools and homes in Kosova to escape Serb police persecutions. According to reports by the "Naim Frasheri" Albanian Teachers' League, during this period, Serbian police have beaten and maltreated more than 3,250 teachers, 300 pupils, and a considerable number of parents who have not agreed to give up Albanian school. The police have killed seven teachers and parents. "This organised terror against Albanian education continues to escalate, and recently, tens of schoolmasters and directors of education institutions have been arrested and kept in jail," ATA reporter informs from Prishtina.
