930109 FT 09 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Platon offer talks terminated Platon International, which in October last year announced it was in talks which might have led to a general offer being made for the company, yesterday revealed that those discussions had terminated. Mr Robin Meyer, chairman of the USM-quoted maker of flow, level and pressure measuring and controlling instruments, said prospects for the second half of the 1993 year remained encouraging. Platon's shares closed down 1p at 21p.
