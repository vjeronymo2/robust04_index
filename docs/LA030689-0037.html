March 6, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Jan Ottosson of Sweden, who missed last month's World Nordic Ski Championships because of illness, won the 90-kilometer Vasaloppet cross-country ski race in 5:09.33 at Mora, Sweden.

Norway's Konrad Unsgard was 2:46 behind in second place, with Sven-Erik Danielsson of Sweden in third, 3:47 back.
