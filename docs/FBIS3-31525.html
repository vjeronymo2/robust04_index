Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian foreign minister, touring the Visegrad Group of countries, has arrived in Budapest. Andrey Kozyrev will hold negotiations with his Hungarian colleague Geza Jeszenszky. The two foreign ministers will give an international news conference about their talks. The Russian foreign minister will also be received by Arpad Goncz, president of the Hungarian Republic, and Prime Minister Peter Boross.
