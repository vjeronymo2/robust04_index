930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Pakistani soldier treated by UN troops at US army field hospital One of five Pakistani soldiers handed over to UN troops yesterday by Somalis loyal to Gen Mohammed Farah Aideed being treated for shrapnel wounds in his eye at a US army field hospital. The soldiers had been missing since gunmen shot dead at least 23 Pakistani UN troops in weekend ambushes. At least two gunmen died yesterday in a heavy exchange of fire with the Pakistanis.
