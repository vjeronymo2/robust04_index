Language: English Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Anatoliy Kuznetsov for TASS] [Text] Nikolayev March 2 TASS -- It is impossible to finish the aircraft carrier Varyag according to its original design, but it may be converted into a floating airfield, Igor Ovdiyenko, director of the Black Sea shipyards, said. This would agree with Russia's plans and tactical and strategical goals, he added. Ukraine cannot afford financing the construction of the ship alone. Meanwhile, the Varyag is blocking the embankment, creating problems and incurring new expenses. The project has already cost the budget a pretty penny and it would be absurd to scrap the ship. Shipyard officials are now in Kiev to discuss the future of the ship.
