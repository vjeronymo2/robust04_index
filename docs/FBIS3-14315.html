Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Security sources in Marj 'Uyun have confirmed that Israel brought in around 500 troops backed by 150 vehicles and three 155-mm artillery batteries in the past few hours and deployed them in the Hasbayya area in the east to the Bint Jubayl area in the west opposite the Lebanese Army and resistance positions. A state of maximum alert was declared all along the international borders with Lebanon since the early hours of dawn today.
