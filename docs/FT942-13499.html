940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Countdown to tighter controls May 3-13 1994. Professor Stephen Littlechild meets the chief executives of the electricity company to discuss the regulations. May/June. Negotiation between Offer and the companies. July. Offer is expected to publish its final proposals after the preliminary results of the electricity companies' appear. August. Companies will have 21 to 28 days to accept or reject the Offer proposals. Rejection of the price control could lead to a reference to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission. April 1 1995. New price controls take effect.
