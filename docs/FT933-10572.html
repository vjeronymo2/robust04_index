930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met improved and widespread demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Bright East Africans gained 2p to 4p, sometimes more with quality. Mediums were a little irregular but generally fully firm; plainer teas were also firm. Brighter Ceylons were fully firm to dearer, but plainer sorts closed easier. Offshore: good demand at firm to dearer rates. Quotations: quality 155p, nom good medium 128p, medium 110p and low medium 90p. The highest price realised this week was 164p for a Rwanda pf. 1.
