Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] UNIAN has already reported that the automatic emergency safety device disconnected the second turbogenerator of the first generating set at Chernobyl Atomic Electric Station. The causes of the activation of the automatic device are being investigated. The first generating set, however, continues to work at the 450-MW load. The station's third generating set is only working at 50 percent of its capacity due to the suspension of nuclear fuel deliveries from Russia.
