Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- Russian lower house speaker Ivan Rybkin will have five deputies, one of them will be first deputy, decided the lower house at its session on Monday [17 January]. Russia's Choice faction nominated Mikhail Mityukov for the post of first deputy speaker. Mityukov headed the committee on legislation for a long time at the disbanded Russian parliament. Later in the day, the lower chamber approved the temporary regulations of the lower house council to be set up for "the preliminary discussion of organisational decisions on the procedure of the chamber's activities". The council includes speaker as well as leaders of parliamentary factions and groups. The speaker informs legislators of all decisions by the council at the nearest session of the chamber. The chamber may abrogate any decision taken by the council which meets every week. Any member of the lower chamber has the right to attend the council's meetings.
