930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Mexican press bribery claimed Mexico's attorney general's office said yesterday drug traffickers were buying and bribing their way into provincial newspapers in an attempt to influence coverage of drug and police matters, Reuter reports from Mexico City. The statement said some journalists and law enforcement officials were taking bribes from traffickers to provide leaks and articles. The attorney general's office leads the fight against drug trafficking in Mexico. It named no individual journalists.
