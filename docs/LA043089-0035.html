April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LUCY'S EULOGY

As sad as we are about Lucille Ball's passing, think how wonderful it is to go as she did, after having heard her own eulogy.

The week's outpouring of affection to her was something most of us would love to have.

Shortly before the end, she was up and joking. Exit laughing.

C. L. STONE

Los Angeles
