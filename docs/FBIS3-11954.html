Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Several dozen Haitian personalities and artists have been invited by the president-in-exile to commemorate in Washington this year the seventh anniversary of the vote approving the Haitian Constitution. The ceremony will take place on 29 March, that is tomorrow, on the premises of the Haitian Embassy in Washington with the participation of painters, authors, and singers living in the diaspora. It should be pointed out that the Constitution will also be honored at the United Nations this week. The week of 29 March to 6 April has been declared Haitian week at the United Nations.
