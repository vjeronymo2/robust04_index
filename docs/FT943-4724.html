940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cementone in sharp fall Following its warning in May, first half pre-tax profits at Cementone, the paint and building chemical manufacturer, fell from Pounds 552,000 to Pounds 24,000. Turnover for the six months to June 30 was also down, from Pounds 8.65m to Pounds 8.41m. An interim of 0.4p was declared. Earnings per share came out at 0.11p. The share price was unchanged at 62p.
