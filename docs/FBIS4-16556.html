BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow, 15 Apr -- This evening at the President Hotel at the end of the meeting of CIS countries' leaders, Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk signed an agreement on a stage-by-stage settlement of the problem of the Black Sea Fleet. The document says the Ukrainian Navy and the Russian Black Sea Fleet will be based separately. Ukraine is to have 15-20 percent of the Black Sea Fleet ships and vessels. An agreement on the parameters for this division is to be drawn up within 10 days. Relevant ministries and departments of the two countries have been given instructions to prepare a full-scale treaty of friendship and cooperation between Russia and Ukraine. At the end of the meeting Leonid Kravchuk invited Boris Yeltsin to pay a state visit to Ukraine in the near future. The invitation was accepted.
