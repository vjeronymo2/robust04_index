Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Honorary Consulate Opened in Brno"] [Text] Brno -- Differing views in Austria and the Czech Republic over energy policy dominated the opening of the Austrian honorary consulate in Brno on Friday [25 February]. Lower Austrian Governor Erwin Proell stated that the impression might be created that differences between the two states are widening in view of the current discussion. However, the establishment of the representation shows that " we attach significance to cooperation and goodneighborly relations."
