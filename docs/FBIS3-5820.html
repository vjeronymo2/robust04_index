Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 11 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to the medical group of the South Hamgyong provincial people's hospital which had produced a new cardiotonic injection with domestic raw materials and technology. The new cardiotonic injection is made from raw materials abundant in Korea. Its cardiotonic effect is better than the old one. They have already produced a large amount of cardiotonic injection and used them for the protection of lives and promotion of health of the working people.
