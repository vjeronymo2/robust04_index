November 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. POLICIES IN CENTRAL AMERICA AND TURMOIL IN NICARAGUA

After reading Pat Buchanan's piece on President Bush ("We'll All Soon Know What Bush Is Made Of," Op-Ed Page, Nov. 5), my immediate response is: Doesn't Buchanan know the difference between a fascist and a Marxist? How can a Marxist-Leninist like Daniel Ortega also be a fascist? That is like Ronald Reagan advocating total government intervention for all problems.

HOWARD COHEN, Sepulveda
