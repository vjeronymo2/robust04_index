Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] Prosperity Party leader Necmettin Erbakan has said that developments following the lifting of the immunity for certain deputies are contrary to the laws. Addressing an open air meeting organized by his party in Kirikkale, Erbakan said the developments are a result of the government's bad administration. He said: [Begin Kirikkale recording] A deputy has the right to appeal to the Constitutional Court regarding his immunity. A deputy might appeal and the court might revoke the decision. If the court endorses the decision, then it is issued in the OFFICIAL GAZETTE. Only then can the decision on lifting the immunity be implemented. These are the requisites of a state of law. [end recording] [passage omitted]
