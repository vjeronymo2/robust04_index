BFN [All figures as heard] [Text] Due to 95 million tenge of debts for electricity, subunits of the ore mining giant Sokolovsko-Sarbayskiy production amalgamation came to a standstill. In its turn, the amalgamation has not been paid by the Karaganda metallurgical combine -- 160 million tenge, and the Magnitogorsk combine -- 24 billion rubles. In Kostanay Oblast, for this or other reasons, enterprises such as the Dzhetygarinskiy asbestos combine, the Avtodiesel works, the largest textile company Kotex, and enterprises of the construction industry also came to a standstill.
