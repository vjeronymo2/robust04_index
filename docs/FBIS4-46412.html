BFN [Text] Gunmen from the subversive organization Hizballah kidnapped six Lebanese and a retired Syrian soldier in the Ba'labakk area last night. It has been learned that members of the so-called Emergency Battalion [katibat al-tawari'] of Hizballah kidnapped three of them from their homes after mistreating them. They kidnapped the other four in public places. Lebanese and Syrian security agencies are holding contacts with Hizballah to seek the release of the those kidnapped.
