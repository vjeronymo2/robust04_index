February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Late Final Edition

ITALIAN WINS SPAGHETTI CONTEST HANDS DOWN

Ever tried eating spaghetti with your hands tied behind your back?

Luigi Ferrari gobbled 14 ounces that way in 36 seconds, breaking by three seconds the previous record in an annual competition in this northern Italian town.

Ferrari, a 24-year-old agricultural technician, had been reluctant to take part because he had already eaten a meal, organizers of the competition said Monday.
