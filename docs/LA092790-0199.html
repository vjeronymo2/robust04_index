September 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

MAN FOUND SHOT DEAD AT PLACE OF BUSINESS

A Cypress man was found shot to death at his business Wednesday morning by employees arriving for work, police said.

The body of Jess Michael Jewett, 36, was discovered in an upstairs room at the business, A-J Sheet Metal Inc., 1845 S. Lewis St., about 8:20 a.m., Police Lt. Marc Hedgpeth said. Hedgpeth said Jewett received "several gunshots to the upper body," but he would not elaborate.

Police said they have no suspects.
