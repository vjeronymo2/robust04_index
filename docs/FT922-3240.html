920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Hawtin improves 5% to Pounds 726,000 Pre-tax profits at Hawtin, the Cardiff-based leisure and fitness group which also has interests in textiles and property, showed a 5 per cent advance to Pounds 726,000 in the six months to March 31. This compares with a restated Pounds 692,000 last time and was struck on turnover up by Pounds 869,000 to Pounds 13.9m. Earnings per share came out at 0.69p (0.66p) Mr Phillip Dovey, managing director, said the result had been achieved in the face of extreme trading conditions with the leisure and fitness division continuing to generate the largest part of the group's profits.
