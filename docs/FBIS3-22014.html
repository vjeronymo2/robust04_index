Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Colonel Faustino Rico Toro was arrested by the Special Antinarcotics Force [FELCN] in Cochabamba last night. Rico Toro was one of former dictator Luis Garcia Meza's close assistants. But he was not tried with the former dictator. He was appointed FELCN commander during Jaime Paz Zamora's government, although he had to resign a few hours later after observations made by the U.S. Embassy, which had suspicions of Rico Toro's links to drug traffickers. His arrest occurred between 2100 and 2300 last night. He is being detained at a FELCN jail in Cochabamba. There is evidence against him. He is being questioned, FELCN Deputy Commander General Rogelio Vargas said. Vargas added that he did not know whether Rico Toro will be transferred to La Paz. This is something that must be decided by prosecutors, he said. In another 48 hours we will know if the Cochabamba prosecutor's office will bring charges of drug trafficking against Faustino Rico Toro. If so, he will spend a few years in prison.
