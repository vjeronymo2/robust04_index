940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): Royal Albert Hall The Proms 100th season reaches its climax this week with the start of an impressive line-up of visiting orchestras. On Thursday and Friday a long queue of promenaders can be expected to see Claudio Abbado conducting the Berlin Philharmonic in Mahler's Ninth Symphony and a Russian showpiece programme. Over the weekend they are followed by the Cleveland Orchestra under Christoph von Dohnanyi, the first of this year's three prestigious American orchestras.
