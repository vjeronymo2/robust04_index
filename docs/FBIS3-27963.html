Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Rafael Caldera Rodriguez upon his assumption of office as president of the Republic of Venezuela. President Kim Il-song in his message expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries of Korea and Venezuela would continue to develop in the idea of independence, peace and friendship and wished him great success in his work for the prosperity of the country.
