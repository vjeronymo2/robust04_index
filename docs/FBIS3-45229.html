Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Asmara, January 13 (XINHUA) -- Eritrean President Isayas Afewerki held friendly talks here today with visiting Chinese Vice- Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen . During the talks, Afewerki said Qian's visit is an indication of the importance China attaches to Eritrea and to the friendly relations between the two countries. The Eritrean Government pursues the policy of enhancing cooperation with China in various fields, the president stressed, saying the cooperation is in the interest of the Eritrean people and helps promote peace and development of Africa. Eritrea is now facing the task of full-scale reconstruction after 30 years of war and it expects assistance from China, the president added. Qian conveyed to Afewerki the greetings of Chinese President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng. He told Afewerki that Jiang expects him to visit China at an early date so that the bilateral relations be pushed to a higher level. The Chinese vice-premier said the trade and economic cooperation between the two countries has had a good start following the establishment of diplomatic ties months ago. It is in the interest of the two peoples that the Chinese Government and Eritrean Government work together to enhance mutual exchanges and bilateral cooperation, Qian stressed. Afewerki briefed Qian on the situation in the horn of Africa. He said maintenance of the regional peace, together with development of economy and elimination of poverty and backwardness, are the major tasks facing Eritrea. The two leaders agreed that the development of economy is the basis of solutions to many problems in the African continent.
