Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hague, March 23 (AFP) -- Yugoslavia filed a complaint against NATO with the International Court of Justice [ICJ], accusing its member states of violating the UN charter by threatening to use force in Bosnia, a source at the court said Wednesday [23 March]. The rump Yugoslavia -- Serbia and Montenegro -- said NATO had used the threat of force without the required authorization of the UN Security Council. Yugoslavia's suit cited the decision taken by the North Atlantic Treaty Organization at a Feburary 9 meeting in Brussels that warned Bosnian Serbs to withdraw heavy guns from Sarajevo or face NATO air strikes.
