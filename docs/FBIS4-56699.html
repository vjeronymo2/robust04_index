BFN [Text] Montevideo, 6 May (EFE) -- Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu announced today that Uruguay is opposed to all unilateral actions in Haiti and will promote a rapid OAS intervention to prevent a deterioration of the situation in the Caribbean island. "Uruguay is a staunch defender of the principle of non-intervention and the self-determination of nations," said Abreu during a news conference with international correspondents in Montevideo. He stressed his country's concern over an eventual unilateral U.S. intervention to resolve the crisis in Haiti.
