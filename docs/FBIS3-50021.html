Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 19/1/1994 -- "Above all, this visit has demonstrated Romania's interest in expanding the relations with China. Second, it is important in the field of direct economic relations, of exchanges", stated State Minister Mircea Cosea, president of the Council on Economic Coordination, Strategy and Reform, on his return on January 19 from a visit to China. While in China, the Romanian Government delegation was received by the vice-president of the republic, the president of the Committee on Reform, and the administrations of provinces in southern China. "We discussed about the reform, got to know the big economic achievements, the fantastic dynamics of the southern zones of China and have established the coordinates of relations of collaboration not only in the domain of reform but also as regards the revigoration of economic exchanges" Cosea added. Expressing satisfaction at the results of the visit, he also assessed it has demonstrated both parties' wish "to further the traditional Romanian-Chinese relations also in the new conditions the two countries are achieving now in the economic and social life."
