Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres will meet with PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat in Oslo. The meeting will take place after the funeral of Norwegian Foreign Minister Johan Jorgen Holst on Saturday [22 January]. U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher is also slated to attend the funeral. The meeting was arranged in a conversation held in recent days between Peres and 'Arafat's adviser, Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, and in contacts between Foreign Ministry Director General Uri Savir and PLO official Abu-al-'Ala'. Unless tangible developments occur before their meeting, Peres and 'Arafat will attempt to advance the Taba talks.
