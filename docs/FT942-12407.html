940429 FT 29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Unilever buys out Japanese partner Unilever, through its National Starch and Chemical Company offshoot, has bought out Kanebo, its partner in Kanebo-NSC, a joint venture based in Osaka, Japan, for an undisclosed sum. The company makes adhesives, resins and speciality chemicals and reported sales of Y17bn (Pounds 105m) in 1993.
