BFN [Text] Baghdad, 16 Apr (INA) -- The Ministry of Justice has urged organizations and bodies connected with international law and the defense of human rights and freedoms to work toward lifting the unjust embargo against Iraq. In cables to these organizations, the Ministry of Justice said Iraq has fulfilled its obligations under Section C of Security Council Resolution 687. The Security Council should therefore implement Paragraph 22 of this resolution and lift the embargo imposed on the Iraqi people. The ministry noted that the continuation of the embargo is a blatant violation of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the Geneva Conventions.
