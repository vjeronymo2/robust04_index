940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, landed improved, and more general demand. Better Assams were well supported at firm rates, while plainer types were irregular but mainly steady. Brightest and good medium East Africans met keen competition and gained 5 to 10p. Mediums also gained 2 to 4p but plainer Africans were slightly easier. Brighter Ceylons were often 6 to 8p dearer with plainer sorts steady. Offshore good demand for brighter teas which were dearer but plainer Africans were neglected. Quotations: quality 170p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 109p/kg, low medium 88p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 194 for a Rwanda pf. 1.
