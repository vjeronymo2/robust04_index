Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yichang, January 8 (XINHUA) -- Building of a gigantic highway bridge for the Three Gorges Project started here today. The Letianxi Bridge will link the site of the main dam of the Three Gorges Project to a 28.6-kilometer-long special express highway to Yichang city, in central China's Hubei Province. The bridge, a key route from the Three Gorges to the outside world, will be 438.8 meters long, with the biggest span 125 meters long. The bridge, being built by the No. 3 Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, is expected to be completed within 26 months.
