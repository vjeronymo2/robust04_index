BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 1 (SNARK) -- The Interparliament Assembly of the CIS can assist in search of the ways of peaceful settlement of Karabakh problem, Medetkan Sherimkulov, the Chairman of the Kyrghiz Supreme Soviet, said at the meeting of the Interparliament Assembly's delegation of the CIS with deputies of the Armenian parliament, in Yerevan, today. Journalists didn't succeed to get more information about the meeting because they were pushed out from a session hall where the meeting took place. The delegation with Special Representative of Russian President Vladimir Kazimirov and Acting Secretary of Inter-parliamentary Assembly Ivan Korotchenia met with Levon Ter-Petrosyan today. Tomorrow the delegation is going to leave for Stepanakert.
