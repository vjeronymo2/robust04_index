930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Rheinmetall Berlin signals lower profits RHEINMETALL BERLIN, the engineering and armament group, said group pre-tax profit in 1992 would be significantly below the DM159.7m (Dollars 96m) in 1991 due to price falls, the cost of restructuring and lower financial earnings, Reuter reports from Berlin. In an interim report to shareholders, Rheinmetall also said 1992 profits at Rheinmetall Berlin - as a management holding - were comparable with the previous year due to the time delay in dividend payments.
