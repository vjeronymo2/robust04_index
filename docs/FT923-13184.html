920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Heiton suffers 16% decline to IPounds 1.48m Heiton Holdings, the Dublin-based steel stockholder, builders' merchant and retailing group, reported a 16 per cent decline in profits for the year to April 30. The dividend is again 2p, the final being 1.35p. Earnings per share slipped to 3.24p (3.57p). Pre-tax profit fell from IPounds 1.77m to IPounds 1.48m (Pounds 1.39m). After extraordinary charges Reflecting scaling down of activities in the UK, turnover dropped from IPounds 62.1m to IPounds 59.6m. of IPounds 1.39m (IPounds 1.06m), there was a net loss of IPounds 458,000 (IPounds 31,000). This year's charges included IPounds 1.05m in respect of Jubilata, the related company investment, by reference to its underlying net tangible assets.
