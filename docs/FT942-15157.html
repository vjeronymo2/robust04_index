940416 FT 16 APR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity Futures and Options Trading Stock index futures closed firmly ahead as hopes of a reduction in UK interest rates returned to the market, writes Joel Kibazo. The June contract on the FT-SE 100 closed at 3,187 after reasonable turnover of 15,792 lots. Thursday's close was 3,133 after volume of 14,698 contracts. However, it was a poor day in the FT-SE mid-250 with only 16 lots dealt as it closed at 3,825. Turnover in the traded options was 44,669 contracts of which 20,172 was in the FT-SE 100 option and Lasmo was the busiest stock option with a total of 2,833 lots.
