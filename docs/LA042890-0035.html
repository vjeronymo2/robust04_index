April 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

IN THE CATBIRD SEAT, NOT THE CUCKOO'S NEST

In response to Gary Fenton's letter inquiring why Jack Nicholson needs binoculars at Lakers even though he sits in the front row, he needs them to get an enlarged view of the Laker Girls. I'm surprised he doesn't bring a telescope.

TOM GERLING

Phelan
