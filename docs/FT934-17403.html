931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / Schade UK to hire 100 extra workers SCHADE UK, a wholly owned subsidiary of the Dusseldorf-based group, is to hire 100 extra workers for its Midlands plant, which supplies body trims, mouldings and windows to Jaguar, Rover, Vauxhall and Ford, Tim Burt writes. The recruitment drive will take the total workforce to 280 at the company's Castle Bromwich plant near Birmingham. Schade UK said the additional workers would join the production line in a new 30,000 sq ft extension to the plant, which is dedicated to making components for Jaguar. Mr David Cowburn, managing director of Schade UK, said: 'This represents a Pounds 4m investment and will increase our production capacity by 80 per cent.'
