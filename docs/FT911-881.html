910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Confidence grows in leasing group THE UK Equipment Leasing Association said yesterday its latest economic survey showed the first upturn in business confidence for 18 months. Half of the association's members report that business is still down in all sectors, particularly vehicles. But they expect an upturn in the next quarter. Mr Brian Hassell, association chairman, said the recent fall in interest rates was encouraging because it eased the financial burden on highly geared companies. Nevertheless, a further fall was needed.
