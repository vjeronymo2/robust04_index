BFN [Text] Prague, April 1 (CTK) -- Head of the Latvian Armed Forces Dainis Turlais was today received at the end of his visit by Czech Defense Minister Antonin Baudys, the Defense Ministry Press Department told CTK. During his three-day visit, Turlais has been negotiating cooperation between the Czech Republic and Latvia in the weapons industry. He showed most interest in the Czech army's excess equipment, and his suggestion that this equipment be offered to the Latvian Army, along with help in training will be given further consideration by the Czech Republic. Turlais was also interested in the Czech soldier's experiences from taking part in peacekeeping missions. Latvia is interested in taking part in such international military activities as part of a joint Baltic force. He passed on to Baudys an invitation from the Latvian defense minister to visit Latvia.
