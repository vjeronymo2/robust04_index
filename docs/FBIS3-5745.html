Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Wakayama, March 10 KYODO -- Police rounded up a group of 106 Chinese early Thursday [10 March] in Susami, Wakayama Prefecture, on suspicion of illegally entering Japan. The Chinese had rented about 10 cars after landing in the coastal town aboard several small boats, and were apprehended while making their way to Osaka and Nagoya, police said. Police suspect there is a syndicate organization which plotted the entry attempt. In late November, a group of nearly 100 Chinese sneaked into the country through a tiny port some 10 kilometers northwest of Susami.
