930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UN envoy restarts Haiti peace effort THE United Nations special envoy to Haiti, Mr Dante Caputo, yesterday resumed his attempt to set up negotiations on restoring democracy to the island after the September 1991 military coup, Reuter reports from Port-au-Prince. Mr Caputo's visit follows that of Mr Lawrence Pezzullo, the Clinton administration's special adviser on Haiti. Mr Pezzullo told the military and the de facto civilian government the US was determined to restore democracy swiftly. In prior visits, Mr Caputo has stressed that his first task to persuade all the parties to agree to the principle of negotiations and to accept the Organisation of American States-UN joint human rights monitoring mission.
