CSO [Article: "Two Men Jailed for Drug Traffic"] [Text] A 41-year-old Pakistani and a 27-year-old Dane were taken into custody yesterday after a closed preliminary examination in Copenhagen's Magistrate Court. They were jailed for 13 days on two separate cases, charged with drug trafficking. The Pakistani had 25 grams on his person when he was arrested, while the police found 26 grams of heroin and 1.5 kilos of hashish in the Dane's apartment in St. Kongens Street.
