BFN [Excerpt] We are satisfied with our working visit to Turkey. The visit was fruitful and promotes beneficial economic cooperation between the two states. This was said by Rahimjon Ghafurov, first deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan, to a correspondent of the KHOVAR news agency. Rahimjon Ghafurov was at the head of the country's delegation that recently visited Turkey. Foreign Economic Relations Minister Izatullo Hayoyev, Finance Minister Normat Yunusov; Sobit Nematulloyev, president of the republican Academy of Sciences; Izatullo Lalbekov, head of the Foreign Economic Bank, and others were among the members of the delegation. The Tajik delegation was received by Suleyman Demirel, president of the Republic of Turkey. A useful, constructive conversation took place on issues related to further developing economic relations and to Turkey rendering technical assistance to Tajikistan. [passage omitted]
