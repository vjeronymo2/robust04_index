November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

STATE ARCHITECT COUNCILS TO ASSESS QUAKE DAMAGE

Chester A. Widom, president of the California Council of the American Institute of Architects and a founding partner of Widom Wein Cohen, Santa Monica, has announced the formation of a task force made up of six CCAIA chapters to assist the state architect with the seismic evaluation of the structures damaged by the Bay Area earthquake.
