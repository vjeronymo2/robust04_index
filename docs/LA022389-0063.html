February 23, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP

In another baseball game:

Cal Lutheran 10, La Verne 8 -- Dan Weiss was three for four and hit two home runs as the Kingsmen weathered a late eight-run rally to win their third consecutive nonconference game at Cal Lutheran.

Southern California College 5, The Master's 3 -- Jeff Cole and Peter Wilson each homered for The Master's (1-9-1, 1-3) in the NAIA District 3 game at Southern California.

SOFTBALL

Moorpark 5, Pasadena 0 -- Sophomore De Dow notched her third complete game and second consecutive shutout as the Raiders won a nonconference game at Moorpark.

Dow (3-0) struck out six and pitched a three-hitter.

CS San Bernardino 9-8, Occidental 1-1 -- The Tigers (0-3) were limited to a combined seven hits in a nonconference doubleheader at San Bernardino.
