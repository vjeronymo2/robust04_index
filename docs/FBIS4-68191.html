BFN [Announcer-read report] [Excerpts] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has said that the fight against the separatist terrorist organization PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] is continuing successfully and added that there is a 300 percent increase in the number terrorists who surrendered in the first four months of 1994 in comparison to the same period last year. [passage omitted] Mentese stated that 500 terrorists -- 186 terrorists killed, four wounded -- were captured in operations conducted against the dissipating terrorist organization during 1-15 May. Mentese added that 197 long-barreled rifles, 37 revolvers, 386 bombs, and large quantities of ammunition were seized during these operations. Explaining that collective surrenders, including members in high- level positions, were taking place for the first time, Mentese stressed that during this period 48 terrorists -- 22 in Sirnak and 7 in Agri and Van [as heard] -- surrendered to the security forces, handing in their weapons. Mentese said that collective surrenders from the terrorist organization's militant cadres in the mountainous region are expected to increase. [passage omitted]
