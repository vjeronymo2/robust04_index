940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / Italy's Premier Berlusconi pledge's new role in Europe Silvio Berlusconi, Italy's new prime minister, has pledged that his government will expand the country's role in Europe and seek to widen its influence in international affairs. Mr Berlusconi, pictured in parliament yesterday, was outlining his government's plans to the senate before tomorrow's vote of confidence Report, Page 3
