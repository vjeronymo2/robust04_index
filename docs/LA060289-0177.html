June 2, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

3RD MOTORCYCLIST DIES ON ISLE OF MAN

A motorcycle rider was killed in practice on Thursday on the Isle of Man Tourist Trophy circuit -- the third to die on the treacherous course this week.

Organizers said Phil Hogg, 23, came off his 250-cubic-centimeter Yamaha when he was practicing for competition later in the day.

Tuesday a rider and a sidecar-team member were killed in separate crashes during practices. The 37-mile circuit on the Irish Sea island has claimed 149 lives since racing began there in 1907.
