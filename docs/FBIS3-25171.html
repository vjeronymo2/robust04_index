Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ghana Government has decided to review its participation in ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] operations in Liberia if no progress is made to ensure the smooth implementation of peace process within one month. A statement from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs says the government, after a thorough review of the Liberian situation, have expressed disappointment at the lack of progress toward the implementation of the Cotonou Peace Accord which sought to bring peace in the war-torn west African country.
