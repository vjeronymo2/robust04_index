BFN [Text] "This Italian government will be the most pro-Israeli in the past 20 years". These were the words with which Foreign Minister Antonio Martino who is currently visiting Washington, sought to reassure the Jewish-American community. At the next G-7 meeting Martino will ask for condemnation of "the ongoing Arab economic boycott of Israel." Martino met earlier with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, who said "he has a lot of faith in the government that resulted from the elections: we are ready to work together."
