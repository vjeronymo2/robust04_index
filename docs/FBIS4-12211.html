BFN [Text] Baghdad, 5 Apr (INA)--Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz today received a delegation from the Jordanian House of Representatives headed by Deputy House Speaker 'Abd Musa al-Nahar. The deputy prime minister explained in detail the human suffering and the [word indistinct] effect of the unfair embargo, which certain tyrannical and domineering parties at the UN Security Council seek to perpetuate, despite the fact that Iraq has fulfilled its commitments, which obliges the UN Security Council to lift the embargo clamped on Iraq in fulfillment of its reciprocal obligations. Tariq 'Aziz commended Jordan's courageous stands in support of the Iraqi people, and urged parliamentarians as well as all organizations and well-intentioned people in the world to redouble their efforts to lift the unfair embargo clamped on the Iraqi people. For his part, the deputy speaker of the Jordanian House of Representatives reiterated the Jordanian people's support for and solidarity with the fraternal Iraqis. He commended the Iraqis' steadfastness and firmness in their confrontation of the aggression and in their handling of the unfair embargo.
