June 22, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

PERFORMING ARTS CENTER APPOINTS LAWYER JAMES MACDONALD TO BOARD

James B. MacDonald, a partner in the Costa Mesa law firm of Speers Dana Teal & Balfour, has been named to the Orange County Performing Arts Center's board of directors.

MacDonald, also a partner in Mission Investments real estate developers, has been chairman of the Center's Endowment Council since 1987. He fills a vacancy on the 53-member board. His term will expire in 1993.
