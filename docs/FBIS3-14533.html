Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas this evening received in Aden, the economic and commercial capital, His Excellency brother Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, minister of state for foreign affairs in the fraternal Sultanate of Oman, and his accompanying delegation. The Omani participants in the Joint Military Committee and some of the Yemeni members of the committee attended. They discussed issues of cooperation and relations between the two sisterly countries and ways to enhance and promote them. The meeting also touched on the work of the Joint Military Committee and the results achieved in the recent phase. Brother al-'Attas expressed the Yemeni leadership's great appreciation for the committee's efforts and for the results of its work. He stressed the need to continue the committee's work under the current political circumstances. Al-'Attas also expressed the political leadership's readiness to cooperate with the committee in order to overcome the difficulties that are bound to obstruct all tasks assigned to the committee.
