Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Coast Guard chief of staff, Prefect General Jorge Humberto Maggi, has joined the Argentine Naval Coast Guard top brass after the promotions to the top-ranking posts for officers of the institution were approved by the Executive Branch. The new command is made up as follows: - Deputy chief of staff: Prefect General Jorge Arnoldo Gantiluomo; - Intelligence chief: Prefect General Carlos Justo Leyes; - Navigation safety police director: Prefect General Fortunato Cesar Benasulin; - Zonal Coast Guard chief: Prefect General Juan Marcos Redon; - Security and law enforcement police chief: Prefect General Raul Celear Dimarco; - Secretary general: Prefect General Juan Carlos Rinaldi; - Administration director: Prefect General Ruben Roberto Astiasaran.
