Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Secret Launch"] [Text] The disputed Eurofigther 2000 is finally taking off. The official date for its first flight is 15 April. As FOCUS has learned from a certain source, however, the multinational combat jet is to take off from the Manching factory of Deutsche Aerospace (Dasa) as early as the middle of this week without any public attendance. Instead of the planned 250 planes, the Air Force will get only about 120.
