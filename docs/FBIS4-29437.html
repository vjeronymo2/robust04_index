BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs and chairman of the National Commission for Environmental Affairs, this afternoon left Yangon [Rangoon] by air to attend the ministerial level environmental conference of the Asia and Pacific Region, Eco Asia '94, to be held in (Saitama) in Japan from 21 to 23 June, at the invitation of Miss Toshiko Hamayotsu, director general of the Environmental Agency, minister responsible for the world environmental problems, and minister of the Government Office. The Myanmar delegation was seen off at the Yangon airport by ministers, responsible personnel, and family members. The members of the Myanmar delegation also includes U Thein Han, Myanmar ambassador to Japan, and Charge d'Affaires U Kyaw Tint Shwe.
