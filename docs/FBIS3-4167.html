Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, March 26 (XINHUA) -- President Kim Yong-sam of the Republic of Korea (ROK), as guest of Chinese President Jiang Zemin, arrived here this afternoon by special plane from Tokyo, Japan, starting his five-day official visit to China. This is Kim's first visit to China since he assumed the ROK presidency. Wu Jichuan, chairman of the Chinese Government reception committee and minister of posts and telecommunications, made a special trip to Shanghai on behalf of President Jiang to greet President Kim and his party. This evening, Shanghai Mayor Huang Ju met with Kim and his party and hosted a banquet in their honor. During the meeting, Kim said that he is glad that Shanghai, China's largest economic center, is the first leg of his China visit. Kim said that with the establishment of diplomatic ties between the ROK and China more than one years ago, bilateral cooperation in various fields has been further enhanced, adding that bilateral trade volume last year was over 10 billion U.S. dollars, which is predicated to be further increased this year. The ROK president expressed his belief that Shanghai will play a bigger role in its economic exchanges with the ROK. This afternoon, right after their arrival here, Kim and his party visited the site of the former provisional government of the ROK.
