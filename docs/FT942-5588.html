940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Wyndeham Press advances to Pounds 1.76m Wyndeham Press Group, the printing and packaging company, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1.02m to Pounds 1.76m for the year to end-March. Turnover, boosted by Pounds 2.95m from acquisitions, was up from Pounds 10.6m to Pounds 13.5m. Earnings per share emerged at 7.1p (4.6p) and a final dividend of 1.5p is proposed, making 2.25p (1.25p) for the year.
