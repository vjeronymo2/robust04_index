April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DEMOCRATIC PRIMARY RACE

For 10 years I have watched in frustration as the Republicans (for the most part) smeared the Democrats with negative campaign advertisements.

Now it appears that the Democrats are going to employ the same disgusting tactics on each other. How delightful it is to face another election in which none of the candidates are worthy of my vote.

One would hope that citizens of good conscience would refrain from voting for a candidate who employs such ads. Unfortunately, the wicked are usually elected anyway.

PAUL E. STEVENSON

Playa del Rey
