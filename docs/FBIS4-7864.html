BFN [Text] Szczecin, 6 Apr--Boguslaw Liberadzki, the minister of transport and maritime economy, who took part on 6 April in a meeting of the Szczecin Maritime Lobby, has announced the introduction in our country of a free register of ships sailing under various flags. In practice, this means the birth of a Polish flag of convenience, similar to that of Panama or Liberia, under which shipowners from various countries will be able to register their vessels. There are many who are interested in "changing the flag" of their ships.
