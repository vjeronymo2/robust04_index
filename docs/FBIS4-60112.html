BFN [Text] The Palestinian police force is not in control of the situation in the Gaza Strip and has even ceased trying to handle traffic. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan has learned from Arab sources that armed and unmasked Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] members have been roaming around the Gaza Strip undisturbed since yesterday. Nabil Sha'th, head of the PLO delegation, arrived in the strip from Egypt last night for a 24-hour visit. He is holding talks with Palestinian police commanders and others on the functioning of the police force in the autonomy, as well as on different matters.
