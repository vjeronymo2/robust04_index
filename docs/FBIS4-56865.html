BFN [Text] With the approval of two-fifths of Congress, 52 legislators from the National [Blanco] Party, the Radical Battleist Ticket No. 15 [Battlismo Radical Lista 15], and the Colorado and Battleist Union (UCB) will promote in early May a constitutional reform project that will be subjected to a plebiscite along with the national elections scheduled for November. Congressional sources told EL OBSERVADOR ECONOMICO that this proposal also is allegedly approved by legislators of the Party of the Government by the People, who disapprove of the agreement with the Battleist Forum. The main proposal reportedly approves multiparty votes [voto cruzado], involving the election of a presidential candidate from a party and a mayor from another party.
