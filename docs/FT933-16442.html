930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / UK Company News: McInerney Props losses lower McInerney Properties, the Dublin-based construction company, reduced pre-tax losses from IPounds 11.85m to IPounds 2.65m (Pounds 2.6m) in 1992 after exceptional charges of IPounds 516,000 against IPounds 5.34m. Depending on the outcome of current discussions with certain non-bank creditors, the company said the figures for 1992 'could be materially altered'. Turnover was lower at IPounds 32.9m (IPounds 34.1m). Losses per share were 6.2p (21.6p).
