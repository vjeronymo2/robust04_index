920626 FT 26 JUN 92 / Havel calls for quick decision CZECHOSLOVAK President Vaclav Havel urged his country yesterday to make a quick decision on whether to break up the federation, saying all citizens would suffer if political paralysis continued, Reuter reports from Prague. 'Every single day of postponement only increases the unfortunate affects of uncertainty - moral, economic, international and legal,' he told MPs. 'The death agony of the common state, its gradual disintegration or a wild break-up would only turn against all our citizens.'
