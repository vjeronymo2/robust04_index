BFN [Text] Brother President Yasir 'Arafat had today a telephone conversation with the Mr. Godal, the Norwegian foreign minister. The conversation dealt with the latest development in the Palestinian-Israeli peace process and the means for bolstering it by starting the rebuilding process as soon as possible. They also talked about the International Coordination Committee of the donor-countries and the need to fulfill its pledges by giving monies to the Palestinian authority in order that the latter would meet its development and rebuilding pledges in the self rule areas.
