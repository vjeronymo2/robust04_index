Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak has received a report from Minister of Interior Hasan al-Alfi on the proceedings of the Arab interior ministers' meetings held in Tunis recently. After a meeting with President Mubarak today, al-Alfi said the main topic of discussion at the conference was security cooperation to prevent crime and combat narcotics, forgery, and incidents of violence and terrorism. Meetings were also held on the sidelines of the conference with most of the Arab interior ministers to discuss various issues, such as the exchange of visits and training. Asked if Egypt is holding contacts with friendly countries to arrest fugitive terrorists abroad, he said: God willing, there are measures that will be taken. On the disappearance of Libyan former Foreign Minister Mansur al-Kikhya and the situation with the multimillionaire of Madinat al-Nasr, Fawzi al-Sayyid, he said that investigations are proceeding to unravel the mystery surrounding the disappearance of the former Libyan foreign minister. The interior minister announced that Fawzi al-Sayyid was arrested in Cairo Governorate at dawn today.
