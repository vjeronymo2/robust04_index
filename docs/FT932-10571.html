930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Labour shows 17-point poll lead LABOUR leads the Conservatives by 17 points, according to a Gallup poll in today's Daily Telegraph. Its lead has narrowed slightly from a month ago. The findings, with last month's figures in brackets, were: Labour 47 per cent (49), Conservatives 30 per cent (30.5), Lib Dems 17.5 per cent (15.5).
