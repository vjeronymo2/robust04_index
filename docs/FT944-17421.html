941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / Disqualification of auditor sought Mr Andrew Magill, the district auditor who in January provisionally recommended that councillors and officers of Westminster City Council should repay Pounds 21.5m after 'gerrymandering' the council's housing policy, yesterday heard legal representations that he should disqualify himself from the case. Mr Anthony Scrivener, the barrister representing Lady Shirley Porter, who was leader of the council when the alleged offences occurred, said Mr Magill had already made his views public, although he had not heard submissions from the respondent.
