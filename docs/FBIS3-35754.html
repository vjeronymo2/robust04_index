Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 31 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- Boris Yeltsin signed a decree today "On extending the state of emergency in the territory of Mozdokskiy Rayon, Prigorodnyy Rayon, and adjacent areas of the Republic of North Ossetia, as well as in Malgobekskiy and Nazranovskiy Rayons of the Ingush Republic". The Russian president's press service says the decree was signed in view of the fact that tensions persist in some areas of the Republic of North Ossetia and the Ingush Republic because of the unsatisfactory implementation of the Russian president's decree of 13 December 1993 "On the procedure for a return to the places of permanent residence of refugees and displaced persons on the territory of the Republic of North Ossetia and the Ingush Republic," in view of the continuing sabotage and exchanges of fire, and also with the aim of ensuring state and public security -- in accordance with the RSFSR law "On state of emergency" and the Russian Federation law "On security."
