940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / Observer: OK Okie A tip for society hostesses preparing for the arrival in London of Admiral William Crowe, the new ambassador to the Court of St James. President Bill Clinton's recent problems mean that Arkansas jokes are in poor taste, but jokes from the neighbouring state of Oklahoma are perfectly acceptable - provided they are told by the admiral, a native of that state. Here's one of Crowe's favourites. A widow attending her husband's funeral is taken aback by the enthusiastic eulogy the preacher is delivering. She turns to her son and says: 'Just take a look in the casket and see if that really is Daddy in there.'
