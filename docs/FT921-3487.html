920313 FT 13 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands climbs to pounds 33,000 at midway Ex-Lands, the investment and leisure group, increased its profit, before and after tax, from Pounds 19,000 to Pounds 33,000 in the six months to December 31, after costs incurred on potential acquisitions of Pounds 64,000 against Pounds 26,000. Turnover was Pounds 10,000 down at Pounds 714,000. Earnings per share for the period amounted to 0.05p (0.04p).
