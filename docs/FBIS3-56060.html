Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Kiev -- U.S. President Bill Clinton arrived in the Ukrainian capital on a visit lasting several hours on 12 January. [passage omitted] During the negotiations between the U.S. and Ukrainian presidents, activists from the Popular Movement of Ukraine [Rukh] and the Union of Ukrainian Students were holding a demonstration with slogans in their hands. In particular, among them were the following: "Clinton, we warn you: first Crimea then Alaska," "disarmament threatens Europe," "No to the new world divide," "All rockets to Zhirinovskiy?," "Bill, would you disarm if you were in the same cage as the Russian bear?"
