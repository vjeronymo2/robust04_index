Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Supervision and Control Commission, CSC, has not reached agreement on the salaries to be paid to the soldiers in the future Mozambique Defense Armed Forces, FADM. Labor Minister Teodato Hunguana has said that the problem must be viewed objectively. He added that solutions must be found that are in tandem with the country's realities. At the CSC meeting yesterday, the Mozambique National Resistance, Renamo, proposed that the number of FADM troops be reduced to 20,000 men so that better salaries can be paid to them.
