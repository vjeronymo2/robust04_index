Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 10 (XINHUA) -- Liu Shuqing, president of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, met here this evening with visiting chairman of the Select Committee on Indian Affairs of the U.S. Senate, Daniel K. Akaka, and his wife. Since his arrival in China last Friday [7 January], the senator has visited the Chinese cities of Shenzhen and Haikou. Liu also hosted a dinner in honor of the senator and his party.
