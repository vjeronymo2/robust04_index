BFN [Text] Prime Minister Ibrahim Boubacar Keita has returned home from Gabon, where he has just completed a five-day working visit. This was the first visit abroad by the head of the government who, in addition to discussions with the Gabonese authorities, met with the Malian community there. Mr. Keita, who returned home yesterday afternoon, stated, while taking stock of his visit, that the behavior of the Malian community in Gabon honors us. However, he added, the emigration of Malians to that country poses a number of problems; there is a need to reconsider our emigration policy.
