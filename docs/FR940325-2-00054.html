OMB Number: 3060&hyph;0478 Title: Informational Tariffs Action: Extension of a currently approved collection Respondents: Businesses or other for-profit (including small businesses) Frequency of Response: On occasion reporting requirement Estimated Annual Burden: 330 responses; 50 hours average burden per response; 16,500 hours total annual burden Needs and Uses: Providers of interstate operator services are directed by Section 226(h)(1)(A) of the Communications Act, 47 U.S.C. Section 226(h)(1)(A), to file informational tariffs with the Commission and to update these tariffs regularly. The informational tariffs will be maintained for public inspection. The Common Carrier Bureau, at the direction of Congress, will also use the informational tariffs in assessing the compliance of the rates charged by operator service providers (OSPs) with the requirements of the Communications Act.
