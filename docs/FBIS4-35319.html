BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz has announced that Foreign Ministry Secretary General Zafir al-Hasan will call the major powers' ambassadors in Lebanon tomorrow. He will discuss Lebanon's request for a Security Council meeting to debate the Lebanese complaint against Israel in the abduction of Lebanese citizen Mustafa al-Dirani. He said Lebanon expects the United States to use its veto power, but the Security Council must live up to its responsibilities. He expressed his belief that the United Nations could restore its credibility by taking appropriate action. [passage omitted] Answering journalists' questions, Foreign Minister Buwayz said: [Begin Buwayz recording] Of course the wavering in dealing with Israel by the Security Council, the United Nations, and several influential and major powers is no longer a secret. We are at a stage where numerous Israeli aggressions are overlooked. Hence, we believe that the United Nations should regain its credibility by taking an appropriate decision on this issue. [end recording]
