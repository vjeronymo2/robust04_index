December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

David and Maddie Get a New Life: "Moonlighting" can light up viewers' lives once again now that the series has been acquired by the Lifetime cable network. The series, which made a star out of Bruce Willis and re-established Cybill Shepherd's career, will begin with the pilot episode on Feb. 5 and be seen Monday through Thursday at 8 p.m. and Saturdays at 9 p.m. CLAUDIA PUIG
