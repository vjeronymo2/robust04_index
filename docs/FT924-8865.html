921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / Observer: Lateral Interviewing patients at a psychiatric nursing home, a researcher asked the first what was 3 times 3. 'Friday,' came the reply. Sighing, the interviewer called the next one and inquired what was 5 times 5. This time the answer was '133'. When the third one responded to 4 times 4 with '16', the researcher brightened up. 'How did you work it out?' he asked. 'Easy' said the patient. 'I just took Friday away from 133.'
