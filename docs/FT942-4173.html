940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / Lisbon 'fastest growing city' Lisbon is set to be the fastest-growing European city in the next four years, closely followed by Dublin, Hamburg, Athens and Lille, according to new UK economic study. The report, by Cambridge Econometrics, predicts Stuttgart will see the slowest rate of economic growth. Stuttgart's economy is predicted to expand by only 1.1 per cent a year, while Lisbon is expected to grow by 3.6 per cent a year. London will grow 2.3 per cent a year, with Paris' growth rate of 1.8 per cent. European Regional Prospects; Cambridge Econometrics, Covent Garden, Cambridge, UK. Ecu100
