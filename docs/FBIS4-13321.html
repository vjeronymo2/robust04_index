BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] had a meeting today in Moscow with Russian Interior Minister Viktor Yerin at the Interior Ministry and briefed him on Palestinian preparations concerning the issue of setting up a Palestinian police and security service. The Russian minister showed his country's preparedness to offer all forms of support, aid, and assistance for the building of the structures of a Palestinian national security service and the setting up of a Palestinian-Russian joint committee for cooperation in national security and police.
