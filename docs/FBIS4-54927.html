BFN [Excerpts] Katowice, 6 May--Adam Jawor, spokesman for the Independent Self-Governing Trade Union [NSZZ] Solidarity Mining and Power Secretariat, said that the 5 May warning strike in some railway stations in the Silesian State Railway District ended last night. According to the State Hard-Coal Agency, 9,828 miners in 19 mines are continuing to strike during the morning shift on 6 May. [passage omitted] Jawor said that on 6 May the strike continued in 22 hard-coal mines and in three zinc and lead mines that form the Boleslaw Mining and Metallurgical Enterprise in Bukowno near Olkusz.
