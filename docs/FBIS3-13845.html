Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz conferred with ambassadors of the nonaligned nations at the UN Security Council. After the session, 'Aziz told journalists he laid out to the envoys Iraq's position on sanctions. The nonaligned membership of the coucil comprises Oman, Djibouti, Pakistan, Nigeria, and Rwanda. The deputy prime minister had earlier received at his residence Permanment Representative Li Xhaoxing, the PRC ambassador at the Security Council.
