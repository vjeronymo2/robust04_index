Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro, minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, today in Cienfuegos attended the event marking the beginning of this year's defense training in the central territory. On behalf of the commander in chief, the Central Committee, the government, and on his own behalf, Raul Castro thanked the people for what they have done, and continue to do, in the defense of the fatherland. Manuel Menendez Castellanos, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Cienfuegos, summarized today's events. He said that the central territory surpassed its tunnel digging plan by 105 percent. During the event, the five most outstanding municipal general staffs in the area received awards. These municipalities are: Jovellanos, Matanzas Province; Camajuani, Villa Clara Province; Cumanayagua, Cienfuegos Province; Yaguajay, Sancti Spiritus Province; and Majagua, Ciego de Avila Province. During his visit to Cienfuegos, Army Gen. Raul Castro said that the result of the work done represents tons of sweat of millions of combatants, militiamen, and members of the production and defense brigades. In statements to Radio Reloj, Raul said that despite the situation confronted by the country, the men and women who make up the Army are aware that the sole goal of their effort is to prepare for war and to avoid war. Raul Castro described today's event, which was held in an area near the Jaime Gonzalez Airport, as one filled with emotion, patriotic, profound, and revolutionary. Following the event, Raul attended a meeting at the Camilo Cienfuegos Military School, where the participants discussed the results of the Central Army's 1993 defense training and the guidelines for 1994. [Havana Radio Rebelde in Spanish at 1800 GMT on 14 January carries a similar report, but adds that Division General Joaquin Sola, chief of the Central Army, also attended the event.]
