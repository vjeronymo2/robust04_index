BFN [Text] Ukraine intends to officially submit a memorandum on foreign trade regulations to GATT at the end of June. This was announced to the press on Saturday by the Ukrainian Minister for Foreign Economic Relations Oleg Slepichev. On Friday a government conference on Ukraine's membership in GATT ended in Kiev. It was attended by a GATT delegation led by the head of the commission on Ukraine's admission Andrew Stoller. According to Slepichev, among other things participants pointed to the need to harmonize the foreign economic legislation of Ukraine with GATT norms. The need was stressed to change laws regulating investments and taxes. Slepichev said Ukraine's foreign partners are alarmed by constant alterations of Ukrainian legislation. Meanwhile, Stoller said that after the conference the American government intends to offer Ukraine technical assistance in harmonizing its legislation.
