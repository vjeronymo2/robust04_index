May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Richie Adubato, coach of the Dallas Mavericks, thinks an NBA referee has a "personal vendetta" against guard Derek Harper.

"It seems like Hue Hollins has given Derek a technical in every game he officiates," said Adubato, after Harper was charged with a technical Saturday night during Dallas' 114-107 playoff loss at Portland.
