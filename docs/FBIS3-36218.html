Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Nguyen Manh Cam, minister of foreign affairs of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Tashkent on an official three-day visit. Today will see talks with his Uzbek colleague and the signing of a protocol on holding consultations between the foreign policy departments of the two states. There will also be a business meeting at the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations. The guest will visit a number of Tashkent enterprises. Tomorrow, the head of the Vietnamese Foreign Ministry will be received by President Islam Karimov. It is the first visit to Uzbekistan by a government delegation from Vietnam.
