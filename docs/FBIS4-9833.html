BFN [Report by Andres de Leon -- live] [Text] Thousands of our Emisoras Unidas listeners have been calling to report the presence of military personnel along the streets and avenues of the capital. We must inform you this is part of the crime prevention operation to provide security for the Guatemalans traveling into the city from various points around the countryside. Police, the Treasury Guard, and the Guatemalan Army are guarding access routes. No abnormal activity has been reported; everything is under control. Military personnel have been assigned to assist the police, and the Treasury Guard is checking those coming in from the countryside or leaving Guatemala City via one of the main access routes. We have also learned military units are controlling the main entrance routes, specifically Puente de Belize, Incienso, and El Trebol, as well as key electricity high-tension pylons in the capital city, as prevention measures against terrorism. We hereby inform Guatemalans there is nothing abnormal going on in the country. The military personnel are assisting with security for those moving in an out of the city.
