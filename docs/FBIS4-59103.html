BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, received in his office today the German ambassador to our country. They reviewed many fields of cooperation and issues of interest to both friendly countries, as well as the ongoing developments in our country. During the meeting, the brother chairman lauded the level of bilateral cooperation and bilateral relations. He also voiced appreciation for the generous German aid to the development march in our country, and stressed our country's eagerness to enhance bilateral relations and expand the scope of cooperation in the interest of both friendly peoples.
