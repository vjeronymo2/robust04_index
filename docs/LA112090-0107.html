November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

YOU KNOW IT'S FAKE: Milli Vanilli has 'fessed up to singing not a note of its top-selling album "Girl You Know It's True" and is returning its Grammy award (A1). . . . "Good for them," says Jon St. James, La Habra-based producer-manager of hit pop artists Stacey Q and Bardeux. . . . "It was an embarrassment that they won it in the first place. Everyone pretty much knew what they were doing. . . . I don't see how they had the nerve to do it." Jim Newton and Jerry Hicks
