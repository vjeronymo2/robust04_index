Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 18 Feb -- Boris Yeltsin today offered the State Duma the building of the former USSR Gosplan [State Planning Committee] in the center of Moscow not far from Red Square. In accordance with a edict signed by the president, the head of state's press service reports, buildings in Okhotnyy Ryad and Georgiyevskiy Pereulok have been assigned to the Duma as its official residence. While repair work is going on in these premises, the Duma will be temporarily accommodated in the building occupied by the Russian Academy of Administration in Prospekt Vernadskogo.
