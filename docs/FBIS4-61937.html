BFN [Text] The leader of the Republican Movement of Crimea, Serhiy Tsekov, was elected chairman of the Supreme Council of Crimea at the Crimean parliament session which opened today in Simferopol. He is a staunch supporter of Crimea's statehood and its integration in the CIS. The Commonwealth of Independent States itself, Serhiy Tsekov believes, must be transformed into a federation or a confederation.
