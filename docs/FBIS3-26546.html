Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, February 9 (XINHUA) -- Xie Fei, secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, has called on Yang Shangkun, former Chinese president, and Yao Yilin, former vice premier, in Zhuhai and Guangzhou respectively shortly before the Spring Festival, which falls on tomorrow. Xie, also a member of the Political Bureau of the party Central Committee, extended festival greetings to the two retired leaders, wishing them good health and longevity. Also, he briefed them on the province' work in the past year in implementing the spirit of the party's 14th national congress and the third plenary session of the 14th party Central Committee. Yang and Yao encouraged local officials to work hard in the new year and make new contributions to the country's reform, opening-up and modernization drive.
