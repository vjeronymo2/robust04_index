BFN [Report by Anatoliy Ladin under the "News" rubric: "Kazakhstan's Stance Is Clear"] [Text] "We are and will be present in Tajikistan legally and as a matter of fact," said Tulegen Zhukeyev, Republic of Kazakhstan deputy prime minister, in a press conference that took place in Alma-Ata [Almaty]. He also said that no decisions to pull out Kazakhstan's military contingent from Tajikistan had been made.
