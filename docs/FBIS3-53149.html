Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Mr. Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah, speaker of the Transitional National Assembly, has declared that peace is near, pointing out the necessity of making preparations for it and following it through. This came in the enlightenment which his excellency presented at the assembly's session today about a parliamentary delegation's visit to the Upper Nile State. Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah explained that the brothers, who were bearing arms, reached the firm conviction of the inevitability of the halting of the war and saw that the war should come to an end. He said he met a number of the leaderships of the united factions of the rebel movement in the Fangak area, who confirmed their decision to stop the fighting and formulate peace and lead the march of construction and development.
