920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / International Company News: UMW improves UMW, the Malaysian motor and heavy equipment group, yesterday reported a 5 per cent rise in 1991 pre-tax profits to MDollars 208m (USDollars 79.4m), but warned that the current year's car sales were likely to suffer, writes Lim Siong Hoon. Turnover rose by 6.5 per cent to MDollars 2.1bn from MDollars 1.9bn, and operating profit was nearly 5 per cent higher at MDollars 205m. A final dividend of 7.5 cents a share is recommended.
