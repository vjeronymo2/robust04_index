940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / International Company News: Poseidon Gold acquisition delayed Poseidon Gold, part of Mr Robert de Crespigny's Normandy Poseidon group, said its planned purchase of a 40 per cent interest in the Boddington Gold Mine from Reynolds Metals had been delayed by court action brought by Newcrest Mining, writes Nikki Tait. The deal - which involves payment of USDollars 116m, plus delivery of 30,000 ounces of gold over seven years - had been due to close on Tuesday. A new completion date will be set when court proceedings end.
