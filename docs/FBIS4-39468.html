BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz June 7 TASS -- "The Caucasus should be viewed as a single whole, local processes influence the public-political situation in the Russian south," Russian Minister for Nationalities and Regional Policy Nikolay Yegorov told a meeting here today. The meeting was attended by North Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov, North Ossetian Government and Presidium members, head of temporary administration Vladimir Lozovoy and representatives of federal ministries currently in the state of emergency zone to discuss the implementation of presidential decrees and governmental instructions on the return of refugees and forced migrants to places of their permanent residence. In the opinion of Yegorov, developments in the conflict zone influence the whole North-Caucasian region, and the refugee problem should be solved with due account of this situation. He stressed that the North Ossetian-Ingush relations should be settled only peacefully and without the use of force. Yegorov, Lozovoy and members of the North Ossetian Government visited the state of emergency zone this morning to meet local residents. The minister has left Vladikavkaz.
