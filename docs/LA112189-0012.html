November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Series Debut for Mitchum: Veteran film actor Robert Mitchum will make his TV series debut as star of a "lighthearted" family comedy next spring on NBC, the network announced Monday. The show has a working title of "Regular Joe" and casts Mitchum as a crusty homeless man who agrees to pose as the grandfather of four orphans so they can stay together in their own home. Arnold Margolin is the creator and writer. CLAUDIA PUIG
