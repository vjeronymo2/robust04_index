920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Nesco Investments edges up to Pounds 28,000 Nesco Investments, the computer systems supplier, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 22,000 to Pounds 28,000 for the half year to December 31 1991. However, after tax of Pounds 16,000 and minorities of Pounds 12,000 the company just broke even. Last time there were losses per share of 0.7p. Turnover rose marginally to Pounds 2.87m (Pounds 2.79m).
