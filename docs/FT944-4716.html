941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Observer: Acidic Uncharitable suggestion from derivatives buffs yesterday. Californian taxpayers living in the area south of Los Angeles and who are staring at Dollars 1.5bn plus of losses on the region's investment portfolio as a result of dealings by one Robert Citron - the treasurer who quit at the beginning of the week - might care to rename their domicile Lemon County.
