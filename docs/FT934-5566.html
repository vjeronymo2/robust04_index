931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Survey of Software at Work: Supplier/Product Profile Product: InSite was originally launched in 1987, version 3 released in April 1992, and the latest, version 4, in September 1993. It is specifically designed for marketing analysis of demographic data. InSite and the information it contains is customised for each individual client and the installation includes loading the user's own data. Suppliers: CACI Information Services was founded in the US in 1962 and in the UK in 1975. It is part of CACI International Inc based in Arlington, Virginia. Turnover: Pounds 11,033,000 with Pounds 653,281 profit in 1993 and Pounds 8,660,000 with Pounds 225,245 profit in 1992. Number of users: 70 in the UK. Price: Typical entry level is Pounds 24,000 plus VAT, but there is no standard price because of customisation, including geographically precise marketing data. Hardware and operating systems: IBM PCs under PC DOS. Sales and support/maintenance: Direct from CACI, although some business partners now offer InSite as part of analysis services. Annual lease includes maintenance and helpline support. Other services available: Training, installation, geocoding prior to installation and customisation of reporting algorithms and formats. Consultancy and bureau projects, including modelling markets, creating new data sources, and integration with other systems. User views: Pros: User friendly, flexible, rich in analysis features and models, proprietary datasets on retail expenditure, population forecasts, etc, excellent support. Cons: Conservative estimates of drive-times over distances. Contact: John Rae, GIS Director, CACI Information Services, tel 071 602 6000
