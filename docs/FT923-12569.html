920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Butte passes its pref dividend BUTTE MINING is to pass the preference dividend due at the end of July. The company said payment 'would not at the present time be prudent' in light of circumstances surrounding its litigation against more than 70 former executives and advisers, and a slower than expected start up to its Australian gold mining operations. However, these operations were now progressing well, the company said. The Serious Fraud Office has launched an investigation into Butte and Richmond Oil & Gas, following a raid on an accountant's office in Jersey. Butte said yesterday it welcomed the inquiry into 'matters which are the subject of the company's law suit'.
