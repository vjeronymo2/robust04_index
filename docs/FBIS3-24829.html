Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] A total of 256 government and 101 Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] soldiers turned up at their assembly areas yesterday. According to the daily UN Operations in Mozambique report, 20,589 soldiers from both sides had been confined to assembly areas by yesterday. Of those, 13,190 are government soldiers, and 7,292 are Renamo soldiers. A total of 18,207 government and Renamo soldiers had already been registered by yesterday.
