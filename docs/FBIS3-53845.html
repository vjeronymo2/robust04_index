Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Official discussions between Sudanese and Iranian officials were concluded in Khartoum yesterday. The two sides signed a joint communique which affirmed the depth of relations between Khartoum and Tehran. The communique included a number of new agreements in the spheres of investment, trade, economic cooperation and infrastructure. Dr. Awad Ahmad al-Jaz, minister of presidential affairs, signed on behalf of the Sudanese side, while Gholam Reza Foruzesh, minister of construction jihad in his country, signed on behalf of the Iranian side.
