920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / Report urges end to 'pay gap' THE PAY gap between senior civil servants and comparable private sector white collar workers is no longer justifiable, according to a union-commissioned report by Coopers & Lybrand Deloitte, the consultancy firm. Senior civil servants have traditionally received less pay than private sector counterparts because their jobs are more secure. The report says there is ample evidence of a higher turbulence and job changing in the private sector.
