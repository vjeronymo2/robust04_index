921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Decline seen in company failures THE NUMBER of companies passing into receivership or administration showed a year-on-year decline last month for the eighth consecutive month, according to figures compiled by accountants Touche Ross from the London and Edinburgh Gazettes, Andrew Jack writes. There were 379 orders in September this year compared with 327 in August and 399 in September last year. The total so far this year is 3,898 compared with 4,327 for the first nine months of last year.
