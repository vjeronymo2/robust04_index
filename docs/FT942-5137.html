940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Arts (Openings): Sadler's Wells The Washington Ballet comes to London for a first visit. Two programmes will show works by Choo-San Goh (the leading choreographer to the troupe until his death), Niels Christe, et al. In Paris, the Opera Ballet at the Palais Garnier offers a triple, bill every evening: vaut le voyage for the glorious dancing in Lander's Etudes and Robbins' In the Night. In New York, New York City Ballet is in mid-and-marvellous season at the State Theater.
