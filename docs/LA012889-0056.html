January 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TITLE MAKER

I must clarify a point concerning Lawrence Christon's Jan. 17 review of the Jerry Seinfeld performance at the Bren Events Center, UC Irvine. Seinfeld did not bill himself as "America's most imitated comedian" or "the King of Observational Comedy." I created those titles as a promotional tool.

I apologize to Mr. Seinfeld.

LANCE MACLEAN

Programs Director

Associated Students

UC Irvine
