BFN [By KHOVAR news agency correspondent Igor Akhmedov for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe May 31 TASS -- A group of Tajik opposition militants who tried to penetrate into Tajikistan from Afghanistan was destroyed by artillery fire opened by Russian border guards of the Moskovskiy detachment in Tajikistan. This was the 17th attempt to cross the border in May. The strength of the group which was heading in the direction of Shuroabad is unknown.
