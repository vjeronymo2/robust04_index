January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

THE STATE

Fontana stonemason Vincent Ronald Monacco said he plans to buy some Italian suits, travel to the French Riviera and buy a new BMW -- but he isn't sure whether he will continue laying bricks. Monacco, 39, won $5.8 million Saturday, sharing the $40.7-million Lotto jackpot pool with six others. Each will collect $232,000 a year, after taxes, for 20 years. Monacco said he will continue to live in Fontana, where he was born. "Fontana is my home," he said.
