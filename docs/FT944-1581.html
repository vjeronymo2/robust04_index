941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Japan plans spending cuts Japan's finance ministry proposed a national budget that would cut spending for the first time for 40 years. Finance minister Massayoshi Takemura unveiled a fiscal 1995 draft budget totalling Y70.99 trillion (Pounds 450bn), down from the Y73.08 trillion for the current year.
