BFN [Text] Baku, 18 May (AZERTAC)--"The agreement, main principles of which was discussed at the meeting held by the defence ministers of Russia, Azerbaijan and Armenia in Moscow on the 16th of May, on cease-fire and withdrawal of the belligerent's troops has not been signed just because of some unfinished items of this document," the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry stated today. The parties' experts are continuing their work on the document. According to the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry, tripartite meeting of the three countries defence ministers will be held in Moscow as soon as the experts have finished their work.
