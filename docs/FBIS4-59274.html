BFN [Text] The Jordanian-Palestinian Economic Committee resumed its meetings at the Prime Ministry today. The Jordanian side was led by Dr. Jawad al-'Anani, minister of state for cabinet affairs and minister of information. The Palestinian side was led by Ahmad Quray', head of the PLO Economic Department. The committee began its meetings yesterday. The agenda includes discussing many economic issues, such as banks, currency, trade exchanges, customs, and other issues.
