920427 FT 27 APR 92 / UK Company News: Ramco Oil halved to Pounds 526,000 Ramco Oil Services, USM-quoted provider of tubes for the oil industry, reported halved profits in 1991 on turnover down from Pounds 4.78m to Pounds 4.11m. Pre-tax profits were Pounds 526,000 (Pounds 1.13m) with the fall at the operating level from Pounds 1.2m to Pounds 343,000. Earnings were down from 3.96p to 2.39p but the dividend is held at 2p per share. Mr Stephen Kemp, chairman and chief executive, said prospects were good.
