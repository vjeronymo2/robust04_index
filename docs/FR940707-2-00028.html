Minority Business Development Agency Business Development Center Applications: Indianapolis, IN agency: Minority Business Development Agency, Commerce. action: Cancellation of notice.

summary: The Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) is canceling the announcement to solicit competitive applications under its Minority Business Development Center program to operate a Indianapolis, Indiana MBDA for a three (3) year period, starting September 1, 1994, to August 31, 1995, in the Indianapolis, Indiana MSA (Closing date May 2, 1994). Refer to the Federal Register dated March 23, 1994. Dated: June 30, 1994. David Vega, Regional Director, Chicago Regional Office.
