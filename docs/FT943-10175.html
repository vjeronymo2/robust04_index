940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Dealers in UK derivatives focused on the favourable industrial production figures, brushing aside the higher than expected US employment data, writes Joel Kibazo. In futures, The September contract on the FT-SE 100 moved sharply ahead to close at 3,189, up 31 on its previous finish and at a strong premium to cash. Volume improved to reach 13,127 lots. The contract touched a high of 3,192 and a low of 3,147. There was also an improvement in turnover in traded options. The day's total was 28,400, of which 5,804 was dealt in the FT-SE 100.
