BFN [Text] Tirana, April 22 (ATA) -- A delegation from the Great Britain, made up of representatives of the Foreign Office, the Major cabinet, and the British Embassy in Belgrade was not allowed by Belgrade authorities to visit Kosova, says a report of the Union of the Independent Trade Unions of Kosova, which had planned a meeting in Pristina with the delegation. The deputy chairman of the union, Aziz Abrashi, said that the delegation would be informed of the situation in Kosova and the discrimination towards Albanian workers. "We shall find the ways so that to give the delegation the information they were interested in," Mr. Abrashi said.
