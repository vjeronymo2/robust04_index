October 15, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

YADIMICHI'S 10 GOALS HELP HARVARD TO TITLE

Hassan Yadimichi scored 10 goals in two matches Saturday as Harvard High won the consolation championship of the Sunny Hills water polo tournament.

After an eight-goal performance in a 19-3 victory over University High of San Diego in the semifinals, Yadimichi scored twice as the Saracens knocked off Mission Viejo, 8-7, in the final.

Shane Loomis led Harvard with three goals against Mission Viejo. The Saracens built a 6-2 halftime lead and were never headed. Goalie Antonio Gomez had 13 saves.
