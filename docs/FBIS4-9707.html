BFN [Excerpts] On 29 March, the Coordinating Committee for National Mobilization presented the text of a resolution opposing Aristide to parliament. This resolution was handed over to the office of the lower chamber and to the unofficial office of the Upper Chamber. Hubert De Ronceray [leader of the Mobilization for National Development], Gabriel Augustin [leader of the Assembly of Democrats for the Republic], Rony Durand, and Herve Leveille [of the Group of Citizens of the Greater North, CCGN] are the principal members of this delegation demanding that parliamentarians either reactivate Article 149 or form an ad hoc government. A member of the delegation told Signal FM that the office of the lower chamber has promised to give a ruling on this resolution soon. Rony Durand demands a Haitian solution to the crisis. The delegation and the office of the lower chamber met in camera. [passage omitted] Herve Leveille of CCGN expects a positive response to their request from the parliamentarians. [passage omitted]
