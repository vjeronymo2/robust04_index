July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENT PAPERBACKS: THE BUS OF DREAMS BY MARY MORRIS (PENGUIN: $7.95)

A collection of competent but uninvolving stories reprinted from various magazines.

Mary Morris can put words together in ways that suggest a character or set a mood, but these brief vignettes involving alcoholic fathers, quarreling families and unhappy lovers lack a distinguishing voice. The stories seem vaguely familiar, as if the reader had skimmed them in the dentist's waiting room a few weeks earlier. If such a thing as generic fiction exists, Morris has pioneered it.
