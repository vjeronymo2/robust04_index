BFN [Text] Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan continued his meetings with political officials. He met with Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri and Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri; their talks dealt with bilateral relations. Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz, who accompanied his Armenian counterpart to the Chamber of Deputies, said the decision to open a Lebanese embassy in Armenia was adopted by the government several months ago. He added that the final decision to name a Lebanese ambassador to Armenia is awaiting several administrative and fiscal arrangements. Minister Papazyan left Beirut for Armenia at 1830 today. Before leaving Beirut Airport, he said that his visit was very good and that ties between the two countries will have legal [word indistinct]. He said discussions dealt with the possibility of operating a direct air route between Lebanon and Armenia.
