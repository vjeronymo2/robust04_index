941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Dunedin Ent net assets rise The net asset value per share of the Dunedin Enterprise Investment Trust increased by 6.7 per cent from 180p at April 30 to 192p at the October 31 interim stage. The improvement, which takes account of the Pounds 5.5m warrant issue, compared with an 8.9 per cent fall in the benchmark FT-SE SmallCap ex Inv Trusts Index. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 388,829 (Pounds 516,248). Earnings came out at 195p (277p) per share and the interim dividend is again 1.5p.
