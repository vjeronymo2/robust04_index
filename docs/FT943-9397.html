940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Ward Holdings Ward Holdings, the housebuilder and property group, returned to the black with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.08m for the six months to April 30. The outcome compared with profits of Pounds 347,000, and was struck on turnover down by almost Pounds 2m to Pounds 17.7m. Earnings per share emerged at 3.8p (0.7p) and the interim dividend is 0.5p (nil).
