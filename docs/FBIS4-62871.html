BFN [Text] Yerevan, 18 May (SNARK)--The Armenian boundary territories at the village of Yeraskhavan (in the south of Armenia) were shelled this night from Nakhichevan. The press-center of the Armenian Defence Ministry told SNARK the fire from mortars and submachine guns lasted for five hours. There are victims among Armenian border-guards. The Armenian side had to take counter measures.
