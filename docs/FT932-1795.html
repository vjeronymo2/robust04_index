930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Polar Polar, the designer and distributor of electronic components, increased pre-tax profit from Pounds 451,000 to Pounds 490,000 in the six months ended April 2. Turnover amounted to Pounds 9.57m against Pounds 8.29m. Interest charges came to Pounds 43,000 (Pounds 25,000). Earnings per share were 4.1p (3.7p) and the interim dividend is again 2p.
