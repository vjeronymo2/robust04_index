BFN [Text] Progress has been achieved in the talks being held in Washington between Turkey and the United States in connection with the Karkuk-Yumurtalik oil pipeline. Foreign Ministry Under Secretary Ozdem Sanberk, who is holding contacts in Washington in connection with the pipeline, has said that the U.S. Administration has quite a constructive attitude regarding the matter. He said the U.S. Administration told him that the United States will display an attitude in line with Turkey's requests at the United Nations. Sanberk stressed that Turkey will act while remaining loyal to the UN sanctions imposed on Iraq. Encouraging developments are taking place, he said, indicating that the oil in the Karkuk-Yumurtalik pipeline will be pumped out this year. After Washington, Sanberk will go to New York to discuss with the permanent representatives at the UN Security Council the need to pump out the oil in the pipeline for technical reasons.
