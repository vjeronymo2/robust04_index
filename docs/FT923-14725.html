920706 FT 06 JUL 92 / UN flies into Mogadishu The first batch of United Nations military observers flew into the war-shattered Somali capital of Mogadishu yesterday as street fighting, which has driven most of the civilian population out of the city, subsided, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. The UN observers are there to monitor the shaky ceasefire agreed four months ago between the main warring groups.
