Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] As is known, the Supreme Council of Ukraine adopted the law "On the State Program for Encouraging Foreign Investments in Ukraine." Many citizens wish to know more about this enactment and its basic provisions. The press center of the Supreme Council of Ukraine reports that according to the law relations connected with foreign investment and the determination of its main trends are regulated by a state program that supplements the law and that is an inseparable part of it. The program outlines the priority spheres for the investment of foreign capital, the standards for the investors who claim additional privileges, the essence of these privileges, ways toward improving the legal regulation of international business, the development of its infrastructure, and the role of Ukraine's regions in encouraging foreign investments. The agro-industrial, fuel, energy, and timber industry complexes; machine-building, metallurgy, and production of materials; light, medical, chemical, oil, and chemical industries; and communications, transportation, and social infrastructures are considered to be a priority for foreign investments. In the near future the above law of Ukraine will be published in the press.
