Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Central Council of the United Democratic Party of Belarus has decided to support the ex-speaker of the Belarusian Supreme Soviet, Stanislav Shushkevich, during the presidential election campaign. It is already the second political party that has declared its readiness to support S. Shushkevich. Earlier, such a resolution was adopted by the Belarusian Social Democratic Assembly.
