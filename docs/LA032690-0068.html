March 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

IN COURT: A SUMMARY OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA-RELATED BUSINESS LITIGATION DEVELOPMENTS DURING THE PAST WEEK.

Traffic School Argues Against Change: An attorney for a traffic school that employs comedians to teach safe driving argued Thursday in Los Angeles Superior Court that a new Department of Motor Vehicles policy would harm the schools and the public.

The school, Comedians Plus Learn From Us, sued the DMV over a proposed policy to list the schools by their state license numbers rather than by name. Judge Barnet Cooperman continued the hearing to April 19. (Filed Dec. 1, 1989; Case No. C745286)
