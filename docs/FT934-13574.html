931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / GPT wins contract worth Pounds 10m GPT, the UK-based telecommunications manufacturing joint venture between GEC and Siemens, has won a contract worth about Pounds 10m to supply exchanges to Southwestern Bell for its UK cable telephone network, Andrew Adonis writes. The order is for up to 10 System X digital telephone exchanges. Southwestern Bell, the regional US operator, is building networks in seven urban areas, including Liverpool, central Lancashire, Telford and Wigan, with Cox Cable Communications. Southwestern Bell is the third largest cable TV and telephone operator in the UK, behind Nynex and Telewest. Its seven franchises cover about 1.2m homes. Most cable operators are installing their own switching equipment.
