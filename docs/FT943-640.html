940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Electricity shares South Wales Electricity has bought another 476,000 of its own shares at a cost of Pounds 3.8m. Swalec now owns 4.1m of its own shares, representing 4 per cent of the total shares in issue at March 31 1994. Meanwhile, South Western Electricity has bought a further 250,000 of its own shares for Pounds 1.88m, bringing its total to 5.5m. This represents about 4.5 per cent of the issued ordinary share capital.
