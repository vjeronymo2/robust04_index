940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: European Leisure The Pounds 2m costs of its financial restructuring completed at the beginning of this year left European Leisure with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 542,000 for the first half of 1994, against profits of Pounds 221,000. On turnover of Pounds 66.1m (Pounds 68.4m), including Pounds 406,000 (Pounds 4.41m) from discontinued activities, operating profits fell from Pounds 8.02m to Pounds 7.13m reflecting a lower contribution from Maygay, the amusement machine manufacturer. Interest costs fell to Pounds 5.67m (Pounds 7.8m). Losses per share were 0.11p, compared with 1.21p.
