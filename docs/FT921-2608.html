920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Amey drives up 27% to Pounds 2.8m Amey Holdings, the private contractor, reported a 27 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.2m to Pounds 2.8m for 1991. Turnover rose 53 per cent to Pounds 145m. The company said as a result of a marketing drive turnover in its roads side improved from Pounds 37m to Pounds 62m while building sales quadrupled to Pounds 31m. Earnings per share were 352p (311p).
