April 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: BEVERLY HILLS;

CLOTHING LINE ON DISPLAY

A line of clothing being marketed under the Beverly Hills High School name will be shown this month at the Dallas Apparel Market Week, a fashion exposition in the Southwest. The clothing line, the subject of a sales and promotional blitz nationwide, is being marketed by the Beverly Hills Unified District to raise money for teachers and educational programs. Licensing and sales of the BHHS line is being handled by 20th-Century Fox. The clothing line is being manufactured by Great Southern Co. of Macon, Ga.
