BFN [Text] As a spokesman for the Russian border troops in Tajikistan reported to INTERFAX on Tuesday, an area close to the Outpost Eight of the Pyanj Border Unit in Tajikistan was mortared from Afghanistan at 1800 on Tuesday, April 11. The enemy is reported to have been beat back by retaliatory fire. No Russian border guard was hurt. Meanwhile, the Turg height near the Outpost 12 of the Moscow Border Unit came under sniper fire from Afghanistan. Russian border guards refrained from retaliation.
