941102 FT 02 NOV 94 / Goodison supports single EU currency The government should bow to the inevitability of a single European currency, Sir Nicholas Goodison, chairman of the TSB bank, said yesterday. 'I believe it will come anyway as trade develops and producers and consumers increasingly see the nonsense of dealing in 12 or more different currencies in the conduct of their day-to-day trade,' he said in a lecture at the London School of Economics. Sir Nicholas said a much greater sense of commitment to the EU was needed. 'Parliament should not be frightened of delegating decisions upwards or downwards.'
