920206 FT 06 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Whessoe (Ireland) sold to management Whessoe, the engineering group, has sold its Whessoe (Ireland) subsidiary to Mr Martin McEvoy, it managing director, for a total consideration of IPounds 705,000 (Pounds 650,000) cash, of which IPounds 105,000 will be paid within the next five years. The sale follows the closure in 1989 of Whessoe's other heavy engineering fabrication facilities at Darlington and Middlesbrough.
