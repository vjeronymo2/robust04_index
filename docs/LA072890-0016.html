July 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BLOOM IS OFF THE ROSE, BUT MANY SAY HE STILL HAS A PLACE IN THE HALL

Pete Rose doesn't belong in the Hall of Fame. He is a liar, cheat, and most of all, a first-class jerk.

On the list of criteria to being elected to the Hall is integrity "on and off" the field. I was at a card show a few years ago when Rose started to get loud and use foul language in front of the kids until someone told him to keep it down.

He might have had a few more hits than anyone, but he played twice as long as the normal player. He lied to his fans and he lied under oath to the courts. He shouldn't be allowed to step in another ballpark, as a player, coach or fan.

GARY SCHLEPER

Lomita
