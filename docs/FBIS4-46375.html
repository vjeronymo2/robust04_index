BFN [Text] Fatah Hawks fired shots and wounded six Palestinian civilians in the Tuffah neighborhood in Gaza after they tried to detain a car thief today. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the Fatah Hawks presented themselves as members of the Palestinian preventive security force. Meanwhile, the Palestinian police forces have started to supervise building activity in Gaza. Today they demolished a house built on public ground in the al-Rumayl neighborhood.
