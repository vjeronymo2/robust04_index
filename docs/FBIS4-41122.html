BFN [Text] Over the past two years, about 15,000 officers have been discharged from the Belarusian Armed Forces. These were basically individuals entitled to pensions or those who did not wish to continue their service. At the same, the number of officers who were born in Belarus has noticeably increased. While such officers used to represent 16 percent of all personnel in the Belarusian Armed Fores, this ratio has risen to 47 percent today. It is estimated that with new graduates from the military schools joining the Armed Forces, this number will climb to 53-55 percent. In as little as one year, Belarus-born servicemen will account for 65 percent of the Belarusian Army, RID agency reported.
