940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Net asset value falls 3% at Murray Split Net asset value at Murray Split Capital Trust fell 3 per cent from Pounds 24.1m to Pounds 23.4m in the 9 months to May 31. Net revenue rose from Pounds 615,000 to Pounds 655,000, equal to earnings per share of 8.19p (7.69p). An unchanged third-quarter dividend of 2.65p is declared , and the trust said it intended to maintain the total at 10.6p.
