940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Inv Tst of Guernsey net assets at 77.3p The Investment Trust of Guernsey had a net asset value of 77.3p per share at June 30, against values of 87.2p at December 31 and 73.8p at end-June 1993. Net revenue for the six month period dipped to Pounds 902,000 (Pounds 917,000) leaving earnings per share of 1.16p (1.2p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.825p gross.
