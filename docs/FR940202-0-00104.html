INTERSTATE COMMERCE COMMISSION 49 CFR Parts 1002 and 1312 [Ex Parte No. MC&hyph;213] Range Tariffs_Fax Filing AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission (ICC). ACTION: Final Rules.

SUMMARY: The ICC is issuing final rules permitting carriers to effectuate range tariffs by filing by fax the actual rates applicable to identified shipments prior to transport of those shipments. Section 5(b) of the Negotiated Rates Act of 1993 requires that, as of June 1, 1994, range tariffs must identify the specific rate or discount (from among the range of available rates or discounts) which applies to each specific shipment, or must contain an objective means for determining that rate. Faxed rates, which are intended for use only where carriers must respond rapidly to changing market demands in the spot market, will have an effective period of only 7 days. EFFECTIVE DATE: These regulations are effective on March 4, 1994, and carriers may begin to use the fax filing process for spot market shipments on that date.
