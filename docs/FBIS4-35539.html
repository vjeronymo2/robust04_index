BFN [Text] Ambassador Nabil al-'Arabi, Egypt's permanent delegate to the United Nations, has stated that an Arab group has submitted an official application to obtain a permanent seat at the UN Security Council. In a statement made today on the sidelines of the Non-Aligned Movement Conference, al-'Arabi said that the African group has also requested two permanent seats on the council. He explained that the UN General Assembly is expected to consider the expansion of the UN Security Council membership and including other countries at its session next year.
