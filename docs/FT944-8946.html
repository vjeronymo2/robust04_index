941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: VTR expands 52% to Pounds 1.34m VTR, which provides video post production facilities, raised pre-tax profits by 52 per cent from Pounds 884,144 to Pounds 1.34m for the year to August 31. The result included a Pounds 106,851 (Pounds 119,465) profit on the sale of equipment and was struck after write-downs which increased the depreciation charge by Pounds 102,038. Earnings per share increased from 7.3p to 10.5p, while a final dividend of 2.55p makes a total of 4p (3.75p), on capital increased by May's Pounds 2.95m rights issue. Mr Philip Lovegrove, chairman, said that with all subsidiaries budgeted to be profitable, the board expects to report further growth in the current year.
