930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Investors Capital Tst Net asset value per share of Investors Capital Trust was 132.8p at the nine months ended June 30, an improvement of 18.8 per cent on the 111.8p at the September 1992 year end. A third quarterly dividend on 1.275p is declared making a total of 3.825p for the year to date.
