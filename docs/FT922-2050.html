920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / World Stock Markets: Sao Paulo shares tumble Sao Paulo shares fell another 8.9 per cent as investors sold heavily amid growing concern over new charges of corruption in the government, brokers said. The Bovespa index ended at 19,964, down 1,901. In Mexico, the stock exchange took its second worst fall ever, closing down 99.65, or 5.89 per cent, at 1,593.3 after touching 1,526.9. Heavy selling set prices on a free fall amid fears of a delay or block in the free trade agreement and continued uncertainty over the status of Telefonos de Mexico shares owned by telephone workers.
