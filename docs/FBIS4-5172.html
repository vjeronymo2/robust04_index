BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- A ten-day film show opened in Korea to celebrate the 15th of April. Showing in the period are documentary films on the history of the revolutionary activities of the great leader President Kim Il-song, including "The Leader Is the Great Father of Our People" and "The Great Leader Has Opened a Broad Avenue for Bumper Crops", and feature films, including "The Star of Korea" (parts 1-10), "The Sun of the Nation" (parts 1-5) and "The Nation and Destiny" (follow-ups). The films impressively show the course of the revolutionary struggle of the respected leader, the national pride and honor of the Korean people dynamically advancing under his leadership and the superiority of the Korean-style socialist system centred on the masses. Opening ceremonies took place in provincial, city and county seats Thursday.
