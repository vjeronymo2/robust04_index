BFN [Excerpts] The latest poll by Gallup Institute was conducted between 4 and 15 June. It was conducted exclusively for O ESTADO. It reveals that if the presidential elections were held today, there would be a runoff election. Those polled were shown the list of candidates. PT [Workers Party] candidate Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva and PSDB [Brazilian Social Democracy Party] candidate Fernando Henrique Cardoso received 37.9 and 22.8 percent, respectively, of the votes. This shows that they would have to participate in a runoff election. These percentages for the two main candidates are very similar to the ones in the previous poll. Compared to last month's poll, the popularity of Lula and Cardoso dropped by 0.4 and 0.7 percent, respectively. [passage omitted] As for the other candidates, Leonel Brizola obtained 7.6 percent, Orestes Quercia received 6.8 percent, Esperidiao Amin received 2.5 percent, Eneas Carneiro received 2.0 percent, and Flavio Rocha received 1.1 percent.
