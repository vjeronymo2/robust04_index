Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] According to initial data for 1994, Poland has fulfilled the standby agreement reached in November 1992 with the IMF. The fulfillment of this agreement is a condition for a 20-percent reduction--that is. about $8 billion in government debts to the states who are our creditors grouped in the Paris Club. The club is to make a decision on this matter at the beginning of April.
