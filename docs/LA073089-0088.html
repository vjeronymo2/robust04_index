July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Sequelmania ad nauseum: Warner's has just registered the title, "Lethal Weapon 3." Meanwhile, over at Disney, they've registered both "Honey, I Made the Kids Invisible" and "Honey, I Sent the Kids to the Moon."

. . . We heard that NBC's "A Different World" will add a new recurring character, an African exchange student, next season. The Hughes Moss Casting Agency in NYC confirmed that they're seeking an African actor for the role, preferably one living in the U.S.

. . . The S.F. Chron's Herb Caen claims that Frank Sinatra now uses a TelePrompTer in his concerts -- can't remember the lyrics!
