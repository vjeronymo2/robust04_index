920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Tabloid folds as Murdoch quits The brash Super] tabloid sold in eastern Germany was closed yesterday after Mr Rupert Murdoch decided to pull out of the one-year-old joint venture, Reuter reports from Berlin. Publisher Hubert Burda said high unemployment, a harshly competitive newspaper market and a lack of advertising revenue made it impossible to make the newspaper profitable.
