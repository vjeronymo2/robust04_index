Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Echo" newscast] [Text] The Mexican Foreign Relations Secretariat has reported in a communique that a group of unidentified persons attacked the Mexican Consulate in the U.S. city of El Paso on 3 January. According to the communique, the attack took place in the early hours of 3 January when some unidentified persons hurled a rock and a Molotov cocktail inside the Consulate. Fortunately, no injuries were reported and material damage was minimal. A similar incident took place at the same Consulate a year ago.
