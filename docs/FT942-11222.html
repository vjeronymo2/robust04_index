940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Emap purchase Emap, the media group, is to acquire FHM - a monthly men's style and general interest magazine - from Tayvale for an undisclosed sum. Mr Tom Moloney, managing director of Emap's consumer magazine division, said that FHM - which has an ABC figure of 76,541 - would enhance the company's portfolio of successful male-oriented magazines and fashion titles.
