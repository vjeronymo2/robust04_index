December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BUSH VISITS U.S. TROOPS

The picture on the front page of your Thanksgiving edition showing President Bush laughing with Saudi King Fahd was obscene. To see Bush laughing with the oil king of Saudi Arabia, when he has ordered thousands of young Americans to die in the desert, as he prepares to unleash a horrifying war that may include chemical and nuclear bombs, is disgusting. Shame!

BASIL LANGTON

Los Angeles
