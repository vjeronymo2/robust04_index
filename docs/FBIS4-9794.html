BFN [Text] Mexico City, 1 Apr (DPA) -- Three other persons suspected of participating in the assassination of Luis Donaldo Colosio, Institutional Revolutionary Party [PRI] candidate for president, have been arrested in Tijuana. According to the radio station Radio Red, three or four persons involved in Colosio's assassination were taken from Tijuana to Mexico City in deep secrecy. The radio station says that everything indicates the persons are Vicente Mayoral Valenzuela and his son Rodolfo Mayoral, security agents during Colosio's visit to Lomas Taurinas, a Tijuana neighborhood, and witnesses to his death. Another person reportedly is Rodolfo Rivapalacio, coordinator of Colosio's security in Tijuana, who hired members of the group known as Tucan for security tasks. The station went on to say that the group includes a fourth person, whom the station has not yet identified. Mario Aburto Martinez, a 23-year-old mechanic, and former policeman Tranquilino Sanchez Vanegas, 57, accused of having assisted the killer, are officially being held at the Almoloya de Juarez Maximum Security Prison. In addition, the Mexico City press today mentioned the possibility of the murder being ordered by drug traffickers, who intended to divert public attention.
