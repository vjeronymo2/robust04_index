Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A new party, the Civilized Dniester [Tsivilizovannoye Pridnestrovye], has been set up in the Dnester region. The party's main social aims are that each family in the Dniestr region should enjoy modern housing with full amenities with at least 20 square meters per person, a car, a telephone, good furniture, and television and video recorder. Creators of this party believe their action program is similar to those of President Boris Yeltsin's team, Yegor Gaydar and Grigoriy Yavlinskiy, as well as programs of democrats and reformists in Ukraine and Moldova. Its leaders stated that their party will strive for the Dnestr republic achieving a status that would guarantee conditions for civilized democratic development, irrespective of what happens in the main territory of Moldova. The party's efforts will be directed to achieving the lasting peace in the region.
