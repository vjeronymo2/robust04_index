June 28, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Comedian Dom DeLuise, who has a featured role in "Orpheus in the Underworld" at the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion, will not return for tonight's performance as originally intended. DeLuise is remaining in New York, where his mother is in critical condition. He has been out of the cast since June 17. Roderick Cook continues in the role of Public Opinion during DeLuise's absence. ALEENE MacMINN
