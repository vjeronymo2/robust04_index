Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam Monday met and had a friendly talk with the visiting Iranian deputy minister of foreign affairs, Manuchehr Mottaki. On the same day, Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, met and had a friendly conversation with the Iranian Foreign Ministry delegation led by the deputy minister at the Mansudae Assembly Hall. On the occasion the head of the delegation expressed the hope that the Korean people under the wise guidance of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il would achieve the reunification of the country at an early date. He wished the Korean people greater success in socialist construction in the three-year period of adjustment.
