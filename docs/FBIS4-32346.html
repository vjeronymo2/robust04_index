BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 22/6/1994 -- Adrian Nastase, speaker of the Chamber of Deputies, met with Valentin Nikolayev Kovalev, vice president of the State Duma of the Russian Federation, and the head of his country's delegation to the third plenary session of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Black Sea Economic Cooperation Organization, hosted by the Romanian capital. The sides agreed that there were no delicate issues between the two countries of a nature to hinder a real and wide expansion of the mutual relations, both sharing the conviction that, through their activities, the two legislative fora could best serve the mutual interests and economic exchanges between their countries. They underlined the importance of finalizing the negotiations on the framework political treaty between Romania and the Russian Federation. Adrian Nastase accepted to visit the Russian Federation next autumn.
