941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: John Swan down John Swan & Sons, the Scottish livestock auctioneer and estate agent, reported a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 233,200 to Pounds 193,300 for the six months to October 31. The 17 per cent decline came on the back of turnover down 3.5 per cent from Pounds 806,800 to Pounds 778,400. The company said the numbers of stock handled had fallen, resulting in less commission. In addition, a provision of Pounds 25,000 had been made for exceptional repairs. Earnings per share were 23.2p (26p).
