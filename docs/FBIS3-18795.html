Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- After the temporary failure of the European Union's [EU] membership negotiations with Norway, FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party) is optimistic that an agreement can still be reached. "We must not give up now but continue to negotiate," he said in Bonn on Wednesday [9 February]. The remaining problems can be overcome and can be solved. The continuing negotiations should link up with a new meeting of the European Union's Council of Ministers on 15 March.
