931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / International Company News: Chinese group to raise HKDollars 262m Shenzhen China Bicycle is launching a rights issue of 32.34m B shares to raise HKDollars 262m (USDollars 34m), Reuter reports from Hong Kong. The group is offering one rights share at HKDollars 8.10 a share for every five B shares, which are aimed at foreign investors. It also plans a rights issue of 21m domestically-held A shares to raise Yn182m (USDollars 31.2m). Net proceeds from both issues, underwritten by Standard Chartered Asia, will be used to finance business in China.
