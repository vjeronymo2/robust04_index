BFN [Text] Sanaa, June 19 (AFP) -- A northern Yemeni delegation will leave here Sunday [19 June] for Cairo to attend ceasefire talks sponsored by the United Nations, an official here said. Sanaa told UN special envoy Lakhdar Ibrahimi that it would send a delegation to Cairo "on condition the (meeting) is held in the context of the Republic of Yemen and not that of two states as the southerners want," he told AFP. The meeting will last one day, said the official who requested anonymity.
