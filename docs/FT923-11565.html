920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Turkish company wins pipeline deal Turkey has won its first major construction contract in Syria. The privately-owned Attila Dogan Company is to build a Dollars 25m (Pounds 13m) natural gas pipeline from Palmyra to the Mhardeh power plant near Homs for the AL-Furat Petroleum Company, writes John Murray Brown in Ankara.
