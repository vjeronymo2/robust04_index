September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The U.S. national soccer team will play Poland Oct. 10 in Warsaw and play a team of Croatian all-stars Oct. 17 in Zagreb, Yugoslavia.

The United States also will play Trinidad and Tobago in Port-of-Spain Nov. 18. The Americans won, 1-0, at Trinidad Nov. 19 to qualify for the 1990 World Cup.
