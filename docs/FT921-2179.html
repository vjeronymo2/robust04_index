920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Asian assets at Dollars 11.41 The Scottish Asian Investment Company, launched two years ago by Murray Johnstone, the fund management group, had a net asset value per share of Dollars 11.41 (640p) at January 31 compared with Dollars 9.35 a year earlier. Losses for the six month period worked through at 10.21 cents per share, or 8.51 cents assuming full conversion of warrants.
