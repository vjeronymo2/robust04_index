940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / Air pollution curbs on Istanbul Turkey has clamped anti-smog restrictions on Istanbul, the country's biggest city, after air pollution exceeded World Health Organisation danger levels by nearly seven times, Reuter reports from Istanbul. Istanbul governor's office at the weekend placed home heating curbs on the city's 10m people. Hospitals, schools, youth hostels and police stations were told to turn down the temperature.
