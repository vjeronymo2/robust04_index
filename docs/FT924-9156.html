921107 FT 07 NOV 92 / Institute suspends accountant MR PETER RAWLINGS, an accountant from Colwyn Bay, north Wales, has been suspended from membership of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales for one year and fined Pounds 2,000 and Pounds 500 costs. He had admitted forging two documents purporting to be company minutes of two electrical companies with the intention of inducing the Inland Revenue to accept them as genuine.
