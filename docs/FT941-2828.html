940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / International Company News: Trump wins licence for casino project Mr Donald Trump, the New York developer and casino operator who came close to bankruptcy three years ago, yesterday said he had won a licence from the Mississippi Gaming Commission to go ahead with a proposed casino project in Gulfport, Mississippi. The casino, due to open early next year, will take the form of a riverboat docked next to the Marine Life Oceanarium, which Mr Trump plans to renovate.
