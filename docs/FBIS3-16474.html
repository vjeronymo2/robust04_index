Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Shtorkh] [Text] Paris March 16 TASS -- Russian chief of General Staff Colonel-General Mikhail Kolesnikov ended his visit to France with a lecture, "Defence Policy and Reorganisation of the Russian Armed Forces," at the French military school on Wednesday [16 March]. Kolesnikov toured the school located in the French capital and had a one-to-one meeting with his French counterpart Jacques Lanxade. During his three-day visit, Kolesnikov met with French military leaders to discuss French military policy in light of the white paper recently issued by the government, France's participation in U.N. peace-keeping operations, its relations with the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe, NATO, the Western European Union and Russian-French bilateral relations. Kolesnikov also had talks with head of the parliamentary defence commission Jacques Bouaillon, visited the air force base Mont-de- Marsan where attended air force exercises and an air force division in Toulouse. The high-ranking French military believe Kolesnikov's visit will help strengthen defence cooperation between Russia and France.
