920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Company News: Tabacalera 4.8% ahead at halftime TABACALERA, Spain's state-controlled tobacco manufacturer and distributor which has a near-monopoly status in the domestic market, raised its first-half pre-tax profits by 4.8 per cent to Pta9.7bn (Dollars 106.6m). Income from tobacco sales and also from monopoly revenue earners such as state paper, including postage stamps, increased by 5.3 per cent in the first six months of 1992 compared with same period last year.
