Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 14 Jan (MENA)--MENA has learned that Dr. Zakariya 'Azmi, head of the presidential office and a People's Assembly member, has submitted a complaint to Attorney General Raja' al-'Arabi against the newspaper AL-SHA'B regarding a report that it published in today's issue. The complaint stated that the newspaper's report is totally unfounded and that the claims made in it are a figment of the writer's imagination. Dr. 'Azmi stressed that the newspaper's report constitutes slander against him and his reputation and therefore is an act punishable by law. AL-SHA'B reported that Dr.'Azmi is a partner of Madinat al-Nasr multimillionaire Fawzi al-Sayyid, who is under arrest for committing violations in the construction of high-rise buildings.
