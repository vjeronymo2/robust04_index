July 24, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

REUTERS EARNINGS INCREASE 27%

Reuters Holdings PLC, the British news agency and financial information services group, said today that earnings jumped 27% in the first half of the year, helped by strong sales in all major markets, particularly Europe.

Reuters said earnings for the six months ended June 30 totaled $188 million, compared with $148 million in the same period a year ago.
