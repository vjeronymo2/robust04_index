BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received at 1230 today Igor Ivanov, first deputy Russian foreign minister and envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, and his delegation. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Russian Ambassador in Damascus Aleksandr Zotov. Ivanov conveyed a message to President al-Asad from President Yeltsin dealing with bilateral relations and the peace process. President al-Asad gave him a message in reply for President Yeltsin. The Russian envoy spoke about his country's concern for the peace process in the region and its willingness to play an active role in this field. He also expressed his country's appreciation for Syria's status and its positive role in the region. President al-Asad welcomed the willingness of Russia, which is one of cosponsors of the peace process, to play an active role to help this process advance on the path to a just and comprehensive peace.
