Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Article by I.K.: "Highway in One Month"] [Text] Croatian Prime Minister Nikica Valentic, presently in Skopje, stated yesterday that the highway between Zagreb and Belgrade will most likely reopen in one month. During the talks between the Croatian delegation headed by Nikica Valentic and representatives of the Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Government, it was announced that the two countries will establish diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level.
