931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / More funds urged for fire service GOVERNMENT underfunding of the fire service is putting lives and property at risk, union leaders told the home secretary yesterday. Mr Ken Cameron, the FBU's general secretary, said: 'The government's penny-pinching panic measures have resulted in the number of non-fatal fire casualties reaching a record high and the UK having the highest fire rate per million in the European Community.'
