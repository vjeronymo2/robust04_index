BFN [Text] Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, a prominent leader in Yemen and representative of Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, arrived in the country this afternoon. He was received at al-Sib International Airport by Sayf Bin-Hamad Bin-Sa'ud, minister of the Sultan's court; Yusuf Bin'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs; and the Yemeni ambassador to Oman. Al-'Attas' visit to the Sultanate comes within the Sultanate's endeavors and eagerness to end the fighting and stop bloodletting among the Yemeni brothers.
