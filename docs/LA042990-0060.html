April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

POOLING THEIR RESOURCES

Believe me, the pool at the Kamari Hotel on Santorini is not the only pool on the island, as Jeff Halstead's article says ("Island of Santorini is One of Greece's Loveliest," April 1). The best pool is at the Atlantis Villas Hotel in Oia.

JIM HERMANN

Beverly Hills
