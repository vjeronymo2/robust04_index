Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of Samara oblast administration, Konstantin Titov, has announced his intention to set up an oblast government answerable to himself alone. The prime minister has already been appointed - 56-year old Yuriy Logoydo, former deputy head of administration and chairman of the committee for the consumer market. The head of administration gave as grounds for his decision to set up a government the need to enhance the oblast's status, which he said had in many respects sunk to second place in the country after Moscow, RIA reports.
