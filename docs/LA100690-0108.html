October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ACTION IN THE FINANCIAL MARKETS

KEY INDICATORS AT A GLANCE

BRITISH STERLING IN DOLLARS

News that Britain would enter the European Community's exchange-rate mechanism sent the pound soaring against the dollar

Friday Close: 1.9630

DOW ON THE HALF-HOUR

The Dow Jones industrial average opened down nearly 60 points on news of the budget failure but recovered on renewed hopes for lower interest rates.

Thursday Close: 2,516.83

Friday Close: 2,510.64 down 6.19

30-YEAR TREASURY BONDS

Already-wary investors continued to shun treasury bonds. The benchmark rate was affected only slightly by the budget news.

Friday Close: 8.81%
