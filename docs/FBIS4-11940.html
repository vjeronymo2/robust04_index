BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, five people were reported burned alive by the Indian troops when a conflagration was caused by excessive mortar shellings by Indian forces during the siege of a vast area in Hindwara. Thirty houses were reduced to ashes in this reckless military action. The situation in the area was described as very tense and reports said the Indian military action was still continuing. Meanwhile, in one area of Srinagar, three people were shot dead in a military crackdown. Similar crackdowns were also carried out in various other localities of Srinagar and Sopor. Another report says two bullet-ridden bodies were recovered in Bijbiara. The dead were victims of custodial killings. In Kulgam, two persons were shot dead by military personnel during a siege and search operation. The freedom fighters in a retaliatory move opened fire at the troops, but the casualties of the occupation troops are not known.
