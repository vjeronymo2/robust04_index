BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] Conflict over the mechanisms implemented to end the fall of the bolivar against the dollar continues. While the Economic Cabinet has blamed the Central Bank of Venezuela for the ineffectiveness of the measures applied in this regard, Central Bank President Antonio Casas Gonzalez recalled that the mechanisms for intervening in the banks was evaluated and accepted by the executive branch. Additionally, there are differences over control exchanges between Finance Minister Julio Sosa Rodriguez and Asdrubal Batista, minister for reforming the national economy. In this regard, Casas Gonzalez admitted that the auction of foreign currency will not resolve in itself the expectations of the public but that this would depend on the policies on banking, fiscal, and public order problems that convey credibility to the community.
