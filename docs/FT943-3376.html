940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Mayborn improves Mayborn Group, the USM-quoted fabric dyes, baby products and florists' sundries group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.9m to Pounds 2.06m in the six months to June 30. Turnover advanced to Pounds 18.9m (Pounds 17.5m). The interim dividend of 2p (1.8p) is payable from earnings of 7p (6.4p) per share.
