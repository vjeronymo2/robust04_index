Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 2 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- The Russian citizen recruited by British special services, who was arrested on 15 January this year, was a leading employee of the SMM (Special Engineering and Metallurgy) joint-stock company, set up on the basis of enterprises belonging to the former USSR Ministry of Defense Industry. SMM worked on state programs in the sphere of armor and artillery systems. This is disclosed in the Wednesday edition of the newspaper `Segodnya', which quotes Aleksandr Mikhaylov, head of the public relations centre of the Federal Counterintelligence Service. In its article on the detained agent, the newspaper says that the level of this post could illustrate the fact that he had the possibility of direct access to representatives of the legal British intelligence mission, which used the embassy as "cover." The arrested person is accused of passing on top secret information to a foreign special service. The information concerned the latest armaments systems and Russia's overall defense potential.
