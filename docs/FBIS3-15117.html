Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 3 TASS -- The Galaktika and Mayak radio broadcasting stations have taken legal steps against Zhirinovskiy: The Liberal Democratic Party [LDPR] paid with forged payment documents for its advertising programmes during the electoral campaign, the SEGODNYA newspaper wrote today. The Liberal Democratic Party, which got four hours and nine minutes of broadcasting time for its advertising, failed to transfer to bank accounts of the broadcasting stations 36 million 719,737 roubles. The Zhirinovskiy party sent to the editorial boards only the copies of payment documents. V. Zagnoiko, chairman of the board of Progressbank, denied the fact of the transfer of money by the Liberal Democratic Party to the broadcasting stations.
