941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Majedie Inv Majedie Investments had a net asset value per share of 227p at the end of the year to September 30, a 3 per cent increase from 221p last time. The FT-SE-A All-Share Index rose 0.3 per cent over the period. Net revenue edged ahead from Pounds 2.81m to Pounds 2.96m. Earnings per share were up at 5.64p (5.35p) and a final dividend of 3.25p is proposed, bringing the total for the year to 5.25p (5p).
