930615 FT 15 JUN 93 / UK Company News: M&W M&W, the food retailer and wholesaler, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 927,000 for the six months to March 28 - a rise of some 40 per cent on the corresponding Pounds 664,000. Turnover improved to Pounds 37.3m (Pounds 33.4m). An interim dividend of 1.1p (1p) is payable from earnings of 3.8p (2.8p).
