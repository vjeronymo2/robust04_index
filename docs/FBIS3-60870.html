Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek December 28 TASS -- Head of the gang of terrorists that kidnapped a group of pupils in Rostov-on-Don on December 23 said he was a resident of the Kyrgyzstan capital. Officials of the Kyrgyzstan Ministry of the Interior stated he had not been registered as a resident of Bishkek. The same ministry stated that Arslan Kasimov, a Kyrgyz, born in 1945, has not been brought to court in Kyrgyzstan.
