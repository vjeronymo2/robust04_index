Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 8 (KCNA) -- Plenary meetings of the provincial (municipal) committees of the Workers' Party of Korea were held recently. The plenary meetings discussed tasks of implementing the resolution of the 21st plenary meeting of the Sixth Party Central Committee. The plenary meeting of the party Central Committee held in December last year, on the basis of a deep analysis of the practical requirements of socialist construction in Korea, decided to set the next three years as a period for adjustment in socialist economic construction and put forth strategic agriculture first, light industry first and foreign trade first policies in this period. The plenary meetings of the provincial (municipal) party committees stressed that the Korean people are now faced with an honorable task of stepping up socialist economic construction to further increase the might of the Korean-style socialism and bring its advantages into fuller play, while consolidating the achievements made in the implementation of the Third Seven-Year Plan. They expressed the determination to implement the party's revolutionary economic strategy of directing primary efforts to agriculture, the light industry and foreign trade in the period of adjustment for socialist economic construction.
