941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Suez cuts some ship tolls The Suez Canal Authority has announced a 35 per cent rebate for liquefied natural gas carriers headed to European markets from the south, in an attempt to attract new customers and deter competition from alternative routes. The discount will apply also to ballast carriers on their southbound trip. A minimal number of gas carriers presently use the Suez Canal.
