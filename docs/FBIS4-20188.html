BFN [Text] Ankara, April 27 (A.A.) -- Turkey and Georgia on Wednesday [27 April] signed a protocol foreseeing cooperation on agriculture. The signatories of the protocol were Agriculture Ministry Under Secretary Cengiz Aysun and Georgian agriculture and foodstuff industry deputy minister, Juza Vasadze [name as received]. Under the protocol these two countries will have technical and scientific cooperation on vegetable production, cattle dealing, animal health, foodstuff industry, fishing and agricultural research. Turkey also offered to sign a cooperation on veterinary.
