940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Sanderson Bramall lifted by acquisitions to Pounds 3.8m Bolstered by acquisitions, profits of the Sanderson Bramall Motor Group advanced from Pounds 1.41m to Pounds 3.77m pre-tax in 1993. Turnover surged from Pounds 106.83m to Pounds 223.06m. The Skipper companies, acquired last July and partly financed by a Pounds 17m rights issue, contributed Pounds 84m to turnover and Pounds 1.41m to profits. In all, acquisitions added Pounds 103m to turnover and Pounds 2.12m to profits. Earnings worked through at 10.09p (5.04p) and a proposed final dividend of 1.74p lifts the total by 20 per cent to 2.4p. In October, the group completed the purchase of 28 per cent of Flightform, the UK franchise holder for Thrifty Vehicle Rental, the US rental company. Earlier this month contracts were exchanged to purchase motor group Petrogate.
