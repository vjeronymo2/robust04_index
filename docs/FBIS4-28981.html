BFN [Text] Upholding the party's intent to hasten the completion of the expressway between Pyongyang and Hyangsan, Korean People's Army soldiers of Comrade Choe Tong-suk's unit are vigorously accelerating the bridge construction assigned to them. The unit soldiers, who have a firm faith to do everything the party decides, have completed placing some 40,000-cubic meters of gravel and building about 4,000-square meters of retaining walls and more than 10 structures by accelerating the construction with the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and fortitude. They have also completed planting trees and grass. Being elated by the completion of these things, they are expediting the construction of the bridge and effect innovation every day. In close cooperation with the working class at the No. 51 construction office of the Hwanghae Steel Complex, they are now accelerating erecting (?pillars) and are scoring gains in this work by overcoming difficulties and obstacles with their own strength.
