941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / The Labour Party in Blackpool: Party support group formed Labour's affiliated unions have formed a new organisation to provide financial and organisational support to the party. The Trade Union Labour Party Liaison Committee will be based in the party's head office with a separate fund, initially of about Pounds 400,000. The committee will be run by a steering group of the leaders of eight unions alongside party leader Mr Tony Blair, deputy leader Mr John Prescott, the party general secretary and party treasurer.
