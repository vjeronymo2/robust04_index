Department of the Army Intent To Prepare a Draft Environmental Assessment (EA) for Project Access Improvements on the Snake and Gros Ventre Rivers, Jackson Hole, Teton County, Wyoming AGENCY: U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, DOD. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: 59 FR 26214, May 19, 1994 announced that the Draft EA would be available on 23 May 1994. Due to unforeseen problems and controversies, the draft EA will not be available until mid September 1994.
