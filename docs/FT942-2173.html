940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / Fraudulent trading alleged A businessman alleged to have lost Pounds 43m of investors' money in the 1980s property boom was yesterday cleared, on the judge's direction, of two charges of using a false instrument. Mr Roy Wharton is alleged to have promised returns of 20 per cent to investors in Castlegate Securities, based in Reading. His trial at Oxford Crown Court on two charges of fraudulent trading continues. He denies the charges.
