Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "In the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers. On the Establishment of a Republic of Georgia Commercial and Economic Representation in the Republics of Austria and Slovakia"] [Text] In order to extend commercial and economic relations between the Republic of Georgia and the republics of Austria and Slovakia and systematize these relations, by a decree of the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers, a Republic of Georgia commercial and economic representation has been established in the republics of Austria and Slovakia (Vienna) and a branch of this representation has been set up in Slovakia (Bratislava).
