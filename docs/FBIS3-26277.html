Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shenyang, February 4 (XINHUA) -- A bonded warehouse at Jinzhou port in Liaoning Province opened for business at the end of January. The first of its kind in the western part of Liaoning Province, the 3,200 square meter warehouse is about 100 meters from the wharf. An official in charge of the construction said that a second phase of the warehouse project is expected to be jointly constructed with foreign counterparts. He said that in the second phase, a 3,000-square-meter closed warehouse and a bonded yard taking up about 750,000 square meters will be built. Jinzhou port opened to shipping in October 1990. It now has three berths with annual handling capacity of 1.88 million tons. By the end of 1995, the port will have six berths including two for oil transportation, and the annual handling capacity will reach 5 million tons.
