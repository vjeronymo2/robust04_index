940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): First Leisure - Other companies Analysts will be particularly keen to hear the annual results on Wednesday of the discos, bingo and bowling group. Its shares slumped from 378p in March to 234p in November on fears of weak leisure spending. The shares have recovered to 285p but the City is still looking for reassurance. Pre-tax profit forecasts are for about Pounds 32m (Pounds 31.1m).
