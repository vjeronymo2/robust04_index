BFN [Text] Russia has no agreements on the import of radioactive waste generated at the nuclear power plants built with the help of the former USSR, Russia's Environment Minister Viktor Danilov-Danilyan told a news conference in Moscow. The minister pointed out that the law on environmental protection, brought into effect at the beginning of 1992, categorically bans the import of radioactive waste for dumping. He denied speculation that at the recent talks with his Norwegian counterpart, Thorbjorn Berntsen, agreement was reached to build a repository for radioactive waste from Norway in the Russian north.
