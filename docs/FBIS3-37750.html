Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Address by Supreme Council Chairman Imamali Rakhmanov to Armed Forces staff on the occasion of Armed Forces Day; date not given -- read by announcer] [Text] Dear comrades, the people of the Republic of Tajikistan will ceremonially mark the anniversary of the creation of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Tajikistan on 23 February [1994]. The Armed Forces of the republic were formed in the extremely difficult conditions of the social and political situation, and the civil war. The first units of the Armed Forces that were formed during a short period of time, together with the military units of the Internal Affairs and Security Ministries of the Republic of Tajikistan, made a huge contribution to the stabilization of the social and political situation and strengthening the defense capability of the republic. The people of the Republic of Tajikistan will remember forever the names of those soldiers and commanders of the Armed Forces who were killed in actions for the noble cause, the freedom and independence of their motherland. I congratulate you, dear comrades on the occasion of the anniversary of creation of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Tajikistan. I wish you sound health, happiness, and success. I am confident that the Command and the Staff of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Tajikistan will do their best to protect and to promote the independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity of the Republic of Tajikistan. [Signed] Imamali Rakhmanov, the head of state and Supreme Council chairman of the Republic of Tajikistan.
