Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 11 Mar -- An IBERIA news agency correspondent has been told by Boris Kakubava, member of the Georgian parliament, that the Abkhazian Liberation Union has drafted a plan to return to Abkhazia: "We will soon be in Abkhazia, in our territory, in our homes. We will wipe away everyone who stands in our way, no matter who they are -- Russian troops or those of the Georgian authorities," Boris Kakubava said.
