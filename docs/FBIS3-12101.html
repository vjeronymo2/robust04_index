Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish at 2300 GMT on 25 March reports that mining officials have announced that Ecuador is preparing to launch an "aggressive mining development" strategy with foreign funds. Santiago Cordovez, president of the Mining Chamber, pointed out that foreign mining companies plan to invest approximately $15 million for exploration in 1994. If successful, plans call for over $20 million to be invested in exploitation by 1995.
