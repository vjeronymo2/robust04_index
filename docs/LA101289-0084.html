October 12, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: WEST COVINA;

HOSPITAL TO OPEN ADDITIONS

Queen of the Valley Hospital will unveil $12 million worth of renovation and expansion at an Oct. 19 ceremony.

Construction began 18 months ago at the 272-bed facility, which has added an 18-bed pediatric unit and a laser surgery center, hospital spokeswoman Kathleen Rodman said.
