November 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WILLIAM ARTHUR FICKLING SR.; MADE MACON, GA., CHERRY TREE CAPITAL

William Arthur Fickling Sr., 87, a real estate magnate who was credited with making Macon, Ga., the cherry tree capital of the country. Fickling had a passion for Yoshino cherry trees and gave away more than 120,000 of them. In Macon on Sunday of emphysema.
