920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Appointments: Bodies politic Professor Anthony Jones is to become the new rector and vice-provost of the ROYAL COLLEGE OF ART from August 1. Jones, 47, was born in Wales and studied sculpture and painting at Newport College of Art and Tulane University, New Orleans. He was director of Glasgow School of Art for six years before becoming president of the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 1986. ***** Brian Cook, md of Bankers Trust Co, has been elected chairman of the American Banking and Securities Association of London. ***** Robert Lacey has been appointed executive director for the Scottish branch of the BRITISH RED CROSS. ***** Richard Genochio, most recently general manager, public and government affairs, at Texaco in London, has been appointed the first chief executive of the DIRECT MARKETING ASSOCIATION. ***** Carmen Callil is appointed a governor of the MUSEUM OF LONDON. ***** John Southworth is appointed secretary and finance director of the Building Employers Confederation. ***** Roger Vaughan, joint chief executive of Swan Hunter, is appointed the 15th and final member of the NATIONAL CURRICULUM COUNCIL. Five of his seven children are still at state schools in Northumberland. ***** Roger Trotman, md of Tufnol, has been elected president of EEF West Midlands. ***** Graham Mackenzie, chief executive of United Engineering Steels, has been elected president of the Engineering Employers' Federation.
