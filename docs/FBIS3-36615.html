Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Two new members of the Estonian Riigikogu started their work on Monday -- Kristiina Ojuland and Peeter Ello, to replace former MPs Heiki Kranich, now minister of finance and Toivo Jurgenson, now minister of economics. Kristiina Ojuland (27, Liberals' faction) was up to now Estonian representative to the Council of Europe in Strasbourg. Peeter Ello (38, Fatherland faction) was the former director general of the Board of Citizenship.
