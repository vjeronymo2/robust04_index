August 13, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

POSITIONING OF FORCES AT IRAQI OIL CHOKE POINTS

En route to Mediterranean: Aircraft carrier Saratoga, the battleship Wisconsin, one guided-missile cruiser and other support ships.

Red Sea: Aircraft carrier Eisenhower, one guided-missile cruiser, one guided-missile destroyer and other support ships.

Persian Gulf: The command ship LaSalle, two guided-missile cruisers, three guided-missile frigates and other U.S. Navy support ships. Also, one British destroyer and two French frigates.

Gulf of Oman: Aircraft carrier Independence, one guided-missile cruiser, one guided-missile destroyer and other support ships.
