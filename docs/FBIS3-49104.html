Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan. 5 -- The joint commission of the Polish Government and the Roman Catholic Church in Poland said after its debate on Wednesday that the government should urgently forward to the Sejm a draft law on the ratification of the concordat between Poland and the Holy See, according to a communique issued by the government press office. Apart from the questions related to the concordat the commission also debated on the scope of tasks and competences of the commission functioning on the basis of the law on relations between the state and the Roman Catholic Church of May 17, 1989.
