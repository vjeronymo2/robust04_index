930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Investment Co lower at Pounds 535,000 The Investment Company, the ultimate holding company of which is New Centurion Trust, saw its net asset value improve from 36.53p to 39.48p over the six months to September 30. The figure was 36.65p a year previously. In the six months to the end of September pre-tax profits were Pounds 535,000 (Pounds 592,000). Earnings per share came out at 1.4p (1.47p). And the interim dividend is being raised to 0.5p (0.375p) to improve the balance between the interim and final payments.
