BFN [Text] Tripoli, 30 Apr -- Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, deputy chairman of the Presidential Council in Yemen, made a phone call to the brother leader of the revolution [al-Qadhdhafi] during which he expressed his concern over the deteriorating situation in unified Yemen. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid stressed that the Arab nation must intervene to save Yemen from a civil war and fratricide.
