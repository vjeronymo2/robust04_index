BFN [Text] Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs, has stated that Egypt is closely watching the situation in fraternal Yemen out of its concern for the Yemeni people's interests and the Arab nation's interests in this strategic part of the Arab homeland. He said that Egypt hopes fighting among the sons of fraternal Yemen will stop as soon as possible. It also hopes that the Arab League and UN efforts on this regard will succeed. Al-Baz made this statement after receiving Lakhdar Ibrahimi, envoy of UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali, who later departed for Sanaa.
