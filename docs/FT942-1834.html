940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Polar Polar, the USM-quoted electronic components company, reported a 59 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for the 26 weeks to April 1. The result was helped by improved UK demand and the group's move into the design of semi-conductors. On turnover 29 per cent ahead at Pounds 12.4m (Pounds 9.57m) profits were Pounds 781,000 (Pounds 490,000). Earnings per share were 6.4p (4.1p) and the interim dividend is raised to 2.3p (2p).
