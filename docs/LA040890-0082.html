April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SUPREME COURT UPHOLDS STAY OF EXECUTION FOR HARRIS

Where do I hide from the death penalty? What about the children? What do they learn when 4 out of 5 adults believe ("Voters Favor Execution by Nearly 4 to 1," front page, April 2) society should be a cold-blooded killer? Opinion doesn't make it right.

GAIL M. JENSEN

Los Angeles
