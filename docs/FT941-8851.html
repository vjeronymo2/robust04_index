940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / International Company News: Pharmacia seeks buyer for unit Pharmacia, the majority state-owned Swedish pharmaceutical group, is seeking a buyer for Pharmacia Deltec, a wholly-owned US subsidiary manufacturing home drug delivery systems, writes Hugh Carnegy in Stockholm. Pharmacia, due to be fully privatised this year, said the sale was in line with its strategy of focusing on selected product areas. Pharmacia Deltec had sales of about Dollars 100m and was a world leader in ambulatory infusion pumps, it said.
