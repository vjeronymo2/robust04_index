[Reporter begins to play a video tape on Li Peng's activities in Kazakhstan] On the morning of 26 April in Almaty, the capital of Kazakhstan, Premier Li Peng presented flowers at a monument dedicated to unknown martyrs. In the afternoon, Premier Li met with figures of Kazakhstan's enterprise circles, during which he set forth six principles for developing economic and trade cooperation between China and Central Asian nations. The signing of several important agreements has laid a solid foundation for developing relations between China and the four Central Asian countries and Mongolia. [video shows Li Peng speaking and witnessing the signing of documents] If there were any light moments during the tour, they must be the short [words indistinct] and cheerful laughter and singing on the special plane.
