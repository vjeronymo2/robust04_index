April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SUE GRAFTON

I enjoyed Ellen Hawkes' article on Sue Grafton ("G Is for Grafton," Feb. 18).

I thought Grafton's (Millhone's) comment about devout Christians was a gross generalization. Of course, anyone can make generalizations. Here's one I like: Being with Sue Grafton is like being with a silly, willful child.

ED SCHAEFER, Tustin
