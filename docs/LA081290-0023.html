August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GAY-BASHING IN THE RECORD INDUSTRY

Thank you, thank you, thank you, Jeff Ayeroff, for proving that record companies and their executives are not just impotent, amoral monoliths incapable of taking a responsible stand.

ROBERTA MEYERS

Los Angeles

Virgin Records president Ayeroff said that he would not put out any record that could be perceived as promoting bigotry.
