930427 FT 27 APR 93 / International Company News: Walt Disney up 25% for second term THE Walt Disney Company, which owns theme parks, resorts and a Hollywood studio, yesterday reported a 25 per cent rise in net profits to Dollars 204.9m for the second quarter of its fiscal year, ended on March 31, writes Alan Friedman. Earnings per share were 23 per cent higher at 38 cents, while operating income climbed by 50 per cent to Dollars 406.6m. Revenues were 24 per cent improved at Dollars 2.03bn. Operating income for the theme parks and resorts business increased by 45 per cent to Dollars 163.7m on revenues that were 4 per cent better at Dollars 775.9m. In the filmed entertainment division, operating income jumped by 84 per cent to Dollars 156.7m on revenues that were 45 per cent higher at Dollars 929.8m. Operating income in the consumer products business was 18 per cent up at Dollars 86.2m on revenues that were 34 per cent higher at Dollars 320.7m.
