BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia, who had been paying an official goodwill visit to Korea upon the invitation of President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Pyongyang today by special plane, concluding his successful visit. His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk and his entourage were seen off at the airport by Vice President Yi Chong-ok, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and officials concerned. Present at the airport were Cambodian Ambassador to Korea Oum Mannorine and embassy officials and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
