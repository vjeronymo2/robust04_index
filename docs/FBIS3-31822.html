Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "Offer to Belgrade?"] [Text] Slovene Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle stated yesterday at a news conference that "Slovenia is considering normalizing relations with the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia [FRY]." According to Peterle, the Slovene Ministry of Foreign Affairs "is actively observing the state of everything that has recently changed in relations between Ljubljana and Belgrade." The diplomatic service in Slovenia is preparing information on what needs to be done to promote contacts between Ljubljana and Belgrade. Peterle pointed out that Slovenia's official position, favoring the normalizing of relations, is in Slovenia's interest, particularly because of the increasingly successful contacts between Zagreb and Belgrade.
