Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Ahmad Husayn Khudayyir has received Jordanian Energy and Mineral Resources Minister Walid Mithqal 'Asfur. The prime minister praised the solidarity demonstrated by fraternal Jordan--its people and government--with the Iraqi people during their confrontation of the 30-state aggression and the unfair embargo. He underscored the depth and strength of fraternal ties between the two fraternal countries. He also reaffirmed Iraq's readiness to promote these ties in various spheres. The prime minister said that Arab solidarity, upholding the nation's principles, strengthening inter-Arab ties, and promoting economic cooperation are the way to confront the imperialist onslaught targeting the Arab nation. He indicated that the transient circumstances being experienced by Iraq are about to end, thanks to the resolve and determination of the Iraqi people under leader President Saddam Husayn, may God protect him. For his part, Jordanian Energy and Mineral Resources Minister Walid Mithqal 'Asfur expressed the Jordanian people's solidarity with the Iraqi people, as well as their strong condemnation of the continued enforcement of the unfair embargo against the Iraqi people. He also underlined the strength of the fraternal bilateral ties of cooperation. The meeting was attended by Oil Minister Dr. Safa' Hadi Jawad, Cabinet Secretary Hamid Salih al-Rawi, and the Jordanian ambassador in Baghdad.
