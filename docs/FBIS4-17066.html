BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow, April 21 (TASS) -- A soldier of the Russian Interior Ministry troops was killed early in the morning today in the state of emergency zone of North Ossetia. A special-duty squad was blasted with fire by a group of armed people near the Volchiya mountain at 05.30 today, Andrey Rodnov, spokesman for the press service of the task force of the North Caucasian Interior Ministry district, told ITAR-TASS from Vladikavkaz. The squad was on duty near the villages of Sunzha and Kamgaron of the Prigorodniy District, where a number of civilians were killed by bandit groups recently and where the provisional administration decided to toughen security measures. It is supposed that the bandits were laying mines on the road. Shooting started, in the course of which the soldier was killed. The bandits disappeared. The provisional administration set up a commission for investigating the incident. According to the information released by commander of the Russian Interior Ministry troops Colonel Anatoliy Kulikov, in 1993 areas of provisional deployment of army units and special-duty squads in the state of emergency area were fired at and attacked 126 times. As a result of it, seven people were killed and 133 wounded. This year 20 attacks were staged. Today's death of the Russian soldier is the first one this year. The press servce of the Russian Interior Ministry troops is not disclosing the name of the soldier in order to avoid mistakes, until an official report comes from the command of the state of emergency area.
