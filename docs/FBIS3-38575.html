Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed BB: "Diplomatic Relations Between Switzerland and Macedonia"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Text] Bern, 1 Feb (ATS) -- Mr. Franz Von Daeniken, head of the Political Section of the [Swiss] Federal Department of Foreign Affairs [DFAE], visited Skopje on Monday [31 January] and officially established diplomatic relations between Switzerland and the former Yugoslav republic of Macedonia, recognized by Switzerland on 12 May 1993. According to a communique released in Bern, Mr. von Daeniken's discussions dealt with the Balkan situation.
