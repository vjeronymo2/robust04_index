941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Premier Land Premier Land, the property company, yesterday reported its first ever annual pre-tax profit, which amounted to Pounds 470,000 in the year to September 30. This compared with a deficit of Pounds 719,000 previously, before Premier had divested its mining interests. The company said yesterday that net assets had advanced to Pounds 14.4m (Pounds 4.7m), or on a per share basis to 6.15p (2.85p). Earnings per share came to 0.29p (0.85p).
