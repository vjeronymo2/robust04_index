BFN [Text] It is now known that members of the parliamentary committee for international affairs met in private on Thursday to discuss the Yeltsin-proposed list of candidates for ambassadorial posts in several countries including the United States. The committee approved the president's nominee, Yuliy Vorontsov, currently Russian ambassador to the United Nations, as head of the Russian embassy in Washington. The post has remained vacant after the previous ambassador Vladimir Lukin was elected to parliament and was appointed head of its International Affairs Committee.
