December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OJAI;

BODY OF WOMAN FOUND NEXT TO ROAD

The body of a woman between 25 and 35 years old was discovered by a sheriff's deputy Monday morning off the side of California 33 between Ojai and Rose Valley, prompting a murder investigation, authorities said.

"We are looking at this as a homicide," Ventura County Sheriff's Lt. Joe Harwell said.

As of early Monday evening, the coroner's office was still attempting to identify the body, found fully clothed.

The woman is described as 5 feet, 8 inches tall, with brown hair and eyes. Wingate estimated that the body had been at the site 12 to 24 hours. GERRY BRAILO SPENCER
