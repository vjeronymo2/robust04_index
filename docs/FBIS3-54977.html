Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lur agency reports that Azerbaijani troops are sustaining losses in their offensive against Karabakh positions. According to the Nagorno-Karabakh army press service, Azerbaijan's losses numbered more than 90 men and officers. Combat operations resumed this morning in the north and northeast of the Nagorno- Karabakh Republic, where fierce fighting has been going on relentlessly for the last few days.
