June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THEATER COMPLAINTS

Mark Karen to be commended for his May 28 letter imploring moviegoers to assert their rights when movies are out of focus, but I don't understand why Karen himself didn't follow his own advice when he went to see "Field of Dreamsa." Why did he sit through the entire movie without rushing to the manager to make a complaint?

J. S. BOYD

Los Angeles
