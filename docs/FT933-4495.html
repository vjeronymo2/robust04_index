930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1993 (2): Record entries for software competition - Business Software Challenge '90s 'Business Software Challenge 90s' - has attracted a record number of entries. Prizes to the value of Pounds 20,000, plus a range of hardware and software, are to be offered to the winners who will be announced in the next issue of the FT's Software At Work, in November. The competition is sponsored and funded by the software vendor, Computer Associates, which founded the event two years ago as a way to encourage students in the UK to write applications for solving real business problems. The competition is aimed at students and recent graduates in higher education, working individually or in teams. Winners in previous years have been responsible for a number of highly promising products - and some have gone on to full-time careers in software design. Although entries for this year closed on August 27, general details about the competition are available from: The Competition Secretary, Business Software Challenge 90s 24-28 Bloomsbury Way, London WC1A 2PX Tel: 071 831 6262; fax 071 831 0689
