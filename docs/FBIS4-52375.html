BFN [Text] Bank Negara's [Central Bank's] gold and foreign exchange and other reserves including SDI (special drawing rights) amounted to RM89,986.72 million as at Jan. 31, 1994. It was RM89,875.43 million a fortnight earlier and RM76,474.43 million at end December 1993 A Bank Negara statement said total assets and liabilities eased slightly to RM116,215.81 million from RM116,331.73 million a fortnight earlier but higher than the RM100,299.47 million as at Dec. 31,1993. Loans and advances were higher at RM2,552.34 million from RM2,479.17 million two weeks ago and RM2,637.07 million a month earlier. The bank's holdings of bills dlscounted amounted to RM3,391.97 million. It was RM3,450.35 million two weeks earlier and RM3,523.94 million a month ago. Currency in circulation amounted to RM16,626.48 million against RM14,835.48 million two weeks ago and RM14,649.04 million a month earlier.
