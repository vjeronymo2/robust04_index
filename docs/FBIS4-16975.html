BFN [Text] Moscow April 20 TASS -- The leaders of Russian regions who met with President Boris Yeltsin to sum up the results of local elections adopted the following statement in Moscow on Wednesday. "We, representatives of Russian regions, support the idea of signing the memorandum on civil peace and accord in Russia. "We are confident that the implementation of the tasks set by this memorandum -- to ensure political stability, exclude any forms of extremism from the arsenal of methods used in political practice, set up lasting mechanisms of accord, establish a new political culture, overcome the estrangement between the society and power and, consequently, create favourable social atmosphere for successful continuation of reforms -- meet the inspirations of the overwhelming majority of people of Russia. "We call on all Russian regions, political parties, movements and public organisations to leave behind their disagreements and join the memorandum on civil peace and accord in Russia in the name of peace and prosperity of our motherland." The text of the statement was released by the presidential press service.
