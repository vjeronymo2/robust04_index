August 22, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

GRAHAM, BRYAN GET TENNIS HONOR

Debbie Graham of Stanford and Steve Bryan of Texas-Austin were named college players of the year today by Tennis magazine and Volvo.

Both won the NCAA singles championships this year.

Graham, a junior from Fountain Valley, won the women's title with six straight-set victories and has a 52-0 record in singles matches. Bryan, a sophomore, has 53 singles victories. He turned pro in July.
