910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Quicks in loss Quicks, the Manchester-based motor distributor, dropped from profits of Pounds 4m into a pre-tax loss of Pounds 650,000 in 1990. The total dividend is halved to 3p. Turnover rose from Pounds 230m to Pounds 238m. The loss was after an exceptional charge of Pounds 2.34m. Losses per share were 3.1p (earnings 19.3p). The final dividend is 1p (4p).
