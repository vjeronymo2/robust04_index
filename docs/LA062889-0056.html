June 28, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"Miami Vice" star Don Johnson and actress Melanie Griffith, whose marriage 13 years ago lasted less than a year, were remarried Monday evening to the strains of a string quartet and the roar of news helicopters. Johnson, 39, and Griffith, 31, exchanged vows about 6:30 p.m. in the woods near Johnson's rustic ranch 10 miles northwest of Aspen, Colo. Roads leading to the marriage site were closed, but two helicopters carrying paparazzi and reporters from tabloid press hovered over the scene as about 30 guests witnessed the brief, nondenominational ceremony.ALEENE MacMINN
