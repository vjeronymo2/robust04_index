940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Law Debenture Net assets per share of the Law Debenture Corporation rose by 30 per cent to 667.3p at the end of December 1993, against 514p a year earlier. Net earnings for the year improved from Pounds 4.57m to Pounds 4.98m, equivalent to 21.91p (20.07p) per share. The dividend total is raised by 1p to 19.25p with a 12.75p final.
