October 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JUST FOLLOWING THE LEADER

It should come as no surprise, but probably will, to the artsy-smartsy View readers who have an abiding disdain for Lt. North, a retired U.S. Marine colonel, that such a man would devote "14 hours a day, five or six days a week . . . and is doing something for us somewhere, every day" according to John Staggers of the Alliance to Save America's Future (SAFE).

This kind of work is tailor-made for North; after all, he has fought his country's battles before with dedication and honor. If we are to fight a "war on drugs," no one is better qualified to carry out that mission into the streets than is North.

EDWIN O. LEARNARD, San Diego
