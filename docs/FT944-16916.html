941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Tunstall damages Tunstall Group, which supplies emergency communications systems for the elderly, has been awarded damages of Pounds 527,000 against Anchor Line over its acquisition of Tann-Synchronome in 1988. The case alleged faults in Tann-Synchronome's fire security products. Tunstall's legal costs, which by the end of March amounted to Pounds 454,000, had been written off as incurred.
