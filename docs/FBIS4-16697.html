BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow April 19 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin met visiting PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat in the Grand Kremlin Palace today. Welcoming the PLO chairman, Yeltsin said that Russia "is making efforts to support you politically, especially now that a negotiating process between PLO and Israel has begun." The Russian president pointed out that "our position alongside the position of the United States is very important". Yeltsin pointed out that Russia understands PLO claims to Israel. In his opinion, after the meeting the negotiating process will go ahead more successfully. Yeltsin pointed out, in particular, that "we had invited Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin to visit Moscow next week" so as to avoid a break in the talks. The meeting was attended by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and presidential aide for international affairs, Dmitriy Ryurikov.
