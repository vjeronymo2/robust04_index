920528 FT 28 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Wool Australian wool prices are holding firm after a brief spell of firming. A distinction is still drawn between better and more stylish wools and the less reliable types, and it is clear that buyers remain very selective. There has been a continuing and for important sectors of the wool-consuming industry a worrying lull in new business, following a widely-experienced surge in February. The recession still has its impact especially in the UK, which the important carpet sector depends on housing sales for good demand on the domestic side.
