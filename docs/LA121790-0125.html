December 17, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

ORLANDO GETS NBA ALL-STAR GAME

The 1992 NBA All-Star Game was awarded to the Orlando Magic today, and Walt Disney World will add its magic touch by assuming a major role in the event.

The game will be played in the 15,077-seat Orlando Arena on Feb. 9, NBA Commissioner David Stern said.

Disney will be responsible for most of the three-day pageantry, player hotels and nearly 6,000 league guests, said Richard A. Nunis, president of Walt Disney Attractions.
