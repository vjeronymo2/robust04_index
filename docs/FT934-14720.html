931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / Jump in insurance fraud probes INVESTIGATIONS into insurance fraud have increased sharply say loss adjusters, who examine claims on behalf of insurance companies. The Chartered Institute of Loss Adjusters says the time spent on investigations has increased by an average of 30 per cent this year. The institute said that one of the main reasons for the increase was the adoption of tighter procedures by insurance companies who have been hit by an epidemic of fraudulent claims in recent years.
