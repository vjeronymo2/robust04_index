940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / Commons accepts coal industry bill The government won a Commons majority of 37 (319-282) last night for the second reading of the Coal Industry bill, which provides for the privatisation of coal mining. Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, said the concept of state ownership was 'bankrupt'. He said the government would be prepared to offer financial support to potential management and employee buy-out teams. Mr Robin Cook. shadow trade and industry secretary, accused ministers of 'smashing' the industry.
