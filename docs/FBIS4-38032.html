BFN [Text] Aden hospital sources say that 12 people were killed and 100 injured in shelling by north Yemeni forces in the northern outskirts of Aden this morning. Medical sources have let it be known that four citizens were killed and 31 injured by northern forces' shelling of the Shaykh 'Uthman, al Mansurah, Dar Sa'd and 'Umar al Mukhtar districts north of Aden. REUTERS quoted south Yemeni officials as saying that more than 250 people were killed and hundreds injured during last week's Yemeni shelling of Aden. A Yemeni military source said that fierce battles with tanks and artillery between the south and north Yemeni forces are continuing on all fronts and that Aden has been facing an intensive northern shelling of residential districts since dawn today. The south Yemeni military communique pointed out that more than nine north Yemeni tanks and three Katyusha launchers were burned and more than 50 soldiers were killed.
