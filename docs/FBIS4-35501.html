BFN [Text] Yesterday evening at al-Rawdah Palace, President Hafiz al-Asad received Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, Jordanian deputy prime minister and minister of higher education. The meeting was attended by Dr. Salihah Sunqur, minister of higher education, and Ahmad al-'Adayilah, Jordan's ambassador in Damascus. The conferees discussed bilateral cooperation. Dr. al-Tall expressed satisfaction with the talks he held with Syrian officials.
