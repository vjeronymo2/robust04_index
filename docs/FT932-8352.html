930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: H Young improves to Pounds 532,000 93 pre-tax profit from Pounds 427,000 to Pounds 532,000. Turnover in the six months ended March 31 1993 came to Pounds 16.3m (Pounds 15.6m) and the operating profit to Pounds 749,000 (Pounds 638,000) with continuing businesses accounting for Pounds 766,000 (Pounds 689,000). Earnings per share worked through at 2.15p (1.72p) and the interim dividend is held at 2p.
