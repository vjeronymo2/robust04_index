Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] The late autumn grain area in Sichuan Province will be 46,360,000 mu, a decline of 420,000 mu from 1993; the wheat area will be 34,280,000 mu, a decline of 640,000 mu, and the area sown to improved varieties of wheat will be 31,500,000 mu, accounting for 91.8 percent of the total area. The cash crop area will be 13,140,000 mu; and the rape area will be 12,060,000 mu, an increase of 1,452,000 mu, or 13.7 percent over 1993.
