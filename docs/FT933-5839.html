930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fraser extends Gibbons offer MR Paul Fraser has received acceptances in respect of his offer for Stanley Gibbons Holdings, the stamp dealer, of 458,200 shares, or 3.51 per cent of the issued share capital. He now holds or has acceptances totalling 6.27m shares, representing 48 per cent of the issued capital. The offer has been extended until September 13.
