December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NOVEMBER RESTAURANT CLOSURES

The Los Angeles County Department of Health Services has the power to shut down food facilities for as long as necessary for the owners to correct conditions that pose a danger to the public health. Here is a list of restaurants closed during November.

American Grill -- 378 Santa Monica Pier, Santa Monica. Closed 11/7 to 11/9. Unsafe food temperatures; vermin infestation; improper sanitizing or storage of multiuse utensils.

Barry's B-B-Q -- 10025 S. Western Ave., L.A. Closed 11/21 and remains closed.* No public health permit.

Carl's Jr Restaurant -- 165 E. Duarte Road, Arcadia. Closed 11/2; reopened same day. Sewage system failure; improper sanitizing or storage of multiuse utensils.

Charlie Chan's Seafood Restaurant -- 5570 N. Figueroa St., L.A. Closed 11/15 to 11/19. Contaminated food; vermin infestation; no public health permit.
