BFN [Text] New York, April 29 KYODO -- Hisashi Owada, Japanese ambassador to the United Nations, on Friday [29 April] presented his credentials to UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali, who welcomed him as an old friend who is also an international jurist. Butrus-Ghali also said Owada is looking forward to strengthening ties between the world body and Japan, Japanese officials said. Owada, father of Crown Princess Masako, began his UN job on Thursday. He replaced Yoshio Hatano.
