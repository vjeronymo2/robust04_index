BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature: by correspondents Igor Porshnev, Vitaliy Trubetskoy, and others] [Text] There is the danger of a major conflict in Crimea unless the situation is resolved at the negotiating table, Defense Minister Pavel Grachev told visiting French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe. He spoke of having information about the redeployment of Ukrainian troops, including commando units, in Crimea. Some reports say Ukrainian troops deployed in Odessa are being put on alert.
