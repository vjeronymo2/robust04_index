Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 9 Jan, (GIA-TASS) -- A delegation led by Jaba Ioseliani, chairman of the provisional committee for the state of emergency, has left for Geneva for the second round of Georgian-Abkhazian talks. A report distributed today through the channels of the Georgian Information Agency says that the delegation includes Deputy Foreign Minister Mikhail Ukhleba, Aleksandr Kavsadze, chairman of the Committee for Protection of Human Rights and Inter- Ethnic Relations, Professor Levan Aleksidze, Zurab Lomashvili, deputy director of the Department for International Organizations, and state adviser Shalva Pichkhadze.
