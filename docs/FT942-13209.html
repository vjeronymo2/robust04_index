940426 FT 26 APR 94 / UK Company News: Lowland Investment Taking prior charges at par, the net asset value of Lowland Investment Company improved to 299.8p per share at March 31. The figure compared with values of 282.4p at the trust's September year-end and 251.7p at end-March 1993. The trust, part of the Henderson Touche Remnant stable, reported net revenue lower at Pounds 847,000 (Pounds 899,000) for the six month period, reflecting lower fixed interest exposure. The interim distribution goes up to 3.3p (3.2p), still covered by earnings per share reduced to 3.61p (3.82p).
