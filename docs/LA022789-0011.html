February 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

TOWARD EQUALITY

I appreciated your special section Toward Equality. You are doing a public service and serving a great need in educating the public about prejudice. I hope that you will continue this service and cover other areas of concern and public values. There is so much on TV and in the papers which destroys traditional values that inserts like this are a refreshing sight.

GERARD P. WEBER

Encino
