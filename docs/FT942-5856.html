940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Acquisitions help Channel to Pounds 1.7m Channel Holdings, a maker of security products and anti-theft devices for cars, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.7m for the year to March 31 against Pounds 308,000 for the previous 15 months. The result includes a full 12-month contribution from Carflow Products and a maiden contribution from CQR Security Components, consolidated from April 19 1993. Turnover amounted to Pounds 15m (Pounds 1.62m). Acquisitions generated sales of Pounds 12.1m and contributed Pounds 1.11m to operating profits of Pounds 1.69m. Earnings were 3.5p (2.4p) and a final dividend of 0.7p is proposed for a 1.2p total (1p for 15 months).
