930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: More time for Control Securities CONTROL Securities, the pubs and property group which has been in refinancing talks since last summer, yesterday extended its standstill agreement again. The new one runs until June 14. However, there are hopes that negotiations are nearing a conclusion. One party to the talks said: 'The big issues have been resolved'. Shareholders were told in March that the company aimed to produce particulars for the relisting of the shares, suspended since October 1991, before the end of June.
