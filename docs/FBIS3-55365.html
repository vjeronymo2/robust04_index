Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Jan 05, BNS -- The number of unemployed in Latvia is 78,327, or 5.3 percent of the country's able bodied residents, according to Jan. 4 estimates by Lidija Lielmane, a specialist at the Latvian Welfare Ministry. In total, 94,000 people are looking for jobs. Unemployment relief is paid to 44,804 people, while the number of longterm unemployed is 25,905.
