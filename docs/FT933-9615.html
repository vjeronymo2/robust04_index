930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Hotel projects RB FARQUHAR has won contracts worth Pounds 1.4m to supply bedroom and bathroom modules for two luxury hotels. The company is supplying 128 bathroom modules for the new De Vere Grand Harbour Hotel in Southampton and 66 modules containing bedrooms, suites and stairwells for a Friendly Hotel being built in Loughborough.
