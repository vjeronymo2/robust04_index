930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: John Swan 7% ahead to Pounds 231,346 John Swan & Sons, the Scottish livestock auctioneer and estate agent, reported a 7 per cent increase in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 216,922 to Pounds 231,346 in the year to April 30. The outcome was struck on turnover up by Pounds 193,000 to Pounds 1.49m. Earnings per share were 25.2p (23.9p) and the dividend for the year is raised by 1p to 16p.
