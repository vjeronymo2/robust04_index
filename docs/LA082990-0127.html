August 29, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

REMODELING PROJECT BEGINS AT CITY HALL

Work has begun on a $90,000 project to refurbish City Hall.

Among the decorative improvements to the council chambers are new carpeting, wall coverings and reupolstered chairs for council members, staff and the audience. New carpeting is to be installed today and Thursday, according to Walter Nutting, Interior designer in charge of the project, and all improvements to the council chambers should be finished by the end of September.

Along with improving the chambers, the City Hall renovation includes paint, new carpet and new windows in five data-processing offices, and carpeting and lighting in the building hallways.
