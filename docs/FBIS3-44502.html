Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, January 4 (CNS) -- The total agricultural production of Guangdong Province in 1993 is expected to show a three percent increase over 1992 and the per capita year-round income of farmers in the province will reach RMB [renminbi] 1,600, RMB 292 over that earned in 1992, accounting for an increase of eight percent, taking the inflationary rise into consideration. According to incomplete statistics, the area under rice cultivation in the province last year was 2.6 million hectares, with 48 percent of the harvest of high quality and an increase of 30 percentage points over the previous year. The province gave much attention to the introduction of high quality livestock with 18,000 breeding hens, 28,000 breeding ducks and a number of breeding pigs brought into the province during last year. The ratio of lean meat of pigs to fat meat increased from 35 percent to 50 percent.
