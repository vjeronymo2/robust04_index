940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Trust of Property net assets ahead Net asset value per share of Trust of Property Shares rose from 57.14p to 106.82p over the 12 months to December 31. By February 11, the figure had risen to 113.54p. Available revenue improved from Pounds 100,892 to Pounds 106,417, equal to earnings of 1.612p (1.529p). The single dividend is increased from 1.503p to 1.5785p. The trust invests in UK-listed property companies. It said 1993 had witnessed a return of confidence to real estate investments by institutional investors and added that the recent successful GATT settlement would benefit the property industry.
