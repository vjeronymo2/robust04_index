920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / GEC offshoot cuts jobs AEI CABLES, part of GEC, announced 95 redundancies from its factory in Birtley, Tyne and Wear yesterday because of the recession in the construction industry. The job losses are the second bout at the plant in only two months; in July AEI said it would have to shed 80 jobs at Birtley, the biggest of its three factories in England.
