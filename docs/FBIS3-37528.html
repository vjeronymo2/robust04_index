Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 16 (SNARK)--Today, about 4 am, the Azerbaijani troops, supported by the considerable quantity of armoured cars and tanks, took the advance on Martuni-Fizuli direction of Karabakh front. However, SNARK's reporter informed from the Press-Service of the Karabakh Defense Army that Karabakh defenders had succeeded to hold in check the enemy, and after that by the 8 am hurled back the enemy to the previous position. The information concerning the losses of both sides is making exact. Yesterday despite a cold and snowfall there were the battles for the important hillocks at the North of the Kelbadzharskiy and Mardakertskiy regions. Today morning the situation on the front was characterized by skirmishes.
