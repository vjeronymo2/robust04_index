BFN [Excerpt] Israel Air Force jets this morning attacked Hizballah targets in the Jabal Safi area of south Lebanon. An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman reported that all our planes returned safely to base. The pilots reported accurate hits against their targets. Our army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the area attacked is under the full control of the Hizballah organization, and the targets served as training bases and staging points for terrorist attacks.
