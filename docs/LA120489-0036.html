December 4, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Summit Wrap-Up: Public radio station KPCC (89.3) will feature two live events pertaining to the Malta Summit on its morning broadcasts today. The station will air President George Bush's news conference from 7:30 to 8:30 a.m. and will also broadcast a live call-in National Public Radio show about the Malta Summit from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. SHAUNA SNOW
