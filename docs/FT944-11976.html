941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Capital Gear Trust Capital Gearing Trust reported net asset value per share down at 510.1p at October 5, against 522.5p six months earlier. Net revenue for the half year to October 5 was Pounds 5,972 compared with losses of Pounds 14,873, for earnings per share of 0.33p (losses 1.34p).
