July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ISRAEL'S PEACE PLAN

Well, there goes one-sided Conrad again with his cartoon showing a careening Star of David peace talk bus going over a cliff with four Israeli leaders all trying to steer the bus at one time.

What happened to the Arab states careening peace talk bus with its 10 or more leaders fighting over their one steering wheel bus?

WALTER LEVINE

Los Angeles
