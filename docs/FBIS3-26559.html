Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On 5 February, the provincial party committee and the provincial government held a forum on the work of supporting the Army and giving preferential treatment to family members of revolutionary martyrs and servicemen and supporting the government and cherishing the people. [hereafter called the "double-support work" for short] Sun Weiben, secretary of the provincial party committee, pointed out at the forum that the double-support work should be carried out frequently, creatively, sincerely, and genuinely. On 5 February, representatives of related party, government, and military organizations met together at the Provincial Heping Assembly Hall to speak glowingly of the province's excellent situation in the double-support work as well as the fish-and-water relationship between the Army and the government and between the Army and the people. Representatives participating in the forum maintained: Over the past year, the province as a whole has extensively conducted the national defense education by conscientiously implementing the guidelines of the national double-support work conference, has done a good job in giving special care to disabled servicemen and to family members of revolutionary martyrs and servicemen, has achieved great results in the activities of Army-civilian joint construction and joint education, has vigorously developed the activities of building double-support model cities, and achieved satisfying results in the double-support work. Sun Weiben pointed out in his speech: The double-support work contains an extremely extensive content. Under the new historical conditions, the double-support work will witness new circumstances and new problems. This requires us to carry out the work in a regular and creative manner. In doing this work, we should also express sincerity and genuineness. The Army should sincerely and genuinely show concern for and support the construction of localities, and the localities should sincerely and genuinely support the Army by carrying forward the glorious tradition of supporting the Army and giving preferential treatment to family members of revolutionary martyrs and servicemen. Shan Rongfan, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, highly appraised the achievements scored by the province in the double-support work in 1993 and called for efforts to further enhance the understanding of the importance of the double-support work, to strengthen the propaganda on the double-support work, to implement existing rules, regulations, and policies related to the double-support work, and to consolidate the already achieved results. Yu Jingchang, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and political commissar of the provincial military district, and (Yan Haipeng), deputy political commissar of a certain combined arms Army stationed in Harbin, also spoke at the forum.
