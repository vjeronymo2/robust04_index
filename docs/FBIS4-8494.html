BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 13 April (TANJUG) -- Vitaliy Churkin, the Russian president's special envoy, arrived from Pale in Belgrade late this afternoon, where he was received by Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic. Their strictly confidential talks were held behind closed doors but there is no doubt that the main reason for Churkin's surprise visit to Belgrade is the newly created situation in Bosnia following NATO air strikes on Serb positions around Gorazde.
