940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Sunleigh breaks even second half Sunleigh, the USM-quoted leisure products concern, broke even in the second half of 1993 leaving annual pre-tax profits at Pounds 208,000, against losses of Pounds 557,000. The result was after taking a charge of Pounds 397,000 for the loss on sale of discontinued activities, offset by a Pounds 250,000 release of provisions. The previous year there was a net release of provisions of Pounds 71,000. Turnover for the year was Pounds 15.7m (Pounds 12.9m) of which Pounds 263,000 (Pounds 1.21m) related to discontinued activities. Earnings per share were 0.05p (losses 0.86p).
