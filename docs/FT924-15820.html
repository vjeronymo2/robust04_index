921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Improvements for researchers urged A RADICAL improvement in the career structure of academic researchers is advocated by the Royal Society, the UK's senior scientific body, in a report released yesterday. It says far too many scientists exist on an uncertain succession of short-term contracts lasting two or three years. They grew in number from 7,500 in 1978 to 19,400 in 1991. The Future of the Science Base. Royal Society. Pounds 11.50.
