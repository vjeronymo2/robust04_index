930429 FT 29 APR 93 / International Company News: Anheuser-Busch in reverse ANHEUSER-BUSCH, the largest brewer in the US, yesterday reported a fall in operating profits to Dollars 348.5m in the first three months of 1993, from Dollars 390.7m. After-tax profits, ahead of the effect of accounting-related changes, dipped to Dollars 194.1m from Dollars 215.1m. Anheuser said that economic weakness in certain areas such as California and on the east coast affected the first quarter, as did some poor weather. Sales slipped to Dollars 2.88bn from Dollars 3.02bn.
