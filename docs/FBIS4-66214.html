CSO [Summary] According to a survey of 1,150 rural households in Liaoning Province, the grain area will be about 303 [as published] hectares, a 0.3 percent decrease from 1993; and the cash crop area will be about 250,000 hectares, a 2.8 percent increase. The cotton area will be 36,000 hectares, a 32 percent increase over 1993.
