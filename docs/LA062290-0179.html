June 22, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

ACTOR GETS REAL IN DRUGS FIGHT

Armed with golf clubs rather than a gun, the actor who portrays "Twin Peaks" FBI Agent Dale Cooper has joined his hometown's war on drugs.

Actor Kyle MacLachlan will appear at a celebrity golf tournament Saturday to raise money for local drug-fighting efforts.

MacLachlan plans to take Lara Flynn Boyle, a co-star on the ABC-TV series, and other actor friends to the tournament at the Yakima Country Club.

Country singer Reba McEntire is the featured performer at a fund-raising concert for the cause in the SunDome tonight.

Organizers hope to raise about $50,000 for city anti-drug programs.
