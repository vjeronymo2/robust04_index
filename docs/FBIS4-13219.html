BFN [Text] Concerning the setting up of the Transitional National Council, debates on whether to take part in it have flavored the lives of political parties. After the National Liberation Front, the Socialist Forces Front, Ennahda, Ettahadi Movement, and the Democratic Movement for the Renewal of Algeria announced they were boycotting the council, the Rally for Culture and Democracy announced that it would not be participating. The Algerian People's Union, however, has agreed to join in.
