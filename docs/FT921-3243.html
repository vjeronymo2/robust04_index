920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Sikh motor scooter gunmen kill 18 Sikh militants riding motorscooters opened fire in Ludhiana, Punjab's largest city, killing at least 18 people, 16 of them Hindus, AP reports from New Delhi. The murders were the second attack on Hindus in an industrial area this week in Punjab, where Sikh rebels are fighting for an independent nation.
