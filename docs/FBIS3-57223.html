Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM-TASS correspondent] [Text] Kiev, 24 Jan -- The first strategic exercise which started today under the leadership of the supreme commander of the Ukraine Armed Forces, President Leonid Kravchuk, is called upon to resolve the tasks of territorial defense. Its aim, the press service of the Ukraine military department announced, is to work out the interaction between the ministries of defense, internal affairs, the security service, the National Guard, border troops and the civilian defense of Ukraine. Leonid Kravchuk drew the attention of the leaders of the power ministries to the importance of such an exercise, and directed them to fulfill with quality the tasks set and to the need to give an objective appraisal of the work done. Under the plan for conducting the exercise, the press service of the Ukraine Ministry of Defense notes, the use of troops is not envisaged.
