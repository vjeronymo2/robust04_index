941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Undersea earthquake kills at least 11 on Pacific islands At least nine people were killed in the Kurile Islands after an undersea earthquake sent tidal waves across the Pacific and rocked buildings on land. On the Japanese island of Hokkaido, two people died - apparently from heart attacks - and 196 were injured. A tidal wave warning was issued for all Pacific islands and coastal areas, including the US and Canada.
