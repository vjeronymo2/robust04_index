940426 FT 26 APR 94 / International Company News: NEC's sales of personal computers up NEC's sales of personal computers in Japan increased 25 per cent from a year earlier to 740,000 units in the second half of year ended March 31, Reuter reports from Tokyo. NEC estimated total 1993-94 sales of PCs in Japan at 1.37m units, up 10 per cent on the previous year.
