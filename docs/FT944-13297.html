941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / World Trade News: SGS-Thomson China venture SGS-Thomson, the Franco-Italian semiconductor manufacturer, yesterday announced that it was establishing a joint venture in China to assemble and test its products. The joint venture, which will be formed with China's Shenzhen SEG High Tech Industrial Company, will involve an initial investment of Dollars 77m. SGS-Thomson will hold a 60 per cent stake in the company, with SEG holding the balance. Construction of the plant, which will supply semiconductors for international customers as well as for consumer products aimed at the Chinese market, will begin before the end of the year. Production is due to start by the end of 1995.
