Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature] [Text] A Russian delegation is due to leave for Kiev on February 8 to draw up, jointly with Ukrainian officials, a set of measures to implement the Russian-Ukrainian-U.S. agreements on nuclear weapons, the head of the delegation said. Yuriy Dubinin told Interfax the delegation would include Nuclear Energy Minister Viktor Mikhailov and a senior Defense Ministry official, Colonel-General Yevgeniy Maslin. Russia "is always open for bilateral negotiations with Ukraine and is ready to help organize a trilateral meeting with the participation of the United States," Dubinin said.
