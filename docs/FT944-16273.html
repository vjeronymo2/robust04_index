941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / The Conservatives in Bournemouth: 'Drunken' youth behaviour probed The Young Conservatives yesterday promised an investigation into the behaviour of its more exuberant members after Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, refused to comment on their behaviour at a late night conference party. The disclosure - on the day of the party's law and order debate - that members of the YCs had indulged in 'drunkenness and nudity' brought a sober response from a party spokesman who promised 'appropriate action' against the offenders
