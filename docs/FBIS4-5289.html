BFN [Text] The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications [MOPT] announced on 7 April that it plans to include mobile telephone systems (car and portable phones) in projects to improve communications infrastructures in developing countries which it plans to support through its official development assistance (ODA) programs. The MOPT made its decision upon receiving a report compiled the same day by the Research Council on the Utilization of Wireless Systems for the Improvement of Communications Infrastructures in Developing Countries (chairman: Naohisa Goto, Tokyo Institute of Technology professor), which is a private study panel under the director general of the MOPT's International Affairs Department. The ministry plans to launch specific projects as early as within fiscal year 1995.
