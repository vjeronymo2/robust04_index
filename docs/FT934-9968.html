931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Multitrust shares suspended at 87p Shares in Multitrust were suspended at 87p on Friday at the company's request. Further to last month's announcement, the company has now agreed in principle a substantial acquisition which, if completed, would constitute a reverse takeover of Multitrust and would result in it losing its investment trust status. The acquisition will be accompanied by a rights issue.
