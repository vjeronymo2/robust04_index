930416 FT 16 APR 93 / International Company News: CSX to take Dollars 93m charge CSX, the US transportation group, is to take a pre-tax charge of about Dollars 93m, equal to Dollars 61m after tax, to reflect restructuring costs incurred by its container-shipping subsidiary, Sea-Land Service, Reuter reports. The group said Sea-Land was taking 'decisive steps to lower its costs in the challenging Atlantic trade, while further streamlining operations and functions throughout the company.' The plans include the elimination of up to 600 jobs.
