930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / UK Company News: British & American Film slips to Pounds 1.25m Pre-tax profits of British & American Film Holdings were marginally down, from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 1.25m, in the year to December 31. Earnings per share improved from 34.4p to 36.81p. The final dividend is being increased to 8.25p (7.32p) making a total for the year of 12.05p (10.695p). Net asset value per share at the year end, excluding film rights, stood at 1,023.1p - against 898.5p six months ago and 880.8p a year earlier. At April 29 this year the figure had risen further to 1,110.2p per share.
