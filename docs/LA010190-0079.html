January 1, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SITTING IT OUT: Those wanting to cheer on Orange County entries in today's Rose Parade will be disappointed. . . . Despite a history of entries that extends, with lapses, back to 1917 when Santa Ana first entered, there will be no float from here this year. . . . "We're cutting back on all the frills," a Santa Ana City Hall spokesman explained. Instead, the city will be represented by the Santa Ana Winds Youth Band, appearing 110th in the line of march. Steve Emmons
