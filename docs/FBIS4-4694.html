BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 4 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Dozens of herdsmen's gers, a national dwelling, and large pasture area were on fire and hundreds of head of cattle perished due to the steppe fire extending throughout the territory of a number of somons of the two eastern aymags of Mongolia, namely Suhbaatar and Dornod ones. Burnt in a fire lasting one and half day was an area 300 km long and 150 km wide. Fortunately, there are not any human casualties recorded in connection with this fire. According to the MONTSAME correspondent from the Suhbaatar aymag, the fire flared up firstly on the territory of the Munhhaan somon of the aymag. So far the reason for the fire to have broken out has not been established.
