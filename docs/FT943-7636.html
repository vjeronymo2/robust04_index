940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Willis Corroon - Other Companies Problems at a number of its US offices will depress profits at the insurance broker when it announces interim results on Thursday. Pre-tax profits are expected to fall to Pounds 50m-Pounds 55m, compared with Pounds 63.1m. The group has also been hit in the US by the defection of a team of construction insurance specialists to a rival.
