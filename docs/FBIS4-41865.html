BFN [Decree No. 132-I SF of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council "On the Draft Federal Law `On the Deadline for Submitting the Draft Federal Budget for 1995 to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly'," dated Moscow, 2 June 1994 and signed by V.F. Shumeyko, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council, plus undated draft federal law "On the Deadline for Submitting the Draft Federal Budget for 1995 to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly," signed by Russian Federation President B.N. Yeltsin] [Text] In accordance with Part 1 of Article 104 of the Russian Federation Constitution, for the purposes of accelerating the elaboration of the draft federal budget for 1995 and also to regularize the budget process in the Russian Federation, the Federation Council decrees that: The draft federal law "On the Deadline for Submitting the Draft Federal Budget for 1995 to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly" (appended) is to be sent to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma. [Signed] V.F. Shumeyko, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council. Moscow, 2 June 1994.

Draft Federal Law on the Deadline for Submitting the Draft
Federal Budget for 1995 to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Article 1. The Russian Federation Government is to submit the draft federal budget for 1995 to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly no later than 15 September 1994. Article 2. The forming of the draft federal budget for 1995 is directly linked with the elaboration and implementation of measures to extricate the country from the economic and financial crisis and also with the development and introduction of a balanced and harmonious system of financial and budget relations between the federal budget and the budgets of the components of the Federation. [Signed] B.N. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation.
