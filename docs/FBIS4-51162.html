BFN [Text] In Beijing on 20 May, Comrade Hu Jintao, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee cordially received the visiting Vietnamese party delegation led by Vo Tran Chi, member of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Ho Chi Minh city party committee. Comrade Hu Jintao warmly welcomed the Vietnamese delegation and expressed the hope that relations between the two countries will be further consolidated and developed. Earlier, the Vietnamese party delegation visited Shanghai and other localities in China.
