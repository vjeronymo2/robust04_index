BFN [Text] An Arab youth was shot and killed during a riot in Nabulus at noon following a demonstration demanding the release of all Palestinian prisoners. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that there were similar demonstrations in other towns in the territories, and in Gaza and Jericho in the autonomy. In Gaza, thousands today attended the funeral of Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activist Nasir Faluha, who was murdered yesterday. The Palestinian police safeguarded the funeral procession. During the funeral ceremony there were calls to kill Faluha's murderer. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan notes that the Palestinian police detained a suspect, 'Abd-al-Salam 'Alwan, a brother of a collaborator murdered several months ago by Hamas' 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam military arm.
