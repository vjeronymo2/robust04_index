BFN [From "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] Six electoral members and two delegates of the Civil Registry office, who were kidnapped in El Cerrito, Santander Department, arrived in the outskirts of El Cerrito after walking on uneven terrain for hours. Marta Vera, Mario Salinas Antolines, Edgar Rodriguez Cruz, Amanda Mogollon, Luis Antonio Cuadros, and Jesus Alberto Campos, as well as Civil Registry delegates Flor Maria Rangel and Maria Pena were released after the 19 June elections. Upon arrival, they said they were treated well by members of the Efrain Pabon Pabon Front of the self-styled ELN [Army of National Liberation] who kidnapped them.
