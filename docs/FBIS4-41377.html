BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent--TASS] [Text] Minsk, 23 June -- A BELINFORM correspondent was told by the Central Electoral Commission that as of 1900, 73 percent of the voters had participated in the elections for the country's first president. The highest turnout in the secret ballot was among the residents of Brest Oblast, with 78.7 percent having cast their votes. In some rayons of the oblast the turnout reached 86-87 percent of the total electorate. The lowest turnout was in the capital, where only 64.4 percent of the electorate had voted by 1900 local time. Some 356 polling stations finished their work ahead of the deadline.
