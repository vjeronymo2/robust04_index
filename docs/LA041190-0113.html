April 11, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Judge Delays Motorola Decision: U.S. District Judge Lucius D. Bunton in Austin, Tex., delayed his decision until June 18 on Hitachi's motion to lift a stay of Bunton's earlier injunction that halted Motorola from selling its key 68030 microprocessor. Motorola said it continues marketing and selling the chip, which controls several popular computers made by Apple Computer, Hewlett-Packard, Sun Microsystems and others.
