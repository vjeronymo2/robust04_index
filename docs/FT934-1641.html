931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Umeco makes Pounds 1.35m purchase Umeco, the USM-quoted aerospace components distributor and manufacturer of aircraft refuellers, has acquired Tortube, a maker of tubular components, for an aggregate consideration of Pounds 1.35m. The consideration will be satisfied as to Pounds 1.05m in cash and the balance by 789,475 new shares issued at 38p each. For the year to August 31 1993 Tortube made pre-tax profits of Pounds 368,000 on turnover of Pounds 3.98m. Net assets at August 31 were Pounds 1.46m.
