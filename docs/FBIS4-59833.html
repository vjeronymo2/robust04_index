BFN [Text] Iraq has once again protested to the United Nations against U.S. aircraft continuing to violate Iraq's airspace for purposes of provocation and surveillance. In a memorandum he sent to the UN Secretary General yesterday, Iraq's permanent UN representative said these aircraft violated Iraq's airspace and carried out 377 hostile air sorties from 1-10 May. Iraq's permanent UN representative requested that the memorandum be distributed as a Security Council document.
