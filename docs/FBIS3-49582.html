Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A communique received from Romania's Government informs that, at an extraordinary session held today, Romania's Government approved -- following a report presented by Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu -- the resignation request submitted by Mr. Paul Everac, director general of the Romanian Television, who had been appointed to that position by Governmental Decision No. 21/27 January 1993. In this respect, a new governmental decision was adopted, Governmental Decision No. 18, according to which Mr. Paul Everac is released from his position as of today. Until a law on the functioning of public radio and television services is passed, Mr. Dumitru Popa has been appointed to the position of general director of the Romanian Television. By another governmental decision, Mr. Dumitru Popa was released from his position as secretary of state, head of the Government's Public Information Department.
