Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], led by 'Ali Salim al-Bid, has stated its full and unqualified commitment to the document of accord recently agreed to by Yemeni parties to defuse the political crisis plaguing the country. In a letter addressed to the Yemeni Political Forces Dialogue Committee, which initialed the document on 18 January, the YSP expressed its willingness to contribute to the implementation of the document in defense of Yemen's unity. In the letter, which was published in Sanaa today, the YSP urged compliance with the provisions enshrined in the document. The YSP also warned against attempts to obstruct the implementation of the document. It reiterated that it is extremely concerned about avoiding media vituperations to help clear the political climate in the country. The Political Forces Dialogue Committee, which includes the Yemeni coalition government parties as well as opposition parties, will hold its last meeting later today to endorse the draft accord that will be signed by the Yemeni president, his deputy, and the secretaries of Yemeni parties in the Jordanian capital on Sunday [6 February].
