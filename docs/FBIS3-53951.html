Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Iran has denounced what it calls Switzerland's unfriendly attitude following a series of disputes which have soured relations between the two countries. The Iranian ambassador in Bern, Mohammad Reza Alborzi, said his Swiss counterpart in Tehran was summoned to the Iranian Foreign Ministry on Wednesday [19 January] to explain Switzerland's position. The ambassador was speaking after the Swiss Broadcasting Corporation suspended a cooperation agreement with Iranian Television. The move follows reports that Iranian Television was used as a front by people involved in the assassination three years ago of a former Iranian prime minister, Shahpur Bakhtiyar.
