940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Claverhouse net assets advance Net assets per share at Fleming Claverhouse Investment Trust advanced from 189p to 238.3p over the 1993 year. Net revenue came to Pounds 3.21m (Pounds 2.23m) resulting in earnings per share of 6.53p (5.54p). A proposed final dividend of 1.75p makes a total of 5.5p, against an adjusted 5.35p.
