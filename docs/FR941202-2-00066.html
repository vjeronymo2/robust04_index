DEPARTMENT OF LABOR Office of the Secretary All Items Consumer Price Index for all Urban Consumers United States City Average Pursuant to the requirements of Public Law 95&hyph;602, I hereby certify that the Consumer Price Index for all Urban Consumers rose 2.6 percent between October 1993 and October 1994 from a level of 145.7 (1982&hyph;84=100) in October 1993 to a level of 149.5 (1982&hyph;84=100) in October 1994.
