May 20, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LAGUNA BEACH;

REPUBLICAN ASSEMBLY CANCELS ART AUCTION

The Laguna Beach area Republican Assembly has canceled its art auction, which was scheduled for Sunday at the Club Postnuclear.

Those who purchased tickets to the fund-raiser can obtain refunds by calling (714) 497-3092. Ticket costs may also be applied to yearly dues for members of the Republican Assembly, or those who wish to become members, according to Assembly officials.
