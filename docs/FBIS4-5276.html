BFN [Text] An H-46 helicopter crashed at the Futenma Air Station on 6 April. In reaction, Mitsuki Tomon, lieutenant governor of the Okinawa Prefectural Government, called on Donald. R Gardner, commander of U.S. Forces on Okinawa, at Camp Zukeran on the morning of 7 April to deliver to the commander a letter demanding a thorough investigation into the cause of the crash and the prevention of a recurrence. The helicopter crashed after an F-15 fighter crashed while taking off from Kadena Air Base on 4 April. Therefore, she strongly protested to the commander about the helicopter crash. She also protested to the U.S. Consulate General in Okinawa. On the afternoon of 7 April, Shigeru Akamine, deputy chief of the Okinawa Prefectural Government Public Affairs Office, will protest to the Naha Defense Facilities Administration Bureau about the helicopter crash.
