Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federal Government has released the list of items banned from being imported and exported under the fiscal and monetary policy for this year. The items prohibited from being imported include vegetables, textiles of all types, domestic articles, and wares made of plastic materials. Those that cannot be exported include raw hides and skins, timber, leather, and raw palm kernel. The items are contained in the monetary and credit guidelines of the Central Bank, just published in Lagos.
