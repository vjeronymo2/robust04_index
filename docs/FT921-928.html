920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / Small businesses cut 1m jobs in 2 years SMALL businesses shed nearly 1m jobs in the two years to 1991, roughly half the number created in the previous three years, according to a survey published yesterday by the Small Business Research Trust, Charles Batchelor writes. Job losses have continued over the past nine months since the researchers finished their work. The recession has reduced the numbers of people working in businesses employing up to 50 people by 16 per cent, the survey found. It based its conclusions on a nationwide study of 120 small businesses in manufacturing and service. The Management of Success in Small Firms, SBRT, Open University, Walton Hall, Milton Keynes KM7 6AA. Pounds 20.
