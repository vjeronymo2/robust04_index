920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / TSW will appeal to Lords TELEVISION South West, the ITV company, yesterday decided to appeal to the House of Lords over the loss of its commercial television franchise. In October the Independent Television Commission rejected TSW's Pounds 16.1m bid and awarded the franchise to Westcountry Television, which bid Pounds 7.8m a year. TSW lost its judicial review in the High Court on Wednesday in a majority decision.
