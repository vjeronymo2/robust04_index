Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours Newscast"] [Text] Ten Colombian fishermen, including two boys, were detained minutes ago by members of the Venezuelan National Guard who entered Colombian territory. The fishermen were in a channel in La Rubiera Zone, east of Arauca. Other fishermen managed to escape and they say they were treated as guerrilla members; adding they still have no information about their coworkers' whereabouts.
