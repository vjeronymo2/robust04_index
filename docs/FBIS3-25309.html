Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government is demanding the resignation of the commander of the National Peacekeeping Force, General Gabriel Ramushwana, as head of the Venda Defense Force. The minister of regional and land affairs, Mr. Andre Fourie, said in a statement in Pietersburg that General Ramushwana was still actively involved in political activities in Venda despite his position in the peace force. Mr. Fourie rejected accusations that he was undermining the peacekeepng force, saying that he was trying to protect the impartiality of the force.
