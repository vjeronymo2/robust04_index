931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / Mielke jailed for Weimar crimes A Berlin court yesterday sentenced Erick Mielke (85), former chief of the hated secret police in Communist East Germany, to six years in prison for two murders committed in the Weimar republic six decades ago, writes Ariane Genillard in Bonn. He also faces charges of abusing human rights during the Communist era, including the killing of people crossing the Berlin wall.
