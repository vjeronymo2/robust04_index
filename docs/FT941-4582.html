940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / International Company News: Chairman quits Swiss group Mr Rudolph Sprungli, the septuagenarian chairman of the Swiss fine chocolate group Lindt & Sprungli, is retiring. The move comes nearly two years after the group was rocked by accusations that his second wife, a former bar hostess and member of a theosophical religious sect, tried to influence management appointments, writes Ian Rodger. Mr Sprungli represents the controlling family interest in L&S.
