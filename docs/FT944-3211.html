941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Abbott Mead buy Abbott Mead Vickers is paying a maximum Pounds 8.35m for Freud Communications, a public relations company. An initial Pounds 2m will be satisfied by loan notes and cash with further profit-related payments. Freud reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 372,000 for the year to June 30 1994, when net assets were Pounds 210,000.
