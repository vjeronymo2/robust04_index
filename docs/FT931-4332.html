930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Peek expands in US with Dollars 4m purchase Peek, the traffic and field data systems concern, has reached agreement to acquire Signal Control and Signal Maintenance for Dollars 4.2m (Pounds 2.9m) which includes Dollars 2.08m cash. Combined sales for 1992 amounted to Dollars 24m. The purchase is from IASCO of California.
