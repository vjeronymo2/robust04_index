Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Hundreds of Jewish residents of Samaria are now demonstrating at the Morasha junction to protest the murder of Tzipora Sason by terrorists on the Trans-Samaria highway last night. Our correspondent Yafa Glick reports that a large police contingent is trying to prevent the demonstrators from blocking the road and that there are sporadic clashes between protesters and the police. Qiryat Arba' residents are currently blocking the main road from Hebron to Jerusalem to protest the recurrent stone throwing at Israeli vehicles. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that army and Border Police forces are at the scene and that Tzvi Katzover, the Qiryat 'Arba local council chairman, has been arrested.
