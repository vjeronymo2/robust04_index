910423 FT 23 APR 91 / Italians awarded telecom contract Italcable, the international telecommunications subsidiary of Italy's STET state-owned group, has won a contract to re-establish all Kuwait's telex links with the outside world, Haig Simonian reports from Milan. A value for the deal, which is for three years at the outset, has not been disclosed. However, Italcable says it also hopes to be involved in future work on developing Kuwait's telecommunications systems.
