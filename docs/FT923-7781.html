920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / Markets (At a glance): Murray Johnstone splits fund Murray Johnstone has split its Far Eastern fund into two, a Japan growth fund and a Pacific growth fund. Existing unitholders have been re-allocated units in both funds at a value equal to their previous single holding. The initial charge on both funds will be 1 per cent, and the annual management charge 1.5 per cent. However, until October 9, those Murray Far Eastern unitholders wishing to make further investments in either of the two funds will receive a 1 per cent discount. During the same period, holders of other Murray Johnstone unit trusts will get a 0.5 per cent discount on investments in either of the two new funds.
