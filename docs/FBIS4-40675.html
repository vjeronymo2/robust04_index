BFN [Text] As already reported, the CSCE Minsk Group delegation dealing with the Nagorno-Karabakh problem arrived in Stepanakert on 16 June headed by Jan Eliasson. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Press and Information Department reported that the CSCE delegation held a meeting on the same day with Karen Baburyan, acting chairman of the NKR Supreme Council; Robert Kocharyan, chairman of the State Defense Committee; and Foreign Minister Arkadiy Gukasyan. Jan Eliasson told reporters that regrettably the conflicting sides have different views regarding the future steps aimed at resolving the conflict. He said that the CSCE Minsk Group is thinking of continuing its efforts in cooperation with the Russian Federation to bring the positions of the conflicting sides nearer. The CSCE delegation visited the front lines and the prisoners of war held in Stepanakert this morning. The talks continued between the Minsk Group delegation and NKR leadership in the first part of the day. After the conclusion of the talks, Jan Eliasson and members of the delegation left Stepanakert for Yerevan in the afternoon. Jan Eliasson and his delegation were received by President Levon Ter-Petrosyan late tonight. Details of the meeting will be aired during our newscasts tomorrow.
