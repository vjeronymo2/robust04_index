940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Net assets decline at Fleming American The Fleming American Investment Trust, which seeks capital growth through a portfolio of mainly listed US stocks, had a fully diluted net asset value of 292.3p per share as at June 30. The figure compared with values of 326.4p at end-December and 295.3p at June 30 last year. Available revenue for the half year improved to Pounds 845,000 (Pounds 590,000), for earnings of 1.26p (0.89p) per share. The interim dividend, however, is cut from 0.85p to 0.4p.
