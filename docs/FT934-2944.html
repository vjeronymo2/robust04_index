931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa The market heavyweights, De Beers and Anglos, led the pack again, adding R3 at R96.50 and R15.50, or 8.9 per cent, at R193. Golds gained 75, or 3.7 per cent, at 2,064 and industrials rose 47 to 5,072, pushing the overall index up 101 to a new closing high of 4,491.
