910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / UK Company News In Brief KALAMAZOO has paid Pounds 2.15m cash for Datawork Computer Services, a supplier of a range of own products and services to the motor trade. Seller was Optim Computers, part of Systems Reliability Holdings. For 14 months to end-December 1990 Datawork returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 190,000 from turnover of Pounds 3.9m. ***** PLATON INTERNATIONAL: Directors warned that as a result of unfavourable trading conditions coupled with exceptional provisions made following a review of activities the company would report a deficit for the 12 months to end-March 1991. Taxable profits for the previous year amounted to Pounds 408,000.
