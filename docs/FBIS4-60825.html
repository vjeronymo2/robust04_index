BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Oleynyk] [Text] Moscow 29 April TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and visiting Irish Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Dick Spring, signed an intergovernment agreement on the prevention of double taxation on Friday. During a news conference the Russian foreign minister said that a declaration on principles of relations between Russia and Ireland signed in Dublin last October was not merely an expression of their good intentions, but it was an important starting point for concrete work. Kozyrev pointed out the successful development of Russo-Irish ties at the level of concrete business-projects. "We have created a system of multi-stage political consultations on problems of the European and world politics," Kozyrev said, adding that he was grateful to Ireland for its assistance in finalizing an agreement on full- scale cooperation with the European Union. Both Russian and Irish foreign ministers unanimously approved the level reached in the relations between the two countries, describing it as a very good and even excellent level and expressed confidence that bilateral relations would successfully develop.
