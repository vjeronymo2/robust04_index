BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar May 17 ITAR-TASS -- The Mongolian Foreign Ministry has delivered a note of protest to the Russian envoy to Mongolia in connection with the assault on, and robbery of Mongolian Vice-Consul in Buriatiya Batsuur and several persons who accompanied him near the Ubienny mountain pass near the town of Gusinoozersk on May 11. According to the note, the Mongols were robbed of cash, valuables and official documents. Batsuur suffered serious injuries, despite the fact that he had identified himself as a consular employee. The Mongolian Foreign Ministry expressed the hope that the criminals who violated the Mongolian consular employee's diplomatic immunity will be identified and brought to account, the press-service of the Mongolian Foreign Ministry said.
