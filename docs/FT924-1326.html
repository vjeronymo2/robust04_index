921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: TGI sharply lower at Pounds 0.35m Pre-tax profits of TGI, a designer and manufacturer of loudspeakers and related equipment, fell from Pounds 671,000 to Pounds 348,000 over the six months to September 30. The corresponding figure included exceptional gains of Pounds 451,000 on the disposal of the Havant property. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 16.1m (Pounds 16.82m). However, the prior year figure was boosted by an Indonesian contract. Earnings declined to 1.5p (2p) but the company is resuming interim dividends via a distribution of 0.5p.
