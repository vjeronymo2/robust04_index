BFN [Text] At the instigation of the Cuban Workers Federation, CTC, the Council of State presented the Order of Lazaro Pena, First Degree, to Louis Vianet, secretary general of the General Confederation of Labor, CGT, and a member of the Politburo of the French Communist Party. As an expression of the acknowledgement by Cuba's workers and people of his contributions within the CGT toward friendship and solidarity with the Cuban Revolution, said Pedro Ross Leal, member of the Politburo of the Communist Party of Cuba and secretary general of the CTC, may this order be proof of our friendship and our respect. This morning, Vianet and the delegation of French labor leaders accompanying him toured economic centers in Guines Municipality, Havana Province. At the Blas Roca Calderio UBPC [Basic Cooperative Production Unit], they donated 120,000 francs to the needs of Cuba's workers. With the check, they presented the CGT emblem, explaining that its two hands represent unity, also, in the French and Cuban workers' struggle against the Yankee blockade. The delegation attended the ceremony on Lenin Hill in Regla Municipality, Havana City Province, on the occasion of the anniversary of Lenin's birth -- the first ceremony to be held in the city of Havana in commemoration of 1 May. Vianet is at this moment holding a news conference on the situation of workers in France and on the international workers movement.
