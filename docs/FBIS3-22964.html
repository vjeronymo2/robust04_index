Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 14 Feb (MENA)--Three unknown gunmen fired at a minibus in the al-Sadat area in the city of Asyut, Upper Egypt. There were no casualties. According to initial reports, the minibus was carrying 10 passengers, two Egyptians and eight Romanian experts working at a cement factory in the region. The culprits fired at the minibus using handguns; they caused damage to the right side of the minibus. Officials of the Asyut Security Directorate, Public Security, and Criminal Investigations rushed to the scene. The prosecution office was informed to begin investigations.
