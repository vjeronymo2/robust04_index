Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Russian Army Only Half Ready for Combat"] [Text] MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS has learned information from reliable sources about the state of the Russian Federation Armed Forces' combat readiness. According to classified documents of the Russian Federation Defense Ministry, only 51 percent of Armed Forces personnel are capable of performing combat missions. Only one division in every military district is capable of taking part in combat operations, the Inspectorate for the Russian Federation Armed Forces' Combat Readiness reports. Only 20 percent of tanks in service can be considered combat ready. As for naval requirements -- putting a ship on full alert within 45 minutes -- only a few ships of the Russian Navy are able to fulfill them.
