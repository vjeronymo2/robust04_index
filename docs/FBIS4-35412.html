BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has said that the government is ready to acquaint parliament members with the latest political developments in the region, especially those related to the peace process. The prime minister was responding to a proposal by a parliament member who called for a special meeting between members of the lower house of parliament and the government. Dr. al-Majali said the parliament has not previously asked the government for such a meeting, adding that the government is always ready to meet parliamentarians' demands. The speaker of the lower house Mr. Tahir al-Masri said the meeting will be scheduled for next week. Mr. al-Masri was speaking this morning during a session of the house in the presence of the prime minister and cabinet ministers.
