931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / International Company News: CSI sells two loss-makers for Pounds 1.35m Cannon Street Investments, the mini-conglomerate, has sold its loss-making fork-lift truck businesses. CSI sold the fixed assets and stock of Cannon Material Handling and Wallright to Harvey Plant for Pounds 1.35m in cash. At the same time, CSI said the obligations of the two companies to various creditor leasing companies had been settled for Pounds 3.8m, roughly the capital liability in CMH and Wallright's accounts.
