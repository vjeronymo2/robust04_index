January 20, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

STAYING ON TOP OF THE SHAKE-UP;

MOTHER NATURE'S RUMBLINGS PUNCTUATE SOUTHLAND SHOWS

A survey of movie houses and major film distributors showed that no mishaps interrupted filmgoers, with theater personnel reporting nothing out of the usual during the quake. Chains such as Mann, Laemmle, Cineplex and AMC had no reports of power outages in any of their theaters. SHAUNA SNOW
