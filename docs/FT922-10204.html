920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Kidnap theory in Exxon riddle US law enforcement authorities believe the mysterious disappearance on Wednesday of a senior executive of Exxon could be a kidnapping, writes Alan Friedman in New York. But New Jersey police investigating the case remain baffled as to what happened to Mr Sidney Reso, the 57-year-old president of Exxon's international division.
