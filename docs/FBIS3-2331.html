Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report by Han Guangqin (7281 1639 2953): "According to Decision of Ministry of Finance, State Land Administration, and PLA General Logistics Department, Army Will Transfer Land Use Rights According to Law"] [Text] The Ministry of Finance, the State Land Administration, and the PLA General Logistics Department recently issued a joint "Circular on Issues Concerning Transfer of Surplus Military Land-Use Rights by the Army With Compensation." According to the circular, surplus military land must be developed in a planned way and under organized leadership. The army units which transfer their surplus military land must, as required by the relevant state regulations, register with the local land administration department at the county level and above for the change in the land-use right, so that they will be exempted from paying charges for transferring land. The proceeds from transferring and leasing surplus military land are collected by the General Logistics Department and then put into a special account and only used as designated. The "Circular" also provides that the state will not require the land users to donate to the budget adjustment fund and the fund for key construction projects including energy and transportation.
