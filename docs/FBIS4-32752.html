BFN [Text] Budapest, 24 June (MTI) -- The Hungarian Socialist Party and the Alliance of Free Democrats signed the two-party coalition agreement a little before 4.30 P.M. local time on Friday [24 June]. The document was signed by chairmen Gyula Horn and Ivan Peto. The parties agreed on the line-up of the new government. The cabinet list is as follows: Cabinet List Prime Minister Gyula Horn Minister of the Interior Gabor Kuncze (also coalition deputy prime minister) Minister of Finance Laszlo Bekesi Minister of Industry and Trade Laszlo Pal Minister of Transport, Telecommunications, Karoly Lotz and Water Management Minister of Agriculture Laszlo Lakos Minister of Labor Mrs. Magda Kosa Minister of Environment and Regional Ferenc Baja Policy Minister of Defence Gyorgy Keleti Minister of Foreign Affairs Laszlo Kovacs Minister of Justice Pal Vastagh Minister of Culture and Education Gabor Fodor Minister of Welfare Pal Kovacs Minister without portfolio in charge of Bela Katona National Security Office
