July 31, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

CENTER MAKES VAN AVAILABLE TO SENIORS

A van equipped with a wheelchair lift is available to senior citizens through the Oasis Senior Center.

The van is offered for medical appointments, grocery shopping and rides to the Oasis center. A $1 donation is requested per one-way ride, and reservations should be made at least two days in advance, center officials said. Grocery-shopping trips will be made on a pre-scheduled group basis.

For more information, call the Oasis Senior Center at (714) 644-3244.
