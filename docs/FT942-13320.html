940426 FT 26 APR 94 / University 'will create 2,500 jobs' The new university planned for Lincolnshire will create 2,500 jobs and boost the county's economy by Pounds 50m, Mr David Rossington, chief executive of the Lincolnshire Training and Enterprise Council, said yesterday. Construction will start in Lincoln next January and the first 500 students will be admitted in 1996. The institution will start life as a university college and become an independent university in the year 2000.
