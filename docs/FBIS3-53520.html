Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 17 Jan (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has attended a mammoth mass rally in the Salah-al-Din Governorate, which was held on the third anniversary of the treacherous 30-state aggression against our mujahid country. The citizens, who were carrying their personal weapons, applauded the president, may God preserve him, expressed their readiness to defend the achievements of the great revolution, and renewed their support for the brave leadership of President Saddam Husayn. Thousands of citizens greeted his excellency and voiced their full loyalty to the beloved one and the people's brave leader. The masses of the governorate expressed their anger over and denunciation of the cowardly aggressive action against our developing country and the building of a new Iraq. Muhammad Yunus al-Ahmad, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and official in charge of the party's organizations in the governorates of al-Anbar and Salah-al-Din, and Ghazi Ahmad Khattab, Salah-al-Din governor, attended the mass rally.
