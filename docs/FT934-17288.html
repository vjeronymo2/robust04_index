931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Helical Bar After a significant reduction in the interest charge, Helical Bar, the property group, reported a 75 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 2.22m for the half year to July 31. Turnover fell from Pounds 15m to Pounds 10.9m. At the operating level profits were down 35 per cent to Pounds 4.58m. Over the past 18 months the group has made substantial sales, thereby reducing borrowings, directors said. As a result gross rentals were lower at Pounds 5.6m (Pounds 7m). Net interest payable fell by Pounds 3.93m to Pounds 2.62m. Earnings per share worked through at 10.89p (5.01p) and the interim dividend is raised from 2.4p to 2.5p. Directors are forecasting a 20 per cent increase in the total.
