940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Exports spark Arcolectric rise Higher exports helped Arcolectric Holdings, the electrical and electronic components supplier, achieve 1993 pre-tax profits of Pounds 403,000, against Pounds 105,000. Turnover improved to Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 10.8m). Earnings per share came out at 5.57p (2.13p). A final dividend of 0.66p is proposed for a total of 1.215p (1.11p).
