BFN [Text] The National Assembly deputies held a plenary meeting this afternoon in the Ba Dinh Conference Hall under the direction of National Assembly Vice Chairman Nguyen Ha Phan. At the beginning of the meeting, Minister Do Quoc Sam, chairman of the State Planning Commission, on behalf of the government, read a report on the draft law on the promotion of domestic investment. Then, Nguyen Thanh Phong, vice chairman of the Economic and State Budget Committee, read an investigative report on the draft law on the promotion of domestic investment. Also at the sitting, National Defense Minister Doan Khue, on behalf of the government, read a report on the revised draft law on military obligations. Then, Pham Loi, vice chairman of the National Defense and Security Committee, read an investigative report on the revised draft law on military obligations. The National Assembly deputies will continue their work in the conference hall tomorrow, 4 June.
