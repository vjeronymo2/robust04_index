December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JORDAN TWINS LEAD UNLV PAST USC

The Jordan twins -- Pauline and Geannine -- combined to score 38 points and grab 24 rebounds to lead 13th-ranked Nevada Las Vegas to a 98-67 win over USC Saturday night in a women's basketball game.

Geannine led all scorers with 23 points while Pauline was the top rebounder with 13.

Freshman Joni Easterly scored 14 points and had five rebounds for USC (3-1).

UNLV is 5-1
