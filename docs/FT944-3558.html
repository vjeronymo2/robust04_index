941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / Welsh investment drive to be launched Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary, is today to launch a drive for Pounds 1bn of private-sector investment in south Wales in the next five years. Speaking in London, Mr Redwood will point to 20 sites from Ammanford to Ebbw Vale which have been earmarked for development. A blueprint to be outlined by Mr Redwood proposes the establishment of new urban settlements in Ebbw Vale, Llanelli, Swansea Vale, Brackla and Blaengwrach and the provision of 5,800 new homes.
