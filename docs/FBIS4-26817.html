BFN [Text] The Supreme Soviet delegation of the Republic of Tajikistan, headed by First Deputy Prime Minister Dostiyev, paid a visit to the provincial people's congress standing committee on the morning of 24 June. Sun Weiben, chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, met with dostiyev and his party. On behalf of the provincial people's congress standing committee, Sun Weiben expressed his warm welcome to the delegation. He said that the delegation's visit will lay a good foundation for establishing friendly contacts and economic and trade cooperation ties between Heilongjiang Province and Tajikistan. Sun Weiben introduced to the guests the province's political and economic situation. He said: Tajikistan and Heilongjiang are economically complementary. Thus, both sides have great potentials for developing economic and trade cooperation. I hope that both sides will be able to strengthen contacts, enhance mutual understanding, and promote cooperation. dostiyev thanked the provincial people's congress for its cordial reception. He said: The people in Tajikistan cherish a deep affection for the Chinese people. So, we should strengthen cooperation and make common development. Qi Guiyuan, Du Xianzhong, Du Dianming, and Liu Hanwu, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; and Liu Fengyan, secretary general of the provincial people's congress standing committee, were present at the meeting.
