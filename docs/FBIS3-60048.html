Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Fewer Drug-Related Deaths"] [Text] Good news from the Federal Office of Criminal Investigation in Wiesbaden: The number of drug-related deaths dropped in 1993. A total of 1,458 drug-addicts died by 30 November, which is 412 fewer than in the same period in 1992 (1,870). During the whole of 1992, 2,099 people died of drug consumption (2,125 in 1991). A narcotics agent gave the following reason for the decline: "Heroin has become cleaner. It is no longer diluted with other substances so often."
