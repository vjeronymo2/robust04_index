930406 FT 06 APR 93 / World News In Brief: Quinlivan remanded in Dublin IRA suspect Nessan Quinlivan, 29, was remanded in custody on four firearms charges by Dublin's anti-terrorist Special Criminal Court. He will next appear in court on April 20. No mention was made of any Scotland Yard application for his extradition to Britain in connection with conspiracy charges and his escape from Brixton jail two years ago.
