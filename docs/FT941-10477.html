940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Utility Cable starts trading The Pounds 8.75m cash and paper reverse takeover of the JP Fitzpatrick companies by Baillie Gifford Technology, the shell investment trust, was approved yesterday at an egm. A change of name, to Utility Cable, became effective on Monday. The meeting also agreed the placing, rights issue, subscription agreement and issue of management warrants announced on January 14. The admission of the 1p ordinary shares and warrants to the Official List has become effective and dealings began yesterday.
