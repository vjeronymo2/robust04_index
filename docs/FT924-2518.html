921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / Security Council authorises battalion of UN forces for Macedonia The Security Council last night authorised a battalion of UN peace-keepers for Macedonia to prevent fighting in the former Yugoslavia from igniting a wider Balkan war, Reuter reports from the United Nations. The resolution, passed 15-0, endorses proposals by Secretary General Boutros Boutros Ghali for 700 troops plus 35 military observers and 26 civilian police to 'stand between forces that might otherwise clash'.
