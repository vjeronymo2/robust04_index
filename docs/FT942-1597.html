940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: MEPC to buy Irish shopping centre MEPC, the UK's second largest property company, has agreed to buy the Castlecourt shopping centre in Belfast from John Laing, the construction and housebuilding group, for initial and deferred payments expected to exceed Pounds 80.5m. MEPC will pay an initial sum of not less than Pounds 72.5m next month, and a further minimum of Pounds 1.65m in February.
