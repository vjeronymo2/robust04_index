930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / Liberal Democrats at Torquay: Delegates approve emergency resolution condeming BR cuts DELEGATES overwhelmingly approved an emergency resolution condemning the recent cuts in services announced by British Rail, attributing them to pressure exerted by the government as it prepared for rail privatisation. The party leadership accepted the need for a properly funded rail network as an essential part of the infrastructure of the regions, and for the protection of the environment and the stimulation of the national economy.
