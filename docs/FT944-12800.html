941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / Arts: (Openings): Saint-Etienne Admirers of French composer Jules Massenet will be flocking to the city of his birth on Friday for a rare staging of 'Panurge', his Rabelais-inspired operatic comedy. It forms the centrepiece of this year's Massenet Festival, which also includes concert performances of 'Le Cid', recitals by young French singers and a special Massenet exhibition. The driving force behind the festival is the conductor Patrick Fournillier.
