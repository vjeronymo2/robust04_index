BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng today called for providing a better environment and mechanism for the nurturing of talents. Li Peng made the remarks at a ceremony held to commend excellent entrepreneurs who have developed notable inventions. He said that the realization of modernization in China depends on scientific and technological progress. He said that it is significant to reward these inventive entrepreneurs. The premier spoke highly of their efforts in transferring scientific and technological fruits into productive forces, making new products for the demands of the market and enhancing economic efficiency in their enterprises. He called on people in various fields to set great store by new inventions, including large, medium and small inventions, so as to contribute to the prosperity of the country. Other Chinese leaders Ni Zhifu, Li Peiyao, Song Jian and Luo Gan also attended the ceremony. At the ceremony, ten inventors were granted the title of "excellent entrepreneur with inventive achievements" and 15 were honored with nomination prizes.
