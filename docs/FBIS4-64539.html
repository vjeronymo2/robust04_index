BFN [Text] Istanbul, May 2 (A.A.) -- Parliament Speaker Husamettin Cindoruk on Monday [2 May] said that relations with France are at a perfect level in all fields and this also reflects interparliamentary relations. He was speaking to reporters at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport before his departure for France where he will be the official guest of his French counterpart Philippe Seguin. During his stay Cindoruk is also scheduled to meet French President Francois Mitterrand, Deputy Parliament Speaker Eric Raoult [name as received] and a reporter from LE FIGARO newspaper on May 2. He will also meet several other senior French officials on May 3- 4, and attend a ceremony which will be organized at the French Parliament. Cindoruk said the visit will favorably contribute to bilateral relations. He is expected back in Turkey on May 4.
