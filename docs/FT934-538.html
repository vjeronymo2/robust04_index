931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / BT contract will save NHS Pounds 15m A contract between the National Health Service and British Telecommunications will save the service up to Pounds 15m a year. The contract was made under BT's Option 2000 plan, which gives discounts of between 14 per cent and 16 per cent to users with telecoms expenditure of more than Pounds 250,000.
