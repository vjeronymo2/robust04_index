941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Copymore in talks Copymore, the USM-quoted office equipment supplier said that it had been in preliminary negotiations with a number of potential buyers of the company. However, Mr Jeff Godbold, joint chairman, said that no official offers had been received. Shares in Copymore have risen strongly throughout this week. They closed yesterday at 180p, up 10p on the day.
