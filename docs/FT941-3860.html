940314 FT 14 MAR 94 / Company News (This week): Nordbanken - Other companies A row over the extent of state aid doled out to keep Sweden's Nordbanken afloat last year is set to deepen as the bank, cleansed of its bad loans, pumped full of new capital and strengthened by its takeover of a similarly laundered Gota Bank, announces 1993 profits on Thursday comfortably higher than any of its rivals.
