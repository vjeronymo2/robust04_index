November 6, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

CLEAN AIR GROUP TO DISCUSS BUSH PLAN

The Orange County Clean Air Assn. will meet this week to discuss President Bush's clean air plan and the congressional bills amending the Clean Air Act.

The topic of the meeting, set for Wednesday at the Garden Grove Community Meeting Center, is, "Will We Be Getting Cleaner Air or Just Hot Air?"

Speaking will be Mark Abramowitz, program director of the Coalition for Clean Air in Los Angeles, and Carolyn Green, deputy executive officer for public affairs for the South Coast Air Quality Management District.

The public is invited to attend. Admission is free and the meeting will be held at the community center, 11300 Stanford Ave., from 7:30 to 9 p.m.
