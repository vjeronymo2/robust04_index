April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

GROUND BROKEN FOR FONTANA RETAIL CENTER

Ground has been broken for the $20.6-million South Ridge Plaza, Fontana, a 125,000-square-foot shopping center at Cherry and Live Oak avenues and Village Drive.

A project of Crossroads Development, it will be anchored by a Ralphs supermarket and a Thrifty Drug.
