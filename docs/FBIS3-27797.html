Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 1 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here today Algerian ambassador to Vietnam Mr. Maamar Ahmed who paid him a courtesy visit. Ambassador M. Ahmed expressed his pleasure at the expansion of the relationship and cooperation between Vietnam and Algeria in the fields of healthcare, education and university. He held that in current international situation Algeria and Vietnam should further develop such relations in the interest of each country, and for peace, friendship and development in the world. He wished that Vice President Nguyen Thi Binh's coming visit to Algeria would be successful, thus ushering in a new phase of the friendship and cooperation between the two countries. For his part, Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet expressed his thanks to the state and people of Algeria for their assistance to the Vietnamese people. He affirmed that Vietnam always ataches importance to the consolidation and development of fraternal friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
