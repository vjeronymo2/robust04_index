BFN [Text] A news just coming in says: In furtherance of the ongoing peace process in Liberia, the leader of the National Patriotic Front of Liberia [NPFL], Mr. Charles Gankay Taylor, has opted to send a high-powered delegation to Monrovia to hold discussions with the Liberian Peace Council [LPC], aimed at resolving the conflict in Rivercess and parts of Sinoe County. In a press release today, Mr. Taylor said while the NPFL is fully aware that the LPC is not a party to the Cotonou Peace Accord, yet because of the love he has for the Liberian people and his desire for peace and national reconciliation, a delegation will leave Gbarnga shortly to hold discussions in Monrovia. The NPFL leader is, therefore, urging the special representative to Liberia and head of UNOMIL [UN Observer Mission to Liberia], Ambassador Gordon-Somers, to provide full security protection and sponsorship for the ensuing talks.
