Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Costa Rican Foreign Minister Bernd Niehaus has said he will reintegrate Cuba into the OAS if he is elected OAS secretary general. In statements to the Colombian radio network Radio Cadena Nacional, Niehaus said, regarding OAS Charter principles, Cuba is a member of the American continent and should be a full member of the OAS. Niehaus expressed confidence about winning the OAS secretary general position, and he revealed he anticipates 22 votes in his favor. Only 18 votes are required to win.
