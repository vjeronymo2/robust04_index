December 2, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Children's Museum at La Habra opens its new 8,000-square-foot wing today at 301 S. Euclid St., La Habra. Permanent displays on science, theater and preschool activities will be offered. Hours: 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Admission: free. Information: (213) 905-9793.
