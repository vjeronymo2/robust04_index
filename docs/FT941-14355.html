940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / International Company News: Gyllenhammer to step down as vice-chairman of SE-Banken Mr Pehr Gyllenhammar is stepping down as a vice-chairman of Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken. 'It is natural that after leaving Volvo, I should also leave the SE-Banken board so that I remain free for the future,' he said. Mr Gyllenhammar has been a board member at SE-Banken since 1979, and vice-chairman since 1991. He is expected to step down at the bank's annual general meeting in April.
