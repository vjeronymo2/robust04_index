BFN [Text] His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id received at his office in Bayt al-Barakah Palace today Robert Pelletreau, U.S. assistant secretary of state for Near East and North African Affairs. The meeting was attended by Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs, and the U.S. ambassador to the Sultanate of Oman. Talks dealt with means of reinforcing the close bilateral relations and aspects of cooperation in all fields. The two sides also discussed developments in the Arab and international arenas and means of achieving security and stability in the region to serve the interests of all its peoples.
