920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Banner Homes Banner Homes Group, the USM-quoted construction and property group, reported a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 688,000 for the year to March 31 compared with Pounds 732,000 previously. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 6.83m (Pounds 6.82m). Losses per share were 3.9p (5.2p). The group plans to divest its industrial and commercial properties.
