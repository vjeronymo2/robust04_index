Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered that pensioners whose service exceeds 25 years and whose total wages and allowances are currently less than 500 dinars be granted an additional cost of living allowance totaling 25 percent of his monthly wages and allowances. A circular issued by the Presidential Office said that the order of the leader president, may God protect him, shall take effect as of 1 February.
