BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev May 16 ITAR-TASS -- Ukraine needs ten years and at least six to eight billion dollars to close down the Chernobyl nuclear power station, Ukrainian Vice-Premier Valeriy Shmarov told a press conference here today. He added that the final decision on the Chernobyl nuclear power station is expected to be made at the Group of Seven countries' summit this summer.
