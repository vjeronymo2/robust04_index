BFN [Excerpt] A responsible source at the Democratic Republic of Yemen Defense Ministry has issued the following statement to the YEMENI NEWS AGENCY, Aden: Four fighter aircraft belonging to the military ruling regime in Sanaa at 1200 raided a number of vital economic installations and residential quarters in Aden Governorate, destroying a number of houses belonging to innocent citizens. Five citizens were martyred, including three children. Our air defenses fought back, shooting down one of the aircraft, which citizens witnessed falling into the Gulf of Aden. [passage omitted]
