920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Gresham Telecomp at Pounds 294,000 Gresham Telecomputing reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 294,000 for the six months to April 30 on turnover of Pounds 3.95m. Pro-forma figures for the six months to March 31 1991 from the combined results of Gresham Telecomputing for the six months and Gresham Computer Holdings for five months showed pre-tax profits of Pounds 293,000 on turnover of Pounds 4.89m. Earnings were 0.57p and there is no interim dividend. The company said that there had been a reduction in revenues as a result of tough trading conditions and concentrating on higher margin products.
