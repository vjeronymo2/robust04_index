941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo finished 2.1 per cent stronger in moderate trade after equity prices were led forward by Telebras, the state telecom, which posted positive results for the first nine months. The Bovespa index rose 1,029 to 48,850, while turnover amounted to RDollars 267.3m. Telebras preferred put on 5.0 per cent at RDollars 42 after the group posted a better than expected RDollars 710.8m net profit in the January-September period.
