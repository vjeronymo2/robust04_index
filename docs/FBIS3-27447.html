Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 22 (XINHUA) -- The Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) in Beijing hosted a banquet here this evening to mark the 35th anniversary of the signing of the agreements of cultural and civil aviation cooperation between DPRK and China. Among those attending the banquet were charge d'affaires ad interim of the DPRK Embassy Pae Yong-chae and Chinese Vice-Minister of Culture Liu Deyou.
