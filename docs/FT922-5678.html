920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Fleming High net asset value lower The Fleming High Income Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 88.7p at April 30 against 91.4p a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months improved from Pounds 1.53m to Pounds 1.8m for earnings per share of 5.88p (5.77p). An unchanged fourth interim dividend of 1.45p is declared for a total of 5.8p (5.75p).
