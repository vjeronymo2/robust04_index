940416 FT 16 APR 94 / Health regulation breach admitted A woman who was appointed to chair two health authorities has resigned from one of the jobs after the government admitted that the law prohibited people from chairing two health authorities at once. Mrs Anne-Marie Nelson, a former Tory councillor, has been chairman of West Kent Health Authority, where she is to step down, and the Special Health Services Authority, on a combined salary of Pounds 33,000 for nine months.
