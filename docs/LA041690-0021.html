April 16, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

AL DAVIS

So, Al Davis will talk about staying in L.A. if he gets $15 million (Metro, April 7)? This is on top of the $10 million from Irwindale to talk about going there!

When I was dating, my boyfriend used to give my little brother a quarter to "go away and leave us alone."

If everyone who would like Al Davis to go to Oakland and leave Los Angeles alone would send him a quarter, maybe he'd get the message in the only language he seems to understand -- money.

KAY BURK

Duarte
