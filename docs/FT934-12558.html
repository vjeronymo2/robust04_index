931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Legal settlement boosts Olives Property to Pounds 0.7m BOOSTED by an Pounds 813,000 exceptional item representing settlement of a legal dispute, pre-tax profits of Olives Property improved from Pounds 141,583 to Pounds 720,392 in the six months to end-June. Turnover, including the exceptional profit, increased from Pounds 1.89m to Pounds 2.15m. Comparatives were restated to comply with FRS 3. Following a Pounds 2.55m net open offer and placing, the property developer installed a new management team in mid-June. As part of the transition the company's two residential sites were sold for Pounds 1.1m. Mr Anthony Grant, chairman, said UK property returns remained higher than borrowing rates, and Olives was exploring opportunities for investment to establish a secure income and capital base for the future. Interest payable fell to Pounds 122,682 (Pounds 196,862). Olives had zero gearing at the period end and net assets stood at Pounds 5.5m. Earnings per share improved to 4.74p (1.36p); there is, however, no interim dividend against 1.5p last time.
