940927 FT 27 SEP 94 / Lecturers' union urges conciliation The ATL lecturers' union yesterday called for the dispute between further education lecturers and college principals over new contracts to be placed in the hands of an independent conciliator. But Mr John Akker, general secretary of the Natfhe lecturers' union, which has held several strikes over the issue, said conciliation would be no use.
