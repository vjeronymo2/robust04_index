August 3, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Eldorado Bancorp, a Laguna Hills holding company for Tustin-based Eldorado Bank, reported net income of $826,000 for the second quarter, a 10% increase over net income of $750,000 for last year's second quarter. Quarterly revenue grew 11% to $7.2 million this year from $6.5 million last year. For the first six months, the company posted net income of $1.8 million, a 28% increase over $1.4 million in the same period last year. Revenue for the period rose 20% to $14.4 million this year from $12 million last year.
