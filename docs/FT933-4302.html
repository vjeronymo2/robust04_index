930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / UK Company News: IDV sets up Chinese venture In a further expansion move into the Asia Pacific region, International Distillers & Vintners, the drinks sector of Grand Metropolitan, has set up a new company to market and distribute IDV brands in China. IDV has appointed Mr TY Wong as managing director of the new company, International Distillers China, who will have his head office in Hong Kong, with three new representative offices in Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou. Mr John McGrath, a GrandMet director and IDV's chief executive, said that with a population of 1.2bn, China had a total spirits market of about 50m cases a year, of which international spirit brands account for 700,000 cases. This sector increased by 40 per cent last year which, he said, demonstrated enormous potential for future growth. Last year IDV set up its own and joint venture distribution companies covering Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand and South Korea.
