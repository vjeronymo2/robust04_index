930428 FT 28 APR 93 / Top table Delegates at the IoD conference tuck in to their lunch boxes yesterday. On the menu were asparagus crowns, fillet of Scottish salmon in a pastry trellis, red apple waldorf salad, cucumber and chervil salad and a vegetable bouquet, followed by raspberry delice, Somerset brie and red Leicester. The meal was washed down with non-alcoholic elderflower champagne
