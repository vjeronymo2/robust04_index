Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has resumed his talks with Uri Savir, Israeli Foreign Ministry director general, at a working lunch hosted by 'Arafat for the Israeli delegation members at his place of residence in the al-Andalus Palace in Heliopolis. Palestinian and Israeli officials declined to give any details on the talks.
