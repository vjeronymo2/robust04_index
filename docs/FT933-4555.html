930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: EFM Income Over the three months ended July 31 1993 net asset value of ordinary shares at EFM Income Trust rose from 45.9p to 50.2p. Similarly, the value of zero dividend preference shares was ahead from 43.7p to 44.9p. On the revenue side, first quarter pre-tax revenue fell from Pounds 303,000 to Pounds 206,000, with earnings per share working through at 1.1p (1.5p). The first interim dividend is cut from 1.2p to 1p.
