930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Interest boost for Rosebys ROSEBYS, the specialist retailer of household textiles which came to the market 12 months ago, achieved an increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.17m to Pounds 2.34m for 1992. Turnover rose to Pounds 45m (Pounds 42.8m). The pre-tax figure was helped by a swing from interest payable of Pounds 303,000 to interest income of Pounds 17,000. Earnings slipped to 7.9p (8.9p) reflecting the increased capital resulting from the flotation. A final dividend of 3p makes a total of 3.9p.
