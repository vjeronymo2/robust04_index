931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: British Inv Trust British Investment Trust raised net asset value per share by 10.6 per cent to 219p at the end of September, against 198p six months earlier. Attributable profits improved to Pounds 8.63m (Pounds 8.04m) for the six months and earnings per share under FRS 3 were 2.77p (2.58p) per share. The interim dividend is raised to 2p (1.9p).
