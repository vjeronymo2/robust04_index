BFN [Text] The Supreme Kenges of the Kyrgyz Republic adopted a decision to incorporate the Susamyr rayon of Naryn oblast into the Chu oblast. The decision reads: The Susamyr rayon of Naryn oblast is to be incorporated with Chu oblast. The account of Susamyr rayon is to be withdrawn from Naryn oblast. In accordance with the Kyrgyz Republic's land code Article 47 [words indistinct] the change of border between Naryn and Chu oblasts should be carried out by the Naryn and Chu oblast soviets. This issue should be presented for examination by the Supreme Kenges of the republic.
