930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / UK Company News: The Property Trust The Property Trust yesterday reported a swing back into the black with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 426,000 for the six months to end-September. Mr Anthony Cheng, chairman of the property investment and development company, said the outcome exceeded the forecast of not less than Pounds 400,000 made at the time of the rights issue last October, and compared with a loss of Pounds 6.3m. The company, which transferred from the USM to the official list last year, incurred a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 7.95m in the twelve months to end-March. Turnover in the latest period was Pounds 1.65m, against Pounds 730,000. Earnings per share worked through at 5.61p (83.2p losses).
