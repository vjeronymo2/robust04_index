940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Biocure reduces deficit to Pounds 0.7m Biocure Holdings, the USM-quoted healthcare products group, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.3m to Pounds 715,000 in the year to June 30. Turnover nearly doubled from Pounds 1.92m to Pounds 3.77m. For the three months to June, Hypoguard, the main trading subsidiary, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 69,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.27m and the company said profitability continued to be satisfactory in the current year. Losses per share were cut to 2.12p (4.16p).
