Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow February 3 TASS -- The Russian Federation and the ex-Yugoslavian Republic of Macedonia have established diplomatic relations on the level of ambassadors. The establishment of full-scale diplomatic relations is not directed against interests of any other third state, and will contribute to maintaining friendly relations with all the countries of the region, Foreign Ministry Spokesman Mikhail Demurin told a briefing on Thursday [3 February]. It would also contribute to "promote stability in the Balkans as a whole", the diplomat stressed noting that Russia is ready to help solve problems between Athens and Skopje upon their request.
