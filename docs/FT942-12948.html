940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Barlows losses cut to Pounds 523,000 Barlows, the property investment and development group, cut pre-tax losses to Pounds 523,000 in 1993. This compared with a restated Pounds 2.54m last time, which was after a Pounds 1.61m provision for losses on disposal of investment properties. Turnover amounted to Pounds 1.57m (Pounds 1.86m). Losses per share were 1.94p (10.65p) and no dividend is declared - last year's interim was 0.825p but no final was paid. The company said it expected to return to profit this year and resume dividend payments. Net assets at the end of 1993 were unchanged at 69.8p.
