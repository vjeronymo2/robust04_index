940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / UN makes Balkan cash appeal The United Nations yesterday appealed for Dollars 532m (Pounds 364.3m) for humanitarian aid from now until the year-end for the 4m people in ex-Yugoslavia. The UN Department of Humanitarian Affairs (DHA), on behalf of 11 UN agencies, said the priority was still 'basic survival needs for the displaced and the destitute' but the UN system was primed to shift efforts to rehabilitation and reconstruction if political developments allowed. The UN refugee agency, which is asking for Dollars 236m for the remainder of the year, is spending humanitarian assistance in former Yugoslavia.
