BFN [Text] Here is a report from Route 68-western Siem Reap battlefield: 1. On 24 April, our forces attacked and completely liberated Angkor Chum District town. We seized a DK-75 and a DK-82. 2. Also on 24 April, we completely liberated Kouk Dong commune and 18 villages around this commune. We also completely liberated 12 villages in Nokor Pheas commune. The people are very happy for having freed themselves from the claws of the archcriminals communist Vietnamese and two-headed government. Villagers have begun to till their lands for rice paddies. They are no longer forced to serve as puppet soldiers. 3. On the same day, we attacked the invading forces at Chongkal, killing two of them and wounding three others.
