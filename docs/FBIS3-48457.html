Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea sent a message of greetings on January 20 to Michael Hicks upon his reelection as general secretary of the Britannic Communist Party. Believing that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties will further develop in the future in the common idea for socialism, the message wished him success in his responsible work for defending the interests of the working masses including the working class and expanding and strengthening the party ranks.
