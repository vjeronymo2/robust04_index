BFN [Text] Derek William Auret, head of the Africa and Middle East Desk at the South African Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Amman today on a visit to Jordan lasting three days. During the visit, he will meet with Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs. He will also hold talks on regional developments and bilateral relations. He was welcomed by the head of the Political Department at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the South African charge d'affaires in Amman.
