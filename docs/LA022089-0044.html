February 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

QUOTES

Here are some quotes I'd like to read in the paper:

-- "We can't loan your country any more money. You see, we're in debt ourselves."

-- "We're going to reduce the debt, not just the deficit. Reducing the deficit only means we're overspending a little less than last year."

-- "We can't just give you a welfare check -- would you be willing to work if excellent, free child-care is provided?"

-- "Just because I've been selling drugs, you're telling me that you're going to lock me up and throw away the key?"

-- "What do you mean that owning an assault rifle is a felony?"

-- "As your congressman, I'm going to start listening to what the people want instead of the NRA."

-- "I'm guilty. I feel that I owe something to society and do not wish to cause the huge expense of a trial."

-- "We are not going to build that $8-million military plane, but instead we are going to use the funds for low-income housing for working families."

ALLEN DAVIS

Newport Beach
