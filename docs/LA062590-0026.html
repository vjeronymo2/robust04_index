June 25, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB;

MAN VS. MICROCHIP

I hit the (chalk)board, making a $37 overall profit when my $30 exacta from Iroquois Park to Saddle Up in the fourth race paid $7.80.

Not much, but I want to make hay until the computer's sun shines again -- probably Thursday.

Bob's Sunday Bankroll: $1,946

Sunday's Profit: $37

Current Bankroll: $1,983

The computer, which previously had bet only allowance races or better, lost $40 when it went slumming in the first two races -- both claimers -- Sunday. It would have lost more, but Ki-Nobre scratched from the second.

Computer's Sunday Bankroll: $1,619.50

Sunday's Loss: $40

Current Bankroll: $1,579.50
