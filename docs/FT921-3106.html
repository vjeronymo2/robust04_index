920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Bracknell school refurbished Two companies within the JOHN LAING CONSTRUCTION GROUP have won contracts worth Pounds 12m. Laing Wessex has won Pounds 9m worth of orders for work at a Bracknell School and a hospital in Reading. The largest is the Pounds 6.9m contract awarded by the Royal County of Berkshire to improve facilities at Easthampstead Park Comprehensive School. Five classroom blocks will be constructed and four buildings refurbished. The contract also provides for a pavilion, expressive arts block, gymnasium and new outdoor synthetic sports pitches and tennis courts. Laing North West has won Pounds 3m worth of orders for refurbishment work at Manchester's Christie Hospital and an extension to Chester District General Hospital.
