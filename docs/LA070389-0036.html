July 3, 1989, Monday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: FACES, PLACES

Newsman Steve Dunleavy of Fox Television's "The Reporters" has emerged after spending 48 hours posing as an inmate on Death Row at a Louisiana prison, his identity known only to the warden. Dunleavy also interviewed an official executioner there. His report on the experience is due to air July 15. The program also includes an interview Dunleavy had with a condemned inmate, Sean Patrick Flannagan, in Nevada State Prison just hours before Flannagan was put to death by lethal injection. ALEENE MacMINN
