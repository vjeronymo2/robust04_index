Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks between His Majesty King Husayn and Yasir 'Arafat, state of Palestine president, started in Amman today. His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan attended. 'Arafat arrived in Amman this morning on a short visit to Jordan during which he will brief His Majesty King Husayn on the details of the Palestinian-Israeli agreement reached in Cairo yesterday. He will also hold talks with his majesty on the latest developments in the peace process. 'Arafat was received at Queen Alia International Airport by Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; Dr. Jawad al-'Anani, minister of state for cabinet affairs and information minister; Dr. Fawwaz Abu-al-Ghanam, minister of state and acting minister of state for foreign affairs; and Dr. Fayiz al-Tarawinah, Jordan's ambassador in Washington and head of the Jordanian delegation to the peace talks. He was also received by al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim, state of Palestine ambassador in Amman and senior Embassy staff. 'Arafat is accompanied on his visit by Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, Ahmad Quray', Faysal al-Husayni, and Akram Haniyah.
