BFN [Text] In a meeting with a delegation from the Islamic Republic of Iran, Azerbaijan's President Heydar Aliyev called for greater expansion of ties between the two countries. According to a Central News Unit report from Baku, members of a delegation representing Iran led by Abdolalizadeh, the governor of the East Azarbaijan Province, which has been visiting Baku since Wednesday 6 April, met and conferred with Aliyev on Thursday afternoon [7 April]. In the meeting, the president of Azerbaijan said: We have been cooperating with Iran for a long time and that is indicative of the fact that relations between Iran and Azerbaijan are widening.
