941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / UK Company News: A&C Black up 27% A&C Black, the publisher, lifted pre-tax profits by 27 per cent from Pounds 244,000 to Pounds 310,000 in the six months to June 30. The increase was helped by a 'further small improvement' in publishing margins, the company said. Turnover rose 9 per cent to Pounds 3.66m (Pounds 3.38m). The interim dividend is unchanged at 4.25p, payable from earnings per share of 13.9p, up from 10.6p.
