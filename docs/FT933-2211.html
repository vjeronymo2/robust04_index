930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / UK Company News: New City Comml asset value at 111.6p New City and Commercial Investment Trust, raised net asset value by 15 per cent to 111.6p per share over the period from February 8 to July 31 1993. The trust was formed as part of the restructuring of City and Commercial Investment Trust, which came to the end of its life at the end of January 1993. Net revenue for the period ended July 31 was Pounds 253,000 and earnings per share came to 2.004p. An interim dividend of 1.3p has been declared.
