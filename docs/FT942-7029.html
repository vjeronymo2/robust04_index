940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Purcell music is sold for Pounds 276,500 A volume containing the only keyboard music by Henry Purcell in his own hand known to have survived sold for Pounds 276,500 at Sotheby's yesterday. The price was at the lower end of the forecast. Letters by the psychiatrist Carl Jung sold for Pounds 29,900 while a letter from Flaubert to Baudelaire realised Pounds 10,350.
