BFN [Summary from poor reception] The law-enforcement bodies of North Kazakhstan Oblast report that due to the worsening economic situation there was a increase by a factor of 2.5 in the number of crimes in the first quarter of 1994 compared to the same period in 1993. Of 1,950 crimes in this period, 380 were committed by young people in their twenties and 321 crimes were committed by unemployed people.
