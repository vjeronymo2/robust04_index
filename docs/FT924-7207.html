921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / International Company News: Kuoni forecasts profits growth REISEBURO Kuoni, Switzerland's largest travel agency, expects net profits to rise sharply in 1992 to between SFr35m and SFr40m (Dollars 24.30m-Dollars 27.70m), thanks to higher sales and cost cutting. Last year, the group's net profit fell 21 per cent to SFr21.3m. Sales in the first nine months were close to SFr2bn, up 18 per cent.
