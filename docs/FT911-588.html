910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Appointments: Senior executives at TI Group TI GROUP has made two senior executive appointments. Mr David P. Lillycrop has been promoted to group company secretary. He was deputy group secretary, and succeeds Mr Denis Saunders who continues on the main board. Mr Lillycrop joined the group in October 1989 from Quaker Oats where he was company secretary. Mr Jan Valdinger has joined TI Group as director of corporate services, with particular responsibility for legal affairs, and merger and acquisition activity. He was with Standard Chartered Merchant Bank. ***** COGNOS INC has appointed Mr John McIntyre as vice-president, European operations, based at Bracknell. ***** R. MEARS & CO, insurance and reinsurance brokers, has made the following appointments: Mr Alec R. Page, chairman; Ms Mairi M. Mayhew and Ms Joan M. Hefford, directors; Mr Darren P. Doherty and Mr Charles C. O'Sullivan, associate directors.
