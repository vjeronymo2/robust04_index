920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Helene raises Pounds 4.6m through placing Helene, the clothing distributor, is raising Pounds 4.58m net through a placing of 24m shares at 20 1/2 p. Existing shareholders will be able to apply for the offer on a 1-for-3 basis. The shares closed at 22 1/2 p last night - a fall of 1 1/2 p. The company said the present year had started well with sales for the first four months ahead of the corresponding period. The proceeds of the issue would be used for working capital to finance the increased turnover.
