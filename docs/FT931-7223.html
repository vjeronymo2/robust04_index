930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Oliver Resources cuts deficit Oliver Resources, the Dublin-based oil and gas company quoted on the USM, cut pre-tax losses to IPounds 184,000 (Pounds 190,000) in the year to October 31. This compared with IPounds 12.9m previously, which included an exceptional charge of IPounds 12.1m. Losses per share came out at 0.2p (14.7p).
