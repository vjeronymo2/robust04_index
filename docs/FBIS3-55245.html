Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko has met with the coordinating council of the fund for transforming the economy. The meeting discusses the Economics Ministry's proposals for using this year's money. The fund's goal is to support with investments reforms aimed at creating a socially oriented market economy and raising its competitiveness. The meeting decided to channel the abovementioned funds into constructing and commissioning priority spheres of the economy, such as the power industry, the oil extraction and gas industry, nonferrous metallurgy, and the production of consumer goods. The meeting also decided to conserve a number of construction sites which cannot bring an immediate profit. When finalizing the Cabinet of Ministry's draft statute, the Economics Ministry, along with other ministries and departments, should take into consideration foreign investments which will be attracted with this purpose in mind.
