BFN [Text] Several shots were fired this evening at the car of Sami Abu-Samahdanah, one of the Fatah leaders in the Gaza Strip. The bullets struck the vehicle, but Abu-Samahdanah was unharmed. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that Abu-Samahdanah lodged a complaint with the Palestinian-Israeli coordinating committee. He asserted that the shots had been fired from inside the Jewish settlement of Netzarim, in an attempt to assassinate him.
