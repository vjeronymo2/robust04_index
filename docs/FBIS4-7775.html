BFN [Text] Cajnice, 6 Apr (TANJUG)-- Joint forces of the army of the Srpska republic this morning continued offensive actions from the direction of Cajnice and Foca towards Drina in an attempt to liberate Serb villages in the area of Podkozara, the command of the Srpska army in Cajnice has announced. The turnabout in fighting in the Gorazde, Foca, and Cajnice triangle took place yesterday afternoon when the Serb fighters smashed the eight-day offensive of the Muslim army, which is in a panicked retreat towards the River Drina. Without suffering any losses, the Serb fighters liberated the village of Biljin, half a km away from Vitkovici, in an unstoppable attack from the direction of Paljika and Trebinjaca yesterday evening, the command says. Military police are deployed on the bridge in Vitkovici and are preventing the retreating Muslim soldiers from crossing to the left bank of the Drina River. The command of the joint forces of the Srpska army says that the Muslim army yesterday suffered huge losses in manpower, thus denying reports by Muslim media about civilian casualties.
