920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Control of overheads boosts Arcolectric Control of overheads helped Arcolectric (Holdings), the electrical component maker, to lift pre-tax profits from Pounds 164,000 to Pounds 378,000 in the six months to June 30. Earnings were 3.96p (1.72p) and the interim dividend is raised to 0.555p (0.53p).
