931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / UK Company News: River/Merc Extra lifts assets 53% Net asset value per ordinary share at River & Mercantile Extra Income Trust advanced 53 per cent - from 81.07p to 123.79p - over the 12 months to September 30. The value for the zero dividend preference shares - issued in June to raise a net Pounds 9.7m - was 102.45p per share. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 3.45m (2.61m), equivalent to earnings per share of 9.3p, up from 7.07p. A fourth interim dividend of 1.96875p is declared, maintaining the total at 7.875p.
