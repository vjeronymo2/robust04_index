BFN [From the "Panorama" program] [Text] In Amman, Hamas spokesman Ibrahim Ghawshah has said that his movement will allow nobody to disarm it as long as there is Israeli occupation. Ghawshah was commenting on statements made by Major General Nasr Yusuf, commander of the Palestinian Police, who announced in Gaza yesterday that he will organize carrying of arms in the self-rule area. Ghawshah stressed that the issue of disarming is a red line, adding that we advise the Palestinian police not to cross this line.
