BFN [Text] Moscow April 4 TASS -- UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev attended the signing ceremony. The documents were signed by Aleksandr Kavsadze, a personal envoy of the Georgian leader, by Abkhazian Prime Minister Sokrat Dzhindzholia, as well as by representatives of Russia, the CSCE and of UN High Commissioner on Refugees.
