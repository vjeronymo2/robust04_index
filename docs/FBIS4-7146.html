BFN ["Great Work Which Enables KPA To Fully Discharge its Mission and Duty" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 24 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN today carries a signed article on the lapse of ten years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il published his famous work "The People's Army Must Defend Its Leader and Party, Political System and Motherland With Its Life". In his speech at the banquet arranged on the 52nd [as received] anniversary of the foundation of the Korean People's Army [KPA], Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, put forward tasks to build up the People's Army more firmly and increase its might, declaring it is the revolutionary Armed Forces of the party and its mission is to guarantee the revolutionary cause of the party with arms. The daily in the article titled "It is Duty of the People's Army to Defend the Party and the Leader and the Political System and the Motherland With Its Life" says: It is a famous work greatly conducive to Army building and the accomplishment of the revolutionary cause as it gives answers to all problems arising in strengthening the People's Army into the Army of the leader and the party. The idea, theory and policy for building the Army and strengthening it, which are specified in the work, have displayed great vitality through practices and their validity and attraction fully demonstrated. The validity and vitality of the work are clearly manifested, above all, in that the backbone ranks of the People's Army have been built up more firmly and the whole Army is filled with the unshakable determination to be intensively loyal to comrade supreme commander and to share the destiny with him. Today the KPA's backbone ranks have grown markedly and their role in Army building and military activities is constantly increasing. Unshakable in its determination to be intensively loyal to General Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, our People's Army has become invincible ranks firmly defending the party and the leader, the socialist motherland without vacillation in any storm. The validity and vitality of the work are also expressed evidently in that the whole Army has become matchless ranks in which an iron discipline of remaining intensively loyal to comrade supreme commander has been established. The intention and plan of comrade supreme commander are quickly and correctly conveyed to each soldier and the whole Army breathes the same breath with him and thinks and acts as he intends. This is the proud looks of the Korean People's Army. The KPA now is an ideological united body of true revolutionary soldiers who are struggling with the idea of General Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, as its unshakable faith. Its validity and vitality are manifested next in that the KPA is fully demonstrating its might as a matchless Army well prepared militarily and technically. It is displaying its honor as strong, revolutionary armed forces equipped with both modern offensive and defensive means. The present reality shows that a revolutionary Army can have invincible might and demonstrate its honor as glorious revolutionary Armed Forces which reliably defend their party and leader, the socialist motherland and the gains of the revolution when it has a genius of military arts and ever-victorious, iron-willed brilliant commander as its supreme commander, the paper stresses.
