941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / International Company News: US insurers in Dollars 1.1bn merger US insurers CNA Financial Corporation and Continental Corporation say they have signed a definitive agreement under which CNA will acquire Continental for Dollars 1.1bn in cash, or Dollars 20 a share, Reuter reports from New York. Both boards have approved the transaction, the companies said. The acquisition may be funded with long-term debt or may include a combination of long-term debt and equity. Continental will continue to operate independently until the acquisition is completed. Mr John Mascotte will continue as chairman and chief executive of Continental for the interim. Also, a transition team composed of key people from both organisations will be formed to ensure a smooth combination. The acquisition is subject to regulatory approval.
