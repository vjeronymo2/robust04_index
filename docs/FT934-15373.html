931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Scholl takes control of Japanese venture Scholl, the international health care group, is taking control of Scholl Japan, a joint venture company formed in 1988, with the purchase of further shares from Chuo Bussan for Y838m (Pounds 5.5m). Scholl's equity holding goes up from 25 per cent to 51 per cent.
