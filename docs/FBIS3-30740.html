Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Jagodina, 7 Feb (TANJUG) -- Vladimir Lukic, prime minister of the Srpska republic, announced today that the republic is joining the monetary system of Yugoslavia. "The Srpska republic will as of tomorrow be a part of the united, reconstructed monetary system together with Serbia and Montenegro, which means that payments will be made in the super dinar," Lukic told Jagodina Radio.
