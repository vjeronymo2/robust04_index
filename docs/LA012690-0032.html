January 26, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Computer Sciences Corp.: The computer services firm's earnings jumped 24% from a year ago to $14.9 million.

Revenue totaled $369 million, up 7%. The El Segundo firm attributed results in part to expanding its activities in commercial and international markets. Revenue from its commercial systems integration group, known as CSC Consulting, rose by 89% to $77.6 million for the quarter, aided by acquisitions in the United States and Europe.
