920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Observer: Spice of life A group of ladies is taking afternoon tea on the balcony of the local golf club while their spouses play a competition round. Missing his final putt a player lets out a string of expletives which cannot but be overheard. Result: a complaint to the committee about the disgraceful language. Decision: ladies are banned from taking tea on the balcony during competition matches.
