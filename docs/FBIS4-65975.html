BFN [Unattributed report: "Iraq: The United Nations Maintains the Embargo, Sanctions"] [Text] On Tuesday evening [17 May], the UN Security Council decided to maintain the embargo imposed against Iraq in August 1990 following its invasion of Kuwait. [Iraqi] Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz had traveled to New York to try to convince the Council members to change their attitude toward Baghdad. The United States and Britain favored maintaining the sanctions, whereas France, the PRC, and Russia pleaded relaxing them.
