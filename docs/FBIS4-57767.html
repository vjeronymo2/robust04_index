BFN [Text] Sao Paulo, 20 May (AE) -- Former Sao Paulo Governor Orestes Quercia, presidential candidate of the Brazilian Democratic Movement Party [PMDB], today met in his campaign headquarters with two incumbent governors and five former governors of his party to officially announce that Iris Rezende will be his running mate on the PMDB ticket. She is the wife of former Goias Governor Iris Rezende -- both husband and wife have the same name. The ticket will be approved by the PMDB national convention to be held in Brasilia on 22 May. Quercia said he chose Mrs. Iris Rezende because he was determined from the outset to have a woman as his running mate as a way of paying tribute to all Brazilian women.
