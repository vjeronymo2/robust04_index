920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa DE BEERS depressed sentiment in Johannesburg ahead of its interim results due today. Analysts are forecasting disappointing half year earnings from the diamond conglomerate. Its shares fell to an intraday low of R72.60 before closing down R2 at R73. The overall index weakened 24 to 3,346 and industrials lost 24 to 4,167. The gold index gained 1 to 1,004.
