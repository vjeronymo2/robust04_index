BFN [Text] The Islamic Resistance has declared that the Martyr Muhammad Darwish Group launched a direct assault at 1830 today against an Israeli patrol composed of two personnel carriers, a Merkava tank, 13 infantry personnel, and a security vehicle on the 'Alman road, using various types of machine guns and rockets. A personnel carrier was burned out and its five crew members were killed or wounded and the security vehicle was hit. There were other casualties among the patrol personnel. Ambulances were seen rushing to the scene to evacuate the casualties. Agent Lahd's [South Lebanese Army] radio reported the operation and that one soldier was wounded.
