BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Apr (MENA)--Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres left Cairo this morning after a one-day visit to Egypt. During the visit he held talks with President Husni Mubarak, Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat, and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christoper on the implementation of the self-rule accord in Gaza and Jericho.
