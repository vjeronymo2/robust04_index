Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chairman of the National Assembly Committee on Kashmir Nawabzada Nasrullah Khan has said Pakistan would continue to extend political, diplomatic, and moral support to the people of Kashmir till their liberation from the Indian yoke. Addressing Kashmiri refugees at Ambore in Muzaffarabad today, he said there was complete consensus between the government and the opposition on the Kashmir issue. He expressed concern over massive killings and human rights violations by Indian forces in occupied Kashmir and assured the refugees that the (?long night) of terror and Indian suppression would come to an end soon.
