BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said the outcome of Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis' meetings in the United States will be seen in the upcoming UN Security Council resolution. The spokesman stressed that the foreign minister's mission was to present the Greek Cypriot side's positions, which were formed at the National Council. He added: At the same time, the goal of his mission was to persuade the UN Secretariat and the UN Security Council members of the correctness of the Greek Cypriot positions.
