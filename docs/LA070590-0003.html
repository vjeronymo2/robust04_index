July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Suarez Co-Anchor Pending: Patti Davis Suarez, who for the last nine years has anchored the news at the CBS affiliate in Oklahoma City, has been named anchor of KTTV Channel 11's 10 p.m. news, and will replace current KTTV anchors Pat Lalama and Dennis Morgino on Sept. 3. KTTV, which has been through several anchor and news management changes since Fox bought the station four years ago, is expected to hire a new co-anchor for Suarez sometime in the next two months. GREG BRAXTON
