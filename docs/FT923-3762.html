920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: North Sea Assets little changed In rough trading conditions, North Sea Assets raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 826,000 to Pounds 890,000. Sales of Pounds 10.1m for the six months to June 30 compared with Pounds 15m in the previous first half, which included Pounds 4.23m from the ships business, since sold. Earnings per share were 1.75p (1.94p).
