BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 22 (AFP) -- United Nations Protection Force [UNPROFOR] commander General Michael Rose ordered a UN convoy heading for the eastern Bosnian town of Gorazde to return to Sarajevo Friday [22 April], an UNPROFOR spokesman said. The order to return, issued around 1:30 p.m. (1130 GMT), was made "for tactical and political reasons," spokesman Rob Annink said. The convoy had been blocked by Serb women protesters.
