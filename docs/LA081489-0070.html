August 14, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

TWA Lowers Fares: TWA announced savings of up to $160 off its lowest fares to attract passengers to the fall and 1990 travel seasons. The company's "lowest fare guarantee" ensures that even if TWA's fares are reduced after tickets are purchased, passengers will be refunded the difference, and they will be allowed one "free" travel date change without penalty. Tickets can be purchased any time before Sept. 1 and from Sept. 9 through Dec. 15 for domestic travel, and for international travel from Oct. 1 through May 21, 1990.
