Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 6 TASS -- The Russian president signed a resolution Thursday confirming four top officials of the former Security Ministry in their corresponding positions in the Federal Counterintelligence Service, according to the press office of the Russian leader. The four are: Major General Igor Mezhakov - head of the personnel department of the service, Major General Yevgeniy Savostyanov - deputy director and head of the Moscow and the Moscow region branch of the service, Colonel General Anatoliy Safonov - deputy director, Major General Aleksandr Serebryakov - head of the financial department.
