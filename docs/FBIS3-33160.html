Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, Chiapas State, Mexico, 17 Feb (EFE) -- Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, announced today that the dialogue between the rebel Zapatist National Liberation Army (EZLN) and the government will begin next Monday at 1100 (1700 GMT). Camacho Solis declined to reveal the site of the meeting, but it is believed to be in a Chiapas city, not in the jungle. Camacho Solis said he will spend Sunday gathering all the Zapatist representatives.
