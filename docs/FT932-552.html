930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / International Company News: Progress in NY exchange talks MERGER talks between New York's two largest commodity exchanges are progressing. In a letter to both memberships last week, the negotiating committee said the New York Mercantile Exchange had increased its initial Dollars 10m cash bid for the Commodity Exchange to Dollars 45m, with about Dollars 15m of that coming from the Comex's own treasury. Traders said the new offer may be approved by members of both exchanges.
