BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 21 (KCNA) -- Former war correspondent of the Korean People's Army Yi In-mo, the incarnation of faith and will, visited the exhibition of the three revolutions in Pyongyang. Being briefed on the brilliant achievements made by the Korean people in carrying on the three revolutions, ideological, technical and cultural, under the wise leadership of the party and the leader, he saw with deep emotion exhibits at the general hall, the heavy industry hall, the light industry hall and other halls. After his visit, he said that the Korean people have brought a great change in socialist economic construction under the party's unique banner of the three revolutions. The exhibition well shows the economic development of the country which is demonstrating its might to the whole world, he noted. "Our country", he stressed, "could build a solid independent national economy with a powerful chuche-based industry and developed agriculture because it was under the tested guidance of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il". At the end of his visit to the West Sea Barrage, he wrote in the visitor's book: "This barrage fully shows the matchless grit of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and the looks of the Korean people united around him in one mind. Yi In-mo toured the revolutionary site on Munsu Hill and the Grand People's Study House in Pyongyang.
