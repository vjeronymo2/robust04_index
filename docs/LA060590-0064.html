June 5, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A mobile home at Coastal Trailer Villa near Bay Park was ruled a total loss after a Monday morning blaze.

No one was home, and no injuries were reported.

The mobile home in the 1500 block of Morena Blvd was destroyed in the 8:40 a.m. fire with $14,000 in damage.

The fire, believed caused by discarded smoking material, began in a cabana area and spread to the mobile home.
