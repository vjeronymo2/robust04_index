920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Kynoch down at Pounds 380,000 Profits of Kynoch Group, a manufacturer of medical equipment, fell from Pounds 460,000 to Pounds 380,000 pre-tax for the half year to June 30. Based on a full tax charge, earnings per share emerged at 1.6p (2.2p). That was 33 per cent ahead of the figure for the second half of 1991 and 14 per cent higher than for the opening half of 1991 after adjusting for the one-off profit arising from Gulf war related business and losses on the discontinued textile operation. Turnover totalled Pounds 6.99m (Pounds 8.33m) and operating profits Pounds 463,000 (Pounds 573,000). Interest charges accounted for Pounds 83,000 (Pounds 113,000).
