930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / International Company News: Canadian Air link approved CANADIAN Airlines International has won national transportation agency approval for a CDollars 246m (Dollars US193.7m) equity infusion from American Airlines. Mr Rhys Eyton, chairman of Canadian and its parent, PWA, said the regulatory body's decision that the proposed Canadian-American link up was not against the public interest 'will allow Canadian to move ahead with its restructuring'. The link will give American a 25 per cent voting interest.
