December 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

MCA Sale Clears Another Hurdle: The Federal Communications Commission said it had approved the transfer of a New York-area television station from MCA Inc. to MCA shareholders, removing a barrier to the sale of MCA to Matsushita Electric Industrial Co. of Japan. Under federal law, Matsushita as a foreign corporation is barred from owning MCA's WWOR-TV, based in Secaucus, N.J.
