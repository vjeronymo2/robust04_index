940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Boxmore expands with Pounds 13.4m buy Boxmore International, the Ulster-based plastic packaging manufacturer, is expanding into mainland Britain through the acquisition of Label Research, a manufacturer of pharmaceutical labels, for a maximum Pounds 13.4m. The deal will enable USM-quoted Boxmore to manufacturer and distribute packaging, leaflets and labels for health care products throughout the UK and Ireland. Mr Harold Ennis, chairman and chief executive, said the companies had agreed terms after four months of negotiation. Boxmore is paying an initial Pounds 10.1m, satisfied by the placing of 3.15m shares at 250p and the allotment of 805,707 shares, 2.94 per centof the enlarged group, which the vendors are retaining. There will be a furtherprofit-related payment of up to Pounds 3.3m, satisfied by loan notes or shares. Label reported profits of Pounds 1.34m (Pounds 1.08m) for the year to June 30 on turnover of Pounds 4.88m (Pounds 4.54m). Boxmore is also raising Pounds 3.21m before expenses to help fund capital spending through the placing of 1.29m shares, also at 250p. Existing shareholders can apply for all the placing shares on a 1-for-5 basis. Credit Lyonnais Laing is underwriting the placing and open offer. Boxmore has also declared a second interim for 1993, in lieu of a final, of 2.75p, making 4p (3.65p) for the year. The payment, said Mr Ennis, reflected a strong second half performance. Pre-tax profits for 1993 are expected to increase 21 per cent to Pounds 4.4m (Pounds 3.6m). The shares closed up 9p at 293p.
