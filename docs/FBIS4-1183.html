BFN [Text] SAPA reports that a bomb exploded in Germiston on the East Rand short while ago killing seven people and injuring 50. The bomb apparently exploded on the corner of Knox and Odendaal streets. No further information is available. Meanwhile the police on the West Rand have confirmed that a bomb exploded at a taxi rank across from Randfontein station this morning, and in Nelspruit it's reported that an Eskom [Electricity Supply Commission] pylon was damaged in an explosion yesterday. Earlier it was reported that explosions rocked polling stations at Steynsburg and a nearby township in the Karoo last night. Another explosion damaged the offices of the Department of Home Affairs at Potgietersrus in the northern Transvaal last night.
