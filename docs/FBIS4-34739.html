BFN [By Yoshinori Nakai] [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 21 Jun -- On 20 June, Colombian President-elect Ernesto Samper and Vice President-elect Humberto de la Calle held their first news conference for the foreign press, including MAINICHI SHINBUN. Touching on economic relations, the president-elect said: "Colombia will give top priority to our relations with Japan, and we want Japan to know more about our country." Concerning the current lack of interest among Japanese companies toward investing in Colombia due to apprehensions over security problems such as terrorism and abduction, both of them pointed out: "Other foreign investors share the same anxieties. Since measures have been taken to ensure security, however, such activities are no obstacle to investment." They also said: "We hope the Japanese people will try to learn more about Colombia. We are sure that measures can be taken to solve the security problem."
