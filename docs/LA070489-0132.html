July 4, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Robert Furtado has been named to the newly created position of vice president of operations for Ingram Micro D. He had been director of operations for Micro D Inc. since July, 1987. Micro D merged earlier this year with Ingram Industries Inc. to form Ingram Micro D. Ingram Micro D, with headquarters in Santa Ana, is a wholesale distributor of microcomputer products.
