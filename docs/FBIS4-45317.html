BFN [Text] This morning customs officials at Beirut airport foiled an attempt to smuggle a quantity of drugs into Lebanon. Husayn 'Ali al-Sahili, born in 1973 in Kafr Zabad in al-Biqa', was arrested upon his arrival from Colombia via Amsterdam, and 2,250 grams of cocaine were seized with him. The drugs were hidden in an innovative and technical way--inside a case with a false bottom and inside two nylon envelopes. The case passed, undetected, through X-ray machines in European airports. Al-Sahili was arrested and referred to the counternarcotics office. The local value of the seized drug was estimated at $70,000 and estimated at $5,000 in Colombia.
