930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Are British managers the best in Europe? British managers think they are the best in Europe but this opinion is not shared by their continental European competitors, according to a survey of Europe's smaller companies, published today, Charles Batchelor writes. German managers and their companies are the most highly regarded by their European competitors.
