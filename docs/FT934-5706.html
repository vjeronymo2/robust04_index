931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / NHS trust chiefs average 8.9% rise The salaries of chief executives in National Health Service trusts increased by a median 8.9 per cent in the 12 months to last March, says a survey published today by Incomes Data Services. The figure covers a diversity of changes - from a 33 per cent rise to Pounds 137,000 at Guy's and Lewisham in London to a fall of 2.9 per cent to Pounds 67,000 at Kingston hospital.
