BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- A meeting took place in North Hwanghae Province on April 27 in celebration of the 48th anniversary of the independence of Syria. Invited to the meeting were Syrian Ambassador to Korea Yasir Farah, his embassy officials and Syrian students studying in Korea. Attending the meeting were chairman of the North Hwanghae Provincial Administrative and Economic Committee Yang Man-kil and officials concerned and working people in Sariwon. Speeches were exchanged at the meeting. The meeting adopted a congratulatory letter to the Syrian president.
