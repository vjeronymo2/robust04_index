May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE/TECHNOLOGY

Meridian Software, a small Laguna Hills company, has received $1.5 million in funding from two California venture capital firms, DSC Ventures of Cupertino and Spectra Enterprise Associates of Westlake Village.

Meridian develops products to aid software programmers who work with the Ada computer language, which the Department of Defense has designated as the standard for most military computer applications.

Meridian said the funds will be used for new product development and marketing.

The privately held company employs 20 people.
