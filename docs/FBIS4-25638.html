BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 10 (XINHUA) -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa will visit South Korea and China Saturday [11 June] and Sunday for talks with his counterparts on the nuclear issue in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK), the Foreign Ministry said today. During his stay in Seoul tomorrow, Kakizawa will meet South Korean Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu to have a wide-ranging exchange of views on such issues as the international situation including the nuclear problem, the ministry said. The Japanese foreign minister will also pay a courtesy call on President Kim Yong-sam. While in Beijing on Sunday, Kakizawa is scheduled to meet Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen to have talks mainly on the nuclear issue, the ministry said.
