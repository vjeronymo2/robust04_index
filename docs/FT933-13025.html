930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: National Power withdraws from Malaysian deal National Power has pulled out of a joint venture to construct and operate two gas fired power stations in Malaysia. It has received assurances from other partners in the project that it will recover its development costs. National Power announced last October that it was investing Dollars 20m to Dollars 30m (Pounds 13.3m to Pounds 20m) for a 10 per cent stake in the venture with Malaysia's YTL. However, the company said yesterday it did not think it was correct to proceed.
