BFN [Text] The brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation met this evening brother Habib Ben Yahya, the Tunisian Foreign Affairs Minister on the sidelines of the 60th session of the African foreign affairs ministers' council. The issues reviewed during this meeting included the agenda of the present session and the coordination of the two countries' stances towards them for the benefit of the one Arab people in the two fraternal countries and the African peoples.
