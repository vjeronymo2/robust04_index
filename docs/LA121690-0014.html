December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BABY BOOMERS RUNNING IN PLACE

As an older baby boomer, I read with interest "The Dead-End Kids."

It occurred to me that you omitted the fastest way to climb the corporate ladder. It's called a variety of names: brown nosing, not making waves, company politics, networking -- anything to get that promotion. You know and I know the old adage: "It's not what you know, it's who you know."

I wonder if it even happens at The Times.

HAL OFFENBERG

La Mirada
