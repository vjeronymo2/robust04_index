930429 FT 29 APR 93 / International Capital Markets: Horsham paper raises CDollars 125m HORSHAM, a holding company controlled by Mr Peter Munk, chairman of America's Barrick Resources, has raised about CDollars 125m by selling 8.5m subordinated voting shares to underwriters at CDollars 14 7/8 each. The shares will be distributed later in Canada, the US and Europe. Gordon Capital leads the underwriting group. Horsham owns 20 per cent of Barrick and 60 per cent of the US Clark Oil & Refining. Perstorp, the Swedish speciality chemicals and plastics group, has agreed a long-term credit facility with 16 international banks which will enable it to borrow up to Dollars 150m over five years. The multi-currency revolving facility was arranged by Credit Suisse First Boston.
