BFN [Text] Honored listeners, an official spokesman at the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] Defense Ministry has issued the following statement to the YEMENI NEWS AGENCY, Aden: At a time when the sons of our Yemeni people, brothers, and friends were looking forward to the declaration of the cease-fire, with effect from 1800 this evening, 9 June, in implementation of UN security resolution No. 924, and in response to the sincere efforts of Mr. al-Akhdar al-Ibrahimi, the UN secretary general's envoy to Yemen, our forces deployed on the Abyan and Lahij axes were subjected to a comprehensive surprise attack and concentrated artillery barrage, which was launched by the forces of the ruling military regime in Sanaa. This attack is still in progress. The Armed Forces of the DRY, which are absolutely and totally observing the cease-fire resolution in effect from 1800 this evening, so as to prevent the shedding of Yemeni blood in respect and appreciation for the good and sincere efforts of the UN envoy, affirm to the masses of our people in the DRY their complete ability to repel the cowardly aggressors, and to force them into respecting the international authority and its resolutions. The Defense Ministry wishes at the same time to praise the good efforts resulting from the high degree of responsibility on the part of the UN envoy, and to appeal to the UN Security Council to take stern measures against this raging aggressor, who not only sheds the blood of our Yemeni people, but defies the will of the whole international community.
