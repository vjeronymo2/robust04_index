940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: FT guide to investment trusts - At a Glance A shake-out looks a real possibility for the investment trust sector, the index for which rose by 46 per cent last year, compared with 23 per cent for the FT-SE-A All-Share index. But although many trust shares are highly priced, both in their own right and in relation to alternatives such as comparable unit trusts, their long-term merits as an investment vehicle remain. The FT Guide to Investment Trusts, to be published with next Saturday's paper, will give them a thorough health check.
