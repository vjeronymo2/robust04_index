Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella has said in Washington that the United States will invite Argentina to join the North American Free Trade Agreement, NAFTA. He added that the United States will make this official before July 1994 and that the decision will not affect Mercosur [Common Market of the South]. Di Tella said this is a very important step toward the original idea of achieving a free trade zone in the Americas.
