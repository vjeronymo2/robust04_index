931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Anagen reduces interim deficit to Pounds 1.78m Anagen, the biotechnology company, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 2.39m to Pounds 1.78m in the six months ended June 30, just two weeks after the company came to market. Turnover of Pounds 1.27m (Pounds 218,000) was derived, the company said, mainly from payments by Organon Teknika following the signing of a worldwide marketing agreement for the AN2000 immunoassay system. Operating losses of Pounds 1.3m reflected continuing development activities, the company said. Interest payments of Pounds 436,000 (Pounds 268,000) were made on short-term debts, since eliminated following the Pounds 14.3m flotation proceeds. Losses per share were cut to 5.6p (9p).
