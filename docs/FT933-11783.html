930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / Councils attack urban agency plan THE CREATION of the Urban Regeneration Agency to take responsibility for bringing derelict land into use reflects 'a continued stagnation in government thinking on urban policy', a report by the Docklands Consultative Committee says. The committee, an association of east London local authorities, criticises the agency's emphasis on property development schemes and its reliance on the 'trickle down' of benefits to the community.
