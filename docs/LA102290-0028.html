October 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

GOLF;

ANOTHER VICTORY FOR TREVINO

Lee Trevino made five birdies over the final seven holes Sunday for a two-stroke victory over Mike Hill in the $500,000 Transamerica Senior Championship at Napa, Calif.

Trevino's seven-under-par 65 earned him $75,000. He finished the 54 holes on the 6,632-yard Silverado Country Club south course at 205, 11 under par.

Trevino has now won seven tournaments and $922,352 during his rookie season on the PGA Senior Tour.
