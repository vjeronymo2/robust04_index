920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Gresham Telecomp Pro forma accounts for the 13 months to October 31 1991 at Gresham Telecomputing showed pre-tax profit of Pounds 751,000 from turnover of Pounds 10.7m. This is the first set of accounts since the April 1991 merger. They represented the combined figures of Gresham Telecomputing for 13 months and Gresham Computer Holdings and its subsidiaries for 12 months. Earnings per share were 1.42p and dividends are resumed with a payment of 0.22p. Restated figures for 1990 showed turnover of Pounds 2.1m, a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 257,000 and losses per share of 4.74p.
