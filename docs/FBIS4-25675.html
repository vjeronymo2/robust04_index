BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 10 (XINHUA) -- Vanuatu Prime Minister Maxine Carlot Korman concluded his four-day working visit to China and left here today. The prime minister and his party arrived here on Tuesday [7 June] morning at the invitation of the Chinese Government. Prior to his departure, Carlot said that the visit was aimed at exploring ways of furthering his country's cooperation with Shanghai in economy and trade. "The trip has been a success," he said.
