BFN [Text] The Seimas [parliament] has decided that the self-government elections will be held according to the proportional system. Members of local councils will be elected for two years, while their current term in office is four years. The election date has not yet been set. According to unofficial reports, the elections should take place this fall. One article in the law on elections for local government councils, approved by the Seimas, states that representatives to election commissions can be nominated only by those parties and political organizations, which have received Seimas deputy mandates according to party lists. The entire law on elections to local government councils will be adopted next week.
