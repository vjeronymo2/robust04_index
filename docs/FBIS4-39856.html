BFN [Excerpt] Emomali Rakhmonov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Council of Tajikistan yesterday received Salman Khurshid, India's minister of state for external affairs who has arrived in our country. In the course of the friendly conversation it was pointed out that the new possibilities have appeared for cooperation in various spheres of economy and culture after the visit of the government delegation of Tajikistan to India in the past year. Rakhmonov stated that Tajikistan intends to develop direct relations with friendly India. [words indistinct] [passage omitted]
