BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow June 21 TASS -- An appeal of the Russian Federation Council requested the State Duma today to take into account its opinion when discussing the draft 1994 budget in third reading. The appeal aims to support the application of the Association of Defence Industry Trade Unions and the League for Assistance to the Defence Plants on the need to prevent the defence industry collapse, Chairman of the Federation Council Budget Committee Nikolay Gonchar said here today. This morning representatives of the defence industries picketed the Federation Council building, and the chairman of the Association of Defence Industry Trade Union's Executive Committee made a report on the branch crisis at the Federation Council meeting at the invitation of the council speaker. The Federation Council offered the State Duma to revise the defence spending item of the budget at an amount of 55 trillion rubles. The lower parliament chamber is expected to discuss the draft budget on Wednesday. The Federation Council will continue its meeting on June 22.
