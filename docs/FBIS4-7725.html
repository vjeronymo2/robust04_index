BFN [Excerpt] Banja Luka, 4 Apr (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces have intensified their attacks on Serb positions west of Brcko, the information service of the First Krajina Corps of the Srpska republic army said in Banja Luka today. The fiercest attacks were launched from the direction of Bajici on the Brcko suburb of Ulice which was targeted with more than 10 mortar shells. There was also fire from antiaircraft and infantry weapons on this part of the battlefield. Srpska republic army units were forced to respond to silence the enemy. Large movements of troops belonging to the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army were observed on the Gradacac part of the front as well as very intense movements of armored vehicles near the frontline and in the enemy's territory.
