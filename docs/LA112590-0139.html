November 25, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

CSUN WOMEN RALLY, ONLY TO LOSE, 60-52

The Southwest Texas women's basketball team held off Cal State Northridge to emerge with a 60-52 victory in a consolation-round game of the Fullerton tournament Saturday.

The Matadors (0-2) closed to 55-50 with 1 1/2 minutes left after trailing, 34-14, but could get no closer.
