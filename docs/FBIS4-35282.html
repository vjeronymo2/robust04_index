BFN [Text] The prime minister, Brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, has expressed his appreciation for the role played by His Highness al-Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al-Nuhayyan, president of the UAE, in [word indistinct] peace in Yemen and his concern about the interests of the Yemeni people and their peace and stability. This was said when His Highness al-Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al-Nuhayyan received at the Presidential Palace this morning Brother Haydar Abu- Bakr al-'Attas in the presence of His Highness al-Shayk Khalifah Bin-Zayid Al-Nuhayyan, the crown prince of Abu Dhabi and deputy supreme commander of the Armed Forces. The meeting discussed the current situation in the Yemeni arena and the efforts aimed at solving the Yemeni crisis and achieving peace and stability.
