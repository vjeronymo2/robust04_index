August 29, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

'CAN WE GO HOME AGAIN?'

Last year, Lynwood commissioned Hintz & Balvin Communications to create a video history of the city so that youngsters and newcomers could appreciate where they are living. In the process, we got to know Lynwood as a city of people who are friendly and care about their city.

The camera crew created curiosity and people on the street were terribly pleased to be the focus of a positive piece on their area. The video gathered together Lynwood old-timers and newcomers in a project that they could all be proud of -- The City of Lynwood.

We concluded that Lynwood seems to have a special sort of strength and staying power. It lives strongly in people's happy memories of its past but even more strongly in the current residents' determination to create there a new All-American City.

BARBARA BOYER WALTER

Hintz & Balvin

Pasadena
