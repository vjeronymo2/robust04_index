BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Velayati, speaking before a gathering of ambassadors, charges d'affaires, and diplomatic representatives of foreign countries in Tehran today, said that the double-standard of some countries regarding the ominous phenomenon of terrorism is an encouragement to the continuation of organized crime. According to a report by our correspondent, the foreign minister of the Islamic Republic of Iran, referring to the recent horrific crime by the hypocrites added: This catastrophe is so profound that no free-minded human being can regard it with indifference. Velayati described the crime as similar to the one in Hebron in the occupied Palestine and said: Such terrorist acts against the great nation of Iran will never shake the determination of our people in confronting criminals. Our country's foreign minister also called on the international community to confront the ominous phenomenon of terrorism decisively and without a selective approach. He regretted some of the foreign states' support for the hypocrites' terrorist group and letting them put propaganda means at their disposal. He added: Without such support the bloody catastrophe in Mashhad would not have happened. According to the same report, after the statement made by our country's foreign minister, the Libyan ambassador in Tehran, as the doyen of ambassadors, expressed regret over that traumatic incident and considered that terrorist act as a violation of human rights.
