May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

What a wonderful surprise "Archie: To Riverdale and Back Again" was. Christopher Rich as Archie was an absolute delight -- the perfect incarnation of red-haired, freckle-faced ingenuousness.

As a fan of Archie comics for over 20 years, I found the entire production impeccably cast and well-written, faithfully capturing all the charm and humor of the comic books.

Susan E. Hashimoto, Sherman Oaks
