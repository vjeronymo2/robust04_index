Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Referring to the economic talks with the Palestinians in Paris, Finance Minister Avraham Shohat said they have run into real difficulties owing to the fact that the PLO delegates presented new positions which are incompatible with the understandings reached in the previous rounds of negotiations. In his meeting with Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'] tomorrow, Shohat said, he will seek to clarify whether the PLO officially changed its positions. Minister Shohat believes a change of policy may be needed, and instead of open economies, a decision will be made to close the economies of Israel and the autonomy to each other.
