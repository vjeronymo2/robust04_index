930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sales growth helps Hamleys into black Hamleys, the privately owned toyshop company, returned to profit in the year to January 31 helped by sales growth and cost control. Pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.22m compared to a loss of Pounds 846,000. Turnover rose 10 per cent to Pounds 17.4m. New stores were opened at Heathrow and Gatwick.
