BFN [Text] Brother Faysal Bin-'Uthman Bin-Shamlan, minister of oil and mineral resources, returned to Sanaa this evening from working visits to the United Kingdom and France. The brother minister told the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', that he held talks during his visit to London with executives of some British investment firms, especially those active in the area of oil exploration in the Republic of Yemen. These firms, he indicated, have shown a readiness to send their experts back to resume work as early as this week alongside the local personnel who have stayed on their job. Brother Bin-Shamlan added that he held similar talks in the French capital, Paris, with officials of the Totale [word indistinct] about the exploitation of the (Jannah) oilfield in implementation of a Cabinet decision. The brother minister said the producing companies, like (Hans) and (Sanvienee Ofti) have not halted their operations.
