Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 24 Dec (XINHUA) -- The All-China Greening Committee recently formed a new committee, with State Councillor Chen Junsheng as chairman. The vice chairmen are: Xu Youfang (minister of forestry), Liu Jimin (State Council deputy secretary general), Fu Quanyou (director of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army [PLA]), Chen Yaobang (vice minister of the State Planning Commission), Hou Jie (minister of construction), Li Yanling (vice minister of finance), and Li Qiyan (Beijing Municipal Mayor). The posts of 26 committee members are held by responsible persons from relevant departments of the State Council, the three PLA general departments, the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Armed Police Force, and mass organizations.
