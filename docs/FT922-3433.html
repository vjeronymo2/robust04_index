920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / Iranians hanged Four Iranians were hanged yesterday for their part in last month's riots in the north-eastern city of Mashhad. The Tehran warned that trouble-makers would be rooted out and discarded like weeds, Reuter reports from Nicosia. More than 100 government buildings, banks and shops and dozens of cars were burned or wrecked and more than 300 people arrested during the riots, according to official accounts.
