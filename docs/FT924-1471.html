921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Dartmoor net asset value declines Dartmoor Investment Trust had a net asset value of 74.2p per share at October 31 compared with 117.4p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months fell slightly, from Pounds 966,000 to Pounds 919,000, to leave earnings per share at 4.02p (4.83p). A second interim dividend of 2.5p (2.4p) is declared, making 5p (4.8p) so far.
