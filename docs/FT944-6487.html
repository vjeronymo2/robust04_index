941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / International Company News: AlliedSignal to shed 1,000 jobs AlliedSignal, the US automotive components and aerospace group, said it would reduce the workforce at its aerospace unit by 1,000 employees over the next eight months, beginning in December, Reuter reports. The reduction follows AlliedSignal's purchase of Lycoming's turbine engine division in October. AlliedSignal said the integration of the Lycoming operations was on schedule, and no charge was expected in connection with the job losses.
