BFN [Text] A round of talks was held at the National Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party [ASBP] today between the ASBP delegation led by Comrade 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, ASBP assistant secretary general, and the delegation of the Chinese Communist Party, led by Comrade Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and head of the Chinese Federation of Trade Unions. Attending the talks on the Syrian side were Comrade Mut'ib Shinan, ASBP National Command member, and 'Abdallah Abu-al-Rish, member of the Party Committee for Supervision and Inspection. On the Chinese side were members of the accompanying Chinese Communist Party delegation. The talks dealt with the progress in relations of cooperation between the two friendly parties in accordance with the program signed between them in 1991. The two sides also reviewed the two parties' activities in their countries to continue the process of development and construction in order to achieve the objectives set by each party in its program of action to develop its country based on the independent option of the people. The two sides also stressed the need to promote cooperation between the two parties in the interest of the two friendly peoples and countries. They also stressed the importance of consultation and coordination on all the international issues and developments of joint interest to both parties and countries. The two sides also reviewed developments in the Arab and international arenas and in southeast Asia, and the current international developments' effects on international relations. The two sides stressed the importance of international cooperation to achieve world peace and security in a way to fulfill peoples' desire to safeguard their independence, to determine their future, and to affirm their independent option to develop their countries.
