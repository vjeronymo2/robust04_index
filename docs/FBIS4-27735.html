BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 3 (KPL) -- Vientiane Mayor Boun-gnang Volachit, who led a Lao delegation of 20 members to visit China since May 19, returned home yesterday. The purpose of the Lao delegation's visit was to attend a seminar to share experience on economic development as well as investment opportunities with its Chinese counterparts, particularly after China opened up its door to the outside world. The Lao senior officials also presented to the seminar the renovation policy of Laos for discussions and experience exchanges.
