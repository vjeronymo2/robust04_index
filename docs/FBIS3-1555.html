Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Bangkok, 27 Feb (XINHUA) -- Qian Qichen, Chinese vice premier and foreign minister, during a short stopover here today on route to Sri Lanka from Bangladesh met with Thai Foreign Minister Prasong Sunsiri. The two exchanged views on issues of common concern. Prasong reiterated that Thailand will abide by the principles laid down in the communique on the establishment of diplomatic relation between Thailand and China and will follow the one-China policy. He emphasized that his country treasures the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two country forged in the past 19 years since diplomatic relations were established and that it will make efforts not to hurt such relations. Qian Qichen will leave here tomorrow for a visit to Sri Lanka.
