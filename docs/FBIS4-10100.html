BFN [Text] The Armed Forces Joint Command issued an official communique stating its loyalty to the president of the Republic on the occasion of the second anniversary of the institutional change that took place on 5 April. It says that the support given to the president's decision was appropriate because it was based on the true situation of a country that is now on the way to achieving the desired national pacification after a period of chaos. The communique signed by the head of the Armed Forces Joint Command, the military commanders, and the National Police general director says they will continue to work to consolidate this process that is the basis and guarantee for a lasting and stable democracy.
