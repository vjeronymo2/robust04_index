BFN [Text] Khmelnitskiy, 16 May, UKRINFORM-TASS -- The first power set of the Khmelnitskiy nuclear power station has once again been switched on to the grid following the completion of scheduled preventive maintenance work that lasted a little over a week. Nothing has been reported regarding the performance of the equipment. The radiation situation at the station and at built-up areas in a 30-km zone is within the norm.
