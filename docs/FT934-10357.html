931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Towry Law to join market with Pounds 29m tag TOWRY LAW, one of the UK's largest independent financial intermediaries, is to seek a listing by way of a placing of 4.92m ordinary shares, equivalent to 32.2 per cent of its equity, which will value the company at Pounds 29m. The placing will be at a price of 190p per share, which represents an historic p/e of 18.6. No new shares are being placed as part of the arrangement, After the listing, the directors will in total own 36.3 per cent of the shares. The holding of Mr Cecil Law, Towry's chairman and founder, together with his family interests, will be reduced from 51.7 per cent to 41.7 per cent. For the year ended June 30, Towry Law reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.43m (Pounds 2.07m). Brokers to the issue are James Capel & Co.
