920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Thornton Pan-Euro Trust lifts dividend Thornton Pan-European Investment Trust reported a slight fall in net assets per share to 29.5p at the end of 1991, against 30p a year earlier. However, as at February 28, 1992 the figure had recovered to 31.2p. Net revenue for the year fell from Pounds 241,015 to Pounds 220,962 after tax of Pounds 105,805 (Pounds 86,914). Earnings per share came out at 1.1p (1.92p), but the dividend has been raised to 1p (0.87p).
