BFN [Text] In connection with the news item broadcast last night by the Israeli Television and quoted by AFP, according to which a meeting between PLO leader Yasir `Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres will be held in Bucharest on 15 April, a possibility assessed by Palestinian sources in Tunis as not very likely, our correspondent Eugen Baboi, who is accredited to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, contacted Ministry spokesman Mircea Geoana, who said the following: The Romanian Foreign Ministry has no knowledge of such an event. The Foreign Ministry sent an invitation to `Arafat and Peres to participate in the proceedings of the Crans Montana Forum that will be held in Bucharest on 21-24 April, but it is still waiting for confirmation regarding the participation of the two officials. Therefore, the Romanian side cannot comment on the information regarding an `Arafat-Peres meeting in Bucharest on 15 April because it has no knowledge of such a meeting.
