The applicant will begin operations with capitalization of approximately $7.5 million and will be a source of equity financings for qualified small business concerns. The applicant will invest primarily in the technology and life sciences businesses located in the states of California, Massachusetts, Minnesota, and Texas. Matters involved in SBA's consideration of the application include the general business reputation and character of the proposed owners and management, and the probability of successful operations of the new company under their management, including profitability and financial soundness in accordance with the Act and Regulations. Notice is hereby given that any person may, not later than 15 days from the date of publication of this Notice, submit written comments on the proposed SBIC to the Associate Administrator for Investment, Small Business Administration, 409 3rd Street, SW, Washington, DC 20416. A copy of this Notice will be published in a newspaper of general circulation in New York, New York.
