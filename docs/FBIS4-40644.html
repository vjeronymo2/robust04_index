BFN [Untitled, unattributed reports under the "Provincial Chronicle" rubric] [Text] Kazan -- A plenipotentiary mission of Tataria [Tatarstan] in Russia has been instituted by an edict of the republic's president. Nail Aminov, deputy chairman of the Russian Federation State Committee on Federation and Nationalities Affairs, has been appointed envoy plenipotentiary. Cheboksary -- Chuvashia President Nikolay Fedorov has suspended by edict some of the articles in the republic's law on the dismantling of state ownership and privatization of republican and municipal property. At the same time he has pointed out that there will be no major changes in the privatization policy. Khabarovsk -- The local kray committee of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation has circulated a statement on Russo-Chinese relations which says in part that the expansion of bilateral relations should take account of the Far Eastern territories' interests and the inviolability of the existing borders. The Communists demand that the Russian Federation Government denounce the 1991 border treaty and hold talks with the Chinese side with the participation of representatives of the kray.
