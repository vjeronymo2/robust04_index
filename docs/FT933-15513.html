930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Bradstock Group BRADSTOCK GROUP has acquired a further 75,738 ordinary shares (7.57 per cent) in its Bradstock, Blunt & Crawley (Aviation) subsidiary for Pounds 1.5m in cash and shares. For the year to September 1 1992 BBCA returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.2m. Also Bradstock announced that Kuwait Investment Office had sold its 12.23 per cent stake in the company.
