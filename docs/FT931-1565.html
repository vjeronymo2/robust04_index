930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Job subsidy scheme to begin PILOT SCHEMES in which employers will be paid a flat-rate subsidy of about Pounds 60 a week for employing the long-term unemployed are to be introduced in east Kent, Tyneside, south London, Devon and Cornwall. The trials were announced in last week's Budget and will offer employment opportunities to 1,000 people. Participating employers will be required to give an undertaking that the subsidised recruit is not displacing an existing employee.
