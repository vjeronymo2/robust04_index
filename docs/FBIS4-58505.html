BFN [Unattributed report: "Talabani: I Did Not Request Arms From the Europeans"] [Text] London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Iraqi Kurdish Leader Jalal Talabani told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT yesterday that he did not ask the officials in Paris and the other European countries he recently visited to supply the Iraqi Kurds with arms. He said that his talks with these officials covered a request for assistance in training local Kurdish police forces for maintaining internal security and protecting employees of the United Nations and the relief organizations operating in Iraqi Kurdistan. He added that French Defense Minister Leotard, British Minister of State at the Foreign Office Douglas Hogg, and Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana "expressed willingness to study that request and inform us of their position later on, and so did the German officials I met with previously."
