May 17, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

SATCHMO TRIBUTE TO OPEN FESTIVAL

A 90th birthday tribute to late jazz trumpet master Louis Armstrong will open this year's Newport Jazz Festival, producers said today.

The Aug. 17-19 event, formally known as the JVC Jazz Festival-Newport, will mark the festival's 10th year back in Newport.

The original Newport Jazz Festival was held from 1954 to 1971, when a riot by gate-crashing rowdies moved the event to New York.

Trumpeter Wynton Marsalis will produce the jazz festival's Aug. 17 Armstrong tribute, featuring Marsalis and New Orleans musicians, along with trumpeters Doc Cheatham, Ruby Braff and Jon Faddis and singer Carrie Smith. Actor Ossie Davis will act as host for the evening.

Saturday's lineup features Miles Davis, Gerry Mulligan, B. B. King, Elvin Jones and Latin jazz artists Celia Cruz and Tito Puente. Sunday's finale will feature George Benson, McCoy Tyner, Harry Connick Jr., the Count Basie Orchestra, the Phil Woods Quartet and fusion keyboard innovator Joe Zawinul.
