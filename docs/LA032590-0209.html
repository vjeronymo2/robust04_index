March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DEADLINE SET FOR WWII VETERANS' MEMORIAL DESIGN

April 9 is the deadline for submission of design for a memorial to the Japanese-Americans of World War II.

The competition is sponsored by the 100th/442nd/MIS World War II Memorial Fund.

The memorial will be erected in the main plaza area of First Street Plaza.

A $10,000 prize will go to the winner of the competition, according to Michael Barker of the Barker-Patrinely Group, part of a team developing First Street Plaza in Little Tokyo.

Design competition guidelines are available from the 100th/442nd/MIS World War II Memorial Design Committee, 1888 Century Park East, Suite 330, Los Angeles, Calif. 90067.
