BFN [Text] Simferopol, 11 June -- Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov issued instructions approving the regulations of the state affairs service of the Republic of Crimea. In compliance with these regulations the service consists of the directorate for the affairs of the Crimean president, the directorate for the affairs of the Crimean Government, the protocol department, the legal service, the information and analysis service, the service for the research of foreign political issues, and the financial service. The service will have 123 people on staff.
