July 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CARDINAL TIMOTHY MANNING

The Times' coverage of the life and death of Cardinal Timothy Manning (June 24) proved three things to me. First, leadership means service; further, it means making a difference for the good of all humankind. Second, such "mild-mannered" leaders as Cardinal Manning are many times more profound than the leaders who take a more reactionary approach. Third, he was truly a good man. I hope that my epitaph reads the same.

SHAUN DI ANDRE STRONG

Brentwood
