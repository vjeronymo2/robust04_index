BFN [Text] Petrovac, 23 Apr (TANJUG) -- Throughout the day and at various time intervals, particularly this afternoon, NATO planes have been violating the air space over Petrovac, Drvar, Kljuc, the Serb municipality of Bihac, Krupa on the Una River, and other municipalities in the western part of the republic of Srpska. Supersonic aircraft carried out low-altitude and illegal flights, demonstrating their force and particularly making their presence felt over the Bihac front where they were taking aerial photographs of the republic of Srpska army's positions.
