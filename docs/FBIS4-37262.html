BFN [Statement issued by "a responsible source at the Democratic Republic of Yemen Defense Ministry"; place and date not given] [Text] Early this morning some of the peaceful populated suburbs of Aden were subjected to barbarous and random attacks by artillery and rockets from the forces of the ruling military regime in Sanaa. The shelling was accompanied by desperate attacking attempts from the same forces to advance on all fronts. However, our brave armed forces intercepted these forces, and inflicted crushing blows on them. They also inflicted heavy human and material losses on them, and forced them to withdraw.
