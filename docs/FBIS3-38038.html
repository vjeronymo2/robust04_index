Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Washington February 25 TASS -- A U.S. daily said that a CIA team, which was reported to be only preparing for a trip to Moscow in connection with the arrest of Aldrich Ames on charges of spying for Kremlin, had already visited the Russian capital. THE WASHINGTON POST said on Friday [25 February] that the CIA delegation failed to achieve what it had expected during the trip and is returning home. In Moscow the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service refused either to confirm or deny the report. Spokesman Boris Labusov told TASS that he has no necessary information to comment on THE WASHINGTON POST report.
