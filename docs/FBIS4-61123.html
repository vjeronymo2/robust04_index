BFN [Text] The management and personnel of the Belarusian KGB held a conference connected with the decision to alter the KGB's structure and the size of its staff. As KGB Chairman Henadz Lavitski pointed out in a speech, the goal of the reform is to bring the KGB structure in line with the main purpose of the special service and its current demands, as well as to avoid any improper functions. The main trends of Belarusian KGB activities will be conducting intelligence and counterintelligence operations, as well as providing the authorities with information necessary for adopting resolutions at the state level. The KGB will also participate in implementing a state program for combating crime, coordinating its activities with other law enforcement bodies and strictly adhering to its responsibilities and existing legislation.
