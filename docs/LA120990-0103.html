December 9, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

NORRIS THEATRE OFFERS DISCOUNTS FOR SHOWS

The Norris Theatre for the Performing Arts in Rolling Hills Estates is offering discounts to businesses and community organizations purchasing blocks of tickets for the theater's five entertainment series. Discounts range from 5% to 50% off regular ticket prices of $25 and $30, depending on the number of tickets purchased. Further information is available from the Norris, 544-0403.
