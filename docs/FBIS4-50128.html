BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- China and Russia held consultations on consular affairs for the first time here from May 11 to 12. The consultations were held between directors of the consular affairs departments of the two foreign ministries Zhang Hongxi and Vinogradov. In a friendly and frank atmosphere, the two sides reached consensus on protection of legal interests of citizens and legal persons of one country in the other, jointly stopping illegal immigrants, cooperation in cracking down on illegal activities and crimes committed by citizens of both countries in the territories of the two countries, and adding more consular affairs offices in both countries. The two sides also exchanged opinions on problems stemming from implementing the agreement on visas for citizens of the two countries signed between the two governments. Both sides expressed that they are willing to enhance contacts and cooperation so as to make more efforts to promote bilateral good-neighborly relations and exchanges in various fields such as trade. The two sides also agreed to continue consultations on some issues through diplomatic channels. Tang Jiaxuan, Chinese vice-foreign minister, met with Vinogradov and his party.
