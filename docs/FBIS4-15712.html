BFN [Unattributed INTERFAX report in the "In Brief" column: "The Government Policy Will Change"] [Text] Boris Yeltsin is serious about the mounting criticism of the government's performance and, in coordination with Viktor Chernomyrdin, will apparently introduce some adjustments into the cabinet's activity. This was announced 8 April by presidential press secretary Vyacheslav Kostikov. In particular, according to him, adjustments are possible in the sphere of tax and customs policy. Mr. Kostikov stressed that the president and the prime minister have a good relationship and bear "no grudge against each other."
