Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Early this morning an armed group of people in military uniform seized the building of the city administration in Vladivostok. There is practically no legitimate authority in the city, it says in a telegram from the deputy head of the city's administration received by ITAR-TASS. A person who did not wish to introduce himself as a spokesman for the administration of the President of Russia in Vladivostok reported the following: [Begin recording of unidentified spokesman] The situation is not like that. It is simply that, in keeping with a decision of the procurator, the head of administration has been removed from his post in connection with the investigation into a criminal case. There were no armed people. The militia were involved, but in the main to ensure that order was maintained. A new head of administration has been appointed. [end recording] At the present time, according to information from ITAR-TASS, the mayor's office in Vladivostok is blockaded by OMON who were brought by Lebedinets, acting governor of Maritime Kray. Viktor Cherepkov has been detained, it states in a report from the mayor's office in Vladivostok.
