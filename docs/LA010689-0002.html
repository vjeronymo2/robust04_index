January 6, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

And the Winner Is: Angela Lansbury, star of "Murder, She Wrote," has been named "Woman of the Year" in broadcasting by the Hollywood Radio and Television Society. Lansbury, who's in her fifth season as mystery writer Jessica Fletcher on the hit CBS series, will be honored March 22 at the group's International Broadcasting Awards at the Century Plaza Hotel in Century City. The 29th annual event also will salute the winning radio and television commercials from more than 60 countries. ALEENE MacMINN
