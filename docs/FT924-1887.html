921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Bank of Italy repays Ecu12.2bn The Bank of Italy has repaid Ecu12.2bn (Pounds 9.7bn) lent by the Bundesbank and the Belgian central bank during the September currency crisis. Over 90 per cent was owed to the Bundesbank and had to be repaid by yesterday. Almost half the funds were repaid in Ecus, the remainder in D-Marks and dollars. A further Ecu2bn owed to the two institutions has been rolled over for another three months.
