March 29, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

SOVIETS SELL AD SPACE -- IN SPACE

The Soviet Union has signed a deal to sell advertising space -- in space, of all places.

Tass press agency said today that Glavkosmos, the Soviet commercial space agency, signed a contract with a Swiss firm that includes selling advertising space on cosmonauts' spacesuits and painting two 6-by-9-foot advertising messages on the hull of the orbiting Mir space station.

Billboards also will be built at launch pads at Baikonur, the main launching area, in Central Asia.
