BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- The 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival opened in Pyongyang and now is going on successfully. Artistes and acrobats from many countries on the five continents and Korean artistes overseas put on stage colourful numbers winning high appreciation of the audience. The festival places becoming more and more animated with each passing day are daily packed to capacity with working people in the city. Festival flags and buntings are fluttering and marks, decorations, large posters and welcome slogans symbolic of the festival can be seen at theatres and halls where performances are given, including the Mansudae Art Theatre, the Pyongyang Grand Theatre and the Pyongyang International Cinema House, and on the streets of the city, adding to the festival atmosphere. The festival which has developed into an authoritative and high-level grand art festival worldwide with the passage of time has been held traditionally from 1982 to celebrate the 15th of April, the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Up until now, more than 700 art troupes and circus groups from almost all countries in the world have participated in the festival and the number of artistes and acrobats involved in it totaled several ten thousands. Among them were many famous artistes including national and international concourse prize winners and people's artistes and merited artistes. More than 100 famous artistes including international music, dance and circus concours and contest entrants are participating in the current festival. During the festival, they will enjoy an impressive, pleasant time with sightseeing, amusement games, etc.
