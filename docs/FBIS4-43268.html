BFN [Text] Following a meeting of party leaders or representatives this morning, House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos said that three Greek-Cypriot parties--DISI [Democratic Rally], AKEL [Restorative Party of the Working People], and the Liberal Party-- and four Turkish-Cypriot parties replied positively to an invitation from the Council of Europe Political Committee to attend a meeting in Strasbourg on 29 June to submit their positions on the Cyprus issue.
