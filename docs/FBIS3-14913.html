Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Vladimir Kazimirov, Russian president's personal representatives in the region, and Russian Deputy Defense Minister Colonel-General Georgiy Kondratyev arrived in Yerevan at noon today. Our correspondent Gevorg Nazaryan reports: [Begin recording] [Nazaryan] The high-ranking guests were received at Zvartnots Airport by Davit Shahnazaryan, the Armenian president's ambassador on special missions and acting head of the State Directorate for National Security, and the Armenian Defense Minister Serzh Sargisyan. The motorcade left for the government guest house, where talks were held. Here is what Valdimir Kazimirov said before the start of the talks. [passage omitted] Russia's mediatory initiative to resolve the Karabakh conflict and issues pertaining to the implementation of the quadripartite agreement signed in Moscow on 16 February among Armenia, Nagorno-Karabakh Republic, Azerbaijan, and Russia were discussed during the talks. The protocol envisages a program for ceasing the fire and the military operations and the withdrawal of the troops from the conflict zone as of 1 March. The Armenian side confirmed that Armenia is ready to assume all the responsibilities emanating from the protocol. [passage omitted] According to plan, the Russian delegation was to leave for Stepanakert today but unfavorable weather conditions canceled the flight. After the talks, President Levon Ter-Petrosyan received Vladimir Kazimirov and Georgiy Kondratyev at 1700. They examined Russia's mediatory initiative for the peaceful resolution of the Karabakh conflict. They stressed that the conflict has now entered a phase in which its resolution is impossible without introducing separating forces. The Russian delegation left for Baku in the evening. [end recording]
