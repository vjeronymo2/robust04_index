BFN [Text] Tehran, May 3, IRNA -- Foreign Ministry here Tuesday appointed Mohammad Taherian as Iran's first extraordinary ambassador and plenipotentiary representative to Bosnia-Herzegovina. The appointment was made at the proposal of Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati and approval of President Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani. Taherian formerly held various positions including Iran's charge d'affaires to Mexico City and to Afghanistan.
