November 17, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

HEADLINE UNFAIRLY RIPPED DOUCETTE

I just had an opportunity to read your "Viewpoint" column in last Saturday's edition of The Times.

I was aghast to see such a large damaging headline applied to such a petty article about Eddie Doucette. I believe Doucette does a great job of selling the Rams without much to work with this year. The article was a small viewpoint; your headline was a large injustice.

THOMAS L. COLLINS

Irvine
