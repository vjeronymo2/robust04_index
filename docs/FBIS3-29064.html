Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Feb. 14 (YONHAP) -- The Economic Planning Board will eliminate 17 jobs for grade one through nine officials, instead of the originally planned seven positions for grade one through four officials, under its downsizing plan. Under the reorganization which will be implemented immediately after President Kim gives the go-ahead next week, the Economic Information and Education Bureau will be reduced to a division responsible for economic information in the Economic Planning Bureau. The three bureau chief-level coordinators under the assistant minister for international policy coordination will be trimmed to two, while a new job of defense budget examination will be created under the assistant minister for budget. The system of sending a bureau chief-level official to a foreign educational institution for study will be scrapped. Several bureau-class offices will be rechristened: For instance, the Fair Trade Policy Bureau of the Fair Trade Commission will be renamed the Policy Bureau. The reorganization plan will also affect some 20 division-class offices: For example, in the Economic Planning Bureau, the manpower development planning division and the social development planning division will be merged into a new manpower division.
