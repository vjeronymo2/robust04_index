940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Receiverships rise by 4% The number of companies entering receivership or administration last month rose 4 per cent to 163, figures published yesterday show. The rise reflected a large number of appointments by banks to companies in the Humberside and Yorkshire areas. But Touche Ross, the accountancy firm that compiled the figures from the official London and Edinburgh Gazettes, said the increase was not significant and there continued to be a general downward trend.
