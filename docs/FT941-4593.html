940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Dartmoor Trust net assets rise Dartmoor Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 127.2p at January 31 against 81.5p a year earlier. Net revenue for the nine months to the end of January was Pounds 2.45m (Pounds 2.36m) for earnings per share of 10.19p (10.16p). An unchanged third interim dividend of 2.7p makes a maintained total to date of 7.7p.
