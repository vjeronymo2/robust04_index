Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In 1993, the province's total national defense science and technology industrial output value registered a growth 31.86 percent higher than the province's average industrial output value growth, while the total amount of taxes and profits realized by the province's national defense science and technology industry recorded a 267 percent increase over the year before. Yang Zhengwu, provincial party committee deputy secretary, at a provincial national defense science and technology industry conference being held at the moment, delivered a speech in which he called on all national defense science and technology industrial enterprises to establish more lateral economic associations in various forms within the province; take the initiative to contract more projects of the province's "huge dragon" and "torch" programs; make persistent efforts to do away with rigid and conservative ideological concepts; cultivate a strong sense of market competition; adapt itself more to Hunan's overall economic growth, major trades and professions development, and international economic development as well; and make unremitting efforts to open up new vistas.
