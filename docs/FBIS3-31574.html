Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Correspondent Ivica Saric reporting for Croatian Radio from Vitez] [Excerpt] According to the HVO's [Croatian Defense Council] operational zone in Vitez [largest Croat enclave in central Bosnia], the town center came under an artillery attack again last night. Two civilians were wounded. According to the same source, the Muslim Army is deploying fresh units on the northern approaches to Vitez. The command of the Vitez operational zone links this with the lifting of the blockade of Sarajevo and a large influx of infantry belonging to the Muslim Army's First Corps [based in Sarajevo] to Croat areas in central Bosnia.
