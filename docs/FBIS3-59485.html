Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Text] Zagreb (HINA) -- On Friday, Croatian President Dr. Franjo Tudjman opened the first mint of independent Croatia at the Croatian Monetary Office, where the Croatian currency, the kuna and lipa (100 lipas to a kuna), will be produced. On this occasion, Tudjman and other guests were greeted by Djuro Crnjak, head of the Croatian Monetary Office, and addressed by academician Dalimir Brozovic, who called to mind how the Croatian currency was created. President Tudjman and his entourage also visited the Croatian Printing Office.
