BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Anatoliy Krasnov] [Text] Moscow 29 April TASS -- Russian-South Korean relations are definitely on the rise, declared Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev at a meeting with South Korean Defence Minister Yi Pyong-tae, who is in Moscow on an official visit. "I am glad that the military aspect is being given due attention in our relations. Now, we are not only not looking at each other through target-sights, but are ready to cooperate in creating a joint security system among states friendly to us," Kozyrev said. During their meeting, the two ministers are expected to discuss the situation on the Korean peninsula, other international problems, and their bilateral relations.
