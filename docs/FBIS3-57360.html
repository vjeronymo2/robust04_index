Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Edict No. 173 of the Russian Federation president "On the Federal Assembly State Duma Draft Decree Declaring an Amnesty," dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 21 January 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] Taking into consideration the many appeals from private individuals and public organizations and requests from ther families of convicted and indicted individuals, guided by the principle of humanity, and in connection with the adoption of the Russian Federation Constitution, I decree that: 1. An act of amnesty should be proclaimed for certain categories of people convicted of crimes which do not represent an increased risk to the public. 2. The Federal Assembly State Duma draft decree declaring an amnesty should be approved so that the Russian Federation president can submit it for immediate scrutiny to the Federal Assembly State Duma in accordance with the procedure laid down in Paragraph "e" of Article 103 of the Russian Federation Constitution. 3. This Edict comes into force from the moment of its signing. [Signed] President of the Russian Federation B. Yeltsin [Dated] Moscow, the Kremlin, 21 January 1994 No. 173
