940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Rise in direct activity helps Bradstock Bradstock, the insurance broker, yesterday reported a 5 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.57m to Pounds 4.81m in the six months to March 31 1994, writes Richard Lapper. Earnings per share increased to 5.35p (5.32p) and the interim dividend is raised by 3 per cent to 1.6p (1.55p). Turnover, including income from acquisitions, amounted to Pounds 16.2m, against Pounds 14.4m, and administrative expenses rose to Pounds 12.6m (Pounds 11.3m). Investment and other income fell to Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 1.48m). A 24 per cent increase in income from direct insurance helped offset a decline in reinsurance business.
