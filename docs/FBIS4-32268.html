BFN [Text] Prague June 22 (CTK) -- Representatives of the Liberal Social Union (LSU) and the Bohemian-Moravian Centre Party (CMSS) have agreed on their parties' merger by the end of September, LSU Chairman Frantisek Trnka said at a press conference today. The new party is likely to be named the Bohemian-Moravian Centre Union, Trnka said. The opposition CMSS and LSU have 9 and 8 deputies in the 200-seat Czech parliament respectively.
