BFN [Text] The Tajik opposition is ready to sign an agreement on a cease-fire along the Tajik-Afghan border for the period of the inter-Tajik talks, Otakhon Latifi, the head of the Tajik opposition delegation at the inter-Tajik talks in Tehran, told INTERFAX on Saturday [18 June]. The opposition intends to present a draft of such an agreement to the delegation representing official Dushanbe at the second round of inter-Tajik talks that have opened in Tehran. It plans to look for ways of settling the political situation in Tajikistan peacefully, to examine the problem of the return home of all Tajik refugees, and the issue of the future constitutional system. The talks are being held under UN auspices.
