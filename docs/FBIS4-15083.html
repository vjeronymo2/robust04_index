BFN [Text] The Kemerovo regional legislature unanimously elected Amangeldiy Tuleyev its chairman. His candidacy was not opposed. He previously chaired the former regional soviet. Tuleyev, 50, is a Federation Council member. In the 1991 presidential elections he won the fourth, last but one, place. Now he is in opposition to Federal Authorities. In its address to the local population, the Kemerovo legislature declared its willingness to continue the constructive work of the former regional soviet.
