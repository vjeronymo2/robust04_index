Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres is currently meeting with U.S. Undersecretary of State Peter Tarnoff to discuss ways to resume negotiations between Israel and the PLO. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz adds that Tarnoff will shortly meet with Yasir 'Arafat's adviser, Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, and will hear about the PLO's demands for the resumption of the negotiations.
