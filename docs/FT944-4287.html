941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / Cabinet reshuffle in Tanzania Tanzania's President Ali Hassan Mwinyi has reshuffled his cabinet after mounting pressure from western donors, angry about corruption. He has sacked Mr Kighoma Malima, his finance minister and replaced him with 42-year-old Mr Jakaya Mrisho Kikwette, a staunch insider of the ruling Revolutionary party. He has fired prime minister John Malecela, a strong reformist, in favour of Mr Cleopa Msuya, who is tipped to become presidential candidate in multi-party elections due next year.
