921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / International Company News: CBS returns to the black CBS, the US broadcasting group which owns one of the country's leading television networks, has reported third-quarter net profits of Dollars 42.7m, a significant recovery when compared with a loss of Dollars 169.1m suffered in the same period of last year. The profit, on revenues 7.5 per cent up at Dollars 672.8m, translates into earnings per share of Dollars 2.76, against a loss of Dollars 11.11 per share a year ago. Third-quarter earnings a year ago included a Dollars 195.5m provision related to broadcasts of professional baseball and football. CBS, controlled by Mr Larry Tisch, had nine-month net income of Dollars 53.8m, against a loss of Dollars 95.2m in the same period of 1991. Revenues were Dollars 2.53bn, against to Dollars 2.09bn.
