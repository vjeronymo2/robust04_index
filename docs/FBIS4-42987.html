BFN [Press release issued by the UK Ministry of Defence: "Nuclear Detargeting"] [Text] In accordance with the agreement signed by the Prime Minister and President Yeltsin in Moscow on 15 February, all of the United Kingdom's strategic missiles were detargeted by 30 May, the guidance computers on UK strategic missiles no longer routinely hold targeting information, and the primary target assignments held in readiness for insertion into the missiles will be points in the open ocean. This step is a positive demonstration of the developing cooperative relationship between the United Kingdom and the Russian Federation.
