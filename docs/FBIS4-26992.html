BFN [Text] Shenzhen, 27 Jun (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE)--A few days ago, at a conference of all the city's cadres at or above the departmental level, Shenzhen City Mayor Li Youwei conveyed Jiang Zemin's comments made on a visit to the city on six aspects of Shenzhen's relations with Hong Kong. They are: One, utilization: utilize Hong Kong to help develop the city; two, reliance: rely on Hong Kong in moving toward the international community; three, taking reference: learn to do things according to Hong Kong and international conventions; four, making ties: establishing ties with Hong Kong and branching out in the hinterland; five, making distinctions: distinguishing between Hong Kong and one country, two systems; and six, convergence: converging with Hong Kong for mutual benefits. Li Youwei said: As the "1997" Hong Kong's transition is drawing closer, Shenzhen will play an important role in ensuring a stable transition, smooth return, and long-term prosperity for Hong Kong Shenzhen is a socialist special economic zone, and at no time is it an extension or expansion of Hong Kong. We must be clear-headed and have a clear understanding of this.
