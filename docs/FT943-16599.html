940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Warning on cost of civil court cases Too many civil court cases cost more than the value of the dispute, the Law Society says today. Even where legal action would be cost-effective, many people cannot afford it and are not eligible for legal aid. The Law Society highlights these as serious problems in the civil justice system in its submission to Lord Mackay, the Lord Chancellor, who is reviewing expenditure on civil litigation and legal aid.
