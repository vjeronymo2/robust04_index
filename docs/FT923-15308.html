920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / International Company News: Loral buys the rest of Loral Aerospace LORAL, the US defence group, announced yesterday that it had agreed to pay 6.1m shares of common stock for the remaining 41 per cent stake in Loral Aerospace-owned Lehman Brothers Merchant Banking Partnerships, a division of Wall Street securities house Shearson Lehman. The deal is worth just under Dollars 200m, and leaves Loral as sole owner of Loral Aerospace. Full ownership of the aerospace company will boost its annual earnings by about 10 cents a share, said Loral.
