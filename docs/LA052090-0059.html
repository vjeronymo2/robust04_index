May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NO PREVIEW FAN

Concerning Dirk and Erik Sanders' May 6 letter on movie previews: They are not alone.

While viewing "Crocodile Dundee II" a couple of years ago, I realized I was totally bored. Why? All the best scenes and lines had already been shown in previews I had seen before the movie's opening.

Now I stand in the theater lobby and wait for the previews to end before I enter.

MARI A. USZLER

Los Angeles
