920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Brussels backs aid for Bull Brussels yesterday approved FFr6.6bn (Pounds 670m) in French government aid for Bull, the loss-making computer company, on the ground it would help make the company viable without giving it an undue boost over competitors, David Buchan in Brussels and Alan Cane report. The European Commission decided France's FFr4bn capital injection in Bull, coupled with a FFr2.6bn R&D grant, constituted a state aid Brussels is empowered to control. The UK called the decision 'disappointing'.
