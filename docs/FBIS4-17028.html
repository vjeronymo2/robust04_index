BFN [Text] Stepanakert, April 20 (SNARK)--Today morning, after intensive artillery shelling, the units of Azerbaijani army passed to offensive at south-eastern sector of Karabakh-Azerbaijan front. Press-service of Karabakh Defence Army informs, a great number of military hardware and infantry is taking part in the offensive. According to data received by midday, there are stubborn fightings at this sector, and both of the sides have losses. Today, it is calm at the others sectors of the front, including southern part of Mardakertskiy region where there have been furious fightings for the last ten days.
