941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Finsbury Trust Finsbury Trust, the special situations trust, had a net asset value per share of 174.4p at the September 30 interim stage, against 181.1p at the March year end and 152.1p at the half-way stage last year. Net revenue for the half year amounted to Pounds 436,000, down from Pounds 580,000, giving earnings of 2p (2.4p). The interim dividend is held at 1.2p.
