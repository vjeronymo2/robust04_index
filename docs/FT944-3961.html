941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Cheam up at Pounds 2.8m Cheam Group, the south-west London and Surrey water supply company, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.98m to Pounds 2.79m in the half year to September 30, on turnover ahead from Pounds 7.75m to Pounds 8.6m. Earnings per share were 25.1p (21.6p) and the interim dividend rises to 5.6p (4.7p).
