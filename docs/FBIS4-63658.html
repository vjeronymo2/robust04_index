BFN [Text] The political situation in the Crimea has been discussed at a meeting held by the medzhlis of the Crimean Tatars in Simferopol. A resolution was adopted on the threat of an armed conflict in the Crimea. The resolution points out the illegal nature of recent edicts issued by the Crimean president and Supreme Soviet that are worsening the political situation. Due to this, the resolution said, all national representative institutions of the Crimean Tatars should comply with the norms of international law, the Ukrainian Constitution and its laws, and the decisions by the kurultay and the medzhlis of the Crimean Tatars. All necessary measures have to be taken to prevent our countrymen from getting embroiled in a standoff with the Ukrainian state, the resolution by the medzhlis of the Crimean Tatars stated.
