BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 10 -- The government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam today decided to recognize the Republic of Macedonia. The same day at the United Nations Headquarter in New York, Ambassador Le Van Bang, acting permanent representative of the SRV to the United Nations, and Dr. Denko Maleski, Macedonian ambassador and permanent representative to the United Nations signed a joint-communique on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries at ambassadorial level.
