DATES: This notice is effective September 22, 1994. Geological Survey LAC Mineral, Inc.; Contribution Acceptance AGENCY: U.S. Geological Survey, Interior. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: Notice is hereby given that the U.S. Geological Survey has accepted from LAC Minerals (USA), Inc. a contribution of $7,500 to support geologic studies of mineral deposits, particularly the studies of samples from the El Indio district in Chile. DATES: This notice is effective September 22, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Information on the work is available to the public upon request at the following location: U.S. Geological Survey, Branch of Central Mineral Resources, Box 25046, MS&hyph;935, Denver Federal Center, Denver, Colorado 80225.
