BFN [Text] Channel Africa reported this morning that the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] has elected Afonso Dhlakama, its leader, as Renamo candidate in the presidential elections scheduled for October. Quoting the Renamo secretary general, Channel Africa reported that the decision was made at the minicongress being held at the Renamo headquarters in Maringue. The source said that Dhlakama's candidacy was unanimously approved by all attending the meeting. Renamo's congress, which was scheduled to wind up today, is now focusing on issues relating to electoral strategy and it could last longer than initially expected.
