BFN [Text] Bonn -- At a Western-Russian foreign ministers' conference a general cease-fire should be agreed with the Bosnian parties to the conflict, and the path for a political solution should be cleared. This was proposed by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev today after talks with his German counterpart Klaus Kinkel. No date has yet been set for the foreign ministers' conference. During his exchange of views with Kinkel, Kozyrev stated that a military intervention should only be the very last resort. The Serbs should know that there is a limit that must not be exceeded. Kozyrev mentioned the shelling of the hospital in Gorazde in this respect.
