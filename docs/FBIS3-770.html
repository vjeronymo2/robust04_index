Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fifty-two magisterial districts have been declared unrest areas as a result of the events in Bophuthatswana. The minister of law and order, Mr. Hernus Kriel, said this step had been taken to prevent the possible spread of violence from Bophuthatswana to South Africa. The areas include virtually all towns and cities in the PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] area, as well as large parts of the western Transvaal, the northern Transvaal the [Orange] Free State, and northern Cape.
