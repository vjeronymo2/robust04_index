930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Venturi Trust Venturi Investment Trust announced an 18 per cent increase, from 244.01p to 287.87p, in net asset value of the capital indexed shares over the 12 months to March 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 241,520 (Pounds 214,856) for earnings per income share of 3.58p to 4.03p. The total dividend is raised from 3.26p to 3.45p, with a final of 1.99p.
