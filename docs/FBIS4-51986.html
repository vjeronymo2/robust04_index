BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 2 (KCNA) -- The Zairean Society for the Study of Kim Chong-il's Works was inaugurated in Kinshasa on April 22. Addressing the inaugural meeting, the chairman of the preparatory committee for the inauguration of the society said: The immortal chuche idea founded by respected President Kim Il-song is being further developed and enriched by His Excellency Kim Chong-il. The chuche idea is a guiding idea of revolution and a banner of struggle to realize independence of the popular masses. He said all people who espouse the chuche idea deem it their supreme desire to have the august name of His Excellency Kim Chong-il, and stressed the need to learn from him and deeply study his works. Messages of greetings to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted there.
