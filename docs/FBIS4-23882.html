BFN [Text] One UN soldier was killed and another one wounded on Friday [17 June] when they were traveling near Shyorongi where fighting was taking place between Rwandan Patriotic Front [RPF] forces and government troops. The RPF had earlier warned the UNAMIR not to use Kigali-Ruhengeri road without the consent of the RPF forces, but contrary to the warning the UN officers went through the roads. Their vehicle ended up landing in an ambush where fighting was taking place. The RPF soldiers fired at the vehicle and on coming closer found it was UN soldiers. One officer, a major by rank, died instantly and one more was injured. The injured was given first aid and later taken to hospital.
