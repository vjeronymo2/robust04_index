941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: Unit trust sales fall sharply - At a Glance Unit trust sales for the third quarter are down to Pounds 1.88bn from Pounds 2.81bn the previous quarter. The third quarter is traditionally one of the weakest for sales but in the corresponding period last year net sales totalled Pounds 2.61bn. Monthly figures also fell. In September they were a net Pounds 470.7m, the second lowest figure this year, down from August's net sales of Pounds 767.1m. However, net sales of unit trusts for the year are still higher at Pounds 7.15bn than for the same period last year when they amounted to Pounds 6.95bn. Last year the unit trust industry attracted a record Pounds 9.14bn of net investment.
