BFN [From the "This Morning" Newscast] [Text] President Violeta Barrios de Chamorro today toured several municipalities in Masaya and Rivas where she inaugurated several judicial offices in San Juan de Oriente, Diria, Potosi, [word indistinct], and San Jorge. At the end of her working tour, in San Jorge, President Chamorro spoke to the accompanying reporters and told them that the military bill will be presented to the Nicaraguan parliament today. She also said that the list of three candidates for the Supreme Court justice position will be sent to the National Assembly this week. Referring to the constitutional reforms, the president said that they are being studied and stressed that she wants true reforms, not a constituent assembly. She was categorical in stating that lay education will continue and will remain unchanged; consequently, people cannot be asked to change their minds.
