7. Midwest Power Systems Inc. [Docket No. ER94&hyph;157&hyph;000] Take notice that on January 4, 1994, Midwest Power Systems, Inc. Niagara Mohawk Power Corporation [Docket No. ER94&hyph;331&hyph;000] Take notice that on December 21, 1993, Niagara Mohawk Power Corporation (Niagara Mohawk), tendered for filing an agreement between Niagara Mohawk and the Village of Richmondville (Richmondville) dated November 9, 1992, providing for certain borderline sales to Richmondville. Niagara Mohawk is filing this under the general amnesty announced by the Commission in its final order issued on July 30, 1993 in Docket No. PL93&hyph;2&hyph;002. The November 9, 1992, agreement provides for Niagara Mohawk sales to Richmondville at various points of delivery near the border of Niagara Mohawk's and Richmondville's service territories in the Village of Richmondville and County of Schoharie, State of New York. The rates contained in the agreement are Niagara Mohawk's standard borderline rates approved by the New York State Public Service Commission under Niagara Mohawk's PSC Tariff No. 207, SC No. 2. The effective date of November 9, 1992, is requested by Niagara Mohawk.
