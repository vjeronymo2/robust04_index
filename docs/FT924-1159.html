921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Kleen-e-ze returns to profit with Pounds 86,000 Kleen-e-ze Holdings, a distributor of consumer products, returned profits of Pounds 86,000 pre-tax for the year to end-August compared with previous losses of Pounds 1.14m. The improvement reflected steps taken in prior years to eliminate loss-making activities together with continuing progress in trading conditions. The shares rose 10p to 66p. Turnover was static at Pounds 45.5m. Exceptional provisions accounted for Pounds 377,000 (Pounds 153,000) and interest charges for Pounds 183,000 (Pounds 444,000). Earnings per share emerged at 0.6p (losses 16.73p). The exceptionals, together with extraordinary debits of Pounds 265,000 (Pounds 675,000), reflected continuing rationalisation of group activities. Kleen-e-ze also announced that it had reached agreement for the sale of Molly Housewares for about Pounds 950,000.
