931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Improved trading for John Tams Improved trading conditions, particularly in the earthenware sector, enabled John Tams Group to report pre-tax profits for the six months to the end of September ahead by 32 per cent at Pounds 501,000, against Pounds 379,000. There were problems at the Royal Grafton bone china operation but the company said the situation had improved since August. Turnover for this USM company was Pounds 11.6m (Pounds 9.93m) with exports increasing from 45 per cent to a little more than half of the total. Trading profit was ahead at Pounds 614,000 (Pounds 606,000), but interest charges were lower at Pounds 113,000 (Pounds 227,000). Earnings per share came out at 1.42p (1.25p) and an interim dividend of 1.6p (1.59p) is declared.
