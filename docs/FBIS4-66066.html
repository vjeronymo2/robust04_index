BFN [Excerpt] Italian Foreign Minister Antonio Martino who is currently in Paris attending the Conference on European Stability, has called the remarks of French President Francois Mitterrand unacceptable. Mitterrand, referring to the new Italian Government and the figure of Silvio Berlusconi, spoke of a fearful inroad on democracy. Martino said that these remarks were isolated and were the only ones uttered by a head of state. [passage omitted]
