May 4, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Cal State Long Beach will play Rutgers Newark and USC will face Ball State in the NCAA men's volleyball Final Four tonight at George Mason University in Fairfax, Va.

The 49ers (27-6) are ranked first and Rutgers Newark (27-14) is No. 12. The Trojans (23-5) are No. 2 and Ball State (27-10) is No. 10.

Tonight's winners will play for the championship Saturday night.
