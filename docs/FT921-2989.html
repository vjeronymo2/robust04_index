920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / UK Company News: ISA Intl declines 25% to Pounds 2.1m ISA International, the Bradford-based computer accessories distributor, reported a 25 per cent contraction - from Pounds 2.83m to Pounds 2.12m - in pre-tax profits for 1991. However, Mr John Parkinson, chairman, said the outcome arrested the decline of the second half of 1990. Net interest charges fell from Pounds 649,000 to Pounds 481,000. Borrowings at the year-end amounted to Pounds 603,000 reducing gearing below 10 per cent. Turnover improved 7 per cent to Pounds 76.4m. Earnings per share dipped to 4.31p (5.84p), but the proposed final dividend is raised to 0.951p for a total of 1.365p (1.288p).
