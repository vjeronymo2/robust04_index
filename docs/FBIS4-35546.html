BFN [Text] Our correspondent has learned that Egypt, in its capacity as chairman of the Non-Aligned Movement [NAM] conference, is currently working to bring the views of Iraq and Kuwait closer on the final statement of the conference, which will be issued Friday [3 June]. The draft Egypt prepared is based on Iraq's recognition of Kuwait, its sovereignty, its safety, and its regional border. The draft also notes progress in Iraq's position and the need to consider the suffering of the Iraqi people.
