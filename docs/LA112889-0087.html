November 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

WEEKEND BOX OFFICE

* In spite of heavy studio competition, Samuel Goldwyn's "Henry V," written, directed by and starring Britain's Kenneth Branagh, is holding its own on four screens. The film grossed $97,902, for a per-screen average of $19,580.

(Figures are for five-day holiday weekend.)
