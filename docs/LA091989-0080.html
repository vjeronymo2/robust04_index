September 19, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

LOS ALAMITOS REMAINS ATOP PREP FOOTBALL POLL

Despite allowing 50 points in two games, Los Alamitos High School remains the top-ranked team in the Orange County Sportswriters' Assn. prep football poll.

Los Alamitos earned all seven first-place votes with second-ranked Mission Viejo earning all seven second-place votes. The top five teams in the poll compete in Division III and three are members of the South Coast League.

The writers' poll:
