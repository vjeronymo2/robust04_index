940408 FT 08 APR 94 / UK Company News: Regal Hotel Regal Hotel Group, the acquisitive operator of 3-star provincial hotels, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 573,000 in the year to January 2. Profit included an exceptional credit of Pounds 1.24m related to its Bramhope offshoot. Turnover in the period under review amounted to Pounds 2.68m.
