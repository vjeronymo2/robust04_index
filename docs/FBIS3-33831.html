Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New York, Jan 31 (INA)--Iraq renewed its protest to the United Nations against the U.S. planes violations of its airspace with the intention of provocation, reconnaissance and dropping flares. Iraq's permanent representative to the United Nations said in a letter to Secretary General Dr. Butrus-Ghali that planes had during January 10-20 flown 728 hostile sorties over various parts of Iraq in addition to dropping flares and surveillance flights. Iraq's representative demanded distributing the letter as a Security Council document.
