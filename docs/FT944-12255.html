941102 FT 02 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Rexmore expands 22% to Pounds 838,000 Rexmore, the upholstery supplier, reported a 22 per cent increase in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 688,000 to Pounds 838,000. Turnover, for the period to October 1, was little changed at Pounds 15.3m compared with Pounds 15.5m. Operating profits showed a 6 per cent improvement to Pounds 962,000 because of tight cost management, the directors said, while interest payments were almost halved, from Pounds 219,000 to Pounds 124,000. Earnings per share were 4.07p (4.44p) while the interim dividend is 1.3p (1p).
