920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Observer: Customer service Watch those water bills. Observer has just received a press release from the old North West Water board, which now bills itself as the 'world's fourth-largest water and wastewater undertaking'. It is spending, or rather 'investing', more than Pounds 800,000 on an 'intensive fellowship programme' for five employees. They are being sent to the US for two years to study the latest developments in artificial intelligence.
