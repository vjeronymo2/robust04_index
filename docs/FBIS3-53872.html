Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat returned to Tunisia last night after a brief visit to Egypt. During his visit he met President Husni Mubarak and the U.S. commerce secretary to discuss the outcome of the Palestinian-Israeli talks on arrangements for self-rule and the U.S. contribution toward consolidating self-rule in the occupied territories. [passage omitted] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, President 'Arafat's political adviser and head of the Palestinian team negotiating with Israel, said that 'Arafat will most probably return to Cairo on Sunday [23 January] after meeting Israeli Foreign Affairs Minister Shim'on Peres tomorrow. Both men will be in Oslo to attend the funeral of the Norwegian foreign affairs minister.
