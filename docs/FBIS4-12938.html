BFN [Text] Dr. Sa'ib 'Urayqat, head of the Palestinian delegation to the peace negotiations, has held Israel fully responsible for the escalation of acts of violence in the occupied territories. In a statement last night, 'Urayqat was quoted as saying that the acts of violence are generated by the Israeli occupation troops' daily massacres targeting the Palestinian people. He stressed that ending the Israeli occupation of the occupied territories and halting the Israeli Army's acts of terrorism is the only way to end the acts of violence.
