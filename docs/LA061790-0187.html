June 17, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

DAMEWORTH THIRD IN INVITATIONAL

Bryan Dameworth of Agoura High placed third in the two-mile in the International Prep Invitational track and field meet in Elmhurst, Ill., Saturday.

Dameworth finished in 9 minutes 4.24 seconds.

Shawn Mackay of East Elgin Secondary School in Canada won the event in 9:00.76 and Andy Maris of White River High in Buckley, Wash., was second in 9:01.62.
