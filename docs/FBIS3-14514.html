Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] People's Assembly Speaker Dr. Ahmad Fathi Surur today called for an objective stance to prevent the proliferation of nuclear weapons in the Middle East. He called on Israel to sign the nonproliferation treaty, emphasizing that Israel must believe that peace is the real and lasting guarantee. Delivering Egypt's speech to the Interparliamentary Union [IPU] meeting in Paris today, Dr. Surur condemned the Hebron massacre and the aggression against unarmed Palestinians. He expressed deep concern at the hazards threatening peace as a result of such incidents, demanding effctive guarantees to maintain security in the occupied territories. The People's Assembly speaker called on the international community to be firm and resolute on the Bosnian problem to avoid the spreading of clashes to other areas in the Balkans. He called also for encouraging an African role in ending the civil war tragedy in Somalia, particularly after the withdrawal of foreign troops. He called on the Somalis to prepare themselves for shouldering the responsibility.
