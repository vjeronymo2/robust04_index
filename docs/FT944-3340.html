941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Hyundai Engineering and Construction - Ventures and contracts Hyundai Engineering and Construction, a member of South Korea's Hyundai group, has won a Dollars 58m contract to lay high-voltage power cables in Kuwait. The construction, due for completion by October 1996, is to connect a thermo-electric power plant at Kuwait's northern town of Sabiya with sub-stations in the western towns of Jahara and Sulaibiya, a company spokesman said.
