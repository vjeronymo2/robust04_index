Special Emphasis Panel in Physics; Meeting In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting. Name: Special Emphasis Panel in Physics. Date and Time: April 15, 1994; 8:30 a.m.&hyph;4:30 p.m. Place: Rm. 1020, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA 22230. Type of Meeting: Closed. Contact person: Dr. Rolf M. Sinclair, Program Director for Special Programs, Division of Physics, room 1015, National Science Foundation. Telephone: (703) 306&hyph;1890.
