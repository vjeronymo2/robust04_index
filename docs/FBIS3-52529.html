Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The official announcement on the resumption of Israeli-PLO talks has just been issued in Jerusalem and Tunis. The statement was made simultaneously by Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, head of the Israeli Liaison Committee, and Abu-Mazin [Mahmud 'Abbas], head of the Palestinian delegation. Following is the text of the announcement: The two sides reaffirm their total commitment to the letter and spirit of the Declaration of Principles [DOP]. They believe that the talks in Norway, Versailles, and Cairo will help to achieve an agreement that will quickly turn the DOP into a new reality on the ground. Once an agreement is reached, it will be impossible to change it unilaterally. The two sides have agreed to resume the Taba negotiations on the basis of the DOP and the understandings reached in Cairo. [Tel Aviv IDF Radio in Hebrew at 0730 GMT on 6 January adds that "the talks will resume on 10 January. Sources in Jerusalem expressed their satisfaction with the compromise formulated by Peres and Nabil Sha'th"]
