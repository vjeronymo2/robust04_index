930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Superdrug claims breakthrough SUPERDRUG, the discount chain which is part of the Kingfisher group, yesterday claimed a breakthrough in its campaign to cut perfume prices by announcing its first contract with a fragrance house willing to supply it directly. The contract, with Worth Fragrances, covers two lesser mass-market brands, Je Reviens and Carven Ma Griffe. Worth welcomed Superdrug's price-cutting drive and said it was lowering prices.
