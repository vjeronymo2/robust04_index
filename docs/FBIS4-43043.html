BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl, CDU [Christian Democratic Union] chairman, has described the showing of the CDU and CSU [Christian Social Union] in the European election as a "very good starting position" for the forthcoming state parliament elections and for the Bundestag election in October. Following meetings of the CDU presidium and executive, Kohl said in Bonn this afternoon that the CDU and CSU have "reached an important stage." However, there are no grounds for sounding an all-clear. In the coming weeks and months, the CDU and CSU will have to fight for every vote, he stated. Kohl is also confident that the Free Democratic Party will again enter the new Bundestag.
