BFN [Interview with Hungarian Socialist Party Chairman Gyula Horn by unidentified BILD reporter; place and date not given: "Germany Will Be My First Trip"] [Text] [BILD] Will the Communists return to power now? [Horn] No. A return to communism in Hungary is out of the question. The wheel of history cannot be reversed. Despite the great election victory, we in the Socialist Party want to rule in a coalition. [BILD] Are you going to stop the privatization? [Horn] No, the privatization will be continued, but under parliamentary control. We do not want to deprive anyone of his legal property. [BILD] What effects will the Socialists' election victory have on German-Hungarian relations? [Horn] Hungarian-German cooperation has great importance in the development of our economy. We need the Germans to expand our economy and become full members of the European Union as quickly as possible. Therefore my first foreign trip will be to Germany.
