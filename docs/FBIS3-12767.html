Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India has sought closer cooperation of the European Union and the United States in dealing with Pakistan's resolution on Kashmir at the UN Human Rights Commission meeting in Geneva. The external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh, told the envoys of the European troika and the U.S. charge d' affaires in New Delhi today that neither the letter nor the spirit of the Pakistan resolution is acceptable to New Delhi. It will also not help in improving the situation in Jammu and Kashmir as it is the direct result of the Pakistan-sponsored terrorism. He asked them to convey India's position on the Pakistan resolution and hoped that the delegations of these countries will work closely with the Indian delegation in Geneva. The meeting was held at the request of the envoys.
