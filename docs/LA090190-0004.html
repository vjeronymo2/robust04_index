September 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FEINSTEIN, WILSON CAMPAIGNS FOR GOVERNOR

The debate over who did what in the S&L debacle is a subordinate issue in the contest for California's governorship.

My concern is "Republicanism."

Years of promoting and provoking anti-women, anti-minority, anti-civil rights and liberties, anti-peace, anti-self-determinism, anti-consumer legislation, policies and attitudes in Washington and Sacramento have solidified my determination to avoid any candidate, local or national, who choses to label him/herself Republican.

LOUIS ST. MARTIN

Pomona
