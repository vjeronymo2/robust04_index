Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kulbitskiy] [Text] Bonn March 10 TASS -- Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev who arrived in Germany on a visit on Wednesday began a tour of the country today. Grachev is in Germany on a visit at the invitation of his German counterpart Volker Ruehe. The Russian defence minister visited the 31st Airborne Brigade stationed in Oldenburg, Lower Saxony. He familiarised himself with the training programme used in the brigade, tested food from the army canteen and attended the demonstration of an automatic device for landing from the Ch-53 helicopter and transportation of a Wiesel combat vehicle by the same craft.
