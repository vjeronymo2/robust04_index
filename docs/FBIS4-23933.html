BFN [Text] The military situation in the city of Kuito remains unaltered. Clashes are under way and the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] forces are being reinforced with contingents from Huambo and Cuando Cubango Provinces. Many people are still dying in the Bie capital. The atmosphere of war in Kuito is characterized by gunpowder, blood and hunger, and the main victims are civilians whom the UNITA rebels have decided to strike with their Machiavellian actions with the aim of taking over the city at all costs.
