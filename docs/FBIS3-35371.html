Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Muhammad 'Allam: "Sha'th Tells AL-HAYAH: No Intelligence Agreement With Israel"] [Text] Cairo -- Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and head of the Palestinian delegation to the Taba negotiations, has stated that there is no cooperation agreement between the intelligence organs of the PLO and Israel. He emphasized that "there was no agreement of that kind" during last week's Taba negotiations. In a statement to AL-HAYAH, he said that the agreement on coordination in military and security affairs was about means of preventing friction between the Israeli and Palestinian police forces and promoting the appropriate mechanism for that. He added: "That is a military-security agreement 100 percent and has nothing whasoever to do with intelligence or exchange of information. There is a big difference."
