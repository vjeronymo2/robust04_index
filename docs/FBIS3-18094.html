Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mahmud Va'ezi, the Iranian deputy foreign minister in charge of Euro-American affairs, accompanied by the Iranian ambassadors to Bonn and Brussels, met for discussions Egon Klepsch, the president of the European Parliament, at the parliament building in Brussels. In the meeting, Mr. Va'ezi stressed the expansion of relations between the Islamic Republic of Iran and the European Parliament, and said such contacts were useful and effective for better mutual understanding. Then Mr. Klepsch, referring to the significance and position of Iran in the region said: the Islamic Republic of Iran is an important country that could help solve regional crises and establish world peace and stability. Finally the speaker of European Parliament said the establishment of a friendship committee between the European Parliament and Iran was necessary for the mutual understanding of both sides.
