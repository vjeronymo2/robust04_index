BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 8 June -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk confirmed on Wednesday that he has not changed his mind to run for presidency in the presidential elections to be held on 26 June. "Many good things have been done during my presidency," Kravchuk told ITAR-TASS. He admitted, however, that there were also mistakes, both subjective and objective. "If I do not embark on the path of building and strengthening the state and pursuing the course of peace and accord, I would not be able to tell the people and myself why I did not do what I could," he continued. "That's why the participation in the elections is the continuation of my desire to do everything possible so that Ukraine could continue to develop," Kravchuk said.
