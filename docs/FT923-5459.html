920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Pounds 18bn investment in N Sea forecast INVESTMENT in the North Sea could total more than Pounds 18bn in the next three years, a rise of 55 per cent over the past three years, according to research by Arthur Andersen's Petroleum Services group. Total output is likely to rise to 2.5m to 2.8m barrels of oil a day by 1995, from 2.0m to 2.1m b/d this year. Government revenues are expected to be about Pounds 12bn over the next three years. Nine offshore fields are expected to start production this year, compared with three last year. British Borneo Petroleum said it had reached a conditional agreement to acquire the 10 per cent interest of Superior Oil, a subsidiary of the US Mobil Corporation, in the North Sea's Victor gas field for about Pounds 21.4m.
