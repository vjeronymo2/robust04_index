June 2, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

REACTION TO ANTIVIVISECTION 'ADVER-TORIAL'

The media have been notoriously pro-vivisection, because the powers that be want it that way.

Big business rides on funds for animal research. The chemical companies and their sister pharmaceutical companies, together with manufacturers that test products on animals, sponsor many of our television shows and place costly ads in newspapers.

I was amazed at The Times' unbiased portrayal of this long-overdue commercial and the SUPRESS organization in general.

GINGER GULLIE

Glendale
