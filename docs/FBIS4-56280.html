BFN [Text] The commissions of the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP] will meet in Havana on 30 April, the day before the special session of the ANPP. The commissions will meet at the Nico Lopez National School of the Communist Party of Cuba and at the headquarters of the ANPP to study issues related to their spheres of work. These meetings come before the May Day special session of ANPP where delegates will focus on the internal economic situation to find solutions to the financial problems Cuba faces.
