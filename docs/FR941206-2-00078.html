Authority Excepted Category D Field office officials are not redelegated authority under this Notice. The authority redelegated in Section B above also does not include the authority to issue or waive regulations. Section D. Authority to Further Redelegate The Authority redelegated in Section B above may be further redelegated in writing by the appropriate Field Office staff to other Field Office staff so as to maximize office efficiency. In cases where authority is redelegated to staff not reporting to that Field Office official, prior concurrence of the Assistant Secretary, or appropriate DAS, is required.
