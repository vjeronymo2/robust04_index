EFFECTIVE DATE: March, 22, 1994. Customs Service [T.D. 94&hyph;30] Customs Approval of Accutest Services, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger AGENCY: U.S. Customs Service, Treasury. ACTION: Notice of approval of Accutest Services, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger.

SUMMARY: Accutest Services, Inc., of Corpus Christi, Texas has recently applied to U.S. Customs for approval to gauge imported petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable and animal oils under §151.13 of the Customs Regulations (19 CFR 151.13). Customs has determined that Accutest Services, Inc., meets all of the requirements for approval as a commercial gauger. Therefore, in accordance with §151.13(f) of the Customs Regulations, Accutest Services, Inc. is approved to gauge the products named above in all Customs districts. EFFECTIVE DATE: March, 22, 1994.
