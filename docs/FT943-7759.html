940820 FT 20 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Atherton fined for dissent England cricket captain Mike Atherton was fined Pounds 1,200 and severely reprimanded for showing dissent after being given out during the second day of the third Test against South Africa at the Oval. England ended the day on 281 for seven in reply to South Africa's 332. Sport, Weekend Xlll
