940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Directors' transactions The Capita Group is involved in various aspects of management consultancy. Its recent final results showed some improvement in pre-tax profit and chairman Rodney Aldridge made a broadly optimistic statement. He and chief executive Paul Pindar (who combines the roles of managing and finance director), plus Richard Benton and John Brameld all sold stock on Friday April 29 at 223p. Directors of Capita Group sell stock regularly at this time of year, but the steady rise in the share price has meant that they have raised more money this time than ever before. Secure Retirement is a newcomer to the market. The company develops sheltered housing - one of the sectors to gain more and more prominence in recent years in light of the increasing longevity of the UK population. Following an introduction to the market, all the directors have been dealing apace, dipping repeatedly into the market. While the overall value of the transactions is not enormous when divided by the number of those dealing, the enthusiasm is certainly great. Virtuality also is a recent arrival on the market.
