Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with the recent seizure of hostages, Russia's Foreign Ministry sent a note to Tehran, requesting it to ensure the safety of hostages, if the terrorists insist on flying to Iran. INTERFAX learned this from the director of the ministry's Press and Information Department, Grigoriy Karasin. According to him, every possible channel has been used for contacts with the Iranian authorities. "For example, the ministry's note will be forwarded not only to Iran's Foreign Ministry, but also to the Iranian ambassadors in Moscow and Baku," he said.
