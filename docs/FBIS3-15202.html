Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow March 3 TASS - The Azerbaijan Army units backed by armour and artillery launched an attack Thursday morning and broke through the Karabakh defence lines advancing towards the town of Fizuli. According to a source in the Karabakh administration, both sides suffer considerable losses while the Karabakh forces try to neutralize the enemy and push it back to the initial position. A Karabakh official has accused Azerbaijan of violating the Russia-brokered ceasefire from March 1.
