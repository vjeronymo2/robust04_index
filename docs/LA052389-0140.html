May 23, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

UCLA SIGNS CAPISTRANO VALLEY'S MULHAUPT

Laurinda Mulhaupt, a four-sport athlete at Capistrano Valley High School, has signed a national letter of intent to attend UCLA in the fall, her father, Rich, said Monday.

Mulhaupt, along with playing tennis and soccer, runs cross-county and track for Capistrano Valley.

She is a three-time South Coast League champion at 800 meters with a best time of 2 minutes 16.38 seconds. Mulhaupt will compete in cross-county and track at UCLA.
