Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Musa Badr Darwish, 15, was shot dead by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers in Dayr al-Balah today. Three locals were wounded in the Jabalyah refugee camp during the dispersal of a riot. An Israeli soldier was wounded in the head by a rock during the riots in downtown Hebron. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that the riots occurred during an interval in the curfew announced by the authorities to enable the inhabitants to stock up on food. Palestinian sources claim that 14 locals were wounded today in the city.
