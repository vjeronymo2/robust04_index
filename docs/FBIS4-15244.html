BFN [Edict No. 662 of the Russian Federation President "On the Procedure for the Entry Into Force of Federal Laws"; dated Moscow, 5 April 1994, and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] With a view to implementing the provisions of paragraph "e" of Article 84 of the Russian Federation Constitution, I decree: 1. Federal laws are subject to mandatory publication in the newly created information bulletin SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII [Collection of Russian Federation Legislation]. Federal laws are also published in ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and are passed on for entry into the data base of official legal documents maintained by the "Sistema" scientific and technical center for legal information. 2. Publication of federal laws in SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII and in ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA constitutes official publication. The texts of official laws distributed in machine-readable form by the "Sistema" scientific and technical center for legal information are also official. Federal laws can also be published in other printed publications and promulgated via television and radio. 3. Federal laws enter into force throughout the territory of the Russian Federation 10 days after the day of their official publication. The date of entry into force of federal laws indicated in this paragraph does not apply in instances when a federal law stipulates another date for its entry into force. 4. Federal laws adopted on matters concerning the ratification and denunciation of international treaties are also published in the BYULLETEN MEZHDUNARODNYKH DOGOVOROV [Bulletin of International Treaties]. 5. SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII is published weekly and consists of three sections: Federal laws are published in Section One, Russian Federation presidential edicts and directives in Section Two, and Russian Federation Government decrees and directives in Section Three. Sections Two and three are subdivided into two parts: Part One contains statutory documents, Part Two contains documents of a nonstatutory nature. 6. Documents published in SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII are placed in entries with corresponding sequential numbers. Appendixes to documents are placed in the same entries as the documents themselves. 7. Further to the Russian Federation President's Directive No. 428-rp of 8 August 1992, the duty of publishing and distributing SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII is placed on the Juridical Literature publishing house under the Russian President's Administration and -- in machine-readable form -- the "Sistema" scientific and technical center for legal information. 8. Further to the Russian Federation President's Edict No. 363 of 4 April 1992 "On Amendments to the Statute on the State-Legal Administration of the Russian Federation President," the State-Legal Administration of the Russian Federation President is to be tasked with monitoring the correctness and timeliness of the publication of federal laws. 9. The Russian Federation President's Administration is to: found the information bulletin SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII instead of the information bulletin SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII [Collection of Russian Federation Presidential and Governmental Acts]; approve the editorial council of the newly founded bulletin; form an editorial staff for the said information bulletin within the structure of the Juridical Literature publishing house, making provision for a corresponding increase in the publishing house's regulation personnel strength; resolve questions linked with the size of the print run and mandatory distribution of the information bulletin. 10. The Russian Federation President's Administration is to ensure the priority dispatch in full of federal laws and Russian presidential edicts and directives to the editorial office of the information bulletin SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII and the "Sistema" scientific and technical center for legal information; the Russian Federation Government is to do likewise for Russian Federation Government decrees and directives. 11. The Russian Federation Government is to resolve within one month the question of printing facilities for the information bulletin SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, the allocation of the necessary quantity of paper, and also the financing of work to publish and distribute this bulletin in accordance with Paragraph 7 of this Edict. 12. The Russian Federation Ministry of Communications, jointly with the editorial staff of the information bulletin SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, is to determine the conditions for the distribution of this publication taking account of the rights of 1994 subscribers to SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII. The Federal Government Communications and Information Agency under the Russian Federation president is to determine within one month, by agreement with the State-Legal Administration of the Russian Federation President, the procedure and conditions for the distribution of SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII in machine-readable form via the system of federal government communications and information agencies. 13. The Juridical Literature publishing house under the Russian Federation President's Administration is to ensure the publication through 1 May 1994 of Russian Federation presidential and governmental acts adopted through 30 April 1993 in the SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII and thereupon cease its publication. 14. This Edict enters into forces from the moment it is published.
