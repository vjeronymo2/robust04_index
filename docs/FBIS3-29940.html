Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's famous work "Abuses of Socialism Are Intolerable" was published in booklet by the Cerberus publishing house of Hungary and the Datina publishing house of Romania on the occasion of his birthday. The Kultura publishing house of Yugoslavia published in booklet his work "The Workers' Party of Korea Organizes and Guides All Victories of Our People." In the preface of the booklet, the publishing house says this work, on the basis of a scientific analysis of the lawful requirement of the present time and the cause of socialism, expounds questions of principle in the building of the revolutionary party of the working class, including defence of the revolutionary character of the party and the strengthening of the unity and cohesion of the party and the relations between the party and the masses. A seminar on his work "Our Socialism Centred on the Masses Shall Not Perish" was held at the Nicaraguan committee for the study of the chuche idea and a seminar on his works "On the Fundamentals of Revolutionary Party Building" and "Abuses of Socialism Are Intolerable" at the Peruvian Group for the Study of the Literary and Art Idea of Comrade Kim Chong-il.
