February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Igor Zhelezovsky of the Soviet Union won the men's 1,000- and 500-meter races in the World Sprint Speed Skating Championships at Heerenveen, the Netherlands.

Dan Jansen finished third in the men's 500, and fellow American Eric Flaim was third in the 1,000.

Bonnie Blair of the United States won the women's 500 meters and was third in the 1,000.
