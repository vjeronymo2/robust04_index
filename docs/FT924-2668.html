921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / People: Finance moves Sreten Bakovljev has stepped down from the CITY MERCHANTS HIGH YIELD TRUST in order to concentrate on his other commitments within INVESCO MIM Management. ***** Denis Long has been appointed general manager of Ak International Bank; he moves from Credit Lyonnais. ***** Kenneth Quinn has been appointed finance director of WISE SPEKE (HOLDINGS); he moved from Exco International. ***** Richard Rhodes, formerly head of fixed interest division at Warburg Securities, has been appointed to the board of BRITANNIA BUILDING SOCIETY. ***** Tony Richards, formerly head of gross funds division at Quilter Goodison, has been appointed a director of HENDERSON CROSTHWAITE. ***** Sandy Geddes, formerly general manager of Philadelphia National Bank's London branch, has been appointed director, banking of REA BROTHERS. ***** Mark Hedderwick, md of Adam & Company Investment Management, has been appointed a director of ADAM & COMPANY GROUP. ***** Martin Rose, a former senior legal adviser to The Securities Association and latterly with the corporate department of Linklaters and Paines, has been appointed director in charge of compliance at SMITH & WILLIAMSON.
