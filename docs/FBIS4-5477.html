BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 10 -- Nearly 8,000 projects throughout the country have got loans of about VND [Vietnamese dong] 420 billion from the national fund since the launching of a credit programme two years ago, it was reported by the Central State Treasury. As a result, the programme has helped create 335,000 jobs nationwide. Leading the programme was Ho Chi Minh City which gave loans of VND 36.13 billion to 317 projects, creating 16,500 jobs. Next was Hanoi which lent VND 24.29 billion to 229 projects attracting 19,622 workers. These projects are usually small and operate in the fields of aquaculture, sea fishing, small industries and handicrafts, building materials production and animal breeding. The state treasury and the concerned branches and services are seeking measures to streamline lending procedures to provide credits in time and help to use them effectively.
