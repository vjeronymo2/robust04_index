930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Dinkie Heel Dinkie Heel, which makes components for the shoe trade, suffered a fall in 1992 pre-tax from Pounds 259,000 to Pounds 201,000. Actual turnover dropped from Pounds 7.29m to Pounds 6.67m. However, adjusting for the disposal of the Enterprise Group, there was a rise of 7 per cent including a 23 per cent improvement in exports. Interest paid was cut to Pounds 86,000 (Pounds 107,000). Earnings worked through at 1.31p (1.82p) and the final dividend is 0.6p for an unchanged total of 0.95p.
