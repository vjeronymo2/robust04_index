November 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

SAUDIS SECRET STAKE IN MARINA

Saudi Arabia's embassy in the United States has great respect for the accuracy and responsibility of The Times but would like to clarify a reference in the article of Nov. 12, regarding a report that two Saudi businessmen had made an investment in Southern California for a younger son of the king. The young man is 16 years old and still in school. Neither he nor anyone else has authorized any Saudi, American nor other business person to handle any financial matter on the young man's behalf in this situation nor any other one.

With regard to another part of the article, the embassy also wants to reiterate its government's firm position that no agent, whether Saudi or foreign, is allowed in connection with any Saudi defense purchase.

As to the rest of the article, the embassy does not comment on the business activities of Saudi firms or individuals or of Americans doing business in Saudi Arabia so long as those activities do not violate our laws or those of the host country.

HABIB A . SHAHEEN

Director, Office of Information Embassy of Saudi Arabia

Washington, D.C.
