940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / International Capital Markets: Bank scraps Dutch unit CS First Boston, the merchant banking arm of CS Holdings, plans to scrap its Dutch equity operations in Amsterdam and move them to London within the next few months, writes Conner Middelmann. Mr Xavier Rolet, managing director of European equities at CS First Boston in London, said that by concentrating equity operations in London, 'we want to regroup our strength, develop our pan-European business and increase our placing power among institutional and corporate clients.' The cost of running the operation was a factor, although it was not decisive, he said. CS First Boston is holding talks with the Amsterdam stock exchange 'to see under what conditions we can continue as foreign-based members or in some form of association,' Mr Rolet said. 'They have been fairly receptive.'
