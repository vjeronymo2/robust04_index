Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Bosnia: Downed Aircraft -- No NATO Meeting Scheduled for Now"] [Text] Brussels, 28 Feb (ANSA) -- NATO sources have stated that no emergency meeting of the Atlantic Council has been scheduled for now to assess the situation in the wake of the downing of four Serbian aircraft this morning. The sources explained that, on the basis of UN Security Council Resolution 816 of 1993, no special decisions whatsover have to be made prior to intercepting and/or downing aircraft.
