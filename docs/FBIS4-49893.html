BFN [Text] Beijing, May 6 (XINHUA) -- The China National Technical Import and Export Corporation (CNTIC) signed a contract here today with the U.S. Foster wheeler energy company to import a boiler island worth 190 million U.S. dollars. The island is being imported for the Ezhou Power Station, 30 km east of Wuhan, capital of Hubei Province. The power station, composed of two sets of turbogenerators with a capacity of 300,000 kilowatts each, is the first thermal power plant built with part of the third batch of loans from the Japanese Government. The station is made up of a boiler island, turbine island and a electrical equipment island. Last March, CNTIC signed contracts with Japan's Itochu Corporation and ABB Ltd. to import a turbine island and an electrical equipment island worth 130 million U.S. dollars. Last year, the Foster Wheeler Energy Company exported a boiler island to Zouxian Power Station in east China's Shandong Province. The construction of the Ezhou Power Station will help ease electricity shortages in Hubei Province, CNTIC sources said.
