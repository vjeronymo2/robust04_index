940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / The Westminister Report: Councillors and officers named The eight people named in the auditor's statement, apart from Dame Shirley Porter and Mr Barry Legg, are: Mr David Weeks: former leader of Westminster City Council who took over after Dame Shirley resigned in 1991 and still a councillor. Works as a marketing executive at the London office of construction company Lovell. He was said yesterday to be on holiday. Mr Peter Hartley: council member for 10 years until he resigned in 1988. He is now consultant at a video shop in Praed Street, Paddington. Mr Michael Dutt: consultant geriatrician at St Albans City Hospital. He was a Westminster councillor for four years until 1990. He unsuccessfully contested Leicester South for the Conservatives at the April 1992 general election. Ms Judith Warner: a Westminster councillor for 10 years, she is now chairman of council's education committee and teaches at a college in Surrey. Mr Bill Phillips: director of NB Selection, a London recruitment consultancy, who was managing director at Westminster council from 1989 to 1991. Mr Robert Lewis: joined the council in 1986 as deputy city solicitor and left in April 1988 to join Freshfields, the City legal firm where he is now a partner. He 'strongly rejects preliminary findings of the report and will be challenging them vigorously'. Mr Graham England: housing director at the council since 1985. Mr Paul Hayler: divisional director in housing department at Westminster since May 1986.
