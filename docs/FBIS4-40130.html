BFN [Text] Elections to the Georgian parliament were held yesterday in the western Georgian rayons of Zugdidi, Tsalenjikha, and Chkhorotsku. Our correspondent reports that preliminary information suggests that in all three rayons the turnout was above 60 percent. Preliminary information also suggests that Zugdidi candidate [and Georgia's Prime Minister] Otar Patsatsia received 70 percent of the votes, Chkhorotsku candidate Avtandil Tkebuchava received about 50 percent, and Tsalenjikha candidate Valeri Chanturia received more than 51 percent. The final results will be known later.
