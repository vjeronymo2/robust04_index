BFN [Text] Visiting Japanese economic mission led by Mr. (Kazuo Haruna), vice chairman of the Keidanren entrepreneurs organization and chairman of the Marubeni Corporation, held talks with responsible departmental officials at the Hall of the International Business Center at 0800 this morning. Also present on the occasion were Lieutenant General Chit Swe, minister of forestry affairs; Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; Brig. Gen. Maung Maung, minister of livestock breeding and fisheries; deputy ministers from industry-1, forests, agriculture, mines, livestock breeding and fisheries, energy, and construction ministries; and responsible personnel from the Yangon [Rangoon] City Development Committee. Responsible personnel from the various ministries and departments explained extensively current departmental activities, foreign investment opportunities, and work schedules with the aid of brochures, pamphlets, and slides. Personnel concerned gave detailed explanations to queries raised by the visiting Japanese mission. The ceremony came to a close at 1700 in the evening.
