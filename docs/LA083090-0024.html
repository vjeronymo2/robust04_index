August 30, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: FESTIVAL NEWS

'Footsteps' Walks: "Pisadas en la Obscuridad (Footsteps in the Dark)," director Reza Abdoh's late-night festival entry, has at last found a home after losing its original downtown venue, Jack's Placita, last week. The show, which mixes elements of mystery-revenge and popular Mexican telenovelas, will now appear at the Park Plaza Hotel. Performance times (Sept. 6-9 at 10:30 p.m.) have not changed. BETH KLEID
