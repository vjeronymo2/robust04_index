BFN [Text] Early this morning the leader of the gang that was transporting 7.5 metric tons [as heard] of cocaine from Tocantins State to Santos, Sao Paulo State, was arrested. Colombian Vicentico Junior [Vicente Wilson Rivera-Ramos] had spent two days hiding in the forest along the Tocantins River's banks. Federal Police now are investigating the gang's links with international drug trafficking. The group is believed to be a Brazilian branch of the Cali cartel. The eight Brazilians and three Colombians arrested during the operation will be transferred to Brasilia.
