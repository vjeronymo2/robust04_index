May 28, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COUNTYWIDE;

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Today is Memorial Day, a day set aside to remember those who have died in wars. The following will be closed in observance of the holiday:

All county, state and federal offices.

All schools, including colleges and universities.

County, state and federal courthouses.

City and county libraries.

Banks and savings and loans.

All city offices.

Mail: All post offices will be closed today, and mail deposited in mailboxes today will be picked up according to the posted holiday schedule. There will be no mail delivery today.

Trash: There will be no trash pickup by city or county refuse workers today. Trash pickups will be a day late in all areas, with the exception of Fullerton and Orange. Residents in those cities are asked to put out their trash on their regularly scheduled day, except those with regular Monday pickups, who should put out their trash on Tuesday.

Buses: Orange County Transit District buses will run on the holiday/Sunday schedule today. There will be no dial-a-ride or car-pool services available.
