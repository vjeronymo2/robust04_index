March 21, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

GIVEN A CHOICE, THEY HAVE TO GO FOR THE TAR HEELS

When it comes to basketball, is North Carolina really the Tar Heel state?

The answer, overwhelmingly, is yes. In this state, they do polls on subjects like that.

Recently, a Chapel Hill marketing firm did a random telephone survey of 520 adults, asking the following question: "If all of the following teams were playing on television at the same time, but not playing each other, which would you watch: Duke Blue Devils, N.C. State Wolfpack, Charlotte Hornets, Carolina Tar Heels or Wake Forest Demon Deacons?"

The results: North Carolina, 34%; N.C. State, 19%; Charlotte and Duke, 12%, and Wake Forest, 3%. A total of 20% said they "don't know."

The Tar Heels carried every region of the state except Charlotte, where the Hornets had a 32%-26% edge. JEFF FELLENZER
