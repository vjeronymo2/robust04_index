941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Hijacker frees two crew members Two crew members were freed from a hijacked plane in southern Russia, leaving only the captain and the hijacker on board, according to the commercial radio station Ekho Moskvy. The hijacker, believed to be acting alone, has already received Dollars 2.3m ransom in exchange for releasing 23 hostages and is believed to want another Dollars 2m.
