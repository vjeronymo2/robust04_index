940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / International Company News: Norske Skog returns to black Norske Skog, one of Scandinavia's biggest pulp and paper producers, announced a return to profit in 1993, helped by a successful cost-cutting programme and despite lower prices for the Norwegian group's main products, writes Karen Fossli in Osli. The group achieved a pre-tax profit of NKr4m (Dollars 536,800) in 1993 against a pre-tax loss of NKr662m the previous year, due to increased production and lower finance costs.
