Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 17 Jan (NA) -- President Carlos Menem cut his visit to Uruguay short and this afternoon he returned to Buenos Aires from the city of Colonia de Sacramento, where he attended the fifth Mercosur [Common Market of the South] presidential summit. Menem arrived at the military area of "Jorge Newbery" Metropolitan Airport at 1545 local time [1845 GMT] and after staying a few minutes in the VIP lounge, he left for the Olivos presidential resident aboard a helicopter. The president made no statement to reporters at the airport, where some minutes earlier Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo also had arrived from Colonia.
