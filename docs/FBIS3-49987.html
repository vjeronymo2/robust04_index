Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Statement by the Information Center of the Travnik War Presidency] [Excerpt] Although we have been sending appeals from Travnik and we cannot say that they have resulted in any real help to our city, the fact that humanitarian situation is getting worse by the day obliges us to continue appealing to humanitarian organizations and the UNHCR, as well as to our country's Presidency and government. There are 27,000 refugees here whom Travnik has been taking care of for a long time and we do not know how we are still managing to help them survive, but the fact that the local population of Travnik is also hungry and that they need humanitarian aid just as the refugees do should [word indistinct] all those people who should and must help us. The local population are getting food mainly in soup kitchens where food reserves are only sufficient for a few days. [passage omitted]
