February 1, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Flu has hit the Los Angeles Philharmonic. Orchestra Manager Nancybell Coe reports that 10 of the orchestra's 75 instrumentalists scheduled to play a Beethoven/Schubert program last week were out with the flu bug. The sick group included Principal Concertmaster Sidney Weiss. "Because the program did not require a full orchestra, and because only one player was taking the week off anyway, we had to hire only two substitutes," says Coe. What will happen when the Philharmonic meets today for its first rehearsal of this week, no one is predicting. . . . ALEENE MacMINN
