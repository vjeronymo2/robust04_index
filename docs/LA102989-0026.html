October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE GODFATHER'S SON

The article glorified the mob, Mafia, organized crime and all they represent by idolizing the lawyer son of an infamous crime figure. Many of the types of organized crime and its roots are in the past, though certainly operant today. It made me wonder if in the not so distant future this same Sunday magazine will be featuring an article on the offspring of our now infamous gangs.

ZELLE HAMMOND

Harbor City
