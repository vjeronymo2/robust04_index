BFN [TA SR report: "A Four-Leaf Clover Is the Symbol of the Left Choice"] [Text] Zvolen--The Party of the Democratic Left [SDL] has received indications that the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] is preparing a referendum through which it would like to shorten the current president's term of office. Such an attempt could lead to doubts being cast on Slovakia in the world and to new tension in Slovak society, said P. Weiss, deputy chairman of the National Council of the Slovak Republic [NR SR] and chairman of the SDL, at a meeting held in Zvolen yesterday with almost 120 town and city mayors from Central Slovakia who were elected on behalf of left-wing oriented parties. Representatives of the election coalition--Deputy Chairman S. Krcho of the Farmers Movement; S. Holes, a member of the Social Democratic Party of Slovakia Presidium; NR SR Deputy A. Chlapikova; and others--were also present. SDL Deputy Chairman D. Dorotin said that the left-wing partners agreed at the Coalition Council on Wednesday, 8 June, to go into the elections under the collective name of "Left Choice" [Lavicova volba], with a program called "Social Democratic and Green Choice for Slovakia," and with a green four-leaf clover as its symbol. The political bodies of these entities have still to approve the proposal.
