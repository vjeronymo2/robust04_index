BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 122/94] [Text] The governing body of parliament registered a new party "Liberals" during it's yesterday's meeting. The members of the new party are Paul-Eerik Rummo (chairman), Tiit Kubin (vice-chairman), Lauri Einer, Heiki Kranich, Aap Neljas and Juri Peld, announced the press.
