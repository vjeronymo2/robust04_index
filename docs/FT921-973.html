920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / Amsterdam votes to curb cars THE people of Amsterdam voted yesterday to halt inroads by the car on their canal-city, AP reports. A proposal to clamp 'drastic' restrictions on motor vehicles in the historic centre got 52.9 per cent of the vote, according to partial results late last night, with 75 per cent of ballots counted. Voter turnout was lower than expected at only 26.6 per cent.
