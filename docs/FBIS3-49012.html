Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Border Guards have reinforced the protection of the Hungarian-Serbian border. They fear that due to the introduction of a travel tax in rump Yugoslavia that is to come into force soon, many people will try to come across to Hungary illegally. Border Guard Spikesman Attila Krisan said that as a result of the new decree, which is valid as of midnight, the number of Yugoslavs entering was down to a minimum at the Tompa, Roeszke, and Hercegszanto border crossing points.
