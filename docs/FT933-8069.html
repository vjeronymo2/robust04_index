930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / Pounds 6,000 damages for terror arrests TWO PEOPLE arrested under the Prevention of Terrorism Act have each been paid Pounds 6,000 damages in addition to legal costs, and received a 'full apology' from the police. Armed officers arrested Ms Sarah Cohen and Mr Sean Hampson during a night raid on their home in March, two months after the Harrods bombing in Knightsbridge.
