Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- An art exhibition opened here Wednesday to mark the 30th anniversary of the publication of the rural theses. On display are more than 70 art works of various forms including Korean paintings "The Great Leader Comrade Kim Il-song Conversing With Peasants To Open a New History of Agricultural Cooperativization," "The Dear Leader Comrade Kim Chong-il Transplanting Rice With Artistes" and "The Dear Leader Comrade Kim Chong-il Walking on a Farm Field in a Bountiful Year" representing the revolutionary activities of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The works show the proud realities of the Korean countryside that has been turned into a modern socialist countryside good to live in under the wise guidance of President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, the vigorous drive of agricultural working people who are carrying through the agriculture-first policy of the party and the working people actively helping the socialist rural communities. An opening address was made by Chang Chol, vice-premier of the Administration Council and minister of culture and art.
