930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Kiwifruit at 15-20p each (15-20p) is a good fruit buy along with whitefleshed grapefruit at 20-25p each (22-28p) reports the FFVIB. Apple varieties remain plentiful with English Cox priced at 35-45p a lb (25-35p) and English Bramley cooking apples at 28-35p a lb (28-35p). Brussel sprouts at 25-30p a lb (30-40p), carrots at 18-20p a lb (18-20p) and broccoli at 50-60p per 8oz prepack (50-60p) are all good vegetable buys. Large heads of celery at 55-65p a head (55-65p), tomatoes at 60-85p a lb (60-70p) and round lettuce at 35-40p a head (35-40p) are star salad buys.
