920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper market rather inactive as both overseas suppliers and industrial buyers have preferred to wait and see, reports Man-producten. Muntok white pepper is available at Dollars 1,350 a tonne spot Europe and at Dollars 1,275 cif for shipment from origin. News that a protocol was signed between the CIS and India to supply 9,000 to 10,000 tonnes of black pepper made the Indian market firmer (Dollars 1,250 cif for MG-1). Main suppliers to the market are Sarawak and Thailand with Dollars 1,100 a tonne cif. The asta grades are offered at about Dollars 1,200 a tonne cif.
