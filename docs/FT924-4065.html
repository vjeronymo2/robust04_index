921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / Kohl welcomes economic liberalisation in China Chancellor Helmut Kohl of Germany said yesterday he welcomed economic liberalisation in China but hoped it would be matched by political reform, Reuter reports from Bonn. Speaking at a reception for the Bonn diplomatic corps, he said Germany wanted to expand its relations with China. 'I welcome the recent confirmation of the Chinese leadership's policy of further liberalising the economy and opening the country to the outside world,' Mr Kohl said. 'We would like this road to lead to internal political reform as well.'
