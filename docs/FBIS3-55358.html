Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Estonia"] [Text] Andres Tarand, Estonian environment minister, has said that the removal of nuclear fuel from the training nuclear reactor in Paldiski is scheduled to start in March-April of this year and will take around four months. The Russian side has set the deadline for completing the removal of radioactive materials for 30 June.
