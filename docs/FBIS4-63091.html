BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("LRAGIR", "AZG" dailies), 05/19/94 -- The Azeri troops continue shelling the Armenian defense positions in Mountainous Karabakh. The enemy shelled the Armenian posts in the Mardakertskiy and Agdamskiy-Martuninskiy directions during the night and in the morning of 18 May, using artillery and tanks. One Karabakh soldier was reportedly injured. Military operations continued later during the day. Two Karabakh fighters were wounded at the village of Gulistan in the Shaumyaninskiy district as a result of Azeri shellings during the night of 17 May. The RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] defense forces refrained from returning the fire. The enemy shelled the Karabakh defense positions at the villages of Seisulan and Karmiravan in the Mardakertskiy district, as well as the village of Yusifdzhanli in the Agdamskiy district, using large caliber machine guns and hand held weaponry. One soldier was wounded. The Karabakh positions at the village of Gulistan were shelled again later in the afternoon. No casualties are reported. At the same time, the Karabakh positions at the villages of Ashagi Abdurakmanli and Kurgan in the Fizulinskiy district have been shelled. The Karabakh side refrained from returning the fire in all mentioned cases.
