Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 459] [Text] Vilnius, March 17, ELTA -- The Lithuanian-Latvian border was restored at the meeting of the delegations of the two states on Thursday [17 March]. Representatives of the land exploitation service from Pasvalys, Rokiskis and Daugpilis reached the agreement on the sectors of the common border and approved the map of redelimitation with the restored border between Lithuania and Latvia.
