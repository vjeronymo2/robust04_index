921102 FT 02 NOV 92 / Turk army 'takes rebel Kurd base' TURKISH troops captured a main Kurdish rebel base at the weekend, killing at least 1,000 guerrillas of the Kurdish Workers' party (PKK), state television reported, writes John Murray Brown in Ankara. It quoted military officials in the area as saying the attack was on the PKK base at Haftanin, 10km inside Iraq, from where rebels have raided Turkish border posts.
