May 4, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Shannon to Dance in Moscow: Michael Shannon, the 20-year-old Angeleno who was accepted in the fall as a soloist with the Bolshoi Ballet, will dance Albrecht in the company's production of "Giselle" on June 1 in Bolshoi Theatre. His Moscow debut in a leading role comes after a dozen tryout performances in tour sites such as Tblisi and Tashkent, and perhaps will determine whether the company will bring him -- among the youngest dancers to be given a leading role -- to Shrine Auditorium in August. BETH KLEID
