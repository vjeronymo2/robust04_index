BFN [Text] Moscow, 17 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Government Chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin today assembled a large conference of the heads of the machinery of government and its structural subdivisions on the results of his working meeting with the president of the Russian Federation. This was announced by the government's press service. The conference was attended by deputy chairmen of the government and the heads of a number of ministries in the economic field. Serious criticisms were made of the staff's style and methods of work, the level of control over the implementation of the decisions accepted, and the time taken for documents submitted to the staff to be considered. Viktor Chernomyrdin told the heads of the apparat's structural subdivisions to adopt exhaustive measures to raise the level of executive discipline and gave a tough indication of the heads' personal responsibility for the conduct of reform and the implementation of concrete measures to stabilize the finances and the economy, the government press service announcement says.
