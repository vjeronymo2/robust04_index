920402 FT 02 APR 92 / International Company News: SNI cuts plant production SIEMENS Nixdorf Informationssysteme (SNI), the loss-making German computer company owned by Siemens, will put 1,600 people on short-time working at its Paderborn plants in northern Germany from this month, writes Andrew Fisher in Frankfurt. It yesterday blamed the move on low capacity utilisation at the plant, where Nixdorf was based before it was acquired by Siemens.
