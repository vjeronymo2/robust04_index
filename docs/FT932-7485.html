930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Big turnout in fairest Cambodian election for decades CAMBODIANS turned out in force yesterday to vote in the country's freest and fairest election since independence from France 40 years ago. Prince Norodom Ranariddh, leader of the main opposition party, is pictured surrounded by bodyguards and journalists as he addresses supporters after casting his vote in the capital, Phnom Penh. The voting continues until Friday. Report, Page 16; Test for Untac chief, Page 4
