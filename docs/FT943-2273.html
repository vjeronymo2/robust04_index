940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / International Company News: NTT to trade in New York Shares of Nippon Telegraph and Telephone will be listed on the New York Stock Exchange on September 29, NTT said in a statement, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The shares will be traded as American depositary receipts (ADRs) with NTT issuing 200 ADRs for one share. NTT also said it planned to list its shares on the London Stock Exchange in October. NTT announced back in July that it intended to list the shares on NYSE.
