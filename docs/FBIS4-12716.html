BFN [Text] The National Liberation Front [FLN] party is one which believes in dialogue and national reconciliation. In response to an invitation from Mr. Youcef el-Khatib, adviser at the presidency, to attend a meeting on the appointment of provisional council members on 20 April, the FLN said that it would not attend this meeting because it was not concerned with the council's formation. The FLN did not attend the national consensus conference and therefore was under no obligation to implement the conference's resolutions.
