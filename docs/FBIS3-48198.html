Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Concern over the possible production and use of nuclear weapons by South Korea and the division of the peninsula were the main topics of conversation between Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana and members of the South Korean Government as they met today, the second day of his visit to this Asian country. Javier Solana, who met the South Korean deputy prime minister and the minister of defense, said that the risk of nuclear proliferation is something that has to be avoided by us all. [Correspondent] Solana also spoke to the defense minister about the sale of 12 cargo planes built by the Compania de Construcciones Aeronauticas Espanolas, CASA, planes that are now being delivered. He also discussed the possibility of another order. Now that his visit to South Korea is over, the minister will continue his Far Eastern tour in Malaysia and Thailand. The tour started last Friday in Japan.
