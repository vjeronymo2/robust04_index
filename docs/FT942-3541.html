940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Trinity contract Mr Geoff Hollyhead, chairman of Trinity Holdings, told the annual meeting that the vehicle manufacturer had been awarded a contract to supply Badgerline with 576 bus chassis. Together with further orders, including 160 chassis for Stagecoach, the contract constituted a record monthly intake for the Dennis Specialist Vehicles subsidiary, totalling some Pounds 33m. This latest contract pushed Trinity's order book for the current year to some Pounds 100m.
