BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The indictment against Samir Ja'ja' and others contains the results of the interrogations of the detainees and the information available to the competent security and judicial authorities. It reveals that many of the accused have received training in Israel and that they had links with Israeli intelligence. The interrogations also showed that some of them are accomplices in other crimes, such as the assassinations of Dani Sham'un and Dr. Ilyas al-Zayik and the attempted murder of ministers Elie Hubayqah and Sulayman Franjiyah. The indictment points out that the accused possessed forged passports and travelled between Cyprus, Juniyah [port in Lebanon], and Israel. It also stresses that the accused received huge amounts of money for implementing their tasks at the direct orders of Ja'ja'.
