941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares generally made modest gains in low volumes, although activity picked up towards the close helped by strength on Wall Street. The overall index added 17 to 5,752, industrials 14 to 6,602 but gold slipped 5 to 2,292. De Beers rose 75 cents to R100.50 and Anglos R2 to R238.
