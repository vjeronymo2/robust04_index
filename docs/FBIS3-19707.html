Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Paris, March 21 (AFP) -- The French Economy Ministry on Monday [21 March] said France was on course to experience a gradual acceleration of growth, whose effects would be felt by the second half of 1994 and above all in 1995. According to estimates which Economy Minister Edmond Alphandery will present Tuesday, France's gross domestic product will rise by 1.4 percent in 1994 on 1993 and by 2.7 percent in 1995.
