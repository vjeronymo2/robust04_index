Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The press service of the Abkhazian mission in Moscow has denied Georgian sources' reports about fighting in Abkhazia's Kodor Ravine. With reference to the Abkhazian Defense Ministry, an Abkhazian spokesman told INTERFAX that fighting continued from 24 March to 26 March, during which Abkhazians occupied the village of Lata, the Georgian stronghold in this region. He said, however, that individual clashes were not ruled out. He rejected Georgian sources' assertions that 60 Abkhazian soldiers were killed on 29 March. Two Abkhazians have been killed since fighting began, he said.
