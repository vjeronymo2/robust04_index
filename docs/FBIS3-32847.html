Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Guatemala City, 14 Feb (NOTIMEX) -- The National Police have disclosed at least three explosive devices were deactivated today by members of the Guatemalan Armed Forces in the northwestern Department (province) of Quiche. Three bombs were placed along public light posts in the Chichicastenango Municipality, 200 km northeast of Guatemala City, and deactivated by Army specialists. The police report adds Army troops are searching the area, attempting to locate the terrorists. According to police, a knapsack with explosive devices and documents linking guerrillas to the attempts were found in the area where the explosives were discovered. The deactivation took place after three electricity high tension wire pylons were sabotaged less than 48 hours in the same area of Quiche. Nine electricity high tension pylons have been destroyed with dynamite charges in the western Department of Quetzaltenango, Solola, and Quiche. The Government Ministry and Army are trying to locate those responsible for these incidents.
