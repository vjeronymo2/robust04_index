August 3, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: HORIBA INSTRUMENTS

Horiba Instruments Inc., a manufacturer of automotive and industrial emissions analysis equipment, purchased this 40,000-square-foot building from Parker Hannifin in August, 1989. After a $1-million refurbishment, the building serves as U.S. corporate headquarters for Horiba, which is a subsidiary of Horiba Ltd. in Kyoto, Japan. Half of the U.S. subsidiary's 150 employees are based in Irvine.
