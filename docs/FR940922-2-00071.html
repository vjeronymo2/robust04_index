DATE: Comments should be received on or before December 21, 1994. [CA&hyph;940&hyph;4210&hyph;06; CACA 7584, CACA 7839, CACA 8048, CAS 3721, CAS 047313, and CAS 076612] Proposed Continuation of Withdrawals; California AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management, Interior. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The United States Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, has proposed to continue withdrawals on 642.95 acres for 20 years, 30 acres for 25 years, and 40 acres for 30 years within the Toiyabe National Forest. The segregative effect on these withdrawals remains unchanged. DATE: Comments should be received on or before December 21, 1994.
