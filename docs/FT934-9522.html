931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Rentokil in Pounds 5m expansion Rentokil, the environmental and property services company, has expanded in both Spain and the US via acquisitions totalling Pounds 5m. In Spain, it has acquired Corporation Internacional Ratin together with Compania Internacional de Desinsectacion y Antiplagas, two related pest control companies based in Barcelona. The cash con-sideration totalled Pta740m (Pounds 3.7m). The US acquisition was of Exotic Plant Rentals of Elgin, Illinois. The price paid was Dollars 1.7m in cash.
