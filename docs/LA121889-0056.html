December 18, 1989, Monday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: PACOIMA MAN DIES IN CRASH WITH PARKED BUS

A Pacoima man died early Sunday after driving his speeding car into the back of a parked school bus, police said.

The man, Angel Saucedo Perez, 22, was pronounced dead by paramedics who were called to the 12:30 a.m. accident on Osborne Street near Glenoaks Boulevard, said Los Angeles Police Officer Richard Breidenthal.

Perez's car hit a 42-passenger school bus and pushed the vehicle 100 feet, into the side of a commercial building, Breidenthal said.
