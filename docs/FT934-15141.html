931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: City of Oxford net asset value improves The City of Oxford Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 38.5p per share as at September 30. The figure compared with values of 17.6p at the same stage of 1992 and 32.7p at the trust's March year-end. Net revenue for the six months to end-September amounted to Pounds 730,219, down from Pounds 781,145, for earnings of 2.43p (2.6p) per share. A second interim dividend of 1.2p brings the total to date to 2.4p.
