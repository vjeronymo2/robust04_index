BFN [From the "Up-to-Date" newscast] [Text] President-elect Dr. Armando Calderon Sol has pledged to fully investigate all complaints of corruption against any of his cabinet ministers. During an interview for the "Up-to-Date" newscast, the ARENA [Nationalist Republican Alliance] leader expressed his resolve to place in court custody all individuals found guilty of corruption. Dr. Calderon Sol has only one goal regarding this issue: to combat institutionalized corruption regardless of its cost to the government. According to the president-elect, he cannot betray the people who supported him during the past elections. Therefore, he says, he will get to the bottom of all complaints of corruption. [Begin Calderon Sol recording] If there are even rumors against any official, we will conduct a full investigation and place that person in court custody for investigation. [end recording] Corruption, says the future president, will be fought at all levels, but especially in those areas involving goods, services, and public bids. Calderon Sol also intends to put an end to government official's involvement in organized crime. [Begin Calderon Sol recording] If any administration officials are thus involved, regardless of who they may be, we will apply the law and break this circle of organized crime. [end recording] Calderon Sol, who will take office in June, warned that during his term he will manage public funds carefully and strengthen the development of the National Civilian Police, which emerged from the peace agreements.
