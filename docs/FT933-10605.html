930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Inoco Inoco, the property development company, swung back in the black with pre-tax profits of Pounds 834,000 for the six months to June 30, against a loss of Pounds 446,000. The turnround was partly due to lower interest charges of Pounds 2.3m, down from Pounds 3.7m. Earnings per share were 0.4p (losses of 0.22p).
