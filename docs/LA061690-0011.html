June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SUPREME COURT STRIKES DOWN LAW AGAINST FLAG BURNING

Well, I expect the reaction to Supreme Court decision will produce some useful changes. The idea that flag burning is protected by the Bill of Rights will almost certainly result in an amendment that will make this ancient document more appropriate to the makeup and needs of the modern American society.

The Constitution was, after all, written by intelligent adults.

JACK R. JENNINGS

Redondo Beach
