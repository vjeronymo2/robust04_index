941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / Conference will bolster Torys, says Hanley Tory party chairman Jeremy Hanley predicted yesterday that the conference in Bournemouth this week would demonstrate the Conservatives' credentials as 'the party of government'. He said: 'Our aim is to produce a permanently stronger United Kingdom and tax cuts will follow.' His comments came as prime minister John Major put defence of the constitution and preservation of Britain's independence in Europe at the centre of his strategy to counter the threat from the Labour party.
