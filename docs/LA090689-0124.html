September 6, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

MASTER CHORALE TO SING WITH BENNETT, JONES

A summer pops concert with Tony Bennett and a Christmas concert with Shirley Jones will round out the Master Chorale of Orange County's four-concert 1989-90 season.

Jones will sing with the Chorale on Dec. 3 at 7:30 p.m. and Bennett will be featured on June 2 at 8 p.m. Both concerts will be at the Orange County Performing Arts Center.

Chorale programs featuring the Bella Lewitzky Dance Company on Oct. 27 and works by Brahms on Feb. 9 have been announced. Information: (714) 556-6262. CHRIS PASLES
