Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lithuanian Constitutional Court decided on Wednesday that the Seimas decision on the main guidelines of agricultural reform was in its form contrary to the Constitution. The document adopted by the Seimas on the initiative of the majority of the Lithuanian Democratic Labour Party last June has provoked dissatisfaction of some strata of the society and of the Seimas opposition. According to them, the main guidelines of agricultural reform were hampering a historically just restoration of land property in the country.
