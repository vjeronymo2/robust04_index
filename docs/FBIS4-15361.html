BFN [From 8 April EESTI SENUMID p 3; "News Bulletin" No. 67] [Text] Chairman of the [Lithuanian] Seimas [parliament] Ceslovas Jurgenas is on an official visit to Estonia. The Speaker of the Seimas met President Lennart Meri, Riigikogu Speaker Ulo Nugis, Riigikogu vice-chairmen Edgar Savisaar and Tunne Kelam and Prime Minister Mart Laar yesterday. Speaker Jurgenas and President Meri denounced the Russian delegation statement rejecting the August 31 deadline. They discussed the Baltic cooperation in politics and legislation. Ulo Nugis and Ceslovas Jurgenas discussed mainly the Baltic-Russian relations. Nugis expressed his opinion that Russia has made a sharp turn in its foreign policy. Their meeting also concerned the Latvian-Russian agreement. The Lithuanian delegation is to visit the National Library and the Diplomatic School today.
