940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: MS Intl surges to Pounds 867,000 Profits of MS International, the Doncaster-based engineer, rose from a restated Pounds 306,000 to Pounds 867,000 pre-tax for the year to end-April. The improvement was in line with directors' expectations and was achieved from turnover of Pounds 29.4m (Pounds 28.8m), which included Pounds 2.02m from acquisitions. Exports advanced from Pounds 3.18m to Pounds 5.99m, representing some 20 per cent of total sales. Earnings emerged at 1.8p (0.7p) and a maintained final dividend of 1.5p makes a same-again 2.5p total.
