August 14, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

SKY DIVERS COLLIDE, FALL TO DEATH

Two sky divers fell to their deaths today after they collided during a jump involving 12 parachutists, authorities said.

Officials said Maurizio Moggi, 37, could not open his parachute in time to avoid a collision with the open parachute of Marco Bertolini, 22.

The force of the impact collapsed Bertolini's parachute, causing both men to plummet to the ground.
