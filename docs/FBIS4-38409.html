BFN [Text] The numbers of operational purpose formations and military units of the internal forces of Russia's Internal Affairs Ministry will be increased by 52,000 units. This is provided for in the federal program for stepping up the fight against crime in 1994-1995, which has been sanctioned by the president. In accordance with the program, the rights of the militia will be expanded. It is proposed that it should be granted the right to inspect the buildings of enteprises and institutions, irrespective of the type of ownership, in cases where information is available that a crime is to be committed. The rules are to be changed regarding the use of weapons by law enforcement bodies staff during the fulfillment of their official duties. It is planned to set up special militia subunits to escort long-distance trains and suburban trains.
