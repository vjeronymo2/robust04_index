930407 FT 07 APR 93 / Fall of 14% in business failures THE NUMBER of business failures in the first quarter of the year fell by 14 per cent to 1,776 compared with the same period last year, according to Trade Indemnity, the trade credit insurer. The number of insolvencies, however, was up by 1 per cent compared to the last quarter of 1992 and Trade Indemnity reported an 'alarming upturn in reported failures towards the end of the quarter'.
