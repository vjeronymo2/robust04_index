941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / Sewer designer's family inspect his work Eight descendents of engineer Sir Joseph Bazalgette, who designed London's sewerage system in the mid-19th century, went underground yesterday to admire his handywork in the Wick Lane sewer at Bow. Most of Bazalgette's sewers are still in place. London has a total of 1,500km of trunk sewers, much of it built by Bazalgette
