BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 1/6/1994 -- An official delegation of the Republic of Turkey arrived this morning on a special plane from Kiev. The delegation is headed by the Turkish President Suleyman Demirel. It consists of more than 140 Turkish ministers, Parliament members, businessmen and journalists. The delegation was welcomed at the airport by President Mircea Snegur, leaders of the Government and Parliament and other officials. After a military ceremony Demirel and Snegur made some short declarations for the press. The Turkish President mentioned the importance of the relations between Turkey and Moldova, both in the international and regional transformations occurring in the Black Sea basin. Referring to the Gagauz population of Turkish origin Demirel expressed his hope that the proportion of Gagauz people in the general development of Moldova will considerably increase. President Snegur declared that taking into consideration the cultural affinity between Turks and Gagauzians, both presidents will visit southern Moldova where they will get acquainted with the life style and preoccupations of the Gagauzians." Snegur added that the parties will sign some agreements of collaboration in the fields of culture, transportation, agriculture, crime control, etc. The main document to be signed is the Treaty on Friendship and Collaboration between Moldova and Turkey.
