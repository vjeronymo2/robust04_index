BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Several people have been arrested in connection with the investigation of the murder of Andrey Ayzderdzis, deputy of the State Duma. Vladimir Kolesnikov, head of the main directorate of the criminal investigation department of the Russian Interior Ministry, announced this to journalists in Moscow on Thursday. We can state that we know who committed the crime, he said; however, the cause of the crime is not yet entirely clear.
