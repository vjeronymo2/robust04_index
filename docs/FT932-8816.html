930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / UK Company News: London Atlantic Net asset value at London Atlantic Investment Trust improved from 80.3p to 87p over the year to March 31. Net revenue for the 12 months to the end of March was Pounds 1.52m (Pounds 1.39m) for earnings per share of 3.33p (3.06p). The final dividend is 2.29p for a total of 3.07p (2.95p).
