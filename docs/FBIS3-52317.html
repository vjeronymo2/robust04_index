Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa at the Royal Hashemite Court today. His Highness Crown Prince Hasan attended the meeting. The meeting reviewed the peace process and its latest developments. The meeting emphasized the importance of contacts and coordination between all Arab parties concerned with the peace process in order to participate in ending the occupation and assisting the Palestinian brothers in the occupied Arab territories in a manner that secures their rights within the framework of comprehensive, just, and lasting peace. The meeting was attended by Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Royal Hashemite Court chief; Marwan al-Qasim, the political adviser to his majesty the king; and Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs. Egyptian ambassador in Amman Ibrahim Wajih Hamdi also attended the meeting.
