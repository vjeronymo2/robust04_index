BFN [Text] The Jordanian News Agency has learned that the ministers tendered their resignations and placed them at the disposal of Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali at noon today. The agency also learned that Dr. al-Majali will introduce a reshuffle that includes a number of portfolios in his government, in which deputies [from the House of Representatives] may not take part at this stage. The reshuffle is expected to be announced soon after a royal decree is issued.
