Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Elizabeth Diaz Otero from the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has reaffirmed that respect for the civilian population's human rights, along with gauarantees that allow media representatives to carry out their duties, is of foremost importance in the search for a solution to the conflict in Chiapas. The president ordered the people responsible for organizations involved in operations in the Chiapas highlands to ensure this. The Mexican president on 5 January asked Jorge Madrazo Cuellar, president of the National Human Rights Commission, to visit Chiapas to correct any situation that might harm civil rights. The Mexican ombudsman headed for San Cristobal de las Casas on the afternoon of 5 January.
