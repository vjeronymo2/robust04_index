BFN [Text] The Civil Defense has made a final assessment of the damage, encompassing an area of over 40,000 square km, caused by heavy rainfall in the eastern provinces. Most of the damage took place in the provinces of Santiago de Cuba, Guantanamo, and Camaguey. The damage includes the partial or complete destruction of 4,360 houses and damages to another 32,720. Over 250,000 people were affected by the recent rains and floods. A significant number of dams, aqueducts, and electric and telephone services were also damaged. Damages were also reported in 4,500 square km of crops, mainly sugarcane, rice, tubers, and vegetables.
