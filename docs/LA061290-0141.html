June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: BREA;

JULY 4TH CELEBRATION RETURNS TO STADIUM

After a three-year hiatus, the Brea Fourth of July celebration will resume this year at the new Brea-Olinda High School stadium.

Tickets for the event, titled "Spotlight on Youth," are available from the Community Services Department at the civic center.

The event will include performances by a Marine Corps band and the Society for Preservation of Big Bands, and will feature several vintage airplanes.

The stadium gates will open at 7 p.m. Tickets are $10. For information, call (714) 990-7753.
