BFN [Own correspondent report under the "News" rubric: "Russian-American Meeting at State Duma"] [Text] Sergey Yushenkov, chairman of the State Duma Defense Committee, has met with U.S. Assistant Defense Secretary Elizabeth Sherwood. During the meeting, they discussed matters regarding Russian-American military cooperation, the prospects for START II ratification and Russia's joining the "Partnership for Peace" program, and also the status of the joint command staff exercises at the Totsk training ground. It is expected that in late May the Defense Committee will play host to a delegation of U.S. congressmen under the Armed Forces Committee chairman.
