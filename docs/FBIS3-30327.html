Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 1 February Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is in Bulgaria on an official two-day visit, met with National Assembly Chairman Aleksandur Yordanov, President Zhelev, and Prime Minister Berov. The possibilities of activating trade and economic cooperation between Bulgaria and Iran was at the center of all the talks. Special attention was devoted to the project of developing new transportation links between the Caucasus and the Black Sea, banking and legal relations, and the manner of making trade payments. The situation in the Balkans also was discussed. The two sides expressed their satisfaction with the failure of the attempt against Iranian President Rafsanjani. Dr. Zhelev assured the Iranian foreign minister that the situation of the Muslims in Bulgaria had fundamentally changed in the last years and that their rights had been restored.
