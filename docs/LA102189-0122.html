October 21, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

FRONTIER LEAGUE

Santa Ynez 54, Calabasas 7 -- Senior tailback Matt Halme scored four touchdowns and rushed for a game-high 148 yards in 18 carries as Santa Ynez breezed at home. Halme scored on runs of eight, six, three and 15 yards to help defeat Calabasas (1-6, 1-1). HANDEL WHITMORE
