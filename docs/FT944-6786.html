941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / UK Company News: American Opportunity American Opportunity Trust had net assets of Pounds 16.8m at the end of the year to September 30. This represented a rise of 18 per cent on last year's Pounds 14.2m, and compared with a 4 per cent fall in Standard & Poor's Composite index. Fully diluted net assets per share were 139.5p (121.6p). Net deficit for the year widened to Pounds 89,000 (Pounds 77,000), with losses per share of 1.18p (1.02p) and no dividend recommended. The Hambro-managed trust changed its name from Leveraged Opportunity Trust.
