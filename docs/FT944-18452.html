941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Citroen price cuts in Japan Citroen's Japanese import unit, Seibu Automobile Sales, will make price cuts of up to 18 per cent on certain models at the higher end of the market to try to increase its market share in Japan. Prices for the luxury XM model will be cut by Y1.1m to Y4.99m (Dollars 50,800) while the Xantia model will be marketed at between Y2.99m and Y3.39m.
