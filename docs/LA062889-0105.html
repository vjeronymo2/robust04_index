June 28, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: MAN SENT TO PRISON IN THREATS TO FONDA

A man who threatened the lives of actress Jane Fonda and her husband, Tom Hayden, has been sentenced to 16 months in prison.

Louis Robert Spallone, 42, of Sherman Oaks, pleaded guilty in May to three felony counts of falsely reporting a bomb to the couple. He was sentenced Monday by Santa Monica Superior Court Judge Robert W. Thomas.

Los Angeles County Deputy Dist. Atty. Richard Stone said at a preliminary hearing that Spallone made numerous "very threatening phone calls" to Hayden's office in 1987 and 1988.
