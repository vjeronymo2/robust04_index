BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, today issued Republican Decree No. 10 of 1994 concerning the setting up of the National Defense Council. Article 1 of the decree provides for the formation of the National Defense Council as follows: The chairman of the Presidential Council will act as chairman. Other members include deputy chairman and members of the Presidential Council, speaker of the National Assembly, prime minister, foreign minister, minister of defense, interior and security minister, finance minister, minister of culture and information, chief of the general staff, and, acting as rapporteur, the adviser to the Presidential Council on military affairs. Article 2 of the decree states that it is effective from the date of its issuance and that it shall be published in the official gazette.
