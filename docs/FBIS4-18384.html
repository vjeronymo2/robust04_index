BFN [Unattributed report: "Numbers of Unemployed Down"] [Excerpt] As a result of recent figures, Social Affairs Minister Josef Hesoun has spotted a turn of the tide on the labor market. "Unemployment is decreasing significantly," Hesoun said. At the end of March, 228,865 people were registered as unemployed, thus 4.3 percent fewer than at the same time last year. Apart from the stimulation of the labor market through the spring season and the mild weather, there was also a stimulation of the economy, Hesoun noted. With an unemployment rate of 4.6 percent, as calculated according to OECD methodology, Austria retains the third best international position. Only Luxembourg (2.7 percent) and Japan (2.7 percent) have more favorable rates. [passage omitted]
