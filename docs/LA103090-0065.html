October 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Morning Becomes a Revolving Door: Public radio station KCRW-FM's acclaimed "Morning Becomes Eclectic" music show is undergoing yet another modification.

The show's longtime host, Tom Schnabel, left the station earlier this month after 11 years to pursue a career with A&M Records. Fellow KCRW staffer Brent Wilcox took over temporarily while the station searched for Schnabel's replacement. Now, the station has decided to audition a variety of hosts over the air, and Wilcox has gone back to his early show "Nightland." CLAUDIA PUIG
