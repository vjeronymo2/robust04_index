930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: Maple Leaf buys 14 pubs from Bass Maple Leaf Inns, the joint venture between Labatt, the Canadian brewer, and Pubmaster, the Brent Walker pub retailer, has bought 14 pubs from Bass. The cost of the deal, which increases Maple Leaf's national estate to 164 pubs, was not disclosed. Maple Leaf acquired its first 77 pubs from Bass last year and bought a further 73 pubs from Allied-Lyons three months ago. Mr John Brackenbury, chairman of Pubmaster, said yesterday that expansion of the joint venture would continue throughout the next two years.
