BFN [Excerpt] Vice President Angel Roberto Seifart will represent President Juan Carlos Wasmosy at the inauguration of Costa Rican President-elect Julio Figueres. Seifart traveled to Costa Rica at 1600 yesterday aboard a Varig commercial flight. Interior Minister Carlos Podesta is accompanying the vice president. PLRA [Authentic Radical Liberal Party] Senator Luis Guanes Gondra and the Colorado Party's Constantino Mencia are part of the official entourage. Seifart advised Congress of his trip to Costa Rica thus fulfilling a constitutional requirement. [passage omitted]
