921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Brazilian debt job Mr Pedro Malan, Brazil's chief debt negotiator, has agreed to stay on under the new government of Acting President Itamar Franco, writes Christina Lamb in Rio de Janeiro. This will come as a relief to foreign creditors worried that the political changes might affect the recently negotiated termsheet over Brazil's Dollars 44bn commercial debt. The Senate is to vote in the next few weeks on whether to approve the text.
