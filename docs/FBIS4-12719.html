BFN [Report by Hitoshi Kato on interview with Tunisian Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia by unidentified NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN correspondent in Tunis; date not given] [Text] Tunis, 3 Apr -- Tunisian Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia granted an interview to NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN at his office in Tunis and commented on the Middle East peace issue. He said: "The PLO's headquarters has been located in Tunis since 1982. If the PLO so desires, we can serve as a mediator between it and other countries." The foreign minister thus indicated that if requested, his country, as a moderate party among Arab countries, is prepared to actively participate in efforts to help establish peace in the region.
