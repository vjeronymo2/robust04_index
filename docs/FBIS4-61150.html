BFN [Text] Nakhichevan, 31 May (TURAN)--On May 2 at 11 o'clock the leadership of Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic headed by the Chairman of Supreme Majlis, Namik Gasanov, and Premier, Shamsaddin Khanbabayev, arrived in Ordubadskiy -- the region centre of the republic. The People's Deputy, Khidzhran Kerimli, told TURAN that several "Ikarus" buses with armed policemen arrived in Ordubadskiy too. The same source reports that President Aliyev issued a decree concerning the removal of the head of Executive Power of Ordubadskiy, Rovshan Shafiyev (supporter of Elchibey), from his duties. Having known about the authorities' intention, Ordubadskiy's citizens began to picket the building of Executive Power and they protested against President Aliyev's resolution. The talks between Nakhichevan's leadership and local authorities lasted for 8 hours but were failure. The population and Nakhichevan's powers did not agree to fulfill President Aliyev's resolution. After that the leadership of Autonomous Republic and policemen left Ordubadskiy. As is known, Ordubadskiy's region of Nakhchivan is the only region in Azerbaijan where Abulfaz Elchibey's supporters hold several offices. After June events last summer Elchibey together with its supporters left Baku and arrived at the village of Keleki of Ordubadskiy region where he lives until now. Since that time Baku several times attempted to remove the head of Executive power of Ordubadskiy from his duties but those attempts failed.
