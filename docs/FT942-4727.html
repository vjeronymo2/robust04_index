940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / UK Company News: DC Cook car sales surge ahead in May DC Cook Holdings, the motor retail group, announced that its new car sales in May had soared by 52 per cent against the same month last year. Mr Derek Cook, chairman, said the increase compared very favourably with the overall national increase of 10 per cent in May for new car sales. Used car sales at Cook also showed a sharp increase of 69 per cent.
