920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Sardinia attack on soldiers Six soldiers were wounded in a hand grenade attack which investigators said was meant to intimidate an army brigade conducting a show of force against Sardinian kidnap and bandit gangs, AP reports from Lula, Sardinia. Investigators said the type of grenade thrown at the soldiers had not been used by the army in four years but was readily available on the black market.
