931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Scrutiny of professions urged BRITAIN'S professions should be scrutinised for restrictive practices by the Office of Fair Trading, according to Mr Graham Mather, president of the European Policy Forum, the cross-party think-tank. Mr Mather yesterday told the Royal Institute of British Architects that professions such as medicine and law had escaped the reforming zeal of Thatcherism. They lagged behind other businesses, the utilities and public-sector organisations in the quality of service they gave to their customers, he said.
