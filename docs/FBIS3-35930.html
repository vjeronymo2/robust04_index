Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shcheglova] [Text] Sochi, 1 Feb -- The situation in Abkhazia's Galskiy rayon has recently been exacerbated, ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned from Beslan Bargandzhia, spokesman of the chairman of the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet. In his words, the Abkhazian side started preparations for deploying the peacekeeping forces along the confrontation line between the Abkhazian and Georgian troops on the Inguri river. At the same time several armed groups of Georgian troops crossed the Inguri river and penetrated deep into the enemy's territory. Exchanges of fire have resumed in Abkhazia's Galskiy rayon. People have been killed or wounded. According to Beslan Bargandzhia, the Abkhazian side lost 25 people in exchanges of fire over the past month. The spokesman believes that there is a danger that the hostilities in the republic will resume. He is convinced that it is necessary to speed up the deployment of the peacekeeping forces in the conflict zone, as envisaged by the Geneva accords.
