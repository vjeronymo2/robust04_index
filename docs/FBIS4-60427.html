BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received brother Dr. Muhsin Khalil, ambassador of the fraternal Republic of Iraq, who handed a message to the brother president from his brother Iraqi President Saddam Husayn dealing with the fraternal relations between the two countries, as well as current developments in our country. The message stresses the support of the brothers in Iraq for Yemeni unity as a pan-Arab gain that should be preserved and safeguarded from all challenges. The brother president expressed his appreciation for the brothers in Iraq and for all the other brothers and friends, who have voiced their great eagerness to support the march of unity, democracy, and stability in our country.
