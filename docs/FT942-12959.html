940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Bunzl Mr Anthony Habgood, chief executive of Bunzl, the distribution and cigarette filters group, earned a Pounds 137,500 performance related bonus in 1993, which took his remuneration to Pounds 521,454 against Pounds 388,373 in 1992. The bonus was based on growth of earnings per share, which rose from 5.6p to 8.3p. Pre-tax profits in 1993 improved from Pounds 40.4m to Pounds 55.8m, with significant help coming from translation of North American profits at a stronger dollar exchange rate. Earnings were also helped by a lower tax rate.
