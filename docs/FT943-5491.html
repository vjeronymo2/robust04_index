940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Torday & Carlisle incurs Pounds 0.55m deficit Torday & Carlisle, the Newcastle-based engineer, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 546,000 for the six months to end-June, refecting the warning in the 1993 accounts of a poor start to the current year. The pre-tax outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 17,000 in the corresponding period, although that included a Pounds 380,000 profit contribution from Elfab Hughes, the safety equipment arm which was sold in the fourth quarter of 1993. Group turnover fell to Pounds 14.1m, against Pounds 17.2m - including Pounds 2.05m from discontinued operations. Losses per share came out at 3.4p (0.4p earnings).
