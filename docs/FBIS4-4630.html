BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 2 -- Chairman of the National Assembly Nong Duc Manh received here today a delegation of the Ministry of Labour and Social Affairs of Iran led by its Minister Hoseyn Kamali. Vietnamese Minister of Labour, War Invalids and Social Affairs Tran Dinh Hoan was also present at the reception. Chairman Nong Duc Manh and Minister H. Kamali compared notes on the friendly relations between the two peoples and the cooperation between the two countries. Chairman Nong Duc Manh affirmed that the Vietnam National Assembly would do its utmost to carry out documents initialed by Vietnam and Iran. He also wished for the further development of the friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
