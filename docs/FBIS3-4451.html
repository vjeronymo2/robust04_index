Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 15 (XINHUA) -- A financial seminar, aimed at boosting the financial cooperation between China and Britain, was held here today. With "Developing a capital market in London -- China's international gateway" as its theme, another session of the seminar will open in Shanghai on March 17. Sir Andrew Smith, visiting chairman of the London Stock Exchange, gave a keynote address on what the London Stock Exchange is to economic development in China. Jing Shuping, chairman of the China International Economic Consultants Inc. and chairman of the Stock Exchange Executive Council, gave an opening address. Jointly held by the China-Britain Trade Group and British Invisibles, the seminar aims to acquaint Chinese financial personnel with the British experience in raising international finances and promote Sino-British cooperation in this and other areas. Some dozen British bankers, lawyers and financial experts delivered lectures to the Chinese audience on British experience in developing its domestic capital market, the requirements for the listing of companies on overseas markets and project financing. About 200 Chinese from relevant government departments, banks and enterprises attended the seminar.
