920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Finance & the Family: 'Get your tax back' offer The Inland Revenue has launched a 'two ticks and your tax back' advertising campaign with a free telephone inquiry service to encourage people to reclaim tax on interest and dividends. An estimated 15m individuals - who have savings or shares that pay interest or dividends, and who have other income or a pension of less than Pounds 60 a week - may be eligible for immediate tax repayments. The Freephone taxback service is open 8am to 8pm seven days a week from February 9 till March 13. The number is 0800-660800.
