940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Fleming EuroFledge net assets advance Fleming European Fledgeling Investment Trust reported net asset value per share up 40 per cent, from 77.6p to 108.9p, over the year to March 31. The rise exceeded that of the FT-A Europe Ex UK Index which increased 21.9 per cent over the same period. Lower investment income combined with higher interest charges left a net loss of Pounds 108,000 for the year to the end of March, compared with profits of Pounds 177,000. Losses per share were 0.27p (earnings 0.44p). There is no dividend (0.15p).
