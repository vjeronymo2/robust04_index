BFN [Text] In Kashmir valley, 20 militants have been killed in inter-gang warfare during the last fortnight. The group clashes between different militant organizations have spread to almost every district of the valley. Yesterday, a self-styled district commander of the JKLF [Jammu and Kashmir Liberation Front] was killed by Hizbul Mojahedin at Chandpura in Srinagar. After the incident, thousands of people came out in the streets in Srinagar shouting slogans against the militants. Last week also, a procession was taken out in (Ashmukan) town by local residents to protest against the activities of the militant groups.
