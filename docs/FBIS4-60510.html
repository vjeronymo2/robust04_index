BFN [Text] A communique distributed in Gaza yesterday and signed by the Fatah Movement, captives movement [al-harakah al-asirah], and all the national and humanitarian organizations, said that the guardian [ra'i] sentenced to life imprisonment was suffering from paralysis of the right leg and a slipped disc after undergoing two surgical operations, with no progress in his state of health. The communique said that the guardian was in solitary confinement at the al-Ramlah prison and that he could die any at any moment. The communique called on all human rights organizations in the world to intervene to put pressure on the Israeli Government to release all the prisoners and appealed to people of conscience to take serious actions to end the suffering and to secure the immediate release of Shaykh Ahmad Yasin, who is suffering from various ailments in addition to almost total paralysis. Prisoners urged the population to stage sit-ins at the offices of the Red Cross, fly black flags, demand the release of the prisoners, and convey their demands to legal and international bodies.
