BFN [Text] A resident of the settlement of Netzarim in the Gaza Strip was moderately wounded this evening when shots were fired at his car from a passing vehicle. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that Netzarim residents reacted to the attack by blocking a traffic axis near their settlement. They are demanding that the commander of the Southern Command meet them and promise that the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] will prevent Palestinians from driving on the road where the attack took place. An Arab youth was seriously injured in the eye today by a rubber bullet fired by soldiers at stonethrowers in the Nabulus casbah. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the youth was evacuated to a hospital in Jerusalem.
