BFN [Text] Runoff parliamentary elections were held in 34 Ukrainian precincts where over half of the voters came to the polling stations Saturday and Sunday. In 27 precincts the voting results were made known to INTERFAX-Ukraine by the election commissions, 17 independents, six communists and one socialist were elected. The list of new deputies includes Agriculture Minister Yuriy Karasik and prime minister's adviser Viktor Suslov. No candidate obtained more than half the votes in three of these precincts. Another round of elections will be held there. Out of the 73 new members of parliament 18 are communists, three socialists, two are members of the Peasants' Party and 12 represent national democratic and national radical organizations. The remainder campaigned as independents. Runoff elections will be held in the remaining 367 Ukrainian precincts on 9 and 10 April.
