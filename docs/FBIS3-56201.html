Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 13 TASS -- Sergey Shakhray, the leader of the Party of Russian Unity and Accord who had long before elections said he would run for the speaker of the state Duma, refused to join the race on Thursday [13 January]. He told lawmakers that he is quitting the race in favour of Vladimir Medvedev nominated by independent deputies. "An independent candidate should be preferred in order not push the Duma into a deadend", Shakhray said.
