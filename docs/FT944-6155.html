941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Dartmoor net asset value slips to 93.7p Dartmoor Investment Trust, which in October won a hostile bid for Sphere Investment Trust, reported net asset value per share down from 114.8p to 93.7p over the 12 months to October 31. At the April 30 year-end it was 118.45p. Net revenue for the six months to the end of October was Pounds 1.81m (Pounds 933,000) for earnings per share of 5.18p (3.89p). A second interim dividend of 2.8p is declared making a total so far of 5.6p (5p).
