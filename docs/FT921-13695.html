920120 FT 20 JAN 92 / Lubbers to scrap S African visit A PLANNED visit to South Africa next month by Mr Ruud Lubbers, the Dutch prime minister, is likely to be called off, the Dutch Foreign Ministry said yesterday, Reuter reports from Amsterdam. Mr Hans van den Broek, Dutch foreign minister, was told during a telephone conversation with Mr Nelson Mandela, the African National Congress leader, that a visit at this time 'was not opportune'. An announcement will be made to parliament tomorrow.
