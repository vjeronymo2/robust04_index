BFN [Text] A Lebanese Hizballah delegation headed by Hasan Nasrallah, the party secretary general, held talks with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam in Damascus last night. In a statement issued in Beirut, Hizballah said the delegation and the Syrian vice president discussed developments in the region and the ongoing and increasing Zionist acts of aggression against south Lebanon. The statement said the two sides stressed the need to continue jihad against the Zionist campaigns against south Lebanon.
