January 22, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

FIRST SPRING GAMES SCHEDULED FOR MARCH 2

Only six weeks and counting . . . the Boston Red Sox and Chicago White Sox open the spring training season March 2 in Sarasota, Fla., and every other team except Cleveland plays the next day.

Atlanta faces Georgia Tech in another game March 2. The World Series champion Dodgers start the next day against Kansas City.

The Grapefruit and Cactus Leagues run through April 2, the day before the regular season begins.
