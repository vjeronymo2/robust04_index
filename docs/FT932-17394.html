930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: MPs to probe photocopiers A CROSS-PARTY committee of MPs was formed yesterday to investigate alleged contractual abuses by photocopying leasing companies and to press for changes in the law to safeguard their customers. An early day Commons motion has also been tabled, alleging more than 3,000 cases of onerous contract arrangements, and welcoming the decision of the Office of Fair Trading to investigate the photocopying market.
