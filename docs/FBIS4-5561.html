BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 10 (KCNA) -- The Organizing Committee of the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival hosted a banquet at the People's Palace of Culture Saturday for the participants in the festival. Invited to the reception were the members of foreign art troupes and members of overseas Korean art troupes. Present there were Chang Chol, vice-premier and minister of culture and art, who is chairman of the festival organizing committee, Kang Hyon-su, chief secretary of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal People's Committee, other officials concerned and artistes in the city. A speech was made at the reception.
