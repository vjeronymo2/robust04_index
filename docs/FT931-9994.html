930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Caledonia required to buy Ralston assets The Liquidators of Ralston Investment Trust have exercised their option to require Caledonia Investments, which owns 75 per cent of the trust, to acquire some of its assets. The net asset value of Ralston was Pounds 15.7m. There will be a cash distribution of 65.4p per share to shareholders other than Caledonia on February 11.
