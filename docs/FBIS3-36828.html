Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Own Information report: "Moscow and Hanoi Decide To Formalize Their Relations"] [Text] Russia and Vietnam will sign a new political document governing the fundamental principles of their relations to replace the 1978 treaty, many of whose provisions have become outdated. Apart from one of the subjects of the treaty, namely the Soviet Union, disappearing, the actual basis of Russian-Vietnamese relations has also changed considerably. The new document will be signed during SRV Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet's visit to Moscow. A separate article will determine the status of the Pacific Fleet Naval Base in Cam Ranh Bay.
