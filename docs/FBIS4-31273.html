BFN [Text] Justyna Matyskiewicz, head of the Katowice Steelworks information office, has announced that, during the first six days of the strike, the plant suffered losses amounting to 40.78 billion zlotys [Z]. According to her, each subsequent day of the strike will result in further losses of about Z10 billion. Late last night, the strike committee began talks with the board of the steelworks. The talks are not being conducted face-to-face but, rather, through mediators from the Ministry for Ownership Transformations. There are signs that an agreement can eventually be reached.
