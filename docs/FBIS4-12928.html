BFN [Text] Occupied Palestine -- The Israeli bus blast in Hadera, north of Tel Aviv, this morning has left six Israelis dead and injured about 30 others, some seriously. Israeli Police Chief Rafi Peled declared that a suicide operation was behind the bus blast. The 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam Battalions--the military wing of the Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas--claimed the operation. It stressed that these operations are to avenge the martyrs of the Hebron massacre. The operation also came one week after the 'Afula martyrdom operation that left seven Israelis dead.
