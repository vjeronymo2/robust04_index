BFN [Text] Paris, May 3 (AFP) -- France Tuesday [3 May] said it is granting strife-torn Rwanda three million francs [Fr] ($540,000) in emergency aid. Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin told the National Assembly that the government had yet to decide on the best way to channel the money. France, he said, "has not stood by passively in the face of this drama, and will continue to show its solidarity."
