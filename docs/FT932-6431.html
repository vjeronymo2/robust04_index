930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / People: Insurance moves Graham Bailey, Ken Evans, Tim Griffiths, Chris Higham, Martin Neal, Steve Tysoe and Nick Vine have been appointed directors of ALEXANDER HOWDEN Reinsurance Brokers. Michael Box has been appointed md non-marine of ALEXANDER HOWDEN Ltd, while the following are also appointed directors: Paul Carter marine & energy, Simon Delchar non-marine, Alan Hyatt services, Ian Johnston non-marine, Gavin Laird marine & energy, Terence Stern services, and Roger Walsh marine & energy.
