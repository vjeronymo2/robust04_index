931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Geared Income debenture issue Geared Income Investment Trust has completed the issue of an unlisted zero coupon debenture to a single institutional investor to raise about Pounds 4.82m net. The debenture, which has a semi-annual gross redemption yield of 8.263 per cent, is repayable in 10 years' time. It is secured by a first floating charge on all the company's assets.
