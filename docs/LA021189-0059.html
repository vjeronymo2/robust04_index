February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

49ERS SHOOT FOR .500 AGAINST FRESNO STATE

Cal State Long Beach will try to reach .500 for the season tonight at 7:30 in a Big West Conference game against Fresno State in University Gym.

The 49ers, who are 10-11 overall and 7-5 in the Big West, are coming off a 60-49 victory over University of the Pacific Thursday night.

Fresno State (11-9, 6-5) defeated the 49ers, 56-55, Jan. 14 at Fresno. DICK WAGNER
