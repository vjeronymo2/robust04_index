931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Aviva incurs Dollars 0.6m third quarter loss Aviva Petroleum, the Texas-based oil and gas company quoted in London, reported a deficit for the third quarter to the end of September of Dollars 632,000 (Pounds 424,000), or losses of 2 cents per share. That compared with a deficit of Dollars 515,000 - losses of 9 cents per share - in the comparable period and brings the deficit at the nine months stage to Dollars 975,000 (Dollars 8.76m) for losses of 4 cents (Dollars 1.65) per share. Aviva also announced it was offering to purchase for cancellation, at 71p apiece, some 170,000 shares of the company's common stock held by owners of less than 100 shares.
