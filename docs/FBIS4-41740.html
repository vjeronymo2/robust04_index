BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 27 Jun -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin met members of the Russian Agrarian Party group in the Kremlin today. The deputies briefed the president on their group's organizational structure and its numerical strength, as well as on their political positions, which they describe as centrist. The conversation was devoted to problems of the agro-industrial complex. Having noted his full awareness of the difficulties Russian agriculture was experiencing, the president stressed the need to develop the agro-industrial complex taking account of the overall strategy of the country's economic reforms. It was agreed to continue contacts between presidential structures and the State Duma's groups, including the Agrarian Party group, to prepare agreed decisions, the Russian president's press service told ITAR-TASS.
