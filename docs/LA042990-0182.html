April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $36 when Tartas, his first horse in his $6 triple, ran second in the seventh race to Zayyani. Bob, who started with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $844.

Bob's bet today is a $30 win parlay from Atrevida in the fourth to Smoke Jumper in the sixth.
