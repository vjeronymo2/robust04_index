941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Shares in Sao Paulo rallied for the second straight day, closing up 2.6 per cent in moderate trading. The Bovespa index of the 55 most-active shares ended up 1,203 at 46, marking a 7.3 per cent rally over the last two sessions. Volume was RDollars 294.5m (Dollars 344.4m).
