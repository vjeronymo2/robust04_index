Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 10 (KCNA) -- Chairman of the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly [SPA] Yang Hyong-sop sent a message of greetings to Jaime Yoshihama Tanaka upon his reelection as chairman of the Democratic Constituent Assembly of Peru. The message expressed the belief that the friendly cooperative relations between the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly and the Democratic Constituent Assembly of Peru would develop more favourably in accordance with the interests and aspirations of the two people and wished him greater success in his responsible work.
