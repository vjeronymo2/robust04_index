930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: MPs to hear Timex managers SENIOR managers from the Timex plant in Dundee, site of an industrial dispute since the end of January, have agreed to appear before the Commons employment committee on April 28, along with representatives of the AEEU engineering and electrical union, to be questioned on the role of employment legislation in industrial disputes. Mr Ernie Ross, Labour MP for the Dundee West constituency, is a member of the committee.
