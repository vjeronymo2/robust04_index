920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / BT staff shortage predicted BRITISH Telecom will face serious staff shortages in some areas if it continues with its redundancy programme, Mr Tony Young, leader of the main BT union the National Communications Union, told the TUC. He said BT should allow this year's 34,000 job losses 'to bed down' before pressing ahead with planned cuts of 10,000 to 15,000 a year for the next two years.
