930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Bank to sell Pounds 650m of gilts THE Bank of England yesterday said it is to start selling gilts worth Pounds 650m from Monday. This is made up of Pounds 250m of 7.25 per cent Treasury stock due 1998; Pounds 200m of 9.75 per cent Treasury stock due 2002; Pounds 50m of 3.5 per cent funding stock due 1999 to 2004; and Pounds 150m of 2.5 per cent index-linked Treasury stock due 2020. Certified prices will be available from the government broker later.
