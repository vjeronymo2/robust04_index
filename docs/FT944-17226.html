941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Exporters report prompt payments The upturn in the European economy means British exporters' customers are continuing to settle their bills more promptly, although Germany remains a problem area, says NCM Credit Insurance. An NCM survey shows that the value of payment delays fell 24 per cent in the year to October compared with a 14 per cent fall in the 12 months to the end of June. The value of payment delays by German companies had risen in the last quarter.
