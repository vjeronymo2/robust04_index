Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait's permanent representative at the United Nations, Ambassador Muhammad 'Abdallah Abu al-Hasan, has warned against the Iraqi regime's recent maneuvres to get around the UN Security Council resolutions, particularly Resolution 786, to benefit from the lifting of the oil embargo imposed against it. Ambassador Muhammad 'Abdallah Abu al-Hasan stressed to the chairman of the UN Security Council, France's permanent representative at the UN, Jean-Bernard Merimee, the need for maintaining the sanctions imposed on the Iraqi regime as long as that regime, which was still refusing to release Kuwaiti POWs and detainees held in its prisons, did not recognize the sovereignty and independence of the State of Kuwait.
