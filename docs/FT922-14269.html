920407 FT 07 APR 92 / International Company News: Kraft set to take stake in IQM INDUSTRIAL Quesera Menorquina (IQM), the Spanish dairy group, has reached an agreement in principle to merge its interests with Kraft General Foods, a unit of Kraft General Foods International of the US, Reuter reports. A spokesman said the plan was for Kraft to acquire a majority stake in IQM, but no further details were available.
