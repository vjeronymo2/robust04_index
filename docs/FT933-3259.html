930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Israel and the PLO: Violence in Beirut Pro-Iranian Hizbollah women chant slogans against Lebanese leaders and protest against the PLO-Israel pact during the funeral in Beirut of eight protesters killed by troops at a rally opposing the peace accord. Meanwhile Iranian President Ali Akbar Rafsanjani pledged in Tehran that the forces of Islam would not rest until 'every last piece' of Palestine had been liberated, writes Parichehre Mosteshar. He condemned those who signed 'the greatest act of betrayal of the Palestinian people'.
