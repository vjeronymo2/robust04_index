October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Cine-Source Stock: An Oct. 16 article incorrectly reported that the stock of Burbank-based Cine-Source Inc., co-producer of the movie "Texasville," fell from $6 a share on Oct. 7 to 21.875 cents a share on Oct. 15.

Cine-Source's common stock, which trades on the NASDAQ system, actually fell from 28.125 cents on Friday, Oct. 5, to 21.875 on Oct. 15. The company's preferred units, which also trade on NASDAQ, declined from $6 each on Oct. 5 to $4.375 on Oct. 15.
