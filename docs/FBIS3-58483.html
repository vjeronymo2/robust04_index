Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening, the Greek Cypriots added another border violation to those they have been carrying out for some time. TAK [Turkish Cypriot News Agency] reports that 10 shots were fired from two different places in the Greek Cypriot sector, some 150 meters into the border near the Lokmaci barricades in Lefkosa [Nicosia]. This provocative act occurred at around 1930. The Turkish Cypriot side did not respond in kind, so there were no casualties or damage to property. The Turkish Cypriot authorities have protested the incident to UNFICYP [UN Forces in Cyprus].
