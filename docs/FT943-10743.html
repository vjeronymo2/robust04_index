940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Exceptionals lift Illingworth Morris Illingworth Morris and Woolcombers Group, both part of a textiles empire controlled by Mr Alan Lewis, yesterday announced their results for the year to March 31. Illingworth achieved a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 3.33m after exceptional credits of Pounds 2.29m (Pounds 58,000 debit). Turnover was Pounds 50m (Pounds 52.7m). At its Woolcombers subsidiary pre-tax profits fell to Pounds 1.84m (Pounds 2.78m) on turnover of Pounds 23.8m (Pounds 27.3m). Illingworth's earnings per share were 7.1p (3.2p) and Woolcombers' 4.3p (9.3p). Following the postponement of Woolcombers' flotation, Jarmain & Son has been transferred to Hartley Investment Trust, the ultimate parent.
