Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] At its session today, presided over by Dr. Haris Silajdzic, the government adopted the report by the delegation of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina on the negotiations and the signing of the Washington agreement. The government examined and adopted the draft Constitution of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina as well as the drafts of other documents stemming from the Washington agreement. The government decided to recommend the adoption of the document to the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly. At its session today the government also examined the draft of a preliminary agreement between the U.S. Government and the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina on economic, technical, and other forms of assistance.
