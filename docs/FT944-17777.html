941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Greenacre expands Greenacre, the nursing homes operator, has acquired The Argyles Nursing Home, Berkshire, for Pounds 1.95m cash. The acquisition brings the total number of homes under operation and development by Greenacre to 13 and the number of beds to 680. The Argyles showed adjusted profits before depreciation, finance charges and tax of Pounds 352,000 in the year to January 31 1994.
