BFN [Text] The operations room in the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate has started its work. It met in the area under the control of the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP] and the Islamic Movement. It is noteworthy that most of the areas north and east of al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate are under the control of the peshmerga and mujahidin of our KDP and the Islamic Movement. The operations room held its first meeting in (Girdanata), situated a few kilometers east of Sayyid Sadiq. The following decisions were made: A comprehensive cease-fire; the formation of a subcommittee for operations from representatives of the INC [Iraqi National Congress] and the three parties--the KDP, the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], and the Islamic Movement. Decision: The operations room decided the following at its meeting in the village of (Girdanata): 1. The PUK should withdraw its forces from the villages of Gamish Tappah and Nawgirdan. The INC forces will be stationed there. The KDP and the Islamic Movement forces shall not enter the two villages. 2. The INC will control the village of (Girdanata) and act as an observation post for the signatories. The decision was signed by Brigadier General Talal al-'Ubaydi for the INC, Jawhar Namiq for the KDP, Mulazim 'Umar 'Abdallah for the PUK, and Kamil 'Ali for the Islamic Movement.
