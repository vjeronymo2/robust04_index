941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust saw a fall in net profits for the six months to September 30 from Pounds 508,000 to Pounds 311,000. Net asset value per capital share dropped to 49.6p (52.4p). Earnings per income share fell from 6.35p to 3.88p. The interim dividend is 2.25p.
