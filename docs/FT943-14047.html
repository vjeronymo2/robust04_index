940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / National Savings slide continues The slide in the contribution of National Savings to government funding continued last month, falling to Pounds 405m from Pounds 424m in May, Scheherazade Daneshkhu writes. Net receipts in June totalled Pounds 194m, with gross sales of Pounds 825m being offset by repayments of Pounds 631m. Accrued interest amounted to Pounds 211m. For the first time since their launch at the beginning of the year Pensioners' Bonds - with net sales of Pounds 116m - last month ceased to be the highest contributor and were supplanted by premium bonds with net sales of Pounds 142m. National Savings' contribution to funding for the financial year 1994-95 to date was Pounds 1.4bn at the end of June. Finance and the Family, Pg V
