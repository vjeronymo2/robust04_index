BFN [Text] President Fidel Ramos has given orders to study the possibility of releasing detained [communist] rebel leader Wilma Tiamzon. Press Secretary Jesus Sison announced that the president has commissioned Justice Secretary Franklin Drilon to submit an appropriate motion to release Tiamzon from the control of the Philippine National Police. The president made this move after personally speaking to Tiamzon at the Philippine Heart Center. Tiamzon will be placed under the custody of the Bishop's Conference of the Philippines. Sison added that the president's move is one step towards accelerating the peace process.
