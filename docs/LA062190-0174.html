June 21, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: 10 HIGH SCHOOL GRADS AWARDED SCHOLARSHIPS

Aerojet Electronic Systems Division in Azusa has awarded 10 scholarships of $2,000 each to local high school graduates who achieved outstanding academic scores and showed strong promise to excel in an engineering, medical or scientific discipline.

The students are Lisa Fischbacker and Rodolfo Marquez of Azusa High School, Kenneth Valmonte and Zinnat Bhojani of Duarte High School, Cesar Atienza and Voltaire Pajo of Gladstone High School, Victoria Oliver and Michael Hohmann of Glendora High School and Heather Hanson and George Delao of Monrovia High School.
