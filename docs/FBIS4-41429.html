BFN [Summary] Apas Dzhumagulov, the prime minister of Kyrgyzstan, paid a visit to Kazakhstan. He was received by the president and the prime minister of the Republic of Kazakhstan. They discussed the issue of Kazakhstan's payment of debt to Kyrgyzstan for power supplies. It was decided that Kazakhstan will provide Kyrgyzstan with 1 million tonnes of coal, 100,000 tonnes of black oil, 3,000 tonnes of gas, and 5,000 tonnes of diesel oil as part of a payment for debt. Apart from Apas Dzhumagulov, the delegation of Kyrgyzstan included Sultanov, the chairman of the republican national bank; the president of the joint stock Kyrgyzenergetika power engineering company; the general director of the Karabalty ore mining combine; and other officials.
