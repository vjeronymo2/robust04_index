BFN [Text] Beijing, May 26 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan and Japanese Deputy Foreign Minister Hiroshi Fukuda will hold the 13th round of regular consultations between the foreign affairs authorities of the two countries in Tokyo on June 1. Speaking to reporters at the press conference here today, Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman Wu Jianmin said that the two sides will exchange views on bilateral relations and international issues of common concern.
