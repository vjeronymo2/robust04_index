BFN [Report by Iulian Chifu: "Mircea Snegur and Igor Smirnov Agreed To Convert Ilie Ilascu's Death Penalty Into 20 Years of Imprisonment"] [Text] On Wednesday, 4 May, sources at the parliament in Chisinau revealed to EVENIMENTUL ZILEI that during President Mircea Snegur's 28 April meeting in Tiraspol with the separatist Dniester leader Igor Smirnov, they have approached the issue of the political prisoners [held in the self-styled Dniester Republic], as well as the issue of the death penalty passed on Ilie Ilascu. The two political leaders agreed to convert his death penalty into 20 years of imprisonment. The same sources revealed that Nicolae Andronic, the chairman of the [Moldovan] parliamentary commission in charge of having the Tiraspol political prisoners freed and restoring the rights of deputy Ilie Ilascu, was not allowed to attend the negotiations by the Dniester party, because of Andronic's inflexible stance regarding the unconditioned release of the six political prisoners.
