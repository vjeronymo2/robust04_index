940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: TJ Hughes shows rise 9% to Pounds 1.6m TJ Hughes, the discount retailer based in the north-west of England, increased pre-tax profits by 9 per cent in the year to January 29 from Pounds 1.47m to Pounds 1.6m. Sales were up 8 per cent, from Pounds 40.2m in the previous 53 weeks to Pounds 43.5m. The final dividend is raised to 1.75p, making a total of 2.5p (2.3p) payable from earnings per share of 5.45p (5.2p). Mr Gurth Hoyer Millar, chairman of the USM-traded company, said that the development of the discount department stores had continued with an opening in Preston, bringing the total to 13. Three further store openings were planned for 1995 and a 130,000 sq ft distribution facility had been acquired. Mr Eric Hodges is to retire as managing director next January and will be succeeded by Mr Steve Boyfield. Mr Hodges will become a non-executive director.
