December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Diceman Steamed: Andrew Dice Clay is upset with 20th Century Fox for deciding to permanently shelve his in-concert feature film, "Dice Rules."

"The whole thing stinks, it's just a load of crap," he said. The film, originally slated for release last fall, "could have been a cool Christmas present for my fans," he said, "to see the Diceman up there on the screen, 40-feet tall. I completely trusted the people at Fox, put my entire career in their hands, which turned out to be my mistake." CLAUDIA PUIG
