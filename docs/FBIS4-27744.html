BFN [Text] Gan Ziyu, vice chairman of the State Planning Commission of China, and his five-member delegation, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Thailand Jin Guihua and Charoen Phokkhaphan Group Vice President Li Shaozhu, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister Chuan Likphai at Government House on the afternoon of 2 June. The other members of the delegation are Xu Tongmao, Zhang Jiushi, Cao Yushu, Li Li, and Li Bin.
