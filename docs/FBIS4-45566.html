BFN [Text] Chengdu, June 23 (XINHUA) -- Some 17,000 grams of confiscated heroin was burned this morning at the Pixian Sports Stadium in Chengdu, capital of southwest China's Sichuan Province. This was an important anti-drug operation by Sichuan Province just before June 26, the International Anti-Drugs Day. Sichuan Province is a passage and transfer station for international drug smugglers. In the first five months of this year, public security organizations cracked 531 cases of drug abuse and smuggling, arrested 706 suspects and confiscated 46,300 grams of heroin and 13,000 grams of opium.
