Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Nov 19 (XINHUA) -- Shell China Ltd. has offered 500,000 H.K. dollars to China in support of the country's environmental protection program. Qu Geping, chairman of the Environmental Protection Committee under the National People's Congress, accepted the donation during a meeting with Tim Brennand, chairman of Shell China Ltd., here today. ``China welcomes foreign enterprises to make investments in China's environmental sector,'' Qu said. Qu added that China is quickening legislative work on environmental protection while seeking a high economic growth and setting up a socialist market economy.
