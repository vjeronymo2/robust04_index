Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (MENA)-- A Norwegian Foreign Ministry delegation led by (Per Haugestad), official in charge of the Middle East desk at the Norwegian Foreign Ministry, arrived in Cairo this morning on a two-day visit to Egypt. The head of the delegation stated that the goal of the visit is to play a role in reconciling the Palestinian and Israeli sides in the peace negotiations and overcoming the obstacles impeding implementation of the Gaza-Jericho Declaration of Principles.
