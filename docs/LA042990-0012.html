April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MIXED MEDIA;

** 1/2 "THE RISE OF NENEH CHERRY" NENEH CHERRY BMG VIDEO VIDEOCASSETTE ($16.98): A PERIODIC SURVEY OF POP-RELATED LASER DISCS, BOOKS AND VIDEOCASSETTES. RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE (POOR) TO FIVE STARS (A CLASSIC.)

This is everything you ever wanted to know about Cherry, who emerged last year as a promising soul singer and rapper -- plus a whole lot you didn't want to know.

While a welcome change from simple clip compilations that offer no extras, this 65-minute video verges on information overload -- trivial information. Do you really want to see her being made up for photo sessions or exchanging inanities with adoring fans during radio station chats? The behind-the scenes footage, while occassionally amusing, is self-serving. Surprisingly, the performance segments, featuring songs like her hit "Buffalo Stance" and "Inner City Mama," aren't the main focus. That may have been a wise decision, because Cherry seems to have limited talent. DENNIS HUNT
