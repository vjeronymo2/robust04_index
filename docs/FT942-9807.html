940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Dutch purchase for Bunzl Bunzl, the distribution and cigarette filters group, has acquired a leading Dutch distributor of disposable products to the catering and hotel trades. It has bought Gebroeders van Hoogstraten Papier en Plastics, which trades as Hopa Disposables. Mr Anthony Habgood, Bunzl chief executive, said the deal demonstrated the group's continuing commitment to the development of plastic and paper disposables, its largest and most profitable business area.
