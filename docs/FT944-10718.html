941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / International Company News: Nissin Food Products ahead midway Nissin Food Products, a leading Japanese food maker, said yesterday its unconsolidated recurring profit - before extraordinary items and tax - for the first half-year to end-September rose 1.3 per cent to Y10.15bn from Y10.01bn a year ago, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Net profits were 11 per cent ahead at Y5.47bn, compared with Y4.91bn, on sales which advanced 1.8 per cent to Y106.08bn from Y104.18bn.
