920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / Foreign cargo at ports rises 2% FOREIGN cargo handled by UK sea ports rose 2 per cent to 305.5m tonnes last year, the British Ports Federation has reported. The increase was mainly because of a 5 per cent increase in exports to 120.6m tonnes. Imports rose 0.4 per cent to 184.9m tonnes. The three biggest ports by non-domestic tonnage were Immingham, Humberside (34.7m tonnes), London (31.8m tonnes) and Tees & Hartlepool (26.4m tonnes).
