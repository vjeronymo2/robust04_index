April 24, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

SPIELBERG, IRVING DECIDE ON DIVORCE

Director Steven Spielberg and his actress wife, Amy Irving, have decided to divorce "in a spirit of caring," ending their three-year marriage, a spokesman for the couple said today.

The couple, who have a son named Max, have already made financial and property arrangements, but have yet to file court papers to end their marriage, spokesman Robert Friedman said in Los Angeles.
