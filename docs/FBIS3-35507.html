Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Hebron, there have been violent clashes between Jews and Arabs at the Tomb of the Patriarchs. An unspecified number of Arabs were reported to have been killed and there is a report that a Jew was hit. There were reports of shots at the scene, but details are still sketchy. The area has been declared a closed military zone. A resident of Har Hadar, north of Jerusalem, was seriously injured last night when stones were thrown at her car near Ramallah. She was taken by ambulance to Hadassah Hospital at 'En Kerem. The incident occurred when the car in which she was travelling encountered a stone roadblock near the village of Biddu. In a stone throwing incident near Tulkarm, two Israelis were lightly hurt when stones shattered the windshield of the Egged bus in which they were travelling. They were taken to hospital. Shots were fired at a vehicle bearing Israeli license plates near 'Imanu'el. No one was hurt and no damage was caused.
