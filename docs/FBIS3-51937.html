Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] National Defense Secretariat Communique No. 26 states that as of today, the Office of Complaints and Attention to the Citizenry [Oficina de Quejas y Atencion a la Ciudadania], which is under the Office of the Inspector and Comptroller General of the Army and Air Force, has been established in San Cristobal de Las Casas. The office will have representatives in Tuxtla Gutierrez, Oxchuc, Ocosingo, Altamirano, Las Margaritas, and Comitan, among other cities. The communique says the measure is being taken in order to see that the actions of military personnel toward the civilian population are aboveboard and as another guarantee of our strict respect for the civil rights of the aforementioned population.
