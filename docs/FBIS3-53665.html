Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 18 Jan (MENA)--Information Minister Safwat al- Sharif has returned to Cairo from Libya after a one-day visit to the Jamahiriyah. The information minister handed Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the Libyan revolution, a message from President Husni Mubarak on bilateral relations between the two countries, the most important current Arab and international affairs, and the Arab and regional situation. The message is part of the continuous consultations between the two leaders.
