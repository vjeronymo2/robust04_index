BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] President Rafael Caldera has admitted the existence of a destabilization plan, as Defense Minister Rafael Montero Revette had reported to the Congress. The president made this statement when he participated in the inauguration of the day of reflection on constitutional reforms held at the Catholic University amphitheater. Caldera indicated that measures have been taken on the alleged destabilization plan by the corresponding intelligence corps and an investigation has been opened on the case. With regard to the constitutional reforms, the president said that although this is not the solution to the nation's problems it is a condition for making the major changes the nation awaits.
