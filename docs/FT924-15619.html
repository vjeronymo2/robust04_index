921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Unit trust sales improve Unit trust business recovered slightly in August, before the sterling crisis, with a net inflow of Pounds 56m following net outflows in June and July. The Unit Trust Association interpreted the rise as showing that private investors' confidence in the UK stock market had recovered. The net inflow to UK Growth funds from private investors was Pounds 73m, while Pounds 59m came from institutions. Other UK sectors also recorded strong inflows.
