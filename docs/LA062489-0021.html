June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR A CHANGE, MAHORN TAKES A FEW SHOTS

Mike Downey's article on the Detroit Pistons' Rick Mahorn was so brutally vicious that I will certainly remember it for a long time.

Downey engaged in merciless butchery of the "Bad Boy," outright destruction of Mahorn's dignity.

There is only one thing that I can say to Downey after he so utterly attacked Mahorn, the Charles Manson of the basketball court . . .

Thank you.

STEVE LEVIN

West Los Angeles
