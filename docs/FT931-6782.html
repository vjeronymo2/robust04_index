930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: BBB Design reduces deficit to Pounds 165,000 BBB Design Group, which is involved in design, marketing and computer related activities, cut its pre-tax losses from Pounds 189,000 to Pounds 165,000 in the six months to October 31 1992. Turnover improved to Pounds 874,000 against Pounds 836,000. Mr Philip O'Donnell, chairman of the USM-quoted company, said the design, publishing and marketing operations had been profitable. He said the group would be looking towards an upturn in sales, particularly in the computer services division which had reported reduced losses during the first half. Losses per share increased to 1.99p (1.77p).
