May 15, 1989, Monday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

2 JAILED IN N. HOLLYWOOD SHOOTING

A man was shot about half a block from a North Hollywood police station, and officers arrested two men after the victim staggered into the station, Los Angeles police said today.

Jerome Prince, 32, was listed in fair condition in a local hospital, where he was being treated for a wound to his abdomen.

Investigators had not determined a motive for the shooting, which occurred shortly before midnight near the station on Tiara Street east of Lankershim Boulevard.

Police arrested Terrence Lee, 19, of San Bernardino and David Boone, 31, of Van Nuys on suspicion of attempted murder, Detective Mike Coffey said.
