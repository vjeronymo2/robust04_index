BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Apr (MENA)--An official source in the Arab League today denied a report by a weekly opposition paper that the State Security Detectives Service at Cairo International Airport detained Iraqi Justice Minister Shabib Lazim al-Maliki, who arrived in Cairo yesterday to attend the meetings of the Arab Justice Ministers Executive Bureau in the league secretariat. The source said that the Iraqi minister was received at the airport by the Arab League assistant secretary general. In a statement today, the source said that the assistant secretary general for legal affairs and Iraq's permanent delegate to the Arab League received the Iraqi minister in the VIP lounge and that they all left the airport without any problems shortly after the minister's arrival.
