Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] This is Ivica Saric reporting for Croatian radio from Vitez [passage omitted] The HVO [Croatian Defense Council] units have the initiative now and they are trying to retake control of the Vitez-Busovaca road. Fierce hand-to-hand combat is still going on. Muslim artillery attacks are continuing. More than 100 shells landed on Vitez by 1600 and over 200 shells of various caliber were fired on the defense lines. Five elderly people and one child were killed and 35 civilians were wounded, including many children. The defenders have also suffered casualties in heavy fighting. [passage omitted] As I am filing this report, dozens of shells are falling on Vitez and according to military sources, the Muslim army is bringing in fresh units and military equipment from Zenica so that new, even stronger, aggressor attacks are expected during the night or morning on this part of the front in the Lasva river valley.
