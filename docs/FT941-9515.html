940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / Portugal trims interest rates The Portuguese escudo was stable against the D-Mark after surprise cuts in the central bank's key money-market intervention rates yesterday, Reuter reports from Lisbon. The rate for injecting funds was cut to 10.125 per cent from 10.25 and the rate for absorbing liquidity to 9.125 per cent from 9.25.
