BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sharip Asuyev] [Text] Groznyy April 17 TASS -- Chechnya praised the Russian leadership for seeking a peaceful solution to the social and political crisis in the North Caucasian republic. Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev told TASS on Sunday that his analysts were thoroughly studying the decree by Russian President Boris Yeltsin, which instructs the Russian Government to hold necessary consultations with the Chechen side. The republic's government will gather in an extraordinary meeting tonight to make an official return statement by the Chechen leadership. The analysis of the current situation in Chechnya gives shows that the republican authorities are likely to search for a mutually acceptable solution to the Chechen problem [sentence as received]. Talks may stumble over demands that Chechnya recognize itself a subject of the Russian Federation, as well as over Russia's repeated appeals to forces in opposition to President Dudayev.
