920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / Observer: U-turn The skirmish in the normally friendly world of motor dealers is turning nasty. T Cowie, bidding Pounds 26m for Henlys, is going through the usual process of condemning Henlys' management, rubbishing its strategy and picking apart its businesses. So why, puzzles Robert Wood, Henlys' chief executive, if Henlys is such a dud outfit, did Gordon Hodgson, chief executive at Cowie, twice buy Henlys shares for his PEP last year and this? Hodgson is, by the way, showing a tidy profit on the shares.
