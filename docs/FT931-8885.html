930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Currency influences buoy TR Pacific The strength of Far Eastern currencies, especially after the decline in sterling, helped TR Pacific Investment Trust increase net asset value from 79.9p to 114.6p per share over 1992. The trust concentrates on capital growth. Net revenue dipped to Pounds 490,000 (Pounds 696,000) for earnings of 0.731p (1.039p) per share. A dividend of 0.35p is proposed for the year. The distribution for 1991 comprised a final of 0.25p and a special of 0.45p.
