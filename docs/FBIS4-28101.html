BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 7 KYODO -- The Export-Import Bank [Ex-Im Bank] of Japan said Tuesday [7 June] it has extended credit to Thailand's Start Petroleum Refining Co. for an amount not exceeding 334 million dollars. The Thai company will use the loan to purchase Japanese goods and services for building and operating an oil refinery in Map Ta Phu [place-name as received]. The International Finance Corp. and a syndicate of Japanese, American and European commercial banks will also provide credit for the project.
