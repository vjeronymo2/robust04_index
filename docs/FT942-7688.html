940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / International Company News: Property sell-off CSR, the Australian sugar and building products group, has announced plans to sell 31 surplus properties with an estimated value of more than ADollars 50m (USDollars 35.9m) through a sealed bid tender process, writes Bruce Jacques. CSR directors said the properties, to be sold through Kennedy-Wilson International, ranged from ADollars 100,000 for a small light industrial development in South Australia to more than ADollars 20m for a large site in Coomera, Queensland.
