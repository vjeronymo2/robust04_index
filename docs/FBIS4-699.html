BFN [Text] The speaker of the National Assembly, Dr. J.T. Kolane, this morning adjourned the house until tomorrow after he informed the members that some members of parliament had been abducted by armed soldiers. Dr. Kolane said he was of the opinion that the members could not concentrate on debates under these circumstances. The speaker said he was disappointed by these despicable acts. Although the speaker did not mention the names of the members concerned, it is believed that they are the deputy prime minister, the Honorable Selometsi Baholo; the honorable minister of justice, Mr. Kelebone Maope; the minister of trade and industry, the Honorable Shakhane Mokhehle; the minister of education, the Honorable Pakalitha Mosisili; and the minister of natural resources, the Honorable Monyane Moleleki. It is understood that the ministers were arrested at their homes and offices, but there have been no details of the circumstances of their arrests.
