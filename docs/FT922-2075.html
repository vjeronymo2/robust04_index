920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Mountview raises dividend by 20% Mountview Estates, owner and dealer in real estate, is raising its dividend by 20 per cent for the year to March 31, despite a 17.5 per cent drop in earnings. Turnover slipped to Pounds 13.7m (Pounds 14.1m), while the pre-tax profit declined from Pounds 8.07m to Pounds 6.56m after reduced interest charges of Pounds 662,000 (Pounds 913,000). The final dividend is 10p for a total of 18p (15p). Earnings came out at 95.8p (116.1p).
