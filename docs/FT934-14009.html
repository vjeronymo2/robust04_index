931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: McLeod Russel makes ADollars 3.4m sale McLeod Russel Holdings, the paint manufacturer and environmental engineering group, is selling the manufacturing operations of its Australian subsidiary Atlas Wright (Australia) to Leibert, an offshoot of Emmerson Corporation. The consideration, which equates to net asset value, amounts to ADollars 3.4m (Pounds 1.48m) of which ADollars 2.9m has already been paid. The proceeds will be used to repay bank borrowings. Atlas Wright is engaged in air conditioning service and manufacture.
