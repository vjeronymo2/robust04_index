BFN [Text] New Aspiration Party [NAP] leader Chawalit Yongchaiyut, admitting yesterday the current political situation is unstable, said it may be time for a political change. The Interior Minister said his NAP was expected to meet today to assess the situation and renew its role in the coalition. The NAP has always supported the coalition, he said, but the situation is fluid and so the NAP will discuss if the situation poses an obstacle to the party's continued participation in the coalition.
