Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent a birthday table to Kim Kyu-chol, a centenarian in Sonpong-up, Sonpong County. Upon receiving the birthday table imbued with loving care amid blessings of her family, relatives and villagers, the 100-year-old woman said she had wholeheartedly felt through her life how good her country was. "Because the dear leader pursues good politics for the people, I have been able to live 100 years and enjoy happiness today," she said. In the DPRK, the number of macrobians is on a steady increase under the popular policies of the state. The average life expectancy at birth is 74.5 years in Korea.
