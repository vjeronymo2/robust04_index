931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Chrysalis Japanese sale Chrysalis, the music and media group, has reached final agreement to sell a 25 per cent stake in its new record label, The Echo Label, to Pony Canyon, which is the music division of Fujisankei, the Japanese media and communications company, for Dollars 17.5m (Pounds 11.7m). The directors stated that following further share subscriptions, Chrysalis, Mr Steve Lewis, who is the chief executive of the group's music division, and Pony Canyon will respectively own 56.25 per cent, 18.75 per cent and 25 per cent of Echo.
