May 6, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SPORTS RUNDOWN ON WEEKEND TV

Basketball playoffs? Baseball games? For the avid horse racing fan, there is but one big event this weekend -- the Kentucky Derby.

ABC picks up the first leg in racing's Triple Crown starting today at 1:30 p.m. (7)(3)(10)(42).

Meanwhile, the NBA playoffs can be found on CBS. Golden State plays Phoenix today at 12:30 p.m., with two other games airing Sunday at 10 a.m. and 12:30 p.m. on Channels 2 and 8.

In baseball, the California Angels and Toronto Blue Jays take the field Sunday at 10:30 a.m. (5).
