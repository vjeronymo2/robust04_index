November 27, 1989, Monday, Valley Edition

QUESTIONS ON ROCKWELL SHIPMENT

Myron Levin's article on the U.S. probe of Rockwell's shipping of "hot" materials brings up some questions:

Is Rockwell's non-"proper compliance with procedural requirements" resulting in a box emitting radioactivity 2 1/2 times the permitted level an isolated incident?

What about the effect on the health of the driver who was constantly exposed during the trip?

Were the state police and the members of the emergency response team who escorted the truck after the accident overexposed?

What about the workers who handled the box at Rocketdyne?

SOL LONDE

Northridge
