Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Jan.07 (MAP) - A meeting was held Thursday here on the final pact of the world multilateral commercial negotiations of the Uruguay round adopted last December in Geneva. The meeting, attended by Moroccan Foreign Trade Minister Mourad Cherif, Secretary of State to Foreign Affairs Taieb Fassi el-Fehri, top executives from various departments, managers and businessmen, stressed the importance of the conclusion of the final pact of the Uruguay round in the liberalization of the international commerce and world economy. The signing ceremony of the pact due in Morocco as well as the kingdom's role in the world trade negotiations, GATT's regulations and the role of the pact in promoting investments and world trade were also reviewed during the meeting.
