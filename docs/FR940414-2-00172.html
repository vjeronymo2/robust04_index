12. Members Up to thirty Advisory Committee members shall be appointed by the President for a term of one year, which may be extended by the President. Committee members shall not be compensated for service on the Committee. Membership shall be fairly balanced in terms of points of view represented. 13. Chairperson and Executive Director The facilitator serving the Advisory Committee shall serve as Chairperson. A member or representative of the steering committee shall serve as Executive Director.
