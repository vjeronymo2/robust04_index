Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Muslim Government of Bosnia-Herzegovina hopes that more members of the Nonaligned Movement [NAM] will support Bosnia-Herzegovina in its struggle against Serbian and Croatian aggressors. Bosnian Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic expressed this hope before reporters after calling on President Suharto in Jakarta. He was accompanied by Foreign Minister Ali Alatas during the meeting. The Bosnian prime minister briefed the NAM chairman on the latest situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina. According to Haris Silajdzic, Bosnia-Herzegovina hopes that the NAM members will continue to extend diplomatic support to Bosnia-Herzegovina in the United Nations. Bosnia-Herzegovina is also hoping for humanitarian aid from the international community. The Bosnian guest, who arrived in Jakarta yesterday, today received an assistance fund from the National Committee for Solidarity with Bosnia-Herzegovina. The fund was handed over by Probosutejo, the committee chairman.
