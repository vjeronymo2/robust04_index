910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / Observer: 'Bed' bugged Britain's biggest bed-maker Silentnight Holdings is evidently nodding off a bit after recovering from its disastrous distribution deal with failed retailer Lowndes Queensway. Silentnight's official Stock Exchange announcement boasts that while 'many Queensway suppliers incurred sizeable bed debts, no bad debt was incurred by our group.'
