931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / Ford appoints Byrne FORD of Britain, which has suffered three years of heavy losses and a steady erosion of its share of the UK market for new cars, yesterday appointed Mr Paddy Byrne as sales director, Kevin Done writes. The move follows the appointment of Mr Ernie Thompson as chief executive of the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders. Mr Byrne, who has been Ford of Europe's director of East European sales and export operations for two years, was previously chief executive of the troubled Waterford Wedgwood glassware and china group.
