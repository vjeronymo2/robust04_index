June 1, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff has been asked to explain 76 allegations lodged against the school's football program by college football coaches, according to a Pine Bluff newspaper.

The allegations involved player eligibility, playing non-registered players and improper transfers of students from other schools. The article said the complaints were lodged by coaches, both in and out of the Arkansas Intercollegiate Conference.

Archie Cooley, UAPB head football coach and athletic director, denied the allegations.
