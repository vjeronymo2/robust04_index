931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / Kawasaki buys Devon factory Kawasaki Heavy Industries of Japan is buying the Staffa Motor plant at Plymouth, Devon, to establish a European manufacturing base. Staffa Motor is part of the Vickers Systems Division of Trinova, the US power controls company. The plant, which has 180 employees, makes hydraulic radial piston motors for use in winches and the mining industry. Kawasaki will retain Staffa's workforce and management but appoint a managing director and some other senior staff.
