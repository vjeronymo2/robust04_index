940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / Auschwitz lie to be nailed Leaders of Germany's governing coalition agreed yesterday to back legislation making it a criminal act to claim publicly that the Nazis' mass murder of Jews is a historical lie. Leaders of Chancellor Helmut Kohl's conservative coalition said they expect passage of the law next Friday by parliament's lower house, the Bundestag. Disseminating the so-called 'Auschwitz Lie' would be punishable on conviction by up to three years in prison.
