BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov and Gennadiy Kulbitskiy] [Text] Bonn 28 April TASS -- The Russian foreign minister, Andrey Kozyrev, arrived in Bonn for a flying visit on Thursday. He will have a consultative meeting with the federal minister of foreign affairs of federal Germany, Klaus Kinkel in the Petersberg governmental residence. The key topics of the political consultations of the two foreign ministers will be questions pertaining to the preparation for a forthcoming official visit to Federal Germany by the president of the Russian Federation, Boris Yeltsin, scheduled for 11 May through 13 this year, as well as the search for the settlement of the Bosnian crisis.
