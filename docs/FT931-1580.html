930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Fine for breach of copyright A FORMER cinema manager was yesterday convicted of breach of copyright after showing the film A Clockwork Orange. Ms Jane Giles, 28, a former programme manager at London's Scala cinema, was conditionally discharged and ordered to pay Pounds 1,000 towards Pounds 3,300 prosecution costs. The case was brought by the Federation Against Copyright Theft.
