Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, March 9 -- Democratic Left Alliance Deputy (SLD) Andrzej Lipski declared during a Wednesday meeting of the SLD parliamentary floor group that the group of deputies who lost membership in the SLD caucus after a Saturday vote against the 1994 budget, will not join the opposition and will continue to support the SLD programme. Four SLD members: Piotr Ikonowicz, Andrzej Lipski, Cezary Mizejewski (Polish Socialist Party -- PPS) and Stanislaw Wisniewski (All-Poland alliance of Trade Unions -- OPZZ) were expelled from the caucus after they violated the caucus discipline in the budget vote. "I want to stress clearly that we are not forming an opposition. we will create the PPS circle, but we will still be supporting these activities of the cabinet, which will aim at the implementation of the SLD programme," said Lipski.
