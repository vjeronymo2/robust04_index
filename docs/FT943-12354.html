940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / Linklater quits as Scotsman editor Mr Magnus Linklater is to stand down as editor of The Scotsman newspaper to pursue other interests, it was announced yesterday. He will step down on July 31 but there was no immediate indication who his successor might be. Mr Linklater said: 'I have enjoyed the privilege of editing one of the great newspapers in Britain and hand it over in the expectation that its role as a national newspaper, speaking for all of Scotland, will be further strengthened.'
