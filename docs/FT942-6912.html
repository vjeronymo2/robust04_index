940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Govett High Income revenue at Pounds 1.67m Govett High Income Investment Trust reported net revenue of Pounds 1.67m in its first five months of trading to April 30. Earnings per share were 3.49p and an interim dividend of 2.08p is declared. In future dividends will be paid quarterly with two more payments for the 11 months to end-October. Net asset value at April 30 was 89.38p or 91.15p on a fully diluted basis.
