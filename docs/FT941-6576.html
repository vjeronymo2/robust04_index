940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: IDV International Distillers & Vintners, the drinks sector of Grand Metropolitan, has established its own importing and distribution companies in Poland and the Czech Republic. IDV Poland has been established in Warsaw to serve a vodka market almost as large as that of the US, with annual sales of an estimated 35m cases a year. IDV Czech Republic has been established in Prague and will also serve neighbouring Slovakia.
