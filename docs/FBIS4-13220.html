BFN [Text] A communique of the security services mentioned that the terrorist Sayah Atia died last February following injuries he sustained during a clash with the security forces. This was confirmed by terrorists close to Atia who were arrested by the security services. The source pointed out that Sayah Atia, alias El Khan, who carried out several assassinations, was seriously injured by security forces during a manhunt in the Medea province on 13 February 1994. The security services announced that their forces were able to kill four terrorists and arrest 17 others between last Sunday and Thursday in Jijel, Blida, Boumerdes, Tiaret, and Bouira. The security forces recovered a Mat 49 pistol stolen during an aggression carried out in Lakhdaria in February 1992. On the other hand, the security services pointed out that three out of the four terrorists who were killed in Mila province on 5 April were identified as being those who escaped from the re-education prison of Tazoult on 10 March 1994.
