920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Chinese shares well received THE first public offering to foreigners of shares in a Chinese company was about four times over-subscribed after a three-day placement, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. Some 800,000 Shanghai Vacuum Electronic Devices shares were underwritten by SBCI Finance Asia, Salomon Brothers and Sung Hung Kai with 200,000 shares handled by Shanghai underwriters. The shares, with face value of Yn100 (Dollars 18.5), were offered at Yn420 each, to raise Yn420m (Dollars 78m).
