930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: First half fall hits Real Time shares Shares of Real Time Control fell 15p to 76p yesterday on news that profits for the half year to September 30 had fallen from Pounds 507,000 to Pounds 258,000 at the pre-tax level. Turnover was virtually static at Pounds 3.1m. The USM-quoted group develops and services computer systems. Earnings per share were 2.5p (4.9p). The directors said the results in the UK epos market reflected the high cost of obtaining business in the recessionary period.
