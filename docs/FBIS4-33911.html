BFN [Text] Dr. Gustavo Sierra, who helped discover the meniningitis-B vaccine, has said that Cuban research into an AIDS vaccine is progressing. Sierra told Latin American delegates to the Second Infectious Diseases Seminar that many researchers believe such a vaccine will be found by the end of the century, while others believe it will be found much sooner. Sierra went on to say: We are sure of one thing. If we are not among the first, we will not be the last and we will not give up.
