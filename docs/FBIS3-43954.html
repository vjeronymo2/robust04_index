Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Afonso Dhlakama, president of the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo], met at his Maputo residence yesterday with Richard Edis, British ambassador to Mozambique. The meeting dealt with the Mozambican peace process, particularly the training of government and Renamo instructors at Nyanga, Zimbabwe, as part of the formation of the future Mozambican army. The Renamo president and the British ambassador also reviewed the confinement of Renamo and government forces.
