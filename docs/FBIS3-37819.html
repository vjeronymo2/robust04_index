Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 22 Feb (INA)--An Iraqi delegation led by Foreign Ministry Undersecretary Riyad al-Qaysi held two rounds of talks last night with Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Kolokolov at the Russian Foreign Ministry. Al-Qaysi told an INA correspondent in Moscow that he briefed the Russian side on Iraq's talks with the Special Commission, the UN Security Council members, and the International Atomic Energy Agency, and on Iraq's implementation of its commitments as stipulated by the UN Security Council resolutions, particularly Resolution 715 on future monitoring. He indicated that the two sides also discussed the subject of developing bilateral relations, particularly in the political and economic fields. They agreed to continue dialogue and consultation, in addition to discussing methods of bilateral cooperation within the framework of the Russian movement in the UN Security Council. Al-Qaysi stressed that the atmosphere of the talks was extremely cordial.
