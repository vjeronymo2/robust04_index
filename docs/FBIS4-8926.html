BFN [Text] Banja Luka, 19 Apr (TANJUG) -- Muslim and Croatian forces resumed attacks around 1600 today against the Serbian positions around Brcko, the information service of the republic of Srpska army's First Krajina Corps says in a statement issued in Banja Luka. The attack by the Muslim forces was launched this morning from the direction of Vranovaca hill, 8 kms south of Brcko, and the suburb of Boderiste, south of Brcko. At the same time, Croatian Army forces, using the territory of the Republic of Croatia, together with the members of the Croatian Defense Council, fired 24 rockets from multiple rocket launchers and several artillery shells on the same Serbian positions. Two Serbian soldiers were seriously wounded in these attacks, and several residential buildings were damaged. Today's attacks are a part of the intensified offensive activities by the Muslim and Croatian forces in Posavina and part of the big spring offensive by the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, which is frequently joined by Croatian Defense Council units. The attacks are aimed at cutting the road links between the two Serb krajinas and Semberija and Yugoslavia.
