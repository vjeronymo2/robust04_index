940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Iraqis sentenced to death in Kuwait over Bush plot A Kuwaiti court on Saturday sentenced five Iraqis and one Kuwaiti to death for plotting to kill former US president George Bush when he visited the emirate in 1993, Reuter reports from Kuwait. Another five Iraqis and two Kuwaitis were sentenced to prison terms ranging from six months to 12 years for offences including possession of explosives, liquor smuggling and illegal entry. One Kuwaiti was acquitted. The death sentences are subject to review by another court and by the ruler of Kuwait, the emir. During the last session in April of the trial, which opened nearly a year ago, defence lawyers said the testimony of the sole prosecution witness, state security Colonel Abdul Samad al-Shatti, was based on assumptions.
