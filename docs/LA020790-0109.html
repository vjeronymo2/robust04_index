February 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The sentencing of John L. Molinaro and Donald P. Mangano Sr., convicted of more than 30 counts of bank fraud and conspiracy concerning now-defunct Ramona Savings & Loan in Santa Ana, was delayed Monday and rescheduled for Feb. 13 . . . Virgin Atlantic Airways announced that it is introducing completely nonsmoking flights between New York and London starting May 18.
