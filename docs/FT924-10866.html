921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / Freeze on mail costs continues FIRST AND second class mail costs are to be pegged at least until the end of March, the Post Office announced. The last increase was in September last year when first class stamps rose 2p to 24p and second class 1p to 18p. Earlier this year the Royal Mail pledged to freeze prices for three months until the end of the year. The Post Office disclosed 'record' reliability results for April to September this year, showing that 91.5 per cent of first class mail reached its destination the day after posting.
