940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Alexon slashes pre-tax losses Alexon, the womenswear group, reduced pre-tax losses in the 26 weeks to July 30 from Pounds 9.98m to Pounds 2.21m as the corrective measures initiated by Mr John Osborn, brought in as chief executive after the departure of the previous management in April 1993, began to have an effect. At the operating level and before exceptionals, losses were cut to Pounds 1.86m (Pounds 4.71m). Mr Mike Jones, finance director, said the Dash brand had been brought back to 'a more profitable core' and the badly performing Dash concessions had been closed.
