940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / International Company News: Gustav & Grete to sell interest in brewery Gustav & Grete Schickedanz Holding is selling its 97 per cent interest in brewer Patrizier Braeu and raising stakes in three Quelle-group insurance companies, Reuter reports from Nuremberg. Schickedanz will raise its share of Quelle Lebensversicherung and Quelle Sachversicherung to 70 per cent from 50 per cent, and its interest in Quelle Krankenversicherung to 100 per cent from 74 per cent. Schickedanz did not disclose the terms of the transaction. It was recently announced that Schickedanz was negotiating to sell its VP-Schickedanz tissue-paper unit to Procter & Gamble, the big US detergents group. This deal is reportedly facing opposition from the European Commission.
