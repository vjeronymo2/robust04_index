Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] On the sidelines of the Arab League Council meetings, Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' met with Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz this afternoon. Talks dealt with developments in the peace process and with several issues pertaining to coordination and cooperation between the two fraternal countries. They also exchanged views on the topics listed on the agenda of the Arab League Council. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' also met with Sudanese Foreign Minister Dr. Husayn Abu-Salih this afternoon. Talks focused on the situation in southern Sudan. The Sudanese foreign minister expressed Sudan's appreciation for Syria's positions supporting the unity of the Sudanese territory and people.
