Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Jordanian Transport Minister Adib Halasah has affirmed that the Iraqi people's steadfastness under leader President Saddam Husayn in the face of the treacherous 30-state aggression has thwarted the imperialist-Zionist schemes to undermine Iraq's sovereignty and national unity. During a meeting with the Babil deputy governor today, Halasah indicated that stepped-up hostile conspiracies against the march of great Iraq were the inevitable outcome of the growth of Iraq's pan- Arab role in the efforts to rehabilitate the Arab nation's values and principles and confront external ambitions in the region. The Jordanian minister visited the historical city of Babylon and acquainted himself with its archaeological and historical sites.
