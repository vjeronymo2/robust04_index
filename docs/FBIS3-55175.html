Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Information that an explosive device has been planted in the White House, where the Russian Government is to have its premises and where Prime Minister Chernomyrdin and some of his deputies have already moved in, and that an explosion there was imminent turned out to be false, INTERFAX has learned from (Litvinov), duty officer at Moscow's Main Internal Affairs Directorate. At the same time, he said, operational measures to find the person who made the anonymous telephone call concerning the imminent explosion are continuing. Such anonymous calls can be regarded as malicious hooliganism and can entail imprisonment between one and five years in accordance with the criminal code. The spokesman said that the caller, who telephoned at around 1730 on Tuesday, was a woman.
