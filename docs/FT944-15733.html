941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Scholey retires from BT board Sir David Scholey, who steps down as chairman of SG Warburg next year, has retired as a non-executive director of British Telecommunications. Sir David, 59, joined the board of BT in 1985 and is leaving on the completion of his third term of office. Sir Iain Vallance, BT chairman, paid warm tribute to Sir David's work for the company during the most tempestuous period in its existence. Sir David said he was leaving when BT was 'acknowledged as a global market leader within the telecommunications industry'.
