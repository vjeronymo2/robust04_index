March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MITCH GREEN COMMENTS AREN'T FIT TO PRINT

I cannot find one good reason that Mitch (Blood) Green continues to attract media coverage. Mr. Green is not news. Mr. Green is not a heavyweight contender. He's not even in the top 10. Mr. Green is unintelligible. Mr. Green is not funny.

Maybe it's just a subconscious effort to rid boxing of all undesirables by showing how utterly unsavory these kinds of chaps truly are.

RODNEY BOSWELL

Los Angeles
