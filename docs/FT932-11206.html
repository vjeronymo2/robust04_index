930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / International Company News: USAir offering raises Dollars 231m USAir, the US carrier in which British Airways invested Dollars 300m for a 19.9 per cent voting interest earlier this year, has raised a net Dollars 231m from its sale of new ordinary shares at Dollars 20.75 per share. The underwriters exercised their over- allotment options and a total 11.5m new shares were sold. In addition to the money from the offering USAir will receive another Dollars 99.2m from British Airways - bringing the total raised to around Dollars 330m.
