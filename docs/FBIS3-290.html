Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Abuja, 3 Mar (AFP) -- A source close to the Nigerian presidency stated that Nigerian President Sani Abacha discussed the border dispute between his country and Cameroon with Togolese counterpart Gnassingbe Eyadema today. The source did not specify the content of the discussion, but indicated that the meeting -- which took place in the presidential lounge of the Abuja International Airport -- was "frank and cordial." The Togolese head of state held talks earlier with Cameroonian President Paul Biya.
