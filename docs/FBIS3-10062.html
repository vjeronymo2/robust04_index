Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Transport Ministry Margarida Coimbra today submitted a note of resignation to President Itamar Franco, but she has not yet received a reply to her request. Coimbra is being pressured to leave her post. Last week the newspaper O GLOBO reported that Coimbra's husband, Carlos Henrique Siqueira, had tried to use his influence to obtain a release of funds for construction work on the Rio de Janeiro-Niteroi bridge. The work is being carried out by the Noronhe Engineering Company, for which Siqueira works.
