Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Reports from occupied Kashmir speak of widespread demonstrations and strikes against the Indian military siege of Dargah Hazratbal [shrine] to prevent the devotees from ziyarat [paying respect to] of mue-i-mubarak [holy hair] on the occasion of Merajunnabi [anniversary of prophet Mohammad's ascension to heaven]. The demonstrations were particularly violent in Srinagar, Baramula, Kupwara, Anantnag-Islamabad, Sopor, Badgam, and Pulwama. The demonstrators raised slogans and demanded complete withdrawal of Indian troops from around the dargah. Meanwhile, repressive siege and search operations by the occupation forces continue unabated all across the valley. The mojahedin in retaliation attacked the Indian troops and killed three of them. Three mojahedin were martyred in a clash near Badgam. Three bullet-riddled bodies suspected to be killed by the Indian troops have been recovered in Kupwara.
