BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 21/4/1994 -- A delegation of the Socialist Party of Labor of Romania [PSM], headed by President Ilie Verdet, met on 20 April evening with a delegation of the Palestine Liberation Organization, headed by Yasir 'Arafat, PLO Executive Committee president, now in Romania for the Crans Montana forum proceedings. The Palestinian party noted during the talks the traditional relations between Romanians and Palestinians, the fact that the Palestinian movement has always enjoyed full support, as regarded the right to self-determination of the Palestinian people and the negotiated settlement of the issue. President Yasir 'Arafat said he felt encouraged by that meeting with the Romanian socialists. The Socialist Party of Labor's leadership emphasized the possibility of expanding the relations between the two parties on multiple planes and the economic relations as well, to the benefit of the Romanian and Palestinian peoples.
