October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GUBERNATORIAL CANDIDATES DEBATE ISSUES

Obviously, Feinstein and Wilson lack the leadership to make hard choices as evidenced by their responses to your Commentary debate issue, "Given $20 Million for Drug Enforcement or Treatment, I'd Choose . . ." (Oct. 8).

I, too, will make the easy choices and vote for neither of these invertebrates.

DON WILLIAMS

South Pasadena
