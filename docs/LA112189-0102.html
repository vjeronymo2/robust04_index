November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: KENFIL SIGNS CONTRACT FOR AUSTRALIAN DISTRIBUTION

Kenfil Distribution, a Van Nuys-based distributor of computer software, said it signed an agreement with the Australian company Algar Burns to have its software distributed in Australia.

Earlier this year, the privately held Kenfil entered the European market by acquiring Philipson's Products BV, a Dutch software distributor, and it entered the Asian market by purchasing the distributor Asiasoft in Hong Kong.
