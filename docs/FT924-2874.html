921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Increased export sales boost Titon A further significant increase in annual profits was announced by Titon Holdings, a maker of window fittings and accessories. For the year ended September 30 sales rose by 14 per cent to Pounds 10.8m while pre-tax profits surged 28 per cent to Pounds 1.94m. Earnings per share rose to 11.69p (9.31p). A final dividend of 2.44p, accompanied by an anniversary bonus of 0.13p, makes a 3.7p (3.25p) total.
