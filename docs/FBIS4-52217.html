BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 6 (KCNA) -- Polish Ambassador to Korea Ryszard Baturo hosted a film show and a cocktail party at his embassy Thursday evening on the occasion of the national holiday of the Polish people. Present on invitation were vice-chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries Kim Chin-pom and other officials concerned. The participants saw a Polish documentary film. Then followed a cocktail party.
