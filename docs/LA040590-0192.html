April 5, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

LYLES SPARKS ORANGE COAST IN VOLLEYBALL

Matt Lyles had 11 kills to lead Orange Coast College to a 15-4, 15-4, 15-5, victory over El Camino in a South Coast Conference volleyball match Wednesday at Orange Coast.

Al Vistaunet added 10 kills for Orange Coast, which leads the conference with a 15-1 record.

In another South Coast Conference match:

Golden West 3, Long Beach 2 -- Eric Zeno had 20 kills to lead Golden West to an 11-15, 11-15, 15-5, 15-4, 15-12 victory.

In Orange Empire softball:

Fullerton 2, Orange Coast 0 -- Angelique Romo (1-3) allowed seven hits while shutting out host Orange Coast (22-7, 8-2). Fullerton is 6-21, 5-7.
