940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: Police fired on during loyalist riots in Belfast Police were fired on and several vehicles, including a bus, were hijacked as hundreds of masked loyalist hardliners were reported to have taken part in overnight riots in Belfast. Troops and police reinforcements were drafted in during the riots, the most severe in Northern Ireland this year. The violence was believed to be linked to recent arrests over loyalist paramilitary terrorism. There were no confirmed reports of serious injuries or deaths.
