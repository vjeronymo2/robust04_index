920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / International Company News: Coca-Cola to buy back shares SHARES in Coca-Cola, the soft drinks company, bucked a sagging stock market yesterday, after the Atlanta-based group announced a new 100m share repurchase programme, through to the year 2000. The news lifted Coca-Cola shares Dollars 1/2 to Dollars 41 3/8 , despite 40-point drop in the Dow Jones Industrial Average. But in late trading, the market had recovered some ground, while Coca-Cola had eased Dollars 1/8 to Dollars 41.
