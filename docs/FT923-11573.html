920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / Abu Nidal aide assassinated Walid Khaled, the spokesman for Abu Nidal's Fatah Revolutionary Council (FRC), the most extreme Palestinian group, was assassinated by gunmen in Beirut yesterday, writes Lara Marlowe. Khaled, whose real name is unknown, publicly negotiated the release of seven French and Belgian citizens kidnapped in the Mediterranean from the ship 'Silco' in the late 1980s.
