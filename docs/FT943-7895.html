940820 FT 20 AUG 94 / ICI to restructure explosives division Imperial Chemical Industries, the UK's largest chemicals group, yesterday announced it was restructuring its UK explosives operations. It blamed a decline in demand for the division's products on the coal industry. ICI said the restructuring would involve job losses but it did not give details. The division employs about 750 people at Ardeer, Strathclyde; 125 at Wigan and north Wales, and about 20 technical staff.
