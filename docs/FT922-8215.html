920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Needler reduces first quarter loss Needler Group, a Canadian building materials combine quoted on the USM, reduced its seasonal pre-loss to CDollars 2.93m (Pounds 1.38m) for the first quarter of 1992. In the comparative three months the group incurred a deficit of CDollars 3.48m, but by the end of 1991 had turned in a profit of CDollars 1.74m. Losses per share for the quarter were 10.4 cents (12.5 cents). The group produces aggregates, asphalt, concrete block and paving stone, and operates chiefly in the Province of Ontario and New York State.
