Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn has expressed extreme pain over and denunciation of this crime. In a statement to journalists following Friday prayers, his majesty said: [Begin recording] I am deeply pained over the heinous crime which was perpetrated at the Ibrahimi Mosque today. We denounce and condemn this crime. Our hearts and souls are with our people and brothers in the occupied territories and Hebron. We beseech God to be with them under these difficult and hard circumstances, and to have mercy on the souls of martyrs. Concerning the incident, I reiterate my denunciation of and my extreme suffering over it. My feelings about this incident are the same as the feelings of all the believers in God and all human beings who enjoy human characteristics. We beseech Almighty God that this will mark the end of acts of violence whatever and wherever they maybe in this world. [end recording]
