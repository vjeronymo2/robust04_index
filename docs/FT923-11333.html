920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Thornton Pan European assets dip Net asset value per 10p ordinary share of Thornton Pan-European Investment Trust slipped from 32.8p to 30.3p over the 12 months ended June 30. Net revenue for the first half of 1992 fell to Pounds 74,339 (Pounds 125,235) after tax of Pounds 28,221 (Pounds 42,233). Earnings worked through at 0.37p (0.63p).
