Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] On Saturday [1 January] evening, the State Security Intelligence Department arrested Salah al-Hakash, secretary of the Nasirite Party in al-Daqahliyah Governorate, on charges of distributing leaflets inciting hatred of the regime. Al-Hakash was referred to a prosecutor, who decided to keep him in custody for two weeks. Meanwhile, Ahmad 'Abd-al-Wahhab, a member of the Nasirite Party committee in al-Daqahliyah, continues to be detained on charges of inciting the students of al-Mansurah University to demonstrate to protest the semester system.
