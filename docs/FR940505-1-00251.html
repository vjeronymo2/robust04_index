Procedure F.2 in appendix B of 40 CFR 52.741 is used to obtain F B . (C) Recordkeeping and Reporting. ( 1 ) All affected facilities must maintain a copy of the capture efficiency protocol submitted to EPA on file. All results of the appropriate test methods and capture efficiency protocols must be reported to EPA within sixty (60) days of the test date. A copy of the results must be kept on file with the source for a period of five (5) years. ( 2 ) If any changes are made to capture equipment, then affected facilities must notify EPA of these changes and a new test may be required by EPA. ( 3 ) All affected facilities must notify the Administrator 30 days prior to performing any capture efficiency test. At that time, the source must notify the Administrator which capture efficiency protocol will be used. ( 4 ) All affected facilities utilizing a PTE must demonstrate that this enclosure meets the requirement given in Procedure T in appendix B of 40 CFR 52.741 at all times. ( 5 ) All affected facilities utilizing a TTE must demonstrate that their TTE meets the requirements given in Procedure T in appendix B of 40 CFR 52.741 for a TTE during testing of their control device. The source must also provide documentation that the quality assurance criteria for a TTE have been achieved.
