June 5, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Stay Up Some More With Jay and Dave: Night-owl TV fans who tune in talk-show host David Letterman and fill-in funnyman Jay Leno have years to enjoy more wise-cracking monologues, witty interviews and stupid pet tricks. NBC said Monday it has signed Letterman and Leno, the sole guest host on "The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson," to new multiyear contracts. No further details were disclosed. GREG BRAXTON
