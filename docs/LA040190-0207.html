April 1, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

WRONG CROWD COUNT

Regarding the letter (March 20) on "Meet Your Judges Night," the judges of North Orange County Municipal Court want to correct a remark that only about 100 people showed up: We had more than 200 people in our Spanish-speaking courtroom and more than 200 in our English-speaking courtroom, for a total of more than 400 people.

The judges of this court were very much pleased that so many people came. We have been asked by Loara High School to send a panel of our judges so that those young people who could not attend March 6 could ask us questions.

BETTY L. ELIAS, Chairperson
