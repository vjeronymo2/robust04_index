Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Top 10 Macedonian Politicians"] [Text] The agency for the research of pubic opinion BRI-MA, which cooperates with the renowned GALLUP, has conducted a research of the rating of the Macedonian politicians, in Macedonia. It was concluded that the list of the ten most trusted politicians is head by Kiro Gligorov with 70 percent. The second place belongs to Vasil Tupurkovski, who also notes a high rate of trust, with 50 percent of the people polled. The third position is occupied by the minister of the interior, Ljubomir Frckovski, with 50 percent, followed by Stojan Andov with 48 percent, and Prime Minister Branko Crvenkovski with 46 percent of the votes. Sixth on the top-list is the minister of defense, Vlado Popovski, seventh Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski, eighth Petar Gosev, ninth Todor Petrov, and 10th Nikola Popovski.
