June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: A MIRACLE, A UNIVERSE SETTLING ACCOUNTS WITH TORTURERS BY LAWRENCE WESCHLER (PANTHEON: $22.95; 300 PP.;)

" . . . brilliantly depicts how in the face of extreme repression, there were courageous individuals who pointed the way back to freedom. Weschler demonstrates the importance of truth-telling for that journey and for the healing of whole societies." Margaret Crahan
