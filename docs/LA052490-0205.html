May 24, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Eric Sigman has been named vice president of planning and development for Ingram Micro D, a Santa Ana distributor of microcomputer products.

In his new position, he will assist in developing strategic and financial plans, and he will supervise the market research department. He joined the company in 1986 as director of corporate planning. Before that, Sigman worked in the planning department of Times Mirror Corp. and in the finance department of Carter Hawley Hale Stores.
