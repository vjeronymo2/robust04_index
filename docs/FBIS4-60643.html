BFN [Text] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, has received a cable of congratulations on the fourth anniversary of the Republic of Yemen's National Day from South Africa's President Nelson Mandela. The president sent a cable of thanks in reply. The brother Presidential Council chairman also received two cables of congratulations on the fourth anniversary of the Republic of Yemen's National Day from Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani and Eritrean President Issaias Afeworke. The brother Presidential Council chairman sent cables of thanks in reply.
