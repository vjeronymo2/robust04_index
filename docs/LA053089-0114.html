May 30, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

EVANS NAMED HEAD OF UNITED ARTISTS

Qintex Entertainment Inc. said today it named David Evans president and chief executive officer of United Artists Corp.

Qintex said the appointment is effective immediately and will be formalized after the completion this summer of its acquisition of United Artists.

Qintex agreed on March 31 to acquire United Artists for about $1 billion.

Evans was previously president and CEO of Qintex.
