BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Security forces have captured 26 persons after determining that they caused an outbreak during an unauthorized demonstration in Ankara yesterday condemning the intense Serbian attack in Bosnia-Herzegovina. According to a statement issued by the Ankara Governor's Office, it was determined that these persons provoked the crowd, and caused the stoning of the UN representation, the U.S. Embassy, the TRT General Directorate building, and the True Path Party headquarters. Noting that these persons were captured with Molotov cocktails, hammers, and assault knives in their possession, the statement adds that an investigation into the incident is under way. On the other hand, an inquiry was started in Istanbul into the unauthorized demonstration condemning Serbian aggression which was held in Taksim. The Prosecutor's Office opened an investigation into two private television channels who aired the demonstration held in Taksim. Officials of the Bakirkoy and Kucukcekmece Chief Public Prosecutor's Office stated that they placed a request to TGRT [TURKIYE Radio and Television] and Inter Star for the videotapes of the demonstration. The officials stated that, if necessary, the concerned parties of the two private channels may be taken to court on charges of provoking and encouraging the public to commit crimes. The authorities further stated that the slogans and placards used during the demonstration in Istanbul's Taksim Square are being analyzed and if found offensive necessary legal steps will be taken.
