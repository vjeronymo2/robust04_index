BFN [Text] The foreign minister of Libya, Mr. 'Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir, called on the president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, and discussed matters of mutual interest. Mr. Muntasir reiterated his country's support for the just cause of Kashmir and said over the years his country had supported various Muslim causes like Palestine, Kashmir, and Bosnia as a matter of policy. It was agreed that the two countries would endeavor to cement their existing cordial relations, especially through the joint commission. The Libyan minister also conveyed to the president warm greetings from the Libyan leader Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. The Libyan foreign minister also called on the prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, and assured that his country would support Pakistan's stand on Kashmir issue at all fora. The prime minister briefed the Libyan minister on the human rights violations in occupied Kashmir. She assured Libya of Pakistan's support for an amicable solution to the issue of UN sanctions against Libya. The Libyan minister was also assured that Pakistan's permanent representative at the United Nations will once again raise the question of Libyan hajj flights with the sanctions committee. Mr. Muntasir proposed that the meeting of the Joint Ministerial Commission should be convened at an early date to settle outstanding bilateral economic issues and to explore new avenues for cooperation.
