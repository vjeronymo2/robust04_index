921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / World Commoditie Prices: Spices Activity in spices has been rather quiet in the run up to a meeting at the end of next week of the International Produce Association of London and an international spice confernece in Goa, India, reports Man Producten. The spot price for Seychelles cinnamon was Dollars 1,500 a tonne, with the shipment price at Dollars 1,300 a tonne. Cloves prices were a little firmer; spot at Dollars 1,050 a tonne and shipment at Dollars 790-Dollars 800 a tonne, cif. Nigerian ginger was offered at between Dollars 300 and Dollars 600 a tonne, ex-warehouse, depending on quality. Mexican pimento was quoted at Dollars 1,700 a tonne, spot.
