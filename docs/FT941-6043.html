940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Courtaulds acquisition Courtaulds Textiles has completed the Pounds 45.4m acquisition of Hartstone Group's French sock and UK hosiery businesses. Hartstone is to allow Courtaulds a Pounds 1-for-Pounds 1 reduction in the price if insurance proceeds fail to cover the weather damage to the French Cogetex facility just before completion.
