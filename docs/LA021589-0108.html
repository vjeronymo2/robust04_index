February 15, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NEWPORT HARBOR WINS WILD-CARD SOCCER MATCH

Tommy Walker scored in the second overtime to give Newport Harbor High School a 1-0 victory over La Habra Tuesday in a Southern Section 1-A wild-card soccer match at Newport Harbor.

Josh Klein had six saves for Newport Harbor (9-6-5), which plays four-seeded Artesia Thursday. Mario Munoz had nine saves for La Habra, which ends the season 10-8-4.
