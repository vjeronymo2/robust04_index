Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On 29 December, in the evening, Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan had four telephone conversations with the president of Azerbaijan, Mr. Heydar Aliyev. The Armenian president demanded from his Azeri counterpart to stop the bombardment of the Noyemberyan, Idzhevan and Daush districts of the Republic of Armenia at once or else the Armenian side will similarly respond to the Azeri side. The ARMENPRES news agency, quoting Armenian Defence Ministry sources, said that after the telephone conversation of the Armenian president with Aliyev the bombardment of the northeastern districts of Armenia stopped at once. The situation there is reported calm during the night and during the day.
