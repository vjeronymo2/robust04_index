June 21, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

FOOTNOTES;

DUTCH CLUB DEALING FOR RAMOS

JC Roda of the Netherlands' first division has offered U.S. midfielder Tab Ramos a three-year contract. The U.S. Soccer Federation is asking for a $750,000 transfer fee, a figure the club has balked at.

Ramos would prefer a two-year or one-year deal, but nothing can happen until the transfer fee is worked out.
