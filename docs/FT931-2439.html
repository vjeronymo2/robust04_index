930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Forum meets PM MR JOHN MAJOR, the Prime Minister, yesterday endorsed the work of the Asian business community at the Westminster launch of a business forum. Mr Major led a cross-party welcome for the National Organisation of Asian Businesses, a Birmingham-based group with 1,000 members. He said: 'The secure place the Asians have built in this country is secure right across the political establishment.'
