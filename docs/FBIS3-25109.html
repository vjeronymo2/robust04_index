Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Feb 5 SAPA -- Bophuthatswana police stormed the Winterveldt Development Centre offices where a voter education meeting was to be held, and burnt African National Congress [ANC] voter education pamphlets on Saturday [5 February] morning, SABC radio news reports. Local ANC Branch Chairman Shadrack Dlamini said police told them the organisation was outlawed in the homeland. An ANC delegation is negotiating with the Bophuthatswana police to get permission for the meeting.
