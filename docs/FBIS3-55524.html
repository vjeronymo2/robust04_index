Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentin Pavlov] [Text] Barnaul January 10 TASS -- Altay is preparing for the elections to the regional legislative assembly, scheduled for March 13. According to the working council's decision, the legislative assembly will have one chamber in which 50 deputies will sit, some of whom will work full time on a salary. Fifty single-mandate electoral districts have been formed for the elections. Each of them has an average of 37 thousand voters. Russia's Central Electoral Commission appointed 12 members of the Altay Regional Electoral Commission.
