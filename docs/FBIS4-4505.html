BFN [Text] In a statement issued by Foreign Ministry Spokesman Chang Ki-ho on 1 April, the government appraised it was an appropriate measure that the UN Security Council [UNSC] urged North Korea to accept additional inspections and requested the Secretary General of the International Atomic Energy Agency report the result of the negotiation. The government said the UNSC presidential statement reflects the international community's deep concern over the seriousness of the North Korean nuclear issue and the resolute will to resolve the issue. He urged North Korea to immediately respond to the presidential statement.
