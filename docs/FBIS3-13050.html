Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 6 Mar (INA)--A delegation representing the Eastern Catholic patriarchs left Baghdad today following a one-week visit to Iraq. In a statement to INA, Maronite Church Patriarch Bulus Asil Sa'adah praised the support and care given by President Saddam Husayn to the places of worship, churches, and clergymen. Patriarch Sa'adah said President Saddam Husayn is a courageous and wise leader. [passage omitted]
