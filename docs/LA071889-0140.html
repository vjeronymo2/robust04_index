July 18, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: MISSION VIEJO;

NEW NAME FOR ROAD LEADING TO COUNTRY CLUB

Golfers have a new kind of drive at the Mission Viejo Country Club.

The street leading to the country club and golf course has been changed -- from Goleta Drive to Country Club Drive.

The public street, south of Oso Parkway between Marguerite Parkway and Interstate 5, received the new identity by a vote of the City Council. The change was requested by the Mission Viejo Country Club, which will pay $107.13 to cover costs.
