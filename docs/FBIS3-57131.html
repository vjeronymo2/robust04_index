Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Untitled ITAR-TASS report] [Text] By all accounts, Yuriy Yarov will retain the post of vice premier in the new government, Valeriy Vorontsov, the vice premier's adviser, has told ITAR-TASS. He explained that Yuriy Yarov will continue to manage the social sphere for the government. Furthermore, he added, the vice premier will also be responsible for questions of the Cabinet's current work. Let us recall here that Yuriy Yarov is chairman of the Commission for the Burial of the Remains of the Tsar's Family and is also responsible for commemorating the date of the siege of Leningrad. However, the vice premier's adviser noted, the management of the Ministry of Health will in all likelihood be transferred to the control of First Vice Premier Oleg Soskovets.
