June 21, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

MAJOR LEAGUE TRYOUT CAMP THIS WEEKEND AT OCC

Local baseball players will have a chance to compete in front of scouts from many major league baseball teams at a tryout camp Friday and Saturday at Orange Coast College.

The camp, which begins at 9 a.m. both days, is sponsored by the Major League Scouting Bureau and is open to anyone between the ages of 16 and 25. American Legion players must bring a note from their coach with permission to try out.
