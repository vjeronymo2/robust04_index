BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA) -- [Egyptian] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met this morning with his Iranian counterpart, 'Ali Akbar Velayati, on the sidelines of the Non-Aligned Movement [NAM] Conference. Velayati described the one-hour meeting as extremely constructive and positive and said that NAM and international cooperation issues were discussed. In a statement he made following the meeting, Velayati added that Egypt and Iran expressed their desire for constructive bilateral cooperation in the international arena, NAM, and the Islamic Conference Organization. He said that he and Musa discussed various issues, including the situations in Yemen and Bosnia and regional matters. Velayati refused to answer questions on bilateral issues between Egypt and Iran or on the criticism by British Foreign Office Minister Douglas Hurd that Iran is sponsoring terrorism.
