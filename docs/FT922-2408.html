920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute Spot and shipment sales for the week ended 12 June 1992 amounted to 126 tonnes against 437 tonees in the previous week. Sales were low and occured mainly in West African, Central Asian and Israeli growths.
