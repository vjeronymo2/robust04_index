931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Boost for Karachi shares The KSE-100 index on the Karachi Stock Exchange, Pakistan's largest stock market, yesterday broke through the 1500 point level, after a week-long rise, writes Farhan Bokhari from Islamabad. The index gained 15.76 points on the day to close at 1501.44 in a rise which began last week, following Ms Benazir Bhutto's appointment as prime minister.
