February 21, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

La Jolla-based Agouron Pharmaceuticals reported a net loss of $1.1 million for the second quarter ended Dec. 31, compared to a $742,000 net loss the previous second quarter. Revenue rose to $530,000, up from $$173,600 during the like quarter a year ago.

Agouron reported a $1.9-million net loss for the six-month period ended Dec. 31, compared to a $1.3-million net loss a year earlier. Revenue rose to $1.1 million, up from $345,200 a year ago.
