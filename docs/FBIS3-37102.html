Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tiraspol BASAPRESS, 12/2/1994 -- The press service of the president and government of the so-called Dniester Republic has published a declaration on the future elections for the Moldovan Parliament. The document states that the Tiraspol government does not intend to hinder the participation of the Dniester inhabitants in the elections, but they will be able to do that "solely on the territory of the Republic of Moldova." International observers are invited to make sure that elections will not be held in the Dniester Republic. Previously, Tiraspol leaders tried to dam elections in Moldova's eastern regions, by introducing the emergency state and visa regime for entering the Dniester Republic.
