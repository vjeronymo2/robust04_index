February 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

IRAN HANGS 70 FOR DRUG SMUGGLING

Seventy drug smugglers were hanged in Tehran and 25 other Iranian cities Tuesday, an official report said. It was the largest number of traffickers executed in a single day of a ferocious anti-narcotics crackdown.

Sixty-seven men were hanged in public at dawn in a blinding snowstorm and three women were executed inside Iranian prisons, the official Islamic Republic News Agency reported.
