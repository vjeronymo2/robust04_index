Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 22 Jan (INA)--Tariq 'Aziz, member of the Revolution Command Council and official in charge of the Arab Liberation Movements Bureau, has received a message for President Saddam Husayn from Dr. George Habash, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP]. 'Abd-al-Rahim Malluh, member of the PLO Executive Committee and member of the PFLP Political Bureau, conveyed the message when he was received by Tariq 'Aziz today. The meeting discussed the close brotherly relations between the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and the revolution in Iraq on one hand and the PFLP on the other, as well as developments in the Palestinian arena.
