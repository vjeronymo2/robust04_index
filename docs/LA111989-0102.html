November 19, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

YOUTH'S SLAYING CALLED GANG-RELATED

A 17-year-old San Diego youth was killed early Saturday morning in an apparent gang-related Mira Mesa shooting, San Diego police said.

Raymond L. Jose was shot about 12:20 a.m. near the intersection of Reagan Road and Mira Mesa Boulevard, as he approached a dark-colored car, police and coroner's deputies said.

After the shooting, Jose's companions carried him to a nearby fast-food store, where paramedics arrived but found him dead, police said.
