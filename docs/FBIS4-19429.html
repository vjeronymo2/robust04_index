BFN [Text] Carlo Scognamiglio is the new speaker of the Senate. The candidate of the majority parties (Freedom Alliance) was elected in the fourth ballot with 162 votes. Spadolini won 161 votes. [Rome TV Teletext in Italian at 0919 GMT on 16 Apr 94 reported that at the third ballot, Carlo Scognamiglio and Giovanni Spadolini polled 159 votes each. As provided in the rules for the election of the Senate speaker, there will therefore be a further vote between the two, which is scheduled for this afternoon. The details of the third ballot are as follows: Scognamiglio, 159 votes; Spadolini, 159 votes; Cossiga, 2 votes. [There were four blank papers. The attendance and the voters numbered 324. The required number of votes for election is 163.]
