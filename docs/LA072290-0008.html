July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BRODERICK MURDERS

My soul, mind and heart go out to Betty Broderick. I have been in her position as far as the kind of betrayal she has endured by her husband, the courts, judges, lawyers, her own family and cronies of her husband.

No one knows or understands this kind of situation until they have been through it. I am totally 100% in Betty Broderick's corner.

SUSANNA HAHN

Malibu
