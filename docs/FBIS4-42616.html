BFN [Text] Schwerin (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl has called for consistent continuation of the policy of European integration. Following German-Spanish government consultations in Schwerin, he said today that to a great degree, European integration had contributed to the prosperity and freedom of nations. He and his Spanish counterpart Felipe Gonzalez agreed to continue to press for the integration of Europe. Kohl went on to say that both sides had agreed during the two-day summit in the Mecklenburg-Western Pomeranian capital that they would intensively prepare and coordinate in advance for the forthcoming European Union Council meeting in Corfu. Against the background of the D-Day celebrations, Kohl said Germany has learned the lesson of history.
