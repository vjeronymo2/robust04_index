BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl on Tuesday morning [19 April] discussed the situation in Gorazde with Russian President Boris Yeltsin over the phone. As government spokesman Dieter Vogel said, they talked about a potential solution by means of negotiations and a cease-fire in the interest of the civilian population. Kohl affirmed his view that "all efforts must be made to put an end to the bloodshed in the region and to bring about a lasting peace settlement." On Monday, Kohl had telephoned U.S. President Bill Clinton and French President Francois Mitterrand.
