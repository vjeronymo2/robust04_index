Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoriya] [Text] Tbilisi January 22 TASS -- Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev said plans have been made to bury Georgian ex-president Zviad Gamsakhurdia in Groznyy. In an exclusive interview with the World Television News Company obtained by ITAR-TASS by its courtesy, Dudayev said "we plan to bury Zviad Gamsakhurdia in Groznyy, on the square of the memorial complex which is being built to commemorate the beginning of the deportation of the Chechen people (celebrated in the republic annually on February 23). This will do honour to all Chechen people." A Georgian delegation which returned to Tbilisi last night had held talks in Groznyy on Gamsakhurdia's re-burial. The delegation included relatives of the late president, scientists and representatives of the georgian public prosecutor's office. ITAR-TASS learnt from one of the members of the delegation that in Groznyy Gamsakhurdia's relatives met with his wife Manana.
