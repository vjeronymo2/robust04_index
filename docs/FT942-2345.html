940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Arts: Samois Sur Seine The 15th Festival de Jazz Django Reinhardt opens on Friday for the weekend. Held on an island in the river at Samois sur Seine, two miles from Fontainbleu, the event is a mecca for gypsy guitarists from all over Europe. Both Manouche and Gitane tribes display their different accoustic styles and Babik Reinhardt, Django's son, a Manouche, takes the tradition electric.
