940927 FT 27 SEP 94 / Observer: A rash move? So Philip Jarrold, 44, managing director of Peaudouce, the nappy manufacturer, is moving to head Ladbroke's Vernons pools division. This should give hacks in the trade press a chance to test their headline writing skills. How about 'Forty-four and finally out of nappies', or 'Jarrold arrives at Vernons with reputation unsoiled'. Observer's suggestion is 'From the bottom to the top' but a real pro could do much better.
