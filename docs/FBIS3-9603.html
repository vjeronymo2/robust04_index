Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Banja Luka, 27 Mar (TANJUG) -- Radovan Karadzic, president of the Srpska republic, said at a meeting today with the Banja Luka municipal committee of the Serb Democratic Party [SDS] that the Muslims' large-scale spring offensive, which has already begun, must be halted. "If they (the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army) do not stop the offensive, I will order a counteroffensive and they will not be able to obtain the territories they could obtain through political negotiations," Karadzic said. He added: "There is no way they can get the territories where they are now killing our people." Karadzic believes the international community will soon exert more pressure to stop the war in former Bosnia-Herzegovina.
