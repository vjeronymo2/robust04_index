BFN [Text] Five persons were slightly injured in an explosion aboard a passenger ferry in Istanbul's Eminonu Quay. According to a TRT correspondent, an explosion occurred aboard the Rumelikavagi at 1315 [1015 GMT] today, as it was preparing to leave Eminonu for Uskudar. Five citizens were slightly injured by the explosion in the stern of the boat. They were taken to Hasiki State Hospital for treatment. Hospital officials report that the injured are in good condition. Security officials said that the boat was taken by tug to open waters and that the investigation into the explosion is continuing.
