BFN [From the "Telemetro Reports" newscast] [Text] [Announcer] The authorities have arrested an armed man who was near Ernesto Perez Balladares, presidential candidate for the Democratic Revolutionary Party [PRD]. Here is a report: [Reporter] As Perez Balladares was walking down from stage No. 1 on Balboa Avenue, his security forces subdued a man who pulled a gun. [Begin PRD leader Francisco Sanchez recording] A man who was standing about 2 meters from our candidate, Dr. Ernesto Perez Balladares, pulled out a gun. Thank God our security forces did a good job and arrested him. We will file charges. The electoral delegates took this man to their headquarters to investigate the incident. We regret this incident and urge all citizens and our opponents to avoid actions of this sort, because they will bring no good to Panama. [end recording]
