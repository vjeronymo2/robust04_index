BFN [Text] The brother President 'Arafat received today His Excellency the British Ambassador to Tunisia Sir Michael Tait, who delivered to him a message from British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd. The meeting was an opportunity, during which the brother president reviewed with the ambassador the latest developments at the Palestinian scene in light of the signing of the agreement of implementing the self-rule in the Gaza sector and Jericho. The meeting was attended by brothers Nabil Sha'th and Dr. Sami Musallam.
