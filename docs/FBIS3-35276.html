Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Ministry of Defense and the General Staff of the Armed Forces has informed the Yemeni News Agency, SABA' that the regrettable military incident that took place in Abyan Governorate was contained this morning. The source added: Once Presidential Council Chairman brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, and Presidential Council Vice Chairman brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid were informed of this incident, they instructed that the incident be contained, that a rapid investigation of the circumstances surrounding it be conducted, and that full compliance with instructions be enforced. The Military Committee has been asked to monitor the implementation of the instructions, and to take the necessary relevant measures. The source asserted that this incident will not undermine the joy that has swept our people in the aftermath of the signing of the accord document in Arab Amman, the city of accord and reconciliation, under the sponsorship of His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal and the Jordanian people. Moreover, this incident will not deter all from continuing to mobilize efforts to implement the document of accord in order to build a modern Yemeni state, a state of institutions, law, and order, the source concluded.
