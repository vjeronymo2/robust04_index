940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / UK Company News: National Grid takes Pounds 33m p&l hit The National Grid Company, which is jointly owned by the privatised electricity companies, has redeemed two Pounds 150m tranches of government debt. The aggregate redemption amount is Pounds 333.3m, meaning that Pounds 33.3m will be charged to the profit and loss account this year. However, the company said that the redemption would result in a significant reduction in interest costs.
