Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 25 -- Two hundred and three Vietnamese nationals returning from a refugee camp in Japan arrived in Hai Phong yesterday. They belong to the first batch of 263 returnees from Japan. Most of them are young people from Kien Thuy, Do Son town and Le Chan precinct of Hai Phong Port City. These returnees are not beneficiaries from the fund provided by the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees because they illegally left the country after Sept. 27, 1991.
