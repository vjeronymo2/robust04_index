Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Indonesian Government cannot accept the U.S. blockade against Cuba because every country has the right to adopt its own political and economic system without the interference of other countries. Speaking to reporters after he met with Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina in Jakarta today, Foreign Minister Ali Alatas said Indonesia has firmly adopted such a stand as the basis of its international relations, adding that Indonesia also believes that a country should not impose its will on another country, especially by imposing a blockade. Speaking to reporters, Cuban Foreign Minister Robaina said his visit to Indonesia is part of his tour of seven Asian countries in his country's efforts to oppose the economic blockade imposed by the United States for the last 30 years.
