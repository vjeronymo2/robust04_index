BFN [Text] Hostilities resumed at daybreak today in the area of the Karabakh conflict after a relative lull lasting one day. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has been told by the Karabakh army's press service that Azeri troops supported by armor attempted to break though the defensive lines of the Karabakh forces in the northeast, in the Agdam sector. After a 90-minute long battle, all the attacks of the Azeri side were repelled, the press service noted. According to the source, the Azeris sustained significant losses in manpower and were forced to retreat to their initial positions. There were no losses on the Karabakh side. According to reports from Stepanakert, the headquarters of the Karabakh army is receiving information about a massive concentration of Azeri troops and combat hardware in the north of Mardakertskiy Rayon.
