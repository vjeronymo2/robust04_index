930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / International Company News: OCP suitors withdraw bid FRENCH pharmaceutical goods distributor and manufacturer Cooperation Pharmaceutique Francaise and distributor Bergen Brunswick of the US have dropped their bid for Office Commercial Pharmaceutique (OCP), Paris Bourse authorities announced, AP-DJ reports from Paris. The move appears to leave the field to the counterbid by Germany's Gehe, which offered FFr985 a share (ex-dividend) for the group, substantially more than the FFr865 with dividend offered by Cooper and Bergen Brunswick. Gehe, a distributor and generic drug maker, intends to merge its distribution activities with OCP's, creating a French-registered company with FFr50bn of annual revenues. Gehe's bid closes on July 9.
