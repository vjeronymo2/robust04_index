941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / Number of Law Lords rises to 12 The government is to increase the maximum permitted number of Law Lords and Appeal Court judges to cope with the increasing workload of the higher courts. The maximum number of law lords will be increased from 11 to 12. There were 10 in post at the beginning of November. This is the first time an order has been made to increase the statutory ceiling on their number. The number of Appeal Court judges will rise from 29 to 32.
