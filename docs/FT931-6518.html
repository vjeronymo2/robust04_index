930301 FT 01 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore Value net assets advance Gartmore Value Investments, an investment trust, reported a net asset value of 28.3p per share as at January 31, up from 27.4p at the April year-end. The figure for the zero dividend preference shares was 79.1p (72.1p). Net revenue for the nine months to end-January was Pounds 1.09m (Pounds 987,000). Earnings per share emerged at 2.18p (1.99p). A third interim dividend of 0.9525p (0.925p) is declared, making 2.8575p to date. The directors expect to announce a reduced fourth interim of 0.9525p (1.5p) cutting the total to 3.81p (4.275p).
