920404 FT 04 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices White pepper was available at USDollars 1,300 a tonne and the shipment price for April/May delivery was Dollars 1,275 a tonne cif. We do not foresee drastic changes in the near future. The undertone of the black pepper market was easier because of the growing selling pressure from the Far East (Malaysia and Vietnam). The spot European price for grade 1 black pepper was Dollars 1,175 a tonne, while that quality could be obtained on shipment basis at about Dollars 1,100 a tonne cif.
