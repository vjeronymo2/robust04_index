BFN [Text] The provincial meeting on the general inspection of projects using World Bank loans ended in Jinan on the afternoon of 6 April. Huang Kehua, director of the provincial finance department, (Xin Liqun), acting head of the World Bank Department of the Ministry of Finance, and (Nicholas Hof), director of the China and Mongolia Bureau of the World Bank, each delivered closing speeches. Under the leadership of Vice Governor Song Fatang, pertinent leaders of the provincial planning commission introduced six new projects to officials of the World Bank for possible funding after the closing ceremony. These six projects include: The environmental protection and comprehensive improvement of the coastal economic zone of Shandong Province; the rebuilding of communications facilities of the capital city of Jinan; the comprehensive development of water conservancy works on Huang He Delta; the construction of communications facilities of Shandong; the development of power industry of Shandong; and the construction of the rural well-off-type residences to the north of China's Chang Jiang. At present, the province has already applied for $1.65 billion in loans from the World Bank. Upon completion of these projects, the environmental quality of key cities, coastal open cities, and tourist cities in Shandong is expected to improve.
