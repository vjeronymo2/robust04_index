920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 14.5m orders won by Ballast Nedam BALLAST NEDAM CONST RUCTION has been awarded eight contracts worth almost Pounds 14.5m. They include a Pounds 1.79m design and build contract with Nuclear Electric in Heysham, a Pounds 2.4m three-year term minor works contract for Shell at Ellesmere Port, a Pounds 1.05m private hospice in Middlesbrough, projects valued at Pounds 2m concerning a pharmaceutical complex in the North East and, for client Arlington, a Pounds 2.15m Royal Mail building at Hatfield. The awards include work worth over Pounds 5m won in Lancashire, involving a process engineering building and the extension of a machining facility centre. The contracts are shared between Ballast Nedam's eight UK regional offices. The company is a member of British Aerospace and part of the international contracting group Ballast Nedam BV
