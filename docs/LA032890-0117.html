March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SLICK AD: On Monday -- just in time for spring break -- USA Today will carry a full-page ad that extols the joys of Huntington Beach and Newport Beach. . . . Produced by city tourism officials, the ad was placed by British Petroleum at a cost of about $143,000. . . . InterCommunications Inc, a Newport Beach-based agency, took a reduced fee for the ad, which makes no mention of February's oil spill. "We felt people would get the message," said the agency's Toni Alexander. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
