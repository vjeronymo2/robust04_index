910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Daily Telegraph falls to Pounds 7.9m Profits of The Daily Telegraph fell from Pounds 10.9m to Pounds 7.9m pre-tax for the three months to March 31. Turnover declined by Pounds 4.8m to Pounds 53.4m and operating profits by Pounds 3.7m to Pounds 6m. Taxable profits were buoyed by a Pounds 600,000 rise in investment income to Pounds 1.8m. After tax of Pounds 1.9m (Pounds 2.8m) earnings came to 4.5p (6.1p).
