BFN [Text] Pakistan and Russia are interested in developing their economic and political cooperation. This will contribute to the strengthening of peace and cooperation in Asia and will be an important factor in maintaining stability in the region. This opinion was expressed in an interview for ITAR-TASS by Anatoliy Adamishin, first deputy foreign minister of the Russian Federation. He completed his working visit to Islamabad today. During his visit, the Russian diplomat discussed the situation in the region, and particularly in Tajikistan, with President Faruq Leghari and Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto. The Pakistani leaders said that Islamabad was ready to strain every effort so as to reduce tension on the Tajik border and to help the Tajik opposition and the government to find a common language. Also today, Adamishin has arrived in Delhi on a working visit.
