920711 FT 11 JUL 92 / Finance & The Family: Smaller companies still gloomy Small companies endured another bad week. The County NatWest smaller companies index fell 3.26 per cent to 915.26 over the seven days to Thursday, July 9. Over the same period the Hoare Govett index (capital gains version) slipped 2.55 per cent to 1148.24. Hoare Govett attributes these problems to a familiar source - the continuing non-appearance of a consumer-led recovery, and its effect on confidence.
