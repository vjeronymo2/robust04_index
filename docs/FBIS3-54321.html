Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] After the speech delivered by the president at the meeting of foreign ministers of the Economic Cooperation Organization [ECO], a ceremony was held transferring the annual presidency from Pakistan to the Islamic Republic of Iran. Having become the meeting's chairman, Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati delivered a speech on the reasons for holding the meeting and ways in which ECO members can cooperate. Mr. Velayati said: In order to achieve its aims, ECO needs to benefit from the experience and financial and technical cooperation of specialist and international organizations. In this connection, it is willing to provide part of the costs of implementing joint projects. The foreign minister said that progress will not be possible unless ECO members make serious and constant efforts. He added: It is vital for the organization to find new ways to expedite decisionmaking and the implementation of projects. He also said that the Islamic Republic of Iran is of the opinion that part of the region's fundamental needs will be met with the commencement and expansion of the activities of institutions affiliated to ECO, such as the bank for development and commerce, the reinsurance company [bimeh-ye etteka'i], ECO's science foundation, ECO's cultural foundation, the shipping and airline transport organizations, as well as other planned ECO organizations. Our correspondent's report states that the session continued with speeches delivered by a number of other participants.
