920407 FT 07 APR 92 / World News In Brief: Souness to undergo heart surgery Liverpool Football Club manager Graeme Souness, 38, went into a private hospital near Stockport for a triple heart bypass operation. The four-hour operation and subsequent recuperation will keep Souness out of football until July. Souness saw success as a player with Liverpool and the Italian club Sampdoria before going into management with Glasgow Rangers in 1986.
