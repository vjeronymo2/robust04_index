941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Oceana Cons ahead Oceana Consolidated Company, the stockbroker and investment manager, increased interim pre-tax profits 14 per cent from Pounds 877,000 to Pounds 1m. Turnover in the six months to September 30 was Pounds 6.88m, a rise of 9 per cent from Pounds 6.29m. Charles Stanley, the group's principal subsidiary, more than doubled fee income to Pounds 1.84m. Earnings per share came out at 8.62p (7.28p) and the interim dividend rises to 0.625p (0.5p).
