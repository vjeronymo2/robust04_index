940415 FT 15 APR 94 / World Trade News: Digital VCR standards agreed Fifty American, European and Asian companies agreed yesterday on technical standards for home digital video cassette recorders, which provide better picture quality and cleaner copies of tapes, AP reports from Tokyo. The agreement means average consumers could be able to buy a digital VCR as soon as early next year, industry officials said. A conference in Tokyo that included the leading Japanese makers announced standards for VCRs used with conventional televisions and for those used with Japan's high-definition TV system, called Hi-Vision.
