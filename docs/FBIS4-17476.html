BFN [Text] As a European Union [EU] communique on the Ukrainian elections says, the EU with pleasure and enthusiasm assumes that the elections were free and legal. This has been confirmed by observers' reports, in which they praise the organizational efforts. The EU expects that the election results will allow Ukraine to continue political and economic reforms. The EU has voiced concern over increasing tensions in the Crimea. It fully supports Ukraine's territorial integrity, according to the UN Charter and the principles of the CSCE Final Act.
