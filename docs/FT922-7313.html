920519 FT 19 MAY 92 / SIB announces four committees THE Securities and Investments Board yesterday announced the appointment of four sub-committees to examine how its planned self-regulatory organisation to oversee all retail investment business would work in practice. A committee has been working since early this year on the proposal. The new organisation will be put together from two existing regulatory bodies: Lautro, the life insurance and unit trust overseer, and Fimbra, which looks after independent intermediaries.
