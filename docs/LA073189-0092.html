July 31, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

ENTERTAINMENT;

ROBIN WILLIAMS, WIFE HAVE BABY GIRL

Robin Williams and his new wife, Marsha, became parents today.

Marsha, who met the actor while she was employed as nanny for Williams and his first wife, gave birth in New York to a 9-pound, 6-ounce girl whom they named Zelda, a spokesman for Williams said. The couple were married in April.
