940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Equity Markets The implications of the European elections for equity markets will be felt this week. It was good news for Austria, which also voted at the weekend in favour of joining the European Union; the Vienna market was the best performer in local currency terms among the constituents of the FT-Actuaries World index. The Austrian decision could have a positive short-term effect for those Scandinavian markets which are due to vote on union this autumn, although Unibank Securities reports that the 'no' vote campaign continues to lead polls in both Sweden and Norway. ----------------------------------------------------------------------- MARKETS IN PERSPECTIVE
