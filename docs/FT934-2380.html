931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Strong growth lifts Norbain shares Shares of Norbain jumped 21p to 290p yesterday after the expanding USM-traded security products group built upon a strong showing last year with a buoyant interim performance. Profits before tax for the six months to October 31 amounted to Pounds 724,000, against Pounds 224,000, reflecting good growth throughout the group, according to Mr John Nicol, chairman. Turnover was Pounds 15.8m (Pounds 7.62m), including a contribution of Pounds 4.37m from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 6.69p (2.16p) basic and 6.59p (2.1p) fully diluted.
