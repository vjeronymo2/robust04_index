December 28, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

SUSPECT PLEADS GUILTY IN SPIKED PASTA AND SEX CASE

A man who fled to Italy after allegedly feeding drug-laced spaghetti to two women and then sexually assaulting them pleaded guilty Thursday to felony sex charges. Nicola Iannone, 35, is being held without bond pending sentencing Jan. 24 on two charges of gross sexual imposition.
