October 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Rapping for Free Speech: Rappers 2 Live Crew, Ice Cube, Ice T, N.W.A and the Geto Boys have all contributed songs to a new anti-censorship compilation titled "Explicit Rap" due out in November on Priority Records. The album's artwork features a 12-inch tongue-in-cheek blow-up of the official Recording Industry Assn. of America's voluntary parental warning sticker: "WARNING, PARENTAL ADVISORY. EXPLICIT RAP." CLAUDIA PUIG
