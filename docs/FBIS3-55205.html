Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [SEGODNYA report: "`Newspapers' Strike' Might Not Materialize"] [Text] Yesterday, editors-in-chief of key Russian publications were still considering calling a strike to protest the newspapers' critical economic situation. It was decided to call a warning strike in the first "parliamentary week" -- from 10 to 17 January. Three newspapers disagreed with the decision, including ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, NOVAYA YEZHEDNEVNAYA GAZETA, and also KRASNAYA ZVEZDA (spokesmen for the last newspaper expressed their solidarity with the "strikers"). It soon transpired, however, that Mr. Chernomyrdin is ready to meet with the editors-in-chief today to talk about all the existing disagreements.
