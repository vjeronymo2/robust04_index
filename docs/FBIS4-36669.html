BFN [Excerpt] A responsible source at the Defense Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has categorically denied that Rayyan Airport [serving al-Mukalla] had been rendered inoperable or had suffered any kind of damage. The source said all democratic Yemen's airports with the exception of that at Ataq were operational. The source expressed amazement that it was Israel radio which had carried the report by the military spokesman in Sanaa that northern forces yesterday evening allegedly shelled al-Mukalla's airport.
