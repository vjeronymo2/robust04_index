BFN [Text] The Russian Foreign Ministry's Africa Department has informed us that the Russian Embassy in Rwanda was totally evacuated today due to the unpredictable situation in the capital of that African country. Embassy staff, Aeroflot representatives, and their families were taken to the capital of Burundi. Only women who are Russian citizens married to Rwandans remain in Rwanda. Interethnic clashes which started six days ago in Rwanda have left at least 10,000 people dead.
