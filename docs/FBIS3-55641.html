Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev arrived today on an official visit to Uzbekistan. The leaders of the two countries signed a package of documents necessary for the creation of a single economic space between Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan. [video shows nazarbayev arriving at airport] [Begin Nazarbayev recording] I think we will open the borders for trade, we will remove the customs barriers, we will remove licensing and quotas; when we are in different monetary systems and national currencies this can only be beneficial. [end recording] The heads of the Central Asian states are to discuss another global problem, the problem of the Aral, which they will visit tomorrow. [video shows President Karimov at airport] [Begin Karimov recording] If today we do not take this initiative then it will be impossible to solve this problem, and its consequences will be felt not only in the lives of people but -- this is most terrible -- on the genetic pool of people, and one cannot look at the future of this region as a whole, and not just this region, without a solution to the problem of the Aral, the Aral problem. [end recording]
