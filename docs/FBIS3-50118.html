Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] More than 100 120-mm mortar shells landed on the free [Croat-held] part of Mostar this afternoon. Four children were killed by the shells: Damir Rizvan, Armin Rizvan, Emanuela Meskic, and Emil Meskic. Four children and four adults were wounded, the command of the Mostar operational zone has reported.
