930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Abbey National chairman's pay up 33% The salary and benefits of the chairman of Abbey National rose 33 per cent last year, it was disclosed yesterday. The annual accounts said that Sir Christopher Tugendhat received Pounds 211,575 last year after being paid Pounds 95,156 in 1991. Abbey this month announced a 9 per cent fall in pre-tax profits to Pounds 564m for 1992 after doubling bad debt provisions. Sir Christopher was paid only part of the 1991 total of Pounds 159,525 after becoming chairman in July that year. Mr Peter Birch, chief executive, received Pounds 257,566 in pay and benefits, but no performance bonus. In 1991, he was paid Pounds 216,756 in salary and benefits, and a further Pounds 45,150 in performance bonuses. Abbey paid Pounds 266,000 compensation to Mr Richard Baglin, former managing director for new business, who retired early in August. It paid Pounds 1.6bn in directors' fees, salaries and benefits.
