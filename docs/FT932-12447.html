930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Britannia deficit cut to Pounds 393,000 Britannia Group, the Cheltenham-based construction and housing company, cut its pre-tax loss from Pounds 690,000 to Pounds 393,000 for the year to end-December. Turnover was up from Pounds 27.1m to Pounds 29.3m. Losses worked through at 3.3p (5p) and the net asset value per share stood at 60.7p (65.8p). There is no dividend (1p).
