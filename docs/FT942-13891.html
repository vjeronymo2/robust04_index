940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Ldn Amer Growth Net asset value per share of London American Growth Trust rose from 55.9p to 64.5p over the 12 months to March 31. The comparative figure has been adjusted for the capital repayment of 18.5p a share last August. After-tax revenue amounted to Pounds 105,000, against losses of Pounds 14,000. There were earnings per share of 0.07p against losses of 0.08p.
