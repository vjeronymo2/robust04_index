940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / UK Company News: 96% acceptance for Gosforth Park offer The final acceptance level of Northern Racing's recommended cash offer for High Gosforth Park is 95.84 per cent of issued capital, representing 87,174 shares. The offer has now closed. Northern Racing will compulsorily acquire all outstanding shares in High Gosforth Park, which owns and runs Newcastle racecourse.
