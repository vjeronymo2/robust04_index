BFN [Report by A. Thomson] [Text] Pretoria April 28 SAPA -- Fifteen magisterial districts in the Western Transvaal were on Thursday [28 April] declared unrest areas, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported. The areas, listed in a special Government Gazette, include Ventersdorp, Potchefstroom, Lichtenburg, Schweizer-Reneke, Klerksdorp, Rustenburg, Koster and Christiana. The Gazette gave no reasons for the declaration. Earlier on Thursday Afrikaner Weerstandsbeweging [AWB, Afrikaner Resistance Movement] supporters gathered near Rustenburg to pledge support for Afrikaner freedom. Police later confirmed right-wingers were also gathering near Koster. They said they were monitoring the movements of AWB supporters.
