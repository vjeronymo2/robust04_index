Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, Feb. 10, IRNA -- Syria is ready to enter into joint transportation and road building projects with Iran. Syrian Transport Minister 'Abd-al-Karim Mufid made the remarks last evening in a meeting with the Iranian ambassador here Mohammad Hasan Akhtari. The need to strengthen Tehran-Damascus cooperation in air, land, sea and railway transportation were stressed by the two officials. In the meeting Akhtari handed the Syrian transport minister an invitation from his Iranian counterpart, Akbar Torkan, to pay a visit to Iran.
