Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A source close to the Lebanese security forces has reported that the Lebanese police arrested a Palestinian who admitted that he assassinated Jordanian diplomat Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah. The source added that the Palestinian, whose identity has not yet been revealed, is a member of an armed Palestinian group. A judicial source said two Palestinians have been arrested in connection with the assassination. The source, however, did not confirm the reports that one of them is the perpetrator of the crime, but announced that the investigation has reached an advanced phase.
