930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Arts: Fischer-Dieskau retires Dietrich Fischer-Dieskau has announced his immediate retirement from the concert platform, writes Andrew Clark. The German baritone, who is 67, gave no reason for his decision, but said he would continue to give masterclasses. His last performance was at a gala concert at the Bavarian State Opera in Munich on New Year's Eve.
