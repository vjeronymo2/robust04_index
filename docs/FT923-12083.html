920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Physiotherapists set to join TUC THE Chartered Society of Physiotherapy has voted to affiliate to the Trades Union Congress. The move was backed by 85 per cent of those who took part in the vote. The society's council will take the formal decision to affiliate in September. 'Given the clear majority in favour, it is extremely likely to endorse affiliation,' the society said. It has been in dispute with several National Health Service trusts over negotiating rights and pay and conditions.
