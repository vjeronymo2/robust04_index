930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / International Company News: Kepphil Shipyard KEPPHIL Shipyard, the Philippines shipbuilder, plans to offer 250m shares to the public next month: 100m are new shares while 150m are existing shares the company wants to divest to the public, Reuter reports from Manila. The proposed offer price is two pesos per share. Proceeds will be used for expansion and modernisation. The company is presently owned 92.8 per cent by Keppel Philippines Holding. After the public offer, Kepphil will be 26.9 per cent publicly owned.
