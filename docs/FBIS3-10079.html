Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The News" newscast] [Excerpt] President Rafael Caldera has reported that the suspension of economic guarantees is directed at dealing with the country's financial crisis. Regarding the Value Added Tax [VAT], Caldera said very soon the government will present to Congress a general tax reform bill that calls for the definite elimination of the VAT on consumers, substituting it with other collection mechanisms that will allow those who have more to pay more. This announcement was welcomed by the community. [passage omitted]
