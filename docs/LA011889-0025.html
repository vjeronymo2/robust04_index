January 18, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: CHULA VISTA

A 52-year-old Chula Vista woman was found stabbed to death Tuesday in her car in front of her apartment building in the 500 block of D Street, police reported.

Helen Mary Roscoe was found in her blue Ford Torino by her apartment manager about 8 a.m., Chula Vista Police Officer Jeff Smith said.

The woman, who worked as a nurse's aide, was fully clothed when found. There was no weapon at the scene, and police have no suspects in the case.
