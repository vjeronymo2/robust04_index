BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin has authorized the entry of another 4,000 Palestinian laborers from the territories to work in Israel. Approximately half of them will be employed in industry while the other half will work in agriculture. This authorization applies to married Palestinians over the age of 27 who have worked in Israel for over five years. Our correspondent 'Ofra Nehman notes that with the addition of these workers, the number of laborers from the territories working in Israel is now equal to the number of those employed prior to the decision to impose a full closure.
