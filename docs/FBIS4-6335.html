BFN [Text] Malaysia considers the development of defense among the ASEAN member countries a contribution toward the maintenance of the region's security. Defense Minister Datuk Sri Najib Tun Razak said Malaysia does not feel threatened by such a development and the ASEAN member countries increased their purchase of defense equipment. Malaysia considers the purchase of defense equipment by ASEAN member countries a part of the armed forces modernization process. Datuk Najib told this to newsmen after opening the fourth Asian Defense Services Expo and Conference in Kuala Lumpur. He said Malaysia also is planning to upgrade its armed forces capabilities in an effort to maintain peace and security along with its neighbors in this region. Malaysia was of the opinion that the ASEAN member countries need to further upgrade their bilateral ties, particularly in the defense sector.
