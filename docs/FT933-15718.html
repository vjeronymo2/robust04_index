930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa THE GOLDS index slipped back moderately from an earlier high, having risen to an intraday peak of 2,112 as the price of bullion broke through the Dollars 390 an ounce level. The index was finally up 72, or 3.6 per cent, at 2,090. Industrials gained 17 at 4,719 on expectations of a cut in interest rates next month, while the overall index put on 36 at 4,210. De Beers rose R1 to R86 on better than expected diamond sales figures.
