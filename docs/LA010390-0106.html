January 3, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

Yet as the market surged Tuesday (A1), two of the three were up a bit, with only Comp Care down 1/8. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
