December 31, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Monday is New Year's Day and the following will be closed in observance of the holiday:

* All county, state and federal offices.

* Schools, including colleges and universities. Public elementary, junior and high schools will reopen Jan. 2.

* County, state and federal courthouses.

* City and county libraries.

* Banks and savings and loans.

* City offices will be closed only on New Year's Day with the following exceptions: In Buena Park, Cypress, Garden Grove, La Palma, San Juan Capistrano and Stanton, offices will be closed Monday and Tuesday.

Mail: All post offices will be closed New Year's Day, and mail deposited in mailboxes on those days will be picked up according to the holiday schedule.

Trash: There will be no trash pickup by city or county refuse workers on New Year's Day. Trash pickups will be a day late for the rest of the week.

Buses: Orange County Transit District buses will run on the holiday schedule on New Year's Day. Call the district at (714) 636-7433 for information.

Parks: All city- and county-run parks will be open during regular hours on New Year's Day.
