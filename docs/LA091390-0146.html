September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: DUARTE;

SIGN ORDINANCE GETS OK

Merchants will be able to fly promotional and seasonal banners for up to 63 days a year, under a sign ordinance that received preliminary approval from the City Council Tuesday.

If the ordinance receives final approval Sept. 25, banners for special events could stay up for 21 consecutive days three times a year. Previously, banners were only allowed for 40 days a year, and they could not be flown for more than 30 consecutive days.
