BFN [Text] At the invitation of Samdech Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen, first and second prime ministers of the government of Kingdom of Cambodia, our Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet left Hanoi this morning 2 April for an official visit to the Kingdom of Cambodia. Escorting Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet were the Foreign Affairs Minister Nguyen Manh Cam; Trade Minister Le Van Triet; Tran Hong Quan, minister of education and training; Customs General Director Truong Quang Duoc; Nguyen Ngoc Tran, chairman of the central Overseas Vietnamese Commission; Le Minh Nghia, chairman of the government Border Commission; Nguyen Quoc Mau, deputy head of Government Office; Vu Quoc Tuan, assistant to the prime minister; and Tran Huy Chuong, Vietnamese ambassador to the Kingdom of Cambodia.
