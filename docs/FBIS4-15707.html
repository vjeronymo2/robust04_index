BFN [Text] Moscow, April 8 (SNARK)--The agreements signed in Moscow between Georgia and Abkhazia trace a course of progress in Georgian-Abkhazian conflict regulation, Vladislav Ardzinba, a chairman of the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet, told SNARK. According to Ardzinba, Abkhazian side will do all the best to realize the agreements concluded in Moscow.
