BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow, 12 Jun -- Delegates of the founding congress being held in the Russian Parliamentary center, have unanimously voted to approve the name of the new party, which will be called Russia's Democratic Choice [Demokraticheskiy Vibor Rossii]. The party has been created on the basis of the Russia's Choice movement and faction. But Yegor Gaydar, leader of the faction, pointed out that the word "democratic" was added to the name for the sake of definition and to indicate what kind of choice we are offering Russia. The delegates of the congress have also adopted, as a basis, the program and the rules of the new party.
