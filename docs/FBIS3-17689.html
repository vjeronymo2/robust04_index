Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi March 28 TASS -- The Abkhazian military has claimed that it finished an operation on neutralizing Georgian groups of saboteurs high in the mountains of the Kodor gorge. According to a source in the Abkhazian Defence Ministry, the Georgian side lost three T-55 tanks, several anti-aircraft missile launchers and an armoured personnel carrier. The Abkhazians have seized a great number of automatic weapons. "Life is returning back to normal in the mountain villages", the source said.
