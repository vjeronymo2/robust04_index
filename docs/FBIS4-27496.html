BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists staged an exercise of surprise strikes at the strategic depth of the northern half of Korea in the sky above Yongwol and Tanyang of South Korea on May 31 with the massive mobilization of formations of overseas-based assault planes and pursuit-assault planes and flying corps of their airforce stationed in South Korea and the puppet airforce of South Korea, according to military sources. On the same day, they staged an operation of airlifting a large military strength and combat equipment to their Air Force bases in South Korea with Pacific-based large transport planes. Meanwhile, that day, 105 mm artillery groups of the puppet army in Chorwon and Yanggu Counties, South Korean Kangwon Province, fired some 50 shells into an area near the demilitarized zone along the Military Demarcation Line and heavily armed bandits of the puppet army in Paju and Yonchon Counties, Kyonggi Province, fired hundreds of large calibre machinegun bullets, coming in attack toward the Military Demarcation Line.
