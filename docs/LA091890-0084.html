September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

GIRL WHO WAS HIT WHILE SKATING DIES

A-9-year-old girl who was struck by a car while roller skating died Sunday of her injuries, a spokesman from the Orange County coroner's office said.

The girl, Stephanie Hoelzer of Trabuco Canyon, was struck about 6:20 p.m. Friday.

She was taken to Mission Hospital Regional Medical Center in critical condition and died at 2 p.m. Sunday, a deputy coroner said.
