941108 FT 08 NOV 94 / International Company News: Mexican subsidiary for Societe Generale Societe Generale said it had received approval to set up a Mexican subsidiary, which will be called Societe Generale SA and open in the first half of next year, Reuter reports from Paris. The French bank has had a representative office in Mexico since 1963. The Mexican unit will have capital of Dollars 50m and will be 100 per cent owned by the French parent. It will offer domestic lending, export financing, market operations, and advisory and financial engineering services. The subsidiary will be headed by Mr Jean Ponsard, who was a senior executive in Spain.
