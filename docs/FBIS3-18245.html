Language: Dutch Article Type:BFN [Article signed KvdB/GSC: "Hebron Killings Leading To Disorders in Brussels"] [Excerpt] The bloody killing of Palestinian Muslims by a Jewish settler in Hebron, has repercussions in our own country. Yesterday night, rather unexpected riots broke out in Brussels. They were caused by two events occurring close to each other. On the one hand, there was a lecture (with a movie performance of Schindler's List) by Jewish Nazi hunter Simon Wiesenthal in Hotel Metropole at the Place De Brouckere, and on the other hand a demonstration of the Anti-Imperialistic Union, associated with the PvdA [Belgian Party of Labor], and of Youngsters for Palestine at the Place de la Monnaie. The latter learned about Wiesenthal's lecture, and headed for the Place De Brouckere. [passage omitted]
