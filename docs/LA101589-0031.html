October 15, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BIG BEAT

Claudia Puig's puff piece, "Music to Someone's Ears," details how demographics were molded to make a case for dumping the classic KFAC. Given the bottom-line, throwaway mentality at work here, it sounds more like a dismal demonstration of demonographics.

As if the air in Los Angeles isn't bad enough already, the Texas-based Evergreen Corp. is now pouring its noxious audio fumes into our cultural atmosphere.

Let's hope the grandiose pipe dreams of those who now own and run KKBT lead to their speedy demise in a medium already up to its ears in noisome offal. It would serve these carpetbaggers right.

ED JAMES

Los Angeles
