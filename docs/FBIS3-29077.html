Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 14 (KCNA) -- The Tokyo Society of Working People for the Study of Kim Chong-il Works and the Kyoto Society for the Study of Kim Chong-il Works were inaugurated in Japan. An inaugural meeting of the society of working people for the study of Kim Chong-il works held in Tokyo on January 16 elected designing engineer Yoshio Watanabe chairman of the society and deputy director general of the Japan-Korea Cultural Interchange Association Jun Shimizu and former Tokyo Metropolitan Diet Member Sataharu Hirota advisors. The society consists of teachers, technicians and working people of different strata. At an inaugural meeting of the society for the study of Kim Chong-il works held in Kyoto on January 15 chairman of the society Kazuhiko Matsuhiro delivered a report. He referred to the study of the great and unique idea and theory of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and said that the society would strengthen the work with young workers as well as learned and literary men in Kyoto. A letter to Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting.
