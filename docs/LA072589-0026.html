July 25, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"Zorro" will be buckling his swashes on cable TV's Family Channel in a new half-hour series that will make its debut in January. Duncan Regehr will play Zorro and Zorro's alter ego, Don Diego. Shooting will start in August on location outside of Madrid, Spain. "Zorro" is a coproduction of New World Television, the Family Channel and Ellipse Programme/Canal Plus of France.ALEENE MacMINN
