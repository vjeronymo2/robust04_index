930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / Demonstrations outside Rosyth naval dockyard Workers from the Rosyth naval dockyard in Fife demonstrated yesterday outside the Ministry of Defence. The government will decide in the next few weeks whether work on refitting Trident nuclear submarines should be carried out at Rosyth or at Devonport in Plymouth. Mr Tom Clarke, the shadow Scottish secretary, challenged the prime minister to visit Rosyth to see the facilities available for submarine refits and the damage that would be done to the workforce if the dockyard closed
