Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Premier Chernomyrdin in Rome"] [Text] Rome, 27 Jan (ANSA) -- Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin arrived at Rome's Ciampino Airport Thursday [27 January] morning at the start of a two-day official visit to Italy, the foreign ministry said. On hand to meet Chernomyrdin at the airport was Foreign Trade Minister Paolo Baratta. Today's engagements for the visiting Russian premier include talks with his Italian counterpart Carlo Azeglio Ciampi and with Rome Mayor Francesco Rutelli. Tomorrow will find Chernomyrdin at the Vatican for a private audience with Pope John Paul II. He will also meet with representatives from the Confindustria employers' association and from the state-controlled fuels conglomerate ENI [National Hydrocarbons Corporation]. Italian Head of State will receive Chernomyrdin at the presidential Quirinale palace during his stay.
