BFN [Text] Xian, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Australian Governor-General Bill Hayden toured Shaanxi Provincial Historical Museum here this morning, and wrote in the visitors' book that the cultural relics in the museum can be regarded as important heirlooms of the world's civilization. Hayden and his party arrived in Xian, the capital city of northwest China's Shaanxi Province, yesterday. They visited the city's terracotta warriors and horses of the ancient Qin dynasty and the Huaqing spring in nearby Lintong County yesterday afternoon and were guests of honor at a banquet hosted in the evening by Wang Shuangxi, vice-governor of Shaanxi Province. Hayden and his party left here this afternoon for Urumqi, capital of China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous region.
