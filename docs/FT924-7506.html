921116 FT 16 NOV 92 / Construction Contracts: New court buildings in Nottingham HIGGS AND HILL MIDLANDS has been awarded a Pounds 19m contract by Nottinghamshire County Council for the construction of a magistrates court, an office block and a bridewell in central Nottingham. The project is due for completion in spring 1995. The courthouse building will be five storeys high, comprising three towers around a central glazed atrium with a flat roofed single-storey court holding area and two-storey links to family courts and an office building. The gross floor area will be about 8,000 sq metres. The three-storey office building will have a two-storey link to the adult courts and a bridge link to the car park. The family courts building, also of three storeys, is linked to the adult courts and bridewell, a four-storey building of some 3,000 sq metres.
