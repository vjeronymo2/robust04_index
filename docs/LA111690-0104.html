November 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Jimmy Ward, popular right wing with the defunct Montreal Maroons of the NHL, died of cancer in Portland, Ore.

He was 84.

Ward, a 12-year NHL veteran, was a member of the Maroons' Stanley Cup champions in 1935. He retired in 1939 after suffering a shoulder injury while playing with the Canadiens.

Ward scored 274 points and had 147 goals.

A memorial service will be held Sunday at Ross Memorial in Portland. In lieu of flowers, the family asks that a donation be sent to Hospice, 6040 N. Cutter Circle, Suite 309, Portland, Ore. 97217. Hospice is an organization of visiting nurses who help terminally ill patients suffering from cancer.
