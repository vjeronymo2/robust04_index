BFN [Text] The Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat held a meeting with some members of the group entrusted with administering the self-rule in Gaza and Jericho in Tunis this evening. Palestinian sources said that more than ten members of the group participated in the unofficial meeting, and that the other 18 members concerned would arrive tomorrow in Tunis to take part in the first official meeting of the group during which they were expected to be sworn in and prepare the group's working program for the forthcoming period. Palestinian sources had previously announced that tomorrow's meeting would also be distinguished by announcing the names of the remaining members of the self-rule administration group; the Cairo agreement on implementing self-rule stipulated that it would comprise 24 members.
