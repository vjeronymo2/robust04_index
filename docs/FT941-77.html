940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Laporte disposals Laporte, specialty chemicals company, is selling two of its smaller companies, Sterling Technology and Rushden Granulating Company for Pounds 5m in cash. The combined 1993 sales and operating profits for the two companies -acquired as part of the Evode purchase - were Pounds 6.9m and Pounds 700,000.
