Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 24 KYODO -- The government signed an agreement Monday [24 January] to provide a total of 119.6 billion yen in loans to India, officials said. The loans are to fund six projects, including construction of a coal-burning power plant in western Bengal. The 2.6 percent loans are repayable over 30 years after a 10-year grace period, the officials said.
