February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Late Final Edition

NATION;

NEWSMAN HELD IN RIVAL TV BREAK-IN

Michael Shapiro, assistant news director of television station WTSP, St. Petersburg, was arrested today and charged with breaking into the newsroom computer system of rival WTVT in Tampa with his home computer.

Shapiro, a former assignments editor at WTVT, was arrested by agents of the Florida Department of Law Enforcement and booked into the Hillsborough County jail on 14 counts of computer-related crime. Bond was set at $14,000.
