920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / EC to pay for Somalia force The European Community yesterday decided to take Dollars 27m (Pounds 13.5m) from unspent development funds to pay for Belgian paratroopers who will help protect UN relief operations in Somalia, writes David Gardner in Brussels. The controversial decision, adopted despite misgivings by the UK, is intended to ensure that aid gets past local warlords and reaches the starving.
