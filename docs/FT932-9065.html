930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Chiltern Radio deficit widens Losses at Chiltern Radio rose from Pounds 179,000 to Pounds 325,000 pre-tax for the six months ended March 31. For the full year to end-September 1992 the group incurred a deficit of Pounds 392,000. The first half loss took account of an exceptional provision of Pounds 77,000 in respect of a permanent diminution in value of the company's investment in Independent Radio News. Turnover of Pounds 2.75m compared with Pounds 2.55m. Losses per share emerged at 3.4p (1.9p).
