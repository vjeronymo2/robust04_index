920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / International Company News: Alcoa plans Bahrain venture ALCOA, the world's largest producer of aluminium, and Al Zayani Investments WWL of Bahrain are to form a joint venture to manufacture plastic bottle caps for the growing Middle Eastern beverage market, writes Barbara Durr. Under the deal, to be completed next month, the venture, called Gulf Closures, will immediately begin construction of a new plant in Bahrain. The plant is scheduled for start-up in the first quarter of 1993 and will produce over 200m compression-moulded plastic closures by the end of next year. Al Zayani Investments has interests in banking, services tendering, hotels, property and light manufacturing.
