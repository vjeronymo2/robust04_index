BFN [Excerpts] The Spanish delegation, led by Foreign Minister Javier Solana, ended its visit to Lebanon after the foreign minister and his Lebanese counterpart, Faris Buwayz, met with the president, prime minister, and speaker of the Chamber of Deputies. The accompanying delegation members also concluded their talks with Foreign Ministry officials. The outlines for agreements on Lebanese-Spanish cooperation in economics and culture were formulated. [passage omitted] The Spanish delegation left Beirut Airport this afternoon after being seen off by Ambassador Zafir al-Hasan, the Spanish ambassador to Lebanon, several government and security officials, and Spanish Embassy staff members.
