Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 12 Mar -- On the border between Armenia and Azerbaijan, a meeting between Babken Ararksyan and Rasul Guliyev, the heads of parliaments of those two republics, began this afternoon. The first stage of the meeting is being held in Azerbaijan's Kazakhskiy Rayon, near the border. The meeting will subsequently be continued in Idzhevanskiy rayon in the northeast of Armenia. The talks are taking place in camera, but it is known that the heads of the legislative bodies of Armenia and Azerbaijan are discussing the problem of settling the conflict in Nagorno-Karabakh -- specifically, ways of achieving a cease-fire in the region affected by the conflict.
