920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / International Company News: Arco pulls out of joint venture ARCO Chemical, the chemicals arm of oil producer Atlantic Richfield, and Yukong, South Korea's largest oil refiner, said they have reached a preliminary agreement whereby Arco Chemical will withdraw from their joint venture, Yukong Arco Chemical, divesting its entire equity interest to Yukong, AP-DJ reports. Arco said the agreement provides that it make an equity payment to Yukong Arco and be released from all obligations to Yukong with respect to the debt of Yukong Arco. At March 31, Arco was liable for Dollars 204m. Arco said the transaction will result in a one-off charge, expected to be Dollars 56m, on second-quarter 1992 earnings.
