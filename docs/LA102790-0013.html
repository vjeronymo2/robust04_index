October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WARNING: THESE LETTERS COULD BE HAZARDOUS TO DIE-HARD A'S FANS

Jim Murray takes the fans to task for all the flak we're giving Jose Canseco.

No, Mr. Murray, Jose Canseco did not kill Santa Claus or the Easter Bunny. But you promoted him as the next Babe Ruth. Now you blame us when he turns out to be the next Baby Huey.

RAY LASKA

Los Angeles
