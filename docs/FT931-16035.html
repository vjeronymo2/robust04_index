930109 FT 09 JAN 93 / China seeks better US ties Less than two weeks before US President-elect Bill Clinton takes office, Chinese Premier Li Peng made a strong pitch yesterday to improve the nations' strained relations, reports AP from Beijing. Li told two visiting US senators from Mr Clinton's Democratic Party that Beijing attached great importance to US-China relations and wanted to expand and improve those ties. 'There is no reason for China and the US not to develop a friendly relationship of co-operation,' he said.
