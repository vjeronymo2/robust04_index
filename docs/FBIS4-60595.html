BFN [Text] The Islamic President of Iran Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani arrived in Mashhad this morning. He was welcomed at the airport by Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Va'ez-Tabasi, custodian of the Razavi holy shrine and the vali-ye faqih's representative in Khorasan; Mr. Mofidi, the governor-general; and local officials of the province. After arriving at Mashhad airport, Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani told a correspondent of the Central News Unit that the objective of this visit is to pay his respect to Imam Reza, peace be upon him, to open a consulate office of the Turkmenistan Republic in Mashhad, and to hold a series of economic talks with President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan. Simultaneous with arrival of the Islamic president of Iran in holy Mashhad, Mr. Saparmurad Niyazov also arrived at the head of a high-ranking delegation. He was welcomed at the airport by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani.
