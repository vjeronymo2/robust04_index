Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 28 (YONHAP) -- Beijing will post a military attache to its embassy in Seoul next month, a Foreign Ministry official said Friday. The Chinese Government recently called on the South Korean Government to allow a Chinese military attache to work at the Chinese Embassy here, he said. "We finished considering whether to accept the colonel-grade Chinese military attache and found no problem, so he will be able to posted here in February." Seoul's military attache began his service in Beijing on Dec. 25 last year.
