940101 FT 01 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Goldsmiths trading 'very good' The directors of Goldsmiths Group, the quality jewellery retailer, stated that trading in its 111 outlets throughout the UK for the month to December 24 was 'very good' with sales up 13 per cent compared with last year. Sales for the 10 months to that date were ahead by 11 per cent and the directors believed that results for the period ended January 29 1994 'will show a profit in excess of market expectations.'
