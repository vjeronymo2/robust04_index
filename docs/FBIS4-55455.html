BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency, has had a telephone conversation with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. Christopher called President Izetbegovic to inform him about the U.S. standpoint on the resolution of the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The United States believes the Bosnian side will not miss this opportunity for peace and that a solution can be found in the plans put forward by the international community's contact group for Bosnia-Herzegovina. President Izetbegovic reiterated our resolve to persevere with the peace process and said that the Bosnian side was very clear on this subject and that at the same time, it also demanded respect for the vital interests of Bosnia-Herzegovina as a unitary state.
