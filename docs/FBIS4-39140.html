BFN [Excerpts] Dushanbe, 4 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Lieutenant Colonel Sergey Leontikov, a Russian border guard officer, was killed this morning in a Dushanbe housing estate by unidentified criminals. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the command headquarters of the Russian border guards in Tajikistan that he was shot not far from his personal garage. An investigation has started. This is the third instance since the beginning of the week of physical reprisals against Russian servicemen. [passage omitted] An ITAR-TASS correspondent heard that the command of the group of Russian border guards in Tajikistan is to make an emergency announcement today.
