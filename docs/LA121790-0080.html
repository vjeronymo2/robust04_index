December 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

CENTER OFFERS HELP TO MALL SHOPPERS

Shoppers interested in picking up a bus schedule, fingerprinting their child or getting information on a bicycle license can do so at one place.

The city of Mission Viejo and the Orange County Sheriff's Department are sponsoring a community resource center at the Mission Viejo Mall.

The center, which will be manned six days a week by volunteers from the city's Senior Community Activity Team, is open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., Monday through Saturday, and will be on ongoing project at the mall.

Residents will be able to fingerprint their children for identification purposes and pick up an Orange County Transportation District bus schedule.
