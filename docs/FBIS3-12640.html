Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Algeria, the independent newspaper AL-KHABAR said today that it is likely that the leaders of the Islamic Salvation Front [FIS] will be released within the next few hours. The newspaper, which was quoting informed sources, said that the amnesty decision was signed some days ago and that it stipulated the release of Abassi Madani, the leader of the FIS, and his deputy Ali Belhadj, in addition to another group of prisoners.
