910425 FT 25 APR 91 / UK Company News: Net assets fall at Ldn Amer Ventures London American Ventures Trust reported net asset value down to 57.31p per share at March 31 1991, against 66.41p a year earlier. Pre-tax profits for the year were Pounds 36,000, against losses of Pounds 122,000 last time. Tax took Pounds 12,000 (Pounds 166,000 credit) and after minorities, the deficit per share came to 0.04p (0.07p earnings).
