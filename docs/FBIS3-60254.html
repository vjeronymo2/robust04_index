Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The environmental protection program of the government has received its much needed boost with the signing of an agreement to enforce a nationwide campaign to protect and preserve coastal water sites. The accord also provides for the prosecution of violators of laws covering marine areas. The agreement was signed by heads of the offices of the Executive Secretary, Defense, DILG [Department of Interior and Local Government], Justice, DENR [Department of Environment and Natural Resources], among others.
