Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation throughout the front line in the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] was tense yesterday and last night. Despite their casualties, Azerbaijan's Armed Forces made a significant effort to advance against the Armenian defense positions on various parts of the front line. Bagram Aghadzhanyan, our correspondent in Stepanakert, quotes a statement by the NKR Defense Forces that Azerbaijan's military units tried to attack Matavis and [placename indistinct] in Mardakertskiy Rayon early yesterday morning. Serious clashes took place and both sides had casualties. An effort is being made to determine the outcome of the fighting. Elsewhere, NKR units rebuffed the latest enemy attacks in Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. Six of Azerbaijan's military aircraft violated NKR airspace for 10 minutes and bombed several peaceful residential areas in Askeranskiy and Mardakertskiy Rayons yesterday.
