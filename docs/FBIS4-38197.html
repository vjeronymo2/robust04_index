BFN [Text] The prime minister left New Delhi this afternoon for Moscow on a four-day official visit. Mr. Narasimha Rao was seen off at the Delhi airport by several union ministers, the three service chiefs, and senior officials, among others. Mr. Rao is heading a high-level delegation and will have wide-ranging talks with the Russian president, Mr. Yeltsin, and other leaders. Our correspondent, Kajal Das, accompanying the prime minister, reports that bilateral trade which witnessed a slump in recent years will get a boost from this visit. Russia has already offered to set up joint ventures out of the Rupee-Ruble Repayment Fund. The high-level delegation accompanying Mr. Narasima Rao includes union ministers Mr. Pranab Mukherjee, Mr. Ghulam Nabi Azad, Mr. R.L. Bhatia, and Mr. Bhubanesh Chaturvedi, and defense and foreign secretaries.
