Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Tehran, March 5 (AFP)--The Iranian parliament on Saturday [5 March] called for a Palestinian armed struggle against Israel until the Jewish state disappeared from the face of the earth. "We declare that the problem of Zionism will only be solved through armed struggle against Israel until the cancerous tumor disappears from the map of the world," said a statement from deputies, read out in parliament. [passage omitted] On Friday Iranian spiritual guide Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i called for a massive uprising against Israel, with the backing of the Islamic world. Fathi al-Shiqaqi, head of the fundamentalist Islamic Jihad in Palestine group, vowed here Saturday that Palestinian militants would soon launch revenge attacks against Israel in retaliation for the Hebron massacre.
