Name: Council for Continental Scientific Drilling. Date and time: February 9 & 10, 1994; 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. each day. Place: Room 360; National Science Foundation; 4201 Wilson Boulevard; Arlington, VA 22230. Type of meeting: Open to the public. Persons may participate in the meeting as time and space permit. Contact: Dr. James F. Hays, Division Director, Division of Earth Sciences, room 785, National Science Foundation, Arlington, VA 22230 (703) 306&hyph;1550; and Donald W. Klick, ICG/CSD Executive Secretary, 922 National Center, U.S. Geological Survey, Reston, VA 22092, (703) 648&hyph;6346. Minutes: May be obtained from the Contact Person(s) listed above.
