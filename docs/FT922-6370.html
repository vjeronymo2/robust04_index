920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / World News in Brief: Montedison Montedison, big Italian chemical and agroindustrial concern controlled by the Ferruzzi family, reported 1991 net profits down 70 per cent to L168bn (Pounds 76m). The company blamed the drop on the fact that 1990 earnings had been boosted by the sale of its Enimont stake.
