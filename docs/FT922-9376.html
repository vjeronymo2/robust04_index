920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / International Company News: Burlington Coat Factory Warehouse third-quarter earnings of dollars 1m Burlington Coat Factory Warehouse, the clothing retailer, had third-quarter earnings of Dollars 1m, or 6 cents a share, unchanged from a year earlier. Sales rose to Dollars 217m from Dollars 209m. For the nine months ended March 28, earnings rose to Dollars 31.4m, or Dollars 1.77 a share, from Dollars 27.8m, or Dollars 1.34, in the year ago period. Sales advanced to Dollars 809m from Dollars 759.4m.
