December 18, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

P. M. BRIEFING;

BRANIFF SEEKS AIRPORT SLOTS SALE

Attorneys for bankrupt airline Braniff Inc. prepared to argue today for the emergency sale of landing and takeoff slots in Chicago and New York to American Airlines.

A hearing was scheduled this afternoon in federal bankruptcy court here.

The sale of slots at Chicago's O'Hare and New York's LaGuardia would provide Braniff an estimated $400,000, about a week's worth of cash, the airline said.

That sum, in addition to about $1.3 million on hand, would give Braniff roughly one month's operating expenses.
