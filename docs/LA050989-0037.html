May 9, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Rickey Henderson and Mel Hall of the New York Yankees had a fight before last Saturday's game at Chicago, according to the New York Daily News.

Henderson and Hall were joking in the training room before the Yankees' game against the White Sox when the incident escalated into a brawl. It was broken up by Manager Dallas Green and other players.

Hall and Henderson would not comment.
