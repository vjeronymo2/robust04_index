Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 3 (ATA) -- Some 13 tanks and 10 military trucks with Serbian officers and soldiers were deployed on March 1, 16:00 pm, in Cukat e Komoranit, near Glogovac, in Kosova, according to the Information Commission of the Glogovac Democratic League of Kosova. 200 Serbian soldiers were deployed also in the "Silcapor" factory in Kacanik as well as some tanks and heavy artillery of various sizes. According to the latest reports the Serbian soldiers are going to stay for a long time at the factory because from the beginning the Serbian officers and soldiers have been very interested in comfortable living conditions.
