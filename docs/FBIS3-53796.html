Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani has stressed the need for cooperation between Iran and Pakistan for the resolution of regional conflicts. During a meeting with visiting Pakistani Chief of Army Staff General Abdul Waheed in Tehran yesterday, the Iranian president highlighted the importance of exchanging views to find ways and means to help resolve the disputes in Afghanistan and occupied Kashmir. The Iranian president also proposed promoting bilateral economic and cultural ties and expanding mutual cooperation to ensure border security and to check narcotics smuggling. Gen. Waheed expressed support for Hashemi- Rafsanjani's proposal for mutual cooperation to ensure border security and checking narcotics smuggling.
