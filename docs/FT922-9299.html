920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Static revenue at Glasgow Income Net asset value per share of Glasgow Income Trust was 39.4p at March 31 1992, down from 47.32p at the September year-end and 46.22p at the same stage of 1991. However, it had risen to 43.1p by April 30 reflecting higher equity values following the election. Net revenue for the six months to end-March was virtually unchanged at Pounds 393,000 for earnings of 1.33p (1.36p) per share. A second interim dividend of 0.6p brings the total to date to 1.2p.
