September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

RAM GAME SELLS OUT; NO BLACKOUT

The Rams' game against the Philadelphia Eagles sold out Thursday, meaning the game will be televised by Channel 2 at 1 p.m. The announcers will be Pat Summerall and John Madden.

The Channel 4 game at 10 a.m. will be Miami at the New York Giants, with Dick Enberg and Bill Walsh reporting. The TNT game at 5 p.m. will be Detroit at Tampa Bay, with Skip Caray and Pat Haden reporting.
