921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / Azeri-Armenian clash in enclave Fighting flared in the disputed mountain territory of Nagorno-Karabakh yesterday, with Azeri and Armenian antagonists both reporting lives had been lost, Reuter reports from Moscow. The fighting in Nagorno-Karabakh centred on the vital Lachin corridor, the only overland route connecting the territory's Armenian-speaking population with neighbouring Armenia.
