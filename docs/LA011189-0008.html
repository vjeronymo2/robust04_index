January 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Actress Dawnn Lewis, who plays Jaleesa Vinson on NBC's "A Different World," is co-hosting "Dream of Freedom: The Words and Wisdom of Martin Luther King Jr.," a Bailey Broadcasting Services docudrama airing Saturday at 9:30 a.m. on KJLH-FM (102.3). ALEENE MacMINN
