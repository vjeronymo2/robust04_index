Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Liberation Front is the third party so far to announce its boycott of the national conference; the Socialist Forces Front was the first party to announce its boycott which was followed by the Workers Party [WP], which is led by Mrs. Louisa Hannoun, whose Central Committee has decided, following an emergency meeting held during the last two days, to boycott the national conference. In a statement received today by the Algerian Press Service, Mrs. Louisa Hannoun, WP's leader, pointed out that her party refuses to take part in the conference because the state of emergency still exists, political prisoners continue to be detained, and democratic freedoms continue to be absent.
