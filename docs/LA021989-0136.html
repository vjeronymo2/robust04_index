February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FAMILIAR PARIS

Always saving the Travel Section for last, what an extra treat to see the wonderful picture of Paris (Jerry Hulse, Feb. 5). I noticed that he took the beautiful photograph of the Seine and Notre Dame from Pont de la Tournelle. At that exact location I was married aboard a 60-foot cruise boat with Notre Dame as a background. We lived in Paris two years.

STEVE LEONARD

South Laguna
