Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baha'-al-Din Ahmad, speaker of the Legislative Council of the Kurdistan Autonomous Region, and Ja'far 'Abd-al-Karim al-Barzanji, chairman of the Executive Council of the Kurdistan Autonomous Region, have commended the great and prominent role leader President Saddam Husayn, the architect of autonomy, has played in reaching a just and peaceful settlement of the Kurdish issue on democratic and fair terms on the basis of the doctrine, creed, and principles upheld by the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party. In statements to the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY on the 24th anniversary of the issuance of the historic 11 March statement, the two Kurdish officials added that settling the Kurdish issue and halting infighting in the Kurdistan region were only some of the significant accomplishments of the glorious 17-30 July revolution. They reiterated the Kurds' allegiance to the historic and unique leadership of leader President Saddam Husayn. They also reaffirmed their willingness to make sacrifices to safeguard Iraq's unity and thwart the enemies' schemes, which aim to undermine Iraq's sovereignty and national unity. The two Kurdish officials noted that the lawlessness, instability, insecurity, anarchy, murders, and thefts currently prevailing in the Kurdistan region are cogent proof of the false claims made by archtraitors and archagents, who have accepted subservience to their foreign masters, the enemies of Iraq. Concluding their statements, they asserted that agents and traitors will be defeated, with God's help and the determination of zealous Kurds, particularly since the zealous Kurds have always demonstrated their support for the courageous leadership of leader President Saddam Husayn.
