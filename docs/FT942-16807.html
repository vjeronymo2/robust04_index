940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Radio bidder hires lobbyist Associated Newspapers has Hired Westminster Strategy, a political lobbying group, to improve its chances of winning the licence to run Britain's third national commercial radio station. Last month Talk Radio UK bid Pounds 3.8m a year for the 'predominantly speech' licence - more than Pounds 1m above the bid by Newstalk UK, a consortium led by Associated. The licence will go to Talk Radio UK provided its business and programme plans are viable.
