940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / International Company News: Reader's Digest advances 25% Reader's Digest exceeded Wall Street's expectations by posting a 25 per cent improvement in net income in the three months to end-December, writes Frank McGurty in New York. The New York-based publishing group announced net income of Dollars 88.8m, or 76 cents a share, in its second fiscal quarter. This compared with Dollars 70.3m, or 59 cents, in the corresponding period in 1992. On an operating basis, profits were 30 per cent ahead after stripping out the adverse effect of currency fluctuations.
