BFN ["Brief commentary" by station commentator Mulatov from the "Half an Hour With Wang Xiao" program] [Text] The seven agreements between Russia and China signed during the Russian delegation's visit will strengthen and continue the equal and mutually beneficial cooperation achieved over the last few years in economics, trade, science and technology, and other fields. Setting their sights on the future [words indistinct] the two countries have decided to raise the neighborly and friendly relations between them to a new level of development -- says a report on the outcome of [words indistinct] visit to China. The document, which sums up the outcome of the visit, also says the two countries will take full advantage of their geographical closeness and make full use of the complementariness between the two countries' economies. Both Moscow and Beijing understand that the neighborly and friendly relations and the mutually beneficial cooperation between them are in line with the fundamental interests of the two countries and their peoples and are an important factor for preserving peace and stability and for ensuring development in the Asia-Pacific region as well as in the whole world. We also like to point out that the Russian delegation's visit to China was widely reported in detail by the media of Eastern and Western countires. At the beginning, some Western media doubted that Russia might have an intention to develop bilateral military technology cooperation with China. Such cooperation will be developed under the condition that the two countries fulfill their international obligations -- this should not cause anyone to be suspicions. During the talks, the two sides stressed the importance of reaching an agreement on taking measures as soon as possible to reduce their troops and armament and military trainings in disputed areas. They believed this will not only enhance mutual trust and understanding between the two countries, but also will contribute to preserving peace and stability in the Asia-Pacific region. During their talks, Chernomyrdin and Li Peng also discussed international issues of common interest. The two countries' identical and similar stands on some major international issues were confirmed. This will be the foundation for the two countries to strengthen their constructive cooperation in regional and world affairs. In short, the dialogue between the two governments has yielded good results. Currently, the two sides are preparing for Chinese President Jiang Zemin's visit to Russia. The two countries believe that positive dialogues at all levels, including the highest level, are prerequisites for promoting neighborly and friendly relations and mutually beneficial cooperation between them.
