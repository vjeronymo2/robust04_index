BFN [Text] Pristina, June 12 (ATA) -- The Serbian Government removed 4 from 5 so called "District chiefs" of the Serbian leadership in Kosova, Behlul Jashari, ATA correspondent in Pristina reported today. As it was reported the changes has been proposed by the Socialist Party of Serbia, (for Kosova). Satisfying the desire of Socialists, the Belgrade government nominated Zhivorad Igic as ambassador of the so called Federal Republic of Yugoslavia in Albania. For public opinion in Kosova and broader, Igic is known to be anti-Albanian. In regard to the nomination of Igic, the BUJKU newspaper of Pristina reacted on June 11 with a commentary entitled "Fresh Belgrade Provocation Against Tirana". The commentary says that "the nomination of Zhivorad Igic as ambassador of the self-proclaimed Yugoslavia in Tirana who is one of the main responsible persons for the apartheid in Kosova is a witness of the continuation of Belgrade's anti-Albanian policy.
