BFN [Text] Moscow April 11 TASS - Russia suggested on Monday that a contingent of UN peacekeepers be sent urgently to Gorazde. The Foreign Ministry also called in a statement to withdraw Serb troops from the besieged Moslem enclave and for "the demilitarization of the safety zone, including the disarming of Moslem units acting in the area". "We see the task of the UN Security Council in actively promoting these goals", the statement said.
