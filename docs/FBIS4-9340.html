BFN [Report by Nada al-Isa from Zagreb] [Text] Returning from Washington via Brussels, Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency member Ejup Ganic today stopped in Zagreb. He used this stopover in Zagreb for political talks with the representatives of the Croatian leadership. He first held talks with Croatian Foreign Minister and deputy Prime Minister Mate Granic, while tonight he is due to meet with Croatian President Franjo Tudjman. The topic will be an exchange of opinions on the latest political situation after the tragedy in Gorazde, and the decisions of the U.S. Administration for wider involvement in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Washington Agreement was also discussed, particularly ways of implementing it and in connection to this, future measures of the international community aimed at reaching lasting peace in Bosnia-Herzegovina and Croatia. This has been Nada al-Isa reporting from Zagreb for Radio Television Bosnia-Herzegovina.
