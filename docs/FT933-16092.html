930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Exhibition centre in record profit NATIONAL Exhibition Centre in Birmingham made record net profits of Pounds 11.64m in the year to March 1993, it was announced yesterday. This compared with Pounds 2.98m the previous year. The profits go to Birmingham City Council, NEC's owner, and help to offset the losses on the International Convention Centre and National Indoor Arena.
