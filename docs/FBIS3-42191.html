Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Guatemala Flash" newscast] [Text] Alleged URNG [Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity] cells downed and destroyed three power transmission towers last night. This happened in Las Ilusiones, Zone 18, Villa del Dorado, Zone 8, and the Ulises Rojas neighborhood at the 21-km marker on the Pacific highway, Villa Nueva Jurisdiction. Powerful charges downed the towers, causing a blackout in Guatemala City and the interior of the country. The blast hit Lirio Bolanos Rosas, a National Police employee of the National Police, on Zone 18, and had to be hospitalized. Last night and early this morning, technicians of the electricity company and INDE [National Institute of Electrification] estimated the damage caused at 500,000 quetzales. They did not make estimates on the serious damage the blackout caused to homes, hospitals, clinics, and computer centers, as well as to citizens who were the victims of crimes perpetrated under cover of the hour-long blackout in Guatemala City. The bomb squad defused a powerful device in the Ulises Rojas Neighborhood.
