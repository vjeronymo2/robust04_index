921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / International Company News: Coca-Cola goes ahead to Dollars 540m COCA-COLA, the US soft drinks company, yesterday posted strong third-quarter earnings with an 18.5 per cent rise in after-tax profits, to Dollars 540.6m, and a 20.6 per cent improvement in earnings per share, at 41 cents. Sales were up 10.6 per cent at Dollars 3.51bn. For the first nine months of the year, after-tax profits were Dollars 1.5bn, 19.5 per cent up on the Dollars 1.26bn in the same period of 1991.
