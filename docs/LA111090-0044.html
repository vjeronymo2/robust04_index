November 10, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

RAMS MAY BE BAD, BUT CAN EDDIE DOUCETTE CALL A DECENT GAME?

The only thing worse than watching the Rams this year is listening to Eddie Doucette call the play by play.

Is it just us or does anyone have a clue as to what's happening on the field when Eddie is calling the game? He is a disgrace. An NFL franchise in a major market such as Los Angeles should have competent announcers.

KMPC must have the same philosophy as do the Rams and John Shaw, find the cheapest guy available.

BRUCE DUDLEY, PHIL JELSMA and STEVE YOUNG, San Diego
