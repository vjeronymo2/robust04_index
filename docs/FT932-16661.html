930405 FT 05 APR 93 / Call to encourage private schools THE GOVERNMENT should pay the private sector to provide education through a new type of publicly regulated school, the Social Market Foundation, an independent think-tank, says. It recommends that all state schools should become grant-maintained then the private sector should be invited to create new schools financed by the same size of grant. Schools and the State.
