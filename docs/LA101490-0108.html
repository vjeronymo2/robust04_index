October 14, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

In other Mission Conference games:

Rancho Santiago 24, Pasadena 21 -- Rich Fanti completed 11 of 19 passes for 166 yards and threw for two touchdowns to lead Rancho Santiago (3-2, 2-2).

Orange Coast 22, Southwestern 17 -- Greg Angelovic completed 17 of 32 passes for 280 yards and two touchdowns and Mike Wade kicked three field goals to lead host Orange Coast (4-1, 3-1). Keith Labus had seven receptions for 155 yards, including a 70-yard third-quarter score that was Orange Coast's longest play from scrimmage this season. Southwestern is 1-5, 0-4.

Saddleback 37, San Diego City 2 -- Matt Carvalho rushed for 121 yards in 17 carries and scored twice to lead host Saddleback (3-2, 3-1) to its third consecutive victory.
