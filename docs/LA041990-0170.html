April 19, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: WEST COVINA;

COUNCIL NAMES NEW MAYOR

The City Council on Tuesday voted to elect William Tarozzi mayor and Bradley McFadden mayor pro tem. Both offices carry one-year terms.

Tarozzi replaces retired Mayor Robert Bacon, and McFadden replaces Dick Lewis, who was unseated in the April 10 election.
