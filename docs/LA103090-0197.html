October 30, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

CAPRIATI RANKED 10TH IN WOMEN'S TENNIS

Jennifer Capriati cracked the top 10 rankings in women's tennis by winning the Puerto Rico Open.

Capriati, seeded second, defeated top-seeded Zina Garrison 5-7, 6-4, 6-2 on Sunday to take the $150,000 event, her first victory since turning pro in March.

The victory boosted her to No. 10 in the world in the latest rankings.

At 14 years, 6 months, 29 days, she became the third-youngest woman to win a tennis tournament. The youngest was Tracy Austin, who was 14 years, 1 month, 4 days when she won at Portland in January, 1977.
