930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Explaura Explaura Holdings, the USM-quoted company which quarries limestone aggregates in Newfoundland, showed an increased loss of Pounds 2.37m for 1992, compared with Pounds 2.11m. From turnover of Pounds 1.56m (Pounds 1.92m) operating profit was Pounds 517,000 (Pounds 263,000). A reduction in costs and expenses was outweighed by an exceptional Pounds 774,000 bad debt arising on the cessation of New York distributorship. Losses per share were 1.61p (1.66p). Inco, the principal shareholder, continued to give assistance. On the financial side it had made available a facility of up to Dollars 450,000 (Pounds 300,000) to continue operations. Royal Bank of Canada agreed to defer payments of principal and interest during the reorganisation.
