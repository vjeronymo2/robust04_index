920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UN sends mission to Nagorno-Karabakh The UN Security Council yesterday approved urgent dispatch of a fact-finding mission to study the conflict between Armenia and Azerbaijan over the enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh, Reuter reports. Mr Francesc Vendrell, a Spanish UN official, is expected to head the team. Mr Cyrus Vance headed a similar mission in March.
