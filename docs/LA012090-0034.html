January 20, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN VOLLEYBALL TEAM RALLIES TO POST VICTORY

The slow-starting Cal State Northridge volleyball team needed another rally to post its second consecutive win, defeating San Diego State, 6-15, 15-17, 15-7, 15-13, 15-11, in a nonconference match.

Neil Coffman led the visiting Matadors (2-1) with 30 kills, and Raphael Tulino and Coley Kyman each had 20 kills. Doug Potter led the Aztecs (0-1) with 19 kills.
