September 16, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Paraclete 13, Rosamond 0 -- Paraclete (2-0) recovered two fumbles, made two interceptions and limited Rosamond to 119 yards in total offense as the Spirits won on the road. Tim Salado scored on an eight-yard interception return in the first quarter. Quarterback Chuck Lang capped a 93-yard, second-quarter drive with a four-yard run.
