Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Mar (MENA)--A huge peaceful march was staged in Cairo this evening by thousands of youths and women. The participants condemned the Israeli actions in the occupied territories and the bestial massacre of innocent Palestinians in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. Thousands of worshippers came out of the 'Amr-Ibn-al-'Ass Mosque after evening prayers and joined the march, which proceeded to the al-Rawdah and al-Manyal areas chanting slogans and carrying placards condemning that abominable crime. Police vehicles escorted the peaceful march to protect it.
