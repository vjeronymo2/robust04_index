March 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Einstein Footage Found: Nearly 4,000 feet of rare film on Albert Einstein that was reported missing after it fell out of a Federal Express delivery truck more than a week ago was returned to the University of South Carolina Wednesday. The films and other missing packages were returned by a Columbia, S.C., bail bondsman who apparently was given the parcels by a man who picked them up shortly after they fell off the truck.

The films had been on their way from their home at the university to be copied for a French film maker. A university spokesman said Federal Express will get another chance to transport the films, which have historical value to researchers and historians, to their New Jersey destination. SHAUNA SNOW
