941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / Strike plans at TSB postponed Bifu, the finance union, has postponed an announcement on dates for a series of strikes over pay at TSB to allow last-minute talks with management at Acas, the conciliation service, tomorrow. The two sides are to try and resolve the dispute over an imposed 2 per cent pay rise and plans to for performance-related pay. Bifu has called for a 6 per cent pay increase from TSB. Members voted last month by 3,073 to 2,980 to hold a series of selective strikes.
