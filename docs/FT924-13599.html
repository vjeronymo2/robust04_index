921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / UK Company News: TJ Hughes pays first dividend TJ HUGHES, the Liverpool-based discount retailer, reported a 25 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for the 26 weeks to July 25. Turnover rose 51 per cent but on a like-for-like basis, excluding new openings, the rise was 13 per cent. The company, which came to the USM in May this year, is paying a maiden dividend of 0.75p from earnings per share of 0.16p (losses of 0.11p). On turnover of Pounds 17.8m (Pounds 11.8m) profits were Pounds 143,000 (Pounds 114,000). In the second half a new store has been opened in Manchester bringing total space to 292,420 sq ft, an increase of 45,860 sq ft during the year. LET, the property and development division of SPP, the Swedish mutual life company, holds a 45 per cent stake.
