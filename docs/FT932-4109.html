930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / International Company News: Peak trading for US banks THE Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation reported that US commercial banks earned record profits of Dollars 10.9bn in the first quarter, Dollars 3.3bn more than in the same period last year, writes Damian Fraser. Only one out of 20 banks was unprofitable, and the prognosis for the near future is excellent, said the Washington-based organisation.
