Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 27 Jan -- With the coming into force of the Constitution of the Russian Federation and for the purposes of regulating the activity of commissions and councils under the Russian Federation president, Boris Yeltsin has signed an edict "On Established State Posts in Commissions and Councils Under the Russian Federation President". The edict establishes that work in commissions and councils under the Russian Federation president is done on a voluntary basis and does not represent federal state service. Established state posts in these commissions and councils can be introduced by decision by the Russian Federation president. The edict comes into force on publication. This was reported by the press service of the Russian Federation president.
