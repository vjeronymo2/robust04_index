BFN [Text] Sanaa, 27 Apr (MENA)--Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has received a message from Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak urging the Yemeni parties to stop the clashes and avoid a military confrontation to prevent shedding Yemeni blood. The message was delivered by Egyptian Ambassador in Sanaa 'Ata Mahmud Harun when the Yemeni president received him this evening. President Mubarak called for dialogue among the parties to the conflict and for a halt to violence to preserve Yemeni unity. The Egyptian ambassador also telephoned Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid this evening to convey a similar message from President Mubarak. [Cairo MENA in Arabic at 2052 GMT on 27 April, in another Sanaa-datelined item, reports that the Yemeni foreign minister received a similar message from Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa delivered by the Egyptian ambassador in Sanaa.]
