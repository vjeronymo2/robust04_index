BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri told reporters that he received a phone call from Cambodian Foreign Minister Samdech Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut this morning to inform him that the Cambodian Government plans to organize a roundtable meeting with representatives of all Cambodian factions on 2 May. The Cambodian foreign minister said that the Cambodian Government, with its intention to clear up any skepticism regarding Thailand and create mutual better understanding between the two countries, one of the issues to be raised for discussion during the meeting will be the alleged Thai assistance to the Khmer Rouge. Touching on the plight of some 1,000 displaced Cambodians who fled the fighting in Cambodia to take refuge in Ta Phraya District of Sakeo Province, the Cambodian foreign minister said that Cambodian officials in collaboration with the Office of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees will take care of them. In this regard, Squadron Prasong Sunsiri informed his Cambodian counterpart that Thailand has not pushed any displaced people out of the country. However, if they return to Cambodia, they do so on a voluntary basis.
