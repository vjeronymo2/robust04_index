940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Black pepper prices continued firm in Europe while white pepper's stabilised, reports Man Producten. The black ASTA grades were steady in most origins, like Indonesia, India, Malaysia and Brazil. Prices for the faq grades again went up considerabely. Malaysia and Vietnam were out of the market as local prices were very high. In the US spot black pepper remained at 86 cents a pound; June-July shipment was at 85 cents. In Europe the faq grade increased further to Dollars 1,700 a tonne. Grade 1 was traded at Dollars 1,775 a tonne, spot Rotterdam. White pepper prices improved slightly in Europe. Muntok faq spot Rotterdam was at Dollars 2,950 a tonne, with June/July shipment at Dollars 2,875, cif. Markets for other spices were generally unchanged.
