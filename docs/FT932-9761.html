930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / International Company News: James Crean James Crean, the Dublin - based industrial holding company, suffered a pre-tax loss of IPounds 15.4m (Pounds 15.1m) for the 1992 year reflecting a IPounds 30.7m total write-off of its investment in IAS, an aircraft leasing subsidiary in which it first invested in 1986. Pre-tax profits for 1991 amounted to IPounds 11.4m. A final dividend of 4.635p is recommended for a total of 12.5p (18.34p).
