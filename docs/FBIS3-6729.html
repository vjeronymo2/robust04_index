Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN ["Battle Report from Pailin Battlefield on 16 and 17 March"] [Text] 1. On 16 March, troops of the two-headed government continued to proceed along Route 10, Phnum Veng, and Kamping Puoy. On Route 10 and south of the Phnum Veng fronts, they were routed by our forces; over 20 bodies were left on the battlefield and a number of other soldiers were wounded. On the Kamping Puoy front, they were also routed by our forces; over 10 bodies were left on the battlefield and some other soldiers were wounded. 2. On 17 March, on the Phnum Veng front, we routed and destroyed two T-54 tanks on the spot.
