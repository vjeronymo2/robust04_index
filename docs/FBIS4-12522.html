Success JN0804122594 Sanaa Voice of Palestine in Arabic 1800 GMT 7 Apr 94 JN0804122594 Sanaa Voice of Palestine Arabic BFN [Text] Brother Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO's Political Department, has said that he explored with top officials in Hanoi prospects for Vietnam helping in the effort to rehabilitate the Palestinian economy and infrastructure, which has been destroyed by the Israelis. Qaddumi was speaking at a news conference shortly before departing Ho Chi Minh City airport at the conclusion of a three-day visit yesterday. Qaddumi reached agreements with top Vietnamese officials about economic cooperation. Vietnam, he added, can help in the area of technical expertise and personnel training. Asked to evaluate the chances of the ongoing talks between Palestinians and Israelis in Cairo, brother Qaddumi said he was neither optimistic nor pessimistic. Calling the talks tough, he said the Israelis are delaying [words indistinct] withdrawal from the occupied Palestinian territories.
