Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (December 31) TURAN -- In line with the President Aliyev's decree, since January 1 1994 only Azerbaijan's national currency manat will have purchasing power of money on the territory of the country. Ministries, concerns, unions and enterprises irrespective of form property are ordered to transfer all financial operation to manats. If the enterprises have roubles on their accounts, the Banks must exchange roubles in manats (correspondingly 1 manat amounts to 10 roubles). Bank-notes with value 1, 3, 5, and 10 roubles issued in 1961- 1992 can be exchanged in manats during December 31 (1 manat 10 roubles).
