930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Mitie With all areas of activity increasing both turnover and customer base, Mitie, the building services group, was able to record an improvement in annual pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.81m to Pounds 2.4m. Turnover of continuing operations rose from Pounds 52.3m to Pounds 73m over the year to March 31. Earnings advanced to 13.3p (11.6p) and a final dividend of 2p makes a 3.5p (2.75p) total.
