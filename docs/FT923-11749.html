920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: M and S takes toehold in east Europe store venture Marks and Spencer, the international clothing and food group, is moving into eastern Europe with the opening of a pilot store in a prominent part of Budapest, Hungary. M and S will run the store with Demexco Handelsgesellschaft, a Vienna-based trading company, and S Modell, an established Hungarian retailer. It will supply a range of men's and women's wear and support and train the staff, although the venture will be managed by Modell. M and S said: 'This is a tentative toe in the water.
