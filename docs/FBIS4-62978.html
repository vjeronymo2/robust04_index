BFN [Text] Tbilisi May 18 TASS -- The headquarters of the Russian peace-keeping troops in Tskhinvali, South Ossetia, was attacked last night, the press service of the Russian Defence Ministry quoted reports from the headquarters as saying on Wednesday. A group of about 20 people attacked the headquarters at 3 a.m., using a grenade launcher, a large-caliber machine-gun and sub-machine guns. The assailants arrived in three UAZ jeeps. The attackers had to retreat to the village of Tzunari after Russian soldiers fired in retaliation. No casualties or damage were reported.
