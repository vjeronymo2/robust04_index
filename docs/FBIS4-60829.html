BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow 1 May -- The Russian foreign minister and the American secretary of state intend to meet on May fourth in Cairo, where they are due to arrive as co-sponsors at the signing ceremony of the peace agreement between Israel and the PLO on Jericho and the Gaza Strip. Andrey Kozyrev and Warren Christopher set up their meeting during a telephone conversation on Sunday. The Russian Foreign Ministry press department reported that the conversation was held upon the initiative of the American diplomat.
