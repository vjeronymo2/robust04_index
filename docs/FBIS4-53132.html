BFN [Text] Yunnan Governor He Zhiqiang of China and his delegation, accompanied by Interior Minister General Chawalit Yongchaiyut, who is concurrently president of the Thai-Chinese Culture and Economics Association, paid a courtesy call on Prime Minister Chuan Likphai at Government House today. The Yunnan governor is leading a delegation which also comprises private businessmen on a visit to Thailand for talks on investment cooperation. During the talks, the two sides discussed cooperation in various fields, including joint investment in infrastructure projects, especially land and water transportation projects, to help link and develop the quadrangle economic zone.
