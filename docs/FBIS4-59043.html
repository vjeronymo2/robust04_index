BFN [Text] Tehran, May 7, IRNA -- According to an official of the Ministry of Guidance only the journals that follow the strategy of the Islamic system will be supported. The managing director for the domestic press section of Culture and Islamic Guidance Ministry Mojtaba Hamedani told IRNA here Friday that the press should take into consideration objectives of the system, press duties, existing realities and the demands of the majority of the people if they claimed they were concerned over the cultural growth of the country. "The press and publications acting in accordance with the law will be supported," Hamedani said. He added that criticism and discussing practical strategies to solve the problems was the main raison d'etre of the press. He also added that there would be some facilities in the future for the press such as a press house, press club and other facilities for journalists.
