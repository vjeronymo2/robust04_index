941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / International Company News: Indosat shares rise on Jakarta exchange debut Shares in Indosat, the Indonesian state-owned satellite telecommunications company, made their debut on the Jakarta Stock Exchange yesterday after a listing of American Depositary Shares in New York earlier this week, writes Manuela Saragosa in Jakarta. Indosat shares ended the day in Jakarta at Rp8,475, up from the initial price of Rp7,000. The ADSs, equal to 10 Indosat shares, closed in New York at Dollars 39, up Dollars 1.88 on the day and up from the initial price of Dollars 32.05.
