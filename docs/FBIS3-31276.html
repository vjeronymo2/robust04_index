Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Vitaliy Churkin, special envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, departed Zagreb for Sarajevo this morning. He is expected to discuss the end of the siege of Sarajevo and its demilitarization with Alija Izetbegovic and top officials of the Muslim authorities in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Churkin will return to Zagreb this afternoon. He is expected to meet officials from the Croatian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and to hold a news conference.
