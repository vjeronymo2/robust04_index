Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nicosia, Jan 14 (CNA)--The UN Force in Cyprus (UNFICYP) was not aware of any shooting Thursday [13 January] along the buffer zone in this war-divided island its spokesman, Waldemar Rokoszewski told CYPRUS NEWS AGENCY Friday. Turkish Cypriot newspapers and radio station Bayrak, controlled by the illegal regime in the Turkish-occupied part of the island, reported that ten shots were fired from two different directions some 150 metres into the occupied area on Thursday. Asked to comment on the reports Rokoszewski said "UNFICYP, which mans positions round the clock in Nicosia, as elsewhere along the buffer zone, is not aware of any shooting having taken place across the buffer zone in the Nicosia area on 13 January."
