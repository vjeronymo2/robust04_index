BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow April 14 TASS -- As a result of consultations among delegation members and experts, the joint meeting of the Council of CIS Foreign Ministers managed to agree upon a draft declaration on sovereignty, territorial integrity and inviolability of borders of CIS member-countries. Insignificant differences were settled in the course of discussion. The Armenian delegation is most likely to refrain from signing the declaration. A draft convention for ensuring the rights of national minorities was subjected to more substantial criticism. Participants in the meeting decided to send the document for reconsideration by experts.
