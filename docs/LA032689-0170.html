March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Lawrence Taylor, the New York Giants' All-Pro linebacker, had no drugs in his system when he was arrested on an alcohol-related charge, police said.

"There were no drugs," state police Capt. Thomas B. Gallagher said in reporting on the results of a urine test at the New Jersey State Police laboratory in Little Falls.

The sample was taken after Taylor was arrested early Friday and charged with driving under the influence of alcohol after police found him sleeping behind the wheel of his Jeep on the shoulder of the Garden State Parkway.
