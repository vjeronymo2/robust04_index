BFN [From the "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] Attention: The first problem of the Fourth Ibero-American Summit in Cartagena arose when terrorists blew up the Caracol and Radio Cadena Nacional [RCN] repeater stations. The attack, for which no group has claimed responsibility, destroyed the stations' tuning boxes and insulators. Caracol is using special emergency frequencies and mobile telephones to cover the presidential summit.
