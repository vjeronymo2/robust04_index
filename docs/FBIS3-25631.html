Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] has intensified offensive actions throughout the country, creating a bleak picture among the people. Two days ago, UNITA ambushed three civilian vehicles between Maria Teresa and Zenza do Itombe, killing five civilians. Sources contacted today by Luanda Radio revealed that the UNITA offensive actions are mainly directed against civilian targets in the country's central, southern, and northeastern regions.
