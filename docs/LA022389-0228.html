February 23, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

SARHOOB, FORLITANO LEAD ARCADIA FIELD

A field of nine headed by Sarhoob and Forlitano was entered today to run in Saturday's $100,000-added Arcadia Handicap at Santa Anita.

Sarhoob will be ridden by Chris McCarron and was assigned high weight of 120 pounds for the 30th running of the one-mile race for 4-year-olds and up. Forlitano will be ridden by Patrick Valenzuela and was assigned 118 pounds.

If all nine entries go to the post, the total purse will be $111,700 and the winner will earn $66,700.
