January 30, 1989, Monday, Late Final Edition

4 KILLED IN STORE THOUGHT TO BE DRUG BUSINESS FRONT

Three men and a woman were killed Sunday in a grocery store in a drug-related attack by one or more assailants who shot them and slashed their throats, police said. The grocery store, which was littered with drug paraphernalia, appeared to be a front for a drug operation, police said. They said they had no suspects.
