920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Dev net assets down 5% A 5 per cent decline in net asset value was reported by Kleinwort Development Fund. The figure fell from 337.93p to 320.89p per share over the 12 months to July 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 825,045, up from Pounds 718,762, for earnings of 13.64p (11.88p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 7.75p brings the total for the year to 10.5p (9.5p).
