930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / International Company News: BNP names sale managers BANQUE Nationale de Paris has listed the financial institutions which will manage the sale of the government's stake. BNP itself will co-ordinate and lead-manage the French public offering. Lazard Freres will act as adviser to the government and joint lead. Co-leads will be Credit Agricole, Caisse des Depots et Consignations, Credit Lyonnais and Societe Generale. The French institutional offering will comprise the same groups plus Banque Indosuez, Banque Paribas and Credit Commercial. The UK issue will be led by BNP Capital Markets with eight European co-managers. The US sale will be led by Merrill Lynch, with Lazard Freres, Goldman Sachs and JP Morgan Securities as co-leads. Outside France, the UK and the US, BNP will lead, Lazard Freres will be joint lead and senior co-lead will be Dresdner Bank of Germany. Co-leads will also include Morgan Stanley International and Goldman Sachs International.
