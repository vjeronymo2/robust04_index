BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Vukolov] [Text] Stockholm 20 April TASS -- The Russian president's special envoy for Nagorno-Karabakh Vladimir Kazimirov and head of the CSCE Minsk group for Nagorno-Karabakh Jan Eliasson held here consultations on Karabakh settlement on 19-20 April. The two officials exchanged information on the high-level talks and contacts over Nagorno-Karabakh settlement during the recent Moscow meeting of the Council of CIS Heads of State, as well as on the meeting of the CSCE Minsk group in Prague. Participants in the consultations also exchanged views on possible further steps by the Russian mediator and the CSCE Minsk group in the Karabakh settlement and around it. They stressed the importance of the declaration on Karabakh settlement signed in Moscow by the Council of CIS Heads of State. Both sides confirmed their commitment to coordinated and harmonized mediation efforts. Russia's special envoy and the CSCE official agreed to maintain close contacts between the Russian mediation mission and the Swedish presidium of the CSCE Minsk group. Kazimirov was received by Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha Af Ugglas.
