BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- FRG Chancellor Helmut Kohl has stressed Germany's vital interest in further reforms in Russia. On Thursday [16 June] during a meeting with Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the Russian State Duma, the chancellor paid tribute to his balancing role in Russian politics. According to Government Spokesman Dieter Vogel, both agreed that the relations between Bonn and Moscow are free from basic and unsolvable problems. At the beginning of his four-day visit to Germany, Rybkin, who is close to the communist agriculture party in the State Duma, also met with President Richard von Weizsaecker and Bundestag President Rita Suessmuth. In addition, talks with Cabinet members and the chairmen of the Bundestag groups were scheduled. Rybkin wants to visit Cologne and Berlin over the weekend.
