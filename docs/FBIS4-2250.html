BFN [Text] Beijing, 8 Apr (XINHUA) -- A concerned department of the State Council today issued a circular on readjusting the plan for launching the first "Fengyun 2" satellite. The text of the circular is as follows: China originally planned to use its Long-March 3 carrier rocket to launch the first "Fengyun 2" stationary meteorological satellite at the Xichang Satellite Launching Center in the near future. But, because an accidental event took place in the process of testing the satellite in the technical zone, damaging the satellite and workshops, the launching plan has been readjusted. China will make great efforts to complete the follow-up work to ensure the completion of other satellite launching plans for this year.
