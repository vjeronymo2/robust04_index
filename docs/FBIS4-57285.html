BFN [From the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] The PRD was harshly criticized when its presidential candidate Cuauhtemoc Cardenas Solorzano met yesterday with the general command of the EZLN in Guadalupe Tepeyac, Chiapas State. Subcommander Marcos said that a better Mexico might surface if EZLN demands are taken into consideration. [Begin Subcommander Marcos recording] We want to thank all the men, women, children, and old folks, who got together to receive and feed Mr. Cardenas Solorzano and the persons accompanying him. These children, old folks, women, and men are the true support of our EZLN, its [words indistinct]. If any government were to be based on them, it would be a good government. [end recording] That is what Subcommander Marcos said. In addition, he told Cuauhtemoc Cardenas that the PRD is not a democratic party. Meanwhile, Diego Fernandez de Cevallos, the presidential candidate from the National Action Party, ruled out any possibility of meeting with Subcommander Marcos. Diego Fernandez de Cevallos says he cannot meet with a person who covers his face, even though his reasons may be justified.
