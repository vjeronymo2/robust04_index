Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Grachev] [Text] Vladivostok February 7 ITAR-TASS -- A gang of Azeris suspected of involvement in a variety of crimes was liquidated last Friday by the local department in charge of combatting drug trafficking. "We planned the operation quite thoroughly to capture all members of the gang in one week," said Vladimir Kolosov, chief of the Internal Affairs Department of the Maritime Territory. "Ten chieftains of the Azeri diaspora were detained, including one criminal authority who controlled the fund which provided assistance to criminals and their families." One of the chieftains had on him raw opium and syringes, was armed and carried 2,640,000 roubles and 900 U.S. dollars in his wallet. Forty Caucasians residing in Vladivostok without proper registration were also detained. There was no shooting or violence in the course of the operation.
