940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Baillie Gifford Shin Nippon Baillie Gifford Shin Nippon lifted its net asset value by 46 per cent from 123.1p to 179.6p over the 12 months to January 31. The investment trust, which seeks long term capital growth through a portfolio of small Japanese companies, reported a net deficit of Pounds 132,577 (Pounds 110,971), leaving losses per share at 0.83p (0.69p).
