940402 FT 02 APR 94 / UK Company News: Murray Ventures Murray Ventures, the investment trust, had a net asset value per share of 411p at January 31 against 312p a year earlier and 346.6p at the July 31 year end. Net revenue for the six months to the end of January increased from Pounds 1.7m to Pounds 2.03m for earnings per share of 8.18p (6.87p). The interim dividend is raised from 3.4p to 3.5p.
