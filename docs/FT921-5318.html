920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Butte Mining Butte Mining, the London-listed gold mining company, reported a Pounds 52,000 pre-tax profit for the six months to end-December against a Pounds 931,000 loss. Turnover was Pounds 5.34m (Pounds 565,000). The profit included Pounds 1.38m from the closure of some gold forward sales agreements and gold loans by the VAM subsidiary in Australia. Minorities took Pounds 255,000 (nil) leaving losses of 0.1p a share (0.8p). The company also announced it had hired Deutsch & Frey, a New York contingent fee law firm, to represent it in potential suits for damages in connection with its property in Butte, Montana, which is now shut down.
