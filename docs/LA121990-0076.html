December 19, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

FISH REPORT: LAKE PYRAMID STRIPERS BITING INSIDE MARINA

Fishermen at Lake Pyramid are finding it unnecessary to explore the picturesque coves and coastline as striped bass continue to feed on shad and small trout right inside the marina.

Kent Morgan of Canyon Country caught a 29-pound striper just beyond the boat dock in the Los Angeles County reservoir. Bob Hanley of Westchester, fishing the same area, caught three stripers averaging between 12-15 pounds.
