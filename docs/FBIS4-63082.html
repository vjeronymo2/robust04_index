BFN [Text] A delegation from Bulgaria led by Kadir Kadir, deputy chairman of the National Assembly, arrives on a one day working visit in Bishkek tomorrow. During the visit the Bulgarian delegation is scheduled to meet with members of the Kyrgyz Government and the Foreign Ministry and discuss the development of Kyrgyz-Bulgarian relations and the strengthening of ties between the two countries. At the government building a ceremony to hand over a consignment of humanitarian aid brought from Bulgaria for the population of the areas which suffered from the natural calamity will take place.
