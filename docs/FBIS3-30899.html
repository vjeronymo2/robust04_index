Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Hungary and Ukraine will cooperate in the Partnership for Peace program. The defense ministers of the two countries have agreed on this at their meeting in Budapest. Army General Vitaliy Hryhororovich Radetskyy, speaking at a press briefing, said that they also discussed regional cooperation from which they do not want to exclude Russia. They also decided that a committee of experts would be set up to examine the possibility of military industrial cooperation. Hungary is interested primarily in obtaining parts for aircraft, tanks, and armored combat vehicles.
