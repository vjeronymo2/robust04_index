Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 5. (SNARK) -- The striving of the peace making forces to achieve the coordinated solution to the Karabakh conflict settlement was highly appreciated by Vahan Papazyan, the Foreign Minister of Armenia. In Papazyan's opinion despite the independent attempts of Russia and CSCE to stop bloodshed in the region, up-to-date reality dictates the necessity of convergency of both parties' positions, and it will lead to the more noticeable results in the conflict settlement. "The Armenian diplomacy makes all efforts in this direction, and today's visit of Jan Eliasson, the chairman of the CSCE's Minsk Group to Armenia mainly will be connected with the question of convergency the Russia's and CSCE's positions in Karabakh problem'' Papazyan said.
