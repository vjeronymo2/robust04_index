BFN [Text] Beijing, April 30 (XINHUA) -- China is ready to enhance economic cooperation with German businessmen, Beijing Mayor Li Qiyan said today while meeting with Dr. Hans-Dietrich Winkhaus, chief executive officer of Henkel KGAA, a leading German manufacturer of chemicals. Li said that the German firm is welcome to invest in Beijing, especially since Germany is an economically developed country and possesses advanced technological know-how. A memorandum has just been signed between the firm and Beijing to set up an enterprise, with an investment of eight million U.S. dollars from the German side, to produce glue and chemicals for civilian construction use. As the world's leading manufacturer of chemicals, the firm's volume of business for 1993 reached 13.4 billion U.S. dollars. So far it has set up six joint ventures in China's Shanghai, Guangzhou and Tianjin cities.
