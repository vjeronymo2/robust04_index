June 4, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

DISPUTE ON HOTEL TAB BRINGS ARREST OF THEFT SUSPECTS

A man and woman who refused to pay a hotel bill were jailed Friday on charges of robbing a Milwaukee bank.

The two were arrested after police found a bag containing "a substantial amount" of money, some of it tainted with a red dye banks use to identify stolen money.

Karen Ray and Rufus Patterson had refused to pay their bill at the Quality Inn, so employees called police.
