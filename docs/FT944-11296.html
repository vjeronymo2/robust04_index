941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Company News (this Week): Sharp recovery for insurance sector British Airways - Other Companies Pre-tax profits of between Pounds 210m and Pounds 220m in the second quarter are forecast, up from Pounds 172m last year. Analysts expect the core airline business to perform well. The performance of its US partner USAir, BA's French affiliate TAT, and its German partner Deutsche BA, will all come under scrutiny when the group reports on Tuesday.
