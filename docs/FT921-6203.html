920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / Modest inflow of funds to unit trusts UNIT TRUSTS had only a modest inflow of funds last month in spite of the launch of 12 trusts. Net investment (gross sales minus repurchases) was Pounds 26.8m, which was the worst figure since September 1990. The number of unitholder accounts fell slightly again to 4.44m Gross sales for the month were Pounds 668.5m and repurchases were Pounds 641.7m. The value of the funds under management was boosted by buoyant stock markets to Pounds 57.5bn.
