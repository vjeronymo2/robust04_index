930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Corp Services Corporate Services, the employment service group which derives 80 per cent of revenue from temporary and contract labour, cut its 1992 deficit from Pounds 2.13m to Pounds 604,000 after turning round to a profit of Pounds 248,000, from a loss of Pounds 1.6m, at the trading level. An exceptional charge of Pounds 282,000 related to restructuring, and interest costs came to Pounds 570,000 (Pounds 467,000). Turnover in the second half was some 28 per cent more than in the first, with a subsequent bias regards profitability. Losses came through at 1.85p (9.32p).
