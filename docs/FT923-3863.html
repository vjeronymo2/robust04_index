920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Boxes plant to be built near Chester AMCOR, Australian paper and packaging group is investing ADollars 68m (Pounds 25m) in a corrugated box manufacturing plant near Chester, north-west England. The plant will have a capacity of 70,000 tonnes and is expected to start production early in 1994. It will create up to 150 jobs. It will increase Amcor's total corrugated fibreboard production capacity in Europe to about 300,000 tonnes a year.
