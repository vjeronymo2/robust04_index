Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting yesterday in the capital, Sanaa, the Political Forces Dialogue Committee concluded its agenda regarding preparations for the signing of the accord document and guarantees for immediate implementation. In two meetings on Saturday and Sunday [12, 13 February], the Political Forces Dialogue Committee approved the formation of a committee to consider views regarding the necessary security guarantees to begin implementation. The Political Forces Dialogue Committee also approved the formation of another committee to prepare the necessary appendices to the document and draft a statement announcing the signing of the document. A third committee will take care of protocol arrangements for the signing ceremony, which will be held in the Jordanian capital, Amman, on 10 Ramadan, corresponding to 20 February, per consultations with the brothers in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. The Political Forces Dialogue Committee agreed to meet next Thursday to see what these committees will come up with and make appropriate decisions. The Political Forces Dialogue Committee has said it will continue to be in session, in line with the constitutional, legal, and procedural guarantees approved by the committee late last week.
