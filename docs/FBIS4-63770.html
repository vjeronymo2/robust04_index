BFN [Unattributed report: "A Statement at the Ukrainian-Russian Border"] [Text] Representatives of the command of Ukrainian and the Russian Federation's troops, Customs services of the two states, leadership of the ministries of internal affairs, and state administrations of Kharkiv and Belgorod oblasts met with journalists at the Ukrainian-Russian border. As is known, after signing the memorandum on border cooperation between Ukraine and Russia, some mass media disseminated a "sensation" on the renewal of the system of single borders of the CIS countries. It was stressed at the news conference that Ukraine and the Russian Federation are building their relations on the principles of friendship and good-neighborly relations and do not intend to violate the sovereign rights of the close neighbor. Each side strives to respect each other's state attributes, including state borders.
