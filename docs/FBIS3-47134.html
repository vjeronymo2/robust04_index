Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrade Li Peng, premier of the PRC State Council, on 8 January held talks with the Japanese foreign minister and stressed the need to resolve the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula through dialogue, according to XINHUA news agency. Referring to the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula, he pointed out: China supports the stance of realizing the denuclearization on the Korean peninsula and maintaining security and peace on the peninsula. We hope that the nuclear issue will be resolved reasonably through dialogue. We do not approve of using means of imposing pressure or sanctions since such ways can bring an adverse effect and produce discrepancy on the issue.
