Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1200 on 5 March, His Excellency [H.E.] Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], received a courtesy call from Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the Kingdom of Cambodia National Assembly, at Hokham Hall in the capital of Vientiane on the occasion of the Cambodian's official friendship visit to the LPDR. Their conversation took place in an atmosphere of intimacy and mutual understanding. H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan hailed the high-level Kingdom of Cambodia National Assembly delegation and extended his warm welcome. H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan and Samdech Chea Sim highly assessed the traditional relations of friendship between the Lao and Cambodian peoples. H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan stressed that the intimate solidarity and affection between the Lao and Cambodian peoples represent fraternal, neighborly, and comradely ties nurtured by sharing weal and woe ever since ancient times. On the same occasion, Samdech Chea Sim expressed his full appreciation for and satisfaction with the warm welcome accorded by H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan. He highly valued the continuously increasing and developing relations of friendship, cooperation, and assistance between the two states and peoples.
