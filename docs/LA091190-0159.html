September 11, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

County economy -- A bar graph accompanying an article published Sunday about the county's economy was mislabeled. The graph made it appear that the total amount of leased office space in 1990 is about 300,000 square feet. Actually, that figure is the projected net increase in leased office space this year, a figure roughly equal to the increases of the previous two years.
