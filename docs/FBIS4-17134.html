BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow April 20 TASS -- "The political situation in Russia began to stabilise", Russian President Boris Yeltsin told a meeting with regional leaders on Wednesday. He confirmed that the civic accord agreement is to be signed in the Kremlin on April 28 and said that nearly 140 political and piblic organisations are going to join it. Yeltsin urged all branches of authority, political parties and public organisations to sign the agreement and called on all Russian regional leaders to follow suit. The president proposed to regional leaders to "meet regularly, in small groups" in order to "frankly speak and discuss all regional problems".
