BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher at the Foreign Ministry this morning. Christopher arrived in Cairo from Saudi Arabia last night as part of his tour of the region. Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat arrived at the Foreign Ministry leading a delegation and joined the meeting between Musa and Christopher. They will discuss ways to remove the latest obstacles blocking the signing of the Gaza-Jericho Accord between the Palestinians and Israelis. The delegation accompanying 'Arafat includes the head of the Palestinian side at the Gaza-Jericho talks, Dr. Nabil Sha'th, PLO Executive Committee member Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, and Marwan Kanafani.
