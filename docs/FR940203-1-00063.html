The Gas Piping Technology Committee (GPTC) Guide for Gas Transmission and Distribution Piping Systems, Volume 1 The GPTC guide contains information and methods to assist gas pipeline operators in complying with the federal pipeline safety regulations by providing ``how to'' information related to the standards. The GPTC Guide contains minimum federal safety standards together with the design recommendations, material reference, and recommended practices of the GPTC.
