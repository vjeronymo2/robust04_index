931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Brasway improves 27% to Pounds 420,000 A 27 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits from Pounds 330,000 to Pounds 420,000 was achieved by Brasway in the six months to October 30. Following its further redirection into value added products, the group now has two main divisions, Europower (hydraulic hoses and couplings) and Excelube (industrial and automotive lubricants). Turnover fell to Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 16.4m) but trading profits advanced to Pounds 624,000 (Pounds 576,000). Earnings per share rose from 0.28p to 0.36p and the interim dividend is held at 0.24p.
