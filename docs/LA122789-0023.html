December 27, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

GOODWILL

On behalf of the disadvantaged and disabled people Goodwill would like to express appreciation to all those who have in some way given assistance to people whose main desire has been to be able to help themselves.

These are (people) who have hired the disabled, who have given cash donations and who, when we sent a plea for help when donations were down, came to our rescue.

These include the donors of repairable materials and the businesses that have contracted for packaging, assembly, etc. in our industrial contracts department.

We want to commend you and your readers for the assistance you have given this past year. Through your help disadvantaged and disabled people have had daily work here at Goodwill. Many of them have gone on to full-time jobs in local businesses.

RUTH C. STEINMETZ

President

Goodwill Industries

Long Beach
