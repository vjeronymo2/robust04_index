September 28, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

THE STATE

Gov. George Deukmejian appointed Maureen Higgins of Sacramento, his chief deputy legislative secretary since 1987, as director of the state Department of Housing and Community Development. A Republican, Higgins, 37, replaces Christine Diemer Reed, who resigned to return to the private sector. The $90,526-a-year job requires state Senate confirmation.
