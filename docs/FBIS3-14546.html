Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Israeli delegation departed for Cairo this morning to conclude the discussions about the security arrangements in Hebron and pave the way for the resumption of the negotiations with the PLO. The delegation is headed by Major General Amnon Shahaq, who is accompanied by Foreign Ministry Director General Uri Savir, the prime minister's political adviser Jacques Neriya, and Foreign Ministry's legal adviser Yo'el Singer. The talks will be held at the Sheraton Hotel in Cairo.
