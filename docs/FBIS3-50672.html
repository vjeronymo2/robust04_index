Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Panama City, 5 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- Commerce and Industries Minister Roberto Alfaro announced his resignation today, which will go into effect on 1 February, and he also announced a three cent per gallon drop in the price of gasoline. In statements to the media, the official denied that his resignation was prompted by political reasons, and he said he only wants to spend more time with his family and business. He is the owner of Financiera de Seguros S.A. (FISESA) and an associate of another insurance company called La Union. [passage omitted]
