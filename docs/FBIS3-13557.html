Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A demonstration was held outside the American Embassy in New Delhi today to protest against the U.S. Government's policy to support the terrorist activities of Pakistan in Jammu and Kashmir. The demonstrators led by a former minister and chairman of Rashtriya Ekta Nirman Shakti, Mr. Anil Shastri, handed over a memorandum to the embassy which called for noninterference in India's internal affairs and stopping support to Pakistan for its terrorist activities in India.
