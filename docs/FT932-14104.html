930420 FT 20 APR 93 / Baby Bells: Ringing the changes Ameritech: Revenues Dollars 11.2bn, net income Dollars 1.35bn. HQ Chicago Area served: northern midwest. The last Bell to diversify out of its traditional business, but now being shaken up by chairman William Weiss, who has started a fundamental reorganisation and offered to accept competition, in return for greater regulatory freedom. Bell Atlantic: Revenues Dollars 12.6bn, net income Dollars 1.34bn HQ Philadephia. Area served: eastern US seaboard from New Jersey to Virginia. Probably the most aggressive Baby Bell in meeting new competition head on. It is challenging in court the ban on phone companies owning cable interests and is testing an innovative video-on-demand service using ordinary phone lines. BellSouth. Revenues *** Net Income ****.
