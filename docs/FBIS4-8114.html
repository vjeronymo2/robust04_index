BFN [Text] Kragujevac, 8 Apr (TANJUG) -- Borisav Mikelic, Krajina prime minister-designate, stated today that full agreement had been reached with the Democratic Party of Krajina on a government of national unity. He told Kragujevac Radio that Milan Babic would be appointed deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs, and in the next few days more details would be disclosed regarding ministers and departments of the new Krajina Government. Stressing that the basic aim was now to include in production those people returning from the front, Mikelic said there would be no place for war profiteers in Krajina. "We shall fight them with all means at our disposal, primarily the means of a law-governed state," Mikelic said.
