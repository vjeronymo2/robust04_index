940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / People: Phillips Petroleum Company UK Allyn Risley has been appointed chairman and managing director of Phillips Petroleum Company UK. He succeeds Bill Parker, who is returning to the company's head office in Bartlesville, Oklahoma. Risley, who has had a previous posting in the UK, hopes to expand the company's British activities, especially in the growing market for natural gas. The company is developing new North Sea gasfields, and has a joint venture with Southern Electricity to market gas directly in the UK market. A petroleum engineer by training, Risley has spent 17 years away from his native America, including stints in Indonesia and Singapore as well as the Middle East. Risley and his family will be moving to Phillips UK headquarters in Woking.
