BFN [Text] Beijing, May 14 (XINHUA) -- Chi Haotian, state councillor and minister of national defense, met with Jorge Lucar Figueroa, deputy commander-in-chief of Chilean Army, here today. During the meeting, the two sides exchanged views on furthering ties between the armed forces of the two countries as well as issues of common interests. Li Jing, deputy chief of general staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was present.
