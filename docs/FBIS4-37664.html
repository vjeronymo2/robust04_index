BFN [Text] Foreign Affairs Minister Habib Ben Yahya held talks at the ministry this afternoon with Mr. Luo Gan, state councillor in the People's Republic of China, who is on a three-day working visit to Tunisia. After the meeting, Ben Yahya and Luo Gan cochaired a working session that included delegations from the two countries. The Chinese official later said that the two sides had agreed on the need to expand cooperation between the two countries, with a view toward bolstering bilateral relations in the economic and commercial fields, and also in various other fields.
