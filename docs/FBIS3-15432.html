Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] A Georgian delegation led by Eduard Shevardnadze heading on an official visit to the United States stopped over in Prague. The Georgian leader met with Vaclav Havel, president of the Czech Republic. Then a news conference was held. Current international political affairs, the settlement of the Abkhazian conflict, and the contribution of international organizations in it were the issues touched upon at the conference. Vaclav Havel noted at the news conference that Eduard Shevardnadze has played a remarkable role in establishing new international relations in the world. He thanked the Georgian head of state on behalf of the people of Czech Republic. Eduard Shevardnadze called Vaclav Havel's activities legendary. He expressed hope that future close relations between the Czech Republic and Georgia will fully meet the interests of the peoples of both countries. The Georgian delegation left Prague for New York with yet another stop on the way, in the capital of Iceland, Reykjavik. This information has been supplied by Kontakt news agency.
