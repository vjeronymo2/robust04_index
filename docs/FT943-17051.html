940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / Vaz protests at Abu Dhabi move Mr Keith Vaz, Labour MP for Leicester East, yesterday called on the Lord Chancellor to investigate a petition made to the Legal Aid Board by the solicitors to the Ruler of Abu Dhabi. In an early day motion he criticised Simmons & Simmons for writing a letter to the board suggesting there was no cause for legal action in a proposed case from former employees of the collapsed Bank of Credit and Commerce International. Mr Vaz said he considered it 'an outrage' that Abu Dhabi, the majority shareholder in the bank, was questioning possible legal aid in the UK to former employees when the emirate had claimed immunity from jurisdiction in the courts. Simmons & Simmons said last night: 'We were acting perfectly properly in accordance with those procedures developed by the Legal Aid Board over the years.'
