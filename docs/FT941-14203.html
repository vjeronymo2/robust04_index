940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / People: Insurance moves - Bowring Nicholas Bacon and Alexander MacKenzie Smith have been appointed directors of BOWRING Worldwide Insurance Brokers; Sheridan Edwards a director of Bowring Aviation; Mark Cubitt, Philip Lundberg, Peregrine Muncaster and Tom Smith directors of the marine and energy division of Bowring Marine; and James Summers a director of its marine reinsurance division.
