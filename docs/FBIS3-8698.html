Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] An agreement on political cooperation and allied relations between the Bulgarian Social Democratic Party [BSDP] and the Civic Alliance for the Republic [GOR] was signed on 14 March by the two party leaders, Dr. Petur Dertliev and Aleksandur Tomov. Assuming the mutual commitment to act for the construction of a political alternative of the confrontation bipolar model, the two formations express confidence that their cooperation will contribute to the democratic development of society and improving political life in Bulgaria.
