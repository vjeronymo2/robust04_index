December 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

Congratulations to the writers and sponsors of the following shows who had the courage to create viable and dignified parts for older men and women as well as minorities: "Matlock," "Island Son," "In the Heat of the Night," "Murder, She Wrote" and "Dallas." And a rap on the knuckles of the "Falcon Crest" writers for turning a fairly decent series into a nonsensical waste of time with their ridiculous and poorly done love scenes that have no redeeming value.

Marianne Della Marna, Alta Loma
