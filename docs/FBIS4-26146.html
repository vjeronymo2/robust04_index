BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Brian Howe, Australian deputy prime minister and minister of housing, local government, and community services, his wife and party arrived in Shanghai on 15 June on a visit at Vice Premier Zhu Rongji's invitation. Vice Mayor Xia Keqiang met with Deputy Prime Minister Brian Howe at Xinjinjiang Hotel in the evening. Xia Keqiang said: Shanghai is revising its urban planning for the 21st century, and will complete the revision next year. By then, we will seek advice from experts of other countries. We welcome Australian experts to take part in providing the advice. We welcome Australian entrepreneurs to take part in Shanghai's housing construction and in rebuilding its old houses. Brian Howe said Australia is willing to further promote economic cooperation and trade with Shanghai.
