Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 17 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam said Thursday that he will travel to Russia in the near future after visiting Japan and China this month. "Visits to these countries are not only for security reasons but also to strengthen economic cooperation to beat off the competition," Kim told top-grade graduates of 126 universities and their parents whom he invited over for lunch. Government officials say it is yet to be decided when Kim will visit Russia, but a likely date is around June. A European tour timed with his visit to Russia is also being considered, they say.
