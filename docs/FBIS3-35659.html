Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, January 28 (SNARK)--The parliamentary minority for many times raises the question on the abolition of the additional authorities of the head of the Georgian State. Speaking to SNARK in his exclusive interview Eduard Shevardnadze said he would say his "yes" to the abolition. "Everything I do is being done for the blossom of Georgia", told he. Speaking on the Abkhazian problem solution Eduard Shevardnadze said, "I am looking for the solution of session of the UN Security Council. The most important point is the decision the Security Council accepts in the coming future. The perspectives of the Georgian-Abkhazian conflict regulation depend upon this decision".
