Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] General Ratko Mladic, the commander of Serb formations in Bosnia-Herzegovina, had a meeting yesterday in Drvar, at his own request, with General Jean Cot, the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] commander for the former Yugoslavia, the Zagreb heaquarters of UNPROFOR announced today. Mladic has agreed to meet with General Rasim Delic, the commander of the Muslim forces, on Tuesday 15 February at Sarajevo Airport, the statement says. The aim of this meeting is to continue, and flesh out, the concrete measures to ensure the success of the cease-fire, which is already being implemented in and around Sarajevo. This means collecting up and withdrawing heavy weaponry and reducing forces on front lines, a statement issued by UNPROFOR's public relations office says.
