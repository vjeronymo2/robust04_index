BFN [Text] A roundtable discussion with the topic: The Right of Displaced Persons and Refugees To Return, which was held in Sarajevo today, gathered numerous government ministers, representatives of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees and of the Army, experts, and public personalities who discussed a possible return of 1.5 million refugees from Bosnia-Herzegovina.
