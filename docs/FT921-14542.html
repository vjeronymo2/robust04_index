920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / Share ownership body planned THE Wider Share Ownership Council is being wound up after nearly 34 years. It is being replaced by the Share Ownership Movement, backed by the Confederation of British Industry and the Stock Exchange, which will come into being on February 20. Sir John Harvey-Jones, the former chairman of Imperial Chemical Industries who was president of the council, will be president of the new body. Sir Peter Thompson, formerly chairman of the transport company NFC, will be chairman.
