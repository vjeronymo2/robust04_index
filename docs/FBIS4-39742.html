BFN [Text] President Boris Yeltsin has signed an edict on measures to implement the treaty on public accord. A conciliation commission [soglasitelnaya komissiya] is being set up to deal with this. Sergey Filatov, the head of the president's administration, has been appointed its chairman. The conciliation commission will cooperate in its work with the Federal Assembly's Federation Council and State Duma, as well as with parties, social and public movements, and other organizations whose representatives signed the treaty on public accord.
