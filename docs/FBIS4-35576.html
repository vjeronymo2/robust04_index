BFN [Text] Today's edition of the high-circulation Rome newspaper CORRIERE DELLA SERA reports in its banner headline that Israel has decided to cool its relations with Italy due to the inclusion of neofascist ministers in its government. This is reported by our correspondent Yosi Bar. Reacting to this report, Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin said that the Israeli Government has not yet adopted any position with regard to the worrisome developments in Italy and that the Foreign Ministry is currently formulating its policy. This is reported by our correspondent Hayim Hecht.
