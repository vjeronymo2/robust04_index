Abuse Established AB1704151394 Lagos Radio Nigeria Network in English 0600 GMT 17 Apr 94 AB1704151394 Lagos Radio Nigeria Network English BFN [Text] A nongovernmental organization aimed at combatting drug abuse and trafficking in Africa has been set up in Lagos. It is known as the Society for the Eradication of Drug Abuse in Africa, SEDA. A statement by the organization says the continent is becoming a major drug consumption center with health and social consequences. As part of its plans to combat the problem, SEDA is organizing an international conference on drug abuse and trafficking. It will also embark on visits to prisons and hospitals in parts of the world to offer counseling to jailed drug couriers.
