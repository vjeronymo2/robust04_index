BFN [Text] Commenting on North Korea's moves to test-fire a missile at a news conference on 30 May, Chief Cabinet Secretary Hiroshi Kumagai said, "The government has information on those moves in that country." In this way, the Japanese Government officially confirmed for the first time North Korea's moves to test-fire the missile. At the news conference, however, Chief Cabinet Secretary Kumagai stated: "The government does not know details of North Korea's plans to test-fire the missile. The government also does not know whether North Korea's moves to test-fire the missile are related to the issue of international inspections of its nuclear facilities. I, therefore, would like to refrain from making any further comment at the present stage."
