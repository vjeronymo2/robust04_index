Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Gustav Hochenbichler, head of the Police Bureau for Foreigners and one of the highest State Police officials, is suspected of having spied for the former East Bloc. His home was searched today. No arrest warrant has been issued. No further details are known. The Justice Ministry has confirmed a relevant report in the 14 January edition of NEUE KRONEN-ZEITUNG, and said a news blackout has been imposed so as not to jeopardize investigations.
