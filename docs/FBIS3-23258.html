Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 7 Mar (MENA)--An official source in the Interior Ministry has stated that at around 0330 today, Monday, unidentified persons fired at trains no. 996 destined for Luxor from Cairo and no. 990 heading from Cairo to Asyut in an area between Dayrut and al-Qusiyah. The source added that four people were wounded in the first train and two in the second. The wounded people, all Egyptians, were taken to Asyut General Hospital. Their conditions are reassuring.
