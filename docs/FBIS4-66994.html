Border 94WD0322I Bombay THE TIMES OF INDIA in English 28 Feb 94 p 3 94WD0322I Bombay THE TIMES OF INDIA English CSO [Text] New Delhi, Feb. 27 (UNI [United News of India]): Pakistan has dumped tonnes of narcotics along the Rajasthan border and the drug pedlars are waiting for security lapses to push the contraband into India, according to the Border Security Force (BSF) chief, Mr. D.K. Arya. The drugs are worth hundreds of crores of rupees in the international market. The move is a part of Pakistan's larger strategy to weaken India economically and expose New Delhi to international pressure in yet another arena -- Narco-terrorism.
