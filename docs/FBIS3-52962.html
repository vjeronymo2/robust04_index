Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 10 Jan (INA)--Informed Yemeni sources have said that opinions in Sanaa are moving in the direction of forming a new government in the coming phase. Such a government would represent the political parties and forces as part of an approach to resolve the political crisis. The weekly AL-HIWAR cites these sources as saying that the new government will be entrusted with implementing the political agreement which the coalition and opposition parties are drafting to end Yemen's political crisis, tackle the country's problems, work out development programs, and promote the unity of the state. Observers here believe that the agreement's articles will ensure the bases for merging the two armies, end the military manifestations throughout the country, entrench security, and crystallize sound national action in the field of news media, economy, and politics.
