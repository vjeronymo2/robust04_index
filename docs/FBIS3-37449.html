Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, February 16 (SNARK)--The internal troops of the Georgian Home Affairs Ministry start to guard the gas pipe-line which delivers gas to Armenia -- it's an agreement achieved during the recent visit of Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan to Tbilisi, Vladimir Chikovani, Commander of the Georgian internal troops, told SNARK. Mr. Chikovani also added the Georgian internal troops will provide for the safety of the repairing workers who are reconstructing the damaged part of the pipe-line on the territory of the Marneulinskiy region of Georgia. It needs two weeks to restore the line. Vladimir Chikovani mentioned it seems as if the problem connected with the permanent safety measures for the open parts of the gas pipe-line will be solved.
