940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies suffer further - At a Glance The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies index (capital gains version) was down again last week, by 2.3 per cent to 1770.29 over the week to March 24. Over the same period the FT-SE A All-Share index fell 3.6 per cent. Since the beginning of the year the Smaller Companies index has dropped by 5.2 per cent, the All-Share by 5.3 per cent and the FT-SE 100 index by 7.9 per cent.
