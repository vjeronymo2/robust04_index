June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD

DOG RUN -- Hundreds of dogs will bring their owners to Rancho Park in Century City today for the Two-Mile Fun Run or the One-Mile Walk which starts at 9 a.m. and will benefit the Los Angeles Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals. Entry fees are $16, which includes T-shirts, a dog leash and a bandana.
