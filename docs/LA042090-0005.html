April 20, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

LA JOLLA HALF MARATHON WILL BE RUN SUNDAY

The La Jolla Half Marathon, billed as the fourth-largest race of its type in the nation, begins at 7:30 a.m. Sunday.

The course starts at the Del Mar Fairgrounds, heads south along the coast and winds up at La Jolla Cove Scripps Park. Also included in the activities are 2-mile and 5-mile jaunts.

The race, which offers a $500 prize to the male or female that breaks the course record, is limited to 4,000 entrants. Deadline for registration is Saturday, and no sign-ups will be accepted the day of the race.

All profits will go to the La Jolla Kiwanis Foundation to help youth projects there.

For more information, call 272-8316.
