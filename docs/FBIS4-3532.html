BFN [Text] Beijing, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Ni Zhifu, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC), met here today with a delegation of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and they exchanged opinions on issues of common interest. The two-member delegation, headed by the general secretary of the World Federation of Trade unions, A. Zharikov, began a visit to China on April 18 at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
