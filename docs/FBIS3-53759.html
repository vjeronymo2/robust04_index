Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening, three residents were injured by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] fire in Hebron after an IDF unit came under a barrage of rocks near the Muslim college in the city. The three suffered moderate to serious wounds. Our correspondent Ron Nesi'el reports that hundreds of residents took part in the riot. The incident took place near a mourning tent, erected last weekend after four Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] terrorists were killed in a gunfight with the IDF.
