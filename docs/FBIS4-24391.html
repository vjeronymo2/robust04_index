BFN [Text] Beijing, 27 May (XINHUA) -- The chairmanship meeting of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] held its 15th session in Beijing today. The session decided that the Seventh Session of the Eighth CPPCC Nation Committee Standing Committee will be held in Beijing in late June and suggested that the central topic for discussion in this session be agricultural problems. Entrusted by Li Ruihuan, chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, vice chairman Ye Xuanping chaired today's session. The Seventh Session of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee is going to hear reports by leading comrades concerned from the State Council concerning the agricultural situation and conditions in villages; examine and discuss the reports (in written form) of the Standing Committee members of the CPPCC National Committee on their visit to Anhui and of the members of the CPPCC National Committee on their visits to Henan and Shanxi for the inspection of agricultural production, rural work, and conditions of village and town enterprises; and examine and discuss the reports (in written form) on CPPCC National Committee Vice Chairman Wu Xueqian's visit to Korea and the delegation of CPPCC National Committee's visits to Jordan and Djibouti. Attending today's session were vice chairmen Wu Xueqian, Yang Rudai, Wang Zhaoguo, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Seypidin Aze, Hong Xuezhi, Qian Zhengying, Sun Fuling, Zhu Guangya, Wan Guoquan; and Secretary General Zhu Xun.
