February 19, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

NBC Shuffles Lineup: NBC has announced several changes in its prime-time lineup, including moving the comedy series "Alf" to Saturdays at 8 p.m. and bringing back "My Two Dads" on Mondays at 8 p.m. "Alf" will replace "227," which will go on hiatus, to return to the schedule later in the season. The changes, which are scheduled to go into effect the first week of March, are the first of a series of shuffles that will make room for tryouts of "close to a dozen" new NBC series, according to NBC Entertainment President Brandon Tartikoff.
