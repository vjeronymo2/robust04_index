Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 14 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il have shown deep loving care for the researchers of the agricultural research centre to register big successes in carrying on the green revolution. They sent a total of more than 6,300 kinds of seeds to the centre on some 180 occasions over the past thirty years after the publication of the socialist rural theses. The great leader sent a new rice seed to the institution in July 1968 and a high-yielding maize seed in March 1986. The dear leader sent to it various kinds of rice seeds respectively in December 1977 and in July 1978, two kinds of quick-growing and high-yielding onion seeds in August 1986 and a peculiar seed of water melon which looks beautiful and heavy in weight in March 1988. He sent more than 490 kinds of crops seeds on nearly twenty occasions last year and different kinds of maize seeds in last February, so that they could be used in researches. Under the great loving care of the great leader and the dear leader the agro-scientists have achieved signal successes in their research into high-yielding seeds of crops, succeeding in the cultivation of more than 400 new strains over the past thirty years. Each time they were told of successes registered in carrying on the green revolution the great leader and the dear leader congratulated the agro-scientists upon their successful researches and personally named the newly-developed crops.
