940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: CIA expands in Europe with AB buy CIA Group, the advertising services company, has acquired a 34 per cent stake in AB Media, the Paris-based independent media concern. The USM-traded group will acquire the outstanding 66 per cent within 28 days. Consideration amounts to FFr19m (Pounds 2.2m) cash and the issue of 283,423 shares to the vendors. An additional FFr3m has been placed in escrow, payable in June 1997. A further profit-related payment of up to FFr10m may become payable in 1998.
