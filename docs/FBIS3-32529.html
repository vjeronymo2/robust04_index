Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 25 January 1994 on page 6 reports that the Swedish Association for International Development (ASDI) will invest $5.8 million starting in February to finance housing, community, and small-business programs in Leon, Chinandega, Ocotal, Somoto, and Esteli. The Local Development Program (Prodel) offices in those cities will be implemented by the ASDI programs. Small business owners will have to repay loans made for home and business improvements, according to the article. Prodel is also planning sewer and water projects as well as school and street improvements.
