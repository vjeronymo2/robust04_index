BFN [Text] President Chiluba has sent a message of congratulations to Mandela on his victory in the just ended all-race elections. In a congratulatory message issued to ZNBC [Zambia National Broadcasting Corporation] news this evening, Mr. Chiluba said it was Zambia's prayer that Mr. Mandela gets God's blessings to attend national reconciliation. The message sent on behalf of the president by Vice President Levy Mwanawasa, described the African National Congress victory to end the apartheid regime as the world- led victory. In a similar message to outgoing President F.W. de Klerk, Mr. Chiluba lauded the last white leader of the apartheid country for handling the transition to democratic elections properly.
