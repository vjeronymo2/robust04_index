Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Aydin, Mar. 29 (A.A.) -- Seventeen people were captured in Aegean province of Aydin including five members of the Democracy Party (DEP), on the grounds that they were involved in a number of terrorist activities, it was reported on Tuesday [29 March]. A statement from Aydin Governor's Office said that during operations carried out by security forces 17 were captured, among them DEP representative in Aydin and another senior official of the party. It was determined all had taken part in terrorist activities such as the killing of a civilian, outlawed propaganda prior to the local elections, and extortion, forcing shops to close down as well as providing logistic and material help for a terrorist organization.
