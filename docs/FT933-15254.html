930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Diesel power helps AB Engineering A strong recovery at its diesel power engineering division helped Associated British Engineering report a sharp increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 35,000 to Pounds 216,000 in the 12 months to March 31. This also marked an improvement from the half-way stage when the group declined into losses of Pounds 46,000. Sales advanced 21 per cent to Pounds 41.5m (Pounds 34.2m). Diesel power contributed profits of Pounds 149,000 against a Pounds 12,000 loss previously. Middle East operations increased their input to Pounds 635,000 (Pounds 543,000) and marine diesel spares and service put in Pounds 381,000 (Pounds 272,000). However catering equipment suffered an increased loss of Pounds 366,000 (Pounds 141,000). The dividend is maintained at 0.05p on earnings per share of 0.01p (losses 0.05p).
