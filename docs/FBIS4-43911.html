BFN [Unattributed article: "Martino Mission to Sarajevo, Zagreb, Belgrade"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, June 22 -- Italian Foreign Minister Antonio Martino will carry out a mission to Sarajevo, Zagreb and Belgrade on June 27-28 to further Italian efforts to achieve a peaceful settlement in former Yugoslavia, the Foreign Ministry announced this evening. Martino will be the second Italian foreign minister to travel to Sarajevo since the start of the Bosnian war. Emilio Colombo visited on February 3, 1993, meeting with UN commanders and Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic after talks in Zagreb with Croatian President Franjo Tudjman. Martino's trip will come after the Corfu EU [European Union] summit and before the Naples G-7 summit, two occasions on which Bosnia will be one of the top agenda items.
