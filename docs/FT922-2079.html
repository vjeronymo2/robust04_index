920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Craig & Rose falls Pounds 20,000 into loss Craig & Rose, the paints, household goods and hardware group, fell Pounds 20,000 into the red in the year to December 31. For 1990 there were pre-tax profits of Pounds 126,000. Losses per share worked through at 5p (earnings 21.75p) but the directors are recommending a final dividend of 12.5p (13p) for a total of 14.5p against 15p. Turnover was static at Pounds 5.62m (Pounds 5.59m).
