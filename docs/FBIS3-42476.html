Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article by Lt-Col Beltsov, Press Center of the Air Force of the Russian Federation: "10,000 Apartments for Military Pilots"] [Text] Today 11 construction firms from Germany, Finland, Turkey, South Korea, and India are building comfortable housing on the territory of four military districts of Russia for air force officers and warrant officers withdrawn from the Western Group of Forces. Most of the housing is being built in the Moscow and North Caucasus military districts. A town with 1,058 apartments has already been turned over in the garrisons of Shaykovka (Moscow Military District) and people have moved into cottages at Siverskaya Air Base (Leningrad Military District). Altogether 9,400 apartments for pilots will be put into use by the end of 1994.
