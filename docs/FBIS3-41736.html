Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Reported by BETTA and Postfactum information agencies: ``Under the Banner of Hepatitis Control''] [Text]The residents of the Sakhalin village, Makarovo, will celebrate the First of May by fighting the hepatitis epidemic caused by decomposition of a cattle burying ground. As established by the staff of the rayon sanitary and epidemiological station, the hepatitis epidemic in this region has reached catastrophic proportions, and the number of cases has reached 82 cases to date.
