BFN [Text] Yerevan ("HAYASTANI HANRAPETUTYUN" daily), 05/14/94 -- Defense Ministry press center reports, the Azeri and Nakhichevan's armed forces restarted shelling north-eastern and south-western borders of the Republic of Armenia in the evening of 12 May. Starting from 1930 and till 2300 the enemy units stationed in the Ghzl Haj military base in the Kazakh district shelled, using mortars, tanks and machine guns, the Armenian defense positions at the villages of Sarigyugh and Berkaber in the Idzhevanskiy District. No casualties are reported. A machine gun fire, lasting from 2230 till 0100 was opened from Nakhichevan in the direction of the Armenian defense positions at the village of Shaghat in the Sisianskiy District. The Armenian frontier forces obeyed the cease-fire and refrained from returning the fire in both cases.
