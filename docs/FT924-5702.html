921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / International Company News: SHL reduces loss to CDollars 9.5m SHL Systemhouse, a fast-growing North American systems integrator, reduced its losses sharply in the year ended August 31 as revenues rose 7 per cent to CDollars 739m (USDollars 586.5m). The Dollars 9.5m net loss, equal to 25 cents a share, included a Dollars 6.4m special charge for discontinuation of the company's alliance with BCE, Canada's biggest telecommunications group. A year earlier, the loss was Dollars 19.9m.
