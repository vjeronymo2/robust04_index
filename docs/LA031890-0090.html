March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: AZUSA PACIFIC NURSING SCHOOL OPEN HOUSE

Azusa Pacific University, 921 E. Alosta Ave., Azusa, will hold an open house for prospective students in its School of Nursing from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. March 30.

The event will include a tour of the Nursing Skills Center, a luncheon with faculty and students, and information on career opportunities in nursing.
