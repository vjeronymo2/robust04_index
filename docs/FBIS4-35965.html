BFN [Text] At his private residence in Irbil last night, Brother Jalal Talabani, secretary general of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan, received Major General Hasan al-Naqib, member of the Presidium of the Iraqi National Congress [INC], who was accompanied by Salah al-Naqib. After Hasan al-Naqib and Salah al-Naqib expressed their happiness over the safe return of Brother Talabani from abroad, the two sides exchanged views on the present situation in Kurdistan, the recent regrettable incidents in the region, and the means of normalizing and calming the situation.
