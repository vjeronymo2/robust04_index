Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 29 KYODO -- Japan will extend to Bangladesh a 21 billion yen official loan during the visit to Japan by Prime Minister Begum Khaleda Ziaur, who arrived in Tokyo on Monday [28 March], the Foreign Ministry said Tuesday. The ministry said in a press release that the 21.56 billion yen in official development assistance (ODA) will go toward construction of a bridge over the Jamuna River, which divides the country into the east and west regions.
