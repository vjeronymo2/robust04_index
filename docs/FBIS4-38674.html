BFN [Text] Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan signed a decree today to form an Armenian Republic state delegation to hold talks with the Russian Federation. It established an Armenian Republic state delegation to formulate a system of legal foundation for bilateral relations between the Armenian Republic and the Russian Federation, to resolve emerging practical problems, and to coordinate the talks held with Russia through various departments. The Armenian State Minister Armenak Gazaryan has been appointed the head of the delegation.
