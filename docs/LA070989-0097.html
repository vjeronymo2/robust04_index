July 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

SAN DIEGO SHOPPING CENTER CHANGES HANDS AND NAME

Mercury Village Shopping Center is the new name for Mesa Plaza Shopping Center in San Diego's Kearny Mesa, which was recently purchased for $4.12 million.

The 28,516-square-foot center located on a 1.89-acre site at 8006-8080 Clairemont Mesa Blvd., was sold by Mesa Plaza Ltd. to San Diego investors. John Burnham & Co. represented all parties in the sale.
