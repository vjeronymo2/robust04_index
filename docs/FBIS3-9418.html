Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Serbian Interrogators Even Use Chemical Substances"] [Text] Ismet Veliqi, Avdi Behrami, and Azem Gashi, former military officers and members of the former people's defense forces of Skenderaj [Srbica], whom the Serbian police arrested a few days ago, are being brutally maltreated in Mitrovice Prison, the Information Committee of the Kosova Democratic Alliance's Mitrovice branch reports. It is reported that they are being tortured at night by employees of the Serbian security services, who come specially from Prishtina. It is reported that the Serbian interrogators are using chemical substances, electric prods, and other methods to torture the former Albanian officers. The detainees' condition is very serious. Torture during the investigation process suggests the preparation of another political show trial, this time of former Albanian Army officers in Kosova.
