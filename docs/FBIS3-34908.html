Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] At 2000 this evening Czech President Vaclav Havel and the delegation accompanying him arrived at Kuwait international airport for a short visit lasting a few hours. [passage omitted] After his arrival President Havel paid a visit to his highness the amir at his residence in Tasmana where they held a round of talks. The minister of amir's affairs [Shaykh Nasir Muhammad al-Ahmad] said that the talks had dealt with a number of issues of mutual concern to the two friendly countries and the most serious vital issues on the international scene. The talks were held in a cordial atmosphere characterized by understanding and a spirit of friendship.
