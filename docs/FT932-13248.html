930423 FT 23 APR 93 / UK Company News: Marine Midland Banks improves First quarter net earnings of Marine Midland Banks, the US subsidiary of Hongkong and Shanghai Bank, surged from Dollars 2.6m to Dollars 34m (Pounds 22.5m). No provisions were made for loan losses, against Dollars 40.5m for the opening quarter of 1992. Non-accruing loans were 40 per cent lower than a year ago and the quality of Marine's classified loans continued to improve significantly.
