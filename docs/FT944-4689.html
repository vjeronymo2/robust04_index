941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Fletcher King falls into the red Reflecting the 'severe downturn in activity in the commercial property market during late spring and summer', Fletcher King, the commercial estate agency, swung into the red in the six months to October 31. Turnover for the period fell to Pounds 1.88m (Pounds 2.34m) and the loss before tax amounted to Pounds 599,000, against a profit of Pounds 74,000. Losses per share were 6.9p (0.5p earnings), but the interim dividend is held at 0.5p.
