BFN [Text] Beijing, May 20 (XINHUA) -- Cheng Siyuan, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with Ennio Grassi, member of the Chamber of Deputies of Italy, and other Italian visitors here this afternoon. The Italian guests arrived here on may 15 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries. Besides Beijing, they have traveled to Guangzhou and Shanghai.
