930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / Technology (Worth Watching): Favourite mobile phone of the year The Cellnet award for the best mobile phone of the year has gone to Panasonic for its I Series phone. The phone also won the sub-categories for features innovation and pocket-to-car mobility - the back light colour (green or orange) even changes to match the dashboard display lighting in the car. The overall runner-up was the Motorola MicroTac II, which also won the award for ergonomic excellence. Cellnet: UK, 0753 504000. Panasonic: UK, 0344 853239. Motorola: UK, 0256 817474.
