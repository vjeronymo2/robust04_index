BFN [Text] Beijing, June 14 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor Li Guixian met with visiting Director Istvan Hagelmayer of Hungary's state audit office here today. Director Istvan Hagelmayer and two other Hungarian visitors arrived here Monday [13 June] as guests of China's Auditing Administration on a six-day visit to Beijing and Xiamen city in east China's Fujian Province.
