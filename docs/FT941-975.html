940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / China to act over patents China, embarrassed by US charges of soaring copyright piracy by private and even state companies, said at the weekend that it would host a forum on protection of intellectual property rights, Reuter reports from Beijing. The symposium in May would bring together more than 200 Chinese and foreign experts to discuss strategies for patent, copyright and trademark protection, Xinhua news agency said. Vice-President Rong Yiren said last week that much needed to be done to improve intellectual property protection in China, whose laws on the issue are less than three years old.
