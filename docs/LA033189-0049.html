March 31, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

GARRETT INELIGIBLE AT OCEANSIDE

Oceanside High athlete Jerry Garrett has been ruled academically ineligible for the second time this school year and will no longer compete in track.

Garrett, a star quarterback, was also ruled ineligible midway through last football season.

Garrett ran the 100 meters, long jumped and anchored the 400 relay before leaving the track team.
