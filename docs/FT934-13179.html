931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / International Company News: Inland Steel issues profits warning INLAND Steel, the US integrated steel company, warned fourth-quarter results would be adversely affected by costs related to the relining of a blast furnace. It said it was evaluating part of its coke-making operation, and any decision to close it could lead to charges in the final three months of the year, writes Richard Waters in New York. Inland announced third-quarter net income of Dollars 16.9m, or 25 cents a share, against a loss of Dollars 45m in the same period a year before, on sales of Dollars 972m.
