Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (XINHUA) -- An agreement of judicial assistance in civil and commercial matters and cooperation in arbitration between China and Thailand was signed here today. The agreement was signed by Chinese Minister of Justice Xiao Yang and Thai Minister of Justice Sawai Phatthano, on behalf of their respective governments. Following the signing ceremony Xiao Yang said that the signing of the agreement marked a new stage in judicial cooperation and exchanges between the two countries.
