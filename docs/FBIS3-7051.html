Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- Shafqat Ali Cheema, charge d'affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy here, hosted a reception at Pyongyang Koryo Hotel Wednesday on the day of Pakistan. Invited to the reception were Yang Hyong-sop, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, Chong Ha-chol, chairman of the Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and chairman of the Korea-Pakistan Friendship Association, Cho Kyu-il, vice-minister of foreign affairs, Kim Chong-u, vice-chairman of the State External Economic Affairs Commission, Chon Yon-ok, vice-chairperson of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and other officials concerned. Diplomatic envoys of different countries here were also invited. Speeches were made at the reception.
