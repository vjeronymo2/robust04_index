February 24, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MARKETS

Your editorial "Making It in the Inner-City" (Feb. 9) was right on target!

When South Los Angeles citizens stop patronizing those markets that refuse to locate in their areas, those deserters will return.

If Boys Markets can operate in the black communities and make a profit, the other markets surely can, too.

BRAD PYE JR.

Assistant Chief Deputy to Supervisor Kenneth Hahn

Los Angeles
