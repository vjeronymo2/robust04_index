921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: LAIT improves 15% to Pounds 554,000 Profits of London & Associated Investment Trust, the property company, rose to Pounds 554,000 pre-tax for the half year ended June 30, an improvement of 15 per cent over last time's Pounds 483,000. Gross income increased by 17 per cent to Pounds 1.97m. Earnings amounted to 0.52p (0.72p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 0.05p. The directors said current group annual rental income from the UK retail property portfolio of over 500 shops was running in excess of Pounds 4m. Gearing was at a 'modest level' and cash deposits stand at Pounds 3m.
