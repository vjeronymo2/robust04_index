Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis expressed particular satisfaction with the results of the EU [European Union]-Cyprus joint parliamentary committee meeting. Speaking upon his return from Brussels this evening, Mikhailidhis described as very significant, the statement of Hans Van Den Broek, EU commissioner on foreign affairs, that Cyprus should not be a hostage to never-ending negotiations on accession into the European Union, and that whatever the result of the proximity talks, Cyprus should not be obstructed from its direction toward Europe.
