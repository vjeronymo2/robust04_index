BFN [By Olga Solodova for CRIME-PRESS] [Text] Moscow, 14 Jun -- Aleksey Ilyushenko, the acting procurator-general of the Russian Federation, has made an application to the State Duma requesting that it consent to criminal prosecution being brought against Federal Assembly deputy Vladimir Zhirinovskiy for war propaganda (Article 71 of the Russian Criminal Code) and for inciting ethnic hostility and tension (Article 74 of the Russian Criminal Code). The information and public relations center of the Russian Procuracy General stated that the conclusions of a preliminary investigation which is now being conducted by the Russian Procuracy General into a criminal case instituted on 26 January of this year in connection with the publication and distribution of Vladimir Zhirinovskiy's book "The Last Thrust to the South" formed the grounds for this application.
