INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT COOPERATION AGENCY Agency for International Development Housing Guaranty Program; Notice of Investment Opportunity The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) has authorized the guaranty of a loan to the Government of the Kingdom of Morocco (``Borrower'') as part of USAID's development assistance program. The proceeds of this loan will be used to enhance land development for shelter projects for the benefit of low-income families in Morocco. At this time, the Borrower has authorized USAID to request proposals from eligible lenders for a loan under this program of 20 million U.S. dollars (US$20,000,000).
