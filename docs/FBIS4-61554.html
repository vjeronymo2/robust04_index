BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 8 May -- The Nagorno-Karabakh leadership today gave the order to its forces to cease-fire unilaterally along the entire front at midnight of 8-9 May. Stepanakert is thereby demonstrating its respect for the appeal addressed to the warring sides by participants in the Bishkek meeting of CIS parliamentarians on a settlement in Nagorno-Karabakh. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed of this in the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic press and information department. It was reported from Stepanakert at the same time that this morning the Azerbaijani side embarked upon a large-scale offensive in the north of Markadertskiy Rayon towards the villages of Chailu, Leninavan, and Karmiravan. The press service of the Karabakh Army staff has announced that its subunits "are successfully repulsing all attacks by the enemy, inflicting on the enemy tangible losses in men and military equipment." According to official sources in Stepanakert, positional exchanges of fire and localized fighting took place on other sectors of the Karabakh front last night and this morning.
