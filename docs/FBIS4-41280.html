BFN [Text] The Nagorno-Karabakh Defense Army Headquarters reports that the situation on the Karabakh front line has not changed. there were no serious incidents over the past day. However, Azerbaijan's military forces fired on the Armenian defense positions with various types of arms from time to time. The Armenian side refrained from returning the fire. No serious incidents occurred on the border between Armenia and Azerbaijan during the past day. The situation is relatively peaceful. However, it is difficult to predict how long the peaceful situation will continue. Everything depends on Azerbaijan. As a rule, Azerbaijan's aggressive operations cause serious incidents in the region. The Armenian side continues to heed the cease-fire agreement along the border.
