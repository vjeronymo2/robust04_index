BFN [Text] The Diyala criminal court has passed hand amputation sentences on two culprits and ordered that a third partner be apprehended for the crime of stealing five carpets from the Buhruz al-Kabir Mosque in the Ba'qubah District on the night of 20 January 1994. The culprits are 'Aziz Sa'd Dahash, a 32-year-old employee; Nadir 'Ali Fulayyih, a 23-year-old soldier; and Faruq Ahmad, a 37- year-old retiree and fugitive. The perpetrators used a government vehicle belonging to the Water and Sewage Department of the Kan'an Subdistrict.
