BFN [Text] The National Democratic Front [NDF] and other militant groups are asking for the immediate release of arrested rebel leader Wilma Tiamzon and a 300 other political detainees. The request was made by NDF Vice Chairperson Luis Jalandoni and the hierarchy of the League of Filipino Students. The group said that Tiamzon's rights and and those of her comrades should be respected so that the peace process being pushed by the Ramos Government can be successful. According to NDF, Tiamzon should be freed since no charges have yet been filed against her in court.
