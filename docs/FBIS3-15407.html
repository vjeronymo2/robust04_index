Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] Turkish President Suleyman Demirel received Kazakh Ambassador Kanat Saidabayev. He conveyed special greetings from President Nursultan Nazarbayev, and informed him of some recent aspects of internal and foreign policy of our Republic. The head of the Turkish state expressed his gratitude to Nursultan Nazarbayev for the detailed and warm letter, which, in his view, was another proof of the development of friendship and cooperation between Kazakhstan and Turkey, and the close personal relations between the two leaders. The Turkish president noted that the mutual cooperation on political, economic, cultural, and humanitarian spheres are deepening and widening. For example, he said that Turkish Government credit is being implemented successfully, the International Kazakh-Turkish University named after Hoja Ahmed Yasevi is already functioning, and more than 1,500 students are studying in Turkey. Suleyman Demirel sent his warm and sincere greetings to President Nursultan Nazarbayev and all Kazakhstani people.
