941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Clayhithe recovers Clayhithe, the investment holding company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 301,000 for the six months to September 30, against losses of Pounds 210,000. Turnover was Pounds 13.6m, against Pounds 16.3m including Pounds 4.03m from discontinued activities, giving underlying growth of 11 per cent. Earnings per share were 0.4p (losses 3.3p) and the interim dividend is raised to 0.8p (0.75p). The company said Horstmann Timers showed considerable improvement and further progress had been made with the reorganisation of the engineering companies.
