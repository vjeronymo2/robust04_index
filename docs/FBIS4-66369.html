CSO [Text] Recently, the Northwest Petroleum Geology Bureau of the Ministry of Geology and Mineral Resources verified a medium-sized oil and gas field with impressive geological reserves. The field is located in the northern part of the Tarim Basin in Luntai Xian, Xinjiang Province. The field has been named the "Western Daliya" oil and gas field and it covers an area of 11.5 square kilometers. The deposit has a thickness of 17.4 meters.
