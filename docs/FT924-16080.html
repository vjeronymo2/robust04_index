921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / Thames Riverbus wins a reprieve RIVERBUS, THE loss-making Thames passenger service running between central London and Docklands, won a temporary reprieve yesterday, hours before it was due to close at midnight. The administrators of Olympia & York's Canary Wharf development in Docklands and other interested parties have agreed to keep the service for up to a month to allow time for negotiations with the government over Canary Wharf's future.
