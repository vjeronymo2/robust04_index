941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / Letters to the Editor: Result could hardly be otherwise Sir, Your report on council reforms stated that there was overwhelming support in Herefordshire for a unitary authority ('Council reform faces further setback', November 1). The result could hardly be otherwise because we were not asked if we wished for no-change. David Delaney, The Mill House, Mortimers Cross, Nr Leominster, Herefordshire HR6 9PE
