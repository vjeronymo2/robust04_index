Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Esteemed Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani chaired a session of the cabinet today. The Afghan president's press department told the Bakhtar Information Agency that opening the meeting, cabinet members presented reports concerning problems and difficulties in the work of some ministries. Then the esteemed president spoke in detail about the country's military situation and recent developments as well as about the conspiratorial measures and coup d'etat launched by the Golboddin-Dostam militia. The cabinet meeting made fundamental and important decisions about the current situation in the country.
