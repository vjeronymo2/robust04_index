Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Ahmad Shakir] [Excerpts] Amman--Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah has said that the Yemeni Government and people are looking forward to His Majesty King Husayn's blessing of the national reconciliation between President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. He explained that it is premature to set a date for this reconciliation, but added it will take place within the next few weeks, and no later than the end of this month. In a telephone statement to AL-DUSTUR yesterday, Basindwah said that immediately after his return from his recent visit to Jordan, Yemeni Deputy Prime Minister Brigadier General Mujahid Abu-Shawarib briefed the Yemeni Council of Ministers on the results of his meeting with His Majesty King Husayn. Abu-Shawarib also conveyed to the Council of Ministers his majesty's extreme interest in maintaining the scared Yemeni unity and the accomplishments that have been achieved so far. [passage omitted] Asked whether other Arab leaders will attend the reconciliation ceremony, the Yemeni foreign minister said that His Majesty King Husayn will be the only sponsor at the historic meeting. He said King Husayn had a key role in containing the crisis because he is respected, appreciated, and loved by the Yemeni president, vice president, and people. [passage omitted] In another development, AL-DUSTUR has learned that a grand official and popular ceremony will be held at the al-Jund Mosque in Ta'izz before the end of this month. The ceremony will be attended by the Yemeni president, vice president, and all official and popular leaders to vow at the home of God to resolve problems and maintain the sacred Yemeni unity.
