CSO [News Report: "Eight Kilogram Heroin Recovered From Car"] [Text] Peshawar (Staff Reporter)--The Excise and Customs Squad of Bannu established a check point near Bannu Township and recovered eight kilograms of heroin from hidden compartments of a car. The driver fled taking advantage of night's darkness. A case was registered against the unknown smuggler. According to information made available the Customs Intelligence Squad of Bannu received information that a large amount of drug was to be smuggled by a Suzuki car from Gair to Punjab. A check point was established near Bannu Township on Kohat-Bannu Road. Around 8:00 p.m. a car, tag number DNC-9317, was seen coming from tribal area. When the car came closer, the driver was signaled to stop the car. He ignored the signal and sped away. The squad chased him. The driver on seeing the danger, left the car on the roadside and made good his escape under cover of darkness.
