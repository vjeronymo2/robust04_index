Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Mar (MENA)--PLO Executive Committee member Mahmud 'Abbas, also known as Abu-Mazin, arrived in Cairo this evening on a two-day visit to Egypt. Arriving, he said he will meet later tonight with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa to discuss the latest Palestinian-Israeli contacts and the UN Security Council debate and to search for a suitable solution to resume talks with the Israelis to implement the Gaza-Jericho Agreement. Abu-Mazin said the Palestinian leadership is always eager for contacts and consultations with Egypt as a full partner to the negotiations.
