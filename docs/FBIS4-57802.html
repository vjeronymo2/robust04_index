BFN [Report by Antonio Marcello and Pamela Nunes] [Excerpt] Brasilia, 24 May (AE) -- The next president of the Republic will be elected for a four-year term and will not be able to seek reelection. The Constituent Congress today approved for the second time a constitutional amendment that reduces the presidential term from five to four years. Of the 357 congressmen in attendance, 323 voted in favor of the amendment, 29 voted against it, and five abstained. The possibility of an incumbent president seeking reelection already had been denied during the first vote taken on the issue. [passage omitted]
