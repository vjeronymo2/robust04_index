BFN [Text] Seoul, May 19 (YONHAP) -- The United States Forces in Korea (USFK) Thursday [19 May] launched a three-day defense readiness drill involving USFK dependents and American civilians residing in the country, a spokesman for the Korea-U.S. Combined Forces Command (CFC) said. The semi-annual drill called Non-combatant Evacuation Operation (NEO) will include the checking of manpower, paperwork and the operation to evacuate non-combatants in the event of war on the Korean peninsula, the spokesman said. The spokesman added, "the drill is just part of the routine semiannual program having nothing to do with the current situation in which tension has escalated due to the standoff involving Pyongyang's nuclear weapons program."
