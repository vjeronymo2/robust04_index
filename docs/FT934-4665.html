931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 197 tonnes for the week ended 26 November, against 60 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases, mainly in Bali and Cameroon descriptions. Benin growths made some headway. JUTE September/October c and f Dundee: BTC Dollars 465, BWC Dollars 490, BTD Dollars 440, BWD Dollars 455. c and f Antwerp: BTC Dollars 455, BWC Dollars 455, BTD Dollars 425, BWD Dollars 425.
