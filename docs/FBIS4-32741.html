BFN [Text] Tirana, June 28 (ATA) -- The achievement of a rapid economic growth constitutes our greatest trial, it is a historic challenge that we must win, President Berisha said yesterday at the National Council of the Democratic Party in Tirana. Mr. Berisha said that the achievement of a rapid economic growth will also assure to make up for the lost valuable time. Referring to an IMF information last February which ranked Albania the first in Europe for the rapid economic growth of about 10 percent, Mr. Berisha said that realization of these short term rapid rates marks a great achievement for Albania and that a long term, not short term, guarantee of these high norms and rates will be Archimedean Screw for its integration into the bosom of the developed countries of Europe. Although the Albanian economy will continue for many years to have a real income and consumption per capita among the lowest one in Europe, an open economic policy based on freedom would help maintain an economic growth of 8-10 percent per annum and for a long -term period, President Berisha said, basing the rapid economic growth on the intellectual potential, geographical position, natural resources and a favourable market of labour power at home.
