931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met strong general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Selected best & good medium north Indians ruled fully firm, but plainer sorts were again easier. Bright East Africans remained firm while coloury mediums advanced 3-5p. Plainer types were unchanged. Ceylons met good demand & prices moved 4-8p. Offshore: Strong competition at dearer rates. Quotations quality 200p/Kg, good medium 127p/Kg, medium 114p/Kg. low medium 95p/Kg. The highest price realised this week was 216p/Kg for an Assam pf.
