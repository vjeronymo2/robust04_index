930430 FT 30 APR 93 / International Company News: Charge puts Wang Labs into the red WANG Laboratories, the US office computer systems company attempting to emerge from bankruptcy proceedings, reported third-quarter net losses of Dollars 87.2m, or 51 cents a share. These included estimated restructuring charges of Dollars 75.8m related to a reorganisation plan. Last year there was a first-quarter loss of Dollars 6.3m or 4 cents, when Wang recorded a pre-tax gain of Dollars 29.4m from asset sales and changes in its US pension plan. Last month, Wang filed a restructuring plan with the bankruptcy court describing its plans to end manufacturing operations and transform itself into a software and services company. For the nine-month period, the net loss was Dollars 153.4m or 90 cents a share, against a net loss of Dollars 22.9m or 14 cents for the same period last year.
