Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Minister of Interior Bisharah Mirhij has refused to disclose the identity of the Palestinians who assassinated the Jordanian diplomat. He said: We are not yet sure of their affiliation with any Palestinian faction. Mirhij affirmed that the extraordinary security measures adopted by the Lebanese security forces and Army will remain in force to deal with the upcoming political phase, denying any security slackness. He stressed the maintenance of security for all embassies and diplomatic missions in Lebanon.
