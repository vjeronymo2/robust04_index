BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher in the People's Palace at 1130 today. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Dennis Ross, the U.S. Middle East peace coordinator. Christopher conveyed to the president a message from U.S. President Bill Clinton in which he emphasizes the U.S. desire to continue its efforts to realize substantive progress in the peace process. President Hafiz al-Asad expressed his appreciation for the U.S. efforts and stressed Syria's desire to continue the peace process to realize a just and comprehensive solution for the Arab-Israeli dispute in accordance with international resolutions and the land-for-peace principle. Mr. Christopher briefed the president on the results of his talks in Israel which demonstrated that the differences are wide and great because of the large distance between the Israeli stand and the pillars of peace which were established at the Madrid conference. In this regard, the president reaffirmed Syria's stand and its adherence to the bases of the peace process based on UN Security Council resolutions which commit Israel to full withdrawal to the 4 June 1967 borders in order to realize just and comprehensive peace in the region.
