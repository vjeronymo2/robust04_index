930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / World News in Brief: Security watchdog 'an option' A watchdog panel of privy councillors to oversee the security services is 'an active option' being considered by the government, officials said. This follows allegations of MI5 involvement in the so-called Camillagate tape, said to be an intimate telephone conversation between the Prince of Wales and his friend Camilla Parker Bowles.
