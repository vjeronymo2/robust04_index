Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] MOSCOW, 13 January -- Deputies of the lower house of the Federal Assembly have decided to hold a secret vote, using ballot papers, for the post of chairman of the State Duma tomorrow, 14 January, in the morning. Following a rating vote, the highest number of votes among the candidates for this post was obtained by Ivan Rybkin from the Agrarian Party faction and Yuriy Vlasov from the "Russian Path" group of deputies. One of them will thus be elected to this post.
