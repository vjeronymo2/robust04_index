Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] The total crop area in Guangxi will be 740,800 hectares, a 1.6 percent decrease from 1993. The sugarcane area will be 24,900 hectares, a 93 percent increase; the vegetable area 254,200 hectares, a 23 percent increase; the cereal area 19,800 hectares, a 22 percent decrease; and the green manure area 249,700 hectares, a 23 percent decrease. The oil crop area will increase 10 percent over 1993, and the grain area will increase 1.7 percent.
