BFN [Text] New Zealand Prime Minister Jim Bolger said he is confident that the East Asia Economic Caucus, EAEC, will not cause strain or stress to the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation, APEC, should the caucus remain within APEC. He said this at the end of his three-day visit to Malaysia. Mr. Bolger added that Prime Minister Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir Mohamed had briefed him on the caucus during their discussions.
