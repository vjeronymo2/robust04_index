920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Sleepy Kids deeper loss after provision Sleepy Kids, the USM-quoted co-producer of Potsworth & Co and other animation and character merchandising, incurred an increased pre-tax loss of Pounds 109,000 for the six months to April 30, against Pounds 62,000. The outcome this time was after an exceptional charge of Pounds 69,000 representing a provision against income receivable from Hanna-Barbera. The company also said it planned to raise up to Pounds 301,000 in a private placing. A maximum of 4.3m new ordinary shares will be placed at 7p per share by Shaw & Co with a limited number of institutional and other investors. Turnover fell to Pounds 80,000 (Pounds 110,000) and there was an operating loss of Pounds 50,000 (Pounds 91,000). Losses per share came through at 0.55p (0.38p). Sleepy Kids has exercised an option for a sole and exclusive worldwide licence for the intellectual property rights (excluding certain book rights) over the Budgie character, already well known from the children's books written by the Duchess of York.
