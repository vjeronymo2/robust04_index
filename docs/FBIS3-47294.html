Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 10 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Sunday sent a message of condolences to the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] upon the death of Phoumi Vongvichit, advisor of the LPRP Central Committee. The message says that upon hearing a sad news over the death of Phoumi Vongvichit, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea expresses deep condolences to the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party, the government of the Lao People's Democratic Republic and the Lao people.
