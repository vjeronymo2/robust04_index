940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Greenfriar Inv Greenfriar Investment reported a 24 per cent advance, from 395.4p to 491.8p, in net asset value per share over the 1993 year. Attributable profit improved to Pounds 822,000 (Pounds 767,000), equivalent to earnings of 7.13p (6.66p). A recommended final dividend of 4.5p brings the total for the year to 6.65p (6.35p).
