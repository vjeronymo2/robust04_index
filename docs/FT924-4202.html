921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Substantial cut in Quiligotti losses Quiligotti, which makes and installs terrazzo and other specialised industrial flooring, made substantial inroads into its losses in the half year ended September 30. Although turnover fell Pounds 1.58m to Pounds 8.45m, pre-tax losses declined from Pounds 646,000 to Pounds 228,000. The cost reduction programme took Pounds 80,000 (Pounds 202,000) in exceptional charges. Losses per share were 0.33p (2.38p).
