BFN [Text] At 1000 today President Ilyas al-Hirawi received Syrian Minister of Culture Najah al-'Attar at the Presidential Palace in B'abda. Also attending the meeting were Michel Iddih, minister of culture and higher education, and Nasri Khuri, secretary general of the Higher Lebanese-Syrian Council. Discussions focused on the Lebanese-Syrian cooperation agreement on higher education and scientific research which was signed in Damascus yesterday.
