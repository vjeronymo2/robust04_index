BFN [Text] Nicosia, May 25 (XINHUA) -- The 19th Cyprus International Fair opened here this evening with the participation of more than 1,600 companies from Cyprus and some 40 countries and regions. The inaugural ceremony of the fair, which covered a total space of 41,000 square meters, was opened by Cyprus President Glavkos Kliridhis. More than 10 Chinese companies from Shandong Province and Hangzhou City are also taking part in the 10-day fair, exhibiting light industrial products, chemicals, motorcycles and silk clothes. Touring the China pavilion covering some 250 square meters after the ceremony, President Kliridhis praised the Chinese products and wrote down in the visitors' book: "Well done. Very glad to see the Chinese exhibits."
