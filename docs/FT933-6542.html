930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / Danish shipyard subsidies Danish shipyards will receive a DKr600m (Pounds 57.7m) subsidy package following an agreement yesterday between the industry ministry and key opposition parliamentary parties, AP-DJ reports from Copenhagen. Unemployment in the Danish shipbuilding sector has risen significantly in the last six months because of declining orders. The subsidy will take the form of loans at favourable interest rates for shipping lines ordering vessels.
