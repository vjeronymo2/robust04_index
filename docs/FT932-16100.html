930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Metsec losses rise to Pounds 731,000 Despite an improved second half performance, Metsec, the USM-quoted building, electronic and engineering products concern, turned in higher pre-tax losses of Pounds 731,000 in 1992, against Pounds 257,000 previously. Turnover fell from Pounds 79.8m to Pounds 60.2m. US and Australian losses had a sizeable impact on results, but those losses had been stemmed and prospects for a return to profits had improved. Overall, the group's profitable trend had continued into 1993. Gearing stood at 65.7 per cent and it remained the group's priority to reduce this to more acceptable levels. Losses per share increased from 2.07p to 5.59p, and there is no dividend (6.4p).
