Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Guatemala City, 20 Feb (ACAN-EFE) -- At least two firemen were killed and five other people injured when a grenade exploded at a fire station in San Lucas Sacatepequez near Guatemala City. The Volunteer Firemen's press office told ACAN-EFE that the device was thrown inside the station yesterday by unidentified men and exploded in the hands of Noe Morales De la Rosa. De la Rosa and Boris Manfredo Bonilla were killed on the spot. Victor Rolando Aguilar Castro, Byron Cisneros Camey, and three members of the Guatemalan Scout Association (Alba Ramirez, Emili Rodriguez and Miriam Rodriguez) were injured. The Volunteer Firemen are demanding that the authorities investigate the attack and that its perpetrators be punished.
