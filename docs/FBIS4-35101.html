BFN [Text] Cordoba, 28 Jun (NA) -- Manuel Mondino, the National Commission for Atomic Energy [CNEA] chairman, has denied that the construction of a new nuclear power plant in Cordoba is being reconsidered and holds journalists responsible for disseminating inaccurate reports on this issue. The CNEA chairman categorically stressed that "no decision has been made." He added that "a decision may be made by the end of the year, if it is made at all." Mondino was very angry with journalists. He stressed that neither "the National Government, the CNEA, nor anyone else has ever discussed the construction of a second nuclear power plant." Mondino described as "nonsense" reports released during President Carlos Menem's visit to Canada, where Menem addressed the issue.
