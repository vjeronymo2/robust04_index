BFN [Text] Loans granted by branches of the Cooperative Agricultural Bank in Diyala Governorate during the last four months have reached 1,660,120 dinars. The loans granted to peasants and farmers were used for different agricultural purposes as follows: 750,000 dinars for carrying out animal resources projects and for poultry farms, 650,800 dinars for well drilling, and 260,120 dinars for purchasing agricultural equipment and developing orchards.
