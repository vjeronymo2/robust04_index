Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat, 17 Jan -- The first congress of the National Revival Movement, which took place in Ashgabat today, ended with the setting up of a National Council. The National Council will unite the activities of public organizations in Turkmenistan, directing its efforts toward reviving the spiritual and moral values of the Turkmen people. President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan was elected chairman of the National Council.
