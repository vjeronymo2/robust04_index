Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Nearly 130 people's organizations, which met at Le Palace Hotel on 27 February, created the Confederation of Haitian People's Organizations for Development [COPHADEV]. According to its general coordinator, Jean-Saurel Beaujour, the organization's purpose is to work toward alleviating the economic and political stagnation in which Haiti is plunged. [passage omitted]
