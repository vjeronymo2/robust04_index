March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEW GROUP READY TO SUPPORT MUSIC CENTER

A new Music Center support group, Applause for the Music Center, will be host of a St. Patrick's Day kickoff party at 6:30 p.m. Friday in The Founders room of the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion.

Prospective members will be welcome.

Applause is for professional men and women between the ages of 35 and 55. The group will hold monthly meetings in the evenings. Information: Lorraine Saunders, (213) 972-7244.
