December 22, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS

Robert A. Wright has been appointed vice president in charge of Dean Worldwide's household goods division for the Southern California region. Previously, he was general manager at the Huntington Beach-based freight forwarder. Before joining Dean, he handled freight forwarding, U.S. trucking and port operations for a military forwarder and managed flatbed trucking operations for Bekins International.
