Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Haskovo, March 6 (BTA correspondent) -- 15.556 kilos of heroin were seized at 1.30 a.m. on Sunday by customs officers at the Kapitan Andreevo checkpoint on the Bulgarian-Turkish border. The drug was found in 28 heavily perfumed bags hidden in the door sills of an Audi car. Arrested were Imam Yetkinbuak and Ali Mutlu, both aged 37, resident in the Netherlands for many years. Both are unemployed. One of them is scheduled for heart surgery. An investigation is under way in Svilengrad. Under Bulgarian law the trafficking of large amounts of drugs carries a 5 to 15 years' prison term.
