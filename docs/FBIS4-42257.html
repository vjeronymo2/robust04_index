BFN [Text] According to reports from Greek mass media correspondents in North Epirus [southern Albania], the Albanian police in Sarande arrested Sotirios Kiriazatis, chairman of [Greek minority organization] Omonoia, late last night and took him to Gjirokaster for interrogation. Mr. Kiriazatis was supposed to visit Tirana tomorrow to testify in the case of the six leading members of the minority organization who are now being detained under serious charges.
