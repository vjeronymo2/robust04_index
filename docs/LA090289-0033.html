September 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RATHER'S STAND-UP ROUTINE

So CBS has announced that Dan Rather may stand to read the news. And Calendar reported this in an 8-inch article, plus a photograph ("Dan Rather May Try a Stand-Up Routine" by Jay Sharbutt, Aug. 19). Wow! Please continue to keep us informed of such earthshaking events. A breathless nation waits.

TERRY FISHER, West Los Angeles
