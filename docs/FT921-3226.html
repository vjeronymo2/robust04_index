920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 20m orders won by Lovell Group The construction division of the LOVELL GROUP, comprising Lovell Construction, Bullock Construction and Walter Lilly, has won contracts worth Pounds 20.5m. In London, the largest, worth Pounds 3.8m, is for refurbishment and the provision of a dealing room for Bank Paribas in Wigmore Street. At Deptford, a Pounds 2.6m project is for 48 flats and 10 commercial units for South London Family Housing Association, while for Homebase at Richmond, a Pounds 2.46m scheme will provide a retail centre. Two contracts, each worth Pounds 2m, are for refurbishment and extension of a research centre at Harefield for Pitman-Moore and refurbishment at two residential blocks at Sunningdale's Civil Service College. In the Midlands, housing improvements for the City of Birmingham and Dudley Metropolitan Borough Council account for Pounds 3m and Pounds 1.42m respectively.
