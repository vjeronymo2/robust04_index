BFN [Text] Ankara, May 11 (A.A.) -- Turkey on Wednesday [11 May] expressed concern about the political crisis in Yemen and about the chance that it might take the shape of armed clashes. Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman said at his weekly briefing that Turkey calls for the Yemeni leaders to stop the conflict in harmony with their people's hope for unity and peace, and wishes that further bloodshedding would stop for the good of the country. `During this critical stage which is going on in the country, Turkey calls for moderation between the two sides and hopes for an agreement to be reached as soon as possible,' Ataman said.
