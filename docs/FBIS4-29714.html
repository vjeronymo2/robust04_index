BFN [Text] Sydney, June 16 (AFP) -- The Australian government has summoned Chinese Ambassador Shi Chunlai to protest his country's June 10 nuclear test blast, officials said Thursday. "Senior department officials protested to him about the test yesterday (Wednesday) and reiterated our view that it was deeply disappointing," said a spokesman for the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade in Canberra. China carried out the underground blast, which had a strength of 10 to 40 kilotonnes of TNT according Australian scientists, at its Lobnor test site on Friday.
