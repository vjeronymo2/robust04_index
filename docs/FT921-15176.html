920111 FT 11 JAN 92 / World News in Brief: IRA bomb explodes near Downing Street An IRA bomb exploded 300 yards from Downing Street, minutes after prime minister John Major left Number 10 to go to Conservative Central Office. The 5lb bomb, concealed in a leather briefcase, was left near government buildings in Whitehall Place. It caused no casualties but damaged cars and some windows in the area. The IRA warned of further attacks unless 'British interference' in Irish affairs ended. IRA bombers exploit gaps in defences, Page 4
