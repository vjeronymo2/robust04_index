930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables The best fruit buys this week are apples from New Zealand reports the FFVIB. Choose from Gala or Gala varieties at 50-55p a lb or Braeburn at 60-75 a lb. Strawberries remain good at 60-65p per 8 oz punnet. along with cherries at 25-30p a 1/4 lb. Homegrown new potatoes at 12-20p a lb are superb this week, together with Primo cabbage at 30-35p a lb carrots at 30-35p a lb and broccoli at 45-55p per 8oz prepack. The English Iceberg lettuce season has started with samples priced at 55-65p each. Other good salad buys include English and Dutch tomatoes at 55-65 a lb.
