Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 21 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree allowing regional authorities to announce elections to local self-government bodies for Autumn, the press service of the Russian leader said on Monday. "The decree says that bodies of state power of the subjects of the Russian Federation taking into account local conditions can announce elections to legislative bodies of local self-government for Autumn 1994", the press service said.
