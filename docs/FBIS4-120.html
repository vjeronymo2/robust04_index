BFN [Text] Durban March 31 SAPA -- Hundreds of kwaZulu civil servants in Umlazi near Durban have vowed not to return to work before the kwaZulu government addresses their demands for their pensions to be paid out, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Thursday [31 March]. The civil servants, some of them from places outside Umlazi, have been meeting at a stadium outside the township since the beginning of the week. Meanwhile, the launch of an African National Congress election campaign for Umlazi pupils has been cancelled by the Congress of South African Students after school principals refused to allow the pupils to attend a meeting on Thursday. School principals are reported to have said that pupils were busy with their quarterly examinations.
