940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Drayton Far Eastern assets rise Net asset value per share of the Drayton Far Eastern Trust rose from 107.3p to 164.8p over the 1993 year. Available revenue improved from Pounds 1.09m to Pounds 1.22m, equal to earnings of 1.05p (0.94p). A proposed final dividend of 0.55p makes a 0.675p (0.625p) total.
