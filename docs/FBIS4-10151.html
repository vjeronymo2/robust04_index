BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 7 April. The Mercantil Bank has issued commercial papers worth $3 million on the international market. The papers were bought by U.S. investors and financial organizations. This is the first time a Peruvian bank carried out such an operation on a short-term basis. (Lima EXPRESO in Spanish 25 Mar 94 p A35) On 25 March, representatives of the State Petroleum Agency and Shell signed an agreement designed to conduct studies on the production and marketing of gas in the Camisea reserves, the results of which will be known in May 1995. (Lima LA REPUBLICA in Spanish 26 Mar 94 p 3)
