Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Guatemala City SIGLO VEINTIUNO in Spanish on 10 March, page 6, reports that, on 9 March, Claudio Sapelli, World Bank official on a two-week visit to Guatemala, affirmed that the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development will make its third loan disbursement during the second half of 1994. Guatemala has already received $48 million of the $120-million loan for the Economic Modernization program.
