Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Buenos Aires, 10 Mar (EFE) -- Gustavo Villalobos Sepulveda, executive secretary of the Chilean National Congress for Drug Control [Congreso Nacional para el Control de Estupefacientes de Chile], was today elected new president of the Inter-American Committee for the Control of Drugs and Drug Abuse (Cicad). Villalobos, who will replace the Costa Rican Monica Berger, will be aided by Panamanian Attorney General Jorge Ramon Valdez. The new Cicad president was elected to this post during the 15th Cicad Congress that began on 8 March in Mar del Plata, 800 km south of Buenos Aires. The congress will end 11 March. [passage omitted]
