Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Eduard Nalbandyan, the Armenian charge d'affaires ad interim in Egypt, and Bogos Akobov, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary, who arrived in Abu Dhabi today on an special mission, met the Deputy Foreign Minister of the United Arab Emirates [UAE] Shaykh Adham Al Nuhayyan and handed him a message from the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan to the President of UAE Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan. The Armenian Foreign Ministry press and public relations service reported that matters pertaining to the establishment and development of ties between the two countries were discussed in a friendly and warm atmosphere.
