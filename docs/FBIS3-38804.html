Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] State Minister Cemil Erhan has left for Tehran to attend the joint economic committee meetings to be held there. In a statement at Ankara's Esenboga Airport before his departure, Erhan said that last year imports from Iran amounted to $590 million, while exports totaled $235 million. Erhan said that during the talks, the two sides will discuss ways of increasing exports to Iran, eliminating the delays in payment, and the issue of receiving additional crude oil in return for goods. He added: We will also discuss border trade, the Iran-Turkey-Europe natural gas pipeline, and cooperation in the fields of agriculture, petrochemicals, transportation, and communications. Erhan will return to Turkey on 10 February.
