Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Examining Magistrate Fawzi Daghir, who is investigating the bombing of Sayyidat Dayr al-Balamand, today passed the death sentence against the six detained criminals. He dropped charges against defendant 'Abdallah al-Hilu, who is now dead. Judge Daghir instructed the security service to arrest the real motivators. He dropped charges against two defendants because of a lack of evidence. Judge Sa'id Mirza demanded that the death sentence be passed against 16 people -- three detained and 13 fugitive -- on charges of killing Jordanian diplomat Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah in January.
