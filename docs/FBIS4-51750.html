BFN [Text] Nagoya, April 27 KYODO -- A group of auto parts makers affiliated with Toyota Motor Corp. said Wednesday [27 April] it will accept parts operations of General Motors Japan Ltd. as its members. The group consists of auto parts makers in Tokyo, Osaka and Nagoya regions. Joining the group are parts and power divisions of the Japanese subsidiary of the major U.S. automaker and electronic control makers affiliated with them, the organization said. They are the first members affiliated with the major U.S. automaker although four automotive firms have already joined the group, the organization said.
