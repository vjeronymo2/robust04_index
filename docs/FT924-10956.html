921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Bradford Property ahead to Pounds 11.8m Bradford Property Trust lifted pre-tax profit from Pounds 11.2m to Pounds 11.8m in the half year to October 5. Operating profit came to Pounds 11.5m (Pounds 10.6m) comprising surplus from property rentals of Pounds 5.3m (Pounds 4.4m) and surplus on sales of dealing properties of Pounds 6.2m (Pounds 6.18m). Earnings per share were 5.43p (5.19p) and the interim dividend is raised to 2.4p (2p).
