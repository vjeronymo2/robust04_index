BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi said the United States should not have made negative statements on the proposed East Asia Economic Caucus [EAEC] until the East Asian nations had given their views. He said the United States should first listen to the views of the East Asian nations. He said ASEAN will hold a meeting in East Asian countries soon to discuss the matter officially. He added that when all the East Asian countries have stated their views, then the other countries can give their comments. He said this after receiving the Russian Minister of Culture Yevgeniy Sidorov and the Secretary General of Libyan People's Congress on Foreign Affairs (Chad Mujabba) at his ministry in Kuala Lumpur. He said for the first time, the East Asian countries should discuss the matter first as the issue affects them. Datuk Abdullah was asked about the government's efforts to make the EAEC a reality after the U.S. has opposed the proposal.
