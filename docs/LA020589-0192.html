February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX;

POP MUSIC;

TUNING IN TO THE WORLD

Looking for a musical journey? The music of the world awaits listeners on the following radio programs:

Time/Program/Description/Host/Station

SUNDAY

6 a.m.-6 p.m. "Alma del Barrio." Mixture of Latin and South American music shows with multiple DJs; bilingual. KXLU-FM (88.9).

8-10 a.m. "La Voz Latina." Latin music, principally salsa. Abe Hernandez, Jr., & Don Griego. KCSN-FM (88.5).

10 a.m.-12:05 p.m. "L' Chayim." Israeli/Jewish music. Cantor Michael Russ. KCSN.

2-5 p.m. "The Reggae Beat." Reggae music from Jamaica. Hank Holmes & Chuck Foster. KCRW-FM (89.9).

7-9:30 p.m. "American Continental Hours." German music. Wolfgang Schneider. KCSN.

9:30 p.m.-12 midnight. "Music From Land of Windmills." Dutch music. Hans Sauer. KCSN.

MONDAY

9 a.m.-12 noon. "Morning Becomes Eclectic." International pop styles are included in its varied musical mix. Tom Schnabel. KCRW. Also Tues-Friday.

9:30-11:30 a.m. "Morning Concert." Eclectic world music. Mario Casetta. KPFK-FM (90.7).

WEDNESDAY

1-1:30 a.m. "Reggae Revolution." Reggae music. Roberto & the Swedish Eagle. KROQ-FM (106.7).

9:30-11:30 p.m. "Spectrum." Black music from Africa and the Caribbean. Hamilton Cloud & Paul Hall. KPFK.

THURSDAY

9:30-11:30 a.m. "Sounds of Brazil." Brazilian music. Sergio Mielniczenko. KPFK.

9-11 p.m. "Vibes . . . So Nice." Reggae music. Barbara Barabino. KFOX-FM. (93.5)

10:50-11:30 p.m. "Usted, Nosotros y Nueva Cancion. "New song" (socially conscious folk music) from Latin America. Produced by the "Tajaro Latino" group. KPFK.

FRIDAY

9:30-11:30 a.m. "Belize/Caribbean Pulse." Caribbean sounds. Produced by "BREDA" organization. KPFK.

11 p.m.-12 midnight. "Latin Dimensions." Latin music, principally salsa. Nina Lenart & Alan Geik. KCRW.

SATURDAY

6 a.m.-6 p.m. "Alma del Barrio." See Sunday listing. KXLU.

2-5 p.m. "The African Beat." Pop music from Africa. C. C. Smith & Solomon Solo. KCRW.

2:30-5:30 p.m. "Sounds of Jamaica." Reggae music. Rita Wirewaist. KPFK.

6-7 p.m. "Groove Time." World music. Big Red. KXLU.

7-8 p.m. "Reggae Music Forward." Reggae music. Tomas. KXLU.

7-9:30 p.m. "Canto Tropical." Salsa/Afro-Caribbean music. Hector Resendez. KPFK.

8-9 p.m. "Thistle and Shamrock." Irish traditional music. Fiona Ritchie. KUSC-FM (91.5).

9:30-12 midnight. "Fusion and Beyond." World pop music with a jazzy bent. Dr. Don Mizell. KPFK.
