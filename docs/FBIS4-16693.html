BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasir 'Arafat begins an official visit to Moscow on 19 April. On the eve of his visit, Yasir 'Arafat gave an exclusive interview to a correspondent for our information agency at the PLO headquarters in Tunis: [Begin 'Arafat recording in Arabic with superimposed Russian translation] Russia has played an active role in the Near East peace process ever since it started. The Russian foreign minister and the U.S. secretary of state stood alongside myself and the Israeli prime minister when the Washington agreement on the principles of a settlement was signed. It was precisely thanks to the recent increase in Russian diplomatic activity, Yasir 'Arafat continues, that certain difficulties which arose in the negotiating process were overcome, allowing it to resume in Cairo. The peace talks are being held not just in the interests of the Palestinians or the Israelis. They are in the interests of all mankind. Realizing this, and undertaking efforts in this direction, we have achieved those results which only became possible because of the peace talks. [end recording] [Correspondent] To conclude his short interview, Yasir 'Arafat noted that there has been a durable and friendly relationship between Palestinians and Russia for many years now. But in looking ahead he would like to point out that in future there could be new developments in this relationship because of the possibility of cooperation in the repair of infrastructure in the Palestinian autonomous areas, the training of personnel, and other spheres. He takes this opportunity to pass on the warmest and most sincere greetings to the peoples of Russia.
