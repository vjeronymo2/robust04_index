BFN [Text] Likud Knesset Member Ari'el Sharon says he has decided to bid for the premiership in the next elections. He says he is working to set up a national coalition which will include a number of personalities and groups. He mentioned in particular the Likud's former foreign minister, David Levi. In an interview with Qol Yisra'el, Sharon also said that he was not leaving the Likud party as part of his bid for a leadership role. He hoped there would be primaries to head the national coalition.
