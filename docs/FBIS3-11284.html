Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The presence of Professor Rodrigo Alvarez Cambras in Ciudad Bolivar and Puerto Ordaz, Venezuela, is proof of the interest on the part of Venezuela's public health leadership in developing aspects of holistic and sports medicine. Alvarez Cambras said he had been able to hold exchanges with Venezuelan scholars on spinal surgery and [word indistinct] in ankle lesion surgery at the Social Security Hospital in Puerto Ordaz. Alvarez, who is director of Havana's Frank Pais orthopedic teaching hospital, said he performed six operations and held a practicum workshop during which aspects of orthopedics were assessed. Alvarez Cambras explained that two protocols of intent were signed: one, on exchange of information in metallurgy and the development of orthopedic instruments and parts; the other, with the Social Security Hospital where our doctors will go several times a year to train specialists.
