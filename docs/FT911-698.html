910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / International Company News: United Engineers turns in 35% first-quarter surge UNITED Engineers, the Malaysian cement, construction and engineering group, yesterday unveiled strong first-quarter results. The company lifted pre-tax profits for the first quarter by 35 per cent to MDollars 35m (USDollars 12.6m) from MDollars 24m during the same period last year. Turnover advanced to MDollars 99m, up from MDollars 88m previously, while quarterly net profit rose 17 per cent to MDollars 20m to give earnings of 4.4 Malaysian cents a share, compared with 3.7 cents earlier. Other companies within the group, Time Engineering, Cement Industries (Cima) and Kinta Kellas, all reported a surge in pre-tax profit during the same period. Both Time and Kinta Kellas reversed their previous operating losses for gains of MDollars 0.3m and MDollars 8m, respectively, on MDollars 44m and MDollars 14m in turnover. At Cima, operating profit rose 41 per cent to MDollars 8m on sales of MDollars 37m. Net profits, which soared by 73 per cent and more among the three companies, were MDollars 6m for Time, MDollars 5m for Kinta Kellas, and MDollars 11m for Cima.
