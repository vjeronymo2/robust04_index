941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Undervalued Assets Undervalued Assets Trust, managed by Scottish Value Management, had a net asset value per share of 96.4p at September 30. This figure represented a 0.3 per cent rise since the trust was incorporated in March, during which time the FT-SE-A All-Share Index fell 5 per cent. Net revenue for the period March 17 to September 30 was Pounds 466,000. Earnings per share came out at 0.87p.
