931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Delegates call for banning of BNP THE party yesterday called for a ban on the British National Party (BNP), which recently won a council seat in an East London by-election, Kevin Brown writes. Delegates approved an emergency resolution calling for a ban on 'all organisations which pursue policies and actions which constitute an incitement to racial hatred and violence'. Mr Bill Morris, the TGWU general union leader, said there was 'no right of free speech for fascists'.
