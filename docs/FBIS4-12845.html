BFN [Text] The Islamic resistance forces last week carried out two brave operations in which they succeeded in blowing up and destroying large parts of the road that links the al-Kahla' district to the subdistrict of al-Musharrah at various points. In addition, they blew up the (al-Ghutayji) bridge across the (al-Ghutayji) River, which is a tributary of the al-Musharrah River and pours into the al-Qubbah marshland east of the al-'Amarah Governorate. He added that the targets were considered two of the most important roads of communication used by the regime's forces in transporting equipment and troops recently. A source in the Islamic resistance forces clarified that resistance groups distributed leaflets in the places where the regime's forces were deployed. They warned these forces against becoming involved in the regime's bloody schemes. It called on them to disobey orders and direct their weapons at tyrant Saddam's aides and his collapsing regime, which is on the brink of annihilation.
