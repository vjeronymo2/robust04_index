Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Paraguayan President Juan Carlos Wasmosy yesterday acknowledged the existence of poverty and landless peasants, adding that the administration will need more funds all the time to finance agriculture and development. He said this to Argentine Chaco Province officials who visited him at Government House. Speaking about the country's needs, the president stated that in its quest for the wellbeing of all those who live all Paraguay, his administration is committed to doing everything it can to help the peasants. The president also mentioned other urgent issues such as education and health. He explained that his administration will do everything it can to satisfy the various strata of the population.
