940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Near-47% rise in Finsbury Smaller net asset value A near-47 per cent growth in net asset value per share, from 116.7p to 171.4p, was yesterday reported by Finsbury Smaller Companies Trust for 1993. The performance outpaced the trust's benchmark - the Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index - which rose 37 per cent over the same period. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 436,000 (Pounds 283,000) for earnings of 3.2p (3p) per share, all of which, following a proposed final dividend of 1.4p, will be distributed to shareholders.
