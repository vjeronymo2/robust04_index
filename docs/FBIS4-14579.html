BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Denis Perkin] [Text] Moscow April 1 ITAR-TASS -- The United Nations secretary-general, Butrus Butrus-Ghali has arrived for an official visit to Moscow on Monday. He told Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who welcomed him to the Russian capital at Sheremetevo airport, he was glad to be in Moscow again. The five-day visit agenda includes meetings and talks with President Boris Yeltsin, the foreign and defence ministers, and chairmen of the State Duma and Federation Council committees. Butrus Butrus-Ghali is also due to meet the Russian Orthodox Church head, Patriarch Aleksey of Moscow and all Russia.
