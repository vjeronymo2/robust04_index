Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 25 Feb (MENA)--The PLO is holding the Israeli Government responsible for the criminal and dangerous massacre committed by the armed Israeli settlers against Palestinian citizens while they were praying at the Hebron mosque at dawn this morning. In a statement it issued this morning, the PLO asked the U.S. and Russian cosponsors of peace, the permanent and nonpermanent members of the Security Council, the European Union, the nonaligned countries, the Islamic countries, and all the international community to stop these dangerous crimes and massacres against the Palestinian people. In addition, it asked that international protection be provided immediately for the Palestinian people. The PLO asked the masses of the Palestinian people to be careful and alert to these premeditated schemes, to confront them in united rank, and to consider the coming three days as days of mourning and general strike. The statement notes that the Palestinian leadership will follow up the situation on all levels in order to confront all possibilities.
