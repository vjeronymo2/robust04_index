BFN [Text] Doha, 20 Apr (WAKH) -- The Amir of Qatar, Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani has received a verbal message from President Alija Izetbegovic about developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina and the ongoing aggression on the defenseless Bosnian Muslims. The QATARI NEWS AGENCY reports that the message also deals with Islamic and international efforts to put an end to Serbian aggression, particularly in Gorazde. The message was conveyed by the Bosnian Foreign Minister Dr. Irfan Ljubijankic, when he called on the amir here today.
