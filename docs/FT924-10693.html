921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Thai net assets off 9% The net asset value of Abtrust New Thai Investment Trust was 68.48p at August 31 - a fall of 9 per cent on the trust's year-end figure of 75.34p reflecting the sterling's strength against the Thai baht. Net asset value at end-August 1991 was 68.79p. Attributable revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 129,241 (Pounds 163,226), equivalent to earnings of 0.86p (1.09p).
