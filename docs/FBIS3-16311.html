Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vyacheslav Bantin] [Text] Tokyo March 15 TASS -- The expected meeting between Japan's Minister of Foreign Affairs Tsutomu Hata and Russian President Boris Yeltsin has been cancelled, Kyodo TSUSHIN news agency reported today, citing source in the Japanese Foreign Ministry. Tsutomu Hata, who arrives in Moscow on March 19, will hold talks with Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. Boris Yeltsin will be unable to receive the Japanese minister, because he is out of Moscow for a vacation in the Black Sea coast resort of Sochi.
