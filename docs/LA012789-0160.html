January 27, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BANDS TO LOCK HORNS IN BATTLE AGAIN AT BOGART'S

The Rock City Rumble, an ongoing series of battle-of-the-bands rock shows by Orange County and Long Beach groups, continues tonight at Bogart's in Long Beach where the Pivot Foots, the Creed, Lovingkindness, Violet Burning and Lifestyle will compete from 9:15 till closing. Winner and runners-up will be determined by votes of the audience and a panel of judges. Bogart's is in Marina Pacifica mall, 6288 E. Pacific Coast Highway. Admission: $5. MIKE BOEHM
