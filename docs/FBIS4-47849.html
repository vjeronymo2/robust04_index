BFN [Excerpt] President Yoweri Museveni has had a meeting with the special representative of the UN secretary general in Rwanda, Dr. Jacques-Roger Booh-Booh, who is also the UN under secretary general. The meeting took place at the president's office in Kampala. Dr. Booh-Booh delivered a special message from the UN secretary general, Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali, and afterwards briefed the president on the situation in Rwanda and informed the president that the United Nations is carrying out a wide range of consultations to see how best the UN can contribute to the alleviation of the suffering of the people in Rwanda. Dr. Booh-Booh also briefed the president on the present efforts in Arusha aimed at bringing the Rwandan groups to dialogue. President Museveni welcomed the efforts to promote dialogue between the Rwandan groups and expressed deep regret at the killings and suffering taking place in Rwanda. He pledged support to the initiatives to promote dialogue and expressed hope that such could result into a solution. [passage omitted]
