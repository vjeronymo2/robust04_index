Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on insurgent activities in Peru compiled from Peruvian papers monitored through 3 February. The Ayacucho Political-Military Command announced the arrest of seven "terrorists" southwest of Ayacucho Department. One of the main leaders has been identified as Pablo Condori Vega, 44, "Comrade Castro," who is considered a leader in Puquio, the capital of Pucanas Province. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 20 Jan 94 p A13) "Terrorists" exploded 10 kg of dynamite in downtown Huancayo at 1915 on 23 January in the parking lot of Hotel President, destroying three vehicles and seriously damaging the hotel. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 25 Jan 94 p A7) General Alfredo Rodriguez Rivera, new commander of the Huallaga Political-Military Command, reported that 2,200 "terrorists" who repented have surrendered in the region. They have asked for protection under the repentance law. Rodriguez added that 1,200 belong to the Tupac Amaru Revolutionary Movement and the rest to the Shining Path. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 27 Jan 94 p A11)
