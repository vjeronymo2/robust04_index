930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / International Company News: Genting invests in Subic Bay GENTING, the Malaysian conglomerate with a minority stake in Lonrho of the UK, is one of a group of Malaysian companies planning to invest nearly USDollars 100m in recreation facilities at Subic Bay, the former US naval base in the Philippines. Genting and Resorts World, a sister company, will be lead investors in developing a 400-room hotel, a golf course, a casino and various other entertainment facilities at Subic. In the year to December 1991, most of Genting's pre-tax profits of MDollars 581.5m (USDollars 221m) came from Malaysia's only casino, outside Kuala Lumpur.
