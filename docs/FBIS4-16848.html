BFN [Text] As has already been reported, five candidates were nominated for the post of speaker yesterday: Abish Kekilbayev, Gaziz Aldamzharov, Sultan Sartayev, Olzhas Suleymenov, and Kuanysh Sultanov. At the afternoon session Suleymenov withdrew his candidature. Today after voting, two candidates had the majority of votes: Abish Kekilbayev and Gaziz Aldamzharov. Ninety-two deputies voted for and 84 against Abish Kekilbayev; and 62 for and 107 against Gaziz Aldamzharov. So, Abish Kekilbayev was elected chairman of the Supreme Kenges of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
