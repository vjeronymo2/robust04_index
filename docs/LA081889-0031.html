August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Former KCBS anchorwoman Terry Murphy will join the staff of a new syndicated news program, "Hard Copy," as a co-anchor. Also anchoring the program will be Alan Frio. "Hard Copy" is a half-hour news program produced by Paramount Television that debuts Sept. 18 on KNBC at 7:30 p.m. The show will also be seen in 129 other markets nationally. Most recently , Murphy was an anchor for the 6 p.m. and 11 p.m. news broadcasts on KCBS. Frio was a news anchor at a Philadelphia station. CLAUDIA PUIG
