920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / Finance & the Family: Share centre launches Pep The share centre has launched a self-select personal equity plan, with a 'maintenance fee' of Pounds 12.50 per quarter. Dealing commissions are 1 per cent on the first Pounds 2,000, subject to a Pounds 10 minimum; 0.8 per cent on the next Pounds 3,000; 0.5 per cent on the Pounds 5,000 after that. Those who take up a Pep before the end of September will get their first deal free. There is also a self-select Pep with a 'maintenance fee' of 0.375 per cent per half year, with a minimum of Pounds 12.50 plus VAT.
