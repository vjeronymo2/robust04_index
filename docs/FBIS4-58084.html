BFN [Report by Roni Shaqed and Orli Azulay-Katz] [Excerpt] Yasir 'Arafat is considering the possibility of making a two-day visit to Jericho on 5 May, the day after the signing of the agreement between the PLO and Israel. This was discussed yesterday in talks between PLO and Israeli representatives. 'Arafat said he wants to visit to drum up support for the PLO and weaken Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] and those opposed to the agreement. [passage omitted]
