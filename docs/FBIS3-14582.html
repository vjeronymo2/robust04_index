Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, Indian troops have martyred 12 more people including six in custody during the past 24 hours. Thousands of people attended the funeral of three youths martyred in custody in Anantnag-Islamabad. These youths were arrested in a crack down in (Datharin) 25 kilometers from Anantnag-Islamabad. Bullet-riddled bodies of two youths were recovered in Kupwara. Mojahedin, in retaliation, carried out rocket attack on an army camp at (Gamir) in Rajaori area inflicting heavy casualties on Indian forces and damaging the camp.
