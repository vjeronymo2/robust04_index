Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 28 (XINHUA) -- China has seen good progress in the implementation of the joint-stock system. So far, over 13,000 enterprises have adopted the new system, with more than 208.6 billion yuan in stock capital and some 25 million shareholders. Statistics show that these firms are located in 30 provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities in the country. The three provinces of Shandong, Zhejiang and Sichuan have over 800 such companies each, and Henan, Hubei and Jiangsu Provinces have more than 600 each. The Tibet Autonomous Region has set up four such enterprises, with a total stock capital of 200 million yuan. So far, 122 shareholding companies have floated stocks on the market, mostly in the cities of Beijing, Shanghai and Tianjin, and the provinces of Liaoning, Jiangsu and Guangdong.
