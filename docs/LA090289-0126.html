September 2, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

COUPLE SING ELIAS' PRAISES

My wife and I attended Diane Elias' recital at St. Andrew's Presbyterian Church, and we were disappointed with Bruce Burroughs' review ("Mezzo Diane Elias in Hometown Newport Recital," Calendar, Aug. 8).

We thoroughly enjoyed the choice of songs and the singing as well as the opportunity to hear Miss Elias sing.

We do not agree with Mr. Burroughs' review.

Mr. and Mrs. ROBERT D. HUNTER, Huntington Beach
