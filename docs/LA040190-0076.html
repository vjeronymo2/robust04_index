April 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FINANCIAL AID FOR CAL STATE STUDENTS

Minority and disadvantaged students attending any of the 19 California state universities have until April 30 to apply for scholarships and internships totaling $30,000, administered by the California State University-Real Estate & Land Use Institute.

Applicants must be enrolled in a real estate program or major in a program oriented toward a real estate career, must be at least a half-time undergraduate or a classified graduate student and must have a minimum grade point average of 2.25 for undergraduates and 3.0 for graduate students.

Awards are expected to range from $322 to $1,518 and will be distributed by August. Applications and additional information: Scholarship Selection Committee, CSU-Real Estate & Land Use Institute, 650 University Ave., Suite 201, Sacramento, Calif. 95825. Telephone: (916) 929-6633.
