Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Doha-QNA--Press reports said yesterday that the Jordanian Government has made an initial decision to abrogate the secondary and tertiary boycott of Israel, and that it is waiting for the Arab League decision on ending the primary boycott. Quoting official Jordanian sources, newspaper AL-SHARQ said the new official Jordanian move has come following the recent visit to Amman by U.S. Commerce Secretary Ronald Brown, during which he demanded ending the secondary and tertiary boycotts of Israel that restrict U.S. companies' activities in the Middle East.
