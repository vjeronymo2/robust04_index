April 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

ABORTION: 'LONG LINE'

The editorial on abortion was excellent. I sincerely appreciate it. I have been waiting and watching for a historical story.

There is so much time, energy and money wasted on fetuses and almost no concern or compassion for newborns with AIDS or drug dependencies, or babies lacking proper care.

There is much too much preaching instead of support for more facts, more knowledge and more education.

MARGARET GARCIA

Homeland
