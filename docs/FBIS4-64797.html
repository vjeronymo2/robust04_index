BFN [Excerpt] Baltasar Garzon has resigned. The secretary of state for the National Plan Against Drugs says that his decision is irrevocable. It is not yet known whether he will resign from his seat as a deputy in Congress. Garzon ran as an independent, as number two on the Spanish Socialist Workers Party list for Madrid in the last electons. There have been rumors about Garzon's resignation ever since the prime minister announced yesterday that the Ministries of the Interior and Justice will be combined. In fact, Garzon was number two in the Interior Ministry. [passage omitted]
