BFN [Text] Seoul, April 11 (YONHAP) -- South Korea and Vietnam held a second round of working-level meetings to discuss signing a pact on double taxation avoidance at the Finance Ministry here Monday. At the meetings to end on Friday, Seoul will seek ways to support Korean companies' advancement into Vietnam and to prevent them from being discriminated against. Tax rates at the source of income, conditions for fixed work sites and guarantees on equal tax treatment for domestic and foreign nationals are also on the agenda.
