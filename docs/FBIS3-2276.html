Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporters Chen Ming (7115 6900) and Rong Song (2837 2646)] [Text] Tehran, 5 Mar (XINHUA) -- Iranian President Hashemi-Rafsanjani said here today that both Iran and China are countries with a long history and a long-term task of developing their economies and improving the people's living standards, and that the two countries should strengthen cooperation to raise their bilateral relations to a new height. Hashemi-Rafsanjani made the above remarks during a meeting with the visiting Qian Qichen, Chinese vice premier and foreign minister, in the presidential office. The two exchanged views on bilateral relations and the regional situation during their cordial and friendly meeting. Hashemi-Rafsanjani said: Iran attaches great importance to developing its relations with China. The bilateral relations between the two countries have grown smoothly in recent years and have maintained a momentum for development, but Iran is not yet content with them. Qian Qichen said: The relations between China and Iran are growing smoothly, their economic relations and trade are expanding constantly, and good cooperation and coordination exist between them in international affairs. He said: Iran is a big country in the Gulf region. China attaches importance to developing friendly and cooperative relations with it and is willing to take a further step to strengthen the bilateral cooperative relations. This not only conforms to the interests of the people of both China and Iran but is also conducive to peace and stability in the Middle East. Qian Qichen added: Under the new international situation, developing countries, for the sake of their own interests, hope that a situation of peace, stability, and development will exist in all regions of the world. For this reason, they should strengthen unity and cooperation among themselves. During the meeting, Qian Qichen conveyed greetings from President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng to Hashemi-Rafsanjani, while Hashemi-Rafsanjani asked Qian Qichen to convey his regards and good wishes to Jiang Zemin and Li Peng.
