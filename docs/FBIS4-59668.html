BFN [Text] Shaykh 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, speaker of the Yemeni House of Representatives, has asserted that the forces loyal to President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih have decided the military situation in their favor following the seizure of the southern major base of al-'Anad. In a statement to the Qatari News Agency over the phone, al-Ahmar said that the crisis has ended, affirming that the southern units, which have joined the northerners, are now fighting against the rebellious forces in the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] led by 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Al-Ahmar rejected the YSP's call to form a national salvation government and said: This means nobody is running the country's affairs.
