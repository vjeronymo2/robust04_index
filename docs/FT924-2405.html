921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Conference and Comice pears are excellent this week at 35-55p a lb (35-55p) reports the FFVIB. Citrus fruits such as satsumas at 35-40p a lb (35-40p), clementines at 40-45p a lb (40-45p) and oranges at 15-25p each (15-25p) are all superb fruit buys. Most vegetables have remained at a stable price this week including Brussel sprouts at 15-25p a lb (15-25p), homegrown cauliflower at 35-50p a head (35-50p) and parsnips at 20-25p a lb (20-25p). Iceberg lettuce at 60-75p each (55-75p), Round lettuce at 30-35p each (25-35p) and tomatoes at 75-85p a lb (75-85p) are good salad buys.
