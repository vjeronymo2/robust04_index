920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: CSC The net asset value of CSC Investment Trust was 108.42p at December 31, a rise a 4.3 per cent over the year. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 84,706 (Pounds 87,272), equal to earnings of 5.15p (5.31p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 3p brings the total for the year to 5p (7.2p).
