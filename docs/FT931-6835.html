930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / Aid for Ankara Britain is to make a Pounds 22.7m grant to help fund a Dollars 660m metro project in the Turkish capital, Ankara, being built by a Canadian-UK consortium, writes John Murray Brown in Ankara. The grant, disbursed under the UK's Aid and Trade Provision, will be used to cover 36 per cent of the value of work of British contractors, GEC and Westinghouse UK.
