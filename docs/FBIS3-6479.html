Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 18 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the party members and farmers of the Komsan cooperative farm in Sukchon County who set an example in carrying out the technical revolution in the countryside. This cooperative farm set a target a few years ago for all farmers to learn how to operate tractors and other farm machines and sent 520 farmers to the Sukchon agricultural mechanization school to study while working on the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the publication of the rural theses. Managerial and junior officials of the farm and three revolution team members dispatched there themselves learned how to drive tractors, leading farmers. Thus, 520 farmers have graduated from the school and been able to operate all farm machines.
