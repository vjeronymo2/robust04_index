Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 29 KYODO -- The coincident index of economic indicators for January stood at 85.0 percent, rising above the "boom-or-bust" 50 percent line for the first time in four months, the Economic Planning Agency said Tuesday [29 March]. The leading index, which projects economic conditions several months ahead, stood at 60.0 percent, showing improvement after staying below the 50 percent line for eight consecutive months, the agency said.
