Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 1 (KCNA) -- Brief biography of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was carried by the Russian papers PATRIOT, ISTOKI and GLASNOSTI, the Bangladesh papers BANGLA BANI and SANGBAD, the Nigerian paper THE REPUBLIC, the Indian papers WORUM and TAMAKA DARBAR, the Ethiopian paper MONITOR, the Nepalese paper NEPAL POST and Palestinean magazine PALESTINE AL THURA.
