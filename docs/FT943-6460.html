940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / International Company News: Formosa Plastics drops 3.9% Taiwan's Formosa Plastics Corp, a leading manufacturer of petrochemical products, saw pre-tax profits for the year to June 30 fall 3.9 per cent to TDollars 2.45bn (Dollars 91m) as a result of a TDollars 348m loss on its US operations, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei. First-half revenues were up 19 per cent from a year earlier, to TDollars 15.41bn, on steady sales growth, the group said. It added that earnings in the first half of last year were lifted by TDollars 440m from land sales and other income. The company took a loss of TDollars 348m in its US operations.
