930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / International Company News: Skoda Plzen in German tie-up SKODA PLZEN, the Czech republic's largest engineering group, said yesterday it had signed a joint venture with Dorries und Scharmann, a German machine tool company, Reuter reports. The deal was concluded yesterday with the Skoda Plzen subsidiary Obrabeci Stroje (Machine Tools), which said that Dorries would invest DM10m (Dollars 6.3m) of capital in the venture and a further DM15m in three years.
