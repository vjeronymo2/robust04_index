BFN [Excerpt] The chetniks today showed us yet again in the most brutal way that, as far as they were concerned, no UN decisions on protected areas in Bosnia-Herzegovina had any validity. Between 1500 and 1600 GMT this afternoon they fired several artillery shells on Tuzla. This attack against civilian targets caused fresh casualties: One 22-year-old girl was killed, one person seriously, and three lightly wounded. [passage omitted]
