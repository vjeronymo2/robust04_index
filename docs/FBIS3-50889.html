Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Mario Lopez; from the "Upon Awakening" newscast] [Text] A car loaded with explosives blew up early Saturday morning, 8 January, in the parking lot of Plaza Universidad Mall, located between Universidad and Parroquia Streets, south of Mexico City. A report by the Mexico City Police indicated that a Nissan car, in which the explosives were allegedly placed, was destroyed. No victims were reported, although property damage was extensive. The parking lot and the mall were empty. Rene Monterrubio Lopez, general secretary of protection and highways, came to the site. [Begin Monterrubio recording] Just one vehicle was damaged. All of the other vehicles inside the building are intact. [end recording] The car bomb exploded entirely and damaged the central roof, part of the parking lot structure, and window panes. The police began an inquiry, although no group has claimed responsibility for the attack.
