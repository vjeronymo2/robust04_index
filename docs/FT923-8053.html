920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / Observer: Bubble And what do you think about in the bath? Design guru Sir Terence Conran, former husband of Superwoman Shirley and ex-chief executive of Storehouse, told London's Evening Standard that he likes to lie in his bath and 'think about how the towel-dispenser is going to fit into the wash basin at Quaglino's'.
