Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 11 TASS -- A spokesman of the Russian Counterintelligence Service confirmed on Friday that a Russian citizen had been arrested on charges of spying for Germany. "As investigation is still under way we refrain from any comments", the spokesman told TASS on Friday and asked not to press for detailed information. "In general I am against such inappropriate persistence of the press. We are doing everything which is in the interests of maintaining security of this country. We report about our successes when this does not impede our work", he said.
