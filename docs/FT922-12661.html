920416 FT 16 APR 92 / UK Company News: Peek expands with DM1m German buy Peek, the traffic control and field data systems group, has acquired Unicomp, which is involved in the development and sale of products and systems for traffic control in eastern Germany. The consideration is DM1m (Pounds 352,000), consisting of DM300,000 cash on completion and an additional DM700,000 two years later to be satisfied by the issue of 439,830 Peek ordinary.
