BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- Spring tree-planting is in full swing throughout the country. According to data available at the Forestry Management Department of the General Bureau of the Land Administration, nearly 200 million trees were planted across the country in recent 20 days. Many counties of South Hwanghae Province and Kaesong and Nampo municipalities have already fulfilled their plans for spring tree-planting. Korea has set the target to create 200,000 hectares of respective forests of oil-bearing trees and industrial trees in a few years and channelled big efforts to the work. April, a good season for tree planting, was set as a tree-planting month and trees are planted through a social movement. The nation's forests have been assigned to organs, enterprises and organizations and protected and managed by them with much care. Afforestation in cities is being pushed ahead. In recent years alone, thousands of hectares of forests have been created and a number of parks and recreation grounds built in major industrial and residential quarters. In Pyongyang, more than 250,000 trees have already been planted in streets, residential quarters, parks and recreation grounds this year.
