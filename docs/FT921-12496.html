920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Liquidators of Mentor settle out of court A protracted legal case brought by the joint liquidators of Mentor Insurance, a Bermuda-based insurance company, against Murphy Exploration & Production, its New Orleans-based parent, and KPMG Peat Marwick, its auditors, has been settled out of court. Under the confidential settlement, Mentor will receive Dollars 40m (Pounds 22m) in cash, including some money from an earlier settlement, and withdrawal of Dollars 90m in claims against the company from several of its bankers.
