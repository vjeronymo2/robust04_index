920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Cheques found in rubbish skip BANK OF Scotland said last night that it could not explain how 20 envelopes containing cheques for payment for the second instalment of ScottishPower shares had come to be dumped in a rubbish skip in Edinburgh last week. According to the Edinburgh Evening News, a refuse worker opened some of the envelopes and saw cheques, including one for Pounds 26,000. Bank of Scotland is handling the payment of the second instalment of 70p on shares in ScottishPower and Scottish Hydro-Electric, which was due by April 29. Bank of Scotland said shareholders who did not receive certificates by May 15 should contact the bank.
