930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Brussels move on insurance The European Commission is likely to produce new proposals for further harmonising the operation and supervision of the insurance industry, Mr Vanni d'Archirafi, EC Commissioner responsible for the Single Market, hinted yesterday, writes Lionel Barber from Brussels. He served notice that proposals for a new directive might cover areas such as prudential supervision, financial conglomerates and an improved system of car insurance.
