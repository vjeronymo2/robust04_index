July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BUSH CONFUSION

President Bush says he wants to lead the space industry "back to the future."

He is already leading the rest of the country "forward to the past."

I am an old man, and I am a little confused. How can I look ahead to where I was and back to where I will be?

It's tough enough just being old, without problems like that.

ARCHIBALD MacPHAIL

La Mesa
