930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort raises Pounds 28m for Emerging Markets Trust KLEINWORT Benson Investment Trusts has raised Pounds 28.2m for its recently launched Emerging Markets Trust, exceeding the minimum target of Pounds 15m. The placing two weeks ago brought in Pounds 13.6m, and a further Pounds 14.6m has come through the public offer. Mr Simon White, managing director of KB Investment Trusts, said the level of demand from private investors demonstrated that emerging markets have become increasingly popular. The trust has a simple structure of ordinary shares priced at 100p with one warrant attached for every five shares. There is no wind-up date. Dealings are expected to start on Thursday.
