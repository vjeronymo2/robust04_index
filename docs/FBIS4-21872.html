BFN [Excerpt] Sayyid Hasan Nasrallah, the secretary-general of the Lebanese revolutionary movement Hizballah, at a meeting with Deputy Majles speaker Hasan Ruhani this morning [in Damascus], thanked the esteemed leader [Khamene'i], the president, the Majles speaker, and the people of Iran for their attention to the justice-seeking struggles of the Muslim people of Lebanon.
