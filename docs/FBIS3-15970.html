Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Yevgeniy Bovkun: "Pavel Grachev Meets With German Colleague"] [Text] Bonn -- The Russian and German defense ministers agreed in Bonn to develop cooperation between their departments. The question of the possible redeployment of Russian forces in Kaliningrad Oblast was raised during the talks. Grachev announced that the contingent would be reduced and reorganized, and it was not excluded that some units would be moved to the vicinity of St. Petersburg and to the North Caucasus.
