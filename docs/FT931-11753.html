930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Wentworth deals resumed Trading in the shares of Wentworth International was restored yesterday, following the publication of its report and accounts for the year to March 31 1992. The shares of this USM-quoted plastic packaging group were suspended on October 20 at 3p, pending the delayed results, which showed losses of Pounds 4.05m, against restated profits of Pounds 63,000. In December the group announced pre-tax losses totalling Pounds 1.82m for the six months to September 30.
