November 5, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

MAN KILLED DESTROYING OLD EXPLOSIVE AGENTS

A man burning old explosive agents was killed and a fellow worker seriously injured Friday in a blast felt by residents as far as two miles away, police said.

The explosion left a hole about 15 feet across at the site of the fire, a few hundred yards from a Nitro-Chem plant, which manufactures explosives for mining companies, authorities said. The cause of the blast was under investigation.
