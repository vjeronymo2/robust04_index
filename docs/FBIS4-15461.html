BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] It appears that the visit by the Latvian President to Moscow will take place after all. Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman Grigoriy Karasin has said that the preparations for this visit to Moscow have now entered the plane of practical actions. The plane of practical actions evidently means that the somewhat confused situation connected with the mistake in President Yeltsin's instructions about setting up military bases outside Russia, including in Latvia, is being resolved.
