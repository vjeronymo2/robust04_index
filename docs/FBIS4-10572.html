BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] The controversy between the weekly magazine SEMANA and Julio Mario Santo Domingo over alleged wiretapping has not ended. SEMANA issued a communique today saying that it will wait for a ruling from the Colombian judicial authorities as this is not merely a case of gossip but a serious crime. Santo Domingo had previously issued a communique denying a report published by the magazine linking him to an alleged case of wiretapping.
