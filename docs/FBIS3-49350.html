Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On 9 January, President Zhelyu Zhelev answered questions put forth by listeners of Khorizont radio. I hope that by the end of my mandate, in approximately three years, the basic reforms will have been implemented in Bulgaria. Afterward, other people can continue, the president said, stressing that he will not seek a second term as a president. [passage omitted] If any political force tries to hinder settling the foreign debt issue, it would be committing an act of national treason, the president said. He noted that in his upcoming address to the National Assembly, he will stress the necessity of rapidly adopting the budget and the bills that are extremely necessary for foreign debt negotiations and the IMF agreement. According to the president, it is very important to achieve a consensus in the parliament on measures needed by the country. He would like to see the meeting between Filip Dimitrov and Zhan Videnov contribute to this end. [passage omitted]
