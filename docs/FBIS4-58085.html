BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Oct (MENA)--Informed Palestinian sources in Cairo have said that agreement has been reached with Israel for the release of 8,400 Palestinian detainees held in Israeli prisons. In a statement to MENA, the sources said that the Palestinian-Israeli agreement will cover all the detainees, irrespective of their political affiliations. The sources added that a detailed program is currently being prepared for the release of these detainees, and that 5,000 of them will be released within one month of the signing of the agreement for self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. They said that 2,500 of the 5,000 will be released two days after the signing, 2,000 at the start of the Israeli withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho, and 500 after the withdrawal is completed. The sources explained that a program will be prepared for the release of the remaining 3,400 detainees, which will begin with the start of negotiations for the implementation of self-rule in the West Bank.
