930102 FT 02 JAN 93 / UK Company News: AAH pays Pounds 2.4m for retail outlets AAH Holdings has acquired four retail pharmacies in Norfolk from J & AL King for an initial consideration of Pounds 2.35m, satisfied by the issue of 465,000 shares. The shares will be retained by the vendors for not less than 12 months. A further sum not exceeding Pounds 450,000 will be payable in cash on ascertainment of the aggregate net asset values.
