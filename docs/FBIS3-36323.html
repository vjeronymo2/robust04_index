Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with the law of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated 10 December 1993, with regard to the temporary delegation of additional powers to the president of the Republic of Kazakhstan and heads of local administrations, the president of Kazakhstan, by his decree, has endorsed the republican budget for 1994. The budget has been presented by the Kazakhstan government. The revenues total a figure of 40,299,685,000 tenge, and its expenditures 48,111,806,000 tenge; the maximum size of the deficit stands at 7,812,121,000 tenge.
