Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Mar (MENA)--A responsible Egyptian source has stated that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin are expected to sign the final measures for the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles in the coming few weeks in Cairo. The same source noted that early empowerment may also apply to the Hebron area, in addition to Gaza and Jericho, adding that a Palestinian police force will be permitted in Hebron and a Palestinian mayor will be appointed there. The same source stressed that the recent outlawing of the Kakh and Kahana terrorist movements in Israel was a considerable gain by the Palestinian negotiators, noting that president 'Arafat played all his cards with great skill during the negotiations that followed the recent massacre at the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron.
