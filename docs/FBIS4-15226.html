BFN [Text] Today the Federation Council approved a decree by the Russian president on introducing a state of emergency in the conflict zone on the territory of North Ossetia and Ingushetia. The discussion was held behind closed doors and lasted four hours. However, the issue was not settled, and the problem will be discussed tomorrow. After the session, Ingush President Ruslan Aushev stated to an ITAR-TASS correspondent that he may leave it. He disagrees with the approaches used to settle the conflict. North Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov refrained from commenting on the content of the presidential decree.
