BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 19/4/1994 -- President Mircea Snegur signed today a decree on paying off the arrears and stabilizing the financial situation of Moldovan enterprises and organizations. According to the decree, the National Bank and commercial banks will compensate the mutual debts using the financial means of the enterprises and organizations and budgetary means as well. The Republican and local commissions must compile by 15 May the list of the enterprises with debts and deposit and hard currency accounts opened in the Moldovan commercial banks and abroad; they must count the sums thus deposited. They will have to count the sums of unrepatriated hard currency for the export of goods and services. According to the decree, the Ministry of Finance, the State Central Fiscal Inspectorate and the Financial Guard will levy the delayed payments to the budget from the enterprises' hard currency accounts. The state enterprises and organizations were allowed to sell at auctions as IOU's their debts. According to sources from the Ministry of Finance the mutual debts are over 400-500 million lei.
