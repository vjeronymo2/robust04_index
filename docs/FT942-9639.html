940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: City of Oxford The City of Oxford Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 38.2p per ordinary share as at March 31, up from 32.7p a year earlier. Net revenue, taking in a charge of Pounds 101,000 related to the extension of the trust's life, was Pounds 1.51m (Pounds 1.53m), leaving earnings at 5.04p (5.11p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 1.4p maintains the year's total at 5p.
