September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ON THE COVER:

The answer is: Alex Trebek and Gail Eichenthal.

The question: Who will co-host KCOP's 2 1/2-hour telecast of "Live From the Hollywood Bowl" Saturday at 8:30 p.m. on Channel 13?

Trebek, the emcee of "Jeopardy!," and classical music host Eichenthal will do the honors at the season-ending concert -- complete with fireworks finale -- for the second consecutive year. It's the third year in a row for Trebek and KCOP.

Guest conductor Heiichiro Ohyama will lead the Los Angeles Philharmonic in works by Dvorak, Tchaikovsky and Handel. A simulcast of the performance can be heard on KUSC-FM (91.5). STEVEN LINAN
