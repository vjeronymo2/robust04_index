Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] As reported in the Republic's media, a working meeting between Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich and Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin was held on 18 February in Moscow. Summarizing the results of the meeting, the two prime ministers emphasized that this was a final discussion on the mechanisms for both countries' functioning under a single monetary system. At the prime ministers' request, groups of experts will complete drafting documents in two or three days. On the Belarusian side, the government team is headed by CIS Executive Secretary Uladzimir Zalamay. The final signing of the documents is expected in the near future. At the meeting, the main principles of unification of the monetary systems were outlined -- absolute unification of the price policy and mutually beneficial exchange rates of currencies of both states.
