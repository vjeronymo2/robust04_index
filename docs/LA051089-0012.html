May 10, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Political correspondent Linda Wertheimer, a familiar voice to National Public Radio listeners, will become a host of NPR's "All Things Considered" program starting Monday. She'll be part of a rotating roster with Robert Siegel and Noah Adams that will allow each of the hosts time away from the microphone to pursue signature pieces and in-depth stories. Wertheimer has been covering politics for NPR since 19771. ALEENE MacMINN
