BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow May 6 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has met Chairman of the Tajik parliament Emomali Rakhmonov in the Kremlin. The meeting is being held behind closed doors. However, it was reported that the talks are focused on economic issues, and primarily, Tajikistan's admission into the rouble zone. Although Russian roubles are in circulation in Tajikistan, this is considered to be a temporary measure yet. During the talks the two leaders are expected to discuss the internal political situation in Tajikistan and the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border.
