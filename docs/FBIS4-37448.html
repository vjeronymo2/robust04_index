BFN [Text] Baghdad, 18 Jun (INA) -- Today, Planning Minister Samal Majid Faraj met with Milord Unkovic, minister of external economic relations and the head of the Yugoslav side on the Joint Economic Iraqi-Yugoslav Committee, and his delegation. The two sides discussed the scope of economic and developmental cooperation and ways to invigorate the joint committee in the best interests of the two countries. The meeting was attended by Yugoslavia's charge d'affaires in Baghdad and a number of Yugoslav company managers. Dr. Unkovic also met with Dr. Ahmad Murtada Ahmad, minister of transport and communications. They discussed cooperation in transportation and communications, with a special emphasis on the areas of railroads and the telephone. The Yugoslav delegates condemned the sanctions. They said they are prepared to join in development ventures in Iraq.
