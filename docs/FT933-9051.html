930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / Mates urges cut in Ulster funding MR Michael Mates, the former Northern Ireland minister, yesterday suggested that cutting the financial subsidy to Northern Ireland could act as 'an incentive for change'. He told the Belfast Telegraph that a cut in funds could force the province's politicians to go to the negotiating table and thrash out a settlement. Policewomen in Northern Ireland are to be armed for the first time, the RUC said.
