BFN [Text] The NWFP [Northwest Frontier Province] Assembly held its session in Peshawar this morning with Mr. Abdul Akbar Khan in the chair as presiding officer. The house elected Mr. Aftab Ahmed Khan Sherpao as leader of the house. He secured 42 votes and none voting against. Speaking after his election as leaders of the house, Mr. Aftab Ahmed Khan Sherpao thanked the assembly members for reposing confidence in him. He invited the opposition members for cooperation with the ruling alliance for smooth running of the business of the assembly. Earlier, the presiding officer, Mr. Abdul Akbar Khan, read out the governor's order asking the former leader of the house to prove his majority. Mr. Akbar Hussain Shah in a resolution proposed that Mr. Sabir Shah should prove his majority, while Shahad Mohammad Khan seconded. (?As the) former leader of the house and members of the treasury benches had abstained from the proceedings, Pir Sabir Shah was declared unsuccessful in proving his majority. Later, the name of Mr. Aftab Ahmed Khan Sherpao was proposed in four identical resolutions for leadership of the house. He succeeded in obtaining 42 votes and was declared elected unopposed leader of the house. The session was later prorogued.
