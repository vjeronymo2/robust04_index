930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / Angola peace talk hopes ANGOLAN government officials and their rebel Unita opponents arrived in Addis Ababa yesterday for talks to end war raging across Angola, Reuter reports from the Ethiopian capital. Ethiopian officials said a four-man team of Unita generals had arrived and that a first round of talks with a government team was due to start this morning. Although a temporary cease-fire was negotiated, fierce fighting continued yesterday around the city of Huambo, UN officials told AP in Luanda. Thousands have been reported killed in three weeks of fighting there.
