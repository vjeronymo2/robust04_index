Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The new parliament of the Republic of Moldova began its activity this morning. President Mircea Snegur, members of the government, representatives of diplomatic corps, and numerous journalists were present. The report was read out on the final results of the elections and the Supreme Court decision regarding the approval of the elections. Then, the list was presented of the names of the 104 deputies elected to the new parliament, among whom was Ilie Ilascu, a deputy for the Christian Democratic Popular Front Alliance, who could not be present because he is still imprisoned. According to reports by our Chisinau correspondent, Cristian Nita, Petru Lucinschi was elected as parliamentary president and Dumitru Motpan, leader of the Agrarian Democratic Party, and Nicolae Andronic, representing the same party, were elected as vice presidents.
