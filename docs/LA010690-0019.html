January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE DEBATE CONTINUES: IS MIAMI, NOTRE DAME OR SAN DIEGO ST. NO. 1?

Thank you Mike Downey for setting the polls straight. Yes, the Irish beat four major conference champions and the East's two best teams and, yes, they got a raw deal.

Miami has too fine a football program to continue their scheduling scam of cream puffs before every quality opponent.

DON GALIAS, Pacific Palisades
