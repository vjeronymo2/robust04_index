930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / World News In Brief: Asil Nadir reported to have left UK The Serious Fraud Office was early today investigating reports that Asil Nadir, founder of Polly Peck International, the bankrupt fruits-to-electronics conglomerate, had left Britain for North Cyprus. An Istanbul news agency said Turkish Cypriot-born Nadir, who faces trial on charges of theft and false accounting in September, left on board a private aircraft.
