July 23, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

BABY BORN DURING 'DIE HARD'

The nonstop action in "Die Hard 2" may have been too much for Kami Core, who went into labor midway through the movie and gave birth 20 minutes later in the theater restroom.

Core, 18, wasn't particularly concerned going to the theater Saturday since she wasn't due for almost two months.

But about halfway through the action thriller, Core began feeling labor pains. She went to the restroom, where paramedics were summoned. About 20 minutes later, she gave birth to Cortney Brion Samuels on the restroom floor.

Seven weeks premature, the infant and her mother were taken to Kennestone Hospital. Core was to be released Monday but Cortney won't go home for another two weeks.

"Stuff like that happens on television, but it never happens in real life," Core said.
