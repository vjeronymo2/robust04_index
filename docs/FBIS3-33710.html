Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Dawud al-Mani dispatch from Damascus] [Excerpt] Hizballah's Deputy Secretary General 'Ammar al-Musawi denied having information that Israeli navigator Ron Arad, missing since 1986, is being held by Hizballah. He added: We have always and repeatedly said that Hizballah has nothing to do with Ron Arad. He confirmed that Hizballah is holding only two Israeli soldiers. [passage omitted]
