December 12, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFLY

Quiksilver Extends Expiration Date: Newport Beach-based Quiksilver Inc. has agreed to extend the expiration date of its offer to buy the French company that distributes its surfwear in Europe, Na Pali S.A. Quiksilver said it agreed to the extension because a pre-closing audit is not yet complete.

Quiksilver Chairman John C. Warner said in a statement Monday that the audit is expected to be done next month. The two companies "will proceed as rapidly as possible" to complete the sale, Quiksilver said. Na Pali, which reported sales of $21 million for its fiscal year ended May 31, agreed to the acquisition in a deal announced in October.
