BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- The second-term inaugural ceremony of the South Korean Kangwon Provincial Federation of General Student Councils was held on May 4 with the attendance of more than 700 students from fourteen universities, a South Korean newspaper reported. At the inaugural ceremony Chong Chae-ho, chairman of the General Student Council of Sangji University, called for checking the liberalization of farm produce import, saying the 60,000 fellow students in the province should fight in unity, not compromising with injustice and assuming responsibility for the destiny of the country and of their own.
