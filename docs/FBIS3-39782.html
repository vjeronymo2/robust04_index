Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenia is asking for Turkey's help in making peace with Azerbaijan. Zhirair Liparityan, deputy foreign minister of Armenia, arrived in Ankara last night and met Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin this morning. The meeting was held at Armenia's request. Armenia, which has become a supplicant for peace because of the latest Azerbaijani military successes and worsening economic conditions, expects Turkey to contribute to peace in Caucasus.
