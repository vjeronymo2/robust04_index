Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Taba, 19 Jan (MENA)--The Security and Civilian Committees stemming from the Gaza-Jericho Committee resumed their meetings in Taba this morning. The Security Committee is led on the Palestinian side by Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, and on the Israeli side by General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff. The Civilian Committee is chaired by Attorney Jamil al-Turayfi for the Palestinian side, while the Israeli side is chaired by Gadi Zohar, in charge of the civilian administration in the West Bank.
