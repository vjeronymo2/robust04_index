DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR Office of the Secretary [WO&hyph;150&hyph;1820&hyph;00 1A] California Desert District Advisory Council; Renewal AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management, Interior. ACTION: California Desert District Advisory Council_Notice of Renewal.

SUMMARY: This notice is published in accordance with Section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463). Notice is hereby given that the Secretary of the Interior has renewed the Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) California Desert District Advisory Council. The purpose of the Council is to provide counsel and advice to the BLM District Manager concerning planning and management of the public land resources within the BLM California Desert District and implementation of the comprehensive, long-range plan for the management, use, development, and protection of the public lands within the California Desert Conservation Area.
