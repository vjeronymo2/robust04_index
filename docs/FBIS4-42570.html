BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday Political Spring Chairman Andonios Samaras once again asked the Greek Government to bring the issue of Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] to the European Union [EU] Summit in Corfu. At the same time, he called on the government to capitalize on the Greek presidency and to deal with the problems faced by farmers and the amendment of the Common Agriculture Policy. Samaras was also asked about reports of U.S. Envoy Matthew Nimetz's proposal for a double name for Skopje. He said: "There should be an immediate and categorical rejection by the government."
