931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Celsis Intl Pounds 561,000 in the red CELSIS International, the specialist in rapid microbial testing which came to the market in a Pounds 12.4m flotation in July, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 561,000 for the six months to end-September, compared with a deficit of Pounds 460,000. Turnover was up from Pounds 67,000 to Pounds 79,000. Losses per share were 1.05p (1.03p). Net interest income amounted to Pounds 185,000 against a Pounds 16,000 charge last time. Cash on deposit at the end of October was Pounds 12.2m.
