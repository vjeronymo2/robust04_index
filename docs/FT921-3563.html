920313 FT 13 MAR 92 / Tapie fined for Le Pen insult Mr Bernard Tapie, the French businessman, was fined yesterday for calling the far-right leader, Mr Jean-Marie Le Pen, and his supporters 'bastards' at an election rally, Reuter reports from Marseille. Mr Tapie, running against Mr Le Pen with the backing of the ruling Socialists in regional elections in southern France, was fined FFr1,500 (Pounds 153) by a court on a public insult charge.
