940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / Coal job-creation arm spent Pounds 10.1m British Coal Enterprise, British Coal's job-creation arm, committed more than Pounds 10.1m to investment in enterprises in 1993-94, says the organisation's annual report. BCE said yesterday that this was nearly double the previous year's figure and resulted in projects that created 4,602 jobs in areas hit by colliery closures. BCE assists people leaving mines to get new jobs.
