BFN [Report by TIA correspondent Konstantin Parshin for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe, 11 Jun -- Indian Minister of State for External Affairs Salman Khurshid announced today that India will grant Tajikustan a 10-year loan worth $5 million in accordance with a program of mutually advantageous coperation and with a whole range of documents that were signed yesterday evening. Of this money, 85 percent will go toward implementing joint Tajik- Indian projects in the areas of enterprise and industrial installation construction, while the remainder will be used for acquiring medical equipment. Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Olimov said at a press conference that was devoted to the visit by the Indian minister and a delegation of Indian entrepreneurs accompanying him, that relations between the two countries were entering a new stage. The fact that a plenipotentiary ambassador of India to Tajikistan would be appointed in Dushanbe in the near future was yet more proof of this.
