Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Relayed by TV Polonia network] [Text] The political council of the Movement for the Republic has decided to join the coalition, formed on 10 November, of Center Accord, the Christian-National Union, and the Polish Peasant Party-Peasant Accord. The coalition of these four parties has approached the minister of foreign affairs demanding that Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, the leader of the Russian nationalists who is planning to visit us at the end of January, not be allowed into Poland.
