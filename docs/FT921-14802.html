920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Kenyan scientist arrested A prominent environmentalist yesterday became the third person arrested in connection with rumors President Daniel arap Moi planned to stage a coup to forestall Kenya's first multiparty elections in nearly 26 years, AP reports from Nairobi. Ms Wangari Maathai, a former professor of microbiology at the University of Nairobi, was taken by two policemen from her house in Nairobi where she had barricaded herself since Sunday, a journalist at the scene said. Mrs Maathai heads the Green Belt Movement, a grassroots organisation devoted to planting trees in deforested areas. She was one of 10 members of the opposition Forum for the Restoration of Democracy party who told reporters Friday that the president planned to hand over the government to the military.
