930408 FT 08 APR 93 / UK Company News: Lucas Inds Lucas Industries received a total consideration of Pounds 40m at current exchange rates for the recent sale of its Fluid Power Systems business to Sophus Berendsen. LFPS has assets of Pounds 29m and in its last full year made an operating profit of Pounds 900,000. Completion of the transfer of ownership of the Canadian operations, which represents Pounds 7m of the total, is subject to final approval by Canadian authorities. Lucas will use the proceeds to reduce borrowings.
