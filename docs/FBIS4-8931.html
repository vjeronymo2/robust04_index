BFN [Text] Belgrade, 19 Apr (TANJUG) -- The cabinet of Nikola Koljevic, vice president of the republic of Sprska, today informed the aid organization Medecins Sans Frontieres (MSF) that the republic of Srpska authorities have authorized this organization, too, to carry on with its humanitarian convoy operations. It was announced yesterday that, under an agreement between the Serbian authorities and Yasushi Akashi, the UN secretary general's special envoy, the UNHCR, the International Committee of the Red Cross, and Handicap International can resume their humanitarian convoy operations. In the TANJUG report, Medecin Sans Frontieres was left out by mistake.
