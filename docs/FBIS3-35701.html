Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 28 TASS -- President Yeltsin has signed a decree on the creation of the Federal Commission on Legal Information Under the Russian President, a spokesman for the president's press service told ITAR-TASS. The commission, which will be a permanent consultative body, will ensure the drawing up of a package of measures for the creation and functioning of the state system of legal information and the creation of Russia's single information-legal space. Ruslan Gennadievich Orekhov, head of the State-Legal Department Under the Russian President, was appointed chairman of the commission.
