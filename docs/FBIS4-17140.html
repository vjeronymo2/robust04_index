BFN [Text] At a plenary session of the Saeima today, deputies passed a vote of confidence in the government. The confidence motion, for which 51 deputies voted in favor, was proposed by the Latvian Way group. After the vote, Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs said: [Begin Birkavs recording] I am satisfied, not because I have kept my post, but because the government will have the opportunity to further implement the declaration intended by the government. [end recording]
