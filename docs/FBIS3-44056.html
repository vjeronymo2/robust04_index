Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Jan 21 SAPA--The African National Congress [ANC] released on Friday [21 January] in Johannesburg its national election list for the country's future national assembly. Top of the list was ANC President Nelson Mandela, followed by Secretary General Cyril Ramaphosa, National Chairman Thabo Mbeki and former SA Communist Party chief Joe Slovo. The highest woman on the list was Ms Albertina Sisulu at number 10, followed by Gertrude Shope at number 22 while estranged wife of the ANC leader, Winnie Mandela, was placed on the official list at number 30. Transkei's military ruler, Major-General Bantu Holomisa, was number 13 and former congress of SA Trade Unions Secretary General Jay Naidoo was number 6 (six) on the list. Rebel policeman and founder member of the Police and Prisons Civil Rights Union, Gregory Rockman, was at number 46 and Jan van Eck at number 48 was the first of former Democratic Party members to appear on the list. ANC information head Pallo Jordan, who was number 5 (five), said the list could still be subject to alterations, as the individuals concerned could decide to pursue other interests or party assignments.
