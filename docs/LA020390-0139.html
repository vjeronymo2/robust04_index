February 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DOCTOR DIES; HAD TRIED TO RESUSCITATE HIS WIFE

A doctor apparently trying to revive his wife who was suffering a fatal asthma attack collapsed and died of a heart attack, authorities said.

Paramedics found Betty Wrenn, 66, dead and her husband, Frank Wrenn, 67, slumped over her in cardiac arrest on the floor of their home Thursday, officials said.

Wrenn was pronounced dead at Greenville Memorial Hospital half an hour later.
