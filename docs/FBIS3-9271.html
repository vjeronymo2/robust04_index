Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Statement by the Press Center of Ukraine's Ministry of Foreign Affairs: "A Briefing by the Ambassador"] [Text] Ukraine's Ambassador in Poland H. Udovenko met with J. Milewski, the leader of the National Security Council [NSC], subordinated to the Polish president. Milewski is also deputy minister of defense and cochairman of the Ukrainian and Polish Presidents' Consultative Committee. The Polish leader showed interest in the ambassador's briefing on the results of President L. Kravchuk's visit to the United States. He also stressed that President L. Walesa had repeatedly stated that Ukraine's sovereignty, independence, and territorial integrity are of great significance for the entire European continent. The head of the National Security Council expressed confidence that the forthcoming official visit by Ukraine's Minister of Foreign Affairs A. Zlenko will give a further impetus to Ukrainian-Polish relations in all spheres. It is planned to discuss, during the visit, an extensive range of issues of bilateral cooperation within the framework of the NATO "Partnership for Peace" program.
