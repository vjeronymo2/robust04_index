931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: Two new fixed-rate mortgages - At a glance Woolwich building society is offering two new fixed-rate mortgages; a seven-year fix at 8.15 per cent and a four-year at 6.99 per cent. The APRs on repayment loans are 8.8 per cent and 7.5 per cent respectively; the mortgage is also available on an endowment, pension plan and Pep basis. The redemption penalty is six months interest on both loans; application fees are Pounds 250-Pounds 275.
