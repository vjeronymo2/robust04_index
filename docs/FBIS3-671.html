Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] It is reported from Ciskei that all units of the homeland's defense force have been recalled to barracks, pending action the military plans to take to secure pensions under a new dispensation. It is believed that the military ruler of Ciskei, Brigadier Oupa Gqozo, is holding a cabinet meeting behind closed doors. All attempts to contact the Council of State have been unsuccessful. Our East London news staff confirmed that military guards had been withdrawn from all government buildings and installations. The Ciskeian police have taken up positions around key points. Brig. Gqozo has been apparently in conference with his advisers throughout the day. The situation in the state is reported to be calm.
