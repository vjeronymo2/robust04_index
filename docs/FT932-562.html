930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / International Company News: Electric and General improves Net asset value of Electric and General Investment Company increased from 152.6p to 185.8p over the year to May 31. Net revenue for the 12 months amounted to Pounds 2.9m (Pounds 2.84m) for earnings per share of 3.21p (3.14p). The final dividend is an unchanged 1.6p for a maintained total of 3.1p. Mr Richard McClean is joining the board as a non-executive director. He was formerly deputy chief executive of the Financial Times and is about to become chief executive of the International Herald Tribune.
