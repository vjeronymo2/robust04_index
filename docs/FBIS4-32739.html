BFN [Text] Prishtina, June 27 (ATA) -- The interrogation of 14 Albanians accused of allegedly forming the Ministry of Defence of the Republic of Kosova ended after eight sittings in the Prishtina Court. In the last sittings, all the accused rejected as a whole the indictment brought against them. They explained in detail that they were severely treated by the State Security, saying that the accusation was based on slanders and statements made in the Investigation Office under pressure. The other sitting of this framed trial will be held on June 27 with the interrogation of the witnesses.
