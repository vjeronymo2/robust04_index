920406 FT 06 APR 92 / Election 1992: Lib Dems may seize Conwy THE Liberal Democrats may be poised to oust a government minister from his Welsh seat, according to a poll for the Liverpool Daily Post. Welsh minister Sir Wyn Roberts, who had a majority of 3,730 in 1987, could lose his Conwy seat to Mr Roger Roberts. The poll of 674 voters by Independent Market Research gave the Liberal Democrats 27 per cent, Conservatives 23 per cent, Labour 22 per cent and Plaid Cymru 5 per cent.
