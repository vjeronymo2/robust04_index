941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: Continental Airlines in venture deal Continental Airlines of the US has signed a deal aimed at forming a joint venture involving Amadeus, the European computer reservation system, and General Motors' Electronic Data Systems, Reuter reports from Houston. The deal would involve Continental's computer reservation system unit, System One, being operated as an Amadeus National Marketing Co, the companies said. Each company is expected to own a one-third interest in the company and the venture should be finalised during the first part of 1995. The companies said no financial or further operational details were available. Amadeus' shareholders include Air France, Spain's Iberia and Lufthansa. The aim is to expand business and project relationships.
