DATES: Thursday, May 5, 1994, 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. Joint Service Committee on Military Justice: Public Meeting AGENCY: Joint Service Committee on Military Justice (JSC). ACTION: Notice of public meeting.

SUMMARY: This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda of a public meeting of the JSC. This notice also describes the functions of the JSC. DATES: Thursday, May 5, 1994, 10 a.m. to 12 p.m.
ADDRESSES: Building 111, Washington Navy Yard, Washington, DC. FUNCTION: The JSC was established by the Judge Advocates General in 1972. The JSC currently operates under Department of Defense Directive 5500.17 of January 23, 1985. It is the function of the JSC to improve Military Justice through the preparation and evaluation of proposed amendments and changes to the Uniform Code of Military Justice and the Manual for Courts-Martial. AGENDA: The JSC will receive public comment concerning its 1994 Annual Review of Manual for Courts-Martial, United States, 1984, as published on April 14, 1994.
