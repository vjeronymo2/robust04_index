October 5, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: NAMES

Former House Speaker Tip O'Neill seems to have found a busy new career in commercials. The retired politician first did advertisements for American Express, Miller Lite and Comfort Inns and Wednesday he was seen in East Coast newspaper ads for the Trump Shuttle. O'Neill is pictured sitting on a plane next to Alexander Haig, the former secretary of state. O'Neill appropriately is on the left side of the page with Haig on the right.

All Smiles: The American Dental Hygienists' Assn. says the best smiles around belong to model Cheryl Tiegs, Mary Hart of "Entertainment Tonight" and Tony Danza of "Who's the Boss?" ALEENE MacMINN
