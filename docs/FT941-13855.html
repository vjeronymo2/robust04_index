940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: BBC's Brian Redhead dies Broadcaster Brian Redhead, anchorman of Radio 4's Today programme for 18 years, died in hospital in Macclesfield, Cheshire. He was 64 and had been due to retire from the programme in March. He joined it after leaving the editorship of The Manchester Evening News and had previously been The Guardian's northern editor.
