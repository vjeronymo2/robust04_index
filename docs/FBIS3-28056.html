Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by National Planning and Economic Development Minister Brigadier General Abel, which visited Yunnan at the invitation of the governor of Yunnan Province of the People's Republic of China to discuss economic and bilateral cooperation matters and to promote bilateral goodwill, returned to Yangon [Rangoon] via Bangkok by air this evening. The delegation was welcomed at Yangon airport by Lieutenant General Tin U, secretary-2 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council; Brig. Gen. Win Tin, minister of finance and revenue; U Than Aung, minister of cooperatives; Mr. Liang Feng, ambassador of PRC; directors general from the Ministry of National Planning and Economic Development; responsible personnel, and relatives.
