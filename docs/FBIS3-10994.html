Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on developments related to the Panamanian elections scheduled for 8 May 1994. Today, Ruben Blades, Papa Egoro Movement (MPE) presidential candidate, visited the neighborhoods of El Chorrillo, San Felipe, and Santa Ana. He was accompanied by supporters and MPE candidates for other elective posts. Commenting on recent acts of violence, he pledged his party will never incite violence. (Panama City Circuito RPC Television in Spanish 2300 GMT 9 Mar 94) Today, candidates to different elective posts signed the Electoral and Ethics Pact at the Santa Maria La Antigua University, whereby they pledged to conduct their political campaigns without violence. Attending the ceremony were top leaders of the different political parties and legislator, representative, and mayor candidates in the Panama Province. (Panama City Circuito RPC Television in Spanish 2300 GMT 9 Mar 94)
