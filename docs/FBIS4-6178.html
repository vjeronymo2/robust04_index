BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, April 19 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- The marine police Monday found two assault rifles and a grenade launcher in a Vietnamese fishing boat being detained at Cenderung fishing port in the east coast Trengganu state since March 7 for illegal fishing in Trengganu waters. Marine Police officer Zainul Abidin Hassan said his men also recovered 97 rounds of rifle ammunition and 14 rounds of ammunition for the grenade launcher hidden in the boat. He said the marine police found the arms and ammunition, believed to be China-made, following a thorough inspection of the boat, which was detained by the Fisheries Department Marine Division. All the 13 crew members of the boat were fined a total of [Malaysian ringgit] RM850000 (US$1=RM2.70) or 54 months' jail on March . They did not pay the fines. According to the Fisheries Department, no arms or amunitions were found on the boat at the time of arrest.
