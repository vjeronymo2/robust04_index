Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] Tehran, (IRNA) -- At the closing ceremonies for the Second Exhibition of Photographs of Geological and Mining Phenomena, the minister of mines and metals said: More than 15 billion rials has been allocated to raise the educational level of this ministry's personnel in the area of mines and metals. Engineer Hoseyn Mahluji said: In the area of training in the Second Five-Year Plan, this ministry will spend twice as much on the aggregate under its authority. He said: The Ministry of Mines and Metals is prepared to assist any college or university that is active teaching in the mines and metals sector, and it will place the resources of the organizations under its authority at the disposal of these institutions. This exhibition, which opened on 27 Azar [18 December], closed on Sunday with the presentation of prizes to the photographers who took the 10 best pictures.
