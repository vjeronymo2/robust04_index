920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / Observer: Object lesson A US economics professor is walking along lecturing a money trader on the high state of development of financial markets, when a Dollars 20 note comes blowing along the sidewalk. 'That just can't be,' he says, 'With our efficiency, windfall profits like that can't happen any more.' With a shrug, the trader picks up the note and pockets it.
