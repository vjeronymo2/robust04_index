Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, January 4 (SNARK)--There created the joint commission on the regulation of the conflict in the Southern Ossetia. Aleksandr Kavsadze, Shevardnadze's representative in the conflict zones, told SNARK the commission will give the chance to organize the meetings with the participation of the Georgian, Southern Ossetian, Russian and the CSCE representatives. The conflict should be solved in two directions -- political and economic directions. The first task is to deliver food to the Georgian population of Southern Ossetia.
