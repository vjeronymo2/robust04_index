940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Rothmans International - Other companies Rothmans International: The tobacco group now shorn of its luxury goods interests, is expected to report a lower annual profit on Thursday due to the Pounds 48m costs associated with the demerger of Dunhill. BZW, the broker, is forecasting a pre-tax figure of Pounds 440m, down from a pro forma Pounds 470m the previous year.
