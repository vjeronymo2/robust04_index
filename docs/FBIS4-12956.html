BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received Hamid Yusuf Hammadi, Iraqi minister of culture and information and envoy of Iraqi President Saddam Husayn. Hammadi conveyed to the brother Presidential Council chairman a message from his brother President Saddam Husayn dealing with the fraternal bilateral relations and issues of interest to our Arab nation. The message also dealt with the suffering of the sisterly Iraqi people as a result of the economic blockade imposed on Iraq, which requires a pan-Arab position to mitigate this hard human suffering of the Iraqi children, youths, and old people due to the continued blockade, especially since Iraq has stressed its commitment to all UN resolutions, led by Resolution 687. The brother chairman expressed our Yemeni people's solidarity with the fraternal Iraqi people's suffering as a result of the blockade. He asked the Iraqi culture and information ministers to convey his greetings to his brother President Saddam Husayn and his wishes of further progress and prosperity for the Iraqi people. The meeting was attended by Information Minister brother Hasan Ahmad al-Lawzi and Dr. Muhsin Khalil, Iraqi ambassador to our country.
