BFN [Text] The center for regional information problems of the National Academy of Sciences has held a poll in Kiev on people's attitudes to the Ukrainian presidential elections. The results of the poll reveal that 73 percent of the electorate plan to come to the polls on 26 June. The majority --56 percent -- intend to vote for Leonid Kravchuk, while 19 percent prefer Oleksandr Moroz, 11 percent -- Volodymyr Lanovyy, 7 percent -- Leonid Kuchma, and up to 7 percent plan to vote for the other three candidates, Ivan Plyushch, Petro Talanchuk, and Valeriy Babych.
