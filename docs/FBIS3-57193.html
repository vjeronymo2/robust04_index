Language: English Article Type:BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow January 21 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin held a meeting with Tatarstan's President Mintimer Shaymiyev in the Kremlin on Friday. Development and legal foundations of relations between the republics were the main theme of the conversation. The need to speed up the drafting and signing of an agreement on the separation of powers was noted. Negotiations on this matter will shortly be resumed. On the same day Yeltsin met with President of Mari Republic Vladislav Zotin and Chairman of the State Assembly of the Republic Anatoliy Smirnov. The participants on the meeting told the Russian president about the political situation in the republic after the adoption of a new constitution. Economic and social problems, specifically problems of regulating the population's employment arising during the conversion of the military-industrial complex were discussed, the presidential press office noted.
