Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 1 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the great September first revolution of the great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, on the occasion of the 17th anniversary of the proclamation of the Jamahiriya in Libya. President Kim Il-song in the message wished him and his people greater success in their efforts to defend the sovereignty and dignity of the country and build a prosperous new society. He expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would grow stronger and develop.
