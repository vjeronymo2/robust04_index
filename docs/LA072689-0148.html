July 26, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

EX-REDSKINS OFFICIAL TO JOIN NBC

Former Washington Redskins General Manager Bobby Beathard and Ralph Wiley, a senior writer for Sports Illustrated, are joining NBC's pro football pregame show, "NFL Live."

The network said today that Beathard and Wiley will combine on a weekly inside-the-NFL segment called "The Insiders."

Beathard was general manager of the Redskins for 12 years and helped build teams that won two NFL championships before resigning in May. Wiley joined Sports Illustrated in 1982 after six years with the Oakland Tribune.
