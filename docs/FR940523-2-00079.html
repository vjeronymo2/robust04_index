Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB Proposal: Housing Authority Law Enforcement and Security Personnel Costs Office: Office of Resident Initiatives Description of The Need For The Information and Its Proposed Use: This information collection is required in connection with Public Housing Drug Elimination Program (PHDEP), and other public housing funding which funds law enforcement and security personnel in public housing. Information from this form will allow the Department to measure current funding levels supporting law enforcement and security personnel and to report those measurements to the Office of Domestic Policy at the White House.
