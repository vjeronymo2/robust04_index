Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, January 31 (KPL) -- The Foreign Relations Board of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee, on January 30, signed with the Foreign Relations Board of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee a cooperation agreement between the two boards for 1994. Signing the agreement were foreign minister Somsavat Lengsavat, who is deputy head of the Party CC [Central Committee] Foreign Relations Board of Laos, and Mr. Do Vanh Tai, deputy head of the Party CC Foreign Relations Board of Vietnam. The cooperation agreement provides that the Vietnamese side will give assistance in expertise through the party channel to the Lao side. It also will help train some more political staff for Laos. Mr. Do Vanh Tai and his delegation left here for home in the afternoon of the same day, ending a week-long visit to Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic].
