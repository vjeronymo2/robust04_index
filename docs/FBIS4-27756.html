BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 6 -- A delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee led by Nguyen Van Hon, member of the CPV CC, paid a five-day visit to Cuba from May 30, the last leg of its Latin America visit. While in Cuba, the Vietnamese delegation had working sessions with senior officials of the Cuban Communist Party's Foreign Relations Committee and the Cuban-Vietnam Friendship Association. It was received by Jose Ramon Balaguer, Politburo member of the Cuban Communist Party (CCP) and in charge of ideology and external relations. Host and guest affirmed the wishes of the parties and Governments of Vietnam and Cuba for further strengthening and developing the friendship, solidarity and multiform cooperation between two the countries.
