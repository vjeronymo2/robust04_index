940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: GM 'hid truth on losses' General Motors executives hid the truth about losses which reached Dollars 10bn (Pounds 6.7bn) in 1992, executive vice-president Bill Hogland said on BBC Television's The Money Programme. 'I think it was scarier than the outside world knew. It was certainly scarier than we told the inside people,' he said, adding that GM expected to break even this year before interest and taxes.
