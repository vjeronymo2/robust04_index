Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [From NAEWOE News Agency] [Text] It has been learned that North Korea has upgraded Najin-Sonbong area -- which was developed into a free economic trade zone in December 1991 -- into a direct-supervision city. The free economic trade zone, which has an area of 621 square km, will include some part of Undok County near the zone. As a result, the trade zone will have a total area of 764 square km.
