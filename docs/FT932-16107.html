930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Sycamore rights issue to raise Pounds 1.5m Sycamore Holdings, which makes wooden and metal furniture and associated items, plans to raise Pounds 1.5m through a 2-for-5 rights issue at 5p. The issue, of up to 37.08m new shares, is underwritten by SG Warburg Securities. Royal Bank of Scotland has agreed to provide a new facility of Pounds 8m once dealings in the new shares begin and pending receipt of the issue proceeds. Conditional on the new shares being admitted to the Official List, Mr Andrew Johnson, formerly chief executive of Charringtons Fuels, will become chief executive. He will take over from Mr Peter Hopkins, who will remain on the board.
