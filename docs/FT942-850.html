940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The order of currencies in the EMS grid was unchanged in spite of bond market volatility that caused a flight of investors to the safe haven D-Mark. In a week during which trading was dominated by dollar turbulence, the spread between the weakest and strongest currencies narrowed to just over 5 per cent from nearly 6 per cent a week ago.
