BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports economic developments monitored through 10 May. Agriculture Minister Juan Rafael Lizano reported on 4 May that agricultural exports represented 67 percent of the total exports in 1993 and have increased 4.5 percent over the past four years. (Panama City ACAN in Spanish 2052 GMT 4 May 94) Costa Rican President-elect Jose Maria Figueres announced on 7 May cooperation agreements with Taiwan in the financial, infrastructure, and agricultural areas. Taiwan will maintain deposit certificates for $40 million in the Central Bank of Costa Rica, will finance the construction of a tourist port in Puntarenas and a road from Naranjo to Florencia in the province of Alajuela, and the expansion of the ports of Limon and Mohin on the Atlantic coast. A Costa Rican-Taiwanese Foundation also will be created to facilitate credits to farmers. (Mexico City NOTIMEX in Spanish 2037 GMT 7 May 94)
