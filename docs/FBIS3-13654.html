Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The brother president leader Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] last night received the special envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, who delivered to him a message from the Russian leadership on the results of the Vladivostok meeting that was held on Monday between Andrey Kozyrev, Russian foreign affairs minister, and Warren Christopher, U.S. secretary of state. During the meeting, the recent developments and contacts currently under way at the UN Security Council for issuing a resolution on the massacre in the holy al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron in the occupied West Bank were reviewed. [passage omitted] The brother president leader Abu-'Ammar also received yesterday afternoon Ibrahim 'Allam, ambassador of the Arab Republic of Egypt in Tunis. The Egyptian ambassador delivered to brother Abu-'Ammar a message from the Egyptian leadership on the recent developments of the peace process in the Middle East and the consultations and contacts currently under way at the different levels.
