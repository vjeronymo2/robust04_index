920425 FT 25 APR 92 / Finishing touches The old Spitalfields fruit and vegetable market building on the edge of the City of London reopens tomorrow, with a temporary market offering antiques, crafts, organic food and sports. The market is a joint venture between Spitalfields Development Group, which plans to develop the site, and Urban Space Management, which cre-ated the weekend market at London's Camden Lock. Spitalfields Development Group is awaiting planning permission for a large office, retail and residential development there.
