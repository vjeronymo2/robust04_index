Language: German Article Type:BFN ["vwd"-attributed report: "Bonn: Talks on Iranian Debts Continue"] [Text] Bonn -- The Federal Government and the Iranian Government are interested in refinancing some 3 billion German marks [DM] in Iranian debts through credits covered by Hermes export guarantees. A spokeswoman for the Federal Economics Ministry stated on Tuesday [22 February] that, as far as Bonn is concerned, the point is to prevent financial damage, while Iran did not want to cut itself off from the possibility of being granted further Hermes guarantees. Both sides have differing views regarding, among other things, the beginning of the repayment of debts and the number of installments. Before the beginning of a new round of talks, the Iranian side will be contacted by telephone at the end of the week, the spokeswoman said.
