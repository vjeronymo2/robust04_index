931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Melville Street net assets rise Net assets per share of Melville Street Investments increased to 154p as at October 31, against 140p six months earlier. After tax of Pounds 132,370 (Pounds 130,139) revenue of this investment trust emerged ahead at Pounds 516,248, compared with Pounds 390,768, giving a per share value of 2.77p (2.09p). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p net.
