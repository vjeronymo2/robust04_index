Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Abu 'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], the president of the state of Palestine and the chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, today received Mr. Viktor Posuvalyuk, envoy of Russian President Yeltsin, for the second time in the last 24 hours. Brother Abu 'Ammar had received the Russian envoy yesterday evening and during the meeting was handed a message from Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev that stressed Russia's determination to help with all its resources in the implementation of the clauses of the UN Security Council resolution after the Israeli massacre in the holy al-Ibrahimi mosque in Hebron. The Russian minister said Russia's stance was to regard this resolution as of the greatest importance, as well as the need to ensure international protection for Palestinian citizens in the occupied territories and Russia's return to participating in practical measures regarding an international presence in the occupied territories. The meeting provided an occasion for President Abu 'Ammar to brief the Russian envoy on the latest developments in the situation after the UN resolution. He also asked the Russian Government in its capacity as a co-sponsor of the peace process to hasten the implementation of the UN Security Council resolution.
