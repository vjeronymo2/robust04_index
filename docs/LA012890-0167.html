January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE GUARD MAKES 15 THREE-POINTERS

Shawnee State's Joe Smith made 15 three-point field baskets while scoring 58 points in a 118-84 victory Saturday night over Wilmington College.

Smith, a 6-foot-2 senior guard, hit 21 of 31 shots, including 15 of 24 three-point attempts and his only free throw.
