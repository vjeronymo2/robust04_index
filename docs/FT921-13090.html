920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: UAE and Indonesia announce plans to cut oil production The United Arab Emirates and Indonesia announced yesterday that they were cutting oil production by 50,000 and 25,000 barrels a day respectively with immediate effect, reports Reuter. The moves follow recent pledges to cut production by seven other members of the Organisation of Petroleum Exporting Countries, notably Saudi Arabia, in an effort to boost prices. Yesterday's announcements bring the total of these cuts to about 375,000 b/d.
