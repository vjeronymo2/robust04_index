Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Leonid Levitskiy: "Estonia Wants To Prosecute LDPR Representative"] [Text] Tallinn -- Estonian Justice Minister Kaido Kama has called for proceedings to be instituted against Petr Rozhok, the representative of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia [LDPR] in Estonia. The minister considers his activity hostile and aimed against the Estonian state. Juri Pihl, general director of the security police, replied to the minister through the press. He confirmed that Rozhok has an LDPR party card. There are around 20 members of this party in Estonia.
