January 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

In other league games:

Kennedy 66, Bolsa Grande 41 -- Valerie Ruiz scored 26 points and had six assists to lead Kennedy (10-9, 6-3) at home.

Lynly Matsumoto scored 18 points and had 10 rebounds and six steals for Kennedy.

Santiago 39, Pacifica 30 -- Becky Elcock scored 24 points and Pricilla Demuth added 10 to lead host Santiago.

Los Amigos 48, Garden Grove 46 -- Los Amigos (9-9, 4-5) overcame a scoreless first quarter, trailing 8-0, and a 22-11 halftime deficit to win at home. May Ly had 18 points -- 11 in the fourth quarter -- and scored a baseline jump shot to give Los Amigos the final margin with 15 seconds left.
