941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Berjaya Second Nanjing Yangtze Bridge A Malaysian consortium and Nanjing city authorities formed a joint venture to build a Dollars 450m bridge over the Yangtze river in China. Berjaya Engineering and Construction (HK) and the Second Nanjing Bridge Office agreed to set up a bridge development company called Berjaya Second Nanjing Yangtze Bridge for the project.
