BFN [Text] All speculations about Azerbaijan's plans to withdraw from the CIS, to be announced during the CIS summit on April 15, are unfounded, an informed source in the Azerbaijani president staff told Interfax, commenting on an article in the Moscow newspaper Kommersant (April 8, 1994). "The authors seem to be engaged in wishful thinking. In actual fact, Baku's policies differ cardinally form what is said in the article. We are planning to work for putting into operation the CIS mechanism of curbing external and internal aggressors," he said.
