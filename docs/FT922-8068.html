920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Cronite stake - Correction AFE, the French engineering company, yesterday said it owned or had agreed to acquire 26.1 per cent of Cronite, the Birmingham engineering company for which it has mounted a Pounds 7.3m recommended bid, and had undertakings in respect of a further 3.6 per cent. In yesterday's Financial Times, Mr James Lindsay-German, Cronite's chief executive, mistakenly stated that AFE spoke for 40.8 per cent.
