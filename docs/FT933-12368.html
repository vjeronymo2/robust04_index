930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Baring Tribune Net asset value per ordinary share of Baring Tribune Investment Trust improved from 274.7p to 340.5p over the 12 months ended June 30. At the December 1992 year-end the figure stood at 318.7p. Available revenue for the six months to end-June slipped to Pounds 1.79m (Pounds 1.85m), equal to earnings of 3.5p (3.62p). The interim dividend is a same-again 1.7p.
