921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / Liberal Moscow News attacks arrest of a scientist The liberal Moscow News newspaper yesterday attacked the arrest of a scientist who alleged Russia was producing a new kind of chemical weapon, saying the move could lead to a new era of state censorship, Reuter reports from Moscow. Mr Vil Mirzayanov was arrested last Thursday on charges of disclosing state secrets after he wrote an article in Moscow News last month saying the institute where he worked had been working on chemical weapons. 'This dangerous precedent could become the basis for a return to . . .(state) censorship,' Moscow News said in a front page editorial.
