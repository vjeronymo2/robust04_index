930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Bellwether backs down from approach Bellwether Exploration, the Nasdaq-quoted resources company, yesterday announced it had backed down from its hostile approach to Aberdeen Petroleum, the USM-quoted oil and gas group. Bellwether first approached Aberdeen with merger proposals last month on the basis of 3.75 Aberdeen shares for each of its own. Aberdeen's shares - which were 11p at the time of the approach - rose 1/2 p to close last night at 15p.
