BFN [Text] A first contingent numbering some 100 French UN soldiers has returned to France from the Glina region in Croatia where they have just spent six months as part of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] in the former Yugoslavia. The soldiers belong to the 7th Armored Division based in Besancon. In all, slightly more than 900 soldiers of the 7th Armored Division are due to return to France during the first half of June. Ukrainians, Poles, and Jordanians will replace them.
