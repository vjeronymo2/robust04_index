DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Agency Forms Under Review by the Office of Management and Budget DOC has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget for clearance the following proposals for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. Chapter 35). Agency: Bureau of Export Administration. Title: Semiconductor Infrastructure Assessment. Agency Form Number: None assigned. OMB Approval Number: None. Type of Request: New Collection. Burden: 300 hours. Number of Respondents: 100. Avg Hours Per Response: 3 hours. Needs and Uses: Commerce is conducting an assessment of key suppliers to the U.S. semiconductor industry to identify weaknesses in the supply base which could inhibit this critical industry's ability to meet commercial and defense production requirements as defined by the industry's 15 year business plan. The information will be used to institute remedial action where weaknesses are found to exist. Affected Public: Businesses or other for&hyph;profit institutions. Frequency: One time. Respondent's Obligation: Mandatory. OMB Desk Officer: Don Arbuckle, (202) 395&hyph;7340. Agency: International Trade Administration. Title: Commercial News USA. Agency Form Number: ITA&hyph;4063P. OMB Approval Number: 0625&hyph;0061. Type of Request: Revision of a currently approved collection. Burden: 917 hours. Number of Respondents: 2,200. Avg Hours Per Response: 25 minutes. Needs and Uses: As part of its export promotion activities, ITA publishes COMMERCIAL NEWS USA. The purpose of the publication is to promote new American products and technology to overseas buyers. Firms wishing to participate must submit an application. The information is used to determine if the product meets the program's criteria. Affected Public: Businesses or other for&hyph;profit institutions, small businesses or organizations. Frequency: On occasion. Respondent's Obligation: Required to obtain or retain a benefit. OMB Desk Officer: Don Arbuckle, (202) 395&hyph;7340. Agency: International Trade Administration. Title: Foreign Trade Zone Application. Agency Form Number: None. Type of Request: Extension of the expiration date of a currently approved collection. Burden: 8,470 hours. Number of Respondents: 90. Avg Hours Per Response: Varies but ranges between 19 and 128 hours. Needs and Uses: The Foreign Trade Zone Act requires that a one&hyph;time application be made for authority to establish a foreign&hyph;trade zone project. The information on the facility, financing, operational plans, proposed manufacturing, and economic needs is used in making a decision on whether or not to approve the request. Affected Public: State or local governments, businesses or other for&hyph;profit institutions, small businesses or organizations, non&hyph;profit institutions. Respondent's Obligation: Required to obtain or retain a benefit. OMB Desk Officer: Don Arbuckle, (202) 395&hyph;7340.
