BFN [Excerpts] Head of State Liamine Zeroual received today Mr. Said Ben Mustafa, the Tunisian secretary of state for Maghreb affairs, who delivered a message from President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali. The meeting was attended by Mr. Ahmed Attef, the secretary of state to the foreign minister in charge of cooperation and Maghreb affairs. After his meeting with the head of state, the Tunisian envoy made the following statement to the national radio: [Begin Mustafa recording] I had the honor to convey a personal message from President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali to President Liamine Zeroual. The message falls within the framework of continued consultations in all forms. [passage omitted] The situation in Yemen also was a subject of consultation between President Zeroual and President Ben Ali, and these consultations are going on. Among my tasks was to convey to the head of state the views of President Ben Ali about the recent developments in Yemen. Both presidents are working towards helping the brothers in Yemen to overcome this difficult period. [end recording]
