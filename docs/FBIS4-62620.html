BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent Leonid Tratsevskiy for TASS] [Text] Minsk May 18 TASS -- The first train with missiles launchers and other equipment of the strategic missile complexes RS-12m Topol has left a station near the Belarusian city of Slutsk, heading for Russia. This is the beginning of the withdrawal of one of the regiments of a Russian missile division which was stationed in Belarus. Two other divisions of the Russian strategic force will be withdrawn from Belarus before the end of 1995. Thirty-six launchers will be transported to Russia before the year's end.
