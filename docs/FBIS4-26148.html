BFN [Text] Beijing, June 17 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Zou Jiahua met with John Ralph, deputy chairman and chief executive of Australian CRA Limited, and his party here this afternoon. CRA Limited is the biggest transnational corporation in mining industry in Australia. The Australian businessmen are here for discussion with China National Nonferrous Metals Industry Corporation on jointly setting up an alumina plant in Australia's Bowen.
