February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

ABORTION

In response to Conrad's continuing coat-hanger commentary on the Republican Party (Jan. 28) and the Supreme Court (previous cartoons):

Let him also depict the other side -- the innocent, defenseless human being within the uterus as the hands of the doctor (who has taken an oath to preserve life) approach with instruments to scrape, salinize or vacuum the child out.

Sadly, in these times of self-seeking attitudes, the unborn are treated as nonexistent or unimportant.

They are the product of conception and a woman's property and lacking civil rights.

Pro-choice is a misnomer; the child has no choice. One day abortion will be recognized for what it is -- sanitized, sanctioned murder -- and it will hang heavy on the conscience of the nation.

DONNA EVICK

Culver City
