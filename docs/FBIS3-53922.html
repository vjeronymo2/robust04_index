Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] I have just heard that the Ministry of Interior and Local Communities has announced the immediate lifting of the measures concerning house arrest at the national level, and the release of people detained at the two security centers in Oued Namous and in Amguel. The decision comes within the framework of the openness [infiraj] measures to facilitate the success of the national dialogue, the Ministry of Interior and Local Communities' statement said.
