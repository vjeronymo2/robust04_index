May 16, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

CONTRACTS REPORT

Federal Contracts Report is a summary, compiled by States News Service, of select prime contracts in excess of $25,000 that the federal government has awarded to recipients in the San Diego area.

C. E. Wylie Construction won a $6,341,300 Navy contract for site improvements and building conversions, alterations and upgrades at the Navy regional medical center in San Diego.
