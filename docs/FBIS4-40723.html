BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 17 June -- A procuracy with special powers is to be set up in the state of emergency zone in North Caucasus. This was announced on 16 June by Aleksey Ilyushenko, acting Russian Federation procurator general, who is visiting Vladikavkaz. He said that this decision was made in order to ensure that laws are complied with and to coordinate the work of investigative bodies. Aleksey Ilyushenko is touring the North Caucasus in order to familiarize himself with the situation in the region and to prepare proposals for a meeting of the Russian Government which will examine progress in implementing Russian presidential edicts and cabinet decisions on the return of refugees to their former homes.
