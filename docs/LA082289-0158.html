August 22, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

EX-REP. BARBARA JORDAN IN HOSPITAL

Former U.S. Rep. Barbara Jordan was in fair condition today at St. David's Hospital, where she was undergoing treatment for a urinary infection, a spokeswoman said.

"She is improving," said Jan McCrory, assistant vice president for public relations at the hospital. Jordan probably will remain in the hospital for several days to a week, she said.

Jordan, 53, has a form of multiple sclerosis and uses a wheelchair. Last year, she was hospitalized after she nearly drowned in her swimming pool.
