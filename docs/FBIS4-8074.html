BFN [Text] The two American F-16 fighters, which this afternoon attacked Serb positions around the town of Gorazde, have returned safely to the U.S. Air Force base at Aviano in northeast Italy. In the meantime it has been revealed that the two planes were flying a reconaissence mission over Bosnia when they received the order -- using a secret code -- to intervene in Gorazde. Tomorrow a permanent intervention squadron will be installed at Aviano, the first of its type at an Italian airbase.
