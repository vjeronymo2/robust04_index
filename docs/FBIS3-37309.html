Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fierce fighting continued yesterday in the north of Azerbaijan's Kelbadzhar Region for establishing control over the road linking Kelbadzhar and Agdere (Armenian name - Mardakert), the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry announced on Tuesday. The same source reports that Armenians have lost several dozen people and a tank. In the southern section of the front (Azerbaijan's Fizuli Region) intensive shelling occured on Monday. On the same day, a tank of the Karabakh Army was put out of action. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry stated that populated areas of Azerbaijan's Kedabek Region had been shelled. No one was injured. Interfax has not yet received reports from the Karabakh Defense Army.
