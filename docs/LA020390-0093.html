February 3, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

FIRST-PLACE WESTMONT TO PLAY SCC TONIGHT

Southern California College will take on first-place Westmont at 7:45 tonight in a Golden State Athletic Conference men's basketball game at SCC.

Westmont (20-1, 6-0 in conference) beat SCC, 84-77, last month at Westmont. SCC is 15-7, 4-2.
