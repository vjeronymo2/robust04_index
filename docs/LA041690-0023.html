April 16, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

CARTER MEETING WITH ARAFAT

A most depressing picture appeared on the front page of the Los Angeles Times (April 5).

It was Jimmy Carter shaking hands with PLO chief Yasser Arafat after their meeting with French leader Francois Mitterrand in Paris at the Elysee Palace. The former U.S. President said that the reason why he met with the PLO chairman is because Arafat wanted "peace" in the Middle East. Yeah! "Piece" of the West Bank, "piece" of the Gaza Strip, "piece" of Israel, "piece" of Palestine and so on.

CLARENCE B. SANTOS

Los Angeles
