910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / Rover raises output SHARPLY rising exports to Continental Europe enabled Rover Group to increase slightly its total vehicle production in the first quarter of this year, in spite of the steep drop in the UK car and commercial vehicle markets. The British Aerospace subsidiary produced 129,422 cars, vans and four-wheel-drive Land Rover and Range Rover models in the first three months, a rise of 1.9 per cent over last year's first quarter.
