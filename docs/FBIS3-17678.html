Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nicaragua became the 90th country to establish or to restore diplomatic relations with Lithuania, the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry said. Anicetas Simutis, Lithuania's ambassador to the United Nations, and Erich Vilchez Asher [name as received], assistant head of the Nicaraguan mission at the United Nations, signed a communique in New York Wednesday [23 March] on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries. After the signing ceremony, the two ambassadors expressed hope that the two countries will develop friendly and mutually beneficial relations.
