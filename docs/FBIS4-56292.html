BFN [Text] A French humanitarian organization denounces the effects of the embargo on the Haitian population. The organization called International Action Against Hunger denounced the harmful effects of the UN sanctions on the civilian population in Haiti and thinks that the country is threatened by a major catastrophe. In a communique issued in Paris, the association calls attention to the alarming deterioration of the situation in Haiti, where terror expands day by day and where car traffic is almost impossible because of lack of fuel.
