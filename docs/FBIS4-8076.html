BFN [Rebroadcast of Pale Serb Television news item; from the HTV "Slikom Na Sliku" political magazine program] [Excerpts] The army of so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina today again launched a desperate attack from Trebesko Brdo, Mount Bula, and the valleys of the Drina and Praca against the front lines of the Serb army's 2 Drina Brigade near Ustipraca. The attack with mortars, recoilless guns, and anti-aircraft guns began early this morning. Then the artillery also joined in. Well prepared for this sort of surprise, the Serb soldiers responded with all available weapons. [passage omitted] The enemy attack was halted and the green berets withdrew deep into their territory. One Serb soldier was killed and two were wounded. General Ratko Mladic today visited the first combat lines of the Serb army at the Gorazde front. He had lenghty talks with the commanders and soldiers.
