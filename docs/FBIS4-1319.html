BFN [Report by Dirk van Zyl] [Text] Cape Town April 26 SAPA -- By mid-morning there had been no security problems at any of the voting points for special votes in the Western Cape, an Independent Electoral Commission spokesman said on Tuesday [26 April]. "The security forces have not reported any major problems," he said. An administrative hitch in the Mitchell's Plain area -- where ballots had not reached the right destinations -- had been sorted out, he added. And a claim that the African Christian Democratic Party had distributed pamphlets on Tuesday at the Buffeljags Prison at Swellendam, in contravention of the ruling that all electioneering cease on Monday, was being investigated.
