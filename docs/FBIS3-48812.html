Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 21 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At present Mongolia maintains trade relationships with some 47 world's countries. The country's leading trading partner remains Russia on share of which falls some 48 per cent of the total foreign trade turnover of Mongolia. After Russia there are the People's Republic of China 24 per cent, Kazahstan 7.5 per cent Japan 4.9 and the USA 2.9 per cent respectively. In 1993 year Mongolia realized foreign trade turnover of 722 million USD [U.S. dollars] worth, 160.9 million out of which constitute export and some 161.5 million import. [figures as received] Over 37 per cent of foreign trade for the last year account for the private sector's enterprises. This implies that their share in the foreign trade contacts of the country has been doubled in comparison with that for the year of 1992.
