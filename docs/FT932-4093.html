930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / International Company News: Perstorp 31% up at 8 months PERSTORP, the Swedish speciality chemicals and plastics group, yesterday forecast increased profits for its current year, after lifting income after financial items 31 per cent to SKr210m (Dollars 29m) in the eight months to April 30. The company expects a full-year profit of SKr300m, after SKr228m in the year to August 1992. It said it picked up momentum in the January-April period, when profits were SKr129m, against SKr81m in the first four months.
