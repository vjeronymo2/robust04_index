Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In the presence of the members of the Presidential Council of the unified Iraqi National Congress [INC], brother struggler Mas'ud Barzani, His Eminence Dr Muhammad Bahr al-'Ulum, and Mr. Hasan Mustafa al-Naqib, the INC Executive Council held its first ordinary meeting at the INC hall this morning, Tuesday, 1 February 1994. The meeting was attended by Executive Council Chairman Dr. Ahmad al-Jalabi, Vice Chairman Hani al-Fakiki, and Executive Council members 'Izzat al-Shahbandar, 'Aziz 'Alyan, Dr. Muzaffar Arslan, Shaykh Muhammad Muhammad 'Ali, Latif Rashid, Muhsin Diza'i, Hushayr Zibari, Sami 'Abd-al-Rahman, Sami 'Azarah al-Ma'jun, 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Safi, Nizar Haydar, and Riyadh al-Yawar. The meeting began by discussing the political situation in Iraq, the region, and the world with an analysis of recent events, such as the international steps to help the Iraqi people and the democratic experiment in Iraqi Kurdistan. The meeting reviewed the INC's previous decisions and the activities of its bodies in addition to other issues related to the INC's internal organization and the development of its departments. The INC meetings will continue.
