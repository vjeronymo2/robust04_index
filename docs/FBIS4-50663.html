BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (XINHUA) -- A six-member delegation from the Foreign Affairs Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC) left here today for a friendly visit to Romania, Slovenia, France and the Netherlands. The group, led by Zhou Jue, vice-chairman of the NPC Foreign Affairs Committee, went for the visit at the invitation of the Foreign Affairs Committee of Romania's Chamber of Deputies, the International Relations Committee of Slovenia's Assembly, the Foreign Affairs Committee of the French National Assembly and the Permanent Committee for Foreign Affairs of the Second Chamber of the Netherlands.
