Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received Kuwaiti Foreign Minister Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah at 0915 today. The Kuwaiti ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. Talks dealt with the results of the meetings held by the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration states, the Arab situation, and bilateral relations. Khaddam also received Saudi Foreign Minister Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal at 1300 today. Talks dealt with the outcome of the meetings held by the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration states, the Arab situation, and bilateral relations.
