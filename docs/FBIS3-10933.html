Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Juan Carlos Mateus from Narino Palace; from the "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] President Cesar Gaviria, after casting his vote today for congressmen and the official Liberal Party presidential candidate, reacted to reports that members of the Simon Bolivar National Guerrilla Coordinating Board participated in a human rights summit in Cuba: [Begin Gaviria recording] No, what we have at this time...[pauses] there is no evidence, at least the government has no evidence that a decision of that nature was made. Obviously, due to international [words indistinct] we cannot take action until the news is verified. [end recording] The president urged Colombian citizens to vote en masse to elect senators, representatives, and the Liberal Party's presidential candidate for the 1994-1998 term.
