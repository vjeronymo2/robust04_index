June 9, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

U.S. BLAMED IN FLOW OF REFUGEES TO HONG KONG

The United States is partly to blame for a huge influx of Vietnamese "boat people" into Hong Kong, a relief official said Thursday.

Chris Bale, Hong Kong director of the British-based charity organization Oxfam, said Washington has applied pressure on Southeast Asian nations to provide asylum to boat people fleeing Vietnam.
