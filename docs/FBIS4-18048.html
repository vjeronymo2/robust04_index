BFN [Text] Flight Control Centre (Moscow Region) April 29 TASS -- The Cosmos-2280 satellite was put into orbit with a Soyuz booster from the Baykonur Cosmodrome on April 28, 1994. The satellite will be used in the interests of the Russian Defence Ministry. The satellite was put into orbit with the following parameters: initial period of revolution -- 89 minutes, apogee -- 290 kilometers, perigee -- 198 kilometers, orbital inclination -- 70 decrees. The satellite's onboard equipment functions normally.
