BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 10 Jun (NA) -- President Carlos Menem this afternoon accepted the resignation tendered by Argentine Culture Secretary Jose Maria Castineira de Dios, who will reportedly be replaced by writer Jorge Asis. In a brief chat with journalists accredited to the Olivos residence, Media Secretary Raul Burzaco said that the president had accepted the resignation tendered by Castineira de Dios, and offered this post to current Argentine Ambassador to UNESCO Jorge Asis. The reasons for the culture secretary's resignation were not officially reported. Castineira de Dios' replacement, who is still the Argentine ambassador to UNESCO, recently made a public announcement that he plans to run in the Buenos Aires Justicialist Party primaries for Buenos Aires mayoral candidate in the upcoming elections.
