931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Venturi net asset value improves 75% The undiluted net asset value per ordinary geared share of Venturi Investment Trust stood at 25.78p at September 30, an improvement of 75 per cent over the 14.71p standing six months earlier. Net revenue for the half year to end-September edged ahead from Pounds 100,742 to Pounds 110,471. Earnings per income share improved to 1.84p (1.68p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 1.56p (1.46p).
