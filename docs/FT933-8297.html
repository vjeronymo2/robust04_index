930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / Knightstone Underwriting KNIGHTSTONE Underwriting yesterday announced it was transferring its members' agency business at Lloyd's to P. W. Kininmonth. The transfer will involve 330 active Names. A further 900 inactive Names will become the responsibility of a new run-off company. Mr Trevor Bradley, managing director, said Knightstone wanted to focus on the UK domestic insurance market through its six managed syndicates. The company is believed to have held discussions with merchant banks recently about becoming a vehicle for corporate capital at Lloyd's.
