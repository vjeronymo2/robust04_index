Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Martti Ahtisaari received firsthand information on the situation in Russia after his half-hour talks with Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. Currently, a luncheon in honor of the Russian prime minister is beginning at the presidential palace. Chernomyrdin started his two-day official visit to Finland in the morning. To begin with, there were bilateral talks between Chernomyrdin and Prime Minister Esko Aho. Prime Minister Chernomyrdin visited the Confederation of Finnish Industries and the Finnish Employers' Confederation in the morning. Representatives of Finnish industries raised the problems of financing in the trade between Finland and Russia. They hoped for concrete measures, especially in solving the problem of counterguarantees. An extensive business delegation is accompanying the Russian prime minister on his visit.
