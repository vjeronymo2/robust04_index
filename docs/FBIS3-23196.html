Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Nairobi, March 8 (AFP) -- The hijacking of a Saudi airliner forced to land here while flying from Ethiopia to Saudi Arabia ended late Tuesday, the Saudi embassy said. No details were immediately available about how the siege of the plane ended, but unconfirmed reports said security forces stormed the plane. [passage omitted] A Saudi embassy official had said one of the three hijackers was a woman and that the trio were demanding the plane be refuelled to fly to London.
