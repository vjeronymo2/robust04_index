Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said the government is particularly pleased because reason and unanimity prevailed and the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina has been resolved. He added that this is proof of how effective nonmilitary measures can be in handling a crisis. Venizelos said: Diplomatic efforts must now intensify to find an immediate peaceful solution. The Greek Government, both as a government and as the European Union president, will make every effort to find a solution because Greece has the sensitivity of a Balkan country. The government spokesman concluded: This nonmilitary outcome is very important.
