940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / British Steel to sell two tube plants British Steel last night ended months of speculation by announcing it had agreed to sell its heat treatment and threading facilities at the Clydesdale Works, Bellshill, and the Imperial Works, Airdrie, to Vallourec of France, along with commercial and technical service facilities at Aberdeen. The operations, which employ 480 people, make tubes mainly for the North Sea oil and gas industry. Terms were not disclosed, but British Steel said the deal would not have a material affect on its assets.
