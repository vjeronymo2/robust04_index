Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sources say that at least 15 people were seriously injured in the oil rich Cabinda Enclave after two government soldiers threw hand grenades into a crowd of people in a market in the city of Cabinda, the provincial capital. The sources say that the two soldiers had been arrested after the incident. They say the soldiers had thrown the hand grenade into the crowd to create confusion to enable them to steal food from the local hawkers.
