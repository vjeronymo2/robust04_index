Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The session of the National Council of Slovakia continues in Bratislava. Our correspondent Renata Havranova, editor of the PRACE daily, has details of this morning's deliberations. [Havranova] The Slovak parliament has again debated and passed the bill on privatization. After the fiasco suffered by Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar when the parliament refused his idea of personal union of the government and the National Property Fund, Meciar had the law returned by the president to the parliament. However, the deputies confirmed the original decision, according to which the prime minister cannot hold the post of chairman of the presidium of the National Property Fund and the finance minister cannot hold the post of chairman of the fund's control board.
