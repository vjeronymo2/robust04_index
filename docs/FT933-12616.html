930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / Land databank plan unveiled DOOMSDAY 2000, a computerised databank which its creators hope will list the ownership, use, tenure and price of all land and property in Britain, was unveiled yesterday by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors. The databank, funded by property company Capital & Counties, is likely to be of use to developers, land agents, builders and retailers.
