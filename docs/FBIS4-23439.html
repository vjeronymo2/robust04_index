BFN [Text] The police say a thorough investigation has produced no evidence of an alleged plot between German neo-Nazis and South Africa ultra right-wingers to assassinate President Nelson Mandela during his inauguration in Pretoria last month. According to a newspaper report the extremists had planned a mortar attack on the Union Buildings from a nearby hill. The police said they had followed all leads, including German neo-Nazi activity in South Africa. German right-wingers have been arrested after a gun battle with police near Pretoria, but there has been no evidence of assassination plans.
