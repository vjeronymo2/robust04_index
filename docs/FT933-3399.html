930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Merchants Trust net assets rise Merchants Trust raised net asset value by 8.5 per cent to 255.7p at July 31, against a restated 235.6p six months earlier and 196.9p a year before. The trust said the result included a positive contribution to capital growth from the investment of a large part of the proceeds from the additional borrowing raised last June. Interim net attributable earnings fell from Pounds 6.61m to Pounds 5.89m and earnings per share came to 5.75p (6.46p). A second quarterly dividend of 2.65p (same) has been declared making an unchanged 5.3p to date - a total of not less than 11p (10.6p) is forecast for the current year. Results have been prepared in accordance with FRED 3 and comparisons restated.
