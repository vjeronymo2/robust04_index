940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / International Company News: GTE offers to buy remaining Contel shares GTE, the US cellular telephone operator, has offered Dollars 22.50 a share cash for the 10 per cent of Contel Cellular held by the public in a deal worth Dollars 224m, agencies report. GTE already owns the other 90 per cent. Under the terms of the proposal, holders of about 10m Contel Class A common shares would receive cash while the Class B shares owned by GTE would be converted into shares of the merged entity. After the merger, GTE's combined cellular businesses would be operated under the GTE Mobilnet identity.
