BFN [Text] Riyadh, May 11 (AFP) -- Northern Yemeni leaders offered an immediate ceasefire in the civil war on Wednesday if their southern rivals agreed to accept President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih's government, a Yemeni envoy said. Salih and his followers have ousted Vice-President 'Ali Salim al- Bid and other prominent southerners from the government since fighting broke out last week, accusing them of wanting secession. Planning Minister 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, an envoy of Salih, said that the "political leadership in Sanaa is ready to accept an immediate ceasefire, on condition that they (the southerners) respect the constitution."
