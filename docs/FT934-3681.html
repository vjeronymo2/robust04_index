931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / Rover unions 'aided changes' Rover Group would not have been able to introduce widespread changes to working practices as swiftly as it did without trade unions, according to Mr George Simpson, its chairman. Mr Simpson, who was giving evidence to the Commons employment committee inquiry into the future of trade unions, said: 'If unions can do what they have done at Rover in terms of helping to develop best practice then they ought to have a secure future.'
