August 10, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: LA MESA

A 29-year-old motorist was listed in fair condition Thursday after his car ran off the road and overturned on the Severin Drive on-ramp to Interstate 8 in La Mesa, authorities reported.

Darren Aho was taken to Sharp Memorial Hospital by Life Flight about 7:20 a.m., California Highway Patrol Officer Nick Ozanne said.

The on-ramp was closed for about an hour.
