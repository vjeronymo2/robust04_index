930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Booker shortlist The six novelists on this year's Booker Prize shortlist are Roddy Doyle for Paddy Clarke Ha Ha Ha; Tibor Fischer for Under The Sky, Michael Ignatieff for Scar Tissue, David Malouf for Remember Babylon, Caryl Phillips for Crossing The River and Carol Shields for The Stone Diaries.
