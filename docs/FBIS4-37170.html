BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, 14 Indian soldiers have been killed in clashes with mojahedin. Six Indian soldiers were killed when mojahedin attacked a military convoy in Rafiabad area; seven others were shot dead in a clash with mojahedin in Kishtwar. Mojahedin also suffered five casualties. Reports say a three-day strike is being observed in the held valley to protest the killing of the Jihad Force chief commander, Nazir Ahmad Shah, by Indian soldiers. Thousands of people raising anti-India slogans took part in the funeral of the martyred mojahed in Bandipura. A big protest demonstration was also reported from Kishtwar where a mosque was desecrated by Indian forces.
