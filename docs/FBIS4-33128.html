BFN [Text] Caracas, 1 Jun (EFE) -- Venezuelan President Rafael Caldera announced today that the economic guarantees established in the Venezuelan constitution have been reinstated. Caldera, who took over on 2 February, had suspended economic guarantees in order to implement several measures to reorganize the Venezuelan economy. The reestablishment of economic guarantees is one of the petitions that had been made by Venezuelan entrepreneurs to the Christian Democratic president.
