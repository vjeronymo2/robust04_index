BFN [Text] Beginning 1 June and prior to the actual unification of monetary systems of Belarus and Russia, all settlements on the territory of Belarus will be implemented in Belarusian rubles. A resolution of the Board of the Belarusian National Bank on this issue indicates that such a step is prompted by the length of the interim period in the unification of monetary systems of both states. An official exchange rate will be introduced for the Russian ruble, by which Belarusian banks and other economic entities should be guided.
