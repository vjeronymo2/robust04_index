930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: Atherton takes over as England captain Lancashire batsman Mike Atherton is to captain England's cricket team for the last two Tests against Australia and may lead England in the West Indies this winter. He takes over from Graham Gooch, who resigned the captaincy on Monday after his team's crushing defeat by Australia in the fourth Test. Australia leads the series 3-0 and has retained the Ashes.
