BFN [Text] The government move to secure the arrest and extradition of former SS captain Erich Priebke "was dictated by a repudiation, which has deep roots in our country, of crimes against humanity, of racism, and of anti-Semitism." The declaration was made by Prime Minister Berlusconi to two representatives from the Simon Wiesenthal Center, Rabbi's Abraham Cooper and Simon Samuels, during a meeting at the prime minister's office. Justice Minister Biondi gave assurances that the documentation asking for Priebke's extradition would be sent to Argentina within the next few days.
