920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / Aston Martin car ASTON MARTIN, which is 75 per cent owned by Ford, said yesterday that it would start production of a new sports car in 1994. A subsidiary, Aston Martin Oxford, has been created to oversee development of the model at premises near Oxford owned by Jaguarsport, a joint venture between Jaguar, a Ford subsidiary, and TWR Group, which operates Jaguar's racing activities.
