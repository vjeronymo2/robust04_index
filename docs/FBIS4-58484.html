BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 May (MENA) -- President Muhammad Husni Mubarak this afternoon received a telephone call from U.S. President Bill Clinton, during which the latter congratulated him on the signing of Palestinian-Israeli agreement. Clinton described the signing of the agreement as a historic event, the beginning of an era, and a fresh start toward peace and prosperity not only for Israel and the Palestinians, but for the entire Middle East. The U.S. President also congratulated President Mubarak on his birthday, which is today.
