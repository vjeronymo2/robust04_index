921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Fund managers handle Pounds 620bn THE Institutional Fund Managers' Association has estimated that the value of funds managed by its members was about Pounds 620bn at the end of last year. About three quarters of the association's 77 members replied to a survey which also found that overseas clients accounted for more than 17 per cent of total funds. Fund Management Survey.
