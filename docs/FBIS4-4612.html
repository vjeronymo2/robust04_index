BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 2 (KCNA) -- The national conference of party cell secretaries which opened here Thursday [31 March] ended today, winding up its work successfully. The conference initiated by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was the first historic meeting of its kind ever since the foundation of the Workers' Party of Korea (October 10, 1945). The great leader President Kim Il-song sent a letter of congratulations to the delegates to the conference to warmly congratulate the delegates and all party cell secretaries throughout the country and clearly indicate the highly important tasks and ways of effecting a new turn in party cell work as required by the development of the party and the revolution. Many delegates took the floor at the conference, including Kim Yu-pong, a party cell secretary of the February 8 Chiktong youth coal mine; Ko Tae-chon, a party cell secretary of the Hwanghae iron and steel complex; Kim Si-pong, a party cell secretary of the Pyongyang locomotive corps; Won Chol-hui, a party cell secretary of the Sariwon textile mill; Yi Yang-nam, a party cell secretary of the Haeju cement factory; Yu Sok-kun, a party cell secretary of Kim Il- song University; and Pak Won-u, a party cell secretary of the Taedoksan cooperative farm in Changpung County. The speakers said the conference was a meeting of loyalty which demonstrated the rock-firm faith and will of the cell secretaries and members of the whole party to share the destiny with the party forever and accomplish the socialist cause of chuche, and a historic occasion of great importance in building up the party ranks into a steel-like unit and strengthening the driving force of the Korean revolution in every way. They praised dear Comrade Kim Chong-il as the outstanding leader of a revolutionary party of the working class in the present time who comprehensively expounded the unique idea and theories of party building and successfully solved all theoretical and practical matters in enhancing the function and role of party cells. They renewed their determination to creditably discharge their honorable mission and duty as party cell secretaries in constantly strengthening the WPK into an impregnable party and increasing its militancy and leadership ability in every way under the leadership of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in hearty response to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song's letter of congratulations to the delegates to the conference. A letter of pledge to President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the conference.
