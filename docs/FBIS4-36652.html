BFN [Text] Sanaa, 11 Jun (MENA)--Yemeni Planning Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani has said that what he described as "the forces of apostasy and secession" have rejected the new initiative by President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to stabilize the cease-fire in Yemen; the proposal would have gone into effect at 1800 yesterday. In a statement to a MENA correspondent in Sanaa he said that the "rebels" are continuing to fight with all types of weapons. He stressed that the "the forces of unity and legitimacy have completely abided by the cease-fire." He said that the "rebels" had refused to attend the Yemeni Military Committee meeting in the (Ja'walah) area, between the villages of Sabir and Darb al-'Amr near Aden, which had been scheduled for 1100 Saturday morning.
