Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Leipzig (ADN) -- Verbundnetz Gas AG (VNG), Leipzig, and Wintershall Erdgas Handelshaus GmbH (WIEH), Berlin, have reached a compromise one day before Russian natural gas deliveries were due to stop. The two companies announced in a joint statement this evening that an agreement on price was reached "at the last minute." The parties willl sign a long-term supply contract for Russian gas before the end of the month.
