921124 FT 24 NOV 92 / Observer: Leaks Rumours circulating before last Saturday's rugby test with Australia at Cardiff Arms Park that Spillers, the pet food manufacturer, was thinking of sponsoring the revitalised Welsh team next season, have proved groundless. While the sponsorship would benefit the team's finances, it was felt that it wouldn't do much for the company's well-known brand - Winalot.
