BFN [Text] Seoul, June 13 (YONHAP) -- Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu talked with his Japanese counterpart Koji Kakizawa, who is visiting China, over the telephone Monday and agreed to push for gradual sanctions against North Korea. In their telephone conversation lasting 10 minutes from 12:40 p. m. (KST), Kakizawa briefed Han on his meeting with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen to discuss the North Korean nuclear problem, Ministry spokesman Chang Ki-ho said.
