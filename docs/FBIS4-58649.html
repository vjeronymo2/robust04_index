BFN [Text] Dozens of international observers slated to enter Hebron next Sunday [8 May] arrived in Israel today. Our political correspondent Gadi Sukenik reports that out of the 160 members of the force, 114 observers from Norway, Denmark, and Italy arrived today. They brought their special uniforms, and will use rented white Fiats bearing special identification marks. Some of the observers will start the overlapping and briefing process in Jerusalem and Hebron this weekend.
