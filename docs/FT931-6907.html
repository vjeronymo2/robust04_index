930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO, up 9.2 per cent last Friday before three days of carnival, enjoyed a further climb of 6.1 per cent despite a late wave of profit-taking and concern about inflation. The Bovespa index closed at 14,289, up 828, after Mr Paulo Haddad, the economy minister, said that he planned to stretch the maturity of Brazil's public debt.
