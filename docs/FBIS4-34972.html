BFN [Text] Havana, 27 Jun (EFE) -- Today, official sources reported that on Sunday [26 June], two Cuban citizens left the Belgian ambassador's residence in Havana. Sixty-nine of a total of 124 persons who jumped over the fence of the residence to obtain political asylum on 28 May remain at the diplomatic mission. Since then, a total of 55 persons, among them several women and children, have left the diplomatic mission, accepting the guarantees offered by the Cuban authorities that they will not be subject to legal proceedings for entering the embassy. A week ago, the Cuban Government announced it will not authorize the departure from the country of any Cuban who forcibly enters a diplomatic mission. A total of 21 persons remain at the German Embassy in Havana, while the eight occupants of the Chilean Consulate General left the mission last week.
