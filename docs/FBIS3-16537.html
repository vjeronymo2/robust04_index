Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow March 16 TASS -- The Russian General Prosecutor's Office has received more than 90 reports about violations of the Election Law by local bodies of state authority in Russian regions, the State Duma was told today. Such violations were registered in 23 component parts of the federation. The worst was that citizens, servicemen in particular, were not allowed to run for seats in local bodies of power. The Russian State Duma reaffirmed on Wednesday [16 March] its intention to pass a bill on guarantees of voters' rights during elections to local government. The president as the guarantor of the Constitution is recommended to take measures to avert such violations.
