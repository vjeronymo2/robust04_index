940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / International Company News: Archer Daniels posts steady rise to Dollars 138m Archer Daniels Midland, the US-based international agribusiness company, reported fourth-quarter earnings of Dollars 138m, or 42 cents a share, up from Dollars 124m, or 36 cents, in the same 1993 quarter. Full-year net earnings were Dollars 484m, or Dollars 1.47 a share, compared with Dollars 567m, or Dollars 1.66, after special tax credits, in fiscal 1993. Sales data for the year and the quarter will only be released as part of official filings to the Securities and Exchange Commission, the company said. ADM is one of the world's leading maize processors, and is also engaged in flour milling and soyabean crushing. It is a primary producer of high-fructose corn syrup.
