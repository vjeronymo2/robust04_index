Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 23 (XINHUA) -- The Beijing Maritime Signals Satellite Ground Station, the only one of its kind so far in the country, passed tests here Tuesday [22 February]. In its trial operation the station handled nearly 200,000 domestic phone and fax calls, and 100,000 international calls. The station will improve links among mobile telecommunications systems users in the Pacific and Indian Oceans and users of stationary equipment on land.
