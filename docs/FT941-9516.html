940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / French surplus grows France had a balance of payments surplus in November of FFr23.84bn (Pounds 2.73bn), compared with one of FFr11.44bn in October and FFr39.15bn in November 1992, the economy ministry said, Reuter reports from Paris. The cumulative surplus in the first 11 months of 1993 was FFr33.9bn, against FFr119.49bn in the same period the year before. The current account showed a seasonally adjusted surplus of FFr3.13bn in November after a FFr7.48bn surplus in October.
