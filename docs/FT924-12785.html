921019 FT 19 OCT 92 / Setback for Finn coalition THE four-party coalition of Finnish Prime Minister Esko Aho suffered losses in municipal elections yesterday, AP reports from Helsinki. Preliminary results indicate the coalition lost 5.5 percentage points of the vote compared with municipal elections in 1988, with Mr Aho's Center party winning 19.3 per cent, down 1.9 points, and the Conservative party falling 3.9 points. The main opposition Social Democratic party gained 1.8 points, with 27.1 per cent of the vote. The Green party won 6.8 per cent, a gain of 4.4 points.
