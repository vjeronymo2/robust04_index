940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / World Trade News: Qatar power deal signed A European consortium led by Asea Brown Boveri, the Swiss-Swedish engineering group, has won a contract worth about Dollars 1.1bn to build a power station in Qatar and equip it with a sea water desalination plant, Andrew Baxter writes. The deal will bring work worth Pounds 67m to Weir Westgarth, part of the Glasgow-based Weir Group, on the Dollars 250m desalination plant, which it will build in partnership with Belleli, the leading Italian fabricator.
