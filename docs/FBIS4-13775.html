BFN [Text] President Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari and Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto have greeted President Borhanoddin Rabbani and Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar on the occasion of the Islamic Revolution Day of Afghanistan. The president in his message said the people of Pakistan are proud that they stood shoulder-to-shoulder with their Afghan brethren in the difficult time of jihad. They are, however, deeply saddened that the fighting in Afghanistan continues. He appealed to the Afghan president to jointly take appropriate steps with other Afghan leaders so that a cease-fire is enforced immediately and the process of conciliation restarted. The prime minister in her message to Engineer Golboddin Hekmatyar said that the people of Pakistan are concerned with the continued fighting in Afghanistan, which is depriving them of the fruits of jihad. She called on the Afghan prime minister and other Afghan leaders to settle their differences politically by being flexible in approach and contemplation so that peace and stability are restored. She assured Pakistan's total support and cooperation in this respect.
