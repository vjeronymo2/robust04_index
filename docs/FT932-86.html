930630 FT 30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Unigroup gets offer for Pharos stake Unigroup, the timber and building materials group, has received an approach from Antah Holdings, one of its shareholders, to acquire its remaining 60 per cent holding in Golden Pharos. As part of the deal Antah is willing to offer other Unigroup shareholders the opportunity to acquire shares in Pharos. Earlier this year Unigroup floated 35 per cent of Pharos on the Kuala Lumpur stock exchange.
