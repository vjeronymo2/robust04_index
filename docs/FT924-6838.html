921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / Observer: Big Spender Yet another sign of hard-pressed times, stagnating economies and collapsed consumer spending yesterday: Britain's home secretary, Kenneth Clarke, approved prize increases for public slot machines. Now their limits are Pounds 3 for a cash prize (formerly Pounds 2.40) and Pounds 6 for a non-cash prize (up from Pounds 4.80). Obviously designed to pull in holidaymakers otherwise tempted by Las Vegas.
