April 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 when Nice Serve, the front horse in his win parlay, ran second in the sixth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,080.40.

Bob's bet today is a $10 triple from Pro For Sure, Far Out Star and Smoke jumper in the sixth to Saudi Desert in the seventh to Ruhlmann and Criminal Type in the eighth.

Saturday's Bankroll: $1,110.40

Saturday's Loss: $30

Current Bankroll: $1,080.40
