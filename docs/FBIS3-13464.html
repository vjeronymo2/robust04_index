Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Mar (MENA)--A responsible security source at the Ministry of Interior today stated that unidentified men opened fire at train No. 85 from a field between the village of Bani Qurrah and al-Qusiyah city, Asyut Governorate. The train was heading to Cairo from Aswan. The source noted that none of the train's passengers was hurt in the incident. The train continued its trip to Cairo. Police departments continue their efforts to arrest the assailants and the prosecution office has been notified to start an investigation.
