930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / Graduate starting pay 'is Pounds 13,450' THE STARTING salary for a typical graduate recruited by a large employer is likely to be Pounds 13,450 in the private sector and Pounds 12,600 in the public sector, according to Incomes Data Services, the pay research body. Graduate recruitment fell 15 per cent last year against 1991 and is expected to fall 4 per cent this year.
