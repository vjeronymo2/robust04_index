941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Creighton's slips 6% Interim pre-tax profits at Creighton's Naturally, the toiletries and fragrances company, dropped 6 per cent from Pounds 819,000 to Pounds 773,000, on turnover 3 per cent down from Pounds 4.85m to Pounds 4.72m. Operating profits for the half year to September 30 fell from Pounds 693,000 to Pounds 571,000 . Associates contributed Pounds 230,000 (Pounds 158,000) and net interest payable was reduced to Pounds 28,000 (Pounds 32,000). Mr Richard Collard, chairman, said the company had 'enjoyed mixed fortunes' during the half, with encouraging developments in the domestic market offset by 'continuing confusion' in North America. Earnings per share were down 0.6p to 11p and the interim dividend is held at 2.2p.
