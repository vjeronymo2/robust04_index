January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski posted a $20 profit Wednesday when his $80 win bet on Tarascon ($3) in the eighth hit and his $20 win bet on Spectacular Bold in the ninth ran second. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,223.

Bob's bets today is a $30 win parlay from Manon Letsgo in the third to Rictorious in the seventh.
