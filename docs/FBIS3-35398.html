Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received in his office at the Prime Ministry this morning Mahmud Muhammad Mahmud, Egyptian minister of economy and foreign trade. Dr. Rima Khalaf, Jordanian minister of industry and trade, and the Egyptian ambassador in Amman attended the meeting. Talks dealt with ways to boost and upgrade cooperation between the two sisterly countries in the commercial and economic fields. Mahmud told reporters he conveyed to the prime minister Egyptian Prime Minister 'Atif Sidqi's greetings, adding that the meeting was extremely cordial and fruitful. He said talks dealt with bilateral trade relations in particular and Egyptian-Jordanian ties in general. He added they discussed ways to bolster trade exchange and enhance the march of rapprochement between the two fraternal peoples--a march that needs no confirmation. He said it was agreed to hold the higher joint committee meetings immediately after 'Id al-Fitr when an Egyptian delegation led by 'Atif Sidqi will arrive in Jordan to take part in these talks, which will cover all areas of bilateral relations.
