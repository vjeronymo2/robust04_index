October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RAMPAGE IN BERLIN STAGED BY 60 YOUTHS

Youths, some masked, went on a rampage, smashing shop windows and vandalizing cars in Berlin early Saturday, police said.

At least 60 youths rampaged through four districts in western Berlin, a police spokesman said. One group tried unsuccessfully to set fire to a bank in the suburb of Wilmersdorf.

There were no arrests, and the motive for the attacks -- the first in Berlin since leftists rioted to protest Wednesday's unity celebrations -- was unknown.
