940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Growth for Real Time Control Better sales of systems and services into the epos market helped Real Time Control, the USM-quoted computer services company, increase pre-tax profits from Pounds 258,000 to Pounds 424,000 in the six months to September 30. The company added that sales continued to be buoyant. Turnover advanced to Pounds 3.58m (Pounds 3.13m). Earnings per share were 4.1p (2.5p).
