Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn -- The halt on the deportation of Bosnian refugees from Germany has been extended by six months to 30 September. A spokesman of the Interior Ministry confirmed in Bonn today that the interior ministers of the laender have agreed on this measure. Without this decision, the ban would have expired by the end of this month. The spokesman declined to comment on the number of refugees who are currently in Germany.
