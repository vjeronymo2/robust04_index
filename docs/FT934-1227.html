931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Nato deputy chief appointed Mr Sergio Silvio Balanzino, Italy's ambassador to Canada, has been appointed deputy secretary general of Nato, the alliance said yesterday, Reuter reports from Brussels. It said Mr Balanzino, would start his new job on February 1. He will replace fellow countryman Mr Amedeo de Franchis, who is to take up a senior position in the Italian Foreign Ministry.
