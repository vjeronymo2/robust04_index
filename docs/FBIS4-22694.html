CSO ["Edict of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan: On the Formation of a Department of Public Law and Analysis Center Under the President of the Republic of Tatarstan"] [Text] 1. To convert the Legal Department of the Office of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan into the Department of Public Law of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan. 2. To form an Analysis Center under the president of the Republic of Tatarstan. 3. To confirm the Statute on the Department of Public Law of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan (appended). 4. The leader of the Office of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan shall within a week's time submit proposals pertaining to the structure and prescribed size of the subdivisions which are created within the established wage fund with regard to the abolition of the positions of presidential advisers and other branches of the office. 5. The Department of Public Law of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan shall submit proposals on revisions to enactments of the president of the Republic of Tatarstan in connection with the adoption of this edict. [Signed] M. Shaymiyev, president of the Republic of Tatarstan txt2}The Kremlin, Kazan txt2}14 April 1994
