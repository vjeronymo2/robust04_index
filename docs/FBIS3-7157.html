Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 25 -- France Telecom on Wednesday became an important partner in the development of Vietnam's telephone network by signing the agreement in principle for a sizeable long-term cooperation project with Vietnam Posts and Telecommunications [VNPT]. The project, estimated by VNPT sources at USD 500 million, makes the French state group a major financial and technological investor in the country. Signing for France Telecom was M. Roulet, France Telecom's president who is leading a mission of five on a visit here. The mission, which includes representatives of France Cable and Radio and France Telecom Mobiles International, has been received by Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai.
