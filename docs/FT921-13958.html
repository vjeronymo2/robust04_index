920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / UK Company News: St Andrew Trust assets decline At December 31 net asset value of St Andrew Trust stood at 226.1p after prior charges at par. Six months earlier it was 237.7p and at the end of 1990 it was 204.2p. The core of the portfolio - 62 per cent - was invested in the UK. Gross revenue for the year slipped to Pounds 4.14m (Pounds 4.22m) and earnings fell to 7.12p (7.36p). The final dividend is 4.85p for a total of 7.55p (7.2p).
