BFN [Text] Our country's Foreign Minister Mr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is in Germany, met German Chancellor Helmut Kohl this evening and submitted a written message from President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. The meeting took place immediately after the arrival of our country's foreign minister in Bonn. In the course of the meeting, bilateral relations, particularly in the political, economic and industrial fields, as well as regional and international developments were discussed.
