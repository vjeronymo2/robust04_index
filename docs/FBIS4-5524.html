BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a letter of solidarity from Werner Schulwese, chairman, and Hans Wauer, vice-chairman, of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany, supporting the struggle of the Korean people to defend the dignity of the nation and the sovereignty of their country. Expressing full support to the policies of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to cope with an armed intervention of imperialists, the letter said that the members of the Communist Party of Germany would stand firm on the side of the Korean people determined to defend socialism at a time when the imperialists are becoming undisguised in their campaign for pressure on Korea. In a message to Comrade Kim Chong-il, Genaro Ledesma Izquieta, chairman of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students of Peru, expressed deep apprehensions for the grave danger of war created by the United States on the Korean peninsula recently after totally reversing the agreed conclusions reached between the DPRK and the USA in New York, and extended full support and solidarity to the Korean people in their struggle for the cause of socialism.
