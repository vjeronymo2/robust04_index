BFN [Text] Yesterday, President Islam Karimov received a delegation from the Swiss bank Credit Suisse led by Rayner Gut, chairman of the Council of Directors. During the two sides discussed the successful development of mutually beneficial ties between Uzbekistan and the world famous bank Credit Suisse. Exchange of views took place on developing these contacts. The leader of Uzbekistan noted the expediency of opening a Credit Suisse branch in Tashkent, stating that this would become an important factor in the expansion and deepening of mutual cooperation. Rayner Gut spoke about the readiness of Credit Suisse to contribute toward developing the Uzbek economy, including the training of highly qualified specialists in banking for our Republic. Sultanov, deputy chairman of the prime minister of Uzbekistan [name and title as heard], participated in the talks.
