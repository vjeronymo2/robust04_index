BFN [Text] Six Indian soldiers were killed and seven civilians were martyred in clashes between the freedom fighters and Indian troops in various areas of the valley in occupied Kashmir. Four soldiers of the Indian paramilitary force were killed and one freedom fighter was martyred in a clash at (Rayar) in Badgam today. Biswajit Mandal, a military officer of the Border Security Force, was killed and four personnel were injured in Batamalu. A report says that an Indian soldier shot and killed his officer, Man Singh, at Nagrota camp. In Rafiabad, 24 people were arrested while 12 houses and shops were set ablaze in the Kangan area of Srinagar. Meanwhile, the All Party Hurriet Conference at a meeting in Srinagar reaffirmed its stand that the Kashmir issue can be resolved only when the Kashmiris are granted an opportunity to exercise their right to self-determination under UN auspices. The meeting, presided over by Mirwaiz Umar Farooq, rejected any attempt by India to initiate the so-called political process.
