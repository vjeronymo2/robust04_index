BFN [Text] Tbilisi, June 7 (SNARK)--The Russian peace-keeping troops are entering the zone of Georgian-Abkhazian conflict, said to SNARK the Chairman of the Extraordinary Situations' Committee Jaba Ioseliani. In Jaba Ioseliani's words, the process will take 10 days. Then two more weeks and the refugees are planned to bring back to the Gali region of Abkhazia. The special staffs will do with the people's return to their places of living, the staffs will work in Sukhumi, Zugdidi and Gali.
