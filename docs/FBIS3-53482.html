Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Egyptian security service has divided antigovernment activities into the following categories: 1. Al-Shawqiyun specialize in robbing Christian-owned jewelry shops to finance their movement; 2. The operations of al-Jihad group and al-Jama'ah al-Islamiyah are confined to military activities against tourism and tourists; and 3. The Vanguards of Conquest specialize in carrying out assassinations and explosions against officials and security men. It is worth noting that all these groups also participate in acts of violence and attacks in all places.
