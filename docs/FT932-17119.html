930402 FT 02 APR 93 / Former Maxwell treasurer charged MR ALBERT Fuller, the former group treasurer of Maxwell Communication Corporation, yesterday appeared before City of London magistrates charged with conspiracy to defraud Credit Suisse bank. He was remanded on conditional bail until July 19 and ordered to surrender his passport. Mr Fuller, who was arres-ted earlier this week, is charged with conspiring with Mr Robert Maxwell, Mr Kevin Maxwell, Mr Larry Trachtenberg and Mr Robert Bunn to defraud Credit Suisse by making false representations about the ownership of shares when making loan transactions.
