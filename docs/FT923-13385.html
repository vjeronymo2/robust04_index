920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea TEA There was good general demand reports the Tea Broker's Association. Landed bright liquoring East Africans proved irregular but mostly firm with quality. Medium descriptions met increased competition and tended dearer. Plainer sorts showed little change. Central Africans remained firm with brighter teas dearer. Offshore improved demand. East Africans teas were firm to dearer and Central Africans about steady with a few brighter sorts dearer. The highest price realised this week was 201p for a Rwanda pd. Quotations: quality 165p, nom good medium 135p, medium 112p, low medium 80p.
