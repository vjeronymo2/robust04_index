Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, the Armenian president has issued a decree on convening an extraordinary session of the Armenian Supreme Council. The decree says: Guided by Point 5 of Article 8 of the Armenian Republic Law on the Armenian Presidency, I hereby decide to convene an extraordinary session of the Armenian Supreme Council at 1000 on 12 January 1994 with the following agenda: 1. The 1994 program for the privatization and denationalization of the Armenian Republic's state enterprises and unfinished construction projects. 2. Confirmation of the president of the Armenian Republic's Central Bank and his first deputy.
