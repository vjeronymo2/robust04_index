April 7, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN YSIDRO

Police are asking for help in identifying a heavy-set woman who apparently suffered a stroke and was found Tuesday in a grassy field in San Ysidro.

The woman was lying in a field north of Sanyo Avenue and Old Otay Mesa Road, said Bill Robinson, a police spokesman. A migrant worker told police the woman had been in the field since Sunday.
