BFN [Text] A mysterious object suspected to be a part of a surface-to-surface missile was dredged out of the sea at (Payoli) near Calicut by some fishermen. The district authorities have called for the services of experts from Cochin Naval Station and the Vikram Sarabhai Space Center, Trivandrum, to make an on-the-spot study of the object. The object with a stabilizing rudder and the wings has Russian marks on it.
