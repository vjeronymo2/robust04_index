940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Business Travel: Haiti air ban The now-total commercial air ban, which began on June 24 with American carriers ending services to Haiti, is the latest international economic sanction intended to pressurise the island's military coup leaders to cede power. The army, which ousted elected President Jean-Bertrand Aristide in 1991, has defied a worldwide trade and oil embargo and US ban on most financial transactions with Haiti.
