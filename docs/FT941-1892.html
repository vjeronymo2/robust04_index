940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: B Gifford Japan Net asset value per ordinary share of the Baillie Gifford Japan Trust stood at 789.2p at February 28, a decline of 3.6p on the figure standing at the August 31 year-end. Available losses for the half year to end-February totalled Pounds 209,562 (Pounds 68,827) and losses per share were 1.9p (0.63p).
