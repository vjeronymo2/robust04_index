920528 FT 28 MAY 92 / EBRD decides on strategy for Russia The European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) has agreed a strategy for Russia which will concentrate on funding financial sector reform, privatisation, the conversion of military industry, energy projects and agro-business, writes Anthony Robinson, East Europe Editor. Mr Jacques Attali, EBRD president, told a European Community-organised aid conference in Lisbon last week that the bank was authorised to devote up to 40 per cent of its resources to projects in the former Soviet states and up to Ecu4bn (Dollars 4.96bn) of its own funds to projects in Russia and the other republics over the next three years.
