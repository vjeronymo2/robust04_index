BFN [Text] A short while ago, the Knesset defeated the three motions of no confidence in the government submitted by the opposition. A majority of 52 members of the coalition voted against the motions, while 41 members of the opposition voted for them. Knesset Member [MK] 'Azran of SHAS [Torah Observing Sephardim] abstained. Before the vote took place, Moledet MK's Ze'evi and Ba'gad raised black umbrellas, pointed them at Foreign Minister Peres, and chanted Chamberlain, Chamberlain. They were removed from the plenum upon the order of the Knesset speaker, but were allowed to return for the vote.
