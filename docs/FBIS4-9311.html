BFN [Text] This morning, following the expiry of the NATO ultimatum, our correspondent filed this report at about 0000 GMT. Twelve minutes past midnight, two UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] armored carriers carrying Ukrainian soldiers entered Gorazde. They were welcomed by antiaircraft weapons fired from chetnik positions at the approach to the town. In the town center, they came across sniper fire from the hills on the right bank of the Drina. The rest of the convoy started entering Gorazde at 0145 hours. French General Soubirou, the commander of the Sarajevo sector [word indistinct] also arrived aboard this convoy. No changes have been sighted at the chetnik positions. The snipers are still firing at the main communications and crossroads. At 0100 hours sharp, a large-caliber shell landed in the town center, followed by several explosions on the outskirts of the town. According to initial reports, some people were wounded. At 0200 hours, the chetniks on the right bank of the Drina were still only a few hundred meters from the hospital. Fahrudin Becic reporting for Bosnia-Herzegovina Radio and TV from Gorazde.
