BFN [Text] By a decree of Prime Minister Lyuben Berov, Deputy Defense Minister Grigor Khandzhiev has been relieved of his post. The date of his release is 20 May 1994. The dismissal is on the basis of Article 328-A, which states that the labor contract of a person who has a responsible post can be discontinued when this is in the best operational interest. This was reported by reporter Khristo Khristov, who will try to learn more details about this rather surprising dismissal of the deputy defense minister.
