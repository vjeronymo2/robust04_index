BFN [Sergey Bablumyan report: "Union of Unrecognized States Being Set Up?"] [Text] Yerevan -- Robert Kocharyan, chairman of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic State Defense Committee, has told journalists in Stepanakert that an organization uniting unrecognized states will soon be set up. It is expected that it will include -- apart from the Crimea and the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic -- Abkhazia, Ajaria, South Ossetia, and the Dniester Republic. Robert Kocharyan expressed confidence that both the United Nations and the CSCE, as well as certain states, would have to take account of this newly created community.
