BFN [Unattributed report: "Additional Serbian Army Forces Deployed in Dragas"] [Text] Alongside with the frequent movements of the Serbian army throughout Kosova, additional units of these forces have been recently deployed in the areas adjacent to Albania. Today's BUJKU reports that over the last couple of days several additional units of the Serbian army have been brought in the military barracks in Dragas (town near the Kosovo-Albania border). As reported, the Serbian army has recently commenced to amend and repair the military barracks near the border, which have not been used for decades.
