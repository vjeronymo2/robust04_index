930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE C and F Dundee: BTC Dollars 315, BWC Dollars 325, BTD Dollars 295, BWD Dollars 300. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 305, BWC Dollars 305, BTD Dollars 280, BWD Dollars 280. COTTON Liverpool- spot and shipment sales amounted to 42 tonnes for the week ended 16 July, against nil in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably in the CIS and Mali range.
