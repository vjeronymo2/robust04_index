BFN [Text] Baku (June 4) TURAN -- According to Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry a relative calm is observed along the front line. At the same time, having crossed the state border of Azerbaijan and having started a new offensive towards Kemerli (Kazakh region of Azerbaijan) Armenian forces in result of fights mastered height Melicli, which is of strategical importance. Turan was told by local sources that remaining loyal to cease fire agreement Azerbaijan did not answered to this military actions of Armenia. Military sources told Turan that Armenian Military forces continue to gather military hardware and soldiers on the north front.
