920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / Fugitive ex-mayor of Nice sentenced Mr Jacques Medecin, former mayor of the Riviera resort of Nice, was sentenced in his absence yesterday to a year's imprisonment and fined a total of FFr2.5m (Pounds 250,000) for misuse of public funds, Reuter reports from Grenoble. Mr Medecin, 62, who fled to Uruguay in September 1990, was found guilty of using city funds to pay for a magazine backing his political campaigns.
