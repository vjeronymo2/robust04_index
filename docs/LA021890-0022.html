February 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENTS: A GUIDE TO THE BEST OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA;

FASHION;

WHERE DEER AND ANTELOPE PLAY

EAT YOUR HEART out, Ralph Lauren. Take notice, Vogue. Dana Korbin, owner of the quirky cow-motif shops, Udderly Perfect, in Santa Monica and Manhattan Beach, is now selling her beautiful collection of vintage Western, hand-tooled leather handbags.

In buttery cowhide and adorned with squiggles and swirls or the Western deer and antelope, the bags usually have a zippered pocket and sometimes a velvet lining.

Udderly Perfect, 740 16th St., Santa Monica, (213) 451- 5506, and 1201 Highland Ave., Manhattan Beach, (213) 546- 5322. ROCHELLE REED
