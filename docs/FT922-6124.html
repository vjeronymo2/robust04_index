920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / International Company News: National Bank of Kuwait in profit NATIONAL Bank of Kuwait (NBK), the largest of Kuwait's six commercial banks, managed to make net profit of 44.2m Kuwaiti dinars (Dollars 151m) in the two years 1990-1991, despite the disruption of its operations by the Iraqi invasion of Kuwait, AP-DJ reports. The results came in accordance with directives to local banks to combine the two years' results. The last full-year result of the bank was in 1989, when it announced a 13 per cent rise in its net profit to 34.2m dinars.
