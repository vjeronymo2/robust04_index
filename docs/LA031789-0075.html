March 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

DOGS SHOT AFTER KILLING 7 ANTELOPE IN ST. LOUIS ZOO

Seven antelope were killed Thursday by a pack of dogs that got into their compound at the St. Louis Zoo, authorities said. When security guards discovered the attack on the herd of eight South African springbok antelope, they shot one of the dogs to death in the compound. Three other dogs were chased from the area and later killed.
