940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / 400 jobs lost at engineer group Four hundred jobs have been lost from a workforce of about 550 at 20 sites run by Kentz C&M (UK), an electrical and mechanical engineering contractor based in Sunbury, Surrey. The company is the British subsidiary of Kentz Corporation, based in Clonmel, Republic of Ireland, which is in insolvency proceedings in Dublin. Ernst & Young, administrative receivers, said that some staff including some directors had been retained 'to enable us to continue negotiations with third parties to take over certain contracts'.
