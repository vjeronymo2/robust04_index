940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / International Company News: Anglovaal unit advances 18% Anglovaal Industries, the industrial arm of Anglovaal, the South African mining house, increased attributable profit for the year to June by 18 per cent to R407.1m (Dollars 110.6m) from R343.7m for the previous year. Pre-tax profits rose 8 per cent to R763.8m from R704.4m, but an 8 per cent decrease in tax paid to R220.3m from R238.8m helped to lift bottom-line earnings. Turnover rose 16 per cent to R9.67bn from R8.32bn, of which 5 per cent was the result of acquisitions. The dividend was increased to 230 cents from 195 cents. However, due to reduced cash resources and additional borrowings, net financing costs on the year were R14.4m, a sharp reversal from the previous year's R18m in net interest income.
