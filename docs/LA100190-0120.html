October 1, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

SWEDISH GROUP TO BUY U.S. FIRM

AmBase Corp. said today it has agreed to sell its Home Insurance Co. property and casualty insurance subsidiary to a Swedish-led investment group in a deal valued at $970 million.

The buyer, TVH Acquisition Corp., is led by Trygg-Hansa Holding AB, Sweden's second-largest insurance company. It includes European financial services and real estate investors Vik Brothers International USA Inc., International Insurance Advisors Inc. and Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Inc.

AmBase said the purchase price includes a cash payment of $507 million, assumption of $178 million in debt and leaving in place $285 million of Home's preferred stock.
