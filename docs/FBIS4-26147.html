BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Brain Howe, Australian deputy prime minister and minister for housing, local government, and regional development, and his wife and entourage departed Shanghai for Tokyo yesterday [17 June] after successfully concluding a goodwill visit to China. During their stay in Shanghai, the Australian visitors viewed a housing project and recreation area on Penghai Road in Nanshi District, as well as attended the closing ceremony for the first Australian-Chinese business seminar sponsored by the Shanghai Institute of Urban Construction.
