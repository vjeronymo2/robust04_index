Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 11 Mar (MENA)--Four people died and three others were wounded this evening when unidentified gunmen fired in the direction of the gate of al-Muharraq Monastery in al-Qusiyah, Asyut Governorate, and escaped. A responsible security source at the Interior Ministry said that at about 1830 today, unidentified men fired at the gate of al-Muharraq Monastery in al-Qusiyah, 45 km north of Asyut in Upper Egypt. The source added that Safwat Sami Shawqi, a 13-year-old student; Najib Sa'id Yunan, a 30-year-old employee of Ghabbur Company; Sayf Shafiq Sayf, a 23-year-old peasant; and the monastery's priest Nur-al-Qums Binyamin, 45, died as a result. The source said the three wounded people were taken to al-Qusiyah Hospital for treatment. Officials of the Asyut Security Directorate, the Public Security Authority, the Criminology Investigation Department, and the Criminology Laboratory went to the scene of the incident to investigate. The area is being searched and ambushes are being set to arrest the culprits. The prosecution has been informed to begin an investigation.
