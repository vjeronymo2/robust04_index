Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A meeting to mark the 45th anniversary of the signing of the agreement on economic and cultural cooperation between the DPRK and the Russian Federation was held in Moscow on 17 March. [sentence as heard] Present at the gathering were (Vladimir Torsikov,) chairman of the central council of the Russian society for friendship and cultural cooperation with the DPRK; vice chairmen; Yi Tong-chol, chairman of the united association of Korean people in Russia; functionaries of the Eastern Affairs Research Institute of the Academy of Science; war veterans, and Koreans residing in Moscow. Son Song-pil, ambassador of our country to Russia, and members of the DPRK Embassy staff were invited. Speeches were delivered at the meeting. The speakers said the agreement on economic and cultural cooperation between the DPRK and Russia was concluded on the basis of the principle of complete equality and that this agreement opened the beginning of the development of cooperation between the two countries and peoples. Pointing out the achievements made by our people in the past in the fields of industry, agriculture, science, education, public health, literature, and art, they said that the Korean people are brilliantly implementing the gigantic social and economic tasks put forward by the Workers Party of Korea. They stressed that the DPRK has become a model of socialist countries.
