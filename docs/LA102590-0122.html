October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: HARVEST FESTIVAL FRIDAY AT MAYFIELD JR. SCHOOL

The annual Harvest Festival, a student-run carnival at Mayfield Junior School to raise money for charity, will be Friday afternoon at the Pasadena school.

Proceeds go to charities benefiting children. Last year's event raised more than $6,500, co-Chairwoman Leigh Winter said.
