910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Net assets fall 37% at Pacific Horizon Net asset value at Pacific Horizon Investment Trust fell 37 per cent during the six months to January 31 1991, dropping from 45p per share to 27.28p. The company blamed the uncertainty in world markets created by the Gulf war. Nav has since risen to 36.59p. The trust incurred a net loss of Pounds 23,679 for the six months against profits of Pounds 125,570 previously. Losses per share were 0.06p (earnings 0.31p).
