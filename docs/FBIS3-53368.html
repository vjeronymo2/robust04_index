Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Jan. 13 (MAP) -- Members of the opposition Democratic Bloc: the Istiqlal Party (PI), the Socialist Union of Popular Forces (USFP) and the Organisation of Democratic and Popular Action (OADP) issued Tuesday a joint manifesto on the occasion of the 50th anniversary of the independence manifesto day celebrated Jan. 11 in Morocco. In their manifesto, the three parties said they will remain loyal to the spirit of the independence manifesto and to democracy. They also renewed their attachment to their common unity action for the edification of a democratic, prosperous and modern Morocco under the leadership of a constitutional, democratic and social monarchy ensuring Moroccans freedom, justice, equality and dignity. The manifesto also calls for a number of constitutional reforms for the consolidation of democracy in the country.
