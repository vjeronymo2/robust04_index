BFN [Text] Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev received Cuban Deputy Prime Minister Lionel Soto on 21 June. [Reporter Sakit Ocakli] Matters of mutual concern to both states were touched upon during the meeting. The guest conveyed Cuban leader Fidel Castro's greetings to Geydar Aliyev, and invited the president to pay an official visit to Cuba whenever he wanted. The Azerbaijani president thanked him for the sincere invitation, indicated that he has great respect for the Cuban leader Fidel Castro and the Cuban people, and in his turn invited the Cuban leaders, the Fidel and Raul Castro brothers, to visit Azerbaijan. Geydar Aliyev briefed the guest on the developments in Azerbaijan in recent years, especially on Armenia's occupation of Azerbaijani soil. He added that the international organizations, particularly the CSCE and the Minsk Group, are trying to find a peaceful resolution to the conflict. Geydar Aliyev said that our brave sons, who are defending their motherland and waging a struggle for the indivisibility of the homeland, are courageously facing the enemy. The guest said that Cuban society knows about the Karabakh conflict, and the members of the Cuban delegation indicated that, moved by the adage that you find out who are your true friends when times get tough, they are ready to help Azerbaijan in every way. Matters pertaining to cooperation between the two states were broached during the meeting.
