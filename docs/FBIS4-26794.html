BFN [Text] The friendship and economic delegation of Shandong Province, headed by Jiang Chunyun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Shandong Provincial Party Committee, returned to Jinan on the afternoon of 26 June, after satisfactorily winding up its visits to Sweden and Germany. Zhao Zhihao, Li Chunting, and Li Wenquan met the delegation at the station. During its visits, the delegation was warmly received by the figures on political and various other circles of Sweden and Germany. Kohl, chancellor of Germany, and Fiskesjo, speaker of the parliament of Sweden, respectively met with Jiang Chunyun and other members of the delegation. Having attained the goals of deepening understanding, strengthening friendship, and promoting cooperation, the current visits have obtained a satisfactorily success and achieved fruitful results.
