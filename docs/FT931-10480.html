930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: BP to sell US nitrogen unit to Arcadian BP Chemicals Inc, the US arm of British Petroleum's chemicals operations, will sell its nitrogen unit to Arcadian Partners, a company specialising in nitrogen chemicals, for Dollars 100m (Pounds 66.2m). BP will continue to operate the nitrogen plant in Lima, Ohio, which includes ammonia, urea, nitric acid and solution blending facilities. BP said the move will enable the company to concentrate on the parts of its chemicals business which it believes are of more strategic significance.
