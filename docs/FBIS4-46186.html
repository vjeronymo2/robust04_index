BFN [Text] Two people have been slightly injured after four concussion grenades [ses bombasi], set with a timing mechanism, exploded in Istanbul's Harem Station. The concussion grenades, planted at the train station by unidentified people, went off at short intervals beginning at approximately 2300 last night. Sevki Mustafaoglu was wounded in the leg and taken to Haydarpasa Hospital, and Murat Yaman was treated at the site. Officials stated that one of the concussion grenades was placed inside a phone booth, another at a coffee shop, and another at the back entrance to a police station in the train station, adding that an investigation is under way. Meanwhile, at around the same time, a Molotov cocktail was thrown at Sisli's Bag-Kur [Social Security Association for Tradesmen, Artisans, and Independent Workers] building. No one was injured in the incident.
