BFN [Text] Ukraine and Austria, which are separated by only a few hundred kilometers, should influence one another, and my meetings with Ukrainian officials are yet another step toward preserving peace in Europe. This was said at a news conference by Austrian Defense Minister Werner Fasslabend, who is currently on an official visit to Ukraine. As Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy noted, both countries advocate the development of mobile and effective armed forces. The exchange of experience at all military levels between Ukraine and Austria will play an important role in ensuring European stability. The defense ministers spoke in positive terms about NATO's Partnership for Peace program, which envisions equal conditions for all countries. They also announced the signing a joint statement on cooperation between the Ukrainian and Austrian Defense Ministries. Dr. Werner Fasslabend also met with Dmytro Chernenko, Ukraine's minister of machine-building, defense industries, and conversion.
