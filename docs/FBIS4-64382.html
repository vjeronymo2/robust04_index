BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) has also appealed to the European Parliament to agree to the enlargement of the European Union [EU] on Wednesday [4 May]. The Federal Government advocates EU membership of the four countries because this will strengthen Europe in political, cultural, and economic terms, Kinkel said in Bonn on Monday. The decision on EU enlargement is an important decision for the future of Europe, [Kinkel said].
