BFN [Text] Corfu, Greece, June 25 (AFP) -- Dutch Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers and British EU Trade Commissioner Sir Leon Brittan have withdrawn from the race to become European Commission president, diplomats said here Saturday [25 June]. The only remaining official candidate for the post is Belgian Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene, but Britain is maintaining its veto on his candidacy, the diplomats added. Italian Foreign Minister Antonio Martino announced Lubber's withdrawal on the second day of a two-day EU summit. He said the decision made it probable that Dehaene would replace Jacques Delors as head of the EU executive on January 5 next year.
