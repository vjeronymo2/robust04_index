Language: Dutch Article Type:BFN [Article signed CET: "Europe's Honor and Future At Stake in Yugoslavia"; as released by Brussels BELGA Database] [Excerpts] Brussels, 07/02 (BELGA) -- "The outside world will judge us by our attitude toward former Yugoslavia. We cannot exclude a single means of obtaining peace." [Belgian] Foreign Affairs Minister Willy Claes said this at the opening of the European Council of Foreign Ministers on Monday [7 February]. [passage omitted] Minister Claes said he hoped the EU would "reach a clearly shared position, not just in words, but also in action" where "no single avenue is excluded." "As for the values of our civilization, we must hold a standard and we no longer have the right to step back," Minister Claes stated.
