Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan March 28 TASS -- Yerevan has not received an official statement by the Iranian Foreign Ministry saying that the Iranian C-130 military cargo plane was shot down on March 17 by a missile "fired by Armenian troops deployed in Karabakh," an Armenian presidential staff official told ITAR-TASS on Monday. He said neither the Armenian Foreign Ministry, nor the Armenian Embassy in Tehran had any information about the document. The Iranian Embassy in Yerevan also keeps silent.
