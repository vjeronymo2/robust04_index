October 31, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MOTION IN LIMINE WINS GUSHER

Motion In Limine overhauled-Dynawite in the final furlong and went on to a two-length victory in Tuesday's $29,425 The Gusher purse at Churchill Downs.

The winner, under Dean Kutz, covered the mile in 1:37 3/5 and paid $8.60. Fashion White finished second with Dynawite third, another half length back.
