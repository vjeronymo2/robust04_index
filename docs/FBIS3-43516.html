Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Lome, 7 Jan (AFP) -- Mortar shells were fired early this evening in northern Lome, Togolese military sources told AFP. According to these sources, a shell "fired from Ghanaian territory" fell at 1800, local time and GMT, on Maman Ndanida ward located in the northwestern suburb of the Togolese capital, destroying a house and seriously wounding his owner. Witnesses, telephoned by AFP, also described bursts of automatic gunfire being heard at 1830.
