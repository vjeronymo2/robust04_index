BFN [Text] Beijing, April 27 (XINHUA)--A 2,500-cu-m [cubic meter] blast furnace went into operation Tuesday [26 April] afternoon at the Maanshan Iron and Steel Co. Ltd, one of the country's major iron and steel producers, in east China's Anhui Province. At 3:18 P.M. on Tuesday, the furnace successfully produced molten iron. The total investment in the furnace reached three billion yuan (about 345 million U.S. dollars). The designed annual output of the furnace is 1.75 million tons. With this furnace in production, the company will be able to double its annual output to four million tons of iron and steel. This also paves the way for it to increase its annual output to six million tons of iron and steel.
