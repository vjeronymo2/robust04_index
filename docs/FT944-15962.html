941015 FT 15 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: Iraqi troop movements give oil market food for thought - At a glance The movement of Iraqi troops close to the border with Kuwait caused an initial tremor in the oil markets but prices have dropped as chances of the US lifting the oil embargo against Iraq receded. The two-month forward price for Brent crude has fallen by more than Dollars 1 since the incident began at the end of last week.
