921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / Management unions join forces THREE management unions are to form a federation as an alternative to merging with one of the small number of 'super unions'. The 132,000-strong Federation of Professionals and Managers has been created by the IPMS Civil Service union, the Society of Telecom Executives and the Communication Managers Association. The union will concentrate on integrating common services for research, education, publications and media relations.
