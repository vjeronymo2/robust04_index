920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / UK Company News: First Philippine net asset value rises Net asset value per ordinary share of the First Philippine Investment Trust improved from 41.6p to 48.8p over the 12 months ending April 30. Net revenue for the six months to end-April fell to Pounds 52,540 (Pounds 151,129), equal to earnings of 0.11p (0.3p). At April 30 the trust was 97.3 per cent invested in Philippine equities with just 2.7 per cent held as cash. Directors said the decline in earnings was a result of this position.
