Self-service card-operated copiers are located in research rooms in the National Archives Building, the Suitland Research Room in the Washington National Records Center, and the National Archives at College Park. Researchers using microfilm reader-printers may be limited to three copies when others are waiting to use the machine. Additionally, debitcards may be purchased with cash, check, money order, credit card, or funds from an active deposit account from the Cashier's Office located in room G&hyph;1 of the National Archives Building between the hours of 8:45 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, except Federal holidays.
