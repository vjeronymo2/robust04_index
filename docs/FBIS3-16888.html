Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Help for Magnitka"] [Text] The Magnitogorsk Metallurgical Combine will be receiving more than 120 billion rubles in state support. This was the result of a Moscow meeting between Russian First Vice Premier Oleg Soskovets and representatives of the combine's workers' collectives. It was noted at the meeting that state support for the Magnitogorsk Combine is needed because modernization of the production facilities there has been halted owing to a shortage of funds.
