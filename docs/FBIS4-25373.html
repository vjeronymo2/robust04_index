BFN [Text] Macao, June 8 (XINHUA) -- A signing ceremony on a 1.2 billion Hong Kong dollar (155 million U.S. dollars) debt issuing was held here today between the Macao Airport Franchise Company and China Development Finance Company (Hong Kong) Ltd. According to the agreement, the 155 million U.S. dollars debt will be raised through bond-issuing in Hong Kong, with the China Development Finance Company as its arranger and lead manager. The Macao Airport Franchise Company is the borrower and the Bank of China Macao branch provides unconditional guarantee. Half of the bonds with fixed interest rate will expires in 2001 and the other half with floating interest rate will be expires in the year 2002. So far, the Macao airport construction financing Structure is as follows: three billion patacas in equity, 3.2 billion in bank loans and 1.1 billion in development gains. The total investment to the airport project will be 7.3 billion Macao patacas (912.5 million U.S. dollars). The Macao international airport of the Taipa Island is scheduled to be in trial use in next July and to become fully operational in January of 1996.
