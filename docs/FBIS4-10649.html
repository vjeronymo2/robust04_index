BFN [Text] Today, the second day of his official visit to Chile, PRC Defense Minister Chi Haotian met with Army Commander General Augusto Pinochet. Colonel Chi Haotian is responsible for one of the largest armed forces in the world. The PRC land, sea, and air forces comprise more than 10 million soldiers. Chi Haotian came to Chile in response to an invitation by the Defense Ministry and met with Chilean Army Commander Gen. Augusto Pinochet. The PRC minister is accompanied by seven high-ranking PRC People's Liberation Army members. The two military commanders introduced each other to their respective successors [as heard] and exchanged gifts, after which they held a 25-minute meeting. One of Chi Haotian's most important activities in Chile will take place this afternoon when he will visit the FAMAE [Army Ordnance]. There is a chance the PRC will want to purchase Chilean-made war materiel.
