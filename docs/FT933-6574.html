930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Dollars 18m Aids award A man who contracted the Aids virus from his fiancee was awarded Dollars 18m (Pounds 12m) by a Miami jury in what may be the biggest award in an Aids-related case. It is also thought to be the first damages to have been awarded against a woman for knowingly infecting a man with HIV.
