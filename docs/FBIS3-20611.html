Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Text] At 2357 hours on 15 December, the Tongjiezi hydropower station in Sichuan Province added 150,000 kilowatts in installed capacity to the grid. Tongjiezi is a major state construction project built by the 7th Water Conservancy and Hydroelectric Power Bureau. The project was completed three and one-half months ahead of schedule. This brought the total added hydropower capacity for the whole nation in 1993 up to 3.09 million kilowatts, exceeding the state plan for 3 million kilowatts in added capacity for the year.
