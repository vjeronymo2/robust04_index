931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / Bemrose buys US calendar publisher for Dollars 27m 1. Bemrose to buy: Bemrose Corp., the Derby security and promotional printer, is to pay Dollars 27m (Pounds 17.9m) cash for McCleery-Cumming, the second largest US publisher of advertising calendars. 2. Playboy to religion: Styles range from religous subjects, scenic wildlife and American art to 'Playboy' glamour calendars. 3. Geo. Washington bedrock: Two-thirds of sales are generated from the 'George Washington' range of calendars sold through more than 3,000 independent US distributors. 4. Blue chip: The balance of sales are customer-specific designs produced for large commercial customers like Ford, General Motors, John Deer, Wal-Mart and Texaco. 5. Selling out: Four of McCleary-Cumming's management team own the majority of the company's shares. Selling to Bemrose, they will retire as multi-millionaires. 'Quote to come: we only did it for the grandchildren'. 6. Printing profits: It posted pre-tax profits of Dollars 5.05m in the year to February, up from Dollars 4.45m a year earlier, on sales of Dollars 31.8m (Dollars 32.5m.) Net assets at the end of February were Dollars 8.49m. 7. 90 years young: The McCleery-Cumming calendar business started in 1903 and expanded by buiyng the calendar division of Newman Rudolf of Chicago in 1944. 8. A chunk of 'Hawkeye' state on the Skunk The company, which employs up to 350 people including 200 staff who work seasonally. It has a 20-acre site on the Skunk River in Washington, Iowa. 9. Shareholders tapped: Bemrose will fund the deal with a 1-for-4 rights issue at 325p per share, underwritten by SG Warburg, to raise a net Pounds 19.5m. The shares closed up xp at 3xxp yesterday. 10. Bemrose booms: Bemrose forecasts pre-tax profits, before exceptional credits, of at least Pounds 10m (Pounds 6.25m) in the year to January 1, for earnings per share of 27.90p (22.18p). 11. 'Excellent fit': Mr Roger Booth, Bemrose's chief executive, said the acquisition was 'an outstanding opportunity'. Bemrose, already number three in the US market, moves a whisker shy of number one slot. The deal will ehance earnings in 1994. 12. Make-up-your-mind time: The deal is subject to shareholder approval at an extraodinary meeting on December 23.
