940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Romanian reforms approved The Romanian senate last night approved a tough economic reform package agreed with the International Monetary Fund that will clear the way for an 18-month stand-by arrangement and loans worth Dollars 700m (Pounds 467m), writes Virginia Marsh from Bucharest. Under the arrangement, the Romanian government has agreed to liberalise the exchange rate by April, to take measures to cut inflation which jumped to 296 per cent in 1993, and to restructure and privatise the state sector.
