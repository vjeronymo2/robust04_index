Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 16 TASS -- The Croatian Embassy in Moscow has denied today the reports that Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, was allegedly invited to visit Croatia. An official invitation for Vladimir Zhirinovskiy to visit the Republic of Croatia does not exist, Tonny Stanicic, first secretary of the Croatian Embassy in Moscow, told ITAR-TASS today.
