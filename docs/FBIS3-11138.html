Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Rosario Navas Morata, the new Cuban ambassador to Spain, today presented her credentials to King Juan Carlos in a ceremony held at the Royal Palace in Madrid. In a friendly and cordial environment, the new ambassador exchanged opinions with the Spanish king on Cuban-Spanish bilateral relations. According to Cuban diplomatic sources cited by PRENSA LATINA, the chat lasted longer than the time established for such activities by protocol.
