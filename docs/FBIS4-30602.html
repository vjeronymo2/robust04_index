BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 28 KYODO -- The Social Democratic Party (SDP) [Social Democratic Party of Japan -- SDPJ] wants to discuss forming a new government with the ruling coalition, the key strategist of the coalition said Tuesday [28 June]. Ichiro Ozawa, secretary general of Shinseito, a key party in the coalition, told reporters the coalition has accepted the SDP's offer to meet. The SDP, which is the second largest opposition party, faces a crucial choice of whether it would return to the coalition or forge an alliance with the boggest opposition Liberal Democratic Party (LDP), SDP's archrival so far. The LDP was ousted from government last August after 38 years in power.
