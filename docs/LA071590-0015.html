July 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

A BREATHLESS SEARCH FOR THE MOST EVIL VILLAIN IN 'DICK TRACY'

The most evil, cunning, two-timing, ruthless murderer that Tracy came up against was Breathless Mahoney, chillingly portrayed by Madonna. I shudder to imagine an impressionable child meeting up with someone who resembles that villain. I hope America's youth can contain their horror and treat that disfigured person with respect.

STUART BAILEY

Beverly Hills
