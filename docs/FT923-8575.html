920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Caledonia heads Wallem consortium Caledonia Investments is leading a consortium of investors acquiring Wallem, a Hong Kong-based shipping services company, from TSB for Pounds 15m. Caledonia has taken a 81 per cent stake, equal to Pounds 13.8m, although it will hold only 26 per cent of the voting rights. The other investors are Seattle Shipping, Wallem's management and Mr Anthony Hardy, a former Wallem chairman. Wallem made pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.1m in the year to October 31 and had net assets of Pounds 8.2m.
