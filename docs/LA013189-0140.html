January 31, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Robert Pavlik, manager of National Bank of Southern California's real estate and construction loan division, has been named senior vice president. Pavlik has been with the Santa Ana-based bank since 1982. Before that, he spent 9 years with the Colwell Co., a mortgage banking firm, where he was responsible for construction loans and appraisals; and 4 years with Transamerica Financial Corp., where he was credit/collection manager.
