Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Polish Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski has received Gennadiy Udovenko, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Ukraine. Prospects for the development of bilateral Ukrainian-Polish relations were discussed during the conversation. The Polish minister stressed the necessity of strengthening good-neighborly relations with our state in all areas of cooperation, and noted that fast changes in its economic life and Poland's active help in this will facilitate the strengthening of Ukraine's statehood and independence. In his turn Gennadiy Udovenko stressed the importance of continuing Poland's course toward closer cooperation with Ukraine, and informed his interlocutor in detail about the contents of the tripartite Ukrainian-American-Russian statement regarding nuclear disarmament.
