January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

COMMUNITY DIGEST: HUNTINGTON PARK;

NOMINATIONS OPEN FOR 3 SEATS ON CITY COUNCIL

The nomination filing period has opened for residents who plan to run for three seats on the Huntington Park City Council.

The period for filing nomination papers ends Feb. 1, but the deadline will be extended five days if one of the incumbents decides not to run for reelection, City Clerk Marilyn A. Boyette said.

Councilmen William P. Cunningham and Jim Roberts said they will seek reelection. Councilman Herbert A. Hennes Jr. has said he is undecided.

The council terms last four years. Councilmen now earn $550 a month for their services. After the April 10 election, they will be paid $600 a month.

Candidate kits containing nomination papers and information on election regulations are available at the city clerk's office at Huntington Park City Hall, 6550 Miles Ave.
