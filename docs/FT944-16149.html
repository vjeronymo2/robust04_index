941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Barrow repays pref Barrow Hepburn Group, a wholly owned subsidiary of BTP, the specialist chemicals group, is proposing to repay and cancel its 7.75 per cent cumulative preference shares. There are 998,572 of the Pounds 1 shares in issue. The company will pay holders 150p per share from the share capital. All accrued dividends will be paid up to the date of repayment.
