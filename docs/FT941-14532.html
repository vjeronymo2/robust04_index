940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / Adams on radio after 20-year ban Sinn Fein will make a formal reply to the Downing Street declaration 'as quickly as we have clarification on the outstanding issues,' Mr Gerry Adams, the organisation's president said last night on the Dublin independent radio station Classic Hits 98FM. His remarks were to have been broadcast immediately after the end at midnight of a 20-year ban in the Irish Republic on representatives of paramilitary groups and organisations linked to them.
