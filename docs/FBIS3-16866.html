Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Statement by the Armenian Civil Aviation Department] [Text] An Iranian C-130 aircraft flying from Moscow to Tehran deviated by some 100 km from its established flight path for as yet unknown reasons, appeared over the airspace of Nagorno-Karabakh, and crashed 5 km from Stepanakert at 2230 [1930 GMT] on 17 March. The circumstances of this incident are being determined.
