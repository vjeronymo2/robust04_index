940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / International Company News: McGraw-Hill confident McGraw-Hill, the US publisher and broadcaster, expects to show financial progress in 1994 after flat fourth-quarter earnings, and a loss for 1993 due to an acquisition charge, Reuter reports from New York. It said it took a non-recurring charge of Dollars 160.8m, or Dollars 3.27 a share, for a 50 per cent interest in MacMillan/McGraw-Hill School Publishing. As a result, 1993 earnings were Dollars 11.4m, or 23 cents, against Dollars 28.6m, or 58 cents. For the fourth quarter ended December 31, the company reported earnings of Dollars 44.9m, or 91 cents, compared with Dollars 44.7m, or 91 cents, in the fourth quarter of 1992.
