Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Belarusian Government will continue its course of economic reform and will do everything to make the transition to a market economy free of social unrest and decreasing standards of living. This is the gist of a Council of Ministers' statement distributed by the Belarusian Embassy in Russia. Belarusian foreign policy will preserve its democratic ideals directed at the development of cooperation with all states, and the strengthening of economic union with the Russian Federation and the CIS.
