BFN [Text] After recent clashes between the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP] of Iraq and the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], which resulted in many casualties among the forces of the two sides and the innocent people of the region, and despite the agreements between the officials of the two mentioned parties to refrain from attacking each other's offices, today the PUK forces, led by Jalal Talebani, captured the towns of Shaqlawah, (Panjbeyn), and Chamchamal. On the other hand, many Barezani tribesmen, affiliated to the KDP, led by Mas'ud Barezani -- under the name of Barezan Corps -- cleared the town of Salah ad Din from the PUK and captured the PUK television station that covered the area.
