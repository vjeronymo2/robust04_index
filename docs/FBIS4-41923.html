BFN [Text] BELINFORM has been told by [name indistinct], head of the press service of the Belaya Rus-Slav Assembly Party, that early in July the Russian State Duma plans to discuss the possibility of creating a confederation between Belarus and Russia. Representatives of this party were invited to attend the discussion. The Belaya Rus-Slav Assembly press service also reported that the Party Duma addresses the Belarusian Supreme Soviet with a proposal to hold -- during the second round of presidential elections -- a referendum with regard to rendering official status to the Russian language in Belarus, the creation of a confederation with Russia, and the introduction of dual, Belarusian-Russian, citizenship.
