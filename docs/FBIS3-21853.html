Language: Vietnamese Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] The Ho Chi Minh City TUOI TRE published an article on page 1 of its 18 December issue on the arrest on 15 December of Nguyen Manh Som, a cadre of the Lai Chau Agricultural Development Bank, and Hoang Duc Hai of the Dien Bien Gold and Silver Shop by the public security service in Phong Tho District, Lai Chau Province. The two men were caught transporting 129 kg of opium in a car owned by the Lai Chau Agricultural Development Bank] To date, the Lai Chau public security has arrested four other suspects from the Dien Bien Gold and Silver Shop, including the shop owner. The suspects had used some 47 million dong (about $4,350) from the shop to purchase the opium.
