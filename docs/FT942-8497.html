940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / Cigarette factory will create 150 jobs Japan Tobacco is to spend Pounds 47m on a new cigarette factory in Trafford Park, Greater Manchester, as part of its attack on European and other overseas markets. The group bought the Manchester Tobacco Company two years ago but has now outgrown its existing factory. The new plant will create about 150 jobs, bringing the workforce to 400. It will start production in 1996 with a capacity of about 10bn cigarettes a year.
