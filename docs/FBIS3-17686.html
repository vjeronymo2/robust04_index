Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenia is shortly due to cut down the size of its government, informed sources close to the government told Interfax. Thus, the Trade Ministry is to merge with the Ministry of Material Resources, the Economics Ministry with the Finance Ministry, the sources said. The Agriculture Ministry should merge with the Procurement Ministry, and the Ministry of Higher Education with the Ministry of Public Education. The Ministry of Light Industry is to be abolished and replaced with a department in the Ministry of Industry. The aim of the reform is to raise the government's efficiency, the sources said.
