Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [See SELECTED VIDEO REPORTS in the front of this Terrorism Report for TV coverage of this incident] [Text] An Algerian aircraft is being held at l'Altet airport in Alicante, apparently hijacked by one or several persons. The latest details from Rafael Bermejo. [Bermejo] Good afternoon. Sources at Alicante airport have confirmed to Radio Nacional de Espana the presence of the Algerian airlines -- Air Algerie -- aircraft at this airport. It is now known that it is a Boeing 727, registration 7TVB. The aircraft was reportedly on a domestic flight in Algeria, and according to various sources -- without official confirmation yet -- the hijackers are supposedly three in number, and they are in constant touch with Alicante airport. The number of passengers on the hijacked aircraft is not known at present.
