March 21, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: CAMARILLO;

GUNMAN ROBS PIZZA RESTAURANT

A robber made off with an unspecified amount of money when he held up a Round Table Pizza early Tuesday morning.

The gunman ordered employees at the shop to open the safe, give him the money inside and lie on the floor, said Senior Deputy Chet Williams of the Ventura County Sheriff's Department.

The man left the restaurant at 370 N. Lantana St. in Camarillo on foot.
