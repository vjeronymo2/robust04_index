August 11, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Burke Commercial Real Estate, Orange, reported revenue of $350 million for its 1989 fiscal year. The real estate brokerage increased its number of transactions by 20% this year, the company said.

Burke operates in Orange and Riverside counties. The firm handled the second-largest transaction in its 20-year history this year, the $19.7-million sale of State College Park business center in Anaheim. The property was bought by Burke clients Hutton Development Co. and Japanese investor Tobishima Co.
