920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / International Company News: CDollars 50m for Quebecor QUEBECOR, the North American printing and publishing group, has raised CDollars 50m by private placement of 2m units at CDollars 26 a share with BBN James Capel, writes Robert Gibbens. Each unit comprises one Quebecor class B subordinate voting shares and one half warrant. A full warrant entitles the holder to buy one Quebecor Class B share at CDollars 28.
