940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Honda to export from Canada Honda has begun exporting Canadian-made Civic cars to Latin America and Asia, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Honda of Canada in Alliston, Ontario, will export 350 Civics to Brazil and 650 to Taiwan in the year to March 1995, the company said. This is the first time Honda of Canada will export to markets outside North America. The company produces 100,000 Civics per year, of which 80,000 are exported to the US.
