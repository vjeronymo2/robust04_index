920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Star Computer returns to the black As forecast at the interim stage, trading at Star Computer Group showed a marked upturn in the second half with the result that the company ended the year in the black. The computer services concern achieved a pre-tax profit of Pounds 119,000 for the year to end-June, against a loss of Pounds 886,000 last time. The outcome was struck on turnover down by some Pounds 6.4m to Pounds 7.33m. The pre-tax figure was boosted by receivable interest of Pounds 32,000 against a charge of Pounds 123,000 last year. The company said that both trading companies - Star Computers and Pinnacle - had returned to profitability, with a positive contribution from all areas of business. The current year had started well, the directors added, with cash funds of Pounds 804,000, no borrowings, a strong order book and a healthy level of recurring revenue. Earnings per share emerged at 1.9p (12.6p losses).
