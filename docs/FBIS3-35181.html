Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Terrorists last night shot and killed a woman resident of the Samaria town of Ari'el. The attack took place as she was traveling with her husband and two children near the village of Qarawat Bani-Hasan west of Ari'el. Two bursts of fire were directed from a stationary vehicle. The woman, whose name has not yet been released, was hit in the head. Her husband did not stop the car, and rushed to a nearby Army base, from where she was evacuated by ambulance to Beilinson Hospital in Petah Tiqva. She died soon after. Our reporter says she was in the fifth month of pregnancy. At the site of the attack, the casings of Kalashnikov bullets were found. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] has imposed a curfew on the area, and a hunt is on for the terrorists.
