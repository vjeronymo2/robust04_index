BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 24 Apr (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], and Egyptian President Husni Mubarak resumed their talks this morning. The talks, attended by members of the Egyptian and UAE delegations, continued discussing issues tackled by the two leaders yesterday. The EMIRATES NEWS AGENCY said the two leaders held a closed meeting following the talks that were attended by the two delegations.
