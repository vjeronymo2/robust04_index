BFN [Text] The State Statistics Committee reports that the rate of industrial slump in Russia reached almost 25.5 percent during January-April. This sets a record for the entire period of reform in the country. The biggest decline occurred in sectors connected with the chemical and petrochemical industries, light industry, and engineering. The fall in production was between 35 and 45 percent in those sectors. Russia's gross domestic product during the first four months of this year fell as a whole by 17 percent, compared with the corresponding period last year.
