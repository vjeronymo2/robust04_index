Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just received the following: The following statement was issued a short time ago by the Army command: Within the context of the on-going investigations into the recent explosions, particularly the Sayyidat al-Najat Church bombing in Zuq Mikha'il, units from the army were this afternoon given the task of raiding some positions and sites in Zuq and Tabarja where preliminary investigations have shown that they were used as centers and a cover-up for some of these activities. The aforementioned units were also put in charge of these sites and tasked with putting them at the disposal of the investigations until further notice.
