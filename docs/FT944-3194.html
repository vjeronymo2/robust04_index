941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: ADR issue for Colombian retail group Pricing of an American Depositary Receipts issue by Colombia's largest retailer, Gran Cadena de Almacenes Colombianos, is expected in the next few days following the completion of the company's road-show. The issue, a private placement under the SEC's rule 144a, has been managed by Merrill Lynch. Cadenalco aims to to raise up to Dollars 60m. Four other Colombian companies have raised capital in international markets this year. Cementos Diamante raised Dollars 51.8m in May; Carulla & Cia some Dollars 25.5m in June, Cementos Paz del Rio Dollars 66.1m in September and Papeles Nacionales Dollars 25.5m in October.
