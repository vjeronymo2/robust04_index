Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 7 Jan (GIA-TASS) -- The press secretariat of the head of the Georgian state made the following announcement this evening: "By order of the head of the Georgian state, appropriate groups from the law enforcement authorities are currently in Groznyy, and in those regions of western Georgia where the death of Zviad Gamsakhurdia is preliminarily thought to have taken place. "According to information received from the wife of Z. Gamsakhurdia in Groznyy, Manana Archvadze, from a deputy to the former Supreme Soviet wishing to remain unidentified, and from other witnesses, reports of the death and burial of Z. Gamsakhurdia are confirmed, but witnesses are not yet stating the place of burial." The press secretary of the head of the Georgian state has the authority to say that there were no military operations or any other military actions in the region where the incident with Gamsakhurdia could have taken place. The head of state, Mr. Eduard Shevardnadze, said, if Gamsakhurdia's corpse is found, Georgia is ready to receive representatives and specialists from appropriate international organizations of any level to carry out the necessary examination of the body by specialists. As for the situation in Georgia, according to Eduard Shevardnadze, "this fact will not have any effect on the political or any other situation." The group of investigators that are currently in Groznyy have reported that the wife of the ex-president demands that Zviad Gamsakhurdia should be reburied in Groznyy. Groups of investigators are working at the location to carry out the necessary measures."
