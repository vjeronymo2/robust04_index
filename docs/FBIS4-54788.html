BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 4 -- President Lech Walesa received on Wednesday Chief of the General Staff of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, General Anatoliy Lopata, the presidential press office reported. "Our countries have won independence and we do not have to fight there where our interests are not involved. The old threats have gone but new ones have emerged. We have to see that and build common future in the integrating Europe," President Walesa said greeting his guest in the Belweder presidential office. "There is no freedom when there is no free Ukraine. Without free Ukraine there can be no free Poland," Walesa stressed. General Lopata conveyed to President Walesa greetings from the President of Ukraine, Leonid Kravchuk, stressing that he wanted good relations between the Polish and Ukrainian armed forces. The meeting was attended by Ukrainian Ambassador Gennadiy Udovenko and Chief of the General Staff of the Polish Armed Forces, General Tadeusz Wilecki.
