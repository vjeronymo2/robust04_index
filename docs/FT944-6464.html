941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa pulls back from high futures Johannesburg pulled back from early highs to finish little changed after worries resurfaced about the prospects for further declines on Wall Street later in the week. Traders said golds were attracting interest at current levels, but needed a higher bullion price before a sustained rally could be expected. The overall index collected 2.5 to close at 5,833.9, after an early peak of 5,850.9. Industrials rose 4.9 to 6,934.2 and golds put on 10.3 at 2,056. De Beers finished 50 cents weaker at R93.50.
