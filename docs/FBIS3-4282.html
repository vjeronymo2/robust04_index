Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The provincial party committee auditorium, which has a capacity of 1,000 seats, was packed full on the afternoon of the 24 March. Chief responsible persons of various organizations directly under the provincial authorities, army units, institutes of tertiary education, and scientific research institutes gathered here to hear provincial party committee Secretary Wang Maolin's report on changing concepts under the condition of the socialist market economy. The year 1994 is a crucial one in quickening the pace in building the socialist market economic structure as well as one in which the greatest number of reform measures are to be presented. To help the broad cadres have a clear picture of the role of various reform measures in building the socialist market economic structure, the provincial propaganda department, and work committees of organizations directly under the provincial authorities decided to sponsor a series of report meetings on deepening reform and quickening the pace in building the socialist market economic structure. The first report would be made by provincial party committee Secretary Wang Maolin. Wang Maolin's report made yesterday had three parts: First, to realize renovation of concepts and make a leap in thinking is the objective requirement of building the socialist market economic structure; second, to outline several major aspects in changing concepts and making a leap in thinking; and third, how to realize this change in concepts and make a leap in thinking.
