931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Shires asset value ahead Shires Investment had a net asset value per share at September 30 of 296p, against 259.7p six months earlier. Fully diluted, the values were 282p and 250.3p respectively. Attributable revenue for the six months improved to Pounds 2.24m (Pounds 2.14m) for earnings of 8.81p (8.5p) basic and 8.49p (8.26p) fully diluted. The second interim dividend is held at 4.2p to make an unchanged 8.3p so far. Directors said the total would not be less than 16.8p. The trust, which has assets of Pounds 98.5m, is managed by Glasgow Investment Managers.
