April 14, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MILKEN, MINKOW

Congratulations to Kim Murphy on a mesmerizing article on Minkow. It was novel quality!

This article should serve to caution ZZZZ Best investors: Don't throw in the towel just yet! While Barry's bubble may have burst, there are potential globules of gas rising from the saga of Tom and Debbie, aided by Mark Morze, the Amazing Mathematician, and the good services of the butler brothers, Jack and Jerry Polevoi. Now those are the big, unbreakable, bankable bubbles of a successful daytime soap!

Again, my compliments.

NIQUIE HUTCHISON

Westlake Village
