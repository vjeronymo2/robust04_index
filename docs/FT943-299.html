940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / EBRD to take stake in bank The European Bank of Reconstruction and Development is to make a Dollars 35m (Pounds 22m) equity investment in Tokobank, providing Russia's fourth largest commercial bank with additional capital to service corporate clients. The EBRD, which will gain 14 per cent of Tokobank's common voting stock and gain a seat on its council, claims this will be the first large-scale equity investment by an international financial institution in the Russian banking sector. Mr Victor Yakunin, Tokobank's president, said the EBRD's investment would help it expand its products and markets, especially for long-term credits needed for project financing. Tokobank was founded as a joint stock company in 1991 and has built up a network of 16 branches. The bank has 400 corporate shareholders and provides banking services to large and medium-sized enterprises, mainly in the energy sector. As of September 1994, Tokobank had total assets of Dollars 1bn and common share capital of Dollars 250m.
