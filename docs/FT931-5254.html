930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / Finance and the Family: Good week for smaller companies - At a Glance Small company shares had another good week. The County Index rose 2.3 per cent from 1055.57 to 1080.21 over the week to March 4, while the Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) rose 1.3 per cent to 1349.05 to 1366.23 over the same period.
