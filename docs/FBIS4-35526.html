BFN [Text] The Palestinian leadership has appointed lawyer Jamil al-Tarifi general coordinator for the civilian authorities with the rank of minister and head of the Civil Coordination Committee, enjoying all the privileges of the National Authority and attending its meetings. His will have direct contact with President Yasir 'Arafat. It is worth noting that al-Tarifi is head of the Palestinian team at the negotiations on civil authority and the transfer of privileges. He is considered to be one of the people closest to the PLO chairman. 'Arafat asked al-Tarifi to come to Tunis last week, along with a number of other figures, to take part in the expanded meetings of the Palestinian leadership.
