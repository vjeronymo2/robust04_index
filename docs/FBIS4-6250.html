BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- Talks between the delegations of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and the Workers' Party of Ireland [WPI] were held here today. Present at the talks on the Korean side were Hwang Chang-yop, secretary; Kim Yang-kon, vice department director, of the C.C. [Central Committee], the WPK; officials concerned, and on the opposite side were the members of the delegation headed by General Secretary of the WPI Pat Quearney. The sides informed each other of activities of their parties and exchanged views on the development of friendly relations between the two parties and some other matters of common concern. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
