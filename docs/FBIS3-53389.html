Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 13 Jan (INA)--Trade Minister Muhammad Mahdi Salih today received a visiting French delegation, headed by Serg Mathew, member of the French Senate. During the meeting, the minister gave a full explanation of the economic circumstances facing Iraq as a result of the continuation of the unjust blockade and its negative repercussions on the lives of the Iraqi people, including a shortage of food and medicine. He also reviewed the system of ration cards in Iraq, as well as the government's support for people covered by this system, including Arabs, Iraqis, and foreigners. Serg Mathew and the delegation accompanying him stressed the French people's desire to establish developed relations with the Iraqi people in all areas to serve their joint interests.
