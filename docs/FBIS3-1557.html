Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At the invitation of the National People's Congress [NPC] standing committee, Nong Duc Manh, member of the Political Bureau of the Vietnamese Communist Party Central Committee and chairman of the national assembly, and his entourage, accompanied by Liu Suinian, member of the NPC standing committee, arrived in Jinan from Beijing by train on the evening of 24 February to begin their friendly visit to Heilongjiang Province. When Chairman Nong Duc Manh arrived in Jinan, Li Zhen, chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Xu Xuemeng, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Zhao Linshan, secretary general; and leading comrades of the Jinan city people's congress standing committee, welcomed and greeted them at the railway station.
