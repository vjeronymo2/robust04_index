Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, December 31 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The central Mongolian press widely publish the materials devoted to the results of the going 1993 year including information concerning the year's awards by miscellaneous public and creative organizations. The newspaper "ARDIYN ERH" informs that president of the country P. Ochirbat has got acquainted with the suggestions and proposals relating to an economic distribution of Mongolia worked out by the National Development Department in cooperation with other competent organs. The president noted that the economical distribution of the country is of great importance for the implementation of structural policies on the territorial level, the real attitude of aymags and other local units towards market relations, renovation of the territorial administration system. According to him, the proposals for economic distribution are expedient to be considered by the council for national security prior to submitting them for consideration to the Great State Hural of Mongolia. The newspaper publishes the interview of its correspondent with Minister of Demographical Policies and Labour E. Gombojab which was devoted to the approval of regular measures on ensuring social guarantees of living standards for the population. E. Gombojab noted that the above mentioned measures are being taken in connection with the growth of prices in order to guarantee proper minimum subsidence level for citizens especially the vulnerable ones. The measures intended for by the government would be realized beginning since 1994. It is planned to increase considerably salaries especially for those engaged in intellectual [word indistinct] and to increase tensions and allowances as [word indistinct]. The newspaper commenting on the activity of the Great State Hural over the year of 1993, noted that it was fruitful especially in relation to the laying down legal basis for state organs functioning in accordance with the Constitution. The Mongolian parliament has paid much attention to the problems of transition of the country to a market economy [words indistinct]. It is noted that there is a considerable rise in the number of herdsmen owning over a thousand head of cattle. The newspaper "ERH CHOLOO" issued by the Mongolian National Democratic Party [MNDP] underlined a creation by the government the working group for study and implementation of proposals put forward by the MNDP on approving pressed measures for restoration and development of domestic production. The MNDP in its statement stressed that the wrong creditory custom and tax policies of the government is hindering efficient development of production in the country. The newspaper publishes an article by chairman of the city committee of the MNDP T. Enhtubshin who underscored the necessity for steady carrying on policies for increasing treasure. It is connected as the author noted, with the fact that over the last 70 years because of the domination of one party there has been squandered the property of the country. The author considers that society and economy are possible to develop only on the foundation of private interests of people which is in conformity with human tendencies.
