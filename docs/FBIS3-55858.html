Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] While the Armenian workers were repairing the blown up sectors of the pipeline in Marneulskiy Rayon, yet another explosion took place on Georgian territory. It surpasses the others in its magnitude. A 300-meter sector of the 1,000-mm gas pipeline was blown up and put out of order at the place where it crosses the Kura River in Azerbaijani-inhabited Gardabanskiy Rayon. Moreover, a 800-meter-long sector of the pipeline was displaced by some 5 meters. Specialists from the Armenian State Gas Enterprise have left for the site of the accident. In their opinion, the Kura River should be diverted and large-scale earth moving should be carried out at the location of the explosion in order to be able to repair the pipeline.
