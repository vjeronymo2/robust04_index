Under Title III of the Clean Air Act Amendments of 1990, the Agency is required to regulate any of the 189 hazardous air pollutants (HAPs). Ethylene oxide is a HAP, and the user is alerted to follow all upcoming regulations concerning the use of ethylene oxide, whether used alone or in a blend. Manufacturers and users are alerted to the fact that the Agency has issued a Proposed Rulemaking which includes EtO used in all sterilizers except hospital systems (59 FR 10591, March 7, 1994).
