Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 5 (XINHUA) -- More than 10,000 Chinese and foreign women gathered in Beijing today to mark the 84th anniversary of March 8 -- International Women's Day. The gathering also aimed to greet the World Women's Congress which is to be held in Beijing in 1995. Chinese Premier Li Peng attended the gathering, which was presided over by Chen Muhua, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and president of the All-China Women's Federation. Speaking to the gathering, Li Peng expressed regards to Chinese women of all nationalities, of Taiwan, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC) and the State Council. He said that Chinese women constitute a great force for China's reforms and opening up and modernization. Since China adopted economic reforms and opened to the outside world, there have been great changes in Chinese women's outlook, with remarkable progress in the quality of the women. He said that Chinese women were actively taking part in economic development and had made important contributions in political, cultural and social sectors. He noted that Chinese women enjoy a high status in society and they really hold up half the sky in building socialism with Chinese characteristics. In 1995, the United Nations will hold the Fourth World Women's Congress in Beijing. Li Peng said that the congress is an important meeting to study women's issues of the world. He said that it is a great honor for China to have the opportunity to prepare for and host the congress. He said that the Chinese Government, Chinese people, and Chinese women will go all out to prepare for the congress, and will ensure its success. The premier noted that now China is at an important development period. It is the common will of all Chinese people, including Chinese women, to turn China into a modern and socialist country based on prosperity, democracy and civilization. He said that China's future is bright and the world future is broad. Other party and government leaders, including Hu Jintao and Rong Yiren, attended the gathering. Some foreign guests also attended the gathering. Before his speech, Premier Li Peng and other leaders met a group of outstanding women who have been giving reports of their achievements in different parts of the country.
