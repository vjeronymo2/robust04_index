921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton & Jute COTTON Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool for the week ended 4 December amounted to 116 tonnes against 20 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in Mali and Syrian descriptions. Central and South American growths made some headway.
