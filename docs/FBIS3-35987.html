Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Tbilisi Awaits Boris Yeltsin..."] [Text] As a POSTFACTUM correspondent has been told in circles close to the Georgian Ministry of Security and Ministry of Internal Affairs, 20 of the Russian Federation president's security personnel have arrived in Tbilisi. The routes that Boris Yeltsin will take have been divided into five zones, and each of these into several sectors. For their part, the Georgian special services are also adopting extraordinary security measures. In particular, 5,000 policemen and around 300 Ministry of Security staffers intend to ensure security in the zone of the route that will be taken by the Russian Federation president 3 February. As the Georgian Foreign Ministry has reported, B. Yeltsin will be accompanied by Russian Federation Foreign Minister A. Kozyrev, St. Petersburg Mayor A. Sobchak, and Defense Minister P. Grachev.
