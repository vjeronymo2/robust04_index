Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Liberals Split -- A New Party Is Born"] [Text] Rome -- The latest split among the Liberals: At the end of the party congress, one faction has created the Center Union [UDC], with Stefano De Luca as its chairman, and another one has set up the Federation of Liberals. "De Luca and the UDC are aiming to become a satellite of Forza Italia," Raffaello Morelli, vice president of the Federation has said.
