920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / Time targets British Rail yesterday published the first figures to show its performance against targets on a line-by-line basis since the Passenger's Charter was introduced two months ago. The chart shows how four of the 15 Network SouthEast routes and five out of the seven InterCity routes are missing the punctuality targets in the BR Passenger's Charter. Regional Railways, the provincial network, is doing rather better: only two of its 22 routes (Cross Country and East and West Midlands) are failing to meet the required standard.
