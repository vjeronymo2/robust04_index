921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Waste Management ahead 62% to Pounds 40m Waste Management International, the UK-quoted arm of Waste Management of Chicago, yesterday reported a 62 per cent increase in third quarter profits on sales ahead 41 per cent. Pre-tax profits for the three months to end-September rose from Pounds 24.7m to Pounds 40.1m Third quarter earnings per share rose from 4.7p to 6.1p (12.5p for nine months).
