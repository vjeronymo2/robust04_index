Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [KYODO/YONHAP report from Beijing] [Text] Chinese President Jiang Zemin invited North Korean President Kim Il-song to visit China at the latter's convenience some time this year. The Chinese Government news agency XINHUA reported in a news dispatch from Pyongyang. XINHUA said that Li Shuzheng, head of the CPC International Liaison Department, conveyed President Jiang Zemin's desire for the invitation when she, on a visit to Pyongyang, visited President Kim yesterday. XINHUA said that President Kim expressed gratitude for this and expressed his desire to visit China when the opportunity arises.
