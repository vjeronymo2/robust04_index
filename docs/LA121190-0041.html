December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: COLLEGE BASKETBALL

Louisiana State moved from No. 18 to No. 12 in the Associated Press poll with its 92-82 upset of second-ranked Arizona Saturday.

Nevada Las Vegas, with 63 of 65 first-place votes, retained the No. 1 ranking.

Arizona dropped from second to fourth, Arkansas moved from third to second and Syracuse moved up a spot to third.
