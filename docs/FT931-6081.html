930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / People: Cater Allen Lloyd's Edward Bloxham, 50, currently chief executive of RHM Outhwaite (Underwriting Agencies), is to take over as group chief executive of Cater Allen Lloyd's Holdings, which handles the group's Lloyd's agencies interests. Bloxham, a Yorkshireman, spent the early part of his career in the publishing industry. A certified accountant, he joined the Corporation of Lloyd's regulatory department in 1983; he took over as finance director of Outhwaite in 1985, becoming chief executive in 1988.
