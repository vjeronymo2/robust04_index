Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] RIA has learned that Russian President Boris Yeltsin's address to deputies of the Federal Assembly planned for Friday [18 February] was postponed to 24 February. A source close to the president, who wished to remain anonymous, said that the formal reason for the postponement could be the fact that the Kremlin's Marble Hall is not ready to hold such an event. However, it is not ruled out that the text of the annual address of the president may undergo some changes. At today's session of the Russian State Duma's Committee on Budget, its chairman, Mikhail Zadornov, told deputies that they could become acquainted with the text of the address, which had been received by the Duma.
