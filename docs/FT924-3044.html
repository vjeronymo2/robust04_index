921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / Polish strike to go ahead Poland's Solidarity trade union will go ahead with plans for a two-hour general strike next week after talks with the government failed to satisfy its demands, a union spokeswoman said yesterday, Reuter reports from Warsaw. Ms Ewa Tomaszewska, a member of Solidarity's national commission, said the government had not agreed to ease wage curbs enough to compensate falling real wages.
