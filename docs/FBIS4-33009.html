BFN [Text] An electoral court on 25 May ordered an inquest to investigate if Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva, the Workers Party [PT] presidential candidate, violated the law when he used a sound truck from a union affiliated with the Sole Workers Center [CUT] in an election rally. Judge Alex Vilenozski ordered Lula, the PT, and the union to refrain from repeating this action and ordered the Civilian and Military Police to prevent the use of union equipment by presidential candidates in Sao Bernardo do Campo. Lula commented on the court ruling in Rio de Janeiro. He said: Between what is legal and what is just, I believe the latter is more important.
