940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Arts: Ambassadors Theatre - Openings Although Oleanna, his controversial broadside on the sex war, has just ended its extended West End run, David Mamet is still to be found on London stages. Glengarry Glen Ross recently opened at the Donmar Warehouse; and his latest play, The Cryptogram, a study of family betrayal, opens on Wednesday at the Ambassadors Theatre, starring Lindsay Duncan and the award-winning comedian Eddie Izzard.
