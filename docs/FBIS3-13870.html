Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Mar (MENA)-- Prosecutor General Counselor Raja' al-'Arabi today decided to refer Labor Party Leader Engineer Ibrahim Shukri; Majdi Ahmad Husayn, chief editor of the party's mouthpiece AL-SHA'B; and Muhammad Zaghlul and 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Abbas, journalists working in the paper, to the criminal court on the charge of slandering and libeling People's Assembly Deputy Thurayya Labanah. The prosecution statement said that in handling the issue of the Madinat Nasr billionaire, the defendants published in issues No. 809 and 810 of the paper stories in which they accused Mrs. Labanah of helping some law breakers in return for obtaining apartments.
