BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Celso Amorim said on 27 May that Brazil's participation in the UN Security Council is "legitimate and effective." Amorim noted: "It is of growing importance for all countries, not only the rich countries or those with nuclear weapons, to participate in this council." The minister participated on 27 May in the First National Meeting of Strategic Studies at the Strategic Affairs Secretariat (SAE)'s Strategic Studies Center. The SAE-sponsored meeting's goal is to promote an exchange among the country's think tanks involved in "strategy" affairs and demonstrate the government's interest in the scattered studies conducted in Brazil in this area today. Representatives from public and private organizations and universities participated in the meeting.
