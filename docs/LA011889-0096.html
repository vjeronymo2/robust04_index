January 18, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

BRIEFLY

Marriott Corp. and Huntsman Chemical Corp. have agreed to provide and package meals for passengers of the Soviet airline Aeroflot. Soviet Ambassador Yuri V. Dubinin held a joint news conference with the heads of the two U.S. companies, J.W. Marriott Jr. of Washington and Jon Huntsman of Salt Lake City, to give details of the joint ventures set up without pubic announcement in Moscow last month. Aeroflot already has contracts with Marriott to provide meals on its airplanes at several airports outside the Soviet Union.
