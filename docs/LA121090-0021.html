December 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Kidder Suit: Actress Margot Kidder on Friday sued the producers of a television series, claiming that she may have suffered permanent injuries in a car accident during filming. In a claim filed Friday in British Columbia Supreme Court, Kidder said she has continuous muscle spasms in her neck and back, numbness and lack of circulation in her left leg and nerve damage to her left arm, left shoulder and neck. The suit seeks damages from Toronto-based Nelvana Ltd. and N.D. Productions B.C. Ltd. of Vancouver from an October accident in which Kidder was injured during filming of a series based on the Nancy Drew mystery books. CLAUDIA PUIG
