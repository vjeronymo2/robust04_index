921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / FT boosts business readership MORE captains of industry read the Financial Times than any other newspaper, according to a Mori survey among chairmen, managing directors and other main board directors of Britain's 500 largest companies. The FT's coverage among the group rose from 81 per cent to 89 per cent last year compared with 48 per cent for The Times, the next title. The FT's regular readership among leading industrialists rose from 81 per cent to 92 per cent. The Times was second with 42 per cent. On Sundays the Sunday Times leads with 66 per cent, down from 75 per cent. The survey was based on a sample of 110, representing a 53 per cent response rate.
