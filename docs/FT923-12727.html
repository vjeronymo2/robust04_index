920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: Waste Management advances WASTE Management, the US waste disposal group, yesterday reported an 11 per cent improvement in underlying second-quarter net income, to Dollars 213.8m, or 43 cents a share, from Dollars 193m, or 39 cents, a year earlier. Earnings in both years were distorted by special items which brought reported 1992 second-quarter net income to Dollars 329.5m, or 66 cents, compared with Dollars 206.9m, or 42 cents, last year. The latest quarter included an extraordinary gain of 23 cents a share. A year earlier, net earnings included one-time gains of about 3 cents a share. Revenues rose to Dollars 2.18bn from Dollars 1.88bn. For the first half, Waste Management had net income of Dollars 450m, or 91 cents, on revenues of Dollars 4.2bn, compared with earnings of Dollars 372.6m, or 76 cents, on revenues of Dollars 3.63bn in the first six months of 1991. Stripping out one-time items, the company said its first-half net earnings rose 13 per cent to Dollars 405.9m, or 82 cents, from Dollars 358.6m or 73 cents.
