940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Northamber back in the black Northamber, the supplier of computer hardware and software, maintained its first-half improvement to end the year to April 30 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 669,000, against Pounds 973,000 losses previously. Turnover on continuing operations climbed by 18 per cent to Pounds 114.5m. Profits included an exceptional surplus of Pounds 96,000 from the sale of a property bought in 1985. Earnings per share were 3.1p (3.9p losses) and the single final dividend has been raised from 0.6p to 0.8p.
