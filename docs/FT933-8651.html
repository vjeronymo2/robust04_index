930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / National Savings contribution falls NATIONAL Savings contributed Pounds 103m to government funding in July compared with Pounds 220m in June. Gross sales of Pounds 638m were outweighed by repayments of Pounds 727m, with accrued interest amounting to Pounds 192m. Fixed-interest certificates contributed the most to the total, at Pounds 53m, followed by capital bonds at Pounds 38m. The total amount invested in National Savings at the end of July was Pounds 44.9bn.
