BFN [Text] Occupied Jerusalem -- Jawad Hashim, Iraqi opposition figure and former minister, has paid a secret visit to Israel and met with Yitzhaq Rabin, Israeli prime minister and minister of defense. He also met with many Israeli officials, including Shim'on Peres, the foreign minister, and Moshe Shahal, minister of police and energy. Moreover, Hashim met with several members of the Labor and Likud parties in the Knesset. It is noteworthy that this visit by Jawad Hashim, who has been to Israel more than once during the past few years, lasted for several days last week. Minister Hashim has lived in Europe since the Gulf crisis.
