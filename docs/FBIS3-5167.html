Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] The friendship visit by His Excellency [H.E.] Mongolian President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], which began on the evening of 28 February, concluded with brilliant success this morning. The Mongolian president, his wife, and entourage today left the capital of Vientiane for Hanoi by special plane to pay an official friendship visit to the SRV. During his stay in the LPDR, H.E. Punsalmaagiym Ochirbat held talks with H.E. LPDR President Nouhak Phoumsavan and paid a courtesy call on H.E. Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee and LPDR prime minister. He also visited the construction site of the Mekong Friendship Bridge and the Mongolian Embassy in Laos and toured Vientiane Province, the Nam Ngum Hydropower Plant, and the Nam Ngum Reservoir. Wherever he went, he was given a warm welcome by cadres, workers, local administrators, and the people. Before departing for Hanoi today, H.E. Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat and H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan signed a treaty of friendship and cooperation between the LPDR and Mongolia. The two excellencies also attended a signing ceremony for an agreement on the promotion and protection of investment between (?the ministries of trade) of the two countries. A farewell ceremony was held later at Ho Kham Hall, attended by H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan, his wife, and other (?Lao leaders).
