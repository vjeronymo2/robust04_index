October 12, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Hold the organ music: Friday the 13th has apparently exacted its curse on Valentina Vitale and Anthony Nunzio, the title characters of "Tony n' Tina's Wedding." The producers of the environmental theater piece that re-creates an Italian-American wedding celebration have canceled this Friday's press performance. The reason: anticipated noise interference from a Rip magazine party-concert (featuring Faster Pussycat, Wolfsbane, Phantom Blue, Love Hate, Johnny Krash, Mimes) that will be going on in a room directly above a ballroom at the Park Plaza Hotel, where the mock wedding is staged. ALEENE MacMINN
