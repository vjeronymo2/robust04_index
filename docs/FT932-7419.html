930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: Improving laboratory facilities CS PROJECT CONSULTANTS, the project management company of Cyril Sweett & Partners, has been appointed by the Ministry of Agriculture to act as project managers manager for the preparation of to prepare a redevelopment proposal for the central laboratory at Weybridge in Surrey and the progression of Phase 1 of the development. The overall redevelopment which is expected to cost more than Pounds 200m will be phased over 10 years and will involve complex logistics management in decanting, demolition, new-build and re-occupation while maintaining CVL's business.
