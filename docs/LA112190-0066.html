November 21, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: VENTURA;

MAN TRAPPED UNDER HIS MOBILE HOME

A 53-year-old Ventura man who crawled under his motor home to check on a suspension problem Tuesday morning was trapped beneath the vehicle and found unconscious there by Ventura Fire Department personnel, officials said.

Augie Luna was reported in serious condition at Ventura County Medical Center on Tuesday night.

Fire personnel jacked up the motor home on Channel Heights Court and discovered that Luna had no pulse. They revived his heartbeat before taking him to the hospital about 10 a.m., department spokesman Barry Simmons said. KIRSTEN LEE SWARTZ
