BFN [Text] In the first reaction to north Yemen's decision to implement a cease-fire as of midnight tonight, Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has said that Sanaa's decision is important. It is a response by the Yemeni leadership to the efforts that have been made to stop the bloodshed in Yemen, he said. He added that the decision is undoubtedly a positive first step that needs speedy work to prepare the right climate for the resumption of the dialogue between the brothers in Yemen to achieve security and stability and stop the bloodshed in compliance with Security Council Resolution No. 924.
