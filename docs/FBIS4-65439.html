BFN [Text] Saarbruecken (DDP/ADN) -- Saarland will not deport any Kurds who originate from southeastern Turkey until further notice. This was announced by State Interior Minister Friedel Laepple (Social Democratic Party [SPD]), on Wednesday [18 May]. He stressed on Saarlaendischer Rundfunk that Saarland supports the halt to deportations which the SPD-governed states of Rhineland-Palatinate and Schleswig-Holstein have decided on.
