930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Peru wins rescheduling PERU said yesterday the Paris Club of western creditors had agreed to reschedule its official debts, Reuter reports from Paris. As a result of the deal, the Dollars 3.1bn Lima was due to pay out in 1993-95 is to be reduced by about Dollars 1.88bn. A statement said Peru had won exceptional terms; the deal rescheduled payments falling due over a 39-month period, a record.
