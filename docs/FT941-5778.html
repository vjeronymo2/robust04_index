940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / UK Company News: AAH acquires Peak Systems AAH, the pharmaceuticals distribution group, has expanded via the acquisition of Peak Systems and two associated companies. Peak, a supplier of computer systems to community care providers, will operate as a business unit within AAH Medital, which supplies computer systems to general practitioners. The initial consideration of Pounds 1.1m has been paid in cash. Further amounts not exceeding Pounds 1.4m may be payable depending on future profits.
