April 27, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

3 BASKETBALL PLAYERS ARRESTED

Three members of Northwestern State University's women's basketball team -- including the top scorer last season -- have been arrested and accused of burglaries in two parishes.

The three Lady Demons told authorities they "were just out drinking . . . and lost their minds," said Pleasant Hill Marshal Clyde Callens, who arrested two of the players.

Booked Thursday in DeSoto Parish on two counts of burglary were Iris Dixon, 20, a junior starting guard and the leading scorer; Tammy Blankenship, 19, a sophomore guard; and Tami Knotts, 18, a freshman point guard.

They also were charged with burglary in Sabine Parish.
