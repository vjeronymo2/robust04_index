DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Cooperative State Research Service Special Research Grants Program, Aquaculture Research; Solicitation of Applications; Correction AGENCY: Cooperative State Research Service, USDA. ACTION: Notice of correction of application submission deadline.

SUMMARY: In notice document 94&hyph;1730 in the issue of Thursday, January 27, 1994 (59 FR 3974), two different dates were given as the application submission deadline. This notice corrects all application submission deadline dates to read April 18, 1994 as follows: On page 3976 in the second column in the third paragraph, the regular mail postmarked date was specified as March 14, 1994. The date should be changed to April 18, 1994. On the same page and same column in the fourth paragraph, the hand-delivered proposal submission date was specified as March 14, 1994. The date should be changed to April 18, 1994. Dated: February 8, 1994. John Patrick Jordan, Administrator, Cooperative State Research Service.
