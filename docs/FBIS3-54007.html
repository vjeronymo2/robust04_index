Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Less than 36 hours before the convening of the national conference, a number of political parties that have weight in the political arena have announced that they would boycott the conference. Meanwhile some parties, mass and vocational organizations, have announced that they would participate. Others will remain in a state of anticipation and waiting until Monday morning before deciding on their final stance regarding participation, including the Islamic al-Nahdah Movement, whose leader Abdallah Djaballah said on 23 January that the official decision would be announced 24 January. [passage omitted] Among the parties that have declared their intention to participate are the National Democratic Socialist Party, which yesterday called for unity to ensure the success of the national conference and confirmed its intention to participate in it. [passage omitted] The Algerian Party for Justice and Progress yesterday confirmed its intention to participate in the national conference and called on all the political parties to ensure the success of the national consensus conference. At the end of its emergency session, the national council of the national organization of war veterans announced the organization's intention to participate in the national conference.
