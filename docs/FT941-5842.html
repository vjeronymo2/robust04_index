940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Seiko wristwatch keeps global time A quartz wristwatch which can tell the time in 28 selected cities is to go on sale in duty-free shops from April. Developed by Seiko in Japan, the watch incorporates a traditional clock face with a mini liquid crystal display. At the press of a button the Pounds 250 watch, which is designed for international travellers, scrolls through the different cities and the appropriate time there. Seiko: Japan, 3 3563 2111
