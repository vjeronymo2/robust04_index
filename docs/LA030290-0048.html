March 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ARTS

Send Resumes: Curator Franklin Kelly is leaving the Corcoran Gallery of Art in Washington, which has been the target of criticism over cancellation of a controversial photography exhibition last year, to return to the National Gallery of Art as research curator of American art. Kelly complained of increasingly heavy administrative duties at the Corcoran. The Corcoran is still searching for a successor to former director Christina Orr-Cahall, who resigned last year, and has two other top-level openings. BETH KLEID
