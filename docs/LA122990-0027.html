December 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DEALING WITH SOCIOPOLITICAL WORLD CHANGES

The prologue to "Shifting Realities in Global Power" by McManus and Wright (World Report, Dec. 11) was so riveting and so well written that I fully expected the last paragraph to read, "For answers to such questions, please turn to Page H2."

Would that the new world could be that simple.

REG JONES

Corona del Mar
