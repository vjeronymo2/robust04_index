941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / Stagecoach deal referred to MMC Stagecoach, the private bus operator, has been referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission for its purchase of a 20 per cent stake in SB Holdings, the former municipal bus operator in Stratchclyde which remains dominant in the region. The Department of Trade and Industry said it was acting on advice from the Office of Fair Trading, which had urged an MMC inquiry because of concerns about the impact of the acquisition on competition in Stratchclyde. The MMC reference is the latest in a series of investigations by the OFT and MMC into the competition implications of Stagecoach's ambitious expansion activities.
