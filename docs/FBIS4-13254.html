BFN [Text] Army has foiled yet another bid by Pakistani infiltrators to smuggle in a large quantity of arms and ammunition through the northern sector into the Kashmir state. A Defense Ministry spokesman told newsmen in Srinagar that this is the biggest cache of arms recovered so far this year. It includes 32,000 rounds of ammunition, 92 hand grenades, 203 antipersonnel mines and a large quantity of incriminating documents. A massive hunt has been launched to trace the arms couriers. Meanwhile, security forces launched a huge search operation this morning in the Batmalu area and adjoining localities to nab militants. Elsewhere in the state, 10 militants have been killed during the past 24 hours. Of these three were killed in intergang rivalry.
