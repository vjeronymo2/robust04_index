Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] As an ITAR-TASS correspondent was told by the head of the operational headquarters of the Russian Government, First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets, today, on behalf of the Russian Government, an ultimatum was issued to the terrorists at Mineralnyye Vody airport. The essence of the ultimatum boils down to the following: The hostages must be released immediately. The terrorists are instructed to surrender under a guarantee of personal safety. It has also been stated that their actions have given rise to a sharp protest among the Russian population.
