930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Boustead achieves 90% advance Boustead, the industrial products and technical services group acquired last year by Jack Chia-MPH, the Singapore-based trading company, lifted pre-tax profits by 90 per cent from Pounds 806,000 to Pounds 1.53m in the half year to June 30. There were losses of Pounds 1.6m for the 1992 year. The advance was on turnover of Pounds 33.1m (Pounds 39.8m). Earnings improved to 1.1p (0.2p) but there is no dividend (0.35p).
