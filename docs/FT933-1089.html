930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: United Energy United Energy, the small oil and gas company which operates mainly in the US, turned round from a loss of Pounds 52,000 to a profit of Pounds 40,000 in the first six months of this year. This followed the acquisition of AmBrit International early last year. Turnover rose from Pounds 834,000 to Pounds 1.58m, and production has grown from 107,000 barrels of oil equivalent to 145,000 barrels.
