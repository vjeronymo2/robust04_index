L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science (NSF announces the following meeting: Name: Special Emphasis Panel in Engineering Education and Centers. Date & Time: July 11&hyph;12, 1994; 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. each day. Place: NSF, Room 580, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Closed.
