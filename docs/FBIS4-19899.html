BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Ayhan Halit Acarkan, speaker of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus who is on an official visit in Ankara, and his accompanying delegation have been continuing their contacts. The guest speaker visited the leaders of the political parties at the parliament and exchanged views. Acarkan first visited the Republican People's Party leader Deniz Baykal. Later, he visited the Nationalist Action Party leader Alpaslan Turkes and communicated to him that the Turkish Cypriots supported continuing with the negotiations however, he added, the confidence-building measures were in favor of the Greek Cypriots. Turkes, in turn, said that the Cyprus issue is costing the Turkish nation, but notwithstanding the Turkish nation, nobody can give concessions on the Cyprus issue. Subsequently, Acarkan and his delegation visited Democratic Left Party leader Bulent Ecevit. Ecevit stated that it is incomprehensible that those who are not intervening in the cruelty in Bosnia-Herzegovina are intervening in the peace in Cyprus. Lastly, Acarkan and his delegation visited the Prosperity Party leader Necmettin Erbakan. Erbakan stated they do not recognize an issue such as Cyprus, that peace was established in the island in 1974, and that the issue was resolved then.
