Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Feb 28 (CTK) -- Czech Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec met with Speaker of the Polish Sejm (parliament) Jozef Oleksy on Monday [28 February] to discuss the questions of European security, integration into the European structures and the course of transformation in the Czech Republic and Poland. Oleksy has been on a three-day visit to the Czech Republic since Sunday [27 February]. The two politicians expressed satisfaction over good relations between Poland and the Czech Republic. Oleksy showed interest in the Czech Republic's efforts at integrating in Europe and in its opinion of the NATO Partnership for Peace initiative as well as on its relation to NATO and the European Union. Zieleniec voiced the view that countries attempting at joining these institutions should first become fully ready. The two countries' relations toward Russia and Ukraine and the possibility for these countries' participation in European structures and the future European security system were also discussed. Zieleniec and Oleksy also paid attention to the regional cooperation which embraces, along with cultural and economic relations, also cooperation in some political problems.
