BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 21 April. The Labor Ministry reported today that during 1993, unemployment insurance was provided to five times more individuals than during the previous year. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS 2048 GMT 19 Apr 94) The Association of Motor Vehicle Manufacturers, ADEFA, reported today that the production of motor vehicles during March 1994 totalled 33,758 units, a 300 percent increase with respect to February of this year and a 30 percent increase with respect to March 1993. ADEFA also reported that total production during the first quarter of 1994 was 34 percent higher than during the first quarter of 1993, and that the total number of units exported during the period increased by 121 percent with respect to the same period of last year. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 2315 GMT 14 Apr 94)
