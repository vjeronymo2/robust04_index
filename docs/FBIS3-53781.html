Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A Foreign Office spokesman said in Islamabad today that Pakistan attaches highest priority to providing humanitarian assistance to displaced people in Afghanistan due to continued fighting. Pakistan is making comprehensive arrangements to provide them adequate relief in collaboration with United Nations and other relief organizations. The spokesman said the Pakistan-Afghan border has never been sealed since the establishment of the mojahedin government in Afghanistan. He said according to the latest information, fighting in Kabul has been continuing with low intensity due to inclement weather. The situation in Mazar-e Sharif is, however, peaceful.
