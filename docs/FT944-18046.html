941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: UniChem buys shops UniChem has strengthened E Moss, its high street chemists chain, with the acquisition of six retail pharmacies in the Norwich area from Reads Pharmacy. The consideration, up to a maximum of Pounds 2.48m plus stock at valuation, will be satisfied in cash. The deal will bring E Moss's total number of outlets to 309. The Reads pharmacies produced turnover of Pounds 3.42m in the 12 months to October 1993.
