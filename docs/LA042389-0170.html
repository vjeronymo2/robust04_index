April 23, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

PANORAMA CITY MAN DIES AFTER STABBING

A 23-year old Panorama City man was stabbed to death early Saturday morning at an apartment in the 14700 block of Titus Street.

Officials said Darren Brown died at Panorama City Hospital shortly after his fight with a teen-age boy. They said they did not know what caused the dispute.
