Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] conferred last night with Otto Stich, president of the Swiss Confederation. The brother president reviewed with Mr. Stich the latest developments in the Middle East region as well as development in the Palestinian problem, particularly those dealing with the peace process in the Middle East and the need to implement the Declaration of Principles in addition to bilateral relations.
