BFN ["I.R." report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Glazyev on the Economic Situation"] [Text] Speaking in his yesterday's press conference, Sergey Glazyev, Duma Economic Policy Committee chairman, stated that his own prognoses and those of his colleagues made in March have been confirmed by the findings of the Economic Conditions Center under the Russian Federation Government: The production slump in 1994 will be around 25 percent, that is to say, it will become "precipitous." Besides, "there are no special signs of recovery in sight that could indicate that this is a turning point," Mr. Glazyev stressed. He presented "State's Economic Policy Guidelines" developed by a group of economists. Their main idea is to bring order into all the sectors of the economy and in the system of its control, for which purpose specific measures are proposed. Remarkably, in the expression of Mr. Glazyev himself, measures which can indeed be implemented have been specially selected from a multitude of suggestions.
