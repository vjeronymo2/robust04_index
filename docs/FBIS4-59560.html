BFN [Excerpt] A Syrian ministerial economic delegation arrived in Amman via the al-Ramtha border post today. The delegation includes Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, the minister of economy and foreign trade; Dr. Ahmad Nizam-al-Din, the minister of industry; and Muhammad al-Sharif, the Syrian Central Bank governor; as well as several specialists. The delegation will represent Syria at the meetings of the joint Jordanian-Syrian ministerial committee due to open in Amman this evening. [passage omitted]
