940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / New Zealand balances books The New Zealand government expects to balance its books in the financial year to March 31, Minister of Finance Bill Birch said yesterday, writes Terry Hall from Wellington. Last October, the government forecast a deficit of NZDollars 1.8bn (Pounds 686m) this year, and balance by 1996. However, tax revenues were running NZDollars 800m higher than expected, Mr Birch said, while government spending was NZDollars 500m less than forecast. He predicted a surplus of NZDollars 300m next year.
