BFN [Text] Kyrgyz President Askar Akayev has issued a decree on the state commission for reorganization and liquidation of insolvent enterprises. This was announced to INTERFAX by the Kyrgyz president's press service. The same source told INTERFAX that this had been done in order to eliminate broke enterprises, to make the state budget healthy, to achieve financial stabilization in Kyrgyzstan and to carry out structural reorganization. The commission will be headed by Kyrgyz Premier Apas Jumagulov and will include the chairperson of the national bank, the finance minister and others. The commission will focus on implementation of programs concerning unprofitable enterprises. The presidential decree must be implemented within a month.
