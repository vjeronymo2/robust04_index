930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Emess static at Pounds 1m Pre-tax profits at Emess, the lighting and electrical accessories group, remained static at Pounds 1.1m for the half year ended June 30. However, the comparative figure included a Pounds 1.3m contribution from discontinued operations. Turnover of continuing operations edged ahead from Pounds 59.1m to Pounds 59.5m for trading profits of Pounds 2.1m (Pounds 1.7m). Interest costs were Pounds 1m (Pounds 1.9m). Fully diluted earnings emerged at 0.5p (0.6p).
