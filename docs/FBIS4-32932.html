BFN [From the "Morning Information Review" newscast] [Text] Cuba will work for unity and strengthening the international prestige of the Nonaligned Movement [NAM], Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina said in Cairo. He will participate in the meeting of NAM foreign ministers beginning on Tuesday. Shortly after arriving at the Egyptian capital, Robaina said that not only will positions be established at the meeting, but decisions must be made on NAM actions with regard to the growing imbalance in international relations, a unipolar world, and the concentration of economic power in northern countries.
