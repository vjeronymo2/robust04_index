931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: FT-SE Actuaries seminar The FT-SE Actuaries Classification Committee is holding a seminar for professional investors to discuss the recent changes in the classification of UK equities. The seminar will be held at the Financial Times on December 7. Admission is free. Those interested in attending are asked to contact Liz Leech of the FT on 071 873 3229 or by fax on 071 873 4610.
