January 10, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A 29-year-old Linda Vista resident was shot to death in a pool hall Tuesday afternoon, San Diego police said.

Tho Van Truong was found shot in the head in the 6900 block of Linda Vista Road at 3:57 p.m. He was taken to Sharp Memorial Hospital where he died at 4:30 p.m., according to the San Diego County coroner's office.

Police are seeking a 5-foot-8, 170-pound Asian man, about 25 to 30 years old, who was wearing a charcoal gray sweater with a cream-colored design and dark pants.
