BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- North Korean Foreign Minister Kim Yong- nam stopped over in Berlin on his flight from Kiev to Pyongyang. On Friday [10 June], the Foreign Ministry in Bonn said the minister arrived on Thursday night and would continue his trip on Friday night. He had not asked for any talks and there were no plans for talks either. It was said that it was a transit stop for "technical" reasons. The North Korean had visited the Ukrainian capital before. He was actually scheduled to go to one of the central Asian republics but changed his plans at short notice. His flight back to Pyongyang went through Berlin.
