BFN [Text] Baghdad, 31 Mar (INA)--'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, left Baghdad en route to Moscow today at the head of an Arab Socialist Ba'th Party delegation to attend meetings of the conference of the Russian Liberal Democrat Party [LDP] that is due to convene there next week. In a statement to INA, 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur said that the party delegation will hold talks with Russian parties and political forces to promote ties between the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and these parties and forces. The party delegation will also explain to these Russian parties and forces the implications and dangers of the unfair anti-Iraq embargo that continues due to the wishes of the United States and Zionism, although Iraq has fulfilled its commitments per relevant UN Security Council resolutions, including Resolution 687. 'Abd-al-Ghafur added that the improved positions of a few UN Security Council permanent members, including Russia, demonstrate the need to implement Paragraph 22 of UN Security Council Resolution 687, and immediately lift the unfair embargo clamped on Iraq.
