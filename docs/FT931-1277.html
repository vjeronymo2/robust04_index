930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / 20,000 support Yeltsin's call for referendum in mass rally A naval officer and army officer hoist an imperial Czarist flag atop the Winter Palace column in St Petersburg when 20,000 people joined a mass rally to show their support for Boris Yeltsin in his call for a national referendum. Earlier in the week the president, as commander-in-chief, had ordered the armed forces to stay out of politics
