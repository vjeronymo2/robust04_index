940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): International - Other companies Two important Japanese companies report next week. Sony, the big consumer electronics group, is expected to show gains from the weaker yen plus restructuring efforts when it unveils third-quarter results on Thursday. On Wednesday Sumitomo Chemical - struggling to overcome problems related to the sector's overcapacity - announces results for the full year. In Paris, the Euro Disney banks hope this week to receive the final version of an audit of the troubled leisure group's finances from KPMG Peat Marwick.
