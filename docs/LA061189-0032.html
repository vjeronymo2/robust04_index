June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CONGRESS AND ETHICS

Neither the media nor the Republican Party are responsible for "Donkeygate." Rather Wright and Coelho and all of the other Democrats who have shed crocodile tears over the so-called sleaze of the past few years, while wearing their mantle of pristine virtue and their holier-than-thou coronet, are solely responsible.

The media should be proud, as should all citizens, that the First Amendment is not only intact but works. Keep it up and continue the bipartisan investigations.

STUART von RATHJEN

Los Angeles
