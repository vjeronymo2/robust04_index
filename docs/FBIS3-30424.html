Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Zagreb GLOBUS in Serbo-Croatian on 28 January on pages 2 and 9 carries an approximately 3,500-word report on the results of GLOBUS' "big" poll on the popularity of Croatian politicians and political parties. The report, entitled "Although More Than 90 Percent of Croats Are Dissatisfied, President Tudjman's Popularity Is Rising," does not contain any information on how the poll was conducted. The only information concerning the size of the sample is that "2,120 citizens were approached" and that "more than 1,100 did not refuse to answer the questions." The results of the poll's question on the popularity of Croatian President Franjo Tudjman and Drazen Budisa, leader of the main opposition party, the Croatian Social Liberal Party (HSLS), are
