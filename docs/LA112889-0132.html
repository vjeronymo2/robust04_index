November 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Horse racing -- Horlicks, the New Zealand mare who won the Japan Cup Sunday, set a world record for 2,400 meters with her time of 2 minutes 22 1/5 seconds, not for 1 1/2 miles as was reported in Monday's editions. The distance she ran is about 15 yards short of 1 1/2 miles.
