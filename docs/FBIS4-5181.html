BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- A national memorial service took place here Tuesday on the tenth anniversary of the death of O Paek-yong, a communist revolutionary fighter who was intensely loyal to the great leader President Kim Il-song, (on April 6, 1984). O Paek-yong was a steadfast communist revolutionary fighter who fought all his life for the freedom and independence of the country and the accomplishment of the cause of socialism and communism and a courageous and talented military commanding officer who performed great feats in the revolutionary wars against U.S. and Japanese imperialism. Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chief of the general staff of the Korean People's Army, in his memorial address referred to the brilliant life of O Paek-yong and precious feats he performed in struggle. O was born into a poor peasant's family in Hoeryong county, North Hamgyong Province, on October 24, 1914, he joined in the revolutionary struggle in the 1930 and firmly defended the great leader politically and ideologically and with his life as an ordinary soldier, then a platoon leader, a company commander and a regimental commander in the whole period of the anti-Japanese armed struggle. After the liberation of the country he worked hard to strengthen the People's Armed Forces and defend the gains of revolution from the aggression of the enemies, upholding President Kim Il-song's line of Army building and, during the fatherland liberation war, made a great contribution to repelling the invasion of the U.S. imperialists and defending the independence of the country as a divisional commander of the Korean People's Army. Later, holding important posts of the Ministry of Interior, the Ministry of National Defence, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central People's Committee and the National Defence Commission, he performed big feats in defending the headquarters of the Korean revolution and implementing the party's military line of self-reliant defense. In the memorial address Choe Kwang said the life of O Paek-yong was a brilliant life of a communist revolutionary who dedicated his all to the party and the leader, the country and the people and the accomplishment of the cause of socialism and communism, and the intense loyalty to the party and the leader, a high degree of revolutionary spirit and devoted services for and modesty toward the people which ran through his whole life serve as a valuable example for all the party members and working people. Attending the memorial service were O Chin-u, member of presidium of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and minister of People's Armed Forces, Yi Chong-ok and Pak Song-chol, Politburo members of the WPK Central Committee and vice-president, Kim Pyong-sik, chairman of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Korean Social Democratic Party and vice-president, and other senior party and government officials and anti-Japanese revolutionary fighters. Papers here today dedicate articles to the occasion.
