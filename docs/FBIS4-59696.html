BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahya had talks today with Mr. Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, Jordanian minister of state for foreign affairs. [passage omitted] Following the meeting, Mr. Talal Sat'an al-Hasan made a statement to Tunis-Afrique Press Agency in which he pointed out that his visit to Tunisia had been within the framework of consultations and coordination between the two countries to bolster their bilateral cooperation in all spheres. The Jordanian foreign minister added that the talks had dealt with a number of political issues, particularly those revolving around Jordan's efforts to give momentum to the Middle East peace process to allow the Palestinian people to exercise their legitime rights. He indicated, furthermore, that the two sides had examined the means for bolstering political coordination between the two countries and for finding the suitable mechanism for making such coordiantion more successful and more effective and achieving the best possible results out of it.
