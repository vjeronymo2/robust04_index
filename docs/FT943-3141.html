940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Capital Inds up slightly at Pounds 2.5m Pre-tax profits at Capital Industries, the financial services and packaging materials group, edged ahead from Pounds 2.41m to Pounds 2.51m in the first half of 1994, a period of consolidation, said Mr David Rhead, the chairman. The industrial side contributed operating profits of Pounds 2.62m (Pounds 2.52m) on turnover of Pounds 35.8m (Pounds 32.4m) while financial services contributed a static Pounds 282,000 on sales of Pounds 2.8m (Pounds 2.23m). Earnings per share improved from 6.9p to 7.2p and the dividend is lifted to 2.1p (2p).
