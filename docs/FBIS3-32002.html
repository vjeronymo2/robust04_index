Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The General Command of the Haitian Armed Forces [FADH] denounces the motive behind the 26 January statements by by the U.S. Embassy spokesman in Haiti, which announce the cancellation, over the next few days, of officers' visas. This calculated measure is shameful blackmail aimed at destabilizing another national Haitian institution and taking it hostage, the General Headquarters press release adds. The communique issued by FADH General Headquarters stresses that the ideal of the Haitian military, nourished by the lessons of its national history, is concerned with duty to country, thus enablling the unified military institution to continue defending the ideals of freedom, sovereignty, and greatness, which have always roused the Haitian people.
