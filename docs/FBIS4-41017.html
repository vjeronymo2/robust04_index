BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has addressed Russian President Boris Yeltsin, proposing to set up a Russian-Ukrainian oil company as a transnational corporation. The proposal was made after Kravchuk's visit to Lugansk's Lisichansk Petroleum Organic Synthesis joint stock company. He believes that setting up a transnational corporation would be another step along the path of strengthening friendship and developing cooperation between Ukraine and the Russian Federation. The president of Ukraine familiarized himself with the state of affairs at the joint stock company. At a meeting with the enterprise's leadership, work force, and specialists, the prospects for the development of the oil processing sector and ways of curbing its economic crisis were discussed. On the same day, Kravchuk met representatives of labor collectives, local authorities, parties, and movements from the towns of Lisichansk, Rubezhnoye, Pervomaysk, as well as (?Kalininskiy) and some other rayons of Lugansk Oblast. He described the economic situation in Ukraine, spoke about his vision for resolving current problems, and answered numerous questions from the electorate. Participants in the meeting expressed themselves in support of the president's course and praised his plans of further nation building.
