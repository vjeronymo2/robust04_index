920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / International Company News: Bank of Melbourne changes share issue BANK of Melbourne said it will make a one-for-four rights issue instead of the convertible preference share issue it announced on January 20, Reuter reports from Melbourne. The bank said it believed a rights issue would be in the better interests of shareholders after the Australian Tax Office cast doubts on the franking of preference share dividends. The bank said a prospectus was expected to be released in early March. Bank of Melbourne shares closed 4 cents firmer at ADollars 4.35. The bank has 79.27m shares on issue.
