July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DATE, SITE SET FOR LEONARD-DURAN III

Sugar Ray Leonard and Roberto Duran will fight outdoors Dec. 7 at the Mirage Hotel in Las Vegas for Leonard's World Boxing Council super-middleweight title, Top Rank promoter Bob Arum announced Friday.

The third Leonard-Duran bout was announced earlier in the week, but the date and location were undetermined.

"The Mirage people more than satisfied my technical people," Arum said. "The fight will take place in a tent-like structure specially insulated from the wind."

Leonard, 33, ended his retirement last Nov. 7 by stopping Donny Lalonde for the WBC middleweight and super-middleweight titles. Leonard relinquished the middleweight title now held by Duran, 38.
