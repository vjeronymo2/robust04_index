Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Enrique Correa Rios, Chilean minister secretary general of government, today in Santiago, Chile, announced that a special line of credit for Cuba was approved today by the National Bank of Chile. The line of credit, in the amount of 17 million German marks, approximately $10 million, will be used to purchase food. Correa said he believes this operation will benefit Chilean producers. Correa also stressed President Patricio Aylwin's participation in the approval of the credit given Cuba.
