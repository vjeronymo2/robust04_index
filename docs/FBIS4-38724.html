BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The end of check privatization will give the government the opportunity to depart fully from the present economically fruitless tactic of denationalization. At a recent meeting between [Prime Minister] Viktor Chernomyrdin and the heads of regional administrations, it was said that the government intends to share responsibility for changing the tactics of reform with regions which have not yet even defined their attitude to it.
