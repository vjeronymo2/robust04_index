August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

SURELOCK, apparently still suffering from an electronic letdown after Thursday's big day, lost $40 Saturday on his exactas on Navy Flag (fourth) and Michael's Flyer (second) in the seventh race.

The computer's bets for today are a $20 exacta box, High Rank-Agiwin, in the fifth race and $40 to win and place on Annual Reunion in the eighth.

SURELOCK's Sat. Bankroll: $3,315

Saturday's Loss: $40

Current Bankroll: $3,275

Janice Goldinger made a $32.50 profit for the day when short-odds favorite Simply Fabulous ($4.40) won the fourth race. Janice lost the first two races of her innovative four-race place parlay.

Janice's bet for today is a $25 exacta box, Big Conviction-Blaze O'Brien, in the seventh race.

Janice's Sat. Bankroll: $1626.75

Saturday's Profit: $32.50

Current Bankroll: $1,659.25

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.
