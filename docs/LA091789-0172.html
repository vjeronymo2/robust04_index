September 17, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

RAIN WASHES OUT RACING PROGRAM AT SAUGUS

Saturday night's racing program at Saugus Speedway was postponed until later this week because of rain.

Track spokesman Lynn Pherigo said that racing will resume Saturday with the addition of Sportsman and Street Stock division races to the regularly scheduled Hobby Stock division program.

Drivers in all divisions will be competing in their final points races of the season.
