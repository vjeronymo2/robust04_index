Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 13 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, extended thanks to the officials of the Yongsong Trading Corporation, Sinjin Joint Company and Namhae Trading Corporation who participated in the manufacture of latest-type comprehensive broadcasting facilities, and saw to it that the facilities were sent to People's Army units. Those who received thanks have made some 200 sets of comprehensive broadcasting equipment with an amplifier, a recorder, a mike, a sound conditioning device and a loudspeaker which would be conducive to the politico-ideological education and cultural and emotional life of the People's Army soldiers.
