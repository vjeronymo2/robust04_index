BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- The 12th April spring friendship art festival which opened with splendor here on April 9 closed today. Present at the closing ceremony held at the February 8 House of Culture were Kang Song-san, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and premier of the Administration Council, officials concerned and working people and men of culture and art in the city. Also present were foreign artistes, Korean artistes overseas and foreign diplomatic envoys and embassy officials here. Certificates of merit and prizes of the festival were awarded to art troupes and artistes that proved successful in the festival. Letters of thanks to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the ceremony on behalf of the participants in the festival. Chang-Chol, vice-premier of the Administration Council, minister of culture and art and chairman of the organizing committee of the festival, addressed the closing ceremony. He said that through the festival, the participants had made a sizable contribution to strengthening friendship and solidarity among countries and nations and making their cultural cooperation and ties closer in accordance with the noble idea of independence, peace and friendship and enriching the treasurehouse of culture and art of mankind. "During the festival, artistes from different countries presented many works revering the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to delight the Korean people and make the April holiday more significant and auspicious," he said. The ceremony was followed by a closing performance of the festival.
