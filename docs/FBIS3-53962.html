Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres today addressed members of the Labor Party Ef'al circle. Mr. Peres stressed that no foreign army will cross the Jordan River, Jerusalem will not be divided, and the settlements in Judaea and Samaria will not be uprooted. Mr. Peres added, however, that peace with Syria cannot be attained without Israeli concessions. Our party affairs correspondent reports Mr. Peres added that the Palestinians have nowhere to retreat to, whereas Israel is in no hurry and can shift the emphasis to the negotiations on Syria.
