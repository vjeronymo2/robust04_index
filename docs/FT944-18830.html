941001 FT 01 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares posted steady gains as currency factors and window-dressing on the last day of the quarter pulled stocks higher. The overall index finished 40 firmer at 5,676, industrials were 27 better at 6,294 and golds collected 14 to 2,441. De Beers rose R1.75 to R101.75.
