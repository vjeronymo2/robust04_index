BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 May (MENA)--IMF Executive Director Mr. Charles Enoch and the delegation accompanying him arrived in Cairo this evening on a visit of several days to Egypt for talks with officials of the Central Bank and the Economy and International Cooperation Ministry. The talks will deal with the stages of implementing the economic reform program. These talks are considered a continuation of the contacts between Egypt and the IMF and World Bank regarding the requirements for this program. An Egyptian economic delegation led by Dr. Kamal al-Janzuri, deputy prime minister and minister of planning, recently held talks in Washington with World Bank officials on this issue. The bank officials endorsed the economic reform program Egypt is implementing.
