920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / Rethink on energy policy urged THE REMOVAL of distortions in the energy market could cut emissions of carbon dioxide in the UK by 40 per cent and put money back into the economy, says a study published today by environmental pressure group Friends of the Earth. The report says failure to take action would mean market distortions continuing to misdirect Pounds 2bn a year on investment in uneconomic and carbon-intensive technologies. Friends of the Earth's proposals include linking mortgage tax relief to energy efficiency in the home and a carbon tax. Efficiency Without Tears. Friends of the Earth, 26-28 Underwood St, London N1. Pounds 10.
