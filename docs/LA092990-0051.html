September 29, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

WEIR LEADS CORONA DEL MAR WATER POLO

Ryan Weir scored six goals to lead top-ranked Corona del Mar High School to a 9-5 victory over Tustin in a South Coast water polo tournament quarterfinal game Friday at Tustin.

In other South Coast tournament games:

Costa Mesa 12, University 8 -- Julian Popov scored six goals and Corey de la Hunt scored four to lead Costa Mesa (6-5) at Irvine.

Marina 7, Long Beach Poly 6 (OT) -- Steve Fontes scored with 1:13 left in overtime in a man-advantage situation to lead Marina (5-4). Eric Goodwin had 12 saves for Marina.

In the Magnolia water polo tournament:

Mater Dei 11, Loara 1 -- Phil Mayer and Darien Osborne each had four goals to lead Mater Dei (4-6) at Savanna.

Brea-Olinda 11, Cerritos 7 -- Steve Krebs scored four goals, Kyle Kilpatrick scored three and Brian Noyes scored two to lead Brea-Olinda (11-2) into today's round-robin play.

Brea-Olinda 13, Kennedy 7 -- Bryan Noyes had three goals and Kyle Kilpatrick and Steve Krebs each added two for Brea-Olinda.
