Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Azerbaijani Defense Minister Mamedrafi Mamedov has stated that the Armenian Army started to attack on all fronts but that it was forced to withdraw as a result of the large number of soldiers wounded and killed following the Azerbaijan national army's resistance. Mamedov was interviewed by Ismail Karadavud, TRT correspondent in Baku. [Begin Karadavud recording] In a statement he issued to TRT, Mamedov pointed out that the Armenians have not been able to advance during the last month, adding that approximately 200 to 300 Armenian soldiers were being killed in clashes every day. Mamedov reported that 19 Armenian tanks and a large number of military vehicles have been destroyed since the beginning of the new year. Mamedov further reported that approximately 100 Azeri soldiers were killed and 50 wounded in clashes between the Azerbaijani national army and Armenian units over the last month. Pointing out that both the Azeri people and their army will defend their territory until the last drop of their blood, Mamedov noted that the people of Kelbadzhar have started returning after the Armenians evacuated the area. Mamedov stated that a large number of mercenaries from various nations are fighting in the Armenian Army, announcing that one of those mercenaries was being held by the Azerbaijani Army. [end recording]
