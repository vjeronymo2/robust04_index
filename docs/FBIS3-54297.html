Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Afghanistan's foreign minister and the minister of state for foreign affairs, who are in Tehran to attend the Economic Cooperation Organization session, met with the Foreign Minister Mr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati this evening. During this meeting the Afghan foreign minister presented a report on the situation in his country and conveyed greetings and gratitude from President Borhanoddin Rabbani for the stances adopted by the Islamic republic of Iran [IRI] and its efforts to resolve the dispute and to restore peace in Afghanistan. Mr. Velayati, while expressing concern about the situation in Afghanistan, said: Unfortunately the power of the Afghan nation, instead of being utilized to rebuild and develop the country, is engaged in a conflict that has caused many losses and casualties. Mr. Velayati also declared the IRI's readiness to undertake any effort to restore peace and tranquility in Afghanistan and stressed the need for comprehensive and all-embracing solutions acceptable to all of the mujahedin groups.
