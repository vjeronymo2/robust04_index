Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by BELINFORM-TASS] [Text] Minsk 21 March, TASS -- Belarus will receive loans worth $150 to $160 million thanks to a memorandum, signed by chairman of the Belarus Government Vyacheslav Kebich and head of the World Bank mission Wafik Grace. The loans will be granted for a period of 15 years with the postponement of the first payment. The loans to be received as a result of the signing of the memorandum will be spent on the implementation of projects in the spheres of forestry, agriculture, power engineering, transport and communal economy. Private enterprise and the utilisation of harmful industrial waste will be further promoted. The World Bank has already granted two loans to Belarus -- some 17 million dollars for reforming management authorities and $120 million for the import of vitally necessary goods and materials.
