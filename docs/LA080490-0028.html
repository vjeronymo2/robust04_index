August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ROSEANNE BARR'S RENDITION OF 'THE STAR-SPANGLED BANNER'

Barr failed to realize that her fans -- who eat "ketchup on white bread" -- become angry when anyone insults our flag or national anthem. Like Gabor, Trump and Hart, Barr suffers from the fatal flaw of hubris.

MARY E. HANSON

Whittier
