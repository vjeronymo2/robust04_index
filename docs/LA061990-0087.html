June 19, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Martin Lawrence Limited Editions promoted Barry R. Levine to president and created an office of the chairman that includes Chairman Martin S. Blinder, Levine and Chief Financial Officer Allen A. Baron.

Levine previously was executive vice president of the Van Nuys-based operator of art galleries.

In addition, Martin Lawrence Limited Editions promoted Rick Barry to vice president of wholesale sales. He previously was the company's director of wholesale sales.
