Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Feb (MENA)--An Interior Ministry security source has stated that while Nadi Raja'i Ahmad, a State Security detective with the rank of warrant officer, was returning home in Asyut at 2020 today, he glimpsed an individual at the end of the street leading to his house. He ordered him to halt but the latter fired a shot and fled. The warrant officer gave chase; exchanged fire with the suspect, hitting him in his left arm; arrested him, and seized his weapon. The security source said that after the suspect was put through an identification process, it was discovered that he is 'Atif 'Ali Farghali Ahmad, recently released from custody and formerly indicted in connection with several terrorist operations. He was transferred to the hospital for treatment and placed under armed guard. Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi ordered a £500 reward for the warrant officer for his prompt use of his personal weapon to arrest the terrorist.
