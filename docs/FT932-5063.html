930605 FT 05 JUN 93 / Finance and the Family: What you will pay for your deals THE FOLLOWING is a guide to the dealing rates charged by some of the major banks and execution-only brokers. Barclays bank offers a dealing service in 50 popular shares, available from any branch. The commission rate is 2 per cent with a minimum of Pounds 15. The bank's certificated share dealing service charges 1.65 per cent on the first Pounds 10,000, with a minimum of Pounds 28; 1.125 per cent on the next Pounds 10,000; 0.5 per cent on the following Pounds 20,000; 0.4 per cent on the next Pounds 60,000; and 0.3 per cent thereafter. Midland bank charges 1.5 per cent on deals worth up to Pounds 7,000, with a minimum of Pounds 20; 0.55 per cent on the next Pounds 8,000; 0.5 per cent on the following Pounds 115,000; 0.4 per cent on the next Pounds 170,000; and 0.3 per cent on the following Pounds 600,000. At Midland Shareshops, available at 128 locations, the maximum commission is Pounds 150. National Westminster bank has a special touchscreen service at 279 of its branches. The commission is 1.5 per cent on purchases between Pounds 1 and Pounds 9,999, with a minimum of Pounds 20. At all other branches, rates are 1.65 per cent on deals between Pounds 1 and Pounds 5,000, with a minimum of Pounds 25; 1.5 per cent on deals between Pounds 5,001 and Pounds 12,500; 0.5 per cent on Pounds 12,501 to Pounds 25,000; and 0.4 per cent on Pounds 25,001-Pounds 100,000. There is a postal service at a 1 per cent rate (with a minimum of Pounds 9.95). A telephone dealing service starts at 1.5 per cent (1.25 per cent for Gold Card-holders) on deals between Pounds 1 and Pounds 5,000. Sharelink (021-200-2474) charges 1.5 per cent on deals worth up to Pounds 2,500, with a a minimum of Pounds 20. Deals worth more than Pounds 2,500 face commission of 1.5 per cent on the first Pounds 2,500; 0.75 per cent on the next Pounds 2,500; and 0.1 per cent on amounts above that. Fidelity (0800-222 190) charges a minimum commission of Pounds 25 on deals worth less than Pounds 2,500. Above that level, dealing charges are: Pounds 2,501-Pounds 5,000 (Pounds 50); Pounds 5,001-Pounds 7,500 (Pounds 60); Pounds 7,501-Pounds 10,000 (Pounds 65); Pounds 10,001-Pounds 15,000 (Pounds 70); Pounds 15,001-Pounds 20,000 (Pounds 75); Pounds 20,001-Pounds 30,000 (Pounds 85); Pounds 30,001-Pounds 40,000 (Pounds 95); above Pounds 40,000 (0.25 per cent, with a maximum of Pounds 250). US shares. US discount broker Charles Schwab (071-495 7444) opened recently for business in London and charges an over-riding minimum of Dollars 39 for dealing in US shares. Based on transaction sizes, commission rates are: up to Dollars 2,500 (Dollars 30 plus 1.7 per cent of principal); Dollars 2,500-Dollars 6,250 (Dollars 56 plus 0.66 per cent); Dollars 6,250 - Dollars 20,000 (Dollars 76 plus 0.34 per cent); Dollars 20,000-Dollars 50,000 (Dollars 100 plus 0.22 per cent); Dollars 50,000-Dollars 500,000 (Dollars 155 plus 0.11 per cent); and Dollars 500,000-plus (Dollars 255 plus 0.09 per cent). These figures are subject to minima of 9 cents a share for the first 1,000 shares, plus 4 cents a share therafter (for stocks trading below Dollars 5 a share) or 5 cents (for stocks above Dollars 5). Fidelity deals in US securities at rates which vary according to the following scale: deals worth less than Dollars 2,500 (Dollars 29.50 plus 1.7 per cent of principal); Dollars 2,501-Dollars 6,000 (Dollars 55.50 plus 0.66 per cent); Dollars 6,001-Dollars 22,000 (Dollars 75.50 plus 0.34 per cent); Dollars 22,001-Dollars 50,000 (Dollars 99.50 plus 0.22 per cent); Dollars 50,001-Dollars 500,000 (Dollars 154.50 plus 0.11 per cent); and Dollars 500,001 plus (Dollars 254.50 plus 0.09 per cent). Sharelink also trades in US shares through its Liberty service. The minimum commission is Dollars 38 on deals worth up to Dollars 2,533. Deals valued at between Dollars 2,534 and Dollars 5,000 cost 1.5 per cent, with a further charge of 0.75 per cent on the next Dollars 5,000 of value and 0.19 per cent on amounts over Dollars 10,000. There is an over-riding minimum charge of 2.5 cents for every share bought or sold.
