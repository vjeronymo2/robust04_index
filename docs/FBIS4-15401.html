BFN [Text] The Russian Prosecutor General's Office has started criminal proceedings against Former Security Minister Viktor Barannikov and Former First Deputy Interior Minister Andrey Dunayev, suspected of taking bribes and abusing their authority, and against their wives, suspected of contraband. The proceedings were started on April 5 by Deputy Prosecutor General Vladimir Kravtsev after he saw records which the Moscow City Prosecutor's Office earlier dismissed as giving no grounds for criminal action.
