Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] A Foreign Affairs Ministry statement said today that a constructive fraternal atmosphere prevailed during this meeting in which Mr. Dembri conveyed the greetings of the head of state, Lamine Zeroual, to his brother Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. Al-Qadhdhafi in turn charged Dembri with carrying an appreciation and gratitude message to President Lamine Zeroual in which he concentrated on the strength of the cooperation and solidarity relations which link the two fraternal countries. The statement also added that the ways and means of consolidating cooperation between Algeria and Libya, in addition to other issues, particularly the march of the great Arab Maghreb, were discussed during the meeting. The statement also pointed out that during this meeting points of view on the Aouzou Strip were exchanged within the context of good neighbourliness and cooperation.
