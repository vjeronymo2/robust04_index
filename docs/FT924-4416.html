921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: ACT Pounds 8.85m placing for NMW purchase ACT Group yesterday placed 6,495,240 new 10p ordinary shares at 137p per share to raise about Pounds 8.85m cash, net of expenses. Net proceeds from the placing will be used to finance in part the acquisition of NMW Computers which was declared unconditional on Monday, and to retire NMW's bank indebtedness. Application will made for the new shares to be admitted to the Official List. It is expected that dealings in the shares will commence on December 8.
