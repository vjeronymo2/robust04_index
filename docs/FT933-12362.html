930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Sharelink offer for sale subscribed 7.9 times THE PUBLIC offer for sale of shares in Sharelink Investment Services, the telephone-based stockbroker, was 7.9 times subscribed and smaller applications will go to a ballot for shares. It is the second ballot this month, following the flotation of Field Group, the carton maker, which was the first for several years to adopt the procedure. The company offered 3.95m shares to the public after placing the same number with institutions. Sharelink received applications for 31.4m shares in the public offer. The issue was priced at 250p and dealings are due to start on July 30. Applications for 100 shares will go into a ballot for 100 shares and applications for 200 shares will go into a ballot for 150 shares. Applications for between 300 and 2,000 shares will go into a weighted ballot for 50 per cent of each application while applications for between 2,500 and 10,000 will go into a weighted ballot for 1,000 shares. Applications for 15,000 shares or more will be allocated 1,000 shares. Lex, Page 24
