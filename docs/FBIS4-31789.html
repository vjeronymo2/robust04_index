BFN [Text] Prague June 10 (CTK) -- Premier Vaclav Klaus said he was surprised by the resignation of National Property Fund (FNM) Chairman Tomas Jezek and the appointment of Deputy Privatisation Minister Roman Ceska as his successor, Klaus's advisor Vadim Petrov told CTK today. The premier wonders whether the change was not overly hasty and whether the new chairman will be able to carry out much-needed improvements in the activities of the fund. He told Nova Television today that he had not been informed beforehand of Jezek's intention, but got the news from CTK.
