BFN [Text] Russia still insists that before imposing sanctions against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea [DPRK], an international conference on resolving the nuclear crisis on the Korean peninsula should be convened. Russian Vice Foreign Minister Panov reiterated this view in a meeting with the Chinese ambassador in Moscow on Thursday [9 June]. He had a similar talk with the DPRK ambassador earlier. The idea of calling an international conference was proposed by President Yeltsin, who maintains that North Korea, South Korea, Russia, the U.S., Japan, and China should participate in the conference.
