BFN [Excerpt] Peace talks between the government and rebel delegations resumed today. A morning morning consultative session was held between the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development ministerial mediation committee and one representative from each of the three delegations attending the negotiations. The SUNA correspondent in Nairobi reports that each of the delegations has put forward a working paper to the meeting containing its views on actions to be taken by the committee to reach a peaceful solution.
