Language: Somali Article Type:BFN [Text] Addressing local and foreign journalists, Professor Issa Mohamed Siad, the United Somali Congress foreign affairs secretary, today said the Somali National Alliance [SNA] supporters were not responsible for anything that could bring about insecurity and problems. Mr. Issa Mohamed Siad said the individuals who always told about the impending civil war after the foreign troops departure from Somalia were behind the explosion that occurred at the Mogadishu port. He said the SNA officials and supporters wished the American troops and others a safe departure from the country. Elmi Osman Farah, our reporter who filed this report, also adds that Prof. Mohamed Siad called on the SNA supporters to be vigilant against such a conspiracy.
