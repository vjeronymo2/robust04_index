July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

FRANKLIN WEST TOWERS TO BECOME CONDO UNITS

The Franklin West Towers at 7250 Franklin Ave. in Hollywood has been sold for $22.75 million to New York investors who plan to complete conversion of the rental apartments to individual condominium ownership.

The seller, Real Estate Dynamics, was represented by the Weaver & Co. of Century City and the Zohn Corp., also of Beverly Hills, represented Franklin Towers Joint Venture.

The dual 13-story apartment structure contains 190 units, including two penthouse floors with units consisting of up to 2,650 square feet.
