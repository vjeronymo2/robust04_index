930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Independent Kier makes Pounds 5.6m Kier Group, the construction company which was bought by its employees from Hanson in July last year, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 5.6m in the period from April 21 1992 to June 30, on turnover of Pounds 512.2m, down 6 per cent on 1992. Earnings per share came out at 120p. The company is now owned by 763 employee shareholders, 'a unique position within the industry,' said Mr Colin Busby, chairman. KPMG Peat Marwick, auditors, yesterday advised that the ordinary shares were worth 250p, the price at which employees may trade them in the half yearly internal market, against 100p a year ago. After a Pounds 22.4m investment in residential land and work in progress, the cash balance stood at Pounds 53.9m.
