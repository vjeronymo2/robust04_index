941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / Microsoft's share price falls sharply Microsoft's share price fell sharply yesterday when the software company announced another delay in the launch of 'Windows 95', its new version of the Windows personal computer program. Shipping of the product may be delayed until August from the expected date of May or June. It has already been delayed by more than a year. The shares fell by more than 4 per cent to Dollars 59 7/8 at the close. Report, Page 17
