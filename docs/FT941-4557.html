940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / International Capital Markets: Tofas domestic share issue undersubscribed The Turkish government's local offering of shares in Tofas, the Turkish car assembler part-owned by Fiat of Italy, has received subscriptions for less than 16 per cent. The deal raised around TL57bn for the government, with just 160,000 shares sold out of a total of 5m. The 20m share international offering, which was successfully concluded last week, raised Dollars 330m. With no banks prepared to underwrite the issue, Finansbank, one of two local banks advising on the deal, was left taking 875,000 shares for its own portfolio.
