920727 FT 27 JUL 92 / Charity shops beat downturn CHARITY shops are opening at the rate of almost six a week while the recession drives other stores from the high street, according to Corporate Intelligence Group, the retail analysts. In a report it says that shoppers spend Pounds 200m a year in 5,500 charity shops, which are moving upmarket to attract customers. It adds that it expects 1,000 further charity shops to open over the next three or four years.
