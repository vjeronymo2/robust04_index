940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / BT to sponsor 12 orchestras British Telecommunications, the biggest private sponsor of the arts in the UK with a budget of Pounds 1.7m a year, yesterday announced that it would support 12 British orchestras through a three-year programme worth Pounds 500,000. With the Association of British Orchestras, BT has commissioned the Scottish composer James MacMillan to write a work which will be played by all the orchestras in 1994-95. The first performance will be at the Barbican in London on September 21 and will feature the London Symphony Orchestra. In subsequent years the commission will go to another composer.
