931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: Unit trusts celebrate record October sales - At a Glance The unit trust industry had its best October on record last month, with net sales reaching Pounds 845.9m, up from Pounds 668.6m in September. Net sales to retail investors were Pounds 575m, up from Pounds 411m in September, according to the Association of Unit Trusts and Investment Funds (Autif). So far this year, net unit trust sales have been Pounds 7.8bn, compared with building society net receipts of Pounds 2.7bn over the same period.
