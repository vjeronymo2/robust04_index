931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Embassy Property back in black Embassy Property Group has turned in pre-tax profits for the year to March of Pounds 1.67m after an exceptional gain of Pounds 6.72m from its financial restructuring and a Pounds 3.59m provision on its properties. For the previous year losses of Pounds 7.8m were incurred. Turnover amounted to Pounds 11.9m (Pounds 13.4m) for the 12 months and earnings per share came to 3.03p against losses of 84.1p.
