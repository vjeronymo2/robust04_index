Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Tel Aviv IDF Radio in Hebrew at 0830 GMT on 28 December reports that "a 27-year-old resident of Holon was caught yesterday in possession of over 2 kg of heroin. A search of his home uncovered marijuana for personal use." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1400 GMT on 31 December reports that "it was announced today that the police uncovered a gang of drug smugglers and dealers in Tel Aviv and arrested eight suspects for interrogations. Some 12 kg of marijuana and 1.5 kg of cocaine valued at 600,000 shekels were seized in two locations, in an apartment near the beach and in a hotel near Dizengoff Square. Our police affairs correspondent Shim'on Vilna'i reports that the police suspect the gang was led by 31-year-old Ron Mazor from Tiberias, considered one of the largest drug dealers in the north, and 36-year-old Hasan Abu-'Ammar from the Bedouin town of Rahat."
