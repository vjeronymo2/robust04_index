February 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

LAZERS LOSE, 3-2

Dale Ervine scored with 11:32 to play Thursday night as the Wichita Wings beat the Lazers, 3-2, in a Major Indoor Soccer League game at the Forum. The loss ended the Lazers' home winning streak at seven games.

The loss prevented Los Angeles (14-14) from taking over second place. Wichita improved to 13-15.
