940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Court procedures may be simplified Lord Mackay of Clashfern, the Lord Chancellor, announced yesterday that a review of civil court procedures in England and Wales is to be carried out by Lord Justice Woolf. He said in a Lords written reply: 'My aim is to reduce the cost of litigation, simplify the rules and remove unnecessary distinctions of practice and procedure.' The review, expected to last two years, also aims to improve access to justice.
