Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Muhammad 'Abd-al-Fattah] [Text] Investigations carried out by the State Security [Prosecution Office] in the case involving recent bank bombings indicate that deceased terrorist Yasir Zaki collaborated with defendants held in connection with this case, providing them with explosives before his death. An official security source says that the electric circuits [al- dawa'ir al-kahraba'iyah] found at the bomb sites were made in Afghanistan, the timers in China, and the explosive material in Russia. According to the same source, police have discovered the hideouts of key terrorists and started a search in six governorates to arrest them.
