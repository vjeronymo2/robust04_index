940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Baring Emerging Baring Emerging Europe Trust, which came to the market in January, had a net asset value per share at end-July of 79 cents. At January 31 the value was 96 cents. Net available revenue in respect of the year to July was Dollars 380,000 (Pounds 240,500), equivalent to earnings per share of 0.32 cents. A dividend of 0.2 cents is proposed.
