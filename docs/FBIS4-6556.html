BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 21 -- A National Tender Consideration Council (NTCC) has been set up to act as a consultative body for the prime minister in inspecting and evaluating the tender results of state-funded projects worth more than 100 billion dong (about US 10 million) or smaller ones decided personally by the P.M. State-funded projects include those using money from the state budget, bank loan, foreign aid and state capital in business establishments. The National Tender Consideration Council's duty is to organize the inspection and evaluation of tender results of projects from ministries localities and business enterprises, and then submit them to the prime minister, for selection. Inspection and [word indistinct] time is no more than 15 days after the availibility of all documents about tender results. Prof. Do Quoc Sam, minister and head of the State Planning Committee, is the NTCC chairman.
