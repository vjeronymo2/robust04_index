BFN [Statement issued by the Presidential Press Center] [Text] The agreement signed between the Israelis and the Palestinians in Cairo today on the transfer of Gaza and Jericho to Palestinian autonomy constitutes a hopeful turning point in the Middle East peace process and has been received with great satisfaction. President Suleyman Demirel congratulates the Palestinian and Israeli leaders, whose efforts and sacrifices have enabled this agreement, and Husni Mubarak, president of the friendly and brotherly Arab Republic of Egypt, who has exerted efforts towards and made significant contributions to the realization of this agreement. Turkey is determined to continue its contribution toward the speedy advancement, in the right direction, of the Middle East peace process.
