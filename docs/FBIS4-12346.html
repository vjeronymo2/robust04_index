BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Apr (MENA)--Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat held a meeting this evening at his place of residence in the guest palace in Cairo with the troika ambassadors, who are from Greece, Germany, and Belgium. The Palestinian president explained to the troika ambassadors about developments in the Middle East peace process, the obstacles Israel is placing in the way, and the Palestinian stance on the issues. The meeting was attended by Acting Palestinian Ambassador Zuhdi al-Qidrah, the Fatah representative in Cairo. The troika is made up of three members of the European Union [EU]: the country that is currently chairing the EU, the country that chaired it during the previous round, and the country that will chair it during the second half of this year.
