BFN [Article signed JQ/RP: "Ciba To Create Joint Venture in China"] [Text] Basel, 21 Apr (ATS) -- The Swiss chemical-pharmaceutical group Ciba has decided to set up a joint venture in China. Two months after it established a branch in that country, Ciba's Plants Protection Division signed an agreement with the Qingdao Pesticides Factory in Qingdao on Thursday [21 April], the Basel-based chemical group said. The new company will produce active substances as well as compounds. The Chinese partner will hold 45 percent of the assets, the other 55 percent being the property of Ciba, which also will designate the director. The new company, which will start its activities in 1996 once its production units have been completed, will employ around 180 people.
