BFN [Text] A UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] position at the Brezovaca location on Mt Igman has come under heavy fire from the aggressor's positions. It ceased the moment the UN members responded accordingly. The chetniks also fired at a UN vehicle as it was passing along the Visoko-[word indistinct] road. Fire has also been opened at a UN helicopter overflying the (Vele) area, the 1st Corps says in its statement.
