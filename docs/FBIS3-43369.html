Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister in the Presidency Feliciano Gundana said he is not aware of the involvement of General Domingos Fondo, from the government forces, in selling firearms to South Africa. Feliciano Gundana was contacted by Radio Mozambique this morning following Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] leader Afonso Dhlakama's accusations that General Fondo is involved in the firearms traffic and that this is already known by President Joaquim Chissano. Radio Mozambique is still trying to establish contact with General Domingos Fondo.
