940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Loganair enters arrangement with BA Loganair, the subsidiary of Airlines of Britain which serves the highlands and islands of Scotland, is to lose its separate identity and enter a franchise arrangement with British Airways. Airlines of Britain, whose principal subsidiary is British Midland, will also consider an approach from Loganair's management to buy the airline. Mr Scott Grier, managing director of Loganair, said he was securing financial backing for a management buy-out which he hopes to conclude in the next two to three months. The franchise arrangement does not depend on this.
