940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Former Albania president jailed Albania's former president Ramiz Alia, the country's last communist leader, was convicted by a Tirana court of abuse of power and other charges, and sentenced to nine years in jail, Reuter reports from Tirana. Mr Alia, 68, had pleaded not guilty to the charges and accused the government of Albanian President Sali Berisha of subjecting him to a political trial.
