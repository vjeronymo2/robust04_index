Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 10 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- Today, Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree "On Accountability for Violation of the Working Rights of Citizens," which said that the procuracy of the Russian Federation should increase control over the observation of Russian legislation on work, collective treaties and agreements by enterprises, institutions and organizations, irrespective of forms of property and departmental subordination. The procuracy should take special measures to avoid delays in payment of salaries. It has been recommended that courts should pay special attention to the necessity of taking measures directed at revelation and elimination of reasons and conditions that cause these violations when they investigate cases on violations of Russian legislation on work, collective treaties, and agreements.
