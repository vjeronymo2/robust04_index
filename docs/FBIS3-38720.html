Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' has arrived in Istanbul to participate in the tripartite Turkish-Syrian-Iranian meeting to be held tomorrow. Faruq al-Shar' was received at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. Prime Minister Tansu Ciller received Faruq al-Shar' in Istanbul and conferred with him for some time. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin was also present at the meeting. In a statement after the meeting, Cetin said they discussed issues that can promote relations between Turkey and Syria. Regional issues were also discussed, he said, adding that the two sides agreed to make the necessary efforts together for further developing their bilateral relations.
