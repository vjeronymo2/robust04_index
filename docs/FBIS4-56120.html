BFN [Text] Tirana, May 27 (ATA) -- President Berisha had today an warm and friendly meeting with a group of Swiss parliamentarians, the Press and Information Office at the presidency reported today. The Albanian president thanked the Swiss parliamentarians for the appreciations they made to the democracy development and the market economy reforms in Albania. Mr. Berisha said he was especially grateful of the assistance given to Albania in this transition period by the Swiss people and Government. The assistance has been important primarily in renewing the Albanian telecom, in education field, in training the Albanian diplomats and other important projects. President Berisha informed the Swiss parliamentarians of the development of the democratic processes, the economic reforms and the human rights situation in Albania. Mr. Berisha spoke especially of the Greek minority in Albania saying that it is enjoying all the rights and freedom like the Albanian citizens. But on the other side, the president said, the minority is using as a prey of manipulations by certain ultranationalist and chauvinistic circles. Referring to the crises in the region, President Berisha said it is very serious. President Berisha expressed once more the determination of Albania for self temperance in the dialogue and not moving the borders by violence. The dialogue between Pristina and Belgrade on the Kosova problem would be especially important in the presence of a third side, the president said. The Swiss parliamentarians appreciated the approaches of Albania and expressed their support for a peaceful solution in the Balkans.
