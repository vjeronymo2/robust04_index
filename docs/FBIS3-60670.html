Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Asif Ahmad Ali has said that Pakistan will not sign the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty [NPT] in a situation in which India is doing everything for the proliferation Aof nuclear weapons. In an interview with a Bangladeshi newspaper, THE TELEGRAPH, he said that Pakistan has been obliged to face a difficult situation through the linking of aid with the signing of the NPT in the Pressler Amendment, while India is acquiring nuclear technology and introducing weapons to carry nuclear warheads to their targets and [nuclear] submarines. The foreign minister said that Pakistan believes in nuclear nonproliferation. However, the security issues of relevant countries should be viewed from a regional perspective. He urged India to review its role in the region.
