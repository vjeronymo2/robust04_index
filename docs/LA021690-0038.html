February 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Breakfast With Michael: Michael Jackson will be honored by CBS Records Tuesday in the Grand Ballroom of the Regent Beverly Wilshire Hotel at a sit-down breakfast. Walter Yetnikoff, president and CEO of CBS Records, will give Jackson an award to commemorate worldwide sales of his albums of more than 100 million units, which makes him the top-selling artist of the '80s. The publicist for the event said that Jackson, known as a man of few words, is expected to make a statement. BETH KLEID
