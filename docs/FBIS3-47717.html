Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, January 15 (KPL) -- There were last year three Chinese companies coming to make investment in the province of Oudomsai, with a total value of more than US $1 million. The Chinese companies are from Sibsong Panna, Yunnan, and Simao. The wholly Chinese investment was in the fields of services, hotel and restaurant. According to the regulations concerning foreign investment in Laos, foreign investment ventures will be transferred to the Lao side upon the termination of a 15-year agreement.
