Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 16 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC), left here today by a special plane for a 15-day official goodwill visit to three West European nations. The three nations are Germany, Switzerland and Austria. It will be the first visit of Chinese NPC chairman to West European nations. Li Ruihuan, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, went to see the NPC chairman off this morning in the Great Hall of the People. Qiao's entourage includes his wife Yu Wen, Cao Zhi, secretary-general of the NPC Standing Committee, Ye Gongqi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Municipal People's Congress, Raidi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Regional People's Congress, Dai Jie, member of the NPC Standing Committee and vice-chairman of the NPC Financial and Economic Committee, and Jiang Enzhu, vice-minister of foreign affairs. Also present at the send-off ceremony were NPC Vice-Chairmen Tian Jiyun, Wang Hanbin and Li Peiyao as well as the three nation's ambassadors to China.
