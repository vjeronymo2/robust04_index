June 24, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CAT LOVER

Re "Cat Lover Digs In for Dogfight" (June 14): Would Leisure World's lawyer and the "no pets" faction refuse a healing medication to a sick resident?

According to recent magazine and newspaper articles, pets are "therapy." Who can predict what dividends such a prescription might yield?

Give this lady a break.

SALLY GRAB MONK

Laguna Niguel
