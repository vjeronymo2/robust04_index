Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the "Names and Notes" column] [Text] A delegation headed by Roberto Muller, secretary of science, technology, and economic development of the State of Sao Paulo, will leave for Cuba on 13 February on a cultural and technical-scientific mission at the invitation of the Cuban Ministry of Education. The delegation also includes Jose Fernando Perez, scientific director of the Fapesp [Sao Paulo Federation to Support Research]; Bauru Mayor Tidei de Lima; Antonio Marcio da Costa, cabinet chief of Unesp [Sao Paulo State University]; and Fernando Costa, representative of the Unicamp [Campinas State University] and of the Health Secretariat. The Cuban Government said that the invitation is aimed at strengthening cultural links and technical and scientific cooperation between Cuba and the State of Sao Paulo.
