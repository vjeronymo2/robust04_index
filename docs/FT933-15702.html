930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / People: The Dow Chemical Company John Scriven, the UK lawyer who is vice president and general counsel for Dow Europe, has been appointed to the position of associate general counsel for The Dow Chemical Company in Michigan. A law graduate from Cambridge University - the British rather than American one - he is also visiting professor at McGeorge School of Law, University of the Pacific. He takes over from Wayne Hancock, who is retiring.
