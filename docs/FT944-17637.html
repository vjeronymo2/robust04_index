941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Two-hour strike to be held at TSB The banking union Bifu will call its 15,000 members at the TSB out on strike for two hours next Friday if the bank does not improve its 2 per cent performance-related pay offer. Further talks are planned between the two sides at the conciliation service Acas on Monday. The stoppage will last from 9am to 11am and will involve all staff except for those employed in the bank's computer centres, the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man.
