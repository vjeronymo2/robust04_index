Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] On the morning of 3 February, Jinan Military Region sponsored a tea party of veteran cadres to happily mark the Spring Festival. Attending the party were leading comrades of the Jinan Military Region, including Zhang Taiheng, Song Qingwei, Cai Renshan, Qian Guoliang, Han Naida, Huang Xuelu, and Shan Jilin. Also attending the party were leading comrades from the Shandong provincial military district, including Yi Yuanqiu and Liu Guofu. Veteran comrades attending the tea party include Yao Shoukun, Fan Chaoli, Pan Qiqi, Xu Chunyang, Guo Fuzhou, Zhang Rushan, Qu Jining, Zuo Qi, Zhao Jingban, Fang Zheng, Li Huining, Zhang Feng, Ouyang Ping, Wang Jinquan, Xu Hongyun, Feng Guogui, Li Kelin, Sun Youmin, Zhao Zhong, Li Daqing, and Wen Zhixiang. During the party Song Qingwei delivered a speech on behalf of the party committee, organs, and the broad masses of commanders and fighters under the military region, in which he extended festive greetings and cordial regards to veteran comrades.
