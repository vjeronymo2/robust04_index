BFN [Report by Lorraine Braid] [Text] Cape Town April 27 SAPA -- Law and Order Minister Hernus Kriel said on Wednesday [27 April] morning he thought arrests were possible in connection with the Jan Smuts Airport blast. Speaking shortly before casting his vote at the Parow Centre Mr Kriel said car bombs were extremely difficult to detect before the time unless every single car was searched. Security had however been stepped up.
