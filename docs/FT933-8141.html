930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / London Stock Exchange: MB-Caradon strong Building materials and printing group MB-Caradon rose sharply after confirmation that it was in talks with RTZ, the international mining and metals group, over the possible sale of RTZ's Pillar building and electrical products unit. Weekend press reports suggested that RTZ was looking to sell Pillar for around Pounds 750m, and an early morning statement yesterday confirmed that the two groups were in discussions. MB-Caradon advanced 16 to 303p in active trade of 3.5m. RTZ firmed a penny to 687p in light volume. The market has been eagerly waiting to see how MB-Caradon would use the proceeds from the sale earlier this year of its stake in Carnaud Metalbox, the French packaging group, although fears of a rights issue to help finance an acquisition remain. Mr Robert Donald at NatWest Securities said he thought there would be a deal with RTZ, but cautioned: 'The key question is, will it be sufficiently earnings enhancing to justify the share price, and for that to happen it needs to acquire Pounds 1.25 of sales for every Pounds 1 it invests.'
