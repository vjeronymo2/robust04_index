920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 1.7m French buy for Ash & Lacy Ash & Lacy is paying FFr16.7m (Pounds 1.7m) to acquire a third galvanising company in France. The company, based in northern France, has been newly formed from a division of Nordgalva, part of GPRI which is ultimately held by Usinor Sacilor, the large French steel group. The new company will trade under the name of Galvanisation de L'Artois.
