930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Spirax-Sarco in Pounds 5.4m disposal Spirax-Sarco Engineering has sold two subsidiaries, Drayton Controls (Engineering) and Oreg Drayton Energietechnik, to Unitech for a total cash consideration of Pounds 5.4m. The businesses manufacture a range of automatic temperature and time controls for the domestic central heating market in the UK and Germany, a market that does not figure in Spirax-Sarco's long term strategy. For the year to end-December 1992 the businesses made pre-tax profits of Pounds 650,000. The book value of the assets being sold amounts to Pounds 6.3m.
