BFN [Text] U Nyunt Swe, deputy foreign minister, has left for Vietnam and Laos by air yesterday evening to pay a goodwill visit at the invitation of Mr. Vu Khoan, deputy foreign minister of Vietnam and Mr. Thongsavat Bouppha, deputy foreign minister of Laos. Deputy Foreign Minister U Nyunt Swe was seen off at the airport by U Ohn Gyaw, foreign minister; responsible officials from the Foreign Minister; Mr. Kideng Thammavong, Lao ambassador to Myanmar [Burma]; and Mr. Tran Viet Tan, whose appointment as Vietnamese ambassador to Myanmar has been agreed upon.
