940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / Sudan peace talks held Sudan's government and rebels met in Nairobi yesterday at the start of a fresh initiative to try to end almost 11 years of civil war, Reuter reports from Nairobi. The presidents of Kenya, Uganda and Ethiopia and the foreign minister of Eritrea - co-sponsors of the peace initiative - first held a closed-door meeting at Kenya's State House with Lt Gen Omar Hassan al-Bashir, the Sudanese leader. Gen Bashir left State House without speaking to reporters and flew back to Khartoum. He did not meet the rebel leaders present in Nairobi.
