BFN [Text] A state of extreme caution prevails in various areas in the south and western al-Biqa'. The occupation forces last night machine-gunned the Habbush valley and al-Wadi al-Akhdar from their position in al-Suwayda'. They directed sniper fire at the Habbush road. Israeli helicopters flew over al-Tayyibah and al-Qantarah at 2315 yesterday. Helicopters also flew over the villages of the western sector and the plain south of Tyre after midnight last night, dropping flares over these areas. Enemy aircraft were seen over Tyre at 1200 today. Security sources in southern Lebanon have reported that the occupation forces brought into the occupied border strip tanks, armored personnel carriers, and long-range artillery and deployed them on the hills overlooking the areas adjacent to the occupied part of the south.
