940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Books: Artists houses in London Valentine Prinsep, protegee of Edward Burne-Jones, poses in the studio of his purpose-built Holland Park house in the 1870s. Prinsep, celebrated Victorian painter of 'The Delhi Imperial Dunbar', commissioned thehouse from architext Philip Webb, whose work was characterised by Gothic forms and hybridised medievalism. From 'Artists' Houses in London' 1764-1914 by Giles Walkley (Scolar Press Pounds 50, 281 pages)
