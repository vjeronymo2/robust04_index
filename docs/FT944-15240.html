941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Wm Bedford warns William Bedford, the USM-traded antique dealer and restorer, staged a profits recovery as sales almost doubled in the six months to June 30. On turnover of Pounds 1.24m (Pounds 651,556), the pre-tax line confirmed the recovery shown in the second half of the preceding year with profits of Pounds 96,706 against losses of Pounds 10,898. Earnings per share were 1.8p (losses of 0.2p). However, Mr John Bedford, chairman, warned that margins remained 'under considerable pressure' and sounded a warning on current trading. 'The recovery in sales (in the first half) has not so far been maintained in the second half. Sales in July were significantly below those of last year, while August and September have been exceptionally quiet.'
