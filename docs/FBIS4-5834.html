BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 12 (KPL) -- A delegation of the Office of the National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam led by Vu Mao, member of the CPV CC [Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee], head of the Standing Committee and head of the Office of the National Assembly of the S.R. [Socialist Republic] of Vietnam left here for home on April 10 ending its official visit since April 3 to the Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic]. During its 7 day visit to the country, the Vietnamese delegation had talks and signed a protocol with the Lao side which was led by Chaleun Yiapaoheu, member of the LPRP CC [Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee], member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly and head of the Office of the National Assembly of Laos. The Vietnamese delegation also called on Mr. Saman Vi-gnaket, Politburo member of the LPRP CC, and president of the National Assembly of Laos. The delegation also visited Vientiane and Luang Prabang provinces where it saw a number of economic and cultural bases. Seeing the delegation off at Wattai Airport were Mr. Chaleun Yiapaoheu and Vietnamese Ambassador to Laos, Mr. Bui Van Thanh and other high ranking officials of the National Assembly.
