Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, February 26 (XINHUA) -- The second session of the eighth people's congress of Guangdong Province closed here today. More than 700 deputies discussed the work report delivered by Governor Zhu Senlin and other reports. According to the reports, in 1994, Guangdong's gross domestic product will increase by 15 percent, the industrial output value will increase by 21 percent and the gross agricultural output value will grow by 5 percent over 1993. The investment in fixed assets will increase by 13 percent and the import and export value will grow by 16 percent. The deputies called for enhancing agricultural development and increasing input into the services of science, technology, education and culture. The session also passed two provincial regulations concerning the system of people's congress and elected five new members to the standing committee of Guangdong people's congress.
