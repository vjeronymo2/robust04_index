941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / GEC-Marconi wins torpedo contract A Pounds 630m contract for Royal Navy submarine torpedoes was awarded to GEC-Marconi Naval Systems yesterday. Mr Roger Freeman, defence procurement minister, said the deal for Spearfish torpedoes would create 40 jobs at the company's plant at Waterlooville, Hampshire, and safeguard jobs in other companies - 80 per cent of the manufacturing and processing work will be sub-contracted.
