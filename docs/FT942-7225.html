940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / UK Company News: BSG enhanced share alternative take-up BSG International yesterday announced it had received elections for the enhanced share alternative in lieu of the final dividend for the 1993 year in respect of holdings totalling 244m ordinary shares - more than 93 per cent of the issued capital. Of these elections Panmure Gordon has received acceptances for its cash offer in respect of 55m shares - 22.6 per cent.
