BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 31 (SNARK) -- The problems of the Karabakh conflict peaceful settlement and the increase of the humanitarian aid to Armenia were discussed by the UN Secretary-General Butros Butrus-Ghali and Zhirair Liparityan, the first deputy of the Armenian Foreign Minister at their meeting in New York. According to press-service of the Armenian Foreign Ministry, Butrus-Ghali said "at the UN they attentively watch events in the region and are ready to assist the development of the peaceful settlement process." The Secretary-General pointed out the importance of the meeting on the eve of his visit to Moscow.
