920810 FT 10 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Steelwork ventures WESCOL STRUCTURES has won three contracts with a total value of over Pounds 2m. Wescol will be the steelwork contractor for a bulk sugar storage building for Tate & Lyle at Silvertown, Isle of Dogs. The other contracts are for the steelwork, cladding and metal deck at Dundee Technology Park for Micklegate Group, and for steelwork and decking for military buildings at Brecon for PSA Projects.
