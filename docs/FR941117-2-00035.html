[Docket No. GT95&hyph;2&hyph;000] Transcontinental Gas Pipe Line Corporation; Refund Report November 10, 1994. Take notice that on October 31, 1994, Transcontinental Gas Pipe Line Company (TGPL) tendered for filing with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission a Refund Report showing a refund received from the CNG Transmission Corporation (CNG) and flowed through to its FT&hyph;NT customers in accordance with Section 4 of TGPL's Rate Schedule FT&hyph;NT. The report states that on October 27, 1994, TGPL refunded $3,085.53, including $316.53 in interest, due its FT&hyph;NT customers for the period November 1991 through November 1992. The report summarizes TGPL's computation of the refund pursuant to the Commission Order dated December 16, 1993 in CNG's Docket No. RS92&hyph;14&hyph;000 et al. Any person desiring to be heard or to protest said filing should file a motion to intervene or a protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, N.E., Washington, D.C. 20426, in accordance with rules 211 and 214 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure (18 CFR 385.211 and 385.214). All such motions or protests should be filed on or before November 18, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceeding. Any person wishing to become a party must file a motion to intervene. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for the public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
