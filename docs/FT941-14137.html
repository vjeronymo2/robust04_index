940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Intercare Group INTERCARE GROUP has acquired E Bittner, London-based optical distributor for Pounds 760,848 - Pounds 423,240 cash and the balance via the issue of 208,400 Intercare shares. In 1993 Bittner showed a pre-tax loss of Pounds 158,369 and had net assets of Pounds 368,132 at December 31.
