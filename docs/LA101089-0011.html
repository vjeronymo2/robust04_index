October 10, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

U.S. RELATIONS WITH SOVIETS

Are we Americans really any different than Russian citizens? I think not. Consider the words of Soviet citizen A.P. Zaitseva: "I love Russia with a heavy heart. I hate our untalented leadership. But we, because of our silent obedience, are to blame."

Substituting "America" for "Russia," those words could just as easily have been said by me.

MOLLY SHORE

Los Angeles
