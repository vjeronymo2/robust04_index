October 5, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: FOUNTAIN VALLEY;

WOMAN DIES OF INJURIES FROM TRAFFIC COLLISION

A Fountain Valley woman died Wednesday morning at UCI Medical Center in Orange after a traffic accident Tuesday night.

Michelle Ann Camacho, 19, died at 3 a.m., the county coroner's office said.

Police Sgt. Larry Griswold reported that a 16-year-old girl was making a left turn from Falcon Avenue onto Ward Street in Fountain Valley when her 1979 Chrysler collided with Camacho's 1964 Volkswagen.

The 16-year-old and her 15-year-old sister, who was riding with her, were treated for minor injuries at the scene and released.

Police are investigating the accident to determine responsibility.
