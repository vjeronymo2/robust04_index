Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Mar (MENA)--Detectives arrested two individuals on charges of manufacturing firearms in Cairo's al-Sharabiyah area early today. Major General Mahmud Wajdi, head of Cairo's General Investigation Department, stated that detectives from his department arrested Hasan Shihatah Husayn, 41, and Taha Muhammad al-Sayyid Ibrahim, 30, on charges of the criminal manufacture and commerce in unlicensed firearms. He added that the following items were found in the possession of the two suspects: three locally manufactured pistols, three Russian pistols, three modified Beretta pistols, two Hulwan-style pistols, 41 bullets of various caliber, four pistol clips, 18 bullets, 12 9-mm bullets, 16 lengths of galvanized pipe ready for use in arms manufacture, and a number of apparatuses used in manufacturing firearms. The suspects were referred to the Prosecutor's Office for interrogation.
