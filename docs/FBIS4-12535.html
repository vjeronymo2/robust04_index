BFN [Text] Press reports have come in that the Kashmir militants are now turning their guns on their Pakistani trainers. Newspaper reports from Pakistan-occupied Kashmir say that 12 clashes have taken place between the Kashmiri militants and the Pakistani Army. Both sides suffered casualties. Another report says over 50 clashes have taken between rival militant groups this year to gain supremacy in the Pak-sponsored militant activities. The terrorists have also been resorting to abduction of militants of rival groups.
