BFN [Excerpts] The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] has once again begun to intensify the shelling of the Kuito and Kunje in Bie Province. About 200 wounded people could die in the coming five days because of a lack of medicines and medical care. [passage omitted] What is more, Jonas Savimbi's men continue to shell the town of Tchindjenje in Huambo Province. The aim is to take Tchindjenje District, but government troops are preventing the rebels' advance. Apart from shelling the town, UNITA is laying mines on paths leading to peasants' farms.
