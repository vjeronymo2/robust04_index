BFN [Unattributed report: "Lithuania Refuses To Meet Kaliningrad Oblast's Demands"] [Text] A working meeting of expert groups of Lithuania and Kaliningrad Oblast on matters concerning transit to the Russian exclave on Lithuanian territory continued in Vilnius yesterday. Jonas Mackevicius, chief of the Foreign Ministry consular information department, was not optimistic about its possible chances of success. In his words, representatives of Kaliningrad set a number of requirements, particularly on granting them the right to drive by car without visas from Russia to Kaliningrad Oblast and back again, which Vilnius refused to meet.
