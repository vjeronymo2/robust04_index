920914 FT 14 SEP 92 / Market focus on Maastricht: Economics SPECULATION about the outcome of the French referendum on the Maastricht treaty, to be held on Sunday, will dominate the week. Mid-month UK economic statistics are expected to show that the economy staggered through the summer, with virtually no signs of growth. A flicker of activity in manufacturing output in the first half encouraged some economists to suggest it had 'bottomed-out' in the first half of the year. However, the latest forecasts suggest economists believe the growth may not be sustainable Other economic events this week follow.
