Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian president's and Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers' press service has just reported that agreement has been reached on a consultative meeting between Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and U.S President Bill Clinton to be held in Kiev on 12 January to discuss issues of bilateral relations. It is also envisaged that a Ukrainian-Russian-U.S. summit will be held in Moscow on 14 January in order to complete the recent trilateral negotiations.
