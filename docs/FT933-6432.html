930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Net assets rise at Murray Income Murray Income Trust had a net asset value per ordinary and B ordinary share of 317p at June 30, compared with 260.2p a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months slipped from Pounds 8.87m to Pounds 8.75m and earnings per share came through at 10.49p (10.64p). A final dividend of 4.15p (4p) is proposed for an improved total of 10.9p (10.6p). For the year to June 1994 the directors are forecasting a final of 4.3p. The trust is managed in Glasgow by Murray Johnstone.
