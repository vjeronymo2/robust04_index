Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The following is the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry report for the 27 January battle situation: The Armenian units, which are trying to aggravate the situation in the southwest of our Republic, launched a new attack yesterday against Goradiz, which is a communications hub. Our soldiers fought heroically and brought the Armenians' efforts to naught. Having failed to break through our Army's lines, the enemy withdrew after losing nearly 200 men. Our Armed Forces' units are continuing to consolidate the new positions they occupied in Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. The strategically important motor roads in this area are under the control of our Army.
