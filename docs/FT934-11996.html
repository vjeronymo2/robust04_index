931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Canada Trustco profits up 11% CANADA Trustco (CT), the country's biggest trust company, posted third-quarter profit of CDollars 49m (USDollars 37m) or 36 cents a share, up from CDollars 44m or 34 cents a share a year earlier. Nine months' earnings were CDollars 121m or 90 cents a share, against CDollars 166m or CDollars 1.32 a share. CT is almost fully owned by Imasco, the tobacco products, fast food and retailing group, through CT Financial Services.
