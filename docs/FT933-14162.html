930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / International Company News in Brief: Banco Central Hispano Banco Central Hispano is about to buy a 10 per cent stake in Antena 3 Television from the private television station's former chairman, according to El Pais, the Spanish newspaper, Reuter reports from Madrid. BCH said the bank would pay approximately Pta5.5bn (Dollars 44.3m) for the stake owned by Mr Javier Godo, a Spanish financier who holds interests in print and broadcast media. Banesto's industrial arm, La Corporacion Banesto, reportedly controls 25 per cent of Antena 3 while the Spanish publishing group Grupo Zeta holds 24 per cent.
