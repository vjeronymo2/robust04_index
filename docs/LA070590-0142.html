July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WEST HOLLYWOOD;

BUSINESS ORDINANCE PASSED

The City Council passed a business registration ordinance requiring all businesses to register with the city each year.

The registration ordinance will help the city keep track of businesses and assist in the establishment of zoning regulations, city officials said.

The ordinance is expected to take effect Aug. 16. A registration fee, as yet undetermined, will be charged.
