Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Mar (MENA)--An Israeli delegation headed by Deputy Chief of Staff Amnon Shahaq arrived in Cairo at noon today for talks with a Palestinian team on the implementation of Security Council Resolution 904 concerning international protection for Palestinians in Hebron and the occupied territories. The two delegations are expected to sign an agreement in this regard if particular obstacles concerning international protection are overcome. The two sides will then resume their negotiations on implementing the Gaza-Jericho Accord. Those negotiations broke off after the Israeli massacre in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron on 25 February.
