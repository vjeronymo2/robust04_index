920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / Britons sue over Kuwait flight British Airways said yesterday it was being sued by six Britons in Edinburgh held hostage from a flight that landed in Kuwait hours after the Iraqi invasion two years ago, Reuter reports. BA is also being sued by 65 French passengers and 48 relatives of passengers on BA's flight 149 who demanded compensation for mental anguish. The French lawsuit demands FFr52m (Pounds 5.3m).
