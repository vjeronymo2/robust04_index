940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: JLI Group JLI GROUP has paid Pounds 2.75m cash for part of the packing and distribution business of Kernels Foods. Further maximum consideration of Pounds 500,000 is dependent on performance targets. For the year to end-March 1994 the acquired business will show pre-tax profits of Pounds 500,000 on sales of Pounds 4m.
