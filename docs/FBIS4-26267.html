BFN [Text] Larnaca, Cyprus, June 19 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, state councillor and secretary general of China's State Council, and his delegation left here for Tunisia today winding up a three-day good will visit to this Mediterranean island country. During his stay in Cyprus, Luo and his party met Cyprus President Glavcos Kliridhis and President of the House of Representatives Alexis Galanos [name as received]. Both sides expressed satisfaction over the smooth development of good relations between the two countries. The delegation also held talks with Cyprus Acting Foreign Minister, Interior Minister Konstandinos Mikhailidhis, during which bilateral relations, economic and trade cooperation as well as the cyprus issue were discussed. Cyprus is the delegations' first leg in their tour in the region, which will also take them to Tunisia and Libya.
