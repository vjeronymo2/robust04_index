BFN [Text] Havana, 9 May (PL) -- Today, the Cuban Civil Aeronautics Institute described as an "act of air piracy" the hijacking to Miami of a Cubana de Aviacion flight (CUB-9100) with several passengers on board. In a press note released in this capital, the institute said the passengers of the hijacked plane, which was covering the Havana-Nassau route, were transported to their destination from that U.S. city. The official note said the AN-24 airplane returned to Havana with the rest of the crew, and confirmed that the plane was hijacked yesterday by pilot Basilio Garcia Breto.
