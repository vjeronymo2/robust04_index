BFN [Text] If the Polish-Ukrainian talks provide good results on such delicate issues as defense, this testifies to the fact that relations between our people and states are based on understanding and confidence. This was said by Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk during a meeting with Polish Defense Minister Piotr Kolodziejczyk. The head of the Ukrainian state and the chief of the Polish defense department discussed a wide range of issues concerning deepening [word indistinct] cooperation, including defense, as well as protecting peace and stability in the region. The official visit of the Polish Armed Forces delegation to Ukraine continues. Today, the Polish servicemen also met with Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov, and visited the Kiev Military Institute of Administration and Communication and the Antonov Scientific Technical Center.
