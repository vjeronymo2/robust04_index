BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow April 11 TASS -- There are wounded among the personnel and children of Russian sailors as a result of an assault of the Conservation Division of the Black Sea Fleet, deployed in Odessa, by the Ukrainian Special Task Force in the evening of April 10. Windows were smashed in the premises of the unit, the civilians working there were beaten up and transported to the populated locality of Chabana, suburb of Odessa, ITAR-TASS learned today in the morning from Andrey Grachev, head of the press centre of the Black Sea Fleet. According to Grachev, the room where the division's weapons were kept, as well as premises of the secret and financial services, were sealed and are now being guarded by the Ukrainian Special Task Force. Commander of the Black Sea Fleet Eduard Baltin described the incident as "actions of uncontrollable gangs". Russian sailors cannot understand how Ukrainian soldiers can be so malicious and cruel to their Slavonic brothers. They displayed utmost cruelty during the capture of the division of the Black Sea Fleet.
