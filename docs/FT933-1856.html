930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Scottish power charges proposal PROF Stephen Littlechild, director-general of electricity supply, has proposed that Hydro-Electric, the smaller of the two Scottish electricity companies, should keep rises in transmission charges over five years to 1.5 percentage points below the retail price index, compared with the present rate of RPI minus 0.5 points. The ScottishPower yardstick remains at 1 point below RPI.
