October 9, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Olympic medalist Rod Dixon of New Zealand broke a course record in the masters' division of the Infiniti California Mile in San Francisco, finishing the steep run up Nob Hill in 5:07.8.

Olympian Wilson Waigwa of Kenya finished second in 5:22 and defending champion Steve Ferraz of San Francisco finished third in 5:36.3.
