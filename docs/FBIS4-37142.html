BFN [Text] Vienna, June 16 (AFP) -- OPEC ministers failed to agree here Thursday [16 June] on the choice of a successor to OPEC Secretary General Subroto of Indonesia, delegates said. Winding up a two-day meeting here, they agreed Libya's oil minister, 'Abdallah Salim al-Badri, should oversee the activities of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries secretariat until the next ministerial meeting in November, Saudi Arabia's oil minister Hisham Nazir said.
