BFN [Text] A new political party, the United Socialist Party, was launched in Lobatsi yesterday bringing the total number of political parties in Botswana to nine. Its leader, Mr. (Nehemia Mudebula), told supporters that his party is set to change politics in Botswana. He said the launching of his party marks the beginning of politics for the liberation of Botswana from ignorance, hunger, illiteracy, unemployment, and poor wages.
