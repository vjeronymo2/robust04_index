February 2, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

PARK CLEANUP PLAN APPLICATIONS SOUGHT

Fullerton is accepting applications from nonprofit community groups interested in making weekly cleanup visits to one of 25 city parks.

The 15-week adopt-a-park program pays $500 to each group that picks up litter for the entire period. Fullerton residents must make up at least half the membership of groups joining the program. Active community groups will be given preference.

Adopt-a-park applications must reach the Community Services Department by Feb. 8. Call (714) 738-6575 for information.
