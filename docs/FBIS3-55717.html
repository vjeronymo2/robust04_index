Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Chairman of the Central Electoral Commission Nikolay Ryabov has reported that 58,187,000 people, or 54 percent of the total electorate, took part in the vote on the draft new Constitution of the Russian Federation. A total of 32,937,000, or more than 58 percent, voted in favor of the Constitution. After the elections, 150 complaints were registered and some of these had been submitted for examination in the courts. The election campaign cost 160 billion rubles. Nikolay Ryabov also reported that representatives of 29 nationalities had been elected to the Federation Council. Russians make up 76 percent of the total number of deputies in the upper chamber.
