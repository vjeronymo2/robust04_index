Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] According to the latest report on the massacre that occurred in the Sayyidat al-Najat church in Zuq Micha'il, there are 10 dead and approximately 40 injured. They have been taken to hospitals in the area. Papal Nuncio Pablo Puente inspected the bombed church a short while ago. Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri also inspected the church before proceeding to Bkirki, where he is now meeting with Maronite Patriarch Sfayr. [passage omitted] Interior Minister Bisharah Mirhij is now visiting casualties in the Sayyidat Lubnan Hospital in Juniyah. In a telephone call a short while ago he affirmed to us that Lebanon is stronger than such incidents and will survive through its sons' unity and cohesion. He stressed that morale is high, that the Lebanese people will adhere to their basic principles, and that no event will disturb the general national security and peace. He said that the casualties suffer from serious injuries but morale is high, particularly among the families of the victims, doctors, and nurses. He stressed that the security forces are continuing their investigations and that sappers had managed to defuse the explosive charges that were set to explode after the initial explosion, which occurred during mass.
