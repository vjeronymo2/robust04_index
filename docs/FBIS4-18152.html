BFN [Text] Istanbul, Mar. 30 (A.A.) -- Belgian Foreign Minister Willy Claes left Istanbul on Wednesday [30 March] after concluding a two-day official visit in Turkey. Speaking to reporters before his departure at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport, he said that his visit in Turkey has been very successful in all fields. He said during his contacts Turkey and Belgium agreed to develop bilateral political, economic, and cultural relations and international issues were also discussed on which both countries share similar opinions. Claes also said that a delegation headed by the Prince of Belgium will visit Turkey in July in a bid to develop bilateral economic relations.
