930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / People: Borthwicks William Zick, formerly vice-president marketing of IFF's flavour division, has been appointed chief executive of Globe Extracts Inc, the US operation of BORTHWICKS. He succeeds Kirk Veal who returns to the UK to become group marketing director and md of Barnett & Foster; Graham Ashworth, currently md of Barnett & Foster, becomes director of corporate development of Borthwicks.
