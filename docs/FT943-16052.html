940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Royal Bank of Scotland expands in United States Royal Bank of Scotland yesterday announced a further expansion of Citizens Financial Group, its fast-growing US retail banking subsidiary, through acquiring the business of the failed Old Stone Federal Savings Bank, which is based in Rhode Island. The bank said that Citizens had been negotiating with the Resolution Trust Corporation, an American government agency, to acquire the 28 branches, USDollars 1.1bn deposits, and selected loans of Old Stone, which was placed in receivership in January last year. The bid has been accepted, but the bank is not allowed by the RTC to disclose the price until the completion of the deal which is expected to be within the next week or so. The acquisition of Old Stone, the fourth largest bank in Rhode Island, is the fifth Royal Bank has made within the past year,and the eighth since it acquired Citizens in 1988. Mr George Mathewson, Royal Bank's chief executive, said the purchase was in line with the aim of gaining market share in southern New England. Royal Bank said that after concluding the Old Stone deal, Citizens was expected to grow to about USDollars 10bn, making it one of the 60 largest US banking groups.
