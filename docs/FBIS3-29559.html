Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 19 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a gift from a delegation of the Social Action Party of Thailand on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Hwang Chang-yop by head of the delegation Bunphan Kaeowatthana, deputy leader of the Social Action Party of Thailand.
