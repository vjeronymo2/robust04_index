930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Saatchi makes Dollars 14.8m disposal Saatchi & Saatchi is raising about Dollars 14.8m (Pounds 9.6m) from the sale of the Howard Marlboro Group, which comprises three companies engaged in various forms of in-store marketing. The companies are being sold to MarkitStar, a US group whose shares are traded on Nasdaq. The companies - Marlboro Marketing, Creative Displays and HMG Europe - are all indirectly wholly owned subsidiaries of Saatchi and operate in New York, Chicago and the Netherlands respectively. Their preliminary results for 1992 indicate turnover of Pounds 37.1m, pre-tax losses of Pounds 500,000 and net tangible assets of Pounds 5m at the year end.
