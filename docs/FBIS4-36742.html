BFN [Text] In the Kashmir Valley, militants fired a rocket and resorted to heavy firing last night on a building in Srinagar which houses the transmitter of the commercial service of Radio Kashmir. It however did not cause any damage to the transmitter. The militants fled when security fores returned the fire. Meanwhile, three militants and two jawans [soldiers] of paramilitary forces were killed and 11 others injured in separate incidents during the past 24 hours. The security forces have sealed off the Batamaloo area in central Srinagar to flush out militants. Authorities in Srinagar are hopeful of securing an early release of the two British trekkers who were kidnapped by militants five days ago. An official spokesman said the safety of the kidnapped persons is the first priority of the authorities. The father of one of the kidnapped persons told AIR [All India Radio] Srinagar correspondent that efforts are under way for an early release of the hostages. He also appreciated the role of the authorities in this connection.
