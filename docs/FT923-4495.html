920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / 'Mafia boss' arrested Italian police yesterday arrested Mr Giuseppe Madonia, a mafia boss believed to be implicated in the killings of leading anti-Mafia magistrates Mr Paolo Borsellino and Mr Giovani Falcone, writes Robert Graham in Rome. Mr Madonia has been wanted by police since 1984. The Madonia family controls the city of Gela in Sicily. Yesterday, police said Mr Madonia was probably number two in the Cosa Nostra hierarchy.
