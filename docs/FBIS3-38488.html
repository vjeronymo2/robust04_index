Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Syrian Social Affairs and Labor Minister 'Ali Khalil arrived in Cyprus this evening for an official visit. The Syrian minister will hold talks with Labor and Social Insurance Minister Andreas Mousiouttas to improve the cooperation between the two countries in the areas of responsibility of the two ministries. At the same time, there will be negotiations for a social insurance agreement between the two countries. During his visit to Cyprus, Khalil will be received by President Glavkos Kliridhis. He will also meet House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos and other officials.
