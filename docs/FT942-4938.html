940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / International Company News: GMA lifts stake in Dominion Mining Gold Mines of Australia, the Perth-based mining company which launched a surprise all-share bid for the larger Dominion Mining group on Friday, said yesterday it had lifted its share stake in the target company to 9.9 per cent, writes Nikki Tait. The shares were bought at an average price of just under 44 cents each, costing GMA about ADollars 17m (USDollars 12.2m). The bid is worth around ADollars 180m.
