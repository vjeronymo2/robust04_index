Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, February 4 (SNARK)--At the meeting with the leadership of the Russian Defence Department the representatives of the Georgian delegation put a question on additional redistribution to Georgia the additional content of Russian troops within the intermediary mission, Colonel Pavel Flyagin [name as received], the expert of the Transcaucasia Ethnic Relations of the Russian Defence Department, told SNARK. According to Flyagin, Armenia also is interested in the Russian troops presence on her territory. Mr. Flyagin said the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan discussed the problem at the recent negotiations with the Heads of the Group of the Russian Troops in Transcaucasia. Speaking on the results of the negotiations which took place between military Heads of Russian and Georgia, Pavel Flyagin informed of the meeting's agenda - the sides discussed on the legal and other kinds of guarantees for the Russian soldiers as the latter are waiting for the solution of their social, life and other problems. Mr. Flyagin hopes the sides will do their best to solve the problems in the coming future.
