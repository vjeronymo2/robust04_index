Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 14, IRNA -- Uzbekistan is to open its embassy in Tehran early in April, Uzbek Deputy Foreign Minister Murad Khakimov told Iran's ambassador Mohammad-Musa Hashemi Golpayegani in Tashkent Monday. Khakimov also named Parviz Aliyev as Uzbekistan's temporary charge d'affaires to Tehran, and said Aliyev would be in Tehran to open the embassy. The two officials discussed promotion of mutual relations, establishment of a direct air link between Tehran and Tashkent and setting up of a joint economic commission.
