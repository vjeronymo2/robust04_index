Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, February 6, RIA -- Russian-Turkish relations are acquiring a more expressed constructive shape, which gives way to real prospects of a mature political partnership between the two countries, as was emphasised during Russian First Deputy Foreign Minister Anatoliy Adamishin's meeting here with Turkey's Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. The Turkish side put a special emphasis on its interest in implementing the Russian Federation president's official visit to Turkey according to the agreement achieved, reported the information and press department of the Russian Foreign Ministry, based on information received from Russia's consulate-general in Turkey.
