930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Bula Resources Bula Resources, the Irish exploration company, turned in a substantially higher loss of IPounds 2.57m (Pounds 2.5m) in 1992, compared with IPounds 201,000. Some IPounds 2.3m of that was attributable to exceptional costs. Turnover fell to IPounds 1.58m (IPounds 2.2m) following a fall in US oil production. Costs of sales were also reduced, from IPounds 1.73m to IPounds 1.37m. Losses per share were 0.48p (0.04p).
