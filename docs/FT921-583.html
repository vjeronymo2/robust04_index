920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Sandell Sandell Group, the USM-quoted office partitioning company, suffered increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 267,000 in the year to September 30, against Pounds 137,000. Turnover was down at Pounds 3.22m (Pounds 4.18m). Losses per share were 6p (4.4p) and the final dividend is again passed. Last year there was a total payment of 1.7p.
