November 2, 1989, Thursday, South Bay Edition

HEALTH CENTERS TO OFFER FREE INFLUENZA VACCINE

Free flu immunizations will be available at local county-operated health centers and other community sites. It is recommended that immunizations be received annually by people over 55 years old and by anyone with a chronic heart, lung or kidney disease or certain other chronic conditions.

People with serious egg allergies or those who have a serious illness should consult a physician before receiving the flu vaccine.
