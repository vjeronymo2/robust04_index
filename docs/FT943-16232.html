940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / International Company News: Generali in control at Swiss insurer Generali, the Italian insurance group, has taken control of Swiss insurer Fortuna, Reuter reports. Generali has acquired 27.7 per cent of Fortuna's capital and 55.5 per cent of the shares with voting rights attached. The purchase price was not disclosed. The deal has to be approved by Swiss and European Union authorities.
