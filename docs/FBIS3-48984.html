Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, Jan 2 (AFP) -- Bosnian wage earners are to pay 10 percent of their net income towards the costs of defending and rebuilding their country, Bosnian television said Sunday [2 January]. The new defence and reconstruction tax, which is effective as of this month, was agreed in a law passed by Bosnia's collective presidency, the television said. It will be levied on Bosnians living in Bosnia-Herzegovina and on Bosnian nationals working abroad. Observers noted were the main targets of the new legislation [sentence as received]. A presidency statement said: "Bosnian workers abroad will thus be able to participate in an organised way in efforts to defend the country."
