July 20, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY: CONSUMER NEWS

Ford Recalls 110,000 Vehicles: Ford will inspect about 110,000 model E- and F-Series pickup trucks and, if necessary, replace a snap ring in the transmission. In some cases where the snap ring has broken, Ford said, it may not be possible to engage the "park" mechanism even though the transmission shift control level is in the park position, causing the vehicle to roll when parked on an incline.
