940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Bullers anticipates return to the black Bullers, the giftware and decorative products group which earlier this year expanded into media services, yesterday reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.23m for the six months to December 31. The deficit, which included losses of Pounds 284,000 from discontinued operations, compared with losses of Pounds 1.42m for the year ended June 30 1993 and was struck on turnover of Pounds 1.6m (Pounds 2.43m). Mr David Cunningham, chairman, stressed that the 'poor' outcome preceded the acquisition of Clashfleet for a maximum Pounds 2.8m, funded by a placing and open offer, and a substantial capital reorganisation and debt conversion. He anticipated that 'all the group's subsidiaries will be firmly into profit' in the second half of the current year with 'significant profits' for 1995. Losses per share emerged at 0.93p (1.22p).
