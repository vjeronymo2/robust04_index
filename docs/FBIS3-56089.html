Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By TURKMENPRESS-TASS correspondent Anna Kurbanova] [Text] Ashgabat, 10 January -- The signing of an agreement on cooperation between the foreign political departments of the two states was the main result of Pakistani Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali's visit to Turkmenistan which has ended. A communique circulated here today says that possibilities of building a Turkmenistan-Pakistan gas pipeline via Afghanistan were examined at the talks, too. The Pakistani minister was received by Turkmenistan President Saparmurad Niyazov, who confirmed his agreement to pay an official visit to Pakistan in early February.
