[Finance Docket No. 32470] Greater Shenandoah Valley Development Company, d/b/a Shenandoah Valley Railroad Company, and Buckingham Branch Railroad Company_Operation Exemption AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission. ACTION: Notice of exemption.

SUMMARY: The Commission exempts from the prior approval requirements of 49 U.S.C. 11343 the operation by Buckingham Branch Railroad Company of Greater Shenandoah Valley Development Company's 20.2-mile line of railroad in Rockingham and Augusta Counties, VA, subject to standard labor protective conditions. DATES: This exemption will be effective on July 23, 1994. Petitions for stay must be filed by July 15, 1994. Petitions to reopen must be filed by July 18, 1994.
