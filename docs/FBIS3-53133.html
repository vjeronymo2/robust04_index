Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] HAMAS [Islamic Society Movement] issued a statement in which it appealed to the Algerian people and all the sides in the opposition to shoulder the historic responsibility before God and before the nation. In its appeal to the authority and the Islamic Salvation Front (FIS) and other parties and forces, Hamas underlines the need to participate in ending bloodshed, the destruction of installations, the threatening of worshipers, and for the need to stop statements for scoring one-upmanship and for bargaining. All must work for restoring tranquillity and must safeguard the supreme interest of the homeland.
