BFN [Text] Members participating in the resumed discussion on the working of the Defense Ministry in the Rajya Sabha today called for increased allocation for defense research and modernization of the armed forces. They complimented the defense scientists for the breakthrough achieved in indigenous missile technology. Members asked the government not to succumb to any pressure and slow down the pace of self-reliance in this field. Members from both sides welcomed the improved ties with China and some of them suggested that India should go in for joint defense ventures with China and Iran.
