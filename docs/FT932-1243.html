930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Rubicon shares suspended at 148p Shares in Rubicon Group, which makes storage and handling systems for retailers, were suspended at 148p yesterday at the company's request following a substantial move in its share price. The company said it was in detailed discussions relating to a substantial acquisition of a company engaged in broadly similar engineering activities. It expected to send a circular to shareholders no later than July 2, following which dealings would recommence.
