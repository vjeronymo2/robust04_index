930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Medical side helps Shiloh rise SHILOH, the spinning group which also makes health care products and protective clothing, reported a 50 per cent jump in pre-tax profits for the year to March 27. Profit increased to Pounds 757,000, against Pounds 503,000, on turnover ahead at Pounds 25.6m (Pounds 23.5m). Earnings per share worked through to 8.74p (5.82p) and the final dividend is 2.025p for a total of 2.9p (2.75p). Directors said a strong performance from medical healthcare in the second half and the benefits of cost reduction in the spinning side contributed to the improved outcome.
