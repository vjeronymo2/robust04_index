931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / Technology (Worth Watching): Putting nature in the dishwasher Soap powder-to-toothpaste specialist Colgate-Palmolive has turned to nature to produce its latest tough-acting dishwasher powder, Palmolive Ultra. Palmolive Ultra, which is on sale in the US, contains a cocktail of enzymes which has been specially selected to attack dried food or ingredients which produce particularly stubborn stains. Colgate-Palmolive has applied for patents to cover the enzyme action, a process which took five years to develop. Colgate-Palmolive: US, 212 310 2000.
