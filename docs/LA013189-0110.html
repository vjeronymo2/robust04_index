January 31, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

Infrasonics reported a $57,300 net loss for the second fiscal quarter ended Dec. 31. The company reported a $123,700 net loss for the same quarter the previous year. Revenue rose by 27% to $1.4 million, up from $1 million the year before.

Infrasonics reported a $163,500 net loss for the six-month period, less than the $331,400 net loss a year earlier. Revenue for the six-month period rose by 28% to $2.5 million, up from $1.9 million.
