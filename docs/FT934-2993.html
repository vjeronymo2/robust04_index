931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Gt Portland chief sells shares Mr Richard Peskin, chairman and managing director of Great Portland Estates, sold on Tuesday 750,000 shares, representing a little more than 25 per cent of his holding in the company. The shares were placed by Cazenove at 225p. Mr Peskin said he had no intention of selling any further beneficial shares within the next 18 to 24 months. He and his family still retain a holding of more than 2.1m shares.
