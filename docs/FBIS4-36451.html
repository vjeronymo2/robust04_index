BFN [Text] Al-Basrah, 7 Jun (INA)--On 3 June, the aircraft of the vile 30-state aggression dropped four flares southeast of the headquarters of the 3d Army Corps in the al-Dayr area in the al-Basrah Governorate, east of the Shatt al-'Arab waterway. An INA correspondent in the al-Basrah Governorate said that the four flares which were dropped by the evil planes extinguished in the air.
