931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / Accountancy firm faces investigation Somers Baker, a London-based accountancy firm, is to be investigated by the Joint Disciplinary Scheme, the profession's highest disciplinary body, on its role as auditor and adviser to War on Want, the third-world charity. The firm was criticised for its work in a Charity Commission report and paid Pounds 309,500 to War on Want in an out-of-court settlement.
