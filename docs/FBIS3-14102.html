Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Political Forces Dialogue Committee held a meeting this morning under the chairmanship of brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd- al-Ghani, member of the Presidential Council. At the beginning of the meeting, the minutes of the previous session were read and endorsed with remarks. The committee then reviewed the results of the subcommittees' work. The committee expressed satisfaction with the political leadership's response to the decisions to stop media campaigns by official organs, affirming that the media should return as it was before the crisis and that all campaigns by all official media organs should stop. The committee continued its discussion of the issue of reunifying the institutions in light of the proposals reached by the committee formed for this purpose. Following consultations with the competent quarters, it has been agreed that the Council of Ministers will meet in the presence of all its members on Wednesday and Thursday [23 and 24 March] in Aden. The Council of Ministers will then meet in Ta'izz and then in Sanaa. Following consultations, it has been agreed that the Presidential Council will hold a meeting in Sanaa on Monday [28 March] to prepare a working agenda for the meeting of all its members. The Dialogue Committee will continue its meetings tomorrow, Wednesday [23 march] in the economic and trade capital Aden to continue discussion of topics on agenda.
