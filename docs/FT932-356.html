930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sycamore sells Thos Ashworth Sycamore Holdings, a maker of foundry products and moisture testing equipment, is selling Thomas Ashworth to Presbar Diecastings, a privately owned company based in Manchester, for Pounds 1.2m cash. The sale of Ashworth - which trades as Ashworth Diecasting - follows the disposal of Ashworth Instrumentation to Bowthorpe last month for Pounds 1.5m. The latest sale, which is subject to a retention of Pounds 75,000 and to adjustment to reflect the net asset value on June 25, will be completed on July 19. Proceeds will be used to reduce borrowings.
