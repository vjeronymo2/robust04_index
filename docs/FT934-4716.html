931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Rowlinson Secs doubles to Pounds 1.1m Rowlinson Securities, the Cheshire-based property group, doubled first half profits from Pounds 515,000 to Pounds 1.11m pre-tax in the six months to September 30. Gross rental income was Pounds 1.87m (Pounds 1.68m). Earnings per share were 5.96p (2.76p). The interim dividend is held at 0.24p.
