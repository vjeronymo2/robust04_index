BFN [Excerpt] Many political and other organizations here at home and abroad have sent messages of congratulations and best wishes to the president-elect, Mr. Bakili Muluzi, in the presidential election. In his message, the president of the United Front for Multiparty Democracy, UFMD, Mr. (Edward Nchika), cautioned Mr. Muluzi that the task ahead of him is enormous. Mr. (Nchika) asked Mr. Muluzi to reconcile Malawians of various ethnic origins, which, he said, is the only guarantor of peace and stability in this country. He also expressed his profound gratitude to His Excellency the president, ngwazi [paramount chief] Dr. Kamuzu Banda, for accepting the results of the election. In his message, the president of the Malawi Democratic Party, MDP, Mr. (Kamlepu Kalwa), said he was happy with the results of the election. He congratulated Mr. Muluzi for his victory. Mr. (Kalwa) said that his party will work together with the government in all matters of national interest and be [words indistinct] injustice. [passage omitted]
