940721 FT 21 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Pillsbury paid Dollars 170m for Martha White Grand Metropolitan, the food and drinks group, said yesterday that its Pillsbury subidiary had paid Dollars 170m for its previously announced acquisition of Martha White, the US dessert and baking mix manufacturer. The business, which is being acquired debt free, made operating profits of Dollars 20.5m on turnover of Dollars 165m last year.
