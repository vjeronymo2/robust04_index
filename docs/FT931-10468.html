930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: ASH pays initial Pounds 6m for TSL AUTOMATED Security (Holdings) has purchased Telecom Security from British Telecommunications for Pounds 6m cash, plus an additional deferred consideration of up to Pounds 1.4m, based on the performance of the subscriber base. TSL, which installs and monitors residential electronic security alarm systems, has built up a customer base in excess of 36,000 systems. The business generates annual income of Pounds 6m and is expected to make a positive contribution to Automated Security's pre-tax profits, after financing costs, during the current year.
