March 3, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

HEWLETT-PACKARD TO BUY 5% OF 3COM OF SANTA CLARA

Hewlett-Packard Co. says it will acquire as much as 5% of the stock of 3Com Corp., a Santa Clara computer networking company.

The giant electronics firm, which sells a range of computers as well as test and measurement equipment, also said it will develop and market software with 3Com.
