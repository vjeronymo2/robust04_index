Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The PLO has demanded from the Israeli delegation that is conducting negotiations in Cairo the reinstatement of the municipal and village councils that either resigned or were dismissed, foremost of all the Hebron municipal council. Former Hebron mayor Mustafa 'Abd-al-Nabi al-Natshah told our correspondent Fayiz 'Umar that he welcomes this decision and that he is ready to return to his post. Bayt Jala Mayor Bisharah Da'ud welcomed this demand and said that its implementation will enhance the peace process and the efforts being exerted for its resumption.
