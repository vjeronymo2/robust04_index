920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Tullow Oil in the black with IPounds 2m Tullow Oil, the USM-quoted oil and gas exploration and production company based in Dublin, reported a pre-tax profit of IPounds 2.08m (Pounds 1.96m) for the half year to June 30. Last time there were losses of IPounds 64,872. Mr Thomas Toner, chairman, said it had been a successful six months in which Dollars 9.55m (Pounds 5m) had been generated in cash and phased payments through the partial sale of its Yemeni interests. He said the drilling programme, which would begin in September, would contain at least six wells over the next year. Turnover amounted to IPounds 562,156 (IPounds 485,090) generating an operating profit of IPounds 243,458 (IPounds 205,546). Amortisation took IPounds 321,962 (IPounds 270,418). There was an exceptional gain of Pounds 2.15m. Earnings per share worked through at 1.42p (0.04p losses).
