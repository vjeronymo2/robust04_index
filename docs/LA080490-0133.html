MISDEMEANOR MOVING VIOLATION ARRESTS

Misdemeanor moving violation arrests are made for various offenses committed behind the wheel, including but not limited to hit and run resulting in property damage; reckless driving; illegal speed contest; reckless driving in an off-highway vehicle; failure to appear in court, and failure to pay a fine imposed for a violation.
