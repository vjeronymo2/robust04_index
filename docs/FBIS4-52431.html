BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 10 (KCNA) -- Foreign media reported famous works of the great leader President Kim Il-song "answers to the questions raised by the U.S. WASHINGTON TIMES news coverage group," "answers to the questions raised by the U.S. CNN news coverage group" and "answers to the questions raised by the NHK news coverage group." The Yugoslav paper BORBA said the peaceloving remarks made recently by President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea would be conducive to stabilizing the situation of the Korean peninsula. The Algerian TV reported the works, screening a portrait of President Kim Il-song. The Algerian papers LE MATIN, AL CHAAB, HABAR, AL MASSAA, EL MOUDJAHID, OPINION and EL WATAN, the Peruvian papers EXPRESO and EL COMERCIO, the Nicaraguan paper BARRICADA, the Syrian papers TISHRIN and AL BA'TH, the French papers LE MONDE and LA LIBERATION, the Thai paper BANGKOK POST, the Russian paper PRAVDA, the Vietnamese paper NHAN DAN, the Hungarian papers NEPSZABADSAG and NEPSZAVA, the German paper GENERAL ANZEIGER, the Polish paper TRIBUNA and the Indian paper HINDU and other leading papers and televisions of this country reported the works under the titles "President Kim Il-song Denies Nuclear Arms Development," "President Kim Il-song Declares North Korea Has No Nuclear Weapon," etc.
