BFN [TA SR report: "Slovak Television Perceived as Unobjective"] [Text] Bratislava--The Press and Information Department (TIO) of the Slovak Government Office regards the coverage of the parliamentary debate on the Slovak Government Policy Statement in the first issue of STV's [Slovak Television] "Aktuality" program on 12 April as incompatible with the STV's legal status as a public corporation. This is stated in a statement by the TIO of the Slovak Government Office that was made available to TA SR yesterday. The statement draws attention to the fact that the TIO of the Slovak Government Office considers the failure to make public a single idea from Slovak Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik's speech, while at the same time including a statement by Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] Deputy Olga Keltosova, a violation of the objectivity of information. The STV is legally obliged to maintain objectivity, the statement says.
