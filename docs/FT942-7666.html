940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: America leads the way America led the way last week, the only sizeable impetus as the FT-Actuaries World Index rose by 1.05 per cent. US stocks climbed on the tightening of US monetary policy. Canada followed its neighbour, but its equity market got an additional boost from its base metal content. Mexico, depressed this year, and extremely sensitive to interest rate action, gained most.
