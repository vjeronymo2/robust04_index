940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Flagstone losses increase Pre-tax losses of Flagstone Holdings, the marine, leisure and property concern, increased from Pounds 161,000 to Pounds 278,000 for the six months to July 31. Directors explained that results of this USM-quoted company related entirely to the marina activity, the costs of investigating new acquisitions and interest payable. Turnover fell from Pounds 388,000 to Pounds 252,000. Losses per share were 0.2p (0.12p).
