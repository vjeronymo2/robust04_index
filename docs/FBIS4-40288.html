BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Excerpts] Moscow, 16 Jun--European security and the role of the CSCE in strengthening its foundations, the situation in the former Yugoslavia, and NATO's "Partnership for Peace" initiative are the subjects that are expected to be the focal point of Russian- Swiss talks in Moscow today. They will be held this morning between Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and Flavio Cotti, federal adviser and chief of the Swiss Foreign Affairs Department. [passage omitted] Mikhail Demurin, a spokesman for the Russian foreign political department, said in Moscow on Wednesday that a protocol on cooperation between the Russian Foreign Ministry and the Swiss Federal Department of Foreign Affairs is expected to be signed during the talks. "Since a political dialogue between the sides has been developing constructively as of late," the diplomat said, "one may be confident that there are good prospects for Russian-Swiss cooperation."
