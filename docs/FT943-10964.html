940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore Emerging net assets at 153.8p Gartmore Emerging Pacific Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 153.8p per share as at June 30. The figure compared with values of 191.7p at the December year end and 112p at end-June 1993. The trust, which concentrates on achieving long term capital growth, incurred net losses of Pounds 219,000 (profits of Pounds 98,000) for the period, for losses of 0.34p (earnings 0.15p) per share.
