Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Raul Tukhvatulin] [Text] Ufa 9 March TASS -- Razil Sitdikhovi Musin, deputy chairman of Bashkortostan's Parliament, appointed as head of administration of the Dyurtyulinsky district a week ago, was killed on Wednesday morning in an explosion of the bomb planted at the door of his house. Musin was appointed to the post last week by President Murtaza Rakhimov despite opposition of all local leaders in the district. Bashkortostan's president sacked the previous head of administration to give another office to Razil Musin.
