921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Letter: A lively read for students in Wales Sir, In response to Barry Riley's Business Books review, 'The high priest of Moggonomics', he might like to know that the 'long lost world' of students reading the Financial Times is very much alive and kicking in the University of Glamorgan Business School where more than 150 business studies students receive a daily copy under the FT educational discount scheme. Peter Randall, course tutor, Business Studies Degree, University of Glamorgan Business School, Pontypridd, Mid Glamorgan
