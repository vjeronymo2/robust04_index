November 14, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ABORTION: Pro-choicers demonstrated by the thousands Sunday, and now they're moving into South County to round up allies from an unlikely but potentially vote-rich source: Leisure World. . . . A Planned Parenthood auxiliary group has been established in the Laguna Hills retirement community -- the first such seniors group in the western United States, the organization says. . . . Pro-choicers, realizing that seniors are much more likely to vote than others, note that Leisure World has about 21,500 residents. Steve Emmons and Dana Parsons
