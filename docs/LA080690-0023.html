August 6, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Today: Will Spike Lee's "Mo' Better Blues" give some blues to the new summer movie front-runners "Presumed Innocent" and "Ghost" in box-office figures to be released?

Tuesday: L.A. Festival officials hold a press conference at Bruno's restaurant in West Los Angeles to announce the establishment of a club hangout for artists participating in the festival. . . . Protesters may show up when the Bolshoi Ballet touches down for its L.A. engagement at the Shrine Auditorium. GREG BRAXTON
