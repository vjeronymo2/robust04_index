Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Thai Princess Maha Chakkri Sirinthon and her entourage, a total of 23 people, arrived in Harbin by train on the evening of 13 January. Vice Governor Chen Yunlin received her at the railway station. The vice governor extended a warm welcome to the Thai princess. The princess thanked him for this. Princess Maha Chakkri Sirinthonn visited China at the invitation of the Chinese Government. Before visiting our province, she has visited Beijing Municipality, Liaoning Province, and Jilin Province. She will pay a five-day visit to our province's Harbin and Heihe cities. This is the princess's fifth visit to China.
