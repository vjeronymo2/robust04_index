BFN [Text] Today, Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle talked with his Uruguayan hosts all about economic cooperation and current events in Europe and Latin America. Minister Peterle will travel to Argentina tomorrow and talk with diplomats there about cooperation within the United Nations, as this country is a nonpermanent member of the Security Council. In the two countries, Peterle will discuss the lifting of visas and cooperation in culture, education, and science. He will also meet representatives of Slovenes living there.
