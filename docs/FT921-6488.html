920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / Creditors object to Drexel plan SOME 70 creditors are reported to have filed objections with the US bankruptcy courts over the reorganisation plan put forward by Drexel Burnham Lambert, the now-defunct US investment bank, report Nikki Tait and agencies from New York. Hearings at which the plan could be 'confirmed' by the courts have been scheduled to begin on March 5. One objection comes from the Internal Revenue Service, the US tax authority, which seeks guarantees on Dollars 107m promised to it as part of a settlement agreement made last year.
