Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Bomb at NATO Offices in Rome"] [Text] Rome, 10 Jan (ANSA) -- A caller to the Neapolitan daily IL MATTINO claimed that the Nuclei Communist Combattants (NCC) were responsible for the two-kilogram bomb which exploded outside the NATO Command Defense College offices in Rome shortly after midnight Sunday [9 January] evening. The caller said other sites had been targeted in various Italian cities, including the La Brera railway car plant in Pozzuoli, some ten kilometers outside Naples. Police were investigating the credibility of the claim that the NCC was responsible, a group which has boasted of other attacks in the past.
