940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Murray Income assets growth Net asset value per ordinary and B ordinary shares of Murray Income Trust stood at 367.8p at the six months ended December 31, up from 316.9p six months earlier and 293.5p a year ago. Net revenue rose to Pounds 4.47m, against Pounds 4.17m for the comparable period. Earnings emerged at 5.47p (5p) per ordinary share and 5.42p (4.95p) assuming conversion of the B ordinary shares. As forecast, the directors are recommending an increase in the final dividend from 4.15p to 4.3p, raising the total for 1994 to 11.3p (10.9p).
