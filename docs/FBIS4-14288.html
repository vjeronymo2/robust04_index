BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 28 Apr (MENA)--U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher left Cairo this evening after a two-day visit to Egypt as part of a tour of the region. Christopher's tour aims to intensify efforts to promote the peace process and achieve progress on all tracks, especially the Palestinian-Israeli track. Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat conferred at his place of residence in al-Andalus Palace with Christopher this evening. Palestinian sources said the meeting addressed the obstacles still impeding a Palestinian-Israeli agreement on the implementation of the Declaration of Principles and the Gaza-Jericho First accord.
