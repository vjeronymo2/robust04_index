940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / Trade unionists protest government moves on pensions Mr Bill Morris, general secretary of the TGWU general union, and other trade unionists gathered outside parliament yesterday with an 18ft inflatable model of Robert Maxwell to protest at government moves over pensions. Under a banner saying: 'Ministers - don't do a Maxwell on our pensions', the union leaders, representing 1m members of the Local Government Superannuation Scheme and 1m pensioners, claimed the moves threaten the future of the scheme. Mr Morris said: 'The government aim is to force increasing numbers out and then break up the Local Government Superannuation Scheme. First the personal pensions disaster and now this attack on the LGSS threaten poverty in old age for a generation of pensioners in the 21st century'
