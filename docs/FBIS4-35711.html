BFN [Text] Balasore -- India's indigenous surface-to-surface missile Prithvi will be test fired at the interim test range at Chandipur-or-the-sea between June 4 and 6, reports UNI. An official notification was issued here for the usual temporary shifting of the population around the range to nearby shelters. Scientists of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) have already started arriving here.
