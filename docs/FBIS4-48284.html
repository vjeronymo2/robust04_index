BFN [Text] The National Education Coordinating Committee, NECC, SADTU [South African Democratic Teachers' Union], COSAS [Congress of South African Students] and Sasco [South African Students Congress] say they are looking forward to a constructive and mutually beneficial relationship with the new education ministry. The organizations said in a statement in Johannesburg that the time had arrived for them to recommit themselves to education. They undertook to keep the disruption of education to a minimum this year. The NECC general secretary Mr. (Sipho Tjele) said apartheid education could not be phased out overnight.
