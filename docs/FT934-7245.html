931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / International Company News: Diversification leaves Minebea with net loss MINEBEA, the world's leading maker of miniature ball bearings, lifted operating profits, but suffered heavy losses from diversifying into semiconductors, writes William Dawkins. Pre-tax profits rose 8.5 per cent to Y8.93bn (Dollars 83.5m) in the year to end-September, on sales up fractionally to Y201.6bn. It swung from a net profit of Y2.73bn last year into a net loss of Y59.75bn. It will pay no dividend this year, having paid Y6 per share in 1992.
