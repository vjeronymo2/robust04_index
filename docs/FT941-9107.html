940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Whitchurch placing oversubscribed Whitchurch Group has raised Pounds 1.28m through a placing of shares at 47p which values the company at Pounds 6.27m. The meat processing and distribution group said the placing was 'well oversubscribed', and the shares, dealt under Rule 535(2), opened for dealings yesterday at 51 1/2 p. Proceeds will be used to fund expansion.
