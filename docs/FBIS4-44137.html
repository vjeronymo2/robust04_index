BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Azerbaijani Deputy Prime Minister Abbas Abbasov and the delegation accompanying him are holding contacts in Ankara. Abbasov called on State Minister Necmettin Cevheri this morning. Referring to the developing bilateral relations, Cevheri said that the issue of wheat exports to Azerbaijan was discussed at the meeting. He added that views were also exchanged on initiatives Turkey might take to market Azerbaijani export goods in third countries. Abbasov, in turn, expressed his satisfaction over Turkey's economic assistance.
