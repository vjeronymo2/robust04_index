920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Algerian moslem fundamentalists facing death sentence Some of 13 Moslem fundamentalists after they were sentenced to hang at a military court in the Algerian desert town of Ouargla on Monday. They were convicted of an attack on a border post in which three guards were killed. Seven more went on trial yesterday, accused of a raid on a naval post in Algiers last February in which 10 people died. Gunfire erupted in the city's Bab el-Oued Moslem stronghold last night as police surrounded the area.
