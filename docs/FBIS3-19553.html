Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Polish National Defense Minister Piotr Kolodziejczyk, in Ankara on an official visit, has met with General Dogan Gures, chief of the General Staff. Indicating that Turkey and Poland have very good historical relations, Gen. Gures said that Turkey appreciates the democratization process in Poland and supports its entrance into NATO. The Polish defense minister was later received by President Suleyman Demirel at the Cankaya Mansion.
