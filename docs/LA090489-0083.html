September 4, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: ANAHEIM;

WOMAN LOSES CAR, PURSE IN ROBBERY

Anaheim police are looking for two young women in connection with an incident in which a third woman was pushed to the ground outside a Disneyland-area restaurant, where her car and purse were stolen.

The incident was reported at 6:21 p.m. outside Bob's Big Boy at 1350 S. Harbor Blvd. The victim, whose name was not immediately released, was treated at the scene by Anaheim Fire Department paramedics but was not seriously hurt.

Police late Sunday had issued a bulletin for the victim's car, described as a white Toyota Corolla with California license plate No. 2KOZ874.
