BFN [Text] On the evening of 18 June at the meeting hall of the National Defense Ministry, a grand ceremony was held to confer meritorious medals on comrades in the leadership of the National Defense Ministry. The ceremony was attended by more than 100 senior and medium-ranking military officers representing many departments and units. A presidential decree was read on the conferment of the First Class Freedom Order on Comrade Lieutenant General Choummalai Sai-gnason, national defense minister, for his outstanding achievements with other senior military officers in organizing and leading the Lao People's Army [LPA] to fulfill their national defense and construction tasks in recent years. Then, Comrade Major General Soulima Bounleut, deputy chief of the LPA General Political Department, acting on behalf of the organizing committee, invited Comrade Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly, to confer the First Class Freeddom Order on Comrade Lt. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason amidst loud applause. After that Comrade Maj. Gen. Soulima Bounleut invited Comrade Lt. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason, national defense minister, to confer the Second Class Order of Heroism on Comrade Lt. Gen. Siphon Phalikhan and Lt. Gen. Ai Souli-gnaseng.
