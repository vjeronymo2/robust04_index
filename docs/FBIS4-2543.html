BFN [Text] The border-defense police brigade stationed in Dalian development zone cracked down on an appalling case of smuggling cigarettes. As soon as receiving the report given by the masses at 2300 on 31 March, the border-defense police brigade in the development zone under the Dalian border-defense police detachment launched an urgent attack and seized a large amount of smuggled cigarettes at Dongsiergou cargo transferring point in Meiyaozi village of Dalian development zone in the early morning of 1 April. The brigade also detained the three trucks loaded with smuggled cigarettes; arrested on the spot the smugglers, including (Chen Aiqing), (Zhu Ruishan), and (Li Shulin); and brought them to justice. It seized more than 600 cartons of cigarettes worth almost one million yuan. The case is being further handled.
