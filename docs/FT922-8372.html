920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / Observer: Sticky problem Meanwhile, in Brussels - a city already awash with lobby groups and trade associations - it is hardly surprising that the European Association for the Promotion of Handhygiene has so far gone unnoticed. But the EAPH (slogan: United For Handhygiene) looks set to make a big splash with a press conference next week, when it will reveal the results of its inquiry into the behaviour of users of public toilets. . . .
