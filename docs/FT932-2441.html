930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Brockhampton Brockhampton Holdings, parent of Portsmouth Water, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 4.57m on turnover of Pounds 23.9m in the year to end-March. That compared with profits of Pounds 2.85m from Pounds 22m turnover. This year's result included a Pounds 367,000 profit on the sale of fixed assets and was struck after net interest payable of Pounds 1.55m (Pounds 1.51m). Earnings per share came out at 36.3p (21.1p) and the final dividend is being increased by 60.6 per cent to 5.3p making a total for the year of 7.5p (4.95p).
