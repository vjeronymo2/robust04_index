September 1, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: ANAHEIM;

CHARISMATIC CATHOLIC CONVENTION UNDER WAY

The annual Catholic Charismatic Convention starting today in Anaheim has drawn 6,000 registrants -- the most in its 18-year history, officials said.

Up to 3,000 more participants are expected to register at the door, according to a spokesman for Serving Catholic Renewal in the Church, the organization commissioned by the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Los Angeles to serve the charismatic movement within the church.
