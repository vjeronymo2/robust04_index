930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / International Company News: Universal Building Society improves Universal Building Society, based in Newcastle, achieved a 20 per cent improvement in 1992 net profits to Pounds 1.25m while assets at year-end of Pounds 173m represented growth of 10 per cent. The society lent some Pounds 25m in 'exceedingly difficult' market conditions. It also improved its capital base in terms of both reserves and gross capital.
