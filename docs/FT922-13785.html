920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Sterling Trust Sterling Trust, the USM-quoted second mortgage company, returned profits of Pounds 3.36m pre-tax for 1991, a 49 per cent fall from last time's Pounds 6.6m. The 1990 figure, however, included Pounds 3.8m profit on investment activities. In December of that year the company repaid shareholders Pounds 32m in cash. Turnover amounted to Pounds 18.8m (Pounds 23.1m). Interest charges were cut to Pounds 9.44m (Pounds 11m) and tax took Pounds 1.53m less at Pounds 1.09m. Earnings emerged at 10.7p (7.8p). There is no final dividend as a second interim of 3p was paid in January making 5p for the year. For 1990 shareholders received 10p, including a 6p special payment.
