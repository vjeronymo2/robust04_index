940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical net assets advance Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust reported net asset value per capital share of 56.8p at December 31, against 35.6p a year earlier. Net revenue for this split capital trust for the nine months to end-December was Pounds 683,980 (Pounds 676,781), for earnings per income share of 8.55p (8.46p). An unchanged third interim dividend of 2.25p is declared, making 6.75p to date.
