941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Regal buys again Regal Hotel Group, which operates provincial hotels, has made its eighth acquisition this year with the the purchase of the Parkside Inne, Pontefract, West Yorkshire for Pounds 1.75m in cash. Set in around five acres of land, the Parkside Inne is a recently-converted nineteenth century farmhouse which has been extended. The hotel has 28 bedrooms, a restaurant, carvery and three conference rooms.
