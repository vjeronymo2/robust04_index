940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Birse BIRSE has received applications for 32.6m new shares (26.1 per cent) available under the open offer. Following the placing and a financial restructuring, Mr Peter Birse, Mrs Birse and associated trusts will hold 21.3 per cent of the company's issued share capital, Midland Bank 20.3 per cent and Bilfinger & Berger, the German contractor, 9.7 per cent.
