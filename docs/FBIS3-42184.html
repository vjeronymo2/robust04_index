Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Jan 29 (AA) -- Terrorists from the outlawed separatist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization abducted two reporters in Idil township in southeastern Sirnak Province on Friday. AA was told that the reporters were working for a private television channel (TGRT) and they were in the southeast to prepare a programme. No information was available from the reporters Kutlu Esendemir and Recep Ozturk. An investigation has been launched into the incident.
