940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / International Company News: Malaysian TV group drops 3% Sistem Televisyen Malaysia (TV3), the country's only privately-owned television station, reported pre-tax profits for the year ending August 31 1993 of MDollars 33.2m (Dollars 12.2m), a drop of 3 per cent on the previous year's figure, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. TV3 said in spite of a 5 per cent rise in turnover to MDollars 148m, an increased investment in news-based services had produced a reduction in overall earnings.
