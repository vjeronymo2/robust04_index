BFN [Text] Russia's air force is holding command post exercises. As part of these, cruise rockets have been launched against aerial targets on one of the long-distance aviation's test ranges. The execution of the missile launches was described as excellent. They were executed by a TU-22M long-range bomber crew led by Lieutenant Colonel Vladimir (Melikhan) and Major Vladimir (Nikitin). This information came from the Russian air force's press center.
