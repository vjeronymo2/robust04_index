July 8, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SET YOUR VCR

Re-Animator Stuart Gordon's 1985 film of a 1922 H.P. Lovecraft story is a real throat-grabber, one of the best, funniest Grand Guignol horror pictures of the last decade. Jeffrey Combs stars as a medical student whose genius teeters on madness and who is determined to conquer brain death. (2 hours) Edited. (KCOP Saturday at 3 p.m.) Kevin Thomas
