January 8, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SITUATION REGARDING TENURE AT UC IRVINE

It's about time that someone in the media exposed the disgraceful situation regarding the granting of tenure to women and minorities at UC Irvine (Dec. 18)!

This situation has gone on for years while renowned teachers have been forced to leave. The only other alternative has been to remain and fight as the courageous and distinguished physician, Dr. Marjorie Mosier, is now doing!

VIVIAN HALL

Irvine

Vivian Hall is co-chair of the Women's Network Alert.
