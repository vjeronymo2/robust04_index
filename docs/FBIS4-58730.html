BFN [Text] Following the escalation of clashes between brothers in the Republic of Yemen, His Excellency President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali today telephoned his brother president, Liamine Zeroual, the head of state of the Algerian Republic and chairman of the Presidential Council of the the Arab Maghreb Union. His excellency the president suggested that the Union would undertake urgent efforts and make representation to the Yemeni leaders in an effort to achieve conciliation between them, and to spare the blood of the fraternal Yemeni people and safeguard the unity and safety of their territory. His excellency the president also entrusted the foreign minister to consult with his Algerian counterpart and to contact the Arab League secretary general in order to discuss the means of a common action, to make efforts to restore harmony and understanding between the Yemeni leaders, and to save Yemen from the dangerous complications now threatening its unity and security.
