Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stockholm, March 18 (AFP) -- Sweden will hold a referendum on joining the European Union (EU) November 13, Swedish Prime Minister Carl Bildt announced Friday [18 March] after meeting with political party leaders. "Finding a date that makes membership possible by January 1, 1995 while giving all political parties a chance to campaign and spread information about the EU were the decisive factors in selecting the date," Bildt said. "Despite the fact that a June referendum would have been the most direct and best alternative, I have taken into consideration the strong request by the Social Democrats and chosen a later date," he added. The Social Democratic opposition had campaigned for a November referendum.
