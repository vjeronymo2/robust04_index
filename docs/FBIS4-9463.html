BFN [Text] Kalisz, April 25 -- The All-Poland Strike Committee of the brown coal mines addressed a letter to the prime minister expressing the hope that the stand of the industry minister Marek Pol did not represent the position of the government, press spokesman of Solidarity's strike committee at the "Konin" mine Zdzislaw Stasiak announced on Monday. We are expecting the prime minister to immediately lift the decision concerning the restructuring of the brown coal mining industry, Stasiak said. According to him, the responsibility for the further development of the situation now rests entirely on Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak. Stasiak added that since the industry minister's messages fail to propose any new solutions and only increase tension among the strikers "There is no need to further exchange correspondence with him".
