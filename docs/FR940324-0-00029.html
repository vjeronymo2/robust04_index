SUMMARY: On July 30, 1993 (58 FR 40996), the Department published in the Federal Register , a final rule that implemented various provisions in the Housing and Community Development Act of 1992 that related to FHA single family and manufactured home loan limits, veterans exemption from certain equity requirements, establishment of mortgage insurance premiums, and the correction of defects in certain FHA insured homes. The purpose of this document is to correct certain editorial errors contained in 24 CFR parts 203 and 234 of that final rule. EFFECTIVE DATE: August 30, 1993.
