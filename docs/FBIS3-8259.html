Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] By 1200 the HVO [Croatian Defense Council] units had fully implemented the agreement between [Bosnian Croat army commander] General Roso and [Bosnian Army commander General Rasim] Delic. Under the agreement, all heavy weapons of more than 12.7-mm caliber are to be placed under control of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] or redeployed at an agreed distance from the line of separation. Part of the weapons have been moved to the agreed location, which is under the control of UNPROFOR and HVO soldiers, while the other location is under HVO control only. After 1200 UNPROFOR members began a tour of artillery positions and the line of separation, HVO spokesman Veso Vegar has reported.
