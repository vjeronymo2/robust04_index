920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Seet Seet, the textiles and retailing group, reduced its losses from Pounds 637,000 to Pounds 455,000 pre-tax in the six months to October 31. UK losses were cut to Pounds 368,000 (Pounds 652,000) but Homemaker Shops, the US chain of bed-and bath-linen shops in which Seet bought a 46 per cent stake in 1988, incurred losses of Pounds 87,000 (profits Pounds 15,000). Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 3.27m (Pounds 3.16m) and losses per share emerged at 10.5p (16.3p).
