931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / OFT clears Times over price cut THE OFFICE of Fair Trading yesterday cleared The Times of predatory pricing over its controversial 15p price cut to 30p on weekdays. A formal complaint was made against the newspaper by The Independent which has apparently suffered little if any circulation loss because of The Times price cut. Sir Bryan Carsberg, director-general of fair trading, said: 'I am satisfied, on the basis of the information available, that the reduction in the cover price of The Times reflects a calculated commercial decision by News International (publisher of The Times) which I would not be justified in treating as predatory.'
