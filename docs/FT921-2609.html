920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Henderson Highland assets edge ahead Net asset value per share of the Henderson Highland Trust edged ahead from 93.9p to 96.7p over the year to end-February. Attributable revenue dipped to Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.63m) for earnings of 5.74p (6.27p). A proposed final dividend of 1.4p makes a 5.6p (5.25p) total. The board said revenue for the 1990-91 year had benefited from high income from cash deposits in the months following the launch of the company.
