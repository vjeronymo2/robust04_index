910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Two stockbrokers remanded on bail TWO stockbrokers were remanded on bail until July 30 when they made a second appearance before Guildhall magistrates yesterday charged with conspiracy to defraud The Securities Association. Mr Patrick Mahon, chairman of TC Coombs, and Mr Andrew Kent, the firm's corporate development manager, have each been on Pounds 500,000 bail since their arrest on February 1.
