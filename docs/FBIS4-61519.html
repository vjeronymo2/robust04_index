BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 4 May (SNARK)--The volume of industrial productivity in a period of first quarter of 1994 sharply decreased for 71.6 percent in comparison with the first quarter of 1993. The quantity of those workers and employees who are being involved into the industrial process decreased for 468 thousand people or for 22.8 percent, the Committee of Social and Economic Information of Georgia told SNARK. In a period of the first quarter of 1994 the melting decreased for 11.7 percent, the energy -- 39 percent, metal-cutting lathes -- for 64.8 percent.
