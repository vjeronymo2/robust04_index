Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 30 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea sent a message of greetings to Armando Kossuta on his reelection as chairman of the Communist Reformative Party of Italy. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations forged between the two parties would continue to develop on good terms and wished the chairman greater success in his responsible work to defend socialism and strengthen and develop the party.
