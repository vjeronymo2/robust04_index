921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Teacher wins equal pay fight A FEMALE teacher who was paid less as head of a school business studies department than male colleagues heading economics and general studies departments has won a four-year campaign for equal pay. Mrs June Gill, currently deputy head of sixth form at Mexborough School, Doncaster, is to receive about Pounds 3,500 in settlement of an industrial tribunal claim against Doncaster council.
