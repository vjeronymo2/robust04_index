November 16, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

General Automation Inc.: The Anaheim-based computer company reported a first-quarter profit of $54,000, which includes an extraordinary gain of $233,000. Last year, the company lost $879,000 for the first quarter. Sales for the quarter ended Sept. 30 declined 6%, to $9 million from $9.6 million.
