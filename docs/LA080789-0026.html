August 7, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

The misuse and abuse of drugs, alcohol, food, sex, gambling, relationships and other addictive behaviors will be the focus of a new radio talk show bowing tonight on KIEV-AM (870). Jay Cavanaugh, founder of various recovery and treatment programs, will host "The Recovery Show" Monday through Friday from 9-9:30 p.m. ALEENE MacMINN
