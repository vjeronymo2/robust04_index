Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 26 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairperson of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with a delegation from the Royal Dutch-Shell Company here this afternoon. The delegation, headed by Lane Sloane, coordinator of the Asia- Pacific Region of the company, arrived here yesterday. In addition to Beijing, they have also visited Guangzhou and other Chinese cities.
