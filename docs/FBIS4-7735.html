BFN [Text] According to the ALBANIAN TELEGRAPHIC AGENCY, Albania and The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] are holding negotiations to reach an agreement on the border regime between both countries. This was confirmed today in the meeting between Deputy Foreign Minister Arian Starova and Nikola Todorcevski, FYROM ambassador to our country, at the latter's request. The talks also focused on the question of the population census to be held in Macedonia.
