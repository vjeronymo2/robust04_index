January 3, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Paintings, Furniture Stolen: Thieves raided an Italian villa designated to house a collection of the Guggenheim Foundation, stealing four paintings and a piece of furniture valued at $115,000, police said Tuesday. The stolen paintings were not part of the 110-piece collection of minimal and conceptual art, mostly by U.S. artists, which will be donated to the American foundation by Giuseppe Panza di Biumo.Shauna Snow
