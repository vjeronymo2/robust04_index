BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Lyudmila Aleksandrova, Nikita Yermakov, Yuriy Sizov and Vladimir Shekhovtsov] [Text] Barcelona April 13 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin arrived in the Catalonian capital of Barcelona today morning. This is the second lap of his official visit to Spain. At the Barcelona international airport the head of the Russian state was welcomed by Jordi Pujol, chairman of the Catalonian autonomous government, and other officials. At Hotel "Juan Carlos 1" Yeltsin met the directorate of the Barcelona hospital, where he was operated in 1990. He thanked the Barcelona physicians for the medical aid given him at that time.
