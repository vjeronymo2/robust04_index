931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / International Company News: General Mills names president GENERAL Mills, the US foods and restaurants group, has named Mr Stephen Sanger as president of the company, a new position, agencies report. Mr Sanger was vice-chairman with responsibility for Big G cereals, Red Lobster and The Olive Garden restaurants, Yoplait yoghurt and the consumer foods sales division. General Mills named Mr Stephen DeMeritt as chief executive officer of Cereal Partners Worldwide. He is a senior vice-president at General Mills
