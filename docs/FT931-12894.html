930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / Amec wins Pounds 9.3m stadium contract AMEC Building is to design and build Britain's first purpose-built velodrome -an indoor cycling race track and stadium - the government said yesterday. Thirty companies from several countries competed for the contract. Work on the Pounds 9.3m stadium, part of Manchester's bid for the 2000 Olympics, will start in February. It will be Britain's national cycling centre.
