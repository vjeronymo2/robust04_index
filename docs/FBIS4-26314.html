BFN [By Benjamin Yeh] [Text] Taipei, June 20 (CNA) -- Swazi Prime Minister Prince Jameson Mbilini Dlamini accompanied by his wife, flew into Taipei on Monday [20 June] at the start of an eight-day official visit. While here, Mbilini will visit President Li Teng-hui, Premier Lien Chan, the Council of Agriculture, the Council of Labor Affairs, the Central Bank of China, and the Ministry of Economic Affairs. Mbilini also will undergo a medical checkup at National Taiwan University Hospital before his departure on June 27.
