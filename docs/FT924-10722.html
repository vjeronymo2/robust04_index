921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / Irish coalition shaken after PM's claim THE coalition government in Ireland came one step closer to collapse yesterday following a statement by the Progressive Democrats, the junior partners in the government, that remarks made by Mr Albert Reynolds, the prime minister, had 'damaged the effectiveness of the government', Tim Coone writes from Dublin. Mr Reynolds had told a parliamentary inquiry into the beef industry on Tuesday that Progressive Democrat leader Mr Des O'Malley - minister for industry and commerce - had been 'reckless, irresponsible and dishonest' when giving his own evidence to the tribunal earlier this year. The Progressive Democrat statement called Mr Reynolds' remarks 'groundless'.
