Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The commander of APLA [Azanian People's Liberation Army], Mr. Sabelo Phama, has been killed in a road accident in Tanzania. PAC [Pan-Africanist Congress] officials said in Johannesburg that the vehicle in which Mr. Phama was traveling with a bodyguard had been involved in a collision with a lorry while on his way to the Zimbabwean capital, Harare, from Tanzania. The bodyguard is in a critical condition in a hospital in Tanzania. The officials said Mr. Phama had been on his way to Harare apparently to prepare for his return to South Africa where he was to assume a major role in the PAC's election campaign. He is expected to be buried in Transkei.
