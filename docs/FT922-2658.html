920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / International Company News: GBL to repay debt GROUPE Bruxelles Lambert, flush with cash from recent divestments, will use some of its liquidity to repay debt, Mr Michel Delloye, the general director said, Reuter reports from Brussels. GBL is Belgium's second-largest holding company. It has about BFr25bn (Dollars 771m) in cash and BFr17bn of long-term debt. BFr11bn of this debt will become due next year.
