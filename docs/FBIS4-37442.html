BFN [Text] Brother Jalal Talabani, secretary general of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan, has received an important message from French Minister of Foreign Affairs Alain Juppe. Juppe stressed France's determination to support the Kurdish people in their fair struggle for democracy and legitimate rights within the framework of Iraqi national unity and implementing Security Council Resolution 688, which was originally proposed by France. He also noted that he now has better understanding of the Kurdish cause after reading the documents that Talabani submitted to him during their meeting in Paris in April.
