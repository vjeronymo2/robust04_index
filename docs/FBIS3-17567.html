Language: English Article Type:BFN [By GIA-TASS] [Text] Tbilisi March 26 TASS -- "The situation in Abkhazian Svanetia, including near the Lata settlement, remains tense. Fierce fighting has been reported from the area of settlements of Verkhnyaya Lata and Azhara. The enemy is using heavy materiel and artillery, there is loss of life, civilians are being evacuated," says a report of the Georgian Defence Ministry circulated by the ministry's press service here today. "Abkhazian units opened intensive grenade launcher, mortar and automatic rifle fire in the area of the Khurcha and Nabakevi Georgian settlements on the bank of the Inguri river at 2 p.m. on Saturday. There are casualties among settlement civilians," head of the Zugdidi regional administration Edisher Chargazia told Georgian News Agency today.
