BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] Tuesday, thousands of Tlaxcalan residents took to the streets to demand the resignation of Governor Jose Antonio Alvarez Lima. Dissident teachers of sections 31 and 35 of the Trade Union of Education Workers [SNTE] seized the Government Palace and kidnapped local Government Director Jose Luis Arhuijo. The teachers demand solutions to their salary-increase demands. Most businesses are closed and government offices and banks have closed.
