940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / Unpaid miners threaten strike Russian coal miners, many of them unpaid for months, are to demonstrate outside government buildings, Reuter reports from Moscow. Mr Boris Kravchenko, of the independent coal miners' union, said: 'If these measures prove ineffective, then we'll stop all coal supplies. Then, if the government still refuses to negotiate and to pay its debt, we'll raise the question of declaring a general strike.' Miners are owed more than Dollars 300m, he said.
