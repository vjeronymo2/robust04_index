BFN [Excerpts] Today Kuanysh Sultanov [deputy prime minister] received Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet and his accompanying delegation. During the meeting, issues on cooperation between both countries was discussed. [passage omitted] The mutual wish was expressed to enhance bilateral contacts on a parliamentary level. Taking part in the meeting was Altynbek Sarsenbayev, minister of press and mass media of Kazakhstan. Sultanov also received Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Morteza Sarmadi. During the meeting it was agreed that it is necessary to enhance contacts in order that both peoples may learn about each other's culture more deeply. [passage omitted] The deputy chairman of the Supreme Kenges expressed his confidence for the smooth implementation of the mutual agreements and enhanced prospects.
