921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (42): GB Mailing Systems - Product Profile The supplier, GB Mailing Systems was incorporated in the UK in December 1989, and started trading October 1990. Its turnover in 190-91 was Pounds 500,000. The company has 26 employees. With the Royal Mail's support, GB Mailing Systems have developed a broad rage of innovative postcode-based marketing database systems, using the Royal Mail's Postcode Adddress Files (PAF), which are available across a wide range of platforms. Number of sites: 350 hardware and operating systems: PC, Unix and other midrange hardware. Sales and support: the product is licensed on an annual basis, with volume discounts for the number of copies. Other services available: training and bespoke development. Price: this varies for midrange platforms: for example, first copy of premises file on a PC, Pounds 895; dropping to Pounds 58 in subsequent years. User views: 'A variety of hardware platforms' welcomed.
