April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

SECTION FOCUSES ON WORLD AFFAIRS

World Report, a new weekly section devoted to international news, debuts today.

Each Tuesday the section will provide in-depth analysis of major world events and a forward-looking perspective on international trends.

Today, Nicaragua bureau chief Richard Boudreaux assesses what will become of the Sandinistas as they turn over power to President-elect Violeta Chamorro.
