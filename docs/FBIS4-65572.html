BFN [Text] Finland will continue its aid to former Yugoslavia. Necessities worth 16.5 Finnish markkas will shortly be transported to the area, says the Foreign Ministry's department for development cooperation. The consignment consists mainly of Finnish products, including more than 70,000 plywood boards. Finland will also deliver necessities to schools and nurseries. Finland has earmarked a total of 40 million Finnish markkas to help the disintegrated Yugoslavia.
