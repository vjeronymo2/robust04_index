Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid met here tonight with Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf. The two officials discussed the situation in the Arab world and the Arab League's resolutions at the current League session. The Iraqi minister briefed Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid on conditions in Iraq under the siege imposed on it and on the Security Council stand. The meeting was attended by Ambassador Nabil Najm, Iraq's envoy to the Arab League.
