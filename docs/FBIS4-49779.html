BFN [Text] A six-member delegation of "KULLOJA," a theoretical periodical of the Central Committee of the Workers Party of Korea, headed by Han Kum-yong, director of the editorial bureau of this magazine house, arrived in Jinan on 25 April to start its friendly visit to Shandong Province. On the morning of 25 April, accompanied by Luo Shugang, director of the political and theoretical department of QIUSHI under the CPC Central Committee, the delegation successively visited the no. 2 Jinan machine tool plant and DAZHONG RIBAO Office. At DAZHONG RIBAO Office, Liu Xuede, director of the newspaper house; and Yu Guanshen, deputy chief editor, introduced to the Korean guests DAZHONG RIBAO House's development history and theoretical propaganda work. The delegation also visited the printing house of the newspaper house. On the same evening at Qilu Guesthouse, Chen Guanglin, executive deputy director of the propaganda department under the Shandong Provincial party committee, met with and feted the delegation. Responsible comrades of DAZHONG RIBAO House and the provincial foreign affairs office were present to help entertain the guests.
