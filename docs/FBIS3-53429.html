Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The political and religious sermons of the Friday prayers of Tehran were held today by the interim Friday prayer leader of Tehran, Ayatollah Emami-Kashani. The interim Friday prayer leader of Tehran in the sermon on the occasion of the 40th day after the demise of Grand Ayatollah Golpayegani elaborated on the life of this divine scholar and commemorated him. Ayatollah Emami-Kashani, in continuation of the prayers, while commemorating martyrs of the Islamic revolution and the imposed war, praised the disabled, the missing in action and the POWs of the Islamic Iran. The interim Friday prayer leader of Tehran, pointing to the enmity of the arrogant powers with Islam said: The United Nations and the West commit crimes against Muslims in Bosnia-Herzegovina, and the world arrogant powers and the Zionism commit crimes in Palestine and Lebanon, and their intervention in Afghanistan has made the Afghan groups fight each other. The interim Friday prayer leader of Tehran then stated that the arrogant powers must know that their crimes only disgrace them in the eyes and opinion of the world nations and they will never succeed in destroying Islam.
