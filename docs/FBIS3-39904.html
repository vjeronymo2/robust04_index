Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Export Success to East Europe"] [Text] Austria's exports to East Europe in 1993 rose by 5 percent to 59.3 billion schillings; the surplus now already amounts to 16.3 billion schillings. The most important partner is Hungary (7.9 billion schillings), which is the fifth largest buyer of Austrian goods in the world. Exports to The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia, Croatia, Albania, and Slovenia experienced a particularly strong upswing.
