June 13, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

AUTO REPAIR SHOP DESTROYED BY FIRE

An auto repair shop was destroyed Tuesday in an early-morning fire, authorities said.

When firefighters arrived at the Hyundai Repair Shop at 1:51 a.m., the structure at 9535 Garden Grove Blvd. was "100% involved," Garden Grove Fire Information Officer Ellen Covey said.

It took 16 firefighters about 30 minutes to contain the blaze, which caused $125,000 in damage to the building and its contents, she said. No one was injured in the fire, Covey said. MATT LAIT
