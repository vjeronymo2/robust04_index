921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Substantial growth at Colorgen Colorgen, the USM traded colour matching specialist, expanded pre-tax profit from Dollars 378,000 to Dollars 928,000 (Pounds 569,325) in the first half of 1992. Net sales grew to Dollars 11m (Dollars 9.35m), while earnings per share worked through at 5.5 cents (2 cents).
