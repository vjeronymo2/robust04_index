920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Wintrust falls sharply to Pounds 1.54m Profits before tax of Wintrust, the bank and discount house, fell from Pounds 2.71m to Pounds 1.54m over the six months ended September 30. The directors described conditions over the period as the worst for fifty years. Earnings fell to 9.77p (17.25p) but the interim dividend is being held at 3p.
