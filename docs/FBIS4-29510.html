BFN [Summary] "Today the deputies continued their debate at the conference hall on the draft law on the organization of people's councils and people's committees." The session was held under the direction of National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh. In the morning, "the deputies passed chapter 1 of this draft law, which contains generalities. They also debated chapter 2 which deals with people's councils." In the afternoon, Comrade Nguyen Van Yeu, member of the session Secretariat, re-read the articles already revised in chapter 2 of the draft law. Several deputies called for adding a nationalities and religious affairs committee to each people's council. "The National Assembly later decided to temporarily postpone the debate on chapter 2 so the secretariat can make some changes." Afterward, Mr. Phan Ngoc Tuong, minister of state in charge of the government committee for cadre organization, solicited the views of the deputies on chapter 3 which deals with people's committees; on Chapter 4 which provides stipulations for changes in the level of control or in territorial limits of administrative units; and on chapter 5 which provides a clause for enforcement. "Tomorrow, the National Assembly deputies will continue their debate at the conference hall on this draft law."
