931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Triumph wins planning approval Triumph Motorcycles, the manufacturer set up in 1990 to revive the famous British marque, has been given local authority planning approval for a second factory at Hinckley, Leicestershire. Hinckley and Bosworth borough council said the plant would be capable of producing up to 40,000 motorcycles a year and held out the prospect of 350 more jobs. The existing plant, nearby, employs 210. Current production is said to be more than 10,000 a year, with 80 per cent exported.
