Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tabriz (East Azarbaijan Province) Feb. 2., IRNA -- Head of the Judiciary Ayatollah Mohammad Yazdi said here today that the Iranian Government is ready and willing to offer every evidence to international organizations in demonstration of full respect to the human rights. He said the foreign allegations of human rights abuses in Iran are unfounded and aimed only to disrepute Iran. Yazdi observed that the primary duty of the judiciary is to ensure due implementation of the laws and investigate any instance of unstatutory conduct of the laws throughout the state.
