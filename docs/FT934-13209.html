931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / International Company News: L'Oreal turns in 10.5% rise L'OREAL, the world's largest cosmetics group, achieved pre-tax profits of FFr2.45bn (Dollars 422m) in the first half of the year, a 10.5 per cent increase over the same period in 1992, writes John Ridding. The company anticipates growth in profits for the full year will be at least the same as in the first half. In 1992, the group reported pre-tax profits of FFr4bn. The rise in first-half profits was achieved on a sales increase of 7.1 per cent.
