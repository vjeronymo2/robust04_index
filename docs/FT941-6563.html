940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Arcon After normal and one-off depreciation charges totalling IPounds 3.03m (Pounds 2.91m), pre-tax losses at Arcon International Resources, the Dublin-based mining group, deepened from IPounds 1.21m to IPounds 3.24m in the year to August 31. The outcome was struck on turnover up from IPounds 860,000 to IPounds 1.53m and after interest payable static at IPounds 90,000. Losses per share emerged at 5.68p (2.34p).
