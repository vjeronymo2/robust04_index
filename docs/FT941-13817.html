940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / C&W to invest Pounds 27m in cabling Cable & Wireless, the UK telecommunications group, is to invest about Dollars 40m (Pounds 27m) in a new undersea cable system for the eastern Caribbean. The system, 1,730km long, will link 14 islands in the eastern Caribbean from the British Virgin islands in the north to Trinidad in the south. The cost is expected to be Dollars 60m. France Telecom and AT&T, the US long-distance operator, will take initial investment shares of 18 per cent and 6 per cent respectively. C&W's initial share is 66 per cent.
