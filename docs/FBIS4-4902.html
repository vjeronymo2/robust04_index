BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent 60th birthday tables and birthday tables to delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries who had greeted their birthdays during the conference. 60th birthday tables were conveyed to three delegates and birthday tables to 263 delegates on April 2. The delegates vowed to fully prepare themselves to be ardent revolutionaries who devotedly work for the party and the leader, for the country and the people and to be standardbearers who vigorously arouse the masses in the struggle for defending and accomplishing the cause of socialism, deeply cherishing in their mind the deep love shown by Comrade Kim Chong-il.
