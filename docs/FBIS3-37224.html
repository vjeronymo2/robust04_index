Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow February 14 TASS -- Germany and Russia are getting ready to celebrate the final withdrawal of all Russian troops from German territory, which should happen late this year. Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev, at a meeting with General Inspector of the German Bundeswehr Klaus Naumann, said Russian President Boris Yeltsin and German Chancellor Helmut Kohl are working on the programme of the celebration. Grachev hopes to discuss the details of the programme with the leadership of the Bundeswehr during his visit to Germany, which is scheduled to start March 9.
