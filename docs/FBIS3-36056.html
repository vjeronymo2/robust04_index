Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 1 Feb (ARMENPRES)--A meeting of the plenipotentiary representatives of the Armenian Republic and Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic took place in Araratskiy Rayon. It was the continuation of the talks that started on 14 January. The aim of these talks is to stabilize the situation on the borders. A joint statement was issued in which the sides agreed to tighten control over the armed formations all along the border and henceforth not allow any hostile action against each other, such as the shelling of each other's positions and the violation of borders. Chief Border Commissar Gegam Harutyunyan, who was heading the Armenian delegation, told an ARMENPRES correspondent that the dialogue with Nakhichevan is taking place in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and that this inspires hope that there will be an end to incidents on the Armenian-Nakhichevan border.
