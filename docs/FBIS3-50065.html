Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 22 Jan (TANJUG)--At its session on 21 January, the Government of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia adopted a decision on the meeting between Federal Deputy Prime Minister Zeljko Simic and Mate Granic, deputy prime minister and foreign minister of the Republic of Croatia, scheduled for 27 January in Geneva, the Federal Information Ministry announced today. The representatives of the Yugoslav and Croatian Governments will discuss topical humanitarian issues, as well as possibilities for the further normalization of Yugoslav-Croatian relations that will not prejudge a final political solution, the announcement says.
