921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Heavy decline at Minstergate Full year pre-tax profits of Minstergate, which is involved in property management and development, declined from Pounds 2.16m to Pounds 1.2m. Sales for the period, to August 31 1992, jumped from Pounds 2.96m to Pounds 5.66m. Earnings per share came through at 0.72p (59.1p). Dividend pay-outs absorb Pounds 1.26m (Pounds 2.31m) leaving a loss transferred to reserves of Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 96,000).
