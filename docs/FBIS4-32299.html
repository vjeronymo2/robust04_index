BFN [Text] At its extraordinary meeting today, Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik's Cabinet did not approve the wording of the agreement to be concluded between the Governments of the Slovak Republic and the Russian Federation on the debts of the former Soviet Union owed to our country. In spite of this, Deputy Prime Minister Brigita Snognerova will leave for Moscow this afternoon to hold talks with her partners. Jozef Trojak, state secretary in the Finance Ministry, and Anna Jostiakova, director of the trade policy department in the Ministry of Economy, are also part of the delegation.
