Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In a clamp down on the hideouts of the terrorists Fawzi Mahran in Asyut and Muhammad al-Sayyid Abu-Zayd in Suhaj, the security forces found papers and lists bearing the names, whereabouts, and movements of a number of extremist organization members, including al-Jihad and the Islamic Group, since the beginning of last year. The security agencies have also found the cipher organization members use in their communications inside and outside the country, in addition to booklets showing how to make explosive devices from simple material.
