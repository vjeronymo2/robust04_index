Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Feb 7 (CTK) -- Czechs travelling to the Yugoslav Federal Republic (Serbia and Montenegro) will be required to have visas as of Tuesday, February 8, the Yugoslav Foreign Ministry has informed verbally the Czech diplomatic mission in Belgrade, CTK learnt today. Zita Senkova from the Czech Foreign Ministry told CTK today that the information had not been officially conveyed to the Czech Republic. The Czech Republic has been requiring visas from citizens of the Yugoslav Federal Republic, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Georgia, and Tajikistan since January 15.
