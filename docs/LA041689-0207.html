April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: HAROLD MACMILLAN, POLITICIAN, 1894-1956 BY ALISTAIR HORNE (VIKING: $24.95; 502 PP.)

"Horne's portrait is at its richest when he writes of Macmillan in wartime, in North Africa, Italy and Greece. Macmillan's memoirs and diaries write of these missions with wit and grace."Richard Eder
