Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovene Foreign Ministry has announced that from the beginning of 1994 citizens of the so-called FRY will have to obtain an entry or a transit visa from the closest diplomatic or consular mission of the Republic of Slovenia abroad prior to entering our country. The conditions for issue of visas remain unchanged, but as of tomorrow the Slovene border authorities on international border crossings will no longer issue visas to the Yugoslav citizens.
