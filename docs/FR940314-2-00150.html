UNITED STATES INFORMATION AGENCY Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition; Addendum On September 14, 1993, notice was published at page 48089 of the Federal Register (58 FR 48089) by the United States Information Agency pursuant to Public Law 89&hyph;259 relating to the exhibit ``The Age of the Baroque in Portugal.'' This exhibit will continue to be presented at the National Gallery of Art, Washington, DC until on or about April 3, 1994. In addition to this venue, the exhibit will also be on display at the San Diego Museum of Art, San Diego, California from on or about May 12, 1994, to on or about August 7, 1994. A copy of the list of objects covered by this notice may be obtained by contacting Mr. R. Wallace Stuart of the Office of the General Counsel of USIA. The telephone number is 202/619&hyph;5078, and the address is room 700, U.S. Information Agency, 301 Fourth Street, SW., Washington, DC 20547. Les Jin, General Counsel.
