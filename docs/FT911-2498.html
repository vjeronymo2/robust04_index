910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: Static year for Expedier Expedier, the USM-quoted group which provides services to the sporting, entertainment and exhibition industries, came through 1990 with taxable profits virtually static at Pounds 1.27m. The outcome compared with the previous year's Pounds 1.25m, and was struck from turnover ahead Pounds 6.47m at Pounds 17.03m. It included only a six months' contribution from Space-Time Systems (Holdings), which was acquired in June 1990, and took account of exceptional provisions of Pounds 417,000 (credits Pounds 189,000) and interest charges of Pounds 760,000 (Pounds 84,000). A final dividend of 0.5p makes a 1p (nil) total. Earnings per share emerged at 3p (5.7p). Year-end gearing amounted to 80 per cent. The directors' objective is to reduce borrowings via internal cash flow and continuing sales ofnon-core assets.
