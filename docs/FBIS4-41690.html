BFN [Report by Anatoliy Ladin: "Secret Services Cooperate Successfully"] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty] -- In the words of Major General Sat Tokpakbayev, chairman of the National Security Committee [NSC] of the Republic of Kazakhstan, recent cooperation between the Kazakhstani NSC and the Russian Federal Counterintelligence Service has been producing results. Amongst other things in the area of combatting crime and drugs trafficking. A joint operation lasting several months ended recently. As a result Russian and Kazakhstani citizens involved in the transportation and sale of narcotics were detained.
