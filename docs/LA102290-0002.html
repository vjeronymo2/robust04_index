October 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

WOMEN IN MEN'S LOCKER ROOMS

As an old male chauvinist, the article by Lutdke caused me a pain in my limited thinking processes.

Since I have been consistently pro-choice on the abortion issue and feel this is a gender issue that should be decided by women, I have to believe that the right to privacy is valid.

Now we have the locker room issue. Do these athletes have a right to privacy?

You missed the obvious solution to the problem. Throw all of the reporters out of the locker rooms -- male and female. Women have many fights to win in the battle of the sexes. This one should end in a draw.

GLENN R. YOKUM, Riverside
