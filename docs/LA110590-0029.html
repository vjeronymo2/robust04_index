November 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Eddie Munster Arrested: Patrick Allan Lilley, who portrayed the werewolf-like boy Eddie in the 1960s television comedy "The Munsters," has been charged in Chicago with robbing a limousine driver of $130.

Lilley, 37, of Austin, Tex., was in Chicago last week doing personal appearances for Halloween and was arrested Friday. Police said no weapons were involved and that Lilley, who used the stage name Butch Patrick as a child actor, was arrested along with another man who was also charged with robbery. Police said the two men were riding in the limo and allegedly robbed the driver during a stop at a service station. CLAUDIA PUIG
