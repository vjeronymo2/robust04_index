Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Following a night lull, Azeri forces resumed offensive in the south and southeastern areas of the Armenian-Azeri war zone, a spokesman for the Karabakh defense told Interfax. As of 10:30 Moscow time, Karabakh units were engaged in heavy fighting, repelling the enemy attack. Stepanakert reports that a pitched battle is raging in the north of the Kelbajar District of Azerbaijan. In a successful counter attack the Karabakh forces have reportedly advanced several kilometers on the Omar mountain pass. Meanwhile, an official of the Azeri Defense Ministry told Interfax that fighting was underway in the Mardakert District of Nagorno Karabakh, where the Armenian units were still trying to mount a large scale offensive on the town of Ter-Ter. The town was shelled during the night. Baku reports that the Azeri units repelled all enemy attacks and knocked out one enemy tank and two armored personnel carriers.
