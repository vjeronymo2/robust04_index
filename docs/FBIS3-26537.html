Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 9 (XINHUA) -- Jiang Zemin and other top leaders, Monday [7 February] called on Peng Zhen and Wan Li, both former chairmen of China's National People's Congress Standing Committee, on separate occasions. On behalf of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC) Central Committee and the State Council, General Secretary Jiang Zemin, Liu Huaqing and Hu Jintao, all Standing Committee members of the Political Bureau of the party Central Committee, extended spring festival greetings to the two veteran leaders. At the residences of Peng and Wan, Jiang presented them with fresh flowers and asked about their living and health. He briefed the two retired leaders on the main work done by the party Central Committee and the State Council last year, and the basic principles governing this year's work. Jiang expressed the hope that retired veteran leaders will continue making proposals regarding the work of the party central committee, and make greater contributions to the country's economic construction and reforms and opening. Peng and Wan asked the visitors to convey their thanks and spring festival greetings to the leaders of the party Central Committee and the State Council. Peng and Wan expressed their gratification over the achievements China scored in reforms and opening and in construction over the past year. They said they hoped that the country will win even greater victories in the new year in economic construction, reforms and opening up and in maintaining social stability.
