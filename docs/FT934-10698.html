931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / Redwood seeks training ideas EMPLOYERS in Wales are being asked by Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary, to help develop a government strategy for training, education and enterprise. Launching a consultation document yesterday, Mr Redwood said: 'We want to share and seek ideas on how to establish the training and enterprise culture that Wales needs to succeed.' In the light of responses, he plans to publish a strategy statement. People and Prosperity: A Challenge to Wales. 0222-380753.
