930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Cons Venture Trust Consolidated Venture Trust saw net asset value leap from 275.5p to 412p over the 12 months to January 31. Net revenue edged ahead to Pounds 243,000 (Pounds 226,000), equivalent to earnings of 2.28p (2.12p) per share. The recommended single distribution is reduced from 1.5p to 1.2p. The trust intends to change its name to North Atlantic Smaller Companies Investment Trust.
