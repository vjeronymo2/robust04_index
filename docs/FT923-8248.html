920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / Kabul claims rout of rebels Afghan government forces claimed victory yesterday after two days of fierce fighting with a dissident mujahideen group, saying the dissidents had been routed from positions south of the capital Kabul, Reuter reports from Islamabad. 'The Islamic army crushed their attacks, and pushed back the enemy from their positions,' the Defence Ministry said on state-run Kabul Radio. The hardline Hezb-i-Islami group said fighting was still going on around Kabul and offered conditions to end their bombardment of the city that has left more than 1,000 dead or injured.
