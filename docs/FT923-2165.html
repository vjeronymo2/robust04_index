920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / Four arrested in mortgage inquiry FOUR men were arrested yesterday in connection with an alleged Pounds 15m mortgage fraud in raids codenamed Operation Joystick. They were taken to Aylesbury police station in Buckinghamshire. Thames Valley police said that at least 140 domestic house mortgages were involved. Operation Joystick concerned a large-scale conspiracy to defraud banks and mortgage lenders, police said.
