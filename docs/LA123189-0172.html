December 31, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

CALGARY CLASSIC

Westlake 81, Bishop Carrol 62 -- John Van Spyk scored 23 points and pulled down 13 rebounds for the Warriors (5-4) in the third-place game.

GIRLS

Westlake 62, Palmdale 41 -- Dani O'Byrne scored a game-high 21 points, including 11 in the third period as Westlake won the championship of the Thousand Oaks tournament.

The Warriors (11-1) carried a 50-31 margin into the final period, but Palmdale (10-3) embarked on 7-0 run. Westlake answered with a 10-0 run. MIKE GLAZE
