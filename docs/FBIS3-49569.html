Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The session of the People's Assembly of the Srpska republic ended last night with a discussion behind closed doors on the peace negotiations in Geneva and Brussels. The deputies did not study possible corrections to the present platform as the political basis of the Serbian negotiating team. It was decided not to change anything in the essence of the political stands presently contained in the platform. The final decision regarding the platform will be made at the next assembly session in Bijeljina on 17 January, one day before the talks in Geneva.
