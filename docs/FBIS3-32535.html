Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President Itamar Franco spent the day at Alvorada Palace recovering from food poisoning caused by a chicken pie served during Sunday's dinner. The president has felt ill since yesterday. He has had a 38.5-degree-Celsius temperature and nausea. According to Carlos Alberto Farias, presidency of the Republic physician, the symptoms have already disappeared. The president has taken some medicines and is on a diet. Dr. Farias said that President Franco will resume his activities tomorrow. He said: [Begin recording] The president and his entourage had food poisoning resulting from food they ate during a dinner on Sunday night. The other guests had the same symptoms. He is currently recovering and will resume his normal activities tomorrow if he is feeling better. [end recording] Dr. Farias said Franco had no sign of stress, as some newspapers have reported.
