October 6, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

NORTHERN CONFERENCE

Belmont 24, Verdugo Hills 0 -- Verdugo Hills was held to just three yards rushing by a strong Belmont defense, and Belmont running back Tajh Evans gained 160 yards and scored on a three-yard run.

Verdugo Hills quarterback Noel Alvarado completed six of 12 for 99 yards. KEVIN ASSEO
