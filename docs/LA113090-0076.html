November 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NFL;

PACKERS PROBABLY WON'T HAVE MAJIK

Quarterback Don Majkowski missed practice because of a shoulder injury and probably won't play Sunday when the Green Bay Packers face the Minnesota Vikings.

Anthony Dilweg took most of the snaps during the practice, held in 36-degree weather.
