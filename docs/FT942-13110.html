940426 FT 26 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Banco Santander wins fight for control of Banesto The Spanish bank Banco Santander has won the auction for the 73.45 per cent stake of Banco Espanol de Credito with its bid of Pta772 per share, Bank of Spain governor Luis Angel Rojo said last night. Santander will pay a total of Pta347.4bn (Dollars 2.52bn) for the 450m shares. It won over the Pta667 per share bid from Banco Bilbao Vizcaya and the Pta566 per share offer from Argentaria, Corp Bancaria de Espana.
