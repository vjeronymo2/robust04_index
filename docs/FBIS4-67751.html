BFN [Unattributed article: "Council Decides Contribution To EBRD Nuclear Safety Account"; as released by Brussels Textline database] [Text] The Council of the European Union has adopted the decision on conclusion of the agreement (in the form of an exchange of letters) negotiated by the European Commission concerning the Community contribution of 20 million ECU's to the EBRD's (European Bank for Reconstruction and Development) nuclear safety account. The money is to be used to improve the safety of nuclear power stations in Central and Eastern European countries and the republics of the ex-USSR.
