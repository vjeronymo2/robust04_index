Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jan (MENA)--Guinean Foreign Minister Ibrahima Sylla arrived in Cairo this afternoon on a visit that will last for several days. The Guinean minister will deliver a message to President Mubarak from Guinean President General Lansana Conte. On his arrival the Guinean minister said that he will brief President Mubarak, in his capacity as OAU chairman, on the results of the elections that were held in Guinea on 17 December. He praised relations between Guinea and his country, noting that his visit presents an opportunity to develop them further. He added that he will discuss issues of common interest with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa.
