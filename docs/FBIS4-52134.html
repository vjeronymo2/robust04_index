BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, chairman of the Myanmar [Burma] Education Committee and secretary-1 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council, received Thai Education Minister Samphan Thongsamak and his party at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 0800 this morning. Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw, Education Minister U Pan Aung, and Thai Ambassador to Myanmar Wirasak Futrakun were present at the meeting.
