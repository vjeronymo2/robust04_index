Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 5. (SNARK) -- The official visit of the Foreign Minister of Armenia to Israel, that was postponed according to a mutual agreement, will take place in several months. Vahan Papazyan, the Foreign Minister, told SNARK about it. According to him the beginning of the dialog with Israel has a great importance for Armenia, "which is not only the member of the SIC [expansion unknown] but also the Near-Eastern country." In his opinion existence of the numerous Armenian community in Israel should be taken into account, and it is useful to establish close relations with the community.
