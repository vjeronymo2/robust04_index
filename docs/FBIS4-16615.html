BFN [Text] Representatives of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) arrived in Dushanbe yesterday [15 April]. They held talks with Erkin Rahmatulloyev, the first deputy foreign minister of the Republic. Issues relating to cooperation between Tajikistan and this organization were examined during the course of the meeting. Similarly, the UNESCO representatives held talks with Khujiahmad Khidiraliyev, deputy chairman of the Supreme Council of Tajikistan.
