930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Dewhurst Record deliveries of products to the rail industry by Dewhurst, the advanced control technology group, boosted pre-tax profits by 32 per cent for the year to September 27. On turnover of Pounds 9.12m (Pounds 7.81m), profits worked through at Pounds 849,801 (Pounds 642,810). Earning per share rose from 4.03p to 5.1p. A final dividend of 1.26p is recommended to make a total of 1.89p (1.8p).
