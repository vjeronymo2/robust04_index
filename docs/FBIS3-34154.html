Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, Feb 3, INA -- Health Minister Dr Umid Midhat Mubarak today received chairman of the German-Iraqi Friendship Society Professor Walter Sumerfield. The minister reviewed in the meeting the negative impact resulting from continuation of the iniquitous blockade represented in sharp shortage of food and medical and treatment prerequisites which caused the death of thousands of children, women and elderly people. Mr Sumerfield expressed the society's readiness to maintain its cooperation with Iraqi medical and health institutions in the way contributing to mitigating the burden on these institutions and enable them to render its services to the citizens.
