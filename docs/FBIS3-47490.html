Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Yangon [Rangoon], Jan. 13 KYODO -- The ruling junta in Myanmar [Burma] and the Karen National Union (KNU), a major ethnic rebel group, are expected to hold peace talks soon, official sources said Thursday [13 January]. Representatives from the military government -- the State Law and Order Restoration Council (SLORC) -- and the KNU have been in contact to try to arrange the talks, the sources said. [passage omitted]
