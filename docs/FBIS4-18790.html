BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister Yeoryios Papandreou today met U.S. Ambassador Thomas Niles and discussed Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] and the preparations for the prime minister's trip to the United States. Correspondent Mina Asimakopoulou reports: [Begin recording] After meeting Niles, Papandreou said that the United States is willing to contribute to the resolution of the Skopje issue, as shown in U.S. presidential envoy Matthew Nimetz's mission. Papandreou and Niles discussed several issues, including Skopje, and the deputy foreign minister said he cannot predict developments. They also discussed the final preparations for Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou's visit to the United States. [end recording]
