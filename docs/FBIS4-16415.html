BFN ["Decree of the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers on the Compulsory Registration of Foreign Economic Deals in the Republic of Georgia"] [Text] In order to protect state interests during the transfer of the Georgian economy to the principles of a market economy and during the conclusion of foreign economic deals, the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers decrees: 1. To institute the compulsory registration of export, import, barter, compensation, and other foreign economic deals made by legal figures registered in the Republic of Georgia and representing the state sector who certify these deals and documents (henceforth "contract") after their signing. 2. To authorize the Georgian Committee for Foreign Economic Ties: To ensure the registration of these contracts; To send a copy of the registered contracts together with the registration certificate to that authorized bank where the organization participating in the contract has opened a currency account. 3. To authorize the Republic of Georgia Customs Department not to conduct a customs examination and not to issue a license for the transfer of the cargo without written permission from the Committee for Foreign Economic Relations. 4. To establish rules for the compulsory registration of export, import, barter, compensation, and other foreign economic deals after their compulsory registration. 5. This decree comes into force on 1 June 1994. 6. To publish the decree in the mass media. [Signed] O. Patsatsia, prime minister of Georgia.
