921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Steamlined Serif Serif, a streamlined version of former Serif Cowells, reported a return to profit in the first half of 1992 with Pounds 143,000 before tax, against previous losses of Pounds 1.13m. The USM-quoted group embarked on a restructure at the end of last year after reporting losses of Pounds 2.21m. It said its continuing businesses of specialist printing and packaging achieved sales of Pounds 12.2m in the first half against Pounds 16.3m, which included businesses sold or closed. Earnings per share came to 0.6p (4.6p deficit).
