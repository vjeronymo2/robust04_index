Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 24 KYODO -- A high-ranking government source said Monday [24 January] the announcement of the planned economic stimulus package will be delayed to February. The official, who did not want to be identified, said that the delay is inevitable because of political turmoil after the government's defeat Friday of political reform bills in the House of Councillors. The economic measures had been widely expected to be implemented in late January.
