941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Sheriff doubles Sheriff Holdings, the USM-quoted plant hire group, reported doubled pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.71m to Pounds 3.58m for the year to September 30. Turnover rose by Pounds 7.4m to Pounds 20.2m, bolstered by Pounds 3.42m from acquisitions. Earnings came out at 23.9p (11.5p) per share. A final dividend of 4p (2.5p) is proposed for a 5.75p (3.75p) total.
