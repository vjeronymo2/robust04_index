Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report: "Even Missilemen's Oath Must Be Strategic!"] [Text] Tugelen Zhukeyev, Republic of Kazakhstan state counselor, has said that around 10,000 Kazakhstanis are serving in Russian troop units of the strategic forces stationed on Kazakhstan's territory. They have to make a choice when taking the military oath: As citizens of one state, should they swear loyalty to another? Tugelen Zhukeyev believes that a special version of the CIS strategic nuclear forces' military oath would eliminate the whole thorny problem.
