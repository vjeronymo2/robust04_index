March 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Continental Announces Expansion: Continental Airlines announced its first schedule expansion in two years, boosting daily flights by 100 to 1,417 and beginning service to Panama, Costa Rica and several U.S. cities. Beginning May 15, Continental will operate flights from Houston to Panama City, Panama, and San Jose, Costa Rica, giving the airline the largest number of U.S. flights to Central America.
