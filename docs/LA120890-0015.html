December 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EX-JOINT CHIEFS ON GULF CRISIS

Oh, that we had elected a facsimile of Harry Truman! He wasn't about to let countless American youths be slaughtered invading an alien land, not when he had an ace up his sleeve.

He weighed the odds, then kaboom!

He carried a big stick and he used it. May our current leader love our young that much should all hell break loose!

PHYLLIS de LEON

Malibu
