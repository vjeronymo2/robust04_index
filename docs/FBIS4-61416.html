BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 5 TASS--Russian State Duma deputies attach great importance to the activities of the Parliamentary Assembly for the Black Sea Economic Cooperation, Valentin Kovalyov, deputy chairman of the State Duma and head of the Russian delegation participating in the parliamentary assembly, told a news conference on Thursday. The news conference was devoted to the results of the work of the committee for culture, education and social activities of the parliamentary assembly. This is the form of the parliamentary integration, Valentin Kovalyov stressed, which has prospects for the development of friendly relations between countries of the Black Sea basin, a spokesman for press service of the State Duma of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation told ITAR-TASS. The participants of the news conference urged to speed up the ratification of the convention on cooperation in the field of culture, education, science and information. In addition, the committee of the parliamentary assembly for the Black Sea economic cooperation plans to resolve the problem of the mutual recognition of diplomas and scientific degrees in member countries, organize more often creative meetings, book fairs, and widen and maintain contacts in the field of culture and education. Representatives of the committee told journalists that a resolution was adopted on Wednesday which proposed to give a new impetus to the program for historical study of the Black Sea basin.
