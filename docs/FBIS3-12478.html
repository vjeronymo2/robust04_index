Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The pursued Fatah hawks strugglers inside and outside the occupied territories have issued a statement saying: The heinous crime and the dastardly massacre perpetrated against our people's sons at the Ibrahimi Mosque, a house of God, in the blessed month of Ramadan is cogent proof of the impossibility of peace or coexistence with the enemies of God and mankind. The statement added: We, the pursued Fatah hawks inside and outside the occupied territories, demand that the 'Arafat leadership halt its negotiations with the Zionist enemy. Similarly, we demand all fighting Palestinian factions to unify themselves in the crucible of the revolution and armed struggle against enemy troops and the hordes of settlers, who are engaged in murder and terrorism against our Palestinian people, who are fighting the Zionist injustice and repression. Concluding their statement, the Fatah hawks said: Our people's dignity cannot be safeguarded unless we extricate our national rights by force, just as they have been usurped by force.
