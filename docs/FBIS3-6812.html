Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 21 (YONHAP) -- Army Chief of Staff Kim Tong-chin met with the Turkish Commander of Ground Forces, Ismail Hakki Karadayi, Monday morning and discussed military exchanges and cooperation between Seoul and Ankara. Karadayi, who arrived here on Sunday, will also meet with the defense minister and the chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. He will visit the Special Warfare Command and Changwon industrial complex before returning on Thursday.
