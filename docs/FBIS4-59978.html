BFN [Text] Lebanon today received an official invitation to participate in the fifth session of the multilateral talks Economic Development Working Group, which will meet in Morocco from 15 to 16 June. The invitation was conveyed to Foreign Ministry Secretary General Zafir al-Hasan by the Moroccan and Russian ambassadors. U.S. Charge d'Affaires Vincent Battle did not attend the meeting. A diplomatic source at the Bustrus Palace [Foreign Ministry premises] said Lebanon has reservations about participating in the meeting of any group emanating from the multilateral talks, because the bilateral talks have produced no tangible results.
