BFN [Text] The Palestine National Liberation Movement, Fatah, has asserted that the Jabalyah camp massacre is a premeditated murder meant to liquidate Fatah elements. In a statement distributed in the occupied territories yesterday, the Fatah Movement added: This new massacre will negatively affect the course of the negotiations. The Fatah Movement held the Israeli Government and the Israeli military establishment responsible for the massacres committed against our people. It reiterated that the six Palestinian victims were deliberately killed by the bullets of the terrorist Israeli undercover units.
