Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] A Pyongyang municipal report meeting to mark the 75th anniversary of the 1 March popular uprising in 1919 was held at the Central Workers' Hall on 28 February. [passage omitted] Present at the meeting were Comrade Yang Hyong-sop, candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly [SPA]; Comrade Pak Nam-ki, chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Administrative and Economic Committee; Yu Mi-yong, chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party; Chong Tu-hwan, chairman of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of Fatherland; Cho Song-il, chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea; Paek Nam-chun, director of the Secretariat of the Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of Fatherland; responsible functionaries of the sectors concerned; and workers of different strata in Pyongyang. The meeting began with the performance of national anthem. Chairman Chong Tu-hwan delivered a commemorative report at the meeting. [passage omitted]
