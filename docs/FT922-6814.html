920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Ralston Inv Trust At the March 31 year-end the net asset value per share of Ralston Investment Trust stood at 60.25p, compared with 63.25p a year earlier. Net revenue fell 18 per cent from Pounds 459,000 to Pounds 377,000. The trust said that the portfolio of listed equities rose in value but this was outweighed by the need to write-down certain unquoted holdings, the value of which was now Pounds 580,000. The final dividend is again 1.275p for a total of 2p (2p). Earnings were 1.57p (1.91p).
