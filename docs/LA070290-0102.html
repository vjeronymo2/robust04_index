July 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Chrysler to Raise Car Prices: Chrysler said it will raise prices on most of its 1990 passenger cars today.

The increases will start at $60 for Dodge Daytonas, which will have a base price of $9,805, and most Chrysler LeBarons, which will cost $12,555. The biggest planned increases are $160 on Chrysler Fifth Avenue and Chrysler Imperial, bringing their base prices to $21,020 and $25,655, respectively. The No. 3 auto maker said the boosts will cover higher operating costs.
