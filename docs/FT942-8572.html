940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / International Company News: Viva denies deal with Star TV Viva, the music company consortium, is believed to have had initial talks with Star TV, the Hong Kong-based satellite group, about buying part of a music channel, writes Michael Skapinker in London. However, it is believed no agreement has been reached. Members of the consortium dismissed reports that a HKDollars 350m (USDollars 45.3m) deal had been agreed with Star, which is controlled by Mr Rupert Murdoch's News Corporation.
