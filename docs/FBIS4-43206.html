BFN [Text] Seventeen members of the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terror organization who were involved in a murder case and various terrorist incidents have been captured in operations carried out in Izmir. According to information given by the Izmir Security Directorate's Antiterror Department, the suspects were involved in the murder of Mehmet Oztep in Karsiyaka, in throwing Molotov cocktails in various quarters of the city, and in hanging posters. Two revolvers, cartridges, medicine and medical equipment, and organizational documents have also been seized. The PKK's Karsiyaka regional official is among those captured.
