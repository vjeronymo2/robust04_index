Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 6 Jan -- Today Viktor Chernomyrdin, the chairman of the Russian Federation Government, met the leadership of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation, within the framework of consultations with leaders of electoral blocs. During the meeting they discussed prospects for the development of the Russian economy in 1994 and the progress of the implementation of the Russian Government program, approved by Boris Yeltsin, for the country's socioeconomic development in 1993-1995. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from the press service of the Russian Federation Government.
