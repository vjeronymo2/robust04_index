930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Romania loan tranche withheld The World Bank yesterday said it was withholding the final Dollars 150m of a Dollars 400m structural adjustment loan for Romania because of the country's poor economic performance, Reuter reports from Bucharest. The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has imposed delays on its lending to Romania for similar reasons. Last week the government's negotiations with the IMF stumbled when an IMF team left Bucharest without reaching a new standby credit agreement with Romania for 1993.
