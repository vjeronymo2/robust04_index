May 11, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The luncheon and fashion show on April 22 at the Newport Marriott raised an estimated $5,000 for the Boys Republic's scholarship fund. Marian Evans was chairwoman.

The fashion show on April 29 at the Forum Theater in Yorba Linda raised an estimated $3,000 for North Orange County Community College District Community Services. Gaye Krpan was chairwoman.

The "Une Soiree d'Art" dinner and auction on April 29 at the Newport Beach Marriott Hotel raised an estimated $30,000 for the Newport Harbor Auxiliary of Children's Home Society. Sandee Kerr was chairwoman.

The luncheon held at the Turnip Rose in Costa Mesa on May 3 raised an estimated $6,000 for the Guardian Angels of Human Options. Frances Applegate was chairwoman.

The Cinco de Mayo party at Koll Center Newport on May 5 raised an estimated $10,000 for the Cabaret chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performing Arts Center. Bob Carlson and Lynne Spencer were co-chairmen.

The dinner honoring restaurant critic and author Paul Wallachon May 7 at Le Meridien Hotel in Newport Beach raised an estimated $25,000 for the Sante chapter for the City of Hope. Bernard Jacoupy was chairman.
