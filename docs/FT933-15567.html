930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Miners set output record for week MINERS HAVE set a new productivity record as the industry copes with the effect of pit closures, figures showed today. Production last week at British Coal collieries hit a new peak of 8.82 tonnes per miner per shift. This beat the previous week's 8.50 tonnes, itself a record. Productivity at pits is 25 per cent higher than a year ago, according to British Coal.
