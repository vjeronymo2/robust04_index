940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Nadir associate visits police in London Elizabeth Forsyth, an associate of Asil Nadir, fugitive former head of Polly Peck International, visited the Serious Fraud Office in London to answer questions. She worked for South Audley Management, a company with close ties to Mr Nadir, and went to northern Cyprus before Mr Nadir fled there after breaking his bail conditions in May 1993.
