940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: London Share Service changes As from April 1 the following companies, which are investment trusts but which are not eligible for inclusion in the FT-SE Actuaries Share Indices, will be shown below the existing Investment Trusts grouping: Brazilian Investment Trust; East German Investment Trust; Korea Europe Fund; Korea Liberalisation Fund; Latin American Investment Trust; Mediterranean Fund; Oriental Smaller Companies Investment Trust Company; Schroder Korea Fund; and Scottish Asian Investment Company.
