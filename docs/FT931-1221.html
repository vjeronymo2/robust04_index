930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / International Company News: Jardine Strategic announces 12 per cent rise in net profits JARDINE Strategic, the investment holding company of Hong Kong's Jardine Matheson group, yesterday announced a 12 per cent rise in net profits to USDollars 262m for 1992, compared with Dollars 233m the year before. Turnover increased by Dollars 143m to Dollars 4.89bn, while net assets per share appreciated 24 per cent to Dollars 4.29.
