July 28, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

SANTA CLARITA COUNCIL VOTES TO HIRE LOBBYIST

The Santa Clarita City Council, saying it wants to build up the young city's political muscle, has hired a lobbyist to represent the city in Los Angeles, Sacramento and -- if need be -- Washington.

The council agreed Tuesday night to retain Capital Representation Group, a Sacramento-based firm, for $5,000 a month.

One of the first issues the lobbyists will tackle will be proposals by a refuse firm and the city and county of Los Angeles to open a dump in Elsmere Canyon, two miles east of Santa Clarita.
