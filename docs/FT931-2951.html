930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Scapa's Pounds 5m French buy from Canning Scapa Group, the international industrial materials company, has signed an agreement with Canning to acquire its wholly-owned subsidiary SABA (Societe des Adhesifs de Bellegarde Ain). The purchase price is about Pounds 5m which will come from Scapa's existing cash resources. Completion is due on April 2.
