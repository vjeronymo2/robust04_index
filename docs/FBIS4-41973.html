BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Bychkov] [Text] Moscow June 30 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Indian Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao began a meeting in close circle on Thursday. They are assisted only by experts and translators. Addressing the Indian guest before the Kremlin meeting, Yeltsin expressed hope that the talks on a wide range of bilateral relations would be successful.
