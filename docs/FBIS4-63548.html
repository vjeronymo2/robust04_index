BFN [Text] As of 20 May in Lvov (West Ukraine), Ukrainian nationalists have been manning groups of volunteers to be subsequently dispatched to Crimea, a Ukrainian lawmaker Oleg Vitovich, in charge of the Lvov branch of the Ukrainian National Self-Defense Assembly, has told INTERFAX. Asked about the goals of these groups, he disclosed that they "will advocate national independence and territorial integrity for Ukraine." By his estimates, more than 200 men have volunteered to be sent to Crimea.
