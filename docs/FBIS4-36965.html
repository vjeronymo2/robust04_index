BFN [Text] Police Minister Moshe Shahal has announced that Palestinian women, regardless of age, and youths up to 16 years old can enter Israel freely again. The move eased an entry ban imposed on Palestinians from the territories following the wave of bombings in early April. Shahal said he hoped the move would lead to a return of normal life but this would depend on the Palestinian side. A Qol Yisra'el reporter adds that despite the closure of the territories, police have been arresting several hundred Palestinians weekly for illegal entry into Israel.
