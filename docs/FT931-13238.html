930125 FT 25 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Mixed batch ALFRED McALPINE BUILDING has won contracts totalling Pounds 30m. They include the redevelopment of Huddersfield Football Stadium (Pounds 10m), the redevelopment of a Marks & Spencer store at Middlesborough (Pounds 4m), the construction of a teaching facility at Wolverhampton University (Pounds 4m) and a social housing refurbishment in Plymouth (Pounds 5m).
