BFN [Text] The Croatian Parliament's Chamber of Counties has resumed its work. It is debating a draft law on income taxation. The House of Counties is also expected to discuss the remaining nine points on its agenda today. Opposition deputies are not taking part in today's session, which is scheduled to begin at 1300. The interparty consultations are expected to begin at 1130. We have learned unofficially from Deputy Ivan Matija that the opposition will suggest to the HDZ [Croatian Democratic Community] at these consultations that a parliament inquiry should be launched into a letter allegedly sent to the U.S. Congress by Mesic and Manolic.
