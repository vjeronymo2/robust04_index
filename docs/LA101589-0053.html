October 15, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WHAT HAPPENED TO THE EXECUTIVES AT U.S. STEEL?

Your story about the troubled Monongahela River Valley steelworkers needs a follow-up ("The Struggle to Rebuild After Big Steel Moved Out" Sept. 27).

What happened to the top managers, the chairman of the board, and the directors of U.S. Steel who made the decisions, years ago, which resulted in the losses of jobs for steelworkers and the closing of plants? I'll bet few of them were racked economically.

R.C. CZAPIEWSKI

North Hollywood
