Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed news agency brief] [Text] Sochi -- Yet another person infected with the AIDS virus has been discovered in the city. He turned out to be a citizen of the United States working at one of Sochi's joint ventures. It is known that the American sought the services of Sochi stomatologists. In the opinion of Nikolay Kochetkov, director of the Sochi Center for the Fight Against AIDS, the American was infected while still in his homeland. This is the 11th AIDS patient discovered in Sochi.
