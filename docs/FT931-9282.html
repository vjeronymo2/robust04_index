930215 FT 15 FEB 93 / Observer: Last ditch Proclaiming himself sure to win the coming international rally, an ace Swedish driver told reporters he'd worked out that nine in every 10 bends on the course could be taken far faster than his competitors would dare. So he'd just go flat out all the time. 'But what happens on the 10th bend that can't be taken so fast?' a pressman asked. Surprised, the driver thought for a moment then answered: 'I crash, I suppose.'
