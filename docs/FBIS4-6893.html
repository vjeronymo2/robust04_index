BFN [Text] In a letter sent to Robert Gallucci, U.S. assistant secretary of State, Kang Sok-chu, North Korean vice foreign minister, proposed a working-level contact to prepare for the third round of North Korean-U.S. high-level talks. This was revealed by a relevant government official on 22 April. On 22 April, the government official said that "Vice Minister Kang sent a letter to Assistant Secretary Gallucci on 19 April and revealed the willingness to allow the presence of an inspection team from the International Atomic Energy Agency when nuclear fuel rods are replaced at a five-megawatt nuclear reactor in Yongbyon, and proposed holding a working-level contact." The government official then added that "the working-level contact is expected to be held during next week."
