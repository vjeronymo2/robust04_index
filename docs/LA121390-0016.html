December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

GUN BENEFIT PARTICIPANTS

I was shocked and hurt by the letters (Dec. 2) about my niece Sasha Graves and our friends, the Gun Owners REACT Committee.

They said Sasha was crying when Michael McNulty tried to help her pop some balloons with a pellet gun.

Sasha didn't cry any time during her visit at the gun range.

Sasha's reaction in the picture with your article was shyness when all those reporters crowded around her, and she couldn't see through the sight so she scrunched up her eyes and rubbed them after trying.

Why didn't you use the picture you took of Sasha waving at all the folks when they introduced her to the crowd? She was really smiling when they cheered for her, and with the help from the gun owners she is smiling now.

DEIRDRE POPE

Long Beach
