940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: 130 feared dead as Boeing crashes near Pittsburgh Up to 130 people were feared killed when a USAir Boeing 737 crashed near Pittsburgh international airport, Pennsylvania. Pieces of the aircraft and baggage were scattered throughout a wooded area. A witness said the Boeing was flying low when 'its engines suddenly died and there was a big boom. There was black smoke, nothing was left of the plane, it just blew up'.
