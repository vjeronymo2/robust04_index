BFN [Text] During a 31 May session of the House of Representatives Budget Committee, Yoshimi Mino of the opposition Social Democratic Party of Japan indicated, "North Korea has a feeling that the UN Security Council and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) are under the control of the United States," and he then asked, "Does the DPRK not aim to negotiate with the United States for a normalization of relations between the two countries?" Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa replied, "The DPRK appears to be intending to settle the issue of the replacement of fuel rods at the experimental nuclear reactor in North Korea through talks with the United States." The foreign minister also stated, "I consider what Mr. Mino just indicated to be our view."
