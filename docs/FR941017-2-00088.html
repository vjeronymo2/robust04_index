The fourth measure is Executions in Size Greater than BBO, which measures the number of market and marketable limit orders which exceed ether BBO size and are executed in size larger than the BBO size. Thus if a specialist receives a total of 10 market and marketable limit orders which exceed the BBO size and executes nine of the orders in size larger than the BBO size, his/her raw score would be 90% and would receive 8 points since it falls in the 86 to 90 percent range. This calculation will comprise 25% of the overall evaluation program.

Statutory Basis The basis under the Act for the proposed rule change is Section 6(b)(5) in that the SPEP results weigh heavily in stock allocation decisions and, as a result, specialists are encouraged to improve their market quality and administrative duties, thereby promoting just and equitable principles of trade and aiding in the perfection of a free and open market and a natural market system. B. Self-Regulatory Organization's Statement on Burden on Competition No burden on competition is perceived by the adoption of the proposed rule change.
