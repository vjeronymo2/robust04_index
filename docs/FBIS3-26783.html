Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 12 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese Foreign Ministry today lodged a protest to the Indonesian and Philippine envoys here against the two countries allowing visit and stopover by "high-ranking officials" from Taiwan, including Li Teng-hui. On February 9, Li and other "high-ranking officials" from Taiwan arrived at Bali island of Indonesia for a "vacational visit" and on February 11, Indonesian President Suharto met with Li on the island, according to sources of the Foreign Ministry. On his way to Indonesia, Li stopped over at Subic and met with Philippine President Fidel Ramos, the sources said.
