930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / International Company News: Pepsi in China deal PEPSI-COLA, the US soft drinks business, and Asia Beverage Company, based in China's Guangdong province, are forming a joint venture to produce and market Asia Beverage's soft drinks throughout China, writes Nikki Tait. Pepsi will hold the majority share - 60 per cent - in the venture company.
