910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Revenue higher at Albany Investment Albany Investment Trust saw net asset value increase by 3.56p to 105.49p over the year to end-February. Net revenue improved 8.5 per cent from Pounds 377,000 to Pounds 409,000. Earnings per share came out at 4.08p (3.76p). A recommended final dividend of 2.5p makes a total for the year of 3.7p (3.4p).
