931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Phoenix Timber cuts deficit Losses at Phoenix Timber, the flooring group, were trimmed from Pounds 583,000 to Pounds 525,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. Turnover of Pounds 9.57m compared with Pounds 10.5m. Interest payable accounted for Pounds 262,000 (Pounds 309,000) and losses per share worked through at 3.6p (4p). Directors said the refinancing programme has been successfully completed and banking facilities newly agreed.
