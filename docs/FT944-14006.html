941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Powell Duffryn Powell Duffryn has expanded its fuel distribution interests with the purchase of MB Gas, the UK downstream LPG distribution arm of Elf Antargaz of France. The price was not disclosed, but Powell said there had been no payment in respect of goodwill. Net assets at completion were Pounds 5m. MB Gas has annual sales of Pounds 4.5m.
