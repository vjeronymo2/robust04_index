(f) Business background . (1) The business background, for the five years preceding the date of the disclosure document, of: (i) The commodity pool operator, the pool's trading manager, if any, each major commodity trading advisor and the operator of each major investee pool; and (ii) Each principal of the foregoing persons who participates in making trading or operational decisions for the pool or supervises persons so engaged. (2) The pool operator must include in the description of the business background of each such person the name and main business of that person's employers, business associations or business ventures and the nature of the duties performed by such person for the employers or in connection with the associations or ventures. (g) Principal risk factors. As applicable, a discussion of the principal risk factors of this investment. The pool operator must disclose: (1) The types of commodity interests or other interests the commodity pool operator intends that the pool will hold or trade, with a description of the trading program that will be followed and any restrictions or limitations on such interests or trading required by the pool's organizational documents or otherwise.
