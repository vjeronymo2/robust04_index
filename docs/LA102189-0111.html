October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COMMENTS ON THE TIMES' NEW FORMAT

Boy, you really got organized! The paper's never looked better, been more comprehensive or easier to read.

Having been a subscriber for almost 20 years, I'm more impressed than ever before. It really makes a terrific difference. I read the paper at breakfast every morning and this makes it much more enjoyable.

ANNE KEMPNER FISHER

Los Angeles
