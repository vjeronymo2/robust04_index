930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / International Company News: Spain extends credit facility THE Kingdom of Spain is to increase its recently-announced borrowing facility to Ecu5bn from Ecu4bn, reflecting the willingness of banks to participate in the deal, Sara Webb writes. The three-year, multi-currency revolving credit facility is the largest financing to be put together by a European government this year. It was arranged by NatWest Capital Markets.
