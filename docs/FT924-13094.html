921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / UN given a new voice Mr Francois Giuliani, who has served as principal spokesman for three UN secretaries-general, including the incumbent Mr Boutros Boutros Ghali, is being replaced, writes Michael Littlejohns, UN Correspondent, in New York. Mr Joe Sills, who served as his deputy before taking on other media responsibilities, will replace him on Monday. Also leaving is Ms Nadia Younes, the current deputy. Mr Giuliani described his transfer to another high UN post as 'amicable'. Mr Sills, a former vice-president of the UN Association of the United States, is highly regarded by reporters.
