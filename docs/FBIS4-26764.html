BFN [Text] Yue Qifeng, secretary of the provincial party committee, went to the headquarters of the provincial people's armed police force to call on Lieutenant General Ba Zhongtan, commander of Chinese People's Armed Police Force, and his entourage on the afternoon of 14 June, who were making an inspection tour in the province. During the visit, Yue Qifeng spoke highly of the achievements scored by the provincial people's armed police force since its founding. Ba Zhongtan extended heartfelt thanks for the concern and support given to the people's armed police force by the provincial party committee and the provincial people's government. Commander Ba Zhongtan and his entourage arrived in Harbin on the morning of 13 June. On the afternoon of 13 June Shan Rongfan, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee; Xu Yandong, assistant to governor; and leading personnel from the provincial public security department; went to the Huayuancun Hotel to call on Commander Ba Zhongtan and his entourage. During their stay in Harbin, Ba Zhongtan and his entourage inspected the fifth armed police force detachment, which is in charge of mobile units. They paid tribute to the strict management, assiduous training, and rapid reaction of the detachment. During the forum with the police officers and fighters, Commander Ba Zhongtan repeatedly urged them to enhance their study; to safeguard stability; and to become loyal guards who are cherished by the people and feared by enemies.
