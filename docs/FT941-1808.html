940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / China's inflation rate rises Figures indicating a rise in China's inflation rate were published in the Chinese press yesterday, our Foreign Staff writes. The reports said retail prices in the first two months rose 20 per cent nationwide over the same period last year, against 13 per cent for all of 1993. The State Statistical Bureau report showed industrial output grew by 18.2 per cent in the first two months; capital construction surged 41.8 per cent in the same period.
