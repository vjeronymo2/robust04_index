930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / International Company News: Northern Telecom in tie-up with US group NORTHERN Telecom of Canada and Bell Atlantic of the US have formed a partnership to market and service private telecommunications systems, primarily in the mid-Atlantic region of the US, Reuter reports from Nashville. The Canadian telecoms group said it would have a majority interest and a managing role in the partnership. Other terms were not disclosed. The partnership, Bell Atlantic Meridian Systems, has acquired substantially all the assets of Bell Atlanticom Systems, a Bell Atlantic subsidiary. Northern Telecom said the 800 or so Bell Atlanticom employees would join Bell Atlantic Meridian Systems. The partnership will sell and service Northern Telecom's Meridian 1 private branch exchange systems and Meridian Norstar key systems, as well as most other product lines supported by Bell Atlanticom. Northern Telecom has similar partnerships with Nynex and Pacific Telesis of the US.
