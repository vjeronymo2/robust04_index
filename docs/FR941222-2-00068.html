[ES&hyph;960&hyph;4730&hyph;02; ES&hyph;046131, Group 60, Louisiana Notice of Filing of Plat of Dependent Resurvey, Suspended On Thursday, June 10, 1993, there was published in the Federal Register, Volume 58, on page 32546, a notice entitled, ``Louisiana: Filing of Plat of Dependent Resurvey.'' Said notice referenced the filing of the plat of the dependent resurvey of the west boundary of Township 7 South, Range 9 East, Louisiana Meridian, Louisiana, accepted May 24, 1993. This plat officially filed on July 22, 1993, is hereby suspended pending the consideration of a protest against the survey. Dated: December 14, 1994. Carson W. Culp, Jr., State Director.
