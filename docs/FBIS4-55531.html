BFN [Text] After visiting Russian troops serving with the UN Protection Forces in Sector East, Russian Ambassador to Croatia Leonid Kerestedziyants told reporters that they discussed the implementation of the Zagreb agreement and agreed that the Croatian Army must withdraw the remaining weapons by 1600 tomorrow and the Serbian side must do the same by 2400 tomorrow. The Zagreb agreement has to be fully implemented in all protected areas by 2400 tomorrow, the Russian ambassador said. If this does not happen further steps will be taken by both the Russian and U.S. embassies and the European Union, for what has been signed has to be respected. This is not a piece of paper which has been signed and can then be forgotten, he said.
