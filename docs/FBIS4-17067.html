BFN [Text] The press service of the president of Ingushetia has reported today that President Aushev has appointed a new deputy. He is Petr Kosov, first deputy ataman [warlord] of the Union of Cossacks of the Don Army region. According to INTERFAX, the new aide will become one of the main figures in coordinating the activities of the Cossacks in southern Russia and North Caucasian mountain republics.
