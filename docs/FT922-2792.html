920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Arts: York Early Music Festival There is a strong Italian flavour to this year's York Early Music Festival from June 25 to 28, with the British debut of the Italian madrigal group Fosco Corti (June 24 and 25 at the Guildhall, 3.00pm) and music from 16th and 17th century Italy. The festival takes place in medieval churches, guildhalls and concert halls throughout York. Tickets from: Festival Office, PO Box 226, York, North Yorkshire, YO3 6ZU. (0904 658338).
