BFN [Text] Baku, 6 May (AZERTAC)--Fighting was going on in the zone of the conflict between Armenia and Azerbaijan yesterday. According to the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry, the most heavy fighting took place in Agdamskiy, Terterskiy and Geranboy [Shaumyanovskiy] where the Armenians were trying to fight their way through the Azeri defences. The Azeri National Army has repulsed the enemy's attacks. The Armenians have suffered heavy casualties.
