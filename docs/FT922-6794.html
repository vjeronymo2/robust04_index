920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / International Capital Markets: Alcatel Alsthom in NYSE listing ALCATEL Alsthom, the French telecommunications and engineering group, has become the first privatised French company to get a full listing on the New York Stock Exchange with a Dollars 236m share sale in the US, writes Richard Waters. The company has issued 10m American Depository Shares, five of which are equivalent to one Alcatel share, at a price of Dollars 23 5/8 per ADS. The company had raised a total of Dollars 184m equity in the US before, through private placements in 1987 and 1990.
