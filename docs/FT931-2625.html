930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Rea Brothers jumps to Pounds 805,000 Rea Brothers, the banking group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 261,000 to Pounds 805,000 in 1992. Earnings per share amounted to 0.82p against losses of 0.42p and the proposed unchanged final dividend of 0.25p holds the total at 0.5p.
