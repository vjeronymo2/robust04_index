930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / International Company News: Atreus subsidiaries meet expectations Atreus, the shower screen and mirror supplier which obtained a listing in March via the reverse takeover of URS International, has announced results of its trading subsidiaries for the year to January 31. The subsidiaries, the DB (UK) group, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.11m compared with the estimated Pounds 1.1m given in the listing document. Turnover was Pounds 6.23m against the estimated Pounds 6.19m. Atreus, which has a March 31 year end, acquired DB for Pounds 5.5m in loan notes and shares on March 26 1993 and at the same sold URS to Howmac for Pounds 1. Results of the enlarged group for the half year to September 30 will be published in December.
