940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Manila 'drug police' claim Ten per cent of metropolitan Manila's police force are protectors or members of drug syndicates, the Manila newspaper Today quoted Police Director Ricardo Sarmiento as saying yesterday, Reuter reports from Manila. It claimed he said some were involved with Asian drug rings, including Hong Kong's 14K Triad. All members of the country's police force were ordered to undergo drug tests, to fight the rising number said to be using drugs.
