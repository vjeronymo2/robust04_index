920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Wills recovery continues Wills Group, the engineering equipment distributor, continued its improvement following reconstruction. After a return to profits of Pounds 154,000 at the halfway stage, the pre-tax figure for the year to June 30 came out at Pounds 519,000 on turnover of Pounds 15m. In the 18 months to June 30 1991, a Pounds 1.49m loss was incurred on sales of Pounds 22.7m. The board is proposing to return to the dividend list for the first time in four years with a final of 0.1p. Earnings were 0.77p basic (losses 17.7p) or 0.64p fully diluted.
