920429 FT 29 APR 92 / UK Company News: Currency exchange lift for Sennah Sennah Rubber, an investment company with holdings in tropical plantations and financial services companies, reported profits of Pounds 824,879 pre-tax for 1991. The increase from the previous year's Pounds 767,352 came despite lower gross income of Pounds 970,467 (Pounds 1.05m) and reflected a turnround to profits on currency exchange. Earnings per share worked through at 1.2p (1.09p) and the single distribution for the year is held at 0.5p.
