941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / People: Spirax-Sarco Engineering At Spirax-Sarco Engineering, the Cheltenham-based steam equipment specialist, Chris Tappin has retired as executive chairman but will remain part-time chairman. Tim Fortune, who was named managing director in 1992, became chief executive on November 1. Tappin, 57, said the change was 'one little click further in our succession planning'. Tappin joined Spirax-Sarco in 1964 and was appointed to the board two years later. From 1988 to 1992 he was chairman and chief executive.
