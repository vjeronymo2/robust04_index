Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi hosted a working dinner this evening in honor of British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd. It was attended by Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz, and the British ambassador to Lebanon. Earlier, the British official held a round of talks with Foreign Minister Buwayz at Bustrus Palace [Foreign Ministry]. Their discussion touched on the peace talks, developments in the region, and the participation of specialized British firms in the reconstruction of Lebanon. In a joint news conference with Minister Buwayz, Hurd expressed Britain's support for UN Resolution 425. He praised the Lebanese people's efforts to rebuild their country. He pointed out that he is not mediating the peace process, but he noted his country's interest in facilitating the peace process on all tracks. Hurd viewed the Lebanese-Syrian coordination in the peace process as a source of momentum for this process. The British secretary is scheduled to leave Beirut tonight for occupied Jerusalem, the second leg of his Middle East tour, which will also take him to Jordan.
