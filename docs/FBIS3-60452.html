Language: English Article Type:CSO [Text] Ahmedabad, Nov 23--The entire walled city of Ahmedabad has been declared jaundice-affected and its suburbs face the threat of the epidemic, which has affected at least 380 persons and claimed three lives. The declaration to be gazetted empowers the health authorities to force residents replace leaky domestic water supply lines, the root-cause for the outbreak of the epidemic, contamination of water supply by sewage.
