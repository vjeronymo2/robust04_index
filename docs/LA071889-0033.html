July 18, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

22 WOMEN TO COMPETE IN GOLF QUALIFYING

Qualifying for the United States Golf Assn. Women's Amateur Championships will take place today at Rancho Santa Fe Golf Club at 7:45 a.m.

Twenty-two women with handicaps of five or better will compete for eight places. The national tournament will be in Pinehurst, N.C., beginning July 31.
