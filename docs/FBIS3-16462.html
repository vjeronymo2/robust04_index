Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 15 TASS -- Former U.S. President Richard Nixon met Tuesday with Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Russian nationalist "Liberal Democratic" party. The meeting was held behind closed doors. The press service of Zhirinovskiy's party told ITAR-TASS that the two politicians focused on Russian-American relations "taking into consideration the geopolitical interests of the two countries", and on inter-parliamentary contacts. Zhirinovskiy handed to Nixon a personal message addressed to President Bill Clinton, the press service said. According to press reports, Nixon wanted to meet Zhirinovskiy during his week-long visit to Moscow to get a clearer idea of the broad spectrum of Russian political leaders capable of influencing developments in the country. Zhirinovskiy, however, denied last week that he wanted to meet the American ex-president.
