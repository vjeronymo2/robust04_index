June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CARTOON OPERAS

Martin Bernheimer's review ("Some Sonic Sendak at the Music Center," June 9) of the two cartoonish operas current at the Music Center Opera, "Higglety-Pigglety Pop!" and "Where the Wild Things Are," while typically (for Bernheimer) snide and oh-so superior, misses what I consider the main objection to this repertory.

Lest I create the impression I am a wizened reactionary, let me say that I'm in my mid-30s and a relative newcomer to opera. But this season I did not purchase season tickets. I have no interest in cartoons to music or tired musicals. Los Angeles for many years had no indigenous professional opera. Now that we do, couldn't they stick to the real thing?

DAVID STUDHALTER

North Hollywood
