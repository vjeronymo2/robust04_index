BFN [Text] The security forces have detained two Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] leaders from the Gaza Strip -- Nidal Ruhani and Ghassan al-Siyam -- and fifteen activists from 'Izz-al-Din al- Qassam, the military arm of Hamas. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that in an operation in the al-Shati' refugee camp and Gaza city, which lasted several days and nights, the security forces detained Nidal Ruhani and Ghassan al-Siyam, and 15 more Hamas members were detained in the course of the two men's interrogation. Following a search of their homes, large sums of money, documents, and other materiel were seized, and it was learned that they were key elements in the Hamas operations against the security forces in the Strip. Brigadier General Doron Almog, the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commander in the Gaza Strip, commended the Shin Bet personnel and the other security forces for the capture of the Hamas members.
