Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 2 Mar (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak has reiterated Egypt's denunciation of the massacre at the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron, which he described as the epitome of cruelty and extremism. President Mubarak noted that he is following up the situation and the contacts held with U.S. President Bill Clinton, Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat, and the Israeli president and prime minister. During an expanded meeting held at the presidential office today, President Mubarak stressed that Egypt urges the international community to adopt a clear-cut decision to back the Palestinian people's right to protection under international law, disarm the settlers, and take an interest in the security requirements of the Palestinian people to ensure the security and safety of Palestinian civilians. Following the three-hour meeting, Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif stated that its purpose was to discuss domestic affairs on which the president gave instructions at an earlier meeting.
