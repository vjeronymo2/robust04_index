BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has arrived in Donetsk oblast on a working visit to discuss the problems in the coalmining and metallurgical sectors with miners, (?chemical), and metallurgy industry workers. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned that Leonid Kravchuk is also to hold a brief meeting behind closed doors with his representatives from Dnepropetrovsk, Donetsk, Zaporozhye, and Lugansk oblasts.
