March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JEOPARDY!

Regarding "Backstage at 'Jeopardy!' " (by Patricia Ward Biederman, Jan. 29): I was a one-time champion on "Jeopardy!" last March, winning $12,801, a consolation-prize armoire and color TV, and a headful of golden memories. People ask me all the time what the experience was like, and rather than talk myself hoarse, I may just lend them your article, because you really captured it beautifully.

LESLIE GOODMAN-MALAMUTH

Washington, D.C.
