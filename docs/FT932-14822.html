930416 FT 16 APR 93 / UK Company News: Kingspan tumbles to IPounds 1.17m Kingspan Group, the Co Cavan-based building components manufacturer, saw pre-tax profits drop from IPounds 2.56m to IPounds 1.17m over the year to December 31. Turnover was IPounds 53.1m (IPounds 56m). Earnings per share fell from 7.5p to 3p, but the final dividend is held at 1.2p for a maintained total of 2.2p.
