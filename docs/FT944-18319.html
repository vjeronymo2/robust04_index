941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: London & Assoc Inv Pre-tax profits at London & Associated Investment Trust, the property investment company, were 21 per cent ahead for the half year to June 30, from Pounds 713,000 to Pounds 865,000. Turnover from property and listed investments rose 16 per cent to Pounds 2.54m (Pounds 2.2m). The company's net assets increased by 27 per cent to Pounds 35.8m. Earnings per share were 0.74p, compared with 0.64p, and an interim dividend of 0.05p is maintained.
