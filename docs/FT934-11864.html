931029 FT 29 OCT 93 / Call for two-point interest rate cut INTEREST rates should be cut by up to 2 points on Budget day to underpin recovery in the housing market, the National Association of Estate Agents said yesterday. It also urged the government to postpone any plans to cut mortgage interest tax relief and called for tax incentives for landlords to stimulate the market for rented homes. British Midland, the airline, said it would lose Pounds 24m in revenues if valued added tax were put on domestic air travel. Sir Michael Bishop, chairman, said more than 10 per cent of its scheduled passengers would not fly and warned of 'significant job losses' as a result.
