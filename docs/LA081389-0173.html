August 13, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

LYON'S DEALING WITH HUD ON RANCH PROJECT

Maj. Gen. William Lyon is a man of immense integrity. His business savvy and ability to negotiate are second to none. He certainly does not need to seek unfair advantage in his business dealings.

As one who has worked with him on community and political projects, I can assure you he is unwilling to bend his own standards for short-term gain. He does not compromise his integrity to win. Your articles failed to emphasize the premium value of the William Lyon Co.'s all-cash offer over a leveraged deal.

Finally, it is critical that the people of Orange County know the huge contribution that Gen. and Mrs. Lyon have made not only to the political process and our national security but to opportunities for children, to the arts, to health, to education, to law enforcement and to a myriad of other activities that make our county so great. It could be said that Bill Lyon has positively impacted and made a difference in the life of each and every Orange Countian.

When this current matter is cleared up, I hope you will put that story on the front page.

NATHAN OWEN ROSENBERG

Corona del Mar
