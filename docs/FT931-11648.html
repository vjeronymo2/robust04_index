930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / World Trade News: Ericsson expands Japanese presence Ericsson, the Swedish telecommunications group, has further increased its presence in Japan through a Dollars 70m contract with Kansai Digital Phone Co for the expansion of the mobile telephone system in the Osaka region, Christopher Brown-Humes reports from Stockholm. The order follows an earlier SKr600m (Pounds 55m) contract from the same Japanese company and will approximately double the subscriber capacity of 75,000 envisaged under the first deal.
