931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: E & G sales raise Pounds 15m Estates & General has sold two properties from its central London investment portfolio, raising more than Pounds 15m. The premises at 20-24 Broadwick Street has been sold for Pounds 9.5m, and Gaywood House, 29 Great Peter Street, was sold for Pounds 5.4m.
