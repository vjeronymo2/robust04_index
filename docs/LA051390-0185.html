May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TIS JULIET WINS SHUVEE HANDICAP

Calumet Farm's Tis Juliet caught favored Survive in midstretch and went on to a 3 1/4-length victory in the $171,300 Shuvee Handicap at Belmont Park Saturday.

Carrying 113 pounds and Richard Migliore, Tis Juliet covered 1 1-16 miles on a fast track in 1:43.

Survive finished second in the seven-horse field, three lengths ahead of Dreamy Mimi.

Tis Juliet's victory was her third in seven starts this year.
