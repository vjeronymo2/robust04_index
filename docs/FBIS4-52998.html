BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, received a gift from a high-level delegation of the Ministry of National Defence of Laos on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to member of the Political Bureau of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army Choe Kwang by member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and Minister of National Defence Sai-ngason Choummali.
