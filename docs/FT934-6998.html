931122 FT 22 NOV 93 / International Company News: Stagecoach makes Pounds 4.4m bus buy Stagecoach Holdings, the Perth-based regional bus service operator, floated in April, has acquired all the issued share capital of Grimsby-Cleethorpes Transport for Pounds 4.4m in cash. In addition, a pre-acquisition dividend of Pounds 300,000, financed by Stagecoach, has been paid to the vendors.
