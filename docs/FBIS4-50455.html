BFN [By Central People's Broadcasting Station reproter Liu Zhenying (0491 2182 5391) and XINHUA reporter Ding Jianming (0002 1017 6900)] [Excerpts] Beijing, 29 Apr (XINHUA) -- The State Council held a teleconference at Zhongnanhai this afternoon in order to ensure that further efforts will be made to do a good job in issuing state treasury bonds. Luo Gan, state councillor and secretary general of the State Council, spoke at the conference. Luo Gan said: A good start has been made in issuing the two-year term and three-year term state treasury bonds this year. [passage omitted] According to statistics, as of 27 April, 41.6 billion yuan worth of treasury bonds had been sold throughout the country, representing 47.8 percent of the planned total number of bonds to be issued. The figure included 15.8 billion yuan worth of two-year term state treasury bonds and 25.8 billion yuan worth of three-year term state treasury bonds. [passage omitted] Luo Gan said: The issuance of state treasury bonds this year is very significant. It concerns the overall situation of reform, development, and stability. To maintain a sustained, rapid, and healthy development of the national economy, safeguard political stability, and promote overall social progress, we must fulfill our financial plans, especially the plan for the issuance of state treasury bonds, according to schedule this year. Luo Gan pointed out: We have done pretty well in issuing state treasury bonds in the past month, but to fulfill this year's plan we still have arduous tasks to do. The State Council believes that at present, governments at all levels should lose no time in concentrating on work that is related to the issuance of state treasury bonds. [passage omitted] Vice Minister of Finance Xiang Huaicheng and Vice President of the People's Bank of China Dai Xianglong spoke at the teleconference. They endorsed the guidelines contained in Luo Gan's speech and called on financial and monetary departments at all levels to further do a good job in issuing state treasury bonds. The conference was presided over by He Chunlin, deputy secretary general of the State Council. Finance Minister Liu Zhongli and leaders of relevant departments under the State Council and major monetary organizations attended the conference.
