940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: I&S UK Smaller beats benchmarks Ivory & Sime UK Smaller Companies Trust lifted its fully diluted net asset value by 16 per cent - from 97.08p to 112.39p - over the 12 months to March 31. The trust showed a rise in net asset value of 8 per cent over its second half, beating the FT-SE SmallCap Index (excluding investment trusts) and the FT-SE-A All-Share Index, up 7 per cent and 3.7 per cent respectively over the same period.
