Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Own Information" report under the "News" rubric: "Session of Council on Military-Technical Policy"] [Text] Moscow -- A session of the working group of the Council on Military-Technical Policy under the Russian Federation Ministry of Defense has been held at the Ministry of Defense under the chairmanship of First Deputy Minister Andrey Kokoshin. Major defense industry scientists, experts, and representatives of military science took part in the session. In accordance with the Security Council decision "On Programs To Develop and Manufacture Promising Types of Weapons and Military Hardware" dated 19 January 1994, an amplified draft of a long-term weapons program was examined.
