941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / Observer: Foolgood factor Camelot, the operator of the UK's National Lottery, says it's 'absolutely delighted' with ticket sales of about Pounds 7m a day. How to contain the euphoria over the next seven years as it moves towards its Pounds 32bn target? Easy. To reach that figure Camelot will need to average not Pounds 7m but Pounds 12.5m in sales every day of the year.
