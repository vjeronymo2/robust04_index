Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov, commenting in an interview with Interfax on the results of yesterday's referendum among those who live in the Crimea, has said that they confirm the correctness of the course chosen by the Crimean leadership. First we must act upon the results of the referendum, he said. I rely on myself, my administration, on the support of the Black Sea Fleet that the results of the poll will be put into force, he said in an interview with Interfax.
