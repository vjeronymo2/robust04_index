BFN [Text] Three important and strategic enemy positions in Dar-e Kayhan, Baghlan Province, have fallen into the hands of the Mojahedin. Security sources report that as a result of successful operations carried out today, three strategic positions in Dar-e Kayhan were captured from the militia. Another enemy stronghold at Zendanak was captured in another successful Mojahedin operation. In addition, as a result of these activities, which were led by Commander 'Abdol Wali, Commander Baz Muhammad, and Commander 'Abdol Ahad, 12 militias were killed and a large depot containing various types of weapons were seized by the security forces. The activities of the Islamic Army are continuing in these regions.
