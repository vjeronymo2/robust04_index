920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / International Company News: National Semi edges ahead in first quarter NATIONAL Semiconductor, the US semiconductor chip manufacturer, reported slightly higher than expected first-quarter earnings and a 15 per cent increase in sales. Net earnings for the quarter were Dollars 21.9m, or 17 cents per share, compared with a loss of Dollars 168m, or Dollars 1.64 per share in the same period last year when the company took a restructuring charge of Dollars 149.3m as it consolidated manufacturing operations. Sales for the quarter rose to Dollars 472.4m from Dollars 411.0m a year earlier. 'Earnings for the first quarter reflect on-going improvement in profitability as we continue our business turnaround activities,' said Mr Gilbert Amelio, president and chief executive. 'We have now had four consecutive quarters of profitability.' The company said that its manufacturing lines are now working at approximately 79 per cent of capacity, up from about 70 per cent a year ago.
