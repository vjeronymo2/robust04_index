FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS COMMISSION 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 91&hyph;303; RM&hyph;6707, RM&hyph;6799, RM&hyph;7754, RM&hyph;7813, RM&hyph;7996, RM&hyph;7997, RM&hyph;7998 and RM&hyph;7999] Radio Broadcasting Services; Butler, Evergreen and Jackson, AL, and Bay Springs, MS AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This document makes changes to the FM Table of Allotments in response to petitions filed by Butler Broadcasting Corp., Cotton Valley Broadcasting Co. and Wolff Broadcasting Corporation. See 56 FR 55648, October 29, 1991. We shall substitute Channel 228C2 for Channel 228A, Butler, Alabama, at coordinates 32&hyph;08&hyph;10 and 88&hyph;15&hyph;00; substitute Channel 232A for Channel 228A, Bay Springs, Mississippi, at coordinates 31&hyph;59&hyph;00 and 89&hyph;13&hyph;50; and substitute Channel 227C1 for Channel 227C2, Evergreen, Alabama, at coordinates 31&hyph;14&hyph;48 and 86&hyph;48&hyph;32. In response to a counterproposal filed by Radio Station WHOD, Inc., we will substitute Channel 233C2 for Channel 233C3 at Jackson, Alabama, at coordinates 31&hyph;30&hyph;35 and 88&hyph;02&hyph;39. The petition filed by G. Dean Pearce for Ellisville, Mississippi, has been withdrawn. The petition filed by Faulkner-Phillips for Bay Minette, AL, Butler, AL and Waynesboro, MS has also been withdrawn. The counterproposals filed by G. Dean Pearce, James Ducker and Thomas William Hickman, III and Newton Broadcasting Company, Inc., have been dismissed. With this action, this proceeding is terminated. EFFECTIVE DATE: August 19, 1994.
