December 9, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THIS FIGHT NEEDED MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE

No mas, no mas.

Pay-per-view fights should come complete with a money-back guarantee. If the performance isn't appealing by the midway point, you can call in and ask for a refund. I'd rather watch a Rocky film. Or the WWF.

Cease boxing's Senior Circuit before one of them actually has to throw a punch. The Hearns-Leonard draw was a joke and I've seen better fights in the Coliseum stands than the Duran-Leonard waltz I got suckered into paying for Thursday night.

GARY MIEREANU, Northridge
