Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Albanian President Sali Berisha has sent a greetings telegram to Croatian President Franjo Tudjman and Bosnia-Herzegovina President Alia Izetbegovic on the agreement to create a confederation which has been signed in Washington. Berisha stressed the importance of this agreement in achieving a fair peace in Bosnia-Herzegovina, which gives broader hope to the region. In a telegram sent to U.S. President Bill Clinton, Berisha expressed his high assessment for the very important role of President Clinton and the U.S. Government in achieving this agreement as well as his conviction on Washington's further commitment to resolve the crisis in our region.
