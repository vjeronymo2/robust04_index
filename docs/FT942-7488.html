940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Warnford declines 9% to Pounds 6.95m Warnford Investments reported a 9 per cent contraction in profits before tax -from Pounds 7.66m to Pounds 6.95m - during the 12 months to December 25. Turnover at the property investment group dipped to Pounds 11.4m (Pounds 12.1m). After tax and minorities, earnings per share emerged at 12.1p (13.65p). A proposed final dividend of 5p brings the total for the year to 7.75p (7.5p). The group's investment property portfolio was valued, on an open market value basis, at Pounds 104.9m at the period end, up from Pounds 89.9m a year earlier.
