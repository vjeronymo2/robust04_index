920404 FT 04 APR 92 / Earth summit threat The UN Earth Summit in June faces the risk of failure with delegates in sharp disagreement on how to fund huge environmental programmes in the midst of a worldwide recession, writes Michael Littlejohns at the UN in New York. Mr Jean-Claude Faby, spokesman for the 175-nation conference, said the key questions of finance, the transfer of technology to poorer countries and institutional arrangements had defied solution in five weeks of intensive New York talks.
