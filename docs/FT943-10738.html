940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: BPI makes Pounds 1m Scottish purchase British Polythene Industries has purchased Edinburgh Plastics for Pounds 1.05m, comprising Pounds 329,712 in cash and the balance by the issue to vendors of 161,932 ordinary shares, unchanged at 445p yesterday. EP, which extrudes, prints and converts polythene film and bags, is based in Cowdenbeath, Scotland. At October 31 it had net assets of Pounds 1.16m. Turnover for the year to end-October was Pounds 2.51m and pre-tax profit Pounds 99,019.
