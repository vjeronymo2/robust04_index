February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Jerome J. Filiciotto has been elected to the board of directors of Scientific NRG Inc., Anaheim.

Filiciotto, a licensed engineer, was previously president and chief operating officer of Rohr Industries in Chula Vista in 1978. In 1980 he left Rohr to purchase SAS Inc. of El Cajon and later sold the company. Scientific NRG manufactures and markets energy efficient lighting products for commercial, industrial and residential use.
