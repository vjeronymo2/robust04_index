Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 28 (SNARK)--During the last three days the Azerbaijani troops lost about 20 military machines, among them - 16 tanks. Despite the considerable losses the Azerbaijani Army suffers in man power and military hardware, Azeris are going on with the attacks. This morning the battles at the northern Mardakertskiy and Kelbadzharskiy regions again started. According to the data received from the Press-Service of the Karabak? Defense Army, the Azerbaijani side is concentrating forces at the other parts of the front. Meanwhile, yesterday the Karabakh forces repelled the enemy's attacks. In the battles at the Martuni direction Azeris lost 2 tanks and 70 soldiers. The Karabakh side lost 7 soldiers, 18 soldiers were wounded.
