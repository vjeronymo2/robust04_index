Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Madrid RNE-1 Radio Network in Spanish at 1349 GMT on 28 February 1994 carries further reportage on a hijacking of an Algerian aircraft to Spain. In a report from Alicante airport, RNE's reporter said that "three hijackers with small arms are holding 121 passengers, including a baby, and the crew of seven, demanding political asylum in our country." The reporter says that the Civil Governor of Alicante is talking via an interpreter to the hijackers from the control tower; a "diplomatic representative of the Algerian Government" arrived at the airport a few moments ago, the reporter adds.
