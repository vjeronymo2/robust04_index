September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: CAL POLY EDUCATOR NAMED TO TEST PANEL

Edward Walton, associate professor of chemistry at Cal Poly Pomona, has been appointed to the national College Board Committee that develops the Chemistry Achievement Test for the Admissions Testing Program.

Walton, who has taught chemistry at Cal Poly since 1987, also designed and teaches a course at Cal Poly's School of Education on how science should be taught in elementary school.
