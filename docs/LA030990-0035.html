March 9, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

ST. JOSEPH'S RIPS HOLTVILLE

St. Joseph's more than doubled Holtville's output in a 71-32 victory in the Southern California Regional Division IV semifinals in Santa Maria Thursday night.

Anna McConnell of St. Joseph's (27-3) led all scorers with 28 points.

Denise Gaddif scored 12 for Holtville (19-10), and teammate Teresa Ibara added seven.
