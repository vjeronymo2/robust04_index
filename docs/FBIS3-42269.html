Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman -- AL-QADISIYAH monitoring -- Jordanian authorities have disclosed an attempt to assassinate Mr. Yasir 'Arafat during his latest visit to Amman. They arrested a number of Palestinians, who were armed with RPG's with the intention of hitting 'Arafat's plane upon landing at the airport. The suspects admitted that they were planning to assassinate the Palestinian leader for signing the Gaza-Jericho accord with the Zionist enemy. [Baghdad AL-THAWRAH in Arabic on 3 February publishes a front page MENA report citing Palestinian sources in Amman as saying that Jordan's intelligence services aborted an attempt to kill 'Arafat during his latest visit to Amman by downing his plane as it landed at the airport].
