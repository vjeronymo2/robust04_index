940316 FT 16 MAR 94 / International Company News: Finmeccanica reduces holding Finmeccanica, the Italian state-controlled engineering group, is further reducing its stake in its quoted Ansaldo Trasporti subsidiary, in a transaction expected to raise up to L72bn (Dollars 43m). Lehman Brothers and Paribas Capital Markets were appointed joint lead managers yesterday for the placement of 10.6m ordinary shares in Ansaldo Trasporti with international investors. Finmeccanica's stake will fall to 50.1 per cent from 63.6 per cent.
