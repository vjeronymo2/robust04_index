BFN [Text] The Philippines and Malaysia are working at strengthening their joint defense cooperation. This was revealed by Defense Secretary Renato de Villa after his meeting with Malaysian Defense Minister Datuk Sri Najib Tun Razak in Kuala Lumpur. According to De Villa, Tun Razak proposed the formulation of plans for the exchange of defense equipment between the two countries. De Villa said he likewise proposed launching joint patrol operations to stop high-sea smuggling in the area between the Philippines and Malaysia.
