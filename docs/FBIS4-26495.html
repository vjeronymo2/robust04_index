BFN [Text] Beijing, June 22 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), said here today that China attaches great importance to developing friendly ties with the Republic of Gabon. Li, meeting with Edmond Nkogho, president of Gabon's Economic and Social Council (ESC) [name and title as received], and his party, said there have always been good relations between China and Gabon, and between the CPPCC and ESC. The group's visit will serve to further exchanges between the two countries in economic, trade and other spheres, Li noted. China, while engaging in large-scale economic construction, needs a stable and harmonious domestic situation as well as a friendly and peaceful international environment, Li said. Only under these conditions can China focus all its attention on economic construction and develop exchanges and cooperation with other countries, Li said. Nkogho said Gabon desires to further its friendly ties of cooperation with China. Speaking highly of China's achievements in reform and opening to the outside world, Nkogho said that Gabon, which is also committed to economic development, is willing to draw on China's experience. Nkogho and his party arrived here on June 20 at the CPPCC's invitation.
