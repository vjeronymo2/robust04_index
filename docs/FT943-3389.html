940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Record rises 22% Record Holdings, the Sheffield-based tools maker, lifted pre-tax profits by 22 per cent from Pounds 984,000 to Pounds 1.2m in the first half of 1994. Turnover advanced from Pounds 13.8m to Pounds 15.2m with overseas sales exceeding UK sales for the first time at Pounds 7.62m (Pounds 6.42m). The interim dividend is being maintained at 1.15p, payable from earnings per share of 2.22p (1.76p).
