930417 FT 17 APR 93 / UK Company News: Golden Vale has 99% of Leckpatrick Golden Vale, the Irish dairy group which has made an agreed offer for Leckpatrick Holdings, announced that by the first closing date it had received valid acceptances in respect of 6.48m Leckpatrick shares - 99 per cent of the target's issued capital. Accordingly, the offer has become unconditional as to acceptances and is held open until further notice. Leckpatrick is a private dairy processor based in Northern Ireland, and Golden Vale's offer was made through Anorra Holdings, its UK investment vehicle. The offer, which consists of 10-year loan notes, redeemable after one year, valued each Leckpatrick share at 332p and included a partial cash alternative up to a total of IPounds 4.34m.
