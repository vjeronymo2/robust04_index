March 19, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SAY CHEESECAKE: Playboy photographer David Chan is due next month at UC Irvine and Cal State Fullerton for the magazine's fall pictorial on the "beautiful co-eds" of the Big West Conference. . . . Expected feminist protests won't bother Chan, says a Playboy spokesman: "He loves that. It's always good for business." . . . In 1987, Ellen Stohl, a paraplegic student at Cal State Fullerton, was featured in the magazine and Deborah Driggs, a former Saddleback College cheerleader, is Miss March. Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
