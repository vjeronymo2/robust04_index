BFN [Text] An official source at the Yemeni Foreign Ministry has welcomed the statement the U.S. State Department issued yesterday evening on the current developments in our country. The source noted the statement's emphasis on the importance of convening the Constitutional institutions and the return of the official leadership to Sanaa in order to work as one team. He also noted the statement's call for disengaging the units and refraining from the use of force. The official source expressed Yemen's appreciation of the American interest in preserving unity, democracy, security, and stability in our country and in the need to be guided by Constitutional legitimacy. The source said the American statement goes in the direction of what the legitimate and Constitutional leadership in the Republic of Yemen is proposing on the need to resolve the political crisis by peaceful means and within the framework of the pledge and accord document unanimously endorsed by the Yemeni people.
