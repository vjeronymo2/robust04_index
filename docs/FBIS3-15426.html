Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Juri Luik had a meeting with the Japanese ambassador today. Foreign Ministry press representative Mari-Ann Rikken told BNS that the talks focused on Estonian-Russian relations, the Russian troop withdrawal from Estonia, and the situation in Russia. According to Rikken, the establishing of an Estonian Embassy in Japan was also discussed. Estonia will open an embassy in Japan during the current year, she said. Mari-Ann Rikken added that during the meeting Estonian-Japanese cultural and education-related relations were also discussed.
