940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Girl, 12, killed by masked man in classroom attack Nikki Conroy, aged 12, was killed and three other children were injured after a masked man wearing combat gear and brandishing a gun and two knives burst into a classroom at a school in Middlesbrough, Cleveland. He was overpowered by two teachers after lining the children up and lashing out at them. Police later charged with murder Stephen James Wilkinson, aged 29, from Middlesbrough.
