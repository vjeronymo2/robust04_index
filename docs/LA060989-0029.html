June 9, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO INDUSTRY NOTES

Mail Boxes Etc. reported that net income rose by 43% to $671,700 for the fourth quarter ended April 30, up from $470,000 during the previous fourth quarter. Revenue rose by 56% to $5.4 million, up from $3.5 million a year earlier.

Net income for the year ended April 30 rose by 29% to $2.4 million, up from $1.8 million a year earlier. Revenue rose by 55% to $19.4 million, up from $12.5 million a year earlier.
