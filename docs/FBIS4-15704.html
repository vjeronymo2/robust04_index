BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 9 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and his Ukrainian counterpart Anatoliy Zlenko expressed concern over the Odessa April 8 incident involving the hydrographic vessel of the Black Sea Fleet. Sources at the Russian Foreign Ministry told ITAR-TASS that a telephone conversation of the two ministers resulted in an agreement on further contact on the issue as well as measures to clarify the circumstances of the incident and normalize the situation. The Russian side stressed the need for normal activities of the Black Sea Fleet and maximum objectiveness and restraint in consideration of the facts. In his turn, Anatoliy Zlenko shared this point of view, said the sources.
