Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The preparatory committee of the [word indistinct] grouping has mourned the martyrdom of Shaykh 'Abd-al-Karim al-Jihmi, adviser to the prime ministry and member of the grouping's preparatory committee. While regretting this incident, which was carried out by vicious hands, the committee hopes that the competent security circles will quickly take the necessary measures, chase the culprits, and bring them to justice, so they can receive a just punishment. The committee also extends condolences to the martyr's family and kinsfolk. Funeral services for martyr al-Jihmi will be held at the martyrs' cemetery this morning, after prayers at the al-Shuhada' Mosque.
