940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Observer: Last man in There is something rather reassuring about the sight of 59-year-old Barry Pearl leading Winterflood Securities into the gilt-edged market this morning. After all, Pearl was the last gilts jobber to quit the floor of the stock exchange after Big Bang. Pearl soldiered on alone, making prices at his podium by telephone or face-to-face with the occasional broker who wandered by, long after his competitors had departed the stock exchange floor for their high-tech skyscrapers. But in 1988 a storm cut off all lines of communication, forcing him to retreat up into the office and take his place in front of a screen. Since then, he has never looked back.
