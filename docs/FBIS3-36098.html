Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Half an hour ago, the 13th Supreme Soviet began its work. Deputies began the day with a discussion of miscellaneous issues. However, at the beginning, Supreme Soviet Chairman Myechyslaw Hryb declared that he received a proposal from more than 20 people's deputies to listen to a speech by the Belarusian Strike Committee chairman. This issue was voted on and was not supported by a majority of deputies. The deputies began discussing miscellaneous issues.
