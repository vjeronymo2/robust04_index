BFN [Text] Beijing, May 25 (XINHUA) -- Li Tieying, Chinese State Councillor and minister in charge of the State Commission for Restructuring the Economy, met here today with Rosalia Arteaga, Ecuadorian minister of education and culture. Li said that the Chinese Government attaches importance to developing its relations with Ecuador and other Latin American countries. Arteaga is here as guest of the Chinese Ministry of Culture. Yesterday the Ecuadorian minister, on behalf of the Ecuadorian Government, conferred a medal on the Chinese minister of culture, Liu Zhongde, in recognition of his dedication to the development of cultural undertakings.
