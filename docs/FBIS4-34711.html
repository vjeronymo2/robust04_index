BFN [Text] Yesterday, Congressman Antero Flores Araoz assumed, for the second time, the Collegiate General Secretariat of the Popular Christian Party (PPC) replacing Alberto Borea Odria who resigned. Flores Araoz said he accepted the secretariat in compliance with party internal regulations but called for complementary internal elections as soon as possible to fill vacancies. In accordance with party regulations, Florez Araoz, Lourdes Flores Cano, and Ricardo Amiel Meza are PPC leaders. Flores Araoz was installed by PPC President Luis Bedoya Reyes in the presence of the entire Enlarged Political Committee.
