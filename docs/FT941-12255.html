940131 FT 31 JAN 94 / Mandela appeals for restraint Nelson Mandela appealed to right-wing whites on Sunday to avoid any action which could provoke conflict in South Africa, Reuter reports from Rustenburg. The African National Congress leader was speaking during a tour of the western Transvaal province, following a rally by right-wing Afrikaners on Saturday which demanded that violence remain an option in their quest for self-determination.
