March 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CHAMORRO'S UPSET WIN IN NICARAGUA

Since the United States now has to come up with more money to support the new Nicaraguan government, what say we take all the leftover arms from the Contras and sell them to moderates in Iran. Then we turn over the profits to Chamorro.

Sounds like a "neat idea," don't you think?

MARTIN E. KANTOR

San Diego
