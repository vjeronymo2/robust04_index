941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / International Company News: Riyad Bank falls 15% to SR559.1m Riyad Bank, Saudi Arabia's second largest bank, saw net profits drop for the first nine months of 1994, Reuter reports from Manama. Net profits fell 15 per cent to SR559.1m (Dollars 149m) from SR657.7m in the same period in 1993. Shareholders' equity rose to SR7.47bn from SR6.97bn. Customer deposits fell to SR26.31bn from SR26.83bn. Total assets also dropped to SR52.15bn from SR54.13bn.
