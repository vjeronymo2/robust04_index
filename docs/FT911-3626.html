910424 FT 24 APR 91 / UK Company News: Conder sale set to raise Pounds 23m The Conder Group is to raise a maximum of Pounds 23m from the sale of 61 per cent of Conder Products, which supplies petrol forecourt canopies. Conder Group will receive an initial Pounds 15m plus a performance related Pounds 8m. CIN Venture Managers will hold 56 per cent and the mangers will hold the other 5 per cent.
