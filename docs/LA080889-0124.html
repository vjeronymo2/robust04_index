August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Hollywood Redevelopment -- Due to incorrect information provided by Los Angeles Councilman Michael Woo, an article Friday stated that none of his appointees to a new advisory group on Hollywood redevelopment had previously served as elected members of the Hollywood Project Area Committee. At the time the council voted in May to disband the committee, two of the appointees were elected members of the panel, according to the Community Redevelopment Agency.
