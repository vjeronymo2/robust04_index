BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 6 (KCNA) -- The seventh session of the Ninth Supreme People's Assembly [SPA] of the DPRK opened here today. The great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were present at the session. The following agenda was adopted: 1. On successfully fulfilling the tasks of socialist economic construction in the period of adjustment set forth by the Workers' Party of Korea. 2. On the review of the performance of the state budget of the DPRK for 1993 and the state budget for 1994. 3. On the endorsement of the laws adopted by the DPRK SPA Standing Committee during the recess of the SPA. Deputy Kang Song-san, premier of the Administration Council, made a report on the first item of the agenda.
