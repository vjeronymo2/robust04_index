910419 FT 19 APR 91 / International Company News: Canadian mines group up 19% AMERICAN Barrick Resources, one of North America's top three gold producers, benefited from strong production growth at its Goldstrike mine in Nevada, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The group reported a 19 per cent gain in earnings in the first quarter to USDollars 15.6m, or 11 cents a share, up from Dollars 13.1m, or 10 cents, a year earlier. First-quarter revenues were Dollars 73m, up from Dollars 53m. Average price realised was Dollars 428 an ounce. Production was 171,980 ozs, up from 131,111 ozs, including 110,249 ozs from Goldstrike, against 70,123 ozs.
