930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: Pounds 425,000 loss for John I Jacobs John I Jacobs, the ship owning and broking group, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 425,000 for 1992, against a profit of Pounds 612,000. Turnover fell from Pounds 4.43m to Pounds 2.25m. Losses per share were 2p (earnings 1.9p). Including an extraordinary Pounds 2.98m (Pounds 266,000) on the sale of Jacobs Offshore, they became earnings of 11.5p (3.1p). There is no final dividend, leaving a total of 0.5p (1.9p). As already announced, a first interim of 1.4p is being paid for the current year.
