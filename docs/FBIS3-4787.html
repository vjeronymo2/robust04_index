Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. teams will on Saturday launch their first searches for forces missing in action in Vietnam since the lifting of the American embargo. Experts from the MIA Task Force will be sent out across 19 provinces in the north and south of Vietnam to investigate sites where the remains of U.S. forces might be found. The month-long mission will look into 59 cases specified as top priority by the U.S. MIA Office.
