Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Rodolfo Mejia Fontalvo in Barranquilla; from "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] The presidential candidate for the 19 April Movement Democratic Alliance [AD/M-19], Antonio Navarro Wolff, has said he would continue pushing alternatives for change despite the defeat suffered in the last elections. Navarro Wolff, accompanied by his vice presidential candidate, (Jesus Pena Cue), said: AD/M-19 members have been fighters for many years and will continue to fight, and we never consider fear. We shall continue to fight for the good people everywhere, Navarro Wolff has said. He has said that AD/M-19 needs to minimize ambitions within the party and an in-depth reorganization, which must be peacefully carried out.
