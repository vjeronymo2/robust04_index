BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 24 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on June 23 sent a message of greetings to Joaquim Alberto Chissano, President of the Republic of Mozambique, on the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the independence and foundation of the Republic of Mozambique. President Kim Il-song in the message expressed the belief that the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries would grow stronger and develop and wished the Mozambican president and people success in their work for peace and independent development of their country.
