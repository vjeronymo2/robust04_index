Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In an interview with Interfax, Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze said Russia was playing an enormous role in the efforts to settle the Abkhazian conflict. In fact, he said, he could talk of coordinated efforts for a settlement. However painfully, the Commonwealth had come to realize the need for constructive cooperation for reaching enduring peace in Georgia and, consequently, Transcaucasia as a whole, and peace in that region was of vital importance to Russia, Shevardnadze noted.
