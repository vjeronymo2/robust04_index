931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Finance and the Family: Halifax negative equity plan - At a Glance Halifax building society is launching a new scheme to help existing customers with negative equity to move house. The scheme will allow borrowers who meet certain conditions to carry forward any shortfall on selling their property, and at the same time take out a 100 per cent mortgage on a new property. A maximum of Pounds 25,000 negative equity can be carried forward, as long as the total new mortgage does not exceed 125 per cent of the value of the new property. Interest on the element will be charged at prevailing rates but will not be eligible for tax relief.
