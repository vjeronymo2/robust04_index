September 23, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP

Agoura 14, Simi Valley 0 -- Quarterback Josh Smaler threw two touchdown passes to Greg Ochoa as host Agoura handed Simi Valley its third consecutive loss.

Agoura (2-1) took advantage of a poor quick kick to take a 6-0 lead with one second remaining in the first half when Smaler hit Ochoa for an 11-yard score. The touchdown followed a five-yard punt by Simi Valley (0-3) on a third-and-six play from its own six-yard line. LANCE O'FALLON
