940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Equitable Life in Pounds 60m office deal Equitable Life Assurance Society has bought an office building in Reading for Pounds 60m. The 147,000 sq ft building at 121 King's Road is let to Prudential, the insurance company. It has been sold by OIL Property Investment, the UK property arm of German Hypo Bank, which bought it for about Pounds 45m in March 1993.
