Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of 803 Lao refugees from 63 families has returned home from southern China. The ceremony to hand over the refugees was held on 27 January at the Lao-Chinese border checkpoint between Bosin, Laos and (Bohan), China. The ceremony was jointly officiated by Ang Chitdala, head of the Luang Namtha Province Labor and Social Welfare Service, and (Li Tokou), head of the Civil Service in Yunnan Province, PRC. Witnessing the event were representatives of the Lao Consulate in Kunming and of the UNHCR representative offices in Laos, the PRC, and Geneva. This is the first repatriation of Lao refugees in 1994. So far, more than 2,900 out of approximately 4,000 Lao refugees in southern China have returned to their motherland.
