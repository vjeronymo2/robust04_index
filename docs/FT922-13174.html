920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Hindus take ritual bath in Ganges Some of an estimated 3m Hindus who took a ritual bath in the Ganges yesterday in and around the town of Hardwar, Uttar Pradesh, at the start of a salvation festival. Hindus believe that bathing in the Ganges at this time washes away sins and opens the way to nirvana, a state of perpetual bliss
