Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 9 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese State Council and the Central Military Commission extended congratulations to staff and workers engaged in launching satellites. China successfully launched a scientific satellite and a simulated satellite into space from the Xichang launch site in southwest China yesterday afternoon. In a message of congratulations today, the State Council and the commission lauded the launching, which it called "another important achievement in China's high-tech space research field." The message went on, "It will have a notable impact on efforts to accomplish China's strategic objective of developing a new generation of multi-purpose satellites, and expanding exchanges and co-operation with overseas in the space field."
