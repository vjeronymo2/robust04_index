920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Aviva looks to Colombia MR DENYS Milne, chairman of Aviva Petroleum, told the annual meeting that the directors and management continued to believe that the 'brightest prospect' for the company involved further development of the Colombian assets. He said oil revenues were now being received from new production in Colombia and that operations in the US were now cash flow positive. Following the restructuring, approved by shareholders at the extraordinary meeting following the agm, the Royal Bank of Scotland will own 16 per cent of Aviva's capital.
