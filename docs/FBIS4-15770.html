BFN [Text] In order to ensure citizens' rights to housing and on the basis of the law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On temporary delegation of additional powers to the president of the Republic of Kazakhstan and heads of local administrations," the president of the Republic of Kazakhstan issued an edict "On ensuring citizens' right to housing." In conformity with the housing code of the Republic of Kazakhstan, residential areas in buildings are a component of the residential fund and include dormitories, hotels, shelters, houses of the flexible fund, retirement homes for lonely and elderly people, invalids, and veterans. For those residing in dormitories and hostels, taking into consideration the number of people in the family, a residential housing unit consisting of two or more rooms excluding a kitchen, hallways, and other auxiliary rooms, can be allotted. When privatizing objects, which include residential homes and areas, of the state and departmental fund, the conditions and purpose of using the abovementioned objects are preserved and the rights of citizens for housing envisioned by the Republic's law are guaranteed. The full text of the edict will be published in the press.
