920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Baker talks with Israel Mr James Baker, US secretary of state, discussed Israel's request for Dollars 10bn in housing loan guarantees with the Israeli ambassador in Washington yesterday, but no final decision was made, writes Lionel Barber. He is understood to have repeated US doubts about Israel's policy of building in the occupied territories. The Israelis said, however, that the US remained committed to helping Israel deal with the recent influx of immigrants.
