BFN [Text] The speaker of Iranian parliament, Hojjat Ol-Eslam Nateq-Nuri, has castigated the atrocities being perpetrated on Muslims in occupied Kashmir, Bosnia-Herzegovina, and Palestine during a call on Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto in Islamabad today. The Iranian speaker expressed his admiration for Pakistan's principled stand on various issues facing the Islamic world like Kashmir, Bosnia, and Palestine. He said the Iranian ministers and his delegation would discuss with their counterparts the implementation of major projects such as gas pipeline and Pakistan-Iran oil refinery. Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto said relations between Iran and Pakistan are very strong. She said that all the projects identified for cooperation between the two countries would be completed. The speaker of the National Assembly Syed Yusuf Raza Gilani and Deputy Speaker Syed Zafar Ali Shah were also present during the meeting.
