July 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SHE MIGHT BE A COURT, BUT SOME READERS SAY SHE SHOULD NOT JUDGE

Margaret Court says Martina Navratilova is a poor role model for young tennis players.

Although Ms. Court professes to be a born-again Christian, she seems to have forgotten Matthew 7:1: "Judge not, that ye be not judged." Martina has displayed skill, determination and grace over the years. What more could be asked of a role model for young athletes?

JULIE RAFFETY

Santa Barbara
