940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: Barclays trebles interim profits - At a glance Barclays trebled its interim profits to Pounds 1.04bn before tax for the six months to end June, thanks partly to a Pounds 680m cut in bad debt provisions. The interim dividend was increased by 23 per cent to 8p a share. Chief executive Martin Taylor commented that Barclays had made 'lousy returns' in the past few years.
