BFN [Text] The International Monetary Fund has announced to grant Vietnam a loan of $50 million for the rehabilitation of economic development in Vietnam in 1994. Meanwhile the World Bank lent Vietnam credit for the upgrading of the section from Ho Chi Minh city to Can Tho and the section between Hanoi and Nhatrang on Highway No.1. The World Bank also provided loans for the improvement of primary education system and agricultural development program, including three such projects for agricultural credit, rubber plantation, and intensive farming. The World Bank expert said that in the coming three or four years, international credits to vietnam will reach $315 million per year.
