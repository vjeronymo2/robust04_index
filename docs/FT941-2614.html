940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto remained ahead, helped by buoyant golds and the triple-witching effect, in spite of weak world markets and continued interest rate uncertainty. The TSE 300 composite index added 23.32 to 4566.61 in volume of 80.44m shares. Advancing issues outpaced declines 445 to 333. The gold group rose 2.28 per cent. Other sizable gains were posted by energy, up 1.77 per cent, and mining, up 1.04 per cent.
