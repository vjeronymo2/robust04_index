January 4, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Tyne Daly of "Cagney & Lacey" TV fame will star in a $3 million revival of "Gypsy" at the Orange County Performing Arts Center in Costa Mesa in July as part of a pre-Broadway national tour, producer Barry Brown said Tuesday from New York. Brown said the "Gypsy" tour will begin in Miami in May with stops in Detroit and St. Louis before arriving in Costa Mesa for show's sole West Coast engagement (July 18-23). The tour will continue to Denver and Washington culminating in a Broadway opening in September, he said. It will mark Daly's Broadway debut. ALEENE MacMINN
