Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A comprehensive draft antinarcotics law has been finalized to replace the existing law to provide death penalty and seizure of assets as maximum punishment for those involved in drug-related crimes. The federal interior minister, retired Major General Nasirullah Babar, told a news conference in Islamabad today that the law is likely to be adopted by the parliament in a few month's time. He reiterated the commitment of the government to eradicate the drug menace from the society. The minister said over 64,000 kg of narcotics were seized during the 100 days of the present government.
