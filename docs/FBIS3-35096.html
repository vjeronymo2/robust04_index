Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In the biennial elections to the Rajya Sabha, the Congress-I has bagged all the four seats from Karnataka, while the ruling Left Front has gotten all the five seats in West Bengal. Out of the eight seats in Bihar, the Congress-I has gotten two, the Janata Dal, 4; and the CPI [Communist Party of India] and the BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] one each. The lone seat in Sikkim has gone to the Sikkim Sangram Parishad. [passage omitted]
