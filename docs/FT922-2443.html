920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Manufacturing loss hits Chillington A Pounds 1.49m downturn into operating losses by its UK industrial interests left Chillington Corporation with 1991 pre-tax losses down from Pounds 905,000 to Pounds 646,000. However after lower extraordinary charges of Pounds 163,000 (Pounds 1.13m) the loss for the year was much reduced at Pounds 777,000 (Pounds 1.95m). The final dividend is being passed leaving the total payment at 0.5p (2.75p). Turnover was Pounds 48.6m (Pounds 55.3m). There were improvements in operating profits for tropical agriculture at Pounds 1.59m (Pounds 1.24m) and agricultural tools at Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 547,000). Manufacturing had losses of Pounds 948,000 (Pounds 543,000 profits).
