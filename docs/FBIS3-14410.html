Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 27 Mar (INA)--Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid has warned against a scheme aimed at fragmenting the Arab and Islamic region under the guise of religion to divert the Arabs and Muslims from the course of development. In a statement published today in AL-MUSTAQBAL newspaper, the paper of the Yemeni Socialist Party, al-Bid disclosed that the signs of this serious scheme are represented by the acts of violence, terrorism, and crime from which many Arab and Muslim countries, including Yemen, are suffering. Al-Bid noted that this scheme, which stands against every national and pan-Arab tendency toward unity, takes religion as a guise. He added that reason and religion reject such backward plans.
