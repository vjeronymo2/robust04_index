940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Disasters cause Dollars 11.6bn damage Natural catastrophes and accidents in 1993 caused insured damage worth Dollars 11.6bn worldwide, less than half the record 1992 amount of Dollars 26.4bn, according to Swiss Reinsurance, Reuter reports from Zurich. 30,000 people died in the 30 catastrophes in 1993. The largest single event, windstorms in the US in mid-March cost the insurance industry Dollars 1.8bn.
