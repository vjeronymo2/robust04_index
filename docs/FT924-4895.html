921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / Observer: Bob a job Overheard during discussion of redundancies on train from Birmingham to London: 'Yes, my brother's been out of work since he lost his job in the summer . . . and he was chairman of BP.' The other Horton went on to say that brother Robert is in the small-print stages of becoming head of an unidentified public service outfit. 'Not the coal-mining review body, you know, but something comparable.'
