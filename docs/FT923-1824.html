920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / International Company News: Omron slashes profits forecast A DECLINE in Japanese companies' capital spending has forced Omron, the electronic control equipment manufacturer, to slash its parent company profits forecast, writes Steven Butler in Tokyo. Omron expects pre-tax profits of Y7.0bn (Dollars 56m) in the 12 months to March, compared with Y13.55bn the year before.
