941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Seeboard - Other Companies The company will kick off what is expected to be a controversial interims season for the England and Wales regional electricity companies when it reports on Thursday. Profit figures for the first half of the year are always of limited significance but the recs' dividend rises always provoke criticism. Seeboard is expected to be relatively restrained, with analysts forecasting rises of about 16 per cent. However some of its peers are likely to go beyond 20 per cent. The Labour Party seems bound to renew calls for a windfall tax on utilities.
