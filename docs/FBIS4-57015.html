BFN [Excerpt] The Army General Staff will participate in the installation ceremony of the new provisional president of the Republic. The secretariat of the Army General Staff has confirmed the presence of its members at the ceremony to install Emile Jonassaint as provisional president this afternoon. After the ceremony, which will take place at the Senate of the Republic, attorney Jonassaint will go to the National Palace to take office and receive military honors. AFP has noted that the two guns usually used to fire the traditional 21-gun salute given all new presidents in Haiti were seen under their [word indistinct] on 11 May at the Army General Headquarters.
