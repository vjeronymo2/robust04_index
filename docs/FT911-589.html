910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Appointments: Insurance companies' top posts Mr John Curtis has been appointed chairman and Mr Leslie Goodman chief executive of Jardine (Lloyd's Underwriting Agents), the members' agency arm of JARDINE INSURANCE BROKERS GROUP. Mr Mark Dawson becomes deputy chairman. The appointments follow the resignation of Mr David R. Walker who has been appointed chief executive of CLAREMOUNT HOLDING and Claremount Underwriting Agency from June 1. Mr Walker remains a non-executive director of JLUA. Mr Curtis, a director of Matheson & Co, has been responsible for the Jardine Group's shipping and property interests for many years. Mr Goodman joins from Barclays de Zoete Wedd where he was a director. Mr Dawson has been with JLUA for many years, latterly as managing director. ***** Mr Nick Cosh has been appointed group finance director of Jardine Insurance Brokers Group. He was finance director of MAI, where he remains a non-executive director. ***** Mr Mojtaba Ghassemian has been appointed senior vice-president of CSC EUROPE, based in London. He was managing director of CSC Inforem. Both companies are subsidiaries of Computer Sciences Corporation, US. ***** Mr Christian Sulger-Buel has been appointed executive director in charge of international private banking at the London branch of BANQUE PARIBAS in succession to Mr Alexandre Starker. Mr Sulger-Buel was in the private banking department in the bank's Paris headquarters.
