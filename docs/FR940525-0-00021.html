SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The United States Housing Act of 1937 (1937 Act) requires the Secretary to publish Fair Market Rents (FMRs) periodically, but not less frequently than annually, to be effective on October 1 of each year. The 1937 Act also requires that the assistance contracts signed by owners participating in the Department's Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments programs provide for annual or more frequent adjustment in the maximum monthly rentals for units covered by the contract to reflect changes based on fair market rents prevailing in a particular market area, or on a reasonable formula. The FMR and AAF Notices published on April 6, 1994 (59 FR 16408), and April 26, 1994 (59 FR 21832), respectively, announced the revised FY 1994 FMRs and Annual Adjustment Factors, but contained some technical errors.
