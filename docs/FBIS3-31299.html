Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Skopje, 16 Feb (TANJUG) -- It was stated in Skopje today that NATO Secretary Manfred Woerner has received a letter from Macedonian Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski informing NATO of Macedonia's intention to join and actively participate in the Partnership for Peace program. According to the statement, the Macedonian request will be discussed at an upcoming NATO meeting. [passage omitted]
