Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 24 Feb (TANJUG) -- Hans van den Broek, EU commissioner for foreign affairs, arrived in Skopje from Athens this afternoon to convey the Greek reply to a proposal that the Macedonian president sent to Greece yesterday on overcoming the dispute and the lifting of the Greek blockade. Upon his arrival at Skopje's Petrovac airport, van den Broek stated that he was bringing some positive messages to Kiro Gligorov, president of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], and the government. On the other hand, he added, much remained to be done, but in any case a dialogue must be restored in the forseeable future.
