941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Thorn Transit Systems International - Contracts & ventures Thorn Transit Systems International of the UK, will be awarded a contract worth about HKDollars 200m (Dollars 26m) to design, manufacture, install and commission an automatic fare collection system for the airport railway in Hong Kong. The 34km railway will provide two separate services, a fast passenger link to the new airport and a domestic service called the Lantau Line.
