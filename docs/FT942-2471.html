940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / US accepts six Haitian refugees Six Haitian boat people were granted refugee status yesterday after being interviewed as part of a new US immigration procedure aboard a navy ship off Jamaica. They were among 35 Haitians picked up at sea this week. All were interviewed by US immigration officials and lawyers from the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees. The other 29 were found not to qualify for refugee status and will be returned to Haiti on Monday, officials said.
