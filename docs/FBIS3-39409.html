Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Feb 15 (A.A) -- Eleven terrorists were killed in clashes with security forces in southeastern Mardin and Sirnak Provinces, the regional Governor's Office said on Tuesday [15 February]. Another fourteen people were also arrested in security operations in Diyarbakir, Bitlis, Sirnak and Van Provinces, seven of them belonging to a separatist organization, the other seven to an outlawed leftist organization. Firearms and ammunition were also seized in a series of opertions by security forces in the same area, the statement said.
