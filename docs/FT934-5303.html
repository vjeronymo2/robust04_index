931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met strong and active demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Coloury Assams, particularly pd's, were a very strong feature, gaining 5 to 15p or more. Poorer sorts were steady. Bright east Africans were often dearer by 5 to 10p, while mediums also advanced 3 to 5p. Ceylons met improved enquiry with brighter types often 3 to 8p higher. Offshore: good demand at firm to dearer rates. Quotations: quality 205p/kg (200p/kg), good medium 140p/kg (126p/kg), medium 109p/kg (102p/kg), low medium 97p/kg (95p/kg). The highest price realised this week was 220p/kg for a Rwanda pf. 1.
