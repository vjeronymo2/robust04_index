910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Appointments: Moves at Skandia Life SKANDIA LIFE GROUP has appointed Mr Alan Wilson, previously operations director, as group managing director, managing director of Skandia Life, and managing director of Professional Life. He also becomes chairman of Royal Skandia. Mr Paul Bradshaw, who previously held all these posts, has resigned. ***** Mr Geoffrey Bignell, chief executive, has been promoted to chairman of PAINEWEBBER INTERNATIONAL BANK. He succeeds Mr Robin Baillie who is retiring as chairman, but remains a non-executive director and chairman of the audit committee. Mr Brian Tuck, chief financial officer, has been additionally appointed managing director. ***** Mr Michael Cottrell has been appointed to the main board of MATTHEW CLARK, Guildford, with particular responsibility for fine wines. He was with Russell Reynolds, and has been managing director of Saccone and Speed, and of Courage. ***** FEDERAL EXPRESS BUSINESS LOGISTICS has appointed Mr Bill Parsons as international business development director for the UK. He was business development director for Systemline, the division's British logistics operating company. ***** JACQUES VERT has appointed Mr Brian Heilbron as group retail director. He was managing director, retail division. ***** JOHN CARRINGTON & CO has appointed Mr Michael Cartwright as marketing director. ***** NAYLOR BROS (CLAYWARE), Barnsley, has appointed Mr William Mayou as technical director. He was general manager, group technical services, with Ibstock Building Products. ***** Mr Angus Grant has been appointed technical director of WEIR PUMPS. He was general manager, engineering, and succeeds Mr Mike Ryall, who has retired. ***** BARCLAYS BANK has appointed Mr Mike Kill as business sector marketing director, replacing Mr Alan Vaughan who has become the bank's advances director, central advances department. ***** Mr John Church has been appointed chairman, and Mr Lewis McGibbon becomes non-executive deputy chairman at CHURCH & CO. Mr Ian Church retires as chairman but remains a non-executive director. Mr Jack Seabright, a non-executive director, is retiring.
