BFN [Telephone interview with George Habash, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, by correspondent Muhammad Bishkar "soon after the signing of the Palestinian-Israeli accord"; place not given -- recorded] [Text] [Habash] It [the Palestinian-Israeli accord] fully undermines the sacrifices and struggle of the Palestinian people over more than 25 years. It undermines the right of the Palestinian people to self-determination and the resolutions of the national councils, even the UN resolutions that we do not believe achieve all the Palestinian people's demands. This accord has also given up on these resolutions. Needless to say, the economic accord recently signed in Paris which places not only Palestine, but also the entire Arab region and the Middle East [sentence incomplete as heard] and opens the door to the Arab and Middle East region. This issue also concerns you, I mean Iran. Why? Because this accord opens the door to the entire Middle Eastern region.
