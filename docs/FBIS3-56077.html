Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ojars Kehris, Latvia's minister of economics, and Samad Sadykov, vice premier of Azerbaijan [title as received], today in Baku, signed an agreement on trade and economic cooperation between Latvia and Azerbaijan. Moreover, Kehris and the Azerbaijani foreign minister, Gasan Gasanov, signed a protocol on the establishment of diplomatic relations between Latvia and Azerbaijan. According to the agreement, Azerbaijan will supply Latvia with fuel and oil products. Latvia, for its part, will supply Azerbaijan with engines, wagons, as well as products of the instrument-making industry.
