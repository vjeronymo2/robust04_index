BFN [Excerpt] The People's Party [PP] has been named the winner in elections held on Sunday [12 June] in Spain to the European Parliament. It received 40.2 percent of the votes cast and 28 seats. The Spanish Workers Socialist Party received 30.62 percent of the vote and 22 seats. United Left received 13.5 percent of the vote and nine seats, while Convergence and Union will have three seats and the Nationalist Coalition will have two seats. [passage omitted]
