920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Baggeridge Brick Profits of Baggeridge Brick, the West Midlands-based brickmaker and supplier of building materials, fell from Pounds 1.07m to Pounds 761,000 pre-tax for the half year ended March 31. Turnover totalled Pounds 11.9m (Pounds 13.3m). Interest accounted for Pounds 649,000 (Pounds 819,000) and tax for Pounds 251,000 (Pounds 364,000). Earnings worked through at 1.29p (1.79p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 0.75p. Operating profits of the land filling operations rose to Pounds 546,000 (Pounds 193,000) while those from building materials fell to Pounds 864,000 (Pounds 1.7m).
