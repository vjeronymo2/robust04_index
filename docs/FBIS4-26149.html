BFN [Text] Wellington, June 20 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councilor Li Tieying arrived here this morning to exchange views with New Zealand [NZ] officials on reforms conducted in their respective countries. Li, also minister of the Chinese State Commission for Restructuring the Economic Systems, will meet officials of the Wellington City Council, the NZ Reserve Bank and Treasury later today. The hosts will brief Li and his party on the reforms carried out in NZ in the past ten years. And this evening Li will make a speech at the NZ treasury on the current reforms in China. Li is scheduled to meet Prime Minister Jim Bolger and Finance Minister Bill Birch tomorrow. Li arrived in Auckland in northern NZ on Saturday afternoon.
