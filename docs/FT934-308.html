931229 FT 29 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Fenner disposal talks terminated Discussions over the sale of Fenner's power transmission division have been terminated. The industrial products company originally announced that talks were taking place with a potential purchaser when it launched a Pounds 17m rights issue in September. Mr Colin Cooke, chairman, said the group would now 'devote its full attention to running the business and implementing the programme to improve its profitability which was outlined at the time of the rights issue.'
