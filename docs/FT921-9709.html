920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / Levitt faces further 37 charges MR ROGER LEVITT, chairman of the collapsed Levitt group, yesterday faced a further 37 charges, bringing the total cash allegedly involved to Pounds 20m. He was remanded on bail until April 27 at Bow Street magistrates' court in London with three other men. Mr Robert Price, 30, of north London, formerly financial director of the Levitt Group, appeared for the first time charged with seven offences including conspiracy to defraud and fraudulent trading. Mr Mark Reed, 38, of Hampstead, north London, is now accused of 29 offences. Mr Alan McNamara, 27, of central London, is accused of 20 offences. Mr Levitt, 41, of Highgate, north London, now faces 62 charges including false accounting and conspiracy to defraud.
