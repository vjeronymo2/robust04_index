BFN [Text] The Venezuelan National Guard killed a Brazilian miner in the village of Parima, in the country's Amazon region. According to official reports, the Brazilian miner -- who was known as Franco -- shot at a Venezuelan patrol when the patrol found an illegal camp in Parima, which served as a base for gold and diamond mining. Brazilians Antonio Edilmar Ribeiro and Cicero Eduardo Britto were arrested and are being held in Puerto Ayacucho, the capital of the Amazon province.
