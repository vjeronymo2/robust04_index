April 4, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

ISRAELI PAPER QUITS

An English-language Israeli daily newspaper, the Nation, ceased publication Monday, seven months after it was launched to challenge the 56-year-old Jerusalem Post for an estimated 100,000 native English speakers.

Editor and Managing Director Hesh Kestin said directors will reorganize and refinance the Nation, which has suffered from high employee turnover since its September launching.
