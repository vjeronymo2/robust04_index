BFN [Text] The Chechen leadership may ask Russia to extradite Ruslan Khasbulatov and three opposition leaders: Doku Zavgayev, Chechen-Ingushetia's last Communist Party leader; Yaragi Mamodayev, head of the so-called government of popular trust, as well as Abdulla Bugayev, leader of Freedom political movement, and Salambek (Gadzhiyev), who are the most active opposition leaders. Official Groznyy accuses them of collusion with Russia and actions against the Chechen people. Also today, the Chechen Foreign Ministry called upon all foreign states to refrain from any contacts with the opposition.
