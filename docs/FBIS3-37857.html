Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [by ITAR-TASS special correspondents Aleksandr Potemkin and Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Krakow, 23 Feb -- The working visit to Poland by Andrey Kozyrev, foreign minister of the Russian Federation, ended with the laying of flowers on the grave of Soviet soldiers beside the walls of the old tower in the center of Krakow. The ceremony was also attended by representatives of the city authorities and by the inhabitants of the ancient capital of the Polish kings. A. Kozyrev then headed for Balice airport, from where he left for home. The Russian minister was seen off by Polish officials, the president and governor of Krakow, and also by the Russian ambassador to Poland.
