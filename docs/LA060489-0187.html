June 4, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Rambo Dancer, ridden by Jose Santos, upset favored El Senor by a nose in the $120,600 Red Smith Handicap for 3-year-olds and up at Belmont Park in Elmont, N.Y.

The Canadian-bred 5-year-old son of Northern Dancer-Fair Arabella covered 1 1/4 miles in 2 minutes 1 second.
