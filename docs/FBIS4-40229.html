BFN [Text] Ukraine does not support Russia's intention to seek a special status within the framework of NATO' Partnership for Peace program, the chief of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry's political analysis department, Igor Kharchenko, told IF. In reply to a question from the INTERFAX-Ukraine news agency Kharchenko said that "practically all member-countries were against any advantages being granted to any country involved in that program, contrary to statements by Russian politicians that Russia's opportunities were far wider than those of other countries." Kharchenko said that Russia's relations with NATO were crucial to European security. The Ukrainian Foreign Ministry official said that Ukraine was conducting "a consistent policy in order to take a decent place in the system of collective European security and not to drift either eastwards or westwards."
