BFN [Text] No'am Arnon, spokesman of the Jewish residents in Hebron, was arrested this morning in Qiryat Arba'. Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0900 GMT on 31 March adds that Arnon was arrested ``after he refused to turn over his gun.'' Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports that Arnon was scheduled to lead the right-wing rally in Qiryat Arba' today, and the organizers of the rally announced that they will not begin the rally before Arnon is released from custody. The police have not yet reacted.
