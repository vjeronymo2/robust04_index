930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Mappin site plan clears hurdle THE Department of Transport has given conditional consent to the closure of a road running through a controversial property scheme proposed by Lord Palumbo in the City of London. Permission to close Bucklersbury was the last official hurdle for the redevelopment at 1 Poultry. The scheme involves the demolition of several listed buildings on the Mappin & Webb site and their replacement with a building designed by Mr James Stirling.
