921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Cardwise sale cleared by DTI The Department of Trade and Industry has cleared the sale of part of Cardwise, a credit card protection company owned by American Express, to Credit Card Sentinel, the European arm of Datacard Corporation of Minneapolis. An inquiry into the sale was started by the Office of Fair Trading in June, but the OFT recommended against referring the sale to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission. Cardwise was formerly owned by the banks in the Access consortium.
