Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 12 (XINHUA) -- Two Chinese leaders expressed condolences today on the death of Phoumi Vongvichit, member of the Advisory Committee of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and former acting president. Ding Guangen, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretariat of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, and Li Peiyao, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), called at the Laotian Embassy here and signed the condolence book. Wreaths sent by the CPC Central Committee, the NPC Standing Committee Chairman Qiao Shi and some other departments of the Chinese Government were set up inside the embassy. Ding wrote: "Heartfelt condolences on the death of Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit, who was a member of the Advisory Committee of the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party, and a close friend of the CPC and the Chinese people." Li wrote: "Heartfelt condolences on the passing of the Chinese people's close friend Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit."
