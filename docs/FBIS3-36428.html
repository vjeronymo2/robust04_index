Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Central Electoral Commission of the Republic of Moldova has rejected the proposal put forward by the president and by the presidium of the Moldovan Parliament to organize simultaneously with the parliamentary elections on 27 February a sociological poll in order to confirm the independence of the Republic of Moldova, Chisinau Television reports. The Commission justified this decision by saying that it is not entitled to carry out such polls because the only duty of the commission is to organize parliamentary elections.
