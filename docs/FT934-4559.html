931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Bank staff safety guide published Guidelines on preventing violence aimed at bank and building society employees were published yesterday by the Health and Safety Executive. The HSE is urging managers to set up a strategy to protect staff, including advice on the use of security equipment, the issuing of personal attack alarms and training on how to react in an emergency. Prevention of violence to staff in banks and building societies. HSE Books, PO Box 1999, Sudbury, Suffolk, C010 6FS. Pounds 6.50.
