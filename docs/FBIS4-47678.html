BFN [Text] Durban May 2 SAPA -- Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi was at work on Monday [2 May], having partly recovered from a serious bout of influenza, KwaZulu government spokesman Mr T C Memela said. Mr Memela said a caucus meeting of the KwaZulu legislative assembly was planned for Wednesday when the elections and the way forward would be discussed. The homeland government, Mr Memela added, was adopting a "wait and see" attitude towards the integration of public administrations in KwaZulu/Natal and the inauguration of the new provincial government.
