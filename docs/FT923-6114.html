920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / International Capital Markets: Sears Canada plans share issue SEARS Canada plans to issue 10.5m common shares at a price of CDollars 7.25 per share for a total value of CDollars 76.125m before expenses, Reuter reports from Toronto. Sears said its major shareholder, Sears, Roebuck, will subscribe to 5.25m of the shares. Proceeds from the issue will be used to reduce debt.
