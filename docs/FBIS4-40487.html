BFN [Text] A regular session of the Security Council was held today chaired by Nursultan Nazarbayev. Under discussion were the issues of provision of the armed forces of the Republic of Kazakhstan with weapons, material and technical resources, a concept for formation and implementation of long term defense program, and the development of the defense complex of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Minister of Defense Sagadat Nurmugamabetov and Minister of Economy Mark Urkimbayev addressed those present. The president of Kazakhstan summarized the results of the discussion. The Security Council endorsed the concept for the formation and use of long term defense programs and the development of defense complex. It also approved certain changes in its composition. In particular it approved Bulat Baykenov as its secretary.
