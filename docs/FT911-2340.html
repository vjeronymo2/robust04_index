910430 FT 30 APR 91 / Commodities and Agriculture: Soviet petrochemical plant begins production A petrochemical plant which the Soviet authorities hope will produce more than 30m tonnes of oil a year began production early this month, according to the Communist Party paper Pravda yesterday, The output of well KTL-1 in the Tengiz complex near the Caspian sea is 3m tonnes a year, with a second well ready to go into production soon. The plant will also produce 460,000 tonnes of sulphur a year, replacing present imports.
