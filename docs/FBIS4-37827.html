BFN [Text] The brother president, Yasir 'Arafat, met in Tunis this morning with His Excellency Yemeni Deputy Parliament Speaker 'Abd-al-Wahab Mahmud and the delegation accompanying him. His Excellency 'Abd-al-Wahab briefed the brother president on the latest situation and the seriousness of the fast-moving and delicate developments which concern Yemen and its fraternal people. President 'Arafat stressed the importance of continuing efforts to find a just solution which would safeguard the interest of the fraternal Yemeni people and their effective role as one of the pillars of our Arab nation in its endeavour toward a better future. His Excellency 'Abd-al-Wahab Mahmud conveyed an important message from Yemen's Parliament Speaker Shaykh 'Abdallah al-Ahmar to the brother president, Yasir 'Arafat. The meeting was attended by his excellency the Yemeni ambassador to Tunisia.
