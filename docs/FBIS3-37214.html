Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Text] UN Headquarters, 14 Feb -- Russia put forward an important initiative at the UN Security Council on urgently demilitarizing Sarajevo and introducing UN control in this town, Yuliy Vorontsov, Russian Federation permanent representative at the United Nations, said at today's open discussion of the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina at the Security Council. "We note with satisfaction the agreement that UN Protection Force [UNPROFOR] leaders have attained with Bosnian Serbs and the Government of Bosnia-Herzegovina on a cease-fire and measures to ensure that all the sides, both Serbs and Muslims, urgently put their heavy armaments in the area of Sarajevo under UNPROFOR control or withdraw them from there. This is close to our positon. Russia has already more than once put forward an initiative of urgently demilitarizing Sarajevo and putting this town under UN control. We regard such measures as an important step toward settling the entire Bosnian conflictm," Vorontsov said.
