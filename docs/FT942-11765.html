940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore European net assets ahead Gartmore European Investment Trust raised net asset value per share by 11 per cent to 151.4p at March 31, against 136.3p six months earlier. Net losses for the six months came to Pounds 194,000 (Pounds 77,000) giving a deficit per share of 1.13p (0.38p). At present, the company estimates positive earnings for the year. The trust reaffirmed its policy that dividends were of secondary importance to capital growth and future payments would be paid out of earnings. Last year's final was 0.9p.
