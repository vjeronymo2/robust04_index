BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency, today received Kresimir Zubak, president of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina; General Rasim Delic, supreme commander of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army; and General Ante Roso, commander of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO] Supreme Command. Several issues were covered at this meeting concerning the work of the Joint Command of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army and the HVO, and the tasks facing it.
