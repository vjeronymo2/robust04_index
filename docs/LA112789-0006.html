November 27, 1989, Monday, Southland Edition

12-YEAR-OLD BOY FOUND DEAD ATOP ELEVATOR CAR

A 12-year-old boy was found dead atop a Harlem apartment building's elevator car, where officials believe he had been playing. The body of Walter McMillan was found Saturday evening when the car was at the 27th floor, a Housing Authority police spokesman said. The cause of death was not released immediately.
