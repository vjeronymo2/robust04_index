920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / Reserves fall by underlying Dollars 86m THE UK's gold and currency reserves fell an underlying Dollars 86m in July, indicating small-scale intervention by the Bank of England to support sterling in the exchange rate mechanism of the European Monetary System last month. The Treasury said that stripping out proceeds from the privatisation of British Telecommunications, the official reserves fell a net Dollars 296m.
