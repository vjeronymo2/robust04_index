940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Tate & Lyle Pounds 7m purchase Tate & Lyle, the sugar and sweeteners group, has paid Pounds 7.2m for Hartham Holdings, a feed and fertiliser business owned by Cultor of Finland. Hartham comprises FSL Bells, a farm feed supplement business, and Soil Fertility Dunns, a specialist fertiliser company.
