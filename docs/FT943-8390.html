940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / International Company News: Shinawatra posts sharp advance Shinawatra Computer and Communications, the Thai telecoms and computer group, has almost doubled net profits, to Bt1.21bn (Dollars 48.3m) for the first half, compared with Bt632m the same period last year, writes William Barnes in Bangkok. The buoyant figures were helped by a sharp rise in net profits, to Bt784m, at its mobile phone subsidiary, Advanced Information Services, compared with Bt384m last time.
