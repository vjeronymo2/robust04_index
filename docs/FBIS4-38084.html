BFN [Text] In continuation of the past two days' clashes around Kabul, the Afghan capital, heavy weapons fire was exchanged again today between the warring factions. According to the Central News Unit from Kabul, central areas of Kabul were the target of hundreds of rockets and several surface-to-surface missiles during today's clashes, as a result of which several buildings were destroyed, dozens of people were killed, and 276 were injured.
