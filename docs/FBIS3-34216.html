Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has ended a several-day visit to Germany, during which he held talks with German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel and other senior German officials. 'Abd-al-Majid held a news conference at the end of his visit to Bonn last night. He said he and German officials discussed ways to promote Arab-European cooperation, especially with Germany, and efforts to resolve the Middle East problem. He added that the talks also dealt with Arab viewpoints on various issues, the Arab-European dialogue, and expansion of the Security Council's permanent membership. He said that he and the German foreign minister discussed the Bosnia-Herzegovina issue and the international community's stand on it, with which the Arab League is not satisfied.
