BFN [Text] Katowice, April 25 -- Chairmen of Solidarity hard coal workplace committees decided here on Monday [25 April] to start a "rotary and indefinite-time" strike in hard coal mines on Monday night, head of the Solidarity black coal section Karol Luzniak said on Monday. Luzniak stressed the strike would be legal as Solidarity has entered into a collective dispute with the government. Meanwhile, 50 Solidarity factory committees confirmed their participation in a two-hour warning strike, to be held in Poland's central-eastern region on Tuesday, secretary of the region board in Lublin Waldemar Dudziak said in Lublin on Monday.
