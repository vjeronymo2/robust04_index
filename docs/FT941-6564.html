940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Select Industries Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.03m were announced by Select Industries, the distributor of the AirBoss wheel system in Europe, for the half year to December 31. Losses last time were Pounds 547,000. Turnover rose to Pounds 459,000 (Pounds 251,000). However there was a jump in costs and overheads to Pounds 1.54m (Pounds 868,000) including the costs associated with the group's admittance to the USM in September. Losses per share were 0.45p (0.34p).
