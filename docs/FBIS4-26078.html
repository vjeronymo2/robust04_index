BFN [Text] Beijing, June 17 (XINHUA) -- A leader of the Communist Party of China (CPC) today called for common efforts from both sides of the Taiwan Straits toward the goal of China's reunification. Li Ruihuan, a Standing Committee member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, reiterated that Taiwan is an inseparable part of China's territory and China's reunification is the "common wish and noble mission" of all Chinese people, including Taiwan compatriots. Li, also chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, made these remarks during a meeting with Ping-ti Ho, vice-president of the National Association of Chinese-Americans (of the United States). Li expressed the hope that people from all walks of life on both sides of the Taiwan Straits will make joint efforts to contribute to the great cause of China's reunification. He also hoped that exchanges and cooperation in economy, culture and other aspects across the straits would be strengthened. Li expressed appreciations to Ho for his long-time efforts in promoting Sino-U.S. relations and the exchanges across the Taiwan Straits. Wang Zhaoguo, head of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Sidney Ho, Ping-ti's son and president of Skyho AG, a Swiss company, were present at the meeting.
