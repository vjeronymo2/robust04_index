920401 FT 01 APR 92 / UK Company News: Baillie Japan In the six months to February 29 net asset value of Baillie Gifford Japan Trust fell 6.6 per cent, from 596.2p to 556.6p. Gross income fell from Pounds 314,000 to Pounds 159,000, and the loss available for ordinary holders increased to Pounds 255,000 (Pounds 66,000), or to 2.32p (0.6p) per share.
