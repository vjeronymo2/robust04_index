BFN [Text] Clashes in Yemen today took place mainly in the oil-rich governorate of Shabwah. Foreign correspondents who visited al-Mukalla area in Hadhramaut said the southern forces are reinforcing their positions in Shabwah and that military aircraft are flying over the governorate, parts of which the northern forces claimed earlier they had captured. Civilians in Hadhramaut have been called up to assist the forces in Shabwah. Meanwhile, political sources in Sanna say President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih is working on achieving swift military gains to pressure, to the greatest possible degree, the south should a cease-fire be reached.
