Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, the foreign ministers of Cuba and Cambodia ratified the strong and friendly ties between the two nations and expressed that they are in favor of extending the ties of cooperation in all possible areas, especially the economy. During his meeting with his Cambodian counterpart Norodom Sirivut, who is currently acting as the head of government, Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina reiterated the friendly ties that unite the two nations. Robaina also stated that his visit to Cambodia marks a new stage in bilateral relations and concurred with his counterpart in the hope of promoting an atmosphere of cooperation on economic and trade issues as well as updating the existing agreements between the two states. Tomorrow, Cuban Foreign Minister Robaina will meet with Chea Sim, the acting head of state and president of Cambodia's National Assembly. Prior to his departure for India, the next stop of his tour, Robaina will hold a news conference.
