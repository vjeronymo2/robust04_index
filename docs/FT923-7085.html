920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / Observer: Pardon moo Meanwhile, Japanese scientists are doing their bit to blow away another noxious pong. At a cost of Y80m, they plan to find new micro-organisms which would produce methane-free belches from cows. It seems cows, sheep and goats - not to mention swamps and paddy-fields - produce 15 per cent of the global-warming gas. Will tripe ever be the same again?
