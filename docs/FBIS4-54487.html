BFN [Excerpts] Banja Luka, 1 May (TANJUG) -- Three young women and a young man were killed this morning by shells fired from the well known Muslim strongholds of Matuzic and Tesanj at the town of Doboj. This is reported by the Serbian republic army first krajina corps' information service in Banja Luka. Another four people were seriously wounded, surgeon Milovan Miljanovic confirmed at the Doboj hospital. [passage omitted] After this morning's attack the number of people killed in Muslim attacks on Doboj over the past 20 days has risen to 11, with a further 34 being wounded. All are civilians. A general alert is in effect in Doboj today, Easter Sunday.
