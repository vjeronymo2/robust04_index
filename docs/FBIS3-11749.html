Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Relay from Mexico by correspondent Hugo Guzman] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The condolence message sent by President Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle to his Mexican counterpart, Carlos Salinas de Gortari, already has been received. Frei strongly condemned the brutal murder of Independent Revolutionary Party presidential hopeful Luis Donaldo Colosio. Chilean Foreign Minister Carlos Figueroa also conveyed his condolence and solidarity to his Mexican counterpart, Manuel Tello. Chilean Ambassador to Mexico Hugo Miranda this morning attended Colosio's wake and expressed his sorrow and condemned the attack. [passage omitted]
