BFN [Quotation marks as received] [Text] Sevastopol, 28 May -- "For the first time the negotiating process is proceeding constructively, Vice Admiral Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, Ukraine's Navy Commander, reported at a news conference on the results of the latest Russian-Ukrainian Black Sea Fleet talks, which he conducted on 27 May. The Russian side has taken up a softer position. The fact that for the first time we are discussing details in depth and are working on each facility in detail adds optimism. The Russian delegation was discussing issues that it had avoided at the talks in Sevastopol. One feels that Russia's delegation has a purpose: To resolve the issue," Mr. Bezkorovaynyy emphasized.
