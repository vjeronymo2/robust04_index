BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa today met the North Korean ambassador in Cairo. Afterward the ambassador said he briefed the foreign minister on developments in the Korean-U.S. crisis and highlighted that Democratic Korea [DPRK] has stressed its desire to solve the crisis through negotiations. He added that Musa conveyed to him Egypt's satisfaction with the positive developments in the Korean peninsula and said that Egypt hopes the crisis will be resolved peacefully. The ambassador praised the close relations between Egypt and his country and stressed the two countries' desire to develop and strengthen ties in every field.
