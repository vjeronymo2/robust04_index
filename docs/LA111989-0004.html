November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ON THE COVER

Spanning the era of two world wars, "Till We Meet Again" is a Judith Krantz story about the adventures of three women and the men in their lives.

The two-part, five-hour CBS miniseries can be seen 9 to 11 p.m. Sunday, with the conclusion airing 8 to 11 p.m. Tuesday on Channels 2 and 8.

The cast includes, clockwise from top left, Mia Sara as actress Delphine de Lancel, Michael York as her father, Lucy Gutteridge as her mother, Barry Bostwick as flight instructor Terence McGuire, Bruce Boxleitner as pilot Jock Hampton and Courteney Cox as flier Freddy de Lancel.
