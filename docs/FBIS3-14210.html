Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 22 Mar (MENA)--Victor Pasuvalyuk, special envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, arrived in Cairo tonight from Tunis on a three-day visit to Egypt during which he will hold talks with Egyptian officials on the Middle East peace process. In an arrival statement, Pasuvalyuk said that he wishes to keep Egyptian officials fully briefed on the peace talks, as Egypt is a major partner in the peace process. He added that Russia and all those concerned with the peace process appreciate Egypt's contribution to the peace efforts. The Russian official said that he conveyed to Yasir 'Arafat a message from Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev in which he states Russia's eagerness to play an effective part in the implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 904 and reiterates Russia's desire to see the negotiations restarted. He added: I discussed with 'Arafat arrangements and practical measures that could help restore the situation to normal following the Hebron massacre, which we strongly condemn.
