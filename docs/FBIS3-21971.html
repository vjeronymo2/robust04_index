Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Seven members of two separate drug networks have been captured in Istanbul. According to a statement issued by the Istanbul Security Directorate, 3.5 kg of heroin were seized in an operation in a hotel in Istanbul's Beyoglu quarter. Five persons believed to be dealing in drugs were taken into custody. Two persons were captured and 24 kg of hashish were seized in another operation launched by narcotics bureau teams in Bagcilar District.
