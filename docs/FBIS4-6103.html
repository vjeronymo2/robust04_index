BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- "Your visit is opening a new page in the history of Mongolian-Australian relations and I am sure that it would be of great value in the development of bilateral relations the government and the people of Mongolia are grateful that Australia since the very beginning has been steadily supporting the democratic renovations in Mongolia, said the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat at the dinner given in honour of the Governor General of Australia Bill Hayden staying here on an official visit. [no closing quotation marks as received] In his speech Bill Hayden said that the present visit gives him an opportunity to express on behalf of the Australian people to all the Mongolians the best wishes for success in the political and economic spheres. Australia has been persistently supporting the processes of democratization started in Mongolia since the disintegration of the Soviet Union. Australia respects the establishing of the democratic Mongolian nation. The heads of the two countries have exchanged opinions on the possibilities of developing cooperation including the issues of attracting the Australian investment into the Mongolian economy, processing of raw materials of the country, introducing the experience of Australia on keeping an extensive cattle breeding, developing infrastructure in the countryside and etc.
