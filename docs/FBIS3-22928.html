Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Guatemala City, 14 Feb (NOTIMEX) -- The National Police have disclosed at least three explosive devices were deactivated today by members of the Guatemalan Armed Forces in the northwestern Department (province) of Quiche. Three bombs were placed along public light posts in the Chichicastenango Municipality, 200 km northeast of Guatemala City, and deactivated by Army specialists. The police report adds Army troops are searching the area, attempting to locate the terrorists. According to police, a knapsack with explosive devices and documents linking guerrillas to the attempts were found in the area where the explosives were discovered. [passage omitted]
