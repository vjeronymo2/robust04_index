September 2, 1990, Sunday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

20 MISSING AS BUS SKIDS INTO RIVER

At least 20 passengers were missing after a bus slid off a highway bridge into a river about 35 miles southeast of Seoul. Four people managed to escape through windows after the bus plunged about 60 feet into the river near Yochon, according to Yonhap, the South Korean news agency. The accident occurred when the bus tried to maneuver around a car parked in the middle of the bridge.
