June 27, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

The Kid's All Right: New Kids on the Block member Donnie Wahlberg is scheduled to rejoin the group on its tour Thursday, four days after falling on stage and injuring himself during a concert. Wahlberg was released Tuesday from Saratoga Hospital after being treated for cuts and bruises on his chest and mouth. The New Kids revamped their concerts Tuesday and today in Auburn Hills, Mich.. GREG BRAXTON
