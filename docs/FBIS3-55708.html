Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Azerbaijan's television network has begun to telecast a program by the Iranian television network in the Azeri language. The telecasting of the program began on Tuesday [4 January]. The 60-minute program will be telecast daily, from 1330 to 1430 Baku time -- that is, from 1300 to 1400 Tehran time. It includes Koranic recitations and political, social, and cultural news.
