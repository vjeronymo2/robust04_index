BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received the family of compatriot Chong Il-sim in Russia on a visit to the homeland. Present there were Kim Yong-sun, secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee] the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials concerned. The family warmly congratulated President Kim Il-song on his birthday and wished him a long life in good health. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for it, had a cordial conversation with them and gave a luncheon for them. The family presented a gift to President Kim Il-song.
