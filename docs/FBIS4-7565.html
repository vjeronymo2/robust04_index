BFN [Text] Tirana, March 30 (ATA) -- The committee of the Pen International recently has sent a letter to the Serbian president, Slobodan Milosevic, expressing its concern for the maltreatment of the Albanian writers in Kosova and urging him to stop the violation of the writers' rights there. The imprisonment of Murteza Nura and Shaip Broiri is considered in the letter as an unlawful act because they are being kept in prison for a non-violent activity.
