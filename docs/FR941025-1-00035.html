If the label is not visible to the consumer when the garment is offered for sale at retail, the same figures and letters must appear legibly on the package of the garment; and. (7) When displayed for sale to consumers, is clearly and conspicuously labeled with the following statement: ``Garment is not-flame resistant. For child's safety, garment should be tight-fitting. Loose-fitting clothing is more likely to contact an ignition source and burn.'' (15 U.S.C. 1193; 15 U.S.C. 2079(B)) Dated: October 17, 1994. Sadye E.
