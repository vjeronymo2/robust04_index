940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Newly-acquired Cunard ship leaves London The Royal Viking Sun cruise ship, bought by Cunard in July for Pounds 113m, leaves London. An increase in vessels using the ports of Tilbury and London from 44 in 1991 to 102 so far this year has been attributed to investment in the Tilbury cruise ship terminal, new moorings and the D-day commemorations
