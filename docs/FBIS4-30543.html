BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- A bulletin was published on June 20 by the Mozambican Committee for Friendship With the People of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Maputo City Committee of the Mozambique Liberation Front Party in joint name on the occasion of the month of international solidarity with the Korean people. Printed in it are articles headlined "U.S. imperialists, the provoker of the Korean war," "U.S. imperialists, sworn enemy of the Korean people," "U.S. imperialists, murderers," "U.S. imperialists, disturber of peace", "U.S. imperialism denounced" and other articles. Under the title "U.S. imperialists, the provoker of the Korean war" the bulletin says the United States ignited the Korean war by instigating the South Korean puppets on the prearranged script. Whatever intrigues they may resort to, the U.S. imperialists can never conceal their true color as the provoker of the war, says the bulletin.
