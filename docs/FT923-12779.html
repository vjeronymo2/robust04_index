920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / TUC attacks government body THE Trades Union Congress yesterday complained that the Commissioner for the Rights of Trade Union Members, a body set up by the government to support union members taking legal action against their unions, was a waste of public money. The TUC said that the commissioner's annual report showed that only six people were granted 'material' assistance last year in spite of a cost of Pounds 250,000 for running the office.
