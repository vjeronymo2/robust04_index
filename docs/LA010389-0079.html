January 3, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Three Soviet players scored in the first period and Dyatcheslaz Fetisov, long pursued by the New Jersey Devils, added a goal in the second to lead the Central Red Army team to a 5-0 victory over the Devils at East Rutherford, N.J. The Red Army team extended its record to 3-0-1 in the NHL Super Series.
