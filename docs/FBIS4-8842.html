BFN [Text] Serbian forces have detained two more UN military observers from Indonesia serving in Sarajevo. This brings to six the total number of Indonesian military observers detained by the Serbs. A press release issued by the Indonesian Armed Forces headquarters in Jakarta said the six Indonesian observers are Major (Didi Sumardi), Major (Muhiddin), Major (Toto Sudarto), Major (Dwi Istianto), Major (B. Irawan), and Captain (Suryawan). They are in good condition but are not allowed to leave their post. In addition to the Indonesian officers, UN observers from Bangladesh, the Netherlands, and Jordan have also been detained by the Serbs. Efforts to free them are now being made by the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] commander and UN officials by holding talks with the Serbian and Russian officials.
