Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cape Town Dec 31 SAPA -- Thursday night's terror attack on Cape Town's Heidelberg tavern should not be allowed to provoke decent people into taking revenge, Anglican Archbishop Desmond Tutu said on Friday. Archbishop Tutu said the attackers, who left four people dead and five injured, were "agents of evil...who know they are on their last lap while the country progresses to freedom and peace", SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio reported. The archbishop is a former recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize.
