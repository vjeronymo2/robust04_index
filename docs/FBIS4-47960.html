BFN [Excerpts] Dar es Salaam, 6 May (KNA/PANA) -- The UN Secretary General, Butrus Butrus-Ghali, has endorsed an appeal by former Tanzanian president, Mwalimu Julius Nyerere, calling for urgent action to resolve the Rwandan situation. [passage omitted] In a press statement made available to Shihata, the UN secretar - general said he had given full support to the appeal, according to a press statement issued today by the UN Information Centre in Dar es Salaam. [passage omitted] Meanwhile, the UNHCR has said it urgently needed $38.5 million to provide humanitarian aid for 250,000 Rwandan refugees in Tanzania over the next three months. Aid workers have been battling torrential rains and sanitation problems to ease the plight of the refugees, who are crowded into makeshift camp near Ngara.
