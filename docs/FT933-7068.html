930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Cowies realise Pounds 5.24m from share disposal Sir Tom Cowie, who is stepping down at the end of the year as chairman of T Cowie, the motor dealer and bus and coach operator he founded, has sold part of his holding in the company. Sir Tom and Lady Cowie have disposed of 1m and 500,000 shares respectively at 260p each, in line with yesterday's market price of 261p. The disposal realised Pounds 5.24m and leaves the chairman with a stake of 4.69 per cent.
