Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] After two days of deliberations, the emergency session of the National Organization of War Veterans was concluded with a final statement. A report by Rafiq ech-Cheikh sums up the statement: [Ech-Cheikh] The statement by the council called for the crisis to be overcome and participation in putting an end to the bloodshed. The council called on all parties, national figures, and social organizations to put an end to the violence. It believes that the time has come to look forward to the future and to continue the message of peace. The statement also expressed its satisfaction with the speech by the national defense minister. It shared his view that participation in overcoming the crisis is a national issue. The statement also referred to the dialogue and to the need to persuade everyone to strengthen it. [Begin unidentified person recording] The National Council supports the position of the national secretariat that was adopted within the framework of its participation in all the stages of national dialogue, and it urges it to continue and intensify its contacts with the various parties which are hesitant, and with the rejectionists, and to persuade them to reexamine their position on participating in the national dialogue for the sake of serving the higher interests of the country. [end recording]
