Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Leonor Beleza could not resist the political and personal pressure that has now led her to hand in her resignation as deputy speaker of parliament and member of parliament. Prime Minister Cavaco Silva and Parliament Speaker Barbosa de Melo have already received the resignation. Beleza argues that, despite proof that she has no civil or criminal responsibility for irregularities when she was health minister, she is not willing to continue with a sacrifice she considers disproportionate.
