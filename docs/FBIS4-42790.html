BFN [Text] A protocol on educational cooperation between Turkey and Turkmenistan has been signed in Ankara. It was signed by National Education Minister Nevzat Ayaz and by Turkmen State Art Minister (Bayramnur Suyunov). Speaking at the signing ceremony, Ayaz said that Turkey will open a Turkish educational center in Turkmenistan. (Suyunov) said that Turkmen students studying in Turkey receive a good education.
