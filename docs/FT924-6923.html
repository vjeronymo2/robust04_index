921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / Brussels probes Del Monte deal The European Commission is to hold an inquiry into the acquisition of Del Monte Foods International by the Imerman family investment consortium and Anglo American Corporation of South Africa. The deal involves an Imerman sub-consortium and an investment consortium controlled by Anglo-American setting up a joint voting arrangement to control Royal Group Holdings, which in turn will control Del Monte, the Commission said. Interested third parties have 10 days in which to file comments during the first phase of the month-long inquiry under the EC's merger regulation.
