BFN [By Bear Li] [Text] Taipei, May 19 (CNA) -- Secretary-General Hsu Shui-teh of the Kuomintang on Thursday [19 May] released an article attributing Taiwan's political and economic achievements to the able leadership of President Li Teng-hui. Hsu wrote the article, entitled "Opening a New Era With Wisdom and Courage," to mark the fourth anniversary of Li's inauguration. In the article, Hsu says Li has successfully led Taiwan in pushing ahead with political reforms while maintaining high economic growth during the past few years. "Taiwan is now using its political success and economic strength to make a breakthrough in the diplomatic arena and exercise its influence in the mainland with its market-oriented reforms," the article says. Hsu said Li's major achievements have been implementing the constitutional reforms initiated by the late President Chiang Ming-kuo; adopting a flexible policy in order to expand Taiwan's diplomatic arena and increase its presence in the international community; and reducing cross-strait tension through increased exchanges with the mainland.
