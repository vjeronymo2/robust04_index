May 15, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: L.A. COUNTY

James R. Brewer has been promoted to executive vice president-credit administration division manager for Mitsui Manufacturers Bank. He succeeds Yuji Okada, who has returned to the parent Mitsui Bank in Tokyo. Brewer has been responsible for Mitsui's loan adjustment and real estate special assets functions. In another move, Nobushige Sakurai, senior vice president and deputy general manager, has been promoted to general manager of the bank, replacing Kunio Nagasaki, executive vice president, who has returned to Tokyo.
