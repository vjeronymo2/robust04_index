July 21, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The New York Stock Exchange reported that short interest fell 44.27 million shares to 484.82 million in the month ended July 15 . . . The American Stock Exchange reported that short interest fell 436,718 shares to 48.67 million shares . . . The Investment Company Institute reported that the assets of the nation's 468 money market mutual funds rose $2.69 billion in the week ended July 19 to an all-time record of $330.82 billion.
