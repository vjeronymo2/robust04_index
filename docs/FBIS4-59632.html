BFN [Text] Following the crime perpetrated by Zionist settlers in Hebron yesterday, an official spokesman for the Palestine National Liberation Movement, Fatah, [dissident] has stated that what happened in Hebron yesterday was definite proof exposing the falsehoods that have been going on for some time, concerning the agreements that Yasir 'Arafat concluded with the Zionist enemy. The spokesman added that what happened in Hebron yesterday also confirmed the extent of both 'Arafat's involvement and his standing in the Zionist enemy's trench. He asserted that our militant people will continue their intifadah and daily struggle and will not pay attention to the falsehoods and deception pursued by the 'Arafatist group regarding its agreements with the enemy. The Zionist attacks and massacres will not go unpunished, he said.
