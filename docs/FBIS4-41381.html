BFN [Unattributed NEGA report in the "Uzbekistan" column] [Text] Informed sources say that Uzbek President Islam Karimov recently had a kidney removal operation. In this connection all scheduled meetings with various delegation were cancelled. True, he was present at a cabinet of ministers session devoted to the introduction of the national currency. According to eyewitness accounts, he did not look very well and was present for only 20 minutes. His visit to Turkey, scheduled for 23 June, has not been canceled, though. In the course of the visit a number of earlier signed agreements bearing on credits, investments, student and teacher exchanges, and so forth, are expected to be revised.
