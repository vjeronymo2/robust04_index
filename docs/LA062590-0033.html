June 25, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Honeywell Drops Minolta Complaint: Honeywell said it was withdrawing its week-old campaign before the International Trade Commission to halt imports of Minolta Camera Co. auto-focus cameras because an expedited trial date of Sept. 3, 1991, was set in a corresponding civil suit. But Minolta said Honeywell dropped its ITC action because it is bound under contract to settle the dispute in court.
