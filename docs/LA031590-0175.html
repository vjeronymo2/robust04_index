March 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

NO. 1 TITANS, NO. 5 LONG BEACH SPLIT SOFTBALL DOUBLEHEADER

Top-ranked Cal State Fullerton split a Big West Conference softball doubleheader with No. 5 Cal State Long Beach at Fullerton Wednesday.

In the first game, Charlotte Wiley scored the winning run on Kim Kostyk's error in the 10th inning to give Fullerton (17-8, 4-3 in conference) a 2-1 triumph.

Wiley singled and stole second and third to set up the winning run.

Long Beach (17-9, 4-2) won the second game, 1-0, when Kostyk singled home Sandra Ross.

In nonconference baseball:

Pepperdine 3, UC Irvine 0 -- Jeff Pearce hit a two-run triple in the fourth inning to lead host Pepperdine (13-11). Ed Luna had two hits for UC Irvine (16-9).

UC Riverside 5, Cal State Fullerton 4 -- Cal State Fullerton (9-8) led, 4-0, after seven innings but then UC Riverside (20-8) rallied. Riverside trailed, 4-3, in the bottom of the ninth when Pete Weber hit a two-run double.

In nonconference men's tennis:

UC Irvine 5, North Carolina 4 -- Host UC Irvine (10-5) was led by Trevor Kronemann, who defeated Don Johnson, 7-6, 6-1.
