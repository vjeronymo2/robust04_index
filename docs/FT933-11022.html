930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Heritage recovers to Pounds 115,000 Heritage, the USM-quoted distributor of housewares, reported its first full-year profit since 1989 in its results for the 12 months to April 30. Continuing the recovery seen at the interim stage when profits were Pounds 53,000 the year-end figure was Pounds 115,000, against losses of Pounds 177,000. Turnover was Pounds 11.6m (Pounds 11m). Since the end of February business confidence and consumer demand had improved and in the present period sales were ahead of last year. Earnings per share were 2.14p (losses 3.29p).
