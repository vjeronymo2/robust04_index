920427 FT 27 APR 92 / UK Company News: Girozentrale in public offering GIROZENTRALE, Austria's third largest bank, plans an initial public share offering later this year aimed at widening its ownership beyond the saving banks which presently control it, writes Eric Frey in Vienna. Mr Hans Haumer, chairman, said Girozentrale has approved capital of ASch500m which it can offer as common stock to the public and convert its publicly traded profit-sharing notes, a hybrid between debt and equity, into preferred shares. Timing or terms were not specified.
