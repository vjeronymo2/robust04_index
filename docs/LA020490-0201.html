February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PRESERVING YOSEMITE

How ironic! We live in a time of the business philosophy "bottom-line mentality."

Give only if there is a large profit. MCA has too long applied this principle to our national wonder, Yosemite. Hey, that's a public park. Get the rascals out. Create a nonprofit organization and apply the profits to saving and preserving the park.

Always amazing how the private sector exploits the public one. Another example of using and abusing without a care. I am also referring to the public bailout of the Lincoln Savings & Loan mess. Where are those enlightened folks who realize there is more to this life than money, profits and greediness?

BYRON KANODE

Huntington Beach
