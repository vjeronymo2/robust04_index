BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa received Chinese Ambassador to Cairo Yang Fuchang today. The Chinese ambassador said after the meeting that his country welcomes President Husni Mubarak's visit to China. He added that Mubarak's visit to his country comes when strong relations link the two countries and hoped that the visit will promote and develop the ties between the two friendly countries. He emphasized that the two countries' views on various world issues are identical and stressed China's support for the Middle East peace process. He expressed his hope that a comprehensive and just peace will be achieved in the region and said his country is trying to advance the peace process. The ambassador said China supports the need for the Korean Peninsula to be free of all nuclear weapons and for the problem between the two Koreas to be resolved through negotiations.
