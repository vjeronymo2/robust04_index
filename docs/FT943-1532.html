940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cassell halves losses to Pounds 179,000 Cassell, the book publisher, almost halved its pre-tax losses in the six months to 30 June, from Pounds 345,000 to Pounds 179,000, on the back of turnover up 9 per cent from Pounds 8.92m to Pounds 9.69m. The company, which came to the market in June, reported an interim operating profit of Pounds 51,000 against a loss of Pounds 43,000. Losses per share were reduced from 17.7p to 7.3p.
