921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / International Capital Markets: Stockbroker to focus on Asia BARING Securities (Australia) and McIntosh Securities said their newly-merged stockbroking unit would focus on Asia for expansion, Reuter reports from Melbourne. 'We have long sought to strengthen McIntosh's linkages with Asian financial markets and this merger provides a very effective way to do so,' said Mr Bruce Parncutt, McIntosh managing director.
