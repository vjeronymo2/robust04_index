940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / Lesotho truce agreed The two warring factions in the Lesotho army have agreed to stop fighting and meet the government for talks to settle their dispute, Reuter reports from Maseru. The government spokesman, Mr Seeiso Serutla, said yesterday the two sides were selecting delegates and would meet the government for talks he expected to extend over several days. The British Commonwealth yesterday said it had sent two envoys to talk with the Lesotho government and military and political leaders.
