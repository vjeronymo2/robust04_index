940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Exmoor Dual Trust net assets rise Exmoor Dual Investment Trust, the split-capital trust which concentrates its portfolio primarily on similar such trusts, had a net asset value per ordinary share of 68.3p at the three months to November 30 against 22.44p per share a year earlier. Attributable revenue amounted to Pounds 153,873 (Pounds 165,583) for earnings of 1.74p (1.88p) per income share and a first interim dividend of 2.25p (2.5p) is declared. The directors said it was their intention in the current year to pay dividends totalling not less than 9p (10.55p) per income share and 1.58p (1.85p) per ordinary share.
