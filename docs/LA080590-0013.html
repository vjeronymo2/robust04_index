August 5, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

REPLYING FOR THE DEAD

R.A. Lee's July 29 letter regarding stars supposedly "outed" (alleged to be homosexual) since their deaths merits response, since they cannot reply.

Buster Wiles, Errol Flynn's stunt double and close friend for 24 years, scoffed at claims Flynn was attracted to men.

In 1980, Cary Grant sued Chevy Chase for commenting "I understand he is a homo." In a deposition, Chase admitted he didn't know what he was talking about.

The later holds true for tabloid biographers, eager to feast unfairly on the dead, for money.

WILLIAM DONATI

Los Angeles

"My Days With Errol Flynn" by Buster Wiles with William Donati, was published in 1988.
