921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Letter: Attitude on castle style 'amazing' Sir, Your architectural correspondent applauded ('A foolish plan to reinvent Windsor', November 30) the renovation of run-down Windsor Castle by its 'complete transformation' in the 1820s by Jeffrey Wyatville in the style of his time. However, he then declared himself amazed that the Royal Institute of British Architects has reacted to the fire at Windsor Castle by suggesting that a distinguished architect of today should also respond to the fire in the style of his time. Michael Manser, Manser Associates, architects, Bridge Studios, Hammersmith Bridge, London W6 9DA
