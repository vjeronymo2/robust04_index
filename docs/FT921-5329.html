920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: WH Smith buys 59 US music stores WH SMITH is to acquire 59 recorded music retail outlets in the US from Record World of Long Island, New York. The stores are located in New York State, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Virginia and Connecticut. The acquisition gives WH Smith a chain of 165 music stores in the north eastern US and a significant share of several metropolitan markets in Pennsylvania and New York State. The acquisition is subject to certain regulatory approvals including clearance under the Hart Scott Rodino procedure.
