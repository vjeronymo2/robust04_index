EFFECTIVE DATE: February 8, 1994. EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY COMMISSION 29 CFR Part 1601 706 Agencies AGENCY: Equal Employment Opportunity Commission. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This document effectuates a name change of a fair employment practice agency (706 Agency) which is authorized to investigate civil rights violations in employment.
