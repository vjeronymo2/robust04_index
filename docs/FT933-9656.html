930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / Blast kills Hindu militants A powerful bomb killed at least three Hindu militants in India's southern Tamil Nadu state yesterday, their group's spokesman said, Reuter reports from Madras. He said the explosion ripped through the headquarters of the right-wing Hindu Rashtriya Swayam Sevak Sangh (National Volunteers Corps) in Madras, capital of Tamil Nadu. Several people were injured.
