BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- Kim Chong-su, chairman of the Association of Koreans in Japan for Peaceful Reunification, in a statement April 20 denounced the U.S. imperialists' criminal deployment of Patriot missiles in South Korea as a very dangerous act of pushing the military situation on the Korean peninsula to the brink of war, the Tokyo-based Korean News Service reported. He demanded that the U.S. authorities renounce their hostile policy of stifling the DPRK, immediately stop the reckless war rackets and sincerely approach DPRK-USA talks for a fundamental solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula. He strongly urged the South Korean authorities to discard their flunkeyist, treacherous policy and not refuse but sincerely accept the proposal of convening a national conference to find the way of the great unity of the whole nation, a proposal made at a joint meeting of the DPRK Government, political parties and public organizations.
