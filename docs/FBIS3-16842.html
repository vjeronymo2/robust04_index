Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, MARCH 19 (SNARK) -- Today there has been relative quietness at the Karabakh front since morning. SNARK was informed by the Karabakh Department of Information and Press at some places of the front the quietness was disrupted by separate skirmishes time by time. Meanwhile, according to data of the same source, the Azerbaijani side is accumulating its troops at the eastern part of Karabakh front.
