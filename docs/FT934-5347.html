931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: BCE deficit at Pounds 162,000 BCE Holdings, the USM-quoted distributor of snooker, billiard and pool products, announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 162,000 for the six months ended September 30, compared with Pounds 168,000, on turnover down from Pounds 1.98m to Pounds 1.82m. Losses per share were 0.58p (0.6p)
