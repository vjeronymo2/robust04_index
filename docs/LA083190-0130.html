August 31, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

BAY AREA ROASTERS PLAN A NEW BLEND

In a deal that unites the area's most venerable gourmet coffee roasters, Peerless Coffee of Oakland plans to buy bankrupt United Coffee Corp. of San Francisco for $3 million.

The deal is expected to be completed by Sept. 20, but the purchase price needs approval from the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in San Francisco.

The sale should help United Coffee pay off its listed $4.1-million debt to creditors. The company owes $2 million to Wells Fargo Bank alone.
