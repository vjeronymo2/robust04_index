BFN [Text] The defense of Gorazde has fallen, and I believe that the situation is out of control, said Vitaliy Churkin, the Russian president's special envoy, today at Zagreb Airport before returning to Moscow. He believes that at present the most important thing for the Serbs is the lifting of international sanctions against them. All they have to do is to stop attacking Gorazde. However, the Serbs are not prepared to do it, he said.
