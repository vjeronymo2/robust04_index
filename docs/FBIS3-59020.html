Language: English Article Type:BFN [UK press release from the Ministry of Defense: "Contract for New FRS 2 Sea Harriers Confirmed"] [Text] 20 Jan -- A contract for 18 new FRS2 Harrier Aircraft was confirmed today by Jonathan Aitken, Minister for Defence Procurement. Answering a parliamentary question from Keith Mans MP, Mr Aitken said: "In order to sustain the Royal Navy's sea Harrier force well into the future, I am pleased to announce the decision to purchase a total of 18 new FRS2 aircraft and to convert a further 5 FRS1s to FRS2s under the current Mid Life Update programme. Contracts for the programme are being placed as satisfactory terms are agreed, under the Department's `No acceptable price, no contract' -- NAPNOC procedures, in order to secure best value for money. The contract for the 18 new aircraft has now been placed with British Aerospace at a value of some 200 million pounds."
