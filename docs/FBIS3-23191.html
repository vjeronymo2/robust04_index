Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [From the unattributed "Secrets" page] [Text] Is it an operation to send the fighters away preparatory to beginning the peace phase or is it in preparation for external military operations? Many security circles are trying to answer this question after noticing that Hizballah has started sending its groups and active cadres in the external operations division to Brazil and some other Latin American countries. It was learned that these groups joined bases the party has established there following a tour of these countries by two Hizballah officials who traveled there to make the necessary arrangements for receiving and settling the Hizballah men.
