Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 1 (SNARK)--The Azerbaijani side violated the armistice on cease-fire from 00:00, 1st of March. The armistice was achieved through the mediation of the Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev. The Press-Service of the Karabakh Defense Army told SNARK last night and this morning the positions of the Karabakh troops at some parts of the front, in particular, at the northern Martuninskiy region, were under the artillery shellings. The Karabakh side suffers 3 soldiers wounded. Following the terms of the armistice treaty Karabakh defenders made no retaliative actions. In connection with the action Seyran Oganyan, the Head of the Staff of the Karabakh Defense Army, made a declaration which says that the Karabakh side will have to undertake the adequate measures if Azeris go on with the armistice violations.
