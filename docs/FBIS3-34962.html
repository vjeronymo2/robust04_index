Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President 'Umar al-Bashir has issued a decree appointing Police Major General George Kongor Arop as second vice president of the Republic. His Excellency also issued a republican decree relieving the governors of states, their deputies, and ministers from their posts while appointing new governors. Commissioners have been charged with running the affairs of the new states at their headquarters until new governors are sworn in and entrusted with their duties.
