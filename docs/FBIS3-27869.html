Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 31 KYODO -- The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications said Monday [31] it has eased regulations in seven areas of international telecommunications service, including leased circuit and collect call services. The ministry said its approval will no longer be needed for domestic common carriers to begin additional overseas leased circuit service or for providing collect call service for countries with which Japan has regular telephone link ups. Notification of the new measures were made to three overseas telecommunications operators, KDD (Kokusai Denshin Denwa Co.), International Telecom Japan Inc. and International Digital Communications Inc.
