941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / UK Company News: West Trust expansion West Trust, the specialist food group, reported a substantial rise in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 172,000 to Pounds 736,000. Sales in the six months to September 30 advanced to Pounds 10.8m. Last time's Pounds 4.92m included Pounds 2.31m from discontinued operations. The interim dividend has been raised to 0.5p (0.2p) to reduce the disparity with the final payment. Earnings per share came to 1.24p (0.74p restated for share issue).
