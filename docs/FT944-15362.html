941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Vietnam seeks foreign tenders Vietnam is asking foreign companies to tender for two hydro electricity plants, Ham Thuan and Da Mi, which will require investment of Dollars 6.5m. Mr Tran Tuan Anh, director general of Vietnam's state planning committee, said a feasibility study for the plants has been completed with help from the Japanese government. The plants will be built on tributaries of the Dong Nai River, 150km north-east of Ho Chi Minh City.
