January 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Investors Buy 5% of Southwest Airlines: Hill Investments Ltd., a partnership owned by Dallas billionaire Margaret Hunt Hill -- sister of ex-oil tycoon Nelson Bunker Hunt -- and her three children, has acquired 5.34%, or 1.4 million shares, of Southwest Airlines as an investment. The group said it acquired the shares between Nov. 28 and Jan. 8 at prices from $21 to $25.79.
