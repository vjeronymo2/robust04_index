930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / New Oxleas plan A NEW PLAN to save the ancient Oxleas Wood in south-east London was put forward yesterday by the British Road Federation, the road user lobby group. The plan, the latest in the long saga to preserve the 8,000-year-old wood, proposes linking the East London River Crossing with the A2016 at Thamesmead south of the river rather than with the A2 at Falconwood.
