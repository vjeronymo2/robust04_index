BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow 19 April TASS -- The first round of inter-Tajik negotiations has reached its major goal of the beginning of a political dialogue between the Tajik Government and the opposition. The negotiations were held under the U.N. aegis and with active participation of the Russian Federation, special envoy of the United Nations Secretary-General Ramiro Piriz-Ballon told a news conference here today. In his words, the Tajik Government and the opposition are determined to find a peaceful solution to the complicated political problems in order to achieve national reconciliation in Tajikistan and restore national economy. The sides have managed to agree on the agenda and sign an appeal on all sides to abstain from actions that may endanger the results of political negotiations. It was an important discussion, but the sides understood it is impossible to achieve a comprehensive decision at the first round of negotiations. They will be continued in other places. Iran and Pakistan have offered their territories for further inter-Tajik talks, said Ramiro Piriz-Ballon.
