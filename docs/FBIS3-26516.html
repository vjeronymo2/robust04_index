Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] A person in charge of the provincial Taxation Bureau revealed that the province's taxation showed a strong trend of growth last year. Last year tax payments amounted to more than 36.3 billion yuan, an increase of 12.56 billion yuan over 1992, up by 52 percent. As learned, in the last 14 years of reform and opening up, the province's industrial and commercial taxes have increased at an annual rate 17.7 percent, maintaining simultaneous growth with the province's gross domestic product and national income. The proportion of its taxation to its GDP has remained at approximately 14 percent, far exceeding the country's average.
