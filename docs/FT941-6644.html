940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / Business and the Law: Courtly cantatas - Legal briefs UK Lord Chief Justice, Lord Taylor, created a stir last year by appearing on BBC Television's flagship current affairs talk show, Question Time. But even he cannot match the efforts of two US counterparts, Supreme Court Justices Ruth Bader Ginsburg and Antonin Scalia, who last month donned wigs and gowns of a more theatrical kind to appear in the Washington Opera's opening performance of Richard Strauss's Ariadne auf Naxos.
