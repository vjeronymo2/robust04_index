October 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Kids on the Block dolls, World Wrestling Federation figures and Go-Go My Walking Pup.

Lotus Development Corp.: The Cambridge, Mass.-based software maker reported that third-quarter profit plunged 63% to $8.6 million. Revenue was up 2% to $157.4 million. Lotus said costs associated with release of the latest version of its 1-2-3 spreadsheet program dampened earnings.
