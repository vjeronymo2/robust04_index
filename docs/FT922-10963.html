920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Lowland Investment steps up interim Lowland Investment Company is raising its interim dividend from 2.75p to 3p and expects to hold the final at 5.35p for the year to September 30. For the six months to March 31 pre-tax revenue rose to Pounds 1.32m (Pounds 1.25m). Earnings advanced to 4.19p (3.98p) per share. The net asset value stood at 201.2p, against 202.5p a year earlier.
