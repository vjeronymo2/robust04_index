BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov and Aleksandr Mineyev] [Text] Brussels, 22 Jun -- Today, Russia joined the "Partnership for Peace" program proposed by NATO leaders. At the alliance's headquarters, Russia's Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev signed a framework document on the general principles of participation in this program, the aims of which have already been supported by 20 states of Central, Eastern, and Northern Europe.
