BFN [Unattributed report: "Program for Building Communication Sites Was Adopted"] [Text] The Belarusian Cabinet of Ministers has approved a program for the building and upgrading of municipal facilities and roads in Braslav Rayon, Vitebsk Oblast, to provide for the safety of the population in case of an accident at the Ignalina nuclear power plant [Lithuania]. The program will be implemented in 1994-98.
