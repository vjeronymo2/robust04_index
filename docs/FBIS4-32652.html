BFN [Text] Minister of Defense Lajos Fur and Laszlo Szendrei, political state secretary at the Ministry of Defense, have said farewell to leaders of the Ministry of Defense and the Hungarian Army at a staff meeting. At the meeting Army Commander Janos Deak, speaking about the new leadership, expounded the view that all arrivals who take up the cause of defense would be accepted at the ministry. It was also announced that Zoltan Pecze, deputy state secretary at the Ministry of Defense, would be relieved from his post, effective the middle of July. Csaba Hende, state secretary at Prime Minister's Office, and Laszlo Szoeke, head of the Rehabilitation Office, have also been relieved from their posts, effective mid-July. They have all waived their claim to the lump sum payment to which they are eligible in accordance with the law on civil servants.
