Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] In a meeting presided over by Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi last night, the Cabinet discussed a program for national action during the current stage that includes an integrated strategy for all fields of action, Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif said at the end of the meeting. During the special session, Dr. Sidqi noted that elements of stability in Egypt strengthen its ability to play a pivotal role in the region and the world. He pointed out that the phenomenon of terrorism does not affect Egypt's political or economic progress, and the people of Egypt, with their culture and tenacity, will continue the march of development and progress and face all challenges with complete confidence in a bright and stable future. [passage omitted] The Cabinet also discussed a program for international cooperation to fight terrorism. The program includes a report prepared by the Cabinet expressing appreciation for the positive role played by several countries in providing important information for coordinating the fight against terrorism. The report notes the need to extend bridges of cooperation with more countries to collectively confront terrorist organizations, expedite the conclusion of cooperation agreements with a number of countries on combating terrorism, and regularly supply countries with the facts about terrorist practices and the popular reaction to these crimes.
