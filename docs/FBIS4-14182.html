BFN [Excerpt] Hani al-Hasan and 'Abbas Zaki, members of the Fatah Central Committee, and al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim, the Palestinian ambassador to Jordan, arrived in Amman this morning, to represent the Palestinian leadership in the funeral of Her Majesty Queen Zayn al-Sharaf and to extend condolences to His Majesty King Husayn on this solemn occasion. [Begin al-Hasan recording] We we were all shocked by the death of the queen mother. The Palestinian leadership immediately decided to form a delegation to extend condolences to His Majesty King Husayn. Today we come to fraternal Jordan in the name of the Palestinian people to offer condolences from the bottom of our hearts. In any case, who gave birth to His Majesty King Husayn remains alive.
