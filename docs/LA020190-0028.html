February 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Grammy Show Shaping Up: Don Henley, Fine Young Cannibals, Bonnie Raitt, Billy Joel and Miles Davis are among the Grammy nominees who will perform live on the Feb. 21 awards telecast, which will also feature special tribute segments to lifetime achievement honorees Davis and Paul McCartney. Meryl Streep will present McCartney with his award, while Sting, Larry Carlton, Herbie Hancock and Branford Marsalis will perform in the tribute to Davis. The Grammy-sponsoring National Academy of Recording Arts & Sciences also announced that Paula Abdul and Milli Vanilli are among those slated to serve as presenters on the show, which will take place at the Shrine Auditorium. BETH KLEID
