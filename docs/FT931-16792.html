930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Steel forging plant to close UNITED Engineering Steels, the joint venture between British Steel and GKN, is to close its Darlaston automotive forging plant in the West Midlands with the loss of 97 jobs. Mr John Dawson, UES Forgings divisional managing director, said: 'Demand from the automotive industry remains at a very low level.' The company's other forging businesses are unaffected by the decision.
