Title: Payor Information Form (PIF) Oil and Gas OMB Approval Number: 1010&hyph;0033 Abstract: Data collected on PIF's are used to establish payor accounts for all mineral leases on Federal and Indian lands using accounting identification numbers assigned by the Minerals Management Service (MMS). Changes in the paying responsibilities on the lease are also reported on the PIF. The MMS is then able to maintain, reconcile, and audit lease accounts through the use of its computerized Auditing and Financial System. This information will enable MMS to determine payors responsible for paying rentals and royalties to MMS and the percentage of sales or production on which royalties are to be paid. Bureau Form Number: MMS&hyph;4025 Frequency: On occasion Description of Respondents: Oil and gas lessees, onshore and offshore.
