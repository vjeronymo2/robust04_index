BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 31 May (SNARK) -- Today Georgian Parliament accepted a decision to create a special commission to struggle against the mafia. The decision is the result of the discussion the deputies were taking round the worsening criminal situation existing in Georgia. Irina Sarishvili, one of the leaders of the National-Democratic Party, said at today's session, "no doubt, mafia is heading Georgian state."
