920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Prism Leisure beats targets Prism Leisure, whose core business covers the wholesale and distribution of pre-recorded music and computer games, raised pre-tax profit from Pounds 303,000 to Pounds 374,000 in the six months to September 30. Mr Geoff Young, chairman, said the plan was for moderate growth. Sales of the USM-quoted group rose to Pounds 5.83m (Pounds 5.24m). The core business performed well, Mr Young said. He reiterated his confidence that full year figures would show a solid advance. Pre-tax profit for last year was Pounds 761,000. Earnings per share came to 6p (4.6p) and the interim dividend is again 1.5p.
