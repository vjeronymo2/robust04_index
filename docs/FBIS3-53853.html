Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 20 Jan (SPA) -- The Saudi side in the joint Saudi-Yemeni border committee arrived this evening in Riyadh led by His Excellency Dr. Muttalib 'Abdallah al-Nafisah, chairman of the body of experts at the council of ministers, after the conclusion of the seventh round of negotiations between the two sides. The following joint press communique was released by the joint Saudi- Yemeni border committee of experts: The joint Saudi-Yemeni border committee of experts held its seventh round in the city of Sanaa 17-20 January 1994. The committee resumed its deliberations about its tasks with regard to the borders between the two countries. A friendly and cordial atmosphere prevailed at the meetings. The two sides agreed to continue the negotiations and the discussions in a future meeting to be held in the city of Riyadh starting on 25 April 1994. The Saudi side to the joint Saudi-Yemeni border committee of experts left Sanaa for Riyadh and was seen off by the head and members of the Yemeni side of the committee. The Saudi side expressed its thanks to the head and members of the Yemeni side for the warm welcome and hospitality.
