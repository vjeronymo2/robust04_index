BFN [Text] Ways and means of strengthening OPEC were assessed in today's meeting between Mr. Erwin Arrieta, Venezuelan energy and mines minister, and Mr. Gholam Reza Aqazadeh, oil minister of the Islamic Republic of Iran. According to the Central News Unit, the need for greater cooperation among OPEC members, in order to further mutual economic interests, was stressed at the meeting. Mr. Aqazadeh reiterated that Venezuela and five oil-producing countries of the Persian Gulf currently control two thirds of oil reserves and 70 percent of exports. Despite the existence of different points of view, they could take joint steps toward the strengthening of OPEC, he said. The Venezuelan mines and energy minister, who met Kuwaiti oil officials before meeting the oil officials of our country, has said that, in order to achieve better prices, his country supports a reduction in oil production.
