January 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

4 ARRESTED AFTER SHOTS HIT BUILDING

Four young men were arrested Friday on suspicion of firing a rifle at an apartment building.

No one was injured by the gunfire, which broke out about 10 a.m. at a house in the 8000 block of Dracaena Street, Buena Park Police Officer Sharon Parsons said.

She said the youths apparently took turns firing a .22-caliber rifle, and some of the rounds hit an apartment complex in the 100 block of Ridgeway Street.

Seven youths were detained and four were arrested on suspicion of firing a weapon into an inhabited building.
