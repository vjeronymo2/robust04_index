BFN [Russian Federation President's Directive No. 279-rp] [Text] With a view to creating the necessary conditions for information analysis backup for the legislative process: 1. There shall be support for the proposal of the chairmen of the Federal Assembly chambers on the transfer of functions to do with the management of the Russian Federation parliamentary center to the Federal Assembly chambers. 2. The Russian Federation president's administration of affairs shall: --implement within the established procedure financial and material and technical backup for the activity of the Russian Federation parliamentary center and also socioconsumer services for its workers; --ensure, in accordance with existing legislation, the payment of wages and other payments to workers of the Russian Federation parliamentary center for the period from 13 December 1993 through the moment the functions to do with the management of the parliamentary center are transferred to the Federal Assembly chambers. 3. The Russian Federation parliamentary center shall be accommodated in Moscow, at number 2 Tsvetnyy Boulevard. [signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin, 6 June 1994.
