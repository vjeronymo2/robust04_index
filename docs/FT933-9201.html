930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Curbs planned for young drivers YOUNG DRIVERS convicted of offences such as speeding and jumping red traffic lights would have to retake the driving test under proposals released by the Department of Transport yesterday. Mr John MacGregor, transport secretary, said the government was determined to reduce the 'tragic and unnecessary waste of young lives' on Britain's roads. Drivers aged under 21 are involved in more than a quarter of all fatal car accidents.
