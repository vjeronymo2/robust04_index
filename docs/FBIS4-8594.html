BFN [Text] After a prolonged medical consultation, it was announced at a news conference at the Council of Ministers that in the last three to four days an acute illness developed in Professor Berov's liver. There are tendencies toward improvement in the clinical and laboratory indices. The patient has slight hepatitis but with a tendency to heal within a brief period of time. There are two versions about the basic cause: Either a mechanical clogging of the gall bladder vessels, which occurred and passed rapidly, or a light form of an acute hepatitis virus. This was announced by gastroenterologist Professor Konstantin Chernev. A specific blood test of the patient will be carried out abroad. The result will be known within several days.
