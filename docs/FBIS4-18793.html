BFN [Text] National Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis described Albanian Government claims regarding a Greek provocation on the Greek-Albanian border as unfounded. Yesterday, six masked men attacked a guardpost, killed two Albanian soldiers, and wounded another three. Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos rejected the Albanian charges and stressed that Greece detests and condemns every act of violence and is not willing to put up with any provocation or any attempt to associate the Greek Government with such acts.
