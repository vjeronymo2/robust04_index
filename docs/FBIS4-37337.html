BFN [Text] The Arab League has again appealed to the Yemeni leaders to stop the fighting immediately in implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 924 and in response to Arab and international efforts. It has called on them to begin negotiations immediately to safeguard the remaining resources of the fraternal Yemeni people. An Arab League official has said that Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid is continuing his intensive efforts with all the parties concerned with the crisis, the UN Security Council member states, and the UN secretary general to work for implementation of Resolution 924. The Arab League expressed regret for the noncompliance with the appeals for a cease-fire, affirming that it is necessary to stop the fighting in order to begin an effective dialogue to resolve the current Yemeni crisis.
