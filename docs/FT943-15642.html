940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / Building societies report planned The Commons Treasury and civil service committee is planning to publish an interim report on building societies in September to coincide with the government's consultation paper, Roland Rudd writes. Mr John Watts, chairman, said the committee had decided to speed up its investigation following Wednesday's announcement by ministers that societies will be able to offer a wider range of services while being more accountable to their members. The committee has been reviewing the role of societies as part of its wider investigation into financial services.
