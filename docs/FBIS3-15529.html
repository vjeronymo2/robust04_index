Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian presidential press service reported that on 7 March Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan received Jan Eliasson, chairman of the CSCE Minsk Group, and members of his delegation who are visiting the region to obtain first-hand information on the situation. The entire complex of matters pertaining to the Karabakh conflict, including the peace proposals of CSCE and Russia, were discussed during the meeting. Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan; Armenian First Deputy Foreign Minister Zhirair Liparityan; and Davit Shahnazaryan, the Armenian president's ambassador on special missions, were present at the meeting. Babken Ararktsyan, chairman of the Armenian Supreme Council, also received Jan Eliasson and his delegation today. The chairman of the Supreme Council stressed the importance of the mission for the [words indistinct] establishment of a cease-fire and political settlement of the conflict. In summarizing the results of the CSCE representatives' meetings with the party and deputies' groups at the Armenian Supreme Council, Ararktsyan affirmed that all political forces in the parliament, first of all the Armenian Pannational Movement that commands a relative majority, are in favor of the speedy establishment of a cease-fire and a negotiated settlement of the contentious issues. Babken Ararktsyan also noted that it is possible to expect maximum results from the peace efforts only if all mediation efforts are coordinated within international organizations, particularly within the CSCE framework. Of course, this does not preclude the discussion of various other proposals. In his turn, Jan Eliasson noted that the visit to Baku, Stepanakert, and Yerevan has convinced him about the existence of a real possibility for the establishment of a stable and supervisable cease-fire. Readiness was expressed to consistently continue holding consultations and regular contacts at various levels to search for effective mechanisms for the termination of military operations. During the meeting, Babken Ararktsyan also briefed the guests on the work being carried out on economic and political reforms in the republic, and on the future plans. CSCE Minsk Group Chairman Jan Eliasson held a news conference this evening at the Armenian Foreign Ministry.
