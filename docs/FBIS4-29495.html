BFN [Text] According to a VNA correspondent from Moscow, on the evening of 15 June at the Russian Foreign Ministry villa, Russian Foreign Minister Kozyrev met Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam who had just arrived in Moscow together with Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. Before the talks, Foreign Minister Kozyrev said that Russia and Vietnam share many common interests. Discussing the talks, Foreign Minister Kozyrev said that the visit to Moscow by Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet is the greatest event in the history of the development of our relations. Russia is building its relations with old friends on a firm foundation. We all share great common interests, including political interests and great interest in the region. Russia and Vietnam have very concrete, fine, firm, and friendly relations.
