October 24, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN JUAN CAPISTRANO;

APPLICANTS SOUGHT FOR PLANNING BOARD

City officials are trying to fill a vacancy on the Planning Commission that was created by the Oct. 17 resignation of former commission chairman Roy Nunn.

City residents interested in applying to serve on the commission, which meets at 7 p.m. on the second and fourth Tuesdays of the month, are asked to contact the city clerk's office at (714) 493-1171.
