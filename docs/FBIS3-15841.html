Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent anna melnikova] [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- A meeting has begun in the Kremlin between President Boris Yeltsin and representatives of an array of public and political movements, the Federation Council, State Duma groups and the government designed to draft a memorandum on civil concord and peace in Russia. "The time of confrontation is over. Russia has embarked on a new path," Boris Yeltsin said in his opening address. He proposed working out a document to bring together all political movements and representatives of the bodies of state authority.
