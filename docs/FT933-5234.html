930904 FT 04 SEP 93 / Letters to the Editor: 'Rationalists' oppose Maastricht Sir, Many people throughout Europe (and other parts of the world) have serious reservations about the Maastricht treaty, believing it to be leading European Community countries in the wrong direction with potentially disastrous consequences. We are neither Euro-sceptics nor anti-European; Euro-rationalists is a more accurate description. Paul G Byard, 6 Lobster Bay Villas, Tai Hang Hau, Clearwater Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong
