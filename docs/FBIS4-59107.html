BFN [Text] The speaker of the Libyan Parliament described Iran's policy of defending the rights of Muslims and Islamic values as independent and a source of pride for the world of Islam. At a meeting in Tripoli with our country's ambassador, which was for the purpose of handing over a written message from Majles Speaker Nateq-Nuri, the Libyan parliamentary speaker said: The free will and resistance of Islamic Iran in dealing with the problems of the world of Islam, and her defense of Islamic values, raise the morale of Muslim peoples in tackling problems. At the meeting, our country's ambassador outlined the common viewpoints of Iran and Libya on important global issues. He called for more activity by the two countries' parliamentary friendship groups. Also a written message from Nateq-Nuri about the painful and dangerous conditions in Bosnia-Herzegovina was handed to Nabih Birri, the speaker of the Lebanese National Assembly. According to a Central News Unit report from Beirut, our country's ambassador in Beirut told reporters on Monday, after delivering the message, that he had discussed ways of cooperation and coordination between parliaments of Islamic countries in the defense of the innocent and oppressed people of Bosnia-Herzegovina and to bring to an end to the massacre of that Muslim nation. Our country's ambassador added: The Gaza-Jericho agreement is, in fact, a security agreement, which is aimed at 'Arafat's police crushing the Intifadah and fighting the Palestinian nation.
