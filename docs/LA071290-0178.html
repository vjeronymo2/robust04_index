July 12, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

VOLUNTEER HELPERS OF ALL AGES SOUGHT FOR USO FACILITY WORK IN LONG BEACH

The USO of Greater Los Angeles is looking for volunteers at the USO facility in Long Beach in the following categories:

Senior volunteers -- helping with registration, information, checking, snack bar and personal services.

Program volunteers -- planning and participation in programs such as dances, recreation and community events. Must be between 17 and 25 years of age, out of high school and single.

Senior volunteer advisers -- to support program volunteers in all their programs and activities.

Evening and weekend hours are available. A military background is helpful but not required for volunteer positions. Call the USO at 437-2008 or write USO, 230 Pine Ave., Long Beach, 90802.
