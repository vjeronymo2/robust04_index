BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- At the beginning of his two-day visit to Germany, Polish Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak met Helmut Kohl on Thursday [14 April]. In their talks, Kohl and his guest will mainly discuss the extension of economic relations and Poland's desire for early European Union membership. Poland's wish to get closer to NATO will also be discussed. During his first official visit to Germany, Pawlak will also meet representatives of the Federation of German Chambers of Industry and Commerce, Bundestag President Rita Suessmuth, and Hans-Ulrich Klose, chairman of the Social Democratic Party of Germany Bundestag group. Pawlak will hold more economic talks in Stuttgart, where he is scheduled to arrive tomorrow.
