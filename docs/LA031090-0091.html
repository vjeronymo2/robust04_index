March 10, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ABORTION PILL

As a native California woman, I was proud to learn that physicians of my state who met in Anaheim took a progressive stand on the abortion issue (State Medical Group Urges Tests of French Abortion Pill, March 6).

I realize the manufacturer of this product has been harassed, much as women exercising their legal right to surgical abortions have been, but I hope the pharmaceutical company will show the courage these women have shown and begin procedures to market RU-486 in the United States.

Availability of this product, that women can use in privacy, will help to take abortion out of the public and political arenas.

MARTHA ABELL

Seal Beach
