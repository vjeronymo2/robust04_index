941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Quadrant improvement continues in first half Quadrant, the photographic and video group, continued to improve in the first half to August 31. On sales from continuing operations up 11 per cent to Pounds 25.1m pre-tax profits came to Pounds 154,000. In the previous first half pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 1.02m, because of an exceptional profit of Pounds 1.3m related to the disposal of discontinued activities. In the period under review, the precision engineering and systems division contributed a profit of Pounds 333,000 (Pounds 85,000 loss) on sales of Pounds 6.53m against Pounds 2.86m. Property and investment costs of Pounds 114,000 were connected to the group's Coventry office building, which is held for sale.
