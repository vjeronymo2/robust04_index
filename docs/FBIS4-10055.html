BFN [From the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] Demetrio Sodi de la Tijera, member of the Legislative Assembly for the Federal District, explains that his departure from the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] is because he does not agree with the way his party's new presidential candidate was elected. [Begin Sodi de la Tijera recording] I am leaving because of the way the new candidate was chosen. As far as the candidate is concerned, I have no rapport with nor do I believe he has the experience to become president of the republic. It would not be compatible to belong to a party and not agree with the candidate or the aspiring candidates. This is why I decided to resign. [end recording] It is expected that tomorrow, Thursday [as heard], he will make his departure from the PRI official. Meanwhile, Rene Bejaranom, Democratic Revolutionary Party leader in Mexico City, said his party would be willing to offer a Senate candidacy for the Federal District to Demetrio Sodi de la Tijera.
