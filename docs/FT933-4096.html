930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / Cut sought in steel imports THE ISTC, Britain's biggest steel union, is campaigning for a 10 per cent cut in steel imports in the next three years by urging employers and consumers to buy British steel products. Mr Keith Brookman, ISTC general secretary, said: 'We will be asking consumers to stop and think before they buy their next washing machine, saucepan and car.'
