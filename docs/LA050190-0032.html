May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BOB HOPE AGREES TO TRANSFER 5,700 ACRES FOR PARK SITES

No one questions Bob Hope's charity -- more than one generation would say "Thanks for the memories."

The problem with the private land sale is the continued unbridled development of this area in the face of water shortages, traffic standstill, etc. -- and simply too few wild places left in Southern California.

We must applaud the conservancy deal, for then future generations will give thanks for more than just memories.

DOUGLAS CUMMINGS

Santa Barbara
