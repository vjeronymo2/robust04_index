BFN [Text] Bophuthatsawana co-adminstartor Tjaart van der Walt has denied reports that the situation in the former homeland is getting out of control. Dr. van der Walt said that while he and his co-administrator, Mr. Job Makgoro, had held heated talks with Bophuthatswana police on pension payouts, he believed that the police were still committed to maintaining law and order. He said that he and Bophuthatswana and South African Police officials were unanimous in feeling that while the situation was volatile, it was not out of control. Dr. van der Walt said that efficiency in the civil service was improving and that it was certain that free and fair elections would be able to go ahead in that territory.
