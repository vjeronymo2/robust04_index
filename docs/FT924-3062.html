921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / International Company News: SA mines in R3.5bn project SOUTH AFRICA'S two mining giants - Anglo American and Gencor - have confirmed that they will be proceeding with their R3.5bn (Dollars 1.1bn) joint venture stainless steel project. Construction of the plant, about 120km east of Johannesburg, will start in January. The project is a joint venture between Anglo and Gencor and the Industrial Development Corporation who will be equal equity partners. The expansion will quadruple South Africa's stainless steel capacity to 500,000 tonnes a year.
