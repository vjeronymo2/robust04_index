940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Metal Bulletin expands Profits at Metal Bulletin, the international business publisher, surged from Pounds 1.86m to Pounds 2.53m pre-tax over the 12 months ended December 31. The shares reacted with a 22p rise to 380p. Mr Trevor Tarring, chairman, said another strong performance by the metals information division was enhanced by outstanding contributions from the derivatives divisions and from World Textile Publications in its first year. The 36 per cent improvement in profits, the group's sixth year of growth, was achieved on turnover Pounds 2.6m higher at Pounds 15.3m. Earnings emerged at 17.2p (13.2p) and a final proposed dividend of 7.1p makes a 10p (8p) total. Mr Tarring said the momentum experienced throughout 1993 had continued into the current year.
