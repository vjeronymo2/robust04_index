921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Cambridge Group declines 43% Cambridge Group, the Dublin-based asset financing and confirming company, saw pre-tax profits fall 43 per cent in the six months to August 31. The company warned in September of the fall and blamed the costs of focusing on core businesses and restricting business before the investment of IPounds 10m (Pounds 10.9m) in convertible loan stock by Electra. Profits were IPounds 1.43m (IPounds 2.51m) on turnover of IPounds 21.4m (IPounds 17m). Earnings per share were 1.71p (3.15p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.45p. During the period debt facilities were increased by IPounds 9m to IPounds 74.5m and recently Mitsui Leasing and Development joined its senior bank group with participation of Pounds 4m.
