April 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WEEKEND HIGHLIGHTS FOR L.A. INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL

The American Film Institute Los Angeles International Film Festival continues at the Cineplex Odeon Century Plaza Cinemas this weekend. Highlights from today's and Sunday's program follow.

TODAY

'Criminal Law'

United States, 1989, 112 minutes

8:15 p.m.

Nervy performances by Gary Oldman as a hotshot Boston attorney and Kevin Bacon as a rich psychopath aren't enough to save this contrived psychological thriller, directed by Martin Campbell and written by Mark Kasdan, from seeming morbid and incredible. Mechanical rather than persuasive or suspenseful from start to finish. KEVIN THOMAS
