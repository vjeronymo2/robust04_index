Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On the morning of 10 February, more than 2,000 people of the military region gathered happily at Bayi Auditorium to attend the Spring Festival gathering and celebrations for organs of the military region. Leading comrades of the military region and of organs of the military region, including Zhang Taiheng, Song Qingwei, and veteran leaders of the military region, zealously celebrated the Spring Festival and spent the festive day along with the people. Political Commissar Song Qingwei presided over this gathering and Commander Zhang Taiheng addressed it. First of all, he wished a happy Spring Festival to veteran leaders of the military region, and to all cadres, soldiers, workers, and staff families of organs and subordinate units of the military region, and extended festive greetings and cordial regards to comrades on behalf of the standing committee of the party committee of the military region. He said: The past year was one during which the officers and men at the higher and the lower level throughout the military region worked with one heart and one mind, exerted vigorous efforts to forge ahead, and won greater progress in comprehensively building the Army. The whole Army was full of vigor and vitality. We should attributed all this to the regular hard work of the officers and men throughout the military region. This year, the work tasks of Army building will be heavier and more arduous. We should strive to meet the demands of the new situation, actively strengthen the self-development of various organs, realistically turn them into a group of leading organs with a strong political sense, good professional skills, solid work style, and high working efficiency, lead the Army units across the military region to comprehensively implement Chairman Jiang's proposal of building the Army into a politically qualified Army with a perfect mastery of military skills, strict discipline, and good work style that can defend the country effectively, and successfully fulfill all tasks entrusted by the higher level. Commander Zhang Taiheng called on commanders and fighters across the military region to work with one heart and one mind, engage in arduous struggle, actively seek new development in Army building, and strive to create a new situation in building the Army under the correct leadership of the party Central Committee with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the nucleus and the Central Military Commission. Colorful large-scale Spring Festival celebrations were held after the gathering.
