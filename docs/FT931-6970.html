930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Murray Intl lifts dividend by 4.6% Murray International Trust, managed by Murray Johnstone, is increasing its dividend for 1992 by 4.6 per cent, from 10.9p to 11.4p, with a final of 3.5p. The trust also declared an interim of 8.1p for 1993, compared with a forecast 7.9p, and is forecasting a final of 3.5p. Holders of the B shares will receive a scrip issue on the basis of 3.95679 shares for every 100 held on March 26. Net assets at December 31 stood at Pounds 351.8m compared with Pounds 304.3m -equal to 293.3p (253.7p) per share. Net revenue was Pounds 12.9m (Pounds 13.4m) - equal to 10.9p (11.31p) per share.
