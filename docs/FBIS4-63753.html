BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Belorussia's National Bank Is Not To Be Put Under Central Bank Of Russia"] [Text] "Our parliament will not have to amend the Constitution," the prime minister of Belorussia [Belarus] stated yesterday. "I have agreed with the leadership of Russia on cutting from the text of the treaty Article 5 (on making the National Bank of Belorussia a branch of the Central Bank of Russia)," he pointed out. In the Belorussian Council of Minister they declined to either confirm or deny the prime minister's claims.
