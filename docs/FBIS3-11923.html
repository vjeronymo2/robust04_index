Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Montevideo, March 18 (CTK) -- Czech Deputy Prime Minister Jan Kalvoda, who has been on a three-day official visit to Uruguay since Thursday night [17 March], met with State Secretary of the republic's Presidium Pablo Garcia Pintos today to discuss political developments in both countries. Kalvoda also met with Uruguayan Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu Bonilla. Bonilla intended to visit the Czech Republic this June at the latest, Kalvoda told CTK after the meeting, adding that both countries would welcome if talks of experts on a Czech-Uruguayan trade agreement took place during Bonilla's visit. Kalvoda is due to meet Uruguayan President Luis Alberto Lacalle and representatives of the parliament and judiciary later in the day.
