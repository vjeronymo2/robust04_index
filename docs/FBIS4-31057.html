BFN [Excerpt] Geneva, 2 Jun (TANJUG) -- Kresimir Zubak, leader of the [Bosnian] Croat delegation and president of the Croat-Muslim Federation [of Bosnia-Herzegovina], said he will not take part in the cease-fire conference, which was to have been held in Geneva this afternoon, if a Muslim delegation does not show up. Zubak made this statement in the presence of Ante Roso, commander of the Croat forces in Bosnia. [passage omitted]
