BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 29 Apr (TANJUG) -- The newly formed group for negotiations with the Serbs and Muslims in Bosnia, consisting of U.S., Russian, European Union, and UN representatives, arrived in Pale today and immediately began talks behind closed doors with a republic of Srpska delegation led by President Radovan Karadzic. Members of the group held talks with Muslim leader Alija Izetbegovic in Sarajevo last night.
