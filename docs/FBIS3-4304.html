Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi, March 26 (XINHUA) -- A joint Sino-Vietnamese working group concluded its first round of talks on the demarcation of Beibuwan (Gulf of Tonkin) here on Friday [25 March] with the signing of the minutes of talks. During the talks beginning Tuesday, the two sides discussed the working procedures of the group and the issues relating to the demarcation. Vietnamese Deputy Foreign Minister Vu Khoan and Chinese Ambassador Zhang Qing attended the signing ceremony.
