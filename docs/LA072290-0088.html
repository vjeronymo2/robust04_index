July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GETTING FREE INFORMATION

Guides and brochures on discount shopping are available at no cost.

For brochures on Milford and Norwalk Malls and other outlets, contact Connecticut's Tourism Department, Department of Economic Development, 865 Brook St., Rocky Hill, Conn. 06067, (203) 258-4290.

For "Maine/New Hampshire Factory Outlet and Off-Price Handbook," contact Portsmouth Press, Granite State Publishing Co., 871 Islington St., Portsmouth, N.H. 03801, (603) 436- 0775).

For "Mt. Washington Valley Outlet Guide," contact Mt. Washington Valley Outlet Assn., P.O. Box 2264-D, Conway, N.H. 03818, (603) 447-5295).

For a "Visitor's Guide," contact the Freeport Merchants' Assn., P.O. Box 452, Freeport, Me. 04032, (207) 865-1212.

The Massachusetts Office of Travel & Tourism, 100 Cambridge St., 13th Floor, Boston, Mass. 02202, (617) 727-3493), offers various brochures and a listing of factory outlet locations.

For "Vermont Factory Outlets," contact the Vermont Chamber of Commerce, Department of Travel and Tourism, Box 37, Montpelier, Vt. 05601, (802) 223-3443. Jennifer Merin
