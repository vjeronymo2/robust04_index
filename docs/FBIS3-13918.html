Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn this evening visited the wounded victims from the Holy Ibrahimi Mosque's massacre, who are receiving medical treatment at the al-Husayn Medical Center in Amman and reassured himself about their condition and the treatment they receive from their brothers in this esteemed medical establishment. The wounded patients expressed thanks and appreciation to His Majesty King Husayn for his generous Hashemite initiative toward the victims of the ugly massacre, committed in the holy Ibrahimi Mosque during the blessed month of Ramadan, and Jordan's honorable pan-Arab position, under the leadership of his majesty in support of their brothers the sons of the Palestinian people and the holy shrines in all circumstances. His Majesty King Husayn had ordered the formation of a higher committee to raise donations for the massacre's victims. His majesty donated a sum of 100,000 dinars for the victims, and ordered that everything possible be provided to assist the wounded in Jordanian hospitals. King Husayn was accompanied by His Highness Prince Hamzah Bin-al-Husayn, and al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Royal Hashemite Court chief.
