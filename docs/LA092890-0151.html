September 28, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PAYCHECK: Divorced parents who welsh on their child support may soon find it harder to get a loan or a credit card. . . . Credit reporting agencies have started logging delinquent support payments in some California counties, and Orange County will be included before year's end. The data comes from the state, which gathers it from county family support enforcement agencies. . . . "Pilot programs show collections increase tremendously," says a county administrator. . . . The flip side: If you pay on time, that's reported, too. Nancy Wride and April Jackson
