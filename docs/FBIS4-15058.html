BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow April 5 TASS -- Prime Minister of the Czech Republic Vaclav Klaus has ended his visit to Russia and flew home today. During talks between Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Vaclav Klaus the documents signed were an agreement on encouraging and protecting mutual capital investments and a protocol on mutual supplies of goods in 1994. The two sides also agreed to work out a mechanism in the near future for clearing Russia's foreign debts. Vaclav Klaus was received by Russian President Boris Yeltsin. He also held talks with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev.
