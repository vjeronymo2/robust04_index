Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Lima, 5 Jan (EFE) -- On 5 January the government extended for 60 days the state of emergency in four provinces of Apurimac Department, in the southern Andean region, to expedite antiterrorist activities. For that reason, civil liberties remain suspended in the Provinces of Chincheros, Andahuaylas, Abancay, and Aymaraes, and the Armed Forces are in charge of internal order. The official newspaper EL PERUANO carried the corresponding decree today. [passage omitted]
