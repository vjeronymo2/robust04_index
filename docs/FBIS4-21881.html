BFN [Text] Palestinian sources yesterday announced that Fatah and the Islamic Resistance Movement, HAMAS, have each kidnapped an active member of the other group. According to these sources, HAMAS, which opposes the trend of compromise by the PLO with the Zionist regime, kidnapped Kamal 'Abd-al-Majid in the Gaza Strip. The Fatah faction, in turn, kidnapped Khalid Hindi.
