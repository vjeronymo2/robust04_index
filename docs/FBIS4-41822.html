BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 27 TASS -- "A separate meeting between the Armenian and Azeri leaders would be senseless since the comprehensive settlement of the Karabakh conflict can be reached only by the three sides involved Armenia, Azerbaijan and Nagorno-Karabakh," said Levon Zurabyan, the Armenian president's aide and his acting press secretary. "If such a meeting takes place, it should be attended by the Nagorno-Karabakh leader Robert Kocharyan," the aide said. The representative of the Armenian president positively assessed the results of today's talks in Yerevan conducted by the Russian president's special envoy, Vladimir Kazimirov. The Armenian president's aide pointed out the importance of such working meetings for the clarification of positions assumed by the sides on the Karabakh settlement.
