920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / Beijing in Indo-China peace role Qian Qichen, the Chinese foreign minister, sets off today on a tour of Cambodia and Vietnam that highlights Beijing's new role as peacemaker and trading power in a war-shattered region, Reuter reports from Beijing. He first flies to Cambodia for a visit delayed since December after a mob in Phnom Penh almost lynched leaders of the Chinese-backed Khmer Rouge.
