BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 31 May (NA) -- President Carlos Menem announced this morning that Radical Civic Union leader Raul Alfonsin "last night denied" establishing a "pact" with Grand Front leader Carlos Chacho Alvarez to prevent the Justicialist Party from including a clause [in the constitutional reform] to allow the reelection of governors. Menem told Radio 9.90: "We have only one pact here -- the Olivos Pact," which Menem signed with Alfonsin. The chief of state did not hide his disquiet about some headlines in Buenos Aires newspapers which report an alleged "pact" between Alfonsin and Alvarez, or on the comparison he made between Argentina and Colombia [regarding the World Soccer Cup].
