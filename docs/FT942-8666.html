940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / International Company News: Ennex International ENNEX INTERNATIONAL, Dublin-based mineral exploration company, reported pre-tax losses of Dollars 336,000 (Pounds 224,000) for 1993 against Dollars 2.79m. Attributable losses were Dollars 335,000 (Dollars 2.78m) for losses per share of 0.4 cents (3.32 cents). The company's shares trade on the USM.
