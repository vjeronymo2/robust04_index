941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Pay drop reported Half the vacancies available in jobcentres in Greater Manchester pay less than Pounds 100 a week, the independent Low Pay Unit has found. Hourly earnings have fallen in real terms from Pounds 3.70 in 1992 to Pounds 3.69 in 1994. Forty per cent of the job vacancies are for part-time work.
