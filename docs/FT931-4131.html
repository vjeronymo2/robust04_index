930312 FT 12 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Bottomley rejects routine Aids tests HEALTH SERVICE workers who believe they have any condition, including the Aids virus, which might put their patients at risk must seek medical advice, Mrs Virginia Bottomley, the health secretary, told the Commons yesterday. But she rejected calls for the routine testing of NHS staff for HIV infection on the grounds that it was not justified and might result in the issue being driven 'underground'.
