BFN [Text] Major General Ilan Biran, commander of Central Command, today decided to impose a closure on Jericho to allow the heads of the Palestinian police in the city to organize. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] wishes to make sure that all the Palestinian policemen stationed in the city are clear on their authorities and powers pertaining to Israeli citizens traveling inside Jericho. The decision came in the wake of a series of incidents over the past few days, including this morning's incident.
