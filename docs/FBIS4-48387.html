BFN [Text] Umtata May 17 SAPA -- The chief of the South African Air Force, Lt-Gen James Kriel, assured members of the former Transkei Defence Force on Tuesday that their jobs would be safe in the new South African National Defence Force. Speaking after meeting with members of the former Transkei Defence Force Gen Kriel said the integration of the airwings of the former homeland armies would not pose a problem. "People should not fear that they will lose their jobs and benefits because of this new setup. There will be no discrimination on the colour of the skin", Gen Kriel said. The Umtata airwing will close at the end of May and its aircraft will be transferred to Bloemfontein in the Orange Free State.
