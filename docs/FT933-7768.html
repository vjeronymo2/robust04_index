930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / International Company News: Newbridge Networks up sharply to CDollars 32m NEWBRIDGE Networks, the Canadian maker of specialised telecom switching gear, reported first-quarter profit of CDollars 32m (USDollars 24.4m), or 41 cents a share, up fivefold from Dollars 6.2m, or 9 cents a share, on sales of Dollars 115m, against Dollars 56m, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal.
