BFN [Text] Suhaj, 30 Apr (MENA)--Seven dangerous terrorists were killed today in Tahta in Suhaj Governorate. The State Security Investigations Department has received information that the fugitive terrorists are hiding in a desert area west of Tahta. When the security forces tried to storm the terrorists' hideout, they were fired on. The security forces returned fire and killed the seven terrorists. A Grenof [as received] gun, one automatic rifle, and three Enfield rifles were found in their possession. The seven killed terrorists are considered to be some of the most dangerous fugitives. They were involved in many terrorist incidents in southern Upper Egypt.
