941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Arthur Shaw advances 65% to Pounds 402,000 Arthur Shaw & Company, the USM-quoted builders and engineering products supplier, reported pre-tax profits ahead 65 per cent to Pounds 402,000 for the 26 weeks to October 2, against Pounds 243,000. Turnover rose from Pounds 9.85m to Pounds 10.3m. The company also said it had sold part of Jackdaw (Tools) to its management for a net asset determined sum not exceeding Pounds 537,700. Jackdaw incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 281,000 in the year to April 3 1994. The Shrewsbury engineering services and tools division is being retained. Earnings per share were 0.77p (0.46p) and the interim dividend is raised from an adjusted 0.125p to 0.2p.
