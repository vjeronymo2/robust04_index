BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Fourteen terrorists have been killed near Bingol's Dallitepe region and Diyarbakir's Silvan District following clashes with the security forces. A terrorist in Sirnak was captured alive after the bomb he was trying to place in a school exploded in his hands. In Bitlis Province and Tatvan and Ahlat Districts, 24 persons were captured on charges of cooperating with the terrorist organization.
