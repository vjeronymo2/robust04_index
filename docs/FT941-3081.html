940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / International Company News: Sales at Visa exceed Dollars 500m for first time Visa International said its worldwide sales volume for 1993 rose to Dollars 542.2bn, up 16.9 per cent from 1992's Dollars 463.7bn. This is the first time sales have topped Dollars 500bn, Reuter reports from San Francisco. The company said the number of cards issued worldwide rose to 333.1m compared with 304.6m in 1992, a gain of 9.4 per cent. It attributed the growth to the brand prominence of Visa and to renewed consumer confidence. Visa said its US sales volume was Dollars 229.9bn, up 18.2 per cent
