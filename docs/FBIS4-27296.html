BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 28 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea headed by Kim Yong-nam, vice-premier and foreign minister, left here today to participate in the foreign ministers meeting of non-aligned countries scheduled in Egypt. It was seen off at the airport by Kim Chang-kyu, vice-minister of foreign affairs, and Ahmed Shaheen, charge d'affaires ad interim of the Egyptian Embassy, and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese ambassador to Korea.
