BFN [Text] In implementing the party Central Committee's Third Plenum Resolution on renovating and revamping the party, party committee echelons in Ha Bac Province have satisfactorily screened party members to help them rectify shortcomings and mistakes. They also expelled from the party degenerated and deviant elements and those who violated discipline or acted at variance with party and state policies and lines. So far, disciplinary measures have been taken against 6,000 party members, including 2,200 who were expelled from the party for poor conduct.
