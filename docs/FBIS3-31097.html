Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Peter Weiss, first deputy chairman of the Slovak National Council, has presented to Ivan Gasparovic, chairman of the parliament, the official draft of the constitutional law on shortening the term of the Slovak parliament. The proposal says that, in keeping with the law on local elections, the elections should be held on 10-11 November. Vladimir Meciar and Jozef Pokorny, chairmen of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia and the Green Party in Slovakia, spoke in favor of shortening the term of the parliament and calling early elections. Leaders of the Party of the Democratic Left, the Hungarian Christian Democratic Movement, and Coexistence regard early elections as the best solution. At their meeting yesterday, they agreed that these elections should be held on the same date as local elections. The executive committee of the Conservative Democratic Party-Democratic Party also supports this alternative.
