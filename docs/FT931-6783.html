930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: SEET reduces deficit to Pounds 65,000 Pre-tax losses at SEET, the Edinburgh-based textile company, were reduced to Pounds 65,000 in the six months to October 31. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 455,000 for the comparable period and with a loss of Pounds 630,000 for the year to April 30. Sales for the half year increased by 39 per cent, from Pounds 3.27m to Pounds 4.54m, and the group was able to reduce borrowings by more than half with the Pounds 1.26m proceeds from the sale of Kenneth Mackenzie Holdings. Losses per share were cut to 3.2p (10.5p).
