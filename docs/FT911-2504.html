910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: Deceleration at Bletchley Motor Increased interest charges of Pounds 1.43m, against Pounds 1m, pushed pre-tax profits at Bletchley Motor Group, the garage, vehicle and contract hire company, down by 12 per cent to Pounds 623,000 for 1990. Turnover was ahead 17 per cent at Pounds 55.9m. A final dividend of 3.75p makes a 7.85p (7.5p) total.
