Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, March 8 (CTK) -- Sixty-nine percent of Slovaks consider Slovakia's integration into Europe successful and rate its military cooperation positively, according to the results of a January poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Office. Fifty-six percent of respondents, on the other hand, think Slovakia's economic performance internationally has been unsuccessful. The activity of Slovak embassies abroad is judged as a disappointment by 42 percent. Twenty-nine percent think there are still too few embassies and consulates abroad, while 12 percent reckon there are too many. Slovaks are most satisfied with the performance of Slovak sportsmen abroad and the representation of Slovak culture, with two-thirds rating it positively.
