930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / People: Geographical divide at SmithKline SmithKline Beecham, the Anglo-American drugs and consumer products group, has reorganised its European and intercontinental pharmaceuticals operations. Bradley Wilson has been appointed chairman of the European drugs business. His responsibilities will include eastern Europe, previously included in intercontinental. Wilson, 46, was previously senior vice-president of the intercontinental division, which covers Japan, the Pacific, Africa, the Middle East, Latin America and India, which has been renamed the international division. He replaces Charles Woler, who has resigned for family reasons. Mitchell Cybulski, also 46, previously senior vice-president Japan and Pacific, has been appointed chairman of the new international division. He joined SmithKline from Bristol-Myers Squibb in 1991.
