Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Feb (MENA)--The Higher State Security Court-Emergency ruled today that it will give its verdict in the case of the al-Fayyum gathering which involves 48 defendants led by Shaykh 'Umar 'Abd-al-Rahman on 28 April and ordered that the defendants remain imprisoned and the fugitives be brought back.
