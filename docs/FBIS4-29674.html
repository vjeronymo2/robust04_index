BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 17 [date as received] (KPL) -- The Vietnamese Archive Department has agreed to help the Lao Archive Department in training archivists and data collection. A memorandum of understanding on the assistance was signed here between the deputy director of the Lao department, Thongsivang, and his Vietnamese counterpart, who is on the current visit to Laos. According to the memorandum, the Vietnamese side will help train Lao technicians in this field and collect information on Lao archives. Both sides also will exchange technicians concerned with archives.
