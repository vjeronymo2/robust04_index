940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / People: Chrysalis Film Productions Ltd Lyndsey Posner, 40, has been appointed managing director of Chrysalis Film Productions Ltd, a subsidiary of Chrysalis Visual Entertainment. Posner is a former executive vice president, worldwide business affairs, at Columbia Pictures. Prior to this latest post she worked as a partner at the London law firm Simon Olswang & Co. She remains a consultant to the firm.
