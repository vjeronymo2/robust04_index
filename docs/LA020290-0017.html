February 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $40 Thursday when Monsieur Maison finished third in the third race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now down to $963.

Bob's bet today is a $35 win parlay from Solar Launch in the seventh to Silver Circus in the eighth.
