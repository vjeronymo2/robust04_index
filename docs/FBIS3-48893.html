Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 26 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The newspaper ARDYN ERH publishes an article by its correspondent trying to clear up the circumstances around the scandalous case with an "Mi-8" helicopter with 27005 number on its board. The helicopter crossed illegally the Mongolian frontier bordering on the Russian Federation on June 28 and up to now is on the Buyant-Uhaa Airport. According to a competent officer of the investigating board under the Central Police Department by August 20 the general board on ensuring state security by its former name had instituted proceedings in connection with the helicopter and carried out proper investigations. It was found that pilots of the helicopter had returned home after taking it to Mongolia. The other day Deputy Director of the Russian "Godli" firm B. B. Gomboyev came to Ulaanbaatar with the purpose of receiving money for the helicopter having been sold to the Mongolian "Oyuny Undraa" firm. In response to it we have demanded to send here their pilots having violated the law of the country, he said. According to him, the Mongolian "Oyuny Undraa" firm did not purchase the helicopter for itself but played only the role of a mediator in the selling of the helicopter.
