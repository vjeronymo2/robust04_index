BFN [Excerpt] Mr. Ernesto Zedillo, Mexican presidential candidate for the Institutional Revolutionary Party [PRI], speaking before the Mexican medical community, condemned the Guadalajara, Jalisco State car bomb attack. [Begin Zedillo recording] Anonymous violence is one of the most brutal criminal actions; it attacks Mexican society's most appreciated values and must be strongly rejected. We demand that the circumstances regarding this crime be clarified and that those responsible receive harsh punishment. We Mexicans want to live safely, with tranquillity, and with unity. Criminal forces must not be allowed to disturb our country's social peace. [end recording] The PRI candidate sent a message of solidarity to the people of Guadalajara: [Begin Zedillo recording] Once again, Guadalajara and all Jalisco residents suffer events that have absolutely nothing to do with these civic-minded and hard-working people. We join them in their demands for justice. [end recording] [passage omitted]
