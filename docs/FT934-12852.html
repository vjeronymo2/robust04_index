931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / New limits on banks' exposure THE AMOUNT of business that banks can conduct with single clients or business partners will be limited from the end of the year under European Community rules, the Bank of England has announced. Banks will not be allowed to incur an exposure to a single counterparty or group of counterparties that exceeds 25 per cent of its capital base. Exposures that individually exceed 10 per cent of a bank's capital base should not in total exceed eight times its capital base.
