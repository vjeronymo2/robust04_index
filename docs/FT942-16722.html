940408 FT 08 APR 94 / UK Company News: Ugland Intl makes Pounds 1.5m acquisition Ugland International has acquired Gerrards Rederi, the holding company of a ship owning and management group, for Dollars 2.25m (Pounds 1.51m) cash. Mr Andreas Ugland and family, have a 66.67 per cent interest in Gerrards.
