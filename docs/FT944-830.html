941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / Observer: Drooling Yule For some peculiar reason people doing unusual jobs are at this time of year granted space to tell us how they will spend Christmas day. In yesterday's Evening Standard magazine, television announcer Richard Straker says that, in such an exposed job, 'as soon as something goes wrong everybody notices'. How true. Nevertheless, he's looking forward to working on December 25: 'I actually enjoy working on Christmas Day. In the past 10 or 11 years I must have done it a dozen times.'
