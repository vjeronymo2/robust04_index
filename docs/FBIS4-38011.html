BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 26 Jun -- The Kuwaiti ministerial council held its weekly meeting today under the chairmanship of Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah, heir apparent and prime minister, and discussed a number of important issues. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dakhil, minister of state for cabinet affairs and minister of planning, said that the council issued a resolution appointing the Ministry of Finance and the General Commission for Compensations to prepare a system of rules and procedures according to which compensation would be paid for the damage and losses resulting from the Iraqi aggression against the State of Kuwait, in keeping with UN resolutions regarding the payment of the first installment of compensation. He added that the council entrusted the compensation commission with the disbursement of compensation and with following up with the UN committee with regard to measures required to pay those Kuwaitis and others who suffered damage.
