930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Dakota declines IPounds 1.08m into the red Dakota Group, the Dublin-based packaging and paper company which has a USM quote, announced pre-tax losses of IPounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.12m) for the year to September 30. Last time profits were IPounds 1.18m. Directors said the group had an extremely difficult year, primarily in its non-core computer paper and software manufacturing operations. Action had been taken to deal with the trading difficulties and the business had been refocused around the printing and packaging operations. Turnover fell to IPounds 26.9m (IPounds 29.8m). Losses per share worked through at 4p (3.3p earnings) and there is no dividend. A total of 1.1p was paid last time. The group also announced the disposal of its 81 per cent interest in Technodisc, the software manufacturing subsidiary,to BG Turnkey Services, for a nominal consideration. The loss on disposal amounts to IPounds 400,000, which will be reflected in the current year's accounts.
