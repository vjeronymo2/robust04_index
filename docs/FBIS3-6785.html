Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president of the Republic of Botswana, Sir Ketumile Masire, called on Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong at the Istana [State palace] today. Sir Ketumile, accompanied by his wife and several ministers, is on a four-day visit at the invitation of Mr. Goh. Sir Ketumile later called on President Ong Teng Cheong at the Istana. During his stay he will be visiting the National Productivity Board, the Housing and Development Board, and Jurong Town Corporation. Earlier, the foreign affairs minister of Botswana, Dr. G.K.T. Chiepe, called on her Singapore counterpart, Professor S. Jayakumar.
