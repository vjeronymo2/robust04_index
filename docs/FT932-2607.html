930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / Clinton hails UN Somalia 'success' The UN operation in Somalia was declared to be 'over and a success' by US president Bill Clinton early today following the attack by UN forces on the compound of Somali warlord General Mohammed Farah Aideed in Mogadishu yesterday. US troops are pictured returning fire at snipers outside the compound. Mr Clinton told reporters that Gen Aideed's forces had been 'crippled'.
