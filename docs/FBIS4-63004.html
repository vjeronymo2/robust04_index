BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 19 May -- French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe arrived in the Russian capital today on a three-day official visit. The head of French diplomacy will hold talks with Russian President Boris Yeltsin, with heads of the Russian Government and parliament, with Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. The Russian-French talks will mainly cover bilateral economic and political cooperation, improved interaction between the two countries in solving urgent international problems, including the Bosnian crisis, and strengthening stability and security in Europe. During his stay, the French minister will also visit Nizhniy Novgorod and St. Petersburg.
