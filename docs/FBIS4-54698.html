BFN [Text] In the zone covered by the 4th Corps, yesterday passed as usual. Usual means that constant infantry and artillery fire was opened on civilian places and positions of the fighters of Bosnia-Herzegovina from the positions of the Serbo-Montenegrin aggressor. The situation was such on the Mostar battlefield, the region of Blagaj, where chetniks operated with an antiaircraft machine gun on Ravnica. On the opposite side of the zone, in the region of (Bjelimica), the chetniks used an antiaircraft gun, antiaircraft machine guns, and a tank to operate on the villages of Mokro and (Jargul) and in the morning, they acted with a ZIS gun and an antiaircraft gun on the village of Ocrkavlje. Night was calm on the Konjic battlefield, as well as in Jablanica, the Neretvica Valley, and the region of (?Prozor). This was a report by the Information Service of the 4th Corps.
