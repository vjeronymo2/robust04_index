September 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

IRVINE VALLEY WINS MIRACOSTA SOCCER TOURNEY

Brad Wilson scored in the final minute of the first half to give Irvine Valley College a 1-0 soccer victory over Consumnes River on Sunday in the championship of the MiraCosta Tournament in Oceanside.

Aaron Ryan had three saves for Irvine Valley (5-0-1). The victory was the first tournament championship for the college.
