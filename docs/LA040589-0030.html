April 5, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

WEIL PITCHES HART TO 1-0 SOFTBALL VICTORY

Hart High won a battle of windmill pitchers as junior right-hander Kym Weil outdueled Cathy Lewis of Saugus to give Hart a 1-0 nonleague victory Tuesday night at Santa Clarita Park.

Weil (12-1), who has not allowed an earned run this season, struck out 11 and pitched a two-hitter. Lewis (7-6), a senior left-hander, pitched a five-hitter and struck out 10.

Jamie Dean scored from third base in the sixth for Hart (13-1) on a throwing error by Saugus catcher Heather Lonsberry.
