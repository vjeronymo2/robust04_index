940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: M Grenfell Equity Net asset value per share of Morgan Grenfell Equity Income Trust stood at 146p undiluted at March 31. That compared with 139.7p at the September 30 year-end and with 119.1p at end-March 1993. The fully diluted figures were 138.6p, 133.1p and 122.8p respectively. Available revenue for the six months to March 31 amounted to Pounds 597,000 (Pounds 541,000). With more shares in issue, earnings slipped to 1.74p (2.22p). The interim dividend is a same-again 2p.
