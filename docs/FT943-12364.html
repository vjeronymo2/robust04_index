940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Caribbean trade group set up Twenty five countries in the Caribbean Basin have signed an agreement to create the Association of Caribbean States (ACS) trade grouping, writes Canute James in Kingston. The ACS has a market of 200m people and includes Colombia, Mexico and Venezuela, the countries of Central America, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Haiti and Suriname and the 13 members of the Caribbean Community.
