BFN [Text] According to a Voice of Vietnam correspondent based in Iran, President Le Duc Anh and the comrades accompanying him left Tehran today, 2 May, at 1800 Iran time, or 2030 Hanoi time, thus successfully concluding their official visit to Iran. Before leaving Tehran, President Le Duc Anh and President Rafsanjani had a private meeting and witnessed the signing of a number of documents between Vietnam and Iran.
