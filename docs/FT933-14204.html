930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Bullers BULLERS is to purchase the assets and continuing business of Michael Sutty for Pounds 70,000 cash, with a further maximum Pounds 50,000 payable as royalty sales over a two-year period. Finance has been provided from bank facilities made available by Marmara Bank of Turkey, whose chairman, Mr Atilla Uras, is a Bullers director and who through his company, Auric Holdings, owns 29.9 per cent of the Bullers equity.
