BFN [Unattributed report under the "Week by Week" rubric] [Text] Administration head Yuriy Matochkin has issued a resolution on the establishment of a permanent mission of Kaliningrad Oblast to the Government of the Russian Federation in Moscow. The mission is being set up with the aim of creating conditions for operative interaction between the Kaliningrad and federal bodies of power. Anatoliy Shulgin has been appointed permanent envoy of Kaliningrad Oblast to the Government of the Russian Federation in Moscow.
