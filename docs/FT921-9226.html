920213 FT 13 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Overseas asset value at 185p Net asset value of Kleinwort Overseas Investment Trust fell to 185.39p at December 31, against 194.1p six months' earlier. At the end of 1990 it stood at 157.67p. The trust, which is 29.6 per cent invested in Europe, 26 per cent in North America, and 18.9 per cent in Japan, saw gross revenue maintained at Pounds 5.1m in 1991. Earnings per share slipped to 3.34p (3.58p) and the dividend is again 3.2p with a final of 1.7p.
