July 22, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Marine National Bank reported net income of $183,334 for the second quarter, 67% higher than the $109,814 posted for the comparable period in 1988. Revenue rose 39%, to $2.1 million from $1.5 million. Net income for the first half of the year totaled $376,058, compared to $172,058 for the same period last year. Revenue increased 44% to $4.1 million from $2.8 million. Marine National has offices in Irvine and Santa Ana.
