930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Penna back in the black with Pounds 1.94m Penna, the USM-quoted holding company for the Sanders & Sidney outplacement consultancy, returned to profits in the year to March 31. On turnover of Pounds 10.8m (Pounds 7.27m) the pre-tax line emerged at Pounds 1.94m against losses last time of Pounds 84,436. Earnings per share were 26.4p (losses of 1.6p) while the final dividend is 7p (1p) making 10p (2p) for the year.
