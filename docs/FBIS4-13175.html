BFN [Text] As many as 12 hilltop security posts of the militia in the direction of Kayan Dara in Baghlan Province have been captured by the mojahedin. A responsible security source of the country told a military correspondent of BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY that in a successful mopping-up operation, the mojahedin captured 12 militia posts in Nawbahar, Alokag, Poshtak, Debak, and Kataposta areas. In the operation 10 militia were killed and 10 others were taken prisoner. General Yaqub, a comander of the militia, is also among the prisoners. Similarly four posts of the (?coordination council's) militia in Hassantal and the old town of Baghlan, in Baghlan Province, have been captured by mojahedin and forces of the Islamic army. A mopping-up operation in Baghlan Province to clear the Baghlan Province of the militia continues.
