930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Shirescot asset value improves to 124.8p Shires High-Yielding Smaller Companies Trust, which was launched on August 28 last year, announced a net asset value per share of 124.8p at March 31 against 111.5p three months earlier. Fully diluted, the values were 120.7p and 109.6p respectively. Net revenue for the three months amounted to Pounds 97,000 equivalent to earnings of 0.78p per share basic and 0.91p fully diluted. A first interim dividend of 1p is declared.
