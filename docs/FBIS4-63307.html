BFN [Text] A two-day working meeting of Russian and Belarusian military delegations has ended in Minsk. The delegations prepared agreements on bilateral cooperation. In particular, they discussed procedures for financing military formations of strategic forces, which are temporarily deployed on Belarusian territory, as well as those for using Russia's firing ranges by formations and units of the Belarusian Air Defense. The Russian delegation was headed by Ambassador at Large Veniyamin Popov, while its military section was headed by Colonel General Gennadiy Gichkin, deputy chief of the General Staff of Russia's Armed Forces. The Belarusian delegation was headed by Lieutenant General Mikalay Churkin, chief of the General Staff of the Belarusian Armed Forces.
