Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A working meeting of experts from the Russian Federation and the Republic of Kazakhstan is continuing in Moscow. They are discussing the status of the Baykonur cosmodrome and the town of Leninsk and conditions for their use. At the moment the Kazakh side is still refusing to recognize Baykonur as a Russian military base and is insisting on its demilitarization, since Kazakhstan's constitution stipulates that there must not be any foreign military bases on the republic's territory.
