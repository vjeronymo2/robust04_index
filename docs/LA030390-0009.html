March 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION BOYS ROUNDUP

Faith Baptist 65, Brethern 59 -- The Contenders became Southern Section 1-A Division champions after their overtime victory over Brethren at Biola University.

Faith Baptist (19-10) won despite making one field goal in the fourth quarter and being outscored, 12-6, in the final 3:32.

Alex Estrada scored 11 of his game-high 26 points in the third quarter to help increase Faith Baptist's 29-24 lead to 45-35.

But Chris Beck tied the game, 55-55, with two free throws with 28 seconds left in regulation.

Brethren finished 19-9. JIM COLEMAN
