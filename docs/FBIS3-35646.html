Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN ["Text" of military communique by the Ministry of Defense Press Service on 30 January; read by announcer] [Text] The Armenian military forces are trying to take the initiative in the region, regardless of the many losses they suffered in December 1993 and January 1994. They intensely shelled Gadzylar in Kedabekskiy Rayon, (?Zengishakhly) in Agdamskiy Rayon, and Sadarak in the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic with artillery units on 28 January. One person was killed and two others were wounded in (?Zengishakhly). The Armenian military forces uninterruptedly fired on our positions in Terterskiy, Agdamskiy, and Fizulinskiy Rayons on 28 and 29 January. Violent clashes took place in those rayons during the past two days. All the enemy attacks were rebuffed. The Armenians lost seven tanks, two infantry combat vehicles, and a mortar unit. A (?ZIL) and a Kamaz vehicle were also destroyed together with their personnel. Azerbaijan's military forces suffered only a few casualties.
