August 4, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

MOCKETT GAINS U.S. AMATEUR SEMIFINALS

Cathy Mockett of Newport Beach won twice Thursday and advanced to the semifinal round of the U.S. Women's Amateur golf championships on the No. 2 course at Pinehurst County Club in North Carolina.

Mockett defeated Sarah LeBrun of Owings Mills, Md., 4 and 3, in the morning match. She then ousted Kiernan Prechtl, of Ormond Beach, Fla., in 19 holes.

Mockett plays Californian Brandie Burton of Rialto in the semifinal.
