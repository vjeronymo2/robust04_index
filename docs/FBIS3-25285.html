Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article by M.F.C.: "Israeli Mercenaries Are in Brazza; They Are Reported To Have Traveled via Belgium"] [Text] The implementation of the contract signed by the Israelis and the Congolese Government has already started. According to information received by LA LIBRE BELGIQUE, on 26 January about 10 Israelis reportedly traveled by Sabena to Kinshasa, which is separated from Brazzaville by no more than the Zaire River. We have not received confirmation of this information, but an investigation by the criminal investigation department is currently under way.

Already Paid
In its issue of 3 February, the Paris-based LA LETTRE DU CONTINENT reported that "following an assessment mission, 15 Israeli instructors have just set up in Brazzaville for 16 months, in exchange for a payment of US$49 million. The first instalment at least has already been paid into a Swiss bank account." According to LA LETTRE DU CONTINENT, it was the Israeli ambassador in Kinshasa, Shlomo Avital, who had "taken care" of finalizing this "assistance agreement," although the contract is officially a private one. LA LETTRE DU CONTINENT also indicates that "two Israeli advisers have joined the presidential staff in an individual capacity. One of them is a financial adviser and a member of the delegation currently visiting the IMF and the World Bank in Washington. The other is a traditional habitat adviser.... They have joined the president's new security adviser...."
