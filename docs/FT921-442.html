920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / 'Deloitte' dropped COOPERS & Lybrand Deloitte, the accountancy firm, is to drop the last word of its name in June. The move follows confusion among clients caused by the merger that created the combined firm in 1990. Deloitte Haskins & Sells merged with Coopers & Lybrand in the UK, but with Touche Ross in most other countries to become Deloitte Ross Tohmatsu.
