940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Western Selection Western Selection, a strategic investment company, reported profits before tax of Pounds 69,900 for the six months to March 31, up from Pounds 24,400 last time. The outcome was ameliorated by a partial write-back of Pounds 41,400 for a provision for permanent diminution in investment values no longer required. The company, which holds stakes of 42 per cent in Burlington Group and 15.2 per cent in Creston, reported a net asset value of 24.67p at March 31, up from 22.1p at the September year-end. Earnings per share improved to 0.41p (0.16p).
