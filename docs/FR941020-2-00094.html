[ID&hyph;942&hyph;05&hyph;1420&hyph;00] Filing of Plats of Survey; Idaho The plat of the following described land was officially filed in the Idaho State Office, Bureau of Land Management, Boise, Idaho, effective 9:00 a.m., October 13, 1994. The plat representing the dependent resurvey of portions of the west boundary, the subdivisional lines, and adjusted 1874 meanders of the left bank of the Snake River, and the subdivision of sections 19, 20, and 21, and a metes-and-bounds surveys in sections 19 and 20, T. 9 S., R. 29 E., Boise Meridian, Idaho, Group No. 861, was accepted, October 3, 1994. This survey was executed to meet certain administrative needs of the Bureau of Reclamation. All inquiries concerning the survey of the above described land must be sent to the Chief, Branch of Cadastral Survey, Idaho State Office, Bureau of Land Management, 3380 Americana Terrace, Boise, Idaho, 83706. Dated: October 13, 1994. Duane E. Olsen, Chief Cadastral Surveyor for Idaho. [FR Doc. 94&hyph;26031 Filed 10&hyph;19&hyph;94; 8:45 am] BILLING CODE 4310&hyph;GG&hyph;M
