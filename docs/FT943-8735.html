940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / Tesco jobs boost More than 5,000 people have applied for 400 jobs at a Tesco warehouse in at Llandevenny, Gwent, due to open next month. The supermarket is looking for junior managers, clerical staff, warehouse hands and lorry drivers for the first phase of its warehouse development and hopes to take on a further 350 when the distribution centre is fully operational.
