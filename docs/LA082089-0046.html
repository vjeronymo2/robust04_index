August 20, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Downy fabric softener's coupon tie-in ads for the 50th anniversary video issue of "The Wizard of Oz," is downright musical: "$5.00 off to see the wizard."

. . . Tough tour of duty: credits for "Casualties of War" list two personal assistants to soldier Michael J. Fox -- plus, his personal make-up person and personal hair stylist.
