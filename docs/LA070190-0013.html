July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EVERYBODY'S A CRITIC

We would like to add: India Oven on Pico Boulevard -- great Indian food, even if it is on paper plates.

And Emerald Restaurant on Venice for Thai/Chinese. Forget the Chinese and go for the fabulous soups, especially hot and spicy soup with chicken and shrimp. Great chicken curry, pad Thai noodles, et cetera.

DEBORAH R. ISHIDA

Beverly Hills
