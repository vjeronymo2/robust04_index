Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan. 10 -- Some 55 percent of respondents to a poll conducted by the Public Opinion Polling Centre (OBOP) reported that they were rather fond of Waldemar Pawlak. 46 percent liked Primate Jozef Glemp, while 45 percent were positively disposed towards Ombudsman Tadeusz Zielinski. Other popular politicians are Jacek Kuron (39 percent of the poll respondents), Aleksander Kwasniewski (36 percent), Hanna Suchocka (35 percent), Andrzej Olechowski (31 percent), Jozef Oleksy (30 percent), Wlodzimierz Cimoszewicz (28 percent) and Ryszard Bugaj (24 percent). 50 percent of the poll respondents were not sure what it is exactly that Michal Strak (head of the cabinet office), Marek Borowski (deputy prime and finance minister) and Aleksander Luczak (deputy prime and education minister) do. 48 percent dislike Centre Alliance (PC) leader Jaroslaw Kaczynski, 39 percent -- Lech Walesa, while 38 percent are rather ill disposed to Confederation for an Independent Poland leader (KPN) -- Leszek Moczulski.
