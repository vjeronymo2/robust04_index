Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] has criticized President Clinton's statement on human rights in Kashmir and said it violated diplomatic norms. In a statement, the party president, Mr. L.K. Advani, said Mr. Clinton's reference to Kashmir constitutes an unwarranted interference in India's internal affairs. He said the recent statements of U.S. Administration show that it seeks to dismember India. He urged the government to convey the nation's sentiments against such utterances which will embolden terrorists aided by Pakistan.
