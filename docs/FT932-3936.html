930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: 12p premium for Quayle Munro Shares in Quayle Munro Holdings, the small Edinburgh-based merchant bank, closed at 130p yesterday on their first day of trading, a gain of 12p on the offer price. The 10 per cent premium was less than analysts had expected. The bank, which has a portfolio of holdings in unquoted companies, has a market capitalisation of Pounds 5.2m at yesterday's closing price.
