921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 19,370 packages for the day reports the Tea Brokers Association. Landed Assams met increased competition with medium and better quality teas mostly 5 to 15p dearer while prices for plainer descriptions also tended to advance. East Africans also attracted more widespread support at 5 to 10p dearer rates with pekoe dusts again a strong feature. Offshore improved demand and prices were fully firm to dearer. The highest price realised this week was 276p for an Assam pf. Quotations quality 200p,nom good medium 142p, medium 130p, low medium 85p.
