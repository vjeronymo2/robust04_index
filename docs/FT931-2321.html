930320 FT 20 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Spot at USDollars 1,750 a tonnes, and cif supplies at Dollars 1,700. Sarawak and Indian black pepper was slightly cheaper with Sarawak black label spot at Dollars 1,100 a tonne and cif at Dollars 1,050. Malabar mg 1 was quoted at Dollars 1,075 a tonne, cif. Madagascan cloves were on offer at Dollars 770 a tonne, cif and Dollars 925 spot, while Zanzibar cloves were fetching Dollars 900 a tonne, spot. Indian sannam s4 chillies were priced at Dollars 1,100 a tonne and Mexican pimento was slightly easier at Dollars 1,700 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 1,650 cif. Jamaican pimento was unchanged at 2,275 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 2,140, shipment.
