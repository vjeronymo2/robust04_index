Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] According to reports from Tajikistan, since the beginning of January armed provocations on certain sections of the Tajik-Afghan border have become much more frequent, first and foremost, in the assigned area of responsibility of the Moscow and Pyandzh detachments of the Russian border troops. Posts and outposts periodically come under unprovoked fire from Afghan territory. In the last five days the border guards of the Pyandzh border detachment stopped three attempts of armed groups of mojahedin of the Tajik opposition to break through the border from Afghanistan. Fortunately, there have been no losses among the Russian servicemen.
