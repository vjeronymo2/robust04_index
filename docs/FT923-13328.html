920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / Remand prisoner continues fight MR LORRAIN OSMAN, Britain's longest-serving remand prisoner, yesterday began a High Court hearing to block moves to extradite him to Hong Kong where he is wanted on multi-million dollar fraud charges. Mr Osman, 61, a banker in his seventh year in prison, is also making an eighth application for a writ of habeas corpus to free him.
