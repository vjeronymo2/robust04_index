930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Uzbek sentence Uzbekistan's top court sentenced Mr Shukrullo Mirsaidov, a former vice-president, to three years' jail for abuse of power, but then granted him a presidential pardon, Reuter reports from Moscow. But the court ruled that he would have to pay back Dollars 5.6m, the amount of damage he was alleged to have caused the state.
