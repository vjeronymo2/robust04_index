Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Report by Sin Tong-chae] [Text] It has been learned that all of North Korea has entered a wartime system and an emergency order [pisangryong] was handed out to the whole Army. Defense Minister Yi Pyong-tae and Kim Tok, director of the Agency for National Security Planning, reported this to Prime Minister Yi Hoe-chang during a security-related ministers' meeting held at the prime minister's official residence on 25 March. They revealed that North Korea has suspended issuing travel permission to its residents and is heightening an atmosphere of willingness for war [iljon pulsa punwigi]. According to the report, the North Korean Army intensified military exercises and examined its emergency communications network. It has also increased construction of defense facilities, including installing barriers. Meanwhile, North Korean media, including radio broadcasts, claim that "a Southward war is inevitable." [taenam chonjaengi pulgapi hada] According to this, Prime Minister Yi instructed relevant ministers that a crisis management system should be concretely worked out to cope with an emergency and this system should be thoroughly examined.
