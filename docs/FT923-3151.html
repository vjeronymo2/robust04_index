920914 FT 14 SEP 92 / Harrods outlet for Singapore HARRODS, the Knightsbridge department store, is to open a 4,000 sq ft 'signature' shop carrying a range of goods in the Japanese Takashimaya department store due to open in Singapore next August. Harrods already runs 14 food halls and four signature shops in Mitsukoshi stores in Japan and has signed an agreement with Mitsubishi Corporation to open two accessory shops this autumn.
