940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / International Company News: Lockheed ahead at Dollars 135m Lockheed, the US aerospace group, has increased fourth-quarter income to Dollars 135m, or Dollars 2.13 a share, from Dollars 119m, or Dollars 1.95, a year ago, on revenues of Dollars 3.74bn against Dollars 2.92bn, Reuter reports from Calabasas. For the full-year, the group reports a net profit of Dollars 422m, or Dollars 6.70 a share. This compares with a loss of Dollars 283m, or Dollars 4.58, for 1992 after a one-time accounting charge. Excluding the charge, there would have been a net profit of Dollars 348m, or Dollars 5.65 a share, for 1992. Revenues for the full year totalled Dollars 13.07bn, up from Dollars 10.1bn in 1992.
