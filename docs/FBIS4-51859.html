BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 1 (KNS-KCNA) -- [dateline as received] Societies for the study of Kim Chong-il's works are inaugurated in Japan one after another. The Maiden Hair-tree Society and the Ryukyu House of Okinawa, societies for the study of his works, were recently inaugurated in Osaka and Okinawa. The chairmen, advisers and secretaries of the societies were elected at the inaugural meetings.
