BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin has notified PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat that the dialogue between them will go on in order to continue the implementation of the agreements between Israel and the PLO. Our political correspondent Gadi Sukenik has learned from sources in the PLO that this remark was made in a personal letter Rabin sent to 'Arafat yesterday morning. The letter was taken to Tunis by Jamil al-Tarifi. The letter is warmly worded and in it, Rabin expresses his commitment to the peace process and to cooperation with 'Arafat. In the letter, Mr. Rabin also expresses a commitment to help the autonomy settle in, but it does not contain any financial commitment.
