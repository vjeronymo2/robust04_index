May 5, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DANA HILLS BOUNCES BACK

Dana Hills High School scored twice in the seventh inning to beat Capistrano Valley, 5-4, in a South Coast League baseball game Friday at Dana Hills.

Mike Robertson had a two-out triple to score Ryan Delfs to tie it, 4-4, and Albie Sukut followed with a single to win the game for Dana Hills (13-10-1, 7-6 in league).

Capistrano Valley took a 4-3 lead in the top of the seventh inning when Chris Lugo scored on John Mackey's two-out double.

Dana Hills tied the score, 3-3, on Keith Barnett's two-run home run in the fourth inning. He also doubled and scored Dana Hills' first run in the second.
