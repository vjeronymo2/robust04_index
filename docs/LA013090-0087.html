January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

QUOTABLE

'Anything good, we're not going to turn down. I expect she could convince you of most anything.

She's a beautiful girl. I think I'd have to vote for her if she ran against me."

-- Henry Edward Braselton, mayor of Braselton, Ga., the town that Kim Basinger has bought a large portion of. Basinger needs government approval to build a planned movie and recording studio complex in the town.
