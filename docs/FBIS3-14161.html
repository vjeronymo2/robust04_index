Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] has sent a message to Indonesian President Suharto, chairman of the Non-Aligned Movement, concerning the meetings that took place in Tunis in recent days with the U.S., Russian, and Israeli delegations. The message also dealt with the Palestinian requests related to the implementation of Resolution 904 and the provision of international protection for our people under occupation. Brother Abu-'Ammar urged Suharto to exert efforts with the concerned sides and at the United Nations for the rapid implementation of the resolution. Brother Abu-'Ammar handed the message to the Indonesian charge d'affaires when he received him this afternoon.
