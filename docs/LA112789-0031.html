November 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

AIRPLANE COMFORT

Your story about Delta Airlines' huge order for new aircraft (Part A, Nov. 15) confirmed what we frequent fliers with long legs have long known to be the painful truth.

I quote:

"The MD-90's fuselage will be 57 inches longer than the MD-80's, which will allow 10 additional passenger seats."

Double-decked, I hope!

DICK WOOLLEN

North Hollywood
