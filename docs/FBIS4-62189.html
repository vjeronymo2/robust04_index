BFN [Text] Baku, 12 May (AZERTAC)--The Azeri Armed Forces have been fighting for more than 24 hours, trying to restitute villages of Seysulan and Gapanli, which are situated in the district of Terterskiy and were occupied by the Armenian Armed Forces yesterday. According to the Defence Ministry of Azerbaijan, Azeri soldiers drove out the enemy from the village of Gapanli yesterday in the evening. The Armenian Armed Forces resumed their offensive in this sector of the front today in the morning. Units of the Azeri Army approached the southern outskirts of the village of Seysulan in the afternoon, the Defence Ministry reported. The Azeri defences are subject to heavy shelling in the districts of Geranboyskiy and Kelbadzharskiy.
