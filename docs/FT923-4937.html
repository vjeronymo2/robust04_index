920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Cambridge issues profits warning Cambridge Group, the Dublin-based asset financing and confirming company, yesterday warned that current year profits would fall short of the IPounds 5.02m (Pounds 4.75m) recorded in the 12 months to February 29 1992. Directors blamed the economic downturn, closure costs - two of the group's operating locations are to be closed - and investment in management and support systems.
