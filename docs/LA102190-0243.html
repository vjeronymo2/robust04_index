October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

AUCTION: VAN NUYS CONDOS

An auction offering 49 contemporary condominiums at Cabrito Park Villas in Van Nuys will be conducted at 1 p.m. next Sunday by Kennedy-Wilson Inc. Minimum bids for the sale start at $85,000.

The Mediterranean-style units at 8020 Langdon Ave., Van Nuys, range in size from 970 to 1,472 square feet, have 2 to 3 bedrooms, 2 1/2 baths, cathedral ceilings and wood-burning fireplaces; common areas include courtyards and pool.

Financing has been arranged by the seller. For further information regarding terms of sale, call (800) 933-0305 or visit the on-site information office, open daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
