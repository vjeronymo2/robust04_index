BFN [Text] On the morning of 18 June, President Kim Il-song, secretary general of central committee of the Korean Workers' Party and president of the DPRK, received in an atmosphere of warmth and had discussions with a high ranking military delegation from Vietnam led by Senior General Doan Khue, National Defense minister, which is on an official visit to the DPRK. President Kim Il-song welcomed the friendly official visit to the DPRK by the Vietnamese high ranking-military delegation. The president thanked Senior General Doan Khue for the fine comments in his speech which reflect his fine sentiments toward the people and the People's Army of the DPRK, and hoped the visit would contribute to promoting the friendly ties between the two armies and peoples.
