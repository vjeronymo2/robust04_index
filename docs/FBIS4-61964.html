BFN [Text] The new composition of deputies of Ukraine's Supreme Council will begin its work on 11 May. An agreement on creating a parliamentary centrist bloc was approved at a meeting of leaders of several deputies' groups and factions. This was stated by Vyacheslav Chornovil, head of the Rukh group in the Supreme Council, at a news conference in Kiev on 10 May. He noted that representatives of the "Tsentr" [Center] group, the Rukh group, "Derzhavnist" [Statehood], and "Za Reformy Dlya Narodu" [in Support of Reforms for the People] had joined the bloc. Chornovil stressed that a meeting of the bloc will discuss the groups' positions on basic issues, which are to be submitted for parliament's consideration.
