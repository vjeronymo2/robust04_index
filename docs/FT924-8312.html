921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / Southern Russia's rebel leader threatens war on Moscow Southern Russia's rebel Chechen leader yesterday threatened to declare war on Moscow if it did not withdraw troops from around his republic's borders, Reuter reports. General Dzhokhar Dudayev imposed a state of emergency, mobilised most adult males and called on people 'to rise up in defence of Chechen independence'. He claimed Russian forces were on Chechen territory and gave them until today to pull out peacefully.
