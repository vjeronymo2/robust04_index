BFN [Text] There is more on our report on the storming of Fatah Revolutionary Council [FRC] centers in al-Rawdah and 'Ayta al- Fakhkhar in al-Biqa' this morning. Lebanese Army units, supported by Syrian Arab Forces, this evening completed emptying the depots. They seized a large quantity of weapons, ammunition, artillery rounds, Grad rockets, and military hardware estimated at 25 truckloads. They also seized a number of trucks, military vehicles, and tractors.
