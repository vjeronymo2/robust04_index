Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz has called for efforts to be intensified and for objections to be more loudly raised in the region and the world against the unjust ones and aggressors who covet peoples' rights and threaten their security and legitimate interests. He stressed the need for justice and peace to prevail among peoples. Receiving a delegation of Eastern Catholic patriarchs, Tariq 'Aziz reviewed the dimensions of the plot that targets all countries of the region and their peoples' security, stability, and peace. He said that the unjust blockade, which the arrogant powers, especially the United States and Zionism, insist on imposing on Iraq, constitutes one of the landmarks of this plot. He stressed that the blockade must be lifted since Iraq has honored its obligations in accordance with Resolution 687. Bishop Rafael (Bidawid), head of the delegation and the Babylon Chaldean patriarchate, stressed the need to put an end to the suffering of the patient Iraqi people through serious [word indistinct] at all forums to lift the unjust blockade and to establish peace and justice in the region.
