Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, Mexico 22 Jan (EFE) -- The rebel Zapatist National Liberation Army (EZLN) is willing to trade former Chiapas Governor Absalon Garcia for the guerrillas arrested by the Army during the first clashes. Manuel Camacho Solis, government delegate for peace and reconciliation, accepted in a televised speech at 1900 GMT the Zapatists' proposal and agreed to meet with them at an unspecified place.
