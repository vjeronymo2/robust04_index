940414 FT 14 APR 94 / UK Company News in Brief: St James Beach Hotels ST JAMES Beach Hotels: 6.5m new ordinary shares have been placed at 120p each raising Pounds 7.2m net of expenses. The placing values the company, which owns three luxury hotels in Barbados, at Pounds 24.3m. It is fully underwritten by the Bank of NT Butterfield & Son.
