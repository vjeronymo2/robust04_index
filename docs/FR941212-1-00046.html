Section 3.21 is proposed to be revised to read as follows: §3.21 Identification of patents and patent applications. An assignment relating to a patent must identify the patent by the patent number. An assignment relating to an international patent application which designates the United States of America must identify the international application by the international application number (e.g., PCT/US90/01234).
