920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / The Lex Column: Lloyd's It is not much comfort for those members confronted by the reality of their losses at yesterday's annual meeting. But there is evidence that Lloyd's is not as fragile as the critics insist. The market's solvency has improved - as measured by the 13 per cent increase in the society's resources - and premium rates have risen over the last three months in most of the main business classes. Things may just be looking up.
