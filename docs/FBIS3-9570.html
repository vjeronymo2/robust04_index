Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] At its session in Prague today, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Bohemia [KSCM] expelled five deputies from the Left Bloc. They are: Jaroslav Ortman, chairman of the Left Bloc group and deputy chairman of the party Left Bloc, and members of the group Josef Hajek, Jaroslav Soural, Vlastislav Kuchar, and Zdenek Vorlicek. KSCM Chairman Miroslav Grebenicek said that the aforementioned deputies had practically departed from the Communist Party by initiating the emergence of the new party, the Left Bloc.
