920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Independent News up 35% to IPounds 7m Independent Newspapers, the Irish publishing group headed by Mr Tony O'Reilly, announced a 35 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from IPounds 5.2m to IPounds 7m (Pounds 6.6m) for the half year to June 30. Turnover rose by 4 per cent to IPounds 77.4m (IPounds 74.5m). Mr O'Reilly said the result reflected improved margins in the Irish publishing operations which had come from circulation increases, advertising market share improvements, and introduction of new technology. He said the group had also recently acquired a 2 per cent stake in the Mirror Group. Earnings per share rose by 29 per cent to 16.2p (12.6p) and an interim dividend of 5.5p (5p) is declared.
