Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Shahid Khan, Pakistani state minister for the economy, has arrived on an official visit to Ankara. He was received at Ankara's Esenboga Airport by State Minister Nafiz Kurt. In a statement at the airport, Kurt said that talks in Ankara will primarily focus on problems concerning a highway tender, which was taken on by a Turkish firm and later canceled by the Pakistani Government. Khan remarked in turn that bilateral economic relations as well as the problems in connection with the tender will be reviewed during his visit. Khan announced that he will convey invitations from the Pakistani prime minister and president to President Suleyman Demirel and Prime Minister Tansu Ciller.
