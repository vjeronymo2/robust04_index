920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Cont Stationery in management buy-out Continuous Stationery, the printing group which incorporates the Prontaprint chain, has been acquired in a 'public to private' management buy-out. The buy-out - backed by 3i, the investment capital group - values the company at Pounds 6.8m. Following the management's offer of 40p per share in cash, 88.69 per cent of Continuous Stationery is now owned or controlled by the new company.
