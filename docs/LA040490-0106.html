April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE/MUSIC

More Entertainment: The Metropolitan Opera Assn. has selected Entertainment Corp. U.S.A. as the exclusive presenter of summer attractions at the Metropolitan Opera House from 1991-1994. The agreement marks the first time since the Sol Hurok era that an outside presenter has entered into a long-term agreement with the Met. BETH KLEID
