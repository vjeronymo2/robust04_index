July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GRAND OPENINGS;

150 HOMES FOR SALE IN 9 NEIGHBORHOODS

More than five years of planning and development is being capped today as 150 homes in nine different neighborhoods go up for sale in the 4,000-acre Moreno Valley Ranch.

The smallest homes have three bedrooms, two bathrooms, about 1,500 square feet of living space and start at about $135,000. The largest homes have about 2,500 square feet, five bedrooms and three baths.

All of the homes have either Spanish Colonial or Spanish Monterey designs. The development will have 7,500 homes when it is completed in about five years.

The project has a 27-hole golf course designed by Peter Dye and a 35-acre lake for boating and fishing. It also has a large clubhouse, Olympic-size swimming pool and many other recreational amenities.

Several schools and a day-care center are also under construction on the site, which is being masterdeveloped by Warmington Co. of Costa Mesa. More than 1,700 acres have been set aside as parks or other open space.

Most of the development is open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., although some of the builders keep other hours. The project can be reached from the southbound Perris Boulevard exit of California 60.

Agoura Development Has 45 2-Story Homes

Silver Valley, a 45-unit residential development, has opened at 5684 Silver Valley Ave. in Agoura.

The project, developed by Haseko-Stone, offers two-story, single-family homes with four or five bedrooms and three or four baths, ranging in size from 2,870 to 3,364 square feet.

The homes, which sell from $445,000 to $560,000, have been designed in a Spanish architectural style with red tile roofs and textured stucco.

The interiors have vaulted ceilings, soaring pillars, fireplaces, sun decks and in some models, a winding staircase.

Three models are available for inspection Saturday through Thursday, noon to 5 p.m.

Seagate Colony Models Open for Viewing

Six fully furnished models with one- and two-bedroom floor plans are open for viewing at Seagate Colony, a 416-unit townhouse development in Aliso Viejo.

The 27 homes for sale in phase 1 range in size from 606 to 1,125 square feet and are priced at $120,900 to $166,900. Monthly dues are $173.

The project is being built by Kathryn G. Thompson Development Co. and move-ins are expected in November. The models are open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. daily at 4 Jaeger Lane, reached from the California 133 exit of Interstate 5.

61 Homes Now Open at Site in Mira Mesa

The grand opening of Fieldstone Classics features 19 of 61 single-family homes being built in Mira Mesa by Fieldstone Classics.

The two-story dwellings, ranging in price from $213,990 to $221,990, were designed by Hales-Langston of Orange in a California contemporary style with red tile roofs, vaulted ceilings and arched doorways.

The four-bedroom homes feature an optional loft or bonus room and 2 1/2 to 3 baths. Two plans are offered with two-car garages and range from 1,781 to 1,895 square feet.

The project, with sales offices at Blakstad Court and Penara Street in Mira Mesa, is open daily, except Thursday.
