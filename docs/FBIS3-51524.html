Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina will travel to Namibia today. The Cuban foreign minister's official visit to Namibia will conclude a tour of several African nations. Robaina will arrive in Namibia from Zambia, where he met with Zambian President Frederick Chiluba. During the meeting with Robaina, President Chiluba thanked Cuba for helping Zambia in the health field. Chiluba also stressed that ties between Lusaka and Havana are very strong. Robaina delivered a message to Chiluba from President Fidel Castro and, on behalf of the people and government of Cuba, thanked the people of Zambia for their solidarity.
