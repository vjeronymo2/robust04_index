May 13, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ANOTHER FAN IS TIRED OF DICKERSON'S SLAMS

I think Eric Dickerson is out of line in knocking the Rams.

Didn't he sign a contract and then try to deny his signature?

What if he had a bad year? Does that mean the Rams could have said to Dickerson, "We didn't mean it . . . You're going to make less next year?"

GREG MURPHY

Pacoima
