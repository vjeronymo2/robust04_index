BFN [Text] The minister of state for defense, Mr. Mallikarjun, has asserted that India will not succumb to pressure from any country to slow down its missile development program. Talking to newsmen in Madras today, he said that Prithvi [surface-to-surface missile] has been successfully tested and it is for the technical experts to decide about its induction into the armed forces. Mr. Mallikarjun also expressed concern over the possibility of a military imbalance in the region if Washington supplies F-16 jets to Pakistan.
