BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow May 27 TASS -- Russian Communists have made yet another attempt to torpedo the Russian president's six decrees aimed at deepening economic reforms. On the whole yesterday's situation repeated. Valentin Martemiyanov, deputy from the faction of Communists, again proposed to consider "the president's anti-constitutional decrees". This time more deputies than yesterday voted for his proposal but still the number of votes was not enough to include this issue in the agenda of a plenary meeting of the session. Representatives of the parliamentary lobby believe that the opposition will continue attempts to impede the implementation of Boris Yeltsin's decrees.
