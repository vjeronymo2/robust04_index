Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchyogoleva] [Text] Sochi March 19 TASS -- President Boris Yeltsin, Krasnodar Territorial Administration Chief Nikolay Yegorov and Sochi Administration Chief Nikolay Karpov flew over the south western areas of Kuban territory on Friday in a helicopter. They also surveyed the border area of Russia, the water areas of Novorossiisk and Tuapse ports, rice plantations, vineyards, and health resort complexes of Anapa and Sochi, a spokesman in the press service of the Sochi City Administration told ITAR-TASS on Saturday. Most pressing problems of territorial development were discussed after the long flight. The president devoted special attention to matters connected with the accommodation of Russian Army units in those regions. Migration problems, the development of inter-ethnic relations in the territory and various aspects of action to fight crime were discussed as well. The president instructed that the entire package of these matters be prepared to be discussed by Russia's Security Council session. During the survey of the Tuapse and Novorossiisk ports, a programme for the development of the entire transportation complex of southern regions of Russia was discussed. The president issued a number of instructions concerning the preservation of land reclamation systems in Kuban. A decision was taken to draft a special decree concerning the development of the children's health resort of Anapa.
