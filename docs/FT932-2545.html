930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / Ulster payout budget is Pounds 100m THE GOVERNMENT will pay almost Pounds 100m in compensation to victims of terrorism in Northern Ireland this year. The Compensation Agency has been given a budget for the financial year of Pounds 100.5m. A total of Pounds 66.4m has been set aside as compensation for criminal damage - most of it resulting from IRA bombings. A further Pounds 26.4m has been earmarked for criminal injury compensation and Pounds 2.5m for emergency provisions compensation.
