BFN [Report by Volodymyr Prytula, MOLOD UKRAYINY special correspondent: "Cossacks Have Arrived!"] [Text] Last Saturday [28 May], the so-called "Great Cossack Assembly" with the participation of leaders of the Ukrainian and Russian cossackdom was to be held in Simferopol. However, in all likelihood, the organizers of the action had a purely political goal -- to help local separatists. That is why nothing came out of the "Great Assembly." Representatives of the majority of Russia's cossack regions did not arrive at the assembly. Ukrainian cossacks stated their protest to organizers of the provocation. Hetman [cossack leader] of the Ukrainian Zaporizhzhya cossackdom General V. Mulyava qualified the holding of the "assembly" as interference into the internal affairs of our state. Regional organizations of the Ukrainian cossackdom received a telegram signed by Hetman Mulyava ordering them to be ready to defend Ukraine's territorial integrity. The make-believe cossack conference concluded with the report from Simferopol's Kievskiy Rayon Department of Internal Affairs: In the "Moskva" hotel, a group of "cossacks" raped a woman, who was also staying at that hotel....
