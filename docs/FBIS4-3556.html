BFN [Text] Beijing, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Wang Bingqian, vice-chairman of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with a Mongolian delegation here this morning. The delegation, headed by N. Bayarsayhan, chairman of the Permanent Commission for Food, Agriculture and Husbandry of the Great People's Hural, arrived here Wednesday [19 April] at the invitation of the NPC Financial and Economic Committee.
