BFN [Text] The Georgian Foreign Ministry's press service told the CONTACT news agency that a delegation of the Spanish Foreign Ministry, led by (Raul Minaus), vice chancellor of the Foreign Ministry, arrived in Georgia today on a working visit. The Spanish delegation will meet with leaders of Georgia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The sides will discuss both Spanish-Georgian relations and a wide spectrum of issues connected with the situation in the Transcaucasus. (?Preparation) of the draft governmental agreement on economic cooperation between the two countries is also planned. The economic situation and the course of reforms in Spain and Georgia will be discussed together with the issues connected with cooperation between the two countries' foreign ministries.
