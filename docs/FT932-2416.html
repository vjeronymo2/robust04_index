930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / International Company News: Westpac issue goes ahead WESTPAC Banking Corp-oration, the Australian bank, yesterday went ahead with the ADollars 500m (USDollars 333m) convertible preference share issue it foreshadowed last month. Westpac said the bank had executed an underwriting agreement for the issue with Were Stockbroking and County NatWest Securities. Shares worth ADollars 300m offered to institutions had already been fully subscribed. The rest, with oversubscriptions up to a further ADollars 100m, would be for Australian and New Zealand investors.
