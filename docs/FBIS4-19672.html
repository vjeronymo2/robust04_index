BFN [Text] House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos today received Montenegrin Prime Minister Milo Djukanovic and Deputy Prime Minister Rade Perovic, who have been in Cyprus since yesterday. Speaking after the meeting, Djukanovic said that Montenegro expects Cyprus' support in its efforts to find a solution to the Yugoslav problem. Galanos said that they discussed the situation in the Balkans and the danger to international peace from Turkish expansionism. He also said that they discussed some initiatives at the parliament level of some countries that have close and traditional relations, and religious ties.
