940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Overseas net assets fall Net asset value per share of Kleinwort Overseas Investment Trust fell by 5 per cent to 277.1p in the six months to end-June, against 292.2p at the December year end. That compared with a fall of 0.8 per cent in the trust's benchmark - the FT-Actuaries World Index - but represents a 9 per cent advance on the year ago figure of 253.6p. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 1.53m, against Pounds 1.76m. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p, payable from earnings of 1.89p (2.18p) per share.
