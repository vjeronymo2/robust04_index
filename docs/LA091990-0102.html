September 19, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: RAYMOND VECCI NAMED PRESIDENT OF ALASKA AIR

Raymond J. Vecci, 47, has been named president and chief executive of Alaska Airlines. He replaces Bruce R. Kennedy, 52, who remains chairman, president and chief executive of the airline's parent, Alaska Air Group.

Vecci, a 15-year veteran of Alaska Airlines, will continue in his current position as chief operating officer until a replacement is found.
