November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Touchstone Software Corp.: The Huntington Beach software company said third-quarter earnings increased to $56,652 from $3,335 for the comparable 1989 quarter.

Revenue rose 85%, to $741,951 from $400,494. For the nine-month period, earnings jumped to $121,214 from $42,986 in the year-earlier period. Revenue more than doubled to $2.5 million from $1.1 million.
