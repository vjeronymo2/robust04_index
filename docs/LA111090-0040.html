November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

COLORADO IS A FINE ROLE MODEL FOR YOUTH

Colorado, thanks to its faulty victory over Missouri, might well win the national title this year.

Colorado Coach Bill McCartney has once again shown us that it's OK to cheat in college sports either purposely or by accident, just as long as you don't get caught.

What a fine example Colorado must be for the millions of young people who are now involved in college sports. Who knows? Maybe there's another Gordon Liddy, Charles Keating or future President in the bunch?

ROLAND L. MAMEA, Etiwanda
