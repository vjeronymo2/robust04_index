July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY OVER SUPREME COURT'S ABORTION DECISION

Regarding the abortion controversy ("Dislike Abortion Ruling? We Voted for It," by Martin Schram, Op-Ed Page, July 10), are there others, like me, who have had their pro-choice leanings strengthened because of the actions of the pro-life demonstrators? They seem to show so much hostility to the women who have made this choice. I get the feeling many of them want the women punished for perceived sexual transgressions.

I do not think a baby should ever be a punishment.

LaVONE DRUMMOND

Buena Park
