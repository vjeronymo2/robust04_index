April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PIANIST WILL CONDUCT, SOLO IN COSTA MESA

Pianist Leon Fleisher will conduct and appear as soloist with the Pacific Symphony on May 23 and 24 at the Orange County Performing Arts Center in Costa Mesa.

Fleisher replaces Sergiu Comissiona, who bowed out of the engagement to assume his new duties as music director for the Vancouver Symphony.

Fleisher was already scheduled to appear on those dates as soloist in Ravel's Concerto for the Left Hand. He will conduct that work from the keyboard and also will assume the podium for Rachmaninoff's Symphony No. 2 and Beethoven's "Coriolan" Overture.
