August 14, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: USD'S JEROME PICKED BY YAKIMA

John Jerome, who broke the single-season scoring record at USD last year, was a fifth round draft choice (80th selection overall) of the Yakima (Wash.) Sun Kings in Monday's Continental Basketball Assn. draft.

TENNIS

Ditta Huber of La Jolla defeated Laura Suttile of Darien, Conn., 6-1, 6-3, in the first round Monday of the National Grass Court championships at the Philadelphia Cricket Club.
