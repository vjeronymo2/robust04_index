940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Worth Inv Trust Net asset value per ordinary share of Worth Investment Trust declined from 26.73p to 22.63p over the 12 months ended March 31. The trust invests predominantly in luxury and design-related businesses in Europe and the decline in asset value reflected the recent sale for FFr17.9m (Pounds 2.08m) of Seguin Moreau, the trust's largest investment. Available revenue for the year slipped to Pounds 87,000 (Pounds 110,000) for earnings of 0.28p (0.35p). The single dividend is a same-again 0.1p.
