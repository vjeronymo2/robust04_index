January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MARINARO HELPS LAZERS TO A 4-3 VICTORY

Hector Marinaro scored 2 first-half goals Saturday to spark the Lazers to their sixth consecutive home victory, a 4-3 Major Indoor Soccer League victory over the Tacoma Stars at the Forum.

The Lazers led at halftime, 4-1, and held off Tacoma to even their record at 12-12.

Michael Collins and Gary Heale each collected a goal and an assist for Los Angeles, which won despite being outshot in the second half, 27-4.

Lazer rookie goalkeeper Kris Peate improved his record to 5-1.

Preki had two goals -- his 26th and 27th of the season -- and an assist for the Stars.
