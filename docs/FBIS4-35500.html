BFN [Text] Mahmud al-Zu'bi, chairman of the Council of Ministers, received at 1100 today Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, deputy prime minister and higher education minister of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, and the delegation accompanying him. The meeting was attended by Dr. Salihah Sunqur, minister of higher education, and the Jordanian ambassador in Damascus. Dr. al-Tall conveyed Jordanian Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali's greetings to Mahmud al-Zu'bi, who also requested the Jordanian official to convey his greetings to Dr. al-Majali. Talks during the meeting centered on the existing cooperation relations between the two fraternal countries. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' also received Sa'id al-Tall and his delegation this morning. The Jordanian ambassador in Damascus attended. Talks during the meeting revolved around the situation in the region, as well as the Arab situation, especially current events in fraternal Yemen. The two sides voiced their concern about Yemen's unity and stressed the need to end the bloodshed and infighting as soon as possible. The two sides also tackled developments in the peace process, as well as the continuation of coordination with Syria and fraternal Lebanon. They then discussed relations between the two fraternal countries, Syria and Jordan.
