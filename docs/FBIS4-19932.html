BFN [Text] Moscow (DPA) -- Federal Economics Minister Guenter Rexrodt has asked the Russian Government to create better conditions for foreign trade partners and investors. The trade volume between Germany and Russia is not being fully utilized, Rexrodt said after talks with his Russian counterpart, Aleksandr Shokhin and government head Viktor Chernomyrdin in Moscow on Thursday [21 April]. Rexrodt emphasized that, despite bureaucratic obstacles and lacking legislature, the German economy was ready to increase its investments in Russia.
