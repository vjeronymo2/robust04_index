March 16, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The "Scholarship Benefit Luncheon" on March 8 at the Registry Hotel in Irvine netted about $7,000 for the Newport Harbor Panhellenic. Grayne Price was chairwoman.

The "Cameo Classics" luncheon and fashion show on March 8 at the Disneyland Hotel netted about $11,000 for the Cameo Auxiliary of Big Brothers/Big Sisters of Orange County. Skipper Fort, Betty Hiltscher, Bea Hinrichs, Opal Rudolph and Corky Spranger were chairwomen.

The "Night of Your Dreams" bachelor auction on March 9 at the Irvine Marriott netted about $18,000 for the Muscular Dystrophy Assn. O

The "Top o' the Mornin' " luncheon and fashion show on March 11 at the Irvine Hilton & Towers netted about $15,000 for Florence Crittenton Services of Orange County. Kathleen Pritchardwas chairwoman.

A cocktail party on March 12 at California Pizza Kitchen in Newport Beach netted about $7,000 for Orangewood Children's Foundation. Jim McNamara was chairman.
