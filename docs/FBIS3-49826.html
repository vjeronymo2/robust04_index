Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Five Members of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] Detained in Serbia"] [Text] It has been announced on 14 January in Belgrade that two transporters carrying members of the U.S. and Norwegian units of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] in Macedonia crossed into Serbian territory on 13 January, near the army barracks Slavovec, whereby the soldiers, who did not carry any weapons, and did not give any resistance, were apprehended by the Serbian border guards. After ten hours of interrogation, they were returned to Macedonia at the border crossing Tabanovce. According to an anonymous UNPROFOR officer in Macedonia, the three Americans and two Norwegians were treated well by the Serbian border guards. "It is possible that they could have crossed into Serbian territory while they were trying tot save one of the vehicles," the officer said. Otherwise, a number of such incidents have occurred at this frontier, as it has not been strictly defined.
