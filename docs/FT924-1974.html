921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Treating water in Kuwait The BRISTOL BABCOCK process control group, part of FKI, has won a Pounds 35m contract from Kuwait's Ministry of Electricity and Water to repair a desalination treatment plant in Kuwait City damaged in the Gulf War. FKI has now won total contracts worth more than Pounds 45m from the electricity and water ministry. Work on the contract to repair the Shuwaikh desalination treatment plant in Kuwait City will take about 13 months to complete.
