Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] The Eurasian Metalworkers Federation has been established by the metalworkers unions of sixteen countries including Turkey. Eurasian Metalworkers Federation Chairman Mustafa Ozbek, at a meeting in Ankara, stressed the importance of establishing international social and collaboration organizations during this period of political and economic globalization. Turk-Is [Turkish Confederation of Labor] Chairman Bayram Meral, stated that the Eurasian Metalworkers Federation will contribute to the happiness of the people by organizing work and capital distribution among world countries. The Eurasian Metalworkers Federation consists of the metalworkers union of Turkey, Azerbaijan, Bulgaria, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, Hungary, Macedonia, Moldova, Uzbekistan, Romania, Russia, Slovenia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Ukraine.
