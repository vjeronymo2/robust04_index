920222 FT 22 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Dunton Group Dunton Group, the USM-quoted building and property concern, cut its pre-tax loss from Pounds 2.71m to Pounds 252,000 in the six months ended November 30 1991. At the operating level it was in the black but, while the recession remained, was unlikely to return to overall profitability in the short term.
