941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / Contracts and Ventures: Atlantic Richfield US oil company Atlantic Richfield (Arco) signed a contract with the China National Offshore Oil Corp (CNOOC) to explore for oil and gas off China's southern Hainan island. The contract was Arco's sixth with CNOOC. The deal was CNOOC's 100th with a foreign company.
