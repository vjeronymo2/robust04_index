BFN [Text] Since January, the Central Region Oil Drilling and Extraction enterprise has delivered more than 385,000 tons of oil, thus surpassing its projected goals by 3 percent. The enterprise director, Engineer Marcos Aguilera, told national reporters that the workers' efforts were paired with positive results from foreign companies in charge of exploiting several wells and the regular arrival of tankers. Engineer Aguilera added that the current situation allows us to believe that this overage can be maintained or surpasssed by the end of the first semester, at which time they expect to have extracted 410,870 tons.
