BFN [Text] Beijing, April 7 (AFP)--The secretary of Wei Jingsheng, China's leading dissident, is undergoing police investigation, a Ministry of Public Security spokesman said Thursday. Tong Yi, who disappeared on Monday, "is currently being investigated," the spokesman said, using a term indicating that she was also in police detention. Tong has acted as chief spokesperson for Wei since he was released from prison in September, acting as go-between for the dissident and foreign journalists. Tong was travelling in a car with Wei when he was picked up Friday as he attempted to return to Beijing. The spokesman refused to specify what crimes Tong was being investigated for.
