BFN [Text] Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the House of Representatives, and Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development, left Sanaa International Airport for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia this afternoon. They are carrying a message from brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, to his brother King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the two holy mosques. It deals with the current developments in our country and issues of concern to the two sisterly countries. Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah also left Sanaa airport for Cairo with a message from brother Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, to his brother President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, president of the Arab Republic of Egypt.
