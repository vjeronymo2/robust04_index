BFN [Text] Kuwait has shown keen interests in employing more Thai workers to help in reconstruction of the country. Vice President of the Kuwaiti Parliament [Salei Yusuf Al Fathala] met Minister of Labor and Social Welfare Phaithun Kaeothong on Friday for talks on Thai labor in Kuwait. There are currently about 3,000 Thai workers in Kuwait. The country wishes to increase the number of Thai workers to 7,000, the same number employed in Kuwait before the Gulf war. The vice president of the Kuwaiti parliament asked Thailand to help urge Iraq to release Kuwaiti prisoners of war. Kuwait intends to cooperate more in Thailand in diplomatic, trade, and labor areas. Trade between the two countries last year amounted to 2,000 million baht. Kuwait has invested over 1,000 million baht in the petroleum business in Thailand. Kuwait has also invited Minister Phaithun to visit that country.
