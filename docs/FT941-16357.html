940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / 460,000 packs of cereal recalled Quaker Oats, the breakfast cereal maker, is recalling 460,000 packs of Sugar Puffs and Quaker Puffed Wheat, with a retail value of about Pounds 800,000, after 'rough grain husks' which could irritate the throat were found in some packs. About 5,000 packs are believed to be affected by the problem, thought to be the result of a breakdown in the sieving process. The company said there was no danger to children or adults.
