BFN [Text] A two-day session of the first congress of the Communist Party of Slovakia [KSS] ended in Levice today with the adoption of final documents. The party's policy reaffirms that the KSS is a Marxist party, the objective of which is to build socialism, a democratic society with political and economic plurality, a society that would be self-administered, prosperous, and socially just. The party's policy says that if this party fails to win a partner for a joint course of action, it will go into the elections as an independent political entity. The delegates reelected Vladimir Dado as KSS chairman, and (Ladislav Jaco) was elected KSS central secretary.
