930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands offer and placing to raise Pounds 15m EX-LANDS, a property inv-estment and management group, has announced a placing and open offer to raise Pounds 15m. It is also buying a Pounds 5.8m portfolio of property in South Kensington, comprising 13,730 sq ft of offices, 13 retail units and nine flats. The remainder of the funds will be available for further purchases of high yielding property and additional working capital to fund the development of golf courses in continental Europe. Ex-Lands is managed by Mr Robert Bourne and Mr Graham Bourne, who used to run Local London before it was sold to Priest Marians in 1989. The Saatchi brothers, founders of the advertising group, own 10 per cent of the shares. The offer entitles shareholders to apply for Pounds 1 nominal of 7.5 per cent convertible unsecured loan stock 2020 for every five shares held. The offer has been underwritten by Paribas. The company said it expects to make a pre-tax profit for the year to June 30 1993 of at least Pounds 900,000. It intends to pay a dividend of 0.35p for the year.
