February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ON PREVIN'S EDITING OF ERICKSON'S 'CORONA'

I was especially upset with Previn's motioning for the orchestra to rise before the last sounds had finished reverberating and by his hurried exit, as if to say: "We've fulfilled our obligations. Now let's get on with the rest of the concert."

CHRISTIAN HERTZOG

La Jolla
