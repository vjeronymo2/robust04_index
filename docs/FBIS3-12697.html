Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An official spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation said that the Kingdom of Morocco learned with deep sorrow and grief of the massacre in Hebron of dozens of innocent Palestinians who were performing their religious rites in the venerated month of Ramadan and in a place sacred to the revealed religions. The official spokesman added that Morocco, as it pays homage to the souls of the martyrs, who were martyrs of blind extremism, strongly condemns this hideous crime which was perpetrated at a time when the international organizations were expecting that peace would soon prevail in the region and were according particular importance to the recent Palestinian-Israeli agreement. The spokesman concluded by saying that Morocco, while calling for the perpetrators of this hideous crime to be severely and decisively punished and for the escalation of violence to be halted, considers that such acts must be an incentive to international organizations to make preparations for guaranteeing peace, security, and the legitimate rights of the Palestinian people.
