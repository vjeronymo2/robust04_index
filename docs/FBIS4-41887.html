BFN [Report by Sergey Sidorov: "Russia and Mongolia: Cooperation in the Security Sphere"] [Text] Regional and international security, the strengthening of Mongolia's nuclear-free status, and the fight against crime, international terrorism, and trafficking in arms and narcotics are the priorities in cooperation between Mongolia and Russia in the security sphere as defined by J. Enhsaihan, secretary of the Mongolian National Security Council, during his visit to Moscow. A joint communique was signed on the results of his talks with Russian Federation Security Council Secretary Oleg Lobov. Questions relating to the settlement of Mongolia's foreign debt and the development of military-technical cooperation were also discussed during the talks. An accord was reached on a return visit to Ulaanbaatar by the Russian Federation Security Council secretary.
