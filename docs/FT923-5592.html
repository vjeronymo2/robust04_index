920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / International Company News: Hudson's Bay dips 29% to CDollars 7.3m HUDSON'S BAY, Canada's leading department store group, continues to feel the retailing recession. Second-quarter profit dipped 29 per cent to CDollars 7.3m (USDollars 6.13m), or 14 cents a share, from CDollars 10.3m, or 20 cents, a year earlier, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Revenues fell 3 per cent to Dollars 1.14bn. The company has been cutting prices aggressively to gain market share.
