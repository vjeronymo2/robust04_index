Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 4 Jan -- Madeleine Albright, President Bill Clinton's special envoy, will arrive in Poland on 7 January. The visit by the American delegation is to take place a few days before the NATO summit in Brussels, and before a meeting of the Visegrad Group leaders with President Bill Clinton, a Foreign Ministry Press Department statement received by PAP today says. Madeleine Albright is the U.S. permanent representative to the United Nations and a member of the cabinet. Among members of the delegation is General John Shalikashvili, head of the joint chiefs of staffs of the U.S. Armed Forces. "The delegation will held a number of high level meetings," the statement says. As a PAP journalist has learned from a reliable source, it can be expected that the delegation will be received by the president and the head of government.
