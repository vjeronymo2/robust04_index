BFN ["Message of His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk to First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh and Second Prime Minister Hun Sen"; dated 22 April -- read by announcer] [Text] Beloved Prince Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen: Today, I was deeply moved to receive the message of support from the RGC -- with both of you as first and second prime ministers -- for my 22 April 1994 statement concerning a round-table meeting for peace and national reconciliation. I would like to express my deepest and warmest thanks to you and the RGC for your constant demonstration of responsibility, which is imbued with sublime and fine patriotism. Both of you and the RGC members, please accept my warmest and deepest affectionate regards. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 22 April 1994.
