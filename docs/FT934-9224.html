931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Personal Assets shows improvement Personal Assets Trust raised net asset value to Pounds 86.23 per share at the end of October, against Pounds 75.18 six months earlier. Net revenue for the six months increased from Pounds 90,000 to Pounds 164,000 and earnings per share came out at 1.08p (0.6p). The interim dividend has been raised to 0.95p (0.85p).
