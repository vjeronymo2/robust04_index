BFN [Text] It was learned that Defense Minister Yi Pyong-tae and U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry used a hotline for about 30 minutes on the morning of 4 June to discuss preparations to strengthen ROK-U.S. joint military rapport in connection with the tense situation surrounding the Korean peninsula due to the North Korean nuclear issue. A high-ranking Defense Ministry official revealed: "It is known that they discussed the issue from various angles on ways to make preparations to strengthen the military rapport between the two countries for possible provocations by North Korea if economic sanctions are imposed." This is the first time that the two ministers talked over the hotline, with the exception of the test conducted when it was installed last month.
