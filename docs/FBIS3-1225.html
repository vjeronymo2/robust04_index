Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lebowakgomo March 23 SAPA -- Six troop carriers with soldiers stopped marchers from entering government buildings at Lebowakgomo in Lebowa on Wednesday [22 March], SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported. This followed an uprising by non-commissioned police officers in Lebowa earlier in the day during which senior officers were evicted from their offices and the police complex occupied. According to the radio report, two senior agriculture officers were being held hostage by marchers.
