940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / World Stock Markets: Johannesburg strengthens South African shares were supported in slow trade, but dealers said sentiment remained mixed amid concern about world equity market direction. The overall index put on 19 to 5,568, industrials 46 to 6,420 and gold 3 to 2,050. De Beers added 50 cents to R108.75 and SAB 75 cents to R86.75. Richemont picked up 75 cents to close at R38.25. Driefontein fell 50 cents to R63.00, while Freegold gained R1.50 to R58.50. Gencor lost 5 cents to R11.55 ahead of today's announcement of the details of its deal to purchase base metals mining group Billiton from Royal Dutch Shell.
