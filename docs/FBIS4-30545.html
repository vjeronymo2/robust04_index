BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 25 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Thursday sent a message of greetings to president of the Republic of Madagascar Albert Zafy on the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the independence of Madagascar. President Kim Il-song in the message hoped for the prosperity of the Republic of Madagascar, expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would continue to develop.
