Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 1 Feb -- The result of the afternoon session of the parliament session was the adoption of the state budget of Ukraine for 1994, with 255 deputies voting for its adoption and 36 against. According to the new budget, income and expenditure are equal, at 335.067343 trillion karbovantsi. In the opinion of people's deputy Leopold Taburyanskyy, head of the People's Party of Ukraine, the new budget will lead to the further impoverishment of the population. In his opinion, expenditure on defense -- 16.82 trillion karbovantsi -- and on holding the March parliamentary elections -- 1.15 trillion -- is excessive.
