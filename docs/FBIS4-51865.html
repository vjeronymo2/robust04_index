BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- C.R. Aslam, president of the Pakistan Socialist Party; Ghulam Nadikalr, chairman of the Workers and Peasants Party of Pakistan; Miraj Khalid, chairman of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organisation of Pakistan; Mazari Ali Khan, chairman of the Pakistan Peace Council; and 7 other leading officials of political parties and organisations of Pakistan made public a joint appeal on April 14 on the occasion of the first anniversary of the publication of "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" advanced by the great leader President Kim Il-song. Noting that the great leader President Kim Il-song is making consistent efforts to achieve the independent and peaceful reunification of the country, the appeal called upon the government, the people and all political party leaders of Pakistan to positively support the 10-point programme. The appeal called upon the South Korean people to put pressure upon the South Korean authorities to discard the idea of submitting to outside forces, get the U.S. troops and nuclear weapons withdrawn from South Korea and pull down the concrete wall. The appeal called upon the people of the United States to urge the U.S. administration to immediately stop the military provocations in the Korean peninsula and withdraw the U.S. troops and nuclear weapons from South Korea and upon all member nations of the United Nations to bar the United States from using international organisations in threatening and blackmailing the sovereignty of the DPRK.
