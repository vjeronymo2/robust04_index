September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Waiting for Godfather: Movie buffs who've been waiting 16 years for another entry in Francis Coppola's "Godfather" series will have a to wait a little bit longer for "The Godfather, Part III." Paramount Pictures has moved the opening date of the film from Nov. 21 to Christmas Day. The $50-million-plus gangster epic, which reunites Coppola with stars Al Pacino and Diane Keaton, was shot in Italy and New York last winter and spring and recently went back into production for two weeks of additional shooting. BETH KLEID
