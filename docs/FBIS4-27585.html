BFN [Text] Seoul, May 29 (YONHAP) -- The secretary-general of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party (DLP) Mun Chong-su returned from six-day tour to China Sunday morning. Mun, who was invited by the Chinese Communist Party, met with senior leaders in China such as Communist Party Secretariat member Hu Jintao, vice chairman of the Political Consultative Conference Wu Xueqian and Vice Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan and discussed North Korea's nuclear problem and ways to expand economic cooperation between Korea and China. Mun and head of the International Liaison Department of the Chinese Communist Party Li Shuzheng also agreed to exchange senior party officials and to cooperate on training of party officials and holding seminars. DLP chairman Kim Chong-pil and the Chinese Communist Party Secretariat member Hu are expected to visit the other country respectively this year.
