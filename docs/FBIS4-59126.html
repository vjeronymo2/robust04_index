BFN [Text] Muhammad Nazzal, Hamas representative in Jordan, has said Hamas in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank will not take up arms against either the self-rule authority or Fatah members. In the last press statement he made in Amman before Hamas moved its media activity from Amman to Damascus, Nazzal said: We only call on the self-rule authority not to stand against Hamas Movement's program calling for the end of Israeli occupation.
