940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Flagstone losses cut to Pounds 424,000 Flagstone Holdings, the USM-traded leisure concern, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 730,000 to Pounds 424,000 for the year ended January 31. Turnover grew from Pounds 521,000 to Pounds 607,000, of which Pounds 110,000 related to acquisitions. The deficit was after a Pounds 142,000 profit on the sale of an investment, compared with a Pounds 573,000 write down of investment property before, and took in a Pounds 228,000 charge for an abortive acquisition. Net interest payable was lower at Pounds 122,000 (Pounds 208,000). Losses per share narrowed to 0.13p (0.53p).
