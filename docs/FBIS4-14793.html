BFN [Text] A delegation from our country that is investigating the causes for the Iranian C-130 plane crash in Karabakh met Azerbaijani President Gaydar Aliyev Thursday afternoon. In the meeting Mr. Aliyev expressed sorrow and condolences to the families of the victims of the crash of our country's plane, reports Central News Unit from Baku. He said: From the very first day of this incident I have kept a close watch on related issues and in a telephone conversation with Iranian President Hashemi-Rafsanjani reported on what has taken place. The Azerbaijani president also told the meeting that all relevant organizations in his country were prepared to provide documents and evidences to the Iranian investigation team regarding the event.
