BFN [Excerpt] Russian President Boris Yeltsin's special envoy, who is visiting Kuwait, stressed the importance of Iraq's compliance with international resolutions pertinent to Kuwait's liberation war. This statement was made during a meeting between envoy Yevgeniy Shaposhnikov and Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad Al Sabah, first deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs, held at the Kuwaiti Foreign Ministry. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad, quoting the Russian envoy, told correspondents that Moscow was committed to its firm position on the necessity of Iraq complying with international resolutions, including Resolution 833, related to the Kuwaiti-Iraqi border demarcation, and those concerned with releasing Kuwaiti POWs from the prisons of that regime.
