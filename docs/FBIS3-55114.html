Tsybulskyy under the "Ukraine and World" rubric: "There Are More Aspirants"] [Text] The Central Electoral Commission for Ukraine's legislative elections has examined another list of applications and related documents submitted by political parties hoping to field candidates in the 27 March elections. Central Electoral Committee Chairman I. H. Yemets issued registration certificates to the Civil Congress of Ukraine and the Ukrainian Party of Justice.
