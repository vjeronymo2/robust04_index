BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, 16 May (TANJUG) -- Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic received Aleksiy II, the patriarch of Moscow and All Russia, and Serbian Patriarch Pavle in the Presidency building today. Greeting the distinguished visitor from Moscow, President Milosevic said: "We are happy to be able to receive you and greet you in Serbia." [passage omitted] After the welcoming speech, official talks began behind closed doors in the Presidency.
