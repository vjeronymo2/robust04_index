BFN [Text] A responsible source in the Defense Ministry has stated the following: A dangerous development in the miltiary incidents being directed by the ruling family in Sanaa has occurred. A number of Republican Guard, Central Security Force, tank, and armored vehicle units have taken up positions around the 1st Artilley Brigade in Yarim. The brigade's position became dangerous around 1200 today, when crowds [as heard] successively bore down on them. The Defense Ministry has briefed local, Arab, and international public opinion on the acts being carried out by the military command in Sanaa, and it places full responsibility on these rulers, whose appetite has been whetted by in-fighting among the sons of the homeland, the waste of their blood, and the resulting grave consequences. These events place the country on the brink of a devastating civil war that will destroy everything.
