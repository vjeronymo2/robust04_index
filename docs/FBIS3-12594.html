Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Two of Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin's close advisers secretly met in Paris with former Lebanese President Amin al-Jumayyil. Our political correspondent Gadi Sukenik has learned that the director general of the Prime Minister's Office and his political adviser met with al-Jumayyil on 8 February, approximately three weeks ago. The former Lebanese president was the one who initiated the meeting.
