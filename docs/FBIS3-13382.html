Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] 'Allamah Sayyid Muhammad Husayn Fadlallah believes that the Sayyidat al-Najat explosion was meant to be a mine undercutting the Lebanese people, because what was required was to create a limited security threat that would then be turned into a political earthquake to divert the attention of the people from economic issues to concentrate on security matters. He said violating security harms both Christians and Muslims. He noted that whoever throws a bomb at a church will also do the same to a mosque. Whoever places a bomb near a cultural center somewhere will also place it in a cultural center here.
