Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [By Yi Chae-mu from Tokyo] [Text] An ASAHI SHIMBUN article dispatched from London on 12 January reported that the British military journal JANE'S DEFENSE will carry in its 15 January issue an article reporting that the margin of error [ocha] of North Korea's ballistic missile "Nodong No. 1," which was test-fired in the East Sea at the end of May 1993, is 700 meters and that the missiles will be deployed for actual combat around 1996.
