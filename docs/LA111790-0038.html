November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DEAL FOR STRAWBERRY SEEMS TO MAKE FANS OF BOTH SIDES HAPPY

Should the Dodgers elect to keep Kirk Gibson, they would certainly have three of the more pleasant human beings in the history of the game together in one outfield, now that Darryl Strawberry has joined Kal Daniels on our merry blue crew. It serves to remind us once again that it's not the person but the performance that endears the athlete to our hearts.

JEB STUART

Anaheim
