Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, in a solemn ceremony, Commander of the Revolution Juan Almeida Bosque and Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina Gonzalez received the credentials of (Juan Franklin Analla Vasquez) which accredit him as ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Republic of Bolivia. The Bolivian ambassador was accompanied by his adviser Mr. (Enrique Sorianao). At the conclusion of the ceremony, the ambassador and his adviser deposited a floral wreath at the Jose Marti monument.
