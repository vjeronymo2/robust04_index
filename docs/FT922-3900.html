920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / International Company News: Telecom seeks Spanish growth NORTHERN Telecom de Espana, the Spanish subsidiary of the Canadian telecommunications group, Northern Telecom, wants to tighten links with Spain's Telefonica, Mr Paul Stern, executive president, said yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. He said Northern Telecom had valuable technology and experience to offer. Northern Telecom de Espana and Telefonica last week signed an accord to collaborate in data networking.
