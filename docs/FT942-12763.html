940428 FT 28 APR 94 / Digital plans to move Scots plant Digital Equipment plans to move its personal computer manufacturing operations in Scotland to a separate facility near its plant in Ayr. Digital said the move would have no impact on jobs but would create room for possible future expansion. Its Ayr payroll has increased from 1,200 to 2,000 in the past year. It would not comment on reports that the new plant would be at Irvine, 14 miles from Ayr.
