BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 3 -- A delegation of KYODO NEWS SERVICE of Japan led by its President Yasuhiko Inukai who is also chairman of the Organization of Asia-Pacific News Agency (OANA) pays a working visit to Vietnam from May 31 to June 6, at the invitation of Mr Do Phuong, director general of Vietnam News Agency (VNA). President Inukai and his entourage called on party General Secretary Do Muoi on June 2. During the meeting, President Inukai said that he had honour to be received by and have cordial talks with party General Secretary Do Muoi. He expressed he rejoice [as received] over the successes of Vietnam's renovation with political stability, economic development and broaden international relations. In a cordial atmosphere, party General Secretary Do Muoi expressed determination of the Vietnamese people in implementation of objectives of peace and stability at home and in the region as well as maintaining its national independence, overcome difficulties in order to build Vietnam into a country of prosperity and social equality and civilization. He stressed that the foreign policies of the Vietnamese party and state aim at broadening its cooperation with all countries, especially the countries in Asia. General Secretary Do Muoi expressed his welcome and wish the mutual cooperation and assistance between VIETNAM NEWS AGENCY and KYODO NEWS SERVICE be developed further in order to contribute their parts into peace, stability, cooperation and development of the two countries as well as Asia-Pacific region and the world as a whole. Earlier, President Inukai and his party were received by Vice Prime Minister Nguyen Khanh. During the meeting, Vice Prime Minister Nguyen Khanh highly valued cooperation between VIETNAM NEWS AGENCY and KYODO news over the past 20 years. He affirmed that the Vietnamese Government strongly supports VNA to participate OANA. During its stay, the KYODO news delegation and the VNA leadership exchanged views in order to strengthen further cooperation between the two news agencies, especially in exchange of economic news in the current situation. The KYODO news delegation paid floral tribute at the Ho Chi Minh mausoleum, visited his home and office and some cultural economic establishments in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Quang Nam-Danang, Thua Thien-Hue and Vung Tau Provinces.
