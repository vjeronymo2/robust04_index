931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Backing for Canary Wharf plan Unsecured creditors of Olympia & York, the company which built Canary Wharf, have given unanimous backing to a Pounds 1.1bn rescue plan for the London Docklands complex. Their support clears the way for Canary Wharf to come out of administration later this month.
