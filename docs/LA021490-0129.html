February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ESTANCIA BEATS FULLERTON IN WILD-CARD SOCCER

Jose Medrano and Carlos Alcazar each scored in Estancia High School's 2-0 victory over Fullerton in a 1-A division wild-card boys' soccer match Tuesday at Estancia.

Will Bohuslaviski had three saves for Estancia (10-8-3), which will play at El Segundo in a first-round match at 3 p.m. Thursday. Fullerton finished with a 6-13-2 record.
