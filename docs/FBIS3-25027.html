Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] has already admitted that the city of Cuito has been taken over by government forces. Five hours were enough for the government forces to take over the city. Luanda is blaming UNITA. In an interview to RDP moments ago, spokesman for the Angolan President Aldomiro da Conceicao said he hoped this would not happen again: [Begin Conceicao recording] I expect fighting will continue in Cuito, but you should not blame the resumption of fighting in Cuito on the government forces. UNITA has been engaging in offensive operations in several parts of the country and, obviously, the government has to react. I hope the fighting that resumed on the 2d in Cuito will end soon. [end recording] [passage omitted]
