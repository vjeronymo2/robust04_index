Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 05, BNS -- Scheduled winter maneuvers of the Estonian defense forces began in the region of Tapa-Kadrina-Rakvere-Assamalla, northern Estonia, early today, a spokesman at the Central Staff said. About 1,000 servicemen from the Kalevi and Viru infantry battalions and members of the Defense League paramilitary units, as well as about 30 armored vehicles take part in the three-day maneuvers.
