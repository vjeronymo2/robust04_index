BFN [Text] The final phase of ELDYK's [Greek Forces in Cyprus] exercise TAMS EVAGORAS 94, which took place this morning in the area of Vizakia, concluded successfully and with the use of live ammunition. The exercise included an offensive and takeover of a hill that was occupied by the enemy. A platoon of National Guard LEONIDHAS tanks also participated in the exercise. ELDYK Commander Colonel Evangelos Briasoulis expressed satisfaction with the exercise and added that ELDYK is always ready to carry out its mission. The exercise was observed by the Lt. Colonel Ioannis Fotiadhis, National Guard deputy commander, who expressed satisfaction and added that the exercise is an example to follow.
