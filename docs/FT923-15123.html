920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / World News in Brief Seles v Graf at Wimbledon Monica Seles beat former champion Martina Navratilova 6-2 6-7 6-4 to reach the Wimbledon final. She will face Steffi Graf, who won 6-3 6-3 against Gabriela Sabatini. John McEnroe beat Guy Forget and Andre Agassi defeated Boris Becker in the men's quarter finals. Picture, Page 7
