920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: High Gosforth Park in red at midway High Gosforth Park reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 64,168 for the six months to June 30, against profits of Pounds 5,897 in the previous first half. The 1991 year ended with losses of Pounds 46,000. The operator of the Newcastle racecourse said gate receipts and commission from on-course betting had suffered from smaller fields due to dry weather and recession. Turnover in the six months rose to Pounds 592,167 (Pounds 555,590) but cost of sales was Pounds 572,600 (Pounds 467,038). Losses per share of 70.5p compared with earnings of 0.2p and there is no interim dividend.
