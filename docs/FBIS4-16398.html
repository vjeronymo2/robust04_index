BFN [Text] This evening, Radio Liberty informed about a news conference by Igor Kasatonov, deputy commander of the Russian Navy, which was held in St. Petersburg. [Word indistinct] according to Igor Kasatonov, Russian units located in the Crimea were ordered to stand on alert. The admiral's reason for this was Ukraine's alleged intent to capture naval bases in [name indistinct], Mykolayev, and Ochakov. The Defense Ministry press service commented on this statement by the Russian military leader calling it disinformation intended to create hysteria around the Black Sea Fleet. The press service considers Kasatonov's statement about Ukraine's plans to capture the mentioned bases as absolutely unfounded. At the same time, the press service stresses that there are no Russian military units on Ukrainian territory; the only units there are those subordinated to the presidents of Russia and Ukraine.
