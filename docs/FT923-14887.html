920704 FT 04 JUL 92 / Kenya to sell state assets The Kenyan government yesterday put up for sale its stake in 207 companies in an effort to woo back western aid, worth more than Pounds 300m a year, suspended last year, Reuter reports from Nairobi. The Finance Ministry list, which covered everything from the state airline to a quaint mountain fishing inn, is one of the biggest privatisations Africa has seen since the west and aid donors began pushing for reform.
