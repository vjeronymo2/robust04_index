921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / International Capital Markets: GE Capital in deal with UMW UMW Group of Malaysia has entered into a deal with General Electric Capital Asia Investments to set up a financial services joint-venture in Malaysia, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Under the deal, GE Capital, part of the General Electric group of the US, will take a 49 per cent equity stake in UMW's Seabanc Factors, which will operate as a joint-venture holding company. Perusahaan Otomobil Nasional (Proton), the manufacturer of Malaysia's national car, reported a 36 per cent fall in pre-tax profit for the six months to September. Sales dipped 12 per cent to MDollars 908m (USDollars 363.2m).
