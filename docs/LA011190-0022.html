January 11, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHAPMAN NARROWLY TOPS OREGON STATE IN TENNIS

David Seline, Chapman's No. 2 singles player, defeated Ted Ruben, 6-4, 4-6, 6-3, to lead the Panthers to a 5-4 tennis victory over Oregon State Wednesday at Palm Desert.

In another key match, Alan Juinio of Chapman defeated James Hazard, 6-2, 7-6 (8-6), in the No. 3 singles match.
