940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Adscene A strong performance from its publishing side helped Adscene Group to lift pre-tax profits by 45 per cent from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 3.05m for the year ended May 31. Turnover of the group, which also has printing interests, rose 13 per cent to Pounds 15.8m. Earnings per share rose 37 per cent from 10.1p to 13.8p and the recommended final dividend of 4p (3.2p) makes a total pay-out of 6.5p (5p).
