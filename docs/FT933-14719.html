930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / Observer: Neighbours 'Is Charlie in?' asked the morning caller as the door was opened to him by a sad-faced woman. 'You mustn't have heard,' she faltered. 'Soon after he came in from the pub last night, he collapsed and died.' The visitor staggered. 'I'm terribly sorry,' he said. 'Were you with him when it happened?' The widow nodded. 'Well, did he say anything about lending me a tin of red paint?'
