920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / Bechtel signs HK deal The Hong Kong government yesterday signed a HKDollars 195m (Pounds 14m) contract with International Bechtel, a subsidiary of Bechtel Group, for the large US construction company to advise it for another two years on the colony's new HKDollars 98.6bn airport and related infrastructure development, writes Simon Holberton in Hong Kong. For the past two years International Bechtel has been advising the colonial government on planning, programming and design work for the airport and nine related core projects including a bridge, a high-speed rail link and another tunnel under Victoria harbour.
