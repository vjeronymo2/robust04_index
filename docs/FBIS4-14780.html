BFN [Text] An Armenian Government delegation headed by Armenian Deputy Prime Minister Vigen Chitechyan paid a two-day official visit to Krasnodarskiy Kray. The aim of the visit was to regulate the trade and economic ties between Armenia and Krasnodarskiy Kray, and solve the problem of air freight flights that were canceled two months ago following the crash of a plane flying from Krasnodar to Gyumri. The delegation held a number of official and business talks with the leadership of the kray and with the representatives of various departments and organizations. Three agreements were signed on the principles of trade and economic cooperation for 1994, on cultural, educational and scientific cooperation, and on [words indistinct] freight transportation by air. The Armenian delegation also met with representatives of the Armenian community in the kray at the Center of National Culture in Krasnodar. The Armenian Government delegation returned to Armenia today.
