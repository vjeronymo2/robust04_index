Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 1 (SNARK)--During a period of last 6 months Azerbaijani military aircraft more than ten times entered air space of Armenia and "layed eggs" in the border adjusting regions, the letter of Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan sent to Beniamino Andreata, an acting Chairman of the CSCE, and Manfred Woerner, NATO General Secretary. Document also says that during a period of 1993 Azerbaijani aircraft did more than 50 military air-raids to the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh. Mr. Papazyan attracts the attention of the CSCE and NATO leadership to the fact that Azerbaijan doesn't follow his obligations mentioned in the Treaty of Conventional Armaments in Europe. In particular, making a 1992-1993 annual report Azerbaijan deviated from supplying with the information on military airdromes where the military air-raids are undertaking from. A considerable lack of correspondence on the existence, reduction of the warplanes quantity and their losses is typical for the informational documents.
