July 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SUMMER BASKET CASES;

ORDER ONE OF THESE BOWL PICNICS AND THE ONLY THING YOU'LL HAVE TO WORRY ABOUT IS WHETHER OR NOT YOU'VE REMEMBERED THE CORKSCREW.

Biltmore Hotel, 506 S. Grand Ave., Los Angeles, (213) 624-1011 and ask for room service. Style: This classy downtown hotel offers four summertime baskets. Orders placed before 10 a.m. are ready for pick-up by 4 p.m. the same day. Baskets include: Biltmore Bowl Picnic -- pasta salad, croissant sandwich, Brie, fresh fruit, pastry and French roll ($20 per person); The French Picnic -- watercress salad with goat cheese and lemon dressing, poached salmon, pate, baguette, Brie, fresh fruit and a bottle of Fume Blanc ($55 for two people); Grand Avenue Bar Picnic -- an assortment of pates and cheeses, roasted chicken breast, fresh fruit and pastry ($24 per person).

Bristol Farms, 606 Fair Oaks Ave., South Pasadena, (818) 441-5450; 837 Silver Spur Road, Rolling Hills Estates, (213) 541-9157 Style: Upscale supermarket offering seven varieties of picnics. All orders must be placed 24 hours in advance. Baskets include: The Maestro -- herbed roasted filet mignon ($14.25); The Concerto -- poached salmon with caper-mustard sauce ($13.95); Pops Medley -- ham, turkey or roast beef croissant sandwich ($9.75). Each basket comes with spaghetti salad, fresh fruit and cookies.

Chez Sateau Patisserie, 860 S. Baldwin Ave., Arcadia, (818) 574-0702. Style: There are nine picnic styles to choose from. Call 24 hours in advance to reserve a box dinner. Baskets include: Picnic Italian-Style -- chicken breast piccante with pasta salad ($8.50); Sample Plate -- smoked salmon with pate, sliced cheese, Roquefort dip and fresh fruit ($8); Lobster Royale -- steamed lobster with shrimp, crab, smoked salmon and vegetables ($19.50).

Jurgensen's, 842 E. California, Pasadena, (818) 792-3121. Style: This designer supermarket offers you a choice of five bowl baskets. Orders must be placed 24 hours in advance. Baskets include: Intermezzo -- layered buffalo mozzarella, tomatoes and basil; lobster tail or shrimp on eggplant pasta salad, roll, dessert ($27); Vivace -- deviled eggs, fried chicken, choice of two salads, muffin, watermelon, dessert ($14); Salad Trio -- gazpacho, choice of three salads, roll, fruit and cheese ($13.50).

Patina, 5955 Melrose Avenue, Los Angeles, (213) 467-1108. Style: Five varieties of posh picnics. Orders must be placed 24 hours in advance. Ask for Alex or Clara. Baskets include: Mozart Menu -- asparagus salad with sun-dried tomatoes and basil, roast chicken with red potatoes and scallions in mustard vinaigrette, croissant chocolate pudding with whiskey sauce ($25.50); Bach Menu -- artichoke salad with aioli sauce, Nicoise salad, rhubarb and strawberry pie with vanilla bean sauce ($29.50); Wagner Menu -- lobster salad with asparagus, veal medallion with grilled baby vegetables and vinaigrette, assorted cheeses, berry tart with raspberry coulis, chocolate truffles ($46.50).
