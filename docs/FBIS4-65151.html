BFN [Text] Edouard Balladur has said he does not believe it is France's job to shoulder endlessly the duties and responsibilities concerning Bosnia that others reject. The prime minister, who was addressing the National Assembly this afternoon, added that the time for making a choice is approaching and that everyone must be aware of it. He demanded the unconditional release of the 11 French people in the organization Premiere Urgence who are still being held by the Bosnian Serbs.
