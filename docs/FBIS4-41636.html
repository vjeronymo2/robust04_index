BFN [Text] Odessa, 26 Jun -- The "Ukrayina" Oblast Electoral Committee has received telephone calls about violations of the Electoral Law. For example, at most polling stations in Suvorovskiy, Kievskiy and Ilyichevskiy rayons of Odessa, the election posters of Vladlen Ilyin, representative of the president in Odessa oblast and candidate for the post of oblast council chairman, were removed, while posters of his rival, Ruslan Bodelan, were prominently displayed at all polling stations. There was open electioneering for Lev Vershynin, candidate for deputy to Odessa town council, and Leonid Kuchma, candidate for president of Ukraine, at polling station No. 18 of central electoral district 366 of the town of Odessa. When an observer remarked on this, the response was a threat to call the militia.
