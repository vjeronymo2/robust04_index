920330 FT 30 MAR 92 / International Company News: IBM Japan slides 32% IBM Japan, the Japanese subsidiary of the world's largest computer company, has reported a 33 per cent decline in pre-tax profits last year, to Y103.5bn (Dollars 784.1m). Sales were down to Y1,272.1bn from Y1,326.5bn. Domestic sales were off by 6.8 per cent to Y903.8bn.
