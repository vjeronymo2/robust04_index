March 23, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

TIMES TO HONOR PREP ALL-STARS

The 1990 Times All-South Bay boys and girls basketball teams will be announced in next Thursday's edition of the South Bay section.

The two 10-player squads were selected by sportswriters based on a poll of coaches and discussions with scouts. Players will be honored with a breakfast and awards ceremony at 9 a.m. April 1 at the Anaheim Hilton, 777 Convention Way.

All-stars from 12 Times circulation areas will be honored. A boys and girls player and coach of the year from each circulation area will also be announced.
