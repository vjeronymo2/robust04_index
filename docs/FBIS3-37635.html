Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Estonia and Russia are close to concluding an agreement on dismantling the nuclear training center at the former Soviet naval base in the town of Paldiski near Tallinn. [passage omitted] During the talks, Russian Vice Admiral Viktor Ovchinnikov confirmed readiness to allow IAEA [International Atomic Energy Agency] specialists into the center, however, only after the removal of the nuclear rods and secret equipment.
