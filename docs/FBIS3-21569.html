Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents] [Text] Washington March 5 TASS -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk announced late on Friday that the first train with nuclear charges "is on its way from Ukraine to Russia" where they are to be dismantled. "Ukraine will fulfil its commitments", Kravchuk told a press conference in the White House referring to the agreement according to which Ukraine gives up its nuclear weapons. "I am also sure that other countries will fulfill their obligations", he added, evidently referring to the nuclear security guarantees and financial assistance promised to Ukraine by Russia and the United States.
