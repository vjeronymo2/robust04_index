January 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LAKER FAN FINDS ENJOYMENT IN CLIPPERS

At long last, the Clippers are on their way. For too long the stepchild and underdog in town, they are fast approaching respectability.

Will attendance go up? Will they have to tear down the Sports Arena and build a new one, like they want to dismember the Coliseum? I hope not. How much insanity can one city take?

GARY TRAXLER, North Hollywood
