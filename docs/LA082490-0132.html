August 24, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ON A ROLL: The impertinent skateboarder who steals the ball from Dominique Wilkins in the current Reebok TV commercial is 12-year-old Jesse Roach of Laguna Beach. . . . It's the second TV spot for the skateboard whiz. The first, for Levi's jeans, was "dumb," but this one was fun, Jesse says. . . . Regardless, both were "gifts from God," says Jesse's mom, Joan Roach. "They paid for his college." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
