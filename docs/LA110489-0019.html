November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THEY DON'T PULL PUNCHES OVER TROJAN-IRISH GAME

Sour grapes? Stupidity? I guess it's a little of each. Jim Murray's column on Notre Dame really smacks of both. What is a Cadillac Catholic? Is he a BMW atheist? What's his message? Are all Catholic schools supposed to inform all their coaches now to lose, so they won't have a bad impression of their schools? Come on.

Give Notre Dame credit, they came back in the second half and beat a good USC team.

Obviously, Murray was rooting for USC. Better luck next time or maybe Notre Dame should drop USC, not much of a challenge there anymore.

ANTHONY CAMARRA, Westminster
