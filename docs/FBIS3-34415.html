Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting between the head of the Ukrainian Parliament's Foreign Policy Commission and the Islamic Republic of Iran's deputy foreign minister, Mr. Va'ezi, the expansion of bilateral relations and regional and international issues were discussed. In the meeting the two sides stressed that in case peace and stability are established in the region, the little countries would be able to expand cooperation in proportion to improvement of their situation. The trip of the Iranian president and the speaker of the Islamic Consultative Assembly in response to visit of the Ukrainian president and the speaker of the parliament to Tehran was also reviewed in the meeting.
