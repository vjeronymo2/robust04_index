Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A press release from the crisis committee and the presidents of the employers associations informs members and the general public that exchanges with national and international organizations, labor unions, national institutions, and the various sectors representative of the civilian society are increasing. The population is increasingly adhering to the objectives expressed in the 25 January resolution, says the press release. It states, and I quote, let us continue to reinforce our solidarity in order to save Haiti, unquote. It should be pointed out that the members of the employer sector are reportedly to meet with the members of the union sector on 1 February at the Haitian Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
