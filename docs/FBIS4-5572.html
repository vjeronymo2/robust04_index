BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu met with Hans Blix, director-general of the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA], in Tokyo on 12 April. They shared the view that North Korea should allow an early additional inspection by the IAEA and again urged North Korea to allow inspections. Minister Han, who arrived in Tokyo today on his way to Russia, urged the above during a one-hour talk with IAEA Director-General Hans Blix, who was visiting Japan.
