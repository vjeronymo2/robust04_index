July 10, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: BY THE NUMBERS

Soviet Film Industry

Number of studios -- 39 (20 produce feature films)

Annual production of short films -- 1,500

Average cost of feature production -- 450,000 rubles (approx. $692,300)

Number of cinemas -- 5,00

Number of mobile theaters -- 150,000

Average ticket price -- 29 kopecks (50 cents)

Average exhibition period -- 15 days

Average number of releases -- 150 Soviet films, 150 foreign films

Attendance per capita -- 14 a year

Number of videorecorders -- 2 million

Price of a recorded videocassette -- 50-120 rubles (approx. $75-$185)

Average price of daily rental of videocassette -- 2 rubles (approx. $3)

Average price of Soviet videorecorder -- 1,200 rubles (approx. $1,846)

Export volume of Soviet films in 1988 -- 383

Import volume of foreign films in 1988 -- 102 (59 from Socialist countries

Average license fee paid for U.S./French film -- $100,000

SOURCE: Variety. (USSR ruble = U.S. $0.65)
