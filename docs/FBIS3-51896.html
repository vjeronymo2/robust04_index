Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Charallave, Venezuela, 26 Jan (AFP) -- High school and university students demonstrated today in this small city, 50 km southeast of Caracas, marking the second consecutive day of street violence . The students protested the death of a 16-year-old high school student, who was killed yesterday when a tear gas bomb hit her in the head. The incident took place amid disturbances in the bedroom community of Los Teques, 20 km southeast of Caracas.
