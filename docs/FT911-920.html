910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Appointments: Fairey changes Mr DJ Kingsbury (pictured), chairman and chief executive of the FAIREY GROUP, who led the management buyout from Pearson, is stepping down from full time executive duties, but will continue as non-executive chairman. Mr JW Poulter, group managing director, becomes group chief executive from January 1 next year. Mr A. Hayward retires at the end of June and will be succeeded by Mr PC Boughton.
