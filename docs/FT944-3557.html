941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / Pounds 57.5m motorway link completed A 3.2-mile stretch of the M4 motorway near Swansea in south Wales was opened yesterday to complete a continuous motorway of more than 200 miles between London and Pont Abraham in Dyfed. Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary, said the completion of the Pounds 57.5m link 'will prove a tremendous boost to the economic future of the whole of south Wales'.
