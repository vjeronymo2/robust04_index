940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Confident outlook at Capital Inds Shares of Capital Industries rose 9p to 157p yesterday after the finance services and packaging group reported an encouraging start to the current year. Mr David Rhead, chairman, told shareholders at the group's annual meeting that current trading was in line with expections in both divisions. The group would 'continue to exploit the growth opportunities offered by an increasingly buoyant UK, and a recovering European economy'.
