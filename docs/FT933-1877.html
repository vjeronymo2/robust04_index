930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Gunmen kill 24 near Johannesburg In the second random attack in a day, gunmen opened fire from a speeding van last night on a busy road south of Johannesburg and killed at least 18 people, police said, AP reports from Johannesburg. Hours earlier, six people died when their taxi-van was ambushed by men with AK-47s on another highway south of Johannesburg.
