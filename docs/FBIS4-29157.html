BFN [Text] The National Assembly Office issues Communique No. 15 as follows: On 15 June the National Assembly worked at the conference hall under the direction of National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh. At the beginning of the working day, all deputies stood in a minute to commemorate Comrade Nguyen Van Cu, National Assembly deputy from Hanoi City, who passed away on 13 June. Then the National Assembly deputies put questions to some members of the government cabinet. 1. Tran Dinh Hoan, minister of labor, war invalids, and social welfare, answered questions on changes to policies regarding wounded combatants, families of war dead, and other people with meritorious deeds; one-off severance pay for cadres and employees leaving their positions; adjustment of pension for cadres and employees in accordance with the new wage policy; the situation of spontaneous immigration and measures to resolve the issue; and the situation and measures to deal with drug addiction. 2. Construction Minister Ngo Xuan Loc answered questions on issues related to the house price appraisal policy. 3. Trade Minister Le Van Triet answered questions on the cases of numerous motorcycles imported without permits by some business establishments through Saigon and Haiphong Ports; imported paper and its impact on the local paper industry; and the detainment of 38 motorcars imported by some business establishments at the Binh Dinh Market Control Force Office. 4. Dau Ngoc Xuan, minister chairman of the State Commission of Cooperation and Investment, answered questions on the elimination of irritating procedures in licensing foreign investment projects in Vietnam; and the situation and prospects regarding foreign invested projects. 5. Phan Ngoc Tuong, minister chairman of State Commission of Organization and Personnel, answered questions on changes to the Decree No. 46 on the policy on grassroots cadres at ward and village levels; the cases of cars imported temporarily then exported again in Dong Nai, Song Be, and Tay Ninh; the situation and results of the fight against corruption and smuggling; and government directions regarding this fight. 6. Tran Hoan, minister of culture and information, answered questions on issues related to state management to cultural activities. After Ministers Tran Dinh Hoan, Le Van Triet, Dau Ngoc Xuan, Phan Ngoc Tuong, and Tran Hoan finished their answers, 17 deputies raised other issues that needed further explanation. They are: Tran Thi Bach Tuyet from Dong Nai; Tran Quoc Thai from Ha Tinh; Dam Van Nguy from Cao Bang; Vu thi Ngoc Dau from Thai Binh; Le Quoc Khanh, Nguyen Don, Nguyen Ba Khanh from Quang Nam-Danang; Cu Hoa Van from Lao Cai; Trieu Thi Nhay from Yen Bai; Tran Thi Le Thu, Vo Van Luoc from Binh Dinh; Nguyen Trong Khanh from Vinh Phu; Dang Thi Thanh Huong from Quang Ninh; Tran Van Cua from Minh Hai; Nguyen Quoc Thuoc from Nghe An; Le Van Tri from Thanh Hoa [name as heard]; and Nguyen van Rang from Minh Hai [name as heard]. The aforementioned ministers continued to answer the newly-raised questions. On 16 June the National Assembly will hold a plenary session at the conference hall.
