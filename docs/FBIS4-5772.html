BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 12 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- As is reported from well informed sources, yesterday there took place a secret meeting between the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat, the chairman of the Parliament N. Bagabandi and the Prime Minister P. Jasray. They have exchanged opinions on the latest events in the political life of the country. As is informed from those sources, today there has been scheduled a meeting of the Mongolian prime minister with the general secretary of the ruling Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party (MPRP), chairman of the Mongolian National Democratic Party on the initiative of the head of the government. However the meeting was not take place due to, as was formulated, illness of the MPRP leader. [sentence as received]
