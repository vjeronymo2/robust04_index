August 24, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

WOJAROWICZ FORUM IN SANTA MONICA

The Santa Monica Museum of Art and the Coalition for Freedom of Expression have scheduled a series of eight discussions about the museum's current "Tongues of Flame" exhibition by controversial New York-based artist David Wojarowicz.

Artists -- including Jacki Apple, Renee Petropoulos, Diane Buckler, Ed Landler, John O'Brein and Margaret Lazzari -- will talk about Wojarowicz's work and address issues of censorship that have been raised by the show.

The free discussions will begin at 1 and 3 p.m. this Saturday and Sunday and Sept. 1 and 2 at the museum, 2437 Main St., Santa Monica. Information: (213) 399-0433 or (213) 851-0737.
