April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CAPITALISTIC BEHAVIOR: IS IT AN EXERCISE IN GREED OR BRILLIANCE?

True capitalism demands that all human interaction be conducted on a voluntary basis, appealing to the judgment of men, instead of on a mandatory basis, depending on the subjugation of men. It is a moral system. Let's stop feeling guilty about our new CD or the human freedom that created it.

CASEY FAHY

La Canada
