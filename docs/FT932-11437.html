930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / International Company News: 3M improves to Dollars 330m 3M, the US manufacturer of adhesives, magnetic tapes and other industrial and consumer products, reported first-quarter earnings of Dollars 330m, or Dollars 1.51 a share, up from Dollars 303m, or Dollars 1.30, last year. Sales edged up to Dollars 3.52bn from Dollars 3.44bn in the quarter, while operating earnings jumped to Dollars 530m, from last year's first-quarter Dollars 509m. The results were slightly higher than Wall Street's projections of Dollars 1.50 a share. Mr LD DeSimone, chairman, said the company's largest US business sector, industrial and consumer products, saw a 10 per cent volume gain in the first quarter. Total US volume gained 7 per cent in the quarter, while international sales grew by about 6 per cent.
