BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 24 May (ITAR-TASS) -- At a meeting in the Kremlin today Boris Yeltsin and Viktor Chernomyrdin discussed the development of mechanisms to implement the latest package of presidential decrees on economic issues, aimed at intensifying and invigorating the reforms. The Russian president and prime minister considered a number of draft decrees on the economy which are under preparation. The Russian Federation president's press office told ITAR-TASS that personnel issues were also discussed at the meeting. Viktor Chernomyrdin told Boris Yeltsin about the progress in talks with Ukraine's acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy in connection with the situation in Crimea.
