Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Own information" report: "Russia Still Needs Strong Special Services"] [Text] A press conference was held yesterday at the Russian Federation Federal Counterintelligence Service. It was stated that recently, since the Russian president's edict on disbanding the Security Ministry and creating the Federal Counterintelligence Service, the staffers have been certified and the special service's directorates and subdepartments had been formed with a consideration for the reforms which are being carried out in the country. According to Oleg Lobov, secretary of the Russian Security Council, there has been no purge. The staffers were selected on a professioanl principle, for their professional and personal qualities. The Russian president's desire to create a strong Russian special service has been considered as far as possible.
