August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SONG SUNG BLUE: WHAT THEY'RE SAYING ABOUT BARR-MANGLED BANNER

Thank you, Roseanne Barr, for proving my point. For years I have been telling friends that, instead of a talented comedienne, you are a disrespectful swine who uses brash vulgarity to mask your own self-pity and feelings of insecurity. To paraphrase the man, it is better to be thought a pig, than to open your mouth, spit on hallowed ground and remove all doubt.

JEFF WEBER

Irvine
