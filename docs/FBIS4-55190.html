BFN [Excerpts] High-ranking sources within the Srpska army confirmed today the AFP report on the Muslim army's deployment of heavy weapons and troops on the southern foothills of Mount Igman [south of Sarajevo]. According to the same source, the weapons and troops were transferred via Sarajevo airport from the Muslim-held part of the city. [passage omitted] According to the same source, the Muslims have transferred 5,000 soldiers from Sarajevo to Mount Igman and Bjelasnica and, together with the forces deployed in the area previously and forces brought in from Konjic and Pazaric, they now have around 10,000 troops in the area, deployed in seven brigades.
