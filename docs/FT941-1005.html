940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Italy goes to polls under new system Achille Occhetto, leader of the seven-party Progressive Alliance, signals his hopes for victory in the Italian general election which began yesterday. Italy's 48m voters have started two days of polling in what is seen as the country's most unpredictable general election since democracy was restored after the second world war. The election marks the formal end of the postwar dominance of Italian politics by the Christian Democrats and their allies. Report, Page 14
