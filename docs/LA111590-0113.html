November 15, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

LEARJET WINS AIR FORCE MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

Learjet Inc. has won a $15.4-million contract to provide the Air Force with maintenance on C-21A aircraft.

Parts and maintenance for 83 Air Force and Air National Guard aircraft will be provided by GLASCO, a Learjet subsidiary with employees at domestic and overseas Air Force bases.

The C-21A is the Air Force's version of the Learjet 35A.
