18. Section 86.094&hyph;2 of subpart A is amended by revising the introductory paragraph and by adding the following definitions in alphabetical order, to read as follows: §86.094&hyph;2 Definitions. The definitions of §86.093&hyph;2 remain effective. The definitions listed in this section are effective beginning with the 1994 model year. * * * * * Gaseous fuel means natural gas or liquefied petroleum gas. * * * * * Liquefied petroleum gas means a liquid hydrocarbon fuel that is stored under pressure and is composed primarily of species that are gases at atmospheric conditions (temperature = 25 ○C and pressure = 1 atm), excluding natural gas. Multi-fuel means capable of operating on two or more different fuel types, either separately or simultaneously. Natural gas means a fuel whose primary constituent is methane. Petroleum fuel means liquid fuels normally derived from crude oil, excluding liquefied petroleum gas. Gasoline and diesel fuel are petroleum fuels. * * * * * 19.
