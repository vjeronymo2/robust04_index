BFN [Text] Amman--AL-RA'Y learns from well-informed sources that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat is expected in Amman tomorrow, Monday, for talks with His Majesty King Husayn and senior officials. Sources say that discussions will center on effective joint coordination and the declaration by the Palestinian side that they have agreed to Jordanian proposals pertaining to the plan of action related to the Joint Economic Agreement. The sources further say that during his stay in Amman, 'Arafat is expected to undergo medical checkups to ascertain that his health condition is stable. However, the sources say that this will depend on whether or not he will have enough time to spare and provided nothing new is added to his heavy schedule in the wake of preparations for his visiting the autonomous region next week.
