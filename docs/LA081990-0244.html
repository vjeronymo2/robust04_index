August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CRITIC'S PICKS

'MURPHY'S ROMANCE'

James Garner (pictured with Sally Field) won a much-deserved Academy Award nomination as an independent-minded, widowed pharmacist in a small Southwestern town who conducts a roundabout pursuit of a struggling, much-younger divorcee (Field). A genuinely adult romantic comedy, written by Irving Ravetch and Harriet Frank Jr. and directed by Martin Ritt. A true delight.

(KCOP Sunday at 6 p.m.) Kevin Thomas
