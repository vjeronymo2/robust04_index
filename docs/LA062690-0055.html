June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Off the Wall: Ken and Bob, the morning duo on KABC-AM (790), will take it to the wall when they host live broadcasts from the Berlin Wall starting today and continuing through Friday. Station officials said that Ken and Bob will be conversing with Berlin residents from all walks of life, observing their lifestyles and exploring their emotions about the past and the future. The show will cover everything from politics and economics to freedom and fashion. The program will air daily from 5 to 9 a.m. GREG BRAXTON
