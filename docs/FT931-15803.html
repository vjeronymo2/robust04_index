930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 50m orders won by Donelon Tyson DONELON TYSON has secured orders totalling Pounds 50m, which are evenly spread across the group's construction divisions. JF Donelon & Co, the specialist tunnelling and civil engineering subsidiary, recently won Pounds 20m of new 'water spend' orders including a Pounds 15.5m contract for the construction of a sewer in Cleethorpes. Tysons, the building and joinery manufacturing arm, has won orders totalling some Pounds 30m including two contracts of Pounds 8.5m each to Tysons Construction for work on the Aintree Industrial Estate in Liverpool and Valley Road Business Park on the Wirral.
