Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Second Session of the Eighth Henan Provincial People's Congress called a preparatory meeting in Zhengzhou yesterday afternoon [27 March]. The meeting was attended by 131 delegates, which constituted a quorum. Li Changchun, chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, and Vice Chairmen Zhang Zhigang, Liu Guangxiang, Fan Lian, Hu Tingji, Hou Zhiying, Qin Kecai, Zhong Lisheng, and Wang Hongfan took their seats on the front row of the rostrum. Entrusted by Chairman Li Changchun, Vice Chairman Zhang Zhigang presided. He said: The current preparatory meeting for the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress is convened under the new situation in which the people across the province have earnestly studied the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, and the first enlarged plenary session of the ninth provincial party committee in order to quicken the pace in building the socialist market economy structure. It is a major event in the political life of the province's people. [passage ommitted] The preparatory meeting of the second session of the provincial people's congress elected its presidium and secretaries-general. The presidium comprises 106 delegates.
