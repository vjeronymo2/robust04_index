931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / International Company News: Mars plans to invest Dollars 10m in India MARS, the New Jersey-based company, plans to invest Rs320m (Dollars 10m) to set up two factories in India to tap into the Rs8bn-a-year market for chocolate and cocoa-based products, AP-DJ reports from New Delhi. The company will employ 300 workers and production is to start in 1996.
