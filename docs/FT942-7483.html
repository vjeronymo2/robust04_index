940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Royal Bank's US offshoot expands Citizens Financial Group, the US subsidiary of Royal Bank of Scotland, has acquired seven branch offices and Dollars 117m(Pounds 78m) in deposits of the Coastal Federal Savings Bank from the Resolution Trust Corporation, for Dollars 10.7m cash. Coastal, which is based in New London, Connecticut, went into receivership in June 1992.
