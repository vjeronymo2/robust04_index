BFN [Text] Vienna, June 15 (AFP) -- OPEC oil ministers agreed Wednesday [15 June] to maintain the organization's production ceiling unchanged at 24.52 million barrels a day (MBD), Libya's oil minister 'Abdallah Salim al-Badri said. OPEC Secretary-General Subroto said the organization did not plan any more meetings before next November 16. The ministers are slated to continue talks Thursday morning on choice of a new secretary-general. On another point, Subroto said the ministers had decided to keep OPEC headquarters in Vienna, despite having received proposals from the city of Geneva, Switzerland, to house the organization's secretariat. Subroto said that OPEC members had respected their quotas in May to a "satisfactory" extent, and that prouction for the month averaged 24.922 million barrels.
