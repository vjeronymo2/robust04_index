Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The German Cabinet today approved the 1994 annual economic report submitted by Economic Affairs Minister Guenter Rexrodt. A spokesman for Rexrodt said the cabinet also approved a draft for the abolition of the Rebates Law. According to the annual economic report, the German Government is expecting a further increase in the number of unemployed by around 450,000 to nearly four million. Economic growth should amount to 1-1.5 percent with the rate of inflation running at around 3 percent. Rexrodt is to give a news conference (at 1300 GMT) to discuss details of the report.
