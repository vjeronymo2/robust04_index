920408 FT 08 APR 92 / International Company News: Warner Music, Ticketmaster deal WARNER Music Group and Ticketmaster have agreed to form a joint venture for computerised ticketing operations in Europe, AP-DJ reports. The new company, called Ticketmaster Europe Group, will use a computerised system that Ticketmaster developed and operates in the US to establish similar ticketing businesses throughout Europe, according to the partners.
