930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Babangida 'will step down' Nigerian military ruler Ibrahim Babangida, pressed by trade unions and pro-democracy groups to hand over power, will step down tomorrow. a senior aide said. 'I can assure you that he is leaving on Wednesday', said the unnamed aide. Nigerian military faces protests, Page 4; Oil prices rise on Nigerian unrest, Page 21
