Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" nescast] [Text] The National Executive Council of the PRI released a communique at 0300 local time today reporting on a meeting with President Salinas. This is the text of a paragraph of the communique on whether another presidential candidate could be designated today: In this private meeting, it was said that the indignation and grief of the PRI will not permit decisions that only PRI members should make. To honor Colosio and in memory of his message of unity and harmony among Mexicans, we will meet our commitments and our political responsibility over time.
