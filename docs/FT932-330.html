930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / International Company News: Talks continue at Northwest Airlines THE situation at Northwest Airlines, the heavily-indebted US carrier which is expected to file for bankruptcy protection if it cannot secure significant labour concessions, remained unresolved late yesterday, writes Nikki Tait. The company said directors of Northwest's parent company were briefed on the status of talks with the airline's pilots, which had continued over the weekend, and were in favour of reaching 'a voluntary restructuring agreement'.
