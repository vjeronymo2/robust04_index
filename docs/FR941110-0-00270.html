A new §498.4 is added to read as follows: §498.4 NFs subject to appeals process in part 498. A NF is considered a provider for purposes of this part when it has in effect an agreement to participate in Medicaid, including an agreement to participate in both Medicaid and Medicare and it is a_ (a) State-operated NF; or (b) Non State-operated NF that is subject to compliance action as a result of_ (1) A validation survey by HCFA; or (2) HCFA's review of the State's survey findings. 7.
