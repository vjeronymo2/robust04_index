Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A prominent Muslim leader denied the existence of an Islamic jihad in the Philippines. MNLF [Moro National Liberation Front] Reformist Group Chief, Makapanton Abbas says they have not heard any local chapter of the Islamic jihad, a group formed by Iranian extremist during the civil war in Lebanon. The group which claimed responsibility for the bombings in Davao City had called itself Islamic Jihad. Abbas maintains that rightist military elements are behind the bomb attacks.
