930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE February/March c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 360, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 325, BWD Dollars 340, C and F Antwerp BTC Dollars 340, BWC Dollars 340, BTD Dollars 315, BWD Dollars 315. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 713 tonnes for the week ended February 5, compared with 129 tonnes in the previous week. Renewed purchasing in many specialist styles attracted much attention. CIS and American qualities as well as Tanzanian and Syrian growths moved off freely.
