941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / Weekend Money (Companies): Directors' dealings The largest transaction of the week was at Gerrard and National Holdings, a financial house that announced interim results on October 27 showing a good increase on the same period last year, writes Vivien MacDonald of The Inside Track. Chairman Robert Williamson was also upbeat in his message to shareholders. For personal reasons, however, executive director Mark Davies has sold 359,000 shares at 470p. Hewden Stuart, a Glasgow-based plant hire company, announced improved interims last month.
