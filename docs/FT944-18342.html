941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bisichi Mining Bisichi Mining raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 102,000 to Pounds 193,000 in the first half of 1994, after including this time an exceptional Pounds 141,000 mostly from the sale of an investment in a company recovering diamonds from the ocean floor of Namibia. Earnings per share were 1.72p (0.74p). Net assets grew 10 per cent to Pounds 5.49m at June 30, against Pounds 5m a year earlier.
