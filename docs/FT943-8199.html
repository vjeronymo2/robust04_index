940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / International Company News: Paribas French shows improvement Net asset value per share at Paribas French Investment Trust ended the six months to June 30 at 133.33p, against 124.57p a year earlier. Net revenue increased from Pounds 253,800 to Pounds 383,700. Earnings per share were 1.28p (0.85p).
