930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / Welding tank for offshore divers A Pounds 340,000 tank where trainee offshore divers will undergo examinations in underwater welding inspection is to be built in Middlesbrough, Cleveland, by the Welding Institute. The land-based tank, expected to deal with more than 250 examinees a year after it opens in mid-1994, will replace examinations currently held in open sea near Plymouth.
