921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Court grants Bank injunction THE BANK of England yesterday obtained a High Court injunction against Sylcon Finance of Brook Street, London, to restrain it from taking deposits and freezing the assets of three managers, Mr Harry Reuben, Mr Ian Craig Reuben, and Mr Michael Reuben. Sylcon Finance has total assets of Pounds 1.75m and 36 customers.
