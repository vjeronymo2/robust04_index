Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 14 Feb -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has not accepted Ella Pamfilova's notice of resignation from the post of social protection minister said Valentin Sergeyev, head of the government's press service, to an ITAR-TASS correspondent. According to Sergeyev, the head of the government thinks that Ella Pamfilova "is needed by the government, the ministry and by those who are in want of social protection. She must and will continue to work in spite of all the difficulties of the present economic situation".
