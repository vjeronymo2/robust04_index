April 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

Eastwood Sues Tabloid: Actor Clint Eastwood has filed a $20-million lawsuit against the Globe, accusing the supermarket tabloid of fabricating a front-page story headlined, "Clint's Life Threatened by Nazi Cult." Eastwood's lawsuit, filed in Los Angeles Superior Court, contends that the tabloid's Jan. 1 story, featuring a photo of Eastwood holding a handgun, has put the actor's life in danger. The Globe story says a group called the Aryan Nation had put a $100,000 bounty on Eastwood's life because the actor refused to speak at the organization's convention in Washington. CLAUDIA PUIG and GREG BRAXTON
