920110 FT 10 JAN 92 / International Company News: SBC to acquire Chicago group SWISS Bank Corporation (SBC) is to acquire The O'Connor Partnerships, the Chicago-based specialists in equity and currency options. The move, subject to approval by the US Federal Reserve Board, comes a little more than a year after SBC invested SFr90m (Dollars 69.2m) in an 80 per cent stake in a joint venture with O'Connor, called SBC-OC Services. SBC said the proposed acquisition was aimed at improving the bank's penetration of US investment banking markets.
