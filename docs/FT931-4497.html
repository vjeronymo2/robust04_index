930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News: USDC lifts net asset value by 13% USDC Investment Trust lifted net asset value from 192.1p to 216.4p per share, last year. The trust, managed by GT Management, reported net revenue of Pounds 2.05m (Pounds 1.95m), equivalent to earnings of 5.59p (5.33p) per share. The recommended final dividend is held at 3.25p to bring the total for the year to 4.5p (4.25p).
