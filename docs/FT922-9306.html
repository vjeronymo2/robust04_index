920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Delyn slips 15% to Pounds 524,000 Delyn Group, a manufacturer of consumer packaging, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 524,000 for the year to February 2, a 15 per cent shortfall on the previous year's Pounds 614,000. Turnover showed little change at Pounds 13.1m (Pounds 13.5m). There was an exceptional provision of Pounds 42,000 but interest charges were cut to Pounds 406,000 (Pounds 638,000). A maintained final dividend of 1p holds the total at 1.45p. Earnings declined to 3.32p (4.47p). Year-end gearing stood at 28 per cent (54 per cent).
