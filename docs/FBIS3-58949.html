Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report signed "cob.": "Instead Party Goes Beyond Hamburg's Borders"] [Text] Hamburg -- In Hamburg, the voters association Statt Partei [Instead Party] has decided in favor of nationwide expansion. Its participation in the forthcoming Bundestag election was not ruled out by Markus Wegner, the founder of the Instead Party, and other speakers. "We want our share in controlling the power in Bonn; we want to be there," Wegner said. Some candidates who were defeated in a vote announced that they would resign from the party.
