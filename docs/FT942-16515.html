940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: House prices continue to inch upwards - At a Glance UK house prices rose fractionally last month according to Halifax, the largest building society. They increased by 0.2 per cent on a seasonally adjusted basis in March compared to February - the third monthly increase running. Nationwide, the second largest society last week reported a higher monthly increase of 0.6 per cent. Over the past year, house prices have risen by 2.6 per cent and the average house now costs Pounds 63,122, according to Halifax which predicts that prices will be up by 5 per cent over 1994.
