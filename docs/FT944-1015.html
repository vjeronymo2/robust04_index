941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Shares fall 9p at Brit Thornton The profits warnings made by British Thornton Holdings in October was borne out yesterday when the packaging and specialist furniture group reported a decline in interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 304,000. The shares fell 9p to 31p on news of the fall from a previous Pounds 1.15m. Mr Brian North, chairman, said that in the six months to October 31 lower sales in the video games market had reduced turnover in Masterpack's contract packaging business by Pounds 1m. Group sales fell to Pounds 4.66m (Pounds 4.88m). The interim dividend is cut to 1p (2.5p) and the group expects to repeat this payment in the second half. Earnings per share were 1.33p (6.56p).
