September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

DRIVERS' USE OF SHORTCUTS

The City of Los Angeles, the City Council of Los Angeles and the County of Los Angeles need to put the public safety ahead of special-interest groups.

Please let's get the trucks off the freeways during the peak traffic hours. Let's get the commuters off the residential streets and back on the freeways. Los Angeles did a great job during the 1984 Olympics.

LAURIE KELSON

Encino
