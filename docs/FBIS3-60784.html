Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Following are reports on insurgent activities in Peru compiled from Peruvian papers published on 11 December. The National Counterterrorism Directorate on 18 November arrested Shining Path "terrorist" Edison Tomas Zarate Untiveros, 23, in Lima's La Victoria District. He carried ammunition, "subversive" literature, and handwritten notes. He had sought refuge in Argentina, from where he returned to Peru at the request of People's Aid of Peru to head its defense department's military command and to prepare car bombs for the organization. Zarate reportedly was involved in car bombs that exploded between September and November. Police also arrested six other Shining Path "terrorists" during operations in Lima between 24 November and 2 December. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 11 Dec 93 p A16)
