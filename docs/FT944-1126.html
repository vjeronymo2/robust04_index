941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Greece awards Athens casino licence Greece has awarded an Athens casino licence to a Greek-South African consortium for Dr11bn (Dollars 45m). The casino, due to open at the end of 1995, will be managed by Sun International, owned by Ms Sol Kerzner, South African hotelier and casino operator. The consortium, in which Sun International has a 10 per cent stake, also agreed to invest Dollars 400m in building a 300-bed hotel, marina and conference centre on a waterfront site leased from the Greek state. The other participants are four Greek construction companies and Marracom, a Greek-backed investment company.
