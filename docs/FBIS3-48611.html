Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry has reported on Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri's official visit to Brazil on 24 January as guest of the Brazilian foreign minister. According to the report, both sides agreed to expand bilateral relations, particularly in the fields of trade, investment, and tourism, and to broaden economic cooperation by setting up a commercial center in Sao Paolo. On bilateral cooperation, Thailand and Brazil will continue cooperation in the framework of the United Nations, the GATT, and the Cairns Group. They will expand cooperation between the economic organizations in their regions, such as between the Rio Group and ASEAN. The Thai and Brazilian foreign ministers signed the agreement on visa exemption for holders of diplomatic and official passports. They also discussed the desire and ways for both sides to further strenthen bilateral relations and cooperation.
