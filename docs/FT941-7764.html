940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / UK Company News: R&M Smaller shows advance in asset value River & Mercantile Smaller Companies Trust raised its interim net asset value to 164.29p at the end of January 1994, against 116.75p a year earlier and 132.2p at July 31 1993. Gross revenue for the six months was lower at Pounds 698,000, against Pounds 845,000 which included a substantial exceptional dividend from TV-am. Net earnings fell to Pounds 317,000 (Pounds 443,000) equivalent to 1.26p (1.77p) per share. The interim dividend is held at 1.25p.
