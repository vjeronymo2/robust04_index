BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Bundestag President Rita Suessmuth met the Latvian Saeima Chairman Anatolijs Gorbunovs in Bonn today. Gorbunovs briefed Suessmuth on the most recent political and economic developments in his country. He also spoke of the problems regarding the return of Russian troops still in Latvia, the Bundestag press office announced. Frau Suessmuth stressed during the talks that Latvia's regained independence could not be endangered by controversial questions which remain between Russia and the Baltic states. The two politicians also discussed the possibility of integrating Latvia into Europe and the development of political and parliamentary relations between the two states. Gorbunovs is in Germany for a four-day visit at the invitation of the Bundestag president.
