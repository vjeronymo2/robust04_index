920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / Engineering 'diplomat' SIR James Hamilton, an aeronautical engineer and former director-general of the Concorde project, was yesterday named as the 'roving diplomat' to help establish a single body to act as a focus for the engineering professions. Sir James, a former permanent under-secretary at the Department of Education and Science, will be an executive member of a steering group announced last month as the next stage in a plan to resolve difficulties caused by the UK's fragmented professional engineering structure.
