BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 Apr (MENA)--An official Palestinian source has told MENA that the visit to Cairo by Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has been postponed to Tuesday [5 April] or Wednesday. The source pointed out that the postponement is due to Egyptian and Palestinian engagements. 'Arafat was expected to arrive in Cairo this evening or tomorrow morning for consultations with President Husni Mubarak and senior Egyptian officials on the ongoing peace negotiations with Israel in Cairo to pave the way for implementing the Gaza-Jericho agreement. The agreement is expected to be signed in Cairo before 13 April.
