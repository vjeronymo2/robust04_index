940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Observer: One last puff Remember Timothy Leary, the acid-popping Harvard professor of the 'tune in, turn on, drop out' slogan? Well, the man whose promotion of the use of LSD in the 1960s made him an idol of US college campuses, has been breaking the law again. He has been booked by the police at Austin airport, Texas, for smoking a cigarette in a no-smoking zone. The times, they are a'changing . . .
