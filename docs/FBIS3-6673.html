Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Pyongyangites Greet 1st Anniversary of Yi In-mo's Return Home" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 19 (KCNA) -- Working people in Pyongyang held a joint meeting to mark the first anniversary of the return of a former war correspondent of the Korean People's Army Yi In-mo, to the bosom of the Workers' Party of Korea. Put up at the meeting place was a board carrying the words of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il: "Our party remembers Comrade Yi In-mo, an incarnation of faith and will". On display at the meeting place were silk banners, scrolls and gifts sent by people of all strata to Yi In-mo. Kim Ki-nam, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, in his speech at the meeting said that Yi In-mo's return to the socialist homeland with an unshakable faith and will for the party and the leader was a historical event which demonstrated the brilliant victory of Korean-style socialism centred on the popular masses over capitalism. "Yi In-mo could fight resolutely with an unshakable faith in the party and the leader, unflinchingly defending the principle of a member of the Workers' Party of Korea, not yielding to the sufferings in prison for 34 years in South Korea, because he revered the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, the mental pillars of the Korean people, the speaker said, and stressed: "Through Yi's destiny, the Korean people have become more convinced and keenly felt that the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il is the destiny of our country and nation, the destiny of socialism and a great symbol of all our victory, glory and happiness". Yi In-mo said that it is the dear leader who has brought him back to life from the jaw of death and extended warm thanks to the dear leader who has brought him the happiness today. He said he came to the meeting with a floral basket, wishing the great leader President Kim Il-song and general Kim Chong-il a long life in good health. Yi Hyon-ok, daughter of Yi In-mo, referring to the loving care shown by the dear leader to her father, said that the dear leader is the destiny and a tender-hearted father of all our people. Yi In-mo and his family sang a song overflowing with deepest reverence for the dear leader to the accompaniment of the piano sent by him. Poems written by Yi In-mo in praise of the greatness of general Kim Chong-il were read at the meeting. Anti-Japanese revolutionary fighters and workers, farmers, youths and students of the new generation present at the meeting said that they would defend and glorify Korean-style socialism, loudly singing the song of faith that we would certainly win in any difficulty because we are guided by supreme commander general Kim Chong-il. Gifts from people of all strata to Yi In-mo were conveyed at the meeting.
