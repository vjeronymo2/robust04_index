December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THE AMERICAN MILITARY ACTION IN PANAMA

The U.S. action against Noriega has provided yet another illustatrion of the invariably critical, antagonistic and negative stance of the media. The same commentators and reporters who severely criticized the Administration for not taking more decisive action at the time of the attempted coup now exhaust every possible extreme to discredit the positive action taken.

W. H. COLEMAN

Cambria
