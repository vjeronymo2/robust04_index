October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PLACING THE BLAME FOR A LACK OF MORAL FIBER

I agree wholeheartedly with Josephson. But he forgot to mention one other reason (for young people's lack of ethics) besides parents, teachers and employers: television.

I blame TV for lots of things, including crime and murder. Children see all these shows with killings and shootings, and they become desensitized and think it's no big deal to shoot somebody.

BRIGITTE CHRISTE

Calabasas
