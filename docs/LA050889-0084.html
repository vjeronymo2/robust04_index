May 8, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Reed Buys News Corp. Divisions: Reed International said it has agreed to buy the worldwide travel and electronic publishing divisions of Rupert Murdoch's News Corp. for $825 million in cash. Reed, a London-based publisher of travel information in Asia and Europe through its ABC International subsidiary, said the acquisition, subject to shareholder approval, would give it a stake in the fast-growing U.S. travel market.
