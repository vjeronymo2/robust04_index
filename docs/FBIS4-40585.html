BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Text] United Nations June 17 TASS -- Russia is holding bilateral consultations with the United States and the DPRK on peaceful settlement of the North Korean nuclear problem at the United Nations headquarters, Russian Permanent Representative to the United Nations Yuliy Vorontsov said here today. "We still want a peaceful solution to this problem, and it is possible to do it. That is why, we continue to discuss the problem at the bilateral level with the United States and North Korea. We would like the Security Council to take a decision that would help a political solution," said Vorontsov.
