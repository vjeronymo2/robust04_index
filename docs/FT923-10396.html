920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Company News: IRI reports net loss of L312bn IRI, Italy's large state holding company, reported a consolidated net loss for the 1991 full year of L312bn (Dollars 279.9m), compared with a net profit of L1,108bn the previous year, Reuter reports from Rome. The industrial sector swung into a L938bn loss in 1991 against a L280bn profit the year earlier. Its banking sector posted a profit of L895bn, down from L1,048bn. IRI gave preliminary figures in May.
