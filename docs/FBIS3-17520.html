Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Turkmenia [Turkmenistan] intends to start cooperating with NATO in the near future, according to Boris Shikhmuradov, the republican prime minister. Turkmenia's participation in the Partnership for Peace program was the center of attention at the talks between the Turkmenistani leadership and a NATO delegation in Ashkhabat [Ashgabat]. The talks also discussed prospects for NATO's assistance to Turkmenia and in particular, assistance in training personnel for the national armed forces.
