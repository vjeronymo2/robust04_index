April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GRADE A ON THE B&B

My wife and I just returned from a week's stay at the House of Blue Ginger, a B&B at 47-504A Lulani St., Kaneohe, Hawaii 96744 on Oahu. We confirm Jerry Hulse's tip in an earlier column. It is a marvelous alternative to staying in Waikiki.

HELEN AND GERALD LERMAN

Culver City
