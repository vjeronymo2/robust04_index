930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: Hambros Inv Trust net asset value up Hambros Investment Trust had a net asset value of 47.11p per share at March 31 against 46.44p six months earlier and 46.78p at the previous year end. Net revenue for the 12 months fell to Pounds 31,843 (Pounds 49,065). Losses per share were 0.02p (0.01p earnings).
