930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: North Sea Assets down Reduced demand for drilling support services meant pre-tax profits of North Sea Assets slipped from Pounds 2.08m to Pounds 1.95m in 1992. Turnover dropped to Pounds 21.6m (Pounds 29.1m). Earnings per share were 3.33p (4.16p), and the dividend is 1p (0.75p).
