940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Fleming High Income Investment Trust Net asset value per share was 117.9p as at January 31 compared with 94.1p at April 30 1993. Available revenue for the nine months to end-January amounted to Pounds 1.11m (Pounds 1.32m) after tax of Pounds 284,000 (Pounds 523,000). Third interim dividend 1.1p (1.45p). Earnings per share were 3.45p (4.19p).
