BFN [Text] It has been revealed that the five North Koreans who were recently deported from Russia for attempting to obtain nuclear materials [haek mulchil] were diplomats. (Basilev), spokesman for the Counter-Intelligence Headquarters of the Russian Federation, said today that the five North Koreans who tried to obtain materials [mulchil] for use in manufacturing nuclear weapons were designated as figures to be avoided [kipi inmul], that is, unwelcome diplomats in the country where they work, and were deported from Russia in the spring.
