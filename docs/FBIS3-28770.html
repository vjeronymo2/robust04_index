Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 9 (KCNA) -- The birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il is being celebrated in many countries. A celebration meeting was held in Lagos on February 3 under the cosponsorship of the Nigeria-the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Friendship Association and the Nigerian Committee For Supporting the Peaceful Reunification of Korea. Chairman of the Friendship Association, Yemi, Oyeneye, in his report said that the birth of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, an outstanding thinker and theoretician and a tender-hearted teacher of the world's revolutionary people, was a great sunrise illuminating the future of Korea. He enjoys deepest respect and reverence not only of the Korean people but also of the progressive people of the world for the undying exploits he has performed for the human cause of independence, the reporter said, and stressed: "The world people highly praise the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il as the greatest of the great men of the century and the sun of mankind." A celebrating meeting was held in Dani Province, Togo, on February 1. Korean film shows and photo exhibitions were held at the Malagasy parliament, in Lome of Togo, Sikasso of Mali and by the Sikasso City committee of the African Party for the Solidarity and Justice of Mali. The Zimbabwean paper STAR January 28 carried the gist of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il's work "Our Socialism Centred on the Masses Shall Not Perish" and an article titled "The Korean People Are Celebrating the Birthday of Their Leader, Full of Self-confidence". His Excellency Kim Chong-il has devoted his all to bringing the advantages and vitality of Korean-style socialism into fuller play and made an active contribution to strengthening and developing the Non-aligned Movement in conformity with the demand of the world people fighting for the building of a new independent world and promoting friendly and cooperative relations with the peoples of all countries, the paper stressed. The Peru-Korea Institute of Culture and Friendship published a special issue of its bulletin on January 31.
