BFN [Text] Finland will send 45 UN observers to South Africa's first democratic elections. Representatives of all races in South Africa are allowed to vote in these elections during the last week of this month. Finland will send 15 observers to South Africa, and they will be placed under the United Nations. In addition, the Foreign Ministry has hired 30 observers directly for the UN. The first Finnish group of observers will leave for South Africa on 17 April.
