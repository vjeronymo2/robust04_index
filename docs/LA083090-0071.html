August 30, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR: MORE BARS:

Newport Landing, 503 E. Edgewater Ave., Newport Beach. Open daily till 2 a.m., Mondays through Fridays from 11:30 a.m., Saturdays from 8 a.m., Sundays from 9 a.m. (714) 675-2373.

It's on the glitzy side, it tends to be crowded, the waitresses are a trifle slow and the drinks ain't cheap. But there's a great view of the harbor and the ferries that makes it all worthwhile. A fine place to park yourself on a Sunday afternoon.
