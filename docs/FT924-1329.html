921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Amberley incurs Pounds 79,000 loss Amberley Group, a provider of building preservation services, ran up a loss of Pounds 79,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. That compared with previous profits of Pounds 75,000. First half turnover totalled Pounds 1.62m (Pounds 1.59m). Losses per share were 1.44p (0.59p earnings).
