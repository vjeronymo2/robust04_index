BFN [Text] Tskhinvali, 4 May -- Ludvig Chibirov has been elected chairman of the state nikhas [parliament] of so-called South Ossetia. Zaur Gasiyev has been elected first deputy chairman, and A. Khabisov deputy chairman. Feliks Gasiyev has been elected chairman of the Council of Ministers and Eduard Guzitayev as his first deputy. Anatoliy Chekhoyev, who is a former first secretary of South Ossetian oblast (?Communist Party) committee, has been appointed ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the so-called republic of South Ossetia to the Russian Federation.
