BFN [Text] The extremist Islamic Group organization has claimed responsibility for the attack that targeted a tourist train last night as it was passing through al-Nakhilah village in Abu Tij, Asyut Governorate, on the way from Aswan to Cairo. The incident did not result in any casualties. The security services searched the area surrounding the scene of the incident. This is the ninth attack against trains in the past two years and the first since last March.
