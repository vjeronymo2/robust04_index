BFN [Text] The Dominican Republic has decided to respect the almost total embargo imposed on Haiti by the UN Security Council on 21 May. According to Fabio Herrera Cabral, the Dominican Government intends to deploy soldiers in the border areas to prevent leaks. Herrera added that Dominican Navy troops will be sent to prevent any and all Haitian initiatives to stock pile fuel via the sea. Haitian vessels that may try to reach the Dominican coasts to buy gasoline will not be authorized to pull into any Dominican port.
