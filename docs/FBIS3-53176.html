Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A ranking Syrian military delegation today visited President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul, and Army Commander General Emile Lahhud. The Syrian delegation is made up of Major General Ibrahim Safi, the new commander of the Syrian forces in Lebanon; Major General 'Isa Shidyaq, former commander of the Syrian forces in Lebanon; and Brigadier General Ghazi Kan'an, chief of Syrian military intelligence in Lebanon.
