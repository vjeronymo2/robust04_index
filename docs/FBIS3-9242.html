Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Arrest of Albanians -- Political Case"] [Text] In reference to the latest arrests of Albanian citizens, the Albanian People's Democratic Party appealed to all government bodies to order an urgent release of all citizens imprisoned in this political and fabricated process. They warn the government has an obligation to treat this case in the same way it did similar attempts by certain Macedonians to organise themselves. Otherwise, they say the Macedonian Government takes on a responsibility for possible worsening of the situation in the country, which is something Macedonia [FYROM -- The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] needs the least at the moment.
