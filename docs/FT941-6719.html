940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / Millions of Romanians strike Romania's two largest unions said some 2m workers participated in yesterday's general strike, in the biggest union action since the overthrow of communist rule in 1989, writes Virginia Marsh in Bucharest. The strike, in protest at the lack of economic reform, hit hardest in the chemical, petrochemical, metal, transport and food industries, the unions said. Harbour workers closed the Danube port of Galati and also disrupted shipping at Constanta, the main Black Sea port, Radio Bucharest reported. Health and education were also seriously affected but roads and telecommunications werre unaffected.
