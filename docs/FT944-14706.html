941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Davenport Knitwear Davenport Knitwear, the knitted fabrics and garments group, increased pre-tax profits by 41 per cent from Pounds 607,000 to Pounds 855,000 in the first half of 1994, on reduced turnover of Pounds 4.26m, against Pounds 4.47m. Earnings per share came to 33p (23.5p).
