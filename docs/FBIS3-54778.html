Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Kebich Meets With Russian `Gazprom' Leadership"] [Text] Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich has received the leadership of the Russian "Gazprom" Association. During the meeting, both parties discussed issues regarding the delivery of Russian gas to Belarus until the year 2010. Participants in the meeting signed an agreement according to which Russia will pump 16 billion cubic meters of gas to Belarus. In the future, this volume is likely to exceed the aforementioned amount by two times.
