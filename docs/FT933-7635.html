930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / People: Non-executive directors Michael Walker, chairman of Sidlaw Group, has become chairman of BRITISH INTERNATIONAL HELICOPTERS, the management buy-out from the Maxwell private group of companies, which operates helicopters in the North Sea oil and gas industry. Walker, well known in the Scottish oil industry through a subsidiary of Sidlaw, Aberdeen Service Company North Sea, takes over as chairman from David Wills, md of Brown Shipley Venture Managers, one of the investors in the mbo.
