930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Australian PM says he told Queen of plan for republic Australian prime minister Paul Keating confirmed he has told The Queen of his intention to declare his country a republic. In a surprise move, he issued a statement in Dublin, where he began an official visit, about his meeting with the Queen at Balmoral. He said he told her the Australian Government's view was that, if the Australian people approved the move at a referendum, the country would become a republic by 2001. Buckingham Palace would not comment on the discussions.
