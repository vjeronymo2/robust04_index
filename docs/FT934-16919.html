931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / People: Sturge Holdings Roger Field, Terence Pitron and Nigel Sherlock have been appointed executive directors of STURGE HOLDINGS. Field has been the active underwriter of syndicate 204 since its inception; Pitron is deputy underwriter of the combined syndicate 545/960; Sherlock is a director of Wise Speke, the group's stockbroking subsidiary.
