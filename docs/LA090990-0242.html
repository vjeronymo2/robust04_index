September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GARDENER'S CHECKLIST

For dedicated gardeners, here are suggestions from the California Assn. of Nurserymen on what to do in the garden this week:

Continue feeding chrysanthemums every three weeks until their buds show color.

Remove dried up, faded flowers and replant containers and flower beds with more annuals to continue the color show well into fall.

Because warm weather continues well into fall, be sure to maintain a regular watering schedule with deep, thorough soakings.
