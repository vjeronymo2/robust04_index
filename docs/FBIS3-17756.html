Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The American Congress has adopted an amendment to the draft budget for next year. It states that the USA will resist attempts by the Russian Federation to intimidate, to use military force, or economic pressure to create a sphere of influence in the former republics of the USSR and also in the countries of central and Eastern Europe. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Defense of Russia responded urgently to this decision today, by issuing a joint statement. It says that Russia is not setting its efforts against anyone at all and is not demanding special status or an exclusive role for itself.
