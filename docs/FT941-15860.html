940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Chichester still on red flood alert The National Rivers Authority yesterday continued to downgrade its alerts on rivers in southern England, but maintained a red alert on the river Lavant which runs through Chichester. West Sussex County Council officials, the army and fire services were draining water from the Lavant and shoring up the eastern part of the city to avoid further flooding. The Association of British Insurers yesterday set up a caravan in the city centre to advise flood victims.
