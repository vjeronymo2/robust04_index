Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report: "Joining Forces in Regional Military Cooperation"] [Text] The Kazakhstani, Uzbek, and Kyrgyz defense ministers are scheduled to meet in Bishkek 27 January, where the heads of the three states' military departments will discuss the possibility of jointly tackling military problems and the immediate prospects for cooperation. It will in particular be a question of the sides exchanging certain surplus pieces of hardware, spare parts, and other material and raw-material resources. Questions pertaining to the joint use of the former Fifth Central Aviation Cadre Refresher Training Course base will probably also be broached during the negotiating process.
