December 15, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

BLACKBURN SCORES 18, BREA POUNDS KATELLA

Tammy Blackburn had 18 points to lead Brea-Olinda High School to a 70-43 victory over Katella Thursday in a championship quarterfinal game of the Brea-Olinda girls' basketball tournament.

Aimee McDaniel had 17 points, Jinelle Williams had 13 and Jody Anton added 10 for Brea (6-0).

Karen Jensen had 16 for Katella (3-2).

In other Brea-Olinda tournament games:

Tustin 64, South Torrance 41 -- Christine Garner had 23 points and 13 rebounds to lead Tustin (6-2) in its second-round game.

Esperanza 45, Fontana 44 (OT) -- Jenifer Harney had 18 points, including the winning basket with about a minute left in overtime for Esperanza (6-0).

At the El Dorado tournament:

Canyon 44, Whittier Christian 40 -- Cori Daldey had 15 points and Sheri Rhyne had 13 to lead Canyon.

In the Santa Ana Valley tournament:

University 42, Santa Ana Valley 34 -- Denise Gandara had 23 points and 20 rebounds to lead University (5-2).

In nonleague play:

Valencia 41, Capistrano Valley 39 -- Guard Sig Lo had 14 points and Hollie Keeton 10 points and 19 rebounds to pace Valencia (1-3) at Valencia.

El Dorado 47, Sonora 41 -- Briana Yoshina had 11 points and Theresa Korte had 13 to lead El Dorado (4-1).
