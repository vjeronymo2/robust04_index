BFN [Text] A spokesman for the U.S. State Department has voiced his government's belief that Sudan harbors members of what he described as international terrorist organizations. It is well known that Western countries -- foremost among them the United States -- are the ones who harbor terrorist organizations, provide them with weapons, and allow them to train on their territory. The United States and other Western countries are the ones who abet the leaders of these terrorist organizations and send them to carry out assassinations, acts of subversion, and destabilization plots in many parts of the world.
