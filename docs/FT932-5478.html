930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / International Company News: BBV doubles stake in Probursa to 20% BANCO Bilbao Vizcaya has doubled its shareholding in the Mexican financial services group Probursa to 20 per cent, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. It has fully subscribed to a Pta6.6bn (Dollars 52.5m) capital increase, taking its total investment in Probursa to Pta14.1bn. BBV is now Probursa's largest institutional investor, and has nine seats on the 33-member board.
