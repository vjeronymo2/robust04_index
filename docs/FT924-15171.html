921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Welsh Industrial up 40% to Pounds 140,000 Net revenue of the Welsh Industrial Investment Trust improved by 40 per cent to Pounds 106,009 in the year to April 5 1992. Earnings per share increased to 7.56p (5.29p) and the dividend for the year is being stepped up from 4.5p to 4.7p.
