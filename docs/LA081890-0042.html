August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE PROBLEMS OF THE LOS ANGELES FESTIVAL

I am upset by the lack of publicity for the L.A. Festival, and particularly by director Peter Sellars' comments in an Aug. 4 article that the festival will "run by word of mouth" and that people who care will be there.

I am a person who cares! Finally, I had to call and request a brochure, which (as I write this letter) I still haven't received.

If the festival loses money due to lack of attendance, it will be difficult if not impossible to stage another event of such magnitude.

MARCIA S. ROSS

Vice President of Talent

Warner Bros. TV, Burbank
