A temporary section 165.T02&hyph;035 is added, to read as follows: §165.T02&hyph;035 Safety Zone: Upper Mississippi River. (a) Location. The Upper Mississippi River between mile 685.5 and 688.5 is established as a safety zone. (b) Effective dates. This section becomes effective on June 10, 1994 and will terminate on July 7, 1994. (c) Regulations. Under the general regulations of §165.23 of this part, entry into this zone by all downbound vessels towing cargoes is prohibited without helper boat assistance.
