EFFECTIVE DATE: June 29, 1994. [Investigation No. 731&hyph;TA&hyph;686 (Final)] Certain Steel Wire Rod From Belgium AGENCY: International Trade Commission. ACTION: Termination of investigation.

SUMMARY: On June 20, 1994, the Commission received a letter from counsel for petitioner in the subject investigation (Wiley, Rein & Fielding) withdrawing its petition. Accordingly, pursuant to §207.40(a) of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure (19 CFR 207.40(a)), the antidumping duty investigation concerning certain steel wire rod from Belgium (investigation No. 731&hyph;TA&hyph;686 (Final)) is terminated. EFFECTIVE DATE: June 29, 1994.
