930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / International Company News: Ercros sells fertiliser operation ERCROS, the bankrupt chemicals arm of the Kuwait Investment Office's Spanish investment company, Grupo Torras, has reached an agreement to sell its large and troubled fertiliser businesses to the Moroccan state-controlled phosphates group, L'Office Cherifien des Phosphates (OCP), writes Peter Bruce in Madrid. If the arrangement goes ahead, it would be one of the biggest North African industrial investments inside the European Community. The KIO is pulling out of Spain after losing Dollars 5bn in the country.
