BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] At the press center of the Azeri Ministry of Defense, INTERFAX was told of a deterioration of the situation on the Agdam and Terter sectors where according to reports from Baku the Armenian forces have gone on the offensive. In the conflict zone, exchange of prisoners continues. According to ANI agency, through the mediation of the International Red Cross seven Azeri hostages have been returned to Baku. The arrival of another plane with three hostages is expected. They will be exchanged for five Armenians captured on the territory of Azerbaijan. All the Azeri hostages are civilians who were captured about a year ago in Kelbadzhar and Agdam, ANI reports.
