Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has expressed shock at the Serb attack on the Bosnian capital, Sarajevo, which claimed 68 people and wounded over 200 others. In a statement issued in Islamabad, the Government of Pakistan urged the world community to punish those responsible and get the siege of the capital lifted. It has also asked the UN Security Council to lift the arms embargo against Bosnia to enable the Bosnians to defend themselves against the aggression. The statement says failure of the world community in taking any action would seriously undermine the credibility of the United Nations.
