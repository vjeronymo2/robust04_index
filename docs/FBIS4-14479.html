BFN [Text] Moscow, 31 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- A meeting took place in the Kremlin today between Russia's President Boris Yeltsin and State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin. This is reported by the president's press service. The two discussed a broad range of economic and political questions and ways of resolving a number of the country's urgent problems, the most important of which is to achieve mutual understanding, and future cooperation between the various political forces in Russia. The president handed Ivan Rybkin the text of the memorandum on national accord, with the understanding that this document, which is important for the fate of the country, will be comprehensively studied and examined by the deputies of the lower chamber of Parliament.
