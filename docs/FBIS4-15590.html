BFN [Text] The Abkhazian Defense Ministry told Interfax that Georgian troops had been subjecting the populated center of Upper Lata in the Kodor Cave to a second day of artillery fire. Casualties were reported among the civilian population and serious damage to several houses. First Deputy Chairman of the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet, Stanislav Lakoba, said the UN, other international organizations and Russia must respond to Georgia's gross violations of the documents recently signed in Moscow.
