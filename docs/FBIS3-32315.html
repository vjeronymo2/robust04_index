Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In the final stage of his six-day official visit to Brazil, Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage today met with Rio de Janeiro Governor Leonel Brizola and had lunch with Roberto Marinho, president of the GLOBO NEWS AGENCY. He also met with leaders of the Workers Party and Brazilian businessmen from the tobacco and energy areas. Previously Lage had met with President Itamar Franco. Tomorrow the Cuban delegation will leave for Bogota, Colombia.
