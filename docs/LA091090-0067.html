September 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Los Angeles-based Whittaker Corp said a management-led group bought its Water Management Systems business for an undisclosed price. The operation, which will be known as Yardney Water Management Systems, designs, engineers and manufactures water quality improvement systems for agriculture and industry . . . Genentech and Roche Holding completed a $2.1-billion merger that leaves Roche owning 60% of Genentech's equity.
