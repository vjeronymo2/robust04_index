931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Siebe improvement in North and South America Mr Allen Yurko, chief operating officer of Siebe (left) with Mr Barrie Stephens, chairman and chief executive, after reporting interim results. They will be the last figures presented by Mr Stephens in his joint role. At the end of the month Mr Yurko steps up to become chief executive. Mr John Prohaska is retiring from the board at the end of the year and Mr GW Sarney, president and chief operating officer of Siebe control systems, joins the main board from January 1. Mr Stephens said Siebe continued to experience an improvement in certain of its markets, namely North and South America, and to a more limited extent the UK. He said: 'The group's second-half order book is ahead of the level seen at the beginning of the financial year, which is encouraging.'
