March 24, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Rumors of marital problems aside, Amy Irving is flying in from London, where she is on a publicity tour for "Crossing Delancey," to honor husband Steven Spielberg at the Moving Picture Ball on April 1. Irving is one of a dozen stars who will take part in what the ball's organizers are mysteriously calling "an unusual journey into the world of Steven Spielberg." Other Spielberg friends to perform that night are John Candy, Sean Connery, Danny DeVito, Richard Dreyfuss, Harrison Ford, Goldie Hawn, Penny Marshall, Rhea Perlman, Patti Austin, and the man behind "Roger Rabbit's" voice, Charles Fleischer. ALEENE MacMINN
