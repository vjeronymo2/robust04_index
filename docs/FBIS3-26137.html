Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The three-day Fifth Standing Committee Meeting of the Seventh Qinghai Provincial Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] Committee wound up on the morning of 29 January. The meeting decided to hold the second session of the Seventh Qinghai Provincial CPPCC Committee in Xining on 25 February. The meeting's closing session was presided over by Bainma Dandzin, provincial CPPCC committee vice chairman. The meeting participants studied the spirit of the Fourth Standing Committee Meeting of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee and the spirit of the second plenary session of the Eighth Qinghai Provincial CPC Committee, made the above decision on convening the Second Session of the Seventh Qinghai Provincial CPPCC Committee, and adopted the following five documents: 1. The provincial CPPCC committee standing committee work report; 2. The provincial CPPCC committee's motions examination committee report on motions examination work since the first session of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee; 3. The agenda, schedule, and panels composition of the second session of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee; 4. The namelist of conveners of the second session of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee; 5. The namelist of the provincial CPPCC committee standing committee by-election candidates. At the end of the closing session, provincial CPPCC committee Chairman Han Yingxuan delivered a speech in which he spoke of the provincial CPPCC work in 1994, saying: The provincial CPPCC committee should implement to the letter arrangements made by the provincial party committee, closely adhere to economic construction as the center, unite all forces that can be united; mobilize all positive factors,; vigorously build socialist market economic structure, socialist democracy, socialist legal system, and socialist spiritual civilization, and contribute more to the province's political stability, reform, opening up, economic development, and social progress. Speaking of the provincial CPPCC committee's major tasks in 1994, Han Yingxuan stated: We should continually and profoundly study Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, make a success of all CPPCC meetings, bring into full play the CPPCC's democratic consultation role, conscientiously carry out investigation and study, promote fast-paced reform, opening up, and economic construction, and further develop functions of all specialized CPPCC committees. Han Yingxuan maintained: The CPPCC organizations have played a unique role in promoting solidarity among all nationalities and in safeguarding social stability as well. We should take consolidating solidarity and safeguarding social stability as one of the major tasks of the CPPCC organizations at all levels. The standing committee meeting was attended by Song Bu, Gu Jiasai, Han Shenggui, Zhaxi Anjia, Bajia Lesangcidai, Tan Zhengyun, Li Xihong, and Ma Dingxiao, provincial CPPCC committee vice chairmen; Cai Dan, provincial people's congress standing committee vice chairman; and CPPCC national committee member Ga Bulong.
