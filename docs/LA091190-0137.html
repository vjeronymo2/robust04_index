September 11, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN JUAN CAPISTRANO;

TRI-CITY RECYCLING PROGRAM PLANNED

Eager to ease the flow of trash into overflowing landfills and meet a looming state limit on waste disposal, three South County cities are gearing up to begin curbside recycling programs in their communities.

Flanked by brand-new recycling trucks, the mayors of San Juan Capistrano, San Clemente and Dana Point will unfurl plans for the recycling effort during a ceremony at San Juan Capistrano City Hall today at 2 p.m.

The three cities are launching the recycling programs in an effort to meet a new state law that requires all municipalities to reduce solid waste levels by 1995. Cities that do not comply with the state mandate could be hit with substantial fines. FRANK MESSINA
