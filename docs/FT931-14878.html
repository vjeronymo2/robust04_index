930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Judge revokes Santander bond for possible fraud AN INVESTIGATING judge at Spain's senior monetary court has revoked an order forcing Banco Santander to post a Pta8bn (Dollars 70.7m) bond as surety against possible fraud charges. Judge Miguel Moreiras, who ordered the bond on December 23, upheld an appeal by the bank against the surety but said he would continue investigating an alleged tax avoidance loan assignment instrument promoted by Santander between 1986 and 1989.
