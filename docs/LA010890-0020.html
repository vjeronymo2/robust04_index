January 8, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $100 when both of his horses in a $30 parlay, Go For Broadway in the first and What Has Been in the seventh, ran out and Beaufort, on which he wagered $70 to win, finished second in the sixth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,057.
