Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 30 Dec (TANJUG) -- It was announced in Skopje today that the Macedonian Minister for Foreign Relations Stevo Crvenkovski, and Henrik Kurc, the charge d'affaires of the Dutch Embassy in the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, exchanged diplomatic notes on the establishing of diplomatic ties between Macedonia and the Netherlands. It was also indicated today that a Polish Embassy would soon be opened in Skopje.
