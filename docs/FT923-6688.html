920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: London Finance & Investment Group Net asset value of London Finance & Investment Group was 19.77p a share at June 30, down from 31.17p a year earlier and 20.84p at the December 31 year end. The share of losses from associates at the interim stage was Pounds 100,000, against Pounds 22,000 in the comparable period. This left pre-tax profits for the half year down from Pounds 290,000 to Pounds 120,000. After tax and extraordinaries the attributable loss was reduced to Pounds 99,000 (Pounds 1.17m). Earnings per share came through at 0.24p (0.47p).
