940406 FT 06 APR 94 / International Company News: Rhone-Poulenc increases stake Rhone-Poulenc, the French chemicals and drug group said yesterday that it held more than 85 per cent of the shares of Co-operation Pharmaceutique Francaise (Cooper) after launching a friendly takeover bid for the company, AP-DJ reports from Paris. Last February, Rhone-Poulenc said it would offer 18 of its own shares or FFr2,400 (Dollars 406m) cash for every Cooper share.
