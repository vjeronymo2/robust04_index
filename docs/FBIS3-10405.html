Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Capsules" column] [Text] There is great discontent over the Clinton administration's appointment of Myles Frenchette as the new U.S. ambassador to Colombia. Frenchette was responsible for the failure of the Coffee Pact, which harmed the country greatly. In addition, the Brazilian Government did not accept him as ambassador. Despite press reports, the Foreign Ministry has denied the placet was given to this enemy of the world's coffee growers.
