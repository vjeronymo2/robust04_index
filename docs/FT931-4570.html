930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / International Capital Markets: CIBC offer raises CDollars 407m CANADIAN Imperial Bank of Commerce is raising CDollars 407m in new equity through a public offer of common shares at CDollars 27.15 a share, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The proceeds will be used to bolster tier I capital, providing a ratio of 6.7 per cent. The issue increases its capital ratio to 9.5 per cent after two preferred stock offerings raised CDollars 400m.
