Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Excerpts] Moscow, 16 Jan -- "I am forced to decline an offer from the chairman of the Government, and I am not accepting the post of first vice premier." This was stated by Yegor Gaydar at a meeting with journalists today. He added that he cannot be in the government and in opposition to it at the same time. [passage omitted] At the same time, Yegor Gaydar spoke out for continuing to work with the Government. Touching upon the question of personalities, the leader of the "Russia's Choice" also spoke out for joining Grigoriy Yavlinskiy's government [as received]. [Moscow ITAR-TASS in English at 1214 GMT on 16 January, in a similar item, renders this sentence as: "Gaydar came out for Grigoriy Yavlinskiy to join the government."] The leader of the "Russia's Choice" told journalists that he spoke with Boris Yeltsin on 13 January before making his decision not to join the new Government. Yegor Gaydar said that he intends to "concentrate his attention on work in the State Duma." He noted that in this connection "Russia's Choice" intends to do everything possible not to allow representatives of the Liberal Democratic Party to join the key committees of the lower chamber -- that is the committees for defense, security, and international policy.
