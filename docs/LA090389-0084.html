September 3, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

QUAYLE'S VISIT

Thanks to your Aug. 25 article on Dan Quayle's Orange County visit, I have a much clearer understanding of what it takes to become vice president.

Your quote from reception guest Ari Babaknia says it all: "He's a very good-looking guy. . . . And he's not dumb."

Illuminated again!

SUSAN MENNING

Costa Mesa
