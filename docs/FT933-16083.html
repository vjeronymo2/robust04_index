930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Opt-out schools inspection pledge OPTED-OUT schools will not have to face inspections from hostile school inspectors, Baroness Blatch, the education minister, said yesterday. Most contracts to inspect schools, which have been opened to tender for the next academic year onwards, have been won by local education authority inspection teams. Opening a conference organised by the Centre for Policy Studies, Baroness Blatch said: 'I do have to say that there's a concern where local authorities have been so thoroughly hostile, for example to grant-maintained schools, and where they may win the bid to be registered inspectors.'
