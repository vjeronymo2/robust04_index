BFN [Text] Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene is now an official candidate for the presidency of the European Commission to succeed Jacques Delors. The announcement was made a moment ago at the end of today's cabinet meeting. (Philippe Valcoviaque), can you tell us how the news was announced? [Valcoviaque] It is now official: The government is backing Jean-Luc Dehaene to succeed Jacques Delors as president of the European Commission. This was announced at the end of today's cabinet meeting. The government also said it hoped a consensus could be found in support of Dehaene's candidacy at the Corfu European summit next week. After weeks of uncertainty, Jean-Luc Dehaene and the Belgian Government have broken their silence. Jean-Luc Dehaene himself remained cautious. He hoped the decision would be made in Corfu. It would be good for Europe, he said, if things did not drag on too long. As to his possible successor as Belgian prime minister, Jean-Luc Dehaene said there was no reason to worry about the problem until the situation came up.
