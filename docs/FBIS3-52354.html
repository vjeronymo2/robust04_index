Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 1 Jan (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has sent a congratulatory cable to his brother Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan al-Bashir, president of the Republic of Sudan, on the national day of sisterly Sudan. The cable reads: On the national day of the sisterly Republic of Sudan, I am pleased to extend to Your Excellency my heartfelt greetings and brotherly feelings, wishing Your Excellency good health and happiness and wishing the fraternal Sudanese people continuous progress, strength, and prosperity. I hope that fraternal relations between both our people and our sisterly countries will witness further development and growth for the wellbeing of our glorious Arab nation. Peace be upon you.
