BFN [Text] Ivana Anyzova reports on today's talks between Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus and Albanian Prime Minister Aleksander Meksi. [Anyzova] The two prime ministers signed an agreement on trade and technical relations and one on the support and mutual protection of investments. They exchanged information about the situation in their countries, foreign policy initiatives, and bilateral relations. They expressed a wish to develop mutual contacts in science, culture, and tourism. They also agreed that the Czech side will grant Albania a long-term loan to purchase farming and food-processing equipment. Although economic cooperation has receded over recent years, our side regards as advantageous the fact that Czech goods have been offered to Albanian agriculture and the food-processing industry. Vaclav Klaus emphasized that such cooperation is a useful form of policy aimed at boosting exports.
