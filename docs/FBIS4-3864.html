BFN [Text] United Nations, April 23 (XINHUA) -- U.N. Secretary- General Butrus Butrus-Ghali today expressed his regret of the death of former U.S. President Richard Nixon, according to a U.N. spokesman. Nixon, the 37th president of the United States, died of stroke on Friday [22 April], at the age of 81. The spokesman said "the secretary-general had known Mr. Nixon for many years. The former President's impact in the field of international relations was especially significant." The secretary-general has extended his condolences to the Nixon family, as well as to the government and the people of the United States, he said.
