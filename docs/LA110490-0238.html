November 4, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL

Camp Kilpatrick 20, Hamilton 0 -- Michael Black rushed for 74 yards in 15 carries and Bobby Wilson gained 76 yards in eight carries and scored on an 11-yard run to lead the Mustangs in a Freedom League game at Hamilton.

EIGHT-MAN

L. A. Lutheran 25, Pilgrim 22 -- Senior quarterback Darren Brown completed 12 of 25 passes for 170 yards and rushed for 61 yards and two touchdowns to lead L. A. Lutheran (6-3, 5-2) in a Heritage League game at Lutheran.
