BFN [Text] Information Minister Harmoko has stated that President Suharto will not issue a presidential decree on foreign investment in mass media although such investment is permitted under Government Regulation No. 20/1994. Speaking to reporters after meeting the president at Merdeka Palace this afternoon, the information minister urged the general public to stop making a fuss over the matter because the president's policy is clear. Harmoko subsequently urged the general public to stop questioning Government Regulation No. 20, particularly the clause on possible foreign investment in mass media through joint ventures. He also informed the head of state of the indefinite postponement of the Islamic Conference Organization information ministers conference.
