BFN [Text] In a written statement today, President Glavkos Kliridhis stressed that the signing of the Israeli-PLO agreement has already gone down in history as a courageous and significant step toward peace that will have a positive effect on the Middle East and the rest of the world. In his statement, Kliridhis hails the international community's interest in the Middle East issue and appeals for greater support by the international community, particularly the Security Council, to resolve the Cyprus issue. Kliridhis said: If the Turkish side remains a prisoner of the past, then the Cyprus issue will remain a source of disputes and danger that could poison the atmosphere in the entire region.
