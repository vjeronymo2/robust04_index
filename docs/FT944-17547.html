941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Morgan Grenfell Morgan Grenfell Latin American Companies Trust which came to the market in February, had a net asset value of 111.2p per share at August 31. Fully diluted, the value was 109.3p. For the period from February 21 to August 31 net available revenue amounted to Pounds 238,000 for earnings per share of 0.37p.
