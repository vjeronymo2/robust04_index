930402 FT 02 APR 93 / World Trade News: Swedish group signs new telecoms deal with China ERICSSON, the Swedish telecommunications group, yesterday signed a Dollars 300m contract for AXE switching equipment with China's Guangdong Post and Telecommunications Bureau, its biggest ever contract in one of its fastest growing markets. The accord formalises a framework agreement reached last June. The deal covers AXE-10 switching equipment, transmission and cooling equipment which will be delivered from Sweden between 1993 and 1995. Last year Ericsson signed framework agreements and memorandums of understanding for more than Dollars 900m with China, which is now its eighth largest market.
