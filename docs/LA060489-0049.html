June 4, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SHARING CREDITS

Leon Whiteson's recent story ("Chairs on Exhibit: The Bottom Line," May 15) kindly described me as the designer of the Evolutionary Chair, which was part of the recent "Chair as Art" display at the Gallery of Art on Main Street in Santa Monica. To be fair, my colleagues at Gwynne Pugh and Associates -- Gwynne Pugh and James Dove -- also should receive the credit due them for their work on that piece.

LARRY SCARPA

Santa Monica
