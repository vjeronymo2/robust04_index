Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cizre, (AA) -- A group of terrorists have opened fire in Sirnak's Cizre District. One police officer was martyred and four others wounded with one terrorist getting killed. According to information received from the State of Emergency Region Governor's Office, a group of terrorists opened fire from the mountainous area at the public buildings situated at the district center. One police officer was martyred and four wounded during clashes which erupted when the security forces returned fire at the terrorists. One terrorist was killed during the clash. It was reported that operations are under way in the region to capture the escaped terrorists.
