921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / Observer: Stitch in time . . . Whitehall's Home Office, scarcely known for its generosity with information on anything whatsoever, seems peculiarly anxious that the FT should not be left in the dark. One correspondent alone has been sent no fewer than 13 press releases reminding him that Britain's clocks are to be put back an hour at 2am on Sunday.
