941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo closed off 2.1 per cent in volatile trade as short and long investors arm-wrestled ahead of the futures index and options markets settlements tomorrow and on October 17. The Bovespa index of the 55 most-active shares ended down 1,061 at 49,260 points. Turnover was a high RDollars 588.4m (Dollars 705.6m) due to a surge of options deals. The amount of open interest Bovespa futures index contracts was high prior to the settlement. Brazilian markets will be closed today.
