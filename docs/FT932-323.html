930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Bolivian oil plan agreed PAN ANDEAN Resources, the oil and gas exploration company, has agreed a joint venture with Australia's BHP to explore for oil in central Bolivia. BHP will finance a Dollars 6m seismic programme to gather geological information on the area. Pan Andean was set up in 1988 to exploit resource opportunities in South America's the Andean region.
