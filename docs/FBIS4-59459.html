BFN [Text] Salih Abu-Bakr Bin Husaynun, Yemeni oil minister and special envoy of Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, arrived in Damascus today accompanied by a delegation. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Dr. Nadir Nabulsi, minister of Oil and mineral resources, senior officials from the Foreign Ministry and the Oil and Mineral Resources Ministry, and the Yemeni ambassador in Damascus and the embassy staff. In a statement to SANA, Bin-Husaynun said the aim of the visit is to hand a letter to President Hafiz al-Asad from his brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Yemeni vice president and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, dealing with Yemeni developments especially after the outbreak of war in Yemen. Bin-Husaynun praised Syria's stands and its concern for Yemen. He said: We are aware that Syria is pained by the ongoing fighting between Yemeni brothers. It stands by Yemen in this affliction. This is not a novelty, because Syria has always shared the pain and always stood by any Arab state facing threats.
