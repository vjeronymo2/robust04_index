930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Ingham set to buy Moss Europe Ingham, the worsted spinner, has signed a letter of intent which may or may not lead to its acquisition of Moss Europe, a private UK company, for Pounds 4m in shares. Moss Europe is a subsidiary of California-based Milard, a wholesaler and retailer of new car parts for British classic sports cars.
