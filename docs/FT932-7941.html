930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / Peres visits the Great Wall of China Shimon Peres, Israeli foreign minister, visits the Great Wall of China yesterday. The two countries established diplomatic relations in January 1992. Diplomats said Beijing promised Israel it would not sell missiles to Iran or Syria as it did not want to put obstacles in the way of Middle East peace. China sold Silkworm missiles to Iran during the 1980s, according to western military observers.
