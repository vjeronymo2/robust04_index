931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Lloyd's trust offer withdrawn Corporate Membership, an investment trust designed to exploit the introduction of corporate capital to the Lloyd's of London insurance market, has withdrawn its subscription offer following lack of interest. The subscription fell substantially short of the 25 per cent required for it to succeed. Corporate Membership sought Pounds 8m-Pounds 20m to invest in Lloyd's syndicates but raised less than Pounds 2.5m.
