920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables English strawberries are plentiful this week and are reasonably priced at 90p-Pounds 1.20 reports the FFVIB. Other good buys include New Zealand kiwifruit at 20-25p each, Spania apricots at 75p-Pounds 1.10 a lb and Spania and Italian peaches at 20-30p each. Broad beans are this weeks star vegetable buy priced at 30-35p a lb. Jersey royals and English new potatoes at 15-20p a lb. English and French carrots are at 28-35p a lb, English peas at 45-55p alb and homegrown cauliflower at 40-55p a head. English Iceberg lettuce is superb this week at 55-65p each, other good lettuce buys include Round lettuce at 25-35p each.
