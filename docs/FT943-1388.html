940924 FT 24 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Mining provision hits Europe Energy A provision of Pounds 679,884 against the permanent diminution of its mining assets left Europe Energy Group with pre-tax losses up from Pounds 132,817 to Pounds 951,968 for the year ended March 31. New management took control in January. Changes have been made to address the difficulties of the mining operations and the board is confident the problems have been contained. The motor dealership acquired earlier this year was profitable and had a successful August. Turnover, including the acquisition, rose to Pounds 2.64m (Pounds 2.12m). Losses per share came to 2.96p (0.45p).
