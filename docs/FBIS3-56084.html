Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] The rumors about the blockade of the roads leading to the city of Dushanbe by Regarskiy and Gissarskiy fighting groups have been confirmed. According to the report by Sharif Rrahmonpur, a KHORASAN news agency correspondent in Dushanbe city, the confrontation between the government and Safarali Kenjayev, the former parliament speaker, who is at the head of Regarskiy and Gissarskiy formations, is growing day by day. The blockade of the roads leading to the capital and clashes between these formations and Kulyab fighters are evidence of that. The correspondent reports that a clash took place between the fighting sides near the village of Almaasi of Gissarskiy Rayon on Monday. According to available information, the fighters of Regarskiy and Gissarskiy demand the resignation of Abdulmajid Dostiyev [first deputy chairman of the Supreme Council], Yokubjon Salimov [internal affairs minister], and [Saydamir] Zukhurov [National Security Committee chairman], the representatives of Kulyab Oblast, and restoration of Safarali Kenjayev to his former post [Supreme Council chairman].
