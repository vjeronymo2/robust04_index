920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Capital Markets: Laurentian Bank advances 10% LAURENTIAN BANK, the banking arm of the Laurentian financial services group, posted a 10 per cent gain in third-quarter profit to CDollars 10.1m (USDollars 8.4m), or 53 cents a share, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Nine months' profit was CDollars 28.9m, or CDollars 1.53, against CDollars 25.4m, or CDollars 1.52, on fewer shares outstanding.
