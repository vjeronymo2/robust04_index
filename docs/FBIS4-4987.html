BFN [From MAINICHI SHIMBUN 5 April Evening Edition] [Text] At an 5 April news conference, Defense Agency Director General Kazuo Aichi commented on the situation on the Korean peninsula, which has become tense over the DPRK's (North Korea) suspected nuclear development, stating: "There is an increasing possibility that the worst will occur. We should be more strained and should be ready." He thus indicated that the situation could become more serious depending on North Korea's future actions.
