August 30, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

CENCOR PURCHASES WRITERS INC.

CenCor Inc. said today it has purchased Writers Inc., a California-based temporary services company, for an undisclosed amount.

Writers Inc. reported revenues of $2.6 million for the first half of 1989.

The company consults with and places technical writers and editors. Most clients are in high-tech industries, banking and public utilities.

CenCor Inc.'s operations include consumer finance, temporary employment services and equipment leasing.
