Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] During a samba parade in Juiz de Fora, Minas Gerais, on the evening of 14 February, President Itamar Franco spoke for the first time about the incident with model Lilian Ramos at Rio de Janeiro Sambodromo [Carnival parade ground]. [passage omitted] He said: [Begin recording] I want my right to privacy as president and citizen to be respected. There was nothing wrong with what happened in Rio de Janeiro. It was just an outward show of merriment, a sign of esteem for Rio de Janeiro Carnival. I was invited by the chairman of the samba schools association and I attended the parade in my capacity as president of the Republic. And yet, I also attended it as a human being, as a citizen. Therefore, I do not see that I have done anything wrong. [end recording] [passage omitted] He added: [Begin recording] For a year and a half we have been grappling with inflation, abject poverty, squalor. I would like to underscore that Finance Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso has fulfilled his mission as a true patriot. Now, if he decides to run for the presidency, he will undoubtedly have my support. [end recording].
