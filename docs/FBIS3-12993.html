Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An Islamic Jihad cell, which tried to obtain weapons, among other things, was uncovered in northern Israel, our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports. He says the cell was uncovered by the Shin Bet and the Bureau for Special Tasks in Ha'amaqim District. Cell members come from Janin and Umm al-Fahm. An Umm al-Fahm resident assisted the cell members in forming ties with arms dealers in the Israeli underworld and obtaining information on Israel. The cell leaders were arrested and their interrogation is continuing.
