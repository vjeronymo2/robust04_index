BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and his brother, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the 1 September revolution in Libya, have spoken on the telephone. During the conversation, the two leaders discussed current developments in our country in light of the crimes committed against the homeland by the rebellious gang within the leadership of the Socialist Party, by igniting war between the sons of the Yemeni homeland and al-Bid's secessionist declaration. Brother Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi reiterated the support of the Libyan leadership, people, and government for Yemeni unity. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council expressed appreciation for the principled position of Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi and the Libyan Arab people in support of their brothers in Yemen, as well as their concern about Yemeni unity as a national and pan-Arab gain for the entire Arab nation.
