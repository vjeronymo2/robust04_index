920206 FT 06 FEB 92 / International Company News: Waste group in Dollars 1bn flotation WASTE Management, the large US waste disposal group which is planning to float shares in its international subsidiary on the UK stock market, has announced that it filed a 'shelf' registration with the US Securities and Exchange Commission for offerings of up to Dollars 1bn in unsecured debt securities, writes Nikki Tait. It said yesterday that the proceeds would be used to retire commercial paper and other debt, and 'for general corporate purposes, including acquisitions'.
