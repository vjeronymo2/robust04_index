BFN [Excerpt] Kiev 7 Apr -- The press service of Ukraine's president and Cabinet of Ministers has reported that on 7 April Valeriy Shmarov, Ukraine's deputy prime minister for the military-industrial complex received Polish Defense Minister Piotr Kolodziejdzyk who is on the second day of an official visit to Ukraine. A wide range of issues of mutual interest were discussed. Anatoliy Lopata, chief of the general staff of Ukraine's Armed Forces took part in the talks from the Ukrainian side, and Jerzy Kozakiewicz, the Polish ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Ukraine, took part from the Polish side.
