April 13, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TONIGHT: They appeared in a handful of '60s beach movies but never toured together during their heyday as clean-cut teen idols.

Now Frankie and Annette (Avalon and Funicello) kick off a 35-city tour at Knott's Berry Farm (F24). Times: 5 and 8 p.m. today and Saturday. Tickets: included with park admission of $15 to $21. Information: (714) 827-1776. Dave Lesher and Mark I. Pinsky
