BFN [Text] New York, May 17, INA--Iraqi Deputy Premier Mr. Tariq 'Aziz today held a series of meetings with the ambassadors of Oman, Djibouti, Pakistan, Nigeria and Rwanda, now members of the Security Council. Mr. 'Aziz also met the ambassadors of New Zealand, Argentina, Spain and Czech [as received]. The Security Council is scheduled to conduct its consultations over the attitude toward the embargo imposed on Iraq today, Tuesday.
