920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG gold shares were firm with the JSE all-gold index rising 29 to 1,275. Vaal Reefs was up R6 to R225. The industrial index gained 6 to 4,408 with the all-share up 1 to 3,606. Trading was cautious awaiting foreign reaction to tax proposals on investments.
