940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: TT/Dale TT Group yesterday sent its offer document to shareholders of Dale Electric International. The recommended offer, of 70.25p cash per share, values the North Yorkshire-based generator manufacturer at Pounds 16m and represents a premium of 17 per cent over the Dale share price prior to the announcement. There is also a 1-for-5 share alternative. TT owns or has agreed to acquire 3.61m Dale shares, representing about 15.8 per cent. Including acceptances, it has 19.3 per cent.
