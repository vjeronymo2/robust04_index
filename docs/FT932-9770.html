930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Gold Crown in Pounds 16m buy-out Gold Crown Foods, a tea and coffee packer, has been acquired by a management buy-out team backed by 3i and Henderson Venture Managers. It is the second MBO at the company in six years. Total funding raised for the buy-out was over Pounds 16m. Originally part of Argyll Group, Gold Crown was bought by its management in 1987. This second MBO enables three of the four original buy-out team and its backers to sell out.
