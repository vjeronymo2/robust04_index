CSO [Unattributed article: "Hashish Hidden in the Bumpers"] [Text] A 41-year-old Norwegian businessman, living in Copenhagen, has been taken into custody for four weeks for attempting to smuggle in 42 kilos of hashish from the Netherlands. The man was detected Saturday evening in Rodby Havn, when the special police and customs officials, with the help of a narcotics dog, found the hashish hidden in the bumpers of the Norwegian's car.
