Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Israeli forces continue to besiege the townships of Rumaysh, 'Ayta al-Sha'b, and 'Aramta. Our correspondent reports that enemy forces bombarded the approaches to al-Nabatiyah and Kafr Tibnit with mortars. Meanwhile, an Amal Movement group attacked enemy positions in Hula, using rockets and medium machine guns. A clash ensued for 10 minutes between the group and the enemy troops. Enemy helicopters flew over the scene of clashes. Another group attacked the occupation position in al-Shumariyah, inflicting heavy material losses on the position. Immediately afterward, the enemy forces shelled Zawtar al-Sharqiyah and Zawtar al-Gharbiyah.
