Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (INA) -- Iraq has informed the general secretariat of the Arab League that Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers continue to make base facilities available to American, British, and French aircraft to violate Iraqi airspace. A letter from Iraq's permanent Arab League delegate to its secretary general also points out that the action poses a serious threat to Iraq's sovereignty and national unity. Last Friday, Saturday, and Sunday [21, 22, 23 January], these aircraft mounted 148 sorties, having taken off from Saudi and Kuwaiti bases. Iraq's delegate asked that the letter be circulated among the League members.
