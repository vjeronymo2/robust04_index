940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Dencora higher at halfway A sharp rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 370,000 to Pounds 905,000 was announced by Dencora, the property group, for the six months to June 30. Turnover grew to Pounds 13.3m (Pounds 10.4m) with rental income slightly down at Pounds 4.44m (Pounds 4.57m) reflecting the sale of two investment properties and other commercial property held for trading purposes. Mr John Bushell, the chairman, said the lettings market had continued to improve. Net interest payable was Pounds 2.73m (Pounds 3.02m). Earnings amounted to 2.3p (0.6p).
