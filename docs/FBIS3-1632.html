Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On 26 February at the forum cosponsored by the provincial party committee and the provincial government, Li Wenquan, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, set forth that the party organizations at various levels, the party- member cadres, and the broad masses of the people across the province should go all out to safeguard political and social stability and ensure the smooth progress of reform, opening up, and modernization. Li Wenquan said in his speech: In the preceding stage, the province had a situation characterized by political stability, social stability, economic development, and market prosperity, and in which the people lived and worked in peace and contentment. However, we must notice that there are still factors affecting social and political stability and the tasks for keeping stability are still arduous. The party and government leading comrades at various levels should approach from the high plane of the overall situation and from the high political plane to grasp and well handle the relationship among reform, development, and stability; go all out to safeguard the stability of the overall situation; and never, in the slightest degree, be vague on or shake this matter of principle. At present, we should emphatically grasp the basic work of the grass roots, the improvement of administrative honesty, the improvement of ideology and work style, and the setup of legal system to ensure and promote the smooth progress of reform, opening up, and modernization. Sun Shuyi, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and secretary of the provincial commission of political science and law; and Wang Yuxi, vice governor of the provincial government, attended and addressed the forum.
