930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 24.5m housing projects for Lovell The partnerships division of the LOVELL GROUP has won a series of contracts across England and Wales in partnership with local authorities and housing associations worth Pounds 24.5m. The projects will provide 768 units of affordable social housing for rent, shared ownership and sale to local authority and housing association tenants and those on waiting lists. The largest group of work for a single client is in south Wales for SHAW Home Care Housing Association where the partnership in six schemes worth Pounds 5.88m will produce a combination of 225 family houses, flats and bedsitting rooms for elderly people at Caerphilly, Pontypool, Newport, Brackla, Port Talbot and Bridgend. In the west and south west 42 new and refurbished flats are being constructed at Plymouth with Cornwall Housing Association at a cost of Pounds 2.59m; in Wiltshire, 10 dwellings at Castle Coombe for English Villages Housing Association (Pounds 396,000) and 25 homes at Amesbury for Shaftesbury Society Housing Association (Pounds 941,000); and at Kemble 18 units valued at Pounds 700,000 will be built in collaboration with Gloucestershire Housing Association. In the Midlands, Lovell Partnerships is also working on a Pounds 1.12m project with the Gloucestershire Housing Association at Cheltenham to provide 39 units; at Uttoxeter 51 units will be built in a four-way partnership and 16 bungalows will be constructed at Burton-upon-Trent.
