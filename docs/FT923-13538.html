920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / Police investigate share dealing POLICE are investigating alleged illegal share dealing in recently privatised Scottish Power and Hydroelectric stock. A spokesman for Lothian and Borders police said: 'We have recently taken possession of documents from Price Waterhouse (the accountants) and in the near future we will be consulting with the Crown Office when an appropriate course of action will be decided on.' It is understood that the police investigation concerned multiple share applications.
