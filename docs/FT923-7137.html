920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / International Company News: Repsol plans flotation REPSOL, the Spanish state-run oil and gas conglomerate, will float up to Pta100bn (Dollars 1.07bn) in shares at the end of the year, in an offer aimed at institutional investors, Mr Oscar Fanjul, chairman, said yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. Repsol will float between Pta50bn and Pta100bn in shares.
