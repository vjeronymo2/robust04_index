February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

New Helmsley Ads to be Queen-Less: Leona Helmsley is abdicating her three-year reign in advertising as a tiara-wearing hotel queen, her new ad agency, Smith-Greenland Inc., announced. A new advertising campaign in April for the New York Helmsley hotels will no longer feature Mrs. Helmsley, recently convicted of federal tax fraud.
