Language: English Article Type:BFN [by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow February 15 TASS -- Russia and Germany are in different stages of their internal development, but this does not lessen the importance of constructive, equal and mutually- advantageous relations between them, also along the military- political lines. This idea was emphasised during an interview with ITAR-TASS on Tuesday by First Russian Deputy Defence Minister Andrey Kokoshin after his meeting with Bundeswehr's Inspector-General Klaus Naumann. Problems of military-political cooperation and armed forces development were discussed during the dialogue. Special attention was given to the shaping of the military budget and the financing of research. The sides expressed the confidence that Russo-German relations are of huge stabilising importance not only for Europe but also for other regions. Klaus Naumann will meet on Tuesday with Colonel-General Viktor Samsonov, chief of staff for coordination of military cooperation of the CIS country. During the visit to last till February 18, Klaus Naumann will address listeners and teachers of the academy of the general staff and will visit the 27th Guards Division of the Russian peacemaking forces in the Volga Military District. He will also visit Volgograd and St. Petersburg.
