Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Indonesian Foreign Minister brother Ali Alatas and his delegation arrived in Sanaa last night on a short visit to our country. During the visit, he will meet with brother officials at the Foreign Ministry and the Planning and Development Ministry, as well as senior state officials. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA, Alatas said that he and his delegation are pleased to visit our country. He indicated the deep-rooted, historical relations between the Yemeni and Indonesian peoples, particularly [words indistinct] the tolerant Islamic faith. He said that many Indonesian citizens are descended from Yemeni origins, and that he takes pride in his Yemeni roots. He was welcomed by Foreign Minister brother Muhammad Salim Basindawa, a number of brother officials, and the Indonesian ambassador and embassy staff.
