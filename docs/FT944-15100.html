941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Heseltine's peace mission Mr Michael Heseltine, UK trade and industry secretary, yesterday proposed that Malaysian and British companies work together to exploit opportunities in Europe and in the Asia Pacific region. 'There is great scope if you seize the opportunity to use the UK as a springboard into Europe,' said Mr Heseltine, the first British minister to visit Malaysia since it lifted a ban on doing business with British companies in September. In February Dr Mahathir Mohamad, the Malaysian prime minister, banned British companies from government contracts in retaliation for British press reports which hinted at corruption among senior figures in Malaysia.
