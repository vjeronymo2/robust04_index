BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow, 24 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Douglas Hurd, British secretary of state for foreign and Commonwealth affairs, discussed mainly economic issues at a meeting this morning. Viktor Chernomyrdin informed the British minister about the package of decrees on stepping up structural transformations in the Russian economy, which were prepared by the government and signed by President Boris Yeltsin Monday. They are designed, in particular, to optimize the government's taxation policy and attract foreign and Russian capital investments. Douglas Hurd, for his part, noted that after meeting with British businessmen in Moscow he arrived at the conclusion that business people in his country take an extremely optimistic view with regard to the prospects for economic cooperation between Russia and Britain. Douglas Hurd confirmed the invitation given by British Prime Minister John Major to Viktor Chernomyrdin to visit London officially at the end of 1994 or the beginning of 1995.
