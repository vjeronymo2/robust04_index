920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / Germans sentenced for Iraq deal Three German steel executives were yesterday given suspended sentences for illegally exporting rocket engine parts to Iraq before the 1991 Gulf War, Reuter reports from Bochum. The three, from subsidiaries of German steel and engineering giant Thyssen, were convicted of knowingly selling Iraq turbo-charged pumps declared as oil and water drilling equipment but intended for military purposes.
