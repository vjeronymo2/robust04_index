920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables English Victoria plums are this week's best fruit buy at 50-65p a lb, reports the FFVIB. French Guyot pears at 30-45p a lb (35-40p) remain plentiful, along with nectarines at 15-20p each (15-20p). English Discovery apples are also superb value at 38-48p a lb (35-50p). Runner beans at 50-65p a lb (70-80p) and courgettes at 40-50p a lb (40-55p) are excellent quality buys, while Corn-on-the-cob remains abundant at 25-35p per cob (30-40p). Tomatoes from the UK, Holland and Channel Islands continue to be superb quality at 30-40p a lb (30-45p). With Cucumbers at 40-50p each (40-50p), rounding up this week's best buys.
