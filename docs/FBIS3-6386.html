Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Human resources development is the main topic to be proposed by Thailand in the upcoming informal meeting of the finance ministers of Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation, or APEC, countries. Foreign Minister Prasong says the meeting -- to be held in Honolulu, Hawaii, during 18-19 March -- will be regarded as a consultation, not a negotiation. Finance Minister Tharin Nimmanhemin will represent Thailand at the meeting. [passage omitted] Minister Prasong says the United States has proposed the setting up of a business convention center in Asia and the Pacific, if APEC countries will select two business people to attend the business conference as proposed by the United States. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs has entrusted the Board of Trade of Thailand and the Federation of Thai Industries to select qualified persons to take part in the business conference.
