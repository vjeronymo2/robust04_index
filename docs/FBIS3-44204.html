Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President of the Republic Jose Eduardo dos Santos this afternoon received a message from Brazilian President Itamar Franco. Ambassador Jose Parecido de Oliveira went to Futungo de Belas to deliver the message from the Brazilian head of state. The message notes the need and importance of creating the community of Lusophone states and also deals with issues linked to cooperation between Brazil and Angola. Ambassador Jose Parecido Oliveira told the president of the Republic that Angolans should end the absurd war that is ravaging the country and its people.
