941015 FT 15 OCT 94 / International Company News: K-C regroups assets Kimberly-Clark, the Dallas-based international tissue products group, is putting all its North American pulp and paper assets into a new company, Ontala Forest Products. It will sell all the equity through an international IPO to raise around CDollars 900m (USDollars 668.2m). Ontala's production assets will include K-C's northern Ontario pulp mill and the Coosa Pines pulp and newsprint mill in Alabama. Reports by Tony Jackson (New York), Christopher Brown-Humes (Stockholm) and Robert Gibbens (Montreal)
