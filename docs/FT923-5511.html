920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / UK Company News: New Frontiers sells Swire venture share New Frontiers Development Trust of Edinburgh has sold its 40 per cent stake in Swire Aviation to Swire Pacific, its partner in the joint venture, for HKDollars 130.6m. Swire Aviation's principle activity is Hong Kong Air Cargo Terminal Ltd. The sale lifted New Frontiers' net asset value by 2.5p to 55.8p
