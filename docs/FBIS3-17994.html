Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Elections to the so-called supreme council of the South Ossetian Republic ended today in Tskhinvali (Samachablo). According to the staff of head of state's office, this action is illegal and unlawful and is aimed at tearing away Samachablo-Shida Kartli from the Republic of Georgia. It has become known that the population of Samachablo's Georgian villages --the territory that is being controlled by the Ossetian side -- were forced to participate in the elections.
