Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Yuriy Fadeyev, ambassador of the Russian Federation, arranged a banquet on the evening of 17 March at the Embassy to commemorate the 45th anniversary of the signing of the economic and cultural cooperation treaty between the DPRK and Russia. Functionaries concerned, including Yi Song-tae, chairman of the External Economic Committee; (Yun Kyong-nam), vice minister of Culture and Art; (Kim Yong-su), vice chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Yi Pong-hui, chairman of the DPRK-Russia Friendship Association and vice chairman of the DPRK Radio and Television Broadcasting Committee were invited to the banquet. Speeches were delivered at the Banquet.
