BFN [Text] Bariloche (Argentina), 16 May (EFE) -- Argentine President Carlos Menem announced on 16 May that his country will request the extradition of Margaret Thatcher, the UK's former head of government, "in case it is proved" that the General Belgrano cruiser's sinking during the Malvinas war was a war crime. During a news conference today in Bariloche, located at the foot of the Andes Mountains, where he opened the Fourth Ibero-American Computer Science and Justice Congress, Menem said: "It has to be determined whether the ship's sinking was a war crime or not."
