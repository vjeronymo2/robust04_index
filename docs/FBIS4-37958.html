BFN [Excerpt] Aden, 27 Jun -- Fierce artillery and tank fighting continued between northern and southern troops on all fronts yesterday night and this morning. A southern military communique said that northern troops, after being reinforced, this morning tried to attack Aden's defenses using large forces of tanks and heavy artillery. The communique added that the southern fighters repulsed the northern attack with the help of air and naval forces and managed to drive back the northern troops. The southern military communique made clear that over nine northern tanks were destroyed, three katyusha launchers were burned, five field cannons were seized, and over 50 soldiers, most of them young, were killed. The communique said that battles around Aden are still raging, and that from dawn till 0900 today the residential quarters of the city faced intensified shelling. Fierce fighting erupted between northern and southern troops in and around Aden, with each side accusing the other of violating the cease-fire. [passage omitted]
