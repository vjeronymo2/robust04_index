October 26, 1989, Thursday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

TIRE BLOWOUT KILLS OC WOMAN

A tire blowout caused a car to veer off the Garden Grove Freeway and flip, killing a 47-year-old woman and injuring another woman and three children, the California Highway Patrol said today.

Joana Marinch of Garden Grove was thrown from the driver's seat and killed when the car flipped in the accident at 7:45 p.m. Wednesday, officers said.

She was traveling at about 55 m.p.h. when the tire burst, pulling the car sharply to the right. The car struck an asphalt curb and overturned in the shrubbery next to the freeway, west of Lewis Street.

The passengers were hospitalized for treatment of minor injuries. They were Maria Mazilu, 48, of Garden Grove, her two 10-year-old boys. and Marinch's 10-year-old daughter.
