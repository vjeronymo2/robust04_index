BFN [Text] Moscow, 10 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has signed a decree on "Measures for Implementing the Treaty on Public Accord [TPA]." The presidential press service released the full text of the decree today:

Measures for Implementing the Treaty on Public Accord.
To ensure that the articles of the TPA are implemented: 1. A Conciliation Commission for Implementing the TPA [CCITPA] is to be created in accordance with section 7 of the TPA. 2. S. A. Filatov, the head of administration of the president of the Russian Federation, is to be established as chairman of the CCITPA. 3. The chairman of the CCITPA is to set up the CCITPA within two weeks with the agreement of the Federation Council and the State Duma of the Federal Assembly, the government of the Russian Federation, and the Public Chamber under the president of the Russian Federation. In its work, the CCITPA interacts with the Federation Council and the State Duma of the Federal Assembly, with the government of the Russian Federation, with parties, with social and political movements, and with other organizations and establishments whose representatives signed the TPA. It also coordinates the implementation of the treaty. 4. Co-chairmen of the Public Chamber under the president of the Russian Federation are to define more precisely the composition of the Public Chamber under the president of the Russian Federation, to increase the number of its members taking into account the signing of the TPA; and to organize the analysis and the formulation of recommendations as to the implementation of fundamental provisions of the said treaty. 5. To set up commissions within the Public Chamber under the Russian Federation president: on political stability; on social and economic problems; on the federative structure and on interethnic relations; on security; and on science, education, and culture. They are to analyze the progress of the implementation of the TPA and to work out recommendations that will be considered at a session of the Public Chamber under the president of the Russian Federation and presented to the CCITPA. 6. The Public Chamber under the Russian Federation president is to ensure the preparation of coordinated proposals on the implementation of the TPA. 7. The chairman of the CCITPA and co-chairmen of the Public Chamber under the Russian Federation president are to brief the president on a monthly basis about the progress of the implementation of the TPA. 8. The department of the president's administration for the interaction between parties and public movements and the steering department of the administration of the Russian Federation president are to ensure the implementation of the TPA by the CCITPA.
