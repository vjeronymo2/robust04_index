940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Iranian gas for Pakistan Iran and Pakistan yesterday agreed to build an oil refinery in Pakistan and export Iranian natural gas and refined oil products to Pakistan, Reuter reports from Tehran. An official said the Rs22bn (Dollars 719m) cost of the refinery would be shared by the two countries. Iran last year signed an agreement with India to study the building of a Dollars 5bn, 2,200km pipeline to export Iranian gas to India.
