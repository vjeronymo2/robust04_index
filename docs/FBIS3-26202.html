Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, February 3 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Minister of Communications Huang Zhendong arrived here this evening for a five - day visit at the invitation of General Vasco Joaquim Rocha Vieira, governor of Macao. During his stay in Macao, Huang will hold talks with Macao governor and other concerned local government officials on key construction projects in Macao. Moreover, Huang will also listen to the introduction of local officials about Macao's infrastructural construction and visit the Macao International Airport, now under construction, the new Macao-Taipa Bridge and other large-scale facilities.
