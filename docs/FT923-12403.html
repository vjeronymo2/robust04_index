920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / Nablus accord Israeli troops and 3,000 Palestinian students yesterday ended a four-day stand-off at the biggest university in the occupied West Bank, Reuter reports from Nablus. The army agreed to lift its siege at An-Najah University in Nablus. In return, six activists sought by the army will leave the campus to face expulsion to Jordan for three years.
