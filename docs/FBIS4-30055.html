BFN [Text] Seoul, June 23 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam called on leading company presidents Thursday to take the lead in building up the nation's international competitiveness and bringing about smooth labor-management relations. At a Chongwadae [presidential offices] luncheon he hosted for 28 company presidents, Kim stressed that for labor-management harmony, good human relations should be maintained between the two sides and companies should be managed clean-handedly without any spot of suspicion. Presidential spokesman Chu Ton-sik quoted the president as telling the attendees that Korean industries should increase competitiveness through technological development and productivity elevation. Industries should also respect the order of fair competition and establish firm industrial ethics, the president said. Among the attending company heads were Pae Sun-hun, president of Daewoo Electronics Co.; Yi Tae-won, president of Samsung Aerospace Co.; and Kim Chu-yong, president of Hyundai Electronics Co.
