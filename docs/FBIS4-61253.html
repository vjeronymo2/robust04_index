BFN [Text] A government delegation led by Bozgul Dodkhudoyeva, deputy chairman of the Tajikistani Council of Ministers, today arrived in Khorog. The delegation will visit the Shughnon and Ishkashim rayons and other rayons of Gorno-Badakhshan, will hold meeting with labor collectives of enterprises and organizations, and will meet with representatives of the agro-industrial complex in order to ascertain for themselves the state of the political and economic situation in the oblast. The main objective of the visit is to explain the new draft constitution of the Republic of Tajikistan to the people of the Pamirs. The delegation will also try to find out the reasons behind the complicated sociopolitical situation along the border localities of Gorno-Badakhshan.
