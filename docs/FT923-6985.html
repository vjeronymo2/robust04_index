920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / Employees buy bus-body company NORTHERN Counties, one of the UK's leading makers of bus bodies, has been bought by its employees for Pounds 1.9m, 15 months after going into administration under Grant Thornton, the accountants. The deal has been led by Mr Ian Murray and Mr Nick Parsons, co-managing directors, and Mr Lindsay Khan, finance director. Employees will be able to acquire substantial shareholdings. The Bank of Boston's UK venture-capital arm has provided Pounds 515,000 of equity, while Pounds 600,000 of senior debt has come from Lloyds Bank.
