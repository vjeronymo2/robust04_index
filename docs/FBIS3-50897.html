Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Leonardo Rosas Gonzalez; from "The City" newscast] [Text] Charged with the infliction of injuries, homicide, criminal association, and the use of exclusive Army regulation weapons, the Attorney General's Office [PGR] has requested that criminal proceedings be brought against Guatemalan Jesus Sanchez Galicia. The Guatemalan has admitted to PGR officials that he had participated in armed attacks in Tecun and Ocosingo, Chiapas. He has confirmed that he had been arrested by Federal Judicial Police members at the highway checkpoint at the Catahashi-Chiapas crossroad, Tabasco State. Jose Sanchez Galicia has revealed that he is a native of Ticul Municipality in Guatemala, the country from where he received military training. Sanchez has been identified as one of the main commanders of the armed groups that are breaking the law in Chiapas. He said he is an expert in the handling of automatic weapons, pistols, revolvers, and rifles. He has been placed under the charge of the district criminal judge in Tuxtla Gutierrez, Chiapas.
