930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / People: Commercial Union Robbie Graham has been appointed deputy group actuary at Commercial Union with effect from July 1. Graham, who is in his early 40s, is a Fellow of the Institute of Actuaries, and currently director of financial management and operations, life assurance, within the UK division of CU. He will succeed Howard Webb as group actuary when Webb retires a year later on July 31 1994.
