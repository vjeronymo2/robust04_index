941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Gold shares saw moderate gains in a quiet trading session. The sector index made 15 to 2,185, while the overall was up 17 at 5,866, and industrials by 27 at 6,771. De Beers rose 50 cents to R100, Vaal Reefs R5 to R405 and Sasol 65 cents to R35.10.
