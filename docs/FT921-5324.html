920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: New Cavendish Ests Increased rental income and continuing reduction in overhead costs led New Cavendish Estates to pre-tax profits of Pounds 161,700 for the half year ended December 31, compared with Pounds 5,000. Net rental income came to Pounds 455,700 (Pounds 408,000) and finance and administration costs to Pounds 388,000 (Pounds 804,000). Earnings per share were 1.08p (0.04p). Net borrowings have been cut from Pounds 1.14m at June 30 1991 to under Pounds 800,000. Noro-Buckfield, the majority shareholder, intends to sell its 54.51 per cent stake.
