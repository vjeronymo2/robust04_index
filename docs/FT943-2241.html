940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / Observer: Happy families So Ramon Pajares has finally succumbed to the blandishments of Rocco Forte and the Savoy Group. He is packing his bags at the Four Seasons and heading for the office of Giles Shepard, who made his exit last week. As much the preferred candidate of both the Savoy and Forte, he was no doubt under considerable pressure to accept - perhaps even from his nearest and dearest. For his daughter, Maria, as PR manager of Claridge's, must surely have been a trifle parti pris.
