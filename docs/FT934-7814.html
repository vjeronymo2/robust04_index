931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Eurotunnel seeks share issue approval Eurotunnel, the company which will operate the Channel tunnel, is to seek the approval of its shareholders at an extraordinary meeting on December 15 to issue 192m new shares. Eurotunnel has said it needs a further Pounds 1bn of working capital on top of the existing capital of Pounds 8.9bn to meet its financing needs in the period following its opening next May. It is not expected to break even on a cash basis until 1998. The additional finance to be raised roughly equally in the form of equity and loans. The company said it the size of the authorisation sought was no indication of the shares which might eventually be issued or of the terms of any rights issue. The number is the same as that obtained by its French counterpart, Eurotunnel SA from its shareholders in June.
