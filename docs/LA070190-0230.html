July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GARBAGE COLLECTION RATE INCREASES TO BE CONSIDERED

An increase in garbage collection rates will be considered by the City Council at its July 10 meeting.

The current rate for once-a-week collection is $7.97 per month. The rate for twice-a-week collection is $9.98. If the council approves the increases, the rates would jump to $8.49 and $10.64 per month, effective immediately.
