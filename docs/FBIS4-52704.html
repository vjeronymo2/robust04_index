BFN [Text] The Japanese Government reportedly explained to the ROK Government, in regards to the discovery of 70 kg of undeclared plutonium in the Tokaimura nuclear fuel plant, that they will soon provide clarification of the truth. A relevant ROK Foreign Ministry official said on 12 May, that the ROK Government has received an initial reply from the Japanese Government to its request for clarification on the discovery of a large quantity of undeclared plutonium, and added that the ROK Government will take necessary measures after watching over how Japan proceeds on its clarification of the truth.
