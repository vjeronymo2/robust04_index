National Institute of Mental Health; Notice of Meetings Pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given of the meetings of the advisory committees of the National Institute of Mental Health for November 1994. In accordance with the provisions set forth in sections 552b(c)(4) and 552b(c)(6), Title 5, U.S.C. and section 10(d) of Public Law 92&hyph; 463, the entire meeting of each review committee will be closed to the public for the review, discussion and evaluation of individual grant applications.
