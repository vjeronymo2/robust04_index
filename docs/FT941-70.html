940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Hogg halved to Pounds 6m before goodwill write-offs Hogg Group, the insurance broker, yesterday reported halved pre-tax profits halved to at Pounds 6.01m for the year to end-December before goodwill write-offs of Pounds 4.96m on discontinued items. Turnover rose 4 per cent to Pounds 122m (Pounds 118m), while investment and other income fell 15 per cent to Pounds 6.12m (Pounds 7.22m) and operating expenses rose 9 per cent to Pounds 124m (113m). The board recommends a reduced final dividend of 2.5p (5p), compared with 5p last year, making a total of 5.65p for the year (8.15p). It said the reduction was 'prudent' and 'a healthier base from which to develop into the future'.
