BFN [Text] Commander of Ukraine's Naval Forces, Vice Admiral Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, has reported that the formation of the Western naval region is under way. Today, Ukrainian commanders will be introduced to the commanders of the Black Sea Fleet units in Izmail, Ochakov, and Nikolayev with the aim of organizing interaction. The above formations, control bodies, and their headquarters are being set up in advance for them to grow into the situation and get ready for the acceptance of the ships after the Black Sea Fleet division issue is solved. The newly set up formations are not charged with the task of interfering in the activities of the existing naval units. This news was circulated today by the Ukraine Defense Ministry press center via the TV and radio news agency Novyny.
