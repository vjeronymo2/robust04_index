BFN [Text] Eftim Ancev, agriculture, forestry, and water management minister of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], arrived in Turkey today on an official visit. The visiting minister and his delegation were received at Ankara's Esenboga Airport by Refaiddin Sahin, agriculture and village affairs minister. In a statement at the airport, Sahin said Turkey attaches special importance to relations with Macedonia and is exerting efforts to develop these relations. Agricultural cooperation and assistance will be discussed during the visit, Sahin reported, adding that a scientific, economic, and technical cooperation protocol will be signed in the field of agriculture. Ancev, in turn, expressed pleasure over visiting Turkey. He declared that Turkey's support is extremely important in the face of the Greek embargo on his country.
