December 24, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DRIVING IN THE CAR-POOL LANE WITH CHILDREN AS PASSENGERS

In response to letters by R.F. Craig and Andrea Kinney about children as passengers in car-pool lanes (Dec. 17):

I disagree with their contention that I am not taking another adult driver off the freeway when I have only children in my car.

I am in fact taking two adult drivers off the freeway by car-pooling my children and theirs to school.

I hope that the next time they see someone with children in the car-pool lane they will think that just maybe that person is keeping an adult off the freeway by taking their children someplace they both might have driven to.

MELINDA DAVIS

Huntington Beach
