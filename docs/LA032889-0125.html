March 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

Simon Kornblit, Sally Van Slyke, David Sameth and Perry Katz, four marketing executives at Universal Pictures' motion picture division, have been promoted. Kornblit has been named executive vice president-worldwide marketing. Van Slyke, Sameth and Katz have all been named senior vice presidents-marketing. The promotions are effective April 1.
