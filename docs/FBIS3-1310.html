Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The head of state, General Sani Abacha, will today in Abuja launch a campaign to safeguard the Lake Chad Basin. The launching is part of the summit of the Lake Chad Basin Commission, which began in the Federal Capital Territory yesterday. Addressing the opening session, Gen. Abacha called on the international community to give African nations the freedom to solve their internal problems. [passage omitted] The Lake Chad Basin Commission, established in 1964 in response to the resolution of the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations, is a joint venture between the Governments of Chad, Niger, Cameroon, and Nigeria. The Chadian delegation to the summit is being led by President Idriss Deby while President Mahamane Ousmane is at the head of Niger's team. The minister of planning and environmental management, Mr. Augustin Kodock, is leading the Cameroonian team to the two-day summit. [passage omitted]
