August 25, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CHUNG'S 'AGGRESSIVE' BID TO BE A MOM

It certainly takes a lot of chutzpah for Chung to announce to the world that she and her husband are going to take a "very aggressive approach" to conceiving a baby.

Pity any baby she may have; the child probably will have to make an appointment to see her between "prime time" and "specials."

MARILYN D. LAIKIN

Los Angeles
