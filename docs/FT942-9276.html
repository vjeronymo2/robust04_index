940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / People: Finance moves - Hambros Bank David Lewis has been appointed a vice-chairman of HAMBROS BANK, chairman of Hambro Pacific and a director of Hambros Australia, based in Hong Kong. Thomas Candy and Ken Williams have been appointed executive directors of Hambros Bank, and Ian Beauchamp, Dolf Campman, David Curtis, Andrew Hamilton, Harvinder Hungin, David Jenkins, Graham Jones, Paul Remington and Vesa Suurmunne have been appointed directors.
