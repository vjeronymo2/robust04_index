May 23, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

DUN & BRADSTREET TO SELL DIVISION

Dun & Bradstreet Corp. said today that it is seeking a buyer for its Donnelley Marketing division, excluding the Donnelly Marketing Information Services unit.

Donnelley Marketing is a direct-mail distributor of discount coupons, a marketer of consumer merchandise via direct mail and a provider of consumer mailing lists and letter shop services. It had revenues of about $225 million last year, excluding Donnelley Marketing Information.
