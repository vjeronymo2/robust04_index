BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Patriarch Aleksiy II of Moscow and All Russia today received PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat at his residence in St. Daniil's Monastery. In his introductory speech, 'Arafat said that the relations connecting our countries are very stable and friendly, and that he sees Russia as an example of great humanism. After his introductory speech, the press were asked to leave and the talks were continued behind closed doors.
