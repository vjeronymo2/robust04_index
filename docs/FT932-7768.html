930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Chesterfield Props Chesterfield Properties' share price rose 21 per cent from 272p to 330p yesterday after the company reported a Pounds 1m increase in 1992 pre-tax profits to Pounds 7.66m. Since the year-end borrowings had been reduced by Pounds 79m. Net asset value fell by 25 per cent to 398p (534p) due to the reduced value of the investment portfolio. A reduced final dividend of 7.5p (11.5p) has been recommended, for a total of 11p (18.5p) on earnings per share of 15.71p (12.02p).
