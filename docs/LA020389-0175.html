February 3, 1989, Friday, Orange County P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

EDMONTON STATUE TO HONOR GRETZKY

A $75,000 life-sized, bronze statue of Wayne Gretzky will be erected at the Northlands Coliseum to honor the former Edmonton Oilers center's contribution to the city.

A public rally that the hockey star will attend also is in the works, said Olivia Butti, chairwoman of a task force set up to decide on a tribute for Gretzky.

The statue will be unveiled at the rally, probably in September.

It will be a likeness of Gretzky with his arms raised, holding the Stanley Cup.
