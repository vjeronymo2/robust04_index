Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 13 (XINHUA) -- Jiang Zemin, Chinese president and general secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, today met with the delegates to the Art Working Conference of the People's Liberation Army (PLA). Jiang, also chairman of the Central Military Commission, said, "Our art is people's art and socialist art, and its position and function are very important." He said that successful art work can become spiritual strength to promote reforms and development, for it can improve people's morality and aesthetic judgment, and encourage people to work hard to revitalize China. Before the meeting, Jiang had cordial talks with senior writers and artists of the PLA. Zhang Zhen, vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, and other leaders of the commission, were present during the meeting.
