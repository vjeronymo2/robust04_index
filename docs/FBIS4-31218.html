BFN [Text] Sarajevo, June 6 (TANJUG) -- Snipers from the Moslem-held part of Sarajevo on Monday [6 June] continued to fire at Serb parts of the city, violating the agreement on a ceasefire on the Sarajevo battlefront, the Sarajevo-Romanija Corps of the Bosnian Serb republic army said on Monday. Snipers from the Moslem section of Dobrinja-Five and Stupsko Brdo were firing at the Serb section of Nedjarici and the Kasindolska Street, while they were opening fire from Browning machine-guns at the Serb section of Hadzici from their stronghold on Mt. Ormanj. Moslem units also contimued to fortify their positions in the sections of Mojmilo and Debelo Brdo. On Monday, the Moslems went on with their attacks launched eight days ago on the defence positions of the army of the Bosnian Serb republic around Srbobran (formerly Donji Vakuf), said the 1st Krajina Corps of the Bosnian Serb republic army.
