BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 27 Apr (AKP) -- Some 40 Khmer Rouge [KR] rebels recently infiltrated Andeuk Hep commune in Ratanakmondol District of Battambang Province. They robbed many houses and took away four rifles belonging to local militiamen. During the night of the same day the Khmer Rouge left this commune and spread out in Chrap area with the intention of attacking Ratanakmondol District and Snoeng commune. Half of the local population took refuge in neighboring districts (Battambang and Bavel). According to Battambang District Police Chief, a group of 30 Khmer Rouge on 20 April infiltrated into Battambang District with the intention of launching attacks. In Ek Phnum District, about 30 Khmer Rouge, under the command of Sok, retreated from Boeng Thmar and Kbal Chor positions in the direction of Pailin area.
