Thursday May 19, 1994 Part V Department of Labor Mine Safety and Health Administration 30 CFR Part 75 Underground Coal Mine Ventilation; Safety Standards; Proposed Rule Federal Register ␣/␣Vol. 59, No. 96␣/␣Thursday, May 19, 1994␣/␣Proposed Rules DEPARTMENT OF LABOR Mine Safety and Health Administration 30 CFR Part 75 RIN 1219&hyph;AA11 Safety Standards for Underground Coal Mine Ventilation AGENCY: Mine Safety and Health Administration, (MSHA) Labor. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: This proposed rule revises stayed provisions of the Mine Safety and Health Administration's existing safety standards for ventilation of underground coal mines. The proposal also revises, clarifies or reproposes certain other provisions in the rule, includes some new provisions, and addresses concerns raised during public informational meetings and later discussions. DATES: Written comments must be submitted on or before July 18, 1994. Commenters are encouraged to send comments on a computer disk along with their original comments in hard copy.
