BFN [Text] Khartoum, 14 Jun (SUNA) -- President General 'Umar al-Bashir met Monday [13 June] at the General Command the visiting U.S. president's representative Melissa Wells and the accompanied delegation. The meeting reviewed the recent initiatives toward realizing peace in southern Sudan. General al-Bashir welcomed the representative's visit, stressing the government eagerness to solve the problem through the peaceful means, adding that the attempts for imposing sanctions has negatively affected the peace endeavors. Wells in [a] statement following the meeting, described the visit as constructive, saying that she will leave for Wau to meet representatives of the other side (southerners). A message to General al-Bashir from Bill Clinton dealing with realization of peace in the south was conveyed by Mrs. Wells, Dr. Ghazi Salah al- Din, state minister, said.
