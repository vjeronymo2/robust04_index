940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Second quarter rise for Marine Midland Marine Midland Bank, the US subsidiary of HSBC Holdings, increased net income by 26 per cent from Dollars 45.3m to Dollars 57.1m (Pounds 36.8m) in the second quarter of 1994. The increase meant a 38 per cent rise in the first half of the year from Dollars 79.3m to Dollars 109.7m. The bank said the increase reflected strong net interest income and other revenues, while operating expenses were cut by 8 per cent.
