BFN [Relay from Jorge Chaves International Airport by Walter Samuel Neira] [Text] Ecuadoran Foreign Minister Diego Paredes has left for his country after spending the night in Peru. Paredes was on his way back from a meeting of Latin American foreign ministers with European representatives in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, last weekend. The Ecuadoran minister, who avoided the media before leaving, was taken in an official vehicle straight to the foot of the aircraft steps.
