BFN [Text] Moscow June 13 TASS -- Director of the Federal Counterintelligence Service (FCIS) Sergey Stepashin flew to the Far East this Monday on a working trip, during which he will visit Vladivostok, Nakhodka and the Kurile Islands. ITAR-TASS learnt at the FCIS that the director will meet population in the Far East, counterintelligence officers and will study the situation to map out measures to cut crime in the region and to realise the comprehensive crime-control programme.
