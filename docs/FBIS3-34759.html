Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] As a result of rocket attacks against the positions of the forces of Afghan President Borhanoddin Rabbani in Kabul yesterday 19 people were killed and 50 injured. Reporting this news, REUTER added: The joint forces of Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar and General 'Abdorrashid Dostam attacked areas in south and east Kabul. Kabul radio reported that in those attacks more than 600 rockets were fired. Secretary General of the Islamic Conference Organization Hamid al-Ghabid met with the officials of the two warring factions in Kabul yesterday and called on them to stop the clashes during the holy month of Ramadan.
