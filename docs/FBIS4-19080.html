BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said today that Greece regrets the incident on the Greek-Albanian border and stressed that when real incidents occur, Greece is willing to cooperate to clear up the issue on the level of the judicial authorities. Venizelos noted that Greece will not get carried away by the climate of tension cultivated by the Albanian side, because its goal is to safeguard peace in the southern Balkans. The spokesman added that the only information Greece has in connection with these incidents is that provided by the Albanian side. Venizelos added: No one can rule out the existence of extremist elements but these are elements that are handled by a state that operates in an excellent fashion. The spokesman concluded by saying that Greece will accept no provocation against it.
