941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Suter disposals Suter has sold its 12 acre site at Fareham, Hampshire to Legal & General Assurance Society for Pounds 6.75m cash, subject to a 25 year leaseback to Suter Environmental. Suter also announced the disposal of Deritend Engineering for a nominal sum. DEL, a non-core business, makes printing and die cutting machinery for use in the box making industry and was acquired as part of the James Wilkes group in May.
