BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song on April 18 received a letter of thanks from the participants in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival which was held in Pyongyang. This festival was a great international art gathering in which the artistes of different countries exchanged achievements and experiences with each other and further developed interchanges and friendly relations, says the letter. The letter notes that they were privileged to participate in the grand celebrations on the 82nd birthday of President Kim Il-song and that it is a great glory that the president personally saw their performances and highly praised them. The letter stresses during their stay in Korea they directly experienced the passion and fighting will of the Korean people who have brought about world-startling reforms in all fields including culture and art under the wise leadership of President Kim Il-song and the Workers' Party of Korea. The letter wishes President Kim Il-song good health and long life.
