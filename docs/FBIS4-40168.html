BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Minayev] [Text] Luxembourg June 14 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin will sign an agreement on partnership and cooperation between Russia and the European Union on the Greek island of Corfu on June 24, Greek Deputy Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos [title as received] said on Tuesday. According to the diplomat, who chaired the two-day sitting of the EU Council of Foreign Ministers in Luxembourg, after that, leaders of Austria, Norway, Finland and Sweden will sign a treaty on their counries' joining the European Union. A day before the ceremonies, Yeltsin and heads of member-countries of the EU will discuss pressing international problems at a working dinner. According to Pangalos, the text of the Russian-EU agreement has been discussed by the ministers. During the next few days the amendments concerning the activities of five foreign banks on the Russian territory will be introduced in the agreement by the EU's Committee of Permanent Representatives.
