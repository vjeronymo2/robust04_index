Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Ahmed Asselah, the head of the Higher Fine Arts School was assassinated this morning at about 0930 by an armed group inside the school, which is located near the capital. His son who was accompanying him was gravely wounded. Ahmed Asselah was 55 years-old and was the brother of martyr Hocine Asselah, one of the well-known martyrs of the Algerian revolution.
