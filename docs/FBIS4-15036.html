BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yefim Shvartsman] [Text] Simferopol, 5 Apr -- Ukraine President Leonid Kravchuk has assured Crimean Government Vice Prime Minister Yevgeniy Saburov that in the future, visits by representives of the leading departments of the Ukraine and the Crimea will be made with his knowledge and be agreed upon with the leadership of the Crimean Republic. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was told this by Valeriy Grishankov, head of the Crimean president's staff. It was reported earlier that a large group of top officials of the leading ministries of the Ukraine -- the ministries of defense, national guard, and internal affairs -- led by Anatoliy Lopata, chief of staff of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, had suddenly arrived this afternoon in the Crimea from Kiev. The head of the Crimean president's staff also said the reason for such a mass and unexpected visit by the top military brass to the Crimea was explained by officials of the Ukrainian president's staff as being a coincidence, since it turned out that each of the generals had his own plans for the peninsula.
