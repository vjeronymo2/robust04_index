940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / International Company News: Two Mexican financial groups plan to merge Two of Mexico's largest financial groups, Grupo Financiero Serfin and Grupo Financiero Inverlat, plan to merge in a move that will create the country's second-largest bank. The merger plan, which has been approved by share-holders, will begin a process of full integration between the two groups, which control the Serfin and Comermex banks. The new institution will have assets of about Dollars 36bn, placing it just above the current second-placed bank, Bancomer.
