BFN [Editorial Report] Buenos Aires LA NACION in Spanish on 13 May publishes on page 21 an unattributed 200-word article on an agreement between Corrientes Province and Russia. The article says: "Corrientes Governor Raul Romero Feris and Russian Trade Representative Anatoliy Timoschenko signed a protocol to promote and enhance integration and review trade possibilities." The article says: "Russian entrepreneurs are interested in raw materials, including oranges, lemons, rice, meat, and various types of hides, and Corrientes Province is interested in road maintenance and electric equipment." The article concludes by stating: "Romero Feris expressed the province's interest in the participation of Russian enterprises in energy market operations."
