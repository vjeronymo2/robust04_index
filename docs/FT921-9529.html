920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: Salomon Brothers names Keegan and Posford as co-chief executive officers Salomon Brothers has named Mr Dennis Keegan (left) and Mr Stephen Posford (right) as co-chief executive officers at the group's Europe operations, writes Sara Webb. Both will sit on Salomon's Europe management committee, joining Mr Charles McVeigh (chairman of Salomon Brothers Inter-national), Mr Peter McSloy (chief administrative officer), and Mr William Strong (head of European investment banking). Mr Posford has also been appointed to the executive committee of Salomon Brothers Inc.
