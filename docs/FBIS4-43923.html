BFN [Text] Halle/Saale (DDP/ADN) - The SPD [Social Democratic Party of Germany] chairman, Rudolf Scharping, was officially elected Social Democrat candidate for the chancellorship at the SPD congress in Halle today. Scharping, who was unopposed, received 95 percent of the delegates' votes. Scharping received 479 of the 502 valid votes cast. There were 14 votes against and nine abstentions.
