Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Sidorov] [Text] London March 23 TASS -- President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan has concluded his official British visit. The Kazakh president held negotiations with Prime Minister John Major, had a meeting with Queen Elizabeth II, made a speech before representatives of business circles and conversed with editors-in-chief of British leading newspapers. The sides signed a number of important bilateral agreements, among which are the joint declaration of friendship and mutual understanding, the agreement on cooperation in education, science and culture, the joint declaration of economic cooperation, the convention on prevention of double taxation, the agreement on air transportation, the declaration of cooperation between the foreign ministries. On Wednesday Nursultan Nazarbayev left for Spain.
