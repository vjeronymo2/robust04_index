BFN [Text] Most Chileans support President Eduardo Frei's administration even though this support is less than President Aylwin received during his first 90 days of government. According to an Adimark poll conducted in Greater Santiago, 54 percent support President Frei's administration, 15 percent disapprove, while 31 percent neither approves nor disapproves. Comparing these figures with the first 90 days of former President Aylwin's government, the support level is lower. Concerning pending matters, 41 percent believe the government badly handled the case of [Carabineros Director General] Rodolfo Stange, 36 percent believe it handled it well, and 22 percent said they do not know. Most Chileans believe Gen. Stange should resign.
