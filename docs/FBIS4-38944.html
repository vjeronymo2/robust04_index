BFN [Text] Prime Minister Birkavs has decided on Georgs Andrejevs' continuation in the post of Foreign Minister. Birkavs's decision was only disclosed on Monday, after the session of the Latvian Way faction, in which the faction's deputies questioned Andrejevs himself, Birkavs told journalists. It is possible that the issue of confidence in Andrejevs will not after all be brought up in the Saeima. The confidence vote was proposed by Andrejevs himself in an open letter published in DIENA, in which there was an admission of his collaboration with the State Security Committee of the former USSR.
