BFN [Text] Rabat, 24 Apr (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Government agricultural delegation, led by State Councilor Chen Junsheng, arrived here today for a four-day visit to Morocco. The Chinese delegation was welcomed at the airport by Mustapha Sahil, Moroccan minister of fisheries and merchant marine, and other officials as well as Chinese ambassador to Morocco An Guozheng. Speaking to the press at the airport, Chen Junsheng said: This visit is to strengthen Sino-Moroccan friendly and cooperative relations, particularly in agriculture and the fishing industry. During the visit, Chen Junsheng will hold talks with Moroccan leaders on issues of common interest.
