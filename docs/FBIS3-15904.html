Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Oleksandr Yemets, Ukrainian minister for nationality and immigration, has met with Israeli Cabinet Secretary Elyaqim Rubenstein. As the ministry's press center has reported today, the officials discussed issues concerning the protection of the rights of ethnic minorities in Ukraine. Oleksandr Yemets touched upon possibilities of developing and deepening cooperation between Ukraine and Israel. Israeli Ambassador to Ukraine Tzvi Magen also attended the meeting.
