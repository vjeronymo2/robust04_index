BFN [Text] Ankara, April 4 (A.A) -- Turkey and Albania on Monday [4 April] signed an agreement to prevent double taxation. Official talks between President Suleyman Demirel and visiting Albanian President Sali Berisha and the two country's delegations started in Cankaya Palace. Demirel speaking before the talks said existing cultural and historical relations will be enhanced between Turkey and Albania. Demirel recalled that a "friendship and good neighboring agreement and promoting investment" agreements were signed in 1991. [Ankara TRT Television Network in Turkish at 1700 GMT on 4 April reports that the two sides also exchanged the approval documents of the Turkish-Albanian friendship and cooperation agreement signed earlier.]
