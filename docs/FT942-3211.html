940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Rocard puts job on the line French Socialist leader Michel Rocard staked his political future on a confidence vote on Sunday within his party and challenged rival bidders for the French presidency to stand against him. This follows what he called a 'very serious setback' for the Socialists in the European elections when the party won only 14.5 per cent of the vote, its worst performance for a quarter of a century.
