BFN [Text] The Israeli enemy forces have committed another crime to add to their bloody record. At 1845 today, Israeli artillery located in 'Alman and al-Shumariyah bombarded the township of Frun. Hasan Hayik, 35, Dalal Hayik, 25, and Munirah Ahmad, 54, were martyred in the bombardment. Ahmad Hasan Hayik and Ibrahim Ja'far, 16, were slightly hurt and Muna Hayik was critically injured. They were all in the home of Qasim Bazzih, which was burned down. The UN Interim Force in Lebanon [UNIFIL] deployed in al-Ghanduriyah prevented citizens from entering Frun because the town was still being shelled. The Israeli forces also pounded the villages of Qa'qa'iyat al- Jisr, Zawtar al-Gharbiyah, and Zawtar al-Sharqiyah and the al-Litani River basin. The shelling was accompanied by Israeli helicopter flights over the al-Litani River basin.
