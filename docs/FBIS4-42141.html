BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] President Suleyman Demirel received Gafarov, Tajikistan's first deputy prime minister, at the Cankaya Mansion today. Demirel said at the meeting that the relations between Turkey and Tajikistan have great potential. He recalled that Turkey was the first country to recognize Tajikistan's independence. Since then, he said, our relations have been going on with no interruption. [Begin Demirel recording] We are ready to develop all our relations with Tajikistan. We are aware of Tajikistan's needs. We are ready to contribute to its construction. [end recording] Gafarov said that during its civil war, the greatest support Tajikistan received was from Turkey.
