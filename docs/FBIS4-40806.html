BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "United States Leads in Creating Joint Ventures in Russia"] [Text] It has been reported at the Russian Federation Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations that U.S. entrepreneurs lead Western investors in the creation of joint ventures on Russian territory. Thus far, 1,400 joint ventures with the participation of American capital have been created in Russia with private investments reaching $400 million and more than 300 firms having opened their offices.
