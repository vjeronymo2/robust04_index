BFN [Text] President Fidel V. Ramos has written Portuguese President Mario Soares, explaining the position of the Philippine Government to ban foreign participants in the recently concluded Asia-Pacific Conference on East Timor [APCET]. The president said the government had to ban foreign participants in APCET, including some Portuguese, to uphold the national interest and the democratic processes in the Philippines. At the same time the Chief Executive thanked President Soares for his message of felicitations on the 96th anniversary of the proclamation of Philippine independence last 12 June.
