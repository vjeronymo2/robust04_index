May 26, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

EX-NASCAR STAR RICHMOND INJURED

Former NASCAR star Tim Richmond is listed in fair and stable condition in a Florida hospital from injuries he suffered in a recent motorcycle accident, the Charlotte Observer reported. Details on the accident were not available, the newspaper said.

Richmond, who won six races during the 1986 Winston Cup season, came down with pneumonia in late 1986 and hasn't competed on the Winston Cup circuit since a limited schedule in 1987.
