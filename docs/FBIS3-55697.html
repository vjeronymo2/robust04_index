Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian Federation does not intend to establish official contacts with Serbian Krajina. The statement was made by a high-ranking Foreign Ministry official in Moscow. According to sources in Belgrade, a delegation of the self- proclaimed Republic of Serbian Krajina intends to arrive in Moscow on Tuesday. The republic is a Serbian-populated area in Croatia, conflicting with Zagreb, and favoring unification of Serbian lands into a separate state. A Russian diplomat said such a republic does not exist for Russia. Russia maintains contacts with Croatia, which is a member of the United Nations.
