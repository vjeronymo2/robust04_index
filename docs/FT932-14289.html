930420 FT 20 APR 93 / Amerada Hess's Scott field platform lifted into place The platform is lifted into place for Amerada Hess's Scott field, which is expected to be the largest oilfield development in the North Sea this decade. The field, which is thought to contain 450m barrels of oil and 287bn cu ft of gas, will produce 10 per cent of the UK's oil needs when production starts in November. The oilfield will be operated by Amerada Hess, whose partners in the development include Amoco, Deminex, Enterprise Oil, Mobil, Kerr McGee and Pict Petroleum.
