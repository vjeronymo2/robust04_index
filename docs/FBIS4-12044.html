BFN [Text] Fire has broken out briefly in Amman's Forte Grand Hotel, killing an Italian tourist and injuring at least 10 other people. Security officials were able to evacuate 430 people from the nine-story hotel and put down the blaze in a short period of time. An Italian Embassy spokesman identified the dead tourist as (Michel Otorino). He had tried to escape the fire by jumping from the second story. Thick smoke reached the ninth floor and at least 10 people were hospitalized for broken limbs and smoke inhalation. Security officials are investigating the cause of the fire. The hotel is equipped by self-operated fire extinguishers, but were not used due to panic that gripped the hotel staff.
