931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Cabinet reshuffle in Portugal Portugal's prime minister, Mr Anibal Cavaco Silva, appointed new ministers of finance, health, education and employment in a cabinet reshuffle yesterday, Reuter reports from Lisbon. The new finance minister is Mr Eduardo Almeida Catroga, a little-known economics professor. He replaces the highly unpopular Mr Jorge Braga de Macedo, who has presided over a slowdown in Portugal's once booming economy, growing unemployment and a mushrooming budget deficit.
