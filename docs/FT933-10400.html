930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Pacer incurs Dollars 235,000 loss Pacer Systems, the US-based electronics group traded on the USM, incurred a pre-tax deficit of Dollars 235,000 (Pounds 157,000) for the half year to June 30. That compared with previous profits of Dollars 320,000. The company said it expected to return to profit in the second half. Turnover slipped to Dollars 14.3m (Dollars 14.5m). Losses per share emerged at 3 cents (earnings 4 cents). The interim dividend is held at 3 cents.
