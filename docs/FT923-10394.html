920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Company News: McCaw remains in red McCAW Cellular, the leading US provider of cellular communications, yesterday reported second quarter income from operations of Dollars 165.9m, up from Dollars 123.3m a year ago. However, its bottom line remained in the red, with net losses of Dollars 85.6m, down from Dollars 133.7m. McCaw, a fast growing company in a rapidly expanding sector, has taken on a heavy debt burden to fund acquisitions and also has heavy non-cash depreciation and amortisation charges. Its income from operations - before depreciation and amortisation - compared with interest expense of Dollars 125.4m, down from Dollars 146.7m, and was struck on revenues 25 per cent ahead at Dollars 426.4m. Net revenues from cellular interests were up 29 per cent at Dollars 313.6m and cellular cash flow grew 41 per cent to Dollars 143.5m.
