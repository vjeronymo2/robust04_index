[Docket No. TC94&hyph;2&hyph;000] Columbia Gas Transmission Corporation; Proposed Changes in FERC Gas Tariff September 22, 1994. Take notice that on September 15, 1994 Columbia Gas Transmission Corporation (Columbia) tendered for filing as part of its FERC Gas Tariff, Second Revised Volume No. 1, the following proposed tariff sheets to become effective November 1, 1994. First Revised Sheet Nos. 657&hyph;666 Columbia states that the listed tariff sheets set forth the revised Index of Entitlements and are filed pursuant to 281.204(b)(2) of the Commission's Regulations and §32.1(f) of the General Terms and Conditions of Columbia's FERC Gas Tariff, Second Revised Volume No. 1. Columbia states that copies of the filing were served upon the Company's jurisdictional customers and interested State Commissions. Any person desiring to be heard or to protest said filing should file a motion to intervene or protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426, in accordance with Rules 211 and 214 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedures (18 CFR 385.211 or 385.214). All such motions or protests should be filed on or before September 29, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceedings. Any person wishing to become a party must file a motion to intervene in accordance with the Commission's rules. Copies of Columbia's filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
