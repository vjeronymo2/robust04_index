Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov is expected to visit Moscow on 1 February. Circles close to the Republic's leaders have informed a HABER SERVICE NEWS AGENCY correspondent that Gasanov will hold talks with Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. The two ministers will exchange views on the forthcoming meeting of the presidents of Azerbaijan and Armenia in Moscow. The sources said that Gasanov will demand the unconditional withdrawal of all Armenian military units from Azerbaijan. In a recent statement to the HABER SERVICE NEWS AGENCY correspondent, Azerbaijan's plenipotentiary representative in Moscow said that Azerbaijan's leaders will not agree to hold talks while Armenian military forces remain in Azerbaijan.
