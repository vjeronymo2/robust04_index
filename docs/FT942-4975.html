940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Simon Engineering completes US sale Simon Engineering has completed the sale of Simon Hydro-Search to Tetra Tech, of Pasadena, California, for Dollars 6m (Pounds 4m) in cash dependent on net assets on completion. Proceeds will be used to reduce indebtedness, the directors said. Both Simon Hydro-Search, which had sales of Dollars 18.5m and trading profits of Dollars 800,000 for 1993, and Tetra Tech are environmental consulting, engineering and remediation companies.
