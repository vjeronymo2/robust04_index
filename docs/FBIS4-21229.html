BFN [Text] Turkish Cypriot (Khalil Oguskun) from Louritzina was arrested at a Larnaca hotel today as he was waiting to sell one kilo of heroin. The Turkish Cypriot also had a loaded handgun. This afternoon the arrested man was taken to Larnaca District Court, which issued an eight-day detention order. Testifying in court, Police Superintendent Onisifors Ioannou said that police had information that the suspect, along with other persons, were planning to distribute heroin worth 10,000 Cyprus pounds [$20,000] in the free areas of the republic. The arrest of the Turkish Cypriot was achieved in a joint operation between the Antinarcotics Unit of police headquarters and the Larnaca Criminal Investigation Department.
