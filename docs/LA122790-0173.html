December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ROSY PROSPECTS? Oregon, which plays Colorado State in Anaheim's Freedom Bowl Saturday, has followed Washington's progress with interest. . . . Washington plays in the Rose Bowl New Year's Day but preceded that with the Freedom Bowl last year and the Independence Bowl three years ago. Oregon has been following the same trail, starting with last year's Independence Bowl. "Next year, with the guys coming back, who knows, maybe the roses," says Oregon center Scot Boatright. Robyn Norwood and Ann Conway
