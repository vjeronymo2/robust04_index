January 24, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

Correction Appended

HIGH SCHOOL GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

In another City Western League game:

Mission Bay 34, La Jolla 26 -- Lara Foley scored 17 points and had 14 rebounds for Mission Bay (6-12, 1-2). La Jolla is 2-13, 0-3.

City Central League

Lincoln 45, Madison 38 -- Center Sheila Dixon scored a game-high 31 points for Lincoln (4-0, 10-5). Madison (0-3, 5-9) was led by Jill Weeks and Tanya Hamilton with nine apiece.

City Eastern League

Point Loma 54, Mira Mesa 40 -- Tyeast Brown led visiting Point Loma (17-3, 4-0) and all scorers with 22 points. Stacy Kasimatis led Mira Mesa (9-11, 2-1) with 13. Ericka Crittenden, who Point Loma Coach Lee Trepanier calls the best defensive player in the county, didn't play because of a knee injury.

Serra 45, Patrick Henry 34 -- Lynn Thoms scored 16 points to lead host Serra (10-8, 1-2). Patrick Henry is 2-14, 1-2.

City Harbor League

Our Lady of Peace 65, Clairemont 28 -- Tammy Tavares led Our Lady of Peace (13-3) with 22 points. Kristy Linaugh led Clairemont with 10.

Palomar League

Torrey Pines 70, Fallbrook 40 -- Karyn Armstrong scored 19 points and had six rebounds to lead Torrey Pines (10-6, 4-3). Adrian Breland added 11 points and 10 rebounds. Marsha Jeffries led Fallbrook (2-5, 5-12) with 13 points.

Orange Glen 61, San Dieguito 52 -- Melody Hroch scored 26 points and Carol Clinger had 16 points and 16 rebounds for Orange Glen.

Rancho Buena Vista 61, Poway 59 -- Shawna Edge (19 points) and Stephanie Howells (18) led Rancho Buena Vista (13-4, 6-1). Poway is 14-2, 6-1.

Avocado League

Oceanside 32, San Marcos 24 -- Mildred Conston led host Oceanside with 21 points.

Grossmont 3-A League

Mt. Miguel 56, Granite Hills 45 -- Monique Anderson led Mt. Miguel with 19 points and 23 rebounds.

Grossmont 2-A League

Santana 86, Valhalla 20 -- Desiree Weimann scored 29 for Santana and Rebecca Aase added 26.

Coastal League

La Jolla Country Day 58, Parker 28 -- Isabel Schoeplin scored 23 points to lead La Jolla Country Day, which led 33-12 at halftime. LJCD improved to 12-2, 3-0. Parker is 5-7, 1-1.

Nonleague

USDHS 44, Morse 29 -- Casey Burke led host USDHS with 10 points.

San Diego 54, Marian 43 -- Dolmesha Stallings, a sophomore forward, scored 21 points and had 19 rebounds to lead host San Diego (8-8).

Metro

Bonita Vista 61, Southwest 41 -- Torril Purvis scored 23 points to Bonita Vista.
