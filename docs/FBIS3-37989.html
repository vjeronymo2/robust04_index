Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol -- In an interview with a UNIAN correspondent on 24 February, Crimea's President Yuriy Meshkov stated: "For the time being there is not going to be any reaction on the part of Crimea's authorities to Ukraine's Supreme Council resolution regarding conformity of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea's Constitution with Ukraine's Constitution and laws. There are more important concerns." According to Meshkov, the resolution "is inexpert from the legal point of view. Kiev is scarcely managing to destabilize the situation on the peninsula in this way," noted Crimea's president.
