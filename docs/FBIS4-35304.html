BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister brother 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi received brother 'Ata Harun, the Egyptian ambassador in Sanaa today. They reviewed Yemeni developments and stressed that every effort must be made so Yemen's stability and security are spared. The meeting also addressed bilateral ties. Both noted the need to clear the air of Arab relations. The deputy prime minister also received brother Mansur 'Abdallah al-'Awadi, the Kuwaiti charge d'affaires in Sanaa. They discussed Yemeni developments. Both were in agreement as to the need to maintain Yemen's unity, security, and stability. They also underscored the brotherly relations that bind the Yemeni and Kuwaiti peoples.
