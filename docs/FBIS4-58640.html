BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and the delegation accompanying him at the Royal Hashemite Court today. His Highness Crown Prince Hasan attended the meeting. At the outset of the meeting, President 'Arafat extended, in his own name and on behalf of the Palestinian leadership, condolences to His Majesty King Husayn and the esteemed Hashemite family on the death of Queen Mother Zayn al-Sharaf. President 'Arafat also briefed his majesty on the latest developments in the negotiations between the Palestinian and Israeli sides, and what has been reached in the Cairo agreement. He expressed the Palestinian leadership's appreciation for Jordan's role, particularly His Majesty King Husayn's efforts, in supporting and backing the Palestinian people. His Majesty King Husayn reiterated Jordan's firm support for the Palestinian rights and the PLO in its capacity as the sole legitimate representative of the Palestinian people. He also stressed Jordan's concern about supporting the kinsfolk in the occupied territories, and about alleviating their suffering. Attending the meeting for Jordan were Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister, al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court, Marwan al-Qasim, political adviser to his majesty the king, and Talal al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs. On the Palestinian side were Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, Yasir 'Amr, Salim al-Za'nun, father Iliya Khuri, and the Palestinian ambassador in Amman.
