Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] [Sergey Vozyanov] The Russian Federation president's press service announced today that Boris Yeltsin has issued a decree on the structure of the federal executive authorities. Under the decree, the number of ministries is reduced to 23. The head of the Russian government will now have four deputies, including two first deputies. The decree has been given priority for examination by the Federal Assembly.
