Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By 'Awdah 'Awdah] [Excerpts] Ibrahim Ghawshah, official spokesman of the Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, has stated that the first meeting of the Alliance of Palestinian Forces [APF], which was formed in the Syrian capital on 5 January, will meet in Damascus today. In an exclusive statement to AL-RA'Y, Ghawshah said that the alliance's 20-member leadership (two members from each faction) will discuss the results of the Syrian-U.S. summit and its implications for the Palestinian people's cause at this important and serious stage. [passage omitted] Answering a question, Ghawshah described the Syrian-U.S. summit in Geneva as "an important political meeting but we do not expect quick or direct results from it. The first result could be an improvement in Syrian-U.S. relations."
