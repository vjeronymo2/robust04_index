Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow, 2 Mar -- An agreement on cease-fire in the zone of the Karabakh conflict has been reached. It was mainly maintained since midnight on 1 March. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned about this today from Georgiy Kondratyev, the deputy defense minister of Russia, who has returned from Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan. He believes that this agreement is the main result of his working visit to Yerevan, the capital of Armenia, and Baku as a head of the Russian Defense Ministry commission on the settlement of the Karabakh conflict, as well as his meetings with the heads of the states and the defense ministers of Armenia and Azerbaijan.
