941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / World Commodities Prices: Nickel prices continue retreat at LME - Market report NICKEL prices came under heavy selling pressure at the London Metal Exchange yesterday as further liquidation was prompted by a poor technical close on Monday and reduced concern over the likely impact any problems at Russia's Norilsk complex. At the London Commodity Exchange COFFEE futures fell heavily after the US Department of Agriculture raised its estimate of 1994-95 world ouput.
