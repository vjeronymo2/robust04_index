January 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ALL ABOUT TRAVEL

In Jerry Hulse's Travel Tips (Dec. 4) there was a recommendation for "personalized itineraries" under the heading of "Tailored Itineraries." What caused us to take notice was the assertion that "travel agents are skilled at obtaining bargain fares," but it is necessary to go to a consultant who is not an agent for expert itinerary planning.

We at Newport Travel Concepts pride ourselves on providing our clients with the most up-to-date faring and routing expertise and thoroughly researched and personalized itineraries. We have created specialized trips throughout the globe, including Africa, Asia and literally around the world. We provide service with respect to every aspect of travel and believe that your readers should know that there are travel agents who "do it all."
