BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Ali Alatas today left for New York to attend a conference on Indonesia, the United States, and the world to be held from 13 to 15 April 1994. Irawan Abidin, director general of foreign information of the Department of Foreign Affairs, said Minister Ali Alatas will deliver a keynote paper on global political developments during the conference. Besides Ali Alatas, the conference will also be addressed by Indonesian Ambassador to the United States Arifin Siregar; U.S. Ambassador to Indonesia Robert L. Barry; and Thomas Hubbard, U.S. deputy assistant secretary of state for East Asian and Pacific Affairs. The annual conference organized by the Asia Society will also be held in Houston, Los Angeles, and Seattle.
