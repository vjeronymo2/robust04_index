Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Kray News"] [Text] The Gorkom [city committee] of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation has held a plenum in Khabarovsk. It discussed the results of the elections to the Federal Assembly, the referendum on the draft Constitution, and the party's preparations for elections to the kray Duma, and the local self-governing bodies. The communists discussed the nomination of possible deputies, both party and nonparty members. The plenum examined the issue of accounts and elections within the party organizations directed at further strengthening the party ranks.
