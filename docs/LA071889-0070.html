July 18, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

June Lockhart and Jon Provost will be together again with Lassie on a silver-anniversary reunion episode to be shot next week for MCA-TV's new syndicated "Lassie" series. Provost was the orphan Timmy on CBS' "Lassie" series from 1957-64, and Lockhart was on the same show from 1958-64. The new show stars Dee Wallace Stone, Christopher Stone, Will Nipper, Wendy Cox and Lassie.ALEENE MacMINN
