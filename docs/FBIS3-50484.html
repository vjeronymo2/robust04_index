Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Lima, 31 Dec (AFP) -- The police reported today that at least two persons were injured this Friday, when a rebel commando detonated an explosive device at a substation of the Peruvian Telephone Company in the Santa Anita District of Lima. According to preliminary reports, an armed group arrived at the substation in a vehicle and exploded the device when the two guards were sleeping. It was also reported that the explosion caused heavy damage to the office and to adjacent buildings. [passage omitted]
