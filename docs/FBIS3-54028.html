Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Jan 23 (MENA)--Different police units this morning encircled and combed different randomly built areas in east of Cairo within the context of a new strategy drawn up by the Interior Ministry to uproot all that could compromise the stability and security, Major General Mahmud Wadi, head of the General Police Investigation Department, said. Some 200 suspects involved in criminal and political wrongdoings were arrested during these campaigns. It was discovered that two among them had received death sentences while in absentia.
