July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RECORD RACK: IN BRIEF;

*** GREEN ON RED, "THIS TIME AROUND," CHINA/POLYDOR. ALBUMS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE (A CLASSIC).

This time around the veteran L.A. subterranean rock band -- now just founder/singer Dan Stuart and guitarist Chuck Prophet plus various sidemen -- has come up with its leanest, meanest album yet. Dylanstones in the desert on the hottest day of the year, with some surprising country tenderness to provide a little shade. STEVE HOCHMAN
