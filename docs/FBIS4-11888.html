BFN [Text] An official source at the General Committee of the General People's Congress [GPC], in a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', has expressed the GPC command's amazement at a report aired by the London-based Middle East Broadcasting Center, MBC, yesterday. The report says that brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman and GPC secretary general, will meet with his deputy, brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, in Cairo on 4 April. The source said that the GPC command knows nothing about this report, and that the brother chairman of the Presidential Council will pay an official visit to the Sultanate of Oman today, Sunday, at the invitation of his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id.
