BFN [Text] Tomiichi Murayama, chairman of the Social Democratic Party of Japan, in an interview with a local TV station in Oita City on 29 April, indicated the possibility of the House of Representatives being dissolved for a general election once the fiscal 1994 budget is passed. He said: "Some people say the next election should be held only under the single-seat electoral district system. But if there is no prospect for finding a breakthrough in the current political stalemate, there will be no other choice [but to hold the election under the current medium district system]."
