920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / Thai telephone boss wins support About 2,000 employees of Thailand's state telephone company demonstrated yesterday to protest against the removal of their boss pending a corruption investigation, Reuter reports from Bangkok. Communications Minister Nukul Prachuabmoh had ordered the removal of Mr Paiboon Limpaphayom, the director-general of the Telephone Organisation of Thailand, while corruption investigations take place. Under Mr Paiboon, the organisation granted a concession to a private company to install 2m telephone lines in the capital and 2m in the provinces.
