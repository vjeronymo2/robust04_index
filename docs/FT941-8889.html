940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Embassy Property sale nets Pounds 1.9m Embassy Property Group, the USM-quoted development and construction company, said that its share of net proceeds from the sale of its joint venture site in Edmund Street, Birmingham, was Pounds 1.92m. This compares with a book value of the site at March 31 1993 of Pounds 1.16m. Total consideration for the sale amounted to Pounds 4.25m. Embassy Property's joint venture partner in the Birmingham site is Five Oaks Investments.
