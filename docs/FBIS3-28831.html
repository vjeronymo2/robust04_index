Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 4 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In connection with the change in the structure of the Mongolian Government the First Vice-Director of MONTSAME T. Galdan and the Vice-Director T. Avirmed are relieved of their posts. C. Tumendelger, former correspondent of MONTSAME in Moscow, is appointed the vice-director and the editor-in-chief of the agency. At the government sitting also discussed was an issue about renewal of the agreement signed in 1965 between the Mongolian Government and the UNICEF. The government considered expedient to sign a now agreement. [sentence as received] The First Vice-Minister of External Relations of Mongolia J. Choinhor was empowered to sign an agreement on the Mongolian side. In accordance with the programme of cooperation with Mongolia the UNICEF is rendering assistance to the country for 1991-1995 worth of 2,5 mln [million] US dollars, which is by 10 times more as compared with the previous five years, informs the press service of the Government of Mongolia.
