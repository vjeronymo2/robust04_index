940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg put in a positive performance as bullion steadied around Dollars 383 an ounce and lagging industrials were selectively bought. The overall index rose 57 to 5,643, industrials advanced 52 to 6,644 and golds put on 23 to 2,019. Anglos gained R6.50 to R239.
