921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / The Conservative Party At Brighton: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference urged reform 'alongside' the Maastricht treaty to ensure that unnecessary influence from Brussels is halted and congratulated the government on its 'leader ship' on foreign affairs, especially as European Com munity president; Welcomed local government reforms 'based on principles of accountability and value-for-money services'; Gave its backing to the Citizen's Charter; Welcomed reform of the European Common Agricultural Policy. TODAY: Conference will debate social security, education, trade and industry, home affairs, transport and Northern Ireland.
