920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Rise found in venture funding LOCAL-AUTHORITY pension funds have become the largest source of venture capital in the regions, according to a survey by Pensions Investment Research Consultants (PIRC). Over two years the funds have increased their investment in venture capital by 51 per cent to Pounds 271m, PIRC says. This was in a period when the venture capital industry experienced a general slowdown in rates of growth. PIRC, which advises local authorities on investments, found that a total of 62 local-authority pension funds, 63 per cent of the total, had made venture capital investments. Local Authority Pension Fund Venture Capital Investment. PIRC, 40 Bowling Green Lane, London EC1R 0NE.
