Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Colonel Raul Luks, the commandant of Paldiski, has confirmed to the Estonian Telegraph Agency that everything is ready for the dismantlement of the nuclear reactor at the Paldiski training center. Estonian and Russian specialists met at Paldiski today to discuss cooperation for the period of the dismantlement. Luks said that they agreed about joint activity, the collection of initial information, and ways of cooperation. Luks could not answer a question on when the actual dismantlement of the reactor will begin. [passage omitted]
