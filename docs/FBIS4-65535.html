BFN [Report by Hans V. Haumer: "OIAG Unanimously for Abu Dhabi"] [Excerpt] Vienna -- The board of directors of OIAG, the nationalized industry holding company, voted unanimously in favor of the Abu Dhabi International Petroleum Investment Company, IPIC, buying part of OMV [Austrian Mineral Oil Administration]. The Arab investors will buy 20-percent of the shares at a total of 4.5 billion Austrian schillings, OIAG chief Karl Hollweger told DER STANDARD. Of this, 3 billion schillings will go to the public treasury. For this amount, the IPIC will receive a 13 percent share in the state oil company. Another 7 percent will be purchased by IPIC with an increase in the OMV share capital that is scheduled for June. [passage omitted]
