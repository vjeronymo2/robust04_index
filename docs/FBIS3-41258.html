Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Al-Sallum, 10 Feb (MENA)--Undercover agents at the al-Sallum border crossing today seized 8 kg of crude opium oil [Zayt al-Afyun al-Kham] in bottles concealed inside the tires of a vehicle driving from Libya. The driver, Hafiz 'Abd-al-Sattar Muhammad, said he is employed by Ayman Khidr, the proprietor of an import-export firm in Alexandria. He added that he took delivery of the vehicle from its owner in the Libyan town of Darnah. The owner told him he would follow him in three days. Sa'd Mutawalli, chief prosecutor in Marsa Matruh Governorate, remanded the driver for four days pending investigation and sent a specimen of the seized merchandise to the forensic laboratory for examination. He also issued instructions to arraign the export firm proprietor.
