December 19, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

What Would Herman Say?: Patrick Lilley, the actor who played Eddie Munster in the television series "The Munsters," pleaded innocent Monday to charges that he robbed his limousine driver outside a suburban gas station near Chicago. Lilley, 37, who used the stage name Butch Patrick in the hit series, was in Chicago making appearances at Halloween parties and signing autographs when the alleged robbery occurred. BETH KLEID
