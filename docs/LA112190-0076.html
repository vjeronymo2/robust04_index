SHOPPING MALL POSTAL BOOTHS

Many county shoppers can buy gifts and mail them to their out-of-town relatives without leaving the shopping mall. The U.S. Postal Service has set up booths in selected malls throughout the county. The booths offer the services found in any post office, such as parcel post and express, priority, insured and registered mails. Products available include holiday stamps, padded envelopes and shipping cartons. The booths will open Friday and continue to operate through Dec. 23, except as noted.
