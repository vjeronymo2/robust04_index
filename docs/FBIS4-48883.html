BFN [By reporter Hu Xiaoguang (5170 2556 0342)] [Text] Almaty, 26 Apr (XINHUA) -- Premier of the State Council Li Peng, who is now on a visit here, met this afternoon with the entire staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Kazakhstan and representatives of the Chinese enterprises in Kazakhstan at the state guesthouse. Premier Li Peng gave a positive appraisal to the efforts that the staff members of the embassy have made for developing the Sino-Kazakh relationship. He also encouraged them to work harder to contribute to the friendship between China and Kazakhstan.
