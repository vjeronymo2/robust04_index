Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report under the "In Brief" rubric: "A Version of the `Latvian' Incident"] [Text] Jazep Antsan, deputy general prosecutor of Latvia, has told a number of Moscow correspondents over the telephone that reasons for the attempted seizure of the complex of buildings by the local authorities from the Russian Army are not very clear. According to the prosecutor, the building that was the site of illegal actions by Latvian executive committee guards was a cultural center comprising a costly hotel, swimming pool, and sports facilities. The greater part of this complex is rented out to representatives of local commercial organizations of Latvia.
