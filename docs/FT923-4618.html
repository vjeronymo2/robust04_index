920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Medeva pays Pounds 4.9m for SKB licences Medeva, the pharmaceuticals group, has paid Pounds 4.9m to acquire the licences and trademarks of 28 products, with annual sales of Pounds 2.5m, from SmithKline Beecham. This is the fifth acquisition by Medeva this year, and all have expanded its product range. Payment will be made in three instalments before March 31 1994. SmithKline Beecham will continue manufacturing the products.
