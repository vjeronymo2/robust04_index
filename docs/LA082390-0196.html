August 23, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR: MORE BARS:

Dillman's, 801 E. Balboa Blvd., Newport Beach. Open daily, 9 a.m. to 2 a.m. (714) 673-7726.

A noble place, resolutely unfashionable. Dillman's satisfies the most important requirements of the serious bar-goer: Keep it dark, keep it quiet and keep 'em coming. MIKE FLAGG
