920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Arts: Death and the Maiden - Duke of York's A completely new cast has taken over in Ariel Dorfman's Death and the Maiden which opened at the Duke of York's Theatre in February. Penny Downie is Paulina Salas, the woman scarred by her experience of a dictatorship. Danny Webb plays Gerardo Escobar and Hugh Ross is Dr Roberto Miranda.
