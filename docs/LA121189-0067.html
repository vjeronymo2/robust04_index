December 11, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Comaneci Controversy: "Hard Copy" will feature an exclusive interview tonight with Maria Panait, wife of Romanian immigrant Konstantin Panait, the 34-year-old builder who arranged Romanian gymnast Nadia Comaneci's defection late last month and has been romantically linked with the Olympic gymnast. On the show Maria Panait, the Florida mother of four, will discuss her husband's affair with the gymnast. The show airs at 7:30 p.m. on KNBC Channel 4. Claudia Puig
