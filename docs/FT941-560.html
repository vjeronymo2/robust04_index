940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves Chemicals and protective equipment maker Blagden Industries dropped 26 to 127p after recording a sharp loss in 1993 and warning of a Pounds 20m rights issue before the end of May. Manchester United tumbled 30 to 639p after losing the Coca-Cola cup final on Sunday, dashing its hopes of a clean sweep of this season's domestic trophies.
