940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Lager sales help lift Everards Everards Brewery, the Leicester-based independent brewer, reported pre-tax profits more than doubled over the 12 months to September 25. On turnover ahead 5.4 per cent at Pounds 35.3m, operating profits were Pounds 2.91m (Pounds 2.12m). After a reduced net interest burden, the pre-tax line emerged at Pounds 1.86m (Pounds 899,000). The decision to reappraise its lager portfolio, resulting in the introduction of Carling Black Label and Labatt's, led to higher volume and profits. Earnings per share improved to 67.3p (50.5p). The final dividend goes up to 6.8p for a total of 13.6p (13p).
