941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / Letters to the Editor: Life under the big spenders Sir, According to Joe Rogaly, 'today's Conservative ministry has trapped itself on the shores of free-market minimalism' ('Echoes of Tory voices', October 7). Rogaly seems to have forgotten that nearly half the British economy - more than 44 per cent - is in the public sector. What would it be like under a Labour ministry, full of politicians who were actually keen on spending other people's money? Richard Bacon, 86 Gloucester Street, London SW1V 4EE
