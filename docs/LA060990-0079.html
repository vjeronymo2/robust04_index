June 9, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB;

MAN VS. MICROCHIP

All good things, even hypothetical ones, have to end sometime. After a flying start with my new bankroll Thursday, I came back to Earth when my $40 win bet Friday, Rejim in the eighth race, finished third.

But it's far too early to start worrying. After all, the computer's pick finished last. Take that, silicon breath!

My bets for today are $20 to win on Classic Overlay in the fourth race and $60 to win on Patches in the sixth.
