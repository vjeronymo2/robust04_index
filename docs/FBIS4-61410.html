BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Karlov] [Text] Moscow, 6 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The fire which broke out at Beloyarsk nuclear power station (Sverdlovsk oblast in the Urals) at 0924 at the third power unit of Beloyarsk AES was still not extinguished by 1300 today. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed of this in the Information and Public Relations Directorate of the Ministry of Atomic Energy of the Russian Federation. In the opinion of specialists, the workforce, population, and environment are not being affected by radiation. The accident has been given a preliminary assessment of level one on the international scale of nuclear incidents (the levels are graded one to seven -- Chernobyl was grade seven). The fire broke out during repair work on the third power unit which has been under repair since 23 April. There was a leak of non-radioactive sodium from the drainage of the secondary circuit in one of the machine rooms. The sodium ignited. Efforts to extinguish the fire are continuing.
