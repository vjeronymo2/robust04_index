Backfit Analysis The NRC has determined that the backfit rule, 10 CFR 50.109, does not apply to this proposed rule and, therefore, that a backfit analysis is not required for this proposed rule. The proposed rule does not involve any provisions that would impose backfits as defined in 10 CFR 50.109(a)(1).
