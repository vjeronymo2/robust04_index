920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / World News In Brief: Death toll in Turkish pit disaster may reach 300 Officials believe some 300 miners have been killed after methane gas explosions at the Incirharmani mine in northern Turkey. Rescue workers yesterday abandoned hope for about 200 trapped miners. 'The passages were full of bodies as we ran for the upper levels,' a survivor, Salih Yanik, said in hospital. News agency reports early today said 121 bodies had been recovered.
