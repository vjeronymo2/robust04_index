920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: 6% improvement to Pounds 9.1m at CMG Computer Management Group, a privately-held computing services company with strengths in financial services and public utilities, last year shrugged off the UK recession to record improved sales and profits. Turnover for 1991 rose by 7 per cent to to Pounds 103m (Pounds 96.2m) while pre-tax profits advanced by 6 per cent to Pounds 9.1m. Earnings per share were 39.8p (37p), and a dividend of 13.1p (12.7p) is recommended. UK sales were flat at Pounds 39m but cost cutting measures ensured that the UK activities made a profit of Pounds 600,000 last year compared with a loss of Pounds 1m in 1990.
