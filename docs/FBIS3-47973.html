Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 19 -- President Le Duc Anh today received Mr. Issa Kalantari, minister of agriculture of the Islamic Republic of Iran who is here for a visit to Vietnam. During the reception, Mr. Isa Kalantari expressed his pleasure at his first visit to Vietnam and his sincere thanks for President Le Duc Anh's reception. He also conveyed the Iranian President Rafsanjani's best regards to President Le Duc Anh and invited him to pay a visit to Iran. He introduced to President Le Duc Anh the Iranian people's achievements in their economic restoration and national reconstruction and appreciated the cooperation between the two countries in the fields of economy, especially agriculture, agricultural products exchanges, sciences, technology and training experts. He also expressed his wishes for the above-said cooperation to further expand and develop in various fields. For his part, President Le Duc Anh welcomed Mr. Isa Kalantari's Vietnam visit and expressed his pleasure at the achievements recorded by the Iranian people. On behalf of the Vietnamese people President Le Duc Anh thanked the Iranian Government and people for their support and assistance to the cause of Vietnam's former national liberation and current national construction. He noted that Vietnam's foreign policy is to be friends with all countries in order to expand the cooperation in the interest of each country, for peace, stability and development in the region and in the world. He believed that the Iran-Vietnam cooperation in the fields of economy, agriculture and trade will be constantly expanded and developed. President asked Mr. Kalantari conveyed his best regards and wishes to President Rafsanjani and thanked him for his invitation to visit Iran and wished the Iranian people prosperous and happy [sentence as received].
