Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 31 Jan (MENA)--Nabil Sha'th, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat's political advisor, arrived in Cairo this evening from Switzerland after taking part in the meeting between 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres in Davos. Upon his arrival, Sha'th stated that 'Arafat and Peres will meet in Cairo on Sunday [6 February]. He noted that the Palestinian and Israeli delegations will meet tomorrow to prepare for the 'Arafat-Peres meeting on Sunday. Sha'th expressed his belief that the Davos meeting has made some progress with respect to the difficult problems surrounding the Gaza-Jericho agreement. He said that if we reach agreement on Sunday, we will have overcome the stage of conflicts and advance to the signing of the agreement. Sha'th clarified that an international presence at the crossings has been ruled out, but it is still our right to demand a temporary international presence in Gaza for a period of one or two years.
