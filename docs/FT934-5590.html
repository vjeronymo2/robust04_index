931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Survey of Software at Work - Winter 1993 (14): Supplier/product profile Product: Pafec GIS was launched in 1991 and is currently at Level 1.1. It is an easily customisable system that has raster and vector data mix, an object oriented data structure, fast viewing and retrieval of map-related information, and graphical display of analysis of results. Suppliers: Pafec Ltd is an independent limited company formed in the UK in 1976, to develop, market and support a wide range of computer solutions for engineering and cartographic applications. Turnover: Pounds 12m for the past two years. Employees: 180 in UK, with distributors in 40 countries. Number of users: 2,500 sites worldwide Price: Modular: average first seat costs about Pounds 10,000. Hardware and operating systems: All leading Unix workstations, including HP, IBM, Sun, DEC and Silicon Graphics. Sales and support/ maintenance: 14 per cent of system cost. Other services available: Specification consultancy, post-sales customisation, consultancy and training. User views: Initial problems with data loading. 'Gets easier the more we use it'. For custom-built systems, good for handling large files. Contact: Amanda Ward, Pafec Ltd., Strelley Hall, Nottingham NG8 6PE. tel 0602 357055.
