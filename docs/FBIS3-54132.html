Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN ["YEDI'OT AHARONOT public opinion poll conducted by the Dahaf Institute, directed by Dr. Mina Tzemah"] [Text] In view of the rumors concerning Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad's deteriorating health, can Israel count on his commitments? Twenty eight percent of the interviewees gave an affirmative answer; 67 percent gave a negative answer, and 5 percent did not respond. The poll, conducted over the weekend, included 506 interviewees who constitute a representative sample of the adult population in Israel. The maximum margin of error is four percent.
