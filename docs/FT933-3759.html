930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: Directors' transactions The sales by nine LWT directors made headline news when they were announced. The sale was of more than 1m shares and raised Pounds 5.3m following the conversion of management shares into ordinary. The directors still retain considerable stakes in the company and have undertaken not to sell any further shares without exceptional cause during the course of the coming year. Berkeley Group's managing director, Anthony Pidgeley, sold 500,000 shares at 431.5p. The group is involved in private residential housebuilding and commercial property. The company has seen its share price outperform the index by some 72 per cent over the past year.
