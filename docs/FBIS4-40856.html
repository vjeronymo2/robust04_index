BFN [Text] Yerevan. [dateline as received] Price growth at the consumer market of Armenia is going on. The State Department of Statistics told SNARK the growth of foodstuff prices in May made up 65.3 percent, as compared with April. The specialists mention that the prices on bread increased more than two times, on fruit -- 1.8 times, on vegetables and potatoes -- 1.6 times. During the same period (April-May 1994) the prices on manufactured goods increased 25 percent. Thus, the prices on non-ferrous metallurgy production increased 66.6 percent, food industry -- 52.3 percent, power engineering -- 41.6 percent, industry of building materials -- 31.9 percent. The tariffs in the chemical, oil and biotechnology industries increased 2.2 times. The average growth of prices on manufactured goods in May 1994 made up 57.4 percent, as compared with last May.
