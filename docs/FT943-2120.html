940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / High Court milk hearing rescheduled The dairy trade's application to the High Court for leave for a judicial review of the government's handling of deregulation of the milk market has been rescheduled to tomorrow to allow more time for counter-arguments. It had been due to be heard yesterday. The Dairy Trade Federation said the government and Milk Marque, which is due to succeed the Milk Marketing Board on November 1, were strongly contesting its case. Commodities, Page 32
