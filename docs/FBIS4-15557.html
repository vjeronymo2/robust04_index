BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kochuk] [Text] Moscow April 8 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev denied the existence of a presidential resolution about military bases in the CIS which had appeared in the press, but said the document is being drafted. The document is being drafted and positions of CIS countries and of the "near abroad" will be (undoubtedly) taken into consideration, Kozyrev said after talks with his Columbian counterpart Nohemi Sanin Posada de Rubio. The "near abroad" includes all former Soviet republics. "The issue of deploying Russian military bases will be discussed only with those countries which display such an interest", the Russian foreign minister said.
