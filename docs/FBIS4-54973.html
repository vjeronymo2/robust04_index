BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, 9 May (TANJUG) -- The Serbian Ministry of Internal Affairs has given its permission to the Serb National Renewal to hold a rally in Belgrade's Republic Square later today to mark 9 May -- the day of victory against fascism. TANJUG has learned from Milos Vukobrat, head of the internal affairs department in the Stari Grad municipality, that this morning the Ministry of Internal Affairs accepted the Serb National Renewal appeal against the decree banning the rally. [passage omitted] According to the SNO, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, the president of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, will also address a rally scheduled for 1530 today.
