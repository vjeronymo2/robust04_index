Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 18, IRNA -- Deputy Foreign Minister for Euro-American Affairs, Mahmud Va'ezi, and the visiting Turkmen Foreign Minister, Khalykberdy Atayev, discussed the latest development in the region here Tuesday. Atayev described as fruitful the visit to Iran of Turkmen President, Saparmurad Niyazov, earlier this month and said that the visit will have significant impact on promotion of Tehran-Ashgabat ties. Va'ezi and Atayev emphasized implementation of agreements between Iran and Turkmenistan signed during Niyazov's visit. Atayev is here to take part at the seminar on the process of development in Central Asia and Caucasus which opened in Tehran on Monday [17 January].
