BFN [Text] A vessel carrying 58 people believed to be from China has docked at Christmas Island in the Indian Ocean, north of Western Australia. The 11 women and 47 men were met by Australian federal police, and immigration and customs officials when they landed. The radio messages had been intercepted by Christmas Island officials. Federal police spokesman, (Richard Crobbers), says it is believed that fishing vessel had left China about one month ago. He says they will be moved to mainland Australia within the next few days.
