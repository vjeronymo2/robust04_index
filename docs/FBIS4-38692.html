May Attend EU Corfu Meeting LD0106152794 Moscow ITAR-TASS in English 1331 GMT 1 Jun 94 LD0106152794 Moscow ITAR-TASS English BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Yuriy Kozlov and Valeriy Sevryukov] [Text] Moscow June 1 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin may visit the Greek island of Corfu (Kerkyra) in June to take part in the ceremony of signing an agreement between Russia and the European Union [EU], Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said on Wednesday. The announcement was made after his talks with Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias who is in Moscow on a three-day official visit. Relations between Russia and the EU featured prominently at the talks, Kozyrev said. He recalled that at present Greece holds presidency in the European Union and praised its support and assistance in the development of the agreement. "I think that the history of this cooperation will continue and important events may take place before Greece hands over its presidency," he said. Kozyrev made it clear that the work on the agreement may be completed before June 24 when the EU leaders gather for their meeting in Corfu.
