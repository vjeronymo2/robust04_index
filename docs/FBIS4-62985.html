BFN [Unattributed report: "The Viennese Do Not Only Engage in Waltzing"] [Text] Dr. Werner Fasslabend, the Austrian Republic's federal minister of defense, is presently in Kiev on an official visit. Yesterday [16 May] he met with Head of the Ukrainian Defense Department Vitaliy Radetskyy. The sides discussed the question of the development of cooperation in the military sphere between Ukraine and Austria, the participation of the two states in the "Partnership for Peace" Program, and their part in assisting the UN peace-keeping actions, as well as the situation in Central and Eastern Europe. On the same day, a joint Ukrainian-Austrian statement was signed. W. Fasslabend also met with Ukraine's Minister of Foreign Affairs A. Zlenko and Ukraine's Minister of Machine Building, Military-Industrial Complex, and Conversion Dmytro Chernenko. The program of the visit by the esteemed guest also envisages a visit to the Antonov Research and Development and Production Complex [ANTK] and to the Kiev Military Institute of Communications Management.
