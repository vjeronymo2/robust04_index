Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 7 KYODO -- Mitsui and Co. will supply the city of Haiphong in Vietnam with Northern Telecom Canada Ltd.'s telephone exchanges, the trading company said Monday [7 February]. The Vietnam Post and Telecommunications Ministry has put in orders for digital exchanges and optical telecommunications systems worth 6.1 million U.S. dollars, it said. Mitsui will help the Vietnamese side make the purchase. European and U.S. companies are actively entering the Vietnamese telecommunications market which is thought seen to have strong growth potential.
