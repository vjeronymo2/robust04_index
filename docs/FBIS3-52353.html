Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 1 Jan (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has sent a congratulatory cable to Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, on the national day of the sisterly state of Palestine. The cable reads: On the anniversary of the launching of the Palestinian revolution, the national day of the sisterly state of Palestine, I am pleased to extend my greetings to Your Excellency, wishing you good health and happiness and wishing the Palestinian Arab people success in achieving their national objectives. Peace be upon you.
