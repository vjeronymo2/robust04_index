941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Cameco - Contracts and Ventures Cameco, the big Canadian uranium producer, and a group of international institutions will invest Dollars 340m in a new open-pit gold mine in the former Soviet republic of Kyrgyzstan. Capacity will eventually be 500,000 ounces a year. Cameco is putting up Dollars 90m, a group of international banks led by Chase Manhattan Dollars 120m and the European Bank for Reconstruction Dollars 130m. Production starts in 1997 and reserves are sufficient for an 11-year mine life.
