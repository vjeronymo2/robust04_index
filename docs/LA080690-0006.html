August 6, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

RTD TO OFFER FREE RIDES ON TARDY BUSES

Patsaouras has got to be kidding. He wants to speed up the bus service by offering free rides if the bus is behind schedule.

How many of us have been rudely and dangerously shoved aside by a smoke-belching RTD bus controlled by an egotistical maniac?

What I foresee evolving from this dumb idea is a madhouse competition among bus drivers for their share of the glory.

Hopefully, the other board members have retained their senses and realize that things are out of control in Los Angeles. No half-baked promise will change the traffic conditions on our city streets. No half-baked promise will solve the RTD's credibility problem.

ROBERT BEALL

Encino
