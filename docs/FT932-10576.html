930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / Rise in reported race incidents POLICE REPORTS of racial incidents in south-east London more than tripled in five years to reach 811 last year. Mr Charles Wardle, home office minister, said in reply to a written question from Mr Peter Bottomley, the Tory MP for Eltham, that the figures - covering the London boroughs of Southwark, Lewisham, Bromley, Greenwich and Bexley - also show a doubling of incidents recorded between 1990 and 1992.
