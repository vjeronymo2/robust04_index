941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Martin International Martin International, the clothing manufacturer and Marks and Spencer supplier, plans to raise Pounds 5.9m through a rights issue and subscription. The fully underwritten rights issue of 12m new ordinary shares, on a 1-for-2 basis at 27p, will raise Pounds 2.9m net. A further Pounds 3m will be raised through an issue of preference shares and warrants to Throgmorton Preferred Income Trust. Proceeds will fund development and reduce core bank borrowings. The company also announced a turnround from pre-tax losses of Pounds 270,000 to profits of Pounds 267,000 for the first half of 1994. Turnover advanced to Pounds 35.2m (Pounds 33.9m) including Pounds 569,000 (Pounds 1.53m) from discontinued operations. Earnings per share emerged at 0.8p (1.6p losses) and the interim dividend is 0.45p (0.4p). Directors expect to recommend a final of at least 0.65p. The shares closed down 4 1/2 p at 30 1/2 p.
