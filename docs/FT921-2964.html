920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: CLBN secures further loan guarantees CREDIT Lyonnais, the French state-owned bank, has provided an extra Fl 3bn in loan guarantees to Credit Lyonnais Nederland Bank (CLBN), its 94 per cent owned Dutch subsidiary, Reuter reports from Rotterdam. Credit Lyonnais now stands as guarantor for nearly Fl 5bn of CLBN's debts, up from Fl 2bn in mid-1991. CLBN yesterday announced that net profits in 1991 more than halved to Fl 30.1m from Fl 63.4m the previous year. Credit Lyonnais last year provided Fl 2bn in guarantees for CLBN loans to Mr Giancarlo Parretti, the Italian financier. The Dutch bank had loans of USDollars 888m outstanding to Mr Parretti as of July 1991. The bulk financed his 1990 takeover of MGM, the US film studio which sank into financial crisis last year. Westdeutsche Landesbank Girozentrale (WestLB) said it and the Sudwest-Deutsche Landesbank Girozentrale (SudwestLB) have combined their activities in Switzerland.
