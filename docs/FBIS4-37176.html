BFN [Excerpts] President Alpha Oumar Konare, president of the Republic of Mali, and the delegation accompanying him arrived in Tripoli this evening on a visit to the Great Jamahiriyah. [passage omitted] President Alpha Oumar Konare made a statement to JANA on arrival in which he said: [Begin Konare recording, in French with superimposed Arabic translation] It is with great pleasure that I am paying this visit to fraternal Libya. It is normal that we hold consultations regularly on all problems of concern to us. I was here in Libya a few months ago and I felt all the interest and friendship that Libya and its high-ranking officials devote to Mali. It is with the will to further consolidate these relations that I am here with an important delegation. There are no taboo subjects between Libya and us, and we have a common desire to protect our countries' interests. I am convinced that this visit will further consolidate the very good relations between us. [end recording] [passage omitted]
