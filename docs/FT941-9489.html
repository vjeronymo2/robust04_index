940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UN appeals for Sudan cash The UN yesterday appealed for urgent cash for Sudan where heavy weekend fighting between rebels and government forces in the south means greater distress for more than 100,000 displaced people, Reuter reports from Nairobi. 'After a weekend of further military conflict, two UN agencies today urge the international community to provide funds immediately to assist war- and drought-affected people in Sudan,' an official said.
