930419 FT 19 APR 93 / Pay deals respond to low inflation NEARLY three quarters of pay settlements made so far this year have resulted in rises of between 2 per cent and 4.9 per cent, new figures show, Robert Taylor writes. The latest pay survey from Incomes Data Services says that negotiators in the private sector have responded to lower inflation while the government's 1.5 per cent pay limit in the public sector is holding.
