Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran's ambassador to the United Arab Emirates met with that country's minister of Islamic affairs and awqaf today. During the meeting, the UAE minister called for the expansion of mutual ties between the two Islamic and neighboring countries and announced his country's readiness for any kind of cooperation. The expansion of cultural relations and increasing of contacts between the two countries' Islamic institutions, foundations, and scholars and ways of cooperation at Islamic, regional and international sessions were also discussed.
