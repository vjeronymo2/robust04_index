940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / UK Company News: BLP surges but shares slip 16p The share price of BLP Group dipped 16p to 174p yesterday, despite the fact that pre-tax profits more than trebled for the six months to June 30. The wood laminates group reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 700,000 (Pounds 194,000) on turnover up 11 per cent from Pounds 13.5m to Pounds 15m. Operating profits doubled to Pounds 738,000 (Pounds 305,000), because of a strong performance in Canada and a reduction in costs in the UK following rationalisation of the London manufacturing operations. Earnings per share were 5.1p (2.2p losses) and an interim dividend of 1p is proposed (nil).
