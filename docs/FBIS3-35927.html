Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the Abkhazian Defense Ministry, during the past few days several assaults on Abkhazian military servicemen have been committed in the Galskiy region of the republic. A car of the Abkhazian Armed Forces was shelled near the village of Otobaya on January 29. As a result, two soldiers were wounded and one killed. Another 4 mutilated corpses of Abkhazian soldiers were found near the village of Sida. The Defense Ministry said that "all this was done by subversive groups of the Georgian Armed Forces." A statement made public in Sukhumi on Monday says that in January 1994 units of Georgian gangsters had become more active in the Galskiy region. Several crossings into the territory of Abkhazia by Georgian military groups were registered in January. According to reports from Sukhumi, as a result of subversive and terrorist acts in the past 20 days alone 25 Abkhazian servicemen and 12 civilians were killed, and about 15 soldiers wounded. The Defense Ministry stated that "it cannot tolerate such actions by Georgian group of gangsters and their supporters in the Galskiy region" and "will take decisive measures to eliminate them."
