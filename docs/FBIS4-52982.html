BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 13 (KNS-KCNA) -- A forum on human rights of Koreans in Japan sent a message on May 12 to the prime minister, the chairman of the state security committee, the director of the police agency and the chief of the Osaka prefectural police headquarters of Japan protesting the unprecedented outrages of the Japanese police authorities against the Osaka prefectural headquarters of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) and its branches. The forum in the message denounced the forcible search by the Osaka prefectural police on the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon and its branches as a wanton encroachment on the freedom and human rights of Koreans in Japan. The message strongly demanded an immediate stop to the political suppression, watch and violation of human rights of the Chongnyon organisations and Koreans in Japan.
