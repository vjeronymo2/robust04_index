Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Telephone report by Juan Sebastian for the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] [Sebastian] Here is a three-point communique that has just been issued: 1. To contribute to the prompt start of the dialogue for peace with dignity that all Mexicans want, and as a sign of the sincere willingness of the EZLN [Zapatist National Liberation Army], we hereby announce that on Wednesday, 16 February 1994, Division General Absalon Castellanos Dominguez will be released. 2. Gen. Castellanos is to be handed over to Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, and intermediary Father Samuel Luis Garcia in Guadalupe Tepeyac, Las Margaritas Municipality, Chiapas. Gen. Castellanos Dominguez's health at the time of his release must be verified by medical personnel of the International Committee of the Red Cross. 3. To contribute to detente in the conflict area during the time the dialogue for peace with dignity is held, the EZLN Clandestine Committee announces its decision to suspend war tax collection in areas under the control of our troops effective 17 February 1994. That is the communique. [Anchorman Jacabo Zabludovsky] This will happen in Guadalupe Tepeyac, but no time has been given? [Sebastian] The time was not given. The town is approximately five hours from San Cristobal de las Casas.
