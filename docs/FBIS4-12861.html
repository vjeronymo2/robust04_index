BFN [Text] Al-'Ayn, 13 Apr (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyn, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], has received a written message from Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk. The message deals with relations of cooperation and friendship between the two countries and ways of enhancing them. The message was delivered by visiting Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Yuvitoho [name as received] to Shaykh Sultan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, deputy prime minister, during their meeting today. His highness welcomed the organization of the first Ukrainian industry fair, which is currently under way in al-Sharjah. He hoped that the fair will create more prospects for cooperation between the two countries in the various fields of investment in the interest of the two countries and peoples. For his part, the Ukrainian deputy prime minister briefed his highness on a proposal by Ukraine to form a joint committee to organize cooperation and open new prospects for economic and scientific cooperation between the two countries.
