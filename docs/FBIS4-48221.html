BFN [From the "African news"] [Text] Voting scheduled for today in the three parliamentary by-elections in Togo has been postponed. An official announcement gave no reason for the postponement but said the elections would be held as soon as circumstances permitted. Reports say the technical preparations have not been completed, and there has been no campaigning. Opposition parties were declared the winners in the three constituencies in the elections last February, but the Supreme Court has ordered a revote.
