Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrade Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee and Chinese president, met with Comrade Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Workers' Party of Korea Central Committee, this morning in Beijing. During the meeting, he said that China, as always, would make every effort to continuously strengthen and develop the traditional friendship between China and Korea and strengthen exchange and cooperation between the two countries in various fields. Comrade Jiang Zemin also said that the CPC always very much treasures the traditional friendship between China and Korea and is persistently making efforts to constantly strengthen and develop the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries. He also said that the traditional friendship between the two countries, the two parties, and the two peoples was founded and fostered by leaders of the old generation of the two countries and sealed with blood by the peoples of the two countries. Comrade Hwang Chang-yop praised the brilliant achievements the Chinese people have brought about in building socialism with Chinese characteristics. He also said that the Korean people regard these achievements by the Chinese people as their own and are boundlessly pleased with them. He wished that the Chinese people would bring about greater achievements under the leadership of the CPC, with Comrade Jiang Zemin as its core. Comrade Hwang Chang-yop asked Comrade Jiang Zemin to convey President Kim Il-song's greetings to Comrade Deng Xiaoping.
