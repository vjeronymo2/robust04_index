November 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Australian David Brabham, driving cautiously in a race shortened by accidents and mechanical problems, won the the 36th Macao Formula 3 Grand Prix by 12.12 seconds over Julian Bailey of Britain. Brabham, 24, finished second in the first leg and first in the second leg in his Ralt RT33 VW Spiess.
