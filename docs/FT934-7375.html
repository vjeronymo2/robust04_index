931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICAN equities ended higher after an erratic session characterised alternately by strong demand and profit-taking after Wednesday's US approval of Nafta. The IPC index gained 17.68 at 2,166.18 after veering between 2,145.52 and 2,174.51. Some analysts expect the market to rise 30 per cent by next March.
