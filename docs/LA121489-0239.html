December 14, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

NATION;

BRADY UNDERGOES HIP SURGERY

Treasury Secretary Nicholas F. Brady successfully underwent elective surgery today to replace his arthritic right hip, the department reported.

It is uncertain when Brady will be released from the hospital, but recovery is expected to last three to six weeks, the official said. During that time, Deputy Secretary John Robson will serve as acting secretary.
