June 28, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

BARYSHNIKOV WILL TOUR U.S.

Mikhail Baryshnikov will dance in the United States for the first time this year during an 18-city tour starting Oct. 24, it was announced Wednesday.

The tour will be called the White Oak Dance Project. Dances, including two new ones, will be by modern dance choreographer Mark Morris.

The troupe will perform between Oct. 24 and Nov. 18 in Boston; Minneapolis; Madison, Wis.; Toledo, Columbus, Youngstown and Dayton, Ohio; Charleston, S.C., Savannah, Ga.; Jacksonville, Daytona Beach, Miami and Tampa, Fla., East Lansing and Detroit, Mich.; Louisville, Ky., and two cities to be announced.
