DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 1930] United States Organization for the International Telegraph and Telephone Consultative Committee (CCITT); Meeting Cancellation The United States Organization for the International Telegraph and Telephone Consultative Committee (CCITT) published notices of meetings in the Federal Register , Vol. 58, No. 218, Monday, November 15, 1993 which included a scheduled meeting on January 11, 1994. The CCITT meeting dealing with Preparations for the Kyoto Plenipotentiary scheduled for January 11, 1994 is hereby cancelled. Hereafter, these meetings will be held under the International Telecommunications Advisory Committee, which will incorporate the CCITT, upon completion of the steps necessary to establish and provide required notices in accordance with Federal Register rules and regulations. For further information, contact the Office of William Jahn (202) 647&hyph;2592. Dated: December 27, 1993. Earl S.
