931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / Queen addresses Commonwealth leaders In a lighthearted reference to Australia's move towards republican status, the Queen told Commonwealth leaders at a banquet yesterday she would 'certainly not be betting on how many of you will have the head of the Commonwealth as your head of state' in 40 years. But she was confident the Commonwealth and its ideals would survive - 'changed, adapted, maybe even weatherbeaten, but still there, a force for peace and good government.'
