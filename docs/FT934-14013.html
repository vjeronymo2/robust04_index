931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Frogmore pays Pounds 13m for shopping centre Frogmore Estates, the property investor and developer, has acquired a freehold shopping centre in Fleet, Hampshire, for Pounds 13.3m from Norwich Union. The Hart Centre consists of 45 shop units together with two stores let to Boots and WH Smith and a Waitrose supermarket. Its contracted rent roll is currently Pounds 1.55m of which 80 per cent is secured by covenants. The acquisition brings the retail content of Frogmore's investment portfolio to 36 per cent.
