BFN [Report by Cecilia Pachano; from the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] Interior Minister Ramon Escovar Salom has asserted that the government will not allow violence to seize the country. He, thus, concurred with the president who said violence will be vigorously repressed throughout the country. [Begin Escobar recording] He is bound by the Constitution and the laws of the country to guarantee public order and he is willing to comply with this obligation. I, as interior minister and as a result of my interpretation of the president's decision to implement this policy, am willing to take all necessary measures to stop violence from seizing the country. [end recording] The interior minister also stated that national authorities recognize the tricks used by foreigners to illegally enter the country in the past. He added that the government would not allow such a situation to continue and he categorically stated that he was referring to Haitians and any other foreigners.
