941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Observer: Not at liberte? Having endured months watching vast quantities of red ink haemorrhaging from the state-run French bank, staff at Credit Lyonnais apparently reckon the worst is now over. That, at least is the promising-sounding finding of an internal opinion poll, assiduously leaked to a number of French newspapers last week. But then, just how believable is a survey in which it also emerges that 89 per cent of staff purport to be 'very' or 'fairly' satisfied with the level of interest in their jobs?
