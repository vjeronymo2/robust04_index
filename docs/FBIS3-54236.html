Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, commander of the Northern Command, told our army affairs correspondent Alon Ben-David tonight that Hizballah has been trying for 24 hours to escalate the confrontation in Lebanon. [Begin recording] [Ben-David] Now that the meeting with President Clinton is over, have the Syrians let the reins loose again in Lebanon? [Mordekhay] It was anticipated that on the day the talks open in Washington they will escalate the situation and try to make Hizballah and the confrontation in Lebanon the top news item, since this has usually been the way they have acted when talks begin. I believe we have the necessary answers and the confrontation continues in the security zone. [Ben-David] Do you think that as long as the negotiations continue and no progress is made there the Syrians will continue to conduct the war in Lebanon? [Mordekhay] We believed in the past and still believe that if the Syrians want, they can significantly restrain Hizballah activities in south Lebanon against the security zone, the SLA [South Lebanese Army] and against Israel.
