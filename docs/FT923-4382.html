920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / International Company News: Cranswick buys food company Cranswick, the USM-quoted pork products-based food company, is buying FT Sutton & Son (Rossendale), a Lancashire manufacturer and wholesaler of cooked meats and fresh pork products. Initial consideration is Pounds 1m with a further payment of up to Pounds 750,000 dependent on profits. The initial payment will be met via the issue of 761,233 new shares and Pounds 22,500 cash. For the year to June 27 1992 Sutton achieved a pre-tax profit of Pounds 227,880 on sales of Pounds 3.1m. Net assets at the period end totalled Pounds 518,000.
