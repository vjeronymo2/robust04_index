Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the National Coalition Government of the Union of Burma -- dated 17 February] [Text] The National Coalition Government of the Union of Burma, NCGUB, welcomes the meeting between U.S. Congressman Bill Richardson and Daw Aung San Suu Kyi. This meeting is one of the results of pressures for reform in Burma. The NCGUB also agrees with U.S. Congressman Bill Richardson's proposal for a meeting between Daw Aung San Suu Kyi and the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC]. The NCGUB also firmly believes that the political problems in Burma can only be resolved through a comprehensive political dialogue and calls on the international community to continue pressure on the SLORC until this end is achieved.
