920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / Management: Training spotlight turns to the ladies - In A Nutshell A business initiative intended to provide free, intensive training courses for women who want to set up in business has been launched in the West Midlands. The Zillwood Trust will run nine-week training courses covering management skills, financial management, marketing, confidence-building and commercial law, followed by three weeks of vocational training. The aim is to help create between 50 and 60 new businesses a year. Contact Linda Ward, Zillwood Trust, Knightsford House, Carriers Fold, Church Road, Wombourne, Wolverhampton WV5 9EX.
