Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] A high-level delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] led by Vu Oanh, member of the CPV Central Committee Politburo and person in charge of guiding mass organizations of the party Central Committee, arrived in Vientiane this morning to attend the funeral of Phoumi Vongvichit, adviser to the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee, former member of the LPRP Central Committee Political Bureau, former acting president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], former chairman of the Lao Front for National Construction [LFNC] Central Committee, and former deputy prime minister and minister of education, sports, and religion. He passed away at age 85 at 0822 on 7 January 1994. The CPV delegation was welcomed upon its arrival at Wattai Airport by Maisouk Saisompheng, member of the party Central Committee, chairman of the LFNC Central Committee, and member of the committee in charge of organizing the funeral for Phoumi Vongvichit. Bui Van Thanh, SRV ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to the LPDR, along with a number of high-ranking cadres concerned, were also on hand to welcome Vu Oanh and his delegation at the airport.
