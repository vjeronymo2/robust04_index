June 22, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

RECYCLING WOES ARE JUST PLAIN GARBAGE

How could you print such a negative article when we are all doing our best to make recycling a success?

I cannot believe recycling can be the chore Kennedy purports it to be.

She and her nine housemates are all obviously young men and women since most of them are students. They, of all people, should be ready, willing and able to do their part in making our world a better place to live.

If we senior citizens can make the effort and take the time to recycle our papers, glass and plastic, surely the younger generation can do the same. I rest my case.

GENEVA WILLIAMS

Los Angeles
