921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Bilton rises 7% to Pounds 8.88m PRE-TAX profits of Bilton, the property investor and construction company, moved 7 per cent ahead in the six months to June 30 from Pounds 8.33m to Pounds 8.88m. Turnover from trading activities fell from Pounds 9.27m to Pounds 5.55m. Property and investment contributed more at Pounds 11.4m, against Pounds 11.1m last time, while there was a fall from Pounds 594,000 to Pounds 386,000 in construction and housing. The pre-tax result was after administrative expenses of Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 1m) and interest charges which fell from Pounds 2.32m to Pounds 1.9m. An unchanged interim dividend of 5.67p is being paid from earnings per share of 13.9p (13.1p).
