November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TALES OF JAY LENO, MR. NICE GUY

I went to the Long Beach Grand Prix with only one intention -- to see Jay Leno, who was one of the celebrity drivers. As I peered through a fence, he came out of an outhouse. I yelled out, "Hey, Jay!" He turned and did his little "chin thing" . . . funny without even saying a word.

OK -- so maybe it was a little rude to bother him at such a moment, but I sure wish that I'd had my camera!

PAM LLOYD

Long Beach
