Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri has said all the signs mean that Israel is seeking war, not peace. He added that Israel is behind the Sayyidat al-Najat Church explosion in Zuq and emphasized that the al-Ibrahimi Msoque massacre in Hebron is against the region's peace process. He noted that the Arabs are facing real terrorism and that peace cannot be achieved unilaterally. He said the bloodshed in Palestine and south Lebanon proves Israel's failure to make peace.
