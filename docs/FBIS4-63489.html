BFN [Report by UKRINFORM-TASS correspondent Lev Ryabchikov] [Text] Simferopol 20 May TASS -- At its evening meeting on Friday [20 May] the Crimean parliament decided to suspend on the territory of the peninsula the Ukrainian president's decrees "on departments of Justice of the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine" and "on the Main Department of the Security Service in the Crimea." Deputies adopted this decision on the basis of the restored constitution of the Republic of the Crimea of 6 May 1992. A similar resolution concerning the Ukrainian president's decree on the main interior department of the Crimea under the Interior Ministry of Ukraine was adopted by deputies of the Crimean parliament on 19 May this year.
