BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- A high-level delegation of the Ministry of National Defence of Laos headed by Sai-ngason Choummali, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and minister of national defence, left Pyongyang today, wrapping up its six-day visit to Korea. It was seen off at the airport by Politburo member of the C.C., the Workers' Party of Korea and Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army [KPA] Choe Kwang, Vice-Marshal Kim Kwang-chin, and generals and officers of the KPA. A farewell function for the delegation took place at the airport. While staying in Korea, the delegation paid visits to Mangyongdae, the Tower of Chuche Idea, the Grand People's Study House, KPA units, the victorious fatherland liberation war museum and other places.
