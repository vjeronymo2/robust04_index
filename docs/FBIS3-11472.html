Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the Joyce Pascowitch social column] [Text] When he assumes as the new OAS general secretary, [Colombian President] Cesar Gaviria Trujillo will be able to relax, knowing he is protected from the Colombian drug cartels. Once he assumes the post he will be provided the security to which all OAS general secretaries are entitled, but this time supplied by the U.S. Government.
