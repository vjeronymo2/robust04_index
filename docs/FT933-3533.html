930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / Trichet departure prompts financial administration reshuffle Mr Trichet's departure from the head of the Treasury, has prompted a reshuffle at the top of France's financial administration. His post is to be taken by Mr Christian Noyer, the director of Mr Alphandery's private office, John Ridding reports. Mr Noyer, 42, has a similar background to Mr Trichet, who is 50. Both are graduates of the elite Ecole Nationale d'Administration and both have spent most of their careers in the financial administration. Mr Noyer will be replaced in the private office of Mr Alphandery by Mr Patrice Vial. Mr Vial, previously a chief forecaster in the economics ministry, is currently director general of Banque Pallas-Stern, the French merchant bank.
