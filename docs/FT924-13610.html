921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Khmer Rouge warned by UN THE UN Security Council adopted a resolution yesterday warning Khmer Rouge guerrillas that elections for Cambodia will go ahead as scheduled, whether or not they participate, Reuter reports from New York. The document, adopted by a unanimous vote, sets in motion a last-chance series of diplomatic moves to convince the Khmer Rouge to disarm and join the election process by November 15 or face further unspecified action.
