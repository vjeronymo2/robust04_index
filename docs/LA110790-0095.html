November 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

UPS Orders 25 Boeing Jets: United Parcel Service Inc. has ordered 25 Boeing 757 jet freighters.

Boeing Commercial Airplane Group said the total value of the order, including spare parts, is $1.7 billion. Fifteen of the orders were converted from options that UPS previously held for the twin-engine, mid-sized jets. UPS has 55 757PFs in its fleet or on order and holds options for an additional 41.
