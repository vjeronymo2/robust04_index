BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA) -- Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today received 'Umar al-Muntasir, secretary of the Libyan General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison "foreign minister." After the meeting, al-Muntasir said they discussed the Libyan-Western dispute and events in the Arab world, particularly the Yemeni crisis and the Arab and international efforts to end the fighting in that country. Al-Muntasir added that Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid informed him that the nonaligned foreign ministers with whom he met on the sidelines of the nonaligned conference voiced support for Libya's stance on its dispute with the West, particularly as Libya has been responsive to all international demands. 'Abd-al-Majid said the Arab League wishes to solve the Libyan-Western dispute in an internationally legal framework while at the same time safeguarding Libyan sovereignty.
