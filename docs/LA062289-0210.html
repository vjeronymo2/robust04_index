June 22, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

N. HOLLYWOOD LOTTO TICKET A WINNER

An unidentified player who purchased a ticket in North Hollywood correctly guessed all six numbers Wednesday night in the California Lottery's Lotto 6/49 game and is eligible for a pool of $4.9 million, lottery officials said today.

Four players picked five numbers plus the bonus number and will divide a prize pool of $1.3 million. Those tickets were sold in Fontana, Glendora, Long Beach and Santa Barbara. Players with five of six will share $700,000; four of six will share $636,000. Three of six is worth an automatic $5 per winner.
