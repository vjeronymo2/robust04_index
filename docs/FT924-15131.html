921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG fell in line with overseas markets but gold shares ended firmer as the financial rand weakened against the dollar. The overall index lost 66 or 2.1 per cent to 3,114 and industrials 78 to 4,103. The gold index advanced 15 to 902 with Vaal Reefs up R6.50 at R136.00.
