BFN [Text] Baghdad, 1 Jun (INA)--His Excellency President Saddam Husayn, secretary of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, today presided over a meeting of the party's Baghdad branch commands. Sa'di Mahdi Salih and 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, members of the Iraq Command of the Ba'th Party, were in attendance.
