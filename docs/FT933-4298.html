930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Cakebread Robey achieves Pounds 106,000 Cakebread Robey & Co returned to profit in the first half to June 30 with Pounds 106,000 before tax, against a loss of Pounds 667,000 in the previous first half. Sales were Pounds 8.87m against Pounds 11.4m. Cost controls helped produce operating profits of Pounds 155,000 (Pounds 58,000 loss). Earnings per share came through at 1.8p compared with losses of 11.1p. The directors decided that it was not yet time to reinstate dividend payments.
