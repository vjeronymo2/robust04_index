BFN [By UKRINFORM for TASS] [Text] Kiev April 1 TASS -- A resolution of the Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers was issued here today to start the construction and social facilities in the Russian Federation and Ukraine in payment for natural gas delivered by the Russian Gazprom joint stock society. The resolution instructs the Ukrainian State Committee for Oil and Gas and the Ukrstroi Corporation to start the design and complete construction of housing and social facilities in Russia and Ukraine in 1994. Apartment houses and social facilities will be built in the town of Urengoi and the settlements of Mortka, Mezhdurechye and Kuminsky in 1994-1996. Similar facilities with an area of 20,000 square meters will be annually built in the towns of Tobolsk and Surgut. The resolution enumerates other facilities to be built in populated localities of the Stavropol and Krasnodar territories and the Lipetsk region of Russia. The State Committee for Oil and Gas will pay the work done by the Ukrstroi Corporation with proceeds from gas sales to Ukrainian consumers.
