April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DIANNE FEINSTEIN

I read and enjoyed every word of the Feinstein article, but I was flabbergasted that such a large article didn't contain a word about the most important event in San Francisco since 1908 -- the recent earthquake.

In spite of your overuse of that four-letter word lazy, I think she's the gal for the job.

EARL S. DRAIMIN

Van Nuys
