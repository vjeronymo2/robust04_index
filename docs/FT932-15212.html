930415 FT 15 APR 93 / World Hews in Brief: Briton dies in avalanche Briton Olivia Marchington, 22, from Didmarton, Gloucestershire, was killed by an avalanche while skiing off piste with friends near the resort of Meribel in the French Alps, police said. Meanwhile, intensive searches failed to find any trace of four British ski-mountaineers missing in the Caucasus mountains since Friday.
