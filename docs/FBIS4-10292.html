BFN [From the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] Members of peasant, Indian, and mass organizations from various states marched today from the Altar to the Fatherland Monument to the Zocalo to mark the 75th anniversary of the death of General Emiliano Zapata. The march, which began at 1110 and ended at 1420, was held without incident. Walls along the route were painted with social demands. The march passed along important Mexico City avenues such as Reforma, Insurgentes, and Juarez. The police reported that some 300 officers were deployed along the route to maintain order and divert traffic.
