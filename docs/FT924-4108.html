921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Scottish spending 'to rise by Pounds 1bn' Government spending in Scotland in the coming financial year would total Pounds 1bn more than in 1992-93, Mr Ian Lang, Scottish secretary (above) told MPs yesterday. In spite of 'tight' limits on public expenditure, a net sum of Pounds 13.45bn would be available - the largest-ever annual allocation to the Scottish office. He said the allocation would help deliver economic growth and create jobs
