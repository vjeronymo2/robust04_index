BFN [Text] Tehran, April 14, IRNA -- The visiting Vietnamese deputy foreign minister, Nguyen Dy Nien here today called for expansion of Tehran-Hanoi cooperation in the international forums. In a meeting with Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, he expressed the satisfaction of Vietnam's high-ranking officials with expansion of relations with Tehran and hoped that the foundations of direct relations between the two countries would strengthen with the imminent visit of Vietnam's president to Tehran. Velayati in response referred to the historical background of Vietnamese people against the occupiers and domineering powers and said that Iran attached special importance to its relations with Hanoi. He voiced Iran's readiness to host the Vitenamese president and hoped that the visit would have positive and fruitful results in expansion of bilateral relations. Nien will deliver a message from Vietnam's president to President Hashemi-Rafsanjani.
