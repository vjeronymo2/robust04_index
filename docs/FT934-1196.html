931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / North Sea oil output to rise The rise in North Sea oil and gas output - a factor in falling oil prices - will be boosted this week when the East Brae field off Scotland comes on stream. The first oil should flow today or tomorrow, according to Marathon Oil, operator of the field. Investment in the field totals Pounds 867m. Output from the UK sector of the North Sea was 2.43m b/d in November, compared with 2.13m b/d a year ago. Production will rise next year as new fields approach peak flows.
