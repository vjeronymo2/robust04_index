BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengis Pachkoria] [Text] Tbilisi April 7 TASS -- The Georgian Defence Ministry issued a statement today accusing separatist Abkhazia of resuming hostilities in the Kodor Gorge, high in the Caucasus mountains, on Wednesday. Despite a recent Moscow-brokered truce accord, Abkhazians have delivered an artillery strike on the village of Kvabchara, the press centre of the ministry said adding that the Georgian side abiding by the truce accord did not fire back.
