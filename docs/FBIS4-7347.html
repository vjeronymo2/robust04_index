BFN ["Conferences of Party Cell Secretaries at Provinces, Cities, Counties and Complexes" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- Conferences of party cell secretaries took place in provinces, cities, counties and complexes after the national conference of party cell secretaries was held in Pyongyang. The letter of congratulations sent by the great leader President Kim Il-song to the delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries was conveyed to the conferences. Reports and speeches were made at the conferences. The conferences laid stress on carrying through the highly important tasks laid down in the letter of congratulations sent by the great leader to the national conference of party cell secretaries and in the letter "Let Us Strengthen the Party Cells" sent by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to the participants in the national short-course of the party cell secretaries, in order to make the party cells militant organisations which are unfailingly loyal to the party and the leader and defend and add lustre to the cause of socialism. The conferences of party cell secretaries fully demonstrated the revolutionary faith and will to accomplish the revolutionary cause of chuche with credit by enhancing the militant function and role of the party cells, the lowest basic organisations of the party, and steadily strengthening and developing our party into an invincible revolutionary party. The conferences adopted letters of pledge to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il.
