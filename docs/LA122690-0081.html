December 26, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

TOLLWAY AGENCY MEMBERS CHOSEN

Two City Council members have been appointed to the Transportation Corridor Agencies that are building three tollways in eastern and southern areas of the county.

Former Mayor William S. Craycraft was selected by the council to represent the city on the Foothill-Eastern Transportation Corridor Agency.

Recently elected Councilwoman Sharon Cody will sit on the board of the San Joaquin Hills Transportation Corridor Agency.

The tollways, expected to cost about $2 billion combined, are scheduled for completion in the mid- to late 1990s. FRANK MESSINA
