Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mersin, March 15 (AA) -- Unidentified people threw a firebomb at the ruling major coalition True Path Party (DYP) party's election office in southern Mersin Province, it was reported on Monday [14 March]. Two people were wounded in the fire that erupted after the blast. AA reporter was told that the incident took place in the afternoon and the fire was extinguished by neighbors. An investigation was started, police said.
