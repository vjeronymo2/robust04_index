940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / International Company News: Czech brewer in fundraising Prazske Pivovary, the fourth-largest Czech brewer, is to raise up to Kc3bn (Dollars 107m) to fund a five-year modernisation of its three brewing divisions and expand overseas sales of its leading brand. The brewer, in which Bass of the UK has a 34 per cent stake, expects to tap the Czech capital markets for the funds.
