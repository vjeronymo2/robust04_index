BFN [Text] Technical and engineering teams in Karbala' Governorate have completed a campaign for maintaining and repairing a number of medical and electrical equipment, as well as machinery belonging to health departments in the governorate. The campaign involved the maintenance of 25 electric washing machines in al-Husayni, Maternity, and al-Hindiyah Hospitals, seven ambulances, 785 beds, and 55 medicine cupboards. The campaign also involved maintenance of internal doors in 10 health centers. All this was done according to the Iraqi method of prudence.
