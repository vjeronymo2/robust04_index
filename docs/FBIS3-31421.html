Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States is examining the possibility of sending antiaircraft missiles to Hungary, an English language paper in Budapest writes. A BUDAPEST SUN reporter quotes the former American representative of the NATO military committee, who talked about this at a Washington conference. According to a NEPSZAVA article, Rudolf Joo, state secretary at the Defense Ministry, and Ivan Baba, deputy state secretary in the Foreign Ministry, also participated in the consultations, and they confirmed that Hungary asked for American antiaircraft missiles and radar equipment, as it is feared that the Yugoslav war might spread.
