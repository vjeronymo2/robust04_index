BFN [Text] The first national day on "Prevention and Control of Iodine-Deficient Diseases" fell on 5 May. On 5 May, more than 600 people in Changchun took to the streets to conduct the propaganda on prevention and control of iodine-deficient diseases. Six cars were mobilized to help conduct the propaganda. More than 30 propaganda centers were set up on the streets. More than 100,000 copies of propaganda bills were distributed. Papers carrying more than 100 propaganda items were published. More than 150 banners with such slogans were carried. It was informed that our province is one of the areas with serious iodine-deficient diseases. The first general investigation made in 1978 showed that 58 counties (cities) in the province had areas with iodine-deficient diseases, 750,000 people or 5.6 percent of the people in the province suffered endemic goiter, about 70 percent of the people in the villages where these diseases were seriously spread suffered the diseases, and 0.22 percent of the people suffered cretinism. So far, our province has 270,000 patients suffering endemic goiter and more than 2,000 patients suffering cretinism.
