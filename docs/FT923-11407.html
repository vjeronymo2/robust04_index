920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Exmoor Dual net asset value lower Exmoor Dual Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 63.2p at May 31 compared with 93.6p a year earlier. Values for the income shares and zero coupon preference shares were 60.7p (64.2p) and 158.4p (140.1p) respectively. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 558,975 (Pounds 697,317) for earnings per income share of 6.34p (7.9p). The third interim dividend is unchanged at 2.7p.
