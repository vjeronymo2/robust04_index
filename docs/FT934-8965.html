931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Densitron denies expansion in Japan Mr David Ling, finance director Densitron International, the electronic components manufacturer, said yesterday the company had 'no specific' plans to expand into Japan. Earlier, speculation that the company was planning to make a significant acquisition in the country had lifted the company's share price by 6p to 36p. Mr Ling said he knew of no other reason for the rise in the share price.
