BFN [Text] In an expression of their patriotic sentiments, and to contribute to the efforts to safeguard Yemeni unity, and protect national sovereignty, the brother members of the Yemeni community in the fraternal State of Qatar have launched a fundraising campaign among Yemeni expatriates in Qatar to aid the war effort and support the national endeavors of the Yemeni people's masses throughout the homeland to defend Yemeni unity and protect the homeland's sovereignty and independence.
