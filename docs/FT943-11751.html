940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / International Company News: Fletcher profit grows by 70% Fletcher Challenge Canada, the integrated forest products group, posted a fourth-quarter net profit of CDollars 10.4m (USDollars 7.6m), or 8 cents a share, up 70 per cent from a year earlier, on sales of CDollars 462m, a rise of 5.5 per cent, writes Robert Gibbens. Full-year profit was CDollars 26.4m, or 21 cents, including a special CDollars 62m gain, against a loss of CDollars 24.6m, or 26 cents.
