January 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

AT&T May Seek Mutual-Aid Pact: AT&T, embarrassed by last week's massive long-distance network failure, is considering asking its competitors, MCI and US Sprint, for a mutual-aid agreement to prevent similar nationwide shutdowns. AT&T officials say they are still only studying a possible pact, but that a proposal could be ready this week. MCI and US Sprint said they are willing to consider the idea.
