940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Clayhithe sells Laser-Scan stake Clayhithe, the finance and management provider, has sold its 29.9 per cent stake in Laser-Scan Holdings to the Causeway Smaller Quoted Companies Fund. Net proceeds amounted to about Pounds 1.15m. The disposal was in line with Clayhithe's policy of realising non-core investments.
