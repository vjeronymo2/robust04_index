931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / London Stock Exchange: Virtuality excels A sparkling debut from Virtuality, the specialist software producer, saw the shares zoom to a premium of 119p over the listing price of 170p. The shares closed at 289p with turnover reaching 5.7m, more than one third of yesterday's placing. Dealers said the shares, which went to around 80 institutions, moved to an immediate subtantial premium before gathering momentum during the session. They hit a bid high of 315p in the afternoon before coming back at the close. Virtuality's strong showing carries on the successful run of market debuts. Last week, Parkdean Leisure went to a 6 per cent premium on its first day. It closed a penny ahead yesterday at 133p, up 13 from its float price.
