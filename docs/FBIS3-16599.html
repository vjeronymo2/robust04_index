Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Law on restrictions on the implementation of amnesty acts; date of issue not given -- read by announcer] [Text] With the aim of strengthening the struggle against the criminal acts committed against the Azerbaijani Republic's sovereignty and territorial integrity, to ensure real accountability of people involved in such crimes, and to improve the country's defensive capability, the Azerbaijani National Assembly decides that: The application of the amnesty acts is hereby banned in the case of people involved in crimes connected with the illegal introduction of the Soviet troops into Baku on 19-20 January 1990, the massacre in Khodzhaly, and the occupation of the Azerbaijani Republic's territory by the Armenian Armed Forces. [signed] Geydar Aliyev, president of Azerbaijan.
