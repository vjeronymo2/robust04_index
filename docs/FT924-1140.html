921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Wassall wins 0.23% Evode ordinary Wassall, the mini-conglomerate bidding Pounds 94.3m in cash for Evode, had won acceptances from only 0.23 per cent of the chemicals and plastics group's ordinary shareholders and 0.33 per cent of its convertible preference holders by the first closing date. Wassall already owns 3.51 per cent of Evode's shares, bought when it launched the bid last month. The offer has been extended to January 11.
