June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Two-time Olympic gold medalist Roger Kingdom and 1984 silver medalist Danny Harris gave Americans a sweep of the men's hurdles at an international track and field meet in Madrid, Spain.

Kingdom, the world record-holder in the 110-meter high hurdles with a time of 12.92 seconds, won in 13.60, running into a strong wind. Harris won the 400 in 47.77.
