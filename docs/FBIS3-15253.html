Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow March 2 TASS -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin is meeting Uzbek President Islam Karimov in Moscow to discuss economic cooperation between Russia and Uzbekistan. After their one-to-one meeting, the talks will continue on an extended basis. They will be also attended by Uzbek Prime Minister Abdulkhasim Mutalov. The sides are expected to sign a package of bilateral agreements, including an agreement on the principles of logistics support for the armed forces, on air transportation cooperation and an agreement to avoid double taxation. Russia and Uzbekistan also plan to sign a consular convention, a program of cooperation between the foreign ministries in 1994, an agreement on cooperation between Russian and Uzbek customs offices and mutual assistance in preventing the smuggling of cultural values and on customs inspection of personal baggage.
