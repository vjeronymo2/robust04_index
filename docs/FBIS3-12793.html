Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A well informed Palestinian source in Tunis last night confirmed that Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, the head of the PLO Information Department would arrive in Paris today to hold talks with French officials regarding the conditions of resuming Israeli-Palestinian economic talks, halted since the Hebron massacre. This is within the framework of what the PLO Executive Committee decided in its recent meetings regarding sending envoys to Western countries to explain its [PLO] conditions for resuming talks with Israel on the implementation of the agreement on Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. A French Foreign Ministry spokeswoman said yesterday that France, who is chairing the [UN] Security Council this month, was hoping for the Security Council's approval of a resolution very soon. The spokeswoman raised the [issue of the] need for the immediate implementation by the Israeli authorities of the measures taken against the settlers and those to the benefit of the Palestinians.
