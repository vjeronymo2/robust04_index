931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Amicable Smaller may increase size Directors of Amicable Smaller Enterprise Trust said they were considering, with their advisers, a placing and offer of additional shares to increase the size of the trust. Any raising of additional capital will be structured to ensure no dilution of the net asset value of existing shares. The offer will be made available to all existing shareholders, including those holding shares through the Scottish Amicable personal equity plans.
