940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / F&C German lifts net asset value 24% Foreign & Colonial German Investment Trust reported a 24 per cent advance in net asset value per share, from 121.5p to 150.9p, over the year to March 31. Fully diluted for exercise of warrants, the figure improved 20 per cent, from 123.8p to 148.4p. The benchmark FT-A Germany Index rose just over 21 per cent over the same period, while the FT-A Austria Index jumped 28.7 per cent. The trust, which aims for long term capital growth, reported reduced attributable revenue of Pounds 219,000 (Pounds 321,000), equivalent to earnings of 0.56p (0.82p) per share. Nevertheless, the recommended single distribution is maintained at 0.5p.
