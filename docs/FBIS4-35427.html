BFN [Text] The People's Assembly Arab Affairs Committee expressed its deep regret for the unacceptable provocative measures that the Sudanese Government has recently adopted against Egyptian property and nationals in Sudan. In a statement it has issued, the committee noted that these measures are just part of the unacceptable actions that have become a characteristic of the ruling Sudanese regime because of the internal and external crises gripping it. The committee affirmed that Egypt cannot be provoked and deeply cares for the interests of the Sudanese people and the close relations between the two peoples, which no irresponsible behavior can harm.
