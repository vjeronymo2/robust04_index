940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cattle's Holdings CATTLE'S HOLDINGS, the financial services company, has acquired Reedham Factors for Pounds 5.9m, of which Pounds 3m is payable in cash and the balance in loan notes. Further payment of up to Pounds 0.3m may become due depending on Reedham's performance in the period to March 31 1995.
