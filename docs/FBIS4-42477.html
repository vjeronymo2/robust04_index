BFN [Text] Geneva, June 3 (AFP) -- The United Nations Friday [3 June] warned it would cancel scheduled peace talks on Saturday if Bosnian Serb forces did not fully withdraw from around Gorazde in eastern Bosnia. Talks on a truce should have begun here Thursday, but Bosnian Moslems refused to attend so long as Bosnian Serbs continued to occupy the UN-declared exclusion zone around Gorazde from which they had promised to pull out.
