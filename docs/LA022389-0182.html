February 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

GARY COLE GETS LEAD ROLE IN NBC MOVIE

Gary Cole, star of NBC's "Midnight Caller," will turn up in another NBC program March 6. He's got the lead role in "Those She Left Behind," a TV movie about a businessman whose wife dies, leaving him to care for their newborn child. Joining him in the cast are Joanna Kerns (of "Growing Pains"), Colleen Dewhurst and Mary Page Keller.
