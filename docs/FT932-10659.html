930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: London American capital repayment London American Ventures Trust is recommending implementation of the reduction of capital proposed in the circular of November 1991. Subject to approval that would result in the repayment of 25 per cent of ordinary shareholders' funds, redemption of the debentures and repayment of preference shares. Following Court sanction, payment would be expected in July. Net asset value per ordinary share rose to 74.4p, from 70.9p a year earlier. In 1992-93 total income was Pounds 718,000 (Pounds 780,000) but there was a net loss of Pounds 14,000 (Pounds 55,000). Losses per ordinary share were halved to 0.08p.
