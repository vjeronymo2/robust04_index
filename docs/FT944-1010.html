941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Herald Observer Herald Observer Newspapers has been bought out by Standard Newspapers, a new company, in a Pounds 1.8m deal backed by Gresham Trust. Standard was formed by Mr Chris Bullivant, who is credited with founding the UK's free newspaper industry in the 1970s. Midlands-based Herald was itself a joint venture between Mr Bullivant and the Tamworth Herald Company.
