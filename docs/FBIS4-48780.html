BFN [Text] The foreign ministry today stated that Ethiopia has accepted the request of the UN to send her troops to Rwanda. Ethiopia accepted the request in accordance with the UN Security Council's resolution No. 918 on 17th May 1994, asking all member states to send troops toward the UN peacekeeping force in Rwanda, which was passed to Ethiopia through the secretary general of the organization. The ministry of foreign affairs added that Ethiopia has acknowledged her acceptance of the request in a letter written to Mr. Butrus-Butrus Ghali dated 20th May 1994.
