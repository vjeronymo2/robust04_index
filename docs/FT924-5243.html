921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / UK Company News: N American Gas lifts asset value North American Gas Investment Trust had a net asset value of 70.7p per share at October 31 against 68.6p a year earlier. Net revenue for the first quarter amounted to Pounds 100,000 (Pounds 121,000) for earnings per share of 0.29p against 0.34p.
