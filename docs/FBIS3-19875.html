Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar. 22 (A.A.) -- Turkey and Kuwait held political consultations here Monday [21 March]. A statement by the Foreign Ministry said the talks led by the deputy foreign ministers of both countries were conducted in a positive and constructive atmosphere. Bilateral, regional, and global political developments were taken up in the talks as well as the recent developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina, the Caucasus, and Middle East since last June when the consultations were held. Also during the talks Turkey reportedly stressed the importance of Iraq to take its place in the international community by abiding by the UN Security Council resolutions. Information was given to the Kuwaiti side on the losses Turkey has suffered because of the embargo imposed on Iraq. The Kuwaiti side expressed their government's desire to increase investments in Turkey and to remove double taxation.
