BFN [Excerpt] Brussels (DPA) -- Germany plans to ratify the membership treaties of Austria, Sweden, Finland, and Norway with the European Union [EU] before the Bundestag elections in October. This was stated by Ursula Seiler-Albring, state minister in the Foreign Ministry in Bonn, in Brussels on Monday [16 May]. During a break in the meeting of the EU foreign ministers, the state minister stressed that Germany thus wants to make sure that the four new members will be accepted as of 1 January 1995 in any case.
