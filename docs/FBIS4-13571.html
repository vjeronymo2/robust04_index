BFN [Text] Ukraine has sought India's assistance in setting up medium and small-scale industrial complex. Addressing a meeting organized by the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry, FICCI, in New Delhi today, the visiting Ukrainian foreign minister, Mr. Anatoliy Zlenko, said his country has many heavy industries, but it lacks the know-how to run medium and small-scale industries successfully as India does. He said the three agreements that have been signed between the two countries on economic, scientific, technical, and cultural cooperation will go a long way in boosting the traditional ties between the two countries. Mr. Zlenko also announced that the Ukrainian chambers and FICCI would set up a joint business committee to promote business between the two nations. Later, the visiting minister called on the president.
