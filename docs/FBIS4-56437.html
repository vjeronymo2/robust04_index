BFN [Editorial Report] Paris AFP in Spanish at 1730 GMT on 26 April reports that despite the overall increase in exports, sales to Asia and members of NAFTA decreased by 2 percent during the first quarter of the year compared to the same period in 1993. Exports to the European Union increased by 20 percent, and exports to Central America, Costa Rica's third-largest trading partner, increased by more than 7 percent higher than during the first quarter of 1993.
