940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / Assam separatists free hostage Militants fighting for an autonomous Bodoland within the state of Assam yesterday released Mr B. Bordoloi, a senior manager of Tata Tea, India's buggest plantation company, after holding him hostage for nearly 11 months, writes Kunal Bose in Calcutta. His release was unconditional.
