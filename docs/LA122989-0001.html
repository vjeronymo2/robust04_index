December 29, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: BOOKS WANTED FOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

The city of Compton is asking people to donate books that will be given to high school students in early January in commemoration of Martin Luther King Jr.'s birthday on Jan. 15.

Books may be dropped off at the lobby of Compton's City Hall at 205 S. Willowbrook Ave. through next Friday. They will be distributed to students from Jan. 8 to Jan. 15. All kinds of books, especially those about King and African-American history and literature, are welcome.
