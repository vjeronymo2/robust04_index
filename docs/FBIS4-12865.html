BFN [Text] Brother Hamid Yusuf Hammadi, minister of culture and information in the fraternal Republic of Iraq and personal envoy of brother Saddam Husayn, president of the Republic of Iraq, arrived in Sanaa this evening on a working visit to our country. He was received by Information Minister Hasan al-Lawzi, representative of the protocols of the Presidential Council, brother Dr. Muhsin Khalil, ambassador of the Republic Of Iraq to our country, and a number of embassy staff members.
