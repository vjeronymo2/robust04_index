BFN [Text] Industry Minister Eduardo Ache on 25 March fired Industry General Director Andres Merino Pacheco because he said he would resign if the government approved last week's gasoline price increase. Sources close to Merino Pacheco told EL OBSERVADOR that he sought to obtain "political profit" inside his faction and this annoyed Minister Ache. Merino Pacheco is a nephew of Jorge Pacheco Areco, Colorado Party and Bettleist Union leader. He was also one of former Industry Minister Carlos Piran's trusted men during the previous government. Merino Pacheco will be replaced by current Deputy Secretary General Santiago Urioste, of the Herreraist faction [National Party faction].
