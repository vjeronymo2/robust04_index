BFN [Text] Amman-AKHIR KHABAR--Instructed by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat, PLO Executive Committee member Yasir 'Amr left for Damascus for talks with Syrian officials following the increase in tension between Damascus and the PLO. Sources close to the PLO said 'Amr's mission is to try to ease tension and stop the media campaigns Damascus is launching against the PLO leadership as a result of the signing of the Oslo and Cairo agreements, which Damascus views as deviation from unified Arab position in the Arab-Israeli negotiations. The sources said 'Amr will brief Damascus about what is going on in Cairo, and will discuss the cordon states meeting and the possibility for the PLO to participate in it. 'Amr was a member of the Syrian Arab Socialist Ba'th Party during the 1960's and 1970's, and he was a former member of the party's Regional Command. The visit is taking place at a time when the PLO leadership is facing a real crisis as a result of Israeli procrastination and blackmailing in Cairo talks.
