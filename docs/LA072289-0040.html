July 22, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

SDNB Financial Corp., parent of San Diego National Bank, said its second-quarter profit was $230,000, up from $54,000 over the same three months last year. For the six months ended June 30, profit was $446,000, contrasted with $232,000 over the same two quarters in 1988. Assets as of June 30 were $161.8 million, up 5% from $154.6 million a year previous.
