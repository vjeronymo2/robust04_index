920222 FT 22 FEB 92 / Finance & The Family: Financial Times A-Z of Personal Finance Too often, ordinary investors are offered financial products they do not understand, by people whose motives they understandably suspect. The FINANCIAL TIMES A-Z of PERSONAL FINANCE, published with next Friday's paper and repeated with Saturday's, cuts an alphabetic swathe through the money jungle, from Annuities to Zero-dividend securities, stripping away the jargon to offer a down-to-earth guide to many types of product.
