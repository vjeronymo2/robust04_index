Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 13 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Cyprien Ntaryamira upon his assumption of office as president of the Republic of Burundi. Wishing him success in his work for the promotion of national reconciliation and stability and for the prosperity of the country, the message expressed the belief that friendly relations between the two countries would continue developing.
