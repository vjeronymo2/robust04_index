February 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JORDAN HASN'T PAID DUES TO EARN ALL-TIME HONORS

To rate a place on the NBA all-time starting team, a player must be impressive on several counts -- including longevity, statistics and winning.

Two of Scott Ostler's recent all-time picks, Magic Johnson and Larry Bird, are approaching serious all-time consideration.

However, Michael Jordan, another of Ostler's selections, has less than four full seasons in the NBA. So far, Jordan is a flash in the pan in comparison to Jerry West and Oscar Robertson.

MARTIN MARKOVICH

Los Angeles
