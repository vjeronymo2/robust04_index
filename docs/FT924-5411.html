921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / Technology: The nose always knows best - Worth Watching Cyrano de Bergerac may well have approved of the latest instrument for assessing air quality - the human nose. Researchers at TNO, the Netherlands organisation for applied research, is investigating the use of trained 'sniffing' panels to supplement chemical and physical measurement systems when detecting sick buildings. The organisation expects to have its first panels working within 12 months. TNO: Netherlands, 15 694975.
