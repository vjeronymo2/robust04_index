930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / International Company News: NZ fish farmer launches bid for rival SALMOND Smith Biolab yesterday launched a shares and cash NZDollars 28m (USDollars 15.4m) takeover bid for rival New Zealand fish farmer, Regal Salmon. The bid follows a fall in the Regal Salmon share price following a turnround in earnings from a NZDollars 2m profit last year to a NZDollars 3.3m loss in the year ended March. Regal Salmon shares have fallen from a high of NZDollars 2.60 late last year to around 80 cents prior to the bid.
