920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / Azerbaijan denies accord on talks Azerbaijan yesterday denied it had agreed to tripartite peace talks with Armenia and representatives from Nagorno-Karabakh, showing the two republics to be as far apart as ever on the Transcaucasian enclave, writes Leyla Boulton in Moscow. But last night Mr Mahmud Vaezi, the Iranian deputy foreign minister, said after a shuttle diplomacy mission that a meeting would be held early next week with negotiators from Armenia, Azerbaijan, Iran and Russia.
