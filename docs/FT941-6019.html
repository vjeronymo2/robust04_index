940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Interest rates hit Stat-Plus Stat-Plus Group, the legal stationer, blamed lower interest rates for a 5 per cent drop in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.61m to Pounds 3.42m for 1993. Although gross margins had been maintained and overheads reduced, enabling operating profits to advance 14 per cent to Pounds 2.58m (Pounds 2.26m), net interest income fell from Pounds 1.35m to Pounds 834,000. Bank balances increased from Pounds 13.9m to Pounds 14.2m. Turnover rose to Pounds 11.7m (Pounds 11.4m). Earnings per share were 10.7p (11.3p) while a final dividend of 4.8p lifts the total to 8.8p (8p).
