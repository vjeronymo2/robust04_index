940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Hobson shares suspended Shares in Hobson, the household products and commodities trading company, were suspended yesterday at 27p, up 1/2 p, the highest level for the past 12 months, pending a further announcement about a large acquisition. At the end of January, with the announcement of its interim results, the USM-traded company said it was negotiating for the purchase of a 'very substantial business'.
