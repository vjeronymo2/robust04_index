Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 21 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam had lunch with the chairmen of 30 leading business groups and the heads of economic organizations at Chongwadae [presidential office] on Friday and called on them to make efforts to help the government in its primary task this year of revitalizing the economy. To increase competitiveness, industrial peace is absolutely necessary and business groups should do their best for smooth industrial relations, Kim said. He encouraged the business leaders to expand exports by making ambitious investments and developing technology, and he called on them not to raise prices. Among the lunch guests were Yi Kon-hui of Samsung, Chong Se-yong of Hyundai, Kim U-chung of Daewoo, Ku Cha-kyong of Lucky-Goldstar, Choe Chong-hyon of Sunkyong, Cho Chung-hun of Hanjin, Kim Son-hong of Kia, Pak Song-yang of Kumho, Yi Chun-yong of Daelim, and Kim Sang-ha of the Korea Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Missing were Kim Sok-won of Ssangyong, Sin Kyok-ho of Lotte, and Cho Sok-nae of Hyosung because they were on overseas trips or for personal reasons.
