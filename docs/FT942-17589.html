940402 FT 02 APR 94 / UK Company News: Martin Intl Martin International Holdings, the clothing manufacturer, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.25m for the year to December 31 after exceptional reorganisation costs of Pounds 1.94m. There were profits of Pounds 1.27m in 1992 after exceptionals of Pounds 664,000. The comparable figures have been restated in accordance with FRS 3. Turnover totalled Pounds 85.4m (Pounds 83.4m) including Pounds 2.47m (Pounds 7.66m) from discontinued operations. Operating profits on continuing operations fell from Pounds 4.44m to Pounds 1.91m. Interest took Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.48m). Losses per share amounted to 10.1p (2.8p earnings). A final dividend of 0.6p (2.7p) is proposed, cutting the total for the year to 1p (4.4p).
