Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense Secretary Renato De Villa has reported that there has been no violation on the extended holiday cease-fire between government forces and communist rebels. The suspension of military offensives against the communists is the longest the government has made so far. Meanwhile, De Villa said that it is still uncertain whether the planned peace talks in Vietnam with the National Democratic Front will push through this month. [Begin De Villa recording in progress, in English] ...we have a cease-fire with the Moro National Liberation Front. We are informally [pause] we are conducting this in accordance with the written agreement that have been agreed upon by the two cease-fire committees on both sides. [end recording]
