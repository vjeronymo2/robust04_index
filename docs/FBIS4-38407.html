BFN [Text] As has been reported by the press service of the president of Ukraine and the Cabinet of Ministers, the government has approved a resolution on converting state-owned property into the communal property of oblasts and the cities of Kiev and Sevastopol. The resolution contains a detailed list of incomplete construction facilities, children's pre-school institutions, health protection establishments, housing and utilities. Relevant ministries, departments, and corporations are to ensure that the above conversion be completed by 1 July this year.
