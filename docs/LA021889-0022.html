February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HERE'S A SLAM DIRECTED AT JORDAN AND WILKINS

It's a shame Michael Jordan and Dominique Wilkins had to skip the All-Star slam dunk contest just because Wilkins thinks the $20,000 is chump change and darn Mikie burned up so much energy at last year's contest that he hardly made it through the rest of the season.

TRACY LOTZ

Torrance
