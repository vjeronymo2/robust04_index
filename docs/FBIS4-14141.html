BFN [Text] The government has approved setting up of a joint venture company with Germany in the field of engineering. It will have collaboration between the Mettalurgical and Engineering Consultants India Limited, MECON, and LPOAG [expansion unknown] of Germany. The new company will have an initial paid-up capital of 40 lakh rupees with the German company contributing 74 percent. The company will deal with designing and manufacturing of electrical, electronic automation, and electro mechanical equipment for industrial plants. The union cabinet also approved another joint venture company by MECON with a U.S. computer software company. It will harness information technology for mettalurgical, chemical, and refining industries. It will have equity of two and a half crore rupees.
