941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / Eggar dismisses EU withdrawal Britain outside the European Union would be 'just an irrelevance', Mr Tim Eggar, energy minister, warned in the Commons yesterday in dismissing suggestions that withdrawal was a viable option. He did not dispute assertions by Labour MPs that his words were principally aimed at Mr Norman Lamont, the former chancellor, who recently branded as 'simplistic' those who argue that such a course is unthinkable. Business leaders believe developments in the EU during the past five years have helped to increase trade for their companies, says a MORI survey of 101 directors for American Express released yesterday.
