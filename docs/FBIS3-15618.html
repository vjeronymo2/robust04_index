Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Aleksandr Yegorov report from the "News" roundup: "Conversion Has Begun in Earnest in Moscow, It Seems"] [Text] Moscow -- At a joint sitting of the Moscow Government and the board of the Russian Federation State Committee for the Defense Sectors of Industry, a draft decree "On the `Conversion for the City' Regional Program for the Conversion of Moscow Region Defense Enterprises" was approved on the whole. On the proposal of Moscow Mayor Yuriy Luzhkov, who did not conceal his negative attitude toward privatization "Chubays-style," a supplement concerning the flotation of the capital's "defense sector" was excluded from the draft. Yuriy Luzhkov stated that Moscow requires particular terms and methods for denationalizing industrial enterprises. As for financing the program, according to the premier's assurances, at least 147 billion rubles will be made available in 1994.
