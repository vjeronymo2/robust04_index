930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Fired Earth sinks to Pounds 23,000 Fired Earth Tiles, the marketer and distributor of terracotta and glazed tiles, experienced a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 302,000 to Pounds 23,000 for the year to end-December. Turnover fell 5 per cent to Pounds 4.98m, of which exports accounted for 6.8 (2.2) per cent. Earnings per share declined from 3.58p to 0.18p.
