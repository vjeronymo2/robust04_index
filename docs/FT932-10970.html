930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / IRA bomb to cost BAT Pounds 25m THE latest IRA bomb in the City will cost BAT an estimated Pounds 25m in insurance claims, much more than its Pounds 8m exposure to the 1992 bomb. The total includes Pounds 10m from commitments to pay the first Pounds 100,000 of claims made under Pool-Re, the scheme established between the government and insurance industry to cover terrorism. About Pounds 15m relates to the group's 7 per share of the exposure to the damaged NatWest Tower.
