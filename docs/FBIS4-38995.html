BFN [Text] The Moscow government has granted permission to several opposition organizations to hold a rally and a march in the center of Moscow on June 12 (the anniversary of the adoption of the declaration of state sovereignty of the Russian Federation.). The rally is allowed gather about 40,000 people. Its slogan will be, "To preserve Russia -- to restore the Union!" The rally has been initiated by the National Salvation Front (led by Ilya Konstantinov), the Russian Communist Workers Party and the Working Russia movement (led by Vladimir Anpilov), the Rebirth Liberal Patriotic Party (led by Valeriy Skurlatov), the Russian Communist Union (led by Aleksey Prigarin), the All Union Bolshevik Communist Party (led by Nina Andreyeva), the Officers Union (led by Stanislav Terekhov), the so-called USSR People's Deputies Congress Permanent Presidium (chaired by Sazhi Umalatova) and other opposition organizations. The initiators were warned against violation of public order and reminded of their personal responsibility for this.
