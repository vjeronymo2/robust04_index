BFN ["Press Release of the Ministry of Agriculture and Food in Regard to its Delegation Visit in The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia" -- ATA headline] [Text] Tirana, June 15 (ATA) -- The Ministry of Agriculture and Food reported through a press release that the talks between the Albanian and Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Ministry of Agriculture delegations were characterized by a spirit of understanding and respect of the mutual interests. The two sides agreed on all the questions they discussed concerning the problems of the mutual interest. The Albanian party insisted that the documents to be signed should refer to Macedonia with the name that the United Nations has given to this republic since Albania too has recognized its eastern neighbour by this name. The press release said that there was no personal approach on this question. The minister of agriculture and food, Hasan Halili, who headed the delegation, clarified that the stand of the delegation was in conformity with that of the Albanian Government on this issue. The press release informs the public that issues about which the two delegations talked will be reflected in a protocol which is expected to be signed soon.
