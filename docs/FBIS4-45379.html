BFN [Text] Security officials of Herat Province yesterday seized 6,000 kilograms of hashish and 557 kilograms of opium. A source from the south-western zone told the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY that the drugs, placed in small bags and transported by two motor vehicles, were captured by officials of the Jehadi 21st Division of Shindand District. Six carriers in the case were captured with 1,500,000 afghanis and were handed over to legal sources for investigation. The source said the captured drugs were taken to Herat, and the consuls of Iran, Saudi Arabia and Turkmenistan, a number of domestic and foreign reporters, and officials of Herat Province inspected the drugs in Herat.
