Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Trucks Still Blocked"] [Text] At the Greek border point Evzoni, on the Macedonian-Greek border, numerous trucks still remain blocked by the Greek authorities, due to the trade embargo. For already the fifth day, cargo vehicles from Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], but also from a number of European Union member states, are waiting to exit Greece. The measure introduced by the Greek Government have grounded vehicles transporting a range of goods. What will happen to them will be known after the European Union meeting, as there are no official explanations from the Greek side, which only adds to the indignation of the drivers.
