Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Colombian Navy has announced that the U.S. Marines will return to their bases at the beginning of March. The Marines are building a river base in Puerto Lopez, Meta. The Colombian Navy also denied that the U.S. Marines are building an airport in Juanchaco, Valle. Admiral Hernando Garcia Ramirez, commander of the Colombian Navy, has asserted that the U.S. troops will return to their bases in the United States once they finish building the school and the health center. He also denied charges that the U.S. Marines, stationed in Malaga Bay, are building an airport in Juanchaco.
