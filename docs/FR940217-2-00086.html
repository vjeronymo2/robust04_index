SUMMARY: The U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, proposes to withdraw 4,921 acres of National Forest System lands to protect the recreational and visual resources of the Elk Wild and Scenic River Corridor in the Siskiyou National Forest. This notice closes the lands for up to two years from mining. The lands have been and will remain open to mineral leasing.
