BFN [Text] Zugdidi, 19 Apr -- An IBERIA correspondent reports that a meeting was held on 18 April between the Rescue Corps' commission for the exchange of prisoners of war and Abkhaz representatives. The Georgian delegation included commission leader Paata Zakareishvili and Gia Baramidze, a deputy in the Georgian Parliament. The meeting was held in a calm atmosphere. An exchange of prisoners took place. The Georgian side would like to thank the Abkhaz representatives, especially Rusudan Kishmaria and Vitaliy Adleiba, for their contribution to the success of the meeting.
