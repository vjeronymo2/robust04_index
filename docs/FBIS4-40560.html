BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Text] Moscow, 16 Jun -- A treaty on the bases of friendly relations between Russia and Vietnam was signed this evening in the Russian Government residence on Sparrow Hills. The signatories of the document were Viktor Chernomyrdin, chairman of the government of the Russian Federation, and Vo Van Kiet, prime minister of the government of Vietnam. Three other agreements were also concluded: an agreement to encourage and provide mutual protection for capital investments, an agreement on economic and scientific-technical cooperation in the sphere of the agro-industrial complex, and an agreement on cooperation in the sphere of fisheries.
