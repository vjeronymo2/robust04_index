Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow, 31 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- The Federation Council approved the Russian president's decree on extending the state of emergency by two months in the conflict zone on the territories of Ingushetia and North Ossetia. This was reported by Aleksandr Dzasokhov, State Duma deputy from North Ossetia, who is attending the Federation Council for the discussion of this issue. Aleksandr Dzasokhov reported that "the Federation Council set up a special commission which within 24 hours is to work out measures for stabilizing the situation in the conflict zone." In the deputy's personal opinion, "in order to settle the conflict the leaders of the two republics should, above all, increase their role in the settlement of the conflict by dint of their own personal capabilities with the participation of the Provisional Administration of the conflict zone." The closed session of the Federation Council continues.
