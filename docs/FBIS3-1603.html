Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 28 (XINHUA) -- General Chi Haotian, Chinese State Councillor and minister of national defence, met with General Abdul Waheed, chief of staff of the Army of Pakistan, and his party here this afternoon. This evening, Lieutenant General Xu Huizi, deputy chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), hosted a banquet for General Waheed and his party. General Waheed and his party arrived here yesterday as guests of the PLA General Staff. Apart from Beijing, they are also scheduled to visit the cities of Xian, Guilin and Guangzhou.
