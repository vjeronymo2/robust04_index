Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Accompanied by Lin Botang, vice chairman of the provincial People's Congress Standing Committee, and Qingyuan City party and government leaders, Tian Jiyun, member of the CPC Central Committee's Political Bureau and vice chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, together with his entourage, inspected Qingyuan's economic development experimental zone yesterday. In the morning, Tian Jiyuan and his entourage went to the Xinbeijiang printing and dyeing factory in the development zone. This is a joint-venture enterprise with an annual industrial output value of more than 100 million yuan. Subsequently Tian Jiyun visited the Yuejiang automobile company.
