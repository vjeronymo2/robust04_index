October 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: ALHAMBRA;

DRUG PROGRAM FOR SCHOOLS

Using games, songs, chants -- even a makeshift mailbox for children to send anti-drug promises to President Bush and Gov. George Deukmejian -- the Monterey Park chapter of Kiwanis International on Monday kicked off a $10,000 effort aimed at promoting an anti-drug message in local public schools.

The hourlong program will be brought to 13 elementary schools in the Alhambra School District, said David H. Ma, organizer of the event.
