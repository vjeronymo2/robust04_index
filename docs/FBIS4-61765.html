BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 10 May -- The situation in Abkhazia's Galiyskiy Rayon is still difficult, with armed Georgian groups continuing to infiltrate the territory of the rayon, attacking civilians and carrying out acts of sabotage. A well-informed source in the republican government told the ITAR-TASS correspondent this today. According to the aforementioned source, about 250 armed men are currently on the territory of Galiyskiy Rayon, having crossed the confrontation line between the Georgian and Abkhaz forces which runs along the river Inguri.
