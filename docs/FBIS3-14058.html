Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, arrived in Cairo tonight from Tunis, having taken part in two days of talks between the Palestinian leadership; Dennis Ross, U.S. coordinator of the Middle East peace process; and an Israeli delegation. Dr. Sha'th pointed out that he will head the Palestinian delegation to the meeting that will be held on 24 March by representatives of 21 countries to discuss assistance for the Palestinian police force. He added that Ross will lead the U.S. delegation to that meeting. The Palestinian official said that the Israeli delegation is expected to return to that meeting with some answers to the Palestinian demands, particularly the one concerning security in Hebron, following consultations with the Israeli Government.
