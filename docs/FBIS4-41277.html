BFN ["Temporary Rules of Armenia-Iran Border Trade"--SNARK headline] [Text] Yerevan, 22 June (SNARK) -- Armenian citizens were given the right for a tax-free export and import of goods into the trade zone at the Armenia-Iran border, for a total sum not exceeding $1,000. In the case the amount is surpassed, a tax is imposed on goods under the valid agreement. According to Armenian Government decision issued today, barter trade is also allowed in the zone.
