BFN [Text] On 29 April, Zambian President Frederick Chiluba and the high-level delegation accompanying him traveled to Santiago de Cuba Province where they are scheduled to meet with Zambian students in schools in Santiago de Cuba, Camaguey, and Moa, among other activities. This morning, President Chiluba rendered homage to Jose Marti at Santa Efigenia Cemetery following his arrival at Antonio Maceo International Airport where he was greeted by Esteban Lazo Hernandez, member of the Politburo and first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in the province. President Chiluba, accompanied by Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina, also visited the Antonio Maceo Revolution Square where he took in aspects of Maceo's thoughts and actions against Spanish colonialism. Chiluba and his delegation are now visiting the African Cultural Center and will visit, this afternoon, the Granjita Siboney Museum and the former Moncada Barracks, as well as meet with Zambian students in Santiago de Cuba.
