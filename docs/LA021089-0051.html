February 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

NBC program chief Brandon Tartikoff, addressing an industry lunch Thursday in New York, had a complaint similar to that of Rodney Dangerfield -- no respect. Asked to assess the worst part of this season for NBC, he said it's the "sometimes exaggerated" knocks NBC has taken for airing some shows critics found tasteless or even sleazy. "I'd just like to see a little respect out there from the critical community. I think the only person who's had worse press than me has been (executed serial murderder) Ted Bundy." ALEENE MacMINN
