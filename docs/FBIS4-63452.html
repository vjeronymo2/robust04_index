BFN [Text] Today, about 30 policemen burst into the headquarters of the Musavat opposition party in Baku. All those on the premises were beaten up with truncheons and several people were arrested, including Musavat General Secretary Niyazi Ibrahimov. According to eye-witnesses, 40 to 50 people in all were detained. The National Resistance Movement was planning to hold an anti-government meeting at 1500 on Freedom Square. Approximately an hour before this, the whole square was cordoned off by police. The police beat up with truncheons those who had gathered to take part in the rally, and tens of people were detained. At present all the buildings within a one kilometer radius of the square are being combed and searched by the police.
