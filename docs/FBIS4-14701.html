BFN [Text] According to the CONTACT news agency, the Georgian head of state [Eduard Shevardnadze] met Barukh Birenbaum, Israeli ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Georgia [name and title as heard]. Mr. Shevardnadze familiarized the visitor with the current complicated political and economic situation in Georgia, which caused the postponement of his official visit to Israel until a more suitable time. The Israeli ambassador accepted the explanation given by the Georgian head of state with understanding and expressed hope that the visit would take place in the near future.
