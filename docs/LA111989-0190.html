November 19, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

JUNIOR COLLEGE FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Mt. San Jacinto 21, Antelope Valley 8 -- Brian Williams caught a five-yard pass from Cole Speer with two minutes left in the third quarter for Antelope Valley's only score in the Foothill Conference game at Hemet Junior High.

Derek Black rushed 23 times for 107 yards and two touchdowns for Mt. San Jacinto (5-5, 4-4). Quarterback Vince Daley completed seven of 11 for 86 yards.
