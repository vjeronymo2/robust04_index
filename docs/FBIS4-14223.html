BFN [Text] An Arab armed with a knife tried to stab a soldier at an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] roadblock in Policeman's Square in Hebron this morning. The soldier was not hurt. The terrorist was shot and apparently killed. Disturbances and stone-throwing incidents broke out after the event. Two Arabs arrived at al-Ahali Hospital with slight wounds sustained during the disturbances. The IDF imposed a curfew on the center of Hebron.
