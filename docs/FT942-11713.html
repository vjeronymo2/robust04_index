940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa The euphoria surrounding South Africa's first all race elections propelled Johannesburg higher last week, continuing a rising trend seen this year as US funds have begun to flow into the market. Analysts expect a measure of hesitancy in coming months as investors assess the new cabinet and the government's first budget. In the medium term, good economic growth prospects and a view that the ANC will want to co-operate with business should prove positivefor the stock market.
