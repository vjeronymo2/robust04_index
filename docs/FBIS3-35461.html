Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] By means of demonstrations, the residents of Kabul today strongly condemned the continued fighting and the closing of communication lines with Kabul, and asked that the United Nations, using its influence, bring pressure to bear on the enemies of the people of Afghanistan as soon as possible in order to open supply routes to Kabul. The demonstrators asked the United Nations to send more aid to the people of Afghanistan so that residents of Kabul can be rid of the famine caused by the putschist road blockers. Expressing repulsion and hatred at the acts of putschists, the demonstrators asked the United Nations to secure world aid to rid the people of Afghanistan of famine and hunger, which has been caused by the closing of the Torkham border post and communication lines.
