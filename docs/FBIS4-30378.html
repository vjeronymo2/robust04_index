BFN [Text] Malaysia will make continuous efforts to give a clearer perception on the concept of East Asia Economic Caucus, EAEC, among the various countries such as the U.S. and Japan. The international trade and industry minister, Datuk Sri Rafidah Aziz, said such a step had been taken to enable that EAEC could be launched as soon as possible. Speaking to newsmen after officiating the Family Day of the Forest Research Institute of Malaysia, FRIM, in Kepong, she said at present the U.S. has not made any official stand on the EAEC concept. She added that the U.S. had expressed uncertainty on EAEC and had sought views from other countries.
