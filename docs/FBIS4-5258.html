BFN [Excerpt] Vientiane, April 8 (KPL) -- His Majesty King Phumiphon Adunyadet of Thailand and Her Majesty Queen Sirikit, arrived here today for an official visit to Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] at the invitation of Lao President Nouhak Phoumsavan. An official ceremony to welcome their majesties the king and queen of Thailand with their party including Her Royal Highness Princess Sirinthon took place at the National Assembly. This morning the king and the queen attended a ceremony of presenting the friendship bridge across the Mekong River. The handover ceremony was performed by Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating, who paid an official visit to Laos on April 5-6. This was part of the first ever visit to Laos by the King of Thailand.
