BFN [Text] Hanoi May 24 -- A delegtion of the Communist Party of China (CPC) led by Yin Keshang member of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the party committee of Qinghai Province, paid a visit to Vietnam from May 14-22 as guest of the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] Central Committee. During the visit, the delegation paid a floral tribute to President Ho Chi Minh at his mausoleum and visited his home and office. The Chinese guests were received by Pham The Duyet, member of CPV Central Committee and secretary of the Hanoi party committee. Mr. Duyet expressed his belief that the exchange of visits to explore the possibilities for cooperation will strengthen the friendship between the two peoples. Mr. Yin Keshang welcomed the achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in their renovation process and wished them bigger successes. The Chinese party delegation also had working sessions with concerned offices, and party committees of Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City and Provinces of Thanh Hoa, and Ben Tre, and visited a number of economic and cultural establishments.
