BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah returned to Sanaa this afternoon at the end of a working visit to the Qatari capital, Doha. During the visit he delivered a message from Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, to his brother His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of the State of Qatar, dealing with the latest political developments in the Yemeni arena. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the foreign minister expressed his satisfaction with the results of his visit to the State of Qatar. He said that during his meetings with the amir of Qatar and ranking Qatari officials he conveyed the greetings and appreciation of the Yemeni political leadership, led by Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman and commander-in chief of the Armed Forces, for the principled and firm pan-Arab positions the State of Qatar on various Arab and fateful issues, including the Yemeni crisis. The minister added that he reviewed with the Qatari officials relations of cooperation between the two sisterly countries and issues of bilateral interest.
