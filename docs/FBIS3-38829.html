Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Brussels, Feb 7 (AFP) -- NATO ambassadors will consider the situation in Sarajevo at their regular weekly meeting Wednesday [9 February], rather than call a special session, a source close to the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation said Monday. This follows a call by EC foreign ministers for the North Atlantic Council to consider all possible means, including air strikes, to break the 22-month-old siege of Sarajevo by the Bosnian Serbs.
