921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Hills to press Taipei on trade Mrs Carla Hills, US trade representative, arrived in Taiwan yesterday, the first cabinet-level US official to visit since Washington switched diplomatic recognition from Taipei to Beijing in 1979, Reuter reports from Taipei. She is expected to press Taiwan to widen import markets and offer contracts -under Taipei's six-year, Dollars 300bn (Pounds 197.3bn) development plan - to US companies.
