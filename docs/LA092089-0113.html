September 20, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Dynatem Inc. posted net income of $15,240 for the quarter ended Aug. 31, contrasted with a loss of $33,862 for the comparable period last year. Revenue for the first fiscal quarter declined 9%, to $355,089 from $392,244. Eileen DeSwert, president and chief executive, attributed the improvement in net income to increased productivity and decreased operating expenses. Dynatem, with headquarters in Irvine, manufactures microcomputers, computer peripherals and software.
