Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] As the Ukrainian Interior Ministry General Fire Department has reported, on 16 March at 2045 [local time], a fire erupted in the engine room at the first power unit of the Khmelnytskyy nuclear power plant in a cable box housing power and control cables. The chiefs of the plant's fire department decided to deploy firefighters in the danger zone to extinguish the 6,000-volt cables. The firefighters wore special gear. In addition, to the firefighters' action, the plant personnel stopped the operation of the first power unit reactor. [passage indistinct] The fire was extinguished at 2110. The damages are currently being assessed. According to preliminary assessments, the fire was caused by a short circuit in the main power cable. The radiation level remains normal.
