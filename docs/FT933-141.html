930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Radiotrust Radiotrust, which invests in companies involved entirely or mainly in sound broadcasting, had a net asset value of 48.6p at July 31 compared with 49.9p a year earlier and 48.3p at the trust's January year end. Retained profits for the half year amounted to Pounds 10,662 (Pounds 21,416 losses) for earnings per share of 0.12p (0.25p losses).
