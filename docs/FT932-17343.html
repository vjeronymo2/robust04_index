930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Observer: Basic image Whatever next? Could the 30th Pirelli calendar feature clothed women? Probably as famous for its sexy calendars as for its tyres, the Italian company's image would seem to have upset its managing director once too often. Last year a full-page ad for its bedding unit featuring a naked man provoked an uproar from Marco Tronchetti Provera. The ad was never seen again and the subsidiary sold. But the cleansing is only skin deep. With a placing for its shares in Continental, the bigger German rival it had tried unsuccessfully to buy in 1990, possibly only hours away, Pirelli yesterday unveiled a heavily suggestive new TV advertising campaign featuring 'Basic Instinct' star Sharon Stone.
