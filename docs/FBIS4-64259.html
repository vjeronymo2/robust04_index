BFN [Unattributed report: "No Trust in Assurances"] [Excerpt] During their visit to Bavaria and talks with representatives of the CSU [Christian-Social Union], the SPD [Social Democratic Party of Germany], the FDP [Free Democratic Party], and the Greens, representatives of the Turkish DEP [Democratic Party], the only pro-Kurdish party in the Turkish parliament, warned against sending Kurds back to Turkey. Under no circumstances must the German authorities trust the assurances of the Turkish authorities, said DEP deputy Ali Yigit. "Hundreds of people have disappeared while in police custody. Every Kurd sent back from here will meet a similar fate," said Yigid, who also met the mayor of Munich, Christian Ude. [passage omitted]
