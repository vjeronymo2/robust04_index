940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Directory of unit trusts - At a glance A new directory of unit trusts has been published by Professional and Business Information. The Unit Trust Industry Review and Directory 1994 lists all the fund management groups with information on their funds and performance comment. There are industry statistics which include sales and distribution figures. The directory costs Pounds 375 (Pounds 280 to past buyers) from PBI, Munro House, 14 St Cross Street, London EC1N 8YY and is available in most university and main libraries.
