Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Announcer] American Ambassador to the United Nations Madeleine Albright has arrived in Zagreb. Tihomir Vinkovic reports for Croatian Radio: [Vinkovic] Upon arrival at Zagreb aiport Ambassador Albright told journalists there were three reasons for her visit to Croatia: First is the stand of the American Administration that there is no justification of aggression, war crimes, and thwarting dispatch of humanitarian aid. The American Administration supports the sovereignty and the territorial integrity of the Republic of Croatia. Currently occupied Croatian territories have to be returned in a peaceful way and through a political solution, ambassador Albright said. The U.S. Government will support efforts for the restoration of peace and dispatch of humanitarian aid, particularly in Bosnia-Herzegovina. In the course of her visit to Croatia Ambassador Albright is to meet President Tudjman, Croatian Foreign Minister Mate Granic, representatives of the UN Protection Forces, UN High Commissioner for Refugees, and other humanitarian organizations.
