931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Scottish exports growing fast Scotland's exports of manufactured goods rose considerably faster last year than those of the UK as a whole, figures yesterday showed. UK exports of manufactures rose by 0.2 per cent by value in real terms and Scottish manufactured exports rose 6.3 per cent. Scotland's share of UK manufactured exports rose from 9.6 per cent in 1991 to 10.1 per cent.
