Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, March 16 (AFP) -- The formal adoption of an accord to reopen roads around the Bosnian capital was postponed for a day Wednesday at the request of Bosnian Serb officials, the commander of UN forces in Bosnia said. General Michael Rose said the Serbs requested the delay, the second in three days, so that experts could be brought in from Belgrade. [passage omitted]
