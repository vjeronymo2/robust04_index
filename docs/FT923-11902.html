920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / CIS to train own peacekeepers Russian troops will be trained from next year to carry out UN-style, peacekeeping operations inside and outside the Commonwealth of Independent States, Defence Minister Pavel Grachev said yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow. As well as their traditional task of repelling possible aggression, Mr Grachev said the Russian troops would have to preserve the country's territorial integrity.
