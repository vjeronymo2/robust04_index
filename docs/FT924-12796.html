921019 FT 19 OCT 92 / International Company News: News Corp scales down debt offering NEWS Corp, the international media group, has cut its planned debt offering from Dollars 1bn to Dollars 850m, Reuter reports from New York. The group priced the senior debt securities with the interest rate on Dollars 550m of senior notes due October 15 1999 to be 9 1/8 per cent. The rate on Dollars 300m of senior debentures due October 15, 2012 will be 10 1/8 per cent.
