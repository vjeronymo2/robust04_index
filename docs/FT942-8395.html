940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: TR Property TR PROPERTY Investment Trust: EGM approved plans to acquire a property portfolio from PosTel for Pounds 39m, implement placing and offer for subscription of up to 61m C shares and bonus issue of warrants. 40m C shares were placed firm with institutions at 100p per share, while valid applications were received for subscription offer for 1.1m C shares. All applications satisfied in full.
