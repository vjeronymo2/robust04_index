940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Rackwood ahead of forecast Rackwood Mineral Holdings, the opencast mining group which obtained a listing in January, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 29,000 for 1993. That compared with a prospectus forecast of Pounds 10,000 and was achieved on turnover of Pounds 2.97m. Interest charges accounted for Pounds 221,000. Basic earnings per share emerged at 0.36p; on a pro forma basis the figure was 0.18p. The shares, placed at 40p in January, rose 2p to 48p.
