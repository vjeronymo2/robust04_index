921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Dickie reports sharp upturn to Pounds 298,000 James Dickie, a manufacturer of engineering components and assemblies, saw pre-tax profits rise from Pounds 206,000 to Pounds 298,000 in the year to August 31 following a sharp improvement in the second six months of the year; the first six months saw a loss of Pounds 98,000. Turnover fell from Pounds 26.5m to Pounds 15.4m following consolidation of manufacturing activities, including the closure of Coalville Sheet Metal and the sale of James Dickie Forgings to its management. Earnings per share came through at 3.4p against 5.8p after tax of Pounds 89,000 (Pounds 152,000 credit). A single distribution of 1p (nil) is proposed.
