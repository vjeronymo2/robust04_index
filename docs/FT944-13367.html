941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Intel in China accord A unit of US chipmaker Intel has signed a co-operation pact with Jitong Communications an information and telecom vendor under China's electronics ministry. Under the memorandum of understanding, signed by Intel Architecture Development of Shanghai, Jitong will open an exhibition centre in Beijing to show Intel networking and personal conferencing products this year and later will be contracted as an authorised dealer for those products. Intel indicated the Jitong pact would lead to lucrative contracts in China's national data network development.
