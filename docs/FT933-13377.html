930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / Sugar legal move is adjourned A LEGAL move by Mr Alan Sugar, the Tottenham Hotspur chairman, to force Mr Terry Venables, the ousted chief executive, to pay money into court before Mr Venables attempts a buy-out was adjourned yesterday until Thursday next week. Mr Sugar is seeking a High Court order requiring Edennote, Mr Venables' company, to put up security for costs to cover all or part of Spurs' legal expenses in the event of the buy-out bid failing.
