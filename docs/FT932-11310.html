930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: NHS waiting lists hailed THERE is now not a single National Health Service patient who has been waiting for longer than 18 months for either a hip replacement or a cataract operation, Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary, told MPs yesterday. But Mr Win Griffiths, a Labour MP, said the waiting-list reduction stemmed partly from a variety of 'devices'. One of these was for consultants to put cases 'in their drawers' when they knew money was not available to fund the required treatment.
