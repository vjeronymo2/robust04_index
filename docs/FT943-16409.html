940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Court move to stop Sunday bill fails A campaigner against Sunday trading yesterday failed in a High Court bid to stop parliament seeking royal assent today for the Sunday trading bill. Mr Roy Edey told Mr Justice May that it would be unconstitutional to advise the Queen to break her coronation oath as defender of the faith by assenting to the bill. The judge ruled that the courts had no power to interfere with the passage of a bill through Parliament.
