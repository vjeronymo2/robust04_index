May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THAT SMART LOOK

Re Gene Simmons' letter of April 23 about the cherished "old adage" that most critics like Elvis Costello because most critics look like Elvis Costello:

E.C.'s intelligence, wit and integrity are as apparent on his face as his trademark hornrims. We might wish that more critics -- and rock stars -- looked so good.

Maybe Neanderthals like Simmons and David Lee Roth should take a harder look in the mirror.

KAT JOHNSON

Los Angeles
