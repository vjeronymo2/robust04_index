930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / International Company News: Unidare rises to IPounds 2.7m UNIDARE, the Dublin-based industrial group, saw an increase in both sales and pre-tax profits for the the half year to June 30, boosted by its acquisition of Nasco of the US. Pre-tax profits were ahead 10 per cent at IPounds 2.68m (Pounds 2.53m) against IPounds 2.45m. Turnover was Pounds 63.3m (IPounds 59.9m), including IPounds 9.39m from acquisitions. Earnings per share came out ahead at 12.52p (11.71p) and the interim dividend is 4.4p (4.3p).
