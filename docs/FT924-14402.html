921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / International Company News: Danish banks downgraded MOODY'S the US rating agency, downgraded the long-term credit rating of two Danish banks yesterday and put the rating for Den Danske Bank, under review. The rating for Unibank was reduced from A2 to Aa3 for long-term deposits and from A2rto Baa1 for floating-rate subordinated debt. Baltica Bank's rating went down from Baa1 to Baa3 and for Euro commercial paper from Prime 2 to Prime 3.
