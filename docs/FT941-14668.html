940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Meggitt forms new business unit Meggitt is forming a new unit within its electronics division by bringing together five companies with related products and markets. The strategic business unit, Meggitt Petroleum Systems, is being created to ensure a co-ordinated strategy for European and world markets. With a combined turnover of Pounds 25m, MPS embodies five trading companies: Micrelec; SA Micrelec; Triscan; Lectronic Specialists and Land Restoration Systems. Their products include supply of retail and commercial fuel controls and management information systems as well as specialist service operations.
