940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / Observer: Guardez l'argent Who said inflation was dead? Harrods, the Knightsbridge souk, yesterday started charging female customers one pound to 'spend a penny'. The store says that its new loo, complete with marble floors and high-tech flushes, offers customers 'a chance to get away from it all'. But with prices like that, Observer is reminded of the old adage that if you look after the pennies, the pounds take care of themselves.
