920706 FT 06 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Agassi and Graf are champions Andre Agassi of the US (left) defeated Goran Ivanisevic, the Croatian left-hander, in a five-set battle in the Wimbledon tennis final to win his first Grand Slam title. Agassi won 6-7 (8-10), 6-4, 6-4, 1-6, 6-4. The women's title went to Germany's Steffi Graf, who beat Monica Seles of Yugoslavia 6-2, 6-1 on Saturday.
