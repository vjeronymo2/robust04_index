Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By BUSINESS-TASS] [Text] Moscow, 20 Jan--In an interview with an ITAR-TASS correspondent today, Aleksandr Shokhin, whose appointment as Russian Federation economics minister was also announced today, stated his determination to continue the market reforms. Shokhin expressed the hope that he would have sufficient levers of power to continue the policies of market reform. Shokhin is the fifth economics minister since Russia started its radical economic reforms in January 1992.
