Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by C Molusi] [Text] Johannesburg March 8 SAPA -- Negotiations on a proposed election pact between the African National Congress [ANC] and the Pan Africanist Congress [PAC] have collapsed, PAC Secretary General Benny Alexander said in Johannesburg on Tuesday [8 March]. Mr Alexandra said talks floundered after a media statement attributed to ANC President Nelson Mandela demanded a declaration of assets ahead of the election pact. "The PAC will contest the election on its own, in its own name," he said. Mr Alexander said the ANC was pursuing the election pact as a merger between two companies, which indicated a lack of commitment on the part of the organisation.
