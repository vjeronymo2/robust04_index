931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / International Company News: Deloitte faces Dollars 77m damages A COURT in Houston, Texas, ordered Deloitte & Touche, the US accounting firm, to pay Dollars 77m in punitive damages to investors in a limited partnership which invested in cattle, writes Richard Waters. The accounting firm had been negligent in preparing a tax return claiming an allowance for grazing land which the firm could not show the cattle had actually used, the court decided. Investors in the Limited partnership had paid Dollars 35m plus additional penalties to the US's Internal Revenue Service after agreeing a settlement over the matter in 1991. Deloitte is expected to appeal.
