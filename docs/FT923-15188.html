920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Company News in Brief BUNZL has acquired Camelot, a redistributor of coarse paper and plastic products, based in Atlanta, Georgia. Camelot, valued at Dollars 8m (Pounds 4.3m), had sales of Dollars 36m in the year to October 30 1991. ***** HENDERSON EUROTRU The offer for subscrip- tion attracted applications for 17.31m units comprising one ordinary share of 5p and one zero dividend preference share of 20p at a sub- scription price of Pounds 1. All applications have been allotted in full. ***** SHANGHAI FUND (Cayman): Dealings in the shares of the fund, which specialises in listed Chinese securities, have begun. The fund raised Dollars 17.7m (Pounds 9.5m) via a placing by Financiere Indosuez of 1.77m participating shares at Dollars 10.4 per share. The shares are listed on the London Stock Exchange. ***** WILLOUGHBY'S CONSOLIDATED (a mining and ranching company in Zimbabwe controlled by Lonrho): Pre-tax profits Pounds 1.83m (Pounds 270,000) for the six months ended March 31 1992. Turnover was Pounds 5.96m (Pounds 5.67m). Earnings were 17.6p (1.7p) and the interim dividend is 1p (same). ***** TLS RANGE has received valid acceptances in respect of 3.19m (56.5 per cent) of the 5.65m new ordinary shares on offer in its recent rights issue. The balance of 2.46m shares has been taken up by under- writers.
