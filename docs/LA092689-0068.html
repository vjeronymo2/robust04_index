September 26, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: CALIFORNIA AMPLIFIER POSTS $79,000 PROFIT

California Amplifier, a maker of devices that boost electrical signals, reported a profit of $79,000 on sales of $2.9 million for the three months ended August 31, compared to a loss of $74,000 on sales of $2.1 million for the same period a year earlier.

Barry W. Hall, chairman and chief executive of the Camarillo-based company, attributed the turnaround for the quarter to a 74% increase in sales of products intended for use in satellite receiving dishes.
