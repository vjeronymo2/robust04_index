940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Ensor deficit lower at Pounds 180,000 Ensor Holdings, USM-quoted supplier of building materials, reduced its pre-tax loss from Pounds 2.96m to Pounds 180,000 in the 12 months ended March 31. The result last time was after a Pounds 1.31m loss on disposal of discontinued operations. Turnover of continuing operations was static at Pounds 13.46m and the operating deficit of continuing operations amounted to Pounds 152,000 (Pounds 401,000). Losses per share came through at 0.7p (24p).
