June 9, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ON THE BEACHES;

RIDING TO THE WAVES

Especially for the younger set, summer means taking the bus to the beach. Below are 15 OCTD bus routes that take landlocked residents of inland Orange County to sun, sand and surf. Major stops are listed; buses arrive approximately every 20 minutes during the week and every half-hour on weekends. Daily times for first and last runs vary; call for specific schedule information.

INFORMATION: (714) 636-RIDE.

FARES: 80 cents (85 cents beginning July 1). Exact change required.

SURFBOARDS: Only those less than 6 feet allowed on buses.

MOST POPULAR ROUTES: 29, 35, 43, 53 and 91.
