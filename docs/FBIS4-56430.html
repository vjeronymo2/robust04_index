BFN [Text] Three political parties in Port-au-Prince recommended a vote on the Amnesty Law in order to release the country's crisis. They are the Union of Democratic Patriots, led by Rockfeller Guerre; Alliance for the Liberation and Progress of Haiti, led by Reynold Georges; and the Liaison of Democratic Forces, led by Daniel Narcisse. These parties demand the resignations of Army Commander in Chief Raoul Cedras, General Philippe Biamby, and Colonel Michel Francois to avoid a catastrophe to the country through reinforced sanctions.
