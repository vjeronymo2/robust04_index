BFN [Text] As a full-fledged European state, Ukraine should play a considerable role in the European economic and political space. Ukrainian Foreign Affairs Minister Anatoliy Zlenko said this at a joint news conference with Leon Brittan, deputy chairman of the European Union commission and commissioner for external economic affairs. Zlenko emphasized that the legal and treaty basis for cooperation should be the agreement on partnership and cooperation between Ukraine and the European Union, which opens up the prospect for associate membership in this structure. The minister expressed the hope that the agreement would be signed by the head of the Ukrainian state. Zlenko also said that the direction of cooperation with the European Union in the areas of agriculture, nuclear safety, energy provision, and macroeconomics had been discussed and that an exchange of opinions on issues of European security and other international problems had taken place during the course of Brittan's stay in Ukraine.
