BFN [Text] The new inter-regional Presidential Republic of Russia [Rossiya-Prezidentskaya Respublika] Party intends to promote the benefits of presidential power and supply broad social support for the institution of the presidency in the Russian Federation. The party was set up at a meeting in Samara by representatives of Samara, Penza, Ryazan, Ulyanovsk, and Saratov Oblasts and the Chuvash Republic.
