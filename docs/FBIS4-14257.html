BFN [Text] The National Assembly was informed today that Pakistan has thus far paid $658 million to the United States for the F-16 planes. In a written reply during the question hour, Defense Minister Aftab Shaban Mirani disclosed that the delivery of these aircraft to Pakistan was scheduled to begin in April 1993, but the supply was stopped due to the Pressler Amendment. He said negotiations are under way with the U.S. Government on this issue.
