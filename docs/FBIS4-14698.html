BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchogoleva] [Text] Sochi April 4 TASS -- The Georgian-Abkhaz agreement signed today "contains an unequivocal and clear recognition of Georgia's territorial integrity with guarantees of a broad autonomy for Abkhazia", State Duma Committee Chairman Konstantin Zatulin said in Sochi today. He explained that for this reason, the agreement suits both sides. Zatulin recalled that the agreement signed between Georgia and Abkhazia with the participation of the Russian Federation and the United Nations contains two documents: "One is related to the refugee problems, and the other concerns political settlement of the conflict between Georgia and Abkhazia". Zatulin described accords reached in Moscow as "very positive, because any other solution would have threatened war".
