920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / UK Company News: GT Venture Invest net assets ahead GT Venture Investment Company saw its basic net asset value increase from 144p to 146.2p over the 12 months to June 30. Diluted assets improved from 137.8p to 139.7p. Net profits were Pounds 438,000 (Pounds 468,000) for earnings per share of 3.01p (3.2p). A single unchanged final dividend of 2.5p is proposed. The trust has reached agreement with holders of its outstanding Pounds 18.4m nominal of convertible unsecured loan stock 1999 to redeem the stock at a significant discount to the nominal amount plus accrued premium totalling Pounds 20.2m. Holders have agreed to cancellation of conversion rights and interest will become payable annually instead of being rolled up. As a consequence the company warned that there would have to a substantial reduction in future dividend payments. The company is planning to change its name to Pantheon International Participations.
