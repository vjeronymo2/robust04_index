November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE / TECHNOLOGY

Above Software Chosen: Above Software Inc. said it recently was chosen as a subcontractor on a government contract awarded to Zenith Electronics Corp.

The Santa Ana computer software firm said it will provide copies of its Above Disc software program, which increases the amount of data that can be stored in certain personal computer applications.

Above Software did not specify the value of the contract to the company.
