930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / International Company News: BCE arm warns on provisions MONTREAL Trustco, the financial services arm of BCE, recorded a second-quarter loss of CDollars 22.8m (USDollars 17.5m) after special charges, against a deficit of CDollars 58.5m a year earlier after special items, writes Robert Gibbens. The company warned that further special provisions were likely in the second half. MT is one of BCE's last two non-telecommunications assets and may be sold soon. Talks have been held with at least two Canadian banks.
