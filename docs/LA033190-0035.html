March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MESSING WITH THE RULES IS STRICTLY BUSH LEAGUE

Way to go, Orel Hershiser! Start the season off with a positive professional attitude. Perhaps you should be able to sit and watch the balloons and bands, because, as you stated, without incentive clauses in your contract, your record is incidental. What with your high salary and numerous endorsements, why worry about opening day and your team?

L. CRAIG

Pasadena
