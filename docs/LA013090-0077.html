January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: SANTA ROSA FIRM BUYS LACADRE OF CHATSWORTH

Major Aviation Corp. of Santa Rosa, Calif., said it acquired LACADRE Inc., a Chatsworth aeronautical engineering firm.

Terms were not disclosed.

Major Aviation said LACADRE's projected revenues for 1990 are $6 million to $7 million. LACADRE will continue to be based in Chatsworth, with a field office at Sonoma County Airport, near Santa Rosa. Major Aviation modifies and converts airplanes.
