940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Gowrings to buy Burger King outlets Gowrings, the motor trading and leisure company, has agreed, through its Gowrings Food Services subsidiary, to buy seven Burger King franchises, excluding debtors and cash, from Grand Metropolitan for Pounds 3.3m cash. The company also said it had entered a conditional option agreement with Central & Provincial Properties for the sale of most of the land at Newbury for Pounds 2.45m, and a further conditional option agreement for the sale of the rest of the Newbury site for Pounds 350,000. The site is currently occupied by Gowrings of Newbury. Approval for both the acquisition and option agreements will be sought at an extraordinary meeting on August 5.
