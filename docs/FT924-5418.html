921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / International Company News: Carolco Dollars 33.1m in red CAROLCO, the cash-hungry Hollywood studio, yesterday posted a third-quarter loss of Dollars 33.1m, or Dollars 1.16 a share, and said it had failed to comply with some of its credit covenants. The company said it was in talks with its lenders to extend its credit. A year earlier, Carolco had a deficit of Dollars 43.7m, or Dollars 1.53, including a loss of Dollars 11.9m from discontinued operations. Revenues rose to Dollars 180m in the latest quarter from Dollars 165.6m. In October, the Standard & Poor's ratings agency lowered its rating on Carolco's senior debt to double C and kept the ratings on credit watch, reflecting the company's continuing debt and liquidity pressures. For the first nine months, Carolco had a net loss of Dollars 51.9m, or Dollars 1.90.
