BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 9 Jun (NA) -- During an audience with Cuban Ambassador to Argentina Miguel Brugueras del Valle, President Carlos Menem today sent "greetings" to Commander Fidel Castro. This was reported to the NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS news agency by people who attended the meeting, in which the Cuban diplomat said goodbye to Menem after completing his mission in our country. In an attempt to inquire as to the condition of the Cuban leader, with whom he has great disagreements at a political level, Menem asked Brugueras del Valle: "How is the commander?" "He is very well," responded the diplomat, after which Menem asked him to convey his greetings to the Central American island's chief executive.
