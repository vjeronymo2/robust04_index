June 19, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

GOODMAN ON THE SEMANTICS OF THE ABORTION CONTROVERSY

I strongly agree with the Bush Administration. Hopefully, clinic officials will not be able to give their own advice or opinions and women will not be encouraged or discouraged to have or not have an abortion. Many women would probably take the advice of a professional because they supposedly know what is best, or right.

MARITEZ J. APIGO

Playa del Rey
