May 22, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'Peter Pan' to Fly on Video: "Peter Pan," the classic 1955 television production starring Mary Martin as the boy who never grew up, will soon be flying into your video store -- but only for a limited time.

The video will retail for $24.99 and be distributed through the GoodTimes Home Video Platinum Label from September through February 1991. The version will run 104 minutes, including four minutes that have not been seen by the public in nearly 20 years. A portion of the proceeds will go to the Hospital for Sick Children in London, which was bequeathed the rights to the Peter Pan story by author Sir James M. Barrie. GREG BRAXTON
