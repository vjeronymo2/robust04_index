BFN [Text] Brussels, April 10 (AFP) -- NATO warplanes Sunday carried out airstrikes over the Serb-besieged Moslem town of Gorazde in Bosnia-Herzegovina, a NATO spokesman said here. The North Atlantic Treaty Organisation carried out the attacks at the request of UN forces, the spokesman said. Serb forces were Sunday pressing home an attack on Gorazde after a massive artillery barrage following a two-week siege. The ongoing attacks against Serb positions by two NATO F-16C aircraft were confirmed in the Bosnian capital Sarajevo by UN Protection Force spokesman Major Dacre Halloway.
