BFN [Text] On 18-20 May, a conference on science, technology, and environment held in Hanoi for all provinces and cities directly subordinate to the central level. Nearly 200 delegates representing the central organs and departments for science, technology, and environment of 53 provinces and cities attended the conference. Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai addressed the conference. He introduced issues relating to science, technology, and environment to the delegates. The conference evaluated that the local activities on science, technology, and environment have contributed significantly to the national socioeconomic development. The role of science and technology have been confirmed in accelerating the national economic development. In 1993, all localities carried out 1,269 research projects, experimented with 170 production projects, applied 241 modern technologies, and imported 66 new technologies. The conference outlined directions for the 1994 activities on science, technology, and evironment. The directions are to concentrate on regional advantages in setting up socioeconomic development goals in each province and city.
