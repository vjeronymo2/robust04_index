Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] Prime Minister Tansu Ciller held a question-and-answer dinner meeting with reporters at the prime minister's residence last night. In answer to a question on what will happen during Nevruz, Ciller said: I do not think anything will happen during Nevruz. If we could die together during the War of Independence, we can also celebrate the holiday together in order to protect the undivided integrity and independence of this country. Ciller further noted: I will make Nevruz a national holiday. Nevruz will be the holiday of all of Turkey. We will enter 1995 with Nevruz as an official holiday. We will celebrate Nevruz together. If we could die together, then we can also celebrate holidays together. [passage omitted]
