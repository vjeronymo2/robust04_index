BFN [Text] On 4 May, Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin and Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina signed the ratification of a cultural agreement, dating from 1978, between Colombia and Cuba, at the Cuban Foreign Ministry. Sanin and Robaina also signed an agreement that standardizes higher education diplomas and titles. Before signing these agreements, Sanin visited Casa de las Americas, where she donated typical Colombian items to the Latin American art exhibit. She also met with Marcia Leyseca, vice president of Casa de las Americas, Pablo Milanes, Sintio Vitier, Senen Paz, and others.
