Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Feb 7 (CTK) -- Deputy Foreign Minister Alexandr Vondra said on Czech Television tonight that the Czech Republic would not support a decision to carry out aerial strikes against Serb forces surrounding Sarajevo. That would be an extreme measure that would not solve everything, this can only be done by the warring parties themselves, he added. Sixty-eight Sarajevans were killed and nearly 200 injured when a Serb artillery shell exploded in a crowded marketplace on Saturday [5 February], causing NATO and United Nations diplomats to consider anew, now with greater likelihood, launching air attacks against the Serb besiegers.
