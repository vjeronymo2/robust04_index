Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A historic seizure for Amsterdam's customs officers; they seized 2.5 tonnes of cocaine in a warehouse this weekend. The Dutch police also arrested 14 people. The cocaine came from Venezuela. It had arrived in Amsterdam by ship. The street value of the drugs is colossal: The cocaine would have fetched some 3.5 billion Belgian francs, but it was destroyed by Dutch customs officers.
