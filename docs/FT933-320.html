930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton Jute September/October c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 345, BWC Dollars 355, BTD Dollars 320, BWD Dollars 330; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 330, BWC Dollars 330, BTD Dollars 300, BWD Dollars 300. Cotton Liverpool- No spot or shipment sales were recorded for the week ended September 24, against 426 tonnes in the previous week. Activity was severely restrained and business was on narrow lines. Cost of raw cotton deterred users from increasing their purchases.
