October 6, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ASK THE 'OLDER' AUDIENCE

If controversy of any kind repels La Habra theatergoers, maybe "West Side Story," the next production, might be a little too much with its Puerto Ricans. I hope the Depot Playhouse board will realize that racism and bigotry are as destructive to community values as illegal drugs, domestic violence or political corruption.

ALEX M. INGLES

Whittier
