941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: East Timor protesters leave US embassy East Timorese protesters flashed victory signs as their bus left the US embassy in Jakarta, where they staged a 12-day sit-in. The 29 protesters were on their way to catch a flight from Indonesia on the first leg of their journey to Portugal, where they have been granted political asylum. Timorese youths have been in the vanguard of fresh protests against Indonesian rule of the former Portuguese colony.
