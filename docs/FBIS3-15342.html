Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 03, BNS -- Russian delegation on talks with Russia experts group, led by Sergejs Zotovs, arrived in Riga Thursday to discuss the draft agreement on Russian military pensioners' social guarantees, the Latvian Cabinet's press center informed BNS. The Russian experts' group will meet Wednesday [9 March] with Latvian experts at Foreign ministry and the discussions will be continued on Friday, said Edgars Skuja, assistant of Latvian delegation head. The next round of talks between Latvia and Russia is scheduled for March 14-16 in Moscow. As BNS already informed, the delegations of both states met in Moscow last week to harmonize the above mentioned draft accord but they did not come to agreement. The draft agreement is one of the documents included in the package of agreements on Russian army withdrawal from Latvia.
