930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Citizen's charter payouts top Pounds 2mMORE than Pounds 2m has been paid in compensation under the prime minister's Citizen's charter initiative, government figures show. The Benefits Agency, which handles social security benefits including income support and state pension, has paid out the most - more than Pounds 1m in just five months. Next largest was British Rail, which handed out Pounds 1m in the first year of its charter.
