BFN [Text] Moscow May 31 TASS -- A total of 350 guards of a separate battalion of the Ukrainian national guards were shifted to Sevastopol between May 17 and 27. The battalion with motorised equipment is stationed on the grounds of an air defence unit of the Ukrainian Defence Ministry, the public relations centre of the Sevastopol Militia Department reports on Tuesday. The report notes that Lieutenant-Colonel Igor Kurovskyy, commander of the guards unit stationed in Sevastopol, said that a decision on deploying such subunits in all the Ukrainian regional centres was taken by President Leonid Kravchuk in 1993. He noted that the main tasks of guardsmen are to protect the Ukrainian constitutional system and to help border troops in detaining border transgressors. In the near future, guardsmen intend to participate in measures to protect law and order, which were planned by the Sevastopol Militia Department.
