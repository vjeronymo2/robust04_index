931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / Shipyard wins Nato contract A & P Appledore's shipyard at Falmouth, Cornwall, has won the contract to refit Nato's research vessel Alliance, which is used to detect and track submarines. The Italian-based vessel is to get new acoustic and oceanographic equipment including an advanced undersea mapping system. It is the only craft jointly owned by all 16 Nato members.
