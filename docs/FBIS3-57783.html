Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl has promised to back the Armed Forces in the dispute over the equipping and functions of the Bundeswehr. [passage omitted] Kohl announced on the private Berlin channel Hundert,6 [as received] today that the Bundeswehr would continue to receive the means necessary to fulfill its functions. While it could not be excepted from savings measures, its efficiency could not be jeopardized in the process, he said. Kohl described proposals to reduce further or even do away with the Bundeswehr as "foolish." "Those who look attentively at the world know that the security situation is not improving," the chancellor said. In this context he mentioned the events in the Balkans and the results gained by the Russian nationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy in the parliamentary elections. [passage omitted]
