January 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

"What Are We Doing to Our Children?," KTLA's program on the foster care and juvenile justice system, presented an honest, realistic and disturbing portrayal of what happens when parents fail and children are left at the mercy of an overwhelmed and ineffective governmental agency. What is more alarming is that, with the increase in drug use, domestic violence, infant abandonments, etc., we are seeing more and more children swept into a whirlpool of instability and danger.

Gary Traxler, North Hollywood
