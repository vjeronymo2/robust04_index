BFN [Text] Strasbourg, June 20 (AFP) -- Swedish parliamentarian Daniel Tarschys took the reins of the 32-nation Council of Europe on Monday [20 June], replacing France's Catherine Lalumiere as secretary general, the organisation said. The Liberal Party MP was elected last month by the Council's parliamentary assembly by 113 votes against 109 for Lalumiere, who had hoped to stay on for a second five-year term.
