BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Makarov] [Text] Syktyvkar, 9 Jun -- The Komi Government resigned en masse on Tuesday [7 June] as Yuriy Spiridonov has taken up the post of head of the Republic of Komi, which, under the new Constitution, is also head of the government. He will have five deputies and it is thought there will be 17 ministries, as well as the administration of the head of the Republic of Komi, working as a single body ensuring the operation of the government he heads. At a session of the republic's Supreme Soviet, Spiridonov warned that in his government the number of civil servants would be cut back considerably, although the professionals would remain on the job. A special parliament resolution passed on Wednesday made provision for appropriations for the material-technological and financial working of the head of the Republic of Komi, setting the salary for the post at 50 times the minimum monthly salary set by Russian legislation.
