BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Leonid Vinogradov] [Text] Vladivostok May 15 TASS -- The fire which broke out at aviation depots of the Pacific Fleet has been extinguished. According to reports which came from the area of emergency, the rain helped fire-fighting brigades and teams of the military put out the flames. Reports which came to ITAR-TASS from unofficial sources say that the explosion was caused by a spark on the territory of the depot. A decision was adopted to return people who had been evacuated from the settlements of Novonezhino, Smolyaninovo, Romanovka, which were in the greatest danger, back to their residential places. The size of the damage caused by the fire is being specified. No casualties were reported. An investigation into the accident has begun.
