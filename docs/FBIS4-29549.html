BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 19 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists Friday staged a frantic war exercise against the North by massively hurling nuclear-capable F-15 fighter-bombers, FA-18 pursuit-assault planes, AV-8 VTOL [Vertical Take-Off and Landing] assault planes, AE-6 electronic jamming planes, KC-135 refueling tankers based in the Pacific and fighter planes of the U.S. Air Force present in South Korea into the sky above Tokjok Islet, Pyongchang, Yongwol, Tongduchon and Kapyong of South Korea, according to military sources.
