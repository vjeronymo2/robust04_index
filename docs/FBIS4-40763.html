BFN [Text] Rio de Janeiro, June 10, RIA -- Russian leaders have taken a general decision to establish an office of the Russian permanent representative at the Organization of American States, Deputy Russian Foreign Minister Georgiy Mamedov said in Belem where the 24th ga OAS takes place. Mr. Mamedov, who heads the Russian delegation to the event, believes that this will "help us to use the enormous potential for cooperation." The russian diplomat and the OAS general secretary signed a memorandum of understanding.
