Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] President Le Duc Anh paid a visit to Ninh Binh Province on 5 and 6 January. He discussed a number of socioeconomic problems in the province with local officials. [passage omitted] Catholic bishops and priests of Phat Diem Diocese expressed their honor to welcome the president during his visit in the district and at the Papal Nuncio Office. They pledged with the president that they would increase their efforts to better their mundane and celestial activities and to develop the traditional unity between Catholics and non-Catholics in order to contribute to building the country.
