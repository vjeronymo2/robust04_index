931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Investment unit set up in Leeds Singer & Friedlander, the merchant bank, is to set up a development capital subsidiary in Leeds, to operate throughout the UK. It will be funded from the merchant bank's resources and will invest upwards of Pounds 500,000 in management buy-outs and buy-ins, capital restructuring, organic expansion and acquisitions. It will not support start-ups or property specul-ation.
