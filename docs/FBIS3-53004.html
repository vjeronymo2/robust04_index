Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Presidential Council vice chairman, received in Aden this afternoon brother al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Jordanian Court, who conveyed to the brother Presidential Council vice chairman a message from his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. The message deals with the brotherly bilateral relations and issues of concern to the two sisterly countries. During the meeting, the two sides discussed means to strengthen the warm brotherly relations. They also exchanged views on current developments. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid expressed his appreciation for the sincere efforts exerted by the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan to help our country overcome the challenges and difficulties. He said he valued the positive role played by the Kingdom of Jordan and its stand that supports unity and democracy in our country. The meeting was attended by brother Salih Nassar (?al-Shini), Aden governor; Colonel Qasim Yahya, commander of the Eastern Operational Sector; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, adviser to His Majesty King Husayn; and Staff Major General 'Id Salim, Jordanian assistant chief of staff.
