BFN [Text] Tunis, 27 Apr (INA)--On behalf of Tunisian President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali, Tunisian Prime Minister Hamed Karoui today received Barzan al-Tikriti, President Saddam Husayn's personal envoy. During the meeting, Barzan al-Tikriti delivered a message from President Saddam Husayn to his brother Tunisian President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali. The message deals with the latest developments at the UN Security Council, Iraq's implementation of UN Security Council resolutions, the UN Security Council's obligations toward lifting the blockade, and the required inter-Arab coordination and consultation to achieve this objective. The message also relays Iraq's stand on the current Arab situation, which requires that the leaf of the past be turned and Arab solidarity revived. During the meeting, Hamed Karoui expressed the solidarity of the Tunisian leadership and people with Iraq. He stressed that Tunisia will spare no effort in the move to put an end to the suffering of the Iraqi people, especially since Iraq has implemented the relevant resolutions. The Tunisian prime minister asked Barzan al-Tikriti to convey President Zine El Abidne Ben Ali's greetings to President Saddam Husayn.
