BFN ["BS-Press" report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "An Attempt on the Lives of Tengiz Kitovani and Tengiz Sigua Was Being Prepared"] [Text] Weapons discovered during a search of the plane leaving Moscow for Tbilisi on 7 May were to be used to kill former Defense Minister Tengiz Kitovani and former Prime Minister Tengiz Sigua, both deputies in the Georgian Parliament. This information was given in a statement delivered by Irakliy Kereselidze, assistant to Tengiz Kitovani, with reference to reliable sources. A sniper rifle, two Kalashnikov assault rifles, and two Makarov pistols and ammunition for them were discovered during a search of the plane at Moscow airport. Kereselidze stated that proceedings had been initiated and the case was being investigated by the Moscow transport militia.
