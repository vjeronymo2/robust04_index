April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DOCTOR DISCIPLINE

I applaud your efforts to enhance the quality of medicine by publishing the medical disciplinary actions taken by the Medical Board of California during the period of Aug. 1, through Nov. 30, 1989 (Part B, April 12).

While we will continue to advocate to ensure that physicians are afforded due process and that reporting is fair and consistent, the Los Angeles County Medical Assn. also recognizes that it is in everyone's best interest that the highest standards of medical care be maintained. I hope that The Times' efforts will help ensure that quality and I will watch for future publications with interest.

CHARLES B. McELWEE, M.D.

President

Los Angeles County Medical Assn.
