BFN [Report by Eytan Rabin and Gay Bekhor] [Excerpts] Joint Lebanese and Syrian Army units raided Hizballah arms caches in the Ba'labakk area two days ago and confiscated weapons, equipment, and drugs. Israeli security sources believe that such activity, if it continues, could have political significance. [passage omitted] The joint raid was directed against arms caches in a Hizballah stronghold in the Ba'labakk area. Large amounts of weapons, explosives, and drugs were seized. Over the past few weeks, the Lebanese Army raided Christian strongholds several times and detained several persons. Israeli sources believe that the Lebanese Army is trying to demonstrate its control in Lebanon and show it is also operating in the northern part of the country. [passage omitted]
