920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / Trucking group secures Y26bn loan A group of 14 creditor banks led by Sanwa and Sumitomo will offer loans of up to Y26bn (Pounds 116.07m) to the Sagawa Kyubin Group to help Tokyo Sagawa Kyubin, which is facing difficulties due to its involvement in a loan scandal, Reuter reports from Tokyo. They are expected to offer Y5bn this month as a first round of emergency operating funds for Sagawa, Japan's second largest trucking group. Banks are understood to have outstanding loans of Y545bn to the Sagawa Kyubin group.
