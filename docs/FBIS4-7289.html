BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the press conference held here for Mongolian and foreign newsmen there has been announced about the creation of the Foundation for Development of Mongolia named after Joseph Laidey [name as received]. As was said by the founders, the new foundation is sets itself the goal of propagating the ideas by the US scholar Joseph Laidey put forth in 1857 the hypothesis claiming that it was Mongolia that was the cradle of humankind on our planet. Besides this main goal the foundation is to promote the development of Mongolistics, preservation of the values of the nomadic civilization and especially its ecological culture. It was reported at the press conference also that the founders of the foundation made up by more than 12 public organizations of the country are intending to institute the prize named after Joseph Laidey which is to be awarded to Mongolian and foreign scholars, figures of culture, art and literature for their contribution to the distinctive development of the Central Asia.
