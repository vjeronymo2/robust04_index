920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / Election 1992 (The Manifestos): Pledges of aid for Wales are issued NEW POWERS for the Welsh Development Agency to work with banks and financial institutions, a new Welsh exports service and an emergency economic programme including public works were promised by Mr Gordon Brown, the shadow trade and industry secretary, in Cardiff yesterday. Mr David Hunt, the Welsh secretary, said the Tories would set up a Welsh Economic Council 'as part of a national economic development drive for Wales'.
