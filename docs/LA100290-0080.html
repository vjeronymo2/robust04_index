October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Bergen Brunswig Corp.: The Orange-based company announced net profit of $66.1 million for the fiscal year, up 39% from the previous year, on revenue of $4.4 billion, up 13% from fiscal 1989. The distributor of pharmaceuticals, health-care products and prerecorded videocassettes attributed the improvement to increased sales and controlled operating expense.
