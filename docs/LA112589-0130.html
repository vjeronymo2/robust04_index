November 25, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN JUAN CAPISTRANO;

DEPARTMENT TO OFFER CHRISTMAS PROGRAMS

Two programs are scheduled by the Community Services Department for the holiday vacation season.

Starting at 4:15 p.m. Monday, a three-week class titled "Visions of Sugarplums" teaches the art of making gingerbread men, stenciled tree ornaments and other goodies. The registration fee is $26 in addition to a $15 supply fee.

From 6:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Dec. 7, a one-day workshop called "Bake With Mom" is offered for parents and children ages 6 or older. The registration is $16, and there is a $10 supply fee.

To sign up, call (714) 493-5911.
