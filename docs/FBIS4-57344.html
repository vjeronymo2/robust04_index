BFN [Text] Finance Minister Rubens Ricupero has ruled out the possibility of the government converting the real in dollars or in other currencies. He declined, however, to make comments on the exchange rate. The minister said savings accounts will not be modified, and that they will continue to be adjusted by the TR [Reference Interest Rate]. He did not identify, however, the factors that will be taken into account for calculating the TR. Ricupero announced that this week he will set the rules for conversion of school monthly fees into real value units.
