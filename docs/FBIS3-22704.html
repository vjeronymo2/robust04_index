338-340 54004804A Beijing ZHONGHUA LIUXINGBINGXUE ZAZHI [CHINESE JOURNAL OF EPIDEMIOLOGY] Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [English abstract of article by Ding Xueliang [0002 1331 5328], Fan Yuling [5400 3768 3781], et al. of the Institute of Prevention and Treatment of Endemic Diseases, Lanzhou] [Text] During April-May 1991, by using SAT, CFT, and Coomb's methods an epidemiological surveillance on brucellosis among human beings and animals in some villages and pasturelands of Su Nan County was conducted. The results were that the serum brucella antibody was found positive in 61 cases (19.06 percent out of 320 human beings) and 49 cases were diagnosed as brucellosis. An investigation on 497 cattle and 1989 sheep with SAT methods was also made. The result showed that 15 sera showing a positive reaction all came from sheep. The rate was 0.75 percent.
