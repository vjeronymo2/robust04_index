BFN [Unattributed report: "Survey"] [Text] According to a survey by the Forsa Institute, Kohl enjoys increased support. Forty-one percent of respondents said they would vote for the chancellor (Christian Democratic Union) in an election, compared to 37 percent the previous week. Twenty-nine percent said they would vote for Scharping, the Social Democratic Party of Germany's [SPD] candidate, compared to 29 percent the previous week. For the first time, the coalition leads the SPD in the popularity table, with the support of 47 percent of respondents (Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union 42 percent and Free Democratic Party 5 percent). The SPD enjoyed the support of 36 percent of the respondents, Alliance 90/Greens 10 percent, Social Democratic Party [PDS] 4 percent, and Republikaner 2 percent. Last week, Alliance 90/Greens and Republikaner had the support of 3 and 2 percent of respondents respectively.
