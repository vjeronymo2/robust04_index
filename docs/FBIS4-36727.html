BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani's written message was handed over to President Francois Mitterrand in Paris by Va'ezi, the deputy foreign minister for Euro-American affairs. According to the Central News Unit, during this meeting the French president stressed the importance of Iran in the region and described the broadening of Tehran-Paris relations. He said: Fortunately, the problems that existed in the path of the expansion of relations between the two countries are in the process of being eliminated. Francois Mitterrand added: France is seeking durable and reliable relations with Iran and the two sides should work toward this end. He expressed his pleasure at the message from President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and called for more frequent contacts between the officials of the two countries. During this meeting, Va'ezi declared the Islamic Republic of Iran's readiness to broaden relations with France in all fields.
