BFN [Text] An Indonesian delegation led by Forestry Minister Jamaludin arrived at Yangon [Rangoon] airport by air this evening at the invitation of Lieutenant General Chit Swe, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of forestry affairs. The visiting minister and delegation later paid a courtesy call on the forestry affairs minister, Lt. Gen. Chit Swe, at the International Business Center on Prome Road at 1630. High-level official talks were held and Lt. Gen. Chit Swe and Mr. Jamaludin delivered opening addresses. Later, Forestry Affairs Minister Lt. Gen. Chit Swe and wife hosted a dinner in honor of the visiting Indonesian minister and entourage at the People's Park Restaurant.
