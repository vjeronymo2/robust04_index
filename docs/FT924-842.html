921222 FT 22 DEC 92 / Tour operator ceases trading NOVOTOUR of Borough Green, Kent, has ceased trading, the Civil Aviation Authority said last night. Novotour's Air Travel Organisers' Licence authorised it for up to 52,500 passengers. The company traded as Cyprus Passport, Passport USA, Florida Passport, Turkey Passport, Airspace, Family Fun Passport, Passport to Florida, American Passport, Flight Passport, Greece Passport, Bulgaria Passport and Summer Fun Passport. The CAA said the few holidaymakers abroad would fly home as planned and there would be a full refund to about 9,500 customers.
