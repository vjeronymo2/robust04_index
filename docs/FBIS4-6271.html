BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 17 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song saw joint performances given by artistes of different countries participating in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival. When he appeared in the auditorium amid the playing of the welcome music, stormy cheers of "hurrah!" and thunderous applause shook the hall. The performances were seen by Kang Song-san, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and premier of the Administration Council, and Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-chu, Politburo members of the WPK Central Committee and vice-presidents, Kim Pyong-sik, vice-president and chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Social Democratic Party, and other cadres and working people in the city. Among the audience were Yi Chin-kyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean residents in Japan (Chongnyon), Choe Hong-hui, president of the International Taekwon-do Federation, and other Korean figures overseas. Invited to see the performances were foreign guests on a visit to Korea, including Huudu Yahaya, general secretary of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana; P.V. Svoik, co-chairman of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan; and Pat Quearney, general secretary of the Workers' Party of Ireland; and foreign diplomatic envoys here. The performers included international and national concours prize winners of different countries and famous artistes who came with boundless feelings of respect for and trust in President Kim Il-song and warm sentiments of friendship toward the Korean people, and Korean artistes from overseas and at home. They extended the highest glory and warmest congratulations to President Kim Il-song on his birthday and gave an impressive artistic representation of his immortal feats and greatness. They also highly sang of their ardent reverence for the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il who has exalted Korea, the homeland of chuche, and is victoriously advancing the socialist cause with his clairvoyance, matchless grit and rare leadership ability. They well showed the sacred struggle, creative labour and customs of different nations who have turned out to accomplish the cause of global independence. The performances were highly acclaimed by the audience for their high ideological and artistic value. President Kim Il-song sent floral baskets to the performers in congratulation of their successful performances. He met foreign artists and delegations of Korean artists from overseas who had participated in the performances, and delegations of Chongnyon and the delegation of the Chinese Ministry of Culture and foreign diplomatic envoys here and posed for souvenir pictures with them.
