930410 FT 10 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Top trust records 332.04% rise - At a glance The best performing unit trust over the past five years was Hypo F & C US Smaller Companies, with a rise of 332.04 per cent, according to Micropal. But Hong Kong and Far Eastern funds hold eight of the top ten positions (see Serious Money). The list of the ten worst funds over the period include the usual suspects such as Waverley Australasian Gold and three Japanse funds.
