BFN [Text] Riga, Apr 20, BNS -- The majority of the Saeima (parliament) factions' leaders support Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis decision to meet with Russian President Boris Yeltsin on April 21, Homeland and Freedom faction head Maris Grinblats told reporters Tuesday [19 April]. As reported, Russian Ambassador to Latvia Aleksandr Rannikh handed over Tuesday to Ulmanis Russian president's invitation to meet with him on April 21. Grinblats said it was not yet clear if the signing of the Latvian-Russian initialed agreements would be possible during this visit. But it is clear already now that the agreement on Russian military pensioners' social guarantees will not be signed in Moscow, said Grinblats. As reported, the Saeima will decide Wednesday, during its extraordinary plenary meeting, if it will authorize Ulmanis to sign the initialed Latvian-Russian agreements.
