Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 19, IRNA -- Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Tehran, Hua Liming, in a meeting with Minister of Cooperatives Gholam Reza Shafe'i here Wednesday briefed him on operation of China's cooperatives and economic development plans. The two sides also discussed economic issues and growth indices of China after introduction of market economy as well as Tehran-Beijing industrial ties. Shafe'i called for further cooperation between the two countries in cooperative related affairs and exchange of experiences and informations on privatization of economic and industrial institutes.
