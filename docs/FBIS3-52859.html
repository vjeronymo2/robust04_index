Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] has commented on the Betzelem report about Arabs cooperating with the authorities in the territories. It says this is the first time that Betzelem has focused on the activities of Arab terrorist organizations vis-a-vis the internal Arab population. During the intifadah, the IDF says, most of the 964 Palestinians murdered by other Palestinians did not cooperate in any way with the IDF. The Army stressed its obligation to do everything required to support those individuals who have aided the Israeli authorities over the years.
