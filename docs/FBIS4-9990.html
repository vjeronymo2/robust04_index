BFN [Editorial report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic developments monitored through 5 April. The Ecuadoran Government has announced the inflation rate for March was 2.64 percent, bringing the yearly total to 31.62 percent. Juan Jose Illingworth, director of the Institute of Statistics and Census, told reporters during a news conference in Quito the government is still determined to attain its goal of reducing inflation to 20 percent by the end of 1994. (Mexico City NOTIMEX in Spanish 2027 GMT 31 March 94) The government has decided to increase prices for super gasoline to 3,000 sucres per gallon. The March currency exchange rate, according to authorities, caused the increase. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 1 April 94) Industry Minister Jose Vicente Maldonado has announced Ecuador will ask the Andean Group meeting to postpone discussions on the common import tariff. The group is scheduled to meet on 4 and 5 April in Caracas. The decision comes in response to a request by private industrialists. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 1 April 94)
