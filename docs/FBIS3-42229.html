Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Khulud al-Ja'uni] [Excerpt] Interior Minister Salamah Hammad has stated that seven people were detained for interrogation in connection with the bomb blasts in Salma cinema house in al-Zarqa' and the Rivoli cinema house in Saqf al-Sayl Street in downtown Amman. He said some of these people were released after interrogation. Hammad said that the dimensions and motives for the two blasts are still unknown. Meanwhile, a team set up by various security agencies is continuing its investigation to identify the perpetrators of the bomb blast that occurred in Salwa cinema house in al-Zarqa' the day before yesterday.
