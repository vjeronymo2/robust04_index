Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Two Palestinian leaders condemned this morning's Zionist crime, during an interview with the Central News Unit in Beirut. They called on the international community to take serious action over the Zionist regime's continuous creation of catastrophes. In this connection, and in a telephone interview with us, Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] spokesman Ibrahim Ghawshah said: [Begin Ghawshah recording, in Arabic with superimposed Persian translation] Such a crime reflects the true criminal nature of Rabin and the Zionist regime. The Palestinian community will continue to walk on the path of martyrdom and struggle until the full liberation of the occupied territories. [end recording] Ahmad Jibril, general secretary of Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine -- General Command [PFLP-GC], also spoke to us in an interview. He emphasized: The ugly crime at the mosque of his holiness Abraham, peace be upon him, today in the holy month of Ramadan reflects the height of savageness and terrorism by the Zionist regime and adds a bleak page to their ugly record alongside the Sabra-Shatila [massacre in September 1982]. He continued: We know the true nature of Zionism, and on this bleak day we say: Has not the time come for the Islamic and Arab world to stand up to rescue the Palestinian people and support them in their struggle against the Zionists and America?
