Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander of the Russian 14th Army in the Dniester region, Lieutenant General Aleksandr Lebed, has issued an order to the units in his command to be firm to the point of using arms in stopping any attempted attacks on installations and servicemen. As the commander's press secretary reported, the decision was made in connection with previous instances of attacks on Russian soldiers and threats from armed mafia groups to harm them and members of their families. This information was received through ITAR-TASS channels.
