February 3, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

MASTER'S WOMEN ROMP BEHIND VARGAS' 23

Kim Vargas scored 23 points and had 7 assists to lead The Master's College to a 65-52 women's basketball victory over Southern California College on Thursday at Master's.

The Mustangs (6-14) led, 26-22, at the intermission and extended their lead throughout the second half.

Susie Sparks scored 13 and Laurel Hudgens and Michele Spink each added 9.
