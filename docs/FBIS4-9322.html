BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 22 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serb forces trapped a UN convoy Friday [22 April] when it began retreating to Sarajevo after failing to reach the besieged Moslem enclave of Gorazde, UNPROFOR spokesman Richard Pernod told AFP. The convoy, made up of some 100 French, British and Ukrainian peacekeepers from the UN Protection Force [UNPROFOR], was on its way to relieve Gorazde when it received orders to head back to Sarajevo but Serb forces stepped in and have stopped its retreat, he said.
