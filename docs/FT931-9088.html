930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Second Alliance net assets improve Net asset value of The Second Alliance Trust stood at Pounds 15.70 per stock unit at the six months to January 31, against Pounds 12.79 at the July 31 year end and Pounds 13.62 a year ago. Available revenue showed a 9 per cent improvement over the 12 months, from Pounds 3.42m to Pounds 3.74m, equivalent to earnings of 19.49p (17.83p) per unit. The interim dividend is raised to 12.5p (12p).
