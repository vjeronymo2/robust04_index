Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] By the decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Georgia, and in order to widen trade and economic cooperation between the Republic of Georgia and the Islamic Republic of Iran, a trade and economy mission of the Republic of Georgia has been established in the Islamic Republic of Iran, the headquarters of which will be set up in the town of Tehran. Teimuraz Patsuria has been appointed the trade and economy representative of the Republic of Georgia in the Islamic Republic of Iran.
