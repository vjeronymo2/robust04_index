941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / Observer: Full stop So the greatest living novelist isn't going after all? The reason why Lord Archer is not at the Conservative party conference, says Jeremy Hanley, party chairman, is that the millionaire author is far too busy in the House of Lords, where Labour's front bench yesterday demanded publication of the Department of Trade and Industry's report into Anglia TV share dealing, which gave Lord Archer star billing. But no, he wasn't there either. The plot curdles.
