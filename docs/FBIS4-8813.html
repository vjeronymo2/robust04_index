BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina, has sent a letter to U.S. President Bill Clinton in which he asks him to find a way of helping the population of Gorazde. The lives of many people and also the reputation of the United States are at stake. If force and deceit triumph, this will not only be a defeat for Bosnia, but also for many important institutions in which we have acted together. We must prevent this defeat, the letter from President Izetbegovic says. President Izetbegovic sent this letter to the heads of state or government of Russia, France, Great Britain, Germany, Turkey, and Saudi Arabia.
