Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Sergey Bablumyan: "Armenian Border Trade with Iran Being Set Up"] [Text] Yerevan -- The Armenian Government has authorized the republic's Megrinskiy Rayon to begin border trade with Iran's neighboring province. Meanwhile, even before the official document was signed, Iranian goods had become a permanent feature on the Armenian market. In the very center of Yerevan there is a brisk trade in paraffin stoves, oriental sweetmeats, textiles, and other goods in increased demand.
