October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH JANICE;

THE REMATCH

A wise woman once said: "It is better to have a short price than a long face." On Saturday, Mobile Phone finished first paying $3.60. Today, I will wager $100 to win on Barrage in the sixth.

Saturday's Profit: $80
