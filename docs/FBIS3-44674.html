Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 5 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren expressed the hope here today that China-Azerbaijan cooperation could be further expanded and its level enhanced. During a meeting with visiting Azerbaijan Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov at the Great Hall of the People this morning, Rong said it was the common aspiration of the two countries to further promote the development of bilateral economic and trade ties with substance. He proposed that bilateral cooperation be developed in various ways and some joint ventures be set up in accordance with the actual needs of both sides. "The Chinese Government encourages its departments, companies and enterprises to carry out multi-form economic cooperation with Azerbaijan," he said, while voicing the hope that the Azerbaijan Government would in turn create a favorable environment for this cooperation. Reviewing the bilateral ties, Rong said the Chinese people cherish fond feelings toward the Azerbaijan people and that bilateral ties have marked progress in every field since the setting-up of diplomatic relations. In response, Gasanov said the Azerbaijan leaders put China on the list of priority countries with which Azerbaijan should develop ties. The two countries should foster even closer ties in more fields, he said, adding that the Azerbaijan Government speaks highly of China's policy of non-interference in other countries' internal affairs and of developing friendly ties on the basis of the five principles of co-existence. During the meeting, Rong also briefed the visitors on China's reform and opening to the outside world. He expressed the belief that Azerbaijan's economy would pick up quickly, given a stable domestic situation and a favorable international environment. This morning, Wu Yi, Chinese minister of foreign trade and economic cooperation, also met Gasanov. They exchanged views on the expansion of bilateral economic and trade relations.
