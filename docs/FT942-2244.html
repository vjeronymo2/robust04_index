940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): The Federal National Mortgage Association (Fannie Mae) - Other Companies The US mortgage agency, will price a Dollars 1.5bn issue of 10-year bonds, its first global offering of securities, on Wednesday. The bonds, arranged by Merrill Lynch and JP Morgan, are likely to yield about 25 basis points more than the comparable US Treasury bond. A global offering for the other large US mortgage agency, the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation (Freddie Mac), arranged by via Salomon Brothers and Goldman Sachs, will follow in the coming weeks.
