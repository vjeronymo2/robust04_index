BFN [Text] The sides that signed the Bishkek meeting's protocol on the peaceful settlement of the Karabakh problem--which was signed by Nagorno-Karabakh, Armenia, Russia, and Kyrgyzstan but not by Azerbaijan--have declared that the agreement is open for signature. Rasul Guliyev, the chairman of the Azerbaijani Parliament, telephoned Babken Ararktsyan, the chairman of the Armenian Supreme Council, on 7 May informing him that Azerbaijan will sign the Bishkek protocol. This was reported by the Supreme Council press service, which also noted that the Armenian Supreme Council has not yet received the document ratifying that protocol.
