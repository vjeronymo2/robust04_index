BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 23 May -- "The Crimean issue is too important to be used as a card in the presidential election campaign in Ukraine." This was said today by Russia's State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin at a meeting with Douglas Hurd, Britain's minister for foreign and Commonwealth affairs [title as received]. The speaker of Russia's lower house also said "that everything happening in Crimea is Ukraine's internal affair," and that "the peoples of the peninsula have the right to self-determination, but within the framework of Ukraine and on the basis of the principles of the CSCE." Cooperation between the two countries' parliaments was also discussed during the meeting. On behalf of the House of Commons, Douglas Hurd invited a Duma delegation headed by Ivan Rybkin to visit Great Britain this December.
