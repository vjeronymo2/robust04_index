December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

IN THE LA QUINTA TOURNAMENT:

Savanna 52, Santa Ana Valley 24 -- Carrie Frederick had 16 points and Stacey Aliff 14 for Savanna (9-1).

Dana Hills 39, Esperanza 36 -- Deanna Ward had 13 points for Dana Hills (7-3).

In the Simi Valley tournament:

Westlake 69, Fountain Valley 43 -- Julie Murdant had 12 for Fountain Valley (4-4).

Santiago 72, Orange 32 -- Becky Elcock scored 23 points for Santiago (2-2).

In the Cypress tournament:

Valencia 85, Hawthorne 17 -- Catheryn Sweet had 23 points for Valencia (6-2).

In a nonleague game:

Magnolia 46, El Dorado 35 -- Diana Cervantes scored 13 for host Magnolia (5-1).
