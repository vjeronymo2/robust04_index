Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] A Pakistani parliamentary delegation led by Senate Chairman Wasim Sajjad arrived in Beijing last night. Speaking to the ASSOCIATED PRESS OF PAKISTAN at the airport, Wasim Sajjad said that during its talks with the Chinese leaders, the delegation will brief them on the latest situation in occupied Kashmir. The regional situation and the problems caused by India along the Pakistani border will also be discussed during the talks. Wasim Sajjad urged China to exercise its rights as a permanent member of the UN Security Council to stop the gross human rights violations and atrocities in occupied Kashmir.
