November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: BASEBALL

Olympic Stadium, home of the Montreal Expos, will be given a $30-million facelift over the next two years that will include a reduction in seating capacity and a new sound system. Expo President Claude Brochu, who is trying to put together a consortium to buy the team from Charles Bronfman for $100 million, stressed that the team will not move to another city.
