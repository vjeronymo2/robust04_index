BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin said he foresees a united Jerusalem under Israeli sovereignty and as Israel's capital also in the permanent arrangement with the Palestinians. Mr. Rabin was speaking to our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky at a reception hosted by Mayor Ehud Olmert at Jerusalem's City Hall to mark the beginning of the Jerusalem Day celebrations. Mr. Rabin noted that Jerusalem is the heart of the Jewish people and, consequently, is the heart of hearts of the State of Israel.
