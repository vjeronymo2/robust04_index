April 14, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: THE ARTS

Mayor Tom Bradley recommended an additional $1.9 million for grants to cultural organizations in his fiscal 1989-1990 city budget proposal Thursday. The recommendation jibes with interim spending plans for the proposed $20 million Los Angeles Endowment for the Arts, the city's new cultural funding program, to become fully operational in July, 1990. If the City Council approves Bradley's proposal, which contains $450,000 for a new citywide arts festival project and an additional $155,000 for the city's mural program, $3 million in grants will be awarded in July. Bradley's action shows "a great effort to upgrade city support for the arts immediately," said Cultural Affairs Department General Manager Al Nodal. ALEENE MacMINN
