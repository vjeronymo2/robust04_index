940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / World Commodities Prices: London coffee futures rally strongly - Market Report London COFFEE futures rallied in thin trading following a sharp rebound in New York as well as industry and arbitrage buying. At the close the November position stood at Dollars 3,414 a tonne, up Dollars 98. COCOA ended lower on technical selling and a downturn in the New York market. Compiled from Reuters
