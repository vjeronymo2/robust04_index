BFN [Text] Munir Hunayn, the judge investigating the assassination of Dany Sham'un, has indicted Dr. Samir Ja'ja, leader of the Lebanese Forces [LF] party, and 12 other LF members of treason. According to article 549 of the criminal code, this offense carries the death penalty. Judge Hunayn referred the suspects to the Judicial Council for trial.
