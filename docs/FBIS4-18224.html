BFN [Ute Semkat report: "Russians Withdraw From Brocken"] [Excerpts] Magdeburg -- The Gauss Point, the highest point on Brocken Mountain, which is 1,142 meters and thus the highest mountain in the Harz region, has been free of military troops since yesterday. After 47 years of occupation the commander of the Russian intelligence unit handed over the former Warsaw Pact monitoring post to the Federal Property Administration yesterday morning. [passage omitted] At the official transfer ceremony yesterday Minister-President Christoph Bergner (Christian Democratic Union) stated that the departure of the Western Group of Russian Forces (WGT) will not be the end of relations between the peoples of the former Soviet Union and the Germans but the beginning of a new, good coexistence.
