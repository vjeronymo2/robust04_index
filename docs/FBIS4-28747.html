BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 10 (KPL) -- A delegation of the Lao Federation of Trade Union (LFTU) headed by Khampan Philavong, member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee (LPRP CC) and LFTU president, returned home on June 8 afternoon ending his working visit to China, Mongolia, and Thailand. During the visit, the LFTU delegation had meetings with its counterparts to inform each other of the situation in their respective countries and drew lessons from each other. The visit was aimed at enhancing solidarity and cooperation with the trade unions of those countries in the future. The delegation also visited economic and cultural sites in each country.
