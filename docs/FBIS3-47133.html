Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Chinese Premier Favors Negotiated Solution of Nuclear Problem" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 10 (KCNA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng at a talk with the Japanese foreign minister Saturday emphasized that the nuclear problem on the Korean peninsula would be resolved through negotiations, according to a XINHUA report. Li said China supports the non-nuclear proposal and stability and peace there. "We oppose any coercive way or embargo, which will inevitably make the problem more complicated," Li said.
