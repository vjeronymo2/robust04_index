940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / Ex-Nissan chief to appeal to Lords Former Nissan UK managing director Mr Michael Hunt was yesterday given leave to appeal to the House of Lords against his conviction for involvement in a Pounds 55m tax fraud. The Court of Appeal, which rejected Mr Hunt's initial challenge in May, certified that the case raised a point of law of general public importance enabling a petition to the law lords for leave to appeal.
