Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada has also commented on the clashes that occurred in Chapare. During a news conference, the president commented on the drug trafficking issue. He said Bolivia and his administration are coexisting with a time bomb because Law 1008 obliges the government to eradicate 1 hectare of coca for every cocaine maceration pit that is found. He said this is the cause of the problems. [Begin Sanchez de Lozada recording] This causes the problem: We have been finding large production centers of cocaine base, which is purchased by the main cocaine producers who transform this base into cocaine. This concerns us. [end recording] Asked if his administration will defend coca production, he said: [Begin Sanchez de Lozada recording] Of course we will defend it. Coca is part of our society. It is a consumer product, but we cannot protect coca production in the Chapare region where it is grown exclusively for drug production, for cocaine production. You know very well that if you offer Chapare coca leaves to miners, they will refuse to chew them. Miners and peasants only chew coca grown in Yungas. This, however, is no reason to simply eradicate this coca and fill our country with violence. We must find solutions to this problem and these solutions can only be found through dialogue. Pressure from abroad is great and domestic damage is also great. We cannot forget that Bolivia is already beginning to have (?addicts) and drug consumption problems. Cocaine base is a very bad drug. [end recording]
