Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 7 Mar (MENA)--An explosive device was detonated in front of the Badr Hotel near Asyut's railroad station. The loud explosion, which occurred shortly after the fast breaking evening meal today, rocked the area but caused no casualties or property damage. The security forces and explosives experts defused another time bomb in the Banks Square in city. The security forces cordoned off the area and mounted a search for possible suspects.
