921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Blackland Oil losses rise to Pounds 203,000 Blackland Oil, the USM-quoted gas and oil exploration and production group, reported increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 203,000 for the first half of 1992, against Pounds 132,000. The figure was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 91,000 arising from the reorganisation and the cost of the extraordinary meeting in April. Gross revenues were Pounds 172,000 (Pounds 179,000). Losses per share came out at 2.04p (1.46p).
