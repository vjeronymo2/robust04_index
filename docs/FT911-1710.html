910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / Appointments: Lloyds Bank post Mr Martin Cruttenden, a general manager, corporate banking, at LLOYDS BANK, has been appointed general manager, risk management, from June 1. He has been with the bank for 36 years, latterly responsible for capital markets activities and Lloyds Bank Registrars. He will be succeeded by Mr Iain Cheyne, currently general manager, strategic planning. ***** Mr MF Burgess, chairman and chief executive of The James Crosby Group, has joined the main board of THE BERKELEY GROUP, with responsibility for developing operations in north UK. The Berkeley Group has acquired The James Crosby Group.
