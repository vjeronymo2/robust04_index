931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Delyn falls Pounds 142,000 into the red EXCEPTIONAL charges of Pounds 591,000 pushed Delyn Group, the manufacturer of consumer packaging and materials, Pounds 142,000 into the red at the pre-tax level for the 26 weeks ended August 1. That compared with previous profits of Pounds 500,000 and was struck from turnover of Pounds 6.71m (Pounds 6m). Losses per share worked through at 3.55p (earnings 3.94p). The interim dividend is held at 0.5p. The exceptional provision related to Delyn's holding in Plastella which was disposed of in May.
