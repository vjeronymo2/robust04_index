BFN [Text] Yasir 'Arafat is demanding that Israel immediately transfer to the Palestinians self-government powers in all of the West Bank. 'Arafat raised this demand during his meeting with President 'Ezer Weizman in Pretoria. This was learned by our correspondent 'Imanu'el Halperin. Prime Minister Rabin was briefed about this development. The Swiss president, who also met with Mr. Weizman in South Africa, proposed to establish a sixth committee in the framework of the multilateral talks. His proposal calls for establishing a committee to deal with human rights in the region. The deliberations of this committee would be coordinated by Switzerland. Israel will examine the proposal.
