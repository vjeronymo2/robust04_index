BFN [Text] His Excellency Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN secretary general's special envoy to Yemen, arrived in the country this afternoon on a visit to the sultanate. He was received on arrival by His Excellency Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs, and a number of ranking Foreign Ministry officials. Upon arrival, his excellency said during his visit to the sultanate, he will have talks with a number of ranking officials focusing on the situation in Yemen. He said he held similar talks in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. He said the Sultanate has known amicable relations with all parties in Yemen, and that he counts on the officials in the sultanate, led by his majesty the sultan and his excellency minister of state for foreign affairs, to facilitate the mission and to render it successful, thanks to these relations and the Sultanate's known role on the Arab level.
