April 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ANTI-ABORTIONIST STIRS STRONG REACTIONS

Thanks for your article on Susan McMillan, the outspoken pro-life activist.

Many papers are shrinking from presenting objective coverage on people who are "on the wrong side" of the abortion issue -- those who believe it takes life. Susan has generated much controversy because of her stance against abortion and because she is articulate, wealthy, and visible. I felt your reporter captured McMillan's warmth and depth of conviction fairly, and let her quotes stand for themselves.

KRISTY HILLER, Irvine
