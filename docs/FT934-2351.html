931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports; landed good general demand. Coloury Assams were well supported at fully firm rates but poor types were again difficult of sale. Brightest liquoring and better medium East Africans were mainly dearer by 3 to 8p, others were generally fully firm except the plainest. Ceylons sold well at fully firm rates although plainer sorts drifted lower. Offshore fair demand with brighter Kenyas tending dearer, Ceylons fully firm. Plainer Kenyas met less demand at lower levels. Quotations: quality 220p/kg, good medium 145p/kg medium 112p/kg low medium 98p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 256 for a Rwanda pd.
