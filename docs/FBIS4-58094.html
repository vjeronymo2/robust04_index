BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 May (MENA)--The Fatah Central Committee, the PLO mainstream faction, has approved the draft Palestinian-Israeli agreement scheduled to be signed by Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin in Cairo on 4 May. The approval came during a four-hour meeting which the Fatah Central Committee held today under 'Arafat to discuss the final draft of the agreement. Palestinian chief negotiator Dr. Nabil Sha'th last night briefed the committee on the details of the agreement. The agreement was approved with a majority of votes of the 18-member committee. Hani al-Hasan, Khalid al-Hasan, and 'Abbas Zaki were absent from the meeting. For over a week the committee discussed the details of the draft, which consists of over 90 pages containing the details of the agreement which the Palestinian and Israeli negotiators have reached after months of talks in Cairo.
