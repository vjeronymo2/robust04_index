Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Geydar Aliyev Recognizes Karabakh a Conflict Side"--SNARK headline] [Text] Moscow, March 12 (SNARK) -- The declaration recently done by the Azerbaijani leader Geydar Aliyev could be considered a sensation, Felix Mamikonyan, an Armenian ambassador to Russia, told journalists at his press-conference. Geydar Aliyev said he is ready to consider the status of the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh if only the Karabakh troops leave for their territory. Thus, the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh is officially recognized a conflict side, though Azerbaijan was always avoiding doing it. But, according to Mamikonyan, the Aliyev's declaration gives no mechanism of conflict regulation. Mr. Mamikonyan also confirmed Karabakh for numerous times declared he has a claim on the lands of Azeris and is ready to set them free as soon as the problem of Nagorno-Karabakh security is solved.
