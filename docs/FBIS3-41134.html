Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of fileworthy reports on antinarcotics operations in countries covered by FBIS London Bureau from 21 January to 3 February. Sources are given at the end of each report.

Italy
Carabinieri from the Caserta (southern Italy) Anti-Mafia Investigative Directorate discovered a cocaine refinery at Coppola Pineta Mare on the coast near Unizzio. Sixty kilograms of cocaine were seized and five people were arrested. (Rome RAI Due Television Network in Italian 1045 GMT 26 Jan)
Kyrgyzstan
A cargo of marijuana was detained at the airport of Kyrgyzstan's capital, Bishkek. Six and a half kilograms of marijuana were hidden in a defunct refrigerator in the waiting hall. The border guards found it just before the departure of a foreign plane. (Moscow Radio Moscow World Service in English 0800 GMT 1 Jan)
Spain
Three people have been arrested in La Linea de la Concepcion (near Gibraltar). They are being accused of belonging to a drug trafficking network after they were found in the possession of 149 kilos of hashish. (Madrid RNE-1 Radio Network in Spanish 1600 GMT 29 Jan)
