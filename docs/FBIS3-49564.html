Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 10 Jan (PAP) -- Two hundred and forty-nine railway transit journeys of Russian Federation troops (3,627 cars) passed through Poland from Germany (?to Russia in the fourth quarter) of last year. This accounted for 71 percent of all transport planned for that period, according to Major General Zdzislaw Ostrowski, the Polish Republic Government plenipotentiary for the stationing of Russian Federation troops in Poland. Transport operations were also carried out by air: During the same period 198 aircraft flew from Germany to Russia and 281 from Russia to Germany. The Russian military mission submitted reports on 83 railway transit journeys for January. During the Russian troops' transit operations no incidents, accidents or "other events interfering with the transit process or public order" were noted, the plenipotentiary's statement notes.
