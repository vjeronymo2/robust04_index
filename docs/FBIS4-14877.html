BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow 4 April, TASS -- Prime Minister of the Czech Republic Vaclav Klaus arrived in Moscow on Monday to discuss trade relations and the problem of Soviet debt inherited by Russia. He is to meet his Russian counterpart Viktor Chernomyrdin. An agreement on promoting investments and a protocol on shipments of goods for 1994 are expected to be signed. Later in the day Klaus will address students and professors of the Financial Academy on Czech experience of market reforms.
