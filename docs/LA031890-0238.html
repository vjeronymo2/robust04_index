March 18, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP SOFTBALL ROUNDUP

In the 4-A Division of the Canyon tournament:

Sunny Hills 1, Moreno Valley 0 (eight innings) -- Suzanne Hernandez doubled in Bernie Fancuberta with none out in the bottom of the eighth inning to advance Sunny Hills to the championship third round.

La Habra 5, Walnut 2 -- Stephanie Bakki had two hits, including a solo home run, for La Habra (5-2).
