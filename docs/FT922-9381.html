920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / World Trade News: Ansaldo group in US rail deal Union Switch and Signal, the US subsidiary of Italy's state-owned Ansaldo Group, has won a L72bn (Pounds 32.6m) contract to build and install the first driverless light railway system in the US, writes Haig Simonian in Milan. The contract is for the new 'green line' of the Los Angeles subway system. The 20-mile line, covering 14 existing stations and two under construction, will be entirely run from a single control unit, which is also to be supplied and installed by the company. The line is expected to begin operation at the end of 1994.
