930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD shares held on to early gains in spite of a late fall in the bullion price to below Dollars 370 an ounce. The golds index put on 5 at 1,752 as industrials slipped 5 to 4,628 and the overall index 6 to 4,028. Anglos lost 50 cents to R141.50.
