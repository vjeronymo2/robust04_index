Measures LD0904165694 Moscow ITAR-TASS in English 1554 GMT 9 Apr 94 LD0904165694 Moscow ITAR-TASS English BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Gennadiy Kochuk] [Text] Moscow April 9 TASS -- Bogota is ready to share its experience in anti-drug trafficking measures with Moscow, Colombian Foreign Minister Nohemi Sanin Posada de Rubio told ITAR-TASS today. The minister had a meeting with correspondents at the Russian Foreign Ministry's Diplomatic Academy. Colombia understands Russian concern over the fact Russia has attracted drug dealers, said Nohemi Sanin. Colombia has been taking anti-drug trafficking measures for many years and has gained certain experience in this sphere it is ready to share with Russia. Drug business can be counteracted with joint measures, which are envisaged by the treaty on fundamentals of bilateral relations signed by Nojemi Sanin and Andrey Kozyrev on results of their negotiations.
