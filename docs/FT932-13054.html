930424 FT 24 APR 93 / UK Company News: Pressure on margins affects Air London Air London International, the USM-quoted air charter broker, blamed the continuing downturn in activity and increased pressure on margins for a decline in profits in the six months to January 31. On turnover down from Pounds 7.2m to Pounds 6.95m the pre-tax result fell to Pounds 411,000 against Pounds 671,000. Earnings emerged at 3.17p (5.03p) and the interim dividend is held at 1.6p.
