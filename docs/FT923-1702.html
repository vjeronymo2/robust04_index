920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Winning clients and influencing people - In A Nutshell Large numbers of senior managers made redundant during the recession have become independent consultants without any training in their new role. This may make them less than effective in marketing themselves to clients or in providing a satisfactory service. Four-day courses in becoming a successful consultant will be run by GMS Executive Leasing* from October, covering subjects such as consulting skills, financial management and winning clients. Courses start at Pounds 850 rising to Pounds 1,950. *Tel 0582 666970.
