941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Fleming raises Pounds 25m Fleming has so far raised Pounds 25m during the placing stage for its Natural Resources investment trust, which will specialise in companies involved in extracting, cultivating and processing commodities worldwide. A public offer to raise up to a further Pounds 25m closes on November 23. The trust will have a life of between two and a half and five years, to make the best use of the commodities cycle. A commodities investment trust launched last month by BZW raised Pounds 78m from a placing and public offer.
