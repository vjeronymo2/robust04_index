Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, December 30 (ATA) -- Most of the people arrested at the sport banquet for declaring the best sportsmen of the year organized on December 28 and which was broken up by Serbian police were set free. Nevertheless Dr. Mazllum Belegu, president of the Sports Federation of the Republic of Kosova, and Azis Saliu, deputy chairman of the Olympic Committee of Kosova and president of Full-Contact Federation of Kosova, remain under arrest.
