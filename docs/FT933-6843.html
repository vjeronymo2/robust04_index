930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / UK Company News: River Merc Smaller River & Mercantile Smaller Companies Trust saw its net asset value rise from 99.27p to 132.2p per share over the 12 months to July 31. The 33 per cent advance matched the rise in the FT-SE SmallCap Index (Excluding Investment Trusts), and outperformed the FT-A All-Share Index, which rose some 27 per cent over the same period. Attributable revenue improved to Pounds 1.02m (Pounds 924,000) for earnings of 4.07p (3.69p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 2.75p lifts the total for the year to 4p (3.9p).
