July 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MOBSTER GENE GOTTI GETS 50-YEAR PRISON SENTENCE

Gene Gotti, brother of reputed Gambino mob boss John Gotti, was sentenced Friday to 50 years in prison for running a multimillion-dollar drug ring. Co-defendant John Carneglia also was given 50 years, and both defendants were fined $75,000.
