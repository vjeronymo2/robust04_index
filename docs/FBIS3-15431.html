Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Yakovlev] [Text] Prague, 6 Mar -- Peace in the Transcaucasus cannot be attained without Russia's decisive participation, Eduard Shevardnadze, chairman of Georgia's State Council, stressed here today at a meeting with journalists. En route to the United States, he made a brief stopover in Prague, where he had a meeting with Vaclav Havel, president of the Czech Republic. The Georgian leader pointed at "mutual understanding" in his relations with Russia and with Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin. A Georgian embassy is expected to be opened in Prague in the near future to represent the republic's interests in the Czech Republic and in other Central European countries.
