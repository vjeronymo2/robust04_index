941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Worthington up 60% Worthington Group, the buttons and trimmings manufacturer, yesterday announced a 60 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 508,000 to Pounds 811,000, helped by a contribution of Pounds 233,000 from Henderson Holmes & Reiss, acquired in February. Sales in the period to September 30 rose 64 per cent to Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 7.49m) and included Pounds 4.1m from the acquisition. The interim dividend has been increased to 0.7p (0.6p). Earnings per share on increased capital came to 2.38p (1.89p).
