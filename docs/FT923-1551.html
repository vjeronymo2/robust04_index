920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / International Company News: YSL in sharp fall at halfway YVES Saint Laurent (YSL), the Paris fashion house, suffered a sharp fall in net profits from FFr41m in the first half of 1991 to FFr2.6m (Dollars 506,000) in the same period this year, reflecting the depressed state of the global luxury goods market, writes Alice Rawsthorn. The company stressed that the first half was traditionally the slackest part of its year.
