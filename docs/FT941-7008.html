940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / Finance and the Family: Barclays' gold card perk - At a Glance Barclays is introducing free annual travel insurance on its Premier Gold Card, a chargecard carrying an annual fee of Pounds 80. The insurance, available from March 1, covers the cardholder, spouse and dependent children aged under 18 for unlimited worldwide trips. The main benefits are Pounds 1m medical expenses, Pounds 1m personal liability, Pounds 200,000 personal accident, Pounds 3,000 cancellation and Pounds 2,500 baggage cover.
