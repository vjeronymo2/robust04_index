921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Tribune in IPounds 2m loss Tribune Group, the Irish newspaper company, reported a pre-tax deficit of IPounds 2.03m (Pounds 2.15m) for the 15 months to July 1. That compared with losses of IPounds 2.34m for the year to end-March 1991. The board intends to eliminate the group's two-tier structure by making the publications side a wholly owned subsidiary of the newspapers arm.
