Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Security forces in Gaza have captured a Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] member on the wanted list. Subhi Abu-Khalil is suspected of having killed Palestinians who were believed to have cooperated with Israel. In the village of 'Anabta in Samaria, a Palestinian was wounded when soldiers opened fire on stone-throwers. [passage omitted] An Israeli was lightly wounded in the head when he was hit by a stone while he was driving on the Ben Shemen-Jerusalem road, near the village of Bayt 'Ur al-Tahta. The Tel Aviv Magistrates Court has sentenced Gaza resident Masrah Barbah to life in prison, after finding him guilty in the murder of Moshe Becker, a citrus grower from Rehovot, two months ago. Another suspect in the murder, Hani Abu-Sittah, denied the charges and is currently being tried.
