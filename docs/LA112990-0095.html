November 29, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

Petite France, 1835 Newport Blvd., Suite D-156, Costa Mesa. Open daily, except Sunday, 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. (714) 650-9363.

The quality of the food -- from beef Bourguignon in red wine sauce to prawns sauteed with cilantro -- is light years beyond the nearest Happy Star. Yet most entrees are under $5. RANDY LEWIS
