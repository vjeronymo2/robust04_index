August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL EARNINGS: PERFECTDATA POSTS STRONGEST 1ST QUARTER IN ITS HISTORY

PerfectData Corp., a Simi Valley maker of cleaning and maintenance supplies for computer equipment, reported a $138,000 profit in its fiscal first-quarter that ended June 30.

A year earlier, the company earned less than $1,000 in the quarter.

The higher profit came on an 84% jump in PerfectData's sales, to $1.42 million from $773,000. The company attributed the gains to recent restructuring efforts and said it was the strongest fiscal first quarter in its history.
