January 12, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

SAN JOSE STATE WOMEN DEFEAT UC IRVINE

Lora Alexander scored 20 points to lead San Jose State to a 70-51 victory over UC Irvine in a Big West Conference women's basketball game at San Jose State Thursday.

It was San Jose State's first victory ever over UC Irvine (1-11 overall, 0-3 in conference play).

In nonconference women's basketball:

Sheldon Jackson (Alaska) 72, Southern California College 67 -- Gayle Brown had 26 points and 10 rebounds for Southern California Christian (8-5).
