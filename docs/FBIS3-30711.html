Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bratislava, Feb 3 (CTK) -- [passage omitted] Slovakia overdrew the credit in the clearing with the Czech Republic by $50 million, Vice-Governor of the Slovak National Bank (NBS) Marian Jusko tells the opposition SME. The balance is exceptional and it should be reduced substantially in next months, he adds. Half of the January amount resulted from paying installment for credit drawn sooner, the balance will presumably be lower later on, Jusko says. The foreign reserves of the NBS, excluding gold, reached $384.7 million as of January 31, Jusko reports. [passage omitted]
