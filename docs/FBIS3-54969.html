Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 5 Jan -- The ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned from sources at the command headquarters of the Russian frontier troops in Tajikistan that yesterday afternoon a well armed grouping of Tajik fighters tried to invade the territory of Tajikistan from Afghanistan in the sector of the outpost of the Pyandzh frontier detachment (in the south of the republic). As a result of the armed clash, which lasted approximately five hours, two of the trespassers were killed and the remainder returned to the adjacent bank of the river. Yesterday two armed groups also tried to break through in several sections of the Pyandzh frontier detachment from the territory of Afghanistan. They were, however, halted by the fire of the Russian frontier guards. There are no casualties among the Russian servicemen. At the present time, it was stressed at the command headquarters, the situation in the sector of the Pyandzh frontier detachment is being kept under control by the joint actions of the Russian frontier guards and the sub-units of Tajikistan's National Security Committee.
