940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Spielberg film scoops the Oscar pool Holocaust drama Schindler's List swept the board at the Academy Awards in Los Angeles, winning seven Oscars for Steven Spielberg, including best director and best film. It was Spielberg's first Oscar success after a string of box-office hits. British animator Nick Park (left) won the Oscar for best animated short film for his 30-minute feature The Wrong Trousers. Tom Hanks won the best actor award for his role in Philadelphia, the first mainstream film dealing with Aids, and Holly Hunter was named best actress for her portrayal of a mute in The Piano.
