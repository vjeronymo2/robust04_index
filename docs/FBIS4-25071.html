BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 6 (XINHUA) -- The Hangzhou Intermediate People's Court today announced that it will hold an open trial of suspects in the Qiandao Lake incident, in Hangzhou city, capital of Zhejiang Province on June 10. The suspects in the case were accused of involvement in the March 31 robbery and murder of 32 Taiwan and mainland tourists and the subsequent torching of their tour boat on Qiandao Lake in Zhejiang Province. They were detained on April 18, arrested on April 19 and sent to the procuratorate on April 24.
