June 28, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

FLORIDA LOAN OFFICE DEATH TOLL REACHES 10

A worker who was wounded last week when a gunman opened fire in a loan office died Wednesday, raising the death toll to 10.

Jewell Belote, 50, a stenographer at General Motors Acceptance Corp., died of complications at University Medical Center, hospital spokesman Vince Scolaro said.

The 10 dead include James Edward Pough, who turned the .30-caliber rifle on himself after his June 18 rampage.
