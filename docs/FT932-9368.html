930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / Daiwa ex-employees held Two former employees of Daiwa Securities, Japan's second-largest brokerage, were arrested yesterday on suspicion of defrauding two companies of Y2bn (Pounds 11.7m), AP reports from Tokyo. They told the unidentified companies they were conducting a campaign to expand Daiwa's business of storing stocks and bonds, police said. The companies handed these over and the two then allegedly diverted them for their own use.
