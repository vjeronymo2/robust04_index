Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 21 (SNARK) -- The so called "regime number 8" is retained in Yerevan. It signifies that the population receives electricity only for an hour or 40 minutes a day. Samvel Mavisakalyan, the Deputy Mayor of Yerevan, has informed of it SNARK today. In his opinion it would be possible to supply people with 4 hours of electricity only if necessary amount of gas reach Armenia. Mavisakalyan told Yerevan receives the 40 per cent of all the electricity produced in Armenia.
