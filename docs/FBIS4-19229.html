BFN [Unattributed report: "Official Start of Macedonian Embassy"] [Text] The newly appointed and authorised Ambassador of Macedonia [FYROM -- The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] to Great Britain and Northern Ireland Risto Nikovski formally began his activities, by delivering credentials to Anthony Feagis, head of Britain's diplomatic corps and under secretary in the Foreign Office. Ambassador Nikovski will hand over credentials to Queen Elizabeth at a special ceremony in the Buckingham Palace, during the second half of May.
