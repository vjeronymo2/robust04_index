930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / International Company News: Kepco up 6.2% on higher sales KOREA Electric Power (Kep-co), the largest capitalised company on the Seoul bourse, reported a profits rise of 6.2 per cent to Won736.6bn (Dollars 930m) in 1992. Sales rose by 17.4 per cent to Won6,695bn. Earnings were lower than had been expected by analysts.
