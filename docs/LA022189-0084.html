February 21, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Eli Lilly & Co. has announced a two-for-one stock split, which will take the form of a 100% stock dividend payable to shareholders of record on March 31 . . . General Communication Inc. announced an agreement in principle to acquire Transalaska Data Systems. Terms weren't disclosed . . . Deerfield, Ill.-based Baxter International Inc. will increase the dividend on the company's common stock 12%. The dividend is payable April 3 to stockholders of record on March 15 . . . Mayo Clinic announced plans to double the size of its Sun Belt facilities in Scottsdale, Ariz., and Jacksonville, Fla. Total cost for the two projects is expected to be $61 million.
