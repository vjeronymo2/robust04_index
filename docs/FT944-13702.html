941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks ended down, pulled lower by a sluggish Wall Street, interest rate jitters and a slumping US dollar, traders said. The TSE 300 closed down 23.25 points to 4,242.77 on volume of 46.20m shares valuled at CDollars 534m. Declines led advances 502 to 252, with 330 issues unchanged. Weak sectors were led by gold and precious metals, down 82.05 points to 10462.57. Placer Dome was the most active gold issue, down CDollars 5/8 to CDollars 31 1/2 on 558,513 shares. All 14 sub-indices on the Toronto exchange ended weaker, led by losses in gold, forestry and transportation.
