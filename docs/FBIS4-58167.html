BFN [Text] Tehran, May 2, IRNA -- Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i received the visiting Vietnamese President Le Duc Anh here Monday and praised the bravery and resistence of the Vietnamese people in defending their dignity during the war. The leader described as "highly positive" the perception of the Iranians of Vietnamese people and pointed to their resistance against the aggressor as a common point between the two nations and a suitable ground for the expansion of future relations. The Vietnamese president in response called as very "fruitful" his talks with President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani and lauded the Iranians' resistance during the eight-year imposed war on Iran. Anh also stressed that Hanoi was interested in cementing cordial relations with Tehran and expanding bilateral cooperation in political, economic, cultural, scientific and technological fields. President Rafsanjani and a number of high-ranking officials were also present in the meeting. The Vietnamese president arrived here last Saturday on a three-day official visit. In another development, Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati and Vietnamese president discussed expansion of relations in all fields, upgrading of Tehran-Hanoi cooperation at international forums and other issues of mutual interest in a meeting here Sunday. During the meeting Anh reiterated promotion of Tehran-Hanoi ties in all fields and said that formation of the joint commission had played a significant role in further cementing bilateral cooperation. He also stressed the need for further contacts between the two countries as a means to pave the way for future agreements. Referring to the first session of Iran-Vietnam joint commission which was recently held in Tehran, Velayati said it indicated that the two countries have gained major achievements in bilateral relations. Pointing to regional issues including the Non-Aligned Movement (NAM), Velayati said that Tehran-Hanoi cooperation in the organization was a good example of south-south relations. At the end of the meeting Anh underlined the need for Tehran-Hanoi further cooperation in NAM, cementing of ties between South-South countries and further rapprochement of the South-North states.
