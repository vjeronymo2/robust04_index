May 13, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. EARNINGS: FIRST AMERICAN BOUNCES BACK IN FIRST PERIOD

First American Financial Corp., the Santa Ana parent company of First American Title Insurance Co., reported net income of $852,000 for the first quarter, contrasted with a loss of $1.3 million for the first quarter of 1989. Revenue declined 1% to $163.6 million.
