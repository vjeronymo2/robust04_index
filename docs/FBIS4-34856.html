BFN [Text] Rene Diaz Olivera, vice minister of domestic trade, stated that effective 1 July, stores that sell articles for travel abroad will close down. Diaz explained that this measure was taken in light of the current shortage of supplies, which became more acute at the end of last year and continues this year. For this reason, stores such as El Baluarte, La Internacional, and Lujos, which sell shoes, clothing, and other goods for travelers, will close down on 1 July. Diaz added that organizations and enterprises with an established clientele will receive a letter giving more details.
