BFN [Text] AL-NAHAR has learned from well-informed Palestinian sources that prominent members of the Fatah Central Committee have refused to return to Jericho. During the committee's recent meeting, the members said they were not prepared to enter Jericho under the current circumstances. Only Intisar al-Wazir, alias Umm Jihad, and Salim al-Za'nun have agreed to enter Jericho. The Central Committee members who refused to return are Faruq Qaddumi; Mahmud 'Abbas, alias Abu Mazin, who signed the agreement; 'Abbas Zaki; Muhammad Jihad; Sakhr Habash; Muhammad Ghunaym; Hani al-Hasan; and Khalid al-Hasan.
