BFN [Text] There are efforts to arrange a meeting between President 'Ezer Weizman and Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', both of whom are due to arrive in South Africa today to attend Nelson Mandela's inauguration ceremony as president. The Syrians have shown interest in a meeting, but have not made any commitment. President Weizman, who leaves for Johannesburg this morning by Air Force plane, will meet with a number of political figures, including PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat, Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, U.S. Vice President Al Gore, and perhaps Jordan's King Husayn, during his visit.
