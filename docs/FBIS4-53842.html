BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 23 (KCNA) -- A delegation of NODONG SINMUN headed by Deputy Editor-in-Chief Kim Taek-hun left here today for a visit to China. It was seen off at Pyongyang Railway Station by So Tong-pom, deputy editor-in-chief of NODONG SINMUN, and a councillor of the Chinese Embassy in Pyongyang.
