930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Incentives urged for training plan THE GOVERNMENT was yesterday urged to introduce incentives for employers to take up Investors in People, the national standard which measures how effectively training is related to a company's business needs. Mr Edward Roberts, chairman of G10, the group comprising chairmen of the Training and Enterprise Councils which deliver government training schemes in England and Wales, suggested tax incentives for small to medium-sized companies that took up the scheme.
