Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Talca, Carabineros have arrested Misael Apablaza Basolado, 30, the third in command of the Manuel Rodriguez Front, FMR, Autonomous Faction. He was captured on Highway No. 5 at Talca, where he was going to contact other criminals, but was intercepted by Carabineros who already were alerted to his arrival. The terrorist was wounded in one leg when he tried to escape. He was admitted to the Talca regional hospital, where he is recovering. Apablaza is regarded by police as the FMR Autonomous Faction leader responsible for recruiting minors for the FMR Autonomous Faction.
