BFN [Text] Sergey Filatov, the Russian president's chief of staff, expressed his regret that the democratic forces of the country were not united and called for convening a Congress of Russia's Democratic Forces. Talking to journalists outside the hall where the founding congress of the Russia's Democratic Choice was held, he said that such a forum could be convened next fall to overcome the disunity. Asked whether he would join Russia's Democratic Choice, whose congress he had helped to organize, Filatov said that he would do so if the platform of that organization suited him.
