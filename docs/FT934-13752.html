931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Golden Vale Pounds 5.4m purchase Golden Vale, the County Cork-based dairy products company, has agreed to acquire Haslington Cheese Company and Haslington Food Ingredients for a total of Pounds 5.35m. Of the consideration, Pounds 2.5m is in cash, Pounds 1.75m in loan notes and the balance in shares - 1.1m at the current share price of 100p.
