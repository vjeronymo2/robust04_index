November 5, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

The Dana Point Planning Commission will hold a public hearing Tuesday on a proposal by the Stein-Brief Group Inc., which hopes to build its headquarters on the northwest corner of Ritz-Carlton Drive and Coast Highway.

Stein-Brief proposes building a "modernistic structure" with two levels of subterranean parking. The structure, designed by Chicago architect Helmut Jahn, would have an exposed steel frame with sides of teak and clear glass.

The public hearing will begin at 7 p.m. at 34052 Del Obispo St.
