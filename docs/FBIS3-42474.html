Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article by RIA: "President Blessed `Contractees'"] [Text] On 18 January, President of the Russian Federation Boris Yeltsin signed a decree "On the Contract Recruitment of Citizens for Military Service in the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation in 1994." In accordance with the decree, the Ministry of Defense is instructed to accept up to 150,000 men for military service by contract in 1994 for the purpose to ensuring the gradual transition to manning of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation with citizens recruited by contract.
