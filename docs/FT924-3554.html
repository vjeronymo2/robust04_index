921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Hambros Investment net asset value lower Hambros Investment Trust, a subsidiary of Hambros, had a net asset value of 46.44p at September 30 against 46.54p a year earlier and 46.78p at the March 31 year end. There was a net loss of Pounds 175,000 for the half year compared with profits of Pounds 71,000, which translated into losses per share of 0.34p (earnings 0.08p). The deficit resulted from the sale of and revaluation of investments.
