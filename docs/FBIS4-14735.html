BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol March 31 TASS -- By supporting the Rossia Bloc's programme in the Crimean plebiscite, residents confirmed the outcome of the recent presidential elections in the peninsula, newly-elected president Yuriy Meshkov told today's news conference on the results of the people's vote and Crimean parliamentary polls. According to official results, over one million Crimean residents came to the polls, of which 78 per cent spoke in favour of restoring the provision of the 1992 Crimean Constitution stipulating that relationship between the Crimean republic and Ukraine should be regulated on the basis of a treaty and an agreement. Nearly 83 per cent of those casting their ballots in the plebiscite backed the idea of dual citizenship. When asked about his further steps, Meshkov said the point at issue was not Crimea's independence but its economic autonomy.
