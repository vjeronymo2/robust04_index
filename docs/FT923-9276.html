920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / International Company News: MIM aims to cut ADollars 40m from costs MIM Holdings, the leading Australian mining group, has revised its procurement policies aimed at cutting about 5 per cent, or ADollars 40m (USDollars 30m), from its costs over the next two years, writes Bruce Jacques. The company is setting up a central procurement group to use group purchasing power more effectively.
