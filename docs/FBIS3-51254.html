Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] Attention: A few minutes ago some U.S. military planes overflew Barranquilla several times. The control tower at Ernesto Cortizos Airport reported that it did not receive notification of the presence of the aircraft, adding that the crew did not respond to calls from the control tower asking them the purpose of their flight and destination. The airplanes continued their flight, possibly toward Fort Clayton military base in Panama.
