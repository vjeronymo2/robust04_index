Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Secretary General of the ruling MPLA [Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola] party in Angola, Lopo do Nascimento, is in Namibia to discuss business-related issues with his SWAPO [South-West African People's Organization] counterpart Moses Garoeb. Garoeb told NBC [Namibian Broadcasting Corporation] news that the visit is a strictly party-to-party business mission, although political issues will also be discussed. He said both the MPLA and SWAPO have business interests which are supposed to help them become independent and self-reliant within the multiparty political environment. Garoeb revealed that some of the business interests to be discussed between the two parties will include fisheries and transportation.
