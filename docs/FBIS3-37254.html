Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, February 9, RIA -- At its plenary meeting held today the State Duma turned down the proposal, submitted by Deputy Aleksandr Mikhailov (the group of the Communist Party of Russia), "On the State Duma's Initiative To Hold Joint Meetings with the Parliaments of Ukraine and Belarus on Political and Economic Consequences of the Byelovezh Agreements, Concluded in December 1991, With a View To Denouncing Them". Though 183 deputies backed up this proposal (125 voted against it), it failed to win the necessary majority of votes.
