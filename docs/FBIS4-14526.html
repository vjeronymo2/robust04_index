BFN [Excerpts] A delegation headed by Meditkan Sherinkulov, head of the CIS Interparliamentary Commission, and Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian president's plenipotentiary representative on Nagorno-Karabakh, arrived in Azerbaijan today. Afiaddin Dzhalilov, deputy chairman of the Azerbaijani Supreme Soviet; members of the National Assembly; and Russian Ambassador to Azerbaijan Valter Shonia welcomed the guests at Bina Airport. [passage omitted] [Begin Dzhalilov recording] We highly appraise the visit of this delegation to Azerbaijan. In accordance with a decision of the CIS Interparliamentary Council, Mr. Sherinkulov is heading the delegation of the Interparliamentary Assembly. We sincerely welcome Sherinkulov, [name indistinct], and Kazimirov to Azerbaijan and hope that this will be yet another step toward the peaceful resolution of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict. [end recording]
