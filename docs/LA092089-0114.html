September 20, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

HOME BUILDER BUYS 7.5% STAKE IN PHOENIX INC.

County home builder Peter M. Ochs has acquired a 7.5% stake in the Phoenix Group International Inc., an Irvine investment company, according to a federal filing.

Ochs, chairman and president of the Fieldstone Co., a Newport Beach residential real estate development company, bought 195,000 Phoenix shares Aug. 25 for $335,400, according to a Sept. 19 filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Phoenix, a company headed by Charles W. Missler, owns or controls several high-technology concerns, including Resdel Industries of Newport Beach and American PC Corp. of Irvine.

Last week, Phoenix announced formation of a joint venture with the Soviet Union to help provide 6 million personal computers to schools and businesses in that country. DAVID OLMOS
