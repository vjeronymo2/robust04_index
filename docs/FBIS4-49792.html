BFN [Text] Stockholm, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the national committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), left here today for Norway after a five-day official visit. During his stay here, Li held talks with Swedish Prime Minister Carl Bildt, Foreign Minister Margaretha Ugglas and other high- ranking executive and legislative officials on bilateral ties and broader economic and trade cooperation between the two countries. Li also visited factories and enterprises, saying that he was deeply impressed. Li is currently on a tour of five European nations which has already taken him to Finland and Sweden and includes Norway, Denmark and Belgium.
