Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Irkutsk, 11 Mar -- At this time Irkutsk airport is closed: a search is going on in the building for an explosive device. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned at 1300 hours local time today that a phone call was made by an unidentified individual to the main militia department of the city of Irkutsk, reporting that the airport building was mined. The passengers and staff were evacuated.
