December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Mark Taper Foundation -- A recent $1-million grant to the Los Angeles Central Library was made by the Mark Taper Foundation, as reported in a news article that appeared Nov. 27. That article and an editorial that appeared Nov. 30 also referred to the grant as a donation from financier and philanthropist Mark Taper. Although Taper is the sole contributor to the foundation, it was the foundation that provided the grant.
