BFN [Text] As INTERFAX learned from a well-informed source in Azerbaijan's Defence Ministry, in the past 24 hours all was relatively calm in the Karabakh conflict area. At the same time several population centers in two border districts of Azerbaijan -- Kazakh and Tauz -- came under fire. This resulted in neither casualties nor destruction. In the meantime, the headquarters of Nagorno-Karabakh's Defence Army reports that Sunday [15 May] night and Monday morning Azeri troops opened fire on the positions of Karabakh forces in the Mardakert, Agdam and Askeran directions, wounding two soldiers. According to the headquarters, the Armenian forces refrained from opening retaliatory fire.
