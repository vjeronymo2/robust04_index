BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 6 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop today met and had a talk with Selig Harrison, senior associate of the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace in the United States. Present on the occasion was Pak Kyong-son, a deputy department director of the Central Committee of the WPK.
