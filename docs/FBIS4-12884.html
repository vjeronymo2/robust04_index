BFN [Excerpt] Phalangist Party leader Dr. George Sa'adah has affirmed the need to speed up investigations and reveal the perpetrators of the two crimes at the Sayyidat al-Najat Church and the Phalangist Party headquarters. He pointed out that the battle over freedoms has started and will not end until positive results are achieved, which can only take place through the restoration of newscasts and political programs to the audio and visual media. During the expanded Phalangist Party meeting that was held yesterday at party headquarters, Sa'adah said: During the al-Ta'if meetings, the issue of these varied and numerous media was presented. But the decision was made then to cancel all the militias, while there was no mention of canceling the media, but of reorganizing it. [passage omitted]
