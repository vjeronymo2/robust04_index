930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: World of Leather World of Leather, the USM-quoted furniture retailer, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 71,000 for the six months to June 30, against Pounds 94,000 in the comparable period. Turnover improved some 10 per cent to Pounds 14m but according to Mr Ramon Benardout, chairman, the benefits of additional sales were offset by increased overheads, including Pounds 134,000 in legal fees, and costs of providing extended interest free credit. Earnings per share emerged at 0.9p (1.2p).
