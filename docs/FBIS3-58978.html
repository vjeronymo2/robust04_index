Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] A bomb exploded on the ground floor of the Diyarbakir Governor's Office building killing one person and wounding seven. Our correspondent has learned that the explosion occurred in the restroom of the Registry at 1000. The bomb was placed by terrorists. One child was killed instantly. The seven injured were taken to hospitals in Diyarbakir. The explosion damaged the building, as well as surrounding buildings.
