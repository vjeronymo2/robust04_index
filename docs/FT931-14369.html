930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / Observer: Propped-up As Michael Heseltine prepares his White Paper to save the coal industry, the question arises of what to name the coal levy which is bound to feature in his plans. The electricity analysts at Robert Fleming Securities are proposing Coal Energy Central Intervention Levy, or CECIL, summoning the ghost of Lord Parkinson, the erstwhile energy secretary. Observer prefers PITS for Pour In The Subsidies.
