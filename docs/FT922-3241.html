920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Phoenix Timber losses fall to Pounds 1.96m Phoenix Timber Group reported lower pre-tax losses for the year to March 31 of Pounds 1.96m, against Pounds 2.42m. Turnover fell from Pounds 55.6m to Pounds 23.1m reflecting the withdrawal from its former core activity of timber importing. Costs relating to this decision were taken as an extraordinary charge of Pounds 3.56m (Pounds 2.8m). Losses per share came to 13.6p (14p) and the final dividend is again passed. The company said trading conditions for ongoing activities - property, building supplies manufacture and property maintenance products - remained extremely difficult.
