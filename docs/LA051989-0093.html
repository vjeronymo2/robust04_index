May 19, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Lainie Kazan has left the cast of CBS' fall series "The Incredible Teddy Z" after taping six episodes, Columbia Television and Kazan's publicist have confirmed. Kazan, who was cast as the mother of young Hollywood agent Teddy Zakalakis (John Cryer) in the series from producer Hugh Wilson, quit "because she did not like the way her character was being written," according to publicist Jim Dobson. "They wanted her to be an old woman, and she wanted to play someone younger, more vital." No replacement has been named; Columbia says it will re-shoot Kazan's scenes in the already-taped episodes.
