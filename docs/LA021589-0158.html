February 15, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

COLORADO BACK CHARGED WITH RAPE

Prosecutors on Tuesday filed charges of sexual assault and trespassing against University of Colorado running back Marcus Reliford.

Reliford, 18, the Buffaloes' fifth-leading rusher last season, is accused of raping an 18-year-old woman after breaking into a dormitory room through a window.

Boulder County Judge Thomas Reed scheduled a March 8 preliminary hearing on the evidence against Reliford.
