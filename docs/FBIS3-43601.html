Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The minister of foreign affairs, Dr. Obed Asamoah, has said that the Government of Ghana would take steps to ensure that the Togolese Government pays compensation to the families of the Ghanaians killed during last week's political disturbances in Lome. Speaking to the GBC [Ghana Broadcasting Corporation] in Accra, Dr. Asamoah said the government will insist that Togo pay reparations for damages caused to Ghana.
