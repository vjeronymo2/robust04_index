April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski, who ended the week with a $60 loss on Sunday, will try to get back on the winning track today when his wager is a $12 triple from Native Pocket, Risen Port and No Doubles Match in the sixth to Somethingdifferent in the seventh to Olympic Prospect in the eighth. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,020.40.
