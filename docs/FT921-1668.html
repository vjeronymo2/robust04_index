920323 FT 23 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Charitable - Ballot Box Controversy continues over last week's incident in Bolton, when John Major was jostled by a crowd he later dubbed 'the ugly face of Labour'. Norma Major, writing in yesterday's Sunday Express, says she paused to take photos of the jostlers: 'They were not very photogenic. I wonder if they will bother to vote]' The ugly face of Labour not bothering to vote?
