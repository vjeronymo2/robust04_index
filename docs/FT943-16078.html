940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / Observer: House cleaning Chris Smith, Labour's environmental protection spokesman, obviously has a sense of humour. Tony Blair, expected to be crowned Labour leader on July 21, made his name as shadow home secretary with the slogan Tough on Crime; Tough on the Causes of Crime. Smith's new slogan? Tough on Grime; Tough on the Cause of Grime.
