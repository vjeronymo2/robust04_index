Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 19 Jan (SPA) -- Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud received a telephone call last night from His Excellency President Bill Clinton, the U.S. President, who briefed the custodian of the two holy mosques, may God protect him, on his talks with His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad, president of the Syrian Arab Republic, in Geneva recently.
