December 31, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PACKETT'S LANDING WINS ROBB STAKES

Packett's Landing, carrying 124 pounds and Jose Santos, assumed command entering the stretch and went on to a one-length victory in the $120,600 Alex M. Robb Stakes for 3-year-olds and up Saturday at Aqueduct. Diamond Anchor was second in the field of 10, a neck ahead of Bert.
