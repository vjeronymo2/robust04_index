BFN [Text] Today, 6 April, Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana met Egyptian President Husni Mubarak, to whom he handed an invitation to visit Spain from King Juan Carlos. Solana is also due to meet PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat in Cairo. Solana and Mubarak decided to hold an informal ministerial meeting of 10 Mediterranean countries next July in the city of Alexandria. Today in Cairo, Solana also met Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, the general secretary of the Arab League, with whom he discussed the Middle East peace process, the dialogue among Mediterranean people, and the situation in the Arab countries. Regarding the meeting with 'Arafat, Solana has repeated at every opportunity during his trip to Egypt Spain's willingness to help toward a successful conclusion of the peace process. This is the main purpose of his meeting today with the Palestinian leader.
