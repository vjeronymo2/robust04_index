910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Investors Capital assets improve Net asset value per share of the Investors Capital Trust stood at 113.9p at March 31, an improvement of 5.5 per cent on the 108p standing 12 months earlier. Available earnings for the six months to end-March totalled Pounds 6.21m (Pounds 5.83m), equal to 2.511p (2.357p) per share. A second interim dividend of 1.25p (1.2p) makes 2.5p (2.375p) to date.
