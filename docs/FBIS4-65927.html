BFN [By Parliamentary Staff, PRESS ASSOCIATION News] [Text] The Government tonight announced a fresh order for RAF [Royal Air Force] missiles awarded to British Aerospace. Defence Procurement Minister of State Jonathan Aitken said in a Commons written reply: "The contract for development and production of an initial batch of Advanced Short-Range Air-to-Air Missiles awarded to British Aerospace in March 1992 is proceeding well." He added: "Following a recent review of the operational requirements of the RAF's Harrier and new European Fighter aircraft, the contract for the follow-on order of a further quantity of missiles has been awarded to British Aerospace." Mr Aitken said the 66 million pound order was "excellent news" for British industry.
