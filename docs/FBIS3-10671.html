Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The News" newscast] [Text] An emergency law presented to Congress by Treasury Minister Julio Sosa Rodriguez was approved this evening after Congress debated it for three hours in a bicameral session. The only thing that remains now is for the legislative branch to send the document to the executive for confirmation. In a bicameral session, Congress approved a law dealing with the nation's financial crisis. MAS [Movement Toward Socialism] Deputy Walter Marquez, who has closely followed the Banco Latino case, as president of the commission investigating the case, gave his opinion on this law: [Begin Marquez recording] The most important points approved guarantee bank deposit insurance from 1 to 4 million bolivares and the appointment of representatives for the depositors to the Intervention Board. Meanwhile, the Intervention Board is authorized to pay senior citizens over 60 and people who are in a difficult economic situation enough to cover their daily expenses. Likewise, the mechanisms to handle financial emergencies were established, and in the same manner, mechanisms were set up to obtain temporary financial aid. [end recording]
