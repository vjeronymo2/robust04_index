941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / International Company News: Westcoast steps into Australia with BHP link Westcoast Energy, an expanding western Canada natural gas producer and owner of a big Ontario distributor, is stepping into the Australian market with Broken Hill Proprietary as partner. The two companies will jointly study the feasibility of building a USDollars 300m 400-mile pipeline to bring gas from Bass Strait to the Melbourne and Sydney areas. Westcoast would supply pipeline construction and operating expertise. BHP controls large gas reserves in the Bass Strait off the south east coast, and its industrial operations are potential gas customers. The Australian gas market is developing quickly because of environmental concerns about coal.
