941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / International Company News: Navan Resources Navan Resources, the Dublin-based natural resources producer, announced that 'strong progress' had been made in the 1994 first half, with pre-tax profits advancing by 85 per cent from IPounds 297,000 to IPounds 549,000 (Pounds 543,000) for the period. Turnover grew from IPounds 3.1m to IPounds 3.78m and earnings came out at 0.57p (0.07p) per share.
