940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Faber Prest Pounds 8m cash call for acquisition Faber Prest yesterday announced an Pounds 8.3m rights issue to be used partly to fund the acquisition of the remaining 50 per cent of Appleby Slag Reduction - another step in the expansion of its industrial services division. The purchase price is Pounds 5.35m, plus a Pounds 1m loan to enable Appleby to repay a loan from Clugston Group, the vendor. Faber, which is also involved in distribution and car retailing, is issuing 2.1m ordinary shares at 425p on a 1-for-4 basis. Of the proceeds, Pounds 3.25m will satisfy the cash requirement of the acquisition and Pounds 1m will repay the loan. Faber reported a 71 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.65m to Pounds 2.83m. The interim dividend is increased to 5.5p (5p) on earnings per share more than doubled to 21.76p (10.25p). The shares added 10p to 499p.
