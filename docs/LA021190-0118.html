February 11, 1990, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

ST. LOUIS MAN KILLS 2 STEPSONS, WOUNDS SELF

A man took his two young stepsons hostage Friday after a dispute with his estranged wife, then fatally shot the boys and wounded himself in the shoulder, police said.

The man was identified as 28-year-old Marcus Atkins. He was listed in stable condition at St. Louis University Hospital.

Police broke down the apartment door after hearing shots and found the dead boys, ages 8 and 5.
