BFN [Text] Two Molotov cocktails were thrown last night at a bus close to the village of Biddiya on the Trans-Samaria highway. No one was hurt and there was no damage. South of Hebron, another firebomb was tossed at a bus. Again, no one was hurt. A Qol Yisra'el reporter says throughout the day there were disturbances in the Hebron, Jericho, and Tulkarm areas. Soldiers used tear gas and rubber bullets to disperse stone throwers. The center of Hebron was open yesterday to commerce and trade.
