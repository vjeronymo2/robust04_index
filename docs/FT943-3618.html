940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Murray European asset value up 20% The net asset value per share at Murray European Investment Trust improved by 20 per cent from 51.6p to 62.1p over the year to June 30. For the six month period net available revenue slipped to Pounds 167,000 (Pounds 224,000), equivalent to earnings per share of 0.56p (0.75p).
