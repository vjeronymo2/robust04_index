April 23, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MAN SHOT, KILLED IN APPARENT ROBBERY

A Santa Ana man, believed to be the victim of a robbery attempt, has died of a gunshot wound to the head.

Santa Ana police said Noe Razo Garcia, 20, was rushed to Fountain Valley Regional Hospital and Medical Center on Friday night after he was found lying in front of his residence in the 2100 block of West Borchard Avenue.

Officers said it appeared Garcia was shot when someone tried to rob him.

Police had no suspects in the case.
