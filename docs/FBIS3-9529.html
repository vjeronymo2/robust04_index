Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 23 (ATA) -- Valer Xheka was re-elected president of the Union of Independent Trade Unions of Albania [UITUA], and Vilson Qendro, its secretary general, after the election of the National Council made up of 52 members on the second day of the first congress of the UITUA on March 22. Minister of Labour and Social Assistance Dashamir Shehi conveyed his greetings to the participants in the congress and called for an open and constructive dialogue between the greatest trade union organization opposite to the democratic government, for the best possible solution of the sharp problems that have emerged during the implementation of the economic reform, the reduction of the number of the unemployed and inflation. Trade union compatriots from Kosova and The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia as well as representatives of trade union organizations and confederations from various countries of the world read their greetings in the congress.
