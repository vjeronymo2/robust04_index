Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] Turkey has exported state bonds worth $725 million to Japan. According to an agreement signed in Tokyo, of the total loan 45 billion yen will be paid back in eight years and with an interest rate of 5.45 percent. The remaining 30 billion yen will be paid back in 10 years at an interest rate of 5.75 percent. [passage omitted]
