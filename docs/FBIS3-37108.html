Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Today is the 15th anniversary of the independence of the Islamic Republic of Iran. On this occasion, Tajik Supreme Council Chairman Imamali Rakhmanov has sent a message of congratulations addressed to Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. The message says: With extreme pleasure, on behalf of the people of Tajikistan, I congratulate you and the friendly and fraternal people of Iran on the occasion of the national day of the Islamic Republic of Iran. I am confident that the deep-rooted relations between the peoples of Iran and Tajikistan--which are based on common historical, cultural, and spiritual links--will be expanded further, on a new level of interest by the two countries. Taking this opportunity, I pray to almighty God for [words indistinct] health, and prosperity and wish success to the fraternal people of Iran.
