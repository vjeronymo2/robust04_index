Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Jakarta, Jan 20 (ANTARA) -- Chief of the Indonesian Armed Forces (ABRI) General Feisal Tanjung said that ABRI's socio-political role will in future become more rather than less conspicuous as the challenges to be faced by the nation will be concerned with socio-political problems. [passage omitted] In this context, the general referred to the message of President Suharto on the occasion of ABRI's 48th anniversary that ABRI's role was expected to remain as stabilizer and dynamiser of the nation's development.
