920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Iata worried by passenger trend The air transport industry is increasingly worried by a slower than expected recovery in international passenger traffic, writes Paul Betts, Aerospace Correspondent. Passenger traffic rose 10 per cent in July over the same month last year, but the International Air Transport Association (Iata) said the latest figures were worryingly low, as air travel in 1991 had been hit by the Gulf war.
