940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / UK Company News: JF Utilities Net asset value per ordinary income share at Johnson Fry Utilities Trust was 98.1p at July 31, compared with 94.4p at July 14 1993, when the trust started trading. Net revenue was Pounds 2.43m and earnings per ordinary share were 7.99p. A final dividend of 2.4p is recommended, making a total of 7.2p.
