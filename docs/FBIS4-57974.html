BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 26 May. A Social Policy Analysis Unit study shows the poverty rate increased 28.4 percent in all department capitals. La Paz registered the highest rate with 32.3 percent; Tarija, 30.4 percent; Cochabamba, 29.3 percent; Trinidad, 29.1 percent; Oruro, 27.3 percent; and Santa Cruz, 27.7 percent. The lowest rates were registered by El Alto with 26.9 percent; Sucre, 26.8 percent; and Cobija, 26.9 percent. (La Paz PRESENCIA in Spanish 16 May 94 p 4) Capitalization Minister Alfonso Revollo reported the World Bank will grant a $50 million credit to Bolivia for the capitalization project. (La Paz HOY in Spanish 19 May 94 Economic Section p 21)
