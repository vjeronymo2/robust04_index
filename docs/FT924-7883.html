921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Washington Red Delicious apples are a star fruit buy this week reports the FFVIB at 50-60p a lb. French Granny Smith at 35-45p a lb and English Cox at 35-45p a lb are also star buys. English conference pears at 35-45p a lb, Comice pears at 40-50p a lb, Spania grapes at 55-60p a lb and bananas at 45-60p a lb all remain of the highest quality. English Cauliflowers at 35-50p each are plentiful. Carrots at 15-20p a lb, parsnips at 25-30p a lb onions at 15-25p a lb, and swede at 15-25p. Crunchy celery at 35-45p a head is superb this week, together with tomatoes at 55-65p a lb.
