BFN [Text] "Armenian units launched an offensive in the Agdam direction on Saturday, so Azerbaijani formation had to take adequate measures," the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry told INTERFAX. During fighting 1 tank and two trucks were destroyed and about 200 soldiers killed, says the Azerbaijani source. Azerbaijani losses are being specified. On Sunday morning, following artillery preparations, Armenian formations launched an offensive in the Geranboy direction. The Azerbaijani army is doing everything it can to regain its positions, stated the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry. The Karabakh Defense Army reports that on Sunday morning, after a night lull, the Azerbaijani army resumed attacks in a number of sections of the Karabakh front. This sources says that the attack was targetted against the northern section of Karabakh's Mardakert Region, where heavy fighting has continued for more than six days now. Combat operations also continued in the south-eastern section in the direction of Fizuli. The Karabakh forces put a third Azerbaijani tank out of actions over the past 24 hours.
