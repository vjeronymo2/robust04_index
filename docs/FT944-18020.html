941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / International Company News: KNP merger with US group called off KNP BT, the Dutch paper, packaging and office products group, said its US office products division had failed to reach agreement on a planned merger with Ivan Allen, an office products distributor based in Atlanta, Georgia. The Dutch company had hoped the link, announced by its US division in August, would give it wide access to customers in the south-eastern US, where Ivan Allen is the largest local distributor.
