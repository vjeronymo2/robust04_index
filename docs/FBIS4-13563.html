BFN [Text] Knesset Members [MK's] Darawshah and al-Sani' of the Arab Democratic Party have asked Prime Minister Rabin to remove the closure on the territories. At a meeting with the premier this morning they said that the measure was making life very difficult for Arab residents and had increased their bitterness. The two MK's also reported on their recent visit to Damascus. Prime Minister Rabin said he was optimistic about the chances of promoting negotiations with Syria, and in this regard was awaiting the visit here of U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher at the end of the month.
