930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Derwent Valley Holdings DERWENT VALLEY Holdings is to purchase a freehold office property at 60-66 Rochester Row and 59-67 Greencoat Place, London, for Pounds 1.85m. Current annual income is Pounds 425,000, which reflects a yield, after costs, of about 22 per cent.
