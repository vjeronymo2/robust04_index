BFN [Text] The Palestinian National Authority [PNA] announced today that it had appointed Morgan Stanley Asset Management, MSAM, to manage the funds given to administer and develop the West Bank and the Gaza strip under the leadership of the PNA. This institution, which manages funds estimated at 50 billion dollars, is a subsidiary of the Stanley Morgan Group [as heard], a large financial institution that has offices in the world's main financial centers. The PNA said that it had chosen this institution to help in reassuring the donor states and agencies that the funds they had given to the PNA for the administration and development of the West Bank and Gaza were being managed with complete transparency and in a proficient and professional way through one of the world's eminent financial institutions.
