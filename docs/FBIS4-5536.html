BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on April 8 sent a message of greetings to Ugo Mifsud Bonnici on his assumption of office as president of the Republic of Malta. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continuously develop, the message wished him success in the work for the prosperity of the country.
