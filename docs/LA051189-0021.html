May 11, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WRONG TARGET IN THORP VS. BEAM?

The lack of responsibility that the alcoholic Candance Thorp demonstrated both before and after the birth of her Fetal Alcohol Syndrome-afflicted son was disturbingly sad. By her own admission she sometimes drank herself unconscious during her pregnancy. It is apparent that a warning label on the drinker's whiskey bottle would not stop such irresponsible and reckless behavior.

DAVE and JOANN MOINE, Rolling Hills
