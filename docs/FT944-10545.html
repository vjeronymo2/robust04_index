941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / Power station to create 700 jobs Up to 700 jobs are to be created in building a Pounds 520m gas-fired power station near Grimsby, Humberside County Council said yesterday. The station is being built by a consortium including Midlands Electricity, the regional power company; IVO, the Finnish electricity company, and Tomen, the Japanese trading company. The station is due to open in 1997.
