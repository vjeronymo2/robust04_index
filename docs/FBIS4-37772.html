BFN [Text] According to Palestinian sources, Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will meet with high-ranking Palestinian figures, 27 June. These figures include Dr. Nabil Sha'th, responsible for planning in the Palestinian Authority, and Faysal al-Husayni, a member of the Authority. The prime minister's meeting with these figures comes before the beginning of talks slated for next week between Israel and the PLO on the transfer of self-rule powers in the West Bank.
