930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Staff evacuated from Thorp plant after gas leak Staff were evacuated yesterday from Thorp, the controversial nuclear reprocessing plant in Cumbria, after a gas leak during tests. British Nuclear Fuels said the work being carried out was non-radioactive, and that the gas escape posed no danger to the public. The leak comes at a sensitive time in the debate about whether the Pounds 2.8bn plant should get a licence to start operation.
