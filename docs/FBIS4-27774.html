BFN [By Akio Tamesada; from NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 4 Jun Morning Edition] [Text] Vienna, 2 Jun--In reference to the conclusion reached by the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) on 2 June that effective inspection of fuel rods in experimental reactors has become impossible, a DPRK (North Korea) source in Vienna on 3 June once again insisted that the fuel rods taken out by North Korea are being stored in "a way to make future IAEA inspections possible." The North Korean source said the IAEA was informed of this in a letter dated 2 June.
