Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The territories are under full closure since this morning and their residents are banned from coming to work in Israel until further notice. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that a closure was also clamped on Qiryat Arba'. In practice, however, it is not being observed and every two hours a convoy of cars and a bus to Jerusalem leave Hebron and Qiryat Arba'. The convoys are escorted by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] troops due to stone-throwing incidents in the area of Halhul. [passage omitted on rioting]
