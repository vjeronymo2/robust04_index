920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / Investment fund for Teesside A Pounds 15M FUND earmarked for equity capital investment in companies in Teesside was launched yesterday by 3i, the venture capital group. The Teesside Growth Fund, the latest in a series of 3i regional funds and the second in northern England, is intended to complement structural changes in Teesside's economy by encouraging the expansion of medium-sized private companies.
