Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 6 Feb (TANJUG) -- I condemn the crimes in Sarajevo with the utmost bitterness, said Republic of Serbia President Slobodan Milosevic in a statement to TANJUG today. "The dead and injured in Sarajevo are not the victims of war but the victims of war criminals. We all expect that these criminals to be found in the very near future and put on trial for the crime they have committed." "The revulsion felt by the Yugoslav and international public is justified, and I sincerely hope that no political reasons will hamper the rapid discovery of the criminals and diminish their responsibility," said Slobodan Milosevic, president of the Republic of Serbia.
