931102 FT 02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Lloyds Chemists in Scottish buy Lloyds Chemists has acquired John Hamilton Pharmaceuticals for Pounds 4.65m in cash. Hamiltons is an established pharmaceutical wholesaler, operating from a 45,000 sq ft freehold site at Clydebank, north of Glasgow. In the year to May 31 1993 it reported turnover of Pounds 45.4m.
