930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Platon and Wills trade insults Platon International, the USM-quoted instrumentation group fighting a hostile Pounds 2.88m bid from Wills Group, traded insults with its would-be suitor yesterday after Wills again urged shareholders to support its all-paper offer which closes tomorrow. Wills, an industrial, electronic and automotive products company, wrote to shareholders yesterday urging them to ignore the 'weak arguments' contained in Platon's defence document, which was issued at the weekend. In response Mr Robin Meyer, Platon's chairman, defended the document which he said had 'delivered a powerful and stinging response to Wills opportunistic and derisory offer.'
