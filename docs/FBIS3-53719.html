Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 17 Jan (WAKH) -- French Defense Minister Francois Leotard left Abu Dhabi this morning following a short visit to the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. He was seen off by Staff Major General pilot Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, the UAE chief of staff, senior Armed Forces officers, and the French ambassador to the UAE.
