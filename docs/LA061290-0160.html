June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Television and music video choreographer Rey Lozano will head a new commercial dance program to open in the fall at the Orange County High School of the Arts, school officials announced Monday.

Among recent credits, Lozano has worked with Paula Abdul in choreographing the 1990 Academy Awards show, the 1990 American Music Awards and several of Adbul's music videos.

The commercial dance program will complement the classical dance program by offering dance training for stage, film, television or videos. Information: (213) 596-1435.
