BFN [Text] AL-SHA'B has learned that Israel has prepared a plan to assassinate Hamas leaders in response to the hostile operations Hamas mujahidin have carried out inside the occupied territories that have killed and wounded scores of Israelis during the past two weeks. The Israeli security services, military intelligence, and Mosad last week approved the final plan, which consists of several phases. The first phase envisages the assassination of 200 Hamas leaders while the second phase envisages the assassination of 400 leaders. The assassination operations will target Hamas leaders in Britain, Jordan, Sudan, Lebanon and other countries. New methods will be used in these operations. The Israeli military intelligence service has agreed to finance a number of Jewish terrorist organizations that will carry out these operations. Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin has approved the plan. The Israeli Foreign Ministry has asked its embassies abroad to gather the necessary information to carry out the assassinations. The Israeli Embassy in Cairo has been asked to gather information on the movements of Hamas leaders in neighboring Arab countries.
