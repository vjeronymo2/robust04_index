Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Urumqi, January 14 (XINHUA) -- Temur Dawamad, vice- chairman of the Chinese National People`s Congress Standing Committee and former chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Regional Government, left here this morning for a seven-day goodwill visit to Malaysia. Temur, who is heading a delegation of the autonomous regional government, is the guest of Malaysian Prime Minister bin Mohamed Datuk Sri Mahathir. He will exchange views with Mahathir on development and cooperation in the fields of trade and economy, culture and travel. He will also meet with the president of the Malaysian Senate and the speaker of the House of Representatives to consult them on the exchanges between the two countriess parliaments.
