April 11, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP;

MONTCLAIR PREP POWER SHOCKS EL CAMINO REAL

Home runs by Keyaan Cook and Dylan Jones knocked El Camino Real High from the ranks of the unbeaten Tuesday as Montclair Prep posted a 7-6 victory in the semifinal round of the Thousand Oaks baseball tournament.

Cook, a senior, belted a three-run home run off junior right-hander Terry Morrow (0-1) to give Montclair Prep a 3-0 first-inning lead.

Jones, a junior designated-hitter, added a solo home run in the second inning. Montclair Prep (14-2) added three runs in the third.

Senior right-hander Kevin Grant (7-1) scattered eight hits and stifled a seventh-inning rally for the win. With one out and runners at first and second, Gregg Sheren hit a fly ball to the warning track in center field. Both runners advanced on a wild pitch before Jason Cohen flied to left to end the game.

Montclair Prep plays Agoura today at 1 p. m. for the tournament championship.

In an earlier quarterfinal game, El Camino Real (10-1-1) scored 11 runs in the fourth inning en route to clobbering Canyon, 12-1. Junior right-hander Corey Bromberg (1-0) struck out eight while pitching a four-hitter.

Cohen hit a three-run home run and Sheren had a solo homer.
