BFN [Text] Belgrade, 20 Apr (TANJUG) -- Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, president of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, has accepted an invitation from Mirko Jovic, president of the Serb National Renewal [SNO], to visit our country at the beginning of May, the SNO says in a statement issued today. The statement says that the visit will last three days and will take place within the framework of cooperation between the two parties and the talks on developing a "pan-Slav Christian union".
