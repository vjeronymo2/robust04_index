940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / Norway scraps fuel tax plan Norway yesterday announced it will scrap a controversial plan to revise taxation of the petroleum industry. The government last autumn began drafting changes to the petroleum tax regime in which it was considering reducing tax concessions to oil companies that use high levels of debt to increase their tax allowances. The measure was meant to offset government revenue losses suffered after special tax concessions were introduced in 1992.
