DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Agency Form Under Review by the Office of Management and Budget DOC has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for clearance the following proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. chapter 35). Agency: Bureau of the Census. Title: Quarterly Summary of Federal, State, and Local Tax Revenue. Form Number(s): F&hyph;71, F&hyph;72, F&hyph;73. Agency Approval Number: 0607&hyph;0112. Type of Request: Extension of the expiration date of a currently approved collection. Burden: 6,057 hours. Number of Respondents: 6,006. Avg Hours Per Response: 15 minutes. Needs and Uses: The Census Bureau conducts this survey on a quarterly basis to gather information on tax revenues collected by state and local tax collecting agencies. The Bureau uses the data to publish benchmark statistics on public sector taxes. The data are also used by the Bureau of Economic Analysis, the Federal Reserve Board, the Department of Treasury, and others to provide the most current information on the status of state and local governments. Affected Public: State or local governments. Frequency: Quarterly. Respondent's Obligation: Voluntary. OMB Desk Officer: Maria Gonzalez, (202) 395&hyph;7313.
