BFN [Text] Beijing, May 22 (XINHUA) -- Liu Huaqing, standing committee member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, today met and had a cordial talk here with a joint delegation of veteran fighters from the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS). The delegation, with Chairman N. Vasilievich of the coordinating committee of the Veteran Fighters' Union of the CIS as its leader and Chairman M. Petrovich of the Veteran Fighters' Committee of Russia as its deputy leader, arrived here on May 20 at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). Liu, also vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, said the veteran Chinese fighters have cherished a profound friendship with their counterparts of the CIS. He noted that 1995 will celebrate the 50th anniversary of victory in the anti-fascist war, which he described as having historic significance not only to the people of the CIS, but also to the Chinese people. "So we should hold celebrations on the occasion," he said. During the meeting both Vasilievich and Petrovich shared the hope that the veteran fighters from the CIS should do their best to strengthen their friendly exchanges with their Chinese counterparts and push forward their friendship. During the meeting, Liu also briefed the visitors on China's reform and opening to the outside world. Hong Xuezhi, vice-chairman of the national committee of the CPPCC, participated in the meeting.
