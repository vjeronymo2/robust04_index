Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the general headline "The Search for Accord in Russian Society Today Is Being Really Threatened by Political Ambitions, Mutual Distrust, and 'Double' Morals" and under the "In the Parliament" rubric: "Members of Factions See Document in Various Ways"] [Excerpt] A working group of parliamentarians is being formed in the State Duma, which is to prepare a draft Agreement on Civic Peace and Accord. Alevtina Fedulova, deputy chairman of the chamber, has been instructed to head it. "We are only just beginning this work, and the actual deputies' group is still at the formation stage," Alevtina Fedulova reported. She noted that the deputies' group for elaborating the draft will include representatives of all the parliamentary factions, who have to take upon themselves the task of elaborating various aspects of this document. Alevtina Fedulova stressed that the deputies will work in the closest contact with the working group, set up by Boris Yeltsin, for the preparation of the Agreement on Civic Peace and Accord under the leadership of Boris Topornin, chairman of the Russian presidential Public Chamber.
