920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / UK Company News: St Andrew Trust Net asset value at St Andrew Trust rose from 226.1p to 232.3p over the six months ended June 30 1992. A year earlier it stood at 237.7p. In the half year a good return was achieved from the UK portfolio, while overseas markets were disappointing, particularly because of the the fall in Japan. Main constituents of the portfolio (in percentages) were UK 62, Europe 12, North America 8, and the Pacific basin 11. The six months produced a 10 per cent rise to Pounds 2.02m in total revenue. Earnings per share moved up 17.5 per cent to 3.56p (3.03p) and the interim dividend is again 2.7p.
