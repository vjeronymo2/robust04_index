940409 FT 09 APR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading The derivatives sector had one of its most quiet days in recent sessions as the equity account drew to a close. The June contract on the FT-SE 100 finished at 3,121, down 16 on its previous close and around 5 points below its estimated fair value premium. Volume at 7,869 was just over half of Thursday's total. The Liffe FT-SE Mid 250 finished at 3,780 on volume of 45 lots. It was also a dull day in traded options. Turnover was 21,766 contracts of which 8,330 was in the FT-SE 100. BAT was the busiest stock option at 1,091 lots.
