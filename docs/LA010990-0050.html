January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

DEMONSTRATIONS BY AIDS ACTIVISTS

The AIDS activists who tried to disrupt the parade were almost right in the wording of their sign.

They missed it by only one word. Instead of reading "Emergency. Stop the parade. 70,000 dead of AIDS," it should have read "Emergency. Stop the promiscuity. 70,000 dead of AIDS."

EMERSON JOHNSON

San Juan Capistrano
