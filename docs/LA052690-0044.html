May 26, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SAN LUIS OBISPO RALLIES IN SEVENTH TO DEFEAT CANYON

San Luis Obispo High School rallied for two runs in the seventh inning to defeat Canyon, 4-3, Friday in a Southern Section 4-A baseball playoff game at San Luis Obispo.

It was the third consecutive time San Luis Obispo has rallied for two runs in the seventh to win, 4-3, in the playoffs.

Canyon (14-13-1) had scored a run in the second and two more in the fifth to take a 3-1 lead. San Luis Obispo added one in the sixth to cut the lead to 3-2.

Josh Simmons opened the seventh with a single for San Luis Obispo (19-5). A throwing error by Canyon pitcher Greg Tippen put runners on first and second.

Grant Munger's RBI single tied the score, 3-3, and left runners on first and second. A passed ball moved the runners up and an intentional walk loaded the bases.

Charles Emery hit into a force play at home, but a wild throw to first allowed the winning run to score.
