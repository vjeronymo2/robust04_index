930416 FT 16 APR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Bespak tumbles Healthcare manufacturing group Bespak suffered a sharp fall as the company broker cut profit forecasts in reaction to US distribution worries. The group achieves 25 per cent of its sales through US Surgical which last week warned of lower profits and announced significant distribution changes that are expected to hit Bespak's sales. Subsequently BZW, cut its 1994 forecast for the group to Pounds 13.3m from Pounds 14.5m. The shares which had already fallen by more than 70 tumbled 66 to 561p.
