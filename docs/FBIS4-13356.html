BFN [Text] Baghdad, INA--Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God protect him, has visited the archaeological city of al-Hadar. Upon his arrival in the city, he was received by the engineer in charge of restoring and rehabilitating the archaeological features in al- Hadar. Meanwhile, several workers and citizens gathered around him to express their jubilation and happiness over the president's visit. Having toured the temples of the historical city, during which His Excellency President Saddam Husayn listened to a detailed explanation of the city, its history, and some of the significant archaeological sites discovered as a result of the continuous excavation activities there, the leader president inquired about the preparations currently under way for the launching of the first al- Hadar International Festival. Then, the leader president, may God protect him, attended part of the rehearsals conducted by an art group in preparation for the festival. Later, the leader president, may God protect him, left the city after he had been accorded a farewell ceremony by the director of the al-Hadar restoration project, workers, and citizens.
