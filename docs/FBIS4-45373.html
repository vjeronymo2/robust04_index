CSO [Editorial Report] The 21 May HANGUK ILBO reported on page 31 that the Pusan District Prosecutor's Office has arrested Yi Chang-sung for distributing philopon [crystal methamphetamine] and heroin from Thailand. The authorities also have arrest warrants for Kim Chong-kyu who is suspected of engineering the smuggling, and two others for carrying the drugs into the country. Yi was arrested with 226 grams of philopon and 287 grams of heroin. The Golden Triangle heroin has been found in the past, but this the first time Thai-manufactured philopon and Thai-processed heroin were found in Korea. According to the Prosecutor's Office, in February, Kim Chong-kyu smuggled one kilogram each of philopon and heroin worth eight billion won from Bangkok, Thailand, and Yi has helped sell these drugs since March.
