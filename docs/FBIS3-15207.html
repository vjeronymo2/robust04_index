Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Illegal Search in PFA [People's Front of Azerbaijan] Member's Flat"--TURAN headline] [Text] Baku, March 2 (TURAN) -- Yesterday in the Ismailly district of the republic in the flat of the National Deputy of Azerbaijan Tahir Kerimli, the former chairman of the Surpreme Court, a search was conducted by the local police. Notifying TURAN agency, Mr. Kerimli stressed that the police searching his flat had no warrant from the procurator's office permitting the search. According to him, the search took place at 2:00 a.m. The police insulted the members of his family. He also noted that as a result of the search a few bullets belonging to his hunting rifle and a kitchen knife were confiscated. Kerimli sees this action as the violation of his civil rights and his rights as a deputy.
