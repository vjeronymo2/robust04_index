BFN [ITAR-TASS report under the "From the Chronicle of These Days" rubric: "High Rate of National Income"] [Text] The national income in Turkmenistan is to increase at least 45 percent over three years, and the increase in industrial and agricultural production will reach 30 percent. This is envisaged in the "Basic Forecast Indicators of Turkmenistan's Economic Development for 1994-96," which were proclaimed by President Saparmurad Niyazov and adopted at a session of the highest organ of people's power. The program that has been drawn up proposes the accelerated development of the fuel and energy complex, the food complex, and the consumer complex, the saturation of the market with commodities, and the strengthening of the national currency.
