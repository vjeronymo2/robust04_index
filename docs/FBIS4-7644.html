BFN [Text] The cease-fire agreement signed in the Russian Embassy in Zagreb [on 30 March] between a Croatian delegation and the Serb rebels from the occupied Croatian areas came into effect at 0900 today. According to reports from the political department of the Split operational area, the cease-fire is being observed. The Zadar-Biograd area is quiet today, as it was last night. The Sinj and Sibenik-Drnis areas are also quiet.
