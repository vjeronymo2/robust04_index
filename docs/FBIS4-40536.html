BFN [Text] A meeting was held in the Surkhandarya oblast mayor's office [hakimiyat] devoted to the results of the check carried out by the special commission for increasing the pace of reforms in agriculture and also supervision of the implementation of the president's decrees and the government's decisions on this question. It was chaired by Jurabekov, first deputy prime minister of the republic and chairman of this commission. Reports by Nuraliyev, mayor [hakim] of the oblast and the mayors of the neighboring rayons were heard. Measures for quickly overcoming the serious shortcomings, discovered by the commission, which emerged during implementation of the reforms in agriculture, privatization, and providing farmers with all-round support were defined.
