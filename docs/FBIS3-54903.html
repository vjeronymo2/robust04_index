Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Lvov -- At a conference of the "Lvivprylad" production association on 3 January, Vasyl Dzera, chairman of the Frankivskyy rayon executive committee and a member of the oblast council commission for checking on foreign trade activity of the oblast state administration, asserted that the oblast authorities has sold a missile base near Brody to the private firm "Konza" for $13,000. Apart from a missile silo the former nuclear base includes working independent thermal and power resources, purification structures, and independent power supply systems. According to Vasyl Dzera, the procurator general's office of Ukraine has instituted criminal proceedings regarding the sale of a former military facility by the oblast state administration to "Konza."
