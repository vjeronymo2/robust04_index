920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / World Trade News: Polish deal for Northern Telecom Northern Telecom, a UK subsidiary of the Canadian telecommunications and switching systems supplier, has established a joint venture worth Dollars 10.5m (Pounds 5.8m) with Elwro, one of Poland's largest computer manufacturers, writes Christopher Bobinski in Warsaw. The new company, called Northern Telecom Elwro, will be producing NT's DMS-10 switching systems and telephone receivers.
