941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Acquisitions help Lynx leap to Pounds 2m A substantial contribution from acquisitions helped Lynx Holdings, the acquisitive computer services and leisure products company, to raise annual pre-tax profits from Pounds 619,000 to Pounds 2.01m. Lynx said that companies acquired during the year to September 30 had performed ahead of expectations, and had produced Pounds 1.44m of group operating profits of Pounds 2m (Pounds 619,000). Overall sales of Pounds 21.5m (Pounds 10.8m) included Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 9.03m) from continuing operations, Pounds 8.32m from acquisitions and Pounds 1.4m (Pounds 1.78m) from discontinued activities. The dividend for the year is raised 12 per cent to 1.4p (1.25p) via a final of 1.04p. Earnings per share came through at 4.5p (4.27p).
