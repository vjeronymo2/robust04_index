Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Mar (MENA)--Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and the delegation accompanying him arrived in Cairo from Sanaa this evening for a brief visit to Egypt that will last for a few hours. Presidents Mubarak and Salih are scheduled to hold a session of talks later in the day on bilateral relations and ways to remove any obstacles blocking the implementation of the Pledge and Accord Document for Yemeni reconciliation. President Mubarak led the reception party for the Yemeni president on his arrival at Cairo Airport's VIP lounge. Ministers and senior state officials also were on hand to receive the Yemeni president.
