BFN [Text] The European leaders will resume consultations at 1000 [0700 GMT] today in Corfu on the appointment of a new Commission president. The [Greek] Government spokesman stressed that the Greek Presidency will exhaust all efforts for Jacques Delors' successor to be appointed today. At last night's working dinner in Akhillion Palace and during the second unscheduled supplementary meeting, the 12 European state leaders were leaning toward Belgian Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene.
