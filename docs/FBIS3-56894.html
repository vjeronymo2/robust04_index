Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moldovan Prime Minister Andre Sangheli said at a news conference in Chisinau Thursday that the leaders of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic are threatening to cut off the gas and energy supply to the western bank of the Dniester. He talked with the Tiraspol leaders on the telephone Thursday in an attempt to avoid this development. He did not think that there were good reasons for cutting off the gas supply. Sangheli said that Tiraspol wanted to discontinue the energy supplies because Moldova had not paid for them.
