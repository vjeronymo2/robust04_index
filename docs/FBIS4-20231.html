BFN [Text] Madrid, April 27 (CTK) -- Spain will support Slovakia in its coming closer to European institutions, Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana said in a meeting with his Slovak counterpart Eduard Kukan today. Kukan, currently visiting Spain, was received by King Juan Carlos I of Spain and Premier Felipe Gonzales. The two foreign ministers told reporters that their views on international topics, including the situation in the former Yugoslavia, were fully identical. From Spain Kukan will move to Portugal to meet with Premier Anibal Cavaco Silva and Foreign Minister Jose Manuel Durao Barroso. Kukan will also hold talks with parliament vice-president and the minister of trade and tourism.
