Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] German Bundestag President Rita Sussmuth arrived in Turkey on an official visit last night. Sussmuth and the parliamentary delegation accompanying her were received at Esenboga Airport by Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly. In a statement at the airport, Sussmuth said that interparliamentary contacts are very useful at a time when Europe is going through a process of change. The talks to be held in Turkey within this framework will make important contributions, she said. Sussmuth stressed that Turkish-German friendship is multifaceted, adding that Germany's aim is to ensure that the 2 million Turks living in Germany are happy. (Thomas Kosanli), head of the Turkish-German Parliamentary Friendship Group, stressed that the intensive cooperation between the parliaments of the two countries is important for the future of the two peoples. In turn, Cindoruk said that Sussmuth's visit will be extremely constructive for the development of the relations between the two countries.
