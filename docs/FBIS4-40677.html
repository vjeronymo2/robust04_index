BFN [From the "Business SNARK" file] [Text] Yerevan [dateline as received] -- The Presidium of Armenian Supreme Council has determined by its resolution "the order of partial unpaid privatization of the state enterprise property, and their transformation into joint-stock companies". According to this document, the property ownership certificate shall be granted to the personnel who served for more than one year at a particular enterprise. The share of each stock-holder is determined in the certificate and depends on the average salary, total length of service, and some other factors. Privatization order has been developed by the Armenian Government under the law "On Privatization of State Enterprises and Unfinished Buildings", according to which 20 percent of fixed assets of privatized enterprise may be freely passed to its staff. At the same time, a long list of more than 300 enterprises was approved by the Presidium, that are not subject to unpaid privatization. The list includes, in particular, those Armenian state enterprises which function on the territory of other CIS countries.
