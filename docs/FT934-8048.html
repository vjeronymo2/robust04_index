931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Baring Stratton lifts asset value Baring Stratton, an international investment trust concentrating on capital growth, reported a net asset value of 238p per share as at September 30. The figure compared with 215p at the trust's March year-end and 182p at end-September 1992. Net revenue for the six months to September 30 dipped from Pounds 285,000 to Pounds 228,000, equivalent to earnings of 1.8p (2.26p) per share.
