Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The 11th session of the Council of Arab Interior Ministers, which opened yesterday, concluded this afternoon in Tunis. A final communique was issued which dealt in detail with all the decisions of the Tunis meeting. In addition, two statements were released: the first condemns terrorism and acts of violence; the second concerns developments of the Western-Libyan conflict over the Lockerbie issue.
