BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 25 (KPL) -- Deputy Prime Minister and President of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation Khamphoui Keoboualapha, on April 23, received a delegation of the Chinese Province of Liaoning headed by its deputy governor, Gao Guozhu. The Chinese delegation told the Lao leader of its purpose to visit Laos and the social and economic development in the Chinese province. Khamphoui Keoboualapha in return told the delegation about the socio-economic development in the country during the process of implementing the renovation policy. He pointed out the state of foreign investment in Laos, the construction of various roads with foreign companies as contractors. He praised the economic growth of Liaoning as well as China, which would bring a positive impact on countries in acceleration of their economic development. In the afternoon of the same day, the Chinese delegation also met with Deputy President of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation Leuan Sombounkhan. At the meeting both sides stated their wishes to promote cooperation between the two countries, and identified prospects for the Chinese side to do construction contracts, roads and airports. They also discussed the expansion of economic cooperation between Liaoning and the Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic], especially in mining, construction, power development, and in the building of residential quarters for public servants to rent at concessional price. They also studied the possibility of operating business partnership with Laos in areas such as light industry and food supply.
