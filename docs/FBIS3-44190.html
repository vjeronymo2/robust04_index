Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transvaal Agricultural Union has warned the ANC [African National Congress] that farmers would become part of the problem and not the solution if their demands are not met. The president of the union, Mr. Dries Bruwer, said in a letter to the ANC's Mr. Thabo Mbeki that the union demanded that attention should be given to such matters as the establishment of a volkstaat [homeland]. He said it was a small price to pay for peace in South Africa.
