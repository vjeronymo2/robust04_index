930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Group Development assets increase At March 31 1993 net asset value of Group Development Capital Trust stood at 51.3p, an increase of 24.8 per cent since end- September and of 30.1 per cent since March 1992. Gross revenue for the half year came to Pounds 138,000 (Pounds 136,000) and net loss was Pounds 12,000 (profit Pounds 5,000), equal to losses of 0.05p (0.02p profit) per share. The losses per share were wereprimarily the result of the increase in expenses occasioned by the management fee calculated on net assets. However, the directors expressed confidence that the year's dividend would be maintained at 0.3p.
