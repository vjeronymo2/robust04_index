940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / World Stock Markets: Caracas at new high Caracas stocks closed stronger for the fourth successive session on optimism over the direction of Venezuela's economy. The Merinvest composite index rose 1.87 per cent to 155.38 on 5.3m shares worth 326m bolivars (Dollars 1.9m). "There is a perception that the country's economic problems are being resolved and that the government wants to attack the deficit, inflation and the financial crisis with its macroeconomic plan," one broker said. Brazil Concerns over possible restrictions to foreign investments in the country combined with lingering jitters over Brazil's presidential race pushed stock prices down 2.1 per cent. The Bovespa index of the 55 most-active shares ended off 1,144 at 51,774 points.
