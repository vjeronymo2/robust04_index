940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Horace Clarkson Mixed fortunes in the bulk shipping markets resulted in a fall in pre-tax profits at Horace Clarkson from Pounds 2m to Pounds 1.3m in the six months to June 30. Sales declined to Pounds 19.6m (Pounds 22.8m) including Pounds 13m (Pounds 16.6m) from shipbroking. Profits from this source were halved to Pounds 1.1m. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p from earnings per share of 2.5p (4.3p).
