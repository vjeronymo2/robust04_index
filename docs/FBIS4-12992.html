BFN [Text] A youth from al-Zahiriyah was injured tonight by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] gunfire as he was hurling stones at soldiers south of Hebron. He sustained moderate injuries and was treated at a nearby Army clinic and then transferred to the hospital. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that in the Jabalyah refugee camp, a 10-year-old child was slightly wounded by rubber bullets as he and his friends were hurling stones at IDF soldiers around midday today. He was transferred to a hospital in Gaza. A Molotov cocktail was thrown at No. 25 bus on the Neve Ya'aqov-French Hill road tonight. No one was hurt and no damage was caused.
