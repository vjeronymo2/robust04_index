BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Apr (MENA)--Tonight, in his place of residence at the guest palace in Cairo, Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat met the French and British ambassadors in Cairo separately. Zuhdi al-Qidrah, the Fatah representative in Cairo, attended. During the two meetings, they reviewed the Middle East peace process and the results of the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations last week as well as the obstacles Israel is placing before the peace process. Earlier today, 'Arafat met the ambassadors of Russia and China and the American charge d'affaires in Cairo. So he has met the ambassadors of the five permanent members of the Security Council.
