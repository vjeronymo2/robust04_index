BFN [Text] Odessa, 20 May -- "The aggressive aspirations of the imperial-military Kiev, which is striving to trample down Crimea's sovereignty, deserve resolute condemnation. Yuriy Meshkov will not be right if he yields to blackmail and agrees to compromises," this is how Lev Vershynin, the cochairman of the Civil Congress of Ukraine, commented on the latest developments in Crimea in an interview with a UNIAR correspondent. He said that the Odessa branch of the Civil Congress will issue a relevant statement in connection with the latest events in Crimea.
