920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / Russia no 'poor relation' of the west New partners: leaders of the world's leading industrialised countries, prime minister John Major, president Francois Mitterrand, and chancellor Helmut Kohl yesterday welcomed Russia as a partner and not a 'poor relation' of the west. The G7 summit in Munich ended with pledges of support for the economic reforms and political leadership of president Boris Yeltsin (third from left). Report, Page 22
