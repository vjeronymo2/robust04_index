941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: British Assets Trust British Assets Trust's net asset value per share fell from 110.4p to 102p over the year to September 30. Over the same period the FT-SE-A All-Share Index was virtually unch-anged. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 21m (Pounds 21.5m). Earnings per share came out at 4.2p (4.38p) and a proposed final dividend of 1.11p raises the total to 4.38p (4.28p). Investors Capital Trust, in which British Assets has a 65.2 per cent holding, also reported a fall in net asset value, from 139.7p to 134.2p per share. Net revenue rose from Pounds 13.2m to Pounds 13.5m and earnings improved from 5.32p to 5.43p. A fourth quarterly dividend of 1.3p makes a 5.2p (5.125p) total. The trusts are managed by Ivory & Sime.
