BFN [Text] At 1200 today, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi received a CPC delegation, led by Comrade Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and head of the Chinese Federation of Trade Unions. The Chinese ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. Comrade Jianxing conveyed greetings from Chinese Premier Li Peng to Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi who also asked Jianxing to convey his greetings to Mr. Li Peng. The talks dealt with the means of promoting the existing relations of cooperation between the two friendly countries. Comrade Dr. Sulayman Qaddah, assistant regional secretary of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party [ASBP], earlier today received Comrade Wei Jianxing and the accompanying delegation. The Chinese ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. During the meeting, the two sides reviewed relations of friendship and cooperation between the ASBP and the CPC and means of promoting them. The ASBP assistant regional secretary briefed the guest delegation on the developments of the political situation in the region and the peace process. He also explained the obstacles placed by Israel to abort the peace process and to divert it from the bases, principles, and formulas it was based on. Comrade Qaddah expressed Syria's concern about the establishment of a just and comprehensive peace based on the principles of international legitimacy, UN resolutions, and the land-for-peace formula that guarantees the liberation of the entire Golan and withdrawal to the pre-5 June 1967 border. Comrade Jianxing praised Syria's stands under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad, its desire to achieve a just and comprehensive peace, and its central and decisive role in this regard. He also reiterated China's support for the implementation of UN Security Council Resolutions 242, 338, and 425, which constitute the basis of the peace process.
