July 30, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

The Superior Court arraignment for James Hydrick, a karate expert and self-proclaimed psychic accused of vandalism and trying to break out of jail, is scheduled for Thursday. In a separate trial, Hydrick is also facing molestation charges involving a small group of boys he befriended while living in Huntington Beach last year.
