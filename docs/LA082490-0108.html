August 24, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Bowl Protest: The American Federation of Musicians Local 47 is protesting -- with a program insert -- the engagement of the Seattle Symphony, a non-union orchestra, at Hollywood Bowl.

According to Bernie Fleischer, AFM Local 47 president, program inserts in 10,000 Bowl programs this weekend will notify concertgoers. Led by music director Gerard Schwarz, the Seattle orchestra plays tonight and Saturday. A spokesman for the L.A. Philharmonic Assn. said Thursday of the insert: "We are complying with a request from the Musicians Union, which represents our Philharmonic players." BETH KLEID
