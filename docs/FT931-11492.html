930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Dale Electric sells French businesses Dale Electric International, a maker of AC and DC power systems, is selling three of its businesses in France to Kaufel of Canada. The businesses, which have a combined annual turnover of FFr80m (Pounds 9.5m), are Comptoir General Impex, Ratec and Elaul. They make emergency lights and rechargeable battery systems for EC and north African markets, and have a 15 per cent market share in France.
