BFN [Text] Almost 100 Haitian boat people were repatriated on 25 April to Port-au-Prince by a U.S. Coast Guard patrol ship that intercepted them. The 98 refugees, who were trying to reach Florida on board a makeshift boat, were intercepted on 22 April in the Windward Passage between Haiti and Cuba. The boat people, including three children without parents, sailed from the island of La Tortue. It should be recalled that on Saturday, 95 Haitians, survivors of a shipwreck on an island of the Bahamas, were repatriated by plane from the Bahamas to Port-au-Prince.
