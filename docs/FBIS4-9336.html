BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 23 Apr (TANJUG) -- A nine-member delegation of the Russian State Duma, led by Nikolay Bezbirodov, deputy chairman of the Commiteee for Defense and Security, arrived in Pale today and began talks with Vladimir Lukic, prime minister of the republic of Srpska. The two sides are discussing the present political and security situation in the wake of last night's NATO ultimatum to Serbs in the republic of Srpska and the agreement which Slobodan Milosevic, president of the Republic of Serbia, and Radovan Karadzic, president of the republic of Srpska, reached with Yasushi Akashi, the UN secretary general's special envoy, in Belgrade last night. The Russian parliamentarians arrived in Pale with specific proposals involving various aspects of economic and cultural cooperation between the Russian Federation and the republic of Srpska.
