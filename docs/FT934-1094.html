931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / International Company News: Kells Minerals in reverse takeover World Fluids, the specialist chemical and additives manufacturer, is coming to the market through its reverse takeover by Kells Minerals, the Dublin-based exploration company. Kells is paying Pounds 5.5m via the issue of 45.9m shares to Mr John and Ms Geraldine Marett, giving them control. A further 3.58m shares are being issued to the trustees of the World Fluids pension scheme for World Fluids' principal property in Bury St Edmunds, Suffolk. The name is to be changed to World Fluids (Holdings). World Fluids, of which Mr John Marett is managing director, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 692,000 for the six months to September 30. Kells' shares were suspended at 21p, up 1/2 p on the day.
