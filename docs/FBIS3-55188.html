Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian government has earmarked $500 Mn for the construction of a new parliament complex in Moscow. This was reported on Wednesday [5 January] to the organizing commission of the new parliament by Pavel Borodin, a presidential spokesman. He said that by the end of January contracts are going to be signed with a number of foreign construction companies. According to Borodin, construction will begin on February 1 and end by January 1 next year. He said there was every guarantee that the project would be completed in 10 months. The complex will consist of an administrative building with a floorspace of 360,000 square meters and a 700-room hotel. It will be built in place of a stadium between Krasnopresnenskaya metro- station and the former building of parliament.
