January 26, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DERAILED: The weird vehicle you see humming along city streets this morning is exactly what it looks like: one of the original Disneyland monorail cars recently converted to a street machine. . . . Inside will be some Orange County mayors intent on seeing an 18-mile route proposed for a private Irvine-to-Anaheim monorail system. . . . Disney's "Mouseorail" soon heads out on a cross-country promotional tour. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
