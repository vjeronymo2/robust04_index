940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Goodwin advances to Pounds 180,000 Goodwin, the mechanical and refractory engineering group, announced a pre-tax profit of Pounds 180,237, up from Pounds 80,651, for the half year to Oct 31. Turnover rose from Pounds 6.6m to Pounds 6.8m. Earnings per share came out at 1.68p (0.75p). Mr John Goodwin, chairman, attributed the improvement to the group's continuing investment programme, but noted that prevailing market conditions remained highly competitive.
