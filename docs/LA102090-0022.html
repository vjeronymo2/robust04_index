October 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EVERYONE IS COMING TO THE RAMS' DEFENSE, OR, RATHER, LACK OF IT

What's wrong with the Rams?

1. The owner is incompetent and uninterested.

2. The general manager is incompetent.

3. The coaching staff is living in a dream world, thinking that they have a team full of superstars.

4. The team is full of underachievers who think they are superstars.

DICK ANDERSON

Oxnard
