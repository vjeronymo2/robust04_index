Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Brussels, March 11 (AFP) -- Moldovan President Mircea Snegur will sign an agreement at NATO headquarters here Wednesday making his country the 12th to join the partnership for peace program, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization said Friday [11 March]. The scheme, an alternative to full NATO membership offered to former East Bloc states, provides for joint maneuvers, planning, and peace maintenance operations. Other countries that have joined the program are: Romania, Lithuania, Poland, Estonia, Hungary, Ukraine, Slovakia, Bulgaria, Latvia, Albania, and the Czech Republic.
