Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 7/3/1994 -- A communique issued in Bucharest on 7 March informs that, in compliance with the recommendation of the Supreme Council of Defense, the Romanian Government has adopted a resolution for the appointment, organization and functioning of a government board for the restructuring of the defense industry. The restructuring of the defense industry is part of the reform programme developed by the Government of Romania, and its purpose is to adjust production capacities to the technical endowment requirements of the forces forming the national defense system, as well as to modernize and update certain units, to keep under conservation strictly specialized equipment, to convert, partly or entirely, some manufacturing units, to offer the personnel to be laid-off professional readjustment opportunities. The main aim of the government board for the restructuring of the defense industry is to adapt the defense industry to the new demands of the market economy without affecting Romania's defense capabilities.
