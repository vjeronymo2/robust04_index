Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu met with the NATO under secretary for political affairs, Mr. Gebhardt von Moltke, at Victoria Palace today. After the end of the talks, a brief news conference was held within the framework of which Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu emphasized the importance of this event for Romania, which represents the second stage of the Partnership for Peace, namely the preparation of the presentation document. In his turn, the guest expressed satisfaction with the warm reception given him, with cooperation with Romania, and with the stage of preparations, which Romania is making to ensure better cooperation with NATO.
