Negative Determinations NAFTA&hyph;TAA NAFTA&hyph;TAA&hyph;00060; B and B Garment Works, Inc., Parsons, TN The investigation revealed that criteria (3) and criteria (4) were not met. There was no shift in production from the workers' firm to Mexico or Canada during the relevant period. The investigation further revealed that the contractors from who B and B Garment Works received production orders did not contract work with firms in Mexico or Canada. The contractors are experiencing increased production orders.
