940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Business Travel: Air Macau to launch Air Macau will launch in the second half of 1995, with four jets and a staff of 300, including up to 40 pilots and 100 flight attendants. 'Our first routes, during the first two years of operations, will obviously be in south-east Asia, within a three and a half hour flight radius of Macau,' said David Young, director-general designate of Air Macau.
