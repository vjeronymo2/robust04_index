941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / Observer: Watch this space It didn't take National Parking Corporation long to climb back into its shell. With the last-minute collapse of the buy-out by Prudential, the founders reverted to form and yesterday asked the FT's reporter to leave the agm. Even the stock market's greatest basket cases nowadays begrudgingly admit the press. Unlike the car-parkers - who have long held the media to be nosy-parkers. The FT is still waiting to hear how many car parks NPC operates, a question posed in June.
