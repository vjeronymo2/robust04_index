BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 26 -- Lao Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat and his wife arrived in Hanoi this afternoon for an official visit to Vietnam as guest of Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. The Lao Foreign Ministry and his party [as received] were welcomed at the government guest house by Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, Chairman of the government Commission for Mountain and Ethnic Minorities and President of the Vietnam-Laos Friendship Association Hoang Duc Nghi and other Vietnamese officials. Lao Ambassador to Vietnam Bouasi Chaleunsouk was also on hand. Speaking at the welcome ceremony, the two foreign ministers expressed their wish and belief that the traditional fine relations between the two nations would be further consolidated for the interest of the two countries and prosperity, cooperation and stability in the region. In the evening, F.M. Nguyen Manh Cam and his wife gave a banquet in honour of Mr and Mrs Somsavat and the Lao delegation.
