BFN [Report by unidentified correspondent on National Assembly debate on cabinet changes proposed by Prime Minister Lyuben Berov at the National Assembly in Sofia -- live] [Text] Today's session of the National Assembly has just ended. After the National Assembly rejected the structural changes to the cabinet proposed by Prime Minister Lyuben Berov, the prime minister declared that he will have to ask for a vote of confidence in his cabinet. Under the Constitution, the Cabinet has to submit a proposal in writing for such a vote.
