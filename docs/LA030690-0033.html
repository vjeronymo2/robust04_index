March 6, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: MEN'S BASKETBALL

Christian Heritage meets Southern California College in the NAIA District 3 championship game at 7:30 tonight at Whittier College.

The Hawks (29-5), in only their fourth season of intercollegiate basketball, have won 11 consecutive games and 20 of their last 21.

Southern California (24-8) defeated Westmont, 88-83, in overtime Saturday and the Hawks rallied to an 84-69 victory over Fresno Pacific to reach the final.

The winner advances to the NAIA National Tournament beginning March 13 at Kansas City.
