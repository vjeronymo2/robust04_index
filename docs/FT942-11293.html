940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / 98% of schools order tests Copies of national curriculum tests which 14-year-olds in England and Wales are due to take next week have been ordered by 98 per cent of schools, government officials announced yesterday. However, the NUT teachers' union, which is continuing its boycott this year, played down the figures' significance. It said: 'We are confident that the boycott will be very strongly supported.'
