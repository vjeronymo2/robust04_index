Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Doha, 8 Jan (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar, has received a letter from His Highness Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, amir of Kuwait, dealing with developments in the region, the march of the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC], and bilateral relations. The letter also thanks Qatar for its position in support of Kuwait. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad, Kuwaiti first deputy prime minister and foreign minister, delivered the letter when his highness the amir of Qatar received him here today. During the meeting, they discussed regional, Arab, and international developments and ways to enhance bilateral relations in various fields. Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad's visit to Qatar is part of a tour of the GCC states during which he will deliver similar letters to their leaders.
