June 25, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THAT P.R. FLAIR

It seems to me that Okamoto would tap the minority resource, especially Japanese-Americans already in the film business in Hollywood. White Hollywood has ignored the masses of minority talent here.

Okamoto is a godsend for Asians, blacks, Native Americans and Mexican-Americans who've been standing in the wings.

PAUL R. CARTER

Hollywood
