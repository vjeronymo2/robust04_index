December 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WINE OF THE WEEK AND BEST NOUVEAU OF THE YEAR

1990 Quady Electra -- This low-alcohol (4%) wine from Andy Quady is 100% Orange Muscat with a hint of carbonation.

You could drink three glasses and get the alcohol equivalent of just one. It's delicious -- sweet, but not too sweet. It's a grand experience -- at $9.50 a bottle.
