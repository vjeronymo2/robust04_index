Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0900 GMT on 10 February reports: "Valleys Subdistrict detectives last night arrested a Nazareth resident following surveillance. They found 300 grams of heroin in his shoes." Tel Aviv YEDI'OT AHARONOT in Hebrew on 15 February, in a page-10 report by Mikhal Goldberg, states: "Yosef Zalmanovich, 41, from Jerusalem, a former police detective, was yesterday sentenced to 10 years in prison in the Jerusalem District Court. He had been convicted of participating in the `Colombian connection' affair, in which 120 kg of heroin were smuggled from Colombia to the United States." Writing on page 13 of the same issue of YEDI'OT AHARONOT, Yehezqel Adiram reports that a police raid on a Lod home uncovered 1.5 kg of heroin hidden in a toy. Five family members were detained. A report by Yoram Yarqoni on page 15 of the same newspaper states that Dov Alexander, 45, from Ramat Hasharon, who had been convicted of an attempt to smuggle 49 kg of heroin from Turkey to Israel aboard a yacht, was yesterday sentenced to nine years in prison.
