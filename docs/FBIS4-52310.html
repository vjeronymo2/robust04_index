BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 7 -- Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai received here yesterday afternoon a delegation of the External Relations Committee of the parliament of the Republic of Korea led its chairman Chung Jei Moun [name as received] now on a visit to Vietnam. Speaking to his guests, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai affirmed new step of the development in the relations between Vietnam and the Republic of Korea (ROK) in recent years, and hailed the ROK's successes in the cause of renovation and socio-economic development in the past time, the deputy prime minister expressed his belief that the delegation's visit to Vietnam would further promote the friendship and multifaceted cooperation between Vietnam and the Republic of Korea. Mr. Chung Jei Moun, for his part, expressed his admiration at the great achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in their cause of national construction and renovation. He said he hoped that Vietnam and ROK would promote the exchange of their experience, thus contributing to further development of the friendship and cooperation between the two legislatures, two governments and peoples.
