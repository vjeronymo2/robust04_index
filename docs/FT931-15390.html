930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / IT acquisitions sharply down ACQUISITIONS in the information technology sector fell sharply last year but many companies are now returning to the acquisition trail, according to industry analysts. The number of acquisitions fell by 13 per cent to 233 from 269 in 1991, while 1990's figure was 283, according to Regent Associates, acquisition specialists, and Richard Holway, an IT business consultant. Total value of the deals fell 45 per cent to Pounds 1.98bn from Pounds 3.59bn a year ago.
