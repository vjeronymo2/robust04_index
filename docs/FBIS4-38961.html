BFN [Text] Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov announced in the Crimean parliament that he had not as yet signed the law on restoration of the constitutional basis of Crimean statehood adopted by the Crimean parliament on May 20. Crimean Minister of Justice Lyubov Yeliseyeva-Bora said that the Crimean constitution of May 6, 1992, had not been published in media and thus was not enacted. The Crimean constitution of May 6, 1992, provides for greater autonomy for Crimea within Ukraine and for dual citizenship for Crimean inhabitants.
