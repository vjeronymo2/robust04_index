940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Rolls-Royce - Other companies The power generation and aero-engines group, is expected to reveal a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 32m to between Pounds 39m and Pounds 46m for the first half of 1994 when it reports on Thursday. Also on Thursday, analysts expect Vickers, which makes Rolls-Royce cars and tanks, to have all but doubled pre-tax profits in the first half to between Pounds 15m and Pounds 18m.
