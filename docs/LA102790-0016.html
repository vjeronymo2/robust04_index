October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ERROR WAS CAUGHT WITH BACK TO PLATE

In your sports section of Oct. 16, under Past World Series results, the 1954 winner was New York (NL), not New York (AL), over Cleveland (AL), 4-0.

Dusty Rhodes and Willie Mays also will thank you when you make this correction. The Cleveland Indians were too embarrassed to notice.

WAYNE L. SNIVELY

Pasadena
