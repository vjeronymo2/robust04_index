Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, PLO Executive Committee member, has said that the PLO welcomes the UN Security Council resolution which denounced the al-Ibrahimi Mosque Massacre in Hebron and regards it a step toward helping resume the peace process. In a statement to REUTER, 'Abd-Rabbuh said that the PLO will receive an Israeli delegation within the next few days to discuss measures that will provide security for Palestinians in the occupied territory. 'Abd-Rabbuh added that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat has no plans to meet with Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres in Cairo tomorrow, as was reported earlier. 'Abd-Rabbuh said that what is important for Palestinians is to implement the UN Security Council resolution on the Hebron massacre and for the Israeli Government to adopt full and tangible measures in this regard. 'Abd-Rabbuh said that the U.S. coordinator, Dennis Ross, proposed such a meeting in a telephone call to 'Arafat last Thursday.
