BFN [Text] Algiers, March 28 (AFP) -- A Russian embassy employee was murdered Monday at Saoula, south of the capital, the fourth Russian slain in the North African country in six months, security officials said. Two Frenchmen were slain last week, victims of the war being waged by Islamic fundamentalists against the military-led government. More than 30 foreigners have been killed in the country in the past six months.
