940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: United Inds recovers United Industries has returned to profit with Pounds 563,000 pre-tax for the six months to July 2, compared with losses of Pounds 1.5m for the previous nine months. It is paying its first dividend in more than three years with an interim of 0.1p. Turnover was Pounds 23.6m (Pounds 19.2m) including acquisitions of Pounds 7.87m and Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 1.39m) from discontinued operations. Earnings per share were 0.36p (3.77p losses).
