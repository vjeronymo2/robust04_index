930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Adam & Harvey falls to Pounds 4.86m Adam & Harvey, the international distribution and steel stockholding concern, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 6.4m to Pounds 4.86m for the year ended March 31, from turnover behind at Pounds 40.4m against Pounds 42.7m. Earnings per share were 54.2p (69.9p) while the dividend is stepped up to 12p (11p) with an increased final of 7p.
