Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] St. Petersburg February 8 ITAR-TASS -- The Council of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the Commonwealth of the Independent States today elected Vladimir Shumeyko council chairman by secret ballot. The vote was taken soon after the opening of the council meeting, which is attended by representatives of all parliaments of the Commonwealth member countries, except Uzbekistan. Georgia is for the first time attending as a fully-fledged member. Moldova and Ukraine have sent observers. Vladimir Shumeyko is speaker of the Russian federal legislature's upper chamber. All eight leaders of the parliamentary delegations from Commonwealth states voted for Shumeyko, whose candidature had been previously approved by the parliament leaders in some Commonwealth countries.
