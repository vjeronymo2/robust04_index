BFN [Text] Mohammad Hashemi, Iranian Foreign Ministry first assistant [title as heard], who arrived in Ankara on an official visit, has started official meetings. Hashemi was received by Interior Minister Nahit Mentese who stressed that the Turkish-Iranian border is a stable region unchanged for centuries. In answer to a question, Mentese said that Iran handed over to Turkey 18 PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] members as well as the bodies of 10 PKK terrorists. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin received Hashemi and conferred with him for some time.
