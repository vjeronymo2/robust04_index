941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / Pension schemes shift holdings UK pension schemes were unusually heavy sellers of shares in foreign companies in the third quarter, data from the Central Statistics Office show. Pension schemes sold Pounds 1.9bn of foreign shares in the quarter, continuing a trend started in the second quarter when their holdings fell by a net Pounds 694m. The outflow from foreign shares was greater in the third quarter than the total net inflow by pension schemes for the whole of last year.
