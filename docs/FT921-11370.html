920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Little change at Continental Assets Continental Assets Trust, which concentrates on smaller companies in continental Europe, reported undiluted net asset value of 165.2p per share at end-December, virtually unchanged on the 164p at the end of 1990. At June 30 1991, it was higher at 177.59p. Attributable profits amounted to Pounds 339,000 (Pounds 387,000) for earnings of 1.56p (1.78p) per 75p share. A dividend of 1.25p (0.7p) is proposed.
