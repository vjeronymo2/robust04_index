BFN ["M.P."-signed report: "Name Sign Scandal in Transylvania, Too"] [Text] On 21 June in Mures county, county police and road maintenance workers knocked down and smashed bilingual (Hungarian- Romanian) signs on the order of the prefect. Law enforcement units entered the private territories where the bilingual signs were located without the permission or agreement of the owners. The police removed signs in more than 20 localities in Mures county. The statement of the county organization of the Democratic Union of Hungarians in Romania [RMDSZ -- UDMR in Romanian] published on 22 June condemns the anti-Hungarian provocation in Mures county. The statement specially emphasizes the Council of Europe documents and recommendations the Romanian government undertook to observe and whose edicts are ignored by the county prefect, the police, and the employees of other state offices.
