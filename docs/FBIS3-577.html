Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mmabatho March 9 SAPA -- Bophuthatswana police have denied reports of South African Police involvement in a march by striking teachers on the South African Embassy in Mmabatho on Wednesday [9 March]. In a statement, Bophuthatswana police rejected the report which said South African Police were called in to assist when the teachers clashed with Bophuthatswana policemen. "This is untrue. Although a Bophuthatswana police contingent was present, these men kept their distance as they had been requested to do," the statement said.
