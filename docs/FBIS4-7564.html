BFN [Text] Tirana, March 31 (ATA) -- "An American initiative would have positive impact on the Kosova question," Dr. Ibrahim Rugova, president of the Republic of Kosova, said in an interview with ZERI published in Pristina. President Rugova said that "the American policy is more pragmatic." In regard to the negotiations with the Serbian side on the Kosova question, Mr. Rugova said he had asked an American initiative for intermediation in the presence of European allies and in consultation with Russia. Concerning his approach in possible talks, Dr. Rugova said he will observe the referendum of September 1991 and will represent the will of the Kosova citizens expressed in the referendum.
