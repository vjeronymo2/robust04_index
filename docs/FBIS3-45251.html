Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday some cadres at the regimental-level and above, as well as advanced individuals from different units of the Chengdu Military Region, gathered in Chengdu to participate in a meeting on studying Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, his thought on Army building in the new period, and General Secretary Jiang Zemin's instructions on Army building. Among present in the front row at the meeting were: Chengdu Military Regional leaders Li Jiulong, Zhang Zhijian, Liao Xilong, and Zhu Chengyou; provincial party Secretary Xie Shijie; and Govenor Xiao Yang. Commander Li Jiulong addressed the meeting amid warm applause. After reviewing work in the last five years, he said: The objective for the military region's reform and construction in the coming five years is to profoundly implement the spirit of the 14th CPC National Congress and the strategic policy for the new period, to arm the three services with Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and his thought on Army building, to ensure stability in the armed forces and border areas, to maintain social stability, and to provide a powerful safety guarantee for the country's reform, opening up, and modernization.
