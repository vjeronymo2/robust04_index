921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / International Company News: Laidlaw plans Italian purchase LAIDLAW, the North American waste services and school bus operator, is about to make a direct investment in Europe by acquiring a mid-sized Italian waste technology company. Laidlaw said yesterday the company would not be identified until its employees and customers had been told of the takeover. The Italian concern has annual sales of Dollars 40m to Dollars 50m and specialises in hazardous waste treatment. Laidlaw expects to use the Italian company's expertise in its US and Canadian operations.
