Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Kigali, 22 Feb (AFP) -- Martin Bucyana, chairman of the Coalition for the Defense of the Republic, which seeks to defend the interest of Hutus, was killed today by an angry crowd following the assassination yesterday of Felicien Gatabazi, Rwandan minister of public works and energy, Rwandan radio announced. Bucyana was attacked by demonstrators of Gatabazi's party, the opposition Social Democratic Party, at Butare in the south of the country. He died after being admitted to hospital, the radio explained.
