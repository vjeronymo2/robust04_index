BFN [Text] Mersin, Mar 26 (AA) -- A gunman was killed and a policeman was wounded in an overnight election-related clash in this southern city, police said on Saturday. The clash started when gunmen, fleeing after throwing a bomb at the election office of the pro-Islamic Welfare [Prosperity] Party, fired on police, who urged them to surrender. One of the gunmen was killed, the others fled. Five other people, including a 12-year-old boy, were killed and 26 were wounded in the last couple of days in several assaults on party offices and candidates in the run-up to Sunday's nationwide municipal elections.
