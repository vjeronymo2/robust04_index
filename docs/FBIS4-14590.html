BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 1 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin received Jozef Oleksy, marshal of the Polish Sejm, in the Kremlin on Friday [1 April]. Oleksy heads a Polish parliamentary delegation which came to Moscow for an official visit. During the conversation Yeltsin expressed satisfaction with the development of parliamentary ties between Russia and Poland and declared for the further development of Russo-Polish relations on a basis of law, says a report of the Russian president's press office. Oleksy noted the importance of interparliamentary contacts between the two countries and stressed that various sections of the Polish society wish to strengthen and develop ties with Russia.
