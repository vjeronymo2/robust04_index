BFN [Text] Seoul, May 3 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam Tuesday appointed Kim Myong-yun, permanent adviser to the ruling Democratic Liberal Party (DLP), as senior vice president of the Advisory Council on Democratic and Peaceful Unification. Kim Myong-yun has decided to resign as permanent DLP adviser and chairman of the district party chapter in Myongju-Yangyang in Kangwon Province and leave the party in order to concentrate on the Advisory Council's work. He replaces Yi Hong-ku, who became deputy prime minister and minister of the National Unification Board.
