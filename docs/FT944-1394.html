941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / International Capital Markets: Honda Motors Honda Motor of Japan yesterday signed an agreement with Turkey's Anadolu Endustri Holding to set up a joint venture to manufacture and market motorcycles in Turkey. The Japanese company said the two companies would modify an existing plant owned by Anadolu to start producing 125cc motorcycles in mid-1996 at an initial rate of 6,000 units a year.
