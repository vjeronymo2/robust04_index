November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CELEBRITY ADVOCATES FOR POLITICAL CAUSES

If one has the guts and the drive to determine the relative worth of a political issue, regardless of who is for or against it, he doesn't need to swallow the celebrities' pitch.

Celebrities are media creations who live in airy fantasy. It's only when one realizes that there are as many air-heads among the consuming public as there are among "celebrities" that the subject even deserves attention.

LAWRENCE M. KATES

Los Angeles
