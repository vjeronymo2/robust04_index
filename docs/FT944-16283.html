941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Trade News: GE plans company in Shanghai - Contracts & Ventures General Electric, the US conglomerate, will establish a holding company in Shanghai to oversee its rapidly expanding business in China, including plans to invest between Dollars 300m and Dollars 400m over the next three to five years. Mr John Welch, GE's chairman, said in Shanghai that the holding company would 'provide services to GE's joint ventures and affiliates, including currency management, marketing, purchasing and after sales support.' GE has invested some Dollars 120m in China in joint ventures producing medical equipment, lighting and plastic compounds. It also has more than Dollars 1bn in aircraft and containers on lease to China.
