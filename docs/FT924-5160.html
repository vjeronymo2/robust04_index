921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / NMB acquires stake from Steel Burrill Steel Burrill Jones, insurance broker, has sold its 21.2 per cent shareholding in Newman Martin and Buchan for Pounds 2.47m in cash. NMB is an insurance broker which specialises in energy, marine and reinsurance broking. It has acquired SBJ's holding pursuant to a repurchase of its own shares. SJB said it had relinquished its investment as it now owned SBJ Regis Low which competed with NMB for business.
