February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY PEOPLE

Your Staff, a Woodland Hills provider of personnel administration services for businesses, appointed James R. Conner and Marc E. Stein as senior vice presidents.

Conner, who most recently was vice president of operations, will manage Your Staff's operations and financial activities. Stein, who will oversee the company's marketing and sales programs, previously was vice president of marketing.
