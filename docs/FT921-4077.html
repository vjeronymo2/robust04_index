920311 FT 11 MAR 92 / Housing stock transfer agreed BROMLEY Borough Council in London was given government permission yesterday to transfer its stock of nearly 12,000 homes to the recently established Broomleigh Housing Association for Pounds 117.6m. Sir George Young, housing minister, said Bromley's would be the first such transfer in Greater London. Seventy-five per cent of tenants voted in a ballot, and 55 per cent supported the transfer. Receipts from future sales will be shared between the council and the association.
