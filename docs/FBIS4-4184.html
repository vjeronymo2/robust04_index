BFN [Text] Islamabad, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Ambassador in Pakistan Zhou Gang donated here today a total of 555 Chinese books and some 90 audio and video cassettes to the National Institute of Modern Languages of Pakistan. Pakistan Federal Minister for Education Syed Khurshid Ahmad Shah, who attended the donation ceremony, expressed his thanks to the ambassador and also praised the role of the institute, especially its Chinese language department, in promoting the friendship and understanding between the two countries. Over 700 people, mostly from the three services of Pakistan Armed Forces, got trained in the Chinese department of the institute since its establishment in 1970.
