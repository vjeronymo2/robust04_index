October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MIXED MEDIA: *** "ONE FAIR SUMMER EVENING" NANCI GRIFFITH ; MCA MUSIC VIDEO LASER DISC ($24.95);

ITEMS IN THIS PERIODIC SURVEY OF POP-RELATED BOOKS, VIDEOCASSETTES AND LASER DISCS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE (A CLASSIC).

What more can you ask of a video than it turn someone who was only mildly interested in a veteran performer into a fan? Part of the credit on "Evening" goes to director Bud Schaetzle, who has done an excellent job of capturing the intimacy of a Griffith club performance in this modest but winning 45-minute package. But the video's most important element is Griffith herself. A singer-songwriter whose artful, warm music blends elements of country and folk, Griffith sometimes seems a bit too understated on record, but she blossoms here with a series of songs that are equally rich in detail and spirit. ROBERT HILBURN
