BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 5 TASS -- A second stage of signing an agreement on civic accord will be held in the Kremlin today. The document will be signed by representatives of industrial, banking and entrepreneurial associations, president's chief of staff Sergey Filatov told ITAR-TASS that over 200 public organisations declared that they were ready to sign the agreement. [sentence as recevied] The agreement provides for main guarantees of stability in society, making amendments to the constitution by means of conciliatory procedures, pledges not to stage political campaigns for the purpose of holding early elections and a ban on the use of force methods in the settlement of conflicts. The first stage of signing the civic accord pact was held on April 28, when the document was signed by leaders of executive and legislative branches of power, subjects of the federation, representatives of political, public and religious organisations. According to Filatov, the agreement on civic accord has "positively affected the general climate in society and clearly showed a strong will of the Russian people to attain peace and calm." Filatov is convinced that the agreement on civic accord will help avoid political turmoils and create normal conditions for carrying out economic reforms.
