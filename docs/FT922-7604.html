920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / International Company News: M&S to sell off Canadian chain WISE STORES has agreed in principle to buy the Peoples division of Marks & Spencer's Canadian unit for about CDollars 24m (USDollars 20m), AP-DJ reports. The final purchase price will be determined by the Peoples chain's asset values at the effective date of sale. Peoples, which operates 81 junior department stores in eastern Canada, lost CDollars 1.95m on sales of CDollars 164m in the year ended March 1992. Wise, with 49 junior department stores in eastern Canada, expects to sign a definitive agreement to buy Peoples about June 1.
