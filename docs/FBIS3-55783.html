Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Yevgeniy Solomenko: "Swift `Falcon' Will Bring Moscow and St. Petersburg Closer"] [Text] St. Petersburg--The "Rubin" marine technology central design bureau has completed the development of the "Falcon" ["Sokol"] super-high-speed train. This express train, which can cover 350 km in one hour, will run between St. Petersburg and Moscow. The plan has been handed over to the "Vysokoskorostnyye magistrali" Russian joint-stock company. The superexpress is "Rubin's" first conversion venture. Until now the central design bureau has specialized in the creation of nuclear submarines and other warships.
