941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Equities closed 1.06 per cent lower suffering mainly from a sharp decline in share prices of Mexican media giant Grupo Televisa. Transactions were dominated by movements of Televisa shares traded locally and on Wall Street as ADRs. Televisa's CPO shares were crushed in the sell-off, dropping 13.72 per cent, or 12.9 new pesos, to 81.1 new pesos. The key IPC index closed off 28.16 points at 2,621.63. Volume totaled a moderately light 601.3m new pesos, on 34.7m shares traded.
