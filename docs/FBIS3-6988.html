Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 22 -- By mid-March, the whole country had cropped 232,260 ha with winter-spring rice, accounting for nearly 99 percent of the plan. In general the paddy is growing well. Meanwhile the southern provinces had harvested 540,140 ha of early winter-spring rice. According to initial reports, the Mekong River delta achieved a yield of 4-6 tons/ha.
