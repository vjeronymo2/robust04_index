BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Korean Christians Federation in a statement Wednesday said that the surprise raid and forcible search of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) and its officials by the Japanese police, part of the hostile policy toward the DPRK, is a deliberate and premeditatedicrime to destroy Chongnyon organisation and block its patriotic activities. It strongly demanded that the Japanese authorities immediately stop their hostile policy toward the DPRK and anti-Chongnyon campaign, strictly punish those who are responsible for it and take a responsible step so as to prevent the suppression of the Chongnyon and infringement upon the human rights of Koreans in Japan. The statement expressed the hope that all the Christians of the world who respect love and reconciliation, peace and justice and broad segments of the Japanese people would pay a serious attention to the grave obtaining situation, help stop the Japanese authorities' hostile policy toward the DPRK and anti-Chongnyon campaign and continue to express support and solidarity for Chongnyon in its righteous activities for democractic national rights and national reunification.
