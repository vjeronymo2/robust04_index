November 25, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

VERMEIL'S ASSESSMENT OF USC IS ALL WET

Dick Vermeil has lost it! After Notre Dame piled up 266 yards and four touchdowns on the ground against USC's vaunted "NFL-type defensive players," Vermeil says USC is No. 1. This is based on USC's victories against powerhouses Stanford, Oregon State and Arizona, no doubt. Why bother to play the games?

RICH BARTON, Orange
