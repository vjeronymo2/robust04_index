January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I and many of my friends take strong exception to the letter that said "Anything But Love" is "Anything But Funny."

This show is one of the freshest comedies of the season. The writing is exceptional and the acting is superb. My friends and I would like to commend the creators of this unique and refreshing show and would highly recommend it to anyone who wants to enjoy a good laugh.

Paul R. Mickelson, Los Angeles
