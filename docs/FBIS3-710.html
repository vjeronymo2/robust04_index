Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A coup d'etat has supposedly been foiled in our country. According to an independent Niger newspaper quoted by the NIGER NEWS AGENCY [ANP], the coup plot was aimed at arresting the head of state, the prime minister, the speaker of the National Assembly and his first deputy, and the chief of Army staff. The operation was slated for last night at the mosque where the grand prayer of the Night of Power was scheduled to take place. According to the newspaper, the conspirators included Major Moumouni Boureima and Mr. Wantche Oumarou Mallam, who have been arrested by the Niger Armed Forces chief of general staff. Still quoting the newspaper, ANP said several other officers said to be involved in the coup plot are being questioned at the Army Headquarters. However, no official source has confirmed or denied the news.
