Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian president's official representative, Ambassador Vladlen Vasev, has confirmed Moscow's position of standing for "the territorial integrity of Moldova and the overcoming of all discord by political means." The Moldovan president's press service announced that Moldovan President Mircea Snegur and Russian Ambassador Vasev had met on Friday. They agreed that settlement of the Transdniestrian issue would contribute to restoration of stability in the region, which conformed with the interests of the population. Moldovan President Snegur stressed that the democratic parliamentary elections and the sociological poll, during which most of the population had spoken for independence of Moldova, were the basis for normalization of the situation in Moldova's eastern regions. Namely, Snegur favored granting a special legal status with a high level of autonomy to this region.
