Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Mar (MENA)--Intensive security campaigns have been launched in various parts of Cairo to arrest fugitive terrorists and outlaws. Regular units from the police force, central security, the state security intelligence, Cairo intelligence department, public security, and the traffic department took part in these campaigns which began early this morning. The forces that took part in these campaigns are continuing their tasks in the various slum areas in 'Ayn Shams, al-Basatin, certain areas in central Cairo, and the suburbs. These campaigns are scheduled to extend to the areas east, west, north, and south of Cairo and will continue for several days.
