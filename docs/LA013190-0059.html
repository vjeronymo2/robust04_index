January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: SYLMAR TO STAGE BASEBALL FUND-RAISER

The Sylmar High Baseball Alumni Assn. will sponsor a golf tournament fund-raiser at El Cariso Golf Course in Sylmar on Feb. 23.

Proceeds will go toward the establishment of a college scholarship for a Sylmar ballplayer.

The $40 cost includes greens fees, golf cart and prizes.

Information: 818-367-1971.
