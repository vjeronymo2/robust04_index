930605 FT 05 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market was firm and active this week, reports Man Producten, reflecting lower crop estimates in the majority of growing areas and that exporters were withdrawn from the market. White Muntok spot was quoted at Dollars 1,800 a tonne, May/Jun delivery at Dollars 1,700 and Jun/Jul at Dollars 1,725. Sarawak, Thai, Chinese and Brazilian white pepper were off the market. Among the black peppers Sarawak black label spot was at Dollars 1,150 a tonne and Jun/Jul delivery at Dollars 1,100. Sarawak yellow label spot was Dollars 1,200 and Jun/Jul Dollars 1,150. Brazilian grade 1, Jun/Jul was offered at Dollars 1,035 a tonne and India MG1 at Dollars 1,100 for June delivery.
