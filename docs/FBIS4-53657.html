BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 21 (KCNA) -- The International Liaison Committee for the Reunification and Peace in Korea printed a bulletin on May 10. The bulletin carried articles entitled "Struggle of South Korean Democratic Forces Against the U.S. Imperialists' Aggression", "Members of the International Environmental Organisation 'Greenpeace' Participate in Demonstration Against Nuclear Waste Pollution in South Korea" and so on. The bulletin in an article headlined "The Korean Armistice Agreement Must Be Replaced by Peace Agreement Without Delay" says that the introduction of latest arms equipment into South Korea by the United States is a gross violation of the Korean armistice agreement. It calls on all the democratic forces and peace organisations of the world to join in the movement for putting an immediate end to the escalation of the dangerous tension on the Korean peninsula and for replacing the Korean armistice agreement with a peace agreement. It carried the gist of the April 29 statement of a spokesman for the DPRK Foreign Ministry denouncing the hostile moves of the Japanese authorities against the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon).
