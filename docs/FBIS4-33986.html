BFN [From the "Telemetro Reports" newscast] [Text] Foreign Minister Jose Raul Mulino has stated that the application of economic sanctions and the trade embargo decreed against Haiti will harm the Haitian people more than their leaders. Mulino made this statement during his visit to Colombia, where the Fourth Ibero-American Summit begins tomorrow. According to the Panamanian foreign minister, the Haitian case is similar to the U.S. blockade against Cuba.
