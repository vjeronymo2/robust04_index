921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: JO Walker cuts loss via savings JO Walker, the Leicester-based timber importer, reduced its pre-tax loss from Pounds 294,000 to Pounds 162,000 in the first half of 1992. That reflected the state of the industry caused by the recession and the effects of the cost saving procedures. Turnover came to Pounds 6.33m (Pounds 6.93m). After a tax credit of Pounds 8,000 (Pounds 100,000) losses per share were cut to 19.2p (24.2p).
