920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / BT offers discount on domestic bills BT YESTERDAY launched a discount scheme for residential customers who run up high phone bills. Option 15 offers discounts ranging from 5 per cent to 10 per cent, depending on how many direct dial calls are made. Subscribers who join have to pay Pounds 4 per quarter. BT says the breakeven point, when a customer receives enough discount to cover the fee, is Pounds 73.04 spent on calls. The option could save subscribers Pounds 13 on a Pounds 380 bill.
