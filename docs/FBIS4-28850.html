BFN [Report from KBS General Bureau in Pusan] [Text] In connection with North Korea's nuclear issue, a ROK-U.S. joint inspection has been conducted of a ROK vessel that will be mobilized to transport U.S. military supplies in an emergency. The inspection was conducted at Point No. 1 of the Central Pier of Pusan Harbor on 10 June. It has been confirmed that an inspection was conducted of the facilities of the vessel; of the procedure to hand over the vessel in an emergency; and of the status of the fuel storage of No. 9 Donam, a 4,998 tonne-class cargo vessel belonging to the Hung-A Maritime Company. An official concerned at Pusan Harbor said: As South-North relations have recently become tense, I understand an inspection has been conducted of a vessel that will be mobilized in an emergency. In an emergency, several vessels, including tankers and passenger ships--besides the aforementioned vessel--will be handed over to the U.S. Armed Forces.
