BFN [Text] Today, Chief of the Russian Foreign Intelligence, Yevgeniy Primakov, arrived in Kishinev [Chisinau]. He met Moldovan National Security Minister, Vasile Calmoi. They discussed joint actions by the Russian and Moldovan special services in struggle against terrorism, organized crime, drug business and illegal arms traffic. The schedule of the visit also provides for a meeting between Primakov and Moldovan President Mircea Snegur.
