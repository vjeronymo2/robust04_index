Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Algerian President Meets With Leaderships; `Group' Claims Responsibility for Frenchmen's Murder"] [Excerpts] Algiers, Paris, Washington, Jeddah, AL-HAYAH -- [passage omitted] The Algerian "Armed Islamic Group" [GIA] has claimed responsibility for the two Frenchmen's murder in the Birkhadem area near the capital, Algiers, the day before yesterday. A statement issued by the group, copies of which AL-HAYAH has received in Peshawar by fax from abroad, says: "The Armed Islamic Group in Algeria claims responsibility for the murder of two French crusaders [salibiyyayn]." The statement adds: "This operation falls within the program drawn up by the group to eliminate any presence of Jews and Christians in the land of Islam in Algeria." In its statement the group said that the presence of the Jews, Christians, and polytheists in the land of Islam is a direct support for an infidel and illegitimate government which should be eliminated by the blade of the sword.
