941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Hungarian Inv Tst Hungarian Investment Trust ended the six months to August 31 with net asset value at Dollars 95.57, against Dollars 94.97 at the same stage of 1993 and Dollars 108.88 at February 28 1994. Net revenue in the first half fell from Dollars 968,000 to Dollars 891,000 (Pounds 561,614). Earnings per share came to 89 cents (97 cents).
