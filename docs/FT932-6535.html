930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / US oilmen abducted in Yemen TWO US oilmen working in a remote part of Yemen have been abducted by tribesmen, Reuter reports from Sana. The two Americans and their Yemeni driver, all working for Dallas-based Hunt Oil, were ambushed on Tuesday afternoon in an area where Hunt has a big concession. The government was negotiating for their release, a company source said. Several Hunt employees have been abducted in the past two years by tribesmen. All have been released unharmed.
