May 9, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: WESTERN DIGITAL TO SELL ITS FULL LINE OF PRODUCTS THROUGH COMPUTERLAND;

SCIENCE/TECHNOLOGY

Western Digital has signed an agreement with ComputerLand Corp. under which the giant retail chain will sell Western Digital's full line of products.

Western Digital, an Irvine-based manufacturer of personal computer components, did not disclose a dollar value for the contract.

ComputerLand is the world's largest franchised computer chain, with 750 stores in 34 countries.

Western Digital said the agreement covers its complete line of computer-storage controllers, communications and graphics products.

"We view ComputerLand as a strategic partner in delivering our products to the Fortune 1000 marketplace," Western Digital Chairman Roger Johnson said. David Olmos, Times Staff Writer
