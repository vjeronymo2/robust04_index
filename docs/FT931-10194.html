930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Togo peace talks break down Talks aimed at rescuing the west African state of Togo from bloodshed broke down yesterday, French Co-operation Minister Marcel Debarge said, Reuter reports from Colmar. Representatives of hardline President Gnassingbe Eyadema left Colmar in eastern France during the night, after talks with the country's pro-democracy movement had been postponed indefinitely, he added.
