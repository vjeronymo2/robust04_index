920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / International Company News: H-P to buy out partner HEWLETT-Packard said it will buy out its Hungarian partner Controll Rt from a joint venture, Reuter reports from Paris. The US computer firm gave no financial details of the deal. It said it had a minority stake in the joint venture since May 1991. The firm will now be wholly owned by HP and known as Hewlett-Packard Magyarorszag Kft. The firm's general manager will stay on. The venture had expanded more quickly than expected, Hewlett-Packard said.
