Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Claims that Yasar Kaya, former leader of Democracy Party [DEP] and owner of OZGUR GUNDEM, has escaped abroad are gaining acceptance. It is being reported that Kaya is already in Germany and will ask for political asylum there. In the meantime, Istanbul State Security Court ordered Kaya's detention in absentia. Kaya is being tried on charges of engaging in separatist propaganda in his articles in OZGUR GUNDEM. OZGUR GUNDEM officials say that Kaya has not contacted them at all.
