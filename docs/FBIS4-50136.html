BFN ["Congratulatory message" from Gyaincain Norbu, chairman of Tibet Autonomous Region, to the China Tibet Expedition; from the "News" program] [Text] I am glad to learn that nine members of the China Tibet Expedition which is scaling 14 mountains higher than 8,000 meters in the world -- (Wangjia), (Ciren Duoji), (Bianba Zhaxi), (Daqiong), (Jiabu), (Dajimi), (Renna), (Luoze) and (Akebu) -- after a tenacious struggle, reached the top of Mount Xixabangma at 1255 hours on 7 May 1994. I hereby extend my warm congratulations to you. I hope you will continue to exert yourselves, carry forward the traditions of mountaineers of this autonomous region, achieve more success in scaling the other 11 mountains 8,000 meters or higher, and win honor for the people of all nationalities in Tibet. [signed] Gyaincain Norbu, chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Region and chief adviser to the China Tibet Expedition. [dated] 11 May 1994
