940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): T Cowie - Other Companies T Cowie: An improved performance from the contract hire and coach distribution is expected to lift interim pre-tax profits at the vehicle leasing and motor trading group by about 27 per cent tomorrow. The upturn to nearly Pounds 20m is likely to be fuelled mainly by the finance division, dominated by Cowie Interleasing.
