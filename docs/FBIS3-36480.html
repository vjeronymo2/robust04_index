Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russia's president has signed a ruling on measures to assist the activity of the Russian-American Enterprise Fund. The Russian State Property Management Committee has been charged with coordinating work to assist the fund's activity. The committee may call on the Finance Ministry for help. Robert Talbin, the fund's general director, has said the fund's activity will cover economically advantageous Russian enterprises with up to 2,500 staff, with production of consumer goods being the object of its attention. As initial capital, the fund will receive 340 million dollars from the U.S. Government. In the future, the fund intends to attract contributions by organizations and private persons.
