Language: French Article Type:CSO [Text] Interior Minister Charles Pasqua played a determining role in the Paris visit of Tariq `Aziz last October. Iraq's number two man, officially in Paris to seek medical attention, had numerous political and economic conversations. At an emergency meeting of the appropriate ministers, it had been decided that no contact would take place. The decision came too late: Pasqua had already met with `Aziz the previous day.
