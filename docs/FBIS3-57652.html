Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "Hold the Front Page!" rubric: "KGB Continues To Fragment"] [Text] Information has come to MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS from trustworthy sources about a new reorganization of the Russian state security structure. The president's staff is processing documents on the creation of yet another special service. Plans are in the works to remove the military intelligence directorate from the Russian Federation Federal Counterintelligence Service, and to create on its basis a separate state security structure. This is being done in order to place the military counterintelligence men in control of the army directly under the president of Russia.
