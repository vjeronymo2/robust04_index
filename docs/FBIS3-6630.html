Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The well-known figure of the opposition forces of Mongolia, State Great Hural member D. Ganbold on his way from Germany to homeland stopped in Moscow, where he had a meeting with the leader of the party "Vybor Rossii" ("Choice of Russia") Yegor Gaydar. The chairman of the Mongolian National Democratic Party D. Ganbold also earlier, holding the post of the first vice prime minister of the country, made attempts to establish contact with Yegor Gaydar. Two years ago his meeting with Gaydar had failed as a plane with him on board has arrived in Moscow late.
