940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): Amsterdam A major survey of Renaissance prints opens at the Rijskmuseum on Saturday. The exhibition is devoted to the years 1470-1500, and aims to show how masters from different countries, including Mantegna, Durer and Lucas van Leyden, contributed to the rapid development of printmaking techniques and functions. The selection includes some private loans which have never been exhibited before.
