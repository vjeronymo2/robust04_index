920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Central Motor halved CAPITAL SPENDING on site development contributed to a halving of pre-tax profits at Central Motor Auctions. Auction proceeds, excluding VAT, for this USM-quoted company, fell Pounds 3m to Pounds 152.8m for profits of Pounds 432,000 (Pounds 861,000). The result was struck after a downturn from interest received of Pounds 156,000 to Pounds 112,000 payable as a result of the capital expenditure, and included exceptionals of Pounds 165,000 (Pounds 256,000) from property sales. Earnings per share came out at 2.7p (5.1p). The interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
