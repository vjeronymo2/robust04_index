921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / W. German industry plans more production cuts West German industry is planning more production cuts after a further fall in new orders during October, the Ifo economics institute in Munich said yesterday, writes Christopher Parkes in Frankfurt. Business conditions again worsened and manufacturers had become even more pessimistic, a monthly survey said. A modest reduction in west German inflation from 3.7 per cent in October is widely expected to be followed by further rises.
