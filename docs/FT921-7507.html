920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Smaller Companies Inv net assets slip Smaller Companies Invest-ment Trust reported net asset value of 82.9p per share at December 31, against 84.6p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 404,000 (Pounds 116,000) for earnings per share of 2.7p (0.77p). A final dividend of 1.3p (0.7p) brings the total to 2.5p (0.7p).
