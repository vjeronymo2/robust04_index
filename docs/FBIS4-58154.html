BFN [Excerpts] Edib Bukvic, deputy prime minister of Bosnia-Herzegovina, who is in Tehran at the head of a delegation, was received today by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani. According to a report by our correspondent, during the meeting the Bosnian deputy prime minister described the struggle of the people of Bosnia-Herzegovina against the Serbian aggressors as a struggle for safeguarding the Islamic aspirations of the whole world. Thanking the humanitarian support by the Iranian Government and people for the tyrannized people of Bosnia, he said: Iran is among the greatest friends and supporters of the people of Bosnia which, side by side, through its political and diplomatic efforts, continues that support in a practical way through humanitarian assistance. The Bosnian deputy prime minister added: At the moment our nation pays the price for adhering to religious principles and its love of freedom. Speaking about the latest situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina, he said: The Serbian aggressors are preparing themselves for another attack against the free areas in Bosnia so that by occupying more land in that area push our people to areas where there are no facilities for living. Continuing, the Bosnian deputy prime minister pointed out: Through reliance on God and through the efforts of the military forces, our honorable people, and the all- round political support by freedom loving states in the world we will thwart the enemies in achieving their ominous objectives. According to the report, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, praising the brave defense of the people of Bosnia-Herzegovina in the face of the aggressors, said: The patience, endurance, jihad, and the oppression of Bosnian Muslims will be registered in the history of that country as an honorable example and juncture. [passage omitted comparing the current situation in Bosnia with others in history] Hashemi-Rafsanjani also stressed the need for increasing international efforts by Islamic states in order to prevent the annihilation of the Bosnian nation.
