Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspsondent] [Text] Moscow January 5 TASS -- A joint meeting of the Organizational Center for Preparations of the First Meetings of the Russian Federal Assembly chambers and the Commission on Formation of State Duma Commissions was held on Tuesday in Kremlin's Oval Hall, the Russian Federal Assembly's press service reports. The meeting discussed aspects of formation of State Duma committees and commissions. The deputies concluded the State Duma shall form 17 committees to include: 1. The committee of constitutional legislation, legal reform and human rights; 2. The committee on public associations and religious organizations; 3. Committee on labor and social protection; 4. Committee on health care and ecology; 5. Committee on education, culture and science; 6. Committee on affairs of women, children and family; 7. Committee on budget and taxes; 8. Committee on economic reform, property and privatization; 9. Committee on banks, credits and money circulation; 10. Committee on agrarian issues; 11. Committee on industry, power engineering, transport and communications; 12. Committee on natural resources and ecology; 13. Committee on defence; 14. Committee on international affairs; 15. Committee on security; 16. Committee on affairs of the federation, national and regional policy, local government; 17. Committee on organization of work of the state duma. The work of the Commission on Formation of State Duma Commissions will continue on January 5.
