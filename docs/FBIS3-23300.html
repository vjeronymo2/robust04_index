Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A statement by the Ahmad Jibril's Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC] says that the PFLP-GC captured a pro-Israeli militiaman in southern Lebanon and turned him over to Hizballah in exchange for women and girls detained in Israeli and militia prisons. The statement adds that Francois Nazir 'Awn was captured in an ambush in al-'Ayshiyah, in which three militiamen and two civilians were killed. A spokesman for the militias confirmed that Francois 'Awn was captured or kidnapped.
