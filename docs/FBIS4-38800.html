BFN [Text] Aleksandr Vengerovskiy, deputy chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia [LP], on Tuesday hosted a delegation of North Korea's Labor Party arriving in Moscow at the LDP's invitation. The Korean delegation pointed to the LDP leadership's active position on restoring and maintaining good relations between the two countries, an LDP spokesman said in a statement on Wednesday. LDP representatives handed the visiting delegation a stellar of Corinthian marble to help strengthen the foundation of a monument to "the Chuchkhe Ideas" in Pyongyang, says the statement. The North Korean delegation is expected to visit Vladimir, Kovrov, and St. Petersburg to meet members of LDP local cells, visit defense-related plants, and go on a sight-seeing tour of St. Petersburg.
