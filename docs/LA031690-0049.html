March 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'melissasomething' Spinoff?: Melanie Mayron, who plays Melissa on "thirtysomething," may spin off onto her own half-hour show.

According to the Hollywood Reporter, the show would air next spring. Ed Zwick and Marshall Herskovitz, "thirtysomething" executive producers, as well as Mayron, would be involved in producing. Sources say the story line would probably have Melissa taking off for another big city and trying to make it as a photographer. BETH KLEID
