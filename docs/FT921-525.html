920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / Briefcase, Q&A: Power of attorney MY MOTHER wishes to give me power of attorney under the 1986 Enduring Powers of Attorney Act. Please would you let me know an address from which I can obtain the necessary form and of the court of protection, who I understand have issued an explanatory leaflet. The Court of Protection is at Stewart House, 24 Kingsway, London, WC2B 6HD (tel: 071-269-7000). Forms can be obtained from Law Stationers Company shops. No legal responsibility can be accepted by the Financial Times for the answers given in these columns. All inquiries will be answered by post as soon as possible.
