920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / International Company News: Imasco boosts earnings 60% IMASCO, the financial services, fast-food, tobacco and retailing group, boosted fourth-quarter net profits to CDollars 94.4m (USDollars 81.3m) or 74 cents a share, an advance of 60 per cent on the CDollars 59m, or 44 cents, recorded a year earlier. Revenues rose 2 per cent to CDollars 1.38bn, writes Robert Gibbens. The company, 40 per cent controlled by BAT Industries, the UK-based tobacco group,earned CDollars 331.6m or Dollars 2.56 for the year, up 12 per cent from CDollars 295.1m or Dollars 2.25 in 1990. Revenues rose 4 per cent to CDollars 5.4bn.
