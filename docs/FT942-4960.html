940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Allied London Props makes Pounds 11.3m buy Allied London Properties has bought two properties in Glasgow for Pounds 11.3m through a new subsidiary, Allied London & Scotland Properties, of which 20 per cent is held by Barrie Clapham, a Glasgow-based property developer and investor. The properties, a light industrial, office and storage development and an office, leisure and warehouse building, have a rent roll of Pounds 1.07m and have been valued at Pounds 11.3m.
