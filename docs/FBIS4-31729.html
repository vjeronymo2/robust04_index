BFN [Unattributed report: "Serbian Soldiers at Macedonian Point"] [Text] At yesterday's session, the members of the Kriva Palanka local assembly asked the Interior Ministry to inform them about the situation on the northeastern part of the Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]-Serb border. The session concentrated on the disturbing news that Serbian soldiers had taken over an observation point on the tip Sraza, 250 meters inside Macedonian territory. According to the unofficial data tabled at the session, 10-15 Serbian soldiers set up tents near the border villages Luka and Podrizikonj about a month ago, and took over the most important strategic points for observation of the Macedonian territory. The [words indistinct] noticed that trenches were being dug next to the tents, as well as movements of transport vehicles and material.
