August 25, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Integrated to Sell Some Resources: Integrated Resources Inc., New York, has reached a tentative agreement to sell part of its holdings for $310 million to the Whitehall Financial Group partnership of Canada and Great Britain. Whitehall would buy Integrated Resources Life Insurance Co., Integrated Resources Equity Corp., Integrated Resources Asset Management Corp., Resources Trust Co. and Financial Clearing and Services Corp.
