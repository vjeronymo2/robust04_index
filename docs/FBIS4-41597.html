BFN [Text] The CONTACT news agency has supplied us with information from the head of state's press secretariat. A group of journalists from the head of state's press secretariat is working in the conflict zone of Abkhazia. According to their information, the distribution of the CIS peace-keeping forces deployed at the Roki hippodrome to initial positions started during the morning of 26 June. Three convoys have already left for Jvari, Anaklia, and Rukhi. Engineers who are preparing border checkpoints together with signallers who are to establish a connection have joined the positions of the southern zone. Blue helmets have appeared for the first time today bearing the caption: "Peacekeeping forces." There are approximately 21 units of heavy weaponry that have been distributed to Analkia, Jvari, and Rukhi.
