Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The ad interim Prime Minister Robert Malval is unable to assemble his ministers. According to an informed source, the government is reportedly immobilized due to Mr. Malval's inability to assemble a majority of the government's members. At least six members of the team that resigned 15 December have reportedly refused to participate in the ministers council convened by Robert Malval. This situation involves the ministers who dissociated themselves from Mr. Malval following the leak of a letter addressed to the president of the Republic, Jean-Bertrand Aristide. The government is reportedly unable therefore to take measures to execute decisions related to certain day-to-day matters of state.
