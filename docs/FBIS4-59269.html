BFN [Text] The South African Government [as heard] canceled a scheduled meeting between President 'Ezer Weizman and Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' at the last moment. This was learned by our correspondent 'Imanu'el Halperin, who accompanied the president on his trip to South Africa. The meeting had been planned in advance, but at the last moment, the Syrians canceled it and apologized. President Weizman returned tonight from his visit to South Africa, where he attended the inauguration of President Nelson Mandela.
