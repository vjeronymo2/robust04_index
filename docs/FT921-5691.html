920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Securiguard board takes pay cut Directors at Securiguard, the security, cleaning and personal services group, have taken a pay cut. The report and accounts for the year to November 3 1991 show a reduction in the top management pay bill from pounds 679,000 to pounds 523,000. Remuneration of the chairman and highest paid director was cut from pounds 195,000 to pounds 162,000. There was also a pounds 287,000 payout for loss of office.
