BFN [Excerpt] Banja Luka, 5 Apr (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces have resumed their offensive activities against Serb lines in the zone of responsibility of the First Krajina Corps of the Srpska republic army today, the information service of the corps said in Banja Luka this evening. The strongest Muslim attacks have been registered at the Ozren battlefield, particularly at its southern part in the Vozuca area. Reinforced by fresh units and with artillery backing, Muslim forces attempted to break through the Serb lines at this part of the front. The Teslic battlefield was not peaceful today either because of fierce attacks by the Muslim army on almost all parts of the front in which 82-mm mortars, and antiaircraft and infantry weapons were used. The fiercest attacks were launched northeast of Teslic. Muslim attacks were resumed west of Doboj and 82-mm mortars were used. [passage omitted]
