BFN [Text] Piotrkow Trybunalski, April 26 -- The All-Poland Strike Committee of the brown coal mines agreed on Tuesday [26 April] to deliver coal in quantities sufficient to generate some 25 percent of the energy generated by individual power plants. The decision was announced following industry minister's declaration during talks with union leaders here that he is cancelling orders concerning the restructuring of the brown coal sector. Spokesman for Solidarity's national commission said that the decision on resumption of coal deliveries will be lifted at 2000 hrs on Thursday if the government side fails to adopt a time-table, approved by Solidarity, of dealing with the demands put forward by the union.
