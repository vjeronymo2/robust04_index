930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Civil servants face probe SENIOR civil servants are facing an inquiry by MPs that could have wide-ranging repercussions on their responsibilities. The cross-party Treasury and Civil Service committee has decided in principle to set up a subcommittee charged with examining the way senior civil servants' jobs are defined. The terms of reference of the inquiry - to be entitled The Future of the Higher Civil Service - are expected to be decided at the first meeting of the subcommittee, set for next week.
