Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bloemfontein Jan 18 SAPA -- An explosion in a building in Bothaville's main street on Monday night caused extensive damage to the African National Congress [ANC] office and adjacent shops, police reported. No one was injured. [Orange] Free State liaison officer Maj Johlene van der Merwe told SAPA on Tuesday the blast occurred at about 10PM and was believed to have been caused by commercial explosives attached to the front of the building on the ground floor in President Street. Police counted 69 windows shattered in the offices and adjacent chemist and fashion shop. No estimate of the damage was available. Maj van der Merwe said police were investigating and believed the explosion was related to a similar blast at the ANC offices in Harrismith on Saturday.
