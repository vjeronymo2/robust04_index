December 6, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Boris Becker and Steffi Graf were selected as world champions by the International Tennis Federation, which followed the rankings in the women's award but gave extra weight to major-event victories in the men's.

Becker, rated second in the world on computer points, was chosen for the men's title over No. 1-ranked Ivan Lendl, whom he beat in both of their meetings this year.
