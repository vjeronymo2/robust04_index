Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 16 Mar (MENA)--It was learned here today that the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], one of the ruling coalition parties in Yemen, used tanks and artillery in the Shabwah Governorate, one of the southern governorates, to surround the governorate headquarters and the Security Directorate headquarters to force Governor Dirham 'Abduh Nu'man, member of the General People's Congress General Committee, and Colonel Ahmad 'Ali Muhsin, the governorate security director and leading member of the General People's Congress in the governorate, to leave the governorate immediately. The governor and the security director received from the military commander, who belongs to the YSP led by 'Ali Salim al-Bid, and the YSP secretary in the governorate an urgent ultimatum to immediately leave their workplace in the governorate or they would be driven out by military force. It is worth noting that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih is the General People's Congress secretary general in Yemen.
