920427 FT 27 APR 92 / UK Company News: Downiebrae declines Pre-tax profits at Downiebrae Holdings fell from Pounds 634,000 to Pounds 423,000 in 1991, and the dividend is halved to 0.5p per share. Turnover improved to Pounds 1.56m (Pounds 1.5m) but trading profit slumped to Pounds 5,700 (Pounds 53,000) as margins came under pressure. Pipe flange manufacturing lost Pounds 9,000 (profit Pounds 3,000) and profile cutting made profits of Pounds 15,000 (Pounds 50,000). Net rental income was static at Pounds 54,500, interest on cash deposits fell to Pounds 85,000 (Pounds 124,000) and investment income to Pounds 393,000 (Pounds 499,000). Earnings per share worked through at 1.93p (3.19p).
