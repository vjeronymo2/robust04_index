October 14, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

NO. 1 GOLDEN WEST ROLLS IN WATER POLO

Edvard Van Linjin scored five goals to lead top-ranked Golden West College to an 18-6 victory over Saddleback in a nonconference water polo game Saturday at Saddleback.

Golden West improved to 15-2 with its only losses to No. 2 Cuesta.

Ron Johnson had four goals for Saddleback (6-13).
