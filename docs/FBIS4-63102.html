BFN [Text] Moscow May 6 RIA -- Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev has said the Asia and Pacific region needs its own security system. Speaking at a press conference Friday he said the region where strategic interests of Russia, Japan, China, and the United States are closely intertwined lacks a mechanism to dovetail them. He said Russia saw as its long-term strategic goal the formation of a collective security system involving all states situated in the region, and voiced confidence this initiative would be backed up by all states interested in maintaining peace and stability in the region.
