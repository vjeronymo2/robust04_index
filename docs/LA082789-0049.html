August 27, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MICHAEL'S MESSAGE

James Kiel, please do some research before you go shooting your mouth off about the alleged meaning of someone's song (Letters, Aug. 13). If you would really listen to George Michael's songs, you might realize he is trying to promote monogamous relationships, not portray "explicit sexual imagery" (that borders) on the pornographic."

JODY E. KNIGHT

Los Angeles
