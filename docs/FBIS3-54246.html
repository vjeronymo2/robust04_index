Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] There was a stabbing attack at the Rose Garden Junction in Rishon Leziyyon this morning. A man, aged about 60, was stabbed to death by an Arab, who managed to escape. Our correspondent 'Ofer Vardi gives us more details: The incident took place a short time ago. The man who was stabbed, aged about 60, was working in his orange grove near the junction when an Arab, apparently a laborer working in the area, suddenly attacked him with a knife. The man died a few minutes later. The assailant fled, and large police forces are conducting a manhunt for him.
