BFN [Report by Zlata Petral from Mostar] [Text] Having visited Konjic and Jablanica, Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina, arrived in Mostar. Upon arrival, President Izetbegovic was received by Mostar Mayor Safet (Dorucevic) and members of the commune's Presidency. As I report, talks in the Presidency of the Mostar Commune are under way. After a brief [word indistinct], Alija Izetbegovic, president of Bosnia-Herzegovina, will visit Muslim cemeteries [sehitluk], where Mostar's heroes have been buried. President Izetbegovic's visit to Mostar is really extensive and we shall be covering everything in our newscasts.
