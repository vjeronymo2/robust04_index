BFN [Text] Khartoum, 25 May (SUNA) -- The Armed Forces today recaptured the town of (Pageri) in Western Equatoria State, along the Sudanese-Ugandan border. A statement issued by the Armed Forces' general command said that the Sudanese Armed Forces routed the forces of John Garang, recaptured the town [word indistinct], which is the most important base for the Garang forces of the rebellion [word indistinct].
