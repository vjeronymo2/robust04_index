Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin has signed an edict on some issues relating to the activity of the Russian tripartite commission for the settlement of social and labor relations. The document has been adopted for the purpose of providing, within the framework of that commission, for regular agreement with the position of the president, for uniting the employers, and bringing together the trades unions in the sphere of socio-economic policy. Yuriy Yarov has been appointed the coordinator of the commission. Boris Yeltsin also issued an edict on measures for providing for reforms in local government. It is established that bodies of state power of the subjects of the Russian Federation can set the date of elections to the representative bodies of local government in autumn this year.
