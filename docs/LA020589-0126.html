February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

'LETTER' TO EX-PRESIDENT PRAISED FOR ITS HONESTY

Thank you, thank you, thank you for having the courage to speak out. The groveling and genuflecting to the Reagans is an anathema, disheartening and disgusting to me.

It is wonderful to find a person gutsy enough and not afraid to speak out and tell it like it is.

CYNTHIA CRISS

Los Angeles
