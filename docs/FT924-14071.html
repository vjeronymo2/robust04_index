921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / International Company News: Hafnia assets value continues slide HAFNIA Holding of 1992, the new holding company for Danish insurer Hafnia which went into payment suspension in August, continues to bleed, Hilary Barnes reports from Copenhagen. The value of assets it took over from the original Hafnia, including stakes in Baltica, the Danish insurer, and Skandia, of Sweden, have fallen to DKr3.2bn (Dollars 559m) from DKr5.9bn when it was set up.
