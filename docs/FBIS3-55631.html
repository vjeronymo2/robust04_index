Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the latest information obtained from the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army Press Service, the Azerbaijani side has been carrying out a large-scale offensive all along the war front since 0700 this morning. The enemy directed its main thrust in the direction of Matagis and Hakop Kamari [Lulasaz] in Mardakertskiy Rayon in northern Karabakh, Agdam-Askeran in the east, and Fizuli-Gadrut in the south. In the afternoon, the Armenian forces managed to halt the offensive and then regrouped and launched a counterattack. The Karabakh Defense Army units managed to encircle and destroyed a large enemy Army unit made up of two battalions, that is, 300 soldiers, which was moving in the direction of Matagis and Hagop-Kamari. (Two) were killed and 15 injured on the Armenian side. Again, following fierce battles in the direction of Agdam-Askeran, in the afternoon the Karabakh formations managed to halt the Azerbaijani advances and completely destroy an enemy battalion, nearly 200 soldiers. An important post was ceded to the enemy in Martuninskiy Rayon in the morning. However, it was retaken in the afternoon. Here, Azerbaijanis lost 30 soldiers. There are injured on the Armenian side and their numbers are being determined. The Karabakh Defense Army units' operations were more successful in the southern war front in the direction of Fizuli-Gadrut. There, they halted the enemy's attack and then launched a counteroffensive and penetrated 12 km into enemy territory. According to preliminary reports, both sides have losses in this sector, and casualty figures are being determined. The same source reports that the Azerbaijani side used the Air Force today. The aircraft violated NKR's airspace but did not take part in the combat operations, apparently their purpose was reconnaissance. Battles were still continuing all along the Karabakh fronts as of 1830 today.
