March 16, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

HUNTINGTON BEACH;

SCHOOL DISTRICT TRUSTEES APPOINT SUPERINTENDENT

Trustees of the Oceanview School District appointed Monte McMurray, assistant superintendent of business services, as superintendent to replace Dale Coogan, who died last week.

McMurray, 53, has been with the district since 1971 as principal and assistant superintendent of educational services as well as business services.
