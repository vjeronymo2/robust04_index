921024 FT 24 OCT 92 / Beijing's Gatt hopes rise CHINA'S attempt to rejoin the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (Gatt) took a step forward yesterday with the decision of a working party considering Chinese membership to start negotiations on entry terms. The US and the European Community had wanted more information on China's present and future trade policy, but yesterday they agreed to seek this information in parallel with the membership negotiations.
