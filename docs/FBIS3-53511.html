Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [passage omitted] At 1700 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received at his place of residence Otto Stich, president of the Swiss Confederation, the Swiss foreign minister, the governor of Geneva, and the Swiss ambassador in Damascus. Dr. 'Abd-al-Ra'uf al-Kasm, head of the National Security Office, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the meeting. President Stich welcomed His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad by saying that he is happy and Switzerland is proud to receive his excellency the president for the third time. He said he greatly values the efforts his excellency the president has exerted to establish peace in the Middle East. President al-Asad thanked President Stich for the warm welcome he was accorded and emphasized Syria's work for a just and comprehensive peace in the region. He said that he valued Switzerland's role in working for peace. At 1900 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali. Dr. 'Abd-al-Ra'uf al-Kasm, head of the National Security Office, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the meeting. The two sides reviewed several international issues and the Middle East peace process.
