September 28, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Search Warrants Served on Sun Hing Bullion: Investigators served search warrants on four California offices of Sun Hing Bullion Ltd., whose investors have allegedly lost hundreds of thousands of dollars. The warrants were served in Monterey Park, Alhambra, San Francisco and Millbrae in Northern California. The district attorneys' offices in Los Angeles, San Francisco and San Mateo counties are investigating Sun Hing, allegedly for deferred delivery of gold and foreign currencies.
