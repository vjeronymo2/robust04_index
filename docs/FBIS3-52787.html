Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [passage omitted] In an interview with our reporter on the return of the Afghan refugees or their settlement in Iran, Interior Minister Mr. Besharati-Jahromi said: At present some 700,000 Afghan refugees live in our country. On the basis of a project prepared by the UNHCR a number of Afghan immigrants voluntarily returned home, before the recent civil war and fratricide in Afghanistan. As of next Nowruz [21 March 1994], the said project will be followed up; however, if there are some who desire to stay in Iran, then we will issue cards for them. [passage omitted] The interior minister said Iraqi refugees in Iran numbered around 50,000.
