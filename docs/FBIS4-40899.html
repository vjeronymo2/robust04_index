BFN [Unattributed report: "Wages for South Urals Defense Workers"] [Text] Some 230 billion rubles have been transferred for the express purposes of extinguishing indebtedness in respect of wages due to workers at defense enterprises in the South Urals. This was announced by Deputy Finance Minister Andrey Astakhov at a joint sitting of the government commission, the Chelyabinsk Oblast leadership, and defense sector enterprise directors. Thus, the first step toward remedying the critical situation prevailing here has been taken. The arrival of an authoritative Russian Government commission at one of the country's leading industrial regions and its tour of a number of major enterprises and the Defense Complex Design Bureau was seen by Igor Velichko, general designer at the State Missile Center, as a show of genuine interest on the part of the leadership of ministries and departments in ensuring the stabilization of the work of the military-industrial complex.
