930421 FT 21 APR 93 / Fighting goes on in Central Bosnia Gunmen have killed families, gang-raped women and forced civilians from their homes under threat of death during savage Moslem-Croat fighting in central Bosnia, UN sources told Reuter in Sarajevo yesterday. They said UN relief workers had found bodies of execution victims - unidentified gunmen were reported to have shot entire families in the head at point blank range -and seen homes looted in the Vitez, Zenica and Travnik areas, where battles erupted last week between the nominal allies in Bosnia's civil war. Heavy fighting continued yesterday, but Bosnian radio reported the two sides had agreed to order a ceasefire.
