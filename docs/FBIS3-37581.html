Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (February 16) TURAN--Azerbaijan considers that mechanism of the CIS Treaty on Collective Security must be implemented not only in case of foreign aggression against the CIS-member state, but in case of the aggression of one CIS-member state against the other member, Azeri Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov said on February 15 during the meeting with the Georgian Foreign Minister Aleksandr Chikvaidze. As Gasan Gasanov said "in this context the Treaty on Collective Security of the CIS-member states need serious revision." The both ministers shared the opinion that domestic conflicts in the CIS seriously threat the perspectives of the Commonwealth of Independent States.
