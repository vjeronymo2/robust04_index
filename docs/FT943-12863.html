940722 FT 22 JUL 94 / Observer: Shuffle off At last, good news for the UK's Tory government. The annual report from the Invest in Britain Bureau - part of the department of trade and industry - yesterday showed a record number of new jobs created by inward investment last year. How satisfying for Tim Sainsbury who, as junior minister at the DTI wrote the report's foreword. Only one hiccup. Sainsbury was a victim of John Major's government reshuffle - and thus absent from the presentation.
