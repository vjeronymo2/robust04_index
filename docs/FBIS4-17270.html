BFN [Text] Yerevan, 20 April (SNARK) -- Two meetings of the Armenian and Nakhichevan representatives took place yesterday at the border of both republics. The press-center of the Armenian Defence Ministry told SNARK today that representatives of the local authorities and military leadership of nearby regions discussed measures on preventing the possible incidents, issues of communication reconstruction and safety of agricultural works in the border region. The authorities of Ordubadskiy region in Nakhichevan -- obeying to Azerbaijan People's Front -- didn't come to the meeting and all talks were held only with military commanders of the region. Meanwhile, the Armenian Defence Ministry informed there was calmness in all parts of the Armenian-Azerbaijan border last day.
