931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Nafta vote pushes Dow lower Wall Street stocks turned lower in heavy trading yesterday leading up to the crucial vote on the North American Free Trade Agreement. The Dow Jones Industrial Average was off 6.42 points from Tuesday's record high to end at 3,704.35. Share prices opened higher in Tokyo today and the Nikkei average had risen 146.82 points, just over 0.8 per cent, by lunchtime to 18,255.53. Endorsements for Nafta, Page 4 World stocks, Page 47
