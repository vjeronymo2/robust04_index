On March 10, 1977, and April 13, 1987, The Depository Trust Company (``DTC'') filed proposed rule changes (File Nos. SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;77&hyph;03 and SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;87&hyph;07) and on March 1, 1977, the Midwest Securities Trust Company (``MSTC'') filed proposed rule change (File No. MSTC&hyph;77&hyph;01) with the Securities and Exchange Commission (``Commission'') under section 19(b)(1) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. 1 File numbers SR&hyph;MSTC&hyph;77&hyph;01 and SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;77&hyph;03 proposed to establish agreements whereby depositories would not charge each other for depository interface services and to establish participant fees for interdepository interface services. File number SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;87&hyph;07 proposed changes to the interdepository interface participant fees. The Commission published notice of file numbers SR&hyph;MSTC&hyph;77&hyph;01, SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;77&hyph;03, and SR&hyph;DTC&hyph;87&hyph;07, in the Federal Register on March 18, 1977, 2 March 24, 1977, 3 and May 20, 1987, 4 respectively.
