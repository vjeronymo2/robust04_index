940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Laird Plastics makes Dollars 1.4m acquisition Laird Plastics, a US subsidiary of Laird Group, maker of car components and industrial products, has acquired certain assets of Ain Plastics, based in greater San Francisco and Los Angeles, California, for Dollars 1.4m (Pounds 950,000). Laird Plastics distributes semi-finished sheet, rod and tube products. The business acquired, had sales of Dollars 7m in 1993.
