930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Karachi palm oil plan opposed MALAYSIAN and Pakistani palm oil processors are opposing a plan to build a USDollars 40m palm oil storage and processing complex near Karachi. The complex, to be funded by the Westbury consortium of Pakistan and the Malaysian government and private concerns, aims to sell refined oil to the Gulf states and central Asia. Malaysia's processors already have excess refining capacity, while Pakistan's are fearful for the country's small-scale processing industry.
