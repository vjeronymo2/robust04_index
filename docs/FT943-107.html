940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / International Company News: Namibian Minerals listed in Windhoek The Namibian Minerals Corporation, already listed on the Vancouver stock exchange, listed on the Windhoek stock exchange yesterday, becoming the first to list in southern Africa and North America. Namco is focused on the development of offshore diamond resources in Namibia and has acquired concessions off the Namibian and South African coasts. Namco intends to use advanced remote vehicle technology to exploit the high grade diamond-bearing sands, and hopes to be producing up to 3 per cent of global gem diamond output by 1998. The company's listing is being effected via a R30m (Dollars 8.45m) share placing through South African brokers Simpson McKie. Its market capitalisation in Canada is CDollars 115m (USDollars 85.64m).
