August 5, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ASTRO PITCHER GETS OFF SCOTT FREE IN EYES OF HERSHISER, DODGERS

What does The Times have against bike racing?

There has been minor coverage of a few local events, some wire-service stories on international races and attention paid to Greg LeMond's Tour de France victory. But during this period The Times has carried three in-depth cycling-related stories: on Barbara Buchan's lawsuit against the U.S. Cycling Federation; on Cindy Olavarri's problems with steroids, and Randy Harvey's follow-up piece on Greg LeMond -- featuring the rumor that his last pro team sponsor wanted him to take a banned drug.

That is too much scandal for the small substance The Times prints about bike racing.

HOWARD DOERFLING

Glendale
