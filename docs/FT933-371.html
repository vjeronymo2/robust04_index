930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Europa board says 'take no action' Europa Minerals, the UK mining finance house which is in merger talks with Burmine, the Australian gold mining concern in which it has a 38.5 per cent stake, has advised shareholders to take no action in relation to a bid for Burmine by Mount Edon Gold Mines (Aust). Under the offer, Burmine shareholders will be offered ADollars 2.30 (98p) cash plus one 1995 Mount Edon option - exercisable at ADollars 1.50 per Mount Edon share - for each Burmine share acquired. The Europa board has indicated that, at this stage, it intends to proceed with its proposed three-way merger with Burmine and Austmin.
