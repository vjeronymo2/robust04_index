940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / International Company News: MIM sells stake in Nunachiaq MIM Holdings, the Queensland-based metals group, has sold its half share in Nunachiaq, a private investment company, to Canada's Teck Corporation, for CDollars 139.7m (USDollars 103.4m), writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. Nunachiaq's main assets comprise a 27.7 per cent interest in Cominco, the North American mining and smelting group, and a 22.46 per cent interest in the Polaris zinc-lead mine joint venture.
