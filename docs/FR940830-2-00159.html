DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Office of the Secretary [Order 94&hyph;8&hyph;44; Docket 49504] Application of Exec Express II, Inc.; d/b/a/ Lone Star Airlines for Certification Authority AGENCY: Department of Transportation. ACTION: Notice of Order to Show Cause.

SUMMARY: The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue an order finding Exec Express II, Inc. d/b/a/ Lone Star Airlines fit, willing, and able and award it a certificate of public convenience and necessity to engage in interstate scheduled air transportation of persons, property, and mail. DATES: Persons wishing to file objections should do so no later than September 6, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Objections and answers to objections should be filed in Docket 49504 and addressed to the Documentary Services Division (C&hyph;55, Room 4107), U.S. Department of Transportation, 400 Seventh Street, S.W., Washington, D.C. 20590 and should be served upon the parties listed in Attachment A to the order.
FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Delores King, Analyst, Air Carrier Fitness Division, X&hyph;56, Room 6401, U.S. Department of Transportation, 400 Seventh Street, S.W., Washington, D.C. 20590, (202) 366&hyph;2343.
