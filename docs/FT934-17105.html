931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and Vegetables English and French apples are this week's best buy, reports FFVIB. English Cox's Orange Pippins are 30-55p a lb (35-55p) and French Royal Gala are 35-55p a lb (30-55p). Another good buy is Italian black seeded grapes at 45p-Pounds 2.00 a lb (60p-Pounds 1.50). English Brussels sprouts are the best vegeatble buy at 20-45p a lb (20-60p). English and Dutch leeks are 40-65p a lb (40-65p). Corn-on-the-cob is in peak season, available at 15-30p a cob (20-35p). There is a tremendous variety of tomatoes available, making them the best salad buy. The Spices report was unavailable.
