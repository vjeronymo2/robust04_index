July 29, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

ERROR ON ART

In David Nelson's review of La Jolla Seas restaurant ("A Downscale House in an Upscale Neighborhood, La Jolla Seas Takes Middle Road," July 13) an error was made in the description of the artwork.

Rather than being lithographs, the art, commissioned by Art Consultants-West and painted by La Jolla artist Mirto Golino, is original. Each piece was created specifically for the La Jolla Seas project. Windansea Beach and La Jolla Cove are two local sites depicted in Mirto's original works on paper.

VICTORIA TEAGUE

San Diego
