Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The United Democratic Party [UDP] has condemned the pelting of the presidential motorcade by the people of Western Province. UDP interim national chairman Mr. (Kapemba Phiri) said it was wrong for people in Mongu to behave in such a manner. He said the president should be respected regardless of which party he belonged to, and urged all leaders to discipline their members. The motorcade was pelted with stones and mangoes when President Chiluba went there to campaign for the Mongu Central by-election.
