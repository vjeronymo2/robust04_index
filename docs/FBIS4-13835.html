BFN [Text] Semnan, April 22, IRNA -- Defence Minister Mohammad Foruzandeh said here last night that the Armed Forces of Iran are ready to offer support for the Muslim Bosnians. He referred to the recent speech of the commander in chief of the Armed Forces of the Islamic Repubic of Iran and said the Armed Forces are ready to offer every type of assistance to Bosnian Muslims and defend them against the Serbs. He said the Islamic Republic now has a grave responsibility for building a new civilization which needs sacrifice and material resources, human resources, and thought. The meeting was attended by commanders of the armed forces in Semnan (300 kms east of Tehran).
