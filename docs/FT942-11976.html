940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust Euro Undiluted net asset value per share of Abtrust European Index Investment Trust, excluding revenue reserves, rose from 77.5p to 91.2p over the year ended February 28 1994. Earnings were 0.7p (0.53p) per share from an available amount of Pounds 238,836 (Pounds 182,040), while the final, single dividend is increased to 0.8p (0.3p).
