Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa held a one-hour meeting with Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres tonight in an attempt to remove obstacles delaying the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho accord. After the meeting, Musa, Peres, and the Israeli delegation members left for al-Andalus Palace to meet Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat. Musa and Peres refused to give any statements, but Israeli Environment Minister Yosi Sarid said: We are continuing the negotiations and I believe this will go on throughout the night. He noted that the talks will take a long time.
