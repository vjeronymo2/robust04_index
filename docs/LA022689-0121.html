February 26, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SDG&E MANEUVER

As a taxpaying individual, I was very cool to the idea of a taxpayer takeover of San Diego Gas & Electric. Now that SDG&E is trying to prevent us from finding out the facts related to this issue, I can see I was wrong.

They, obviously, have something to hide or they wouldn't prevent the Water Authority from spending a relatively small amount of money.

Bad move, SDG&E, you lost my vote.

V.R. EWALD

San Diego
