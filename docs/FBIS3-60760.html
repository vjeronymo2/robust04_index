Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Port Elizabeth Dec 29 SAPA -- A bomb explosion caused minor damage to a block of flats in Port Elizabeth's Swartkops industrial area on Tuesday night, but no casualties were reported. SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news on Wednesday reported that shortly after the blast police found large amounts of explosives in several places at a block of flats in Algoa Park, about 15km from Swartkops.
