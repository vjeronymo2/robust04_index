BFN [Text] Baghdad, 20 Jun (INA) -- 'Amir Muhammad Rashid, director of the Military Industrialization Organization [MIO], today met with a delegation of French businessmen representing 28 French firms dealing with various industrial, oil, transport, communications, and health fields. During the meeting, they reviewed Iraqi-French relations with a view to activating civil industries. The French firms showed readiness to seriously contribute to enhancing bilateral relations and to effectively participate in various developmental projects and technical industries, including the pharmaceutical and food industries, as well as the manufacture of cars and agricultural equipment and machines, and petrochemical industries, in addition to the iron, steel, and aluminum industries and other industries in a way that will enhance the friendly relations and bolster the common interests of both Iraq and the French firms. The delegation's visit is part of a French move under the current circumstances designed to bolster these firms' contributions to the Iraqi civil industries. The under secretary of the Ministry of Industry and Minerals attended the meeting.
