930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: New Frontiers net asset value improves New Frontiers Development Trust, the emerging markets trust managed by Ivory & Sime, had a net asset value of 84.1p per share at March 31 against 61.6p at the September year-end and 73.5p a year earlier. Fully diluted, the value at end-March was 83.7p. Net revenue for the six months improved to Pounds 395,000 (Pounds 309,000), equivalent to earnings per share of 0.39p (0.31p).
