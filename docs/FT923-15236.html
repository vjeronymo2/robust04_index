920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Hong Kong talks to start today TALKS on the future of Hong Kong airport are to take place in Beijing starting today, the government announced yesterday. Downing Street said that Sir John Coles, deputy under-secretary at the Foreign Office had been appointed Mr John Major's personal emissary for the negotiations. He will lead the British team, which includes representatives of the Hong Kong government.
