Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Islamic Resistance announced that an explosive charge was detonated as an occupation patrol passed along the Shama' road in the security strip a few minutes after 0900 today. The occupation forces shelled Wadi al-Nafkhah and (Wadi Hinn) from their position in Tayr Harfa. The shelling continued intermittently until 1030. The Resistance also claimed the detonation of another charge at 1230 today as a patrol of the militia [South Lebanese Army, SLA] passed along the Shama'-Tayr Harfa road. The blast destroyed a red Mercedes 280 in which the militia's official in Tayr Harfa was riding. [passage omitted on military operations]
