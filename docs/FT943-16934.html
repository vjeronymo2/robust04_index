940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: I&S Optimum Tst I&S Optimum Income Trust reported attributable revenue ahead from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 2.2m during the year to May 31. Earnings per share rose to 7.61p (7.25p) and a fourth interim dividend of 1.95p brings the total for the year to 7.6p (7.4p). After adjusting for the entitlement of zero dividend preference shares, net assets per ordinary share dipped 5 per cent, from 95.02p to 90.08p.
