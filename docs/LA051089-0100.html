May 10, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

2 BRITISH NAVY FLIERS KILLED IN PLANE CRASH

Two British navy airmen were killed Monday when their aircraft crashed into a harbor during a demonstration display, a Defense Ministry spokesman said Tuesday.

He said the Jetstream trainer aircraft failed to pull out of a dive and crashed into Portland harbor, near the southern English town of Weymouth, in full view of 500 naval cadets. The airmen's bodies were recovered by divers.
