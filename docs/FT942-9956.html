940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / Bank Leumi ex-chief 'guilty' Former Bank Leumi chairman Ernst Japhet was found guilty of fraud yesterday, the 10th executive convicted for a 1983 scandal that cost the government more than Dollars 9bn, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. The Jerusalem district court sentenced the nine others to up to eight months in jail last month. Japhet, 72, was tried separately, having only returned to Israel in January after a long, self-imposed exile to New York. His sentence is due to be imposed next week.
