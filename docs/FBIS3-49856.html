Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Geneva, 19 Jan (TANJUG) -- Talks between the delegations of the FRY and Croatia ended in Geneva this morning with an agreement on the normalization of mutual relations. It has been learned unofficially that during the day a joint declaration on this will be signed and made public in the Palais des Nations. It has been learned from well-informed sources in Geneva that the two delegations agreed that representative offices of Croatia and the FRY will be opened in Belgrade and Zagreb. It has also been learned that the representative offices will be opened before mutual recognition is achieved. As for the normalization of relations, apart from the opening of the representative offices, the opening of the Belgrade-Zagreb highway and telephone and telex links is also envisioned.
