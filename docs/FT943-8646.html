940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Celtic Gold CELTIC GOLD plans to enter into a call option agreement with a group of Coyle Hamilton shareholders, under which Celtic will have the right to buy 293,637 ordinary shares in Coyle (42 per cent) at IPounds 15 (Pounds 14.85) per share at any time up to January 31 1995.
