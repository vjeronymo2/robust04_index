BFN [Text] Paris, March 31 (AFP) -- The number of people unemployed in France rose by 0.2 percent to a total of 3,312,000 official seasonally-adjusted figures showed on Thursday [31 March]. In each of the three previous months the number of people unemployed had risen by 0.1 percent. In September the number had increased by 0.8 percent and in October by 1.2 percent. The figures for February, which were published by the Labour Ministry, showed that the number of people unemployed continued to increase beyond the point of three million which was reached in January when it rose to 3,307,100. The rate of unemployment, on the basis of a calculation used by the International Labour Organisation, remained steady at 12.2 percent of the workforce. Figures published by the official French INSEE statistical institute, which were also published on Thursday and which are based on an ILO calculation, showed that 3,061,000 people were unemployed in February compared with 3,055,000 in January and 2,787,000 in February 1993, representing an increase of 274,000 in 12 months.
