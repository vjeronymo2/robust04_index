Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed HC; SH: "Breaking Windows at Iranian Embassy in Bern; Mujahideen Deny Being Implicated"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Text] Bern, 25 Jan (ATSA) -- The mojahidin of the people of Iran have rejected any implication in the attack on the Iranian Embassy in Bern on Monday [24 January]. In a communique, the Bureau of the Mojahidin in Paris denies the "story fabricated" by IRNA, the official Iranian press agency. The spokesperson at the Public Ministry of the Confederation said the Iranian Embassy in Bern has three months to file a complaint. Municipal police arrested two Iranian nationals on Monday for throwing stones at and breaking four windows of the [embassy] building. They were released early in the evening. According to the mojahidin, the Iranian regime hopes to impede the extradition of the murderers of Professor Kazem Radjavi by tying this action to their movement.
