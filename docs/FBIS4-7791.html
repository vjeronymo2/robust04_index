BFN [Text] In his letter to State Chamber Chairman Herman Rigelnik, Prime Minister Dr. Janez Drnovsek proposed that the State Chamber discuss relieving Jozica Puhar, minister of labor, family and social welfare of her duty and appointing Rena Klinar to that duty at its April session. In his letter to Herman Rigelnik, Prime Minister Drnovsek also said that he has no intention of withdrawing his proposal to replace Minister Puhar.
