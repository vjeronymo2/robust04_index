BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered the allocation of 8 million dinars for poor families in the governorates of al-Najaf, al-Muthanna, and al-Ramadi due to the approach of the blessed 'Id al-Adha. The comrade secretaries of al-Najaf, al-Muthanna, al-Anbar, and al-Jazirah branches of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party said leader President Saddam Husayn's generous contribution confirms his excellency's interest in alleviating the suffering of his people. This suffering is a result of the continued blockade imposed on the people of Iraq and seeks to infringe on their steadfastness and free will.
