941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Ocean Wilsons Pre-tax profits at Ocean Wilsons Holdings fell 26 per cent from Pounds 3.09m to Pounds 2.28m in the six months to June 30. Turnover at the investment holding and shipping company rose from Pounds 46.4m to Pounds 58.1m, an increase of 25 per cent. Earnings per share were 4.32p (4p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 1p.
