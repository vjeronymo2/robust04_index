941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Arts (Openings): Amsterdam An unusual exhibition of late medieval art opens at the Rijksmuseum on Saturday. Entitled 'The Art of Devotion 1300-1500', it brings together 50 paintings, miniatures, goldsmiths' work and prints. Among the artists are Mantegna and Memling. The objects - small in size and made with precious materials - represent some of the most beautiful works of art of the period.
