930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / International Company News: Poor reception for Shanghai Gaoqiao offer CHINA'S first public offer of Class 'B' shares in Hong Kong has been undersubscribed, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. Sources close to Shanghai Outer Gaoqiao Free Trade Zone Development, a property developer within China's first free trade zone in Pudong, Shanghai, said the company had received bids for just over 70 per cent of its HK125m (USDollars 16.1m) public share offer. The offer for foreigners closed on Monday in Hong Kong. The 'B' shares are to start trading on the Shanghai Securities Exchange on July 26. The 'A' shares for Chinese domestic investors were listed in May.
