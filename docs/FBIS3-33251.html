Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "National" newscast] [Text] Guerrilla activities have caused special security council meetings to be held today in several cities throughout the country. Government Minister Fabio Villegas traveled to Santander to preside over a Ministers Council meeting where electoral guarantees for Boyaca, Santander and Norte de Santander Departments would be discussed. In Bucaramanga, the Democracy Plan has been presented in the presence of the government minister, the commander of the military forces, governors, and police commanders from the area of the Army's 2d Division. Alvaro Salgar, governor of Santander del Norte Department, said the following: [Begin Salgar recording] We hope to support election day. We hope to have an airplane available to transport units to the many voting areas and also a couple helicopters for any emergencies. [end recording] Although the subversives have announced a plan to sabotage the elections, state forces have guaranteed that they will be at all the voting centers. Members of the National Civil Registry Office are also participating in the meeting. They will establish which voting tables will have the most dangers, which will allow them to provide the required security for the upcoming elections. The Democracy Plan will also be presented this afternoon in Yopal, Casanare.
