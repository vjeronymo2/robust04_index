May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

A baseball doubleheader between the University of Nevada Reno and the University of San Diego scheduled for this Saturday has been canceled in the aftermath of a bench-clearing brawl that occurred last Friday between the teams.

San Diego Athletic Director Tom Iannacone made his decision to forfeit the games after his coach, John Cunningham, said he would not field a team under any circumstances.
