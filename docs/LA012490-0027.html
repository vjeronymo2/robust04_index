January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

They Got the Beat -- Again: The Go-Go's are reuniting, at least for one performance. The five members of the Los Angeles all-female rock band are holding a press conference (along with Jane Fonda) Thursday at the Bel Age Hotel in West Hollywood to announce that they'll play March 28 at the Universal Amphitheatre, headlining a benefit concert for the California Environmental Protection Initiative.

No other Go-Go's projects are planned at this time. The group, which in 1982 hit No. 1 with the debut "Beauty and the Beat" album and No. 2 with the song "We Got the Beat," broke up in 1985. All five have pursued other musical endeavors, though only lead singer Belinda Carlisle has had consistent chart success. Claudia Puig
