Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The periphery of Kafr Rumman came under artillery bombardment from Tallat al-Suwayda' this morning. Several shells fell on Mlita, Jarju', and al-Luwayzah. Bursts of machine-gun fire were directed against the Iqlim al-Tuffah hills from the Sujud position. Later in the morning the Israelis shelled al-Nabatiyah al-Fawqa and Kafr Tibnit. The resistance fired four rockets against the Israeli position in al-Tuhrah this morning. The al-Suwayda' position was also attacked.
