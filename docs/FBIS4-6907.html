BFN [Text] A China party official said China attaches great importance to its traditional friendship with Vietnam. At a reception given to a Vietnamese party delegation led by Secretary of the party Central Committee Mr. Hong Ha in Beijing on Friday, Mr. Qiao Shi said China also attaches importance to the improvement of neighborliness and cooperation between the two countries. He stressed that, in the present world situation, what is important to the two countries is to maintain contact and regular exchanges of views.
