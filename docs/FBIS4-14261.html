BFN [Text] The security forces, in an operation code named Jayamaga against the LTTE [Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam], began their task to extend the Army's area of control by (?acquiring) 75 square km in the Vavuniya District. A military spokesman told LANKAPUVATH that the troops -- directed from (Aptran) by Army Commander Lieutenant General Gerry De Silva -- had already extended their forward defense localities by 35 square km last afternoon. They also liberated 500 farmer families from the LTTE control in Vavuniya's green rice belt. The operation has been launched with a view to rescuing farmer families from the LTTE. The Army commander had toured the new forward defense locality. He also held a series of meetings with the farming families. Arrangements have been made to immediately supply emergency stocks of logistics. In another incident in the Eastern Province, troops of Operation (?Fighting) located and destroyed three terrorist bases and 24 huts in which the LTTE cadres were accommodated. They also recovered 43 bags of rice, stocks of medicines, and antipersonnel mines. A report from Amparai says that STF [Special Task Force] commanders have destroyed two LTTE hideouts at Kanchikudichiyaru and located an abandoned terrorist camp and destroyed it.
