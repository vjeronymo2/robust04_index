February 17, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION BASKETBALL PLAYOFFS

North Hollywood 61, Palisades 34 -- Jacinda Sweet had 24 points, 10 rebounds and seven steals for visiting North Hollywood (13-7) in a 4-A Division game to give seventh-year Coach Rich Allen his 100th win.

North Hollywood raced to a 10-2 advantage in the first quarter before Palisades (14-5) scored eight unanswered points to tie the score at the end of the period.

North Hollywood broke the game open in the second period with a 20-3 run.

Grant 45, Los Angeles 33 -- Karla Parade scored 13 points and Shenia Smith added 12 points and 13 rebounds for host Grant (10-5) in a 3-A Division game.

Monique Young had 12 rebounds and Leah Wood scored 10 points for Grant.

Maria Coronado scored 18 points for Los Angeles, which was only 12 of 62 (19%) from the field.

Van Nuys 62, San Pedro 55 -- Senior center Stacie Toon scored a game-high 26 points and forward Feltina Means added 12 for host Van Nuys in a 4-A Division game.

San Pedro trailed, 30-20, at the half and never led in the game.

Canoga Park 53, Wilson 39 -- Soo Yoon and Kelli McCaskill combined for 30 points to lead the Hunters (8-3) in a 3-A Division game at Wilson (9-4).

Washington 107, Cleveland 38 -- The top-seeded Generals (24-3) advanced with an easy win over the Cavaliers.
