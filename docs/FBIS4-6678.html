BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 19 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The tense situation shaped down in the country due to the political hunger strike announced by the activists of the Mongolian Democratic Association (MDA) in the capital might be solved positively shortly. Such hope is arising in connection with today's events around the hunger strike participants of that for the fifth day are demanding the resignation of the government by P. Jasray. At 13.43 the former vice president of the country, chairman of the Social-Democratic Party, member of the parliament R. Gonchigdorj has joined in the hunger strikers. At the afternoon sitting of the parliament the leader of the MDA and member of the parliament T. Elbegdorj said that he refuses his deputyship and walked out the sitting. The president of the country P. Ochirbat asked in his speech addressing the parliament to discuss at the morning meeting both the demands of hungers and the opinion of the prime minister on this point. At present the leaderships of the ins and outs are holding meetings in their headquarters. It is likely that the president has delivered them the draft of compromising solution of the problem and attaining an agreement between the main political forces. In order to provide preparations for a meeting the president has received the representatives of hunger strikers. The meeting lasted more than three hours. The meeting were attended by representatives of all organizations participating in the hunger strike -- the MDA, Union for New Progress, the Union of Mongolian Students and the SDP as well.
