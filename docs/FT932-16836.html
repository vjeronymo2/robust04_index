930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: Ulster TV sharply up at Pounds 4.2m Ulster Television, the indep-endent programme contractor, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.95m to Pounds 4.18m in 1992. The dividend is stepped up from 7.5p to 10p with a final of 5.75p. Earnings per share increased to 24.2p (11.26p). The profit was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 1.79m (Pounds 743,000) being provisions and reorganisation costs.
