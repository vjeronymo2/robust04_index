BFN [Text] Al-Jarf, 11 May (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], this evening received Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, Yemeni minister of oil and mineral resources. The Yemeni minister briefed the UAE president on the latest developments in Yemen and issues of common concern. During the meeting, His Highness Shaykh Zayid stressed the importance of containing the current crisis and preventing its exacerbation as this will reflect on the future of Yemen and on security and stability in the region.
