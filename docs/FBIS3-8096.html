Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Wroclaw, 4 Mar--At a news conference in Wroclaw today, Tomasz Wojcik, chairman of the Lower Silesian Solidarity Region, stated that there would be no strike action in Wroclaw on 7 March. Wojcik said that if there were a strike staged at a later date in Wroclaw, the action would be carried out in the most unconventional manner, "which would cause disruption, especially to the authorities." Among other things, there will be disruption in telephone services or cuts in central heating in municipal offices, as well as in those institutions that provide services for the government.
