941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / Weekend Money: Gilt issues - best value v tax status --------------------------------------------------------------------- Your capital gain on a gilt - a UK government bond - is tax free. However, you pay tax on the interest. Therefore, gilts which deliver a higher proportion of their total return as capital gain are more tax efficient, and - other things being equal - more attractive to higher rate taxpayers.
