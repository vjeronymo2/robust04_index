920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Sharp decline at CA Sperati CA Sperati (The Special Agency), the south London-based button and trimming merchant, saw profits dip to Pounds 12,302 pre-tax for the six months to April 30. The decline from the comparable Pounds 26,658 reflected a 'dull trading climate' and conditions remained 'unpredictable' directors said. Turnover fell 8.8 per cent to Pounds 340,045 (Pounds 372,882). Earnings per share emerged at 8.1p (18.55p).
