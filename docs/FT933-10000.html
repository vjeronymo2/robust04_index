930806 FT 06 AUG 93 / People: Electronic switches Peter Alden has been appointed md of CAMBRIDGE CABLE, the Comcast & Singapore Telecom joint venture. British-born, he has worked on both sides of the Atlantic, starting his career as a design engineer with Marconi; he has been md of Videotron UK, an executive vice-president of Warner Cable in New York, md of Westminster Cable, and joins from Chase International in Connecticut where he was ceo of Polish Cable Television.
