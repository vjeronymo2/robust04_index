BFN [Excerpt] This morning the proceedings at the al-Djezair Hotel of the Algerian-Libyan preparatory committee for cooperation have been concluded with the signing of a bilateral agreement on multifaceted cooperation. The Algerian side at the meeting of this committee was represented by Mr. Ahmed Attaf, secretary of state for cooperation and Maghreb affairs, and the Libyan side by Mr. Sulayman al-Shahhumi, general secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity in charge of the Arab Maghreb Union's affairs.
