Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou has described his cooperation with European Commission President Jacques Delors as productive, useful, and effective, adding that their views coincide. The members of the Greek Government and the European Commission conferred today on the goals of the Greek Presidency and coordinating efforts to integrate the European Union. Papandreou and Theodhoros Pangalos, who is presiding over the Foreign Ministers Council, referred to the priorities of the Greek Presidency, stressing that the prevailing issues are unemployment, implementing the Maastricht Treaty, expanding the European Union, and addressing the Balkan crisis. [passage omitted] The European Commission's president and members left Athens this afternoon.
