Name: Special Emphasis Panel in Geosciences (1756). Date and Time: December 12, 1994; 8:00 a.m._5:00 p.m. Place: Room # 770, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Closed. Contact Person: Dr. Judith L. Hannah, Program Director, Education and Human Resources Program, Division of Earth Sciences, Room 785, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA, 22230. Telephone: (703) 306&hyph;1557. Purpose of Meeting: To provide advice and recommendations concerning proposals submitted to NSF for financial support.
