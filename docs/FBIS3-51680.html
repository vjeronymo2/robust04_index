Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "National" newscast] [Text] President Cesar Gaviria has just signed a decree whereby he freezes government workers' payroll. That means that as of today there will be no new appointments to government posts until the electoral process is concluded. The goal is to grant the elections honesty. Here is what Planning Director Armando Montenegro said: [Begin recording] [Unidentified reporter] Mr. Montenegro, why was the decision to freeze the government payroll made today? [Montenegro] This is a normal decision during the period before the elections to guarantee impartiality. The appointments of employees and officials run the risk of mid-level officials using them for political reasons. [Reporter] How long will this decision remain in force? [Montenegro] This will remain in force until after the elections. [end recording]
