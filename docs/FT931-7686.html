930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Essex Furniture advances 65% Essex Furniture mounted a buoyant performance in its traditionally stronger first half to end-December. On turnover ahead 61 per cent to Pounds 5.19m (Pounds 3.23m), pre-tax profits improved 65 per cent, from Pounds 375,000 to Pounds 619,000. The company has opened two Furniture Workshop showrooms since July, and plans to open at least three more by December. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.5p (1.25p), payable from earnings per share of 3.73p (2.46p) on capital increased by last June's rights issue.
