930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: Colchester housing scheme MANSELL has been awarded Phases 3 and 4 of a residential design and build scheme for clients The Guinness Trust. Providing a 123-unit development in Highwoods, Colchester, the scheme has a value of Pounds 4m with completion anticipated mid-1994. The design of the scheme has been prepared in collaboration with the John Groom Association, providing groups of bungalows for the disabled. The bungalows comply to the John Groom building specification incorporating extra wide doors for ease of wheelchair access; ramps and dropped kerbs around the entire development; specially equipped toilets, bathrooms and kitchens.
