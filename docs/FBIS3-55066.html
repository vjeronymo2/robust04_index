Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Military operations are escalating on the Karabakh front every day. Disregarding huge losses in men and material, the Azerbaijani Army Command is launching uninterrupted new offensives against the defensive positions of the Karabakh forces. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic's Press and Information Department reported that the enemy troops launched a new offensive at around 0600 this morning all along the frontline from the Iranian border to the Murov mountain range in the north of Mardakertskiy Rayon. The Karabakh formations were still successfully halting the Azerbaijani attacks at 1100. According to preliminary reports, an enemy tank was hit and some 150 soldiers and officers are surrounded in the southeast of the front. Casualty figures for the previous day have been determined. Karabakh fighters killed 140 Azerbaijani soldiers, destroyed four tanks, and an infantry vehicle. Five fighters were killed and one tank was hit on our side. We have no news from the Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service on the situation in Armenia's border regions.
