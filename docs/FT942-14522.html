940420 FT 20 APR 94 / People: Central Independent Television Clive Jones, managing director of London News Network which provides regional news and sports programmes for both Carlton Television and LWT, has been appointed managing director of Central Independent television. Jones, 45, will be responsible for a range of Central activities including Central Productions, the facilities operation The Television House, and the regional transmission and programme division, Central Broadcasting. He replaces Andy Allan, who is now chief executive of Carlton UK Television.
