940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / Observer: Spin tried Absurd but true. Dutch newspapers are now carrying advertisements from Unilever proclaiming that Lever Nederland is '100 per cent behind' its newest product, Omo Power, a brand of soap powder. Inspired by the dirty laundry being publicly washed by its arch-rival, Procter & Gamble, Unilever is thus now in the ridiculous position of paying good money in order to tell consumers that it likes its own product.
