BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The joint commission on compliance and inspection is continuing its work in Geneva. This temporary body was set up to implement the treaty on reduction of strategic offensive weapons, START-1 and START-2. Representatives of five powers -- Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan, and the United States -- belong to the commission. Ukraine, Belarus, and Kazakhstan are to join the treaty as non-nuclear states. [begin recording] Russian Representative M. Streltsov -- identified by caption: We have already made a certain amount of progress. A whole series of agreements and joint statements by our commission have been signed. These regulate issues involved in the multilateral fulfillment of the treaty. We believe that the Ukraine will accede to the nuclear non-proliferation treaty in the very near future. This will create all conditions necessary to ensure that the treaty comes into force. [end recording]
