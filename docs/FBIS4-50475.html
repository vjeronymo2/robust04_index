BFN [Text] Guangzhou, May 17 (CNS) -- Guangzhou, capital of Guangdong Province, earned export value of U.S.$1.097 billion in the first four months this year, 32 percent up over the same period of last year. Enterprises engaging in foreign trade in the city have developed their businesses from single way of foreign trade to diversified businesses including domestic trade, real estate and manufacturing other than foreign trade. Its construction of production bases for export products have been further strengthened, its international business further developed and the market structure for its export improved with its exports to the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) countries in the first quarter this year increased by 70.63 percent over the same period of last year, that to African countries by 111.97 percent, that to the Middle East countries by 89.56 percent, that to the South American countries by 77.45 percent and that to neighboring countries by 155.79 percent.
