920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Marginal assets fall at Jersey Phoenix Jersey Phoenix Trust reported net asset value of 90.5p per share at June 30 against 90.8p a year earlier. Net profits for the year amounted to Pounds 797,652 (Pounds 813,275) equal to earnings of 5.3p (5.4p) per share. A final dividend of 1.5p holds the total at 5.25p.
