BFN [Text] Indonesia has established diplomatic relations with the Commonwealth of Dominica at the ambassadorial level. A press statement issued by the Foreign Ministry in Jakarta today stated that the signing of the bilateral agreement to establish diplomatic relations was conducted in Washington on 19 April. A joint communique was signed by Indonesian ambassador to the United States Arifin Siregar and Dominica's ambassador to the United States (Eduard Wati), who represented their respective governments. In his address, Ambassador Arifin Siregar, among other things, said that the establishment of diplomatic relations will further enhance bilateral cooperation in the fields of politics, economy, and culture between the two countries. According to Arifin Siregar, as developing nations, Indonesia and the Commonwealth of Dominica have a joint responsibility to establish a new world order which is free from strife, poverty, and injustice. Meanwhile, in his address Ambassador (Eduard Wati) said that the Republic of Dominica should emulate Indonesia's fine example of development.
