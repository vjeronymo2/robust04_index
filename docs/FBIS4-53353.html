BFN [Text] On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the 18 May Kwangju democratization movement, various rallies, attended by citizens and college students, were held throughout the country. A national meeting to inherit the spirit of the May resistance struggle was held in Chongmyo Park in Seoul under the auspices of the National Alliance for Democracy and National Reunification, and other rallies are being held at this moment in about 20 places across the country including college campuses. However, there is no report of clashes with police.
