Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Presidential Palace in Ta'izz today, brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, received brother al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of Jordan's Royal Court, who relayed a message from his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the fraternal Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. The message concerns brotherly ties and developments of concern to the two fraternal countries, as well as the brotherly efforts exerted by His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal to bring viewpoints closer and overcome the current situation in our country. During the meeting, the conferees reviewed several developments, issues of mutual concern, and relations between the two brotherly countries. The brother president expressed appreciation for the role played by His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal and the brothers in Jordan and the great concern they display toward the process of unity and democracy in our country. He praised the warm brotherly ties between the two countries. He also asked al-Sharif Zayd to convey his greetings to His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal and wishes for continued progress and prosperity to the fraternal Jordanian people. The meeting was attended by brother Mujahid Abu-Shawarib, deputy prime minister; Muhammad Salim Basindwah, the foreign minister; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, adviser to His Majesty King Husayn; and Staff Major General 'Id Salim, Jordanian assistant chief of staff.
