931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / People: Finance moves Thomas Wyman, non-executive chairman of SG Warburg & Co Inc, has been appointed a vice-chairman of SG WARBURG GROUP. Peter Bass, joint head of the fixed interest and treasury division of SG Warburg & Co Inc, and Peter Twachtmann, joint head of the same division at SG Warburg & Co Ltd, have been appointed to the group board.
