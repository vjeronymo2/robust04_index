September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LIFE WITHOUT KFAC: WHEN ROCK ROLLS OVER BACH

Not all classical music fans are aging bluestockings who hate popular culture. I am 25, middle class and nonwhite, and I enjoy rock as much as anyone else.

But there are more than enough rock stations for me to choose from. And I would like Evergreen to know that the station that replaces KFAC will never be among my choices.

FELICIA A. LEE

Los Angeles
