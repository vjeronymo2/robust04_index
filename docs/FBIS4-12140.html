BFN [Text] Khartoum, 4 Apr (MENA)--The Sudanese authorities today suspended AL-SUDANI AL-DUWALI newspaper. The decision was taken under the emergency laws. A statement by the Sudanese Presidency, carried by Omdurman radio today, said that the paper cast doubt on the struggle of the Sudanese Armed Forces and called for separation and abandoning the unity of the country and disintegrating national unity. It also dealt lightly with the prestige of the revolution and its symbols without any justification.
