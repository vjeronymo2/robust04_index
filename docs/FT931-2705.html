930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Clinton 'ready to help' on N Ireland US president Bill Clinton told Irish Republic prime minister Albert Reynolds that the US was 'ready to help' end violence in Northern Ireland. His proposal to send a US envoy to Ireland remained under 'serious consideration'. Mr Reynolds, who met Mr Clinton in the White House, said the US had 'the potential to be uniquely helpful' in contributing to a solution.
