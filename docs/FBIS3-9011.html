Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks between the military delegation led by Army General Vitaliy Radeskyy, Ukrainian minister of defense, and Gheorghe Tinca, Romanian minister of national defense, accompanied by other prominent figures of the Romanian Army leadership, took place today in Bucharest. The talks emphasized the mutual determination to consolidate ties between the armies of the two countries and focused on various ways and means to expand cooperation on many planes. The Ukrainian minister expressed the desire to extend the military contacts at various echelons in order to achieve a better mutual understanding, to consolidate the relations of good neighborliness, and to positively solve problems of mutual interest. At the end of the visit paid to our country by the Ukrainian military delegation, a cooperation accord between the Ministry of National Defense of Romania and the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine will be signed -- a communique received from the Army's press bureau reads.
