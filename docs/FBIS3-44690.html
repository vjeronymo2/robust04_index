Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 6 (XINHUA) -- China has set up exchange and cooperation relations in agricultural science and technology with 141 countries and international organizations including the World Bank, the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization and the World Food Program. According to the Ministry of Agriculture, over the past 15 years, China has introduced a batch of programs of agricultural technology involving chicken raising, artificial insemination of livestock, agricultural remote-sensing and plastic sheets on fields. The ministry alone invited more than 2,500 experts from overseas in the period and, plus the number of foreign experts invited by provinces, the figure is estimated to be more than 10,000. The World Food Program helped 30 provinces, regions and municipalities in the country in setting up 55 grain production project worth more than 700 million U.S. dollars. The World Bank also provided loans of 850 million U.S. dollars for China's 16 agricultural projects. Statistics show that China now has more than 400 agricultural projects which are funded by foreign loans totaling 2.6 billion U.S. dollars.
