920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / World News In Brief: Sevilla pays Dollars 7.5m for Maradona Former Argentine soccer captain Diego Maradona is to join the Spanish club Sevilla. His Italian club, Napoli, agreed to sell him for Dollars 7.5m (Pounds 4.2m). Maradona, aged 31, was suspended from football for 15 months until June this year for cocaine use.
