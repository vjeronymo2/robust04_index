920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Assets decline at Fleming Eastern Net asset value at Fleming Far Eastern Investment Trust was 229.25p at March 31, down from the 244p reported a year ago. Net revenue in the year to March 31 declined to Pounds 2.33m (Pounds 3.42m). Earnings dropped to 1.53p (2.19p). The dividend for the year is reduced to 1.5p (2p).
