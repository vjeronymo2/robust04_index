April 9, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

BROWNS SIGN 10 FREE AGENTS

The Browns today signed 10 free agents, including Jeff Graham, a quarterback on the team's developmental squad last season, and defensive back Charlie Wright, the brother of Browns veteran safety Felix Wright.

They also signed defensive end Bob Buczkowski, 25, a No. 1 draft pick of the Raiders in 1986, and four free-agent offensive linemen: Jeff Lucas, Matt Patchan, Bob Sims and Dave Smith. Also signed were tight ends Charlie Darrington and K. D. Dunn and cornerback Ellis Dillahunt.
