Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, March 4 (CNS)--According to relevant departments under the Guangdong provincial government, the province will set up a military recreation development zone in which a number of military entertainment items including a shooting range, a site for heavy weapon manoeuvres and a sky-diving club will be built. The General staff headquarters of the People's Liberation Army (PLA) and Gaoyiao City in Guangdong Province will join in the construction of this development zone which has been designed to be located on the banks of the Xijiang River, south of the city with a total area of two square kilometres. It will be divided into four functional sub-divisions with the military tourism zone occupying 174,000 square metres and facilities including an indoor shooting range, horse-racing track, a site for heavy weapon manoeuvres, a site for combat exhibition and a sky-diving club. The other three sub-divisions will be a central district with a total area of 82,000 square metres with an administration centre, restaurants, commercial and office buildings, a residential area able to house 15,000 households totalling 53,000 residents and a villa district with a total area of 41,000 square metres. With the completion of the development zone, the province will offer another enjoyable place for entertainment, sight-seeing and holidaying for both domestic and foreign visitors.
