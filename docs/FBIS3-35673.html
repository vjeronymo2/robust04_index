Language: English Article Type:BFN [From ELTA "NEWS BULLETIN" No. 425] [Text] Vilnius, Jan 28, ELTA -- The first meeting of a working group on "Partnership for Peace" took place in Foreign ministry on Friday [28 January]. The meeting considered arrangements to prepare an individual programme of Lithuania's partnership with NATO and issues connected with the visit of NATO officials next week. Among other members of the group, Deputy Foreign Minister Albinas Januska, Deputy National Defence Minister Sarunas Vasiliauskas, head of Lithuanian Armed Forces Jonas Andriskevicius participated in the meeting.
