BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, April 15 (AFP) -- International mediators David Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg and special UN envoy Yasushi Akashi were to try to meet Bosnian Serb leaders Friday in a bid to end Serb harassment of UN troops and an upsurge in Serb attacks on Moslem enclave, UN military sources said. They said the three would travel to the Serb stronghold of Pale, where Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic is based, for a meeting scheduled for 2:00 p.m. (1200 GMT). They did not elaborate. Vitaly Churkin, Russia's special envoy to former Yugoslavia, has been in Pale, east of Sarajevo, for several days. [passage omitted]
