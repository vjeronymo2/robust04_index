January 14, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING / EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

The Costa Mesa City Council will be asked at its meeting Monday to delay the eviction of the Share Our Selves charity from the Rea Community Center.

Directors from SOS will ask the council to put off the ouster until the charity can buy and remodel a new headquarters. The council can either order the charity's eviction effective Tuesday, grant the directors' request or come up with an alternative plan. The meeting begins at 6:30 p.m. in the council chambers at Costa Mesa City Hall, 77 Fair Drive.
