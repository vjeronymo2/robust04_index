930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Booth hit by delayed contracts Delayed overseas contracts were behind a dive into pre-tax losses of Pounds 575,578 in the year ended March 31 at Booth Industries Group, the structural steelwork and engineering group. The deficit compared with profits of Pounds 677,281 last time. Turnover tumbled 39 per cent to Pounds 18.9m (Pounds 30.85m). The final dividend has been omitted, leaving the 0.7p interim to compare with the previous year's 3.2p total. Losses per share were 11.53p (10.58p earnings). The shares fell 16p to 42p.
