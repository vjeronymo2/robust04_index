National Institute of General Medical Sciences; Notice of meetings Pursuant to Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given of the meetings of the committees of the National Institute of General Medical Sciences for November 1994. These meetings will be open to the public to discuss administrative details relating to committee business for approximately one hour at the beginning of the first session of the first day of the meeting. Attendance by the public will be limited to space available. These meetings will be closed thereafter in accordance with provisions set forth in sections 552b(c)(4) and 552b(c)(6), Title 5, U.S.C. and section 10(d) of Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, for the review, discussion, and evaluation of individual research training grant and research center grant applications.
