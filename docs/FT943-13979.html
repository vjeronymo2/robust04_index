940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: C & G will not appeal - At a Glance Cheltenham & Gloucester building society has decided not to appeal against the recent High Court veto of the original plan to distribute the Pounds 1.8bn offered by Lloyds bank, which wants to take over the society. It is confident that it can make an alternative proposal which will conform with legal restrictions on which C & G customers can receive sweeteners. This plan is expected in mid-August.
