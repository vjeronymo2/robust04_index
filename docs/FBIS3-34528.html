Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, has sent a message to the opposition leader in the National Assembly, Mr. Mohammad Nawaz Sharif. The message was delivered by the federal minister for water and power, Malik Ghulam Mustafa Khar, in Lahore this morning. Later talking to newsmen, the minister said that the message was in the overall context of national reconciliation. Replying to questions, he said the message was reflective of the prime minister's keen desire for national reconciliation. He said the present government does not want to enter into any sort of confrontation with anyone.
