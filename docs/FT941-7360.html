940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Independent bid talks continue Directors of Newspaper Publishing, owners of the Independent and Independent on Sunday, were last night locked in talks over the future ownership of the newspaper. The board committee of independent directors was considering whether to recommend to shareholders a takeover bid from a consortium led by Mirror Group Newspapers. Meanwhile, members of the National Union of Journalists at the two newspapers sent an open letter to Mr Ian Hay Davison, board chairman, calling for the consortium bid to be rejected because it had 'failed to come up with a bid proposal that guarantees the independence of the titles or provides a stable framework of ownership and control'.
