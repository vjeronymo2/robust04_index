BFN [Text] The heads of the Israeli and Palestinian delegations to the economic talks in Paris believe that the talks will be wrapped up next week. Avraham Shohat and Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'] issued a joint statement saying that the talks will not be concluded today, and emphasizing the good atmosphere in the deliberations. Our correspondent Gid'on Kouts reports that the imports policy and customs duties are the most outstanding problems in the talks.
