BFN [Text] Moscow June 2 ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has signed a decree ordering the sale of the state-owned enterprises unable to pay off their debts. The decree is signed to promote the realization of the state policy aimed at preventing bankruptcy and negative social effects associated with the reorganization and liquidation of insolvent enterprises, should they be declared bankrupt. The decree goes into effect from the moment of its publication.
