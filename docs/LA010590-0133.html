January 5, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PACTEL LAYOFFS: Pacific Telesis, announcing a work-force reduction of 11,000 employees over the next five years (D1), hopes most will result from early retirement and attrition. . . . Its subsidiary, PacBell, has 5,976 employees in 72 Orange County locations. Among them: a small-business marketing center in Orange and a residential service center in Tustin, each with 1,000 workers, and a dispatch center in Anaheim with 350 workers. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
