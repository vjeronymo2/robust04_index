Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Foreign Ministry's Press Service has revealed that the Azerbaijani Foreign Ministry has conveyed a protest note to the Armenian Defense Ministry protesting the killing of Azerbaijani POW's in Armenia. Condemning the act of violence, the Azerbaijani Foreign Ministry demanded that Armenia investigate all the aspects of the criminal action without wasting time and seriously penalize the culprits behind the tragic incident.
