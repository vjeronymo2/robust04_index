Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow, 14 Jan -- Today's session of the Federation Council of the Russian Federal Assembly has started with discussion of the candidates for the posts of deputy chairman of the upper chamber of parliament. Vladimir Shumeyko, speaker of the Federation Council, has presented five canditates from whom the members of the Federation Council are to elect three deputies, including one first deputy. The candidates are Ramazan Abdulatipov, first deputy chairman of the State Committee of the Federation; Petr Romanov, general director of the "Yenisey" concern; Valeryan Viktorov, chairman of the Council of Ministers of Chuvashia; Aleksandr Kovalev, head of administration of Voronezh Oblast; and Bolot Ayushev, who is temporarily not working.
