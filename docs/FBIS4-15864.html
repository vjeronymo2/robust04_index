BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 475] [Text] Kuwait, April 11, ELTA -- Lithuanian state delegation headed by Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius visited Kuwait on April 9-11. The delegation was received by Kuwait State Amir Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah, Prime Minister Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah and members of the government. Jabir was extremely interested in Lithuania's relations with the neighbouring countries, situation in the Baltic states and Lithuania's efforts to cooperate with European and other countries. During the meetings the officials of Kuwait emphasized that they estimated positively the determination of the Lithuanian Government to strengthen relations with small countries and increase its activities in the international organizations. Jabir invited Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas to visit Kuwait and emphasized that Kuwait was ready to provide help for Lithuania. During the visit both sides discussed the economic cooperation, investments and other issues. The memorandum fixing the dates for signing the agreements between the two countries was signed by Lithuanian delegation and officials of Kuwait. Slezevicius invited Prime Minister of Kuwait to visit Lithuania. The delegation left for the United Arab Emirates [UAE] on Monday [11 April].
