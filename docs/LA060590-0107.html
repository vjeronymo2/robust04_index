June 5, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Depeche Blue: Depeche Mode has added a second Dodger Stadium concert for Aug. 5. Their Aug. 4 show sold out one hour after tickets went on sale last Saturday. Tickets for the added show go on sale this Saturday at 9 a.m. at Music Plus stores and through Ticketmaster. GREG BRAXTON
