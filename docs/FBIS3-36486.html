Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Moscow February 7 TASS -- "Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev met Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam to discuss preparations for the visit to Russia by the Vietnamese prime minister scheduled approximately at the end of June," Galina Sidorova, a political adviser of the Russian foreign minister, told ITAR-TASS upon completion of the meeting. Sidorova said that Russo-Vietnamese commission for trade, technical and economic cooperation would meet in session in April, 1994 as part of the preparations for the visit. The commission will discuss a number of other economic agreements, Sidorova said. She pointed out that Russia's goal is to sign a political document on basic principles of relations between Russia and Vietnam during the visit. During the talks the Vietnamese foreign minister shared his impressions of the visit to Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan. For his part, the Russian minister told about his recent official visit to China. The sides exchanged opinions on the current situation in the Asian pacific region and problems of cooperation on security in the framework of ASEAN. Special attention at the talks was devoted to the present situation in Bosnia.
