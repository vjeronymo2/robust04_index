Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina is currently visiting Switzerland, where he will spend the weekend and will meet with Swiss Foreign Affairs Secretary (Jacob Kellenberger). Cuban official sources have reported that Robaina has stopped over in Berne on his way to Africa and will take advantage of this stopover to meet with Swiss authorities and visit the capital city, particularly the Parliament. Robaina will leave Berne on Sunday. This is the second time Robaina has visited Switzerland; his first visit was in 1993 when he addressed the Social and Economic Council to explain the Cuban situation. At that time Robaina also met with Swiss authorities, UN officials in Geneva, Latin American ambassadors, and other ambassadors to the United Nations.
