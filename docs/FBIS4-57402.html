BFN [Text] As of this afternoon, Cuba Vision's viewers have a new newscast, one that will allow them to learn, early on, of the most important stories that will appear on the National Television Newscast [Noticiero Nacional de Television -- NTV]. Every day, Monday through Friday at 1927 [2327 GMT], our newsroom personnel will have ready for you a summary of the main events making the news in Cuba and in the world. We will thereby inform you, quickly and directly, about the subjects that will be reported at greater length over NTV at 2000 [0000 GMT].
