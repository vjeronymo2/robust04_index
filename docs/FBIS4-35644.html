BFN [Text] The BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] has reiterated that the country should be firm in its stand on the Prithvi missile test. The party spokesman, Mr. K.L. Sharma, said in New Delhi today that there should be no question of shelving the test. Referring to some reported remarks by the U.S. ambassador-designate to India, Mr. Frank Wisner, Mr. Sharma accused the United States of adopting double standards. He said while the American Government helped Britain in carrying out its Trident missile test, it is opposing India's Prithvi tests.
