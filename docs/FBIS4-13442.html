BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Apr (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has denied that he will meet with any Israeli official during his visit to Romania, which will begin tomorrow. He was answering a question from MENA on press reports suggesting that he was likely to meet with the Israeli delegation participating in the annual meeting of the forum on political and democratic prospects in the world that will be held in Bucharest.
