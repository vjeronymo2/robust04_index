January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AGIRLFROMARS EASY WINNER

Agirlfromars overtook Collective Joy entering the stretch and drew off for a 4 1/2-length victory in Friday's $47,000 feature race at Santa Anita before a crowd of 17,602.

Agirlfromars, the even-money favorite ridden by Robbie Davis and carrying 114 pounds, covered one mile on Santa Anita's turf course in 1:33 2/5 and paid $4, $3 and $2.60.
