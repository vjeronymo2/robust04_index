BFN [Text] Beijing, June 16 (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng will pay official visits to the Republic of Austria, the Federal Republic of Germany and Romania form June 29 to July 12, a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman announced at a weekly press conference here this afternoon. According to the spokesman, the Chinese premier will pay visits to the three above countries at the invitation of Austrian Prime Minister Franz Vranitzky, German Chancellor Helmut Kohl, and Romanian President Ion Iliescu and Prime Minster Nicolae Vacaroiu.
