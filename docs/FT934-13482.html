931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Scotia Holdings SCOTIA HOLDINGS: Of the 14m new ordinary shares proposed to be issued to raise Pounds 40.6m, 2.76m have been allocated to non-UK investors and 11.24m to UK investors. Of the latter, 5.6m shares have been allocated to clients of Kleinwort Benson Securities and Hoare Govett Corporate Finance. A further 5.6m shares have been allocated to intermediaries, independent marketmakers and employees of Scotia. Applications from employees and independent marketmakers have been met in full. Intermediaries will receive 97 per cent of their applications.
