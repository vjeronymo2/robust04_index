Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] With the installation of an oil tank, the Qal'eh-Nar oil field around Andimeshk township started production on 11 January. To begin with, the oil field's production capacity will be around 5,000 barrels per day, which is to be increased gradually to 15,000 barrels per day. The construction and installation of the oil tank has cost around 5.3 billion rials. The total area of this oil field is 54 square km, and is situated 35 km north of Andimeshk, between the Kabud and Lab-Sefid oil fields. According to an IRNA report, the Qal'eh-Nar oil field holds a total of 1,360,000,000 barrels of oil, out of which 117 billion barrels are estimated to be exploitable.
