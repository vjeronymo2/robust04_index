Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovak delegation at the Prague meeting of representatives of Visegrad Group countries with U.S. President Bill Clinton will be composed of President Michal Kovac, Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Jozef Moravcik. It will probably also include deputy prime ministers Jozef Prokes, Sergej Kozlik, and Julius Toth, and Minister of the Economy Jan Ducky. Our delegation will leave for Prague tomorrow afternoon. Michal Kovac will also hold talks on Wednesday with Czech President Vaclav Havel and Hungarian President Arpad Goncz. He will also meet his Polish counterpart, Lech Walesa. Bilateral talks of prime ministers and ministers of foreign affairs of the Slovak Republic and the Czech Republic and, naturally, bilateral talks of our representatives with the U.S. delegation are also expected.
