930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: BAT's Brazilian arm shows advance Souza Cruz, BAT Industries' 75 per cent owned Brazilian subsidiary, has reported its results for the 1992 year. Net income totalled Cr2.08bn, against Cr673.5m last time. Net profit per share was Cr6,819.96 (Cr2,203.28) and the proposed final dividend is Cr2,778.5 making Cr3,846.9 (Cr1,403.06) for the year. The figures for both years were adjusted for inflation and expressed at December 1992 exchange rates. BAT advised that before consolidation into the group's results the figures would have to be adjusted significantly to bring them into line with UK accounting principles.
