940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Berry Birch & Noble issues profit warning Directors of Berry Birch & Noble, the USM-quoted concern which provides insurance, pension and financial planning services, warned that profits for the year to January 31 were likely to fall short of market expectations. The shares dropped 30p to 155p. The directors stated that indications pointed to some Pounds 200,000 for the second half, making Pounds 660,000 for the 12 months, against Pounds 1.06m last time. They said, however, that turnover would be about Pounds 7m, compared with Pounds 6.4m. Mr Derek Berry, chairman, said the outlook was encouraging and that the final dividend should be maintained at 3.3p.
