BFN [Text] President Joaquim Chissano will be the first citizen to be registered in the Mozambican capital to vote in the forthcoming October elections. The president of the Republic will be registered in the Josina Machel School in Maputo. About 85 percent of electoral registration posts in Maputo will begin operating today. The process is also scheduled to begin today in all provinces, although in some areas it will be delayed due to logistical problems. In some provinces, the Mozambique National Resistance has not permitted the beginning of electoral registration in areas under its control.
