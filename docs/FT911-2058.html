910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / Nigeria sends troops Nigeria has airlifted 500 troops to Sierra Leone to help the government fight an incursion of Liberian rebels, William Keeling writes from Lagos. Up to 1,000 Liberian rebels entered areas in the south and east of Sierra Leone in late March. Captured rebels have claimed to be fighting on the orders of Mr Charles Taylor, the main Liberian rebel leader. He has denied involvement.
