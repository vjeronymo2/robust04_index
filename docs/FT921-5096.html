920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / UK Company News: William Sinclair declines to Pounds 1.68m William Sinclair Holdings, the Lincoln-based supplier of garden leisure and pet products, reported a 5 per cent fall in pre-tax profits for the six months to end-December. The decline - from Pounds 1.77m last time to Pounds 1.68m - came on turnover ahead some 10 per cent to Pounds 16.3m (14.9m). The interim dividend is raised by 0.1p to 1.7p, payable from earnings of 5.8p (6.8p) per share.
