Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 20 Feb (EFE) -- The Venezuelan Foreign Ministry has announced in an official communique that Carlos Gottberg, the Venezuelan consul general in Colombia, was not hurt in a terrorist attack which took place in the Colombian city of Medellin early this morning. The bomb explosion at the offices of the Venezuelan consulate did not inflict any casualties but it did cause serious property damage. When informed about the situation, German Carreras Damas, the Venezuelan ambassador to Colombia, immediately made contact with the consular offices in Medellin and requested an explanation from the Colombian Government. Foreign Ministry sources told EFE that the Venezuelan Government will formally ask the Colombian authorities for their cooperation in providing the proper protection to its diplomats on the territory of the neighboring country.
