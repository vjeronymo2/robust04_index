Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 11 (XINHUA) -- Chi Haotian, Chinese State Councillor and defense minister, met with and hosted a banquet here this evening in honor of Visiting Kazakhstani Defense Minister Sagadat Nurmagambetov and his party. This is the first visit to China by a defense minister of Kazakhstan since the country won its independence. As Chi's guests, Nurmagambetov and his party arrived here this morning. In the afternoon, Chi hosted a welcoming ceremony for his Kazakh counterpart and held talks with him.
