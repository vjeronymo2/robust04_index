April 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CHANGE OF NETWORKS FOR BASEBALL COVERAGE HAS FAN APPRECIATION

Thumbs down to CBS' postgame show hosted by Keith Olbermann. This newly installed drab of a half-hour is the quintessence of CBS baseball: Boooring! Olbermann's rehash of the game just seen and his so-called humor makes for the worst half-hour of sports on TV. Come on, CBS, give us something worth watching.

JASON INSALACO, Glendale
