Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Prime Minister Mohamed Karim Lambrani today presided over a Council of Ministers meeting, during which a number of draft decrees were discussed. [passage omitted] After that, the Council [of Ministers] discussed the treaty banning the development, production, stockpiling, and use of chemical weapons, along with the destruction of those weapons, as well as the draft law on the approval, in principle, of this agreement under which the signatory countries undertake not to develop, produce, possess, keep or transport chemical weapons, either directly or indirectly, under any circumstances. The signatory countries also undertake to destroy the chemical weapons they possess and accord paramount priority to ensuring the safety of people and the protection of the environment in the course of transporting, storing, or destroying these weapons.
