930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Murray European net assets rise Net asset value per share at Murray European Investment Trust improved to 51.6p as at June 30 1993. This compared with 46.3p six months earlier and 44.6p as at end-June 1992. Earnings per share rose from 0.15p to 0.6p for the six months' period on net available revenue of Pounds 179,000, compared with Pounds 46,000. The directors stated that in line with capital growth investment policy, there is no interim dividend.
