930414 FT 14 APR 93 / UK Company News: Buy-out at Maiden Advertising Maiden Outdoor Advertising, the independent private poster contractor, has been acquired by its management in a buy-out financed by Morgan Grenfell Development Capital. Morgan Grenfell and NatWest Acquisition Finance have respectively underwritten the institutional equity and debt commitment of the financing package which amounts to about Pounds 21m. The funds will finance the acquisition and provide for future growth.
