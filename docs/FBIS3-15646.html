Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine has issued instructions containing a permit to privatize the property of Zaporozhye's state steel-rolling plant, Kherson's combine harvester plant, and Khartsyzsk's state steel wire and cable works. The State Property Fund is to endorse plans for the privatization of these enterprises and to agree them with the relevant ministries and Ukraine's Antimonopoly Committee.
