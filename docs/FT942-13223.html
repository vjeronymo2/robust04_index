940426 FT 26 APR 94 / UK Company News: Capita CAPITA has bought John Crilley, the UK's second largest provider of bailiff services to the public and private sectors, for Pounds 1.05m in convertible preference and convertible redeemable preference shares. Crilley, which achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.5m in the 12 months to April 30 1993 on turnover of Pounds 7.55m, was the main competitor to Capita's Roach offshoot.
