BFN [Excerpts] As reported earlier, Days of Kazakhstan will be held 23-27 May to promote the development of mutual cooperation between fraternal countries in the socioeconomic and educational spheres and to strengthen friendship between our peoples. Yesterday, a scheduled session of the city committee for preparing and holding Days of Kazakhstan in our Republic was held. The meeting was chaired by Jurabekov, first deputy prime minister of the republic and chairman of the organizational committee. Deputy Prime Minister Karabayev spoke in detail about the progress of preparatory work for the Days of Kazakhstan. [passage omitted] Khalilov, acting chairman of the Uzbek Supreme Soviet; Jurayev, state adviser to the president; Deputy Prime Ministers Sultanov and Usmanov; Fazylbekov, hakim of Tashkent City; and the heads of ministries and departments and deputy hakims of the Republic's oblasts participated in the meeting of the organizational committee.
