BFN [Text] Khartoum, 26 May (SUNA) -- Sudan has expressed its condemnation of all attempts at division and fragmentation and the lies of the advocates of the right to self-determination for illusory racial entities. The Foreign Ministry said in a statement issued yesterday to mark the 31st anniversary of the OAU's establishment, that the anniversary comes at a time when the continent is becoming marginalized as part of the so-called new world order. It called for more solidarity, vigilance, and unity in the face of the conspiracies being hatched against the African continent.
