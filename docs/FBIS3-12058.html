Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso has said that he is a candidate for president of the Republic. Minister Cardoso today received the PSDB [Brazilian Social Democracy Party] leaders. Speaking in the name of his party, PSDB President Tasso Jereissati invited Cardoso to become a presidential candidate. Jereissati said his party considers Cardoso's candidacy indispensable. In his speech to the PSDB leaders, Minister Cardoso said he accepted the nomination and would carry the banner of victory because, according to him, it is the best way in which the economic plan can be complied with. Cardoso said that the country is tired of corruption, inflation, and poverty. He told his PSDB colleagues that he will officially inform President Itamar Franco of his decision to step down from his ministry to become candidate for president of the Republic during the cabinet meeting that is taking place right now at Planalto Palace.
