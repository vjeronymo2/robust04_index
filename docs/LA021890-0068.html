February 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Toby Rothschild, executive director of the Legal Aid Foundation of Long Beach, has been elected to a second term as president of the Long Beach Jewish Community Center . . . Nancy Latimer, founder of the Coalition to Preserve Historic Long Beach, has been named Long Beach Heritage Coalition's 1989 Preservationist of the Year . . . The Commerce City Council has renamed the Rosewood Park Community Center the Kathryn Pahl Community Center after one of the city's founders, a member of the first City Council.
