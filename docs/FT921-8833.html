920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / Hydro to raise prices by 4.9% HOUSEHOLDS in northern Scotland face a 4.9 per cent rise in charges in April from Scottish Hydro-Electric, the first electricity company to announce increases for the coming year. Hydro's rise last year was 9 per cent. Hydro said it had undercharged last year and was entitled to recover its costs from customers.
