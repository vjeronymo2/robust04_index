Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking last night at a dinner to collect funds for his Democratic Party -- which garnered more than $3 million -- U.S. President William Clinton said he will not lift the blockade on Cuba. During the dinner, Clinton seemed worried about the fact that he will have to testify again before the special prosecutor in the Whitewater case. [Former] Judge David Hale had said that through transactions Clinton provided some $100,000 to the Whitewater company, through which Clinton and his wife Hilary owned some properties when Clinton was governor of Arkansas.
