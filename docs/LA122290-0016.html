December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AGASSI SHOULD PRACTICE WHAT HE ADVERTISES

On several occasions last weekend, I observed a rather irritating ad on TV showing many short scenes of Andre Agassi in different settings swinging his racket as a manly man. At the end of the ad we were shown a black background with three little words: Just do it.

Shouldn't that have read: Just don't do it.

MICHAEL HANNIN

Woodland Hills
