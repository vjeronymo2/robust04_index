BFN [TA SR report: "They Need Quality Statistics"] [Text] Bratislava--Michal Kovac, president of the Slovak Republic, appointed Stefan Condik chairman of the Slovak Statistical Office. The newly appointed chairman of the Slovak Statistical Office took over the letter of appointment yesterday, in the presence of Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik. The president of the Slovak Republic stressed that the Statistical Office chairman has the professional qualifications to execute this important post. He drew attention to the fact that, in the process of transformation, the government and other central bodies require statistical data of high quality.
