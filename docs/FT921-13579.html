920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Lloyds signs bilateral deal with Spanish bank LLOYDS BANK and Banco Bilbao Vizcaya of Spain are to allow their business customers access to each other's national branch networks. BBV is the fifth European bank with which Lloyds has signed a bilateral agreement. It already has similar arrangements in France, Italy, Germany, and the Netherlands. Lloyds said that by using the branch networks offered by selected banks, it would be able to give its business customers full banking services throughout the EC. The agreement gives them access to 1,900 BBV branches in Spain. Lloyds already has a 19-branch network there Business customers of Lloyds will be able to open accounts, obtain advice on local business practices, and other services from banks with bilateral agreements. The arrangements do not operate multilaterally between Lloyds' partners.
