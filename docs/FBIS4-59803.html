BFN [Text] The strategic area of Yerganik is completely in the hand of the forces of the Islamic Army, and the mojahedin are defending it confidently. Quoting a responsible security source, a BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY correspondent reports: After their consecutive defeats in the northern provinces of the country, the militia recently mounted a failed assault on the Yerganik Pass. As a result of the bravery of the Islamic Army units and the mojahedin, the attack was repelled and heavy loss of life and damage was inflicted on the aggressors. The report adds: The militia began their attack at a time when Islamic Army units had confronted the enemy with humiliating defeats in various parts of Baghlan Province and conquered their positions one after another.
