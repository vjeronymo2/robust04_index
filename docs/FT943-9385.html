940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Shirescot net asset value declines 5% Shires High-Yielding Smaller Companies Trust reported a fully diluted net asset value of 139.4p per ordinary share as at June 30, a decline of some 5 per cent on the year end value of 146.3p. The trust reported a 3.5 per cent fall in its total return for the six months, outperforming the FT-SE-A All-Share Index, which dropped 11.3 per cent over the same period but lagging the Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index, down just 1.7 per cent. Some Pounds 7.1m of June's Pounds 10.8m placing and offer offer of C shares had been invested by end-July. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 406,000 (Pounds 388,000) for fully diluted earnings of 2.79p (3.05p) per share. A second interim dividend of 1.2p brings the total to date to 2.4p; the directors reiterated their intention to pay not less than 5.4p for the full year.
