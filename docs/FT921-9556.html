920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Mid Wynd Intl net assets ahead Net asset value per share of the Mid Wynd International Investment Trust rose from 233.3p to 279.4p over the year to December 31, having risen to 290.5p at June 30. The net asset value rose by a further 5.8 per cent to 295.5p in the month to end-January. Available revenue for the half-year to December 31 was little changed at Pounds 207,743 (Pounds 212,127) and earnings amounted to 4.14p (4.22p) per share. The interim dividend is lifted to 2.4p (2.3p) and directors hope to recommend 'some increase' in the final - 3.4p was paid previously.
