BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al-Maa [May] 5, JAMAHIRYAH NEWS AGENCY -- 'Ali Salim Al-Bid the vice-president of the Presidential Council in sisterly Yemen made a telephone call with the leader of the revolution expressing his concern for the development of the situation in Yemen. Following the call, the leader of the revolution made a telephone contact with President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih asking for details of the situation in Yemen. The Yemen president debriefed the leader regarding the situation in Yemen and assured him on the situation there. Contacts still continue between the leader of the revolution, President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice-President 'Ali Salim Al-Bid to contain the situation and preserve the unity and stability of Yemen.
