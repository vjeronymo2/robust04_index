June 16, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MEMORIAL MASS TO BE HELD FOR FRAN ALLISON

A memorial Mass for television actress Fran Allison will be held at 10 a.m. Saturday at St. Francis de Sales Church in Sherman Oaks. Conductor and entertainment personality Frank de Vol will deliver the eulogy.

The star of "Kukla, Fran & Ollie" and many other television and radio shows was 81 when she died Tuesday.
