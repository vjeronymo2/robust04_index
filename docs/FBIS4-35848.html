BFN [Text] Mir Murtaza Bhutto, member of Sindh assembly and brother of Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto, was granted bail in Shah Bandar case by the special court for speedy trial consisting of Mr. Ali Ahmad Junejo in Karachi today. The court, while granting bail, directed Mir Murtaza Bhutto to furnish two sureties of 500,000 rupees each. It also bound him not to leave the country until the trial of the case is completed.
