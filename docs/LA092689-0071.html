September 26, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: CANADIAN DISTRIBUTOR TO SELL TRUVEL PRODUCTS

Truvel Corp., a Chatsworth-based manufacturer of computerized scanning equipment, said a Canadian computer-products distributor agreed to sell Truvel's products in Canada.

The agreement with McKenzie Brown, which will likely cover $1 million in Truvel products over the coming year, is Truvel's first venture in Canada, the company said.
