Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] After a massive artillery preparation approximately at 6:00 a.m. on January 19, the Azerbaijani forces started an offensive along the whole front line, namely from the Omar mountain pass in the north to the village of Goradiz near the Iranian border in the south, the press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh republic's press service [as received] reported to INTERFAX. The largest formations are being waged against the positions of the Karabakh army near the regional center of Mardakert where the Azerbaijani party used artillery, about twenty tanks, and other armored vehicles. As of 10:00 the Karabakh forces had still managed to restrain the attack. The press center also reported that on Wednesday morning Karabakh fighters waging hostilities near the populated center of Karakhanbeili in the southeastern direction witnessed a fight between two Azerbaijani battalions with the support of artillery which lasted for an hour. The reasons for this armed clash in Stepanakert are still unknown. Meanwhile, a source in Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry reported an attack of the Karabakh forces in the early morning hours of January 19 against the positions of the Azerbaijani army in the north of Nagorno-Karabakh's Mardakert region. One APC of the Armenian formations was destroyed during the fight. On Tuesday night and Wednesday morning the fight was also going on in the south of Azerbaijan's Fizuli region where the Azerbaijani detachments still managed to rebuff attacks of the Karabakh fighters.
