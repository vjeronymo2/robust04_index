940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Beverley Shares of Beverley Group, the engineering company which is the subject of a proposed reverse take-over by the Hong Kong-based Far East group, were suspended at 6 1/2 p yesterday, pending full details of the deal. The company is proposing to purchase a 51 per cent stake in a Chinese boiler manufacturer from Far East Consortium, in exchange for new shares, which would give the Hong Kong company up to 70 per cent of Beverley.
