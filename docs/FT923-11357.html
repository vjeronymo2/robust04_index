920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Call to end rundown of mines A MORATORIUM on the further rundown of Britain's deep mines was urged yesterday by politicians, trades unionists and industry experts in the north-east as part of a campaign to highlight the strategic importance of coal as an energy source. A conference at Bedlington, Northumberland, organised by the independent Regional Energy Foundation, called for an EC ban on the dumping of cheap imports from outside the community.
