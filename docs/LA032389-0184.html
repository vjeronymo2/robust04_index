March 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PREP BASKETBALL ALL-STAR TEAMS

The Times 1989 San Gabriel Valley boys and girls high school All-Star basketball teams are featured on Page 12 of today's edition.

All players will be invited to The Times High School Basketball Awards brunch at 9 a.m. April 2 at the Anaheim Hilton, 777 Convention Way. Each will receive a trophy and certificate. Players and coaches of the year for each of The Times 12 circulation areas will be announced.

The teams were chosen by a poll of area coaches. George Yardley, former college and professional star, will speak.
