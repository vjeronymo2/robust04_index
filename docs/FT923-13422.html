920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / Observer: Healthy sceptic Absent-mindedly, a man steps off the pavement and is knocked down by a bus. As he lies there, clasping his injured leg, a would-be Samaritan jumps off the bus and comes to his assistance. 'Don't worry,' he says, 'I can help you; I work in the health service.' 'That's all very well,' the casualty replies, 'but are you a purchaser or a provider?'
