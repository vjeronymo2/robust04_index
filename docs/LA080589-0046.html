August 5, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

PANAMA SCHOOLS CLOSED AFTER DEATH OF STUDENT

Schools and universities closed down in Panama on Friday for at least three days after the death of a student in a clash with Panamanian riot police.

The Education Ministry said public and private schools will be closed until further notice, and classes were suspended at universities for three days.

The 24-year-old student died of gunshot wounds in the chest Thursday when riot police charged dozens of demonstrators protesting against calls for new elections.
