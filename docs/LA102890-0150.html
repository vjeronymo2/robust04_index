October 28, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

RACQUETBALL DUO MOVES TO FINAL

The team of Anthony Buccino and Chad Solter advanced to today's A Division final of the American Amateur Racquetball Assn.'s national doubles championships in Salt Lake City.

Buccino, of West Hills, and Solter, of Chatsworth, both 19, on Saturday defeated George Dodson and David Morris of Wilmington, N.C., in a tiebreaker match, 13-15, 15-7, 11-3. They will face Mark Bustos of Burlingame and Americo Amat of Miami this afternoon.
