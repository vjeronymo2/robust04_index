941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Strength in gold and other metal shares was not enough to keep the Toronto market from posting a slight loss in moderate trading. The TSE 300 index ended 5.4 easier at 4,327.0 and declines led rises by 415 to 308. Volume was 45.2m shares valued at CDollars 600m. Remarks by the federal finance minister Mr Paul Martin reiterating the government's commitment to tackling the country's deficit problem lent some strength to the Canadian dollar.
