920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Drinks-can plant for Istanbul CarnaudMetalbox, the Franco-British packaging group, is building a Dollars 40m (Pounds 20.9m) drinks-can plant in Turkey to take advantage of the fast-growing drinks-can market there, writes William Dawkins in Paris. The greenfield plant, to open at Izmit, near Istanbul, next summer with an initial capacity of 500m cans a year, will double Turkey's drinks-can making capacity.
