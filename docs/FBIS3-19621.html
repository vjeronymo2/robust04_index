Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis described the decisions made by the Greek and Cypriot Defense Ministries as significant steps toward strengthening Cyprus' defense. In a statement this afternoon, after meeting Greek and Cypriot Defense Ministers Yerasimos Arsenis and Konstandinos Iliadhis, who briefed him on the decisions made, Kliridhis described the work that has been made as significant. He added that he is happy because Cyprus' defense is safeguarded by the decisions made. He also expressed his respect for the Greek prime minister and defense minister for implementing the joint defense doctrine. Arsenis said he was satisfied because the agreement is being realized. He added that measures have been taken to strengthen Greek-Cypriot defense cooperation in all sectors. He added that the two ministers agreed to meet regularly to follow developments in the implementation of the decisions. The National Defense General Staff and the National Guard General Staff will establish a joint committee to examine and suggest measures about the plans according to the situation at that time. He said that many of the measures that have been decided during the talks cannot be announced. Iliadhis said the National Guard will be reinforced with new members and new arms systems. He added that National Guard members will participate in joint military maneuvers.
