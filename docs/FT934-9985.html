931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / Observer: Ticked off Good news for the unemployed lining the streets of Washington. Now all those high-earning Republicans thrown out of their jobs with the defeat of George Bush can count the minutes till the end of the (first?) Clinton presidency. All they need is an Apple Mac computer, and a program called Siegewatch will display a caricature of the present incumbent together with a real-time countdown in years, months, days, hours and minutes, of exactly how long he has left in office.
