BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 2 June Morning Edition] [Text] A Foreign Ministry source on 1 June spoke in connection with the DPRK's (North Korea's) suspected nuclear arms program, and said: "We must think about providing an incentive to enable North Korea to soften its stance." Thus he confirmed that Japan is reviewing the possibility of providing multinational support to North Korea in cooperation with the United States, South Korea and international organizations if the country decides to accept all nuclear inspections.
