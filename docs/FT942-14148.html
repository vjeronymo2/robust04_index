940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Shires High-Yielding net assets rise Shires High-Yielding Smaller Companies Trust reported net asset value per share of 160.5p at March 31, compared with 155.6p at December 31 1993. The fully diluted figures were 150.5p and 146.3p. Net revenue for the three months to end-March was Pounds 126,000 (Pounds 97,000) for earnings of 1.01p (0.78p). A first interim of 1.2p (1p) is declared.
