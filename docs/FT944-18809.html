941001 FT 01 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: October, a taxing time - At a glance You have until the end of the month to fill in your tax return for 1993-94. The forms should be returned by October 31 to avoid the risk of being charged interest at 6.25 per cent on any outstanding tax. Once the Revenue receives the form, it will send out an assessment and you have 30 days to appeal against it.
