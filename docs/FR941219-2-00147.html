ACTION: Notice of Application for an order under the Investment Company Act of 1940 (the ``1940 Act''). APPLICANTS: Providentmutual Life and Annuity Company of America (``Providentmutual''), Providentmutual Variable Life Separate Account (the ``Account''), and PML Securities Company (``PML''). (Providentmutual, the Account, and PML shall be referred to herein collectively as ``Applicants.'') RELEVANT 1940 ACT SECTIONS: Order requested under Section 6(c) of the 1940 Act for exemptions from Section 27(c)(2) of the 1940 Act and Rule 6e&hyph;3(T)(c)(4)(v) thereunder.

SUMMARY OF APPLICATION: Applicants seek an order permitting them and other separate accounts that Providentmutual may establish in the future to support flexible premium variable life insurance contracts (the ``future accounts'') to deduct from premium payments received an amount that is reasonable in relation to Providentmutual's increased federal tax burden resulting from the application of Section 848 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended.
