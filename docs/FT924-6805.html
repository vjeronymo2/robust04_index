921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: ACT offer extended by two weeks The date when ACT's offer for NMW Computers becomes unconditional has been extended by two weeks to December 2, it emerged last night, after the Office of Fair Trading said it was still considering the implications of the deal. At issue is the share of the UK market for stock processing software - estimated at between 20 per cent and 25 per cent - which would be held by the merged company.
