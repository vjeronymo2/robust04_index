Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Army units in Tauzskiy Rayon resumed shelling of border positions in Tavushskiy Rayon yesterday evening. The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service reported that tank, howitzer, and grenade fire was directed at Artzvaberd, Aygepar, and Chinari. The shelling lasted for 15 minutes. There were no casualties. No serious incidents have taken place in the other border regions of Armenia since last night. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army headquarters reported that no substantial changes took place on the war fronts last night and this morning. The Karabakh Army units successfully repulsed all attacks by the enemy in the northern region of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. The Azerbaijani troops suffered substantial losses in men and material. The Azerbaijani Army launched yet another offensive at the southeastern sector of the front this morning. Only positional battles are taking place in the other sectors of the war front.
