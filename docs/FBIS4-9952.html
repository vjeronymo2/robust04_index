BFN [Text] Comrade Eugenio Cuevas Ibanez, who had an outstanding record as a student leader and member of the Union of Young Communists [UJC] and the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC], has died at the age of 45, victim to a painful illness. Among the many positions he held, Cuevas was the first UJC secretary in Santiago de Cuba, a professional member of the Santiago de Cuba provincial PCC bureau, and first secretary of the PCC municipal committee in Palma Soriano. In 1992, he was elected first secretary of the Guantanamo provincial committee, a position he held as long as his health condition permitted. At the time of his death, Cuevas was a member of the Central Committee of the PCC and a deputy of the National Assembly of the People's Government. His body can be viewed at the Bartolome Funeral Home in Santiago de Cuba. He will be buried today, 5 April, at the Santa Efigenia Cemetery at 1400 GMT.
