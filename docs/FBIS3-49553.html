Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The border guard wishes to obtain at least 100 armored carriers from China in exchange for China's state debts. The vehicles would be given to the 19 border patrol companies. The Nikex joint stock company, which has been commissioned by the Ministry of the Interior to conclude the deal, announced that a Hungarian delegation is to travel to Beijing in the next few days in order to gather information. China owes Hungary over seven billion forints.
