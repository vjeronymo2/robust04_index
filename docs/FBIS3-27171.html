Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] A seven-member government delegation from the Kherson Oblast of the Republic of Ukraine headed by (Valinikov Aleksandr Gikhonovich), head of the oblast, paid a friendly visit to our province recently. The major purpose of the visit was to explore the possibility for the province and the oblast to establish friendly ties. Provincial Governor Zhao Zhihao cordially met with the delegation at the provincial government office on the afternoon of 21 February. He extended a welcome to the delegation, saying: Shandong and Kherson have achieved substantial results in their economic and trade cooperation over the past few years. They have also proved highly capable of mutual economic cooperation and have displayed the tremendous potential and bright prospects for strengthening cooperation and exchanges. It is hoped that both sides will conduct further exchanges and cooperation in more fields in the future. During the meeting, Zhao Zhihao, governor of Shandong, and (Gikhonovich), head of Kherson Oblast, signed an agreement on the exchanges and cooperation between Shandong Province and Kherson Oblast. Provincial Vice Governor Song Fatang also held a working discussion with the delegation on the same day.
