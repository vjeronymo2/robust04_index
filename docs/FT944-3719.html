941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Arts (Openings): Leeds Bayreuth's resident Wotan turns up in a new guise in Leeds on Thursday. As well as singing the title-role, John Tomlinson will be making his debut as a director in Opera North's rare production of Verdi's first opera, 'Oberto'. David Porcelijn is the conductor and the cast also includes Rita Cullis, Linda Finnie and David Maxwell-Anderson.
