931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Charge takes toll on US West A ONE-OFF charge of Dollars 3.2bn helped drive US West, the 'Baby Bell', to a third-quarter loss of Dollars 3.55bn, or Dollars 8.50 a share, compared with a profit of Dollars 266m, or 65 cents a share, in the year-earlier period.
