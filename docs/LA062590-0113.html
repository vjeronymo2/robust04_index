June 25, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

SIEBE $656 MILLION BUYS FOXBORO

Siebe PLC, a British security systems engineering group, has agreed to acquire Foxboro Co., a U.S.-based manufacturing equipment maker, for $656 million, the companies said today.

Siebe is offering $52 a share for Foxboro's 12.62 million outstanding shares, the companies said.

Foxboro stock soared on the news, rising $12.75 to $50.87 1/4 a share in early New York Stock Exchange trading.
