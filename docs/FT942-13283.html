940426 FT 26 APR 94 / People: Lloyds Bank Sir David Walker, 54, deputy chairman of Lloyds Bank since 1992 and chairman of the Securities and Investments Board between 1988-92, has been appointed a non-executive director of Reuters Holdings. Sir David was in the treasury between 1961-77 and was on secondment to the IMF in Washington between 1970-73. He became chief adviser to the Bank of England in 1977, rising to the post of executive director, finance and industry.
