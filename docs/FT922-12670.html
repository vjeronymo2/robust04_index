920416 FT 16 APR 92 / UK Company News: Hanson sells off Beazer interests Hanson, the Anglo American conglomerate, has sold the Australian construction and property interests of Beazer, its wholly-owned subsidiary, for ADollars 32.5m (Pounds 14.4m) cash. The interests, primarily in New South Wales and Queensland, have been bought by B&B Asia, a Hong-Kong-quoted company controlled by Bilfinger and Berger, the German construction company.
