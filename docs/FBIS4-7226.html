BFN [Text] On 20 April, the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea and the people cooperated in attacking the 79th Battalion of the 4th Division in Sdau township on Route 10, killing five and wounding a number of others. We seized 18 AK rifles and a big truck. Also on 20 April, we attacked and completely liberated a position of the communist Vietnamese puppets in Andaeuk Hep, seizing three AK's five AR 15 rifles, 10 SK rifles, 25 hand grenades, and captured 12 policemen and commune officials.
