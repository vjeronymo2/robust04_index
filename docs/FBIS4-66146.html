BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Jeszenszky stated that representatives of the Hungarians of Slovakia and Romania must also be involved in the preparatory discussions of the Paris conference on stability in Europe. His comments came at his address to the conference. Jeszenszky said: the Hungarian delegation will be formulating a separate opinion in the form of an explanatory declaration, which will call attention to the fact that the three million Hungarians living outside Hungary are not represented by anyone at the Paris talks.
