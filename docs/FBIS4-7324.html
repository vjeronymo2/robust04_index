BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by Colonel Aung Thaung, deputy minister of trade, returned to Yangon [Rangoon] by air at 1500 this afternoon after attending the 75th Anniversary Spring Festival held in Guangzhou, People's Republic of China [PRC]; and the PRC Machinery and Electronics Fair'94, held in Shenzen. The delegation was welcomed at Yangon Airport by Lieutenant General Tun Kyi, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of trade, and other responsible personnel.
