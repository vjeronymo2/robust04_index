BFN [Text] A reporter of the VIETNAM NEWS AGENCY based in Moscow reveals that on 27 May, Russia's Federal Immigration Agency and the Vietnamese Businessmen Association in Russia signed a cooperation agreement on the employment of foreign workers in Russia and Vietnam. According to the agreement, Russia's Federal Immigration Agency and (?Vinencaso) will help each other in resolving issues such as attracting and employing Russian and Vietnamese workers in their respective countries. (?Vinencaso) will cooperate with the Russia's Federal Immigration Agency to create conditions to attract and employ Russian and Vietnamese workers in companies and enterprises which Vietnam businessmen helped to build up. The Russia's Federal Immigration Agency will help (?Vinencaso) find Vietnamese workers for enterprises that are operating or in the process of rebuilding in Russia, and will send Russian workers to Vietnamese enterprises and agencies upon the request of (?Vinencaso). This agreement does not affect other agreements signed in this field between the Russian and Vietnamese state authorities.
