Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] DPRK and U.S. delegates are now holding discussions at Panmunjom onlocating the remains of airmen who fell to their death from the skies of Korea during the 1950-1953 war. According to a DPRK statement, the discussions will be held for two days. Welcoming the fact that Pyongyang has agreed to the discussions, the United States expects that this will contribute to improving relations between the United States and the DPRK.
