940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Appd Holographics Applied Holographics, the USM-quoted producer of holographic products for security and packaging uses, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 276,395 for the year to the end of March. Thiscompared with losses of Pounds 2.26m last time, came on turnover ahead to Pounds 5.8m (Pounds 4.96m) despite a fall to Pounds 700,066 (Pounds 1.37m) in hot stamping foil sales. Losses per share were 1.3p (losses of 11.2p). The company's deficit now stands at Pounds 17.5m.
