BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Apr (MENA)--The Higher State Security Court-Emergency issued a ruling today in the case of the al-Fayyum rioting. It sentenced the following five defendants to seven years of hard labor: 'Umar Ahmad 'Abd-al-Rahman, 'Ali al-Sayyid 'Abd-al-Jayyid, Naji Sayyid 'Abdallah, Nabil 'Ali Jum'ah, and Khalid 'Abd-al-Majid Muhammad. The court also sentenced 16 defendants to five years of hard labor and five other defendants to three years of hard labor. It acquitted 21 defendants and dropped criminal cases against two defendants because they had died.
