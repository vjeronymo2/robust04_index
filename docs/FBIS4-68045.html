BFN [Text] We have learned from a reliable source that Jean-Verdy Bastien, owner of the Celeste Borlette Banks [lottery ticket offices] and of the private radio station, Celeste FM, was assassinated on the night of 4 May at about 2200 in upper Delmas. His driver was also injured during the attack against him by a group of armed men who had set up an ambush. According to our source, Jean-Verdy Bastien's body was transported to the general hospital morgue.
