941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / International Company News: South Africa plans global bond in dollars South Africa has taken an important step to return to the international capital markets with the announcement that it plans a global bond denominated in US dollars jointly managed by Goldman Sachs, the US securities house, and Swiss Bank Corporation, writes Martin Brice in London. Goldman Sachs has been acting as financial adviser to the South African government this year during the rating process and the filing in the US of the Dollars 2bn 'shelf registration statement', which means the Republic may issue up to that amount in bonds.
