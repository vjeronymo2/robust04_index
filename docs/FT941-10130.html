940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Former terrorist chief McGlinchey killed in ambush Dominic McGlinchey, 40, the former terrorist chief whose brutality earned him the nickname Mad Dog, was shot dead by three gunmen in an ambush at Drogheda, County Louth, in the Irish republic last night. Mr McGlinchey had been leader of the outlawed Irish National Liberation Army, a breakaway faction from the IRA, and boasted of taking part in 30 murders. He was released from the republic's Portlaoise jail last year after serving seven years of a 10-year sentence for firearms offences. Pressure on Sinn Fein stepped up, Page 10
