BFN [Text] Auckland, June 23 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese Economic System Reform delegation led by State Councillor Li Tieying left here this morning at the end of its six-day visit to New Zealand. The delegation, which flew to Melbourne to start its visit to Australia, arrived here Wednesday morning. Wednesday, the delegation visited the Fay Richwhite Company and the Fletcher Challenge Company. Officials of the two companies briefed the Chinese visitors about the set-up of their organizations and how they manage their business. During its stay in NZ [New Zealand], the delegation headed by Li, also minister of the State Commission for Restructuring the Economic Systems, had extensive contacts with NZ officials and business people to exchange views on the reforms in China and New Zealand.
