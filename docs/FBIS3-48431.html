Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- The chairwoman of the Central Guidance Committee of the Korean Chondoist Association [KCA], Yu Mi-yong, today sent a telephone message to O Ik-che, ordinary of the South Korean Central Office of Chondoism, proposing a working-level contact between representatives of Chondoists of the North and the South to discuss the joint celebration of the centenary of the peasants' war in 1894. The message said that the joint celebration by the North and the South of the anniversary of the war waged under the slogan "Reject the West and Japan" and "Promote national interests and welfare of the people" would be of weighty significance in opposing outside forces and their lackeys, promoting national harmony and unity and accomplishing the noble cause of reunification in the '90s, inheriting the spirit of national independence and the patriotic spirit displayed by Chondoists and other participants in the war. The message said three delegates led by Han Yong-su, vice-chairman of the Central Guidance Committee of the KCA, would be sent to the conference room of the neutral nations supervisory commission at Panmunjom at 10:00 on February 3 to discuss the matter with the South side.
