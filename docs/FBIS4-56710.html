BFN ["Report" by Panamanian Electoral Tribunal President Eduardo Valdes Escoffery from the Atlapa Convention Center in Panama City -- live] [Text] In the number of voting sites accounted for, an abstention rate of 27.48 percent has been reported. Therefore, 72.52 percent of the registered voters participated in the elections. This is the breakdown of 11.8 percent of voting sites tallied. You will be given more details, for consultation purposes, as soon as we finish giving this report. Candidate Ernesto Perez Balladares of the United People Alliance received 32.8 percent of the valid votes. Next comes Mireya Moscoso de Gruber of Democratic Alliance with 30.12 percent. Ruben Carles of the Change '94 Alliance comes in third with 16.92 percent. Ruben Blades of the Papa Egoro Movement placed fourth with 15.89 percent. Edy Vallarino of the Christian Democratic Party comes in fifth with 2.54 percent. Sammy Lewis Galindo of Concertacion Nacional attained 1.44 percent and occupies sixth place. Jose Salvador Munoz of the Doctrinaire Panamenista Party brings up the rear with 0.21 percent. These figures will be made available in a few minutes through the television system we have set up for you. This information will be updated approximately every 10 minutes from now on via the systems you people have been trained to consult. Thank you.
