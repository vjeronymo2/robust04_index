Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Customs Department scored two big successes today in combating the international network of narcotics trafficking. At Kipoi, Evros, a car belonging to German citizens who entered from Turkey was inspected, and small packages of heroin totaling 37 kg were found and confiscated. In Khania, customs officials found and confiscated 442 kg of Indian cannabis after searching a British yacht coming from Yemen.
