August 10, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV/VIDEO

Best Home Videos: VCR lovers, the favorite video flicks of video business insiders are in. The winners of the Video Software Dealers Assn.'s 1990 Homer Awards are "Rain Man," "Look Who's Talking," "Lethal Weapon 2," "Honey, I Shrunk the Kids" and "Dead Calm." Arnold Schwarzenegger was voted star of the year. BETH KLEID
