BFN [Statement issued by the ANC on the SAPA PR Wire Service on 26 April] [Text] ANC President Nelson Mandela will cast his vote at 7.30 AM at the Ohlange High School outside Durban. The school was founded by the first president of the ANC, John Dube. The late Comrade Dube is also buried in Ohlange. Provisions have been made for 1,200 journalists to cover the event. We appeal to the press to be there and ready by 7 AM.
