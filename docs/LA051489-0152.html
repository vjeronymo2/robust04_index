May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JAPANESE INVESTMENT

Catherine Collins' article is to the point and she is absolutely right. She should be commended for her fortitude in expressing publicly a notion that is very unpopular with the real estate industry and, alas, our government.

In an article of mine, published in the January, 1989, issue of the National Real Estate Investor magazine, I expressed similar views.

As a concerned citizen, I followed up by writing to some of our elected government officials asking them to give this point of view serious thought and consideration. So far, it would appear, my appeal fell on deaf ears.

I hope Collins' article might achieve better results.

ALEX LISTON

Los Angeles

Liston is president of Continental Brokerage Co. Inc.
