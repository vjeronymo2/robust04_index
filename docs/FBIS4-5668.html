BFN [Text] Cairo, April 12 KYODO -- Egypt's President Husni Mubarak is canceling a scheduled visit to Japan later this month because of the resignation of Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, the Middle East News Agency reported Tuesday [12 April]. Mubarak was scheduled to visit Japan as a state guest from April 18 to 21. The Egyptian News Service said he will visit China starting April 21 as originally scheduled. In Tokyo, a Foreign Ministry official said Japan has not received any official notice of the cancellation of Mubarak's visit.
