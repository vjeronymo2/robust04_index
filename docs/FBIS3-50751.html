Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Army commander in chief, General Augusto Pinochet, today visited Defense Minister-designate Edmundo Perez Yoma in his home together with four major generals. The visit was qualified as a protocol visit. Today at noon, Perez Yoma went to the office of President-elect Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle on Hindemburg Street to inform him about his meeting with the Army commander. It was a cordial meeting, Perez Yoma said, adding that he told Pinochet of his intention to maintain extremely good relations with the military during the next administration. [Begin recording] [Unidentified reporter] Gen. Pinochet never paid a visit to Defense Minister Rojas. What do you think of this? [Perez Yoma] I did not know that, and I greatly appreciate the fact that Gen. Pinochet, together with four other generals, visited me in my home and presented these major generals to me. So I am very happy about it. [end recording] Perez Yoma was asked whether the visit was a demonstration of Gen. Pinochet's subordination to political power. [Begin Perez Yoma recording] I think Gen. Pinochet has demonstrated he is willing to submit to the political power. I think his visit expresses his good intentions regarding this subject. [end recording]
