BFN [Text] [Announcer] A red phone, Moncloa-Kremlin, hot line linking Madrid and Moscow is to be one of the improvements in bilateral ties with Russia that is being prepared 70 hours before Boris Yeltsin's first official visit to Spain. Over to Maria Jesus Chao at the Council of Ministers in Moncloa Palace. [Chao] The Council of Ministers has agreed to install a direct confidential telephone line linking Moncloa Palace to the Kremlin, as well as the signing of seven agreements between Spain and Russia. The occasion is Boris Yeltsin's official visit to our country. These agreements are contained in the friendship and cooperation treaty between both countries and are meant to strengthen economic, industrial, cultural, educational, and environmental cooperation. They will also cover social security and labor relations issues.
