Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 17 Feb -- Valeriy Kazakov, Ukrainian deputy industry and military industrialization minister, announced here today that the Ukraine parliament approved the opening of a Ukrainian Embassy in Kuwait. Kazakov, who is visiting Kuwait at the invitation of an international Kuwait exhibition company, said that "this decision received the full approval of Kuwaiti high-ranking officials, particularly in the Kuwaiti Foreign Ministry."
