921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / North-west business boost INWARD, the regional development agency for north-west England, said 17 businesses invested Pounds 17m in moving to the region in the six months to September 30, creating or safeguarding 1,600 jobs. Mr Tom Weatherby, chairman, said it was the agency's most successful six months. In spite of difficult worldwide conditions, projects under consideration rose 12 per cent on the same period last year.
