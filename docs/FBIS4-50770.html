BFN [By reporter Wan Chengcai (8001 2052 2088)] [Text] Vladivostok, 20 May (XINHUA) -- Admiral Kasatonov, first deputy commander in chief of the Russian Navy, had a meeting today with Vice Admiral Wang Jiying, commander of the Chinese Navy's North Sea Fleet, and principal members of his entourage, who are currently here on a goodwill visit. The meeting took place on board the "Changxingdao," the flagship of the Chinese Naval Fleet. During the one-hour meeting, Kazatonov and Wang Jiying recalled the friendly contacts between the Chinese and Russian Navies, and expressed hope that friendship and cooperation between the peoples and armed forces of the two countries would continue to develop. Present at the meeting were Deynekin, commander of the Russian Naval Air Force; Khmelnov, acting commander of the Russian Navy's Pacific Fleet; and Lieutenant General Cong Guimin, ground, naval, and air force attache of the Chinese Embassy in Russia. The Chinese naval fleet, headed by Wang Jiying, arrived in Vladivostok (Haisenwei) on 17 May for a goodwill visit.
