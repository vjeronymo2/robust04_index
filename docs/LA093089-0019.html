September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A RAIDER MOVE WOULD BENEFIT TV VIEWERS

It really upsets me that some Ram home games will not be seen on TV, even if the Rams sell out 72 hours before game time.

Why not? Because we are forced to see an away Raider game. I hope the schedule-makers think about what they are doing before they make this mistake again.

Another good solution would be for the Raiders to move.

BENJAMIN KARTER, La Palma
