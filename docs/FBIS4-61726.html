BFN [Text] The fulfillment of the state plan for supplying Ukraine with electric energy in 1994 is impossible due to a number of reasons. This is the essence of a statement by managers of Ukraine's nuclear energy complex, which was made public yesterday. The statement notes that there are no funds for purchasing nuclear fuel and spare parts. According to the authors of the statement, an urgent decrease in tariffs on electric energy and a state credit for the Ministry of Energy for repaying the debt of nuclear power plants are necessary.
