Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Jovan Vlajkovic] [Text] As of 0800 tomorrow, one soldier of the Serbian army of Krajina and one member of the ministry of internal affairs of the republic of Serbian Krajina [RSK] together with the members of the Nepalese Battalion of the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] will control the crossing of the RSK state border on the Bridge of Serbian Unity. This is a result of an agreement between [UNPROFOR] General Jean Cot and General Milan Celeketic, reached today on the bridge itself. They also discussed other similar locations in the RSK; however, the details were left to future negotiations. According to General Jean Cot, the entire situation concerning the bridge should not have reached the dimensions it did. All future disputes will be resolved through talks. It is not the first time that I have talked with General Celeketic. Knowing him, I am sure that all misunderstandings will be resolved through negotiation.
