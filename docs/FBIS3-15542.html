Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Belarusian Army Is Being Reformed"] [Text] Lieutenant General Mikalay Churkin, first deputy Belarusian defense minister, stated at a session of the Defense Ministry Collegium that in 1992-93, more than 900 units of the Belarusian Armed Forces underwent a reform in which numerical strength corresponded to the quantitative parameters foreseen by international agreements for Belarus.
