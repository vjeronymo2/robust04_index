In the case of any conflict between the rules set forth in this subpart and the rules set forth in part 13 of this chapter or the rules set forth for specific services in parts 80 through 97 of this chapter (other than rules relating to Commercial Mobile Radio Services contained in part 90 of this chapter), the rules in this subpart shall govern. In the case of any conflict between the rules set forth in this subpart and the rules relating to Commercial Mobile Radio Services set forth in part 20 of this chapter or in part 90 of this chapter, the rules in part 20 or part 90 of this chapter shall govern. 4.
