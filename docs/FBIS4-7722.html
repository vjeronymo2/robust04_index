BFN [Text] News from Bosnia-Herzegovina: The Serbs committed a new crime in Usora today. Shrapnel from a grenade fired by the Serbs wounded 13 civilians who were lining up for humanitarian aid, the department for political activites of the 110th brigade of the Usora Croatian Defense Council has announced. Doctors in a hospital in Tesanj are fighting to save their lives. One child has already died from the shrapnel wounds. According to the same source, since 1300 this morning Serb artillery has been pounding the villages of the free Croatian community of Usora from several directions. A humanitarian aid convoy and the spiritual center of the Usora Croats -- the parish church of St. Anthony in Sivsa -- are coming under the fiercest attacks. Considerable material damage has been caused to residential buildings.
