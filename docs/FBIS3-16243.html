Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Vladimir Beskomnyy and Denis Perkin] [Text] Moscow, 15 Mar -- Russia and Latvia initialled today the treaty on the withdrawal of Russian troops from this Baltic republic. Sergey Zotov, the head of the Russian delegation at talks with Latvia, described the event to ITAR-TASS as "the victory of common sense", and added that the document "paves the way to a meeting between the Russian and Latvian presidents and to the signing of the prepared documents in the course of the summit meeting". In the opinion of the Russian diplomat, this meeting may take place as early as the first half of April this year.
