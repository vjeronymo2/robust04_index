Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Hanan Kamal] [Text] Angry demonstrations protesting the Hebron massacre have taken place in the Cairo universities for the second successive week. The demonstrations extended to regional universities, most notably Alexandria and al-Zaqaziq. In al-Zaqaziq University, the students of the Nasirist Ideology Club led a demonstration of thousands of students the day before yesterday protesting Arab inaction and the surrender of the PLO under 'Arafat. Last Thursday, at Alexandria University, the security forces stormed the campus and used shotguns, tear gas canisters, and electric clubs [al-'usi al-kahraba'iyah] in an attempt to disperse thousands of student demonstrators, wounding over 100 students, including eight who were taken to the university hospital and the Jamal 'Abd-al-Nasir Hospital.
