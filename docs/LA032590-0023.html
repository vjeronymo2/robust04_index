March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: CAL STATE L.A. TEAM WINS COLLEGE BOWL

Cal State L.A. has been awarded the Ann Reynolds Cup after placing first among California State University College Bowl teams in the Assn. of College Unions-International Region 15 College Bowl Match.

Students Robin Creason, Steve Pencall, Waheed Boctor and Mat Gleason competed with teams from 18 universities in California, Arizona and Hawaii.
