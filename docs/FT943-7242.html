940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Eastern net assets dip 10% Scottish Eastern Investment Trust reported net assets down 10 per cent at 95.3p as at July 31, against 105.4p six months earlier. The result, however, was 10 per cent higher than at the same stage in 1993. Net revenue for the six months dropped from Pounds 5.12m to Pounds 4.48m, while earnings per share came to 0.7p (0.8p). The interim dividend is held at 0.52p.
