BFN [Text] Bonn -- In the future, Germany will receive 99 instead of 81 seats in the European Parliament. The planned increase in the number of mandates after German unification has now been confirmed by the signatures of all 12 member states of the European Union, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) announced in Bonn today. The increase in the number of seats in the European Parliament, which is to be newly elected, will enter into force on 1 May.
