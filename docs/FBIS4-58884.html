BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 May (MENA)-- The Democratic Unionist Party today elected Ibrahim Muhammad 'Abd-al-Mun'im Turk as party chairman to replace Muhammad 'Abd-al-Mun'im Turk, who passed away recently. In a statement after his election today, Turk voiced support for President Mubarak's successful foreign policy, which helps the establishment of peace in the Middle East. He reiterated the party's support for the national dialogue called for by President Mubarak, noting that this call embodies the climate of democracy, freedom, and the supremacy of the law that prevails in Egypt.
