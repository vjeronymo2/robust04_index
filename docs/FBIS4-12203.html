BFN [Excerpt] Today at a meeting with Syrian People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah, Deputy Majles Speaker Ruhani stressed the necessity for strengthening the parliamentary ties between Iran and Syria to combat the conspiracies of the Zionist regime. According to a Central News Unit report from Damascus, at the meeting the deputy Majles speaker, who is heading a delegation in Damascus at the invitation of the Syrian speaker, referred to the fact the Iranian Majles deputies have always monitored closely the political developments in the Middle East. He said: We are currently following up the plan to set-up an inter-parliamentary body from Muslim countries. Some Islamic countries have declared their consent in the matter. At the meeting, in a speech, 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah praised the views of His Holiness Imam Khomeyni, the esteemed leader His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, and the president, as well as the stance taken by the Majles on Palestine. He described as wise the stance of the Islamic Republic of Iran on Palestine. Describing Syria's stance on the political issues of the Middle East and the conflict between the Arabs and the Zionist regime the Syrian speaker said: We try to not to forget our goals and principles. Until the occupied lands of the Arabs are returned to them there will not be peace in the region. [passage omitted]
