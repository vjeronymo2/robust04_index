930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Molyneux to buy out joint venture MOLYNEUX Estates, the USM-quoted property investment group, is paying Pounds 3.44m to buy out a joint venture with a subsidiary of Palmerston Holdings, another property company. To acquire Palmerston's share in the venture, which owns Overgate, an eight-acre shopping centre in Dundee, Molyneux will pay Pounds 500,000 and repay Pounds 2.89m in loans and interest advanced by Palmerston. Earlier this week, Palmerston put three of its other subsidiaries into receivership. Molyneux will fund the deal with an Pounds 8.73m placing and open offer of 18.2m new shares, fully underwritten by James Capel and John East and Partners. They are priced at 50p on a 1-for-1 basis for qualifying shareholders.
