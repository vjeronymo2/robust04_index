920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / International Company News: TWA registers improvement TRANS WORLD Airlines, the US carrier owned by Mr Carl Icahn and operating under the protection of Chapter 11 of the Bankruptcy Code, reported a Dollars 28.7m operating loss for July. This was significantly smaller than the Dollars 43.3m deficit seen in the previous month. At the after-tax level, however, TWA moved into the black, showing a Dollars 92.7m profit. This was due to proceeds from assets sales, which totalled Dollars 125.1m in July. In June, the net loss for the carrier was Dollars 50.3m. July revenues were Dollars 332.9m, against Dollars 304.8m in June.
