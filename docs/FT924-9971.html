921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Decline to Pounds 3m at Ocean Wilsons Following the July 1992 reconstruction via a scheme of arrangement, Ocean Wilsons Holdings, an investment holding company, released results for the six months to June 30. Pre-tax profits declined from Pounds 4.78m to Pounds 3.1m in spite of a rise in turnover from Pounds 40.1m to Pounds 43.1m. Earnings per share worked through at 3.57p (5.69); a gross interim dividend of 1p is declared.
