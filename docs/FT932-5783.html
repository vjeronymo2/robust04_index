930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / Observer: Burnside issue The Burnside call sign is clearly a passport to prominence in aviation public relations. No sooner has David, the erstwhile PR director of British Airways, set up David Burnside Associates, than fellow Ulsterman, Alan, of the confusingly named Alan Burnside Associates steps in to run PR for the sale of Belfast Airport. There are not a lot of Burnsides in Northern Ireland, but DB insists that he's not related to AB. They are not even members of the same lodge.
