Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime ministers of Pakistan and Turkey will visit Bosnia-Herzegovina on 2 February to express their solidarity with the oppressed people of Bosnia. This was stated by the Bosnian prime minister while talking to newsmen after his talks with Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto in Karachi this evening. The Bosnian prime minister said we attach great importance to this visit, because it is taking place at a time when we need to mobilize the international public opinion in favor of our just stand.
