Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 16 (CTK) -- Brigita Schmognerova, economist by profession, was appointed Slovak deputy prime minister today. Schmognerova was born on November 17, 1947, in Bratislava. She graduated from Bratislava's School of Economics (VSE). In 1974-1976 she completed her postgraduate studies at the Natural Science Faculty of Comenius University in Bratislava. Three years later she completed an external postgraduate studies at VSE with a thesis on economic sciences. In 1971-1976 Schmognerova worked at the VSE National Economy Faculty as an expert assistant. Since 1976 she has been employed at the Slovak Academy of Science Economics Institute as a scientific worker and chief of the Microeconomic Department. After 1989 she was briefly an adviser to a Slovak deputy prime minister and later a consultant in a number of commissions in the Economy Ministry. Schmognerova is a member of the Economic Club of the Democratic Left Party (SDL).
