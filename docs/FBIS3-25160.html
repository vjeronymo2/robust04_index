Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] has moved to take over control of broadcasting licenses from the government, and wants the Independent Broadcasting Authority to be in operation by the middle of next month. A member of the TEC Management Committee, Mr. Mac Maharaj, said there was concern about the way in which Home Affair Minister Danie Schutte had granted temporary licenses to 15 radio stations. He proposed that the TEC authorize the management committee to create a substructure to advise it on a process of short-listing applicants to the Independent Broadcasting Authority.
