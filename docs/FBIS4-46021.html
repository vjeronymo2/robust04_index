BFN [Text] The foreign minister, Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali, has said that the government is committed to nuclear nonproliferation without any discrimination. He said in Islamabad today that Pakistan will not accept any measures which only apply to Pakistan. He said Pakistan is willing to continue consultation with the United States within the framework of the ongoing dialogue on security and nonproliferation in South Asia and will give serious consideration to any specific proposals the United States may make in these consultations.
