931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / International Company News: Japan's suit makers ahead JAPAN'S discount suit makers saw profits for the six months to September surge as consumers turned to cheaper products due to the economic downturn. Aoyama Trading, the leading manufacturer and retailer of office workers' blue suits, reported a 34.5 per cent jump in non-consolidated pre-tax profits to Y12.2bn (Dollars 114m) on a 38.7 per cent rise in sales to Y76.6bn. Net profits soared by 41 per cent to Y7.2bn. Aoki International's pre-tax profits rose by 8.3 per cent to Y5.3bn, helped by an expansion in sales outlets. Half-year sales advanced by 10 per cent to Y36.1bn, while after-tax profits rose by 3.5 per cent to Y2.5bn.
