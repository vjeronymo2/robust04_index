BFN [Text] A Hungarian military delegation headed by Miklos (Csurgai), commander of Hungarian antiaircraft forces, is in Ukraine on a working visit. The visit's agenda includes meetings and consultations with Ukrainian counterparts and visits with Ukrainian antiaircraft units and subunits. The Ukrainian and Hungarian antiaircraft forces commanders, Lieutenant General Mykhaylo Lopatin and Major General Miklos (Csurgai), are expected to sign a relevant protocol on the results of the visit on 13 May.
