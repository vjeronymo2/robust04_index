920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / The Lex Column: Lonrho Mr Tiny Rowland has never been one to let public opinion influence his friendships with maverick leaders of obscure African countries. But even by his standards, the Pounds 177m issue of shares in Metropole Hotels to Libya's official investment agency is peculiarly insensitive. Although the deal suggests Metropole is in the books at a realistic value, it is difficult to imagine that the remaining two-thirds of the business will ever attract another buyer.
