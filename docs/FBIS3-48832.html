Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh AKP January 31 -- Acting State President and Chairman of National Assembly Chea Sim said that the Khmer Rouge had not showed its fair attitude towards national reconciliation. During a meeting with representative of Canadian diplomatic mission to Cambodia David Martin Colagott, Samdech Chea Sim said the faction still refused to recognise the Royal Government of Cambodia and conditions proposed by King Norodom Sihanouk on cease-fire, the faction's troops demobilisation and integration into the royal army, and the return of its controlled areas of Pailin and Preah Vihear to the royal government. He also told the Canadian diplomat that the Khmer Rouge also increased its strategy in order regain power in the country. The acting state president also expressed thanks to the Canadian government for its efforts and assistance to the peace process and national reconstruction of Cambodia. For his part, David Martin expressed pleasure over the unity between the two main parties of FUNCINPEC [National United Front for an Independent, Neutral, Peaceful, and Cooperative Cambodia] and the CPP [Cambodian People's Party] in the government, stressing that it was the firm aspiration for Cambodians.
