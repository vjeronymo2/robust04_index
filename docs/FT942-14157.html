940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: GGT backs Paris agency Gold Greenlees Trott, the advertising and marketing services group, is, through GGK/GGT Worldwide, its 80 per cent-owned offshoot, back-ing a new agency in Paris. GGK/GGT will hold 35 per cent of the new agency, which will be called BG/GGT, with the balance held by local management.
