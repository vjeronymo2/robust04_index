920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Olympics in Brief: Johnson eliminated US track star Michael Johnson, hot favourite for the 200 metres title, was eliminated in the first semi-final which was won by Frankie Fredericks of Namibia. Johnson, regarded as a virtual certainty to win gold before the Games began, ran a sluggish race and finished only sixth. Britain's John Regis came second behind American Mike Marsh in the other semi-final to win a place in the final. Linford Christie, in fifth place, failed to qualify.
