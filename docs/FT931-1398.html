930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Platinum closes at dollars 375.25 a troy ounce on London bullion market Platinum was fixed at closed at Dollars 357.25 a troy ounce on the London bullion market yesterday afternoon, up Dollars 3.25 on the day. Dealers said the Rustenburg news was continuing to support the market, although it was being seen more as a powerful signal than anything else. 'The news was good for Dollars 2 or Dollars 4, but there is some pretty stiff resistance at Dollars 358,' said one dealer.
