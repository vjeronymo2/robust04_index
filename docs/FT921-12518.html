920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Canary Wharf move by Masius OLYMPIA & York, the Canadian property developer, yesterday announced two letting deals at Canary Wharf in London's Docklands. D'Arcy Masius Benton & Bowles, a US-owned advertising group, has taken 90,000 sq ft while Maersk, a shipping group, has taken 38,000 sq ft.
