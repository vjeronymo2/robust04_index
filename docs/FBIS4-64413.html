BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, May 2 (A.A.) -- Deputy Prime Minister Murat Karayalcin said on Monday [2 May] that the government would evaluate reopening the Habur border gate which borders Turkey and Iraq. Karayalcin speaking to reporters at his arrival in southeastern Diyarbakir Province for studies on the Habur border gate which is near the township of Silopi in the province of Sirnak said reopening this border gate was very important for trade in the southeast.
