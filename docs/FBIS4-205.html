BFN [Text] The electoral commission has released a breakdown of the parliamentary constituencies and their candidates as well as presidential candidates to stand in the forthcoming elections. A press release issued by the commission said there are five presidential candidates, and these are: His Excellency the President Ngwazi [Paramount] Dr. Kamuzu Banda, representing the Malawi Congress Party; Mr. Chakufwa Chihana, representing the Alliance for Democracy, AFORD; Mr. Kampelo Kalua, representing the Malawi Democratic Party, MDP; Mr. Timothy Mangwazu, representing the Malawi National Democratic Party, MNDP; and Mr. Bakili Muluzi, representing the United Democratic Front, UDF. On parliamentary candidates, the electoral commission said there are 613 candidates in all and the breakdown is as follows: The MCP and UDF each has 177 candidates, AFORD has 162 candidates, the United Front for Multiparty Democracy, UFMD has 32, the MDP has 30, the MNDP has 11, the Congress for the Second Republic, CSR, has 6, and the Malawi Democratic Union, MDU, has 2 candidates. The commission also said there are 13 independent candidates.
