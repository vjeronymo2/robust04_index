Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, December 31 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin had a 30-minute talk with Lu Chakwan, a noted Chinese painter residing in France, here today. Earlier, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress Qiao Shi called on the 90-year-old painter in a hotel he is staying and spoke highly of his patriotism. Lu has announced his decision to donate part of his painting works and cultural relics to China. He is scheduled to leave for France on Monday.
