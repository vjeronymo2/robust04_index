Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 3 SAPA -- The Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front -- AVF] on Thursday [3 March] denied that there was "any talk" of a split in its ranks. AVF media secretary Stephan Maninger said in a statement: "The National Party media is usually inclined to reach new heights of the imagination shortly before the elections." The statement followed speculation that some rightwingers considered forming a breakaway organisation to contest the elections in defiance of those who persist in their hard-line stance to boycott the polls.
