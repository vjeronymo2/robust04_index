BFN [From BTA's 19 April "Press Review"] [Excerpt] Sofia, April 19 (BTA) -- All newspapers report that the trainload of UN military equipment bound for Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], crossed Bulgaria through Ruse and Sofia and is now in Kyustendil where the convoy is expecting an Austrian company to unload it and transfer it to Macedonia. The dailies report that the convoy had been robbed before it arrived in Bulgaria and publish photos of the train with the military equipment. [passage omitted]
