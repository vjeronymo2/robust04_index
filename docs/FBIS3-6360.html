Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 17 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent a sixtieth birthday table to Mun Kuk-chong, deputy director of the hospital with Comrade Chong Man-sok as its director. Over the past thirty odd years he has cured many people by traumatic and orthopedic treatment and in this course unsparingly donated his blood, flesh and bones. He has trained many medical workers, including five associate doctors, and wrote more than sixty medical books and university textbooks to help develop the medical science of the country. He became a merited doctor in 1985 and later was awarded the honorary title of People's Doctor and academic degrees of doctor of medicine and assistant professor.
