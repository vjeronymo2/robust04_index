Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, March 28 (SNARK) -- In the Mountainous Svanetia positioning battles went on on March 28. On March 27 Georgian home guards offered resistance to Abkhazians at the battle near Kvemo-Lata village. Losses of Abkhazian side were 10 people. According to the data of Georgian intelligence service the Abkhazian forces are strengthening their positions behind Inguri River. On the words of the wounded home guards Georgian villages are being shelled by "Grad" launcher. SNARK was told about it by Mr. Klikadze, a deputy head of the Georgian Defence Ministry's press-center.
