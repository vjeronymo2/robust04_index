930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: British Dredging hit by interest rate cuts British Dredging saw its pre-tax profit slump from Pounds 2.47m to Pounds 1.06m in 1992. It is cutting the final dividend to 2.6p for a total of 5.2p, against 7.4p. Earnings per share came to 4.26p (9.89p). Interest income dropped from Pounds 908,000 to Pounds 457,000, the result of the progressive drop in interest rates and the investment of Pounds 2.6m. Turnover improved to Pounds 33.7m (Pounds 32.6m). A breakdown of operating profits showed distribution of building materials Pounds 352,000 (Pounds 716,000) and ship repairing Pounds 81,000 (Pounds 110,000) with concrete products suffering losses of Pounds 107,000 (Pounds 105,000).
