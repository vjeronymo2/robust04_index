BFN [Text] The Yemeni Oil Company, department of gas stations, warns the gas station owners in all parts of the Republic of Yemen that they should strictly abide by the prices of the oil byproducts as they have always been and as follows: One liter of gasoline: six rials One liter of diesel oil: three rials. One liter of kerosene: three rials. Whoever violates this list will have his license withdrawn and the gas station will be closed. Strict legal measures will also be taken against him. The Company will launch a monitoring campaign on all stations in coordination with the security authorities to maintain the prices. The Company appeals to the brother citizens to cooperate and abide by the official price list and to immediately report any violations so that strict measures can be taken against the violators. All are requested to abide by these prices and cooperate.
