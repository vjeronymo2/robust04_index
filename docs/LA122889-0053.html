December 28, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: BEVERLY HILLS;

ADULT SCHOOL REGISTRATION

Registration is open for classes in art appreciation, English as a second language, tax planning, printmaking and other subjects in the Beverly Hills Adult School's winter term.

The classes are offered during the day and in the evening. One-day Saturday seminars include such topics as French pastry, astrology and "50 Romantic and Unusual Weddings."

Classes begin Jan. 8. Adults may preregister at 255 S. Lasky Drive from 8 a.m. to noon and 1 p.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday. Call (213) 277-4747 for more information.
