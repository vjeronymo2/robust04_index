930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Marks and Spencer moves into Austria Marks and Spencer is to enter the Austrian market by opening two franchised stores in Vienna in the next two months. The stores will sell lingerie, toiletries and cosmetics. The stores will be operated by Thomas Feldmann Handelsgesellschaft, a new venture formed by a member of the family which owns Albert Matzner, a leading Austrian women's wear retailer.
