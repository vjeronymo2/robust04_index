BFN [Text] The Council of State's Decree Law 147, approved on 21 April and endorsed [refrendado] by Comrade Fidel Castro, establishes a total of 27 ministries and five institutes to constitute henceforth the central administration of the state. The Council of State decree also creates ministries for new organizations or those experiencing name changes. These new ministries and their respective ministers are: Rosa Elena Simeon Negrin, Ministry of Science, Technology, and the Environment; Antonio Rodriguez Maurell, Ministry of Economy and Planning; Jose Luis Rodriguez Garcia, Ministry of Finances and Prices; Ignacio Gonzalez Planas, Ministry of Metallurgy and Electronics Industry; Francisco Linares Calvo, Ministry of Labor and Social Security; Osmany Cienfuegos Gorriaran, Ministry of Tourism; and Ernesto Melendez Bachs, Ministry of Foreign Investment and Economic Cooperation. Officials relieved because of the elimination of their organizations are: Sonia Rodriguez Cardona, minister president of the State Committee for Technical and Material Supply; Fidel Vascos Gonzalez, minister president of the State Committee for Statistics; Ramon Darias Rodes, minister president of the State Committee for Standardization; Arturo Guzman Pascual, minister president of the State Committee for Prices; Samuel Savariego Capuano, president of the National Automated Systems and Computing Technology Institute; and Rafael Sed Perez, president of the National Tourism Institute.
