May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

More GM Incentives: General Motors Corp. announced car and light truck incentive programs to include cash rebates ranging from $500 to $2,500 and an interest rate as low as 7.9%. The incentives, which vary from car to car and truck to truck, run until Aug. 2 for some Buick, Pontiac and Oldsmobile vehicles and either June 11 or July 9 for certain Chevrolet vehicles, the company said. The program, which extends incentives on some models, is effective today.
