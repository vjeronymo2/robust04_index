Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 13 (KCNA) -- A letter to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at an Indian national seminar of journalists on the chuche idea and the role of mass communication in the era of independence which was held in New Delhi. The letter says that Indian journalists held the seminar on the occasion of the 20th anniversary of the proclamation of the programme of modeling the whole society on the chuche idea by Comrade Kim Chong-il in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. It further says: Your excellency, an outstanding theoretician, broke a new ground in the ideological and theoretical development in the era of independence and has performed great exploits by formulating the revolutionary idea of the great leader his excellency President Kim Il-song as an integral system of idea, theory and method of chuche and further developing and enriching it. Your excellency, a great statesman in our times, have won deep respect and reverence of the world people by lighting the way ahead of mankind like a beacon with your most scientific insight into and wise analysis of the present international situation and international relations. The Indian journalists will lift up louder voices supporting the just cause of the Korean people and Korea's reunification and always advance side by side with the Korean journalists in the way of accomplishing the just cause in the future. The letter wishes Comrade Kim Chong-il a long life in good health.
