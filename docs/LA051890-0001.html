May 18, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PROPOSITION 111 PROS AND CONS AND ENDING TRAFFIC GRIDLOCK

Ironically, we are faced with a situation where gridlock isn't slowing us down but actually speeding up our arrival with disaster.

It is time we focus our energies towards solutions instead of creative ways to occupy our time while stuck in traffic.

I can't count how many times I've longed to be on a commuter train, reading, rather than inhaling pollutants from hundreds of cars around me. My eyes and lungs burn to the point of having to keep my windows shut.

Props. 111 and 108 are solutions that will produce noticeable results from our conscious efforts. I intend to vote yes on Props. 111 and 108.

ALYCIA ANDERSON

Thousand Oaks
