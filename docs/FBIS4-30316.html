BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 24 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received a delegation of the upper house of the Kingdom of Thailand led by speaker Michai Ruchuphan on a visit to Korea. Present there were Chairman Yang Hyong-sop and Vice-Chairperson Yo Yon-Ku of the Supreme People's Assembly [SPA], member of the SPA Standing Committee Yu Ho-chun and Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Cho Kyu-il. President Kim Il-song had a cordial and friendly talk with the guests. He arranged a luncheon for them.
