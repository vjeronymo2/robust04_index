Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] More and more citizens of Russia believe that the return to the communist past is possible, an opinion poll taken by the All-Russian Center of Public Opinion Studies (VCIOM) among 3,901 residents of cities and villages of the Russian Federation in mid- March shows. Thus, according to the data provided by VCIOM to INTERFAX, to the question "Will the Communist Party be able to become a real political force in Russia in future?" in May 1993 15 percent said "yes", in March 1994 -- 22 percent. The negative answer was given by 59 percent in May, and in March -- 45 percent. Those who found it difficult to answer were respectively 26 and 38 percent. The respondents were also asked: "Will the Communist Party be able to return to power in future?" Experts believe that they do not favor the restoration of the old regime.
