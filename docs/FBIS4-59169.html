BFN [Text] Jalal Talabani, secretary general of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], has addressed an appeal to the peshmerga forces in Kurdistan and the Kurdish masses. He stressed the need for unity and solidarity among the Kurdish people in general and between the PUK and the Kurdish Democratic Party in particular. Talabani said that making the democratic experience in Kurdistan a success and the survival and promotion of the government of Kurdistan is contingent upon the unity of the people's ranks. He emphasized that all resources must be devoted to protecting liberated Kurdistan and liberating the country from the dictatorship and establishing a federal democratic Iraq. He called on the struggling Kurdish parties to enhance their struggle and efforts to entrench peace and fraternity in dear Kurdistan.
