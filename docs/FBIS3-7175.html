Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] President Kim Yong-sam, meeting with ROK correspondents in Tokyo this morning, said that the remark made by the North Korean delegate at the inter-Korean working contact held last week threatening that North Korea is ready for war was premeditated and that he regards it as the remark of the highest person in authority in North Korea. President Kim Yong-sam said that he thinks China basically does not want North Korea to develop nuclear weapons since China does not want to be isolated as a member of international community. President Kim said that he will be able to have frank exchange of views with Chinese President Jiang Zemin through summit talks with him on the measures to cope with the critical North Korean nuclear issue.
