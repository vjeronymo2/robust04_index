Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 1 KYODO -- Former Ibaraki Governor Fujio Takeuchi pleaded innocent Tuesday [1 February] to a prosecution charge that he received a 10 million yen bribe from the country's leading contractor, Shimizu Corp., in 1992 while he was in office. Takeuchi, called to answer the charge at the Tokyo District Court, categorically denied receiving the money from Shimizu.
