November 4, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

NORTH VALLEY LEAGUE

San Fernando 19, Chatsworth 8 -- Michael Wynn completed seven of 11 passes for 72 yards and one touchdown and ran for another score to lead the Tigers in their 75th homecoming game.

Wynn's 28th career touchdown pass leaves him one shy of tying the school record held by Anthony Davis, the former USC star.

LaKarlos Townsend scored on a three-yard run in the second quarter for San Fernando (5-3, 5-0). Townsend gained 27 yards in six carries. San Fernando running back Johnny Carter led all rushers with 91 yards in 14 carries.

Chatsworth's lone score came on Kyle Beck's seven-yard touchdown in the fourth quarter. Beck rushed for 50 yards in 16 carries. Chatsworth (2-4-2, 1-4) was limited to 30 yards in total offense. Quarterback Brandon Ruedy had minus-42 yards in nine carries. TODD JACOBSON
