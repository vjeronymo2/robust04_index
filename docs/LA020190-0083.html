February 1, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

CIF ROUNDUP

St. Bernard 62, Verbum Dei 61 -- Sophomore forward Chris Keldorf, taking an inbounds pass from Terry Sullivan on the right wing, hit a three-point shot at the buzzer to lift the Vikings to a Camino Real League victory at home.

Center Wyking Jones scored 19 points and guard Martin Smith added 15 for St. Bernard, which snapped a three-game losing streak and improved to 10-10 overall and 5-5 in the league.

Verbum Dei fell to 7-11 and 4-6.

St. Anthony 92, Serra 82 -- Despite a game-high 37 points for guard Kenny Davis, the Cavaliers suffered an Camino Real League loss at St. Anthony. Serra dropped to 9-9 overall and 5-5 in the league. St. Anthony (12-4, 8-2) got 25 points from center Dan Morgan and 21 from guard Damaine Powell.
