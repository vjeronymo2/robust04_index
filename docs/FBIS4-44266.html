BFN [Unattributed article: "Rwanda: Italian Defense-Foreign Ministry Joint Mission"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, June 25 -- Italy early next week will send a joint delegation, from its foreign affairs and defense ministries, on a fact-finding mission to ascertain whether Italian troops could contribute to humanitarian efforts Rome has already embarked on there, a statement from the Foreign Ministry said. The Farnesina [Foreign Ministry] added how the decision was made this morning during a defense-foreign coordination session and fell within "the framework of initiative by the Italian Government in favor of the populations in Rwanda and following the recent proposal to initiate consultations on a WEU (Western European Union) level and followed up by contacts the premier and foreign minister had in Corfu." The ministry went on to state how this morning's session considered actions of protection and support of health structures, escorting convoys of prime necessities and refugees, as well as offering air transport.
