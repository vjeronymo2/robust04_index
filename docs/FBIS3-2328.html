Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 3 Mar (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE)--This reporter has learned from relevant quarters that China has chosen the Maoziding hill, Pengze county, Jiujiang city of Jiangxi as the site for its third nuclear plant. It is learned that the nuclear plant will house six 600,000- kilowatt nuclear power units, and that upon completion, it will become a key point for electric networks in central and eastern China. At present, the Ministry of Power Industry has officially confirmed and approved this nuclear power project and preliminary stage preparatory work is already underway. Upon completion, the nuclear plant will be able to thoroughly resolve the electricity shortage of Jiangxi province. At present, China already has the Qinshan Nuclear Plant in Zhejiang and the Daya Bay Nuclear Plant in Guangdong. Jiujiang's Nuclear Plant will have the largest electricity-generating capability, however.
