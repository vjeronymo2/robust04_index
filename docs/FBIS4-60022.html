BFN [Text] Amin al-Hindi, who was involved in planning the murder of Israeli athletes at the Munich Olympic Games in 1972, arrived in the Gaza Strip this afternoon through the Rafah border crossing. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that he was accompanied by his wife and several bodyguards. Al-Hindi is slated to be one of the commanders of the Palestinian security forces in the territories. Al-Hindi was in charge of security at the PLO offices in Tunis.
