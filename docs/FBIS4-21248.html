BFN [Text] Istanbul, April 17 (A.A.) -- Istanbul Security Chief Mehmet Agar and Unal Erkan, state of emergency region governor, left for the United States on Monday. Agar told reporters at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport before his departure that during his visit they will meet with Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) officials and Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) officials. During the meetings, possibilities for new methods of fighting drugs and organized crime will be discussed. Agar said it will be a very useful visit in terms of strengthening present positive relations. Both officials will return to Turkey this weekend.
