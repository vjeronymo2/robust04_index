BFN [By Chen Xi (7115 2569) "The Second Session of the Eighth Beijing Municipal CPPCC Committee Opens"] [Excerpts] The second session of the Eighth Beijing Municipal Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] Committee ceremoniously opened on the afternoon of 1 April. Among those who were invited to the session's opening ceremony were Chen Xitong, secretary of the municipal party committee; Li Qiyan, mayor of the municipality; Zhang Jianmin, chairman of the municipal people's congress standing committee; and Li Zhijian and Chen Guangwen, deputy secretaries of the municipal party committee. Seated on the session's rostrum during the opening ceremony were Wang Daming, chairman of the municipal CPPCC committee; Feng Mingwei, Sun Fuling, Shen Rendao, Chen Zhongyi, Zhu Chenyu, Chen Dabai, Wang Shuhuan, Lu Songhua, Zhang Lianyun, and Qian Yi, vice chairmen of the municipal CPPCC committee; Li Bokang, adviser to the municipal CPPCC committee; and Du Shenwei, secretary general of the municipal CPPCC committee. Chairman Wang Daming presided over the opening ceremony and delivered a speech. [passage omitted] Wang Shuhuan, vice chairman of the municipal CPPCC committee and who was entrusted by the municipal CPPCC standing committee, made a work report at the session. [passage omitted] During the session, the participating members also approved the resolution on the namelist of members for the drafting committee and heard the report given by the motions examination committee on the work of handling the motions raised by members since the first session of the eighth municipal CPPCC committee. Also attending the session's opening ceremony were leading comrades from the municipal level organs, including Zhang Baifa, Wang Baosen, Wang tong, Zhang Baokang, Ouyang Wenan, Yang Chaoshi, Duan Bingren, Qiang Wei, Li Yongan, Meng Zhiyuan, Tie Ying, Xia Qinlin, Tao Dayong, Hao Yichun, Mei Xiangming, Tao Siping, Xu Bingzhong, Lu Yucheng, He Luli, Li Runwu, Hu Zhaoguang, Meng Xuenong, Duan Qiang, Su Zhongxiang, Wang Zhaoyue, and Fan Yuanmou; as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties and mass organizations.
