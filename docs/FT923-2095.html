920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Creston loss at Pounds 1m Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.09m against Pounds 554,0000 were announced by Creston for the year to June 30. Group turnover fell to Pounds 8.89m (Pounds 14.3m). The group is pursuing a policy of reducing the size of operations and releasing working capital. At the year end it had no bank borrowings. Losses per share increased to 5.68p (2.82p).
