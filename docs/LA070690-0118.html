July 6, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BAD START? It won't be good news for the Orange County Transportation Commission. . . . An advisory committee, hoping to marshal public support for a county transportation tax, invited 2,500 people to two public meetings. Only 75 showed. . . . Worse, most were from the opposition, the anti-car-pool Drivers for Highway Safety. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
