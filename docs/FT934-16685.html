931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Ardagh at IPounds 1.67m after exceptional Ardagh, the Dublin-based glass manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits lower at IPounds 1.67m (Pounds 1.59m), against IPounds 4.21m, for the year to June 29. The outcome was struck after an exceptional charge of IPounds 2.07m. Turnover was IPounds 30.4m (IPounds 32.5m) and earnings per share came out at 4.29p (11.18p). The dividend is increased from 3.115p to 3.265p with a proposed final of 2.54p.
