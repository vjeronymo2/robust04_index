September 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

MCGRUFF TO APPEAR AT PLAZA

McGruff, the Crime Prevention Dog, will make his annual appearance at the Plaza Pasadena on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

Throughout each day, he will sing songs, hand out coloring books and give safety advice to children.

The Pasadena Police Department will show off a helicopter, patrol car and motorcycle. The Fire Department will have a truck on hand. And youngsters older than 3 will be fingerprinted for free.
