BFN [Text] Iran and Vietnam will practice cooperation after signing three memoranda of understanding in the diplomatic, scientific, educational, economic, commercial, and agricultural fields this afternoon. At the end of his three-day visit to Tehran Mr. Le Duc Anh, the Vietnamese president, said this afternoon that he has held successful talks on all matters with the Iranian authorities and that the two countries are also to practice international and regional diplomatic cooperation. The Vietnamese president added: By forming a joint economic committee, the two countries will speedily follow up the expansion of ties. The Vietnamese president said: Vietnam utilizes Iran's good and successful experience in rebuilding the damage caused in the war against America and is grateful for the clear instructions of his eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the leader of the Islamic revolution, and President Hashemi-Rafsanjani on that score. Thanking the revolutionary Iranian government and nation for their hospitality, the Vietnamese president said, referring to the manner of cooperation among Third World countries to overcome their problems: Through regional and international cooperation, particularly the Non-aligned Conference, we could help global peace and stability. During the ceremony to see off the Vietnamese president, Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani said: There are no limits on cooperation between Iran and Vietnam. We agree on all matters.
