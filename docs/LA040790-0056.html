April 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HE'S TIRED OF THEIR STERLING PERFORMANCE

On March 24, the Clippers got beat at home by 33 points.

Their 11 points in the fourth quarter was the fewest points in any quarter this season. Their 79-point total was also a season low.

In games elsewhere, three ex-Clippers totaled 134 points: 60 for Tom Chambers, 40 for Terry Cummings, 34 for Ricky Pierce.

Mr. Sterling, some people have an undeserved reputation. You've earned yours.

ANDREW J. BYRNE, El Monte
