Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tiraspol BASAPRESS, 3/01/1994 -- The state committee for radio and TV of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic has decided to deny the accreditation of Lev Rozenberg, reporter of the Russian EXPRESS KHRONIKA news agency at the Tiraspol Supreme Soviet. The reason is an article published in EXPRESS KHRONIKA No. 45 regarding some actions of the Tiraspol authorities last October, including the voyage of commandos to Moscow to defend the White House. Although many Moscow publications proved that Tiraspol was involved in the bloodshed, it is the first time when Tiraspol authorities deny the accreditation of a Russian journalist.
