Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army press service reported today that active military operations continued on most of the Karabakh war fronts. NKR Defense Army units successfully repulsed all the attacks of the Azerbaijani Army in the sectors around Madagis and Hakop-Kamari in the north of Mardakertskiy Rayon, as well as on the [word indistinct] borders of Agdamskiy Rayon. Positional battles are also continuing in the other sectors of the battlefront. Casualty figures are being determined. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reported that a relative calm has been prevailing on Armenia's borders since last night.
