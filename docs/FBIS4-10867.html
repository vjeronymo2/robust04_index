BFN [Text] Peruvian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Efrain Goldenberg, who is in Morocco, today expressed optimism about the decision to create the World Trade Organization. Goldenberg is heading the Peruvian delegation that is participating in the GATT conference. Goldenberg said that the World Trade Organization, which will be created after the conclusion of the GATT conference and will supervise world trade, is the outcome of the liberalization policy advocated by Peru. Regarding the so-called social clause advocated by the United States and several European countries, Goldenberg was against mixing the labor jurisdiction of the International Labor Organization with the trade jurisdiction of the future World Trade Organization.
