BFN [Text] One civilian was killed and three injured in last night's Serb artillery attack on Usora [Croatian enclave in northern Bosnia]. Doctors in Tesanj war hospital are fighting to save the lives of the wounded. There is fresh damage to residential buildings. Serbs are heavily shelling locations adjacent to the front line. From their strongholds in Putnikovo Brdo, Zeravica, and Stanovi, Serbs were shelling defense lines and the free Usora territory with mortars, tanks, and long-range shells until late last night. They also attempted an infantry breakthrough but Usora defenders prevented it. Fresh explosions were heard in the free Usora territory again this morning.
