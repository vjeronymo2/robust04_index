930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / People: Abbey National Bob Knighton, formerly director, group IT, has been appointed md, operations division, and Gareth Jones, formerly director retail operations, is appointed Treasurer of ABBEY NATIONAL. They both join the board. Charles Toner, formerly md, new businesses, becomes md, retail division. John Bayliss, the deputy chairman, will retire at the end of the year.
