930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Advertising industry 'threat' ADVERTISING agencies could face an exodus of key staff, off to find richer pickings from competitors once the recession lifts, according to a study of the industry by accountants Arthur Andersen. The firm also says that only 16 per cent of agencies made a direct link between individual performance and reward while 87 per cent of employees who felt there was no link would like one.
