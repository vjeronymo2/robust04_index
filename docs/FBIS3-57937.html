Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] A large delegation from the Republic of Slovakia began a two-day official visit to Cyprus today. The delegation is headed by Foreign Minister Josef Moravcik. Economics Minister Jan Ducky is also a member of the Slovak delegation. This is the first visit by a high-ranking Slovak delegation to Cyprus since Czechoslovakia was dissolved a year ago. The visit is aimed at strengthening bilateral relations between Cyprus and Slovakia. The Cypriot side also wants to brief the visiting delegation on the latest developments in the Cyprus issue. Furthermore, both Cyprus and Slovakia are interested in developing close cooperation in the fields of trade and cooperation through European international organizations.
