Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 25 (ATA) -- The third election assembly of the Democratic Prosperity Party [DPP], the branch of Gostivar, was held on January 24, 1994, this branch reports. Taking part in this assembly were: the representative of the Albanian Democratic Party, Shaban Memia, deputy of Albanian parliament, representatives of the branches Karshiake, Qender, and Gazi Babe from Skopje, and of the branches Tetove, Struge, Manastir, Diber, Kercove. The assembly called for holding of a unique congress according to the agreement reached in Tirana between representatives of the Provisional Leading Council and representatives of the group of the DPP. The assembly also emphasized that the DPP belongs to no one, therefore it should not be instrumentalized. Attending the assembly were representatives from 46 sub-branches out of 48 that are altogether. Participants in the assembly elected delegates to the untimely congress, the members of the central assembly and the new presidency of the branch. Refik Dauti was elected chairman.
