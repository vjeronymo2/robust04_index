BFN [Text] Dr. Carlos Jeque, former vice president of the Mozambique United Front-Democratic Convergence Party [Fumo-PCD], has told SAVANA that he will enter the presidential elections race as an independent candidate. Jeque, 41, is a lawyer. He told SAVANA that his decision comes in the wake of advice given by political and business individuals, adding that the latter are, quote, worried about the absence of a civilian alternative in view of their (?disappointment) in Dr. Domingos Arouca, whom we all trusted until very recently, unquote. President Chissano, Afnso Dhlakama, leader of the Mozambique National Resistance, and FUMO-PCD President Domingos Arouca have already announced that they will run for the presidency.
