BFN [Text] A telephone conversation was held yesterday evening between Presidential Council Chairman Lieutenant General brother 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his brother His Majesty King Fahad Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz of Saudi Arabia and custodian of the two holy mosques. They talked about current developments in our country, issues of shared interest, and brotherly ties between the two brotherly countries. Also yesterday evening, a telephone conversation was held between Presidential Council Chairman Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his brother His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the brotherly United Arab Emirates [UAE]. They reviewed Yemeni developments and ties between the two brotherly countries.
