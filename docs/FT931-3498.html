930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / UK Company News: United Uniform climbs to Pounds 3.65m Although 1992 proved to be a more difficult year than anticipated 12 months ago, United Uniform Services, maker of fitted uniforms and corporate clothing, raised pre-tax profits by 7 per cent from Pounds 3.4m to Pounds 3.65m in 1992. Turnover rose 6 per cent to Pounds 57.1m. Earnings per share were 10.9p (10.1p) and the dividend total is maintained at 3p with an unchanged final of 2p. The company is to change its name to that of its principal subsidiary, Horace Small Apparel.
