930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Tuskar cancels USM quotation Tuskar Resources, the oil and gas explorer, has cancelled its share dealings on the Unlisted Securities Market. The company's 262.66m ordinary shares have now been admitted to the exploration securities market in Dublin. Regarding the offer from Animex, directors said the proposals contained 'nothing of any significant value' and should be rejected.
