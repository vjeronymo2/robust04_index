BFN [Text] Cairo, 11 Apr (MENA)--The Palestinian-Israeli negotiations on the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement resumed here again today. The Palestinian delegation to the negotiations is being led by Dr. Nabil Sha'th, Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat's political adviser, and the Israeli delegation by Amnon Shahaq, deputy Israeli chief of staff. The Security, Civilian, and Legal Affairs Committees are meeting during this round of negotiations.
