Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, February 1 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- A meeting of chairmen of the metropolitan and prefectural offices of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) was held in Tokyo over January 27-28. Yi Chin-kyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon, delivered a report, which was followed by speeches. The speakers called for making a new advance in strengthening Chongnyon as a chuche-based organization of overseas Koreans united in one mind, true to the new year's teachings of the great leader President Kim Il-song. They vowed to fully establish the chuche-based leadership system in Chongnyon by invigorating the movement for learning from the greatness of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. They also resolved to make Chongnyon a patriotic organization full of vitality with its roots struck deep among the compatriots by strengthening its branches and chapters, radically enhancing the role of organizations of various social strata and thus uniting a greater number of compatriots.
