Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Mirudin Aldobasic from Tesanj] [Text] From their well-known strongholds, the chetniks launched several hundred shells at the Tesanj field hospital. Schools and other civilian buildings in Tesanj were also shelled one after another. We have as yet no information on the casualties. In the Tesanj area, and in the area of the free territories of Doboj and Teslic, the population is in shelters. The joint units of the 7 South Operative Group are decisively and courageously defending their positions. The villages of the free territories in this area, particularly Grlic, Planje, Kalosevic, Mijonac, and Krakov, are [words indistinct]. Therefore, the aggressor is not giving up on his criminal intentions aimed at capturing these free territories, particularly the Doboj-Teslic road communication.
