BFN [Excerpt] Kamenz (DDP/ADN) -- Hoyerswerda has become the first German town to elect a PDS [Party of Democratic Socialists] mayor. In the second round of voting in yesterday's local elections in Saxony, Horst-Dieter Braehmig gained 51.6 percent of the vote, clearly beating his SPD [Social Democratic Party] opponent and thus providing the most sensational result of the runoff elections.
