Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Lukavica, 18 Feb (TANJUG) -- In accordance with the Russian initiative the Serb side today began withdrawal its heavy artillery around Sarajevo. A column of 30 pieces of heavy artillery started being withdrawn from Lukavica at 1300 [1200 GMT], a TANJUG correspondent in Lukavica reports. "By order of the Supreme Command, the artillery pieces were collected and are being moved to certain locations and placed under control," [as received] Colonel Tadija Manojlovic, the commander of the Sarajevo-Romanija Corps artillery said. "The Serb side has done this in the interest of peace, and not in response to the NATO ultimatum," Manojlovic said.
