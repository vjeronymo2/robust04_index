Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "World News" program] [Text] China says reports by foreign mass media about the detention of Chinese dissidents are groundless. The Chinese Ministry of Public Security made the statement on Monday [7 March] after overseas news organizations carried lists of dissidents detained during the weekend. The ministry explained some of the dissidents listed are under investigation because they are suspected of inciting turmoil and disrupting social order. Others named have not been detained. The dissident Wei Jingsheng, one of those named by the foreign media, has applied for permission to leave Beijing on a private matter. The ministry said his application has been approved.
