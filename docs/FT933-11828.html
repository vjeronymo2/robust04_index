930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / Spain's current account deficit narrowed Spain's current account deficit narrowed to Pta215bn (Pounds 1.07bn) in April from Pta294bn in April last year, the Bank of Spain said yesterday. The trade deficit also fell to Pta179bn from Pta297bn. Exports rose to Pta705bn from Pta556bn and imports to Pta884bn from Pta853bn.
