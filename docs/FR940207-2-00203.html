Furnace Petition CP 93&hyph;1 The staff will brief the Commission on petition CP 93&hyph;1 which requests that the Commission issue regulations to protect toddlers and infants from burn injuries from gas-fired floor furnaces. 2. Clacker Balls The staff will brief the Commission on final amendments to the Commission's regulations for clacker balls, 16 CFR 1500.18(a)(7), 1500.86(a)(5). These amendments would clarify the definition of clacker ball and establish exemption criteria specific to small, light clacker balls.
