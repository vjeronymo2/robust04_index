940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Observer: Dog's breakfast Collectors of bizarre acronyms may be tickled to learn that the Asean Regional Forum, a talking shop embracing south-east Asia, the US, China, Russia and others to discuss Asian security issues, will hold its first meeting in Bangkok on July 25. Asian diplomats working on the meeting are puzzled by their Australian counterparts making the noise 'arf arf' on every conceivable occasion. Well, it is the year of the dog, after all.
