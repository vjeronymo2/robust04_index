930430 FT 30 APR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Pears are this week's best buy, reports the FFVIB, at 40-55p a lb, depending on variety. Other good fruit buys include Bramley apples at 30-35p a lb, Cape Golden Delicious at 45-50p a lb, bananas at 45-55p a lb, avocados at 40-55p each and white fleshed grapefruit at 25-30p each. English and Dutch onions are priced at 20-25p a lb, cauliflower at 40-55p a head, spring greens at 35-40p a lb and broccoli at 40-50p per 8oz pre-pack. Supplies of English lettuce are increasing with round lettuce at 25-35p each, tomatoes at 65-75p a lb and peppers at Pounds 1.20-1.60 a lb depending on colour.
