BFN [Text] A communique released by the Alfa Cartel [Trade Union Confederation] says, quote, the government continues to spread misleading information. The protesters did not resort to violence, did not even get close to the sidewalk [in Victory Square], but the authorities continue to incite, unquote. The communique requests support from all state institutions to adopt a positive stance as soon as possible to avoid possible regrettable violence. Another communique released by the National Trade Union Bloc, in its capacity as a participant in the rally organized by Alfa Cartel, informs public opinion about, quote, the constant and arrogant refusal of the executive to solve the problems of the trade unions and, at the same time, the obviously hypocritical way in which dialogue is being mimed, unquote.
