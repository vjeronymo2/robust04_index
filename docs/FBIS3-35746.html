Language: English Article Type:BFN [By KHOVAR correspondent Negmat Ochilov] [Text] Dushanbe January 28 TASS -- A resolution of the Tajik government commission for exchange of 1961-1992 Russian roubles was circulated here today. Under the document, deposits of permanent Tajik residents placed at the Tajik savings bank and joint stock-commercial banks shall not be re-estimated if they did not exceed one million roubles at November 1, 1993. State securities sold to the population in 1993 shall not be re-estimated as well. All deposits larger than 200,000 roubles that are liable for re-estimation regardless the time of their making shall be returned in due time on condition of a declaration on income and a certificate on tax payment to be issued by the local taxation bodies. One-, three-, five- and ten-rouble banknotes of the 1961 model shall no longer be in use in the republic with the exception of payment for city transportation and bread at specialized shops.
