April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BLEACH KITS

I support distribution of bleach and condoms to drug abusers.

The demonstrators at the L.A. City Council meeting chanting "let them die," supposedly trying to save the L.A. taxpayers money, will, in fact, cost the taxpayers millions by their position, humanitarian concerns aside.

Preventing AIDS transmission (bleach, condoms) will save the L.A. taxpayers millions in AIDS treatment costs.

JEFF ZIEGLER

West Hollywood
