941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bowater in talks with Nampak Bowater, the packaging and printing group, is in discussions with Nampak, South Africa's largest packaging group, which might lead to the merging of their UK blow moulded plastic container businesses. Bowater's Polysystems business could be merged with that of BlowMocan, a UK-based company owned by Nampak, in return for equity in BlowMocan. The businesses have a combined turnover of about Pounds 26m.
