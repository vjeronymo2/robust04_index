940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / Jump in number of convicted directors Thirteen company directors were convicted in criminal cases in the first quarter of the year compared with three in the previous quarter, the Department of Trade and Industry said yesterday. A further four directors were disqualified for periods of between two years and seven years after civil proceedings. There were 382 requests for investigations during the period, bringing the total for the year to the end of March to 1,126, compared with 1,069 in the previous 12 months.
