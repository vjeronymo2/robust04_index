Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Police Minister Moshe Shahal said there was no chance that the closure on the Gaza Strip would be lifted within the next few weeks. He recommended extending the closure for as long as possible in order to prevent terrorists from crossing the Green Line in order to carry out attacks. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the police minister was speaking during a visit to Beersheba prison where he inaugurated two new structures which can accommodate 300 prisoners.
