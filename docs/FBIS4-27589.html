BFN [Text] Seoul, May 30 (YONHAP) -- South Korea and Slovenia signed an agreement Monday on scientific and technological cooperation that will lead to the formation of a joint committee. Foreign Ministers Han Sung-chu of South Korea and Lojze Peterle of Slovenia signed the agreement calling for exchange programs between government and private research organizations as well as universities. The two countries set up a joint cooperation committee to pursue and arrange various projects.
