October 16, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

STREEP AWES CO-STAR MACLAINE

Actress Shirley MacLaine says her relationship with Meryl Streep while filming "Postcards From the Edge" was nothing less than "awesome."

The last time Old Hollywood met New Hollywood -- when MacLaine and Debra Winger co-starred in "Terms of Endearment" -- there were a series of skirmishes between the two actresses.

According to the latest Entertainment Weekly magazine, Hollywood insiders predicted similar quarreling between MacLaine and Streep. But it didn't happen.

"Meryl is a genius," MacLaine said. "You don't say her name in the same breath as Debra Winger's. Or mine. She's got a whole other thing going there.

"It's more akin to channeling than anything else. It's awesome."
