BFN [Text] Beijing, June 21 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng today expressed the hope that the General Electric Company (GEC) would expand cooperation with Chinese enterprises on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, during an hour-long meeting with GEC Chairman Lord Prior. The premier spoke highly of Lord Prior's efforts to promote economic cooperation and technological exchange between Britain and China, to boost cooperation in power industry in particular. Li said that the GEC's products have a good reputation in China, laying a sound basis for cooperation. He said he hoped that the two sides will strengthen their cooperation on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. Lord Prior briefed Li on his plan for expanding cooperation with China.
