940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): International- Other companies On Tuesday Mr Michael Jordan, new chairman of Westinghouse Electric, the troubled US conglomerate, will brief Wall Street analysts for the first time since his appointment last June. JP Morgan, the US bank, should enjoy a temporary respite from the furore over its entanglement with the Spanish bank Banesto when it reports fourth-quarter results on Thursday. Market projections for earnings per share for the year are Dollars 8.20-Dollars 8.50, compared with Dollars 5.63. Bidders for Italy's second mobile communications network will be finalising their preliminary proposals for the pre-qualification deadline on Saturday.
