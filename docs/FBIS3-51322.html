Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 14 Jan (NA) -- Uruguayan President Luis Alberto Lacalle and Paraguayan President Juan Carlos Wasmosy arrived here this morning to participate in a series of meetings with Argentine President Carlos Menem. Lacalle and Wasmosy were met by Menem at the military sector of the Jorge Newbery Airport. They immediately departed for the South Coastal Avenue where they will participate in the ceremony of the opening of the bids for dredging a stretch of the Parana River, an undertaking within the framework of the so-called river highway that will join the port of Caceres in Bolivia with the port of Palmira in Uruguay. The three chiefs of state will then have lunch at the Olivos residence where they will discuss matters pertaining to the Mercosur [Common Market of the South].
