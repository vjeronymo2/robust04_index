BFN [Text] Tehran, April 30, IRNA -- President Le Duc Anh of Vietnam arrived here Saturday on a three day official visit and was greeted at Tehran Airport by President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. In remarks upon his arrival, the Vietnamese president said that his visit to Iran was aimed at expanding and deepening mutual relations in political, economic and technical areas. He termed his visit to Tehran a "turning point" in the history of Tehran-Hanoi relations. The ministers of foreign affairs, commerce and agriculture are accompanying the Vietnamese president. President Hashemi-Rafsanjani told reporters that Tehran and Hanoi share close views on international political issues. The Iranian president said that during the Vietnamese president's visit to Tehran, efforts will be made to expand scientific, technical and commercial cooperation between the two countries. He said to the Iranian people Vietnam is an attractive country because of its glorious independence struggles in the past. The similar experiences of both countries would be effective in deepening and expanding Tehran-Hanoi cooperation, Hashemi-Rafsanjani said.
