July 28, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

RAMP CLOSURES

Caltrans will close the southbound Interstate 15 off-ramp to Main Street near National City today from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

The connector ramp from northbound Interstate 805 to eastbound California 94 will be closed Sunday from 6:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.

The closures are due to bridge repair work.
