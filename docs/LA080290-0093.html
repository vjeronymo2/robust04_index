August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

'Rockford' Legal Files: The creator of the 1970s "Rockford Files" TV series has sued Universal Studios, claiming he is owed $5 million in profits.

The Los Angeles Superior Court suit claims Roy Huggins originated the series concept, and was promised 25% of net profits from the detective show. Huggins claims the show, which ran from 1974 to 1980, grossed more than $120 million. A Universal attorney refused to comment. GREG BRAXTON
