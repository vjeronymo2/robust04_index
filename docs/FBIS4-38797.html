BFN [Text] Yerevan, 31 May (SNARK) -- The 37-year-old Eduard Zuloyan has been nominated as the Deputy Foreign Minister of Armenia. Before the appointment, Zuloyan headed the military-political office in the same ministry. As SNARK newsman was told in the press bureau of Foreign Ministry, earlier Zuloyan served in the national security organs, Physical [Physics] Institute, and Transport Ministry.
