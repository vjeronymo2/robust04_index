920323 FT 23 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Building offices in Brussels BERNARD SUNLEY & SONS has been appointed construction consultants by Cominiere SA for a Pounds 9.4m office development near the European Community headquarters in Brussels. Work recently commenced on site on the nine-storey office development in the heart of Brussels' commercial sector. In 1990 a decision was made to develop the location by demolishing the existing stone-clad 1920's office building. Designed by Les Ateliers de Bruxelles, the multi-storey development will provide 12,474 sq metres of air-conditioned office accommmodation within an L-shaped building.
