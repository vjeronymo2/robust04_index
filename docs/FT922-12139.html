920422 FT 22 APR 92 / Output decline seen to slow THE DECLINE in manufacturing output is slowing and there are signs the recession is beginning to lift, according to a survey published yesterday by the London Chamber of Commerce and Industry. A total of 38 per cent of manufacturers said orders for the first quarter this year were up for the first time in two years, although 30 per cent reported a fall. The balance of 8 per cent is the first positive figure for two years. A balance of 7 per cent of companies in the service sector reported a fall in orders compared with a balance of 1 per cent expecting a rise in the previous quarter.
