BFN [Text] The Palestinian Association for Human Rights today appealed to the amir of the State of Kuwait for an immediate pardon to 10 Palestinian students who were sentenced to death by the Kuwait State Security Court on 12 June last year. The association requests the amir of Kuwait to respond to the appeal of the families and kinfolk of those sentenced to pardon their sons during these holy months and on the occasion of the blessed 'Id al-Adha. It is worth mentioning that the appeal court will issue its ruling about the sentences against the students on 30 May in response to the appeal submitted by the convicted students.
