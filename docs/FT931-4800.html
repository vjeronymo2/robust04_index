930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Aircraft must be modified The Swiss government has told the maker of trainer aircraft ordered by the South African government to modify them so that they cannot be used for military purposes, Ian Rodger writes from Zurich. The manufacturer, the Pilatus subsidiary of the Oerlikon-Buhrle armaments and engineering group, said it would do whatever it could to find a solution to the problem.
