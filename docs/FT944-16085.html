941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks opened higher but slid through the rest of the day to close slightly lower. In a quiet session, the big losses were in heavily-weighted golds and financial stocks. The TSE 300 composite index was down 11.66 points, or 0.27 per cent, to 4,343.37, with losers ahead of winners by 410 to 353. Volume of 71.06m shares was up from yesterday's 64.67m, while today's value of CDollars 912.5m was slightly lower than Wednesday's CDollars 923.7m. Overall, seven of the 14 sub-indexes were up, led by transportation, real estate and pipelines. Golds - down 1.45 per cent - and oil and gas shares posted the biggest losses.
