930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / Tory party to shed 60 staff THE Conservative party is to prune its 300-strong national organisation by a fifth as part of a drive to cut costs and improve its finances. Mr Paul Judge, the party's recently appointed director-geneneral, told staff yesterday that the 60 redundancies would take effect next month. The party expects to save about Pounds 2m with the move.
