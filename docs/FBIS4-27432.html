BFN [Text] Yoshiro Mori, secretary general of the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP], gave a news conference on the morning of 29 May in Sapporo city, in which he discussed the possibility of dissolving the Diet, and calling for general elections immediately after the passage of the fiscal 1994 budget. He said, "The ruling parties believe that holding elections under the multiseat constituencies system means a sabotage of political reform. This is aimed at creating confusion inside the LDP and the Social Democratic Party of Japan. It is possible for all party heads to gather and decide on certain necessary measures." This indicated that Mori believes that if all parties agree to introduce the "heiritsu" system of combining single-seat constituencies and proportional representation in the election after the next one, it is also possible to hold the next election under the multiseat system.
