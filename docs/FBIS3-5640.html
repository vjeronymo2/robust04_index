Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 8 (KCNA) -- An annual general meeting of the Zimbabwe-Korea Solidarity and Friendship Association was held in Harare, Zimbabwe on February 24. Chairman of the association N.T. Mawema stressed in his report that the Korean people achieved great success in socialist construction, pushed aside the anti-DPRK and the anti-socialist moves of the imperialists and renegades of socialism and firmly defended the sovereignty of the DPRK under the tested guidance of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il last year. He called for conducting the active solidarity movement for defence of socialist Korea. The general meeting discussed the question of significantly celebrating the 82nd birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song and a series of measures for firm solidarity with the Korean people in their efforts to solve the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula through the DPRK-USA negotiation.
