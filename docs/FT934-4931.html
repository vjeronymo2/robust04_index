931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazilian stocks seen fluctuating in narrow range Sao Paulo shares were down moderately in midday trade as prices revovered a little from the day's lows after some institutional investors returned. The Bovespa index was quoted 150 off at 27,495 by 1 pm. Traders said prices were seen fluctuating in narrow bands, but they did not discount stronger activity in the afternoon session after a block trade in Petrobras, the oil group, was concluded. Petrobras has said it would sell part of its stock portfolio in other companies in a block trade operation, scheduled to take place later yesterday. The block trade was to include 130.3m Eletrobras preferred shares, 41.9m Telebras common shares, 21.8m Telebras preferred shares and 352,554 Labo Eletronica preferred stock. Turnover was Cr29.9bn. Petrobras preferred shares were up 2.7 per cent at Cr26.50.
