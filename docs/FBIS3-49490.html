Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, Jan 9 (CTK) -- Local representatives and mayors of villages have no authority to hold the kind of meeting that took place in Komarno, southern Slovakia, on Saturday [8 January], Slovak National Party (SNS) deputy Vladimir Miskovsky told Slovak Television this evening. Answers to the call on Slovak constitutional officials by the meeting of ethnic Hungarian representatives would legitimize the demands expressed at the meeting, he said. Miskovsky also said that the issues of two official languages and ethnic autonomy for the Hungarian minority in Slovakia were unacceptable for the SNS.
