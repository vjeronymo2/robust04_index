Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 18 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- For the last years some 27 thousand people having been subjected to repression for political reasons have been rehabilitated. At present there are cases of about eight thousand people having been victims of the 30s- 40s repressions are still awaiting to be checked and settled. This was said by the State Rehabilitation Commission.
