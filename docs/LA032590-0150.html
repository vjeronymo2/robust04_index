March 25, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: MOORPARK;

DRIVER THROWN FROM CAR KILLED

A 24-year-old woman wearing a seat belt was killed when she was thrown from her car after she missed a curve at high speed on a windy road north of Moorpark.

Her two passengers were not injured.

Lillian Garcia of Oxnard was pronounced dead at the scene at 5:10 a.m. Friday, 40 minutes after the single-car accident, said Dale Zentzis of the Ventura County coroner's office.

Garcia was heading east on the Moorpark Freeway just north of Moorpark when she missed the 90-degree turn in the road at Broadway, the California Highway Patrol said. JOANNA MILLER
