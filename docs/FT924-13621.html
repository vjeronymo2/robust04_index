921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Observer: Comparative Nice to see that Henderson Administration's employees have retained a sense of a humour. At yesterday morning's meeting to announce the proposed 'merger' with Touche Remnant, one of the company's senior executives was describing Touche's recent investment performance in not altogether flattering terms. An unkind wag at the back of the room was heard to mutter: 'Well they'll fit in here nicely then, won't they?'
