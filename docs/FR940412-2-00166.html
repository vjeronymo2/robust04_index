United States International Telecommunications Advisory Committee Radiocommunication Sector Study Group 9; Meeting Notice The Department of State announces that the United States International Telecommunications Advisory Committee (ITAC), Radiocommunication Sector Study Group 9, will meet on April 28, 1994, at 9:30 a.m. in room 315 at the Federal Communication Commission, 1919 M Street, NW. Washington, DC 20554. This is the initial meeting to prepare for the international meeting scheduled to be held November 1&hyph;11, 1994. Additionally, the meeting will consider other activities affecting the work of Study Group 9 such as the work of Task Group 2/2 (Sharing with the MSS and BSS under 3 GHz), Task group 4/5 (Feeder Links), sharing with the space science services and any other matters within the competence of this Study Group. Members of the General Public may attend the meetings and join in the discussions, subject to the instructions of the Chairman. Those planning to attend the meeting should contact Mr. Alex Latker by phone at (202) 632&hyph;3214 or by fax at (202) 634&hyph;1382. Dated: March 31, 1994. Warren G.
