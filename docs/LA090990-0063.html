September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SITTING OUT 'DANCE'

What a disappointment! When I read Kirk Douglas' autobiography, "Ragman's Son," I was enormously impressed with this fine actor's ability as a writer.

I looked forward to reading his recent book and first novel, "Dance With the Devil" (July 22). I should have saved my time and money.

Recently, in a late-night TV interview, possibly defending his novel, Douglas said, "Well, you know there is more to my book than just raw sex."

Not much, Mr. Douglas, not much.

MERYL MEYERS

LOS ANGELES
