BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa conferred at the Foreign Ministry this evening with Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat and his accompanying delegation. Discussion during the meeting focused on developments relating to the Palestinian issue. They also continued to discuss the topics that President Mubarak reviewed with 'Arafat this morning. The meeting was attended by Sa'id Kamal, Palestinian ambassador in Cairo, and the Arab League assistant secretary general for Palestinian affairs.
