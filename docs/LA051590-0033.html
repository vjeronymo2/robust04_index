May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BUGGED BY WATER ISSUE

Regarding water conservation. The people are asked to cut back on our water usage, which we do, and as a result, we save a certain percentage of water from our efforts.

But when people OK new housing and continue to allow more and more new homes to be built, all that we conserve then goes to these new homes. Where is the logic in this?

Sooner or later the building will have to stop because there just will not be enough water, no matter how much we conserve. I hope someone opens their eyes to this obvious future problem.

YVONNE AUMILLER

Long Beach
