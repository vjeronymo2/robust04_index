BFN [Text] An Armenian-Azerbaijani meeting took place on the border near Yeraskh [an Armenian village bordering Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic] on 2 June. The Armenian side asked the Nakhichevani delegation about the misinformation disseminated by the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry and the press service of the National Assembly on 31 May, which say that allegedly the Armenian Army units have invaded the Nakhichevani territory and occupy a strategically important hill. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service informed ARMENPRES that Brigade Commander Colonel Farman Aliyev, who commands all the army units in Nakhichevan, has declared that he himself heard that news from Moscow's Vesti television program for the first time, and added that it does not conform to reality.
