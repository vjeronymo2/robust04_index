Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1900 yesterday evening at the National Assembly Hall, His Excellency [H.E.] Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly, hosted a banquet in honor of the delegation of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the National Assembly of Cambodia. The social function was held on the occasion of an official friendship visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic by the Cambodian visitors. Attending the banquet from the Lao side were H.E. Phao Bounnaphon, minister attached to the Prime Minister's Office; H.E. Osakan Thammatheva, minister of information and culture; H.E. Boun-gnang Volachit, lord mayor of Vientiane Municipality; H.E. Khambou Sounisai, deputy chairman of the National Assembly; H.E. Dr. Khamliang Phonsena, chief of the National Assembly's Commission for Foreign Affairs; deputy ministers; National Assembly members; and a number of invited guests. The Cambodian side included H.E. [name indistinct], chairman of the Interior, National Defense, Investigation, and Suppression Commission; and other members of the delegation. The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere filled with hospitality, relations of friendship and cooperation and neighborliness.
