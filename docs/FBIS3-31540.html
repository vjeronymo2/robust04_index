Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] The Czech Foreign Ministry today expressed fundamental disagreement with the action of Defense Minister Antonin Baudys, who, at the end of last week, submitted to some foreign representatives his initiative for solving the situation in Bosnia. According to the Foreign Ministry's statement, the action of [Defense] Minister [Antonin] Baudys has created a totally unacceptable situation for the foreign policy interests of the Czech Republic, has damaged its reputation, and is a clear infringement of a government minister's jurisdiction.
