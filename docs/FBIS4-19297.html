BFN [Statement to the press by Prime Minister Tansu Ciller following her meeting with President Bill Clinton at the White House -- live] [Text] [Ciller] I reviewed Turkish-U.S. relations with President Clinton. We particularly discussed the recent incident. I want to take this opportunity to extend my condolences to the families of the brave soldiers who lost their lives. It is a very regrettable incident. We will study the details more closely. Other than that... [pauses] May they rest in peace. We discussed other issues as well. We reviewed Turkish-U.S. relations. I explained the economic stability program. Mr. Clinton clearly said that he will support it. Naturally, we are pleased about that. Other than that, we had the opportunity to discuss in detail other aspects of Turkey's relations with the United States, as well as Bosnia-Herzegovina and Azerbaijan. It was a very fruitful meeting. Thank you. [Unidentified reporter] Did you discuss the latest political developments in Turkey? [Ciller] Yes, a short assessment of the latest election results was made. It was a very fruitful meeting. May it be auspicious for our country.
