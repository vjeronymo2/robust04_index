Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today at the court session on the case of the SCSE [State Committee for the State of Emergency] in the military collegium of the Russian Supreme Court, a decision was reached in accordance with which the criminal proceedings against the defendants Anatoliy Lukyanov, Valentin Pavlov, Vladimir Kryuchkov, Oleg Shenin, Dmitriy Yazov, Oleg Baklanov, Valentin Varennikov, Yuriy Plekhanov, Vyacheslav Generalov, Aleksandr Tizyakov, and Vasiliy Starodubtsev were halted. This decision was taken by the court after the defendants had expressed agreement with the resolution on the amnesty adopted on 23 February in the State Duma and had called for a complete stop to the judicial examination.
