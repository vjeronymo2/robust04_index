940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Whitbread Mr Peter Jarvis, chief executive of Whitbread, the brewer, received a performance related payment of Pounds 102,917 (Pounds 67,000) in the latest year and pension contributions of Pounds 33,430 (Pounds 2,542) in addition to a basic remuneration of Pounds 368,496 (Pounds 345,209). The company saw pre-tax profits for the year ended February 26 rise from Pounds 177m to Pounds 234m on continuing turnover up from Pounds 2.32bn to Pounds 2.36bn.
