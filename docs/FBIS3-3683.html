Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 22 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), met here today with a Iraqi delegation from the country's Arab Baath Socialist Party. Wei exchanged views with the delegation on issues of common interest. The delegation, led by Abdul Razzak Hashimi, director of the Foreign Relations Office of the party, arrived here March 19 as guests of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee.
