941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / Drop of 4% in building orders Construction orders in the third quarter this year were 4 per cent lower than in the previous three months and 1 per cent lower than in the same period last year, Department of the Environment figures show. The biggest fall was in public housing and housing asso-ciation orders, which were 28 per cent lower than in the previous quarter and 40 per cent lower than in the same period last year. Private-sector housing orders were 2 per cent lower than in the previous quarter but 16 per cent higher than a year ago.
