920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / Insurance body board in shake-up MR MICK Newmarch, chief executive of Prudential, and Mr Martin Broughton, chief executive of Eagle Star, were yesterday elected to the board of the Association of British Insurers, which has come under fire from a number of its member companies. Mr Ian Rushton, the former chief executive of Royal Insurance, will continue as chairman and Mr Brian Richardson will remain deputy chairman. The board was reduced from 14 members to 12. The changes were announced after a short extraordinary general meeting yesterday, which was called following strong criticism from some life insurance companies.
