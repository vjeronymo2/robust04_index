BFN [Text] President Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, this morning received Mr. John McCarthy the ambassador of the United States in Tunis. During the meeting President 'Arafat received two important messages from the U.S. Administration pertaining to the current situation and the implementation of the Palestinian-Israel agreement and also the U.S. Administration's efforts with the donor states before the meeting scheduled for 9 June 1994. The meeting was an opportunity to review the current developments of the take over of the Palestinian authorities in Gaza Sector and Jericho. President 'Arafat had asked the donor states to speed the fulfillment of their pledges to enable the Palestinian National Authority to discharge their duties and implement their obligations in development and construction each of which is an urgent and fundamental necessity to strengthen the foundations of peace. On his part, the U.S. ambassador expressed the interest of the U.S. Administration in the success of the peace process and in enabling the Palestinian authorities of discharging their duties successfully and as soon as possible. The meeting was attended by Dr Sami Musallam, director-general of President Yasir 'Arafat's office.
