BFN [Text] Dushanbe, April 29, RIA -- The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of Tajikistan appointed Bozgul Dodkhudoyeva, former minister of education, to the post of deputy chairperson of the Cabinet of Ministers. Izbraghim Usmanov, secretary of the Central Committee of the Tajik Communist Party, was appointed to the post of chairman of the State Committee on Radio and Television.
