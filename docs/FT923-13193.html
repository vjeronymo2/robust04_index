920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Hays expands with Pounds 4.6m acquisition Hays, the business services group, has acquired Citidata Storage for Pounds 4.6m cash. London-based Citidata operates a secure storage and retrieval service for computer data held on magnetic media. It returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 520,000 from turnover of Pounds 2.1m in the year to April 30. Total assets amount to Pounds 400,000 and the company is free of debt. Hays is also purchasing the assets of Citipac, a crate hire business, for an undisclosed sum.
