930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: I&S Optimum Net asset value at I&S Optimum Income Trust amounted to 95.02p at May 31, against a restated 97.44p a year earlier. Attributable revenue of the Ivory & Sime managed trust totalled Pounds 2.1m (Pounds 2m). Earnings per share were 7.25p (6.98p). A fourth quarterly dividend of 1.85p brings the total for the year to 7.4p (7.25p), requiring a Pounds 44,000 (Pounds 78,000) transfer from the revenue reserve.
