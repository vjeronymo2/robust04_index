940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Churchbury Estates CHURCHBURY ESTATES, a wholly owned subsidiary of Greycoat, returned profits of Pounds 908,000 (losses Pounds 5.83m) pre-tax for the half year to end-September. Gross rental income fell to Pounds 2.03m (Pounds 3.1m). Earnings per share emerged at 6.9p (losses 44.9p).
