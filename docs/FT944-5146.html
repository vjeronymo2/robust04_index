941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Macedonia Macedonia's privatisation agency is understood to be pressing the government of the former Yugoslav republic to allow it to set up a stock exchange to aid structural reform. The agency has sold some 20 state-owned companies, worth Dollars 8m, out of a total of 1,800 since it was set up at the beginning of this year. Edited by John Pitt.
