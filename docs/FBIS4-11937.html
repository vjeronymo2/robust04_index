BFN [Text] In continuation of the Golboddin-Dostum militiamen coming over to the side of government in the country's northern regions, today 100-man armed group, led by Mohammad Nasim, joined the Islamic army's division 24 in Fariab province. The military correspondent of Bakhtar Information Agency reports from Fariab province that since the start of clearing-up operation of the country's northern regions carried out by the Islamic army's forces and Mojahedin, thousands of militiamen have abandoned the Golboddin-Karmal-Dostum infamous triangle ranks and came over to the side of the Islamic army's forces and Mojahedin stationed in these regions. The report adds that the mopping-up operation by the Mojahedin and the Islamic army has been continuing vigorously in Fariab province. The inhabitants and the Muslim people of Fariab province have been always earnestly cooperating with Mojahedin and the units of the Islamic army for the elimination of the last nests of communism in the country, and they sincerely discharge their Islamic duty.
