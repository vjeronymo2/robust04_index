BFN [Text] DPRK Deputy Foreign Minister Pak Myong Gu this morning held talks with State Secretary for Cooperation Johnny Eduardo Pinnocky with whom he discussed bilateral cooperation in the technological sector. The talks lasted about half an hour and the two ifficials spoke about the possible implementation of technological relations between Angola and the DPRK. At present the DPRK is facing a difficult political and military situation in connection with the dispute with the United States and South Korea over the dismantling of nuclear arms. [Begin recording] [Unidentified correspondent] At present what is the DPRK's position? [Pak] Recently the United States sent patriotic missiles to South Korea, and our position depends on the behavior of the United States. If they want to hold talks we are ready for them, but if they choose a drastic measure against our country we will respond in kind. We do not fear war; we can face the United States. [Pinnocky] A joint commission will meet in Pyongyang where identified projects will be presented as well as others. [end recording]
