October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Crime figures -- State Department of Justice crime figures in a chart that appeared Friday in The Times were incorrectly transcribed in three cases.

In Anaheim, there were 20,057 major crimes reported overall and 5,496 were solved, a rate of 27%. In Fresno, 8,095 burglaries were reported and 1,133 were solved -- a rate of 14%. In San Francisco, 380 cases were reported and 204 were solved -- 54%. The changes do not alter Long Beach's rate of solving major crimes -- 14%.
