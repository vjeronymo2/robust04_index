October 19, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NIXON FLAG? Even during Richard Nixon's darkest hours, Yorba Linda never forsook its most famous native son. . . . Now, the city that has honored the former president over the years with a library, park, highway, school and holiday is considering redesigning the city flag to picture the Nixon Library and birthplace (B3). . . . Says resident Mark Rhodes: "You've got to forgive the guy for being a crook. He's still intelligent."
