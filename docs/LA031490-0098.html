March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Petra Kronberger led an Austrian four-woman sweep in a World Cup slalom at Vemdalen, Sweden, and took a big step toward her country's first overall title since 1979.

Kronberger, who competes in all disciplines and broke into the top echelon this season, had a combined time of 1:24:40.
