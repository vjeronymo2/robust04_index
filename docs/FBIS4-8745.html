BFN [Text] Brussels, April 16 (AFP) -- The United Nations called for NATO air strikes over the UN-declared "safe haven" of Gorazde, a spokesman said here Saturday [16 April], shortly after the downing of a NATO jet over the eastern Bosnian Moslem enclave. The NATO spokesman said the downed Harrier jump-jet, from which the pilot reportedly ejected, was shot down during a "non-offensive" operation over the enclave.
