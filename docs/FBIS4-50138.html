BFN [Text] A 10-member cultural inspection group sent by the Education and Culture Committee under the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] National Committee is carrying out a round-the-island tour to inspect Hainan's cultural market as of 11 May. On the evening of 10 and 11 May, Governor Ruan Chongwu, secretary of the provincial party committee; Do Qinglin, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee and chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Yao Wenxu, chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee; Liu Xuebin, member of the provincial party committee standing committee, director of the provincial propaganda department, and special assistant to the governor; and Hu Kai and Lin Mingyu, vice chairmen of the provincial CPPCC committee; met with all members of the group in Haikou. Qian Liren -- member of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, executive deputy director of the CPPCC Education and Culture Committee, and former director of RENMIN RIBAO -- is chief of the cultural inspection group, while Wang Jifu -- member of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, deputy director of the CPPCC Education and Culture Committee, and former vice minister of culture -- is deputy chief. Members of the group include Yu Yang, member of the CPPCC National Committee, member of the CPPCC Education and Culture Committee, and head of the Artists' Theoretical Troupe under the Beijing Film Studio; (Chen Duo), member of the CPPCC National Committee, member of the CPPCC Education and Culture Committee, and deputy director of the Liaison Committee under the Central Committee of China Association for Promoting Democracy; Li Weikang, member of the CPPCC National Committee, member of the CPPCC Education and Culture Committee, and former director of the Second Troupe under the China Beijing Opera Theater; (Zhao Qing), member of the CPPCC National Committee and member of the Executive Committee under Chinese Dancers' Association; and others. The 10-member inspection group arrived in Haikou from Beijing on the afternoon of 10 May. Lin Mingyu, vice chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee, and others met the group at the airport. In a meeting hall located on the eighth floor of the provincial government office on the morning of 11 May, all comrades of the inspection group listened to reports by leaders of the provincial cultural and sports department on the development of Hainan's cultural market. In the afternoon and evening of the same day, members of the group inspected some cultural, book and journal, and audio-video markets, as well as some amusement places, such as song and dance halls, in the Haikou area. On 12 May, they will continue their inspection around Haikou. On 13 May, they will start their around-the-island inspection of the long cultural corridor along the eastern highways.
