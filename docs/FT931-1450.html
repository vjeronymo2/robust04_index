930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Oil Edinburgh Oil & Gas, the USM-quoted exploration and production group, suffered a drop in net profits from Pounds 283,000 to Pounds 55,000 in the year to December 31. Earnings fell to 0.32p (1.63p) per share. Turnover improved to Pounds 1.9m (Pounds 1.82m).
