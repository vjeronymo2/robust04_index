BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] -- On 20 April in Tashkent, the Government of Uzbekistan signed a memorandum with the U.S. Agency for International Development on the provision of help to the population of the Aral region. Khorezm Oblast and the Republic of Karakalpakstan will receive technical assistance, materials, and equipment to improve the quality of drinking water and the sanitary-hygienic and ecological education of the population. This scheme is part of the overall package of help measuring $15 million for the Republics of Central Asia, which was announced by U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher last autumn. The implementation of the scheme will allow an improvement in sanitary and hygienic conditions for 500,000 people in the Aral region.
