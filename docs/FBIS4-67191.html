BFN [Text] Some 2,380 kilos of dry and raw marijuana seized in Vientiane Province were destroyed on 12 May by provincial narcotics control and suppression authorities in the presence of several officials from other services concerned in the province. The destroyed marijuana was discovered and seized by provincial economic police authorities in their search for marijuana plantations operated by certain opportunists in many localities in the province since mid-1993. The destroyed haul was valued at 12.8 million kip [Lao currency]. Fifty-three marijuana planters, mostly residents of Kasi District, were reprimanded and fined by the local authorities concerned in accordance with the law.
