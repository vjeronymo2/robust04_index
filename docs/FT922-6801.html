920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / International Company News: Carter Hawley stems losses CARTER Hawley Hale, the California-based department store group operating under Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection, suffered a net loss of Dollars 20.37m, or 70 cents a share, in the first quarter to May 2, compared with a loss of Dollars 32.39m, or Dollars 1.12, in the same period last year, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Reorganisation costs were Dollars 3.59m, down from Dollars 7.96m. Sales rose to Dollars 433.62m from Dollars 430.11m last time.
