BFN [Text] President Ali Hassan Mwinyi has sent a congratulatory message to President-elect al-Haji Bakili Muluzi of Malawi, as well as to his United Democratic Front party, for winning the country's first democratic election. In his message, President Mwinyi told Mr. Muluzi that his election victory was a testimony to the wishes of the Malawi people in opening a new chapter to a democratic government which respects human rights. President Mwinyi expressed his wishes that the new government of Mr. Muluzi will continue to boost the existing good relations and cooperation between the Governments of Tanzania and Malawi. In his message, President Mwinyi also thanked the people of Malawi for showing tolerance and maturity during the election period, and hailed outgoing President Mr. Kamuzu Banda for respecting the wishes of the people.
