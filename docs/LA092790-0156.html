September 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

VOLUNTEERS SOUGHT FOR 4-HOUR HOSPITAL SHIFTS

Dominguez Medical Center is seeking volunteers to work in guest relations, reception, nursing and clerical units.

Individuals must be 16 or older. Persons with bilingual skills are encouraged to apply. Volunteers should be willing to commit a minimum of four hours per week. They will be provided with a uniform and receive a free meal after every four-hour shift.

For more information, call the Volunteer Services Program at 609-3822.
