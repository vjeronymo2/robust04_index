BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 27 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here this afternoon Lao Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat, who is now on a working visit to Vietnam. At the reception, Minister Somsavat Lengsavat informed Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet of the results of this talks with the Vietnamese foreign minister. He highly valued the efforts of the Vietnamese Government and people in the implementation of the agreements signed between the high-ranking officials of Vietnam and Laos in the recent time which, he said, have made important contributions to bring the traditional friendly and cooperative relations between Vietnam and Laos to a new step of development. Speaking to his guests, Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet hoped that with its role the Lao Democratic People's Republic (LDPR) would make positive contributions to the implementation of the Paris agreement on Cambodia, thus promoting the process of peace in Cambodia. He wished that the Lao people under the clear sighted leadership of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party headed by President [title as received] Khamtai Siphandon would continue to obtain greater achievements in their cause of building a Lao country of peace, prosperity, and development. Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet asked the Lao foreign minister to convey his regards to Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon.
