The judiciary chief said the recent action of the Zionist regime was overt theft and blatant kidnapping. He declared: Such ugly behavior by the illegitimate offspring of world arrogance is not surprising, particularly since the raison d'etre of the Zionist regime is usurpation and delinquence. The deathly silence of the international forums and organizations who talk about human rights is surprising and regrettable, however.
