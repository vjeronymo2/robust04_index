910430 FT 30 APR 91 / International Company News: PT Inco hit by lower deliveries PT INTERNATIONAL Nickel Indonesia (PT Inco), which was floated on the Jakarta stock exchange in April last year, saw first-quarter earnings fall by half, from USDollars 18.6m, or 8 cents a share, to Dollars 9.6m, or 4 cents, writes Kenneth Gooding, Mining Correspondent. Lower nickel deliveries, down from 17.7m to 14.9m lbs, as the company was recovering from a boiler explosionwhich cut output, and higher production costs were only partly offset by higherrealised nickel prices. PT Inco, 58 per cent owned by Inco of Canada, realised Dollars 3.02 a lb for its nickel in the quarter, against Dollars 2.74 last time.
