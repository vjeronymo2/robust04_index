920427 FT 27 APR 92 / Demirel leaves for Asian tour Turkey's prime minister, Mr Suleyman Demirel, sets out today for a week-long trip to the Turkic speaking central Asian states, aimed at underlining Turkey's ambitions as a leading regional power and economic model for these former Soviet republics, writes John Murray Brown in Ankara. Mr Demirel's visit, the first by a western leader, will seek to bolster Turkey's linguistic, cultural and religious links with central asia, reinforcing its support for the leaders of the six republics - Uzbekistan, Kirghizia, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Azerbaijan - all of whom have visited Ankara.
