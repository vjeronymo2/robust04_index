Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Portugal and Israel today abolished the need for visas between the two countries. Good relations between Portugal and Israel were marked today by the ratification by the foreign minister of an accord abolishing the need for visas. The minister, Durao Barroso, who is visiting Israel, said that in addition to cultural, economic and tourist relations, human relations are now also facilitated by this bilateral accord on the abolition of visas.
