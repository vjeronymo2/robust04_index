Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "(lek)": "Falbr at the Head of the New Trade Union"] [Text] Hradec Kralove--Richard Falbr, until now the chairman of the Czech-Moravian Service Employees Trade Union, has been elected leader of the UNIOS trade union, which was established on Friday [18 February] by the merger of trade unions representing workers in services, the gas industry, and foreign trade. The new union has approximately 80,000 members.
