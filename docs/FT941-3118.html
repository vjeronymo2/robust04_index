940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News In Brief: Dalgety DALGETY is to purchase Jaeger Participations, a privately-owned French food ingredients business for an undisclosed sum. EFG (garden centres and horticultural products) has won its appeal against enforcement notices issued by Aylesbury Vale District Council. The notices alleged breach of planning control at World's End garden centre at Wendover, Buckinghamshire.
