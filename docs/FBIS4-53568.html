BFN [Text] Fukuoka, May 20 KYODO -- A senior member of the coalition government's core party said Friday [20 May] it is possible to postpone enacting a bill on redistricting single-seat constituencies until an extraordinary Diet session in autumn. Kozo Watanabe, acting secretary general of Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party], said at a speech in Fukuoka that the postponement is possible if the ruling and opposition parties agree to discuss the matter.
