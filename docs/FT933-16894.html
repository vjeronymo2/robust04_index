930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: NW Water head gets 35% rise North West Water, the privatised UK utility, awarded Mr Bob Thian, chief executive, a 35 per cent increase in pay and bonuses last year. Pre-tax profits increased 7 per cent during the period. Mr Thian received total remuneration, including performance pay and pensions contributions, of Pounds 284,000. Mr Dennis Grove, the outgoing chairman, saw his total package increase 44 per cent to Pounds 267,000. Mr Steve McAdam, who resigned from his position as finance director, received compensation for loss of office of Pounds 90,000. North West also bought a property from him for Pounds 240,000. The company said the price had been certified by an independent chartered surveyor. Price Waterhouse, the accountancy firm, received non-audit fees from North West of Pounds 9.3m during the year. The fees covered due diligence work on acquisitions and general consultancy work.
