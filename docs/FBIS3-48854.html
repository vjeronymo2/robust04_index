Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 30 (KCNA) -- The New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song was reported by the Jordanian paper ALASR ALJEDID, the Dutch paper N.R.C. HANDENSBLAD, the Mexican paper COMBATIENTE, the Russian paper PATRIOT, the Romanian paper EUROPA, the Ecuadoran television Tele Andina 23, the Peruvian state television and the Kazakh radio. A seminar on the New Year address was held by the all India Indo-Korean Friendship Association. The speakers said the New Year address is a highly important work clearly indicating the way of stepping up the socialist construction in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and accelerating the country's independent and peaceful reunification. They expressed full support to the proposal set forth by President Kim Il-song to find a peaceful solution to the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula and held that the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula should be settled without fail through the DPRK-U.S. talks. They stressed the reunification of Korea should be realized through confederacy formula based on one nation, one state, two systems and two governments on the three principles of independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity indicated by President Kim Il-song. Reading sessions were held by the Central Accra branch of the Ghanaian National Institute of the Chuche Idea and in Stockholm, Sweden.
