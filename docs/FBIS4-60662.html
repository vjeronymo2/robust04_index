BFN [Text] The Armed Forces Command has issued the military communique No. 27, saying that our armed forces today continued their crushing blows on the invading forces at all battlefronts, particularly in Abyan Governorate and in al-Anad-Karish-al-Rahidah axis. The communique said that our forces in Abyan continued their offensive operations as suicidal forces stormed the strongholds of these forces. The total estimates of these operations were destruction of six tanks, two missile vehicles, and that five tanks were captured by our forces.
