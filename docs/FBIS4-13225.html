BFN [Text] Dear listeners, we have just received the following statement from the presidential council of the Kurdistan region on the destruction of the two helicopters in the airspace of Kurdistan: O esteemed citizens, o masses of our struggling people; on 14 April, two helicopters belonging to the coalition states, which were entrusted with defending the security of Kurdistan, were destroyed when they were fired upon by mistake by coalition fighter planes. The incident left all the passengers of the two helicopters dead. As a sign of our appreciation for their sacrifice and spirit of cooperation in the service of human rights and democracy in Kurdistan, we have decided to declare a three-day general mourning period, effective today, 15 April. Prayers will be performed in all mosques and bells will be tolled in all churches for this painful event. All media networks in Kurdistan shall comply with this decision.
