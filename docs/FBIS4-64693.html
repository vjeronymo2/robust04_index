BFN [Text] Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev has arrived on a visit in Ankara. Aliyev and the delegation accompanying him were received by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin and other officials. Replying to reporters' questions, Aliyev said that the Partnership for Peace plan signed by NATO member countries in Brussels will be very beneficial for the countries that have joined the partnership. He added that the partnership principles will make significant contributions to the solution of the Azerbaijani-Armenian conflict. Aliyev was later received by President Suleyman Demirel at the Cankaya Mansion. Aliyev will return to Azerbaijan today.
