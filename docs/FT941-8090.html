940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met strong demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. The small quantity of Assams on offer readily featured firm to dearer but closed about steady. Brighter liquoring ceylons moved 3 to 5p higher while prices for most others were irregularly lower. Offshore: good demand. Brighter teas appreciated 3 to 5p and plainer types sold well at last levels. Quotations: quality 195p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 116p/kg, low medium 84p/kg.
