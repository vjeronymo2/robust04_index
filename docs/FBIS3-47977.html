Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 21 -- Malaysian National News Agency (BERNAMA) held a ceremony this evening to inaugurate its bureau here. Among those present at the event were Vice Foreign Minister Le Mai, General Director of VIETNAM NEWS AGENCY Do Phuong, Editor-in-Chief of the daily NHAN DAN newspaper Huu Tho, General Director of Vietnam Television Ho Anh Dung and other Vietnamese officials. On the Malaysian side were Editor-in-Chief of BERNAMA Abdul Rahman Sulaiman and Ambassador to Vietnam Cheah Sam Kip. Addressing the ceremony, Mr. Sulaiman thanked Vietnamese concerned offices for their assistance to BERNAMA to open its bureau in Hanoi, which, he said, constitutes an opportunity to contribute to the strengthening of mutual understanding between the two countries for the sake of peace, stability and development in the region. In his turn, Vice Foreign Minister Le Mai noted that in recent years, the relations of friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Malaysia had constantly developed. The opening of a BERNAMA bureau in Hanoi is a vivid manifestation of the said relations, he added. Mr Mai further said Vietnamese side would create favourable conditions for BERNAMA to perform its professional activities in order to further consolidate the fine relations between the two countries.
