930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / London Stock Exchange: BAe in demand British Aerospace (BAe) featured strongly. SG Warburg was said to have cleared an overhanging line of stock but then sought to replenish its trading books. The shares were 13 1/2 ahead at 272 1/2 p by the close. Further buying came from agency broker Henderson Crosthwaite, a long-time bull of the stock. Mr Brian Newman at the house predicted a sharp turnround at the Rover cars subsidiary as well as further defence contracts. He now believes the break-up value of the group to be in the region of 600p. Trading volume in BAe reached 4.1m shares.
