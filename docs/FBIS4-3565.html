BFN [Text] Beijing, April 22 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor Chen Junsheng, leading an agricultural delegation, left here today on a goodwill visit to Morocco and Mauritania. The 12-member delegation will explore ways with officials of the two countries on bilateral cooperation in the fields of agriculture, animal husbandry and fisheries. Among those seeing the delegation off at the airport were Liu Jimin, deputy secretary-general of the Chinese State Council, Wu Yixia, vice-minister of agriculture, and diplomatic envoys from Morocco and Mauritania.
