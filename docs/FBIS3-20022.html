Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Madrid, Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez is meeting Nurulstan Nazarbayev, president of the Republic of Kazakhstan, with whom he will hold a joint news conference on his state visit to Spain. Nazarbayev will have lunch with Gonzalez and will visit the Senate and the Madrid town hall during this second day of his official visit to Spain. On 23 March, during the dinner held in honor of President Nazarbayev, King Juan Carlos I of Spain highlighted his appreciation of Kazakhstan's contribution to stability in Europe and the world -- a contribution made by joining the nuclear nonproliferation treaty and by signing disarmament treaties. Earlier, Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana and his Kazakhstan counterpart signed agreements that form the legal basis for cooperation. Solana announced that the Spanish state will guarantee credits of up to $25 million for Spanish exports to Kazakhstan.
