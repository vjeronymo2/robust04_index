October 7, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

In other nonleague games:

Orange Lutheran 33, L.A. Baptist 14 -- The Knights lost despite outgaining Orange Lutheran, 231 yards to 147.

Three touchdowns were scored in 25 seconds in the third quarter after Orange Lutheran had built a 20-0 lead. L .A. Baptist scored on quarterback Dane Brown's seven-yard run. Phil Matheson returned the ensuing kickoff 91 yards for an Orange Lutheran score, then Orange Lutheran's Lee Back scooped up a fumble on the following kickoff and ran 21 yards to give the hosts a 33-6 lead with 4:38 left in the third quarter. TONY SOLTIS
