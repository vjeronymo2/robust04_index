920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / Observer: Persistent Not everyone at yesterday's Saatchi and Saatchi AGM seemed overjoyed by the hard work the advertising giant had put into getting the Tory Party re-elected. One questioner reminded chairman Maurice Saatchi that, on being asked last year about the truth of newspaper reports that Saatchi was again going to work for the Conservatives in the election, he had just fallen back on the old cliche about not believing everything that you read in the press. 'I now see in the newspapers that Saatchi is going to work for Ross Perot in his bid to become US president. Is that true?', asked the questioner. With scarcely a pause for breath, Maurice Saatchi said: 'I am afraid the reply is the same this year, too.'
