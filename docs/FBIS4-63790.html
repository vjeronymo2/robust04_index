BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow May 25 TASS -- Early Wednesday morning, Georgian artillery began shelling Abkhazian positions in the Kodor gorge region. The firing is coming from the village of Kapchar, controlled by Georgian military groups, ITAR-TASS learned from Abkhazia's official representation in Russia. According to the Abkhazian representatives, more than 250 shells and mortars were fired at the Abkhazian positions on Tuesday. The shelling began at 18:20 and stopped at 22:30, and resulted in the wounding of four Abkhazian military personnel.
