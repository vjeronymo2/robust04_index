December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FORE THE RECORD: VINES PLAYED BING AT LAKESIDE

The major account on Ellsworth Vines (Dec. 11) was splendid. However, may I note that Ellie never played rounds with Bing Crosby at Lakewood Country Club in Toluca Lake. It was and is Lakeside Golf Club.

NATHAN A. TUFTS

Burbank
