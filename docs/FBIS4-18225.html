BFN [Excerpt] Landshut, Germany, March 31 (AFP) -- Numerous Germans have lodged complaints of an inflammatory statement by the leader of the extreme right-wing Republikaner party, Franz Schoenhuber, the Landshut prosecutor's office said Thursday [31 March]. A senior official said they had received complaints from outraged private citizens "by the pound" and expected to receive more, after Schoenhuber's remarks about the president of the Council of Jews in Germany, Ignatz Bubis. He said prosecutors were studying the possibility of legal action against the former Waffen-SS officer and member of the European Parliament, who Saturday accused Bubis of being "the worst inciter of race hatrid in Germany."
