Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Sarajevo, 7 Feb (TANJUG) -- At a news conference in Sarajevo today, Srpska republic president Radovan Karadzic described the U.S. Administration's request that Saturday's crime at a Sarajevo market be investigated as "based on principles." [passage omitted] Commenting on the talks yesterday with Akashi, Stoltenberg, and Owen, Karadzic said that a draft agreement on placing Sarajevo under a UN protectorate and on putting an end to the fighting in the area had been a good idea, but that it had never been signed due to the behavior of the Muslim side. "We are ready to revive this issue in Geneva on 10 February in order to reach a partial peace, since the solution of the Sarajevo issue would lead to the resolution of the conflict in other areas of Bosnia-Herzegovina too," Karadzic said.
