931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Total Systems falls to Pounds 12,000 Slow economic recovery was blamed by Total Systems, the USM-quoted computer systems company, for a slump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 424,436 to Pounds 12,330 for the half year ended September 30. Sales fell to Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.36m). Operating losses were Pounds 31,690 (Pounds 364,791 profits) but there was interest income of Pounds 44,020 (Pounds 59,745). Earnings were 0.078p (2.85p) and the interim dividend is passed (1.5p last time).
