930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Exceptional leaves Comac at Pounds 60,000 Comac Group, the USM-quoted computer consultant, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 60,000, against Pounds 44,000, for the half year ended June 30. These were the first results since Mr Philip Swinstead, the founder and former chief executive of SD Scicon, took a controlling interest and installed a new management. The profit was after an exceptional charge of Pounds 120,000 (Pounds 50,000). Turnover was Pounds 5.3m, compared with Pounds 4.91m. Earnings per share were up from 0.44p to 0.65p.
