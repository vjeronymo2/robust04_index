Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on March 21 sent a message of greetings to President Abdur Rahman Biswar of the People's Republic of Bangladesh on the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of the independence of Bangladesh. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the president and the people of Bangladesh success in their work for the independent development and prosperity of the country and expressed the belief that the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries would develop further.
