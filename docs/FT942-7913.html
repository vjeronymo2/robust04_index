940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Viag - Other companies The transport to energy conglomerate will present its 1993 results on Wednesday, with earnings expected to be about 20 per cent lower than the DM371m (Dollars 220m) reported for the previous year. Analysts said earnings per share were forecast at about DM16, with the dividend likely to remain unchanged at DM9. The group recently took over Bayernwerk, Germany's third largest utilities group, and with annual turnover of about DM40bn, it is among Germany's top 10 companies in sales terms.
