920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 80m work for Tilbury Douglas The construction division of the recently created TILBURY DOUGLAS GROUP has been awarded a range of new contracts worth more than Pounds 80m. They include Pounds 14m-worth of work for the privatised water companies; a Pounds 10m package for clients in the petrochemical industry; more than Pounds 2m for British Rail; and over Pounds 3m of work for the privatised electricity companies. Tilbury Douglas has also been awarded two separate contracts for mechanised letter sorting offices by the Post Office, together worth in excess of Pounds 11m. Other smaller building contracts include awards of over Pounds 4m from the public sector and more than Pounds 15m for large private sector clients. They include projects for Safeway, British Steel, Arlington Developments and the Royal Bank of Scotland, along with Pounds 3m office projects for both Siemens Plessey and Refuge Assurance.
