940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / UK Company News: United Industries United Industries, the precision tools and springs maker, has sold its Scandura Railko offshoot - just five months after its acquisition as part of a package from BBA Group. The lossmaking business, which makes heat shields and gasket materials, is being sold to Hofland (UK), a subsidiary of DNI Holding of the Netherlands, for Pounds 605,000. United will retain debtors and creditors, estimated to be worth Pounds 200,000.
