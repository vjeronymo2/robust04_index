Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The war situation prevails in Lunda Sul Province, with government troops on the offensive. This was revealed yesterday by the Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FALA] North West Command. The source said that over the weekend the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party [MPLA-PT] troops attacked the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] in Caiaza area, on Saurimo-Ndala and Lucapa-Saurimo roads. There were also clashes in the Mona Quimbundo area, where the People's Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FAPLA] abducted some peasants in the villages. The North West command has described these operations as coward provocations to which FALA responded promptly, killing 12 FAPLA soldiers.
