940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / International Company News: Paramount business to reorganise Paramount Publishing, which is to acquire Macmillan, is to reorganise divisions, cut the number of imprints, and lay off up to 10 per cent of the two companies' 10,000 workers, The New York Times reported, Reuter reports from New York. Paramount's Dollars 535m purchase of Macmillan is expected to be completed in February. The result would give Paramount Publishing, parent company of Simon & Schuster and subsidiary of Paramount Communications, about Dollars 2bn in annual revenues.
