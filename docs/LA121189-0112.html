December 11, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

HOTEL, OFFICE PROJECTS AWAIT COUNCIL VOTE

Major decisions on two of Garden Grove's biggest redevelopment projects, a downtown office-retail complex and a three-story hotel, are expected at tonight's City Council meeting. The office-retail project would be at the northwest corner of Garden Grove Boulevard and Euclid Street, an 8.5-acre site which has been vacant for nearly a decade.

IDM of Long Beach would build a five-story office building and several one-story retail and office structures. Construction would begin in January, 1991.

The council also is scheduled to vote on La Quinta Inns' plan to build a three-story, 168-room hotel on a 2.8-acre site at the northwest corner of Garden Grove Boulevard and Haster Street.
