910426 FT 26 APR 91 / UK Company News: Brixton Estate nears Pounds 24m Profits before tax of Brixton Estate, the property investment group, rose from Pounds 20.43m to Pounds 23.58m for 1990. Net rental income expanded by Pounds 7.61m to Pounds 43.32m. After tax of Pounds 5.8m (Pounds 4.86m) earnings emerged at 10.79p (9.45p) per share. A final dividend of 4.55p makes a 7.1p (6.1p) total. At the year-end net asset value per share was down 11.8 per cent at 246p. Gearing was 66 per cent compared with 51 per cent at the end of 1989.
