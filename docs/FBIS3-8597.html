Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Presidency of the Party for Democratic Prosperity [PDP] branch in Switzerland reports that in a meeting in Zurich on 12 March, the presidency decided that this party branch will meet a part of the unpaid obligation of the Albanian population in the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia concerning the investments for the satellite program project. This branch will ensure a sum of 300,000 Swiss francs until 1 May 1994. The money will be deposited in the account of the satellite program of the Albanian Radio-Television.
