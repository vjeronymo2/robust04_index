930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / UK Company News: ICV acquires SE's Market-Eye service ICV, a specialist collector and distributor of real-time data services, is acquiring Market-Eye, the Stock Exchange's real-time datacast price information service, from August 1. Mr David Taylor, managing director of ICV, which has been managing the service for six months, said Market-Eye would complement its new Topic3 product range to be launched in September.
