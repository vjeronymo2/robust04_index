940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Countrywide Bank Countrywide Banking Corporation, the New Zealand subsidiary of Bank of Scotland, achieved pre-tax profits of NZDollars 41.5m (Pounds 16m) for the year to December 31. The post-tax figures showed a profit of NZDollars 27.5m, against a loss of NZDollars 50.7m, after extraordinary costs of the merger with United Bank. Over the year assets grew by 21 per cent to NZDollars 4.25bn (NZDollars 3.41bn).
