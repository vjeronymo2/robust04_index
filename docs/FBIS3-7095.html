Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Report by Cha Man-sun, from Vienna] [Text] International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] Director General Hans Blix will participate in the unofficial UN Security Council [UNSC] on the afternoon of 24 March to give an oral report on the results of North Korea's inspection and on the details of how the IAEA Board of Governors came to adopt a resolution against North Korea. At the meeting, Director General Hans Blix will express deep regret over the fact that North Korea's nuclear issue has fallen into a deadlocked situation due to North Korea's uncooperative measures and will emphasize that strong measures at the UN Security Council level are unavoidable. The IAEA will refrain from taking independent action regarding future inspections on North Korea, but will act upon the UNSC's countermeasures.
