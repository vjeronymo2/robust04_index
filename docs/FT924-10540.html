921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Cullens swings Pounds 16,000 into the red Cullens Holdings, the food retailer, swung into the red with a loss at the pre-tax level of Pounds 16,000 for the six months to August 30. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 370,000 last time and profits of Pounds 754,000 at the March 1 year end. The deficit was struck after exceptional costs of Pounds 113,000 (Pounds 38,000 profits) which comprised mainly abortive site development costs and losses incurred on new sites prior to full trading. Gross turnover for the six months was Pounds 10.9m, against Pounds 10.1m in the comparable period. Losses per share came out at 0.1p (earnings 1.3p).
