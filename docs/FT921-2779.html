920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / Letter: Devaluation Sir, I am surprised to find the fear of sterling devaluation among business chiefs. In my opinion, devaluation within the ERM is a good boost for economic recovery and ultimate expansion of British business and reduction of unnecessary imports. F S Dalal, F S Dalal & Co, 119-123 Cannon Street Road, London E1 2LX
