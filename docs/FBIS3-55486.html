Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Groznyy, 10 Dec (CHECHENPRESS-TASS) -- The body of Georgian ex-president Zviad Gamsakhurdia will be buried in Chechnya, Shamsuddin Yusef, the Chechen Republic's foreign minister announced at a briefing in Groznyy today. He added that this was in accordance with the wishes of Gamsakhurdia's family. According to Shamsuddin Yusef, necessary permission for burying the ex-president in Chechnya has been received from the Georgian leadership.
