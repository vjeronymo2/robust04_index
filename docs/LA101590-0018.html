October 15, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Humans vs. Terminator Again: It seems there's no terminating Arnold Schwarzenegger in the role of the murderous and unstoppable cyborg.

Schwarzenegger will star in a sequel, set for release next summer. Also appearing in "Terminator 2: Judgment Day" is former "Beauty and the Beast" star Linda Hamilton. Schwarzenegger returns on a mission to kill Hamilton's son, who is destined to lead the Human Resistance against machines. James Cameron will direct the film for Carolco Pictures. Cameron co-wrote and directed the original "The Terminator" and has also written and directed "Aliens" and "The Abyss." CLAUDIA PUIG
