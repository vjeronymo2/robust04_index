Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A total of 500 Malaysian troops serving with the United Nations, UN, have been deployed in Bosnia-Herzegovina after being delayed in Croatia. Defense Minister Datuk Sri Najib Tun Razak said 65 men in two platoons were deployed around the Mount Igman area overlooking the besieged capital of Sarajevo while the rest were in Konjic and Visoko. He said the delay was due to the land route through neighboring Croatia to Bosnia proper being blocked by obstacles and the winter weather conditions. He told this to reporters after receiving 7,200 packets of goodies for the end of Ramadan celebration. The goodies will be flow by Hercules transport aircraft to the Malaysian troops in Bosnia. A total of 2,555 Malaysian military personnel are serving with the UN peacekeeping operation worldwide, including Somalia and their staff officers at the world body headquarters in New York.
