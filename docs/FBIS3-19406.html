Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Algerian Embassy in Paris has announced that Hocine Djoudi, Algeria's ambassador to France, was received today by Philippe Seguin, president of the French National Assembly. Their talks covered issues of mutual interest and cooperation between the two countries, particularly through the French-Algerian Friendship Society, chaired by Hocine Djoudi. The two men also discussed problems concerning the Mediterranean region.
