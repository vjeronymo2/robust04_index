July 28, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

DH Technology: The computer firm reported a profit of $1.5 million on second-quarter revenue of $10.5 million, up from a profit of $859,000 on revenue of $8.3 million over the same three months last year. A supplier of computer printer heads, DH Technology reported a six-month profit of $2.7 million on revenue of $19.9 million, up from a profit of $1 million on revenue of $11.4 million for the same two quarters last year.
