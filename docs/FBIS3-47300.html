Language: English Article Type:BFN [Statement by "the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea spokesman" issued 9 January; place not given -- read by announcer] [Text] The National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK]'s spokesman rejects the fallacious accusations about the Thai Army helping the Khmer Rouge by firing artillery shells against the puppet troops. 1. Of late, there have been accusations about the Thai Army helping the Khmer Rouge by firing artillery shells against communist Vietnamese puppet troops. 2. The NADK spokesman categorically and totally rejects those (?shameless) accusations. 3. It is common knowledge that the communist Vietnamese puppet troops of the two-headed government has been conducting the military offensives against the Democratic Kampuchea party and the Cambodian people at the current 16th dry season. Those fallacious accusations are part of their bad political scheme aimed at concealing their defeats on the battlefield and (?against) the national reconciliation policy and the five-point guideline of his majesty the king toward national reconciliation, peace, and national unity in Cambodia. [Dated] 9 January 1994
