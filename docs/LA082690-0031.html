August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD NOTES

Caltrans plans the following closures on area freeways this week:

All lanes of the eastbound Foothill Freeway (210) will be closed today in Pasadena from the Windsor Avenue over-crossing to the Foothill Freeway-Ventura Freeway (134) interchange from 6 a.m. to 3 p.m.

One lane of the eastbound Pomona Freeway (60) will be closed Tuesday in Monterey Park from Eastern Avenue to Atlantic Boulevard from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.

Two lanes of the westbound San Bernardino Freeway (10) will be closed Wednesday in West Covina at the Sunset Avenue-Vincent Avenue on-ramp and at the Sunset Avenue off-ramp from 9:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.
