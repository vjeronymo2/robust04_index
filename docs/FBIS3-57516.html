Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with the agreement of Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and U.S. President Bill Clinton, a Ukrainian Government delegation led by Economic Minister Roman Shpek has arrived in Washington. The delegation also includes Viktor Yushchenko, Ukrainian National Bank chairman, several deputy ministers, and a group of leading Ukrainian experts. The delegation was met at the airport by Oleh Bilorus, Ukrainian ambassador to the United States, and embassy officials. The delegation is scheduled to hold negotiations on a wide range of issues connected with bilateral economic cooperation. U.S. aid intended for stabilization, restoration, and radical reform of the Ukrainian economy based on the market principles will be central to the negotiations. The Ukrainian and U.S. delegations held a plenary meeting at the U.S. State Department where they discussed principles and priorities of economic cooperation between the two countries. The Ukrainian delegation also visited the U.S. Department of Agriculture where it met with Secretary of Agriculture Michael Espy. Members of the Ukrainian delegation met with executives from the Foreign Private Investment Corporation and the U.S. Trade Development Agency.
