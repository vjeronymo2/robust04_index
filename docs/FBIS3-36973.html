Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow February 12 TASS -- The State Duma of Russia has demanded today from the Russian Government to take urgent measures to resolve the crisis in the northern regions. A resolution adopted by the Duma has proposed to the executive and legislature to form a working group jointly with trade unions of the north for promptly adopting decisions to find a way out of the social and economic crisis that prevails in northern regions, and also to monitor the implementation of these decisions. While preparing a draft budget for 1994, the government has also been recommended to foresee the purposeful formation of economic mechanisms for finding a way out of the crisis and to ensure social guarantees to citizens living and working in the northern regions. The State Duma has instructed the Federal and Political Affairs Committee of the house, in particular, to prepare a draft concept of a federal law on state policy in northern regions of the Russian Federation and present for consideration of the Duma by April 20 this year. This State Duma has made it binding on this committee to prepare and present for consideration of the house proposals about introducing changes and addenda into the Russian Federation law "on state guarantees and compensations to people living and working in areas of the farthest north and equated to them localities." The Duma considers to give priority to review this draft bill in this year, and also other documents, which will be prepared in context of the social-economic situation created in the northern regions of Russia.
