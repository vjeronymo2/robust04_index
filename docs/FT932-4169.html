930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sanderson Elect static at Pounds 1.8m Despite the economic climate and a disappointing contribution from associates, Sanderson Electronics held first half pre-tax profits at Pounds 1.8m. The group, which supplies a range of computer solutions and maintenance and support services, lifted turnover 14 per cent to Pounds 12.1m and trading profit 8 per cent to Pounds 1.97m. Associates' contribution fell to Pounds 13,000 (Pounds 189,000). Mr Paul Thompson, chairman, said 74 per cent of overheads were covered by annually recurring revenues from maintenance, software licence fees and service and support contracts, which currently provide turnover of about Pounds 10m per year. Profit reflected an 8 per cent increase in the UK although five of the subsidiaries were still operating at 'well below peak levels of profitability'. Earnings per share came to 14.2p (16p) after a higher tax rate. The second interim dividend of 4.5p makes 9.9p (9p).
