921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Hands off the small firms directorate Eleven European organisations representing small and medium-sized businesses have issued a plea for the European Commission to retain a small firms directorate following what they call 'persistent rumours' that DG 23, currently responsible for small firms, is to be dismantled. The business organisations also called for the procedure for business impact assessments, applied to new legislation, to be enhanced.
