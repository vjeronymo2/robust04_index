940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / Lesotho soldiers fight in capital Heavily armed soldiers yesterday tried to storm a rival barracks in street fighting between army factions in the Lesotho capital, Reuter reports from Maseru. 'There have been several civilian casualties, the wounded have been taken to hospital,' according to a western diplomat. He did not know if any civilians had been killed. Diplomats said about 600 soldiers from Makonyane barracks on the outskirts of the city attacked rivals at a smaller barracks in Maseru. There were also reports of further action in the south near the airport. State radio advised people to stay off the streets and not to attempt to drive from the city and the international airport south of the city.
