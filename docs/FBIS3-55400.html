Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko yesterday received Turkish Minister of Transport Mehmet Kestepen and the Turkish ambassador to Kazakhstan. [passage omitted] Both sides understand the importance of facilitating construction of the Trans-Asia railroad and the need for opening in the near future a regular bus service along the Kazakhstan-Uzbekistan-Turkmenistan-Iran-Turkey route. A convoy of cars from Kazakhstan will make the first trip along this route in February. They also supported a proposal by the Turkish side to invite a Turkish-based company to participate in the construction of rail cars, and the prime minister suggested that Germany should take part in implementing this project. The Turkish minister expressed readiness to immediately start addressing some of the specific issues. The head of government expressed hope that cooperation between the two countries will help the joint development of seagoing vessels.
