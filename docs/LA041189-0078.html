April 11, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Rick Wilson, who set a record pace, out-dueled Mark Martin and Rick Mast to win the Budweiser 200 Grand National stock car race at Bristol, Tenn.

Wilson, who set a race qualifying record last Thursday with a lap of 119.299 m.p.h., led on four times for a total of 161 of 200 laps in the 106-mile race.
