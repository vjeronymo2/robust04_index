920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Provident Financial sells Car Care Plan Provident Financial has sold Car Care Plan (Holdings) to Motors Insurance Corporation, a US subsidiary of General Motors. The consideration was Pounds 4.6m cash and a further maximum Pounds 500,000 may be received dependent on Car Care's performance in the rest of 1992. Last year its pre-tax losses were Pounds 1.4m.
