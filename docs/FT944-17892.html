941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / The Labour Party in Blackpool: Euro-leader calls for action on jobs Europe needs a 'socialist soul' to put unemployed people across the European Union back to work, Mrs Pauline Green, leader of the socialist group in the European parliament, told delegates. 'We want action in December at the next European Council meeting to see real investment in human resources, proper training to reskill and upgrade the skills of the workforce,' she said.
