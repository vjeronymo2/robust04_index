940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Wyko makes South African disposal Wyko Group has disposed of Wyko Bearings & Electronics, its wholly owned South African subsidiary, to Hudaco Trading for approximately R10.72m (Pounds 2.1m) in cash. The value of assets and liabilities disposed of is estimated at R11.22m as at December 31. The company, which distributes bearings and power transmission components from 11 branches throughout South Africa, reported pre-tax profits of R348,646 for the year to April 30. Net assets at that date amounted to R5m.
