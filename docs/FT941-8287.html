940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / Save & Prosper to cut card rate Save & Prosper, the investment house, is to cut the interest rates on its Visa card to below 1 per cent from the beginning of March, Andrew Jack writes. The charge of 0.95 per cent a month, which equates to annualised interest of 13.9 per cent, is believed to be the lowest rate charged since credit cards were launched in the late 1960s. Save & Prosper caused a stir in the sector last September when it launched its credit card at rates substantially below the industry average. The investment house said: 'This is to show that we react to cuts in interest rates and are much more responsive than the larger issuers.'
