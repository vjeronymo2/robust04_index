940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Green Property Green Property, the Dublin-based investment group, announced sharply improved interim profits as it drew benefit from the acquisition of Na Mara Investments in February. Pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30 jumped to IPounds 2.05m (Pounds 2.02m) against IPounds 860,000 last time, and took in a surplus of IPounds 1.09m from the sale of properties acquired with Na Mara. Earnings per share were 6.78p against a restated 3.42p. The interim dividend is again 1.2p, payable on capital increased by the rights issue earlier this year.
