BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 29 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng left here for home at 05:15 P.M. local time today following a two-day official visit to Mongolia, the last leg of his 12-day five- Asian-nation tour. During Li's stay here, the two sides signed a series of documents, including a treaty on friendly relations of cooperation between the two countries. Mongolian Prime Minister P. Jasray saw the Chinese premier off at the airport.
