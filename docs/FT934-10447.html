931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Business loan plan being considered THE LABOUR party is investigating the use of tax incentives to establish an investment fund to make low-cost loans to small businesses. Mr Robin Cook, shadow trade and industry secretary, said yesterday no decision had been taken on whether a Labour government would inject public money into the fund. 'At this stage of the parliamentary cycle it is vastly too early for us to get round to commitments on where we might put our money three or four years from now,' he said.
