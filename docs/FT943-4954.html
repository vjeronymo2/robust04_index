940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good general demand this week reports the Tea Brokers Association. The first new seasons Assams opened to widespread interest,with selected best lines well in excess of 200 pence. Good mediums were readily absorbed at around valuation. Ceylons were fully firm to several pence dearer for the brighter lines. Quotations: best available 200p, nom good 145p,good medium 117p, low medium 80p. The highest price realised this week was 250p for an assam pf.
