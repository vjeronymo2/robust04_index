940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Investors may get compensation Customers of three financial services firms who have been declared 'in default' may be eligible for compensation, the Investors Compensation Scheme said yesterday. The three, former members of Fimbra, the self-regulatory organisation for financial advisers, are: Brokeraid, trading as Morgan Associates of Worthing; Highcliffe Financial Services of Lymington; and Siltala Fund Managers of Bishop's Stortford.
