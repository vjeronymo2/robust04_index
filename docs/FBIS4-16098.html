BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The management of Russia's unified power grid has proclaimed a state of emergency in the industry. For the first time ever, fuel reserves have dropped to a critical level that poses a real threat of whole regions being cut off from the power supply. In order to bring the situation back to normal, the power engineers have asked consumers to repay their debts and the government to grant the industry credits on preferential terms.
