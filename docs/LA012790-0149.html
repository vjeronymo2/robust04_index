January 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DRINKING TIED TO FATAL MOTORCYCLE ACCIDENT

A motorcyclist who police said apparently had been drinking died Friday night after losing control of his cycle and crashing into a parked truck.

Vincent Gardiner, 21, of Riverside was driving through the parking lot of an apartment complex on the 200 block of West Olive Street when he hit a bump, went airborne and struck a parked truck, La Habra Police Sgt. Phil Stufflebean said.
