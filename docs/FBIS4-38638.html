BFN [Report by unidentified news agency: "Border Barrier Saves Raw Materials"] [Text] Operation "Barrier" -- aimed at putting a stop to smuggling and other illegal activity -- was conducted 16 through 20 May on the border with Estonia and Latvia by units from the Northwest Border District in conjunction with Belorussian [Belarusian] border guards. As a result, contraband worth 905 million rubles [R] -- including 56 tonnes of steel and 25 tonnes of titanium alloys -- was seized in the course of the five days. In total in 1993 the troops of the Northwest Border District seized contraband worth R4.3 billion, and the figure since the start of 1994 is R2 billion. The export of around 2,000 tonnes of nonferrous metals and 600 tonnes of fuels and lubricants has been prevented.
