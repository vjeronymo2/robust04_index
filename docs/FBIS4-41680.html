BFN [Text] A Chinese fishing boat was detained for illegally fishing in Russian territorial waters on Sunday. The arrest follows an operation codenamed Zaslon against poaching and illegal border crossing, launched by the Far Eastern Border District last Thursday. The amount of fines imposed on Russian and foreign vessels since the launching of the operation runs at Rbl 1 billion. More than Rbl 230 million worth of smuggled goods were seized and 38 people, most of them Chinese, were detained for illegal border crossing. Out of 36 Russian vessels inspected, one was impounded on charges of trying to smuggle out a load of non-ferrous metal and large diameter pipes.
