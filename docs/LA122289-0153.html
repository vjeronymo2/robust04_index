December 22, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT: COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE;

A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: WATTSON CHARTER BUILDINGS

Wattson Charter Buildings in Irvine will consist of two four-story office buildings and a four-level parking structure. The project is located on a 259,000-square-foot parcel of land and will contain more than 192,000 square feet of office space. Ground-breaking is scheduled for September, 1990, with completion expected in October.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: Irvine Center Drive and Barranca Parkway

Developer: Wattson Co., Newport Beach

Architect: Strock Architects

Primary Contractor: Lusardi Construction, San Marcos

Number of floors: 4

Square feet: 192,000

Use: Office

Cost: $36 million
