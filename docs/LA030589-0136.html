March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

A slide show of biking and walking tours in Europe will be presented by Progressive Travels, a Seattle-based specialist in adventure-travel itineraries, at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday in the Plaza Room, Hyatt Wilshire, 3515 Wilshire Blvd., Los Angeles. Admission free. For more information, call (800) 245-2229.

-- -- --

A slide show on "Sharing the Canadian Wilderness" will be presented by REI, an outdoor gear and clothing store, at 6:30 p.m. Thursday at the store, 3029 E. University Ave., San Diego. Admission free. For more information, call (619) 295-7700.

-- -- --

The British Columbia Trade and Tourism Office has officially opened at 2600 Michelson Drive, Suite 1050, Irvine 92715. For travel information, call (800) 663-6000 or (714) 852-1054.

-- -- --

For the latest information on skiing in Austria, call (213) 479-0940. This report is updated twice a week, Mondays and Wednesdays. Also, skiing information is available at the Austrian National Tourist Office, 11601 Wilshire Blvd., Suite 2480, Los Angeles, or call (213) 477-3332. NORMAN DASH
