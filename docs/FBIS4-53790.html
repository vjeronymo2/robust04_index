BFN [Text] Seoul, May 20 (YONHAP) -- A North Korean logger smuggled himself to South Korea on Friday after escaping from a North Korea- run logging yard in Siberia, the Agency for National Security Planning (NSP) reported. NSP said the man, identified only as Chang Il-un, age 30, sought an asylumn in the South on arrival at the Inchon harbor aboard a foreign freighter which he boarded clandestinely. NSP did not say where he got on board the ship. The agency was looking into the background leading to his coming to South Korea.
