Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 2 -- A delegation of Vietnam's National Assembly [NA] led by Chairman Nong Duc Manh held talks here this afternoon with a visiting delegation of the Cambodian National Assembly led by Chairman Samdech Chea Sim. Speaking at the talks, the two chairmen informed each other of the activities of the two legislative bodies, the socio-economic situation of the two countries and international and regional issues of common concern. The two sides also exchanged views on important issues in order to strengthen the friendship and mutually beneficial cooperation between the two NA, the two peoples, thus contributing to peace, development and cooperation in the region. In the evening, Chairman Manh gave a banquet in honour of Samdech Chea Sim and his party.
