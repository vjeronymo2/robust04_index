April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Norton Agrees to $1.9-Billion Takeover: Norton Co. accepted a friendly $1.9-billion merger offer from Compagnie de Saint-Gobain of France, thwarting a rival hostile bid from a British conglomerate.

The $90-a-share bid by Saint-Gobain, a French maker of fiberglass and industrial ceramics, surpasses the $75-a-share tender offer made by BTR PLC of Britain. BTR's bid was worth about $1.64 billion. Worcester, Mass.-based Norton is a leading manufacturer of machine abrasives.
