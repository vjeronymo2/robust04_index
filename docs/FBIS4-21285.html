1993 Reported 94WD0317A Kingston THE DAILY GLEANER in English 20 Feb 94 p 24 94WD0317A Kingston THE DAILY GLEANER English CSO [Article: "Canadian on Drug Rap"] [Text] Police have arrested and charged the second Canadian in eight days for breaches of the Dangerous Drug Act. Arrested and charged is Clivette Wood, 33, of Ontario, Canada. She is scheduled to appear before the Montego Bay RM Court tomorrow. According to the police report, Wood was in the process of boarding an outgoing flight to Canada, from the Donald Sangster International Airport on Friday, when she was searched and .45 kilograms of ganja found strapped to her body. Last weekend another Canadian, 41-year-old Jean Lamount, of Quebec, was held at the Donald Sangster International Airport and a quantity of hash oil found in his possession. Last year 1,096 foreigners were arrested and charged under the Dangerous Drug Act; 1,030 were held for attempting to export ganja, 52 were held for cocaine and 14 for smoking ganja, 144 of the offenders were from Canada. Meanwhile, the police are also reporting that 22.7 kilograms of ganja was found in an empty container at the Montego Bay Freeport yesterday morning. Security personnel carrying out routine check found the drug concealed in the container, which was about to be loaded on a cargo ship destined for Chicago, United States. No arrest was made.
