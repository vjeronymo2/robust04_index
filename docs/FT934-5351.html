931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Strong asset growth at China Inv Trust China Investment Trust, which came to the market in May, reported a net asset value of 111p per share as at September 30. The figure had increased to 142.2p per share by November 25. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 47,259 in the period to end-September, for earnings of 0.3p per share.
