BFN [Text] The rains that have fallen in Santiago de Cuba Province since 16 May have caused considerable agricultural damage. The sugar harvest has been completely paralyzed because of road conditions and flooding in the sugarcane fields. Meanwhile, other crops have also reported serious damage. In Segundo Frente Municipality, 50,000 plantain plants, 2,500 coffee trees, a coffee plant nursery, and many caballerias of mixed vegetables have been lost. In the Julio Antonio Mella region, 50 caballerias of sugarcane are flooded. Provincial authorities estimate losses throughout the province.
