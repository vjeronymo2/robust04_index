941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Europe Energy Europe Energy Group, which is transforming itself from a mining group, has announced plans to acquire Helston Garages, a multi-franchise motor dealership based in the south-west of England. Its shares, which trade on the USM, were suspended at 5 1/2 p yesterday, pending a further announcement. The terms of the deal have been agreed in principle and contracts are expected to be signed in the next few weeks. The consideration of about Pounds 10m will be financed via a share issue, which will double the size of its capital.
