April 2, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

IDENTITY OF BIRDS IN PHOTOS HARD TO SWALLOW

I wonder how many comments you will receive about your error in bird identification (March 22)?

The foraging shore bird you pictured, identified as "tern" bears no resemblance to a tern. Most likely it is an American avocet -- which, by strange coincidence, you have pictured in the same edition, in the sports section.

Just wanted you to know we do read the paper carefully!

FRANK ELLIS

Los Alamitos
