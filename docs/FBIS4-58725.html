BFN [Text] This morning, President Liamine Zeroual talked by phone with Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and also with 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Yemeni vice president and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party. During these contacts, the head of state expressed Algeria's great concern over the developments in fraternal Yemen and its hope that there would self-restraint endowed with wisdom, and that they would embark on brotherly and constructive dialogue. A statement by the Foreign Affairs Ministry said that Algeria has received with extreme concern the news this morning from the Yemeni capital Sanaa. The statement adds that Algeria has previously warned of the dangers of this escalation and hopes that the brothers in Yemen will listen to the call for prudence. Algeria also renews its readiness to exert all its efforts to contribute toward containing this crisis.
