April 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FUSS OVER ETHICS AND OFFICEHOLDERS

Despite his numerous personal and political merits, Wright has become an embarrassment to his party, his Congress and his nation. I would urge him to follow the advice given more than a century ago to a leading figure of another nation:

Worthy Speaker, we implore:

Go away and sin no more.

Or if the effort be too great,

Go away, at any rate.

CHARLES BELL

North Hollywood
