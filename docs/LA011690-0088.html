January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

CARBON MONOXIDE CITED IN DISCO DEATHS

About 2,000 people filled the city's main cathedral Monday for a one-hour funeral Mass for the 43 people killed in a discotheque fire.

Meanwhile, medical examiners found fatal levels of carbon monoxide in blood samples taken from nearly all of the victims of the fire early Sunday, said Judge Javier Seoane, who is heading up the judicial investigation in this northeastern city.
