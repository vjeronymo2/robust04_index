October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: THOUSAND OAKS;

2-CAR COLLISION KILLS 1, BLOCKS ROADWAY

One person was killed in an accident Tuesday evening on Santa Rosa Road northwest of Thousand Oaks.

The name of the victim was not available late Tuesday.

The two-car accident, which occurred about 6:50 p.m. between Penelope Place and Las Posas Road, briefly blocked the two-lane road. KIM KASH
