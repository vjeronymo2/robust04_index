BFN [Text] Terje Larsen, the UN secretary general's special envoy, visited Jericho today and met with Sa'ib 'Urayqat and Brigadier General Isma'il al-Hajj. Larsen also spoke with a group of released prisoners in Jericho and heard their complaints about the problems they were encountering in the town. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that a special envoy of French President Mitterrand also arrived in Jericho today. He invited Palestinian policemen to a seminar in France on criminal identification, forensic science, and drug interdiction. The French envoy promised that Paris would allocate $50,000 for the Palestinian prisoners released by Israel. The envoy noted that Holland had contributed $25,000 to the same end.
