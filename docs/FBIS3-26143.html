Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 3 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Wannian, a senior leader of China's armed forces, has pledged to promote the construction and reforms of the People's Liberation Army (PLA) in the new year. Speaking at a meeting here yesterday, Zhang, chief of the general staff of the PLA, said that 1994 is a year crucial to the success of China's reforms, opening up and economic construction. "Present developments make it imperative for us to promote the PLA construction and reforms and comprehensively enhance PLA fighting capabilities in order to provide a powerful guarantee of safety for China's reforms, opening up and modernization drive," he told senior officers. According to General Zhang, in the new year all PLA officers and soldiers must follow Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and his ideas on army construction during the current period. Zhang, who is also a member of the Central Military Commission, said that it is imperative for PLA army officers and soldiers to take the study of Volume 3 of the "Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping" and the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics as a primary task. The general also stressed the importance of improving political consciousness and military skills of officers and troops, and of stepping up the reinforcement of leading bodies at all levels.
