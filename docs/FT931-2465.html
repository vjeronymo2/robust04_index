930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Observer: Norman not stormin' Wearied by the Imro investigation, writs from Maxwell pensioners, staff defections and so on, senior staff at Invesco MIM in London sat down with the new boss of the UK and European business, Norman Riddell, hoping for some fresh inspiration. Alas, instead of fire from the belly of this low-key if determined Scot, came the opening gambit: 'Well . . . what can I say?'
