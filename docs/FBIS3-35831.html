Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with the results of a poll held by the Center of Research in Political Culture of Russia, the State Duma's Speaker Ivan Rybkin and leader of the biggest faction Russia's Choice Yegor Gaydar were named among the most influential deputies of the parliament's lower chamber. Specialists in political science and journalists specializing in analysis of the parliament's work participated in this poll organized by the independent center of political science led by doctor in historical sciences, Sergey Vasiltsev. The rate of popularity of the 20 most influential politicians of January assessed on a 10-point scale is as follows: Rybkin 8.0, Gaydar 6.4, Zhirinovskiy 6.3, Zyuganov 5.7, Baburin 5.5, Lukin 5.5, Travkin 5.3, Shakhray 5.1, Mityukov 4.9, Yavlinskiy 4.4, Lukyanov 4.4, Lapshin 4.4, Shokhin 4.4, Yushenkov 4.3, Zatulin 4.0, Kovalev 4.0, Poltoranin 3.9, Isakov 3.7, Burbulis 3.6 and Vlasov 3.4.
