921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / UK Company News: CountyGlen in talks and dealings halted Shares of CountyGlen, the Irish property investor and trader, were suspended at 60p yesterday following the announcement that it was selling a property and was in talks which might lead to an offer. The company has received an offer of IPounds 900,000 (Pounds 980,000) for a property. It will be recommending acceptance at an extraordinary meeting. If agreed, the company would be left with cash, receivables and some property which is already on the market.
