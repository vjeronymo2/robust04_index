BFN [Text] Following preliminary artillery [fire], 500-strong Armenian divisions, backed by armored vehicles, invaded Azerbaijan's Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic last Monday. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry told INTERFAX that the Armenians launched an attack on the village of Gyuinnet in Nakhichevan's Sadarak region, killing four Azerbaijani servicemen, wounding two and taking three hostage. Baku says that Armenian formations advanced about 5 kilometers into Nakhichevan, consolidating their positions on the occupied heights. INTERFAX has not yet obtained any information from the Armenian Defense Ministry.
