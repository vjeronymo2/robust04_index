930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: Computer plant MILLER CONSTRUCTION has won the contract to design, manage and build a Pounds 20m computer assembly facility at Faulds Park, Gourock. The plant will be occupied by the computer electronics manufacturer Mimtec. Using 'fast-track' construction methods, the facility, located on the side of a hill on former farm land overlooking the Firth of Clyde, will be completed by the end of this year. At its peak, the project will lead to the creation of 200 jobs by Miller Construction.
