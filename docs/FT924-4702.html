921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: No referral for Tomkins/RHM bid The agreed bid by Tomkins for Ranks Hovis McDougall, will not be referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission, the Secretary of State for Trade and Industry said yesterday. The announcementcame as Tomkins' shareholders were deciding on whether to take up their part of the Pounds 653m rights issue which will partially pay for the acquisition. Tomkins share price closed down 1/2 p at 228 1/2 p, well clear of the 200p rights issue price. Tomkins advisers are expected to announce today how much of the rights issue was taken up.
