BFN [Text] Rome, June 2 (AFP) -- The International Atomic Energy Agency has told UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali it can no longer guarantee that North Korea has not used uranium for military purposes, a senior U.S. official said here Thursday [2 June]. An IAEA report to that effect, delivered to Butrus-Ghali Thursday, is also being distributed to the 15 members of the Security Council, he said. Earlier the official said that as soon as the IAEA report was published the United States would ask the UN Security Council to impose sanctions against North Korea.
