BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Yuriy Kozlov] [Text] Moscow, 3 May -- The official visit of Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias to Russia, which was planned for 3-5 May, has been postponed to a later date at the request of the Russian side. The date of the visit has not yet been determined, Grigoriy Karasin, director of the Press and Information Department of the Russian Foreign Ministry, told ITAR-TASS today. The main reason for the postponement is linked to the signing of the agreement "Gaza-Jericho -- First" between the PLO and Israel, which will take place in Cairo tomorrow and which will be attended by Russian Foreign Minister A. Kozyrev, who left for the Egyptian capital today.
