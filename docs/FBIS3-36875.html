Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Popular Front [BNF] Secretary Viktar Ivashkevich has told a BELAPAN correspondent that the BNF leadership has received unofficial information concerning attempts by the Council of Ministers to ban the activities of the BNF and to strip leader Zyanon Paznyak, of the legislative opposition, of his deputy powers. The opposition newspaper SVABODA reported the same in a recent issue. According to SVABODA, the Cabinet of Ministers leadership have requested that the Prosecutor's Office, KGB, and the Interior Ministry find legal grounds to ban the BNF. The Prosecutor's Office reportedly has been asked to consider the possibility of initiating criminal proceedings against Zyanon Paznyak. However, as has been pointed out by Viktar Ivashkevich, the BNF leadership thus far knows nothing about any concrete legal measures taken by law enforcement bodies.
