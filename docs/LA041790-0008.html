April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BONE MARROW DONOR BABY

This was a very private choice. It is, therefore, very disturbing to hear bioethicists criticize that decision and those parents on suspiciously unbecoming grounds.

If it is wrong to have more than one child or a large family in order to protect the family unit, civilization has had it wrong since man moved off of the savannah and into caves. For billions of people throughout the world and for a million years before there was a lady bountiful or a welfare state, having many children was, and remains today, social security and child support and Meals on Wheels all rolled into one. If one child has to go to work in order that the family may eat and pay the rent or even so that siblings may go to school, is it any concern of professional do-gooders?

ARTHUR D. SILK MD

Garden Grove
