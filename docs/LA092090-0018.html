September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Gubernatorial News Hour: Robert MacNeil of PBS' "The MacNeil/Lehrer NewsHour" will talk to California's candidates for governor. MacNeil's conversations with Dianne Feinstein and Pete Wilson will air Oct. 24 at 8 p.m. on KCET Channel 28. The program will be offered to other PBS stations in California. BETH KLEID
