930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System Conditions in the exchange rate mechanism were very calm last week because the D-Mark was under pressure against most currencies as a result of the crisis in Russia. Only 6.18 percentage points separated the strongest currency, the Dutch guilder, from the weakest, the Danish krone. Of the currencies fluctuating in wider bands, the Spanish peseta was the best performer and was closest to the D-Mark in the grid. Currencies, Page 31 Editorial Comment, Page 17
