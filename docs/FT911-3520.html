910424 FT 24 APR 91 / Seven-year statement states record number of power stations being built RECORD numbers of power stations are being built in England and Wales, the seven-year statement issued by National Grid Company disclosed yesterday. Nine new stations, with a total capacity of 7,400MW, have signed agreements for connection to the national electricity grid, NGC said. New stations planned by National Power and PowerGen count for about 3,000MW of the new capacity. The rest is being built by independent power projects, notably the ICI and Enron consortium, which is planning an 1,875MW station on Teeside.
