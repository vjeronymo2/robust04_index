Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (February 2) TURAN--According to local reports, early morning on February 2 Armenian forces launched a large-scale offensive against the TerTerskiy region of Azerbaijan from the town of Agdere (Mardakert). The Armenians managed to advance into some sections of Azerbaijani territory. The same day the village of Burudj of TerTer region was shelled. As a result, 3 civilians died. In the Agdamskiy Region, the units of the Azerbaijani army sucessfully repulsed the attacks of the Armenians.
