940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / London Stock Exchange: Drugs firmer Talk of a merger between Zeneca and Wellcome kept the fire burning for the two stocks which defied the black mood of yesterday's market and improved their prices. Shares of both companies have traded actively in recent sessions with both being held up as potential bid targets. Zeneca gained 8 to 841p and Wellcome improved 14 to 699p. The merger story excited market-making desks which continued to anticipate some significant move in the sector.
