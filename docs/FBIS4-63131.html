BFN [Text] Yerevan ("AZG" daily), 05/07/94 -- The number of employed population in Armenia decreased by 2 percent during the year and reached 511,000 by April 1. According to statistics, 116,000 people have registered in employment offices, seeking for a job. Though the current number of available job positions is about 1684, these are mainly for low skilled labor, and only 233 people were employed. 107,400 people, 68,100 of which women, had the status of unemployed by April 1. The number of unemployed increased 1.4 times compared with the previous year. The number of those receiving unemployment grants decreased by 8,900 compared with the previous month. Only 22.3 percent of unemployed are currently receiving grants. Average unemployment grant was 95.4 drams by April 1. Unemployment ratio in the republic was 6.6 percent in March of this year, having increased by 1.9 points compared with the previous year.
