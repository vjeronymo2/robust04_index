October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL EARNINGS: VALENCIA NATIONAL BANK INCOME RISES TO $169,733

Valencia National Bank said that its net income for the third quarter ended Sept. 30 jumped 36% to $169,733 from $125,044 a year ago. The bank said its total assets on Sept. 30 were $54 million, a 20% increase from a year before, when it had assets of $45 million.
