April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Kevin O'Neill, who earned a reputation as one of the nation's top basketball recruiters during three years as an assistant at Arizona, was named coach at Marquette.

O'Neill, 32, replaces Bob Dukiet, who was fired in March after three tumultuous seasons marked by player-coach conflicts and a 39-46 record, including Marquette's first consecutive losing seasons in 24 years.
