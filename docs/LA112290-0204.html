November 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BACK IN THE U.S.S.R.: The first of the Rev. Robert H. Schuller's new series titled "From Spirit to Spirit" airs Sunday, but to see it you'll have to hop a flight to the Soviet Union. . . . One of Schuller's half-hour inspirational messages will be televised monthly over the next year as part of a contract with Soviet broadcasters signed last October. Carla Rivera and Anne Michaud
