BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, minister of planning and international cooperation in the Palestinian National Authority, said today that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat will visit Jericho at the end of June. Sha'th also announced that the Palestinian-Israeli peace negotiations will resume in one week to settle all the issues left unresolved by the Gaza-Jericho Agreement. Sha'th added that he agreed with Amnon Shahaq, head of the Israeli delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli Liaison Committee, on convening the committee on 27 June to discuss the hand over of the rest of the West Bank to Palestinian self-rule earlier than planned.
