920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / Union deal to end GM strike THE United Auto Workers union said it had reached a tentative agreement with General Motors to end a four-day strike at GM's Lansing, Michigan, car body plant, Reuter reports from Lansing. Mr Ted Hartman, local president of the union, said a vote by its 4,200 members to ratify the agreement was scheduled for today. Mr Hartman declined to comment on the details of the agreement, but said he was pleased with it.
