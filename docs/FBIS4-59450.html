BFN [Text] The U.S. Embassy in Sanaa today issued a statement on the current events in the country. The statement says: The Embassy of the United States of America strongly denounces and condemns any use of missiles and weapons of mass destruction in the current conflict, as this will result in civilian casualties. It calls for the cessation of the use of such weapons. The statement explains that the continued hostilities will lead to more civilian casualties on both sides. The German Embassy, representing the European Unity [EU] chairmanship in Sanaa, issued an EU statement, saying: The ambassadors of EU member states to the Republic of Yemen deeply regret and denounce any use of missiles and illegitimate weapons in the ongoing conflict, as this will result in casualties among the civilians. They urge the immediate cessation of the use of such weapons. The statement explains that reliable reports have been received about civilian deaths and injuries as a result of the use of such weapons.
