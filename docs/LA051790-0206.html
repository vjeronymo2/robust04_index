May 17, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CAL STATE FULLERTON SIGNS FOUR IN SOCCER

Ricky Rodriguez of Marina High School is among four athletes who have signed national letters of intent to play soccer for Cal State Fullerton, school officials announced Wednesday.

Rodriguez, a forward who was an All-Southern Section and All-Sunset League selection, joins Raul Haro of El Camino College, Michael Murphy of West Torrance High School and Chris Walker of Chino High School.
