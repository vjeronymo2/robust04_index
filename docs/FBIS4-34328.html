BFN [From the "First Thing" program] [Text] Cuba and Bahrain have signed a document establishing diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level. The document also notes an interest in strengthening friendly and cooperative relations in different areas of interest. The document adds: This cooperation will be based on respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, noninterference in the two countries' internal affairs, and the pledge to fulfill the UN goals and principles for consolidating international peace and security. The document was signed by Raul Barzaga, director of the Foreign Ministry's North Africa and Middle East Directorate, and (Raboul Rahman Salman Hadj), director of the Bahraini Foreign Ministry's international affairs.
