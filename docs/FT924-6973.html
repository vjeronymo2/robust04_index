921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / International Company News: Bramalea office partnership files for Chapter 11 BRAMALEA, the struggling property group controlled by the Bronfman family, said a limited partnership it owns through one of its US units has filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the US bankruptcy code, Reuter reports. Bramalea said the filing would not affect Bramalea's financial restructuring plan. The limited partnership's sole asset is an office property at Clay Street, Oakland, California. The first mortgage on the property is USDollars 27.5m and is held by a syndicate of three financial institutions.
