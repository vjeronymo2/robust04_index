Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Chernov] [Text] Moscow, 7 Jan -- Law enforcement officials sent by the Georgian head of state to the alleged site of Zviad Gamsakhurdia's death are continuing their work. A duty officer of the Georgian Ministry of Security told ITAR-TASS over the phone that no news has come overnight. The former president's death is not being denied, but no version has been confirmed so far, and no information on the location of the body has been obtained. The duty officer of the Georgian Security Ministry believes that information that can shed light on the death of Zviad Gamsakhurdia can be expected by late morning or early afternoon.
