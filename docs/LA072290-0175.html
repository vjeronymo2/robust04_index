July 22, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

WHY RUSH TO VEGAS?

Am I missing something, somewhere, along the way? Admittedly, there are a lot of projects under way these days that I don't understand. Some of them are highly technical, and thus are beyond me.

But there is one basically simple one that is nonetheless beyond my comprehension.

Will somebody please tell me: What is the burning need -- or for that matter, any need at all -- for a high-speed rail line between Anaheim and Las Vegas?

With so many really worthy projects that could better use the money, I have to ask: Who needs to get from Anaheim to Las Vegas -- or back -- in a hurry? For what reason? And if it's that important, why can't they do it by air?

ALLYN M. SUFFENS

Irvine
