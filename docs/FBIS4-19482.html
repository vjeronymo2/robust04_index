BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- In a telephone conversation with Russian President Boris Yeltsin, Chancellor Helmut Kohl has stressed that the bloodshed in the east Bosnian town of Gorazde must be stopped. "Every effort must be made" to bring this about, Kohl told Yeltsin this morning, according to government spokesman Dieter Vogel. The chancellor called for a lasting peace settlement in the region. Kohl talked to U.S. President Bill Clinton and French President Francois Mitterrand about the situation in Gorazde by telephone yesterday.
