931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / Rebels capture Georgian town Forces aligned with ousted President Zviad Gamsakhurdia yesterday captured Samtredia, an important town in western Georgia, driving back troops loyal to President Eduard Shevardnadze after fierce fighting that left at least 40 people dead, AP reports from Tbilisi. Samtredia is situated on the main road leading from the Georgian capital Tbilisi to the Black Sea port of Poti.
