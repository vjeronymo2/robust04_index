Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow March 14 TASS -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin appointed Aleksandr Vronts Russian deputy communications minister, says a resolution which came to ITAR-TASS today. Viktor Chernomyrdin signed a decree appointing Yuriy Portnov chairman of the Russian Federation's Committee for Affairs of the Youth, Physical Culture and Tourism.
