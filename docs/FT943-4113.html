940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Trafford Park Trafford Park Estates, the property company, reported pre-tax profits for the year to June 30 ahead by 42 per cent from Pounds 4.12m to Pounds 5.84m, including a Pounds 365,000 contribution from property sales. Turnover increased 13 per cent to Pounds 11.5m (Pounds 10.2m). Earnings per share came out at 5.89p (4.34p) and the dividend is raised to 3.15p (2.8p) with a recommended final of 2.2p.
