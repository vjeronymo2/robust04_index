Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Higher Military Committee held a meeting in Sanaa today and discussed the results of the work of the military subcommittees, which are overseeing the return of the military units to their permanent camps before the recent crisis. The committee then issued the following statement: The Higher Military Committee is pleased to announce to the brother citizens that the military commands' response to the committee's decision was very positive. The military units completed their withdrawal from the Ta'izz, Lahij, Ibb, Bab El Mandeb, Ma'rib, Shabwah, and Abyan Governorates. The withdrawal of units toward al-Bayda' and Mukayris also began last night. The return of the units to their previous positions is expected to be completed within the next 24 hours. The Higher Military Committee, while stressing that the spirit of accord, the atmosphere of detente, and the eagerness to maintain the unity of the Armed Forces have prevailed in the circles of all the sons of the Armed Forces, appeals to the political leaders and the military commanders to preserve these achievements, which have satisfied the citizens, and to work to move to the second stage to apply the provisions of the accord document pertaining to the Armed Forces. On this occasion, the Military Committee thanks the political leadership, the military commanders, and the sons of the Armed Forces for their cooperation and their response to the committee out of their national sense and their eagerness to maintain the safety and unity of the dear Yemeni soil. Blessed 'Id and many happy returns.
