930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The white pepper market continued firm with producers reluctant to sell and demand stronger in Europe and the US reports Man Producten. In the European market: white: muntok faq spot was trading at USDollars 2,000 a tonne with Jul/Aug at Dollars 1,975 and Aug/Sep at Dollars 2,000. In the black pepper market Sarawak black label spot was unquoted but Jul/Aug was offered at Dollars 1,200 a tonne. Sarawak yellow label spot was unquoted with Jul/Aug at Dollars 1,250. Sarawak brown label was at Dollars 1,375 for Jul/Aug. Brazil grade 1 spot was at Dollars 1,225 a tonne and Jul/Aug at Dollars 1,150. Indian MG-1 spot was at Dollars 1,250 a tonne and Jul/Aug at Dollars 1,140.
