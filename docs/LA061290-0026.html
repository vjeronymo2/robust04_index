June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Kennedy Crest?: After twice failing the New York Bar exam and having it splashed across the front pages of the state's newspapers, John F. Kennedy Jr. may have felt as if his life were a soap opera.

Now he may star in one. The only son of slain President John F. Kennedy is "in serious negotiations" for a starring role in an upcoming soap opera titled "Families," said the show's executive producer, Ruben Malaret. "He's taking the bar in July and if he fails the exam, he's moving to the West Coast." GREG BRAXTON
