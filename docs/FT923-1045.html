920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: EFM Dragon assets buck dollar trend EFM Dragon Trust had a net asset value of 12.03p at August 31, ahead from 11.75p over the year despite an unfavourable dollar/sterling exchange rate. The trust, which seeks long-term capital growth through investments in the Far East, excluding Japan and Australia, reported net revenue of Pounds 345,000 (Pounds 329,000) for earnings of 0.11p (0.105p) per share. The single dividend for the year is maintained at 0.06p.
