Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh, January 17 (XINHUA) -- A government delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia left here this morning for a five-day official visit to China at the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Peng. The delegation, led by Norodom Ranariddh, Cambodia's first prime minister, and Hun Sen, second prime minister, is expected to visit Beijing and the southern Chinese provinces of Hainan and Guangdong.
