940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / 1993 was a good year for China's advertising industry China's advertising industry had a good year in 1993, with spending 40 per cent up on 1992 in an otherwise flat world market, Saatchi & Saatchi's Beijing representative said yesterday, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. Real advertising growth is expected to continue at 40 per cent for China in 1994, though rates are likely to decline.
