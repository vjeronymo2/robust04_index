BFN [Excerpt] The information and study workshop on the prospects for the Algerian economy, which was set up by the presidency and government and in which members of the government and the main social and economic operators participated, has issued its recommendations. In the recommendations, the participants urge that a start be made this year on drawing up a comprehensive and coherent program for institutional reforms and restructuring, in order to free the initiative of economic operators and reintroduce a market economy mechanism. The participants also recommended that as soon as possible work should begin on drawing up a coherent program to restructure the public sector in order to increase output; work should also begin on the reorganization and diversification of the activities of banks. They also recommended the reintroduction of a system that would encourage partnership. Concerning the social sphere, the participants stressed the importance of bolstering the program for social and economic development, as part of the embodiment of social justice, through the consolidation of the national system of social security and the protection of the purchasing power of those strata who are below the poverty line. They also stressed the importance of bolstering dialogue and national solidarity. [passage omitted]
