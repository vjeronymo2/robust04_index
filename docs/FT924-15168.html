921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: N American buys for Halma Halma, the environmental technology group, is buying IPC Resistors of Toronto and IPC Power Resistors International of Kentucky for Dollars 1.61m (Pounds 900,000) cash with further performance-related payments of up to Dollars 300,000, adjusted to include a Dollars 170,000 discontinued management fee. Assets were valued at Dollars 497,000.
