BFN [Text] According to the central electoral commission, not a single candidate has been registered in Kiev a week before the final date for registration of contenders for candidate for president of Ukraine. Ivan Hulchenko, head of the section for working with parties, has informed UNIAN that as of 18 April the Central Electoral Commission (TsVK) had registered the Socialist and Social-Democratic parties of Ukraine as intending to nominate a contender for candidate for president of Ukraine. Apart from them another four parties (Communist Party of Ukraine, Labor Congress of Ukraine, Party of Free Peasants of Ukraine, and the "State Independence of Ukraine" association) have submitted registration documents. According to Ivan Hulchenko, they have to submit certain information, after which they will receive forms to collect 100,000 signatures in support of their contenders. According to Stepan Hamanyuk, an employee at the registration section of the Central Electoral Commission, to date, a week before the final date for registration, the Central Electoral Commission has not registered a single contender for candidate for president of Ukraine.
