910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Forward Group returns to the black Forward Group, the USM-quoted supplier of specialist services to the electronic industry, returned to the black in the year to January 31. Taxable profits were Pounds 955,000, against losses of Pounds 690,000 last time and Mr Ray Chamberlain, chairman, said: 'I shall be disappointed if the current year, with all the difficulties of a continuing recession, does not show another satisfactory result.' Turnover rose to Pounds 10.5m (Pounds 8.46m) and operating profits emerged at Pounds 1.23m (Pounds 369,000). After an exceptional gain of Pounds 118,000 (debit Pounds 720,000) relating to money received from a property disposal offset by re-organisation costs, and a tax charge of Pounds 307,000 (credit Pounds 208,000), earnings per share came out at 8.95p (losses 6.66p). A final dividend of 1.2p (nil) is proposed for a total of 2.2p (1p).
