BFN [Text] Havana, 6 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- The Cuban Border Guards Unit refrained from attacking a vessel on which 61 persons fled the island last weekend, a government source said today. The guards, who had sighted minors aboard, limited themselves to escorting the vessel until it had left Cuban jurisdictional waters. The U.S. Coast Guard rescued the vessel on Saturday in international waters off the Florida Keys; there were seven wounded aboard. The Cuban Foreign Ministry refused to comment on the incident.
