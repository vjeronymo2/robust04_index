BFN [Text] The country's Scientific Research Council warned today that the irregular increase in the consumption of oil products and energy waste will threaten our country's oil exports and create more problems for the country's oil-related income over the next decade. According to our correspondent's report, the Scientific Research Council's energy committee today issued a report entitled "Perspectives of Energy Research in the Islamic Republic of Iran" at a seminar in which lecturers, researchers, and the country's executive officials in the energy sector were present. The report says: It is estimated that our country consumes more than one million barrels of oil each day. Even with the present low oil prices, the country is consuming around $5 billion worth of oil each year. That makes the need to rationalize our country's energy consumption, to develop consumption patterns, and to expand research into energy saving unavoidable . The secretary of the state Scientific Research Council's energy committee also said: If the country's present trend of energy consumption continues, we will need to build a new refinery each year and that would cost a yearly investment of more than $1.5 billion. He added: Savings of 5.5 percent in energy consumption is realistic and can reduce foreign currency expenditure equivalent to $1.5 billion for investment in refining oil.
