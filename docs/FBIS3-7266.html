Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, March 26 (OANA/BERNAMA) -- Malaysian national oil corporation (Petronas), is prepared to expand its scope of cooperation with Vietnam, particularly in providing training for Vietnamese in gas and petroleum operations. Petronas Senior Vice-President (Finance) Mohd Hassan Marican said Saturday the oil corporation had trained more than 200 Vietnamese to date in various aspects of gas and oil operations. Speaking to reporters after the visit by Vietnamese Communist Party Secretary-General Do Muoi to the Petronas Penapisan (M) Sdn.Bhd. [Malaysia Private Limited] Refinery in Tangga Batu, in Southwestern Melaka state, he said the training was held in Vietnam and Malaysia.
