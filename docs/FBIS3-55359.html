Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Due to Latvian State President Guntis Ulmanis' illness, talks between him and Max van der Stoel, the CSCE's high commissioner for national minorities, scheduled for today did not take place. Max van der Stoel is visiting Riga to discuss with Latvian Government representatives and parliament the problems of minorities and the Latvian bill on citizenship which is being discussed by the Saeima.
