931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pimento market was firm on increased buying interest from central europe, reports Man Producten. Spot supplies in Europe were very limited. Mexican supplies were offered at USDollars 1,850 a tonne, cif, while Jamaicans were unchanged at Dollars 2,300 for spot and Dollars 2,100 for shipment. Madagascan cloves were at a premium with Dollars 925 a tonne charged for cif supplies and Dollars 1,000 for spot. Indonesian cassia prices were unchanged with VA/KA sticks at Dollars 2,400 spot, and Dollars 2,300 afloat. Seychelles spot supplies were not available with new arrivals awaited from local suppliers.
