Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Mar (MENA)--Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf and Talal al-Hasan, Jordanian minister of state for foreign affairs, left Cairo this afternoon for home after a three-day visit to Egypt. During their visits, the two ministers participated in the Arab League Council's 101st session that began at the Arab League headquarters last Saturday.
