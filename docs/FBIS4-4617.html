BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- A ceremony for awarding state decorations and gifts to delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries was held here Saturday. Present at the ceremony were Kye Ung-tae, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], Kim Chung-nin and Kim Kuk-tae, secretaries of the WPK Central Committee, leading officials of the WPK Central Committee and the delegates to the conference. Citations of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were awarded to 121 delegates to the conference who have set examples in implementing the party policy and carrying out their revolutionary tasks by pushing ahead with the three revolutions, ideological, technical and cultural, in hearty response to the programme of modelling the whole society on the chuche idea. The Order of National Flag first class was conferred on 56 delegates, the Labour Order on 852 delegates, the Order of National Flag second class on 2,957 delegates and the Order of National Flag third class on 4,305 delegates. Gifts of the WPK Central Committee were awarded to the entire delegates.
