BFN [Text] Sanaa, May 20 (AFP)--The Yemen Government will observe a three-day cease-fire following 15 days of fighting with southern forces, the northern-controlled SABA' news agency reported Friday. The ceasefire, effective from Saturday 0:00 a.m. (Friday 2100 GMT), was declared on the occasion of the Muslim 'Id al-Adha festival prior to Sunday's celebration of the fourth anniversary of the unification of South and North Yemen, the agency said.
