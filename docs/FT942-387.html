940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Alfred McAlpine rights 76% taken up Alfred McAlpine has received 9.52m acceptances for its 12.6m rights issue at 205p per share, representing a 75.7 per cent take-up. Subscribers have been procured for the remaining 3.05m shares for which acceptances were not received, at a premium of 0.1p per share. The company announced the issue on June 2 with a call for Pounds 25m to finance land purchases. The shares fell 1p to 206p on the news.
