November 9, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LET'S PARTY: After campaigning for months for lieutenant governor, after staying up election night to see herself lose by more than 700,000 votes, after barely two hours' sleep, what did Marian Bergeson do Wednesday morning? . . . She and six campaign staffers went to Disneyland and stayed until the park closed. "The staff had planned it, but her joining us was spur-of-the-moment," said one staffer. Laura Michaelis and Dallas M. Jackson
