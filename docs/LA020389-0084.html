February 3, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

The Los Angeles County Museum of Art Assn. has received a $30,000 grant from the National Endowment for the Arts to support its "Evenings for Educators" program that provides visual arts resources and training for elementary and secondary teachers. In all, the National Endowment awarded $440,590 to 18 projects that promote arts education in schools across the country. ALEENE MacMINN
