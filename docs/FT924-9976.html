921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Stag leaps 21p as bid talks confirmed Shares in Stag Furniture, the Nottingham-based cabinet furniture manufacturer, yesterday jumped 21p to 96p following rumours, confirmed by the company, that it was a bid target. The company said it was in discussions with another party that could lead to an offer. In September, Stag announced a 66 per cent fall in first-half pre-tax profits to Pounds 317,000. The company said margins were under pressure and the interim dividend was cut from 2.5p to 1.5p.
