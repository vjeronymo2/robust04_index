941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Glasgow Income Glasgow Income Trust had a net asset value per share of 45.29p at September 30 against 47.68p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year improved from Pounds 831,000 to Pounds 894,000 for earnings per share of 2.93p (2.81p). An unchanged final dividend of 0.95p holds the total at 2.75p.
