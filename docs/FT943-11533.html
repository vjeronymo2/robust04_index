940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical The split capital Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 48.3p per capital share as at June 30, a modest increase on the value of 47.8p a year earlier. Net revenue for the three months to end-June amounted to Pounds 125,000 (Pounds 249,000) for earnings of 1.56p (3.1p) per income share. The first interim dividend is held at 2.25p.
