BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Efim Shvartsman] [Text] Kiev 5 April, TASS -- The press service of the Ukrainian Defence Ministry denied reports that Minister Vitalyy Radetskyy was to arrive in the Crimea today. "Radetskyy is in Kiev now and planned no trips for today including to the Crimea," the press service told ITAR-TASS. However the source has confirmed that a group of the Ukrainian top military consisiting of Intelligence Chief Vegunnikov, Airforce Commander Antonets, Chief of Staff of the National Guard Mokhov and Counterintelligence Chief Kravchenko has arrived in the Crimea today "separately and by different means of transport." According to reliable sources in the Crimea, the aim of their visit to the Crimea was not immediately known either to the Crimean administration or the command of the Black Sea Fleet. The arrival of high-ranking officers without preliminary notification, has evoked protest on the part of the Crimean leadership.
