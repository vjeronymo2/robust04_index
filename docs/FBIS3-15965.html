Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin met with visiting Italian Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta on Thursday to discuss key issues concerning the situation in Europe and the world, a statement issued by the presidential press service said. Yeltsin stressed the need for joint collective activity of all countries in order to strengthen stability and security, and prevent and settle conflicts. The recent progress in resolving the situation around Sarajevo highlighted the importance of a coordinated policy of the international community, he said. Speaking about the issues to be discussed at the upcoming G-7 summit meeting to be held in Naples in July, the president expressed readiness to take steps aimed at developing closer cooperation and stick to the concept of G-8 in political interaction.
