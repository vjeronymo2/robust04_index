941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Auditors for Orange County The governor of California yesterday ordered a team of state auditors to be sent to bankrupt Orange County to assess the damage done to its devastated investment portfolio. The auditors should review all activities in the county's funds since June 30 and all audit reports of the last three years, Governor Pete Wilson said. Orange County, which estimates its investment losses at Dollars 1.5bn, last Tuesday became the largest local government in US history to declare bankruptcy.
