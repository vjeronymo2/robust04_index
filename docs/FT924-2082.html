921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Fund of assistance for enterprise - In a Nutshell A Pounds 300,000 low-interest rate fund to back small businesses in London has been launched by Greater London Enterprise, Midland Bank and the nine London Training and Enterprise Councils. The London Business Growth Fund will provide unsecured loans of up to Pounds 10,000 over three years at a rate of 2 percentage points below base rate to established small firms. The fund will also provide loans of up to Pounds 5,000 to start-up businesses run by people with disabilities, members of ethnic minorities, women and the long-term unemployed. This fund brings to 17 the number of local small business funds backed by Midland and the amount of finance available to Pounds 3.57m, much of it at rates below base rate. The selection and monitoring of businesses backed is carried out jointly by the bank and local business organisations such as Tecs or enterprise agencies.
