Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Statement for Belgrade Radio by Captain Nikola Miljanovic, head of the Press Center of the Serbian Army of Krajina; place and date not given -- recorded] [Text] The command of the 21st Corps of the Serbian Army of Krajina has lodged a sharp protest with the commander of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] for Sector North because of the observed and established intelligence activities of NATO aircraft over Krajina, especially over Lika. We have not received a reply from UNPROFOR to this day.
