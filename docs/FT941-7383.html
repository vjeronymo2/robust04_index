940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Indonesian activist jailed A human rights activist yesterday received a four-year sentence for putting up stickers that were ruled to be insulting to Indonesian President Suharto, AP-DJ reports from Jakarta. Nuku Sulaeman, 29, was charged with putting up stickers that called Suharto 'the mastermind of all disasters' - a reference to reported human rights violations during Mr Suharto's 25-year presidency.
