November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: COSTNER'S LAST STAND?

Meanwhile, Columbia's "Revenge" -- which wrapped last Dec. 14, also with Costner starring -- isn't due out until February. The violence-laden love triangle, directed by Tony Scott and set in Mexico, has been in post-production for months . . . and months.

"There are absolutely no problems," said a rep for producer Ray Stark. "It's just going through the traditional editing process." Pat H. Broeske
