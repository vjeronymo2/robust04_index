Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister Salamah Hammad has received a flood of cables and telephone calls from various popular sectors and citizens, commending the rapid and effective measures the Ministry of Interior and security agencies have taken to apprehend the culprits who carried out explosions in two cinemas, one in Amman and the other in al-Zarqa'. The citizens denounced these criminal acts and demanded that deterrent punishment be meted out to the culprits. For his part, the interior minister asserted that the Ministry of Interior and the security organizations, which are careful to safeguard this country's security, will be firm and punish whoever entertains the idea of undermining and weakening the security of this steadfast Arab country. He appealed to citizens to demonstrate further watchfulness and vigilance, and to cooperate with the security organizations whenever they find any suspicious object that could destabilize security in this steadfast and unyielding country.
