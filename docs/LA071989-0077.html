July 19, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: VISTA

A Vista man who was stabbed July 10 in Vista died Tuesday, authorities said.

Corey Gerald Hale, 26, of the 2800 block of East Vista Way was pronounced dead at 2:50 p.m. at Scripps Memorial Hospital in La Jolla, Deputy Coroner George Dickason said.

Sheriff's Department homicide investigators were continuing their investigation.
