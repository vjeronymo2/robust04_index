Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother leader, Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], received for the second time last night, the Norwegian envoy, Mr. Kari Nordheim Larsen, undersecretary of the Norwegian Foreign Affairs Ministry, within the context of the intensified contacts currently under way with all sides for ensuring the faithful, precise, and immediate application of the Declaration of Principles, and finding the best means for protecting the Palestinian people from the massacres committed by the Israeli settlers in the occupied territories.
