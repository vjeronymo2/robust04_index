BFN [Text] "In case violence escalates on the territory of Bosnia-Herzegovina either because of massive NATO air strikes or combat activities of the sides directly involved in the conflict and (in case of) a threat to the personnel of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force], it cannot be ruled out that the question of the necessity of further stay of peace-keeping forces in Bosnia will arise", Karasin said adding that Yasushi Akashi, the envoy of the UN secretary general, had recently mentioned the possibility. He stressed that a number of states whose peace-keepers are in the zone of conflict had several times noted that in case of a real threat to the personnel of their peace-keeping forces the question may arise about withdrawing them. Russia will follow suit in this case, according to Karasin.
