Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "REUTER Partner in CTV"] [Text] Bratislava--The Slovak CTV television company, which is seeking a license to broadcast in the Slovak Republic, has acquired another foreign partner. It is REUTER, the world-famous news agency. Besides acquiring a minority stake of a maximum of 15 percent, REUTER is also interested in participating in news production. The first foreign partner in CTV was the German Commerzbank, which remains its exclusive financial representative. The Slovak company retains a majority stake of 51 percent.
