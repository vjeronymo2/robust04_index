December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

IN NONCONFERENCE MEN'S BASKETBALL:

Saddleback 80, Southwestern 73 -- Matt Thompson had 18 points and Dee Boyer had 10 points, 14 rebounds and eight blocked shots for host Saddleback (43-38). Ritchie Bethune had 20 points and 11 rebounds for Southwestern (3-8).

In nonconference women's basketball:

Golden West 87, Grossmont 62 -- Bits Sirchia scored 22 points for host Golden West (13-1).
