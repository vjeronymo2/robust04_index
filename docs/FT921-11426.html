920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / World Trade News: Kuwait given credits boost by France FRANCE is to grant Kuwait FFr5bn (Pounds 510m) worth of export credits to boost bilateral trade, Mr Pierre Beregovoy, the French finance minister, said yesterday, Our Foreign Staff writes. Mr Beregovoy, speaking at the end of a visit to the emirate, said France had agreed to allow the Kuwaitis to set up an investment company in France to help channel more money into the French economy. Kuwaitis would be exempt from a French wealth tax, in a similar concession already granted to Saudi nationals.
