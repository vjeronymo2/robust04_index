940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / International Company News: Fisher and Paykel slips to NZDollars 27m Fisher and Paykel, the Australasian white-goods manufacturer, yesterday reported a 2.9 per cent decline in operating profit to to NZDollars 26.9m (USDollars 15.8m), but the chairman, Mr Colin Maiden, said the outlook was strong, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. Mr Maiden said sales of white goods were buoyant - up 31 per cent to NZDollars 172m in Australia and 8 per cent to NZDollars 173.6m in New Zealand - but earnings from other products were down. The company declared an unchanged final dividend of 8 cents, making a total of 16 cents a share.
