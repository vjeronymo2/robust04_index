BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 13 -- A delegation of the French high- ranking Military Research Institute led by its director, Gen. Francois Bersson, left Ho Chi Minh City today ending its 8-day working visit to Vietnam. While in Ho Chi Minh City, the French guests visited the 7th Military Zone, and other establishments. They also paid a visit to Ba Ria-Vung Tau Province.
