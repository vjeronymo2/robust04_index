941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust Emerging Abtrust Emerging Economies Investment Trust had net revenue of Pounds 393,472 for the period to September 30. Undiluted net asset value per share amounted to 101.84p, compared with 95.92p at launch and 98.06p at the interim stage. The trust, which was incorporated on September 24 last year, had earnings per share of 0.79p. The board has announced an ordinary dividend of 0.2p and a special dividend of 0.4p.
