Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [YONHAP from Beijing] [Text] A Western source in Beijing said on 19 January that Kim Chong-u, vice chairman of North Korea's External Economic Committee, who had been scheduled to visit China sometime this week, postponed the visit without a prior notice and that this has perplexed the Chinese side. This source also said: The Chinese side faxed a message to North Korea's External Economic Committee and asked why he postponed the visit and when he would visit. However, the North Korean has not made a reply. Kim was scheduled to visit China and discuss the construction of a road between the Najin-Sonbong area and Chongjin and other ways to promote cooperation for the Tumen River project.
