Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Peru monitored through 20 January. The Spanish Government approved a $40 million loan for Peru that will be used to buy Spanish goods and services to implement a fiber optic transmission project with the participation of the Spanish telecommunications company Alcatel. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 31 Dec 93 p 1) The National Institute of Statistics and Information Science on 31 December 1993 reported that 1993's inflation rate was 39.5 percent, the lowest since 1977. The sectors registering the highest increase were: transportation and communications, 54.2 percent; teaching and cultural services, 46.8 percent; health and medical services, 43.7 percent; rental, oil, and electricity, 43 percent. The GDP increased 6.8 percent compared with 1992. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 1 Jan 94 p 1)
