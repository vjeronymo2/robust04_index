BFN [Text] Pakistan and Turkmenistan have agreed to cooperate in defense training. Pakistan Air Force delegation led by Air Vice Marshal Farooq Usman Haider which visited Askhabad recently held extensive talks with defense officials of Turkmenistan. Pakistan has offered initial training for Turkmenistan pilots in Pakistan and also to help set up a pilot training academy in Turkmenistan. The delegation was shown an impressive air display of MiG-29's at (Merz) Air Base. The delegation also visited the historic mausoleum of (Sultan Sanghar) at (Merve).
