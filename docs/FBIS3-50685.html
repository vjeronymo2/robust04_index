Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Entrepreneur Pedro Collor will not join the PSB [Brazilian Socialist Party]. The party national executive board decided to cancel his membership after his name was rejected by eight regional party boards. Former President Fernando Collor's brother has not yet decided on his possibly joining the PMN [National Mobilization Party]. Pedro Collor, who pressed the charges that led to the creation of the CPI [Congressional Investigating Committee] that investigated entrepreneur Paulo Cesar Farias, said he will remain a member of the Alagoas Popular Leftist Front. The PSB supported this decision.
