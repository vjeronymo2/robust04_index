930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Group of MPs that went to northern Cyprus MR ANDREW FAULDS, the Labour MP for Warley East, who led the group of MPs that went to northern Cyprus this Easter, said the MPs who accompanied him were: Conservatives: Ann Winterton (Congleton), Nicholas Winterton (Macclesfield), Richard Spring (Bury St Edmunds), Sir Ivan Lawrence (Burton), David Wilshire (Spelthorne), John Biffen (Shropshire North), Stephen Day (Cheadle). Labour: Roy Hughes (Newport East), Bob Litherland, (Manchester Central), Jamie Cann (Ipswich). Others: Margaret Ewing (SNP, Moray) Ken Maginnis (UUP, Fermanagh and South Tyrone).
