920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / UK Company News: East German Investment net asset value ahead THE EAST German Investment Trust reported a net asset value of DM2.81 (96p) as at June 30 1992. The outcome compared with asset values of DM2.78 12 months earlier and DM2.71 at the December 31 year-end. The trust, which concentrates its portfolio in unquoted companies located or operating in the former east Germany, came to the market in February last year following a placing by County NatWest and Berliner Bank. Net revenue for the six months to end-June dropped from DM1.67m to DM904,000. Earnings per share emerged at DM0.019, down from DM0.042 last time. During the period under review, the trust invested DM54m in a further eight investments across a broad range of activities including white goods, television operations, property development, laboratories and building products. This lifted its total investment to some DM86m across 13 unquoted companies -representing 55 per cent of total assets.
