BFN [Report by Aleksandr Bugay from the "Fax News" roundup in the "Russkoye Oruzhiye" section: "`Mailbox Numbers' Need Help"] [Text] Kazan -- Defense enterprises in Tataria [Tatarstan] have found themselves in the gravest of socioeconomic positions. According to M. Shakhmeyev, chairman of the Radio-Electronic Workers Trade Union Republic Committee, one-half of the 130,000 workers at former "mailbox number" enterprises are currently working on a reduced schedule. At gatherings of workers' collectives, which have taken place at virtually all defense enterprises, it has been proposed that the government and president of Tataria resolve the issue of financing conversion programs in full by 1 July, reserve in enterprise accounts for emergencies and the payment of wages no less than 30 percent of the amount of the payment, maintain contributions to the social insurance fund at a level of 5.4 percent, and settle a number of other questions.
