December 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

COLLEGE APPLICATION FORMS AVAILABLE

Application forms for first-time students planning to enroll at Orange Coast College are available in the college's admissions and records office.

Students who file their applications early will have a better chance of getting the classes they want, said Susan Brown, the college's associate dean of admissions and records.

Spring semester classes begin Jan. 22. Registration by appointment is under way.

The admissions and records office is open Monday through Thursday from 8 a.m. to 7 p.m. and Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Open registration, without appointment, begins Jan. 17.

For spring semester information, call (714) 432-5772.
