BFN [Excerpt] Zagreb, April 24 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serb forces appear to have complied with a NATO ultimatum to withdraw three kilometers from the center of Gorazde or face air strikes, the head of UN forces in the former Yugoslavia said Sunday [24 April]. In an interview with AFP here, French General Bertrand de Lapresle said the Serbs were observing a ceasefire and "have withdrawn beyond the three-kilometer line, as far as we can tell."
