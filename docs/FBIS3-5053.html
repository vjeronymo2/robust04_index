Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong- nam sent a message of greetings to Jean-Marie Ngendahayo upon his appointment as minister of State for External Relations and Cooperation of Burundi. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop on good terms, the message wished the state minister success in his responsible work.
