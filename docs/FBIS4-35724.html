BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA) -- In a statement today, 'Umar al-Muntasir, Libyan secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation -- foreign minister -- said that "Libya is against secession in Yemen no matter what the price." He said that Libya supports "imposing unity by the force of arms." Responding to a question on his opinion should the price for forced unity be 50,000 dead, al-Muntasir said: "We support it even if the price were to be 100,000 dead; we are unity crazy." It is worth noting that al-Muntasir is participating in the Nonaligned Movement Conference currently being held in Cairo.
