930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / Teesside plans energy park PROPOSALS for a Pounds 100m energy park, offering research and development facilities for energy-related companies, were unveiled yesterday by Teesside Development Corporation. The corporation, whose chief executive Mr Duncan Hall dismissed science parks as 'somewhat passe', said the energy park is a new concept which will establish Teesside as an international R&D centre for the energy industry. Under the plans, a communications network will link the park at Stockton-on-Tees to more than 30 universities and educational institutes in the US, Japan and Europe.
