Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 19 (KPL) -- Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic] President Nouhak Phoumsavan, yesterday received the letter of credentials from the new ambassador of the People's Republic of China, Mr. Li Jiazhong. The Lao president, on the occasion, had an exchange of views with the new extraordinary and plenipotentiary ambassador of the PRC on matters of bilateral cooperation. The Lao president satisfactorily observed that the fine relations between Laos and China had been positively expanded as indicated, among others, in the increasing number of exchanges of delegations of various levels and sectors between the two countries. Mr. Nouhak Phoumsavan hailed the conclusion of the agreements on socioeconomic cooperation and mutual assistance between the two sides. On the same occasion, the Lao president conveyed through the new ambassador his warm regards and best wishes to the leadership of the Chinese party and government as well as to the friendly people of China. He wished that the ties of friendship and cooperation between the Lao People's Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China be for evermore expanded for the interest of both nations. Mr. Li Jiazhong confirmed that he would spare no effort to contribute to the consolidation of the Sino-Lao relations of friendship and would seek more Chinese assistance for socioeconomic development of Laos.
