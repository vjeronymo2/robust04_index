940509 FT 09 MAY 94 / Inquiry over bleach in drink Police and environmental health officials in Derby are investigating how bleach found its way into a juice carton after a four-year-old girl was treated in hospital for burns to her lips and throat. Refreshment Spectrum of County Durham, which manufacturers the Jurassic juice drink on behalf of the Coca-Cola Company, said initial examinations suggested the contamination was an 'isolated incident'.
