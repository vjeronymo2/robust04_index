BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, receieved this morning the ambassador of the Russian Federation. During the meeting, the brother president Abu-'Ammar charged his excellency the Russian ambassador with conveying a message to the Russian leadership in connection with the recent developments of the situation and the exerted efforts to implement Security Council Resolution 904, particularly after the Israeli replies, which were brought by the Israeli envoy in response to our stances following the Hebron and Gaza massacres.
