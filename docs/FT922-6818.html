920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Wardell Roberts Difficult economic conditions throughout the year in the UK and later in the Irish Republic left pre-tax profits at Wardell Roberts, Dublin-based food processor, 15 per cent lower at IPounds 2.42m (Pounds 2.2m) in the 12 months to March 31 against IPounds 2.86m. Turnover fell to IPounds 45.1m (IPounds 48.1m) including IPounds 3.89m (IPounds 10.5m) from discontinued activities. Earnings per share came out at 8.4p (10.6p) and a maintained final dividend of 1.936p is proposed for a total payment of 3.206p (3.146p). There was an extraordinary charge of IPounds 2.59m relating to the withdrawal from the poultry business in the UK.
