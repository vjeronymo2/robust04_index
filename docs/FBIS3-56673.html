Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- The Russian State Duma, the lower house of the parliament, turned down by a narrow margin on Monday [17 January] the proposal to include into the agenda the issue of an amnesty for organisers of the 1991 Soviet abortive coup and of the armed opposition revolt in Moscow in October 1993. The proposal, made by lawmaker Vasiliy Lipitskiy, was supported by 205 votes and lacked just 18 votes for approval.
