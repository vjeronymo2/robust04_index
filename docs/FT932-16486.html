930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Gunmen murder 10 ANC supporters GUNMEN killed 10 sleeping African National Congress supporters in a house south of Durban yesterday, Reuter reports from Durban. Black gunmen in camouflage uniforms opened fire with AK-47 assault rifles where youths were sleeping, police and ANC officials said. Police and ANC officials said at least 23 people, all but one of them black, have been killed in South African violence since Friday.
