BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Three changes were made within the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] on 11 April. Mr. Lievano Saenz Ortiz left the Secretariat of Information and Propaganda to become the private secretary of PRI presidential candidate Ernesto Zedillo. Lievano Saenz has held several posts in the government. He was a senior officer at the Secretariat of Social Development, director of property and furnishings of the defunct Secretariat of Urban Development and Ecology, and general director of the Panamericana Sulphur Company [Azufrera Panamericana] and the Istmo Exploration Company. The PRI's new secretary of information and propaganda is Mr. Carlos Salomon Camara, formerly the Federal District's secretary general of social development, a political delegate in Coyoacan, and commercial director of Fertimex [Mexican Fertilizers]. He has been a PRI member for 19 years and is a columnist for the newspaper EXCELSIOR and the magazine SIEMPRE. Mr. Esteban Moctezuma Barragan will hold the newly created post of first adjunct secretary; he was a senior officer at the Secretariat of Programming and Budget, a senior officer at the Secretariat of Public Education and, until recently, deputy secretary of public education. These appointments were disclosed by Mr. Fernando Ortiz Arana, president of the PRI's National Executive Committee, who swore in the three new appointees. Mr. Ortiz Arana stated the following about the three appointees: [Begin Ortiz recording] They will give credit to the rules that have governed their conduct in the many positions they have held. They have personal and political honesty, integrity, intelligence, and a determination to serve foremost the causes of the Mexican people. [end recording] The brief ceremony was also attended by Mr. Ignacio Pichardo Pagaza, the recently appointed PRI secretary general.
