BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 1 (SNARK)--The Armenia Parliament Speaker Babken Ararktsyan told during a meeting with a delegation of inter-parliament CIS assembly in Yerevan that Armenia remains a supporter of peaceful settlement of Karabakh problem. He is satisfied with the same opinion of the delegation's members. Mr. Ararktsyan expressed hope that a visit of the inter-parliamentary assembly to the conflict zone will be an important step towards organizing of a meeting in Bishkek between Armenia, Azerbaijan and Karabakh. Mr. Ararktsyan said "last time the Russian initiatives with constructive ways to the Karabakh problem solution find understanding." [sentence as received]
