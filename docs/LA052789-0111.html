May 27, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: TUSTIN;

POLICE HUNT FOR MAN WHO ROBBED BANK

Police are searching for a man who robbed Sunwest Bank of an undisclosed amount of money on Friday.

A man entered the bank at 535 E. 1st St. about noon and handed a teller a note demanding money, Tustin Police Sgt. Brent Cicarelli said. The robber fled on foot, and no one was injured.

Police describe the man as a Latino about 35 years old, 5 feet, 10 inches and of slim build.
