BFN [Excerpt] Brcko, 23 May (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces targeted Brcko with tank shells four times in less than two hours this evening, TANJUG's correspondent has reported. Six people were wounded in the attacks. Twenty year-old Sinisa Raslic suffered the most serious injuries and was transferred to the Military Medical Academy in Belgrade. All Muslim targets were civilian, and great damage was done to residential buildings in Brcko. [passage omitted]
