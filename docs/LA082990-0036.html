August 29, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Dangerfield for Cabbies: Rodney Dangerfield has a new charity -- Las Vegas taxi drivers.

His Rodney's Cab Driver Relief Fund will raise money for Vegas cabbies who have been the victims of violent crime, and the comedian will headline a Sept. 2 concert at Bally's to raise money for the fund. "I have been fighting for respect for many years," Dangerfield said. "Now I want to do something for the 1,800 cab drivers in Las Vegas who have also been fighting for respect." BETH KLEID
