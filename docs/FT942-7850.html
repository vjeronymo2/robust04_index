940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / BBC white paper promised soon Mr Peter Brooke, the national heritage secretary, told the Commons yesterday that a white paper on the future of the BBC would be published 'fairly shortly'. He referred MPs to the recent announcement of the BBC's partnership with Pearson, the media and entertainment group which owns the Financial Times, when questioned about the type of services in which it might engage in in the future. The BBC announced a 'global strategic alliance' with Pearson this month to develop a range of satellite television channels around the world.
