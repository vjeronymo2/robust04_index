920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Major plans Indian visit Mr John Major, the British prime minister, is to visit India in the autumn, following a meeting with Mr P V Narasimha Rao, his Indian counterpart, at the United Nations summit in New York, writes Alison Smith. Mr Major used his meeting with Mr Li Peng, the Chinese prime minister, to renew his pressure on the Chinese government over human rights. He handed over a list of political prisoners, updated from his visit to Beijing last year.
