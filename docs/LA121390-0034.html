December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Nose for News: In a seesaw battle for afternoon news ratings, it appears that once-dominant KNBC Channel 4 has reclaimed the ratings lead it lost to KABC Channel 7 during the November ratings sweeps. With anchorwoman Christine Lund back after a four-year hiatus, Channel 7 picked up 90,000 households to finish first at 4 in November and also edged KNBC at 5. CLAUDIA PUIG
