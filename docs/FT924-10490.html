921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / World News in Brief: Leading ballet figure dies Sir Kenneth MacMillan, principal choreographer of the Royal Ballet, collapsed and died backstage at the Royal Opera House, Covent Garden, during last night's performance of Mayerling, one of his own works. He was 62. Sir Kenneth was one of the world's leading choreographers and had been associated with the Royal Ballet for more than 40 years.
