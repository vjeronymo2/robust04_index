October 10, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Defending champion Slobodan Zivojinovic of Yugoslavia advanced to the second round of the Australian indoor tennis tournament, defeating Darren Patten at Sydney, 5-7, 6-2, 6-4.

Australian Richard Fromberg, ranked 160th, upset sixth-seeded Eric Jelen of West Germany, 4-6, 7-6 (7-2), 6-2, in another first-round match.
