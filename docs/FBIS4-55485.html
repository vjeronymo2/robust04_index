BFN [Text] The fighters of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army 2nd Corps have also marked a success on the Kalesija-Zvornik front. In a successful counterattack, the fighters of the 205th and the 206th Brigade, together with additional units, have liberated the important strategic points Mala Jelica and Velika Jelica, while a part of the enemy forces remain surrounded in [word indistinct]. The defense line in this region now stretches in the direction of (Zavrsko Brdo) [word indistinct]. The chetnik losses are considerable. Mayor Krste (Medic), commander of the 1st Semberija Infantry Brigade, and the chief of staff of the same brigade were killed in the fighting. Due to their heavy losses, the chetniks have to carry out forceful mobilization of their population. This was reported by the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army Press Center.
