Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Now we have a report from ANI [Agentstvo Novostei Informatsii]. The central Georgian newspaper `Respublika Gruziya' is publishing data from a lightning poll carried out in Tbilisi on the theme: what political and economic changes are expected after the visit by Shevarnadze to the United States? More than 41 percent of those asked expect an improvement in the economic and political situation in the republic. More than 16 percent of those polled expect positive changes only in the economic sphere, and around five percent say only in the political field. And 27 percent believe that nothing will change.
