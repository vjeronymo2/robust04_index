940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Arthur Wood rises Arthur Wood & Son, the earthenware product manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits up 15 per cent from Pounds 16,402 to Pounds 18,846 in the six months to June 30. Turnover rose 16 per cent from Pounds 1.53m to Pounds 1.78m, with a 53 per cent increase in exports. Earnings per share rose slightly to 0.62p (0.55p).
