December 17, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

JACKSON LEADS GOLDEN WEST PAST OCC WOMEN

Forward Claudette Jackson had 22 points and forward Stacee Johnson had 15 to pace unbeaten Golden West to a 67-53 victory over Orange Coast College in the championship game of the Cuesta women's basketball tournament Saturday in San Luis Obispo.

Center Donna Gondringer had 10 points and 18 rebounds for Golden West (15-0). Orange Coast dropped to 14-3.
