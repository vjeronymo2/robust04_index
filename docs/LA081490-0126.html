August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SUDS UP: Beach Beer Inc. of Newport Beach makes beer in Dubuque, Iowa, that it calls "Trestles Lager," after the South County beach that took its name from the nearby rail line.

Other surf labels include "Zuma" and "Rincon.". . . Company President Michael Imeson, who used to surf here as a teen-ager, now works out of Seattle and hasn't surfed at Trestles in six years. Still, he says he is donating 1% of Trestles' gross profits for beach protection, "to have some vehicle to make sure those beaches stay surfable for years to come." Kristina Lindgren and John Needham
