BFN [Excerpt] President Yoweri Museveni has received the Libyan minister of state for foreign affairs, Mr. (Abduh Musa), who paid a courtesy call on him at his office at the parliamentary building in Kampala today. The president and Mr. (Musa) discussed matters relating to the ongoing Pan-African Congress conference in Kampala, where he represented and gave a speech on behalf of the Libyan leader, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi.
