940428 FT 28 APR 94 / Hutchison issues Orange tariffs Hutchison Microtel yesterday announced five price plans for its Orange mobile telephone network, which is launched today. There are to be no monthly charges. Its plan for occasional users costs Pounds 15 a month including 15 minutes of airtime. Orange to Orange calls after that cost 12.5p. Heavy users can have 540 minutes of airtime a month for Pounds 100, after which calls cost 7p a minute. Hutchison's Orange, Page 31
