July 25, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

"Les Miserables," which closed Sunday at the Shubert Theatre, broke the all-time box office record for a week's take at the theater in the final week of its run here. The $614,708 gross beats the show's own record-breaking figure of $610,287 reported last January. "Les Miz" had been at the Shubert since June 1, 1988. ALEENE MacMINN
