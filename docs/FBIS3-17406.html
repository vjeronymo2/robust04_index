Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan ("RESPUBLICA ARMENIA" daily), 03/17/94 -- The Chairman of the RA [Republic of Armenia] Central Bank's press department Mesrop Harutyunyan informed the "RA" correspondent, the circulation of the old "soviet" rubles (issued in 1961-1992) is stopped in the republic today. The citizens who still possess the banknotes of 500 rubles or below have two days (on March 17 and 18) to exchange them with drams and lumas at the branches of Ardshinbank, Armeconombank or Armagrobank.
