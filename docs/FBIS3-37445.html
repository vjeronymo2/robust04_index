Language: English Article Type:BFN [By non-staff ITAR-TASS correspondent Zurab Argun] [Text] Sukhumi February 16 TASS -- A military grouping of Georgian troops is being intensively concentrated in the Zugdidi district of Georgia, the press service of the Abkhazian Defence Ministry reported today. According to information from the Defence Ministry of Abkhazia, "over 2,000 soldiers and officers, more than 40 armored cars, a large quantity of artillery guns and mortars have been concentrated there. Living force and materiel are being brought to the state border." The augmentation of living force and materiel is also going on in the Kodor canyon. Similar military preparations by the Georgian side is a clear proof that Georgia did not give up the idea of resolving the problem of relations with the Republic of Abkhazia in a military way, a statement of the Abkhazian Defence Ministry said.
