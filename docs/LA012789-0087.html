January 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Kaufman & Broad Home Corp.'s annual profit rose 35% to $48 million. Revenue rose to $903.4 million, up 34%. The Los Angeles-based firm, which is the largest single-family home builder in California and third-largest in France, attributed its sixth consecutive earnings record to continued strength in its regional home building markets in California and France as well as the successful performance in commercial development in France.
