For the above species, there are either no records or very few records of any specimens in trade. The Service was therefore not able to make the determination required by the statute that the above species are regularly bred in captivity and that the importation of specimens of the species is not detrimental to the species survival in the wild. The Service recognizes that with increased captive breeding efforts for these species, they may be able to meet criteria for approval in §15.31 in the future.
