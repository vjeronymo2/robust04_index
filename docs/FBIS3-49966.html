Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The 30-minute meeting between President Zhelyu Zhelev and French President Francois Mitterrand has ended. At the end of the meeting the French president told reporters that the purpose of his visit was to check at what stage Bulgaria's preparations are for hosting the Council of Europe session in May. Another goal of his meeting is to strengthen Bulgarian-French economic and cultural relations, stressing economic contacts in particular. The French president said that stimulating foreign investments is of special importance for Bulgaria. They must be directed with priority toward small and medium-size enterprises. President Mitterrand promised to support Bulgaria's efforts to achieve an alleviation of the burden of foreign debt.
