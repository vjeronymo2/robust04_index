BFN [Text] Beijing, 24 May (XINHUA) -- According to the China Satellite Launch Tracking and Control Department: The accident of the meteorological satellite, the "Fengyun II," will not affect China's plan of launching satellites for foreign countries. Chinese authorities, after consulting with the Hughes Aircraft Corporation of the United States, have decided to proceed with the original plans of launching the three foreign satellites -- the "Asiasat I," the "Asiasat II," and the "Optus B3" -- during the second half of the year.
