July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: GARDEN GROVE;

COCAINE SEIZED IN BUST AT ADULT VIDEO STORE

Police seized a kilogram of cocaine, and arrested the manager and an employee of the Bijou Adult Video store and three other men on suspicion of selling cocaine, a Garden Grove police spokesman said Tuesday.

All five were arrested as a result of a two-month police investigation that allegedly involved several cocaine sales made by the store's manager, Raymundo Gonzalez, 24, of Garden Grove, and employee David O' Harrold, 21, of Garden Grove, Sgt. Douglas Morrill said.

The other men include John Manuel Solis, 43, of Santa Ana; Sabino Montes, 24, of Los Angeles; and Cesar Garcia of Los Angeles.

On Monday, Gonzalez was arrested at his residence in the 1880 block of Shetland Road after he allegedly sold an undercover narcotics officer a kilo of cocaine, Morrill said. The cocaine has a street value of $100,000.
