920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: GEC venture strategy put to test GEC built a ring of joint ventures around itself in 1989 when it was threatened by takeover. Now generating a third of group turnover, they make it unlikely the group will ever be broken up. But is there a risk of GEC dissolving gently as it loses control to partners with stronger management? Part three, Page 17
