OSM announced receipt of the proposed amendment in the January 14, 1993, Federal Register (58 FR 4376) and, in the same notice, opened the public comment period and provided opportunity for a public hearing on the adequacy of the proposed amendment. The public comment period ended on February 16, 1993. The public hearing scheduled for February 8, 1993, was not held because no one requested an opportunity to testify. During its review of the amendment, OSM identified concerns related to Iowa Administrative Code (IAC) 27&hyph;40.1(3), General; 27&hyph;40.3(207), General; IAC 27&hyph;40.4(10), Full water year; IAC 27&hyph;40.21(207), Areas designated by an Act of Congress; IAC 27&hyph;40.31(207), Requirements for permits and permit processing; IAC 27&hyph;40.32(207), Revision, renewal, and transfer, assignment, or sale of permit rights; IAC 27&hyph;40.34, Permit application_minimum requirements for legal, financial, compliance, and related information; IAC 27&hyph;40.39(1), Requirements for permits for special categories of mining; IAC 27&hyph;40.61(1), Permanent program performance standards_general provisions; IAC 27&hyph;40.63, Permanent program performance standards_surface mining activities; IAC 27&hyph;40.67, Permanent program performance standards_coal preparation plants not located within the permit area of a mine; IAC 27&hyph;40.73(2)g, Enforcement; IAC 27&hyph;40.74, Civil penalties; and IAC 27&hyph;40.75, Individual civil penalties. OSM notified Iowa of the concerns by letter dated May 10, 1993 (Administrative Record No. IA&hyph;381). Iowa responded in a letter dated July 8, 1993 (Administrative Record No. IA&hyph;383), by submitting revised language for the proposed amendment to address the concerns raised by OSM. On July 21, 1993, OSM published a notice in the Federal Register (58 FR 38991) announcing receipt of revised language for the proposed amendment and inviting public comment on its adequacy. The public comment period ended August 5, 1993. By letters dated August 20, 1993 (Administrative Record No. IA&hyph;388), and August 30, 1993 (Administrative Record No. IA&hyph;389), Iowa provided OSM with additional information to clarify and correct three editorial errors. These clarifications are discussed in the appropriate findings to follow. III. Director's Findings 1. Provisions Not Discussed Iowa proposes revisions to its rules that involve minor editorial and word changes, and recodification. Iowa also proposes to revise its current incorporation by reference of OSM's regulations from those in effect as of July 1, 1987, to those in effect as of July 1, 1992.
