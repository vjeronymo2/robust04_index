Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Former National Bank Director Mikhail Vranopoulos died at 2125 [1925 GMT] at Evangelismos Hospital. Doctors announced that following surgery, there were complications, and violent hemorrhaging caused his death. [passage omitted] In a declaration to the private television station SKY, the terrorist group 17 November claimed responsibility for the attack on the former National Bank director. The declaration said that Vranopoulos and the former New Democracy government were responsible for the scandalous sale of the public cement company Ayet Iraklis. The declaration added that many people believed 17 November had dissolved because it had not attacked for some 13 months. Police are investigating the declaration. According to other information, 17 November said the police have wrongly accused it of attacks it did not perform.
