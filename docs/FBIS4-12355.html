BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today met with Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana, who is currently visiting Egypt. After the meeting, Solana said that he and 'Abd-al-Majid discussed the latest developments in the peace process in the Middle East. He expressed his country's interest in the achievement of stability in the region. He noted that he reviewed the results of the seminar on cooperation in the Mediterranean which concluded recently in Cairo. They also discussed ways of strengthening ties and broadening cooperation between the Arab League and the European Union. Solana described his talks with 'Abd-al-Majid as friendly and candid. He said that he extended an invitation to Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid to visit Spain for further discussions and exchange of views on issues of common concern to both parties.
