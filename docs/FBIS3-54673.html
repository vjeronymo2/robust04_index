Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense Ministry press service reported that tension persists on the Armenian-Azerbaijani border. The defensive positions around Dirants in Idzhevanskiy Rayon were shelled from Azerbaijani territory at 1320 on 30 December. The enemy fired toward Sevkar in the same rayon between 1730-1750. The artillery shelling of Dirants resumed in the early hours of 31 December. The enemy also opened fired on the border post near Vahan in Krasnoselskiy Rayon during the daytime on 30 December. The defensive positions around Yeraskhavan in Araratskiy Rayon were fired upon from Nakhichevan in the evening of 30 December. There were no casualties. The Armenian side refrained from retaliating.
