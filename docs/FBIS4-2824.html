BFN [Text] Beijing, April 13 (XINHUA) -- Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wu Jieping extended profound condolences to the embassies of Burundi and Rwanda over the death of their heads of state. Wu offered solicitude this afternoon for the death of President Juvenal Habyarimana and Prime Minister Uwilingiyimana Agathe of Rwanda and President Cyprian Ntayaminas of Burundi. Chinese President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng sent wreaths to the embassies. Wu conveyed deep condolences to the people of Burundi and Rwanda and the families of the dead, and hoped they would be united and overcome the difficulties now facing them. The two presidents died in a plane crash on April 6, and the Burundi prime minister was killed the next day in the Presidential Office.
