BFN [Text] The central electoral commission of Ukraine registered Leonid Kravchuk and Ivan Plyushch on 17 May as contenders for the president of Ukraine. Kravchuk received 323,600 signatures in support, while Ivan Plyushch got 265,041. The central electoral commission has examined applications from registered initiative groups of electors, signature letters, and protocols of signature-raising in support of potential candidates for the president of Ukraine, as well as contenders' statements of consent to stand in the election, their programs, and last year's tax declarations. It was established that the nomination of these potential presidential candidates and signature-raising in their support was conducted in adherence to the requirements of the electoral law.
