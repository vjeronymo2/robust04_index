July 8, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: IRVINE;

PROFESSOR TO SPEAK ON DEMOCRACY IN CHINA

Zheng-yuan Fu, a visiting professor in UC Irvine's department of politics and society, will talk on "China's Bid for Democracy -- The Plan That Failed" at a meeting of the Democratic 69th Assembly District on July 19.

Fu, who also is a professor at the Chinese Academy, was imprisoned for many years during the Cultural Revolution.

The meeting will be at 7 p.m. at the University Community Assn. Clubhouse, 4530 Sandburg Way, Irvine.

The meeting is free and open to the public. More information may be obtained by calling (714) 786-8481.
