BFN [Text] Sofia, June 22 (BTA) -- The leader of the Bulgarian Socialist Party (BSP) Zhan Videnov today met with Yugoslav Ambassador Rados Smilkovic, the BSP Press Office reported. At the meeting, which was held at the ambassador's request, the two shared views on bilateral cooperation on the state and political-party level. Smilkovic commended the BSP role in the Bulgarian-Yugoslav dialogue, said the Press Office. Smilkovic showed interest in the assessments and decisions of the 41st Congress of the BSP, the party's new leadership structures and its home and foreign policies.
