940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / N Sea oil round attracts interest Growing oil company interest in the relatively unexplored area west of Shetland was confirmed yesterday when the government announced that blocks in the region had attracted the largest number of nominations in the latest offshore licensing round. Mr Charles Wardle, a junior industry minister, said other relatively unexplored areas in the Irish Sea and Cardigan Bay also '. . . attracted high numbers of nominations.' Many of the west coast blocks are in environmentally sensitive areas. Mr Wardle said that a total of 329 blocks had been nominated by 29 companies. The government plans to offer 150 blocks after studying the nominations.
