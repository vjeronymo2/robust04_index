920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: London Forfaiting well ahead PRE-TAX PROFITS of the London Forfaiting Company rose from Pounds 6.61m to Pounds 8.42m for the six months ended June 30. Trading income improved by Pounds 2.98m to Pounds 10.9m. Bank interest receivable and income from certificates of deposit totalled Pounds 1.92m (Pounds 4.38m) while interest payable was cut to Pounds 312,000 (Pounds 2.29m). That left net income before expenses up 25 per cent at Pounds 12.5m, against Pounds 10m. Earnings per share worked through at 6.41p (5.02p) and the interim dividend is stepped up to 2.9p (2.625p). The directors said that during the period borrowings were further reduced and the company continued to be a net depositor with bank interest received exceeding interest payable by Pounds 1.6m. During the six months the USM-quoted company, which provides finance in connection with international trade, concentrated on strengthening its overseas offices. Directors believe trading will continue 'satisfactorily' in the second half.
