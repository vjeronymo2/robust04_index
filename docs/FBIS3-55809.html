Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Juri Luik [newly appointed foreign minister], who has up until now been the leader of the Estonian-Russian talks delegation submitted today to the government the nomination of Vaino Reinart as the new Estonian-Russian talks delegation leader. Vaino Reinart has so far been working at the Foreign Ministry, on national security. The government has decided to endorse this proposal. A few days ago the president awarded to Vaino Reinart the diplomatic rank of ambassador, which brings him to the same rank as Vasily Svirin, the Russian delegation leader. Vaino Reinart has been attending the talks all along.
