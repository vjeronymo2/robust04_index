BFN [CTK report: "Drugs at the Borders and at Ruzyne Airport As Well"] [Text] Prague -- Folmava customs seized over 30 kg of heroin worth 55 million korunas [Kc] from a Turkish truck yesterday. The hiding place was professionally manufactured in the brake cylinders and perfectly disguised. The screws were covered with putty, and the hiding place was sprayed with paint. On Tuesday [12 April] at Ruzyne Airport, customs officers discovered more than two and half kg of cocaine, worth almost Kc5.5 million, in the false bottom of a Dutch citizen's suitcase.
