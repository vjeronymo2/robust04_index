December 30, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

WIZ OF A FILM

Thanks, NBC, for "The Dreamer of Oz: The L. Frank Baum Story" (Dec. 10).

We felt this rich, moving, quality production was TV at its best-the caliber of a Hallmark Hall of Fame program. (John) Ritter's performance was excellent. My wife still has a lump in her throat.

George J. Sweikis, Alta Loma
