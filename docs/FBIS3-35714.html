Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Myrzadai Zholdasbekov, Kazakh ambassador to Iran, announced today that he did not give an Iranian newspaper an interview from which it was inferred that Almaty had allegedly changed its position regarding the republic's nuclear weapons. Earlier this week, quoting the ambassador, the newspaper JOMHURI-YE ESLAMI reported that Kazakhstan was not planning to destroy the nuclear weapons located on its territory or hand them over to anyone.
