September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Wild Wear: Sammy Hagar, the wild man lead singer of Van Halen, is introducing his own line of men's clothing that reflects the band's high-energy trademark. Red Rocker Hyper-Active Wear will be introduced at next week's Action Sports Retailer Convention in San Diego. Hagar said the full line of loose-fitting clothing will include more than 50 pieces. BETH KLEID
