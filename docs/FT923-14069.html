920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / Observer: Smokelore An Indian takes a trip from his reservation to a Las Vegas casino. But his luck is out, and he loses every cent. There being no telephones on the reservation, he puts out a smoke signal: 'Send money, send money, send money . . .'. Alas, it happens to be a day when the Atomic Energy Authority is testing at nearby Yucca Flats, and his message is almost obliterated by a great mushroom cloud. Even so, he is soon rewarded with word from home. 'Sending money, sending money, sending money . . .' comes the signal back. 'And there's no need to shout.'
