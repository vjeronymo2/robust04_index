Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA/LOGOTIP-KP roundup column report under the general heading "In Brief"] [Text] Turkmenistan -- Universal issue of passports will begin in the Republic this year. The first new passports were issued to government members on the eve of the Turkmenbashi's birthday. The passport for a citizen of Turkmenistan, like the state flag, is green, and the cover portrays the country's coat of arms. It is much larger than the old Soviet passport, containing 32 pages. All entries are made in three languages -- Turkmen (in the Latin alphabet), Russian, and English. With the introduction of the new passport, the need to issue foreign travel passports lapses.
