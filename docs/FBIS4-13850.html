BFN [Text] Bucharest, April 23, IRNA -- Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati here today reiterated the full support of the Iranian people and government for the oppressed people of Bosnia against the aggressor Serbs. He made the remark in a meeting with his Bosnian counterpart Haris Siladzic on the sidelines of the Crans-Montana forum here, where the two sides also reviewed the latest developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina particularly the Gorazde crisis. Informing Velayati on the details of the strong resistance of the people in Gorazde against the wide-scale Serbian invasion, Siladzic said his country was pessimistic towards the "ambiguous function" of the international circles. He termed the aid rendered by the Islamic Republic of Iran to the Bosnia people as "Islamic and brotherly" and appreciated the Iranian people and government for their sympathy with the Bosnian Muslims.
