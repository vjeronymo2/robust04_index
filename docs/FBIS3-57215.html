Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Varlamov] [Text] Tokyo January 25 TASS -- Ukraine plans to announce at the regular CIS summit this March its decision to accede fully to the economic union of the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS), Ukrainian Vice-Premier Valentin Landyk told the Japanese newspaper "NIHON KEIZAI". His interview is published in today's issue of the daily. "The Ukrainian leader," the newspaper says, quoting his pronouncements, "has stressed that his republic should rely primarily on relations with the states of the former Soviet Union and the East European nations in order to improve the economic situation in the country. "To make the CIS economic union function effectively," Landyk believes, "it is necessary, first and foremost, to create a system for mutual settlements, based on a specialised inter-state bank."
