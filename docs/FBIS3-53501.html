Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 14 Jan (MENA)--The Sudanese Satellite Channel will start broadcasting its television programs in Arabic and English for all Sudanese citizens tomorrow. There will be three transmission periods daily. Broadcasting will last one and a half months after which a coded service will be available only for subscribers. The board of directors of the Sudanese Satellite Channel Company held a meeting at which it approved the weekly and monthly programs now that a coded service has become available for subscribers. The company, which will receive satellite channel transmissions and rebroadcast them for subscribers only, was formed in partnership by the National Television Organization, the Telecommunications General Organization, and the Ministry of Culture and Information.
