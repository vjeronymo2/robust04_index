January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SAVORING SAFARI

Having recently returned from a safari in Kenya, I thought you might want to know about the marvelous accommodations, great restaurants and amenities of the Safari Park on the outskirts of Nairobi.

The rates were reasonable and the extensive park-like grounds were lush, green and extremely well kept.

I heartily recommend the Safari Park Hotel, P.O. Box 45038, Nairobi, Kenya, as one of the best.

THEODORE H. FINNEY

Culver City
