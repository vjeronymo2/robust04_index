Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 11 (SNARK) -- In the short hours of quietness the Karabakh soldiers are busy with burying numerous bodies of the Azerbaijani militaries. It can be explained by the absence of the Azerbaijani leaders' desire to take the bodies of the soldiers and officers to conceal the true quantity of losses they have in the clashes. According to the data received from the Staff of the Karabakh Defense Army, the great number unburied bodies -- about 350 -- are at the suburbs of the Madagiz village, Mardakertskiy region. During the period of last 20 days about 800 soldiers and officers of the Azerbaijani Army were killed there. They were the 18-19 years old, the residents of Baku and Sumgait. Recently at the border of the Martuninskiy (Karabakh) and Agdamskiy (Azerbaijan) regions more than 200 lost Azeris militaries were buried in the common tomb. Their documents witness the fact -- they were within the Mingechaur infantry battalion.
