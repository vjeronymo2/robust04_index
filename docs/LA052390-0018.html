May 23, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A 38-year-old woman was found stabbed to death in her bedroom late Monday, authorities said.

The partially clothed body of Elissa Keller was found by her daughter shortly before midnight, said San Diego Police Department spokesman Bill Robinson.

The woman lived in the 5200 block of Trojan Avenue in East San Diego, Robinson said. No murder weapon was found.
