BFN [Text] Shenzhen, May 6 (XINHUA) -- The No. 2 generating unit, with a capacity of 900,000 kilowatts, at the Daya Bay Nuclear Power Station in south China's Guangdong Province, officially went into commercial operation today. The generating unit had been in trial operation for 700 hours and had passed various functional tests before it was officially put into operation. This indicates that the Daya Bay Nuclear Power Station has entered the stage of production from the stage of construction, according to the leading official of the power station. The first generating unit of the nuclear power station officially went into commercial operation on February 1 this year.
