September 11, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Standard Logic Inc.: The Anaheim-based manufacturer of electronic assemblies and systems reported a third-quarter net loss of $30,548, contrasted with net income of $239,334 for the like 1989 period.

Sales for the quarter ended July 28 dropped 71% to $211,882 from $730,295. For the nine-month period, the company lost $139,785, compared to a loss of $285,460 a year earlier. Sales declined 49% to $676,260 from $1.3 million.
