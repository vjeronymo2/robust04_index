Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The State Bank has granted a credit line of approximately $10 million to the National Bank of Cuba. This will allow Cuba to buy Chilean agricultural products. Minister Secretary General of Government Enrique Correa said this is a humanitarian credit which will also benefit Chilean producers. State Bank President Andres Sanfuentes said Cuba is living through a market-opening process, which represents a new business source for Chilean businessmen.
