Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, January 13 (XINHUA) -- Non-governmental scientific research institutes in south China's Guangdong Province have flourished in recent years, according to government officials. There are now more than 2,000 non-governmental scientific and technological organizations employing more than 20,000 people. With their technological advantages and flexible operating mechanism, these institutes are striving to apply more and more scientific and technological results to practical production by cooperating with other economic entities. In addition, a number of such institutes are aiming at pushing their high-tech products onto international markets by setting up Sino-foreign joint ventures. Last year their total output value hit 1.5 billion yuan (about 174.2 million U.S. dollars).
