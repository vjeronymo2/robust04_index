February 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRADLEY SHARES LPGA LEAD AT 66 WITH LEROUX

Pat Bradley had six birdies but played down her success in the first round of the Oldsmobile LPGA golf tournament.

Bradley shot a six-under-par 66 Thursday to share the lead with Nicky LeRoux.

Patty Jordan was next at 68, with Meg Mallon, Patti Rizzo, Chris Johnson, Amy Benz and Laura Hurlbut following at 69.

LeRoux's 66 was a career-low round. Her previous best was 69 in 1987.
