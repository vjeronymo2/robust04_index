BFN [By reporter Wu Ping (0702 1627)] [Text] Copenhagen, 17 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, was accorded a warm and friendly reception when he visited the Royal Danish University for Veterinary Science and Agriculture and the Nuovo Corporation this morning. The Royal Danish University for Veterinary Science and Agriculture is Denmark's only institute of higher learning for agriculture as well as one of Europe's oldest agricultural colleges. In the company of University President Dr. (Ben ser Nielsen), Li Ruihuan visited the university's veterinary studies department and heard a general briefing on the university. Dr. Nielsen expressed his hope for strengthened educational and scientific and technological exchanges between Danish and Chinese universities. Later, Li Ruihuan visited the world-famous Nuovo Corporation. Last year the corporation recorded a business turnover of 12.2 billion kroner and captured a world market share of 50 percent for its insulin and over 40 percent for its enzyme products, respectively. In the afternoon Li Ruihuan visited Denmark's largest foodstuff producer -- the (Danisco) Corporation.
