BFN [From the "News at Ten"] [Text] Four members of the new Palestinian National Authority [PNA], which will administer Gaza and Jericho, met Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali. The Palestinian delegation included Ilyas Frayj, Zakariya al-Agha, 'Azmi al-Shu'aybi, and 'Abd-al-Hafiz al-Ashhab. Prime Minister al-Majali reaffirmed Jordan's support to the new authority which will govern Palestinian self-rule areas, and said Jordan's coordination with the new authority is an absolute necessity. Mr. Frayj said their talks with the prime minister also touched on the makeup of the new authority: [Begin recording] [Frayj in progress]...with full openness with the prime minister, and we have one strategic aim is to achieve full cooperation and coordination with our Jordanian brothers. [Correspondent Maha al-Sha'ir] Have you discussed specifics with respect to the coordination between both governments? [Frayj] We come here to Jordan to discuss, each one of us, how to do the best coordination with the ministries he is in charge of. [Al-Sha'ir] When do you expect the signing of the economic agreement between the PLO and Jordan? [Al-Agha] I expect it will be within the next few days. There will be a visit by Palestinian officials, including Chairman 'Arafat, to Jordan, and all these issues will be discussed and concluded during this visit. [end recording]
