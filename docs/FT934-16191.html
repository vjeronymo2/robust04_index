931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / International Company News: Canadian freight merger in prospect CANADIAN PACIFIC and Canadian National want to merge or create a joint venture of their eastern Canada freight operations by next spring to staunch losses, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. CP's chairman, Mr William Stinson, told analysts that negotiations for linking up both systems were well advanced and the new federal government being elected on October 25 would be pressed for quick approval. The eastern Canada rail operations of both companies have been losing money for two years.
