August 1, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

U.S. STRATEGY FOR NUCLEAR RESPONSE

Toth left out the greatest threat our country faces: if (Soviet President Mikhail S.) Gorbachev prevails and gets his military to accept a unilateral end to the arms race, how will our economy survive? It can be done, but would require even greater planning than merely drawing up a new nuclear strategy.

EARL BUDIN

Los Angeles
