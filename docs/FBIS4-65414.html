BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, May 17 (A.A.) -- Fifteen terrorists and five security members were killed during clashes that broke out in the southeastern Provinces of Hakkari, Sirnak and Bingol, it was reported on Tuesday [17 May]. A statement from the Regional Governor's Office said that six terrorists were killed in Caltepe, Hakkari, six in Gabar and Besta region of Sirnak, and three in Yedisu, Bingol. Five members of security forces were killed by terrorists. The statement also said adding [word indistinct] long-range firearms and 22 hand grenades were also seized in these clashes. In a series of security operations in Elazig center 17 members of a terrorist organization were captured. Handguns, rockets and ammunition were seized in places shown by the terrorists. It was also determined that they were responsible for the killing of 11 people, four attacks on official buildings, a bank robbery and logistic and financial support for the organization, the statement said, adding that 14 of them were later arrested.
