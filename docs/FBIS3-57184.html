Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By Vladimir Gondusov, ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 21 January -- Colonel Yuriy Naumov has been appointed new commander of the 201st Russian Motorized Rifle Division stationed in Tajikistan. Before moving to Dushanbe he served as deputy commander of a formation of airborne forces. Colonel Viktor Timofeyev, who was the 201st Division's commander before him, has been relieved of this post "in connection with operational requirements," the infantry press center reported today.
