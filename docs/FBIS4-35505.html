BFN [Text] Damascus--Egyptian Petroleum Minister Hamdi al-Banbi and his delegation left Damascus to return to Cairo last night following a visit of several days to Syria. The Egyptian minister held a number of meetings with Dr. Nadir Nabulsi, minister of oil and mineral resources, and senior officials from the ministry and companies affiliated with it. Their talks focused on promoting cooperation between Syria and Egypt in all oil areas. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Dr. Nadir Nabulsi, senior ministry officials, and the Egyptian ambassador in Damascus.
