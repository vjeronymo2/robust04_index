BFN [Text] Nanning, June 19 (XINHUA) -- Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, telephoned Zhao Fulin, secretary of the CPC committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Saturday [18 June] evening to express his solicitude for the people in the flooded area of the region. Jiang asked about the flood situation, the local people's efforts to fight the disaster, and the central and local governments' relief efforts. He expressed his sympathy and solicitude for the people of various nationalities in Guangxi and for the cadres, common people, and officers and men of the People's Liberation Army, armed police force and public security force who are combating the flood. After hearing Zhao's report, Jiang noted that the Xijiang River in Guangxi is still at a flood peak and the water level remains high. "Even when the flood peak passes," he said, "efforts should be made to help the local people to tide over the disaster. Party committees and governments at all levels in the region should concentrate during the current period on the work of combating the flood and delivering disaster relief." He said the party, government and people should spare no efforts in the fight against the floods and in safeguarding the people's lives and property. Leaders at all levels should go to the frontline of the battle against the flood, keep abreast of the flood situation, organize the masses to overcome difficulties and reduce losses and make adequate arrangements for their livelihood. Leaders of the Guangxi regional party committee and government have held a meeting to discuss how to carry out General Secretary Jiang's instructions to mobilize all the forces of the party, government and people to overcome the disaster.
