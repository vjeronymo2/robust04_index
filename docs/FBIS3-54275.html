Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Robert Urbain, Belgian foreign trade and European affairs minister, and his accompanying delegation arrived in Damascus yesterday on a visit to Syria lasting several days. The visit is in response to an invitation by the Ministry of Economy and Foreign Trade. The Belgian minister was received at Damascus International Airport by Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, minister of economy and foreign trade, the Belgian ambassador, and Embassy staff in Damascus. In a statement to SANA, the Belgian minister said: I will offer the Belgian Government's condolences to President Hafiz al-Asad and his family over the painful loss of his son Basil al-Asad. I thank the Syrian Government for the warm reception despite the prevailing grief in Syria over the loss of President Hafiz al-Asad's son.
