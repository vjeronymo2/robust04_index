920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / International Company News: Seagram faces 'difficult year' SEAGRAM'S main wine and spirits businesses face a 'very difficult year,' Mr Edgar Bronfman, chairman of the international drinks group, said yesterday, Reuters reports from Montreal. After the annual meeting, Mr Bronfman declined to say how the 1992 results would compare with the previous year's earnings of USDollars 727m, or Dollars 7.67 per share, which had showed a small decline from 1990.
