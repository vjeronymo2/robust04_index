Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Civic Alliance for the Republic [GOR] appeals to the political forces to continue working on fulfilling the national budget and concluding deals with the creditor banks of the London Club and the IMF, instead of exploiting Prime Minister Berov's state of health to increase tension in the country. According to GOR leader Aleksandur Tomov, the situation at the moment should not be dramatized, especially because no one currently wants new elections. Tomov assesses the recent visit of the GOR delegation to Russia as useful and timely.
