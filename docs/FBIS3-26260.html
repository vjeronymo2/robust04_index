Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, February 4 (CNS)--According to relevant departments under the Guangzhou municipal government, the Guangzhou police and the cultural administrative department have in recent months severely cracked down on pornographic activities and the "seven social evils," catching a large number of law-breakers. Between September and December last year, the city discovered more than 13,000 people involved in the "seven social evils," with more than 3,000 of them being prostitutes and their visitors and over 9,000 gamblers. At the same time, over 500 criminal gangs were apprehended. In addition, the city also cracked down on more than 1,000 women working as hostesses, seized 59 kilograms of drugs, more than 4,000 copies of pornographic books and over 6,000 pornographic video tapes.
