941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): The author John Murray, 44: novelist, short-story writer and publisher of Panurge, a bi-annual magazine publishing new writers. Politics: Labour. Wanted: Any rise in VAT, or imposition of VAT on books, would be a big burden. Said yesterday: 'I'm pleased he didn't put VAT on books; the only thing in the budget which really affects me is the petrol and car tax increases. When he was talking about investment of private capital in everything under the sun that made my socialist hackles rise.'
