Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich has described the assessment of prospects for Russian statehood, which was made by the Russian Federation president in his annual address to the Federal Assembly, as deep and comprehensive. According to the address, the prime minister noted that it is important that Russia is not giving up its reforms. The fact that priorities of the reforms are to be slightly changed totally conforms to the Belarusian Government's position.
