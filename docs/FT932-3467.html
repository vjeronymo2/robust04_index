930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: Business Technology Pounds 6m in red RESTRUCTURED Business Technology Group, which sells and services office equipment, announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 6.31m for 1992. Losses last time of Pounds 7.44m have been restated in accordance with FRS 3. Turnover amounted to Pounds 20.2m (Pounds 42.4m) and the pre-tax result was after a loss of Pounds 271,000 on disposal of fixed assets, a Pounds 3.82m loss on the sale or termination of businesses, and Pounds 792,000 of costs associated with the reorganisation of the group's activities. Losses per share were 32.7p (38.4p).
