BFN [Excerpt] The cease-fires unilaterally declared by the two sides in the Rwandan conflict did not hold. It is true that the fighting was less fierce today than it was these past few days in Kigali, but it did continue. The United Nations took advantage of the lull to bring in a plane with food and medicines. But the respite looks like being a short one: one of the leaders of the Rwandan Patriotic Front has just announced that the airport will be the next target of his movement. He says the government forces are using it as a military base. [passage omitted]
