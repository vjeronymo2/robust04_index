920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / French charge man in Bakhtiar murder A French judge yesterday detained and charged an Iranian suspected of involvement in the murder of former Iranian prime minister Shapour Bakhtiar, in France last August, officials told Reuter. Zia Sarhadi, 25, extradited hours earlier from Switzerland, was charged with being an accomplice. Mr Bakhtiar, Iran's last premier before the Islamic revolution, was stabbed to death at his home near Paris. France is holding three other suspects, all Iranians.
