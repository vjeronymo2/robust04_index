BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Novikov] [Text] Murmansk June 15 TASS--The nuclear power station on the Kola peninsula can operate until its design service life expires, says a conclusion made by an IAEA commission which has just ended its inspection of the station. The commission checked whether recommendations given to the personnel of the station by IAEA experts two years ago had been followed. It was recommended then to enhance the reliability of the safety systems of the first and second nuclear reactors and replace outdated equipment by a modern one. The IAEA inspection pointed out that half of these recommendations had been already fulfilled.
