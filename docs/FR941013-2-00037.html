[Docket No. RP94&hyph;295&hyph;001] Gasdel Pipeline System, Inc.; Compliance Filing October 6, 1994. Take notice that on October 4, 1994, Gasdel Pipeline System, Inc. (Gasdel) tendered for filing to be a part of its FERC Gas Tariff, First Revised Volume No. 1&hyph;A, the following tariff sheets effective October 1, 1994: Third Revised Sheet No. 5 First Revised Sheet No. 11 First Revised Sheet No. 12 First Revised Sheet No. 24 First Revised Sheet No. 27 First Revised Sheet No. 31 First Revised Sheet No. 40 First Revised Sheet No. 44 Gasdel states that the purpose of this filing is to comply with the Commission's September 30, 1994, Letter Order in Docket No. RP94&hyph;295&hyph;000. Gasdel states that copies of the filing have been mailed to all parties to this proceeding and to Gasdel's customers. Any person desiring to protest said filing should file a protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426, in accordance with Rule 211 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedures (18 CFR 385.211). All such protests should be filed on or before October 14, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceedings. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
