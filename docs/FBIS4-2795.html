BFN [By reporter Wang Zengxiang (3769 1073 4382): "Heihe Economic Television Station Begins Broadcasting"] [Text] The Heihe Economic Television Station--Heilongjiang's first economic television station officially approved by the state--began broadcasting on 28 March. Heihe is one of China's first group of border cities opened to the outside world. Facing Russia's Blagoveshchensk across Heilong Jiang, it is China's largest border trade outlet. Aiming at promoting the economic and trade activities of China and Russia by establishing ties with the south and opening to the north, this television station carries programs, such as "Heihe Economic Information," "Golden Bridge," and "Collection of Border Trade Information," in Chinese and Russian languages beamed at Heihe and Blagoveshchensk every evening from Monday through Friday. The station applies enterprise management and hires personnel publicly. It is responsible for its own profits and deficits.
