BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol 18 April, TASS -- Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov and Vice-Prime Minister Yevgeniy Saburov called on the CIS leaders to support the return of the deported people of the Republic. In an appeal issued on the eve of the 50th anniversary of the deportation of Crimean Tatars, Armenians, Bulgarians, Greeks and Germans from the Crimea, they stressed that the disintegration of the Soviet Union and the emergence of new independent states have complicated their return to their homeland. Ukraine cannot finance the programme alone, the appeal said. Meshkov and Saburov called on the CIS presidents and governments to support the programme and help restore the rights of the deported people.
