Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A terrorist from the 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam organization has been killed and another terrorist was wounded and captured in a protracted shoot-out with IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers which started this morning. The incident took place in the village of Abu-Dis near Hebron. The terrorists belonged to a squad which is responsible for the murder of eight Israelis: Shin Bet agent No'am Kohen, IDF soldier Tziyon Alqobi, and Israeli citizens Mordekhay and Shalom Lapid, Eli Levin, Me'ir Mendelovich, Shalva Ozana, and Yitzhaq Weinstock. May they all rest in peace.
