October 20, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: RESEDA MAN FOUND BLUDGEONED TO DEATH

A Reseda man was found bludgeoned to death Thursday in his home, Los Angeles police reported.

A roommate discovered the body of Fredrick Wander, 28, upon returning home from work about 4 p.m., Lt. George Rock said. It was not known when Wander was killed, police said.

The roommate, whose name was withheld, last saw Wander when he left for work about 8 a.m., Rock said.

The house, owned by the roommate, is in the 6700 block of Balcom Avenue, Rock said.

Rock would not say what type of bludgeon was used.
