Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 31, IRNA -- Increase in prices of natural gas, gasoline, kerosene, gas oil and fuel oil, predicted in the next year budget bill, was voted down by the Majles deputies Monday. In its open session today, the Majles deliberating the 1373 (starting March 21) budget bill, did not approve the paragraph (a) of the note 25 of the proposed bill. The paragraph had envisaged that prices of each liter of gasoline, kerosene, gas oil and fuel oil were to increase to rls. 80, 20, 15 and 10 respectively and each cubic meter of gas to rls. 12.4. After the proposed amendments for the paragraph were not approved, the paragraph itself was voted down by the deputies. According to the rules, when the draft of an article is not approved, the Majles votes for its elimination. In continuation of the session, the Majles voted for elimination of the paragraph (a) of the note 25. In its open session last night, the Majles voted down the elimination of the note 25 and then paragraph (a) of the note.
