Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A provincial banking work conference was convened in Xining yesterday. At yesterday's conference, provincial Vice Governor Liu Guanghe delivered a speech in which he stated: This year, the province's banking work should focus on raising construction funds through all channels. Last year, the province's bank deposits registered an 18.86 percent increase and loans an 28.27 percent increase, totaling more than 3 billion yuan, while the province's insurance premiums income exceeded 110 million yuan. Vice Governor Liu Guanghe noted: This year, the province's banking departments should make continued efforts to raise funds through all channels and should whole-heartedly support provincial wide economic construction. To this end, he said, first, the province's banking departments should carry out basic work in a down-to-earth manner, exert their utmost to increase bank deposits, and take basic work as an important criterion for appraising overall work performance. Second, they should make flexible use of funds, speed up free flow of funds, and achieve higher funds utilization efficiency. The principal leaders of various localities and professional departments should take personal charge of loan recovery work in the same way they do loan provision work. Third, the province's banking departments should work hard to explore and discover new capital coordination channels to absorb as much idle capital in the society as possible. Moreover, banking departments should make every possible endeavor to ensure smooth issuance of local construction and enterprise bonds, safeguard and rectify the banking order, and ban irrational capital outflow. Fourth, the province's banking departments should make active efforts to raise all sorts of funds, develop insurance undertakings, increase insurance premiums income, and increase credit and loan sources.
