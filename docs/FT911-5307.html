910415 FT 15 APR 91 / International Company News: Executive Life awaits decision by regulators THE NEW York State insurance department said it would decide early this week what action to take over Executive Life of New York. Executive Life of New York is the smaller of the two main operating units within First Executive, the big Californian life insurance company. The Californian Insurance Department seized control of First Executive's larger operating unit, Executive Life, last week. Problems at First Executive stem from the insurer's heavy exposure to 'junk bonds'. The company was a big customer of Drexel Burnham Lambert, the investment bank which pioneered the growth of the high-yielding securities. First Executive's operating units have some Dollars 50bn of life insurance in force.
