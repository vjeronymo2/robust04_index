BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- On Tuesday [29 March] the police increased their protective measures for ``Israeli objects'' in Bonn. Among other things, the police closed the main thoroughfare where the Israeli Embassy is located. In the next few days, residents will have to identify themselves if they want to get to their houses. Normal traffic is being diverted on a large scale. According to the police, the German security authorities have been given tip-offs that Israeli ``objects'' might become targets of terrorist attacks.
