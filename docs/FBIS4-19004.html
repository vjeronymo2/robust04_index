BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- According to Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel, of the Free Democratic Party of Germany, Germany will continue to support China's market economy reforms, but is also expecting reforms in the sphere of human rights. In talks with the mayor of Shanghai, Huang Ju, in the Foreign Ministry in Bonn on Tuesday [12 April], Kinkel pointed to German investment, which totalled 280 million German marks in Shanghai alone in the first half of 1993.
