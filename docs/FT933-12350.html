930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices White pepper rose by USDollars 50 a tonne over the last five days, reports Man-producten. Malaysian and Indonesian exporters are reluctant sellers, and Vietnam seems to be sold out. The major player continues to be India, though shipments are delayed due to heavy monsoon. Brazilian exporters are staying away until nearer the harvest. Current availabilty is limited. White: Muntok/Sarawak faq spot Dollars 2,175, Jul/Aug Dollars 2,100, Aug/Sep Dollars 2,125. Black: Sarawak black label spot Dollars 1,225, Jul/Aug Dollars 1,200, yellow label spot Dollars 1,250, Jul/Aug Dollars 1,225, Brazil grade 1 spot Dollars 1,225, Jul/Aug Dollars 1,200, India mg-1 spot Dollars 1,250, Jul/Aug Dollars 1,200.
