BFN [Text] Tbilisi, April 9 (SNARK) -- The representatives of the Georgian authorities and a special investigating group of the Georgian Home Affairs Department left today for the Sadakhlo station where the Tbilisi-Yerevan train No. 675 was exploded at 3:00 a.m. The Head of the Transport Department of the Georgian National Security Department Colonel Ivan Kuldjanishvili told SNARK three passengers were killed and 5 wagons burnt as a result of explosion. The investigation has started.
