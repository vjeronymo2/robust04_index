940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Gestetner Gestetner's Australian subsidiary, Super Color, is making a recommended conditional offer for the remaining 50.1 per cent of Rabbit Photo not already owned by Super Color and Hanimex, another wholly owned Australian subsidiary of Gestetner. The offer - 25 cents cash per share - values Rabbit, a photofinishing business with retail outlets throughout Australia, at ADollars 14.7m (Pounds 7.2m).
