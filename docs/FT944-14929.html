941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / International Company News: Record sales help Cummins to Dollars 61.9m Cummins, the Indiana-based diesel engine maker, yesterday said third-quarter income rose to Dollars 61.9m, or Dollars 1.48 a share, from last year's Dollars 40.7m, or Dollars 1.11, writes Laurie Morse in Chicago. Sales for the quarter were a record Dollars 1.2bn, up from Dollars 988.3m in last year's third quarter. Strong sales in US medium-duty and heavy-duty truck markets lifted results. The company said it expected North American heavy-duty truck production to reach a record 205,000 units in 1994, up 20 per cent over 1993.
