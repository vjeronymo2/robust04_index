940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Dual net assets expand Net asset value per capital share of Throgmorton Dual Trust rose to 849.4p as at January 31, compared with 718.3p six months earlier and 667.5p at January 31 1993. Available revenue came out at Pounds 659,000 for the six month period, against Pounds 832,000, giving a per share value of 2.87p (3.62p). There is an unchanged second interim dividend of 1.75p making 3.5p (same) at the halfway stage.
