August 13, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

REP. STARK'S COMMENTS ABOUT LOUIS SULLIVAN

In regards to the remarks of Rep. Stark calling Sullivan "a disgrace to his position and to his race":

As a physician, I am familiar with previous derogatory statements by Rep. Stark.

One of his favorite ways of referring to doctors is "those bastards."

I submit that Rep. Stark is a real "hater" and that he truly despises anyone who fails to support his positions.

I suspect that he is furious that any black would not only disagree with him, but would dare to be a physician also.

KENNETH L. CLAY, MD

Santa Maria
