October 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AS IF O'BANNON DIDN'T FEEL BAD ENOUGH ALREADY

It's a shame about Ed O'Bannon's injury. Funny how fate is.

If he had kept his commitment to UNLV, this most likely wouldn't have happened.

With all the time he's going to have on his hands, O'Bannon might think about things like the value of one's word, commitment, life without basketball, etc.

E.A. MILLER

Palm Desert
