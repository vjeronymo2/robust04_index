BFN [Text] South Africans have been advised not to visit Lesotho, or to be careful if they do. The South African ambassador to Lesotho, Mr. Gerhard Fisser, said the situation in the country was tense because of the strike by policemen. He confirmed that the country's information minister, Mr. Mpho Malie, had been abducted in Maseru. Reports say he was abducted by striking policemen. Mr. Fisser said Lesotho's mining minister, Mr. Monyane Moleleki, had fled the country, but said it was not clear whether he was in South Africa or Botswana.
