BFN [Text] According to a report from XINHUA News Agency, China conducted an underground nuclear test on the morning of 10 June. While making an announcement on this, the spokesman of the Chinese Foreign Ministry said that China will completely ban and thoroughly destroy nuclear weapons, and that in this connection, it consistently supports the realization of a comprehensive nuclear test ban. He said that stemming from this fundamental position, China has been manifesting great self-restraint from the beginning in conducting nuclear tests, and that the number of nuclear tests conducted by China is very limited. He said that China completely respects and understands the position and worry of many nonnuclear countries in connection with the issue of nuclear tests. The spokesman said that currently China is actively taking part in the negotiations on the comprehensive test ban treaty, and through this kind of negotiations, China supports the conclusion of such a treaty before 1996. He said that such a treaty must be a measure for a complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons. He said that China demands that other countries that possess nuclear weapons must give up their policy on restraining nuclear weapons and clearly promise the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons. The spokesman said that to quickly realize this ultimate goal, China is willing to continue to persistently make efforts with the international community.
