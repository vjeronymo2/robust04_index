Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unsigned message No. 61-1 SD from the Russian State Duma "To Defenders of the Fatherland," dated Moscow, 18 February 1994 and published under "In the State Duma" rubric] [Text] The deputies of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma have a high opinion of the performance of Russian Armed Forces personnel who are fulfilling their duties to defend the fatherland's state interests and security in the current difficult socioeconomic situation. Cordially greeting all Army and Navy personnel, veterans of the Great Patriotic War, and former internationalist soldiers on Defenders of the Fatherland Day, we express our confidence that every one of you will continue to give exemplary service to the motherland and will make his contribution to the cause of the rebirth and renewal of a great Russia. In its legislative activity the State Duma will always keep its attention focused on questions of creating a legal basis for strengthening the country's defense capability and for providing social guarantees to Russian Army and Navy personnel, members of servicemen's families, Armed Forces and Great Patriotic War veterans, and former internationalist soldiers. [Dated] Moscow, 18 February 1994
