Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Report under the rubric "Military Reports From Abroad": "Ukraine Has Offered To Sell Iran a Submarine"] [Text] The Saudi newspaper AS SHARQ AL AWSAT has reported that Ukraine has offered to sell "a considerable portion of its share of the former Soviet Navy" to countries of the Persian Gulf. Citing a "Washington source," the newspaper states that the sale would include missile and torpedo boats, destroyers and minelayers. Ukraine has also offered to sell a submarine to Iran.
