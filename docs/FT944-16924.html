941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Regent Corp buy Regent Corporation is to acquire Rayford Holdings, which has interests in sites valued at Pounds 5.06m. The consideration part comprises Pounds 814,000 in new shares; Regent has also agreed that, on completion, Rayford will pay Pounds 3m cash to Mr Ray Horney, one of the Rayford shareholders, as part repayment of a Pounds 5.6m loan made by him to Rayford.
