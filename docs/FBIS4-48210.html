BFN [Text] The chief of staff of the army division of the NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia], Lieutenant General (John Teah), says the purpose of the scheduled meeting today between the commanders and commander in chief of the NPFL is to educate senior NPFL officers on the peaceful implementation of the Cotonou Accord. In an interview with Radio Liberia, Gen. Teah said that it is expected that after the meeting the various commanding generals and chiefs of staff of the NPFL will be able to instruct their men how best they can implement the Cotonou Peace Accord in Liberia.
