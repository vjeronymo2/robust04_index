920416 FT 16 APR 92 / International Company News: Ruling on San Miguel shares THE anti-corruption court in the Philippines has ruled that the presidential commission on good government may continue to vote a bloc of sequestered shares in San Miguel Corporation, the country's largest industrial enterprise, that previously were controlled by local businessman Mr Eduardo Cojuangco. Mr Cojuangco had petitioned the anti-corruption court to restrain any party, including the presidential commission, from voting the bloc of shares, which represent 30 per cent of the company's shares.
