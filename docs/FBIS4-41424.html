BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe June 24 TASS -- One passer-by was killed and another wounded on Friday in Dushanbe when unknown gunmen fired a grenade at a bus carrying children of Russian military servicemen in Tajikistan and their guards. Nobody among the bus passengers was wounded, TASS was told at the Tajik Interior Ministry.
