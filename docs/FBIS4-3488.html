Phase SK1804093894 Shenyang LIAONING RIBAO in Chinese 5 Apr 94 p 1 SK1804093894 Shenyang LIAONING RIBAO Chinese BFN [Text] The second phase of the Yingkou Bayuquan harbor area project, one of the large and medium-sized construction projects covered in the state's Eighth Five-Year Plan, formally started construction on 1 April. After the completion and operation of the first phase of the Bayuquan harbor area project, the state approved the second phase in 1993 and also brought it into line with the Eighth Five-Year Plan. The total investment in the project's second phase is 367 million yuan. The second phase involves the construction of five 10,000- ton-class deepwater bulk and sundry goods berths and is expected to be completed and go into operation by 1997. Upon completion of the project's second phase, Yingkou harbor, the second largest harbor in northeast China, will increase 2,200,000 tons of loading and unloading capacity annually.
