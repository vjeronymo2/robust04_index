BFN [Text] Jacques Delors, who was the guest of Arte Television's Transit program this evening, said he entirely agrees with the NATO ultimatum given to the Serbs. The president of the European Commission in Brussels thinks that an international peace conference should be held as soon as possible: [Begin Delors recording] As things stand now, that is what has to be done. Then if the Serbs have finally understood, an international peace conference should be organized in order to find a solution, which will unfortunately be based on the partition of the territory, which is now inevitable. This is the second time I have felt ashamed in my life. The first time was when I ran away, walking a 500-km distance, running away from the German troops in 1940 during the invasion of my country, when there were no French troops to defend us. This is the second time I have felt ashamed of myself. The first time I was 12 or 13 years old. The construction of Europe gave me hope that this would all change. I am nearing the end of my career and I fear that I will bear this shame until the end of life. Yes I am ashamed! [end Delors recording]
