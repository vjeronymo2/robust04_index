Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] According to [Spanish Defense Minister Julian] Garcia Vargas, the Spanish soldiers deployed in Sarajevo will not intervene even if NATO were to bomb the Serbs, because: We do not belong to the air task force. Spain answered NATO by saying that it would not increase the number of blue helmets in Bosnia; however it can send more observers, between five and 10 officers. The Spaniards will stay in the Herzegovina area. For his part, PSOE [Spanish Socialist Workers Party] spokesperson Solana [Spanish foreign minister], said that the NATO ultimatum had been successful. Now let us think about the next step.
