941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / International Company News: Loblaw rises in third quarter Loblaw, Canada's biggest food distributor, posted a 37 per cent rise in third-quarter net profit to CDollars 33.5m (USDollars 24.69m), or 39 cents a share, on sales of CDollars 3.1bn, up 7 per cent. Loblaw, controlled indirectly by the Weston family, said sales were up 10 per cent in eastern Canada, flat in the west and up 9 per cent in the US. Operating net income was up 36 per cent to CDollars 75.7m. Nine-month net profit was CDollars 84.3m, or CDollars 1 a share, up from CDollars 67.3m, or 76 cents, on sales of CDollars 7.6bn against CDollars 7.2bn.
