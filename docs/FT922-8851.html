920511 FT 11 MAY 92 / Privatisation is attacked PRIVATISATION of British Coal and British Rail 'give a green light for a new round of the white-collar rip-off, costing the country dear, but leaving the City rolling in fat fees', Mr Frank Dobson, shadow energy secretary, said yesterday. 'Millions of pounds will be poured into the pockets of city firms,' he said. 'Accountants and bankers, brokers, underwriters, estate agents will all make a fortune. The City is in for a bonanza. Privatisation is a system of outdoor relief for the advisory classes.'
