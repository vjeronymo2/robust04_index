Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Corrections"] [Text] According to the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission, the published list of deputies of the State Duma of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly elected to federal electoral districts from the "Liberal-Democratic Party of Russia [LDPR]" Electoral Association (ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA 28 December 1993) should include the following: Viktor Grigoryevich Vishnyakov, born 1931, Russian, higher education, member of the LDPR, thereafter as published; Aleksey Alekseyevich Zuyev, born 1971, Russian, secondary technical education, nonparty member, temporarily out of work, thereafter as published; Nina Viktorovna Krivelskaya, born 1948, Russian, higher education, nonparty member, senior scientific staffer at the Russian Federation Ministry of Internal Affairs Psychology and Personnel Work Scientific Training Complex, thereafter as published. The published list of deputies of the State Duma of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly elected to single-seat electoral districts (Tomsk Rural Electoral District No. 174) should include: Stepan Stepanovich Sulakshin, born 1954, Russian, higher education, member of the Republic Party of the Russian Federation, member of the "Russia's Choice" Electoral Association, thereafter as published.
