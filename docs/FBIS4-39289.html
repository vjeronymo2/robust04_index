BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniya Lents] [Text] Vladivostok, 7 Jun -- Republic of Korea President Kim Yong-sam has ended his visit to Russia. Today he flew from Vladivostok to Khabarovsk, from where he will set off back home. During his stay in Maritime Kray, which has a large Korean diaspora, Kim Yong-sam met with the Maritime Kray administration and visited a Pacific Fleet vessel.
