BFN [Foreign Ministry Spokesman Wu Jianmin's Weekly Press Briefings; from the "Current Affairs" program] [Text] Finally, the Foreign Ministry spokesman restated China's sovereignty over Tibet. Wu Jianmin said the Central Government has made repeated contacts with personal representatives of the Dalai Lama, the spiritual leader of Tibet. Wu Jianmin said the door is always open for talks between the Central Government and the exiled Dalai Lama. However, he stressed, the talks can cover anything but Tibet's separation from the country.
