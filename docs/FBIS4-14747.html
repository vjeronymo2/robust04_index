BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Nekrasova] [Text] Lvov April 4 TASS -- The situation in the uranium industry is not controlled by anybody, said Konstantin Rudya, head of the national department for nuclear and radiation safety at the Ministry for Environmental Protection, after the end of his inspection trip to a number of uranium producing enterprises in Zheltiy Vodiy, Dnepropetrovsk region. He stressed that the extraction and processing of uranium were control-free in the region. Sanitary control authorities are not given the needed information, and there is no normative base in that sphere.
