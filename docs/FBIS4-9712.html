BFN ["Text" of letter from Venezuelan Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Burelli Rivas to Colombian President Cesar Gaviria on Gaviria's victory in the OAS election for new secretary general] [Text] Caracas, 28 March 1994 Your Excellency Dr. Cesar Gaviria Trujillo President of the Republic Bogota Mr. President: Please accept my congratulations on your election to the office of secretary general of the OAS. I offer you all the cooperation I can give in the hopes that you, as secretary general, will be able to coordinate the expectations and spirit of this hemisphere that shakes because of its internal social differences and the stupor of the unusual world disorder unleashed by the most hurried changes in history. I trust that in your hands this universe [as published] of ours will be deserving of the name of "Continent of hope." Cordially, [Signed] Miguel Angel Burelli.
