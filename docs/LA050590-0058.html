May 5, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS

* Kenichi Komaya has been named executive vice president of finance for Kawasaki Motors Corp., U.S.A. in Irvine.

He was previously vice president of the department and also serves as treasurer and controller.

Komaya joined the company in 1988 after working in the finance department of Kawasaki Heavy Industries.

In addition, Bob Shepard was promoted from vice president to senior vice president, administration and information systems.

Shepard, who joined Kawasaki in 1977, previously held positions with Western Airlines and Boeing Corp.
