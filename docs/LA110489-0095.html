November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

TWENTYNINE PALMS;

MARINE UNHURT AS JET CRASHES IN DESERT

A Marine jet on a training exercise crashed Friday in a remote area of the Southern California desert, but the pilot ejected safely, military officials said. The crash of the A-4M Skyhawk occurred at 10:20 a.m. at the Marine Corps Air Ground Combat Center, a sprawling base in Twentynine Palms. The cause of the crash was under investigation. The pilot, whose identity was withheld, was examined at a base hospital as a precaution.
