BFN [Text] Practically all proposals on the division of the Black Sea Fleet ships have been completed at talks between the Russian and Ukrainian delegations, Ukrainian Navy Commander Vladimir Bezkorovainy told INTERFAX-Ukraine. He said that the parties had just begun to consider the issue on division of the coastal infrastructure and air forces. The Russian delegation is headed by Russian Navy Commander-in-Chief, Admiral Feliks Gromov. Ukrainian Vice Premier Valeriy Shmarov leads the Ukrainian delegation. INTERFAX learned from the Black Sea Fleet headquarters that Gromov had visited Donuzlav (one of the main Black Sea Fleet bases in Crimea, near Yalta). According to non-official information, Russia offered this base to the Ukrainian Navy.
