Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Lyuben Berov today stated his view that the reports in certain newspapers to the effect that Stoyan Denchev had active contacts with private business circles correspond to the truth, adding that this is incompatible with Denchev's position as chief secretary of the Council of Ministers. Denchev has reportedly been offered a diplomatic post in London or Helsinki, but apparently no official decision on the affair has yet been adopted.
