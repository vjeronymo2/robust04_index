October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: FATHER, SON ARRESTED IN 'SEX PARTY' FOR 200

A father and son were arrested Thursday on warrants charging them with felony pandering for allegedly organizing a "sexually explicit" party at a Windsor Hills banquet hall last month attended by about 200 men and a dozen paid female strippers, sheriff's deputies said.

Allen Williams Sr., 56, and Allen Jr., 30, were arrested at their home in the 1100 block of 43rd Place in Los Angeles and are being held in the County Jail in lieu of $5,000 bail.

Deputies said evidence seized in searches of a house in Los Angeles and one in San Bernardino County allegedly link the Williamses to similar parties at other locations.

While checking out a complaint of a disturbance at Ashton's Shatto in the 3700 block of Slauson Avenue last month, deputies discovered a wild "sex party" where naked women were "performing various sexual acts with the men." The Williamses were not present at that party, deputies said.
