Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku February 16 TASS -- Armenia rejected mediation by the authoritative international organizations "Foundation Global for Community" and "International Alert" in arranging a meeting of Armenian and Azerbaijani experts, which was scheduled to be held in Oslo later this month. The meeting was to concentrate on possible ways of settling the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict. Armenia accounted for its refusal by the fact that Nagorno-Karabakh's Armenian community was barred from negotiations, while Armenia thinks it necessary to grant this community the status of a party in the conflict, Azerbaijan's Foreign Ministry reported tonight.
