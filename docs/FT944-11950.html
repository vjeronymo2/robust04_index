941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / International Company News: Seagram opens S Africa marketing operation Seagram, the Canadian drinks company, has launched Seagram South Africa, a wholly-owned sales and marketing operation to service the southern African region, writes Mark Suzman. At the new company's launch in Cape Town yesterday, Mr Jake Scott, vice-president of Seagram for Europe and Africa, said the company was committed to building up local employment and would sponsor a social responsibility programme aimed at South African youth.
