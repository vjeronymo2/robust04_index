BFN [Text] Beijing, April 13 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin said here today that the varied cooperations between China and Nepal have been satisfactory. He made the remarks at a meeting with Beni Bahaour Karki, chairman of the upper house of Nepal's Parliament. China and Nepal are good neighbors, and the Nepalese-Chinese friendship has a long history, Jiang said. He said that the two countries' cooperation in the fields of politics, culture, economy and trade in recent years is satisfactory. Extending a welcome to Karki on his first visit to China, Jiang said he hoped that the guests will gain a better understanding of China during their stay. Karki said that one of the purposes of his visit is to see the changes brought about by the Chinese Government's policies of reform and opening to the outside world. He also said that the Nepalese people and government would never forget the aid and support China has offered for the economic development of Nepal. Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), was present at the meeting. The Nepalese visitors arrived in Lhasa, capital of the Tibet Autonomous Region, April 9 to begin a ten-day visit to China as guests of the CPPCC National Committee.
