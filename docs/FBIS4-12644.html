BFN [Text] Israel and the PLO have reached an understanding on the agricultural section of the economic talks being held in Paris. Our correspondent Ya'aqov Balsenboim reports that the understanding provides for open borders between Israel and the autonomy subject to tight control by Israeli security, customs, and veterinary officials. The Palestinians will be allowed to export poultry, eggs, and produce to Israel. Industry and Trade Minister Mikha Harish conferred last night with Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'], the PLO representative to the Paris talks, and explained to him the circumstances which led to the closure imposed on the territories in the wake of the murder in 'Afula and other terror attacks.
