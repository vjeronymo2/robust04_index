November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

STEVE GARVEY

Steve Garvey was my favorite Dodger, and I tried to keep an open mind in regards to his "problems" -- until I read his "side of the story." I can only tell you, if he was my husband or my friend or my associate, I would become hysterical and need counseling just being around this "pillar of perfection." All I can say to Cyndy Garvey is, I wish you and your daughters all the best.

TERESA BIRD, Torrance
