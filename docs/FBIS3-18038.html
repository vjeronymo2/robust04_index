Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- According to the Federal Press Office, Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl and Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel have reacted with "shock and outrage to the terrible butchery in the mosque of Hebron." The Federal Government is expressing is deepest sympathy to the families of the victims and hopes that the initiated peace process between Israel and the PLO will not be endangered. Bonn calls on all responsible forces to prevent an escalation of violence.
