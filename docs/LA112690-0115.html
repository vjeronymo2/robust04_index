November 26, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

S. AFRICA BLACK CHILDREN CLUBBED

Neo-Nazis in uniforms with swastika-style insignia whipped and clubbed a group of 400 black children on a Sunday School picnic in a white South African town, injuring at least seven, witnesses said today.

Several people were hurt in another town Sunday when brown-shirted whites attacked a meeting of the mainly black National Union of Mineworkers, the NUM said.

About 25 whites in neo-Nazi khaki uniforms lashed out at the 400 black children, aged between 7 and 14, at the picnic in a park at Louis Trichardt.
