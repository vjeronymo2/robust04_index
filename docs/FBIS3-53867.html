Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, received the ambassadors of the EC member states in Ta'izz today. They reviewed aspects of relations between our country and the EC member states and discussed developments in our country in light of the results of the ambassadors' previous meetings with the brother president and the vice president and in light of the Document of Pledge and Accord, worked out by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee to build a modern Yemeni state. The ambassadors said their countries' welcomed and gave their blessings for the document as a positive step toward ending the crisis peacefully and achieving stability in Yemen. They also expressed hope that the document would be implemented effectively. They reaffirmed their countries' support for unity and democracy, their appreciation of dialogue as a basis for solutions, and their objection to violence and military action. They also said that the EC member states are eager to maintain fruitful cooperation and to support the march of social and economic development in the Republic of Yemen. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah and General People's Congress member 'Abd-al-Karim al- Iryani.
