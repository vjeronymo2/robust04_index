Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Iran's foreign minister, Mr. Velayati, has invited his Pakistani counterpart to take part in the ministerial meeting of the Economic Cooperation Organization [ECO] to be held in Tehran from 25 to 26 January. The invitation was handed over by the Iranian Foreign Ministry's director general for west Asia in a meeting in Islamabad on Tuesday [4 January]. During the meeting the two sides also discussed issues of mutual interest.
