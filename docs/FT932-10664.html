930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Asset growth at First Ireland Over the year to March 31 First Ireland Investment saw net asset value rise 26.6 per cent in sterling terms. In Irish currency the increase was 17.4 per cent, which compared with a rise of 10.6 per cent in the Irish equity market. Total revenue rose to Pounds 1.33m (Pounds 1.03m) and net outcome to Pounds 505,000 (Pounds 389,000). Earnings per share came to 1.7p (1.3p) and the single dividend is lifted to 1.44p (1.18p).
