Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Foreign Ministry press and public relations service has reported that Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati sent a message to Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan on 13 January. The message says: Being a supporter of the establishment of peace in the Caucasian region, last year Iran used its mediation efforts to attain this important objective. Dr. Velayati also noted that war and bloodshed have had harmful affects on Armenia and Azerbaijan, and the continuation of the conflict could disrupt relations between all the regional countries, and could be used as pretext by those who want to (?escalate) the crisis. Next, the message highly assessed the Armenian Republic's important and valuable role in the effort to establish peace and stability in the Karabakh conflict zone. In this connection, Dr. Velayati noted that his country is ready to take all possible steps [passage indistinct]. At the end of his message, Dr. Velayati referred to Iran's (?efforts) to bring about the immediate termination of the military operations and bloodshed -- which is one of the principles of Iranian foreign policy -- and hoped that in the new Christian year [words indistinct].
