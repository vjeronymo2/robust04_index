BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 19 (KCNA) -- The 4th National Inventions and New Technology Exhibition opened at the Three Revolutions Exhibition here on Wednesday. More than 5,000 pieces of inventions and technical innovation proposals of some 2,000 kinds are on display in the form of real objects, models, visual aids, mock-ups, video recordings, etc. The opening ceremony was attended by Choe Tae-pok, alternate member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, officials concerned, scientists, technicians and working people. Choe Hui-chong, chairman of the State Commission of Science and Technology, made an opening address. At the end of the opening ceremony the participants saw round the exhibits.
