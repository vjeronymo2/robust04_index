Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Ivan Semykin report: "Agrarian Workers Coordinate Plans"] [Text] The latest meeting of the Intergovernmental Council for Agro-Industrial Complex Questions, to sum up the results of the council's work in 1993 and coordinate plans for 1994, was held in Barnaul 24 March. Statutes on the council's working group, the interstate scientific and technical center, and the interstate council for questions of agrarian science and information were examined and approved, and the chairman of the Intergovernmental Council on Agro-Industrial Complex Questions for 1994 was elected. A.Kh. Zaveryukha, deputy chairman of the Russian Government, has been elected to this post. It was deemed advisable for council sessions in 1994 to examine questions of coordinating agrarian policy and creating a common agrarian market, as well as the problems of coordinating the states' activity in combating diseases and pests affecting agricultural crops and livestock.
