EFFECTIVE DATE: June 3, 1994. 21 CFR Parts 510, 520, and 524 Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products; Change of Sponsor AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect a change of sponsor for five new animal drug applications (NADA's) from Purina Mills, Inc., to PM Resources, Inc.
