BFN [Text] Secessionist fortifications in the al-Dali' area were blown up the day before yesterday [14 May]. Their destruction was attended by a large number of people who announced their rejection of the despotic and secessionist gang that ruled tyrannically over the people of al-Dali' throughout the period of the abominable totalitarian rule. Many people arrived in al-Dali' city declaring their support for the legitimate authority. They also declared that they were joining with the forces of constitutional legitimacy that have returned life to normal and provided them with all means of security and protection from any threat posed by the remnants of secession and apostasy, which have been completely uprooted from the region.
