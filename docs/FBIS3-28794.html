Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 10 KYODO -- Popular support for the cabinet of Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa has fallen to 59.2 percent this week from 62.8 percent in December, a KYODO NEWS SERVICE poll showed Thursday [10 February]. It was the lowest since Hosokawa formed the coalition cabinet last August. The KYODO poll, taken Tuesday and Wednesday, also indicated that those who do not support the Hosokawa cabinet came to 29.7 percent, up 5.3 percentage points. Of those polled, 54.5 percent said they opposed the proposed "national welfare tax," which Hosokawa presented on February 3. The proposal was later revoked.
