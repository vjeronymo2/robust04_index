March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ARTS

Humanities Endowment Head Reappointed: President Bush announced that he has nominated Lynne V. Cheney, chairman of the National Endowment for the Humanities, for reappointment to another four-year term.

Cheney, 48, was first named to the post by former President Ronald Reagan in May, 1986. Her appointment requires reconfirmation by the Senate. Cheney is the wife of Defense Secretary Dick Cheney. BETH KLEID
