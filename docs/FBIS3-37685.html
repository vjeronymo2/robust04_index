Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Georgiy Shmelev and Aleksandr Yakovlev] [Text] Prague, 19 Feb -- Vaclav Havel, the president of the Czech Republic, today met Russian Federation Foreign Affairs Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who is visiting the republic during his tour of the Visegrad group countries. They discussed issues linked to the setting up a new structure of European security and other topical international problems, and the role of the North Atlantic alliance and the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe in tackling them.
