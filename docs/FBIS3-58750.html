Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl (Christian Democratic Union) has reaffirmed Germany's willingness to expand political and economic relations with the PRC. Outstanding importance attaches to China in the context of the German Government's Asia policy, Kohl said today in Bonn during talks with Qiao Shi, chairman of the PRC National People's Congress. According to government spokesman Dieter Vogel, the chancellor underlined that the dialogue with China encompassed all issues.
