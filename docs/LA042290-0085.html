April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WHERE IT IS AND WHAT IT COSTS

Sooke Harbour House, 1528 Whiffen Spit Road, R.R. 4, Sooke, B.C. V0S 1NO, Canada, (604) 642-3421.

Room rates: about $80 to $200 a night (May 1 rates rise to $110 to $210), including light breakfast and light lunch. (Breakfast room service costs an extra $4.50 per person.) Reservations must be canceled eight days in advance.

Three-course dinner for two (including a bottle of Canadian wine): about $75 to $100. Special three-night honeymoon packages available for $490 to $695 for two, including three champagne breakfasts and light lunches and one multi-course special dinner, served in the dining room or guest room.

Visa, MasterCard and American Express are accepted. Colman Andrews
