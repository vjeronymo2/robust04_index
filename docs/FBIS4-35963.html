BFN [Text] Brother Jalal Talabani, secretary general of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK] received a delegation of the Iranian Kurdistan Democratic Party Political Bureau in his office in Irbil at 1700 yesterday. The delegation came to welcome his return home. PUK Political Bureau members (Nushiru Mustafa), Dr. Fu'ad Ma'sum, and Dr. Kamal Fu'ad also attended the meeting. During the meeting, they discussed the situation in Iraqi Kurdistan, brother Talabani's peaceful overture, and ways of ending the tense situation in Kurdistan. They also discussed Iranian interference in Kurdistan's affairs. The delegation expressed appreciation for brother Talabani's peaceful initiative and indignation at the interference by certain forces which are supported by Iran.
