Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The staff of the Banja Luka UNHCR office today left the town. We received this piece of information a few minutes ago. The head of the office, Fridrich Rotwate, informed Mayor Predrag Radic that he had received the order from New York. He said that he did not have any other details, or did not want to give them. The residents of Banja Luka who are working as haulage operators and drivers or are performing other jobs in that organization have stayed behind. The officials of the International Red Cross stayed in Banja Luka and are performing their duties. Their head, Christian Brunner, told the mayor that they do not have plans at present to leave the town.
