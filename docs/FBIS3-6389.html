Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Datuk Joseph Pairin Kitingan today resigned as Sabah chief minister, a post he has held since 1985. Pairin -- who is also president of the Sabah United Party, PBS -- handed his letter of resignation personally to the governor, Tun Mohamed Said Keruak, at 1.50 pm [0550 GMT] at the Palace in Kota Kinabalu.
