920222 FT 22 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Jamaica pimento sold out in US until Apr/May shipment, reports Man-producten. Europe quoting Dollars 2,500 a tonne spot. Indian chili market very firm; stemless quoted at 92 to 94 cents. Cassia/cinnamom - no change. Cloves - Madagascar/Commores/Zanzibar resellers Dollars 1,425-Dollars 1,400 for feb/mar shipment, afloat Dollars 1,500; spot hardly available. Ginger - Indian, Cochin very firm, prices up from Dollars 925 to Dollars 1,100 for Mar/Apr shipment; Nigerian available spot Dollars 850 ex-warehouse. Nutmegs/mace - no change. Turmeric firm; Madras fingers from Dollars 820 to Dollars 875, ground from Dollars 910 to Dollars 975, all cif Europe, Feb/Mar shipment.
