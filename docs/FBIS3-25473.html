Language: Tigrigna Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni President General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his delegation were seen off today from Asmara International Airport by President Isayas Afewerki; Foreign Minister Muhammad Ahmad Sharifo; Ali Sayyid Abdallah, the minister of internal affairs; (Yesuf Yeshaq), head of the Middle East desk at the foreign office; and Mahmmud Jabra, the Eritrean ambassador to Yemen. President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih told journalists before leaving that he was pleased with his visit in Eritrea and his meetings with President Isayas and other Eritrean Government officials. He also said his visit would have a positive effect on the efforts being made to maintain Yemeni's unity and the relations between the two countries. He added that he was now leaving for Amman, Jordan, to sign an agreement with the various Yemeni political parties. He expressed the hope that the agreement would solve Yemen's problems. He urged the three main political parties working to create a coalition to implement the agreement seriously. Noting Eritrea's role in solving Yemen's problems, President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih said Eritrea's efforts had been very successful. He said Eritrea's role in bringing the various political parties together had been very significant.
