920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Economics: Looking for 'green shoot' indicators of UK recovery A HOST of indicators on the the UK economy will be released this week. Evidence of economic recovery is still thin so the figures - in particular data on industrial production, retail sales, and unemployment - will be scrutinised for signs of 'green shoots'. After May's sunny beginning there were high expectations for the month's retail sales, but reports from individual retailers suggest that the half-hearted post-election spending spree dwindled last month. There are higher hopes for manufacturing output, which has risen for two consecutive months. On a three-monthly trend basis the decline in output appears to be over and the forecast is for another modest rise. The following are some of the other economic highlights and events of the week.
