October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MEDICARE SURTAX

A minority of wealthy old people, by promoting the repeal of Medicare's catastrophic care program, have mortally wounded a majority of not so wealthy old people.

Never in our history have so few hurt so many. If our nation's history should last a thousand years they will say:

This was their most selfish hour!

FRANK A. FERRONE

El Cajon
