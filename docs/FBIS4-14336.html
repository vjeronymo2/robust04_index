BFN [Text] Israel and China signed a memorandum of understanding to cooperate in the energy field. Our correspondent Arye Schorr reports that Israel might buy coal in China for the Israel Electricity Corporation and an experimental shipment will arrive here within several days. China is interested in technology to produce electricity from solar energy and from oil shale developed in Israel, as well as cooperation with a cable company to manufacture electricity cables in China. The memorandum was signed by Energy Minister Moshe Shahal and the Chinese electric power industry minister, who is visiting Israel.
