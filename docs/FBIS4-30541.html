BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 26 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on June 26 sent a message of greetings to President Hassan Gouled Aptidon on the occasion of the 17th anniversary of the independence of Djibouti. President Kim Il-song in the message expresses the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries will grow stronger and wishes the Djiboutian president and people greater success in the work for the prosperity of the country.
