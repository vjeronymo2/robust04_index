930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Zaire MPs caution Mobutu Zaire's transitional parliament decided yesterday that President Mobutu Sese Seko had no right to sack opposition Prime Minister Etienne Tshisekedi, Reuter reports from Kinshasa. Mr Mobutu had signed an order firing Mr Tshisekedi, blaming him for army riots in late January in which several hundred people were killed.
