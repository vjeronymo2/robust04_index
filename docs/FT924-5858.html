921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / 'Horrible Year' For Queen John Major leans over Lord Mayor Sir Francis McWilliams to talk to the Queen after her address at a Corporation of London lunch at Guildhall celebrating her 40 years on the throne. The Queen referred to 1992 as 'an annus horribilis' and said scrutiny of the royal family could be just as effective 'with a touch of gentleness, good humour and understanding'. Report, Page 10
