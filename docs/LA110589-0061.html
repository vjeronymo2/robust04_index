November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TEXAN FRUITCAKES

I thoroughly enjoyed David M. Kinchen's article about Corsicana fruitcakes and Texas courthouses ("Locate the Fruitcake and You Get a Lone Star," Oct. 15), but I must voice my disappointment that he did not include the courthouses in Hillsboro and Marshall -- both singled out by art historians as being outstanding examples of Victorian architecture in Texas.

VIRGINIA GAUSTAD

Redlands
