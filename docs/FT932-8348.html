930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Hartlepools Water marks time Hartlepools Water turned in pre-tax profits for the year to March 31 marginally lower at Pounds 1.22m against Pounds 1.24m for 1991-92. Turnover was a little better at Pounds 5.31m compared with Pounds 5.09m. Mr Jeremy Ropner, chairman, said that trading conditions were difficult throughout the year, but said the outlook for the current year appeared to be a little more promising. With a lower proportionate tax charge, earnings go up from 117p to 126p per share and the proposed final dividend is 33p (29p) to make a total of 55p (50p).
