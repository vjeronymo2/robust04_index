October 7, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: FULLERTON;

SPORTS ALLIANCE DONATES $3,000 TO JUNIOR HIGHS

The Fullerton Athletic Alliance, an organization that raises funds to assist the Fullerton School District's after-school sports program, has donated $3,000 to help Fullerton's three junior high schools participate in the North Orange County Athletic League.

John Charlton, director of the league, said the money will be used to purchase uniforms, soccer nets, paint to mark fields and chalk for the track fields at Nicolas, D. Russell Parks and Ladera Vista junior high schools.
