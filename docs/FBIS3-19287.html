Article Type:BFN [Report by Paul Verbraecken: "United Nations Requests Belgian Military for Tuzla Airport--Air Force To Man Control Towers of Disputed Airport"] [Excerpt] On his way to Rwanda, Defense Minister Delcroix confirmed on Thursday [10 March] that the United Nations had formally requested Belgian military support for the reopening of Tuzla airport in Bosnia. The United Nations requests a contingent of 25 Belgian Air Force staff to man the control towers of this disputed airport. There is no mention of pilots.
