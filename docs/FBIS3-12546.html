Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Mar (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that the shooting down of four Serb aircraft by NATO jet fighters is proof of NATO's seriousness in carrying out its ultimatum addressed to Serbia within the framework of UN resolutions. In a statement today, he said that this action was supposed to have been done earlier, but is still good nonetheless. He said that according to the reports we have received, the resolution on moving heavy weapons 20 km from Sarajevo has been violated. He stressed the need to strike at the forces that violate this resolution.
