920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / Roh quits S Korea ruling party South Korean President Roh Tae-woo said yesterday he would quit the ruling party, yielding to a key opposition demand that he remain neutral in elections for his successor later this year, Reuter reports from Seoul. Mr Roh also pledged to reorganise his cabinet to try to avert a political storm over an election fraud which has severely tainted the image of his government.
