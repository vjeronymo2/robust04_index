Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- Members of the "Sunflower Society" looking up to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il as the lodestar of the nation recently met in Suwon to discuss this year's action program, according to a report of Radio Voice of National Salvation from Seoul. At the meeting Chief of the Society Kim Mu-chin said that the organisation was named "Sunflower Society" from motives of holding in high esteem and following only the dear leader Mr. Kim Chong-il as the sunflower invariably follows the sun any time and anywhere. He said: "The dear leader Mr. Kim Chong-il who imbues us with the spirit of independence, unfolds a great program of reunification and embraces the 70 million fellow countrymen in his bosom is the destiny of our nation, its unified motherland and its future. "Let us all become the loyal sunflower following the dear leader Mr. Kim Chong-il, the sun of guidance, through generations." Member of the Society Sin Chong-su said that the hearts of the members of the "Sunflower Society" following the dear leader Mr. Kim Chong-il as sunflower follows the sun at all times remain firm like a rock unshaken in any whirlwind and are upright like pine and bamboo.
