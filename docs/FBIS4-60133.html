BFN [Text] A telephone contact has taken place between the brother leader of the revolution and President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in fraternal Yemen. In the telephone conversation the brother leader expressed his deep concern over the events in Yemen and the grave developments of the situation there. Their views agreed on rejecting session and fighting between the brothers in fraternal Yemen. Another telephone contact has taken place between the brother leader of the revolution and President Husni Mubarak, president of Egypt, during which they exchanged congratulations on the occasion of 'Id al-Adha and expressed deep concern over the events of Yemen. Their views agreed on rejecting session and fighting between the brothers in fraternal Yemen.
