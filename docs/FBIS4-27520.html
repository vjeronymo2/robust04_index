BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- His Excellency Khieu Samphan, president of the democratic Cambodia side, and his entourage left here today by air after participating in the roundtable conference for peace and national reconciliation of Cambodia. They were seen off at the airport by Vice Foreign Minister Cho Kyu-il and Cambodian Royal Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea Oum Mannorine.
