Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kawasaki, Kanagawa Pref., Feb 28 KYODO -- Homes of two senior officials of the Transport Ministry in Kawasaki and Yokohama were attacked Monday night by suspected left-wing extremists, police said. No one was injured in the attacks, police said. Explosive devices were set off simultaneously by time igniters at 9:40 p.m. [1240 GMT] at the homes of the director of the Civil Aviation Bureau in Aso Ward of Kawasaki and the chief of the bureau's engineering department in Totsuka Ward in Yokohama.
