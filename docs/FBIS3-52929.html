Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Manama, 8 Jan (WAKH)--Malcolm Rifkind, British secretary of state for defense, and the accompanying delegation arrived in Manama today on a visit to Bahrain. During the visit, he will hold talks on bolstering relations between the two friendly countries. Rifkind was welcomed on arrival by Staff Lieutenant General Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Ahmad Al Khalifah, Bahraini defense minister and deputy commander in chief of the Bahrain Defense Force; Hugh James Junnec, UK ambassador to Bahrain; and a number of senior Bahraini officers.
