Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS string correspondent Semyon Ivanov] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- Military space forces of Russia plan to launch the first direct television relay space apparatus "Gals" from the "Baykonur" (Kazakhstan) space vehicle launching site today. Satellite "Gals", designed at the scientific production amalgam of applied mechanics (Krasnoyarsk-26 city), is the new generation of tv relay satellites. This launch will pave the way to the creation of a new television satellite system of five similar space apparatuses. The satellite is equipped with a three-mast onboard relay- transmitter, ensuring retransmission of regional television programmes through small dimension 90 cm. diameter individual receiving antennas. The space apparatus will be put into a near geo-stationary orbit of 90 degrees eastern longitude approximately seven hours after the blast off (scheduled for 12:53 hours Moscow time). Later it will be manouvered to a stationary position of 44 degrees eastern longitude, where it will be used for the main purpose it is meant for. "Gals" will be the first automatic satellite to be launched by the military space forces of Russia from the Baykonur cosmodrome in 1994.
