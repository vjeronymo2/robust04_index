930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / Brushing up Chile's image A small US advertising company has been awarded a Dollars 2m public relations account to improve Chile's image, the company said, Reuter reports from Portland, Maine. Holt, Hughes and Stamell said many Americans either did not know where the South American country was or had a bad impression of it because of the 16-year dictatorship of Augusto Pinochet.
