Persons wishing to present statements or obtain information should contact the RTCA Secretariat, 1140 Connecticut Avenue, N.W., Suite 1020, Washington, D.C. 20036; (202) 833&hyph;9339. Any member of the public may present a written statement to the committee at any time. Issued in Washington, D.C., on July 19, 1994. Steve Zaidman, Acting Designated Officer.
