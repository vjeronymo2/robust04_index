930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / International Company News: Qatar National Bank net profit increases 13% QATAR National Bank recorded a 13 per cent increase net profit to 305m riyals (Dollars 87m) for 1992, Reuter reports from Dubai. It said total assets grew by 9.4 per cent to 14.8bn riyals during the year. The bank, which is a 50-50 joint venture between the government and Qatari nationals, said that it planned to back investments in the industrial sector. These would mainly be in developing the country's vast North Field gas reserve, one of the largest in the world. Qatar National Bank also announced plans to increase its paid-up capital to 283.5m riyals from 189m riyals through a one-for-two scrip issue.
