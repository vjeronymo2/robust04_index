Observing the criminal law of Afghanistan and other laws of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, Afghanistan's embassies and diplomatic missions abroad, while looking at the status of Afghan nationals in those countries, shall fulfill their duties for the preparation of ground for the extradition of that kind of person. 5.
