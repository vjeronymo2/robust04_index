BFN [Text] The General Secretariat of the Liberation Front Party has cabled Presidential Council Chairman Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and members of the council, the speaker and members of the House of Representatives Speakership Council, and the Cabinet, to denounce the secession declared by the war criminal and traitor al-Bid and his clique. The message dubbed the move a grievous crime and high treason as well as being in clear violation of the popularly-endorsed constitution. The secession was also described as a brazen deviation from the aims and principles of the Yemeni revolution for which droves of martyrs have fallen. This criminal action, the cable read, is the latest in a series of acts of sabotage, intrigues, and treason typical of the secessionist circle in the Socialist Party.
