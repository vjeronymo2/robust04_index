Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Last weekend Caritas received 500 tonnes of foodstuffs from the European Union. Caritas Sources say that the aid comes at an opportune time and the goods will immediately be distributed to the needy population in the Luanda, Malanje, Menongue, Huambo, Uige, M'banza Congo and Sumbe areas. Meanwhile, Caritas is one of the few humanitarian organizations which distributes donations to all Angolans without political or ethnical discrimination. Other organizations, which claim to be impartial, have been showing that their sole aim is to supply areas controlled by the Luanda regime.
