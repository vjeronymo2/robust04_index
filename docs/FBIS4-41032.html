BFN [Text] On 16 June, Leonid Kuchma, one of the candidates for Ukraine's president, visited Kharkov. At the news conference he pointed out that the Interregional Bloc of Reforms had been a declaratory structure and attempts had failed to form a party on its basis. Speaking about the election of Vitaliy Masol to the post of Ukraine's prime minister, Kuchma voiced his discontent, saying that "in economic terms this was a step back." Leonid Kuchma stated that he was not sure whether he would be able to win the elections and suggested that Leonid Kravchuk might become the winner.
