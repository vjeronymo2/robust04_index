BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today met with Lakhdar Ibrahimi, the UN envoy who is trying to find a solution to the Yemeni crisis. They reviewed the latest developments in the crisis and the efforts being made to implement a cease-fire. The UN envoy briefed the Arab League secretary general on the results of his talks with the Yemeni parties and the Arab leaders he met during his visit. Ibrahimi also briefed him on the results of the talks with the North and South Yemeni delegations in Cairo last night to end the crisis. An Arab League source stated that the meeting took place within the framework of the continuing coordination and cooperation between the Arab League and the United Nations on the Yemeni crisis.
