June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pickens Walks Out: Texas oilman T. Boone Pickens Jr. and 50 other Americans stalked out of the annual shareholders meeting of Koito Manufacturing Co. in Tokyo after the Japanese auto parts company refused to give him a seat on its board or answer his questions.

Pickens, with a 26.4% stake in Koito, sought at the meeting to win approval for seven proposals, including four seats on the 20-member board and an increase in the company dividend. A majority of shareholders voted down all seven items. Pickens is Koito's largest shareholder.
