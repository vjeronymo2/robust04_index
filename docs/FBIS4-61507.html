BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow May 5 TASS -- The Russian Foreign Ministry regards Russian President Boris Yeltsin's forthcoming official visit to the Federal Republic of Germany as a great event in the face of a new stage of relations between Russia and Germany opening with the accomplishment of the Russian troops withdrawal from Germany, Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing here today. According to Grigoriy Karasin, the Russo-German summit talks which will be held on May 11-13, will "focus on the issues connected with the prospects for the further development of bilateral relations and their progressive advancement on the road of strengthening neighborliness, mutual understanding and the establishment of many-sided partnership cooperation". Both leaders will also exchange views on a wide spectrum of problems of a world policy and the interaction between Russia and Germany on the world arena, the Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman pointed out.
