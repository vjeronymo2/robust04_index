DEPARTMENT OF LABOR Office of the Secretary Glass Ceiling Commission; Closed Meeting

SUMMARY: Pursuant to section 10(a) of FACA, this is to announce a meeting of the Glass Ceiling Commission which is to take place on Wednesday, October 5, 1994. The meeting will take place by teleconference. TIME AND PLACE: The closed meeting by teleconference will be held on October 5, 1994, from 1:00 p.m. to 1:30 p.m. (Eastern Standard Time). The Commission will meet in closed session in order to discuss commercial characteristics of applicants for the Frances Perkins-Elizabeth Hanford Dole Award. The closing of this meeting by teleconference is authorized by section 10(d) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act and Section (c)(4) of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. 552b(c)(4)). This closing allows the Commission to discuss matters which if disclosed in an open meeting would reveal information that would not customarily be released to the public by the applicants.
