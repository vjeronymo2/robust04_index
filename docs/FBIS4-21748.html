BFN [Text] At its session on 28 March, the special court of Algiers judicial council passed eight death sentences in absentia and another sentence of eight years imprisonment against an armed group of nine members on charges of forming and belonging to an armed group that aims at infringing on state security, killing, spreading sabotage, and premeditated murder. According to a statement issued by the special judicial prosecutor, the details of the case followed the investigation and interrogation of one member of the group who was arrested. This is Sedrat Rabih, whose involvement in this group was proved. He revealed that he used to collect and coordinate information in the region surrounding Boudouaou, and steal rifles from citizens. He assassinated three policemen in Hey 850 Maskan in Boudouaou.
