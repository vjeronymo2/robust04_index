941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Rolfe & Nolan up Rolfe & Nolan, the futures and options computer bureau and software specialist, reported a 22 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits, having halved its losses in North America. The Pounds 742,000 outcome for the half-year to August 31, compared with Pounds 609,000 last time, was struck on turnover up from Pounds 6.1m to Pounds 7.1m. The fall in the pre-tax figure in the company's European operations from Pounds 1.21m to Pounds 1.04m was after an investment of Pounds 230,000 in Lighthouse, a new global treasury product. Earnings per share came out at 2.9p (3p) with the interim dividend maintained at 1.4025p.
