Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Trofimov] [Text] Ulan-Ude, 18 Feb -- Vladimir Tarmayev, Buryatia's health minister, was relieved from his post by resolution of the Buryatia government. The reason for dismissal was gross violation of legislation and regulations governing expenditure of the budget funds, exposed by a special investigation. In particular, due to lack of control, the ministry has sold off 17 vehicles to various commercial organizations. The damage from unauthorized business trips made by the ex-minister and a number of his subordinates abroad and within Russia is estimated as very serious. At a modest estimate, financial "slips" made by Tarmayev cost the taxpayers more that 20 million rubles. The government of Buryatia has forwarded the evidence of the investigation to the republic's procuracy.
