October 19, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: TEMPLE CITY;

FREE DIAL-A-RIDE TRIPS

Residents will again be able to use the city's Dial-a-Ride program for free this Christmas season.

The 50-cent one-way fare for the program, under which residents can call for door-to-door van service to locations around the city, was waived last December to encourage ridership during the holiday season.

The free service proved so popular that the City Council decided Tuesday to offer it again this year. The fee waiver is allowed under Los Angeles County Transportation Commission guidelines.
