BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has sent a letter to the residents of the Republic of Crimea. It says in part: Please accept my sincere greetings in connection with the glorious date of the 50th anniversary of the liberation of Crimea from the German fascist invaders. The defense and liberation of Crimea is one of the brightest pages in the war, an example of the heroism and courage shown by people defending their native land. We now have to live through a difficult time. The changes our society needs are taking place with great difficulty. But, the latter says, we have to go down the path of change and I hope we will manage to go down it, preserving what people have that is most precious -- accord, mutual understanding, friendly relations between one another.
