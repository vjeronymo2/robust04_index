930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Iran claims US in psychological war Iran vowed yesterday to resist what it termed the west's 'psychological war' launched against it through charges of terrorism and militarism, Reuter reports from Nicosia. The Majlis (parliament) speaker Ali Akbar Nateq-Nouri described a series of recent accusations and condemnations issued mainly by US officials as 'psychological warfare with specific political aims,' Tehran Radio reported.
