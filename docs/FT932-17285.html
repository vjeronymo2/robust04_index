930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: GKN GKN's agritechnical products division has acquired Getriebewerk Kirschau, an agricultural gearbox manufacturing business in Saxony, eastern Germany. GKN Agritechnical has bought the plant, machinery, stocks and leases valued at DM16m (Pounds 6.4m) for a nominal consideration of DM1. In addition, GKN expects over the next five years to invest up to DM17m in restructuring and capital expenditure and to maintain a minimum level of employment at the plant.
