BFN [From the "News" column] [Text] Christian Democratic Movement Chairman Jan Carnogursky yesterday held talks with Karl Lamers, CDU/CSU [Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union] foreign policy spokesman in the German Bundestag, on Slovakia's domestic and foreign political situation. Lamers said, among other things, that a possible victory of Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia in the coming elections would be an obstacle to Slovakia's integration into Europe.
