931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Saddam to free westerners Iraqi President Saddam Hussein yesterday ordered a Frenchman and German to be freed from prison where they were serving eight-year terms for entering the country illegally, Reuter reports from Baghdad. The order to free Mr Jean-Luc Barierre and Mr Kai Sondermann followed visits to Iraq by two French MPs and a senior German politician.
