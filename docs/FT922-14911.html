920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Election 1992: Labour takes lead in Fields' seat A POLL by the Liverpool Echo newspaper last night suggested Labour is well ahead of the Liberal Democrats and Mr Terry Fields, who was expelled by Labour for alleged Militant links, in the city's Broadgreen constituency. Ms Jane Kennedy, the Labour candidate, has 43 per cent support of those who have decided, with Ms Rosemary Cooper, the Lib Dem, on 28 per cent. Mr Fields, Labour Socialist, has 16 per cent.
