April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

In other baseball games:

Saugus 4, Ridgecrest Burroughs 2 -- With the score tied, 2-2, Saugus (9-2-1, 6-0) scored two runs in the sixth when John Albert walked with the bases loaded and Chris Hernandez hit a sacrifice fly in a game at Saugus.

Antelope Valley 6, Canyon 4 -- David Cooke had three hits, scored a run and drove in another as the visiting Antelopes (3-8, 2-4) upset second-place Canyon (6-5, 3-3) in a Golden League game.

Palmdale 9, Quartz Hill 3 -- Jim Carson (4-1) scattered seven hits and walked none, and Jim Ortega had three hits and three runs batted in for host Palmdale (8-3, 4-2) in a Golden League game.

Hart 4, Alhambra 0 -- R. J. Simone's first-inning grand slam held up for first-place Hart (9-1-2, 6-0) in a Foothill League game at Hart.

Junior right-hander Keith Halcovich (2-1) and senior left-hander Andrew Lorraine combined on a one-hitter.

St. John Bosco 18, Alemany 9 -- St. John Bosco scored nine runs on five walks, four hits and two errors in the seventh inning of a Del Rey League game at Alemany. Joey Rosselli drove in four runs with a double and triple for Alemany (7-8, 2-4).

SOFTBALL

Newbury Park 3, Westlake 0 -- Senior Lea Lopez threw a perfect game, striking out 14 of the 21 batters, to lead Newbury Park to a Marmonte League win at Westlake.

It was Lopez's second career no-hitter and first perfect game.
