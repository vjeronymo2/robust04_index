March 25, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

MILO'S 1-HITTER LEADS LAGUNA HILLS TO SWEEP

Rob Milo pitched a one-hitter and had eight strikeouts to lead Laguna Hills High School to a 6-0 victory over San Dimas in the first game of a nonleague baseball doubleheader Friday at Laguna Hills.

In the second game, Milo had a triple and a single with two RBIs as Laguna Hills (9-3) won, 7-2. Milo also was two for two with two RBIs in the first game.

In another nonleague game:

Loara 8, Cabrillo 2 -- Andy Hall was three for four with a home run and two RBIs to lead visiting Loara (5-2).
