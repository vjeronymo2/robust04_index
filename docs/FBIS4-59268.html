BFN [Public opinion poll conducted by the Dahaf Institute under the management of Dr. Mina Tzemah] [Text] Question: After the signing of the agreement on the implementation of autonomy in Gaza and Jericho first, what are the chances for the establishment of peace between Israel and the Palestinians? - very high -- 7 percent - high -- 28 percent - low -- 35 percent - none -- 27 percent - no reply -- 3 percent The poll was conducted on 4 May and covered 566 interviewees who constitute a representative sample of the adult Israeli population. The maximum margin of error is 4 percent.
