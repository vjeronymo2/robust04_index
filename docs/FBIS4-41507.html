BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow June 25 TASS -- The Russian Government has instructed the Ministry of Fuel and Power, together with the government of the Republic of Komi, to work out by the beginning of 1995 a comprehensive programme to restructure coal-industry enterprises in the Pechora coal fields, according to a government resolution made available to ITAR-TASS on Saturday. The programme is expected to suggest solutions to such matters as reconstruction, a rise in the efficiency of promising coal mines, the construction of new ones, the employment of mineworkers thus released, a gradual relocation of the disabled residents of Vorkuta and Inta to other regions of Russia, etc. At the same time the ministry is to substantiate the expediency of the continued operation of four coal mines -- Yun-Yaga, Promyshelennaya, Yur-Shor, and Yuzhnaya by October this year. The government resolution provides for a number of other measures as well.
