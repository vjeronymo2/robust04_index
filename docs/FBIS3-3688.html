Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, March 18 (XINHUA) -- Ecuador President Sixto Duran Ballen arrived here this afternoon by air from Tokyo to start a state visit to China. His entourage includes his wife, foreign minister, finance minister, agriculture and livestock minister, commander general of the army, and some entrepreneurs. On behalf of Shanghai Mayor Huang Ju, Vice-Mayor Xu Kuangdi welcomed the ecuador president. This evening, Sixto Duran Ballen toured the city and the bund.
