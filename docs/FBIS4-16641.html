BFN [Text] Russia's Defense Minister Pavel Grachev supports the idea of uniting the CIS armed forces. He believes that this question may be discussed at the end of the year. He told Interfax that after the creation of joint armed forces one could then talk about the formation of a CIS defense union. "This is something far more significant than the collective security agreement," he said. He noted that the military tackle not only tactical but also strategic problems. "Our strategic future lies in a joint armed forces and a defense union," said Grachev.
