920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Mitie rises 53% and makes purchase Mitie Group, the building services company, reported interim pre-tax profits ahead 53 per cent and is making a further acquisition. It is acquiring 58 per cent of Trident Maintenance Services for Pounds 400,000, satisfied by the issue of 242,000 shares which have been placed at 165p. Mitie is placing a further 248,000 shares at the same price to go towards buying a property in Southampton for Pounds 465,000. The balance of Trident is held by its four directors who will be able to sell their interests to Mitie at specified times up to October 31 1999. The profit-related consideration would result in a maximum total price of Pounds 2.5m. Pre-tax profits for the six months to September 30 were Pounds 726,000 (Pounds 474,000) on turnover up 28 per cent at Pounds 22.2m (Pounds 17.3m). Earnings per share were 5.1p (4.9p). The interim dividend is raised to 1.25p (1p).
