930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Davenport Knitwear Davenport Knitwear, the Birmingham-based maker of knitted fabrics and garments, improved pre-tax profits by 18 per cent from Pounds 1.23m to Pounds 1.45m in 1992. Turnover advanced to Pounds 8.65m (Pounds 7.54m). Earnings per share amounted to 56.72p (47.66p) and the proposed single dividend is lifted from 8.25p to 8.525p.
