920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Readymix tumbles 14% to IPounds 1.5m Intense price competition in a stagnant market was blamed by Readymix, the Dublin-based concrete and building materials group, for a 14 per cent decline in pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30. The result, down from IPounds 1.76m to IPounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.42m), came from turnover of IPounds 15.7m (IPounds 15.3m). Earnings fell to 3.35p (3.95p) but the interim dividend is held at 0.55p.
