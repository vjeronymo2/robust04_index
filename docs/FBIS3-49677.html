Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Novi Sad, 13 Jan (TANJUG) -- Vojvodina farmers failed to take advantage of the exceptionally favorable weather conditions, not recorded for a long time, to deep-plough almost 1 million hectares of land in preparation for spring sowing. The main reason is the lack of fuel which the farmers expected to receive immediately after the autumn harvest. The province's Secretariat for Agriculture, the Vojvodina Chamber of Economy, and the Vojvodina Cooperative Union note that both the federal and the republic's government have, on several occasions, been asked to secure urgent diesel fuel deliveries but with to avail.
