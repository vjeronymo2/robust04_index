931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / International Company News: Tuskar Resources Tuskar Resources, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration company, cut its pre-tax loss from IPounds 30.8m to IPounds 2.2m (Pounds 2.07m) in the year to March 31. During the year the company said it had undergone a transformation. It also avoided being taken over by Aminex. It sold its interest in Coplex Agreements, incurring a cost of IPounds 1.04m and there were reorganisation costs of IPounds 549,000. In the previous year there was a loss on the sale of its Colombian interests of IPounds 27m.
