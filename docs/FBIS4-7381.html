BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- A symposium of trade union officials was held here Wednesday on the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the publication of "On further improving the work of the trade unions", a famous work of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. Comrade Kim Chong-il in the work says that the trade unions are mass political organizations of the working class and dependable assistants and defenders of the party and gives answers to all the theoretical and practical questions arising in the building of the trade unions and their activities. He defines it as the basic duty of the trade unions to educate the trade union members and rally them closely around the party and powerfully mobilize them in the revolutionary struggle and constructive work. He sets it as the most important tasks of the trade unions to energetically carry on the ideological, technical and cultural revolutions and vigorously rouse the working class and trade union members in socialist economic construction. Speakers said that the work of the dear leader was an immortal banner which the trade unions must tightly hold in accomplishing the revolutionary cause of chuche and a highly important guideline which clearly indicates the main orientation and target of the trade union activities.
