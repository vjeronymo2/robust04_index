931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: IAWS expands 28% to IPounds 10m Growth throughout its expanding agribusiness and food operations helped Dublin-based IAWS Group lift pre-tax profits by 28 per cent over the 12 months to July 31. Helped also by reduced interest charges, profits emerged at IPounds 10.1m (Pounds 9.57m), up from a restated IPounds 7.85m, on turnover from continuing operations of IPounds 402m (IPounds 399m). Strong operating cash flow helped IAWS reduce borrowings to IPounds 9.5m at the year-end. Adjusted earnings were 7.7p (6.5p); a final dividend of 1.1p brings the total to 2.1p (2p).
