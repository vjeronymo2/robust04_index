May 18, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

Fifth graders from throughout Los Angeles County will be at the Music Center today and Friday for the 19th annual Blue Ribbon Children's Festival. They'll see the Joffrey Ballet perform "Billy the Kid." More youngsters will attend performances next week, with about 26,000 expected in all. Two performances for another 6,000 students have been canceled because of the Los Angeles teachers strike. ALEENE MacMINN
