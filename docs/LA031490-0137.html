March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFLY

Trump Shuttle to Offer Deal for Late Landings: Trump Shuttle passengers arriving more than 15 minutes late at Boston, New York and Washington airports now will be compensated even if the weather is to blame for the delay, the airline said. Starting today, late arrivals will be offered a choice of a 5,000-mile credit to Trump's frequent flyer program, a free companion ticket for their next weekend Trump shuttle flight or a $20 American express restaurant ticket.
