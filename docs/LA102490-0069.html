October 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Left-hander Ted Higuera of the Milwaukee Brewers and nine other players filed for free agency, raising the total to 31 in the first three days since the World Series.

Also filing were Atlanta Brave infielder Jim Presley, Boston Red Sox outfielder Tom Brunansky, Detroit Tiger pitcher Dan Petry, Kansas City Royal pitcher Steve Farr, Dodger second baseman-outfielder Juan Samuel, Minnesota Twin outfielder John Moses, Pittsburgh Pirate outfielder R.J. Reynolds and St. Louis Cardinal third baseman Terry Pendleton and pitcher John Tudor.
