October 25, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

SWEDEN LEADS IN AMATEUR GOLF

mer soldier Mathias Gronberg shot a 2-under-par 70 today as Sweden took a seven-stroke lead after the first round of the world amateur team golf championship for the Eisenhower Trophy.

Gronberg was supported by the teen-age duo of Klas Eriksson and Gabriel Hjertstedt. Eriksson, 19, carded a 72 and Hjertstedt, 18, had a 73 as Sweden compiled a team total of 215.

New Zealand, South Korea and Argentina were tied for second at 222.

Defending champion Britain and nine-time champion the United States both slumped.

The United States finished 12 strokes back in seventh place.
