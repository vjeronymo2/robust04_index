BFN [Text] Beijing has lent its support to Hong Kong manufacturers who are concerned about quota restrictions imposed by the European Union on toys made in China, according to officials of local trade organisations. A delegation led by Dennis Ting, chairman of the Hong Kong Toy Council and the Trade Development Council's toy committee, received positive feedback from the Chinese government during a recent visit, according to sources in the Federation of Hong Kong Industries. "The delegation has been talking to senior Chinese officials including Gu Yongjiang, vice-minister of the Ministry of Economic and Foreign Trade," the source said. "They have been guaranteed by Mr Gu that support will be given by Beijing."
