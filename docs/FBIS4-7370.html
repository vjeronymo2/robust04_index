BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 27 (KCNA) -- Bulletins were brought out by the Algeria-Korea Friendship Club on April 12 and by the Burundi-Korea Friendship Association on April 13 in celebration of April 15. Carried in the bulletin of the Algeria-Korea Friendship Club are articles titled "President Kim Il-song Who Has Been Devoting His Whole Life for the People," "`10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country' Put Forward by President Kim Il-song" and "Signature Campaign Supporting Peace and Reunification of Korea". The bulletin published by the Burundi-Korea Friendship Association came off the press under the title "Best Wishes for Longevity to President Kim Il-song". The Venezuelan Committee for the Reunification of Korea published the April issue of its bulletin. Conveyed in the bulletin is news that political figures and organisations of India and Zimbabwe called for grandly celebrating April 15.
