Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ante Djapic, vice chairman of and spokesman for the Croatian Party of Rights [HSP], commented at a news conference in Rijeka today on the decision to open an office of the Republic of Croatia in Belgrade and an office of the Federal Republic in Zagreb. He said that the HSP, the Croatian Pure Party of Rights, and the Croatian National Democratic League are demanding the urgent convening of a Croatian Assembly session at which the president of the republic and the foreign minister should reveal all the circumstances under which the agreement with the FRY was signed. They are also calling for the rejection of all agreements with the FRY until the Serb occupation army withdraws from the territory of the Republic of Croatia and the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, until all expelled persons are allowed to return to their homes, and until the FRY explicitly recognizes the Republic of Croatia and the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina and their internationally recognized borders. Djapic also disclosed that the merger of the three parties into the Croatian Party of Rights can be expected in the near future.
