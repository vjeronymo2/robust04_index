Regulatory Flexibility Act The DOI certifies that this rule will not have a significant economic effect on a substantial number of small entities under the Regulatory Flexibility Act, 5 U.S.C. 601 et seq. The rule does not distinguish between small and large entities. This determination is based on the findings that the regulatory changes contained in this rule would serve to reduce the costs incurred by OSM and State regulatory authorities in making routine inspections of abandoned sites. Therefore, the rule will not add to the cost of operating a mine under an approved regulatory program.
