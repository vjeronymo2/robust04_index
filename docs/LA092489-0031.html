September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ZSA ZSA'S TRIAL

What's all the uproar over Zsa Zsa Gabor's trial? Get a clue, folks. Do you really think she deserves the credit for making a mockery of our legal system? In America everyone is entitled to a trial by jury. With legal fees at $400 an hour (Part I, Sept. 18), this is a system only the rich can afford. So what's the fuss? We are supporting this show, and I for one am delighted to be getting something back for my entertainment dollar.

MARY SCHWARTZ

Santa Ana
