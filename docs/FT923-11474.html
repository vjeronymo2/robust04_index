920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Kinnock EC post speculation An unscheduled lunch yesterday between Neil Kinnock and John Major has raised speculation that the former Labour party leader may be offered the post of second British European commissioner when a vacancy arises at the end of this year. By convention, nominations for the post are in the gift of the leader of the opposition, but the ultimate decision remains with the prime minister.
