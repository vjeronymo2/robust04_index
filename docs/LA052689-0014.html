May 26, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SANTEE

A 28-year-old Santee man died Wednesday evening after the car he was driving crossed the center line in the 9200 block of Winter Gardens Boulevard into oncoming southbound traffic, the coroner's office said.

Marvin Barnett was northbound when he lost control of his vehicle and spun out, the office said.

Barnett was ejected from the car and suffered massive head injuries. He was pronounced dead at the scene.
