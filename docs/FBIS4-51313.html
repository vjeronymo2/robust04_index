BFN [Text] Shenzhen, May 24 (XINHUA) -- President Pascal Lissouba of Congo is scheduled to leave here tonight for home, after a six- day state visit to China. Mayor Li Youwei of Shenzhen yesterday met and hosted a banquet in honor of Lissouba and his party, who arrived yesterday afternoon for a tour of this special economic zone. Lissouba, noting that Congo is rich in natural resources, invited Shenzhen's enterprises to invest in his country.
