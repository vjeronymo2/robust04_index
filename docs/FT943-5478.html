940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmegs and mace; Local Far East prices have increased considerably in last few weeks, reports Man Producten. New shipments of Grenada nutmegs arrived in Rotterdam and quality is extremely good. White pepper: muntok is a shade easier this week, Malaysia remained steady. The availability of China white pepper appears to be diminishing. Spot positions in Europe/USA are tight. Muntok faq spot Rotterdam usDollars 3200/mt, Sep/Oct shipment usDollars 3075/mt cif. Black pepper: market steady to firm. Indonesia practically withdrawn with only high quotes. New crop pepper seems disappointing. Brazil, closely watching Indonesia, maintains higher price levels. India only offering limited quantities as local supply remains tight. Black faq: usDollars 2000 spot Europe usDollars 1975 cif Sep/Oct shipment black asta: usDollars 2250 spot, usDollars 2200 cif Sep/Oct shipment.
