BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 25 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- The Cosmos-2278 artificial Earth satellite launched yesterday by the Russian military-space forces from the Baykonur cosmodrome has been placed in an orbit with the following parameters: period of revolution: 102.0 minutes, orbital inclination: 70.99 degrees, apogee: 880.78 km, perigee: 851.95 km. The satellite was launched by a Zenit-2 rocket-launcher. ITAR-TASS was told at the Russian military space forces that Cosmos-2278 is the 1,075th satellite launched from Baykonur in the 37 years of its history.
