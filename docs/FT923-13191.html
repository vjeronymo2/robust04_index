920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Lloyds Chemists to buy further 87 outlets Lloyds Chemists, the acquisitive retailer which in March took control of rival Macarthy, is buying up to 87 sites from the receivers of Cavendish and Castle, the greetings card retailing chain. Financial details of the purchase have not been released but the price is understood to be less than Pounds 4m. The sites, presently tobacco and greetings card outlets, are located throughout the UK and are mainly held on short term leases. They will be converted into outlets for Lloyds' Holland & Barratt healthfood chain and Supersave Drugstores.
