940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Wilshaw ahead 53% to Pounds 3.25m Wilshaw, the specialist metals and building products group, reported pre-tax profits up by 53 per cent from Pounds 2.13m to Pounds 3.25m on turnover ahead some Pounds 5m to Pounds 36.6m for the year to end-March. Earnings came out at 2.4p (1.6p) per share and a final dividend of 0.4p makes 0.6p (0.5p) for the year.
