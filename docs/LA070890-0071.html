July 8, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

COURTESY TITLE TRENDS

There is a sensible trend in the use of titles for addressing men and women that should satisfy Lynn Spear Merles (Letters, June 21).

Men have long been addressed as Mr. whether married or single. Addressing all women as Miss (written Ms. but not pronounced "Mizz") is being used more and more often. Many women do not want to be called Mrs., and it should not make any more difference than when addressing men as Mr. W. POWER

Costa Mesa
