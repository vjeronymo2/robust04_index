920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / New plan for HK airport China has put forward new plans to resolve the conflict over financing of Hong Kong's new airport, Simon Davies reports from Hong Kong. The Chinese are recommending part of the estimated HKDollars 21bn (Pounds 1.36bn) proceeds from the sale of land along the airport rail link be used to fund the project. It is also believed to suggest the project should be separated from the accounts of the existing Mass Transit Railway Corporation.
