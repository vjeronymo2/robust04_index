BFN [By Liu Zhenying (0491 2182 5391), Central People's Broadcasting Network reporter; RENMIN RIBAO reporter Chen Weiwei (7115 4850 0251), and XINHUA reporter Zou Aiguo (6760 1947 0948)] [Excerpts] Beijing, 12 May (XINHUA) -- The 10th national conference on civic affairs, which has been convened by the State Council, opened at Huairentang in Zhongnanhai this morning. On behalf of the party Central Committee and the State Council, Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee and state president, extended his cordial regards and high respects to representatives attending the current conference, advanced units of civic affairs work, advanced individuals, and the broad masses of cadres and workers who have been working very hard on the civic affairs front. Prior to the opening of the conference, Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Zhu Rongji, Hu Jintao, Wen Jiabao, Ren Jianxin, Chi Haotian, Li Guixian, Luo Gan, and other leading comrades met with all representatives attending the conference and had a group photograph taken. The main tasks of the current conference are: Under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the party's basic line, the conference should implement the guidelines of the 14th CPC National Congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee; sum up work experiences, analyze the situation; and study and decide the guiding ideology, strategic goals, and working tasks for civic affairs work in the next five years and until the end of this century in order to better suit the requirements for establishing a socialist market economy; serve the reform, development, and stability of our country; and make due contributions to attaining the second-step strategic objective by the end of this century. Jiang Zemin pointed out in his speech: Civic affairs work is very important. Comrade Chen Yi appropriately summed up civic affairs work as "helping the party Central Committee overcome problems and as solving problems for the people." Jiang Zemin said that for decades, comrades on the civic affairs front have displayed the spirit of "being like an ox," have served the people wholeheartedly, and have worked hard and not been upset by criticism. They have struggled in unity and have done a lot of arduous and meticulous work in showing concern for and solving problems for the masses and alleviating social problems. They have made great achievements and have made important contributions to ensuring state and social stability, promoting reform and opening up, and developing the national economy. The party and government are grateful to you, and the people are also grateful to you. Jiang Zemin said that the party and the people are placing great hopes on the comrades on the civic affairs front. It is hoped that you will continue to make progress with a pioneering spirit, seriously implement the party's basic line and the principle put forward by the party Central Committee this year on "seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, opening up wider, promoting development, and maintaining stability"; strive to do a good job in civic affairs work, and make new contributions to the cause of building socialism with Chinese characteristics. [passage omitted] Today's conference was presided over by State Councillor Li Guixian. Entrusted by the State Council, Minister of Civic Affairs Doje Cering gave a report to the conference entitled: "Speed Up the Reform and Development of Civic Affairs Work, Protect People's Basic Living Rights and Interests, Maintain Social Stability, and Make Contributions To Attaining the Second-Step Strategic Objective." [passage omitted] More than 400 people attended the conference, including responsible persons in charge of civic affairs work in various provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities; representatives of civic affairs departments at various levels; representatives of outstanding townships; representatives of advanced workers on the civic affairs front; and responsible persons from the departments concerned of the party Central Committee and the State Council.
