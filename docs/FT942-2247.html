940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Gaviria casts vote in Colombian election President Cesar Gaviria - barred by the constitution from seeking re-election - was one of the first to vote yesterday in the final round of Colombia's presidential elections after a dead heat in the first round. Opinion polls put Liberals and Conservatives neck and neck. Polling was subdued after Colombia's defeat by Romania in the World Cup on Saturday night. Report, Page 5
