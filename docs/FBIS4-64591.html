BFN [Text] Prime Minister Gonzalez has accepted the resignations of Asuncion and Albero, ministers of the interior and of agriculture. Albero's resignation was presented this evening. Gonzalez will appear at 1000 tomorrow before the press at the Moncloa Palace. He will explain the two resignations. It is probable that the names of the new ministers of the interior and agriculture will be made public tomorrow. If all goes as planned, the new ministers will be sworn in by the king before the Council of Ministers begins on Friday [6 May].
