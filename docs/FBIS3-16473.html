Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Richard Nixon: There Is No Alternative To Yeltsin Today"] [Text] There is no alternative to President Boris Yeltsin in Russia right now. This was stated to journalists by Richard Nixon after his speech at the State Duma committee for international affairs. "I held meetings with a number of leaders and concluded that right now there is no one who would be ready to take his place," the U.S. ex-president stressed. "They will appear but for the moment there are no people like that," ITAR-TASS reports. Speaking of his meetings with Grigoriy Yavlinskiy and Sergey Shakhray, Richard Nixon noted: "They are both young, vigorous, and highly educated. I think they can be viewed as potential leaders. But I would not rule out others with whom I met. In particular I was impressed by the Communists' leader Gennadiy Zyuganov. But he will not become president since Russia does not intend to return to communism."
