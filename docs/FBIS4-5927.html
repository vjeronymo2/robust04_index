BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, arranged a grand banquet at the Kumsusan Assembly Hall today for the visiting high-level delegations of different countries and delegations of overseas Koreans. When President Kim Il-song accompanied by heads of foreign high- level delegations appeared in the banquet hall amid the playing of the welcome music, the participants warmly welcomed him with loud applause. Invited to the banquet were the delegations of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana, the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan and the Workers' Party of Ireland and other high-level delegations and personages from different countries. O Chin u, member of the presidium of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and minister of People's Armed Forces; Kang Song-san, Politburo member of the WPK Central Committee and premier of the Administration Council; Vice Presidents Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-chu, Politburo members of the WPK Central Committee; Vice President Kim Pyong-sik, chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Social Democratic Party; and other senior party and state officials were present at the banquet. Also present were the congratulatory group of Koreans in Japan for celebrating April 15 led by Yi Chin-gyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean residents in Japan (Chongnyon), the delegation of old cadres of Chongnyon led by Paek Chong-won, chairman of the Sports Federation of Koreans in Japan, and other delegations of Chongnyon, Choe Hong- hui, president of the International Taekwon-do Federation, and congratulatory groups and overseas Koreans from different regions and organisations. Premier Kang Song-san made a speech at the banquet. Authorized by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, he warmly welcomed on behalf of the WPK Central Committee and the DPRK government the delegations of foreign parties, friendship envoys and congratulatory groups and overseas Koreans from different regions and organisations, who came to Korea to share joy with the Korean people on the occasion of the Korean people's auspicious holiday of April. He said the respected leader Comrade Kim Il-song has victoriously led the Korean revolution with outstanding and tested leadership and tireless devoted efforts for a long time since he embarked on the road of revolution and performed immortal exploits for the country and the revolution, the times and history. "Thanks to Comrade Kim Il-song's outstanding and tested leadership, the Korean people are powerfully demonstrating the advantages of Korean-style socialism centred on the masses, effecting a constant upswing in socialist construction and valiantly advancing toward the denuclearization of the Korean peninsula and the independent and peaceful reunification of the country without the slightest vacillation under the complex and difficult conditions created by the continued challenges and obstructions of the imperialists and reactionaries," said the premier. "Our party and the DPRK Government," he stressed, "will invariably and consistently implement the foreign policy of independence, peace and friendship and actively contribute to peace and progress of mankind and the cause of global independence." General secretary of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana Huudu Yahaya spoke next. He said President Kim Il-song founded the immortal chuche idea and indicated the road ahead of the popular masses and that this idea is shining and encouraging all the progressive people of the world beyond the boundary of Korea. "As regards the question of national reunification, the supreme desire of the Korean people," he said, "we support the confederation formula based on one nation, one state, two systems and two governments put forward by President Kim Il-song." Yi Chin-gyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon, in his speech said the greatest blessing in the world is to be guided by a great leader. "The overseas Koreans will share the destiny with the people in the homeland to defend and exalt the dignified socialist motherland and make a tangible contribution to accomplishing the noble cause of reunification, closely united in one mind and purpose under the banner of the ten-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation," he stressed. The banquet proceeded in a cordial atmosphere overflowing with friendly feelings.
