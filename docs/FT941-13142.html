940127 FT 27 JAN 94 / Talks continue on Mirror claim Talks aimed at reaching an out-of-court settlement over the Pounds 175m legal claim by Mirror Group Newspaper pension fund trustees against three securities houses continued yesterday, John Mason writes. The trustees are seeking the return of assets and damages from Invesco MIM, Capel-Cure Myers Management and Lehman Brothers International over their handling of pension fund assets controlled by the late Mr Robert Maxwell. The High Court trial due to start yesterday was postponed for a second time to give lawyers more time to reach agreement. The hearing was adjourned to Monday.
