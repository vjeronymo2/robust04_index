December 24, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

7 W. GERMAN SOLDIERS KILLED IN CAR CRASH

Seven young West German army reservists celebrating the end of their military service were killed when the overcrowded car in which they were traveling smashed into a truck, Munich police said Friday.

The men, all in their early 20s, died when their car swerved out of control on the Salzburg-to-Munich highway during the night and hit a Romanian truck.

The driver of the truck was uninjured.
