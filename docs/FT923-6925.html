920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / Gorbachev may be called in Honecker trial DEFENCE lawyers for Mr Erich Honecker, the former East German leader, said yesterday that they might call Chancellor Helmut Kohl and the former Soviet president Mikhail Gorbachev to testify when Mr Honecker goes on trial for manslaughter, Reuter reports from Berlin. Mr Honecker is charged with causing the death of East Germans killed trying to flee across the Berlin Wall
