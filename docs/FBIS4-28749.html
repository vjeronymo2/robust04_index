BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 13 (KPL) -- A delegation of the Ministry of Labour and Social Welfare of the Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] led by its minister, Thongloun Sisoulit left here on June 11 for Geneva, Switzerland, to attend the annual conference of the International Labour Organization to be held there from June 7-24. At this conference, the Council of Labour from each member country will make a report on the labour situation of their countries. The conference will also discuss many issues concerning labour, management and use of labour and labour welfare, as well as the protection of the just right of the working people.
