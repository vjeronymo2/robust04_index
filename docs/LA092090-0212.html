September 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

OFTEN MY LOVE: One of the most-performed songs in history is "Never My Love," made a hit in 1967 by the Association, whose members, brothers Larry and Del Ramos, still live in Orange County. . . . According to music giant BMI, the song's been performed 5 million times. "We did 192 dates last year, and we have to do that tune," says Del. "That's what they come to hear from us." Zan Dubin and Lucy Chabot
