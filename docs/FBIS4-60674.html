BFN [Text] There have been some 100 attacks and attempted attacks, in which four Israelis have been killed and 13 have been wounded, since the signing of the Gaza-Jericho agreement. There have been no attacks inside the Green Line, security sources told correspondents. They said that immediately after the signing of the agreement there was a sharp increase in attempted attacks with live ammunition against IDF [Israel Defense Forces] installations set up in the Qatif Bloc and against IDF patrols. The sources stressed that some 200 wanted men, mostly Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] and Islamic Jihad members, remained at large in the territories.
