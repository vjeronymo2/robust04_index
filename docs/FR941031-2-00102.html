Alternative Use of Resources The principal result of this action does involve the use of resources beyond the scope anticipated in the Environmental Impact Statement issued August 1973, for normal plant operations; however, this additional use of land is not significant, as the area involved is located underneath the upper parking lot. This action involves no other critical materials or resources. Agenices and Persons Consulted The staff consulted with the State of Michigan regarding the environmental impact of the proposed action. The State had no comments. Finding of No Significant Impact The Commission has determined not to prepare an environmental impact statement for the proposed action. Based upon the foregoing environmental assessment, the staff concludes that the proposed action will not have a significant effect on the quality of the human environment.
