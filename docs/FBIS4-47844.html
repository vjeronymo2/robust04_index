BFN [Text] We have just heard that the first session of the National Assembly will only take place next Monday [9 May] in Cape Town and not, as initially planned, on the 6 May. The Transitional Executive Council's management committee has decided that the first meeting of the provincial legislature will be held on Saturday, 7 May, at various meeting points. This decision by the management committee was taken on the basis of information received by the Independent Electoral Commission. The change of date will not affect the inauguration ceremony for the state president at the Union Buildings in Pretoria on Tuesday, 10 May.
