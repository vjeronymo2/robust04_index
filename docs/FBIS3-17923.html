Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow March 30 TASS -- According to the specified, but still preliminary information, 38 per cent of the electorate took part in the Sunday elections to the representative bodies of power in subjects of the federation, a spokesman for the department for work with the territories at the president's administration told ITAR-TASS. Elections can be regarded as valid, if 25 per cent of electors took part in them, but still the activity of the population can hardly be described as high, although it is a little higher than during the previous stages of the electoral campaign. In eight regions the voter turnout was more than 40 per cent. The figure was 63 per cent in the Ust-Ordynsky Autonomous District of Buryatia, which was the peak of activity. The population of two other autonomous districts also showed high activity: The representative bodies of power were fully formed in the Taymyr Peninsula and in the Evenk Autonomous District. Activity was the lowest in the Samara and Magadan regions -- 23 and 27 per cent respectively. Local dumas were not formed there.
