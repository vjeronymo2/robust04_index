Record access procedures: Contact the addressee under notification procedures and specify the exact information you desire. Requests must include the full name and social security number of the individual concerned. Prior written notification of personal visits is required to ensure that the records will be available at the time of visit. Photographic proof of identity will be required prior to release of records. A military identification card, driver's license or similar document will be considered suitable identification. Contesting record procedures: Contact the addressee under notification procedures and specify the exact information or items you are contesting and provide any documentation that justifies your claim.
