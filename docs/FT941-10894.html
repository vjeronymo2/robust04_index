940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Italy's biggest privatisation - Other companies It will be a busy week for Banca Commerciale Italiana, Italy's fifth biggest bank, and the latest in the privatisation line-up. Observers hope the Consob stock market watchdog will approve the listing prospectus today, allowing the bank to call a press conference on Friday to outline the deal. Floating the Iri state holding company's 57 per cent stake in BCI promises to be Italy's biggest privatisation, raising more than L2,500bn (Dollars 1.5bn).
