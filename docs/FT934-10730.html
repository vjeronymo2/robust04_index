931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / World Trade News: Renault in link with Czech group French motor manufacturer Renault is entering a joint venture with Karosa, the Czech maker of buses and fire engines, Patrick Blum reports from Vienna. Renault will invest about FFr200m (Dollars 35m) in the venture - based at Karosa's main factory at Vysoke Myto, eastern Bohemia - over the next five years. Its initial 34 per cent stake will rise to 51 per cent.
