BFN [Text] Colombo, June 20 (AFP) -- Sri Lanka President Dingiri Banda Wijetunga on Monday [20 June] reinstated a senior minister sacked after his refusal to accept a new portfolio in a mini reshuffle, official said. Ruling party stalwart Alick Aluvihare was sworn in as minister of Public Administration, the job he refused Friday after his Shipping and Ports ministry was given to a newcomer. Officials said Wijetunga's United National Party (UNP) had been concerned over opposition moves to woo Aluvihare ahead of presidential elections due later this year.
