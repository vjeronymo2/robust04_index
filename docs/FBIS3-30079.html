Language: English Article Type:BFN ["`Theses on Socialist Rural Question in Our Country Is Great Programme for Rural Construction'" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The world public is unanimously praising the "Theses on the Socialist Rural Question in Our Country" published by the great leader President Kim Il-song as a great programme for the socialist rural construction which gives a perfect scientific exposition of all the theoretical and practical questions arising in the solution of the rural question for the first time in human history. Aubrey G. Thomas, chairman of the group for the study of the chuche idea of Comrade Kim Il-song in Goedverwagting, Guyana, said that the ideas expounded by respected President Kim Il-song in the rural theses were the unique idea elucidating a clear vista of solving the rural question and great idea indicating the right way of completely liberating the farmers freed from exploitation and oppression even from the fetters of old ideas and nature. The Egyptian paper AKHER SA'A wrote that President Kim Il-song created the chuche method of farming after his earnest study while personally raising crops in the garden for the settlement of the rural question and applied it to farming. The Central African news agency said that the development of agriculture in Korea was made entirely by the wise guidance of the respected leader President Kim Il-song, adding that Korea has firmly laid the powerful material and technical foundations which make it possible to endlessly increase agricultural production, unaffected in any unfavourable condition. The Syrian paper NIDAL PALYAHIN said that the farmers of Korea are now leading a rich and cultural life; like in towns. The Zambian paper ZAMBIA DAILY MAIL in an article stressed that the countryside of Korea built by the great President Kim Il-song reminds us of paradise or utopia pictured by ancient people to themselves.
