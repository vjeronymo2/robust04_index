940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Radamec drops to Pounds 710,000 Radamec Group, the electronics and precision mechanical engineering concern, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 832,000 to Pounds 710,000 in 1993, on unchanged turnover of Pounds 11.9m. Attributable earnings dropped from Pounds 841,000 to Pounds 655,000, equivalent to 3.6p (4.6p) per share. The final dividend is 1.2p for a total of 1.7p (1.5p). Gearing rose to 24 per cent (20 per cent) on net borrowings of Pounds 1.24m (Pounds 967,000).
