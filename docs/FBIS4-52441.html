BFN [Text] The central report meeting marking the 20th anniversary of the publication of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's work on 7 May 1974 was held at the People's Palace of Culture yesterday. Hung in front of the meeting hall were the portraits of the great leader of our party and our people Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. Also hung were the slogans reading "Long Live the Great Leader Comrade Kim Il-song!" and "Long Live the Glorious Workers Party of Korea [WPK]!" The report meeting was participated in by Comrade Kim Ki-nam, secretary of the Central Committee of the WPK, responsible functionaries in the press and publications sector, reporters, and journalists. Hyun Chun-kuk, responsible editor-in-chief of the NODONG SINMUN who is the chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists' Union, made a commemorative report at the report meeting. The rapporteur said: While seeing through the new demands for the building of our party's press and publications materials, the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il issued the historic work on 7 May 1994, thereby developing our press and publications materials as the model of a new-type chuche-oriented press and publications materials. He noted: In the historic work, the dear comrade leader illuminated in a scientific way the nature, mission, principles for action, and basic duties of our party's press and publications materials, thus putting forward the policy as to the revolutions in newspapers, press, and publications. Because of this, a cornerstone for ages on which our press and publications materials can contribute in a genuine fashion to the implementation of the revolutionary cause of chuche, has been laid, and a revolutionary turn has been effected in the work of press and publications. He continued full of pride: Over the past 20 years, our reporters and journalists have traversed the path to victory and glory along the road illuminated by the historic 7 May 1974 work of the dear comrade leader. And, a great golden age has been unfolded in the whole work of press and publications. He emphasized: The proud path along which a new history has been unfurled in the building of our era's press and publications is brilliantly embroidered with the shining achievements of the leadership of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, the great master of journalism and the genius of revolution. He then expressed the greatest honor and the warmest thanks with the heart of warm admiration and boundless loyalty to the dear Comrade Kim Chong-il, the great leader [yongdoja] and teacher of our party and people, who has strengthened and developed our party's press and publications materials as authoritative chuche-oriented press and publications materials, and has opened the bright path of socialist journalism. The rapporteur said: The imperialists and reactionaries are now viciously maneuvering, more than ever before, to crush [apsal] our-type socialism which is marching forward victoriously upholding the revolutionary banner. He said that our reporters and journalists, standing on the foremost frontline of ideology, should more wonderfully fulfill their duty and play their role, wielding their revolutionary pen. He touched on the way to successfully carry out the tasks facing the press and publications sector. He stressed the need to more vigorously kindle the flames of the revolutions in newspapers, press, and publications. He said: To effect a new revolutionary turn in the work of press and publications, reporters and journalists should firmly prepare themselves as warriors of writing, who are thoroughgoing in their revolutionary view of the (?leader), and all-round reporters and all-round journalists. The rapporteur stressed: In single-hearted unity around the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, all should vigorously struggle to achieve a new upsurge in the work of press and publications and accomplish the socialist cause of chuche, overflowing with the conviction of sure victory and revolutionary optimism, while highly upholding the program for making the whole society chuche-oriented.
