May 5, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: VENTURA;

$10,000 DUMP TRUCK DESTROYED IN FIRE

A Ventura city dump truck worth about $10,000 was destroyed after catching fire in the city garage Friday, authorities said.

A sprinkler system in the City Maintenance Garage at Thompson Boulevard and Sanjon Road put out the fire after it started about 4:30 a.m., preventing significant damage to the building, Ventura Police Officer Diane McCaskill said.

Smoke caused $500 worth of damage to the building, McCaskill said.

The fire was caused by an electrical short under the dashboard of the truck, which was being repaired, McCaskill said. CAROL WATSON
