930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies join the fun - At a glance With the FT-SE 100 Index continuing to reach new highs, smaller company shares are joining in the euphoria. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 0.8 per cent from 1577.03 to 1590.21 over the seven days to August 26.
