BFN [By Amjad al-'Umari and Zaki Abu-al-Halawah] [Text] Cairo -- Informed Egyptian sources have said that a security agreement on cooperation and coordination between Egypt and the PLO will be signed in Cairo within days. The sources said that Hasan al-Alfi, the Egyptian interior minister, and Amin al-Hindi, head of the Palestinian intelligence service, will sign an agreement to enhance cooperation in security and the exchange of information. Moreover, Egypt is to provide training and technical assistance to the Palestinian police force that will be transferred to the Gaza Strip via Egypt. According to the same sources, al-Hindi presented the draft agreement to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat last week for approval. Senior Egyptian and Palestinian security officials are currently holding joint meetings to discuss security cooperation during the coming period.
