930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / UK Company News: M Grenfell Equity Net asset value per ordinary share of the Morgan Grenfell Equity Income Trust stood at 122.8p at March 31 1993. That compared with 89.7p a year earlier and with 94.1p at end-September 1992. Available revenue for the half year to end-March totalled Pounds 541,000 (Pounds 323,000), equal to earnings of 2.22p (1.32p). The interim dividend is being doubled to 2p.
