January 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

18-INCH PYTHON FOUND AFTER 4 DAYS IN MALL

An 18-inch python that roamed a Salem shopping center for nearly four days was back in its cage Tuesday.

Pet store owner Helen Floyd said the 5-month-old snake was found in a hallway Monday near her shop. She said the snake was harmless, but its presence caused concern among some shoppers.

The man who spotted the snake received a $75 reward from Floyd and her husband, Dennis.
