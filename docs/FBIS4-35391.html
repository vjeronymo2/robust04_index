BFN [Text] Doha, 31 May (WAKH) -- Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, arrived in Doha this morning on a short visit to Qatar within the framework of his current tour in the region. He was received on arrival at Doha International Airport by 'Abd-al-Rahman Sa'd al-Dirham, Qatari minister of Social Affairs and Housing.
