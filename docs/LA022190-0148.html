February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Orange County Crazies improvisational comedy troupe will audition additional cast members on March 4.

The group, which had its debut in January with a revue called "This Is Orange County," is looking for performers at least 18 years old with some degree of experience in comedy. The new cast members will be part of the troupe's next revue, "Behind the Orange Curtain," which opens April 28. Information: (714) 840-1406.
