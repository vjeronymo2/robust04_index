940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fairey sells surplus land for Pounds 2.8m Fairey Group has sold surplus land next to its manufacturing facilities at Tamworth, Staffordshire for Pounds 2.8m, the amount at which it was valued in the balance sheet for December 31 1993. The surplus is expected to be Pounds 2.1m. Proceeds will be added to cash resources.
