BFN [Text] A joint security operation with ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring] and local security yesterday foiled an attempt to destabilize the peace and security of Monrovia. The press secretary to the field commander of ECOMOG, Mr. Francis Akinola, confirmed the operation, and said [words indistinct]. According to him, ECOMOG, the (?state security), and the Defense Ministry [word indistinct] carried out a surprise dawn check on some residences in Paynesville, including that of Mr. Alhaji Koromah. Mr. Akinola told journalists that some 26,000 thousand rounds of ammunition, 30 assorted weapons, 18 RPG launchers, 16 RPG bombs, and some communication equipment were found during the operation. Those arrested by ECOMOG are helping the peace keepers in efforts to ascertain where more arms and ammunition may have been kept.
