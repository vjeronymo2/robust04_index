BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- MINJU CHOSON today dedicates an article to the independence day of Senegal. The independence of Senegal was a historical event of great significance, the brilliant fruition of the protracted struggle of the Senegalese people for freedom and liberation, the author of the article says, and continues: After the independence of the country, the Senegalese people have made achievements in their efforts to attain political stability and build the national economy and culture. Pursuing a non-alignment policy externally, the Senegalese Government opposes imperialism, colonialism and racism and is developing friendly relations with many countries. The peoples of Korea and Senegal established bonds of friendship. The Korean people will make every effort in the future to expand and develop the friendly relations with the Senegalese people in various fields. They wish the Senegalese people greater success in their endeavours for the independent development and prosperity of the country.
