BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov received the Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Murtuz Sernavin [name and title as heard] who arrived in Baku yesterday on a working visit. Matters related to the broadening of bilateral ties were discussed at the meeting. The Azerbaijani president's upcoming visit to Iran was also discussed. It goes without saying that the Azerbaijan president's visit to Iran will lay a strong foundation for the development of our ties with the neighboring states.
