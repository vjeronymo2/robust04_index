BFN [Text] Tehran, April 20, IRNA -- Majlis Speaker Hojjat ol-Eslam 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri winding up his 10-day tour of Pakistan, Sri Lanka and Malaysia, arrived in Tehran today. During his three-nation tour, Nateq-Nuri held talks with leaders of the three countries on bilateral ties, situation in the region and in the world as a whole. Setting up parliamentary friendship groups to promote bilateral relations was the main theme of his talks with the officials of the three states. In Pakistan he was accorded an unprecedented welcome by the people and government. In his speech at Pakistan's national assembly, he expounded the plots of the World Arrogance and the United States to create split among the world Muslims and called on the Muslims throughout the world to foil the U.S. conspiracies. In another meeting with Pakistani religious leaders including Shiites and Sunnis he called for unity and equality. The Majlis speaker also attended the tomb of the great philosopher and persian language poet, Allama Iqbal Lahori and the founder of Pakistan, Mohammad Ali Jinnah and paid tribute to them. In Colombo and Kuala Lumpur, Nateq-Nuri held negotiations with the top-ranking officials of Sri Lanka and Malaysia to brief them on the policy of the Islamic Republic of Iran about various issues. Some members of the cabinet, deputy ministers and members of Majlis accompanied the speaker.
