BFN [Text] The rally of solidarity with the Netzarim settlement has ended in the Qatif Bloc. Approximately 10,000 people from every part of Israel participated in the rally and the preceding marches. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the rally was addressed by right-wing Knesset members, including Ari'el Sharon, Beni Begin, and Rehav'am Ze'evi. Ari'el Sharon told our correspondent that the settlers must resist settlement evacuation nonviolently. Approximately 2,000 of the participants in the Qatif Bloc events marched to Netzarim, violating a High Court of Justice ruling and understandings with the IDF [Israel Defense Forces], and the Army was forced to escort the marchers.
