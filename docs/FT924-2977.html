921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Atomic authority faces prosecution THE Atomic Energy Authority is being prosecuted by the Nuclear Installations Inspectorate over the release of radioactive tritium on April 25. The release was in the active handling bay of the Pluto reactor building at the Harwell Laboratory, Oxfordshire, during decommissioning. The authority faces a second prosecution under the Radioactive Substances Act 1960 for alleged breaches of consents on radioactive emissions.
