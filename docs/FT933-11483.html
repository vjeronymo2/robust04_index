930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Letter: Knock-out definition for diarist Observer ('Sheehy's lore', July 26) intends to be offensive but actually needs a dictionary. The Oxford Concise, which is probably detailed enough for a diarist, defines 'narcotic' as '(substance) inducing drowsiness, sleep, stupor or insensibility'. Perhaps Observer is writing a narcotic column] Michael Prideaux, director of group public affairs, BAT Industries, Windsor House, 50 Victoria Street, London SW1H 0NL
