July 17, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BOY, 5, KILLED BY PICKUP AS HE RUNS INTO STREET

A 5-year-old boy was struck and killed by a pickup truck Monday when he ran into traffic, police said.

Bren Mangubat of Cerritos suffered massive head injuries and was pronounced dead at La Palma Community Hospital shortly after the 5:40 p.m. accident, Cypress police dispatcher Cyndy Dodinez said.

The boy, who was visiting his grandmother on the 4200 block of Opal Street, "ran out into traffic . . . and was struck by a slow moving pickup truck," Dodinez said.
