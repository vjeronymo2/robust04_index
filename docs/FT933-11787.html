930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / Holiday industry surge forecast NEARLY 8m people are expected to take package holidays this year, bringing demand back to 1989 levels, Lunn Poly, the UK's largest travel agency, said yesterday. Mr Richard Bowden-Doyle, marketing director, said the forecast was for an increase of 5 per cent to 6 per cent on last summer. He added: 'We are confident that the travel industry has now emerged from the recession.' Lunn Poly said top destinations this summer were Majorca and Florida, followed by Cyprus, Turkey, Tenerife, Ibiza, the Portuguese Algarve, Corfu, Minorca and Crete.
