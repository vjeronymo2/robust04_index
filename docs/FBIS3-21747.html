Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] An important MRTA [Tupac Amaru Revolutionary Movement] political command leader was captured by Army forces in Bagua. The terrorist leader, identified as Humberto Pantaleon Campos, alias Seis por Dos, participated in raids against the towns of (Loindia) Grande and Jaen, and in a clash with the Army in Pampa (Cominca). In addition to capturing the MRTA political command leader, the Army also managed to capture two alleged narcoterrorists, identified as Avelino Carlos Auiche and Miguel Iriarte Tellez. In Amarango District, the 5th Infantry Division destroyed six maceration pits, four decantation pits, 20 hectares of coca crops, and various materials used to process cocaine paste.
