January 4, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL;

SIMI VALLEY DEFENSE THWARTED BY CHANNEL ISLANDS' TAUAUA

Despite Simi Valley's double- and triple-teaming defense, sophomore center Sal Tauaua of Channel Islands scored a game-high 26 points to lead the Lady Raiders to a 68-58 victory Tuesday in a Marmonte League opener at Simi Valley.

Shannon Solway had 14 points, 15 rebounds and 6 assists for Simi Valley (9-5 overall).

In other games:

La Reina 61, Moorpark 22 -- All 11 players scored for La Reina in the Tri-Valley League opener at La Reina.

Heather Freeman led La Reina (6-6) with 13 points. Moorpark, which trailed, 38-8, at halftime, failed to score in the third quarter.

Bell-Jeff 47, Burbank 40 -- Seniors Lisa Negrete and Andja Vucurevic had 12 and 10 points, respectively, to lead Bell-Jeff (11-3) to the nonleague win at home.

Senior forward Jennifer Helsley had 18 points for Burbank (1-8).

Alemany 56, Antelope Valley 44 -- Alemany came back from a 5-point halftime deficit to beat Antelope Valley in a nonleague game. Andrea Hoffman had 23 points and Sylvia Castaneda had 10 to lead Alemany.
