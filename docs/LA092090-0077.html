September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WESTWOOD;

AUCTION TO AID CHILD CARE

UCLA Child Care Services will hold its Bids for Kids auction Oct. 3 at the UCLA Tennis Center Clubhouse on the Westwood campus.

Proceeds from the annual event provide child care for low-income student families.

Vacations in Hawaii and Bermuda, dinner on Catalina Island and a Hanna Barbera animation cel are among the items to be auctioned.

Actor Kip Baker will serve as auctioneer at the event, which begins at 6 p.m. Admission is $25 per person and includes dinner. For further information, call (213) 825-5086.
