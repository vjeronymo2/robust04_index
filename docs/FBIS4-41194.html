BFN [Text] Reports from the zone of the Ossetian-Ingush conflict, where the state of emergency was extended until 30 June by the Russian president's edict, say the situation there is stabilizing. This has also been confirmed by military sources. No interethnic conflicts have been registered there in the past day. The Russian peacekeeping forces, jointly with the local law enforcement bodies, continue to ensure that the state of emergency is being observed.
