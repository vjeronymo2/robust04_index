BFN [Unattributed report: "Oil Exports Planned To Go Up"] [Text] The Fuel and Energy Ministry has requested that the Russian Government allocate an extra quota to export 2-3 million tonnes of crude oil with a corresponding overall increase in Russian enterprises' oil export quotas for 1994. The ministry thinks that oil exports will guarantee that Western banks will give loans to restructure the coal industry and to close mines that have no future prospects. At present, the Fuel and Energy Ministry and the Rosugol company are conducting talks with representatives of German and Portuguese firms and the World Bank on possible cooperation in the implementation of this program.
