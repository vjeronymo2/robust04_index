BFN [Text] Emomali Rakhmonov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan met with the chairmen of the executive committees of the towns and rayons of the Republic, today. The issues related to the social and political situation in Tajikistan, provision of the population with food as well as the course of the spring sowing field-work were discussed. The head of state answered a number of questions of those present.
