BFN [Text] Croatian Prime Minister Valentic concluded a three-day visit to Tehran and left today after signing several memoranda of understanding on cooperation in cultural, transport, and commercial fields. First Deputy President Habibi accompanied the Croatian prime minister to the airport. Prior to departing the Croatian prime minister told our correspondent that if the international community continues to neglect its duties, the Serbs will expand their aggression to Kosovo, Sandzak, and Macedonia. Referring to the recent agreement between the Croats and Muslims to form a united front, the Croatian official said that Croatia guarantees to maintain security of the route for the transport of food and medicine to Bosnia.
