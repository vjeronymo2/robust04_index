930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: China's propaganda coup on Hong Kong China scored what is seen as a propaganda coup with news that it is to appoint Sir David Akers-Jones (left), Hong Kong's former top civil servant, to the ranks of its advisers on the colony's affairs. He would be an important figure in the building of a 'united front' against proposals by British governor Chris Patten for wider democracy. He was formerly chief secretary of Hong Kong and served for a time as acting governor.
