930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Emerging Markets ahead Fleming Emerging Markets Investment Trust raised fully diluted net asset value by 26 per cent from 104.5p to 131.3p at the end of June. The figure had risen to 140.4p at September 9. As announced in May, the company is not paying a dividend for the year (1.2p). Net income dropped 71 per cent to Pounds 215,747 and this amount has been transferred to revenue reserve. Earnings per share were 0.22p (1.22p). Total assets at June 30 more than doubled from Pounds 63.2m to Pounds 133m, helped by the raising of Pounds 50m - before expenses - by way of a 'C' share issue.
