BFN [Text] Intense clashes between President Borhanoddin Rabbani's forces and the opposition forces continued in the Shindand region of Herat Province for the second consecutive day. According to a report released by the coalition opposed to Rabbani's presidency, 12 people were killed and 100 others were wounded. The coalition sources added that fighting continues in Qurmash region as well as in Badgheys Province, and the opposition forces have captured some positions of the government troops in this region.
