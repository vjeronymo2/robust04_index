Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng presided over the 14th executive meeting of the State Council in Beijing today to examine a draft law on labor and a set of draft regulations on overseas financial agencies in China. The draft law of labor is designed to meet the development of the socialist market economy, to protect the legal rights and interests of workers and regulate labor relations in China. The draft regulations on the management of overseas financial agencies in China are aimed at improving management and supervision measures in this field. The conference approved the two draft documents in principle. After further revision, the draft law on labor will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation. The regulations on the management of overseas financial agencies will be promulgated by the State Council after further revision.
