930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / International Company News: Burns Philp to shed hardware operations BURNS Philp plans to shed its hardware retail operations in an ADollars 400m (USDollars 271m) to ADollars 500m public flotation and focus on expanding its food business, Reuter reports from Sydney. The food and hardware group intends to float off its BBC Hardware unit as a separate company in a year or so. Burns Philp shareholders were likely to be offered a chance to take part in the flotation.
