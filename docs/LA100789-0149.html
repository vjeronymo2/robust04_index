October 7, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: HOTELS/TOURISM

The Radisson Suite Hotel has opened in Fullerton. The 98-room hotel is located off the Orange Freeway at Nutwood Avenue. . . . The Disneyland Hotel has named Linda McCoy as director of travel industry sales. McCoy joined the 1,200-room hotel in March, 1984, and is responsible for all travel-industry related matters. . . . The Anaheim Hilton and Towers has added Brit Osgood as assistant director of its sports and fitness center. . . . Airport Coach of Anaheim has named Al Patten as general manager. Patten previously was manager for the western region of Avis Leasing Corp. Airport Coach runs ground transportation to and from airports in Orange County, Long Beach and Los Angeles.
