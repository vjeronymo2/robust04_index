Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian sources have stated that Yasir 'Arafat has registered large amounts of PLO funds in the name of his wife, Suha Tawil. These funds were originally in 'Arafat's name. The sources said large amounts, estimated at millions of dollars, have been placed in Suha Tawil's name in Asian banks and investment companies, including ones in Singapore and Indonesia.
