940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Sanderson Bramall completes sale Sanderson Bramall Motor Group, the West Yorkshire-based motor distributor, has completed the sale of its Vauxhall car dealership based in Gloucester for about Pounds 2m cash. The sale was necessary to comply with Vauxhall franchising requirements. The buyer is Baylis (Gloucester), a newly-formed company set up with the backing of Vauxhall Motors. In 1992 the dealership incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 17,000, and had net assets of Pounds 1.2m.
