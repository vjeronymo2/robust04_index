July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EVERYBODY'S A CRITIC

Having been a regular at L'Orangerie for some years now, I couldn't believe that it wasn't included amongst Los Angeles' finest. I was even more shocked when Reichl stated: "L'Orangerie offers a gorgeous setting, but the last meal I had there was so disappointing -- amateur service, high prices and food that was little more than bistro cooking." I have, to date, never had a bad meal or bad service at the restaurant.

MICHAEL CAINE

Beverly Hills
