May 19, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

THE NATION

Foreigners with AIDS will be allowed to seek 30-day visas to enter this country on business, attend conferences or visit relatives, the Justice Department said. New regulations to be issued by the Immigration and Naturalization Service allowing such travel were drafted following last month's dispute over the detention of a Dutch AIDS patient en route to a conference in San Francisco on the deadly disease, department spokesman David Runkel said.
