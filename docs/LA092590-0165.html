September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

S&L Takeovers -- An article in Sept. 21 editions of The Times incorrectly attributed remarks that the federal takeovers of troubled savings and loan institutions in 1988 involved "insiders" and "sweet deals" to Sen. Robert Kerrey (D-Neb.)

.They should have been attributed to Sen. John F. Kerry (D-Mass.).
