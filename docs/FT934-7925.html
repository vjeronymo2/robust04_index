931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Six-year path to majority rule: From Zulu wars to multi-racial democracy December 1993 Current parliament passes constitution. Multi-party Transitional Executive Council (TEC) appointed to oversee government in the transition and ensure free and fair elections. Independent Electoral Commission (IEC) set up to organise and supervise voting. Independent Media Commission (IMC) established to ensure fair news coverage by state-controlled media April 27, 1994 Elections to constituent assembly/national legislature and to regional legislatures May 1994 President elected by national assembly at its first sitting May 1994 to May 1996 Constituent assembly drafts final constitution Early 1996 Local government elections 1996-1999 New constitution phased in, government of national unity remains in place 1999 Government of national unity replaced by majority government
