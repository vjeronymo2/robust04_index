930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Aluminium talks planned A MULTILATERAL meeting is expected to be held in Moscow in the second half of October to discuss the problem of cheap Russian aluminium exports, according to a European Commission official, reports Reuter from Brussels. Major producers, such as the US, Canada and the EC would attend, he said after talks here on Friday between the commission and Russian officials. 'The meeting went well. It showed a renewed willingness for a multilateral solution.'
