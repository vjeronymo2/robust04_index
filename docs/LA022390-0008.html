February 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $36 when none of his multiple triples hit Thursday. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $671.80.

Bob's bets today are $10 doubles from Yo Mama Mia in the first to Free Bail, Whiskey Jack and Reb Roy in the second.
