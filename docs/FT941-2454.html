940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Business Travel (Update): US expansion KLM Royal Dutch Airlines will fly to 28 more US destinations from Amsterdam this summer, and add Osaka in Japan to its scheduled flights in September. Flights to Ho Chi Minh city in Vietnam will become bi-weekly, instead of weekly. Schipol, the Netherlands' main airport, had 21m passengers last year, up 11 per cent.
