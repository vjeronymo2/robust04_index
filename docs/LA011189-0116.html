January 11, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

BRUNER, STEPHENSON LEAD OCCIDENTAL WOMEN

Sharon Bruner scored 16 points and Kelly Stephenson added 14 as Occidental defeated Pacific Christian, 47-44, in a women's nonconference basketball game at Occidental on Tuesday.

Trailing by a point at halftime, Occidental (4-7) took the lead early in the second half and maintained it.
