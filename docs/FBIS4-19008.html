BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DPA) -- Christian Democratic Union Chairman [CDU] and Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl expressly thanked the Free Democratic Party on Tuesday [12 April] for the intention expressed the previous day to continue the coalition with the CDU/Christian Social Union after the Bundestag election. In the first coalition talks after the Easter recess, Kohl noticed a considerable improvement in the atmosphere. The "spirit of friendly cooperation" will lead to a success of the coalition parties in the Bundestag election, Kohl was quoted by participants in the talks. [passage omitted]
