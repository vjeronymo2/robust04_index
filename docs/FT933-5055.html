930906 FT 06 SEP 93 / Consumer confidence improved in August says survey CONSUMER confidence improved last month but it is still too early to predict a steady recovery in spending, says a survey carried out by NOP for Verdict, the retail market research group, Neil Buckley writes. The index for August was 118, up from 112 in June and July - and well above the 109 in August last year - but still below the 125 reached in May.
