BFN [By UKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev May 27 TASS -- President Leonid Kravchuk of Ukraine met with a delegation of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT), led by Andy Stoler, chairman of commission on Ukraine's joining the GATT, on Thursday. The visitors are taking part in the governmental conference on Ukraine's joining GATT and the World Trade Organisation in Kiev. The process of Ukraine's joining GATT has already begun, Kravchuk told the delegation. It is rather important for our economy and development of the country on the whole. We are aware that the process of entering GATT has its laws and regulations and will do our utmost to pass it as soon as possible, Kravchuk said.
