Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 6 Feb (SUNA) -- The Libyan leader, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the first of September revolution in the Jamahiriyah, has expressed his country's concern over the incident at the mosque at locality [al-harah] One in Omdurman's al-Thawrah quarter last Friday [4 February], when armed foreigners fired at worshippers in the mosque. A responsible source at the Republican Palace said that al-Qadhdhafi had asked the Sudanese Embassy to convey his warmest condolences to the families of the victims of the incident.
