940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / People: Videologic Videologic, a subsidiary of Avesco, has appointed two non-executive directors. Geoff Shingles, 55, was formerly chief executive of Digital Equipment since 1983 and its chairman since 1991, having been with the company in a variety of roles since 1965. Keppel Simpson, 60, spent 25 years at Management Consultants PA Consulting group.
