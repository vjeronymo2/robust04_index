BFN [By Neide Maria Silva] [Excerpts] Sao Carlos, 12 Jun (AE) -- Two Unified Social Workers Party (PSTU) leaders were murdered early today in Sao Carlos. Jose Luis Sundermann, 37, vice president of the Sao Carlos University Labor Union, and his wife Rosa Hernandes Sundermann, 37, were shot in the head while in their home. Jose Luis was shot once, and Rosa twice. [passage omitted] Sundermann had been president of the Union for the past two years. He was also a member of the national executive board of the Federation of University Workers, and was a member of the executive board of the regional CUT [Sole Workers Central] branch. Rosa was a member of the PSTU national executive board, but joined the PT at the end of the 1970's. [passage omitted] Sao Carlos University Labor Union President Antonio Donizete da Silva termed the murder of the two leaders a "political execution." [passage omitted]
