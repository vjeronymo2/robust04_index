920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Safeland halved to Pounds 167,000 Safeland, the property group, yesterday reported that pre-tax profits were halved, from Pounds 335,000 to Pounds 167,000, in the year to March 31. Turnover declined by Pounds 3.3m to Pounds 8.6m. At the interim stage the company reported profits of Pounds 81,000 from turnover of Pounds 3.26m. The final of 0.14p (0.3p) makes a total of 0.28p (1p) and is more than twice covered by earnings of 0.6p (1.1p).
