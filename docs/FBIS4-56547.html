BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic developments monitored through 3 May. The National Institute of Statistics and Census (Inec) has reported prices rose this year by 3 percent in April, while in March they rose 2.64 percent. Inec added prices rose 3.6 percent in April 1993. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 2 May 94) On 1 May, Agriculture Minister Mariano Gonzalez reported Ecuadoran banana exports surpassed 1 million tons in the first 4 months of this year, which represents a record figure. The minister added that foreign currency revenue between 1 January and 15 April amounted to more than $123 million and that 54 percent of the sales went to the countries of the European Union. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 2305 GMT 1 May 94)
