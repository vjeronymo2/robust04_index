Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 31 Jan (ARMENPRES) -- An Azerbaijani reconnaissance battalion violated Armenia's borders in Tavushskiy Rayon and launched an attack against the defensive positions near Movses in the early hours of 31 January. The enemy suffered human losses at the fighting, which continuing for a few hours, and was thrown back. There were no casualties on the Armenian side.
