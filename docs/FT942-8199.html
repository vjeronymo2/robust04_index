940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices White pepper - prices in general remained unchanged with a slightly steadier undertone for new crop positions. Spot offered at Dollars 2600, July/Oct shipment is offered at Dollars 2550 cif. Handpicked quality unavailable until new crop. Black pepper - some pressure on prices as some sellers from india and lampong liquidated some stocks. Although buying interest is still low, prices recovered towards the end of this week. Faq spot at Dollars 1575 and grade 1 at about Dollars 1650. Cassia and cinnamon - spot positions tighter. Better qualities are fully booked until Aug/Sep nutmegs - Far East prices remain high. Coverage is advised at current attractive levels. Pimento, cloves unchanged.
