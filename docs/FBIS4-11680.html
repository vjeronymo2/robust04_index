BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Velayati arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, today, on the first leg of his tour of a number of African countries. According to a Central News Unit correspondent's report from Conakry airport, Mr. Velayati said his trip was aimed at expanding ties between the two countries in various fields. Before arriving in Guinea Mr. Velayati made a short stopover at N'djamena, the capital of Chad, during which he met and conferred with Chad's foreign minister.
