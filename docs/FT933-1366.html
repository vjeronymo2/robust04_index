930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / People: Minet Consultancy Services Graham Blewett, formerly national sales director of Frizzell Life & Financial Planning, has been appointed md of MINET Consultancy Services. John Telford also moves from Frizzell to become a divisional director; and Mike O'Sullivan moves from Halifax Independent Financial Advisers to become a divisional director. David Edwards is appointed national development director; he moves from Godwins in Sevenoaks.
