940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / Airport users up The number of passengers using leading airports rose by 5.4 per cent last month compared with January 1993, operator BAA said yesterday. Its seven airports handled 5.6 million passengers. Southampton airport had a 21 per cent increase, Stansted 20 per cent and Heathrow 7 per cent. Gatwick, meanwhile, stayed the same. In Scotland, Edinburgh was 6.2 per cent up, Glasgow 3.0 per cent up and Aberdeen 0.1 per cent up.
