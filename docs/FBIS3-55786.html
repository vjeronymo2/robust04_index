Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Nikolay Gritchin: "Kabardino-Balkaria Constitution Brought Into Line With Russian Constitution"] [Text] Kabardino-Balkaria President V. Kokov has suggested to the republic's parliament that it alter and supplement the articles of the Kabardino-Balkar Republic so that it complies with the new Russian Federation Constitution. After a close study of the president's package of proposals at separate sessions of the chambers and in conciliation commissions, the republic's parliament has approved them.
