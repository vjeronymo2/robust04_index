940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Albrighton Acquisitions helped Albrighton, the USM-quoted quarrying concern, report pre-tax profits of Pounds 766,000 for the year to March 31, against losses of Pounds 358,000. Turnover improved from Pounds 2.45m to Pounds 5.11m, including Pounds 2.13m from acquisitions, for operating income of Pounds 892,000 (Pounds 9,000 loss) with Pounds 418,000 from acquisitions. From earnings per share of 1.2p (losses 0.6p) a total dividend of 0.5p (nil) is being paid with a proposed final of 0.4p. Mr Humphrey Wood, chairman, said that the closing months of the year had yielded a more satisfactory flow of orders with signs of improvement in margins and prices.
