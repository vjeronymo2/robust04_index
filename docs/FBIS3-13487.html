Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu 'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] yesterday sent a written message to Russian President Boris Yeltsin in reply to his important political message conveyed by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev to brother President Abu 'Ammar the day before yesterday in Tunis. In his message brother President Abu 'Ammar expressed his great satisfaction at the high level of cordiality and sincerity in the current dialogue between the PLO and friendly Russia, which confirms the standing and development of the relations of friendship, cooperation, and consultation between the Palestinian and Russian peoples. Brother President Abu 'Ammar valued President Yeltsin's eagerness for the peace process and negotiations to continue, the important steps taken by President Yeltsin and the Russian Government to prevent the escalation of violence in the occupied Palestinian territories, and also Russian efforts in the UN Security Council in getting issued an effective resolution that would fulfill the Palestinians' just demands. In his message brother Abu 'Ammar also expressed his confidence that friendly Russia, as a co-sponsor of the peace conference, would continue to make efforts to ensure the implementation of the Declaration of Principles agreement.
