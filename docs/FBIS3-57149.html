Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The document on the reform of the presidential administration apparatus will probably be signed next week, Sergey Filatov reported to an ITAR-TASS correspondent. According to Filatov, at the moment its work with the Federal Assembly needs to be intensified. In connection with this, a new structure is being set up to work in this area with factions and deputies' groups. Among other areas of the presidential administration's work in need of improvement, Filatov also mentioned the increase of the control functions over implementation of edicts and instructions issued by the president, improvement of the analytical service and relations with the media. As Filatov noted, the document stresses the important role to be played by the department engaged in working with the territories. In his opinion, the analysis of the pre-election campaign and elections has revealed various shortcomings in the work of local presidential representatives. As Filatov reported, in future, the candidates for the post of presidential representatives will have to be approved by heads of the local administration.
