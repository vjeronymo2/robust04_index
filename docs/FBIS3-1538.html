Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] A senior Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola [FALA] officer in Luanda Norte has told the Voice of Resistance of the Black Cockerel that every mercenary, either South African or of any other nationality, who fans the Angolan war alongside the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party, will not be spared by FALA. The source said UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] is not responsible for any mercenary killed or likely to be killed in action.
