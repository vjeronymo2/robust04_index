BFN [Text] An agreement on the release of prisoners from prisons under the control of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army and the Croat Defense Council has been signed. Those charged with or sentenced for the most serious criminal offenses will not be released if the signatories show the Red Cross by Sunday that charges have been pressed or sentences passed. An international committee of experts is to be founded to interview those accused of the most serious criminal acts within 30 days and examine all evidence relevant to the criminal procedures. After that the committee will make decisions on the further detention of the accused. The agreement says that the Red Cross will determine the dynamics and mode of implementation of the prisoner release.
