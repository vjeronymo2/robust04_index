BFN [By reporter Wei Yunting 7614 6663 0080] [Text] Shenyang, 22 May (XINHUA) -- The Eighth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Eighth Liaoning Provincial People's Congress today accepted the resignation of Yue Qifeng as governor of Liaoning and appointed Wen Shizhen as acting governor of Liaoning. Wen Shizhen, 54, has served as deputy secretary of the Liaoning provincial party committee and vice govenor of Liaoning.
