BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has left for Romania as the official guest of Romanian Interior Minister Ioan Doru Taracila. In a statement at Istanbul Ataturk Airport before his departure, Mentese said that the relations between the two friendly countries are good. Pointing out that domestic security issues will also be discussed during his contacts in Romania, Mentese added that he will also obtain information concerning allegations that the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terror organization is active in Romania as well. Mentese will return to Turkey on Friday [8 April].
