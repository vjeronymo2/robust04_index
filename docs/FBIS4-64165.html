BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Excerpt] Dushanbe, 31 May -- Lieutenant Colonel Vladimir Borisenkov, a senior member of the Russian Federation border troops staff in Tajikistan, became the latest victim of terrorists in Dushanbe today. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned from the staff of Russian Federation border troops in Tajikistan that Vladimir Borisenkov was fatally wounded by unidentified people while carrying out his duties at about 0730 [0230 GMT]. The officer died some time later in a military hospital without regaining consciousness. A judicial investigation into the crime has begun. Lt. Col. Vladimir Borisenkov had worked on the staff of the Russian Federation border troops since 1980. [passage omitted]
