April 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $36 Wednesday when none of his three horses, Native Pocket, Risen Port and No Doubles Match, won the sixth, the front end of his $12 triple. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $984.40.

Bob's bet today is a $20 win parlay from Oak Portal in the first to Festin in the seventh.
