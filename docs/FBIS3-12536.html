Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by political correspondent Aluf Ben] [Excerpt] Israel will be asking the United States to broaden the terms for the use of the U.S. loan guarantees in order to finance the building of a security infrastructure beyond the Green Line. Among other things, the money would be used to build new roads in the aftermath of the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] redeployment after the withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho. The use of the guarantees will be discussed in Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin's meetings with the heads of the U.S. Administration during his upcoming trip to the United States. [passage omitted]
