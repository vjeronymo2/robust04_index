940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: EFM aims to double Small Cos Trust's size Edinburgh Fund Managers' Small Companies Investment Trust is aiming to almost double in size with a conversion share issue. The trust - launched last August - invests in UK quoted companies mainly capitalised at under Pounds 100m. It has net assets of Pounds 58m. The maximum size of the C share issue has been set at Pounds 50m, after the placing drew irrevocable undertakings for Pounds 20m-worth of C shares at 100p. The public offer opens today and closes on March 23. Warrants will be attached on a 1-for-5 basis. Conversion will take place by September 30. The Foreign & Colonial Income Growth investment trust, the group's first investment trust to concentrate on the UK, has raised Pounds 7m from a placing. The public offer, which closes on March 25, has been given a Pounds 250m limit, to avoid any need to scale back applications.
