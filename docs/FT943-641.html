940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: JBA at Pounds 264,000 In its first set of results as a listed company since its flotation in June, JBA Holdings, the software company, turned in an interim pre-tax profit of Pounds 264,000 against a loss of Pounds 1.07m. Turnover for the six months to end June was up by 29 per cent to Pounds 38.5m (Pounds 29.9m). There is an interim dividend of 0.8p, a proforma increase of 33 per cent. Earnings per share came out at 0.19p against losses of 2.87p.
