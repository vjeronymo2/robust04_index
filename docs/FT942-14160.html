940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wiggins buys Fairfield Hospital Wiggins Group, the property developer, is acquiring Fairfield Hospital, Lower Stotfold, Bedfordshire, from the Department of Health for a minimum Pounds 7.25m cash. The deal is conditional on the grant of planning permission and listed building consent for a development of between 300 and 450 homes together with other uses. Consideration amounts to a proportion of the sale proceeds of each residential unit, subject to the minimum, and is payable in tranches between 1994 and 1998. A non-returnable deposit of Pounds 150,000 has been paid.
