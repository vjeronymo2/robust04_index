BFN [By special correspondent Zhang Dai; from the "Morning News" program] [Text] Zhejiang's Chunan county [where the Qiandao Hu Boat tragedy occurred] has taken comprehensive measures to further rectify public security, transportation, and tourism order. Key measures taken to improve tourism include setting up communication facilities and fire fighting equipment, as well as deploying public security personnel in all tourist attractions. For those tourist attractions that are not qualified for the abovementioned conditions and not approved by the tourism authorities must stop accepting tourists. Tourism personnel shall be trained in turn in two groups. They must pass an examination and have a license before performing their duty. Guesthouses without a special license are not allowed to accommodate nonresident tourists.
