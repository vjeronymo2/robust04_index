BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Brussels, 24h May -- Russia's Defense Minister Pavel Grachev arrived in Brussels today where he will make a report on the new Russian defense doctrine to the NATO leadership and the defense ministers of the alliance countries this evening. He was met by Belgian Defense Minister Leo Delcroix and by other officials at Melsbroek military airport. Replying to journalists' questions, P. Grachev said that Russia will sign a framework document of the NATO "Partnership for Peace" program at the same time as an agreement on bilateral cooperation between Russia and NATO when this agreement is finally ready.
