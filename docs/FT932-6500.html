930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / Mexican reward offer THE Mexican government has offered a reward of nearly Dollars 5m (Pounds 3.2m) for information leading to the capture of four drug traffickers thought responsible for the murder of Cardinal Juan Jesus Posadas Ocampo, who, investigators say, was killed after being mistaken for drugs boss Joaquin 'Chapo' Guzman Lorea, Damian Fraser reports from Mexico City. Cardinal Posadas was being driven in a Grand Marquis car of the kind often used by drug traffickers, and was wearing black, supposedly the drug traffickers' favourite colour.
