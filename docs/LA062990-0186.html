June 29, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

NO PARDON FOR BILLY THE KID

Gov. Garrey E. Carruthers has rejected a writer's request that Billy the Kid be pardoned for his role in the 1878 shooting death of Sheriff William Brady.

A letter is being prepared telling Las Cruces author Opal Lee Priestley of the governor's decision, B. J. Wagner-Adam, the governor's administrator of pardons and extraditions, said Thursday. Priestley is the author of "Billy the Kid: The Good Side of a Bad Man."

Wagner-Adam said a full investigation of all facts surrounding the case would be impossible because it happened so long ago.
