940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / International Company News: Steel Authority of India boosts sales by 15% The Steel Authority of India (Sail), the country's biggest steel producer, has posted a 29.83 per cent rise in net profit to Rs5.5bn (Dollars 175m)for the year to the end of March, on sales 15 per cent higher at Rs94bn. The company had to pay a levy of Rs3.23bn to the Steel Development Fund, since abolished, and absorb Rs3bn with higher input costs. Despite the world recession, Sail has raised exports by 121 per cent to Rs5.61bn. Mr MRR Nair chairman, is confident that domestic demand for steel will be better this year. Sail's plants produced 8.3m tonnes of steel last year.
