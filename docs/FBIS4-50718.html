BFN [Text] Taiyuan, May 18 (XINHUA) -- Shanxi Province in north China has applied 332 scientific and technological achievements in agriculture over the past decade. A local government official said that this scientific progress has resulted in increases of 20.29 billion kilograms of grain and 27.8 billion yuan in farmers' income over the past ten years. The province has used more than 100 fine seeds in 3.2 million hectares, an area which accounts for 80 percent of the total arable land in the province. The Shanxi Academy of Agricultural Sciences, Shanxi University of Agriculture and other agricultural research institutes have played key roles in the popularization of agricultural technology. Of the more than 300 items of scientific research achievements completed by the Shanxi Academy of Agricultural Sciences, 60 percent have been applied to farm production. For instance, research achievements in raising grain yield per hectare and enhancing crops' resistance to drought have been successfully applied. The official said that to popularize scientific achievements in agriculture, the province has mobilized more than 10,000 technicians to work in rural areas. The province now has 200,000 hectares of high-yield fields for the application of new technology.
