December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEW CRACK FUELS OPPOSITION TO GARVEY RESERVOIR

Metropolitan Water District of Southern California officials say they have almost remedied the flooding from the 41-acre Garvey Reservoir in Monterey Park, which damaged two homes on Fulton Drive.

But Monterey Park officials are continuing to oppose the reopening of the reservoir, particularly since a second crack near its base was found this week about 1,000 feet from the first one.
