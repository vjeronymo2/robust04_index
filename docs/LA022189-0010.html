February 21, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

"Legs Diamond" creator-star Peter Allen drew the first of two standing ovations during the final performance Sunday of the $5.3-million Broadway bomb when he rose from a coffin at the start of the second act and declared, "Not even critics can kill me." The musical about a big-time New York mobster closed less than two months after it opened to dismal reviews in December. Shauna Snow
