November 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE

Architecture Awards: LPA, one of the larger architecture firms based in the county, recently won three of the 25 awards presented by the National Assn. of Industrial and Office Parks.

Formerly Leason Pomeroy Associates, the Irvine-based firm won a grand award for a small office building called One Venture, built with The Koll Co. in the Irvine Spectrum.

The firm received two other awards for a high-tech building in Santa Ana, designed for Bank of America, and for the North Valley Business Center in Sylmar, designed for the Hewson Co.
