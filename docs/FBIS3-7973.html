Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Protest Postponed for 8 Days"] [Text] About 3,000 workers protested in front of the Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Parliament yesterday. The workers' delegation was received by representatives of the parliament deputy groups and the Minister of Health and Social Politics Iliaz Sabriu also joined the meeting later on. After the talks the workers from insolvent firms decided to postpone the general strike until next Tuesday, thus leaving additional 8 days for the Government to meet their demands. If no solution is offered by then, the workers announced massive protest marches.
