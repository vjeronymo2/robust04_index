April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: LOST IN TRANSLATION A LIFE IN A NEW LANGUAGE BY EVA HOFFMAN (DUTTON: $18.95; 280 PP.)

"Eva Hoffman is a masterful storyteller, her voice rich and melodic, her recollections intimate, vivid, poignant. She is a passionate survivor who is haunted by history and, above all, by language." Jonathan Kirsch
