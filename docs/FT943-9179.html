940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Drayton Far Eastern static Net assets per share of Drayton Far Eastern Trust were little changed at 165.03p at the half year to June 30, against 164.8p six months earlier. After-tax revenue for the period dropped from Pounds 721,000 to Pounds 406,000 and earnings per share came to 0.351p (0.624p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.125p.
