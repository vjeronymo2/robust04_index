BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 May (INA) -- [Iraqi] Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met here today with Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina. The foreign minister briefed his Cuban counterpart on the recent positive developments and new atmosphere at the UN Security Council in favor of Iraq, represented by the majority calling for lifting the embargo clamped on Iraq after Iraq has fulfilled its obligations toward UN Security Council Resolution 687. The minister stressed the importance of maintaining the spirit and essence of the non-aligned states' principles so as to reinforce their vital interests and entrench their independence. Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf also briefed the Cuban foreign minister on the Iraqis' achievements in the field of reconstructing their country and safeguarding its national security despite the unjust economic embargo imposed on Iraq. The INA correspondent in Cairo cited the Cuban foreign minister as reiterating his country's support for Iraq's persistent efforts to lift the embargo, and its solidarity with the Iraqi people. Al-Sahhaf and Robaina stressed the importance of promoting cooperation between the two friendly countries in the interest of their peoples. Dr. Nabil Najm, Iraq's permanent representative to the Arab League, attended the meeting.
