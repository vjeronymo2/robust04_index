Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 5 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Friday [4 March] sent a message of greetings to Ghanaian President Jerry John Rawlings on the occasion of the 37th anniversary of the independence of Ghana. He wished the Ghanaian president and people success in the efforts for the stability and prosperity of the country. He also expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Ghana would further develop in the common interests of the two peoples.
