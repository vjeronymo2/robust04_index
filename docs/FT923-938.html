920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / Observer: Show-down Unlike most of the white-collar breed who get the sack these days, David Mellor at least has another job to go to - in pantomime. Screaming Lord Sutch yesterday publicly offered him Pounds 2,000 a week to play the 'Raving Loony Captain' opposite Eartha Kitt in Robinson Crusoe, due to tour nationally later this year.
