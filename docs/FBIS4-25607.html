BFN [Text] Hefei, June 10 (XINHUA) -- A furnace with a volume of 2,500 cubic meters went into operation in Maanshan Thursday [9 June], a major iron and steel production center in east China's Anhui Province. The furnace is designed to produce 1.75 million tons of pig iron annually. It was built by the Maanshan Iron and Steel Company at a total cost of 3.23 billion yuan. Chinese Vice-Premier Zou Jiahua attended a ceremony to mark the operation of the furnace, a top-priority construction project in China.
