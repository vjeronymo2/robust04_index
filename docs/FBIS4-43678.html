BFN [Excerpt] Lampertheim -- The Social Democratic Party of Germany's [SPD] deputy chairwoman, Heidemarie Wieczorek-Zeul has spoken out for a red-green coalition. A shift in power cannot be achieved "with the parties that are responsible for the desperate situation in Germany: Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union, and Free Democratic Party," she told the SPD Hesse South party conference at Lampertheim on Friday [17 June]. As far as the Bundestag election campaign is concerned, the Social Democrats must quickly draw the conclusions from their defeat at the European election, Wieczoerek-Zeul said. [passage omitted]
