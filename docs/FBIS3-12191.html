Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The government will issue administrative detention orders for five leaders of the Kakh movement, as well as restriction and banishment orders for 30 of its activists. The orders will be issued in accordance with the government's decision at noon today to confiscate the weapons of several Kakh activists. [passage omitted] The cabinet was told today that according to the debriefings conducted by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and the Israel Police, 39 people were murdered in the Cave of the Patriarchs and 6 were killed in the city of Hebron in ensuing clashes. Major General Dani Yatom reported that Dr. Barukh Goldstein fired 111 bullets.
