940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Company news this week: Farnell Electronics - Other Companies The electronic components group, which paid Dollars 69.8m for Multicomponents in October, today reports its first final results since Mr Howard Poulson took over as chief executive. Pre-tax profits of Pounds 47m-Pounds 48.5m are expected, up from Pounds 41.6m.
