920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Everest Foods rises to Pounds 1.77m Everest Foods, the frozen food producer and wholesaler, yesterday reported a 20 per cent increase in sales and profits for the half-year to November 30. Pre-tax profits advanced from Pounds 1.47m to Pounds 1.77m and came from turnover up from Pounds 14.9m to Pounds 17.9m. Earnings per share increased by 22 per cent to 11.7p (9.55p) and as forecast in December 1991, when the group took up a full listing, the interim dividend is maintained at 2.2p.
