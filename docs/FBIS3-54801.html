Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 30, TURAN--Colonel-General Andrey Nikolayev-the commander of the Russian frontier troops is visiting Baku. Today at 4 p.m. General Nikolayev was received by President Aliyev. As a reliable source at the presidential staff told TURAN agency the questions of the further on cooperation between Russia and Azerbaijan in the guarding of the southern borders of the CIS were discussed at the meeting. Last November Nikolayev told journalists in Moscow that Azerbaijan had appealed to Moscow with a proposal of the cooperative guarding of the southern borders and this question is being working out at present. But the official Baku rejected this report then. During the today's meeting Andrey Nikolayev said, that the parties intend to discuss the problems of the cooperative guarding of the southern borders of the CIS. In his turn, Geydar Aliyev said that Azerbaijan would like to solve the problems of guarding its borders in complex. For Azerbaijan the guarding of the border with Armenian is much more important than the guarding of border with the other states, he said. In this connection he pointed out, that 170 km of Azerbaijani-Iranian border is under the control of the Armenian Army. After this the meeting went on behind closed doors.
