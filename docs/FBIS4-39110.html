BFN [Text] The AZERBAIJAN NEWS SERVICE AND PRESS AGENCY [AZERBAYCAN NEWS SERVICE, PRESS AGENTLIGI] has reported that the situation between the Armenian and Azerbaijani forces has worsened over the past few days. A complex situation has emerged in Sadarak Rayon in the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic, which is a part of Azerbaijan. The Armenian aggressors have made several attempts to attack important heights in that area over the past few days. Consequently, some 100 Armenian soldiers have been killed or wounded. The enemy units have been forced to retreat to their previous positions. Several Armenian tanks and other military vehicles have been hit in the clashes. Meanwhile, positional fighting continues on other parts of the front line.
