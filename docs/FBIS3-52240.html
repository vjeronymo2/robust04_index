Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. and Arab sources stated today in Washington that Israelis and Syrians met in Europe secretly several times since last May and worked out an agreement on security arrangements and sovereignty on the Golan Heights. The sources added that the Israeli and Syrian personalities who participated in the meetings were civilians as well as former military personnel, including university professors and journalists. The sources said that the meetings were held in Oslo, London, and Rome and that eventually a one-page deocument was drawn up containing a solution to the disagreement between Israel and Syria over the sovereignty of the Golan Heights.
