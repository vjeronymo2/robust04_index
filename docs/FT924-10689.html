921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Secs Tst Scotland asset value static Over the six months to September 30 net asset value at Securities Trust of Scotland rose by 0.4 per cent, from 70.1p to 70.4p. On September 30 the year before it stood at 77p. Of the portfolio 66 per cent was held in the UK, 8 per cent on the Continent, 17.5 in the US, and 7 per cent in the Pacific Basin. Gross revenue totalled Pounds 8.91m (Pounds 8.96m), reflecting lower deposit interest income as a result of reduced cash holdings and falling interest rates. Earnings per share were again 1.53p and the interim dividend is held at 1.08p.
