920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Danger driving sentence to rise HOME secretary Kenneth Clarke said yesterday that the government was planning to double the maximum prison sentence for drivers who kill by dangerous driving or while under the influence of drink or drugs. When parliamentary time permits, legislation will be introduced increasing the maximum jail term will from five years to 10 to allow courts to reflect the seriousness of the worst cases, Mr Baker said in a Commons written reply.
