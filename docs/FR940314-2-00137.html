DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 1962] Delegation of Authority 211; Authorization of Testimony and Production of Documents By virtue of the authority vested in me by 22 CFR 172.4(b) and section 4 of the Act of May 26, 1949 (63 Stat. 111, 22 U.S.C. 2658), I hereby delegate to Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary for Consular Affairs the authority conferred on the Assistant Secretary for Consular Affairs pursuant to 22 CFR 172.4 and 172.5 concerning approval of testimony and production of documents by Department of State employees. This authority may be redelegated to another Deputy Assistant Secretary. Notwithstanding this delegation of authority, the Assistant Secretary for Consular Affairs may at any time exercise any authority conferred upon that officer by 22 CFR part 172.
