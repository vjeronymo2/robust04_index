September 29, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Former astronaut William A. Anders will replace Stanley C. Pace as chairman and chief executive of General Dynamics Corp., St. Louis. Anders is senior executive vice president-operations and a director of Textron Inc., which has interests in aerospace technology, commercial products and financial services. He will join General Dynamics on Jan. 1 as vice chairman and will become chairman and chief executive when Pace retires Jan. 1, 1991. Pace has been chief executive since 1986.
