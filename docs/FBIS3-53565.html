Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on the results of the Syrian-U.S. summit in Geneva, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri said in a statement tonight: We have followed with great satisfaction the positions expressed by President Hafiz al-Asad after his talks with U.S. President Clinton. These are the positions to restore balance to the peace process and open a new chapter to which we attach importance toward achieving the just, lasting, and comprehensive peace for which we all hope. Al-Hariri said the Israeli occupation of south Lebanon formed a basic part of the talks, in which President al-Asad adopted serious and honorable positions that are certainly appreciated by the Lebanese people and Government. He added: In my name, and on behalf of the Lebanese Government, I salute President al-Asad, who made UN Resolution 425 a main topic on the agenda of the historic summit.
