April 16, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

BACK TO SCHOOL

Guilford Dudley III ("Death Knell for Noblesse Oblige," April 2) may have had noblesse oblige instilled in him during his prep school years, but clearly spelling must have been a second-class subject; "Eaton," indeed.

JANET R. GOFF

Cardiff

Editor's note: The preparatory school in England is spelled "Eton."
