Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 24/1/1994 -- Today, President Mircea Snegur met at his request with heads of Moldovan justice bodies, says a communique of the presidential press service. Ways to solve the problem of the so-called Ilascu group prisoners in the context of proposals by CSCE and the Council of Europe groups of experts and by the European Union and Russia were discussed during their meeting. It was stressed that the support of international organizations was very important. The participants in the discussion expressed their readiness to analyze the suggestions of international experts, taking into the account the statements made by President Snegur. According to his statements, the prisoners should be transferred to Moldovan constitutional justice bodies, states the communique.
