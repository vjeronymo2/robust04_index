941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: York Waterworks York Waterworks raised turnover by 5 per cent from Pounds 3.92m to Pounds 4.1m in the six months to September 30 and pre-tax profits moved ahead by 9 per cent from Pounds 1.41m to Pounds 1.55m. Mr Richard Stanley, chairman, said this progress was expected to be maintained in the full year, although seasonal factors would again result in the second half performance not being as strong as the first. Earnings per share were 1p higher at 16.7p and the interim dividend has been stepped up to 3.55p (3.3p).
