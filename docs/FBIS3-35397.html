Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Mahmud Muhammad, Egyptian minister of economy and foreign trade, arrived in Amman today on a visit aimed at reinforcing trade relations between the two fraternal countries. The visit by Mahmud Muhammad, Egyptian minister of economy and foreign trade, is meant to reinforce trade and economic relations between Jordan and Egypt and to increase commercial exchanges between the two fraternal countries. [Begin Muhammad recording] We are here to sign, God willing, a protocol after an agreement has been reached on the list of Egyptian and Jordanian commodities. As I told you, the aim is to reinforce and give momentum to commercial exchanges between our two countries. I also want to take this opportunity to say that, God willing, I will have the honor of meeting with some officials in this fraternal country to exchange views with them, benefit from their experience, and inform them of our views on the Egyptian experiment. As you know, Egypt went through very successful stages of economic transition and reform. It became a model for economic reform. We hope that this operation will serve the Egyptian people and upgrade the level of economic performance in Egypt. [passage omitted] [end recording]
