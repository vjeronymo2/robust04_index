Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 8 SAPA -- About 50 light weapons had been stolen from the Air Force's 10 Air Depot, Air Force Headquarters said in a statement on Tuesday [8 March]. It was suspected three or four people were involved in the weekend burglary which was discovered on Monday morning. Air Force police and the South African Police [SAP] were immediately called in to investigate, the statement said, but the SAP on Tuesday denied it was involved in the investigation. The Air Force said further details could not be released at this stage because it could hamper the investigation.
