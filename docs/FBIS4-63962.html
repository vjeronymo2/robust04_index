BFN [Text] Simferopol, 25 May -- Sergey Ozerov, spokesman for the Black Sea Fleet's press center, refused to confirm reports that the Black Sea Fleet Command is preparing to transfer a tank battalion and an antiaircraft missile division from the Black Sea coastguard force, deployed in Crimea, to Sevastopol. Meanwhile, sources close to the Black Sea Fleet headquarters say an order to that effect was imminent and that the redeployment of the subunits was prevented by the information leak.
