Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ottawa, Feb 6 (AFP) -- Canada expressed outrage Sunday [6 February] over the Sarajevo marketplace attack that killed scores of civilians and left nearly 200 injured. Foreign Minister Andre Ouellet described Saturday's attack as the "deliberate killing and wounding of hundreds of innocent civilians." He added, "This latest atrocity underscores the urgent need for concerted international action to prevent further violence against the innocent civilian population of Sarajevo." Ouellet said he had consulted with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd on Sunday about a response to the attack.
