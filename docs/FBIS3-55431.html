Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] The problem of social protection of servicemen discharged from duty will continue to deteriorate this year. According to the Defense Ministry's data, there were about 10,500 unemployed officers in the republic at the end of 1993. In particular, the number of jobless servicemen amounts to 4,097 people in Minsk Oblast; in Mogilev Oblast -- 1,388; in Vitebsk Oblast -- 753; in Grodno Oblast -- 1993; and in Gomel Oblast -- 734 people.
