November 21, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRITISH GIRL DENIES GUILT IN BANGKOK HEROIN CASE

A teen-age British girl pleaded not guilty Tuesday before a juvenile court to charges of heroin smuggling.

Patricia Cahill, 17, and a friend, Karyn Smith, 19, were arrested in July on charges of possessing 58 pounds of heroin, which police said they were trying to smuggle inside liquid-soap bottles.

If found guilty, Cahill could get a life sentence. Smith has pleaded guilty and asked for mercy.
