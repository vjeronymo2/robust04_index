920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Finance & The Family: Beale Dobie's endowment hotline Beale Dobie, a company which markets secondhand endowment policies, has started a freephone service for those who wish to sell an endowment. It promises to provide a quote on the price you could receive for the policy within 48 hours of your call. Policies must have been in force for at least a quarter of their term. The number is easy to remember - simply dial 100 and ask for Freefone SURRENDER.
