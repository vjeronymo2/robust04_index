December 25, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

U.S. FORCES INVADE PANAMA, OUST NORIEGA REGIME

U.S. military forces are to be thanked for overthrowing a dictator and installing the Panamanian people's elected government. Let the Soviets criticize, they have a vested interested in dictators, and a great fear of vigorous, free democratic states.

C. MULROONEY

Los Angeles
