921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Printing industry faces more job cuts MORE THAN 80 per cent of the printing industry is not working at full capacity and more redundancies are expected, according to the British Printing Industries Federation. Half the industry shed jobs in the past three months and one in five printers expect to cut more jobs this quarter. More than 39,000 job losses were announced by manufacturers between July 16 and October 8, bringing the year's total to more than 94,000, according to the AEEU engineers and electricians' union.
