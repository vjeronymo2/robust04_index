930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Good tourist season boost for Jenners The benefits of an early and buoyant tourist season, with an added improvement in home market share enabled Jenners Princes Street, the Edinburgh department store, to more than double pre-tax profits from Pounds 437,000 to Pounds 928,000 in the half year to July 31. Jenners said the second half had started satisfactorily and a good autumn was expected but with 'our usual caution at this important time'. However, the company said it would be 'unreasonable to expect the full year to match the improvement in the first half'. Turnover grew from Pounds 15m to Pounds 16.1m. The interim dividend has been raised to 32p (25p).
