BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- An international signature campaign and other extensive activities for solidarity with the Korean people have been launched upon the lapse of one year since the great leader President Kim Il-song published "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country." More than 360 million people including heads of party, state and government and other political and public figures of India, Nepal, Syria, Egypt, Guinea, France, Austria, Peru, Nicaragua and many other countries and international and regional organizations signed the signature paper supporting the 10-point programme in April. And various forms of solidarity functions such as meeting, seminar and lecture were held and statements and appeals were issued. The International Liaison Committee for the Reunification and Peace in Korea (Cilreco) published an appeal and a signature paper on the first anniversary of the publication of the 10-point programme. The appeal said the 10-point programme put forward by President Kim Il-song is a great programme for Korean reunification indicating a way for the Korean people to found the Democratic Confederal Republic of Koryo independently and called for an international signature campaign supporting it in all countries. In response to the appeal, political parties and organizations of Ghana, Mali, Russia, the Czech Republic and many other countries formed committees for sponsoring and promoting the signature campaign supporting the 10-point programme. The signature campaign is going on worldwide.
