921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Delyn advances Reduced interest payable and an exceptional credit helped Delyn Group increase pre-tax profits from Pounds 218,000 to Pounds 500,000 in the 26 weeks to August 2. Sales were 10 per cent lower at Pounds 6m (Pounds 6.63m) and interest payable declined to Pounds 108,000 (Pounds 224,000) reflecting action to reduce borrowing levels. An exceptional Pounds 104,000 was related to the sale of surplus development land. The manufacturer of consumer packaging has raised the interim dividend to 0.5p (0.45p) on earnings per share 3.94p (1.27p).
