Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, Feb 01, BNS--The Estonian and the Russian negotiators met today for another round of talks near Moscow. On the agenda are the withdrawal of the Russian troops from Estonia, the social protection of Russian military pensioners living in Estonia, and other questions of bilateral relations. Vasily Svirin, Russia's top negotiator, said earlier Russia will now present the detailed timetable for the troop withdrawal from January through August. Svirin said the timetable contains the dates for the pullout of all units as well as the number of men serving in them. "We hope this gesture of good will inspire also Estonia to constructive action," the Russian diplomat said. As reported earlier, Estonia accepted Russia's proposal to withdraw its troops from Estonia by Aug. 31, but insisted that Russia present a detailed schedule for the pullout.
