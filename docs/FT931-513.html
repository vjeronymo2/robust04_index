930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Former Nissan UK director admits fraud MR FRANK SHANNON, the former finance director of Nissan UK, the Japanese car importers, has pleaded guilty to one charge of corporation tax fraud. His plea means he will not now appear at the Old Bailey to be tried alongside Mr Michael Hunt, the company's former deputy chairman and assistant managing director, whose trial starts on April 20. Mr Shannon will be sentenced at the end of the trial of Mr Hunt, who denies four tax fraud charges. Four other tax fraud charges against Mr Shannon will lie on the file.
