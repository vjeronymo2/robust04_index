BFN [Text] India and Angola have agreed to cooperate at multilateral fora to counter the danger posed by cross-border terrorism and fundamentalism. The issue came up for discussion at the talks between the minister of state for external affairs, Mr. Salman Khurshid, and the minister of external relations of Angola, Mr. Venacio da Moura, in New Delhi today. Mr. Moura reiterated Angola's commitment to develop all around relations with India. Mr. Khurshid hoped that the current peace talks in Lusaka will lead to an agreement to end the internal conflict in Angola.
