Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Czech President Vaclav Havel and his accompanying delegation left the country at 2300 this evening after a short visit to the country. [passage omitted] President Havel held a news conference at the end of his visit in which he reiterated his country's stance supporting all of Kuwait's just causes and the need for the Iraqi regime to implement all the international resolutions in connection with Iraq's aggression against Kuwait. President Havel said that his visit to Kuwait was aimed at exploring avenues for bolstering the good relations between the two countries in the political and economic spheres. In addition, Havel said that issues of common interest, such as the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina, were discussed.
