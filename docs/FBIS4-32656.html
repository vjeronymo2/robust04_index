BFN [Report by Antoaneta Puncheva] [Text] The National Coordinating Council of the SDS [Union of Democratic Forces] today discussed the resolution of the SDS parliamentary group analyzing the boycott of parliament and the negative assessment of a large number of SDS deputies on taking part in full parliamentary sessions on a selective basis. In the view of the National Coordinating Council, in the future authorized members of the SDS parliamentary group should enter the plenary hall of parliament to represent SDS interests. Today the SDS deputies hotly debated their strategy for three hours, but failed to reach any decision. The Ecoglasnost National Movement has disseminated an address in which it declares that it supports the decision adopted by the Sixth SDS National Conference to boycott parliament and conduct extraparliamentary actions.
