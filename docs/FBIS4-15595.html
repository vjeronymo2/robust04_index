BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 9 (SNARK) -- Three people are killed and one is seriously wounded as a result of the explosion under the last carriage of the Yerevan-Tbilisi passenger train. As SNARK was told in the information center of the Emergency Situations Department of the Republic of Armenia, the tragedy took place today at 3 a.m. during the customs examination of the train at Sadakhlo station (Georgia). The explosion brought about a fire that affected the adjacent carriages. Luckily, the passengers had managed to evacuate. The contact wire at that part of the railway is damaged. It is already being reconstructed. The victims were taken to the hospital of the frontier town Noiemberian [spelling as received].
