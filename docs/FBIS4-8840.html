BFN [Article by "(jis)": "The Establishment of a New Party"] [Text] Kolin--At its extraordinary nationwide assembly, the Movement of Retired People for Social Security decided to transform itself into a political party--Pensioners for Social Securities. This change will not reportedly have any impact on the political line and the party's decision to fight on behalf of socially endangered individuals among our people. The main stress will be laid on the existential interests of the pensioners. According to Otakar Skalsky, press spokesman of the party, thousands of new members have joined the party recently.
