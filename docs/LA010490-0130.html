January 4, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

IRAN TO CRACK DOWN ON REBELS, DRUG DEALERS

Iran's paramilitary Revolutionary Guards will launch an operation in the southeast of the country next week against rebels and drug traffickers, Tehran Radio said Wednesday.

The announcement followed a report Tuesday that paramilitary guards clashed with a 12-man gang of drug traffickers in the southeastern Iranian desert, killing five and wounding seven.
