October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

KILLER HANDS: Four serial killers, including Randy Kraft and William Bonin from Orange County, spend their free hours on San Quentin's Death Row playing bridge, says the current Vanity Fair (E1). . . . Told of the card games, Deputy Dist. Atty. Bryan Brown, who prosecuted both Kraft and Bonin, was incredulous. "We introduced evidence that Kraft had a death list of more than 65 people and Bonin had confessed to at least another 22 people, and they're up there playing bridge?" Chris Pasles and Tom McQueeney
