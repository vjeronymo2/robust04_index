940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / Nigerian union leader held Nigeria's military regime arrested Mr Frank Kokori, leader of the oil workers' union Nupeng, in Lagos yesterday on the third day of the union's strike called to demand the end of military rule and the release of Mr Moshood Abiola, whose victory in last June's presidential election was annulled, writes Paul Adams in Lagos. Mr Abiola appeared before a federal high court in Abuja yesterday and was charged with treason.
