Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by political affairs reporter Salim 'Ali Dhiyab] [Text] His Highness Crown Prince Hasan, the regent, has received Mr. Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, the minister of state for foreign affairs in Oman. He conveyed a verbal message from His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id to his brother His Majesty King Husayn, dealing with matters of shared interest and Arab and world issues of mutual concern to the two brotherly countries. Mr. Bin-'Alawi had this to say to Jordan television about the message and Oman's role in Arab fence-mending: [Begin Bin-'Alawi recording] I communciated to His Highness Prince Hasan a message from his majesty the sultan to his brother his majesty the king. The message is a link in the chain of consultations that have marked the personal relationship between their majesties on matters related to joint Arab action, Arab solidarity, and other issues. Just like Jordan, the Sultanate of Oman wants to promote inter-Arab reconcilation. We hope both their majesties' efforts will yield results in order to advance the interests of the Arab nation. [end recording] The Omani minister left Amman this afternoon concluding a visit to Jordan lasting several days. On hand to receive and bid farewell to the minister were Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, the minister of state for foreign affairs; the director general and the chief of protocal at the Foreign Ministry; and the Omani ambassador in Amman.
