BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 20 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The meeting which was supposed to be held today on the initiative of the Mongolian Movement of Voters at the Erh Choloonii Square in the Mongolian capital has not been held. The reason for that is not being released. The Mongolian Movement of Voters has issued yesterday a statement in connection with the current complicated situation in the country arising due to the political hunger strike taken at the Suhbaatar Square. The statement by the movement demanded the Mongolian Government to carry out a nationwide referendum on the issue of vote of confidence in the Great State Hural. The movement also urged to set up a commission for national concord intended for creation proper conditions for well-being of the people and all the electorate of Mongolia, developing a democratic political system and establishing social justice.
