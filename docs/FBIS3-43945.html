Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Jan 21 SAPA -- Three people were killed and at least six others were seriously injured when unknown gunmen ambushed a minibus in Vosloorus on the East Rand on Friday morning [21 January], police said. Spokesman Lt Deon Peens said unkown gunmen opened fire on the taxi from both sides of the road outside the women's hostel in the township at 6.20am. Fifty AK-47 cartridges were found on the scene. More people were apparently injured in the attack but Lt Peens could not confirm how many as they had been taken to hospital in private vehicles.
