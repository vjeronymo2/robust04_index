October 20, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SCR COMMISSIONS PLAYS BY HISPANICS

South Coast Repertory has awarded commissions for new plays to Edit Villarreal, Octavio Solis and Edwin Sanchez, all who participated in the theater's 1989 Hispanic Playwrights Project, a play development program.

The Costa Mesa theater will also publish plays by Villarreal, Sanchez and Oliver Mayer, who also took part in the project.

In addition, tickets for SCR's 10th annual production of "A Christmas Carol," running Dec. 6 through 24, go on sale at 10 a.m. Monday at the theater's box office, 655 Town Center Drive. Information: (714) 957-4033.
