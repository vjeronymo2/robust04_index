November 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: HOLIDAY WATCH

Everyone Loves a Parade: Arnold Schwarzenegger will lead the pack at the 59th annual Hollywood Christmas Parade down Hollywood Boulevard on Nov. 25. As grand marshal, Schwarzenegger joins the ranks of such previous grand marshals as Bob Hope, Jimmy Stewart and Sammy Davis Jr. Schwarzenegger will ride on a float, accompanied by young cast members from his upcoming Universal movie, "Kindergarten Cop." BETH KLEID
