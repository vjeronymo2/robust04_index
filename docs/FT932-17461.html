930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Austrian takeover is opposed Plans by Austria Tabakwerke (ATW), the state-owned tobacco monopoly, to acquire the Head sports equipment group have met opposition from the People's Party, the conservative junior partner in the Austrian coalition government, Eric Frey writes from Vienna. Mrs Maria Fekter, state secretary in the ministry of economics, said she feared a market distortion if a monopoly concern used its large profits to buy a private company.
