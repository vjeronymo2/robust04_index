930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Wool The Australian Federal Government accepted all the main recommendations of the Garnaut Wool Industry Review Committe, appointed in April to consider the wool crisis. The major wool consuming countries have expressed for stockpile disposal caused particular anxienty, but the Australian Government has only relented to the extent of postponing their introduction until July 1994. The wool market itself is firm or slightly dearer this week mainly as a result of Chinese buying for early shipment. The AWC market indicator reached 418 cents yesterday, against 411 a week before.
