March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WALK LIKE A MAN

I am very sorry that Littwin has met with such negative experiences; they can happen to anyone, of either sex. I might lend her the same advice that I lent to someone else who complained to me about the crowded conditions at our health club: "If you'd really like to see something done about the crowds here, why don't you go home?"

ALLEN R. SMITH

Los Angeles
