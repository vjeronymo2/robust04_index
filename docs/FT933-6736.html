930826 FT 26 AUG 93 / World Trade News: Taiwan link for Hicks, Muse HICKS, MUSE, a Dallas-based private investment company, yesterday became the latest foreign concern to sign a strategic alliance with Taiwan's ministry of economic affairs, reports Daniel Green in Taipei. Other companies which have done so include AT&T, the US telecommunications giant; Motorola, the communications and electronics company; General Motors, the vehicle maker; and GE, which makes aero-engines. At least five more companies, US and European, are preparing to sign similar deals with the ministry, said Mr Paul Hsu, a lawyer in the alliances.
