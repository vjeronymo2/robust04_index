930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Sheffield Insulations up to Pounds 3m In the first six months of 1993 Sheffield Insulations, the distributor of thermal insulation and related products, boosted pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.36m to Pounds 3.09m from turnover of Pounds 74.6m against Pounds 66.7m. Earnings per share were virtually doubled at 5.5p (2.8p) after tax up from Pounds 556,000 to Pounds 1.13m, while the interim dividend is maintained at 1.8p. Mr Norman Adsetts, chairman, said that all operating subsidiaries increased both profits and sales in difficult market conditions.
