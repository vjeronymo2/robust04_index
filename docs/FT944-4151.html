941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Wellman in black aided by margins Wellman, the specialist engineer, returned to pre-tax profits in the six months to the end of September. During the period the company increased in size fivefold by the Pounds 46m acquisition of businesses from FKI. However Mr Geoffrey Iley, chairman, said that the biggest impact on the figures was the improvement in sales and margins at the existing businesses. Profits were Pounds 1.88m pre-tax, against Pounds 527,000 losses from turnover of Pounds 26.6m (Pounds 9.87m). Acquisitions provided operating profits of Pounds 854,000 on turnover of Pounds 11.3m for the period from August 13. Existing activities reported turnover up 55 per cent and operating profits of Pounds 1.07m (Pounds 454,000 losses). At the end of the period the company had net cash of Pounds 2.7m. Gearing stood at 36.4 per cent 12 months earlier. From earnings of 2.7p (losses 1.8p) the interim dividend is lifted to 0.4p (0.3p).
