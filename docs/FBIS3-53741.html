Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent in the security strip reported today that an explosive device went off at 1030 today under a South Lebanese Army [SLA] patrol on the road to al-Shumariyah. At the same time, artillery fire hit the al-Shumariyah and al-Qantarah positions belonging to the SLA. Our correspondent reports that the shelling is continuing at this hour, but he did not report any injuries among the ranks of SLA. Our correspondent reports from Tyre that the Israeli forces fired four mortar shells at Wadi al-Hujayr in the central sector. They also directed raking fire from their positions in al-Qantarah Wadi al-Saluqi and Wadi al-Hujayr. At around 1015 today, Israeli warplanes broke the sound barrier over Tyre and the Palestinian camps.
