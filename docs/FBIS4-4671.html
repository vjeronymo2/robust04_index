BFN [Text] A high-level Malaysian House of Representatives delegation led by His Excellency [H.E.] House Speaker Tan Sri Datuk Mohamed Zahir bin Haji Ismail has concluded its friendly visit to Laos with glorious success. The delegation left Vientiane for Phnom Penh this morning on a special Lao Air Force place to visit to the Kingdom of Cambodia in response to an invitation. A farewell ceremony was held for the Malaysian delegation at the National Assembly Hall in the capital, Vientiane, [words indistinct]. The ceremony was attended by H.E. National Assembly Chairman Saman Vi-gnaket along with the vice chairmen of the National Assembly, National Assembly Standing Committee members, and many National Assembly members. Also attending were H.E. Abdul Wahab Harun and H.E. Khek Lerang, respectively the Malaysian and Cambodian ambassadors to Laos, together with staff members from the two embassies. During its five-day friendship visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], the high-level Malaysian House of Representatives delegation paid courtesy calls on H.E. LPDR President Nouhak Phoumsavan and H.E. LPDR Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon. The delegation also held a meeting with a high-level LPDR National Assembly delegation. It also visited many economic and cultural establishments and historical sites in Vientiane Municipality and Xieng Khouang Province.
