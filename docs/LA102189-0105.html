October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MASSIVE EARTHQUAKE JOLTS BAY AREA

I find the quick response of the Bush Administration to the San Francisco area earthquake interesting compared to the Administration virtually ignoring the Alaska oil spill, and its sluggish and inadequate response to Hurricane Hugo's devastation.

How many electoral college votes does California have in comparison with both Alaska and South Carolina? Surely federal disaster response is humanitarian -- or is it another calculated political move?

MARCELLA S. McGLOTHLIN

Malibu
