Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Ministry and National Insurance Institute officials have reached agreement on compensation to the victims of the Hebron massacre. Unmarried men who were badly injured or the family of a bachelor who was killed will receive 85,000 shekels. Payments to the families of married men will range from 120,500 shekels to a maximum of 210,000 for those with six children or more. Those who suffered less severe injuries will receive lesser sums. The compensation agreement still needs to be approved by Finance Minister Shohat.
