940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / Observer: Gloom speaks John Major's renewed interest in seeing British MPs working 'sensible hours' is not to the taste of some members of the House of Lords. The peers have recently been burning the midnight oil on the bill to privatise British Coal. Nearly nine hours after making the initial speech from the Labour front bench on Monday, Lord Morris said he was glad the House did not meet at 9 o'clock in the morning. 'I get better as the light gets darker,' he confessed. Rather like mushrooms, really.
