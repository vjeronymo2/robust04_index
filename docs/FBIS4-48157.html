BFN [Text] The NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia] nominee to the Liberian National Transitional Government [LNTG] for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mrs. Dorothy Musuleng-Cooper, has finally been accepted in government. The acceptance of Mrs. D. Musuleng-Cooper comes after several weeks of conflict, first, over which faction would take the Foreign Ministry and later, the individual nominated for the portfolio even though the NPFL was given it (?under) the Cotonou Accord.
