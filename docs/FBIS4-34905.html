BFN [From "The Voz Andes Newscast"] [Text] The country will learn the Supreme Court Constitutional Chamber's ruling on the unconstitutionality of the agrarian law dictated by the Court of Constitutional Guarantees in approximately three weeks. Constitutional Chamber Magistrate Hugo Ordonez has asserted that they will expedite the ruling but that this does not mean it will be decided with undue haste. [Begin Ordonez recording] I would not like to estimate the exact duration of this proceeding because it cannot be predetermined. We have to see if it takes the regular course. However, I surmise--and I do so only to answer the question you say currently concerns the country--that the matter could be resolved in approximately three weeks. [end recording] Ordonez stated that the verdict will not depend on the magistrates' political leanings but instead on whether the law adheres to the Constitution.
