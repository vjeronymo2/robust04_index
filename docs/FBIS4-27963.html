BFN [YONHAP Report from Geneva] [Text] North Korea made it clear yesterday hat it had test fired a missile in the East Sea, stressing that it was a routine exercise. In a news conference, (Han Cha-won), North Korea's permanent representative to the United Nations, said that the test firing of the missile was a regular and routine exercise and that North Korea's missiles are completely for defensive purposes. The Japanese and U.S. Governments revealed at the end of last month that North Korea had tested a missile -- an improved model of China's Silk Worm missiles. Until now, North Korea has not confirmed that it had test fired a missile.
