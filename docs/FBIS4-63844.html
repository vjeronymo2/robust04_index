BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Excerpt] Moscow, 24 May -- Russian diplomacy is focusing its efforts on resolving the task of bringing closer the positions taken by Damascus and Tel Aviv in order to arrive at practical accords between them as soon as possible, Grigoriy Karasin, the head of the press and information department of the Russian Foreign Ministry, said at a briefing today. In his words, Moscow believes the Israeli-Palestinian breakthrough does not a priori mean an irreversible turn in the direction of a comprehensive and just peace in the region. It is obvious that now is the time to resolutely move forward in all areas of the Arab-Israeli talks, and particularly with regard to Syria, the Foreign Ministry spokesman said. [passage omitted]
