February 14, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Joe Klecko of the Indianapolis Colts, a veteran of 12 National Football League seasons, announced his retirement, saying "little guys" like him (6-feet-3, 265 pounds) are "throwbacks to the old days."

Klecko, 35, spent 11 years with the New York Jets, earning four Pro Bowl trips, before coming to the Indianapolis Colts last season.
