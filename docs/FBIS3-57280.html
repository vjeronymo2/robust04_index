Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with a great festival of the Orthodox world, Epiphany, and to overcome the most serious political and economic crisis in Georgia, the unification of all parties and political movements representing agriculture in the republic happened today. They were joined by a number of members of the Democratic Party and the societies Lemi and Mamuli [Homeland]. A unified Agrarian Party of Georgia was founded. The head of the Agrarian Commission of the Georgian Parliament and an agricultural sciences doctor, Roin Liparteliani, was unanimously elected party chairman.
