941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / International Company News: Stelco lifted by strong demand for sheet steel Strong demand for sheet steel from the North American car industry is fuelling a turnround at Stelco, one of Canada's two biggest steel makers. Third-quarter net profits were CDollars 350m (USDollars 261m) or 48 cents a share, up from CDollars 2m, equal to a loss of 2 cents after preferred dividends, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 682m, up 11.5 per cent. Average revenue per tonne was CDollars 636 against CDollars 557. Nine-month net profit was CDollars 66m, or 57 cents, against a loss of CDollars 59m, or 74 cents, Sales were CDollars 2.05bn, up 14 per cent.
