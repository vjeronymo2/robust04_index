BFN [Text] Another six Katyusha volleys were fired at the Galilee overnight. There were no casualties and no damage was caused. Residents of the northern towns spent the night in shelters. Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports that IDF [Israel Defense Forces] contingents, including convoys of tanks and artillery, moved overnight toward the northern border. Our monitor Mikha'el Gurdus reports that Radio Beirut reported that the IDF shelled areas north of the security zone last night and that Israeli helicopter gunships carried out overflights, mainly over Tyre. The sites shelled by the IDF artillery are the locations the Katyusha rockets were launched from.
