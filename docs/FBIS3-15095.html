Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi -- The press center of the National Democratic Party [NDP] of Georgia gave IBERIA NEWS AGENCY a statement by NDP chairman Giorgi Chanturia, which says: "The grave situation in Georgia, in particular the parliament's unlawful decision on 1 March with regard to Georgia joining the CIS, has stripped the Georgian people and the whole of our population of every illusion: Georgia is being voluntarily dragged into the foundation of the new Russian empire at a phenomenal speed. "In connection with this and other issues, I resort to the widely spread practice in the West and am challenging the head of state Eduard Shevardnadze to live television debates. "As on the eve of the visit by the head of state to the United States, when it became physically possible to adopt hastily an unlawful resolution, then I consider that there will also be time to hold television debates between us, provided that the head of state, beside having arguments, has the wish to participate in debates."
