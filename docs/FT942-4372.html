940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / Pakistan extends sales tax Pakistan last night extended an existing general sales tax to cover up to 279 items, including many consumer goods, in the country's annual budget, Farhan Bokhari reports from Islamabad. The new tax will exclude food, medicines and some clothing, but a 15 per cent tax will be applied on shoes, tyres, cement, glass and steel goods. Factories with annual turnover of Rs500,000 (Pounds 35,000) must now pay the tax before goods are sold to retailers.
