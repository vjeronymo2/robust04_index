Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Katsuhiko Iino] [Text] Beijing, 22 Feb -- Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Political Bureau, met with a delegation of the Japan New Party (with Diet Member Banri Kaieda as delegation chief) currently visiting China on 22 February. During the meeting, Hu commented on Tibetan spiritual leader Dalai Lama XIV's recent proposal for a federation between the Chinese Government and a Tibetan autonomous government. Hu indicated that "the Dalai Lama has only changed his approach on the issue. There is no change in his basic stand." Thus, he disclosed that there is no change in China's stand of rejecting dialogue with the Dalai Lama.
