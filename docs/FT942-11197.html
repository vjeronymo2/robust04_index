940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Credit Suisse rating 'could be affected' Standard & Poor's, the international ratings agency, said an acquisition by CS Holding of a substantial stake in Creditanstalt-Bankverein, Austria's second largest bank, would be likely to affect the credit quality of CS Holding's main unit, Credit Suisse, Reuter reports from New York. CS Holdings has confirmed that it is interested in buying a large stake in Creditanstalt, which S&P rates A-1, from the Austrian government.
