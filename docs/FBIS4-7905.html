BFN [Text] Budapest, April 5 (MTI) -- "They should go home!", opposition MP Zoltan Trombitas of the Federation of Young Democrats (Fidesz) shouted after Deputy Speaker of Parliament Vince Voros had greeted the Chinese justice minister [Xiao Yang] and his delegation in the box of honour at Hungarian Parliament today. Late, after the scheduled departure of the Chinese delegation, Trombitas received a public warning from Voros in front of the plenary session. Fidesz sources say Trombitas was protesting against Chinese treatment of dissidents, now being arrested in China, while some leaders of the 1989 student protests are still in prison there.
