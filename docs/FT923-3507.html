920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Rathbone rises 24% and makes purchase Rathbone Brothers, the banking and asset management group, reported interim pre-tax profits ahead 24 per cent. Mr Oliver Stanley, chairman, said that there had been a considerable increase in the funds under management. The company, which moved up from the USM to a listing during June, is acquiring Hilbre Investment Management, which will add Pounds 80m to client portfolios bringing the total to Pounds 780m. The Pounds 1.31m consideration is being satisfied by shares. Profits for the six months to June 30 were Pounds 2.02m (Pounds 1.63m) on turnover of Pounds 7.84m (Pounds 5.88m). Earnings were 8.03p (6.85p). The interim dividend is raised from an adjusted 1.25p to 1.5p.
