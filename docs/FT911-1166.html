910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / International Company News: Saab to receive SKr5bn capital injection BETWEEN SKr5bn (Dollars 806m) and SKr6bn is to be poured into loss-making Saab Automobiles by the company's joint owners, the Swedish auto and aerospace group Saab-Scania and General Motors of the US. Saab-Scania's new chief executive, Mr Lars Kylberg, said the money, equal shares of which would come from each of Saab Automobile's joint owners, would go into covering the losses being suffered by the company at present and to invest in new resources over the next five to six years. Precise details of the injection of capital into the troubled company are expected later this summer.
