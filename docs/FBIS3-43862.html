Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Aldo Ajello, special representative of the UN secretary general in Mozambique, has said in Maputo that demobilized troops from the government and the Mozambique National Resistance could receive salaries for 18 to 24 months. Aldo Ajello is quoted by DOMINGO weekly as having said that the payment of salaries for 18 to 24 months will depend on the extent of the international community's contribution toward the peace process. The issue was raised last month at the meeting the Mozambican Government with the Paris Consultative Group, made up of the World Bank and donors.
