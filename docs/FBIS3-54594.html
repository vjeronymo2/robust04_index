Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Jammu and Kashmir, Pakistani troops fired six rockets toward (Degwar) village near the Line of Control this morning without any provocation. AIR [All India Radio] Punch correspondent reports that one of the unexploded rockets was picked up by a civilian which exploded injuring 14 persons, six of them seriously. The Army later launched a search operation in the area and recovered three more unexploded rockets. Punch town observed a bundh [strike] today protesting against the increasing militant activities at Pakistan's behest in the area.
