940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Wagon acquires Evercheck pref Wagon Industrial Holdings has acquired the preference share capital of Evercheck, holding company of Montan Group, from the Royal Bank of Scotland for Pounds 2.5m. The consideration will be 534,188 shares which have been conditionally placed. Wagon acquired Evercheck's ordinary share capital on March 31 for a nominal consideration.
