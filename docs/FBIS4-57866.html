BFN [Report by Darcey Quinn from the "24 Horas" newscast] [Text] Judicial branch leaders expressed their solidarity with the Constitutional Court on 25 May. Prosecutor General Gustavo de Greiff, Supreme Court President Ricardo Calvete, State Council President Amado Gutierrez, and High Council of the Judiciary President Jose Alejandro Bonivento met for a luncheon that lasted more than five hours at the apartment of Constitutional Court President Jorge Arango Mejia. [Begin recording] [Gutierrez, in progress] ...the court presidents and the nation's prosecutor general. We discussed a statement that will probably be issued on the afternoon of 26 May. [Reporter] Against President Gaviria? [Gutierrez] No, no, no. A statement simply reaffirming constitutional principles. [end recording] We learned, however, that the purpose of this inopportune meeting was to draft a communique in which the entire judicial branch protests the meddling of President Gaviria and the executive branch in the judicial branch's rulings. [Begin recording] [Reporter] Do you think the government is meddling too much in judicial branch affairs? [Gutierrez] No, I cannot tell you anything, because that is what the statement will deal with, and the statement will be issued on 26 May. [end recording]
