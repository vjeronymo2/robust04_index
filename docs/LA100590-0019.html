October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Riot in Uruguay: Hundreds of rock fans fought with riot police in Montevideo on Wednesday night after being locked out of a concert at which English guitarist Eric Clapton was performing. Five policemen were hospitalized and 50 arrests made, according to a police report. Among those arrested were three journalists sent to cover the event. BETH KLEID
