[I.D. 042294D] Marine Mammals AGENCY: National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Commerce. ACTION: Issuance of public display permit no. 915.

SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: On Thursday, July 8, 1993, notice was published in the Federal Register (58 FR 36655) that an application (P530) has been filed by Birds and Animals Unlimited, Universal Studios Florida, 1000 Universal Plaza, Orlando, FL 32837. A public display permit was requested to obtain the care and custody of two California sea lions ( Zalophus californianus ). The requested permit has been issued subject to the provisions of the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972 (16 U.S.C. 1361 et seq. ), the Regulations Governing the Taking and Importing of Marine Mammals (50 CFR part 216), and the conditions hereinafter set out.
