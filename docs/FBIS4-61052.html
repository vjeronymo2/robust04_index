BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Denis Perkin] [Text] Moscow 3 May TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left for Cairo today morning. He will attend the signing of the "Gaza-Jericho first" agreement between the Organisation of Palestine's Liberation (PLO) and Israel, which is to take place in the Egyptian capital on Wednesday [4 May]. Kozyrev will also meet U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher in Cairo. Russia and the United States are mediators in the Middle East peace process.
