940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto closed mildly higher in moderate trading, the TSE 300 index moving above the 4,200 level for the first time in more than two weeks. The index gained 11.8 at 4,206.0, while advancing issues outnumbered declines by 386 to 348. Volume totalled 56.1m shares valued at CDollars 626m. Ten of the 14 sub-indices gained ground, led by transportation, up 1.2 per cent. Forestry rose 1.1 per cent and mining was ahead 1.0 per cent.
