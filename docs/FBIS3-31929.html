Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "News Patrol" newscast] [Text] Deputy Communications Minister Jose Manuel Recinos Leiva told "News Patrol" the government was ready to discuss the increase rate on transportation charges of cement with the representatives of the National Transportation Coordinating Board, but unfortunately they withdrew from the negotiations and said they will strike on 1 February. He added that even if the members of the National Transportation Coordinating Board ignored the economic cabinet's invitation to talk, a meeting was held and the seven previously approved points were discussed and sent to Finance Minister Susana Ordonez de Molina. Ordonez de Molina coordinates the group of high-ranking authorities, and she will gather all the information to present it to the president.
