930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / Former FT home wins award THE restructured Bracken House in London, the former home of the Financial Times, is one of four winners of national awards for architecture named by the Royal Institute of British Architects. The building was designed by Michael Hopkins and Partners. The others are the Reuters Building in London, the new Stansted airport in Essex and Sackler Galleries at the Royal Academy of Arts in London.
