BFN [Excerpts] The bombing of Gorazde is continuing without respite. Shells have hit the hospital and neighboring houses. Twenty people have been killed and several injured. [passage omitted] There have been active international efforts to find a solution to the Bosnian tragedy. Francois Mitterrand has had a long telephone conversation with Bill Clinton this evening, and spoken in favor of the urgent holding of a conference between the United States, Russia, the European Union, and the United Nations. This was also demanded by Boris Yeltsin who also phoned Bill Clinton. The U.S. President is due to hold a news conference within the next few minutes.
