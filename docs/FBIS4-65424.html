BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] President Suleyman Demirel has received Spanish Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas and has conferred with him for some time. At the meeting, Demirel thanked Spain for the support it has shown for Turkey's full membership in the European Union. Recalling the measures that are being implemented in certain European countries against the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terror organization, Demirel expressed the belief that Spain will also take various measures in that direction. Vargas said that long term cooperation in various fields, primarily in the field of defense industry, can be establish between the two countries. Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan attended the meeting.
