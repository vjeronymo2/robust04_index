February 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: RESIDENTS PLAN TO FIGHT SALVAGE YARD EXPANSION

Wilmington residents are gearing up to fight a proposed expansion of an auto salvage yard, saying they want to reverse the community's longstanding reputation as the junkyard and heavy-industry capital of Los Angeles.

As an amendment to the area's community plan -- still to be approved by the Los Angeles City Council -- the proposal would increase by half the size of Ecology Auto Wrecking at 1710 Eubank Ave.
