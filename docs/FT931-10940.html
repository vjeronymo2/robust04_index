930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Pressure for VAT-free news THE government came under further pressure in the Commons yesterday not to impose VAT on newspapers and periodicals. Mr Tony Lloyd (Lab Stretford) reminded Sir John Cope, the paymaster general, that he recently stated that the government had no plans or proposals to do so. Sir John said: 'I am not taking back anything we have said. At this point we cannot discuss any tax changes so near the Budget.' Urging the government not to restrict the scope of the zero rate of VAT for short-term revenue reasons, Sir Terence Higgins (C Worthing), a former treasury minister, warned that any change would be likely to prove irreversible.
