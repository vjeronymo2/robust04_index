BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 14 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet, his wife and his party left Yangoon [Rangoon] for home this afternoon, concluding his official visit to Myanmar. The prime minister and his party were seen off by Vice Chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council Gen. Maung Aye, First Secretary of the Council, Gen. Lt. Khin Nyunt, Deputy Prime Minister of Myanmar, Lieut. Gen. Tin Tun and Foreign Minister of Myanmar Ohn Gyaw and other high-ranking officials. While in Myanmar, the prime minister and his entourage visited an oil refinery in the suburbs of Yangoon and the Yangoon University.
