940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Latin America Telmex ADRs rose Dollars 7/8 to Dollars 61-5/8 in New York, while locally the A and L shares were each up 1 per cent. Grupo Financiero Bancomer C shares led gains, rising 3.2 per cent. Autrey rose 2.4 per cent, Bancomer B series, added 2.3 per cent, and Texel, advanced 2.2 per cent. Oil monopoly Petrobras PET. SA preferred fell 1.7 percent to 169 cruzeiros reais. Mining giant Vale do Rio Doce VAL. SA rose 2.8 percent to 148 cruzeiros reais. News that the government of Espirito Santo state failed to sell a 20.7 percent stake in federal power utility Espirito Santo Centrais Eletricas (Escelsa) also contributed to the market's sluggishness.
