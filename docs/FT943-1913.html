940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Pakistan to buy French submarines The Pakistani government yesterday announced it would purchase three Agosta class 90B submarines from France in a deal worth Dollars 950m, writes Farhan Bokhari in Islamabad. The deal is one of Pakistan's largest defence purchases. The selection of French submarines was made after close evaluation of offers from Britain, France, China and Sweden. The government gave no details on the financing of the deal but said, the credit terms were favourable and repayments long-term. Delivery will be in the next four years and three submarines will be built in Karachi within a technology transfer agreement.
