January 12, 1989, Thursday, South Bay Edition

Harbor 78, Trade Tech 71 -- Despite a 15-point halftime advantage, the Los Angeles Harbor College basketball team had to endure a fierce rally by the visiting Los Angeles Trade Tech Beavers to post a 78-71 victory.

The Seahawks remain unbeaten atop the Southern California Athletic Conference at 3-0, 10-8 overall.

The Beavers came to within 3 points at 74-71 with 10 seconds remaining but could climb no closer.

Keith Billingslea scored a game-high 28 points for Harbor before fouling out late in the second half.

Jack Hamler led Trade Tech with 15 points. KEN BANKS
