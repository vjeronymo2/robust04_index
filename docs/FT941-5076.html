940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / International Company News: SNC-Lavalin SNC-Lavalin, Canadian engineering and construction management group, posted a 74 per cent gain in 1993 net profit to CDollars 16.3m (USDollars 12m), or CDollars 1.08 a share, on revenues of CDollars 797m, up 6.5 per cent, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal.
