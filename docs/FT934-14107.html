931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / Toxic fumes 'killed 300 cows' TOXIC FUMES from a chemical incinerator operated by Rechem International, the waste disposal company, caused the deaths of almost 300 cows in Scotland, the High Court heard yesterday. A Scottish farmer and his wife are seeking Pounds 750,000 compensation from Rechem. The company denies negligence in the use of its former plant at Bonnybridge, Stirlingshire. denies the claim.
