940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Reassurance for Japanese over Rover sale - Issue News Mr Michael Heseltine, the trade and industry secretary, yesterday sought to allay Japanese concern over the sale of Rover to BMW. A written parliamentary question by Mr Nicholas Winterton, Conservative MP for Macclesfield, asked what assessment had been made of the impact of the sale on the attraction of investment from Japan. Mr Heseltine answered: 'The long held welcome which this government and country has given to investment from Japan is as strong as ever.'
