January 31, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"For a long time in the party, the question has been, 'Where was Jerry?' Now the issue is, 'Who is Jerry?' "

-- Los Angeles attorney Ron DiNicola, speaking of former Gov. Edmund G. Brown Jr., a candidate for chairman of the state Democratic Party and a new convert to the anti-abortion cause. DiNicola supports a different candidate.
