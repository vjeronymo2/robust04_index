May 18, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

LAGOMARSINO ON CHILD CARE

Iam outraged that Rep. Bob Lagomarsino plans to vote against his own child-care bill, as he stated in an article in The Times on April 13.

If he is indeed concerned about the cost, then he should vote against the B-2 bomber and save enough money to fund many child-care programs. Children, our most precious possessions, are relegated to the lowest priorities of our budget.

Mr. Lagomarsino, I challenge you to rethink your values and your vote.

VIRGINIA L. UNRUHE

Ojai
