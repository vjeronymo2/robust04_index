Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Two days after its meeting in Harcha Hall [Algiers], the Conference of Scholars issued a clarifying statement signed by figures from the provisional body including Mahfouz Nahnah and Boujerra Soltani. The clarification concerns the address of Cheikh Mahfouz Nahnah in which he announced his intention to set up the Islamic Republican Rally, [TAJ] [crown]. The clarifying statement said that there was no link between the Conference of Scholars and its provisional body with what is called TAJ or with any other organization. The statement added that the body, which the Conference of Scholars intends to set up, has not yet been announced and is under study. The Conference of Scholars is a body that conforms to Islamic law and is independent from all parties, organizations, and official quarters. The statement also said that the aim of this body is to enlighten public opinion with the view of Islamic jurisdiction on the major issues of the nation in cooperation with experts, scholars, and preachers, in order to unite the word of the nation.
