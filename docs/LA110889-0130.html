November 8, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

DANA HILLS' MALL WINS SOUTH COAST GIRLS' TENNIS TITLE

Anne Mall of Dana Hills High School won the South Coast League girls' singles tennis title Tuesday at Dana Hills.

Mall, a freshman, defeated Page Bartelt, a sophomore from Capistrano Valley, 6-2, 6-2, in the final.

In doubles, Kelly Lynch and Jamie Fouret of Dana Hills defeated Farrah Tierney and Angela Arias of Capistrano Valley, 6-1, 6-1.

In the Olympic League individual finals:

Jennifer Bodourian of Orange Lutheran defeated Jenny James of Connelly, 6-1, 0-6, 6-4, to take the singles title at the La Habra Tennis Club.

Cherry Quiambao and Sharon Whang of Whitney defeated teammates Patty Kim and Frances Bacayo, 6-0, 6-4, for the doubles title.
