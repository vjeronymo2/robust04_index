BFN [Text] An official security source at the Interior Ministry has told the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', that at 1345 today brother Dr. Hasan Muhammad Makki, the first deputy prime minister, was the target of a sinful assassination attempt. According to initial investigations, five attackers driving two Toyota cars blocked the path of the brother first deputy prime minister's car as it was driving along (?al-Mathar) Street in front of the Ministry of Local Administration. They opened fire on his car with automatic weapons, wounding him in the shoulder and leg. Three of his escorts were fatally wounded. The brother Dr. Hgasan Makki was rushed to hospital, where he has been receiving treatment and is now in satisfactory condition. Security forces captured the attackers, who are now being questioned. The source expressed deep regret over this deplorable incident. He called on all citizens to exercise alertness, awareness and restraint to forestall seditions in society. He called for their cooperation to help security agencies fulfill their functions under the critical circumstances our Yemeni homeland is experiencing.
