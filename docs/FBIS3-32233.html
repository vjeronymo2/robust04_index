Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Ecuadoran economic developments monitored through 2 February. Source is cited in parentheses following each item. In a news conference, the director of the National Institute of Statistics and Census reported inflation in January was 1.75 percent, the lowest since 1982. He added that the accumulated inflation rate for the past 12 months totals 29.14 percent, which is also a record, the lowest amount in the past seven years. (Mexico City NOTIMEX in Spanish 2058 GMT 31 Jan 94)
