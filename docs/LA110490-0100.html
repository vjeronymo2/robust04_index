November 4, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PRINGLE, UMBERG INSPIRE 'NO' VOTE ON TERM LIMITS

Curt Pringle, the incumbent assemblyman from the 72nd Assembly District, admits that he and his campaign workers intentionally distributed campaign literature known to contain false statements.

In short, he admits he lied to the voters.

What's his excuse? That the material was already printed.

No wonder the California Journal, when ranking 79 Assembly members, put Pringle among the bottom 10 in integrity and the bottom 12 in brains.

KARLA LOUISE BELL, Newport Beach
