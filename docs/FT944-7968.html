941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / UK Company News: F&C Emerging Foreign & Colonial Emerging Markets Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 137.8p at September 30, taking prior charges at nominal value, against 101.7p a year earlier. For the year to the end of September there were attributable losses of Pounds 869,000 (Pounds 164,000 progits). Losses per share were 0.87p (earnings 0.16p) and the dividend has been passed. There was a single final payment of 0.27p last time.
