October 3, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY OVER BOOK ON MOSSAD

I am outraged that Arabist Cockburn can be given so much space and legitimacy to spout his longstanding total hatred of Israel.

He is not "Column Left," but "Column Hate."

How strange it is that at this moment of American crisis we should have such a concentration on the supposed duplicity of Israel. Does one have to be an expert to detect the Arabs' secret weapon? Make Israel the secret culprit and the enemy of America.

JOSEPH SIMON

Malibu
