November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGE SENTENCES ZSA ZSA GABOR TO 3 DAYS IN JAIL FOR SLAPPING A COP

Zsa Zsa Gabor must go to jail for three days, pay $13,000 in fines, do 120 hours of community service for a misdemeanor.

Oliver North was convicted of three felonies, walked away with a slap on the wrist, a small fine and 1,200 hours of public service. He too manipulated the legal process and remains unremorseful.

When the judge insists on psychiatric evaluation as part of the parole that was the final slap in the face for Miss Gabor. I do not condone Miss Gabor's behavior but there seems to be a flaw in the justice system.

ROMAINE HOOPER

Riverside
