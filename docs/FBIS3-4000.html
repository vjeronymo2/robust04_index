Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 18 (XINHUA) -- A 13-member delegation of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC) led by Zhu Qizhen, vice-chairman of the NPC Foreign Affairs Committee, left here for Paris today to attend the 91st conference of the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU). China joined in the IPU in 1984 which was founded in June, 1889.
