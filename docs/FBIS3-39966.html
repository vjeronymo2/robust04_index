Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Greek Deputy Foreign Minister Yeoryios Papandreou said it is important that Skopje is again to the fore with our EU [European Union] partners. He said it is equally important that EU Commissioner Hans van den Broek is dealing with the overall issue of Skopje and not exclusively with the economic sanctions imposed by Greece against the neighboring statelet. Papandreou made his statement upon his return today from Brussels, where, along with Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos, he briefed the EU partners on the Greek measures against Skopje.
