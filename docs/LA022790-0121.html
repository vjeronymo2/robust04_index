February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE'S RIGDON EARNS BIG WEST HONOR

Dylan Rigdon, UC Irvine freshman guard, was named the Big West Conference player of the week Monday.

Rigdon had a career-high 26 points in a 94-76 victory over Cal State Fullerton Saturday.

He made all nine of his shots, including seven three-point baskets. He also had 19 points and eight assists in a 99-77 loss to Nevada Las Vegas Thursday.

A Times' All-County guard at Mater Dei High School last season, Rigdon is averaging 18.3 points since entering the starting lineup seven games ago. Since becoming a starter, he has made 23 of 38 three-pointers (60.5%).

Irvine (4-21 overall, 2-14 in the Big West) finishes the regular season with games at San Jose State (Thursday) and Utah State (Saturday). ELLIOTT TEAFORD
