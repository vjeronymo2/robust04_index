Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cucuta -- On Wednesday, the Army frustrated a terrorist plan prepared by the Guerrilla Coordinating Board (CGR) when the 5th Mechanized Group, based in Cucuta, seized a large amount of arms and ammunition intended for the ELN [Army of National Liberation]. According to the version given by military detachment commander Colonel Henry Dominguez, the seizure dismantled a major part of the CGR's logistical structure tasked with supplying arms and quartermaster supplies to the various groups who operate in the Departments of Santander and Norte de Santander, the southern part of Cesar Department, and Bolivar Department. The operation began at 0500 [1000 GMT] on 2 February and ended 12 hours later. Alfredo Angarita Torres, "Hermes," logistic wing leader of the northeastern war front; Jesus Emilio Ovalle Rodriguez, "Chucho;" and sisters Angelina and Barbara Perez Duran were captured in house No. 3-77 on 21st Avenue, in the Tasajero neighborhood.
