931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / International Company News in Brief: Wilson and Horton WILSON and Horton, publisher of New Zealand's largest circulation paper, The New Zealand Herald, has announced a record increase in half-year profits to NZDollars 19.3m (USDollars 10.7m), and a tax-free one-for-eight bonus issue. Sales were also a record, up 15.4 per cent to NZDollars 176.6m.
