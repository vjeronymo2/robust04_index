940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Slimma In its first figures since coming to the market in February, Slimma, the women's wear company, reported a 11 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 482,000 to Pounds 535,000 for the six months to April 1. Turnover rose 20 per cent to Pounds 8.73m, against Pounds 7.26m. Earnings per share were 4.1p (3.9p) and an interim dividend of 1.3p is declared.
