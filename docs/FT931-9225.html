930215 FT 15 FEB 93 / International Company News: Finnish banks on credit watch FIVE Finnish banks have been placed on credit watch by Standard and Poor's, the US credit agency, which cites the deterioration in the Finnish economy and concern over the government's will to solve the banking sector's problems. The banks are Kansallis-Osake-Pankki, Union Bank of Finland, Skopbank Postipankki and Okobank.
