Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Here is an announcement from the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman: The IDF is significantly reinforcing its troops in Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza in the wake of the incident in Hebron. Also, a curfew has been clamped on Hebron and a closure has been imposed on the Gaza area. Chief of Staff Lieutenant General Ehud Baraq this morning expressed shock at the serious crime perpetrated in Hebron. The chief of staff sharply denounced the murders and shares in the grief of the families. The chief of staff announced that the crime would be investigated thoroughly, and that all the ensuing conclusions would be drawn. The chief of staff called on the Arab and Jewish population in Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza to evince restraint and self-control at this difficult time.
