BFN [Text] Zugdidi, 28 Jun -- The Russian Federation peacekeeping forces have deployed heavy artillery (122mm-caliber E-30 howitzers) and heavy tanks (T-72 tanks) in the security zone of the Georgian- Abkhaz conflict. No peacekeeping force, be it in former Yugoslavia or in Africa, is armed with either heavy artillery or heavy tanks since no such weaponry is necessary to carry out a peace mission.
