March 20, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SANTA PAULA;

CITY SAYS DISNEY WITHDRAWS REQUEST

Walt Disney Studios has withdrawn a request to use Santa Paula's Main Street as the set for filming of a bank robbery for a television pilot, according to city officials.

The company could not be reached for comment. Earlier this year, the Santa Paula City Council debated a proposal regulating film production in the city, with some citizens asking for an outright ban. JANET BERGAMO
