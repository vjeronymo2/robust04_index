921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / Parliament and Politics: Major to offer papers to Scott MR JOHN MAJOR said yesterday that briefing papers on Iraq he used at Commons question times when Treasury chief secretary, chancellor, foreign secretary and prime minister would be available to the Scott inquiry on exports to Iraq. Separately, Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, gave MPs copies of a letter Lord Trefgarne, former trade minister, sent in October 1989 to Mr William Waldegrave, then foreign minister. He was forced to do so by the speaker after quoting from the letter on Monday. Mr Heseltine did not release minutes of a Cabinet committee meeting in July 1990, which recommended revising guidelines on defence related sales to Iraq, to which he had also referred.
