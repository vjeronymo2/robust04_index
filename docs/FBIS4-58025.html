BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Apr (MENA)--People arriving from Libya through the al-Sallum border point have said that the Libyan authorities are currently trying to organize a trip for Libyan pilgrims on camels to perform this year's hajj. The caravan will start from Musa'id city and end in Suez city in Egypt They said that the caravan, which will consist of 300 camels, is meant to express the Jamahiriyah's protest against the air and economic blockade. It should be noted that these pilgrims will be transported from Suez port directly to Jeddah in Saudi Arabia.
