920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Ardagh tops IPounds 4m despite pressures Ardagh, the Dublin-based glass producer, made pre-tax profits of IPounds 4.21m, or Pounds 4.38m, in the 53 weeks ended June 30 1992. That compared with IPounds 4.98m for the previous year, and the directors considered it satisfactory in the light of the pressures on volumes and margins. Sales for the 53 weeks totalled IPounds 32.5m (IPounds 32.8m), while trading profit came through at IPounds 3.43m (IPounds 4.11m). Earnings per share were 11.18p (12.39p) and the final dividend is 2.39p for a total of 3.115p (3p).
