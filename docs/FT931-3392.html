930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, landed fair demand but at a generally easier rate. Coloury medium East African fannings. Grades were about steady but the remainder lost 4 to 6p, sometimes more, with dusts rather weak throughout. Offshore the market followed a similar pattern with selected medium Kenyas firm, others 2 to 4p down. The highest price realised this week was 184p for a Rwanda pd. Quotations: quality 160p/kg, nom good medium 142p/kg, medium 135p/kg, low medium 105p/kg.
