BFN [Text] The central bank, the Bank of the Republic of Haiti, has frozen nearly 15 million Haitian dollars belonging to the resigning government of Mr. Robert Malval. The funds, which have been blocked since the arrival on the scene of the provisional government of attorney Jonassaint, are for social and humanitarian programs. It should be noted that this money was provided by the U.S. Agency for International Development [USAID] and was aimed at financing USAID humanitarian projects and food and work programs for the impoverished.
