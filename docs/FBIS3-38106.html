Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Hashish Deals Busted: Two Men Imprisoned"] [Excerpt] The investigators of the drug group of the Lower Austrian Criminal Investigation Department have found out about flourishing deals with cannabis resin. Nikolaus S. (29) from Vienna-Hernals is said to have smuggled 80 to 100 kg of the "Tscharas" type of the drug from India to Austria in the past eight years. The man is currently in India. Alfred H. (37) and Wilhelm B. (38) from Vienna-Fuenfhaus were imprisoned. It is assumed that they distributed the drugs. During a search of Wilhelm B.'s apartment, 2,850 grams of cannabis resin were seized. Alfred H. is suspected of having sold heroin daily to between five and seven for several distributors in 1992 and 1993. [passage omitted]
