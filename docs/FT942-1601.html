940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Lucas in joint venture in China Lucas Aerospace, part of Lucas Industries, has signed a joint venture agreement with Taikoo Aircraft Engineering Company of Xiamen, China. The venture is to establish a repair and overhaul facility for engine and flight control systems, electrical power generation and other aerospace equipment. It will be 65 per cent owned by Lucas and 35 per cent by TAECO and total investment will be more than Dollars 10m (Pounds 6.5m).
