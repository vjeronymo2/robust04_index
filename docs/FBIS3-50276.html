Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Granic sent a letter to the German Vice Chancellor and Foreign Minister, Klaus Kinkel, following Germany's decision to repatriate Croat refugees in Germany starting from the 30 April of this year. The Croatian foreign minister thanked Germany for its efforts in caring for thousands of Croat refugees but pointed out that one-third of Croatia's territory was still under occupation so that most of the refugees cannot return to their homes. In his letter Mr. Granic called on Germany to help in the return of refugees to their homes but urged it to keep in mind that this process should be carried out in phases in accordance with the normalization of relations and the reintegration of the UN-protected areas into Croatia's legal system.
