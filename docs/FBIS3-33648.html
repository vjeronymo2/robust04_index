Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] This morning, Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and PLO Executive Committee chairman, received Mr. John McCarthy, the ambassador of the United States in Tunis. During the meeting a discussion took place covering the latest developments in the Palestinian-Israeli negotiating process and the deadlock it has reached as a result of the obstacles placed by Israel in the way of implementing the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles agreement. Brother President Abu-'Ammar has sent an appeal to U.S. President Bill Clinton asking him to intervene, as the agreement was reached under his auspices. Brother President Abu-'Ammar also asked for the intervention of the United States in its capacity as one of the two co-chairmen of the peace talks, in order to overcome the deadlock in the negotiations and attain the accurate and faithful implementation of the provisions that were approved in Washington.
