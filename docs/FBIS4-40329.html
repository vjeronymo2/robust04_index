BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 11/6/1994 -- The Moldovan Ministry of Interior is carrying out for the third day "The Square" operation. Petru Harbur, head of the Chisinau Police Commissariat, stated to BASA that the main purpose of the operation is to stabilize the crime situation in Chisinau. According to him, there was a slight increase in the level of crime in the first quarter of 1994. Measures are undertaken to detect illegally held arms, criminals from the other regions of the CIS who have settled in Chisinau, cases of violation of passport regulations and raids on the usual criminal hang-outs-bars, casinos and restaurants. According to Harbur, the first two days of the operation resulted in a two fold decrease in the number of crimes. The police has detained four persons wanted by police and discovered illegally held arms. He said that forecasts about the efficiency of the operation are thus confirmed.
