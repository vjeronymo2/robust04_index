920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Shorco declines 9% to Pounds 105,000 In fierce competition and with margins 'under severe pressure' pre-tax profits at Shorco Group Holdings fell by 9 per cent to Pounds 105,000 in the half year to June 30. However, turnover of this USM-quoted group, which operates within the depressed construction sector, increased to Pounds 3.55m (Pounds 3.26m) due to the performance of Shorco Trench Systems. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.4p on earnings per share of 2.3p (2.5p).
