Because you are a non-defaulting commercial borrower of [name of the predecessor institution], section 21A(b)(4)(C) of the Federal Home Loan Bank Act provides you with an opportunity to appeal this adverse credit decision of the Conservator. You should know that the hearer of the appeal may only reverse the adverse credit decision if the hearer determines that to do so would reduce, or would not increase, the overall losses to the RTC as Conservator.
