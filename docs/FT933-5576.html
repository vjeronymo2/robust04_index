930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Danka Business Systems: DANKA Business Systems is to pay a maximum of Dollars 50m (Pounds 33.70m) for most of the assets of Omnifax, a large independent US retail and wholesale supplier of fax machines, parts and supplies. The seller is Telautograph, a subsidiary of Arden Group. Based in Los Angeles, Omnifax has 51 branches in the US and Canada, generating total annual revenues of about Dollars 69m and pre-tax profits of Dollars 1m.
