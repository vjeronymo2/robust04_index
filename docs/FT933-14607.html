930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / China to cut metal jobs China's metal industry is the latest state-owned operation to announce a big restructuring, promising to cut 330,000 jobs during the next seven years, Reuter reports from Beijing. But few if any of the workers to be laid off by the China National Nonferrous Metals Industry Corp (CNNC) will end up jobless, the China Daily said yesterday. 'Workers made redundant are to be re-employed in service industries and other profitable businesses set up under collective ownership by CNNC's subsidiaries,' the official newspaper said.
