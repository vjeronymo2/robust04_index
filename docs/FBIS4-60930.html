BFN [Text] Among the events that could have a fairly noticeable effect on our further life is the decision by Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers on the indexation of the nominal value of accommodation vouchers. Their value has been established at 420,000 karbovantsi. Further indexation is to be carried out in proportion to changes in the nominal value of privatization property certificates. At the same time, the new value of the vouchers should be 40 percent of the new value of the certificates.
