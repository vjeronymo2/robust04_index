BFN [Itar-Tass correspondent Gennadiy Temnenkov] [Text] Bonn, 5 Jun -- Russian Prime Minister Chernomyrdin arrived in Germany earlier today. An ITAR-TASS was told by a member of his entourage that during Chernomyrdin's 5-8 June private visit to Germany that he will have a medical examination. A German foreign office official confirmed for ITAR-TASS that the Russian leader is on a private trip and has scheduled no meetings or talks with German leaders. "It is a purely private trip," the official stated.
