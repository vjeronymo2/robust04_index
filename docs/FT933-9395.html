930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was strong general demand for landed reports the Tea Brokers Association. Bright and good medium East Africans were firm to dearer but lesser mediums were barely steady. Plainer types remained unchanged. Ceylons met more competition at often dearer rates. Offshore fair demand but prices tended lower. Quotations: quality 155, nom good medium 128, medium 107, low medium 90. The highest price realised this week was 166p for a Rwanda pf. 1.
