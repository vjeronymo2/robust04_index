Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Informed sources have said that the tyrant's military units have been deployed 3 km from the border strip with the State of Kuwait in an area between Umm Qasr and Hafar al-Batin. The sources added that Saddam's authorities are now beefing up their forces deployed there. The sources said that these forces, which consist of armored battalions and artillery batteries, are being moved during nighttime from the Governorates of al-Nasiriyah and al- Samawah and from major points in al-Basrah to the border area with Kuwait. Our sources said that these units, which are mobilized in the border strip with Kuwait, are affiliated with the elite units of the Presidendial Guard which Saddam prepared recently following his continuous defeats before the sons of our revolting people.
