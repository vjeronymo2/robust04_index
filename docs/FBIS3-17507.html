Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense sources in Karabakh told Interfax that the military situation had dramatically deteriorated in the early hours of March 28. Following extensive rocket and artillery barrage, Azeri forces are reported to be attacking the eastern and southern positions of the Karabakh units in the Agdam and Fizuli Districts along a 60 km front. Scores of armored vehicles and over 1000 infantry were supported by combat aircraft and helicopters of the Azeri army. As of 12:00 local time, Karabakh forces were reported to hold their ground, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. Meanwhile, the Azeri Defense Ministry reported that the Armenian artillery had shelled villages of the Kazakh, Tauz and Ter-Ter Districts of Azerbaijan in early hours of Monday. There was no word of casualties. According to the ministry, three Karabakh tanks and one armored combat vehicles were destroyed during the last two days. The Azeri ministry reports that Azeri troops had captured strategically important positions in the Kelbajar District of Azerbaijan which is controlled by Armenian troops.
