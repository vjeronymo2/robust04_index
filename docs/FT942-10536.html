940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / UK Company News: First-quarter loss of Dollars 5.1m for Sotheby's Sotheby's Holdings, the parent company of Sotheby's New York-based auction, finance and real estate operations, suffered net losses of Dollars 5.1m (Pounds 3.5m) for the first quarter of 1994, against Dollars 5.2m. Auction sales amounted to Dollars 161.3m (Dollars 129.6m).
