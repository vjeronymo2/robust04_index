November 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MCDONALD'S WILL DO AWAY WITH FOAM BOXES

Kudos to Rockwell for his tongue-in-cheek parody of moronic anti-environmentalism, complete with pointedly brain-dead humor attempts and justifications for swelling our landfills with Styrofoam (not to mention raping the ozone layer). It was tongue in cheek, wasn't it?

DOUG WILSON, Moorpark
