940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / International Company News: Macronix to build NTDollars 30bn factory Taiwan's Macronix International, a manufacturer of integrated circuits, plans to build a NTDollars 30bn (USDollars 1.13bn) factory to make eight-inch silicon wafers, which are used in the production of chips. Construction of the facility would begin in 1995 or 1996, according to Mr Miin Wu, president. The company plans to list on the Taiwan Stock Exchange later this year.
