Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Admiral Feliks Gromov, commander in chief of the Russian Navy, has summed up the results of the first Russian-Kuwaiti naval exercises, which were held off the coast of Kuwait at the end of last year. At a meeting with journalists on his return from the amirate, Admiral Gromov stated that the maneuvers were successful, actions of all the subunits were assessed as positive, and the tasks of the exercises were fulfilled. Feliks Gromov noted that the maneuvers were held within the context of strengthening relations between the two countries and were aimed at increasing Kuwait's defense capability.
