Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tegucigalpa, 10 Feb (NOTIMEX) -- The Honduran Government today expressed regret over the death of Colombian Ambassador to Honduras Lucelly Garcia de Montoya, who was murdered by unidentified individuals early today near Ibague, Colombia. Honduran Foreign Minister Ernesto Paz Aguilar said that his country's government sent "its most sincere condolences" in the wake of the diplomat's murder. This incident "reflects the dramatic situation facing the Colombian nation, but we are sure they will move forward," he added. Garcia de Montoya, 60, accompanied Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo to the inaugural of new Honduran President Carlos Roberto Reina on 27 January in Tegucigalpa. The foreign minister said that the Honduran president had already contacted his Colombian counterpart to condemn the incident and convey the condolences of the Honduran people and government.
