940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / George Walker trial to begin today The trial of Mr George Walker, former chairman of property and leisure group Brent Walker, begins today at Chichester Rents, the Old Bailey annexe used for fraud trials. Mr Walker faces four charges of theft involving a total of Pounds 17m, two charges of false accounting and one of conspiring to falsify the Brent Walker accounts to overstate the company's profits and assets. His co-defendant, Mr Wilfred Aquilina, a former Brent Walker group finance director, faces one charge of theft involving Pounds 4.5m, three charges of false accounting and one of conspiring to falsify the Brent Walker accounts. Both men deny all charges.
