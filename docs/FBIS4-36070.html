BFN [Text] The director general of the Yemeni oil company, Mr. 'Umar Ahmad Salih, affirmed that, in spite of the fuel depot that has been set on fire as a result of the aggressive air raid carried out by the al-Ahmar gang warplanes against Aden refinery, the vital economic installation in the Democratic Republic of Yemen, to prevent it from carrying out its services roles to the citizens, Yemeni oil company is to continue its activities to provide all the citizens with petroleum and its products in all stations in Aden Governorate, as usual. He pointed out that workers will double their productive and service activities, in order to provide better services for the citizens round the clock. At the end of his statement, the director general of the Yemeni oil company appealed to the citizens not to be worried about what has happened today.
