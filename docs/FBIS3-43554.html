Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just received the news that the Inkatha Freedom Party's [IFP] Central Committee has decided the party will definitely not take part in the 27 April election. It will also not participate in any structure of the transitional government. The Central Committee reiterated during its meeting at Ulundi that it will not participate in the Transitional Executive Council [TEC], nor will it recognize any decisions by the TEC. According to the IFP, it will only reconsider its stance when its constitutional proposals, as contained in the Freedom Alliance's document of 19 December, is accepted by the negotiating council.
