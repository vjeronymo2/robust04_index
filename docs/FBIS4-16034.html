BFN [Text] Moscow, April 13 (AFP) -- Russia warned Wednesday [13 April] it would oppose any fresh NATO ultimatums to Bosnian Serb forces if not consulted beforehand and called for urgent international efforts to defuse military tensions in Bosnia. "We insist on unconditional respect of the procedure for using aviation in Bosnia approved by the UN Security Council, which calls for consultations between the UN secretary general and members of the Security Council," the Russian Foreign Ministry said in a statement.
