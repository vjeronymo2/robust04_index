930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / Regulator presses British Gas SIR JAMES McKinnon, the gas industry regulator, yesterday proposed improved service levels and better promotion of energy efficiency at British Gas. Putting forward two consultation documents, he said British Gas was performing better, but it was vital to build on improvements. The first of the two documents gives details of levels of service to be provided. The second describes ways for British Gas to promote energy efficiency.
