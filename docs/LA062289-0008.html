June 22, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

KNIGHT REAGAN

Ronald Reagan receives a knighthood from Buckingham Palace, and cartoonist Conrad manages to turn even that into an insult ("Now, is this in lieu of my usual $50,000 speaking fee?" June 19).

Is there no end to Conrad's vilification of Reagan? That's a rhetorical question. I already know the answer.

TONY THOMAS

Burbank
