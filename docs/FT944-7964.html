941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Albert Fisher sales Albert Fisher, the food processing and distribution company, has sold Delby's and Quality Food Products to Pietercil Resta Foodbrokers, a Belgian broker, for BFr213m (Pounds 4.3m) in cash. Delby's has also repaid intra-group debt of BFr211m to Albert Fisher. Delby's and Quality Food are importers and distributors of branded and own-label grocery products in Belgium and Luxembourg.
