Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The ministry of internal affairs of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia said today that a firing squad of the 505th Buzim Brigade of the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army, commanded by Izet Nanic, executed nine civilians in the Stabandza area of Cazin municipality, the Western Bosnian News Agency reports. According to the same source, the fate of several arrested civilians is unknown.
