Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Durban Feb 28 SAPA -- Police detonated an explosive device at the Inkatha Freedom Party's [IFP] headquarters in Durban on Monday morning, IFP spokesman Ed Tillet said. Police spokesman Maj Bala Naidoo was not immediately available to comment, but police had cordoned off the area around the IFP office in Albany Grove and were apparently attending to another "bomb" in a nearby parking lot, said Mr Tillet. "The first bomb was in the building," said Mr Tillet."The other was outside in the garage where transport for our national headquarters is kept."
