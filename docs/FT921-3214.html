920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Observer: Won't do nicely A colleague shopping for a new television in London's Tottenham Court Road, home of cheap consumer electronics, was told the set of his choice would cost him a mere Pounds 175 - until he brought out his American Express card. 'Haven't you got Visa?', said the shopkeeper. 'I can't go down to Pounds 175 on American Express.'
