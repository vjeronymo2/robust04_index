931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / Observer: Expensive add-on Such has been the transformation of Berisford International, the one-time commodities and property group which nearly went bust with Pounds 1.2bn of debt, that it has had to buy some new software for its computer. Doubtless, chief executive Alan Bowkett is jesting when he explains that the changeover was due to the fact that the computer simply could not cope with pluses instead of minuses in its cash line.
