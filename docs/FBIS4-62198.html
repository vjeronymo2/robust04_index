BFN [Text] Talks in Sochi on the return of refugees, which were attended by Georgian and Abkhazian delegations, by representatives of Russia and the United Nations, and by experts have ended. Igor Syzov, Sochi city's press secretary, talked to a KONTAKT news agency correspondent today about the talks. [Begin Syzov recording in Russian] The third session of the Commission for Refugees was held here and a protocol adopted. The regulations on rules and the procedure for working out recommendations, prepared by a working group of experts, were confirmed. These recommendations define the rules for creating conditions for the voluntary, safe, and proper return of refugees. The agenda for the commission's next session, which is planned for 30 May in Sochi, has been approved. The meeting proceeded in a constructive atmosphere. [end recording]
