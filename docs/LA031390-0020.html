March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SANTA PAULA;

CITY SEEKS TO REPLACE OUTGOING OFFICIAL

Santa Paula city officials are looking for a successor to the assistant city administrator, Steve Rogers, who resigned last week.

Rogers accepted a position as assistant to the city administrator in Ann Arbor, Mich., City Administrator Bob Livingston said Monday.

After working with Santa Paula for three years, Rogers, 27, was responsible primarily for the Personnel Department. His annual salary was about $33,500, Livingston said.

The deadline for applications is April 6. Rogers is expected to leave the Santa Paula job the same day. PSYCHE PASCUAL
