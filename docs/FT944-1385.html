941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, good general demand. Coloury Assams met less competition and prices declined 3 to 6 pence. However, plainer descriptions were strong and advanced 2 to 4 pence. Bright East Africans were 2 to 4 pence easier except pekoe dusts which were again strong and often dearer. Coloury mediums opened firm to dearer but closed slightly lower. Ceylons were about steady. Good demand at about steady rates. Quotations: best available 160p/kg. nom, good 132p/kg., good medium 122p/kg., medium 106p/kg., low medium 80p/kg., The highest price realised this week was 174p for a Kenya pd.
