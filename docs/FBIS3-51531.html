Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tegucigalpa -- President-elect Carlos Roberto Reina yesterday confirmed the appointment of three more ministers who will be ratified upon his return from the United States, where he is conducting an official visit. Juan Ferrara, current president of the Honduran Council for Private Enterprise, COHEP, will be the new finance and public credit minister, while banker Delmer Urbizo Panting will occupy the post that Carlos Chain will vacate in the Economy and Commerce Ministry on 27 January. In addition, sociologist and economist Guillermo Molina Chocano will be the new planning, coordination, and budget minister. Ferrara, Urbizo, Molina, and Hugo Noe Pine, Central Bank of Honduras president, accompanied President-elect Reina to Washington as members of the economic cabinet. Reina said that the full cabinet will be announced after his meeting with former Liberal Party presidential hopefuls. These three new ministers join those who were confirmed: Ernesto Paz Aguilar at the Foreign Affairs Ministry, Ramon Villeda Bermudez at the Natural Resources Ministry, and Enrique Samayoa at the Public Health and Social Aid Ministry.
