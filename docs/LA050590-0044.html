May 5, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

FIRSICH WINS TWICE AT VENTURA RACEWAY

Jim Firsich of Port Hueneme won both the Street Stock and figure-eight main events at Ventura Raceway on Friday night.

Eric Griffin of Oxnard led the tightly contested 15-lap Street Stock main event until the final turn.

Griffin, Don Green of Ventura and Dave Hume of Ventura all tangled high in Turn 4 and Firsich cut underneath the careening cars to beat Griffin by a car length. Hume was third.

Firsich moved within 10 points of the figure-eight season points leader, John Csorba of Oxnard, with a much easier victory in the 12-lap figure-eight main event.

Firsich led wire-to-wire as Csorba missed the race because of a broken rear axle on his Monte Carlo.

The break was sustained in an earlier heat race.

Cory Kruseman of Ventura won his second consecutive 12-lap Mini-Stock figure-eight main event.
