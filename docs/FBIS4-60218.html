BFN ["Decree No. 1" issued by the National Salvation Council in Aden on 21 May] [Text] In the name of the people, recognizing Articles 12 and 14, paragraph A of the declaration of the establishment of the Democratic Republic of Yemen; confirming the provisional National Salvation Council's statement endorsed in the meeting held on 21 May 1994 regarding the appointment of a speaker for the council, the following has been decided: Article 1: Brother Anis Hasan Yahya is to be appointed speaker of the provisional National Salvation Council, Article 2: The speaker will be entrusted to take charge of the power and duties in accordance with the provisions of the declaration of the establishment of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, Articles 3: This statement is effective as of the date of its issuance, Article 4: This statement shall be published in the official gazette.
