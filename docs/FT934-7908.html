931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Birmingham waste plant planned A JOINT venture including Birmingham City Council plans to build an Pounds 85m plant to treat 350,000 tonnes of domestic waste and generate 25MW of electricity. The plant will be built and run by Onyx and Esys-Montenay, two companies in Compagnie Generale des Eaux, the French group, together with the council.
