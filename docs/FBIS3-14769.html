Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow February 25 TASS -- A report of the Federation Council's Commission for Studies of the situation in Northern ossetia and Ingushetia was presented today to the Upper Chamber of the Russian Parliament by Commission head Valeriy Fateyev. In his opinion, the main regional problem is refugees. Fateyev stressed the Ingush and North ossetian presidents can agree between themselves on the return of refugees to places of their permanent residence. North Ossetian president Akhsarbek galazov supported the pronouncement and said "the interested sides should have a chance to agree between themselves and the federal bodies should listen to the opinion of regional representatives". He added they have agreed with Ingush leader Ruslan Aushev on further negotiations. Aushev agreed with Galazov, but said "they should remember about refugees". In his words, the participation in the conciliatory commission has brought no results and the North Ossetian president should name the exact date of his solution of the Ingush refugee problem. The Russian Parliament deputies decided to continue the problem analysis in committees and discuss their proposals at the chamber meeting.
