920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Trace Computers rises to Pounds 221,000 Trace Computers, the software house, reported increased pre-tax profits of Pounds 221,000 for the six months to November 30 1991 compared with Pounds 31,000. Turnover slipped slightly to Pounds 9.71m (Pounds 9.95m). Earnings per share amounted to 1.59p (0.06p) and there is a same-again interim of 0.55p.
