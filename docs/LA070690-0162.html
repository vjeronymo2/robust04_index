July 6, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

PRINCESS DIANA TO BECOME AUNT

Viscount Althorp, the NBC television correspondent and brother of the Princess of Wales, and his wife are expecting their first child in January.

"We are both delighted," Althorp, 26, said Thursday.

Althorp and fashion model Victoria Lockwood, 25, were married in September at his family home near Northampton, 70 miles northwest of London.

Lord Althorp, Charles Edward Maurice Spencer, is the brother of the former Lady Diana Spencer, who is married to the heir to the British throne, Prince Charles. Lord Althorp joined NBC in 1987.
