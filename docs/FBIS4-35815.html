BFN [Text] Tunis, 6 Jun (MENA) -- Last night Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa received Mahmud 'Abbas, alias Abu-Mazin, PLO Executive Committee member; and Ahmad Quray', minister of economy and trade for the Palestinian National Authority. They reviewed the Palestinian-Israeli peace process and the steps taken so far by the PLO to implement the Palestinian self-rule agreement in Gaza and Jericho signed in Cairo on 4 May. Musa and the Palestinian officials also discussed the difficulties and obstacles facing the completion of the implementation of the agreement and the return of PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and the members of the Palestinian leadership to Jericho and Gaza. The difficulties and obstacles are represented by the lack of necessary funds for the Palestinian National Authority. So far the Authority has not received the funds promised by donor countries.
