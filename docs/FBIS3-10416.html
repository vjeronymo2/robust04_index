Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] They are all for the Washington plan except the people's organizations, perhaps those very close to the president in exile. The political crisis settlement plan that arose from the Washington negotiations and was approved by a majority of deputies in a parliamentary session on 2 March seems to be gathering a maximum of support. The Haitian employer sector also is backing the document for which CONACOM [National Congress Of Democratic Movements], a party close to the president in exile, also gave its support to the parliament. The other political parties are still waiting and have refrained from taking a stand on what the U.S. Embassy spokesman in Port-au-Prince sees as a step toward the exit door in this long, Haitian crisis.
