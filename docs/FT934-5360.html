931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Exceptional boost for Dakota Dakota, the Dublin-based packaging and stationery group, swung from losses of IPounds 2.3m to profits of IPounds 1.85m (Pounds 1.76m) pre-tax for the year to end-September. The figure took account of an exceptional net gain of IPounds 1.11m (IPounds 1.22m loss). Turnover of IPounds 21.8m (IPounds 26.9m) included IPounds 4.67m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share improved to 6p (losses 8.6p).
