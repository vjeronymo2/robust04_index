April 23, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

50 FEARED DEAD, 1,000 HURT IN FIERCE BANGLADESH STORM

A severe storm in northern Bangladesh may have killed up to 50 people and injured more than 1,000, officials said Sunday.

Rescuers have found 30 bodies, and at least 20 people were still missing 40 hours after Friday night's storm, which packed winds of 85 m.p.h., authorities said.
