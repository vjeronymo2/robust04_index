920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: NZ kiwifruit hit by European glut A COLLAPSE of fruit prices in Europe due to a glut of supplies has forced the New Zealand Kiwifruit Marketing Board to cancel further progress payments to orchardists for three months, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. The board is the sole exporter to markets outside New Zealand and Australia. It said a 50 per cent increase in 'lower quality' Chilean kiwi-fruit and an expected boost in Italian production this season had compounded the problem.
