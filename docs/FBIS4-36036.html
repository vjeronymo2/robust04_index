BFN [Text] The National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] held a session this morning under the chairmanship of Mr. Anis Hasan Yahya, the chairman of the assembly. The session began with the national anthem and a recitation from the holy Koran and a minute's pause to mourn the martyrs. At the beginning of the session, the members of the National Assembly were sworn in and then the presidium of the assembly was elected of four members as follows: Sha'fil Umar 'Ali, Ahmad Ubayd Bin Daghr, 'Abd-al-Qawi Muhammad Rashad, and 'Abd-al-Rahim Asim. Mr. Anis Hasan Yahya was elected as speaker of the assembly. The members of the assembly decided to change the name of the provisional assembly into the National Assembly in order to facilitate dealings between it and the state institutions. The National Assembly discussed with vitality the tasks designated to it. It was decided that the assembly continue its sessions in order to review the remaining topics of its agenda after the assembly had decided that it is in a state of permanent session. The attendances [as heard] greeted the big role played by the sons of the DRY to repel the Sanaa rulers' forces, which continued aggression. They praised the role of the Security Council and the positive role played by the Arab countries to stop bloodshed.
