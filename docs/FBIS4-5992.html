BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 17 (KCNA) -- The construction of the Sinyon Joint-Venture Company run by Sinuiju City, a border city in the Northwestern part of Korea, and by the Yanbian Autonomous Region of China was completed and it went into operation on the occasion of the significant April fete. The company will help increase the production of daily necessities and develop economic exchange and friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and China. Its commissioning ceremony took place in Sinuiju on April 15.
