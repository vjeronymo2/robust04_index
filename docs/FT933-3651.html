930913 FT 13 SEP 93 / Lib Dems outline film proposals The Liberal Democrats today will outline a package of measures designed to revitalise the British film industry. Mr Robert Maclennan, Liberal Democrat National Heritage spokesman, said: 'Our objective is to encourage new investment in film production, both from home and abroad.' The number of films being made in Britain continues to fall, the policy document says - from 57 in 1991 to 46 last year.
