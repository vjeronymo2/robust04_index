920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / Armenia says peace talks off Peace talks between the Armenian and Azeri leaders on the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict have been scrapped, an Armenian presidential spokesman said yesterday, Reuter reports from Yerevan, Armenia. Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosian and interim Azeri head of state, Mr Yagub Mamedov, were due to discuss the conflict in Kiev today, the eve of a summit of the 11 leaders of the Commonwealth of Independent States
