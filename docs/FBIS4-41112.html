BFN [Text] Mission Control Centre June 21 TASS -- The Russian scientific research space complex "Mir" continues its flight with the 15th main space expedition members on board. On Tuesday the crew spent a major part of its working time on preventive measures of the space station's temperature control systems, the mission control centre informed ITAR-TASS today. During the day the cosmonauts will prepare for their forthcoming experiments with the "Trek" apparatus, which is meant for research of streams of superheavy nuclei of space rays, and the "Kaf-1000" camera. There are also plans to conduct medical checkup of the cosmonauts. According to telemtric data and crew reports, onboard systems of the orbital complex are functioning normally. The health of cosmonauts Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachev and Valeriy Polyakov is good.
