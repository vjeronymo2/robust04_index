Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat met Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa over a luncheon banquet at the Foreign Ministry today. Members of the Palestinian delegation accompanying 'Arafat, who arrived today in Cairo from Saudi Arabia, also attended. Egypt and the PLO today signed an agreement for economic cooperation. Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Faruq Qaddumi, the head of the PLO's Political Department, signed the agreement. The agreement involves measures to encourage the exchange of national products, calls for settling transactions in convertible currencies, calls for preferential treatment for national products from both sides, and urges mutual incentives for investment and capital movement. The two sides agreed to form a joint committee for technical and economic cooperation cochaired by the foreign ministers of both countries [fi al-baladayn].
