Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Lima EL PERUANO in Spanish on 5 January on page A5 in an unattributed 150-word article reports that Army commander General Nicolas de Bari Hermoza Rios on 4 January presided over the swearing in of Brigade General Alfredo Rodriguez Riveros as new commander of the Upper Huallaga Front, replacing Brigade General Eduardo Bellido Mora. The Army commander also presided over the swearing in of Brigade General Vladimiro Lopez Trigoso as 5th Infantry Division commander, replacing Brigade General Carlos Grigoleto Cortijo.
