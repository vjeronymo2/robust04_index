BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Jun (MENA) -- A responsible security official has denied a report by a news agency on a recent clash in the town of Akhmim in Suhaj Governorate between worshipers at al-Sahabah Mosque and a number of extremist elements. The source said this report is totally false. There is no truth in it, since no confrontation or clashes of any kind took place at all, as the agency claimed. According to the news agency report, six persons, including two extremists, were injured at al-Sahabah Mosque in Akhmim during a confrontation between worshipers and elements described as terrorist.
