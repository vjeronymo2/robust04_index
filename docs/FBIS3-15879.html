Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- Supporters of Kazakhstan President Nursultan Nazarbayev overwhelmed opposition in republic's parliamentary elections held on March 7. Only 23 seats in the new 177-seat legislature went to representatives of different opposition parties, according to the data released on Thursday by the Kazakh Embassy in Moscow. About 75 per cent of Kazakhstan's voters took part in the polls, the release said.
