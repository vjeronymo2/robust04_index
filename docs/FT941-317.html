940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / International Company News: Doubt over Swedish paper group buy Svenska Cellulosa, the Swedish pulp and paper group, said it would not proceed with its purchase of an 89.8 per cent stake in the French packaging company Otor Holding unless it could also acquire the remaining 10.2 per cent held by Stora Billerud, a subsidiary of Stora AB, Europe's largest forest products group, AP-DJ reports from Stockholm.
