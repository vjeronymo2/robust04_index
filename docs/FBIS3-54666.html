Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Kuchina] [Text] Riga January 2 TASS -- After studies in Germany ex-Latvian Premier Ivars Godmanis realized Latvians have little economic knowledge. It was the first detailed TV interview of Godmanis after the resignation of his government. The ex-premier said he will no longer involve in politics after the Popular Front's defeat at the summer parliament elections. Godmanis admitted the reform methods were merciless.
