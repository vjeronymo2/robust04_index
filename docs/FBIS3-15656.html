Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that the Armenian Armed Forces subjected our Army's positions in Fizulinskiy Rayon to intense artillery shelling as of 0830 yesterday morning. Following the artillery barrage, the enemy launched an attack supported by four tanks and over 100 men and reoccupied the rayon town of Ashagy-Seidakhmedli. After suffering many losses in the fighting, the enemy is bringing in reinforcements to this sector in an effort to press ahead with the offensive. Fierce fighting is continuing in Fizulinskiy Rayon. At the same time, the Armenians opened artillery fire on our positions in Agdamskiy Rayon. The Armenian Armed Forces units continued unsuccessful attempts yesterday to capture the Omar Pass in the Murov mountain range. Over 70 enemy troops were killed in this sector in the past two days.
