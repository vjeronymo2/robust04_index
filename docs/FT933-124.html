930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Capital Industries Acquisitions enabled Capital Industries, which is involved in financial services and the manufacture of laminated paper and aluminium products, to report pre-tax profits up from Pounds 988,000 to Pounds 2.41m for the half-year to the end of June. Turnover was Pounds 34.6m, including Pounds 227,000 from acquisitions, against Pounds 12.3m, some Pounds 6.38m of which came from discontinued activities. The pre-tax figure was struck after finance charges of Pounds 386,000 (Pounds 1,000 income). Earnings per share were 6.9p (3.6p) and the interim dividend is 2p (1.6p).
