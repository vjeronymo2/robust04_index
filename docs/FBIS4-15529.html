BFN [Text] A routine meeting of the city council [kenesh] of people's deputies was held today in Bishkek. The meeting examined the following issues: 1. Implementation of the program for socioeconomic reforms and anticrisis measures in Bishkek, which was adopted on 2 April 1993 at the 15th session of the Bishkek city council of people's deputies; amendments to the program in the light of the Kyrgyz Government's 24 April 1994 resolution No. 84 for furthering economic reforms and measures aimed at putting into practice a presidential decree of 10 April 1994; and urgent measures aimed at strengthening state and financial discipline. 2. Endorsement of a comprehensive program by the Bishkek city administration for the targeted social protection of citizens in 1994-95.
