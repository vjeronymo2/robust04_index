BFN [Text] Pristina, April 6 (ATA) -- Serbian Army have deployed troops nearby the Mulliq village in Gjakova Commune along the Kosova-Albania border line, the Gjakova information centre reports. Since last Sunday a military convoy with cannons was deployed near the Frasheri village in Mitrovice Commune. They are conducting military exercises and provoking the inhabitants, states Kosova information centre.
