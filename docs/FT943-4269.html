940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Alliance Resources Alliance Resources, the US-based oil and gas exploration and production company, reported it had cut losses from Dollars 1.63m to Dollars 1.18m (Pounds 760,000) in the year to April 30. Turnover grew from Dollars 631,000 to Dollars 837,000. Interest payable and similar charges were cut to Dollars 125,000 (Dollars 198,000) and losses per share came out at 0.01 cents (0.09 cents).
