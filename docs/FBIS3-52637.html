Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Our country's ambassador to Afghanistan [Gholamreza Haddadi] said today that Afghan President Borhanoddin Rabbani has welcomed the Islamic Republic of Iran's mediation; he has also announced that he is ready to cooperate in an effort to solve Afghanistan's political problems. According to the report of the Central News Unit from Kabul, the ambassador, who was speaking after a meeting with the Afghan president, spoke of more than 2,000 wounded and killed in the battles during the past six days. He added: Under present conditions the most humanitarian and Islamic measure to be adopted in Afghanistan is to put an end to hostilities. Mr. Haddadi stressed that the embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran is making every effort to contact the leaders of warring factions in an attempt to prepare the ground for negotiations among them.
