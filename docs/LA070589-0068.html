July 5, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"We were going to have a (Fourth of July) barbecue. But I guess we had one already. Somehow the fire jumped right over the house.

-- Tammy Wigington after the Puente Hills wildfire swept through her property, charring the jacuzzi and redwood patio furniture but sparing the house on Teton Drive.
