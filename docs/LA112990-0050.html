November 29, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

LOCALLY GROWNPRODUCE AND SEAFOOD

* Seaside Banana Garden -- Offers 50 varieties of locally grown bananas. 6823 Santa Barbara Ave., Ventura.

Call 643-4061.

* Ventura Farmers Market -- Wide variety of locally grown fruits and vegetables, plus produce and other goods brought in by growers from surrounding counties. 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. Wednesdays at Main Street and Mills Road, and 8:30 a.m. to noon Saturdays at Santa Clara and Figueroa streets in Ventura. Call 529-6266.

* Cal Pacifica Seafood-Watch this week for a fresh catch of local halibut. 36 Franklin Lane, Ventura. Call 643-2586.
