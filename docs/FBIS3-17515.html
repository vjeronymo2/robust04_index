Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a decree from President Islam Karimov, a specialized joint-stock commercial bank to assist trade and commerce will be created in Uzbekistan. The new bank will service trade organizations and enact the crediting of trade-purchase operations between enterprizes of trade and other branches of the national economy. According to the presidential decree, the founders of the bank will be the Finance Ministry, the Central Bank, the National Bank of Foreign Economic Activity, the Republican Trade Association, and the Union of Consumer Societies.
