931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Drew Scientific falls into Pounds 0.84m loss As foreshadowed in October and reflecting reliability problems with a specific bought-in component, Drew Scientific Group, the analytical instrument designer and manufacturer, suffered pre-tax losses of Pounds 842,000 for the half year to September 30, compared with Pounds 151,000 profits. The loss includes Pounds 248,000 attributable to continuing research and development expenditure and Pounds 94,000 associated with relocation of activities and rectification of instruments. Turnover dropped from Pounds 1.75m to Pounds 420,000. Losses per share were 3.44p and there is again no dividend.
