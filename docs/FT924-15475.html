921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / UK Company News: B Elliott suspended Shares in B Elliott, the machine tool and engineering company, were suspended yesterday following warnings that it expected to make significant losses for the year to April 3. The company, which has seen its share price fall in the past 18 months from 80p to 3p yesterday, announced the suspension while talks continued with lending banks on restructuring the group. The talks were initiated earlier this year following interim results which showed a Pounds 2.6m trading loss in the machine tools business.
