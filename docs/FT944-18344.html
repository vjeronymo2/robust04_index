941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Densitron up 45% Pre-tax profits of Densitron International, the electronic components group, rose by 45 per cent from Pounds 251,000 to Pounds 365,000 in the six months to June 30. Turnover grew from Pounds 21.3m to Pounds 22.1m and the pre-tax advance was achieved despite reorganisation costs of Pounds 104,000 in its Japanese operation. Earnings per share came out at 1.13p (1.12p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
