BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, has received a congratulatory cable from his brother Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, amir of the State of Bahrain, on the fourth anniversary of reunification and the establishment of the Republic of Yemen. The cable reads: His Excellency brother 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Republic of Yemen: On the national day of the sisterly Republic of Yemen, we are pleased to send to your excellency our heartfelt congratulations, asking the Almighty God to grant you good health and happiness and the Yemeni people wellbeing and prosperity. The chairman of the Presidential Council sent a thank-you cable to his highness the amir of Bahrain.
