930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / Poles pass budget Poland's lower house of parliament yesterday approved the budget by 23 votes after Ms Hanna Suchocka, the prime minister, threatened to resign, writes Christopher Bobinski. The budget goes to the Senate for approval next week. Acceptance of the budget paves the way for a Dollars 660m (Pounds 437m) IMF standby agreement and further debt reductions by western governments and commercial banks, and for World Bank loans of Dollars 500m.
