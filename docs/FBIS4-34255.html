BFN [Text] Mexico City, 16 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in the southeastern Mexican State of Chiapas today announced his decision to leave that presidential post and return to private life. President Carlos Salinas de Gortari designated Camacho Solis peace commissioner on 10 January to seek reconciliation with the armed group that emerged in the north-central area of Chiapas on 1 January to fight for the demands of indigenous groups.
