June 17, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

A MOTTO FOR COUNTY

Orange County's motto: As long as I have mine, to hell with everyone else!

I guess it should come as no surprise that, once again, Orange County, the bastion of conservatism, greed and self-indulgence, goes against the homeless bond (Proposition 107) and two education bonds -- school districts (Proposition 121) and higher education (Proposition 123) -- while the rest of the state voters approved them.

PAMELA D. SMITH

Dana Point
