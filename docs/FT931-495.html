930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Rise in graduates of new universities the former polytechnics - will rise by more than half between 1991 and next year, says the Graduate Employment Service, the co-ordinating body for higher education careers services. Students borrowed an average of Pounds 533 last year from the government's loans scheme, according to figures released by the Department for Education yesterday. About 261,000 university and polytechnic students - about one in three - took a government loan, with loans totalling Pounds 139.2m.
