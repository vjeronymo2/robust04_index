January 17, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

THE STATE

Four gunmen wounded one man and missed an infant by inches when they fired shots outside a home where a fight had erupted hours earlier during a birthday party, Santa Cruz police said. Witnesses told officers that four men pulled up in front of the house in a brown-paneled station wagon and began firing at three men standing in the driveway. A bullet grazed the back of Nickolas Decorse, 24, of Ft. Ord, and other bullets entered the house, nearly wounding an infant and two adults. Decorse's car was also struck. Police had been called to the house earlier to break up a fight, officers said.
