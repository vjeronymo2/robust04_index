April 11, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

CRA policies -- A headline in a Times story March 31 may have created the erroneous impression that accounting firm owner Deborah Morrisette had criticized the Community Redevelopment Agency during a workshop for minority- and women-owned businesses. Additionally, a sentence in the story implied that Morrisette was critical of practices that permitted Bishop H.H. Brookins and other influential members of minorities to get city contracts. Morrissette made no such statement regarding Brookins.
