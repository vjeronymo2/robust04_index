931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Govett Endeavour net assets fall Net asset value per share at Govett American Endeavour Fund, the Jersey-based investment concern, fell to 155 cents as at September 30, compared with 170 cents a year earlier. Total income for the six months rose from Dollars 5.82m to Dollars 6.19m (Pounds 4.15m) while net revenue came through ahead from Dollars 3.4m to Dollars 4.68m. Earnings per share were 8.6 cents (6.25 cents) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 5.52 cents.
