Canadian Commercial Fishery Canadian halibut landings from Area 2B total 5.8 million lb (2,630 mt) through June 20, leaving 4.2 million lb (1910 mt) of the catch limit to be caught. The fishery will continue until all individual vessel quotas have been filled, or November 15, whichever is earlier.
