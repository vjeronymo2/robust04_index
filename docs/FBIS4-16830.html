BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow April 19 TASS -- The renewed escalation of bloodshed around Nagorno-Karabakh is causing serious concern, deputy chief of the Russian Foreign Ministry Department of Information and Press Mikhail Demurin said at a briefing today. According to the official, the Russian Foreign Ministry, acting as a mediator, rejects attempts made by the conflicting sides, in the given case the Armenians of Nagorno-Karabakh, to extend and consolidify [as received] their positions when the situation has long since taken a turn towards peace. Recent attacks in the north of Nagorno-Karabakh are being negatively reflected on the prospects of negotiations, Mikhail Demurin noted.
