Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Dissident Socialists Join Berlusconi Alliance"] [Text] Rome, 2 Feb (ANSA) -- The Federation of Reformist and Libertarian Socialists, a breakaway group that split with Socialist leader Ottaviano Del Turco after his decision to join the Left Democrat-led progressive front, have teamed up with former Liberals, Social Democrats and the smaller of the two ex-Christian Democrat groups behind the Forza Italia [Rooting for Italy] banner of media-to-construction magnate Silvio Berlusconi, it was learned this morning. Thus the Berlusconi cartel now includes the right-wingers of the old Christian Democrat party, grouped in the Democratic Christian Centre, the ex-Liberals and Social Democrats of the Centrist Union, a separate movement headed by (ex-Liberal party) art critic and media personality Vittorio Sgarbi, and the small group of Socialists who stayed loyal to disgraced former leader Bettino Craxi. The entry of the Socialists took place at a meeting last night at Berlusconi's Rome apartment, when agreement was reached on a programme focussing on "responsible liberty, for individuals and business." Sources for the Federation of Reformist and Libertarian Socialists said that, for the moment, any possible alliance with the far-right National Alliance (formerly the neo-fascist Italian Social Movement) was held up by "differences on the programme."
