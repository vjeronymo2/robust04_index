940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Trust net assets up 38% The Throgmorton Trust, which concentrates on smaller UK-listed companies, reported a net asset value per share of 79.9p as at November 30, a rise of 38 per cent on the 57.8p of 12 months earlier. The trust, managed by Framlington Investment Trust Services, lifted net revenue from Pounds 6.68m to Pounds 7.15m over the year, equivalent to earnings of 2.4p (2.35p). A recommended final dividend of 1.4p maintains the total distribution at 2.3p, payable on increased capital following redemption of convertible loan stock.
