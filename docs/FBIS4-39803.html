BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk submitted to parliament his proposals on measures to be taken if the Crimean parliament fails to bring its legislation in line with the Ukrainian Constitution, head of the presidential press office , Nikolai Mikhalchenko, told INTERFAX-Ukraine. However, he declined to indicate what measures were proposed by the president. "What happens next depends on the parliament. The Ukrainian parliament is responsible for relations with Crimea, and the president has no powers to interfere," he added.
