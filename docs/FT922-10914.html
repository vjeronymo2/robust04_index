920429 FT 29 APR 92 / World Trade News: Accountants in China link-ups TWO INTERNATIONAL accountancy firms have formed unprecedented joint ventures in China which give them the same powers to operate as local firms, Andrew Jack writes. Arthur Andersen has linked up with Hua Qiang certified public accountants in Beijing, a branch of China's finance ministry. KPMG Peat Marwick has formed a similar link with a second firm operated as a ministry subsidiary. The agreements, ratified in the past few days, will give the joint ventures the power to certify accounts filed to the Chinese tax authorities.
