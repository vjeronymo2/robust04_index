921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Observer: Plum puzzle So what duties will UK Customs & Excise chairman Sir Brian Unwin be collecting as the barriers come down with the single market? The answer is apparently the plum job at the European Investment Bank, for which Observer has long tipped him. The puzzle is why the appointment has been so long delayed. Despite speculation that other EC community states were sceptical about his credentials, the reason turns out to be nothing directly to do with Unwin. The pussy- footing was over who will succeed him at the end of his term six years hence. Certain smaller states, fed up with their sparse plum ration, wanted assurance that his successor will be one of their citizens.
