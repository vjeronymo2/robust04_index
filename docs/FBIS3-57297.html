Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sergey Kovalev, a State Duma deputy and well-known campaigner, has been appointed a plenipotentiary representative on human rights, Radio Moscow International has reported. Similar institutions of parliamentary missionaries exist in many democratic countries, but it is the first such institution in Russia. The main aim of the new institution is to monitor the work of government offices with the aim of out-of-court protection of human rights, [word indistinct] following up specific complaints made by citizens.
