921123 FT 23 NOV 92 / UK Company News: F&C Eurotrust improvement At September 30 1992, net asset value of Foreign & Colonial Eurotrust was 161.5p, a rise of 1.7 per cent over the 158.8p of a year earlier. Total revenue for the year increased from Pounds 2.64m to Pounds 3.53m, and net revenue came to Pounds 865,000 (Pounds 826,000). That equalled earnings per share of 1.47p (1.61p) and the dividend is stepped up to 1.23p (1.18p), payable on December 23.
