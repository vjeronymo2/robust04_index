940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / Briefcase, Q&A: Anyone here seen Alice? I have found some share certificates in the name of Alice Edwards (Holdings) Ltd. Does this company still exist or has it been liquidated? It changed its name to Brigray Group Ltd on June 30 1969. Brigray was wound up in May 1981 and the Inland Revenue approved the negligible value of the ordinary shares during the 1981/82 fiscal year.
