BFN [Text] The secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation received this morning Ahmad Bakomo, ambassador of the Republic of Mali, Assimi Alkaidi [Toure], ambassador of the Republic of Niger, and Poli Porcpie [as heard], ambassador of Burkina Faso to the Great Jamahiriyah. The two sides examined and reviewed the results of the talks which the leader of the revolution held with the presidents of Mali, Niger, and Burkina Faso during their recent visits to the Great Jamahiriyah. The meetings also dealt with aspects of bilateral cooperation between the Great Jamahiriyah and these countries and ways to bolster them so as to serve mutual interests and the interests of these African states and reaffirm their links and cooperation with the Arab Maghreb Union.
