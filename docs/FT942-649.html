940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 10.3m buy-out at East Lancs Paper East Lancashire Paper Mill has been acquired from British Syphon Industries and Scandinavian Securities in a Pounds 10.3m management buy-out backed by 3i and Midland Bank. The refinancing was arranged by Hambros Bank. ELPM made pre-tax profits of more than Pounds 1m on sales of Pounds 29m in the 1993 year.
