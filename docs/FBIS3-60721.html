Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] During the past few days Army General of Ukraine Vitaliy Radetskyy, minister of defense, has visited units and facilities of the strategic missile troops deployed on the territory of our state. The existing problems have been thoroughly analyzed with scientists and specialists during these trips. As a result specific measures have been implemented in the localities to create a reliable system of control over the state of the missile facilities. By a decision of the minister of defense, their protection has been increased, for which additional subunits and units have been allocated and cooperation between them has been established. A great deal of attention has also been paid to issues of efficiency and technical means, and to the daily life of servicemen and members of their families.
