BFN [Text] Beijing, June 6 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese domestic passenger plane was highjacked to Taiwan today. A spokesman for the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC) said that China Southern Airlines' B-737/2542 passenger plane was serving the CZ3572 flight from Fuzhou to Guangzhou. It took off at 18:58 from Fuzhou Airport, and was highjacked on its flight. At 19:57 it landed safely at Taoyuan Airport, Taibei [Taipei]. The spokesman said that relevant departments of the two sides of the Taiwan Straits are in contact on the incident.
