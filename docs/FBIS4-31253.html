BFN [Report by unidentified correspondent] [Text] The situation in the Gradacac region was reported to be particularly grave this morning. [Correspondent] Tank, howitzer, and mortar projectiles are landing on the central part of the town, the industrial zone, suburbs, and the surrounding villages. The most active aggressor's stronghold is most certainly that on Gornje Krecane, from where the aggressor is firing all types of weapons, including hill guns. They are targeting Rajska and Lukavac. The greatest number of projectiles has landed on civilian houses, which were also subjected to the fiercest barrage fire. The aggressor is thought to have fired more than 400 shells. According to the latest report, three persons were wounded. This morning, Gradacac was subjected to the brutal and wild chetnik artillery attacks, which were witnessed by the representatives of the international forces.
