January 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ROBIN ELSON MURDER ACQUITTAL

The court is to be commended for its wisdom. Robin Elson is to be praised for the courage she finally found to end the life of Jack Elson.

For her three precious children, let us pray that their tortured lives can heal. My advice to Robin Elson would be to quickly sell the rights to this shocking story, in order to raise a great deal of money. Money will allow her to provide the many years of therapy and care she and these children must now have.

BRIDGET MARVIN

South Pasadena
