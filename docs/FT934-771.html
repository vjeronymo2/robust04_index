931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / French spending declines French consumer spending on manufactured goods fell by 1.6 per cent last month, according to figures published yesterday by Insee, the state statistics institute, Alice Rawsthorn writes from Paris. The November fall followed a reduction of 1.2 per cent in October and underlines the pressures on the economy in France where consumer confidence has been depressed by high real interest rates and rising unemployment.
