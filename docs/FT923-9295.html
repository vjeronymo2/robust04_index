920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Trimoco responds to Hartwell statement Trimoco, the Luton-based motor dealer, yesterday urged shareholders to take no action in respect of plans by Hartwell to make an offer for the balance of Trimoco's shares which it does not own. Hartwell said it would pay 17.5p a share for the outstanding equity. Hartwell intends to offer Pounds 175 for each Pounds 100 nominal of the 11 1/2 per cent guaranteed convertible unsecured loan stock of Trimoco Finance that it does not already own.
