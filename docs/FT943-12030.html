940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Temple Bar Net asset value of Temple Bar Investment Trust fell from 384.17p to 331.97p over the six months to end June 30. Attributable profits for the first half fell from Pounds 3.94m to Pounds 3.69m and earnings per share were down at 6.403p (6.835p). The interim dividend is raised to 4.7p (4.6p).
