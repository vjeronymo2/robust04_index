940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Lazard High net revenue at Pounds 1.46m In its first full year results since its launch last August, Lazard High Income Trust turned in net revenue of Pounds 1.46m for the 11 months period to June 30 and an earnings per share value of 6.4p. A fourth interim dividend of 1.067p makes a total for the year of 5.867p and a first interim of 0.573p is declared for the current year. Net asset value slipped by 1.5 per cent to 94.5p over the period.
