920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / Letter: Recruiting and keeping union membership Sir, David Goodhart's article on trade union membership and recruitment (Management: 'Stewards shop for new members', August 6), missed three important points: many of the term trends seen as working against unionisation were also operating during earlier periods of union growth; some unions have gained members in the 1980s; it is not simply a question of union recruitment of members that is critical, but their retention once they have joined. Chris Rowley, lecturer, Cardiff Business School, Aberconway Building, Colum Drive, Cardiff CF1 3EU
