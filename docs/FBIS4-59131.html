BFN [Text] The Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] has claimed responsibility for the attack on an Israeli bus near Ramallah in the occupied West Bank. A statement signed by the vanguards of comrade martyr Wadi' Haddad was distributed in the occupied Palestinian territories today. The statement says that in continuation of the policy of revolutionary violence and armed militancy, the Ibrahim al-Ra'i group has attacked an Israeli bus carrying Zionist settlers. In reference to the Palestinian self-rule agreement, the statement says that agreements will not protect the usurpers of our territories and displacers of our people.
