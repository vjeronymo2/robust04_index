BFN [Report by Falih al-Saghir and Sa'ud al-Rayyis from Cairo] [Excerpt] An Arab League official has said that human losses in 25 days of fierce fighting in Yemen have exceeded more than 50,000 killed from both sides. In a statement to AL-YAWM, Tal'at Hamid, the League's media adviser, said that the material damage for the same period has exceeded $10 billion. He said the Yemeni infrastructure has also been damaged. [passage omitted]
