BFN [Text] The offensive of Golboddin Hekmatyar's militia in Tagab was repulsed. A source in the Ministry of Defense told the BIA military correspondent that yesterday Golboddin Hekmatyar's militia mounted a sudden offensive on the front lines of the front and high points. As a result of the kindness of the great God and the valor of the mojahedin of Islam it was dislodged. As a result of the counteroffensive of the mojahedin of the Islamic army, eight raiders were killed, three were taken prisoner, and three others were wounded. Three rocket launchers, three PK machine guns, and 13 Kalashnikovs were captured from the enemy. According to the security sources' report, the Tagab offensive took place as a result of Dostam's request that Hekmatyar open a second front against the forces of the Islamic army to [words indistinct] on central bases of the militia in the north.
