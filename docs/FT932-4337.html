930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / International Company News: Condor reports 1992 after-tax profit Condor, a unit of Lufthansa, said it had 1992 after-tax profit of DM2.6m after a DM4.6m loss in 1991, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. The holiday charter company said that passenger revenues rose to DM1.33bn from DM1.09bn a year earlier. Condor said that it flew 4.3m passengers in 1992, about 1.1m more than in the previous year. Its share of the German tourism market was up by about 3 percentage points to 24 per cent, Condor said.
