November 22, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MIXED COMPANY: Freshman Republican Assemblyman Curt Pringle of Garden Grove has joined the Hispanic Chamber of Commerce in an effort to repair his image with the Latino community after his involvement in the posting of security guards at poll sites last year. . . . But that hasn't won everybody over. . . . In fact, Pringle's membership has outraged some members to the point that they are threatening to quit, prompting board member Manuel Pena to put the Pringle issue on the agenda for the next meeting. Dana Parsons
