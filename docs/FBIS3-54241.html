Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Egypt is interested in selling gas to Israel and is concerned about the possibility that Israel will purchase natural gas from Qatar. The two countries have decided to set up a joint committee early next month to examine the possibility and feasibility of importing gas from Egypt. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that following Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres' and Energy Minister Moshe Shahal's meetings with Qatari representatives in London, it has transpired that Qatar is interested in implementing the deal with Israel, but its economic viability is still being examined.
