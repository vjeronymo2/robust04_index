920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Making the big break abroad - In A Nutshell A two-day intensive training course to help businesses break into overseas markets will be run by the London Enterprise Agency (LEntA) on November 11 and 18. The course, subsidised by a commercial sponsor, also includes the opportunity for participants to take part in an international exhibition of their choice and six-months of free follow-up consultancy. Contact LEntA, 4 Snow Hill, London EC1A 2BS. Tel 071 236 3000. Course fee Pounds 250.
