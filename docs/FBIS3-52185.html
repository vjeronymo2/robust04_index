Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad today received a phone call from Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi. The two presidents exchanged congratulations on the new year. They also discussed latest developments in the region. President Hafiz al-Asad this evening received a phone call from His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal. The president and his majesty the king exchanged congratulations on the new year. They also talked about issues of interest to the two fraternal countries. The president this evening received a phone call from Lebanese Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, who congratulated the president on the new year. Talks also centered on issues of interest to Syria and Lebanon. President Hafiz al-Asad this evening received a phone call from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, who congratulated the president on the new year. He also conveyed U.S. President Bill Clinton's greetings and congratulations on the new year. The president thanked him, exchanged congratulations with him, and asked him to convey his greetings and congratulations to President Clinton on the new year. During the contact, they also discussed the meeting which will be held between the president and President Clinton in Geneva as well as the upcoming visit to the region by a U.S. congressional delegation to discuss missing Israeli soldiers.
