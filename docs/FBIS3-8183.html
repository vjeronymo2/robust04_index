Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slupsk heat and power generating plant and the timber enterprise in Slawno are the first to join the Solidarity nationwide protest action announced for today. Solidarity National Commission Spokesman Piotr Zak told us that strikes could also start in other parts of Poland. The strike that is just starting will circulate to cover selected regions, industries, and enterprises. The nationwide strike will take place in the metal and mining industries and on the railways. The Solidarity spokesman, who was the guest of the morning program on Radio Lodz, said that the railways would go on strike on Thursday or Friday. If the strike on the railways is repeated, only freight trains will be stopped. Piotr Zak also said the the Solidarity power industry section decided that state administration buildings and enterprises owing money to the power industry will be the first to have their electricity cut off.
