BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow, 20 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk have given each other a promise not to adopt violent measures to resolve the "Crimean question." Boris Yeltsin told journalists this today during an interval between the presentation of credentials to the Russian president by the ambassadors of foreign states. Boris Yeltsin referred to his telephone conversation yesterday with Leonid Kravchuk. Boris Yeltsin stressed in particular that the Crimea is a sovereign republic and is entitled to conduct its own policies.
