BFN [Text] Today, Army General Raul Castro Ruz, minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, visited three Youth Labor Army [EJT] integrated farms dedicated to miscellaneous crops and located in Altemisa and Alquizar Municipalities. Raul conversed with farmers who make up the EJT forces and showed interest in the work they do, their enthusiasm, and living conditions. The farm leadership briefed Raul and his delegation on the progress in achieving production goals and on the farm's efficiency, profitability, and costs.
