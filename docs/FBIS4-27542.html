BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 1 (KCNA) -- Officials and workers of factories under the State Light Industry Commission of Korea have turned out as one in implementing the light industry-first policy of the Workers' Party of Korea, effecting a steady upsurge in production. More than 30 factories including the Pyongyang Textile Combine, the Sinuiju Export Garment Factory, the Hyesan Shoe-Making Factory and the Pyongyang Textile Machine Factory fulfilled their may plan ahead of schedule. The Anju and Palwon Silk Mills had topped their monthly quotas 70 percent in industrial output value and 10 percent in production indices by May 27 by introducing valuable technical innovation proposals in production. The Tongdaewon Garment Factory, the Kyongamsan Export Garment Factory and other factories and enterprises under the Unha Associated Trading Bureau hit their monthly exports production targets more than five days ahead of schedule. Shoe-making factories in Sariwon, Hyesan, Pyongyang and other areas produced more shoes in May than the previous month.
