931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Bula Resources Bula Resources, the Irish exploration company, suffered a pre-tax loss of IPounds 1.39m (Pounds 1.32m) for the first half of 1993, after charging an exceptional item of IPounds 1.14m arising on the disposal of its UK operations. This compared with a IPounds 33,000 profit last time. Turnover from continuing operations was IPounds 581,000 (IPounds 618,000) and the operating loss came to IPounds 174,000 (IPounds 55,000 profit). Losses per share were 0.211p (0.006p).
