May 18, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

ISRAELI BORDER POLICE KILL PALESTINIAN, 13

Paramilitary forces shot and killed a 13-year-old Palestinian in the Israeli-occupied West Bank today, a day after a Swedish human rights group condemned the army's use of violence against Arab children.

The teen-ager was killed when the border police dispersed youths from the Balata refugee camp who threw stones at an oil truck on the outskirts of the camp, military sources said.
