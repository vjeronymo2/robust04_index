Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Biryukov] [Text] Kaliningrad, 17 Mar -- "I am in Kaliningrad Oblast to resolve the issue of setting up a limited operational group made up from the forces of the Baltic Fleet and from the land-based forces." This was stated today by Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev on arrival in Kaliningrad. He has begun a three-day tour of inspection of the country's westernmost oblast. According to the minister, the numerical strength of the group that is being set up "will be determined by the defensive sufficiency necessary to protect this enclave territory and also Russia's interests in the Baltic region." "The final decision on setting up the group and its deployment will be made by a Defense Ministry collegium," Pavel Grachev stressed.
