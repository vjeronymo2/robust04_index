931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff notes share rise Pittencrieff, the natural resources and communications company, said yesterday it had 'noted the movement in the share price . . . over the past three trading days', writes Peter Pearse. Shares rose from 374p on October 27 to 478p yesterday, before settling back at November 2's close of 473p. It said Gleacher & Co, the newly appointed New York investment banking firm, would advise on 'all the options' regarding its 54 per cent stake in its US subsidiary, Pittencrieff Communications.
