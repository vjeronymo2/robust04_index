BFN [Text] In a broadcast on the Russian Kaliningrad region state television, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party, said that there are real possibilities for changing state borders in East Europe. The Baltic News Service reports that, in the opinion of Zhirinovskiy, the German-Polish, Polish-Ukrainian and Lithuanian-Russian borders should be revised. He stated that Russia and Germany have always been the main powers in Europe, therefore their requirements should be satisfied first of all. Asked about the further fate of the Kaliningrad region, the Russian national radical stated that it has to become a bridge between Russia and Germany. Vladimir Zhirinovskiy has stated more than once that the Baltic states must belong to the Russian empire, and he promised to bury radioactive waste near the Lithuanian border.
