930421 FT 21 APR 93 / NTL wins second ITV contract A SECOND significant ITV contract to provide a regional digital network has been won by National Transcommunications (NTL) in an increasingly competitive market with British Telecommunications. NTL, the privatised broadcasting transmission and engineering division of the former Independent Broadcasting Authority, has won a 10-year contract to replace existing BT facilities for the Yorkshire-Tyne Tees television company. The digital network will link Yorkshire-Tyne Tees studios with transmitters in a deal believed to be worth Pounds 10m.
