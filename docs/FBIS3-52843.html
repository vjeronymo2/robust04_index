Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received Bahrain's Foreign Minister Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Mubarak Al Khalifah, at 1630 today. The meeting was attended by Muhammad Hamad al-Muhaymid, Bahrain's ambassador in Damascus. Talks covered the Arab situation, the latest developments in the peace process, and relations between the two fraternal countries.
