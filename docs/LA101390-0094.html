October 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FIRED EMPLOYEE SLAYS 1, WOUNDS 2 AND KILLS SELF

A man who was fired from his job at a car dealership returned to the shop Friday with a gun and killed one man and wounded two others before killing himself, Baltimore County police said.

A police spokesman said the 25-year-old man entered the service area at Fox Chevrolet outside Baltimore and began shooting, then turned the gun on himself.
