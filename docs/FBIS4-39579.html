BFN [Text] Russia intends to increase its gold production substantially in the very near future, says Yevgeniy Bychkov, the head of Russia's Committee for Precious Metals and Precious Stones. This year the country has increased gold production from 149.5 to 157 tons. Gold reserves currently amount to 307 tons. Of this total 157.2 tons are held by the Committee for Precious Metals. The rest is under the jurisdiction of the Central Bank.
