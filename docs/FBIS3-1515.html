Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Port Shepstone March 30 SAPA -- Four suspects were arrested for illegal possession of several firearms and ammunition in Port Shepstone on the Natal lower South Coast on Tuesday [29 March], Durban police spokesman Maj Bala Naidoo said. The suspects are believed to be African National Congress [ANC] officials. A ANC peace desk official confirmed the arrests, adding he had "no clue" as to why they were detained. Maj Naidoo said a vehicle was stopped in Port Shepstone on Tuesday morning and police recovered an AK-47 rifle and magazine, 20 AK-47 rounds, and a 9mm Luger pistol with rounds. "The local offices of a political party", were subsequently searched and a 9mm CZ pistol and a police camoflauge cap were found, said Maj Naidoo. The men are due in court soon.
