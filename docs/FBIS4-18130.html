BFN [Text] Paris, March 30 (AFP) -- Ten sailors died in an accident Wednesday [30 March] aboard the French Navy nuclear-powered Emeraude submarine off the Mediterranean naval base of Toulon, military sources said. They said the accident aboard the sub, armed with ballistic missiles, took place in a turbo engine room where electricity is produced with steam and in no way damaged or affected the nuclear part of the vessel. The 10 men were in the engine room when the accident occurred. No further details on the cause of the disaster were immediately available. The submarine surfaced after the accident and sailed back to base under its own steam. Defence Minister Francois Leotard and Navy Chief of Staff Admiral Alain Coatanea were on their way to the base, the sources said.
