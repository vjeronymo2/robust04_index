BFN [Article signed DGO/ADB: "Rwanda -- Return of First Repatriated Belgians"] [Text] Brussels, 11 Apr (BELGA)-- The first aircraft bringing Belgian nationals back from Rwanda landed at Brussels national airport on Monday morning [11 April] at approximately 0215 GMT. The Sabena aircraft, a Boeing 737 that took off from Bujumbura, the capital of Burundi, had 89 Belgians and 26 Netherlanders on board. The Netherlanders continued their journey to the Netherlands immediately, while the Belgians were taken to the Queen Astrid Military Hospital in Neder-over-Hembeek, where their families were waiting for them. The Federal Government was represented by Vice Prime Minister Elio di Rupo. According to Sabena, the next repatriation flight, this time on board a DC-10 coming from Nairobi, Kenya, is scheduled to land at 2300 GMT.
