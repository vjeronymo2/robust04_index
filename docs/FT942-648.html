940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Unilever makes South American buy Unilever is broadening its food interests in Latin America with the agreed purchase, for an undisclosed sum, of Cica of Argentina, a privately-owned tomato-based products business. The acquisition will restore the ownership and management of Cica of Argentina to Cica of Brazil, a Unilever subsidiary acquired in 1993.
