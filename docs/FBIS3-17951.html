Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, in Kiev, Ukraine's Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko meets with Canada's Foreign Minister Andre Ouellet who arrived on an official visit at Ukraine's capital yesterday. Joint Ukrainian-Canadian documents will be signed, in particular, an intergovernmental agreement on trade and commercial relations as well as a declaration on the special nature [osoblyve] of a partnership between Ukraine and Canada. Today, according to the program of the visit, Canada's foreign minister will be received by Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk. A meeting between Andre Ouellet and Oleh Slepichev, minister for external economic relations, will also be held.
