Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Lord Jeffrey Archer, envoy of British Prime Minister John Major, visited the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate building yesterday. He was received by Al-Sulaymaniyah Governor Sulayman 'Abdul. Afterward, accompanied by Jabbar Farman, minister for peshmerga affairs, and the governor, Lord Archer visited the al-Sulaymaniyah office of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan. He was received by hundreds of people. He delivered a speech stressing that the Kurdish people have many friends abroad. Afterward, he visited the fourth branch of the Democratic Party of Kurdistan. He then visited the town of Halabjah and the village of Sayyid Sadiq.
