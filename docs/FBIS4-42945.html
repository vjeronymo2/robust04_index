BFN [Text] Ankara, June 9 (A.A.) -- Ukranian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko said today that the inauguration of their embassy in Ankara was an indication of the importance they attached on relations with Turkey. He added that his country and Turkey had deep historical relations. Zlenko went on to say that both Turkey and Ukraine attached great importance on bilateral and regional cooperation and `opening of our embassy is important not only for our bilateral relations but also for the regional cooperation.'
