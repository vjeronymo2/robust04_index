941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / International Company News: Alcoa eyes Russian deal Aluminum Company of America (Alcoa) is considering buying a minority stake in a Russian aluminium smelting plant in Siberia, in what would be its first investment in the former Soviet Union, Reuter reports from Michigan. While the initial investment would be small, it could grow to as much as Dollars 500m over the next 10 years, Mr Paul O'Neill, chairman and chief executive officer, said.
