BFN [Text] Doha, 5 Apr (WAKH)--A round of official talks was held in Doha this morning between His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar, and Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The meeting was attended by His Highess Shaykh Hamad Bin-Khalifah Al Thani, Qatar's heir apparent, and senior officials from both countries. The Qatari News Agency noted that the talks dealt with the situation in Yemen, [words indistinct], the peace process in the Middle East, and the overall Arab and regional developments. The two leaders also exchanged views on ways to reinforce Arab solidarity and consolidate and develop bilateral relations. The Yemeni president left Doha today following a two-day visit to Qatar. He was seen off at the airport by His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar, and senior Qatari officials.
