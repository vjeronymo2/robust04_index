October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: RAMONA SCHOOL SENIOR PART OF SCIENCE PROGRAM

Wan Chi, a senior at Ramona Convent Secondary School in Alhambra, has been named a member of the National Science Foundation's young scholars program.

She will work in a laboratory at a local university with a professor, write an abstract paper and make an oral presentation at the annual meeting of the Southern California Academy of Sciences.
