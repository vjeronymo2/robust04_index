BFN [Text] Moscow, 18 May (EFE) -- Today, Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella announced Argentine President Carlos Menem's scheduled visit to Russia will take place in late October. Di Tella, who will end his three-day visit today, pointed out that he and the Russian authorities set Menem's visit for late October, even though the actual date has yet to be determined. Di Tella is visiting Moscow at the invitation of his Russian counterpart, Andrey Kozyrev. Di Tella, who will leave Moscow this afternoon for Buenos Aires, ended his stay with a lecture on the "New Argentina" at the Diplomatic Academy. High-ranking Russian diplomacy representatives, Diplomatic School students, and Latin American ambassadors were present at Di Tella's lecture. The foreign minister described a democratic and trustworthy Argentina at political and economic levels, an Argentina that hopes to join the first world.
