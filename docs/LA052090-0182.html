May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GET TIPS ON SELLING HOME AT BUYERS FAIR

One of the biggest obstacles to buying a new home can be selling your present house in today's soft real estate market. But don't despair, expert help is on the way.

Fair-goers at this Saturday's Los Angeles Times Home Buyers Fair can participate in special two-hour round-table discussions with leading Orange County real estate experts and profit from their advice -- and tips from other home sellers.

Realtors and other experts will talk with you and other frustrated sellers in small informal groups from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. and discuss better ways to market your home, how to price it right, how to prepare it for sale and how to select and work with a real estate agent.

For a guide to the fair, please see the second section of Real Estate.
