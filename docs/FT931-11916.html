930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Rushdie death sentence stays Iranian president Hashemi Rafsanjani reaffirmed the 1989 death sentence decreed by the late Ayatollah Khomeini against Salman Rushdie, author of The Satanic Verses. Meeting foreign reporters for the first time in two years, President Rafsanjani said Tehran wanted better ties with the west.
