March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Lennie Del Genio, the Las Vegas bookie whose uninformed Oscar odds are routinely reported as entertainment news, was heard in a recent radio interview picking Brenda Fritcher (it's Fricker) to win best supporting actress, and Oliver Sloan (it's Stone) to win the best director award over "My Left Foot's" John (it's Jim) Sheridan.
