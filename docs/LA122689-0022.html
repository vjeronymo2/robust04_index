December 26, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

ON THE RECORD

"The ad is about Noriega, and with a scumbag like that involved, I think it's fine."

-- Peter Stranger, president of Della Femina, McNamee WCRS Inc., on his agency's ad for Jiffy Lube, which speculates that the deposed Panamanian leader is not really missing, just "waiting at his dealer for an oil change."
