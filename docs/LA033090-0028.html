March 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MEETING STATE'S WATER NEEDS

So Mayor Tom Bradley, assorted city officials and the MWD want my garden to wither my car to look like the bottom of a bird cage and my body to smell like last week's leftovers (Metro, March 23).

You'll forgive me if I say this won't wash. Not at all, especially when I see huge companies hosing down their sidewalks, driveways, curbs and street gutters.

It all makes me so mad, I think I'll go have a couple double waters, which come from Arrowhead by the way.

AL JACOBS

Burbank
