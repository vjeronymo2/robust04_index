940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: 30 years for IRA attack Englishmen Jan Taylor, 51, a former army corporal, and Patrick Hayes, 41, a computer analyst, were each jailed for 30 years at the Old Bailey after taking part in an IRA bombing campaign in mainland Britain including the Harrods litter bin explosion last year.
