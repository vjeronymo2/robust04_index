BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow April 1 ITAR-TASS -- "Russian troops will arrive in Kansas, U.S. in November to take part in joint exercises," said Colonel General Eduard Vorobyev, deputy chief commander of the Russian land forces. The exercises are designed to upgrade coordination in peacekeeping operations. The Russians will participate in the fall exercises after their American counterparts take part in the exercises code-named Peacekeeper-94, which will take place in Totsk, the Volga military district of Russia, from July 6 through 14. Asked about the advisability of such exercises, Colonel-General Vorobyev said new peace-keeping methods, based on the experience of the best peace-keeping missions of American and Russian Armed Forces. [as received] Vorobyev denied the allegations concerning "the preparations for the occupation of Russia by American troops". He said one American company of the First Battalion of the Third Infantry Division, not more than 250 American men and officers all in all.
