940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / France drops Sarajevo plan A group of French intellectuals led by Mr Bernard-Henri Levy, the populist philosopher, has abandoned plans to field a 'Europe begins in Sarajevo' slate in next week's European elections following its success in raising awareness of the plight of the Bosnian people in the current election campaign. Mr Levy, whose Byronesque looks have made him a favourite of the French gossip columns, is a controversial figure in France where he is criticised as a lightweight by some intellectuals and praised by others for his commitment to the Bosnian cause.
