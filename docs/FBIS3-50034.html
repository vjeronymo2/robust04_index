Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] After the meeting of the Albanian-Greek Joint Commission, which discussed economic and technical issues related to bilateral cooperation, the Greek government delegation conducted other business talks today. Talks began at the Agriculture and Food Industry, where Mr. Papandoniou was received by Agriculture and Food Minister Halili. The Greek economic delegation held talks with Ministers Bitincka [transportation] and Dishnica [finance] and with Deputy Prime Minister Kopliku. During the meetings, talks focused on resolving the problems in which the possibilities and facilities are more present for both sides to contribute to programmed cooperation. In these meetings, Mr. Papandoniou stressed once more the importance of the fact that Greece will head the EC presidency for six months. He mentioned the sum of 350 million [as heard] ECU that the EC has planned to provide for transports and infrastructure during this year. During the talks, they spoke about the possibilities of providing financial support to Albanian transportation in general. The Greek side also presented concrete projects to Mr. Bitincka, assuring him of the Greek Government's support for companies that may invest to improve the Albanian transportation. The delegation continues its visit in Tirana.
