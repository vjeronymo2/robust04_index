October 6, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

NONLEAGUE

Fillmore 21, St. Bonaventure 0 -- Tory Cabral scored three touchdowns, kicked three extra points and rushed for 181 yards in 20 carries to lead Fillmore (5-0) at home. Cabral scored on a 47-yard run on the Flashes' first possession and scored on a pair of four-yard runs.

Fillmore led at halftime, 14-0, holding St. Bonaventure (2-3) to seven yards in total offense, including minus-two yards rushing.

Jose Payan and Toby Noblin, St. Bonaventure's starting fullback and tailback, respectively, were held to 40 yards rushing, and the Seraphs managed only 73 yards in offense. J. SCOTT KLEIN
