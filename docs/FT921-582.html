920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Micklegate A sharp reversal in net interest, from a credit of Pounds 40,000 to a charge of Pounds 593,000 pushed Micklegate Group into the red in the six months to October 31. The USM-quoted industrial and commercial property developer reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 699,000 for the half year compared with a profit of Pounds 22,000 last time. Turnover was up from Pounds 2.22m to Pounds 3.1m.
