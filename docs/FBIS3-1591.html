Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dhaka, February 25 (XINHUA) -- Bangladesh people are conscious of the importance of China's role in the United Nations and other international organizations. As a permanent member of the U.N. Security Council, China shares a great responsibility for the successful realization of the ideals and objectives of the United Nations. Mr. Mirza Golam Hafiz, Bangladesh minister for law, justice and parliamentary affairs and president of the Bangladesh-China Friendship Association made the remarks when hosting a welcoming banquet on Friday [25 February] evening on behalf of the association for the visiting Chinese Vice Premier Qian Qichen. Qian, who is also foreign minister of China, thanked Mr. Hafiz and other members of the Bangladesh-China Friendship Association for their ceaseless efforts in promoting understanding, friendship and cooperation between the two peoples. The Chinese vice premier described the Sino-Bangladesh friendship as "time-tested" and he believed the friendship will be further strengthened and developed through the efforts of the two sides. Qian, who flew here from Pakistan on Thursday, is on a four-day official goodwill visit to Bangladesh, the second leg of his five-nation tour also including Sri Lanka, Maldives and Iran. On Friday afternoon, Qian paid a visit to the No. One Bangladesh-China Friendship Bridge which was constructed by a Chinese firm and is so far the longest of its kind in the country.
