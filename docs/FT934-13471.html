931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Brit & American Film net assets up British & American Film Holdings, the investment company, reported a net asset value, excluding film rights, of 1,120.3p at June 30, against 1,023.1p at the beginning of the year and 898.5p 12 months earlier. In the first half of 1993 pre-tax profits were Pounds 618,456 against Pounds 609,688 restated for the changed treatment of investment sales required under FRS 3. Turnover was Pounds 784,604 (Pounds 747,346) with the majority coming from investments. Film and video revenue contributed a smaller Pounds 41,662 (Pounds 77,531). Earnings per share were 14.29p (16.68p). The interim dividend is raised to 4.275p (3.8p).
