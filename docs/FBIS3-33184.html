Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The 1994 training period for the Territorial Troops Militia [MTT] began today in Havana City Province. Habana del Este Municipality attained the Defense-Ready, Phase II banner, after winning first place in the overall emulative competion last year and an assessment of good during last December's Minfar [Ministry of the Revolutionary Armed Forces] inspection. (Armando Malpica), first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in the province and president of the Municipal Defense Council, congratulated the militiamen for having contributed to Habana del Este's becoming the first municipality in the capital, and the eighth in the nation, to achieve such an important status. During the ceremony held on the esplanade of the Villa Panamericana, a group of militiamen received the Order of Ignacio Agramonte, Grades I and III, as well as the FAR's 23 de Agosto and Distinguished Service medals. In the closing speech, Mayor Pedro Chavez called on residents of the capital to make a special effort this year in building civil defense tunnels and preparing the theater of operations.
