921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Ruling against accountancy firm THE first firm of chartered accountants to have its audit registration publicly withdrawn was announced yesterday. Lewin Atkins of north London will no longer be able to conduct audits as a result of a ruling from the audit registration committee of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales last month. The committee would not give the grounds on which deregistration took place, but the firm is also being scrutinised by the professional conduct staff of the institute.
