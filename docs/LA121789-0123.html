December 17, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HEAVY METAL BANDS AT AMPHITHEATER

I feel that the issue of volume, not curfew, should be raised concerning the Pacific Amphitheatre. Many concerts, especially those of heavy metal bands, are extremely loud and are capable of injuring a person's hearing.

A curfew will not help control the noise the residents of the area are complaining about.

JOSH DeWITT

Westminster
