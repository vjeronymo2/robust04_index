March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

THREE MEXICAN TEAMS IN OXNARD TOURNAMENT

Next week's International Friendship high school baseball tournament at Oxnard High will include eight entries, three from Mexico, one from Monrore, Utah, and four from Southern California.

Opening ceremonies will take place Sunday at 1 p.m. at Oxnard High and games will begin Monday at Oxnard and Hueneme highs.
