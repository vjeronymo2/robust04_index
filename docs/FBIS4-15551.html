BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 9 (TASS) -- "Ukrainian border guards were overexcited with patriotism," Head of the Black Sea Fleet Press Center Andrey Grachev told ITAR-TASS here today, commenting on the Odessa conflict of Russian seamen with Ukrainian border guards. In his words, the Russian Cheleken military vessel arrived in Odessa to load hydrographic equipment and carry it to Sevastopol. However, last night Ukrainian border guards tried to prevent this and even placed armed guards near the ship. Their actions almost provoked a serious conflict. In the opinion of Grachev, Ukrainian military are responsible for the attempt to hamper the planned transportation of Russian property for repairs. He stressed that the Russian hydrographic vessel which has a military crew shall not be liable for customs and border inspection. There are no documents to prohibit such actions in the Black Sea Fleet's operational zone.
