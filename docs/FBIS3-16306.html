Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] A news conference on the results of the Cabinet of Ministers sitting, chaired by Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk, was held on 14 March in Kiev. As was pointed out at the meeting with journalists, the consideration of the draft program of structural reorganization of Ukraine's economy was [word indistinct] at the sitting. Deputy Minister of Economy Leonid Minin stressed at the news conference that this document received, in general, a positive assessment at the sitting. It will be submitted for the consideration of the Supreme Council. It is a conceptual basis for Ukraine's transition toward market principles of economic management with social orientation and its integration into the world system of labor distribution with regard to the specific nature of our state's economic development. The draft envisages the urgent overcoming of emission and hyperinflation and measures to accelerate the privatization processes. Also, a report by President Leonid Kravchuk on the results of the official visit by Ukraine's state delegation to Washington was heard at the sitting. Judging from the agreements achieved there, they managed to bring Ukrainian-American relations to a qualitatively new level. In particular, a large-scale program of economic and other cooperation between the two countries was mapped out. First and foremost, this is manifested in the fact that the United States is ready to grant 2.5 million dollars in aid for the implementation of about 70 specific projects.
