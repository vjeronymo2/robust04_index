930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / International Bonds: Bank of China expected to return to Eurobond market The Bank of China is expected to return to the Eurobond market in the next two months by issuing Dollars 150m-Dollars 200m of floating-rate notes in London, the China Daily quoted a bank official as saying, Reuter reports from Beijing. Huo Tuanjie said the notes would mature in five to seven years.
