June 4, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

COMMENTS ON LETTER ABOUT JURY DUTY

Having read Judy Perry's letter in the May 28 Orange County Edition of The Times, "How Jury Duty Alters View of Legal System," I understand her disappointment with the vacuous, would-be jury panelists surrounding her.

I am reminded, therefore, of the old joke regarding anyone facing justice before the American legal system. It goes like this: "Would you want your life in the hands of 12 people who weren't smart enough to get out of jury duty?"

I rest my case.

STEPHEN C. LEE

La Habra
