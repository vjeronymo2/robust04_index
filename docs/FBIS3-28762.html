Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Osaka, February 7 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- The Japan Liaison Council of Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il was formed. It is a national organization of societies and groups which had been formed throughout Japan since the appearance of the society and group for the study of the works of Kim Chong-il in Tokyo in March last year. The inaugural meeting of the council took place in Osaka on February 5. Present there were chairmen and members of the societies and groups from different parts of Japan and Japanese people, more than 390 in all. Kenichi Ogami, secretary general of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea (IIJI) and advisor of the council, made an opening address, which was followed by speeches of director general of the IIJI Shuhachi Inoue and Director General of the Centre of Socialist Theories and Politics Oen Araki and a congratulatory speech of first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), Yi Chin-gyu. Prof. Takao Kamakura of Saitama University who is chief advisor of the council made a keynote report at the meeting. Stating that the council was being formed on the basis of the activities to study and disseminate the chuche idea, in keeping with the new requirements of the time, he said: "Our primary task is to learn from the idea, theories and methods of Secretary Kim Chong-il through the study of his works. "Firstly, Secretary Kim Chong-il has faithfully and correctly inherited the theoretical and practical activities of President Kim Il-song and further developed in depth the theoretical and practical matters concerning the revolution and construction in compliance with the new requirements of the internal and external situation. "He is the successor to the revolutionary cause of President Kim Il-song, who has carried it forward most correctly and the leader of the era of independence. "Secondly, Secretary Kim Chong-il formulated the president's revolutionary idea as Kimilsongism, an integral system of idea, theories and methods based on the philosophical principle of chuche." Referring to the theoretical activities of Secretary Kim Chong-il, the reporter said: "He said modern imperialism employs a very crafty method of domination and plunder and its aggressive nature remains unchanged. He clarified the reason for the collapse of socialism in former Soviet Union and eastern European countries and theorized the principles of anthropocentric socialism and communism based on the chuche idea. "He has inherited the revolutionary mass line of President Kim Il-song, who has established the method of leadership for enhancing the ideological consciousness of the masses and guiding them to display their independence and creativity under the leadership of the party, and has carried it into practice. "Secretary Kim Chong-il, true to the teachings of the president calling for believing in the people as in heaven and respecting the people as the teacher and learning from them, has served the people, regarding them as the greatest beings." The meeting also heard a report of the secretariat of the council on the course of its inauguration and the characteristics and activities of the societies and the council. Read at the meeting was a declaration on the inauguration of the council. A letter to Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting. Earlier, conferences of societies for the study of the works of Kim Chong-il were held in local areas. The statute of the council and a declaration on its inauguration were adopted and its officials were selected there. A banquet was arranged on the evening of February 5 in celebration of the 52nd birthday of Comrade Kim Chong-il.
