Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, Dec 24 (AFP) -- Four heavily-armed men holding 11 hostages including nine children aboard a helicopter threatened Friday to kill one of the hostages if they did not receive a $10 million ransom, an air force spokesman told AFP. The helicopter took off from the spa town of Mineralnyye Vody earlier, apparently heading for an unknown destination, but returned and has been hovering over the airport since then, the spokesman said.
