BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri returned to Beirut via Damascus this evening after a private visit to Paris. Al-Hariri left the French capital for Damascus this morning where he met with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and had lunch with him. He also met with a number of senior Syrian officials. As soon as he arrived in Beirut, al-Hariri contacted President Ilyas al-Hirawi and Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri to discuss current developments with them.
