Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Democratic Front [NDF] today reiterated that they will remain open for the resumption of the peace talks with the government. Nevertheless, Luis Jalandoni of NDF emphasized that the peace talks should not be limited by an executive order. Jalandoni said that the Hague Declaration should be the guide for the talks. As in the previous statements, however, the government panel stressed that Executive Order 125 issued by the government is in accordance with the Constitution, which sets limits on any peace talks undertaken by the government.
