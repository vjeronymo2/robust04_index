920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Computer systems side buoys Instem A good six months from the computer systems division helped Instem increase interim pre-tax profits 9 per cent from Pounds 507,000 to Pounds 551,000. Turnover in the period to July 3 was lower at Pounds 7.06m, against Pounds 7.87m. However activity in the electronics division of this USM-quoted company was lower because of recession. Earnings per share were 7.96p (7.21p) and the interim dividend is raised to 1.3p (1.2p).
