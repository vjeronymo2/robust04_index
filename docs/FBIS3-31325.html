Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cologne (DDP/ADN) -- A worker for the German aid organization Cap Anamur was kidnapped by Bosnian Serbs yesterday. The chairman of the organization, Rupert Neudeck, said on Deutschlandfunk Radio today that the man in question is a 32-year-old born in Sarajevo who has held German citizenship for a number of years. According to this information he was returning to the Dalmatian coast after carrying out relief work in Tuzla when the Bosnian Serbs kidnapped him from a bus carrying Norwegian doctors. According to available information the man is in good shape. The German Government is making efforts through diplomatic channels to achieve his release. Neudeck expressed the hope that the work of the aid organizations in Bosnia-Herzegovina could continue despite the NATO ultimatum to the Serbs. [passage omitted]
