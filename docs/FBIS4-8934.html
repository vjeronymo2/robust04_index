BFN [Article by "asa": "Alliance of Democrats and Alternative of Political Realism Clubs of Deputies Merged"] [Excerpt] Bratislava--[Passage omitted] I. Gazdik, deputy to the National Council of the Slovak Republic [NRSR] for the Alliance of Democrats [AD], reported yesterday that the NRSR Clubs of Deputies of the AD and the Alternative of Political Realism have merged. He said that, for the time being, the new club of deputies consists of 17 deputies, but he did not rule out its expansion in the near future. He said, furthermore, that one deputy left the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] Club of Deputies yesterday and decided to operate as an independent deputy. Gazdik did not disclose the deputy's name.
