920424 FT 24 APR 92 / World News in Brief: Heath made Knight of Garter Edward Heath, the prime minister who led Britain into the European Community, is to join Britain's highest order of chivalry. The Queen has appointed the MP for Old Bexley and Sidcup and father of the House of Commons a Knight Companion of the Most Noble Order of the Garter. He will now be entitled to be known as Sir Edward. The honour also goes to Lord Sainsbury of Preston Candover and Viscount Ridley.
