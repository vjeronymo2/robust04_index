May 15, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

GLIMPSES

More than 160 young girls were treated for hysteria and fainting at a New Kids on the Block concert in London Sunday night, which had to be rescheduled from Friday because of a power outage. The girls were passing out even before members of the Boston-based group came on-stage, but the supervisor of the arena said the scene still didn't compare to Beatlemania.

"It was nowhere near it," he said. "If there wasn't any Beatles I should imagine this would have been in the history books." . . . The greatest show on Earth isn't a rock group. The band Kiss says it will no longer advertise its tour, which started during the weekend in Lubbock, Tex., as the greatest show on Earth after being hit with a cease-and-desist order from the Ringling Bros. and Barnum & Bailey Circus.
