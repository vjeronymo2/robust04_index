931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: QS drops to Pounds 1.68m after higher costs Higher costs following the devaluation of sterling in September 1992 hit QS Holdings, the clothing retailer, which saw profits for the half year to July 30 decline from Pounds 3.86m to Pounds 1.68m. Turnover moved ahead from Pounds 23.2m to Pounds 24.7m. Earnings per share came out at 2.79p (6.57p); the interim dividend, however, is unchanged at 1.56p.
