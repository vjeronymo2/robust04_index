BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered that the price of purchased cotton seeds be raised from 5,000 to 7,000 dinars per tonne. A responsible source at the Ministry of Industry and Minerals told INA that leader President Saddam Husayn's order to increase the prices of cotton seeds comes in line with the state's general orientation of providing raw materials for industrial crops.
