Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] In a letter sent to Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin, settlers in the Jordan Valley write that if reports are true that Israel agreed to set up three territorial corridors -- which will dissect the Jordan Rift Valley -- this constitutes a declaration of war on settlement in the Jordan Valley. Our settlement affairs correspondent Gil Litman reports that the settlers go on to tell the prime minister: Do not force settlers who merely want to farm to turn into warriors fighting for their homes and lives. The settlers say they will not put up with such lethal blows and will not allow the establishment of a Palestinian corridor anywhere in the Jordan Valley.
