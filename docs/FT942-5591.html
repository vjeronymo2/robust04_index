940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Mid Kent advances 22% to Pounds 7.5m Mid Kent Holdings, which supplies water to Kent and Sussex and provides consultancy services to the water supply industry, lifted pre-tax profits by 22 per cent, from Pounds 6.17m to Pounds 7.51m, in the year to March 31. Turnover advanced to Pounds 36m (Pounds 31.1m) with acquisitions contributing Pounds 2.25m. Earnings rose from 26.5p to 33p and a proposed final dividend of 6.5p makes an 11.5p (10.5p) total.
