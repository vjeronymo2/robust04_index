921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / The Conservative Party at Brighton: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference called on the government to encourage 'personal responsibility' through the benefit system; Urged greater parental choice in education; Asked ministers to broaden the UK manufacturing base in order to increase employment and improve the balance of trade; Called for policies to improve the effectiveness and quality of the criminal justice system; Supported the use of private sector expertise to achieve better service for transport users; Backed strong security, economic regeneration and political development to end terrorism in Northern Ireland. TODAY: Conference will debate employment, the environment, the economy, health, defence and the national heritage.
