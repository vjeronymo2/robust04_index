940105 FT 05 JAN 94 / International Company News: J. P. Morgan sells trust business J. P. Morgan sold its US corporate trust business in the latest sign of consolidation in the fiercely competitive US market for custody and other securities services, writes Richard Waters. Morgan business, which acts as trustee to 3,800 bond issues for 650 US companies, has been bought by First Bank System, a Minneapolis-based banking group. First Bank said this and other acquisitions would make its corporate trustee business the fifth largest in the US.
