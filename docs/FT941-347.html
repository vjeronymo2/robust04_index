940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Henderson Highland Net asset value per share at Henderson Highland Trust improved from 108.1p to 131.89p over the 12 months to February 28. Net revenue for the period came out at Pounds 1.52m (Pounds 1.38m) for earnings per share of 5.82p (5.3p). An unchanged final dividend of 1.4p is recommended for a maintained total of 5.6p.
