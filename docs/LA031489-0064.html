March 14, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Regis Philbin and Kathie Lee Gifford will hop over to the White House on March 23 to preview the Easter activities planned by the First Family. They'll feature their report the next day, Good Friday, on their syndicated "Live With Regis & Kathie Lee" show, seen weekdays at 10 a.m. on KHJ-TV Channel 9. ALEENE MacMINN
