Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Sorokin] [Text] Kazan February 16 TASS -- "The main thing is that the treaty was concluded of the basis of two constitutions now in force -- those of Russia and Tatarstan, not violating these fundamental laws but being guided by them," President Mintimer Shaymiyev of Tatarstan stressed in his speech on Tatarstan television devoted to the treaty between Russia and Tatarstan signed in Moscow. This document, he said, recognizes the right to self-determination, equality, defense of sovereignty and territorial integrity, and the single economic space. Tatarstan exclusively owns and uses its land, bowels of the earth, and movable and immovable property. Simultaneously an instrument of the implementation of this treaty was adopted which represents 11 inter-governmental agreements. The position of Tatarstan, Mintimer Shaymiyev noted, is directed at the democratic formation of Russia. In the concluding part of his speech Mintimer Shaymiyev thanked President Boris Yeltsin for his "invariable and consistent position shown to the very end -- up to the signing of the treaty."
