BFN [Excerpt] The Press Office of the Islamic State of Afghanistan reported to the Bakhtar Information Agency that esteemed Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, received last night esteemed General Mohammad Esmail Khan, general amir of the Southwestern Zone, along with his accompanying delegation. At the meeting, Rabbani spoke in detail about the political-military situation of the country and gave detailed instructions to the governors of the provinces who are included in the delegation with esteemed General Mohammad Esmael Khan. In this meeting, esteemed Lieutenant-General Taj Mohammad Qari Baba spoke on behalf of the country's governors, within this delegation, and expressed his readiness for the consolidation of the Islamic State and defense of the gains of jihad.
