April 29, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SHERIFF'S RACE

A community group in Fallbrook held a meeting with all the candidates for sheriff a week ago, which was most enlightening. I would hope that more groups do the same, because there doesn't appear to be much in the press about this very important race.

One observation made was a statement by one of the candidates, a Mr. (Jack) Drown. He said he has worked for no one else but the Sheriff's Department in his entire adult life -- over 20 years providing us, the citizens of this county, with the best law enforcement possible, claiming that no other candidate matches his experience and skills.

Well, all I have to say is that, if the current state of the Sheriff's Department is due to Mr. Drown's best efforts and vast experience and leadership, I suggest he do us all a favor and step aside and allow change to take place. It's apparent that he and Sheriff John Duffy haven't got it right in 20 years. We can't afford another 20.

DAMON THORNBURG

Fallbrook
