Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Politburo member Carlos Lage has called for the eradication of profiteers who are illegally filling their pockets in the black market. In a meeting with 102 presidents of the Havana People's Councils, Lage, also the secretary of the Council of Ministers, suggested that no more licenses be granted or that they be suspended for craftsmen whose products are made with raw materials of a dubious origin. The participants in the meeting advocated the need to increase taxes on surplus profits and price regulations. Likewise, the presidents of the Havana People's Councils, stressed that domestic messengers ought to pay taxes since they are earning much more than what Interior Commerce established. Other issues discussed in the meeting were Havana's garbage removal system and the delivery of the winter campaign products to the people. Pedro Chavez, president of the people's government in Havana and member of the Central Committee, was also present at the meeting.
