BFN [Text] This morning at the People's Palace, His Highness Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah, the heir apparent and prime minister, received His Excellency Douglas Hogg, the British minister of state for foreign affairs, and the delegation accompanying him, on the occasion of his visit to the country. The meeting was attended by 'Abd al-'Aziz Dakhil al-Dakhil, the minister of state for cabinet affairs and minister of planning, Sulayman Majid al-Shahin, under secretary at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Shaykh 'Ali 'Abdallah al-Ahmad, head of the European section at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and British Ambassador to Kuwait William Fullerton.
