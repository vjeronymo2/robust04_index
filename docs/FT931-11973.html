930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Prior Interest charges, costs relating to its head office move and compensation in respect of a former director, left Prior, the property and architectural ironmongery group, Pounds 467,000 in the red pre-tax in the half year to September 30. Losses last time were Pounds 411,000. Mr James Prior, the chairman, said that virtually all of the turnover, of Pounds 596,000 (Pounds 4.03m), related to the non-property activity, JD Beardmore, which had traded above break-even for the first time in four years. The group had been negotiating with its banks and third parties to restructure and release certain debt. Losses per share amounted to 1.6p (1.4p).
