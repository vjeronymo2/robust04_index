BFN [Text] Speaking at a news conference on the morning of 31 May at the end of a cabinet session, Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa touched on the issue of North Korea's suspected development of nuclear weapons and said, "I have heard that the Japanese Embassy in Beijing is already in contact with the Chinese Government." In this way the foreign minister disclosed that the Japanese Government is asking the Chinese Government through the Japanese Embassy in Beijing to persuade North Korea. At the news conference, the foreign minister also disclosed that Japan and South Korea have continued to discuss the issue with the United States through their embassies and missions in New York and Washington, adding, "Although I cannot disclose all the Japanese Government is doing, the Japanese Government is doing everything it can in an effort to resolve the issue."
