Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 28 KYODO -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rhongi will extend his stay in Japan by one day until Friday [4 March], Foreign Ministry officials said Monday. Zhu, in Japan since last Wednesday for talks with Japanese leaders, was scheduled to leave on Thursday after visiting Osaka, Okayama and Fukuoka Prefectures in western Japan. But the Chinese side requested to extend the stay until Friday without giving any specific reasons, the officials said. Zhu will return home Friday from Fukuoka, they said.
