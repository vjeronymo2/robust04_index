BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali arrived for an official visit to Bonn on Tuesday [12 April] following an invitation by Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl. In his talks, which started with a meeting with Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel and are scheduled to continue on Wednesday in a meeting with Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl, the UN secretary general intends to encourage Germany's participation in a permanent UN reaction force. The Germans have announced that they were seeking closer cooperation with and within the United Nations, including a seat on the Security Council, and the transfer of UN organizations to Bonn. Butrus-Ghali will also meet members of parliament before his departure scheduled for Wednesday afternoon.
