940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Castings Shares of Castings, the maker of iron and precision castings, rose 12p to 260p yesterday on news of a 20 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Pounds 5.25m for the year to March 31. Turnover expanded from Pounds 33.75m to Pounds 39.33m and earnings per share emerged at 16.27p (14.3p). A final dividend of 3.65p (3.05p) raises the total from 4.35p to 5.2p. A 1-for-1 scrip is proposed.
