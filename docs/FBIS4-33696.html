BFN [Text] The government of Judge Emile Jonassaint has decided to release the Haitian Armed Forces from managing detention centers. Following a meeting of the Council of Ministers at the National Palace and a follow-up visit by the ministerial delegation to the national penitentiary, the government of the provisional president has decided to put the management of the detention centers in the hands of civilian authorities. Meanwhile, the provisional government points out that prompt measures to improve the conditions of the prisoners will be taken.
