940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: CSR steps up expansion into Asia CSR, the Australian building materials and sugar group, yesterday stepped up its foray into Asia, acquiring a 50 per cent interest in Corinthian Industries (Singapore) for ADollars 35m (USDollars 25m). The company makes wooden doors and door components in Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia and has sales of around ADollars 120m. The new joint venture will be headquartered in Kuala Lumpar. It is the latest in a spate of Asian investments by CSR, which have included a ADollars 45m investment in a Tianjin-based quarrying and concrete business in northern China last week.
