940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Blockleys shares show further rise Blockleys, the Shropshire-based brick and pavior maker, yesterday said it knew of no reason for the recent increase in its share price. Last night Blockleys' shares closed up 12p at 83p following a 5p rise on Friday. In 1992, the company reported a fall in pre-tax profits to Pounds 352,000 (Pounds 1.06m) and for the first half of 1993 there was a further decline to Pounds 261,000 (Pounds 410,000).
