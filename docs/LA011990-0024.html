January 19, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

DOMINGUEZ HILLS TO PLAY NORTHRIDGE AND CAL STATE L.A.

California Collegiate Athletic Assn. games at Cal State Northridge tonight and Cal State Los Angeles on Saturday await the men's and women's basketball teams from Cal State Dominguez Hills.

The Toro men, who have not won a road game this season, are 0-2 in CCAA play and 4-11 overall. The Lady Toros are 1-1 and 9-7.

The women's games begin at 5:45 and the men start at 8:05 both nights.
