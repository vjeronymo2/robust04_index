921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / ScottishPower to buy more coal SCOTTISHPOWER, the privatised electricity company, is to buy a further 250,000 tonnes of Scottish open-cast coal from British Coal. Last month British Coal agreed to sell 250,000 tonnes of coal to ScottishPower in addition to its regular supply of 2.5m tonnes. ScottishPower says it has bought 5m tonnes of coal this year, of which 80 per cent comes from Scotland.
