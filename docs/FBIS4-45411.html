Year AU1106182894 Chisinau BASAPRESS in English 2000 GMT 9 Jun 94 AU1106182894 Chisinau BASAPRESS English BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 9/6/1994 -- About 12,000 patients are currently under the observation of the Chisinau City Narcotics Department, their number increasing at a rate of 70 percent per year, declared at a session of the Mayor's Office Vasile Galearschi, chief of the city Department for Health Care, cited the newspaper CURIERUL DE SEARA. A similar 70-percent increase, compared to the first three months of last year was registered in the number of cases concerning patients suffering from drug-related psychoses. Over the past three years the number of cases of drug and other toxic substances addiction has risen 2.9 times. Galearschi specified that the City Narcotics Department cannot work at its full capacity because of its disastrous material situation, in the absence of a specialized narcotics unit. According to a decision of the Chisinau Mayor's Office, the program of social-economic development for Chisinau stipulates the construction of a narcotics unit in 1995, stated the cited source.
