May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CHINA'S LOST GENERATION

"China's Lost Generation" is another lesson for us in what our freedoms mean to others and how important our way of life has become to the rest of the world.

"Tyranny Begins at Home" (by Harry Shearer), is a well-worded warning that we must continually work to keep our freedoms.

Thanks for both, and let me say the magazine has become a must-read for me; it is always well-done and thought-provoking.

HELEN SONGER, Van Nuys
