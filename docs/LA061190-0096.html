June 11, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: DANA POINT;

SKATEBOARDER DIES OF HEAD INJURIES

A 19-year-old skateboarder has died of injuries sustained last week when he fell off his skateboard while hanging onto the back of a moving car and crushed his skull on a shopping center driveway, authorities said Sunday.

Joel Christopher Banks, a student at Saddleback College, was pronounced dead about 1:30 a.m. Saturday, said a spokeswoman at Mission Hospital Regional Medical Center in Mission Viejo.

On Wednesday night, Banks and Graeme E. Swayn, a roommate, held onto the back of their friend's Toyota Celica while the car towed them up a steep driveway leading out of the Monarch Bay Plaza shopping center. Banks' skateboard caught a patch of rough concrete, and he was knocked backward onto the ground. Swayn was not injured.

Banks, who was not wearing a helmet, crushed his skull and slipped into a coma.

Banks moved from Dublin, a Bay Area town near Oakland, two years ago.
