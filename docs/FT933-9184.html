930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Pounds 6m Shropshire farmland bought THE Lands Improvement Group, an agricultural property company, has bought 6,746 acres of Shropshire farmland for about Pounds 6m. The North Shropshire Estate at Ellesmere includes 24 tenanted farms and 14 cottages and smallholdings. The land, which yields about 5 per cent, is being sold by Mercantile and General Reinsurance Company, a subsidiary of Prudential Corporation.
