DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY Federal Energy Regulatory Commission 18 CFR Part 284 [Docket No. RM93&hyph;4&hyph;000] Standards for Electronic Bulletin Boards Required Under Part 284 of the Commission's Regulations March 11, 1994 AGENCY: Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, DOE. ACTION: Notice of filing and opportunity to file comments.

SUMMARY: The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) has received a report dealing with the allocation of costs related to Electronic Bulletin Boards. The Commission is affording interested persons an opportunity to file comments on this filing. DATES: Comments due by March 17, 1994.
