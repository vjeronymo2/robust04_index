December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GOING UP: It may be the happiest place on earth, but it isn't the cheapest: Ticket prices are up again at Disneyland (D7), where a $2 increase brings the cost of adult admission to $25.50. Kids now pay $20.50 and parking is up a dollar to $4. . . . Will this hurt attendance? Not likely, say industry experts. Dana Parsons
