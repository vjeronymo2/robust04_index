June 1, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The Celebrity Waiter Luncheon on May 10 at the Holiday Inn in Irvine raised an estimated $12,000 for the American Heart Assn. Karen Quirk was chairwoman.

The tribute to James Roosevelt on May 20 at the Irvine Hilton and Towers earned an estimated $200,000 for Chapman College. Doy Henley was chairwoman.

The Rock Around the Clock musical review on May 20 at the Doubletree Hotel in Orange raised an estimated $8,000 for the Eli Home for Abused Children. LorriGalloway was chairwoman.

The Rock Around the Clock luncheon and fashion show on May 20 at the Anaheim Marriott Hotel earned an estimated $10,000 for Western Medical Center/Anaheim. Kim Weingarten was chairwoman.
