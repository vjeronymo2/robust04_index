920424 FT 24 APR 92 / UK Company News: Securities Trust of Scotland lower Securities Trust of Scotland reported net asset value of 70.1p at March 31, down from the 76.9p of September 30 and 73.4p a year earlier. Net profits fell from Pounds 10m to Pounds 9.69m for earnings per share of 3p (3.1p). A final dividend of 2.17p is recommended for a total of 3.25p (3.1p).
