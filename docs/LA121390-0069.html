December 13, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: VAN NUYS WOMAN FOUND SHOT TO DEATH

A 32-year old Van Nuys woman was found shot to death in a car parked in West Los Angeles, Los Angeles police said Wednesday.

The body of Martha Ramos, shot several times, was found in a car in the 11000 block of National Boulevard near Sawtelle Boulevard about 7:30 p.m. Tuesday, Lt. Ron McCall said.

No arrests have been made.
