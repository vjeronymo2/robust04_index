BFN [Text] Yerevan ("GOLOS ARMENII" Daily-Past), 06/25/94 -- It's hard to say for sure, what is the current situation regarding the construction of an Iran-Armenia gas pipe-line, though official discussions in this regard were being carried for several years. The information from official sources is quite contradictory. According to RA [Republic of Armenia] Governmental press service, the matter is getting no further than negotiations, and representatives of both sides continue discussion of construction details. At the same time, RA Ministry of Fuel and Energy reported that the negotiations have been finished, and projecting work is currently being done.
