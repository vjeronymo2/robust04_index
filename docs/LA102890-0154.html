October 28, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL

Poway Topples Torrey Pines for Title

Poway and Torrey Pines emerged from different brackets and met in the championship match of the Santa Barbara/ASICS National girls volleyball tournament Saturday. The match went five games with Poway prevailing, 1-15, 15-7, 13-15, 15-13, 15-7.

Poway had two players named to the all-tournament team: setter Shana Purvis and middle blocker Heather Burke.

Poway and Torrey Pines meet again Tuesday in a Palomar League match.

Football

Southern Conference

Rasham Salaam ran for 185 yards and four touchdowns on seven carries to lead La Jolla Country Day to a 57-12 victory over Santa Fe Christian. The Southern Conference game was called after two quarters because of the 40-point rule.

Army-Navy 31, Holtville 14 -- James Reed ran 24 times and gained 161 yards and scored a touchdown to lead Army-Navy.
