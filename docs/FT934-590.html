931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / IBM staff to work longer IBM Deutschland Inform-ationssysteme, German unit of International Business Machines of the US, said it would extend working hours for its employees next year without paying them more, Reuter reports from Stuttgart. IBM Deutschland said many of its 12,000 workers would work 38 hours a week from January, instead of 36 hours, without extra pay. IBM, which had sought a 40 hour working week, said it had reached an accord with the DAG white collar union.
