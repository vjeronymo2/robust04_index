BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 11 -- President Le Duc Anh received here today Australian Prime Minister [P.M.] Paul Keating who pays a courtesy visit to the Vietnamese leader on an official friendship visit to Vietnam. Speaking at the reception, Prime Minister Keating sincerely thanked President Le Duc Anh for his reception and highlighted the results of the talks he had held earlier with Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. Mr Keating said that Australia was ready to increase investment capital and aid for Vietnam's development in the fields of education, healthcare, mining, telecommunications, infrastructure construction, commerce and so on. He expressed his pleasure at the achievements gained by the Vietnamese people in their renovation process, and he said he believed that following his visit the friendship and cooperation between Australia and Vietnam would be further consolidated and developed. For his part, President Le Duc Anh welcomed Prime Minister Keating's current Vietnam visit, and he said he considered it a new step of development in the relations between the two countries. He sincrely thanked the Australian Government and people and P.M. Keating himself for their friendly feelings and precious assistance to the Vietnamese people in national construction as well as to the Vietnamese immigrants to quickly integrate themselves into the community life in Australia. President Le Duc Anh informed Prime Minister Keating of Vietnam's foreign policy to make friends with all countries in the world. He expressed his belief that the relations of friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Australia would be increasingly developed, thus actively contributing to peace, stability and development in the region.
