Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The BOTAS [Pipeline and Petroleum Transport Corporation] oil terminal in Batman came under PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] rocket attack yesterday. The attack took place at 1910 last night. Three petrol tanks were hit in the attack, which was launched from the Mardin-Batman highway. Nearly 700 barrels of crude oil leaked from the tanks into the surrounding area but did not catch fire.
