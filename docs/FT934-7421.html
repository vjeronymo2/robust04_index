931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / International Company News: Merger reaffirmed VOLVO yesterday reaffirmed its commitment to the car and truck merger with Renault. The Swedish motor group said 'further clarification' was expected shortly from Paris to help it overcome a shareholder revolt. 'It is our conviction that the transaction with Renault is industrially and commercially correct and should be implemented,' said Mr Soren Gyll.
