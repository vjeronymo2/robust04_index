BFN [Text] A meeting was held in Irbil today between officials from the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK] and the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP] in the presence of a delegation from Socialist International. Party leader Mas'ud Barzani, Dr. Rawj Nuri Shawij, and [names indistinct] attended the meeting from the KDP. From the PUK the meeting was attended by leader Jalal Talabani, Kusret Rasul 'Ali, and [names indistinct]. During a bilateral meeting held between brother Talabani and Barzani, an agreement was reached to invite the political bureaus of the PUK and KDP to a meeting tomorrow, call on the government and the parliament to immediately assume their customary duties, and suspend media campaigns. An agreement was also reached on the need for the two political bureaus to prepare the necessary plans to tackle all the problems between the two sides and invite the leaders of the Kurdistan Front to the same meeting to help resolve these problems.
