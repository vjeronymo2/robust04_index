January 10, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

LOUISIANA INMATE WRITER

It is heartwarming to know that Rideau has prospered in prison instead of succumbing to the usual pitfalls of incarceration. Nonetheless, Rideau is right where he belongs. It matters not that he has learned how to write his feelings.

Rideau's crime was a cold, calculated execution. He has been rehabilitated. Now he is accepting his punishment as prescribed by law.

TOM BASINSKI

Chula Vista
