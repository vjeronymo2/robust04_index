BFN [Text] Sanaa, 2 May (INA)--'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council, has welcomed an initiative by the parties to the dialogue committee from outside the ruling Yemeni coalition, who have called for a meeting among 'Ali Salim al-Bid, President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, and leaders of other parties to put an end to the worsening crisis and infighting in the country. Al-Bid welcomed the initiative when he received members of the dialogue committee from outside the coalition today. The Yemeni president has also agreed to the initiative, deeming it a positive beginning to end the crisis in the country.
