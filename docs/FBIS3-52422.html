Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian sources in Tunis informed our correspondent Yoni Ben-Menahem that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and his special West Bank affairs adviser, Jibril al-Rajub, yesterday called Jamal al-Dik, a senior Fatah West Bank activist who had resigned last week from his organizational Fatah post. In the wake of this contact, Jamal al-Dik agreed to withdraw his resignation. The sources said that during his conversation with al-Dik Yasir 'Arafat expressed understanding for the reasons that led to the resignation and his interest in the demands of the young Fatah leadership in the territories.
