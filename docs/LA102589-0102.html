October 25, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SWALLOWS: San Juan Capistrano (B1) could become the first Orange County city to require an archeological survey before any new construction. . . . The proposed law is expected to come before the city's Cultural Arts and Heritage Commission by the end of the year. . . . Preservationists want it, citing recent historical finds downtown, including previously undocumented foundations of 18th-Century adobes at the site of a planned development. Dana Parsons and Rick VanderKnyff
