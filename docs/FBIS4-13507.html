BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Apr (MENA) -- Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser of Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, has stated that President 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will visit Cairo from 2 to 5 May. Sha'th explained that during the first day of the visit 'Arafat and Rabin will complete the discussion on issues that are still the subject of controversy between the Palestinian and Israeli delegations. He noted that the size of the Jericho area is among the subjects to be agreed upon between 'Arafat and Rabin. He said that the agreement on the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement will be signed the following day.
