BFN [Text] Pristina, April 11 (ATA) -- Great forces of the Serbian Army, armoured vehicles and other military facilities have been deployed in the Han of Elez, near the Kosova-Macedonian border. In the Peldenica village in the same zone, four other armoured vehicles have been sent, the information centre of Kosova reports. It also says that Serbian police has made a check point in a road that links some villages with the town of Hani i Elezit. It controls Albanian citizens who travel in the villages of the border zone. Three tanks have been staying in Ura e Kashanit. Serbian military forces in these areas are provoking citizens and plundering them, the information centre says. Movements and concentrations of the Serbian military and police forces are noticed near Gjakova, bordering Albania. Serbian information sources report that the commander in chief of the Yugoslav Army, Momcilo Perisic, stayed several days in Kosova "on a visit to the units of the Pristina army corps" and to control the fighting readiness of the units in the terrain.
