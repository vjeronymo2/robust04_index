September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

STROUSE SCORES TWICE AS OCC WINS IN SOCCER

Andy Strouse scored twice and Orange Coast College defeated Mt. San Antonio, 7-2, in a nonconference men's soccer match at Orange Coast.

The Pirates (2-2), who had a 2-0 lead at halftime, also got goals from Jason Hooper, Chris Doharty, Kevin Villar, Lance Barker and Darin Courter.
