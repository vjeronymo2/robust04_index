BFN [Text] An agreement has been reached between the government and Marco Pannella. The latter and his supporters will now form part of the ruling government coalition. The news was released during a joint news conference given by Silvio Berlusconi and Pannella. Berlusconi said that the agreement "is a visible signal that the government is of the center." Pannella stressed that some points of the agreement "could bind us for the entire legislative term." The agreement, Berlusconi stressed, is a reply to the demand for change expressed by the country. The agreement envisions, among other things, a single round for elections and establishing a UN tribunal in order to try those responsible for crimes against humanity.
