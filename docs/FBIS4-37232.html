BFN [Text] Cairo, 18 Jun (MENA) -- Mr. Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Presidential Council in the recently declared Republic of Yemen in Aden and assistant secretary of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], has stated that talks will take place tomorrow, Sunday 19 June, in Cairo to discuss a cease-fire in Yemen. He said that a ranking Yemeni delegation has arrived in Cairo to discuss the mechanism for a cease-fire. The delegation will meet with Egyptian officials to inform them of the latest developments in the situation in Yemen and the effects of the war so far. He added that the major objective now is to stop the bloodshed among the sons of Yemen in order to preserve the remaining institutions of the country.
