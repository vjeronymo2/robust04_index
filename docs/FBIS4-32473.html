BFN [Text] Warsaw, 23 Jun--Poland's President Lech Walesa today sent a letter to Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski, assuring him of "the unchanging confidence of the president of the Polish Republic in the minister and in the entire Polish foreign service." Here is the text of the letter, passed to PAP by the presidential press office: ``Minister! ``Polish diplomacy yesterday became the object of a dramatic political debate. Many people who have served the Republic well and with devotion must today feel bitter and disoriented; can they continue to act in the name of the Republic? Can they credibly continue to represent it? ``I ask that at this difficult moment you assure these persons--all the employees of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs--of the unchanging confidence of the president of the Polish Republic in the minister and in the entire Polish foreign service. "[Signed] Lech Walesa."
