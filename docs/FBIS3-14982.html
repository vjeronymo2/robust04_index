Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to unofficial information, which INTERFAX has obtained from the Belarusian parliament's Tallying Commission, the required number of votes has been secured for the introduction of the presidential post in this republic. The Belarusian parliament continues personalized voting on the basic provisions of the draft constitution. The official results will be summed up in the evening of March 1. The basic provisions of the Belarussian Constitution will be adopted if at least 232 deputies vote for them. The Counting Commission told Interfax that by the end of the morning session, more than 240 deputies had cast their votes in their favor.
