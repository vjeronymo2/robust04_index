CSO [Text] Bombay, April 27--Interlink Petroleum Ltd (IPL) has joined hands with Russneft, Russian oil company, to conduct oil exploration projects in India and Russia. As per the agreement, Rossneft will participate in Interlink Petroleum's public issue when it enters the capital market in May. It will also provide technology and equipment in oil exploration to its Indian partner and provisions for allocation of prospective oilfields in Russia in consortium with other companies.
