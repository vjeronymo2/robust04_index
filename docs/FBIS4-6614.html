BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from "NHK News 7" program] [Text] Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata held meetings with Masayoshi Takemura, chairman of Sakigake [Harbinger], and leaders of New Party Mirai [Future] and Jiyuto [Liberal Party] to ask for their cooperation with the new administration. Sakigake plans to support the government from outside the cabinet. New Party Mirai and Jiyuto have recently been formed by defectors from the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP]. Takemura offered the following comments: [Begin recording] These matters are confirmed. I agree with the new administration's intention to continue with and further the reform efforts undertaken by the Hosokawa administration. As to particulars about policies, I have my own views regarding the finer details, but I told Hata I agree in general with the policies worked out among the coalition parties. [end recording] The leaders of New Party Mirai and Jiyuto made the following comments: [Begin recording] [Michihiko Kano, head of New Party Mirai; identified by caption] Since we appraise Foreign Minister Hata for such matters as his basic political stance, I told him we would give our cooperation. [Koji Kakizawa, head of Jiyuto; identified by caption] Since Hata asked for our cooperation, I told him I look forward to giving him a definite answer after discussing the matter with lower house members who are my Jiyuto comrades. [end recording]
