BFN [Unattributed report: "Foreign Ministry Protests"] [Text] In regard to the latest incidents on the Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]-Yugoslav border, involving usurping the place Cupino Brdo and imprisoning a Macedonian soldier by Serbian troops, the Macedonian Foreign Ministry sent a note of protest to the Yugoslav Foreign Ministry, against such behavior of the Yugoslav Army, which violates the Macedonian border, creating conditions for heavy consequences. At the same time, the Macedonian Foreign Ministry demanded an urgent session of the representatives of the two countries' foreign ministries, to agree on undertaking steps to definitely determine the border line. The Macedonian side underlined such negotiations would present basis for development of mutual trust, communication, and cooperation between the two nations.
