July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LOVE/HATE: The Rams have been here for 10 years now (C1), and at times there was little love lost between player and fan. . . . Players have described county fans as bland as white bread -- and Coach John Robinson once said his team played better on the road without the fans. . . . A poll last winter showed that in Orange County, about a third of the people wouldn't miss the Rams if they moved and another third weren't sure. Mark I. Pinsky and Martin Beck
