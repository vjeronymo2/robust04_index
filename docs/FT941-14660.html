940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Tamaris Tamaris, the Berkshire-based long term care provider, returned to the black with Pounds 2,000 pre-tax in the six months to September 30. The outcome, achieved on turnover of Pounds 1.75m (Pounds 1.68m), compared with losses of Pounds 28,000 last time and Pounds 50,000 at the March year-end. Mr William Fitch, chairman, said the improvement reflected continued operating efficiencies, higher occupancy levels and reduced interest rates. Earnings per share were 0.01p, against losses of 1.26p.
