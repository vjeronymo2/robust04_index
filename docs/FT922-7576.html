920518 FT 18 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 19.2m workload for Alfred McAlpine The ALFRED McALPINE CONSTRUCTION company has been awarded Pounds 19.2m worth of contracts all over the UK. Among the clients are Wormold Holdings, Shropshire County Council, British Gas, Leeds City Council and Ossory Estates. The Wormold contract comprises an office block in Manchester, valued at Pounds 3.8m. Shropshire County Council has placed a Pounds 595,000 bridge improvement project and Leeds City Council two school improvement schemes valued at Pounds 2.36m. In the South East, the company has been awarded a Pounds 6m batch of public housing contracts, and in Glasgow, the management company is to undertake a Pounds 5m shopping centre refurbishment project for Ossory Estates in Greenoch, Scotland.
