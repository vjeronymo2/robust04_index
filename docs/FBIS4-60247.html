BFN [Text] Environment Minister Yosi Sarid has warned that if Yasir 'Arafat does not retract his remarks on honoring agreements, Israel will not abide by the accord. Sarid, who has been closely involved in the autonomy negotiations, says that 'Arafat's statements in the Johannesburg mosque earlier this month on signing agreements has created a crisis of confidence. The PLO leader, Sarid said, must declare his statements there null and void and reaffirm his total commitment to the peace process, in particular his support for a determined and open fight against terror. The environment minister was interviewed by Qol Yisra'el reporter Shulamit Schmerling during a visit to Haifa.
