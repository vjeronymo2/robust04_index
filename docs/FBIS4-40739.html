BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 19 June -- By a decision of Russian President Boris Yeltsin a commission has been set up on a parity basis from representatives of the executive bodies of the Russian Federation and the Republic of Tatarstan for the purpose of implementing government agreements and the treaty between the Russian Federation and Tatarstan on dividing areas of responsibility and mutually delegating powers by state bodies of the federation and the republic, and supervising their fulfilment. By the same presidential decision, which was signed on Saturday [18 June], Oleg Soskovets, first vice prime minister of the Government of the Russian Federation, was appointed chairman of the commission.
