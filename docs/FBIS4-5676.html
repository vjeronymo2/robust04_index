BFN [Excerpt] Tokyo, April 13 KYODO -- A Tokyo district court judge rejected again Wednesday [13 April] a request for bail from former Construction Minister Kishiro Nakamura to allow him to vote for a new prime minister. Nakamura's first bail application was dismissed last Tuesday on the grounds he might destroy evidence. Nakamura asked Tuesday to be freed on bail so he can take part in a Diet vote to pick a successor to Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, who abruptly announced his resignation last Friday over financial dealings. No date has been set for the Diet vote yet. [passage omitted]
