Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin met today with Spain's Defense Minister Julian Vargas Garcia. The two agreed that Israel would upgrade Spanish tanks and planes. According to a Qol Yisra'el reporter accompanying the prime minister on his visit, the deal includes the installing of advanced electronic equipment in Spanish Air Force planes. And the speaker of the Knesset Shevah Weiss is being received tomorrow in Tokyo by the Emperor of Japan. His hosts are being quoted as saying this is a special gesture and it follows what was called enthusiastic support in the Japanese Government for the Middle East peace process.
