Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 17, BNS -- Field Marshal Richard Vincent, chairman of the NATO Military Committee, on Thursday [17 March] ended his two-day visit to Lithuania. On Thursday, the NATO official met with Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas and members of the parliament's National Security Committee. Vincent held discussions at the Lithuanian Defense Ministry with members of the working group that was set up to prepare a presentation document for Lithuania's participation in the Partnership for Peace program. Before departure, Vincent also visited the Lithuanian field army brigade Gelezinis Vilkas (Iron Wolf) and met with its commanders.
