931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Gibbon Lyons Increased market share helped Gibbon Lyons raise pre-tax profits by 38 per cent in the six months to September 30. On sales up 10 per cent at Pounds 12.7m (Pounds 11.5m) the pre-tax figure increased from Pounds 712,000 to Pounds 980,000. The USM-quoted maker of printing inks and related products raised its interim dividend to 2.2p (2p) on earnings per share of 6.3p (4.4p).
