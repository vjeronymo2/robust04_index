940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, Good general demand. Bright East Africans were dearer following quality with coloury mediums also fully firm to dearer. Plainer mediums were mainly easier. Better Ceylons were bid up with quality, others were fully firm. Ceylons and brighter Zimbabwes were firm to dearer but plainer Central Africans were easier where sold. Quality 190p/kg nom, good medium 138p/kg, medium 118p/kg, low medium 85p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 248p for a Rwanda pf. 1.
