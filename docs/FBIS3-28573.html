Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar [Burma] and commander in chief of the Defense Services, received a delegation led by Mr. Somsavat Lengsavat, foreign minister of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], which is currently in Yangon [Rangoon], at the Ministry Defense's Dagon House at 0930 this morning. Lieutenant General Maung Aye, deputy commander in chief of the Defense Services and commander in chief of the Army; Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the SLORC; Lt. Gen. Tin U, secretary-2 of the SLORC; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs; Thura U Aung Htet, director general of the Protocol Department; and Kideng Thammavong, ambassador of the LPDR to the Union of Myanmar, were also present at the meeting.
