March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Beach Boys Fuel Up for Concert Tour: Texaco is sending out good vibrations to "Beach Boys" fans by sponsoring a free 10-city concert tour starting May 1 at the San Diego Sports Arena.

No tickets will be sold for the concert; instead, those who want to attend can try to win tickets by playing a scratch-off game at Texaco stations. The Second Harvest National Food Bank Network will also give away tickets in exchange for donations of perishable food. The tour makes stops in Phoenix, Seattle and New York. BETH KLEID
