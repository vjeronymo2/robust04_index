BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Solntsev] [Text] Moscow June 27 TASS -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrives in Moscow on Monday to prepare the second Russian- Chinese summit to be held in September when Chinese leader Jiang Zemin comes to the Russian capital. The Chinese minister will hold talks in Moscow with his Russian counterpart Andrey Kozyrev. Following them, the sides will initial several bilateral documents to be signed by the Russian and Chinese leaders at the summit. Qian will meet speaker of the Russian parliament upper chamber Vladimir Shumeyko and will be possibly received by President Boris Yeltsin and cabinet head Viktor Chernomyrdin. Apart from bilateral relations, the sides will discuss, in the context of preparations for the summit, the most urgent international issues, including the nuclear problem on the Korean peninsula. The chinese minister will make a trip to some former Soviet republics, which will last till July 2. Qian will depart from Moscow for Byelorussia [Belarus] on Wednesday. Then, he will visit Lithuania and Estonia.
