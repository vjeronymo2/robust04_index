910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Newarthill in loss Newarthill, the family-controlled holding company for Sir Robert McAlpine, the civil engineer, announced a pre-tax loss of Pounds 7.8m for 1990 after interest charges doubled to Pounds 18m. Profits last time were Pounds 2.26m. Turnover advanced from Pounds 533.26m to Pounds 648.95m. Losses per share were 92.4p (18.7p). Newarthill lost its stock market listing in 1989.
