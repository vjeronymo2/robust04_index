Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 14 (YONHAP) -- Former President Chon Tu-hwan's "donation" of political funds totaling 14 billion won (17.5 million U.S. dollars), including 100 million won in interest, to the government, had been transferred to the national treasury as of April 25, 1989, Board of Audit and Inspection (BAI) officials said Monday [14 March]. Precisely what happened to the funds is not known because national treasury funds are channeled, irrespective of their source, into the government's fiscal plan, the officials said. The BAI looked into whether Chon's donation was placed in the national treasury at the request of the opposition Democratic Party during the recent parliamentary session.
