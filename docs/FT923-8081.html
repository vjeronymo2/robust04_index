920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / New Zealand deficit narrows New Zealand's balance of payments figures sharply improved in the year to March 31, and the Department of Statistics said this was further proof of a recovery in the economy, Terry Hall writes from Wellington. The country recorded a deficit of NZDollars 329m (Pounds 93m) in the year to March. This compared with a deficit of NZDollars 2bn for the previous year. While imports rose during the March quarter, exports continued to soar and the trade surplus for the year to March rose to a record NZDollars 3.55bn.
