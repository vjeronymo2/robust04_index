July 15, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

Correction Appended

TITAN ASSISTANT ROGERS QUITS FOR RIVERSIDE JOB

Dennis Rogers, an assistant coach for Cal State Fullerton's baseball team, has resigned to become baseball coach at Riverside College, Fullerton officials announced Friday.

Rogers, 37, has been an a assistant with the Titans for the past three seasons. He will replace Tim Brown at Riverside, who was fired.

Rogers graduated from Cal Poly Pomona in 1976. He played 1 1/2 seasons in the San Francisco Giants' organization before an injury ended his career.
