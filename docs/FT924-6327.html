921121 FT 21 NOV 92 / Finance & The Family: At A Glance - New monthly income fund Foster & Braithwaite, the private client stockbroker, is launching a monthly income fund based on investment trusts. To stabilise the income flow F&B will pay a level amount each month for a year. The fund will initially pay 90 per cent of the net dividend yield at the time of purchase, with the remaining 10 per cent credited to an interest-earning account. The minimum investment is Pounds 10,000; initial charge is 3 per cent plus VAT, annual charge is 1.25 per cent and there is a dealing charge of 1 per cent.
