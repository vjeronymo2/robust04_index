940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / Business and the Law: Firms spend less on high-tech than expected - Legal Briefs UK law firms which, on average, last year planned to invest Pounds 72,000 on new information technology systems spent only Pounds 48,000, according to a survey by accountants Robson Rhodes. There has been a modest increase in the number of firms providing a workstation for each partner (up from 11 per cent to 14.7 per cent) and for each fee earner (up from 7 per cent to 10 per cent).
