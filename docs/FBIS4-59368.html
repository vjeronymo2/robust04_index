BFN [Text] Doha, 11 May (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, the Amir of the State of Qatar, this evening received a telephone call from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The QATARI NEWS AGENCY said that the Amir of the State of Qatar was briefed on the latest developments in Yemen. It added that the Amir of Qatar stressed to the Yemeni president his country's concern about restoring stability in Yemen, stopping the fighting among the brothers, and keeping Yemen strong and united in order to maintain its people's gains and achievements.
