BFN ["YONHAP report from Tokyo"] [Text] According to Japan's YOMIURI SHIMBUN, the United States requested Japan to support the emergency evacuation of foreigners in the ROK, including U.S. citizens, when Japan begins to transport Japanese people in the ROK back to Japan in the case of an extremely tense situation on the Korean peninsula. YOMIURI SHIMBUN today quoted a Japanese Government source as saying that, upon the request, the government is hurrying the revision of laws concerning the Self-Defense Force to enable the Self-Defense Force to dispatch its aircraft for the transportation of foreigners. The Japanese Government is also reviewing the immigration and customs process for an emergency, and the deployment of naval vessels for mass transportation. The source said when President Clinton had a telephone conversation with Japanese Prime Minister Hata on 14 June, President Clinton conveyed his intention to build up U.S. Forces in the ROK and asked Japan to help transport Americans.
