BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- Kye Ung-tae, Politburo member and secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, met and had a friendly talk with the visiting delegation of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of China led by Deputy Chief Procurator Liang Guoqing at the Mansudae Assembly Hall today. Present on the occasion were Deputy Prosecutor-general Kim Chang- uk and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
