930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Exporters using private insurance UK EXPORTERS who formerly relied on the Export Credit Guarantee Department of the Department of Trade and Industry now secure 90 per cent of reinsurance for both commercial and political risk from the private sector. Mr Richard Needham, trade minister, confirmed yesterday that transitional support for the ECGD's privatised successor to its insurance services group would continue to December 1994.
