BFN [Text] Al-Basrah, 7 May (INA)--The Maltese ship Harmony Sea [name as transliterated] docked at the silo quay at the Umm Qasr Port at noon today. The ship is carrying a shipment of German wheat imported for the Trade Ministry. An INA correspondent in al-Basrah Governorate said that the silo workers have started unloading the ship.
