930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Learning a degree of management skill - In a Nutshell The Open University (OU) has launched a Certificate in Small Business Management to help owners, managers and advisers improve their management and business planning skills. The certificate can be obtained through two five-month courses covering Core Skills in Small Business Management and Focus on Growth, each costing Pounds 450. The OU has completed regional trials of the scheme and is now running a national pilot programme to cater further to the needs of business and other vocational qualification schemes. The pilot programme is being managed jointly with local Training and Enterprise Councils and chambers of commerce.
