941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / London's biggest bus company sold London General Transport Services, the capital's biggest bus company, has been sold to a management and employee buy-out team for Pounds 28m. It has more than 600 buses, 2,000 staff and annual turnover of Pounds 50m. Volunteer drivers will continue to be allowed to drive non-commercial minibuses without having to take a further test or meet higher health standards, the government announced yesterday.
