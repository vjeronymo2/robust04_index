June 3, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: 20-YEAR-OLD HELD IN DRIVE-BY SHOOTING

A 20-year-old Oxnard man was arrested early Saturday on suspicion of participating in a drive-by shooting, authorities said.

Witnesses told police that a car with its lights switched off parked outside a house in the 400 block of Helsam Street in El Rio and fired into the living room window of a house, said Lt. Joe Funchess of the Ventura County Sheriff's Department.

No one was hurt, Funchess said.

Based on witnesses' accounts, sheriff's deputies arrested Fidel Melendez at his house about 6 a.m. Saturday, Funchess said.

It was not known whether the incident was gang-related, Funchess said.

Melendez was arrested on suspicion of shooting at an occupied dwelling and for several outstanding warrants, including failing to appear in court on a traffic citation, Funchess said.

Melendez was being held Saturday at the Ventura County Jail in lieu of $5,500 bail, Sheriff's Sgt. Bob Larson said. PEGGY Y. LEE
