940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Billam declines to Pounds 385,000 Pre-tax profits at J Billam, the Sheffield-based specialist engineer, fell 20 per cent from Pounds 479,000 to Pounds 385,000 in the 1993 year. The company blamed the recession in the aerospace industry. Turnover advanced from Pounds 5.99m to Pounds 6.67m. Earnings per share were 16p (20.1p) and the proposed final dividend is maintained at 3.1p for an increased total of 5.3p (5p) for the year.
