940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / Palace visitors up 40 per cent on 1993 Fit for a queen: visitors pass through the Grand Staircase in Buckingham Palace yesterday, when it was revealed that attendances are up 40 per cent on last year. In the first four weeks of opening this year there have been just under 208,000 visitors, and palace officials hope to exceed their target profit of Pounds 2.5m.
