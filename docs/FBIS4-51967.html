BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 1 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- A meeting was held in Tokyo on April 28 to hear a report from a delegation of veteran officials of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) which had visited the homeland. Addressing the meeting, the Chief Vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon, Ho Chong-man, said what the members of the large delegation of veteran officials of Chongnyon deeply felt again while staying in the homeland was that they have the illustrious leader in the person of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il who is best in ideology, leadership and love for the revolutionary comrades and the people. He said the veteran officials should play a big role in strengthening and developing Chongnyon through generations into an organization intensely loyal to the illustrious leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and encouraging and inspiring the Chongnyon officials and Koreans in Japan in the patriotic struggle to firmly defend the socialist homeland and Chongnyon organisation and glorify them. He appealed to all the officials and compatriots to make greater efforts for a bigger stride forward in the patriotic work of Chongnyon with optimism and firm faith that they will unfailingly win when there is supreme commander Comrade Kim Chong-il for them. A letter to the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting.
