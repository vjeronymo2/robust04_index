Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 28 (KCNA) -- An inaugural ceremony of the preparatory committee for celebrating the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was held in Maputo of Mozambique on January 20. The meeting elected the minister of interior of Mozambique chairman of the preparatory committee and decided to set 20 days from February 1 as a period of celebration functions. In his speech, the chairman of the preparatory committee said that on this occasion celebration meetings, film shows, photo exhibitions and other functions would be held in cities and rural villages, industrial establishments and organs of Mozambique.
