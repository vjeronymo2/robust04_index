Laws OW1205045094 Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0721 GMT 10 May 94 OW1205045094 Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service Chinese BFN [By reporter Rong Ancai (1369 1344 2088)] [Text] Beijing, 10 May (XINHUA) -- The first symposium on anti-dumping laws, co-sponsored by the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation and the Beijing Municipal Commission of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation, opened in Beijing on 9 May. It has been reported that the 10-day symposium is divided into three stages and will be held in Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou successively. During the symposium, experts from the U.S. Commerce Department will brief Chinese foreign trade officials and enterprise representatives on U.S. anti-dumping laws. According to the sponsor, the symposium is being held to promote mutual understanding on questions concerning anti-dumping laws, eliminating misunderstanding, and further promote economic relations and trade between the two countries.
