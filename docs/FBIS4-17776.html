BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Flight Control Center, 26 Apr -- Cosmos-2279, an artificial earth satellite, was launched from the Plesetsk Cosmodrome today aboard a Cosmos [as received] booster rocket. It is intended for use in the interests of the Russian Defense Ministry. The satellite has been placed in orbit with the following parameters: initial period of revolution, 105 minutes; apogee, 1,019 km; perigee, 973 km; orbital inclination, 83 degrees. The equipment on board the satellite is functioning normally.
