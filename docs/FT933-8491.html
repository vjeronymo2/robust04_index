930816 FT 16 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Christie wins world title Britain's Linford Christie (left) won the world 100 metres sprint title in 9.87 seconds in Stuttgart. The 33-year-old, who now holds the world, Olympic, European and Commonwealth 100 metre titles, cut five hundredths of a second off his personal best time and came within one hundredth of a second of American Carl Lewis's world record. Lewis finished fourth.
