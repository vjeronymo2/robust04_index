BFN [Text] A CIS Interparliamentary Assembly delegation lead by Medetkan Sherimkulov, chairman of Kyrgystan Supreme Council, arrived in Yerevan today. The delegation is made up by a large group of high-ranking officials, including the Russian president's special ambassador, Vladimir Kazimirov. Soon after their arrival, talks started between the CIS parliamentarians and a delegation from the Armenian Supreme Council. In opening the talks, Chairman of the Supreme Council Babken Ararktsyan thanked the guests for their efforts to stabilize the situation in the region. In reply, Sherimkulov expressed thanks for the meeting held in a warm (?atmosphere), and stressed that visits to Baku and Yerevan will give the members of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly delegation the opportunity to gather in-depth information on the situation first-hand, and find various mutually-acceptable views for the solution of the Karabakh problem, which the peace mission has expressly come to examine. The CIS delegation (?will leave) for Stepanakert.
