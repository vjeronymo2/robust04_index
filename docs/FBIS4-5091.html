BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- A symposium of officials and members of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea on the immortal exploits performed by the great leader President Kim Il-song in building trade unions was held here Monday. Speakers there said that one of the immortal exploits performed by the great leader in building trade unions is that he had provided deep roots of the federation by forming the anti-Japanese trade union, a mass political organization of the working class, during the anti-Japanese revolutionary struggle. They noted that another exploit is that he, basing himself on this tradition, founded the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea after the liberation of the country, and has indicated in a scientific way the character and duty of the federation in each period and at each stage of the development of the revolution and wisely led the work to this end. They also recalled that the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il has further developed in depth the idea and theory of building the chuche-orientated trade unions and built up the organizations into a reliable assistant and defender of the Workers' Party of Korea.
