930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Ex-soldier wins race abuse payout A BLACK former soldier who was racially abused and harassed by fellow soldiers has received Pounds 8,500 compensation from the Ministry of Defence in only the second case of its kind. Mr Anthony Evans, a private in the 1st Battalion of the Royal Regiment of Wales for six years, told an army board of inquiry that he had been constantly subjected to racially abusive language and been publicly humiliated by his sergeant. Compensation was settled on Thursday.
