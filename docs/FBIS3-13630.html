Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Unverified reports today about the defusing of explosive devices in certain quarters aroused panic among the citizens. To clarify the truth, the Internal Security Forces General Directorate issued the following statement: Investigations into the discovery of a 60-mm mortar shell in the playground of the Assiriac al-Taraqqi School in Burj Hammud, al-Nab'ah, discovered that the shell was not primed for detonation. Regarding the shell found in the 'Abd-al-Qadir Lecee School in the patriarchate locality, the statement said that a rocket-propelled grenade round was found on the roof of the school's laboratory. A military expert removed it and determined that it was an old remnant of the war.
