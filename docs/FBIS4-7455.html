BFN [Text] Cetinje, 30 Mar (TANJUG) -- At a session in Cetinje the Assembly of Montenegro this evening elected four new ministers in the cabinet of Prime Minister Milo Djukanovic. The new minister of labor and social security is Branimir Bojanic, hitherto director of the Montenegrin Radio and TV. Ana Misurovic is the new environment protection minister, while the new minister of transport and maritime affairs is Vojislav Micunovic, hitherto assistant federal minister of transport and communications. Radovan Bakic, a university professor from Niksic, is the new minister of planning. The four ministerial posts were vacated after the departure of the opposition representatives or when the outgoing ministers took up new posts.
