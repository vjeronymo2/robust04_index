930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / International Capital Markets: Growth phase over for German CP THE PHASE of 'stormy growth' in the German commercial paper market is over, the Bundesbank says in its May monthly report. The German CP market was established in February 1991, much later than in other countries. It grew rapidly and within 18 months had become the fourth largest such market in Europe. But the report says that with the decline in the German economy, demand for short-term funds has fallen sharply. Also, long-term interest rates have fallen, making it more attractive to borrow at the long end of the yield curve. The volume of the German CP market was DM37bn at the end of last year, more than double the level at the end of 1991.
