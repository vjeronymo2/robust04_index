Deputy Volodymyr [name indistinct], member of the legislative subcommission for scientific, social, and legal issues related to the Chernobyl catastrophe and nuclear power engineering, pointed out the parochial attitude toward problems of power engineering and narrow-sighted industry management system among factors that aggravated the condition of power engineering sector in 1993-94.
