BFN [Text] The villages of Qabrikha, Majdal Silm, Tulin, and al-Suwwanah came under a concentrated Israel artillery barrage this morning. The shelling continued intermittently at the rate of one round per minute until 0500 today, prompting residents inhabitants to seek protection in shelters and the innermost rooms of their homes. The bombardment was accompanied by intensive overflights by Israeli helicopters, which fired flares and raked the area. Retaliating for the continuing enemy acts of aggression, which left three dead and dozens wounded in Sidon, resistance groups shelled several Israeli settlements, concentrating on the Galilee panhandle. The enemy admitted that rockets landed in the area but denied any casualties. The Israeli enemy sent in many vehicles and reinforcements, which were spotted traveling along the international highway near Tallat al-'Abbad. These reinforcements have deployed in the al-Tayyibah- al-Qantarah-'Alman triangle.
