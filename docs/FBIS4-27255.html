BFN [Excerpt] Cambodia's elder statesman has emphasised that he opposes a proposal for military assistance to the Cambodian army. "Samdech Son Sann does not support military assistance to the Cambodian Army as he has always believed that it serves only to prolong the war," his assistant, Son Chhai, said in a statement. The statement came after Son Sann was reported to have expressed support for Thailand's position on Cambodia including the matter of military aid. Thailand has repeatedly made clear that it is against military help for the Cambodian army. [passage omitted]
