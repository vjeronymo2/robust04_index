Air Traffic Control Tower; Plainview, TX; Decommissioning Agency: Federal Aviation Administration [FAA], DOT. Action: Notice of decommissioning. Summary: Notice is hereby given that on or about July 29, 1994, the Airport Traffic Control Tower at Plainview, Texas, will be decommissioned. This information will be reflected in the FAA Organization Statement the next time it is issued. 49 U.S.C. 1348, 1354(a); 49 U.S.C. 106(g) Issued in Fort Worth, Texas, on July 6, 1994. Wm.
