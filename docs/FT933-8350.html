930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Egyptian hijacker was businessman An Egyptian who hijacked a KLM airliner to Dusseldorf was a Cairo businessman. Khalid Abdelmoneim Gharib was demanding the release of Omar Abdel-Rahman from a US prison. The 10 1/2 -hour hijack ended when German police seized Gharib unharmed.
