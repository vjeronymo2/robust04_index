920511 FT 11 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Housing students Three universities have awarded contracts to TEAM SERVICES for student accommodation. The first (Pounds 4.1m), for Loughborough University, requires Team to complete ensuite study bedrooms for 402 students. At the University of Stirling (Pounds 3.2m) Team is to provide similar facilities for 249 students in three cruciform buildings on part of the campus between Airthrey Castle and the Memorial Gardens. In Cardiff the company has won a Pounds 1.5m contract from the University of Wales to build 112 rooms on a city centre site.
