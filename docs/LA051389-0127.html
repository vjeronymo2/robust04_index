May 13, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DANA HILLS' TANSLEY TO ATTEND N. ARIZONA

Dana Hills distance runner Michael Tansley has signed a national letter of intent to attend Northern Arizona in the fall, his father, Michael Tansley Sr. said.

Tansley, who led Dana Hills to Southern Section 4-A cross-country titles the past two seasons, chose Northern Arizona over USC. Tansley's twin brother, Andrew, also will attend Northern Arizona.
