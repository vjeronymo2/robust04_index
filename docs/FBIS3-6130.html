Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 13 (KCNA) -- State commendations were awarded to officials in charge of party's ideological work on March 12 with a due ceremony in Pyongyang. Kim Il-song Order was awarded to Choe Hak-chol, secretary in charge of propaganda of the party committee of the Kim Chaek iron and steel complex who had made a great contribution to the powerful mobilisation of party members and other working people in the implementation of party's policies and to the strengthening of the singlehearted unity of the whole society by intensifying education in the chuche idea among them, upholding the leadership of the party, and citations of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to model officials in charge of party's ideological work.
