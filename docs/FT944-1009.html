941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Enviromed purchase Enviromed, the biotechnology and healthcare group, is to acquire CVS, a small technology company based in Ely, Cambridgeshire, for Pounds 1.1m. The consideration will be met by the issue of new Enviromed shares in two tranches. CVS, owned by Innovet of the US, is developing technology for immunodiagnostics. It will be integrated into Enviromed's diagnostics division.
