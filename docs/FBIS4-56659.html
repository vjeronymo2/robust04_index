BFN [Text] Outages have been taking place due to the electric generation deficit of the National Electric System, mainly the shutdown for maintenance of the most powerful generator unit in Cuba, Matanzas' Antonio Guiteras plant. Tonight from 1900 to 0000, outages are scheduled for Guanabacoa, Regla, Cotorro, San Miguel del Padron, Centro Habana, Habana Vieja, Habana del Este, Marianao, Playa, and La Lisa Municipalities as well as areas of other municipalities on the same circuits. On 6 May, Friday, outages are scheduled for Marianao, Playa, La Lisa, Boyeros, 10 de Octubre, and Arroyo Naranjo Municipalities will experienced outages from 0500 to 0900 and from 1300 to 1900. Likewise, Centro Habana, Habana Vieja, Habana del Este, Plaza, Cerro, Guanabacoa, Regla, Cotorro, and San Miguel del Padron Municipalities will experience scheduled outages from 0900 to 1300 and from 1900 to 0000. This information was provided by Arturo Mora de la Cruz.
