March 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

At Warner Bros., Brian De Palma is slated to direct Tom Wolfe's piercing portrayal of New York society, "The Bonfire of the Vanities." Michael Cristofer will adapt Wolfe's best-selling novel for the big screen. No cast has been set. De Palma will begin production on the film this summer after he wraps up "Casualties of War" for Columbia. ALEENE MacMINN
