Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko is continuing his visit to Germany. In an interview to the leading German newspaper FRANKFURTER ALLGEMEINE ZEITUNG, Anatoliy Zlenko said: We did not make a mistake when we thought that the Yugoslav crisis should be settled by political means. A bombardment can only escalate the conflict. The Ukrainian foreign minister emphasized that the search is under way right now for political resolution of the Balkan problem, which is evidenced by the Russian proposal. Asked about Ukraine's participation in the UN-sponsored peace keeping mission in Yugoslavia, Anatoliy Zlenko said that the Ukrainian contingent of the blue helmets will increase by an additional 800 people.
