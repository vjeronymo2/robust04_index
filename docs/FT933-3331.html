930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa THE weakening of the bullion price had a sharp effect on gold stocks as the gold index lost 62 or 4 per cent to 1,481. However, industrial stocks remained firm and the index ended just 2 down at 4,536, while the overall index was off 22 at 3,824. De Beers eased R1.25 to R83.25 while Anglos lost 50 cents to R130.50. Among gold stocks, Vaal Reefs slid R12 to R284 and Southvaal lost R9 to R95. Gold Fields shed R2.25 to R74.85 and Johnnies R2 to R64.
