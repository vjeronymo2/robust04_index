931116 FT 16 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Arafat ally shot Gunmen shot the top official of PLO leader Yassir Arafat's Fatah movement in Lebanon. Mouin Shabaytah was taken to hospital after being hit by seven bullets when his car was riddled by submachinegun fire in Sidon. There were differing reports on whether he had died or was severely injured. He is the second senior Fatah official to be shot in south Lebanon in less than a week.
