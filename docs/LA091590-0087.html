September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

NORTHRIDGE SWEEPS USF IN VOLLEYBALL

Cal State Northridge dispatched the University of San Francisco, 15-13, 15-8, 15-1, in little more than an hour Friday night in a women's nonconference volleyball match at Northridge.

Kathleen Dixon (eight kills) and Jill Trehearne (five) led a balanced attack for Northridge (8-4).

SOCCER

San Diego State 1, Cal State Northridge 0 (2 OT) -- San Diego State scored following a corner kick in the 119th minute of the nonconference match at San Diego State.
