940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News: EFM Dragon Trust Net asset value per share of EFM Dragon Trust stood at 124.17p at end-February, an improvement of 45 per cent over the 85.44p standing at August 31 1993. The available deficit for the half year to February 28 amounted to Pounds 215,000 (Pounds 17,000), equal to losses per 20p share of 0.082p (0.024p).
