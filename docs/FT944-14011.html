941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / International Company News: ED&F Man ED&F Man Group, the agricultural products and financial services company which came to the market earlier this month, has redeemed all its 1.6m cumulative convertible restricted voting preference shares of Dollars 1 each, and all its 90,000 non-voting cumulative non-convertible preference shares of Dollars 1 each. The aggregate amount paid on redemption was Dollars 57.3m (Pounds 36.2m), including Dollars 1.4m accrued preference dividends.
