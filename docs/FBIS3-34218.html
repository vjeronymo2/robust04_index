Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The proceedings of the two-day 14th session of the Arab Maghreb Union [AMU] foreign ministers' council ended this morning. [passage omitted] AMU Secretary General Mohamed Amamou gave us the following statement at the end of this session: [Begin Amamou recording] Every presidency period is a stage in the joint Maghreban action. There is great hope that the stages of Arab Maghreb action will continue in a way that will be in compliance with the aspirations of peoples and work for the resolutions of the presidency council in the various fields in which they have adopted resolutions or future resolutions which they can adopt. Thus, our Maghreban action is always characterized by optimism. [end recording]
