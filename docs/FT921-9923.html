920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Phone bills lower in France UK TELEPHONE charges are more expensive than those in France, about the same as in Germany but cheaper than in Italy, according to figures published yesterday by Oftel, the industry regulator. The figures compare representative residential and business phone bills using BT, with the cost of the same services in the three other large European economies. Residential bills are 22 per cent lower in France, 1 per cent lower in Germany and 5 per cent higher in Italy. Business bills are 8 per cent lower in France, 1 per cent lower in Germany but 33 per cent higher in Italy according to Oftel.
