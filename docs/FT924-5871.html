921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / Pounds 78m paid for London offices NORWICH Union Life Assurance has struck the biggest investment deal in the City for over 15 months by selling an office building to DGI, one of Germany's largest funds, for an estimated Pounds 78m. The 260,000 sq ft building at Ropemaker Place is the European headquarters of Merrill Lynch, the securities house.
