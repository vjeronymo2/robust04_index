931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / International Company News: Spain reshuffles corporate jobs Mr Javier Alvarez Vara, president of the Spanish aerospace company Casa for the past seven years, has been appointed head of Inespal, the aluminium producer. The move is part of a reshuffle of top jobs in Spain's state-owned corporations, writes Tom Burns in Madrid. In a second move, Mr Manuel Fernandez Garca, a former trade unionist who became chairman of the nationalised capital goods manufacturer Babcock & Wilcox in 1985, has been promoted to head the group of companies that form the industrial backbone of the Instituto Nacional de Industria (INI), the public-sector holding.
