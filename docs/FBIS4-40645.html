BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk, 18 Jun -- There was no attempt made on the life of People's Deputy Aleksandr Lukashenko, a candidate in the Belarus presidential elections, Petr Snopok, the press secretary of the Belarus KGB, said today speaking on behalf of the Committee for State Security at a news conference. It was announced at the news conference, in which Deputy Interior Minister Lyutsian Sobolenskiy and Vasiliy Targonskiy, KGB senior investigator for special cases, participated, that the shooting at the car in which Aleksandr Lukashenko was travelling was staged. The information gathered from an investigative experiment and the contradictory stories given by those who were with Lukashenko bear witness to this, it was said at the news conference.
