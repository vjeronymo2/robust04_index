June 8, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

"An Evening at the Carnivale" at Plaza de Cafes in Newport Beach on May 20 raised an estimated $10,000 for Concern II. Karen Raaband Gail Greenberg were co-chairwomen.

The tribute to James Roosevelt on May 20 at the Irvine Hilton and Towers earned an estimated $200,000 for Chapman College. Doy Henley was chairman.

The "Calypso Casino" on June 4 at the Golden Creek Inn in Irvine raised an estimated $8,000 for the Make-A-Wish Foundation. Deanna Mailman and Kate Gischwere co-chairwomen.

The "Classic Car Review & Brunch" on June 4 at the Westerdale Showroom in Huntington Beach raised an estimated $3,000 for Family Services of Huntington Beach. Jessica Uniack and Lindy Smith were co-chairwomen.
