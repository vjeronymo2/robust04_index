April 14, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

National Public Radio's "All Things Considered" will broadcast a special feature today to note the 50th anniversary of John Steinbeck's Pulitzer Prize-winning novel, "The Grapes of Wrath." Actor Peter Coyote will read selections from the novel, interspersed with interviews with farm workers in Oklahoma and California whose stories parallel those in the book. "All Things" airs 3:30-5:30 p.m. KPCC-FM (89.3), 4-7 p.m. KCRW-FM (89.9). ALEENE MacMINN
