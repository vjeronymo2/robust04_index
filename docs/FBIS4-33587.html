BFN [From the "Caracol's Quarter Hour" newscast] [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota -- Army Commander General Jose Hernan Guzman today authorized 9th Brigade Commander Gen. Fernando Millan to act in coordination with the Huila Department governor's office in providing assistance to persons left homeless by the earthquake and the Paez River flooding. Gen. Guzman authorized the use of three Army helicopters to evacuate civilians and bring in food and medicines. Sixth Brigade Commander General Luis Bernardo Urbina Sanchez and 4th Division Chief General Carlos Julio Gil Colorado received similar instructions to cope with the situation caused by the flooding of the Ariari River. The government asked families hurt by yesterday's earthquakes not to return to their damaged homes until experts conduct technical inspections. The risk exists the Paez River in Cauca Department may overflow, so residents must take all the necessary precautions.
