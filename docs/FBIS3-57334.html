Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Andrey Aderekhin report: "Licenses No Longer Needed For Exports From Kuban To CIS Countries"] [Text] Krasnodar -- The Krasnodar Kray Government directive on the licensing of exports of output and goods to the CIS countries has been repealed. North Caucasus Railroad department leaders have been instructed to lift the restrictions on allocating freight rail transport to carry agricultural, industrial, and other products from the Kuban to the CIS countries.
