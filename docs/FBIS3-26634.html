Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Xiamen, February 10 (XINHUA) -- Liu Huaqing, vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, today attended a Spring Festival party in Xiamen city, in east China's Fujian Province. After the meeting, Liu, also a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, went to two ports at Xiamen to convey festival greetings to the on-shift workers. On Wednesday, Liu visited an army unit to see officers and men. He talked with the soldiers and listened to them about their life and military training. He said that at present China enjoys political stability and rapid and stable economic growth. He urged them to seize favorable opportunities to promote economic construction and army construction. He also called on them to study Deng Xiaoping's selected works, learn science and technology and improve the quality of the army ranks.
