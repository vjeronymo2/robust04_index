BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin says a new and aggressive type of religious conversion has evolved in the Arab world, and its seeds are being felt even in the Israeli Arab sector. This is an extreme religious approach whose adherents refuse to recognize any non-Islamic political entity. This credo motivates the activities of Hamas and other radical organizations in the territories. Therefore, an arrangement with the PLO is the only possibility. The prime minister made these remarks in a meeting with kibbutzim members.
