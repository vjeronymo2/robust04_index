BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Jun (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak received Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas this evening. Also attending the meeting on the Yemeni side were Muhsin Bin-Farid, Salih 'Ubayd Ahmad, 'Abdallah al-Asnaj, and 'Abd-al-Jalil Ghilan. Dr. Usmah al-Baz, director of the president's office for political affairs and Foreign Ministry first under secretary, also attended the meeting. During the meeting, the Yemeni leaders reviewed the developments in the situation in Yemen and the ongoing military battles.
