930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / UK Company News: EIO has 52% of St Andrew Following its bid The Ecclesiastical Insurance Office has 52.12 per cent of St Andrew Trust, the smaller companies investment trust. The offer was a technical one, designed to give EIO more than 50 per cent, but less than 75 per cent, of St Andrew's equity As a result of passing the 50 per ecnt mark those who accept the offer will now receive the higher of 237.65p or 93 per cent of St Andrew's formula asset value. The offer will remain open until February 5th.
