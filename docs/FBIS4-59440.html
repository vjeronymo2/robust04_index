BFN [Text] Brother Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, minister of oil and mineral resources, has conveyed a message from brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] and vice chairman of the Presidential Council, to his brother, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the sisterly United Arab Emirates. It concerns the latest developments of the situation in Yemen. The message was conveyed last night when Shaykh Zayid received brother Bin-Husaynun in Abu Dhabi, who confirmed the YSP's stance and its complete adherence to the pledge and accord document. For his part, Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan again stressed the importance of stopping the ongoing fighting in Yemen and of resorting to dialogue to reach a political solution in order to preserve Yemeni unity in a manner which guarantees the interests of the Yemeni people.
