September 14, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

REDS START PLAYOFF TICKET SALES

The Cincinnati Reds, who start a showdown series with the Dodgers tonight, will begin selling tickets Saturday for the National League Championship Series.

Tickets will be available only by telephone, the Reds said Thursday.

Tickets are $25 per game, but orders must be for all four games that could be played in Cincinnati if the Reds win their division.
