February 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Fixed-Rate Mortgage Rates Rise: Fixed-rate home mortgages are averaging 10.17%, according to a national survey by the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp., up from 10.05% last week. On one-year adjustable-rate mortgages, lenders were asking an average initial rate of 8.45%, up from 8.41% last week.
