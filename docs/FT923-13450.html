920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / Test for A-level alternative NEW broad-based vocational qualifications, which will be a non-academic alternative to A levels for 16-19 year-olds, are to be piloted in about 100 schools and colleges this September. Sir Bryan Nicholson, chairman of the National Council for Vocational Qualifications, said: 'This is the first time that the majority of young people who are not the academic minority have a decent qualification in full-time education.'
