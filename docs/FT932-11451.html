930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Benchmark Benchmark Group, the property investor, incurred a loss of Pounds 1.39m pre-tax for the half year ended December 31 compared with Pounds 1.75m previously. The deficit was struck after taking account of an exceptional provision of Pounds 926,000 (Pounds 1.6m) against properties sold. A fall in turnover to Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 8.07m) reflected the disposal in July of the banking division. Extraordinary charges of Pounds 1.69m (Pounds 3.3m) left a retained deficit of Pounds 3.08m (Pounds 5.05m). Losses per share emerged at 0.87p (1.08p).
