February 18, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE TRACK TEAMS TO OPEN SEASON TODAY

The UC Irvine men's and women's track and field teams open their season today playing host to to Cal State Long Beach and Cal Poly Pomona in a nonconference meet.

Decathlete Matt Farmer and All-American distance runner Beth McGrann are among Irvine's top performers this season.

The meet begins with field events at 11:30 a.m. Running events begin at 1:30.
