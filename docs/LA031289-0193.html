March 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

"Indonesia's History Through the Ages" will be the subject of a meeting of the Asia Society at noon on Wednesday on Level C, Arco Plaza, 505 S. Flower St., Los Angeles. Admission free to members, $1 nonmembers. For more information and reservations, call (213) 624-0945.

-- -- --

"Bicycling in Bali" will be the topic of REI, an outdoor gear and clothing store, at 6:30 p.m. on Thursday at the store, 3029 E. University Ave., San Diego. Admission free. For more information, call (619) 295-7700.

-- -- --

A slide-lecture on "South America Beyond Expectations" will be presented by the Bon Voyage Travel Club at 7:45 p.m. on Friday in the Community Room, 9696 Wilshire Blvd., Beverly Hills. Admission $5. For more information, call (213) 472-5726.

-- -- --

A seminar on "Work Your Way Around the World" will be held by American Youth Hostels, Los Angeles Council, at 1 p.m. on Saturday at the AYH Travel Center, 335 West 7th St., San Pedro. Admission $10. Pre-registration is suggested. For more information, call (213) 831-8846 or (213) 831-8847.
