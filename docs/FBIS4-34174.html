BFN [Text] Brasilia, 15 Jun (AE) -- With 312 votes in favor, 59 against, 15 abstentions, and one void, the Chamber of Deputies has stripped Deputy Jose Geraldo Ribeiro (PMDB-MG) [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party-Minas Gerais State] of his political rights. The Congressional Investigating Committee, looking into the irregular handling of budget funds, found Ribeiro guilty of misusing federal budget funds and of unlawful accumulation of wealth. The Chamber of Deputies is now voting on the suspension of Deputy Flavio Derzi (PP-MS) [Popular Party-Mato Grosso do Sul State]. Derzi also is charged with irregular handling of budget funds.
