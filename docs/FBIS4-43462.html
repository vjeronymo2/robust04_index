BFN [Text] Tehran, June 15, IRNA -- Iranian Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammad Besharati, now visiting Turkey, has said that the Islamic Republic of Iran is ready for promotion of ties with Turkey and for closer cooperation between parliaments of the two countries. Besharati, who was speaking to the Turkish parliament speaker Tuesday [14 June], said the Ankara government should adopt a new strategy for its relations with its neighbors in view of the current changes sweeping the world. He added that special conditions in Europe and elsewhere in the world call for strengthening of relations between the two neighboring countries of Iran and Turkey. Besharati invited the Turkish parliament speaker to pay an official visit to Tehran. The Turkish parliamentarian said that the parliament and nation of Turkey were satisfied with the existing "friendly" ties with the Islamic Republic of Iran. He expressed hope that Irano-Turkish ties would further broaden in light of continued talks between the two countries' parliamentary officials.
