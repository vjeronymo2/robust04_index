920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Fairey/Arcom Fairey Group, the specialist engineer, has bought the remaining 37.8 per cent minority holding in its Arcom Control Systems subsidiary for Pounds 1.06m through the issue to the vendors of 252,260 ordinary shares. Arcom's pre-tax profits for 1991 were Pounds 231,000.
