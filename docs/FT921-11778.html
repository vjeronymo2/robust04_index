920130 FT 30 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Brent Walker sells Le Touquet assets Brent Walker yesterday announced the disposal, for Pounds 16.4m, of its hotel, golf courses, property and other interests at Le Touquet, France. Contracts have also been exchanged with Mr John Aspinall and Mr James Osborne for the Casino Du Palais, Le Touquet, for Pounds 2m. Mr Nicholas Ward, group managing director, said the two transactions resulted in the payment of all its French borrowings.
