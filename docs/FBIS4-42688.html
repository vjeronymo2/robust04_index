BFN [Statement issued by the National Security Council Secretariat General on 6 June] [Text] The National Security Council [NSC] held an extraordinary session at the Cankaya Presidential Mansion on 6 June under the chairmanship of President Suleyman Demirel, with the participation of the prime minister, the chief of the General Staff, NSC member ministers, the force commanders, the general commander of the Gendarmerie, and the secretary general of the NSC. The state minister and deputy prime minister also attended the meeting. The developments in northern Iraq within the context of Operation Provide Comfort were discussed during the meeting. The NSC determined that the conditions in the region have not reached the point that call for the termination of the said operation. In view of this situation and in light of the importance of protecting Iraq's territorial integrity, the NSC decided to recommend to the government to extend the mandate of the multinational force as of 30 June for a period to be deemed appropriate.
