940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / Royal Mail to set up office nursery The Royal Mail, in response to requests from its staff, is to set up its first office nursery in its main London sorting office. The nursery, which will open later this year, will cater for up to 60 children with places allocated on a first come, first served basis. The Royal Mail, which employs 1,500 at its Mount Pleasant office, said it wanted to retain experienced staff.
