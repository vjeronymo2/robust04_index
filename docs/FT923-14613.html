920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (17): RAC Enterprises - Company Snapshot Company details: the RAC has 5.2m members and a turnover of Pounds 177m. Its traditional rival, the AA, has 7m members but the RAC claims to be growing more quickly. RAC Enterprises: The holidays and travel services operation in Croydon, south London, is part of RAC Enterprises, which has a turnover of Pounds 17m. The business also includes information services, hotel inspection services, vehicle and dealer inspection services and publishing. The holiday services unit offers self-catering holidays in France, ferry and Motorail booking services and international breakdown recovery services. It has a reservations team of 15 which is increased by 13 seasonal staff for the peak booking period.
