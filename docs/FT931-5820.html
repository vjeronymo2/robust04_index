930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Another executive charged by SFO THE SERIES of prosecutions by the Serious Fraud Office over cases of alleged corruption concerning the awarding of contracts was extended yesterday when a marketing consultant was arrested and charged with three counts of corruption, John Mason writes. The charges against Mr Donald Richards, the head of Donnar Services, a marketing company, relate to the procuring of contracts with NEI Thompson Kennicott, a supplier of water treatment equipment to power stations. The company is a subsidiary of Northern Engineering Industries, owned by Rolls-Royce. Mr Richards, of Gt Missenden, Bucks, appeared at Bow St magistrates court. He was remanded on bail until April 15.
