September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

EVANS TOP AMATEUR SPORTSWOMAN

Janet Evans, winner of three gold medals in swimming at the 1988 Seoul Olympics, was named Women's Sports Foundation Amateur Sportswoman of the Year Friday, the foundation said.

Evans, of Placentia, won the 400- and 800-meter freestyle races and the 400-meter individual medley, in which she set a world record. She now has won 26 consecutive races.

The award will be presented at a banquet Tuesday in New York.
