October 5, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

Tina Schafnitz, Anne Badham, and Kim Cardenas, from left, sport glitz and boots at Sunday night's "Puttin' on the West" bash at the Irvine Hilton that benefited the Associates of the House Ear Institute of Orange County. Proceeds were estimated at $30,000. JoAnne Stewart was chairwoman. Athalie Clark was honorary chairwoman.

The "Let Us Entertain You" luncheon and fashion show last Thursday at the Turnip Rose restaurant in Costa Mesa raised an estimated $4,000 for the Master Chorale of Orange County. Lynn Galt was chairwoman.

The "Guys and Dolls Auction Gala" on Friday at the Newport Beach Marriott raised an estimated $61,000 for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Randy Blackwood and Sherr Anderson were co-chairmen.
