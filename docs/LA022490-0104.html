February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Luther Medical Products Inc.: The Tustin-based catheter maker reported a loss of $187,875 for the fiscal 1990 second quarter, contrasted with a loss of $149,166 for the period the year before.

Revenue for the quarter ended Dec. 31 rose 27%, to $588,961 from $463,532. The net loss for the first six months was $238,180, more than double the $113,201 loss for the year-earlier period. Revenue increased 7%, to $1.1 million from $1 million.
