BFN [From the "Political Week" section] [Text] A new Islamist organization calling itself the "Caliphate Group" has recently appeared. The organization, which espouses the concept of repudiation and accuses all existing Islamic societies and countries of apostasy, is operating in tribal territories adjacent to the Pakistani-Afghan border. It advocates the restoration of the caliphate under the leadership of the group's chief. The membership of the new organization comprises Egyptian, Sudanese, Jordanian, and Algerian nationals. The leader of the group is an Arab millionaire of Palestinian extraction called Abu-'Uthman. He considers himself an extension of Shukri Mustafa, leader of the Repudation and Renunciation group. Last week the Pakistani authorities asked the group's leading members to leave Pakistan.
