BFN [Text] The national media spokesman for the Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front -- AVF), Mr. Koos Kemp, has denied allegations that soldiers from the AVF are undergoing training in northern Swaziland. Earlier a tribal chief said he had handed information about the alleged training to the Swazi defense force. Mr. Kemp also denied having any knowledge of a huge meeting allegedly held by the AVF in the Lubodlwana region of Swaziland last week. The leader of the AWB [Afrikaner Resistance Movement], Mr. Eugene Terreblanche, has also denied knowledge of right-wing troops being trained in Swaziland. He was not prepared to comment on whether the AWB was involved in the training of any other groups. However, he did confirm that training of his own commandos had been stepped up.
