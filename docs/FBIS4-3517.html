BFN [Text] Stockholm, April 20 (XINHUA) -- China wishes to intensify its cooperation with other countries in high technology, a top Chinese official told an international seminar on development aid policy approach to new generic technologies in developing countries which opened here today. Song Jian, Chinese minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission, said in a speech at the seminar that Chinese scientists regard high technology as the key to development since it is a dominant factor for economic growth in the high-tech age. Song, attending the seminar as a scientist, briefed fellow participants on China's potentials, development strategy and research and development projects in the high-tech field, reform of the scientific research system and achievements in the last decade. More than 40 scientists from over 20 countries are taking part in the two-day seminar organized by the board of research and cooperation between Sweden and developing countries. Some top swedish scientists expressed their wishes to further the cooperation between Sweden and China in science and technology when Song met with them today.
