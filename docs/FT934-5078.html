931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Budget 93: Their Budget - The defence executive Martin Jay Lives in Hampshire. Has three children - one in private school, one at university, one employed. Wife is a part-time GP Job: managing director of Vosper Thornycroft, the Southampton-based shipbuilder Earns: Pounds 140,000 Verdict: 'It seems the cuts in defence expenditure are going to be less draconian than had been predicted. The proposals on sick pay I welcome. The chancellor appears to be saying say if you manage your sick leave better, then as a company you will be better off. I have always thought there was scope for better management for sick leave. On a personal level, I welcome the air travel tax. I travel a lot and it has always annoyed me that I have to pay taxes at other airports and people do not have to pay here.'
