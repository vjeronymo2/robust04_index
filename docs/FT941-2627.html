940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmeg and mace prices increased in Indonesia after the end of Ramadan, reports Man Producten. BWP was quoted at USDollars 1,550 a tonne, with shrivels at Dollars 1,750, ABCD Dollars 1,850 and mace Dollars 2,800, all ex warehouse. Grenada mace No. 2 was fetching Dollars 2,700 a tonne, cif, for May/June shipment. No. 1 was unavailable. West Indian suns were at Dollars 1,950 a tonne and guns at Dollars 1,750 for April shipment. Indonesian cassia prices were slightly lower KA/VA sticks were at Dollars 2,300 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 2,160 shipment; KB BR/CL Dollars 1,725, spot, and Dollars 1,500 shipment. Madagascan cinnamon was quoted at FFr6.50 a pound, while Seychelles offerings were fetching Dollars 1,450, cif. Mexican pimento prices were unchanged at Dollars 2,000, spot, and Dollars 2,750 for shipment. Jamaican pimento was at Dollars 2,200, spot, and Dollars 2,100 shipment, cif.
