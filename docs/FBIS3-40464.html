Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Text] On 15 June Israeli Trade Minister Harisshu [phonetic] met with MITI Minister Mori at MITI headquarters in Kasumigaseki, Tokyo. At this meeting they signed an agreement that the Government Industrial Research Institute, Osaka, AIST and Hebrew University will begin joint research next year on the high)level utilization of microalgae. Mr. Mori also proposed that Israel send a team of researchers to Japan. Mr. Harisshu is visiting Japan at the invitation of Minister Mori.
