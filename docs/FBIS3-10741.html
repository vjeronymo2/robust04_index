Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Paraguay monitored through 10 March. National Telecommunications Administration President Miguel Horacio Gini reported that the president of the Republic has authorized the signing of a $26 million loan agreement in Japan. This credit will be used to build a new earth station, to restore the old facility, to implement a digital link, and expand the international telephone communications plant. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 8 March 94 p 18) The Central Bank reported that the consumer price index in the year's first two months rose 6 percent; last year it rose 5.5 percent. The inflation rate was 3.1 percent in January and 2.9 percent in February. Food registered the highest increase with 1.1 percent; other expenses, 1.6 percent; housing, 0.1 percent; and clothing, 0.1 percent. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 8 Mar 94 p 13)
