BFN [Text] Delegations from Italy, Venezuela, Mexico, Puerto Rico, Japan, Cyprus, and Greece have arrived in Cuba to participate in the May Day ceremonies. There are over 250 invited guests already in Cuba; they are currently visiting Havana work centers and participating in May Day ceremonies at unions and workplaces. The international relations department of the Cuban Workers Federation added that the visitors represent over 15 countries and 34 unions and organizations of various leanings. The arrival in Cuba of union delegates from the United States, Pakistan, and the Vasque region is expected tonight. They will participate in the May Day ceremonies scheduled for 30 April in Matanzas, Pinar del Rio, Havana Province, and four Havana municipalities, as well as in the National Assembly of the People's Government session being held in honor of May Day.
