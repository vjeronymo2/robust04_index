920423 FT 23 APR 92 / International Company News: Mitsui merger talks suspended MITSUI Petrochemical Industries and Mitsui Toatsu Chemicals, both part of the Mitsui group, are suspending talks on a possible merger, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Mr Shogo Takebayashi, Mitsui Petrochemical president, said: 'We need a cooling-off period since our merger plans were leaked to the public in their early stages. But we have not yet abandoned the merger plans with Mitsui Toatsu.'
