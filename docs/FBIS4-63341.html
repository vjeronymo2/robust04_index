BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev regards the concentration of Ukrainian troops in Crimea as potentially explosive. According to the information coming in, the defense minister said, about 400 Ukrainian special purpose troops are concentrated in the airport area near Simferopol. Five hundred Ukrainian servicemen have been deployed at the Cape of Khersones and, in the area of Sukhaya Bolka, on the eastern outskirts of Sevastopol--200 servicemen. When troops are being brought in and concentrated in an area, Pavel Grachev said, they can start military activities at any moment. The minister gave assurances that he had given the order for decisive action to stop all provocations directed against the Black Sea Fleet, regardless of who commits them.
