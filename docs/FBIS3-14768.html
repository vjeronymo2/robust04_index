Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow, 26 Feb -- Federation Council deputies today approved Vladimir Nikitov as head of the chamber's administration. The 43-year-old candidate of technical sciences was adviser to Vladimir Shumeyko when he was first deputy chairman of the government. He has also had considerable experience of working in the Supreme Soviet. In reply to deputies' questions about guaranteeing their parliamentary activity, Vladimir Nikitov said, in particular, that the question of deputies' badges had not yet been decided. The matter is being held up because of their cost: The proposed draft badge containing precious metals will cost half a million [currency not specified].
