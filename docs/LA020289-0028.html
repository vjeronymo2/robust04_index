February 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

A tape of an interview convicted murderer Ted Bundy had with Christian anti-pornography crusader Dr. James Dobson shortly before his execution last week will be offered to the public in exchange for a donation of under $30. On the 29-minute videotaped, Bundy blames pornography for helping to turn him into a serial killer. An executive with Dobson's Focus on the Family organization said the videotape would be put "in the hands of people with anti-pornography groups . . . (to( meet whatever demand is there." Internal Revenue Service regulations prevent direct sale of the videotape by the ministry. ALEENE MacMINN
