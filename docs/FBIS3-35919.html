Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 31 (TURAN) -- The day before Geydar Aliyev received the President of American Oil Company "AMOCO", Robert Blanton. During the meeting they have discussed the perspectives of cooperation between State Oil Company of Azerbaijan and "AMOCO". In this connection, Robert Blanton said that the contract concerning development of "Azeri-Chirag" oil-fields by consortium of western oil companies was perspective as well. "AMOCO" is among the companies taking part in this project. The conclusion of this contract is of strategic significance for Azerbaijan. According to Geydar Aliyev, the "terms of the contract are 30 years. That's why before signing such a contract we have to examine everything in details". Aliyev stressed that the leadership of SOCAR [State Oil Company of Azerbaijan] did not agree with the previous terms of the contract worked out by independent expert group led by Manaf Manafov. "We need two-three weeks to clear everything up", said Geydar Aliyev. So, Azerbaijani leader did not answer the main question: will Baku sign the contract with Western oil companies concerning development of Caspian offshore oil-fields or not? Meanwhile, Western companies, the members of oil consortium are afraid that such contract would not be signed at all. One of the officials of oil companies told a TURAN's reporter that "we will wait two-three more months and then we will wrap up our work". The same official said in case if Azerbaijan refused to sign the contract, they would start their activities in Turkmenistan or Kazakhstan.
