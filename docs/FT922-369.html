920627 FT 27 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Losses deepen at United Industries Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.54m were announced by United Industries, the springs and tools maker, for the year to April 4. Last time the loss was Pounds 795,000. Mr Kenneth Coates, the chairman, said that demand had again fallen in the past year resulting in a further 20 per cent drop in turnover to Pounds 32.3m (Pounds 40.5m). Losses at the operating level amounted to Pounds 326,000 compared with profits of Pounds 718,000 and the pre-tax result was after net interest payable of Pounds 1.22m (Pounds 1.51m). Losses per share amounted to 5.59p (1.72p) and there is no dividend for the year (1p).
