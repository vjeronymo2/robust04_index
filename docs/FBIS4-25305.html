BFN [By reporter Zhou Jierong (0719 6043 5554)] [Text] Shanghai, 7 Jun (XINHUA) -- Vanuatu's Prime Minister Maxime Carlot Korman arrived in Shanghai this morning to begin his four-day work visit to Shanghai. Shanghai Vice Mayor Xia Keqiang met the prime minister at the airport. Two young pioneers presented bouquets to the prime minister and his wife. The purpose of Prime Minister Carlot's current visit to Shanghai at the invitation of the Chinese Government is to explore ways and means to further develop economic and trade cooperation with Shanghai and to continuously promote the healthy development of Sino-Vanuatu relationship.
