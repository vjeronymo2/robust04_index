920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / Former chairman faces fraud charges MR Roy Wharton, former chairman of Castlegate, the collapsed Reading financial services group, was yesterday charged with fraud. Reading magistrates were told it was estimated that investors had lost Pounds 34m. Mr Wharton faces two charges alleging that between April 1988 and November 1990 he ran Castlegate Securities and Castlegate Group Holdings for a fraudulent purpose with intent to defraud investors. He was remanded on bail until October 22.
