BFN [Text] The files on the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najah Church and the assassination of Dany Sham'un and his family will be referred to the Judicial Council shortly. The council will set a date for the trial of the defendants now that the bills of indictment in the two cases have been issued. Meanwhile, Lebanon is seeking the extradition of Ghassan Tuma, head of the Lebanese Forces' Intelligence Department, from the United States. Ghassan Tuma is accused of assassinating Sham'un and therefore is an important link in revealing all the implications of the case.
