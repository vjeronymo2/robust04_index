Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The president of Azerbaijan has said that Baku welcomes mediation by regional countries, and specifically by the Islamic Republic of Iran, for peace in the region. IRNA reports that in his meeting with the director general for CIS and Caucasian affairs in our Foreign Ministry, Geydar Aliyev said that, as in the past, the Republic of Azerbaijan still wants to resolve the war with Armenia through talks. During the past few days, the Azerbaijani army has liberated parts of the occupied territories in the republic and has pushed back the forces of the Armenian army. Stressing the need for the expansion of multifaceted relations with the Islamic Republic of Iran, Geydar Aliyev called for the signing of a border agreement between Tehran and Baku.
