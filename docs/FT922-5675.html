920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / UK Company News: British & American asset value jumps British & American Film Holdings reported higher profit and dividend and a substantial lift in net asset value. Over the 1991 year the asset value rose from 701.9p to 880.8p; at May 8 it stood at 903.6p. All valuations excluded film rights. Profit before tax came to Pounds 1.27m (Pounds 1.23m) and earnings per share to 34.4p (32.5p). The final dividend is 7.32p to make 10.695p (9.3p), and there is a bonus of 5p.
