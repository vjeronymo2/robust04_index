921027 FT 27 OCT 92 / Lamont rules out BCCI compensation MR Norman Lamont, the chancellor, yesterday ruled out compensation payments to victims of the collapsed Bank of Credit and Commerce International. MPs Mr Keith Vaz and Sir Rhodes Boyson met the chancellor to ask for government help for depositors. Mr Vaz, Labour MP for Leicester East, said he and Sir Rhodes, Tory member for Brent North, were 'bitterly disappointed' that their request had not been agreed to.
