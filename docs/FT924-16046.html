921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Lincat climbs 14% as demand improves A significant improvement in demand enabled Lincat Group, the USM-quoted catering equipment maker, to eradicate the first half profits shortfall of Pounds 270,000 and end the year to June 30 1992 with pre-tax profits ahead 14 per cent from Pounds 708,000 to Pounds 810,000. Turnover rose to Pounds 11.69m (Pounds 11.5m). Earnings per share rose from 8p to 8.2p and the dividend is held at 5.1p with an unchanged final of 3.3p. Net interest paid was lower at Pounds 274,000 (Pounds 419,000). Mr Martin Craddock, chairman, said the company had reduced borrowings - gearing was down from 76 per cent to 51 per cent - and a further cut this year remained a key objective.
