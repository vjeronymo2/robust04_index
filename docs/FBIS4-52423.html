BFN [Text] Our country's ambassador O Song-hwan paid a farewell call to Nicaraguan President Violeta Chamorro on 29 April. At the meeting the ambassador conveyed the greetings of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to President Violeta Chamorro. The president expressed deep thanks for the greetings and asked the ambassador to deliver his warm greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The president said that Korea's reality shows that the road of socialism chosen by the Workers' Party of Korea and the Korean people under President Kim Il-song's wise leadership is very just. He continued: The world admires the changes taking place in Korea today. Korea has become a most powerful country by embodying the principles of independence in politics, self-reliance in economics, and self-defense in national defense. The Korean people's potential is inexhaustible and no one can match the Korean people's strength. The development of friendly relations between Nicaragua and Korea will be eternal in the future. We will actively support in the future the Korean people's struggle for the realization of the country's reunification.
