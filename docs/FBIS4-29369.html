BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter and his entourage on a visit to Korea saw a performance of "Song of Seasons", a folk dance suite, at the Mansudae Art Theatre here today. Seeing it together with the guests were Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Song Ho-kyong, Vice-Minister of Culture and Art Kim Chong-ho, other officials concerned and working people in the city. The performance was highly acclaimed by the audience for its high ideological and artistic plane.
