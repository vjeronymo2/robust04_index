930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / UK Company News: River & Mercantile American assets up River & Mercantile American Capital and Income Trust reported a net asset value of 49.54p per capital share as at August 31. The figure was marginally ahead of the 49.34p of end-February and represented an advance of 62 per cent on the August 1992 value of 30.6p. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-August edged ahead to Pounds 265,000 (Pounds 250,000), equivalent to earnings of 3.53p (3.33p) per income share. The second interim dividend is maintained at 1.8p, making 3.6p to date.
