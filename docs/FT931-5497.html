930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Intereurope Tech declines 12% Intereurope Technology Services, the technical documentation group, saw profits dip 12 per cent to Pounds 542,000 pre-tax for the six months to end-December. The decline from the comparable Pounds 614,000 came on turnover down from Pounds 5.41m to Pounds 4.83m. The interim dividend is maintained at 2p, payable from earnings of 7.05p (7.95p).
