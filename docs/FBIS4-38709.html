BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 1 TASS -- "Kuwait is prepared to change its legislation and adopt new laws if necessary to promote the development of larger-scale and long-term cooperation with Russia", Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Sa'dun, speaker of the Kuwaiti National Assembly, who leads a parliamentary delegation, told Russian First Vice-Premier Oleg Soskovets here on Wednesday. Mr. Sa'dun expressed gratitude to the Russian Federation for its support to Kuwait in upholding its independence. Soskovets, having expressed appreciation of Kuwait's desire to strengthen its defence capacity, emphasised that high technologies in the Russian military industry would help resolve many a matter concerning Russian-Kuwaiti cooperation in this respect. "But it is up to your parliament to decide on whether to endorse or turn down funds necessary for the purchase of this technology which we could sell to you", Soskovets added. Russia would welcome Kuwaiti investments in various branches of the Russian economy to promote economic reform, Soskovets pointed out.
