Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The International Campaign With Cuba For All Times launched by the Union of Young Communists [UJC] has been promoted among youth organizations and other institutions in Colombia. Ibis Albizu Gonzalez, member of the UJC International Relations Department, has established new contacts in this regard with the Communist Youth, the ruling Liberal Party Youth, a youth organization, trade unions, and leaders of the Solidarity With Peoples House.
