940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Vega Vega, the software and systems engineering group, reported pre-tax profits ahead 45 per cent from Pounds 1.48m to Pounds 2.15m for the year to April 30. Turnover rose Pounds 1.6m to Pounds 9.7m. Last year's figure was after a Pounds 227,000 exceptional charge representing flotation costs. Stripping this out the pre-tax advance was 26 per cent. Earnings per share emerged at 10.01p (6.38p) and an improved final dividend of 2.075p is proposed, making 3.11p (2.73p) for the year.
