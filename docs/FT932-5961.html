930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: Mechanical services A consortium between SULZER INFRA (UK) and RASHLEIGH PHIPPS is currently undertaking building services contracts totalling some Pounds 20m. The latest is a new 180,000 sq ft headquarters for Sony United Kingdom which has just been topped out at Brooklands, the former motor racing circuit in Surrey. This is being undertaken under a Pounds 5m mechanical and electrical services contract for Trafalgar House Construction.
