940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican shares ended 1.25 per cent firmer after a rollercoaster session characterised by a mixture of speculative buying, on the back of brokerage recommendations, and profit-taking. The IPC index of 37 leading shares was finally 29.73 points up at 2,404.91. Turnover came to a heavy 130.7m new pesos.
