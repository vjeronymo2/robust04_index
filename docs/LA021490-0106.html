February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BUSH AGAIN: Last April, President Bush spoke at a former drug dealers' confiscated hideaway (B5) in Orange County -- a coup for Sheriff Brad Gates. . . . Now, Bush may be coming to talk about drugs again, with Gates again a factor. . . . Bush's trip will be paid for by the Drug Use Is Life Abuse foundation -- a Gates project and spinoff of his Citizens Advisory Council. . . . For this visit, which could come in a couple of weeks, presidential advance men have checked out several Santa Ana-area schools. Dana Parsons
