930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Grampian TV Grampian Television returned profits of Pounds 3.21m pre - tax for the year to February 28, a marginal improvement over the previous Pounds 3.18m. Advertising revenue for the Aberdeen-based ITV company declined 3.7 per cent. Turnover totalled Pounds 20.6m (Pounds 20.4m). After tax of Pounds 1.07m (Pounds 1.12m) and an extraordinary credit of Pounds 135,000 (charge Pounds 688,000), being additional tax relief for Channel 3 licence application costs, profit for the year worked through at Pounds 2.28m (Pounds 1.37m). Earnings emerged at 14.75p (14.23p) and a proposed final dividend of 5p makes a 6.1p (5.5p) total. Glenburnie, the property investment and development subsidiary, increased its profits contribution from Pounds 615,000 to Pounds 847,000.
