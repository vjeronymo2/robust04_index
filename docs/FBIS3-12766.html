Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The home minister has reiterated that nobody can question the accession of Jammu and Kashmir to India. Mr. S.B. Chavan also said that Washington should get its facts clear and should stop meddling in the internal affairs of the country. He told the Rajya Sabha today during the zero hour that he stands by his statement on the subject made in the Lok Sabha on Wednesday. Mr. Chavan pointed out that it was India which moved the United Nations as a complainant following the naked aggression of Pakistan on Kashmir.
