Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 11 Mar -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left on 11 March for Tunisia and Israel. The program of the lightning visit by the head of the foreign political department includes meetings with Yasir 'Arafat, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, and with the leaders of Tunisia and Israel. The Russian minister will hand over to the leaders of the PLO and Israel a personal message from Russian President Boris Yeltsin on the problems of achieving an all-embracing peace and security in the region.
