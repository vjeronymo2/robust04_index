Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tajikistan's armed opposition is preparing to subject Russian-manned posts on one of the sections of the republic's border with Afghanistan to mortar attacks in response to a stepping-up of measures to secure that stretch of the border, the press bureau of the Russian border guards in Tajiikistan told INTERFAX. In some areas along that part of the border, which is close to the town of Khorog, the opposition also plans to lay mines made of explosives stolen from state storehouses, the bureau said. It said the border guard command had launched a set of measures to ensure the safety of troops and stabilize the situation on the Khorog section of the border.
