BFN [Text] In line with the continuation of fighting among the involved sides in the capital of Afghanistan, Kabul city center was this morning subjected to strikes by dozens of missiles. According to a report by the Central News Desk from Kabul, the missiles landed on central parts of Kabul city and caused considerable damage, leaving at least 13 people dead and a large number wounded. The hospital sources in Kabul likewise announced that in the course of the past three days' fighting in Kabul 100 people were killed and 500 were injured. A report by AFP states that a spokesman for the UN secretary general has announced: Within the framework of revival of the political presence of this organization in Afghanistan, a special team will visit this country. He added: Until the security situation improves in Kabul, the UN mission will be established temporarily in Jalalabad.
