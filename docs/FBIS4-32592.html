BFN [Text] We have just learned that Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek has accepted the resignation of Justice Minister Miha Kozinc. In the coming days, the prime minister will inform the State Chamber about this and propose a new candidate for justice minister. The prime minister has asked the relevant government expert services to prepare a statement for the next government session and an assessment of the work of the Housing Commission, as well as to prepare guidelines for forming and adopting all necessary documents regarding the problem. This was said in the public announcement the prime minister's Cabinet has just issued.
