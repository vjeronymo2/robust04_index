BFN [Excerpts] Brcko, 12 Jun (TANJUG) -- Ivan Todorovic (62) was wounded in an artillery attack on Brcko today, the press center in Brcko has reported. According to Serb military sources, eight 128 mm multiple-rocket launcher missiles were fired at 1202 today from positions in Rajevo Selo on the left bank of the Sava, from the territory of the Republic of Croatia. [passage omitted] Initial reports suggested that the attack was launched from Vucilovac, but republic of Srpska army observers determined that the missiles were fired from the nearby Rajevo Selo in the Republic of Croatia.
