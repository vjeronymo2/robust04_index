940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Banham calls for Treasury abolition The Treasury should be abolished as part of a drive to refocus policymaking, according to proposals from Sir John Banham, chairman of the Local Government Commission and former head of the Confederation of British Industry, Peter Marsh writes. Sir John, chairman of construction group Tarmac, says in his book The Anatomy for Change, published today, that scrapping the Treasury is vital to make civil servants more accountable and redirect them towards strategic thinking.
