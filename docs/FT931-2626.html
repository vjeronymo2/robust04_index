930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Martin Currie Pacific assets up Martin Currie Pacific Trust improved its net asset value per share from 297.2p to 378.7p over the year to February 28. Net revenue fell from Pounds 134,000 to Pounds 118,000, giving earnings per share of 0.94p, against 1.1p. An unchanged dividend of 0.4p is recommended.
