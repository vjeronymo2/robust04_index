August 18, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: VENTURA;

MAN, 18, KILLED IN TRAFFIC ACCIDENT

An 18-year-old Ventura man was killed in a car accident Friday when another vehicle struck him on the driver's side as he was making a left-hand turn in an intersection, Ventura police said.

The man died of multiple injuries at 3 p.m. in the Ventura County Medical Center's operating room, Deputy Coroner Mitch Breese said.

Breese said he hoped to notify the man's relatives by this morning.

The victim's 16-year-old passenger, a girl from Ventura, remains at the medical center in stable condition, a hospital spokeswoman said.

The man, traveling north on Montgomery Avenue, entered the intersection about 1:20 p.m. to turn west onto Henderson Road, Officer Rick French said. KIRSTEN LEE SWARTZ
