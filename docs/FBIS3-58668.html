Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos today met with Australian Foreign Minister Gareth Evans, who is visiting Greece, and discussed issues on improving Australian-European Union [EU] relations. Evans said he is not very satisfied with Australia's cooperation with the EU. Pangalos said Greece would have wanted to import cheaper products from Australia instead of more expensive ones from EU member states because of communal solidarity.
