October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WEST HOLLYWOOD;

TOWN HALL MEETING PLANNED

The city's Housing and Economic Development Division will sponsor a town hall meeting at 6:30 p.m. Nov. 14 in Fiesta Hall at Plummer Park, 7377 Santa Monica Blvd.

The meeting will focus on a variety of issues, including housing and home services, residential public safety concerns, and code enforcement. For more information, call (213) 854-7475.
