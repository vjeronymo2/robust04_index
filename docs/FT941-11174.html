940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Indonesian mace offerings were scarcethis week, with prices between USDollars 3,400 and Dollars 3,500 a tonne, reports Man Producten. The European nutmeg market remained very firm, with bwp at Dollars 1,650, shrivels Dollars 1,800, abcd Dollars 1,875 and mace Dollars 3,050-Dollars 3,100, all cif. Grenada mace No. 2 was higher again at Dollars 2,900 a tonne, cif, for May/June shipment. West Indian suns were at Dollars 1,900 a tonne and guns at Dollars 1,700, for April shipment. prices were unchanged. Mandagascan cinnamom was quoted at FFr5.70 a pound and Seychelles at Dollars 1,400 a tonne, cif. Mexican pimento pricves were stable at Dollars 2,000 a tonne, spot, and Dollars 2,050 for shipment. Jamaica spot pimento was at Dollars 2,200 a tonne and shipment at Dollars 2,100, cif.
