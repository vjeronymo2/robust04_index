BFN [Text] Baghdad, 3 Apr (INA)--National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih today received a delegation from the Jordanian House of Representatives led by Deputy House Speaker 'Abd Musa al-Nahar. During the meeting, they reviewed Iraqi-Jordanian parliamentary ties and means of promoting them in the interest of the two fraternal countries. They also reviewed Arab and international developments in light of the continuation of the U.S.-Zionist conspiracy spearheaded by the United States and its partners, which is targeting Iraq and its unity. The meeting was attended by the deputy speaker of the National Assembly and several chairmen of the Assembly's permanent committees.
