Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 25 Feb (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE) -- According to the latest statistics compiled by the Information Office of the State Commission for Restructuring Economy, there now are a total of 13,000 joint-stock enterprises in China, with the amount of capital stock and the number of stockholders reaching 208.6 billion yuan and 25 million, respectively. In 1993, 9,440 new joint-stock enterprises were set up in China, including 2,968 joint-stock limited companies. By the end of last year, there were 6,472 joint-stock limited companies, 1,776 internal holding companies, and 122 joint-stock companies listing in the market. Shandong Province has 1,101 joint-stock enterprises, ranking first in the country. There are 870 joint-stock enterprises in Zhejiang, 827 in Sichuan, 684 in Henan, 653 in Hubei, and 645 in Jiangsu. Moreover, there are 176, 92, 54, and 45 such enterprises in Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Guangzhou,respectively.
