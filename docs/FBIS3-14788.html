Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Yarushin] [Text] Moscow February 28 TASS -- Ruslan Aushev has been re-elected to the post of Ingush president, ITAR-TASS learned from well-informed sources in the Ingush capital of Nazran. Vice-President Boris Agapov also managed to retain his post. According to preliminary election results as of 10:00 p.m., the turnout made up over 70 percent. The final results of the presidential and vice-presidential elections, as well as of the consitutional referendum are not to be available until Tuesday, March 1. Results of parliamentary elections will be published in about a week and a half.
