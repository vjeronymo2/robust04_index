Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The director of a U.S. magazine has visited Cuba. Mortimer Zuckerman, executive director of the magazine U.S. NEWS AND WORLD REPORT, called his first visit to Cuba fascinating because it allowed him to learn firsthand about health, the pharmaceutical industry, and biotechnology. During his stay on the island, Zuckerman met with Commander in Chief Fidel Castro, and called the meeting unforgettable and fruitful.
