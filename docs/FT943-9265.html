940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Waterloo strike service changed The South West Trains division of British Rail yesterday said trains would run between London Waterloo and Guildford today but not on Monday and Tuesday as previously announced, because of the signal workers' strike. There will be services as far as Woking on Monday and Tuesday. There will be no services on all three days from Waterloo as far as Yeovil but there will be an hourly service to Salisbury.
