920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / Lloyd's agency to expand activities HAYTER BROCKBANK, the Lloyd's of London agency, is to expand into motor insurance through an office in Cardiff. It will tackle the car market through direct writing, or tele-sales. The company expects to be writing its first business by the start of next year and to build eventually towards a staff of about 230. It intends to diversify into other financial services. The project has been backed by a government grant under a scheme to boost financial services in south Wales.
