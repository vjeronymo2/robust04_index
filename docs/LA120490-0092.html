December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Altman Goes Commercial: Director Robert Altman, whose film credits include "MASH," "Nashville" and the current "Vincent & Theo," will make his debut as a commercial director on a public service announcement for the American Mental Health Fund, which is being filmed in Oradell, N.J.. The commercial's goal is to focus public attention on the warning signs of mental illness in young people. CLAUDIA PUIG
