Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, March 2 (TURAN) -- The Press Service of the Ministry of National Security of Azerbaijan did not confirm the statement made by the Procurator General, Ali Omarov. The statement says that the Popular [People's] Front of Azerbaijan [PFA] scheduled a military uprising for March 18, 1994. The press service of the Ministry of National Security told TURAN that they do not have such information. Due to the complexities involved, TURAN's correspondent asked the press service to obtain detailed information from the Ministry of National Security concerning the "state coup". An employee of press service, Elshad Isayev, did not confirm the statement of the Procurator General. The representatives of the Ministry of National Security suggested that TURAN Agency should ask the Procurator's Office and the Ministry of Internal Affairs since criminal proceedings fall under the aegis of those Ministries.
