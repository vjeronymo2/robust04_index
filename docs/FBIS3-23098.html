Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Feb (MENA)--Unidentified gunmen this evening killed a soldier named 'Abd-al-Fattah Abu-Halab while he was standing near the Idfu checkpoint in Aswan Governorate in southern Egypt. The assailants managed to escape. An official security source stated that officials of the Aswan Security Directorate and the Criminal Investigation Department went to the scene of the incident to investigate.
