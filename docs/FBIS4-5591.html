BFN [Text] Seoul, April 8 (YONHAP) -- A committee was launched here Friday to prepare celebrations for the 50th anniversary of the United Nations. Former diplomat Kim Kyong-won was named chairman of the "Republic of Korea committee for the fiftieth anniversary of the United Nations" comprised of 88 members from the academic, media, business, women's and cultural sectors to prepare commemorative events for the world body's birthday.
