September 28, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

TISH JOHNSON WINS LPBT EVENT

Despite missing back-to-back split conversions, Tish Johnson of Panorama City won her second consecutive Ladies Professional Bowlers Tour title Thursday night, beating Sue Neidig, 180-161, in the final of the Columbia 300 Delaware Open.

Johnson, who earned $12,000 for the victory, won the AMF Cobra Classic last week.

Kim Terrell took third with stepladder victories over Leanne Barrette, 198-179, and Lisa Wagner, 208-186. Terrell lost to Neidig, 201-193, to set up the final between top qualifier Johnson and Neidig.
