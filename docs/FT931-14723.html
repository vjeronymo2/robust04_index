930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Mexican pimento prices have been firm, reports Man-producten. Spot increased from USDollars 1,600 to Dollars 1,850 a tonne ex-warehouse Rotterdam; shipment from Dollars 1,625 to Dollars 1,750-Dollars 1,775 cif; Jamaica unchanged at Dollars 2,350 spot, Dollars 2,175 cif. Nutmegs/mace: more industry interest for nearby and later shipments, but prices unchanged. Cassia: nearby firmer due lack of offers from Indonesia; kor b br/cl spot Dollars 1,600, afloat Dollars 1,400, shipment Dollars 1,335 cif; nsw2 va/ka sticks, spot unchanged Dollars 2,250, shipment Dollars 2,000 cif. Madagascar cinnamom shipment FFr 5.55 cif. Madagascar cloves weaker, Dollars 775 cif.
