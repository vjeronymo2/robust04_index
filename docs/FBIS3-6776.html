Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] and commander in chief of the defense services, attended and delivered an address at the inauguration of National Defense College [NDC] Training Course No. 1 held at the Anawrahta Hall of the NDC at Konemyinttha in Yangon [Rangoon] at 0900. The ceremony was attended by Lieutenant General Maung Aye, deputy commander in chief of the defense services and army commander in chief; Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin and Lt. Gen. Tin Tun, both SLORC members and deputy prime ministers; Lt. Gen. Tin U, SLORC secretary-2; SLORC members; cabinet ministers; the chief justice; the attorney general; Yangon city development committee chairman and mayor; senior military officials; NDC principal and lecturers; rectors from the universities; invited guests; and trainees. SLORC Chairman Sr. Gen. Than Shwe noted that the NDC's Training Course No. 1, which opened today, is the first of its kind to be held in Myanmar [Burma] and the training is intended to nurture reliable and qualified leaders for the country. He expressed the need for the trainees to be diligent in their training and to implement the experiences gained from the course for the betterment of the country. Sr. Gen. Than Shwe then cordially greeted the trainees and departed at 1030.
