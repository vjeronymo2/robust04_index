November 17, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

WEST VALLEY LEAGUE

Granada Hills 68, Chatsworth 21 -- Quarterback Bryan Martin threw for six touchdowns and ran for one to lead the Highlanders (9-1) over the host Chancellors.

Martin completed 22 of 29 passes for 398 yards and three touchdowns. Brett Washington became Granada Hills' all-time leading scorer on his third-quarter, 50-yard touchdown run that put the Highlanders ahead, 55-21. Washington had five carries for 119 yards. GARY HYNES
