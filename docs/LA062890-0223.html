June 28, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A doubleheader featuring Mike Tyson and George Foreman is headed for Atlantic City, N.J., on Sept. 22, where the promoter will be the Trump Plaza hotel-casino of financier Donald Trump.

Tyson is scheduled to fight Alex Stewart and Foreman might face Francesco Damiani or Michael Dokes.

The doubleheader originally was scheduled for Sept. 8 and was expected to be held at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas, but promoter Bob Arum said Caesars officials found the fee too high.
