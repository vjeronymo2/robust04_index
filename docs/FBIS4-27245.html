BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, May 31, (OANA/BERNAMA) -- Prime Minister Dr Mahathir Mohamed Tuesday denied a Cable News Network (CNN) report that Malaysia intends to supply arms to the Bosnians in their struggle against Serb aggression in Bosnia-Herzegovina. "If somebody is providing arms to Bosnia, we know nothing about this thing," he told reporters here when asked to comment on a CNN report Tuesday, claiming that Malaysia, Iran and Turkey will supply arms to the Bosnians. Dr Mahathir said Malaysia was only providing arms to its 1,500 soldiers serving under the United Nations Protection Force (UNPROFOR) in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The CNN report could have misinterpreted this as supplying arms to the Bosnians, he added. We are not in the gun running business, he said.
