BFN [Text] Baku (April 26) TURAN -- This evening the CSCE Minsk Group on Nagorno Karabakh headed by the Swedish diplomat, Mathias Mosberg, will visit Baku for a day. The delegation consists of 14 people, which includes military experts and diplomats. The delegation will meet with the Azerbaijani leadership and will visit the front line. Earlier when Geydar Aliyev met with the Turkish Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin, he called upon the CSCE to take an active part in the peace settlement of the Azeri-Armenian conflict.
