Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 1, IRNA -- The president said today that the Islamic Republic of Iran, could, on the basis on the Holy Koran and the Shari'a, present for the first time ever an ideal model for role of women in society. Hojjat ol-Eslam Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani speaking to officials in charge of the Presidential Bureau for Women's Affairs, termed women's activities in iran as one of the most healthy and progressive when compared to other countries. Referring to women's role in building a healthy society, he stated that managing household affaires and bringing up children is a god-given trait of women. In Islamic Iran, said the president, there is no hindrance to women's education and scientific growth and their holding important and sensitive jobs. Touching on the youth problems faced by Western societies, he said youth in Iran are in "more healthy condition" than any other country across the globe. Hoj. Hashemi-Rafsanjani said that it is an "honour" for Iran's Islamic system to have a consolidated society. Before his speech, the president's advisor for women's affairs, Shahla Habibi, briefed the gathering on the activities of bureau.
