BFN [Text] The Jordanian delegation, led by Chief Chamberlain Prince Ra'd Bin-Zayd and Talal Sat'an al-Hasan [minister of state for foreign affairs], returned to Amman at dawn today after participating in Nelson Mandela's inauguration in South Africa. The delegation was received at Queen Alia International Airport by Nayif al-Hadid, secretary general of the Foreign Ministry; the chief of the Protocol at the Foreign Ministry; and the South African charge d'affaires in Amman.
