941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Howard aide on police charge A personal assistant to Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, has been suspended after being arrested for allegedly breaching Scotland Yard security and taking a Special Branch car. Ms Janine Barnes is reported to have walked into the underground car park beneath Scotland Yard after a row with one of Mr Howard's bodyguards. She was not stopped when she drove an unmarked Special Branch car out of the building. It is understood that security cameras were not working. The car was later found abandoned in Clapham, south London. Ms Barnes went voluntarily to Brixton police station.
