941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Compass - Other companies The acquisitive catering and healthcare group, is expected on Thursday to announce profits of Pounds 57m for the year to the end of September, sharply ahead of the previous Pounds 41.5m. The rise has been driven by acquisitions, the biggest of which is Canteen Corporation, the third largest US caterer. It was bought in June for Dollars 450m, and is likely to contribute about Pounds 7m this time. While underlying growth is seen as steady, this year's rail strike is thought to have cost the group's station catering business about Pounds 1m.
