BFN [Edict No. 1186 of the Russian Federation President "On the Powers of Representatives of the Russian Federation President in Russian Federation Components," dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 10 June 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin] [Text] In connection with the need to establish the status of representatives of the Russian Federation president in republics, krays, oblasts, cities of federal significance, the autonomous oblast, and autonomous okrugs, in accordance with the Russian Federation Constitution and on the basis of Article 83 of the Russian Federation Constitution, I decree that: The Russian Federation president's representatives in the republics appointed at an earlier date under RSFSR presidential directive No. 34-rp "On Approving the Temporary Statute Regarding the RSFSR President's Representatives in Republics Within the RSFSR," and also the Russian Federation president's representatives in the krays, oblasts, cities of federal significance, the autonomous oblast, and autonomous okrugs appointed at an earlier date under Russian Federation presidential edict No. 186 "On the Russian Federation President's Representatives in Krays, Oblasts, the Autonomous Oblast, Autonomous Okrugs, and the Cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg" dated 5 February 1993 are to be regarded as plenipotentiary representatives of the Russian Federation president in the appropriate Russian Federation components. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin Moscow, the Kremlin 10 June 1994 No. 1186
