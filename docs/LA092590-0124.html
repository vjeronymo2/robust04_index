September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Rochelle McReynolds has been hired as the Laguna Art Museum's new development director.

A former director of major gifts for the Boston Ballet, McReynolds began working in Laguna earlier this month. A native of Laguna Beach, she received a bachelor's degree in dance from UC Irvine and a master's degree in mass communications from Emerson College in Boston. She replaces Lynn Kirst, who resigned in May.
