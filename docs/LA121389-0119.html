December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Bergen Brunswig Corp.: The Orange-based company's first-quarter profit jumped 24% from a year ago to a record $14.9 million. Sales totaled $1.1 billion, up 13%. The firm, which distributes prescription pharmaceuticals and other health-care products and prerecorded video cassettes, attributed the results in part to strong sales growth in the hospital sector.
