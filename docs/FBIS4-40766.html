BFN [Text] Moscow, June 20, RIA -- President Boris Yeltsin of the Russian Federation has congratulated the American Chkalov Trans- Polar Flight Committee on its 20th anniversary [as received]. According to the presidential press service, way back in 1937, millions of Russians and Americans had applauded Valeriy Chkalov, Georgiy Baydukov and Aleksandr Belyakov who flew over the north pole all the way to the USA at the time. Yeltsin hailed the committee's noble activities aimed at strengthening mutual understanding between Russia and America and promoting good relations between our two countries, wishing his members and all residents of Vancouver and Portland success in their work, peace, happiness and prosperity.
