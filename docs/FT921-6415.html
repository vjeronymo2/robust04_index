920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Record Hldgs falls 29% to Pounds 3.35m Record Holdings, a maker of hand and bench tools, suffered a 29 per cent fall in pre-tax profits to Pounds 3.35m for the 12 months to end-December. The company described the period as one of its 'worst recorded trading years'. Turnover fell to Pounds 26m (Pounds 34.9m), although Pounds 5.6m of the downturn was accounted for by sold or discontinued activities. Earnings worked through at 6.9p (10.4p). A same-again final dividend of 2.45p makes a maintained 3.6p total.
