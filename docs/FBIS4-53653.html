BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 21 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, president of the Presidential Council of the Republic of Yemen, on the occasion of the fourth founding anniversary of the Republic. The message wished him and the Yemeni people success in the efforts to consolidate the unification and stability of the country.
