920408 FT 08 APR 92 / Bankrupt jailed for 18 months A BANKRUPT who admitted using a false name to obtain Pounds 3.2m credit to fund new business ventures was sentenced to 18 months imprisonment at Southwark Crown Court. The court was told that Mr Leslie Cairns of Hyde, Cheshire, had obtained Pounds 2.95m from Bank of Ireland and Pounds 250,000 from Hallwood Finance. The money had been repaid. He pleaded guilty to five charges of acting as a director while bankrupt, two of procuring the execution of a valuable security and one of obtaining credit while a bankrupt.
