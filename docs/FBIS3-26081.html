Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, February 1 (XINHUA) -- Georgian Defense Minister Gen. Karkarashvili announced his resignation today without giving any explanation for his decision. The news center of the Georgian Defense Ministry had confirmed the resignation, but made no comment on the news, INTERFAX reported. Meanwhile, President Eduard Shevardnadze's news office remained silent about the resignation. Karkarashvili, a veteran of the Afghan war, became defense minister in 1993. He had asked for resignation twice before, but his request was rejected by Shevardnadze. The minister had been scheduled to meet his Russian counterpart Pavel Grachev in Tbilisi on Wednesday.
