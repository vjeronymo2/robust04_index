BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 20/6/1994 -- President Ion Iliescu of Romania received Monday, 20 June, Husamettin Cindoruk, the president of the Grand National Assembly of Turkey. Mr. Cindoruk told the press after the interview with the Romanian president that the main topic of their talk was the Turkish-Romanian cooperation at multiple levels. "President Iliescu told us how important the cooperation in the Black Sea zone is," the guest said, "and our common view is that Turkey and Romania can ensure, by collaboration, the peace in the region." The president of the Turkish Grand National Assembly conveyed to President Iliescu [Turkish] President Suleyman Demirel's invitation to visit Turkey.
