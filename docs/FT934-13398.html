931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / International Company News: Hoechst Hoechst, a leading German chemicals group, said drug sales have started to improve, following a 2.6 per cent decline to DM4.5bn (Dollars 3.4bn) in the first half, writes Christopher Parkes in Frankfurt. Turnover from the pharmaceuticals divisions had increased 0.6 per cent after eight months and full-year sales were expected to improve 3 per cent, according to group executives.
