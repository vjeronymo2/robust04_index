920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / International Capital Markets: National Bank of New Zealand advances 22% NATIONAL Bank of New Zealand, the fourth largest bank in New Zealand, unveiled a 22 per cent increase in net profit from NZDollars 67m to NZDollars 82m (USDollars 44m) for 1991, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. The bank said it expected to see its strong presence in the corporate and retail markets continue in the coming year. Total assets rose 3.7 per cent to NZDollars 7.56bn. Restructuring in the business sector was reflected in a NZDollars 11m increase to NZDollars 38m in provisions for bad and doubtful debts.
