931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Titon moves ahead to Pounds 2m in difficult market Titon Holdings, the building products maker, reported pre-tax profits slightly ahead at Pounds 2m for the year to September 30, against Pounds 1.94m. The USM-quoted company said despite the continued fragile state of the building industry, sales had risen from Pounds 10.8m to Pounds 11.3m. The window refurbishment and replacement market had been the strongest. The pre-tax figure was struck after a Pounds 125,000 provision against a bad debt and lower interest income of Pounds 170,000 (Pounds 182,000). Earnings per share were 12.07p (11.69p) and the proposed final dividend of 2.9p leaves an increased total of 4.2p (3.7p).
