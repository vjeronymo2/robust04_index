BFN [Text] Lefkose, June 21 (A.A.) -- Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus [TRNC] President Rauf Denktas said on Tuesday [21 June] that his country has prepared a draft agreement of the UN proposed confidence-building measures. Denktas speaking to the Anatolian Agency said he discussed the draft agreement together with Foreign Ministry Deputy Under Secretary Tugay Ulucevik and head of Cyprus-Greece desk Ertugrul Apakan. He said TRNC always conveyed that it has the political will to reach an agreement adding that Greek Cypriot leader Glavkos Kliridhis did not want an agreement to reach a lasting solution for the Cyprus problem. Denktas said TRNC will always stand still and continue its struggle until the end.
