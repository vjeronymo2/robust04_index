August 13, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK

On Monday, the Costa Mesa Planning Commission will hear arguments for and against a proposed 24-bed halfway house for recovering alcoholics and drug addicts in the city's College Park neighborhood. The planned expansion of an existing six-bed halfway house operated by a father-and-son team, both recovering alcoholics, has drawn opposition from nearby residents who fear that the facility would attract drunks and homeless people.
