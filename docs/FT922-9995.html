920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Sheltered housing BARRATT CONSTRUCTION has won contracts worth Pounds 5m. In west central Scotland. Work will begin shortly on a development of 47 homes, worth Pounds 1.5m, for Loretto Housing Association in Coatbridge. The project will provide sheltered housing for up to 12 former patients of Hartwood Hospital, as well as for 35 local people who will be nominated by Monklands District Council. In Perth, work is underway on 12 apartments which will form the second phase of a residential development for Quality Street, the private rented housing company.
