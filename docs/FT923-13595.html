920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 11m water projects awarded to Amey AMEY GROUP companies have been awarded over Pounds 11m of water related contracts. Amey Farr, the water division of Amey Construction, has been awarded contracts by Severn Trent, Welsh Water, Thames Water and WRC, including both sewage treatment and potable water. The largest contract is the Pounds 7.2m Netheridge Sewage Treatment Works near Gloucester for Severn Trent. The scheme includes the construction of six aeration lanes, four storm tanks and six final tanks, together with sludge handling facilities, pipework and siteworks. A further contract for Thames Water Utilities is valued at Pounds 1m for additional works at Farmoor Reservoir near Oxford. Amey Mec-Tric, based at Maidenhead, has a Pounds 1m contract in Scotland at the Longmans Pumping Station for Highland Regional Council.
