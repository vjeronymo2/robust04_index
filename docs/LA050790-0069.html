May 7, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $60 Sunday on his $10 exacta box wager in the fifth when his three horses, Lans Manus, Belote and Seize The Chance, ran 2-3-9. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $604.

Sunday's Bankroll: $664

Sunday's Loss: $60

Current Bankroll: $604
