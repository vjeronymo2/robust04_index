940402 FT 02 APR 94 / Move for Sizewell public inquiry County councillors are set to force a public inquiry over plans for a Sizewell C nuclear power station in Suffolk. They are being advised by Mr Edwin Barritt, the county planning officer to lodge a formal objection to the Pounds 3.5bn development proposed by Nuclear Electric, the state-owned generator. An objection by either the county council or Suffolk Coastal District Council will force a public inquiry.
