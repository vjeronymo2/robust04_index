940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / International Company News: TelecomAsia loss TelecomAsia, Thailand's second-biggest company by market capitalisation, reported a net loss of Bt81.55m (Dollars 3.2m) in the first six months, compared with a profit of Bt521.18m a year ago, writes William Barnes in Bangkok. Costs have risen sharply as TelecomAsia moves to supply 2m new lines in Bangkok, the Thai capital.
