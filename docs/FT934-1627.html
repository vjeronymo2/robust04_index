931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Interest costs hit Stewart & Wight Stewart & Wight, the property investment company, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 182,813 to Pounds 170,064 in the six months to end-September. The company blamed an increase in finance charges, which rose from Pounds 1,791 to Pounds 6,846 and lower non-rental income of Pounds 5,608 (Pounds 9,539). Gross rental income was Pounds 201,528 (Pounds 203,429). Earnings per share came out at 145.29p, compared with 155.75p.
