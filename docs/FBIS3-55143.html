Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature by diplomatic correspondents Dmitriy Voskoboynikov, Igor Porshnev, Vitaliy Trubetskoy and others] [Text] Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has signed a decision which says that Russia will provide relief aid estimated at 100 Mn rubles for former Yugoslavia. Hard currency expenditures associated with the relief aid baby food and medicines are not specified. The aid will be handed over to Yugoslavia (Serbia and Montenegro), as well as to Bosnia-Herzegovina. This will be done to maintain the prestige of the Russian Federation and the international community's efforts in providing aid to the population of the former Yugoslavia
