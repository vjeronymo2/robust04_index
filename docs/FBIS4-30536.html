BFN [Text] Moscow, June 25 [dateline as received] (KCNA) -- A meeting for opening the month of international solidarity with the Korean people was held in Moscow on June 24 under the sponsorship of the Russian Association for Friendly and Cultural Cooperation With the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The meeting speeches was addressed by Vladimir Tolschikov, chairman of the Central Council of the Russian Association, and others. The speakers said that the war on June 25, 1950 was ignited by the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean authorities and that the Korean people honorably defended the freedom and independence of the country in the three-year war under the wise guidance of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Noting that Korea has become a country most civilized in the world and the Korean people have become a people most organized in the world, they stressed: The imperialists should know that threat or pressure will have no effect on Korea. The DPRK is confidently advancing along the road of socialism because it is guided by President Kim Il-song, hero of the present times, and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The meeting adopted a letter to President Kim Il-song.
