BFN [Text] The hierarchy of the Communist Party of the Philippines-New People's Army-National Democratic Front [CPP-NPA-NDF] clarified today that the group led by Felimon Lagman, alias Carlos Forte, and the Alex Boncayao Brigade [ABB] have been expelled from the party. According to a statement signed by Comrade Roger Rosal of the CPP-NPA-NDF Information Bureau, the Communist Party and the NDF have no knowledge of and are not responsible for the ABB impostor. Rosal emphasized that the Lagman group members are renegades and have been totally ousted from the revolutionary movement. [Begin Rosal recording in progress] ...the Lagman group and the impostor ABB are no longer part of the Communist Party of the Philippines and the New People's Army. The group headed by Felimon Lagman, alias Carlos Forte, and the militarist group posing as the Alex Boncayao Brigade that is continually using the name of the Communist Party of the Philippines and the New People's Army are all renegades. [end recording]
