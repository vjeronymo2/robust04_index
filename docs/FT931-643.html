930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / VAT on fuel 'will hit poor worst' THE IMPOSITION of VAT on domestic fuel, which was announced in the Budget, will have a disproportionately heavy impact on poorer people, according to a report by university academics. The Microsimulation Unit of the Department of Applied Economics at Cambridge University reports that VAT on fuel will bear more than seven times as hard on the incomes of the poorest 10 per cent of the population as on the richest 10 per cent.
