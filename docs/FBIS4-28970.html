BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam returned home today after attending the conference of foreign ministers of non-aligned countries held in Egypt. It was met at the airport by First Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Kang Sok-chu and other officials concerned and Egyptian Ambassador to Korea 'Ali Hijazi.
