BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 10 May (NA) -- Judge Leonidas Moldes, who is hearing the case of former Nazi Captain Erich Priebke, reported this morning that the latter will make a deposition before the San Carlos de Bariloche federal courts in the next few hours. Judge Moldes confirmed that the former Nazi officer was arrested at Bariloche Clinic, which is owned by Priebke, yesterday evening. He made it clear that "so far no formal extradition request" has been filed by the Italian courts.
