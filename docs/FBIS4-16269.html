BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Yefim Shvartsman] [Text] Odessa, 14 Apr -- A detachment of Black Sea Fleet ships, led by the large amphibious warfare ship Tsezar Kunikov, which was heading for Odessa today from Sevastopol, has turned back. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told this by telephone by Aleksandr Kluban, head of the press service of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry. According to him, the change of course occurred thanks to agreement between the leadership of the Ukrainian military department and the Black Sea Fleet command.
