Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, returned this afternoon to the capital, Sanaa, following an inspection visit to the governorates of Ta'izz and Ibb. During the visit, the chairman of the Presidential Council took note of citizens' affairs and of work at government facilities and agencies in the two governorates. He also met with the brother officials of executive agencies, their eminence the ulema, social figures, the leaders of political party and organization branches, and the leaders of mass, creative, and professional organizations. He discussed with them many national issues and developments, primarily the recent developments in our country. He also spoke to them, stressing the need to unify all efforts and energies in order to shoulder responsibilities and perform tasks so our country can overcome all difficulties and challenges and create good prospects for progress and prosperity. He stressed the need for making efforts to enhance national accord and cohesion of ranks and to advance the march of development to achieve our people's ambitions and hopes for progress and development. During the visit, the chairman of the Presidential Council was accompanied by several officials.
