921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Monks Inv Trust net assets rise The net asset value per share of Monks Investment Trust amounted to 430p at October 31 against 398.9p a year earlier and 409p at the April 30 year end. Net revenue for the six months fell to Pounds 3.34m, against Pounds 3.69m. Earnings came out at 4.3p (4.75p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 2p.
