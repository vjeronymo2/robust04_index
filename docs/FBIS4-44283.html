BFN [Article signed JWI: "No Belgian Military Personnel in Zaire Assures Brussels"] [Text] Brussels, 27 Jun (BELGA) -- No Belgian military personnel will be sent to Zaire as part of the logistical aid which Belgium is prepared to give to France for its Operation Turquoise in Rwanda, said concordant government sources on Monday [27 June]. On Monday afternoon, the defense minister did not rule out that "a few dozen men" who are part of a medical unit would be sent in a few days to western Rwanda. The minister had said in the morning that Paris had asked Belgium to give it logistical aid -- more specifically, a medical unit -- for its Operation Turquoise in Rwanda. Another government source, however, indicated to BELGA that it was unlikely that a single Belgian soldier would be sent to Africa. In this case, Belgian aid will be limited to providing medical aid, but without any human presence whatsoever.
