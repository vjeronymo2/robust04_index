940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / BA passenger's complaints probed Complaints against police at Heathrow airport by a passenger on a British Airways flight are to be passed to the Crown Prosecution Service and the Police Complaints Authority, Scotland Yard said last night. Mr John Gorman, a former detective, alleges that after making complaints to the airline he was subjected to harassment by BA staff and police at Heathrow. BA said it had taken Mr Gorman's complaints very seriously and had passed the results of its own investigation to police.
