BFN [From the press review] [Text] The current aggression by the al-Ahmar gang on the armed forces of unity and democracy has been dealt with by the local newspapers. SAWT AL-UMMAL newspaper in its issue affirmed that the criminal acts by the al-Ahmar military family culminated with the ignition of the civil war and the inter-Yemen war. The newspaper indicated that the ugliest images of envy had become evident in the hundreds of dead and thousands of injured. The newspaper said in this connection: We do not find words to express this criminal tragedy experienced by our Yemeni people today as the result of a sick president and a family which is mainly concerned to preserve its interests and continues to [word indistinct] the people's fortunes and wealth. The newspaper quoted the proverb which says: He who digs a hole for his brother falls in. The newspaper went on to say that the al-Ahmar ruling family has declared confrontation between it and the people. The people must therefore join their armed forces in order to decide the match and normalize the matter by restoring the authority to the people so that the people and their armed forces could stand against this crime, which is commanded by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, 'Ali Muhsin al- Ahmar and his brother, Muhammad 'Abdallah Salih and other highwaymen. SAWT AL-UMMAL said that the conspiracy against the people became complete with the ruling family's decision to declare a state of emergency. By this the family unmasked its ugly face, which is hostile to democracy, unity, and civil and human rights.
