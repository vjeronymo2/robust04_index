BFN [From the "24 Hours Newcast"] [Text] Andres Pastrana has a two-point lead over Ernesto Samper, according to a poll conducted by the National Consulting Center. The poll was contracted by the Bavaria company and released by the Caracol Network. The poll was conducted between 2-8 June in 106 cities and municipalities. Using a copy of the ballot, 10,360 persons were polled. The results were as follows: Andres Pastrana received 49 percent support of those intending to vote, and Ernesto Samper received 47 percent support of those intending to vote. The poll's margin of error is 1.2 percent.
