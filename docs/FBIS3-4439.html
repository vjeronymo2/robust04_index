Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tianjin, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Kim Yong-sam, president of the Republic of Korea (ROK), and his party left here for home at noon today at the end of their five-day official visit to China. They were seen off at the airport by Wu Jichuan, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of posts and telecommunications, and Zhang Lichang, mayor of Tianjin city. After their arrival from Beijing this morning, Kim and his party officials heard a briefing by Zhang on Tianjin's economic growth and the construction of a development zone in the city. Kim also inspected a ROK industrial park and the ROK-funded Sae-Han Precision Corporation Ltd. He described the park as "very useful" to ROK-Tianjin cooperation and to ROK-China cooperation at large. Kim told the hosts that following his visit, more ROK businessmen will invest in Tianjin.
