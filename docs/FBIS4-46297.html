BFN [Text] Yerevan ("LRAGIR," "RESPUBLICA ARMENIA" dailies), 06/03/94 -- A regular explosion blew in a carriage of the train heading to Armenia, between the Sadakhlo and Ayrum stations (on Georgian territory) at 10:30am yesterday morning. "LRAGIR" reports, the bomb was mounted in one of the freight carriages of the train. The explosion damaged the carriage, loaded with mazut (heating oil 1-4), but fire has been prevented. According to "RESPUBLICA ARMENIA," 60 tons of fuel spilled out and cleaning operations are currently carried out at the railroad to restore movement of trains. Spokesman of RA [Republic of Armenia] Special Affairs State Department A.Melkoumian informed "LRAGIR" that the railway wasn't damaged and movement of trains continues.
