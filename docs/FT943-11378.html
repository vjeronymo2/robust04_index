940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Forminster Forminster, the clothing manufacturer, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.2m to Pounds 2.4m for the year to April 30, on turnover up from Pounds 23.8m to Pounds 25.6m. The company is seeking to increase sales by 'organic growth, or acquisition, whenever there is an appropriate opportunity', said Mr Ronald Gulliver, chairman. Earnings per share were 60.5p (53.79p). A final dividend of 11.35p is recommended, to give a total of 15.98p (14.08p).
