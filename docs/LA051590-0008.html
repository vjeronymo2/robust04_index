May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MAYOR BRADLEY'S PROPOSAL FOR MANDATORY WATER RATIONING

Hey, lots of us don't want to take shorter showers! It's time for Mayor Bradley and members of the City Council to get more anti-growth. I don't see our city officials calling on our federal officials like Rep. Henry Waxman (D-Los Angeles) and company to do something about curbing population growth in already crowded Southern California.

Instead of remaining passive to the issue of controlling our borders against illegal immigration and passing more laws increasing legal immigration as well, our members of Congress should be fighting for vastly increased family-planning assistance to the teeming and disadvantaged peoples of the Third World.

HARVEY PEARSON

Los Angeles
