941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: RJB projections in support of bid These are among the figures being issued by RJB Mining in City presentations to support its Pounds 914m bid for the three English regions of British Coal, writes Michael Smith. The company has been chosen as preferred bidder by the government which wants to hand over the assets, including 15 deep mines, on December 24.
