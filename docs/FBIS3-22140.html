Language: English Article Type:BFN [UK press release from the Prime Minister's Office: "Taking Rio Forward - UK Panel On Sustainable Development"] [Text] The Prime Minister, the Rt Hon [Right Honorable] John Major MP, today announced the appointment of a new independent panel to advise Government on the implementation of "Sustainable Development: The UK Strategy." The Panel is to be convened by Sir Crispin Tickell. The other members are Lord Selborne, Lord Alexander of Weedon, Sir John Houghton and Dr Anne McLaren. It will give independent advice to the Government and will be consulted on issues of major strategic importance for sustainable development. The Prime Minister has given the Panel a specific charge: to keep in view general sustainability issues at home and abroad, to identify major problems or opportunities likely to arise, to monitor progress and to consider questions of priority.
