Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by L. Elias] [Text] Mmabatho March 11 SAPA -- The Bophuthatswana government was on Friday [11 March] morning meeting at an undisclosed location. Calls to the government information service were met with this recorded message: "This is the information service of the Bophuthatswana government. Our office has temporarily moved to where the Bophuthatswana government is meeting. Please leave your name and number and we will get back to you. If you want to send a facsimile message, please don't speak but wait for the signal."
