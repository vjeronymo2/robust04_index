920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / Iraq assails Kurdish election Iraq yesterday denounced as illegal next Sunday's elections in rebel-held Kurdistan but ruled out the use of force to derail them, Reuter reports from Baghdad. Mr Mehdi Saleh, speaker of the National Assembly, answered 'I do not think so' when asked by Reuters whether Iraq intended to disrupt the elections by military or other means. 'For the simple reason, we are in dialogue with the Kurdistan Front. True, this dialogue has stopped but it is not severed.'
