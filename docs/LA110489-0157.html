November 4, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: HOTELS/TOURISM

Pan Pacific Offices: Pan Pacific Hotels and Resorts has opened its corporate offices in the 21-story Center Tower office building in Costa Mesa.

Pan Pacific Hotels and Resorts was formed in April by a consolidation of Tokyo-based Pan Pacific Hotels and Hawaii-based Emerald Hotels, both subsidiary management firms of parent company Tokyu Corp.

Pan Pacific includes 17 hotels worldwide, including the 505-room Pan Pacific Hotel in Anaheim.
