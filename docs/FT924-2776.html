921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Winter 1992 (24): Sources of business data ON-LINE PRODUCT PROFILE BT Business Information Supplier: British Telecom. Product: BT Business Information services are aimed at commercial customers. Databases cover company financial information, news, market research, real-time share prices, directories and travel. Services are accessed through Telecom Gold. Information sources include Reuters, Infocheck, Kompass, FT Profile, Jordans and several others. Pricing: Pounds 40 registration fee, Subscription: Pounds 5.50 a month. Standard rate connect charge: 7p a minute. Cheap rate connect charge: 2.15p a minute. Hardware requirements and operating system: PC, modem, communications software. Other services available: Training through Task Force Pro Libra: Pounds 100 a delegate. Contact: BT Customer Services Unit, Network House - PP205, Brindley Way, Hemel Hempstead, HP3 9RR. Tel: 0800-200-700. PRODUCT PROFILE FT on CD-ROM Supplier: Chadwyck-Healey is a private limited company founded in the UK in 1973 Turnover: approximately Pounds 6m Employees: 60 in UK Product: Financial Times on CD-ROM. The Financial Times, with specified exclusions, on CD-ROM is published by FT PROFILE and distributed by Chadwyck-Healey. Current exclusions are the tabular sections, advertisements, television and radio listings, weather information and statistics, sports statistics, graphics and pictures. Some graphics and tabular information are expected to be included in future issues. A CD-ROM in ISO 9660 format is available for each year from 1990 onwards. Earlier years are planned. Quarterly, fully cumulative update discs are published. Pricing: Pounds 500 per archival disc; Pounds 900 for current year subscription. Special prices for multiple years Special discounts of up to 50 per cent for multiple copies. Additional charges for networked use Educational discounts. Other products distributed by Chadwyck-Healey on CD-ROM include the entire texts of the Economist from January 1987, and the entire text of the Independent from October 1988, and the 1981 Census. Prices on application. Financial Card Services. (Data from published annual reports. Updated twice monthly.) CD/Corporate: European M&A - in depth deal information on more than 30,000 UK and European mergers and acquisitions. (Data from IDD Information Services Inc. Updated monthly.) CD/Europa - 60,000 companies across 19 European countries. (Data from two Dun & Bradstreet databases. Includes business description, officers, three years of financial information.) Users/sites worldwide: more than 12,000. Pricing: Products are sold on annual subscription starting at Pounds 9,000. Hardware/operating system: PC-DOS Sales and support: provided direct, plus installation and help desk. Other services available: CD/Corporate: US Public Companies has historical financial information On line links to Dow Jones News Retrieval and into ReportLine. Source of data is transparent to the user. Contact: Lotus Information Services Division, Lotus Development, Lotus Park, The Causeway, Staines, Middlesex TW18 3AG. Tel: 0784 455445. Fax: 0784 469344. PRODUCT PROFILE Perfect Information Perfect Information was founded in 1990 in the UK, and is the subject of a management buyout. Employees: 16 in UK. Product: Perfect Information provides on-line access to document images through tied telephone lines. Changes are afoot to allow access over dial-up or ISDN telephone lines to PCs, Unix and Macintosh systems and company networks. Documents include those produced by UK listed companies - reports and accounts, issue documents, offer announcements etc. All major national newspapers except the Financial Times are provided. Any information with a financial connotation, including stories about individuals, are stored and indexed under approximately 25 different categories. Users: 20 clients/sites worldwide. Pricing: Circulars Pounds 18 a document. Reports and accounts Pounds 18 a document. Announcements Pounds 3 a page. Press Pounds 1.50 per item Foreign company report and accounts Pounds 30 a document. Connection charge: Pounds 1.50 a minute. Subscriptions of Pounds 12,000 to Pounds 60,000 qualify subscribers for lower rates on each service. Hardware/operating system: Digital Equipment VAX 3100, Talaris laser printer, software and maintenance are provided. Sales and support: Sold direct, with help desk. Training chargeable. Contact: 35 Chiswell Street, London EC1Y 4SE. Tel 071 454 0666. Fax 071 454 0668. NEW PRODUCT Companyline Supplier: MAID claims that its merging technology Infosort has made it the largest single source of market research information (Researchline), new information (Newsline), and now company information with its new Companyline service. Employees in UK: 52 Product name: Companyline, launched 4 December 1992, and available from 1st January 1993, is a new service tailored for managers, which brings together information from six existing providers: Dun and Bradstreet, Disclosure, Moody's Extel Financial, ICC and Hemmington Scott. The details from 4.5m companies from 40 countries are presented on screen in an easy-to-access form, as though they came from one single source. Pricing: Either by subscription at Pounds 125 signup (Newsline,Companyline), with 4p per line on access, or Pounds 5950 pa for comprehensive package of all three services including Researchline's 60,000 market reports. Hardware requirements and operating system: PC and modem Sales and support: Direct from MAID Other services available: Training, helpline Contact: Richard Webster, MAID 18 Dufferin St LONDON EC1Y 8PD
