920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Dart in cash acquisition Dart Group has acquired Bourne Aviation Supply, a specialist in the source and supply of modern aircraft parts and engine parts - particularly for the Airbus and Boeing ranges. Total maximum consideration will be Pounds 950,000 cash with a further Pounds 850,000 payable dependent on profits over the next three years. To achieve maximum earn-out Bourne must return aggregate pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.32m prior to July 31 1995. For the 12 months to end-March 1992 Bourne achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 65,000. Net assets at the year-end amounted to Pounds 86,000.
