BFN [Text] Stepanakert, April 13 (SNARK)--In connection with latest bombardments of the Karabakh towns and villages the Government of Nagorno-Karabakh made today an official statement. The statement says that with the connivance of the world community the Azerbaijani authorities go on with the war against the civil population of Nagorno-Karabakh. This morning, the statement mentions, the Azerbaijani war planes again and again bombed the capital of Nagorno-Karabakh - Stepanakert. As a result of bombardment there are victims, the houses were ruined. The war planes also bombed the other settlements of Nagorno-Karabakh. Victims also reported. Azerbaijani military aircraft is going on with barbarous bombardments. Meanwhile the Azerbaijani leader Geydar Aliyev makes cynic appeals to the Armenian population of Nagorno-Karabakh to stop the bloodshed assuring the security for the Karabakh Armenians. Twice in a period of three days the Azerbaijani war planes bombed the capital of Nagorno-Karabakh -- the Azerbaijani authorities showed the real truth of their false statements. The document the Karabakh Government issued states the Karabakh authorities made the Karabakh troops undertake all the measures to defend the civil population. Besides, the Karabakh side reserves the right to retaliate. The document says the Azerbaijani side is responsible for the escalation of the tension in the region.
