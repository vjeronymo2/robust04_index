BFN [Text] On 12 April at the Small Town Square, the meeting hall of the Czech Republic's chamber of commerce, the Heilongjiang people's government delegation held a news briefing to introduce to the people of all circles in Prague the situation of Heilongjiang as well as the fifth Harbin trade symposium that will soon be held. Wang Zongzhang, vice governor of the provincial government and head of the delegation, introduced Heilongjiang and the Harbin trade symposium and invited the businessmen and the personages enterprise circles to participate in the Harbin trade symposium which will be held this June. He ensured that the symposium would provide quality service for the guests from Czech and offer the greatest convenience for trade talks and cooperation.
