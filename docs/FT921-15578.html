920109 FT 09 JAN 92 / Haitian accord PRESIDENT Jean-Bertrand Aristide and a delegation of Haitian political leaders have agreed on a candidate for prime minister, Reuter reports from Caracas. This may well pave the way for Father Aristide's return from exile to power, an Organisation of American States negotiator said yesterday. After two days of talks in Caracas, the president has said he would accept Mr Rene Theodore, Communist Party leader, as prime minister.
