Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, Dec 20 (AFP) -- Ukraine is to dismantle almost half of the 46 SS-24 nuclear missiles in its nuclear arsenal inherited from the former Soviet Union, Deputy Prime Minister Valery Shmarov said Monday. He said Ukraine would dismantle 20 of the 46 strategic missiles by the end of this month. The decision comes only days after last week's meeting of top Ukrainian, Russian and U.S. officials. Until now Ukraine has refused to give up the weapons without security guarantees from the United States.
