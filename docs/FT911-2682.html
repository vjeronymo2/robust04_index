910429 FT 29 APR 91 / FMLN signs accord with Salvadoran government Ana Guadalupe Martinez and other FMLN guerrilla leaders sign an agreement with the El Salvador government in Mexico City at the weekend on constitutional reforms - but this did not involve a ceasefire. Also in Mexico, the first stage of peace talks between the Guatemalan government and left-wing rebels ended with an agreement on ground rules for further discussions.
