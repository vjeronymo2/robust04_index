Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl and Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel have noted the massacre of Palestinians yesterday in Hebron with "devastation and disgust." Today in Bonn, government spokesman Dieter Vogel said that the Federal Government conveyed its deepest sympathy to the relatives of the victims. It expressed the hope that the act of violence would not jeopardize the peace process between Israel and the PLO. All those responsible were called upon to prevent the violence from escalating.
