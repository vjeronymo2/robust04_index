Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 13 Mar (MENA)--PLO Political Department Chief Faruq Qaddumi has said that it is impossible for the Palestinians to return to the peace negotiations without a Security Council resolution providing for international protection for the Palestinian people in the occupied territories and without tangible, concrete measures on the ground to reassure the Palestinian people. He told the Tunisian newspaper AL-SABAH today that the United States remains an obstacle in the way of such a resolution. Washington is asking the PLO to resume negotiations with Israel, although it knows that this is impossible without a Security Council resolution. Qaddumi accused the current team at the U.S. State Department of being biased and sympathetic toward Israel and trying to extort the Palestinian people, as he put it.
