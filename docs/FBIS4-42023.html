BFN [Text] Leonid Kuchma, a Ukrainian presidential candidate, filed a protest with the Ukrainian Central Electoral Commission and the Supreme Soviet Presidium, saying that the state-controlled media gave access only to incumbent President Leonid Kuchma. [as received] In particular, he wrote that the Ukrainian television promoted Leonid Kravchuk even on election day, 26 June, in flagrant violation of the law. Kuchma won nearly 32 percent and Kravchuk nearly 38 percent in the first round of presidential elections.
