Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Bolivian Foreign Minister Antonio Aranibar pointed out yesterday in the capital of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay that Bolivia is willing to gradually -- and with increased strength -- approach the member countries of the Common Market of the South, Mercosur, made up of Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Uruguay. Aranibar is in Montevideo, the capital of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay, where he is participating in a Latin American Integration Association, Aladi, meeting. There are problems regarding the participation of Mexico -- which has already signed an agreement with Canada and the United States -- as far as the free trade agreement is concerned.
