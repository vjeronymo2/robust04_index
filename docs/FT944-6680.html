941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Japan seeks renewed oil rights - Contracts Japan has asked Kuwait to renew promptly Arabian Oil's drilling rights in oilfields off the Gulf emirate. Mr Ryutaro Hashimoto, minister of international trade and industry asked Mr Abdulmohsen al-Mudej, the visiting Kuwaiti oil minister, for an early decision to extend the existing contract, due to expire in 2003. Arabian Oil is the only Japanese operator of oilfields in the Middle East, from which Japan buys the bulk of its oil needs.
