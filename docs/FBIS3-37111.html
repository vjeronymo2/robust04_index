Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Adullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku February 14 TASS -- Contrary to assurances about readiness to stop hostilities for the period of the 1994 Winter Olympics, Armenian armed units operating in the conflict zone intensified shelling today, as well as attacks on positions of the Azeri Army at the Kelbadzhar, Terter and Fizuli sections. A report of the republican Defence Ministry, circulated today immediately after 12.00, says that the attacks were repulsed, and the enemy sustained heavy losses in manpower and materiel. The Defence Ministry reported the activisation of Armenian sharp-shooters on the frontline. They fired at a filming group of the Azeri TV, injuring Adil Nasirov, a TV journalist, and destroying video cameras.
