Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report by Liu Zhenyan] [Text] Parliament President Marut Bunnak held a luncheon for Taiwan President Li Teng-hui at the Central Plaza Hotel yesterday. During the luncheon, Li Teng-hui praised Thailand's high economic growth. He said that Thailand is admired for being able to maintain this despite the stagnation of the global economy. Li Teng-hui said that both Taiwan and Thailand advocate democracy and freedom. He expressed hope that Taiwan and Thailand will enhance cooperation in economy, trade, investment, industrial technology, finance, and training on the basis of mutual benefit. Marut led a Thai parliamentary delegation to visit Taiwan in the middle of January. Others accompanying Marut at the luncheon included Chawarin Latthasaksiri -- Cai Junrong -- Chat Thai Party MP for Rat Buri Province who has visited Taiwan several times; Charoen Khanthawong, Democrat Party MP for Bangkok; Parliament Secretary Phasit Phiphatthanakun; and Zheng Yousu [Thai name: Suthikiat Chirathiwat] of the Central Plaza Hotel.
