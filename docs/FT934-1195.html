931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Smithfield rent decision upheld The High Court yesterday upheld the decision by the Corporation of London to raise the rents of Smithfield traders to pay for refurbishment. Traders are considering an appeal. They are also lodging an objection to a returning officer's ruling in elections for the corporation's ruling council earlier this month, in which two traders otherwise guaranteed to be elected were barred on technical grounds.
