Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] ANC [African National Congress] leader Nelson Mandela and State President F. W. de Klerk have signed an agreement marking the restoration of legitimate local government. After addressing the local government summit at the World Trade Center outside Johannesburg, the two leaders and the 2,200 delegates were presented with an agreement on finance, services, and service rendering. The agreement recognizes the recently promulgated Local Government Transitional Act and commits signatories to promote and expedite the normalization of local government. It says the summit marks the start of restoring legitimacy and credibility to local government finance, services, and service rendering in particular. Meanwhile, earlier in the day Mandela called for an end to the rent and services boycott.
