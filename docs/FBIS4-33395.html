BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] An Amnesty International delegation on Friday expressed concern about the deterioration of human rights in Venezuela, particularly about the questionable activities of security forces on the border with Colombia. The delegation led by Mexican Javier Zuniga released the results of an extensive investigation of (?prisons), places of detention, and torture in general, where flaws in the judicial and public security system were evident. The mission also issued an alert about the aggressive actions of the Metropolitan Police in the application of the vagrants law and about the government's proposal to permit citizens to be armed for their defense in view of the increasing crime rate.
