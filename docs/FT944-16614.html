941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / International Company News: Bajaj Auto up sharply Bajaj Auto, India leading maker of motorcycles, scooters and three-wheeler auto-rickshaws, reported a 196 per cent rise in net profit to Rs1.36bn (Dollars 43m) on a 48 per cent increase in sales to Rs10.32bn during the six months to end-September, writes Stefan Wagstyl in New Delhi. Bajaj, which is also considering plans to build low-cost cars in high volumes in collaboration with a foreign partner, is due to launch a Dollars 167m euroequity issue later this month.
