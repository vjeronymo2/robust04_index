920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Company News: Net profit improves 77% at HK group FIRST Pacific Company, the Hong Kong-based marketing to telecommunications conglomerate, lifted net profit before extraordinary items by 77 per cent to HKDollars 158m (USDollars 20.5m) for the six months to June, writes Simon Davies. The company benefited from a turnround in its loss- making Australian computer software company Tech Pacific and its Philippines unit Metro Drug. First Pacific owns 62 per cent of Amsterdam-listed trading company Hagermeyer, which posted a 25 per cent rise in first-half earnings.
