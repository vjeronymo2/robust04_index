BFN [Text] Beijing, 19 April (XINHUA) -- In accordance with the decision made by the National People's Congress Standing Committee, President Jiang Zemin of the People's Republic of China appointed and removed the following ambassadors: Removing Hu Benyao as the People's Republic of China's ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to the Republic of Austria. Appointing Wang Yanyi as the People's Republic of China's ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to the Republic of Austria.
