930402 FT 02 APR 93 / German output shows fall Industrial output in west Germany fell 1 per cent in February after a modest improvement in January, according to official figures published yesterday, writes Ariane Genillard in Bonn. The sharpest fall was recorded in consumer products where output was 2.5 per cent lower than in January. Output in the west German construction industry, which until last month showed growth, fell by 2 per cent.
