930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / Tandem to open plant in Stirling TANDEM COMPUTERS, the US computer company that makes systems for large users such as banks, government departments and manufacturers, is to set up a manufacturing operation for its products at Stirling in Scotland. The 134,000 sq ft plant will assemble and test its NonStop computer systems. The operation will employ 100 people within two years. Tandem has annual sales of Dollars 650m (Pounds 433.3m) in Europe, accounting for 31 per cent of total turnover.
