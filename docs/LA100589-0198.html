October 5, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

DANA POINT ACTOR JAILED FOR DRUGS

Television actor Lance Kerwin was arrested Wednesday on suspicion of drug possession as he left a motel room near Candlestick Park, police said.

Kerwin, 28, of Dana Point, was taken into custody at 5:15 a.m., after arresting officers said he suddenly blurted: "I'm sorry . . . we've been smoking crack all night, and it's all my fault. I just had a big fight with my old lady and need to forget her."
