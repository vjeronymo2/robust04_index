920428 FT 28 APR 92 / London investors guide launched LONDON'S chamber of commerce is this week launching what it claims is the first recent comprehensive guide aimed at potential foreign investors in the capital. The guide is a directory and promotional effort, necessitated, according to the chamber, by the absence of a capital-wide local authority. It surveys property, financial and labour markets and offers advice on locations, services, transport and local government. Ms Jacqui Ginnane, the chamber's chief economist, said: 'There has been a steady decline in investment in London since the late 1980s and it is vital that someone puts the case for London.'
