BFN [Text] The parliament and the Popular Council (the supreme representative body) of Turkmenistan held a joint meeting in Ashkhabad [Ashgabat] on 13 May in which Saparmurad Niyazov made public his program of developing the Republic's economy in 1994-96 and for the period until 2002. According to the program, the priority tasks are the economic idependence of the state, in the first place, in the food sphere, the restructuring of the economy, the stabilization and strengthening of the national currency -- the manat, further development of market relations and entrepreneurship. In particular, it is planeed to increase the GDP up to 50 billion manats within the next three years -- by 45.3-percent more than in 1993. Oil extraction is to be raised up to 9 million tonnes also in three year's time, gas -- to 90 billion cubic meters over the same period.
