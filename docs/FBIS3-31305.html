Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 15 February Admiral Khristos Limberis, chief of the Greek Army General Staff, who is visiting Bulgaria, consecutively met and talked with his counterpart, Army General Lyuben Petrov, chief of the Bulgarian Army General Staff, and Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov. Issues of mutual interest and certain aspects of the situation in the Balkans were discussed. The two sides noted agreement on their positions on the situation in the Balkans. Later the guest met Nikolay Slatinski, chairman of the National Assembly Commission on National Security.
