April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Walters to Interview Chinese Leader: Barbara Walters has been granted an interview with Chinese leader Jiang Zemin for ABC's "20/20." Jiang Zemin, the Communist Party general secretary, is said to be the first of the Chinese leaders to give a TV interview in over a year. The interview will air June 1. BETH KLEID
