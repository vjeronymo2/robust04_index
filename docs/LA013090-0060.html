January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Helmut Hoeflehner of Austria scored his second World Cup downhill victory in three days, and Steve Locher of Switzerland won his first World Cup race in the super-G at Val d'Isere, France.

Hoeflehner scored his ninth World Cup downhill victory in 1:58.09, and Franz Heinz 1:59.24.

Locher won by only 0.03 of second over Frenchman Armand Schiele, who was clocked in 1:32.16.
