BFN [Text] Aden, 4 Apr (WAKH) --'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, returned to Aden this afternoon at the conclusion of visits to the United Arab Emirates [UAE] and the Sultanate of Oman.
