920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / International Company News: Chevron revives Pennzoil lawsuit A US Federal appeals court yesterday revived a lawsuit by Chevron against Pennzoil charging that Pennzoil illegally masked its intent to gain partial control over Chevron in a 1989 stock purchase, Reuter reports from San Francisco. The court ruled that Chevron had a right to a trial on its claim that Pennzoil lied to the Securities and Exchange Commission when it bought more than Dollars 2bn in Chevron stock. A lower court judge dismissed the case in 1990.
