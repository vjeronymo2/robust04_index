Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chechen leaders are planning to organize neighborhood watch teams and mobile militia groups to maintain order around the clock. According to Interfax sources, such posts will be set up along Groznyy's perimeter and on the approaches to the city. This question was discussed during talks between President Dzhokhar Dudayev and representatives of law-enforcement bodies. The talks also resulted in the decision to establish headquarters for coordinating the newly set-up detachments. The headquarters' work will be controlled by the president, who is also chairman of the Chechen Security Council.
