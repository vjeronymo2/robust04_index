BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 7 (KCNA) -- Russian Ambassador to Korea Yuriy Fadeyev hosted a reception at his embassy Friday evening on the founding anniversary of the Russian Armed Forces. Invited there were Vice Marshal Kim Kwang-chin, Lieutenant General Pak Song-won, and other generals and officers of the Korean People's Army and officials concerned. Present on invitation were military attaches of various embassies in Pyongyang. Speeches were exchanged at the reception.
