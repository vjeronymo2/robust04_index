940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Welsh Industrial net assets up 31% Welsh Industrial Investment Trust lifted net asset value per share by 31 per cent, from 156p to 203.6p, during the year to April 5. Net profits dropped from Pounds 86,727 to Pounds 53,183. Mr Alfred Stirling, chairman, said this reflected the continued trend to lower interest rates and low yields on the trust's selected growth investments. Earnings per share dropped from 6.13p to 3.65p; a maintained single final dividend of 4p is recommended.
