Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Washington, March 3 (YONHAP) -- Ho Chong, the chief North Korean representative at working-level contacts with the United States, will return to Pyongyang this weekend, officials at the North Korean mission to the United Nations said on Friday. Ho has been working as Pyongyang's deputy chief of mission to the world body. A North Korean official disclosed the scheduled return of Ho, saying "he will return to Pyongyang this coming weekend, if there is no problem in reserving an airplane seat." Meanwhile, a source in Washington said Ho may be promoted to an assistant minister-level post for U.S. affairs due to his leading role in Pyongyang-Washington working-level contacts.
