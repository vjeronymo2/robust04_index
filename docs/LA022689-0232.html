February 26, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

The premier of British Columbia, William N. Vander Zalm, will speak Thursday at the opening of the British Columbia Trade and Tourism Office in Irvine, the first foreign trade office in Orange County. Also attending will be John Jansen, the province's minister of international business and immigration, and Bill Reid, minister of tourism and provincial secretary.

California is British Columbia's third largest trading partner, trailing Japan and the Pacific Northwest. The office is being opened to further encourage collaboration between British Columbia and California in the form of increased trade, investment and tourism, officials said.
