920104 FT 04 JAN 92 / Spanish miners begin strike The northern Asturias coal-mining region ground to a virtual halt yesterday as 25,000 Spanish miners and their supporters heeded a general strike to protest at job cuts, AP reports from Oviedo. Unions are protesting at a plan by state-owned Hunosa to cut 6,000 mining jobs by 1995. Hunosa is owned by the state holding company INI.
