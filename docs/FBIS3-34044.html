Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 2 Feb (INA)--The al-Rafidayn Bank in Baghdad has announced it will open special sections to sell and purchase foreign currency in accordance with market rates specified on a daily basis. An authorized source at the bank told the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY that the Central Bank has authorized four branches of the al-Rafidayn Bank in Baghdad and its branches in the al-Basrah and Ninawa Governorates to sell and purchase foreign currency as of 5 February.
