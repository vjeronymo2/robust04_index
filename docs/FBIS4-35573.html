BFN [Text] An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] inquiry has concluded that reserves soldier Shlomo Shitrit, who was killed on the edge of the Gaza Strip earlier this week, was accidentally shot by another soldier. Qol Yisra'el's military reporter Karmela Menashe says that in contrast to initial reports, soldiers did not come under terrorist fire and it transpires there were irregularities in the way the troops acted. Reserves soldiers serving on the border of the Gaza Strip met with the head of Army Southern Command, Major General Matan Vilna'i, this week and complained of uncertainty about the new situation and lack of equipment.
