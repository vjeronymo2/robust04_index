930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: WSG Reduced pre-tax losses were announced by Westminster Scaffolding Group, the USM-quoted scaffolding and property group, for the year to October 31. On lower turnover of Pounds 6.18m (Pounds 8.22m) the pre-tax loss amounted to Pounds 2.19m (Pounds 3.41m) after an exceptional charge of Pounds 143,000 (Pounds 2.03m). Losses worked through at 7.6p (18.3p).
