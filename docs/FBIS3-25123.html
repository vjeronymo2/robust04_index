Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] As part of his continuing private visit to South Africa, Afonso Dhlakama, Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] leader, is to hold meetings today with South African President Frederik de Klerk and with Thabo Mbeki, a senior African National Congress, ANC, official. The Renamo leader will also be meeting officials of the ruling National Party and representatives of the Portuguese community in South Africa. A Renamo source informed Portugal's LUSA news agency that Afonso Dhlakama went to that country to gather political and financial support for Renamo's upcoming electoral campaign.
