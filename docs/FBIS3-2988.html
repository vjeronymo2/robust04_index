Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (XINHUA) -- Vice Premier Li Lanqing was interviewed here today by James Gaines, managing editor of the U.S. news weekly TIME magazine, and Karsten Prager, managing editor of the magazine's international edition. Li answered questions ranging from Sino-U.S. relations, regional affairs, and China's current economic development and reform and opening policies, to its economic and trade ties with other countries.
