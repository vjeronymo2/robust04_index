BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Almaty 17 May TASS -- President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan has highly praised the documents on cooperation, signed here today by the special services of Russia and Kazakhstan. "I do not doubt," he said, receiving director of the Russian Federal Intelligence Service Sergey Stepashin at his residence here, "that the developing cooperation between the security services of the two countries, which is steadily growing stronger, will further strengthen friendship and good-neighbourly relations between Russia and Kazakhstan, will set a good example for close partnership between the industrialists, financiers, and businessmen of the two countries."
