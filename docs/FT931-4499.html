930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News in Brief CANNON STREET Investments has sold Private Mobile Radio to the Securicor Group for Pounds 510,000 cash. In addition, CSI received some Pounds 268,000 being the net amount of all intra receivables between PMR and CSI group companies. Securicor will guarantee PMR's borrowings which stand at Pounds 480,000. CSI has also sold Davron Engineers Sales to a group, including its management, for Pounds 1.
