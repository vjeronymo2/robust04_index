January 8, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

RYAN NAMED SOUTH TEAM COACH

Philadelphia Eagles Coach Buddy Ryan was named today as coach of the South team in the Jan. 20 Senior Bowl all-star football game at Mobile.

Eric Tillman, Senior Bowl executive director, said Ryan and the rest of the Eagles coaching staff became one of the staffs selected to coach the game based on a working agreement between the Senior Bowl and the NFL.

The North coach will be named later.

The annual contest features top college prospects for the professional draft.
