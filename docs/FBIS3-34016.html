Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih has again expressed Sudan's keenness and desire for the efforts of the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development [IGADD] states initiative to produce the expected results in achieving peace. This was stated during separate meetings with the foreign ministers of Kenya and Uganda on the sidelines of the meetings of the ministerial council of the OAU in Addis Ababa. Dr. Abu-Salih explained that during his meetings with the two ministers, he declared Sudan's interest in working to speed up steps to reach a comprehensive and acceptable solution that guarantees complete stability in the country. He said such a solution should end the dispute and its consequences. He added that the two ministers expressed their understanding of the Sudanese Government's stand and its seriousness in striving to find a peaceful solution to the dispute in the south. They also expressed their appreciation for the praise for the efforts of the IGADD states in this respect.
