January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Dayton Hudson Corp. of Minneapolis announced that Marvin Goldstein has been named chairman and chief executive of its Dayton Hudson Department Store Co. Goldstein has been president of DHDSC, overseeing all merchandising and stores functions. Goldstein, 46, started his retailing career in 1966 as a management trainee at Dayton's.
