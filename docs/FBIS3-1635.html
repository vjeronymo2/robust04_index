Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 26 (XINHUA) -- A container ship left Shanghai port in east China this afternoon for Pusan port in the Republic of Korea (ROK). This is the first ship route from Shanghai to ROK. Starting today, each week a container ship will be dispatched to Pusan port. The new route is managed by the Shanghai Jinjiang Shipping Corp. Ltd., a ten-year-old venture which also has ships going to Hong Kong and ports of Southeast Asia.
