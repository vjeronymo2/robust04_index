BFN [REport by Vadim Khusainov under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "U.S. Consulate Opens in Yekaterinburg"] [Text] The official opening ceremony of the U.S. Consulate General in Yekaterinburg has taken place. Jack Siegel has been appointed consul in the Urals. U.S. Secretary of Commerce Ronald Brown; Thomas Pickering, U.S. ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Russia; leaders of state departments; and around 30 U.S. businessmen came to Yekaterinburg for the opening ceremony. In an interview the consul general gave to ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, Siegel said that for the time being only three of the consulate's departments will be working in the Urals: those for business, politics, and culture.
