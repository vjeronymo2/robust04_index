17. Consolidated Edison Company of New York, Inc. [Docket No. ER94&hyph;1218&hyph;000] Take notice that on May 2, 1994, Consolidated Edison Company of New York, Inc. (Con Edison) tendered for filing a Supplement to its Rate Schedule FERC No. 105, an agreement to provide transmission service for Orange and Rockland Utilities, Inc. (O&R). The Supplement provides for an increase in the monthly transmission charge from $0.87 to $1.03 per kilowatt per month thus increasing annual revenues under the Rate Schedule by a total of $192,000. Con Edison requests that this increase take effect on July 1, 1994. Con Edison states that a copy of this filing has been served by mail upon O&R. Consolidated Edison Company of New York, Inc. [Docket No. ER94&hyph;1220&hyph;000] Take notice that on May 2, 1994, Consolidated Edison Company of New York, Inc. (Con Edison) tendered for filing a Supplement to its Rate Schedule FERC No. 66 an agreement to provide transmission service for the Power Authority of the State of New York (the Authority). The Supplement provides for an increase in the monthly transmission charge from $1.06 to $1.12 per kilowatt per month for transmission of power and energy sold by the Authority to Grumman Corporation, thus increasing annual revenues under the Rate Schedule by a total of $7,595.28 requested that the increase take effect on July 1, 1994. Con Edison states that a copy of this filing has been served by mail upon the Authority. Comment date: May 19, 1994, in accordance with Standard Paragraph E at the end of this notice.
