BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Nikolay Geronin] [Text] Moscow April 22 TASS -- A high-ranking Russian diplomat praised Japanese Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata, who has been nominated for the post of the prime minister, for promoting normal relations with Russia. "The Russian side considers that relations with Japan are developing normally, at a good speed. This is a big merit of Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata", Director of the Asian Department of the Russian Foreign Ministry Nikolay Solovev told TASS on Friday. He added that Hata had supported Russian reforms during his recent visit to Moscow. Our two countries, he said, should proceed in developing relations which will give a possibility of solving problems on which it is hard to reach agreements so far. A new Japanese prime minister is expected to be elected by parliament on Monday. The ruling coalition which nominated Hata has a majority of 265 out of 511 seats in the House of Representatives.
