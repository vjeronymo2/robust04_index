Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Tajikistan, Imamali Rakhmanov, has expressed his gratitude to the people and the governments of the CIS, for helping restore the republic's economy, destroyed by the civil war. In an interview for Russia's newsmen, he said the leaders of the republic are seeking peace and accord between the different political forces, but national revival is hampered by the armed opposition, which continues attempts from neighboring Afghanistan to infiltrate deep inside the republic. And in that connection, Imamali Rakhmanov assessed highly the actions of the CIS collective peacekeeping forces stationed in Tajikistan. They play an important role in stabilizing the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border.
