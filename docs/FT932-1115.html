930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Army 'open' to Khmer Rouge A general in Cambodia's army, which has been battling the Khmer Rouge since 1979, has said the guerrillas may join the newly unified armed forces, Reuter reports from Phnom Penh. 'We leave the door open for any factions who have not joined us,' a United Nations official yesterday quoted General Pol Sarin, chief of general staff, as saying.
