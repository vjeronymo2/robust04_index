BFN [Text] Baghdad, 24 Apr (INA) -- Colonel Mustafa al-Kharrubi, personal envoy to his Excellency President Saddam Husayn from his brother Libyan President Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, left Baghdad this afternoon. In a statement to the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY, Col. Kharrubi said he had delivered a letter to President Saddam Husayn from his brother Libyan President Col. Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi dealing with relations between the two fraternal countries, as well as issues of common interest.
