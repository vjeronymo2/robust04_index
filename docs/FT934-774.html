931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / Menem bid overcomes obstacle President Carlos Menem's re-election bid overcame a big obstacle yesterday when the lower house of Argentina's Congress narrowly approved a package he had negotiated with Mr Raul Alfonsn, former president and leader of the opposition Radical party, John Barham writes. The constitution bans consecutive presidential terms, but the Radicals back reform in return for amendments reducing the president's powers and a greater opposition role in government.
