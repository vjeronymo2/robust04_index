BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] The press center of the Volga Military District has announced that in the Sorskiy garrison of the district there was a working meeting of the officers of the peacekeeping forces of Russia and the United States. For three days, experts from the staffs of the Russian 27th Motorized Infantry Division and the 3d Infantry Division of the United States completed work on the plan for the joint command-staff exercise of the peacekeeping forces to be held from 4 to 14 July. Summing up the results of the working meeting, Major-General Anatoliy Sidyagin, head of the exercises on the Russian side, highly appraised the level of professional training of the Russian and American peacekeepers.
