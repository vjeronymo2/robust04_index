Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An official spokesman today strongly refuted press reports that the union home minister's helicopter was fired at during his visit to Doda yesterday. The spokesman said that no such incident concerning the home minister's helicopter took place during his visit to Doda and Kishtwar yesterday. However, militants fired a few rounds from a very distant place from Doda town, which were not aimed at the helicopter or any particular object. Moreover, the home minister's helicopter landed much later than this random firing.
