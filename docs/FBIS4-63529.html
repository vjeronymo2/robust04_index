BFN [Text] Kiev, 21 May -- President Kravchuk met the participants in the international conference "Society During Transitional Period: Experience of Market Reformation for Ukraine" under way in Kiev. The president, in particular, made the following remark: "The opinions expressed at the conference will not be implemented instantly; however, such undertakings are held with the purpose of giving us an opportunity to acquaint ourselves visually with the experience of other countries that had problems similar to those facing Ukraine."
