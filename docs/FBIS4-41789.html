BFN [Text] In accordance with current structural changes in the Georgian Defense Ministry, by an edict of Eduard Shevardnadze, commander in chief of the Georgian Armed Forces, Major General Tamaz Dumbadze has been appointed a deputy defense minister responsible for emergency situations. Defense Minister Lieutenant General Vardiko Nadibaidze ordered that Colonel Vladimer Imnadze be appointed the first deputy chief of the General Staff of the armed forces.
