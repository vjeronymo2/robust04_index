January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Swedish rookie Catharina Glasser-Bjerner, 24, came from behind to win the giant slalom on the U.S. Women's Pro Ski Tour at Stowe, Vt.

Glasser-Bjerner, the tour points leader, trailed Roswitha Raudaschl of Austria after the first heat of the finals, but made up the necessary time in the second run.

Glasser-Bjerner has won giant slaloms this season at Park City, Utah, and Waterville Valley,N.H.
