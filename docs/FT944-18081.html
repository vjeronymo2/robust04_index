941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / Observer: The price is right Kelvin MacKenzie's decision to join the Mirror group as executive director in charge of TV means at least he can again look forward to some fireworks. One of his juniors will be Janet Street Porter, a long-in-the-tooth TV type who is leaving the BBC to run Live TV. MacKenzie has never enjoyed playing second fiddle. But with little TV experience, he might have to - for a while.
