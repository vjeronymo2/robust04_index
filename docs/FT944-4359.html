941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / International Capital Markets: Dual-currency Pounds 300m facility for Britannia BS - Syndicated loans The Britannia Building Society has asked Westdeutsche Landesbank to arrange a Pounds 300m dual-currency revolving loan with multiple maturities. The loan, which has a swingline option, has a commitment fee of 8 basis points over the London interbank offered rate (Libor) for the first four years and a day, rising to 8.25 basis points up to five years and 8.5 basis points for the sixth year. The margin fee is 18 basis points for the first four years and a day, 20 basis points for year five and 22 basis points for year six. Participation fees are 5.5 basis points for Pounds 10m, 7 basis points for Pounds 15m and 8.5 basis points for Pounds 20m or more. A presentation was made on Monday and banks have until December 19 to join the syndicate. A banker close to the deal said there had been a good initial response and the loan should be signed in early January. A Dollars 700m revolving credit for Svenska Cellulosa, one of Europe's largest paper companies, arranged by Chemical Bank and Handelsbanken Markets, has been signed. The loan was oversubscribed in general syndication and increased from Dollars 500m. A total of 31 banks joined the syndicate. A Pounds 120m revolving credit for General Utilities, the holding company for the UK water company assets of Compagnie Generale des Eaux of France, has been increased to Pounds 126m after being oversubscribed in syndication. A total of nine banks joined the syndicate. A Pounds 237.5m revolving loan for MAI Finance, the international financial services and media company, has been signed. It was increased by Pounds 37.5m in syndication. Chemical Bank was the arranger and a further 12 banks joined the syndicate.
