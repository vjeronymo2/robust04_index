September 29, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

INEPT NEWS COVERAGE

I finally cooled down enough to write concerning your ineptitude in covering the news for Ventura County.

I seriously do not believe you even know where to secure most of it; e.g., when was the last time you listed a Ventura County bankruptcy in the business section? You always list San Diego County or San Bernardino County, but never Ventura.

When was the last time you published a stamp collector's column? Or a genealogy column such as "Climbing Your Family's Tree."

And where are all the events going on in Simi Valley and at the general east end of the county? Is it too far for your reporters to drive or telephone?

DAVE BOESHAAR

Simi Valley
