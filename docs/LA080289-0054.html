August 2, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Innocent pleas were entered in justice court in behalf of pro football player Russ Francis, arrested on a variety of charges, including drunken driving and carrying a concealed weapon.

Francis, who played last season with the New England Patriots, is free on $2,800 bail and his trial date is Oct. 12. He was arrested in early July while riding his motorcycle at Incline Village at Lake Tahoe.
