920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / French trade balance starts to improve FRANCE'S foreign trade balance improved sharply in 1991, after several years of steeply rising deficits, the Finance Ministry reported yesterday, writes Ian Davidson from Paris. The trade deficit was held at just over FFr30bn (Pounds 3bn) in 1991, compared with over FFr50bn in 1990, nearly FFr44bn in 1989 and nearly FFr33bn in 1988.
