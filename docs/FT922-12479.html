920418 FT 18 APR 92 / UK Company News: Fitch RS back in black in second half Fitch RS, the provider of design services, returned to the black in the second half of the 1991 year. However, it was not enough to overcome the first half deficit leaving the overall pre-tax loss at Pounds 417,000, against profits of Pounds 51,000. Operating profits were ahead at Pounds 1.61m (Pounds 753,000) but there was an exceptional charge of Pounds 1.18m (Pounds 656,000) relating to the costs of redundancies and the company's unoccupied offices. Net interest charges were Pounds 845,000 (Pounds 7,000 received). Turnover fell to Pounds 18.1m (Pounds 22.6m). After a tax credit of Pounds 51,000 (Pounds 481,000 charge) losses per share were 9.4p (10.8p).
