931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Schroder Split Fund The net asset value per capital share of the Schroder Split Fund stood at 80.93p at the six months ended July 31, an 11.6 per cent increase on the 72.5p at March 11 this year. The split capital trust, launched in February, declared a second interim dividend of 1.6875p per income share making 3.375p to date, payable from earnings of 3.95p. Directors have indicated their intention to pay four dividends a year in approximately equal amounts and a pay-out of not less than 6.75p is anticipated for the year.
