May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

YOUNG SURFERS AT DANA POINT

The local authorities at Dana Point apparently know the identities of the "kids" who are the subject of Linda Blandford's report on the Salt Creek incident.

A copy of Blandford's report should be sent to every parent whose "kid" was involved with a request that the report be read to the "kid" at the family dinner table or wherever the family is assembled.

MICHAEL J. FASMAN

Beverly Hills
