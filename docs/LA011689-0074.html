January 16, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

MONDAY CLOSINGS: MARTIN LUTHER KING JR. DAY

MAIL: There will be no delivery today, with post offices closed.

SCHOOLS: Public schools will be closed today.

BANKS: Most banks and savings & loans will be closed.

GOVERNMENT: Today is a holiday for federal, state and county government offices. City offices in Huntington Beach, Irvine and Santa Ana will be closed; all others will be open.

REFUSE: There will be no residential trash pickup in Santa Ana today; regular pickup schedules for the week will move back one day. Schedules in all other cities will be unchanged.
