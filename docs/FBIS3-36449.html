Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has revised its decision to ban media access to sittings of the Russian Council of Ministers except for the ROSSIYASKAYA GAZETA newspaper. Valeriy Trishin, deputy head of the Cabinet's press service, has told INTERFAX that a decision has been adopted to allow representatives of state news agencies, television and radio companies, and ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA to attend sittings as observers. For other press bodies, Trishin said, briefings and news conferences by Cabinet members will be organized.
