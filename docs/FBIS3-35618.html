Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Simferopol, 30 Jan--Presidential elections have taken place in Crimea. According to previous reports of the Central Electoral Commission, the headquarters of the Republican Party of Crimea and the "Russia" bloc (the bloc of parties that have united around Yuriy Meshkov) in the towns of Simferopol, Sevastopol, Bakhchisaray, Kerch, Yevpatoriya, Alushta, Yalta, and in Simferopolskiy and Chervonogvardiyskiy rayons--on average, 73.5 percent of the inhabitants who turned up at the polling stations--voted for Meshkov. Approximately 21.5 percent of voters voted for Nikolay Bagrov, chairman of the Supreme Council of Crimea. [passage omitted]
