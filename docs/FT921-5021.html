920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Technology: Working in a flower garden - Worth Watching JAPANESE companies are using perfumes in their offices to transform the atmosphere into a flower garden or Alpine pasture in the hope of improving employee performance. According to Shiseido, Japan's largest cosmetics group, the plug-in 'fragrance processor' can be adjusted for strength of scent. Refills are needed about once a month. Shiseido: Japan, 03 572 5111.
