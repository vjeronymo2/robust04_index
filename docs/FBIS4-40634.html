BFN [Text] Yevgeniy Abramov, first deputy interior minister of the Russian Federation, completed a three-day working visit to North Ossetia and Ingushetia on 18 June. A correspondent from the POSTFAKTUM news agency learned at the Russian Interior Ministry's public relations center that Militia Colonel General Abramov met with North Ossetian President Galazov, Ingush President Aushev, and [Aleksandr] Lozovoy, chief of the interim administration in the area of the state of emergency. During the meeting, they planned a package of specific measures to raise the efficiency of the work of the internal affairs bodies in the rayons.
