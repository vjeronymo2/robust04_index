BFN [ATSA/SDAA report: "Swiss Blue Helmets; Martin Chevallez Leaves ASIN Vice Presidency"] [Text] Lausanne, 6 Apr (ATS) -- Colonel Martin Chevallez has left the vice presidency of Action for an Independent and Neutral Switzerland (ASIN), whose president is National Council member Christoph Blocher (UDC [Swiss People's Party]/ZH). He confirmed the report published on Wednesday [6 April] by the dailies 24 HEURES and TRIBUNE DE GENEVE. He had headed the ASIN campaign in French-speaking Switzerland against the European Economic Area (EEA) during the federal vote on 6 December 1992. But he now supports, against his president, the creation of a Swiss UN Blue Helmet corps, on which a federal vote will be taken on 12 June. Martin Chevallez, son of former Federal Council Member Georges-Andre Chevallez, was EMG [expansion unknown] lieutenant colonel and a professional training officer until 1992. After being granted leave by the Federal Military Department to devote himself to his anti-EEA campaign, he became a militia colonel and commander of the Fifth Mountain Infantry Regiment in 1993.
