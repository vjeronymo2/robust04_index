930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / Electronic data worth Pounds 1.75bn THE ELECTRONIC information business in the UK is now worth more than Pounds 1.75bn a year and is growing, although more slowly than before. The third annual survey by the Confederation of Information Communication Industries, covering the years from 1988 to 1991, shows that the value of the sector rose from Pounds 1.18bn to Pounds 1.75bn. Growth in 1991 was 6.4 per cent, the lowest annual rate of the period.
