November 22, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPOTLIGHT: STAGE / NANCY CHURNIN: DIRECTOR O'BRIEN STEPS OUT ON CENTER STAGE FOR 'LETTERS'

PROGRAM NOTES: Sledgehammer Theatre will have three Pay What You Can performances of "Leonce and Lena" Nov. 29 at 8 p.m., Dec. 2 at 2 p.m. and Dec. 12 at 8 p.m. . . .

Moonlight Amphitheatre, which will open its winter season Nov. 29 with "Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat," is holding auditions for "A Man for All Seasons" Dec. 8 from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. and Dec. 9 from 6-9 p.m. at the Winter Playhouse, 1200 Vale Terrace Drive.
