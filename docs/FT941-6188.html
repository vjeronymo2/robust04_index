940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / Russia's Nato link welcomed Mr Warren Christopher, US secretary of state, yesterday welcomed Moscow's decision to join Nato's 'partnership for peace', which offers joint military training, exercises and co-operation in defence planning, Reuter reports from Moscow. No date has been fixed for a link-up, which must still be approved by the Russian parliament. Nato sent a diplomatic mission to Moscow this week to convince the defence alliance's main cold war foe that it should sign up to the partnership.
