BFN [Excerpt] An enlarged meeting of the constitutional court of the Kyrgyz Republic was held on 4 May. Askar Akayev, President of the Kyrgyz Republic, and Prime Minister Apas Dzhumagulov took part in the meeting. Also taking part in the meeting's work were the members of the law division of the commissions of the supreme and arbitration court, representatives of the Ministry of Justice, the procurator, and of the Internal Affairs Ministry. President Askar Akayev, in his speech, expressed his satisfaction for those who drafted the Constitution of the Kyrgyz Republic. It is obvious that this basic law opens the way for further enhancement of the social and economic development of the Republic. [words indistinct] [passage omitted]
