BFN [Text] On the Greek island of Corfu today, the heads of state of the 12 EU member countries will look for Jacques Delors's successor. There are three candidates, and one of them is the favorite: Belgian Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene, who is supported by Germany and France. Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez supports Dutch Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers, a Christian Democrat: [Begin Gonzalez recording] We have already expressed our opinion. We think that all the candidates are very good. If we have to prefer one over the other, however, I have promised to support one Lubbers. I think, however, that Dehaene would do the job perfectly well, and I also told Brittan that we do not hold any grudge against him either. [end recording].
