BFN [Text] French Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin said late yesterday afternoon that French troops would not remain in Rwanda after the evacuation operation. Last night he was at Roissy airport, near Paris, where he welcomed the first French evacuees. He declared that the operation could already be considered a success. [Begin Roussin recording] I would like to use this occasion to thank the troops and the crews of the military transport units who made the necessary planes available and made it possible to evacuate French nationals to Bujumbura and Bangui. We were helped by the people of the Central African Republic [CAR] and of Burundi: This has to be mentioned. Both in Bangui and in Burundi, we were welcomed by our friends. [end recording]
