Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 17 Jan (XINHUA) -- Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and Chinese president, said in Zhongnanhai this morning that China would, as always, continue to work for the consolidation and development of traditional ties between China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). Jiang made the remarks in a meeting with Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, here this morning. The two sides had talks in a cordial, warm and and friendly atmosphere. Jiang Zemin said: Comrade Hwang Chang-yop is our old friend. You have visited China many times. We met in 1990. I am very glad to meet you again today. We welcome your visiting China again. Jiang noted that China and the DPRK are linked together by rivers and mountains and that the traditional friendship between the two parties, the two countries and the two peoples was founded and fostered by leaders of the old generation of the two parties and has been sealed with blood. The CPC has attached much importance to these ties and is willing to make tireless efforts toward the consolidation and development of the friendly ties of cooperation between the two countries, Jiang said. During the meeting, Jiang briefed the visitors on China's reform and opening and the building of its socialist market system under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics. He also recalled his meeting with DPRK President Kim Il-song. He asked Hwang to convey his best regards to the DPRK president and Kim Chong-il, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. Hwang spoke highly of China's great achievements in building socialism with Chinese characteristics. He said the people of his country regard these achievements as their own and they are proud of them. He expressed the wish that the Chinese people would score greater achievements under the CPC's leadership, with Jiang as its core. Hwang conveyed best regards of Kim Il-song and Kim Chong-il to Jiang and asked Jiang to convey to Comrade Deng Xiaoping the best regards from Kim Il-song. Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, was present at the meeting. Hwang and his party arrived here Saturday [15 January] as guests of the CPC Central Committee. Apart from Beijing, they are to visit Guangzhou, Zhujiang and Shenyang. They will conclude this trip on January 23.
