October 25, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The two British boxers whose bout in Southampton, England, last month ended with the mother of one of them hitting his opponent with her high-heeled shoe, will fight again next month.

Promoters said that light-heavyweights Tony Wilson and Steve McCarthy will meet Nov. 15 in Reading, England, about 45 miles west of London.

The mother, Minna Wilson, has been told to stay way.
