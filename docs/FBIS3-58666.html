Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Greek Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis said the old danger from the East and the new situation in the Balkans require Greece to restructure its defense doctrine to effectively defend the country from old and new dangers. In an address at the Larisa headquarters, Arsenis discussed immediately implementing the new program. He said it would soon change the structure and meaning of national defense. Arsenis said: At the very most, the goal is to create Armed Forces that will be well prepared for war and will be ruled with meritocracy.
