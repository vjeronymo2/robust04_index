910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / International Company News: Echo Bay lifts gold output 12% ECHO BAY Mines, a large North American gold and silver producer, had a 12 per cent gain in the gold output in the first quarter and profit was Dollars 4.4m, or 4 cents a share, against a net loss of Dollars 37.9m, or 38 cents a share, a year earlier, including a special USDollars 42.4m write-down, writes Robert Gibbens. Revenues rose 8 per cent from Dollars 73.6m to Dollars 80.2m, with the rise in output out-weighing lower gold prices. Average realisation was Dollars 401 against Dollars 417 per ounce.
