February 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: BY THE NUMBERS;

12 HOLLYWOOD LINES -- THE SEQUEL

1. Trust me.

2. We'll let you know.

3. He's in a meeting.

4. He's still not back from lunch.

5. He's got two calls waiting.

6. I'll call you Monday, for sure.

7. You can depend on me.

8. Of course I remember you.

9. Have I ever lied to you?

10. We've been having trouble with the phones lately.

11. Didn't you get my last message?

12. Let's definitely go to lunch.

SOURCE: Jerry Hopkins' "The L.A. Book of Lists"
