BFN [Text] Visiting Malaysian Primary Industries Minister Datuk Sri Dr. Lim Keng Yaik and delegation paid a courtesy call on Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] and deputy prime minister, at the latter's office at 0930 this morning. At the meeting, cordial and frank talks were held on improving bilateral goodwill relations and economic cooperation, including foreign investment matters. Also present at the meeting were Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; responsible personnel, and Mr. John Tenewi Nuek, ambassador of Malaysia.
