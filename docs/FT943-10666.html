940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / World Trade News: HK land contract Gardiner & Theobald, the UK-based international construction cost consultants, will work on a Pounds 70m (Dollars 107m) land reclamation project at the Yau Ma Tei typhoon shelter in Hong Kong, writes Matthew Kaminski in London. The firm will draw up plans on how to reclaim the existing shelter and dredge the harbour so as to create 70 hectares of land needed to provide road and rail links to the new airport at Chek Lap Kok.
