November 8, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

RAMS GET SELLOUT; GAME TO BE ON TV

Sunday's game between the Rams and New York Giants at Anaheim Stadium is a sellout and will be televised in Southern California, the Rams announced Tuesday.

The 1 p.m. game will be broadcast by CBS and shown locally on Channel 2, with Pat Summerall and John Madden handling the play by play.

The game is the second home sellout for the Rams this season. New Orleans was the other on Oct. 22. LARRY STEWART
