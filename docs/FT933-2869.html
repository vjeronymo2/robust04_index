930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / International Company News: CSFB in pay scheme to unite group CS FIRST BOSTON, the investment banking arm of Switzerland's CS Holding, is developing a company-wide compensation scheme as part of its efforts to meld into a single global investment bank. Compensation has been a controversial subject at the firm, which operated until recently as three separate regional entities tied by revenue-sharing agreements. Mr Alan Wheat, chief operating officer, said the system might not be in place until next year. 'Measurement systems in this company are not what you would want them to be,' he said, adding that not all the issues surrounding compensation had been resolved. Mr Wheat said the arrangements might prompt further departures from the firm, which suffered a number of defections earlier this year. 'I think there are other people who may well leave,' he said.
