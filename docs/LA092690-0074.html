September 26, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A group of residents in Wellesley, Mass., demanded that police apologize to a Boston Celtic rookie who was mistaken for a bank robber last week.

Dee Brown was sitting in his car with his fiancee, Jill Edmondson, when police approached him with guns drawn and ordered him to get out and lie on the ground. He was not arrested.
