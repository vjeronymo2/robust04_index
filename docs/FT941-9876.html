940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / Pickets call for release of Russia's 'economic criminals' Russian businessman and politician Constantine Borovoy (centre) holds a US dollar which he sells for roubles while he and fellow businessmen picketed the state duma in Moscow and staged 'economic crimes' to press for the release of 'economic prisoners'. Russia has about 500 former businessmen serving long jail sentences for crimes such as 'private entrepreneurship'
