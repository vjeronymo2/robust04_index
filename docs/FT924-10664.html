921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / Observer: Bread-winner? Tony Alexander, chief operating officer of Hanson, was last week unwittingly lunching with the enemy. For, just two days before the Anglo-American conglomerate published its offer document for Ranks Hovis McDougall, he was to be seen at the Hyde Park Hotel with Greg Hutchings - Tomkins boss, Hanson alumnus, and author of yesterday's recommended bid for RHM. 'He is a personal friend and this was a longstanding engagement set up three months ago,' Hutchings explained yesterday. Meanwhile, Hanson should know how deadly serious Hutchings is, judging by the code names employed in the negotiations in which Tomkins played Mark Antony to RHM's Caesar. What was all that Mark Antony said about coming to bury Caesar, not to praise him?
