Article Type:CSO [Editorial report] Moscow MIROVAYA EKONOMIKA I MEZHDUNARODNYYE OTNOSHENIYA in Russian publishes, as a "special supplement" to the journal, signed to press 21 December 1993, the 333-page 15,000-word "abridged translation from English" of the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute's [SIPRI] 1993 yearbook, "World Armaments and Disarmament."
