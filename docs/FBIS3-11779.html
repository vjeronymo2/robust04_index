Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Jorge Rogabruna, vice director of hygiene and epidemiology in Havana City Province, said that the law that prohibits the rearing of pigs in the central municipalities of the capital and in apartment buildings and city neighborhoods remains valid. He stated that in Centro Habana, Habana Vieja, Cerro, Plaza de la Revolucion, Playa, and Diez de Octubre the rearing of those animals is prohibited and that it is not authorized in urban localities of other municipalities in Havana City with large concentrations of people. Rogabruna said that there are control mechanisms at the zone and municipal levels through the people's councils which send commissions to visit the owners of the animals and warn them of the infraction they commit by keeping those animals.
