BFN [Text] Riyadh, May 8, SPA -- The General Secretariat of the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) has issued the following statement on the developments of the situation in the Republic of Yemen: "While following with grave concern and deep sorrow the serious and painful deteriorations in the Republic of Yemen as a result of the eruption of fighting and battles between brothers int [as received], the Gulf Cooperation Council states appeal to the Yemeni leaders to put the interests of Yemen and its people above all other considerations, put an end to the battles and fighting and practice the highest degrees of self-restraint to halt the bloodshed and protect lives and properties". "They call on Yemeni leaders to respond to initiatives calling for an end to the fighting and resort to reason and dialogue to resolve differences," the statement said.
