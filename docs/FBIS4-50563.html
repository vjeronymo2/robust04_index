BFN [Text] Beijing, May 12 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Minister of Defense Chi Haotian met with Zbigniew Zalewski, deputy chief of General Staff and chief of logistics of the Polish Army today, expressing the hope for enhancing contacts between the two armies. The armies of the two countries had had the same experience in fighting against fascists and aggression in the past and under the new situation, they also share common issues of economic development and army building in their respective countries, he said. Some experience of Poland's army building is also worth learning, said Chi, who visited Poland in 1992 as chief of the General Staff of the Army. Zalewski said he was deeply impressed by the marked progress scored by the Chinese Army in its army building. He also shared the view of Minister Chi that there exists a great potential for further cooperation between the two armies. Zalewski and his party also traveled to Shanghai and Nanjing during their visit to China.
