October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BROWN SHIRT LEADERSHIP

Re Arnold T. Schwab's Oct. 7 letter attacking Rep. William Dannemeyer: It is incorrect to assert that the Nazis "were heterosexuals" who "ended toleration of homosexuals." In fact, the leadership of the brutal Brown Shirts -- the Sturmabteilung, or S.A. -- were homosexuals. They were instrumental in creating the climate of terror and intimidation that enabled Hitler to come to power. He in turn double-crossed them and they were eliminated in a purge.

For an account of these events, see William L. Shirer's "The Rise and Fall of the Third Reich."

DANIEL PLATT, Los Angeles
