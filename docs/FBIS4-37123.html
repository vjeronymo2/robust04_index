BFN [Text] Resistance fighters attacked the al-Qantarah position, which is manned jointly by Israeli occupation troops and their agent militiamen [South Lebanese Army, SLA] at 1030 today. There were fierce exchanges with various automatic weapons and rockets. Occupation forces bombarded Qabrikha, Tulin, and Wadi Hujayr. The shelling caused fires in Wadi al-Hujayr, which continue to blaze at this hour. The outskirts of Yatar were subjected to artillery fire from the enemy positions in [placename indistinct] this morning. The enemy forces also raked al-Hasbani and Zummarayya areas with machine-gun fire and rockets.
