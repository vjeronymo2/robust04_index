Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In an atmosphere of happiness and optimism, the document of pledge and accord to build the new Yemeni state was initialed this morning. The signing took place during the final session of the Political Forces Dialogue Committee held in Aden, the economic and trade capital, this morning. Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas opened the session with a speech in which he noted that, with God's help and thanks to the efforts of the participants, the committee drafted the document of pledge and agreement. He expressed hope that -- with the efforts of all honest parties in all the Yemeni arena -- this agreement will be embodied and translated on the ground.
