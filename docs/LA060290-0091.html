June 2, 1990, Saturday, Southland Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SAN DIEGO;

GRAND JURY SUGGESTS ASBESTOS EXAMS

County grand jurors recommended that people who work in the aging downtown courthouse -- from judges to janitors -- have physical examinations at least once a year because of concern about cancer-causing asbestos in the 663,000-square-foot building. Grand jury foreman Phil Franklin advised courthouse workers to ask county supervisors to eliminate the health hazard.
