BFN [Text] Beijing, April 1 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) of China, met with an Icelandic women's delegation here today. Chen, also president of the All-China Women's Federation, had a cordial talk with the group, led by S. Petursdottir, president of the Federation of Icelandic Women's Societies.
