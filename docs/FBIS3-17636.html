Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a goverment resolution, Estonian Telecom will today end relays of the Russian Ostankino State Radio and Television Company programs. The government resolution is due to both the contract expiry date and Ostankino's debt to Telecom. Government press representative Ain Saarna has said that Ostankino's debt to Estonian Telecom amounts to 185,000 German marks. Making allowances to viewers' requests, Telecom will continue to relay the Mexican television serial Simply Maria.
