930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Readymix shows drop in margins Readymix, the Irish building materials group, reported pre-tax profits down from IPounds 3.63m to IPounds 3m (Pounds 3.09m) for 1992, on lower turnover of Pounds 30.8m (Pounds 31.7m). The dividend is raised by 5.5 per cent, a final of 2.35p making a total of 2.91p (2.76p), more than twice covered by earnings of 6.57p (8.05p) per share.
