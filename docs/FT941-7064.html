940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Honeysuckle advances 39% to Pounds 834,000 Honeysuckle Group, the USM-quoted designer of women's wear, lifted pre-tax profits by 39 per cent from Pounds 601,000 to Pounds 834,000 in the half year to November 30. Turnover rose 44 per cent from Pounds 8.83m to Pounds 12.7m. Operating profit jumped 44 per cent to Pounds 915,000 (Pounds 635,000). Net interest payable, however, rose from Pounds 34,000 to Pounds 81,000. An interim dividend of 1p (0.75p) is declared, payable from earnings of 6.7p (5.5p) per share.
