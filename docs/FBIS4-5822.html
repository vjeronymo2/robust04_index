BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- A meeting commemorating the 14th anniversary of the foundation of the Republic of Zimbabwe was held here Tuesday under the auspices of the Korea-Zimbabwe friendship association. The meeting was attended by Choe Song-suk, chairman of the Central Committee of the Union of Agricultural Working People of Korea and chairman of the Korea-Zimbabwe friendship association, other officials concerned and working people in the city. A message of greetings to the president of the Republic of Zimbabwe was adopted at the meeting.
