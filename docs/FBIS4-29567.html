BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 18 (KCNA) -- Symposiums of working people's organizations were held to mark the lapse of 30 years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il began to work at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK]. Speeches were made at the symposiums on different subjects such as "The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's idea and theory of building working people's organizations is immortal grand programme of building mass organizations of our times", "Dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is an illustrious leader who has most successfully solved the question of education of young people". Speakers said that from the first days of his leadership of the party work Comrade Kim Chong-il attached great importance to the work of building working people's organizations and has energetically led them to discharge their mission and role as peripheral organizations of the party. Noting that the party and the revolutionary ranks have been cemented rockfirm as militant ranks of loyal subjects and filial people and the country has turned into an indestructible socialist power not wavering in face of any politico-ideological offensive and military threats of the imperialists and reactionaries, they said this is entirely a result of the tireless, energetic activities and leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il. They said the most important of feats performed by Comrade Kim Chong-il is that he has reared young people as lifeguards, death-defying corps loyal to the party, thereby guaranteeing a bright future of the party and the revolution. The five million young people now are firmly prepared to be guns and bombs defending the party and the leader with their lives no matter what mad wind may blow in their way, they declared. Symposiums also took place in the provinces.
