941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Sindall (William) SINDALL (WILLIAM) shareholders approved acquisition of Morgan Lovell and change of company's name to Morgan Sindall. Also, of the 4.41m new ordinary shares subject to the placing and open offer, 2.36m (53.6 per cent) were taken up. Valid applications were received in respect of 506,336 shares, or 19.9 per cent.
