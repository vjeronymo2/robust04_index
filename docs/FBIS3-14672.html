Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Nasir Qaddur, minister of state for foreign affairs, this evening received a delegation of the Federation of Expatriate Unions in Sweden headed by Sa'id Yilz. During the meeting, the talks addressed the ongoing peace process, the Syrian position toward this process, and the regional situation. The minister explained to the delegation the developments Syria has seen in all areas under the historic leadership of His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad. Several members of the delegation expressed their thanks for the hospitality accorded to them in Syria. They also expressed their appreciation of the renaissance seen in all areas and sectors. The meeting was attended by Mamduh Haydar, head of the Expatriates Department at the Foreign Ministry.
