BFN [Text] Baku (April 9) TURAN -- Three people were killed when a blast ripped through a passenger train in Southern Georgia,on Saturday. The train on its way from Tbilisi to the Armenian capital Yerevan, was standing in the station at the Azerbaijani border town Sadakhlo in the early hours of the morning when the explosion occurred. Two man and a woman died and a child was seriously injured in the blast, which sparked a blaze that destroyed three cars. TURAN news agency was informed from the informed sources that it was likely the blast had been caused by a terrorist act. Several trains have been blown up by bombs and in the last five years in the Transcaucasus region, rent by ethnic conflicts.
