BFN [Text] Changchun, April 20 (XINHUA) -- The 33rd session of the Sino-DPRK (Democratic People's Republic of Korea) joint committee for shipping on their border rivers closed here Tuesday [20 April]. During the 10-day meeting the committee discussed last year's work and the plan for 1994 put forward by the two sides. In a friendly atmosphere the two sides reached agreement on issues of navigation safety, surveys and dredging. An accord of the 33rd session of the joint committee was also passed during the 10-day meeting.
