BFN [Text] The Foreign Affairs Ministry of the Islamic State of Afghanistan today handed over an American citizen who had entered Afghanistan illegally to a Red Cross representative in Kabul. According to a report by a BIA correspondent, Mrs. Maria Korolov who is a REUTER news agency reporter, had entered Afghanistan via Tajikistan. She was arrested by the security authorities in Kundoz and was transferred to Kabul. The Red Cross expressed appreciation for this humanitarian action carried out by the government of Afghanistan+. The Red Cross added that Mrs. Korolov will soon be leaving for her home country. Meanwhile, the Foreign Affairs Ministry of the Islamic State of Afghanistan requested that all citizens of foreign countries, who intend to visit Afghanistan, obtain a visa through political representations of Afghanistan abroad and then enter Afghanistan. Otherwise, those who violate the law, will be dealt with in accordance with the current laws in the country.
