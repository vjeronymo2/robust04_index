921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / World News In Brief: Jeweller Gerald Ratner stands down UK jeweller Gerald Ratner, who became famous for applying the description 'crap' to one of his products, has quit the ailing retail group which bears his name. He is thought to have felt under pressure from the group's bankers and shareholders to resign. In August, the company, which owns US jewellers Sterling and Kays, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 122.8m for the year to February 1, compared with a profit of Pounds 112.1m the previous year.
