BFN [Text] A POSFACTUM correspondent has been told by competent sources that the Russian Internal Affairs Ministry [MVD] and the counterintelligence service are buying foreign weapons and ammunition at the instruction of Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. On the list are pistols, automatic weapons, and revolvers manufactured by famous Western firms. Tens of thousands of units of ammunition have been bought for each type of weapon. It seems that the authorities are set to undertake an uncompromising fight against bandits. But what form it is to take now can only be guessed at.
