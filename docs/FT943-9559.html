940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / International Company News: Interim profits jump at Cambior Cambior, a medium-sized Canadian-based gold producer, more than doubled first-half net profit to CDollars 10.7m (USDollars 7.7m), or 22 cents a share, up from CDollars 3.9m, or 10 cents, a year earlier. Revenues for the period were CDollars 151m, up 26 per cent. Gold output was 260,000oz, an increase of 11 per cent. The group's second-quarter net profit ahead 25 per cent at CDollars 3.8m, or 8 cents a share, on revenues of CDollars 77m, an increase of 17 per cent. Cambior is bringing in a new CDollars 100m zinc-copper mine in north-western Quebec and is exploring for gold in Surinam and French Guyana, and for copper in Peru. Total estimated 1994 gold output is 580,000oz, ranking Cambior among the 10 largest producers in north America. Bow Valley shareholders voted 99.8 per cent for a merger with Talisman Energy, the former BP Canada. Talisman becomes a top international oil and gas exploration and production company with assets of more than CDollars 3bn.
