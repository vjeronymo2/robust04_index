BFN [Text] Baghdad, 17 Jun (INA) -- Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf today received Mr. Pascal Jaquino, director of the French Strategic Diplomatic Institute, and his delegation. They discussed relations between Iraq and France and France's potential contribution to lifting the sanctions. Mr. Jaquino said Iraq deserved support and called for the facts of its positions to be explained. They also considered possible cooperation between the Institute of Foreign Service at the Iraqi Foreign Ministry and the French Strategic Diplomatic Institute.
