July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BEST STUFF

I have just come back from a long trip to New York City, England and France. Thank goodness my office saved all the back issues of the Sunday Los Angeles Times. I have just read and re-read Jerry Hulse's pieces on Bora Bora (May 14) and Raiatea (May 21). They are some of the best stuff I have ever read and I have saved them, not only to refer to for my readers but as a source of inspiration. Thank you for your beautiful series.

WILLIAM TOMICKI

Publisher, Entree
