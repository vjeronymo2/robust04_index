November 2, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ROBBERS WOUND 2 MEN IN APARTMENT HOLDUP

Police were searching Thursday for two men suspected of shooting two people during a robbery at a Reseda apartment complex.

The two shooting victims were hospitalized -- one in satisfactory condition, the in other serious condition -- after the 2:30 p.m. shooting at an apartment in the 8000 block of Reseda Boulevard, Los Angeles Police Lt. George Rock said.

Rock said two men entered the apartment carrying handguns and demanded money from the victims, whose names were not released. When the two refused and tried to escape, the robbers fired as many as four shots. Each of the two victims was hit once, Rock said.

The gunmen fled. No motive had been established for the attack.
