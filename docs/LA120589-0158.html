December 5, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

PLAYBOY FEATURES HUGH'S BRIDE

Playboy Magazine today published a special edition featuring founder Hugh Hefner's bride spread across 97 pages in various stages of undress. "Just in time for holiday gift-giving," said the magazine in announcing the publication. Kimberley Conrad, the 26-year-old blonde from Vancouver, Canada, who became the 63-year-old Hefner's wife in June, has had previous Playboy exposure. She was on the cover in August, 1988, the same year she was featured as "Miss January."
