930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Le Creuset up to Pounds 3.57m Le Creuset, the French-based but London-listed maker of cast iron cookware, table ware and corkscrews, yesterday announced a 7.7 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.32m to Pounds 3.57m for 1992. Manufacturing rationalisation at a cost of approximately Pounds 500,000 and the international spread of business helped maintained overall volumes and margins despite adverse exchange rates. Mr Paul van Zuyden, chairman, said capital expenditure would increase to more than Pounds 2m this year. Turnover improved 11.9 per cent to Pounds 37.9m. Interest charges were Pounds 1.3m (Pounds 950,000). Earnings per share were 15p (13.7p). A total dividend of FFr0.43 (FFr0.42) is proposed.
