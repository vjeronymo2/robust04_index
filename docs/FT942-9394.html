940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / International Company News: Recovery at Tabacalera Tabacalera, the partly-privatised Spanish tobacco monopoly, saw earnings recover in the first quarter of the year after a sharp setback in 1993, writes David White in Madrid. Pre-tax profits were 17 per cent higher at just under Pta4bn (Dollars 29m). Net sales reversed last year's trend to increase by 9 per cent to Pta140bn in the three months.
