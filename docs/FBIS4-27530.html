BFN [Text] A delegation of the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly led by Chairman Yang Hyong-sop paid a courtesy visit to chairman of Cameroonian National Council on 23 May. The chairman said at the meeting that the Korean people, who receive a wise leadership of President Kim Il-song, the respected and beloved leader, are great people who solve all problems with their own might, as well as people who receive great praise and admiration from the Cameroonian people. He pointed out that the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula must be solved by dialogue, not by pressure or war. The Cameroonian National Council arranged a banquet that day. Participants in the banquet proposed a toast for the long life of great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, for the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, and for Cameroonian President Paul Biya. On the same day, the delegation participated in a meeting with members of Cameroon-Korea Friendship Lawmaker Association. On 25 May, the delegation paid a courtesy visit to vice prime minister for Cameroonian territory and administration. On 26 May, it met with minister of Cameroonian External Relations. While it was in Cameroon, it visited the southwest area and the maritime province of the country and toured a crude oil refinery plant [wonyu chongje kongjang] and other oil companies [chongyu hoesa] there.
