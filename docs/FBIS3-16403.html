Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 15 (SNARK)--Armenian draftees will henceforth serve in Russian Frontier Troops, located in Armenia. Such agreement has been signed today in Yerevan by Russian Frontier Troops' commander Andrey Nikolayev and Armenian State Minister for Defence Vazgen Sargisyan. General-lieutenant Nikolayev has also met with Armenian Vice-President Gagik Arutyunyan and Prime Minister Grant Bagratyan.
