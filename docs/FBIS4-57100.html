BFN [Text] Qian Quichen, who is the PRC's vice premier and foreign minister, and Jose Alberto Naranjo Morales, Cuban minister without portfolio, met today in Beijing and expressed wishes for increased bilateral relations and friendly cooperation. During the meeting, Naranjo praised the achievements of the current reform and opening processes that are under way in the PRC as well as the great progress achieved in the construction sphere. Qian Qichen said the Cuban official's presence in Beijing will promote friendship and cooperation, and he extended greetings from Beijing's leaders to their Cuban counterparts.
