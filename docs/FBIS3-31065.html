Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Executive [Orszagos Valasztmany] of the Hungarian Democratic Forum [HDF] has decided on the person who should head the national [election] list of the party. In other words, the decision has been made about the person who will be the HDF's candidate for prime minister. Edit Schranz reports: [Schranz] [Current Prime Minister] Peter Boross will head the national list of the HDF. This decision was made by the party National Executive at the proposal of Sandor Lezsak. This means that Peter Boross is the HDF's candidate for prime minister for the next four years. What is more, in accordance with an earlier agreement, Peter Boross will head the Budapest list as well. [passage omitted]
