Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Crimea"] [Text] Officers of the Black Sea Fleet naval engineering service departments in Nikolayev and Odessa have voluntarily taken the oath of allegiance to Ukraine. In a briefing held at the Black Sea Fleet press center devoted to these events, it was however stated that the transfer of Black Sea Fleet naval engineering service departments to the subordination of Ukraine's Navy and Defense Ministry had been taking place with the aid of submachine gunners. The Black Sea Fleet command believes that such actions were sanctioned by Ukraine.
