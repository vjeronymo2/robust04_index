Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [SEGODNYA report: "Zhirinovskiy Continues To Remove Presidents"] [Text] Talking to journalists in the Duma lobby, Mr. Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, who is known to be actively pressing to be elected to the position of chairman of the parliament's foreign affairs committee, suddenly turned to international subjects. He described as "absolute fascism" the joint French-British initiative on a forcible settlement of the conflict in former Yugoslavia. He thus spoke about one of its authors, the French president: "That Mitterrand of yours has grown senile. He will last another half a year; so near his end, he dreams up all sorts of little ideas." The reaction by President Mitterrand and the French Foreign Ministry to this statement is yet unknown.
