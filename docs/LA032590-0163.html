March 25, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

RETIREE'S TICKET

Disbelief and then anger was my response as I read "Retiree Gets a Ticket for Picking Up Trash" (March 13).

As an Irvine taxpayer for 10 years, I wonder if the Irvine Police Department is overstaffed or is undertrained in recognizing criminal behavior.

Nick Grinenko's caring, smiling face greeted my walking partners and me every morning for the two years I lived in Northwood. He walked the same route every morning. He prided himself on collecting cans and bottles from the trash cans at the two parks and then giving the proceeds to charity.

Keep up the good work, Nick. I am relieved my current tax dollars didn't underwrite this incident!

GWEN BYRD

Aliso Viejo
