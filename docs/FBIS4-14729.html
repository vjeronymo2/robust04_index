BFN [Text] Yesterday, in 33 constituencies in Vinnitsa, Donetsk, Zhitomir, Ivano-Frankovsk, Kiev, Ternopol, Kharkov, Khmelnitsk, and Cherkassk Oblasts, Ukrainian Supreme Council runoffs were held. Some two-thirds of the electorate participated in the second round of the runoff in the Kharkov rural area. People's deputies were elected in all the constituencies. Oblast Militia Chief Oleksandr Bandurko; Prime Minister's Adviser Viktar Suslov; Borys Olkhovskiy, senior lecturer at the State Juridical Academy; kolkhoz chairmen Oleksandr Kudrevich, Oleksyy Berezhnyy, and Mykola Bychenko are among them. The Central Electoral Committee and constituencies' commissions are processing the information on the elections.
