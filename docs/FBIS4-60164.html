BFN [Text] Tehran, May 22, IRNA -- Thousands of ground forces of the Islamic Republic's Army Sunday participated in `Ra'ad 7' (thunder) maneuver in Darengon region of Shiraz, Southern Fars Province. The public relations office of the Army reported today that the five-day maneuver is carried out on the 13th anniversary of the liberation of the southern port city of Khorramshahr, Khuzestan Province, occupied by the Iraqi forces during the Iraqi invasion of Iran in 1981. Electronic equipment as well as heavy and light weapons will be used in the military exercise which is being staged to further improve the combat power of the forces, the report said. It also added that three more maneuvers are to be launched in Khuzestan, Khorasan and Tehran Provinces marking the Khorramshahr liberation anniversary.
