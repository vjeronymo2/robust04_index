920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Car deal will create 500 jobs in Wales NEARLY 500 jobs are to be created in West Glamorgan, south Wales, as a result of a contract to supply wiring systems to Japanese car plants in the UK, John Griffiths writes. The contract - the second won by the joint venture company between Lucas and Sumitomo Electrical Industries of Japan - is worth Pounds 20m a year. Lucas SEI Wiring Systems' plant at Ystradgynlais will supply Honda's manufacturing plant at Swindon, Wiltshire, due to go on stream in the autumn.
