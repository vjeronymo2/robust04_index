March 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $36 Thursday when Inquisitorial, the middle horse in his $6 triple, finished out of the money in the seventh race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,032.40.

Bob's bet today is $60 to win on Bruho in the eighth.
