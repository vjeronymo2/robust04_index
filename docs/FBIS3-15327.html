Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 3 [Mar] -- According to an IBERIA correspondent, the Abkhaz separatists' leader Vladislav Ardzinba in his interview given to Russian radio said that if the UN Security Council adopted a decision on deploying peacekeeping forces on the whole of the Abkhaz territory, the Abkhaz population would resist, even if this function were to be delegated to the Russian Army. "Peacekeeping forces must be deployed not on the whole of Abkhaz territory, but only in the zone of the conflict, that is, along the Inguri River", said Vladislav Ardzinba. The similar statement was earlier made in Geneva by the leader of the Abkhaz delegation, Sokrat Jinjolia.
