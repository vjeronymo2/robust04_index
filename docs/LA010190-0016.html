January 1, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Beckett Tribute to Air: A three-hour tribute to dramatist Samuel Beckett will air at noon today on KCRW-FM (89.9). The program will present four of Beckett's plays -- "All That Fall," starring Jack Albertson; "Embers," starring Leonard Nimoy; "Cascando" and "Rough for Radio II" -- all of which Beckett wrote for the radio. The Nobel Prize-winning author, who is perhaps best known for the play "Waiting for Godot," died Dec. 22 at age 83. SHAUNA SNOW
