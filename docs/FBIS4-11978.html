BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al-Rabia'a [March] 29, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- The secretary of the General Peoples Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Co-operation arrived in Beijing yesterday on a visit to the Peoples Republic of China where he will have contacts and consultations on the bolstering of the special relations and co-operation which exist between the Great Jamahiriyah and the Peoples Republic of China in all areas. The Chinese Foreign Ministry yesterday held a dinner banquet in honour of the secretary and his accompanying delegation which was attended by the heads of Arab diplomatic legations based in Beijing as well as the secretary and members of the Libyan Arab peoples bureau in Beijing.
