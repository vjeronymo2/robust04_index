920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Warning on HK A senior Chinese official, Lu Ping, has said Beijing will dissolve Hong Kong's legislature when the colony returns to Chinese rule unless Britain sticks to the constitution planned for the territory after 1997, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. Britain and Mr Chris Patten, the new Hong Kong governor, have upset China by vowing to raise the issue of speeding the pace of Hong Kong's democracy.
