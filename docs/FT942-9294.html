940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / Hitachi to set up design company Hitachi, the Japanese electronics company, is establishing a wholly owned semiconductor design company in the UK as part of a plan to improve engineering services to European customers. The value of the investment in the division, which will be called Hitachi Micro Systems Europe, has not been released but the number of engineers in Hitachi's existing European microsystems design division will roughly double. There are about 30 engineers at present.
