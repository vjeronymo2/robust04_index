931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / International Company News: Roche to sell Indian offshoot ROCHE Holding, the Swiss drugs group, confirmed it will sell its 74 per cent owned subsidiary in India to Piramal Enterprises, AP-DJ reports from Basel. Piramal will take over the Swiss company's interest in Roche Products, of Bombay. The terms were not disclosed. Piramal will continue to make and sell Roche pharmaceutical and vitamin products under license from Roche. It will have the right to sell and make Roche drug products in India.
