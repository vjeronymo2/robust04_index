920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / Recession in textiles goes on THE recession in textiles is continuing in spite of price-cutting, according to the Apparel, Knitting and Textiles Alliance, the industry's trade association. It said clothing prices were 5 per cent lower in January than in the previous month. The UK textiles workforce fell by 30,000 to 410,000 last year, the alliance said. UK exports of clothing rose 12 per cent in the year even though total output from the textiles industry fell 7 per cent.
