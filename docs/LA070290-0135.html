July 2, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

RYAN NO-HITS CLUBHOUSE THIEVES

Fenway Park burglars, as have so many Fenway Park would-be hitters, struck out against Nolan Ryan.

Burglars broke into the home and visitor clubhouses over the weekend and made off with several game jerseys, but got nothing from Ryan.

The Texas Rangers realize how highly sought Ryan memorabilia is among souvenir collectors, so the club takes special security precautions to protect the pitcher's personal possessions.
