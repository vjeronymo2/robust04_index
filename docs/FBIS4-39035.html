BFN [Text] Moscow, 3 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin today received Karolos Papoulias, representing the president of the European Union [Council] and Greek foreign minister, who conveyed a message from Greek Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou, who [currently] presides over the EU. According to the Russian president's press service, the message expresses the conviction that preparations for an agreement on Russia's partnership and cooperation with the EU will soon come to a successful conclusion, and the agreement could be signed during the meeting of the EU heads of state and government on the island of Corfu 24-25 June. A. Papandreou expressed hope that the Russian president might be able to sign the agreement. Boris Yeltsin said that, if the agreement with the EU has been duly prepared by that time, he could attend the signing. During the conversation, according to the Russian president's press service, several aspects of Russian-Greek relations were discussed.
