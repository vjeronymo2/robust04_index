BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 5 Apr (TANJUG) -- The eight-day Muslim offensive launched from the direction of Gorazde towards the Serb territory in Srbinje (formerly Foca) and Cajnice has been crushed, while the units of the Srpska army have reached the right bank of the Drina River, Major General Manojlo Milovanovic, chief of the general staff of the Srpska army said today. "Yesterday I ordered a counterattack to be carried out from the direction of Cajnice, because the Muslim units had persistently been trying to link up with their units in Sarajevo," Gen. Milovanovic said.
