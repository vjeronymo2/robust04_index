BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondents Viktor Demydenko and Mykhaylo Melnyk in Kiev on 10 June: "The Radiation Situation Is Normal"] [Text] An insignificant increase in the technological leak of contaminated water from the reservoir where used-fuel cassettes are stored was recorded at the Chernobyl Atomic Electric Power Plant [AES]. This was stated by Serhiy Nazarenko, chief of the emergency and control administration of Ukraine's State Committee for Atomic Energy. According to him, the controlled system of drainage of technological waters, preventing their escape into the environment, guarantees the reliable accumulation of contaminated waters and their processing. At present, the radiation situation at the Chernobyl AES is normal, he assured.
