920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / International Company News: Nippon Trust Bank in overseas closures NIPPON Trust Bank, a medium-sized Japanese trust bank, is to close its representative offices in New York and London later this year, writes Emiko Terazono. It will be the first Japanese bank to fold its international network. In contrast to the 1980s Japanese banks are now refocusing on the domestic market.
