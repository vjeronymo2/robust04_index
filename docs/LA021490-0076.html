February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

LOYOLA'S SUPERIOR HEIGHT TOO MUCH FOR THOUSAND OAKS IN 5-A LOSS, 100-73

Loyola used an imposing front line -- including 6-foot-11, Cal-bound center Ryan Jamison and 6-7 forward Jamal Adams -- to intimidate Thousand Oaks and cruise to a 100-73 first-round victory Tuesday night at Loyola in a Southern Section 5-A Division playoff game.

Jamison scored a game-high 26 points for Loyola (19-3), and teammates Wayne Stamps and Adams added 16 and 14 points.

Mike Sandlin led Thousand Oaks scorers with 11 points. Teammates Chris Loll and Brian Capella each added 10 points.

Thousand Oaks (12-12), an at-large team from the Marmonte League, was able to pull down just 21 rebounds.
