BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladas Burbulis] [Text] Vilnius April 15 TASS--A statement of Lithuanian Sejm opposition leader Vitautas Landsbergis on the Russian intention to join the Council of Europe and his accusations of "Moscow that allegedly commands the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry" was circulated in Strasbourg during a visit of Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas. A statement of the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry circulated here today says such unjustified accusations affect the prestige of the Lithuanian state. As for Russian membership in the Council of Europe, Lithuania will support it on condition of Russian observation of its international obligations as regards troops withdrawal from Latvia and Estonia and respect to sovereignty of the neighbouring states, human rights and rights of ethnic minorities.
