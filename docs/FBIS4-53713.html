BFN [Text] Seoul, May 21 (YONHAP) -- The secretary-general of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party (DLP) Mun Chung-su will visit China from May 24-29 at the invitation of the Chinese Communist Party. Mun is to meet with senior leaders of the Chinese Communist Party, including Vice Chairman of Political Consultative Conference Wu Xueqian, head of International Liaison Department Li Shuzheng and deputy head of International Liaison Department Zhu Shanqing. Mun and the Chinese leaders will discuss expanding cooperation between DLP and the Chinese Communist Party. Mun will explain to the Chinese leaders that the peaceful solution of the North Korea's nuclear problem is helpful to the continual development of ties between Seoul and Beijing and will ask for China's active mediation on the matter. Mun is also trying to meet with President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng through diplomatic channels and if Mun has a chance to meet with Jiang, Mun is likely to deliver President Kim Yong-sam's message, informed sources said. Meanwhile, the Executive Chairman of DLP Kim Chong-pil is scheduled to visit China in November.
