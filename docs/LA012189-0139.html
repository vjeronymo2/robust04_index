January 21, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Bowers Museum Shop reopens Sunday at its new location, 2036 N. Main St., Santa Ana. Shop hours will be noon to 5 Sundays and Saturdays, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Tuesdays through Fridays.

The Grove Theatre Company has announced receipt of a grant of $2,500 from the Orange County Register.
