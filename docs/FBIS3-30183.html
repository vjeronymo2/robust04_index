Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 27 -- Talks was held here from Feb. 22-25 on the working procedure of the joint Vietnam-China Land Border Working Group by the border officials of the two countries in furtherance of the `Agreement on the Fundamental Principles To Settle Issues Relating to the Border and Territory Issue Between the Two Countries'. The two sides agreed that the second meeting of the joint group will be held in Beijing. On this occasion, Deputy Foreign Minister Vu Khoan received all the members of the joint group. Chinese Ambassador Zhang Qing was present at the reception.
