Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Germany and Romania have initialled a maritime traffic agreement. It is the first agreement of this kind between Romania and a West European state following the political changes in the country, the Federal Transport Ministry announced in Bonn today. The agreement was expected to provide an impetus for bilateral cooperation both commercially and at administrative level. It would regulate, among other things, the equal treatment of ships relating to their access to each other's ports, as well as port services, the recognition of seamen's travel documents and provisions in case of emergency.
