July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The top-seeded team of Sinjin Smith and Randy Stoklos and the fifth-seeded team of Scott Ayakatubby and Rici Luyties advanced to today's semifinals of the $60,000 Miller Lite Open professional beach volleyball tournament in Grand Haven, Mich. In the other semifinal, second-seeded Karch Kiraly and Brent Frohoff will play third-seeded Tim Hovland and Mike Dodd.
