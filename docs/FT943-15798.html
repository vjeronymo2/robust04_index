940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Dudley Jenkins advances to Pounds 0.9m Pre-tax profits at Dudley Jenkins Group increased by 16 per cent from Pounds 779,000 to Pounds 903,000 in the year to April 30 on sales up 19 per cent at Pounds 12.6m. Mr Tylan Bahcheli, chairman of this USM-traded supplier of direct mail services, said there was a general feeling among clients that economic conditions were showing signs of improvement. The company has responded by investing in more databases. The dividend for the year is increased to 3.255p (3.1p) via a final of 2.205p. Earnings per share were 5.01p (4.53p).
