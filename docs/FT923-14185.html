920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Tops Estates advances to Pounds 2.6m Tops Estates, the property investment group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.42m to Pounds 2.61m in the year to March 31. The result was achieved on a rental income of Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 9.89m). Earnings emerged at 4.8p (5.24p) per share and the dividend for the year is 2p via a proposed final of 1.45p (1.3p).
