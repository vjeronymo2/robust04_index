September 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. OPEN SERVES TENNIS WITH POTENTIAL PROBLEM

Thanks to Jim Murray for his "Saliva Test" article on the Andre Agassi decision. We all saw and heard what Agassi did and were revolted by the obvious fear of the supervisor to throw Agassi out of the tournament. We were all made co-defendants of a system that upholds uncontrolled, disgusting behavior for the sake of money.

LEILA McDERMOTT

Granada Hills
