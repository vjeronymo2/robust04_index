September 1, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Santa Fe Springs-based Federal Envelope Co. is changing its name to American Envelope Co.-Los Angeles division as part of an overall change in corporate identity. American Envelope is one of the nation's largest envelope makers . . . The Investment Company Institute reported that the assets of the nation's 464 money market mutual funds rose $53.7 million in the week ended Aug. 30 to hit a new record of $343.4 billion.
