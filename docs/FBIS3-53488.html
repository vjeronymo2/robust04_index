Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security authorities have announced that the security forces had killed eight terrorists in Boumerdes and Algiers between 11-15 January. They also recovered some weapons and ammunition that were discovered inside a mosque in Bellevue in Kouba. The same source said (Moubarak Hacene), who was being sought in the case of the assassination of Mustapha Abada, the former director of the national television, was also killed. According to the same source, (Moubarak Hacene) belonged to the Hattab Tewfik gang, which carried out the assassination of former Prime Minister Kasdi Merbah. The security authorities added that they had killed nine terrorists on 12 and 15 January in Algiers, Jijel, Ghardaia, Bouira, and Boumerdes. The same authorites added they had recovered large quantities of weapons and ammunition.
