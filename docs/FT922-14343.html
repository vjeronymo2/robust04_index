920407 FT 07 APR 92 / Election 1992: Church attacks social record THE Church of Scotland yesterday entered the election debate with a strongly worded attack on the government's social and economic record. The annual report of the church and nation committee says the human cost of unemployment is unacceptable, and that last year's comment by Mr Norman Lamont, the chancellor, that unemployment was 'a price worth paying' to bring down inflation, was 'hollow and cynical'.
