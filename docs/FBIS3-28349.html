Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 7 KYODO -- The public approval rate for Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's cabinet decreased 21.1 percentage points within a week following his announcement early last Thursday of a controversial tax plan, TV Asahi reported Monday [7 February]. The support rate, which hit 77.4 percent on January 30 after Hosokawa succeeded in enacting crucial political reform bills, plummeted to 56.3 percent, TV Asahi said. The television company conducted the opinion poll on Saturday and Sunday, covering 1,000 people aged 20 or older, of whom 70.8 percent responded.
