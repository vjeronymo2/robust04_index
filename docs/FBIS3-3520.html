Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Text] The presidium of the second plenary session of the eighth provincial people's congress held its first meeting this afternoon. The meeting discussed and decided on relevant issues concerning the plenary session. The meeting, which was presided over by Li Zemin, chairman of the provincial people's congress Standing Committee, elected the executive chairman of the presidium by a show of hands; passed the namelist of executive chairmen groups; adopted the schedule of the plenary session; and decided on the choice of persons for deputy secretary-general and those attending the plenary session as nonvoting delegates. The meeting set 1700 on 1 March as the deadline for bill submission by deputies to the second plenary session of the eighth provincial people's congress.
