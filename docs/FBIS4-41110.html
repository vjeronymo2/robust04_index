BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 22 TASS -- The press service of the Russian presudent circulated the following announcement in Wednesday: "The president of the Russian Federation, the President of the United States amnd the prime minister of the United Kingdom are known to have agreed, with a view to enhancing confidence-building measures and reducing nuclear confrontation, that from May 30, this year, the strategic nuclear forces of the three states are no longer targeted on one another and keep operational vigil with zero flight settings. "Today, on June 22, President Boris Yeltsin as the supreme commander-in-chief visited the command centre of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation. "Defence Minister P.S. Grachev and Chief of the General Staff M.P. Kolesnikov demonstrated to Yeltsin the combat vigil arrangements and the procedure for the control of the country's nuclear forces in realisation of the agreement between Russia, the U.S. and Britain. "The Russian Federation has fully met its commitments. Its strategic nuclear forces keep combat vigil in line with new political realities that have arisen since the end of the cold war."
