November 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: WEST COVINA;

HOME REPAIR AID REJECTED

The City Council has rejected a proposal that would have provided $500 grants to residents for minor home repairs.

The council Monday voted 3 to 2 to deny a recommendation by the city's Human Services Department to set aside $10,000 to fund the program. Council members Nancy Manners and Richard Jennings were in favor of the program, which would have provided the one-time grants.
