940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Business Travel (Update): Bucharest hotel Bucharest now has a first-class, western-run, luxury hotel, following the opening this month of the Hotel Sofitel, part of the French chain. Situated in the city's new World Trade Centre, the four-star hotel has 203 rooms, two restaurants, a gym and, from September, a swimming pool and full fitness centre at neighbouring Lake Herastrau. Single rooms start at Dollars 175, double rooms at Dollars 190. For reservations and further information, telephone 40-1-2122998, fax 40-1-2115688.
