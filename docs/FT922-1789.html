920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Bankers Investment Trust assets rise Net asset value per ordinary share of the Bankers Investment Trust stood at 131.8p at April 30. That compared with 123.9p six months earlier and with 116.6p a year ago. Available revenue for the half year to end-April improved to Pounds 2.67m (Pounds 2.39m), equal to earnings of 1.71p (1.53p). A second interim dividend of 0.86p (0.8p) makes 1.72p (1.6p) to date.
