November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: HERE WE GO AGAIN

Other sequels on the horizon:

"Where the Red Fern Grows Part II," a follow-up to the 1974 family film about an Oklahoma boy and his hunting dogs.

"Bill and Ted Go to Hell," to shoot early next year for Orion, in which those dudes from San Dimas battle evil twin robot versions of . . . themselves.

And Sly Stallone -- who suggested in last Sunday's Calendar that the upcoming "Rocky V" would be the last in the series, more recently told Daily Variety's Army Archerd: "Nothing is written in stone." Pat H. Broeske
