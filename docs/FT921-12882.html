920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Albrighton makes further disposal In a further move in its financial restructuring, Albrighton, the USM-quoted building products group, has sold Transplastix, its architectural glazing subsidiary, for Pounds 2.3m cash. The buyer is Yule Catto, the chemicals and building products group. Transplastix's profits before interest and management charges were Pounds 1.01m in the year to March 31 1991; net assets amounted to Pounds 1.45m. Recently Albrighton has sold Frank England, Triad Timber Components, Lovells Confectionery and Insuwall - all to reduce group indebtedness.
