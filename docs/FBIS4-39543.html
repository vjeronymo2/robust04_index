BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Ostankino Television First Channel Network in Russian at 1700 GMT on 8 Jun, in the "Novosti" newscast, carries a report on Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev's arrival in Armenia. Speaking on his latest proposal on the introduction of Russian peacemaking forces in the Azeri-Nagorno-Karabakh conflict zone, Grachev makes the following comment: "The agreement--which I consider a good one--should definitely be signed if the sides want peace. On 11 June I will go from Georgia to Azerbaijan for a few hours. I will plainly tell Azeri President Geydar Aliyev that I support the agreement signed in Moscow. I will tell him that if Azerbaijan does not agree to it I will simply walk away from this issue."
