940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: MBO at GKN vending unit The management of GKN Vending Services has staged a Pounds 7.6m buy-out from GKN, the engineering group, and changed the name of the company to Vendcare Services. In 1993 GKN Vending Services made pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.1m on sales of Pounds 17.1m. The buy-out team, led by Mr Rod Tompsett, managing director, was advised by Touche Ross. Equity funding came from Prudential Venture Managers and senior debt from NatWest.
