Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM-TASS] [Text] Minsk February 21 TASS -- In accordance with the treaty on conventional armed forces in Europe, the NATO military inspection under the flag of West [as received] Germany has begun working on the base for storage of military equipment located near the city of Borisov in Belarus. The place of inspection was chosen by the NATO experts themselves an hour after their arrival to the Minsk-2 Airport as it is stipulated by the treaty. At the present time NATO inspectors examine and count units of materiel on the base. Arriving together with a group of inspectors from West Germany, Belgium, Denmark and the Netherlands were also chief of the Bundeswehr center for problems of control, Brigadier General Heinz Lokwai, and deputy of the Bundestag, Dr. Gunther Mueller. They will familiarize themselves with the process of destroying tanks and infantry combat vehicles at the Borisov tank repairs plant which is being carried out also in accordance with the treaty. The inspection is due to complete its work on February 22.
