August 10, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FIGHTING MAD: Should Iraq's troops invade Saudi Arabia, Saudi students in Orange County say their reaction would be swift. . . . "I'll be on the next plane home. It's my duty to defend my country," said Abdulrahan Al Omer, a Cal State Fullerton engineering student. . . . There are only about 200 Saudi students on Orange County university campuses, but the local Saudi population has been estimated at 50,000. David Reyes and Mark I. Pinsky
