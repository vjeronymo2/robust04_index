Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] In Gaza city yesterday, an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] officer sustained moderate to serious wounds when hit by terrorist gunfire. The terrorists fired shots from a passing car at an IDF patrol on al-Nasir street in the al-Shaykh Radwan neighborhood. The soldiers returned fire but the car managed to escape. The wounded officer was rushed by helicopter to Beersheba's Soraka Medical Center and operated upon.
