February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

* lan A. Weinstein has been named to the newly created position of chief financial officer for Wet Seal, the Irvine-based junior fashion retailer. Before joining the company, he held the same position for Public Image, a young men's specialty clothing chain. Previous positions were as chief financial officer for Tinder Box, a Santa Monica-based tobacco products retailer, and senior vice president for Aca Joe Eastern.
