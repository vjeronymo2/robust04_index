920422 FT 22 APR 92 / UK Company News: Clayhithe lifts Horstmann stake to 64% Clayhithe, the investment company, has increased its interest in Horstmann Group from 41.3 per cent to 63.7 per cent by purchasing a further 527,550 shares under the terms of an offer made last October. Cost of the latest purchase was Pounds 923,213, bringing the total investment to Pounds 2.47m. Clayhithe made its first investment in mid-1987. Horstmann makes timers and controls for the domestic heating market. In the year to March 31 1992 it is expected to improve substantially on the previous year which showed a pre-tax loss of Pounds 165,000.
