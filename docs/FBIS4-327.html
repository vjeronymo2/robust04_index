BFN [Text] The Inkatha Freedom Party leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi says he has information that hit squads were sent to assassinate him in recent weeks. Dr. Buthelezi is quoted in the British newspaper, THE DAILY STAR. In an exclusive interview, Dr. Buthelezi said members of the South African Communist Party who were also members of the ANC were guilty of the attempts. He told the newspaper that he had little hope that the talks with the state president, F.W. de Klerk, and ANC leader Nelson Mandela today would produce any result.
