May 2, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NO SHORTAGE OF ROOTERS FOR SUMMER SQUALL

Should Summer Squall win the Kentucky Derby Saturday, they'll have to draw straws to see who gets into the winner's circle.

There is a 20-person limit in the winner's circle.

The colt has 28 owners who are members of Dogwood Stable's Classic partnership that purchased yearling colts by five leading sires -- Storm Bird, Nijinsky II, Nureyev, Alydar and Danzig.

Cot Campbell, president of Dogwood Stable, said not all the partners would attend the Derby, but there would be a group of 25, including spouses.

The 28 people purchased 40 shares in the partnerships at $55,900 a share.

Holding more than one share are Ray Roncari, Mrs. John Ben Ali Haggin and Paul Oreffice. Oreffice is board chairman of Dow Chemical Co.
