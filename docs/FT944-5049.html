941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / Lottery puts FT reader in the pink Last week's Pounds 6m National Lottery jackpot may have been rolled over to this week but a Pounds 265,637 second prize has changed the life of Mr George McDonaugh, a 63-year-old investment manager for a firm of Edinburgh lawyers. Mr McDonaugh, a dedicated reader of the Financial Times, gave in his notice yesterday and plans to invest the money in his own future rather than anybody else's.
