April 6, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Ken Hatem has been named eastern regional manager for North American sales for General Automation. He was previously the national marketing director for Studebaker-Worthington Leasing Corp., a computer finance company, and spent five years as eastern regional manager for the ADDS subsidiary of NCR Corp. General Automation, based in Anaheim, is a supplier of computer systems.
