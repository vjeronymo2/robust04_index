930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / Man jailed for Pounds 3.9m fraud MR AJIT KOHLI, who swindled finance companies out of Pounds 3.9m in an attempt to prop up his property interests, was jailed for four years yesterday. Mr Kohli, of Kenton, north-west London, was also banned from being a company director for five years by Southwark Crown Court, London. He was found guilty of two charges of fraudulent trading and four charges of obtaining property by deception. He was cleared of four deception charges.
