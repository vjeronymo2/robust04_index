930416 FT 16 APR 93 / UK Company News: Risky contract helps Cradley reach Pounds 1.14m Interim pre-tax profits of Cradley Group, the lithographic printer, surged from Pounds 487,000 to Pounds 1.14m, but the result benefited from a large one-off contract in a 'disappointing half year'. Mr Donald Jordan, chairman, said the period to December 31 saw a shrinking market for print, with profit margins virtually zero. He said the one-off contract for Europe resulted in a considerable over-recovery of costs. In terms of production schedule it was a high risk contract, but the very tight schedule had been met. The magazine printing business, he said, returned a loss of some Pounds 400,000 after tax. Sales increased to Pounds 17.2m (Pounds 13.7m). Earnings per share were 2.3p (1p).
