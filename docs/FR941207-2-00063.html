[TA&hyph;W&hyph;30,491] Vought Aircraft Company Dallas, Texas; Notice of Termination of Investigation Pursuant to Section 221 of the Trade Act of 1974, an investigation was initiated on November 14, 1994 in response to a worker petition which was filed on behalf of workers at Vought Aircraft Company, Dallas, Texas. Active certification covering the petitioning group of workers at the subject firm remains in effect (TA&hyph;W&hyph;29,206). Consequently, further investigation in this case would serve no purpose, and the investigation has been terminated. Signed in Washington, D.C. this 22nd day of November, 1994 Victor J.
