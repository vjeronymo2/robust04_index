BFN [Text] Lusaka, June 17, (XINHUA) -- China will supply and install two sets of 150 kilowatt short wave transmitters at Zambia National Broadcasting Corporation (ZNBC) in Lusaka and Kitwe. An agreement to the effect was signed here on Thursday [16 June]. China, under the agreement, will also supply and instal two sets of 10 kilowatt `FM' transmitters at the mass-media complex and two 10 kilowatt sets at ZNBC Kitwe studios. Zambian Minister of Information and Broadcasting Services Keli Walubita said that after the installation of the transmitters, a good radio reception is anticipated almost throughout Zambia. He thanked China for its assistance to Zambia in the improvement of the radio reception in Zambia.
