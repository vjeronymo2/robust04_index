Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu 'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, received today Sir Michael Burton, assistant undersecretary of state of the British Foreign and Commonwealth Office, and the delegation accompanying him. The peace process, the current negotiations with Israel, and the obstacles facing them which are being put forth by Israel were reviewed during the meeting. The brother President leader Abu 'Ammar entrusted Sir Michael with an important message for John Major, the British prime minister, on the course of the negotiations and the contribution in overcoming the obstacles facing them. The British contribution to the development operations in the occupied Palestinian territories was reviewed in light of the talks held by the brother president during his latest visit to Britain. Palestinian-British bilateral relations and the importance of the British and European role in general for the reconstruction of the Palestinian territories were also reviewed. [passage omitted]
