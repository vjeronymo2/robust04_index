Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Anna Melnikova and Aleksandr Kharchenko] [Text] Moscow February 26 TASS -- Ruslan Khasbulatov, Aleksandr Rutskoy, Albert Makashev, Andrey Dunayev, Viktor Anpilov and Vladislav Achalov were freed from the Lefortovo investigation ward today in accordance with a State Duma resolution on amnesty. All the accused who agree with the State Duma resolution will be freed from Lefortovo today, Deputy Russian Procurator General Vladimir Kravtsev told ITAR-TASS. It is Kravtsev who sets free persons arrested after the October 1993 revolt, as Russian Procurator General Aleksey Kazannik sent in his resignation earlier today. Kravtsev has told a news conference today the four deputies of the Russian procurator general are at one with Kazannik and are ready to resign as well.
