October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TOLL ROAD WARRIOR

"Toll Road Warrior" Bob Poole will never win over the opposition as long as he continues to deflect his critics with comments such as: What's so bad about letting "fat cats in their BMWs willingly pay a bunch of extra money to use their cars?" I happen to be a BMW owner, and I am neither wealthy nor extravagant, as implied by his remark. I am already paying "a bunch of extra money" to use my car through higher license fees, higher insurance and higher maintenance costs, and these are not monies I spend willingly. What makes him think I would willingly pay to make use of his proposed toll roads? The only reason I will make use of the toll roads he proposes will be because my time has a higher value used somewhere other than sitting in stop-and-go traffic on the freeway. Even then, it will be done unwillingly.

CAROL BAKER, Reseda
