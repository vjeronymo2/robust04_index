BFN [Igor Golin report under "KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and Agency Correspondents Report" rubric: "Estonian Military Flexing Its Muscles?"] [Text] Tallinn -- The following BNS (BALTIC NEWS SERVICE) report has been circulated in Baltic countries' mass media: Estonian Defense Forces Commander Alexander Einseln has stated that the Estonian Defense Forces can easily cope with the Russian military grouping stationed in Estonia and, if necessary, can expel the "Russian militarists" by force. "We are ready to to this if we are given such an order," Einseln stated. The agency points out that currently there are approximately 2,500 Russian servicemen in Estonia, the deadline for whose withdrawal has still not been agreed, while the Estonian Defense Forces have between 5,000 and 6,000 soldiers.
