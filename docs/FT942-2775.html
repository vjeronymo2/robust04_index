940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / International Company News: Adare Printing The acquisition and turning round of Waddington Business Forms enabled Adare Printing Group, the Dublin-based printing company to report pre-tax profits for the year to April 30 almost tripled at IPounds 1.48m (Pounds 1.45m), against IPounds 565,000. Since the year end it has acquired Alexander Pettigrew, which is in the business forms sector, for Pounds 835,000 and Label Convertors, a label manufacturer, for Pounds 1.17m. Group sales increased from IPounds 10.7m to IPounds 28.5m, including IPounds 16.1m from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 17.3p (11.56p) and the total dividend is increased to 1.987p (1.892p) with a final of 1.042p.
