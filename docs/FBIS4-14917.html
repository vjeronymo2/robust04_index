CSO ["Election Results in the Rural Districts of Kharkiv Oblast"] [Text] No deputies were elected in any of Kharkiv Oblast's rural electoral districts on 27 March. The following will stand for election in the second round: in Izyum electoral district [e.d.] No. 380 -- Petro Didyk, chairman of the city council, and Viktor Ustymenko, docent of the Academy of Law (Kharkiv); in Kupyansk e.d.
