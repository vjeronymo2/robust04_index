921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Western Selection Pounds 24,800 in the black Western Selection, the principal activity of which is the holding of substantial stakes in two investment companies - Creston and Burlington Group - reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 24,800 for the year to end-September. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 363,000 last year and came from total income of Pounds 103,100 (Pounds 177,100). Earnings per share were 0.15p (losses 2.51p).
