Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Veronika Romanenkova] [Text] Moscow January 14 TASS -- Mrs. Hillary Clinton, the wife of the U.S. President, and Miss Chelsea Clinton, his daughter, arrived in Moscow today in the morning. They were welcomed at the Sheremetyevo-1 airport by Mrs. Valentina Chernomyrdin, the wife of the Russian prime minister. Today's programme of the stay in Moscow of the wife and daughter of the U.S. President is rather extensive. Mrs. Clinton will visit the U.S. residence in Russia (Spaso-House), the Spaso-Perovskiy Hospital of Peace and Mercy, the Assumption Cathedral, the Armoury and the Hall of Facets of the Kremlin, as well as the Ostankino TV Centre. Chelsea Clinton will visit the ballet school.
