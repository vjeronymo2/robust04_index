910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / World Commodites Prices: Fruit and Vegetables Avocado at 35-50p each (60-90p) is the best fruit buy this week, along with French Golden Delicious apples at 40-55p a lb (40-55p) and grapes from Cape and Chile at 75p-Pounds 1.25p a lb (75p-Pounds 1.25p). Kiwifruit at 15-20p each (15-20p), plums at 75p-Pounds 1.20 a lb, and oranges at 5-28p (5-28p) are all excellent fruit choices. Cauliflower at 45-65p (55-70p) is a good vegetable buy, and mushrooms at 60p-Pounds 1.10 a 1/2 lb are plentiful. Cucumbers are good value this week at 55-70p each (55-70p), together with tomatoes at 65-90p a lb (65-90p) and Iceberg lettuce at 60-85p each (60-85p).
