940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / HQ for transport department chosen The Department of Transport is to move next year to a new headquarters in Horseferry Road, Westminster, near its existing headquarters in Marsham Street. The department will move into Great Minster, a 220,000 sq ft building developed by Pearl Assurance, the life company. It has agreed a 25-year lease with options to break the agreement at 15 and 20 years, for which it will pay a rent of Pounds 19.50 per sq ft. The Department of the Environment, which shares Marsham Street with the transport department, has already announced plans to move to Eland House, which will be built close to Victoria station. The Marsham Street building will be demolished after the two government departments have moved.
