920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Mendes retrial An appeals court in the Amazon state of Acre yesterday ordered a retrial of the man convicted of masterminding the murder of Brazilian rainforest campaigner Chico Mendes, the Estado news agency said, Reuter reports from Rio de Janeiro Mr Darli Alves da Silva and his son, Darci were both sentenced to 19 years in prison in December 1990 for the crime, which shocked conservationists around the world. Estado reported that the court turned down Darci's plea for a retrial.
