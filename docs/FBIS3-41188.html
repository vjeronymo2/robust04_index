Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "24 Kilograms Heroin Recovered in Attock"] [Text] Islamabad, 20 December (Monitoring Desk)--Attock police foiled an attempt to smuggle a huge quantity of heroin. According to Pakistan Television, this heroin was being smuggled from Sarhad to Punjab. On receiving information the staff at Attock Khurd police station stopped and searched a car. They recovered 24 kilogram heroin in from its hidden compartment and the gas tank.
