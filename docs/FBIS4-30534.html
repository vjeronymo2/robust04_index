BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received the friendship visiting group of the Chinese People's Liberation Army [CPLA] led by General Wang Ke, commander of the Shenyang military area of the CPLA, on a visit to Korea. Present there were Choe Kwang, chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, General of the KPA Yi Chong-san, Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai and embassy officials. President Kim Il-song had a cordial and friendly talk with the guests.
