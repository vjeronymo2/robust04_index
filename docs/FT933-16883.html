930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Mount Charlotte Mount Charlotte Investments, the hotels group, raised net profit from Pounds 1.5m to Pounds 2.1m in the year to December 27, after a tax credit of Pounds 1.1m. Turnover dipped from Pounds 226.1m to Pounds 217.3m. Mount Charlotte is 70 per cent owned by Brierley Investments.
