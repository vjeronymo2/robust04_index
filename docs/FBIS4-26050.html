BFN [Text] Beijing, June 16 (XINHUA) -- More than 600 people gathered here today to mark the 70th anniversary of the founding of the influential Huangpu Military Academy. The gathering also marked the 10th anniversary of the Huangpu Military Academy Alumni Association. Both Chinese president and chairman of the Central Military Commission, Jiang Zemin, and vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, Liu Huaqing, calligraphed inscriptions of greetings for the anniversaries. Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), said at the gathering that he hoped all alumni across the Taiwan Strait would unite for the reunification of the motherland and the revitalization of China. Li, also a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, said that the reunification of the motherland is the common wish and sacred duty of the people of all nationalities in China, including the Taiwan compatriots. Premier Li Peng and several other senior Chinese leaders, met the people attending today's gathering. Huangpu (Whampoa) Military Academy, which was established in 1924 by Dr. Sun Yat-sen at Huangpu near Guangzhou, was a training school for officers of the national revolutionary army during China's first revolutionary civil war (1924-1927). Li Moan, vice-president of the Huangpu Military Academy Alumni Association, urged all who had attended the academy to be united under the banner of patriotism and to carry out the wishes of Dr. Sun Yat-sen and other revolutionary martyrs.
