BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] The Cuban steel industry has been assembling mopeds since April, in cooperation with Slovakia's (Howritz) firm. The national news agency reports that the mopeds will be earmarked for exportation and tourism and will eventually be assigned to state organizations. A total of 450 mopeds have been assembled to date. These mopeds can reach a maximum speed of between 40 and 50 km per hour.
