940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Business Travel (Update): BA and TWA fare sale British Airways and TWA, have announced a sale for fares between several US cities and the UK. British Airways's offers represent a 33 percent to 47 percent reduction on normal fares. Reservations must be made by midnight Monday on both airlines. Travellers must depart between August 1 and September 7, and complete trips by September 14. A Saturday night stay is required.
