930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / UK Company News: REA disposes of tea estates operator REA Holdings, the plantations holding company, has sold Surmah Valley Tea for about Pounds 1.6m cash, against a book value of Pounds 2.2m. Surmah operates three tea estates in Bangladesh and incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 100,000 in 1991 when REA reported profits of Pounds 883,000.
