Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 3) TURAN -- Headquarter of the Popular [People's] Front is still under the control of armed policemen. All ins and outs of the building are kept under the control as well. The employees of the PFA [People's Front of Azerbaijan] can not work as they are not allowed to enter the building. Deputy Chairman of the PFA, Asim Mollazadeh told Turan that "according to the law, after the search the police must leave the building and give us opportunity to perform our duties". According to Mollazadeh, yesterday, the Chief of police of Baku city told First Deputy Chairman of the PFA, Ibragim Ibragimli that not he but the leadership deals with this matter. Judging by all, the authorities will confiscate the building of the Headquarter of the PFA. This morning there was no metal signboard "Popular Front of Azerbaijan" on the building. Yesterday a high-rank official in the President's staff told Turan that "it was Elchibey who granted the buildings to the PFA and "Azadlyg" newspaper".
