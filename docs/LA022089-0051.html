February 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The Hartford Insurance Group of Connecticut announced plans to shut down clerical operations in Brea in Orange County and Walnut Creek in Northern California, effective May 26. It said 205 workers at the two facilities must either transfer to operations in other states or lose their jobs. Sixty-five employees at a Brea underwriting and service center and 140 employees at a Walnut Creek processing center will be affected. The offices will be closed because of cost-cutting measures and the increased use of automation.
