930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Murray Split The net asset value of Murray Split Capital Trust was 176.1p per capital share at May 31 - a rise of 86 per cent on the 94.6p standing at the August year-end. The value of the zero dividend preference shares rose from 112.6p to 122.1p over the same period. Available revenue for the nine months to end-May was Pounds 615,000 for earnings of 7.69p per share. As forecast, a third interim dividend of 2.65p makes 7.95p to date. The total for the year is expected to be maintained at last year's annualised rate of 10.6p.
