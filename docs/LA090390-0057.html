September 3, 1990, Monday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: GUNMAN KIDNAPS, RAPES TEEN-AGE GIRL

A 17-year-old Van Nuys girl was kidnaped at gunpoint Sunday and raped, Los Angeles police reported.

The girl told police a man forced her into his van at gunpoint near Sherman Way and Reseda Boulevard about 1 a.m.

She said the man raped her and left her near Mureau and Calabasas roads in Calabasas.

She walked to a nearby school to call authorities. Sheriff's deputies found her at the school about 4 a.m. Sunday and contacted Los Angeles police. She was taken to a nearby hospital.

The man -- possibly an Asian in his mid-20s, about 5 feet, 6 inches tall and weighing between 160 and 180 pounds -- drove a dark blue or gray van, said Los Angeles Police Sgt. William Weber.
