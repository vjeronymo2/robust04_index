BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 24 May Morning Edition] [Text] Tatsufumi Tsuboi, chief of the Cabinet Security Affairs Office, touched on the DPRK's (North Korea's) suspected development of nuclear arms at a Budget Committee session of the House of Representatives on 23 May. He disclosed that a joint council -- comprised of bureau chief-level officials at various ministries and agencies -- has been set up within the government and that it is now analyzing the situation concerning North Korea. He made the remarks in reply to questions by Shun Oide (of the Social Democratic Party of Japan).
