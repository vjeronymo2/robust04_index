930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / World News In Brief: Tennis star stabbed during tournament Tennis player Monica Seles was recovering after being stabbed in the back on court at a tournament in Hamburg. Her attacker, an east German man, reached over a barrier from the crowd. Police said the motive was not political. The attacker, who appeared confused, said he was a Steffi Graf fan and wanted to hinder Seles from playing.
