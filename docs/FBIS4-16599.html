BFN [Text] RIGA, Apr 14, BNS - Latvian Foreign Ministry's Iveta Sers and Romanian diplomat Dan Tiron initialed an agreement Thursday [14 April] on trade and economic relations between both countries. The draft agreement provides for the most favored nation status in mutual trade, the Latvian Foreign Ministry said. The trade agreement should be ratified and enter into force until summer. Latvia has already signed similar agreements with the United States, Australia, Austria, Belgium, the Czech Republic, Denmark, France, Greece, Italy, India, Iceland, Ireland, Great Britain, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Portugal, Poland, Spain, Hungary and Germany, as well as with several CIS countries.
