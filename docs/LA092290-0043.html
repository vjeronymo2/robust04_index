September 22, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL;

ROUNDUP

Glendale 30, Bell-Jeff 0 -- Pathon Rucker rushed for 163 yards and scored all four touchdowns, including three in the first half, for Glendale at home.

Rucker, who carried 14 times, scored on runs of two, seven, nine and 52 yards.

Rucker sat out most of the second half and his replacement, Kyiamo-Waki Jones, filled in with 76 yards in nine carries. Glendale quarterback Eric Kiesau completed six of 12 for 81 yards.

Bell-Jeff quarterback Anthony Pawluc connected on eight of 20 for 50 yards, and running back Greg Cervantes led the team with 31 yards in 10 carries. MICHAEL ARKUSH
