Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met with a delegation of the Committee of 100 from the United States and hosted a dinner in its honor here this evening. The nearly 30-member delegation, headed by governor of the Committee of 100 and the well-known architect I.M. Pei, arrived for a visit to China Monday [28 March], at the invitation of the CPPCC National Committee.
