940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / Company News: Barclays - Other Companies The resumption of dividend growth is expected when the clearer announces its interim results tomorrow. After its cut in the full-year payout last year, analysts expect an increase in the interim to between 7.5p and 8p from last year's 6.5p. Bad debts will be down, leading to pre-tax profits forecast to rise to Pounds 670m-Pounds 770m from last year's Pounds 335m.
