BFN [Report by Jovan Vlajkovic from Okucani] [Text] Overnight and yesterday, the Croatian Army on two occasions violated the signed agreement in Western Slavonia. The first instance occurred yesterday at around 0900, when Croatian planes violated the airspace in the region of the village of Zagalici, 7 km east of Okucani. The second instance occurred in the course of the night, when Croat Army troops in the region of the village of Bujici, 4 km northeast of Novska, opened small arms fire on the positions of the Serbian Krajina army. The command of the 18th Corps of the Serbian Krajina army has lodged a sharp protest with the UN Protection Forces and has warned of the possible consequences of such conduct by the Croatian side.
