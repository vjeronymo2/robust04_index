BFN [Text] Foreign minister Saudabayev today received Chen Di, the Chinese ambassador to Kazakhstan. They stressed that the mechanism of summit contacts is working efficiently. The forthcoming visit to Almaty by Prime Minister Li Peng testifies to this fact. In the opinion of both sides, this visit will play an important role in concrete development and implementations of agreements reached in October last year during Nazarbayev's visit to China. The signing of an agreement on the state border between Kazakhstan and China and other documents will be of great importance.
