Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] An unattributed report notes that the State of Chiapas received the equivalent of 1.590 billion new pesos in National Solidarity Program (Pronasol) funding over the last five years, representing 6.44 percent of all Pronasol disbursements to Mexico's 31 states and the Federal District. An earlier report noted Social Development Secretary Carlos Rojas Gutierrez's 15 December statement that Pronasol's national budget for 1994 would total 8.8 billion new pesos. (LA JORNADA 7 Jan 94 and 16 Dec 93)
