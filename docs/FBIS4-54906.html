BFN [Text] Since the situation that would emerge if the anticipated cabinet changes are approved actually means a new mandate, we must accomplish within 50 days what we could not do in 16 months, otherwise we will fail miserably. This position was defended at a cabinet meeting today by Finance Minister Stoyan Aleksandrov. He later explained his motives at a news conference. According to Stoyan Aleksandrov, if the government succumbs to trade union and political pressure and adopts a cheap, populist policy of compensations, a catastrophe will immediately follow. At a meeting with Prime Minister Berov Finance Minister, Aleksandrov said it is not normal to divide the functions of the Finance Ministry leader's important post. At the cabinet meeting today, the prime minister asked all the ministers to present by tomorrow morning at the latest written opinions on possible measures in their separate ministries by the end of June.
