December 25, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: VICA MEMBERSHIP GROWS TO 300 AFTER MERGER

The Valley Industry and Commerce Assn. has merged with the Technology Corridor Assn., which promotes clean-air businesses along the Ventura Freeway between Woodland Hills and Oxnard.

The merger boosts to 300 the membership of the Valley Industry and Commerce Assn., founded in 1949 to attract businesses and residents to the San Fernando and Santa Clarita valleys and Ventura County. There were 260 members before the merger, which was announced Dec. 13.
