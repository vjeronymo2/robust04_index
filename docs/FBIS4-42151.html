BFN [Text] NATO Secretary General Woerner has canceled his participation in the NATO foreign ministers' meeting, which is scheduled next week in Istanbul. In a letter to the 16 member states, which was published today, Woerner says that he is following his doctors' advice in not attending the meeting. After the summer vacations, however, he expects to be able fully to resume his responsibilities again. The former FRG defense minister has been suffering from cancer of the colon since 1992.
