Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] (Andres E. Rivero), first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] in San Nicolas Municipality, Havana Province, has announced that the PCC will pay special attention to economic efficiency in all sectors this year, principally in the production sector. (Rivero) said that in San Nicolas, like everywhere else in Cuba, economic problems are being discussed at the work centers and analyses are being conducted on ways to reduce subsidies to the management and budgetary systems, while producing more with the available raw materials. The PCC municipal leader stressed that the PCC's attention in the province is focused on increasing cane production by 20,000 arrobas per caballeria in the 1994-95 harvest. He said they also aim to continue the tasks of defense and recovery in cattle-raising, as well as the fuel conservation policy.
