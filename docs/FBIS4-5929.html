BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Pedro Margolles Villanueva, director general of the PRENSA LATINA of Cuba, and his companion on a visit to Korea. Present on the occasion were Vice-premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam, Director General of the Korean Central News Agency Kim Ki-yong and Cuban Ambassador E.P. to Korea Jose Ramon Rodriguez Varona. The guests warmly congratulated President Kim Il-song on his birthday and wished him a long life in good health. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this, conversed with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere and answered questions put by the director general. President Kim Il-song arranged a luncheon for them.
