Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Carlos Arce] [Text] We have a last minute report that the Guatemalan Armed Forces has implemented strict security measures along the border with Mexico. Guatemalan Armed Forces Spokesperson Edith Vargas indicated that a military contingent was mobilized to the common border to prevent the flow of weapons. Likewise, she deplored the events initiated by an armed group in the Mexican state of Chiapas. Meanwhile, Chiapas official spokespersons revealed that the Mexican Armed Forces practically have the situation in La Margaritas and Ocosingo under control. However, the situation is still confusing in Altamirano. In addition, official sources have refused to confirm or deny reports that Governor Absalon Castellanos has been kidnapped.
