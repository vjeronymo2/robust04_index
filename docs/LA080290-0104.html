August 2, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

AFFLUENT LATINOS: Orange County Latinos are among the most affluent in the nation, says American Demographics magazine (above).

More than 2.6 million Latinos in the United States now have household incomes of $50,000 or more, reflecting better than a 200% increase from 1972.
