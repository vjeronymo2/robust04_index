BFN [Text] The deputy prime minister of Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mr. Hong Song-nam, paid a courtesy call on the prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, in Islamabad today. During the meeting, the prime minister hoped that the visit of the deputy prime minister of Korea would result in increased economic cooperation between the two countries. Mr. Hong Song-nam conveyed greetings and best wishes of the president of North Korea, Mr. Kim Il-song, for the prime minister which she fully reciprocated.
