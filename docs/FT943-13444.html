940720 FT 20 JUL 94 / Funeral of Kim Il-sung drew 2m, says N Korea North Korea claimed that 2m people, almost a tenth of the population, attended funeral services yesterday in Pyongyang for President Kim Il-sung. A hearse carrying Mr Kim's black coffin, partially covered by the red flag of the ruling Korean Workers' party, was preceded by a huge portrait of Mr Kim and black limousines carrying senior officials. A funeral rite was held in front of the presidential palace, attended by Mr Kim Jong-il, the eldest son and designated successor. Report, Page 4
