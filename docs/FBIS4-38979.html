BFN [Text] The republican Union of Entrepreneurs has set up a special commission to collect information on enterprises which are made bankrupt and are subject to liquidation. The commission's task is to help the leaders of such enterprises to prepare documents on the enterprises' inability to function and their proposals on reorganization. The commission intends to address a letter to the president of Kyrgyzstan and establish cooperation with the state commission on reorganizing and liquidating unprofitable enterprises.
