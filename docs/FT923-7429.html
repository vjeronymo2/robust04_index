920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG staged a technical rally, the overall index rising 35, or 1.1 per cent to 3,174 following last week's 6.8 per cent drop. A firmer bullion price, a weaker financial rand and institutional bargain-hunting helped golds rise 28, or 3 per cent, to 964; industrials put on 30 to 4,003.
