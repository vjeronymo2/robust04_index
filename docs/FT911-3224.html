910425 FT 25 APR 91 / Observer: Steam age The high-tech Blue Arrow trial, expensively kitted out with electronic gadgetry to help the jury, continues to be undermined by steam-age technology. After a week when the air-conditioning wafted the smells first of boiling rice and then onion soup into the courtroom, the system packed up completely yesterday, prompting Mr Justice McKinnon to rule the consequent fug too much for the court to bear. Meanwhile, the judge's war of nerves with the noisy builders next door shows little sign of abating, despite his proposal on one occasion that the court clerk should deal with 'these rogues' by pulling the plug on their power supply.
