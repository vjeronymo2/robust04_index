December 1, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

In other college games:

The Master's 114, Redlands 94 -- Master's Coach Mel Hankinson earned his 300th victory at the collegiate level as The Master's advanced to an 8 p.m. championship tonight against UC San Diego in the Redlands tournament.

Tom Bruner led Master's (4-1) with 20 points and Jason Webster added 19.

BOYS' BASKETBALL

Taft 77, Juneau-Douglas 63 -- The Toreadors used a full-court press to build an early 13-4 lead and never looked back as they won a quarterfinal championship game in the Simi Valley tournament.

In another Simi Valley tournament game:

Kennedy 73, Sylmar 30 -- John Manning scored 15 points, Athesis Harris added 12 and Kenjuan Casey had 11 points for Kennedy in a consolation quarterfinal.

Glendale 73, Canoga Park 45 -- Glendale (1-1) blew open a close game in the third quarter of a consolation quarterfinal. GREG GUSS
