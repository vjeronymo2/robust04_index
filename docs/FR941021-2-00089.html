Social Security Administration Agency Forms Submitted to the Office of Management and Budget for Clearance Normally on Fridays, the Social Security Administration publishes a list of information collection packages that have been submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for clearance in compliance with P.L. 96&hyph;511, The Paperwork Reduction Act. The following clearance packages have been submitted to OMB since the last list was published in the Federal Register on Friday, October 7, 1994. (Call Reports Clearance Officer on (410) 965&hyph;4142 for copies of package) 1. Supplemental Security Income Reply Form_0960&hyph;NEW. The information on form SSA&hyph;417 will be used to contact an applicant, whose claim for Supplemental Security Income (SSI) payments was denied, if he or she checks ``yes'' on this form. The respondents will be applicants for SSI whose claim was denied. Number of Respondents: 60,000 Frequency of Response: 1 Average Burden Per Response: 2 minutes Estimated Annual Burden: 2,000 hours 2. The information on form HA&hyph;4486 is used by the Social Security Administration to update the work background and medical history of an individual whose claim for benefits based on disability has been denied and who requested a hearing.
