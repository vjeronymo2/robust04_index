Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] The news sources of the Islamic Rebirth Party reported that Tajik Mojahedin have been carrying out combat operations inside Kulyab Oblast and other regions of the republic for a long time. In some areas they have infiltrated from three to five kilometres deep into the republic. The Tajik Mojahedin carried out a successful operation in the village of Sarygor, Shurabadskiy rayon, Kulyab Oblast, yesterday, reports the source. Government forces that have been supported by Russian troops have suffered big losses in the course of this operation. Seven servicemen of the Communist regime have been killed by the Tajik Mojahedin as a result of this attack.
