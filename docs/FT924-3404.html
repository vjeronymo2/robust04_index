921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Compco Compco Holdings, the property investment and development company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 318,860 in the six months to September 25, an increase of 33 per cent on last time's Pounds 239,860. Mr David Pickford, chairman, said that net rental income had continued to grow - up from Pounds 804,813 to Pounds 1.05m - despite the difficult economic climate. Earnings per share worked through at 8.21p against 7.05p.
