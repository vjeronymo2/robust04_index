Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina affirmed today in Harare that the greatest satisfaction for our friends in Zimbabwe is not the material aid but what they do in the human and emotional sense for Cuba. During a meeting with the leadership of the Cuba- Zimbabwe Friendship Association, Robaina stressed the [words indistinct] of the Cuban people to confront difficulties. Robaina also engaged in a fraternal dialogue with Cubans who work and live in Zimbabwe, and he conducted a work meeting with the chiefs of the diplomatic and cooperation missions accredited in Zimbabwe. Next, Robaina will visit Zambia and Namibia.
