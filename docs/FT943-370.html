940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Filtronic prospectus The pathfinder prospectus has been published for Filtronic Comtek, a manufacturer of sophisticated micro-components for the mobile telecommunications industry, which is coming to the market next month. The prospectus is expected to be published on October 18 when the shares will be priced, with dealing commencing on October 24. The float, which is expected to carry a market capitalisation of Pounds 60m, is being undertaken through a private placing that will aim to raise Pounds 25m, of which Pounds 14m will be new money.
