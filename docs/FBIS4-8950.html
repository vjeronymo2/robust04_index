BFN [Text] The Czech Republic wants the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development [EBRD] to help, in particular in restructuring Czech industry. This was mentioned in today's speech delivered by Czech Finance Minister Ivan Kocarnik at the EBRD conference in St. Petersburg. According to the minister, the Czech Republic has already concluded the first stage of the transition to a market economy. However, the country's economy is now confronted with great posttransformation challenges. The minister also expressed his appreciation of the positive changes that have occurred in the EBRD since Jacques de Larossiere became its director.
