BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Excerpt] Moscow June 15 TASS -- [passage omitted] Commenting on the situation on the Korean Peninsula, following North Korea's decision to withdraw from the IAEA, [Vietnamese Foreign Minister] Nguyen Manh Cam told ITAR-TASS after his talks with [Russian Foreign Minister] Kozyrev that there is still hope that the conflict can be settled through negotiations.
