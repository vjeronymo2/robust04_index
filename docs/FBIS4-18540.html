BFN [Text] Ankara, April 5 (A.A.) -- Albanian President Sali Berisha, who is currently in Turkey, said on Tuesday [5 April] that Albania gave priority to friendly ties with Turkey. Berisha spoke at a luncheon in his honor. "Turkey and Albania have strong traditional, historical, and cultural relations," Berisha said. "Albanian people will always respect Turkish people and Gazi Mustafa Kemal Ataturk, the founder of modern Turkish Republic." "He (Ataturk) founded the contemporary Turkish Republic with his political and military genius, extraordinary courage, and determination." He also said Turkey is a strong, peaceful, and stable element in its region and played an important role in preserving peace, security, freedom and democracy within the framework of North Atlantic Treaty Organization. The guest president spoke on Turkey's efforts in the Balkans and said that Turkey can have a vital role in the crisis in Bosnia. "The concern that the war in Bosnia could spread to southern parts of the Balkans affected the improvement of the reforms that were put into practice in Albania," he said. Berisha said its impossible to provide stability in the Balkans without finding the true solution for Kosovo problem. He said his country welcomed the presence of Turkish soldiers in Bosnia. "Turkish soldiers are going to Bosnia for peace and will spend efforts to contribute to peace," he said.
