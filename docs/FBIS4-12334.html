BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Apr (MENA)--The PLO has announced that the 'Affula incident yesterday confirms the urgent need to achieve tangible progress to implement the interim agreement and to undertake practical measures to prove the seriousness of the peace process. In a statement by an official Palestinian spokesman in Cairo today, the PLO expressed its grief over the incident, which left several Israeli civilians dead. The organization noted that the series of actions that began with the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre in Hebron and subsequent massacres, most notably that in the Jabalyah refugee camp in the Gaza Strip, where Israeli military forces have killed PLO cadres, confirms the urgent need to establish peace. The spokesman said the PLO extends its condolences to the families of the civilian victims and hopes that all the parties concerned will assume their responsibility of safeguarding the peace process and achieving its goals to ensure a just and balanced solution guaranteeing genuine coexistence between the Palestinian and Israeli peoples.
