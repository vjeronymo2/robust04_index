931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / Sellafield gas power plant HYDRO-ELECTRIC, the Scottish power utility, and British Nuclear Fuels, the state-owned nuclear services company, are to build a small gas-fired power station at BNF's site at Sellafield, Cumbria. The 157MW combined heat and power plant is expected to become fully operational next year. The plant will be run by a jointly owned company, Fellside Heat and Power, using gas supplied by British Gas. It will supply steam and heat to Sellafield and about 100MW of electricity to Hydro-Electric.
