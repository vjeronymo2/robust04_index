July 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The nine worst teams in the National Football League last season will get an opportunity to go quarterback-shopping today in the supplemental draft.

Two of last year's top college quarterbacks, Timm Rosenbach of Washington State and Steve Walsh of Miami, are the only high-grade selections among 12 supplemental hopefuls who opted for the draft despite having collegiate eligibility remaining.
