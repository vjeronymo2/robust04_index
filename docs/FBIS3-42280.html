Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Feb (MENA)--The American University in Cairo [AUC] has confirmed that it has not received any information or specific evidence of threats against the American community in Egypt, including the AUC. This came in a memorandum the AUC president sent to the university staff, students, and employees based on a security statement released by the U.S. Embassy in Cairo on 3 February.
