931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Australia gives nod to T&L sweetener Australia has become the third country, following Canada and Russia, whose government has approved the use of sucralose, Tate & Lyle's new low-calorie sweetener, in a wide range of foods and drinks. Sucralose will be marketed in Australia under the Splenda brand name, by Johnson & Johnson Pacific Pty, a member of the Johnson & Johnson group.
