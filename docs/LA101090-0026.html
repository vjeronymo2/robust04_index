October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Parting Is Such Sweet Sorrow: Jonathan Miller, artistic director of the Old Vic Theater in London since January, 1988, has resigned after the theater's owner, David Mirvish, announced last weekend that the last two productions of the season would be scrapped because of financial difficulties. Miller, who had planned to leave at the end of the season in March, was quoted in the London Daily Telegraph as saying, "Now that the two plays have been cancelled I am leaving right away since there is no point in my staying." CLAUDIA PUIG
