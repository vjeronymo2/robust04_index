930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Thornton Pan-Euro Net asset value of Thornton Pan-European Investment Trust rose from 29.46p to 32.29p over the 12 months to December 31. By February 28 the value had improved to 35.01p. Net revenue for the year dipped to Pounds 181,532 (Pounds 220,962) for earnings of 0.91p (1.1p). An uncovered and maintained single distribution of 1p is recommended.
