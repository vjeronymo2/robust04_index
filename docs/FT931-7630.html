930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 14,439 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Assams met good competition at fully firm to dearer rates, while south Indians also realised improved prices. Bright Africans proved irregular, although price levels showed little change. Coloury mediums came to a firm market but thinner liquoring descriptions were 5 to 10p easier. Offshore: improved demand with brighter teas fully firm to dearer. The highest price realised this week was 213p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 180, good medium 155p, medium 140p, low medium 110p.
