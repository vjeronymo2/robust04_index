BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 19 May (EFE) -- Dominican President Joaquin Balaguer has a 27,385-vote lead over Jose Francisco Pena Gomez in Monday's elections. This is equivalent to a 0.95 percent lead, the Central Electoral Board (JCE) reported today. JCE Bulletin No. 20 indicated that Balaguer and his allies have 1,222,857 votes, while the coalition that backs Pena Gomez has 1,195,472 votes, a difference of 27,385 votes. These figures correspond to the count of 96.58 percent of the electoral tables. Only 325 tables are pending. According to the JCE bulletin, Balaguer obtained 42.36 percent of the valid votes against Pena Gomez' 41.41 percent.
