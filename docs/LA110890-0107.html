November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

UCI PLAYERS ADVANCE IN TENNIS COMPETITION

Andy Joe, Neel Grover and Paul Tracey of UC Irvine won in the singles qualifying round of the Intercollegiate Tennis Coaches Assn. Southern California men's tennis regionals Wednesday at UC Irvine .

Joe defeated Yoram Dambinskey of U.S. International, 6-2, 6-2; Grover defeated Misa Jevtic of Nevada-Las Vegas, 7-5, 6-1; and Tracey beat Rob Seward of the University of San Diego, 6-4, 6-0.
