930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: Fund payouts slip - At a glance INCOME Fund Analysis, which monitors the payouts of income unit trusts, has found that more than half of the funds covered paid lower distributions in the third quarter. Out of the 65 fund analysed, 34 made cuts, 10 kept payouts unchanged and 18 increased distributions (the remaining three switched to quarterly payments). Barclays Unicorn Income has managed to maintain its record of increasing its payout every year since 1981.
