920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / International Company News: Cummins buys Onan stake from Hawker CUMMINS ENGINE, the US diesel engines manufacturer, has acquired the remaining 36 per cent of Onan Corporation from Hawker Siddeley Overseas Investments of the UK for Dollars 64m, Reuter reports from Columbus. Onan, based in Fridley, Minn., makes and sells power generation equipment for consumer and industrial markets. Cummins has been majority owner since 1986. Cummins is to finance the purchase temporarily with short-term borrowings under its bank revolving credit agreement.
