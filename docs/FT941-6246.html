940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: Casio sees new products boost Casio Computer, the leading Japanese digital watchmaker, said it expected its parent profits to jump between 20 and 25 per cent to Y11.5bn (Dollars 109m) in 1994-95 as a result of planned new products, Reuter reports from Tokyo. A spokesman said parent sales were forecast to rise between 5 and 10 per cent to Y350bn.
