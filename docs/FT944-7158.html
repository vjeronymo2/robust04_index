941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Diamond market undermined by Russians says De Beers chief Russia continued to 'undermine the delicate equilibrium and confidence necessary for the diamond industry,' said Mr Julian Ogilvie Thompson, De Beers' chairman, last night. 'Competing interests (in Russia) have not adhered to the necessary disciplines and have been leaking diamonds on to the open market in contravention of the contracts with the Central Selling Organisation,' he complained. This placed Russia 'in the role of competitor with the other diamond producers'. But De Beers had dealt with similar problems in the past 60 years 'and on each occasion the CSO and the industry have emerged stronger than before'.
