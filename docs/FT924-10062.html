921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / World News In Brief: Reward increased for Rushdie's death Iran's reward for the killing of Satanic Verses author Salman Rushdie has been raised by an unspecified amount above the Dollars 2m already on offer by an Iranian religious foundation. The move is apparently in response to the writer's plea in Bonn last week for Germany to use its economic might to have the execution order cancelled.
