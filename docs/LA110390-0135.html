November 3, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

MOORPARK CLINCHES SHARE OF SOCCER TITLE

Shaun Christensen and Billy Kranz each scored three goals and Robert Harris added a pair as Moorpark College defeated host Santa Monica, 10-0, in a Western State Conference soccer match Friday night.

The victory clinched at least a share of the WSC title for Moorpark (15-0, 10-0 in conference play), with one conference match remaining.

Christian Costa and Sergio Pizano each had a goal for Moorpark, the sixth-ranked team in the state.
