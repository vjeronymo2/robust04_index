BFN [Text] Maputo, April 15 (XINHUA) -- China and Mozambique agreed here today that China will continue sending medical teams to Mozambique. A protocal on this issue was signed by Chinese Ambassador to Mozambique Mi Shiheng and Mozambican Deputy Health Minister Maria Campos on behalf of their respective government. Campos said after the signing ceremony that China has been sending medical teams to Mozambique since the Southern African country declared independence in 1975. The Chinese medical teams have played an active role in the development of the Mozambican health sector, he said. There are now eight Chinese medical teams in Mozambique. Under the new protocal, a ninth 11-member medical team will arrive in Mozambique this year.
