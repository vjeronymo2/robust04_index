940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / Lagos wants army measures lifted Nigeria has asked Britain and the US to lift measures against the armed forces imposed after the annulment of civilian elections last June, writes Paul Adams in Lagos. Maj-Gen Chris Alli, chief of army staff, said in Somalia that removing these measures would help Nigeria to fulfil its role in UN peace-keeping operations around the world. Nigeria's forces in both Liberia and Somalia have complained that they lack of pay and equipment.
