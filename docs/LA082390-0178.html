August 23, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: JURY CONSIDERS ASSAULT CHARGE AGAINST BRIDGES

A jury began its first full day of deliberations Wednesday in the assault retrial of actor Todd Bridges, charged with shooting a drug dealer eight times inside a cocaine rock house last year.

The Los Angeles Superior Court jury received the case late Tuesday, but met for only a few minutes and most likely did little more than select a foreman. The panel recessed for the evening and returned to court Wednesday morning.

If convicted, Bridges, 24, a former child star of the television sitcoms "Diff'rent Strokes" and "Fish," could be sentenced to up to four years in state prison.
