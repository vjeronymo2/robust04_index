921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / International Capital Markets: JP Morgan warrants aim at emerging markets JP MORGAN yesterday launched the first issue of warrants on an emerging markets bond index - a further step towards emerging market derivative products. Warrants on equity indices, American depository receipts, foreign bonds and local debt instruments are among the derivative products sold to investors. The issue consists of up to 250,000 one-year call warrants, worth about Dollars 60m, on the JP Morgan Emerging Markets Bond Index. They will be cash-settled. The index was introduced this year to track benchmark dollar-denominated bonds issued as part of the restructuring of countries' external bank debt. The index includes debt totalling Dollars 43bn issued by Mexico, Venezuela, Brazil, the Philippines and Nigeria.
