BFN [Text] Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada, president of the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP], stressed that we must thoroughly explore every possibility to achieve higher economic efficiency. Alarcon made this assertion at the closing of a meeting with deputies, Communist Party of Cuba and government officials, and presidents of the People's Councils in Las Tunas Province. Alarcon stressed that the difficulties of the special period will not be solved through nationally implemented measures alone. The ANPP special session convenes in less than a month to analyze measures aimed at streamlining Cuba's economy, as well as to make it more efficient.
