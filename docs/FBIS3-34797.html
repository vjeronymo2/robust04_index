Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister Brigadier 'Abd-al-Rahim Muhammad Husayn, said after his meeting with His Excellency the ambassador of the Kingdom of Morocco in Khartoum yesterday that the accused Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Halifi, who participated in the attack on worshippers in the al-Thawrah Mosque in the first quarter in Omdurman is not a Moroccan. He said investigations had indicated him to be of Najdi origin and that his family had emigrated in the 1920's to Tunisia and now resided in Libya. The minister said the accused had confessed participating in the hideous incident together with his colleagues. The minister said those named during the investigations were now being interrogated.
