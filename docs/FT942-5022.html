940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / Business and the Law: Mediation costs - Legal Briefs The Centre for Public Resources, the New York-based alternative dispute resolution organisation, has announced that business disputes involving Dollars 1.7bn were resolved successfully last year through the use of mediation and other forms of dispute resolution. CPR says 150 of the companies involved in mediation estimated direct legal cost savings of Dollars 37.5m, an average of Dollars 250,000 per company. Disputes resolved in 1993 by CPR mediators included anti-trust, bankruptcy, construction, employment, energy, environment, insurance, intellectual property, libel, professional fees and asset transfers.
