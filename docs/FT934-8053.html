931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: VTR more than doubled at Pounds 884,000 VTR, which provides post production facilities for television commercials, more than doubled pre-tax profits from Pounds 434,024 to Pounds 884,144 for the year to August 31. The result included a Pounds 110,915 profit from the sale of an outdated original edit suite. Earnings per share grew from 3.5p to 7.7p and a recommended final dividend of 2.55p makes a total of 3.75p, against 3.4p. A noticeable upturn in business for all group companies from the beginning of 1993 had continued - and strengthened - in the second half, with profits up from Pounds 158,000 to Pounds 682,000. Mr Philip Lovegrove, chairman of the USM-traded grouo, said the current year had started well and further progress was expected. Cash flow remained strong, but continued spending meant that gearing edged up to 76 per cent (72 per cent).
