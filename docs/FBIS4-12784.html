BFN [Text] Ozdem Sanberk, under secretary of the Turkish Foreign Ministry, and the accompanying delegation left Amman today after a three-day visit to Jordan. During the visit, Sanberk met with Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister, and Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs. Talks dealt with relations between the two friendly countries and developments of the peace process.
