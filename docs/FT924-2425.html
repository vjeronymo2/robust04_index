921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Big improvement for Parkland Parkland Textile, the woollen yarn, worsted cloth and clothing manufacturer, broke even at the trading level in the six months to August 28 but incurred a loss of Pounds 309,000 pre-tax after exceptional costs. However, that was a substantial reduction on the Pounds 1.37m loss incurred for the comparable period. Losses per share were 4.8p (13.4p) and the interim dividend is again 1p. Turnover rose nearly 23 per cent to Pounds 30.2m. That was a considerable improvement over the last two years but margins were still under pressure as a result of the poor retail marketplace, said Mr Denis Greenwood, chairman. Exceptional costs of Pounds 317,000 related to reorganisation of the garment division on to two sites.
