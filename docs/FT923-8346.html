920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Rea Brothers falls by 29% to Pounds 284,000 Rea Brothers, the banking group, reported a 29 per cent fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 402,000 to Pounds 284,000 in the first half of 1992. The result was helped by lower debt provisions of Pounds 90,000 (Pounds 400,000). Earnings per share came to 0.26p (0.44p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.25p.
