941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / Rules on foreign exchange revised The Inland Revenue yesterday published a legislative package for the modernisation of the tax rules governing foreign exchange gains and losses and financial instruments. The package is the result of three years of consultation with businesses, many of which were concerned at the diversity of present practice.
