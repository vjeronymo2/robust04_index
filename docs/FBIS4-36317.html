BFN [Text] At 1100 today, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi received Kamaluddin Ahmed, India's minister of state for trade [title as heard], and his delegation. The meeting was attended by Dr. 'Abd- al-Rahim al-Subay'i, minister of state for planning affairs, and the Indian ambassador in Damascus. Mr. Ahmed conveyed greetings from Indian Prime Minister Narasimha Rao to Mahmud al-Zu'bi, who asked the Indian minister to convey his greetings to the prime minister. Discussion during the meeting revolved around trade cooperation between the two friendly countries and means of improving it. This morning, Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, minister of economy and foreign trade, also received the Indian minister of state for trade and his delegation. Discussion revolved around means of improving, strengthening, and diversifying trade links between Syria and India and other issues listed on the agenda of the Syrian-Indian joint committee.
