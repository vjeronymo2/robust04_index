February 21, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

HOW THE CITY GREW

Since its incorporation in 1850, the City of Los Angeles has been gobbling up territory through annexation to expand its borders from 28 square miles to more than 464. The map below shows the original city and the parcels acquired along the way. The table shows the city's area and population at the end of each decade.
