941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / UK Company News: NB Smaller Cos NB Smaller Companies Trust, the former North British Canadian Investment, had a net asset value of 145.2p per share at August 31, up from 137.1p a year earlier, but lower than the year end figure of 154.5p. Net revenue for the six months edged ahead to Pounds 479,000 (Pounds 471,000), equivalent to earnings of 1.78p (1.74p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.94p.
