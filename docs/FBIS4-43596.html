BFN [Text] DISI [Democratic Rally] Chairman Ioannis Matsis said in London today that he carries a message from President Glavkos Kliridhis for German Chancellor Helmut Kohl related to Cyprus' application for accession to the EU [European Union]. The message will be conveyed to Chancellor Kohl during the European Popular Party meeting, which will take place tomorrow in Brussels and will be attended by leaders of European Christian Democratic parties. The meeting will deal with issues that will be raised during the EU Summit in Corfu on Friday [24 June]. Matsis, who went to London to attend a meeting of DISI's chapter in Britain, also said that Cyprus' effort for EU accession is the best possibility to promote the Cyprus issue in order to safeguard the safety of both communities and their human rights.
