BFN [Text] Baghdad, 8 Jun (INA)--Dr. Ahmad Murtada, minister of transport and communications, today met with Dr. Erich Ridi [name as transliterated], member of the German Bundestag and the chairman of its Financial Committee. During the meeting, Ridi expressed German companies' readiness to resume their activities in Iraq and to offer their expertise in all fields, especially in the fields of transport and communications. He said that Iraq is a friend of Germany, and that it is very useful to deal with it. He commended the relations of friendship between the German and Iraqi peoples. During the meeting, the two officials reviewed bilateral relations and ways of promoting them, especially in the fields of transport and communications.
