BFN [Russian Federation Presidential Edict No. 845, signed by President Yeltsin and dated 29 April 1994: "On the Retention of State Ownership of Oil Company Shares"] [Text] With a view to ensuring effective state control over the activities of the newly established oil companies, I decree that: The Russian Federation Government shall stipulate during the establishment of the Siberia and Far East Oil Company and the Eastern Oil Company that federal ownership of 51 percent of these companies' shares shall be retained for three years. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin Moscow, the Kremlin 29 April 1994 No. 845
