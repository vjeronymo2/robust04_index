Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The United States, Russia, and Ukraine will sign a tripartite agreement with regard to compensating Ukraine for the removal of its nuclear weapons and also security guarantees. This has been announced to journalists in Kiev by Ukraine President Leonid Kravchuk. In his words, reports Interfax, this will be an agreement which will not affect the interests of any state. He also confirmed that 17 SS-24 missiles have been stood down from combat duty. The president said that they cannot be used, even by Russia.
