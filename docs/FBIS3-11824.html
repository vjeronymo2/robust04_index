Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [TEXT] La Paz, 25 Mar (EFE) -- Carlos Sanchez Berzain, minister secretary general of the presidency, reported that Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada delayed until 26 March the installation of his new ministers following today's collective resignation of his cabinet. Sanchez Berzain said that President Sanchez is putting the final touches on his new cabinet during meetings he held today at his residence in Obrajes, a residential district in La Paz. Political sources said the possibility exists of Sanchez de Lozada appointing a new aide who would be in charge of implementing the Popular Participation project, which currently is being discussed in Congress.
