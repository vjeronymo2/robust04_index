January 11, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

UNREAD MAIL

How many times are we told to "write your congressman -- they care about what you say"?

Even though we scoff, I do write.

For the second time, I received a letter back from state Rep. Dana Rohrabacher (R-Lomita), thanking me for my letter, BUT, once again, he thanks me for a letter about something I didn't write. A case in point: I wrote him to stop sending military aid to El Salvador. His letter explained about how he voted "no" on his new salary raise.

I say, "Don't answer your mail if you don't read it."

EILEEN MURPHY

Huntington Beach
