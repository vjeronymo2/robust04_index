BFN [Text] Seoul, May 18, (YONHAP) -- Argentine Economic Minister Domingo Cavallo, saying that the business climate in his country is favorable, urged South Korean businessmen to invest in Argentina. "Now is a good time for Korean business enterprises to invest in Argentina," Cavallo told a press conference held Tuesday at Hyatt Hotel in Seoul. Argentina's economy is opening up speedily with 90 percent of state-run companies having been privatized, he said, adding: "In particular, we welcome Korean investment in automobile and electronics sectors." Earlier in the day, Cavallo and Korean Vice Foreign Minister Hong Sun-yong signed an investment guarantee agreement. He also met with leaders of Korean business groups including Hyundai, Samsung, Daewoo and Lucky-Goldstar and asked them to invest more in the South American country.
