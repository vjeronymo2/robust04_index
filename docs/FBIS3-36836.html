Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation in Armenia, the problem of Nagorno-Karabakh, and the state of bilateral relations were discussed in London on Wednesday by Great Britain's Prime Minister John Major and Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan, who has arrived there on an official visit. The English side expressed interest in a settlement of the situation in Nagorno-Karabakh as soon as possible. As reported to ITAR-TASS at Major's office, three bilateral agreements were signed on the same day--a declaration on friendship and cooperation, an agreement on air communications between the two countries, and an agreement on cultural exchanges.
