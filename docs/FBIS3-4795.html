Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Fukuoka, March 1 KYODO -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji arrived in Fukuoka on Tuesday [1 March] evening, hailing the potential of economic ties between China and Japan's southwestern region.
