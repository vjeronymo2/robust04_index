931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Hearing set for Drogoul Mr Christopher Drogoul, former manager of the Atlanta branch of Banca Nazionale del Lavoro, will be sentenced next week, a federal judge said yesterday, AP reports from Atlanta. The hearing was scheduled to begin on December 6 and is expected to last all week. Mr Drogoul pleaded guilty to one count of wire fraud and two counts of making false statements in connection with the bank's illegal arms to Iraq.
