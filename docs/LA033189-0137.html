March 31, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

A Feb. 25 story erroneously reported that Los Angeles attorney Albert M. Bernstein was charged with extortion for "financial gain" in the disappearance of millionaire Jean Fay Drexler. In fact, Bernstein was charged with one felony count of having posed "as one who had the ability to gain" the woman's release and "offered to do so for consideration."
