Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 16 Feb (DPA) -- The Guatemalan Army is allegedly preparing to launch "strong military offensives" in the main conflict areas of that Central American country, according to statements made today in the Mexican capital by the Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity (URNG). The coalition of guerrilla organizations did not provide details of the alleged military plans but said they are displaying "the absence of political will at the military staff" regarding the next round of peace negotiations scheduled for March. While accusing the Army of seeking a "military solution" to the Guatemalan crisis, the URNG reiterated "only through negotiations can a firm and lasting peace with social justice be built in Guatemala."
