January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ON THE RIGHT TRACK

My husband and I and several friends traveled to New Orleans with Rodney Pick of Sentimental Rail Journeys. The vintage rail cars are wonderful and Sentimental Rail Journeys does a great job of taking care of everybody. Food aboard the train also is excellent and accommodations and tours are the best! We highly recommend Sentimental Rail Journeys, P.O. Box 4574, Thousand Oaks 91360, (805) 499-9306.

MRS. LYNN FAIRBROTHER

La Mirada
