Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 28 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] on March 28 sent a message of greetings to the Central Executive Committee of the United Workers Party of Saint Lucia on the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the party. The United Workers Party of Saint Lucia has made great progress in the efforts to achieve the political and economic stability of the country and promote the well-being of the people for the past 30 years, said the message. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two parties will develop more favourably in the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the message wished the party success in the work to strengthen itself and achieve prosperity of the country.
