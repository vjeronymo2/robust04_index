BFN [Unattributed report: "U.S. Delegation in Macedonia"] [Text] A U.S. military delegation, led by the Deputy Chief-of-Staff of the U.S. Army, Admiral William Owens, arrived today on an official visit to Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. Admiral Owens will meet with President Gligorov, Macedonian Army Chief-of-Staff General Dragoljub Bocinov, and UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] representatives.
