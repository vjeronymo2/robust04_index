920323 FT 23 MAR 92 / Advice in Balpa election LEADERS of the Balpa airline pilots' union are recommending their 5,500 members to elect Mr Chris Darke, a national officer of the MSF general technical union, as their next general secretary. Mr Darke, 42, a former communist who says he is left of centre in the Labour Party, looks after MSF's members in the chemicals industry. The post became vacant on the death of Mr Mark Young.
