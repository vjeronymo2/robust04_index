April 27, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

REBEL ATTACK KILLS 23 IN KABUL

Rebels pounded Kabul with more than 50 rockets today, killing 23 people and wounding 60 in a daylong attack on the 11th anniversary of the 1978 military coup that brought President Najibullah's Soviet-backed party to power, official radio said.

Najibullah ignored the rocket attack to make a defiant speech ridiculing forecasts that his government would fall to guerrillas, who are armed by the United States, China and Arab nations and based mainly in Pakistan.
