921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / St Mellion Golf and Country Club fined The owners of St Mellion Golf and Country Club in Cornwall were yesterday ordered to pay fines and costs totalling Pounds 800 for illegally taking water for use in the grounds. Mr Martin Bond and Mr Herman Bond, prosecuted by the National Rivers Authority, the environmental watchdog, pleaded guilty before magistrates at Liskeard to taking water without a licence. Each was fined Pounds 150 and ordered to pay Pounds 250 costs.
