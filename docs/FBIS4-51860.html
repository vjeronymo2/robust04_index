BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 1 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- A meeting was held in Tokyo on April 30 to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Kuwol Sobang Publishing House. Ho Chong-man, chief vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), in his speech said that the 40-year-long path traversed by the Kuwol Sobang Publishing House was the course of loyalty and patriotism along which the publishing house, with the edition, publication and dissemination of the press of chuche-type, firmly armed the officials of Chongnyon and Koreans in Japan with the revolutionary ideas of the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il, propagandized within and without the immortal chuche idea and the socialist homeland of chuche and greatly contributed to national reunification and the patriotic work of Chongnyon. Kim Won-taek, director of the publishing house, in his report said that workers of the publishing house would make all their efforts to fulfil their heavy duty as a standard-bearer of the struggle for the development of the movement of Koreans in Japan in depth and a guide in the ideological education work of Chongnyon under the uplifted banner of the great chuche idea in response to the teachings of the great leader. The meeting adopted a letter to Marshal Kim Chong-il.
