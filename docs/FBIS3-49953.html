Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] At its session today the Croatian Government supported a declaration signed yesterday by the Republic of Croatia and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia and concluded that it represented important and great headway towards the normalization of relations between the Serbian and the Croatian sides. Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ivo Sanader stressed that reactions by the international community were favorable, which also confirmed that President Tudjman's peace initiative was giving positive results.
