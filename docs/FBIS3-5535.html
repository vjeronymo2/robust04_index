Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] In response to Prime Minister Hosokawa's decision to put off a cabinet reshuffle, Chief Cabinet Secretary Takemura met with the prime minister at the latter's official residence on the evening of 2 March. During the meeting, the chief cabinet secretary told the prime minister: "I am sorry for causing various troubles. I would like to solicit your continued favors." In this way, the chief cabinet secretary expressed to the prime minister his intention to support the latter as chief cabinet secretary. The prime minister responded, "You and I work in the same office and your apology sounds awkward."
