February 5, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

SHARK BLAMED FOR DEATH OF DIVER OFF THE COAST OF ITALY

A 20-foot-long shark attacked and killed an experienced scuba diver off the Mediterranean coast, news reports said Friday.

Luciano Costanzo, 46, was killed during a fishing outing with his 19-year-old son and a friend, the reports said.

Large dangerous sharks are rare in the Mediterranean.
