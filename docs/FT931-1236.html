930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / International Company News: Baltica sells insurer stake BALTICA, the Danish insurer, has sold its 5 per cent stake in Groupe Victoire, the French insurance group, at a loss of DKr579m (Dollars 92m), Reuter reports from Copenhagen. The Danish group said the shares were sold as part of the insurer's strategy of investing in its core operations. The shares were sold on Wednesday. Baltica declined to name the buyer.
