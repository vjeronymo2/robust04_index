Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 29 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- President of Russia Boris Yeltsin today held a meeting in the Kremlin with Sergey Stepashin, the chairman of the Russian Federation Counterintelligence Service, the Russian president's press secretary reported. During the conversation, which lasted for 40 minutes, they discussed general issues of Russian security and also current business of the counterintelligence service. The Russian president stressed the need for a strong and professional special service defending the interests of Russia and the democratic state. He pointed out the need to enhance the efficiency of the counterintelligence service. Sergey Stepashin briefed the president on the progress and initial findings of the investigation into the appearance and publication of the so-called "Version No 1", about preparations for a coup d'etat, and those involved in this falsification. The Federal Counterintelligence Service and Procuracy have set up a joint group to complete the investigation. According to existing information, the Rssian Federation Procuracy has launched criminal proceedings in connection with the case for defamation (Article 130 of the Russian Federation Criminal Code).
