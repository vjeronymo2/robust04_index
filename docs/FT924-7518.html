921116 FT 16 NOV 92 / International Company News: Northwest Air downgraded AS speculation continues about Northwest Airlines, the fourth largest US carrier, Standard & Poor's, the big New York ratings agency, downgraded the company's senior unsecured notes from B minus to CCC plus. S&P said that the downgrade 'reflects further erosion of the equity base, due to ongoing losses and the bondholders' standing as unsecured creditors of the holding company, which places them in an essentially subordinate position'.
