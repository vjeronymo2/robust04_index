940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Davenport Vernon up 29% A good second half perform-ance left Davenport Vernon, the multi-franchise motor group, with pre-tax profits ahead by 29 per cent from Pounds 1.64m to Pounds 2.11m for the year ended September 30. Turnover expanded by 35.5 per cent to Pounds 154.6m against Pounds 114.1m. Earnings per share were 10p (8.1p) while the dividend is increased to 4.2p (4p) through a final payment of 2.7p (2.5p). Profits at halfway improved to Pounds 895,000 (Pounds 805,000) but the year-end result was boosted by a second half rise of 45 per cent. Of the sales increase, Pounds 35m came from continuing operations.
