EFFECTIVE DATE: October 27, 1994. 7 CFR Part 457 Common Crop Insurance Regulations; Extra Long Staple Cotton Crop Insurance Provisions AGENCY: Federal Crop Insurance Corporation, USDA. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC) hereby adopts regulations for specific crop provisions to insure extra long staple (ELS) cotton. These provisions will supplement the Common Crop Insurance Policy (§457.8) which contains standard terms and conditions common to most crops. The intended effect of this rule is to move specific crop provisions for insuring ELS cotton to the Common Crop Insurance Policy (§457.8) for ease of use by the public and conformance among policy terms.
