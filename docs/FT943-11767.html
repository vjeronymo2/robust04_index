940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Standard Platforms turns in reduced loss Standard Platforms Holdings, the USM-quoted supplier of hardware and software to the document image processing and archival storage market, reported a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 35,640 for the six months to end-March, against Pounds 382,695. Its shares were suspended last March at 36p pending publication of its accounts. They resumed trading at 18p in May when the company announced a Pounds 450,000 rights issue, loan stock conversion, a capital reduction, a share option scheme and board changes. The shares closed unchanged yesterday at 12p.
