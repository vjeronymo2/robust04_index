March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski, who has seen his mythical $1,000 bankroll that he started the meeting with fall to $669.70, will try to get off to a winning start this week when he wagers $9 triples from Carrie's Glory in the sixth to On Easy Street and Proud Irish in the seventh to Real Cash and Magical Mile in the eighth.
