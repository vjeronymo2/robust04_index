BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe April 2 TASS -- Tajik opposition militants are carrying on tactics of armed provocations on the Tajik-Afghan border. On the night of March 31 to April 1, unidentified attackers fired from automatic weapons from a rear area on a border post of the Kazakh battalion of the peacekeeping force in Tajikistan in the sector of border picket no. 1 of the Khorog border-guarding detachment, a spokesman at the press centre of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan told ITAR-TASS on Saturday morning. The border guards returned the fire after which the attackers fled upon wounding a Kazakh serviceman. On Friday afternoon, in the sector of border picket no. 3 of the Panj border-guarding detachment, Tajik opposition militants, who had infiltrated from the territory of Afghanistan, fired on a border patrol. There were no casualities among border guards. The situation on the Tajik-Afghan border remained relatively calm on Friday night and Saturday morning.
