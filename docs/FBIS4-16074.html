BFN [Text] At the invitation of the Kazakh Institute of Strategic Research under the president of the republic, a delegation of the North Atlantic Assembly, a parliamentary organization of NATO member-countries, is currently on a visit to Almaty. The delegation is led by the chairman of the subcommittee for economic cooperation and convergence between the East and the West, Zhirardgo. The guests are interested in the progress of the economic reform and privatization in Kazakhstan, development of democracy, foreign and economic policy, and issues relating to the partnership of the republic with NATO member states. The European parliamentarians will meet deputies of the Supreme Kenges, heads of the security council, ministries of foreign affairs, defense, economy, and justice, the State Property Committee, and the National Bank. They will also visit one of the farm units in Almaty Oblast and the privatized joint stock amalgamation Merey. A round table discussion will be held at the Institute of Strategic Research with participation of its staff and the delegation members.
