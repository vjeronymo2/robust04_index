920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Chieftain makes small advance Taxable profits at Chieftain Group, the USM-quoted supplier of specialist insulation and fireproofing services, showed a small advance in 1991. Profits rose from Pounds 1.04m to Pounds 1.06m, struck on turnover Pounds 2m ahead at Pounds 14.4m. Mr Peter Wardle, chairman and joint managing director, said the group had continued with its strict cash control policy and had ended the year with a cash balance of Pounds 948,000 (Pounds 450,000). In spite of still weak demand, the order book remained firm, substantially unchanged at Pounds 15.2m. Earnings increased to 8.27p (7.79p) per share and a final dividend of 3p is proposed for a total of 4.9p (4.6p).
