Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] In the meeting in Tehran between Georgian Foreign Minister Aleksandr Chikvaidze and Majles Deputy Speaker Rowhani, the two sides exchanged views on issues of interest to both countries and stressed the need for sincere cooperation among the nations of the region. Rowhani referred to the issue of cooperation between the Caspian Sea littoral states and said: The countries of this region are able to resolve their own issues and do not need the presence of foreigners. He also stressed the importance of diplomatic solutions for ending the present disputes and declared the readiness of the Islamic Republic of Iran to mediate in Georgia's internal disputes. The Georgian foreign minister, in turn, referred to the existing situation in his country and the parties involoved in the dispute, as well as to Georgia's decision to improve its relations with the countries of the region. He said: Expansion of ties with a large and strong country such as Iran is a cause for pride. He then alluded to the historial and cultural bonds between the two countries, and expressed the willingess of Georgia to further broaden its relations with the Islamic Republic of Iran and benefit from Iran's parliamentary experiences.
