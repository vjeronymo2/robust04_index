930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: BWD Securities declines to Pounds 1.74m BWD Securities, the stockbroking and personal asset management group, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 2.07m to Pounds 1.74m over the year to November 30. The dividend is raised from 3p to 3.2p, with a final of 1.9p, payable from earnings of 6.4p (7.6p) per share.
