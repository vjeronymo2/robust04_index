910417 FT 17 APR 91 / Observer: Diplomacy Students of international financial protocol are puzzling over the fact that Wim Kok, the Dutch finance minister, has been chairing the various meetings of Jacques Attali's European Bank for Reconstruction and Development this week. Is this the work of a sensitive diplomat, or someone wanting to underline a point? Don't forget Attali's main rival for the coveted EBRD presidency was another Dutch finance minister, Onno Ruding.
