BFN [Text] The inflation rate for April was announced as 24.7 percent. The annual rate of inflation increased by 107.4 percent since April last year. According to a statement issued by the State Statistics Institute, the consumer prices increased by 24.7 percent and the wholesale prices by 32.8 percent during the month of April. This brings the annual increase in the consumer price index to 107.4 percent and the annual increase in the wholesale price index to 125.3 percent.
