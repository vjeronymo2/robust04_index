BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow, 21 Jun -- On Monday Boris Yeltsin approved the statute of a commission for higher military posts, higher military ranks, and higher special ranks of the council for cadre policy under the Russian president. The commission's task is to examine the representations by heads of federal bodies of executive authority of the Russian president on appointing servicemen to top military posts and on relieving them of these posts in the Defense Ministry, the Interior Ministry, bodies and troops of the federal agency of government communications and information, counterintelligence bodies, the main directorate for protection, the security service of the Russian president, the foreign intelligence service, and other formations.
