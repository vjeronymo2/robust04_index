940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Analysts banking on Abbey National surge - Other Companies Abbey National, the home loans and banking group, announces its interim results today. Analysts' forecasts for pre-tax profits range from Pounds 400m-440m, well ahead of the Pounds 301m announced last year. There is more disagreement about the interim dividend, with forecasts varying from 4.6p to 5.6p, against 4.15p.
