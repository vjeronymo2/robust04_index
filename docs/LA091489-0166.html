September 14, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

IRVINE SHUTS OUT CHAPMAN IN SOCCER

Darren Bedolla and John Gunderson scored in the second half to lead visiting UC Irvine to a 2-0 nonconference men's soccer victory over Chapman College Wednesday. Irvine's Jeff Pene assisted on both goals.

Irvine is 2-3, Chapman is 1-2-2.

In nonconference volleyball:

UC Irvine 3, Virginia Tech 0 -- Kim Collins had 13 kills, leading visiting Irvine to a 15-3, 15-6, 15-7 sweep. Irvine is 4-2; Virginia Tech 8-3.
