November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CASE AGAINST PROSTITUTE WITH AIDS VIRUS RESET

A convicted male prostitute was ordered to stand trial in Los Angeles Friday on a felony charge of continuing to solicit sex for money after being informed that he had tested positive for AIDS.

Los Angeles Municipal Court Judge Rand Schrader, who also chairs the Los Angeles County AIDS Commission, reversed an earlier decision and reinstated a case against Demitri Santangelo, 29, of Hollywood.

Schrader's ruling to dismiss the charge last month was overturned on appeal to Superior Court Judge Michael Tynan.
