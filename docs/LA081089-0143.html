August 10, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Units, a women's apparel chain, plans to open its third Orange County store later this month in Laguna Hills Mall.

Units sells brightly colored knitwear pieces designed to be mixed and matched, such as jumpsuits and bandeaux, which are tubular ribbons worn around the waist.

Other Units stores in Orange County are at MainPlace/Santa Ana and Brea Mall.

The Laguna Hills shop will be one of about 60 that Dallas-based Units plans to open this year, bringing the number of stores in the chain to at least 215 by the end of the year, the company said in a release. Units' parent company, Stinu Corp., is a subsidiary of J.C. Penney Co.
