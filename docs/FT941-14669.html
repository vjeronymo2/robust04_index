940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Central Motor Auctions static In difficult trading conditions Central Motors Auctions reported static pre-tax profits of Pounds 862,000 for the year to end-October, against Pounds 853,000. Auction proceeds for the USM-quoted company fell 7 per cent to Pounds 294.3m (Pounds 315.3m), but improved efficiencies enabled operating profits to rise 21 per cent to Pounds 1.16m (Pounds 960,000). There were, however, higher finance costs of Pounds 345,000 (Pounds 292,000) and lower disposals proceeds of Pounds 43,000 (Pounds 185,000). After a higher tax charge earnings per share fell to 4.52p (5.46p). The dividend is held at 4p with a proposed unchanged final of 3p.
