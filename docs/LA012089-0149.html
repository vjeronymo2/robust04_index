January 20, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Kathryn G. Thompson Development Co., an Irvine home builder, said it built 441 units last year and set a sales record of $89 million.

This year the company will open seven new neighborhoods in Laguna Audubon, an Aliso Viejo development master-planned by the Mission Viejo Co.
