Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 6 (KCNA) -- Papers here today dedicate signed articles to the 37th anniversary of the independence of Ghana. The Ghanaian people, who achieved the independence of the country on March 6, 1957, have striven to consolidate national independence and achieve the prosperity of the country, MINJU CHOSON in an article says, and continues: The Ghanaian Government, pursuing the non-alignment policy externally, is making efforts to establish a new fair international order against imperialism, colonialism and racism. Korea and Ghana are far away from each other geographically, but the two governments and peoples established and have long developed the relations of friendship and cooperation. The Korean people, believing that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will further strengthen in the future, sincerely wish the Ghanaian people greater success in their work to develop the economy and build a peaceful and stable country.
