BFN [Text] In accordance with the coordinated agreement reached between the two countries on the repatriation of those who had illegally left Myanmar [Burma] for Bangladesh, a delegation led by Camp Supervisor Mr. Akhtar Hossain from the country on the other side [Bangladesh], transferred 225 returnees from 53 households to our Kanyinchaung reception center on the morning of 20 April. The returnees were brougt in six schooners.
