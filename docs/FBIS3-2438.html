Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] A preparatory meeting for the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] was held in the Great Hall of the People today. The meeting was presided over by Chairman Qiao Shi, who announced: Preparations for the Second Session of the Eighth NPC have been completed. In line with the decision of the fifth meeting of the Eighth NPC Standing Committee, the second NPC session will open as scheduled in Beijing on 10 March and is expected to last 13 days. In his speech, Chairman Qiao Shi said: The Second Session of the Eighth NPC is an important meeting held at a time when China is undertaking the establishment of the socialist market economic structure and is advancing its modernization drive. Qiao Shi pointed out: Ensuring success in this session is of great significance for seizing opportunities, deepening the reform, opening wider to the outside world, promoting development, maintaining stability, and, while focusing on the central task of economic construction, advancing the socialist market economy, socialist democracy, and socialist spiritual civilization. He said: The guiding principle for this session is, under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the party's basic line, implementing in an all-round way the guidelines of the 14th party congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee; emancipating our minds, seeking truth from facts, striving to make the session one for promoting democracy and unity, for exchanging experiences in a pragmatic way, and for advancing by blazing new trails; and further mobilizing people of all nationalities in the country to work hard as one to accelerate the establishment of the socialist market economic structure, to expand socialist democracy and improve the legal system, to maintain the sustained, rapid, and healthy development of the national economy, to safeguard political stability, to promote all-round social progress, and to win victories for reform, opening up, and the socialist modernization drive. Qiao Shi said: The session will conscientiously implement the principle of democratic centralism, draw on collective wisdom and absorb all useful ideas, and pool all efforts of all sectors to ensure success. A total of 2,553 deputies attended the preparatory meeting. After hearing the explanations made by Chairman Qiao Shi about the relevant draft documents, the participants elected a presidium and a secretary general of the session. The presidium consists of 163 people, and Tian Jiyun was elected secretary general of the session. The preparatory meeting also adopted the agenda for the Second Session of the Eighth NPC, the main items of which include the following: -- hearing and examining a report on government work; -- hearing and examining a report on the implementation of the 1993 national economic and social development plan and a draft plan for 1994; -- hearing and examining a report on the implementation of the 1993 state budget and a draft budget for 1994; -- examining a draft budget law and a draft decision of the NPC to empower the Xiamen city peoples' congress and its standing committee and the Xiamen city people's government with legislative power to enact laws and regulations for implementation in the Xiamen special economic zones; -- hearing and examining work reports of the NPC Standing Committee, the Supreme People's Court, and the Supreme People's Procuratorate; and -- electing new members to the Eighth NPC Standing Committee. Qiao Shi said: When the government report is being deliberated by the deputies in the current session, leaders of the State Council and its relevant ministries and commissions will attend the plenary meetings of the various delegations to hear the deputies' opinions.
