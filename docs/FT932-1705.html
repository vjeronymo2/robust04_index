930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Untitled item LABOUR'S determination to exploit Tory embarrassment over the donations from Mr Asil Nadir was underlined last night as a group of Labour MPs tabled two Commons motions relating to MPs' acceptance of expenses-paid trips to Turkish Cyprus. One of the motions expressed concern at one Conservative MP's admission that he was 'involved in accepting' all-expenses paid trips to 'the illegally occupied areas of Cyprus'. The other referred to 'recent revelations' showing the involvement of a significant number of MPs in visits.
