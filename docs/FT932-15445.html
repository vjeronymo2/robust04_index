930413 FT 13 APR 93 / International Capital Markets: Bonds rally Bond prices continued to rally yesterday on hopes of low inflation, with the benchmark 30-year government issue jumping almost another full point to 104 7/32 , pushing the yield down to 6.79 per cent. Investors rushed to buy bonds in response to the announcement on Friday, when markets were closed, that consumer prices rose 0.1 per cent in March
