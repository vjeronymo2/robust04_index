January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEW BOOKS: AROUND HOME;

PAPER PLEASURES BY FAITH SHANNON (WEIDENFELD & NICOLSON; 1987)

WHAT A TREAT: a handsomely illustrated art book that also has practical, easy-to-follow instructions for paper projects, including making paper from natural fibers; decorating, dyeing and marbling paper, even papier-mache, fancy folding and bookbinding. ($18.95) JUDITH SIMS
