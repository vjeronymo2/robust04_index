August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Wolfman Jack Is Back: Legendary radio personality Wolfman Jack returns to the air Saturday at midnight on KRLA 1110 AM. He will be heard every Saturday night from midnight to 6 a.m. Sunday. KRLA adds the Wolfman to a lineup of veteran deejays including Johnny Hayes and Humble Harve, in their effort to reinforce their role as the station with "the music and the DJs you grew up listening to." BETH KLEID
