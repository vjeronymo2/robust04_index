920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / British Gas image seen to improve BRITISH GAS has improved its image with its 17m customers, a survey by Audience Selection found. More than 1,000 people were questioned and 92 per cent were either 'very satisfied' or 'fairly satisfied' with British Gas services. The finding contrasts with 75 per cent expressing satisfaction in a nationwide survey by British Gas three years ago.
