940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Philips wins cable contract Philips, the Dutch electronics group, has won an ADollars 160m contract from Telecom Australia to act as systems integrator for a new fibre-optic and coaxial cable network it describes as a 'digital information superhighway,' writes David Brown from Amsterdam. The contract forms the first part of a ADollars 710m project, which will involve laying 10,000km of cable to 1.1m urban homes in Australia to deliver 67 pay TV channels in 1996.
