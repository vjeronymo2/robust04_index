940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / Observer: Making up What will Dr Mahathir Mohamad, the Malaysian prime minister, make of the departure for New York of Sunday Times editor Andrew Neil? Back in February when the Malaysians imposed a ban on giving government contracts to British companies after the Sunday Times made certain unflattering references, Neil was seen by the Malaysians as the principal villain of the piece. The question now is whether Dr Mahathir feels that with Neil's exit honour has been satisfied and normal business relations can be restored. While several British ministers have been on their diplomatic knees trying to placate Dr Mahathir, John Major has stayed aloof from the whole affair. It's no secret that the two PMs do not get along. The gossip in Kuala Lumpur is that Dr Mahathir is waiting to see if there's a new man in Downing Street before burying the hatchet.
