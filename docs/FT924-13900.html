921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Goodwin drops to Pounds 402,000 Pre-tax profit at Goodwin, mechanical and refractory engineer and metal processor, fell from Pounds 451,000 to Pounds 402,000 in the year ended April 30 1992. Turnover was static at Pounds 14.2m, while margins were affected by the recession, with production costs increased by higher interest rates. Earnings per share were 3.6p (4.6p) and the dividend is stepped up to 0.655p (0.63p).
