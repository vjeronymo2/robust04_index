Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The meeting between Prime Minister Carlo Azeglio Ciampi and Interior Minister Nicola Mancino lasted more than an hour. At the end of the meeting, which was held in Palazzo Chigi [prime minister's office], Mancino told journalists: "We talked about elections; we are in an electoral period." When asked if they had discussed other matters, Mancino replied: "We made a series of evaluations."
