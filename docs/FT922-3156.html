920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / Improvement seen in chemical sector TRADING conditions in the UK chemical industry have improved noticeably during the first quarter, according to Trade Indemnity, the credit insurance group. The improvement has been driven by exports and is in spite of depressed domestic market. More than 25 per cent of UK chemicals companies are still working below capacity, but about a third expect an improvement in over the coming months. Trade Indemnity's Quarterly Business Review, 12-34 Great Eastern St, London EC2A 3AX.
