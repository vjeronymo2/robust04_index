930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore Scotland net asset value up The net asset value of the split-capital Gartmore Scotland Investment Trust was 249p per capital share at July 31, up from 174.6p 12 months earlier. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 1.45m (Pounds 1.64m) for earnings of 10.7p (12.1p) per income share. A proposed final dividend of 2.8p brings the total for the year to 10p, down from 11.6p last time.
