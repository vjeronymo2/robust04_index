February 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SLOUCHING TOWARD THE MILLENNIUM

Your collection of predictions was interesting but largely superficial and insular. Mention of the interconnectedness of our country and the world was minimal, and nothing was written that addressed the basic problem: too many people. Too many people seeking the high standard of living now enjoyed by the few developed nations, and those few already too many in relation to consumption of world resources.

WILLARD OLNEY, Hesperia
