Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Dispatch by Randah Habib from Amman carried within the "Panorama" program] [Excerpt] Jordan's King Husayn has said that the killers of the Jordanian diplomat in Beirut, at the end of last month, belong to an extremist group that received orders from an Arab country. The king did not disclose the name of that country. An official Jordanian source, however, told our radio that the Arab country is Libya and that the group, which carried out the operation, is Fatah-the Revolutionary Council led by Abu-Nidal [Sabri al-Banna].
