920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / International Company News: Merrill to upgrade brokerage in Seoul MERRILL Lynch, the US brokerage, has obtained permission from the Korean ministry of finance to upgrade its Seoul representative office into a full branch, the sixth overseas securities house to do so, Reuter reports. The branch will provide Won10bn (USDollars 12m) in operating funds, allowing Merrill Lynch to engage only in broking.
