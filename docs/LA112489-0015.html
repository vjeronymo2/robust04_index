November 24, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

CS FULLERTON TO MEET LAMAR IN OPENER

Cal State Fullerton opens its basketball season against Lamar at 7:35 tonight at Titan Gym.

Fullerton has four starters back from last season's 16-13 team, led by forward Cedric Ceballos, who averaged 21 points and nine rebounds a game.

Lamar, coached by former Louisville player Tony Branch, was 12-16 last season, Branch's first as coach.
