Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Willy Melgar; from the "Telepress" newscast] [Text] President Ramiro de Leon Carpio has accepted Government Minister Arnoldo Ortiz Moscoso's resignation, which was tendered yesterday. General Marco Antonio Gonzalez Taracena today assumed office as chief of staff of defense. He replaces Gen. Jose Luis Quilo Ayuzo. Gen. Carlos Enrique Pineda Carranza has assumed office as deputy chief of staff of defense. Arnoldo Balconi Turcios has assumed office as army inspector general. The appointment of a replacement for the government minister is being studied. In addition, the president announced the appointment of a special commission to investigate the assassination of Jorge Carpio Nicolle. The commission will be made up of representatives of the Government and Defense Ministries, the Prosecutor General's Office, the Human Rights Ombudsman's Office, and the Archdiocese's Human Rights Office.
