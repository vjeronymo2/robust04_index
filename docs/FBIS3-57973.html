Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Inflation Confirmed at 4 Percent"] [Text] Rome, 30 Dec (ANSA) -- Inflation fell to an annual rate of 4 percent in December, ending the year at the lowest level since 1969, the national statistics bureau, ISTAT, confirmed Thursday [29 December]. ISTAT said preliminary figures taken in eight sample cities earlier this month and confirmed today showed consumer prices remained stock-still in December, bringing inflation to a 24-year low.
