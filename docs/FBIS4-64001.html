BFN [Report by Colonel Andrey Bondarenko of the Volga Military District Press Center: "Cossacks Going to Dniester Region"] [Text] Colonel General Georgiy Kondratyev, Russian deputy defense minister, has visited Totskiy garrison. He familiarized himself with the training of peacemaking troops of the 27th guards motorized infantry division to carry out missions in the Dniester region. In the meantime, three transports carrying personnel, equipment, and arms of the motorized infantry regiment named for the Don Cossacks, set off for the Dniester Region to relieve the 27th division subunits which are serving in the "trouble spot." There are 97 direct descendants of the Don Cossacks from Rostov-na-Donu and Rostov Oblast serving in this unit.
