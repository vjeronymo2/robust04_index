BFN [Text] The Juba political conference of peace forces convened today in the town of Juba and comes as a confirmation and reassurance of the peace process and territorial integrity within the federal system. The conference also confirms that the Sudanese people share a common destiny, reject violence and fighting, and that the rebel movement still has the chance to return to the mainstream of the homeland so that all the sons of the homeland could embark on development and social and economic reconstruction.
