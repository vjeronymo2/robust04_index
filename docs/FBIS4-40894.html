BFN [Text] Ukrainian Parliament speaker and candidate to the presidential post Aleksandr Moroz has denied reports by Ostankino TV company that he may remove his candidacy and support Leonid Kravchuk. "This is a canard of which I was informed last week," he told electors in Zaporozhye. "I respect all candidates to the presidential post, but I have no comrades-in-arms among them," he said.
