March 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

1989 ALL-STARS;

ALL-SOUTHEAST BOYS, GIRLS TEAMS NAMED

Earnest Killum of Lynwood High, who averaged 30 points and 7.5 rebounds a game, is the top scorer on The Times' All-Southeast boys prep all-star team.

In 60 varsity games over two years, Killum was never held under 10 points.

Killum's teammate, Juaquin Hawkins, a junior, is one of four underclassmen on the team.

Cerritos is the only other team with two representatives -- Scott Day and Mark Flick.

The All-Southeast girls team is led by three players who made the team a year ago -- Brenda Pantoja of Bell Gardens, Iesha Smith of Artesia and Angelina Marin of Glenn.

Smith is the top scorer on the team with a 27.2 average.

BOYS -- Earnest Killum (Lynwood), 6-4 1/2 senior, 30-point scoring average; Juaquin Hawkins (Lynwood), 6-5 junior, 11; Scott Day (Cerritos), 6-0 senior, 18; Mark Flick (Cerritos), 6-7 junior, 15; Brandon Titus (Gahr), 6-3 sophomore, 17.6; Paul White (California), 6-8 senior, 21; Ruben Oronoz (Whittier Christian), 6-5 junior, 17; Sam Sybesma, (Valley Christian), 6-2 senior, 17; Chris Beck (Brethren), 6-2 senior, 10; Jason Pietsch (La Mirada), 6-8 senior, 15.4

GIRLS -- Iesha Smith (Artesia), 5-9 senior, 27.2; Brenda Pantoja (Bell Gardens), 5-7 junior, 20; Michelle Esparza (El Rancho), 5-8 sophomore, 22.6; Angelina Marin (Glenn), 5-8 junior, 24.6; Claudia Castro (Montebello), 5-7 senior, 19; Kim Ikeda (Schurr), 5-2 senior, 18; Lori Klein (Valley Christian), 5-8 senior, 17; Nicole Teate (Bellflower), 5-8 senior, 17; Shaveta Collins (Gahr), 5-9 senior, 11; Amy Hughlett (Cerritos), 6-2 sophomore, 11.
