Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- By order of Ukrainian Minister of Defense Vitaliy Radetskyy, generals' posts that were established in the Cabinet of Ministers and large enterprises of the military industrial complex in August 1991 have been reduced from 40 to 29. During a speech summing up the results of courses of action taken for Ukraine's territorial defense, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk approved the step taken by Vitaliy Radetskyy. Radetskyy also gave an order to remove all reserve officers from the military register who have not sworn allegiance to Ukraine.
