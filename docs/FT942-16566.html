940409 FT 09 APR 94 / UK Company News: Black & Edgington Pounds 1.2m in red Black & Edgington, the USM-quoted group which provides portable buildings, tents and other structures for sporting occasions and exhibitions, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 1.2m for the six months to January 31 1994. The outcome was struck on turnover of Pounds 2.21m and compared with a profit of Pounds 704,000 from revenue of Pounds 3.79m last time. Losses per share emerged at 0.25p (0.14p earnings). The directors said, however, that the results had met their expectations and pointed out that the income from the Farnborough Air Show, which is a biennial event, would have its main impact in the second half.
