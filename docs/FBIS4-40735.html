BFN [Text] The Crimean Minister of Internal Affairs Major General Valeriy Kuznetsov told INTERFAX that in the small hours of 16 June an approximately 30-man strong element of the Ukrainian riot police arrived from base near Odessa in Crimea. The Crimean authorities had not been notified. According to Kuznetsov, Kiev kept massing forces in the region in violation of the Ukrainian bill on the division of powers between the Ukrainian and Crimean authorities.
