Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet's press service told the CONTACT INFORMATIONA AGENCY that Georgian Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia met with a delegation of commanders of the Transcaucasian border troops today. Viktor Zemtsov, commander in chief of the border troops, said that both he and his headquarters will be based in Stavropol and that Vyacheslav Chistousov has been appointed head of the Russian border troops in Georgia. On behalf of the Georgian Government, Otar Patsatsia handed Zemtsov a letter of thanks. Zemtsov expressed hope that the friendly relations between the Georgian and Russian border guards will continue.
