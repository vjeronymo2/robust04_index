BFN [Text] Any Russian citizen aged 18 and more can join the newly formed Russia's Democratic Choice party, if he or she shares its fundamental principles, observes its rules and does not belong to other political parties. These are the rules of the new party approved by the founding congress that ended in Moscow on Monday. It also approved as a whole the party program that should be finalized by the Council, a body that will lead the party between congresses due once in two years. In the council each territorial chapter shall be represented by one person plus one delegate for each 80,000 voters who cast their ballots for Russia's Choice in the parliamentary elections in December 1993. The council will consists of a total of 134 persons, according to the party chairman Yegor Gaydar. Closing the congress he said that many people expect the new organization to prevent extremists from coming to power, defend the right of citizens to a free and happy life, "a deserving future of Russia".
