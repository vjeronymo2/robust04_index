Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 16 Mar -- Estonian President Lennart Meri's delegation and Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and government officials have held official meetings and negotiations. Regarding the results of the negotiations, the sides signed agreements on international and cultural cooperation. Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko and Culture Minister Ivan Dzyuba took part in the signing ceremony. The Estonian president also met the Ukrainian Supreme Council chairman [Ivan Plyushch]. Ivan Plyushch emphasized the timeliness of the Estonian president's visit, as Ukraine is currently at the stage of establishing new international relations and the world's attitude toward Ukraine is improving.
