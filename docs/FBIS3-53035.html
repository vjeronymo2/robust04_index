Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign direct investment has crossed 1600 crore rupees last year. An official release in New Delhi says this reflects the increasing response to the new industrial policy and the economic reforms. The investment inflows were a meagre 350 crores rupees during 1991. The year end review of the industrial scene shows that the infrastructure sector has achieved a near six percent growth rate. The release says the government has approved 37 projects proposed under the centrally sponsored scheme of growth centers.
