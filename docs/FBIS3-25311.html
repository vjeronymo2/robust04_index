Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Force is considering tougher steps to halt the deteriorating situation along the South African border with Mozambique and Swaziland. Major General Reg Otto of Eastern Transvaal Command said the activities of armed smugglers, stock thieves, and people who cross the border illegally might compel the Defense Force to switch the electrified border fence from alarm to deadly strength. Gen. Otto said the security situation was becoming a burden on local infrastructure and agriculture.
