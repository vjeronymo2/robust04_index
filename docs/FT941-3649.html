940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / International Company News: Hugo Boss lifts dividend after record result Hugo Boss, Germany's best known menswear manufacturer, yesterday raised its annual dividend after reporting record profits of DM76.5m (Dollars 45.6m). Ordinary shares will receive a DM23 dividend, up from DM21, while holders of preferential stock will receive DM24.50, also up DM2. The company, part of the Italian Marzotto group, said it was optimistic about good cash-flow figures and predicted sales would recover after a fall in turnover blamed on the recession. Overall group sales fell 7.8 per cent to DM846.3m.
