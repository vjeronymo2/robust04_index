940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / Cariplo chief in custody Mr Roberto Mazzotta, former head of Cariplo, Italy's largest savings bank, yesterday surrendered to the Italian authorities at a Swiss border crossing after an arrest warrant was issued last Monday by Milan magistrates on charges of alleged corruption, writes Robert Graham from Rome. Mr Mazzotta, who has temporarily stepped down from the chairmanship of the Milan-based institution, was briefly interrogated then jailed in the city's Opera prison. The authorities are investigating some 300 property transactions involving L1,000bn (Pounds 393m).
