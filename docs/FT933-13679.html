930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: Halifax share deals offer - At a glance Halifax building society is to offer an execution-only share dealing service to all customers from early August, and to selected customers from this week. The service will be operated by Sharelink and will provide telephone dealing service for quoted shares and other investments such as gilts. Commission of 1.6 per cent will be charged on orders up to Pounds 2,500, with a minimum of Pounds 21.50, plus stamp duty and stock exchange contract levy; 0.75 per cent up to Pounds 5,000, and 0.1 per cent above that. A postal selling service for popular shares, such as privatisation issues, will charge 1.25 per cent, minimum Pounds 15. Halifax will also offer an information service with reports on companies.
