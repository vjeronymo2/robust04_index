December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE DADDY TRACK

Those on the Daddy Track have the women's movement to thank for encouraging men to enjoy their families. The crux of the movement is freedom from stereotypes, for women and men. Everyone in the family benefits when fathers allow themselves to be both breadwinner and care giver.

It's been said before: Few people, on their deathbed, would say, "I wish I'd spent more time at the office."

KAREN BERG MARKS, Beverly Hills
