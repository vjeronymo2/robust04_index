BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Several hours ago, a terrorist act was perpetrated in Moscow. Our correspondent D. Matyushin has just returned from the location. [Matyushin] There was a powerful explosion this afternoon at about 0500 hours, near Paveletskiy railway station, alongside the famous Logovaz company. Eyewitnesses stated that as Logovaz General Director Boris Berezovskiy was driving his car out of the gates, a nearby car stuffed with explosives blew up. The driver was killed and the director general's bodyguard was badly wounded. Boris Berezovskiy himself received burns to his face. He was in a state of shock and was taken to the hospital. A driver who was passing by at the time of the explosion was also hurt. The exploded empty car had Lithuanian license plates and a remote control device had been placed in it. According to Yuriy Kolpakov, head of the department of city procuracy, it was a planned attempt on the life of the director of Logovaz. In Moscow alone in the past six months there have been 10 such assassination attempts. [Video shows badly damaged car, people milling around street]
