BFN [Text] Tama, Suhaj, 3 Jun (MENA)-- Four terrorists were killed in the area of Naj' Awlad Qasim in al-Hima village, Tama urban center in the governorate of Suhaj, early this morning. A force of the State Security Detectives accompanied by the Central Security Forces of the Suhaj Security Directorate, surrounded the terrorists' hideout. The terrorists were killed in an exchange of fire with the force. An officer and three soldiers from the force that stormed the terrorists' hideout were wounded during the exchange. Officials from the Suhaj Security Directorate and the criminology laboratory went to the scene of the incident to inspect and the Prosecutor's office was told to launch an investigation.
