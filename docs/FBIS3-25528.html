Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council, arrived in Asmara at noon today on a visit to the State of Eritrea. During his visit he will hold talks on bilateral relations and ways of bolstering them, in addition to developments in the Horn of Africa. He was received at Asmara Airport by Isayas Afewerki, president of the State of Eritrea. [passage omitted] Immediately after his arrival, the Presidential Council chairman held talks with President Afewerki on issues of mutual interest and means of bolstering bilateral cooperation. Developments in the Horn of Africa and international issues of mutual concern were also discussed. Discussions also tackled developments in our country in light of the document of accord concluded by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee, which is due to be signed by the concerned parties in the Jordanian capital, Amman, next Sunday. The brother Presidential Council chairman expressed his appreciation for the good offices extended by Eritrea so that our country can overcome the difficult challenges it faces as a result of the political crisis. He said the accord document will be signed in the Jordanian capital, Amman, on Sunday. He expressed hope that, after the signing of the accord, all will move to the stage of practical implementation in a manner that will bolster Yemen's unity march, our democratic policies, and stability in our country.
