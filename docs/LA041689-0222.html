April 16, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

TRAFFIC SITUATION IN ORANGE COUNTY

I believe that there are some people driving around in Orange County who shouldn't have a license.

The traffic has grown tremendously all over Orange County. Coming home on the freeway, or even the side streets, is almost like a nightmare. No one drives the speed limit any more. No one uses signals when turning or changing lanes. And people are always driving into the bike lane before they plan to turn. And people wonder why there are so many accidents.

If everyone just learned to be patient and accept the traffic, there would probably be less-stressful people and accidents. Leave a little earlier. Don't speed and jeopardize others because you're running late. Be cautious, use your signals correctly, and stay out of the bike lane. Just be courteous to other drivers because the streets are just as much theirs as yours.

JILL SADEY

Mission Viejo
