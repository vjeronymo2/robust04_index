930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Observer: Driving force Legend already has it that while Bill Clinton was out driving with his wife before the inauguration, he pulled into a busy filling station for fuel. Hillary, thinking herself less likely to draw unwelcome attention, got out and filled up then went to one of the paybooths where, to her husband's annoyance, she spent a long time chatting familiarly to the man at the till. 'What a coincidence,' she said on returning. 'We were at college together - in fact, I dated him until I met you.' 'So if I hadn't come along, you'd now be married to a gas station attendant,' said her husband. 'No,' she replied, 'I'd be married to the president of the US.'
