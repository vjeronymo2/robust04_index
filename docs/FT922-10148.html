920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Markets (At a glance): Share dealing links stepped up Stockbrokers Wise Speke has stepped up its share dealing links with the Skipton and Leeds building societies. Leeds is offering postal and telephone share selling service for the privatised companies, starting at Pounds 9.95 per sale. Deals over Pounds 2,500 bear a commission of 0.75 per cent. For non-privatised shares, the minimum is Pounds 19.95 and the rate for deals over Pounds 2,500 is 1.25 per cent. The service is only available to Leeds savers. Skipton has a full dealing service for shares, gilts and other fixed interest stock. A Pounds 5 fee obtains life membership for the service; after that the minimum commission is Pounds 15, with 1 per cent for deals between Pounds 1,500 and Pounds 5,000 and 0.1 per cent for additional sums over Pounds 5,000. The dealing helplines are 0532-347555 for the Leeds and 0532-444095 for the Skipton.
