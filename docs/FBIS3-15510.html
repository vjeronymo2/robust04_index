Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow, 5 Mar -- On 9 March, after the holiday, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy intends to submit an appeal to the Supreme Court against the decision of the Shchelkovo Rayon court which declared invalid the elections to the State Duma for that constituency. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from Aleksey Churilov, assistant to the leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia [LDPR] parliamentary grouping. Let us recall that the ten-day period allowed by law for the submission of appeals expires on 9 March and after this the court decision comes into force. When asked how Vladimir Zhirinovskiy had reacted to the decision to deprive him of his deputy's mandate, the LDPR leader's assistant replied: "negatively".
