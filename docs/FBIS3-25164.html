Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lagos, 8 Feb. (NAN/PANA)--The minister of petroleum and mineral resources, Don Etiebet, left Lagos on Tuesday [8 February] for a one-week visit to Saudi Arabia and Qatar to seek ways of increasing world crude oil prices. Etiebet will meet his Saudi counterpart, Hisham Muhyi-al-Din Nazir, and OPEC President 'Abdallah Bin-Hamad al-'Attiyah, who is also the minister of energy and industry in Qatar. Nigeria, Saudi Arabia and Qatar are members of OPEC. Etiebet said the trip had become necessary in view of the "ridiculous prices" of crude oil and called for measures to shore up the prices.
