BFN [Text] Klerksdorp May 12 SAPA -- The African National Congress' Western Transvaal region on Thursday [12 May] welcomed the verdict handed down on six rightwingers sentenced to death for the killing of four black people on the West Rand last year. A statement issued on Thursday said: "Although the ANC is opposed to capital punishment, we welcome the verdict handed down by the court and hope that this will serve as a deterrent to other rightwingers who were bent on creating havoc in this province."
