BFN [Text] The Palestinian security forces in the Gaza Strip have detained an Islamic Jihad activist. According to Islamic leaders in Gaza the detainee, Hani al-'Abd, is a journalist working in a local communications center which is connected with Islamic Jihad. Islamic Jihad people in Gaza say that Palestinian policemen searched for al-'Abd several days ago. Since he was not at home, he came voluntarily to the police headquarters in Gaza, where he was detained. Palestinian security elements in Gaza confirm that the man was detained. They refused to say why, but noted that he is the only person currently jailed in the Gaza prison.
