April 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CLOTHESLINES

Q: I'm looking for a swimsuit that will disguise my thick midsection.

What is the appropriate design for a 5-foot-6, size 12. -- P.A.T.

A: The blouson suit illustrated here is ideal for you. The top blouses over the midsection, disguising any excesses, and the bottom is hip-whittling black. By Robby Len, the suit is made of nylon, polyester and Lycra and comes with a white top and black bottom (as sketched), all black and all cobalt blue. It's $52, sizes 8 to 16. For retail outlets, write to Robby Len, 1411 Broadway, New York, N.Y. 10018, attention: Rachel Spasser. MARYLOU LUTHER
