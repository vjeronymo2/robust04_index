930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Murray Ventures At the end of the six months to January 31 net asset value at Murray Ventures climbed back to 312p against 286.2p at the end of the previous 12 months and 315.8p in the comparable first half. An unchanged interim dividend of 3.4p is payable on earnings per share of 6.87p (5p).
