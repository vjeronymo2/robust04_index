BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 25 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- It has been announced here that the hunger strike at the central square of the Ulaanbaatar city which lasts 292 hours and was called by the members and supporters of the Mongolian Democratic Association and the Union of Mongolian Students and the Union for New Progress having been demanding the resignation of the incumbent government of Mongolia has been stopped today. As is reported the leader of the Mongolian Democratic Association T. Elbegdorj declaring the decision on stopping the hunger strike said that here that "the present government is not worth dying for it." The events around the hunger strike has shown the actual face of the leadership of the government and the parliament. To stop the hunger strike, he noted in this respect, does not mean the completion of our struggle. It would be continuing and expanding up to an organization of a general strike and our demands are remaining in force. It is not the time yet to speak about who is the winner and who is the loser. We have attained a great success by that in accordance with our demands there has been laid down proper foundations for establishing free means of mass information media in Mongolian society.
