BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 4 Jun (NA) -- Buenos Aires Province Governor Eduardo Duhalde stated on 4 June that he is convinced that the provincial Constituent Assembly will approve the bill allowing Buenos Aires governors to be reelected. He made it clear, however, that if that is not the case, he is willing to be Menem's running mate in the 1995 presidential elections. Speaking in San Vicente, Duhalde said he does not want the people of Buenos Aires Province to push for his reelection because this issue will be negotiated at the provincial Constituent Assembly.
