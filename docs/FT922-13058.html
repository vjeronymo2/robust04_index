920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Chelmsford result In one edition of the Financial Times on April 10, the result of the election in the constituency of Chelmsford was given incorrectly. The correct figures are: Simon Burns (Conservative), 39,043; Hugh Nicholson (Liberal Democrat) 20,783; Dr Roy Chad (Labour) 10,010; Ms Eleanor Burgess (Green 769). Mr Burns retained the seat with an increased majority of 18,260 on a turnout of 84.61 per cent.
