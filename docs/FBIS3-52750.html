Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 6, IRNA -- Membership in General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT) can play a determining role in development and expansion of world economy, said a Foreign Ministry official here Wednesday [5 January]. Head of the international organizations department of Foreign Ministry, Bozorgmehr Ziaran, in an interview with the English daily `TEHRAN TIMES' published today commented on Iran's decision to apply for membership in GATT. "Formation of such a large multilateral trade organization after seven years of exhaustive discussions would strengthen the previous international ties between member states in such fields as agriculture, industry and textile," Ziaran added. On membership of the Islamic Republic of Iran in GATT, he said "It is a matter of utmost importance for our country's decision-makers to consider the articles of this world agreement deeply and meticulously before making the proper final choice. Such an important decision should be made in a way that will minimize the risk of any damage to our national economy," the Foreign Ministry official stressed. As for official stand on the issue, he said "Serious discussions have been going on for quite some time now, but the policy makers of the Islamic Republic have not yet announced their decision."
