Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kirill Ignatyev, the coordinator for the Russia's Choice faction in the State Duma, believes that Yegor Gaydar will not accept any proposal to return to the government, even if the president himself asks him. In an interview with Interfax, Ignatyev remarked that Gaydar would not agree to a proposal to take any posts in the State Duma. According to the faction's coordinator, Gaydar, as its leader, intends to concentrate his efforts on organizing the work of the faction, and also on creating an apparatus and regional structures for the Russia's Choice movement. Ignatyev said that the main aim of such work is to prepare for municipal elections scheduled for the coming months, in which Russia's Choice plans to achieve greater success than during the elections to the State Duma.
