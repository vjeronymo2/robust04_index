Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 8 KYODO -- The Fair Trade Commission (FTC) searched the offices of a contractors' association and over 20 member companies in Tochigi Prefecture on Tuesday [8 March] for evidence of suspected bid rigging, commission officials said. The companies are suspected of illegally conspiring to repeatedly fix bidding prices for municipal and prefectural public works projects. The prefectural association's branch office in Utsunomiya, north of Tokyo, which lists 146 engineering works companies, was subjected to the compulsory search. The searches were the first by the FTC since it investigated a contractors' association in Yamanashi Prefecture last May.
