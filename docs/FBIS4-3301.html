BFN [Text] Kuala lumpur, April 19 (XINHUA) -- Malaysian defense minister Najib Tun Razak today met visiting Chinese General Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese general is here to attend the Defense Services Asia '94 exhibition. During their meeting, Najib and Zhang agreed that reciprocal visits of the leaders of the two countries should continue in the interest of mutual understanding and friendship. Malaysia sees China as a close neighbor, said Najib. Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamed's visit to China last year further strengthened the bilateral relationship, he added. A growing number of Malaysians are visiting China, and they are getting excited about China's economic prospects, Najib said. When introducing China's defense policy to Najib, Zhang said China will not seek hegemony, commit aggression, align with any other country or build military bases abroad, adding the Chinese Army is totally committed to defense.
