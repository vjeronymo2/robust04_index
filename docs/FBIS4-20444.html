Identification of Glycoprotein Gene 40091007L Beijing BINGDU XUEBAO [CHINESE JOURNAL OF VIROLOGY] in Chinese Vol 9 No 3, Sep 93 pp 241-245 40091007L Beijing BINGDU XUEBAO [CHINESE JOURNAL OF VIROLOGY] Chinese CSO [English abstract of article by Guo Wanzhu [6753 8001 2691], Xie Yue [6200 6460], et al. of Sichuan Agricultural University, Yaan] [Text] A genomic library of Pseudorabies (PRV) M III strain was constructed by cloning the BamHI restriction enzyme fragments of PRV DNA into plasmids pBR322 and pUC18. The recombinant plasmids pSAG1-19, pSAG2A-19 were identified by restriction digestion, Southern blot hybridization and detection of antigenicity and immunogenicity of the fusion protein from transformed E. coli. The inserted BamHI-7 fragment, approximately 6.9 kb, of the recombinant plasmids contained the glycoprotein gp50 gene.
