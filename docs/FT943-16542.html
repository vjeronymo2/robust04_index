940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Observer: Mercury rising The heat is on the Press Association. PA has informed its customers that because no-one in Britain understands the Celsius measurement of temperature, it's reverting to Fahrenheit. What about returning to other basics, such as bringing back long-loved British coins like the farthing, the sixpenny piece and the florin? Any chance of the half-crown making a comeback? Come to think of it, Prince Charles has been doing just that.
