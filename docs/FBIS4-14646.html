BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 469] [Text] Vilnius, March 31, ELTA -- The Minister of Foreign Affairs Beniamino Andreatta, in his capacity as Chairman of the Conference for Security and Cooperation in Europe, warmly welcomes the positive completion of the negotiation for the agreement of friendship and cooperation between Lithuania and Poland and the subsequent initialling of the text. The agreement is an example of how it is possible to overcome the incomprehensions of the past in a frame of cooperation and in European spirit, in order to provide the bilateral relations between the neighbouring countries with a new positive dimension. Therefore, the agreement meets fully the fundamental principles of the CSCE and represents an important contribution to security and stability in the whole region.
