Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Washington, 14 Jan, CANA -- [passage omitted] Caribbean member nations of the OAS expressed their pleasure at seeing [U.S.] Ambassador Babbitt take her place as the new head of the [OAS Permanent] Council, and pledged their support for ensuring the success of her efforts during her term. Speaking on behalf of Caricom member nations of the OAS, St. Lucia's permanent representative, Ambassador Joseph Edmunds, expressed the hope that the new chairperson would provide over "some meaningful inter-American action" and would serve the interest of all the hemisphere in pursuit of peace, security, economic, and social well-being of the people of the hemisphere. Several delegates also welcomed the new vice chairman of the council, permanent representatives of Grenada, Ambassador Denneth Modeste.
