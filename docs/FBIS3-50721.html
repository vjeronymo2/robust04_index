Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Julian Rizo, who is a member of the Politburo and first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Camaguey Province, emphasized that encouraging efficiency, authority, and discipline constitute pillars for tackling the current phase of the work being done in Camaguey Province. Rizo said the watchword is to optimize. He insisted on the need to emphasize tasks such as self-sufficiency [in food], health, education, services, obtaining currency, sugar production, and others. Rizo spoke before over 1,200 political and government leaders from Camaguey municipality and, in his speech, alluded to the impact on Cuba's socioeconomic development of the intensification of the Yankee blockade and the collapse of the socialist bloc. He called on those present to collaborate in a determined way to convert the current work phase into a concrete reality.
