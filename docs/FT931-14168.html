930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Minister addresses Maastricht fears THERE was no prospect of the Maastricht agreement leading the European Community towards replacing a sense of national identity with a sense of belonging to a greater European ideal, Mr Tristan Garel-Jones, the foreign office minister, told MPs yesterday. As the Commons debated the specific competence of the Community in European culture at the beginning of the sixth day of the detailed committee discussion of the bill to approve the treaty, Mr Garel-Jones responded to fears voiced by Euro-sceptics by insisting that such concerns were groundless in the context of the agreement.
