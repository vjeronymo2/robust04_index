August 8, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

More Trouble for Stotler Group: The Chicago brokerage said it was served with a class-action shareholder lawsuit that charges that it gave false and misleading information about its financial condition in public filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Stotler was the subject of an involuntary bankruptcy filing last week by three creditors who said they were stuck with more than $1 million in unredeemed loans and investments.
