920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Ward Holdings loss nears Pounds 2m Ward Holdings, the housebuilding, commercial development and plant hire group, sank deeper into the red at the six months' stage and is omitting its interim dividend. At the pre-tax level losses for the half year to April 30 jumped from Pounds 397,000 to Pounds 1.8m. The shares fell 5p to 20p. Turnover totalled Pounds 11.6m (Pounds 14.3m), of which house sales accounted for Pounds 9.18m (Pounds 10m), plant hire for Pounds 830,000 (Pounds 797,000) and gross rental income and property sales for Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 2.99m). A tax credit of Pounds 583,000 (Pounds 135,000) left attributable losses at Pounds 1.21m (Pounds 262,000), equal to losses per share of 2.3p (0.5p). Shareholders received an interim dividend of 0.5p, but no final, last time.
