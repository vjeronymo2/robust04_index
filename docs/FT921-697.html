920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / Election 1992: FT Election Share watch THE STOCK market seemed to judge the Tories the net gainers from the exchanges between the two parties over Labour's health broadcast. Shares that might benefit from a Conservative victory rose by more than the FT-SE 100 index of big company shares, while stocks that might gain if Labour formed a government dropped back. The steady advantage to Labour shown in the first full week of the campaign has faded this week.
