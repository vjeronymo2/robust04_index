Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of 320 Dutch soldiers left the Netherlands today for the Croatian port city of Split. They are to relieve the Canadian and Ukrainian UN forces in the Muslim enclaves of Srebrenica and Zepce in eastern Bosnia. A total of 1,200 Dutch blue berets will be deployed in the enclaves. A group of Dutch Army quartermasters in already in the two towns to prepare for the troops' arrival.
