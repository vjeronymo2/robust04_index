920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Burlington Burlington Group, an investment holding concern, lifted net attributable revenue from Pounds 75,100 to Pounds 90,400 in 1991. Net asset value, taking investments at market value, was 16.87p, against 15.16p. At the year-end over 60 per cent of assets were in gilts (Pounds 1.02m), other fixed interests (Pounds 125,100), listed equities (Pounds 749,300), unlisted equities (Pounds 8,400), and cash (Pounds 592,900). Earnings per share came to 0.61p (0.51p) and the dividend is again 0.5p.
