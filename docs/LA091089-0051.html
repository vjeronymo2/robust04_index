September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BEST SOUVENIRS

We just wanted to thank you for telling us about Student Travel International's escorted tours to Europe, which was described in Tours & Cruises (March 19). Our granddaughter just returned from her STI tour of Europe, happy and exhausted from her travels and just bubbling over with stories about the wonderful places she'd been to and the people she met. She has come home with the best sort of travel souvenirs -- lots of happy memories and new friends!

MARJORIE SEICHI

Simi Valley
