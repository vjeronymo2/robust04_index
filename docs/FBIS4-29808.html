BFN [Text] The president and Mrs. Suharto sent off Cambodian state guests Prince Norodom Narariddh and Hun Sen at the Merdeka Palace at 0950 West Indonesian Time this morning. After the ceremony, the Cambodian visitors proceeded to Bandung, West Java, to visit the Nusantara Aviation Industries and the Asia-African Conference museum. The Cambodian visitors, who arrived in Indonesia on 20 June, are scheduled to return home today. Also present at the farewell ceremony were acting Foreign Minister Susilo Sudarman and Minister-State Secretary Murdiono.
