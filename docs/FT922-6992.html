920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / People: Sales menu for Haagen-Dazs Haagen-Dazs, the ice cream company which runs naughty advertising campaigns, has appointed a new sales director in the form of Steve Ramsay to spearhead the drive to increase the product's availability in the UK. Ramsay, 36, who has a sales and marketing background at Paulig and Nestle, will be responsible for pushing Haagen-Dazs both through grocery supermarkets and food outlets; the company recently concluded deals with the Hilton and Scandic Crown hotels to put the ice cream on their menus. Ramsay has already bolstered the sales team through a series of top appointments and says he will explore new opportunities to develop the company's long-term market position in the premium ice cream sector.
