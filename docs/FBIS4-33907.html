BFN [From the "Cuban Panorama" newscast] [Text] In 1993, Cuba invested more than $1.7 million in social security , some $4 million every 24 hours. [sentence as heard] Orlando Penante, social security director of the Cuban Labor Ministry, has indicated that those figures do not include the costs of social assistance programs, which amount to another $35 million. Penante stated that despite the economic hardships Cuba is facing, the number of retirees and pensioners covered by the social security system has increased in the 50 years of the special period in peacetime. The number of subjects covered by social security also surpasses the funds allocated for this purpose.
