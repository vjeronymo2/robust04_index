940423 FT 23 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Gold shares reversed early gains as a weaker bullion price brought pre-weekend selling in relatively quiet trading. The overall index rose 5 to 5,042 and industrials added 21 to 5,983 but golds gave up 19 to 1,841. Anglos added 50 cents to R214 and De Beers put on 50 cents to R107.50.
