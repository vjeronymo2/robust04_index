Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Russian troops will complete their withdrawal from eastern Germany as agreed by the end of August, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (FDP) and Colonel General Matvey Burlakov, commander in chief of the Western Group of Russian Forces in Germany, stressed in Bonn today. At the 11th session of the mixed German-Russian commission, Kinkel emphasized the organizational skills of the Russian Western Group of Forces and that the remaining 31,436 men would return home by August. A spokesman also said the problems described by Burlakov relating to the regional allocation and completion of the soldiers' accommodation in Russia, which is being financed by the German government, would in no way delay the withdrawal schedule.
