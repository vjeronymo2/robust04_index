Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian President Yeltsin has suspended the decision by the administration of the Krasnoyarsk Territory of 22 February 1994 on a referendum on the draft law on organization of state power in the territory. The spokesman for the Russian President has said that the decree of 26 March reads that a referendum on the draft law which determines in many respects the territorial status is the violation of Article 10, part two of Article 66 and part one of Article 77 of the Constitution of the Russian Federation. On Saturday Yeltsin issued a decree on holding on 26-27 April in Moscow of an international conference "Legal and information problems of the creation of a single CIS economic space." Invited are delegates of the Commonwealth of Independent States, the European Union, leaders of ministries and departments, scientific and public organizations. On the same day Yeltsin greeted the participants and guests of the blitz chess tournament "Kremlin Stars--94." His message says that chess have always been popular sport in Russia. Yeltsin voiced hope that new chess stars will emerge during the tournament, said the president's spokesman.
