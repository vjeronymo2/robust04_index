931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / International Company News: IBM in venture with Kodak INTERNATIONAL Business Machines and Eastman Kodak of the US yesterday announced the formation of a jointly owned computer maintenance service business, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. Technology Service Solutions will provide services to owners of IBM workstations, personal computers and point-of-sale products used in shops. The business is expected to employ about 3,000 technicians, many of whom will be transfered from IBM, and will be based in Valley Forge, Pennsylvania. The venture builds on an existing relationship between IBM and Kodak for the service of terminals located in shops.
