Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] Luis Donaldo Colosio, presidential candidate for the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party], is continuing his campaign. He has urged citizens to work together for the benefit of Mexico to attain the country's economic recovery. Armando Rodriguez Lozano, secretary of the PRI electoral campaign, ruled out the possibility there might be any change in the presidential nomination of Luis Donaldo Colosio. Rodriguez Lozano added that this has been the product of speculation by journalists. [Begin Rodriguez Lozano] With all due respect, you newsmen [words indistinct]. In all sincerity, within the party there are no doubts about our candidate. I firmly state this. Anything else is absurd. [end recording]
