930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / Leigh-Pemberton wants Bank of England to have independence Robin Leigh-Pemberton, outgoing governor of the Bank of England, urged at the lord mayor's banquet that consideration be given to how the Bank might gain more independence while staying accountable to parliament. Sir Andrew Hugh Smith, London Stock Exchange chairman, said the government must alter the tax structure to encourage share ownership. Reports, Page 9
