BFN [Text] Baghdad, 18 Jun (TANJUG) -- Milorad Unkovic, the federal Yugoslav minister for economic relations and president of part of the Yugoslav-Iraqi joint committee, today held talks with Ahmad Murtada Ahmad Khalil, the Iraqi minister of transport and communications. They described their cooperation in positive terms, and both sides expressed readiness for further talks on the building of transport infrastructure in Iraq. They also discussed the imports of Yugoslav equipment, material, and spare parts in the sphere of transport and telecommunications when the UN embargoes, currently imposed on both countries, are lifted.
