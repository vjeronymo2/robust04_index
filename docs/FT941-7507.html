940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: United Breweries in restructuring talks United Breweries, the regional brewer and pubs operator, said yesterday that it was revaluing its estate and was in talks with its bankers regarding a possible restructuring of its obligations. The company also said that it was reviewing its strategy and a number of acquisition opportunities which could significantly broaden the base of its activities. The board hopes to conclude the discussions shortly. In August, United announced a reduction in pre-tax losses to Pounds 189,376 (Pounds 283,607) for the six months to May 31. The possibility of a capital restructuring was mentioned at the same time.
