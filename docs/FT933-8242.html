930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / Observer: Over-clouded Remember Bill Hussey, the mining man who was sent to look for gold in Kazakhstan and found his new mine was only a few mushroom clouds away from the former Soviet Union's nuclear test sites? Observer was reassured to hear that he has taken to carrying a geiger-counter. However, there was some consternation recently when Hussey pointed his gadget towards the Broadgate complex; it registered three times the level of radioactivity found in Kazakhstan.
