BFN [Text] Referring to yesterday's statement by Turkish Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman, who rushed to condemn our country for the incident at the Albanian guard post, Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said that the Turkish side is trying to create tension or is exporting domestic problems. This Turkish interest in Greek-Albanian relations should be noted because it could be useful in the interpretation of various problems or events, the spokesman said. Today, on the pretext of the latest developments in Albania, Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] also appeared provocative. Skopje attacked the Greek Government and described our country as a source of problems in the Balkans. Greece was also accused of seeking clashes between the Albanians of Skopje and Skopjeans.
