BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al Maa'[May] 26, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- 'Abd-al-'Aziz al Dali, the chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee in the Socialist Party in Yemen has highly praised the initiative undertaken by the leader of the revolution to defuse the tragic events in the Yemen. In statements to the Jamahiriyah News Agency, the official applauded the sincere efforts exerted by the leader to initiate action to bring an end to the fighting between fellow Yemenis. The chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee in the Yemeni Socialist Party addressed a letter of gratitude and appreciation to the leader for his unswerving pro-unity and pro-nationalist stance. The chairman said that the efforts exerted by the Great Jamahiriyah to bring a swift end to the suffering of the Yemeni people and prevent further bloodshed was greatly appreciated and welcomed by all Yemenis. The chairman applauded the Great Jamahiriyah's concern for the security and stability of the Yemeni people and for preventing an escalation of the fighting. In statements given elsewhere, Al Dali said that he would be meeting today with Dr. Esmat 'Abd-al-Meguid, the secretary general of the Arab League where he will inform him of latest developments of the current situation in the Yemen saying that the secessionists who declared the breakaway Democratic Republic of Yemen in the south, had submitted an application for membership to the Arab League.
