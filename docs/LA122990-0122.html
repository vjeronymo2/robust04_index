December 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

An arrest warrant is out for Stanley Wilson, former Cincinnati Bengal running back, who failed to appear at a preliminary hearing in connection with a felony burglary charge.

Wilson, 29, was arrested Dec. 13 and arraigned four days later. At the time, bail was set at $10,000.

The former Oklahoma player is accused of breaking into a home in Long Beach and stealing jewelry and clothes.

Wilson is banned from the NFL for drug use. His career ended when he was caught using cocaine the night before the Super Bowl in January, 1989.
