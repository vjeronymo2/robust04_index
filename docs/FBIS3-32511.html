Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] On 8 February, Dominican President Joaquin Balaguer Cabrera met with Higher Education Minister Fernando Vecino Alegret who, as a matter of fact, will complete a three-day visit to the Dominican Republic tomorrow. Balaguer and Vecino Alegret spoke about the observance in the Dominican Republic of the birth of the late Dominican teacher Camila Enrique Surena, who taught at Havana University for many years. Vecino Alegret relayed to Balaguer a message from Cuban President Fidel Castro, which Balaguer reciprocated, according to sources from the Cuba delegation.
