March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

WORK UNDER WAY ON HOAG CANCER CENTER

Construction is under way on the $21-million Patty and George Hoag Cancer Center at Hoag Memorial Hospital, 301 Newport Blvd., Newport Beach.

Designed by Stichler Design of San Diego, the 65,000-square-foot, three-story center will face Pacific Coast Highway and be connected to the hospital by a covered walkway. Fong & Associates of Costa Mesa is the landscape architect.
