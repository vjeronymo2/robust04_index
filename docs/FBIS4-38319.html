BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sharip Ausuyev] [Excerpts] Groznyy, 28 May -- Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev described yesterday's terrorist act in the republic as carefully organized sabotage directed against independent Chechnya. He said on local television today that forces opposed to the slight thaw in Russo-Chechen relations were behind this act. Some details about the terrorist act emerged today. There was a powerful explosion at about 2000 local time on 27 May on the road between the Chechen settlements of Zakan-Lirk and Alkha-Kala just as the president's motorcade was driving past a Zhiguli model nine parked at the edge of the road. [passage omitted] Yaragi Mamodayev, head of the government of national confidence, also gave his opinion about the terrorist act today. In a statement he stressed that the process of the self-destruction of the Chechen people is becoming more profound. The government of national confidence resolutely condemns such actions, no matter by whom, and appeals to the leaders of the regime, to all sociopolitical forces and to the citizens of the republic for nationwide reconciliation and an end to all forms of confrontation.
