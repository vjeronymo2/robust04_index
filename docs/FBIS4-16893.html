BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Styazhkin] [Text] Cherkessk April 20 TASS -- The Supreme Soviet of the Karachayevo-Cherkessian Republic has been transformed into the People's Assembly. All the functions and powers of the representative authority will be transferred to it. This decision was taken by the session of the Republic's Supreme Soviet held in Cherkessk on Tuesday. Viktor Savelyev, president of the Republic's Supreme Soviet, was endorsed as speaker of the National Assembly. The Presidium of the Republican Supreme Soviet has been dissolved. The Nationalities Council of the People's Assembly has been formed. It includes five deputies from each of the native peoples inhabitting Karachayevo-Cherkessia -- Abaz, Karachay, Nogay, Russians, Cherkessians, and two deputies representing other peoples living in Karachayevo-Cherkessia. The speaker of the People's Assembly will be chairman of the Nationalities Council. The draft constitution of Karachayevo- Cherkessia is to be worked out by the Council by July 1. After being considered by the National Assembly it will be put to a natiowide referendum.
