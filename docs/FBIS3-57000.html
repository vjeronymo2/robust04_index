Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kiev, 20 January -- [passage omitted] Answering a question on whether he would use the right granted to him by the constitutional amendment adopted today allowing him to cancel acts by the Republic of the Crimea's bodies of executive power should they contravene Ukrainian laws and presidential directives, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk said: If Crimea's bodies of power violate the constitution and laws of Ukraine, including the law of Ukraine on delimiting powers between Ukraine and the Crimea, I will undoubtedly bring all actions by Crimean authorities into accordance with state interests.
