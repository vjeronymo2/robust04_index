920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Record for Post Office deliveries A RECORD 89.7 per cent of first-class letters were delivered the day after postings during the past financial year, Sir Bryan Nicholas, Post Office chairman, said yesterday. The figure was an improvement on the previous of 85.5 per cent, beating targets agreed with the Post Office User's National Council (Pounc), he said. Pounc welcomed the figures and said it hoped improvements would continue.
