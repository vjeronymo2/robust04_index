940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Business Travel: Emergency Landing A Polynesian Airlines jet, carrying 72 passengers and crew, made an emergency landing last Tuesday after a stowaway, hiding in the right wheel well, jammed the landing gear on that side of the aircraft. The unidentified stowaway was later found dead in the well. None of the passengers or crew aboard the flight from Tonga to Apia, western Samoa, were injured.
