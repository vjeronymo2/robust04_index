BFN [Text] PRC Ambassador to Cuba Xu Yicong began a two-day visit to Las Tunas Province on 22 May. Xu Yicong will witness firsthand the Revolution's accomplishments in the province. Julian Lopez, Central Committee member and provincial first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba, and Fransico Grave de Peralta, provincial People's Government president, greeted Xu Yicong at the house in which Las Tunas patriot Vicente Garcia was born. During his stay in Las Tunas, Xu Yicong will visit places of historical interest as well as service and production centers.
