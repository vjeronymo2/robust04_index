BFN [Excerpts] Kuwait, 4 Jun -- Algerian Culture Minister Silmane Shaykh and an accompanying delegation met today with Kuwaiti Amir Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah. An official statement stated that the minister delivered to the Amir a message from Algerian President Liamine Zeroual. Contents of the message were not disclosed. [Passage omitted] Shaykh and the accompanying delegation arrived in Kuwait 3 June on an official visit.
