931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Clydeport buys Hunterston terminal Clydeport has bought the former Hunterston terminal in Ayrshire from British Steel. The announcement of the Pounds 4.8m deal followed an eight-week detailed engineering study of the deep water facility. No traffics have been handled through the Hunterston jetty since June 1992, following the closure of the Ravenscraig steelworks.
