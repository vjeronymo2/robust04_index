BFN [Report by Guy Rogers] [Text] Johannesburg April 15 SAPA -- The Freedom Front [FF] says Thursday's [14 April] TV debate between President F W de Klerk and ANC President Nelson Mandela was a microcosm of South Africa's present politics -- "an NP[National Party]-ANC go it alone show". The debate was a sterile shadow-boxing contest, the FF said. "History will show the NP-ANC relationship is similar to that of the blackwidow spider using and discarding its mate..." The African National Congress would discard the NP once a transfer of power had been completed, the FF said.
