BFN [Text] The Criminal Court yesterday remanded for 12 more days three Iranian men arrested for suspected involvement in the truck bomb plot against the Israeli Embassy uncovered in March. The suspects are Hossein Shahriari Far, 29, Babak Taheri, 25, and Hammad Habadi Ariwi, 32. They have already been in police custody for 18 days. According to the law, police can first detain criminal suspects for seven days before having to file charges. If they cannot finish the interrogation in one week, they can seek a court order twice remand the suspects for further periods of 12 days each. The first remand was approved by the court on June 8. Police Maj Pakon Kittiwat, who is heading the investigation, asked the South Bangkok Criminal Court to remand the suspects for a further 12 days from today pending further inquiries.
