930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: BZW to sell Scapa scrip shares BZW Securities is inviting bids for the Scapa Group shares, worth about Pounds 1.7m, that it has agreed to buy as a result of its cash offer for shares arising from Scapa's enhanced scrip dividend scheme. The strike price auction will be open to members of the Stock Exchange and institutional clients of BZW.
