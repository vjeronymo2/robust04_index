BFN [Text] Vladikavkaz, May 20, RIA -- Russian peacekeeping troops came under fire on two occasions in the state of emergency zone in North Ossetia and Ingushetia last night. In the area of the village Nizhny Batakayurt in North Ossetia an armoured personnel carrier was shot at from the Niva car driving by. In another incident a post of Russian Interior Ministry troops in Ingushetia came under fire from the direction of the village Dolakovo at 2 a.m.. According to the press centre of the provisional administration of the zone under state of emergency, in both incidents Russian troops returned fire. No deaths or injuries resulting from the shooting were reported.
