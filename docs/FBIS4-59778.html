BFN [Text] Touring the site of this morning's terrorist attack near Bet Hagay, Chief of Staff Ehud Baraq said he foresees a continuation of terror attacks in Judaea, Samaria, and the Qatif bloc. The chief of staff called on all people licensed to carry weapons to do so, and to help the security forces and intelligence personnel protect thousands of vehicles driving through Judaea and Samaria. He added that roadblocks can only prevent terror attacks in their immediate vicinity and that the task of providing additional protection for the population falls to the Israeli Army and the citizens themselves.
