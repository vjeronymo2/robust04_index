940426 FT 26 APR 94 / UK Company News: ML placing ML Holdings, the aerospace, defence and electronics group, is issuing 7.5m shares, which are being placed at 41p to raise about Pounds 3m. The placing follows the four acquisitions the group has made since the beginning of the year for a total of Pounds 1.6m. Mr John Bryson, finance director, said proceeds would be used to reduce gearing and to fund the working capital needs of the acquisitions.
