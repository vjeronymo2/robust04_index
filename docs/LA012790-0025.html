January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SOME SAY TOO MUCH, SOME SAY NOT ENOUGH

Why are you hyping the Super Bowl? I can understand the TV network that is going to broadcast it having a motive, but not the L.A. Times.

It isn't being played in Los Angeles, nor even California. A California team is involved, but I doubt that is an explanation. You ignore plenty of interesting stories to turn over your front page to this non-event.

Please review your coverage of sports. More balance, please.

RONALD BENKERT, Marina Del Rey
