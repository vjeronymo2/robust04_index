920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Computer facility The CAPITA GROUP has been awarded a major contract worth about Pounds 2.5m from Tynedale District Council, for on-site computer facilities management of its data centre at Hexham, Northumberland. The contract, won by competitive tender, will run for five years and marks Tynedale's formal withdrawal from the Northumberland County Council computing consortia. The contract has been secured by Capita's Telecom Capita subsidiary and will provide an extensive range of information technology applications and services including the collection of council tax.
