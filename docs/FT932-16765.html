930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Top annuity rates ANNUITY rates rose slightly last month. According to the Annuity Bureau (11-12 Hanover Square, London, W1R 9BD, tel: 071-495 1495), which produces the table, Generali has raised its rates by 1 per cent and Royal Life by 2 per cent in the past week. This follows an increase in long gilt yields. Life expectancy is the other determinant of annuity rates, and a few companies will 'underwrite' an annuity if medical evidence shows that your life expectancy is reduced. According to Billy Burrows, of Annuity Direct, two companies, Sun Life and Providence Capitol, lead the market, while Prudential and Norwich Union will underwrite an annuity on request. This is done by adding notionally to the number of years - usually up to a maximum of 10 - you have lived already and giving an annuity on this basis. ----------------------------------------------------------------------- ANNUITY RATES ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Level annuity with 100% spouse's annuity ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Male 55/Female 50 Annuity Male 60/Female 55 Annuity Royal Life pounds8723.13 Royal Life pounds9020.07 Prudential pounds8691.12 Prudential pounds8967.84 Scottish Widows pounds8651.40 Scottish Widows pounds8856.84 ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Level annuity with 50% spouse's annuity ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Male 55/Female 50 Annuity Male 60/Female 55 Annuity Prudential pounds9290.28 Royal Life pounds9805.58 Royal Life pounds9271.87 Prudential pounds9782.40 Equitable Life pounds9197.04 Equitable Life pounds9765.96 ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Annuity escalating by 3% per annum, 100% spouse's annuity ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Male 55/Female 50 Annuity Male 60/Female 55 Annuity Prudential pounds6323.76 Royal Life pounds6724.77 Royal Life pounds6315.34 Prudential pounds6678.00 Equitable Life pounds6084.00 RNPF Nurses pounds6518.00 ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Annuity escalating by 3% per annum, 50% spouse's annuity ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Male 55/Female 50 Annuity Male 60/Female 55 Annuity Prudential pounds6946.20 Royal Life pounds7559.30 Royal Life pounds6939.03 Prudential pounds7493.76 Equitable Life pounds6819.00 RNPF Nurses pounds7485.00 ----------------------------------------------------------------------- All annuities are compulsory purchase.
