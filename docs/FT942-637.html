940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Waverley Mining in Pounds 1.16m fund raising Waverley Mining Finance, the Edinburgh-based investment company, proposes to raise Pounds 1.16m before expenses via the issue of 2m shares at 58p apiece. The shares closed unchanged at 57p yesterday. The shares are to be subscribed by investment funds managed by GFM International Investors - a 99.5 per cent-owned affiliate of Metropolitan Life of New York.
