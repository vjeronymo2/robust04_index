920424 FT 24 APR 92 / Slovenia's PM to call poll Slovenia's new prime minister said yesterday he would call early elections and immediately tackle economic problems, after ousting the government that won the republic its independence from Yugoslavia, Reuter reports from Ljubljana. Parliament asked Mr Janez Drnovsek, a 41-year-old former Yugoslav president and economist, to form a government after Christian Democrat Lojze Peterle lost a confidence vote on Wednesday night amid dissatisfaction over economic problems.
