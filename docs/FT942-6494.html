940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Northern Ireland Electricity - Other companies The last of the UK power companies to be privatised is expected to announce annual pre-tax profits of about Pounds 81m on Wednesday, a sharp improvement on the Pounds 58m of 1992-93, helped by cost-cutting. The dividend payment is likely to be about 11.3p, against 10.02p. Unlike the mainland power distributors, NIE is spared the traumas of a regulatory review this summer.
