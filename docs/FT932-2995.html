930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Ldn & Clydeside London & Clydeside Holdings, the USM-quoted housebuilder operating throughout Scotland, turned round from a loss of Pounds 120,000 to a pre-tax profit of Pounds 34,000 in the six months to March 31. Turnover fell to Pounds 8.47m, against Pounds 10.8m, which included a Pounds 2.5m property disposal. Operating profit was down to Pounds 522,000 (Pounds 697,000) because 1991-92 benefited from Pounds 220,000 from associates. Net interest payable, however, dropped to Pounds 488,000 (Pounds 817,000). The interim dividend is held at 1.8p, payable from earnings per share of 0.2p (losses 0.6p). House reservations in January, February and March showed considerable improvement and although the month-on-month increase had reduced, there seemed to be an underlying recovery in the market, directors stated.
