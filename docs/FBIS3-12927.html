Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Algiers, March 4 (MENA)--President Lamine Zeroual is expected to make a mini-reshuffle in his government during the coming days within efforts exerted to surmount the current crisis, MENA correspondent here learned. The reshuffle will involve appointing of a new prime minister, the correspondent said, adding that Reshid Ben Haddi, [name as received] a retired major-general, is the nominated for this post. Former rector of Algiers University Mufd Zakria [name as received] is expected to be minister of education to succeed Ahmed Jebbar, who is nominated to take up a prominent post at the UNESCO.
