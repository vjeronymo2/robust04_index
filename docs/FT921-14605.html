920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE January/February c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 425, BWC N/O, BTD Dollars 400, BWD N/O; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 410, BWC Dollars 410, BTD Dollars 380' BWD Dollars 380. COTTON Liverpool- Spot or shipment sales amounted to 334 tonnes for the week ended January 10, compared with 240 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases, mainly in African and American descriptions. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably in the Middle Eastern range.
