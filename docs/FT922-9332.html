920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / Observer: Salted away Kent police were mystified after foiling an attempt by five men with a bulldozer to smash open an Abbey National cash-dispenser in the coastal resort of Herne Bay, and steal the money. A police spokesman said: 'Four men were arrested, but one ran off into the sea, and has not been found despite a search by the Whitstable lifeboat.'
