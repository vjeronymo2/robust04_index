Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Multilateral consultations will begin in Moscow on Thursday, under the chairmanship of Russia's Choice leader Yegor Gaydar, on the establishment of a new political organization -- "a union or party" -- a competent source told Interfax. He said that consultations would begin at 10:00 at the Institute of Economic Problems of the Transitional Period (5 Ogaryova Street) and will be attended by leading politicians, public and cultural figures, and representatives of trade unions and associations of entrepreneurs. "The talks will concentrate on the establishment of a new political association involving politicians close to Russia's Choice and representatives of a wide range of non-political organizations, said the source. The same problem will be discussed during closed emergency consultations at one of Moscow's suburban recreational facilities over the weekend, involving deputies of the Russia's Choice parliamentary faction.
