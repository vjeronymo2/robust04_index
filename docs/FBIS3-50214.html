Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 22/1/1994 -- The National Council of the Romanian National Unity Party [PUNR] discussed on January 22 the results of the negotiations conducted between the PUNR and the Party of Social Democracy of Romania [PDSR] regarding the PUNR offer to participate in the government, as well as the content of the draft protocol articulated to that end, reads a release issued to the national agency ROMPRES by the PUNR National Council. The National Council endorses the protocol provisions, considering them to be the minimal condition for the PUNR participation in the government. If the PDSR fails to apply the provisions of the respective protocol, the PUNR will withdraw its parliamentary backing of the current government. The PUNR National Council considers that the PUNR participation in the government is a major political decision of a nature to redeem to the nation the confidence and hope for the country's economic recovery and the improvement of the social life, giving the act of governing more authoritativeness, dynamism, competence, responsibility, the release reads.
