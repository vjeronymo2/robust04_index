BFN ["dpa"-signed report: "Weizsaecker Calls Serbs Near Gorazde War Criminals"] [Text] Berlin -- Federal President Richard von Weizsaecker has called the Serb attackers of the Muslim enclave of Gorazde "mere war criminals that have lost the last bit of credibility." He called for "a clear and firm position by the international organizations and their leading member countries." Chancellor Helmut Kohl and Russian President Boris Yeltsin have discussed the situation in Gorazde on the telephone. Government spokesman Dieter Vogel said the topics discussed were a solution through negotiations and a cease-fire.
