August 13, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Today: Box office results to be released should show if "The Two Jakes" will confirm Jack Nicholson's superstar status. . . . Rock legend Chuck Berry performs at the Strand in Redondo Beach tonight in his first L.A. appearance since his recent St. Louis arrest involving marijuana and pornography.

Wednesday: Officials for the financially strapped L.A. Festival are expected to announce whether they will reject a $30,000 grant from the National Endowment for the Arts because of the agency's anti-obscenity stance.

Thursday: Actors' Equity may overturn its decision to bar British star Jonathan Pryce from appearing as a Eurasian in "Miss Saigon" on Broadway. GREG BRAXTON
