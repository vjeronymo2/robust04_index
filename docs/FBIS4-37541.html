BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God watch over him, has ordered an additional cost of living allowance for retirees who had served for 25 years or more and whose salaries and allocations now are less than 500 dinars. The additional allowance amounts to 25 percent of the retiree's monthly salary and allocations and is effective as of 1 February 1994. This came in a notice by the Presidential Office to the Finance Ministry and other competent parties.
