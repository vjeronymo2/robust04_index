Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 15 ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed on February 12 Decree No 298 "on the department of the president's federal civil service" to provide organizational and technical services to the president's Personnel Policy Council, hold a federal personnel data bank, and provide analytical support of the development and implementation of the state personnel policy in the federal bodies of executive power and federal establishments and organizations. The department will be headed by Aleksandr M. Kurbatov, the executive secretary of the president's Personnel Policy Council.
