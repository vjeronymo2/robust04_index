940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Rosebys rises 20% to Pounds 2.81m Rosebys, the curtain and linen stores group, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.81m for 1993, an improvement of 20 per cent over the previous year's Pounds 2.34m. The advance, which included Pounds 220,000 from lease disposals, was achieved against a background of 'patchy' trading conditions was scored from a turnover 8.6 per cent ahead at 48.83m. Earnings emerged at 9.6p (8p) and a final dividend of 3.25p makes a 4.65p (3.9p) total. Rosebys was floated by Cattle's in 1992 to raise additional capital.
