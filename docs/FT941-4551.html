940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Chavimochic land sale gets under way The first stage of the Chavimochic land sale is scheduled to take place today. In this and subsequent auctions on 24 March and 11 April a total of 33 lots comprising 14,770 hectares will be offered to private investors, Peruvian and foreign. The minimum purchase is 100 hectares and the average price per hectare is Dollars 1,450. Buyers are guaranteed supply of 10,000 cubic metres of water per hectare per year - they will pay around Dollars 220 per hectare to the private company charged with supplying water and maintaining and operating the system.
