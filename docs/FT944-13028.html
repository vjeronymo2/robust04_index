941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Dares Estates Dares Estates, the property investment and development group, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 133,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.62m for the half year to June 30. The comparable period saw a profit of Pounds 465,000 on turnover of Pounds 3.4m, although that included a profit of Pounds 2.27m on the termination of operations, against Pounds 693,000 this time. Interest charges were cut from Pounds 4m to Pounds 2.9m, of which Pounds 191,000 was satisfied by the issue of preference shares. Losses per share came out at 0.38p (1.33p earnings).
