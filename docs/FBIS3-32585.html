Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The week of solidarity with the Haitian people began on 8 February. On this occasion, President Aristide's spokesman Jean-Claude Martineau spoke extensively on the planned cooperation between the Canadian Government and the Haitian Government during a speech on the Haitian crisis. Mr. Martineau explained that the Aristide government will conclude once again an agreement with the Hydro-Quebec International Company for the renovation of the drinking water and electric energy distribution systems. Within the scope of this week of solidarity, several major Haitian and U.S. [as heard] figures will call on the Clinton government on 10 February to support the proposal on strengthening sanctions against Haiti.
