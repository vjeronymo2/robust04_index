910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: Dollars 26m purchase for Thorn EMI THORN EMI is to pay an initial Dollars 26m (Pounds 16m) for the 50 per cent interest in the New York-based SBK Records it does not already own. SBK is a joint venture record label between EMI Music, part of Thorn EMI, and SBK Record Productions, a company owned by Mr Charles Koppelman and Mr Martin Bandier. To the initial consid eration of about Dollars 26m (Pounds 16m) will be added the return to Mr Koppelman and Mr Bandier of their original investment of Dollars 5m. Deferred consideration may also be payable dependent on sales and profits of SBK Records this year, next year and in 1993. Thorn estimated that deferred payments would amount to Dollars 100m, but added that they could total as much as Dollars 400m.
