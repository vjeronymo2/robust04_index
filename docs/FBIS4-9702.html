BFN [Text] In one of the first expressions of support in light of the upcoming second round of elections, the presidential candidate for the National Conciliation Party [PCN] that, according to the vote count, came in fourth in the first round of elections, has said that his party will support the Nationalist Republican Alliance [ARENA]. Colonel Roberto Escobar Garcia, a lawyer and PCN secretary general, said yesterday that his party will support ARENA in the runoff. Even though the Supreme Electoral Tribunal has not made an official announcement, there are indications that it will be held on 24 April. According to political observers, ARENA should not pursue an alliance in confronting the left in the runoff because it has enough strength to defeat all the leftist parties combined. They also believe Fidel Chavez Mena does not have the leadership to call on the rank and file to vote for ARENA.
