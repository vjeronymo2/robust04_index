Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 31 January, yesterday, Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister, received His Excellency [H.E.] (Howard Balloch), assistant of the Canadian minister of foreign affairs and foreign trade, for talks. Prince Krompreah thanked Canada profoundly for its attention and assistance to Cambodia regarding the Geneva conference, the Paris peace agreement, and mine clearing. Furthermore, Prince Krompreah proposed that Canada assist in building hydropower dams, communications, and power plants; and in giving professional training to the Khmer Royal Armed Forces. He also suggested that Canada send businessmen to invest in Cambodia. H.E. (Howard Balloch) disclosed that Canada will continue to provide more assistance in clearing mines in Cambodia. As for other proposals put forward by Prince Krompreah, he said he will convey them to the Canadian Government so that the latter can assist in these matters.
