Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Statement issued by the Office of the Defense Minister; place and date not given] [Text] Less than 24 hours after the signing by the president and the vice president of the document giving a mandate to the joint military committee to tackle the deterioration of the military and security situation and the implementation of the security and military aspects of the pledge and accord document, we were surprised by the irresponsible behavior on the part of the giants' [al-'Amaliqah] forces. This behavior was represented by their attack on the 36th Infantry Battalion of the Unity [al-Wahdah] Brigade stationed in Mudiyah Province in Abyan Governorate. The al-'Amaliqah forces used in their barbarous attack Katyusha missile launchers, tanks and artillery, as well as other medium and light weapons. This attack targeting the 36th Battalion was accompanied by another military attack, targeting towns, villages and small villages in Mudiyah Province, when military units of the al-'Amaliqah forces carried out intensive shelling of peaceful inhabitants.
