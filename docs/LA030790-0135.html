March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Wesley F. Nelson has joined Amkly Systems Inc., an Irvine-based computer products company, as vice president of international sales. He had been director of international sales at Autodesk and was vice president of international sales for AST Research from 1985 to 1989. His new responsibilities for Amkly initially will be focused in Europe, setting up dealer and distributor networks.
