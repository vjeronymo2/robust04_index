BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 7 Apr (TANJUG) -- The Radical Party of Serbia [Radikalna Stranka Srbije -- RSS] is a national party of the moderate left. It will advocate a democratic state in which the Serb states will be united, it was stated at today's promotion of this party in Belgrade. The RSS, which was officially registered on 29 March this year, elected Zvezdan Babovic its president at the assembly held on 6 April. Four vice presidents and Zoran Stojkovic, the president of the Executive Committee, were also elected on this occasion. Twenty-six members of the Executive Committee were elected, 21 of them had previously been members of the Serbian Radical Party. [passage omitted]
