BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Apr (MENA)--Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has stated that the Israeli troops' withdrawal from the self-rule areas in Gaza and Jericho will take place the day after the implementation agreement is signed in Cairo. The agreement is scheduled to be signed on 4 May. In an another development, Israeli diplomatic sources close to the intensive negotiations that were held in Cairo today have told MENA that the economic agreement between the PLO and Israel will be signed tomorrow, Friday, in Paris. The same sources pointed out that the signing will take place after all obstacles have been overcome. The agreement will be an integral part of the agreement for the implementation of self-rule in Gaza and Jericho.
