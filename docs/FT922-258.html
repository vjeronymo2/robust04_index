920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / MPs support pension change MORE than three quarters of MPs support the removal of pension fund control from companies as a result of the Maxwell scandal, according to a survey published today. The move would have the support of 79 per cent of MPs - 95 per cent of Labour and 66 per cent of Tory MPs. The survey by Access Opinions showed that 68 per cent of a sample of 100 MPs believed companies should be banned from taking pension holidays when their fund is in surplus.
