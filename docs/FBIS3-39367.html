Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Abdellatif Filali, minister of State for Foreign Affairs and Cooperation, was received at the Elysee Palace this morning by French President Francois Mitterrand. Abdellatif Filali described this meeting -- which took place in the presence of His Majesty's ambassador to France Abbas Fasi -- as very serious and positive due to the nature of relations linking Morocco with France. He said his talks with President Mitterrand, which lasted for 30 minutes, gave him the opportunity to review with him bilateral relations and all issues of common interest.
