Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Highness Crown Prince Hasan this morning received Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Al Thani. They examined ways to foster relations between the two brotherly countries. Prince Hasan and Shaykh Hamad also discussed Arab affairs and how to revive joint Arab action considering the current circumstances prevailing in the world. This morning, the two called at the military general headquarters. Staff General 'Abd-al-Hafiz Mar'i al-Ka'abinah, chairman of the joint chiefs of staff, was on hand to welcome them. He briefed them on regional developments from a military angle and on the Jordanian military's role regionally and globally, noting its participation in peacekeeping operations around the world. Crown Prince Hasan and the Qatari minister were accompanied by Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, Jordan's minister of state for foreign affairs; 'Abd-al-Rahman Bin-Hamad al-'Attiyah, ambassador at the Qatari Foreign Ministry; and the ambassadors of Qatar in Amman and Jordan in Doha.
