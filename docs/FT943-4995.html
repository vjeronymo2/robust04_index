940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Bridgend Group BRIDGEND GROUP is selling SA St Gery to High Street Holdings for BFr30m (Pounds 600,000) cash. SA St Gery owns a long leasehold interest in the Halles St Gery, a property situated in the centre of Brussels. In addition to the disposal proceeds, Bridgend will receive 12.5 per cent of the expected total rental of the building.
