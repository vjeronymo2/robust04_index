920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / International Company News: Union Carbide unit for sale UNION Carbide, the US chemicals producer, has put its OrganoSilicon Products, Systems and Services (OSi) group on the block. It hopes to sell the business by the year-end as part of a previously-announced Dollars 500m asset sale. Proceeds will be used to cut debt. OSi, which reports to Carbide's specialty division, had 1991 sales of Dollars 300m. It manufactures fluids and chemicals for a number of markets including fibre-glass, reinforced plastics and automotive.
