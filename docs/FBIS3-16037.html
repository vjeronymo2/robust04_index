Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] One hundred and fifty deputies of the present Ukrainian Supreme Council have decided to take part in the elections again. This was reported in the press by Ivan Yemets, head of the Central Electoral Commission for the election of Ukrainian people's deputies. The majority of them are standing in their former districts. However, there are several who have decided to relocate, noted Ivan Yemets. Among them are a group of Kiev deputies who are willing to be candidates in rural districts. In total 5,830 candidates for people's deputies have been registered in Ukraine.
