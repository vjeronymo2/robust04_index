930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Overseas In the six months since its June 1992 year end, net asset value per share of Fleming Overseas Investment Trust increased 26 per cent from 214.7p to 271p. At December 31 1991 assets were 222.5p. Earnings rose to 2.16p (1.78p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
