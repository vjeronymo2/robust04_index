Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Contacts to coordinate Lebanese-Syrian positions toward developments, especially the consultations between Arab and Israeli chief delegates in Washington, which have been suspended until 15 February, have been intensified. President Ilyas al-Hirawi's telephone conversation with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad was part of these contacts. Also in this context is the visit to Damascus last night by Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri and Ministers Muhsin Dallul and Jean 'Ubayd and their meeting with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and a number of Syrian officials. Al-Hariri returned to Beirut this morning and later met with the visiting World Bank mission to discuss financing of some projects.
