BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 7 May (WAKH) -- Robert Pelletreau, the assistant U.S. secretary of state for Near East and North African affairs, arrived in Abu Dhabi today from Muscat on a three-day visit to the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. The visit is part of a tour of several countries in the region. During his visit, the U.S. official will hold talks with senior UAE officials on the current developments in the region, the Middle East peace process, and cooperation between the two countries.
