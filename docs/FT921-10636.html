920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa THE JSE all-gold index fell 15 to 1,260 as the gold price traded at some Dollars 356 an ounce. Vaal Reefs fell R7 to R218 and Western Deep declined R2.25 to R126.50. The industrial index shed 1 to 4,407 and the all-share lost 12 to 3,594 in thin trading.
