Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] A total agreement was reached on all issues that were discussed, Defense Minister Aleksandrov said upon his return from Romania, where he had talks with his counterpart, Nicolae Spiroiu. Refraining by all means from interfering in the Yugoslav conflict and a total support of the Yugoembargo was the joint position of the two defense ministers. Regarding the war in former Yugoslavia, the opinion was expressed that diplomatic means to stop the war are far from being exhausted.
