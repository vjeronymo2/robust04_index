August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ABOUT OLD HAWAII

Jerry Hulse's article on old Hawaii is full of interesting items that cause a person who has been to Hawaii to make sure the Big Island is the place to visit or revisit.

I just hope the area around and in Holualoa is not run over by speculators and others bent on destroying the beauty of Hawaii.

JIM ALLEN

Cambria, Calif.

Washington, D.C.
