921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / IMF assails S African tax break The International Monetary Fund has strongly criticised tax incentives provided by the South African government which formed the basis for the Alusaf aluminium smelter and Columbus stainless steel plant expansion, two large capital projects worth more than R10bn (Pounds 2.1bn) approved in the past month, writes Philip Gawith in Johannesburg.
