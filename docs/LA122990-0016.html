December 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AFTER LOSS, HARRICK GETS SOME ADVICE

Jim Harrick's inability to recognize, create and adjust quickly during game situations has cost UCLA more than just last week's Iowa game. For his teams to win crucial games with any consistency, Harrick must not only provide more flexible and effective game plans, but, more importantly, must demonstrate the sensitivity necessary to call for crucial adjustments at appropriate points during the course of the game.

This has been Harrick's major flaw throughout his tenure at UCLA, and it's high time he addressed it.

HALO JONES

Long Beach
