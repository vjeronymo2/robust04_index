Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("LRAGIR" daily), 03/02/94 -- Azerbaijan continues the military operations in spite of its recent failures at the fronts. According to the press service of RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] Defense Army, yesterday in the morning Azeri troops restarted the offensive in the direction to the east from Martuninskiy regional center. Azeri formations tried to break the Armenian defense lines but had to retreat to the initial positions after Karabakh forces retaliated. The battles were also continuing in northern direction. The enemy tried change the situation at the north of Kelbadzharskiy district. Its goal was most probably to provide aid to the surrounded formations near the Omar gorge. According to the latest verified information this surrounded formations involves more than 3,000 soldiers. Armenian formations stopped the attempts of the enemy. Information about losses is being verified. Meanwhile "SNARK" news agency reports, that American and Turkish councillors were sent to Hadzhikabul military bases which is in 80 km. from Baku. The councillors are teaching about 6 thousands of Azeri Army which were especially chosen for this training. According to a western politician, 25 resigned American officers are working now in Azerbaijan paid by the American "Mega Oil" corporation. Training of Azeri army is costing "Mega Oil" some 10- 15 mln. dollars and is a part of the contract signed last year with Azeri official bodies. Russian councillors are training about 400 Azeris in Ganja. Recently some 500 Russian specialists arrived to the military base Goran, near Ganja. Many people in Azerbaijan believe, that Americans are training personal guards of the President Aliyev, while Russians are teaching the supporters of Prime Minister Guseynov. "ASSOCIATED PRESS" reports increasing contradictions between these two.
