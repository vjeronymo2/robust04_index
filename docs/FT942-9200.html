940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Cape buys boards business for Pounds 3.2m Cape has bought as a going concern the Pyrok Cementatious Building Boards business from the receivers of Pyrok Group for Pounds 3.2m cash. Unaudited management accounts for the nine months to December 31 indicate that Pyrok Group sold boards with a value of Pounds 1.7m and incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 200,000.
