Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 26 (KCNA) -- Korean educational workers and scientists in Japan met in Tokyo on Jan. 22 to express their thanks to the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il for the 125th installment of educational aid fund and stipends. In his report at the meeting, chairman of the Union of Korean Teachers in Japan Pak Kwang-taek extended highest honour and warmest thanks to the fatherly leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il for showing paternal loving care for the development of the democratic national education of Koreans in Japan. The reporter called on the educational workers to radically improve national education of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) with the 50th anniversary of the August 15 liberation of Korea, the 40th anniversary of the formation of Chongnyon and the 17th Congress of Chongnyon one year off. Many representatives expressed their resolve. A letter to Generalissimo Kim Il-song was adopted at the meeting.
