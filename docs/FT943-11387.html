940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Rise at BAT Australian offshoot WD & HO Wills, the Australian cigarette manufacturer controlled by BAT Industries, announced a 21 per cent increase in profits before tax and exceptionals in the six months to end-June, to ADollars 42m (Pounds 20.3m), against ADollars 34.8m. Turnover for the company, which is quoted separately on the Australian stock market, increased from ADollars 444m to ADollars 474.7m. Net profits were ADollars 27.2m, compared with losses of ADollars 7.17m after taking account of ADollars 49.2m of exceptional items.
