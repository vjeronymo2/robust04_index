BFN [Text] Baku (JUNE 23) TURAN: Today newspaper "AZERBAIJAN" published a report of a session devoted to current economic problems, held on June 21 by President Aliyev. He noted that despite the taken measures the situation in economic sphere worsens. It was noted at the session that low prices for energy sources at inner market and preferential allocation of credits ruined social and economic sphere. In these connection positive assessment was given to President's decrees on quarterly revise of prices for oil and oil products and preferential allocation of credits. Head of the Parliament Rasul Guliyev noted that despite President's decree "Liberalization of Foreign Trade" there is a great quantity of unsold goods in the warehouses. So in the warehouses of the Ministry for Agriculture are kept unsold goods total 400 million dollars, in SOCAR [Azerbaijan State Oil Company] -- 100 million dollars, State Committee for Metallurgy -- 15-20 million dollars. The amount of unsold goods totals 600 million dollars. Products totalling 5-6 million dollars spoil every day. The issue of privatisation of incomplete construction objects was also discussed at the session. Rasul Guliyev noted again that "economic reforms should be conducted in all spheres and simultaneously".
