BFN [Text] Sanaa, 27 Apr (INA) -- An official Yemeni source has denied reports that Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, Yemeni minister of planning and development, has submitted his resignation. In a statement to an INA correspondent, the official Yemeni source dismissed such reports as completely baseless. In its latest issue, the opposition Yemeni paper AL-WAHDAWI said Dr. al-Irayni had resigned his ministerial post. The paper said the resignation was kept quiet to allow time to convince him to withdraw it.
