930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / Reserves rise The UK's gold and foreign currency reserves rose an underlying Dollars 64m (Pounds 42.6m) last month, a slightly smaller rise than in May, when they rose by Dollars 78m. The overall level of reserves rose by Dollars 168m, taking reserves at the end of June to Dollars 41.9bn compared with Dollars 41.7bn at the end of May.
