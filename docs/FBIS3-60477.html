Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] More than 2.3 Mn krones (about $170,000) have been spent for the struggle with AIDS in Estonia over the last year. This was announced to BALTFAX by a physician of the Tallinn Merimetsa Hospital's AIDS Center, Jaan Martin. As many as 29 AIDS victims currently live in Estonia. As many as three of the infected left Estonia and two AIDS victims died. A total of 5 more AIDS victims were discovered this year. The first AIDS victim was discovered in Estonia in 1987.
