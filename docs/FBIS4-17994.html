BFN [Text] A source close to law enforcement bodies reported to INTERFAX that in the night of 28 April deputy chief of the Tajikabad regional militia department Idibek Salomonov together with his wife and two children was shot dead from a sub-machine gun in his house (Tajikabad is located in the Garm group of Tajikistan's regions in the Republic's north-east). According to the source, the law enforcement bodies do not know anything about the motives of the murder. They are examining several versions, the results will be reported after the conclusion of the investigation.
