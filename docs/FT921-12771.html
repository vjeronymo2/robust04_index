920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / Ferry on East Timor mission An old Portuguese car ferry was preparing to sail from Lisbon yesterday on a voyage halfway round the world to lay a wreath of flowers in an East Timor cemetery, Reuter reports from Lisbon. The 28-year-old Lusitania Expresso will be on a peace mission to remind the world how Indonesian troops killed at least 50 people in the former Portuguese colony on November 12 during a demonstration at the funeral of a pro-independence activist.
