940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Bridon buys three businesses Bridon, the wire rope and engineering products manufacturer, has bought the wire rope distribution and lifting equipment hire business of George Craig & Sons for a cash consideration based on net assets of about Pounds 7.5m and a goodwill premium not exceeding Pounds 1m. Operating profit for the three new businesses, which will continue to trade as UK Lifting Services, Trading International and Pisces, is estimated at Pounds 500,000 for the year to April 30.
