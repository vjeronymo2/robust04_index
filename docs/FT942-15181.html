940416 FT 16 APR 94 / UK Company News: Shorco advances 38% to Pounds 284,000 Shorco Group Holdings, the USM-quoted company which has interests in trench support systems and allied equipment, lifted pre-tax profits by 38 per cent from Pounds 206,000 to Pounds 284,000 in 1993. The improvement, in a year 'when trading conditions remained difficult', was achieved on turnover ahead to Pounds 7.11m (Pounds 6.86m). Earnings per share came through at 6.4p (4.2p) and the proposed final dividend is an unchanged 2.8p which holds the total at 5.2p. The activities of Shorco Trench Systems and Spectra Alignment were brought together with effect from January 1 and the directors expect to see benefits from this in the current year. So far, 1994 had started well, they said.
