Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Zdenko Vranjes] [Excerpts] The extraordinary session of the chamber of deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna began at 1000 in the packed hall of the People's University in Livno. Members of former chamber of citizens of the Bosnia-Herzegovina assembly are also present at the session as well as members of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency Miro Lasic, Franjo Boras and Fikret Abdic. Representatives of the Catholic Church in Bosnia-Herzegovina, municipal governments of Herceg-Bosna and numerous organizations of the Croat nation in Bosnia-Herzegovina are also present. Members of the state delegation of the Republic of Croatia, headed by Minister of Foreign Affairs Mate Granic are also attending the session. [passage omitted] There is only one item on the agenda: the current military and political situation of the Croat nation in Bosnia-Herzegovina's struggle for its rights and survival. In his introductory speech, the president of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna and the Croat Democratic Community of Bosnia-Herzegovina, Mate Boban, recalled that two years ago the well-known Livno referendum question was formulated which rejected the unitary organization of Bosnia-Herzegovina and proclaimed the basic will of the Croat nation in Bosnia-Herzegovina: Equal rights and a right to self-determination. Boban said that this basic goal had not been abandoned.
