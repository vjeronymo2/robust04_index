November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ARTLESS REPORTING OF RAIDERS TOO MUCH

Well, here they go again, continuing the unfair, slanted reporting that many reporters seem to practice unconsciously.

I found it absolutely impossible to believe that nowhere in either Mark Heisler's or Chris Baker's stories on the Raiders last Sunday was the name of Coach Art Shell. Not once.

That's OK as long as they do the same next season after the Raiders lose five of their first six or seven games.

JOHN ViDAHL, Windsor Hills
