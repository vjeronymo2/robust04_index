BFN [Text] President Suharto considers the current global situation as worrisome, even though there are some heartening issues such as the end of apartheid in South Africa and progress in the Middle East peace process. Speaking at a state banquet hosted in honor of the visiting Turkmenistan President Saparmurad Niyazov at Istana Negara [state palace] in Jakarta tonight, the head of state expressed his concern over the outbreak of conflicts in numerous regions. He said the conflicts have resulted in the loss of human lives and the suffering of the innocent. This is due to power politics, ethnic conflicts, and lawlessness.
