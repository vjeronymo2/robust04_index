Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] Yunnan's public security apparatus on 1 October 1993 began a special effort against narcotics and weapons trafficking. Since October, public security components throughout the province have emphasized investigating and solving cases. By 31 October, they had solved 3358 cases of all types, of which 1224 were major cases, achieving a success rate of 76 percent and apprehending 4233 criminals. In the special effort, public security components have solved 463 narcotics cases, seizing 303.3 kg of heroin and other refined narcotics and 190.1 kg of opium. They have also seized 180 guns of all types and 663 rounds of ammunition.
