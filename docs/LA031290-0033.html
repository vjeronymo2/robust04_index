March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

DR. NEIL SOLOMON: FAT SUBSTITUTE

Question: For some time, I've been thinking of going on a diet because I know I should lose about 40 pounds. But I've heard about a new product that is supposed to look like fat and to taste like fat, but which doesn't have any calories.

I think something such as this would make dieting much easier, and I'd like to know if you have any idea when it will be available.

Answer: One of the nation's leading food producers is working on a product called Olestra, which, as you say, is supposed to look and taste like fat but is calorie-free. The product's technical name is sucrose polyester.

Although Olestra has been under development for several years, it will probably take several more years before it reaches the market. There are always rationalizations to delay going on a diet, but the best time is always now.
