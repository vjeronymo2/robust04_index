920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Airsprung buoyed by interest fall SHARPLY REDUCED interest charges, stable raw material prices and improved productivity combined to lift annual profits at Airsprung Furniture Group by 39 per cent. The Wiltshire-based company lifted profits for the year to end-March from Pounds 3.5m to a record Pounds 4.87m pre-tax on turnover marginally ahead to Pounds 58.1m (Pounds 56m). Interest charges, partly reflecting the proceeds of last July's rights issue, dived from Pounds 907,000 to Pounds 130,000. Earnings per share, after release of deferred tax previously provided, amounted to 33.1p (21.6p). A final dividend of 4.87p lifts the total for the year to 7.5p (6.33p).
