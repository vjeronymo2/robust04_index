Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Mar (MENA)--The drug fighting department of the Cairo Security Directorate today caught a paint merchant in the Abu al-'Ila area with 1 kg of pure heroin worth 1.5 million pounds in his possession. Defendant Rushdi 'Abd-al-Hamid Abu-al-Makarim confessed to the prosecution that he bought the heroin through a merchant in the Red Sea area to market it in Cairo.
