Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Mar (MENA)-- MENA has learned that the Palestinian delegation, led by Dr. Nabil Sha'th, has not gone to the hotel where the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations are supposed to be held. Sources close to negotiations say that the delay in going to the venue of the talks is because they want to hold the talks in a secret place, away from reporters and television cameras. The delegation is also waiting for instructions from the Palestinian leadership in Tunis to begin negotiations. The Israeli delegation to the talks arrived in Cairo at noon today.
