931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / Adams calls for demilitarisation NORTHERN Ireland needed demilitarisation, not simply an IRA ceasefire, Mr Gerry Adams, Sinn Fein president, said yesterday. Discussing the ground-breaking talks he has held with Mr John Hume, leader of the Social Democratic and Labour party, Mr Adams said: 'You cannot expect one element in this equation to make a move. What we need is demilitarisation.'
