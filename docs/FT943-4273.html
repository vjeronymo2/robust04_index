940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Bostrom doubles to Pounds 2.25m Bostrom, the engineering, pressings and car seating company, more than doubled pre-tax profits on its continuing activities in the six months to June 30. After Pounds 203,000 profits this time on property disposals, the pre-tax result came out at Pounds 2.25m compared with Pounds 1m. Turnover rose 33 per cent from Pounds 24.2m to Pounds 32.4m. Earnings per share doubled from 4.3p to 8.7p and the interim dividend remains 2.5p.
