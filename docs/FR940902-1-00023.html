The revisions to §21.7640 account for 36,973 hours of the information collection approved under 2900&hyph;2073. This is estimated to average 10 minutes per response. Proposed §21.7654 also contains information collection requirements which will result in a reporting burden. The reporting burden is estimated to average 5 minutes per response for a total of 67,258 hours. The proposed §21.7653(d) contains information collection requirements which will result in a reporting burden. The reporting burden is estimated to average 5 minutes per response for a total of 517 hours. The average estimated time for response for all these reporting burdens includes the time for reviewing instructions, gathering and maintaining the data needed, and completing and reviewing the collection of information. As required by section 3504(h) of the Paperwork Reduction Act, VA is submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request that it approve this information collection requirement.
