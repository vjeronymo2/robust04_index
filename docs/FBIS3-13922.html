Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Jewish Settlements in Judaea, Samaria, and the Gaza Strip [CJSJSG] has decided to oppose the evacuation of Jews from Hebron by any means. At a special meeting in Qiryat Arba', the council made several practical decisions in order to prevent any evacuation. Our settlement affairs correspondent Gil Litman reports that the CJSJSG implied that it would settle several houses in Hebron's Jewish quarter. Council members stressed that the settlers would not resort to physical violence against the security forces or harm them in any way.
