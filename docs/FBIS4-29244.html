BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- Foreign newspapers strongly urged the United States and the South Korean authorities to pull down the concrete wall built by them in the area South of the Military Demarcation Line. The Ghanaian paper GHANA TIMES said that the United States and the South Korean authorities have built the concrete wall for the purpose of perpetuating the division of Korea and blocking the people's movement for reunification. The Dutch paper MANIFEST said that the South Korean authorities, instigated by the United States, have built the concrete wall under the pretext of preventing a fictitious "invasion from the North" and committed an anti-national crime to keep divided forever the Korean people who have lived as a homogeneous nation, boasting of the 5,000-year long history and brilliant culture. The Indian magazine STUDENTS' FORUM said that with the concrete wall built the fellow countrymen in the North and the South are unable to freely come and go and inquire after the health of their families and relatives and overall opening in all realms of politics, the economy and culture between the North and the South is blocked. The Peruvian paper LA REPUBLICA said that there is no wall in the area north of the Military Demarcation Line but wire entanglements marking the boundary line. It said that to pull down the concrete wall is the will of the Korean people and the requirement of the times and the preservation of the concrete wall is a shame of the nation.
