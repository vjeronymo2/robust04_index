October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

CANDIDATES TO TAKE PART IN FORUM

Candidates running for two seats on the Simi Valley City Council and the mayor's post will participate in a question and answer forum tonight.

The forum will begin at 7 p.m. in the council chambers, said Grace Bernstein, director of the local chapter of the League of Women Voters of Ventura County, a co-sponsor.

Each candidate will be given four minutes for an opening statement before taking questions from the audience, Bernstein said.

Mayoral candidates include incumbent Greg Stratton, who is seeking his third term, and Alfred J. Wilson, a retired Los Angeles bus driver.

Candidates vying for two council seats include Councilwoman Ann Rock, who is seeking her third term; Dave McCormick, a Simi Valley businessman; Judy Mikels, chairwoman of the city's Planning Commission; Kenneth Wenning, a Westlake Village attorney; John Etter, a General Motors engineer; Marilyn Maurer, a retired Los Angeles schoolteacher; Sandi Webb, a local businesswoman, and Harold Lawrence Fick, a GM administrative manager.

Simi Valley City Councilwoman Vicky Howard, whose term expires in November, is running for a Ventura County supervisor's seat. CARLOS V. LOZANO
