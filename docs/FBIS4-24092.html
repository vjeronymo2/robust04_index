BFN [Excerpt] Abuja, June 23 (AFP) -- Nigerian opposition leader Moshood Abiola, who was arrested by police at his home in northern Lagos overnight, was transferred Thursday [23 June] to Abuja, informed sources said. The Moslem publishing tycoon was taken to the federal capital at about 0330 GMT, one of his close aides said. Informed sources later confirmed Abiola had arrived in Abuja. Abiola was arrested Wednesday after coming out of hiding to defy the military-backed government. [passage omitted]
