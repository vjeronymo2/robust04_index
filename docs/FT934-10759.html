931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / Greek opposition chief chosen Greece's opposition New Democracy party, which suffered a crushing defeat by the Socialists in last month's general election, yesterday elected Mr Miltiades Evert, a former mayor of Athens, as its new leader, writes Kerin Hope from Athens. Mr Evert, 54, replaces Mr Constantine Mitsotakis, 75, the former prime minister, who resigned the ND leadership when the conservatives lost.
