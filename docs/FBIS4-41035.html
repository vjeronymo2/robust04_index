BFN [Text] Foreign investors will only begin to deposit their capital in the Ukrainian economy if there is political and economic stabilization. This was stated in an interview with the UKRAINSKIYE VEDOMOSTI magazine by Bohdan Havrylyshyn, an independent adviser to the Ukrainian president. He noted that if the current pace of reform is maintained, Ukraine may end up in international isolation and be transformed into an economic refugee. Bohdan Havrylyshyn emphasized that without conducting deep economic transformations, Ukraine cannot count on financial assistance from the West.
