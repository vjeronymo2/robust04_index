December 5, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Kaiser Stock on NASDAQ: Kaiser Steel Resources of Rancho Cucamonga said its stock has qualified for listing on the NASDAQ market system.

Kaiser, which was taken private in 1984, emerged from bankruptcy court protection in November, 1988. The company began public trading again on Oct. 16 in the over-the-counter system. The NMS listing provides brokers immediate computer access to information on the company's shares.
