940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / International Company News: International Paper improves in second term International Paper achieved earnings of Dollars 87m, or 70 cents a share in the second quarter, up 13 per cent from Dollars 77m, or 62 cents, in the same 1993 period, writes Laurie Morse. The second quarter also showed improvement over this year's first quarter, with rising paper and packaging prices boosting profits. 'This quarter's earnings performance reflects the pricing improvements International Paper is now realising in most of our product lines,' chairman Mr John Georges said. Second-quarter sales rose to Dollars 3.6bn, from Dollars 3.5bn in the same 1993 quarter. The company said price increases for containerboard, pulp and uncoated printing papers had held through the quarter.
