BFN [Text] Prague, May 23 (CTK) -- Trade and Industry Minister Vladimir Dlouhy (ODA, Civic Democratic Alliance) is with 87 per cent the most popular Czech politician, a poll conducted by the Centre of Empiric Researches (STEM) in early May revealed. Dlouhy is followed by Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec (ODS, Civic Democratic Party) with 72 per cent, former Foreign Minister Jiri Dienstbier (Free Democrats) with 70 per cent, Premier Vaclav Klaus (ODS) with 68 per cent and Deputy Premier Jan Kalvoda (ODA) with 67 per cent. The question "do you trust President Vaclav Havel" was answered in the affirmative by 66 per cent of those surveyed. Sixty-two per cent of Czechs believe the government is working well, while the figure for the parliament is 37 per cent.
