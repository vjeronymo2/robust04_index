BFN [From the "Seven O'Clock Newscast"] [Text] The peace agreements with the Socialist Renewal Movement [CRS] will give the former guerrillas the possibility of putting two members into the Chamber of Deputies for the 1994 to 1998 term. CRS leaders went to Narino Palace today. President Cesar Gaviria and the former CRS guerrillas made official the peace agreements today. For the first time, the former guerrillas entered Narino Palace to announce to the country that from now on they will be a political alternative within the law. This is the fifth guerrilla movement to set aside its weapons during the past six years to reach a peace agreement.
