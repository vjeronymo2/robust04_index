BFN [Text] The Ukrainian National Guard [NHU] performs its duties, as established by law, regarding the protection of our constitutional system, the state's territorial integrity, the lives and personal dignity of citizens, and their rights and freedoms against criminal encroachment. This was the topic discussed at today's meeting of the Ukrainian National Guard Military Council, which also addressed ways of improving combat training, education, discipline, and solidarity among units and formations. Guards Major General Mykola Melnyk, the NHU deputy commander, delivered a report. The meeting was chaired by Guards Lieutenant General Volodymyr Kukharuk, the NHU commander in chief.
