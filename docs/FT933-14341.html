930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / Abkhazia rebels scorn ultimatum Leaders of the breakaway Georgian province of Abkhazia yesterday scorned an ultimatum to withdraw from villages near the provincial capital, Sukhumi, John Lloyd reports from Moscow. Mr Sultan Sosnaliyev, Abkhazian defence minister, called the ultimatum a bluff. Abkhazian positions based in the villages shelled Sukhumi early yesterday morning, hours after the midnight deadline for the ultimatum had expired.
