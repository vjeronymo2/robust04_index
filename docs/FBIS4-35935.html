BFN [Text] A telephone contact took place yesterday evening between brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the sisterly Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. During this telephone conversation, President Salih briefed King Husayn on the latest developments in our country in light of the signs of blatant interference in Yemen's affairs. The brother leader president renewed his commendation of the lofty, principled pan-Arab stand of His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal and the brothers in Jordan and their support for our Yemeni people, Yemeni unity, democracy, and constitutional legitimacy. The president stressed that despite the challenges to unity imposed by the rebellious and secessionist gang, which sold itself to the devil, our people will defend unity being the great strategic objective. Our people struggled and offered dear sacrifices to achieve this historic national objective and pan-Arab gain. King Husayn Bin-Talal reiterated, during the telephone contact, sisterly Jordan's support for unity, democracy, and legitimacy in our country.
