BFN [Text] The government yesterday declared a state of emergency in La Mar Province (Ayacucho Department) and in the neighboring Kimbiri district (located in Quillabamba Province, Cuzco Department) to expedite operations that the Armed Forces Joint Command is conducting in the area against terrorism and drug trafficking. According to a supreme decree published yesterday in the official newspaper, constitutional rights in the area will be suspended for 60 days. The same edition reports that the state of emergency has been extended for 60 days in Pasco Department and in the provinces of Huancayo, Concepcion, Jauja, Satipo, and Chanchamayo (Junin Department); in Castrovirreina, Huancavelica, and Huaytara Provinces (Huancavelica Department); and in Cangallo, Lucanas, and Huamanga Provinces (Ayacucho Department).
