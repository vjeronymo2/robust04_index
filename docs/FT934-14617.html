931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Graseby offshoot sold to management Graseby, the electronics group, has entered into detailed negotiations for the proposed sale of Graseby Keltek to its management for in excess of Pounds 3.5m. Keltek, a contract electronic design, development and manufacturing business, returned profits of Pounds 35,000 pre-tax on sales of Pounds 11.4m for the year to end-December 1992.
