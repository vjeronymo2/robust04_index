BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from the news segment of the "Dalniy Vostok" program] [Text] Parliamentary hearings devoted to an analysis of the condition and problems of the Russian state border have ended. The State Duma Committee for Geopolitics adopted a decision to set up a working group of qualified experts to comprehensively assess the possibility of concluding a peace treaty with Japan, taking into consideration the 1956 declaration on Shikotan and Habomai. As you know Russia signed this declaration in which the turnover of the islands of Shikotan and Habomai to Japan is stipulated as a condition for the signing of the peace treaty. State Duma deputies do not rule out the possibility of finding a compromise version in the territorial dispute with Japan.
