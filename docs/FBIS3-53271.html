Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening, Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa held a preliminary round of talks with French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe, who arrived in Cairo earlier today on a two-day visit to Egypt. Musa said President Mubarak will receive the French foreign minister tomorrow and that official talks between the Egyptian and French foreign ministers will begin at noon tomorrow. Musa said the talks will deal with European and international developments, the Middle East situation, African issues, and the situation in Bosnia- Herzegovina. The French foreign minister said France appreciates Egypt's role in the Middle East peace process. He noted that NATO yesterday stressed its commitment to a peaceful settlement in former Yugoslavia. Juppe said the OAU under the chairmanship of President Mubarak can play a role in solving the Somali problem.
