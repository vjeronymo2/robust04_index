Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Knin, 9 Mar (TANJUG) -- There has been no letup in attacks by the Croatian Army in the region of Kozjan, east of Korenica, for four days, ISKRA [Croatian Serb news agency] has reported. Fierce fighting took place yesterday and the aggressor used mortars, multiple rocket launchers, and infantry weapons, ISKRA says, quoting the information office of the Serb army of Krajina general staff. Serb defenders have been returning the attacks and are successfully holding the defense lines, the statement says. There was no peace in northern Dalmatia yesterday either. The Croatian Army opened fire from infantry weapons throughout the Drnis area, on the free territory of Kasic, and on villages in the Novigrad hinterland, and in the direction of Benkovac. There were no casualties in the attacks and the Serb defenders responded to all enemy attacks, ISKRA says.
