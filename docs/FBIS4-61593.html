BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 6 May TASS -- Yerevan -- The situation on Armenia's north-eastern border was tense last night, the press service of the Armenian Defence Ministry reported. Late on Thursday Azeri formations launched an offensive on the village of Paravakar in the Taush region of Armenia but had to retreat as Armenians opened fire in retaliation, the same source said. Armenian official sources say fighting continues in the northeast of Karabakh. Baku -- The Azeri Defence Ministry reported today that military operations had been going on in the zone of the Azeri-Armenian conflict throughout the past twenty four hours. Heavy fighting was reported in the Agdam, Terter and Geranboi directions. The Azeri Defence Ministry said that all attacks made by Armenian armed formations to break through the Azeri line of defence are being successfully rebuffed.
