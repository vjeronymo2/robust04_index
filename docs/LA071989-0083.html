July 19, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

INTERCHANGE CLOSINGS

Late-night bridge construction work will temporarily shut several portions of the Interstate 8-California 163 interchange this weekend, according to Caltrans.

The following sections will be closed: east I-8 ramp to north California 163; south California 163 ramp to east I-8; Hotel Circle to east I-8 on-ramp, and the right-hand lane of I-8, from Hotel Circle to its junction with California 163.

The areas will close shortly before midnight Friday and reopen at 8 a.m. Saturday. The areas will again close at 9 p.m. Saturday and reopen at 8 a.m. Sunday.
