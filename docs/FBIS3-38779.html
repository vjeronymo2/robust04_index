Language: Swedish Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "UN Will Not Receive Less Support"] [Text] (TT) -- The Swedish threat to reduce support for UN agencies will not be carried out following promised reforms. UNICEF, the UN children's fund, will receive the same amount as before -- 350 million kronor -- and the UNDP [UN Development Program] will get 146 million kronor, which is 10 million kronor more than in the last fiscal year.
