931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / New Shadow cabinet THE new shadow cabinet is: John Smith, opposition leader; Margaret Beckett, deputy leader and shadow leader of the house; Gordon Brown, Treasury and economic affairs; Jack Cunningham, foreign and commonwealth affairs; Tony Blair, home affairs; Robin Cook, trade and industry; David Blunkett, health; John Prescott, employment; Frank Dobson, transport and London; David Clark, defence; Ann Taylor, education; Donald Dewar, social security; George Robertson, Scotland; Joan Lestor, children and family; Ron Davies, Wales; Michael Meacher, Citizen's Charter; Mo Mowlam, national heritage; Jack Straw, local government; Chris Smith, environmental protection; Tom Clarke, development and co-operation. Appointments outside the shadow cabinet: Harriet Harman, chief secretary to the Treasury; Clare Short, women; Gavin Strang, agriculture; Kevin McNamara, Northern Ireland.
