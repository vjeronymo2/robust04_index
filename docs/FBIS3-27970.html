Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- "Paektusan Prize" games opened at the Handball Gymnasium in Chongchun Street in Pyongyang Tuesday. The annual games held around the holiday of February since 1977 are one of the large-scale sports contests in the country. The games are held in Pyongyang and local areas till the end of February. The participants compete in scores of events including field and track, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, boxing, wrestling (free style and greco-roman style), judo, swimming, shooting and archery. Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Kim Chung-nin, Vice-Premier of the Administration Council Chang Chol and officials concerned attended the opening ceremony.
