BFN [Report by Adilbek Kaipbergenov: "The Sum Does Not Weigh Down the Pocket"] [Text] The first trading has taken place on the Republic currency exchange in Tashkent. In accordance with its results, as of 16 April the Central Bank of Uzbekistan established the rates for foreign currencies against the national currency. Thus, the U.S. dollar is worth 3,637 sum-coupons, and the Russian ruble is worth 2.04 (on the black market -- 10). Another item of news -- for the country's guests. As of last Saturday, according to an explanation by a Central Bank spokesman, they can pay in local bills when buying an air or rail ticket, when checking into a hotel, etc., if they present a corresponding receipt from a legal exchange point.
