June 8, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

STRAWFLY SPECIAL FASTEST QUALIFIER

Strawfly Special won his first start on the West Coast in his Bay Meadows Futurity trial Wednesday night at Los Alamitos Race Course. Strawfly Special won by 2 1/2 lengths in the fastest time of seven time eliminations.

Strawfly Special, with Bruce Pilkenton up, finished 350 yards in 17.7 seconds, only four-hundredths off the stakes record.

The finals are schedule for June 17.
