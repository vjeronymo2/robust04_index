July 27, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BAD VIBRATIONS: Rep. William E. Dannemeyer repeated his frequently-voiced opposition to homosexuality in introducing his motion to expel Rep. Barney Frank from Congress for his relationship with a male prostitute (A1). . . . "There is something about homosexuality that sets Mr. Dannemeyer to vibrating, I don't know what it is," Frank said. "I think that there is a growing sentiment in the House of Representatives that Mr. Dannemeyer and reality only rarely coincide." Zan Dubin and Bob Schwartz
