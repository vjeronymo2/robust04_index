931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Mercury Asset Management Group MERCURY ASSET MANAGEMENT GROUP the UK's largest institutional fund management company, acquired a further 65.4m ordinary shares in Mirror Group Newspapers in the sale by Mr John Talbot of Arthur Andersen, the joint administrator to Robert Maxwell Group, the main private vehicle of the drowned newspaper magnate. MAM now holds some 81.6m shares, or 20.34 per cent of MGN.
