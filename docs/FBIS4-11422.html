BFN [Text] Rosa Elena Simeon, president of the Cuban Academy of Sciences [title as heard], said today in Barbados that highly qualified human resources are Cuba's essential instruments to trigger sustainable development. Speaking during the First International Conference on the Sustainable Development of Small Insular States, Simeon, who is the minister of science, technology, and the environment, said that in our country there are hundreds of thousands of workers, technicians, professionals, and scientists capable of finding alternative ways to progress, despite the difficult situation the country is enduring. The global conference must prepare the Barbados Declaration which will outline the essential guidelines and the financing to implement the sustainable development programs agreed upon.
