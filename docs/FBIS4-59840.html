BFN [Text] For the ensuring of law and order in the city of Kabul, police stations will be reactivated within the security zones of Kabul police. This point was expressed by Lieutenant General Qara Beg Ezedyar, acting minister of internal affairs, to the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY and he added: Because under the provisions of law, police duties are to ensure law and order, the leadership of the Ministry of Internal Affairs recently decided to replace the officials who have failed to discharge their duties in a merited way with faithful and professional people to restore law and order very soon in Kabul. With the reactivation of police stations, all irresponsible armed men will be expelled from the city. For this purpose, the commission of the heads of the Ministry of Internal Affairs departments has been assigned to assess the work and activities of security officials every day in Kabul, and to make due decision for the alleviation of people's problems. Similarly, henceforward uniformed police officials will be installed in Kabul and the movement of irresponsible armed men will be prevented. On top of this, the Ministry of Internal Affairs have worked out such serious plans, that with its implementation law and order situation will further be strengthened.
