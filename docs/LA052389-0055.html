May 23, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPOTLIGHT: SAN DIEGO IN BRIEF

UC San Diego's Dance Department has announced the program for its 13th annual faculty-student concert, at 8 p.m. June 3 in Mandeville Auditorium. The mixed bill will include "Harvest Moon" and "Trilogy," both by Patricia Rincon, "Relax" and "Headlines," by Margaret Marshall, and works by guest choreographers Alicia Rincon, Jean Isaacs and Judith Sharp. Ex-Broadway dynamo Barry Bernal, the newest member of the faculty, will premiere "Escape," a non-traditional jazz dance.
