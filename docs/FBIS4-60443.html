BFN [Text] Mahabad (West Azerbaijan Prov.), May 24, IRNA--Iraq's Kurdish Revolutionary Hizballah forces killed 17 attackers of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK] in Chuman and Galaleh regions over the past two days of fighting in northeastern Iraq. According to a Kurdish Revolutionary Hizballah spokesman here Tuesday [24 May] two combatants of the Kurdish Islamic forces also lost their lives as a result of clashes with Jalal Talabani's PUK. The source said another 11 PUK forces also were shot dead in an attack by Hizballah in the Be'erge and Qasruk regions. In related news from the Iranian city of Sanandaj, 11 PUK forces were killed by the Islamic Movement of Kurdistan (IMK) which managed to capture Sirvan camp, 5 kms off Halabja-Suleymaniya route on Monday. A source close to IMK said its forces also succeeded in gaining control of the strategic Hiro village in Qal'e Dizeh of Suleymaniya in an attack made on PUK positions over the past two days. The source said 15 other PUK forces, including PUK commander, Hassan Rasul Omar, were killed in two other attacks. Earlier, PUK had acknowledged the death of Hassan Kavestani, one of its top members who fell in the battle with IMK forces a couple. [sentence as received]
