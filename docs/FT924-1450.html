921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Successor to MacSharry Mr Padraig Flynn, Ireland's justice minister, has been appointed as his country's new commissioner to the EC commission, to replace Mr Ray MacSharry, who stands down next month, writes Tim Coone in Dublin. A conservative-minded former school teacher and publican, Mr Flynn, 53, is a devout Catholic and member of the Fianna Fail party.
