June 5, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

SUMMER DAY CAMP WILL SOON RESUME

The city will again offer its annual summer day camp, including daily activities and some overnight camp-outs, for boys and girls between the ages of 6 and 12. The camp will run for 10 weeks, from June 25 to Aug. 31.

Each camper must bring a sack lunch and have transportation to and from camp every day.

The cost for one week of camp is $65 per child, which includes all activities, a beverage and an afternoon snack. Hours are from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. The camp is also offering extended hours from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. for $75 per week per child.

Mail-in registration must be postmarked seven days before the first day of camp for the week selected. Mail registration to Camp Costa Mesa, P.O. Box 1200, Costa Mesa, Calif. 92628-1200.

Walk-in registration is from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. Monday through Friday at the Community Services Department at City Hall, 77 Fair Drive.

For more information, call (714) 642-1569 or (714) 754-5300.
