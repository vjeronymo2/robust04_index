920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / UK News (Employment): Oil and gas output expected to rise PRODUCTION of UK oil and gas is expected to rise steadily over the coming years before peaking later in the decade, according to the latest forecast from the Petroleum Services Group at Arthur Andersen, the accountancy firm. The firm predicts that oil output will rise to 2.1m barrels a day this year from 1.9m last year, reaching a peak of 2.7m b/d in 1995. The previous peak was 2.5m b/d in 1986. The firm predicts that gas output, which reached 5.2bn cubic feet a day last year, will rise steadily to a peak of 8.9bn cu ft in 1998.
