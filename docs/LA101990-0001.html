October 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MRS. KEATING'S PLEA FOR HER JAILED HUSBAND

It is now 18 months since many of us investors found out that we have lost $30,000, $40,000 or $50,000 through fraudulent representation by Keating.

First, I must say that Mrs. Keating is lucky she still has a husband; many of us investors do not. We meddle along financially and investment-wise hoping that what we do have will last for our lifetime.

Mrs. Keating, "good solid citizens" do not cheat other citizens out of their life savings. Yes, I do believe you are very naive. If Charlie were "a loving, caring and honest individual," I would still have my $30,000 investment intact and earning interest. I wouldn't be worried about my future.

JEANNE MOSER

Mission Viejo
