940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Smaller Companies Net asset value at the Smaller Companies Investment Trust rose from 124.65p to 136.87p over the year to June 30. However it was lower than the 146.41p achieved at the December 31 year end. Net revenue for the six months end-June was Pounds 718,000 (Pounds 204,000) for earnings per share of 1.46p (1.31p). However, the interim dividend is being held at 1.2p.
