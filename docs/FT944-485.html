941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Vega contract Vega Group, the software and systems engineering company, has won a Pounds 1m plus training contract. It will teach technicians working on the Tornado fighter bomber and, in the future, the Eurofighter 2000. In March, Vega won a competition for the Royal Navy's Sea Harrier F/A2 Maintainer Trainer, and in November it was awarded the RAF's Trade Group 3 Systems & Equipment Emulator.
