BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Vigdis Finnbogadottir, president of the Republic of Iceland, on the occasion of the national day of the republic. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the Icelandic people success in their work for the prosperity of the country.
