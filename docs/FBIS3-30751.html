Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Several SdRP [Social Democracy of the Polish Republic] politicians holding high government offices met this evening at party headquarters. There will be no comment; it was an informal meeting of several SLD [Democratic Left Alliance] ministers, the Social Democracy press spokesman, Zbigniew Siemiatkowski, said after the meeting. He also stated that the SLD would adopt a position on the prime minister's acceptance of Marek Borowski's resignation only after today's [8 February] meeting of the coalition. The spokesman also denied a report by REUTERS that SdRP leader Aleksander Kwasniewski has a candidate for finance minister.
