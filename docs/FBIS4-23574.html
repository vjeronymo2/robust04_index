BFN [Text] Huambo residents are still victims of the excessive ambition of the leading class of the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party, which orders the bombing of the city everyday. On 8 June at least 12 napalm and incendiary bombs were dropped on Huambo, killing 16 people and wounding 35, including women, children, and old people. A communique issued by the Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola General Staff, signed by General Arlindo Chenda Pena Ben-Ben, during the 8 June air raid, reports that 23 people were burned. The killings were executed by Su aircraft coming from the Catumbela Air Base.
