September 24, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

SHELTON SETS AIR RACE RECORD

Lyle Shelton set a speed record Sunday to win his third consecutive Unlimited Gold championship at the National Air Races.

Shelton, 57, of Granada Hills set a race record of 468.6 m.p.h., breaking his own 1988 record by more than 10 m.p.h.

Shelton, in his modified Grumman Bearcat, beat second-place finisher Skip Holm of Woodland Hills to win the $50,000 first prize.
