Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article by Mikhail Ovcharov: ``Tutayev Sour Cream Found to Harbor Dystentery''] [Text] A dysentery outbreak has been documented in Yaroslavl Oblast. One hundred fifty-five persons have fallen ill in the city of Tutayev, including 120 preschool children, while in neighboring Rybinsk 75 of the city's inhabitants are sick. It was discovered that all of them ate sour cream from the Tutayev Dairy. This dairy was closed for general cleaning and disinfection at the direction of the oblast state center for sanitary-epidemiological oversight. Production of cultured milk products has been temporarily banned.
