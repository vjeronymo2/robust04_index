930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Struggle goes on in Somalia A Somali distribution worker fights with a woman for a food bag in which she wanted to hide an extra wheat ration. The queuing for food continued in Mogadishu yesterday as Somali gunmen shot and wounded two American soldiers, one seriously, in an ambush in south Mogadishu. One of the soldiers said he and a colleague were trying to clear a roadblock.
