921027 FT 27 OCT 92 / World Trade News: Asia bus orders Walter Alexander, the Scottish-based bus builder, yesterday announced Pounds 15m worth of orders for double-deck buses from Singapore and Hong Kong, James Buxton writes. It will supply 85 air-conditioned double-deck buses to Citybus in Hong Kong, and 200 double-deck bus kits for assembly in Singapore. The orders will be completed in October 1993.
