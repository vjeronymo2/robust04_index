December 8, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

In the Valencia boys' tournament:

Servite 59, Westminster 48 -- Servite used a stifling man-to-man defense to defeat undersized Westminster, 59-48, for the championship of the Valencia tournament at Valencia High.

Adam Anderson, the only returning starter from Servite's Division III state champion team, led the Friars (4-0) with 19 points.

Anderson, a swingman, added eight steals and was named the tournament's most valuable player.

Guard Matt Kenney scored 16 for Servite and center Ryan Dodd added 10.

Westminster is 2-2.

Esperanza 81, Cypress 48 -- Forward Joe Foss scored a tournament-high 36 points to pace the Aztecs in the third-place game.

Katella 82, La Habra 60 -- The Knights were led by Kevin Carlson's 22 points in the consolation championship.

Valencia 80, Santa Ana 77 -- Santa Ana's furious rally -- it outscored Valencia, 35-22, in the fourth quarter -- fell short and Valencia won the fifth-place game. The Saints are 1-2. STUART MATTHEWS
