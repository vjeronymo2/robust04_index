Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi has stressed that Lebanon wants land in return for peace and wants sovereignty over its territory in return for peace. He said resistance is a result, not a cause of the Israeli occupation, and Israel's withdrawal will return things to their old situation. President al-Hirawi reiterated that there can be no just and comprehensive peace before liberating the south and Western al-Biqa', because this is the will of all Lebanese. President al-Hirawi was speaking at an iftar [fast-breaking] banquet hosted by Mrs. Randa Birri, head of the Lebanese Handicapped Society at the Summerland Hotel. The iftar was attended by Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and a number of ministers, deputies, and foreign diplomats.
