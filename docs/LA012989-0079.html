January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ISLAND REMEMBRANCES

Who is Payne? I turned to the Travel Section and read about two Irishmen gabbing in the middle of the night on the Isle of Jersey, instead of glancing at dry facts on the sights and hotel rates. That's what we all dream about when traveling -- getting close to the locals. And that seldom happens. So keep Payne at work, leading the life we dream of.

CHARLES AINSWORTH

Hollywood
