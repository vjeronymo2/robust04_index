920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / Whitehall sealed off for seven hours No-go area: police sealed off the Whitehall area of London for seven hours yesterday, severely disrupting traffic, after warnings that a bomb had been planted. An unexploded device was found in a phone box in nearby Parliament St. No one had admitted responsibility by early this morning but detectives said the device bore all the hallmarks of the IRA.
