940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Ayrshire Metal Pre-tax profits of Ayrshire Metal Products showed a 38 per cent improvement, from Pounds 572,000 to Pounds 792,000, in the six months to end-June. The advance was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 16.5m to Pounds 17.7m and was boosted by a lower interest bill of Pounds 16,000 (Pounds 63,000). An interim dividend of 1.25p (nil) is declared, payable from earnings of 5p (3.8p) per share. Directors said the outcome for the six months reflected the gradual improvement in the company's markets and all parts of the group were now profitable.
