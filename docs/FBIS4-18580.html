BFN [Excerpt] Deputy Defence Minister Nikolaos Kouris has accused Turkey of attempting to change the status quo in the Aegean. Continuous Turkish air violations over the Aegean were part of "a planned escalation of tensions." Ankara's intention was to create false tension that could lead to a military confrontation and to a Pax Aegean, a peace imposed by "a foreign intervention" as in former Yugoslavia, the minister said yesterday in a state radio interview.
