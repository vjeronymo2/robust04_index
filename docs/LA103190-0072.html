October 31, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP TENNIS ROUNDUP

Angelus League girls:

Santa Margarita 12, St. Joseph's 6 -- The doubles team of Heidi Garmon and Tiffany Brown swept, 6-0, 6-0, 6-4, to lead visiting Santa Margarita (8-4, 5-2).

Garden Grove League girls:

Santiago 14, La Quinta 4 -- Hien-Hahn Do swept, 6-1, 6-2, 6-1 for host Santiago (14-3, 9-2).

Sea View League girls:

Woodbridge 10, Saddleback 8 -- The doubles team of Helen Ho and Jenny Risquez won sets (6-1, 6-1, 6-1, to lead Woodbridge (10-7, 5-4) at Saddleback.

Corona del Mar 15, University 3 -- Keri Phebus won her singles matches, 6-0, 6-0, 6-0, as Corona del Mar (17-2, 9-0) clinched the league title.

South Coast League girls:

Capistrano Valley 13, Irvine 5 -- Paige Bartelt swept, 6-0, 6-1, 6-1, for visiting Capistrano Valley (14-4, 7-2).

In Olympic League girls' tennis:

Orange Lutheran 11, Valley Christian 5 (last two sets called, darkness) -- Jennifer Bodourian won her singles sets 6-2, 6-0, 6-2 and remained undefeated in league and the doubles team of Jennifer Dahlberg and Jaime McLean (6-4, 7-5, 6-2) and Michelle Fry and Jessica Ming (7-5, 6-3, 6-1) each swept for host Orange Lutheran (6-10, 5-4).
