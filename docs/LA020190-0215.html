February 1, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

ROSEVILLE TICKET WINS JACKPOT

One ticket had all six winning numbers in Wednesday night's "Lotto 6-49" game, earning its holder a $4.7-million jackpot, California Lottery officials said today.

The winning ticket was purchased in Roseville, about 15 miles northeast of Sacramento.

Four tickets had all five numbers plus the bonus number, each worth $328,000 individual shares of a $1.3-million prize pool. Those tickets were purchased in La Puente, Chino, Riverside and Fullerton.

The jackpot for next Saturday's drawing is expected to reach $5 million.
