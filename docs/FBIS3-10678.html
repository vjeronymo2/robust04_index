Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Today in Harare, Zimbabwean Vice President Simon Muzenda received the new Cuban Ambassador to Zimbabwe Pedro Nunez Mosquera and extended warm greetings to Cuban President Commander in Chief Fidel Castro. Muzenda expressed his thanks for Cuban aid to his country even before its independence. He stressed the positive development of bilateral cooperation between the two countries. The Zimbabwean vice president also praised the successful teacher training program on the Isle of Youth, south of Cuba. PRENSA LATINA reported that the Cuban ambassador expressed appreciation for Zimbabwean support in the area of international relations, and reaffirmed the Cuban Government's decision to strengthen mutual cooperation.
