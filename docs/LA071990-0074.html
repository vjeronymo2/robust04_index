July 19, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

LAGOONS PROTECTION GROUPS

There are many nonprofit organizations dedicated to saving and improving the North County Lagoons. They welcome new members and volunteers. Here is a partial list:

Bataquitos Lagoon Foundation: Dedicated to protecting and enhancing the lagoon by providing information, reviewing enhancement plans and development proposals in the watershed.

P.O. Box 3103

Carlsbad, Calif. 92009

The Buena Vista Lagoon Foundation: A nonprofit organization whose purpose is the restoration and conservation of this lagoon.

P.O. Box 157

Carlsbad, Calif. 92008

Los Penasquitos Lagoon Foundation: Organization active in enhancing and preserving the lagoon.

P.O. Box 866

Cardiff, Calif. 92007

The San Dieguito River Valley Land Conservancy: Formed to conserve, restore and enhance the river valley.

201 Oceanview

Del Mar, Calif. 92014

The Friends of the San Dieguito River Valley: Dedicated to establishment of open space in the river valley.

P.O. Box 973

Del Mar, Calif. 92014

San Elijo Lagoon Volunteers: County Parks and Recreation Department volunteers who patrol the lagoon for illegal hunting, fishing, dumping and other violations.

Contact Volunteer Coordinator Salley Argules, 694-3044.

San Elijo Lagoon Conservancy: Monitors and assists in maintenance, planning and management of the reserve.

2365 Newcastle Ave.

Cardiff, Calif. 92007
