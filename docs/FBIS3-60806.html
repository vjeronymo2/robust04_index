Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Cairo, 27 Dec (MENA)--A responsible security source at the Interior Ministry has stated that at around 0830 today, unknown gunmen fired on a minibus driving down a street in the Misr al-Qadimah district [of Cairo]. The vehicle belongs to the Express Travel Company and was carrying a group of Austrian holidaymakers. Six of the occupants were wounded and taken to al-Qasr al-'Ayni Hospital. [passage omitted] [In a related report, Paris AFP in English at 1026 GMT on 27 December adds: "Three of the Austrians were in serious condition, including one with serious eye injuries, while eight passers-by who tried to catch the attackers suffered varying degrees of bullet wounds. ["Witnesses said four militants rode up on bicycles as the bus with 16 Austrians on board was pulling away from the 'Amr Ibn al-'Ass mosque, the city's oldest. ["The assailants opened fire and threw a bomb, then escaped after turning their guns on local people who gave chase, police said.] [passage omitted]
