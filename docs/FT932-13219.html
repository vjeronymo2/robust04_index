930424 FT 24 APR 93 / Demirel set to become president Mr Suleyman Demirel, Turkey's prime minister yesterday received the endorsement of his True Path party DYP, the largest party in parliament, to succeed the late Turgut Ozal as president, John Murray Brown reports from Ankara. With the backing of the DYP parliamentary group, Mr Demirel is widely expected to be elected in the first ballot of deputies on May 8.
