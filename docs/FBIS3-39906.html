Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "AUA Participates in Tyrolean"] [Text] Vienna -- Austrian Airlines is going to buy 42.85 percent of Tyrolean Airways and will unite its regional flights with those of the Tiroleans. This will create a regional flight fleet of 23 planes, forming a counterpart to the regional flight plans of Lauda Air, which will begin regular flights with three jets as of March. Talks with Lufthansa about cooperation are to continue, but the basis for the talks has worsened.
