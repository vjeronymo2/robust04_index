930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Swiftcall granted telecoms licence SWIFTCALL, a company selling international calls from Britain to Canada and the US at substantial discounts from standard British Telecommunications tariffs, gained a licence and began operating yesterday. The Department of Trade and Industry granted it an International Simple Resale licence under which telecom circuits are leased in bulk and sold on. Swiftcall's tariff per minute is 25p to anywhere in north America, plus the cost of a local call to the network.
