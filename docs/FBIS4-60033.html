BFN [Text] Khartoum, 19 May (MENA)--Egyptian Ambassador to Sudan Hasan Jad-al-Haq today lodged a strongly worded protest with the Sudanese Foreign Ministry following the Sudanese authorities' seizure and closure of Egyptian buildings and clubs in Khartoum. The Sudanese authorities today seized the Egyptian Club in Khartoum and prevented its Sudanese and Egyptian members from entering. They also seized clubs and buildings belonging to the Egyptian Irrigation Authority in Sudan and the Egyptian military attache in Khartoum. The Sudanese authorities gave no reasons for these measures. The measures began in December 1992, when the Sudanese authorities seized Egyptian education mission schools, the Cairo University branch in Khartoum, and the Egyptian Nasir's Club in the Sudanese capital.
