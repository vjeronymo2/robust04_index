BFN [Text] Tirana, June 20 (ATA) -- Continuing to publish various documents proving the anti-Albanian activity of the Greek chauvinistic circles, the newspaper REPUBLIKA, in its latest issue, carried a letter by Sotir Qirjazati, chairman of Omonia, sent to the Greek Premier Mitsotakis on May 30, 1992. Through this letter, the chairman of Omonia, demands self-determination and autonomy of the Greek minority in Albania. Also, he considers the U.S.A. and some European countries as enemies, only because they are allies of Albania.
