Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Rabat March 14 TASS -- Russian president's envoy in the Middle East, Viktor Posuvalyuk, had a third consecutive meeting with PLO chairman Yasir 'Arafat in Tunis on Sunday [13 March] night. The Russian envoy was reported to deliver an important message from Moscow addressed to the PLO leader and related to Russo-Palestinian consultations held in connection with the aggravation of the situation in the Middle East following the Al-Khalil [Hebron] massacre. During the talks the sides discussed problems pertaining to a resolution expected to be adopted by the U.N. Security Council to ensure measures of international defence of Palestinians on Israeli occupied territories. The PLO leader is expected to visit Moscow at the invitation of the Russian president on April 9. Palestinian sources say that the sides would thoroughly discuss issues of the development of bilateral relations and Russia's role in the process of establishing Palestinian self-rule in Gaza Strip and the town of Jericho.
