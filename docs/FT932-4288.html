930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / US officials report mysterious illness in Gulf war veterans US officials yesterday said some Gulf war veterans may have a mysterious illness and possible causes could include depleted uranium as well as the Kuwaiti oil fires, Reuter reports from Washington. The commander of the army's main hospital, Major Ronald Blanck, told the Veterans Affairs subcommittee that there were a small number of cases in which soldiers may have inhaled depleted uranium dust. 'We have decided that it will be prudent to conduct annual medical evaluations for a period of at least five years' to determine whether the depleted uranium has caused any illness, he said.
