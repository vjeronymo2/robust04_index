940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / Nepalese UN troops killed in Somali clan gunfight Five Nepalese member of a United Nations peace force were killed and another was wounded when they tried to stop fighting between Somali clan militias in south Mogadishu yesterday, a UN military spokesman said, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. Witnesses said several Somalis were killed or wounded in the fighting in a maze of streets near the UN-controlled airport. 'I understand there was inter-clan fighting in the area prior to the attack. The Nepalese went in to mediate and were fired upon,' UN military spokesman Major Chris Budge said. 'I don't know whether they were caught in crossfire or directly attacked. At present I am leaning towards a direct attack.'
