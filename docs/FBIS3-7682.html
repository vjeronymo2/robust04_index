Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 29 -- A high-level military delegation of Cambodia led by General Tie Banh and General Tea Chamrat, co-ministers of national defence, arrived here today, beginning an official visit to Vietnam as guest of Minister of National Defence Gen. Doan Khue. The delegation was welcomed at the guest house of the Ministry of National Defence by Gen. Doan khue, Lt. Gen. Nguyen Thoi Bung, Deputy Minister of National Defence Nguyen Dinh Bin, Assistant to the Foreign Minister Pham Hao, vice president of the Vietnam-Cambodia friendship association, and other Vietnam senior officials. Cambodian Charge d'Affaires to Vietnam I.E. Chuong Tuon, and Military Attache Nomsophat were also on hand. Gen. Doan Khue warmly welcomed the visit and considered it a contribution to the enhancement of the friendly relations between the two countries and armies. In the afternoon, the Cambodian delegation held talks with a high-level military delegation of Vietnam. The two sides compared noted on how to develop the close neighbourliness between the two countries on the basis of the principles stated in the Vietnam-Cambodia joint communiques dated on Jan. 25,1992 and August, 1993 in the interests of the two peoples, and for peace and stability in the region. This evening, Gen. Doan Khue gave a banquet in honour of the Cambodian guests.
