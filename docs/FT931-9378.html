930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / UK Company News: River & Mercantile shows increase River & Mercantile Trust had a net asset value of 150.39p per capital share at December 31 1992 against 111.97p a year earlier. The value per income share was 67.94p compared with 63.18p. Net revenue for the year improved to Pounds 9.82m (Pounds 6.68m). Earnings per share amounted to 10.05p (8.72p) and the proposed final dividend is reduced to 2.45p (3.68p) making 9.2p (8.48p).
