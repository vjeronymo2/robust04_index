Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Indonesia welcomes the decision by the U.S. Government to lift its trade embargo on Vietnam. A Foreign Ministry press statement stated that, even though the embargo is a bilateral problem of the two countries, the United States Government's lifting of the embargo was heartening because this would stimulate trade and economic activities among the various nations. Indonesia attaches great importance to efforts aimed at further enhancing regional political stability, security, and cooperation. As a result, any effort made to develop normal relations with any nation in this region represents a positive approach which will assist toward achieving such an aim.
