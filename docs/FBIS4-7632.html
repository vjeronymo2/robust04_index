BFN [Text] Defense Minister Antonin Baudys today received Dainis Turlajs, commander of the Latvian Armed Forces, at the end of his visit to the Czech Republic. The Latvian representative held talks on mutual cooperation in the armaments industry. He was interested mainly in the surplus and unusable equipment of the Czech Army. The Czech side will consider further his proposal to possibly provide this equipment to the Latvian Army, together with training support and teaching how to operate the equipment.
