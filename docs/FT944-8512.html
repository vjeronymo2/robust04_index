941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Lincat acquisition Lincat Group, the USM-quoted designer and maker of commercial catering equipment, is acquiring Imperial Machine Company (Holdings), a catering and bar equipment manufacturer and supplier, for Pounds 6.1m. The consideration is satisfied by Pounds 501,000 in cash and Pounds 5.62m by the issue of 2.39m shares. It is also proposed that a dividend of Pounds 560,000 net be paid to the vendors. In the nine months to August 31 IMC made pre-tax profits of Pounds 651,000 on turnover of Pounds 4.24m. Its net assets of Pounds 2.5m at end-August will be reduced by the proposed dividend. Following the acquisition, Mr Paul Bouscarle, managing director of IMC, will join Lincat's board as chief executive. Lincat also announced that it was applying for a full quote.
