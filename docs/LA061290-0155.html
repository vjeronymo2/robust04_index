June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Suzi Chauvel has been promoted to executive vice president/corporate creative director for Ocean Pacific Sunwear Ltd. She joined the Tustin-based surf-wear company in 1987 as executive vice president of design. In her new position, Chauvel will continue to head the design department and be responsible for creative direction in other areas, such as advertising, marketing and display.
