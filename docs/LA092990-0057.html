September 29, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

San Marino 39, Hoover 0 -- San Marino amassed 401 yards in offense and limited Hoover (2-2) to 81 yards at San Marino.

San Marino (3-0) rushed for 302 yards and dominated from the outset. Jason Baffa scored on runs of 38 and five yards to give San Marino a 14-0 lead after one quarter. Alfonso Montoya rushed for 111 yards and two touchdowns in 14 carries.

Hoover's Chris Kalaleh had six receptions for 71 yards. Quarterback Rene Gabri completed eight of 26 for 74 yards. BRYAN RODGERS
