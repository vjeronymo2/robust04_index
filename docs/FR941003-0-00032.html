The window period for filing applications will open on November 15, 1994, and close on December 15, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 93&hyph;312; RM&hyph;8394] Radio Broadcasting Services; Gallup, NM AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission, at the request of Lewis J. Wallach, allots Channel 268A to Gallup, NM, as the community's fifth local commercial FM channel. See 59 FR 43, January 3, 1994. Channel 268A can be allotted to Gallup in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements without the imposition of a site restriction, at coordinates 35&hyph;31&hyph;30 North Latitude and 108&hyph;44&hyph;30 West Longitude. With this action, this proceeding is terminated. DATES: Effective November 14, 1994. The window period for filing applications will open on November 15, 1994, and close on December 15, 1994.
