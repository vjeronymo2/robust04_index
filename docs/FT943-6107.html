940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / International Company News: Evergreen posts TDollars 1.68bn profit Evergreen Marine, Taiwan's leading containerised shipping line, reported pre-tax profits of TDollars 1.68bn (USDollars 64m) for the year to June 30, up a flat 2.4 per cent from a year earlier, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei
