March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Another Spin for Sajak: Pat Sajak will continue to host "Wheel of Fortune," it was announced Tuesday by King World Productions Inc. and Merv Griffin Enterprises. Sajak's contract has been renewed through the 1992 season. It was also announced that Vanna White will continue to flip the letters. BETH KLEID
