BFN [Text] Moscow, 9 Jun -- Yevgeniy Ambartsumov, a member of the Russian State Duma who chaired the foreign policy committee in the former Supreme Soviet, has made a strong appeal against the Russian parliament's ratification of a Russo-Georgian treaty. In his words, Armenia has already become Russia's strategic ally in the Caucases; therefore, there is no need to make any concessions to Georgia.
