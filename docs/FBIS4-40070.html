BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow June 14 TASS -- President Boris Yeltsin gave on Tuesday a go-ahead to the Russian peace-keeping operation in Abkhazia, Deputy Defence Minister Georgiy Kondratyev told a press conference. Kondratyev, who is in charge of the operation, leaves for the conflict zone later in the day to provide for the deployment of the peace-keepers.
