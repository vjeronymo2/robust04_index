BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 16 -- The military leaders of Armenia, Azerbaijan and Nagorno-Karabakh are meeting in Moscow today to compare notes, with the mediation of the Russian Federation, on further steps towards the settlement around Karabakh. The CSCE's Minsk conference on Karabakh Jan Eliasson [as received] told ITAR-TASS in Moscow that he hopes that the forthcoming Moscow meeting will become an important phase in the process of the settlement of the Karabakh issue. Eliasson called for the participation of international observers in ensuring the implementation of the ceasefire accords. The mechanism of international verification was in the focus of the consultations held by the joint mission of the Conference for Security and Cooperation in Europe and Russia in the region. The Russian president's special envoy Vladimir Kazimirov, and the leaders of Armenia's defence ministry and the Karabakh army flew to Moscow from Yerevan on Sunday night to join the conference on Monday.
