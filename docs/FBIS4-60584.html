BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin has invited Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto to visit Russia. The visit, to take place at the end of the year, will be preceded by a visit by the Pakistani foreign minister to Moscow. A Foreign Office spokesman said that during the recent visit by Anatoliy Adamishin, Russian first deputy foreign minister, Pakistan had expressed its desire to increase economic and political relations with Russia and the Central Asian states. Answering a question about the purchase of Russian military equipment, the spokesman said there is no hurdle facing us regarding cooperation between the two countries. He said we want cooperation with Russia in all fields.
