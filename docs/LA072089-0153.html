July 20, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

ISRAEL WOMAN DIES; 16TH VICTIM OF BUS ATTACK

A 19-year-old Israeli woman died Wednesday of injuries suffered in the bus crash earlier this month caused by a Palestinian who grabbed the steering wheel and forced the bus into a 200-foot ravine.

The death of the woman, Tova Maimon, a soldier from the Tel Aviv suburb of Or Yehuda, raised the death toll in the July 6 crash to 16.
