Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime minister has said that acceptance of the Dunkel draft will help in accelerating the pace of development in the country. Addressing a public meeting in Nandial, Mr. Narasimha Rao said that the draft is of utmost importance for India to emerge as an economic power. Staying away from the draft will isolate the country in the emerging economic scenario, the prime minister added.
