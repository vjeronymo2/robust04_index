940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / Observer: Communewealth Life will never be quite the same again in Brick Factory Village or October Banner Soviet Farm Central Village, both in the Moscow region. For their pastorally-challenged names - along with a couple of hundred others - are about to disappear, replaced with something a little more in tune with the times. Like Little Bankrupthampton, perhaps, or Crisisbottom-in-the-doldrums. . .
