Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has handed over two informal papers to the Indian high commissioner in Islamabad containing ideas and proposals to take measures required for solution of Jammu and Kashmir problem and other issues. These papers have also been given by the Pakistani high commissioner in New Delhi to the Indian foreign secretary. One of the informal papers comprised modalities for holding a plebiscite in Jammu and Kashmir. He [as heard] said Pakistan -- with a view to extending informal ideas and proposals in developing future useful discussion on Jammu and Kashmir problem -- has sent these informal papers to India. Replying to a question, the spokesman made it clear that giving informal papers does not mean that Pakistan has proposed any schedule for next round of Pakistan-India talks.
