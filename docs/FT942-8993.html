940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / London Transport Advertising bids Four bidders for London Transport Advertising, the London Transport poster subsidiary, have been shortlisted. The four are: a management and employee buy-out team; More O'Ferrall, the UK's largest outdoor advertising contractor; a consortium led by Transportation Displays, the US transport advertising company, which also includes Hambro Group Investments and Baring Communications Equity; and a consortium led by Primesight, the UK poster company, and Giraudy, France's largest outdoor advertising company. The sale is expected to be completed by the end of July.
