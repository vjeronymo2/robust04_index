Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 9 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The president of Mongolia P. Ochirbat received today here the leaders of the parliamentary parties in opposition. There was an exchange of views on present political situation in the country between the head of the state P. Ochirbat, the general secretary of the Mongolian National Democratic Party E. Bat-uul, the chairman of the Mongolian Social Democratic Party B. Batbayar.
