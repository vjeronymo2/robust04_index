BFN [Text] A one month's extension to the emergency was approved by a majority of 46 votes in the parliament yesterday. One hundred and two members voted for the motion while 56 opposed it. Winding up the debate, Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe said that the government is committed to establishing peace in the North and East. It has kept channels open for a negotiated settlement. The government is seeking to restore law and order throughout the island. The prime minister said that the Penal Code will be amended so that offenders could be brought to justice immediately. Mr. Wickremesinghe invited the opposition to suggest amendments to the code.
