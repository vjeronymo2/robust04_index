921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Jenners lifts pay-out as profits rise 15% Jenners Princes Street, the Edinburgh department store, achieved a 15 per cent profits advance, from Pounds 379,000 to Pounds 437,000 pre-tax, in the half year to July 31. The result was struck on turnover up from Pounds 13.7m to Pounds 15m. The interim dividend is being raised to 25p (19p).
