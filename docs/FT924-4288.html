921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Former BBC Lime Grove Studios sold for redevelopment The former BBC Lime Grove Studios in west London has been sold for redevelopment into flats and houses. The 1.5-acre site, occupied by the BBC for nearly 50 years, has been bought by a joint venture between the Notting Hill Housing Trust, a non-profit-making housing association, and Country & Metropolitan, a property developer. The redevelopment of the site into 20 three-bedroom houses and 32 one-bedroom flats will cost Pounds 4.5m.
