940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / International Company News: China Synthetic Rubber up 36% Taiwan's China Synthetic Rubber, a carbon-black maker, posted pre-tax profits of NTDollars 159m (USDollars 6m) for the year to June 30, up 36 per cent from a year earlier. First-half revenues rose 25 per cent to NTDollars 595m. Carbon black is a petrochemical material used in the manufacture of tyres, paints and dyestuffs. Production was lifted by 30 per cent to 83,000 tonnes a year. At the end of this year annual capacity will rise to 92,000 tonnes.
