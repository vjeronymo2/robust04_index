930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Authorities owed Pounds 2.4bn poll tax ENGLISH LOCAL authorities are still owed Pounds 2.4bn in unpaid poll tax, it was disclosed last night. The community charge, which replaced the old rating system in April 1990, was itself replaced by the council tax this April. Junior environment minister Mr Tony Baldry said in a written reply that arrears of Pounds 2.4 bn were still owed to 400 local authorities in England on March 31.
