941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / UK Company News: ADR facility for Cluff Cluff Resources, the minerals, oil and gas exploration group, has been granted permission to establish an American Depository Receipt programme. The facility is being sponsored by the Bank of New York. Each ADR is linked to the underlying value of 10 Cluff ordinary shares and is currently being traded at about Dollars 7.15 (435p). Cluff's gold mining operations in Zimbabwe are expected to produce about 70,000 ounces this year with production rising to 100,000 ounces from 1995.
