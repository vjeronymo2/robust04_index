February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ITALY MAYOR'S SON FREED SHORTLY AFTER ABDUCTION

The 12-year-old son of a Naples politician was freed Saturday, less than 10 hours after being bundled into a car by two hooded men, police said.

Michele Di Falco, whose father is mayor of the Naples suburb of Quarto, was set free outside a movie theater. Police said the boy was in good health and no ransom had been paid.

Police had earlier arrested two youths on suspicion of taking part in Friday's kidnaping.
