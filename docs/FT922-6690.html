920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / International Company News: Seagram backs Du Pont over environment MR EDGAR BRONFMAN, chairman of Seagram, the international drinks group, fully supports Du Pont of the US in its efforts to phase out production of chemicals that may affect the ozone layer and find safer products for refrigeration and other uses, writes Robert Gibbens. Mr Bronfman was replying to Vancouver Greenpeace campaigner Mr John Mate, who said the environmental movement plans a multi-million dollar international campaign against all producers of CFCs and HCFCs, including Du Pont, ICI, and European firms. In a presentation at the Seagram annual meeting, Mr Mate claimed the distiller was condoning the destruction of the ozone layer because it held almost 25 per cent ownership of Du Pont.
