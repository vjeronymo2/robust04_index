931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / Letters to the Editor: Blitz Sir, Your correspondent in Moscow writes (October 25) commenting on a bomb explosiom at the central bank: 'This is not Colombia or Sicily, but Russia.' May I suggest the sentence to be rewritten as follows: 'This is not Colombia or Sicily or London, but Russia.' Carlo Santini, Via G Belli 96, Rome Italy
