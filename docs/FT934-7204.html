931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / Finance and the Family: Building societies attract Pounds 258m of net funds - At a glance Building societies attracted Pounds 258m of net funds last month - just over double the net inflow in September, which ended three months of net outflows. Commenting on the relatively subdued inflow at a time of the year when people traditionally build up savings to finance Christmas spending, Adrian Coles, association director-general, highlighted the stiff competition for retail funds at a time of low interest rates. He said postal savings accounts had been particularly successful in attracting customers.
