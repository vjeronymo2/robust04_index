BFN [Vadim Ogurtsov report: "Smuggler Arrested"] [Text] The Federal Counterintelligence Service's Rostov Oblast Directorate Press Service has announced that a Turkmen citizen, wanted as one of the leaders of a criminal group involved in the illegal large-scale trafficking of drugs from Southeast Asia to Central Asia and Russia, has been arrested in Rostov-na-Donu as a result of operational-investigative measures conducted in conjunction with the personnel of Turkmenistan's National Security Committee [NSC]. Criminal proceedings have been instituted against the detainee under Article 72-2 of Turkmenistan's Criminal Code. The criminal was handed over to this state's NSC 12 May.
