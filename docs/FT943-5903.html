940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / International Company News: Kuhne & Nagel ahead 11.7% Kuhne & Nagel, the freight forwarding group which floated shares on the Swiss and German markets in May, has reported a first half net income of SFr24.9m (Dollars 18.8m), up 11.7 per cent on sales of SFr2.52bn, ahead 14.4 per cent, writes Ian Rodger. The group said it was confident that it would at least reach its full-year net profit target of SFr50m for 1994.
