940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust Undiluted net asset value per share of Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust rose from 177.98p to 255.48p over the 12 months to end-April; fully diluted the figure was 232.18p against 167.57p. Earnings per share were 1.74p (1.29p) from available revenue of Pounds 522,719 (Pounds 385,655). There is a special interim dividend, in lieu of a final, of 0.9p for the year. Last time there was a single, final distribution of 0.75p.
