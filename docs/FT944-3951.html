941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: TR Technology falls Net asset value per share at TR Technology was 202.3p at October 31, compared with 266.38p a year ago and 16 per cent down on 241.17p at the April year-end. The Hambrecht & Quiet Technology Index rose 15.6 per cent over the half. The trust, which invests in an international portfolio of quoted technology companies, had net assets of Pounds 256.7m (Pounds 263.3m) at the period-end. Net revenue for the half was Pounds 1.75m, 10 per cent down on the Pounds 1.94m last time. Earnings per share emerged at 1.68p (2.2p restated).
