Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Where the Budget Will Go -- Defense Privileged"] [Text] Tirana -- Here are some of the main figures in a breakdown of the state budget for 1994. The data are taken from the Budget Bill just submitted to parliament. It is evident that the largest funds of more than 6 billion leks go to the Education Ministry. Most of this, or 4.4 billion leks, will go for local funding and only 500 million will be used for national funding. The Health Ministry comes second, with funds amounting to more than 4.9 billion leks. Two other ministries, those of construction and defense, are financed to the tune of more than 4 billion leks. The state budget also gives priority to the Public Order Ministry and the Ministry of Labor and Social Welfare, both of which receive more than 2 billion leks. Of a total budget of 74.140 billion leks, more than 63.927 billion leks will be used for central funding.
