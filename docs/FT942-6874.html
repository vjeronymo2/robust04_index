940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / International Capital Markets: Representative office in Beijing for Lehman Bros Lehman Brothers, the US investment bank, has opened a representative office in Beijing providing more evidence of the involvement of American financial houses in China. Mr Sherman Lewis, Lehman Brothers' vice-chairman, said the company would involve itself in fund-raising for infrastructure projects, the marketing of 'various debt instruments' and assisting with public offerings for larger enterprises. Lehman acted as sole agent for the Construction Bank of China in a private placement of debt securities in the US last August. It lead-managed last October's Dollars 300m dragon bond and is assisting a large Chinese corporation in a public offering. The US investment house plans to open an office in Shanghai by the end of the year.
