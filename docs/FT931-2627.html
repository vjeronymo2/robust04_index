930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Burford burdened by interest charge A sharp fall in interest income together with losses in its publishing division reduced pre-tax profits at Burford Holdings to Pounds 52,000 in 1992. That compared with previous profits of Pounds 698,000 and came on sales of Pounds 10.3m (Pounds 9.84m). Rental income contributed the bulk of the latter figure at Pounds 8.53m (Pounds 4.97m). The results from the property investment and publishing group were prepared under the FRS 3 standard. Publishing suffered losses of Pounds 143,000 (Pounds 443,000 profit) and property profits fell to Pounds 195,000 (Pounds 255,000). Interest income declined to Pounds 2.26m (Pounds 6.12m), leaving the net charge at Pounds 4.06m (Pounds 482,000). A final dividend of 0.65p makes a 1.15p (0.95p) total. Earnings per share amounted to 2.98p (1.82p).
