Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 7 -- Vice President Nguyen Thi Binh paid an official visit to Guinea-Bissau from March 1-4, as guest of the Vice President of the State Council Mr. Vasco Cabral. While in Guinea-Bissau, Vice President Nguyen Thi Binh paid a courtesy visit to President of the State Council Joao Bernando Vieira. She held talks with Vice President of the State Council Vasco Cabral. During the talks, the two sides discussed orientations and measures to promote the cooperative relations in economic, trade and other fields between the two countries. They expressed their belief that the existing fraternal friendship and solidarity between vietnam and Guinea-Bissau is the `carved stone' for the two countries to enter into new cooperation conforming to the conditions and interests of each country. At the end of the visit, the two sides issued a joint communique and signed agreement on economic, scientific and technological, cultural and trade cooperation. On this occasion, Vietnamese businessmen who accompanied Vice President Nguyen Thi Binh signed minutes of understanding on good exchanges with their partners in Guinea Bissau. Speaking to the press before leaving, Mrs Nguyen Thi Binh expressed her satisfaction at the good results of her visit to Guinea Bissau, the last leg of her Africa tour. She also expressed her belief that the traditional friendship between the two countries will further develop.
