940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / International Company News: Swire increases Coke interests Swire Pacific, the Hong Kong-based property, aviation and trading group, has raised its stake in its bottling joint ventures with Coca-Cola and plans to invest HKDollars 1bn (USDollars 129m) in bottling plants in China, writes Louise Lucas in Hong Kong. Swire will lift its share of Coca-Cola Swire Beverages to 87.5 per cent from 50 per cent. The joint venture will change its name to Swire Beverages, and Coca-Cola will hold the remaining interest. The company will also take an additional 2 per cent of Swire Bottlers and 19 per cent of BC Development, the holding company for the Coca-Cola bottling interests in three China provinces, and 29 per cent of the Taiwan Coca-Cola Bottling Company from Swire Beverages. Coca-Cola will invest the USDollars 18.5m from the sale to develop new operations in the region.
