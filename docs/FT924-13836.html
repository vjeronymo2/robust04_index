921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Brent Chemicals Dollars 5.3m purchase Brent Chemicals International is acquiring certain assets and the business of Chemical Systems of the US for a total of Dollars 5.3m (Pounds 3m). If certain conditions can be met Brent will also acquire the assets and business of Chemsys's Canadian affiliate for a consideration of up to Dollars 850,000. Sales of Chemsys in the 1991 year were Dollars 3.9m. Adjusting the pre-tax figures to reflect the change of structure under Brent's ownership would have shown profits of Dollars 900,000. Net assets at the end of the year were Dollars 600,000. Brent is paying Dollars 1.8m for the assets and business with a further Dollars 3.5m under a sub-contract manufacturing agreement with Chemsys and non-compete and consultancy agreements with the joint owners of Chemsys.
