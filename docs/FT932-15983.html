930408 FT 08 APR 93 / Career loans scheme expands AN EXPANSION of Career Development Loans was announced yesterday by Mr Michael Forsyth, employment minister. The government loans are provided in partnership with banks for vocational education. The minimum loan will be reduced from Pounds 300 to Pounds 200 and there will be no minimum course length. People who have not worked for at least three months will be able to apply for a loan to cover all of the course fees where the local Training and Enterprise Council approves the application.
