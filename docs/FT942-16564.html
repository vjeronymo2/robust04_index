940409 FT 09 APR 94 / UK Company News: Blue Circle to convert bond issue Blue Circle Industries, the cement maker, is to convert all of its Pounds 90m 10.5 per cent convertible capital bonds 2005 into preference shares which will then be redeemed. Each outstanding unconverted bond will be converted on June 21 1994 into one preference share. Interest will be paid from December 31 1993 up to but excluding the required redemption date. The preference shares will be redeemed at their paid-up value. Bondholders remain entitled to exercise their rights and to convert the bonds and exchange the resulting preference shares for ordinary Blue Circle shares. This would be at the exchange price of 260p per ordinary share at any time up to and including June 14 1994.
