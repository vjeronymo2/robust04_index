930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Grosvenor Develop net assets at 147.2p Grosvenor Development Capital, an independent venture capital fund, lifted net asset value from 130p to 147.2p per share over the 13 month period to December 31. The diluted figure was 134.9p (127.3p). Earnings per share were 2.52p (2.83p) or 5.61p (5.24p) diluted; the single distribution goes up to 1p (0.95p).
