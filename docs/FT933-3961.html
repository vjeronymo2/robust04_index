930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / International Company News: Argentina pursues utilities sell-off ARGENTINA intends to begin a new round of flotations of privatised companies in November with the sale of its minority stakes in two Buenos Aires electricity generators, writes John Barham in Buenos Aires. Mr Daniel Marx, the government's top financial negotiator, said the sale of 30 per cent stakes in the Costanera and Central Puerto power stations should raise about Dollars 100m for the government. Central Puerto, with 1,009MW capacity, and Costanera, a 1,260MW station, were privatised in April and May last year. Chilgener of Chile paid Dollars 92.2m for 60 per cent of Central Puerto, and Endesa, also of Chile, paid Dollars 90.1m for 60 per cent of Costanera.
