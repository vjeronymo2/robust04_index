Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today received a written message from 'Umar al-Muntasir, Libyan secretary of the People's Bureau for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation. The message deals with the latest developments in the Libyan-Western crisis in light of the UN embargo on Libya and Libya's contacts with the United Nations and international parties in this regard. The message expresses Libya's appreciation for the efforts being made by the Arab League secretary general and the need for continuing these efforts in the future.
