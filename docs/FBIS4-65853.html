BFN [Text] Erzincan, May 21 (A.A.) -- Villagers attacked Social Democratic Populist Party [SDPP] Deputy Mustafa Kul and 20 party members in Edebuk village in Tercan township in eastern Erzincan Province it was reported on Saturday [21 May]. Kul, speaking to AA [Anatolia Agency] reporters, said they went to the village to express sorrow for the death of nine civilians after an attack by the outlawed separatist terrorist organization which happened last week. He said: "Nearly 100 people attacked us and our cars with sticks and stones". Kul said the attack could be a reaction of the villagers against the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] attack. Erzincan Governor Recep Yazicioglu said he condemned the attack adding that an investigation has been launched into the incident.
