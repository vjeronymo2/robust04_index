940413 FT 13 APR 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 5m tennis club for David Lloyd Shares in David Lloyd Leisure rose 17p to 234p yesterday as the company announced it had acquired a 7.5 acre freehold site near Sutton Coldfield, Birmingham. The site, which is located close to Junction 7 of the M6, has planning permission for a tennis and fitness club similar to the company's latest facility at Renfrew, Glasgow. Construction will start immediately and is expected to be completed by February 1995 at an estimated cost, including fitting out, of Pounds 5m.
