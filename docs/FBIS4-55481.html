BFN [Text] Following the banning of the activities of the Hungarist Movement, the operations of the World National People's Rule Party [VNP] have also been declared illegal. With its first degree ruling, the Budapest Metropolitan Court has effectively dissolved the VNP. The court established that the activity of the party led by Albert Szabo offends the rights of people to live in dignity, which are enshrined in the Constitution. It also offends the rights of the individual and breaches regulations that determine the rights of association. The court's ruling is not absolute.
