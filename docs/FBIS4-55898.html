BFN [Excerpts] Doboj, 23 May (TANJUG) -- Muslim units have intensified their attacks and provocations against Serb defense lines and residential areas on all parts of the front in the Doboj area, the information service of the republic of Srpska army's 1st Krajina corps said this evening. Early this afternoon, Muslim forces fired some 10 shells on Doboj from their positions southwest of the town. Five civilians, including two children, were wounded in the attack and considerable material damage was caused. [passage omitted] At the same time, Muslim artillery fired more than 20 artillery shells on the town of Teslic and its suburbs, and launched an infantry attack on Serb defense lines southeast of Teslic. A number of civilians were wounded in the artillery attack against Teslic and considerable material damage was caused [passage omitted]
