BFN [Unattributed report under the "Chronicle" rubric: "Enough Food for All in Russia"] [Text] The Russian Government is going to adopt a decree "On Developing Wholesale Food Markets" "in the very near future," Russian Federation Vice Premier Aleksandr Zaveryukha stated at the opening of the all-Russia wholesale fair in Sochi Thursday. In his words, the aim of the creation of such markets is "to accelerate the movement of foodstuffs into the trade network and also to form real market prices for food and regulate food payments." The vice premier stressed that "with the stabilization of production," Russia's agrarian sector "is perfectly capable" of supplying the country's population with the necessary quantity of food.
