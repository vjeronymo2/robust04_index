920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Prince issues defence warning THE Prince of Wales has issued a veiled warning over the extent of defence cuts. In a foreword to an officially sanctioned book about the British Army in the Gulf, he says that the armed forces face their most profound changes for 40 years and hopes Britain will always be able to help others in a crisis. The Shield and the Sabre. Nigel Pearce. HMSO. Pounds 19.95.
