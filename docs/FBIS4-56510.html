BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 4 May (NA) -- The Grand Front (FG) and the Socialist Unity (US) today agreed to create a Political Action Commission that will outline a program of cooperation and the election strategy for an eventual alliance, with an eye to the 1995 elections. In addition, the leaders of the two coalitions decided that their members in the Constituent Assembly, which is to be held in the cities of Santa Fe and Parana, will work "together," and thus the two blocs will hold their first meeting in Congress on 6 April. The resolutions were adopted during a meeting at FG headquarters between the FG and the US. Attending for the FG were: Fernando Solanas, Carlos Chacho Alvarez, Patricio Echegaray, Marcelo Vensentini, Carlos Auyero, Anibal Ibarra, and Mary Sanchez; and for the US: Norberto La Porta, Alfredo Bravo, Hector Bravo, Guillermo Estevez Boero, Ernesto Jaimovich, Mario Mazitelli, and Jose Seoane.
