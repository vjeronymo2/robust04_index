921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Kitty Little back in the black Kitty Little, the USM-quoted manufacturer and marketer of consumer products, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 26,000 in the six months to June 30. That compared with losses of Pounds 65,000 for the half year to July 31 1991 and a deficit of Pounds 306,000 for the 1991 year as a whole. Mr Graham Webster, chairman, said it was difficult to give any indication for the full year but no further deterioration in group profitability was anticipated. Turnover for the period was Pounds 2.69m (Pounds 2.05m). Earnings amounted to 0.06p (losses 0.67p).
