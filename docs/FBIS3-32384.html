Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 5 Feb (EFE) -- Jose Pena Gomez, the presidential candidate of the social democratic Dominican Revolutionary Party [PRD], escaped without injuries Friday night from an attack, during which a person accompanying him, Melania Lora, received a bullet wound in her left leg. Lora, the leader of the Education Department of the Federation of Social Democratic Women, explained today at the hospital where she was admitted that the attack took place when Pena Gomez, senate candidate Milagros Ortiz Bosch, and she were participating in a march in Maquiteria, a densely populated east Santo Domingo neighborhood. She explained that the three had been in a vehicle. When they stepped out to initiate a walk in favor of the PRD, rocks were thrown at them from the roof of a nearby building and shots were heard. Lora affirmed that she was wounded because she was beside the presidential candidate and that the attack was directed against him.
