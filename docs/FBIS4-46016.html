BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, has declared that Pakistan will not give up its peaceful nuclear program under any circumstances. She was talking to a group of newsmen in Islamabad this evening. She made it clear that they were pursuing a well-thoughtout policy in this connection and Pakistan could ill afford to dispense with its knowledge in this vital field. She pointed that Pakistan has been subjected to three wars in the past and it was dismembered also. She recalled that Pakistan had never violated the Nonproliferation Treaty and it was India which violated it by exploding a nuclear device.
