April 6, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

The body of an unidentified man was found Thursday afternoon inside a small truck in a Del Mar Heights grocery store parking lot.

A San Diego police homicide team was investigating.

The man had a plastic bag over his head, police said. Employees of the store, at 2606 Del Mar Heights Road, said they recalled seeing the truck in the parking lot since Tuesday.
