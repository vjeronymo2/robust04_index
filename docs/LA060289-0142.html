June 2, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

MAN KILLS HIMSELF IN SHOOTING ACCIDENT

A 19-year-old man from Huntington Beach was killed Thursday night when he accidentally shot himself with a rented handgun at the Firing-Line Indoor Shooting Rangein Huntington Beach, authorities said.

Two friends, both juveniles, witnessed the shooting, Huntington Beach Police Lt. Chuck Poe said.

The name of the victim, who had rented a .380-caliber automatic handgun, is being withheld pending notification of relatives, he said.
