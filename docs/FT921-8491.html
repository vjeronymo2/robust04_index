920217 FT 17 FEB 92 / Post Office wins consumer favour MORE CONSUMERS are satisfied with the Royal Mail's postal service than with any other public utility, according to an NOP opinion poll. The survey, based on a sample of 2,000, found that 78 per cent were satisfied with mail services and 73 per cent with counter service. The least popular utility was BT, which recorded a customer satisfaction score of 60 per cent.
