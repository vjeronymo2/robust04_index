921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / World Stock Markets: Argentina BUENOS AIRES saw a 7.6 per cent rise in the all-share index to 11,544.11 as investors returned to the stock market after a run on the peso petered out. Turnover jumped to 70m pesos from 52m. All 20 blue chips scored big gains with 17 rising by 9 per cent. The biggest risers were Garovaglio and Nobleza Piccardo.
