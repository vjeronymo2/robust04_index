940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Argent ends first day at premium of 4% Shares in Argent, the property investor and developer which came to the market in a placing and intermediaries offer, went to a 4 per cent premium on their first day of trading. The turnover yesterday was 1.57m shares. The shares closed at 266p yesterday, compared with the offer price of 255p. Schroders sponsored the issue of 13.7m shares, which represents 25 per cent of the enlarged share capital. The flotation raised a net Pounds 27.1m for the company, which it will use to take advantage of additional investment and development opportunities. Warburg Pincus, the US venture capitalists, sold 2.2m shares, reducing its holding to 49.8 per cent of the company's share capital.
