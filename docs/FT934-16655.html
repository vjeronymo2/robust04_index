931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / International Company News: SME improves to L65.3bn SME, the Italian retailing and catering group which is being progressively privatised, saw net profits climb to L65.3bn (Dollars 41.1m) in the first half, from L41.8bn last year, writes Haig Simonian in Milan. The company predicted earnings for the full year would be in line with those of 1992. Italgas, the gas and water services group, said pre-tax profits for the first half fell by 4.8 per cent to L199bn.
