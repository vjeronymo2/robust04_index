Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] A contract has been signed with Turkmenistan to buy up to 15 billion cubic meters of natural gas. Also the pipeline that will carry the natural gas from that country to Europe will pass through Turkey. BOTAS [Pipeline and Petroleum Transport Corporation] Director General Hayrettin Uzun held a news conference in Ankara where he said that with the contract, Turkey will no longer be dependent on only one source for its natural gas. Also, he said, the contract will supply our demand for natural gas. Uzun said that BOTAS wants to be included in the consortium that is being set up to build the pipeline that will deliver the natural gas from Turkmenistan to Western Europe. An agreement will be signed on our participation in the committee, he said. Also, talks are under way to increase the 6 billion cubic meters of natural gas Turkey buys from Russia per year to 10.5 billion cubic meters.
