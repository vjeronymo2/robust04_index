Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hanoi Municipality People's Court has so far heard 145 corruption and smuggling cases, with a total of 175 defendants. Many of them formerly held important positions in the municipality's party and state system. Two defendants were sentenced to life, while nine others received prison terms from 15 to 20 years. The court will handle five corruption and smuggling cases in January 1994, including the case of Duong Van Than, a former cadre in the Son La organ of control, who smuggled 36 kg of opium.
