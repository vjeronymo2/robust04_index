930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: Abbey National and Halifax launch new fixed rate mortgages - At a glance Abbey National and the Halifax building society have both launched new ranges of fixed rate mortgages. Abbey National is offering first time buyers 6.99 per cent (APR 7.4 per cent) fixed until May 1996. The arrangement fee is Pounds 90, and buyers must also take out buildings and contents insurance with Abbey. There is a penalty of 90 days' interest for early repayments. Other fixed rate offers, ranging from 6.75 per cent (APR 7 per cent) fixed for 15 months, to 8.95 per cent (APR 9.5 per cent) fixed for 10 years. Arrangement fees vary between Pounds 200 and Pounds 300, and early redemption penalties range between 60 days' interest and 300 days' interest. All mortgages are portable. Halifax's range is from 7.25 per cent (APR 8.4 per cent) fixed until 1996, to 8.95 per cent (APR 9.3 per cent) fixed until 2003. Rates are slightly lower for first time buyers, who must also buy insurance products. Arrangement fees are Pounds 200-Pounds 300, early repayment penalties are variable; mortgages are portable after February 1994.
