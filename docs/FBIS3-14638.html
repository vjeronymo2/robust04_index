Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Jerusalem -- Fatah in the occupied territories has called on the brother strugglers abroad to act immediately in order to overcome all differences, to mend the rifts, to affirm and reinforce the principle of Fatah brotherhood, and to rally around the leadership of the movement's leader, Yasir 'Arafat, with the aim of thwarting the enemies and their henchmen. Fatah also warned those whom it described as conspirators who issue leaflets against some leading officials in the movement.
