BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Twelve members of the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terrorist organization who were preparing to commit a terrorist attack have been caught in Istanbul. According to a statement issued by the Istanbul Security Directorate, the security forces found out that a PKK member who received special training in the use of explosives in a camp near Athens, Greece, had entered Turkey. Antiterrorist teams carried out an operation during which this person and 11 of his friends were caught. During their interrogation, the terrorists admitted that they were getting ready to perpetrate an attack. In the places where the 12 terrorists were staying, police found a Kalashnikov automatic weapon, a pistol, a hand grenade, two Molotov cocktails, counterfeit identity cards, and organizational documents. One of the terrorists has been sent to Kahramanmaras for interrogation.
