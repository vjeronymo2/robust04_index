BFN [Text] With 35 votes in favor and 31 against, the Riigikogu readopted today in an unamended form the law on presidency, which the president had failed to promulgate. In his resolution, President Lennart Meri referred to the law on presidency being in conflict with the Constitution because the law on presidency allows the prime minister the right to be the first to decide whether state requirements are urgent. The Riigikogu first adopted the law on presidency on 23 March this year, and the president failed to promulgate it [words indistinct].
