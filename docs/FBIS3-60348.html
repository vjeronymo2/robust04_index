Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Complete text of article obtained through NIKKEI BIZ DATABASE] [Text] The Environment Ministry summarized the initial requests for the FY94 budget for global environmental protection made by 17 ministries and agencies, including the Environment Agency, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Science and Technology Agency. The total amount requested is ¥566.2 billion, an increase of ¥31.8 billion and 6 percent over FY93's actual budget. Concerning individual items, at ¥445.7 billion, the largest amount requested is for the "Development and Spread of Technologies for Global Environmental Protection," an increase of 3 percent over the previous year. The "Promotion of the Development and Use of Nuclear Energy" (Science and Technology Agency) is requesting ¥276.35 billion. Newly requested items include the "Establishment of Technological Standards for Gas Stations for Low-Pollution Vehicles" and a "Safety Study for a Sulfur Sodium Battery," both by the Ministry of Home Affairs.
