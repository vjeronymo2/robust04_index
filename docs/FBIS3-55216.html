Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, Jan 5 (AFP) - Former Georgian President Zviad Gamsakhurdia has committed suicide, his wife Manana was quoted by Interfax as saying Wednesday [5 January]. Gamsakhurdia's wife, who was in the Russian Caucasus republic of Chechnya, said the ousted president killed himself in a west Georgian village on December 31. Gamsakhurdia was ousted from power in January 1992 after a brief civil war.
