921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / International Company News: TWA deal with Icahn approved A US bankruptcy court approved a deal that brings to a close financier Mr Carl Icahn's stormy control over Trans World Airlines, Reuter reports. The court approved a TWA settlement with Mr Icahn, the airline's creditors, pension guarantors and union, that would wipe out over Dollars 1bn in TWA debt and transfer ownership of the airline from Mr Icahn to its employees and unsecured creditors.
