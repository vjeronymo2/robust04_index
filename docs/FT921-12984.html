920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / International Company News: Yamanouchi acquires 29% of Roberts YAMANOUCHI Pharmaceutical of Tokyo has taken a 29 per cent stake in Roberts Pharmaceutical of New Jersey in a stock purchase valued at about Dollars 95m. Yamanouchi will buy 4m newly-issued shares in Roberts at Dollars 23.84 a share. The Japanese company will have two seats on Roberts board and will have the option of increasing its stake in three years. The move appears to be part of a growing trend of alliances between US and Japanese pharmaceutical companies. The companies said they would explore areas of co-operation, including giving Yamanouchi access to Roberts' development, marketing and sales network in the US. Roberts may have access to Yamanouchi's manufacturing facilities.
