921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Parliament and Politics: Reshuffle of shadow cabinet by Smith MR JOHN Smith, the Labour leader, yesterday announced a mini-reshuffle of his shadow cabinet after this week's election of Mr Ron Davies to fill the gap left by the resignation of former shadow heritage secretary, Mr Bryan Gould, Ivo Dawnay writes. Mr Davies, the Caerphilly MP who had been acting as Labour's agriculture spokesman, will take on the Wales portfolio from Ms Ann Clywd. Ms Clywd now occupies the shadow national heritage position which was vacated by Mr Gould when he quit in October over Labour's policy on Europe. The Labour leader has appointed Mr Gavin Strang, an agricultural scientist, to take over the agriculture portfolio.
