930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Bricom chief appointed BRICOM'S bankers have appointed Mr Tony McCann as chief executive with a brief to develop a long-term strategy for the company. Bricom started as a management buy-out from British & Commonwealth before being taken over in 1990 by a group of Swedish investors led by Gamelstaden. It is now controlled by its bankers after running into financial difficulties. Mr McCann, who has worked for Black and Decker and Littlewoods, said he planned to identify the core-businesses and those which might be for sale. Bricom recently sold Spinneys, a group of marketing and retail companies, to Inchcape, the international services and marketing group, for Pounds 32.1m.
