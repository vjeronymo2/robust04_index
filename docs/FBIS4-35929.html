BFN [Report by Muhammad 'Abd-al-Hadi from Aden] [Text] Sanaa has denied that foreign parties or Iraqi forces are fighting on the side of Sanaa's forces against the southern forces. Aden has officially announced the capture of a number of Iraqis who fought with the northern forces in battles that raged between the southern and northern forces. 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Jifri, vice president of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, has this to say: [Begin al-Jifri recording] Brothers from Sudan and Iraq are helping the brothers-enemies who are attacking us. There are certainly Iraqi pilots. They fly the planes at very high altitude so that their planes will not be brought down and be captured. The planes used are Iraqi and north Yemeni ones. There are so far five Iraqi prisoners and they are in Hadramaut and I have just been told that there is one more. They are six now. [end recording]
