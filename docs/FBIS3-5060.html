Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 3 (KCNA) -- Production is moving upward in different sectors of the national economy of Korea. The Yongsong combined general bureau of machines overfulfilled its January and February plans and is keeping a high rate of production in March. The Musan mining complex, a leading producer of iron headings, beat its February target at 100.1 percent and fulfilled the first-day quota of March at 120 percent. The Sunchon district coal mining complex overfulfilled its February plan and is now carrying out its daily assignments at 110 percent. The Kim Chong-tae general electric locomotive enterprise has brought the production of electric locomotives and passenger coaches to an all-time high. The Pyongyang spinning mill and other spinning mills topped the February goal of silk yarn 20 percent, greatly helping the Pakchon, Yongbyon and Palwon silk mills boost production. The February plans were overfulfilled by the March 17 general hydro-power enterprise, the October 5 general automation works, the Yanggang Provincial combined general bureau of forestry and many other units, which are now expanding the achievements in March.
