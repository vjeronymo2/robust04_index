931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Armour Trust litigation withdrawn Agreement has been reached to settle litigation between Armour Trust, Random Investments of Jersey and Mr Cyril Freedman, a former director of Polco Products and Polcon. Under an agreement dated October 1985, Armour Automotive Group acquired Polco and Polcon and agreed to buy Random's holding at fair value. Armour will acquire Random's holding of AAG loan stock for Pounds 49,323 and will issue 103,000 shares to fund the purchase of Random's 2.75 per cent stake in AAG.
