March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY OVER LEGALIZATION OF DRUGS IN AMERICA

Regarding the article by Sweet: I'll bet the drug dealers who come before him in court feel pretty lucky. Two often used quotations come to mind: "Putting the cart before the horse," and "Throwing the baby out with the bathwater." The costs of alcohol are a poor track record for legitimizing drugs.

JERRY F. JONES

Mission Viejo
