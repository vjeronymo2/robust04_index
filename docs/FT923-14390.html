920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Oceonics sharply ahead to Pounds 2.72m A high level of offshore activity in the North Sea helped Oceonics Group report a substantially improved annual performance. On sales of Pounds 28.9m (Pounds 20.5m) for the year to March 31, the group, now refocused as a precision navigation and marine surveying company, achieved Pounds 2.72m at the pre-tax line, up from Pounds 938,000 last time. Net cash of Pounds 300,000 at the year-end compared with debt of Pounds 2.9m, partly reflecting the successful Pounds 2.6m rights issue late last year. Earnings, after allowing for an unpaid preference dividend of Pounds 324,000, were 1.2p (0.1p).
