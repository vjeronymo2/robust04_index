BFN [Text] Brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, today arrived in the fraternal state of Kuwait, on a visit during which he will deliver a letter from Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council in the Democratic Republic of Yemen, to Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, the ruler of the state of Kuwait. In a statement on arrival in Kuwait, the brother prime minister praised the efforts being made by a number of Arab states toward halting the war currently going on in our country, stressing that these efforts are appreciated by our Yemeni people. Brother al-'Attas said that his visit to Kuwait was being paid in the framework of his effort to provide information on the dimensions and the facts of the situation in our country.
