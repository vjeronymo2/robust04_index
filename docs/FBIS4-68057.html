BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 May (MENA)--Security forces in Asyut have found the body of a 25-year-old bearded man, wearing peasant's clothes on the eastern desert leading to Asyut. The body was riddled with bullets. This was according to an Interior Ministry security source, who added that the security forces found the body lying near an automatic rifle bearing serial number 6245 and 12 bullets, one inside the rifle barrel, and 11 empty cartridge cases. They also discovered one plastic bag three meters away from the body and another ten meters away each containing 50 kg of TNT. An unidentified person telephoned the Abnub police station and said that while he was driving on the desert road to Suhaj at 0300 today he saw people exchanging gunfire which forced him to quickly return to report the incident. He said the site was around two km south of the eastern approach to Asyut. The security forces, the criminal laboratory experts, and the police moved to the area where they found scattered cases used for packing fruits, men's clothes dropped on the sand beside the road, and a small rug. The public prosecution office has been informed and investigations are underway to identify the body. The interior ministry will issue a statement later on the incident.
