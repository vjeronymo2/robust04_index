July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

READERS HAVE THEIR SAY ON DANNEMEYER

Several times in the Dannemeyer piece, the word "sincere" is used to describe (or explain?) the representative's condemnation of anyone who does not live his lifestyle or hold dear his prejudicial attitudes.

I don't recall ever meeting a bigot who wasn't sincere, unfortunately.

KAREN AITCHISON

Los Angeles
