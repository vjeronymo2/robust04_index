BFN [Text] Aden, Yemen, May 6 (AFP) -- Southern warplanes fired missiles at the presidential palace in the Yemeni capital Sanaa on Friday [6 May], officials said here. The attack left the palace in flames, said the officials from the Yemeni Socialist Party of deposed vice-president 'Ali Salim al-Bid. His forces have been battling the northern army of President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih.
