BFN [Text] Our reporter has just told us that a few minutes ago the Croatian and Muslim delegations ended their meeting held at the Croatian mission. They have agreed, among other things, to set up, within the next seven days, a commission in charge of missing persons. The commission will be set up on a parity basis. Toward the end of the month, the Constitutional Assembly of the Bosnia-Herzegovina federation will meet to elect the federation's president and prime minister, and pass several laws.
