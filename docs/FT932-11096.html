930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Gulf Air in Airbus deal GULF AIR, the regional carrier, agreed yesterday to purchase eight new aircraft from Airbus Industrie, Reuter reports from Manamah. Mr Abdullah Saleh al-Manna, Gulf Air chairman and Qatar's transport minister, gave no details of the purchase. In March the company said it would add four Airbus A-320s and four Boeing 767s to its 36-aircraft fleet in 1993.
