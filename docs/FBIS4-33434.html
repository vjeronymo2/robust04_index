BFN [Text] Havana, 5 Jun (PL) -- Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani has sent a message to Cuban leader Fidel Castro with a high-level Iranian delegation that arrived here to discuss strengthening cooperation between the two countries. An official note released here indicated that the document was delivered during a meeting between President Fidel Castro and Minister of Construction Jihad Gholam Reza Foruzesh. During the meeting, they discussed topics of bilateral interest which will help strengthen economic cooperation relations between the two nations, the note added. The source added that Foruzesh arrived in Havana on 1 June. Among other activities during his stay, he held talks with officials, ministers, and scientists.
