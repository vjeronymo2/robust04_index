Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] In Minsk, a regular meeting of the Conference of Patriotic-Democratic Forces has been held with the participation of the United Democratic Party of Belarus, the Belarusian Social Democratic Assembly, the Belarusian Peasant Party, the Christian Democratic Union, the National Democratic Party, the Party of People's Accord, the Society of Belarusian Language, and the Belarusian Association of Servicemen. The participants unanimously described the dismissals of Stanislav Shushkevich and the "power" ministers as a nomenklatura's coup and a path to Kebich dictatorship, which will eventually lead to a collapse of the state and economy. The members of the conference expressed their intention to unify activities of all patriotic-democratic forces to resist the dictatorship. Unification is to be discussed at the next meeting.
