921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Genesis Chile Fund asset value falls Genesis Chile Fund had a net asset value of Dollars 24.83 (Pounds 16.40) per participating share at September 30 against an adjusted Dollars 26.01 a year earlier. Net revenue amounted to Dollars 4.89m (Dollars 4.59m) for earnings per partici-pating share of 60 cents (72 cents adjusted). The dividend is unchanged at 56 cents.
