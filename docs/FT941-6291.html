940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Ocean sells liquid storage arm Ocean Group, the industrial and distribution services company, has completed the sale of its bulk liquid storage operation, Panocean Storage & Transport, for Pounds 7m. Tate & Lyle, through its United Molasses division, has bought Panocean Hull and Panocean's Birkenhead terminal for Pounds 3.7m. GATX Terminals has bought Panocean's Eastham terminal on Merseyside. The UK disposals follow the sale of Panocean's non-UK activities in March last year.
