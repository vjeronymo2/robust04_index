BFN [By Nobuyuki Sakai] [Text] Warsaw, 6 May -- During a meeting on 6 May, Minister of International Trade and Industry [MITI] Eijiro Hata officially informed Polish Foreign Economic Relations Minister Leslaw Podkanski of the partial resumption of underwriting investment insurance for Poland, which has been suspended since 1991. Japan will resume guaranteeing insurance related to investments and short-term (less than two years) trade insurance from 15 May. Hata is visiting Poland to attend the East-West Economic, Industry, and Trade Ministers Conference. It was also decided that Japan would send experts to Poland as advisers to help the Polish Government implement its industrial development policy.
