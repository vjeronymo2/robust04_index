930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Record rises 22% to Pounds 984,000 Despite a background of poor trading conditions, with overseas markets generally remaining difficult, Record Holdings, tool manufacturer, raised pre-tax profits by 22 per cent from Pounds 809,000 to Pounds 984,000 in the first half of 1993. Turnover was lower at Pounds 13.81m (Pounds 13.97m). Interest took Pounds 184,000 (Pounds 8,000 received), but with the company continuing to improve operating efficiency and reduce costs, earnings per share were up 44 per cent at 1.76p (1.22p). The interim dividend has been maintained at 1.15p
