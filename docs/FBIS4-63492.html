BFN [Text] Kiev, 20 May -- The Ukrainian National Assembly-Ukrainian National Self-Defense Organization [UNA-UNSO] people's deputies have submitted a draft resolution on settling the crisis situation in Crimea for consideration by the Supreme Council session. The UNA-UNSO resolution makes the following provision: 1. All Crimean administrative structures should be disbanded. 2. Emergency martial law should be introduced on the territory of Crimea. 3. All Crimean officials who are guilty of inflaming interethnic animosity, separatism and extremism should be arrested and held criminally responsible. 4. Additional Army and National Guard units should be brought into the territory of the peninsula to protect the civilian population from extremists. 5. Crimea's status as a republic should be annulled. 6. The performance of administrative functions in Crimea should be temporarily entrusted to the Ministry of Defense.
