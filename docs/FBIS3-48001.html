Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [YONHAP from Beijing] [Text] According to a Chinese source on 20 January, China has given North Korea at least 7.5 billion won a year in military aid, even after the reduction of its aid to North Korea a few years ago. This source, well versed in North Korean affairs, said that he "had recently heard this directly from a senior official of the Chinese Government," and said, "the details of the Chinese military aid to North Korea are not known. It has been learned, however, that the night infrared detection equipment is one of the items provided by China to North Korea." The source also said that in addition to the military aid, China has annually provided North Korea with large-scale assistance of energy, including oil and coal; and of food including beans, corn, and rice, and that the annual food assistance reached about 15 billion won.
