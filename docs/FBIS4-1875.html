BFN [Text] Hangzhou, April 4 (XINHUA) -- A United Nations-sponsored training course on technologies of small hydropower stations started here today. The two-month course has drawn more than 40 students from 20 countries. Small hydropower stations offer clean energy, and China has more than 60,000 such stations generating a total of 48.2 billion kWh of electricity. To date, 20 training courses have been held since the program was organized in Hangzhou, capital of East China's Zhejiang Province, in 1981.
