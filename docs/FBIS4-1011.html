BFN [Text] Johannesburg April 18 SAPA -- Security forces have cordoned off Thokoza hostel following the Monday [18 April] morning gunbattle between hostel inmates and National Peacekeeping Force [NPKF] troops, SABC TV news reports. News photographer Ken Oosterbroek was caught in the conflict and died before he could receive treatment. Two of his colleagues were injured. NPKF spokesman Col Connie van Rensburg said the situation was still tense on Monday night.
