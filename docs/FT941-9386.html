940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Asset growth at Mid Wynd Net asset value at Mid Wynd International Investment Trust amounted to 439.3p per share at December 31. The figure compared with values of 362.3p at June 30 and 326.8p at end-December 1992, and directors of this Baillie Gifford-managed trust said the value had increased further, to 449.9p, by the end of January. Available revenue for the six months dropped to Pounds 151,650 (Pounds 200,792), reflecting sharply reduced investment income, for earnings of 3.02p (4p) per share. Nevertheless, the interim dividend is raised to 2.5p (2.4p).
