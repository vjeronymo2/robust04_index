Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 1 December -- Today is World AIDS Day. According to the presidential committee for fighting AIDS, there are 87 infected people in Kiev, of whom 27 are Kievites. The Ministry of Health Protection reports that there are 141 registered cases of AIDS in Ukraine, 56 percent of whom are men. In total, 12 people have died as a result of AIDS, and four of these were children.
