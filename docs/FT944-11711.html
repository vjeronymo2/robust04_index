941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: J Smart down 19% to Pounds 1.65m J Smart (Contractors), the building and public works concern, reported a 19 per cent fall in pre-tax profit to Pounds 1.65m for the year to July 31, on sales of Pounds 17.2m. This compared with Pounds 2.04m on sales of Pounds 12.8m in the previous 12 months. The dividend for the year is maintained at 8.5p, including a proposed final of 6.2p. Earnings per share fell to 10.02p (13.26p).
