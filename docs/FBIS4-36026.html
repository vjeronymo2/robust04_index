BFN [Excerpt] Shaykh 'Abd-al-Majid al-Zandani, Presidential Council member and chairman of the Higher Committee for Receiving Donations, and a number of the members of the Higher Committee, attended the expanded meeting which was held at the Cultural Center in Sanaa today. The meeting included ulema, merchants, businessmen, shaykhs, university teachers, officials in charge of public, mixed, and private companies and commercial agencies, and dignitaries in Sanaa. The meeting was aimed at discussing the issue of supporting the war effort and organizing the process of receiving donations and public aid in support of the effort to defend unity and constitutional legitimacy and to ease the damage caused by the battles being waged by the forces defending constitutional legitimacy, unity, and democracy.
