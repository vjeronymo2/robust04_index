November 11, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

US Facilities Corp.: The Costa Mesa-based insurance company reported net income for the third quarter of $307,000, contrasted with a year-earlier loss of $1.2 million. Revenue rose 54%, to $16.4 million from $10.6 million. For the nine-month period, net income rose to $1.3 million from a loss of $1.7 million for the comparable 1988 quarters. Revenue increased 57%, to $45.4 million from $29 million.
