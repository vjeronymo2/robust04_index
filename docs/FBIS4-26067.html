BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Government departments have enacted and begun implementing measures of strategic importance aimed at accelerating Shanghai's economic relations and trade with foreign countries. Wang Zukang, chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Commission, yesterday briefed the press on the specific measures. Among the specific measures were those calling for accelerating implementation of a plan to export leading products; for planning a number of major projects and products in accordance with Shanghai's overall economic development goals and changes in the international market; and for accelerating the transformation of foreign trade operational mechanisms. Also included were those aimed at focusing on building large enterprises and general commercial establishments which combine trade, industry, and commerce and which can increase market share at home and abroad, as well as those aimed at speeding up port construction, improving port functions, and diversifying foreign markets.
