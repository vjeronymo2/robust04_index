BFN [From the "TV Today Newscast"] [Text] Guyanese President Cheddi Jagan arrived this afternoon on an official three-day visit. He was welcomed by Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin, who told the media that the reported presence of Bishop Nel Beltran in Cuba for an alleged guerrilla summit has not affected the good relations between Colombia and the island. [Begin Sanin recording] I will meet with the prosecutor general to hear his final conclusions on this investigation. The Colombian Foreign Ministry always said it believed the bishop. We also said there were indications, but there were no conclusions on the matter. Fortunately, we did not rush into this or jump to any conclusions. When you wanted us to make some sort of statement, we said we would not make any statements until Cuba responded. [end recording]
