BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 3 (XINHUA) -- The Hangzhou City People's Procuratorate instituted a prosecution here today against three accused in the case of the boat-fire tragedy which claimed the lives of 32 people, including 24 Taiwan tourists, in east China's Zhejiang Province. Public trial of the case will be handled soon by the Hangzhou City Intermediate People's Court. A collegiate bench has been formed by the Hangzhou City Intermediate People's Court, in accordance with the law. Copies of the indictment have been sent to the three accused -- Wu Lihong, Hu Zhihan and Yu Aijun. On March 31, the pleasure boat "Hairui" was set ablaze on Qiandao Lake in Zhejiang Province. No one aboard survived.
