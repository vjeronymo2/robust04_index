November 14, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

FUR PROTEST GOING TO 5TH AVENUE

Television game show host Bob Barker kicks off the Christmas gift-buying season by taking his animal rights crusade to New York's swank 5th Avenue.

Barker said Monday he will lead a Nov. 24 demonstration sponsored by Trans-Species Unlimited to publicize the group's objections to killing animals for use by the fur industry.
