941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / Business and the Law: Return to City - Legal Briefs Ms Denise Kingsmill, the employment lawyer who counts Mr Peter Woods of Direct Line and Mr Cyril Stein of Ladbrokes among her clients, has returned to practise in the City. Ms Kingsmill has joined City solicitors Denton Hall after a year working with Sir Patrick Sheehy, chairman of BAT Industries, as a member of his Rainbow consortium's bid for the National Lottery.
