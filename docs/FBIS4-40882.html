BFN [Text] The head of a Ukrainian military delegation, Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy, has held several meetings in Washington's White House. Intensive talks at different levels have been held during the Ukrainian delegation's official visit to the United States, the Ukrainian Defense Ministry Press Service reports. In Chicago, Vitaliy Radetskyy and his colleagues were warmly greeted by the local Ukrainian community. In his address to its members, Radetskyy dwelled in detail on the course of Ukraine's army building. He stressed that the present state of military and technical capability in the Ukrainian Armed Forces is quite sufficient for the defense of the Ukrainian state. At the same time, Radetskyy pointed out the problems of servicemen's social protection. The most urgent of these, he said, is the housing problem, in the solving of which Ukraine could use, as far as possible, the assistance of the Ukrainian community in the United States.
