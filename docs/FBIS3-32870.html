Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Executive Branch, which has called for an extraordinary session of Congress to discuss, among others, the labor reform draft, has ratified that it will insist on its own labor contract draft bill that has the business sector's approval and the labor unions' rejection. On 31 January, the president signed the call for sessions that, when Congress starts to meet in about 10 days, will propose the discussion of some 25 items. Among the items is the laws regulating the universities' financial system and the draft bill on invention patents and traffic, and amendments to the Social Security and film-making laws, the tax on personal assets, and the penal and procedural (regarding preventive custody) codes.
