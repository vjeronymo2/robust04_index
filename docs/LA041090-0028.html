April 10, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Seven new members have been elected to the board of directors of Ballet Pacifica.

New members to serve three-year terms are Georgia Connolly; Tony Cortez; Chuck Daniels; Janet Eggers; Glenn Engman; Martha Morgan, and Anne B. Nutt. Nancy Locke and Charles Colgan were reelected to additional three-year terms. Elected officers are Scott Carnahan, president; Kim Luce, first vice president; Bill Steel, second vice president; Jan Scherer, secretary, and Marilyn Lynch, treasurer.
