BFN [YONHAP from Vienna] [Text] A spokesman for the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] said on 6 May that the IAEA would not dispatch to North Korea its inspection team to observe the changing of fuel rods at North Korea's nuclear reactor. He said: The IAEA will not send its inspection team to North Korea because the letter North Korea recently sent to the IAEA does not provide any ground for the dispatch of the inspection team [chosadanul pagyonhalmanhan patangul chegonghaji anko isso] to North Korea.
