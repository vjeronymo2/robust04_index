[NV&hyph;930&hyph;4210&hyph;06; N&hyph;57922] Second Public Meeting/Hearing on Air Force Proposed Withdrawal Adjacent to Nellis Range Complex, Nevada HJanuary 20, 1994. AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management, Interior. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: This notice sets forth the schedule for a second public meeting/hearing to be held on the Air Force's proposed withdrawal application. This meeting will provide another opportunity for public involvement in the Air Force's proposed withdrawal the purpose of which is to ensure the public safety and the safe and secure operation of activities in the Nellis Range Complex. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Curtis Tucker, Area Manager, BLM Caliente, Caliente, Nevada 702&hyph;726&hyph;8100. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Notice of Proposed Withdrawal for the Air Force proposed withdrawal was published in the Federal Register on October 18, 1993, and a correction notice was published on November 5, 1993. The same notice was also published in the Las Vegas Review Journal. A second public meeting/hearing to gather information as to the proposed withdrawal will be held as follows: ␣ 1Meeting address 1Date 1Time Cashman Field Center, Rooms 203&hyph;204, 850 North Las Vegas Blvd., Las Vegas March 2, 1994 5&hyph;8 p.m. PST.
This meeting will be open to all interested persons, including those who desire to be heard in person and those who desire to submit written statements on the proposed withdrawal. Oral statements will be limited to 3 minutes. Written comments can be submitted at the meeting. Robert G. Steele, Deputy State Director, Operations.
