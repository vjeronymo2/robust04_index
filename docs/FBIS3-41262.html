Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0600 GMT on 2 February reports that "the Haifa District police have arrested 10 drug dealers in the Haifa suburbs. The drug dealers were incriminated by an undercover agent. In the operation, the police seized 20 grams of heroin and thousands of shekels." Tel Aviv DAVAR in Hebrew on 4 February carries an ITIM report on page 5 stating that "48 kg of hashish were seized yesterday afternoon in Upper Galilee, not far from the border fence with Lebanon. The drugs were found in the possession of two known drug dealers from Bet She'an at a roadblock manned by the police Lebanon-border unit and Galilee District policemen."
