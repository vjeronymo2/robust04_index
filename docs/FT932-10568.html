930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / International Company News: Chrysler names financial chief CHRYSLER, the US car manufacturer, yesterday named Mr Gary Valade as chief financial officer in place of Mr Jerome York, who resigned earlier this week to become chief financial officer of International Business Machines. Mr Valade, 50, has been Chrysler's vice-president for corporate personnel since last September and before that was corporate controller of the group. Most of his career has been in controller's office functions. He becomes an executive vice-president of Chrysler and joins the office of the chairman, an inner circle which advises Mr Robert Eaton, the chairman.
