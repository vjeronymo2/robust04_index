November 3, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA;

MCMULLEN IS FINAL CUT BY ATLANTA

Former San Diego State center Mitch McMullen was released by the Atlanta Hawks Thursday as the NBA team reduced its roster to the 12-player regular season limit.

McMullen, a free agent, played in six of the Hawks' eight exhibition games, averaging 1.5 points and 1.8 rebounds per game. STEVEN HERBERT
