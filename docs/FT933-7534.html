930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / International Company News: Quebec government retains Goldman Sachs THE Quebec government has retained Goldman Sachs, the New York investment bank, to help evaluate Sidbec-Dosco, a steelmaker with about 1.5m tonnes annual capacity that it indirectly owns, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The government has been trying to privatise Sidbec for several years, but the company has been losing money because of the recession.
