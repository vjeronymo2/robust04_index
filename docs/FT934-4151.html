931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Baird to make Pounds 3m deferred payment William Baird, the textiles and engineering group, has confirmed that the additional consideration of up to Pounds 3m for the acquisition of Richard I Racke, will be settled in full on March 31 1994 in accordance with the timetable agreed. Baird said it was satisfied the profit targets would be met and now wanted to proceed with the merger of Racke with its existing own-label women's wear activities.
