Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 18 (XINHUA) -- More than 200 Chinese and U.S. scientists and engineers met here today for a two-day seminar on safety control technology. Senior researchers from Triconex of the U.S., a leading international research and manufacturing firm in tripled fault-tolerant technology, said that they will co-operate with their Chinese counterparts in developing and introducing advanced safety and control systems for use in key Chinese industries. Fault-tolerant technology, a by-product of the U.S. space industry, provides critical safety control for the nuclear, chemical, electric utility, aerospace, petrochemicals and other industries. William Barkovitz, president of Triconex, said that this year his firm is planning to form a number of joint ventures in safety and control technology with Chinese industries.
