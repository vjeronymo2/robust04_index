December 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: PRESIDENT SELECTED FOR MT. SAC BOARD

Gary Miller has been selected as president of the Mt. San Antonio College Board of Trustees. Miller, a West Covina resident, has spent five years on the Mt. SAC governing board and is a special-education teacher with the Pasadena Unified School District.

Barbara Booth, an eight-year board member and resident of Diamond Bar, was chosen vice president. Denise Hamilton
