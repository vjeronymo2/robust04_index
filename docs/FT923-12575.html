920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Quality Care Pounds 4m placing QUALITY CARE Homes, a north-east England-based company which owns and operates nursing homes for the elderly, yesterday placed 3.6m shares at 136p raising Pounds 4.05m. Most of the shares were taken up by institutions. The company, until now entirely owned by founder and managing director Mr Duncan Bannatyne and his family, has applied to join the main market. Dealings are due to start on Thursday. The placing comprises 27.2 per cent of the enlarged issued share capital and gives the company a market capitalisation of Pounds 18m. It is forecasting pre-tax profit of not less than Pounds 1.75m in the year to end-October. In the first six months turnover was Pounds 2.77m, against Pounds 3.32m in the full year to end-October 1991. The company has has 11 freehold nursing homes.
