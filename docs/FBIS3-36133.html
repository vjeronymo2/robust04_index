Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Albert Kochetkov and Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Tbilisi, 2 Feb -- Immediately upon arrival at Vazyaniy Airport of the group of Russian troops in Transcaucasia, Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev paid a visit to the 405th motor rifle Regiment stationed here. Accompanied by commanders of the group of Russian troops in Transcaucasia, the head of the military department acquainted himself with the regiment's combat readiness, the professionalism of its personnel, and their morale. During his meeting with commanders and officers of the unit and his tour of the military hardware fleet, of the canteen, and the barracks of the personnel, Grachev discussed the procurement of material and technical supplies, foodstuffs, and finances, as well as maintaining the present infrastructure, which includes military and civilian campuses, schools, and community facilities, in a good condition. In an interview with an ITAR-TASS correspondent, the Russian defense minister expressed satisfaction with his visit to the regiment. "I came to Tbilisi on a working visit today to familiarize myself with the condition of the Russian military facilities located here, and to meet and talk with people, from the rank and file to the generals. But tomorrow I shall be taking part as a member of a Russian Government delegation in talks between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Georgian Head of State Eduard Shevardnadze," the minister said.
