940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Dixon Motors rights issue and listing Dixon Motors, the car dealer which came to the USM last year after reversing into Plateau Mining, is to raise Pounds 6.8m net of expenses via a 1-for-3 rights issue of 3.94m shares at 180p apiece. The proceeds will be used to reduce borrowings and provide additional working capital for site development and acquisitions. The company has also applied to the Stock Exchange for an official listing. The existing ordinary shares will continue to be traded on the USM until admission, which is expected on June 6.
