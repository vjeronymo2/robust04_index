930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / World News In Brief: Union proposes pay overhaul IG Metall, Germany's biggest and most powerful union, has proposed a complete overhaul of pay and conditions strategies as its contribution to overcoming the country's economic crisis. The union would give up its demands for high pay rises 'in the coming years' in exchange for improved working and performance-related conditions and more say in the running of companies.
