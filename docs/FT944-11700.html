941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: German Inv Trust The net asset value per share of the German Investment Trust stood at 112.1p at the half year ended September 30, against 109.4p 12 months earlier. The outcome represented a 6.2 per cent decline from its level of 119.5p at the March 31 year end and compared with a 5.4 per decrease in the FT-A World Index for Germany in sterling terms over the period. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 201,000, up from Pounds 137,000 in the comparable period, and earnings came out at 0.51p (0.35p).
