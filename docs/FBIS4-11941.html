BFN [Text] The resignations of the minister of state for railways, Mr. K.C. Lenka, and the minister of state for finance and parliamentary affairs, Dr. Abrar Ahmed, have been accepted. According to a Rashtrapati Bhavan [President House] spokesman, the president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, accepted their resignations on the advise of the prime minister. The two ministers have tendered their resignations as they ceased to be members of the Rajya Sabha from today.
