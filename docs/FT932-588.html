930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Observer: Over promoted Another myth bites the dirt. In 1992 US manufacturers pumped out 310bn coupons, 6 per cent more than in 1991, with an aggregate face value of some Dollars 180bn, according to Worldwide Coupon Distribution and Redemption Trends, published by NCH Promotional Services of Illinois. Fortunately for the manufacturers and their shareholders the US public actually redeemed only Dollars 4.5bn of the total.
