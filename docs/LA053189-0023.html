May 31, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV/VIDEO

Correspondent Correspondence: Judd Rose has been named a correspondent for ABC's new prime-time news series that is tentatively titled "Prime Time." Rose, a Washington-based correspondent for ABC's "Nightline," will move to New York to be part of a team that includes anchorwoman Diane Sawyer, anchorman Sam Donaldson and chief correspondent Chris Wallace. . . . At CBS, "48 Hours" correspondent Bernard Goldberg has been promoted to special correspondent. Goldberg got the new job title in a new contract. He'll continue to be on "48 Hours" but also will be seen on other broadcasts, including the "CBS Evening News." ALEENE MacMINN
