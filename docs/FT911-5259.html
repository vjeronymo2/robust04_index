910415 FT 15 APR 91 / Construction Contracts: Hall & Tawse Group wins Pounds 7m work HALL & TAWSE GROUP, the construction arm of Raine Industries, has more than Pounds 7m worth of new business in February. The bulk of contracts has been awarded to Hall & Tawse Scotland - particularly the company's joint-venture division, which has clinched more than Pounds 1.5m of new work, including a Pounds 925,000 sheltered housing scheme in St Monans, Fife, in partnership with Kingdom Housing Association. The Aberdeen-based company has also received a Pounds 1.5m contract for a special care housing scheme in Edinburgh for Port of Leith Housing Association.
