Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 12. (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The State Great Hural (SGH) session set to consideration of the draft law on statistics of Mongolia in the first reading. As it was noted at the session of the Mongolian parliament, the task of the law on statistics is to define the basic principles of a single system of statistical information in Mongolia, regulate relations connected with information and statistical research. In the draft law reflected are an idea about official and unofficial statistics, about official and unofficial information, which was not reflected before in the legal acts in this respect. According to the draft law, the state and public organizations, private economic units and individual citizens of the country can carry out official and unofficial statistical activity. Discussion of the draft law on statistics continues.
