October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE GODFATHER'S SON

How offensive! Featuring Anthony Brooklier on the cover, and with the sentimental mush in the article about what an upstanding citizen he is!

He couldn't help being born into that "family" -- and growing up with organized crime -- but he could have used his education to defend victims of their drug-running, murderous "business." M. CAMPBELL

Los Angeles
