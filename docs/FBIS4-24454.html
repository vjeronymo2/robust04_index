BFN [Text] Beijing, May 27 (XINHUA) -- The planned amount of 27 billion yuan worth of two-year state treasury bonds for this year was sold out by the end of May 25, five days earlier than the target date, according to sources from the Ministry of Finance here today. In addition, the public have purchased about 40.25 billion yuan three-year bonds so far, accounting for 67 percent of the total issue of 60 billion yuan. Treasury bond sales has been rather smooth this year, thanks to the sound economic environment and swelling personal purses, said a finance official in charge of the issue. He was confident that the remaining 20 billion three-year bonds would be sold out by the end of June as planned. The interest rate for 1994 two-year treasury bonds is 13 percent, and 13.96 percent for three-year ones. Both are higher than the interest rates for bank deposits of the same term.
