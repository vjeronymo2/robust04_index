Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Informed sources have affirmed that they have seen the tyrant Saddam's forces massing 3 km from the border strip with Kuwait in the area between Umm Qasr and (Hafr al-Batin). Our sources in al-Basrah quote these sources as saying that Saddam's authorities are currently sending reinforcements to their forces there consisting of armored units and advanced tanks, which are being moved at night from various main points in the al-Basrah, al-Nasiriyah, and al-'Amarah Governorates. The sources said the troops massing on the border with Kuwait are among the elite units of the Presidential Guard, which underwent many courses and combat training recently.
