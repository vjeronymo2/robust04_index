930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / International Company News: Chargeurs in TV investment CHARGEURS, the French textile and media group, is expanding its satellite broadcasting interests by investing FFr200m (Dollars 80.8m) in CanalSatellite, the satellite channel launched last autumn by the Canal Plus television group, writes Alice Rawsthorn. The investment from Chargeurs, which is already involved with the BSkyB direct broadcast satellite channel in the UK, forms part of a FFr300m capital increase for CanalSatellite. Canal Plus has launched CanalSatellite as a move into other areas of television.
