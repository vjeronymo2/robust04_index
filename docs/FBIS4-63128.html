BFN [Text] Almaty, May 19 (BTA) -- The Bulgarian delegation visiting Almaty to attend the Days of Bulgaria in Kazakhstan, led by Deputy National Assembly Chairman Kadir Kadir, was received today by Kazakh Foreign Minister Kanat Saudabaev [name and position as received]. Mr. Saudabaev made a high assessment of bilateral relations and stressed the need for the two countries to work actively in implementation of the treaty signed by the presidents of Kazakhstan and Bulgaria -- Nazarbayev and Zhelev, in 1993, a press release of the Bulgarian Foreign Ministry reads. Bulgarian First Deputy Foreign Minister Valentin Gatsinski informed the Kazakh foreign minister of the priorities in Bulgaria's foreign policy. The two sides discussed issues related to bilateral relations and observed the need to further promote trade and economic contacts, the press release says.
