930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / Arts: Today's Television BBC2's generally good series of sports documentaries On The Line deals today with England's first-class cricket umpires. Who knew that after a county match umpires are 'marked' by the team captains? With only a seasonal contract and the sort of intimidation of umpires now practised by players, the job looks pretty unappealing (8.30).
