BFN [Text] According to Palestinian sources, a secret agreement has been made allowing Palestinian National Authority Chairman Yasir 'Arafat to visit Jerusalem and pray at al-Aqsa Mosque after he enters the self-rule areas, reportedly at the end of next week. Notwithstanding the Israeli media campaign against president 'Arafat's announcements during the past few weeks that he will definitely visit Jerusalem, the sources--who are closely watching the developments of the PLO-Israeli relationship--say that during their recent meeting in Cairo 'Arafat and Rabin signed a secret agreement by which Israel will allow the Palestinian president to visit Jerusalem and pray at al-Aqsa Mosque one week after he arrives.
