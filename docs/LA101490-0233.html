October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MAURICE SENDAK

We read with great interest "The Dark World of Maurice Sendak," (Sept. 16.) but felt there was one major omission. Through Oct. 28, Sendak's paintings and drawings for the L.A. Opera Co.'s production of "Idomeneo" will be exhibited at Every Picture Tells a Story, 836 N. La Brea Ave., Los Angeles.

LEE COHEN

EVERY PICTURE TELLS A STORY

Los Angeles
