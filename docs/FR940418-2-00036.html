Army Science Board; Notice of Closed Meeting In accordance with section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub.L. 92&hyph;463), announcement is made of the following Committee Meeting: Name of Committee: Army Science Board (ASB). Date of Meeting: 11 May 1994. Time of Meeting: 0800&hyph;1730. Place: The Pentagon, Washington, DC. Agenda: The Army Science Board's Summer Study Panel on ``Capabilities Required to Counter Current & Evolving Threats'' will meet to hear briefings on and discuss advanced and novel technology forecasts, operational enhancements and future force structure/doctrinal implications. This meeting will be closed to the public in accordance with Section 552b(c) of Title 5, U.S.C., specifically subparagraph (1) thereof, and Title 5, U.S.C., appendix 2, subsection 10(d). The classified and unclassified matters to be discussed are so inextricably intertwined so as to preclude opening all portions of the meeting. The ASB Administrative Officer Sally Warner, may be contacted for further information at (703) 695&hyph;0781. Herbert J. Gallagher, COL, GS, Executive Secretary.
