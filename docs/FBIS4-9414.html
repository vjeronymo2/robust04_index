BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 Apr--The congress of the Freedom Union adopted a resolution today that says the process of rebirth of the state and its economy, initiated in 1989, is under threat. The congress obliged the party authorities to prepare a strategy for "the second step along the road of reforms in Poland." The national congress found essential the earliest possible setting up of specialized secretariats to analyze the situation in particular spheres of social and economic life. "Their joint task would be to formulate the strategy of the second step along the road of reforms in Poland and winning its allies' and social partners' support for it," the resolution reads. People heading these secretariats would constitute a sort of "shadow cabinet."
