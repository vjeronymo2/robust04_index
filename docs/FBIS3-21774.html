Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos described yesterday's decision by the EU [European Union] Commission not to send Greece to the European Court for its measures against Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] as positive. In a statement made today, Venizelos said the EU Commission's decision puts the weight on the political aspect of the issue, something which the Greek Government has continuously stressed. Responding to a question about the revelation in a television program regarding heroin processing plants in Skopje, Venizelos said that the Greek Government will examine the evidence that was reported and bring it to the attention of the other Balkan countries. He said that it is necessary to clarify the situation and the responsible ministers have already been informed and are working on the issue.
