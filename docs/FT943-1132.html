940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): London West End After a successful summer season at the Birmingham Rep, the new New York musical 'Once on this Island', greeted in Birmingham in July as 'infectious', opens in London at the Island Theatre (formerly the Royalty) on Wednesday. An all-black cast, Afro-Caribbean music, a Caribbean theme. Lynne Ahrens has provided book and lyrics; Stephen Flaherty the music.
