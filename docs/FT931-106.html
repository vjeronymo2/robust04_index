930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Allied London Props Allied London Properties, the developer and housebuilder, achieved a 10 per cent advance in pre-tax profits - from Pounds 2.62m to Pounds 2.89m - in the six months to December 31. Net rental income increased by Pounds 691,000 to Pounds 8.98m. An unchanged interim dividend of 1.075p is declared, payable from earnings of 2p (1.7p) per share.
