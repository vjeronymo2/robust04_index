June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: INVESTOR RAISES SHARE IN HCC INDUSTRIES TO 12%

Investor Walter Neubauer announced that he has increased his stake in HCC Industries and may seek control of the company or propose a merger.

In a filing with the federal Securities and Exchange Commission, Neubauer said he had increasing his holdings in the Encino-based company to 205,100 shares, or 12.29% of HCC.

Neubauer said he is "pursuing discussions with a financial institution" about the possibility of a merger proposal or tender offer, and had also discussed the issues with some stockholders.

HCC makes electronic connection devices for the aerospace industry.
