940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Singapore may buy F-18s The Clinton administration yesterday offered to sell 18 McDonnell Douglas F-18 fighter jets and missiles to Singapore for Dollars 1.5bn despite a controversy over the caning of American teenager, Michael Fay, Reuter reports from Washington. The Pentagon informed Congress that Singapore was considering whether to purchase the F-18s instead of buying a similar number of Lockheed Corp F-16 fighter jets at a lower cost.
