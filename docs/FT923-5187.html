920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Pipeline fittings buy for Alumasc Alumasc, a designer, manufacturer and marketer of products for the brewery, building and construction industries, has paid Pounds 2m in cash and loan notes for Superior Pipeline Fittings, a specialist maker of pipeline fittings for the water industry. An additional Pounds 1m is dependent on profits. Superior had net assets of Pounds 500,000 at April 5 and net borrowings of Pounds 150,000. For the 55 weeks ended April 5 pre-tax profits totalled Pounds 460,000.
