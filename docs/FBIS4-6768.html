BFN ["Message from His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk Varaman to Samdech Chea Sim, Chairman of the Cambodian People's Party and National Assembly"; dated 22 April -- read by announcer] [Text] Esteemed Samdech: Today, I have received, with the most enthusiastic emotion, the message of support for my 22 April 1994 statement from the entire Cambodian People's Party [CPP] headed by yourself. I would like to express my deepest and warmest thanks to you and the CPP for your constant demonstration of responsibility, which is imbued with fine and sublime patriotism. Samdech and madam, the CPP Standing Committee, and all the CPP members, please accept my deepest and warmest fraternal affection. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 22 April 1994.
