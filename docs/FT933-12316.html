930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: EFM small companies trust - At a glance Edinburgh Fund Managers is launching a new investment trust, EFM Small Companies Trust. It will invest in small quoted UK companies with market capitalisations below Pounds 100m, with the aim of achieving long-term capital growth. Returns will be measured against the Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index. The minimum application is for 500 ordinary shares at a cost of Pounds 500. Offer closes August 11.
