BFN [Unattributed INTERFAX report: "Most-bank Has Become Azerbaijan's Proxy"] [Text] The Russian commercial Most-bank has been authorized by the Azerbaijan Government to conduct settlement operations between the two countries' enterprises, said Bagif Akperov, chief of the Azerbaijan Finance Ministry's Foreign Economic Relations Department. Under a signed agreement, Most-bank has the right to service and guarantee payments on export/import operations, open correspondence accounts for Azerbaijan commercial banks and enterprises, effect international settlements on special orders from the Azerbaijan government, and, on instructions from Azerbaijan residents, conduct transactions on international currency, and credit and bond markets.
