October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

DAY LABORERS

Drug pushers roam our school campuses. Gang members murder children. People unwilling to work accost us on the street. And the Costa Mesa police spend time arresting men, day laborers ("Intent Clause Fuels Dispute on Day Labor Law," Part I, Oct. 3), whose crime is the desire to do an honest day's work. Where are their priorities?

WENDY A. MUNSTER

Long Beach
