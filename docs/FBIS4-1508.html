BFN [Text] At 1200 on 29 March in Jinan's Qilu Guesthouse, Song Fatang, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and vice governor of the province, cordially met with Dirk Jan Van Houten, ambassador, and (Hualeiwen), commercial counselor of the Netherlands to China, who came to Shandong to inspect and visit. He welcomed the guests. The purpose of the visit of Ambassador Dirk Jan Van Houten and his party was to promote friendly cooperative relations between the North Holland Province of the Netherlands and Shandong Province and to explore opportunities for exchange and cooperation in economic, cultural, and other fields. Vice Governor Song Fatang introduced Shandong's reform, opening up, and economic development to the guests during the meeting.
