BFN [Excerpt] The 15th session of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Uzbekistan of the 12th convocation started its work in Tashkent today. A total of (?450) Uzbek peoples deputies, elected in the Republic of Karakalpakistan and in Uzbekistan's provinces, are participating in the session, which was opened by Shavkat Khalilov, acting chairman of the Republic's Supreme Soviet. [passage omitted] The session's agenda was approved and its secretariat elected. Uzbek President Islam Karimov spoke at the session on the current economic and social situation and on developments in the economic sphere. The session is continuing its work.
