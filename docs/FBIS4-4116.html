BFN [Text] Beijing, April 27 KYODO -- China on Wednesday [27 April] denied a foreign report that paramount leader Deng Xiaoping is in intensive care at a military hospital. A Foreign Ministry official said the report is a complete fabrication. "Comrade Deng is in very good heath," said the official who declined to be identified. South Korea's SEGYE TIMES [SEGYE ILBO] reported Wednesday that Deng has been hospitalized and is in critical condition. The Seoul daily said in a front-page story that rumors are circulating in Beijing about Deng's worsening health, and possible death.
