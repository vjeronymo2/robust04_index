August 17, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ELECTION ISSUES: S&LS, ABORTION

All the politicians, as well as the media, appear to be playing by pro-lifers' rules.

For shame, you pseudo pro-choicers!

No one should ask Souter such a narrow question as to whether or not he is pro- or anti-abortion.

The question is: Does he or does he not respect the rights of half of this nation to choose?

LISA SCHOYER

Pasadena
