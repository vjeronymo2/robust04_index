920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: PE Kemp purchase and name change PE Kemp, the theatrical stage set designer in which Mr Jim Slater, the property entrepreneur, has an interest, has acquired CR Pursehouse for Pounds 3.2m in Kemp shares. Kemp, which requested the suspension of its shares on February 13 pending yesterday's announcement, is also proposing a capital reconstruction on the basis of one new ordinary share for every five held and a change of name to CRP Leisure. CR Pursehouse is a construction and fitting company specialising in pubs and leisure concerns.
