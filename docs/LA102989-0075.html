October 29, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

2 FOUND FATALLY SHOT

Two people were found Saturday afternoon shot to death in a Scripps Ranch condominium, San Diego police said.

Officers found both bodies, and one weapon, inside the Erma Road apartment at 3:15 p.m., Lt. John Gregory said. Both bodies suffered gunshot wounds, he said.

Other details were not immediately available, Gregory said.
