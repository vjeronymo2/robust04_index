910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / Observer: No holes barred Holidaymakers to Eire, beware. The rapid growth in heavy vehicle traffic and the cuts in public spending have elevated the health of Ireland's road system to the status of a national joke. Indeed, a popular radio chat show has started a competition to find the republic's worst pothole. Brendan McGahon, who represents a border constituency in the Irish parliament, says: 'Anglers are fishing in them and wild animals we thought were extinct are actually hiding in them.' A border guard recently warned motorists approaching potholes to avoid other cars trying to get out of them.
