Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by Alex Fishman] [Text] Plans drafted by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] General Staff and relayed in the form of recommendations to the Palestinians propose linking the two self-administration regions, Gaza and Jericho, with three traffic arteries. Free traffic between the two autonomous regions is a part of the Oslo document and the Cairo draft. -- The northern artery: the Erez checkpoint, Ashqelon junction, Bene Deror junction, Bet Lid Junction, Tulkarm, Nabulus, al-Jiftliq, Jericho. -- The central artery: the Erez checkpoint, Kefar Silver junction, Re'em junction, Hulda junction, Latrun junction, Giv'at Ze'ev, al-Jib, 'Anatot, Almog junction, Jericho. -- The southern artery: the Erez checkpoint, Ashqelon junction, Pelugot junction, Bet Guvrin junction, Tarqumiyah, Hebron, Bethlehem, Abu-Dis, Almog junction, Jericho. The Palestinians prefer to build a new axis that will connect the West Bank with Gaza and be controlled by the autonomous Palestinian authority.
