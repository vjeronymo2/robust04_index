BFN [Text] Beijing, 13 May (XINHUA) -- PRC Presidential Decree No. 26: The "Decision of the National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee on Amendments to the `Regulations Governing Officers' Ranks of the Chinese People's Liberation Army [PLA],'" approved by the seventh meeting of the Eighth NPC on 12 May 1994, is hereby promulgated and becomes effective on the day of promulgation. [signed] Jiang Zemin, President of the PRC [dated] 12 May 1994
