BFN [Text] The Belarusian Ecological Party [Belaruskaya Ekolohichnaya Partyya -- BEP], which has recently been registered with the Ministry of Justice, pledges to protect everyone's right to clean air and water, healthy food, and a safe life on one's own land. In their platform statement, members of the new party call on all Belarusian citizens to personally participate in the environmental movement. Only this, in their opinion, can prevent the ecological and physical degradation of our nation. The BEP announces that it intends to join the political battle, to have real influence on the work of the Belarusian Government and parliament.
