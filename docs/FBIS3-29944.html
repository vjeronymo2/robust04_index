Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- Plants which were gifted to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il from different countries are growing at the international friendship greenhouse situated at the foot of Mt. Taesong in Pyongyang. There are more than 1,800 kinds of plants sent by party and state leaders, political and public figures and progressive people of different countries from 1976 up to now. Last year alone, hundreds of rare woody plants and herbs were sent here from China, Poland and other countries. Growing at the greenhouse are Kimilsongia and Kimchongilia well known to the Korean people as well as the world people and other temperate and tropical plants such as hippophae rhamnoides, hemerocallis, platycerium bifrucatum c. chr., camellia japonica and rhododendron indicum sweet. Herbal plants include psidium guajava and prunus mume var. alphandii and cassia occidentalis l. Cassia occidentalis l. is a rare plant for elixir which was discovered by Dr. Keita Aduna, a public figure of Mali, covering a long distance of more than 6,000 kilometres on the birthday of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il.
