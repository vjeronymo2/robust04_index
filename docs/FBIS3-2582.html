Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 10 (XINHUA) -- The Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] opened in the Great Hall of the People here this morning. Qiao Shi, chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, declared the opening of the meeting. Premier Li Peng has begun delivering a government work report. Attending today's meeting are Chinese Communist Party and Government leaders Jiang Zemin, Li Ruihuan, Zhu Rongji, Liu Huaqing and Hu Jintao.
