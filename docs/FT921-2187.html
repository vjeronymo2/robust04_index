920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Courtyard bid talks discontinued Shares of USM-quoted Courtyard Leisure fell 5p to 21p yesterday on news that bid talks with TW Consultants had been discontinued by mutual agreement. TWC acquired a 13.9 per cent holding in Courtyard in January and talks began at the end of last month when TWC made it known that, together with other shareholders deemed to be associates of TWC, it had 27.64 per cent of Courtyard.
