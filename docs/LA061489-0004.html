June 14, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"This means my husband and I can have our baby and not be affected by Arthur Jackson and his release."

-- Actress Theresa Saldana, who is pregnant, reacting to news that the release of the drifter who attempted to kill her in 1972 was delayed for at least nine months because he engaged in an April "rampage" at the Vacaville prison.
