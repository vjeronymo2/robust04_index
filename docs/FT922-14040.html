920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Pegasus Pegasus Group, the USM-traded computer software supplier, saw its interim profits dive from Pounds 774,000 to Pounds 320,000. Turnover for the six months to January 31 declined to Pounds 3.37m (Pounds 4.14m). Earnings per share worked through at 3.8p (8.9p), but the interim dividend is maintained at 3.5p.
