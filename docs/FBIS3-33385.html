Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Article by Isabel Dias de Aguiar] [Excerpt] Brazilians' ability to live with a high inflation rate has caught the attention of people outside our borders. A Russian banking delegation, representing 10 financial institutions, arrived in Sao Paulo in early February with the objective of learning a little bit of the know-how [previous word in English] Brazilians have developed in this regard. The delegation's objective is to learn how to operate a financial system with hyperinflation. [passage omitted]
