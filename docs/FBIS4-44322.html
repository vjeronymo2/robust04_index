BFN [Excerpts] In a second move towards the outside world, the prime minister, Mr Mokdad Sifi, embarks today on a European-Arab tour that will take him to Italy, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia. He arrived this morning in the Italian capital, Rome, leading a ministerial delegation on an official one-day visit to Italy. The head of government, accompanied by the finance, energy, and equipment, ministers, at noon today will confer with Italian President Mr Oscar Luigi Scalfaro. [passage omitted] After Italy, stops in Cairo and Riyadh will enable Mr. Sifi to review the means to bolster cooperation between Algeria and Egypt on one hand and between Algeria and Saudi Arabia on the other. He is carrying two message from Liamine Zeroual, the head of state, to both Egyptian President Husni Mubarak and to King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz.
