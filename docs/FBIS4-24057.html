BFN [Text] The UN Operations in Mozambique [Unomoz] today issued a report saying that, over the past seven days, the government demobilized more than 1,150 soldiers, while the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] demobilized 85 guerrillas. The Unomoz also says that the government currently has more than 16,500 soldiers in assembly areas and Renamo more than 11,100 troops.
