BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayev and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 24 Apr -- Ways to implement the Azerbaijani-Turkish accords reached during Heydar Aliyev's official visit to Ankara were discussed this afternoon at talks held in the Azerbaijani Foreign Ministry between Hasan Hasanov and Hikmet Cetin, the foreign ministers of Azerbaijan and Turkey. The Turkish foreign minister arrived in Baku at around midday on a short working visit. Also examined at the talks were questions linked to the current situation in the area of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict and ways to settle the conflict within the framework of the CSCE Minsk group. According to the information available, the sides held political consultations on questions concerning Azerbaijan's forthcoming entry into the NATO `Partnership for Peace' program and the republic's relations with non-aligned states, as well as a number of problems of mutual interest.
