940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Tor net asset value improves to Pounds 14.89 The net asset value per share of Tor Investment Trust, the split capital investment trust, rose from Pounds 13.60 to Pounds 14.89 at July 31, an increase of 9.5 per cent. Net revenue for the year was up 11 per cent from Pounds 1.32m to Pounds 1.47m. Earnings per income share were 36.28p (32.69p) and the dividend is maintained at 40p with a proposed 10p final. Some Pounds 149,000 has been transferred from revenue reserves.
