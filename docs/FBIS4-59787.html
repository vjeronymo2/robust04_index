BFN [Text] New York, 15 May (INA) -- At his place of residence in New York last night, Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz received Ambassador Inal Batu, Turkey's permanent UN representative. The meeting was attended by Riayd al-Qaysi, [Iraqi] Foreign Ministry under secretary; Nizar Hamdun, Iraq's permanent UN representative; and two aides to the Turkish ambassador.
