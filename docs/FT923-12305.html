920720 FT 20 JUL 92 / International Company News: Aberforth Smaller net assets rise 37% Since its inception in December 1990, the net asset value per share of Aberforth Smaller Companies has grown by 37 per cent. At the end of June it stood at 132.6p, compared to 126.2p six months before and to 113.97p on June 30 1991. Total assets were Pounds 83.6m, partially reflecting the Pounds 53m C share issue, now fully invested, and the medium term loan. Total revenue in the half year came to Pounds 2.45m (Pounds 691,000) and earnings per share worked through at 2.53p (2.73p). The interim dividend is 2.1p (2p).
