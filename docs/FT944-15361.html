941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Toshiba to sell Indian VCRs India's electronics industry has won its biggest export order, to supply 100,000 video cassette recorders worth about Dollars 30m to Toshiba, the Japanese electronics combine. The VCRs will be made by Videocon, a leading Indian consumer electronics group, and will be sold worldwide under the Toshiba brand. Toshiba said it might increase its purchases to 300,000 machines a year and might also buy colour televisions from Videocon. The VCRs use Toshiba technology.
