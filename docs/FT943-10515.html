940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / Explaura reduces losses to Pounds 2.18m Explaura Holdings, the USM-quoted operator of a limestone quarry in Canada, reduced its pre- and post-tax losses from Pounds 2.37m to Pounds 2.18m in the year to December 31. The previous outcome took in an exceptional charge of Pounds 774,000. Turnover improved to Pounds 1.89m (Pounds 1.56m). Losses per share were cut from 1.61p to 1.16p. The figures reported form part of a 15-month accounting period to March 31 1994.
