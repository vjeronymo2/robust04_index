930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / Hungarian scams Hungary has launched an offensive against the corruption and incompetence that have dogged the government's privatisation drive and endangered its support among the public, writes Nicholas Denton in Budapest. The State Property Agency, the privatisation office, yesterday revealed five privatisation scandals where it has initiated investigations, or disciplinary or legal action. The targets include state company managers, consultants and even the agency's own officials.
