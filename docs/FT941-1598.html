940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Aspen hit by exceptionals Pre-tax profits of Aspen Communications, the provider of media communication services traded on the USM, fell from Pounds 1.83m to Pounds 157,000 in 1993. The result was after exceptional charges of Pounds 1.13m including Pounds 630,000 for bad and doubtful debts and redundancies and Pounds 1.23m in respect of goodwill previously written off. There was also an exceptional gain of Pounds 1.1m on the disposal of an associated undertaking. Turnover rose from Pounds 60.3m to Pounds 64.2m. Losses per share emerged at 2.6p (8.6p earnings). A proposed final dividend of 2.9p maintains the total at 4.9p.
