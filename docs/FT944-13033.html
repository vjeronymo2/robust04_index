941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Reece Reece, which makes equipment for the ceramic and glassware industries, distributes cycles and industrial fasteners and also makes door panels, is raising Pounds 1.4m in a placing and open offer of 48.3m shares at 3 1/2 p. BZW is placing the shares with institutional investors; the open offer to shareholders is on a 3-for-8 basis. The proceeds would be used to reduce bank borrowings, directors said. Reece also announced reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 5,000 (Pounds 11,000) for the six months to June 30. Turnover improved to Pounds 6.53m (Pounds 6.14m) generating operating profits of Pounds 92,000 (Pounds 90,000). It is also proposed that each existing 5p share will be subdivided into one 1p share and one 4p deferred share which will have nominal rights and be effectively valueless.
