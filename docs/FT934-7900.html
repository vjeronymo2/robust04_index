931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Awards for FT writers TWO Financial Times journalists last night won awards in the Industrial Society's annual Industrial Journalism Award. The awards, presented in Dockland's last night by Mr Michael Grade, chief executive of Channel 4, are sponsored by Texaco. David Lascelles, the FT's resources editor, won the energy award for the best news story, feature article or series about the energy industry and issues around it. Bronwen Maddox, the paper's environment correspondent, won the environment award for the best reporting of the workplace environment. Both journalists were praised for the range of their writing. Each award carries a prize of Pounds 750.
