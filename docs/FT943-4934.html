940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / Brundtland pushes abortion legalisation Abortion should be legalised, Mrs Gro Harlem Brundtland, the Norwegian prime minister, told the United Nations population conference in Cairo yesterday. She implicitly accused the Vatican and Islamic clergy of hypocrisy for opposing the proposed text for the UN final document on the ground that it condoned abortion and extramarital sex.
