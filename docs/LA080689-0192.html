August 6, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

A coalition of local groups is planning a "Candlelight Peace Vigil" on Wednesday evening at the Garden Grove office of Rep. Robert K. Dornan (R -- Garden Grove). The demonstration, on the 44th anninversary of the atomic bombing of Nagasaki, is a protest of Dornan's support of the B-2 Stealth bomber.
