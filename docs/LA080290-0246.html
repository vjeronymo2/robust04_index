August 2, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

CARSON GOES APE TO AID CHIMP

Johnny Carson didn't monkey around when a zoo in his native Nebraska needed help.

He donated $55,000 to give Reuben the chimp new digs.

Northeast Nebraska Zoo has only one chimpanzee, but the donation from "The Tonight Show" host may be enough to acquire a female companion for Reuben as well as build a permanent primate exhibit, zoo officials said.

The 7-year-old chimp is housed in a fortified mobile home at the zoo in rural Royal, near Carson's hometown of Norfolk, but the monkey has grown too strong for his quarters.

Construction should begin next week on a new enclosure.
