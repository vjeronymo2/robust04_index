930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Lords referendum motion is tabled OPPONENTS of the Maastricht treaty in the House of Lords yesterday set out their bid for a referendum on the treaty, as peers geared up for the beginning of the detailed line-by-line debate on the bill next week. The cross-party amendment was tabled by Lord Blake, a Conservative, Lord Stoddart, a Labour peer, and two independent peers, Viscount Tonypandy, the former Speaker, and the Duke of Devonshire.
