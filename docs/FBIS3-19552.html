Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Polish National Defense Minister Piotr Kolodziejczyk has arrived in Ankara on an official visit. He was received at Esenboga Airport by National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan. In a statement here, Golhan said that relations between Turkey and Poland have always been conducted with mutual confidence and friendship, even during the Cold War. He said Turkey appreciates Poland's efforts to join the Partnership for Peace process. Poland is an important country for European security, he said, and will make valuable contributions to this process. In turn, the Polish defense minister said the warm welcome he received is a sign of the good relations between the two countries. He pointed out that President Suleyman Demirel's visit to Poland last November had a positive effect on the Polish people's attitude toward Turkey. He added that remarks made by President Demirel during the visit also expressed Poland's expectations with regard to NATO and European security.
