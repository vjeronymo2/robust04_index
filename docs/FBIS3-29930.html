Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent a message of greetings on Feb. 22 to Carlos Manuel Castillo, chairman of the National Liberation Party of Costa Rica, on his party's victory in the presidential election. The message expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two parties would strengthen and develop further and wished the Costa Kican Party and its chairman success in the work for the independent development of the country and the strengthening of the party.
