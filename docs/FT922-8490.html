920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Narborough Plants higher at Pounds 176,000 Narborough Plantations, Malaysian-based palm oil and rubber production company, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 146,000 to Pounds 176,000 in the six months to December 31. With no tax, earnings per share were 1.32p (1.1p). There was a difference on exchange of Pounds 82,000 (Pounds 45,000) from conversion of assets in foreign denominations. Estate operating profit was Pounds 60,000 (Pounds 54,000) helped by higher palm oil and rubber prices and increased palm oil output but offset by lower rubber production. An unchanged interim dividend of 0.5p is declared.
