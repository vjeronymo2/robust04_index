BFN [Text] Vukovar, 22 Jun (TANJUG) -- Yasushi Akashi, UN secretary general's special envoy for former Yugoslavia, and General Milan Celeketic, commander of the Serb Krajina army, visited units in Slavonia, Baranja, and Western Srem today, according to the press center of the Serb Krajina army's main headquarters. Yasushi Akashi visited UNPROFOR's units in Beli Manastir and Vukovar and General Celeketic visited the zone of responsibility of the [Serb Krajina] Slavonia-Baranja Corps as part of his regular activities. In a statement for the republic of Serb Krajina television, Gen Celeketic gave a positive assessment of combat readiness for defense of the republic of Serb Krajina.
