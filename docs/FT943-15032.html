940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Dealer arrested A former securities dealer at Swiss Bank Corporation in London has been arrested in connection with alleged fraud, the bank confirmed yesterday. He is suspected of having misappropriated just under Pounds 2m by influencing the price of a bond warrant, the bank said. It said he had traded on his own account for personal gain. No client money was involved and the bank had 'no indication that anyone else is involved'.
