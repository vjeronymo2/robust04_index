920109 FT 09 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Increased midway deficit at Savills Losses at Savills, the surveyor and estate agent, increased in the six months to October 31 and the interim dividend is again passed. In 'generally hostile trading conditions', losses rose from Pounds 433,000 to Pounds 762,000 pre-tax on turnover cut to Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 14.6m). Breaking down the figures, the commercial side made operating losses of Pounds 259,000 (profits Pounds 428,000) on turnover of Pounds 5.05m (Pounds 6.64m) while losses from agricultural and residential were reduced from Pounds 849,000 to Pounds 575,000 on turnover of Pounds 6.76m (Pounds 8m). However, costs had been cut by 17 per cent, or some Pounds 2.5m. Losses per share emerged at 1.7p (0.8p).
