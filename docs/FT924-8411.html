921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / Letter: Time for UK to grasp opportunity in Russia Sir, Re your article, 'Welcome Mr Yeltsin' (November 9), no greater commitment could be signalled to Russia than to provide it with a Dollars 1bn loan. To date, very little has emanated either from the private sector, Export Credits Guarantee Department or NCM credit agency. Surely the time is right to rectify our position and to compete with our apparently braver German and American cousins who are currently providing trade finance. British companies would be delighted to sell their goods and services into such an enormous market. Both NCM and ECGD could provide the Dollars 1bn facility, with repayments made in oil. The British government now has an opportunity to prove its commitment to UK industry, Peter Knowles, chairman, CIC Investments Inc, 211 Piccadilly, London W1
