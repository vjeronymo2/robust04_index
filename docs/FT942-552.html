940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Schneider suspect released Italian Valentino Foto was released without bail by Belgian police who had been holding him on fraud charges connected with the French company Schneider. Mr Fogi and Schneider chairman Didier Pineau-Valencienne were arrested on charges of fraud and swindling after complaints filed by minority shareholders in two Schneider subsidiaries in Belgium.
