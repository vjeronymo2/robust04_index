940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Appleyard Pounds 4.23m property disposal Appleyard Group, the North Yorkshire-based motor dealer, is selling a development property at Redhill, Surrey, to Boots Properties for Pounds 4.23m cash. On completion of the sale in April, Appleyard will enter into a 25-year lease of the motor dealership element of the site at an initial rental of Pounds 150,000 a year. The profit arising from the deal, about Pounds 270,000 after disposal costs, will be used to reduce debt.
