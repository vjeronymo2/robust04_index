BFN [Text] Son Song-pil, DPRK ambassador to Russia, made a skeptical assessment of Moscow's proposal calling on Pyongyang to participate in the international meeting to discuss the situation on the Korean peninsula. He, in an interview with reporters in Moscow, said he doubts whether the meeting is necessary when the majority of parties that will participate in the meeting are pro-U.S. He also pointed out that participating in the meeting would place the DPRK in a very difficult position. Ambassador Son said he sees the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula as a matter between Pyongyang and Washington. If the issue is internationalized, (?finding a solution to the nuclear problem would be even more difficult), he added.
