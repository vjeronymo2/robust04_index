Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tegucigalpa -- On 3 February, President Carlos Roberto Reina swore in 20 deputy ministers who will work at the 12 ministries, as well as Dante Gabriel Ramirez, who will be minister [title as published] in charge of Central American issues. Reina also swore in political adviser Enrique Ortez Colindres and special adviser to the president Mario Reina Idiaquez, the president's brother. It was reported that Jorge Bueso Arias and Guillermo Bueso will be sworn in today as secretaries of state [title as published] in charge of economic matters, Daniel Alfredo Figueroa will be sworn in as vice president of the Central Bank of Honduras, and Mario Espinal Zelaya will be sworn in as executive director of the Honduran Social Investment Fund. President Reina also swore in Juan Ramon Duran as the new press minister [title as published].
