920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Simpsons buys Oxford restaurant Simpsons of Cornhill, the owner of two of the City's most famous restaurants and which is embroiled in a bitter battle for control with dissident shareholders, yesterday acquired La Cantina, one of Oxford's most popular restaurants, from Gillport Hotels & Catering. The consideration was 796,875 new shares worth Pounds 255,000 at yesterday's close of 32p. La Cantina reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 40,000 on sales of Pounds 360,000 in the year to September 30. Sales in the eight months to end-May were Pounds 216,000. As a result of the deal Mr Rory Haisley, Gillport's main shareholder, has been appoint- ed Simpsons operations manager. Gillport will emerge with a 17.7per cent stake in Simpsons.
