Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 8 -- In 1993 nearly 1.5 million of land tenure certificates were granted to farmer households in 47 (out of 52) provinces and cities in Vietnam. The area of land whose ownership was transferred from agricultural collectives to households or individuals accounted for only close to 14 per cent of the plan. The transfer of land ownership (mainly land for agricultural production) was carried [out] in more than 1,330,000 communes in the 47 cities and provinces in accordance with the government's new land law aimed at encouraging agricultural production.
