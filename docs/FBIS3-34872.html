Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 15 Feb (SUNA) -- Iraqi Minister of Transport and Communications Dr. Ahmad Murtada Ahmad Khalil arrived in Khartoum yesterday. In a press statement issued after his arrival, he said his visit was one of a series between the two fraternal countries to discuss various issues. His Excellency said that in the field of transport and communications, Iraq was fully prepared to cooperate with Sudan in railways, sea transport, land transport, civil aviation, as well as communications in the interests of the two friendly countries. He expressed the hope that the result of his visit to Sudan would be fruitful and positive in different fields.
