Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin, January 16 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, arrived here this afternoon for an eight-day official goodwill visit to Germany at the invitation of the Presidium of its Bundestag. This is the first leg of Qiao's visit to three West European nations which will also take him to Switzerland and Austria. The purpose of his visit is to promote the mutual understanding and friendship and enhance the friendly ties and cooperation between China and the three West European countries and their parliamentary exchanges in particular, Chinese parliamentary sources said. Qiao, the first top Chinese legislator to visit Germany, and his wife Yu Wen were greeted at the Berlin Airport by Reinhard Fuhrer, vice president of the Berlin City Parliament. In a written statement released at the airport, Qiao said over the past few years the relationship between China and Germany has entered a new stage of the all-round development owing to the joint efforts of the two sides. During the visit, Qiao is expected to hold talks with his German counterpart Rita Sussmuth [spelling of name as received] and meet other parliamentary leaders. He will also meet President Richard von Weizsaecker and Chancellor Helmut Kohl and exchange views with them on the growth of bilateral ties, especially the ties between the NPC and the Federal Assembly of Germany as well as on international issues of common concern. "It is my hope that the visit will deepen mutual understanding, expand exchanges and strengthen cooperation so as to bring about new development of the Sino-German relations," Qiao said.
