Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent Nikolay Nazarovich for TASS] [Text] Mogilev February 27 TASS -- Ex-speaker of the Belarusian Parliament Stanislav Shushkevich seems to have launched an election campaign for the republic's presidency. Over the past few days, he has been touring Belarusian cities and towns, calling for early presidential and parliamentary elections. Shushkevich held at the weekend two meetings in Mogilev with the city's workers, stressing that "to make economic reforms in the country effective, democratic forces should unite on the basis of statehood, political and economic democracy, that is on the basis of transition to a free market". Speaking about the merging of Russia's and Belarus' monetary systems, he came out for "a decent agreement, on equal terms, without any infringements upon Belarus' interests".
