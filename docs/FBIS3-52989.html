Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Jan.07 (MAP)-Secretary General of the Confederation of Italian Trade Unions (CISL) Sergio d'Antoni paid a visit to Morocco Jan. 3-5 at the invitation of Moroccan trade union `L'Union Marocaine du Travail' (UMT). During his stay, Sergio d'Antoni had talks with UMT Secretary General Mahjoub Benseddik on cooperation prospects between CISL and UMT, the rights of immigrant workers, the unions' independence and role in the protection of the workers rights. They also reviewed ways of consolidating coordination and the possibility of concluding a social security system convention between the two countries.
