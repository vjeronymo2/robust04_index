January 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LAGUNA NIGUEL;

CITY ESTABLISHES A SPORTS COUNCIL

The City Council has established a Sports Advisory Council and is inviting representatives of any organized sports group or team to become members.

In addition, three at-large members will be appointed.

All sports groups and organizations in the city are asked to submit letters to the city indicating their interest in membership and appointing one individual from their organization to serve.

Individuals who are not members of organized sports groups may apply for one of the three at-large positions.

Applications are available at City Hall, 27821 La Paz Road, or by calling (714) 643-1610.

The deadline for submitting applications is Feb. 20.
