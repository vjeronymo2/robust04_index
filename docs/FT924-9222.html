921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons' ice cream expansion Allied-Lyons plans to scoop more profits from Thailand by signing a 50:50 joint venture agreement with the Bangkok-based Central Department Store to build an ice-cream manufacturing plant outside Bangkok. Baskin-Robbins, Allied's US ice-cream making offshoot, entered the Thai market two years ago opening a store in the capital. Since then seven more have been opened in Bangkok and two in Chiang Mai, in the north of the country. Ice-cream for these outlets was shipped from the US, but of Mr Tony Trigg, chief executive of Allied-Lyons Retailing, said:'Trading has exceeded all our expectations.'
