October 13, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

CHANNEL LEAGUE

Dos Pueblos 42, Hueneme 0 -- Running back Cy Brunner gained 243 yards in 26 carries and scored four touchdowns in three quarters of work at San Marcos High to lead the Chargers (2-4, 1-2).

Hueneme (0-6, 0-3) rushed for only 27 yards in the first half and drove to the Charger 17-yard line in the second quarter, but lineman Andy Schulman sacked Viking quarterback David Andrade on fourth down.

Wendell Nunnery led Hueneme with 57 yards in 13 carries. PAUL OLSON
