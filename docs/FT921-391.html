920330 FT 30 MAR 92 / World News in Brief: Levy says he is quitting as Israeli foreign minister Israeli foreign minister David Levy said he was resigning from the government, revealing a deep rift in the ruling Likud party just three months before a June 23 national election. Commentators had predicted Levy would announce he was quitting Likud but he said, in a speech broadcast on radio, that the party was his home and he could not leave it.
