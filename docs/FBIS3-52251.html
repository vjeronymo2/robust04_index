Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ahvaz, Khuzestan Province, Jan. 3, IRNA -- Islamic resistance forces have taken over a part of al-Madinah city in Iraq, an Iraqi opposition source announced here Sunday [2 January]. The combatants of the Islamic resistance have also closed all roads ending to the city in the suburb of al-Basrah, inflicting heavy losses on Saddam's forces, the mojahedin source said. The fighting between Islamic resistance forces and Saddam's troops was continuing and flames and smoke could be seen above different parts of the city, the source added. He concluded that the Islamic resistance forces have also distributed pamphlets inviting people to join the uprising against Saddam's regime.
