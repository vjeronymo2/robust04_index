May 21, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GLENDORA ROTARY 'BEST'

The 67-member Glendora Rotary Club has been named the "Best Club" in its district, which includes 56 clubs in an ara bordered by Pasadena, Rancho Cucamonga, Las Vegas and Alhambra. The club earned the honor for international service, membership development, club service, community service and vocational service.
