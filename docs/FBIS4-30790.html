BFN [Text] The Assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina began work this afternoon. Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency, addressed the deputies at the very beginning of the session. He presented the stands of our delegation at the forthcoming negotiations in Geneva and France. He also proposed that our delegation be made up of Ejup Ganic, Kresimir Zubak, Haris Silajdzic, Efrem (?Bicapi), Mile Akmadzic, and Miro Lazovic.
