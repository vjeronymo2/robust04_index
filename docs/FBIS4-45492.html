BFN [Text] The Kazakh Interior Ministry held an itinerant session in the village of Tolebi, Zhambyl oblast, devoted to curbing drug trafficking. As is widely known, cannabis grown in Zhambyl oblast is used to produce the most widely spread drugs hashish and marijuana. The session noted that drugs smuggling routes connect the Chu valley of South Kazakhstan with China, India, Afghanistan, Italy, and France. New methods in the battle against drugs smugglers were discussed.
