BFN [Excerpt] Zvornik, 11 Apr (TANJUG) -- No humanitarian or logistical convoys have arrived on the left [western] bank of the River Drina today, and no convoys went in the other direction, toward the territory of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, TANJUG's correspondent has learned from the command of the Zvornik garrison of the Bosnian Serb republic army. The same source says that several humanitarian and logistical convoys announced their arrival for today, and the Serb side granted them permission to cross the territory of the Serb republic. However, they have failed to arrive, and officials in Zvornik have not received any official explanation for this. [passage omitted]
