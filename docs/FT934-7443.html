931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Martin Currie net assets improve Net asset value per share of Martin Currie European Investment Trust stood at 117.7p at October 31. That compared with 82.6p 12 months earlier and 93.8p at the April 30 year end. Attributable profits for the half year declined to Pounds 99,000 (Pounds 174,000), equal to earnings of 0.43p (0.76p) per share.
