BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek 12 April, TASS--The American Government is providing humanitarian aid to Kyrgyzstan. One hundred thousand tons of food grain will be shipped to Bishkek during May and June of 1994, the Kyrgyz Government's press service reported on Tuesday. This amounts to about one-third of all grain consumed in the Republic at present.
