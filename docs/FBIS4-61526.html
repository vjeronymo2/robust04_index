BFN [Text] The 19th round of Estonian-Russian talks will end today at Lohusalu. As reported to Estonian Radio by the Foreign Ministry, no breakthrough was achieved at yesterday's meeting. The Russian side did not alter its positions on the issue of military pensioners. Russia demands that social protection for Russian military pensioners should be viewed within a single package containing the troop withdrawal agreement. Estonia continues to stand firmly on its posiiton: the Russian troop withdrawal cannot be linked to any additional conditions. According to Vaino Reinart, the Estonian delegation's leader, the delegations expect to initial an agreement on cooperation in education today. This will lay down the principles for student exchanges, cooperation between higher education establishments, and other matters.
