920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / International Company News: Quaker Oats to sell two Italian units QUAKER Oats, the US foods group, has reached agreement to sell two Italian businesses, Sorba and Pandea, to Garma, an Italian investment group, AP-DJ reports. Quaker said the two businesses, although profitable, do not fit its strategic European core businesses of pet foods and cereals. Sorba, which was acquired by Quaker in 1983, distributes gourmet products in Italy from its base in Milan. Pandea, a producer of breadsticks and crackers, was acquired by Quaker in 1981 as part of its acquisition of Chiari and Forti. The two represented Dollars 45m of Quaker's Dollars 400m annual sales in Italy, Quaker said.
