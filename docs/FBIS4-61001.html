BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 2 May -- The press service of the Karabakh Army headquarters has reported that the Azerbaijani side today resumed attacks on the north and northeast of Mardakertskiy Rayon [Mardakert] of Nagorno-Karabakh, using armored equipment and artillery. Thus, Stepanakert believes, Azerbaijan is violating the cease-fire accords. According to the press service, the Karabakh sub-units are not only successfully rebuffing the enemy attacks, but also dealing it considerable blows in places. Stepanakert pointed out that it was relatively calm on other stretches of the front.
