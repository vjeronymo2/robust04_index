December 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

MAYOR TO SPEAK AT FORUM

Mayor Jess Hughston will speak on citywide issues and his vision for Pasadena's future at a public forum at 7 p.m. Thursday at the Santa Catalina Library, 999 E. Washington Blvd.

The event is a District 2 meeting sponsored by Director Rick Cole. Dessert will be served and free child care will be provided.
