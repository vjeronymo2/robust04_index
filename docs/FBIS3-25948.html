Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 31 January, the united front work department under the provincial party committee and the provincial federation of industry and commerce jointly sponsored a tea party on the occasion of the Spring Festival in Shenyang. Attending the tea party were provincial leaders, including Gu Jinchi, Xu Wencai, Yu Junbo, Xiao Zuofu, Sun Qi, Gao Jizhong, Zhang Chenglun, and Ma Pinfang; as well as representative personages from the non-public units throughout the province. They happily got together to jointly view the Spring Festival. Gu Jinchi, secretary of the provincial party committee, delivered a speech on behalf of the provincial party committee and the provincial people's government, in which he extended cordial regards and festive greetings to the participating comrades. He said: By the end of 1993, the number of individually run industrial and commercial enterprises and privately owned ones throughout the province reached 753 and involved 1.3 million persons. Both the number of enterprises and number of people involved topped previous peaks. Non-publicly-owned enterprises and units have become an important force in the promotion of development in the province's economy. The situation prevailing among these enterprises or units is very gratifying. In his speech, Gu Jinchi urged party committees and governments at all levels to continuously give regular guidance and support to the work of organizations under the provincial federation of industry and commerce. He also urged the organizations of industrial and commercial federations across the province and the representative personages of non-public units to further emancipate their minds; to seek truth from facts; to unite as one; to work industriously; and to strive together with the people throughout the province to realize the second pioneering program, to restore the province's glorious prestige, and to build socialism with Chinese characteristics.
