910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / World Trade News: Bulgarian phone order for UK GPT, the British telecommunications group will today sign the first significant telecommunications joint-venture in Bulgaria to instal a network of 200 card-operated payphones, writes Charles Leadbeater. GPT, which is owned by the UK's General Electric Company and Siemens of west Germany, hopes the payphone venture, called Betkom, will pave the way for its involvement in modernisation of the Bulgarian telephone network. The Betkom deal is the latest in a series of payphone joint-ventures GPT has signed in east Europe, which include schemes in Moscow and Yugoslavia as well as a trial of equipment in Hungary.
