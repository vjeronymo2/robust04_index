Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to final results released by the republican commission in charge of preparing and unfolding the sociological poll "What People Advise" in the Republic of Moldova, 75.1 percent of the citizens who have the right to vote had cast their vote in the poll. Our Chisinau correspondent Cristian Nita reports that, out of the total respondents, 95.4 percent gave an affirmative answer to the question on the voting card, 2 percent gave a negative answer, and 2.6 percent of cards were not valid. The President of the Republic of Moldova Mircea Snegur stated after the results had been announced that in this way an end is put to all speculations that have been circulated so far and stressed that the Republic of Moldova has one single future, that of an independent, united, and indivisible state.
