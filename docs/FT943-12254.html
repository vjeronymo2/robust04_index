940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / UK Company News: WEW WEW Group, the discount retailer, has warned it will have to make full-year provisions for excess stock, particularly clothing from last winter and autumn and Sega computer games equipment. The company added that following the poor spring trading the last six weeks had been over budget. The shares lost 1 1/2 p to close at 34 1/2 p.
