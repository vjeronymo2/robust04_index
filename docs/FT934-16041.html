931008 FT 08 OCT 93 / People: Ransomes John Kerridge, non-executive chairman of RANSOMES, is resigning from the board. He is replaced by John Clement, currently a non-executive director and chairman of Anglo American Insurance. Clement is a former chairman and chief executive of Unigate and chairman of the Littlewoods Organisation. The company's chief executive, Bob Dodsworth, has informed the board of his wish to retire in the near future. The company is seeking to recruit two further non-executive directors besides a new chief executive.
