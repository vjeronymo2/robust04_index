940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: CH Bailey CH Bailey, the ship repairer and leisure company, reported losses of Pounds 206,359 pre-tax for the 28 weeks to October 8, against profits of Pounds 773,256. Turnover was Pounds 1.89m (Pounds 2.34m). The loss was after crediting Pounds 383,622 (Pounds 1.24m) from the sale of investments. Losses per share were 0.34p (1.26p earnings).
