Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Following Iran's proposal of a temporary cease-fire for a few hours in Kabul to evacuate those injured during the incidents of the past few days, the warring sides in Afghanistan declared their acceptance of the proposal. Announcing the news, the Foreign Ministry spokesman said: The representatives of the president and the prime minister of Afghanistan contacted this ministry to declare their acceptance. He added: The Islamic Republic of Iran declares its readiness to receive those injured in the recent incidents and calls on international organizations and the International Red Cross to assist in this humanitarian deed. According to reports from Islamic Republic of Iran correspondents, the hospitals in Kabul cannot cope with the large number of wounded and are in desperate need of space and medical facilities.
