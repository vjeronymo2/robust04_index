BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 9 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the closed sitting of the Mongolian parliament the legislators have discussed and approved the proposal of the Mongolbank on issuing new 5 and 10 thousand bank notes as well as new coins of 20, 50, 100 and 200 tugrug of value. The central bank is substantiating the necessity for such a step by the growing rates in the prices, wage, pensions and allowances. The coins supposed to issue are to be serving as a means of fare payment in the public transport or applying in slot-machines for rendering small services.
