January 12, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RENTERS REACHING FOR 'ABOVE THE LAW'

Steven Seagal was definitely the action/adventure find of 1988. His "Above the Law" continues to be a strong rental -- No. 5 on the Billboard magazine chart. Few expected it to be this high on the chart or to have such longevity (nine weeks on the chart). Seagal is a challenger for the No. 2 spot -- battling Stallone and Chuck Norris -- behind action/adventure king Arnold Schwarzenegger. "Bloodsport," a bloody martial arts flick starring Jean Claude Van Damme, is making a big splash on the rental chart (No. 17 after two weeks).

* "Red Heat," debuting at an impressive No. 11, should be the red-hot rental of the next few weeks. This one wasn't great box-office, but many of those Schwarzenegger fans who missed it in the theaters will probably rent it.
