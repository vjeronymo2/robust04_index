920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Shani expands womenswear side Shani Group, the USM-traded fashion wear company, has acquired three women's suit and coat manufacturers for Pounds 1m cash. A further earn-out, up to Pounds 1.68m cash, will be payable to the vendors dependent on profits for the two years to July 31 1994. The three businesses - Admyra Coats, MF Lampert and Lampert & Smith - are based in east London and supply independent retailers and multiple chains. They achieved profits before tax of Pounds 639,000 for the 1991 year on turnover of Pounds 6.5m. Net assets at that date amounted to Pounds 562,000.
