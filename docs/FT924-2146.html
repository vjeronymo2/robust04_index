921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / EC to pressure UK on HDTV Britain will face pressure from its EC partners today to approve funding for a Brussels-backed strategy to market high definition television to the European public, writes Lionel Barber. EC telecommunications ministers will reconsider a European Commission plan to pump Ecu850m (Pounds 688m) into the promotion of HDTV over five years. Under revised plans, the Commission wants to channel money to consortia for satellite broadcasting, as well as conversion and production of programmes using HDTV technology.
