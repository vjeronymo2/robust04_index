Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 18 (KCNA) -- Yuriy Fadeyev, Russian ambassador to Korea, hosted a reception at his embassy on the evening of March 17 to mark the 45th anniversary of the signing of the agreement on economic and cultural cooperation between Korea and Russia. Invited to the reception were Yi Song-tae, chairman of the State External Economic Affairs Commission, Yun Kyong-nam, vice-minister of Culture and Art, Kim Yong-su, vice-chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yi Pong-hui, vice- chairman of the DPRK Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee, who is chairman of the Korea-Russia Friendship Association, and other officials concerned. Speeches were made at the reception.
