Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow February 24 TASS--Russian Prosecutor General Alexey Kazannik is ready to free anti-Yeltsin rebels pardoned by the State Duma on Wednesday as soon as he receives the necessary documents from the legislature and talks to each of the organisers of the October armed opposition revolt and hard-line May Day demonstration last year. "In case the accused agree with the (Duma) resolution I shall rule to drop criminal proceedings against each of the accused", Kazannik told TASS on Thursday. If the accused refuse to be pardoned the criminal cases against them will proceed in a usual way, he added. Kazannik said he had not yet received the Duma decision, but stressed that his office does not give political assessments to various laws. "It ensures lawfulness and a strict fulfillment of all instructions of competent state bodies", he said.
