Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A katyusha rocket was fired today from Lebanese territory at the Western Galilee. No one was hurt, but light damage was caused to one of the factories in the area. Our correspondent in the north Menahem Horowitz reports that this is the first katyusha to hit the Galilee since Operation Accountability and the understandings made with the Hizballah in its wake. [In live telephone update, Yo'av Aviv reports on Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 2030 GMT that "Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, commander of the Northern Command, this evening confirmed that a katyusha hit the Galilee. Gen. Mordekhay believes that the missile was fired by Palestinian elements who wish to heat up the border area. He said that he holds a grave view of the firing."]
