920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / World Trade News: Mobil in Qatar LNG project Mobil, the US oil company, yesterday signed heads of agreement with the Qatar General Petroleum Corporation to participate in two significant liquefied natural gas (LNG) projects in Qatar, involving investment of Dollars 3bn, writes Neil Buckley. Mobil has taken a 10 per cent interest in the Qatargas project of 6m tonnes of LNG a year, with likely investment of Dollars 350m on development, and the possibility of further investment in tankers.
