BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received congratulatory messages from foreign state leaders on his birthday. They came from Zimbabwean President Robert G. Mugabe, Guinean President Lansana Conte, Ghanaian President Jerry John Rawlings, Moroccan King Hassan II, St. Lucian Prime Minister John George Melvin Compton, Belize Prime Minister Manuel Esquivel, Prime Minister of the Commonwealth of Dominica Mary Eugenia Charles, Jordanian King Hussein I, Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, Maltese President Ugo Mifsud Bonnici, President of the Presidential Council of Yemen 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Cambodian King Norodom Sihanouk and head of state and Chairman of the National Provisional Ruling Council of Sierra Leone Valentine Esegragbo Melvin Ytrasser. The messages extend warmest congratulations to President Kim Il-song and wish him good health and a long life. They express firm solidarity with the Korean people in the struggle for the independent and peaceful reunification of the country and the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the Korean people and their peoples will grow stronger.
