920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Robert Morley dies Actor Robert Morley, who combined a reputation as a clubman, gambler, lover of the turf and bon viveur with a firm belief in left-wing politics, died aged 84 after suffering a stroke at the weekend. He lived in Wargrave, Berkshire. Mr Morley's film successes included Oscar Wilde, Topkapi and The African Queen. He also co-wrote many of the 100 plays in which he appeared.
