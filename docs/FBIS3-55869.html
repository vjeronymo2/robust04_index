Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Baku January 12 TASS -- President Geydar Aliyev received Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian President's special envoy and ambassador at large, here on Wednesday. Kazimirov also met with Azerbaijan Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov. Matters aimed at settling the Karabakh conflict were discussed.
