920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed strong and more active demand. New season's assams met increases support and showed advances of 5 to 15p although Pekoe dusts met less enquiry and proved irregular with plainer sorts easier. Bright liquoring East Africans remained firm with quality but prices for good medium teas opened 3 to 6p dearer and were up to 8p higher towards the close. Offshore good general demand with prices advancing by 5 to 10 pence. The highest price realised this week was 180p for an assam pf. Quotations quality 150p good medium 135p medium 124p low medium 80p.
