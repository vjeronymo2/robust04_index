Part A._Information Required in a Prospectus * * * * * Item 4. Financial Highlights 1. General: *␣*␣* Instructions *␣*␣* 17. Compute the ``ratio of expenses to average net assets'' using the amount of expenses shown in the Registrant's statement of operations for the relevant fiscal year, including increases resulting from complying with paragraph 2(g) of Rule 6&hyph;07 [17 CFR 210.6&hyph;07] of Regulation S&hyph;X regarding fees paid with Registrant's brokerage commissions, and including reductions resulting from complying with paragraphs 2(a) and (f) of Rule 6&hyph;07 (17 CFR 210.6&hyph;07) regarding fee waivers and reimbursements. If a change in the methodology of determining the ratio of expenses to average net assets results from applying paragraph 2(g) of Rule 6&hyph;07 (17 CFR 210.6&hyph;07), explain in a note that the ratio reflects fees paid with brokerage commissions only for fiscal years ending after [the effective date of the final rule amendments]. * * * * * Note: The text of Form N&hyph;3 does not and the amendments will not appear in the Code of Federal Regulations. 10.
