BFN [Text] Alhaji Koromah has reportedly been holding evening meetings with the Guinean and Sierra Leonean ambassadors at Hotel Africa. The nature of Koromah's secret meetings with the two West African diplomats is not known, but sources close to the Sierra Leonean Embasssy in Monrovia say Koromah is soliciting support to continue his fighting against Roosevelt Johnson's Krahn faction in ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia].
