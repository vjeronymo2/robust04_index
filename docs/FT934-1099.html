931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Fletcher recovery Fletcher King, the commercial estate agent and manager, saw a slow recovery in the six months to October 31. Although the improvement was fragile the company said it had experienced an increase in activity. Turnover in the six months fell from Pounds 2.53m to Pounds 2.34m for pre-tax profits slightly higher at Pounds 74,000 (Pounds 71,000). Earnings per share were unchanged at 0.5p and the interim dividend is also maintained at 0.5p.
