BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau], 27 May -- An assassination attempt has been made on Boris Luchik, the procurator of the Dniester Republic. Yesterday evening, when he and his wife entered their house accompanied by guards, a powerful explosion was heard near the lift. The procurator's wife perished, doctors are struggling to save the lives of Boris Luchik and his guards. It was reported to the ITAR-TASS correspondent today by Oleg Gudym, acting minister of state security of the Dniester Republic, that the explosion was carried out with the help of a radio-controlled device.
