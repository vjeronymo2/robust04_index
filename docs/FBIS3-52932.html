Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Manama, 9 Jan (WAKH)--Malcolm Rifkind, British secretary of state for defense, left Manama today after a two-day visit to Bahrain. During the visit, he held talks on enhancing bilateral relations. Rifkind was seen off by Staff Lieutenant General Khalifah Bin-Ahmad Al Khalifah, deputy commander in chief of the Bahrain Defense Force, and Hugh James Junnec, the British ambassador to Bahrain.
