940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / World Trade News: WTO 'will miss start date' France said yesterday the Gatt world trade body's offspring, the World Trade Organisation would fail to meet its start-up goal of next January 1, and threw its weight behind Geneva's bid to host the WTO, Reuter reports from Paris. 'We will not meet the start-up date of January 1, 1995, for the very simple reason that we want to wait for ratification by the US Congress and then, naturally, by the French parliament,' industry and trade minister Gerard Longuet said. Answering a question in the National Assembly (lower house), Mr Longuet said history proved 'it is better for an international organisation to be sure the Americans will ratify (it) before we ratify it so as not to be caught in an uncertain position.'
