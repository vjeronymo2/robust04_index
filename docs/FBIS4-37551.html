BFN [Excerpts] An unprecedented funeral ceremony was held in the holy city of Mashhad today for the martyrs of the Hoseyn's Ashura day incident inside the holy shrine of Imam Reza, amid the participation of the masses. [crowd chants "Death to the monafeqin," "Death to America," and "These are the evidences of the U.S. crime"] The coffins were carried shoulder high by the people of Mashhad. The streets leading to the holy shrine were packed with masses of mourners, who, shedding tears, were offering condolences to Imam Reza for the desecration of his holy sanctuary. The city of Mashhad observed a holiday in solidarity to enable the people to attend the funeral ceremony for the 24 martyrs of the heinous crime of the monafeqin [Mojahedin-e Khalq]. From the early hours of the morning masses of mourners moved to the streets leading to the holy shrine. [passage omitted] At about 1000, all the routes leading to the holy shrine were blocked due to the presence of so many mourners. [passage omitted]
