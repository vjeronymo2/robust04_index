March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Guard Jeff Bregel of the San Francisco 49ers was arrested early Saturday and charged with being drunk in public, according to San Jose police.

"He was standing on a corner with his shirt off and was challenging someone to a fight," police spokesman Mitch Fagan said.

Bregel, 25, was the 49ers' second-round draft pick out of USC in 1987.
