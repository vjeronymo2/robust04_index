November 23, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

ISRAEL;

TROOPS WOUND 12 IN PALESTINIAN CLASHES

Israeli soldiers shot and wounded at least 12 Palestinians in stone-throwing clashes during a general strike in the Gaza Strip and West Bank, Arab hospital officials said. The confrontations came as commerce and transportation ground to a halt in the occupied territories in the strike ordered by Muslim fundamentalists.
