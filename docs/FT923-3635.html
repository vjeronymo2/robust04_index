920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Astra satellite system feeds nearly 40m European homes NEARLY 40m European homes will be receiving satellite television from the Astra satellite system by the end of this year, according to a new survey, The study by Carat, the media group, and the London-based Continental Research covered a total of 19 European countries including Poland, Hungary and Czechoslovakia. The survey, which brings together data on homes receiving multi-channel television direct from satellite, through cable networks and using communal aerials, says the total will be 39.2m by the end of the year, more than 25 per cent of all television homes.
