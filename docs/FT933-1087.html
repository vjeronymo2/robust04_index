930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: John Swire John Swire, the London-based parent company of the Swire Group and one of Britain's largest private companies, reported a 19 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits for the six months to end-June. The advance, from Pounds 196m to Pounds 234m, was achieved on turnover up by Pounds 503m to Pounds 1.9bn.
