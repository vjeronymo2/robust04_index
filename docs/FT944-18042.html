941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Alexanders purchase Alexanders Holdings, the Glasgow-based Ford dealer, has bought Metheringham Car Sales of Lincoln, a privately-owned dealership whose franchises include Jeep, Mazda and Nissan, for Pounds 1.38m cash. Pre-tax profits at Metheringham for 1993 were Pounds 122,000 on turnover of Pounds 11.23m. Net assets at that date were Pounds 1.26m. Applying Alexanders' accounting policies, pre-tax profits and net assets would be Pounds 103,000 and Pounds 1.21m respectively.
