941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Eve acquisition Eve Group, the USM-quoted civil engineer, is buying Dick Claydon, the engineer which was placed in receivership on December 9, for Pounds 1.24m. Eve estimated the value of the assets being acquired to be same as the consideration. Claydon reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 267,000 for the year to June 30 1994.
