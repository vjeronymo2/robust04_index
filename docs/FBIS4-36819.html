BFN [Text] Republican Decree No. 6 of 1994 was issued today on the renaming of the news agency of the Democratic Republic of Yemen. Article 1 of the decree provides for the renaming of the news agency to revert to its former name, the ADEN NEWS AGENCY. Article 2 of the decree instructs all administrative and technical offices to observe the implementation of this decree. The last article of the decree provides for the decree to come into effect from the date of its issuance and for it to be published in the official gazette.
