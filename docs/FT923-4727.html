920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Revenue fall at Merlin Intl Green Net asset value per share of Merlin International Green Investment Trust stood at 90.48p at June 30, virtually unchanged on the 90.53p of 12 months earlier. Available revenue for the half year to end-June fell from Pounds 362,000 to Pounds 269,000, equal to earnings of 1.1p (1.4p) per share. The interim dividend is cut by 0.5p to 1p.
