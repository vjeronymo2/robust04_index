BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, has received a written message from Libyan leader Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. Brother 'Umar al-Muntasir, Libyan secretary of the General People's Committee for People's Bureau for External Liaison and International Cooperation, delivered the message when brother president Abu-'Ammar received him in Tunis yesterday.
