Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Our settlement affairs correspondent Gil Litman has learned that over the past several days, settlers from the 'Etzyon Bloc and the Hebron Hills have been touring the Hebron region and marking dozens of alternative routes to the city should the government decide to evacuate Jews. Dov Weinstock, in charge of the 'Etzyon Bloc fields, has said that the settlers will set up some roads to handle vehicles so that reinforcements can arrive in Hebron and help prevent the evacuation.
