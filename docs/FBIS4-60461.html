BFN [Excerpt] General Antoine Lahd, accompanied by Israeli North Region Commander General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, Ground Army Commander Emmanuel Saqr, and Liaison Unit Commander Gabi Ashkenazi, visited al-Bayyadah yesterday to wish a happy 'Id al-Adha to Druze religious leaders. General Lahd hoped the next year will bring happiness and peace and that all foreign armies, including the Israeli and Syrian armies, will withdraw from Lebanon. But he noted that the Israelis are deployed on a particular part of the Lebanese territory for their security needs. The objectives and motives of the Syrians are known to all. Mordekhay offered his 'Id congratulations and said that the region is enjoying security, thanks to the efforts of its sons, especially members of the South Lebanese Army. He said the Israeli soldiers have been ordered to be careful after information that sabotage elements are trying to kidnap them. He said he currently has no information about any specific reaction by the sabotage organizations in Lebanon to the kidnapping of Mustafa al-Dirani. He said the Israeli Army is prepared for any eventuality. [passage omitted]
