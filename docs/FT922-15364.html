920401 FT 01 APR 92 / UK Company News: Headlam drops to Pounds 321,000 Headlam Group's results for 1991, which reflected its old businesses, showed a pre-tax profit of Pounds 321,000, compared with Pounds 458,000. The original subsidiaries continued to trade satisfactorily albeit in difficult conditions. The five floorcovering distribution businesses were acquired after the year-end and were already making a positive contribution to group performance, directors said. Recently, the group paid Pounds 132,000 for certain safety footwear assets from the receiver of Burlington International. Turnover came to Pounds 21.7m (Pounds 23.2m). Profit was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 215,000 (Pounds 158,000). Earnings worked through at 2.385p (2.9p). The final dividend is 1.65p for an unchanged total of 2.4p.
