May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WASTED TALENTS AND THE SECOND LADY

Marilyn Quayle's responses to your reporter's questions were brilliant. My favorite part was when, apparently, the reporter insisted on bringing a photographer into the vice president's quarters. Mrs. Quayle had already explained that was a violation of family privacy, so she simply invited them both to conduct the interview outdoors. Bravo, Mrs. Quayle!

BRENDA HESTER

Garden Grove
