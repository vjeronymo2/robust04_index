BFN [Text] Leonid Kravchuk, president of Ukraine, intends to ask the parliament for the relevant powers if the supreme soviet of Crimea does not revoke the decision to restore the 1992 constitution, Nykola Mykhalchenko, head of the presidential service on questions of internal policy reported. He did not reply to the question as to whether these powers meant the introduction of direct presidential rule or of a state of emergency in Crimea.
