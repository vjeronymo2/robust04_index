BFN [Text] Belarusian Deputy Prime Minister Mikhail Myasnikovich believes that the Belarusian Supreme Soviet will certainly ratify the treaty with Russia on the unification of monetary systems. Speaking at a news conference at the third annual session of the Council of European Bank Managers, he said that Russian Central Bank Chairman Viktor Gerashchenko accepts the Belarusian approach toward a common emission policy within the framework of the unified monetary system.
