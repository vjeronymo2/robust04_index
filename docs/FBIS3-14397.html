Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Mar (MENA)--Two police officers sustained superficial wounds when three people in a car hurled an explosive charge at a car carrying three officers along the Cairo-Ismailia agricultural road. An official source at the Interior Ministry stated that around 1515 today -- as Captain Wa'il Ghazali, an officer at the Abu-Za'bal Prison in al-Galyubiyah, was driving his car in the company of Captain 'Imad Mustafa, chief detective at the prison, and First Lieutenant Ayman Salah, an officer at the same prison -- three persons hurled an explosive charge at their car. The two accompanying officers, 'Imad Mustafa and Ayman Salah, were wounded as a result. The two wounded officers were taken to the al-Khankah hospital for treatment. The police searched the area to locate the culprits.
