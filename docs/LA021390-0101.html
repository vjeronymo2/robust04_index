February 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Hilton Weighs Buyout Offers: Hilton Hotels Corp. said it is evaluating a number of acquisition proposals but would not identify the bidders.

Last August, Hilton began to publicly welcome buyout offers. The Beverly Hills-based firm would not comment on a report that it may soon receive a bid from a group led by NWA Inc. Chairman Alfred A. Checchi. Hilton's board has reviewed some proposals, but the company said it is still willing to consider new buyout offers.
