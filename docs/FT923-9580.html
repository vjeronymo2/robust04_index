920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / EC agrees food aid for Somalia THE European Commission yesterday approved 10,000 tonnes of emergency food aid to drought-stricken Somalia, Reuter reports from Brussels. The aid is to support operations of the International Committee of the Red Cross. The Commission also approved 140 tonnes of high-nutrition food aid to Somali refugees in Yemen and an extra Ecu4m (Pounds 2.82m) in non-food aid.
