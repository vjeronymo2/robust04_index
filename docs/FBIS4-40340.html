BFN [Excerpt] [No dateline as received] -- Exports from Uzbekistan to Pakistan in 1993 reached $8 million, exceeding imports from that country by a factor of four. Uzbekistan supplied Pakistan with mineral fertilizers, agricultural machinery, pesticides, and technology for cotton production, while Pakistan supplied Uzbekistan with textiles and other consumer goods. These facts were quoted at an exhibition of Pakistani goods which opened in Tashkent. [passage omitted]
