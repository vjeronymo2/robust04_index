940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Russians spend Dollars 1bn in UAE Russian tourists who flock to the United Arab Emirates to buy cheap consumer goods spent over Dollars 1bn in 1993, Russia's UAE ambassador was quoted yesterday as saying, Reuter reports from Dubai. Mr Oleg Derkovsky said 250,000 citizens of the Russian Federation visited the UAE in 1993, as well as 200,000 from former Soviet republics.
