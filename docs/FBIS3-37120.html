Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] We present yet another edict by the Georgian head of state. Taking into consideration the fact that due to the current situation in the country it has become necessary to take urgent measures to speed up the process of forming a national army, increasing its fighting efficiency and creating a military doctrine. I have decided to agree to a request made by the collegium of the Georgian Defense Ministry, and as a commander in chief of the armed forces of the Republic of Georgia I will temporarily carry out the duties of Georgian defense minister. All armed formations of the state will be subordinate directly to the commander in chief of the armed forces. [Signed] Eduard Shevardnadze
