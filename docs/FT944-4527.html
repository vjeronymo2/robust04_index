941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Daewoo Corp signs venture with Chinese transport company Daewoo Corp, trading unit of South Korea's Daewoo Group, has signed a joint venture with China's Sichuan Province Automobile Transport Company to run express buses in the area. The joint venture, Chengdu-Daewoo Automobile Transport Co Ltd based in Chengdu, will have paid-in capital of Dollars 12m and from next July it will run 40 buses on four routes within Sichuan Province. The joint-venture fleet would rise to 100 buses in the near future.
