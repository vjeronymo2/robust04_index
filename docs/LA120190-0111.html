December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SPORTS ON WEEKEND TV

The Rams and Raiders, along with the Clippers, Lakers and UCLA, USC and Pepperdine basketball teams, will be among the weekend viewing choices.

The Lakers put their five-game winning streak on the line against the Phoenix Suns tonight (6:30 p.m., Channels 9, 51 and 63).

The Clippers take on the defensive-minded Minnesota Timberwolves at 6 p.m. Sunday on Prime Ticket.

Sunday's pro football kicks off at 10 a.m. when the Rams (4-7) visit the Cleveland Browns (2-9) (Channels 2 and 8). Channels 4 and 36 counter with a Miami Dolphins-Washington Redskins game at 10 a.m. The Raiders will seek to avoid losing for the fourth time in five games when they visit AFC West rival Denver Broncos (1 p.m., Channels 4, 36 and 39). ESPN's 5 p.m. game matches the Minnesota Vikings (5-6), winners of four consecutive games, and the Green Bay Packers (6-5).

In college basketball, No. 12 UCLA hosts Loyola Marymount Sunday, with Prime Ticket's delayed broadcast beginning at 11:30 p.m. In another Prime Ticket delayed broadcast, USC plays Miami (Fla.) at 2 p.m. today. Prime Ticket also has a live Pepperdine-DePaul telecast at 5 p.m. today.
