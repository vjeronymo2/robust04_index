July 2, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HIGH-SPEED TRAIN, COMMUTER INTERESTS

A high-speed train to take people to Las Vegas, where they lose money, while thousands sweat it out on freeways driving to work means only one thing: The gambling interests are lining the politicians' pockets with bribes.

Who lobbies for the commuter? All he can do is write letters saying how much we need a good transportation system for the commuters.

Maybe we could talk Las Vegas into having the train operate so commuters would benefit too.

ROBERT SHACKFORD

South Laguna
