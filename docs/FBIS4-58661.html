BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi received a thank-you message from U.S. President Bill Clinton for the message of condolences that the Lebanese president sent him on the death of former President Richard Nixon. Before he left for the Syrian capital this morning, President al-Hirawi also received a message from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. In this message, Secretary Christopher voices his regret for not being able to visit Lebanon and explains the reasons. Christopher adds that the United States is continuing to promote peace, as it promised to do.
