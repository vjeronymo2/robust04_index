941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: AB Engineering Good progress in its engineering businesses helped Associated British Engineering to achieve a sharp increase in first-half profits, despite continuing difficult conditions in the catering equipment market. Pre-tax profits of Pounds 315,000 (Pounds 106,000) were scored on sales of Pounds 29.7m (Pounds 25.3m) in the six months to September 30. Earnings came to 0.08p (0.02p).
