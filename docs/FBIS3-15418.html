Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 04, BNS -- The representatives of environmental protection committee at Latvian Environmental and Regional Development Ministry together with biologists and environment protection specialists decided Thursday [3 March] to form a foundation for the research of Skrunda radar station's influence upon environment and human health. The foundation will be formed from the money resources contributed by the state, municipalities and different organizations. The foundation plans to start its activities in the nearest future and invite foreign scientists to participate in the foundation's work. The initiators of the foundation plan to organize an international scientists' conference in June to discuss matters related to Skrunda radar station. Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis took part at the foundation's opening conference and promised to participate in foundation's work.
