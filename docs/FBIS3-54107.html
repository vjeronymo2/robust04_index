Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Palestinian president has held talks with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa. Members of the Egyptian and Palestinian delegations attended the meeting. Musa said after the talks that Egypt is working to advance the peace process on the various Syrian, Lebanese, Jordanian, and Palestinian tracks. He added that PLO Political Department chief Faruq Qaddumi will arrive in Cairo tomorrow and that Egypt will hold active contacts with the parties concerned to boost the peace process. He added that progress is expected on the Palestinian-Israeli track in the next few days.
