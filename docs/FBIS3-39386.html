Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The German defense minister, Volker Ruehe, has arrived in Bern for an official two-day visit to Switzerland. Mr. Ruehe was welcomed by his Swiss counterpart, Kaspar Villiger. The two ministers will hold talks on bilateral security issues and relations with international military organizations. They will also discuss international peacekeeping operations, the development in Eastern and Central Europe, as well as the reorganization of the Armed Forces in Germany and Switzerland. On Tuesday [15 February] Mr. Ruehe will meet the Swiss foreign minister, Flavio Cotti, and visit a Swiss Army regiment.
