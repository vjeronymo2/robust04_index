930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Jersey Phoenix net asset value ahead Jersey Phoenix Trust reported a net asset value of 101.78p per share as at January 31, up from 93p a year earlier. Retained profits for the half year to December 31 amounted to Pounds 17,744 against a comparable deficit of Pounds 43,788. Earnings per share emerged at 2.63p (2.21p). A second interim dividend of 1.25p gross is declared, making 2.5p to date.
