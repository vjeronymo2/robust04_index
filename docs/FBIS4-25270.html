BFN [Text] Beijing, June 8 (AFP)--Police have launched a formal investigation into a prominent Chinese dissident, Bao Ge, who was detained last Friday on the eve of the fifth anniversary of the Tiananmen Square, one of his relatives said by telephone Wednesday. Police told Bao's family that the dissident had "broken the law" and said, without further explanation, that an inquiry had been opened, the sources said. The source added that several police searched Bao's parents' home late Tuesday, and his mother was called to the police station, but it was not known whether she had been released.
