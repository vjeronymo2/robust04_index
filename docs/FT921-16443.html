920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Aquino aide to run for president Mr Fidel Ramos, the former defence secretary who helped install Mrs Corazon Aquino as Philippine president and crushed seven coup attempts against her, declared his Presidential candidacy yesterday, AP reports from Manila. Mr Ramos, 63, launched his campaign after his newly organised People Power party unanimously chose him as its standard-bearer for the May 11 election. The West Point graduate is seeking Mrs Aquino's endorsement, despite opposition from the Roman Catholic hierarchy.
