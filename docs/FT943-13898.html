940718 FT 18 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: British Open golf win for Price Nick Price, 37, holed a 75-ft eagle putt at the 17th and made a safe par on the last green to win the British Open golf championship at Turnberry. The Zimbabwean's first Open title was achieved with a four-round total of 268, one stroke ahead of Swede Jesper Parnevik.
