Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 25 Jan (ARMENPRES)--Things are extremely tense in the Karabakh conflict zone. The Azerbaijani troops resumed their offensive against Mardakert this morning, but the Karabakh Army is halting the enemy attacks. An Azerbaijani tank was hit by 1030. Active combat operations are taking place in the northwest of the war front. The enemy's army is trying to move deep into Mardakertskiy and Kelbadzharskiy Rayons. Again, the attack is being checked. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army Press Service has denied an Azerbaijani Defense Ministry statement that claimed the Azerbaijani Army was successfully moving deep into Kelbadzharskiy Rayon and that the Armenians have suffered enormous losses (300 killed).
