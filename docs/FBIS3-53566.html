Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has described the U.S.-Syrian summit as positive and said he expects it to have a generally favorable effect, particularly as it also dealt with the Lebanese issue. Al-Hariri said he learned this from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher during a telephone conversation yesterday. The prime minister did not rule out holding a Lebanese-Syrian summit soon, but did not mention a date.
