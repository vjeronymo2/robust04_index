BFN [Text] Beijing, May 6 (XINHUA) -- Ni Zhifu, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), met with a delegation from the National Defense Committee (NDC) of Argentine Senate here this afternoon. The delegation, headed by Eduardo Pedro Vaca, NDC chairman of Argentine Senate, arrived here Wednesday [4 May] as guests of the NPC Foreign Affairs Committee. Zhu Liang, chairman of the NPC Foreign Affairs Committee, met with Vaca and his party and hosted a banquet in their honor here yesterday. Vaca and his party are also expected to travel to Dalian, Shanghai, Suzhou and Nanjing during their China visit.
