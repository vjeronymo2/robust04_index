920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / World Trade News: John Brown wins turbine order John Brown, part of Trafalgar House's engineering division, has won a contract worth nearly Pounds 100m to build and supply 12 38MW gas turbines for Abu Dhabi, writes Andrew Baxter. The deal, thought to be the world's largest order for this kind of heavy-duty gas turbine, is further confirmation of the revival of the Middle East power plant market. It is understood that John Brown clinched the order by being able to offer a short delivery time.
