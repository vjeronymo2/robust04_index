BFN [Text] Today in Riga, the Saeima Committee on Defense and Internal Affairs and Defense Minister Valdis Pavlovskis met the members of the Swedish Parliament's Defense Committee, who are currently acquainting themselves with the work of their colleagues in the Baltic countries. The Swedish colleagues were interested in Latvia's military doctrine, the national armed forces structures, the relationship between the Home Guard and the defense forces, the problems of guarding the borders, and women's role in the creation of defense policy. Yesterday, the members of the Swedish parliamentary Defense Committee visited Lithuania; tomorrow, they arrive in Estonia. It should be noted that this is the first instance since the declaration of Latvian independence that the full complement of another country's parliamentary commission has visited Latvia.
