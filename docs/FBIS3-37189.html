Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Feb 14, BNS -- Lithuanian border guards detained three Russian officers Sunday [13 February] night as they attempted to illegally enter Lithuanian territory. The trespassers were caught on a bridge across the Nemunas river in the western Lithuanian town of Pagegiai and were sent back to the Kaliningrad region. Seventeen Russian re-enlistees were taken off the St. Petersburg- Kaliningrad train at the Turmantas border crossing in eastern Lithuania. The Russians, who headed for the Kaliningrad region without permission of Lithuanian authorities, were returned to the territory of Belarus. The weekend "catch" of Lithuanian border guards also included a drug smuggler. Pranas Dragunaitis, head of the Lithuanian Border Defense Service Headquarters, said a man who attempted to smuggle drugs in his train compartment was detained on the Lvov-St. Petersburg train Sunday.
