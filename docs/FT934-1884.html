931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Bogod Profits of Bogod Group, which sells sewing machines, parts and accessories, fell from Pounds 105,000 to Pounds 74,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. Turnover improved to Pounds 3m (Pounds 2.5m). Earnings edged up to 1.26p (1.22p) and the interim dividend on the A shares is held at 0.2p.
