920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Taiwan reserves reach Dollars 84.6bn Taiwan's foreign currency reserves, the largest in the world, surged to a record Dollars 84.6bn (Pounds 44.2bn) at the end of May, the central bank said yesterday, Reuter reports from Taipei. It said the increase was mainly due to the rise of the D-Mark and the yen against the dollar and to interest on foreign currency holdings.
