Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Important Addition"] [Text] The Russian Federation president has added to point 1 of his 2 October 1993 edict no. 1573 "on the draft to military service of Russian Federation citizens who have reached draft age and are studying in daytime departments at general education establishments providing initial and secondary vocational education" the following paragraph: "Deferment from the draft to military service is provided for their entire period of study for Russian Federation citizens who have reached the age of 18 and are studying in daytime departments of secondary specialized educational establishments of civil aviation and maritime and river transport under the jurisdiction of the Russian Federation Ministry of Transport."
