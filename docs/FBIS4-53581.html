BFN [Text] The Department of Interior and Local Government [DILG] will transform the mineral-rich Kalayaan [Freedom] chain of islands [part of Spratly Islands claimed by the Philippines] into one of the country's viable local government unit. The Kalayaan Islands is considered to be the next flash point among the Asian claimant nations. DILG Acting Secretary Alexander Aguirre said the transformation will be in line with earlier plans of the Cabinet Cluster "E" to attract settlers in the area. Aside from the Philippines, the other claimant nations which have already established military units in the areas include Malaysia, Taiwan, Vietnam, Brunei and China.
