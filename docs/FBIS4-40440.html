BFN [Unattributed report: "The Missile Carrier Varyah Will Be Scrapped"] [Text] The missile carrier Varyah will be scrapped. This decision was adopted on 2 June by the board of Ukraine's Ministry of Machine Building, Military-Industrial Complex and Conversion. Ukrainian Vice Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov, in an interview for journalists on 9 June called the decision "forced and tragic." In the past, Ukraine offered Russia to purchase the still unfinished cruiser that stands near the moorings of the Black Sea Ship Building Plant in Nikolayev, but there was no response.
