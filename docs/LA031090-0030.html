March 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $20 Friday when Carokey, the front half of his win parlay, ran out of the money in the fourth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $591.70.

Bob's bet today is a $25 win parlay from Inquisitorial in the second to Colroy in the fourth.

Friday's Loss $20
