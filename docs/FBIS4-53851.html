BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 22 (KCNA) -- Working people here significantly marked the lapse of 48 years since the great leader President Kim Il-song participated in the ground-breaking ceremony of the Potong River improvement project and kindled the first flame of grand nature-harnessing . On May 21, 1946, President Kim Il-song initiated the Potong River improvement project as the first undertaking for carrying into practice the plan of land administration he had matured in the period of the anti-Japanese revolutionary struggle, and took up the first spade at the ceremony. The project was completed in only 55 days thanks to the wise guidance of President Kim Il-song and to the great zeal and will of builders and Pyongyang citizens to build the country. With a view to conveying down through generations the immortal feat of President Kim Il-song, the Workers' Party and people of Korea set up a monument to the Potong River improvement project on the spot and annually celebrate May 21 as "day of builders". A meeting of working people in the capital city took place in front of the monument on May 21.
