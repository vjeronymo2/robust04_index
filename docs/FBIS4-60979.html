BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Kanat Saudabayev paid an official visit to Saudi Arabia from 29 April to 2 May. He was received by His Majesty King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud. The foreign minister gave President Nazarbayev's personal letter to the king. The king expressed sincere gratitude and asked Saudabayev to convey his respect to the Kazakh leader. The king highly assessed Nazarbayev's efforts to reform society and the economy, to preserve peace and stability in Central Asia, and to strengthen security in the region. The king invited Nazarbayev to pay an official visit to Saudi Arabia. Kanat Saudabayev met Saudi Foreign Minister Sa'ud al-Faysal Bin- 'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud. They discussed broadening bilateral relations and other regional and international issues of mutual interest. Kazakhstan and Saudi Arabia expressed their readiness to develop political, economic, and other relations. [passage omitted] The ministers signed a protocol on establishing diplomatic relations at embassy level, and they intend to take appropriate measures to develop friendly cooperation. Thus, Foreign Minister Sa'ud al-Faysal Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud will pay a return visit to Almaty shortly. Saudabayev also met the Saudi oil, trade, and planning ministers and the head of the development fund and discussed future economic and trade cooperation. The two countries agreed to exchanged trade delegations in the near future.
