920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Smith & Nephew Dollars 19m SoloPak sale Smith & Nephew has sold its SoloPak division, which makes pharmaceuticals for the US market, to Ivax Corporation for Dollars 19m (Pounds 10.8m) cash. The disposal was made because the loss-making SoloPak's products did not fit with Smith & Nephew's international healthcare range. Net assets on sale are approximately Dollars 25m: SoloPak's operating losses in 1991 were Dollars 1m. Ivax's interests include chemicals, pharmaceuticals and medical diagnostics.
