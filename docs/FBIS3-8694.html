Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Just after 0600 this morning the Serb aggressor began attacks on the Croatian Army's defense lines on the Lika battlefield, the political department of the Gospic operational area of the Croatian Army reports. The Serb forces opened heavy artillery fire on the residential outskirts of Licko Lesce, and at 1000 the attacks were still in progress, Major Darko Dekovic, the assistant commander of the Gospic operational area said. According to first reports there are no casualties, but damage to residential and industrial facilities is huge. The county information center in Gospic warned the residents to be cautious. The Perusic-Stari Licki Osik-Gospic road is still closed, and traffic is diverted onto the Perusic-Klanac-Gospic road. All school classes have been cancelled until further notice.
