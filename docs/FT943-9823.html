940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / Observer: Sun, sea, and dung A Cologne judge has refused to order a travel company to grant a family a 50 per cent rebate on what they claimed was a particularly malodorous north African holiday. The judge instructed them - as any well-travelled German should have already known - that camel dung and seaweed were not unpredictable hazards of Tunisian beaches.
