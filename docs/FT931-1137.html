930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Sheldon Jones Sheldon Jones, the USM-quoted pet foods and garden and timber products distributor, cut its loss from Pounds 912,000 to Pounds 764,000 in the six months to December 31. Turnover was higher at Pounds 6.55m, against Pounds 6.29m. The figures have been prepared in accord with FRS 3. Last year's reported pre-tax loss was Pounds 663,000. Losses per share came out at 14.77p (16.41p).
