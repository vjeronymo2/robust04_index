August 6, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

STATE;

NO WINNER; LOTTO AT $30 MILLION

No one had a winning grand prize ticket in weekend California Lotto 6/53 game, and the $23.2-million jackpot is estimated to grow to $30 million by Wednesday, lottery officials said today.

Seven players guessed five of the six numbers plus the bonus number drawn Saturday night. Each ticket is worth $112,918.

The tickets for those winners were bought in East Palo Alto, San Leandro, Fairfax, Pixley, Hemet, Santa Monica and Santa Maria.

Another 184 players had five of the six numbers, each winning $4,295.
