Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Yugoslav foreign minister, Mr. Vladislav Jovanovic, has arrived in Accra today on a five-day official visit to Ghana. He was met on arrival by the foreign minister, Dr. Obed Asamoah. Mr. Jovanovic told newsmen that his country hopes to overcome its difficulties soon. He said Yugoslavia appreciates and understands the sympathy shown by Ghana and other friendly countries which are helping her rejoin and play an active role in international life. He hoped Yugoslavia and Ghana will resume bilateral relations and cooperation in all fields. Mr. Jovanovic is expected to inspect Ghana-Yugoslav joint projects and visit places of interest.
