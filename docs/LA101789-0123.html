October 17, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

World Wrestling Federation stars Hulk Hogan, Andre the Giant, Rowdy Roddy Piper, Randy Savage and others may be moving out of the ring and onto the screen. Shane Distribution, pleased with the results of its first feature film production, last summer's "No Holds Barred," has opened a development department to produce films, television and video programming that would spotlight the giant names of the WWF. Shane turned professional wrestling into a craze in the mid-1980s and is the movie-making and financing arm of Titan Sports, Inc., a Stamford, Conn.-based firm which is the parent company of the WWF. ALEENE MacMINN
