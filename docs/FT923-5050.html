920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / Ballot for bank staff SOME 2,000 workers at National Westminster Bank in Manchester are to be balloted on taking industrial action in protest at compulsory job losses. The executive of the Bifu banking union decided yesterday to ballot over what it says are 250 job losses in the past two months, including 70 compulsory redundancies. Union members will be asked to support an overtime ban and, if they do, another ballot will be held on a series of one-day strikes.
