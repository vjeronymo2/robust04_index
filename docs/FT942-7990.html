940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Arts (Openings): RSC Emma Fielding makes her debut with the Royal Shakespeare Company in Stratford-upon-Avon on Wednesday in Ian Judge's new production of 'Twelfth Night'. This is the young actress who made so bold an impression last year in the premiere of Tom Stoppard's 'Arcadia'. Later this year with the RSC she will appear in 'A Midsummer Night's Dream' and in John Ford's rare 1627 'The Broken Heart'
