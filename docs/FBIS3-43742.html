Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The ANC's [African National Congress] draft plan to revamp the mining industry has not only caused ripples locally. Internationally it has also being labeled as nationalization. Share prices on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange plunged sharply right after the announcement with the gold index weakening by nearly 5 percent. Industrial shares dropped by 2.1 percent, and the overall index by 2.5 percent. [passage omitted] The Conservative Party [CP] has condemned the proposal saying that most mines were situated in the area it wanted to turn into a volkstaat [nation state], therefore, the CP would only relinquish mineral rights after a struggle.
