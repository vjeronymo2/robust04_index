BFN [Text] Prague June 9 (CTK) -- The cancellation of the clearing account for trade between the Czech and Slovak Republics would be no big problem for the Czech Republic from the viewpoint of foreign currency reserves, but may create a problem for both sides as regards the volume of trade, Czech Finance Minister Ivan Kocarnik said today. Kocarnik was responding on NOVA Television to yesterday's statement by Slovak Economy Minister Peter Magvasi that the clearing account had outlived its usefulness. He told CTK that payments ought to be liberalised which meant trading in convertible currencies.
