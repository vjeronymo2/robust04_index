930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Lancashire Ents advances 16% Lancashire Enterprises, the economic development arm of Lancashire County Council which was privatised by public offer three years ago, raised pre-tax profits by 16 per cent from Pounds 2.12m to Pounds 2.46m in 1992. Turnover rose 9 per cent to Pounds 12m (Pounds 11m). A final dividend of 8.5p makes 12.5p (11p) for the year. The company specialises in training, economic consultancy and industrial property conversions and has started exporting its Lancashire experience to eastern Europe.
