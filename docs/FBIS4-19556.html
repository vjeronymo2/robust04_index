BFN [Text] Milan, April 18 (AFP) -- Italian police arrested about 100 people Monday [18 April] in a massive crackdown against the Mafia in the Milan region, authorities here announced. They said some 1,000 officers were involved in the ongoing operation dubbed Scorched Earth and were expected to serve a total of 140 arrest warrants in the region. Search warrants have also allowed police to seize hundreds of weapons, including machine guns and pistols, authorities said. The suspects are allegedly involved in drug trafficking and belonging to organized crime.
