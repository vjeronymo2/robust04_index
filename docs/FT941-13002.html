940127 FT 27 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Murray Split Cap edges ahead Murray Split Capital Trust, managed in Glasgow by Murray Johnstone, reported a marginal increase in net assets per capital share from 214.9p to 215.5p in the three months to November 30. Available revenue slipped to Pounds 199,000 (Pounds 206,000) and earnings per income share were 2.47p (2.58p). As forecast, the first quarter dividend is 2.65p with a maintained total of 10.6p expected for the current year.
