June 14, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Meet George Jetson: Get ready for the Jetsons. Hanna-Barbera Home Video will release six classic episodes of "The Jetsons" on video June 28 to gear viewers up for the July 6 opening of "Jetsons: The Movie."

The 30-minute videos will be available in stores for $9.95 per episode. The video package adds new titles to the selection of Jetsons cassettes already available including "The Jetsons First Episodes" and "Rockin' with Judy Jetson." Hanna-Barbera Productions joins with Universal Pictures for the full-length animated feature film. BETH KLEID
