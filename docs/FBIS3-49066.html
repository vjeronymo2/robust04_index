Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Republic of Kosova Information Center reports that the Supreme Court of Serbia continues to detain 10 Kosovar Albanians who were arrested by the Serbian police last November on the charge that, allegedly, they and some other persons, as members of the Kosova Defense Ministry, made preparations for an armed war in Kosova. The counsels of the defendants have declared that the arrest of the Albanian youngsters runs counter to Serbian laws and this presents another flagrant violation of investigation procedures. The counsels of these Albanian prisoners have also declared that the defendants are continuously tortured by the Serbian police.
