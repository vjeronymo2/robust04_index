May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OVERDONE PREVIEWS

Are we the only ones who resent the 20-minute movie previews we are shown in movie theaters? These previews reveal the entire plot, and they show all the major action and events. We end up feeling like we already saw the movie. Previews should entice viewers, not tell the entire story!

DIRK and ERIK SANDERS

Woodland Hills
