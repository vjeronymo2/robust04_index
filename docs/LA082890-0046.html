August 28, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Station Stowaway Found: Police searching the building housing television station WLS in Chicago cornered an armed-robbery suspect in a closet early Monday, two days after the man took refuge in the building and disrupted the station's broadcast schedule.

John Fidel Kane, 28, was found hiding in a seventh-floor closet, Police Lt. Frank Kehoe said. He had a semiautomatic weapon and about $6,000 in cash. Kane allegedly robbed a pizzeria Saturday morning, fled and entered the building. BETH KLEID
