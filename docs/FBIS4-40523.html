BFN [Text] The Georgian Defense Ministry press service has circulated a report stating that, in accordance with a plan agreed upon at the Abkhaz-Georgian Moscow negotiations, the deployment of Russian troops in the conflict zone was intended to begin on 17 June and be completed on 19 June. However, because of curt statements made by some representatives of the parliamentary opposition, urging that the implementation of the mission by a Russian peacekeeping contingent should not take place, the stationing of troops in the conflict zone may presumably be postponed for an indefinite period.
