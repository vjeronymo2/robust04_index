Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Editorial: "Jerusalem"] [Text] President Clinton's remarks about Jerusalem are particularly noteworthy, although they would have sounded better had they not been said in the context of the UN Security Council resolution that is expected to mention East Jerusalem as occupied territory. We have to wait and see if and how the President's commitment to a united Jerusalem as the capital of Israel is translated into the vote of his UN delegation. And, lest we forget the forum in which the remarks were made: The leaders of the Jewish community in the United States -- the same people whose lobbying and donations we so much like to dismiss -- are the ones who heard from the President his renewed commitment to the issue of Jerusalem.
