930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / Fresh social security tax urged A NEW social security tax to replace national insurance contributions paid by employees was proposed by the Liberal Democrats yesterday. It forms part of a programme for guaranteeing a higher-rate state retirement pension for those with no other source of income. Mr Archy Kirkwood, the party's social security spokesman, said: 'Under our proposals no pensioners will be left in a state of poverty.'
