BFN [Text] Kigali, 12 Apr (AFP) -- Rwanda's interim government this morning left the Hotel de Kigali, where it had taken up residence, for an unknown destination, thereby causing panic in the city. Shooting intensified in various districts by midday leading to speculations that rebel Rwandan Patriotic Front reinforcements from the north may have joined those already in the capital. However, there is no reliable information about the exact location of the front.
