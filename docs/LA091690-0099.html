September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENT PAPERBACKS: THE TRANSIT OF VENUS BY SHIRLEY HAZZARD (PENGUIN: $8.95)

Shirley Hazzard's sensual novel of love and lust evokes the power of emotion to eclipse reason, as the planet Venus may cast a shadow across the face of the sun.

Two orphaned Australian sisters, Caroline and Grace Bell, come to London to pursue their separate but linked destinies. Grace opts for a steady domesticity that is simultaneously reassuring and confining, while Caroline pursues romance, risking security for more intense passion. Hazzard's intriguing characters move like the clockwork components of an orerry through a series of surprising yet logical revelations.
