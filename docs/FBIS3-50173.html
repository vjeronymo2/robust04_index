Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 25 Jan--Presidents Lech Walesa and Michal Kovac did not sign, as announced earlier, Polish-Slovak agreements on transborder and regional cooperation and on environmental protection. As Wladyslaw Klaczynski, head of the Foreign Ministry Press and Information Department, explained, the agreements had not been signed for "technical reasons." The Slovak side put forward additional suggestions to the prepared agreements. Klaczynski announced that the agreements "would be signed in the immediate future." A Polish-Slovak agreement on investment protection and on avoiding double taxation is also in the final phase of negotiations.
