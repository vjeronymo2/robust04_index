920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / International Company News: MCI telecom edges ahead MCI Communications, the second-largest long-distance telephone carrier in the US, reported second-quarter net earnings of Dollars 141m, or 53 cents a share, up from Dollars 134m, or 51 cents, in the same period last year, writes Martin Dickson. Revenues rose 10.7 per cent, from Dollars 2.35bn to Dollars 2.61n, while the company said traffic had grown by 14.6 per cent, nearly double the industry average. MCI, locked in a battle for market share with American Telephone & Telegraph and Sprint, said the figures reflected 'major wins in large accounts, continued success in residential services and strong international growth across all markets'.
