930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / People: Constructive careers - Taylor Woodrow David Thurston has been appointed md and Wallace Clarke deputy md of Taymech, part of TAYLOR WOODROW. Stan Hardiman is appointed md and Mike Attwell and Julian Sargent directors of Taymel. These appointments follow John McKenna's move to be md of Taylor Woodrow Construction Holdings and his stepping down as md of Taymech and Taymel.
