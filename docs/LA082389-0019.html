August 23, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

DEFENDANT DENIES FATAL STABBING IN PARK

A Mexican citizen pleaded not guilty Tuesday to charges related to the stabbing death of a man in Balboa Park this month.

Javier Garcia Gutierrez, 19, is scheduled to go to trial Oct. 6 in the July 5 slaying and armed robbery of Franklin James Black, 33.

Garcia, who is being held in County Jail on $500,000 bail, was arrested in the park along with five other undocumented aliens, police said.
