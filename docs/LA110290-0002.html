November 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

SHARING BLAME OF 'MORAL MUTANTS'

Gary Abrams' "Moral Mutants" article on Michael Josephson's ethics research was shocking and sad.

Young American adults have learned selfishness from their parents, public officials and business leaders. It's a sad commentary on the quality of American life and the prospects for the future. My hope is that the shocking truth of low and declining morals and ethical standards will be a catalyst for change, but I'm not optimistic.

JOHN LICHTER

Santa Monica
