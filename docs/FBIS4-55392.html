BFN [Text] Prishtina, May 11 (ATA) -- The Albanians in Kosova are not only arrested by the police and army. Three Serb civilians captured in his house Burim Demiri, born in 1971, was reported yesterday from Gjilan. [sentence as received] They informed his family that Burim is sent to the Serb training centre in Prishtina. This is one of the many cases of the forceful enrollment in the Serb Army of the Albanian youngsters in Kosova.
