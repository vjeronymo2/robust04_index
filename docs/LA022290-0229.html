February 22, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

APPLE REDUCES PRICES ON MACINTOSH, PRINTERS

Apple Computer today announced immediate cuts of 10% to 17% off the suggested retail prices on all models of its Macintosh SE and SE30 personal computers and Laser-Writer IINT and IINTX printers sold domestically.

David Hancock, senior vice president of marketing, said the cuts would let Apple "bring the Macintosh personal computing experience to more people."
