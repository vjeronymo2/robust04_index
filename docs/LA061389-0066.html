June 13, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Thomas W. Gamel said that Hollywood Park has agreed to turn over a list of its shareholders. Gamel had sued to obtain the list, which he hopes to use to gain support for a proposal to convert the race track to other uses.

Gamel, who has a 5.6% share in Hollywood Park stock, has proposed that the 340-acre site be converted to an off-track betting facility or a football stadium.
