BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow, 26 May -- Russia and Ukraine need 10 to 15 days to resolve several outstanding issues and to prepare the documents for signing by the heads of government. Grigoriy Karasin, director of the Foreign Ministry's Press and Information Department, said this at a briefing today in connection with the Russian-Ukrainian talks at heads of government level, which were held this week. In the Russian capital, he noted that the parties did a great deal of intensive work to carry out the instructions of the presidents of Russia and Ukraine contained in the agreement on phased settlement of the problems of the Black Sea Fleet of 15 April 1994.
