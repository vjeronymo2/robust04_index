940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo gained 3.3 per cent in activity muted by the closure of US markets. The Bovespa index finished 789 up at 24,284. Turnover was low at Cr233.7bn (Dollars 126.8m). Telebras, the telephone utility, strengthened 3.8 per cent to Cr71.40. There were unconfirmed reports yesterday that Goldman Sachs, the US investment bank, had been chosen by the Brazilian government to lead manage a Dollars 500m to Dollars 1bn sovereign global bond issue, Reuter reports. The issue would be the first launch of Brazilian treasury bonds since 1980.
