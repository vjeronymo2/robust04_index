BFN [Text] Canada is sending 350 peacekeepers to Rwanda. The foreign affairs minister, Andre Ouellet, made the announcement in Ottawa today. The Canadians are communications specialists and they will be a part of the 4,000 member force the United Nations is putting together. Mr. Ouellet says the Canadians will leave for Rwanda within a few weeks. They'll join 11 other Canadian officers already serving with the UN contingent in Rwanda.
