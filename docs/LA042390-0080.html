April 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Bard's B-Day Readings: Shakespeare Festival/LA celebrates William Shakespeare's 427th birthday today with a marathon reading of his plays and sonnets at the Biltmore beginning at 9:30 a.m. Participants in the free all-day event include Blanche Baker, Roscoe Lee Browne, Richard Moll, Keanu Reeves, and David Ogden Stiers. On the program: "The Comedy of Errors," "Romeo and Juliet," "The Merchant of Venice" and "The Tempest." GREG BRAXTON
