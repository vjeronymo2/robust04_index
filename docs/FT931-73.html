930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / International Capital Markets: Melamed quits Globex post MR Leo Melamed, the Chicago Mercantile Exchange leader who pioneered the creation of Globex, the international electronic trading system for futures and options, announced last night that he would step down as chairman of the Globex Corporation at the end of May after serving one year in the post. Mr Melamed said he was leaving to devote more energy to his futures clearing firm, Dellsher Investment Company. He has been seeking regulatory approval to sell the firm to the Japanese bank, Sakura.
