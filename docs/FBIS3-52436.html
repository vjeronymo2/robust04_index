Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An armed terrorist gang at 1600 hours on 3 January assassinated Djilali Zaabane, president of the Oued Rhiou court of the judicial council of Mostaganem, in his house in Jdiouia. Djilali Zaabane was born on 15 May 1950 in Mascara province. He had been practicing law for 10 years, and had a higher education diploma and license in law. In another development, it has been learned from security services that security forces killed seven terrorists in various parts of the country and arrested nine others in Algiers and Saida. These people had carried out terrorist operations and killed security officers and citizens.
