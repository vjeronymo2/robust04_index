BFN [Text] The Ninth National Assembly's Fifth Session entered its 25th day of work today. In the morning, the deputies held group discussions to draw on the experiences of the session. At 1000, the National Assembly held a plenary meeting at the conference hall to hear Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam report on our state's foreign affairs activities during the past period. The National Assembly approved a resolution on National Assembly personnel and the amendment to Article Four of the Law on Tax for Transfer of Land Use. This afternoon, the National Assembly will hold the closing ceremony of the Ninth National Assembly's Fifth Session.
