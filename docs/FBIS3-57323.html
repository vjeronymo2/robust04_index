Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "A Cooperation Agreement Has Been Reached"] [Text] Lieutenant General Rustam Akhmetov, minister of defense of the Republic of Uzbekistan, arrived in Ukraine on an official visit. He met with Ukraine's Minister of Defense Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy. In the course of the meeting, the leaders of the military departments exchanged experience in building armed forces and discussed questions of cooperation between the military structures of Ukraine and Uzbekistan. As reported by the Press Service of Ukraine's Ministry of Defense, particular attention was given to cooperation in the branches of rear and technical services, air defense, the training of officers' personnel, and so on. The leaders of the military departments of the two states signed an agreement on military cooperation between the ministries of defense of Ukraine and the Republic of Uzbekistan.
