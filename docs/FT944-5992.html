941201 FT 01 DEC 94 / 24-hour shopping starts today Shops will be legally allowed to open 24 hours a day from Monday to Saturday for the first time from today. Mr Michael Forsyth, home office minister, announced yesterday that shop-opening provisions in the Deregulation and Contracting Out Act would come into force today. The change comes less than four months after the Sunday Trading Act made Sunday opening legal.
