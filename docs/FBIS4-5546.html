BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- Papers here today dedicate by-lined articles to the 25th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Korea and Zambia. The establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries marked an important landmark in the development of friendly relations between Korea and Zambia, NODONG SINMUN says. Zambia now is striving for the national unity and prosperity, it notes, and goes on: The Korean people are rejoiced as over their own over the achievements made by the Zambian people in building a new society. They will further strive as ever to develop the friendly and cooperative relations with the Zambian people on the basis of the idea of independence, peace and friendship. MINJU CHOSON says that, pursuing the non-aligned policy externally, the Zambian Government is making every effort to strengthen unity and cooperation among the African countries.
