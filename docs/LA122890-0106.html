December 28, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: FOOTBALL

Dallas Cowboy quarterback Troy Aikman says don't write him off yet -- his separated right shoulder feels much better -- and he wants a second opinion about surgery.

Coach Jimmy Johnson, however, still expects to place Aikman on injured reserve today. Aikman, who was hurt in Sunday's loss at Philadelphia, put off a decision Wednesday regarding surgery because he said the pain and swelling in the shoulder had subsided and its mobility had increased.
