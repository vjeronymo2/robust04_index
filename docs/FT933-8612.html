930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Eng & Caledonian assets improve Net asset value per ordinary share of English & Caledonian Investment rose from 181.8p to 191.2p over the 12 months ended June 30. After-tax revenue slipped from Pounds 245,000 to Pounds 192,000, equal to earnings per share of 4.28p (5.46p). The dividend for the year is being lifted from 3.75p to 4p via an increased final of 2.75p.
