January 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

USF DEFEATS SAN DIEGO

Joel DeBortoli and Shawn Sykes scored 19 points each to lead the University of San Francisco to a 77-67 West Coast Conference victory over the University of San Diego Saturday night.

Pat Holbert led San Diego (7-10, 2-3) with 17 points.

The Dons (6-12, 2-3) scored six consecutive points to start the second half and built a 36-22 lead.
