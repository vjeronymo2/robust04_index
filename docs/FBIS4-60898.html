BFN [ITAR-TASS report under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] Moscow -- In the first three months of 1994, Russia did not sign a single contract with the main foreign grain suppliers, the Russian Federation Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations advises. Grain imports over the period did not exceed 372,000 tonnes, or only slightly more than 12 percent of the same period last year. This grain was delivered under 1993 carry-over contracts. In the first quarter of 1994, the amounts of purchases of many other types of food including meat, white sugar, and pasta continued to decline.
