Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Andrey Kokoshin, Russian first deputy defense minister, has declared that the results of the financing of the Defense Ministry in the first quarter show that the situation regarding the revenue part of the budget is deteriorating. The state's debts to suppliers of defense equipment and to servicemen today have reached 3 trillion rubles. In February and March, 70 percent of servicemen received no monetary remuneration. According to Kokoshin, the Defense Ministry favors joint efforts by the executive and legislative authorities to increase the revenue part of the budget.
