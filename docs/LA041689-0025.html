April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OWENS LAKE

With reference to "Owens Lake's Dust: Airborne Health Hazard" (Part I, April 2):

I hope the tragic lesson of the water diversion and ultimate drying up of Owens Lake that has resulted in the toxic dust bowl we suffer today will not be forgotten in the battle to save Mono Lake.

STEVEN C. VOORHEES

Manhattan Beach
