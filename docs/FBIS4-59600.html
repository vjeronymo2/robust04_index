BFN [Text] In Algeria, the GIA, the Armed Islamic Group, has claimed responsibility for the killing of two French religious figures in the Kasbah, in Algiers, last Sunday. In its weekly publication, the GIA says, I quote, in the framework of the policy of liquidation of Jews, Christians, and infidels in the Algerian land of Islam, a commando laid an ambush and killed two crusaders who have spent many years harming Algeria.
