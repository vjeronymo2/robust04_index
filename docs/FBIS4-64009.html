BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow May 26 TASS -- Russia welcomes Cambodian King Norodom Sihanouk's peace-making initiative on the holding of a round-table with the participation of representatives of all Cambodian political forces in Pyongyang at the end of May. The round-table is aimed at reaching an arrangement on the introduction of ceasefire throughout Cambodia since June 13, Russian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing on Thursday. "We think that the realisation of this proposal would facilitate to implement one of the main internal political goals of Cambodia -- to complete the process of national reconciliation in the country. We hope that the king's appeal will be equally well heard in all corners of Cambodia," the Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman said.
