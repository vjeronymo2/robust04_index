Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The security forces have imposed a curfew on all Judaea and Samaria towns. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the curfew was clamped down on Nabulus, Qalqilyah, Ramallah, and Bethlehem, as well as their environs. The curfew on Hebron and its environs is continuing, and currently over 600,000 Palestinians in the territories are under curfew . Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports that security prisoners are currently rioting at the Megiddo prison in protest against the Hebron massacre.
