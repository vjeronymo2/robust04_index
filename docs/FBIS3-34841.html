Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government proposes to set up an air enclave in Andaman and Nicobar Islands for better surveillance and to prevent poaching by foreign trawlers. The Coast Guard is also being strengthened with more fast patrol vessels and additional aircraft for air surveillance. This information was given by the minister of state for defense, Mr. Mallikarjun, to the members of the parliamentary consultative committee during a recent visit to the islands.
