940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Burmah chief gets 57% pay increase Mr Jonathan Fry, chief executive of Burmah Castrol, has demonstrated that the privileges of leadership can be substantial. Mr Fry became managing director of the company in 1990, but in August last year he was elevated to chief executive. The impact on his full year salary has just been revealed as a 57 per cent increase to Pounds 472,000. This compared with his previous package of less than Pounds 300,000.
