920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea A good demand prevailed reports the Tea Broker's Association Landed Assams were well supported with selected brighter lines often several pence dearer, others firm except plainer dusts which remained rather weak. Coloury Bangladesh teas sold above valuation but lesser sorts were difficult of sale. Best East Africans were irregular with quality while mediums were often 3p to 5p dearer with coloury dusts a strong feature. Plainer types again advanced. The highest price realised this week was 166p for an Assam pf. Price indications for the week: Quality 155p nom, good medium 135p, medium 126p, low medium 85p
