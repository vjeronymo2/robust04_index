941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / International Company News: Sanyo Securities plans Y20bn rights issue Sanyo Securities, the troubled Japanese broker, is to raise Y20bn (Dollars 198m) through a rights issue to its three leading creditor banks - Nippon Credit Bank, Bank of Tokyo and Daiwa Bank - and Nomura Securities, its largest shareholder, writes Emiko Terazono in Tokyo. A total of 41.7m shares at Y480 each will be issued with payment set for the end of this month. Of these, 33.8m, or 81 per cent, will be allotted to Nomura Land Building, a property subsidiary of Nomura, raising the company's stake to 16.3 per cent from 5.3 per cent.
