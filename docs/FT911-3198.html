910425 FT 25 APR 91 / International Company News: Earnings halved Black & Decker, the home appliances and power tools maker, saw its first-quarter earnings halved, but it hoped that an economic revival and low retailer inventories would boost profits later this year, writes Bernard Simon. Net earnings tumbled to Dollars 4.1m, or 7 cents a share, from Dollars 10m, or 17 cents, a year earlier. Revenues rose by 8 per cent to Dollars 1.1bn. Product sales dipped by 7 per cent.
