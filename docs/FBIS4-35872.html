BFN [Text] Brother Hakam Bal'awi, PLO Executive Committee member and representative in the Republic of Tunisia, has categorically denied news circulated by news agencies on the state of health of the brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], which necessitate transferring him to the Medical City in Amman. Brother Hakam Bal'awi said that all the news circulated by the news agencies is untrue, and that president 'Arafat's appointments' schedule continues according to their priorities. The truth is that that he took a rest for a few hours after catching a cold. He concluded his statement by saying that the brother president is well and that everything that the mass media circulated is untrue.
