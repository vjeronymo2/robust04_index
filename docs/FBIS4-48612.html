We hereby inform the Angolan and international community that WFP operations in the province [words indistinct] never seen them in areas controlled by the government. Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola commanders have never witnessed any arrival of donations from Luanda, hence, this is yet more evidence of well-organized actions of corruption [words indistinct] agriculture and livestock services [words indistinct] operations by any humanitarian organization all over the province.
