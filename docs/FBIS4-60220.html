BFN [Text] Brother fighters and listeners, we have just received the following. In the name of God, the compassionate and merciful, the presidential council of the Democratic Republic of Yemen issues decree No. 1 of 22 May 1994. The elected presidential council has met and, after referring to the constitution, has decreed the following: 1. To elect Dr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid chairman of the presidential council. 2. This decree will become effective from the day of its issuance and will be registered in the official gazette. In the name of God, the compassionate and merciful, the presidential council of the Democratic Republic of Yemen issues decree No. 2 of 22 May 1994. The council has met and, after referring to the constitution, has decreed the following: 1. To appoint brother 'Abd-al-rathman Ali Bin-Muhammad al-Jifri deputy chairman of the presidential council. 2. This decree will become effective from the day of its issuance and will be registered in the official gazette.
