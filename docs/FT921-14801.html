920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Iraqi ambassador resigns Iraq's ambassador to the Netherlands, Mr Safa Salih Al-Falaki, announced yesterday he had resigned from his post to protest the dictatorial regime of President Saddam Hussein, AP reports from the Hague. He asked for permission to stay in the Netherlands with his family. Mr Al-Falaki, who shunned publicity during the Gulf crisis that resulted from Iraq's invasion of Kuwait in August 1990, said: 'I am blaming Saddam Hussein.' He did not elaborate.
