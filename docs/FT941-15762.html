940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Investment Co net assets rise The Investment Company, which invests in preference shares and prior charge investments, increased net asset value per share to 49.62p at September 30 1993, against 39.48p a year earlier and 44.78p at the end of March. After-tax revenue for the half year slipped from Pounds 388,691 to Pounds 367,396 and earnings per share came out at 1.32p (1.4p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
