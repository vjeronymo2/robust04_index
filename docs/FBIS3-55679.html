Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] An emergency session of the Latvian cabinet was held during the night from Monday to Tuesday [10-11 January]. It decided to punish those guilty of the provocations against Russian military personnel and diplomats. [passage omitted] The cabinet adopted a number of amendments to laws making it possible for the government to dismiss leaders of local government authorities who break the law. At the same time, Andrejs Rucs was dismissed and criminal proceedings were instituted against him on charges of exceeding his authority and illegal detention of people, Interfax reported.
