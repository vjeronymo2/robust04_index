Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Mar (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa tonight met PLO Executive Committee member Mahmud 'Abbas, who arrived in Cairo earlier today. The meeting, which was attended by Palestinian Ambassador in Cairo Sa'id Kamal and Fatah representative Zuhdi al-Qidrah, reviewed developments in the Palestinian issue. The talks followed Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat's discussions in Cairo. They evaluated the peace process in light of the recent developments and the impact of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre on the peace march.
