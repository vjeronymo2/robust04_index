931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / Welsh agency aid to rise by Pounds 10m Government grant in aid for the Welsh Development Agency, which was cut from Pounds 78.3m in 1992-93 to Pounds 59.6m this year, is to be increased next year by nearly Pounds 10m. Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary, said yesterday that asset sales should allow the agency to have overall expenditure of Pounds 167m in 1994-95.
