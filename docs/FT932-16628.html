930405 FT 05 APR 93 / Construction Contracts: Rugby stadium SEVERFIELD-REEVE has been awarded over Pounds 7.8m in new contracts. The largest contract, worth Pounds 4.5m, is for the new west stand at Murrayfield stadium, for the Scottish Rugby Union. The new two tier stand will be 325 metres long and will seat 30,000 spectators. When completed the cantilevered roof is expected to be the third longest in the world.
