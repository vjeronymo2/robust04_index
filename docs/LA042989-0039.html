April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WAS IT A DAY IN THE SUN -- OR AN OVERDONE DAY?

The Kareem Abdul-Jabbar farewell was a disgrace to the athletic world -- giving a Rolls-Royce as a gift to a millionaire.

Why not give this money in the name of Abdul-Jabbar to help underprivileged children?

LEO FRIEDMAN

Mission Viejo
