BFN [Text] A regional trial court in Quezon Province, 180 kilometers south of Manila, has ordered the transfer of captured Leftist leader Wilma Tiamzon from the national police to the custody of a Joint Peace Committee of the Catholic Bishops' Conference and the National Council of Churches in the Philippines. The court order virtually released Tiamzon to the Joint Peace Committee, which named Bishop Alberto Ramento as Tiamzon's local guardian and who shall be charged to their safety and appearance in court. [sentence as heard] The arrangement was reached after President Ramos earlier announced Tiamzon's release on the recognizance of a responsible institution.
