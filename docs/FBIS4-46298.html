BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan May 27 TASS -- Powerful explosive device went off on the ground floor of the "Ani" hotel, situated in downtown Yerevan, inside the representation office of Armeno-American joint tourist company "Sidon-Travel" on Friday. As luck would have it, there was no casualties. The hotel building suffered badly. Experts from the Armenian State Department for National Security began criminal investigation into the explosion.
