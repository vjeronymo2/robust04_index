BFN [Text] Szczecin, May 10 -- Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Wannian and the accompanying officers, now in Poland, visited the 8th Naval Fleet stationing in Swinoujscie on Tuesday. The Chinese guests toured a transport-mine ship and a mine-sweeper and met the naval fleet's command, its member Commodore Andrzej Grzechowski said.
