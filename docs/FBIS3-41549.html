Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federal Government has announced the $22 million project to help improve environmental management in Indonesia. Development Cooperation Minister Gordon Bilney says the project will span five years and focus on managing hazardous waste, water and air pollution, and coastal water quality. He said Indonesia's rapid industrial growth, increasing population, and urbanization are resulting in serious environmental problems, particularly in East Java. Australian environmental experts will provide technical advice, technology support, and training.
