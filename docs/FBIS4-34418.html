BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] As soon as the public debt bonds are placed on the market, Fogade [Deposit Guarantee and Banking Protection Fund] will be able to return to the Central Bank of Venezuela [BCV] the funds this bank has disbursed to assist those banks that have been intervened. BCV President Antonio Casas Gonzalez is reluctant to disburse additional funds, but he said there is no other option. The public credit law, which aims to provide funds to Fogade, stipulates these bonds should not accrue interest and are nonnegotiable, but they may be redeemed early.
