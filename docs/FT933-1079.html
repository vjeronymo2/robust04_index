930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fired Earth Tiles Fired Earth Tiles, which sells, markets and distributes terracotta, glazed and other tiles, incurred its first-ever loss, amounting to Pounds 94,000, for the six months ended July 2 1993. There was a Pounds 6,000 profit previously. Turnover was down from Pounds 2.46m to Pounds 2.26m. Losses per share were 1.25p (0.07p earnings).
