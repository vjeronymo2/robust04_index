September 6, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

OXNARD'S MEGGS TAKES LBCC JOB

Long Beach City College officials said late Wednesday that Oxnard College Coach Lindsay Meggs has been hired as the school's baseball coach, filling a vacancy created when Ken Gaylord resigned earlier in the day.

Meggs, who was an assistant at Cal Lutheran in 1989, led the Condors to a 14-22 record last season, his first at Oxnard. Oxnard finished 10th among 11 teams in the Western State Conference.

Gaylord, who coached at Long Beach City College for six seasons, accepted a similar position at Cerritos College. Oxnard officials were unavailable for comment regarding a replacement for Meggs. JEFF RILEY
