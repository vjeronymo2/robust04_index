Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] We have received a report from Boris Koltsov, our special correspondent in Tajikistan, who together with his cameraman, Viktor Cherbakov, was at the center of today's events in the 13th subunit of the Moscow border detachment. Around 60 people crossed the border today from the direction of Afghanistan. At 0800 local time the border detail went into battle, together with a Tajikistan national security detachment. The majority of the group were destroyed. It is possible that at 2200 we will receive a report from the scene. We hope so.
