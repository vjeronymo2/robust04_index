930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Estates & Gen loss falls ESTATES & General, the property investor and developer, reported a fall in pre-tax losses from Pounds 10.3m to Pounds 1.53m in the six months to June 30. The comparable figure was after an exceptional provision of Pounds 7.4m. The company also announced it would shortly be entering negotiations for an extension to its banking facilities. In April it reached agreement for continuing support to December 31. It is also continuing discussions with the Co-operative Bank, as the sole holder of Pounds 3.25m of unquoted cumulative convertible redeemable preference shares, concerning their maturity which became due on June 30. Earlier in the year holders of the 11 per cent unsecured loan notes agreed to revised redemption terms and last week holders of the Pounds 21m first mortgage debenture stock accepted a relaxation of the capital covenant requirements. Turnover increased to Pounds 5.01m (Pounds 4.41m) including property development income of Pounds 891,000 (Pounds 182,000). Losses per share were cut from 53.06p to 7.16p.
