BFN [Text] Some rebel activity has been reported in the southern region and in Grand-Anse by the Haitian Army's headquarters. A group of armed men attacked a military outpost atm Camp Perin during the night of 19 June, according to Army headquarters. According to a high-ranking officer of the Haitian Army, one rebel was killed and the Army sustained one injury. The soldier on duty in the Camp Perin subdistrict reportedly received a bullet wound in the leg during an exchange of shots with the rebels who, it is reported, numbered around 20. According to this high-ranking officer of the Haitian Army, following the exchanges, the group spread out to the south and in Grand-Anse, where they are being pursued by military units. In Grand-Anse, a peasant was reported killed by one of the rebels who later took his own life. [words indistinct]
