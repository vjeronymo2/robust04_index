Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A Libyan-Ukrainian meeting was held in Tripoli this morning and was attended by the assistant secretary general in charge of international affairs at the General People's Committee of Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation [Foreign Ministry] and Mikhail (Pavlovich), head of the First Department at the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry. This meeting was also attended by a number of Libyan and Ukrainian experts and specialists from various fields. The two sides discussed and examined ways to bolster and strengthen bilateral relations. They also reviewed draft agreements signed between the two countries in economic, commercial, financial, scientific, technical, and cultural fields, and they discussed ways to implement these agreements in the interest of both the Libyan and Ukrainian peoples.
