September 10, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Today is California Admission Day. A small number of businesses and offices will be closed in observance of the holidays, including:

* All county offices.

* City offices in Costa Mesa, Laguna Niguel and San Clemente.

Other government offices: State and federal offices and county, state and federal courts will be open today. Offices in all cities except those listed above will be open, also.

Schools: All public schools will be in session today, with the exception of the Tustin Unified School District, which begins the new school year on Tuesday.

Banks: All banks and savings and loan institutions will be open on the holiday.

Mail: All post offices will be open, and mail deposited in mail boxes will be picked up according to the posted regular schedule.

Trash: There will be regular trash pickup by city and county refuse workers today.

Buses: Orange County Transit District buses will run on regular schedule. There will be dial-a-ride and car-pool services available on the holiday.

Parks: All city- and county-run parks and recreation facilities will be open during regular hours today.
