931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / People: Horserace Totalisator Board Christopher Sporborg, deputy chairman of Hambros, a member of the Jockey Club and director of Jockey Clubs Estates, a racehorse-owner and permit-holder and formerly the Jockey Club's representative on the Horserace Betting Levy Board, has been appointed a member of the HORSERACE TOTALISATOR BOARD.
