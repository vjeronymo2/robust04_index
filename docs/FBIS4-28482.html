BFN [Text] In the late hours of 9 June, a top official of the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP] commented on the possibility of his party dropping its plan to submit a no-confidence motion against the administration of Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata. He said: "That possibility cannot be ruled out." A top official of the Social Democratic Party of Japan recently announced that it would not align itself with the LDP regarding an LDP-proposed no-confidence motion. The top LDP official was hinting at the possibility the LDP may scrap its plan to submit the no- confidence motion in light of such circumstances.
