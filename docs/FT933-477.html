930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / Local Tories back suspended MP MR RUPERT ALLASON, the Tory MP suspended from the Conservative party at Westminster in July after failing to support the government in a confidence motion, last night received a vote of confidence from his Torbay constituency association. In a statement after the first executive council meeting since Mr Allason's suspension, officials expressed 'support for his work on behalf of the constituency'.
