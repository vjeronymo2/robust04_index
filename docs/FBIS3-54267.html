Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In a session today, the National Assembly's Arab and International Relations Committee hosted Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf. The minister gave a briefing on Iraqi policy concerning relations with neighboring Arab countries and the latest political developments concerning the unjust blockade imposed on the Iraqi people. Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf explained that thanks to the instructions of leader President Saddam Husayn, the Iraqi policy has yielded good results at the current stage, especially with respect to the change in the policies of some international powers. He hoped that the next stage will witness more positive developments in terms of understanding the nature of the existing conflict in the region in a manner that will contribute to bolstering regional security, stability, and prosperity, and to countering the challenges posed to the region's peoples.
