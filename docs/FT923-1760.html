920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / The Lex Column: Taylor Woodrow The resignation of Mr Peter Hedges as chairman of Taylor Woodrow Property is a vivid sign of the times. His skills were in building up the legacy he leaves behind - a high quality, well-let property portfolio - rather than collecting the rents which nowadays are the cash-strapped Taylor's top priority. That would hardly have been an exciting prospect for the man once tipped to be chairman.
