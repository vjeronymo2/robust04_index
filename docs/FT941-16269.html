940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Thorn EMI sells Electron As part of its continuing move to concentrate on its core music and rentals divisions, Thorn EMI has sold its Electron Tubes offshoot to its management for Pounds 6.6m cash. The management, led by Mr Jan Frederiksen, a director and general manager, obtained the company in the face of competition from trade buyers. It raised a total of Pounds 11.4m for the purchase and working capital with 3i underwriting Pounds 4.2m of equity and mezzanine capital and Midland Bank providing Pounds 3m of term debt plus working capital facilities.
