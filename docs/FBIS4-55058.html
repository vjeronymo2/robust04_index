BFN [Unattributed report: "1,307 Cases of Illegal Crossings of Border"] [Text] The Police Department of Debar stated at a press conference that it has registered 1,307 cases of illegal crossings of the Macedonian-Albanian border (1,303 of them being Albanian citizens), in the period of January-April this year. 1,247 of the cases were underage youngsters, who have been returned to Albania by police intervention, whereas criminal charges have been brought against 56 individuals. The police representatives also informed of 11 border incidents near Debar in the same period.
