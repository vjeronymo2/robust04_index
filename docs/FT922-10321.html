920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / Dublin warned on Maastricht Mr Henning Christophersen, European commissioner for economic and social affairs, told Ireland yesterday that failure to ratify the Maastricht treaty in the country's June referendum would be 'disastrous' for its economy, writes Tim Coone in Dublin. Ireland could be sidelined in EC affairs,and at some point might 'no longer be a real member of the Community'. Mr Albert Reynolds, the Irish prime minister, is presenting a similar message to the electorate.
