[License No. 04/04&hyph;0213] Quantum Capital Partners, Ltd.; Notice of Surrender of License Notice is hereby given that Quantum Capital Partners, Ltd. (QCP), 4400 NE 25th Avenue, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33308 has surrendered its License to operate as a small business investment company under the Small Business Act of 1958, as amended (ACT). QCP was licensed by the Small Business Administration on July 7, 1982. Under the authority vested by the Act pursuant to the Regulations promulgated thereunder, the surrender of the License was accepted on November 4, 1994, and accordingly, all rights, privileges, and franchises derived therefrom have been terminated. (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program No. 59.011, Small Business Investment Companies) Dated: November 22, 1994. Robert D. Stillman, Associate Administrator for Investment.
