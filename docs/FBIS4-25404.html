BFN [From the "Xinjiang News" program; by correspondent Su Lati (5685 2139 2251)] [Text] Yesterday evening, Abulaiti Abudurexiti, Xinjiang Autonomous Regional People's Government chairman, and (Kim Kap-yon), president of the Korea-China Group [KCG] of the ROK, signed a letter of intent for long-term economic cooperation between China's Xinjiang Uygyr Autonomous Region and the KCG on behalf of Xinjiang and the KCG. KCG President (Kim Kap-yon) visited Xinjiang from 30 May to 1 June at the invitation of the Xinjiang government. President (Kim Kap-yon) called on Chairman Abulaiti Abudurexiti and other Xinjiang leaders for talks during his visit. [Video shows close-up of Abulaiti Abudurexiti and Korean guest talking] Both sides extensively exchanged views on Xinjiang's current economic situation, potential for development, and long-term cooperation, and reached a common understanding on Xinjiang's infrastructure construction and industrial development. Prior to the agreement-signing ceremony, Abulaiti Abudurexiti cordially conversed with President (Kim Kap-yon). Xinjiang Vice Chairmen Wang Lequan and Wang Yousan participated in the ceremony.
