930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Reuter man charged in Algeria Reuter correspondent in Algeria, John Baggaley, was charged yesterday with spreading false information following an incorrect report about a political assassination, Reuter reports from Algiers. Mr Baggaley, a 54-year-old Briton based in Algiers since May 1991, was detained by gendarmes for nearly 24 hours after being summoned to the Communications Ministry on Wednesday to explain the report. Reuters had issued a story quoting the gendarmerie as saying the sports minister had been killed in Algiers in a continuing wave of violence blamed on Moslem fundamentalists.
