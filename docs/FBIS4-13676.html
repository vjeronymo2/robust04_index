BFN [Report by Nasir Qamash] [Text] In a statement to AKHIR KHABAR, Information Minister Dr. Jawad al-'Anani has said several reasons led to Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali's decision to cancel his visit to Bucharest, where he was due to participate in the Grans Montana Forum, in which politicians and businessmen from Europe and the Middle East take part. He said the most prominent of these reasons is the inauguration of the extraordinary session of the House of Representatives, and the rumors that Prime Minister al-Majali would meet with Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres at the sidelines of the forum meetings. Al-'Anani said these reasons called for adopting the decision not to go. Meanwhile, Dr. al-'Anani said Jordan was not officially informed of the schedule of U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher's tour in the region, and Jordan did not ask for this schedule. Al-'Anani was answering a question on AFP report that Christopher will not stop in Amman and will meet with His Majesty King Husayn in London next Monday.
