BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondents Viktor Demidenko, Aleksandr Litvinov for TASS] [Text] Kiev, 11 Apr -- According to preliminary results, 312 of 450 people's deputies of Ukraine have been elected to the new parliament. This number of deputies is sufficient to ensure that the country's Supreme Council opens and works fruitfully, since it comprises a constitutional majority. Deputies elected in the second round of voting include Ukraine's Acting Prime Minister Yefim Zvyahilskyy, Deputy Prime Ministers Mykola Zhulynskyy and Valentyn Landyk, and well-known politicians Borys Oliynik and Volodymyr Yavorivskyy.
