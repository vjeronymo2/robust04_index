941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Distribution expansion for BOC BOC Group has bought two further distribution companies, taking its investment in the activity to Pounds 50m since October last year. It has bought London Cargo Group, one of the UK's largest independent air cargo companies, and Transports Logistique Organisation, a French distribution company. The distribution division now operates from 56 sites in the UK, France, the Netherlands and Poland.
