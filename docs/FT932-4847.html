930607 FT 07 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: Norfolk water project SIR ROBERT McALPINE has just commenced work on a 2.4km long sea outfall (2Km below low water level) at West Runton, Norfolk for Anglian Water Services. The contract which is worth about Pounds 5.3m forms part of Anglian Water's scheme for Waste Water Management in north Norfolk and is due for completion in June 1994.
