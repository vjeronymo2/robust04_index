940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / International Company News: Wall Street brokerage in property link Smith Barney Shearson, the Wall Street brokerage house, has formed an alliance with Remsen Partners, the property financing group, that will provide funds for the US commercial property market, writes Patrick Harverson. Under the terms of the deal, Remsen will arrange mortgages on commercial property funded by Smith Barney which the brokerage firm will eventually sell as mortgage-backed securities. to investors. The two partners said that they had already put together more than Dollars 100m in mortgage loans, and they expected the venture to generate as much as Dollars 1bn in financing before the end of the year. For Smith Barney, owned by Travelers, the insurance and financial services group, the aim of the alliance is to boost its presence in the huge large US mortgage-backed securities market by linking up with Remsen's network of property owners and mortgage bankers. Last year, Smith Barney was only ranked 15th among underwriters of mortgage deals in the US, even though the firm is was the second largest brokerage house in the US. The appeal of the alliance to Remsen is the speedy fast access the private partnership it will get to Smith Barney's capital and the ability to win business on the back of the brokerage firm's name.
