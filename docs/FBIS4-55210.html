BFN [Text] On 10 May, President Zhelyu Zhelev received a delegation of the Permanent Board of the Bulgarian National Agrarian Union [BZNS] led by BZNS organizational secretary Petko Iliev. The government crisis and the progress of the agrarian reform were discussed. The meeting took place at the initiative of the Agrarians. After the meeting, Mr. Iliev stated that there are no differences between the two sides on the necessity of replacing the cabinet and holding early parliamentary elections. According to Mr. Iliev, President Zhelev expressed skepticism regarding the upcoming changes in Lyuben Berov's cabinet.
