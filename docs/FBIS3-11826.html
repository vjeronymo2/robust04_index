Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] La Paz, 26 Mar (EFE) -- Following a reshuffling of his cabinet, this is the makeup of President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada's new cabinet: President: Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada, leader of the liberal Nationalist Revolutionary Movement (MNR). Vice President: Victor Hugo Cardenas, leader of the Indian Tupak Katari Revolutionary Liberation Movement (MRTK-L). Foreign Affairs and Worship Minister: Antonio Aranibar, leader of the Free Bolivia Movement (MBL). Economic Development and Finance Minister: Fernando Alvaro Cosio (MNR), replacing independent Fernando Illanes. Human Development Minister: Enrique Ipina Melgar (MNR), replacing independent Fernando Romero. Sustainable Development Minister: Jose Guillermo Justiniano (independent). Government Minister: German Quiroga (MNR). National Defense Minister: Raul Tovar of the Civic Solidarity Unity (UCS), replacing Antonio Cespedes (UCS). Social Communication Minister: Ernesto Machicado (MNR), replacing Hermann Antelo (MNR). Justice Minister: Rene Blattmann (independent), replacing Carlos Morales. Labor Minister: Reynaldo Peters (MNR). Presidency Minister: Carlos Sanchez Berzain (MNR). Minister Without Portfolio: Alfonso Revollo (MNR).
