BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("AZG", "YERKIR" dailies), 05/18/94 -- No significant changes have been reported at the Karabakh fronts during the last two days. Nevertheless, the Azeri units have been shelling the Armenian positions at the villages of Seisulan, Karmiravan and Gasangaya in the Mardakertskiy district and Gyullija, Suma and Umudlu in the Agdamskiy district during the night and in the morning of 17 May. The enemy used mortars and grenade dischargers. According to preliminary information, the Karabakh side suffered no casualties. Meanwhile, "YERKIR" reports, quoting "HAYLUR" agency, one Armenian fighter was injured. According to the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] Defense Army headquarters, the enemy shelled the Karabakh positions at the village of Giulistan in the Shaumyanskiy district later in the afternoon. Again, no casualties are reported. By 1700, the Azeri forces shelled the Karabakh positions in the Fizulinskiy district and in the environs of the Umar gorge at the north of the Kelbadzharskiy district. The RMK forces refrained from returning the fire in all cases.
