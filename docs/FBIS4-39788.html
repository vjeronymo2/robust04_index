BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Alexiy Trotsenko for TASS] [Text] Kiev 9 June -- North Korean Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam completed his four-day official visit to Ukraine on Thursday [9 June]. The North Korean foreign minister had meetings with Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and first deputy chairman of the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet Aleksandr Tkachenko to discuss the development of cooperation between the two countries. North Korean Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and his Ukrainian counterpart Anatoliy Zlenko signed a number of official Korean-Ukrainian documents. Speaking at a press conference for Ukrainian and foreign journalists Kim Yong-nam called his visit successful.
