941108 FT 08 NOV 94 / International Company News: Australian engineer up Australian National Industries, the engineering group which owns the UK's Aurora group, said yesterday that it had made a first-quarter profit after tax of ADollars 14.4m (USDollars 10.77m), up from ADollars 12.6m in the same period of the previous year, writes Nikki Tait. Sales in the three months to end-September were ADollars 396.3m, compared with ADollars 344.1m.
