921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Walker & Staff declines 32% Walker & Staff Holdings, a distributor of valve and pipeline equipment, suffered a 32 per cent downturn in profits to Pounds 237,000 pre-tax for the half year to September 30. Turnover fell to Pounds 3.67m (Pounds 4.25m). Staffing levels were reduced by 15 per cent because of falling demand. Resulting savings were reflected in a decline in other operating charges to Pounds 666,000 (Pounds 718,000). Earnings per share fell to 7.3p (10.9p).
