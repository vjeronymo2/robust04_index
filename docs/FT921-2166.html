920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Thornton Asian net assets improve Net asset value per share of Thornton Asian Emerging Markets Investment Trust stood at 68.8p at the December 31 year end against 61p 12 months earlier. Gross income increased 5 per cent from Pounds 2.15m to Pounds 2.27m. A same again dividend of 0.5p is proposed, payable from earnings per share of 0.55p (0.5p).
