921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Slight rise in French prices FRENCH consumer prices rose 0.1 per cent in September to give a year-on-year rise of 2.6 per cent, the Insee statistics institute said yesterday, Reuter reports. Economists had mostly forecast 0.2 per cent, so the franc, Paris shares and government bonds all rose on the news. But the response was muted as the figures showed a continuation of the low inflation trend the government has maintained with high interest rates and tight spending policies for several years.
