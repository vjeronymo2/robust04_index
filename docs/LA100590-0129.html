October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Blockbuster Goes to Japan: Blockbuster Entertainment Corp., owner of the Blockbuster Video chain, will open 15 super stores in Japan under a tentative joint development agreement with Fujita & Co. Blockbuster Entertainment, based in Florida, is the world's largest video retailer, with 1,463 stores. Fujita has franchising agreements in Japan with Toys R Us and McDonald's.
