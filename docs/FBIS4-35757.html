BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA)--Cairo's Security Directorate has carried out extensive raids in various areas of Cairo to surround and arrest the vendors and duplicators of unlicensed cassettes which contain recordings advocating religious and intellectual extremism. The raids were ordered by Major General Mansur 'Isawi, assistant interior minister and Cairo security director. He issued instructions to confiscate the books and cassettes sold without proper licensing and ordered the arrest of the vendors. Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi recently issued strict orders to surround and arrest individuals who trade books and cassette advocating religious extremism. This act comes during an overall campaign to protect society by seizing extremists and the advocates of harmful ideas.
