BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vyacheslav Akimov] [Text] Moscow, 19 May -- A spokesman for the Rosvooruzheniye state company (the state company for the export and import of arms and combat equipment) has refused to confirm a report of a contract for the supply of Russian weapons to Kuwait thought to be about to be concluded in the next few weeks. Asked by an ITAR-TASS correspondent about the size of the forthcoming arms sales to Kuwait, the spokesman for Rosvooruzhniye noted that, in line with usual practice, the parties signing a contract are obliged not to divulge the content of the agreement, i.e. schedules, type, and amount of arms for export.
