BFN [Report by GIA correspondent for TASS] [Text] Tbilisi, 15 May -- A mass rally was held outside the building of the Tbilisi State Armenian Drama Theater, where there was an explosion during a performance today. Speakers at the rally said an attempt had been made to wreck the centuries-long brotherly relations between the Georgian and the Armenian people. They urged not to yield to the provocations of the enemies of both peoples and to oppose them with unity and joint actions for the sake of peace in the Caucasus. Sandro Kavsadze, chairman of the committee on inter-ethnic relations and the protection of human rights of the Republic of Georgia, made a statement describing the crime as "directed against all of us and against Georgian statehood."
