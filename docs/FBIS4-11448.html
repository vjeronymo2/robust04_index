BFN [From the "YSU Radio News" newscast] [Text] The TSE [Supreme Electoral Tribunal] has initiated the final vote count of 24 April's presidential election, which Dr. Armando Calderon Sol of ARENA [Nationalist Republican Alliance] won in a contest against the left-wing coalition. ARENA won 68.27 percent of the valid votes, and the coalition won 31.68 percent [figures as heard], the equivalent of more than 803,000 votes for the right-wing party and 367,000 for the left-wing party. The TSE has announced that within the next five days it will disclose the final results of the elections, which determined who will govern the country from 1994 to 1999.
