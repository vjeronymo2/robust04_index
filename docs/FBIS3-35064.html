Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has received a thank-you cable from Fidel Castro, president of Cuba's Council of State and Council of Ministers, in reply to the two cables of congratulations his excellency sent on the Cuban Revolution Anniversary and the New Year. Following is the text: Saddam Husayn, Republic of Iraq president. With pleasure, I received the two friendly cables of congratulations which you sent to us on the 35th anniversary of the Cuban Revolution and the New Year. I take this opportunity to stress to your excellency that the fruitful ties of friendship and cooperation will continue. I also take this opportunity to express my sincere wishes of good health to yourself and happiness to the Iraqi people. With my deep respect. [Signed] Fidel Castro Ruz, president of the Council of State and Council of Ministers in the Republic of Cuba
