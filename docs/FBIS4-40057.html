BFN [Text] Yerevan, June 13 (SNARK)--The presidential governing is the only acceptable form for Armenia, considers the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan. Speaking to the extraordinary session of the Armenian Parliament which was devoted to the discussion of the project of the new constitution, he said that the parliamentary system may be effective only in those countries where 2-3 powerful parties are established, and one of them takes the parliamentary majority. "Such parties may appear in Armenia only in dozens years. And even if we take we take the parliamentary governing, in some time we will have to come to the presidential governing but at the cost of greater trials", said the president. He also mentioned that Armenia has the unique chance to refuse from offering self-independence to the regions and build the "classic unitary state". According to Levon Ter-Petrosyan, the official project of the new constitution reflects all the basic principles of the international law to let the republic overcome the inter-political crisis and civil war.
