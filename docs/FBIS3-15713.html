Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Pacific Ocean" Program] [Text] According to the KRASNAYA ZVEZDA newspaper, the Russian Defense Ministry soon intends to carry out a reduction in the military mass media -- a reform of military newspapers and magazines. This is brought about by the critical state of their finances. In all likelihood, publication of large-circulation newspapers on vessels, flotillas, and bases will cease. Only the fleet and district newspapers, such as the Pacific Fleet's newspaper BOYEVAYA VAKHTA [COMBAT WATCH], will remain.
