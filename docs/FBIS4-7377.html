BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 27 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN today comes out with an article titled "Our People Proud of Having Great Leader". The author of the article says: Our people have a high national pride and honour as they have the great leader in the person of respected Comrade Kim Il-song peerless in their history of thousands of years. As they have the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and uphold his leadership, they become a heroic people who won great victories in wars against two formidable imperialist enemies and a proud people who have set an example for the century in creation and construction. Their pride in having the great leader has been heightened as they are guided by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il today. As they have respected Supreme Commander General Kim Chong-il, their pride has reached a lofty height and it serves as an important ideological and moral source of all their victories. Their pride serves as a powerful motive force which enables our people to courageously advance along the road of socialism under whatever conditions. Our people have victoriously advanced the cause of socialism under the banner of the chuche idea, following the guidance of the party and the leader. In this course, our people have become firmly convinced that the road of socialism led by the great leader and the dear leader is the most correct road and become proud of building the most advantageous socialism in the world. They are successfully building socialism, not wavering under the complex situation, because of their high pride of having the great leader. Their pride also serves as the source of strength which enables them to demonstrate the dignity and honor of the country in confrontation with the imperialists. Today our people are famous as a most proud and dignified people who resolutely counter any moves of the imperialists and firmly defend the cause of Korean-style socialism without the slightest vacillation. They are countering the imperialists' anti-socialist campaign with the conviction that they will win because the great leader and the dear leader are leading them and with the unshakable will to build Korean-style socialism better under the guidance of the party and the leader.
