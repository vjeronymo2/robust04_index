BFN [Text] Prague, May 25 (CTK) -- The Czech Government today approved legal measures aimed at transferring state-owned real estate to the ownership of the Federation of Jewish Communities and Jewish Religious Communities, Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus told journalists after a cabinet meeting. Individual ministries ought to submit their proposals for specific transfers to the Finance Ministry for definitive approval by June 15, Klaus said. The State Jewish Museum makes an exclusion since in its case, given the complicated nature of the transfer, a proposal should be submitted only in September, he added. Klaus confirmed that all the transfers would be implemented in the form of a gift-given agreement according to the Civil Code.
