921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Capita makes further purchase Capita Group, which supplies computer and management services to the public sector, is acquiring Yates Edge & Partners for Pounds 600,000. Some 171,921 new ordinary shares at Pounds 3.49 each are to be issued to Yates. The supplier of building and energy-related services has undertaken to retain the shares for two years. In the year to August 31 Yates reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 120,000 on sales of Pounds 700,000. Capita's share price yesterday rose 30p to 367p.
