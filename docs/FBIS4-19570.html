BFN [Text] The United States maintains its hopes that progress can be achieved on the confidence-building measures, but doubts whether this progress can be achieved by the end of April, as stipulated in the latest report from UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali and the Security Council's letter to the secretary general. This position was expressed by a U.S. official to a CyBC [Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation] correspondent in New York. In the meantime, Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department's special coordinator on Cyprus, will meet Saturday [23 April] with Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative in Cyprus, to review the proximity talks on the confidence-building measures. The meeting will take place in view of Clark's visit to Nicosia next week.
