BFN [Message from Coprime Ministers Ranariddh and Hun Sen to King Norodom Sihanouk; dated 3 May] [Text] His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of the Kingdom of Cambodia: Your Majesty, we, the first and second prime ministers of the Royal Government of Cambodia, having learned of Your Majesty's idea concerning a cease-fire and roundtable, beg permission to inform Your Majesty of our consent, support, and compliance with your lofty idea as follows: 1. We will respect and comply with Your Majesty's idea for an immediate, complete, and unconditional cease-fire and halt between Khmer Royal Armed forces [KRAF] and the Khmer Rouge group's armed forces. 2. We are ready to send two KRAF generals to take part in a cease-fire observation committee to oversee this cease-fire throughout Cambodia under Your Majesty's lofty chairmanship. 3. We are ready to attend a roundtable meeting under Your Majesty's lofty chairmanship after the cease-fire takes effect throughout Cambodia and is concretely stabilized. We beg Your Majesty to please accept our most sincere fidelity and highest respects. Please forgive any improprieties. [Signed] First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh; Second Prime Minister Hun Sen [Dated] Phnom Penh, 3 May 1994
