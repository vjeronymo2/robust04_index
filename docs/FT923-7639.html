920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Letter: Taking issue on currency Sir, Re Observer's 'Pioneers under fire' (August 13): Q: How much longer are the Scots (and the Northern Irish) going to be allowed to carry on this unpatriotic practice - ie, issuing their own notes? A: Until the currency notes of the UK are no longer issued by the Bank of England. James R Scott, 15 Old Quay Road, Holywood, Co Down, Northern Ireland
