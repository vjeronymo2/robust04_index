BFN [Text] RIGA, Apr 06, BNS - The Saimnieks (Master) political association was created to participate in the coming local governments elections, said Ziedonis Cevers, ex-interior minister and president of the Drosiba (Security) foundation. Saimnieks will nominate practical specialists who have proved their abilities in various spheres of life, Cevers told reporters. Cevers himself plans to run for Riga mayor. BNS asked Cevers to name the priorities of Saimnieks' economic program but he answered that the association was established only recently and its programs are not ready yet. Another leader of the new organization, President of the Private Enterprise Association Valdis Krisbergs, told BNS that the emphasis should be put on small and medium size business development and welfare of people. Krisbergs characterized the Latvian capital, Riga, as "the poorest and the most expensive city in the world." Other founders of Saimnieks are MPs Eriks Kaza, Ernests Jurkans, actor Ivars Kalnins.
