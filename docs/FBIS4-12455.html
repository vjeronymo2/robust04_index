BFN [Text] The Lebanese Army's deserted barracks in al-Nabatiyah came under an artillery barrage from Israeli positions in Tallat 'Ali al-Tahir at 0945 today. The barrage was accompanied by bursts of heavy machine-gun fire from adjacent hills and valleys. Rounds landed in the vicinity of al-Mansuri from Israeli positions in al- Bayyadah. Israeli helicopters flew over the area at midnight last night, illuminating the area from al-Mansuri, Majdal Zun, Zibqin, and Kafra, up to Yatar. An Israeli gunboat cruised the coast of Tyre. Head of the fishermen syndicate in Tyre said that the fishermen are encountering difficulties fishing because Israeli gunboats intercept their fishing boats.
