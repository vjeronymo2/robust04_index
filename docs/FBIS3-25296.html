Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Feb 14 SAPA -- African National Congress [ANC] leader Nelson Mandela had talks with former state president P. W. Botha on Saturday, according to SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news. Mr Mandela told the SABC he did not wish to comment on the talks as they were of a sensitive nature. He said Mr Botha had contributed constructively to the talks.
