BFN [Text] Odessa [no dateline as received] -- Warships that have been mothballed here for two years will soon undergo repair at a specialized Ukrainian Navy shipyard, which is being set up in cooperation with Odessa enterprise No. 108 and other specialized enterprises, Ukraine's navy commander Vice-Admiral Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy told UNIAN on 14 June. He added that the Ukrainian Navy command had cancelled a contract with the commercial company FTF [as received] for the repair and replacement of equipment at the large assault-landing vessel "Ilya Azarov" and other ships. "We are planning to use the renovated ships to improve the combat readiness of fleet units rather than for holiday cruises in the Mediterranean," Bezkorovaynyy said.
