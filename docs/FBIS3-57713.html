Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich gave what was probably the scantest interview of his life. Asked by a BELINFORM correspondent whether the dismissal of Stanislav Shushkevich from the office of Supreme Soviet chairman will affect the upcoming signing of documents on the unification of the Russian and Belarusian monetary systems, he simply said: No.
