910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Anglo Irish Bank improves to IPounds 3.2m Profits before tax of the Anglo Irish Bank Corporation advanced from IPounds 2.9m to IPounds 3.2m (Pounds 2.88m) for the half year ended March 31. Tax of IPounds 927,000 (IPounds 810,000) left available profits at IPounds 2.3m (IPounds 2.09m), equal to earnings of 3.82p (3.47p) per share. The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.36p. Advances for the half year rose 47 per cent to IPounds 421.1m (IPounds 286.1m) and deposits by 44 per cent to IPounds 599.5m (IPounds 415.2m).
