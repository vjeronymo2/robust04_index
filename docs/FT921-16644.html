920102 FT 02 JAN 92 / Swiss may extradite Iranian Switzerland said yesterday it was examining a French request to extradite an Iranian suspected of helping the killers of Shapour Bakhtiar, the former Iranian prime minister, in Paris last August, Reuter reports from Berne. France formally asked Switzerland on Tuesday to hand over Zeyal Sarhadi, 25, who was arrested outside the Iranian embassy in Berne on December 23 on an Interpol warrant issued in Paris. Mr Sarhadi is suspected by a French investigating magistrate of helping Mr Bakhtiar's killers escape after they stabbed him and his secretary to death on August 6 at the Bakhtiar Paris home.
