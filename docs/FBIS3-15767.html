Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that the situation remains tense in the Fizulinskiy sector of the confrontation line. The Armenian Armed Forces have been continuing their unsuccessful attacks against our Army's positions in this sector since 7 March. Our troops are bravely resisting the enemy onslaughts. Armenian artillery has been shelling our positions in Agdamskiy and Terterskiy Rayons since 0500 this morning. A relative calm prevails in the other sectors of the front.
