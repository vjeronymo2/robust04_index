BFN [Text] Kinshasa, May 7 (AFP)--Zaire's Foreign Minister Mpinga Kassenda and a special Tunisian envoy Zine El Abidine Mestiri died early Saturday [7 May] in a plane crash near Kinshasa, Zaire officials said. The plane was returning from the town of Gbadolite where the Tunisian envoy met Zaire President Mobutu Sese Seko.
