Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, January 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Ambassador to Tunis, Wu Chuanfu, held a banquet here Monday [10 January] to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic ties between China and this north African state. "The Chinese Government attaches particular importance to Sino- Tunisian friendship," said Wu Chuanfu, adding that "it is necessary and possible to strengthen our friendly cooperation, which perfectly corresponds with the interests of the peoples of China and Tunisia." Tunisian Secretary of State in charge of foreign affairs, Said Ben Mustapha, who was present at the banquet, commented that relations between the two countries have entered "a mature stage," praising the bilateral cooperation as a good example of south-south cooperation. Last Saturday, the president of the Tunisian and Chinese People's Friendship Association, Ridha M'rad [name as received], gave a reception in Tunis to celebrate the occasion.
