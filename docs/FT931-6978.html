930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Green Property advances 39% Green Property, the Dublin-based property developer and investor, improved pre-tax profits by 39 per cent to IPounds 1.83m (Pounds 1.9m) in 1992 against IPounds 1.32m. The company has implemented the new financial reporting standard, FRS 3, which, it said, had reduced profits by IPounds 370,000 (IPounds 381,000). Net rents and other operating income rose to IPounds 5.32m (IPounds 4.71m). An unchanged final dividend of 2.6p maintains the total at 3.8p, payable from earnings per share of 7.68p (6.02p).
