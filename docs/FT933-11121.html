930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Bananas are this week's best fruit buy, priced at 40-55p a lb (40-55p), reports the FVIB. Other good buys include Spania Galia melons at 60-99p each (60-99p), depending on size. French, Italian and Spania peaches are widely available at 20-25p each (18-25p). Courgettes are the best vegetable buy this week, English, French and Jersey courgettes are still good value priced at 45-65p a lb (40-60p). English broad beans are excellent this time of year, at 30-40p a lb (25-35p). This weeks best salad buy is English celery, priced at 50-60p a head. English and Dutch tomatoes are still excellent, priced at 35-45p a lb.
