930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / SFO seeks Brent Walker arrest SERIOUS Fraud Office investigators probing the affairs of Brent Walker, the property and leisure group, have issued an arrest warrant for Mr Donald Anderson, the former finance director of Goldcrest films, John Mason writes. Mr Anderson was finance director of Goldcrest when it was a subsidiary of Brent Walker before it was sold by Mr George Walker, the former head of the leisure group. He is thought to have left the country. The Serious Fraud Office said yesterday that his whereabouts was unknown. Mr Walker has been charged with four counts of theft and false accounting involving Pounds 12.5m. Mr Wilfred Aquilina, a former Brent Walker finance director, has been charged with two counts of false accounting.
