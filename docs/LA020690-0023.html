February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

GLENDALE POLICE FIND SLAIN MAN IN CAR

A man was found stabbed to death Monday in a parked car in Glendale, the Glendale Police Department reported.

The man, described as being in his early 30s, was found by officers in a vehicle in the 1500 block of Randall Street about 4 p.m.
