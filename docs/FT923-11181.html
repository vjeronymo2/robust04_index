920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Sunday shopping is investigated SUNDAY shopping at supermarkets remains 'an occasional option rather than a regular planned event', according to a study from Nielsen, the market research group. Figures from the company's electronic household panel suggest that 2.8m households in England and Wales went shopping on at least one Sunday in the month up to June 13. But 70 per cent of these only shopped on one occasion.
