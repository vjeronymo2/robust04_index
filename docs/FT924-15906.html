921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: El Oro Mining profit decline El Oro Mining and Exploration, an investment dealing company, saw its pre-tax profit fall from Pounds 1.09m to Pounds 779,000 in the first half of 1992. Interest income fell as a result of lower UK interest rates and two substantial dividends paid since the last half year. Earnings per share came through at 12.55p (16.51p). Group assets at market value were nearly Pounds 25m (Pounds 25.8m). The group held some Pounds 3m in cash, bonds and high yielding preference shares. El Oro owns over 50 per cent of Exploration Company, which in turn is interested in nearly 40 per cent of El Oro. Exploration reports a pre-tax profit of Pounds 967,000 (Pounds 1.34m) and earnings per share of 5.78p (7.57p). Its assets at market value were Pounds 30.1m (Pounds 30.9m). Some Pounds 7m was in cash, bonds and high yielding preference shares.
