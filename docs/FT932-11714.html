930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Downiebrae halved to Pounds 201,967 Pre-tax profits halved from Pounds 422,968 to Pounds 201,967 at Downiebrae Holdings, the engineering group, in 1992. Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 1.6m (Pounds 1.56m). Earnings per share slipped to 0.85p (1.93p) but an unchanged dividend of 0.5p is proposed. The shares fell 3p to 68p.
