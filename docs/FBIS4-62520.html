BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 17 May (ITAR-TASS) -- For the second consecutive day there has been calm in the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict zone, ITAR-TASS learned at noon at the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry. Ministry officials also said that despite the claims of Armenian sources, the Azerbaijani side has not violated the cease-fire accord.
