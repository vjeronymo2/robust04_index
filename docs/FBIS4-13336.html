BFN [Text] A decision has been made to provide every repentant terrorist with a job. In fact, 25 repentant terrorists have been given jobs in Asyut, after it became evident that they were qualified and had university or high school diplomas that they had earned years ago. Dr. Yusuf Wali, secretary general of the National Democratic Party, made this statement. He also added that the party leaders in Asyut and Asyut Governor Samih Sa'id have been instructed to provide jobs immediately for repentant terrorists in the governorates.
