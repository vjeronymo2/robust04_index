Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] By order of Eduard Shevardnadze, the State Commission for Combating Drug Addiction and Drug Trafficking has been created in Georgia. The commission is made up of the heads of the power departments and also the health and education ministers. According to data supplied by the Georgian Internal Affairs Ministry, to date over 60 percent of Georgian young people are using drugs. This was a report supplied by the IMAPRESS agency.
