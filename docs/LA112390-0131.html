November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NATION;

LSU TRIMS ITS LIST TO FOUR CANDIDATES

Louisiana State has cut its list of coaching candidates to four -- Dick Sheridan of North Carolina State, Bobby Ross of Georgia Tech, Curley Hallman of Southern Mississippi and former Pittsburgh coach Mike Gottfried. LSU Coach Mike Archer leaves the job after the Tigers' season-ending game Saturday against Tulane.
