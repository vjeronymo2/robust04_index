BFN [Text] Viktor Antilov, secretary general of the Russian Communist Workers Party [title as heard], has stressed that the Russian people are standing alongside the Palestinian people in their struggle to regain their rights to self-determination, the establishment of an independent Palestinian state, and repatriation. He added that the Russian people also support peace in accordance with the resolutions of international legitimacy. During a meeting with Nayif Hawatimah, secretary general of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP], Antilov also asserted that the people of Russia, as well as its democratic and communist nationalist forces, support the Palestinian people and the Arabs, and that the Russian people will not remain silent on the expansionist Israeli plans. For his part, Hawatimah asserted that the Oslo-Cairo agreement is doomed to failure because it does not meet the minimum requirements of a peace settlement according to the resolutions of international legitimacy and also because the Israeli occupation and settlements continue to exist in Gaza and Jericho, as do the acts of bloody repression throughout the occupied territories.
