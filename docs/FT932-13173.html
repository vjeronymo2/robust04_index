930424 FT 24 APR 93 / UK Company News: Linton Pounds 8m back in the black LINTON PARK, the tea and coffee producer and importer and exporter, made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 7.66m in 1992, compared with a restated loss of Pounds 1.16m following the adoption of FRS 3 accounting standard. Exceptional items in 1991 included Pounds 5.5m written off an investment in an associate in course of disposal previously classified as extraordinary less Pounds 2.07m minorities. Operating profit this year rose from Pounds 6.43m to Pounds 9.06m on turnover of continuing operations of Pounds 129m (Pounds 124.3m). There was a Pounds 500,000 profit on fixed asset investment. Earnings were 31.8p (restated losses 17.3p). The final dividend is 11.5p for a 14p (13p) total. The ultimate holding company is Lawrie Group.
