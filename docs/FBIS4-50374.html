BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- A delegation from the Communist Party of China (CPC) headed by Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, left here for Egypt and Syria today. The delegation is on an official goodwill visit to the two countries at the invitation of the National Democratic Party of Egypt and the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party of Syria. Among those seeing the delegation off at the airport were Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee and Egyptian and Syrian diplomatic envoys in Beijing.
