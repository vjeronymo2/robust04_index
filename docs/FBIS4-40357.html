BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladas Burbulis] [Text] Vilnius June 15 TASS -- Lithuanian parliament speaker Ceslovas Jursenas swore in five new ministers at the morning session of the Lithuanian Sejm on Wednesday. Three of them will be put in charge of absolutely new governmental departments founded in accordance with the law on cabinet activities. Namely, Bronis Bradauskas will be from now on minister of environmental protection; Vladislavas Domarkas - minister of science and education (until recently he was deputy foreign minister); and Dainius Trinkunas - minister of culture (previously, he was at the head of Ministry of Culture and Public Education). In accordance with the above-mentioned law, there will be two separate respective ministries from now on. Another two newly sworn-in ministers stepped in the shoes of their predecessors. Sylviculturist Albertas Vasilyauskas will be minister of forestry and his namesake Aleksandras will be minister of economics. Almost all of the new ministers are either members or supporters of the ruling Democratic Party of Labour.
