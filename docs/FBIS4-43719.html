BFN [Unattributed report: "Safe Seats"] [Excerpt] The Free Democratic Party of Germany [FDP] is looking after its ladies. Uta Wuerfel, top candidate who failed in the European elections, is going to Brussels, even without a mandate. She will become head of the FDP liaison office with the European Union Commission and the European Parliament. This should take care of her worries that she might have to stay at home and do nothing. Ursula Seiler-Albring, state minister (parliamentary state secretary) in FDP Chairman Kinkel's Foreign Ministry, will be given a post as ambassador, probably in Vienna. [passage omitted]
