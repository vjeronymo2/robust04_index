Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, February 7 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- A letter to the great leader Generalisimo Kim Il-song was adopted on February 5 at the inaugural meeting of the Human Rights Association of Koreans in Japan. The letter says: The inauguration of the association is an epoch-making event in guiding the Korean qualified persons and researchers of the problem of rights to uphold the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il with loyal hearts and defend and glorify the socialist homeland of chuche centring on the popular masses and to defend and expand the rights and life of the compatriots in Japan with their special knowledge of law and economics and technique pooled together. The letter says: "You and the dear leader have always wisely led us Koreans in Japan to defend national dignity and rights as dignified overseas citizens of a sovereign state and shown compatriotic love and care for us through state policies. So we have won legal professions in Japan and conducted legitimate activities in special domains. "We will firmly unite the Korean qualified persons and researchers around you and the dear leader to actively contribute to the prosperity of the socialist homeland of chuche and acceleration of the independent and peaceful reunification of the country. "And we will strengthen and develop friendship with the Japanese people and the world peaceloving people who respect human rights and peace and vigorously arouse their support to our struggle for defending the rights of the Koreans in Japan." The letter expresses the resolution of the participants of the meeting to repay the great love and expectation of the great leader and the dear leader with loyalty by fulfilling their honorable tasks in a responsible manner with the inauguration of the association.
