940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / British Gas in pipeline venture A consortium consisting of British Gas, Tenneco Gas of the US and BHP of Australia has been selected to develop a pipeline linking natural gas reserves in Bolivia with markets in southern and south-eastern Brazil. The 2,000 mile pipeline, costed at Dollars 2bn (Pounds 1.2bn), is an important component of Brazil's plans to develop an integrated natural gas system. Construction is due to start in 1995, and gas to flow by 1997.
