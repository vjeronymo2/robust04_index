BFN [Text] The Right Honorable the Prime Minister Dr. Ntsu Mokhehle says the role of the democratically elected government is to achieve peace and stability, which the nation has been deprived off for many years. Speaking at a public gathering in Mohale's Hoek today, the prime minister said the opposition in Lesotho does not appreciate the peace that is currently prevailing and were trying all possible means to disrupt it. He assured those who attended the gathering that the democratically elected government is there to serve the people who elected it and the nation as a whole.
