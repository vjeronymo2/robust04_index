Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Belgrade, Mar 14 (TANJUG) -- Troops of the Fifth Corp of the Bosnian Moslem Army are responsible for the death of a French peace-keeper killed last Friday near Bihac (western Bosnia), the Bosnian Serb News Agency SRNA on Monday evening quoted well informed Serb military sources as saying. The sources said that they had obtained the information from circles close to the command of the French battalion of the U.N. Protection Force (UNPROFOR) stationed in Coralici near Cazin. SRNA recalled that the Serb side was immediately accused of being responsible for the death of the French soldier at one of the UNPROFOR observation posts on the Bihac front although the circumstances and motives for the killing remained unclarified until this very day.
