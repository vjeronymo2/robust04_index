940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Balladur set to win according to latest opinion poll Mr Jacques Delors, outgoing president of the European Commission and leading Socialist contender in the French presidential race, could beat Mr Jacques Chirac, the centre-right leader and mayor of Paris, in next year's presidential elections, but would lose to Mr Edouard Balladur, prime minister, according to the latest opinion poll. The poll, in yesterday's Journal du Dimanche newspaper, puts Mr Balladur in first place with the support of 17 per cent of the public, followed by Mr Delors with 14 per cent and Mr Chirac with 11 per cent.
