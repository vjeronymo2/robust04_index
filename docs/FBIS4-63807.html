BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow May 24 TASS -- A document relating to the division of the Black Sea Fleet between Russia and Ukraine is being prepared for signing after the current talks between the heads of government of two republics. The document will be signed by Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Ukraine's Acting Premier Yukhym Zvyahilskyy after all points are agreed between the two delegations. The joint document will contain specific terms and conditions of the division of the Black Sea Fleet's infrastructure and of the bases of the two national fleets. A press conference will be held after the signing.
