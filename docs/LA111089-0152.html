November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HAPPY DAYS: U.S. policy on emigration from East Germany is yet to be determined (A1), but Orange County's Phoenix Club already has a project in mind. . . . The club, made up largely of German descendants, has offered to sponsor five families, president Hans Klein said, adding that his 69-year-old brother told him on the phone Thursday from East Germany, "Get the bedrooms ready, we're coming." Dana Parsons
