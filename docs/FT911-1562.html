910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Blackland Oil cuts deficit to Pounds 65,000 Blackland Oil, the USM-quoted oil exploration and production company, reduced losses from Pounds 366,861 to Pounds 64,759 in 1990, on increased revenues of Pounds 492,734, against Pounds 259,419. Losses were struck after exploration expenditure written offof Pounds 45,964 (Pounds 271,669). There was no tax (Pounds 99 charge) and after extraordinary debits of Pounds 62,211 (Pounds 48,790) - the cost of admission to the USM - net losses emerged at Pounds 126,970 (Pounds 415,750). Losses per share were 0.72p (4.91p).
