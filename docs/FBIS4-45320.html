BFN [Text] The Swaziland police have confiscated their biggest haul of mandrax. The mandrax has an estimated street value of about $5 million. It was packed in plastic bags hidden in containers on a truck that had entered the country at the Lomahasha border post in the north. Sources say that the Lomahasha area has become a favorite route for smugglers of drugs and arms from Mozambique. Apparently these are destined for South Africa.
