Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] We now know the final decision of Youth and Sports Minister Boubacar Karamoko Coulibaly in the wake of his talks with the head of state yesterday. While respecting the decision of his party, the Party for Democracy and Progress, to withdraw from the government, he says that, as a patriot, he is duty bound to pursue the work that he has started with the support of the Malian youth and sports community, in particular, and the Malian people, in general. As a result, Boubacar Karamoko Coulibaly says that, out of respect for the Malian people and as a citizen, he has decided to remain in the government in order to make his modest contribution to the construction of a democratic and prosperous Malian state.
