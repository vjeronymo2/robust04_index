940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Compco Compco Holdings, the property investment and development group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 310,609 for the year to March 25, against a FRS 3-restated loss of Pounds 25,939. Net income from property fell from Pounds 2.11m to Pounds 1.68m. The single final dividend is held at 12.96p with a 3-for-1 scrip issue also proposed. Earnings per share were 9.25p (3.61p losses).
