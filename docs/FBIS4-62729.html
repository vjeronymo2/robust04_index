BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers of the Republic of Belarus has adopted a resolution "on the procedure for exporting strategically important goods." The document says that the export by Belarusian entities engaged in economic activity of strategically important goods that appear on the lists of the Russian Federation can be done if the entities engaged in economic activity register at the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations of the Russian Federation and receive permission from the Belarusian Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations.
