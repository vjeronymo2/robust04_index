BFN [Text] Beijing, may 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with Jose Naranjo Morales, Cuban minister without portfolio, here today. Qian said that the current China tour, the third one made by Naranjo, will further promote the friendship and co-operation between China and Cuba. Naranjo spoke highly of China's reform and opening-up policy and great progress it has made in economic construction. Qian asked Naranjo to convey greetings from the Chinese leaders to their Cuban counterparts. The Cuban minister and his party are here as guests of the Chinese Foreign Ministry.
