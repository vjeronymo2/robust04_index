940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / International Company News: Alcan considering sale of building products divisions Alcan Aluminium plans to sell its two North American building products divisions, with annual revenues of USDollars 425m, to Genstar Capital, an industrial group whose subsidiaries include Wolverine Tube, a leading US producer of copper tubing. The Alcan divisions employ 1,800 in 18 plants and distribution centres, mainly in the US. They make aluminium, vinyl and steel building products.
