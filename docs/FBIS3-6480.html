Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Comrade Kim Chong-il Sends Tractors and Trucks to Counties" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 18 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent 500 tractors and 250 trucks to rural communities of Chongdan, Yonan and Paechon Counties, South Hwanghae Province. These counties are situated in the Yonbaek plain on the west coast, one of the nation's granaries. Comrade Kim Chong-il gave on-the-spot guidance to the plain several times to clearly indicate the direction and ways of improving the people's living standard and increasing agricultural production, and this time sent many tractors and lorries to the rural communities there so as to follow up the achievements made in the rural technical revolution. The tractors and lorries sent by Comrade Kim Chong-il were conveyed to the counties with due ceremonies on March 17. The speakers there said that the Yonbaek plain under the wise guidance and deep loving care of our party has turned into a modern socialist countryside good to live and work in and a place holding a big share in the nation's production of grain. They vowed to harvest more than ten tons of rice or maize from each hectare this year by doing farming including sowing and rice transplanting as demanded by the chuche method of farming. Letters of pledge to Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the ceremonies.
