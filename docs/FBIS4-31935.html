BFN [Text] Gabor Nahlik, deputy chairman of Hungarian Television, following yesterday's six-party discussions about the future of Hungarian radio and television, today issued the following statement: In response to the 15 June announcement by Prime Minister Peter Boross, I am glad that the appointment and relief of the leaders of Hungarian Television will be the result of a six-party agreement. I consider it natural that the next leader of Hungarian Television should take over after a thorough economic and legal examination. I am at the disposal of any of the examining organizations. Budapest, 16 June 1994 Signed: Dr. Gabor Nahlik, deputy chairman of Hungarian Television
