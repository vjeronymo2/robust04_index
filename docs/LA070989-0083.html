GUIDE LISTS TREES IN PACIFIC PALISADES

Walkers or bicyclists wanting to know more about the trees they see in Pacific Palisades, a guide, "Trees of Pacific Palisades," by Grace L. Heintz is available. A list of street trees is accompanied by a map.

A section of the book describes growth rates of trees, mature size and watering needs. the 272-page book can be ordered for $5 from Palisades Beautiful, Box 1072, Pacific Palisades, Calif. 90272.
