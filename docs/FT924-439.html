921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / EC clears agreement between Ford and VW The European Commission yesterday cleared an agreement between Ford and Volkswagen to produce minivans in Setubal, Portugal, Reuter reports from Brussels. The only condition set by the Commission for the project, which is due to receive Ecu547m (Pounds 442.5m) in state and EC aid, was that Ford and Volkswagen must sell different versions of the multi-purpose vehicle and must use separate sales networks.
