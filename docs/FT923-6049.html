920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Criticism curbed Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk yesterday warned foreigners that criticism of the Ukrainian president or government would be punished by expulsion, writes Chrystia Freeland from Kiev. Speaking to Ukrainian Canadian emigres, Mr Kravchuk said he had told the ministry of foreign affairs to prepare an order according to which foreigners who attacked the government would be declared persona non grata and expelled.
