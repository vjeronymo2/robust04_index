941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / McLaren engines deal likely Mercedes-Benz, the German luxury carmaker, is set to become the official engine supplier for McLaren grand prix racing cars next year. Subject to final detailed negotiations the deal will be announced in Stuttgart tomorrow. The engines are designed and built in the UK by Ilmor Engineering of Northampton, in which Mercedes has a 25 per cent stake. Peugeot group, McLaren's current engine supplier, has agreed to supply Jordan, another British team, next season.
