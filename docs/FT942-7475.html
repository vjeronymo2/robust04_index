940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Warnford Warnford Investments reported a 9 per cent contraction in profits before tax -from Pounds 7.66m to Pounds 6.95m - during the 12 months to December 25. Turnover at the property investment group dipped to Pounds 11.4m (Pounds 12.1m). After tax and minorities, earnings per share emerged at 12.1p (13.65p). A proposed final dividend of 5p brings the total for the year to 7.75p (7.5p).
