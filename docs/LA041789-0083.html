April 17, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA

2 Men Shot: Two young men were shot in a drive-by incident about 9:20 p.m. Sunday as they walked near a fast-food restaurant at Edinger Avenue and Bristol Street, police said. The men, who were not immediately identified, were taken for treatment to Coastal Communities Hospital in Santa Ana. Hospital authorities said one of the youths was in guarded condition and the other was in stable condition.
