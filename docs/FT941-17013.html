940106 FT 06 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 510 tonnes for the week ended 31 December, against nil in the previous week. Renewed purchasing in many specialist styles attracted much attention. American, Central Asian and Greek qualities as well as Israeli, Mali and Turkish growths moved off steadily. JUTE December/January c and f Dundee: BTC Dollars 475, BWC Dollars 500, BTD Dollars 450, BWD Dollars 465. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 455, BWC Dollars 455, BTD Dollars 425, BWD Dollars 425.
