Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has categorically denied that its borders with Afghanistan have been closed and emphasized that recently 2,000 people who possessed a valid visa were allowed to cross into Pakistan. The foreign secretary told reporters in Islamabad today that only Kabul has been affected by the fighting and 28 other provinces of Afghanistan are, by and large, peaceful. He said a team from the Islamic Conference Organization is currently in Islamabad and a UN mission is likely to arrive in the capital soon. Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Iran, and the Central Asian states fully agree on the view that fighting in Afghanistan should be brought to an end.
