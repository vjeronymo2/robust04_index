BFN [Text] President 'Arafat, head of the State of Palestine and chairman of the PLO's Executive Committee, today received Yevgeniy Primakov, director of foreign intelligence of the Russian Federation, and the delegation accompanying him. A full review was made of: developments in the Palestinian arena, particularly in the occupied territories; the results thus far of the implementation of the agreement on Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho as a means of strengthening the pillars of the Palestinian national authority; the need for the withdrawal of Israeli troops from the self-rule territories; as well as the requirements for consolidating the structures of the national authority. President 'Arafat expressed gratitude for Russia's firm stance in its capacity as one of the cosponsors of the Middle East peace process, assessed highly the close relations between Russia and Palestine, and stressed the need for strengthening and developing them. During the meeting, Primakov reaffirmed his country's firm stance in supporting the peace process and in firmly establishing the basis and structures of the Palestinian National Authority.
