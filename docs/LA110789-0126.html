November 7, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: PERCEPTRONICS SAYS DEAL COULD YIELD $16.5 MILLION

Perceptronics in Woodland Hills said it could realize about $16.5 million in sales from its participation in a team of companies that will make tactical tank training systems for the West German army. The training systems are computer simulators that help train the soldiers who operate tanks. Perceptronics makes a variety of such simulators for the U. S. military.
