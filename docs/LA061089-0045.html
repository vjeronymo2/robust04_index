June 10, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

LES MORMONS UNDER FIRE

Shouldn't your headline have been "Les Mormons Attack Prejudice With Prejudice"?

Because of the Mormon Church's legitimate rejection of homosexual activity, these "artists" condemn it.

What happened to freedom of religion, or is that only for the dissidents while the faithful are forced to abandon what they believe? Is the "intolerance" Les Mormons derogate any different from the intolerance they practice?

JOHN ARNERICH

Los Angeles
