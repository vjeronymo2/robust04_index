BFN [Text] The government has extended the state of emergency until 19 July in the Paruro, Acomayo, Chumbivilcas, Canas, and Canchis Provinces, Cusco Department. Houses may be searched without a warrant and restrictions are imposed on peoples' right to move freely from one place to another and to assemble peacefully. Arrests cannot be made without a warrant issued by a court or by the police unless a criminal is caught red-handed. The supreme decree, published in EL PERUANO, states that the state of exception seeks to expedite antiterrorist operations in that region.
