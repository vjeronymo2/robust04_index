930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Increased support for the 3i club - In a Nutshell The 3i Communication Club, established to represent the views of dissatisfied companies in the investment portfolio of Britain's largest venture capital group, has signed up over 250 members or about 10 per cent of smaller businesses with equity backing from 3i. This level of membership indicates there is enough interest for the club to continue its activities.
