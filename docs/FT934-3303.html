931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Airtours acquires Tradewinds Airtours, the UK's second largest holiday tour operator after Thomson, has acquired one of the UK's leading long-haul brand names. Tradewinds has been acquired from International Travel Connections, a privately-owned company based in Chester, for Pounds 250,000 cash. Mr David Crossland, chairman of Airtours, said the acquisition provided a new opportunity to offer exotic holidays to long-haul destinations. The new product would appeal to a segment of the market not presently covered by Airtours.
