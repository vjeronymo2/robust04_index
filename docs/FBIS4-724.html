BFN [Statement issued by the RPA; place and date not given] [Text] 1. The Rwandese Patriotic Army [RPA] would like to remind the international community on the communique signed on 12th April 1994 at 0700 hours local time between the Rwandese Patriotic Front [RPF] on one hand and UNAMIR [UN Assistance Mission in Rwanda], on the other, that the evacuation of foreign nationals should be completed on 14th April 1994 at 0700 hours local time. 2. The evacuation of foreign troops should be completed on 14th April 1994 at 1900 hours local time, after the evacuation of their nationals. 3. Any aircraft or troops entering the territory of Rwanda after midnight local time, 14th April 1994, stand the risk of being considered hostile and as such vulnerable. The RPF will not be held responsible for the consequences. Signed, (Andrew Ruygemba), lieutenant colonel, secretary, RPA high command.
