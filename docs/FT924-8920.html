921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / Strauss-Kahn and 1.7 tons of plutonium Dominique Strauss-Kahn, French minister for industry and foreign trade, gestures in front of the Akatsuki Maru as 1.7 tons of plutonium is loaded. The freighter sailed from Cherbourg for Japan over the weekend after violent clashes between security forces and environmentalists protesting at the lethal cargo. Mr Strauss-Kahn said France would not prevent Greenpeace from tracking the ship, but Greenpeace later said one of the Japanese freighter's escort ships had collided with it in the Channel
