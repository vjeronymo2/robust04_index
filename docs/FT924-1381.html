921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Technology: Bingo lingo - Worth Watching PERHAPS you'd like to give your employers language lessons for Christmas. Language teacher Bruce Nicol has developed a way of teaching English, French or German in a high-tech method based on the game of bingo. BI-LINGO teaches grammar and a minimum of 3,000 words through repetition and exploiting the individual's desire to win. Nicol adapts the game to suit a company's individual needs. Nicol: UK, 0424 431376.
