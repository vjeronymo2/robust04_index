931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / International Company News: Unidare ahead at IPounds 4.49m Unidare, the Dublin-based industrial group, achieved pre-tax profits of IPounds 4.49m (Pounds 4.3m) for the nine months to September 30 against IPounds 3.35m. For the year to December 1992 the company, which has changed its year end, reported profits of IPounds 5.43m. Turnover increased to IPounds 105m (IPounds 85.3m), with acquisitions contributing IPounds 24.9m. Following the acquisition of Nasco, the group has 18 depots across the US with 4,000 distributor customers. Earnings per share came through at 19.45p and a final dividend of 5.7p is proposed, making 10.1p for the period.
