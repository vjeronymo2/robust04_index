930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Venture funds find elusive investors - In a Nutshell Raising finance in the present economic climate is a task most venture capitalists would not wish upon their worst enemy. But against the trend a handful of funds have succeeded in finding investors. Phoenix Fund Managers this month completed the raising of its first fund, of just over Pounds 50m, while European Acquisition Capital, part of the Swedish Enskilda banking group, has raised a Pounds 65m private equity fund to back buy-outs and buy-ins. This followed a Pounds 14m fund-raising by Trinity Capital Partners by the first closing of a European Environmental Fund on behalf of the US investment bank Alex Brown & Sons. Meanwhile, Alta Berkeley Associates raised Dollars 21m towards a planned Dollars 50m for its fourth fund, Alta Berkeley III.
