BFN [Text] Tehran, April 26, IRNA -- University staff and students in Nowshahr (Mazandaran) and Khorramabad (Lorestan) today staged rallies in support of the oppressed Bosnian Muslims and denounced the crimes of the Serb aggressors. The demonstrators called for lifting of the United Nations arms embargo against the aggressed Muslim republic and announced their readiness to defend the hardpressed Bonsians. In related news form Kashan, the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) and Basij (volunteer) forces issued a statement today underlining their readiness to march to the help of Gorazde Muslims.
