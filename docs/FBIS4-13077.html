BFN [Text] Esteemed Pir Sayyed Ahmad Gaylani, leader of the Mahaz-e Meli-ye Eslami [National Islamic Front], asks for a cease-fire, first of all, for solving the country's current problems. After meeting the UN secretary general's special envoy in Peshawar, Pir Gaylani in his interview with a VOA reporter said: After the cease-fire a Loya Jerga [great council] or an urgent invited council should be set up. The council should have the power to appoint the future government and its leader, in order to take the place of the present government after the end of term of esteemed professor Borhanoddin Rabbani's leadership. Pir Sayyed Ahmad Gaylani further said: The above Shura council is to start work under the supervision of the United Nations and Conference of the Islamic Countries and is to take urgent action for the restoration of security. He added: The future government can devise the constitution through the Loya Jerga, in which the people's genuine representatives can take part. Thus, the ground will be prepared for a free election.
