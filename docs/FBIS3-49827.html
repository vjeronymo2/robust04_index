Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Akashi in Skopje"] [Text] The chief of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] and Special Representative of the UN Secretary General Yasushi Akashi arrived on a one day visit to the Republic of Macedonia. The fist visit of this high functionary is within the frame of the tour of the republics of the former Yugoslavia where UNPROFOR forces are stationed. President Gligorov had a meeting with Akashi. He also met with the Minister for Defense Vlado Popovski and the Minister for foreign relations, Stevo Crvenkovski. Akashi also visited the area of operations of UNPROFOR in Macedonia. Otherwise those days the mandate of the current commander of UNPROFOR, Brigadier-General Finn Saemark Thomsen, came to an end. The new commander, the Norwegian Trygve Tellefsch, will arrive in Macedonia on Sunday.
