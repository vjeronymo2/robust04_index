PRESIDENT'S COUNCIL ON SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT Office of Environmental Policy Second Quarterly Meeting of the President's Council on Sustainable Development (PCSD)

SUMMARY: The President's Council on Sustainable Development is a partnership of industry, labor, government and environmental organizations, not-for-profit groups and civil rights organizations. The Council will convene its second quarterly meeting to discuss a vision of sustainable development in the U.S. and establish short- and long-term priorities for developing recommendations to the President.
