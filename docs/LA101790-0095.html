October 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Kathy Kerr, wife of right wing Tim Kerr of the Philadelphia Flyers and former chairwoman of a club charity drive, died of complications while giving birth.

Mrs. Kerr, 30, died in Philadelphia, where she gave birth Oct. 6 to a girl, Kimberly, who survived after being delivered by Cesarean section. Mrs. Kerr received treatment for a pelvic infection but developed a cardiopulmonary complication, according to Lisa Chapman, a hospital spokeswoman.
