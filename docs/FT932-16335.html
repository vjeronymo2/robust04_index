930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Roskel After Pounds 2.4m for exceptional charges Roskel, an installer and distributor of suspended ceiling systems, ran up a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.2m for 1992, compared with a profit of Pounds 1.57m. Losses came to 5.46p (earnings 7.22p). The final dividend is 3p for an unchanged total of 4.3p. Turnover in 1992 rose from Pounds 43.8m to Pounds 47.5m. Again bad debts and related costs took their toll, amounting to Pounds 859,000 (Pounds 930,000).
