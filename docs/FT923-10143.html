920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Net assets up 9.6% at European Assets European Assets Trust lifted net asset value by 9.6 per cent, to Fl 8.24, at June 30. The rise in sterling terms was lower, at 7.3 per cent, reflecting sterling's strength against the guilder. Net income for the six months amounted to Fl 3.49m, against Fl 2.87m. The interim dividend is maintained at Fl 0.08, payable from earnings of Fl 0.14 (Fl 0.11) per share.
