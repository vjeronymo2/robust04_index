BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, seven Indian troops were killed and several seriously injured in a bloody clash with freedom fighters near Bijbiara on the Srinagar-Jammu Highway. A large contingent of Indian troops and paramilitary forces immediately left for Bijbiara to deal with any situation. A report says a youth was tortured to death in Pampur near Srinagar. Another youth under military custody was also martyred. Meanwhile, in retaliatory action, freedom fighters attacked a number of paramilitary troops and injured them. According to Kashmir Media Service, 32 innocent youths were arrested today and taken to interrogation centers, where torture by the Indian Army is common.
