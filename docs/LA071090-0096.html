July 10, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

New Knee for Pearl Bailey:: Pearl Bailey will undergo surgery Wednesday at Pennsylvania Hospital in Philadelphia to replace her left knee. Bailey, 72, entered the hospital Monday, and is expected to stay for about two weeks, hospital officials said. The entertainer suffers from degenerative arthritis, in which the cartilage covering the ends of bones and joints wears away, causing the bones to rub together painfully, officials said. GREG BRAXTON
