July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Harvard University reached two finals at the Henley Royal Regatta, at Henley-On-Thames, England.

The senior heavyweight crew reached the final of the Ladies Plate, the second-most important event for eights, for the second successive year, defeating the University of Wisconsin by two boat lengths in rough water.

Harvard will face London University in the final today.

The Crimson also reached the final of the Thames Cup, the third-most prestigious race for eights.
