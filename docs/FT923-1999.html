920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / International Company News: NCM drops to Fl 23.4m midway NCM, the Dutch credit insurer which last year acquired the short-term businesses of the British Export Credit Guarantee Department, said first-half 1992 net profit fell to Fl 23.4m (Dollars 14m) from Fl 29.7m because of increased losses on reinsurance, Reuter reports from Amsterdam. Losses on reinsurance of policies sold by other credit insurers rose to Fl 18.9m from Fl 3.6m.
