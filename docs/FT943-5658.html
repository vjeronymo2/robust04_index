940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / International Company News: Fyffes Fyffes, the Irish fresh fruit importing and distribution group, has acquired a 33 per cent stake in Sofiprim, a French fresh produce company. The group is understood to have paid between IPounds 5m and IPounds 6m (Pounds 5.94m) for the stake, its fifth purchase in mainland Europe in a 12 month IPounds 45m spending spree.
