Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, February 22, RIA -- An agreement banning the dumping of radioactive and industrial waste in the seas came into force on February 21, 1994. It was signed by 70 countries. Russia has, however, officially announced it is not going to join this agreement. Sada Aksartova, a spokeswoman for the international ecological organisation Greenpeace, in commenting on the Russian Federation's stand, told a RIA correspondent it was a great mistake on the part of the Russian Government, and will evoke a highly negative response from the other countries. Among the major powers, according to Aksartova, only Australia, in addition to Russia, has refused to accede to the agreement, "taking time-out" for two years.
