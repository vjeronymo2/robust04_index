Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jordanian and Palestinian sides resumed their meetings at the Foreign Ministry today. These meetings are a continuation of the two working sessions held yesterday. Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, the deputy prime minister and minister of higher education, headed the Jordanian side, while Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, headed the Palestinian side. The second session was held yesterday during which the two sides discussed bilateral relations, issues dealing with the peace process, and Jordanian-Palestinian coordination on these issues.
