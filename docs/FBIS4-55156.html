BFN [Text] Tirana, May 10 (ATA) -- Albanian President Berisha, who is on an official visit to Bucharest, told the reporters there today that "in order to continue the dialogue for solving problems and improving relations with Greece, we are going to propose another meeting of the foreign ministers of the two countries in Tirana." Mr. Berisha reiterated that "we consider positive the Zurich meeting because the two ministers expressed their clear will to improve relations," adding "we hope that this will and predisposition will be carried ahead and yield their own fruits."
