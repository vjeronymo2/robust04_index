BFN [Text] Riga, Apr 08, BNS -- Latvia's Health Minister Normunds Zemvaldis left for Lithuania Friday [8 April] to participate in the Latvian Medicine Day and presentation of the Minotaur international corporation and the Latvian-U.S. JV Amerilat firm there. Amerilat wholesales medical equipment and apparatuses while Minotaur organizes private practice.
