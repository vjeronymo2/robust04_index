940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, good general demand. Quality Assams met keen competition selling between 175 and 275 pence. Coloury mediums were steady but lesser mediums were irregular. Brightest and good medium East Africans opened on a firm note but eased as the sale progressed. Mediums sold readily at fully firm rates. Brighter ceylons were a strong feature and often advanced, mediums remained firm. Fairly good demand. Brighter teas sold well at last levels but others moved lower. Quotations: best available 200p/kg., good 142p/kg., good medium 132p/kg., medium 120p/kg., low medium 85p/kg., nom. The highest price realised this week was 275p for an Assam.
