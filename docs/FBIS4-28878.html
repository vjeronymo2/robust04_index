BFN ["`actions Against North Korea Could Bring the Korean Peninsula Down the Road to Conflict': Philippine President" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 10 (KCNA) -- Philippine President Fidel Ramos at his weekly press conference on June 8 called for the avoidance of actions against North Korea that might provoke a confrontation, which he said in turn could bring the region down the road to conflict, according to a XINHUA report. The president reiterated the Philippine policy on North Korea's "nuclear issue" that has been to urge patient consultation and creative diplomacy in the search for a solution. He said "the Philippines hope that a peaceful solution could still be found to resolve the issue."
