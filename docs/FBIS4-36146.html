BFN [Text] A separate Kashmir Directorate has been set up in the Foreign Office to highlight the Kashmir issue worldwide. A spokesman of the Pakistan People's Party [PPP] said in Islamabad that the step has been taken on the prime minister's directive. He said the prime minister would like to expose the brutal atrocities carried out by Indian forces in occupied Kashmir, adding that she attaches significant importance to the Kashmir issue whenever she pays a visit abroad. The PPP spokesman said the government after careful consideration has formulated a strategy to secure international support for its just stand on Kashmir. He expressed the hope that its efforts will get a considerable boost as a result of Ms. Bhutto's upcoming visits to the United States and Russia.
