930427 FT 27 APR 93 / Israeli troops kill Palestinian boy Israeli troops shot dead a 12-year-old Palestinian boy during clashes in the occupied Gaza Strip yesterday, Palestinians said, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. They said the victim, Ahmed Hamdan, was shot in Khan Yunis refugee camp and 17 Palestinians were also wounded in clashes with the Israeli army in the strip. A Palestinian mother of six, Najah Abu-Dalal, 30, died in hospital earlier after being shot in the head inside her house in the strip's Nusseirat refugee camp during clashes last Wednesday, Palestinians said.
