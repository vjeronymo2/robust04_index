BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, received today brother Badr Hammam, assistant to the foreign minister of fraternal Egypt, who conveyed a message to the brother president from his brother Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. The message deals with developments in our country, as well as issues of interest to the brotherly relations between the two countries. The message stresses Egypt's firm position and support for Yemeni unity, cease-fire efforts, and dialogue within the framework of unity as the best way to settle the current differences. It also asserts that Egypt is with unity, with the people, and with stability in the Republic of Yemen. The Presidential Council chairman voiced his satisfaction with and great appreciation for the position of the brothers in Egypt, headed by His Excellency President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. He referred to the special Yemeni-Egyptian relations and the profound and strong ties between the two fraternal countries. He also said that Egypt's support for the Yemeni people and their unity is part of its honorable pan-Arab role [words indistinct] in support of the eternal Yemeni revolution.
