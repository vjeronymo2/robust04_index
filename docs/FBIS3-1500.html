Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] There was gunfire in Malange today. A military source said this happened when government troops tried to break through the defensive lines of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] in the region. The source also said that in the face of this provocation, the UNITA Armed Forces reacted with intense gunfire, forcing the enemy's immediate retreat. During the clashes, many government soldiers lost their lives. The situation is presently calm.
