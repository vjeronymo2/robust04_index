BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 24 May (WAKH) -- United Arab Emirates [UAE] President His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan has said he hopes that Yemen will overcome the critical circumstances it is currently experiencing. The Emirates News Agency today said that during a telephone call received from President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the UAE president stressed that the UAE is eager to exert sincere efforts to contain the situation and [word indistinct] good offices to stop the bloodshed in Yemen. His highness called on President 'Ali Salim al-Bid to place the interest of Yemen and its people at the top of the priorities [words indistinct] support him without any hesitation and wisdom to stop this catatstrophe. For his part, President 'Ali Salim al-Bid expressed appreciation for the UAE president's stand and concern for the security and stability of Yemen. President al-Bid briefed the UAE president on the latest developments in the Yemeni arena.
