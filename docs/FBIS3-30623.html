Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The proceedings of the extraordinary congress of the National Liberal Party [PNL] ended this morning. It had been convened by the initiative committee for the organization of the congress. During the two-day proceedings, the congress established the new by-laws, elected the new party president and leadership members, and discussed and endorsed the Program and Political Declaration of the National Liberal Party. The Political Declaration presented by Petre Dragomir declares, quote: We are now able to address the country and we want to participate, through our work and the values that liberalism promotes in society, in the development and modernization of our society, unquote. At the end of the congress proceedings, Radu Campeanu, elected by the congress as president of the PNL, said that following this congress, the members of the leadership bodies who recognize Mircea Ionescu-Quintus as president of the party became mere party members. We do not exclude anyone, we do not wage war with anyone, but we believe that we have to conduct talks, to invite everyone to participate in a dialogue, Radu Campeanu concluded. We recall that at the Brasov meeting, the Central Standing Bureau of the PNL termed the congress in Bucharest, quote: A meeting of Mr. Campeanu's supporters.
