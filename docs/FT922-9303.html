920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Ventilation boost as Titon jumps 51% Increased sales of its core ventilation products helped Titon Holdings report a 51 per cent increase in interim profits. which also makes window fittings and accessories, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 623,000 to Pounds 942,000 for the six months to March 31. Turnover expanded 19 per cent to Pounds 5.38m (Pounds 4.51m). The interim dividend goes up to 1.13p (1.03p), payable from earnings of 5.82p (3.73p).
