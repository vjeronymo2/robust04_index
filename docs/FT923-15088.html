920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Court action on Battersea project TORY-CONTROLLED Wandsworth council in London is taking action in the High Court over an alleged failure by Battersea Leisure, owned by Mr John Broome, to pay Pounds 175,000 outstanding for building control and inspection work carried out by council staff on the disused Battersea power station. Mr Broome plans to turn the building into a leisure park.
