930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: F&C Pacific Foreign & Colonial Pacific Investment Trust reported a net asset value, assuming exercise of warrants, of 305.9p per share as at July 31, up from 237.8p at end-January and 181.5p a year earlier. Attributable revenue for the six months jumped from Pounds 544,000 to Pounds 2.07m for earnings of 1.95p (0.5p) per share. The interim dividend goes up from 0.75p to 0.9p.
