BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Army shelled Armenia's borders on the evening of 20 April and tonight. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reports that the defensive positions around Berkavan and [place name indistinct] in Noyemberyanskiy Rayon, and Chinari and Movses villages in Tavushskiy Rayon came under shelling until midnight from various types of weapons. Our border guards were forced to retaliate. Disregarding the agreement on a cease-fire, the armed formations in Nakhichevan shelled the border positions near Brnakot and Shaghat in Sisianskiy Rayon with an armored vehicle, grenade launchers, and howitzers between 2000 and 2100 on the same day. The Armenian border guards did not retaliate. These same villages were again shelled from Nakhichevan at noon on 21 April. The enemy used grenade launchers. There are no casualties. We have not received any more information on the border situation today.
