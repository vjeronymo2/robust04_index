BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1900 GMT on 5 April reports that "four Arabs were shot and wounded tonight by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] in the village of Bayt Immar near Hebron. One of the casualties sustained serious injuries and was transferred to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that there were many stone-throwing incidents this evening against Israeli cars making their way to Hebron. The IDF beefed up its presence in those areas, but most of the vehicles were stoned and damaged. "A resident of the village of Luban al-Sharqiyah in Samaria was shot by soldiers in a stone-throwing incident." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0800 GMT on 5 April reports that "Hayim Danino, a Jerusalem truck driver, was sentenced to four years in prison for fatally shooting an Arab resident of Ra's al-'Amud after being stoned. The Jerusalem District Court also ordered Danino to pay 30,000 shekels in damages to the family of the victim, 68-year-old Jum'a 'Abd-al-'Aziz Mishaq."
