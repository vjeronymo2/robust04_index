BFN [Report by ITIM] [Text] Seventy Fatah Hawks terrorists were recruited to the Palestinian security forces in the Gaza Strip yesterday. Of the 70 -- who constitute the total number of Fatah's terrorists and fugitives -- some 30 will be incorporated into the Palestinian police force; approximately 20 will join the security service under Muhammad Dahlan's command, while the rest will join the military force, or will renounce this privilege and become civilians. A senior Israeli source said the Israeli security forces were unaware of this agreement.
