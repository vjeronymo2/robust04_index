BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 10 (XINHUA) -- The three men accused of the murder of 32 people in the March 31 Qiandao Lake tragedy confessed their guilt in a court investigation conducted here today. The trial of the three accused Wu Lihong, Hu Zhihan and Yu Aijun opened here today in public in the Intermediate People's Court of Hangzhou city in east China's Zhejiang Province. In the court investigation, the three accused all confessed that they committed the robbery and intentional homicide crime that they are accused of in the indictment. At the court, judges interrogated the accused in detail and witnesses gave evidence. The court argument will be conducted tomorrow according to the trial procedure. The intermediate court also announced this afternoon that the trial of Wu Liming, who is accused of harboring robbed goods, will open in public on June 13.
