941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / International Company News: Lippo Land dips Lippo Land Development, the property development arm of Indonesia's Lippo Group, said net income in the 12 months to June 30 1994 fell to Rp21.5bn (Dollars 9m) from Rp28.2bn a year earlier, writes Manuela Saragosa in Jakarta. Revenues for the year totalled Rp44.1bn compared with Rp41.5bn in fiscal 1993. Sales of apartment units contributed Rp53.4bn to total revenues while rentals of office space increased 7 per cent to Rp9.8bn from Rp9.2 a year earlier. Operating income fell 35 per cent to Rp16.8bn.
