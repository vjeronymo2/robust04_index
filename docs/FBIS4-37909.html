BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jun (MENA)--Counselor 'Abd-al-Sami' Sharaf-al-Din, attorney general at the State Security Prosecution Office, has decided to renew for 15 more days the remand of Ahmad Nasir, former member of the Lawyers Association Council, and lawyers Jamal 'Abd- al-'Aziz, Muhammad 'Abd-al-Mun'im, Ahmad 'Abbas Hanafi, and Sayyid Fathi Ibrahim to complete the investigations currently under way by the prosecutors. The lawyers have been arrested by security authorities and referred to the State Security Prosecution Office on charges of distributing leaflets inciting demonstrations, assembly, and disturbance of public security.
