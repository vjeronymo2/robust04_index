March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

ALYGATOR IS WIRE-TO-WIRE WINNER

Alygator took the lead at the start and was never threatened as she romped to a three-length victory in Tuesday's $22,000 allowance feature at Oaklawn Park.

The winner went the six furlongs in 1:11 under Pat Day and returned $3.20, $2.80 and $2.20 as the favorite in the field of six fillies and mares. Color Me Precious was second with Marie's Whirl third.
