BFN [Unattributed article: "Japan/Italy: Berlusconi Meets Hata"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, May 3 -- Italy's premier-designate Silvio Berlusconi had a half-hour informal meeting this evening with Japan's new premier Tsutomu Hata, at the request of the latter, "to say hello and get to know each other," Berlusconi's spokesman said. Japanese sources explained their premier's wish by recalling the importance he places on the July Naples G-7 summit -- the reason why he chose Italy to start a European tour which will take him to Paris (tomorrow), Bonn and Brussels. Separately, the premier-designate had talks with Italy's Ambassador to the United States Boris Banchieri on U.S. President Bill Clinton's June 2-3 visit to Italy.
