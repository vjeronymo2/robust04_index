BFN [Text] Baku (April 5) TURAN--"The results and decision of the session of Nakhichevan Supreme Majlis, held on 1 April are illegal"-- stated the deputies from democratic bloc of Nakhichevan Supreme Majlis. In a statement signed by 27 deputies is said, that "under the pressure of Chairman of Supreme Majlis of Azerbaijan, first Deputy Chairman of Nakhichevan Supreme Majlis R. Gasanov and special subdivisions of police the election of Chairman was held without alternative candidate, by open voting, that is violation of regulations". "As soon as 27 deputies from democratic bloc left the session in protest on it the meeting of session was held without quorum"--is emphasized in a statement.
