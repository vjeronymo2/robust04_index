CSO [Unattributed report: "Instead of Zone of Conflict--Zone of Trust"] [Text] In keeping with a prior agreement, a meeting of Armenian and Nakhichevan delegations, headed by David Shakhnazaryan, the Republic of Armenia president's special envoy and chief of the Republic of Armenia state directorate for national security, and the chairman of the Nakhichevan Council, Namik Gasanov, met on 14 June in the border area of Eraskhevan. Participating in the meeting were the Republic of Armenia chief border commissioner Gegam Arutyunyan and representatives of military units and executive authority of Ararat and Sadarak Rayons. As we were told at the press center of the Republic of Armenia Ministry of Defense, the Nakhichevan side once again admitted that its military units were at fault in the incident that took place on 27 May on the border of Ereskh. With the purpose of averting such border incidents, the Armenian delegation proposed to turn the border zone between Armenia and Nakhichevan into a zone of trust, and as the first practical step in this direction to set up border trade points here. The Nakhichevan side promised to present this proposal to the Azerbaijani leadership for consideration.
