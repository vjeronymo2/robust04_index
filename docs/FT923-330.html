920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Helping hand on green issues - In A Nutshell The UK government plans to launch a three-year programme to help small and medium-sized companies improve their environmental performance. The programme, details of which have yet to be worked out, is intended to be jointly funded by the government and business. There are plans for a linked series of national, local and sectoral initiatives to provide chief executives with practical guidance on environmental issues.
