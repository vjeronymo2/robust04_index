BFN [Text] A ceremony was held on the evening of 11 June at the Government Guest House to sign a number of cooperation agreements between Vietnam and Cuba. Minister Nguyen Cong Tan, chairman of the Vietnamese section of the Vietnam-Cuba Joint Commission for Economic, Scientific, and Technical Cooperation and head of our government economic delegation, and Minister Arturo Guzman, chairman of the Cuban section of the Cuba-Vietnam Joint Commission for Economic, Scientific, and Technical Cooperation and head of the Cuban Government economic delegation, signed the protocol on the 14th session of the above-mentioned joint governmental commission.
