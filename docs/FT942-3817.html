940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Company News This Week: Severn Trent, Thames and Southern Water - Other Companies Three water companies will be announcing their final results next week with most analysts expecting a reinforcement of the trends seen so far: steady progress in the core utility business and further news on cost-cutting. Severn Trent is pegged to report profits of between Pounds 280m and Pounds 285m, and an 8.5 per cent increase in the payout to 22.9p. Thames is estimated to come in with a 2 per cent decline in profits to Pounds 245m, and a rise in the dividend of just over 8 per cent to 22.7p. Southern Water is forecast to return profits more than 8 per cent ahead to about Pounds 129m, with an 8.9 per cent dividend rise to 23.2p.
