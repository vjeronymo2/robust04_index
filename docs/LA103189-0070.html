October 31, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

California Commercial Bankshares: The parent company of Santa Ana-based National Bank of Southern California, reported net income of $665,667 for the third quarter, a 25% increase from the $531,934 posted for the like period last year. For the nine-month period, net income totaled $1.7 million, 35% higher than the $1.3 million recorded a year earlier. Total assets rose 44%, to $270.3 million from $187.1 million.
