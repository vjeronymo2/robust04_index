910415 FT 15 APR 91 / Construction Contracts: Northamptonshire hall to become conference centre PORTAL CONSTRUCTION, developer and builder of Higham Ferrers, Northamptonshire, has been awarded design and construct contracts totalling Pounds 3.7m. At Chapel Brampton, Northamptonshire, Sedgebrook Hall, built in 1861, is being refurbished and converted into a conference centre for Hayley Enterprises. The Pounds 2.3m contract includes new buildings for 62 bedrooms with en suite facilities, a main conference hall, restaurant, kitchens and a recreation and fitness centre with a swimming pool. E. Woodley & Sons is developing the Higham Ferrers Business Park on the old railway goods yard and has awarded the company the Pounds 300,000 Phase I contract to build roads and sewers. In Corby, Portal has a Pounds 700,000 contract to build a 23,000 sq ft distribution centre for T-Shirt Connection and is also to construct a Pounds 400,000 office and production area extension for Certech International.
