Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] No significant change took place at the front lines during the past two days. The confrontation has been mainly in the form of positional fighting. The disputing sides have refrained from carrying out intensive combat operations. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Headquarters has disclosed that heavy artillery, Grad rocket launchers, and mortars in the eastern part of Agdamskiy Rayon pounded Armenian positions around Salakhly, Chengerli, Novruzlu, and Marzili from 0700 to 0800 on 24 February. The NKR artillery units were forced to open fire. Two enemy fire emplacements were destroyed. Meanwhile, the NKR intelligence units have established that Azerbaijan is massing reinforcements and military equipment in various parts of the eastern front line. The Azerbaijani military units have fired on the Armenian defense positions in Mardakertskiy Rayon. However, the situation at other parts of the confrontation lines between the NKR and Azerbaijan has been relatively peaceful. Several observers believe that tension is subsiding at the battlefronts. Tension continues to exist on the border between Armenia and Azerbaijan. However, the situation is relatively peaceful. Provocative activities have been carried out on the Azerbaijani side of the border from time to time. The Armenian Ministry of Defense press service has disclosed that the situation was peaceful on the entire border between Armenia and Azerbaijan from last night until this afternoon. However, the Azerbaijani military forces fired on Berkaber and (?Kirants) in Idzhevanskiy Rayon with various types of arms last night. No one was hurt. The Armenian border guards were forced to carry out retaliatory operations.
