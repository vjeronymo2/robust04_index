November 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

The Rich and Famous: Entrepreneur Donald Trump will be in Los Angeles Jan. 14 to emcee American Friends of Hebrew University's 20th annual Scopus Awards. The honoree for the evening: his business rival, entrepreneur Merv Griffin. The locale: The Beverly Hilton, which is owned by Griffin. . . . Incidentally, Donald and Ivana Trump, Barbara Walters and Kitty Carlisle are among the famous guests entertained by Harry and Leona Helmsley as "Hard Copy" reports on the lavish lifestyle of the Helmsleys at 7:30 tonight on KNBC-TV Channel 4. ALEENE MacMINN
