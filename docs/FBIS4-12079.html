BFN [Excerpt] Sanaa, 4 Apr (MENA)-- A source close to the General People's Congress has denied reports that the meeting in Salalah, Sultanate of Oman, last night between Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid ended with the former threatening to wage war. He described these reports as groundless. The source affirmed that the dialogue will continue to be the optimal approach to working out all of Yemen's problems. [passage omitted]
