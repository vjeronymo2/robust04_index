January 23, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

3M REPORTS 7.8% HIKE IN ANNUAL EARNINGS

Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing Co. said today that its earnings increased 7.4% in the fourth quarter and 7.8% for the year.

For the three months ended Dec. 31, the company -- also known as 3M -- earned $292 million, or $1.31 a share, compared to $272 million, or $1.21 a share, in the same period a year ago. Fourth-quarter sales rose to $2.95 billion from $2.75 billion in 1988.
