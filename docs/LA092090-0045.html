September 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

PEACE HARD TO KEEP

Does peace have a chance? Yes. Peace has been given a chance many times, and it will come around hopefully without bloodshed.

Are we going to keep it? No. We didn't keep it after WWI, WWII and the Korean War. We start wars, but we do not keep peace. Not for long. We still don't know how.

SOPHIE BINGHAM

Laguna Hills
