940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Bredero sells stake in shopping centre Bredero Properties is selling its 28 per cent interest in the Ashley Shopping Centre, Epsom, to Standard Life Assurance Society for Pounds 17.2m cash, representing an exit yield of 7.25 per cent. After disposal costs, the proceeds represent a surplus of about Pounds 1.4m over book value and will be used to reduce bank borrowings.
