Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nanjing, March 18 (XINHUA) -- Savings deposits of rural residents in east China's Jiangsu Province amounted to 51.32 billion yuan as of March 10. Included were 6.2 billion yuan deposited in the bank in the first 70 days of this year, a local bank official said. He attributed the rise in savings deposits to the rapid development of local rural economy. The booming rural industrial enterprises and service trades have become the mainstays of the provincial economy. According to statistics from the Jiangsu Provincial Statistics Bureau, rural residents' net income last year averaged 1,267 yuan per capita, an increase of 19.4 percent over the previous year.
