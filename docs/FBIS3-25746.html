Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] (?Three) doctors from Garden City Clinic in South Africa who treated His Excellency the President Ngwazi [paramount] Kamuzu Banda when he fell ill in October last year were in the country for a routine check-up on the president at Sanjika Palace in Blantyre. Speaking to MBC [Malawi Broadcasting Corporation] at Chileka Airport in Blantyre on their departure for South Africa, the doctors said they came to Malawi to check the president's condition since the (?past) treatment. The doctors said they were greatly impressed due to the president's physical recovery and that they found him in good condition, which means the president was in good health and was continuing to make good progress. They said that this was contrary to the speculations that were going on in the South African press that the president was critically ill. They said they were going back to South Africa very happy and there are no complications and no infections of any kind with the president's condition.
