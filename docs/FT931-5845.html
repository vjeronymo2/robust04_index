930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Cook warning over Daf plants THE constituencies of at least 19 government ministers would be among those to suffer knock-on effects from the closure or running down of Leyland Daf plants in Britain, Labour alleges this morning. Mr Robin Cook, the shadow trade and industry secretary, claimed that 6,000 companies would be hit if Leyland Daf 'went under'.
