910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Parambe falls to Pounds 112,000 Taxable profits of Parambe, an investment company and art dealer, fell from Pounds 130,972 to Pounds 112,308 for 1990. An unchanged final dividend of 1p is proposed, making a 1.55p (1.5p) total. Earnings per share dropped to 1.1p (1.64p). At the year-end net assets per share stood at at 63p - a 24 per cent decline on the previous year's 82.2p.
