Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] ALgiers, AL-HAYAH -- AL-HAYAH has learned that new administrative appointments were made the day before yesterday in the presidency. Djaballah Belkacem (former member of the local supreme information council) has been appointed as director of information in the presidency, succeeding Mohamed Zehani, who has been appointed as the former's deputy for public relations. Mr. Ben Abbo, former presidential adviser, has been appointed as director general of the department. Well-informed Algerian sources have said that further appointments will be made in the next few days at the presidency, government, and the economic-social council level. Presidency circles have begun to consider a number of measures to ease the severity of the tension the country has been experiencing for a week. The sources pointed to the possibility a general amnesty being issued for a new group of detainees in the coming few days and the curfew imposed on central provinces lifted on the occasion of the blessed 'Id al-Fitr.
