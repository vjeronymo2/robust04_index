BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 28 -- An economic and commercial delegation of Liaoning Province of China led by Mr. Gao Guozhou, deputy governor of the province, paid a working visit to Vietnam at the invitation of the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry (VCCI). The delegation includes many Chinese managers and businessmen in the fields of economy, trade, energy, finance and chemicals. While here, the delegation had working sessions with Mr. Doan Ngoc Bong, VCCI's first vice president. The two sides informed each other of the situations in and possibilities for economic and commercial cooperation between Vietnam and Liaoning. The guests expressed their wish to cooperate with Vietnam in the fields of investment, trade, infrastructural construction, and especially to participate in improvement of factories formerly built with Chinese assistance. The delegation also had working sessions with leaders of the Ministries of Trade, Energy, Construction, and Heavy Industry, and called at several economic and commercial establishments to enquire into possibilities and opportunities for investment, joint-ventures and commercial cooperation between the two sides.
