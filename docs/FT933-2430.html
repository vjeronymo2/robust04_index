930918 FT 18 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: Capel looks to smaller companies - At a Glance Capel-Cure Myers Unit Trust Management has converted its Glenfriars Assets Trust (an international growth fund) into a smaller companies trust. The Capability Smaller Companies Fund will be managed by Crispin Finn, who also runs the N&P Smaller UK Companies Fund (6th out of 50 funds in the sector over the five years to September 1). The initial charge will be 5 per cent and the annual charge 1 per cent.
