930424 FT 24 APR 93 / London bus crews to strike again LONDON bus crews will hold two more one-day bus strikes, on Monday April 26 and Monday May 10, the TGWU general union announced yesterday. The union has already held three one-day strikes in protest at wage cuts and longer hours imposed by London Buses and the company's refusal to give assurances on the future of staff pensions.
