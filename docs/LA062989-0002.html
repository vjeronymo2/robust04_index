June 29, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

U.S. SUPREME COURT UPHOLDS FLAG BURNING AS PROTEST

How inane the reasoning of five Supreme Court justices when they rule that flag burning is just another "expression" of free speech as protected by the First Amendment to the Constitution.

It is one thing to verbally duel at 50 feet with adjectives about Old Glory or any other topic, but a world apart from burning the American flag which a million or so people have died for during our 213 years of independence.

Let's find a way to protect the Stars and Stripes forever.

JAMES E. PETERSON

Sunland
