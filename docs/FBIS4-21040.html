BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of fileworthy reports on counternarcotics operations in countries monitored by FBIS London Bureau from 31 March-7 April. Sources are given in parentheses at the end of each report.

Denmark
What is described as "the largest drug haul so far" was carried out in Denmark. Police discovered 1.5 tonnes of hashish in a Danish vessel that was inspected off the island of Fyn. The police suspect that about 11 tonnes of hashish was removed from the boat before the raid. During the raid, 18 Danish citizens were arrested. The vessel with the cargo of hashish was apparently on its way from North Africa. (Helsinki Suomen Yleisradio Network in Finnish 1000 GMT 5 Apr 94)
Iran
Brigadier General Seyfollahi, commander of the Law Enforcement Forces, reports that nearly 2,800 kg of a variety of narcotic substances had been seized from bandits and smugglers and the offenders arrested during the brief period since the start of the new year. (Tehran Voice of the Islamic Republic of Iran First Program Network in Persian 1630 GMT 3 Apr 94) Law enforcement forces seized 144 kgs of different types of narcotics during the week beginning March 21. According to the deputy head of the ideological department of the forces, 49 people were arrested in this connection. (Tehran IRNA in English 1121 GMT 31 Mar 94)
Russia
Sixteen soldiers belonging to a Russian strategic missile unit in the city of Kopeysk were detained while buying hard drugs from a local dealer, the counterintelligence department for the Chelyabinsk region reported on 7 April. Counterintelligence officers searched the dealer's apartment and found eight kg of drugs. (Moscow ITAR-TASS in English 1029 GMT 7 Apr 94)
Spain
The Civil Guard and the police seized 33 kilos of cocaine and arrested seven people in three different searches conducted at Madrid-Barajas airport between 1400 and 2000 on 3 April. (Madrid RNE-1 Radio Network in Spanish 2000 GMT 3 Apr 94)
Ukraine
A 31 March news release by the presidential cabinet press service reports that Ukraine's Customs House seized 98 kilos of drug-containing raw materials from 23-29 March. (Kiev Radio Ukraine World Service in English 2100 GMT 31 Mar 94)
United Kingdom
Customs and Excise officers raided a house in West Hampstead, north London, on 5 April and seized 30 kilos of heroin worth 3 million pounds. Three men--two from Turkey and one from Mauritius--were arrested. (London PRESS ASSOCIATION English 1300 GMT 6 Apr 94)
