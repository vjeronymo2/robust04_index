BFN [Unattributed report: "On the Withdrawal of Russian Troops from Belarusian Territory"] [Text] BELAPAN has learned that the Sevastopol heavy bomber air regiment of the Russian Armed Forces began its withdrawal from Belarusian territory on 17 May. Five TU-22 aircraft left the air base of the Machulishchi garrison, in Minsk Rayon, and were moved to a storage base in Saratov Oblast [Russia].
