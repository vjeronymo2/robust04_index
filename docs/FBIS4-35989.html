BFN [Text] Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, the Jordanian deputy prime minister and minister of higher education, and his delegation left Damascus this morning after a visit to Syria that lasted for several days. During the visit, a scientific cooperation agreement was signed between the two sisterly countries with the aim of strengthening ties of brotherhood and fostering educational, scientific, and cultural links. The delegation was acquainted with cultural and scientific achievements and visited university buildings established during the era of President Hafiz al-Asad, the sponsor of science and the scientists. Emphasis was placed on the need to exchange scientific expertise between Syrian and Jordanian university teachers, to exchange student delegations, and to conduct joint research.
