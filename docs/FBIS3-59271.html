Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Text] In recent years, Tibet's industrial and commercial individually owned enterprises have seen relatively rapid development, with as many as 37,235 enterprises employing 50,289 people. Self-investment has amounted to 151.91 million yuan, and total sales and revenues have amounted to 735.93 million yuan. Retail volume has reached 38.11 million yuan. Privately run enterprises have also developed somewhat, with 38 enterprises employing 351 people and with 7.41 million yuan in registered capital. This reporter collected this information at the 16 November meeting of representatives of individual industrial and commercial enterprises and privately run enterprises.
