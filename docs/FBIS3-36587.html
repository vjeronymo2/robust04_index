Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] According to the government's press service, Prime Minister Abbas Dzhumagulov received representatives of the mission of the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development [EBRD]. [passage omitted] Under discussion were loans to the Kyrgyz Republic in three spheres: financing of small and medium-sized businesses, finding foreign partners to invest in the private sector, and elaboration of (?technical and economic) basis for state-owned enterprises. The head of government described plans for developing tourism together with foreign investors, ore and gold deposits, setting up enterprises for processing agricultural produce, and production of paper and (?glassware).
