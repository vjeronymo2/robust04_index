BFN [Text] The IEC [Independent Electoral Commission] commissioner for the eastern Cape, Dr. Oscar Dhlomo, says he is satisfied that the region will meet all the requirements for a free and fair election. Addressing traditional leaders in Ciskei, Dr. Dhlomo said he had been encouraged by the positive response he and his delegation had received so far during their tour of the region. Dr. Dhlomo will visit Transkei, Queenstown and Port Elizabeth this week.
