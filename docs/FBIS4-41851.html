BFN [Edict No. 1324 of the Russian Federation President "On Guarantees of Citizens' Rights and Freedoms in the Russian Federation," dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 22 June 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] For the purposes of ensuring the realization of the citizens' rights and freedoms provided for in the Russian Federation Laws "On the Rehabilitation of Victims of Political Repression" and "On the Payment of Pensions to Citizens Leaving To Take Up Permanent Residence Outside the Russian Federation," in connection with the appeals to the Human Rights Commission under the Russian Federation president received from Citizen S.I. Milshteyn and others, and guided by Part 2 of Article 80 of the Russian Federation Constitution, I decree that: 1. The Russian Federation Government is to approve within one month: in fulfillment of Articles 16 and 16-1 of the Russian Federation Law "On the Rehabilitation of Victims of Political Repression" -- the Statute on the Return of Property Illegally Confiscated, Removed, or Otherwise Taken Out of Ownership in Connection with Repression, the Reimbursement of Its Value, or the Payment of Monetary Compensation, and the Procedure for the Payment of Monetary Compensation to Victims of Political Repression and for Its Recomputation; in accordance with Article 3 of the Russian Federation Law "On the Payment of Pensions to Citizens Leaving To Take Up Permanent Residence Outside the Russian Federation" -- the Procedure for Paying Pensions to Citizens Leaving To Take Up Permanent Residence Outside the Russian Federation. 2. The task of monitoring the fulfillment of this edict is to be entrusted to the chairman of the Russian Federation Government. 3. This edict comes into force the moment it is signed. [Signed] B. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation. 22 June 1994.
