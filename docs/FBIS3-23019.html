Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] A very strong explosion which took place at 0130 [2330 GMT] today shook a whole neighborhood in Kallipolis, Piraievs. A small truck with license plate HH 1140, under which the explosive device was placed, was completely destroyed. There was also damage to three other private cars and the showrooms of shops and buildings up to 100 meters away. The explosion took place in Iakovos Rallis Street, where the owner of the small truck, Grigorios Kaflatzis, 63, resides. The truck's owner said that he does not have any differences with anyone. The antiterrorist unit arrived on the scene.
