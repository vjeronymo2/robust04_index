DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 2011] International Telecommunications Advisory Committee; Radiocommunication Sector, Joint Working Party 7&hyph;8R; Meeting The Department of State announces that the United States International Telecommunications Advisory Committee (ITAC), Radiocommunication Sector, Joint Working Party (JWP) 7&hyph;8R, will meet on June 15, 1994, 9:30 am to 1 pm at MITRE, 6305 Ivy Lane, suite 500, Greenbelt, Maryland. ITU&hyph;R Study Groups 7 and 8 have established JWP 7&hyph;8R to consider compatibility between active spaceborne sensors and systems in the radionavigation and radiolocation services. The JWP will undertake studies needed to establish the technical bases for decisions on agenda items related to active sensors identified by WRC&hyph;95 for WRC&hyph;97. Mr. William Long, USA, will serve as Chairman of JWP 7&hyph;8R. The first meeting of the JWP will take place at the ITU in Geneva, from 9 to 11 November 1994. Mr. John W. Kiebler has been appointed Chairman of U.S. ITU&hyph;R JWP 7&hyph;8R. The agenda for the meeting will be to organize a work plan to prepare for the Geneva meeting in November. Draft Agenda 1. Approval of the Agenda. 2. Introductory Remarks. 3. Work Assigned to JWP 7&hyph;8R. 4. Contributions Needed for the November meeting of JWP 7&hyph;8R. 5. Development of a Work Plan for U.S. JWP 7&hyph;8R. 6. Consideration of available Contributions. 7. Time and Place of Future Meetings. 8. Other Business. Members of the General Public may attend the meeting and join in the discussions, subject to the instructions of the U.S. Chairman, Mr. John Kiebler. Anyone planning to attend the meeting is requested to contact Mr. Kiebler, no later than 2 days before the meeting, at (301) 901&hyph;9213. Dated: May 9, 1994. Warren G. Richards, Chairman, U.S. ITAC for ITU Radiocommunication Sector. [FR Doc. 94&hyph;12549 Filed 5&hyph;20&hyph;94; 8:45 am] BILLING CODE 4710&hyph;45&hyph;M
