Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Public Order Ministry spokesman reports that on the eve of the trial of former Prime Minister Fatos Nano yesterday, despite warnings by the Public Order Ministry, former Interior Minister Gramoz Ruci mobilized groups of Enverists [Enver Hoxha followers] and former State Security officers and tried to create conflicts among the people in the cities of Berat and Kucove and in some other centers. With the same scheme, too, former State Security officers as well as leaders of the former Labor Party and the Socialist Party tried to destabilize public order in Tirana yesterday afternoon. The police took legal measures by apprehending several of them. The situation in Tirana is very quiet today and the trial is being held normally.
