August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Brian Lonon, five-time Army boxing champion, has been arrested in Killeen, Tex., and accused of cocaine possession.

Police said they arrested Lonon at his home after finding cocaine valued at about $50,000.

Officials of the U.S. boxing team said they were uncertain whether Lonon's arrest will keep him from competing for the U.S. team in the World Championships at Moscow next month.
