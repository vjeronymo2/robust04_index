940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Software acquisition for Cray Electronics Cray Electronics has completed the acquisition of SAM Systems Services, a software products company. The consideration will be satisfied by the issue to the vendors of 1.48m new ordinary shares, valuing SAM Systems at Pounds 2.7m. For 1993 SAM achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 235,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.77m.
