910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Net assets fall 17% at German Inv Tst The German Investment Trust's first full trading period was hit by political uncertainty affecting its chosen markets and the 13 months to March 31 1991 ended with net asset value down 17 per cent at 80.1p. After expenses of Pounds 522,404 and tax of Pounds 244,046, net revenue came to Pounds 314,491 and earnings per share were 0.81p. Directors recommend payment of a dividend of 0.8p for the year.
