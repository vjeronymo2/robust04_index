BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, deputy chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], received two telephone calls yesterday afternoon from his brothers, Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan al Nuhayyan, the president of the United Arab Emirates. President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and Shaykh Zayid expressed their great concern over the situation in Yemen and the continued fighting there. They stressed their strongest determination to continue making all possible efforts to stop the fighting between the sons of the united Yemen. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid reiterated his thanks and appreciation for the efforts of both presidents, Husni Muharak and Shaykh Zayid. Those efforts were aimed at stopping the bloodshed in Yemen, which began after the launch of the doomed war by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his secessionist and adventuristic gang after he lost patience with the widespread agreement and overall national consensus regarding the pledge and accord document. The brother deputy chairman of the Presidential Council briefed his two brothers, Muhammad Husni Mubarak and Zayid Bin-Sultan, about developments in the military situation along all the axes of the fighting. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid has also spoken on the telephone with his brother, His Majesty King Husayn of Jordan. He briefed him on the situation in Yemen which resulted from the ill-fated war sparked off by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid expressed great appreciation for the role played by Jordan in attempting to stop the deterioration of the situation in Yemen. He confirmed his earnestness in responding to all fraternal and friendly efforts to return to political dialogue, and to submit to the true and serious implementation of the pledge and accord document.
