920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Letter: Lawyer's lucre Sir, The Observer column which referred to Mr Michael Freeman (February 26) used the expression 'he would be leading the crusade when it set off'. Those of us who believe in honouring our commitments rather than employing lawyers to find loopholes in the documents in order to avoid doing so, do not believe that 'crusade' has anything to do with it. He is doing it for the money] Roger Earl, 4 Cumberland Road, Kew, Surrey TW9 3HQ
