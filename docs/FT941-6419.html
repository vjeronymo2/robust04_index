940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / Colleges declare dispute won Further education college employers yesterday declared victory in their dispute with the Natfhe lecturers' union over contracts, after the union said it was abandoning national talks for local bargaining. About 100 Natfhe members who disobeyed union orders and held unofficial strikes yesterday face possible damages and disciplinary action. The Court of Appeal last week ruled that Natfhe's strike, called for yesterday, was unlawful because principals had not been given the names of members balloted.
