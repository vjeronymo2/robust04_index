940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Kinkel seeks Baltic troops deal Mr Klaus Kinkel, the German foreign minister, yesterday urged the foreign ministers of Estonia and Latvia to reach agreement with Moscow on the question of their Russian-speaking minorities, a matter which may hamper the planned withdrawal of Russian troops. The question had 'outstanding significance for the future of relations with Russia', Mr Kinkel said at the start of day-long talks in Bonn, also attended by the Lithuanian foreign minister.
