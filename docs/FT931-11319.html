930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / Arafat in talks with Saddam MR Yassir Arafat, leader of the Palestine Liberation Organisation, yesterday met Iraqi President Saddam Hussein, Reuter reports from Baghdad. Diplomats said the visit, the first since the PLO's public attempts at reconciliation with Saudi Arabia, could be a mediation attempt by Mr Arafat to help bring Iraq back into the Arab fold. It was the first time the two leaders have held talks since January 1992.
