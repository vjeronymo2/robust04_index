Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report from Paris by Badra Bakhus al-Faghali] [Text] AL-DIYAR has learned from informed Western sources that the U.S. Administration plans to dispatch a security team to Beirut to study the various aspects of the situation. The security sources who reported this have not disclosed the date when the team is expected to arrive. They explained, however, that the major objective of this secret visit is to report on the actual security and political conditions in Lebanon so as to help the U.S. Administration decide whether to allow U.S. nationals to travel to Lebanon and resume flights between the two countries or to continue the present ban.
