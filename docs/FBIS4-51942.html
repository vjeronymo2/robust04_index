BFN [Text] During talks with reporters in Naha city on 30 April, Kosuke Uehara disclosed that he would seek to establish a "grand union of doves" by gathering forces advocating protection of the Constitution from Sakigake [Harbinger], the Democratic Socialist Party, and a liberal group in the Liberal Democratic Party. Uehara is a member of the Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ], and former director general of the National Land Agency and Hokkaido and Okinawa Development Agency. On the formation of a "grand union of doves," Uehara said: "It will formulate its policies toward the Japan-U.S. security pact, Self-Defense Forces, and foreign affairs based on the agreement made when the coalition government was inaugurated."
