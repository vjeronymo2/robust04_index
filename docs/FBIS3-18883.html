Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Mar. 8 (A.A.) -- Fourty-five terrorists from an outlawed separatist organization were caught during a series of operations, it was reported on Tuesday [8 March]. Istanbul anti-terrorism officials said security forces, acting on a tip, staged operations against the terror organization and caught the terrorists who are believed responsible for various offenses including bomb and firebomb attacks, setting several state and private buildings on fire and killings. Eight weapons, one hand grenade and a large quantity of ammunition were seized in safe houses belonging to the terrorists. An investigation has been launched into the incident.
