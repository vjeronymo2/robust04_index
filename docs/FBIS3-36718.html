Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Vladimir Akimov and Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Almaty, 9 Feb -- Agreement has been reached at talks with the Kazakhstan leadership in Almaty "to speed up work on the preparation of solutions on the space complex at Baykonur", Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev reported. Replying to a question from ITAR-TASS correspondents at the conclusion of talks today with Tuleutay Suleymenov, head of the foreign policy department of Kazakhstan, Kozyrev said that the sides intended to prepare a package of agreements on this problem in time for the coming Russian-Kazakhstan summit which is due to take place not later than the end of March. Andrey Kozyrev is in Almaty on an official visit.
