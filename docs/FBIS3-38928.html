Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] State Minister Necmettin Cevheri has said that Turkey is determined not to leave Bosnia-Herzegovina alone in the midst of the Christian world. Cevheri was speaking at a meeting with Enest Drakovic, Bosnian minister of education, science, culture, and sports. Cevheri stressed that Turkey displays its stand at every opportunity. He pointed out that the death of 68 people as a result of a Serbian artillery attack on Sarajevo deeply wounded the Turkish people, adding: We know that grieving is not enough. We will continue to be on the side of Bosnia-Herzegovina with whatever is necessary and with all the possible means. Drakovic said, in turn, that the ties between the peoples of the two countries go back in history, and asked for Turkey's help in preparing an Islamic encyclopedia.
