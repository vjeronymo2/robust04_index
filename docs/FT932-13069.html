930424 FT 24 APR 93 / World News in Brief: Thatcher cancels Zagreb trip Lady Thatcher cancelled plans to go to Zagreb University to receive a doctorate because of 'horrific attacks' by Croats on Moslems in central Bosnia. BBC and ITV news bulletins showed pictures of the aftermath of a massacre in Ahinici. Every Moslem home had been burned and the village mosque destroyed. US should not intervene alone, Page 2
