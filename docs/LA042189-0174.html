April 21, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

A new office and warehouse is going up in Orange for Bromar Inc., a Newport Beach food broker. Lusk Construction Co., Los Angeles, is building the 41,000-square-foot, 2-story building off the Orange Freeway opposite Anaheim Stadium.

A Bromar subsidiary, Bromar Southern California, will move into the building from offices in Garden Grove.
