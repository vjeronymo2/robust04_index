920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Majedie net asset value at 341p Majedie Investments reported a net asset value of 341p at March 31. This marked a modest decline from 348p at the trust's year-end in September, but was unchanged on the interim stage of 1991. Net revenue for the six months to end-March amounted to Pounds 1.15m (Pounds 1.19m), equal to earnings of 4.38p (4.51p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.5p.
