November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

49ERS BASKETBALL TEAM WILL PLAY AT UNDEFEATED ARIZONA

The Cal State Long Beach men's basketball team, which has yet to exhibit any of its preseason promise, will play No. 2-ranked Arizona at 3 p.m. Saturday in Tucson's McKale Center.

The game will be broadcast by KORG (1190).

After barely getting by Chicago State, 84-81, Monday night in their home opener, the 49ers are 1-1. They lost last Saturday night at North Texas, 94-82.

Long Beach is shooting only 39% from the field and has made 50 turnovers. Sophomore guard Lucious Harris is leading the 49ers with an average of 28 points a game.

Arizona is 5-0 after beating Western Illinois, 90-51, Monday night.
