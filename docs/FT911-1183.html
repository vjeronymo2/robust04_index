910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Exports boost Toye to Pounds 0.72m Increased exports helped boost pre-tax profits at Toye, the civil and military regalia manufacturer, from Pounds 690,493 to Pounds 720,915 for the year to end-December. Turnover was ahead 12 per cent from Pounds 11.03m to Pounds 12.39m. The increase included a rise of 37 per cent in direct exports, valued at Pounds 1.73m. Mr Michael Prokopiw, company secretary, said: 'With a downturn in certain UK markets the group is seeking alternatives to support manufacturing areas affected, whilst at the same time continuing to undertake a thorough review of overheads.' Export inquiries and orders booked remained at an all time high, he added. Earnings per share stood at 22.02p, up from 20.19p. The single dividend for the year is maintained at 8p.
