940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Green awareness urged on companies The finance directors of Britain's largest companies have been urged to ensure that they are managing their environmental risks effectively. The Hundred Group, which consists of the finance directors of companies in the FTSE-100 share index, has issued a briefing note on the growing environmental liabilities facing companies, with a warning that 'financial backers of business are starting to focus on these issues'.
