BFN [Text] The Federal Government says no amount of pressure, internal threat, or external intimidation will force it to take measures that are likely to create anarchy in the country. The secretary to the government of the federation, Alhaji Aminu Saleh, stated this in Abuja last night at a reception for media executives. He said government was determined to put in place a genuine democratic government as soon as possible. Alhaji Aminu advised some foreign powers against sponsoring terrorism and disorder in the country. He said no country should be allowed to impose its political ideas on the country since Nigeria has never imposed its ideas on any other country. The secretary to the government said the Constitutional Conference was at present the only legally constituted body to carry out the assignment on behalf of the people of this country. He said government would continue to rely on the mass media for an objective assessment of economic policies.
