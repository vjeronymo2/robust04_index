Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Sergey Prokopenko report: "MVD [Ministry of Internal Affairs] Sums Up"] [Text] The results of the operational activities and official duties of internal affairs organs and Internal Troops during 1993 are being considered at a three-day conference-seminar which opened in Moscow 8 February. Command personnel of republic, kray, and oblast internal affairs organs and commanders of Internal Troops districts are taking part in it. The conference was opened by a report from Army General Viktor Yerin, Russian Federation minister of internal affairs, on the results of internal affairs organs' operational activities and official duties, on measures to protect citizens' constitutional rights, and on further strengthening law and order and intensifying the fight against crime. Russian Government Chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin spoke at the conference.
