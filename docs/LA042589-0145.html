April 25, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE/TECHNOLOGY

Advanced Logic Research Inc., an Irvine personal computer maker, has signed a two-year distribution agreement with Gates/FA Distribution valued at $40 million.

The agreement with Gates/FA, a national distributor of personal computer products, provides for the Richmond, Calif., company to distribute ALR's entire line of personal computers, workstations and related products.

The agreement is ALR's first national distribution pact.
