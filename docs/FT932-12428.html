930428 FT 28 APR 93 / International Company News: USX lifts net income to Dollars 50m USX Corporation, the US steel and energy group, raised net income to Dollars 50m on sales of Dollars 4.28bn for the first three months of 1993, compared with profits of Dollars 4m on sales of Dollars 4.11bn a year earlier, excluding the effect of accounting changes, writes Karen Zagor in New York. Operating income rose to Dollars 163m in the latest quarter from Dollars 137m. USX-US Steel, the Pittsburgh-based steel company, saw net income of Dollars 13m, or 18 cents, in the quarter, against an underlying net loss of Dollars 20m, or 41 cents, a year earlier, excluding accounting changes. Sales were flat at Dollars 1.2bn.
