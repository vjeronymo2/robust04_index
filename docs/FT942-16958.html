940407 FT 07 APR 94 / UK Company News: Exide buys Big Batteries Exide Corporation of the US has moved into the European battery market via the acquisition of Big Batteries for a sum in excess of Pounds 20m. Big Batteries, based in Cwmbran, Wales, is the UK's largest privately-owned maker of automotive batteries. Exide has a 35 per cent share of the North American automotive battery market.
