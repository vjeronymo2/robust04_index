930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Provident Financial obtains injunction Provident Financial, the consumer credit and insurance group has obtained a high court injunction to prevent the Halifax Building Society using the name 'Halifax' in connection with motor insurance. Provident Financial has a motor insurance subsidiary, Halifax Insurance, specialising in private motor insurance. Provident said the injunction would stay in force until a full hearing of the case in up to two years' time.
