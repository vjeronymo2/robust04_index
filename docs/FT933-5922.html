930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / Pounds 6m Birmingham property deal POSTEL, the investment management company, yesterday announced a Pounds 6m commercial property acquisition in Birmingham. The company, which bought the 29,000sq ft city centre building at 1 Cornwall Court from Hanover Property Unit Trust, expects it to generate annual rental income of Pounds 526,000. The building has been let to Lloyds Bank, KPMG Peat Marwick and Scottish Mutual.
