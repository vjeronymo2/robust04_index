940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / International Capital Markets: Pakistan to launch eurobond Pakistan is preparing to launch its first eurobond - possibly raising Dollars 200m - in the next two months, writes Farhan Bokhari from Islamabad. It will be the country's first attempt to convert some of its short-term loans into medium-term borrowings. It is also keen to improve the stability of its foreign exchange reserves. 'It is time to go to the international capital markets with a better credit rating than is available in Pakistan and convert some six-month borrowings into a bond,' said the Islamabad MoF.
