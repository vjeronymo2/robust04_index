Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers has approved a statute of the Kiev Oblast and City Board for Protection of State Secrets in Press and Other Mass Media. It was decreed that the board is a local body of the State Secret Protection Committee, as well as a part of the oblast administration due to the principle of dual subordination. The primary task of the board is the protection of state secrets and secret information in printed sources and other means of mass media including papers, journals, books, television and radio programs, documentary films, and exhibitions, as well as textual, audio, and audiovisual materials destined for shipment abroad, disclosure of which can harm military, scientific, technological, and economic interests of Ukraine. The board will give consultative assistance to local authorities, organizations, enterprises, publishing houses, mass media, and expert commissions in order to prevent the disclosure of state secrets and secret information in materials, which are being prepared for publication, broadcasting, or shipment abroad. The board is not entitled to control the ideological contents of those materials. The board carries out its activity on the basis of normative documents adopted for protection of state secrets and secret information in the press and other mass media.
