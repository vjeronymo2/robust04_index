September 30, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'HEAVY-DUTY ANGER' HEATS UP

Ladies, wake up and give us a break.

I was ready to shed a tear for you, but I was too busy washing the bathroom floors and cleaning the toilet bowls.

I don't do windows, but I vacuum the house.

To clear the air, some of us men do quite more than the few chores listed in the article.

So my question is, what's the big deal? If you want more done around the house, ask nicely. You'll be surprised.

JOHN HARSHAW

Torrance
