Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 23 (XINHUA) -- The main station of a satellite ground station of a very small calibre started operation yesterday in East China's Shanghai. The station will be able to provide satellite channels, connect any other ground station of a very small calibre in China and international satellite channels. Customers will be able to enter the main station at any point in Shanghai and set up data communications channels with any other user in China. Shanghai Petroleum Exchange has become the first user of the station. The satellite ground station system with a very small calibre sprung into being in the 1980s and their construction costs half that of ground stations in popular use now. There are now more than 100,000 such systems in the world.
