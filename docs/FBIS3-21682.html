Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] A Nigerian was arrested at Jan Smuts Airport outside Johannesburg today after about 1.5 kilograms of cocaine, with a street value of 405,000 rands, was found in his baggage. The police said the 29-year-old man had been a passenger on a flight from Rio de Janeiro in Brazil to Harare in Zimbabwe. He was to appear in court today.
