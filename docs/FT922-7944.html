920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Apples continue to be superb with different varieties to choose from, including Cape Golden Delicious at 55-60p a lb (60-70p) and Cape Granny Smith at 45-55p a lb (50-55p) reports the FFVIB. Royal Gala & Gala apples from New Zealand at 60-70p a lb (70-80p) are also good apple choices. Potatoes are excellent this week with Jersey Royals at 45-55p a lb (70-80p), Majorcan varieties at 28-30p a lb (25-30p) and Valencia potatoes at 20-28p a lb (20-25p). Good salad buys include British, Dutch, Guernsey & Canary Island tomatoes at 50-60p a lb (50-60p) and British Round lettuce 20-25p each (20-25p).
