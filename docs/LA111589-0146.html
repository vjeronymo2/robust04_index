November 15, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

MUSLIM'S SCARF SPARKS FRENCH TEACHERS STRIKE

Teachers at the Louis-Lomiere school said today they will strike for 15 minutes each morning until a Muslim fellow faculty member agrees to leave her head scarf at home.

The walkouts, to begin Thursday, are directed at Marie-Christine Benmecherene, 35, who began wearing a head scarf to class in early October after three schoolgirls in Creil sparked a national debate on whether Islamic scarfs violate school policies of secularism.
