940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: Atherton fined by England selectors England cricket captain Mike Atherton denied having tampered with the ball during the Test match against South Africa, but admitted not giving a full explanation of his actions to the match referee. Ray Illingworth, England chairman of selectors, fined him Pounds 1,000 for using dirt to dry his fingers and a further Pounds 1,000 for not explaining his actions fully. The fines followed South Africa's triumphant return to Lord's, where they beat England by 356 runs with a day to spare.
