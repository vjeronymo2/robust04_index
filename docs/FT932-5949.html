930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / International Company News: Sears executive gets Pounds 334,000 Mr Liam Strong, chief executive of Sears, the retailing group, received a salary of Pounds 275,000, a performance-related payment of Pounds 38,000 and pension and 'other' contributions of Pounds 21,000 in the financial year ended January, according to the company's annual report. That was 20 per cent above the Pounds 228,000 paid to Mr Michael Pickard, who Mr Strong succeeded in February 1992. The company also paid out Pounds 555,000 to three directors who left in the past financial year. Mr Pickard, Mr John Osborn and Mr John Lovering received Pounds 457,000 in cash and Pounds 98,000 'for augmentation of pension benefits' on termination of their contracts.
