December 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DOGS NEED LOVE, AFFECTION TOO

Reading that Robert Fulghum ("King of Kindergarten," Nov. 26) profoundly hates dogs was a shocker.

He is in a position to influence. I hope he isn't trying to spread the hatred, because my dog's companionship and affection have made my life contented.

The article mentions his bouts of depression. He needs a dog.

ARTHUR PEARSON, Seal Beach
