BFN [Excerpt] Tarapoto -- It has been reported here that former Tupac Amaru Revolutionary Movement (MRTA) militants have made known the location of a common grave in Yurimangas that was dug by the movement's leaders. The clandestine cemetery is 40 km south of Yurimangas. It is believed to contain the remains of MRTA members who died in confrontations with the forces of order. It is also understood that the grave contains the victims of the MRTA's subversive insanity who, after being captured, were murdered without pity and their bodies thrown into the common grave.
