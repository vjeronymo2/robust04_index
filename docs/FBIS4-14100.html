BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Apr (MENA)--General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff and head of the Israeli delegation to the Gaza-Jericho negotiations, has said that the negotiators have completed 50 percent of an agreement. In a news conference at noon today, Shahaq said the Israeli delegation would stay in Cairo until Thursday. If no agreement on all the points has been reached by then, he said, another round of negotiations will be held. Shahaq said he has no information about a meeting in Cairo among Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher.
