October 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

READERS TOUCH ALL BASES ON TOUCHY ISSUE

Who the heck is Mike Downey to preach to me?

His column, "Remember, This Isn't a Show, It Doesn't Have to Go On," was more suited for a soapbox or pulpit than the sports section.

I always thought that sportswriters were frustrated jocks. Now I know some are frustrated philosophers.

JOHN CUSUMANO, Chatsworth
