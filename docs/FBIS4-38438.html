BFN [Yuriy Dmitriyev report under the general heading: "Greeting to Border Guards"] [Text] On the eve of the Russian Border Guards' holiday B.N. Yeltsin met with Colonel General A.I. Nikolayev, commander in chief of the Border Guards, and command personnel. Leaders of districts and groups of troops came from all over the country to meet with the president. The meeting took place in the Lubyanka building and at the Dzerzhinskiy Club. The president briefed the troops on Russia's current border policy.
