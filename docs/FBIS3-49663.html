Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, Romania's President Ion Iliescu promulgated the bill empowering the government to rule by ordinances and authorizing it to contract and guarantee foreign loans, a communique received from Romania's Presidency says. The promulgation was made on the basis of Decision No. 4 of 11 January 1994 of the Constitutional Court, which found that parliament has observed the stipulations of the Constitution when it passed the aforementioned bill.
