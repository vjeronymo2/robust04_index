Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Mar del Plata, 31 Dec (NA) -- Under Secretary of Fishing Fernando Gutierrez submitted his resignation on 29 December to Agriculture, Livestock and Fishing Secretary Felipe Sola, reported reliable sources of that secretariat. Gutierrez apparently conveyed to Secretary Sola his decision to leave his post in order to get involved in political activities for the Justicialist Party. [passage omitted] The sources indicated that Gutierrez' replacement "is not yet known," and that the new appointment will not be made until February, after Sola returns from the vacation he is taking in this city.
