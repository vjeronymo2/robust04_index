ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PHANTOM DONOR: The Costa Mesa woman who made an anonymous gift of $10,000 to keep the Grove Shakespeare Festival alive says she wants no personal glory (F1) . . . . "I'm not part of that circle of millionaires who wants a pat on the back just because they give money to the arts," she said. What does she want? "The satisfaction of doing the right thing."
