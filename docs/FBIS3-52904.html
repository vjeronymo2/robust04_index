Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The general director of the Chabahar free trade zone announced that this free port is prepared to transit goods for Central Asian countries. Presently the port's various dock's have the capacity to handle one million [metric] tonnes of cargo a year, he said in an interview with reporters. He called on Iranian capital holders and merchants who are active in Dubai, Abu Dubai, Sharjeh, Qatar, Oman, and Kuwait to visit the warehouses in Chabahar free port and to obtain their needs from this area, according to an IRNA report. Referring to the actions of some investors in establishing trade offices and private warehouses in Chabahar, Mr Same'i said: There are no restrictions for trade, service, or industrial activity in the port and various economic activities in the region enjoy a 15- year tax exemption. He added: The import of raw materials, machinery, and other authorized commodities is permitted to the region and the zone's regulations allow easy regional investment.
