931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / Fall in bank mortgage lending New mortgage lending by banks fell last month in spite of their attempt to increase their share of the mortgage market. The value of new mortgages approved in October fell to Pounds 1.45bn from Pounds 1.53bn in September and the number of new loans fell from 31,378 to 29,741, according to British Bankers' Association figures.
