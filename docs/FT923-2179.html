920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Halved interest charge boosts Eadie A halving of the interest charge enabled Eadie Holdings, the Glasgow-based engineering company, to report a pre-tax profit of Pounds 85,000 in the first half of 1992, against losses of Pounds 354,000, which included an exceptional charge of Pounds 133,000. Turnover was lower at Pounds 9.22m (Pounds 9.64m) and trading profits improved 16 per cent to Pounds 290,000 (Pounds 250,000). After taking account of acquisitions and disposals the increase was 38 per cent, the company said. Earnings per share were 0.19p (losses 0.95p). There is no interim dividend but the company still intends paying a final of 0.5p.
