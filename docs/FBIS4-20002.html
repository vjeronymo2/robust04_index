BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- On Monday [25 April] a development aid agreement with financial promises of up to 50 million German marks was signed in Bonn by FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) and his Bolivian counterpart, Antonio Aranibar Quiroga. Kinkel encouraged the foreign minister to press for more private German investment during his contacts with business representatives in Germany. Aranibar is in Germany for a three-day visit. On Monday he was also the guest of the Ibero America Association and of the Friedrich-Ebert Foundation, which is affiliated to the Social Democratic Party of Germany.
