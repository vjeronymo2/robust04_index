Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Manuel Sargisyan, adviser to the foreign minister of Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR], said that the Nagorno-Karabakh self-defense forces captured Chinese-made automatic rifles, machine guns, and cannons from the Azerbaijanis. It was learned from the interrogation of the Azerbaijani prisoners that China has sent arms totaling 100,000 pieces to Azerbaijan.
