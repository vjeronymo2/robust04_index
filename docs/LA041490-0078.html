April 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

ARIZONA;

7 SURVIVE AIR CRASH NEAR GRAND CANYON

All seven people aboard survived the crash of a sightseeing plane a half-mile from the Grand Canyon airport.

The plane had just completed a tour over the canyon, Coconino County, Ariz., sheriff's deputies reported. Five of the injured were taken to an area hospital. "We understand that all seven aboard were Swiss nationals," said a hospital spokeswoman. One of the injured was an 11-year old boy. "Two of the injured walked away" from the crash and had only slight injuries, officials said.
