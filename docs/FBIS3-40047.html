Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Finance Minister Alexandros Papadhopoulos and Nikolaos Kiriazidhis, an expert at the Finance Ministry, will be sworn in as finance minister and deputy finance minister respectively. The swearing-in ceremony will be held at noon tomorrow before President Konstandinos Karamanlis and Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou. Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said that the changes in the Finance Ministry were made following consultations between Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou and National Economy Minister Yeoryios Yennimatas.
