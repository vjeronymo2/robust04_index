Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Qatari Foreign Minister Hamad Bin-Jasin Bin-Jabbur Al Thani will visit Havana on Friday, 4 February, at the invitation of Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina Gonzalez. The Qatari foreign minister will be accompanied by a broad delegation made up of representatives from the Foreign Ministry; the Energy and Industry Ministry; the Finance, Economy and Trade Ministry; and leaders from institutions of the industrial and service areas. During their stay in Cuba, the Qatari delegation will hold meetings with government leaders and will visit places of commercial, economic, touristic, and historical interest.
