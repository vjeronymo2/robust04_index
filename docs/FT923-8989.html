920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Sutcliffe Speakman cuts loss to Pounds 2.1m Sutcliffe Speakman, which has restructured to focus on the core carbon and environmental design equipment business, cut its loss to Pounds 800,000 in the second half. That resulted in a deficit of Pounds 2.1m for the year to end-March against Pounds 14.4m the year before. Losses per share were 1.7p (62.6p). Arrears of dividend on the preference capital totalled Pounds 266,000. Shareholders' funds had been turned from a negative Pounds 3.22m to a positive Pounds 10m with net current assets at Pounds 2.34m against liabilities of Pounds 11.8m. Turnover from continuing activities was Pounds 30.7m (Pounds 36.5m) and from discontinued operations Pounds 851,000 (Pounds 12.5m), with operating losses of Pounds 737,000 (Pounds 6.9m) and Pounds 150,000 (profit Pounds 299,000) respectively.
