920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Providing social housing in London A Pounds 10m contract has been awarded to WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION's London region to design and build 200 homes for a London consortium of six housing associations led by Threshold Housing Association. Located on the old St James' Hospital site in south west London, the development will provide homes in two, three and four-storey buildings. Also included is the refurbishment and conversion of a two-storey building for community use. A key element of the project is the construction of a home for elderly people with special care needs. This will be constructed to the requirements of the South West Thames Regional Health Authority. Partners in the scheme are Threshold Housing Association, Servite Houses, Wandle Housing Association, Imani Housing Co-op, Threshold Key Homes and Delta Housing Association.
