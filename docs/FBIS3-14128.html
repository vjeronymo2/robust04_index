Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] After the crash of a C-130 aircraft belonging to the Islamic Republic of Iran [IRI] in the Khankendi [Stepanakert] region, Azeri President Geydar Aliyev held a telephone conversation with IRI President Hashemi-Rafsanjani in which he expressed his sympathies and offered the condolences of the Azeri government and nation to the president. Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani, thanking the Azeri president for his sympathies, said: A group of technical experts will be going to the area to investigate the cause of the accident. Foreign Minister Velayati has also sent a message of condolence to the IRI's ambassador in Moscow on the same occasion. The message expresses deep sorrow for the heartrending incident and stresses: The cause of the plane crash will be determined through the dispatch of an expert group to the scene. The IRI embassy in Moscow issued a statement on Friday expressing great sorrow over the loss of life in the plane crash of a number of Iranians who were resident in Russia, and offering condolences to their families. The embassy declared a week of mourning within the Iranian community in Russia.
