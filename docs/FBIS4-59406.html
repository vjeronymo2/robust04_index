BFN ["Text" of Legislative Decree No. 10 issued by President Hafiz al-Asad on 11 May] [Text] Legislative Decree No. 10 President of the Republic, cognizant of the provisions of the Constitution, decrees the following: Article I: The increase in the heating allowance and the price amendments included in Article I of Legislative Decree No. 5 of 30 April 1994, shall be amended to become 245 lira per month, instead of 145 lira per month. Thus, the total of this allowance will become 620 lira per month instead 520 lira per month. Article II: This legislative decree shall be published in the Official Gazette and shall be effective as of 1 May 1994. [Signed] Hafiz al-Asad, president of the Republic, Damascus on 1/12/1414 Hegira, corresponding to 11 May 1994
