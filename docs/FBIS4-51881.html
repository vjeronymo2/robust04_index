BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 1 (KCNA) -- The April plan has been overfulfilled in different sectors of the national economy in Korea. The Korea general silk corporation fulfilled its monthly production plan by April 29. Notably, the export silk garment factory under the corporation fulfilled its plan for the first half of the year at 107.5 percent till April 14. The Pyongyang silk-reeling mill, the Anju silk mill and the Sinuiju textile mill carried out their monthly assignments respectively at 115, 110 and 102 percent. The general bureau of salt industry fulfilled the monthly industrial output value at 112.5 percent and salt production at 101 percent as of April 26. Monthly coal and mineral production plans were overfulfilled at many coal and ore mines including the Nipsok and Taehyang coal mines and the Songsan and Songchon ore mines. Power stations across the country including the East Pyongyang thermal power station and the Kanggye youth power station also fulfilled their monthly electrical power production plan.
