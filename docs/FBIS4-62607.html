BFN ["News Release"] [Excerpt] The 14th picket in front of the Russian military headquarters building in Tallinn, organised by Estonian National Independence Party every Monday, to protest against the continued Russian military presence in Estonia, stressed that Russian military have 107 days left until the August 31 troop withdrawal deadline. "Russia will be really free only when it stops occupying other nations", chairman of the Unrepresented Nations Organisation Linnart Mull told the picket, adding that the members of the small nations that serve in the Russian army -- Maris, Mordovians, Tartars etc, should instead build up their own countries.
