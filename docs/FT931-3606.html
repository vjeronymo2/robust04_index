930315 FT 15 MAR 93 / Moslem leaders assemble to discuss Bosnian peace plan In Sarajevo, the Moslem leaders assembled to discuss a peace plan for the former Yugoslav republic drawn up by international mediators Mr Cyrus Vance and Lord Owen. About 200 people - including members of the Bosnian presidency, parliament, government and military, together with leading intellectuals and clerics - attended the meeting. Meanwhile, there were indications that the Bosnian Serb delgation might be late in arriving in New York.
