Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Manama, 22 Jan (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, amir of Bahrain; and His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the prime minister, this morning received 'Isa Kalantari, minister of agriculture in the Islamic Republic of Iran. During the meeting, they exchanged views on several issues of common interest, and discussed enhancing the relations of cooperation, particularly in the agricultural fields. For his part, the Iranian minister expressed his thanks and appreciation for Bahrain's eagerness to promote bilateral cooperation in various fields. [passage omitted]
