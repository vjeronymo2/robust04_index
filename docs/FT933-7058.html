930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Trinity Intl offer for Woodhead accepted Trinity International, publisher of the Liverpool Daily Post and Echo, now holds 92 per cent of Joseph Woodhead & Sons, the independent privately owned publishing company for which it made a Pounds 12.6m recommended cash offer earlier this month. The offer has now been declared unconditional as to acceptances but remains conditional upon the consent of the Secretary of State for Trade and Industry. The deal has been referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission.
