BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 18 -- Russian Government head Viktor Chernomyrdin, hot on the heels of his meeting with the Russian president on Tuesday, convened an emergency conference with the leaders of a number of ministries in charge of the national economy to criticise their style and methods of work and demand that control be tightened of the fulfilment of decisions and the deadlines of executing documents. Chernomyrdin demanded an upgrading of executive discipline and stressed the leaders' personal responsibility for reforms and specific measures aimed at stabilising finances and economic performance.
