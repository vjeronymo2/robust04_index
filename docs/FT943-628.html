940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Wm Jacks advances William Jacks, the motor car retailer owned by Johan Holdings of Malaysia, continued to improve with an advance in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 65,000 to Pounds 272,000. Turnover for the six months to July 31 was up by 50 per cent from Pounds 22.6m to Pounds 33.9m. Earnings per share worked through at 1.43p (0.22p losses).
