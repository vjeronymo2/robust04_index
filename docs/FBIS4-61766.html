BFN [Text] An extraordinary congress of the Rescue Corps -- the Mkhedrioni -- has been held in Tbilisi. In addressing the congress, the founder of this organization, Jaba Ioseliani, said that the time had come to hand over to the Georgian Defense Ministry all the weapons belonging to the Mkhedrioni. Owing to the nature of their work, 300 members of the Rescue Corps were given the right only to carry revolvers. It is categorically forbidden to carry or possess automatic weapons. The congress paid special attention to the rendering of material assistance for and placing in a job of the families of Mkhedrioni members killed in Abkhazia and other regional conflicts.
