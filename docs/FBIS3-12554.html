Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] It was learned today from the security services that Mourad Sid Ahmed, alias Djaafar El Afghani, the leader of the Armed Islamic Group [GIA] and nine members of his group were liquidated yesterday in the Algiers heights. The name of Djaafar El Afghani was linked to the kidnapping of three French citizens on 24 October 1993 outside their home in Krim Belkacem street. They were Mr. and Mrs. Tivano and Mr. Allan. The second case in which Djaafar El Afghani was implicated was the kidnapping of Cheikh Mohamed Bouslimani, the chairman of the Irshad and Islah [Guidance and Reform] charity society from his home in Blida last November.
