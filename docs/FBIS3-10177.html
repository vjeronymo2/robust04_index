Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Brazilian President Itamar Franco has bestowed upon Cuban Ambassador Jorge Bolanos Brazil's highest distinction, the Great Cross of the Cruzeiro do Sul Order. In a ceremony at the Foreign Ministry in Brasilia, Foreign Minister Celso Amorim presented the medal to the Cuban diplomat, who has just concluded his tour of duty in Brazil. In his address Bolanos stated that he is returning to Havana with the satisfaction of seeing relations between the two nations on a broad and irreversible path of development; and he praised Brazil's foreign policy, which he described as dynamic and rooted in the best traditions of justice, peace, and respect for the sovereignty and self-determination of peoples.
