BFN [By reporter Kim Chong-tu] [Text] It was learned on 12 June that the ROK Government plans to conduct the ROK-U.S. joint "Ulchi-Focus Lens-94 exercise" on a large scale for 14 days, from 22 August to 4 September, to inspect the nation's readiness for mobilization. To inspect the nation's capabilities to mobilize resources and recover damages, during the 22-27 Ulchi exercise, the government plans to select Seoul as an exhibition city in conducting exercises for preparing against disasters, stabilizing the people's livelihoods, and maintaining the government's functions. According to authorities concerned, government ministries will conduct surprise exercises of their own, as well. For example, the Ministries of Home Affairs and Transportation will conduct an exercise for mobilizing personnel and vehicles; the Ministry of Agriculture, Forest, and Fisheries will conduct a river-crossing exercise for barges loaded with grain and an exercise for distributing grain; the Ministry of Health and Social Affairs will conduct an exercise for guarding against poisonous gas; and the Ministries of Home Affairs and Trade, Industry, and Energy will conduct an exercise for preparing against the rupture of electricity, water, and gas supplies.
