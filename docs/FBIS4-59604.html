BFN [Text] Mr. Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, the minister of oil and mineral wealth, arrived in Riyadh last night in the framework of his tour of several Arab countries. Bin-Husaynun said in a statement upon his arrival, that he would inform the officials in Saudi Arabia on the details of the battles in Yemen and the eight-point initiative put forward by the politburo of the Yemeni Socialist Party to stop the fighting and resort to dialogue.
