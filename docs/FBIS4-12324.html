BFN [Text] The Cabinet convened a session at 1730 this evening chaired by Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri. The session, which is continuing in the Presidential Palace, is addressing several hot topics, primarily the media files and Pope John Paul II's upcoming visit to Lebanon. The session was preceded by a meeting between Prime Minister al-Hariri and media representatives in which the prime minister stressed that freedoms in Lebanon are safeguarded and nobody can harm them. He highlighted that the law sets out the framework of these freedoms and supports their existence.
