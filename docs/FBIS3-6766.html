Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 20 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a gift from the Mexico-DPRK association for friendship and cultural exchange on the occasion of his birthday. The gift was handed to the charge d'affaires ad interim of Korean Embassy in Mexico by the chairman of the association on February 24. The chairman of the association, conveying the gift, said Comrade Kim Chong-il is an illustrious leader of the world revolution who is leading the cause of socialism worldwide to victory, and sincerely wished him good health and a long life.
