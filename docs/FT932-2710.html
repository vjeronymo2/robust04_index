930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: United Drug United Drug, the Irish pharmaceuticals and consumer products group, lifted pre-tax profit from IPounds 1.4m to IPounds 1.54m (Pounds 1.5m) in the half year ended March 31. Turnover rose 69 per cent to IPounds 61.4m, reflecting the first time inclusion of Alchem and satisfactory performance from other divisions. Earnings per share rose only 1 per cent to 7.69p (7.61p). Although the effective tax rate came down from 42 per cent to 34 per cent, the number of shares in issue was greatly increased. The interim dividend is 2.2p (2.05p).
