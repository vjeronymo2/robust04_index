940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Finsbury Avenue debenture stock Finsbury Avenue Estates said that FCS Currency Management had bought Pounds 17.4m of its 11 per cent first mortgage debenture stock 2014. The two companies are subsidiaries of British Land. The stock is not being cancelled and, accordingly, Pounds 40m remains outstanding, of which Pounds 17.4m is held by FCS.
