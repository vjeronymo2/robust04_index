940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Bulgin boosted by acquisitions AF Bulgin, the electronic components and power supplies group, moved from losses of Pounds 212,000 to profits of Pounds 507,000 pre-tax for the year to end-January. The improved results reflected better business conditions and two successful acquisitions. Acquisitions added Pounds 1.59m to turnover of Pounds 15.67m (Pounds 12.61m) and Pounds 167,000 to profits. Earnings per share emerged at 1.56p (losses 0.76p) and the single dividend is being lifted from 0.1p to 0.25p.
