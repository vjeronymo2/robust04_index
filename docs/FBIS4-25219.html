BFN [Text] Moscow, June 8 (XINHUA) -- Russia today lodged a protest with the U.S. Government against the violation of its air sovereignty by a U.S. civilian aircraft yesterday. Russia's First Deputy Foreign Minister Anatoliy Adamishin summoned U.S. Ambassador Thomas Pickering today delivering a statement of the Russian Government on the violation. A U.S. Lockheed C-130 plane, on its way from Frankfurt of Germany to Tbilisi of Georgia, penetrated Russia's air space over the Black Sea town of Adler at 13:54 Moscow time Tuesday, without making any application in advance, the ITAR-TASS NEWS SERVICE said. After the plane ignored warnings from Russian ground stations and flew 80 kilometers deep into the Russian space, Russian military aircraft took off and forced it to land at the Adler airport at 14:10. The Russian statement said Russia wishes the U.S. side would take all measures necessary to prevent the reoccurrence of such incidents. The U.S. ambassador expressed gratitude for Russia's restraint in handling the incident.
