BFN [Text] In continuation of its previous meetings, in implementation of Cabinet Decision No. 39 of 1994, and in line with the military provisions enshrined in the accord document, the Military Committee held a second session of talks in Ta'izz this morning under brothers Defense Minister Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir and Army Chief of Staff Staff Brigadier General 'Abd-al- Malik 'Ali al-Sayyali. A member of the fraternal Jordanian military team currently in Yemen, as well as the friendly U.S. and French military attaches, attended. During the meeting, the conferees agreed to draw up a plan of action for the Military Committee aimed at drafting joint plans for the repositioning of units to be submitted to the Council of Ministers for approval during the meeting it is expected to hold on 20 April. The committee will draw up a detailed and comprehensive plan for rebuilding and redeploying the Armed Forces in a manner that will promote and safeguard unity in line with the provisions enshrined in the accord document.
