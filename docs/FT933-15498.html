930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Pifco pays Pounds 2.8m for subsidiary's freehold Pifco, the electrical appliances group, has exchanged contracts for its Russell Hobbs Tower subsidiary to buy the premises which it currently rents in Wolverhampton. Prudential Assurance will be paid Pounds 2.75m cash for the 19 acre freehold site which houses Russell Hobbs' kettle and holloware manufacturing facility.
