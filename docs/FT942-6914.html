940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Parkside Intl achieves Pounds 1.8m Parkside International, the Yorkshire-based packaging group which gained a stock market quote in February this year, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.82m for the 12 months to end-February. The outcome was struck on turnover of Pounds 30.5m - including Pounds 971,000 from discontinued operations - and compared with profits of Pounds 1.6m on turnover of Pounds 24.9m last time. The 1992-93 figure included Pounds 3.04m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share, following the capital reorganisation on flotation, emerged at 7.9p (8.1p). With flotation coinciding with the year end the first dividend to be paid would be the interim for the year ending February 28 1995.
