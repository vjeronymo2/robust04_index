930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Olim Convertible net assets ahead Olim Convertible Trust reported a net asset value of 79.1p per share as at June 30. The figure compared with values of 67.6p a year earlier and 72.1p at the December year-end. Attributable revenue was Pounds 535,000 (Pounds 735,000) for the six months to end-June, equivalent to earnings of 3.57p (4.9p) per share. The interim dividend is 4.3p (4.2p).
