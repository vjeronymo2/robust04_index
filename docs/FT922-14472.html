920406 FT 06 APR 92 / NHS wins praise from electorate AN increasing number of patients are satisfied with the National Health Service, according to a Harris poll for the Daily Express newspaper. It found that 88 per cent were satisfied with their treatment and 82 per cent with the time they were kept waiting. A similar study last year showed 67 per cent were happy with the time they waited. Harris questioned 1,079 voters who had received hospital treatment over the past two years, in 100 constituencies between last Thursday and Saturday.
