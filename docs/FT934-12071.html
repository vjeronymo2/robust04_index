931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / Observer: Parti pris Meanwhile, Radio Free Europe, the Munich-based station which began life as a CIA organ-isation, has a new admirer. Long hounded by the Soviet authorities for its dissident broadcasts of anti-Communist material, its airwaves must have fairly crackled with glee as the Russian Communist Party solemnly thanked the station for being the only media outlet to give the party's electoral programme full and fair coverage. Not that the competition is very great. With most of the media taking a thoroughly pro-Yeltsin line, the activities of the now 'dissident' communists have been all but ignored by most of the Russian press.
