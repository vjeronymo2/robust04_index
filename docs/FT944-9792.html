941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Company News This Week: British Gas - Other Companies Due to publish third-quarter results on Wednesday, the company usually reports a loss at this time, which covers the months of lowest demand for natural gas. Analysts expect the loss to be around Pounds 200m-Pounds 240m. The results coincide with the Queen's Speech, in which the government is expected to announce legislation to end British Gas' monopoly over 18m residential customers in 1998.
