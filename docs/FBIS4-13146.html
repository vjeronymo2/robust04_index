BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani today received visiting Ukrainian Foreign Minister Mr. [Anatoliy] Zlenko. During this meeting, the Ukrainian foreign minister handed over a message from Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and said: Since independence, Ukraine has been willing to expand cooperation with the great country of Iran in all fields of mutual interests. He described the two countries' potential for strengthening closer ties as very favorable and added: Ukraine is ready to do its best to better utilize these potentials. Our correspondent's report indicates that in return President Hashemi-Rafsanjani expressed gratitude for the friendly message from his Ukrainian counterpart and expressed satisfaction for the promotion of ties between the two countries. He said: We believe our bilateral cooperation can be expanded beyond its present level, and thanks to the two countries' officials, the existing level of cooperation, which will serve the interests of the two countries and the world, should be further accelerated. In his message, the Ukrainian president renewed his invitation to Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani to visit that country.
