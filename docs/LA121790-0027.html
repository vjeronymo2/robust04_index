December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: TOMBA FINDS OLYMPIC FORM AND WINS AGAIN

Alberto Tomba, restored to his 1988 Olympic form by hard training and a diet, won his first World Cup giant slalom in two years and his second race in six days Sunday at La Villa, Italy.

Tomba, who won the slalom and giant slalom at the Calgary Games, left Swiss runner-up Urs Kaelin 1.35 seconds behind.

Marc Girardelli of Luxembourg, was third, 1.45 seconds back.
