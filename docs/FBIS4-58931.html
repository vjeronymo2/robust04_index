BFN [Excerpt] This morning, President Yasir 'Arafat received Yemen Ambassador Ahmad Muhammad Haydar. The talks focussed on the volatile situation in brotherly Yemen and the need for swift solutions to avert bloodshed and preserve the achievements of the brotherly Yemeni people. President 'Arafat stressed his readiness to exert utmost efforts to achieve national reconciliation in Yemen and assigned brother 'Abbas Zaki [member of Fatah's Central Committee and former head of PLO's office in the former People's Democratic Republic of Yemen] to follow up the issue and go to Yemen to exert his efforts within this framework.
