930208 FT 08 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Barcom expands via Hawkins buy Barcom, formerly Venture Plant Group, is buying certain companies within the Hawkins Group, one of the largest privately-owned hirers of operated and non-operated plant, for a nominal consideration of Pounds 1 plus certain professional costs associated with the acquisition up to a maximum of Pounds 90,000, writes Juliet Annetts. The Hawkins companies incurred a loss before taxation of Pounds 529,000 on turnover of Pounds 10.1m in the 10-month period ended October 31 1992. Net debt was Pounds 5.8m.
