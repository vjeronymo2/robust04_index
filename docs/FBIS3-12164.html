Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Jewish Settlements in Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza [CJSJSG] approves of the establishment of a state commission of inquiry into the Hebron killings, but at the same time demands that the commission check all the events in the six years of Arab terror. CJSJSG denounces the release of terrorists from prison, and opposes the proposals to seize weapons from Jews in the territories. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that in a communique released today, CJSJSG once again expresses sorrow over the murders and denounces the killing of innocent people.
