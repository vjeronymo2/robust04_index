920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Strong second half at Assoc Nursing Associated Nursing Services, the USM-quoted private nursing homes operator, consolidated its return to black at the interim stage with a strong second half and finished the year to April 4 with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.53m. The result was struck on turnover of Pounds 18.5m and compared with a loss of Pounds 2.33m on turnover of Pounds 16.6m. The net interest charge fell to Pounds 1.55m (Pounds 1.82m). Gearing, after the Pounds 6.27m placing in February, fell to 87 per cent against 229 per cent last time. Net assets were 166p a share. Earnings per share came out at 25.7p (42.8p losses) - after adjusting for the placing pro-forma earnings were 18.2p. Again there is no final dividend, the last payment being the 1991 interim of 1p.
