940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Powerscreen makes US acquisitions Powerscreen International, the Northern Ireland-based maker of screening and stone crushing equipment, is to buy two US businesses, Simplicity Engineering and Ludlow-Saylor, from Lukens, a Pennsylvania-based concern. The price is Dollars 13.5m (Pounds 9m) based on net assets acquired of Dollars 15m, but will be reduced or increased dollar for dollar dependent on net assets as set out in the completion balance sheet. Simplicity, based near Lansing, Michigan, makes heavy-duty material processing systems, while Ludlow-Saylor, based near St Louis, Missouri, produces woven-wire cloth and cast polyurethane screens. The two companies had combined turnover of Dollars 25.2m and net profits of Dollars 1.7m in 1993.
