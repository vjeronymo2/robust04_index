940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Zetters Zetters Group, the football pools promoter, yesterday reported a 15 per cent increase in annual profits. On turnover of Pounds 23m (Pounds 22m), and despite lower interest receivable of Pounds 181,000 (Pounds 320,000), the pre-tax line for the year to March 31 advanced from Pounds 880,000 to Pounds 1.02m. Earnings per share were 10.1p (8.9p); a recommended final dividend of 4.5p brings the total for the year to 8.5p (8p), the first increase since 1991.
