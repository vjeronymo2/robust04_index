BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today noted that the problem in the Aegean can be pinpointed to violations by Turkish planes, violation of air-traffic rules, and violations of Greek national airspace. Venizelos said that U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher's position is an old U.S. position, adding: Our country's position, however, depends on national sovereignty and the need to defend the defensive region. Venizelos stressed: Greece always acts defensively.
