CSO [Unsigned article: "A New Army Corps Has Been Established on the Eastern Border"] [Text] Major General Viorel Cheptine, commander of the Iasi garrison, has announced the establishment of a new army corps in the municipality. The general reported his decision on the occasion of the swearing-in ceremony for the members of the "Stefan cel Mare" [Stephen the Great] mechanized division. The new army corps has been established in accordance with the new military strategy of Romania and is intended to reinforce Romanian military dispositions in the eastern part of the country, in a particularly sensitive zone. In accordance with the organizational structure of the Romanian Army, an army corps is composed of three divisions.
