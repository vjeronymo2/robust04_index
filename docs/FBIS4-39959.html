BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow June 13 TASS -- Yegor Gaydar has been elected the chairman of Russia's Democratic Choice Party. The results of voting by secret ballot were announced on Monday, the second day of the party's founding congress proceedings. Gaydar's candidacy was carried by 490 votes to 14. The party's political council was elected as well. On the council are, in particular, Anatoliy Chubays, Fedor Shelov-Kovedyayev, Bella Denisenko, Boris Zolotukhin, Aleksandrr Pochinok, Sergey Yushenkov, Ilya Zaslavskiy and other prominent political and public figures.
