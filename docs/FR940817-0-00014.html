Under an existing delegation order, ATF specialists issue label approvals and exemptions from label approvals for both domestic and imported products in the name of the Director. ATF is currently revising this delegation order to authorize specialists within the Product Compliance Branch to approve labels and grant exemptions in their own names. However, the regulations at 27 CFR 4.40(b), 5.51(b), and 7.31(b) specifically require that certificates of label approval (ATF Form 5100.31) bear the signature of the Director before imported alcoholic beverage products may be released from Customs custody. This Treasury decision will amend these regulations so that the Director may delegate to Product Compliance Branch specialists the authority to approve labels for imported products in their own names.
