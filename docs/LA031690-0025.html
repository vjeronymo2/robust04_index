March 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BOB HOPE'S 7,400 ACRES

Walter Annenberg donates $50 million to the United Negro College Fund. Bob Hope wants to make $50 million on 7,400 acres of mountain terrain that could be donated for desperately needed parkland. What a difference in values! One leaves a legacy for the future; the other leaves a legacy of greed.

FRANK M. STRICKLIN

Chatsworth
