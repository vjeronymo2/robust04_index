Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jewish settlers of the Jordan Rift Valley have set up an armed guard which will patrol the roads of the region. They have presented the government with their red lines for the next stages of the political process. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that the Jordan Valley settlers held an emergency meeting this afternoon, in reaction to the signing of the Cairo agreement. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1400 GMT on 10 February adds the following: "In their meeting today, the settlers decided to present the three no's they will not allow to be broken: no Palestinian self-rule in the Jordan Rift Valley; no armed Palestinian police; and no territorial corridors for the Palestinians in the valley."] David Levi, chairman of the regional council, claims that since the Document of Principles has been signed in Washington, Arabs have been illegally building in several locations in the region. He called these deliberate Arab settlements.
