September 9, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BASEBALL FANS MOURN LOSS OF COMMISSIONER WITH GENUINE SORROW

It was questioned, "How could a non-baseball man lead our game into the 1990s?" Maybe Bart Giamatti could lead it because he viewed the game as I, my father and you do, with love and enthusiasm.

With his death I feel a great loss, for he was not only the leader of my beloved game, but he was a fellow fan.

MARK LOPEZ, Lynwood
