941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: River & Mercantile Net asset value at River & Mercantile Extra Income Trust in the year to September 30 declined from 123.79p to 97.74p. Earnings per share worked through at 8.4p (9.3p). The fourth interim dividend of 1.96875p makes an unchanged total of 7.875p for the year.
