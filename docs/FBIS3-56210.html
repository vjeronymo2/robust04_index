Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] By 13:00 on January 13, seven factions had been registered in the State Duma. One more faction - the Communist Party of the Russian Federation - appealed for registration, head of the State Duma's temporary secretariat Sergey Stankevich told the State Duma. He also announced that one group of deputies - the New Regional Policy - had been registered (see the 12:30 news bulletin for details). Stankevich presented information about the number of deputies in the registered associations (as of 13:00). Russia's Choice, led by Vice Premier Yegor Gaydar - 75 members; The New Regional Policy, led by Vladimir Medvedyev - 65 members; The faction of the Liberal-Democratic Party, led by Vladimir Zhirinovskiy - 63 members; The Agrarian Party faction (Mikhail Lapshin) - 55 members; The Communist Party faction (Gennadiy Zyuganov) - 45 members; The Party of Russian Unity and Concord (Sergey Shakhray) - 30 members; The faction of the Yavlinsky-Boldyrev-Lukin bloc (Grigoriy Yavlinskiy) - 25 members; Women of Russia (Yekaterina Lakhova) - 23 members; The faction of the Democratic Party of Russia (Nikolay Travkin) - 15 members. Stankevich noted, however, that those figures were not final because deputies continued to apply for membership in the State Duma factions.
