BFN [Text] Belchatow, April 25 -- "Even if the government withdraws or annuls the decision concerning transformations in brown coal mining and establishment of three holdings, the Solidarity strike committee of the brown coal mines will not end the strike but will only suspend it," Jozef Polkowski, a member of the strike committee, told a press conference at the Belchatow mine on Monday [25 April]. This would mean an increase of coal deliveries to power plants which now receive only strategic amounts of fuel necessary to keep them going. Polkowski added that after the decision has been either suspended or annulled, the strike committee might start negotiations concerning nation-wide demands of the trade union.
