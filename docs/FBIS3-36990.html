Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 11 TASS -- A telephone conversation of Russian President Boris Yeltsin and U.S. President Bill Clinton focused tonight on situation in Bosnia. The Presidents noted similarity of their approaches on many aspects of Bosnian settlement, the presidential press service reports. At the same time, Russia believes only the United Nations Security Council should take decisions on all issues related to the situation in Bosnia, including Sarajevo.
