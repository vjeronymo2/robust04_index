Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB Proposal: Pre-Foreclosure Sale Demonstration Program. Office: Housing. Description of the Need for the Information and its Proposed Use: Defaulting mortgagors with stagnant or declining property values can qualify to sell their homes at current fair market value to third-party buyers. HUD pays the shortfall between sales proceeds and mortgage indebtedness to the mortgagee via the claims process. Mortgagor avoids foreclosure; HUD saves foreclosure, maintenance and marketing costs.
