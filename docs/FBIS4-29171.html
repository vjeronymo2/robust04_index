BFN [Text] It is speculated that talks may possibly be held between the United states and the DPRK (North Korea) around late June on DPRK's suspected nuclear development. Regarding this speculation, Chief Cabinet Secretary Hiroshi Kumagai indicated his negative view at a news conference held on the morning of 15 June, and said: "Judging from the developments so far and the U.S. position, it is difficult to imagine that such talks will be held unless DPRK meets the demands of the international community."
