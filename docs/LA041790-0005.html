April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

GASOLINE TAX

Everyone wants to tax gasoline, state and federal. What happened to the 5 cents a gallon the Reagan Administration nailed on us?

In all my travels, I haven't seen any bridges being repaired and now they want more. Where do all the registration fees go? California's is probably the highest in the nation. There should be gold flakes on the highways.

People should elect some accountants instead of all lawyers, or one of these days some foreign country will buy the United States in bankruptcy court.

CARL MULSON

Lake Isabella
