930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Discussions over fate of royal train DEPARTMENT of Transport officials are discussing with Buckingham Palace what would happen to the royal train once the planned new regime of a track authority and franchise-holders operating services is in place. At present the train is run by British Rail. Meanwhile The royal yacht Britannia has lost its role as a stand-by military hospital ship, Mr Archie Hamilton, the armed forces minister, said last night.
