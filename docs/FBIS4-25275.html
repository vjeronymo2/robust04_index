BFN [Text] Beijing, June 8 (XINHUA) -- Chinese vice-premier and foreign minister Qian Qichen met here today with a delegation from the ASEAN Inter-Parliamentary Organization (AIPO), and Yi Sang-ok, former foreign minister of the Republic of Korea (ROK), respectively. The AIPO delegation, headed by AIPO Chairman Jose de Venecia, made the trip at the invitation of the Chinese National People's Congress Standing Committee. Yi Sang-ok was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
