Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 12 (SNARK) -- Today the Armenian Parliament accepted the 1994 privatization program suggested by the Armenian Government. There will be privatized the units of the uncompleted construction, agriculture, trade and public service. Speaking to the extraordinary session of the Armenian Parliament the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan accepted the fact that the Republic is essentially behind of the privatization process. "Armenia did only two serious `steps' in privatization -- there were privatized only flats and land", Levon Ter-Petrosyan declared. According to Levon Ter-Petrosyan, "the expected 1994 privatization of the state enterprises won't influence the Armenian economy because of the small volumes".
