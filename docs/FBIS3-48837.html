Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] On the night of 25 January, the people and the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK] took the initiative in attacking the Vietnamese communist puppet stronghold in Stoung District, Kompong Thom Province. The people believe that this bridgehead should be burned to prevent them from attacking us. Before midnight on 25 January, the people and the NADK surrounded and attacked the Stoung District township. After an hour of fighting, we had completely occupied the district market and destroyed the district office buildings, two barracks, a police quarters, six vehicles and armored carriers, and a rice warehouse. We killed seven cruel district officials; four puppet officers holding the rank of colonel, major, and captain; and 12 invading enemy soldiers. We also wounded a number of enemy soldiers and captured 38 others. The other puppet troops quickly fled for their lives from the Stoung District township. [passage omitted]
