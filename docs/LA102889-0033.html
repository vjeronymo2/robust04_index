October 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BAY AREA QUAKE AFTERMATH AND FREEWAY SAFETY

"Think hard about this Los Angeles (Orange County and San Diego) . . . how many more people are you going to squeeze into your little arid basin" ("In 15 Seconds, We Are Humbled," by Marc Reisner, Op-Ed Page, Oct. 19). For the sake of humankind, will you developers heed these words!

JIM MUDGE

San Diego
