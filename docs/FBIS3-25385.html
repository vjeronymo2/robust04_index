Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Harare Feb 16 SAPA -- A decision by Mexico to relocate its mission to South Africa has left Zimbabwe "very unhappy", says Zimbabwean Foreign Affairs Minister Nathan Shamuyarira. According to the Mexicans the decision was taken for economic reasons, reports ZIANA news agency. Last month a senior Mexican diplomat was quoted as saying the mission in Zimbabwe had been experiencing financial problems. Mr Shamuyarira said his government was also disappointed at the level of trade between Mexico and Zimbabwe, which he described as "very low".
