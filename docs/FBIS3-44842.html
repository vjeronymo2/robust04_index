Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi, January 7 (XINHUA) -- A delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) led by Wen Jiabao, an alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of its Secretariat, arrived here tonight for a six-day goodwill visit to India. During the visit which is at the invitation of the Indian National Congress Party (I), Wen is scheduled to meet Indian Congress Party leaders, Prime Minister P. V. Narasimha Rao and other senior government officials and exchange views with them on further development of relations between the two parties and the two countries. Sushil Kumar Shindhi, general secretary of Indian National Congress Party visited China in July 1993. It is the fifth visit of the CPC delegation to India since the establishment of relations between the two parties in 1985 and is also the most high ranking CPC delegation to visit India in recent years.
