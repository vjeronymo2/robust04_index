BFN [Text] Carlos Reis, chairman of the Mozambican National Union, Unamo, has said his political organization will boycott the first multiparty elections in Mozambique, scheduled for October. Reis said his movement's decision is due to the fact that the Mozambique Liberation Front, the international community, and the UN Operations in Mozambique have not allocated the money for the political parties' trust fund. Carlos Reis was speaking at a banquet held in the South African Embassy in Maputo marking the inauguration of Nelson Mandela.
