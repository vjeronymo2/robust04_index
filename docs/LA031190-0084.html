March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS;

CAL POLY PROFESSOR WINS EDUCATOR AWARD

Ken J. Schneider, professor of mechanical engineering at Cal Poly Pomona, has been named the 1990 Distinguished Engineering Educator by the Institute for the Advancement of Engineering.

The award is presented to an educator who has contributed significantly to engineering teaching and the profession through work in professional engineering organizations, civic activities and students' extracurricular engineering functions.
