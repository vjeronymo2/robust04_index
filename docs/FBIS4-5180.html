BFN ["`When the Party Is Determined, We Can Do Anything'" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 7 (KCNA) -- The slogan "When the Party Is Determined, We Can Do Anything!" is seen anywhere in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The slogan reflects the revolutionary will and stamina of the people who are determined to share the destiny with the Workers' Party of Korea (WPK) forever. The Korean people have moved mountains and harnessed the sea to carry out the party's decisions, displaying a revolutionary spirit in the historical course of socialist construction. Suffice it to take a glance at economic construction. When the WPK initiated the construction of the West Sea barrage which is of great importance for socialist economic construction, the Korean people and People's Army soldiers accepted the initiative as their own and turned out in its construction. It was a vast and difficult project which would have taken dozens of years with the established method. The project included the building of dam eight kilometres long across the sea scores of metres deep, three locks which can let ships of 2,000 to 50,000 ton class pass simultaneously, tens of sluice gates, railways and motorways on the dam. The soldiers and civilian builders moved ten odd mountains, disposed of 15 million cubic metres of earth, tamped two million cubic metres of concrete and collected and transported 16 million cubic metres of stones and gravels to build the dam of eternal value in five years with their own design, technology, materials and strength. With the construction of the barrage, the coastline diminished 20 kilometres and nine islands including Ok and Horang islands disappeared. The West Sea barrage is a precious product of the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard work of the Korean people who unconditionally accept and carry out the decisions of the party. When the party intended to make a 10,000-ton press, workers of Korea did it without hesitation. The workers of the Ryongsong combined general bureau of machinery wrought a miracle by completing the processing and assembling of the 10,000-ton press and successfully test-operating it in one year or more, though it would have taken 4-5 years according to the experience. When the party decided to launch a "200-day campaign" to glorify the 40th anniversary of the DPRK founding as a grand festival of victors, "200-day campaign youth death-defying corps of loyalty" were organised at more than 3,000 units across the country in the first ten days of the campaign. The entire people rose up as one and achieved great successes in all sectors of the national economy, giving full play to the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and hard work. Dressing plant No. 3 of Komdok with a capacity of 10 million tons built in one year on top of a mountain 1,200 metres above sea level, the Sangwon cement complex with a capacity of millions of tons, the Pyongbuk smeltery, a powerful non-ferrous metal producer, and many other edifices of eternal value are all associated with the feats of the Korean people and People's Army soldiers who accept the party's decisions as their revolutionary will.
