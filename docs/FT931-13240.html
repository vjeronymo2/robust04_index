930125 FT 25 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Wiltshier companies active WILTSHIER CONSTRUCTION GROUP has been awarded contracts worth over Pounds 30m. All Wiltshier regions are represented in a list which ranges from a Pounds 1.5m police station to be built for Eurotunnel at Folkestone in Kent to housing projects valued at a total of Pounds 2.5m in the Glasgow area. Education is represented in the form of a Pounds 1.5m infants school at Herne in Kent, a Pounds 2.1m lecture theatre block for the Metropolitan University of Leeds, and a Pounds 2.6m major education facility in Leeds. The group's recently established Midlands operation at Dudley is reflected in the award of a Pounds 2.8m contract for a 95-bed hotel in Cardiff for Friendly Hotel.
