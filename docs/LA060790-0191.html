June 7, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TURNOUT: The county registrar of voters expected a 50% turnout but got only 38.2% Tuesday -- putting Orange County 50th among the state's 58 counties. . . . Other Southern California counties did even worse: San Diego was 54th; Riverside, 55th; Los Angeles, 57th, and San Bernardino dead last.
