January 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PERTUSSIS REACTIONS

Question: If a child has had a bad reaction to the pertussis vaccine, should he not get any more shots? Or is it better to take the chance that the bad reaction won't happen again?

Answer: For children who have had a bad reaction to the diphtheria-tetanus-pertussis (DTP) vaccine, a vaccine is available that does not contain the pertussis portion.

Q: I can understand why aerobic exercises are supposed to be good for the heart and the lungs. What do all these people who do anaerobic exercises get out of them?

A: The main purpose of anaerobic exercise is to increase muscle strength. The greatest benefits to overall health come from aerobic exercise.

For Mrs. D.S., Montpelier, Vt.: Women who have reduced iron reserves have less energy and will tire faster during exercise.
