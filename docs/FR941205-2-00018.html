[Docket No. CP94&hyph;667&hyph;000] Texas Gas Transmission Corp.; Notice of Technical Conference November 29, 1994. Take notice that a technical conference has been scheduled in the above-captioned proceeding for 10:00 a.m. on December 7, 1994, in Room 3400 D, at the offices of the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, N.E., Washington, D.C. 20426. The purpose of the conference is to discuss matters of interest and concern relating to Texas Gas Transmission Corporation's proposal to expand the utilization of the CIPS/Marathon Delivery Point in order to make direct deliveries of gas to Marathon Oil Company's oil refinery in Robinson, Illinois. All interested parties are invited to attend. For additional information, interested parties may call Philip J. Veres at (202) 208&hyph;0049. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
