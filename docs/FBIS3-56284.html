Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 14 TASS -- Follows the text of the Russian-US statement on the Middle East: The Russian Federation and the United States of America, recognizing their special responsibility as cosponsors of the Middle East peace process, reiterate their firm commitment to achieving the goal of a comprehensive, just and lasting peace in the Middle East, based on resolutions 242 and 338 of the Security Council. In moving towards this goal, Russia and the United States, building on the progress to date in the peace progress, are determined to support the efforts of the parties to implement the Palestinian-Israeli declaration of principles. Implementation of the declaration of principles is critical to changing the relationship between the israelis and the Palestinians. Russia and the United States welcome progress achieved on the Jordanian-Israeli track of the peace process, and underscore their determination to help the parties make progress on the Syrian and Lebanese tracks as well. Both sides hope that in their quest for peace all participants in negotiations will display a high sense of responsibility, necessary flexibility and political farsightedness.
