920428 FT 28 APR 92 / FoE identifies waste dumps MORE THAN 450 waste dumps producing dangerous gases in England and Wales are identified in a report today from Friends of the Earth, the environmental lobby group. FoE says the dumps pose a risk to people and property because they may be explosive. The report was based on information obtained by FoE from the Waste Regulatory Authorities. However, FoE says that many authorities refused to supply information about sites in their area on grounds of confidentiality.
