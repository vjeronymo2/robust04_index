June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JANET ADKINS' CONTROVERSIAL ASSISTED SUICIDE

According to your article, the American Medical Assn. thinks it's OK for a doctor to starve a patient to death ("passive euthanasia"), but it's not OK for a doctor to provide the means for a patient to end his (her) life. What goes on here?

D.S. ALLEN

Pacific Palisades
