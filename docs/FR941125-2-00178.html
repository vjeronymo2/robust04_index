Limitations. Grants may not be used for: institution overhead academic research; acquisition of land, buildings, or capital equipment; general support of agency functions; or political activities. All grants should produce tangible results within one year. Deadlines. Proposals must be postmarked by January 31, 1995. Awards will be announced at the annual meeting of BR managers in the spring of 1995. Principals will receive from the U.S. MAB Secretariat copies of all U.S. MAB/BR review evaluations of their proposals and a written notification of the directorate's decision on their proposal.
