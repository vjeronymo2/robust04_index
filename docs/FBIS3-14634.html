Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother president Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] also received Boris Shchiborin, the Russian Federation ambassador in Tunis, on Monday [28 March] evening. The ambassador conveyed a message to the brother president from the Russian leadership. The recent developments on the implementation of Security Council's Resolution 904 in connection with the international protection for our people in the occupied territories were reviewed. The necessary measures for the resumption of the talks were also discussed.
