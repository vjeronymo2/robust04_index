920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Afghanistan applies to join Islamic body Afghanistan's new government has applied to join Iran, Pakistan, Turkey and five former Soviet republics in an Islamic regional economic club, the Iranian news agency IRNA said, Reuter reports from Nicosia. The application has to be approved by a foreign ministers' meeting of the Economic Co-operation Organisation, founded by Iran, Turkey and Pakistan in 1965. The group was dormant for years but in a summit in Tehran in February it admitted Moslem ex-Soviet republics of Azerbaijan, Kirgizia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan.
