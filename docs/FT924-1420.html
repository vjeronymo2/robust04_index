921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Lecturers move closer to link-up HIGHER education unions yesterday moved closer to a merger after the ruling council of AUT, the university lecturers' union, voted unanimously for a confederation with Natfhe, the university and college lecturers' union. AUT said: 'The confederation will give us greater strength and a united voice in higher education: united we stand, divided we fall.' Mr David Triesman, formerly Natfhe national negotiating secretary, was yesterday narrowly elected AUT general secretary.
