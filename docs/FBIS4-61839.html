BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Deputy Chairman of Estonian Riigikogu [parliament] Tunne Kelam told the 13th picket against continued Russian military presence in Estonia that the last Russian troops in Estonia "do not leave Estonia through the Narva bridge but plain-clothes Russian military absorb into Estonian society." He said that from the Russian military headquarters premises in Tallinn, in front of which the pickets take place, young plain clothes Russian military have been continuously seen to "absorb" to Tallinn. Kelam added that Estonia must not allow such a deception. He said that it was necessary to observe movement of people to the premises of Russian military and out of them.
