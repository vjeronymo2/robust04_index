920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Barclays raises business charges BARCLAYS BANK is to increase its charges to small and medium-sized businesses by between 6 per cent and 8 per cent from June 1. Small businesses with turnover of less than Pounds 100,000 face increases of 6 per cent charges, while companies with sales of more than Pounds 100,000 will see charges rise by 7 per cent to 8 per cent.
