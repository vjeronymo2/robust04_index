March 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pizza Boycott: A group of Detroit Tiger baseball fans trying to preserve Tiger Stadium called for a boycott of the Domino's Pizza chain owned by Tigers' owner Thomas Monaghan. The Tiger Stadium Fan Club said it took the action because of Monaghan's support for the construction of a new, open-air stadium. Monaghan came out in favor of a new stadium on Thursday, saying it would be in the best interest of Tiger fans.
