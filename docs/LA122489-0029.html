December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ANIMAL ACTIVISTS ARE CRITICIZED FOR IGNORING CONSTITUTIONAL AND ENVIRONMENTAL REALITIES

Regarding the article about animal furs, I agree that furs should not be obtained from endangered animals or through the use of inhumane traps, but animals raised for their fur are the same to me as animals raised for their meat.

If an activist leads an existence free of animal byproducts obtainable only through the animal's death, then and only then would I respect his opinion.

STAR MEYER

Los Angeles
