BFN [Excerpts] Today the official visit to France by a Latvian Government delegation ends. The delegation is led by Premier Valdis Birkavs. [passage omitted] During the visit there was discussion with French officials of possible cooperation between the two countries in the economic, defense, and security spheres, as well as Latvia's membership of the European stability pact. Today the delegation plans to meet parliamentarians of the French National Assembly, as well as the president of the biggest French water supply company.
