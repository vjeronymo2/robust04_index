931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: New TV outlet for Cosgrove Hall Anglia Television Entertainment has launched a new production company to be run by Mr Brian Cosgrove and Mr Mark Hall, the children's television animators. ATE is a joint venture set up earlier this year between the ITV franchise holder and Home Box Office, the programme division of Time Warner Entertainment. It will have a 75 per cent stake in Cosgrove Hall Films.
