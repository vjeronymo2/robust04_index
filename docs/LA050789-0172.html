May 7, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HELMETS CAN ALSO PROTECT BICYCLISTS FROM INJURIES

This is in regard of the controversy over helmets being required for motorcyclists. There is much evidence that proves that the helmet can save the rider from serious injury. I am sure that people who ride motorcycles are well aware of the dangers and preventive measures.

These riders that refuse to wear (helmets) want their freedom from the burden. It is obvious they do not mind if they fall and crack their head open. So why should we?

There is really no point to press the issue forward if there is so much resistance. It does not make sense why someone would want to make such a big deal over it if they do not even ride bikes.

STEVEN KIKAWA

Laguna Niguel
