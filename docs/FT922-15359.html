920401 FT 01 APR 92 / UK Company News: Inishtech improves 14% to IPounds 7.09m Inishtech, the Dublin-based disposable products maker, improved pre-tax profits by 14 per cent to IPounds 7.09m (Pounds 6.6m) in the year to December 31 1991. Profits in 1990 were IPounds 6.21m. Fully diluted earnings rose to 36.4p (33.3p) per share and a dividend of 5.5p (5p) is recommended. Turnover advanced to IPounds 40.1m (IPounds 33.6m).
