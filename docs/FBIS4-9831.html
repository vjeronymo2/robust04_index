BFN [Report by Donald Gonzalez] [Text] The Constitutionality Court issued a statement today. The document states, as you all know, that Constitutionality Court President Epaminondas Gonzalez Dubon was murdered yesterday and that the following points must be duly considered: The first point strongly condemns the murder of Constitutionality Court President Epaminandas Gonzalez Dubon. The second point is very important. The Constitutional Court warns authorities to refrain from labeling the crime a priority before concluding the investigations because this [words indistinct] the magnitude of the crime but also mars the victim's outstanding honor. Third, it expresses its sincere condolences to Gonzalez' relatives, and fourth it will send a copy of this statement to the constitutional courts of friendly countries. The Constitutionality Court issued this statement today on the murder of its president, Magistrate Epaminondas Gonzalez Dubon. It is signed by acting President (Daniel Larios Cifuentes) and Magistrates Edmundo Vasquez Martinez, Jose Antonio Monzono Juarez, Manuel Arturo Garcia, Carlos Enrique Reinoso Sill, and (Abel Gonzalez Rivas).
