920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Higher education policy attacked BUSINESS leaders yesterday condemned government higher-education policy as 'unrealistic' and said an increase in funding would be needed to cope with a projected rise in student numbers. The Council for Industry and Higher Education said capital spending of Pounds 2bn to Pounds 3bn would be needed to meet the projected rise in numbers. Investing in Diversity.
