921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Hardly a ringing endorsement - In a Nutshell A quarter of small businesses do not answer their phone even after a total of 15 rings, according to a survey of 7,000 small businesses carried out by Thomson Directories. Twenty-three per cent of businesses contacted did not answer their phone within five and 15 rings while 26 per cent did not answer after 15 rings.
