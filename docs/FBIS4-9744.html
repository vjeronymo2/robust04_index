BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Paraguay monitored through 24 March. Taiwan has agreed to a $10 million "soft" loan that will be earmarked for small and medium-size industries, especially in the country's interior. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 18 Mar 94 p 21) According to the Paraguayan Central Bank, its net international reserves totaled $682.6 million in February. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 27 Mar 94 p 20) Engineer Luis Alberto Meyer, the Pilcomayo River National Commission director, believes Paraguay and Argentina will require $18 million for two projects on the Pilcomayo River that must be implemented this year. Paraguayan and Argentine experts have already asked for the EEC's financial cooperation in the projects. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 28 Mar 94 p 17)
