921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Charter asset value ahead At November 30 the net asset value of Kleinwort Charter Investment Trust stood at 191.37p per share, against 172.25p a year earlier. Net revenue in the 12 months amounted to Pounds 4.17m (Pounds 4.44m), equivalent to earnings of 5.14p (5.49p) per share. An unchanged final dividend of 3.75p has been proposed for a maintained total of 5p.
