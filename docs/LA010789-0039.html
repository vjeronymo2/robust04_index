January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Continental Airlines announced that it will begin service on the Seattle-Tokyo route April 26, with daily Boeing 747 flights . . . Times Mirror Co. said it has completed the previously reported acquisition of Zenger-Miller Inc., a San Jose, Calif.-based, professional training company, for an undisclosed price . . . Smith & Nephew PLC has completed the acquisition of Azusa-based Ioptex Research Inc., an intraocular lens manufacturer, for $236 million.
