940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: MMI MMI is buying MMC Group, public relations company, for Pounds 40,000 cash. For year ended March 31 1993 turnover was Pounds 1.7m and pre-tax profits Pounds 25,000. Also deferred cash consideration equal to 10 per cent of fee income from new clients earned by MMC over next two years up to maximum Pounds 100,000.
