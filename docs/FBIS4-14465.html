BFN [Text] Kiev -- A UNIAN correspondent learned today from reliable sources that run-off elections to Ukraine's Supreme Council will take place on 3 April in 47 districts of eight of Ukraine's oblasts, and on 10 April in 359 districts. Run-off elections to Ukraine's Supreme Council will also take place on 10 April in 18 districts of the Republic of Crimea and in all 28 districts of Kharkov Oblast.
