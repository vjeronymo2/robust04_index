930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Union Jack Oil in Dollars 9.8m disposal Union Jack Oil has sold its 7.5 per cent interest in UK North Sea block 29/5b to Texaco Britain for Dollars 9.8m (Pounds 6.9m) cash and a 3.5 per cent interest in the Claymore field. Directors said the cash would be used to repay debt and to fund future exploration, appraisal and development activities. Union would benefit, they added, from the positive cash flow which the interest in the Claymore field was expected to generate.
