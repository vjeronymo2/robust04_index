941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / International Company News: Water group loan General Utilities, holding company for the UK water company assets of Compagnie Generale des Eaux of France, has returned to the syndicated loan market with a Pounds 120m revolving credit facility arranged by Chemical Bank, writes Martin Brice in London. Chemical Bank said the loan would replace a Pounds 160m deal it arranged in 1991 for the company.
