January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

5TH NAVIGATION SATELLITE TO BE PLACED IN ORBIT

The Air Force announced Monday that it will launch the fifth of a new type of military navigation satellite Wednesday.

The $65-million Navstar satellite is to be carried aloft by a Delta 2 rocket to join a network of spacecraft that can tell American and allied military forces where they are to within 50 feet of any location, regardless of weather.
