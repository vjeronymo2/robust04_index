April 25, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

LOS ANGELES ELECTION

These days voting sites are difficult to obtain. I wish other businesses would follow the example of the mid-Wilshire branch of Merrill Lynch Realty Co. They graciously allowed a portion of their premises to be used as a polling site, furnishing election workers with a fine place to work.

There is a dire need for workers on Election Day. Young people and men in particular should volunteer to work the elections. They pay is small, but the satisfaction derived is wonderful.

ELEANOR GAFFNEY

Los Angeles
