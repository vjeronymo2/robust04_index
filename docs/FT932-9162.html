930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / London Stock Exchange: Shell hits new high on results FIRST-QUARTER results ahead of even the most optimistic forecasts sent Shell Transport surging on exceptionally heavy turnover. Many dealers found it hard to fill the sheer weight of buy orders yesterday and turnover hit 10m shares by the middle of the morning. At the close, the shares were through the 600p barrier, ending a net 20 1/2 higher at 612 1/2 p, an all-time peak, with 16m traded. The rise accounted for more than 4 points on the FT-SE 100 Index. Analysts found it difficult to resist bumping up forecasts. The end-of-year target was lifted as high as Pounds 3.5bn, with some of the more optimistic observers arguing that the shares could hit 700p in the short term. Mr Nick Antill of Hoare Govett, who now predicts Pounds 3.2bn for the year-end and a dividend of 24.5p, said: 'I think these figures are as good as they look.'
