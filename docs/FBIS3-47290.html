Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 9 (KCNA) -- The Mozambique Liberation Front Party fully supports the principle and way of Korea's independent and peaceful reunification clarified by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in his New Year address for this year, said Feliciano Salomao Gundana, secretary general of the party Central Committee, when he met with the Korean ambassador on January 4. He said Korea should be reunified by the confederacy formula and the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula solved through DPRK-U.S. talks. "The United States and the South Korean authorities must refrain from laying obstacles in the way of Korea's reunification and take a sincere position and attitude towards the cause of reunification," he added.
