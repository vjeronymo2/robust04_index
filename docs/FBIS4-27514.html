BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- Japanese authorities' suppression of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) has come under fire in different countries. The Mozambican Committee for Friendship With the People of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in a statement May 20 said the Japanese reactionaries have perpetrated a vicious anti-Chongnyon campaign, while scheming to hurl their Armed Forces into the Korean peninsula by drawing up a "security emergency legislative arrangement", but they are greatly mistaken. The Japanese reactionaries must correctly understand the socialist Korea, the statement stressed, and added: If they do not stop at once their reckless anti-DPRK, anti-Chongnyon campaign, they will bump into curses and condemnations from the Korean people and the world people. The Nepal-Korea Friendship Association in a statement May 20 said the police search of the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon by the Japanese authorities was part of their political intrigues to suppress Chongnyon and a crude violation of the democratic national rights of the Korean citizens in Japan. The Nigeria-DPRK Friendship Association in a statement May 17 strongly urged the Japanese authorities to renounce the hostile policy towards the DPRK and stop at once the suppression of Chongnyon and apologize for it.
