920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / UK Company News: St Modwen chief invests in offshoot Mr Stanley Clarke has invested Pounds 1.56m in Uttoxeter Leisure & Development, a subsidiary of St Modwen Properties of which he is chairman. As a result St Modwen's holding in Uttoxeter, which operates Uttoxeter Racecourse, is reduced from 81 per cent to 32 per cent. St Modwen will be repayed a Pounds 479,000 loan and its balance sheet will lose Uttoxeter's debt to third parties of Pounds 262,000.
