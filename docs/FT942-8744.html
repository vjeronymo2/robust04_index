940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / Unions protest at pay divergence Union leaders pointed yesterday to the growing divergence between private-sector and public-sector earnings. Figures from Mr Chris Trinder of the Public Finance Foundation show that private sector earnings in March were rising at twice the rate of inflation while continuing restraint in the public sector has held rises to 0.8 per cent below inflation. Mr Trinder said the government was being far too complacent about the upward movement in private sector pay. 'This is not just bonuses and overtime, as the government says. The growth of performance-related pay seems to be pushing up pay while basic settlements remain low,' he said.
