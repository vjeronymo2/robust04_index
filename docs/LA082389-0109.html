August 23, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Computer Firm Selects Division Headquarters: Next Inc., a computer company founded by former Apple Computer Chairman Steven P. Jobs, has picked the Union Trust Building in downtown Pittsburgh to be its eastern division headquarters, a company executive said. "A few dozen jobs" are expected initially at the Pittsburgh office, a spokesman said. "Over the course of Next's growth it could be 100 to 200 jobs, but nobody really knows that," he said. Carnegie Mellon University of Pittsburgh owns a small stake in Next.
