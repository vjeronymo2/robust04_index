BFN [Text] Beijing, May 17 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC) and president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF), met with and hosted a luncheon for a women delegation from Myanmar [Burma] here today. The four-member delegation, led by Kyu Kyu Swe, first vice-president of the Myanmar Maternal and Child Welfare Association, has been here as guests of the ACWF.
