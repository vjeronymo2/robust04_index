940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Rank Leisure gets the Zoo & the Cage Allied Leisure, the nightclub and ten-pin bowling group, has sold two nightclubs, The Zoo & the Cage in Bournemouth and The Venue in Poole and the Colonnades bar in Poole to Rank Leisure for a total of Pounds 4.5m The premises contributed profits of Pounds 690,000 before interest and tax for the 11 months ended June 19.
