920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / Market for health foods doubles THE MARKET in health and slimming foods has more than doubled over five years, a report from Euromonitor says. The report suggests that sales of health foods such as vitamins, organic produce, slimming and dietetic aids have grown at an average annual rate of 30 per cent since 1986 to Pounds 492m last year. Germans spend an average of Dollars 32 (Pounds 16.20) a year on such foods, buying more than twice as much as Britons (Dollars 15). Italians buy less than half as much as people in the UK (Dollars 6).
