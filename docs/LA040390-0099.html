April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Perahia Won't Play: Pianist Murray Perahia, scheduled to play a recital in the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion of the Music Center tonight, has the flu and has canceled his appearance, according to a spokesperson for the Los Angeles Philharmonic, sponsor of the event. Ticketholders may obtain refunds at the Philharmonic box office, or write Box Office, 135 N. Grand, Los Angeles 90012. BETH KLEID
