Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, Feb 15 (AFP) -- A key meeting between the top commanders of the Bosnian Moslem and Serb forces at Sarajevo airport Tuesday [15 February] was cancelled after the Bosnian Moslem leader failed to show up. General Rasim Delic, head of the mostly Moslem Bosnian army, has left for central Bosnia and could not attend the meeting, Bosnian Vice President Ejup Ganic told reporters here. "We shall not be sending representatives to this meeting," he added.
