920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / Allegations against Ramos swept aside A JOINT session of the Philippine Congress yesterday formally proclaimed Mr Fidel Ramos the country's next president, sweeping aside allegations by rival candidates of large-scale fraud, Reuter reports from Manila. Film star Mr Joseph Estrada was proclaimed vice president in the session which capped a convoluted six-week counting process following the May 11 national elections. Mr Ramos, 64, a former defence chief, takes over from Mrs Corazon Aquino on June 30.
