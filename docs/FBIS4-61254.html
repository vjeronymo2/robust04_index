BFN [Text] Dushanbe 5 May TASS -- Russian border guards curtailed by artillery barrage an attempt of Tajik militants to break through the Tajik-Afghan border on Wednesday night. The attempt was made at the site of the 10th frontier post patrolled by the Pyandj border-guarding unit, a spokesman for the headquarters of the Russian Frontier Troops in Tajikistan told ITAR-TASS. No losses in Russian personnel were reported.
