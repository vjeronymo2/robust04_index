Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 19 (ATA) -- The chairman of the parliamentary Commission for the Foreign Policy, Mr. Eduard Selami, received yesterday the foreign minister of FYROM [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia], Mr. Stevo Crvenkovski. During the meeting, both persons exchanged opinions about the development of the bilateral relations in the political and economical fields. "The Republic of Macedonia as well as the Republic of Albania is experiencing today great political changes and especially economical changes, said Mr. Crvenkovski. This calls for a closer cooperation between the two countries in this fields, which will serve to the aim of having the same approach in the international arena." Mr. Selami said that Albania also is interested on the strengthening of the bilateral relations, which serves both our countries and the peace in the Balkan region. He repeated once more the request for the guarantee of the rights of the Albanians in Macedonia, rights which are recognized from the international documents.
