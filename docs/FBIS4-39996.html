BFN [Text] Ukraine may soon be a leading exporter of its most sophisticated weapons, says the report by the analytical center of the election team of the candidate for presidency Ivan Plushch, which was released in Kiev on Sunday [12 June]. The document says the most promising export item may be the Kharkov-produced tank T-84 which surpasses in performance characteristics, western tanks and the Russian one, T-80. Experts believe that Ukrainian submarines, air defence missile systems and missile complexes for Navy have good export characteristics. Analysts believe, in 1995 Ukraine plans to start the production of a new submachine gun on the basis of the Kalashnikov. It surpasses the base model by many parameters. The document says that Asia may be the most promising market for Ukrainian armaments. In this respect, Ukraine may be interested in the proposal by the Indian company, Resources Argumentation, which is prepared to give Ukraine a long-term credit for the development of the Ukrainian industry.
