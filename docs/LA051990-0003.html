May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AND ANGEL FANS ARE VERY UNHAPPY, TOO

How convenient it must be for Gene Autry to have both his Western Heritage Museum and Anaheim Stadium to house expensive relics from the past. With the Angels acquiring another high-priced, over-the-hill superstar in Dave Winfield, Autry and Mike Port will have to decide when to make Winfield's transition from the Big A to a nice display case at the museum.

DAN ROSMAN, Redondo Beach
