940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / Malaysian sultan sued A Singaporean businesswoman sued a Malaysian sultan for libel yesterday over a land-sale dispute, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Ms Faridah Begum Abdullah filed the suit against Sultan Ahmad Shah of Pahang at the Malaysian supreme court registry in Kuala Lumpur. The case will be heard in a special court set up after the constitution was amended to strip royalty of legal immunity in their personal capacities last year.
