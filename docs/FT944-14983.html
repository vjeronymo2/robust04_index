941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: L&G moves into investment trust market Legal & General is joining the ranks of unit trust managers expanding into the main-stream retail investment trust market. Its first offering is a recovery investment trust, to be run by the same manager as its existing UK Recovery unit trust. The Pounds 89m unit trust, managed by Ms Lesley Hooper, has been in the top quartile of UK equity growth unit trusts over one, three and five years to October 1, according to Micropal statistics. The group hopes to raise between Pounds 25m and Pounds 50m for the new fund from a placing and public offer, opening towards the end of November.
