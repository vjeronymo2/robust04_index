Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The North Korean Christian Federation has informed its ROK counterpart of its decision not to dispatch a delegation to the 43rd general meeting of the ROK Christian Church Council which is slated to be opened in Seoul on 21 February, disclosed the Christian Church Council on 14 February. The North Korean Christian Federation, in a message sent via facsimile on 14 February, said: "The civilian-level exchange between the North and South has hit the wall since the South Korean authorities, under the pretext of the nuclear issue, held off our delegation from attending the funeral service for Rev. Mun Ik-hwan," adding, "It is regrettable that we are not able to send the delegation to the general meeting."
