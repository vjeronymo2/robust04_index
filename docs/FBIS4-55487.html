BFN [Text] After fierce fighting on the Kladanj front, the free territory of Bosnia-Herzegovina has been expanded by 32 square kilometers. Sabotaging the plans of chetnik generals Milovanovic and Krstic, the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army fighters smashed the Chetnik offensive and penetrated deep into the occupied territory toward Zeravica and Vlasenica. Our units have progressed to the line of (Sokoli), [words indistinct], and (Bijela Stijena). All attempts by the Chetniks to recapture the lost territory have been in vain. Over 100 chetniks were killed in this fighting, 20 of whom were [word indistinct], and the number of the wounded is several times higher. The result of this operation is two tanks, two mobile antiaircraft weapons, two 105-mm howitzers, and one antiaircraft gun destroyed. The chetnik units in this region have been completely smashed, and the volunteers from Serbia are fleeing to where they came from in the first place.
