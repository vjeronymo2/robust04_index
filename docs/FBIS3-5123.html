Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 4 (KCNA) -- Miguel Puentes, charge d'affaires ad interim, and officials of the Cuban Embassy here, Thursday met with Yi In-mo, the incarnation of faith and will. Miguel Puentes said the South Korean puppets could not break the indomitable faith and will of Yi In-mo despite all their attempts to make him recant his belief. The indomitable fighting spirit shown by Yi is a precious example for the revolutionaries of the whole world, he stressed. Yi said he is leading a happy life to his heart's content as he has the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. He expressed thanks to the fraternal Cuban people who had vigorously conducted the solidarity movement for his repatriation.
