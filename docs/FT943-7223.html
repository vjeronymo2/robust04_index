940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / International Capital Markets: MTI climbs to TDollars 159m profit Microelectronics Technology, Taiwan's leading producer of satellite communication and thin-film microwave components, posted pre-tax profits of TDollars 159m (USDollars 6m) for the first half of 1994, up 19.5 per cent from a year earlier, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei. Revenues climbed 23.5 per cent from one year ago to TDollars 1.19bn in the year to June 30.
