September 9, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GYPSUM CANYON IDEAL LOCATION FOR MUCH-NEEDED, NEW O.C. JAIL

It's time for NIMBY to stop. The desert jail is a pipe dream.

The best jail site is Gypsum Canyon. We've already spent millions in planning for that site.

Criminals are being let loose because of NIMBY, and that's not fair to our kids. Let's build a jail in Gypsum Canyon now!

SUZANNE HUTCHINSON

Huntington Beach
