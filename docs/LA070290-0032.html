July 2, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Hollywood, target of the David Mamet comedy "Speed-the-Plow," has been offered the olive branch at South Coast Repertory in Costa Mesa, which is presenting the play.

Members of film-related unions will receive a free ticket with each one purchased for today's and Tuesday's performances. Members need only show their cards from the Producers, Directors, Writers, Editors, Photographers or Screen Actors Guilds. Information: (714) 957-4033.
