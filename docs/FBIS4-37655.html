BFN [Text] Baghdad, June 23, INA -- AL-THAWRAH newspaper of Baghdad said the natural state that realizes peace and security to Iraqi Kurds is the state that rejects concepts of the traitors and agents and commits to Iraq's integrity. A paper article here today said that events taking place in north Iraq scoff at the reported cessation of fighting between the cliques of Mas'ud Barzani and Jalal Talabani. The paper referred to the ongoing operations of kidnapping, physical liquidation and the state of panic and fear that had motivated Kurdish families to evict their homes and villages. The paper said the tragedy of Kurds in the north where cliques of Barzani and Talabani were fighting each other, would continue in their presence, bringing further daily bitter tortures and catastrophes.
