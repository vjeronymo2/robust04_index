BFN [Excerpt] The Army has categorically denied that its commander in chief, Captain General Augusto Pinochet Ugarte, is traveling with a false passport "because he is traveling with an official passport issued by the Chilean Government to public authorities fulfilling official duties abroad." As for reports that Pinochet had to hastily check out of a hotel in the Netherlands, the Army stated through a communique that the reports "are inaccurate" because the high-ranking official "has fulfilled the scheduled itinerary without any changes." The Army communique ends by stating that in Prague, capital of the Czech Republic, Gen. Pinochet was received by Chilean Ambassador Rolando Stein and the Omnipol Company president, who held a reception in his honor. [passage omitted]
