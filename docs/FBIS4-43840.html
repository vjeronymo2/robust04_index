CSO [Unattributed article: "Centrist Opponents of EU Form Organization"] [Text] A group of Finnish Centrists who oppose European Union [EU] membership formed their own organization on Tuesday [7 June] to deal with the issue. Responsibility for Finland, Inc. plans to put in a highly visible appearance at the Center Party congress, among other places. Chief editor Kari Hokkanen is the chairman of the organization, MP Raili Puhakka is serving as vice chairman, and MP Markku Vuorensola as secretary.
