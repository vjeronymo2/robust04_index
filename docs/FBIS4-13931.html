BFN [Text] An attack by Dostam's militia was pushed back in (Garzedana) district, Faryab Province. A report from 'Abdol Karim Arshud, a courageous mojahedin commander, says that this morning at 0500 the Dostam militia launched a broad attack against mojahedin positions but the enemy were forced to retreat, leaving 24 dead. The report adds that sixteen militiamen were captured alive by the mojahedin. One combat machine [as heard] and 16 personnel carriers belonging to the enemy were destroyed. The report adds that six militiamen's corpses are still lying on the battlefield. During the fighting, over 20 heroic mojahedin received light injuries.
