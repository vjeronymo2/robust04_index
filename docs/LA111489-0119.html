November 14, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ONLY IN O.C.: The Irvine Co. didn't exactly get the results it expected when it defended its controversial Laguna Laurel project in big ads last weekend. . . . As thousands marched to protest the proposed development, the company urged readers to phone for its side of the story. . . . Instead, most of the 60 people who called Monday didn't want a brochure -- they wanted to get on a waiting list for the houses. Steve Emmons and Dana Parsons
