BFN [Text] Kuwait, 9 Apr -- 'Abd al-'Aziz al-Dakhil, minister of state for cabinet affairs, has announced that the members of the Kuwaiti Government today tendered their resignations to the heir apparent and prime minister so as to carry out the required government reshuffle. The minister of state for cabinet affairs told KUNA that a meeting was held today in the presence of all the ministers, during which the ministers studied the comprehensive speech that was delivered by the heir apparent and the prime minister to the Kuwaiti people last Monday [4 April], particularly the paragraph pertaining to the need for a government reshuffle in harmony with the requirements of the coming phase. Al-Dakhil announced that the ministers agreed to give the heir apparent an opportunity to carry out the required reshuffle. To this end, all the ministers then submitted their resignations to the heir apparent and prime minister, who is expected to take the necessary measures in this regard. The minister of state for cabinet affairs added that all ministers will remain in their posts until the issuance of the appropriate decrees on the new government composition.
