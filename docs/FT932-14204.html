930420 FT 20 APR 93 / UK Company News: Securities Trust of Scotland lifts net asset value by 20.5% Net asset value of Securities Trust of Scotland increased by 20.5 per cent to 84.5p over the year to March 31. Net revenue after tax for the year rose marginally from Pounds 9.69m to Pounds 9.78m. Earnings were just a shade better at 3.03p (3p) a share and the dividend total is held at 3.25p with a final of 2.17p.
