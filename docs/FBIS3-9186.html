Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The manner in which Meciar's government carried out privatization has been sharply criticized. Our colleague Renata Havranova, editor of PRACE daily, has filed this telephone despatch from Bratislava: [Havranova] The new finance minister, Rudolf Filkus, spoke very critically in an interview today about the privatization methods of former Prime Minister Meciar, in particular about the practices applied in the period starting on 14 February, when efforts to pass a vote of no confidence in Meciar's government became obvious in the Slovak parliament. Over two days, Meciar managed to sell 30 of the most lucrative Slovak enterprises. All of them have gone to his supporters. [passage omitted]
