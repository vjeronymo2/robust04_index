November 18, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS: FORMER EXECUTIVE REJOINS DISTRIBUTED LOGIC IN NEW POST

Dennis I. Edwards has rejoined Distributed Logic Corp., an Anaheim computer products company, in the newly created post of executive vice president.

Edwards had previously been chief operating officer for Future Domain Inc.

In his new position, he is responsible for Distributed Logic's worldwide marketing and sales activities.

Edwards had originally joined the company in 1981 as western regional sales manager.

He was named North American sales manager in 1983 and became vice president of European sales in 1984. He was named senior vice president for worldwide sales in 1985 and held that post until he left in 1989.
