May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: REGION;

COLDBROOK CAMP REOPENS

Coldbrook Campground in the Angeles National Forest, which has been closed since February, will be reopened beginning this weekend. The campground is 18 miles north of Azusa on California 39.

The campground, which accepts no reservations, has 25 campsites available for $8 per day.
