BFN [Text] Konstantin Trenchev, chairman of the Podkrepa Labor Confederation, today met with Ahmed Dogan, leader of the Movement for Rights and Freedoms [DPS], and informed him that continuing support for this cabinet is politically damaging and that the cabinet has clearly demonstrated its inability to rule the country. After the meeting Konstantin Trenchev told reporters about this position. The meeting was requested by Ahmed Dogan. Probably at its next Federative Council Podkrepa will review its relations with the DPS, because it appears that the movement is cooperating with the Communists in its decision to support the cabinet, Dr. Trenchev said.
