Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Addressing a meeting of the World Jewish Congress in Washington yesterday, Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin said that if the government is defeated in the referendum on peace with Syria, it will quit on the spot. [Begin Beilin recording, in English] If the results of the referendum will be negative, we will resign the same day, and then another government which doesn't believe that it is possible to make peace in our generation will take the reins of the rule and will decide for the future of Israel. [end recording]
