Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] (Li Hu Wa), responsible for the Beijing town hall and Beijing's economic reform, today stated that Cuban economic and commercial representatives are welcome to participate in business operations in Beijing. (Li Hu Wa) extended the invitation during an interview with PRENSA LATINA when he said that cooperation with other countries is the main objective of Beijing officials. (Li Hu Wa) referred to cooperation with Cuba when he stressed the effort being made to accelerate the reform and opening process, the objectives of which are cooperation with foreign investing companies and modernization of national institutions. Economic and commercial relations between China and Cuba have increased over the past few years with the exchange of machinery, foodstuffs, medicines, and medical equipment.
