Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Mar (MENA)--MENA has learned that Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, met a short while ago with General Amnon Shahaq, Israel's deputy chief of staff. According to well-informed sources, Sha'th and Shahaq discussed the deteriorating situation in the occupied territories as a result of mounting Israeli terror against Palestinian citizens. According to the same sources, Palestinian-Israeli negotiations concerning security arrangements and international protection in Hebron, which were due to begin this afternoon in Cairo, have not started yet.
