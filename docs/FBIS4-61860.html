BFN [Text] President Yeltsin approved the recommendation by the defense committee of the State Duma on the necessity of increasing defense spending in the budget for the current year to 55 trillion rubles. The recommendation along with Yeltsin's "I consider it expedient to agree" were sent to Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin; according to a well-informed source in the parliament's budget, tax, banks and finance committee. In the draft state budget presented by the government to the State Duma the item for defense totals 37 trillion rubles. Last week several rounds of consultations on this issue were held between representatives of the Duma's defense committee, the Defense Ministry, the Economics Ministry, the Finance Ministry, and the Security Council of the Russian Federation. These consultations were coordinated by the president's aide on national security Uri Baturin.
