Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow February 17 TASS--The decree on the coverage of the State Duma activities by federal TV and radio broadcasting companies was adopted by the lower chamber of the Federal Assembly on Thursday [17 February]. Under the decree, the State Duma Committee on the Information Policy and Communications has been instructed to submit to the lower chamber of the Federal Assembly before March 21, 1994 a draft federal law on the procedure of the coverage by state mass media of the activity of the Russian state authorities. The decree envisages that before this law goes into effect, it is necessary to endorse a rough schedule of the coverage of State Duma activities by television. The schedule should be coordinated with Ostankino company and the Russian Television. The committee for organising the work of the State Duma jointly with the Duma Committee on the Information Policy and Communications have been instructed to organise the press service of the State Duma and to create the necessary conditions for preparing television and radio programmes with the use of the equipment of the Russian television-parliament complex. On Wednesday the State Duma decided on the further working out of the decree. All the amendments to the decree submitted by chairman of the Duma Committee of the Information Policy and Communications, Mikhail Poltoranin, were turned down by deputies at a meeting on Thursday.
