Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From "O Estado column" by Jose Negreiros] [Text] During a 45-minute audience with [Foreign Minister] Celso Amorim yesterday, Chilean Foreign Minister Enrique Silva Cimma took barely five minutes to transfer the Rio Group Executive Secretariat to Brazil. The rest of the time was spent reviewing the case of Brazilian citizen Tania Vaz, who has been tortured in Santiago de Chile under the charges of terrorism. Vaz's liberation is almost an obsession for President Itamar Franco.
