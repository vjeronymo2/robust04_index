Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Ukraine did not sign any military agreements at the meeting of CIS heads of state in Ashgabat in December last year. Colonel General Ivan Bizhan, Ukraine's deputy defense minister, said the Ukrainian military delegation's aim was to establish personal contacts with colleagues from CIS countries. Military accords will be signed in the near future with Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan.
