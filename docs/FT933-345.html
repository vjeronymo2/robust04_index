930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / International Company News: Carlyle Group seeks fresh finance THE CARLYLE Group, a Washington-based investment banking group with former US secretary of state Mr James Baker among its partners, is to seek fresh institutional investment in Europe in the next few weeks to help finance a new Dollars 500m buy-out fund, writes Alan Friedman in New York. Mr George Soros, the New York-based investor, has agreed to put up the first Dollars 100m 'anchor' investment in the new fund, according to Mr David Rubenstein, a managing partner of Carlyle. Among Carlyle's holdings is Vought Aircraft, the Texas-based company that consists of the former aircraft division of LTV. Carlyle also manages defence contractors, airline catering businesses and a book distributor.
