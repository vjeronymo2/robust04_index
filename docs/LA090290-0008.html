SESAME STREET GOES RETAIL

LIKE other show- biz stars, Big Bird, Bert, Ernie and the rest of the gang are now bi-coastal.

The Sesame Street General Store -- the first of its kind anywhere -- opens this month in the South Coast Plaza, showcasing puppets, books and other products. Kids will feel right at home; the store re-creates Bert and Ernie's apartment, at 123 Sesame Street, and Mr. Hooper's General Store.

Sesame Street General Store, South Coast Plaza, 3333 Bristol St., Costa Mesa; (714) 241-1700.
