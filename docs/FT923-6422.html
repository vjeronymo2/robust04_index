920825 FT 25 AUG 92 / Iran to supply gas to Azerbaijan Iran will supply natural gas and other fuels to the Nakhichevan region of Azerbaijan, the Iranian news agency IRNA reported yesterday. Nakhichevan, sandwiched between Iran, Armenia and Turkey, is facing a fuel shortage because of a blockade by Armenia, which has been fighting an undeclared war with Azerbaijan over the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh.
