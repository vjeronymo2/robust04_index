Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Nikolay Kireyev report from the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" roundup: "Oblast Duma To Replace Soviet"] [Text] An Oblast Duma will take the place of the Oblast Soviet of People's Deputies in Smolensk. Administration Chief Anatoliy Glushenkov signed a decree on the representative (legislative) organ of state power, and provisional statutes on the Duma and the election of deputies have been ratified. At its sitting the Orel Oblast Soviet of People's Deputies session examined the questions of reforming the representative organs of power and local self-government. Statutes were ratified concerning the Oblast Duma, local self-government for the transitional period, and elections to these organs of power. They are scheduled for 20 March 1994.
