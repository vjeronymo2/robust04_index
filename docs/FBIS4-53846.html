BFN [Editorial Report] Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 1300 GMT on 19 May carries a one-minute report that Cuban Ambassador to the DPRK Jose Ramon Rodriguez Verona and embassy staff helped with farm work at the DPRK-Cuban Friendship Hwasong Cooperative Farm on 19 May. The report notes that the ambassador expressed the "firm belief" that the Korean people will reap a "rich harvest" this year because they are upholding the "great party" and "great leaders."
