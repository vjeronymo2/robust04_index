Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 7 Feb (XINHUA) -- Decree No. 146 of the State Council of the People's Republic of China: The "Regulations of the State Council on Working Hours for Staff Members and Workers" were passed at the 15th executive meeting of the State Council on 24 January 1994 and are hereby promulgated for implementation effective 1 March 1994. [Signed] Premier Li Peng [Dated] 3 February 1994
