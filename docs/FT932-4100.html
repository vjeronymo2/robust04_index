930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / Survey shows top executives eighth in European after-tax pay league TOP EXECUTIVES of small and medium-sized UK companies are only eighth in the European after-tax pay league, the 1993 survey of top management compensation by Wyatt Data Services shows. Based on the head of a company with a turnover of Pounds 68m, the UK salary is Pounds 34,520 less than for a comparable position in Switzerland, which tops the league table.
