931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / International Company News: Power Corp in Dollars 45m Chinese investment Power Corp of Canada, the chief holding company of Montreal financier Mr Paul Desmarais, and China International Trust and Investment have formed a 50/50 joint venture to invest an initial CDollars 60m (USDollars 45.4m) in a new industrial park at Pudong near Shanghai, writes Robert Gibbens. CITIC-Power Investments, based in Hong Kong, will take a two-thirds interest in the park with one-third owned by Shanghai Zhangjiang Hi-Tech Park Development.
