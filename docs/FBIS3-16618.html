Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] At today's sitting of the Russian-American committee on conversion, Andrey Kokoshin, the Russian first deputy defense minister, and U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry stressed that Russia and America are entering a new phase of their relationship. Their speeches noted that the talk should now be not only about future prospects but also about specific ways to implement the conversion of Russia's defense complex. The American side showed an interest in assisting Russia's conversion plans, not just with advice but also with hundreds of millions of dollars. In particular, William Perry emphasized the wish of the Americans to organize in Russia the manufacture of prefabricated housing for servicemen's families, and noted the mutual benefit of cooperation between the two countries on this issue.
