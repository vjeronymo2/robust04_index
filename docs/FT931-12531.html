930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / National or reginal pay bargaining NATIONAL or regional pay bargaining could replace the wages councils in some industries, says Incomes Data Services, the pay analyst. In a report on wages councils, IDS says medium-sized employers might form 'pay clubs' where employers informally exchanged information about pay. The British Hospitality Association, which represents employers in the hotel, restaurant and contract-cleaning businesses, said last night that it would issue guidelines to its members on pay levels if wages councils were abolished.
