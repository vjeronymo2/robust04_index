BFN [Text] Armed formations backing Dzokhar Dudayev have made an attempt to destroy a political and military opposition group led by Beslan Gantemirov [former mayor of Groznyy]. This action has failed, says ANI, quoting the press center of Chechnya's interim council. Gantamirov's detachments are in possession of armor as well as firearms. They are stationed in the Urus-Martanovskiy rayon, adjacent to Groznyy.
