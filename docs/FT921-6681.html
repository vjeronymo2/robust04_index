920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: TR European Growth lower The shunning of smaller European companies by investors resulted in net asset value at TR European Growth Trust falling to 94p at December 31 against 96.4p a year earlier and 106.3p at June 30. Net revenue was Pounds 219,000 (Pounds 624,000) for earnings per share of 0.6p (1.72p).
