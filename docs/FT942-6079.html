940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / International Company News: Northern Telecom wins Chinese deals Northern Telecom of Canada has signed contracts worth about USDollars 82m to supply advanced telecommunications switching systems to three Chinese provincial governments, Reuter reports from New York. The company said it will install 13 switching systems for the Tianjin Posts and Telecommunications Administration, worth Dollars 40m, and in Shaanxi Province, nine switching systems worth more than Dollars 22m. The company is also installing systems for the Hebei Posts and Telecommunications Administration, worth Dollars 20.6m. Northern Telecom said the 37 digital multiplexing systems can each support up to 100,000 telephone lines.
