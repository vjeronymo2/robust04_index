930630 FT 30 JUN 93 / UK Company News: 16% decline at Walker & Staff In the year ended March 31 pre-tax profit at Walker & Staff Holdings was reduced by 16 per cent, from Pounds 610,000 to Pounds 512,000. Turnover came to Pounds 7.12m (Pounds 8.35m). An unchanged dividend of 5p has already been paid. Earnings per share dropped to 15.5p (18.5p). The group distributes valve and pipeline equipment.
