BFN [Text] His Excellency 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, Presidential Council member and envoy of His Excellency Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, arrived in the country this afternoon on a two-day visit to the sultanate. In a statement to the Omani News Agency upon arrival, his excellency said he is carrying a message to His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id from his Excellency President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih dealing with the current situation in Yemen and bilateral relations. He said he will listen to the views of his majesty the sultan on the current situation and developments in the regional, Arab, and international arenas in light of the wisdom and awareness of his majesty, and the love he enjoys among the Yemeni people. His excellency expressed pleasure for visiting the sultanate to meet with Omani officials. He was received upon arrival by Qays Bin-'Abd-al-Mun'im al-Zawawi, deputy prime minister for financial and economic affairs.
