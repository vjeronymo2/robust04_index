Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] His Majesty the king visited Jordanian troops serving under the United Nations flag in Croatia. Upon arrival in Zagreb Airport, his majesty, the supreme commander of the Armed Forces, expressed hope for the return of peace in the former Yugoslavia. The king said the Jordanians were privileged in playing a peacekeeping role in that region: [Begin recording] [Husayn] The purpose of my visit is, obviously, to have the chance to see Jordanians who've had the privilege to represent our country in the search for peace and security for our friends in this entire region. And it is certainly our hope that, before too long, we shall see things develop in that direction, God willing. [Unidentified correspondent] Thank you so much. [Husayn] Thank you very much. [end recording] [passage omitted] His majesty the king later flew to Austria on the final leg of his overseas trip before returning to Amman.
