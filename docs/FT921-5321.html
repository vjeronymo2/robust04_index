920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Intereurope Intereurope Technology Services, the technical documentation and support group, yesterday reported a marginal decline in interim profits. The fall in the six months to end-December - from Pounds 642,000 to Pounds 614,000 - came on turnover of Pounds 5.41m (restated Pounds 6.36m). The interim dividend is maintained at 2p, payable from earnings of 7.95p (8.1p).
