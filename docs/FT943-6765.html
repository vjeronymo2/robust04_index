940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Shorco advances to Pounds 306,000 Shorco Group Holdings maintained its improvement with pre-tax profits up sharply from Pounds 80,000 to Pounds 306,000 in the first half of 1994. Turnover of the USM-quoted group, which has interests in trench support systems and allied equipment, rose 27 per cent from Pounds 3.33m to Pounds 4.21m. Basic earnings per share reached 6.6p (1.7p) or 6.5p fully diluted and the interim dividend is stepped up to 2.7p (2.4p). A 1-for-2 scrip issue is proposed.
