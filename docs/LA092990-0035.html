September 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MICKEY MOUSE EVENTS NO WORSE THAN MINNIE

If Minnie Minoso had played continuously for six decades, that would truly be something to be proud of.

However, being stuck in a game for one or two at-bats is no accomplishment, for Minoso or the White Sox. Sure, Nolan Ryan is still playing -- he wins games, he strikes people out. If the Sox are willing to put Minoso in for one game, they should be willing to have him on the team. Permanently.

MARY LEER, Riverside
