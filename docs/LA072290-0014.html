July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RECORD RACK: IN BRIEF;

** 1/2 BOOM CRASH OPERA, "THESE HERE ARE CRAZY TIMES," GIANT/WARNER BROS.: ALBUMS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE (A CLASSIC).

The best songs on this Aussie band's second album sound like an apolitical Midnight Oil. The political songs sound like average INXS. Catchy stuff -- even rocks good at some turns. But what's the point of an apolitical Oil or a political INXS? Steve Hochman
