921019 FT 19 OCT 92 / Press body backs self-regulation THE Press Complaints Com-mission argues today that self-regulation of the newspaper industry is now working effectively. The claim is made in evidence sent to Sir David Calcutt, who is investigating the performance of press self-regulation. The commission was set up last year following the Calcutt report into press behaviour. The PCC says that the number of complaints is tiny compared with the number of words printed in newspapers.
