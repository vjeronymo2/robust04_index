BFN [Text] Mashhad, June 20, IRNA -- A special representative of the Interior Ministry announced here Monday that 25 people had died and 70 had been injured and hospitalized as of now (11:00 pm local time) as a result of the bomb blast this afternoon at the mausoleum of Imam Reza (a.s.) [peace be upon him]. These are the first official figures announced after a tally of the eyewitness reports on the number of the dead and the injured showed that 70 people were dead and 114 were injured. The Interior Ministry representative added that some of the injured were in critical condition and that there was a possibility that death toll might rise. "But we are all doing our best to save those injured," he said.
