February 9, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CLOTHESLINES

Q: I will be getting married in March at a simple ceremony with only close friends and immediate family in attendance. As this will be a second wedding for me, I was thinking of wearing a suit.

What do you recommend for someone 36, 5'6", Size 10? -- S.E.

A: How about a lace jacket with a chiffon skirt, as illustrated here? This romantic suit is by Susan Lane, bridal gown designer and owner of Country Elegance, who suggests that you buy the jacket in ivory lace over rose-beige satin and the skirt in blush pink, a shade lighter than rose-beige. Lane says that blush pink is the No. 1 color for all wedding gowns sold at her shop, replacing white and antique ivory. She believes it's especially appropriate for what she calls encore brides. This suit, priced at $450, comes in Sizes 6-16 at Country Elegance, 7375 Greenbush Ave., North Hollywood, Calif. 91605.
