920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Airedale settles with former director Airedale, the holding company of Magnet, the kitchen retailer, has settled out of court a suit for wrongful dismissal brought three years ago by Mr Albert King, Magnet's former finance director. After the High Court ruled that he had carried out his duties without fault, he accepted a sum of Pounds 850,000 including costs from Airedale.
