940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / Telegraph chief takes new role Mr Joe Cooke, 62, one of the key figures in the transformation of The Telegraph into a profitable company in recent years, is to resign as managing director at the end of the month. He will become a non-executive vice-chairman and continue as a director of The Telegraph. He will be succeeded by Mr Stephen Grabiner, deputy managing director. Mr Daniel Colson, executive vice-chairman of the group, will become chief executive.
