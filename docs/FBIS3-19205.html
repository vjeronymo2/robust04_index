Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the defeat of the Social Democratic Party of Austria [SPOe] in the diet elections in Carinthia, the expected reshuffle was decided today: Health Minister Michael Ausserwinkler will replace the unfortunate SPOe Chairman Peter Ambrozy. Ambrozy will resign from all political functions. Ausserwinkler immediately announced his claim to the position of provincial governor. It is logical that the representative of the party that got the most votes should become governor. The goal of the negotiations with the Austrian People's Party is a functioning government, he said.
