910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / International Company News: Exports surge helps Angostura to advance 22% ANGOSTURA Bitters, the Trinidadian distillers, recorded a TTDollars 15.9m (USDollars 3.75m) net profit for the year ending December 1990, 22 per cent higher than for the same period of 1989, writes James Canute in Kingston. The company's turnover increased 2 per cent to TTDollars 163.6m, with export earnings, mainly from rum, up 20 per cent to TTDollars 40m. The board has recommended a final dividend of 13.34 cents per stock unit. Mr Thomas Gatcliffe, chairman, said sales had not been affected as badly as anticipated by the attempted coup in Trinidad in July.
