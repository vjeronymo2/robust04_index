July 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Roger Kingdom, the 1984 and 1988 Olympic gold medalist in the 110-meter high hurdles, Greg Foster, a two-time world champion, and Tonie Campbell, bronze medalist at Seoul last year, have signed to compete in the event at the Jack in the Box Invitational Track Meet, Aug. 6 at UCLA's Drake Stadium.
