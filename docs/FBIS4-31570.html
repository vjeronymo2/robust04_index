BFN [Text] The National Council for Reunification has expressed its indignation over the replacement of the state anthem of the Republic of Moldova, as decided by the Chisinau parliament. In a communique sent today to the ROMPRES news agency, that council assesses that the decision to make such an alteration sanctions, quote, the delusion perpetrated by leaders who call themselves Moldovans and the legitimizing of neocommunist, anti-union, and anti-Romanian forces, unquote. The document also accuses the campaign of depriving the Moldovan people of their national identity and progressively sovietizing territories stolen from Romania, from the inside.
