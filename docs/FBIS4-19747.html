BFN [Text] Portuguese Foreign Affairs Minister Jose Manuel Durao Barroso has said that new requests have been made for Portugal's help in solidifying the Mozambican peace process. After meeting Afonso Dhlakama, Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] leader, in Lisbon, the Portuguese foreign affairs minister described as legitimate, international supplementary support for Renamo so it can become a political party. In turn, Dhlakama insisted that, irrespective of the outcome of elections, the winning party should share power with other parties represented in Parliament.
