Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, Jan 12 (CTK) -- A disagreement with the unilateral decision by Hungary regarding demolition of the circle dam at Nagymaros-Gabcikovo water work was expressed by Ivo Hlavacek, a Slovak Foreign Ministry official, Slovak Television announced today. Today's verbal protest lodged with Ferenc Boros, charge d'affaires of Hungarian Embassy, states that Hungary's unilateral decision (of January 31, 1993) violated the 1977 agreement and that Slovakia would lodge a complaint at the International Court of Justice in The Hague for all damages caused by the "illegal action."
