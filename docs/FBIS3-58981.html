Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] France will make proposals on Bosnia soon. They will be announced as early as this week. This is what Francois Mitterrand told the American secretary of state, Warren Christopher, whom he received for 45 minutes at the Elysee Palace. The American foreign minister also met Alain Juppe and Edouard Balladur. Meanwhile, in Bosnia, British General Michael Rose has replaced Belgian Francis Briquemont as the head of the UN soldiers. He said straight away that he was not coming to organize the departure of the UN Protection Force forces.
