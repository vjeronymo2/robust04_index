January 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SOUTHLAND SPORTS FANS SHOULD GIVE THANKS

I read sports letters in wonderment at the attitude of Southern California sports fans. Being a native Texan and a graduate of the University of Texas, I can tell you how far a football program can fall (let's not even discuss the Dallas Cowboys).

Last year Texas was 4-7 and on the wrong end of a couple of blowouts.

Instead of incessant criticism of a couple of top 10 football teams and coaches, Southern California fans should all stop and remind themselves how lucky they are.

BRADLEY SAINT-LAURENT

Los Angeles
