930424 FT 24 APR 93 / UK Company News: Improving trend continues for VTR VTR, the USM-quoted video editing group, improved its profits in the first half to February 28 1993, against the previous six months, and said the trend had continued into the present period. Pre-tax profit for the opening half reached Pounds 202,000, against Pounds 158,000 in the preceding six months and Pounds 276,000 in the corresponding period of 1992. Earnings per share came to 1.7p (2.4p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 1.2p.
