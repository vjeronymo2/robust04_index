Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received a British parliamentary delegation on 8 January. The meeting reviewed the latest developments in the Middle East peace process as well as the efforts to move it forward and implement the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles agreement on the ground. The social and economic situation in the occupied Palestinian territories and ways of supporting and developing the occupied Palestinian territories were also reviewed.
