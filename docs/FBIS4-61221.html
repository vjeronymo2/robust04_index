BFN [Text] Today at 12:00 pm the fourth reactor of the Kolskiy Nuclear Power Station was closed down due to a technical fault in the security system. Currently, the energy block is functioning at one percent of capacity. However, the fourth block can be repaired only after the third one, which stopped at the end of 1993, renews its work. The first and the second blocks were temporarily closed down about four months ago. A working commission has been set up on the Kolskiy Nuclear Power Station. It is expected to give an expert evaluation of the condition of the reactor which is out of order, an Interfax correspondent learned.
