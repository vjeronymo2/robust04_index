920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Net asset value falls 30% at EFM Java Net asset value per share of EFM Java Trust stood at 25.91p at December 31. The figure represented a 30 per cent decline from the 37.15p a year earlier and a fall of almost 40 per cent from 42.88p at the interim stage in June. Earnings emerged at 0.23p (1.2p) and directors recommended a dividend for the year of 0.06p - 1p was paid for the period from April 25 to December 31 last year.
