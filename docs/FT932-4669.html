930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / UK Company News: AG Holdings AG Holdings is coming to the market through a placing of 8.82m shares at 125p each, leaving existing shareholders with 51 per cent of the group. Of the shares, 6.82m are being sold by existing holders and the balance by the group to raise Pounds 2.05m net for reducing borrowings prior to suitable expansion opportunities. The group operates from Askern, near Doncaster, and is the largest UK maker of despatch and shipping reels used for storing, transporting and dispensing cables, wire rope and similar products. In the year ended July 31 1992 turnover fell from Pounds 17.5m to Pounds 16.4m and operating profit from Pounds 3.32m to Pounds 3m. But crediting non-recurring items pushed up the pre-tax balance from Pounds 3.1m to Pounds 3.87m. In the eight months to March 31 1993 turnover had reached Pounds 10.7m, operating profit Pounds 1.95m and the pre-tax figure Pounds 1.87m. For the full year the pre-tax profit is forecast at Pounds 2.7m with earnings per share of 11.04p. The first dividend will be a special 2p in January.
