Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mohamed (Saidi), chief of the Algerian president's office, arrived in Damascus early this morning, on a visit to the Syrian Arab Republic. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Wahib Fadil, minister of the republic's presidential affairs, and the Algerian ambassador and Embassy staff in Damascus. Saidi told SANA he is very happy to visit Damascus. He said: I have a personal message for President Hafiz al-Asad from Algerian President Liamine Zeroual on current developments.
