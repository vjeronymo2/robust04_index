BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 2 May -- May Day evening and overnight was unsettled on Armenia's borders. The border zone village of Koti, Noyemberyanskiy Rayon, in northeast Armenia was subjected to mortars, artillery, and machine-gun fire from 1845 hours until late in the night from the adjacent Azerbaijani territory, the ITAR-TASS correspondent was told today at the republic's Defense Ministry press center. The village of Artanish in the Krasnoselskiy Rayon of Armenia was fired on starting from 2200 hours. There have been casualties.
