Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, 8 Feb (TANJUG) -- The shell that caused the tragedy in Sarajevo's Markale market was fired from a position 2 km northeast of the scene of the carnage. The shell was lobbed from 1-1.5 km within the territory under the control of Muslim forces, in relation to the separation line with the Army of the republic of Srpska. This was confirmed to a TANJUG correspondent this evening by very reliable and confidential sources in the UN Protection Forces Command who wished to remain anonymous. [passage omitted]
