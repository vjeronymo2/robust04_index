Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report under "News" rubric: "China Has No Territorial Claims on Kazakhstan"] [Text] Army General S. Nurmagambetov, minister of defense of the Republic of Kazakhstan, has paid an official visit to China at the invitation of Defense Minister Chi Haotian. On returning from his trip, Nurmagambetov answered questions from KRASNAYA ZVEZDA's correspondent and other journalists. He said, in particular, that the visit to China was successful. Although no documents were signed, the sides managed to clarify a number of stands on political and military issues affecting mutual interests and to become more closely acquainted. Kazakhstan, which has a 1,700-km border with China, is satisfied that the Chinese side firmly stated the absence of territorial claims on Kazakhstan.
