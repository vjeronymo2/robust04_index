BFN [Text] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev believes that the time is not yet ripe for international sanctions against North Korea. In an interview for INTERFAX before flying to Paris, he stressed that sanctions can be introduced in stages but only after all other possibilities have been exhausted. In the minister's view, the main possibility is an international conference. Andrey Kozyrev also noted that international measures should be introduced and lifted by the UN Security Council alone.
