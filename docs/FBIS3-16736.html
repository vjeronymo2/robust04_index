Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] -- The State Property Committee has named 50 enterprises that will be offered for sale in the first ever specialized coupon auction. The list of enterprises was published in the republican newspapers YEGEMEN KAZAKHSTAN [SOVEREIGN] and KAZAKHSTANSKAYA PRAVDA. These facts were given at a news conference held by Zhanibek Karibzhanov, chairman of the State Property Committee. He stressed that a new, very important stage of implementation of the massive scale privatization program is beginning for investment funds and for floated enterprises, which will be sold at these coupon auctions. Practically all inhabitants of the Republic -- 96.5 percent -- have already received their privatization check books. A new deadline was set and check books will continue to be handed out until 1 April. The first auction will be held on 29 April with the participation of 109 investment funds with licences from the State Property Committee. Their applications for participation will be taken on 28 April from 1500 until 1800 local time. The starting price for a share of each of the 50 enterprises will be announced by the state committee no later than 5 April. Among objects to be put to tender: Profitable light industry enterprises, large construction industry, and processing industry from the various regions of the Republic will be sold. Similar coupon auctions, starting from May, will be held every two weeks. Until the end of the year, at 17 auctions, shares of 1,200 enterprises are planned to be sold for privatization investment coupons, and shares of 2,500 enterprises -- by the end of 1995.
