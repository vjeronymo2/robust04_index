Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bayerbach (DDP/ADN) -- Federal Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (FDP) has spoken in favor of continuing to strive for a "peaceful solution" for Bosnia-Herzegovina. The decision for a military intervention by NATO was not as simple as many citizens imagined, Kinkel said today at the Ash Wednesday meeting of the FDP in Bayerbach [Bavaria]. In the event of a military strike, however, there would be "no lack" of support from the German government within the limits of its legal possibilities.
