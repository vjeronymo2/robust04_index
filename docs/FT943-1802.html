940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / Observer: No idea The effervescent Janet Street-Porter is leaving the BBC, where she has been head of independent television production in the entertainment field, to join the Mirror Group, where she will be managing director of a new cable network. 'I regret leaving a place where ideas are paramount,' she said yesterday. The very idea that ideas may not be paramount at her new employer seems to be one worth pursuing . . .
