921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / Albania oil workers strike Albania's government, fresh from crushing a strike in the key chrome industry, yesterday warned striking oil refinery workers they could lose their jobs if they do not return to work by Saturday, Reuter reports from Tirana. Workers at the Ballshi refinery, south of Tirana, went on strike two weeks ago, demanding a doubling of wages, extra holidays and a five-day week.
