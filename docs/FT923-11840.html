920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / UK Company News: GWR loss nearly doubled to Dollars 13.6m Great Western Resources, the US-based oil, gas and coal company which has a USM quote, fell deeper into the red in the half year to March 31 with a pre-tax loss of Dollars 13.6m (Pounds 7.1m) against Dollars 7.3m. Losses per share were 15 cents (8 cents) and there is no interim dividend (2.5p gross).
