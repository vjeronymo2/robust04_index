December 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Calling All Collectors: At a press conference in Westwood today, three-time Oscar-winner Chuck Jones is scheduled to unveil his "Comic Ball Cards," a series of baseball trading cards featuring Bugs Bunny, Porky Pig, Daffy Duck and other characters from the classic Warner Bros. cartoons in new adventures. CLAUDIA PUIG
