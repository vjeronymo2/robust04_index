BFN [Unattributed report under the "Around the Clock Non-Stop" rubric: "Kazannik Denied Permission to Go Abroad"] [Text] The dismissed Russian General Prosecutor Aleksey Kazannik, who lives in Omsk, was to leave for Germany on 20 May to appear before the student body of Bonn University and elsewhere and to read a series of other lectures in German cities. However, as Aleksey Ivanovich [Kazannik] told MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS in an interview, the Russian Foreign Ministry would not issue him the visa without actually explaining why. Aleksey Ivanovich believes this is simply political revenge in the wake of his criticisms directed at President Yeltsin.
