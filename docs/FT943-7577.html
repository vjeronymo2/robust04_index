940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Stora - Other companies The market is looking for profits of around SKr1.6bn when Europe's biggest pulp and paper group reports first-half results tomorrow. Capital gains and cost-cutting will be the main reason for the upturn from last year's SKr30m profit, although the group will be starting to feel the benefit of rising pulp, fine paper and sawn timber prices.
