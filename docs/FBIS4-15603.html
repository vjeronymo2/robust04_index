BFN [Text] Baku (April 8) TURAN: Today the Russia President's envoy Vladimir Kazimirov arrived in Yerevan in order to conduct a consultation on the peaceful settlement of Azeri-Armenian conflict. The informed sources announced to TURAN that Kazimirov will present the leadership of Armenia some initiatives, which prior was presented to the Azeri leadership during his visit to Baku on April 6. The consultation with the participance of all the sides of conflict will be continued in Prague on April 11-15.
