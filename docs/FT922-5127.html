920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 17,760 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. The Landed market continued selective. Good liquoring east Africans ruled firm to dearer with dusts a better feature, but mediums were 2-3p lower and some plainer lines were neglected. Central Africans also tended easier. Offshore teas came in for limited support and prices were generally easier for all descriptions. The highest price realised this week was 201p for a Rwanda pd. Quotations: quality: 155p, good medium: 132p, medium: 103p, low medium: 86p.
