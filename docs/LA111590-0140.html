November 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The World League of American Football announced it will begin its inaugural season next March with 10 teams in three divisions, including seven North American cities and three European sites.

The Europe division will include London, Barcelona and Frankfurt. North America East includes New York, Montreal, Orlando, Fla., and a Carolinas team based in Raleigh or Charlotte. The North American West has Sacramento, San Antonio and Birmingham, Ala.
