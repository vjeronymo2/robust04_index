November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TONIGHT: In its 12-year history, a Capistrano Valley High School football team has never gone 10-0, but the 9-0 Cougars get their chance tonight against top-ranked El Toro, also 9-0 (C12). The winner gets the South Coast League title and the No. 1 ranking in Orange County. . . . Time: 7:30 p.m. at Capistrano Valley, 26301 Via Escolar, Mission Viejo. Dana Parsons
