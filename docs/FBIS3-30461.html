Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Feb. 1 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Monday explained to Finance Minister Marek Borowski the reasons behind the dismissal last Friday of Deputy Finance Minister Stefan Kawalec. The letter has probably reached the addressee by now, Ewa Wachowicz, the prime minister's press secretary, said on Tuesday. Wachowicz did not disclose the contents of the letter and denied press reports to the effect that Finance Minister Borowski, unsatisfied with the explanation, has allegedly decided to quit. Premier Pawlak dismissed Kawalec last Friday without prior consultations with Finance Minister Borowski. Deputy Prime Minister Borowski Friday asked the prime minister for a written explanation by Monday.
