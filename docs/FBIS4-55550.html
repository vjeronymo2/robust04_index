BFN [Text] If they come to power, the Socialists and the Free Democrats will conduct a foreign policy which gives equal priority to relations with the East and the West and with Hungarians living beyond the country's borders. Laszlo Kovacs and Istvan Szent-Ivanyi, foreign policy experts for the Hungarian Socialist Party [MSZP] and the Alliance of Free Democrats [SZDSZ], respectively, have announced that Hungarian foreign policy will change substantially both in its style and methods. The SZDSZ politician also said that with respect to neighboring countries, they will present a pragmatic policy which is adjusted to Hungary's dimensions and true significance.
