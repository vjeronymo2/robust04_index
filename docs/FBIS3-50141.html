Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 24 Jan (TANJUG) -- A club of deputies of the Democratic Party and the Democratic Party of Serbia was set up in the Serbian Assembly today. The club of deputies has got 36 deputies: 29 of them belong to the Democratic Party, while seven belong to the Democratic Party of Serbia. The chairman of the club of deputies of the Democratic Party and the Democratic Party of Serbia is Zoran Djindjic.
