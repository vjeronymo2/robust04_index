930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / Hard line on Golan Heights Only 17 per cent of Israelis would give up all or most of the Golan Heights in return for a peace agreement with Syria, according to an opinion poll published yesterday, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. The Smith Research Centre poll for Jerusalem Report magazine showed 62 per cent of 1,000 Jews surveyed opposed withdrawing from any part of the strategic plateau.
