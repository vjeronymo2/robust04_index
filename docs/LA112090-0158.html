November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Three current or former Oklahoma State baseball players were in satisfactory condition after a car accident that killed a Pawhuska, Okla., man who was driving the vehicle.

Thomas Barry Carter, a longtime supporter of the team, died when he lost control of the car, the Oklahoma Highway Patrol said.

Derek Brandow and Ritchie Moody, sophomore pitchers for the Cowboys who played in last season's College World Series, and Eric Coca, a former third baseman, were in satisfactory condition at St. Joseph Medical Center in Ponca City, hospital officials said.
