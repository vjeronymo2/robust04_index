BFN [Text] Libya has proposed establishing diplomatic and economic relations with South Africa. The proposals are contained in a letter addressed to President Nelson Mandela from the Libyan Leader Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. Col. al-Qadhdhafi has invited President Mandela to visit Libya following the OAU leaders' meeting in Tunis. The letter was delivered by Libyan Foreign Affairs Minister 'Umar al-Muntasir at the weekend.
