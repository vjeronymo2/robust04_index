940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Zeneca - Other Companies The drugs group's full-year results, due on Thursday, will the first since last summer's demerger from ICI. The figures should show pre-tax profits close to Pounds 650m against a pro forma Pounds 442m for 1992. Its attractiveness to yield-minded investors should be consolidated with a dividend of 27.5p, a yield of about 4.6 per cent.
