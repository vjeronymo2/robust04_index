921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Bogod little changed Profits of Bogod Group, a seller of sewing machines and parts, were little changed at Pounds 105,000 pre-tax for the six months ended September 30. The figure, which compared with Pounds 102,000 previously, was scored from a turnover marginally lower at Pounds 2.52m (Pounds 2.66m). The interim dividend on the A shares is held at 0.2p from earnings of 1.22p (1.19p).
