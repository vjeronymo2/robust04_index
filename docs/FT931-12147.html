930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Observer: Ex-communicated As it's a chairman's job to hire and fire chief executives, it was right and proper that the tricky calls about Chris Greentree's departure from Lasmo should be fielded by Lord Rees, the former Tory MP now in the company's top seat. Nevertheless, he might well have been able to get his message across to the media even better if the man doing the departing didn't happen to be the husband of Lasmo's public relations chief, Hilary Wilson.
