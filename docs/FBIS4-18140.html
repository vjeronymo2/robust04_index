BFN [Text] "We will form a government and do so soon," Silvio Berlusconi said today, following his first [postelection] meeting with Umberto Bossi. Berlusconi said they will meet again on Friday [1 April], adding, "There is a very friendly climate. We are on the right road." Bossi and Roberto Maroni also thought the meeting was "very positive." Bossi said: "We spoke, we started to thrash things out, and, if everything goes as it should, we will be able to give the country a government soon."
