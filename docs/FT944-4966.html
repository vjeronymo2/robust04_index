941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Associated Nursing static at Pounds 1.07m Associated Nursing Services, the USM-quoted nursing homes operator, maintained interim pre-tax profits at Pounds 1.07m against Pounds 1.05m, despite increased development costs. Turnover dipped slightly to Pounds 8.46m (Pounds 9.02m) for the six months to September 30. The nursing homes side raised operating profits by 18 per cent to Pounds 2.27m (Pounds 1.92m). Earnings per share dropped to 5.5p (7.2p), while the interim dividend was 0.75p (0.5p).
