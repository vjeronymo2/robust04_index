BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to the Palestinian president, has affirmed that the Israeli withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho has actually begun and vanguards of the Palestinian police will deploy there within a week. This follows the agreement reached by the Palestinian and Israeli sides on security arrangements for Hebron in the wake of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. The agreement provides for the deployment of 160 international observers to oversee the arrangements.
