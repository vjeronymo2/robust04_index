940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: BM BM Group said its debt reduction programme was on track as it announced the disposal of a lossmaking machinery distribution business in the US, which is expected to cut borrowings by Pounds 14m. BM is selling Illinois-based Roland Machinery for Dollars 7.8m (Pounds 5.2m), plus repayment of a Dollars 1.9m intercompany loan. The sale will also eliminate some Pounds 8m in stock finance previously taken into the parent's Pounds 98m debt figure.
