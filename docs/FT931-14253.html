930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / World News in Brief: Rhone-Poulenc sale The French government is to sell around 10 per cent (6m shares) of Rhone-Poulenc, France's flagship chemical group. The shares sale, postponed last month as a result of erratic stock market conditions, will go ahead today at FFr500 (Dollars 92.60) a share.
