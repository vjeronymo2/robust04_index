November 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The World Cup men's slalom scheduled for Sunday at Park City, Utah, was canceled because of high winds and lightning, and it was unclear when the event would be rescheduled.

The men were scheduled to move on to Waterville, N.H., and Lorne O'Connor, Federation of International Skiing technical director, said officials would meet early this week to discuss holding the race there.
