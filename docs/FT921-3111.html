920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Sulzer contracts total Pounds 13m SULZER INFRA (UK) has won contracts for building services totalling over Pounds 13m. They include an order from Cementation, a Trafalgar House Company, for multi-services on a large development at Feltham in Middlesex, in which Sulzer Infra forms part of a consortium; a mechanical services installation on a fork-lift truck production line for Lansing Linde at Basingstoke; and the installation of mechanical services at the University of Sheffield. Other projects are at Bristol General Hospital; Willesden College of Technology; Tesco Stores, Dudley; and the Bank of China in London.
