931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Forminster advances 11% to Pounds 721,000 Forminster, the women's and men's wear supplier, reported pre-tax profits for the half year to October 31 up 11 per cent at Pounds 721,000, against Pounds 648,000. Turnover expanded 13 per cent from Pounds 11.1m to Pounds 12.5m. Earnings per share came out at 26.74p (22.48p). The interim dividend is raised to 4.63p (3.96p).
