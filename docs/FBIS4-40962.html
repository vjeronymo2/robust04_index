BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] In May, the Russian Government's budget owed enterprises 8.5 trillion rubles. The figure was mentioned during a session of the public chamber of the Russian president by Aleksandr Shokhin, deputy prime minister and minister for the economy in the government. The deputy prime minister believes that using privatization income to set up an extra-budgetary fund, which could be put into anything from the defense sector to the socio-cultural sphere, could be one way out of the difficult financial situation.
