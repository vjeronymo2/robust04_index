930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / Finance and the Family: First for Perpetual - At a glance Perpetual, the Henley-based fund management group, is set to launch its first investment trust. The Perpetual Japanese Investment Trust will be chaired by Christoper Heath, famous for being Britain's highest paid individual while at Barings. The trust will aim to raise between Pounds 25m and Pounds 60m and will have a minimum subscription of Pounds 500. As the graph shows, the Japanese market has rebounded this year, but is a long way below its peak of 38,000.
