920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / UK Company News: BIL names 'future' Gibbs Mew chief Brierley Investments Limited, the New Zealand investment company which has made a hostile Pounds 11m bid for Gibbs Mew, the Salisbury-based brewing and commercial property company, has announced the name of the man who would become the target's managing director in the event of the bid being successful, writes Peter Pearse. He is Mr David Stephen, who since 1991 has been involved in Trent Taverns, the operator of 67 pubs in the Midlands. Mr Roger Gibbs, company secretary of Gibbs Mew, said the announcement was 'silly and extraordinary'.
