920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / Charges allege Pounds 1.3m fraud MR Malcolm Johnson, a businessman, appeared before City of London magistrates yesterday charged with eight counts of fraud involving more than Dollars 2.4m (Pounds 1.3m). It followed his extradition from Austria on Wednesday after a request by the Serious Fraud Office. The charges against him include obtaining Dollars 2.4m by deception from Postel Investment Management by purporting that shares in Duralite Manufacturing were shares in a properly constituted company with assets of over Dollars 20m. Mr Johnson, 43, of Betchworth, Surrey, was remanded on conditional bail until July 9.
