Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] AWB [Afrikaner Resistance Movement] leader Eugene Terreblanche said earlier that if the government and the ANC [African National Congress] continued to stand in the way of an Afrikaans volkstaat [homeland], South Africa would look like Guy Fawkes. Interviewed on the television program "Newsline" last night, Mr. Terreblanche dismissed ANC leader Nelson Mandela's threat to mobilize his guerrillas against right-wing violence. [Begin Terreblanche recording] My good friend, they give me a laugh, and they know it. They can never ever fight a war against the Boer nation. We destroyed them totally in the past, and if they want war, we will do that. [end recording]
