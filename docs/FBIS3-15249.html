Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A high-ranking Russian military official told Interfax on Wednesday that Russia was to begin the withdrawal of strategic missiles from Ukraine at the beginning of March. He also said that the first lot consisted of 60 warheads removed from the strategic missiles of the 46th missile division and 43rd missile army. There are about 2,000 nuclear warheads in Ukraine. He expressed satisfaction that Moscow and Kiev had found a political solution of this problem. He also said that delays in the withdrawal of the strategic nuclear weapons were due to the unpreparedness of corresponding documents. He believes that after the necessary papers are prepared by the Ukrainian customs and other agencies, there would be no further delays.
