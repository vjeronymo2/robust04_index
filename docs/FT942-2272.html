940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Equity Markets: Tokyo - Other Markets While share prices face a short-term correction following the rally in early June, increased trading by retail investors seems to hold the key to a further rise in the Nikkei index this week. However, since participation by individual investors has been based solely on a recovery in sentiment, rather than an improvement in economic fundamentals, Mr Alex Kinmont of Morgan Stanley says the improvement remains vulnerable to negative news. Meanwhile another fall in bonds may prompt a further shift in funds to stocks.
