930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / Bildt wins economy confidence vote SWEDEN'S minority coalition government yesterday won a parliamentary vote of confidence in its economic policies, averting the immediate threat of an early general election, writes Christopher Brown- Humes in Stockholm. The 23 members of the populist opposition New Democracy party, which last week triggered the country's political crisis, abstained in the vote, which the government won by 172 votes to 154.
