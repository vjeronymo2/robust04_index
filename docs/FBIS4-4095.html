BFN [Text] Beijing, 27 Apr (XINHUA) -- A spokesman for the State Council's Taiwan Affairs Office issued a statement today on a Taiwan passenger plane's crash in Japan. The spokesman said: We were shocked to learn about the crash of China Airline Flight 140 while trying to land at Japan's Nagoya Airport on the evening of 26 April. We sincerely express our deep grief over the Taiwan compatriots and other passengers who died, as well as profound solicitude to their families and relatives.
