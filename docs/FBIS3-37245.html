Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Aleksandr Krylovich and Andrey Serov] [Text] Moscow February 14 TASS--British Prime Minister John Major arrived in Moscow on an official visit late on Monday [14 February]. This is his first visit to Russia. At Moscow's Vnukovo-2 airport, Major was met by Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and other officials. The British premier inspected the guards of honour. The anthems of the two countries were played. After the welcoming ceremony, the motorcade left for the British Embassy in Moscow where Major will be staying during the visit.
