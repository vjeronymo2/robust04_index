Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Decree No. 148 of the State Council of the People's Republic of China"] [Text] Beijing, 1 Mar (XINHUA) -- The "PRC Regulations on the Management of Foreign-Funded Financial Institutions" were approved on 7 January 1994 by the 14th executive meeting of the State Council and are hereby promulgated. They will go into force as of 1 April 1994. [Signed] Premier Li Peng [Dated] 25 February 1994
