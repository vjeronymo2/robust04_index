931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / Middlesbrough FC plans new stadium Middlesbrough Football Club is to move from its turn-of-the-century ground at Ayresome Park to a new Pounds 16m, 32,000-seat stadium alongside the River Tees. The move is needed to fulfil the all-seater stipulation of the Taylor report into football ground safety and is part of the regeneration of Middlesbrough Dock. The Teesside Development Corporation said it was the largest relocation, in capacity terms, in the history of the Football League.
