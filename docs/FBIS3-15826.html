Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- Vice prime minister and head of the state property committee Anatoliy Chubays and Vice Prime Minister Aleksandr Zaveruykha are accompanying Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin in his one-day working visit to the Nizhnyy Novgorod region. According to the programme of the visit, the head of the Russian Government will visit the district centre of Gorodets, the collective farms named after Kuybyshev and the 60th anniversary of the October revolution and will attend a land auction. He is expected to take part in a conference of heads of local administrations from some 50 regions, which will discuss the Nizhnyy Novgorod model of the land reform. In Nizhnyy Novgorod Chernomyrdin will have a meeting with senior officials of the local administration and people's deputies. In the evening he will return to Moscow.
