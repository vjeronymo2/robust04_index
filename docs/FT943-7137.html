940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / Channel island sale price halved Lihou Island, the smallest inhabited Channel island, which failed to find a buyer when it was offered for sale at Pounds 1m two years ago, is back on the market at Pounds 500,000. Lihou is a mile off the south-west coast of Guernsey, to which it is linked by a causeway during low spring tides. Its 40 acres include the ruins of a 12th century Benedictine priory.
