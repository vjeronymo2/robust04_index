BFN [Text] In the opinion of Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, relations with Russia are a priority in our foreign policy. In an interview with UKRINFORM, the foreign minister emphasized that [word indistinct] relations between Ukraine and Russia serve not only the interests of our two countries, but also Europe in general. The minister also stated that the process of fulfilling the tripartite agreements of the Ukrainian, U.S., and Russian presidents on our nonnuclear status and guarantees for us has already begun. He emphasized, however, that all the sides must adhere to their commitments, which will ensure fulfillment of the agreements.
