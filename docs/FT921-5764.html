920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK tells Russia to release beef Britain has told Russia it must release immediately hundreds of tonnes of British beef which have been stored in a Moscow warehouse for more than a month, a government minister said on Monday, Reuter reports. Mrs Lynda Chalker, overseas development minister, responding to opposition criticism in parliament over the shipment, blamed the slow distribution on the European Community and what she called inadequate labelling and colour coding.
