Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hadera Magistrate's Court sentenced Binyamin Kahana, the leader of the Kahana Hay movement, to nine months in prison after convicting him of assaulting a policeman and violating an injunction that was issued against him. Our correspondent Nati Zilberman reports that the incident took place approximately two years ago during a demonstration in Umm al- Fahm. Binyamin Kahana was placed under arrest and jailed immediately after today's hearing.
