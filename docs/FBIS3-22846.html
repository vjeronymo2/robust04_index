Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali yesterday requested of President Carlos Menem that Argentina and Brazil expedite their support for the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty, and also praised the Argentine Government's efforts and constant willingness to send peacemaking forces to conflict regions. Butrus-Ghali said that Argentina has become a leading country in Latin America and, despite his request, noted the efforts it has been making to ratify the nuclear weapon non-proliferation treaty. Menem, in turn, promised to hold talks with Brazil to this end. This information was disclosed by Media Communications Secretary Raul Burzaco at the conclusion of the meeting held between Menem and Butrus-Ghali at Government House. [passage omitted]
