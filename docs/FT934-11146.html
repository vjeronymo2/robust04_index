931102 FT 02 NOV 93 / UK Company News: GR Holdings incurs loss and cuts final GR Holdings, which has the Grayshott Hall health hydro, property dealing and sheepskin manufacturing as its principal activities, made a pre-tax loss of Pounds 438,000 for the year ended June 30, against a profit of Pounds 193,000 last time. Turnover was down from Pounds 5.45m to Pounds 4.33m. After increased tax of Pounds 640,000 (Pounds 208,000) there was an attributable loss of Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 15,000) and losses per share of 9.4p (1.3p). A final dividend of 1.4p (1.6p) is proposed, making a 1.8p (27p) total. The comparative dividend included a 25p special payment.
