930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Develop net assets up 11% Net asset value at Kleinwort Development Fund advanced 11 per cent from 320.89p to 354.92p over the 12 months to July 31. Net profits for the year to the end of July came out at Pounds 622,614 (Pounds 825,045) reflecting a fall in investment income. Earnings per share were 10.29p (13.64p). A final unchanged dividend of 7.75p is recommended for a maintained total of 10.5p.
