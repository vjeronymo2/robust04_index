BFN [Text] India intends to restore preferential tariff margin on Sri Lankan clothes. The two-day meeting of the Indo-Sri Lankan Joint Commission held in New Delhi was told that this will be done as early as possible. An External Affairs Ministry press release said that both the countries decided to hold intergovernmental meetings on expansion of bilateral trade, as also the Sri Lankan request for increased market access for some of its products. Sri Lanka appreciated India's decision to extend a new line of credit to implement infrastructural development projects in that country. The two countries agreed in principle to collaborate in setting up an institute of technology and management in Sri Lanka.
