921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Tops Estates slips to Pounds 0.92m Pre-tax profits of Tops Estates, the property investment group, declined by 32 per cent, from Pounds 1.34m to Pounds 918,000, in the six months to end-September. The outcome was struck on rental income after outgoings down from Pounds 5.07m to Pounds 4.98m. Earnings came out at 1.33p (1.92p) and the interim dividend is increased by 5 per cent to 0.5775p (0.55p).
