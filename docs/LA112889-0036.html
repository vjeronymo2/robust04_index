November 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: CRADLE COUNT

A New Akroyd/Dixon: It's a girl for actor Dan Aykroyd and his actress-wife, Donna Dixon. Danielle Alexandra weighed 8 pounds, 13 ounces when she took her first breath of air at 12:05 p.m. on Nov. 18, said Susan Patricola, publicist for the couple. The couple wanted to get the baby out of St. John's Hospital and Health Center and home for Thanksgiving before a birth announcement was made, Patricola said Monday. "They're thrilled with their new arrival. She's healthy and beautiful and they're looking forward to parenthood," Patricola said. CLAUDIA PUIG
