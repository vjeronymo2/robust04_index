940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / IRA suspected in French case French police suspect that the Irish Republican Army may have been the beneficiary of a corruption ring during the construction of the EuroDisneyland theme park at Marne La Vallee on the outskirts of Paris, according to 'judicial sources' cited by Agence France-Presse, the French news agency. The report follows the conviction of Mr Joseph Mehager, 54, a site manager at EuroDisneyland on charges of involvement in a corruption ring that extorted Pounds 20,000 from Spearhead, a company bidding for a water supply system contract at the site. Mr Mehager was sentenced last week by the Paris appeal court to eight months in prison and to pay a FFr15,000 (Pounds 1,800) fine.
