940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / International Company News: Hongkong Land bond issue raises Dollars 360m Hongkong Land Holdings, Jardine Matheson group's property investment arm, has raised USDollars 360m through a convertible bond issue to fund general corporate requirements, including commitments from Trafalgar House and development of the colony's Container Terminal 9. The seven-year bond will carry a coupon of 4 per cent and a conversion price of HKDollars 31.05 - a 15 per cent premium over the current market price. The bond was initially planned to raise USDollars 300m, but this was lifted to USDollars 360m. Approval is being sought to list the convertible bonds on the London or Luxembourg stock exchange. Mr Ian Durant, finance director of the company, said the issue was in line with Hongkong Land's aim of diversifying sources of funding.
