930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / International Company News: ITT moves into gaming industry ITT Corporation is moving into the gaming industry with the purchase of the Las Vegas Desert Inn, writes Nikki Tait in New York. It is buying the hotel and casino, together with the adjoining 18-hole golf course and about 35 acres of vacant land fronting the Las Vegas 'Strip', for about Dollars 160m from Tracinda Corporation, the private investment company run by Mr Kirk Kerkorian, the California-based investor.
