BFN [Unattributed report: "Presidential Election: Balladur-Delors: Identical Approval Ratings"] [Text] According to a CSA [expansion unknown]-LE PARISIEN opinion poll, 47 percent of French people would have confidence in Jacques Delors "as president of the Republic" (up 4 percent in a month), in other words a confidence rating almost identical to that of Edouard Balladur who, with 48 percent, is down 1 percent. Philippe de Villiers shows the biggest increase (up 13 percent) at 22 percent and comes in 11th place, just ahead of Michel Rocard, down 7 percent to 21 percent (the opinion poll was carried out before his resignation [as Socialist Party leader]). Jacques Chirac is in third position with 38 percent (down 2 percent), ahead of Simone Veil and Raymond Barre, who both have 34 percent. Then come Bernard Kouchner at 29 percent (down 1 percent), Jack Lang at 29 percent (down 3 percent), Charles Pasqua at 29 percent (down 6 percent), Valery Giscard d'Estaing at 26 percent (up 2 percent), Francois Leotard at 24 percent (down 4 percent), and Philippe Seguin at 23 percent (down 4 percent).
