Control Point AU0104072594 Bucharest COTIDIANUL in Romanian 29 Mar 94 p 1 AU0104072594 Bucharest COTIDIANUL Romanian BFN [Unattributed note: "Eighteen and a Half Kilograms of Heroin Seized"] [Text] During a border check of a Scania truck belonging to the Iekin Uluslararsi Nakilat company during the night of 25-26 March at the border control point of Giurgiu, Lieutenant Major Ilie Clement and customs officer Emil Horobet discovered 13 packets containing brown powder. Checking further, in the roof of the truck driven by the Turkish citizen Yesil Veli, accompanied by his countryman Yesil Erdogan, they also found 16 packets containing the same substance thus amounting to a total quantity of 18.5 kilograms, which, after an examination, proved to be heroin.
