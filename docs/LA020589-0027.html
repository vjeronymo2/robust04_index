February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

POP EYE

HOT COPY: The always unpredictable (dare we say erratic) ex-New York Dolls guitarist Johnny Thunders is back with a new album, teamed up with vocalist Patti Palladin. Called "Copy Cats," the record features a host of cover tunes, ranging from the Seeds' "Can't Seem to Make You Mine" and Roy Head's "Treat Her Right" to the Chamber Bros.' "Uptown to Harlem" and the Shirelles' "Baby It's You.". . .Our Album Title of the Week goes to folkie Cindy Lee Berryhill's upcoming, Lenny Kaye-produced album, which is titled "Naked Movie Star.". . . And Our Joke of the Week goes to Dr. Bubba from the Scott's Tissue tip-sheet whose rock predictions for 1989 include this highlight: "Roseanne Barr, the Fat Boys and Sam Kinnison will form a band called the Traveling Pillsburies."
