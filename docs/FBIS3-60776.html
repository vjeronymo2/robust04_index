Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The Communist Party of the Philippines [CPP] has strongly criticized the continued operation of the Alex Boncayao Brigade [ABB] in Metro Manila. According to Gregorio Rosal, alias Comrade Roger, head of the CPP Information Bureau, this liquidation squad was dismantled and disarmed in December last year. He reiterated that the current ABB is illegal and is not recognized by the hierarchy of the CPP-NPA [New People's Army]. It is allegedly supported by the breakaway group of the Metro Manila-Rizal Regional Committee under the leadership of Felimon Lagman, alias Carlos Forte. The ABB was recently involved in the robbery of a truck loaded with rice in Binondo, Manila.
