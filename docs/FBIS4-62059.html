BFN [Text] Ukraine's Defense Ministry has so far given no consideration to Russia's program alternative to NATO's Partnership for Peace scheme, Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaly Radetsky said. "We are still working out our vision" of the North Atlantic alliance's scheme, whose implementation poses "many problems" for Ukraine, Radetsky told Interfax-Ukraine on Wednesday. One of the problems is that Ukraine's military doctrine prevents it from joining any blocs, he said.
