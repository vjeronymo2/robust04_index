Date Submitted: July 25, 1994. Submitting Agency: Agency for International Development. OMB Number: None. Form Number: AID 610&hyph;25 and 610&hyph;27. Type of Submission: New Collection. Title: Inquiry for United States Government Use Only (Reference and Employment Inquiry). Purpose: U.S.A.I.D. is required by Executive order that background investigations be conducted on all persons entering Federal Service. 5 U.S. Code 3301 and 5 CFR 5.2 require that investigations and derminations be made concerning the qualifications and fitness of applicants for Federal employment. A National Agency Check and Written Inquiries is the minimum investigation required for employment in any department or agency of the Government as prescribed in section 3(a) of Executive Order 10450.
