November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DUTCH RAID DRUG LAB, ARREST FOUR PEOPLE

Dutch police have raided a large drug laboratory in the southern Netherlands, seizing 4.7 gallons of amphetamine oil and arresting four people, a spokesman said Friday.

The oil, used to make amphetamine powder, could have produced illegal drugs with a street value of about $300,000, the spokesman said.
