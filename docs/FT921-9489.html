920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / Demonstraters call for British soldiers to quit N. Ireland Troop movement: demonstrators calling for British soldiers to quit Northern Ireland staged a protest near Whitehall in London yesterday. At the same time an unexploded bomb was found in a nearby phone kiosk after a warning. No one admitted responsibility but the anti-terrorist squad said the device bore all the hallmarks of the Provisional IRA
