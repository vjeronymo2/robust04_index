931102 FT 02 NOV 93 / Amec to update BP refinery BP OIL has awarded Amec Engineering a Pounds 60m contract to install new equipment at its refinery at Grangemouth in Fife. The work will enable the complex to meet new European Community standards for sulphur content of diesel fuel. The installation of the hydrofiner will allow the refinery to reduce the sulphur content of its diesel fuel from 0.2 per cent by weight to the new EC standard of 0.05 per cent by 1996. About 250 construction jobs will be created.
