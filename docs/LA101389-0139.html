October 13, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FINISHING TOUCHES: Newport Center Fashion Island is about to unveil the last phase of a $100-million remodeling (D6), and Irvine Co. Chairman Donald L. Bren is taking a personal interest. . . . The reclusive Bren is so hands-on, says an official, that earlier this week "he asked that a planter be moved a quarter-turn."
