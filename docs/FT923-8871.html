920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / Finance & The Family (Briefcase, Q&A): No opt-out for self-employed I AM self-employed. Can I opt out of the state pension scheme, and if so, can I get back the money and interest I have paid in? The answer to both questions is no. Contracting-out rebates are only available for employees taxed under Schedule E as the self-employed do not qualify for SERPS. Contracting-out cannot be backdated.
