930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Severe conditions cut UDO to Pounds 1.41m UDO Holdings, which supplies drawing office equipment and reprographic services, saw continuing severe trading conditions cut pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.71m to Pounds 1.41m in the six months to January 31. Turnover dropped by Pounds 1m to Pounds 23.9m. Earnings per share declined to 3.22p (6.23p), but the interim dividend is raised from 1.94p to 2.07p.
