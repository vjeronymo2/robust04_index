BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 2 (KCNA) -- The letter of congratulations of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song to the delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries now in session here has evoked great repercussions on party officials and working people. Yi Yong-kil, secretary of the Yanggang Provincial Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, said: "The great leader's letter of congratulations is an immortal banner to enhance the function and role of party cells, the basic organisations, strengthen our party into an unconquerable party and increase its leadership ability, and a powerful weapon to bring into full play the advantages and vitality of socialism in our country. "The provincial party committee will effectively conduct the organisational and political work to thoroughly fulfil the militant tasks set out in the letter of congratulations so that all party cell secretaries and party members in the province may live and work as the vanguard fighters of the revolution should." Yi Sung-nam, chief secretary of the Kangdong County, Pyongyang municipality, party committee, said: "The great leader's letter of congratulations is a highly important guideline for our people's struggle to strengthen the party and promote the general march of socialism of our own style. I will make my utmost efforts to build up all the party cells in the county to be cornerstones and powerful militant units loyal to the guidance of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il." No Tu-chol, vice-chairman of the state planning commission, said: "The great leader in his letter of congratulations laid stress again on the need to uphold the unified leadership of the party Central Committee with loyalty, thus providing a powerful weapon to immensely strengthen the ideological and moral unity and cohesion of our revolutionary ranks around the dear leader. "We will take it as the key point in our work to establish the unified leadership system of the party, full of pride in having the illustrious leader and confidence in victory of the revolution and, at the same time, creditably attain the main objectives of the party's revolutionary economic strategy, thus positively contributing to defending and exalting our socialism centred on the masses." Chang Chun-sik, a party cell secretary of the Tokchon coal mine, said: "We will conduct the party activities better with loyalty to the party and the leader, confidence in victory in the socialist cause and indomitable will, no matter how the situation may change, so as to build up our party cell into a cornerstone firmly supporting the party and socialism."
