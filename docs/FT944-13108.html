941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican share prices gave back some of their earlier gains in late trading, but were still moderately higher at the end of a speculative session. The IPC index of 37 leading shares was up 12.29, or 0.48 per cent, to 2,592.29. it had earlier risen to 2,621.07. Traders said some investors jumped in and out of the market to make short-term gains. Volume was a low 75.6m.
