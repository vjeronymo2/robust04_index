August 5, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

BERNSON DESERVES CRITICISM

It is not surprising that people's trust in local politics is waning when faced with conduct like Councilman Hal Bernson's.

His response to overwhelming opposition to his own pet project, Porter Ranch, has ranged from disinterest to implied threats. His most recent remarks are truly outrageous. In his reaction to news that the city of Simi Valley is pursuing legal action against the Porter Ranch plan, Bernson says citizens "hoodwinked" and "duped" a government.

There is no don for the Valley. Bernson acts like he wants to make offers that people can't refuse. Let's get a grip and proceed in a democratic, responsible way.

ROGER STRULL

Granada Hills
