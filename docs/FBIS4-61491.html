BFN [Text] Imamali Rahmonov, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Tajikistan, has arrived in Moscow on an official visit. Issues related to the Russian-speaking population in Tajikistan and the strengthening of the Tajikistan-Afghanistan border are being discussed at a meeting with the Russian leadership. Attention is also being paid to the prospects of a dialogue between the government of Tajikistan and the opposition in the republic.
