BFN [Text] Warsaw, June 28 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Tuesday recalled Wlodzimierz Dabkowski from the post of undersecretary of state at the ministry of agriculture and food economy, the government press office reported. On the motion of the agriculture minister, the prime minister appointed Katarzyna Duczkowska-Malysz undersecretary of state at the ministry on Tuesday.
