BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 29 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, sent messages to Indrajit Gupta, general secretary of the National Council of the Communist Party of India, and Youssef Amin Wali, general secretary of the Egyptian National Democratic Party, in reply to their messages of greetings on the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. The reply messages expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the Workers' Party of Korea and these parties would develop more favorably and wished them success in their responsible work to strengthen the parties.
