921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Argentine rates cap Argentine economy minister Domingo Cavallo has announced a package to reduce business costs, writes John Barham in Buenos Aires. Among the measures are a cap on interest rates of 27 per cent a year at state banks, a reduction in employers' social security contributions, and a further round of deregulation. Also, new tax rebates and incentives aim to encourage exports and imports of capital goods. Initial estimates put the cost of the package at the equivalent of more than Dollars 1.3bn a year.
