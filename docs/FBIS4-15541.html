BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, 11 Apr -- The Russian Federation servicemen carrying out peacekeeping tasks in the Sarajevo area suffered no injuries in the air raids by two NATO bombers that on the evening of 10 April carried out strikes against Serb positions near the Bosnian town of Gorazde. Colonel-General Yevgeniy Podkolzin, commander of the air assault troops, informed the ITAR-TASS correspondent of this today, citing the morning report from commanders of both peacekeeping battalions fulfilling tasks in the Balkans. Yevgeniy Podkolzin was very adverse in his response to the NATO generals' decision to inflict strikes against civilian targets located far from the front line, which have caused to casualties. "The Russian Federation soldiers in this conflict see their task to be to bring peace to people, not to resort to such extreme measures", the commander of the air assault troops stressed.
