January 9, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: DEL MAR

About 500 gallons of sewage overflowed from a manhole in Del Mar on Monday, according to County Health Services Department officials.

The spill began at 1:15 p.m. when a manhole at Lamista and Camino Del Mar became blocked for unknown reasons.

The sewage flowed into the street and emptied into the ocean from a storm drain at the end of Ninth Street before the spill was stopped at 2 p.m.

Signs warning of the contamination have been posted at several points along the storm drain, and will remain for 24 hours.
