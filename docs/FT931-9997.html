930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Saints net asset value up 16% Net asset value of Scottish American Investment rose some 16 per cent, from 131.7p to 152.2p, during 1992. Attributable revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 10.6m (Pounds 9.71m), equivalent to earnings of 4.73p (4.34p) per share. A proposed final distribution of 1.11p brings the total to 4.35p (4.12p).
