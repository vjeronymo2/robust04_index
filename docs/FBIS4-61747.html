BFN [Text] Members of the state customs committee of Russia uncovered a batch of illegal weapons on board an airliner on a flight from Tbilisi after it landed at Moscow's Vnukovo airport on Saturday, officials of the anti-narcotics department told Interfax.. A bag containing three AKM automatic guns and six rounds of ammunition was found in the first section of the Tupolev-154 aircraft after the passengers had deboarded. A Russian-made sniper rifle complete with optical targeting and 28 cartridges as well as two pistols were uncovered in other bag held in the cargo bay. There was no tag on it. Prosecuting officers will handle the case after police investigations have ended Police officials say it will be hard to establish the identity of the arms smugglers because passengers on a flight from Tbilisi usually take seats as they please.
