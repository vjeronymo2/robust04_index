BFN [Text] Kiev, As UNIAN reported earlier, Ukraine's Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko was in Turkey on 7 April on a one-day official visit. In the opinion of Ukraine's Foreign Ministry officials, the visit was quite successful. Ways to intensify bilateral economic relations were discussed. According to the Foreign Ministry, the two sides attach particularly great importance to the interaction within the Black Sea Economic Cooperation framework, especially the fulfilment of bilateral and multilateral cooperation projects in investments, first of all in the sphere of infrastructure modernization and integration in sea, automobile, and air transport. Turkey is looking for access to the Ukrainian market and proposes cooperation in such spheres as metallurgy and metal work, food processing, ship-building, carriage and bus construction, textiles, sewn goods, cellulose and paper, mining, coal, chemical and petrochemical industries.
