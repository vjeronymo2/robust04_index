Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 26/1/1994 -- The 8th round of Moldovan-Russian talks on the statute, conditions and agenda of the 14th Army retreat, planned for the end of February in Chisinau has been postponed, stated to BASA Lieutenant Colonel Anatol Cosciug, head of Military Politics and Treaties Section of the Moldovan Defense Ministry. Cosciug specified that for the time being the parties failed to establish the day of beginning the talks, since presently the retreat is discussed at the highest level. The previous round was postponed because Russia demanded to grant special juridical statute to Moldova east regions as a condition for the Army's withdrawal.
