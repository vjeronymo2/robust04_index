920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Sao Paulo fire order for UK Simon Access, part of the UK's Simon Engineering, has won a Dollars 75m (Pounds 39m) contract to supply specialist machines and services to Sao Paulo Fire Service in Brazil, Andrew Baxter writes. The deal is believed to be the largest single order awarded for high-level aerial fire-fighting equipment. Simon will supply over 80 multi-purpose fire-fighting vehicles to supplement the large Simon fleet already in service in Brazil.
