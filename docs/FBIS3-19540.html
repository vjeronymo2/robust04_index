Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Madrid, March 17, IRNA -- The visiting Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Mahmud Va'ezi discussed promotion of Tehran-Madrid ties in separate meetings with Spanish officials Wednesday [16 March]. In a meeting with the Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solano, the two sides discussed the latest international and regional developments. Promotion of parliamentary cooperation was stressed in Va'ezi's meeting with President of the Spanish Senate Juan Jose Laborda Martin. The two sides called for expansion of mutual ties. Accepting an invitation from the Iranian Majlis Speaker Hojjat ol-Eslam 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri, the Spanish official expressed hope to visit Tehran at the first opportune time. Meanwhile, Va'ezi discussed mutual economic and industrial ties in a meeting with Minister of State for Economic Affairs Alfredo Pastor. Voicing his country's willingness to expand ties with Tehran, Pastor called for further contribution of Spanish institutions to various projects in Iran.
