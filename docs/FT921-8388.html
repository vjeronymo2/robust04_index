920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Second Alliance net asset value at Pounds 13.62 Net asset value of The Second Alliance Trust stood at Pounds 13.62p per stock unit at January 31 1992. The figure was virtually unchanged over the six months to that date, but represented an improvement of 25 per cent on the net asset value at the interim stage of 1991. Attributable revenue also showed little change at Pounds 3.42m for earnings of 17.83p (17.82p) per stock unit. The interim dividend is lifted by 0.5p to 12p.
