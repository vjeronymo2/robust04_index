Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In the Dutch city of Maastricht, the Spanish secretary for defense has signed, alongside his German and Dutch counterparts, an international cooperation agreement for a program to construct F-100 class frigates. According to this agreement, the Spanish Navy will obtain four new frigates. The construction of these frigates is to start in 1996 and they will be equipped with high-, medium-, and low-flying antiaircraft missiles, ground-to-ground missiles, and helicopters with air-to-ground missiles and torpedoes.
