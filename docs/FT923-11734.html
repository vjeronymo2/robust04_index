920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / International Company News: Hershey moves ahead to Dollars 34.5m HERSHEY Foods, the largest publicly-traded manufacturer of chocolate and confectionery products in the US, saw second-quarter profits rise to Dollars 34.5m after tax, from Dollars 31.9m a year earlier, writes Nikki Tait in New York. This takes after-tax earnings for the first six months of the year to Dollars 93.4m, compared with Dollars 80.5m in the same period of 1991.
