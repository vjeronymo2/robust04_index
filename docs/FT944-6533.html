941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Daniel Thwaites ahead at Pounds 3.84m Daniel Thwaites, the brewer, wine merchant and public house and hotels operator, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.17m to Pounds 3.84m in the half year to September 30. Last time there was an exceptional Pounds 565,000 loss on the sale of properties and related plant and the result this time was also helped by reduced interest charges of Pounds 1.92m (Pounds 2.26m). Turnover improved to Pounds 40.2m (Pounds 38m). Earnings per share emerged at 12.9p (9.9p) and the interim dividend is raised to 1.3p (1.2p). The company's shares trade under Rule 535 (2).
