940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: New Throgmorton New Throgmorton Trust (1983), the split capital investment trust, reported increased net asset values over the year to March 31. The figure for the capital shares with prior charges at par was 179.2p, against 164.4p. The fully diluted value was 171.3p (133.9p). Lower dividend income contributed to a fall in net revenue to Pounds 1.85m (Pounds 2.15m) for earnings per income share of 4.72p (5.48p). The proposed final dividend is cut from 2.25p to 0.25p leaving the total for the year at 4.75p (6.75p).
