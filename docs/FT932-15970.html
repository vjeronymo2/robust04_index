930408 FT 08 APR 93 / Housing starts rise by 19% NEW HOUSING starts for the first quarter were 40,800, according to the National House Building Council. This is a rise of 19 per cent on the first quarter of last year after adjustment for the high level of registra-tions made in March last year which preceded an increase in the council's fees to builders. Completions for the quarter were 32,200, a fall of 12 per cent on the first quarter of last year.
