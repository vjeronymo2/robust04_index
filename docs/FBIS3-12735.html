Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The IDF [Israeli Defense Forces] continue to implement plans in Gaza and Jericho despite the massacre in Hebron, on the assumption that the negotiations will continue. A senior military source told our military affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe that alternative sites are being built to absorb the forces and command, buildings, towers, antennas, and communications installations. Work is also continuing on a fence around the Gaza Strip. No redeployment is being carried out right now. Our correspondent reports that since the Hebron massacre, the security authorities and the IDF have been holding daily discussions and evaluations.
