BFN [Excerpts] The esteemed leader of the Islamic revolution, His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, has met the chancellor and deputy heads of the Islamic Open University [IAU] and the heads of this university's branches from all over the country. He made a speech praising the role played by the IAU in providing higher education in every region of the country. The esteemed leader stressed the need for maintaining the religious and revolutionary atmosphere ruling over the IAU. During the meeting the esteemed leader welcomed the future IAU plans to upgrade the academic achievements of this university and to promote full supremacy of Islamic values. He said: The IAU atmosphere must be protected from the penetration of poisonous and anti-Islamic thoughts, and the managing board of this private university is justified in preventing the expression of any remarks against the Islamic and revolutionary values in the teaching and research environment. [passage omitted] The esteemed leader of the Islamic revolution referred to the strong religious and revolutionary belief of the people as the main factor enabling them to stand against the all-round pressures of the enemies of Islam. In conclusion, His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i stressed that the IAU graduates, who are blessed with Islamic ethics, will be accepted and relied upon in all departments of the regime.
