BFN [Text] The third memorandum of understanding on economic cooperation between the Islamic Republic of Iran and Thailand was signed in Tehran last night. On the basis of this memorandum, Tehran and Bangkok will cooperate in food import-export, iron, steel, copper, sugar, rice, ghee, palm, agricultural goods, fishing, equipment, telecommunications, and tourism. The setting up of trade and industrial fairs and cooperation in banking and shipping, as well as links between the two countries' chambers of commerce, are among other points in the memorandum of understanding. This memorandum was signed by the Iranian minister of post, telegraph, and telephone and the Thai commerce minister.
