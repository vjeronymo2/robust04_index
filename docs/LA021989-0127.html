February 19, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

EL CAMINO REAL WRESTLERS CLAIM 5TH CONSECUTIVE TITLE

El Camino Real High wrestlers won six of 12 weight divisions and placed second in two others to win their fifth consecutive Valley League title Saturday at El Camino Real.

The Conquistadores scored 202 points to the 163 1/2 of runner-up San Fernando. Canoga Park (146), Birmingham (91), Granada Hills (42) and Chatsworth (34 1/2) rounded out the scoring.

Greg McMurray defeated Marcus Quek of Chatsworth, 6-0, in the 112-pound final to improve his record to 31-2.
