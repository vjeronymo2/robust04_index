940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: US coach banks on high-tech approach It may not guarantee success, but the US team has an array of high-tech gadgets to help them assess rivals teams. Coach Bora Milutinovic's portable fax machine runs almost non-stop, his tape machines hum and the mail brings a steady flow of videotapes and publications. Each US player gets an individualised videotape before each game, analysing opponents' styles and habits. The US team played Colombia last night in Los Angeles in their second Group A game. It is feared that US interest in the tournament will slump if the home team is brusquely bundled out.
