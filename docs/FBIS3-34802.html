Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sudan has said that paragraphs occurring in the report of Gaspard Biru, who is the special rapporteur on human rights in Sudan, represent a deliberate insult to the Islamic religion, and they make it necessary to interrogate the author of the report and be condemned by all states, authorities and organizations, which respect the rights of man and humanity. This is because of the insult against Islam included in the report. In a statement issued yesterday by the Foreign Ministry in this connection, it strongly condemned the insult addressed by the rapporteur against the Islamic religion and its beliefs. The Foreign Ministry demanded the ommission of the paragraphs from the report touching on this matter. The statement pointed out that Biru's report is part of a bigger plot targeting the Islamic religion and its beliefs.
