930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Overseas Trust Net asset value per ordinary share of the Overseas Investment Trust rose from 274.6p to 350.8p over the 12 months ended March 31. At end-September 1992 the figure stood at 268.8p. After-tax revenue for the half year to March 31 slipped to Pounds 452,000 (Pounds 469,000). Earnings per share emerged at 1.19p (1.23p). The interim dividend is lifted to 0.85p (0.8p).
