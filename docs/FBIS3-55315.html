Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoriya] [Text] Tbilisi January 7 TASS -- Late Thursday evening a group of Georgian law enforcement officials took a chartered plane out of Tbilisi to Groznyy, capital of the Chechen republic, Georgian First Deputy Minister of Security Avtandil Ioseliani told ITAR-TASS on Friday. The officials intend to meet with the person who first informed Manana Gamsakhurdia and the Chechen leadership about the death of Zviad Gamsakhurdia, the ousted president of Georgia. The goal of the trip is to determine the accuracy of the reports about Gamsakhurdia's death. Ioseliani stressed that the group's fact-finding trip to Chechnya became possible due to an agreement reached with a delegation from Chechnya, visiting Tbilisi. The Georgian officials from the foreign ministry, security ministry, and procurator's office left for Groznyy together with the returning Chechen delegation.
