Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, received at the Presidential Palace in Ta'izz today the ambassadors of the EC member states to our country. They discussed several issues concerning relations between our country and the EC member states. The ambassadors explained their countries' positions toward developments in our country, stressing the EC member states' support for unity and democracy in Yemen, as well as their eagerness to solve the current political crisis in our country through peaceful dialogue apart from violence, military force, and actions that are harmful to unity. They pointed out that unified Yemen is an important element in the region's stability and world peace. The brother president then expressed his thanks and his appreciation for the EC member states' positions and their support for unity and democracy in our country, stressing that our country is eager to promote relations with their countries to serve the joint interests of our people and their countries' peoples. President Salih also stressed his desire to start a peaceful and democratic dialogue to deal with all issues and differences to promote the march of unity, democracy, and social peace in our country. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwa.
