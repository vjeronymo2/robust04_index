Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yulia Kantor] [Text] St. Petersburg February 8 TASS -- A regional conference of the Communist Party of Russian Federation was held here in an atmosphere of conspiracy, well-informed source told ITAR-TASS. According to the same source, Russian communist leader Gennadiy Zyuganov told the conference his party will not join any coalition, and will run on its own in the elections to the local governments. This declaration met with a mixed reaction on the part of some district party organizations that insist on forming a bloc with the electoral coalition Rodina (Fatherland), with which other communist organisations of St. Petersburg are allied. Yuriy Belov has been elected head of the St. Petersburg regional Communist Party committee. He accumulated vast experience of work in the party apparat in the Soviet era when the country was dominated by a single-party system.
