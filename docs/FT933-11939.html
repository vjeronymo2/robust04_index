930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: CRP Leisure Shares in CRP Leisure, the USM-quoted public house refurbishment company, resumed trading at 8p following a refinancing backed by stockbrokers RA Coleman (North Wales). The shares were suspended at 7 1/2 p on March 26 this year, following financial problems connected with its CR Pursehouse public house refurbishment subsidiary. This was placed into liquidation on March 30. CRP made a loss of Pounds 117,000 (Pounds 196,000) on turnover of Pounds 340,000 (Pounds 1.54m) for the six months ended April 30 1993. Losses per share were 0.7p (1.2p).
