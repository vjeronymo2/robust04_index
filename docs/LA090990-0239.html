September 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DESIGNERS TO HONOR GETTY ART CURATOR

Gillian Wilson, curator of decorative arts at the J. Paul Getty Museum, has been selected by the California Los Angeles chapter of the American Society of Interior Designers as the 1990 recipient of its prestigious Crystal Torch Award.

Wilson will be honored today at the fourth annual Crystal Torch Award luncheon in the Crystal Room of the Beverly Hills Hotel. Event proceeds benefit the Foundation for Interior Design Education Research.
