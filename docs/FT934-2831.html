931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Dawn Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust saw net asset value per share rise from 155.96p to 250.11p over the year to end-October. Net revenue for the six months to October 31 fell to Pounds 185,056 (Pounds 219,365) for earnings per share of 0.62p (0.73p).
