921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Blackwood Hodge in black with Pounds 17.8m Blackwood Hodge, the building and construction equipment and engineering products subsidiary of BM Group, produced a pre-tax profit of Pounds 17.8m for the year to June 30, against a loss of Pounds 45.2m for the previous 18 months on turnover of Pounds 280m compared with Pounds 570.3m. Earnings per share were 5.8p, against losses of 31.5p.
