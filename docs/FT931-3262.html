930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Assoc Nursing plans Pounds 1.5m placing Associated Nursing Services, the nursing homes specialist, is placing 800,000 new ordinary shares at 195p each to raise Pounds 1.5m. ANS is considering making a cash offer for Broadwater Homes for which it will use the proceeds. ANS already owns 20.86 per cent of the Broadwater equity and manages its two nursing homes.
