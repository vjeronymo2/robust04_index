920416 FT 16 APR 92 / International Company News: Maier quits DFC NZ MR SANDY Maier, the former Citibank executive given the task of sorting out the collapse of the former state-owned development bank DFC New Zealand in 1989, has resigned to follow other business interests, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. An American, Mr Maier says he has no other position to go to, but wants to remain in New Zealand.
