920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market has been relatively quiet, both in origin and in consuming markets, and the white pepper price hardly changed, reports Man Producten. Muntok white, faq (fair average quality), is available for spot delivery at USDollars 1,350 a tonne and at Dollars 1,275, cif, for shipment from Indonesia during the second quarter. Brazil is more or less sold out. The trend in india is firmer than anticipated, with MG1 at Dollars 1,250 a tonne, cif. But crops in countries like Thailand and Vietnam are becoming available now and this has created some selling pressure, with faq grades at Dollars 1,050 a tonne, cif
