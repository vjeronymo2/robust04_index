Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Feb (AFP)--In Jerusalem, Ziyad Abu-Zayyad, a member of the Palestinian delegation to the Middle East peace talks, said the peace process must go on. "Despite all the pain of this massacre I hope that it will not prevent the (peace) process because that is a strategic objective." PLO leader on the West Bank Faysal al-Husayni called for an international inquiry into the killing. "First of all we want the settlers to be disarmed, we want international protection and we an international committee of inquiry."
