Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Evening from Mexico" newscast] [Text] In view of the developments in Chiapas State, the Foreign Relations Secretariat [SRE] issued a communique on the evening of 4 January that states the following: Regarding the possible participation of Central American forces in the conflict in the four municipalities of Chiapas State, the SRE reports that, according to information available to the proper authorities, though foreign individuals may be participating, there are no signs that lead us to believe that there is an organizational [organica] participation by the Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity [URNG].
