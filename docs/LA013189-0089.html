January 31, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

Robert G. Friedman has been named president of the worldwide theatrical advertising and publicity division of Warner Bros., Burbank. Friedman previously was senior vice president-worldwide advertising and publicity. Sanford E. Reisenbach has been named to the newly created position of executive vice president-marketing and planning at Warner Bros. Reisenbach previously was executive vice president-advertising and publicity.
