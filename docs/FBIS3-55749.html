Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Efim Schwarzmann] [Text] Kiev January 12 TASS -- President Bill Clinton is due to arrive in Kiev, capital of Ukraine at 19.00 p.m. on Wednesday, despite fears that his "hastily-arranged stopover in Kiev could prove tougher than he thought" by thick fog which could make his landing difficult in the Ukrainian capital. Sources from among the Ukrainian presidential staff told ITAR- TASS Clinton's stop-over will last for about two and-a-half hours. The American and Ukrainian presidents are expected to discuss bilateral relations, military political problems and international security. After the talks, President Clinton will board Air Force One en route to Moscow. President Leonid Kravchuk will join his and President Yeltsin in the Kremlin on January 14 for a trilateral summit which is expected to culminate in the signing of documents, which will ease tension in relations of the three countries.
