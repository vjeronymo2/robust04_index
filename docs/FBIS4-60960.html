BFN [Text] A report was broadcast yesterday by the news program of the Ostankino television that the situation on the border in Badakhshan allegedly became tense, which has resulted in clashes between the border guards and the Tajik opposition fighters from the other side of the river [in Afghanistan]. According to a report by our correspondent from the executive committee of the oblast and the military unit of the border guards of Badakhshan, this report is groundless and is a mere lie. In general, such reports could destabilize the situation and become the source for false rumors. The situation in Badakhshan and its bordering areas is calm.
