June 15, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"It was just a dream you couldn't wake up out of. I'm O.K. . . . I'm going to go walk on the beach."

-- Daniel Rodriguez, 37, of San Pedro, after his rescue following the accidental sinking of the Barcona, a tugboat he was working on, by a naval submarine that snagged the smaller boat and pulled it down off Santa Catalina Island.
