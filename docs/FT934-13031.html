931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Pearson Pearson, publisher of the Financial Times, has established a new division, Television Holdings, to develop its television interests, especially in Europe and Asia. Mr Frank Barlow, managing director of Pearson, is appointed chairman of Television Holdings. Other appointments include Mr Richard Dunn as managing director and Mr Anthony del Tufo as finance director. Mr Stewart Nazzaro is responsible for Asian development.
