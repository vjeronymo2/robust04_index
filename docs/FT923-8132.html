920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: R'sons Westgarth ahead 18% at Pounds 1m In spite of an 'extremely difficult trading environment' Richardsons Westgarth, the steel stockholder, improved sales, profits and earnings per share over the six months to June 30. At the pre-tax level profits rose by 18 per cent to Pounds 1.03m, sales by 23 per cent to Pounds 26.4m and earnings per share by 15 per cent to 2.84p. The interim dividend is stepped up from 1.2p to 1.25p.
