BFN [Text] The Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] Press Center has reported that President Zhelyu Zhelev today met with a group of National Assembly deputies of the BSP, who handed him a petition with the signatures of 122 deputies from all the political parties represented in parliament requesting him to pardon Georgi Atanasov and Stoyan Ovcharov [prime minister and minister of economics and planning in final years of Zhivkov regime], who are now serving prison sentences. According to the BSP deputies, when President Zhelev has discussed the material evidence submitted on the state of health of the two prisoners and considered the signatories to the petition, he should decide to sign a decree pardoning the two former state officials. The group of deputies found that the president showed understanding for their request and expect a favorable result to the petition in the very near future. According to a second fax we received from the Presidency, the Press Center of the Presidency makes the following announcement in connection with the announcement by the BSP Press Center on the meeting between President Zhelyu Zhelev and a group of BSP deputies: Bulgarian President has received a petition handed to him requesting pardons for Georgi Atanasov and Stoyan Ovcharov.
