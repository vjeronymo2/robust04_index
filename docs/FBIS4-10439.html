BFN [Text] On concluding an overall control visit to the Committees for the Defense of the Revolution [CDR] in Sancti Spiritus, Division General Sixto Batista Santana, national coordinator of the CDR and member of the Cuban Communist Party Central Committee, said that the moral and political status of the people of Sancti Spiritus is satisfactory. Gen. Batista added that the CDR members resolutely support the need to adopt measures to streamline the country's finances. He also stressed the province's effort to fulfill the current sugar harvest plan. Gen. Batista said that Sancti Spiritus has increased the flow of currency into the country through tourism and through the efficient work of the people.
