930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Next 5.75% bond cancellation Of the offer by Next, the high street retailer, to cancel up to Pounds 1.5m of 5.75 per cent convertible bonds 2003, some Pounds 942,520 bonds have been tendered and cancelled. A balance of Pounds 553,283 remains outstanding. Under the tender offer 471,567 Next new ordinary shares of 10p will be allotted, credited as fully paid. They will rank pari passu with existing ordinary shares.
