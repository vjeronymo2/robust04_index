BFN [From the "Baltic Business Weekly" feature] [Text] Public data transmission network, DataLatvija, was introduced May 20 in Latvia. The new network will allow Latvian enterprises, state institutions, banks, border guards, customs and inhabitants to transmit quickly and qualitatively data to subscribers and customers in Latvia and abroad. Presently, DataLatvija consists of three primary network exchanges in Riga, Rezekne and Ventspils. The network will be extended in future and it will include all Latvia's districts. The installation of the above mentioned service costs 150 lats, and subscription for a month costs from 15 to 25 lats.
