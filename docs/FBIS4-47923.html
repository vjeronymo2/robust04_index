BFN [Excerpt] President Kamuzu Banda of Malawi who had brain surgery in South Africa last year has fallen ill again. A newspaper owned by his ruling party reported today that President Kamuzu Banda yesterday failed to attend traditional dances prepared for him at the State House in Lilongwe due to illness. The newspaper quoted the secretary general of the ruling Malawi Congress Party, Mr. Gwanda Chakuamba who presided over the dance ceremony, as saying the elderly leader had a slight leg problem. Government officials were not available for comment. [passage omitted]
