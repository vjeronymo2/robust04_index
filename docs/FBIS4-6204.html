BFN [Text] This morning, His Excellency [H.E.] Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamed, prime minister of Malaysia, along with his wife and delegation, left Vientiane for home by a special plane after ending with glorious success, a three-day official, friendly visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] at the invitation of H.E. Khamtai Siphandon, prime minister of the LPDR. A farewell ceremony for Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamed, his wife, and delegation was held at the government's guest house at km marker No. 3 in Vientiane at 1030. At the ceremony, H.E. Malaysian Prime Minister Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamed expressed thanks to H.E. Khamtai Siphandon and wife for the warm welcome accorded him and his delegation. H.E. LPDR Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon also expressed thanks to H.E. Malaysian Prime Minister Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamed and his delegation for the visit and wished them good trip home. During the visit, H.E. Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamed and the delegation paid a courtesy call on H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of our country, and met and held talks with H.E Khamtai Siphandon, prime minister of the LPDR. They also visited some production establishments in Luang Prabang Province and Vientiane Municipality.
