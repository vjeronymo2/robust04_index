Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- Messages hailing the New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song came from foreign party leaders. The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Malta, Vassalo Anthony, said the Central Committee of his party put the New Year address on the agenda of its first meeting in 1994 and discussed it. He declared that the Central Committee of his party unanimously decided on its continuous support to the struggle the Korean people are waging for the interests of the country in face of the ceaseless provocative moves of the United States under the great leadership of President Kim Il-song. General Secretary of the Togolese People's Rally Amoussouvi Vignik Amedegnato, President of the National Republican Movement for the Democracy and Development of Rwanda Ngirumpatse Mathieu, Secretary General of the Sandinist National Liberation Front of Nicaragua Daniel Ortega Saavedra and party leaders of other countries expressed support to the policies concerning Korea's reunification and the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula reiterated by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address.
