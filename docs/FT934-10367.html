931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Coal Investments relist welcomed Coal Investments, formerly Geevor, received a warm reception yesterday when its shares were relisted on the stock exchange after an 18 month absence. The company is chaired by Mr Malcolm Edwards, former British Coal commerical director, and is bidding to operate five pits no longer wnated by British Coal. The shares, suspended at 4.75p in 1991, returned to the market at 10p and rose to 20p before ending the day at 15p. About 2.8m shares, nearly 10 per centof the total, exchanged hands.
