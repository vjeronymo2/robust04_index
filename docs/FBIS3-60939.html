Language: English Article Type:BFN [Names as received] [Text] Algeria, Jan 4 (AFP)--The president of the court in Oued R'hiou, western Algeria, was killed by armed men Tuesday near his home, the justice ministry said. Djilali Zabane was the ninth judge to be killed, allegedly by Islamic fundamentalist groups opposed to the military-backed government, since last May. His death followed the killing in Algiers on December 16 of the president of the court at Tizi Ouzou, Seddik Bentri.
