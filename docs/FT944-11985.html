941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Chinese Inv A fall in China related stocks led to a decline in net asset value at the Fleming Chinese Investment Trust, down to 84.95p on the September 30 year end. This was 12.6 per cent below the post launch net asset value on October 19 1993. Available revenue came out at Pounds 487,000 for earnings per share of 0.81p. A final dividend of 0.5p is recommended.
