940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Zsa Zsa Gabor Zsa Zsa Gabor, flamboyant Hungarian-born actress, filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in Los Angeles, citing liabilities of several million dollars. Gabor was last year ordered to pay actress Elke Sommer Dollars 2.1m (Pounds 1.4m) in libel damages.
