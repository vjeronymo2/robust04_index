BFN [Text] On 8 June, the office of the provincial party committee and the office of the provincial government issued a notice on halting the abuse of power by party and government organs and their working personnel in making use of enterprises' finance without compensation. It has been decided that from June to October this year, the problem of party and government organs at all levels throughout the province and their working personnel making use of enterprises' finance without compensation will be dealt with seriously in four stages. Marked phased results must be achieved to resolutely curb such unhealthy trends.
