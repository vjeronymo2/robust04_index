May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

. . . TILL YOU DROP: Why would FAO Schwarz, the pricey kids' store, choose to open next to the Disney store at South Coast Plaza (D5)? "This end of the mall has nine children's stores," says President Peter L. Harris.

"It's the finest single assemblage of children's shopping anywhere in the country." As for Disney, "I think we complement each other. The combination is pretty powerful, not competitive." Catherine Gewertz and Tony Marcano
