BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Honduran economic developments monitored through 31 May. The Central Bank of Honduras has ordered private banks to transfer 50 percent of the foreign exchange they receive from export transactions to the Central Bank. This measure will worsen the liquidity situation experienced by the Honduran private banks. (Hamburg DPA in Spanish 1847 GMT 25 May 94) The Honduran Government on 26 May ordered a freeze on the price of all petroleum byproducts. The measure is aimed at ensuring that distributors do not arbitrarily increase the price of these products. The freeze will continue in effect until further notice. (Tegucigalpa EL PERIODICO DE HONDURAS in Spanish 27 May 94 p 5)
