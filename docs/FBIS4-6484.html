BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- The Mali-Democratic People's Republic of Korea friendship parliamentary group was formed at the National Assembly of Mali on March 7. Members of the National Assembly of Mali Oumar Kane and Wadessone Ag Simitala were elected chairman and vice-chairman of the group respectively. The group involves 69 of the 116 members of the National Assembly of Mali.
