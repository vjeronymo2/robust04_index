920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: Sharp recovery at Xtra-vision Xtra-vision, the Dublin-based video rental chain, made a strong return to profitability in the year to January 31. With all divisions now trading profitability, the company made a pre-tax profit of IPounds 1.68m (Pounds 1.57m), against a IPounds 2.25m loss previously. Performance in the first two months of the current year had been in line with budget. Turnover was lower at IPounds 22.9m, against IPounds 26.4m, reflecting the disposal of loss making subsidiaries. Extraordinary charges took IPounds 530,000 (IPounds 16.9m) and earnings per share were 1.13p (2.77p losses).
