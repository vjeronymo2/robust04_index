940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Fair general demand, reports The Tea Brokers' Association. Best East Africans and coloury mediums saw a firm market but brighter mediums were often 4 to 8p lower. Plainer Africans also tended easier where sold. Ceylons moved lower, particularly plainer types. Brighter Ceylons and Zimbabwes sold well but prices for lighter, plainer types tended easier. Best avaiable 210p/kg nom (190p/kg), nom good 150p/kg (150p/kg), good medium 130p/kg (134p/kg), medium 115p/kg (116p/kg), low medium 80p/kg (84p/kg) and the highest price realised this week was 211p/kg for a Rwanda pf. 1. NB Last weeks' figures shown in brackets.
