Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Athens, Dec 28, IRNA -- The Islamic Republic Embassy Tuesday dismissed an Italian press report claiming that the Greek terrorist group `17 November' recently held secret talks in Tehran. The embassy described the report as "totally baseless and lies." "The report is absolutely baseless and completely lies and false. It is a wicked attempt to harm the very close and friendly relations between Greece and Iran," an Iranian Embassy source told IRNA.
