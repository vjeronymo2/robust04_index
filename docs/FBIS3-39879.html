Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of President Suleyman Demirel] [Text] President Suleyman Demirel has declared that Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] can use Turkish ports for its transportation needs, following the Greek decision to close the Salonika Port to Macedonia's use. Demirel had a telephone conversation with Macedonian President Kiro Gligorov today. According to a statement issued by the President's Press Center, recent developments in the Balkans as well as Macedonia's transportation needs were discussed during the Demirel-Gligorov conversation.
