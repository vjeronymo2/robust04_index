Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN ["Notification No. 15/94 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council," issued on 30 Jan 94] [Text] Reorganization of Ministry. For more effective implementation of urban and rural development, the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] has reorganized the Ministry of Development of Border Areas and National Races as the Ministry of Progress of Border Areas and National Races and Development Affairs. By the authority vested in me, Signed; Khin Nyunt, Lieutenant General, secretary-1 of the SLORC.
