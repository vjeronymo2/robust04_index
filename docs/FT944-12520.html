941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Caverdale buy Caverdale Group is acquiring its first multiple-franchise motor retail site for Pounds 250,000 cash. The Lincoln site comprises Volvo and Citroen franchises, with room for a third. The deal also includes an adjacent one acre site with planning permission for a fourth franchise. The group will use this space initially for selling used cars. The franchises will trade under the Godfrey Davis name.
