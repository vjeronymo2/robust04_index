Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 22 Mar (NA) -- Presidential Secretary General Eduardo Bauza categorically denied this afternoon that the government plans to promulgate by decree the pharmaceutical patents legislation if Congress delays approving the bill now being debated in the Senate. "Under no circumstance will that happen" said Bauza, adding that the government "trusts" the bill will be approved by the Chamber of Deputies "possibly by the middle of this year."
