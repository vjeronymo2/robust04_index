BFN [Report by Yolanda Gonzalez Gomez] [Text] Hermosillo, Sonora State, 8 Jun -- Federal Treasury Police agents yesterday seized 10,000 rounds of small-arms ammunition of various calibers at the 21-km marker checkpoint in Nogales. The ammunition was apparently on its way to Guerrero State. The Nogales customs office indicated the seizure was made after inspecting a Dodge van bearing Oregon State license plates, from where the shipment destined for Guerrero came. Hidden inside boxes containing second-hand clothing, tires, and home appliances, the authorities discovered 7.62-mm, 9-mm, .38 super-, .380-, .22-, and .30-caliber cartridges. Javier Chavez Rodriguez and Demetrio Goicochea Rodriguez were arrested and turned over, along with the shipment, to the Federal Prosecutor's Office in Nogales.
