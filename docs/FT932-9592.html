930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Labour to abstain on Maastricht bill LABOUR'S leadership last night decided that the party's MPs should abstain when the Commons next week reaches the final stage of the Maastricht bill before it goes to the House of Lords. Labour's decisison means the government should be able to secure approval at next Thursday's third reading, two days after the Danish referendum. The third reading will be cancelled if the Danes vote no.
