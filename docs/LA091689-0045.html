September 16, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Hoover 37, South Pasadena 6 -- Hoover's defense limited South Pasadena to 53 total yards -- three in the second half -- and one first down, and quarterback James Cottrell threw for one touchdown and ran for another at Glendale High.

Walter Ulloa returned a punt 53 yards for a touchdown and kicker David Bronner added field goals of 26 and 29 yards as the Tornadoes (1-1) built a 21-0 halftime lead. RON PIVO
