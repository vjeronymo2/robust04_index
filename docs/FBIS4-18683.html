BFN [Text] Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri stressed that "the functions of the Italian observers in Hebron will essentially be political, and to provide security for the local population." "The fact that all the sides asked for our soldiers to take part in the operation along side troops from Denmark and Norway," Fabbri added, "shows how much prestige Italy has acquired during these peacekeeping operations." Referring to Somalia, the minister said, "It was the most demanding operation that our troops have taken part in since the Second World War, because of the loss of life that we endured."
