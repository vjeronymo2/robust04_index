Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The government issues a statement in the name of Foreign Ministry Spokesman Chang Ki-ho regarding the referral of the North Korean nuclear issue to the UN Security Council by the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] special Board of Governors meeting. It highly assessed [nopke pyongga] the referral, saying this reflected the international community's intent. The government urged North Korea to dissolve the international community's concerns [kukche sahoeui uryorul pulsik sikigo] and to implement its duties by allowing nuclear inspections as soon as possible. The government also urged that North Korea join the trend of the international community represented by reconciliation and cooperation by fulfilling its duty as a signatory of the Nuclear Safeguards Accord and by taking steps for easing tensions on the Korean peninsula and for building up trust.
