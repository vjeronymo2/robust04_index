November 15, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

SENIORS;

FEASTS FOR ALL;

MANY LOCAL GROUPS SERVE THANKSGIVING DINNERS FOR THOSE WHO DON'T WISH TO COOK, OR DINE, ALONE.

It's difficult to shop and cook for one person. On Thanksgiving, it is particularly hard. The traditional holiday meal takes hours to cook and usually there is too much food for one person to enjoy. Then there is the problem of trying to enjoy it alone.

If you'd rather not cook, and if you'd like to share the holiday with other single seniors, there are plenty of dining opportunities available Nov. 21-24.

THURSDAY, NOV. 22

Anyone is welcome to dine at St. Thomas Catholic Church Parish Hall, 130 W. Ojai Ave., Ojai between noon and 3 p.m. Call (805) 646-4338 for reservations.

The Sedona Restaurant, 951 S. Westlake Blvd., Westlake Village, will serve dinner from 12:30 to 5:30 p.m. to people in need. To obtain a free ticket, contact Catholic Charities (805) 496-1113, Lutheran Social Services (805) 497-6207, or the restaurant (805) 373-5900.

Thousand Oaks Elks Lodge No. 2477 will sponsor a dinner for Elk widowers, widows of Elks, bachelor members and guests at 158 Conejo School Road. A social hour at 2 p.m. will be followed by dinner at 4. Call by Saturday for reservations, (805) 495-3369, 10 a.m.-3 p.m. Between 3 and 11 p.m. call (805) 496-4550.

HELP of Ojai's Meals-On-Wheels will deliver a food basket to ill and disabled housebound people of any age. Donation is $3 or $3.25 for meal and evening snack. Call by 5 p.m. Tuesday. (805) 646-5122.

The Men's Club of St. Paschal Baylon Catholic Church, 155 E. Janss Road in Thousand Oaks, can deliver food baskets and vouchers for turkeys to some needy families and elderly in the community. Call (805) 496-0222.

FRIDAY, NOV. 23

The Moose Lodge in Ventura will sponsor a free Thanksgiving dinner at 3:30 p.m. for anyone 60 and older. Call the Senior Recreation Center, 420 E. Santa Clara St. in Ventura, to sign up for dinner and transportation from the center at 3 p.m. (805) 648-2829.

SATURDAY, NOV. 24
