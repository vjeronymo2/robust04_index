940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / UK Company News: John Haggas improves 9% to Pounds 1.07m John Haggas, the West Yorkshire-based worsted spinner, reported pre-tax profits up 9 per cent from Pounds 981,000 to Pounds 1.07m for the six months to December 31. The result was achieved on turnover slightly lower at Pounds 14.2m (Pounds 14.3m). Earnings per share were 3.3p (3p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 1p. The spinning division maintained a high level of activity although margins were under pressure. The fabric division enjoyed better profits and sales.
