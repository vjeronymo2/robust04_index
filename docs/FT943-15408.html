940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Reliance Security The sale of its intruder alarm business and associated losses resulted in Reliance Security Group reporting a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 828,000 for the year to April 29. There was a comparable profit of Pounds 1.11m. However, operating profits on continuing operations for the USM-quoted company showed a 29 per cent improvement to Pounds 2.7m (Pounds 2.1m). Turnover rose to Pounds 70.6m (Pounds 68.1m), including Pounds 1.66m (Pounds 1.77m) from discontinued activities. Losses per share were 6p (3.5p earnings). However, an increased final dividend of 3.6p is proposed for a total of 4.7p (4.5p).
