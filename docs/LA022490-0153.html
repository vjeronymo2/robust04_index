February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

QUAYLE CANCELS VISIT TO CRYSTAL CATHEDRAL

Vice President Dan Quayle canceled a Sunday appearance at the Crystal Cathedral in Garden Grove to attend the funeral of former El Salvador President Jose Napoleon Duarte, who died Friday after a long battle with stomach cancer.

Quayle was scheduled to appear at the church at 9 a.m. The remainder of his appearances during the West Coast visit were private functions, a spokesman for Quayle said Friday.
