April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ADMIRED BEAUTY

Helena Zukowski's article, "Beauty and the Beasties" (March 11), was excellent and reflected the tranquil ruggedness of Kangaroo Island.

Exactly one year ago we were there as part of our EarthVentures Bicycle Tour through Southern Australia. The trip began in Adelaide and finished in Mount Gambier and included two days on Kangaroo Island. The bicycle trip through Southern Australia was truly an adventure. Bill McBride of EarthVentures, 6608 St. James Drive, Indianapolis, Ind. 46217, provides first-class bicycle tours along with unequaled camaraderie. Highly recommended.

BOB & ALBERTA HANSEN

El Toro
