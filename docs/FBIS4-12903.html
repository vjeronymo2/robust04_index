BFN [Text] Beirut, 13 Apr (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has revealed the organization's readiness to bolster and support Jordan's stance on lifting the international boycott on the Port of al-'Aqabah. This boycott is threatening the Jordanian economy. However, he added that the Arab League has so far not received a request from Jordan on this issue. In a statement he made today, 'Abd-al-Majid asked the Security Council and the United States to review this stance, which is affecting Jordan's economy, to achieve peace and stability in the region.
