BFN [Excerpts] Millions of Ethiopian voters are currently casting their votes throughout the country, with the exception of the Somali region and Dire Dawa, to elect members to the Constituent Assembly. Although there are 22.8 million eligible voters in the country, only about 15,021,581 have registered to vote. [passage omitted] According to reports received from ETHIOPIAN NEWS AGENCY correspondents, people began voting even before going to church. No significant problems have been observed during the voting process; it is going smoothly and successfully. In some polling stations there have been problems because of a lack of stewards.
