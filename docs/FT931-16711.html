930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Callhaven seeks to raise Pounds 1m Callhaven, a desk top computer supplier, has launched a fund-raising operation aimed at attracting up to Pounds 1m from private and institutional investors. The group - shares of which are traded under Rule 535 (2) - is offering for subscription up to 4m new 5p shares at 25p each, payable in full on application. Before the opening date of December 29, firm undertakings had been received for 1.48m shares. Directors believed that the minimum subscription of Pounds 600,000 would be comfortably exceeded by the January 29 closing date.
