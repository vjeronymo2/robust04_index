921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Drayton Blue Chip net asset value down Drayton Blue Chip Trust had a net asset value at September 30 of 56.5p per income share, against 89.4p a year earlier and 60.5p at the March 31 year end. Net revenue was static at Pounds 367,000 as were earnings per share and dividend at 2.905p.
