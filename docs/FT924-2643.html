921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / Observer: Taking off What is the new national bird of America? Why, the building crane of course, the state bird of Texas in the go-go years of the 1980s. Now the transition team thinks the beast ought to be revived and allowed to spread its wings further afield given the arrival of Lloyd Bentsen - Texan and ardent lobbyist on behalf of real estate loopholes during his distinguished senatorial career - at Treasury.
