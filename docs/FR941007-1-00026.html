FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS COMMISSION 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 94&hyph;51; RM&hyph;8466] Radio Broadcasting Services; Mamou and Jonesville, LA AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule; dismissal.

SUMMARY: The Commission dismisses the proposal filed by Simla B. Ellis d/b/a SoTo Broadcasting (RM&hyph;8466), requesting the substitution of Channel 266C3 for Channel 266A at Mamou and the respective channel substitution at Jonesville, Louisiana. See 59 FR 32177, June 22, 1994. With this action, this proceeding is terminated. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Pamela Blumenthal, Mass Media Bureau, (202) 634&hyph;6530.
