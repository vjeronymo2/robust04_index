940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Pounds 39m UK defence contract for Canada The UK defence ministry has awarded a Pounds 39m contract for five Merlin helicopter simulators and training devices to Canada's CAE Electronics, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. The contract marks the Toronto-based company's first simulator contract for the UK armed forces, which have traditionally given the bulk of their business to Rediffusion, CAE's main rival in the international flight simulator market.
