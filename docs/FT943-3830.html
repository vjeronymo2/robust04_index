940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Equity Markets: Other markets - Milan The market remains plagued by political developments. Crucial this week could be a planned meeting between the prime minister, Mr Silvio Berlusconi, and the trade unions which have threatened to strike over planned cuts in government spending on pensions. The market would like to see Mr Berlusconi take a firm line, and then win the backing of his cabinet colleagues over the issue.
