930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: BET Pounds 8.5m disposal to Anglian Group BET has sold New England Windows for Pounds 8.5m cash to Anglian Group. New England is a specialist in the public sector replacement PVCu window and door market. The consideration includes a goodwill element of Pounds 6.25m. In the year to March 28 1992 New England earned profits before interest and tax of Pounds 1.15m. It has factories in Basingstoke, Nottingham, Rochdale and Maesteg.
