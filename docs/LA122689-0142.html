December 26, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

WBL ALL-STARS WIN IN BELGIUM

The World Basketball League All-Star team won its second straight Belgium International Tournament, sweeping three games over the weekend.

The WBL edged host SunAir 91-89 Christmas night as Illinois Express teammates Alfredrick Hughes and Andrew Moten had 20 and 14 points, respectively, and Willie Bland of the Youngstown Pride added 15.
