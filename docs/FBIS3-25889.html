Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 31 (XINHUA) -- China and Cuba will strengthen their cooperation in medical equipment, medical and biological products. This was learned here today from the first Sino-Cuban Conference on Medical and Biological Sciences, at which a trade fair was also held on medical and biological products and equipment. A spokesman for the conference said that "gratifying results" had been achieved in cooperation in the field of science and technology between the two countries. He said that Cuba was good at medical and biological technology, which enjoys a superior position in governmental development schemes. Sino-Cuban cooperation has excellent prospects in this field, the spokesman said. The meeting was attended by a ten-member Cuban delegation and 50 experts and enterprise managers from China. It has been decided that the second fair and conference in this field will be held in Havana, the Cuban capital, at the end of this year.
