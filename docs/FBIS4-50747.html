BFN [Text] Singapore, May 19 (XINHUA) -- China can offer "the greatest business opportunity" to the world, Chinese Vice Premier Li Lanqing said here today, reassuring foreign businessmen that investment in China will "definitely produce good returns". "The progress of the world is providing enormous opportunities for China's development. And China's development, in turn, provides many opportunities for the world's prosperity," Li said while addressing the international conference of "Waves of the Future - ASEAN, Vietnam, and China: Opportunities for World Business". More than 800 delegates, mostly high ranking government leaders and officials, successful businessmen, and policy researchers, from 21 countries and regions throughout the world are attending the three-day meeting, which began on Tuesday. "I can tell our friends with assurance that investment in China will definitely produce good returns and opportunities for your business development," the vice premier promised in his keynote speech. He said that from 1979 to 1993, the total foreign capital that China utilized in various forms reached 137.8 billion U.S. dollars, of which, direct foreign investment was 61.8 billion dollars. The number of foreign invested enterprises already in operation in China reached 83,000 by the end of 1993. Most of them have had very good business performance and satisfactory economic returns, he said. Last year, the total output of those enterprises exceeded 33.3 billion dollars and their imports and exports reached 67 billion dollars, accounting for one third of the country's total trade volume, Li added.
