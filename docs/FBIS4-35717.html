BFN [Report by Sana' al-Imam in Damascus] [Text] Syrian Information Minister Dr. Muhammad Salman has asserted that the recent escalation of Israeli attacks on Lebanon is part of Israel's policy of organized state terrorism against Lebanon's sovereignty. In an exclusive statement to Radio Monte Carlo, Dr. Salman said the timing of these attacks is part of Israel's continued attempts to undermine the peace process and obstruct U.S. efforts to end the stalemate in the peace talks, which were launched in accordance with the Madrid frame of reference. He noted that the cause of the stalemate is Israel's hard-line approach that rejects full withdrawal from the occupied Arab territories in return for full peace.
