940131 FT 31 JAN 94 / International Company News: Goodyear sees higher profits Goodyear, the US tyre manufacturer, expects to report net income before extraordinary items of Dollars 485m to Dollars 490m, or Dollars 3.30 to Dollars 3.35 a share, for 1993, about one-third higher than in 1992, writes Martin Dickson. The company, which will not report its results until February 9, said the estimate includes some Dollars 15m, or 10 cents a share, of non-recurring gains from asset sales.
