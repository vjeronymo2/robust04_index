BFN [Text] An official Foreign Ministry source has issued the following reaction to Iranian Foreign Ministry contentions on 16 April about the treatment of some Iranians who are residing in Iraq: The Iranian regime's claim that Iranian clerics who have been residing in Iraq for the past 20 months are now in detention is baseless. It is an attempt by the Iranian regime to mislead and conceal facts from the Iranian public opinion through false allegations. The source said that talk about Iraqi citizens is ridiculous and illustrates the Iranian regime's insistence on meddling in Iraq's internal affairs that runs counter to a sincere desire on the part of Iraq to settle pending issues.
