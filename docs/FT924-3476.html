921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / Hong Kong link will save 220 jobs INMOS, the UK-based semi-conductor company, is forming a new company with QPL International Holdings, a Hong Kong-based electronic components maker. The move will enable Inmos to keep open its factory in Newport, south Wales, and save 220 jobs. The new company, Newport Wafer Fab, will be 70 per cent owned by QPL and 30 per cent by Inmos, which is a wholly owned subsidiary of SGS-Thomson, the Franco-Italian semiconductor group.
