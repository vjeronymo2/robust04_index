September 7, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

TUCK TO TAKE OVER KCBS ANCHOR MONDAY

Michael Tuck, a controversial anchorman and commentator from the CBS affiliate in San Diego, will finally begin work at KCBS Channel 2 on Monday.

Tuck, who was signed by KCBS almost a year ago, will co-anchor the station's 6 p.m. newscast with Tritia Toyota and do regular reporting on the 11 p.m. broadcast. Chris Conangla, who had been anchoring at 6 p.m., will move to the station's early morning newscast and continue his duties on the station's noon show.
