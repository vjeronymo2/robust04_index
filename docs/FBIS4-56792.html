BFN [Text] The Havana City aqueduct has announced that from 9-13 May, the water supply in several Havana municipalities will be affected. Some zones of the San Miguel del Padron Municipality will be affected due to repair work on the supply wells in the [word indistinct] area. Other zones affected are: Reparto Veracruz, San Matias, Diezmero, Caballo Blanco, the higher part of La Rosita, Alturas de Luyano, and Las Delicias. The management of the Havana City aqueduct apologizes for any inconvenience.
