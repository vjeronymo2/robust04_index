BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Sevastopol, 22 Apr -- An agreement in principle to divide the Black Sea Fleet was reached today at the talks between the military delegations of Russia and the Ukraine in Sevastopol. Of the 833 Black Sea Fleet ships and vessels, 669 units will go to Russia, which makes up nearly 85 percent. The Ukrainian side will receive 164 ships and boats. Moreover, proceeding from the previously reached accords, Russia will pay the Ukraine compensation for 255 vessels.
