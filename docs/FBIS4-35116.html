BFN [Text] Seoul -- The delegation of Peruvian legislators visiting Seoul in response to an invitation extended by the ROK National Assembly have expressed the need for strict adherence to nuclear nonproliferation treaties, which they said are the only guarantees for peaceful coexistence. The delegation of Peruvian legislators comprising Oswaldo Sandoval Aguirre, Carlos Blanco Oropeza, and Antero Flores Araoz warned that the nuclear subject is an issue that concerns the entire world and not only the Korean peninsula. The Peruvian legislators also expressed their hope that mechanisms of understandings will be achieved to avoid any armed conflicts. After meeting ROK Parliamentary speaker Yi Man-sop, the Peruvian legislators said: We hope the right of South Koreans to live in peace will be respected. "Our countries want peace not war because only thus we will be able to achieve our countries' development."
