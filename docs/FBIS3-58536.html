Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Several residents were injured during an arson attack on an asylum-seekers' hostel in Ludwigshafen last night. One woman was taken to hospital suffering from smoke inhalation, another suffered serious bruising. A police spokesman said that the arsonists carried bulky refuse from the street into the hallway of the hostel and set fire to it. A 34-year-old man from Ludwigshafen has been arrested.
