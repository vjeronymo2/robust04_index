October 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

David Evans has rejoined Qintex Entertainment Inc., Beverly Hills, as president and CEO. Evans, who resigned from these positions Oct. 19., reconsidered his decision at the board's request and will hold his post until year-end or until a successor is appointed. Jonathan Lloyd, chief financial officer and treasurer, has been elected to the nine-member board. Qintex Entertainment has filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code for itself and its two operating subsidiaries, Hal Roach Studios Inc. and Qintex Productions Inc.
