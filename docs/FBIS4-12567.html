BFN [Text] As part of their plan to deter the regime from committing crimes and oppressive acts against the inhabitants of the southern marshlands, the Islamic resistance forces on 19 March dealt a destructive blow to the al-Nasiriyah Governorate's air base, known as the Imam 'Ali Air Base, attacking it with 120-mm and 82-mm mortars. Our sources in al-Nasiriyah confirm that the base's vital installations were set afire. The sound of explosions continued to be heard for a long time after the operation.
