April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FOR SOME, THE OSCAR SHOW WAS ONE BIG CARR CRASH

Carr deserves a "raspberry" despite all the misplaced accolades he has received in the press and on television. I have had the pleasure of hearing or seeing about 55 of the academy programs in the last 62 years, and by far this was the worst.

RON LAWRENCE

Agoura Hills
