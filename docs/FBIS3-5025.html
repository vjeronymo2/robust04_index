Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hanoi VNA March 2 -- Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong of Singapore and his wife arrived here this afternoon, beginning an official friendship visit to Vietnam as guest of Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. Present at a state welcome ceremony held here this afternoon were Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet and his wife, Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, Chairman of the State Committee for Cooperation and Investment Dau Ngoc Xuan, Commerce Minister Le Van Triet, Finance Minister Ho Te, Mayor of Hanoi Le At Hoi and other senior officials. Members of the diplomatic corps and representatives of several international organizations in Hanoi and Singaporean ambassador to Vietnam Toh Hock Ghim was also present at the event. [passage omitted]
