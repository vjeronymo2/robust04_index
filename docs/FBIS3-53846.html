Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, Jan. 20, IRNA -- Sudan's first deputy foreign minister, 'Umar Berido [name as received], here Thursday termed as baseless the smear campaign launched by the global arrogance both against Khartoum and Tehran to portray them as terorrists. Interviewed by IRNA, Berido said that economic and political stability in Iran and Sudan prompted the global arrogance to make such accusations. He stressed that the global arrogance which attributed terorristic activities to Iran and Sudan has remained silent towards atrocities being committed against Muslims in Palestine and Bosnia-Herzegovina. Berido said that definitely the two countries will overcome the difficulties with the help of God. Commenting on Tehran-Khartoum relations, the Sudanese official said bilateral ties enjoy special sigfnificance and are deep and friendly.
