November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EVEN CALIFORNIA FANS WERE FIT TO BE TIED

The groan you heard in the Coliseum last Saturday when Cal went for its 31st point were from the Bears' rooting section.

I know of not one single Cal fan who found any satisfaction with that tie against USC, when we had victory in our grasp.

DAVE DEALEY, Cal, Class of 1954, Malibu
