940411 FT 11 APR 94 / UK Company News: Clydeport at Pounds 2.4m Clydeport Holdings, operator of port facilities on the Clyde, returned after-tax profits of Pounds 2.4m for the year to end-December. Despite recessionary conditions and the loss of the Ravensgraig and Hunterson traffic, turnover held up well at Pounds 11.8m. Mr John Mather, chairman and chief executive, said the 'excellent start' achieved by the company in its first year of operation had continued throughout 1993.
