940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / EU call for Chinese ban The European Union should ban the sale of goods produced by forced labour in China until Beijing ends the practice and improves human rights, the European parliament said yesterday, Reuter reports from Strasbourg. Calling for the inclusion of a human rights clause in EU trade agreements with third countries, the parliament said it would not approve any new co-operation agreement with China until there were 'significant changes' there.
