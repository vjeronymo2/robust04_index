Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "New Arrests Over Illegal Possession of Arms"] [Text] According to the announcement of the Municipal court in Tetovo, four people have been arrested in the past few days, all of Albanian nationality, "due to the founded suspicion of committing the criminal act illegal possession of firearms." The Court says that a "larger quantity of automatic guns and ammunition" was discovered on the people. Due to these arrests, yesterday's regular session of the Tetovo Local Assembly war interrupted in the morning hours, only to continue at seven o'clock in the evening, with the presence of the Tetovo chief of police, who informed the Assembly about the arrests. The session of the Assembly was held behind closed doors.
