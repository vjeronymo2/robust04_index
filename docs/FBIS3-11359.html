Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Salvador, 20 Mar (ACAN) -- Ruben Zamora, presidential candidate of the Salvadoran left, has said there will be runoff presidential elections, and he rejected the ruling ARENA [Nationalist Republican Alliance] party has won the elections. Zamora has added there were serious irregularities, as former guerrilla commanders reported earlier, that prevented thousands of people from casting their votes. He has vowed these irregularities will not happen again in the runoff elections, which he expects to win with the support of other opposition forces.
