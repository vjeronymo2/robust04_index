BFN [Text] The brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity has sent a message to the brother minister of foreign affairs in Algeria in his capacity as chairman of the Arab Maghreb Union [AMU] foreign ministers' council. The message stresses the need for the AMU to take united action toward the current developments in Yemen, to work toward ending the fighting between the brothers, and to preserve the territorial integrity of Yemen in accordance with the treaty of the Arab Maghreb unity and in implementation of the resolutions of the Union's presidential council.
