940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Lopex at Pounds 207,000 Lopex, the advertising and marketing services company, improved pre-tax profits from Pounds 144,000 to Pounds 207,000 in the first half of 1994. Turnover amounted to Pounds 73.1m (Pounds 74m). After higher tax and minorities losses per share were 0.37p (0.38p). About Pounds 1.7m will be charged to goodwill in the full-year accounts following sales and closures.
