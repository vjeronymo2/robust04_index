May 24, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION SOFTBALL ROUNDUP;

ST. MARGARET'S POUNDS ROSAMOND

Harper Maycock and Wendy Tacquard each hit two home runs to lead visiting St. Margaret's High School to a 20-11 victory over host Rosamond in Southern Section Small Schools Division second-round softball game Wednesday.

Colette McClaughlin (11-4) struck out 13 and also got offensive help from Sam Ruh, who hit a two-run triple, and Calle Anderson, who had two hits and scored four runs for St. Margaret's (11-5).

In a 1-A game:

Western Christian 1, Santa Margarita 0 -- Visiting Western Christian (15-4) scored an unearned run with the help of three errors in the fifth. Santa Margarita had a runner thrown out at the plate for the final out of the sixth inning.

Michelle Methot had two hits for Santa Margarita (20-4) and Katie Beattie (14-4), who pitched a two-hitter, took the loss.
