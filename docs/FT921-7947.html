920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / Arts: Death and the Maiden - Duke of York's Theatre Death and the Maiden, widely acclaimed as the play of the year when it first opened at the Theatre Upstairs, Royal Court last summer, has transferred to the Duke of York's theatre for an unlimited run. Ariel Dorfman's piece is about the transition from dictatorship to democracy in what looks like Chile, but could be almost anywhere. With Juliet Stevenson and Michael Byrne, above, and Bill Paterson.
