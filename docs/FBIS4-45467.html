BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpts] Sofia, June 16 (BTA) -- [passage omitted] There are an estimated 10,000 drug addicts in Bulgaria according to Prof. Todor Stankushev, head of the Center for Drug Abuse Prophylaxis with the National Temperance Council. BTA's correspondent in Blagoevgrad (southwestern Bulgaria) said Prof. Stankushev cited this figure at a conference organized by the town council and sponsored by a private company. Drug abuse is most widespread in Sofia and other cities. Prof. Stankushev also said 90 percent of addicts take hard drugs which, in his view, makes "the heroin country" an apt description of Bulgaria, the correspondent reported. [passage omitted]
