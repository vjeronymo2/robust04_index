Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] In an effort to provide the level of strength needed to ensure the proper combat readiness of the Ukrainian Armed Forces during the transition to a two year period of military service according to clause No. 26 of the law on general military obligation and service, Ukrainian President Kravchuk decreed to prolong the term of military service for six months for servicemen who had been drafted to service in September-December 1992 for 18 months of service and who are performing their service within the Ukrainian Armed Forces -- the Border Forces of Ukraine, the Internal Forces of the Ukrainian Ministry of Internal Affairs, and the Civil Defense Forces of Ukraine.
