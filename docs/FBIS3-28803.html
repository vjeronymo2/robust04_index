Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [By Chi Chong-hak from Paris] [Text] France's LE FIGARO reported yesterday that North Korea proposed the establishment of diplomatic relations with the United States as a condition for abandoning its nuclear weapons development. The paper reported that North Korean President Kim Il-song said this in his letter to U.S. President Clinton, and that President Kim Il-song also expressed his intention to continue developing nuclear weapons if the condition is not met.
