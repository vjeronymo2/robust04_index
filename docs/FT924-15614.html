921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Smaller companies falter The brief rally in the share prices of smaller companies faltered this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) fell 0.93 per cent to 1068.68 over the seven days to Thursday, October 1. The County index fell by 0.94 per cent to 829.19 over the six days to Wednesday, September 30.
