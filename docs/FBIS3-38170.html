Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] Four persons were slightly injured when terrorists bombed a recruiting office and the Nationalist Action Party [NAP] provincial headquarters in Igdir. According to information obtained from Igdir Governor Semsettin Uzun, a group of terrorists threw a bomb at the recruiting office and escaped around 1330. The explosion damaged the building. Meanwhile, at around the same time, a bomb thrown at the NAP provincial headquarters exploded on the ground, where it fell after hitting the balcony. Four passersby were slightly injured. Operations are under way in the region to catch the terrorists.
