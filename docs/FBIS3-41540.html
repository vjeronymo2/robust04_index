Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Manila, Nov 23 (XINHUA) -- The Asian Development Bank has approved a technical assistance grant of 900,000 U.S. dollars to China for the second phase of an environmental impact assessment (EIA) training project. The aim of the project is to train some 20,000 specialists from government departments, companies and institutions in how to use the EIA process as a key environmental planning tool. The assistance is financed from the Japan Special Fund.
