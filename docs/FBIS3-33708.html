Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister al-Hariri said today that Lebanon will do its utmost to arrest the murderers of Jordanian diplomat Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah in Beirut. Prime Minister al-Hariri has been cited as saying that yesterday's killing of al-Ma'ayitah, the first secretary at the Jordanian Embassy, will not affect relations between Lebanon and Jordan. Prime Minister al-Hariri stressed that the Lebanese Government will do all it can to arrest the criminals.
