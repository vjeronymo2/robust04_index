BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Yefim Shvartsman] [Text] Kiev 12 April, (TASS) -- A meeting of the Russian and Ukrainian delegations ended here today after discussing problems of the Black Sea Fleet, including the incident related to the transportation of hydrographic equipment on board of the Cheleken vessel from Odessa. The meeting followed a telephone conversation of Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin as regards the incident. The delegations were headed by Russian Special Envoy Yuriy Dubinin and Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Tarasyuk. The closed-door meeting at the Ukrainian Defence Ministry took the whole day, Head of the Ukrainian Defence Ministry's Press Center Colonel Alexander Kluban told ITAR-TASS. According to his information, no documents have been signed and no comments on the meeting will be made. The Russian delegation will leave for Moscow tonight.
