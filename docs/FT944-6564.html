941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / Observer: Never say neigh Bad news for John Major - facing a possible challenge for the Conservative party leadership tomorrow if disgruntled backbenchers can rustle up signatures from 34 of their colleagues. Sir Anthony Meyer, the indomitable 'stalking horse' who challenged Margaret Thatcher in 1989, was spotted celebrating Albanian national day at a swish London hotel yesterday. Questioned on whether he felt up to another tilt, Sir Anthony seemed very keen.
