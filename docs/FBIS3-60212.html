Language: English Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "Quick Sentence Meted Out to Young Drug Smuggler"] [Text] A 24-year-old Danish male has been sentenced to eight months in prison a mere two weeks after being nabbed in Reykjavik with 3.7 kilograms of hashish. The man initially claimed he had been contracted to funnel the illegal narcotic to Iceland on the basis of an acquaintanceship struck up in Amsterdam, and in return for a $2,500 fee. The illegal stash was then to be picked up in Reykjavik after he had checked into a hotel. During court questioning the Dane related that he feared retaliation from his "employer," of whose identity he had no idea other than that he went under the name of Franck, and was a foreign citizen residing in Iceland. Despite making pronouncements to the contrary, the Dane subsequently claimed he had acted alone in smuggling the hashish into the country.
