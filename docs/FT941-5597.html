940305 FT 05 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Net asset boost for Latin American Latin American Investment Trust had a net asset value of Dollars 2.62(179p) per share at December 31, against Dollars 1.83 a year earlier - a rise of 43 per cent. The fully diluted figure was Dollars 2.32 (Dollars 1.69). Attributable revenue amounted to Dollars 240,000 (losses of Dollars 175,000) for earnings of 0.32 cents per share (losses of 0.23 cents).
