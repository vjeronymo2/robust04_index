940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / Letters to the Editor: UK ahead or alone in Europe Sir, Two-speed Europe? The slow lane for Britain? Some analysts will feel the European issue is successfully marginalised. Of late, UK policy seems to proceed by an adroit isolation of others. In fact, the process is so subtle that we will shortly stand quite alone. And is this success? Chris Terry, Essegger Str. 63, D-71067 Sindelfingen, Germany
