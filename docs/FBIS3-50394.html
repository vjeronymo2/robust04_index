Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Seven brigades of the Croatian Army, assisted by paramilitary formations of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO], are continuing their fierce attacks in the direction of Gornji Vakuf and the river Lasva valley, the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army's press center has announced. Fighters from Sibenik, Osijek, Vukovar, Vinkovci, Varazdin, and Zagreb are amassing around Gornji Vakuf with constant artillery support. Zagreb's assurances that these are not Croatian Army units, but volunteers from Bosnia were already heard at the beginning of the Serb aggression against the internationally recognized Bosnia-Herzegovina.
