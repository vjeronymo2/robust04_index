920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Company News: GM to invest in Argentine plant GENERAL MOTORS is to invest over Dollars 20m in a new plant in Argentina, 13 years after it withdrew from the country, writes John Barham. It will invest about Dollars 20m in cash and an unspecified amount in industrial equipment for the new facility which is to be set up with an Argentine group. The factory will build commercial vehicles for the local market and for export to Brazil to compensate for rising imports from Brazil where GM has a major presence. GM is joining forces with the local owners of Iveco, the Italian truck company.
