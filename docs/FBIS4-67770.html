BFN [Article signed GB/PI/SH: "Ukrainian Nuclear Specialists Visit Goesgen"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Text] Daeniken, 21 Mar (ATS) -- Five Ukrainian nuclear energy specialists took a training course at the Goesgen power station in Soleure Canton last week. The Swiss Association for Atomic Energy disclosed on Monday that the training focused primarily on the security procedures at West European nuclear power stations. The nuclear power plant operated by the five Ukrainian guests is located 150 km north of Odessa. It comprises three reactor units with a capacity of approximately 1,000 megawatts. As far as technology is concerned, it is similar to the Goesgen reactor.
