920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / New MI6 headquarters nears completion On the move: the new headquarters of MI6, the Secret Intelligence Service, is nearing completion. The building at Vauxhall Cross by the Thames in London was bought by the Property Services Agency for Pounds 130m in 1989 and has been developed by Regalian Properties. It will replace the service's offices near Horseferry Road north of the river.
