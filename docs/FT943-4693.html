940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Russians hopeful on grain output Russia's private farmers are expected to harvest about 10m tonnes of grain this year despite a shortage of fuel and agricultural equipment, the head of AKKOR, the private farmers' association, said on Tuesday, Reuter reports from Moscow. 'For the first time since 1917, private farmers will export grain to foreign countries,' Mr Vladimir Bashmachnikov said. Private farms produced about 5m tonnes of grain last year out of total output of 99m tonnes. Officials have predicted a total grain harvest of 90m-100m tonnes this year.
