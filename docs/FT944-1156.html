941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / EC cracks down on fraud and mismanagement The European Commission yesterday ordered member states to reimburse a total of Pounds 1.3bn - 5 per cent of farm spending - as part of Brussels' crackdown on fraud and mismanagement, reports Press Association. Most of the repayment, relating to 1991 accounts, is made up of fines against Italy and Spain for exceeding milk quotas. Italy must repay Pounds 622m and Spain Pounds 370m.
