Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 3. (SNARK) -- By undertaking this morning a large-scale offensive in Nagorno-Karabakh battle, Azerbaijan has threatened the reached cease-fire agreement, told SNARK today Levon Zurabyan, the acting press-secretary of Armenian president. Meanwhile he expressed a hope that "truce is still possible". "Karabakh conflict has grown now into a phase when cease-fire has become impossible without disjoining forces", told Zurabyan. He emphasized that presently only Russia is able to send such forces into the region. And it is clear by all evidences that Russia is very resolutely intended this time, he said.
