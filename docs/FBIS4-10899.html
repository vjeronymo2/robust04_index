BFN [Text] On 18 April, Candido Palmero, member of the Politburo and first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Havana, said that the province will harvest 2 million quintals of potatoes. During a farewell ceremony for the Giron de Victoria unit, Palmero praised the organization, discipline, and productivity of the unit which, in only 45 days of work, harvested approximately 700,000 quintals of potatoes and over 34,000 quintals of miscellaneous crops. The unit has some 2,000 military and civilian members. The Giron de Victoria unit planted approximately 30 caballerias of tubers and vegetables in addition to tending another 32 caballerias. The Giron de Victoria unit also conducted outstanding social work in schools and small towns in the municipalities of Guines, Batabano, Melena del Sur, Guira de Melena, San Antonio de los Banos, Alquizar, and Quivican. At the farewell ceremony, several workers received the production and defense combatant medal and the status of outstanding in defense.
