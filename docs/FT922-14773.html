920404 FT 04 APR 92 / Finance & The Family Smaller companies slide - At a glance Smaller company indices reflected falls in the stock market this week. The Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version) dropped 2.7 per cent from 1163.53 on March 26 to 1131.74 on April 2 and the County Smaller Companies index fell 2.8 per cent from 925.28 to 899.27 over the same period.
