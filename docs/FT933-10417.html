930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Burmah Castrol BURMAH CASTROL is to increase its holding from 40 per cent to 51 per cent in Castrol India. The company plans to subscribe to some 3.5m new shares of Rs10 each, paying a premium of Rs100, at a total cost of Rs389.2m (Pounds 8.3m). The proposal will be put to an EGM in Bombay on September 2.
