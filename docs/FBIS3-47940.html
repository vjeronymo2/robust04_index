Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 21 KYODO -- A small Japanese trading firm which reportedly arranged for North Korea's purchase of 40 obsolete submarines from Russia has been found in Tokyo, the Ministry of International Trade and Industry [MITI] reported Friday [21 January]. The ministry, citing its own investigation, said it plans to ask the company's president about the allegation after his return from a trip overseas. The company and its president were not immediately identified by name. THE NEW YORK TIMES reported Thursday that a Japanese trading firm had arranged the deal between North Korea and Russia. The ministry said it also plans to confirm the report with the Russian government through diplomatic channels.
