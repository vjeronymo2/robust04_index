921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / John Major jokes with UN soldiers in Bosnia John Major joked with British soldiers serving with the United Nations in Bosnia yesterday when he paid them a surprise Christmas visit. The prime minister, who flew out amid utmost secrecy, ended his journey into the war-torn country by travelling along mountainous roads in an armoured Range Rover. He promised the troops: 'Not for a second will we forget or take risks with your security.' Lost survivors, Page 2
