920729 FT 29 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and cotton JUTE August/September: C and F Dundee; BTC Dollars 395, BWC Dollars 405, BTD Dollars 365, BWD Dollars 370. C and F Antwerp; BTC Dollars 380, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 355, BWD Dollars 355. COTTON LIVERPOOL- No spot or shipment sales were reported for the week ended 24 July, compared with 114 tonnes in the previous week. Activity was severely restrained and business was on narrow lines. High cost of raw cotton deterred users from increasing their purchases.
