Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Zdenko Vranjes] [Excerpt] According to Croatian Defense Council [HVO] sources, Muslim forces seriously violated the cease-fire east of Prozor where they launched an infantry attack on the HVO positions near the village of Uzdol. The attack was repelled. The UN Protection Forces have been informed. Snipers were mainly responsible for cease-fire violations on other battlefields. One elderly person was wounded in the western part of Mostar by a sniper shot fired from Muslim positions. According to the Herceg-Bosna ministry of defense, snipers have lightly wounded two persons.
