BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received an Omani Consultative Council delegation led by Council Speaker Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-'Ali al-Qatabi. Attending the meeting were Ibrahim Za'rur, deputy head of the People's Assembly Arab and International Relations Committee, and 'Abdallah al-Kharusi, Oman's ambassador in Damascus. The talks dealt with the situation in the region, the latest developments in the peace process, and relations between the two fraternal countries.
