940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Progress report lifts Fairway shares 12p Fairway Group, the USM-traded stationery and educational supplies distributor, saw its share price rise 12p to 101p yesterday following an optimistic statement at the annual meeting. Mr Gordon Waddell, chairman, said profits for the first two months of this year were above the board's expectations and significantly above the figures for the same period last year. For the 1993 year pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.25m were returned from a turnover of Pounds 42.3m. All the principal companies contributed to the growth, excluding Fairway Business Forms.
