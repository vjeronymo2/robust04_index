920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Chairman's pay soars at Scot Hydro Sir Michael Joughin, chairman of Scottish Hydro-Electric, received salary and benefits of Pounds 126,000 in the year ended March, up from Pounds 50,000 the previous year. He also received in the latest year a Pounds 5,000 performance bonus relating to fiscal 1991 but no bonus the year before.
