921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / Pounds 280m trade credits made available to former Soviet Union Mr Michael Heseltine, president of the UK Board of Trade, said in Moscow yesterday that Pounds 280m of trade credits backed by the Export Credit Guarantee Department would be immediately available to the countries of the former Soviet Union. The credits were first announced in January by Mr John Major, prime minister, subject to an agreement between Russia and the IMF. A preliminary agreement with the fund was made in July, while negotiations on a full standby agreement resume next month.
