BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 16 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a letter from Min Su-yong, a four-year-old girl of the Kuchon kindergarten, Sariwon city, the seat of North Hwanghae Province near Pyongyang, and sent the following autographic letter to her: "I hope you will become a good student. April 27, 1994 Kim Chong-il" In her letter to Comrade Kim Chong-il Min Su-yong said: "Dear leader, I have appeared on TV twice because I was good at composition, word contest and diary writing. "My kindergartener felt very proud of this, saying if I appeared on TV, the dear leader would be pleased to see me. "So, I have dreamed of being received by you. And I am going to keep diaries in a fair handwriting in the hope of having them read by you." Min Su-yong is very inquisitive and has a good memory. She began to read books and learn composition from the age of 3. In compliance with the party's intention to systematically educate children with rare aptitude and talent, the Kuchon kindergarten taught the three-year-old girl how to read and write the mother tongue in various forms and ways. It also taught the girl with keen observation and imagination how to properly see and judge things. With her composition capacity increasing gradually, she was able to write to Yi In-mo, the incarnation of the faith and will, and to soldiers of the People's Army and kept diaries fairly, deeply impressing people. Upon receiving the autographic letter of Comrade Kim Chong-il, Min Su-yong vowed to study hard with brothers and sisters and become a good little writer.
