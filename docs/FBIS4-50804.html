BFN [Text] Beijing, May 22 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), returned here by special plane this morning after his successful official goodwill visits to Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark and Belgium. Li and his party were met at the great hall of the people by Hu Jintao, a Standing Committee member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wu Xueqian, Wang Zhaoguo, Zhu Guangya and Wan Guoquan, vice-chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and other leading members of the parties and departments concerned. Among those who had accompanied Li on the visit and arrived here by the same plane were Zhu Xun, secretary-general of the CPPCC National Committee; Liu Feng, a CPPCC National Committee member and chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; Mei Xiangming, a Standing Committee member of the CPPCC National Committee and vice-chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy. Diplomatic envoys of the five countries here were also on the occasion in the Great Hall of the People.
