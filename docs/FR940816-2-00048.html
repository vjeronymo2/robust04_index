DATES: Comments must be received on or before October 17, 1994. FEDERAL TRADE COMMISSION [Dkt. 9232] Schering Corporation; Proposed Consent Agreement With Analysis To Aid Public Comment AGENCY: Federal Trade Commission. ACTION: Proposed Consent Agreement.

SUMMARY: In settlement of alleged violations of federal law prohibiting unfair acts and practices and unfair methods of competition, this consent agreement, accepted subject to final Commission approval, would prohibit, among other things, a New Jersey manufacturer of the diet product, Fibre Trim, from making any representation about the weight loss benefits, nutrient content, or nutrient related health benefits of any food, food supplement, or drug without competent and reliable scientific evidence to substantiate the claim.
