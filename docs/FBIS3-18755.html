Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today expressed the Greek Government's strong concern for what is happening at the Turkish parliament. We follow Turkish political developments with great attention and particular concern, Venizelos said, and added: The respect of human rights, the prevalence of justice, and, in general, the respect of the whole institutional structure of Turkish civilization are indispensable conditions for both Greece and the European Union. On the same issue, Greek Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos said that this morning he instructed the officials responsible for the political cooperation of the 12 members of the European Union to raise the issue of the immunity of the Kurdish deputies.
