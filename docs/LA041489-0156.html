April 14, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

National Education Corp., Irvine, reported record first-quarter earnings of $7 million, an 8% increase from the $6.5 million posted for the like period last year. Revenue totaled $99.5 million, compared to $99.2 million last year. National Education provides education and training to business, industry and government and trade and technical training to the private sector.
