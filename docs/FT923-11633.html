920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: AB Consultants dives to Pounds 41,000 Profits before tax of Associated British Consultants, which provides engineering consultancy services to the construction and engineering industries, fell from Pounds 1.14m to Pounds 41,000 for the year ended April 30. The final dividend is halved to 2.1p making a 4.3p (6.4p) total. Earnings emerged at 0.3p (9.5p). Net turnover rose slightly to Pounds 11.1m (Pounds 10.8m) reflecting the acquisition of the Brian Ford Partnership.
