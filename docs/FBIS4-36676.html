BFN [Text] Brother Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN secretary general's special envoy, left Sanaa at noon today at the end of a two-day visit to our country. During the visit, he met with Presidential Council Chairman brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, government officials, and the leaders of political parties and organizations. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the international envoy said he thanks all those he met and who made it easy for him to carry out his mission in our country. He said his visit was a good beginning. He added: However, we aspire for much more. We aspire to see the problem that brought us here solved. Brother Lakhdar Brahimi thanked the brother officials of our country for their abidance once again by the cease-fire that was announced yesterday and which made him optimistic. He said he is disappointed because the cease-fire did not hold last night.
