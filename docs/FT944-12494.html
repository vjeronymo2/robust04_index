941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / International Company News: Malaysia to get first tyre cord factory Andayani Megah, an Indonesian tyre cord manufacturer which is part of the Gajah Tunggal Group, and a unit of Malaysia's Hicom Holdings Berhad are setting up a joint venture to build Malaysia's first tyre cord manufacturing plant, writes Manuela Saragosa in Jakarta. The USDollars 60m tyre cord plant, which will also manufacture nylon, rayon and polyester tyre cord, is expected to be completed by 1997. The joint-venture company operating the plant will have a starting capital of Dollars 75m and will be 45 per cent owned by Andayani Megah and 55 per cent owned by the Malaysian party. According to the agreement, the joint-venture company will start marketing Andayani's tyre cord products in Malaysia prior to completion of the plant. Hicom Holdings Berhad is a listed Malaysian investment holding company partially owned by the Malaysian government.
