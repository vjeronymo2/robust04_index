BFN [Excerpt] Bonn/Berlin (DPA) -- The number of illegal border-crossings continues to decline at the German borders to East Europe. In March 1,300 persons were detained within the sphere of responsibility of the Border Protection Presidium East, which is about 150 fewer than in February (1,453), Eduard Lintner (Christian Social Union), parliamentary state secretary in the FRG Interior Ministry, stated in Ahrensfelde in Berlin on Friday [22 April]. In 1993 the number of persons detained declined from 35,000 during the first half of the year to 19,000 during the second. [passage omitted]
