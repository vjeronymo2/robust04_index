BFN ["Message from the Cambodian People's Party to His Majesty the King"; issued in Phnom Penh on 3 May -- read by announcer] [Text] Respectfully to His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of the Kingdom of Cambodia. Your Majesty: We, your children in the Cambodian People's Party [CPP], would like to beg permission to express our support for Your Majesty's clear-sighted idea on an unconditional, immediate, complete, and static cease-fire as a step toward achieving national reconciliation, ending the war of bloodshed, and bringing peace and security to the Cambodian motherland and people. For the cause of the Cambodian nation, motherland, and people, we will always follow Your Majesty's lofty idea. Please, Your Majesty, accept our loftiest salutations. Please forgive any impropriaties. [Signed] Chea Sim, chairman of the CPP [Dated] Phnom Penh, 3 May
