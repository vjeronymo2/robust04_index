921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: Britain braced for more storms after flooding Weathermen warned that more storms were on the way after driving rain and winds of more than 80mph lashed south and west Britain, leaving a trail of damage. One man died after being swept into the sea at Blackpool. In south Wales and Devon, flooding was widespread. Roads were turned into rivers and main railway lines were closed. Several villages were threatened by landslips. Weather, Page 22
