BFN [Text] Ten days before the presidential election in Ukraine, the former Prime Minister Leonid Kuchma is heading the list of candidates. According to opinion polls, 31 percent of respondents support him. Some 20 percent of Ukrainian citizens are ready to support the current president, Leonid Kravchuk. Here is another report indirectly connected with the Ukrainian election. The "Person of the Week" program from the Vid company under Ostankino TV, which was scheduled for tonight, has been taken off the air. It was to have profiled Leonid Kuchma. Our radio station has found out that the broadcast was cancelled by Gennadiy Shipitko, deputy chairman of the Ostankino company responsible for political and information matters.
