941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / Arts: (Openings): Barbican, London The first theatrical performance of the 'Everybody's Shakespeare' season opens on Wednesday when the Dusseldorf Schauspielhaus brings Karin Beier's controversial staging of 'Romeo and Juliet' to the main Barbican Theatre. Downstairs in the Pit, the director John Barton leads a week of performance of 'The Language of Shakespeare'.
