BFN [Text] Manama, 24 Apr (WAKH) -- Egyptian President Husni Mubarak arrived in Manama from Abu Dhabi at noon today on a one-day official visit to Bahrain at the invitation of His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin- Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain. He was received at the airport by His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin- Salman Al Khalifah, the amir of Bahrain; His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, the prime minister; and His Highness Shaykh Hamad Bin-'Isa Al Khalifah, the heir apparent and commander in chief of the Bahraini Defense Force. The chief of the Amiri Court, the chairman of the Shura Council, ambassadors, and senior officials were also present at the reception ceremony. The Egyptian president will hold talks on bilateral relations and ways to promote them in various domains. Talks will also deal with the latest Gulf, Arab, and international developments, particularly the Middle East peace process and issues of common concern. Mubarak's delegation includes Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Minister of Information Safwat al-Sharif. It also includes 'Atif 'Ubayd, minister of public business sector and minister of state for administrative development; the Chief of the President's Office Zakariya 'Azmi; a number of Egyptian Government officials, and a press and media delegation.
