940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: N Midland has deficit of Pounds 14,000 Exceptional wet weather in the second half and consequential disappointing losses on certain contracts were blamed by North Midland Construction for its fall into the red in the 1993 year. The pre-tax deficit of Pounds 14,000 compared with profits of Pounds 159,000. The final dividend is passed (0.3p), leaving the 0.4p (0.3p) interim - lifted after order book improvements in the second half - as the total for the year (0.6p).
