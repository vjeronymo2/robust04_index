Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 15 KYODO -- The ruling coalition Japan New Party (JNP) and Shinseito (Japan Renewal Party) have agreed to form a parliamentary group in the House of Representatives in late April, coalition sources said Tuesday [15 March]. Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, who heads the JNP, Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata, head of Shinseito, and Ichiro Ozawa, secretary general of Shinseito, reached the accord, the sources said. They have also agreed to call for participation of the Democratic Socialist Party, another partner in the seven-party coalition, in the parliamentary group, the sources said.
