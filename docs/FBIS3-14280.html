Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brothers President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the [Yemeni] Presidential Council, and Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad held a closed meeting at the People's Palace in Damascus this evening. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA' has learned that the talks addressed the strong fraternal bilateral ties, the means of promoting them, issues of common interest to the two fraternal countries, and Arab issues. The brother Presidential Council chairman briefed his brother His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad on developments in our country in light of the accord document and the efforts made to contain the crisis and ensure the convening of meetings by the Constitutional institutions to help implement the accord document. The session of Yemeni-Syrian talks was held at the People's Palace at 1730, Damascus time, today. The talks were cochaired by brothers Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad of the Syrian Arab Republic. During this session of talks, the two leaders reviewed current Arab developments and issues of interest to our Arab nation, foremost among which are developments in the Palestine question and the efforts under way to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in the region on the basis of regaining Arab rights. The two leaders stressed the need to promote efforts to clear the Arab atmosphere and restore Arab solidarity so Arabs can cope with the enormous challenges facing our Arab nation, and so just, comprehensive, and durable peace can be achieved in the region. Issues pertaining to the fraternal bilateral ties were also addressed. His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad of the Syrian Arab Republic has reiterated Syria's support for our people, and for our country's stability. He also emphasized that Syria supports all efforts to safeguard Yemeni unity and enhance its march, given that Yemeni unity is a significant pan-Arab accomplishment along the path of Arab unity, and a gain that is dear to all Arabs. He asserted that Yemen's security, stability, and safety concern Syria and all the sons of the Arab nation, given that the pan-Arab security is an indivisible whole. For his part, the brother president expressed his appreciation of the fraternal efforts and stands of the fraternal Syrians, as manifested in their support for the march of unity and democracy in our country. He indicated that the Yemeni-Syrian ties are fraternal and strong. At the outset of this session of talks, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad welcomed the brother Presidential Council chairman and the delegation accompanying him on this visit. He noted the strong and deep fraternal ties binding the two fraternal peoples. He expressed his confidence that this visit will reinforce these strong ties in the interest of the two fraternal peoples and the Arab nation. This session of talks was attended for Yemen by Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi, Minister of Planning and Development Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, Foreign Affairs Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah, Presidential Council Office Chief 'Ali Muhammad al-Anisi, and Yemeni Ambassador to Syria Muhammad Shutfah. For Syria, the talks were attended by Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Nasir Qaddur.
