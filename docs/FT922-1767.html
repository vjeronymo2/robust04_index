920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Cassia market has firmed because of lower supplies from Sumatra, where farmers are picking coffee, reports Man-producten. As there are no large stocks in Europe this could mark the beginning of a firmer market until the end of the season in November. Korintji a, spot Dollars 2,225 a tonne, afloat Dollars 2,050, shipment Dollars 1,900 Korintji b, afloat Dollars 1,300, shpmt Dollars 1,250. Nutmegs - shippers freely offering again. Grenada defectives are landing in perfect shape and can compete with Indonesian quality sound shrivels; defectives are offered at Dollars 1,000 and sound shrivels at Dollars 1,400 cif ebp.
