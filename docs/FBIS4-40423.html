BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 15 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- The law enforcement bodies of Tajikistan have set out their version of the death of 30-year-old Senior Lieutenant Mikhail Osipov, company commander of the 191st Russian Motorized Infantry Regiment stationed in the city of Kurgan-Tyube and forming part of the 201st Motorized Infantry Division. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was told by Major-General Gennadiy Blinov, the Republic's first deputy minister of internal affairs, that the Russian officer died as a result of an accident right on the territory of the military unit, and was not shot by unidentified persons during an alleged evening patrol check in one of the city's streets. The Republic's law enforcement bodies are presently carefully investigating this tragic incident, Gennadiy Blinov stressed.
