Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A special investigation team has been appointed by the South African Police to investigate the weapons theft at the Voortrekkerhoogte Air Force base near Pretoria. The Air Force has confirmed that almost 50 light weapons were stolen from an arms depot. It is suspected that four people were involved and a joint investigation is being carried out by the military as well as the South African Police. The Air Force would not release further details, saying that this would hamper the investigation. Earlier SAPA reported that right-wingers were suspected of carrying out the theft.
