June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Darox Corp. promoted L. Scott Lane to executive vice president of its Darox Interactive unit, where he will oversee the creation of interactive videodiscs that are being produced at the unit's Burbank facility. Darox is based in San Diego.

Lane also owns a video production company called West Coast East Television, which recently opened a studio/post-production facility in Burbank.
