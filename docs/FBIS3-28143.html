Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Australian Government says it condemns in the strongest possible terms an attack in Algeria in which a Frenchman was killed and an Australian injured. French cameraman (Olivier Kenana), age 34, died in the incident and his Australian collegue (Jeff Allan White), age 35, was shot in the back of the head. He is in a serious condition in a hospital. Algerian officials said the two men were attacked by suspected Muslim fundamentalists while filming in the Kazbah district of the capital, Algiers. [They were] there are on an assignment for the American broadcasting company. The foreign affairs department said Australia has diplomatic representation in Algeria but a British diplomat will visit Mr. (White) tomorrow. The French Government has also condemned the attack.
