BFN [Text] Beijing, April 19 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with a delegation from Hong Kong University and Polytechnic Grants Committee (UPGC) here today. During the meeting, Li expressed the hope that the exchange and cooperation in educational and cultural fields between Hong Kong and the mainland would be further strengthened. The delegation, headed by UPGC Chairman Antony K. Leung, arrived here today as guests of the Chinese State Education Commission.
