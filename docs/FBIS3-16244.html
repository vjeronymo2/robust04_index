Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Ivantsov] [Text] Moscow March 15 TASS -- Russia has resumed full deliveries of natural gas to Belarus after cuts were made to make Belarus pay its arrears for the earlier supplies. The deliveries are expected to reach the earlier agreed 54 million cubic meters a day by the end of Tuesday, as agreed by Beltransgas and Gasprom during their negotiations in Moscow at the weekend.
