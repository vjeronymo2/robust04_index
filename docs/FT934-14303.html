931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / More hope in south than north COMPANIES in the south of England are more optimistic about their prospects but their counterparts in the north and Midlands are less hopeful, a survey released today says. Sixty per cent of companies in the south expect sales to increase over the next three months compared with 53 per cent in the Midlands and 49 per cent in the north, according to the 3i Enterprise Barometer survey.
