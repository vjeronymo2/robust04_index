910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Warnford Invs static at Pounds 6.64m Profits were little changed at Warnford Investments in the year to December 25. On sales up 10 per cent from Pounds 10.77m to Pounds 11.83m the pre-tax outcome came to Pounds 6.64m, against Pounds 6.56m. Earnings were 10.85p (10.79p) and directors of the property owner and dealer recommended an increased final dividend of 4.5p, making a total of 7p (6.5p).
