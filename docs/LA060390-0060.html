June 3, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MADONNA: ONE STEP OVER THE LINE

I am 23 years old and have been a Madonna fan since she first arrived on the scene, but after having seen her recent "Blond Ambition" tour in Los Angeles, I am outraged.

Madonna has always been known for her image of sexual promiscuity; however, in her current tour she has managed to step over the line between sexually suggestive and sexually explicit.

I am concerned about the 10-year-old girls who idolize Madonna and use her as a role model.

I feel that the public has a right to be forewarned of explicit sexual acts that take place at concerts such as Madonna's. It seems strange to me that you must be 17 to enter an R-rated movie, yet a child can walk into a Madonna concert and see her have simulated intercourse with her bed.

MARY E. BEACH

Studio City
