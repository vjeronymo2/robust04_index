BFN [Text] Two cubic meters of water continue to leak every hour from a storage facility in the second power unit of the Chernobyl station, its chief engineer Viktor Vasilchenko told INTERFAX-Ukraine. The leakage from the storage of processed nuclear fuel was detected on 11 June. Vasilchenko said the leakage resulted from the way storage facilities of this kind had been installed. Vasilchenko also said that the heating elements would be removed from the storage before Friday and the source of leakage would be found soon after. He thought that even if the storage facility was destroyed, nothing serious would happen. Alexander Yelchishchev, chief of a shift in the station, had told INTERFAX-Ukraine that radiation in neighboring premises was normal.
