Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, mojahedin have intensified their attacks on Indian forces in retaliation to the atrocities committed against Kashmiri people. Mojahedin attacked military installations in Badgam, Srinagar, Anantnag-Islamabad, and Rajaori area killing seven Indian soldiers and wounding several others. In Bijbiara, mojahedin clashed with the rampaging soldiers killing two of them and wounding a number of them. Another report says Indian soldiers during their siege and search operations have martyred six more innocent Kashmiris in Srinagar, Bijbiara, Kupwara, and Pampur.
