941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / International Company News: Incentive buys German cycle unit Incentive, the Danish industrial group, said it had acquired the bicycle accessories activities of German's Union Dortmund. The unit has turnover of about DKr250m (Dollars 41.1m), writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Incentive owns a Danish cycle-lamp company, Basta, and Soubitez, the French dynamo-lamp manufacturer, and said the acquisition would make Incentive a world leader in cycle lights.
