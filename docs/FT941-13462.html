940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / Pakistan angry over Kashmir Pakistan yesterday said it would seek a human rights resolution, condemning India's actions in Kashmir, during next month's UN commission on human rights in Geneva, writes Farhan Bokhari in Islamabad. The move immediately raised speculation over whether Islamabad was backing away from its bilateral dialogue with New Delhi. 'In the new world, India cannot raise a new iron curtain to hide its shortcomings,' said Ms Benazir Bhutto, the prime minister, on television. She said she would address the commission on February 1.
