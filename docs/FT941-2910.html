940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / Observer: Quango Perhaps Mike Espy, US secretary of agriculture, should be signed up as an honorary member of SPUA, the Society for the Prevention of Useless Acronyms. Pontificating on Wednesday to the House Agriculture Committee about the latest Gatt round and the way to increase farm income through exports, he let slip that 'within our arsenal right now we have a number of tools: the EEP is one, we have the SOAP/COAP and the BEPS, we have GSM, 102, 103, and the MPP'.
