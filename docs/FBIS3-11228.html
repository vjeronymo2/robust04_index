Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Salvador, 20 Mar (ACAN) -- Leaders of the former Salvadoran guerrillas have launched strong criticisms against the organization of the general elections which took place on 20 March in El Salvador. They have said the elections "are unacceptable" and that they would be contested in other countries. Former guerrilla Commander Joaquin Villalobos, leader of the Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front (FMLN), has accused the conservative and ruling ARENA [Nationalist Republican Alliance] party of plotting to reduce the number of voters. He added "many people were unable to vote."
