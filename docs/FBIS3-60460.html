Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Ayman 'Abd-al-Hafiz] [Text] Health Minister Dr. 'Abd al-Rahim Malhas has said that in Jordan 23 people, mostly foreigners, have died as a result of AIDS since 1986. In a statement to AL-DUSTUR, he said that those who contracted the disease in Jordan during the past stage are only 94, of which 62 are Jordanian citizens. Out of the 62 cases, 40 contracted the disease outside Jordan because they suffer from hemophilia, which requires regular blood transfusions. He said that the remainder contracted the disease in Jordan during the same period.
