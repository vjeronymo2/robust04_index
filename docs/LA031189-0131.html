March 11, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ROUNDUP;

TUSTIN, MATER DEI REACH NEWPORT-MESA FINAL

Danny Baker pitched a three-hitter and Tustin High School defeated Foothill, 5-0, in the semifinals of the Newport-Mesa baseball tournament Friday at Tustin.

Baker (1-0) struck out six and walked one.

Ryan Ralph hit a bases-loaded triple in the fourth for Tustin (3-0), which plays Mater Dei today at 2:30 in the championship game.

Foothill is 2-1.

In the other semifinal game:

Mater Dei 5, Mission Viejo 4 -- Robbie Ickes hit a three-run homer and Jeff Ciccarella had two RBIs for the Monarchs (4-1) at Mater Dei. Troy Kopp hit a three-run homer for Mission Viejo.

In the consolation semifinals:

Capistrano Valley 3, Orange 2 (9 innings) -- John Mackay doubled home Mike Lavin to give the Cougars (3-2) the victory at Capistrano Valley.

Costa Mesa 3, Tustin JV 1 -- Rich Schones had two hits and two RBIs at Costa Mesa (2-1).

In consolation games:

Estancia 12, Saddleback 5 -- Jason White had four hits and two RBIs for the Eagles (1-2) at Estancia.

Esperanza 11, El Modena 4 -- Jason Perez had two hits and two RBIs for the Aztecs (4-1) at Esperanza.

In the Loara tournament:

Irvine 5, Huntington Beach 2 -- Irvine's Scott Vollmer had two doubles and an RBI at Irvine (3-2).

In the El Segundo tournament:

Westminster 5, Edison 3 -- Joe Tiano hit a two-run single in the sixth to tie the score, 3-3, and RBI singles by Brett Grebe and Tiano gave Westminster (4-0) the victory at Edison in a semifinal game.

In the Glendora tournament:

Sonora 8, Mayfair 3 -- Kevin Cook had two doubles and four RBIs for Sonora (2-2) in a consolation game at Glendora.

In the Serra tournament:

Orange Lutheran 6, Montebello 5 -- Phil Matheson hit two home runs and had four RBIs for Orange Lutheran (1-2) in a first-round game at Montebello.

In nonleague games:

Trabuco Hills 5, Pioneer 2 (8 innings) -- David Lowery hit a three-run homer in the bottom of the eighth for the Mustangs (2-1) at Trabuco Hills.

In the Corona softball tournament:

El Toro 2, Corona 1 -- The Chargers (3-0) scored two unearned runs in the bottom of the seventh in a semifinal game at Corona.

San Clemente 8, Ramona 2 -- Wendy Stewart had three hits, including a home run, for San Clemente (3-1) in a semifinal game at Corona.

In nonleague softball:

Fountain Valley 3-3, Villa Park 0-0 -- Rae Rice pitched a two-hitter with 14 strikeouts in the opener and Janet Miyahara pitched a four-hitter in the second for Fountain Valley (2-1-1) at Villa Park (4-3).

Santiago 9, Newport Harbor 8 -- Martha Huerta's RBI single in the top of the seventh capped a two-run rally for the Cavaliers (1-3).

In nonleague boys' swimming:

Laguna Hills 81, Dana Hills 75 -- Brian Fischer had Southern Section qualifying times in 100-yard freestyle (48.1 seconds) and 200 freestyle (1:48.37) for the Hawks (3-0) at Dana Hills.

In Angelus League tennis:

Mater Dei 16, Bishop Montgomery 2 -- Dax Peterson won his three sets to lead the Monarchs (4-1, 2-0) at Mater Dei.

In Orange League tennis:

Valencia 18, Magnolia 0 -- Danny Yu swept his sets for the Tigers (1-2, 1-0) at Valencia.

Western 13, Anaheim 5 -- Ed Peregrino swept his singles matches for Western (5-1, 1-0) at Anaheim.

In nonleague tennis:

Dana Hills 10, Corona del Mar 8 -- Esteban Saba swept for Dana Hills (2-1) at Corona del Mar.

Mission Viejo 11, Estancia 7 -- Dave Allen and George Larsen swept their doubles sets for the Diablos (5-0) at Mission Viejo.

Sonora 15, Los Amigos 3 -- Alan Entin and Robert Shen swept in doubles for Sonora (5-1) at Sonora.

University 17, Los Alamitos 1 -- Willie Dann won his three sets for University (2-0) at Los Alamitos.
