Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the ANC [African National Congress], Mr. Nelson Mandela, has welcomed the decision of the Inkatha Freedom Party and the Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front] to register for the elections. However, speaking on his election campaign at Mqekezweni in the eastern Cape, Mr. Mandela condemned the president of Bophuthatswana, Mr. Lucas Mangope, for his continuing hard line. Asked if he was prepared to meet Mr. Mangope, Mr. Mandela said he believed a meeting was being set up for next week.
