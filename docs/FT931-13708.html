930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Owen invited to meeting on Bosnia LORD OWEN, the United Nation's peace envoy, has been invited by the prime minister to join a special review today of British policy on Bosnia, to be held at Downing street. The talks will also be attended by Mr Douglas Hurd, the foreign secretary, Mr Malcolm Rifkind, defence secretary, Lady Chalker, overseas aid minister, Sir Nicholas Lyell, the attorney-general, and Mr Douglas Hogg, foreign office minister.
