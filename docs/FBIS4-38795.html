BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] A report from Kiev: 50 Ukrainian members of parliament today demanded that accreditation should be restored to the correspondents of the Ostankino TV company in Donetsk, Odessa, and Kharkov. According to a statement issued by the deputies, the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry's 26 May decision which is in the spirit of the Cold War, should be scrutinized by the Commission for Legal Safeguards for Freedom of Speech and the mass media, INTERFAX reports.
