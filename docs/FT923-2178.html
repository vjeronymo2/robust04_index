920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Spandex displays modest improvement Spandex, the USM-traded supplier of sign-making equipment, reported a modest improvement in profits for the six months to June 30. On turnover of Pounds 28.3m (Pounds 26.8m), the pre-tax balance emerged at Pounds 2.24m (Pounds 2.16m). Mr Charles Dobson, chairman, said that sales of signmaking computers were static but sales of materials had moved ahead strongly. The interim dividend is held at 1.9p, payable from earnings of 11.2p (11.9p) per share.
