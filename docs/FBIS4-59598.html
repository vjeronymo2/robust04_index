BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Arab League Secretary General Dr.'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid. The meeting was attended by Yusuf Shakkur, assistant foreign minister; 'Adnan 'Imran, assistant Arab League secretary general for political affairs; Ahmad 'Adil, assistant Arab League secretary general; and head of the Arab League secretary general's office. During the meeting, the two sides discussed the Arab situation, especially the regrettable bloody events in Yemen. They both called for a cessation of fighting and brotherly arbitration to spare the blood of the fraternal Yemeni people. Talks also dealt with the Arab League's activities in both the Arab and international fields and developments in the peace process.
