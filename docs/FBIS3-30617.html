Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Partnership for Peace offered by NATO is acceptable for Hungary only if it leads to full membership in the military organization. Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky said this when he met with Gebhardt von Moltke, NATO assistant secretary general. There was a discussion on what Hungary could offer in exchange for the peace partnership. The work schedule mapping out the details for joint military exercises and training and peacekeeping actions were also discussed and coordinated.
