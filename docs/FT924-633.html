921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / Observer: Tail spin The spirit of amukadari - the 'descent from heaven' of Japanese civil servants to senior positions in industry - appears to have been turned on its head at Mazda. Kenichi Yamamoto, who developed the carmaker's rotary engine and led its 1980s expansion, has stepped down as chairman, where he is replaced by Norimasa Furuta, to become 'supreme adviser'.
