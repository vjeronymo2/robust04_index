Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party] says the government should take political parties into confidence and apprise them of the details of the foreign secretary-level talks held between India and Pakistan. Talking to newsmen in New Delhi today, the party spokesman, Mr. K.L. Sharma, also wanted the government to spell out its future course of action as Islamabad seems to have taken an unreasonable attitude at the talks.
