940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: American Trust net assets slip Net asset value per share of American Trust slipped to 288.3p at the end of July, against 308.8p six months earlier. Net profits for the six months grew to Pounds 2.74m (Pounds 2.42m) and earnings per share to 3.28p (2.9p) reflecting increased dividends from UK holdings and interest income. The interim dividend has been raised to 1.9p (1.8p).
