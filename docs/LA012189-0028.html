January 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THOMPSON'S WALKOUT DOES NOT GENERATE A GREAT DEAL OF SUPPORT

The only thing Coach John Thompson proved by walking off the court was that his team can win without him.

Now his students have learned how to react to a complicated situation, just simply walk away from it.

Proposal No. 42 applies to all students. I don't see anything racist about pursuing a good education.

CHUCK PERI

Carson
