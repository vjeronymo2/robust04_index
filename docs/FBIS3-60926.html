Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] The Attorney General's Office announced tonight [4 January] that on 31 December in Ocosingo, approximately 200 heavily armed members of the group that has risen up in arms attacked Mexican Petroleum's [Pemex] RSS-6 exploration brigade, from whom they stole 1,566 kg of dynamite, 10,440 detonators, and the truck used to transport the explosives. The Attorney General's Office says there is evidence the explosives may have been used to destroy bridges in La Virgen and La Florida, 15 and 13 km from Ocosingo respectively.
