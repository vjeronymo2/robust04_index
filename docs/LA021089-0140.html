February 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST : LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

FULLERTON;

FUNERAL SERVICES SET FOR TEACHER HIT BY CAR

Funeral services will be held Saturday for Don Hendricks, a Fullerton College art teacher killed when he was struck by a car as he rode his bike to school.

Services for the popular instructor and renowned watercolor painter will be held at 2 p.m. at the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, 2225 N. Euclid Ave. in Fullerton.

In lieu of flowers, the Hendricks family has asked that memorial donations be sent to: The Fullerton College Foundation Memorial Scholarship Fund, Fullerton College, 321 E. Chapman Ave., Fullerton, Calif. 92634.
