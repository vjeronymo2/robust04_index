September 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ROUNDUP;

ZERTUCHE PASSES BISHOP AMAT OVER RUBIDOUX

Senior quarterback Zach Zertuche passed for three touchdowns and ran for another to lead Bishop Amat to a 37-7 season-opening, nonleague victory over Riverside Rubidoux at La Puente Friday night.

Zertuche connected with Art Cuellar for touchdowns of 27, 47 and 25 yards and scored on a one-yard run.

The Lancers, who are ranked third in the Times' Southern Section poll, also had a big game from junior Scott Fields, who scored on a 49-yard run.

Santa Monica St. Monica 7, Van Nuys Montclair Prep 6 -- A failed point-after attempt was the difference as the Mariners defeated the Mounties at Culver City High.

Trailing 7-0, Montclair Prep (0-2) scored with 2:49 left.

With All-American tailback Derek Sparks gone for the season -- he withdrew from school Wednesday afternoon -- sophomore Eliel Swinton scored on a nine-yard run, but the conversion kick was blocked.

Hawthorne 20, Antelope Valley 6 -- Quarterback Jamil Williams connected with Tarrick Brock on touchdown passes of 52 and 28 yards to lead the Cougars at Hawthorne.

Huntington Beach 28, Corona del Mar 0 -- The Oilers outgained the Sea Kings in total yardage, 322-109, and converted two blocked punts into touchdowns at Orange Coast College.
