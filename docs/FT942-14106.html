940421 FT 21 APR 94 / International Company News: Ex-Paramount chief forms new company Mr Martin Davis, former chairman and chief executive of Paramount Communications, has formed a new company, Wellspring Associates, AP-DJ reports from New York. He said Wellspring would specialise in investing in undervalued companies that required restructuring. Mr Davis stepped down as chairman and chief executive of Paramount last month following the completion of the first step of the merger of Paramount and Viacom. Paramount is now a majority-owned subsidiary of Viacom. Mr Donald Oresman, who quit as executive vice-president, is joining Mr Davis at Wellspring.
