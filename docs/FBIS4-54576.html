BFN [Text] This morning, on the invitation of Dumitru Popescu, the minister of heavy industry, Mr. [Mohammad Hadi] Nezhad-Hoseynian, the minister of heavy industries of the Islamic Republic of Iran, arrived in our country. Mr. Nezhad-Hoseynian is also chairman of the Iranian party attending the 10th session of the Joint Romanian-Iranian Commission, which is scheduled to take place in Bucharest over the next few days. After his arrival at the airport, the Iranian guest stated exclusively to our reporter that the volume of the exchange of goods between Romania and Iran is the highest compared to similar exchanges with all other East European countries. Upon turn, Industry Minister Dumitru Popescu stated that they will also approach new ways of cooperation between the two countries, as well as the furthering of Romanian exports.
