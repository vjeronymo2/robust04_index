December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

QUAKE RATTLES POMONA AREA; NO DAMAGE

A mild earthquake rolled through the Pomona area Sunday -- strong enough to rattle a few breakfast dishes, but without the wallop to cause damage or injuries, authorities said.

The magnitude 2.7 temblor hit at 10:06 a.m. and was centered about three miles north of Pomona, said a spokesman for Caltech in Pasadena.
