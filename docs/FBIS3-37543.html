Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] A meeting took place today between Boris Yeltsin and Government Chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin at the Russian president's country residence. They exchanged opinions on the state of the Russian economy, the progress of economic reforms, and the implementation of social programs in light of preparing the president's annual message, and his speech to the deputies of the Federal Assembly on 24 February, as envisaged by the Constitution, and in connection with preparations for an expanded sitting of the government on 25 February. Particular attention was paid to the problems of drawing up the federal budget for the current year and to ensuring finance for various sectors of the national economy. Some structural and cadre issues were also discussed regarding a number of ministries and departments, and measures aimed at creating necessary conditions for the effective work of the State Duma. Their personal schedules were also coordinated.
