920422 FT 22 APR 92 / International Company News: Imperial Oil falls sharply IMPERIAL OIL, Canada's largest integrated oil company, reported a sharp fall in first-quarter net profit to CDollars 40m (USDollars 33.9m), or 21 cents a share, down from Dollars 196m, or Dollars 1.02, a year earlier, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The latest period included a Dollars 14m after-tax loss on divestments.
