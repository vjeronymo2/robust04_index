BFN [Text] A ceremony was held this morning, 14 April, at the Hoa Binh Hydroelectric Power Plant to inaugurate the eighth -- and also last -- group of generators there. Deputy Prime Minister Tran Duc Luong and Russian Deputy Prime Minister Yuriy Yarov attended the ceremony. Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet sent a congratulatory message to Vietnamese and Russian cadres and workers in charge of the project.
