March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PURE GORE

Barbara Grizzuti Harrison wants to be Gore Vidal. Her profile on him screams it from every pretentious line.

I personally am not an admirer of anyone -- Vidal or Harrison -- whose ego so obviously exceeds his talent. Vidal's own utterances reveal him for what he is. Harrison's rhetoric trivializes her own intentions.

I did, however, read to the end of the article, which tells you something about me.

RACHEL BRYANT DRUTEN, South Pasadena
