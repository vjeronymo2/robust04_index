BFN [Text] The Nagorno-Karabakh Defense Ministry press service told Interfax that the Karabakh air-defense forces had shot down a SU-25 Azerbaijani jet which had tried to bomb Stepanakert (the capital of Nagorno-Karabakh). A total of seven Azerbaijani jets had taken part in the air attack, Stepanakert said. However, the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry announced that an Azerbaijani jet had suffered a clash on April 23 when carrying out a regular training flight. The pilot baled out and was saved, Baku affirmed.
