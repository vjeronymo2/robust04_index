940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: Building societies reduceforecasts on house prices - At a Glance Any pick-up in the housing market appears to be extremely slow. Halifax reported an increase of 0.3 per cent in house prices in June compared with the previous month - the first increase after two monthly falls. Nationwide found that the average price of a home fell 0.7 per cent in June after reporting price rises in every month since February. Both societies have reduced their forecasts for price increases this year from 5 per cent to 2-3 per cent. Page XVII
