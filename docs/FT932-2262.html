930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: 24% asset rise for Henderson Strata Over the six months ended April 30 1993 net asset value of Henderson Strata Investments rose 24.4 per cent to 231.8p, compared with 186.3p. And the performance had continued strong since the end of April, the directors said. Net revenue for the half year came to Pounds 47,000 (Pounds 57,000) providing earnings of 0.29p (0.37p) per share.
