BFN [Text] Brother 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the House of Representatives, and Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development, returned to Sanaa today following a visit to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the State of Eritrea. During the visit, they conveyed two messages from brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council and supreme commander of the Armed Forces, to Saudi King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz and Eritrean President Issaias Afeworke. The messages deal with the developments in Yemen and bilateral relations.
