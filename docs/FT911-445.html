910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Consortium wins Spanish rail deal An international consortium formed by Anglo-French engineering group GEC-Alsthom, Germany's Asea Brown-Boveri, Mitsubishi of Japan and the Spanish rolling stock maker CAF has won a Pta33.9bn (Pounds 185.24m) contract to deliver 70 commuter train units in 10 months to Renfe, the Spanish national railways, Tom Burns writes from Madrid.
