Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Our political staff reports that the long-awaited summit meeting between Zulu King Goodwill Zwelethini, State President F.W. de Klerk, ANC [African National Congress] President Nelson Mandela, and the IFP [Inkatha Freedom Party] leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi is expected to be held on Thursday [7 April] and Friday next week. The meeting was originally scheduled for today and tomorrow, but was called off after King Goodwill insisted that it be held after the Easter weekend.
