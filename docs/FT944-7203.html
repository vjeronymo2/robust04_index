941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Secure Retire rises by 21% Secure Retirement, which floated in April, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 254,000 for the half year to September 30, a 21 per cent improvement on Pounds 210,000 last time. Turnover of the company, which builds sheltered housing for the elderly, edged 4 per cent ahead to Pounds 1.22m (Pounds 1.17m) for the period. jEarnings per share were 2.33p (2.09p). No interim dividend is declared. However, the board hopes to be able to consider payment of a dividend at the year-end.
