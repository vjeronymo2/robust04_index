930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Hunger strikers ask for support Eastern German potash workers, on hunger strike for 24 days, yesterday called for nationwide protests to support their attempts to keep open a loss-making mine, Judy Dempsey reports from Berlin. But the federal government and the Treuhand, the agency charged with privatising eastern German industry, said it was not prepared to make any more compromises beyond guaranteeing the miners'jobs until the end of 1995.
