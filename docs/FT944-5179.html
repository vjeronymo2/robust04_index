941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): NFC - Other companies The UK's largest transport and logistics group is expected to announce full-year profits for the year to September of around Pounds 110m before tax and exceptional items tomorrow. The corresponding figure for 1993 was Pounds 95m. Attention will be firmly focused on news of an appointment of a new chief executive, following the company's fall-out with Peter Sherlock.
