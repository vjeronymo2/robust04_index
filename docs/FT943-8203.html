940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / UK Company News: McKechnie makes Pounds 6m disposal McKechnie, the plastics and metals components group, is exiting from the European packaging industry with the disposal of its UK packaging businesses to RPC Group for Pounds 6m cash. In the year to July 31, the businesses - McKechnie PBC and Polytop - had sales of Pounds 8m, producing profits before tax of Pounds 0.6m. At the end of July, the businesses had net assets of Pounds 4.3m.
