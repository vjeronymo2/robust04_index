930831 FT 31 AUG 93 / New home sales fall boosts US Treasury prices News of an unexpectedly large 5.0 per cent decline in July new home sales boosted US Treasury prices yesterday. In late afternoon trading, the benchmark 30-year bond was up 5/16 at 101 27/32, yielding 6.115 per cent, and the two-year note was up 3/32 at 100 1/16, to yield 3.80 per cent.
