BFN [Decree no. 429 of the Russian Federation Government "On Ratifying the Charter of the RUSSIAN INFORMATION AGENCY-NOVOSTI," signed by V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government, and dated 3 May 1994] [Text] In accordance with Russian Federation [RF] presidential edict no. 2278 of 23 December 1993 "On Ensuring the Activity of the RUSSIAN INFORMATION AGENCY-NOVOSTI" the RF Government decrees that: 1. The appended RUSSIAN INFORMATION AGENCY-NOVOSTI Charter adopted by the labor collective of the RUSSIAN INFORMATION AGENCY-NOVOSTI (hereinafter RIA-NOVOSTI) is to be ratified. 2. The RF State Committee for the Management of State Property is to officially register in accordance with prescribed procedure the assignation to RIA-NOVOSTI of property which is in federal ownership, along with property acquired with federal budget funds and used by the agency to pursue its statutory activity. 3. As of 1994 the RF Ministry of the Economy, when deciding the basic indicators of the RF state investment program, is to stipulate for RIA-NOVOSTI in a separate paragraph the limits of state centralized capital investments funded from the federal budget for capital construction and the acquisition of equipment. 4. The Moscow Government is to be advised to consider the question of allocating to RIA-NOVOSTI a downtown plot of land for the construction of an apartment block for agency personnel. 5. The Moscow Oblast Administration is to be advised to consider the question of allocating a three-hectare plot of land in the vicinity of Moscow for individual construction and development by RIA-NOVOSTI staffers. 6. RIA-NOVOSTI is to be authorized to build an editorial and production complex. 7. Point 1, Paragrah 1 of RF Council of Ministers-Government decree no. 922 of 16 September 1993 "Questions of the RUSSIAN INFORMATION AGENCY-NOVOSTI" (SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII No. 38, 1993, p 3577) is to be deemed invalid. [Signed] V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the RF Government [Dated] Moscow, 3 May 1994 Editorial Office note: The RIA-NOVOSTI Charter is not cited.
