940819 FT 19 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Richardsons ahead 9% to Pounds 1.55m In difficult market conditions, Richardsons Westgarth, the steel stockholder and processor, increased interim pre-tax profits by 9 per cent from Pounds 1.42m to Pounds 1.55m. Sales from continuing operations jumped 22 per cent to Pounds 39.4m (Pounds 32.3m). Interest payable and similar charges rose to Pounds 174,000 (Pounds 95,000). Mr Roger Payton, chairman, said increased tonnage had been achieved in both the general and sheet steel businesses, but margins were under pressure. The interim dividend is 1.3p (1.26p), payable from earnings per share of 3.14p (3.22p).
