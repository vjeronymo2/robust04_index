Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A terrorist has been seriously wounded by Border Police gunfire in the Gaza Strip. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that shots were fired at a Border Police patrol from a passing car. The policemen were unharmed and returned the fire. The terrorists fled in a car. Later, one of them arrived with serious wounds at a Gaza hospital and was arrested. An Israeli citizen sustained serious head injuries when stones were hurled at him in central Ramallah. He fled to an adjacent cafe where Arab patrons who tried to protect him were also attacked. The man barely managed to escape to a police station in Ramallah. His car was torched.
