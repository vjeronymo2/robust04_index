Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Dan Andronic item: "Virgil Magureanu Replaces the SRI Heads in Valcea, Braila, and Galati"] [Text] Virgil Magureanu, director of the Romanian Intelligence Service [SRI], visited the counties of Dolj and Gorj a few days ago to replace the chiefs of the SRI in those counties. The tour is continuing. Virgil Magureanu yesterday replaced the SRI director in Valcea, the official reason being the inefficient use of state funds. Sources close to the SRI leadership have informed us that in the coming days Virgil Magureanu will visit the counties of Braila and Galati for the same purpose: to replace the chiefs of the SRI in these two counties. It is worth mentioning that the five chiefs of SRI branches are joined by a further two such chiefs, who were replaced in the counties of Neamt and Vaslui a few weeks ago.
