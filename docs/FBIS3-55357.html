Language: English Article Type:BFN [From 6 January PAEVALEHT, p 3] [Text] Thursday, January 6 -- Commander-in-chief of the Defence Forces General Aleksander Einseln issued a decree yesterday on prevention of corruption in the Armed Forces, head of the Defence Forces Chancellery Mart Tiru announced. General Einseln based the decree on his experience in the U.S. Army and the analogical U.S. code, since no regulations determining corruption or unethical behaviour existed so far in the Estonian Defence Forces. The decree bans, among other things, exploitation of official position for personal gain and the making of decisions exceeding the limits of authority.
