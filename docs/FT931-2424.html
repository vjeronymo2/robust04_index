930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Two Palestinians shot dead Israeli troops shot dead two Palestinians and wounded 53 in clashes in the occupied Gaza Strip yesterday Palestinian sources said, Reuter reports from Khan Younis, Gaza Strip. In the third straight day of clashes with stone-throwing demonstrators in Khan Younis, soldiers shot dead Rayed al-Shana, 19, and wounded 40 other Arabs, blinding a 12-year-old boy in one eye, hospital officials said. Troops in neighbouring Rafah later opened fire on stone-throwing Arabs, killing a 42-year-old teacher at a local UN school, Palestinians said. They said the teacher was shot in the head while visiting mourners, and 13 others were wounded in Rafah. The army said 25 Palestinians were wounded in the Gaza Strip and it was checking the reports of deaths.
