940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Property side lifts Fletcher King 34% A strengthening property market helped Fletcher King, the commercial estate agency, to a 34 per cent increase in pre-tax profits and a 50 per cent dividend rise for the year ended April 30. Pre-tax profit rose from Pounds 224,000 to Pounds 301,000. The improvement came from the property side, with Howard Associates, the building services subsidiary, breaking even. Turnover was static at Pounds 4.93m (Pounds 4.97m). Earnings per share came out ahead at 2.1p (1.6p). A recommended 1p (0.5p) final gives a total 1.5p (1p).
