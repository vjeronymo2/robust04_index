930422 FT 22 APR 93 / International Company News: Indian body joins with JP Morgan INDUSTRIAL Credit and Investment Corporation of India (ICICI), India's leading development finance institution, yesterday launched a joint venture with JP Morgan, the US investment bank. The venture will have an authorised capital of Rs1bn, (Dollars 32m) with an initial issued capital of Rs500m. ICICI will hold 60 per cent of the equity, and JP Morgan 40 per cent, through its subsidiary, Morgan Guaranty International Finance Corp. JP Morgan will invest Dollars 10m in the venture.
