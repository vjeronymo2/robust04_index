BFN [By reporters Sun Zhanlin (1327 0594 2651) and Hu Xiaoguang (5170 2556 0342)] [Text] Almaty, 27 Apr (XINHUA) -- Visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng met again with Kazakh President Nazarbayev this afternoon. As requested during the two-and-a-half-hour meeting, Premier Li Peng briefed the Kazakh president on China's major policies and measures in economic reform and efforts in establishing a socialist market economic system. Showing great interest in Li Peng's briefings, Nazarbayev said that his country is also exploring the path of reform and would like to have a thorough understanding of the conditions of China's reform and opening up. He suggested mutual visits between officials and experts of the economic departments of the two countries. Earlier today, Premier Li Peng held talks with Kazakh Prime Minister Tereshchenko on ways to further strengthen economic cooperation and bilateral trade. They also exchanged views on improving communications and transport conditions between the two nations. Kazakh officials in charge of economics, trade, energy, and communications departments also attended the meeting and expressed their views on cooperation in their respective fields.
