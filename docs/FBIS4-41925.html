BFN [Report by Nikolay Levchuk: "Sovereignty, Anyway!"] [Text] According to a poll conducted by the "West-East" Strategic Initiatives Center, 76.4 percent of the Belarusian people support the unification of the Belarusian and Russian monetary systems. If the unification results in a ceding of powers to the Russian Government or Central Bank, the move will be supported by 39.6 percent of society. If the unification results in a loss of Belarusian sovereignty, only 24.5 will support this initiative.
