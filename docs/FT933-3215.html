930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Labour attacks roads programme THE government's Pounds 23bn roads programme will be disastrous for the environment and goes against worldwide moves to combat pollution and congestion, transport chiefs were told yesterday. Shadow transport minister Ms Joan Walley told the British Road Federation's public affairs committee in London: 'It is now apparent that the government has blatantly ignored the challenge set last year at the Rio earth summit for countries to look for long-term strategies to provide integrated and sustainable transport systems.'
