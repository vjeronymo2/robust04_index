940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican share prices closed higher on foreign buying after dipping from an earlier all-time intraday high. The IPC index rose 27.11 points, or 1.02 per cent, to 2,683.18 on volume of 108. m shares. Its high point was 2,696.72. Brazilians equities closed flat in see-saw trading after a new wave of profit-taking pushed prices down from the day's highs late in the session. The Bovespa index of the 54 most-traded shares ended at 68,918 points, down 0.07 per cent.
