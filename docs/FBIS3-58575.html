Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Defense has made representations to UNFICYP [UN Force in Cyprus] in connection with the Greek commandos that have gone to South Cyprus. TAK [Turkish Cypriot News Agency] reports that the Ministry asked the UNFICYP for information about the Greek commandos. According to information published in the Greek Cypriot press, a planeload of Greek commandos secretly arrived in South Cyprus yesterday. Greek Cypriot sources assessed the situation as a delivery of troops within the framework of the Greek-Greek Cypriot joint defense doctrine.
