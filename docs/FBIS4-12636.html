BFN [Report by Mahmud Bakri] [Text] Available information has confirmed that the United States and Israel are currently planning to set up the most sophisticated military base in the Middle East region. A joint military committee has been formed to set up the base 23 kilometers east of the occupied city of Jerusalem. According to information, a senior Zionist official recently presented U.S. military officials with two separate maps for that purpose. The new base will serve the joint American-Israeli interests in the region. It will be equipped with the most sophisticated U.S. long-range and medium-range missile systems, in addition to multisystem rocket launchers. The base will be automatically connected to Sixth Fleet operations in the Mediterranean and will act as one of its tributaries. American and Israeli military experts will supervise the base. Information indicates that the new military base will help the Israeli plan of planting nuclear warheads in the holy places.
