931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Manakin proposes further distribution Manakin Holdings, the investment trust which went into voluntary liquidation in 1991, had a net asset value of 82p per share at October 15, against 68p six months earlier and 100p at the previous year-end. Since the appointment of liquidators in October 1991 five distributions to shareholders totalling Pounds 70.1m (342p per share) have been made. A further repayment of 8p will be made on January 21. The net loss for the year was Pounds 435,445, against a profit of Pounds 22,067. Losses per share were 2.1p (earnings 0.1p).
