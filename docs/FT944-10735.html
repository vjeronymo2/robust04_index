941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Investment Co The Investment Company, which invests in preference shares and convertible stocks, reported net revenue up from Pounds 493,512 to Pounds 527,850 in the half year to September 30. Total income amounted to Pounds 672,292 (Pounds 626,138). Earnings came out at 1.51p (1.31p) and the interim dividend is increased to 0.75p (0.5p). Net asset value per share at the period end was 47.64p (49.63p). The ultimate holding company is New Centurion Trust.
