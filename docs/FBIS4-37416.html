BFN [Text] Mashhad, June 20, IRNA -- Security sources here have told IRNA that the security officials are busy investigating into the bombing of the mausoleum of Imam Reza (a.s.) [peace be upon him]. Clues found by the security officials point out to the involvement of terrorist Mojhedeen Khalq [Mojahedin-e Khalq] organization in the bombing to mark the anniversary of the beginning of their campaign of terror against the Islamic Republic, June 20, 1981, the sources said. According to the sources the law enforcement forces in the area have found typewritten tracks [as received] which indicate "the followers of the armed uprising of June 20th", calling themselves "the mojahed sons of Iran", have taken responsibility for the bombing.
