BFN [Text] Tehran, June 13, IRNA -- Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati left here for Bonn Monday at the official invitation of his German counterpart Klaus Kinkel to discuss mutual ties and key regional and international issues. During his two-day visit, Velayati will discuss Iran's ties with the European Union in his meetings with Chancellor Helmut Kohl, Foreign Minister Kinkel and other German officials. Political observers attach great importance to Velayati's visit with regard to the fact that Germany will take over the rotating chairmanship of the European Union next month.
