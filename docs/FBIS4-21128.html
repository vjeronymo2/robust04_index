Pastrana Asks Other Presidential Hopefuls To `Demand Clarification' of Charges PA1004172294 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Cadena 1 in Spanish 0130 GMT 10 Apr 94 PA1004172294 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Cadena 1 Spanish BFN [From the "TV Today" newscast] [Text] Tonight, Andres Pastrana sent a letter to 17 other presidential candidates, asking them to demand clarification from the U.S. State Department regarding recent statements in connection with the use of drug money in the current election campaign. In his letter, Pastrana says these statements are very serious, and, therefore, all candidates are obliged to request that specific charges be made, including details on which campaigns allegedly received such money and the origin of any such funds.
