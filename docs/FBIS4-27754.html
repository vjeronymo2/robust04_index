BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 5 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received Saturday afternoon Mr S. Keswick, president of Jardine Matheson Holdings group, who arrived here on June 3 for a three-day visit to Vietnam. P.M. Kiet appreciated the group's early presence in the country, and its desire for cooperation and investment in the construction of infrastructure in Vietnam. He expressed his hope that the cooperative relations between the two sides would continue to develop. For his part, Mr Keswick briefed the Vietnamese prime minister of the results of Jardine Matheson Holdings' activities over the past four years, particularly in the fields of energy, infrastructure and coal exploitation. He affirmed that the group's cooperation and investment in Vietnam would be further promoted in the coming years. He said he believed that Vietnam's open-door policies and its potentials would serve as firm foundation for the success of his group's investment and cooperation projects in conformity with both sides' interest.
