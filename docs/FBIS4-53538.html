BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- A cocktail party was given by Bulgarian Ambassador to Korea Yordan Mutafchiev Thursday on the occasion of the day of culture, education and Slav letters. Invited to the party were Chairman of the State Education Commission and Chairman of the Korea-Bulgaria Friendship Association Choe Ki-yong, Vice-chairman of the Korean Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries Kim Yong-su, and other officials concerned. Speeches were exchanged at the cocktail party.
