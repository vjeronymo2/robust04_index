Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry press service's report for 11 March: A relative calm is prevailing in the aftermath of the recent unsuccessful attacks against our Army's positions by Armenia's Armed Forces. Only localized positional fighting is continuing at various spots. It appears that having suffered considerable losses during their latest attacks, the Armenian side again wants to return to the negotiating table. The Azerbaijani Armed Forces favor a cease-fire and are ready to ensure the necessary conditions for peace talks.
