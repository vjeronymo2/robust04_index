920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Air London climbs 10% to Pounds 671,000 Improved margins in all areas helped Air London International, the USM-quoted charter broker, to report a 10 per cent rise in pre-tax profit, from Pounds 611,000 to Pounds 671,000, in the six months to January 31 on turnover cut from Pounds 8.65m to Pounds 7.2m. The interim dividend is raised to 1.6p (1.4p) on earnings per share of 5.03p (4.46p).
