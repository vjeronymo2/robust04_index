Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 Jan (MENA)--A responsible security source has stated that unidentified gunmen opened fire at the vehicle of Public Security Lieutenant Colonel Mustafa Khalil Tuni while driving from his home in the Na'ilah Khatun area in Asyut at 1105 today. He and his driver, Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Al Salih, were martyred in the shooting. The source said that the commands of the Asyut Security Directorate and the State Security Intelligence Department and various investigation teams went to the scene of the shooting along with a team from the criminal laboratory to investigate. Police agencies have sealed off the area and are searching for the criminals.
