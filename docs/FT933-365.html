930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: S&U shares up on bullish outlook Shares in S&U yesterday jumped 18p to 339p after the consumer credit group reported interim pre-tax profits up 9 per cent to Pounds 2.27m and predicted a more significant increase in the second half. Turnover for the six months to July 31 edged up from Pounds 24.9m to Pounds 25.3m. Earnings per share came out at 13.56p (12.42p) and the interim dividend is increased to 2.6p (2.4p). Mr Derek Coombs, chairman, said the upturn in demand evident at the year end was now increasingly being borne out.
