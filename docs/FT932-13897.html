930421 FT 21 APR 93 / International Company News: Honeywell advances HONEYWELL, the US controls group, reported first quarter net income of Dollars 57.3m, or 42 cents a share, compared with Dollars 48.4m, or 36 cents, on a comparable basis in the same period of last year, writes Martin Dickson. Sales totalled Dollars 1.44bn, down from Dollars 1.48bn, with declines in space and aviation partly offset by gains on the industrial side.
