920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / International Company News: Macy's plans to close eight stores RH MACY is to close eight underperforming department stores by August 1 in order to strengthen its overall performance, AP-DJ reports from New York. The US stores group, in Chapter 11 of the US bankruptcy code, said the decision to close the stores followed a review of the company. The review was based on several factors, including unsatisfactory profitability and productivity, a declining sales trend and a lack of growth potential in the market. Macy's has notified the bankruptcy court and its creditor groups of its decision.
