BFN [Text] Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu received this evening a Chinese military delegation led by Colonel General Zhang Wannian, member of the Central Military Commission and chief of staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who is visiting our country on the invitation of the Romanian Ministry of National Defense. The meeting was attended by Lieutenant General Dumitru Cioflina, chief of staff of the Romanian Army. After conveying to the Romanian prime minister greetings from his counterpart, the guest expressed satisfaction that the good Romanian-Chinese relations, unaltered by the changes that have taken place in international life, continue to develop in the spirit of the old traditions that bind us. The visit of the delegation that he is leading, through its very rich program and through the various exchanges of opinions that it occasions, makes a contribution to a better familiarization of the sides with each other and to the enhancement of relations of cooperation between the two armies and two countries. The prime minister assured the Chinese guest that the Romanian people follow with keen interest China's outstanding achievements on the road to economic reform in the past 15 years and also assured him that this country, with its huge potential, represents one of the current foreign political guide marks of Romania. As a continuation of bilateral contacts in the past months, the visit to Romania of Li Peng, premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, scheduled for summer 1994, is expected with great interest, reads a press release faxed to us by a spokesman for the government.
