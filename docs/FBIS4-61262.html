BFN [Text] Azerbaijani forces have renewed an offensive in the north of Nagorno-Karabakh's Mardakert region late in the day on Wednesday with Chailu and Karmiravan villages now coming under attack, an Armenian military spokesman told INTERFAX. However, he said Karabakh units occupying more advantageous positions were dealing tangible strikes on the adversary, Several strategic heights near Karmiravan are said to have fallen into the hands of Armenian forces by 1730. But there was general calm elsewhere in the region, the spokesman said. In the meantime, no military report has been made available to INTERFAX by the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry yet.
