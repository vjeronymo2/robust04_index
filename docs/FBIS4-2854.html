BFN [Text] Beijing, April 13 (XINHUA) -- The China Youth Research Society, the country's first mass academic organization for the study of youth, was inaugurated here today. Experts noted that this marks a new stage of the research on youth in China. Zhao Shi, member of the secretariat of the Communist Youth League of China (CYLC), was elected president of the society. The society already has 239 members, who come from the CYLC, higher education institutions, from the social sciences and from enterprises. It will concentrate on the study of new trends and new problems involving modern young people and will make constructive proposals with social and theoretical value. Lei Jieqiong, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of the current (Eighth) National People's Congress, attended the inauguration ceremony today. Zhao Zongnai, executive deputy head of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, expressed congratulations on the inauguration of the society, on behalf of all the advisors of the new organisation.
