November 18, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

OCCIDENTAL BASKETBALL TEAMS TO OPEN SEASONS

The Occidental College men's and women's basketball teams will open their seasons tonight in a doubleheader at Occidental.

The women's team, led by Sharon Wada -- a two-time All-Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference guard -- will play host to UC San Diego at 6 p.m.

All-SCIAC center Chris Anderson and all-conference forward John Crawley will lead the men's team against Biola at 8. GARY KLEIN
