940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Ex-Hartstone director gets Pounds 235,000 pay-out A former director of Hartstone has received payments totalling Pounds 235,000 from the struggling hosiery and leathergoods group which last month announced a Pounds 30m rescue rights issue. Mr Trevor Brentnall, former head of the group's leathergoods division, left the company in February. It is believed he is the director named in the accounts who received a compensation payment of Pounds 100,000, in addition to remuneration and pension of Pounds 135,000. Mr Brentnall, a former partner with City solicitors Turner Kenneth Brown, joined Hartstone in 1990. He was appointed by Mr Stephen Barker, former chief executive wholeft Hartstone in June last year with a Pounds 60,000 pay-off, but is suing for a further Pounds 340,000.
