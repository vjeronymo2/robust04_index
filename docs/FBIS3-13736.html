Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Statement by Information Minister Michel Samahah at the Ba'bda Palace on 16 March-- recorded] [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers held an extraordinary meeting under his excellency the president of the Republic. The meeting was attended by the prime minister and the ministers. At the beginning, the president reviewed the situation in the country, focusing on events before and after the explosion in the Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il and the local and regional atmosphere accompanying this crime. The president emphasized the critical stage through which the country is passing and the need to mobilize every effort to reinforce national unity and the peace process in the face of any action against them and against internal stability. He stressed the sovereignty of Lebanese law and its application throughout the liberated Lebanese territory. The prime minister then briefed the Council on security reports regarding the situation in the country and the measures adopted by the Internal Security Council, a meeting of which he chaired on 15 March to reinforce the security situation and confront every attempt to undermine national stability. The Council of Ministers discussed the conditions in the country in light of the review presented by the president and the prime minister on the premise that Lebanese law and legitimate institutions and their services entrusted to implement the law are the authority for imposing security and implementing Lebanese law in every Lebanese area. The Council reconfirmed its decisions at previous sessions to empower the security services to deal with every emergency incident designed to undermine security, stability, and the establishment of state authority. It also confirmed all the measures adopted at the meeting of the Internal Security Council yesterday in implementation of the Council of Ministers' decisions. [passage omitted]
