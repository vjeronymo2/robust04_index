920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: United Drug offers IPounds 5.16m for Alchem United Drug, the Dublin-based pharmaceutical distributor, is making a recommended IPounds 5.16m offer for Alchem, the parent company of Sangers, Northern Ireland's biggest pharmaceutical wholesaler. United is offering 13 new shares for each 8 existing Alchem shares. Alchem shareholders are also offered a loyalty bonus of 3p per share if they hold the United shares for three months after the date of issue. United has received irrevocable undertakings for acceptance from the whole of Alchem's board and certain members of their families who together hold some 18 per cent of the issued capital.
