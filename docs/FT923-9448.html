920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute & Cotton JUTE August/September c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 395, BWC Dollars 405, BTD Dollars 365, BWD Dollars 370; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 380, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 355, BWD Dollars 355. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales for the week ended July 31 amounted to 128 tonnes against none in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialists styles notably in the west African and Russian range.
