920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / International Company News: Norwegian insurer dips VITAL FOSIKRING, the Norwegian insurer, yesterday reported a NKr2m dip in first quarter pre-tax profit to NKr356m (Dollars 56m). Vital boosted first-quarter premium income by 29 per cent to NKr1.077bn from NKr838m in the same period last year, writes Karen Fossli. Gross financial income increased by NKr86m to NKr846m. Vital said that asset values since end-March had risen substantially due to a recovery in prices on the Oslo stock exchange and greater stability in foreign interest rates. Operating expenses rose by NKr2m to NKr156m.
