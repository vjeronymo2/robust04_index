920430 FT 30 APR 92 / UK Company News: Unigroup to float Malaysian offshoot Unigroup, the timber and building products group, has decided to float its Golden Pharos subsidiary on the Kuala Lumpur stock exchange. The offshoot makes and distributes timber products such as doors and windows. It has a 37,000 sq m factory in Malaysia with some 300 employees. It is envisaged that the company, which will remain a subsidiary of Unigroup, will obtain a listing by spring 1993.
