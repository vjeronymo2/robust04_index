940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Southnews advances to Pounds 1.71m Southnews, the London regional newspaper publisher, turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.71m on turnover of Pounds 16.9m for the year to April 2. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 1.58m - including a Pounds 364,000 exceptional gain on the sale of Sussex County Press - from turnover of Pounds 13.9m previously. Operating profit, including a first year contribution of Pounds 144,000 from acquisitions, was Pounds 1.75m (Pounds 1.21m). Earnings per share came out at 7.63p (6.59p) and a final dividend of 2p makes a 2.95p (2.1p) total.
