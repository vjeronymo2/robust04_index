April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

HNB Financial Group: The Huntington Beach-based parent company of Huntington National Bank reported earnings of $335,000 for the first quarter, up 50% from earnings of $223,000 in the year-earlier period. Total assets at the end of March were nearly $121 million, up 54% from the $78.6 million reported for the comparable period in 1989.
