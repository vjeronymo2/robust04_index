BFN [Unattributed report: "Aviation Agreement"] [Text] An aviation agreement between Austria and Belarus was concluded at the Foreign Ministry in Vienna on 29 March 1994. On the part of Belarus, the agreement was signed by Transportation Minister Stepan Shkapich, an on the Austrian side by Ambassador Wolfgang Wolte, head of the Economic Department in the Foreign Ministry. Under the agreement, which is the legal basis for a regular airline service between the two countries, both sides grant each other rights for certain routes. Air traffic between Vienna and Minsk has been started both by Austrian Airlines and the Belarussian air carrier Belevia.
