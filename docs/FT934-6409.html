931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / Prison extension plan announced New cell blocks providing more than 2,000 extra places are to be built to cope with the growing crisis of prison overcrowding, it was announced yesterday. Mr Michael Howard, home secretary, said the blocks - to be built at existing prisons - would take between 15 and 18 months to complete. They are likely to be built in the north-west and the Midlands where overcrowding is most acute.
