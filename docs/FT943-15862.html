940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / People: Insurance moves Bob Hewes, 49, currently director, regulatory services, has been appointed director, finance, of LLOYD'S OF LONDON, to succeed Stephen Hall. Hall, who will have been in the job for about 18 months when he retires, probably at the end of the year, was a short-term appointment. Until his successor is appointed, Hewes will remain responsible for regulatory services.
