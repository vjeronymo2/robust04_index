Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 10 Jan (SANA)--Saudi Foreign Minister Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal has voiced satisfaction with the outcome of the meeting held by the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration member states. In statements to reporters after the conclusion of the meeting, al-Faysal said the principles and foundations of the Damascus Declaration have patched up the remaining Arab cohesion. The declaration, he said, will, God willing, help remedy the Arab situation.
