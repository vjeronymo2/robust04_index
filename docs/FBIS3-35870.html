Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Approximately half an hour ago, the 13th Supreme Soviet session of the Republic of Belarus resumed its work. Supreme Soviet Chairman Myechyslaw Hryb opened the session with an introductory speech. He thanked the audience for electing him Supreme Soviet chairman and called for the consolidation of all forces in society to work jointly to pull the republic out of its crisis and move forward toward the development of society. The Supreme Soviet chairman appealed to the opposition and Belarusian mass media not to go in for idle criticism, but rather to work constructively for the benefit of society. I hope for the wisdom of the people, for the support of all forces, said Myechyslaw Hryb. He spoke about today's telephone conversation with Boris Yeltsin and said that the Russian leader expressed support and understanding. Later, Belarusian deputies began discussing miscellaneous issues. Our newscasts will report in detail on the course of the Supreme Soviet session.
