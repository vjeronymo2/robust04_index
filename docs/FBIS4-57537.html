BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 19 May. Sustainable Development and Environment Minister Guillermo Justiniano has reported that international financial organizations have granted more than $70 million in loans to implement projects for the preservation and appropriate use of natural resources. The Paris Consultative Group granted the largest credit, amounting to $30 million. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 7 May 94 p B2) The Finance and Economic Development Ministry reported that 14 oil companies will invest $81 million in oil prospecting and exploitation during 1993. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 9 May 94 p A2)
