931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Technology (Worth Watching): Extra protection in an emergency The makers of Gore-Tex have developed a clothing fabric for emergency services which meets stringent US requirements and gives extra protection against Aids, writes Andrew Fisher. Called Assist, it uses a membrane engineered with a polymer-based material and also gives protection against contaminated water, pesticides and corrosive and dangerous fluids.
