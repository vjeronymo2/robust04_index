BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Mayak Radio Network in Russian at 0522 GMT on 19 May carries a live report by correspondent Heimar Lenk from Tallinn on the government crisis in Estonia. Reception is good. Lenk says: "Late in the evening the government gathered for an emergency closed sitting. I can tell you that at this moment the Fatherland Party government is still in power in Estonia. However, the party, which has been in power the past year and one-half, has an exceptionally low popularity level among the public. It has been reported that five ministers tendered their resignation at last nights meeting."
