BFN [Text] Our Skopje correspondent Takis Dhiamandis has reported that the Defense Ministry of Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] has denied reports about an alleged concentration of Greek armed forces along its northwest borders. These reports have been circulating for the past few days. Moreover, it was one of the issues Skopjean President Kiro Gligorov raised in his interview with the French newspaper LE MONDE. Tension was noticed in Skopje last night after reports began coming from Gevgelija, which is near the border, about the movement of a Greek tank column. The Defense Ministry of Skopje issued a statement late last night that stressed that these reports are not consonant with the facts.
