February 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $60 Sunday when Present Value, on which he bet $50 to win, ran out in the eighth race, and his $10 triple "ended" when Shimmering Star was fourth in the sixth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll is now at $690.

Bob's bet today is a $30 parlay from Hidden Garden in the third to Over The Pole in the fifth.

Sunday's Bankroll: $750

Sunday's Loss: $60

Current Bankroll: $690
