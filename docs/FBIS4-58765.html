BFN [Text] The talks regarding the return of the Arabs who became refugees in 1967 will start in approximately one month. The number of these refugees is estimated by Israel to be about 200,000. The talks will be held in the framework of the joint Israel-Palestinian-Egypt-Jordan committee, as stipulated by the Declaration of Principles. The talks regarding the clauses which were not finalized before the signing of the agreement with the PLO will resume in Cairo within one week. The negotiations regarding Palestinian early empowerment will also start within one week.
