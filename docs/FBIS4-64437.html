BFN [Unattributed article: "France/Italy: Mitterrand Message to Prime Minister-Delegate"] [Excerpt] (ANSA) Rome, May 2 -- In a message sent last Saturday [30 April], French President Francois Mitterrand congratulated Silvio Berlusconi on his designation as prime minister and wished him success in forming a government. Mitterrand said he hoped that Italy and France "united by ties of deep friendship and close cooperation, will continue to build a Europe of peace and freedom." [passage omitted]
