Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Hassan II, may God grant him victory, has sent the following message of congratulations to His Excellency Liamine Zeroual, president of the Republic of Algeria: On the occasion of your taking over the presidency of the sister Republic of Algeria, we are happy to convey to Your Excellency our congratulations and wishes for success in your duty. On the same occasion, we wish the fraternal people of Algeria further progress, prosperity, and peace. We wish you good health and hope you will kindly accept our highest consideration and fraternal esteem. [Signed] Your brother Hassan II, king of Morocco.
