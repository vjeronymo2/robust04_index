BFN [Text] Yerevan ("YERKIR" daily--HAYLUR), 06/22/94--Russian Ambassador to Armenia Vladimir Stupishin said, status of the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] is to be defined within the framework of international negotiations and should be advantageous for the Artsakh people, giving them real security guarantees. During an interview with Acting Editor in Chief of "MK [Mountainous Karabakh] Republic" newspaper G. Bagdasaryan on June 18, he related recognition of Mountainous Karabakh by Russia with a corresponding decision by Armenia. According to Stupishin, the relations between Armenia and Russia are direct and are based on their independence. The relations with the RMK are dependent on international recognition of the latter.
