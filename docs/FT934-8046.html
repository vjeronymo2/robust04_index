931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: CML Micro ahead 10% at Pounds 1.65m FIRM progress by the UK traffic and US semiconductor business enabled CML Microsystems, the USM-traded specialist electronic products group, to record a near-10 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Pounds 1.65m for the half year ended September 30. Sales rose 35 per cent to Pounds 8.42m and operating profits by 25 per cent to Pounds 1.25m. The pre-tax figure took account of a fall in interest income to Pounds 398,000 (Pounds 503,000). Earnings per share showed an increase from 5.36p to 5.74p.
