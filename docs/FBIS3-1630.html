Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The second session of the seventh Shandong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] opened ceremoniously at the auditorium of Jinan's Dongjiao Hotel on the morning of 27 February. Full of vigor and joy, 708 members from various parties, people's organizations, nationalities, and circles throughout the province attended. Lu Maozeng, chairman; and Zhai Yongbo, Tian Jian, Kong Lingren, Zheng Shouyi, Wang Yuyan, Cui Weilin, Wu Fuheng, Wu Minggang, Wang Zunong, Su Yingheng, Miao Yongming, and Li Gongjiu, vice chairmen of the provincial CPPCC committee; attended. Present at the opening ceremony to extend warm congratulations were leading comrades of the Shandong Provincial CPC Committee, the provincial people's congress standing committee, the provincial government, the provincial discipline inspection commission, the provincial higher people's court, and the provincial people's procuratorate. They were Jiang Chunyun, Zhao Zhihao, Li Chunting, Li Wenquan, Han Xikai, Li Zhen, Han Yuqun, Yu Peigao, Zhao Changfeng, and (Zheng Yitang). The session opened at 0930. Lu Maozeng presided over it. [Begin recording] [Lu Maozeng] Now I declare the second session of the seventh Shandong Provincial CPPCC committee open. [passage omitted] Comrade Zhai Yongbo, vice chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee, will now give a work report on behalf of the standing committee of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee. [Zhai Yongbo] Members, entrusted by the Standing Committee of the seventh Shandong Provincial CPPCC Committee, I will give a work report to this plenary session for its deliberations. The year 1993 was one when the CPC led the people of various nationalities across the country to comprehensively implement the guidelines of the 14th national party congress and advance triumphantly along the road of building socialism with Chinese characteristics. [end recording] In his report, Zhai Yongbo reviewed and summarized the work in the past year. He said: In the past year, the seventh provincial CPPCC committee, under the leadership of the Shandong Provincial CPC Committee, persistently made innovations in the process of inheritance, advanced in the process of pioneering, conscientiously performed the functions of political consultation and democratic supervision focusing on the central work of the party and government, and made positive contributions for the province to seize the opportunity to accelerate development. It intensified political and theoretical study to lay an ideological foundation for opening up a new situation in CPPCC work; strengthened political consultation and democratic supervision to win new achievements in the participation in and discussion of administration of state and government affairs; strengthened the function of service to make new headway in direct participation in economic construction; intensified propaganda work and research of culture and history to expand the social influence of the CPPCC; and stepped up efforts in establishing work organs to further invigorate the CPPCC work. Zhai Yongbo pointed out: Under the leadership of the Shandong Provincial CPC Committee, we should persistently make the party's basic theory and basic line dominant in the overall situation; continue to hold high the banner of patriotism and socialism; conscientiously adhere to and continuously improve the systems of multi-party cooperation and political consultation under the CPC leadership; and, firmly focusing on seizing the opportunity to deepen reform, opening wider to the outside world, facilitating development, and maintaining stability, which meet public interests, unite all the forces that can be united; mobilize all positive factors; and strive to consolidate and develop the broadest patriotic united front in the new year. We should adhere to the principle of giving attending to and discussing major affairs, doing solid work, and seeking realistic results; conscientiously perform the functions of political consultation and democratic supervision; and make new contributions to fulfilling the tasks put forward at the sixth provincial party congress, to establishing the socialist market economy system more rapidly, to maintaining sustained, rapid, and healthy economic development, to safeguarding political stability, and to promoting comprehensive social progress. The Motions Committee of the Shandong Provincial CPPCC Committee submitted a written report to the session on the motions work carried out since the first session of the seventh provincial CPPCC committee. Invited to the session were former chairmen and vice chairmen of the provincial CPPCC committee and pertinent veteran comrades of the province, including Tan Qilong, Liang Buting, Li Zichao, Wang Zhongyin, Zhou Xingfu, Li Sijing, Zhang Zhusheng, Zhou Zhenxing, Xu Wenyuan, and Jin Baozhen; some standing committee members and members of the eighth National CPPCC committee staying in Jinan, including Pan Qiqi, Qu Jining, Xu Chunyang, Yan Qingqing, and Wang Shenyin; and Zheng Guangchen, standing committee member of the provincial CPPCC committee and deputy commander of the provincial military district.
