Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 23 Mar (TANJUG) -- A delegation of Turkey's general staff, headed by General Erol Kirisoglu, arrived on an official visit to Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] today. It has been announced that the Turkish delegation was received in Skopje by Defense Minister Vlado Popovski and Army Chief of Staff General Dragoljub Bocinov. Further military cooperation between Turkey and Macedonia was discussed during the talks.
