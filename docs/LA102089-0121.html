October 20, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

THE NEXT STEP

The Washington-based National Endowment for the Arts is awaiting a review of the Bay Area arts situation by a senior staff member who happened to be in San Francisco at the time of the quake. The endowment said it was prepared to offer disaster relief funds to arts institutions that sustained specific losses of such things as vehicles, costumes and buildings. Affected organizations can contact the endowment program office that oversees their disciplines. The endowment's general telephone number is (202) 682-5400.
