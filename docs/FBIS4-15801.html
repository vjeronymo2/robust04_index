BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Excerpt] Sevastopol 11 April, TASS -- "The command of the Black Sea Fleet is doing everything possible to stabilise the situation in the region," Black Sea Fleet Commander Admiral Eduard Baltin told ITAR-TASS on Monday. Baltin described the recent actions against the Black Sea Fleet by the Ukrainian side as unlawful. He praised the Russian Government for support of the Fleet. Black Sea Fleet First Deputy Commander Vitaliy Larionov quoted Ukrainian Defence Ministry officials as saying earlier today that all Fleet facilities seized by Ukrainian Servicemen in Odessa had been freed as of 1600 Monday.
