Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to an INTERFAX report, after fierce fighting Abkhazian units have occupied the village of Nizhniye Latu in the mountains in Gulripsh Rayon. Casualty figures for the sides have not been reported. Moreover, Zurab Akvali in Tbilisi the head of the autonomous government of Abkhazia, accused the authorities in Sukhumi of destroying the Georgian village of Nabakevi in Gali Rayon.
