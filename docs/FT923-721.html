920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / UK Company News: McLaughlin & Harvey cuts losses Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 306,000 were announced by McLaughlin & Harvey, the construction group, for the half year to June 30. Losses last time amounted to Pounds 4.89m. Turnover fell from Pounds 55.9m to Pounds 31.4m and the pre-tax result was after an exceptional credit of Pounds 491,000 (Pounds 4.05m debit) comprising a property disposal profit less write-down of construction work in progress, bad debts, reorganisation and redundancy costs. Losses per share fell from 75.5p to 5.5p and again there is no interim dividend.
