Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "NTC Newscast"] [Text] Los Granaderos is the name of the 100 men of the antiguerrilla warfare specialized police whose mission is to guarantee the normal development of the forthcoming elections. These nine antiguerrilla police officers are part of an elite group with a specific mission: To halt subversive attacks before and after the elections. Los Granaderos, as they are called in combat, just completed a quick course at the Gabriel Gonzalez School in El Espinal, Tolima Department, a few hours ago. Los Granaderos simulated a patrol action, and they tested their reaction in the face of an ambush. These men have a very clear idea of their mission. [Begin General Octavio Vargas Silva, National Police director, recording] The guerrillas should be duly warned we are aptly prepared and armed to counter any attack. We are going to do to them what David did to Goliath: wipe them out. [end recording] These nine Granaderos are part of a group of 100 men who as of next week will be posted at strategic points. The police director announced other measures this morning for guaranteeing calm during the next elections. [Begin Vargas Silva recording] All vacations, furloughs, and permits have been suspended so that as of yesterday and through 13 March most of the police force will be on the streets, in those places we consider sensitive or vulnerable. [end recording]
