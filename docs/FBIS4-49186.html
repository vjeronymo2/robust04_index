BFN [Text] Nairobi, May 2 (XINHUA) -- Kenyan President Daniel Arap Moi left here today for China for a five-day state visit at the invitation of Chinese President Jiang Zemin. This is President Moi's third state visit to China. The previous visits were in 1980 and 1988 respectively. Seeing him off were Charge d'Affaires Xie Zhiheng and other officials in the Chinese Embassy in Kenya, and Kenyan Government officials.
