April 23, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

LA HABRA SHOOTING CRITICALLY INJURES MAN

A man was in critical condition Saturday evening, suffering from gunshot wounds in what police said appeared to be a gang-related shooting in the 700 block of West 4th Avenue in La Habra.

The unidentified man was taken to a Whittier hospital.

A suspect in the shooting apparently entered a nearby house at 4th Street and Monte Vista Avenue.

A special police team surrounded the house.
