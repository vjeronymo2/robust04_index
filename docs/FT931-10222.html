930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Ofgas chief to step down Sir James McKinnon, head of the gas industry watchdog, who accused British Gas yesterday of 'wriggling and squirming' over a price formula package, is to step down a year early. Sir James said he would leave once the report on British Gas by the Monopolies and Mergers Commission is published. Report, Page 18; Complaint figures disputed, Page 6
