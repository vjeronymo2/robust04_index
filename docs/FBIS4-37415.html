BFN [Text] Cairo, 18 Jun (MENA)-- Faruq Abu-'Isa, a prominent member of the Sudanese opposition, has stated that a radio speaking for the Sudanese opposition will begin transmission early next week. At a news conference today, Abu-'Isa said that the radio will operate from a certain state, which he did not specify. He affirmed, however, that it is a non-Arab state. Abu-'Isa, who is the secretary general of the Arab Lawyers Federation, reiterated that all factions of the Sudanese opposition are coordinating their work through the Sudanese National Democratic Grouping.
