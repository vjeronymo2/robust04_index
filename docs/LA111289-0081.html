November 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

TRAFFIC IN THE SOUTH

Jim Newton's article (Oct. 30) on the problems of Mission Viejo caused by the development going on around the south Orange County area is a well-expressed view of our city's concern about the traffic problem posed by this population explosion.

The puzzling thing about this article, though, is how anyone can do any research, whatsoever, on this subject without underlining and repeatedly emphasizing the obvious lack of responsibility by Orange County's supervisors for permitting this development without first assuring that transportation access was adequate to cope with it.

In this responsibility, our supervisors have been deplorably remiss.

BRUCE H. PLANCK

Mission Viejo
