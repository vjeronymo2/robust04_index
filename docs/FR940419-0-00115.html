Section 92.255 would be revised to read as follows: §92.255 Mixed-income project. (a) Housing that accounts for less than 100 percent of the dwelling units in a project qualifies as affordable housing if the housing meets the criteria of §92.252 or §92.254. Each building in the project must contain housing that meets the requirements of §92.252 or §92.254. See §92.219 for matching contribution requirements concerning mixed-income projects. (b) For purposes of meeting affordable housing requirements for a project, the dwelling units counted as affordable housing may be changed over the affordability period, so long as the total number of affordable housing units remains the same, and the substituted units are, at a minimum, comparable in terms of size, features, and number of bedrooms to the originally designated affordable housing units.
