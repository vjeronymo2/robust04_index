910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / Survey of Cardiff (5): A Welsh song for Europe THIS IS Europe Week in Cardiff. The festival is a promotion of Europe within the Welsh capital and, in particular, of the city's place within Europe. It sees French fashion, German food, Italian music, opera from the Welsh National Opera Company - and, this being Wales, a rugby festival. Cardiff is Europe's youngest capital, created in 1955. Although twinned with Stuttgart and Nantes, it has not had particularly strong links with continental Europe since its coal trade collapsed after the second world war. 'This festival,' says Roger Paine, the city's chief executive, 'is intended to raise the importance of Europe within Cardiff. In doing that, we want it also to raise our profile within Europe.
