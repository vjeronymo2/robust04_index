941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Adare Printing buy Adare Printing, the Dublin-based concern, announced the Pounds 4.15m acquisition of Great Northern Envelope Company and a 1-for-3 placing and open offer to raise IPounds 3.7m (Pounds 3.6m). GNEC, which makes printed envelopes, had pre-tax profits of Pounds 589,000 (Pounds 388,000) in the year to February 28 on turnover of Pounds 4.78m. It is owned by Mr Tony Gill and Ms Jane Gill. Adare will pay Pounds 500,000 in cash, Pounds 1.65m in loan notes and an allotment of 1.08m shares. The 2.01m new shares in the offer will be priced at IPounds 1.85.
