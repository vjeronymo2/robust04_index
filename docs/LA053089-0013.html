May 30, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

CONTRACTS REPORT

Federal Contracts Report is a summary, compiled by States News Service, of contracts in excess of $25,000 that the federal government awarded to recipients in San Diego County.

Science Applications International, a $1,444,173 Defense Nuclear Agency contract to develop characterizations of strategic sites. The firm also won a $883,547 Navy contract for research and development in geophysical plasma processes.
