Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A resident of Ari'el was killed and his wife sustained moderate injuries by gunshots fired at their car near the village of Biddiya on the Trans-Samaria highway tonight. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the two were evacuated to Beilinson Hospital in Petah Tiqva, where the husband died of his wounds. Senior IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commanders arrived at the site to find out why the soldiers had fired at Israeli civilians. Our army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that an initial debriefing shows the soldiers opened fire when they thought they were under fire. [passage omitted]
