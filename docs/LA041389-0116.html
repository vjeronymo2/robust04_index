April 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

ZVEREVA IS UPSET AT AMELIA ISLAND

Fourth-seeded Natalia Zvereva of the Soviet Union was upset by Regina Rajchrotova of Czechoslovakia, 3-6, 6-3, 6-3, in Wednesday's second round of the Bausch & Lomb tennis tournament.

Top-seeded Steffi Graf won her 28th straight match of the year, routing Elise Burgin, 6-1, 6-2.
