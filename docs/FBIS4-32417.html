BFN [Text] President of the Republic of Albania Sali Berisha received General Teoman Erkan, Turkish deputy defense minister, in a special meeting held today. At the meeting, President Berisha pointed out the satisfactory relations with Turkey, including those in the military field. He said that the signing of the joint protocol and Erkan's visit mark progress in the military cooperation between the two countries so as to preserve peace and stability in the region. On his part, General Erkan thanked Berisha for the reception hosted for him in Albania and said that Turkey was determined to further bolster the relations between the two armed forces.
