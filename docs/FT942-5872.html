940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: McKechnie sells La Cornubia for Pounds 4.7m McKechnie, the plastics and metal components group, has sold La Cornubia, a copper sulphate and copper-based fungicides producer, to LC Holdings, a company controlled by Phillip Chemicals of New York. The consideration is FFr40.3m (Pounds 4.68m) to be paid in cash and the purchaser will also assume FFr8.8m of debt.
