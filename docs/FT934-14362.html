931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Footsie closes at record high The FT-SE 100 Index yesterday reached a new trading peak of 3,126.7, fuelled by heavy arbitraging from the stock index futures sector, before closing at a new high of 3,120.8, a gain of 34.5 on the day and 12.2 points on the week. The FT-A All-Share Index also set a new peak, closing at 1,541.91, up 0.9 per cent from a previous close of 1,527.78. London stocks, Page 15 Lex, Page 24 Markets, Weekend II
