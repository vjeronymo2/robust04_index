930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Sinclair Goldsmith halves losses to Pounds 0.26m Sinclair Goldsmith, the principal activities of which are centred in the field of property with estate agencies, rating consultancies and other services, halved its pre-tax loss from Pounds 589,000 to Pounds 265,000 in the six months to November 30. Losses per share worked through at 2.1p compared with 4.8p. There is no interim dividend against 0.5p but last year's final was also passed. Mr Edward Langton, chairman, said that the directors were hoping to improve the trading performance by the year-end. Following the announcement of February 18, discussions with Conrad Ritblat were continuing.
