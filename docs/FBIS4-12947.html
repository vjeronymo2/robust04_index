BFN [Text] Brother president Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] yesterday evening received Mr. Guido di Tella, Argentine foreign minister. The minister conveyed to brother Abu-'Ammar the greetings of Mr. Carlos Menem, president of Argentina. During this meeting, they reviewed the latest developments in the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations. Brother president Abu-'Ammar highlighted the dangers of Israel's resorting to procrastination and delay, and failure to abide by the times agreed upon for the implementation [of the Gaza-Jericho agreement] -- particularly since today is 13 April -- and the resulting serious repercussions on the whole peace process. During the meeting, they also discussed issues of mutual interest to the two countries. The minister gave the brother president a message containing the Argentine Government's decision to open up Palestinian representation in Buenos Aires.
