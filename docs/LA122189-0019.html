December 21, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CAR OVERTURNS, KILLING SAUGUS MAN

A car apparently traveling at an unsafe speed rolled over on Sierra Highway in Agua Dulce Wednesday, killing the 35-year-old driver, the California Highway Patrol reported.

The name of the dead man, a Saugus resident, was withheld until his relatives could be notified. The man was thrown from the car when it went out of control shortly after 5 p.m. on the southbound side of the highway, about three miles east of Agua Dulce Canyon Road, Officer David Moeller said. No one else was injured.
