920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Eadie calls for Pounds 2.37m Eadie Holdings, the Glasgow-based specialist engineering group, is seeking to raise Pounds 2.37m with a two-for-three rights issue at 14p per share. Eadie's shares closed 3p lower at 17p. The company, which is moving up from the USM to a full listing, is buying MCM, the materials handling group, for an initial payment of Pounds 2.5m.
