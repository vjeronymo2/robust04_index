BFN [Report by KAZTAG correspondent Ivan Zakharchenko for TASS] [Text] Almaty 6 June TASS -- "India fully supports Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev's initiative to convene a conference on measures to promote trust and cooperation in Asia, and he is actively participating in its realisation," State Indian Foreign Minister Salman Khurshid said on Monday [6 June]. The minister made this statement after his delegation was received by the Kazakh president. Touched upon the Kashmir problem the Indian diplomat noted that the Kazakh president "is doing everything possible for its fair and peaceful settlement." The two sides discussed the implementation of agreements on developing trade and economic ties between the two countries. Indian businessmen arrived in Almaty to establish contacts with Kazakh entrepreneurs, the Indian foreign minister stressed. During Khurshid's meeting with the president, they also discussed the pace of work on the agreement on mutual investments to provide firm guarantees and the activity of the Council of Business Cooperation with Kazakhstan created by India, which includes directors of private enterprises.
