921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / Standard sues over Bombay losses STANDARD Chartered is sueing 16 banks and financial institutions, including Citibank, for Rs6.5bn (Pounds 150m) of the Rs8.9bn it says it has lost in the Rs35bn Bombay securities scandal, writes Shiraz Sidhva in New Delhi. The claims relate to securities transactions between August 1991 and April 1992 and have been filed to recover 'dishonoured transactions' against Citibank and several Indian banks.
