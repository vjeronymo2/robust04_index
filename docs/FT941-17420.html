940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / Singapore GDP up 9.8% Singapore's gross domestic product grew by 9.8 per cent in 1993 compared with 5.8 per cent in 1992, Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong said in a new year speech. He said government growth forecasts indicated growth between 6 and 8 per cent in 1994. writes Kieran Cooke. Mr Goh said Singapore had continued to attract high levels of foreign investment. In 1993, foreign investments amounted to SDollars 3.9bn (Dollars 2.43bn), up from SDollars 3.5bn in 1992.
