921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / Tajikistan KGB chief killed Unidentified gunmen have killed the pro-Islamic deputy KGB chief in former Soviet Tajikistan, whose deposed Communist leaders have been fighting their way back to power, writes Steve LeVine in Tashkent. Mr Jurabek Aminov, among the warring Central Asian nation's most articulate and effective leaders, was killed in a spray of bullets on Wednesday night as he drove to the airport in the Tajik capital of Dushanbe.
