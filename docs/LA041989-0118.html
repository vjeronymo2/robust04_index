April 19, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Craig Takeover Considered: Yucaipa Capital Partners, a Claremont-based limited partnership, has said in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing that it may seek control of Craig Corp. Yucaipa said in the document that it may add to its 9.3% stake in Craig or sell all or part of that stock. The SEC statement also said Yucaipa may discuss possible corporate restructuring options with Craig. Executives at Los Angeles-based Craig, which owns 50% of the Stater Bros. supermarket company, had no immediate comment. Yucaipa filed the statement Friday. Craig stock closed at $38.50, up 50 cents Tuesday.
