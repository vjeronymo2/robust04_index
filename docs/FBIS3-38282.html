Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] France has reacted today to the appointment of a new president in Algeria through Catherine Colonna, the Foreign Ministry deputy spokesperson. Paris hopes that new Algerian President Liamine Zeroual will rapidly embark on a genuine political dialogue to enable the country to return to the path of democracy and national reconciliation. France also hopes that talks with the IMF will soon lead to concrete results so that the recovery of the Algerian economy might resume.
