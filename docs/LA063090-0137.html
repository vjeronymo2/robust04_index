June 30, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY GIRLS COMPETE IN SUMMER BASKETBALL SHOOTOUT

Brea-Olinda, Costa Mesa and Woodbridge high schools are among the 16 Orange County girls' basketball teams competing at the Summer Shootout, which continues through Sunday at Century High School in Santa Ana.

Twenty-seven teams are playing in the tournament, which started Thursday. The championship game is scheduled for 7:30 p.m. Sunday.
