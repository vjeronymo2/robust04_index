930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Frogmore expands property estate Frogmore Estates is to acquire the issued capital and outstanding debt of Jaguar Properties for Pounds 15.8m in cash and shares. Jaguar's only asset is Harley House in London's Marylebone Road. The mainly late Victorian residential property comprises six blocks containing 91 flats, 21 of which are vacant.
