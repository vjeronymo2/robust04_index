BFN [Text] Rafah, 19 Apr (MENA)--Six Palestinian deportees arrived at the Rafah border crossing point today coming from Libya on their way to the occupied territories in Gaza and Jericho. An informed source stated that this is the second group of Palestinian deportees, over whom agreement was reached with the Israeli side to repatriate them into the self-administrative areas in accordance with the current negotiations. Another group is expected to arrive the day after tomorrow, Thursday, including 11 deportees who were residing in Algeria.
