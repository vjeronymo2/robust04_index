Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "News and Weather" program] [Text] Wu Bangguo, secretary of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee and head of the municipality's communications industry leading group, last evening met and held cordial and friendly conversations with Robert Urbain, Belgium's foreign trade and European affairs minister, and his party. Minister Urbain visited Shanghai in May last year with the large foreign trade delegation led by Belgian King Albert. At the meeting, Wu Bangguo welcomed the accompanying Belgian entrepreneurs to invest and participate in cooperation in Shanghai. Minister Urbain believed that Belgian-Shanghai economic and trade cooperation would develop further. Yesterday, Minister Urbain and his party visited Shanghai's New Pudong District and the Shanghai Bell Telephone Equipment Manufacturing Construction Company, Ltd.
