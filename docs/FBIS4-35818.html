BFN [Text] Al-'Arish, 5 Jun (MENA) -- A group of 190 Palestinian officers and noncommissioned officers arriving from the Palestinian headquarters in Tunis passed through the Rafah crossing-point today. Major General 'Abd-al-Raziq al-Mahayidah, commander of the Palestinian forces in Rafah, said that the group includes personnel from the various specialized police agencies. He said that this group will start work and deploy in the self-rule areas to augment the Palestinian police force in Gaza and Jericho.
