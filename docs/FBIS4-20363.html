CSO [Summary] Liaoning has set the following agricultural goals for 1994: grain output 15 million tons; cotton 40,000-50,000 tons; oil crops 260,000 tons; beets 550,000 tons; flue-cured tobacco 40,000 tons; vegetables 10 million tons, the vegetable area 700,000 mu; and fruit 2,300,000 tons. The per capita net income of farmers will reach 1300 yuan.
