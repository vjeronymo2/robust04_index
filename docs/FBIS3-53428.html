Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Today's Tehran Friday prayers were led by Ayatollah Mohammad Emami-Kashani at Tehran University with the participation of the masses of worshippers. Friday prayers were also held throughout the country. On the occasion of the 40th day of the demise of the scholar associated with the immaculate Ahl al-Beit family, Grand Ayatollah Golpayegani -- sanctified be his soul -- the interim Friday imam of Tehran in his first sermon praised his eminence's knowledge and piety, his services to Islam and seminaries, as well as his struggles to defend Islamic values. He said that his eminence's demise was a great loss to the world of Islam. The Friday prayer leader referred to the point that jurisprudence, piety, knowledge of current affairs and the capability to defend religion are the basic conditions of Marja'iyat. Continuing, he said that considering the personality of Grand Ayatollah Araki, particularly his eminence's knowledge and piety, and that today, the Marja'iyat of the Shi'as is limited to the person of Grand Ayatollah Araki. [passage omitted] The interim Friday prayer imam of Tehran referred to different conspiracies hatched by the word of blasphemy and arrogance against Muslims, and said: The sorrowful situations in Palestine, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Afghanistan, and other crisis-ridden areas of the world of Islam are all due to the hostility of the enemies of Islam, which appear in various forms. He went on to say that their efforts to silence the voice of Islam is futile and the light of Islam, now more than ever, attracts the hearts of the justice-seeking people of the world and will continue to do so.
