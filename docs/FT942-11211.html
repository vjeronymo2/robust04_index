940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Radius makes good start to this year Shares in Radius, the USM-traded computer systems and maintenance company, jumped 4p to 34p yesterday after Mr Mike Roberts, chairman, told the annual meeting that trading in the first three months of 1994 had shown a marked improvement over the comparable period. In the 13 months ended December 31, the company incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.17m (Pounds 1.23m profit for 1992).
