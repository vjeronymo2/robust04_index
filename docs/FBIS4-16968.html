BFN [Text] The Zhogorku Kenesh adopted a resolution to unite the city of Tokmak with Chuyskiy Rayon and the city of Naryn with Tianshanskiy Rayon into a single administrative unit. The deputies believe this merger to be expedient and in all respects positive. The bureaucratic staff will be reduced, management of the national economy in the region will be focused on the one hand, agrarians will be more interested in supplying the urban area with food in order to receive industrial goods in return.
