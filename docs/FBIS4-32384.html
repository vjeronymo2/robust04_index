BFN [Text] Pristina, June 22 (ATA) -- The framed trial against 14 Albanians accused of being members of the "Ministry of Defence" of the Republic of Kosova continues in the Serbian court of Pristina. The first two accused interrogated, Hajzer Hajzeraj, who before abolishment of Kosova's autonomy was deputy minister of the People's Defence and commander of the Civil Defence of Kosova, and Haxhi Ferati, magistrate of the People's Defence, turned down the accusations, saying that there is no "Ministry of Defence" of the Republic of Kosova and what is said in the charge is but slander. The two accused said that they have been arrested and physically and mentally tortured by inspectors of the Serb state security in order to obtain statements through violence.
