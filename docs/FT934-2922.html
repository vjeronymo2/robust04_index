931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: PSB resolutions rejected by ICD shareholders At the International Communication & Data extraordinary general meeting held yesterday, shareholders defeated all resolutions put forward by PSB, which requisitioned the meeting and claims to control 23.6 per cent of ICD's voting rights, for the removal of a majority of the existing directors and their replacement by PSB nominees. The resolutions were defeated by about 33m votes (58 per cent) to about 24m votes (42 per cent). On Wednesday, IMC, the USM-quoted soft drinks and leisure group, exercised its option to subscribe for 6.95m shares at 7p in ICD, the marketing services company.
