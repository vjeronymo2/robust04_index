940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Norex America NOREX AMERICA in which Norex, the financial services group, has a 48 per cent stake reported net income of Dollars 5.73m (Pounds 3.82m) for the three months to December 31, against Dollars 27.8m, for earnings per share of Dollars 0.94 (Dollars 4.55). Net income for the six months was Dollars 8.56m (Dollars 30.8m).
