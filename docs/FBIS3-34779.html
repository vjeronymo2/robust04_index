Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Islamabad, Tehran, Feb. 14, IRNA -- The Iranian consulate in Lahore denied any involvement of the Iranian diplomats in the shooting at the leaders of the Wahhabi Sahabeh Corps in the city. In a press release 13 February, the consulate said that the charges leveled in the complaint of a particular group against the Iranian diplomats were totally baseless and unfounded. It also added, "Iran is against any kind of interference in the internal affairs of other countries." Two leaders of the Wahhabi Sahabeh corps were shot and wounded by unidentified persons in Lahore Friday.
