Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe arrived in Athens last night and was welcomed by Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias. In their brief statements, the two ministers stressed the excellent cooperation relations existing between Greece and France. Mr. Juppe described as especially excellent the activities of the [European Union's] Greek presidency, especially on issues concerning the European Union. Mr. Papoulias said that they will discuss both bilateral and regional issues as well as the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Mr. Juppe will be received by Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou and will also hold talks with Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias and Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos.
