BFN [Text] "The job of improving public finances will be done with a mini-budget which will have effects on this year's deficit and on the 1995 accounts," a communique of the Treasury said. It also analyzed the "turbulence on the financial markets." "To this uncertain climate have been added some internal developments which may increase public expenses. However, these will be balanced by special measures aimed at bringing more money to the state," the communique added.
