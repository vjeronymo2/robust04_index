BFN ["Appeal by the defense minister;" date not given--read by announcer] [Text] Out of our concern for the lives of the misled and helpless pilots and those who are under pressure, and out of our concern for the safety of the remaining warplanes that are still being manipulated by the rebellious, apostate, and secessionist gang--being state-owned planes whose prices have been paid by the people--we call on all those pilots not to obey the orders issued to them by the rebellious, secessionist gang and to fly their planes to any airport under the control of the legitimate authority to ask for landing permission and to join their brothers in the Air Force. They should be confident that they will receive the same treatment that their brothers are receiving in terms of rights and duties. It is better for them to be at the side of the people, unity, and constitutional legitimacy so that they will not be filled with regret after it is useless to be so. [Signed] Staff Brigadier General 'Abd-Rabbuh Mansur Hadi, the defense minister
