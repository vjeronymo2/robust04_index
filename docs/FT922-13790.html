920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Blockleys Blockleys, the Shropshire-based brick and pavior manufacturer, suffered a Pounds 2.23m downturn in profits to Pounds 1.06m pre-tax for the 1991 year. The 68 per cent decline came from turnover 28 per cent lower at Pounds 10.3m. Operating margins fell from 30.9 per cent to 21.4 per cent. Earnings of 2.48p per share compared with 9.16p previously. The final dividend is halved to 1.43p making a 3.38p (4.81p) total.
