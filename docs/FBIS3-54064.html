Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Jan 21, SPA -- The Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz condoled, in a telephone call, Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad over the sad demise of his eldest son Basil al-Asad. King Fahd prayed to Allah almighty to bestow his mercy on the deceased and to grant his bereaved family patience and solace. Basil, 31, an army major, engineer and parachutist, died on Friday in a car crash.
