BFN [Text] Our correspondent in Aden reported a short time ago that fire is illuminating Aden's sky because of missiles being fired by the southern forces to down northern forces' missiles. The corredspondent affirmed that the vice president of the proclaimed Presidential Council in Aden, 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Jifri, contacted him a short while ago asking that an appeal be addressed through the MBC to the international community to halt the war. This comes after the northerners pounded the city of al-Waht 60 km from Aden. Al-Jifri said the attack on al-Waht killed and wounded many of its residents and displaced those who survived. He appealed to humanitarian organizations to supply Aden with doctors and medical aid.
