BFN ["Serb Village Dzindici Under Strong Moslem Fire" -- TANJUG headline] [Text] Bileca, May 3 (TANJUG) -- Moslem forces in Bosnia on Tuesday [3 May] opened strong fire on Serb combatants positions in the village of [word indistinct] by the Dzindici, eight km southwest of Gorazde, it was announced by the commander of the army of the Bosnian Serb republic [passage indistinct] In a simultaneous action, a Moslem unit tried to row across the Drina to its right bank but was forced back by Serb forces. The regular Army of Croatia went into action for a second time in the past four days on the Trebinje battlefront when, from the occupied village of Zavale, it attacked Serb positions in Mareva Ljuta. The Serb side did not respond, it was announced. Moslem forces launched provocations in the upper reaches of the Neretva around the locality of Konjic, opening anti-aircraft gun [word indistinct] sniper fire on Serb positions in this section of the battlefront, but there were no consequences for the combatants of the Herzegovina Corps. In the Doboj region, Moslem forces continued their offensive and provocative combat actions aimed against Serb positions. In addition to intensified small-arms fire along the contact line, Moslem forces acted most fiercely from the direction of Bejici whence they fired 82-mm mortars sporadically on Serb positions west of Doboj.
