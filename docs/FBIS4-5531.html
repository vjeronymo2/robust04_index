BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] hosted a reception for a delegation of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana headed by its General Secretary Huudu Yahaya Monday evening. Speaking at the reception, Politburo member and secretary of the C.C., the WPK Kye Ung-tae said the WPK highly estimated the leadership of Ghana in its strivings to expand and strengthen the party on a broad mass foundation and for the national unity of the Ghanaian people and stability of the country and independent development of the national economy under the correct leadership of respected His Excellency President Jerry John Rawlings. "We express full support to the position of the independent external policy of the party and government of Ghana advocating a new international political and economic order, adhering to the idea and principle of the non- aligned movement and developing South- South cooperation," he said. Noting that the international situation now demands the bonds of friendship and solidarity be further strengthened between the two parties and two countries striving for national independence and independent development of the country, he expressed the belief that the delegation's current visit to Korea would make a new contribution to the development of friendly relations between the two countries. Speaking next, General Secretary Huudu Yahaya said the chuche idea is a great idea expounding the position and role of man in the world, and a new international economic order can be established only on the basis of this idea. Ghana always follows with deep interest the development of the situation in Korea, and holds that the DPRK-USA relations should be solved peacefully on an equal footing, he said. He noted that they learn a lot of things from the great leader President Kim Il-song, the most distinguished man in the present time. The idea of the great leader who has devoted his whole life for the people must be followed in many countries, he stressed.
