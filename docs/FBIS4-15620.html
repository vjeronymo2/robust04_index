BFN [Text] Crimean Deputy Prime Minister Yeveniy Saburov believes that Crimea could become a real bridge between Ukraine and Russia, but for this to happen good will on the part of Crimea has to be accompanied by the willingness of the two states. In Saburov's opinion, confrontation between Ukraine and Russia is doomed since it has no economic basis. At the same time, Russia's friendly attitude to solving Crimea's economic problems inspires confidence, the deputy prime minister said at a press conference in Simferopol.
