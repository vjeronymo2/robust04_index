931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / BIS warning on capital curbs Controls on the movement of money will not repair the European Community's punctured currency grid, Mr Alexandre Lamfalussy, general manager of the Basle-based Bank for International Settlements said yesterday, Reuter reports from Brussels. 'International financial integration has encompassed practically the whole world . . . It would be neither desirable nor indeed technically feasible to isolate Europe from the rest of the world in this respect,' Mr Lamfalussy told a hearing on money market speculation at the European Parliament.
