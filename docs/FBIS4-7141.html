BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The premier of the Administration Council of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kang Song-san, sent a message of greetings to Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah upon his reappointment as prime minister of Kuwait. The message wished him success in his responsible work for the reconstruction and prosperity of the country. Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent a message of greetings to Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir upon his reappointment as foreign minister of Kuwait.
