940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / TSB staff vote on strike action A total of 15,000 TSB staff are being balloted by Bifu, the finance union, on a series of selective strikes over pay. Bifu said it was fighting the bank's plans to impose a 2 per cent pay rise which is linked to an extension of performance-related pay. The union has been seeking increases of 6 per cent.
