BFN [Text] The security services said in a statement today that its forces killed the two Hattab brothers and three other armed persons yesterday in the region of Ben Zerga, Boumerdes Province. The two Hattab brothers were wanted and were implicated in the assassination of the late Kasdi Merbah, the former prime minister and chairman of the Majd movement. They were also involved in the assassination of the late Mustafa Abeda, former director general of national television.
