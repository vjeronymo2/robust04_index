BFN [Text] Chisinau, ROMPRES, 22/06/1994 -- Tomorrow, a delegation of the Moldovan Ministry of Transportation headed by Minister Vasili Iovv, will leave for Bucharest to meet with representatives of the Romanian Transportation Ministry. Vasile Iovv told BASA that "the visit's purpose is to establish cooperation relations in the field of transportation that will yield profits to both states." Nicolae Ciobanu, deputy head of the Roads Department, told BASA that negotiations on building a bridge across the Prut at Radauti-Lipcani will be also discussed. According to Ciobanu, a program on developing and integrating Moldovan highway networks into the European system will be elaborated. A program on repairing the bridges across the Prut at Galati-Giurgiulesti, Oancea-Cahul and Stanca-Costenti and the highways at these border points will be drafted. According to the Galati agreement, these highways will be passed to international regulations. Ciobanu pointed out that a joint administration of the navigation on the Prut river with an autonomous regulation of organization and functioning will be created. Vasile Iovv stated to BASA that the railway gauge will not be yet passed to the Romanian standards as Moldova does not have sufficient funding for such works. The last meeting between Moldovan and Romanian Transportation Ministries occurred in November 1993.
