Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hamburg, January 18 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, visiting chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met and had friendly talks with Mayor of Hamburg Henning Voscherau here this morning. Qiao said relations between China and Germany have always been good. Following Chancellor Helmut Kohl's visit to China last year, bilateral ties have entered a stage of all-round development, he added. "I believe that with the growth of bilateral ties, the relationship between China and Hamburg will be further enhanced," he said. Voscherau said the people of hamburg are looking with admiration at China's successful reform and opening up in the past 15 years. With the development of China, he said, relations between the two countries will grow smoothly. Extending his warm welcome to the Chinese guests, he said Qiao's visit to Germany shows bilateral ties have been further strengthened. Qiao, his wife and entourage arrived here yesterday evening from Berlin.
