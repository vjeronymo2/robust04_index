Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Naples. The science park projects will be launched by the end of January. The announcement was made by Aldo Romano, Science and Technology Park Committee Coordinator for the Ministry of Scientific Research, at a meeting held to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the CNR [National Research Council]. By the end of the year, the organizations whose proposals have been selected will be required to submit their general projects and the committee will see to launching the programs immediately after. Ministry of Scientific and Technological Research Undersecretary Silvia Costa stated that the selection of organizations that had requested funding had been very severe and only 10 out of the 31 proposals presented have been considered "buildable" projects. Undersecretary Costa also explained that no facilities will be built to house the research and manufactuirng activities, but that there will be coordination among all the research institutes and companies connected with them. If projects that necessarily call for the construction of laboratories and plants are presented, these will be examined very carefully by the committee. The Ministry for Extraordinary Interventions in Southern Italy had already allocated 1.1 trillion lire, 300 billion of which just for the start up phase.
