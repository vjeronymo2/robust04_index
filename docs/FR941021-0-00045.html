Background On February 15, 1994, the FAA published a final rule establishing the AMPP for the aviation industry (59 FR 7380). Since the publication of the final rule, the FAA identified six items requiring editorial amendment to correct typographical errors or to clarify provisions to reflect the FAA's actual intent. These revisions will facilitate implementation of the final rule.
