930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Seton pays Pounds 2.8m for Boots brands Seton Healthcare Group has acquired 10 over-ther-counter brands from Crookes Healthcare, the Boots offshoot. Consideration for the products, which include Famel cough remedy, Acriflex antiseptic cream and Sea Legs travel sickness remedy, amounted to Pounds 2.8m cash.
