Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 42/94] [Text] The EMOR opinion research organisation published the results of opinion poll in February. The Coalition Party still leads the popularity list with 18 percent (20 in January). The second is the Royalist Party with 8 percent of support. The Centrists have improved their standing and came third with 7 percent of support. The leading partner of the ruling coalition, the "Fatherland" came fourth with 6 percent, while its partners, the Social Democrats, the National Independence Party and the Rural Centrists won, respectively, 3, 2 and 1 percent of support. The Liberal Democrats were also supported by 1 percent. The recently- formed Association of Lawful Owners has the support of 3 percent of those questioned, the Estonian Citizens League and the Democratic Labour Party (former communists) were also supported by 3 percent.
