Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An agreement has been signed this morning in Tripoli between the Great Jamahiriyah and Sudan. This agreement embraced the consolidation of the integration march between the Great Jamahiriyah and Sudan within the framework of embodying the four-freedoms agreement, i.e. the freedoms of movement, residence, work, and ownership between the sons of the one Arab people in the two fraternal countries. The agreement was signed by the brother secretary general [as heard] at the General People's Committee of Justice and Public Security and brother 'Abd-al-Rahim Muhammad Husayn, interior minister in brotherly Sudan.
