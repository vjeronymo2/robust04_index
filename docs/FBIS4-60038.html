BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 May (MENA)--The Swiss Government has decided to write off 150 million Swiss francs--230 million Egyptian pounds--of Egypt's debts to Switzerland. Dr. Yusuf Butrus-Ghali, minister of state for international cooperation, made this statement today after a meeting with Mr. Kelin Berger, Swiss minister of state for foreign affairs [name and title as received]. Dr. Butrus-Ghali said the terms of the agreement included spending the equivalent of this amount on development projects to create additional job opportunities, and to develop projects that would contribute toward decreasing the burden of economic reform on the low-income sector.
