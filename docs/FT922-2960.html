920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / US prices up by 0.1% in May US consumer prices rose by a modest 0.1 per cent in May, cutting the year-on-year inflation rate to 3.0 per cent, reports George Graham in Washington. The Bureau of Labor Statistics said food prices fell by 0.4 per cent during May, offsetting a 0.6 per cent increase in energy prices. The core year-on-year inflation rate, excluding food and energy, dipped slightly to 3.8 per cent. Currencies, Page 11
