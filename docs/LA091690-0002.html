September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I played the sappy music behind Secure Horizon's "Harry" commercial of which Lorraine Evans (Viewers' Views, Aug. 19) is so tired.

She should realize that the actor who portrays Harry probably will retire comfortably, and I will have my monthly phone bill paid each month by royalties we receive when the commercial is running.

So in the interest of commerce and long-distance rates, hang in there, Lorraine, and hope for the version when Beth doesn't make it.

Peter Siebert, Santa Ana
