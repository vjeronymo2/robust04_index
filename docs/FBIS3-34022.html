Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn has received a telephone call from his brother Yemeni President Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, during which they exchanged views on various Arab and regional issues of joint interest. They also discussed efforts being exerted to implement the agreement reached to settle differences between the brothers in Yemen, and the coming stages to implement the agreement in a way that guarantees the Yemeni people's unity and Yemen's territorial integrity.
