Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, March 15 (SNARK)--The political course pursued by Georgia is positively appraised in the United States. This statement was made today by Eduard Shevardnadze at the Georgian Parliament sitting. Sharing his impressions of the recent visit to the United States, he noted with satisfaction that Washington supports the integrity and indivisibility of Georgia. Speaking about the possibility of bringing UN peacekeeping forces into Georgian-Abkhazian conflict zone, Shevardnadze pointed out that UN Security Council can make such a decision only in terms of mutual agreement of both sides. The Georgian Head also said the Secretary General of the UN has already appealed to the governments of 14 countries with a request to submit military forces for the peacekeeping mission.
