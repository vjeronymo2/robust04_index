BFN [Text] Austrian Chancellor Franz Vranitzky and Prime Minister Vyacheslaw Kebich held negotiations in Vienna today. Local political observers deemed the recently established contacts between the two countries' leaders as fruitful. Austria supports the balanced political line of Belarus and its moving toward achieving accord within the CIS, as well as strengthening ties with both Russia and European states. During the negotiations, emphasis was placed on ways of seeking new forms of mutual cooperation; interest was also expressed in expanding contacts in the areas of politics, economics, and culture, according to an ITAR-TASS correspondent.
