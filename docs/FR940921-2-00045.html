Public Health Service National Institutes of Health Notice of Listing of Members of the National Institutes of Health's Senior Executive Service Performance Review Board (PRB) The National Institutes of Health (NIH) announces the persons who will serve on the National Institutes of Health's Senior Executive Service Performance Review Board. This action is being taken in accordance with Title 5, U.S.C., Section 4314(c)(4) of the Civil Service Reform Act of 1978, Public Law 95&hyph;454, which requires members of performance review boards be appointed to ensure consistency, stability, and objectivity in performance appraisals and requires the appointment of performance review board members be published in the Federal Register .
