Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Soldiers shot and wounded two Arab residents of the Qalandiyah refugee camp near Ramallah last night. The soldiers say they opened fire on the Palestinian car when the driver refused orders to stop. In Rafah in the Gaza Strip, shots were fired last night at a Border Police base. No one was injured.
