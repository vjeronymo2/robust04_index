930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / International Company News: Suedzucker SUEDZUCKER, the German sugar producer, plans to double to 50 per cent its stake in Holding Agrana Beteiligungs, the sugar and starch producer, Reuter reports from Mannheim. The company would finance the purchase of shares with a nominal value of DM33m through a one-for-12 rights issue announced last week.
