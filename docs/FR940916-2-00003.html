Rural Electrification Administration Municipal Interest Rates for Fourth Quarter of 1994 AGENCY: Rural Electrification Administration, USDA. ACTION: Notice of municipal interest rates on advances from insured electric loans for the fourth quarter of 1994.

SUMMARY: REA hereby announces the interest rates for advances on municipal rate loans with interest rate terms beginning during the fourth calendar quarter of 1994. DATES: These interest rates are effective for interest rate terms that commence during the period beginning October 1, 1994, and ending December 31, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Patrick Shea, Financial Analyst, Program Support Staff, U.S. Department of Agriculture, Rural Electrification Administration, room 2230&hyph;S, 14th Street & Independence Avenue SW., Washington, DC 20250&hyph;1500. Telephone: 202&hyph;720&hyph;0736. FAX: 202&hyph;720&hyph;4120.
