BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 23 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In accordance with the directive of the president of the country there has been set up a working group for elaborating the draft law on freedom of press and information headed by the Minister of Justice N. Lubsanjab. Included into the composition of the working group are the editors of the largest organs of the press and mass media means. On discussing by the government the draft law should be submitted for consideration by the spring session of the Great State Hural. [sentence as received]
