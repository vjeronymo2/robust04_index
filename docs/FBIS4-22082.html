BFN [Text] Sanaa, 22 Apr (INA) -- The Political Bureau of the Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, has stressed the continued escalation of military operations against the Zionist occupation forces as long as occupation exists on Palestinian territory. In a statement published in Sanaa yesterday, Hamas urged the unconditional withdrawal of the Zionist forces from the West Bank, Gaza Strip, and Jerusalem as well as the removal of the settlements, the evacuation of the settlers from the West Bank, Gaza Strip, and Jerusalem and the holding of free legislative elections for the Palestinian people inside and outside the occupied territories.
