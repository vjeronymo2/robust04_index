BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' this afternoon received 'Abd-al-Rahman Mansuri, under secretary at the Saudi Foreign Ministry for political affairs. The meeting was attended by Assistant Foreign Minister Yusuf Shakkur and the Saudi ambassador in Damascus. During the meeting, the foreign minister received a message from Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, the Saudi foreign minister, on regional developments and the postponement until next week of the meeting of the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration's signatory states, which was originally scheduled to be held in Kuwait 28-29 June.
