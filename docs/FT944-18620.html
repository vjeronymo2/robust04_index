941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Arts (Openings): Rome Carlo Maria Giulini conducts the Orchestra dell'Accademia Nazionale di Santa Cecilia tomorrow at the gala re-opening of the Auditorio di Via della Conciliazione, Rome's main concert hall. The building, which the orchestra rents from the Vatican, has just undergone a Dollars 1.5m refurbishment, including acoustical adjustments.
