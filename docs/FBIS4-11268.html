BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 22 Apr (NA) -- President Carlos Menem met with Belgian Prince Philippe and a Chinese Communist Party [CPC] delegation headed by senior official Hu Jintao this morning at the Olivos Residence. The CPC delegation was received at the residence's main chalet by President Menem; Eduardo Bauza, the secretary general of the presidency; and Osvaldo Castruccio, Foreign Ministry under secretary for institutional relations. According to unofficial sources, the president and the CPC delegation discussed the need to enhance bilateral trade. Menem later met Belgian Prince Phillipe who was joined by Robert Urbain, the Belgian foreign trade and European affairs minister, and Marc Serbotte, the Belgian Foreign Trade Office ambassador. During this meeting, Menem was joined by Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella and Argentine ambassador to Belgium Victor Massuh. The president and Prince Philippe took advantage of the sunshine to stroll in the gardens of the residence.
