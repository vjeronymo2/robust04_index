920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / International Company News: Pinelands shares soar on sale news SHARES in Pinelands, a New Jersey-based company, soared Dollars 5 7/8 to Dollars 17 7/8 in active trading on Wall Street yesterday on news that the company had agreed to be acquired by Chris-Craft Industries for Dollars 18 a share or about Dollars 310m, writes Karen Zagor in New York. If the deal goes ahead, it will allow Chris-Craft to become one of the largest US non-network television station businesses by giving the group a foothold in the important New York market. Chris-Craft's BHC Communications division operates seven stations. On Wall Street, shares in Chris-Craft slipped Dollars 3/4 to Dollars 26 3/4 .
