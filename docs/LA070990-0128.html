July 9, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

TIMELESS GETS HIGH WEIGHT FOR BEL-AIR 'CAP

Timeless Answer, unbeaten in two sprint handicaps at the current Hollywood Park meeting, will carry high weight of 118 pounds for Saturday's $150,000-added Bel-Air Handicap to be contested at one mile over the main track.

The 4-year-old colt, owned by Ed Friendly and trained by Ron Ellis, was assigned one pound more than Annual Date, who defeated Music Merci in a one-mile race at Hollywood Park in his last outing.

Weights for the Bel-Air:

Timeless Answer, 118; Annual Date, 117; a-Exemplary Leader, 116; a-Prospectors Gamble, 116; No Marker, 115; Exploding Prospect, 114; Hot Operator, 113; b-Trebizond, 113; Charlatan, 112; Music Prospector, 112; b-Kennedy's Knockout, 111; Well Aware, 110 (a-Brian Mayberry-trained entry; b-Robert W. Marshall-trained entry).
