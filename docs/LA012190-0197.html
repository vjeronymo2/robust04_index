January 21, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

SOS letter -- A Jan. 14 letter to the editor from Sylvia Easton, chairwoman of the Irvine Task Force for the Homeless, regarding the closing of Share Our Selves, was incorrectly edited to indicate that about 200,000 needy die in Orange County each year. The meaning intended was that if the 200,000 who seek services from SOS each year were turned away, many could die.
