940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: FT quarterly personal finance review -At a Glance Investing some money overseas is not just a form of risk-spreading - it is also a way of getting a stake in economies that appear to have strong prospects for growth, and it allows investors to participate in industries which do not exist at home. The summer issue of the FT Quarterly Review of Personal Finance, to be published with next Friday's paper and repeated with Saturday's, considers the best ways of investing overseas - and where. The survey section of the Review takes a critical look at Personal Pensions, in the light of recent bad publicity.
