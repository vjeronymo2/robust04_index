BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 21 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, extended thanks to medical workers of people's hospitals who had made active contributions to the promotion of health of People's Army soldiers and set good examples in aiding the People's Army. Among them were medical workers of the clinical section No. 2 of the Neurological Hospital of the Korean General Red Cross Hospital, the Sunchon City People's Hospital and the Sariwon Railway Administration Hospital. They have enjoyed love and respect of soldiers and civilians by showing all sincerity in medical services to the population and in medical treatment of soldiers. Comrade Kim Chong-il also extended thanks to the officials and employees of the Tongdaewon garment factory in Pyongyang. Over the past 20 years the officials and employees of this factory have overfulfilled the factory's annual assignments of the national economy on all indices and done a lot of good job for the society and the people.
