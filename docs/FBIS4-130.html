BFN [Text] March 30, (IRNA) -- Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati in meeting with Speaker of Burkina Faso's National Assembly Bonghessan Arseney here today said that the parliaments of the two countries could play an important role in the expansion of mutual ties. He proposed that Iran could provide its legislative experiences to the national assembly of Burkina Faso. Arseney in reply said that Muslims in Burkina Faso need mosques, clinics and schools, and called on Iran to provide such services to the Muslims of his country. Muslims constitute about 43 percent of the 10 million population of Burkina Faso.
