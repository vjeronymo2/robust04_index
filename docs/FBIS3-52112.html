Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman--AL-WATAN has learned from informed Palestinian sources that a high-level Palestinian delegation will arrive in Amman this week, and that the delegation will sign Jordanian-PLO economic agreements that have been finalized by special bilateral committees. This official visit will come on the heels of recent talks held between His Majesty King Husayn and the Palestinian president, during which the Palestinian side affirmed its concern to maintain the special Palestinian-Jordanian relationship regardless of the impediments entailed. In a separate development, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at his office yesterday Dr. George Habash, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine, who arrived in Amman the day before yesterday. AL-WATAN has learned that there is currently a consensus within the Palestinian side, particularly the PLO leadership, that the Israeli leaders will maintain their procrastination and prevarication regarding the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho accord, and that it is in the interest of the Palestinians to coordinate with various Palestinian factions and forces to ensure harmony within the Palestinian camp, and also to enhance the relationship with Jordan and other Arab sides.
