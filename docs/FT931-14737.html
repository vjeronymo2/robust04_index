930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Flat year for LPA Industries Profits of LPA Industries remained virtually static at Pounds 477,000 pre-tax for the 12 months ended September 30. Turnover slipped marginally from Pounds 6.49m to Pounds 6.24m. Exports totalled Pounds 997,000, an increase of 24.5 per cent. A final dividend of 1.87p makes a 3.52p (same) total. Earnings were at 3.53p (3.69p). The USM-quoted company manufactures industrial electrical accessories.
