BFN [Excerpt] Six deputy chairmen of the Transitional National Council were elected today in accordance with the internal statute passed a week ago. The Transitional National Council today held a session chaired by Mr. Abdelkader Bensalah, the council's chairman, to begin the election of six deputy chairmen from among the 22 candidates. Over to Nacema Kerat. [Kerat] The election was held today by secret ballot and resulted in only two candidates winning in the first round by obtaining the majority required by the internal statute. They were Mr. Mohamed Taher (Dilmi), national secretary of the UGTA [Algerian General Workers Union], and Mr. Abdelmadjid (Monacera), national secretary in charge of information in HAMAS [Islamic Society Movement]. This meant that a second round had to be held to elect the other four deputy chairmen. Following the voting in this round, the following were elected as deputy chairmen: Abdelouahab (Oughidmi) of the Sons of the Martyrs Organization, Abdelhafiz Amoukran (Elhaceni) of the War Veterans' Organization, Nacer (Sakhri) of the National Party for Solidarity and Development, and Hacen (Haider) of the National Union of Algerian Farmers. Thus the council has completed the formation of its bureau that is composed of a chairman and six deputy chairman. [passage omitted]
