930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Tory broadcast a 'pathetic trick' LABOUR last night attacked as a 'pathetic trick' a Tory party political broadcast timed to precede news programmes reporting Mr John Smith's main conference speech. The broadcast, featuring a typical couple sitting in front of a blank television screen, poked fun at the Labour leader, blaming him for the party's tax policy in the run-up to the last election. Labour said the broadcast showed the Conservatives were 'running scared'.
