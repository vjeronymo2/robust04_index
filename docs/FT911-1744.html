910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Overseas Inv Tst asset value recovers The Overseas Investment Trust ended the half-year to March 31 with a net asset value of 277.6p. This was little changed from the 277.8p achieved at the end of the comparable period, but was an improvement on the sharply reduced figure of 213.7p reported at the September year end. Gross revenue fell 30 per cent from Pounds 1.12m to Pounds 809,000. Interest took Pounds 90,000 (Pounds 58,000), administrative expenses Pounds 279,000 (Pounds 264,000) and tax Pounds 186,000 (Pounds 301,000), leaving net revenue of Pounds 254,000 (Pounds 492,000). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p on earnings per share of 0.67p (1.29p).
