940426 FT 26 APR 94 / International Company News: Grasim posts rise to Rs2.27bn Grasim Industries, part of Mr Aditya Birla's industrial group, reported net profits of Rs2.27bn (Dollars 72m), up almost two-thirds on the year-ago figure, on sales up a quarter at Rs22.02bn in the year to March. The dividend has been raised to Rs. 4.75 from Rs4. Grasim produces viscose staple fibre, textiles, cement, sponge iron and computer software.
