BFN ["Own information" report: "Foreign Investment in Russian Economy Declines in First Quarter" -- from the FINANSOVYYE IZVESTIYA section] [Text] According to preliminary figures from the Russian State Committee for Statistics [Goskomstat], the growth of foreign investment in the Russian economy in the first quarter of 1994 came to $290 million -- a decline of 57 percent on the fourth quarter of 1993. Investments in joint ventures account for around a quarter of all investment. Goskomstat specialists note that this year, as in the past, the quarterly dynamics of foreign investment inflows into Russia are very irregular.
