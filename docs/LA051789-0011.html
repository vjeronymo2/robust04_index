May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

CAL THOMAS ON ABORTION

Thomas' column points to a disturbing trend in our culture, that of substituting a more palatable reality for the truth. Children don't do this, and it seems to me that's what really disturbs adults. Somehow they can look at truth and not shy away from it. Thanks for running this column. Thomas consistently finds the ethical questions we all ask and so many others are looking away from.

ANDREA S. LEACH

Fullerton
