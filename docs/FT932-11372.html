930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / IG Metall negotiations collapse NEGOTIATIONS aimed at ending the two-day strike in east Germany collapsed last night when the IG Metall union said employers would not rescind their decision to cancel an existing wage pact, Reuter reports from Dresden. Mr Hasso Duevel, head of the union's negotiators, said after 13 hours of talks that strike action in the east German engineering sector would be stepped up.
