930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: CU Environmental assets improve CU Environmental Trust returned an attributable profit of Pounds 29,000 for the six months ended June 30, compared with Pounds 92,000 for the three months to end-June 1992. Earnings per share for the half year emerged at 0.16p (0.53p for period). Net asset value per share in the first six months rose by 8.4 per cent to 110.9p.
