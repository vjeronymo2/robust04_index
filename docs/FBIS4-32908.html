BFN [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 30 May (AFP) -- This Monday, Army of National Liberation (ELN) guerrillas ambushed and killed Santiago Angarita, a community leader in the Colombian town of Convencion, Norte de Santander, from whom the rebels seized voting cards from this Sunday's presidential elections and burned them. Radio reports from Santa Fe de Bogota stated Angarita was intercepted on his way to the National Civil Registry of Cucuta, a provincial capital bordering Venezuela. The guerrillas stripped Angarita of the sealed ballot box and then killed him. The community leader transported the votes cast at the Municipalities of San Jose, Las Pitas, and Cartagenita and the town of Convencion. Presidential elections held this Sunday in Colombia resulted in a virtual tie between Liberal Party candidate Ernesto Samper and Conservative Party candidate Andres Pastrana, who will vie in a runoff election on Sunday, 19 June.
