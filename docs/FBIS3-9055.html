Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, March 15 (BTA) -- Deputy Prime Minister Evgeni Matinchev and Minister of Industry Rumen Bikov had a meeting with Macedonian Deputy Prime Minister Jovan Andonov, BTA was told by the Government Press Office. The sides considered the transit of Macedonian goods via Bulgaria, the transportation of lamb and live lambs from and to Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], the construction of a railway connection between the two countries, cooperation between Bulgarian and Macedonian companies and the possibility of Bulgaria providing aid to Macedonia. The meeting was attended by Sofia Mayor Aleksandur Yanchulev, experts of the Ministries of Industry and Transport, Bulgarian and Macedonian customs senior officials and business executives. Specialized commissions continued the talks specifying details of bilateral cooperation in transport, industry, customs control and road construction. Minister of Science and Education Marko Todorov and Macedonian Minister for Development Sofia Todorova considered Bulgarian-Macedonian cooperation in science and education, BTA was told by the Press Centre of the Ministry of Science and Education.
