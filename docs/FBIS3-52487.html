Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The second round of talks between Turkmen Vice President Boris Shikhmuradov and Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati was held in Tehran today. In these talks Mr. Shikhmuradov said his country was ready to expand ties in all fields. He also said the two countries shared many common aspects. He then called for efforts to carry out research in the history of relations between the two countries. Welcoming the proposal, Mr. Velayati said the Foreign Ministry's office of political studies had carried out a great deal of research in that field. He then proposed that researchers should be sent to Tehran from Turkmenistan to further study the topic, a proposal that was accepted by the other party.
