November 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

USAir, All Nippon Airways Plan Link: USAir Group agreed to initiate service between Orlando, Fla., and Washington as part of a cooperative agreement with All Nippon Airways.

The agreement allows ANA to offer customers round-trip flights between Tokyo and Orlando -- a popular tourist destination -- with a stop in Washington. Both airlines said they have asked the U.S. government to allow shared flight codes and blocked space arrangements, under which ANA would guarantee USAir a certain amount of revenue for the Washington-Orlando flights. The airlines said they would explore other cooperative arrangements, perhaps on flights between California and Japan.
