BFN [Text] Reports of intermittent sniper firing have been received from Liaqatabad, Khwaja Ajmer Nagri, New Karachi and Gulbahar areas of Karachi Central district. Nine people suffered gunshot injuries. Public transport has been affected, and shops are closed in the Karachi central district. Earlier, two persons succumbed to their injuries. Authorities have imposed Section 144 in the Karachi East district and banned the assembly of more than five people and the carrying of passengers on motorcycles. Reports of gunfire have also been reported from (?several) areas of Hyderabad. Contingents of police and law enforcement authorities are patrolling the affected areas. The district magistrate of Hyderabad in a press note has said that one person was killed and one injured in an incident involving gunfire in village No. 7 of Hyderabad [words indistinct]. A case has been filed, and investigations have begun. Two suspects have been arrested.
