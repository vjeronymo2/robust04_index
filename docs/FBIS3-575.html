Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The flag no longer flies on the roof of the former Italian embassy in Mogadishu. It was lowered at 0718 this morning. Five members of the "Colonel Moschin" battalion lowered the flag -- the same five men who had raised the tricolor on 15 December 1992. There are no more Italian troops in Mogadishu. Some divisions remain in Jowhar, where this morning General Carmine Fiore, the commander of the Italian contingent, will hand over the "Italia" hospital in the town to the non-governmental organization, InterSos. The hospital will be run by Italian and Somali doctors.
