BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said that now, following Prime Minister Rabin's statements on the readiness to evacuate settlements on the Golan Heights in exchange for peace, Syrian President al-Asad had good reason to emit positive signals toward Israel. The foreign minister told our correspondent Hayim Hecht that the change indicated by Rabin's remarks lay in the statement itself and not in its content. The foreign minister added that the government realized that Syria was always slow to react.
