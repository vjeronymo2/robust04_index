941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Utd Energy loss Weak oil prices and exchange rate movements adversely affected United Energy, the USM-traded oil and gas group, in the six months to June 30. The outcome was an interim loss of Pounds 193,000, compared with a profit of Pounds 40,000 last year, on turnover down 21 per cent from Pounds 1.58m to Pounds 1.25m. Losses per share were 0.57p (earnings of 0.12p).
