Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, January 21, RIA -- Issue No. 1 of ROSSIYSKAYA FEDERATSIYA (RUSSIAN FEDERATION), a journal of the government of Russia, is off the press. This is the only specialised periodical in the country designed for those associated with the governmental sphere, deputies and officials of bodies of state administration at every level. The journal will also be of interest to general readership, to anyone concerned with problems of the development of Russia's new statehood. ROSSIYSKAYA FEDERATSIYA means information on how laws are made and how they work in practice, what deputies of the Federal Assembly are engaged in, and how the vital problems of the country and every citizen of Russia are tackled. The journal will give detailed coverage to the course of constitutional reform and analyse the alignment of political forces in bodies of legislation and the positions of political parties and public movements. Every issue will contain legal information, advice by experienced lawyers, answers to questions.
