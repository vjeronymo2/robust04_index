BFN [Text] A protocol on security cooperation and border safety was signed yesterday by the interior ministers of the Islamic Republic of Iran and Turkey. On the basis of this protocol the two countries will increase their cooperation on the security of the two countries' borders, on confronting terrorism, and on fighting drug smuggling. During the signing of this protocol, Besharati expressed satisfaction on the outcome of his discussions with Turkish officials and said that full agreement had been reached on all the topics discussed. The Turkish interior minister referred to various Turkish officials' meetings with the Iranian delegation and stated that Besharati's visit to Turkey has solidified the two countries' friendly relations. [Editor's note: In a similar report carried in its 0330 GMT newscast, Tehran Voice of the Islamic Republic of Iran First Program Network in Persian reports: "The Turkish interior minister has stated that no element acting against the Islamic Republic of Iran in Turkey will be allowed to remain on Turkish territory. The Turkish interior minister said that during his meeting with Besharati, he added that Turkish law-enforcement agents confront with sensitivity any act perpetrated against the Islamic Republic of Iran on Turkish territory."]
