930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Saville Gordon down slightly at Pounds 1.3m J SAVILLE Gordon Group, the Birmingham-based property investment and merchanting concern, reported a slight decline in six month pre-tax profits, but maintained the dividend, in its first results since it wound up its securities and commodities trading division. writes Paul Cheeseright. Pre-tax profits for the six months to October 31 were Pounds 1.26m (Pounds 1.35m). Earnings per share amounted to 0.85p against 0.9p and the interim dividend is held for the third year running at 0.5p. Group turnover was sharply lower at Pounds 13.8m (Pounds 28.5m). With rent reviews coming through, property income is set to increase, leaving the pipeline business as the weaker part of the group. Operating profits in the first half were Pounds 545,000 against Pounds 636,000. Mr John Saville, the chairman, observed a recent improvement in demand, however, which should show through more strongly in figures for the next financial year.
