BFN [Unattributed report: "Palestinian Police Commander Tells AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT: I Requested Watch on Official in Charge of Preventive Security in Jericho"] [Excerpt] Tunis, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Major General Nasr Yusuf, commander of the Palestinian police in the self-rule area, has confirmed that he has asked Hajj Isma'il Jabr, police commander in the Jericho area, to watch the actions of Jibril al-Rujub, official in charge of preventive security in the city, and to verify the information received by him about Jibril al-Rujub. However, in a statement to AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT, he denied ordering the arrest of al-Rujub, and pointed out that reports of excesses committed by him are being investigated. [passage omitted]
