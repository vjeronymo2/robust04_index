BFN [Text] Delhi police averted a major tragedy last night by arresting a Pakistan-trained Kashmiri terrorist trying to enter a cinema hall in Connaught Place in New Delhi with a live bomb. The deputy commissioner of police, Mr. Dharmendra Kumar, told newsmen today that the 22-year-old accused, a member of a banned militant outfit, was responsible for the bomb blast in another cinema hall in Connaught Place in January.
