September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

2 KILLED IN MURDER-SUICIDE

A 21-year-old Los Feliz woman was shot to death in her bathtub by a man who then shot and killed himself, Los Angeles police said.

Kellie Jean Bagley was shot once in the head between 7 and 7:30 p.m. Friday by a man who the victim's mother identified only as Antonio, Police Detective Loren Zimmerman said.

Police have not yet determined the man's full name, age or the nature of his relationship with Bagley, Zimmerman said.

Police have no motive for the killings, he said.

Bagley's mother discovered the bodies after she used her key to enter her daughter's apartment in the 1700 block of North Edgemont Street, Zimmerman said. The woman became worried when her daughter failed to telephone her, he said.

There were no signs of forced entry, leading police to believe that Bagley allowed the man inside and locked the door behind him, Zimmerman said.
