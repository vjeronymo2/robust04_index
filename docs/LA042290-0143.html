April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DIVING INDOOR CHAMPIONSHIPS;

BRADSHAW, WILSON WIN TITLES

Mark Bradshaw won the U.S. Indoor men's three-meter diving championship Saturday night, completing a sweep of the 1990 national springboard titles.

Bradshaw, of Springfield, Ore., won the one-meter title Thursday.

Krista Wilson used a strong finish to win the women's three-meter title earlier in the day.

Bradshaw, of the Ohio State Diving Club, was in a tight duel with Pat Evans of Cincinnati headed into the 10th dive. However, Evans faltered on his 10th.
