BFN [Report by Hasan Darwish from Ba'labakk] [Text] The Al-Fajr television station, which belongs to Hizballah, and the Sky television station, which belongs to 'Abdallah al-Husayni, brother of former Speaker Husayn al-Husayni, yesterday violated the Lebanese cabinet decision that bans the broadcast of news bulletins and political programs. At 2045 yesterday, the two stations, which transmit from Ba'labakk, broadcast the full 15-minute recording of the news conference held by Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah.
