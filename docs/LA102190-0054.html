October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FAULTY MILITARY EQUIPMENT IN MIDEAST

Collaboration of the U.S. and Arab allies to isolate Saddam Hussein is fragile and razor thin. Witness our deference to Arab sensibilities and wholehearted condemnation of Israel's impertinence in defending its citizens from attack.

It's not easy to show sufficient gratitude to Persian Gulf Arabs for kindly permitting us to defend them.

HERB ROSENTHAL

Los Angeles
