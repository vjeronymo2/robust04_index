Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign ministers of Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan have arrived in Tajikistan today. A meeting of the foreign ministers of the five CIS states will take place at the Foreign Ministry of Tajikistan tomorrow. Uzbek Foreign Minister Saidmukhtar Saidkasymov, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, Kazakh Foreign Minister Tuleutay Suleymenov, Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Olimov, and Kyrgyz Deputy Prime Minister Alifbek Tefshenkulov will take part at this meeting. The main issues to be discussed at the meeting are the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border and the activity of the peacekeeping forces there. Emomali Rakhmonov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Council of Tajikistan, will meet them tomorrow. Russian Foreign Minister Kozyrev will arrive in Dushanbe, the capital of our country, tomorrow.
