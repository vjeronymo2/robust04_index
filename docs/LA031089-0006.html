March 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

HOLLYWOOD DONNYBROOK

Our Hollywood Chamber of Commerce is wasting money and time. Ninety-nine and forty-four one-hundredths percent of the world think only of California's Hollywood, no matter where they might see the name.

Any other state using the name is simply providing free publicity for what the civilized world regards as the real Hollywood; the only Hollywood.

JOHN DEGATINA

Los Angeles
