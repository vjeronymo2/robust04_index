BFN [Text] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev is leaving for Brussels on June 21-22 to discuss Russia's joining of the Partnership for Peace program, the Russian Foreign Ministry has told Interfax. In Brussels, Kozyrev will meet U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. According to an agreement reached by the Russian and U.S. presidents in their recent telephone conversation, the meeting will focus on practical approaches to the resolution of the North Korea issue and the Bosnian settlement.
