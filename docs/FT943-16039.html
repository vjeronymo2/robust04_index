940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Gardiner Group falls to Pounds 1.93m Pressure on its principal UK operations, Gardiner Security and Multi-Video Distributors, led Gardiner Group to turn in pre-tax profit for the six months to April 30 down from Pounds 2.36m to Pounds 1.93m. Turnover was ahead at Pounds 40.5m (Pounds 40.2m). Interest charges were reduced to Pounds 339,000 (Pounds 380,000) and gearing fell from 95 per cent to 62 per cent. Earnings per share dipped to 1.18p (1.31p), but the interim dividend is raised to 0.25p (0.23p).
