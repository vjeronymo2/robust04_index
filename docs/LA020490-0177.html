February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: OFFICIAL AT MT. SAC CHOSEN FOR CONFERENCE

Elizabeth Cipres, Transfer Center director at Mt. San Antonio College, has been chosen to participate in an international leadership training program for women administrators at two-year colleges.

Cipres, of Diamond Bar, also teaches psychology at Mt. SAC and is active in the Assn. of Hispanic Professionals for Education and other professional groups.

The seminar, March 3 to 9 in Fort Lauderdale, Fla., is sponsored by the League for Innovation in Community Colleges and the American Assn. of Women in Community and Junior Colleges.
