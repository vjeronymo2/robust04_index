Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers has instructed that all retail trade prices in the republic are to be rounded off to multiples of 10 as of 1 January. All trading organizations, whatever their form of ownership, are obliged, as previously, to accept one, three, and five coupon notes, although in future they will be withdrawn from circulation. Official sources say inflation in Ukraine reached 70 percent a month by the end of 1993, and the largest bank note, 100,000 karbovantsy, is worth approximately $3 at the market rate.
