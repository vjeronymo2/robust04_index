Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentina Ivanova] [Text] Cheboksary January 4 TASS -- On Tuesday [4 January], a new election campaign started in Chuvashia, where the president had been recently elected. Candidates to the state council of the republic and to local self-government bodies of all levels, from cities to regions and villages were nominated. Forty-seven deputies to the new parliament, which will replace the supreme soviet of the republic will be elected on March 13. Nineteen deputies will work in the parliament full-time.
