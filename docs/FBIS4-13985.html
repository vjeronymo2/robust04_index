BFN [Text] The Iraqi Speaker Mr. Sa'di Mahdi, met the chairman, Senate, Mr. Wasim Sajjad, in Islamabad today and discussed various issues, including Kashmir. The chairman, Senate, briefed Mr. Sa'di Mahdi Salih on the different aspects of the Kashmir issue and called for its peaceful solution through UN resolutions. Mr. Wasim Sajjad supported resumption of food and medicine supplies to Iraq and said these items should not be subjected to international sanctions. The Iraqi speaker expressed the hope that his current visit to Pakistan would further strengthen cooperation in trade, commerce, and economic sectors.
