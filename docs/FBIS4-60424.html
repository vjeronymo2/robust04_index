BFN [Text] Brother President 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, received a reply message from his brother Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, king of the fraternal Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and custodian of the two holy mosques. The message stated: We stress that the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, stemming from its firm stands and its everlasting fraternal links with the brotherly Palestinian people, will--God willing--remain as ever supportive of the legitimate cause to accomplish the security and just peace that will guarantee its legitimate rights and realize its benevolent objectives in the return to its homeland and the establishment of its independent state with holy Jerusalem as its capital.
