Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- Publishing houses of Korea have recently brought out many new books of science and technology. "Interpretation of Heat Conduction by Midget Computer" published by the science and encyclopedia press group consists of three parts and nine chapters which explain the method of solving equations of heat conduction with programs. The press group also printed "Medicinal Tea and Health and Longevity". The book gives general knowledge of medicinal tea and describes its kinds, making and drinking, and introduces tonic tea, tea for recovering fatigue and teas for treating diseases. The agriculture publishing house brought out "Comprehensive Mechanization of Agriculture", "Artificial Insemination of Domestic Animals and Technology", "Soil Dictionary" and other books conducive to widening the technical knowledge of agricultural officials.
