930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: FBD Holdings turns in 39% advance Pre-tax profits of FBD Holdings, the Dublin-based insurer, advanced by 39 per cent, from IPounds 5.25m to IPounds 7.3m (Pounds 6.9m), in the half year to end-June. Turnover rose from IPounds 45m to IPounds 50.9m. Earnings per 50p share rose 48 per cent to 11.25p (7.58p) and the interim dividend is up from 2p to 2.3p.
