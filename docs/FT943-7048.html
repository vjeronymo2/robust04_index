940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Latin American net asset value falls 10% Latin American Investment Trust saw net asset value fall 10 per cent to Dollars 2.34 (150p) per share at June 30, against Dollars 2.62 at December 31 and Dollars 1.89 a year earlier. The fully diluted figure was Dollars 2.09, compared with Dollars 2.32 and Dollars 1.74 respectively. Attributable revenue dropped from Dollars 804,000 to Dollars 39,000 for the six months, leaving earnings per share at 0.05 cents (1.07 cents).
