BFN [Summary] "This morning, 27 May, the Ninth National Assembly's fifth session continued at the conference hall under the direction of Vice Chairman Phung Van Tuu. After listening to the National Assembly's Secretariat reading a summary of the deputies' suggestions on the labor code draft, the National Assembly deputies contributed their opinions on the first chapter of the draft". The deputies discussed the minimum working age, bonuses, and other entitlements and issues related to laborers. "In the afternoon, after the report by Tran Dinh Hoan, minister of labor, war invalids, and social welfare, the deputies continued to tender their opinions on other chapters of the draft", discussing such issues as the name of the law, the working age, severance pay, retrenchment pay, and so forth. Tomorrow morning the deputies will continue to discuss the labor code draft.
