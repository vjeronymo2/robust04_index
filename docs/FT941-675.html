940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Observer: Chic-by-jowel Cleansing Italy's Augean stables of corruption has not best pleased hoteliers across the border at Switzerland's chic Alpine resort of St Moritz. They have seen Italian tourist figures drop by more than 20 per cent during the winter season. One local tourism official reckons to know why: 'Some of our best customers are in jail.'
