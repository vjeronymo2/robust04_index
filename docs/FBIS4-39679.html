BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov and Sergey Feoktistov] [Text] Istanbul, 9 Jun -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev arrived in Istanbul where he will take part in a session of the North Atlantic Cooperation Council. Sixteen NATO states, including Russia and other countries of the CIS and Eastern Europe comprise the Council. Relations between the North Atlantic alliance with Russia will be one of the most important issues to be discussed in Istanbul.
