September 5, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: KUDOS

Actress Pauline Collins, the star of Paramount's "Shirley Valentine," has received a Discovery Award from the Los Angeles Film Teachers Assn. The award salutes Collins "for her tour-de-force performance in 'Shirley Valentine,' the movie that reminds us how important it is not to lose touch with our dreams," said Alice La Deane, LAFTA president. ALEENE MacMINN
