941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / UK Company News: FT-SE-A Indices The FT-SE Actuaries UK Indices Committee has approved, as part of the annual review, the following changes to the constituents of the FT-SE SmallCap and FT-SE Actuaries All-Share Indices with effect from January 3 1995: Huntleigh Technology will remain a constituent of the FT-SE SmallCap and FT-SE Actuaries All-Share Indices; Siam Selective Growth Trust will become a constituent of the FT-SE SmallCap and FT-SE Actuaries All-Share.
