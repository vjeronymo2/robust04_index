September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

HANAUER SETS TWO SPEED RECORDS IN GOLD CUP TRIALS

Chip Hanauer, who will go for his eighth consecutive APBA Gold Cup victory today, broke the unlimited hydroplane records for one-and two-lap qualifying runs Saturday on the 2.5-mile Mission Bay course.

Hanauer, of Seattle, piloted his Circus Circus turbine-powered boat to the fastest unlimited lap ever, 158.870 m.p.h. That bettered the record of 156.169 set here last year by Tom D'Eath in the Miss Budweiser.

Hanauer's two-lap qualifying mark of 158.297 m.p.h. broke his old record of 149.031 set here in 1987.
