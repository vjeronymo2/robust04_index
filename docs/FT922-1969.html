920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Widney reduces losses to Pounds 97,000 Widney, the Birmingham-based engineering group, continued its trend of improving results with a reduction of pre-tax losses from Pounds 406,000 to Pounds 97,000 in the six months to March 31. Turnover declined slightly from Pounds 10m to Pounds 9.69m and at the operating level profits improved to Pounds 314,000 (Pounds 94,000). At the year ended September 28 1991 operating profits were Pounds 429,000 against losses of Pounds 687,000 and pre-tax losses fell to Pounds 512,000 (Pounds 1.62m). The interest charge for the current six months was reduced to Pounds 411,000 (Pounds 500,000) and there was an extraordinary charge of Pounds 58,000 (nil) related to the costs of an aborted acquisition. Losses per share were reduced to 0.33p (0.89p).
