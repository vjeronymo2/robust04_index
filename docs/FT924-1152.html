921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Increased loss for United Industries Losses at United Industries, a maker of precision springs for aerospace, automotive, electrical and engineering applications, edged ahead from Pounds 609,000 to Pounds 631,000 pre-tax for the six months to October 3. Turnover totalled Pounds 16.14m (Pounds 16.53m). Losses per share emerged at 5.23p (1.93p).
