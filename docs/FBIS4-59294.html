BFN [Text] Tehran, May 11, IRNA -- Indian ambassador to Tehran Ramesh Chandra Arora and Deputy Foreign Minister for Asia-Pacific Affairs 'Ala'eddin Borujerdi here Wednesday exchanged views on Tehran-New Delhi bilateral cooperation and on issues related to Kashmir. Arora expressed satisfaction with the growing trend of bilateral relations and hoped for further boosting of ties. He also expressed hope that Iran's President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani would visit his country. Borujerdi also hoped for removal of tension between Pakistan and India and said that Iran is ready to assist both India and Pakistan in solving their disputes.
