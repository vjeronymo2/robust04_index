940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Reckitt & Colman - Other companies The household products and toiletries group is expected to report on Thursday a marginal increase in interim pre-tax profits to about Pounds 150m (Pounds 144m). Gains on the disposal of its European Colours business will push FRS 3 pre-tax profits to around Pounds 185m. The interim dividend is expected to rise by 8 per cent to 6.95p a share.
