940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Finsbury Growth net asset value improves Finsbury Growth Trust had a net asset value of 125.4p per share at March 31, up from 112.9p at the September year-end and 102.9p at end-March 1993. Mr Michael Reeve, chairman, said the 11 per cent advance in value over the last six month period outperformed both the trust's benchmarks - the FT-SE 100 and the FT-SE-A All-Share - which rose 1.6 per cent and 3.7 per cent respectively. The trust saw net revenue improve to Pounds 504,000 (Pounds 455,000) in the six months to end-March, equivalent to earnings of 1.1p (1p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.9p.
