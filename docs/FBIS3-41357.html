Language: English Article Type:CSO [Article: "Narcotics Seizure"] [Text] Bombay, Dec. 28. The Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) of Bombay Customs seized 2210 packets of mandrax tablets weighing 1435 kg and valued at Rs. 30 crore in the international market from a export consignment at Indira dock in South Bombay this afternoon. According to NCB sources, the consignment which was marked water heaters was to be shipped to Mozambique. Two persons have been detained in this connection.
