941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Linden in Pounds 16m reverse takeover of Edmond Linden, an unquoted housebuilder which operates in Surrey, Sussex and Cheshire is acquiring Edmond Holdings, the housebuilder, in a reverse takeover worth Pounds 16.1m. Edmond, which operates in east Midlands, East Anglia, Yorkshire and Humberside, is issuing 78.8m ordinary shares, representing 60.7 per cent of its enlarged share capital. Mr Andrew Sells, Linden's chairman, will become non-executive chairman of Edmond and Mr Philip Davies, Linden's chief executive, will become chief executive of Edmond. Dealings in Edmond have been suspended and as Linden does not have a three-year trading record, application will be made for Edmond's admission to the USM.
