920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Shipping bears brunt of Ropner's decline Ropner, the engineering, property investment and shipping group, saw mid-term pre-tax profit fall 24 per cent, but said all divisions traded profitably. From turnover up to Pounds 8.26m (Pounds 7.64m), profit for six months ended June 30 came to Pounds 1.72m (Pounds 2.27m). Earnings per share were 4.4p (6.2p) and the interim dividend is again 3.5p. In engineering, profit before interest was Pounds 170,000 (Pounds 177,000), while Property produced Pounds 440,000 (Pounds 450,000). Shipping had a setback in making Pounds 372,000 (Pounds 829,000), including its 28.6 per cent share of the associate's interim figure of Pounds 278,000 (Pounds 1.06m).
