941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Trade News: VIF Airways - Contracts & Ventures VIF Airways, the Indian airline, has placed an order with Deutsche Aerospace for five Dornier 328 turbo prop regional aircraft. Dasa will start deliveries in November 1994. Dasa now has 52 firm orders for Dornier 328s and 51 options from 15 customers worldwide.
