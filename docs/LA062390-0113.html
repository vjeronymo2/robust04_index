June 23, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DECISION DELAYED IN RAPPAPORT CASE

Relatives of the late actor David Rappaport, who committed suicide last month and did not leave a will, must wait until July 20 to find out whether they will be able to wrest control of the entertainer's U.S. estate from Los Angeles County authorities.

A Los Angeles Superior Court probate administrator on Friday put off a decision on the case until Frankie Leigh, Rappaport's manager and cousin, is formally appointed guardian of the actor's son and heir, Joseph.
