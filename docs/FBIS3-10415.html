Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Jose Penagos; from the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] Luis Fernando Correa, regional director of the Technical Investigations Corp of the Medellin Public Prosecutor's Office, was murdered at 1030 today in (Calasasa), Medellin. The investigator was intercepted by several men traveling in a white Renault Nine. Correa was in charge of escorting Nicolas Escobar, son of Roberto Escobar, out of the country; he also personally directed the transfer of Pablo Escobar's family to Santa Fe de Bogota. Correa was going to pick someone up at this residence in western Medellin when two cars intercepted him; one of them was white, a Renault, with no particular characteristics, which is being sought, particularly in western Medellin, by the authorities.
