August 10, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

Denver-based Fund America Investors Corp., which issues bonds and other securities, announced this week that it has no connection. Last month, Fund of America Inc., a Houston-based mutual fund, changed its name to the Growth and Income Fund. . . . But Connecticut-based Fund American Cos., parent of Fireman's Fund Insurance, has done nothing. "We're kind of hoping it would go away," says an exec. David Reyes and Mark I. Pinsky
