Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES 4/1/1994 -- Romania's President Ion Iliescu received on January 4, 1994, a delegation of the North [as received] Korean Daewoo group, led by Kim U-chung, chairman of the administration board. The Daewoo group intends to explore ways for large-scale investments in Romania in the car-making industry, ship-building, refineries, electronics, tourism and the financial-banking sector. President Iliescu saluted the interest of South Korean businessmen in cooperation actions in Romania and told his guest about the potentialities of the Romanian economy, the opportunities created for foreign investors by the Romanian legislation. The Romanian president encouraged the representives of the Daewoo group to have contacts with Romanian businessmen in view of making mutually advantageous transactions.
