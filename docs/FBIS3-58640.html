Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Magdeburg (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl has supported the call by Interior Minister Manfred Kanther for a pay freeze in the public sector. It is impossible to move outside the general development of the economy, the chancellor said at a news conference in Magdeburg today. Kohl declined to give figures for his expectations for the pay and conditions round. Securing jobs has priority, he said.
