Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just heard that alleged UNITA members attacked the city of Malanje this morning, which prevented a plane carrying three US congressmen from landing. The plane turned back to Luanda. This report comes to us from the LUSA news agency [Portuguese]. According to Angolan national radio, the city of Malanje has been under an intense attack by UNITA since the early hours of the morning. A woman was killed in the Ritondo district, 2 km from the center of the city, and 3 km from the airport.
