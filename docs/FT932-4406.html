930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Automotive Prods returns to black Automotive Products, which serves the car and aircraft industries and is owned by the Guthrie Corporation, a wholly owned subsidiary of BBA, returned to profits in 1992 with Pounds 16.2m pre-tax. Previously the deficit was Pounds 1.1m after a Pounds 6.8m loss on sale and termination of businesses. Directors said there were isolated instances of buoyancy but the long-term recovery had not materialised. Turnover moved up to Pounds 283.5m (Pounds 269.2m).
