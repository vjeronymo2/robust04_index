BFN [Report by unidentified special correspondents] [Text] Cartagena de Indias, Colombia -- President Juan Carlos Wasmosy on 16 June expressed his desire that the changes in Cuba should emerge without any bloodshed. He expressed his hope that Fidel Castro, on his own initiative, will begin the modernization of the Cuban state. "I hope that day is close, I hope Fidel Castro will personally begin this modernization, this change that the whole world is expecting in Cuba. Let the change be implemented slowly, without trauma and without bloodshed," Wasmosy said. He indicated that when the changes happen, Paraguay will open its doors to the Cuban people as a whole because the Cuban people are dear to us all. He maintained that everything has been done to try to bring about more freedom, a greater opening in the Caribbean island. He expressed his sadness regarding the blockade that surrounds Cuba. "We have asked for a program of opening for the Cuban people," Wasmosy said.
