BFN [Text] Riga, April 4 (LETA) -- At the end of the last week, on invitation from the Latvian President, Guntis Ulmanis, the American Ambassador to Latvia, Ints Silins, visited his residence. According to the President's chancellery, Guntis Ulmanis and Ints Silins discussed the urgent questions of the state relations between the two countries and the possibilities of this year's meetings between the presidents of the United States and the Baltic States.
