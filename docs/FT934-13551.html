931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / People: Bodies politic - CBI Wales Ian Kelsall, who is to retire at the end of this month after 28 years as director of CBI Wales, is to become part-time director of Merthyr Tydfil Business Centre. The centre's aim is to help to create and support jobs as part of the regeneration of the south Wales town. Kelsall will continue at the CBI on a half-time consultancy basis until his successor has been appointed.
