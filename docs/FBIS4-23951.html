BFN [Excerpt] The Nigerian head of state, General Sani Abacha, has approved the appointment of Justice Adolphus Godwin Karibi-Whyte of the Supreme Court of Nigeria as chairman of the National Constitutional Conference scheduled to begin on the 27th of this month. The chief press secretary to the head of state, Mr. David Attah, said in Abuja that Justice Mohamed Nasir, a retired president of the Federal Court of Appeal, will be the deputy chairman. Mr. Attah explained that the appointments were in accordance with Section Three Subsection Two of the Constitutional Conference Decree recently signed into law by the head of state. Justice Karibi-Whyte is currently serving with the United Nations in Yugoslavia. Mr. Attah also said the head of state has approved the appointment of Dr. Joseph Wayas, a member of the Constitutional Conference Commission, as deputy chairman of the Commission. [passage omitted]
