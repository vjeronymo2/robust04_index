920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Mansell takes drivers' championship Briton Nigel Mansell celebrates his first Formula One drivers' world championship after finishing second in yesterday's Hungarian Grand Prix behind Brazilian Ayrton Senna. Mansell, 39, driving a Williams, recovered from a poor start and what appeared to be a puncture late in the race to become Britain's first champion since James Hunt in 1976.
