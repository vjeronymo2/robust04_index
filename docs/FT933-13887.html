930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / International Company News: Woolworths beats forecast WOOLWORTHS, the Austra-lian retailer which this week went public with a ADollars 2.45bn flotation, announced yesterday it had beaten prospectus sales forecast for the latest year, writes Bruce Jacques. Directors said sales for the 53 weeks ended June 27 rose 14.2 per cent to ADollars 10.49bn (USDollars 13.32bn) compared with a forecast of ADollars 10.43bn.
