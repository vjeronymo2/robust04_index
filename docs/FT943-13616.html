940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / Benn and Shore set for questions probe Mr Tony Benn and Mr Peter Shore, the Labour leftwingers, are set to be included on the committee of MPs charged with investigating the so-called 'cash for questions' affair, it emerged last night. The full list of MPs who will sit on the powerful committee of privileges is expected to become clear today. The committee of senior MPs, picked by arrangement between the parties, will investigate alleged payments made to MPs for tabling parliamentary questions.
