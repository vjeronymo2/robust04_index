920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / International Company News: Magellan seeks to block Sagasco bid MAGELLAN Petroleum Australia, the Australian gas producer, has sought a Supreme Court injunction to halt the unwanted ADollars 122m (USDollars 89.70m) bid from Sagasco Holdings, the quoted oil and gas group controlled by the government of South Australia. Sagasco is itself subject to a ADollars 560m bid from Santos, a petroleum rival.
