BFN [Excerpt] A communique from the security services said that the security forces killed 20 terrorists, and a number of others were arrested last Saturday [26 March] and Sunday. The security services also said that four out of the six terrorists who carried out the assassination of the Russian diplomat and a member of the Foreign Ministry were killed yesterday. The communique from the security services added that the director of the Algerian Red Crescent of Sidi Rached, in Tipaza province, was killed near his home yesterday by two terrorists. Another citizen was also killed in Tiaret. [passage omitted]
