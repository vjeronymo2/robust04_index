Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Telepress" newscast] [Text] Civil patrolmen of Xalbaquiej Canton, Chichicastenango Municipality, El Quiche Department, reportedly have declared that locality under a state of siege [as heard], the Mutual Support Group, GAM, has announced in a communique issued in Guatemala City. The communique states those who are promoting this form of extreme measure are participating in a coordinated plan directed and organized by military intelligence. According to GAM, this coordinated plan, called The Chain [la cadena], encompasses 60 cantones and covers all the areas of the high plateau. GAM has demanded an immediate explanation from local civilian and military authorities as to why civilian patrolmen have declared a state of siege; it has also urged the ombudsman and the attorney general to review the situation.
