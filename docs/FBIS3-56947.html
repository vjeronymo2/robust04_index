Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The DPRK has denied the report that Japan sold siting apparatus for ballistic missiles to the DPRK. The Korean Central News Agency reported that the criticism of the alleged purchase of spectrum analyzers by the DPRK is aimed at slandering [umyong-ul tonjigo] the DPRK and applying outside pressure. Last week, the Japanese authorities began to investigate a company that is suspected of selling this apparatus to the DPRK through China. The mentioned spectrum analyzers are used in televisual [telebijon] communication.
