BFN [Text] The head of the Army's Central Command, Major General Ilan Biran, has barred for 24 hours the entry of Israelis and Arabs from the territories to the Jericho area in order to allow the Palestinian police to organize themselves and to prevent misunderstandings. This was announced by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman, who said the order will be in effect from 5 PM this evening to 5 PM [both 1400 GMT] tomorrow.
