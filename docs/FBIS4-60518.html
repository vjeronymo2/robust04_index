BFN [Excerpts] This is a communique issued by the security services: Security services said on 25 May that they were able to locate the headquarters of five terrorists and liquidate them during a search operation at about 0700 yesterday in the Harragua region, Ben Zergua municipality. [passage omitted] During the operation, six Kalashnikov guns, a Beretta machine gun, and a shotgun were recovered. The guns were all stolen during attacks against members of the security forces. Four members of the security forces were also wounded during the search operation. The same sources said that the terrorist Hattab Abdelkader, called Mouloud, a cousin of the Hattab brothers, is still wanted by the security forces.
