921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Embassy cuts loss to Pounds 1.5m Pre-tax losses at Embassy Property, the USM-quoted property development, housebuilding and construction group, were reduced from Pounds 2.4m to Pounds 1.45m in the six months to September 30. The improvement was achieved on turnover which declined from Pounds 6.3m to Pounds 5.9m. Losses per share worked through at 9.9p (26.1p). However, as a result of the financial restructuring in September, the group is showing a retained profit of Pounds 4m, against a loss of Pounds 2m in 1991.
