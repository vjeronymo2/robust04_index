930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / Finance & The Family: Smaller companies still rally - At a Glance Smaller company shares resumed their rally this week. The County Small Companies Index rose 1.2 per cent, from 973.25 to 985.11, over the seven days to January 21; the Hoare Govett index (capital gains version) increased 0.6 per cent from 1251.67 to 1258.27 over the six days to January 20.
