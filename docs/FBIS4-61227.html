BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Russian Television Network in Russian at 1920 GMT on 4 May carries a video report by L. Shesterkina on the atomic weapons museum at (Snezhensk), formerly known as Chelyabinsk 70. The museum shows "the most powerful air-delivered hydrogen bomb in the world" which is, Shesterkina stresses, the original weapon, and not a model. Parts of decommissioned ballistic missiles and explosive devices for civilian purposes are also shown.
