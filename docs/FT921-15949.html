920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Employment issues to the fore - In a Nutshell Employment issues were the most frequent reason for calls to the legal helpline run by the Federation of Small Businesses (FSB). They accounted for 21 per cent of calls made to the federation in the year ended September and were followed by questions concerning contracts, which made up 19 per cent. The total number of calls rose by 30 per cent to 22,150. Litigation issues were the third most common question raised, accounting for nearly 8 per cent of calls. FSB Tel. 071 928 9272.
