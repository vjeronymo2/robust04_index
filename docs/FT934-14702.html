931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / Chalfont attacks Radio 5 shake-up LORD Chalfont, chairman of the Radio Authority, last night attacked the BBC for its plan to replace Radio 5 with a news and sports radio network - pre-empting next year's new national commercial channel, Raymond Snoddy writes. 'How can we, in the independent sector, be expected to make rational plans for broadening listener choice in the future?' he asked. The authority is the watchdog for independent radio.
