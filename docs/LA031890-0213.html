March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WEEK'S FILM PICKS BY THE CRITICS: MIDNIGHT RUN

Robert De Niro, as a hard-case bounty hunter, and Charles Grodin, as a soft-shelled embezzler, are the stars of this often murderously funny 1988 chase comedy, a kind of crime movie Odd Couple, a Laurel and Hardy on the run.
