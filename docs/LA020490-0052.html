February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME IMPROVEMENT;

CONVERTING 2 BEDROOMS INTO MASTER SUITE PAYS

Converting two existing bedrooms into a master suite can make an existing home competitive with new construction, according to Remodeling Magazine.

The expected pay back from such a remodeling job averages 79% nationwide.

Experts advise undertaking the project only if the house will still have at least three bedrooms.

An additional bathroom considerably increases the marketability of a home with one or 1 1/2 baths, according to Remodeling Magazine.

Use high-quality materials that are easy to clean. Use lots of mirrors and include ample storage. Skylights brighten interior bathrooms and help to make a small room look larger.
