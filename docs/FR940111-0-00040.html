EFFECTIVE DATE: 0701 UTC, March 9, 1994. 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 91&hyph;ANM&hyph;14] Alteration of VOR Federal Airway V&hyph;220 AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Final rule; correction.

SUMMARY: This document contains corrections to the final rule published on September 10, 1993. This final rule alters the description of VOR Federal Airway V&hyph;220, located in Colorado and Nebraska, to coincide with the scheduled opening date of the new Denver International Airport. This action is necessary to incorporate a recent amendment to V&hyph;220 between Grand Junction, CO, and Meeker, CO, which became effective on November 11, 1993.
