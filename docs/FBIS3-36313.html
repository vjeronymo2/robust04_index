Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation in Abkhazia's Galskiy rayon remains tense. Vladimir Mikanba, deputy head of the Abkhazian Council of Ministers, told ITAR-TASS that armed groups of Georgian troops are still crossing the border along the Inguri river. These formations have recently stepped up their activities. The Abkhazian leadership has information that the Georgian troops are preparing for an offensive. This matter will be reflected in a statement by the republican government which is being prepared today.
