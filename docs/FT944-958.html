941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Gold shares rallied on gains in the bullion price. The overall index was up 56.1 to 5,803.2, the industrial index moved ahead 34.5 to 6,917.9 and the golds index jumped 70.7 or 3.7 per cent to 1,971.5, having earlier touched 1,983. Vaal Reefs climbed R12 to R358 and Kloof R2 to R58.50. De Beers collected R1 at R94 and Anglos put on R1.50 at R236.50.
