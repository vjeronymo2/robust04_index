BFN [Text] "Senator Mancino has not accepted the clear verdict of the sovereign people, and he has not lost the habit of dreaming up new governmental alliances which, unlike those of the government of Silvio Berlusconi, belong to the partyocracy of the First Republic." This was how Gianfranco Fini chose to comment on the veto made against Berlusconi by Mancino and by the Italian Popular Party [PPI], which had asked the prime minister to "modify the political picture" by dropping the National Alliance and forming a new ruling alliance with "the Democratic Alliance and the PPI."
