January 7, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DELIVERING THE NEW YEAR BABY

To use such a precious gift as birth and turn it into a trivial contest of who's first leaves me to question the ethics of not only Dr. Turner Jr., but of the parents of newborn Myra Palmer.

Dr. Turner stated "I knew God had a hand in it." If God had a hand in it he would have used it to slap Turner's face and bring him to his senses!

JOEY R. KRYS

Orange Parks Acres
