Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Siberians Know How To Overcome the Crisis"] [Text] In an appeal to the president, the prime minister, and the chairmen of the Federation Council and the State Duma approximately 60 Novosibirsk Oblast enterprise directors have proposed taking urgent measures to overcome the crisis. The corps of directors proposes, in particular, altering the system of taxation of enterprises by limiting the overall amount of direct and indirect taxes to 35-40 percent of the proceeds, and revising the government decree on revaluing fixed capital and property with regard to enterprises not in state ownership.
