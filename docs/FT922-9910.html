920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / BMW job cuts BMW, the German carmaker, is cutting the workforce of its UK sales and marketing subsidiary after a 39 per cent decline in BMW (GB)'s trading profit last year. Mr Tom Purves, BMW (GB) managing director, said that the company's trading profit had dropped to Pounds 25m last year from Pounds 41.1m in 1990 and a peak of Pounds 59.7m in 1989. Since the start of the recession the BMW (GB) workforce has been reduced by 18 per cent from 450 at the end of 1990 to 370 following a second round of 29 job cuts this week.
