920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Medellin blast kills 9 NINE people were killed yesterday when a 25kg bomb hidden in a milk churn exploded outside a house in Medelln, base of a powerful cocaine cartel, police said, Reuter reports from Medelln. The bomb exploded in the working-class suburb of Belalcazar in Colombia's second-largest city, killing three women, two men and four children, police said. At least seven people were injured and five houses and 14 vehicles were badly damaged. It was the most serious attack in Medelln in 18 months and the second bombing there since Pablo Escobar, the cocaine cartel chief, escaped from jail on July 22.
