940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Scholes 63% ahead to Pounds 7.02m Scholes Group, a supplier of electrical installation equipment, reported a 63 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.31m to Pounds 7.02m for the year to June 30. Turnover grew from Pounds 62.7m to Pounds 66m. In the home market, volumes were slightly below last year, while exports were buoyant, with turnover up 17 per cent. Operating profits rose 50 per cent from Pounds 4.8m to Pounds 7.2m. Earnings emerged at 12.3p (7.7p). There being no final dividend, the 1.7p (1.6p) interim is the sole distribution for the year. Last year's total was 5p.
