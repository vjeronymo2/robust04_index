BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 27 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, on the day of the victory of the Islamic revolution in Afghanistan. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the president and people of Afghanistan success in their work for national reconciliation and stability of the country and expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop.
