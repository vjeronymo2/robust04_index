Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lagos, 9 Feb. (PANA) -- Nigeria's military government has asked its ambassador in Gabon to investigate the reported death of 23 Nigerians in Gabonese cells, according to media reports in Lagos on Wednesday. The Nigerians were among 64 West African illegal immigrants, including 37 Ghanaians, two Togolese and two Beninois, who died in a cell in the Gabonese capital, Libreville. Gabon's Bar Association has called for an independent commission of inquiry and post-mortem examinations to determine the causes of death.
