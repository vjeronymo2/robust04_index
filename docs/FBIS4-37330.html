BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jun (MENA)--'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, arrived in Cairo this evening on a visit to Egypt. In an arrival statement, the Yemeni official expressed his pleasure at visiting Cairo, adding that he is carrying a message for President Husni Mubarak from Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council. The message deals with the current situation in Yemen and relations between the two fraternal countries. The Yemeni official was received upon arrival by Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif, Assistant Foreign Minister Badr Hammam, and members of the Yemeni Embassy in Cairo.
