Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Approximately 80 percent of the members of the agroindustrial production, credit, and services cooperatives in Matanzas Province belong to the 174 peasant surveillance detachments in that province. More than 9,200 members of the National Association of Small Farmers [ANAP] participate in the surveillance of livestock and cultivated areas, and 1,250 assist the police as volunteers. Despite all this, crime rates in rural areas are still high. In the second half of 1993, 330 crimes were reported, of which only 50 percent were solved.
