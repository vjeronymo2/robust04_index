Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] After the savage massacre of worshippers at the shrine of his holiness Abraham, our country's Foreign Ministry, while initiating extensive political moves at the international level to confront that horrific act, in a message to the Secretary General of the ICO, has called for an urgent meeting of the foreign ministers of the member states of that organization. The foreign minister, Mr. Velayati, in an interview with our correspondent, while declaring Iran's readiness to give any assistance to the innocent people of Palestine and for serious action against the Zionists, said: Iran's delegation at the United Nations has called on the Security Council to take measures to end the Zionist atrocity in occupied Palestine. Mr. Velayati added: Despite the Zionists' act, Palestinian Muslims will participate in the arenas of confrontation against the occupiers and that action [massacre] is an answer for those naive people who have pinned their hopes on a peace resulting from the Gaza-Jericho agreement. Our country's foreign minister also held a telephone conversation with his Pakistani counterpart and conferred with him on the need to convene an ICO meeting.
