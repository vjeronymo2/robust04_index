July 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BRANDON'S BOAST

So Brandon Tartikoff is proud of the fact that more people watched "The Trial of the Incredible Hulk" than "War and Remembrance" ("Tartikoff: Thriving in an Unsurvivable Job" by Diane Haithman, July 2)?

It proves he doesn't care what schlock he shoves down the throats of the American public so NBC can remain No. 1 in the ratings.

Talk about Sammy Glick!

JACK KAYE

Los Angeles
