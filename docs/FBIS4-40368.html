BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow June 15 TASS--The acting prosecutor general Alexey Ilyushenko has lodged an appeal with the State Duma, asking to deprive the LDP [Liberal Democratic Party] leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy of his parliamentary immunity. The request was made in connection with the fact that the Russian prosecutor's general office believes that Zhirinovskiy's book entitled "The Last Breakthrough to the South" gives grounds for instituting criminal proceedings against Zhirinovskiy. This was announced by the chairman of the State Duma, Ivan Rybkin, at its session today. The Duma Council is expected to look into the appeal on Thursday.
