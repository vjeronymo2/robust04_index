September 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CHUCK CLUSTKA FOLLOWED WOODEN'S PHILOSOPHY

One of the first of the many John Wooden "boys," Chuck Clustka, passed away Aug. 20, 1989, in Orange County.

Chuck adopted and lived by Coach Wooden's philosophy of life, as a devoted family man and Christian, coach, teacher, educator and inspired fund-raiser for many humanitarian programs and projects in this area.

The entire Southern California community is a better place in which to live because of the influence of Chuck Clustka's life.

He was a true sportsman.

FRANK FRERIKS, Monterey Park
