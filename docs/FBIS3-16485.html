Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] As UKRINFORM's correspondent in Sevastopol, Crimea, reported on Wednesday [16 March] the Ukrainian Navy's press center refuted a report by the Black Sea Fleet's press center about an alleged seizure by the Ukrainian naval personnel of a naval hospital in Ochakov. As the Ukrainian statement says, the hospital's personnel took an oath of allegiance to Ukraine and appealed to the Ukrainian Defense Ministry and Navy command to enroll them as the Ukrainian Navy servicemen. Since then, the hospital has been strictly guarded, as when a similar situation evolved once in Nikolayev. The Black Sea Fleet command attempted to take away medical equipment and drugs from the local naval hospital, the statement explains.
