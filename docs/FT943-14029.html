940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Investment Company net assets increase Net assets per share of the Investment Company grew by 13 per cent from 44.8p to 50.8p in the year to March 31 1994. After-tax revenue advanced from Pounds 808,338 to Pounds 868,241 and earnings per share came to 3.13p (2.91p). The final dividend is again 1p for an unchanged total of 1.5p.
