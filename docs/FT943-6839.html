940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 4.78m non-core sales by UK Safety UK Safety, the footwear manufacturer, has raised a further Pounds 4.78m from the sale of non-core assets acquired when it reversed into TSW-Television South West, the former independent television con-tractor. It has sold its remaining holding in Societe Europeennes des Satellites, the operator of the Astra satellites, for Pounds 4.08m, a surplus over book value of Pounds 2.3m, making a total profit on the whole disposal of Pounds 3m. It is also selling the studios and former offices of TSW in Plymouth for Pounds 700,000, producing a loss of Pounds 550,000 against book value.
