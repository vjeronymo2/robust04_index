BFN ["epd"-signed report: "Bonn Providing DM252 Million for China"] [Text] Bonn -- The Federal Government will provide the People's Republic of China with 252 million German marks [DM] in development aid this year. Development cooperation will continue to concentrate on the fight against poverty, protection of the environment and resources, and political education, the Ministry for Economic Cooperation said. Furthermore, as a result of this year's government talks in Beijing the Federal Government will subsidize China's economic reforms, as well as rural telecommunications and energy supply, which have been impairing economic development. It has been reported that in these talks the German delegation also pointed out the importance of individual human rights. Of the DM252 million, DM168 million will come in the form of low-interest loans, and DM84 million as an outright grant.
