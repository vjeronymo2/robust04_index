BFN [Text] It is necessary to create proper conditions for working people. As far as social protection is concerned, it should be provided to pensioners, children, invalids, and other sections of the population that need it. Presidential candidate, Ukraine's Minister of Education Petro Talanchuk was saying this while in Zhitomir. He believes that it is necessary to carry out a wise policy of taxation and crediting, using all possible means so that people be paid properly for the work they do. Talanchuk pointed out that, if elected to the high post, he would, first and foremost, carry out transformations in the agro-industrial complex. According to the candidate, the countryside would provide Ukraine with ample quantities of food in two years time.
