930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Wilshaw A full year's contribution from its Belgian distribution subsidiary helped Wilshaw lift pre-tax profits by 59 per cent. For the year ended March 31 it produced Pounds 2.13m (Pounds 1.34m) from turnover ahead 34 per cent at Pounds 31.7m (Pounds 23.8m). Earnings per share came to 1.59p (1.07p) and the final dividend is 0.35p to make 0.5p (0.4p).
