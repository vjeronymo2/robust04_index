930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables New season apples are this week's best fruit buy, reports the FFVIB. English Cox's Orange Pippins are 35-55p a lb and Worcester pearmains are 25-50p a lb. French apples are also in abundance, with Golden Delicious at 30-40p a lb. English, Dutch and Belgian Conference pears are another good buy at 25-50p a lb, and Italian Williams pears are 30-55p a lb. This week's best vegetable buy is broccoli. UK and Dutch broccoli is terrific value at 39-80p a lb. English and Dutch leeks are 40-65p a lb. Lettuces are plentiful and are this weeks best salad buy, round lettuces are 20-30p and Iceberg lettuces are 35-60p.
