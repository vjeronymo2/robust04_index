May 21, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

IN COURT

KTTV Sued Over Incorrect Lotto Results: Two negligence suits were filed in Los Angeles Superior Court on Wednesday against KTTV after the TV station broadcast incorrect Lotto results last May.

Both cases claim that the plaintiffs, Charles Ware and Ricardo and Roda Teodoro, suffered bodily injuries and mental distress after they erroneously thought they had won the lottery. (Filed May 16, 1990. Cases No. BC001340, BC001292)
