Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, February 28 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- A meeting of Korean educationists and scientists in Japan took place in Osaka on February 26 to express thanks to the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il for the 126th instalment of educational aid fund and stipends. It was attended by O Hyong-chin, vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean residents in Japan (Chongnyon) and concurrently chairman of the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon, and educational workers and scientists of Chongnyon. In his report, Pak Kwang-taek, chairman of the Union of Korean Teachers in Japan, said the entire educational workers, bearing deep in mind the solicitude repeatedly shown by the great leader and the dear leader, should more successfully promote national education with the meaningful 50th anniversary of the August 15 national liberation, 40th anniversary of the formation of Chongnyon and 17th congress of Chongnyon one year ahead. A letter to Generalissimo Kim Il-song was adopted at the meeting.
