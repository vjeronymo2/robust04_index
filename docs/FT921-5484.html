920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / Letter: Next stop, Spaghetti Junction Sir, Do not knock the railway sandwich, even on the authority of Trollope ('The Golden Age of rail that never was', February 29/March 1). In my youth in New Zealand, the most important feature of the 15-hour limited night express between Auckland and Wellington was the cold, tinned spaghetti sandwich available (only) at Frankton Junction around 10pm and Taumaranui around 1am. Scrumptious] Michael Lake, Kuleili Sokak 15, Gazi Osman Pasa, Ankara, Turkey
