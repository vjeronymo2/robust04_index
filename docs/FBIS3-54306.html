Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Al-'Aqabah, (PETRA)--Commenting on reports by Israeli radio and television yesterday that he received the wife of Elat's mayor and an invitation to visit the city of Elat, al-'Aqabah Mayor Ibrahim Abu-al-'Izz explained that he was told that the Belgian consul in Switzerland would like to visit him, along with his private secretary. Abu-al-'Izz added that upon receiving the two guests in his private office at the municipality, he was surprised to discover that the woman with the Belgian consul was the wife of Elat's mayor who holds a Swiss passport. For her part, she presented a letter from the mayor of Elat and an invitation to visit Elat. Mayor Abu-al-'Izz rejected the letter and the invitation and immediately informed the official quarters. He stressed that he will never send a letter or an invitation to the mayor of Elat.
