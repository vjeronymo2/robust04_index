920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / Perth glass maker bought by Fullers THE Perth-based glass manufacturing business of John Moncrieff has been bought out of receivership by Fullers Investments. John Moncrieff, which employs 41 people making engineering and scientific glassware and high-performance lenses, had been in receivership for 14 weeks. The business will be carried on under the name of Monax Glass.
