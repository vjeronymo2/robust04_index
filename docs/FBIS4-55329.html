BFN [Text] We learned today about yet another chetnik crime in the UN Protected Area [UNPA]. Four persons wearing camouflage uniforms stopped eight civilians who were travelling on tractors in Batinska Rijeka near Daruvar. Having taken them prisoner and tied them up, they then took them to a deserted farm where five of them were killed. Three managed to escape. One young man sustained no injuries. [sentence as heard] Investigating judges from the Bjelovar county court, employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs police and of the Bjelovar police have begun an investigation. Interior Minister Ivan Jarnjak and his aides visited the scene of the crime.
