940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Regalian Props Regalian Properties, the development and investment company, cut its debt to Pounds 6m, for gearing of 42 per cent with the sale of three properties in Kensington Palace Gardens, London. The price was not disclosed. At March 1993 debt stood at Pounds 65m with Pounds 135m a year before. Regalian also announced it had replaced its consortium of six banks, led by Barclays, with facilities from Barclays alone.
