Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Paris--AKHIR KHABAR has learned from a high-level Tunisian source that the Israeli authorities are pressing for the extradition to Israel of Israeli agent 'Adnan Yasin, a Palestinian national. The source added that Israel might use him as a bargaining chip in the ongoing PLO-Israeli negotiations. The source went on to say: The Tunisian authorities, which interrogated this agent after unearthing his espionage activities, do not intend to extradite him to Israel. Any decision in this regard is bound to be influenced by the Palestinian leadership's calculations regarding this issue, the source noted.
