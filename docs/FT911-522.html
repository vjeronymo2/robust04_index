910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / The Lex Column: Gaming The excitements at Granada should not obscure another aspect of yesterday's announcements: Bass's relentless pursuit of market leadership in all its businesses. Yesterday it bought its way to the top spot in a bingo duopoly, and abandoned a trailing position in bookmaking. The decision to sell the Coral Racing betting shops leaves open the possibility of a duopoly there, too, if some outsider picks them up and winkles the William Hill chain away from Brent Walker, where they are none-too-securely perched. The mass-market gaming business is starting to look enjoyably cosy - from the participants' side of the fence, at least.
