BFN [Text] The composition of the executive bureau of the National Economic and Social Council was announced at the Palace of Nations a few moments ago. The council is composed of the following: Mohamed Laid Anane, Abdelmadjid Sidi Said, Mustapha Mekideche, Mohamed Hamida, Abdeslam Bouchareb, Samia Belabbes, Hocine Ait Chaalal, Mehrez Ait Belkacem, and Mohamed Zaaf. The council is under the obligation to be fully prepared and devoted to the job. The members of the council were chosen after long consultations.
