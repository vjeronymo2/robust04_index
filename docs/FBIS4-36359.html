BFN [Text] Paris -- U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher has said Syria seriously wants to push forward the peace talks. In an interview he gave in Paris to the U.S. CBS Network, Christopher noted that he will soon tour some Middle Eastern states, but he declined to set any date. Yesterday, the U.S. State Department hinted that Christopher may tour some Middle Eastern states after attending a meeting of NATO foreign ministers in Istanbul next Thursday and Friday.
