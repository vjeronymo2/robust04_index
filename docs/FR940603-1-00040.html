Authority: 7 U.S.C. 901 et seq ., 1921 et seq . Subpart A_General §§1726.1&hyph;1726.9 [Reserved] §1726.10 Introduction. The policies, procedures and requirements included in this part are intended to implement provisions of the standard form of loan documents between the Rural Electrification Administration (REA) and its electric borrowers. Unless prior written approval is received from REA, borrowers are required to comply with REA's policies and procedures as a condition to REA providing financial assistance for the construction and improvement of electric facilities. Requirements for REA approval of plans and specifications are contained in part 1724 of this chapter. §1726.11 Purpose.
