Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 6 Mar (INA)--Vice President Taha Yasin Ramadan has stressed that Iraqis from all faiths stood united to defend Iraq in the glorious Saddam al-Qadisiyah battle and the immortal Mother of Battles epic and offered sacrifices and martyrs on the path of victory. During a meeting with a delegation of Eastern Catholic patriarchs, Ramadan said that the continued blockade against Iraq and the starving of its people is considered an aggression against humanity because it is immoral and illegal. He said that the world public is now aware of the methods of falsehood and allegations used by the United States and its allies to realize their evil intentions. Taha Yasin Ramadan stressed that the methods of injustice and aggression will not continue and that the principles of justice, right, and humanity will triumph. He stressed that, through their reconstruction drive and determination, the Iraqis have set an example of steadfastness and determination in their defense of their sovereignty and existence. He praised the visit of the Eastern Catholic patriarchs, which they made under the circumstances of the unjust blockade. [passage omitted]
