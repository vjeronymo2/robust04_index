920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / Report on Leeds business property THE BUSINESS property market in Leeds is bucking the recession, with only 2 per cent of new offices empty, according to a survey by Leeds City Council. Although 850,000 sq ft - 10 per cent of the total office space - is unoccupied, 84 per cent is old or refurbished property. About 400,000 sq ft of new space was completed during 1990-91 and 410,000 sq ft taken, which included some new offices unoccupied from the previous year.
