June 30, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

'BATMAN' WHO TOOK ON RAP

With regard to Thompson's "Bruce and Luther can go to hell" comment (in Philips' "Boss Apparently OKs Crew's Use of 'U.S.A.,' " June 26): Let's see, on one hand we have Bruce Springsteen and Luther Campbell in hell, and on the other hand we presumably have Jack Thompson ascending to heaven. Never has hell been made to sound so attractive.

JEFF BERKE

Los Angeles
