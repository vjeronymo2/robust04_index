DATES: Comments should be received by December 21, 1994. [UT&hyph;942&hyph;4210&hyph;06; U&hyph;012576 et. al.] Proposed Continuation of Withdrawals; Utah AGENCY: Bureau of Land Management, Interior ACTION: Notice

SUMMARY: The Forest Service proposes that several land withdrawals embracing approximately 957.4 acres be continued. The lands will remain closed to the mining laws, and in some instances to surface entry, but have been and will remain open to mineral leasing.
