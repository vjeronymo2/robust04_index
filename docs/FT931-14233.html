930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / International Company News: Rong Riren to retire as Citic chairman MR RONG YIREN, chairman of China's state-owned China International Trust and Investment Corp (Citic), is to retire, according to a senior official of the listed Hong Kong unit, Citic Pacific, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. Mr Rong, known as China's 'red capitalist', has built Citic into an internationally recognised conglomerate with operations and investments throughout China and in other countries. Rumours have long circulated in Beijing that he would retire to take up a senior government position.
