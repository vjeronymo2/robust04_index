BFN [Text] Dominique Baudis is the candidate of the French Democratic Union [UDF] to lead the majority's European election list next June. Two men, Dominique Baudis and Jean-Francois Deniau, have been competing for the position. It is the mayor of Toulouse who has been chosen by the UDF to lead the majority's list. The Rally for the Republic [RPR] will undoubtedly agree. The RPR and UDF recently agreed to present a single list for the European elections. This single list will probably be led by Dominique Baudis.
