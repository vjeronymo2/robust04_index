Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, 22 Jan (TANJUG) -- We welcome the decision of the Duma, the lower House of the Russian parliament, which is demanding that Boris Yeltsin lodge an official request with the UN Security Council that the sanctions against the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia be lifted, said Momcilo Krajisnik, speaker of the assembly of the Srpska republic, to TANJUG correspondent Mladen Krsmanovic today. "This will have far-reaching consequences, because it is clear now that it is the Muslim side which does not want peace. The formal justification for the sanctions was the war in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Thus, the sanctions can no longer remain in place, because the Serb side has demonstrated the maximum degree of cooperation and its wish to achieve peace," Krajisnik stressed. [passage omitted]
