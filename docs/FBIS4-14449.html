BFN [Text] On Wednesday the Presidium of the Russian Supreme Court accepted the protest of the Prosecutor General against the closure of the criminal case of members of the State Emergency Committee in August 1991 Gennadiy Yanayev and Aleksandr Tizyakov in connection with the amnesty. Earlier at the trial of leaders of the abortive coup their cases were separated from the rest and united. The Presidium believes the case was closed in violation of the law and should be sent to court again.
