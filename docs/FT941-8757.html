940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Non-stop TV on British Airways British Airways is to launch a non-stop television channel for all passengers on long-haul flights from next month. The single channel available to economy passengers will include light drama, documentaries, comedy, music, films and news. Club and first class passengers will have eight channels to choose from. In addition first class passengers will have a choice of 50 videotapes. The airline hopes to sell more advertising aimed at its 'annual audience of 8m captive viewers and listeners.
