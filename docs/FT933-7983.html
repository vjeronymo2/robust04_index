930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Royal Bank extends offer for Adam The Pounds 10.5m offer by the Royal Bank of Scotland for Adam & Company, the Edinburgh-based private bank, has been accepted in respect of 21,341, or 60.16 per cent, of the founder shares. There were acceptances in respect of 2,200 originator shares (77.19 per cent) and 5.02m ordinary shares (75.26 per cent). The offer has been extended until August 27.
