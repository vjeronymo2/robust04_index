May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

Why has Fox moved "Booker," one of its best shows, to the 10 p.m. time slot on Sundays? Doesn't it realize most of the show's fans have to get up and go to school Monday mornings? Fox, please ax "The Outsiders" and move "Booker" back to 7 p.m. "The Outsiders" is a lame excuse for a TV show anyway.

Michelle Parkinson, Anaheim
