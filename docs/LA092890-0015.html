September 28, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

USC;

SMITH HAD RIGHT NAME FOR MATRIMONY

Now it can be told:

The former Cheryl Neuenschwander, who has been married to Trojan Coach Larry Smith for almost 25 years, told Nancy Mazmanian of USC's sports information office: "I kid Larry that I went around looking for a Smith or a Jones, and the first Smith that I liked, I married -- anything to get rid of that awful last name." JERRY CROWE
