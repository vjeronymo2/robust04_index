Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 20 Feb -- A political source described the visit of the U.S. ambassador to the Sudan, Donald Nerson [name as received] to some areas in the south of the Sudan without permission from the Sudanese Government as the violation of the national sovereignty of the country and that the U.S. ambassador in Khartoum misused of his responsibility and we do not keep silence on this regard [sentence as received]. The source said in a statement to SUNA that this act came in the context of the open alignment to the rebel movement by the United States. The source added that the U.S. ambassador act came in the framework of securing the arrival of the war equipments to the rebel movement saying that it is a desperate attempt to raise the spiritual moral of the rebel forces.
