931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / Ciskei ruler cleared Brigadier Oupa Gqozo, ruler of South Africa's Ciskei black homeland, was found not guilty in the homeland's Supreme Court yesterday of murdering a political opponent, Reuter reports from Bisho. The case followed the January 1991 death of Mr Charles Sebe, former security chief, who had allegedly plotted a coup.
