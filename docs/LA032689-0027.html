March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CONTINENTAL DIVIDED

Radicchio and quiche may come and go but Continental is here to stay.

Cyril's of Los Angeles, 740 S. La Brea, Los Angeles. (213) 552-9745. Style: Enormous feasting of Continental Southern. Setting: Liberace visits the Cotton Club. Recommended dishes: Meat loaf, pan-fried catfish. Cost per person: $15-$25.

Europa, 14929 Magnolia, Sherman Oaks. (818) 501-9175. Style: Heavy for the hungry. Setting: Plants, posters. Recommended dishes: Osso buco on pasta; roast pork loin stuffed with sausage. Cost per person: $10-$20.

La Ve Lee, 12514 Ventura Blvd., Studio City. (818) 980-8158. Style: Continental dinner house with a Middle Eastern accent. Setting: Dim and cozy. Recommended dishes: Appetizer combo plate; shawarma. Cost per person: $15-$30.

Ravel, Sheraton Grande Hotel, 333 S. Figueroa, Los Angeles. (213) 617-1133. Style: Continental mixed with cooking-school creative. Setting: Pluuussssh. Recommended dishes: Seared duck breast with mixed fruits; pepper steak with morels and crayfish. Cost per person: $30-$45.

The Tower, Transamerica Tower, 1150 S. Olive St., Los Angeles. (213) 746-1554. Style: Rich, tender and uninvolving food with a view. Setting: Lots of windows. Recommended dishes: Capon Madagascar; steak tartare; salmon in filo. Cost per person: $30-$50. Kathie Jenkins
