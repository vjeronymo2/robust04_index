February 14, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

2 'STAR WARS' SATELLITES IN ORBIT

Two "Star Wars" satellites rocketed into space today to test a plan to destroy enemy missiles by bouncing deadly laser beams off orbiting mirrors.

A mirror on one of the satellites was designed to reflect laser beams fired from a mountaintop in Hawaii back to a ground target to assess the accuracy of a system for intercepting ballistic missiles.

Sensors on the second payload were to measure laser strikes in a test aimed at correcting laser distortions caused by dust, moisture and gases as the beam shoots through the atmosphere.
