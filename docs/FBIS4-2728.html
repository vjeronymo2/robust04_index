BFN [Text] Harbin city government decided to set a ceiling price for the marketing of grain and oil for a period of time. The ceiling prices will be effective today. The specific standards for the ceiling prices are: The ceiling price for every five gram of first-grade soybean oil is 3.7 yuan; that of first-grade rice, 0.84 yuan; and that of all sorts of flour, 0.79 yuan. Harbin city government has called all state-owned, collective, and individual grain shops, and all enterprises doing business at the country trade fairs to resolutely implement the ceiling prices set for the aforementioned product varieties, put price tags on the products, and sell these products in line with the price tags. Those units that refuse to implement the ceiling prices, abritrarily raise the prices, and sell these products above the ceiling prices will be strictly handled in case they are discoverd.
