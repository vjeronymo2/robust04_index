BFN [Text] Major General Vladimir Osadchiy, a key figure in the Main Military Budget and Financing Directorate of Russia's Defence Ministry, said that the social aspect has become decisive in the reform of Russia's armed forces. Osadchiy believes that if the military budget is implemented in its present form then half of all servicemen will not be able to receive their pay within the term set by law. Two to three months without pay would lead inevitably lead to a worsening of the already difficult situation among the troops.
