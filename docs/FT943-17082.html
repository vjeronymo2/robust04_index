940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / US trade representative postpones Japan decision In Washington, Mr Mickey Kantor, the US trade representative, announced that he was postponing for a month a decision on whether to retaliate against Japan for discriminating against foreign suppliers in government procurement of telecommunications and medical equipment. He said the delay, until no later than July 31, was appropriate because intensive negotiations were still in progress with Japanese officials and because of the change in the Japanese government.
