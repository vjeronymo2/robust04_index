Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] His Excellency President Daniel arap Moi today said Kenya can tremendously benefit from the advanced technology and farming methods practiced in Israel to forge ahead in agriculture. The president particularly noted that Israel had a lot to offer to Kenya in terms of irrigation to convert arid land into rich farmland. President Moi said it was his first time to visit Israel and pointed that he was very impressed by the patriotism of Israelis and their efforts that have transformed their nation from a desert to a green rich farmland. [passage omitted] President Moi extended greetings from Israel to Kenyans and announced that Kenya will send an ambassador to Israel this month. [passage omitted]
