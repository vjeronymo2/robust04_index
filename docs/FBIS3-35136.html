Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, esteemed leader of the Islamic revolution, will lead the spectacular and resolute Friday prayers today on the seventh day of the auspicious month of Ramadan and on the first Friday of this auspicious month. The Tehran Friday Prayers staff has called on all fasting brothers and sisters to joint the enemy-breaking Friday prayers and to further add to its resoluteness and splendor and manifest the unity and unanimity of the Islamic ummah.
