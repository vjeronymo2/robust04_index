BFN [Text] A regular meeting of Belarusian and U.S. experts to discuss issues related to the conversion of Belarusian defense industry enterprises with U.S. assistance was held in Minsk today. The Belarusian delegation, headed by First Deputy Defense Minister Alyaksandr Tushinski, and the U.S. delegation, headed by [name indistinct], chairman of the bilateral program for conversion, detailed the program for building at U.S. expense housing for Belarusian officers and warrant officers who are transferred to the strategic forces reserves. Specific terms for converting giant Belarusian defense industry enterprises -- the Productive Association Integral, BELOMO, and Productive Association of Computer Equipment -- were also discussed.
