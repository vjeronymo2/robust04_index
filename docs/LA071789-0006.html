July 17, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BUSH PAY PLAN

President Bush recently vetoed a small raise in the minimum wage law. It has been years since the minimum wage law has been revised, even though the cost-of-living index has considerably increased.

On July 7 President Bush proposed an 8% to 25% raise for federal judges and 8,000 executive branch government officials.

Think about it. Those who have, get; those who have not -- too bad.

RAE DIBBLE

Montebello
