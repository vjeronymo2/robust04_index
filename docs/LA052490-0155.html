May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 when Cordial Steppe, the front horse in his $30 triple bet, ran second in the fourth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $301.

Bob's bet today is a $9 triple from Pro For Sure, Green Baby and Sumbanaire in the seventh to Saudi Desert in the eighth to Lightest Ray and Dream Of Eileen in the ninth.

Wednesday's Bankroll: $331

Wednesday's Loss: $30

Current Bankroll: $301
