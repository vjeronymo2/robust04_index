BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] An explosive device was left abandoned this afternoon at the Miraflores drug store, located less than 50 meters from the presidential palace and next to the Weapons and Explosives Directorate of the Interior Ministry. According to information obtained at the site, someone left a dark brown briefcase at approximately 1400, when the drug store is most crowded. The store clerks became suspicious and called the Directorate of Intelligence and Prevention Services for help. Officials from the Weapons and Explosives Directorate arrived and took the necessary measures. After x-raying the briefcase, they decided to detonate the explosive device. At this time, the authorities do not know who is responsible for this new terrorist action which could have caused injuries and damage near the presidential palace.
