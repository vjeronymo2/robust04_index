941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / Personnel chiefs 'paid 10% less' Personnel directors are paid an average of almost 10 per cent less than other types of director, a pay consultancy says today. Reward Group says in a report on personnel management pay, published in association with the Institute of Personnel and Development, that personnel staff received a median across-the-board pay rise of 3 per cent in the past 12 months. Personnel directors earn Pounds 45,000 a year on average, says Reward, compared with Pounds 49,920 for all other types of director.
