BFN [Text] Cartagena de Indias, 15 Jun (PL) -- Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina denied today his country feels disappointed by the development of the Fourth Ibero-American Summit taking place in this Colombian Caribbean city. When questioned by the press on the lack of a statement at the important meeting on the U.S. blockade against his country, Robaina replied: "We have no reason to feel disappointed, and our conversations regarding this have continued progressing." The chief of Cuban diplomacy was approached by PRENSA LATINA reporters and two other media representatives at the conclusion of the official photo session of the 21 Ibero-American nations' foreign ministers, before the initiation of the second and last session of the summit.
