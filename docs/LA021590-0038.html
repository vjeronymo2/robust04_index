February 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

4-A GIRLS;

RUIZ, KENNEDY CRUISE PAST EL MODENA

Valerie Ruiz scored 34 points and had 11 steals as Kennedy defeated El Modena, 77-39, in a 4-A girls' playoff game Wednesday night at Kennedy.

Michelle Harris added 17 points, 11 steals and six assists for Kennedy (15-10). Kathy Farina scored 14 points to lead El Modena (7-14).

Katella 52, Garden Grove 44 -- Katella (10-15) defeated the Argonauts (11-12) at Katella.

Cypress 67, La Mirada 44 -- Host Cypress raised its record to 16-9.

Redondo 53, Fullerton 47 -- Redondo (8-14), an at-large entry, defeated Fullerton (16-6), the second-place team from the Freeway League.
