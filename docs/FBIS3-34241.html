Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Massive anti-Pakistan demonstrations were held in many parts of Jammu and Kashmir today. Several thousand people, mostly youths, participated in the demonstrations to protest against the Pakistani role in abetting and sponsoring terrorism in various parts of India, especially Jammu and Kashmir. Effigies of Pakistan Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto and former premier Nawaz Sharif were burned at many places by the activists of the Youth Congress-I and other groups. Several youth organizations, including the Jammu and Kashmir Pradesh [state] Youth Congress-I, Democratic Youth Federation of India, Yuva Janata Dal [Youth Wing of Janata Dal], and Youth Kashmir Pundit Migrants organized protest rallies before the office of the United National Observers Group in Jammu. Later, they submitted a memorandum to the group, demanding that Pakistan be declared a terrorist country. Thousands of youths gathered at the Wagah border to protest against the conspiracy by Pakistan against India. Addressing the demonstrators, the Youth Congress-I president, Mr. Maninder Singh Bitta, asked Pakistan to stop encouraging terrorism in India and work sincerely for peace between the two countries.
