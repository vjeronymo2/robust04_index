BFN [Text] Sukkur (NNI) -- Federal Minister for Defence Aftab Shaban Mirani has said that Pakistan does not intend to build the nuclear bomb but, at the same, we would never disband our muclear programme. Talking informally to newsmen at his residence here on Saturday, he strongly refuted speculations that any deal had been struck with the US on the issue. He said Pakistan would never accept outside pressure on a matter related to our country's national security. He blamed the Opposition's negative role in playing up the issue as "childish". He also categorically ruled out reduction in the national defence budget and added "We had a traditionally aggressive enemy on our borders which had exploded a nuclear device as far back as 20 years ago and was only increasing its defence budget". "How can we ignore this build up?, he asked. [no end quotes as published] He said that Pakistan would continue to strive for declaring our region as a nuclear-free zone.
