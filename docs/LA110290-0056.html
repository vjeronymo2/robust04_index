November 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PREP FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Hart 56, San Gabriel 0 -- Hart bounced back at San Gabriel after last week's upset loss to Schurr, which cost it the Foothill League title.

Hart, which led, 42-0, at halftime, improved to 6-3 overall and 2-1 in league play. San Gabriel dropped to 0-9 and 0-3.

Muir 48, Hoover 7 -- Muir quarterback Sedrick Thomas broke his own school record by passing for 402 yards as the Mustangs routed Hoover in a Pacific League game at Glendale High.

Thomas held the record with 369 yards.

Muir (6-3, 3-1) struck quickly against Hoover (2-6, 0-4) and never let up.

Thomas opened the game by completing 13 consecutive passes. When the streak finally ended, Muir was leading, 28-0, at halftime.
