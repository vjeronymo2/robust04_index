940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Northumbrian Water DM15m buy Northumbrian Water Group has, through its Montec International subsidiary, paid DM15.3m (Pounds 6.2m) to acquire the waste water sampling and distribution activities of Edmund Buhler. The company, based south of Stuttgart in Baden Wurttemberg, claims to be the European market leader in the manufacture and distribution of waste water sampling equipment.
