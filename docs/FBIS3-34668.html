Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tripoli, 14 Feb (JANA) -- An official source here today denied the French news agency's report that quoted a Jordanian official source as having said that Libya is the unspecified state which King Husayn referred to in connection with the Jordanian diplomat's assassination. The source said: Such allegations which some Western information media have been circulating are totally untrue. He said: We are convinced that involving Libya's name in this seeks to harm Libya and, by the same token, to undermine the fraternal good relations that link it with fraternal Jordan. The source stressed that Libya has no connection with such actions which it condemns and rejects.
