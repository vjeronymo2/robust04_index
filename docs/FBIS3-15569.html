Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Pavel Vanichkin and Vitaliy Chukseyev] [Text] Washington March 8 TASS -- Thomas Foley, speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives, said on Monday he will provide every possible assistance to the new Russian Parliament to make it truly democratic and respected world-wide. Speaking at the meeting with visiting Russian State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin in Washington on Monday, Foley said that U.S. lawmakers feel deep respect for the Russian Parliament because it was elected in accordance with juridical and constitutional norms and are looking forward to active cooperation with it. He expressed the hope that the upcoming talks and meetings with members of the Russian delegation will give an impetus to inter-parliamentary cooperation. Rybkin is in Washington as head of the Russian parliamentary delegation that arrived in U.S. at Foley's invitation.
