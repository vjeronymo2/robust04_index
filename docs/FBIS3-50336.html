Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Geneva, 27 Jan (TANJUG) -- Federal Deputy Prime Minister Zeljko Simic arrived in Geneva today for talks with Mate Granic, Croatian deputy prime minister and foreign minister. Simic and Granic are expected to have talks in the Palais des Nations this afternoon on the Bosnia-Herzegovina crisis, the implementation of the Yugoslav-Croatain declaration signed on 19 January in Geneva, and a series of humanitarian issues.
