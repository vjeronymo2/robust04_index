March 17, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: 8001 IRVINE CENTER DRIVE

The 15-story building in Irvine Center is the cornerstone of the Irvine Spectrum's downtown and will serve as the Orange County headquarters of American Telephone & Telegraph Co. A grand opening will take place Tuesday and tenant move-in is scheduled for April 1. Half of the 306,000 square feet of space is leased.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: 8001 Irvine Center Drive

Developer: The Irvine Co.

Architect: Hellmuth, Obata & Kassabaum Inc., St. Louis

Primary Contractor: Turner Construction Co.

Broker: Prentiss Properties

Financing: The Irvine Co.

Square feet: 306,000

Use: Office

Cost (land and building): $50 million

Date completed: April 1, 1989

Percentage of building leased: 50%

Parking capacity: 4 spaces per 1,000 square feet

Rental cost per square foot: $1.95
