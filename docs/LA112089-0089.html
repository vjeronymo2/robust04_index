November 20, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

CHINESE PREMIER ARRIVES IN NEPAL FOR 3-DAY VISIT

Chinese Premier Li Peng arrived in Katmandu on Sunday for a three-day visit that Nepal hopes will generate help for its economy, heavily damaged by a trade war with India.

Li, on his first foreign tour since the Chinese army crushed massive pro-democracy demonstrations in June, arrived from Bangladesh to a welcome from his Nepali counterpart, Marich Shrestha.
