BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, June 20, IRNA -- Tehran-Sanaa cooperation, further promotion of ties between the two countries and the Yemeni internal situation were reviewed Sunday in a meeting in Sanaa between the Iranian ambassador to Yemen Mohammad Jalal Firuz-Nia and the Yemeni speaker of the Consultative Assembly 'Abdallah al-Ahmar. In this meeting Firuz-Nia also explored ways to increase parliamentary cooperation between the two countries and delivered a message from Iranian Majlis Speaker Hojjat ol-Eslam 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri to al-Ahmar inviting Yemeni parliamentary delegation to visit Tehran.
