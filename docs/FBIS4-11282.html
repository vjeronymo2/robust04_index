BFN [Text] Cienfuegos Province sugar mills have attained, during the current phase [of the harvest], 83.2 percent of the plan for self-sufficiency in electrical energy. This is some 2.6 percent more than in the same period in 1993. Scheduled repairs to the sugar installations are designed to restore the areas that generate steam and to relocate four turbogenerators to provide an emergency reserve of 3.6 megawatts.
