931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Aberdeen Steak An improvement in trading volumes helped Aberdeen Steak Houses Group cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 381,000 to Pounds 197,000 in the first half of 1993. Turnover rose 6.5 per cent to Pounds 6.16m, against Pounds 5.78m. Losses per share were cut from 2.7p to 1.8p.
