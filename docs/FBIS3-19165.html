Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Report by Dhimitrios Dhelapaskhos] [Text] A seven-member delegation of the Skopjean [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] government was in Athens for less than two hours today. The delegation, led by Skopjean Assembly President Stojan Andov, will participate in the pan-European conference on transportation, which will be held in Crete over the next few days [14-15 March]. The Skopjean officials did not have any meetings in the VIP lounge of the airport west terminal, they avoided reporters, and continued their trip with a visa which was granted them on a blank paper on which FYROM was written in accordance with the terms set by the Greek Government.
