BFN [Text] This morning the Russia's Choice faction in the State Duma decided to back the draft federal budget in the second reading if 3.5 trillion rubles more are allotted for the needs of defense structures, Russia's Choice member Sergey Yushenkov told Interfax. He stressed that as the Chairman of the State Duma Committee on Defense he thought that the Army must be given 55 trillion rubles instead of the 37 trillion rubles stated in the draft budget. Yushenkov said he would vote for the proposal of the parliamentary Budget Committee providing for the increase in the funds for defense needs by 3.5 trillion rubles and for the amendment put forward by the Committee on Defense demanding that 18 trillion rubles more be allotted for these purposes.
