BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Juri Luik left for Istanbul today where a session of the North Atlantic Cooperation Council will take place on 9 and 10 June. [passage omitted] Juri Luik will be making a speech at the session. The foreign minister is accompanied by Ambassador Clyde Kull, who is the Estonian representative at the cooperation council; Ambassador Vaino Reinart, the head of the number one office at the Foreign Ministry's policy department; and (Aivo Orav), an employee of the office. [passage omitted] Foreign Minister Luik will travel from Istanbul on to Beijing, where he will be accompanying President Meri on his state visit to China.
