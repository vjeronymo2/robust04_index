BFN [Text] In compliance with the amendments of the Liberal Democratic Party's Charter, adopted at the party's Fifth Congress, its leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy appointed his three deputies. They are State Duma Deputies Sergey Abeltsev, Aleksandr Vengerovsky, and Stanislav Zhebrovsky. With Zhirinovskiy they make up the party's Superior Council. This was reported by the LDPR press service.
