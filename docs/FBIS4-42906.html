BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 9 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Staying in Canada on an official visit at the invitation of his Canadian counterpart the Minister of External Relations of Mongolia T. Gombosuren has signed on 8 June in Ottawa the Mongolian-Canadian intergovernmental agreement on trade bargains. At the same day he met with the president of the parliamentary Senate of Canada R. Leblanc and handed in to him a message by the chairman of the Mongolian parliament was holding an exchange of opinions on issues of the development of bilateral relations with secretary of state of the Canadian minister of foreign relations and international trade, Legislator R. Chan and delivered him a message by the Mongolian prime minister. [sentence as received] T. Gombosuren is staying in Canada since June 5.
