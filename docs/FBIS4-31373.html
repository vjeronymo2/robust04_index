BFN [Excerpt] Skopje, 8 Jun (TANJUG) -- In Skopje today, Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] President Kiro Gligorov today received Matthew Nimetz, the special U.S. envoy in the Greek-Macedonian dispute. Their talks lasted for more than two hours. Nimetz arrived on a two-day working visit to the FYROM yesterday ahead of the resumption of negotiations between Skopje and Athens in New York on 13 June. In a press statement following his talks with Gligorov, which were also attended by Macedonian Foreign Relations Minister Stevo Crvenkovski, Nimetz said that he spoke about the topics of great importance for regional developments. According to Nimetz, it is inappropriate to give any forecasts as regards the outcome of future negotiations. [passage omitted]
