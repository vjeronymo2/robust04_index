941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / International Company News: Taiwan banks ahead Taiwan's big three state-run commercial banks have all reported higher pre-tax profits for the July-September quarter compared with a year ago, writes Our Financial Staff. At Hua Nan Bank, pre-tax profits rose to TDollars 2.12bn (USDollars 80m) from TDollars 1.38bn on turnover of TDollars 15.17bn, compared with TDollars 13.53bn last time. First Commercial Bank turnover rose more modestly to TDollars 15.54bn from TDollars 14.78bn. but pre-tax profits leapt to TDollars 2.01bn from TDollars 1.57bn. At Chang Hwa Bank, pre-tax profits advanced to TDollars 1.50bn from TDollars 1.23bn a year ago. Revenues were not reported.
