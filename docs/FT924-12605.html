921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Losses at Gieves rise to Pounds 530,000 INTERIM LOSSES at Gieves Group, which is concentrating on its core retailing, large print and audio book publishing activities, rose from Pounds 467,000 to Pounds 530,000 pre-tax. Turnover for the six months to July 31 amounted to Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 24.3m), reflecting disposals. Losses at the Milan store totalled Pounds 195,000 (Pounds 283,000) and were taken above the line. Losses per share were 3.5p (3.3p).
