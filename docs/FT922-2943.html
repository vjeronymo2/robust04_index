920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / Pledge over NHS 'whistleblowing' NATIONAL Health Service unions yesterday broadly welcomed an undertaking by Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary, to introduce guidance preventing the gagging of 'whistleblowing' staff who publicise concerns about the service. But Labour complained that Mrs Bottomley had not proposed a right of appeal outside the NHS management. British Dental Association members were told yesterday that they are to be balloted on industrial action in a dispute with the government over proposals to cut fees by 7 per cent.
