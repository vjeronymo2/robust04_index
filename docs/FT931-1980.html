930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy back in the black A turnround from after-tax losses of Pounds 27.6m to profits of Pounds 855,000 was announced by Cairn Energy, the independent oil and gas company, for 1992. At the halfway stage losses were Pounds 161,000. The result last time included exceptional write-downs of Pounds 24.7m. Turnover improved from Pounds 12.3m to Pounds 13.1m. Earnings per share amounted to 3.03p (113.66p losses).
