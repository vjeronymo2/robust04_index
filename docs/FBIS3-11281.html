Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of the Revolutionary Armed Forces [Minfar] has reported that an unfortunate accident took place on the morning of 17 March, involving a two-seat MiG-23UB [alphanumeric designation as heard] aircraft carrying out a highly complex training flight over the territory of the Eastern Army. Two first class pilots from Holguin Airbase died in this accident. They are: Major Sinecio Landa Lopez, married, and father of two children, and Major Jose Borrego Pajon, also married, who left a pregnant wife. Both pilots are graduates from our aviation school in Pinar del Rio in 1985 and 1986. They were 36 years old, militants of the Cuban Communist Party, and both fulfilled internationalist missions in Angola. The Minfar has conveyed condolences to all relatives of these two courageous pilots who died in the fulfillment of their tasks.
