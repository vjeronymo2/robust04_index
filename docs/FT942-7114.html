940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / International Company News: Probe into Steyr share trading Austria's state prosecutor is investigating alleged insider dealing in the shares of Steyr-Daimler-Puch, the motor group, writes Ian Rodger. It is the first investigation since insider trading became illegal last October. Steyr shares rose 14.5 per cent after it decided to increase co-operation with the German tractor maker, Klockner-Humbold-Deutz und Fendt.
