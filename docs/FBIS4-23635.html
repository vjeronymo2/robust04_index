BFN [Text] The head of state has left Abuja for Tunisia to attend the 30th summit of the Organization of African Unity, which begins tomorrow in Tunis. He was accompanied by the minister for national planning, Chief S.B. Daniyan, and his special adviser, Chief (Tom Ikedi). He was seen off by the chief of general staff, Lieutenant General Oladipo Diya; minister of internal affairs, Mr. Alex Ibru; capital territory minister, Lt. Gen. John Useni, and other top government functionaries. During the meeting, Gen. Abacha will discuss issues of concern to the continent with other African heads of state and governments.
