BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- The Mansudae Art Studio has created a number of works of art of various themes to greet the Korean People's Army Day (April 25). Included in the works are Korean and oil paintings portraying on canvases of high ideological and artistic standard Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, who is possessed of the chuche-based military idea, extraordinary intelligence, matchless grit and warm love. "Supreme Commander General Kim Chong-il Guiding an Operational Meeting", "Opening the Route of the Era", "Merciless Punishment on Provokers" and other Korean paintings portray Comrade Kim Chong-il who is rearing the KPA into an invincible, matchless army with the chuche-based military idea and unique tactics at the head of the revolutionary armed forces. "Respected Supreme Commander Marshal Kim Chong-il Guiding Combined Tactical Drill", a Korean painting portraying him guiding a coordinated operational drill of mechanized units of the three services on a mountain side with binoculars in his hand also successfully represents the looks of the great brilliant commander. The Korean paintings "Father of Soldiers" and "The Dear Leader Calling at a Post", the oil painting "Supreme Commander General Kim Chong-il Among Soldiers" and many other works show the noble virtues of Comrade Kim Chong-il who always visits soldiers and bestows tender loving care on them.
