BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 19 May -- New information about a concentration of Ukrainian troops on the Crimean peninsula reached ITAR-TASS this afternoon. The agency has learned from sources at the headquarters of the Black Sea Fleet that special-purpose units which may be used to seize Black Sea Fleet units deployed in the port of Izmail are being concentrated around the town of Izmail. According to the same reports, a Ukrainian armed forces military field hospital is being set up in the village of Chernobayevka in Kherson Oblast. Those working there, the same sources reported, apparently do not conceal from the local people that they are preparing to receive wounded people from Crimea in the near future.
