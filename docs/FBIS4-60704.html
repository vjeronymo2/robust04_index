BFN [Text] President Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari arrived in New York from Washington. He is accompanied by Punjab Chief Minister Mian Manzur Watoo and Dr. Maliha Lodhi, Pakistan's ambassador to the United States. Before his departure, the president said that despite being on a private visit, he utilized the opportunity and exchanged views with Secretary of State Warren Christopher and other U.S. leaders on matters of mutual interest. The president said that he told the U.S. leaders during his visit that Pakistan wants the United States to help resolve the Kashmir issue and end grave violations of human rights in occupied Kashmir. The United States was urged to cooperate in arms control and nuclear nonproliferation in South Asia and to help accelerate economic development to bring about prosperity in the region. Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari said that we want to enhance cooperation with the United States in various sectors on the basis of equality and nondiscrimination.
