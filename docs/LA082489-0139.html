August 24, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WEBER FUNERAL TODAY

The funeral service for Jerry Weber, a prominent official of the Jewish community who was slain in a robbery at an automated teller machine, will be held today at 11 a.m. at the Valley Beth Shalom Synagogue, 15739 Ventura Blvd., Encino.

Weber, 49, was the director of the Council on Jewish Life of the Jewish Federation Council of Greater Los Angeles for the last 10 years. He was shot and killed outside a bank in West Los Angeles early Tuesday.
