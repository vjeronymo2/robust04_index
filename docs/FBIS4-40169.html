BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Dmitriy Gorokhov] [Text] Moscow June 14 TASS -- A Russian Foreign Ministry's official said Russia is likely to join the European Council in a few months. "We presume that Russia might become a member of the European Council within the next six months, and not later than next spring", Galina Sidorova, a spokeswoman for Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, told reporters on Tuesday. According to Sidorova, earlier, Russia's entering the EC was conditioned on the adoption of a new constitution. Now Russia has its new constitution adopted. And this "enables her to bid for an equal place among the members of the European Council", she said.
