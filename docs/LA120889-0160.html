December 8, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

CHOOSE AND CUT YOUR CHRISTMAS TREE

Time constraints, modern life styles and weather all conspire to deny county residents many of the trappings of a traditional Christmas. There are not too many sleigh rides to grandma's, for example, when the thermometer lurks around 70 degrees.

But one tradition we can enjoy is cutting a fresh Christmas tree.

Orange County has a nice crop of tree farms where either you or the farm staff can cut a tree. Some farms provide services such as home delivery, flocking and fire-retarding; some provide seasonal treats. Most farms plan to be open until Christmas Eve but will close when their stocks are depleted.
