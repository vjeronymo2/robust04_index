BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- Cambodian King Norodom Sihanouk sent a letter to the embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Phnom Penh on May 7 supporting the statement released by the DPRK Foreign Ministry on April 28 calling for the replacement of the Korean armistice agreement with a peace agreement and for a peace arrangement. The letter declared that the Cambodian people affairm full and eternal support for the independent, peaceful and most patriotic policy advanced by His Excellency Generalissimo Kim Il-song, the respected great leader. It said it is high time that the armistice agreement signed 40 odd years ago was replaced with a genuine, lasting peace one which definitely guarantees a durable peace and security on the Korean peninsula in actuality.
