BFN [Text] Moscow, June 12, RIA -- "I will continue to fight for the observance of laws in Russia and I do not want this to be called a struggle against Yuriy Luzhkov," Anatoliy Chubays, vice-premier of the Russian Government and chairman of the State Property Management Committee, told a RIA correspondent during an intermission in the founding congress of Russia's Democratic Choice Party today. It is to be remembered that President Yeltsin asked First Vice- Premier Oleg Soskovets to tell Chubays that he "should not have anything to do with Moscow." Chubays promised to do all within "authority to punish those who violate laws regardless of their position. Time will show who wins. however, my attitude to privatisation Moscow style did not change after President Yeltsin's famous order," he said.
