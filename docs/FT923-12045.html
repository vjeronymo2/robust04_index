920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Observer: Shoot out The events in the following tale have a reasonable explanation. What is it? (Answers by mail or by fax to Observer on 071-873 3926 to arrive by Thursday when the first plausible one out of the bag will earn a bottle of malt.) A small cowboy steps into a saloon, gulps when he sees the giant gun-toting barman, then walks nervously up and asks for a glass of water. Whereupon the giant whips out his Colt and fires it into the air. 'Thank you,' says the small man with a smile, and goes out again.
