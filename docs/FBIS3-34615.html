Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Golani Brigade has completed an exercise in the Golan. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the troops simulated expected combat situations in the Golan and exercised cooperation between ground forces. After the exercise, Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, the Northern Command commander, said that Syria continues its military buildup, and the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] is aware of the danger from that direction. He stressed that the Army is training in the Golan and preparing the Golani Brigade and the armored forces and artillery systems for every conceivable task.
