Issued in Renton, Washington, on March 30, 1994. [Advisory Circular 25&hyph;18] Transport Category Airplanes Modified for Cargo Service AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration, DOT. ACTION: Notice of issuance of advisory circular.

SUMMARY: This notice announces the issuance of Advisory Circular (AC) 25&hyph;18, Transport Category Airplanes Modified for Cargo Service. This AC provides guidance for demonstrating compliance with the FAR pertaining to transport category airplanes converted for use in all-cargo or combination passenger/cargo (combi) service and the relationship of those regulations to the requirements of parts 121 and 135 of the FAR.
