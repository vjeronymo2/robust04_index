920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Stratagem sharply higher at Pounds 840,000 Stratagem Group, the investment company enlarged via the takeover of Touchstone, the computer services concern, some 12 months ago, increased its profits from Pounds 18,000 to Pounds 840,000 pre-tax for the half year ended February 28. The improvement reflected realisations of quoted investments over the period. The surplus on disposals totalled Pounds 861,000 (Pounds 9,000) while other income rose to Pounds 594,000 (Pounds 288,000). Earnings per share amounted to 11.2p against losses of 1.1p.
