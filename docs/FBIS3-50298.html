Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The American ambassador to Croatia, Peter Galbraith, visited Topusko, Petrova Gora, and Glina in UN Sector North yesterday. A report from the American Embassy in Zagreb says that Mr. Galbraith wished to find out about the situation in that part of Croatian territory under UN protection. Mr. Galbraith described the visit as successful and said that it was important to start communicating.
