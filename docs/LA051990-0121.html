May 19, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION SMALL SCHOOLS BASEBALL;

CAL LUTHERAN IS PASSED UP BY A PASSED BALL

A passed ball in the bottom of the seventh inning gave Woodcrest Christian High School a 1-0 victory over Cal Lutheran Friday in the first round of the Southern Section Small Schools baseball playoffs.

Brad Pluckhan (3-3) pitched a five-hitter for Cal Lutheran (7-8), which had only three hits.

In another Small Schools Division game:

Southern California Christian 1, Garden Grove Claremont 0 -- Southern California Christian won by forfeit because on eight Claremont players showed up for the game.
