April 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BROCCOLI

You may be disinterested to know that gardening is my hobby and that I have hybridized a new strain of broccoli.

It will not be grown as an annual but rather as a shrub.

In honor of our President I have named it the Broccoli Bush. Wish me luck.

Yes, I may use pork rinds for fertilizer.

PARKE LEVY

Beverly Hills
