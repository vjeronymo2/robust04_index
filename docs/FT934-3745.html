931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / International Company News: Dutch groups in alliance talks DSM and Gist-Brocades, the Dutch chemicals groups, said their co-operation talks were at an advanced stage and that they were expected to lead shortly to a joint venture, Reuter reports from Amsterdam. The investigation into co-operation on an equal basis in semi-synthetic cefalosporines and new intermediates for the antibiotics segment had reached the stage where a joint venture could be expected soon, the two companies said.
