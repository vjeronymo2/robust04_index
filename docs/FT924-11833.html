921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: VAW to reduce its primary aluminium capacity VAW, the German metals group, is to reduce its primary aluminium capacity in Germany by 105,000 tonnes to 300,000 tonnes a year. Implementation of the cuts, at its Rheinwer and Innwerk smelters in Neuss and Toging, will begin immediately. 'The decision is a reaction to the dramatic decline in aluminium prices,' Mr Karl Wobbe, board member responsible for the company's aluminium division, said yesterday. 'The main cause for this is the continuing high level of primary aluminium exports from the CIS.'
