Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, Feb 25 (AFP)--The "terrible massacre" of Palestinians in Hebron on Friday [25 February] has dealt a "fatal blow" to the peace process, Hamas spokesman Ibrahim Ghawshah said here. The shooting, in which at least 40 Palestinians died, will provoke a "dramatic escalation in the intifadah and shake the Palestinian people and the Arab nation out of their slumber." The Islamic Resistance Movement Hamas has been at the forefront of attacks on Jews in the occupied territories since the PLO signed its peace deal with Israel in Washington in September. The spokesman said his movement had always warned that "coexistence is impossible with the Israeli enemy which wants the land without its inhabitants."
