930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / UK Company News: S Daniels improves in second half Improved trading in the second six months at S Daniels, an importer and distributor for the food industry, resulted in a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 64,000 for 1992, compared with Pounds 105,000 in the first six months and Pounds 158,000 in 1991. The results have been prepared under the FRS 3 accounting principles. Turnover of continuing operations increased from Pounds 29.8m to Pounds 32.3m. The loss was struck after a Pounds 101,000 deficit on disposal of discontinued operations, less a 1991 provision of Pounds 110,000. The dividend is held at 0.25p. Losses per share were halved to 1p.
