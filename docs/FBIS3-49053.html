Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Social Alliance of Slovenia has invited the leader of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party Zhirinovskiy to visit Slovenia. Besides Zhirinovskiy, the party has also invited the head of the Italian Lega Nord Umberto Bossi and Ljupco Georgievski from the Macedonian VMRO-DPMNE [Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization-Democratic Party for Macedonian National Unity]. All three of them are supposed to be the guests of the congress of the Slovene National Socialists on 12 February 1994. Of course, regarding this we can shortly expect the opinion of the incumbent Slovene authorities.
