BFN [From the "Doing Radio" program] [Text] Military units in Matanzas Province are undergoing an inspection aimed at reviewing the progress of the fulfillment of the Central Army and Revolutionary Armed Forces strategic guidelines. The Central Army inspection will conclude on 1 July. It includes the provincial General Staff and Matanzas and Union de Reyes Municipalities, as well as the Camilo Cienfuegos and Comandante Horacio Schools. The inspection of the provincial defense system will also review the Revolutionary Navy's efforts, as well as the progress of efforts to achieve second phase status in defense preparedness. The combat training of the Territorial Troops Militia, special units, and Production and Defense Brigades in the province will also be evaluated. On 28 June at 2000, the Camilo Cienfuegos Military School held the first provincial defense system amateur artists festival. The most culturally active municipality of the Central Army will be selected tonight.
