ADDRESSES: Building 4200, room P110, George C. Marshall Space Flight Center, Alabama.
FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Mr. Ralph C. Thomas III, Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization, National Aeronautics and Space Administration, room 9K70, 300 E Street, SW., Washington, DC 20546, (202) 358&hyph;2088. The agenda for the meeting is as follows: _Review of Last Meeting and Action Items _Rcap of MBRAC Activites and Accomplishments _Report of the Associate Administrator for Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization _Current SDB Issues Regarding NASA Procurements _Status of SDB Participations in Space Station and Other Major NASA Programs _Chairman's Report _Committee Reports _Invitation for Suggestions by Individuals in Attendance It is imperative that the meeting be held on this date to accommodate the scheduling priorities of the key participants.
