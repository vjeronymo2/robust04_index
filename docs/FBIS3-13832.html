Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] His Majesty King Husayn left the homeland this morning in God's care and protection on visits to the Sultanate of Oman and the State of Qatar. During his visit to Oman, his majesty will hold talks with his brother, His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id, aimed at enhancing coordination and consultations between the two sisterly countries and following up developments pertaining to both Yemeni reconciliation and the joint military Jordanian-Omani committee entrusted with effecting Yemeni reconciliation. During his visit to Qatar, his majesty will hold talks with his brother, His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar, on the regional situation, ways to bolster Arab solidarity, and recent developments in the peace process in the wake of UN Security Council Resolution 904. [passage omitted] In the presence of ministers, His Highness Prince Muhammad, his majesty the king's personal representative, took the constitutional oath as his majesty's regent during his stay abroad. His majesty is accompanied by His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin- al-Husayn; His Highness Prince Hamzah Bin-al-Husayn; His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's political adviser; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser; His Eminence Shaykh 'Izz-al-Din al- Khatib al-Tamimi, his majesty's adviser for religious affairs and the chief justice; Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; the Jordanian ambassador to Oman; and a number of senior civil and military officials.
