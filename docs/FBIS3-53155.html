Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Assembly held an ordinary session this morning under the chairmanship of assembly speaker Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Sa'dun. The assembly examined at this session the report of the committee for legal and legislative affairs on the government's draft law, Number 25 of 1990, on the trial of ministers [which would exempt ministers from being brought to trial]. The assembly decided not to pass the draft law. [passage omitted]
