BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 19 -- Minister for Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Korea Han Sung-chu arrived in Hanoi this afternoon beginning an official visit to Vietnam as guest of his Vietnamese counterpart Nguyen Manh Cam. Minister Hang Sung-chu and his party were welcomed at the government guest house by Mr. Nguyen Manh Cam, Minister of Finance Ho Te and other government officials. Minister Cam later received his guest and affirmed that the visit took place at a time when the relations between the two countries were developing and expressed his hope that it would create a new step of development in these relations. South Korean Ambassador Pak No Su was present at the reception. The Vietnamese foreign minister gave a banquet this evening in honour of his South Korean guests.
