931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / GPT secures Pounds 10m contract GPT, the UK-based telecommu-nications joint manufacturing venture between GEC and Siemens, has won a contract worth about Pounds 10m to supply exchanges to Southwestern Bell for its UK cable telephone network. The order is for up to 10 System X digital telephone exchanges. Southwestern Bell, the regional US operator, is building networks in seven urban areas, including Liverpool, central Lancashire, Telford and Wigan, with Cox Cable Communications. Southwestern Bell is the third-largest cable TV and telephone operator in the UK after Nynex and Telewest.
