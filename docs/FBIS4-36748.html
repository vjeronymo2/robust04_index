BFN [Text] Cairo, 11 Jun (MENA)--Congolese President Pascal Lissouba arrived in Cairo this evening on an official two-day visit at the invitation of President Husni Mubarak. An official reception was held at the Presidential Palace in Heliopolis in honor of President Lissouba. A band played the national anthems of the two countries and the two presidents reviewed the honor guard. President Mubarak held talks with President Lissousba after his arrival in Cairo this evening. The talks dealt with African and international issues of common concern and with bilateral ties.
