April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

KORS, SEAHAWKS REACH AGREEMENT

Former El Camino Real High football standout R. J. Kors has agreed to a two-year contract with the Seattle Seahawks, his agent, Paul Bartelt, announced Tuesday.

Kors, 23, a safety, also played at Taft College and Cal State Long Beach. Seattle drafted him in 1989 but released him in the preseason. STEVEN HERBERT
