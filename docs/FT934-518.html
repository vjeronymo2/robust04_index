931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: CIA acquires rest of Italian arm CIA Group, the UK's largest independent advertising media buyer, is acquiring the remaining 55 per cent of CIA Medianetwork Italia not under its control. The transaction, for 1.6m new ordinary CIA shares, worth about Pounds 5.5m, will create Europe's second largest advertising media buyer - after Carat - with an annual billing of Pounds 650m. Completion is expected in February. CIA took the initial 45 per cent of CIAMNI in June this year for 400,000 shares plus L2.5bn (Pounds 1m). CIA's shares, which trade on the USM, closed 5p up at 352p.
