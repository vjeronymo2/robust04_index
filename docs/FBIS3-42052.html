Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has asked Russia whether the recent foreign news report stating that it has decided to sell additional submarines to North Korea is true. Paek Nak-wan, director-general for the European Affair Bureau of Foreign Ministry, called a Russian minister to the ROK to the Foreign Ministry today and asked him to reveal whether the foreign news report stating that Russia had sold the four Fox-Trot submarines to North Korea and that it has recently concluded a contract to sell 10 additional Golf-class submarines to North Korea is true.
