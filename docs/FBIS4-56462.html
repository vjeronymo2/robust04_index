BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 3 May (NA) -- This afternoon, Eduardo Menem, the provisional president of the Senate, stressed that the higher chamber will approve the draft Patents Law that was submitted by the executive branch "in June at the very latest." Menem made this remark after a meeting between the Senate leadership of the Justicialist Party with Interior Minister Carlos Ruckauf and Eduardo Bauza, the secretary general of the presidency.
