BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Melnik, Ukrinform correspondent Raisa Stetsura] [Text] Kiev, 26 Jun -- "I am giving my vote to the democratic, rule-of-law, independent, and self-governing Ukraine, to an economically powerful state in which my people will live much better," said Ukraine's Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol today commenting on his participation in the presidential and local election. In an interview to journalists at a polling station, he spoke in favor of cooperation between all branches of power in Ukraine. "If there is no such consensus, we will be unable to avoid certain collisions," he said. Vitaliy Masol said that apart from the election results, he would soon propose at the parliament the list of government members. The prime minister declined to comment on a possible cabinet reshuffle.
