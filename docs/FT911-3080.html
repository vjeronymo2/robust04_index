910426 FT 26 APR 91 / UK Company News: Scot Mortgage & Trust lifts assets 5% Scottish Mortgage & Trust, the investment trust managed by Baillie Gifford, unveiled a net asset value of 172.3p at March 31. That was 5.2 per cent higher than the 163.8p last time. A final dividend of 2.45p has been recommended, to make 3.7p (3.35p) for the year. Earnings advanced to 4.48p (3.94p) per share and profits after tax amounted to Pounds 15.94m (Pounds 14.22m). During the year the company bought Pounds 18m-worth of UK equities and sold Pounds 41m of US ones. It plans to start its own PEP scheme.
