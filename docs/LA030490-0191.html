March 4, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

CSUN EDGES SAN DIEGO STATE

Neil Coffman had 22 kills and Mark Root notched seven service aces Saturday to lift Cal State Northridge over San Diego State, 17-16, 15-6, 13-15, 15-5, in a Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn. match at CSUN.

The victory moved Northridge (8-6, 5-4 in WIVA play) within two games of conference-leading UCLA and a half-game ahead of third-place Pepperdine.
