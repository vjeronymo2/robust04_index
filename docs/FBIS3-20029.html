Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] Free trade between Finland and the Czech Republic will not end even if Finland becomes a member of the EU [European Union]. The Czechs have been concerned that the KEVSOS [expansion unknown] agreement between Finland and the Eastern European countries would not be valid if Finland joined the EU. Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus, who is visiting Finland, and Finnish Prime Minister Esko Aho held talks on trade between the two countries in the morning. According to Finland, the ending of the KEVSOS agreement can be replaced with agreements between the EU and the Czech Republic if Finland joins the EU.
