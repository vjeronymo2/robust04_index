BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- Functions took place in various countries to celebrate the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. An April 15 celebration weekly function was held in Lima, Peru, under the co-sponsorship of political parties and organizations. Speaking at the function, Angel Castro Lavarello, president of the National Executive Committee of the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru, said: "The great leader President Kim Il-song is a genius of revolution and construction who has built Korea into a powerful socialist country, independent, self-supporting and self-reliant in national defence, and an iron-willed brilliant commander who defeated two imperialisms in one generation and is firmly defending the cause of socialism. "He is the lodestar of Korean reunification who put forth the three principles of independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity, the proposal for founding the Democratic Confederal Republic of Koryo and the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation and is leading the whole nation to carry them into effect." A letter to President Kim Il-song was adopted at the opening ceremony of the function amidst an enthusiastic applause. A meeting was held at the Jilin Yuwen Middle School in China, a Korean photo and handicraft exhibition and a film show in Giurgiu County, Romania, a lecture and a photo exhibition at the peace hall in Hungary, and a Korean book presentation ceremony in Paris, France. Cultural functions took place in Baktapur, Nepal and at the folklore museum theatre in Peru in celebration of the April 15 fete.
