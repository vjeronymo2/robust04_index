BFN [Text] Tirana, June 9 (ATA) -- A delegation of the Albanian Parliament headed by Pjeter Arbnori, speaker of the parliament, is on a visit to Poland at the invitation of the speaker of the Sejm of the Polish Republic, Jozef Oleksy. The delegation has had meetings with the Polish minister of foreign affairs, Andrzej Olechowski, Premier Waldemar Pawlak, and leaders of various parliamentary parties. Mr. Arbnori and his delegation visited the city of Krakow in which they talked about the experience in local governing and the city of Czestochowa, which has established sister relations with the city of Shkoder, northern Albania. In Czestochowa Mr. Arbnori talked about cooperation in the field of education. Albanian delegation also visited the church Jasna Gora, a centre of pilgrimage for believers of various religion throughout the world.
