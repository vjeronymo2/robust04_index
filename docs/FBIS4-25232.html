BFN [Text] Beijing, June 7 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC), met here today with U Song-il, president of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, and his party. Wei, also president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) exchanged views with the Korean visitors on bilateral ties between China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) and other issues of common interest. The DPRK visitors arrived today for a friendly visit to China at ACFTU's invitation.
