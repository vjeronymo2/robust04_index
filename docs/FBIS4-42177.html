BFN [Text] Mulhouse (DPA) -- The 63d Franco-German summit opened in the Alsace city of Mulhouse on Monday [30 May] afternoon. After a welcome ceremony with military honors, French President Francois Mitterrand and Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl met for a first round of talks. As in the meeting that will follow between Kohl and French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur, talks will focus on European issues. In the parallel talks in Mulhouse, the numerous ministers of both countries will also discuss the coordination of policies in the EU Presidency that Germany will take over on 1 July and pass on to France on 1 January 1995.
