BFN [Text] The Moscow-Kiev talks on sharing out the Black Sea Fleet [BSF] will be continued after the Ukrainian parliament has discussed the issue, Ukrainian Vice Premier Valeriy Shmarov told Interfax-Ukraine on Tuesday. He said Kiev would use the deputies' position as a guideline in talks with Moscow. Shmarov who was involved in last week's talks in Moscow on the future of the fleet said that examination was now under way of BSF's infrastructure in Crimea before the resumption of high level talks a bit later. He invited the Russian delegation to Kiev describing it as a better venue for the second round of talks. In the meantime, Russia's Deputy Defence Minister Boris Gromov and Special Envoy Yuriy Dubinin are due to arrive in Kiev on Tuesday, according to Interfax-Ukraine.
