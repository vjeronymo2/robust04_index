October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CORPORATE MIND?

KKBT general manger Jim de Castro is a college-educated anti-intellectual whose remarks (and those of his staff) consist of yuppie-oriented non sequiturs and reflect a lack of taste.

Equally offensive is De Castro's attitude. He not only feels no remorse in having eliminated one of the longest-operating classical radio stations, but he actually thinks he has something to offer that the other rock stations don't.

ROBERT S. WILSON

Los Angeles
