BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe May 5 TASS -- A delegation of the Tajik Government led by chairman of the Tajik parliament Emomali Rakhmonov is leaving for Moscow today, ITAR-TASS learned from the press service of the head of the Tajik Republic. During the visit the Tajik delegation is expected to meet Russian top leaders to discuss a wide range of questions on deepening and expanding political, economic and military cooperation between the Russian Federation and Tajikistan. The sides are expected to discuss the merger of the Russian and Tajik monetary systems as well. The Tajik delegation includes Chairman of the Tajik Council of Ministers Abdulmadjid Samadov, Chairman of the Tajik Central Bank Kayum Kavmidinov and a large group of experts. Problems faced by the Russian-speaking population in Tajikistan and issues of reinforcing the state border with Afghanistan will be high on the agenda of the Moscow talks held in the framework of the visit to Russia by the Chairman of the Tajik parliament Emomali Rakhmonov which begins today, said Anatoliy Adamishin, Russian deputy foreign minister, on Wednesday. During Rakhmonov's visit to Moscow the sides will discuss issues of Russo-Tajik economic cooperation and an internal political situation in Tajikistan and in partucular, prospects for a dialogue with the opposition. According to Adamishin, Russia has made considerable efforts to put out the flame of civil war in Tajikistan. The Russian diplomat welcomed the results of the first round of talks between Dushanbe and the opposition, and in particular, the fact acknowledged by the sides that a key to the solution of the problem is the political settlement of the situation in Tajikistan.
