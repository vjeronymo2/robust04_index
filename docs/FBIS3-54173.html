Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An atmosphere of solemn melancholy engulfed the city of Damascus for the third day running as people mourned the loss of the young Staff Major Paratrooper Basil al-Asad taken away by death at the apogee of his promising life. Mosques and churches continued to express this sadness through recitations of Koranic verses and the ringing of church bells from the early hours of the morning. As people prayed that God give patience to President Hafiz al-Asad and to enable him to continue the struggle to achieve pride and dignity for his nation. All shops and business markets were closed for the third day running to observe a three-day mourning. In the universities and educational institutions, examinations were postponed as students were still under the impact of this tragic shock. Students and businessmen took to the streets in sad processions to express the depth of their grief. Various popular organizations distributed posters in the main streets expressing condolences to President al-Asad and his family.
