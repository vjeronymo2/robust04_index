Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 17 (ATA) -- "Heroin and hashish can be found in Tirana now," some youngsters who didn't want to be identified told an ATA reporter. According to them, a gram of heroin costs 1,000 leks and after buying they divide it into four parts enough to fill two cigarettes. The youngsters said it is not difficult to notice signs of these drugs even in the public places. Pethidine was introduced in Albania no more than two years ago as an indispensable medicament in the emergency wards of clinics and hospitals but now there are shortages of it. Time before it was given in pharmacies free of charge while now it costs 500 leks and the price may increase again. Doctors at the central clinic in Tirana said that there are cases when youngsters have threatened them in order to get pethidine. According to some youths, pethidine is a weak drug for them to be used.
