October 12, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PROFESSOR SELECTED AS CAL STATE TRUSTEE

Gov. George Deukmejian on Wednesday appointed Gloria S. Hom of Palo Alto to the Board of Trustees of the California State University system. A Republican, Hom, 49, replaces the late Dale Ride of Encino.

She is a professor of economics at Mission College in Santa Clara and served as a member of the state Board of Education from 1984-89.

Trustees are paid $100 per meeting day, plus necessary expenses. The position requires state Senate confirmation.
