930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / Credit card fees deter owners THE ABSENCE of an annual fee is the most important criterion for consumers for choosing a credit card, according to a Gallup survey for TSB. The survey of 1,012 adults found that 46 per cent of cardholders felt no fee was the most important factor when choosing; 29 per cent said a low monthly rate of interest was the most important.
