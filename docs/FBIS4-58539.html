BFN [Text] India and several other countries have welcomed the signing of the historic agreement on Palestinian self-rule. An External Affairs Ministry spokesman said in New Delhi that India has given consistent support to the Palestinians in the peace process. He recalled that India is one of the few countries outside the region invited to witness the signing ceremony at Cairo. The Indian delegation led by the minister of state for external affairs, Mr. R.L. Bhatia, was present when the PLO chairman, Mr Yasir 'Arafat and the Israeli prime minister, Mr. Rabin, signed the landmark agreement.
