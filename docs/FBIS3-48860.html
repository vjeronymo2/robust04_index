Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- The "April 15 technical innovation shock brigades" of Korea have introduced more than 140,000 technical innovation proposals over the last one year, actively contributing to the chuche orientation, modernization and scientization of the national economy. And they helped save 45,000 tons of steel, 407,000 tons of coal, 30,000 tons of oil and large quantities of other fuel and materials, as well as workforce and fund. In this period 1,830 units including 67 factories have received the title of model technical innovation factory (workshop or workteam). Two factories of them have received the title twice. The technical innovation shock brigades were organised at industrial establishments from 1980. They include local skilled workers and technicians. They solve technical problems in production and construction, thus contributing to the economic development of the nation.
