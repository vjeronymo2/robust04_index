920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / Breakfast TV contract awarded THE contract for Channel 4's breakfast television service has been awarded to Planet 24 of which Mr Bob Geldof, the philanthropist and rock star, is a director, in a Pounds 10m, 15-month deal. The new programme, to be called The Big Breakfast, will replace The Channel 4 Daily in September. The most recent audience figures show that 4.9m people watched The Channel 4 Daily, compared with 10.2m for the BBC breakfast service and 14.7m for TV-am.
