Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed "Official" announcement] [Text] Vladimir Fedorovich Rayfikesht has been relieved of the post of Altay Kray administration chief at his own request by an edict of the Russian Federation president as a result of representations by the Russian Federation government chairman.
