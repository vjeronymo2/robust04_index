CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 13 April 1994 on page 10 reports that the National Institute for Agrarian Reform (INRA) is planning to issue land ownership titles for 27 farms totaling 6,545 manzanas in the departments of Granada, Carazo, Masaya, and Rivas. The entire area will be divided among 67 private producers and 52 cooperatives. The ownership titles will benefit 874 heads of families and over 3,000 people, according to the article.
