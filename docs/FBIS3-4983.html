Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vienna, March 3 KYODO -- A seven-member team began Thursday [3 March] inspecting North Korea's nuclear facilities, a spokesman for the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) said. The inspection of seven declared facilities will last through March 15, the spokesman said. The IAEA inspection team arrived in Pyongyang on Tuesday. The inspections followed an agreement reached by the United States and North Korea last Friday at a working-level meeting in New York that Pyongyang fully implement IAEA inspections of its seven declared nuclear plants.
