BFN [Commentary by Yun Pyong-son: "Premeditated Military Buildup Maneuvers"] [Text] The U.S. imperialists' military buildup maneuvers to provoke a new war on the Korean peninsula have become more unscrupulous. According to a report on the testimony at a subcommittee of the U.S. Senate Armed Services Committee on 18 April, (Rowen), assistant secretary of the U.S. Department of Defense, revealed that the United States would deploy anew to South Korea one brigade of military equipment, including tanks, howitzers, and multiple rocket launchers. Stating that the United States is pushing ahead with a new strategy of deploying various types of firing instruments and combat equipment on naval ships in South Korean waters and in the waters surrounding it, (Rowen) babbled that, through this strategy, the United States could more quickly deploy stronger ground forces to battles and that it was correcting the problem of a lengthy deployment of tanks and other heavy weaponry to battle sites, as took place during the Persian Gulf war. This is part of the premeditated military buildup maneuvers designed to take a preemptive attack on our republic and openly reveals the U.S. criminal attempt to ignite a nuclear war. A tense situation still prevails on the Korean peninsula. This is totally because of the treacherous acts by the United States. As everyone knows well, the United States made it clear domestically and internationally through a DPRK-U.S. joint statement that it wants to resolve the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula peacefully, that it will not use force of arms, including nuclear weapons, against the other side, and that it will not even threaten the other side with such a force of arms. Completely overturning the agreement made at the DPRK-U.S. contact in New York, however, the United States is concentrating its large-scale aggression Armed Forces in South Korea and in the area surrounding it, babbling that it will not rule out a preemptive attack against us and that it will suspend the North's nuclear development even at the risk of a war, and, thus, staging every day a provocative war exercise for a northward invasion. Following the recent deployment of offensive-purpose Apache helicopters to South Korea, Patriot missiles arrived in Pusan on 18 April. It is also said that aggression forces and missile equipment loaded on other transport ships will arrive in South Korea soon to complete the deployment of the Patriot missile unit to the frontline area. In addition, U.S. Defense Secretary Perry flew into South Korea and had a conspiratorial war conference with puppet military bosses.
