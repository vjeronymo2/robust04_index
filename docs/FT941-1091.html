940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / Finance and the Family: Allied-Lyons to change name - At a Glance One of the biggest deals of this year was announced by drinks and foods group Allied-Lyons, which is paying Pounds 740m for control of Pedro Domecq group, the Spanish drinks producer. Allied is to finance the purchase with a Pounds 651m rights issue, and plans to change its name to Allied Domecq, dropping the name of J Lyons.
