BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty] 27 April TASS -- China may stop nuclear weapon tests by 1996, visiting State Council Premier Li Peng told a news conference in the Kazakh capital of Alma-Ata on Wednesday. The Chinese leader said his country was now taking an active part in the Geneva talks on armaments control. At present, Russia, the United States, Great Britain and France, which are also parties to these talks, have suspended nuclear tests. The latest regular nuclear test was performed in China last October, after which a rise in background radiation was registered in Kazakh areas bordering on China.
