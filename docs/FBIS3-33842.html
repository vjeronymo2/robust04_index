Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] The Presidency Press Department reported to the BIA that esteemed Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani met this morning with a number of the country's cultural and political personalities. At the meeting a few verses from the Holy Koran were recited by esteemed Mawlana Walajan Wasiq, the deputy minister for publication of the Information and Culture Ministry. The esteemed president of our country then spoke in detail about the political and military situation in the country. A number of the participating personalities, among them esteemed Mawlawi Arsalan Rahmani, the deputy prime minister and the minister of guidance and endowment; esteemed Hojjat ol-Eslam Walmoslemin Seyyed Mohammad 'Ali Javid, the minister of planning; and esteemed Dr. Abdol Elahi, minister counsellor, then [words indistinct]. Then during the course of a comprehensive discussion concerning various drafts, which had been presented to the meeting about the solution of the country's problem, it was decided that such discussions and talks will continue.
