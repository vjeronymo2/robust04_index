CSO [Summary] Beginning 1 April, Jilin Province implemented the following sticker prices for grain and edible oil. In state-run grain stores the sticker price for grade A rice is 0.77 yuan per 500 grams, and grade B rice 0.74 yuan. The market price may not exceed the price in state-run grain stores by 5 fen per 500 grams. The state-run and market price for standard flour is 0.65 yuan. The state-run and market price for soybean oil in Baishan, Tonghua, and Liaonyuan Prefectures is 3.70 yuan, in other prefectures the price is 3.50 yuan.
