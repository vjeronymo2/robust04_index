BFN [Text] Gali, 28 Jun -- An IBERIA correspondent reports from Zugdidi that Abkhaz Armed Forces and civilians have left the territory of Gali Rayon, where Russian peacekeeping troops have been deployed. Only the Gali Rayon governor (an ethnic Abkhaz) and police chief (an ethnic Abkhaz), both of whom were appointed by the Abkhaz side, as well as a small group of their armed bodyguards remain there. It is now calm in Gali Rayon.
