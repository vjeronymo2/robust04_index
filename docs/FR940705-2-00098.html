LEGAL SERVICES CORPORATION Correction of Notice of Intent To Designate Recipient for the Provision of State Support for the Delivery of Legal Services in the State of Louisiana AGENCY: Legal Services Corporation. ACTION: Correction of notice of intention to award grant.

SUMMARY: In a Notice published on May 27, 1994 (59 FR 27585), the Legal Services Corporation announced its intention to make a grant to Louisiana Legal Consortium, Inc. to provide state support services to the Legal Services Corporation's recipient programs in the State of Louisiana. The intended grant for the remainder of 1994 was erroneously stated as $94,314. The correct amount is $78,596.
