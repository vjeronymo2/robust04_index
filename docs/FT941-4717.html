940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Youth wins Pounds 24,000 in sex bias case Ms Karen Bishop, 17, was awarded more than Pounds 24,000 damages yesterday after winning a sex discrimination case against Cooper Thames Ditton, a BMW car franchise holder based in Surrey. An industrial tribunal in Croydon, south London, decided she had been unfairly denied a mechanic's apprenticeship in favour of two 16-year-old boys because she was female. She was 15 at the time. Ms Bishop said after the hearing: 'I passed my test two weeks ago and drive a Mini. I might use some of the money on a new car, but it won't be a BMW.'
