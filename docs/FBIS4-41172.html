BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The peacekeeping operation in the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict zone will start 26 June, Colonel-General Grigoriy Kondratyev, Russia's deputy minister of defense, told INTERFAX. Kondratyev set off for the region today to take personal charge of the peacekeeping mission. Grigoriy Kondratyev said that five Russian battalions totalling 2,500-3,000 servicemen are being brought in for the operation. According to him, two battalions from Batumi have already started moving with their equipment into the area separating the warring sides. The deputy minister also said that military transport aircraft and helicopters started loading the personnel and equipment of two battalions of paratroopers today. Tomorrow, that is, Thursday, the battalions from the Ulyanovsk paratroop division will start leaving for the conflict zone.
