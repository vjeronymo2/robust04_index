BFN [Text] The prime minister has said that India has suggested continuation of dialogue with Pakistan to resolve all outstanding issues, but the continued Pakistan support for terrorism in Jammu and Kashmir is complicating the process of establishing good neighborly relations. Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao made this observation when the visiting foreign minister of Yemen, Mr. Muhammad Salim Basindwah, called on him in New Delhi yesterday. The prime minister expressed appreciation for Yemen's understanding of India's vital concerns.
