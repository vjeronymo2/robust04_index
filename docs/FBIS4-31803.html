BFN [Text] President Zhelev invited ex-Deputy Prime Minister Valentin Karabashev, in his capacity as an expert on privatization, to give his views on the amendments to the Privatization Act that the National Assembly passed. Karabashev advised Dr. Zhelev to return the law to the National Assembly for further drafting because the approved changes contain a number of imperfections. He voiced serious objections concerning the chapter on mass privatization. After his meeting with President Zhelev, Valentin Karabashev declared that he is optimistic that the existing imperfections in the law can be removed when parliament reconsiders it.
