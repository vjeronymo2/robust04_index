Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The parliament of the Republic of Georgia reconvened today [17 March] and was chaired by the Deputy Speaker Vakhtang Rcheulishvili. [name as heard] Eduard Shevardnadze, the chairman of the Georgian State Council and head of state addressed parliament on the procedure of confirming the Cabinet of Ministers to be discussed by parliament on Tuesday 22 March. The agenda was confirmed. The first issue on the agenda was a resolution on high schools addressed by the Chairman of the Education Commission Raul Kuprava. The issue caused heated debates. At the moment parliament is having a break.
