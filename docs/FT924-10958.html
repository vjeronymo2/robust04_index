921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Warnford declines 12% to Pounds 3.67m Warnford Investments, the property investment group, saw pre-tax profits fall 12 per cent, from Pounds 4.17m to Pounds 3.67m, for the six months to June 24. Gross rents and charges dipped to Pounds 6m (Pounds 6.25m). The interim dividend is unchanged at 2.75p, payable from earnings of 6.35p (7.45p) per share.
