February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Former football coach Joe Morrison and his staff at South Carolina "had to know or they were blind" that some players were taking steroids, according to documents released by the school.

Morrison acknowledged several months before his death of a heart attack last year that he had heard rumors of players using steroids. But the university's recently released report to the NCAA offers support to the belief that the popular coach looked the other way when it came to steroids.
