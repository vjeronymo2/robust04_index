Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Zimbabwe's minister of foreign affairs, Dr. Nathan Shamuyarira, says his government has agreed to a United Nations request for a second Zimbabwean contingent to be sent to Somalia. Dr. Shamuyarira said the UN had asked Zimbabwe for more troops because of the good work the first contingent had accomplished in Somalia. He said the new deployment would consist of about 1,000 soldiers. The UN and Zimbabwe are also conducting talks about sending Zimbabwean troops to Liberia.
