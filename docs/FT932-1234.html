930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Hardys edges up to Pounds 3.32m Hardys & Hansons, the Nottingham-based brewer, reported a marginal increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.23m to Pounds 3.32m for the 26 weeks to April 2. The company said the market had remained difficult and sales were down in line with trends in the east Midlands. Turnover rose to Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 14.3m). However, net interest and dividends received were down at Pounds 338,000 (Pounds 488,000). Exceptional profits, relating to the sale of fixed assets, were also lower at Pounds 47,000 (Pounds 66,000). Comparative figures have been restated to under FRS 3. The interim dividend is increased to 3p (2.8p) from earnings of 8.975p (8.743p).
