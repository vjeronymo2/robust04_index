Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Taba, 16 Feb (MENA)-- The Security and Civilian Affairs Committees for the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations to implement the Gaza-Jericho agreement resumed meetings in Taba today in an atmosphere of optimism among members of both delegations. General Amnon Shahaq, deputy chief of staff and chairman of the Israeli delegation to the talks, has stated that the two sides are discussing the items on the agenda to reach an integrated agreement on the various issues pertaining to the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement. After arriving in Taba this morning, Shahaq said that no agreement will be signed regarding the Palestinian police force but an exhaustive agreement will be signed once negotiations are concluded. MENA has learned that the Civilian Affairs Committee has been divided into two subcommittees. The basic committee is cochaired by the lawyer Jamil al-Tarifi and General Gadi Zohar, the official in charge of the civilian administration in the West Bank. It is discussing the transfer of authority over electricity and civilian planning. The subcommittee on communications is cochaired by Radwan Abu-'Ayyash, head of the Palestinian radio and television authority, and Shlomo Fax, head of the Israeli telecommunications authority [name and title as received]. This subcommittee is discussing all the issues pertaining to the establishment of a Palestinian radio and television, radio and television research, telephone communications, and an international code number for the future Palestinian telephone exchange authority. The Security Committee has held a meeting in the residence of the Palestinian delegation cochaired by Nabil Sha'th, adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, and General Amnon Shahaq. MENA has learned that the two delegations are carrying their leaderships' reply to the joint draft reached last night on the Palestinian police force and on security cooperation and coordination. At its meeting today, the committee will discuss the timetable for the release of all Palestinians jailed in Israeli prisons in accordance with the provisions of the Gaza-Jericho agreement.
