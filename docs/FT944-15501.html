941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Saville Gordon J Saville Gordon Group, the property investment concern, said it was a member of Northern Natural Resources, which in turn is a member of Mining (Scotland), the preferred bidder for Scottish Coal. It has agreed to subscribe up to Pounds 3m by way of equity capital if the bid is successful. That would represent about 10 per cent of Mining Scotland's equity. JSG's investment in Northern Natural Resources was Pounds 11,700.
