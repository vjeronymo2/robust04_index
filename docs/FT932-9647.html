930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / Libya urges Rushdie boycott Libya called yesterday for an Islamic boycott of Britain following a meeting between Prime Minister John Major and the author Mr Salman Rushdie, Reuter reports from Tunis. Mr Rushdie has described his private 40-minute meeting with Mr Major on Tuesday as the most important step so far in his campaign against a religious edict condemning him to death after Tehran said his novel, The Satanic Verses, blasphemed Islam.
