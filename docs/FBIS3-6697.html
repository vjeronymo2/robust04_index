Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 21 KYODO -- Japan and Vietnam on Monday [21 March] signed a diplomatic note in Hanoi for a Japanese grant of 3 billion yen, the Foreign Ministry said. The grant will be provided as part of Japan's international commitment made at the 1992 Munich summit of the world's seven leading economic powers, which promised grant money to developing countries, the ministry said. The grant is the first of its kind to Vietnam and will be used to help Vietnam pay for purchases of foreign goods.
