Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India's medium-range missile Agni will be test fired on Friday [7 January] from Chandipur in Orissa. According to a notification issued in Balasore yesterday, the third test firing of the surface-to-surface missile would be done between 0900 [0330 GMT] to 1600 [1030 GMT]. The Agni was first test fired at Chandipur on 22 May 1989.
