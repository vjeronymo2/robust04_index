Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zheng Qingdong (6774 1987 2639)] [Text] Beijing, 9 Feb (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan and Hu Jintao, members of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee, called on Comrades Song Ping, Bo Yibo, and Song Renqiong on the morning of 7 February to extend Spring Festival greetings and cordial regards to them on behalf of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council. Li Ruihuan and Hu Jintao warmly inquired about the living and health conditions of the three veteran comrades, briefed them on the current domestic situation and the basic principle for work in 1994, and also expressed the hope that the veteran comrades will make more suggestions concerning the work of the party Central Committee and the State Council so as to help the central authorities do their work well. After asking Li Ruihuan and Hu Jintao to convey their thanks and New Year's greetings to leading comrades of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council, Song Ping, Bo Yibo, and Song Renqiong said: Since the Third Plenary Session of the 11th CPC Central Committee, the situation in our country has been improving day by day. Its economy has been growing rapidly; its overall strength has expanded somewhat, and the people's living standards have improved to some extent. We must further emancipate our minds, seek truth from facts, continue to advance along the road of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, strive to advance to a new level, and strive to fulfill the second-step strategic objective ahead of schedule to win still greater victories! Those who accompanied Li Ruihuan and Hu Jintao to call on the veteran comrades also included Zeng Qinghong, director of the CPC Central Committee General Office, and Lu Feng, director of the CPC Central Committee Organizational Department.
