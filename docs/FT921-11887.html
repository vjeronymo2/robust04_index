920129 FT 29 JAN 92 / UK Company News: GT Japan net asset value drops 21% Over the six months to December 31 net asset value of GT Japan Investment Trust fell by 21 per cent, from 235.4p to 185.6p. In the latest half year gross income totalled Pounds 697,000 (Pounds 529,000) and earnings per share came to 0.44p (0.43p). The interim dividend is again 0.4p.
