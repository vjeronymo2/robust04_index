940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / People: Anglia Television David McCall has been appointed chairman of Anglia Television on the retirement of Sir Peter Gibbings, and to the boards of MAI, and MAI Broadcasting. Malcolm Wall, deputy chief executive of Meridian, has been appointed md of Anglia, and Mike Hughes, deputy chief executive of Anglia, has been appointed resource and development director of MAI Broadcasting and a director of Meridian.
