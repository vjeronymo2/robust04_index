CSO [Text] About 150 officials of the Bankura and Asansol divisions and the State Excise Department's Narcotics Cell destroyed about 5,000 kg of opium and 45,000 kg of opium seeds valued at about Rs 5.5 crores in the Sonamukhki area of Bankura on Sunday. The 13-hour operation was led by Mr. D.P. Jana, Special Superintendent of the Excise Department.
