941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Observer: Five-gear Pluto Calling all Ford drivers - watch out for re-entry problems. The US carmaker has just signed a deal with a Nasa research centre to share the space agency's technology. Of particular interest to Ford, apparently, are Nasa's skills in advanced materials, air-flow measurement methods and adhesive bonding. Not so much fly me to the moon, perhaps, as Escort me interstellar-wise.
