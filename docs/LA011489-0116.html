January 14, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

CAM Data Systems reported a loss of $701,000 for its fiscal year ended Sept. 30, 1988, contrasted with earnings of $103,000 a year earlier. The Fountain Valley producer of inventory control and tracking systems had revenue of $3.4 million, up 53% from $2.2 million a year ago. For the fourth quarter, CAM Data earned $30,000 on revenue of $1.35 million, contrasted with a loss of $39,000 on revenue of $573,000 a year ago.
