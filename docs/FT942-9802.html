940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury buoyed by interest cut Shaftesbury, the property investment company, reported pre-tax profits sharply higher at Pounds 1.28m for the six months to March 31, against Pounds 344,000. The main factor was a Pounds 720,000 fall in net interest charges to Pounds 1.4m. Earnings per share benefited from a tax refund of Pounds 427,000, coming out at 3.5p (1.2p). There is no interim dividend, but the capital reduction during the period will enable a final payment to be recommended. Sales of properties were lower at Pounds 800,000 (Pounds 1.97m) as turnover fell to Pounds 4.13m (Pounds 5.22m).
