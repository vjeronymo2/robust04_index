BFN [Text] Soldiers of the Korea People's Army unit to which Comrade Sin Sung-chik belongs are striving for the construction of the Pyongyang-Hyangsan Expressway upholding the order from the respected and beloved comrade supreme commander. The soldiers are putting more efforts in the construction of the Chongpung River Bridge. The soldiers have accelerated the construction in their resolve to complete the newly built Chongpung River Bridge as a modern creation of the chuche era. Recently, they are vigorously accelerating concreting the road after completing the V-type pillar in early April. Technician functionaries of the unit are strengthening guidance to every process of the construction while making higher demands for all the soldiers to guarantee the construction quality. The soldiers are also speeding up the construction bearing an iron-like faith that "What the party decides, we will do."
