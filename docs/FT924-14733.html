921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Deficit near Pounds 1m at Select Appts Losses at Select Appointments (Holdings), the USM-quoted recruitment group, increased from Pounds 508,000 to Pounds 948,000 pre-tax during the half-year ended June 30. Turnover was Pounds 9.8m (Pounds 10.08m). Mr Michael Franks, chairman, said trading conditions continued to be 'extremely difficult'. Permanent job vacancies registered with the group fell by 30 per cent. However, Mr Franks said Select actually filled more vacancies during the half than in the comparable period of 1991. First-half losses per share worked through at 0.44p (2.4p). NEWS DIGEST
