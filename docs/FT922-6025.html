920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: JMD cuts losses to Pounds 89,000 JMD Group, the greeting cards maker and novelty products merchandiser, cut its pre-tax loss from Pounds 1.11m to just Pounds 89,000 in 1991. The USM-quoted group made a profit of Pounds 74,000 (Pounds 527,000) before exceptional charges of Pounds 163,000 (Pounds 150,000), and last year before loss on discontinued activities of Pounds 1.49m. Sir Cecil Burney, chairman, said there was still a need to rebuild liquidity but he remained encouraged by the performance of Downpace, which was a good cash generating business and returned profits before management charges and tax of Pounds 400,000 in the year. Turnover fell to Pounds 3.34m (Pounds 4.69m). Losses per share were 0.05p (1.44p).
