Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 10 (SNARK)--Vahan Papazyan, Armenian foreign affairs minister, mentioned today that the appeal to the terror and bloodshed should be punished. Recently speaking in Vanadzor Garnik Isagulyan, deputy of the Armenian Parliament and a representative of the "Dashnaktsutyun" Party [Armenia Revolutionary Federation], accused Armenian authorities declaring that "the Foreign Affairs Minister Papazyan did with the pornoproduction [word as received] trade". In his turn Vahan Papazyan appealed to the "Dashnaktsutyun" making the party specify the attitude to Isagulyan and expressed his sorrow in connection with the fact that "Armenian Parliament is not only unable to express his attitude but to accuse the deputy of his declarations".
