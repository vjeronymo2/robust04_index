BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] Presidency Minister Antonio Lacayo said on Friday that the implementation of economic adjustment measures will provide financing for the agriculture and production sectors. Lacayo, while visiting several communities in La Trinidad, Esteli Department, where he inaugurated several projects, said the agreement with the IMF should definitely be signed. Lacayo has said on previous occasions that with or without the support of the Sandinist National Liberation Front, the adjustment agreement should be implemented. He has also characterized Daniel Ortega's objections as political populism.
