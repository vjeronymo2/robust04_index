Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 21 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, sent thanks to the employees of the Pyongyang Maternity Hospital, the Yongsong district commercial management office and the Potonggang district restaurant management office for their sincere assistance to the People's Army. The officials and employees of the above-mentioned units established the "our post-our office" relations with units of the People's Army in hearty response to the party's idea of army-people unity and have visited these units, on several times, deepening feelings of unity between the army and the people.
