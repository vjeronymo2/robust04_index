BFN [Text] A Sydney-based Australian company has negotiated a telecommunication deal with China which is believed to be worth up to $50 million [currency not specified] per year. (Stand Alite Electronics) has signed a memorandum of understanding with China's System Engineering Electronic Corporation, a semi-government body. The corporation is the largest dealer in communication equipment in China, supplying the railway, defense, and oil industries. Under the deal, (Stand Alite) will design and install cellular radio equipment as well as supplying support servicing. The company has been conducting research in China for two years to assess the country's communication needs and to develop systems to match. (Stand Alite) has already sold one radio system to the Shanghai Port Authority and has two cellular mobile telephone systems installed in Shantou and Maoming in China's Guandong province.
