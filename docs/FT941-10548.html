940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / PIA ombudsman head named Baroness Turner of Camden, Labour employment spokesman in the House of Lords, is to chair the ombudsman scheme for the Personal Investment Authority, the new retail financial services regulator, the PIA board said yesterday. The scheme will be based on recommendations from Lord Ackner, a former law lord. The ombudsman will be able to make awards of up to Pounds 50,000 and may recommend in particular cases an amount beyond that.
