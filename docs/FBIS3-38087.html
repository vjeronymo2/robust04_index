Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Oslo February 25 ITAR-TASS -- Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev arrived here today on a working visit. At the airport he was welcomed by Norwegian Defence Minister Jorgen Kosmo. The ministers discussed security in Northern Europe and possible bilateral cooperation of the armed forces on the Kola Peninsula. Grachev and Kosmo laid wreaths to the monument to Russian servicemen killed in Norway during the Second World War. A meeting of Pavel Grachev and Norwegian Foreign Minister Bjorn Tore Godal focused on bilateral cooperation, ecological problems of the Novaya Zemlya and settlement of the Bosnian crisis. Grachev will also visit the Norwegian parliament to meet heads of several parliamentary committees. On Saturday he will leave for Lillehammer to see the Winter Olympics.
