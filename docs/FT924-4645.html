921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Brokers Association reports an active and dearer market. Landed Assams were well supported and prices continued to advance by up to 10 pence and often more. Bangladesh teas sold readily at firm rates. East Africans met very strong competition with medium descriptions 10 to 15p dearer and brightest liquoring sorts an equally strong feature. Offshore good general demand at about last levels. The highest price realised this week was 212p for a Rwanda pf. 1 and pd. Quotations quality 200p; nom good medium 170p; medium 155p; low medium 105p.
