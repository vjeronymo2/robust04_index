Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Throughout the holidays the operational situation on the Tajik-Afghan border remained relatively calm. According to a report by the press center of the command of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan, no armed provocations were registered on any of the sectors. Incidentally, it notes that the Tajik opposition armed formations continue to mass on the Afghan side. In connection with this, the outposts of the Moskovskiy and Pyandzh detachments of the Russian border troops and also subunits of Tajikistan's National Security Committee are on enhanced combat readiness.
