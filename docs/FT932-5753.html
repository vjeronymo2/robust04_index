930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Govett American Endeavour Fund GOVETT AMERICAN Endeavour Fund, the fixed life fund which invests in small companies in the US, had a net asset value per share of 169 cents at end-March against 168 cents a year earlier. Net profits for the year were Dollars 6.32m (Pounds 4.1m) compared with Dollars 6.82m, for earnings per share of 11.61 cents (12.52 cents). The proposed final dividend is held at 5.52 cents for a total of 11.04 cents (12.51 cents).
