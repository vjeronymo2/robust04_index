BFN [Excerpt] Agricultural departments in al-Najaf Governorate have begun plowing the areas where wheat and barley have just been harvested in order to prepare them for the cultivation of rice. The necessary requirements have been provided, including fertilizers, seeds, and plowing machines. Meetings and discussions are also being held to adopt scientific methods of cultivation, and peasants and farmers are being urged to redouble their efforts in order to cultivate vast areas of land.
