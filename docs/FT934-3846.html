931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Acal little changed at Pounds 1.3m Acal, the USM-traded electronics and industrial control manufacturer, returned profits of Pounds 1.31m pre-tax for the half year to September 31, marginally lower than last time's Pounds 1.34m. Turnover of Pounds 37.7m (Pounds 30.9m) was bolstered by UK growth, a six months' contribution from Transtech and exchange gains. The interim dividend is lifted to 2.25p (2.1p) from earnings of 5.6p (6.3p) per share.
