April 29, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

STILL A CROWDED HOUSE

Regarding the Crowded House review by Chris Willman ("Crowded House: Raising the Roof," Calendar April 8): If Willman thought that Crowded House's concert Thursday at the Pantages Theatre was great, he should have seen them Friday or Saturday!

BETH CLARY (No. 1 Fan)

Balboa
