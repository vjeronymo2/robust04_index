November 20, 1989, Monday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SMALL QUAKE CENTERED NEAR GRANADA HILLS

A small earthquake shook the San Fernando Valley on Sunday, but Caltech Seismology Lab officials said there were no reports of injuries or property damage.

The quake, which struck at 12:39 p.m., measured 2.5 and was centered 1 mile northeast of Granada Hills. Police said they received several calls from residents who felt the earthquake.
