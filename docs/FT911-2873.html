910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Scot Met Prop falls to Pounds 2.72m Sharply higher interest charges at Scottish Metropolitan Property in the six months to February 15 resulted in a drop in taxable profits from Pounds 5.55m to Pounds 2.72m. The decline followed a 16 per cent fall in the previous 12 months to Pounds 10.43m. In the period under review revenue from properties rose 17 per cent to Pounds 9.51m (Pounds 8.11m). Interest payments jumped to Pounds 6.57m (Pounds 2.54m). The interim dividend is maintained at 2.53p on earnings per share of 1.86p (3.97p).
