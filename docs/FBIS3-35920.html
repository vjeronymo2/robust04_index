Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 27 (TURAN)--The government of the UK has decided to give Azerbaijan special equipment, including computers and computer training read the official report from the British Embassy in Baku. This equipment will enable Azerbaijan to switch over to the communication system within the CSCE framework. This communication system will provide direct, uninterrupted 24-hour communication among the capitals of the CSCE-member states. The UK Embassy states that this communication system makes possible the exchange of information within the framework of the Vienna agreement on the Reduction of Conventional Weapons in Europe. The report also states that in contrast with offers to the other CIS states, no other state offered assistance to Azerbaijan in this matter. Taking this fact into consideration, the UK decided to give this equipment to Azerbaijan. The British equipment will be delivered to Baku and begin functioning in the near future.
