BFN [From the "Seven O'Clock Newscast"] [Text] Attention: Radioactive material apparently was stolen from a Buenaventura warehouse, and it could have endangered the lives of many Valle Department inhabitants. Touching the material or simply being close to it entails a serious risk. Doctors at Valle University Hospital warn that those who have handled this radioactive material known as strontium 90 are in danger of contracting skin cancer, gangrene, or nerve cells damage. Cali Police have arrested several people who attempted to sell the material to the hospital, but it is believed that more people could have been exposed to the strontium 90.
