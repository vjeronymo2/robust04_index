930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Kinnock joins reform campaign MR Neil Kinnock, the former Labour leader, has joined the Labour Campaign for Electoral Reform, significantly boosting the lobby group, as the party's special inquiry into electoral systems nears a conclusion, the LCER said last night. Meanwhile, Labour's national executive committee will today consider lifting a ban on party members taking up seats on the management boards of opted-out hospitals and schools.
