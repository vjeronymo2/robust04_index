Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Last year, the development of township and town enterprises in our province was at an all-time high, with their total production output value reaching approximately 17.7 billion yuan, an increase of 58.37 percent over 1992. Total income in this sector was approximately 15.2 billion yuan, an increase of 53.36 percent. Peasants' per capita wage income from township and town enterprises has increased by 130 yuan, accounting for about one quarter of the peasants' per capita net income. Last year, following the official promulgation of the State Council's decision on speeding up the development of township and town enterprises in the central and western regions, the whole province made the most of the resulting favorable situation to set off an upsurge of running township and town enterprises. Throughout the province, township and town enterprises in 14 counties, cities, and prefectures saw their development speed increase by more than 80 percent. In seven counties and prefectures, township and town enterprises doubled their pace of development. Township and town enterprises in Jiayuguan city increased their speed by 124 percent. On this basis, various localities made use of the new characteristic of the rapid development of township and town enterprises to transform them based on joint-stock and collective ownership to further define the relations between production and administrative power and tap investment potential. According to statistics, there are more than 3,700 large township and town enterprises under joint- stock collective ownership, and more than 1.2 billion yuan has been brought in. In the course of developing township and town enterprises, various localities have generally attached importance to scientific and technical input. Last year, through various channels the whole province trained 24,000 qualified personnel with different expertise for township and town enterprises, 269 of whom won middle ranking professional and technical titles for their posts.
