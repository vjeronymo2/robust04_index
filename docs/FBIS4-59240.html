BFN [Text] New York, 12 May (INA)--Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz will today begin a series of political talks with the president and members of the UN Security Council on lifting the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq. INA correspondent in New York said that Tariq 'Aziz will meet with Ibrahim Gambari, Nigeria's ambassador to the United Nations and current UN Security Council president, at the UN headquarters in New York today. Later today, the deputy prime minister is scheduled to separately meet with China's Ambassador Li Zhaoxing, French Ambassador Jean-Bernard Merimee, and Omani Ambassador Salim Bin-Muhammad al-Qusaybi. Tariq 'Aziz arrived in New York last night from Vienna after he held talks with Hans Blix, director general of the International Atomic Energy Agency.
