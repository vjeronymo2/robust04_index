930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Finance and the Family: Age Concern focus on rights - At a glance The charity Age Concern has designated April 19 - 23 'Your Rights Week' in an attempt to ensure that older people are made aware of all the benefits to which they are entitled. A book, Your Rights 1993-94, costs Pounds 2.50 from Dept YR3, Age Concern England, Astral House, 1268 London Road, London SW16 4ER.
