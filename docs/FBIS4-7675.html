BFN [Text] Tirana, April 3 (ATA) -- The FLAKA E VLLAZERIMIT newspaper will come out daily after the Workers Council of the Nova Macedonia Publishing House approved on April 1 a proposal of its staff. Albanian parliamentarians and ministers as well as the Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Government made a significant contribution in supporting the FLAKA E VLLAZERIMIT, a newspaper in Albanian language. The newspaper is expected to start this month. FLAKA E VLLAZERIMIT will issue six copies for each week and one of the copies will be in a summary form. The newspaper will be financed for a certain time by its own but in the future it is expected to receive a financial assistance by the Macedonian state.
