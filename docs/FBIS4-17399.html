BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow, 22 Apr -- Belan Khamchiyev, the permanent representative of the Ingush Republic under the Russian president, told the ITAR-TASS correspondent that the latest events on the border of the two republics once again prove that the Ossetian side is aiming for mass bloodshed. According to him, North Ossetia is shutting off all prospects for the negotiation process. In the situation that has emerged, Khamchiyev believes the entire responsibility for the failure of the latest round of negotiations between the leaders of the two republics rests upon the top leaders of North Ossetia and Russia. Given that all possible means for a peaceful settlement have been virtually exhausted, it is expedient at the next sitting of the Federation Council to consider the question of introducing amendments to the Russian president's decree on introducing a state of emergency and to introduce direct federal rule on the territory of Prigorodnyy Rayon.
