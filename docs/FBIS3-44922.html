Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bangkok, January 11 (XINHUA) -- General Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Chinese Central Military Commission, and his entourage left Bangkok this morning for Indonesia by way of Singapore. Liu, who arrived here on January 6, was the highest-ranking Chinese military leader to have visited Thailand. While in this country, Liu was granted an audience by his majesty the king, and was conferred an order of the knight, first class, by Prime Minister Chuan Likphai on behalf of the king, for his outstanding contribution to the good relations between the armed forces of the two countries. Liu also visited air, naval and special warfare bases of the Royal Thai Armed Forces.
