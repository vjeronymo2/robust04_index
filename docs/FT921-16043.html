920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Airtours links with five agencies Airtours, the package holiday company which was considering buying a travel agency chain, has signed long-term agreements with five large agencies. The agreements, which will operate for three to five years, secure the distribution of Airtours' holidays at commission of less than 12.5 per cent. Lunn Poly is one of the five, as is Pickfords Travel Services. The other three are Thomas Cook & Sons, AT Mays and Hogg Robinson.
