January 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

TRAFFIC CAMERA

The automatic camera being tested by the city of Pasadena, as well as its photo radar, would be considerably less useful if traffic lights were properly synchronized. I believe that the principal attraction of these devices is their revenue-generating potential. The problem for us drivers is that the city gets more return on its "investment" by not synchronizing the lights.

R.L. MITCHELL

Rolling Hills Estates
