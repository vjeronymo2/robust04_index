BFN [Commentary by News Research and Commentary Group] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] In the campaign against narcotics, law enforcement forces discovered and seized a total of 90 tons of various drugs and arrested 459 ringleaders of smuggling bands. In intelligence operations, two tons of narcotics bound for Europe were discovered. In the 11 months of 1372, 65 cases of clashes with insurgents and smugglers occurred, mostly in Sistan va Baluchestan, Kerman, and southern Khorasan. During this period, 14,600 smugglers were arrested and 43,000 addicts were sent to prison. The discoveries in the year 1372 register an increase compared to the corresponding period of the preceding year. The deputy secretary general of the United Nations and the director of narcotics control commended the activities of the Islamic Republic of Iran during the past year in its campaign against the transit of drugs.
