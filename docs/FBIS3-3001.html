Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 15 (XINHUA) -- Jiang Minkuan, deputy head of the United Front Work Department of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee, left here today for Spain to attend the 33rd Congress of the Spanish Socialist Workers' Party (SSWP). Jiang, also a member of the CPC Central Committee, has been invited by the SSWP. He was seen off at the airport by Li Chengren, deputy head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee.
