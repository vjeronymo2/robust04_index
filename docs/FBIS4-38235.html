BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Jun (MENA)--Cairo Lawyers Association Chairman 'Abd-al-'Aziz Muhammmad praised yesterday's decision by the Public Prosecution Office to release all the lawyers held in detention since 17 May following the demonstration at the Lawyers Association's premises. Speaking at a news conference today, 'Abd-al-'Aziz said that he and the rest of his colleagues who were on a hunger strike decided to end their hunger strike immediately after the release of the detained lawyers was announced. 'Abd-al-'Aziz said that the competent authorities showed particular sympathy to the demands of the hunger strikers, chief of which was the release of all the lawyers arrested following the demonstration.
