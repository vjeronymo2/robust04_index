BFN [Text] Moscow June 15 ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin today set up a commission to prepare a bilateral treaty between the Russian Federation and one of its constituent parts, the Republic of Bashkortostan. The treaty is due to divide powers between the federation and its component republic and decide on the delegation of powers between the bodies of state authorities of Russia and Bashkortostan, the presidential press service announced today. Vice-Premier Sergey Shakhray is appointed to head the commission. Earlier, Moscow signed a treaty with Tatarstan, another component republic of the Russian Federation.
