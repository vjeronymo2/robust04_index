BFN [Text] Clashes between the soldiers of the Fifth Corps [Bosnia-Herzegovina Army] loyal to the Muslim authorities in Sarajevo and the forces of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia [APZB] were resumed this morning with undiminished intensity, particularly in Caglica in the municipality of Velika Kladusa and in Mutevelici and Pecigrad in the Cazin municipality, local media of both warring sides have reported, without mentioning the casualty toll. According to Serb intelligence sources, 68 APZB and 36 Fifth Corps soldiers were killed over the past five days in fierce inter-Muslim clashes. According to Muslim Radio Bihac, about 400 APZB soldiers were captured during the latest inter-Muslim clashes.
