September 28, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SONORA'S RECORD IS 1-3 AFTER LOSS TO DUARTE

George Frazier scored two touchdowns to lead Duarte High School to a 19-0 victory over Sonora in a nonleague game Thursday night at Duarte.

Frazier opened the scoring for Duarte (3-1) with a 16-yard touchdown run in the first quarter and closed the scoring with a 95-yard return of a fumble in the second quarter.

Sonora is 1-3.
