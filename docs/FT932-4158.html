930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Carthage Cup WADDINGTON (JOHN) has bought Carthage Cup, which makes high value cups, plates and bowls in Texas, for Dollars 5.4m (Pounds 3.5m). In 1992 Carthage made profits of Dollars 1m on turnover of Dollars 17m. Net assets at the year end amounted to Dollars 4m.
