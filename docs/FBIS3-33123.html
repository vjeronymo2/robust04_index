Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Erica Ramirez from the "National Newscast"] [Text] Presidency Spokesman Dr. Danilo Lacayo confirmed today that he has tendered his resignation to President Chamorro. [Begin Lacayo recording] I leave with affection and respect for the president to dedicate myself to other tasks. Wherever I may be, I will always cooperate with the national project, as I have stated in my letter of resignation. [end recording] Dr. Lacayo appraised the government's performance during the past years. [Begin Lacayo recording] The president of the Nicaraguan people has performed an enormous task by ending a war, entering a period of peace, and building such a difficult democracy. The president has done a great job. Obviously, this is not the most perfect government in the world, but we can say that this administration has given us hope for the first time. It has helped us to achieve reconciliation. Above all, the president of the Republic of Nicaragua is determined to build democratic values and institutions and -- as she has always said -- she is carrying out a mission. It is a difficult mission but, it is the mission that she was given. [end recording]
