October 24, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY PEOPLE

* Larry Smart was appointed president and chief executive of Western Data Systems, a computer software producer in Calabasas. He succeeds Richard C. Lemons, who resigned to start R. C. Lemons Associates, a consulting firm, but who remains a Western Data director.

Smart has been with software companies for 18 years, most recently as chief executive of Transettlements.
