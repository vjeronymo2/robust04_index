930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / People: Non-executive directors Allan Shiach (above right), chairman and chief executive of Macallan-Glenlivet, at SCOTTISH TELEVISION. He is chairman of both The Scottish Film Council and The Scottish Film Production Fund and a former member of the BBC's Broadcasting Council (Scotland) and chairman of The Writers' Guild of Great Britain. Under the pen-name of Allan Scott he wrote the screenplay of Don't Look Now and The Witches.
