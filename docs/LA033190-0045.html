March 31, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN RENEWS BID TO CLINCH BERTH IN WIVA PLAYOFFS

The Cal State Northridge men's volleyball team will make its second attempt to clinch its first Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn. postseason tournament berth when the Matadors play host to No. 5 Hawaii at 7:30 tonight at CSUN.

The Matadors (11-8 overall, 6-6 in the DeGroot Division) failed to clinch a berth Friday night when they were surprised by lowly UC Irvine (7-11, 2-11) in five games.

Last week, Hawaii pulled off a four-game upset of top-ranked USC, which knocked off sixth-ranked CSUN the previous night.

Fifth-ranked Hawaii is 14-7 overall and 9-3 and in fourth place in WIVA's Wilson Division.
