Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--Syrian Information Minister Muhammad Salman has stated that Presidents Husni Mubarak and Hafiz al-Asad will meet in the next few days. In a statement following his meeting with President Mubarak today, Salman said consultations are continuing between the two countries on various issues. He said he conveyed greetings from President al-Asad to President Mubarak. [passage omitted]
