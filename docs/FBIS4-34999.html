BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas has said he is very pleased with Dr. Jorge Carpizo's decision to continue as interior secretary and president of the Federal Electoral Institute. The president also expressed his satisfaction with the people's support for Dr. Carpizo, support which demonstrates, he has said, the broad resolve to progress with democracy in accordance with the law and without violence. Salinas has also reiterated his total support for Dr. Carpizo to continue leading the electoral process in the next few months. Salinas said the recent public expressions of support for Dr. Carpizo's independent, party, and honest attitude reaffirm the conviction the country will meet the task of holding clean and impartial elections in accordance with the law and with respect for the country's political pluralism.
