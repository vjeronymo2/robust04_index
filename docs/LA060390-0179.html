June 3, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SOFTBALL SOUTHERN SECTION CHAMPIONSHIPS;

MARINA WINS 5-A TITLE WITH RUN IN NINTH

Marina High School of Huntington Beach scored on an error in the bottom of the ninth inning for a 1-0 victory and the Southern Section 5-A softball championship at Mayfair Park in Lakewood Saturday night.

Catcher Christa Yorke led off with single, went to second base on a groundball and took third on a wild pitch.

Janet Miyahara hit a shot to center fielder Erin Mutch, who dropped the ball, allowing Yorke to score.

Marina's Miyahara (16-1) got the victory as the Vikings improved to 28-5. Newbury Park ended the season 24-8.

Outstanding defensive plays by both teams prevented runs from scoring.

Newbury Park's Lynn Anderson ripped a high liner up the middle with two on base in the fifth inning, but second baseman Julie Muche made a running back hand overhead stab to save a hit.

Melody Bolger's running catch saved a Marina run in the fifth inning. DONNA CARTER
