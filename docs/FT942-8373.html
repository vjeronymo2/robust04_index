940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Goldsborough nears Pounds 2m In its first results since obtaining a listing in March, Goldsborough Healthcare reported pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 965,000 to Pounds 1.91m for the half year to April 3. Turnover for this provider of independent healthcare rose by 40 per cent to Pounds 19.3m. Earnings emerged at 6.4p (2.9p). As foreshadowed in the prospectus, there is no interim dividend but the board intends to pay a final of not less than 2.2p. Net borrowings at the period end totalled Pounds 6.3m, giving gearing of 11.8 per cent.
