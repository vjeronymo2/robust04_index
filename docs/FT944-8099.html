941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Kumagi Gumi and Cubiertas y Mzov and Entrecanales - Contracts & ventures A consortium, consisting of Kumagai Gumi of Japan and Cubiertas y Mzov and Entrecanales, both of Spain, has been awarded a HKDollars 2.6bn (Dollars 336.3m) civil engineering contract to build an underground railway station in Kowloon, part of the 34km rail link to Hong Kong's new HKDollars 158bn airport complex, Mass Transit Railway (MTRC) announced. MTRC also said that a German-Spanish consortium, consisting of AEG Aktiengesellschaft and CAF, had been awarded a HKDollars 1.6bn rolling stock contract to build 11 express and 12 ordinary trains for the airport link. Thirty-one large contracts are expected to be awarded by MTRC, which is responsible for building the rail link.
