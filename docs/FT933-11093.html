930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: Bentley given limited pardon Derek Bentley, hanged 40 years ago for murdering a policeman, has been granted a posthumous pardon limited to sentence, home secretary Michael Howard announced. Mr Howard said he believed a free pardon remained inappropriate. Bentley's accomplice, who fired the shots that killed the policeman, was too young to be executed.
