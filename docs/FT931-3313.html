930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Iranian jets 'bomb Kurdish hospital' THE WIFE of French president Francois Mitterrand, Mrs Danielle Mitterrand, said yesterday at least six Kurdish civilians were killed when Iranian aircraft bombed a French-run hospital on the Iran-Iraq border at the weekend, Reuter reports from Paris. Mrs Mitterrand, head of a privately-funded human rights group said more bodies were being sought at Raniya after air raids on Saturday and Sunday.
