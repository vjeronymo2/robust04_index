BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers Press Center issued a statement today in connection with the statement that Professor Krustyu Petkov made today at a news conference of the KNSB [Confederation of Independent Trade Unions in Bulgaria] in which he claimed that the telephones of the trade unions that are preparing to conduct national protest actions on 4 May are tapped. The statement denies the KNSB claim. In its own announcement, the Interior Ministry Press Center points out such actions are not allowed, as they would violate the Law on Special Intelligence-Gathering Devices.
