931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Shipowners act European shipowners yesterday sought to defuse a dispute with their customers over freight rates across the North Atlantic, writes Charles Batchelor in London. European Union ministers will later today review a special exemption granted to shipowners which allows them to group together in 'conferences' to agree prices on certain routes. Belgium, as president of the European Union, is expected tofile a motion confirming the exemption for conferences.
