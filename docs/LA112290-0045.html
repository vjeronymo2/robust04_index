November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: BABY WATCH

Life Goes On: Patti LuPone gave birth Wednesday to a baby boy, Joshua Luke Johnston.

The child was born to the actress and her husband, cameraman Matt Johnston, at Cedars Sinai Hospital. But Lupone's character on the ABC series "Life Goes On," Libby Thacher, continues her pregnancy. Her baby will not arrive until the end of the television season. LuPone won a Tony for her performance on Broadway in "Evita." BETH KLEID
