Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 3 (KCNA) -- An estimated 500 students in Kwangju under the Kwangju-South Cholla Provincial Federation of General Student Councils (Namchongnyon) staged a demonstration on March 1 in protest against the recent police search of universities, according to a report from Seoul. After holding a rally, they fought a hit-and-run battle with police in a demonstration, chanting "Stop crackdown upon Namchongnyon!", "We oppose rice market opening!" and other slogans.
