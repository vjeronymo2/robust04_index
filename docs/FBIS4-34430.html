BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari met today with Manuel Camacho Solis, who informed him of his decision to resign from the Peace Commission. President Salinas acknowledged that in carrying out the duties entrusted to him, Camacho displayed skill and a desire to serve. The Government of the Republic, Salinas said, will continue to meet its commitments in Chiapas. The negotiations will be conducted henceforth by the governor of Chiapas State.
