BFN [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty], April 26 (XINHUA) -- Kazakhstan President N.A. Nazarbayev said today that his country will develop a long-term and stable relationship of cooperation with China. The president hosted a grand banquet here this evening to welcome Chinese Premier Li Peng on his official visit to Kazakhstan. Nazarbayev, calling Kazakhstan a bridge between the East and West, said the people of Kazakhstan know well China's long history and industrious people. The Government of Kazakhstan appreciates China's important role in the world and its principles for handling international relations, he said. Li Peng, referring to his talks with Nazarbayev held this morning, said they discussed a wide range of issues in an in-depth manner. President Nazarbayev's remarks on Kazakhstan attaching importance to developing relations with China left a deep impression on him, Li said. Li voiced again his appreciation for Kazakhstan's position on opposing national splittism, adhering to the "One China" policy as well as on the Tibet issue. China appreciates President Nazarbayev's efforts to promote peace and stability in Asia, he said. Today, China and Kazakhstan face the common task of building a new "Silk Road," which should be fully used to seek common prosperity and development, he said.
