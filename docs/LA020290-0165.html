February 2, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

First National Corp. The San Diego-based parent of First National Bank reported fourth-quarter net income of $1.4 million, up from $1.3 million in the fourth quarter of 1988.

For all of fiscal 1989, the seven-office bank holding company reported a profit of $5.3 million, up from $3.3 million in 1988. Assets as of Dec. 31 were $538 million, up 14% from $471.9 million the previous year. Deposits were $490.6 million, up 18% from $415.3 million.
