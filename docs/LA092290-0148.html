September 22, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

LOYAL KIDS DEFEND THEIR NEW KIDS ON THE BLOCK

I do not like the (review) about New Kids. It is rude.

It said in the paper a girl threw a stuffed animal at Donnie, and Donnie picked it up and threw it back and said, 'You oughta stop throwing things." That's not true because I was at that concert. Danny does not sing bad, and Jordan is my favorite, so don't talk about him.

CRYSTAL REYNOLDS

Redondo Beach
