Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Hafiz al-Asad received at 1430 today 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council; Dr. Yasin Sa'id Nu'man, head of the secretariat of the Yemeni Socialist Party's Central Committee; and Muhammad 'Abduh Shutfah, the Yemeni ambassador in Damascus. The meeting was attended by Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. Al-Bid expressed his sincere condolences to his excellency the president on the loss of Staff Major Basil al-Asad. Talks dealt with the Arab situation, developments in the Yemeni arena, and the Yemeni accord document that will be signed in Amman tomorrow. [passage omitted]
