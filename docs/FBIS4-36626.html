BFN [Report by Christian Usi in the Chamber of Deputies in Beirut] [Excerpt] Editors Doyen Milhim Karam has cited Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri as saying that the government will be better protected and more prestigious and dignified if it lifts all restrictions on the audio and visual media, provided that these media are really Lebanese. Birri stressed to the editors' doyen that he is one of the advocates of organized opposition. He renewed his call for General Michel 'Awn to return to Lebanon. If 'Awn needs protection, Birri was quoted as saying, I can guarantee him wider and better protection than that he is now receiving in his place of residence. Birri stressed that General 'Awn was the one who asked for political asylum in France, and said Lebanon is prepared to protect him to practice all his activities in the country. [passage omitted]
