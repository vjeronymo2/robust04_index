March 7, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

HART GIRLS OPEN STATE TOURNAMENT AT HOME TONIGHT

The state high school basketball tournament begins tonight with the opening round in 10 boys' and girls' divisions.

In the girls' Division I field of the Southern California region, third-seeded Hart (25-2), the Southern Section 5-A Division champion, plays host to 5-AA runner-up Fontana (27-2) at 7:30.

Crenshaw, the City Section 4-A champion, is top-seeded in the boys' Division I field. The Cougars (21-2) will play San Diego Section champion Poway at Cal State Dominguez Hills at 7:30.

Defending state champion Manual Arts (24-1), which lost to Crenshaw, 85-72, in the City championship game last Friday, received an at-large bid and will play Capistrano Valley (24-4) at Saddleback College.

Fremont (25-3), the City 3-A champion, will travel to Long Beach to play Millikan (25-6), the Southern Section 5-AA Division champion.

The regional semifinals will be held Thursday with the finals Saturday at the Sports Arena and Cal State Dominguez Hills. LONNIE WHITE
