Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] During a meeting with ROK President Kim Yong-sam on 24 March, Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa expressed his backing to South Korea for its plan to apply for nonpermanent membership of the UN Security Council [UNSC] in 1995. Besides South Korea, Sri Lanka has also announced candidacy for the UNSC seat to be vacated by Pakistan, the present representative for Asia in the council.
