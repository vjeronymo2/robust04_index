Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Gao Haorong (7559 3185 2837)] [Text] Pyongyang, 21 Feb (XINHUA) -- Korea's [DPRK] Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries and Civil Aviation Bureau hosted a banquet today to mark the 35th anniversary of the "Sino-Korean Agreement on Cultural Cooperation" and the "Sino-Korean Agreement on Air Transport." Speaking at the banquet, Chong Chun-ki, chairman of Korea's Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, said: The past 35 years have witnessed numerous cultural exchanges between Korea and China. These exchanges have contributed to socialist construction in both countries, greatly developed the civil aviation sectors of the two countries, and strengthened the bonds of friendship between the two countries. Chong Chun-ki said: The Chinese people have scored tremendous success in its quest for social stability and socialism with their own characteristics. The Korean people are very glad about this, and would like to wish China smooth progress in all its undertakings. He expressed the belief that joint efforts by the two peoples would help constantly develop the traditional Korean-Chinese friendship. Ning Fukui, Chinese charge d'affaires ad interim in Korea, attended the banquet. The "Sino-Korean Agreement on Cultural Cooperation" and the "Sino-Korean Agreement on Air Transport" were signed on 21 and 18 February 1959, respectively.
