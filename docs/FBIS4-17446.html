BFN [Text] Baku, April 23 (TURAN)--Yesterday the parties of "Boz Gurd", United Azerbaijan, Young Azerbaijan and the Azerbaijan National-Democratic Party created the alliance of National-Democratic parties. "The goal of the alliance is to mobilize all forces of the nation to withstand Armenian's aggression, to safeguard territorial integrity and Azeri borders, to protect human rights, principles of democracy, and the traditions and culture of the Azeri nation", according to the mission of the alliance that was read at a press conference. Representatives of the parties criticized the internal and external policy of the current leadership of the country. "The staff appointment policy of the Azeri leadership is based on bribery", said the Chairman of "Boz Gurd" party, Bakhtiyar Akhmedov. According to Iskander Gamidov, "Alliance differs from the Democratic Congress, other blocs and democratic parties. It gives first priority to the national problem." He mentioned that the alliance is in opposition to the Azeri leadership. He said that the Alliance will protest all acts that run counter to the national interests of Azerbaijan will be submitted to the interests of other countries. [sentence as received]
