Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Swiss lower house has followed the Senate in giving its backing to a controversial bill to tighten the law on political asylum. The house approved giving the police rather than a judge the right to decide whether to expel asylum-seekers who try to stay in Switzerland illegally, or hold them in detention. The new law will come into force in July. The house also passed a series of recommendations aimed at boosting cooperation and understanding between the different language regions in Switzerland.
