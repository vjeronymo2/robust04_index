Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The exercise of ferrying NRA [National Resistance Army] troops on a peacekeeping mission to Liberia has ended with the last batch of 95 officers and men leaving Entebbe International Airport aboard a UN aircraft this morning. Today's contingent, which included 10 military police personnel, brings the number of Ugandan peace keepers in Liberia to 767, based in Kakata, Buchanaland, about 25 miles outside the city of Monrovia.
