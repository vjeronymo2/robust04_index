Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, Imamali Rakhmanov, the head of state and Supreme Council chairman of the Republic of Tajikistan received CSCE Secretary General Wilhelm Hoeynck who is on a visit to our republic. The latter is on a visit to some Central Asian states. Imamali Rakhmanov highly appraised the assistance of the CSCE to Tajikistan in the course of the meeting that was held in an atmosphere of mutual understanding. Imamali Rakhmanov said that the republic is ready to use the assistance of this authoritative international organization to organize democratic structures and develop market economy relations. Both sides unanimously expressed that cooperation between the CSCE and Tajikistan should be further developed. Rashid Olimov, the foreign affairs minister of the republic and Karimjon Yuldoshev, the state adviser to the head of state on political and international issues were present at the meeting. Abdulmadjid Samadov, the chairman of the Council of Ministers of the republic received CSCE Secretary General Wilhelm Hoeynck on the same day. Abdulmadjid Samadov familiarized Wilhelm Hoeynck with the present economic situation in the republic, and pointed out the adherence of the republic to strengthening market economy relations. CSCE Secretary General William Hoeynck met Foreign Affairs Minister Rashid Olimov on the same day. Wilhelm Hoeynck gave a news conference for local and foreign journalists after the end of his working visit.
