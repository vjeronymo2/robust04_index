June 18, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

MORE THAN 100 DIE IN SOUTH CHINA FLOODS

More than 100 people have died in floods caused by torrential rains in China's southern province of Hunan, an official newspaper said Sunday.

The flooding last week in Hunan knocked down about 16,000 homes, killed 2,905 farm animals and damaged 30,000 water conservancy projects.
