920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Printech Intl falls 22% to IPounds 2.54m Printech International, the Dublin computer manual printer, suffered a 22 per cent fall in pre-tax profits for 1991. On turnover of IPounds 20m (IPounds 19.3m) profits were IPounds 2.54m (Pounds 2.4m), compared with IPounds 3.27m. Earnings per share were 7.5p (9.7p) and the proposed final dividend is 1.2p for an unchanged total of 2p.
