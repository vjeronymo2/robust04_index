940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / International Company News: Rise for Bank of Montreal Bank of Montreal reports a 26.8 per cent jump in net profit for the three months ended January 31, better than most analysts had forecast. BoM is the first of Canada's big six banks to report for the first quarter of fiscal 1994. All were expected to show gains because of loan loss improvements and peak profits by their brokerage subsidiaries. BoM's net profit was CDollars 197m (USDollars 147m), or 72 cents a share, up from CDollars 155m, or 56 cents, a year earlier. Return on common equity was 14.6 per cent, up from 12.5 per cent. Assets reached CDollars 116bn, up from CDollars 106bn.
