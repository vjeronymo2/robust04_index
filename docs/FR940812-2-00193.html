Implementation of the steam generator tube/tube support plate plugging criteria requires a 100 percent bobbin coil inspection for hot-leg tube support plate intersections and cold-leg intersections down to the lowest cold-leg tube support plate with known outside diameter stress corrosion cracking (ODSCC) indications.
