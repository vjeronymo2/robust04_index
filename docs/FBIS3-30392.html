Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federation of Free and Independent Trade Unions of Engine Drivers of Romania has sent us a communique that emphasizes that following a meeting of the Coordination Council of the National Trade Union Bloc [BNS] on 1 February, it has decided to suspend the one-day strike on 4 February as well as the general strike on 16 February 1994. The Federation of Free and Independent Trade Unions of Engine Drivers of Romania, a member of the BNS, announces that it will abide by this decision and will not go on strike on those dates. During a meeting of the National Steering Council on 8 February, the federation's strategy for the coming period will be voted on, the communique also points out.
