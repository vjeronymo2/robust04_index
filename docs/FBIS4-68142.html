BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] There were three different explosions in Istanbul today. One persons was injured. According to information received by a TRT correspondent from security officials, a bomb placed by unidentified persons in the public toilet in the Sultanahmet Square exploded around 1030. The explosion started a fire in the toilet. Ibrahim Kazman was injured in the incident. He was taken to Hasiki Hospital and he is reported to be in satisfactory condition. Around the same time, another bomb exploded under a bench opposite the rector's office of Marmara University in Sultanahmet Square. The bomb caused material damage. The third bomb was placed in the bus parking lot near the Dolmabahce Palace. The explosion, which occurred around 1100, wrecked a motorcycle in the parking lot, but no one was killed or injured.
