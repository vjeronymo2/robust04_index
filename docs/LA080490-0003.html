August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BARR STRIKES OUT SINGING THE ANTHEM

I was appalled and repulsed by the rude, thoughtless and inconsiderate performance of our national anthem by Roseanne Barr at Jack Murphy Stadium in San Diego. It wasn't bad enough that she agreed to "sing" in the first place knowing full well that she couldn't; but to follow her "performance" with an inappropriate gesture and spitting was totally unacceptable and an insult to those in the stadium and the rest of our great country as well.

KATHLEEN A. CLEMENTS

Los Angeles
