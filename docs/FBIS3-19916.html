Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Nursultan Nazarbayev, the president of Kazakhstan, has arrived in Madrid for a two-day official visit to Spain. He is the first head of state of the Commonwealth of Independent States, created after the demise of the Soviet Union, to visit Spain. The Kazakhstani president and his wife are having a private lunch with King Juan Carlos and Queen Sofia. Later Nazarbayev, who is travelling with the deputy prime minister, the foreign minister, and the economics minister, will receive a courtesy visit from Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez in the Pardo Palace. He [Nazarbayev] will go to the Prado Museum to see Spain's main painting collection and will have supper with the king and queen in the Palacio Real. For his part, the deputy prime minister of Kazakhstan is having lunch with Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana, with whom he is due to sign two bilateral agreements, one about mutual investment protection and another about economic and industrial cooperation.
