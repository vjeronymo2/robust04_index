BFN [Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council Decree No. 136-I SF, signed by Shumeyko and dated 21 June 1994: "On the Use of Russian Federation Armed Forces To Carry Out a Peacekeeping Operation in the Georgian-Abkhaz Conflict Zone"] [Text] Having examined the Russian Federation president's appeal No. Pr-714 of 9 June 1994, and given the repeated requests from E. Shevardnadze, Georgian head of state, and V. Ardzinba, chairman of the Abkhaz Supreme Council, the Federation Council decrees, in accordance with Article 102 Part 1 Paragraph "c" of the Russian Federation Constitution, that: 1. The use be permitted of a contingent of up to 3,000 men from the Russian Federation Armed Forces with an initial mandate of six months to carry out a peacekeeping operation in the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict zone on a voluntary basis. 2. The Federation Council Committee for Security and Defense Issues, the Federation Council Committee for CIS Affairs, and the Federation Council Committee for International Affairs be instructed to monitor the implementation of this decree. 3. This decree comes into force from the moment it is adopted. [Signed] V.F. Shumeyko, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council [Dated] Moscow, 21 June 1994 No. 136-I SF
