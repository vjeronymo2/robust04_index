920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / Sanctions blamed for Libya deaths United Nations sanctions have cost 150 lives and more than Dollars 2bn in lost economic output, Libya claimed at the non-aligned summit, writes William Keeling in Jakarta. The deaths occurred among patients who could not be treated because of lack of medical supplies who were being taken by land to Egypt or Tunisia. A Libyan document submitted to the summit said the UN ban on flights to Libya had led to big falls in agricultural output.
