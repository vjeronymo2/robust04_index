930308 FT 08 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Holiday village development Center Parcs, based in Newark, has awarded the first major contract in the construction programme of its new holiday village at Longleat to G PEARCE & SONS of Devizes. The contract, worth some Pounds 12m, is for the infrastructure works for the village and follows work by the company on the new roundabout and entrance into the village.
