August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Acura Buyers Most Satisfied: Buyers of Acuras are the most satisfied with their cars and their dealers, according to a survey by J. D. Power & Associates. It was the third straight year that the car from the Honda Motor Co. luxury division topped the survey. Buyers of cars made by West Germany's Mercedes-Benz and Japan's Honda and Toyota Motor Corp. followed Acura. The top-ranked U.S.-produced car was General Motors Corp.'s Cadillac, in fifth place.
