920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Updown net assets show marginal fall Halfway through its year, the net asset value at Updown Investment Company had declined to 491.45p, against 493.57p at the end of the previous first half. Attributable profits in the six months to June 30 fell to Pounds 297,000 (Pounds 323,000) for earnings of 7.42p (8.07p) per share.
