BFN [Text of Russian President's Directive No. 287-rp signed by President Yeltsin on 8 June 1994: "On the Procedure and Timetable for the Submission of Proposals on the Expression of Agreement to the Mandatory Nature for the Russian Federation of International Treaties Liable To Be Presented to the Federal Assembly State Duma"] [Text] In order to synchronize the process of the enactment of international treaties liable to be presented to the Federal Assembly State Duma; 1. The Russian Foreign Ministry shall, within 10 days and with the participation of the relevant federal executive organs, draw up a list of international treaties liable for immediate submission to the Federal Assembly State Duma. The federal executive organs shall, within five days, submit the corresponding proposals to the Russian Foreign Ministry. The Russian Foreign Ministry and also other relevant federal executive organs, either in conjunction with or with the agreement of the Russian Foreign Ministry, shall, in the prescribed manner, present to the president of the Russian Federation and the Government of the Russian Federation for submission in June-August 1994 to the Federal Assembly State Duma proposals on the expression of agreement to the mandatory nature for the Russian Federation of international treaties included in the aforementioned list. The Russian Foreign Ministry is charged with the coordination of this work, which shall include the submission of the prepared list of international treaties to the president of the Russian Federation and the Government of the Russian Federation as well as the provision of the relevant information to the Federal Assembly State Duma and Federation Council. 2. It is established that proposals on the expression of agreement to the mandatory nature for the Russian Federation of international treaties liable to be submitted to the Federal Assembly State Duma but not included in the list envisaged by paragraph one of the present directive shall be submitted in 1994 as and when the Russian Side is ready [po mere gotovnosti Rossiyskoy Storony]. 3. Oversight of the fulfillment of the present directive shall be entrusted to the Russian Federation President's Staff and the Russian Federation Government. 4. The present directive shall go into force from the moment of its signing. [signed] B. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation. Directive no. 287-rp, 8 June 1994.
