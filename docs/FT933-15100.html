930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / UK Company News: ICC Bank shows advance to IPounds 5.14m Pre-tax profits of ICC Bank, the Irish state-owned bank, rose by IPounds 483,000 to IPounds 5.14m (Pounds 4.9m) in the six months to April 30. The outcome was achieved on income up from IPounds 61.9m to IPounds 85.8m. An unchanged interim dividend of 6p is declared from earnings of 31.5p (27.3p).
