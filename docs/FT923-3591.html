920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Council decision NORTH Tyneside council decided last night to seek recovery of enhanced redundancy payments made to 396 of its former employees. Last night's decision follows a High Court ruling that North Tyneside's enhanced severance scheme was unlawful. Of Pounds 2.54m in payments made under the scheme in 1990-91, Pounds 1.81m was made without specific statutory authority, the court was told.
