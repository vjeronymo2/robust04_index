BFN [Interview with Panamanian Education Minister Marcos Alarcon by reporter Luis Alberto Jimenez at the Palace of the Herons in Panama City on 14 April--recorded] [Text] [Alarcon] We all were watching, let's say, what the television stations were analyzing. Nothing at all was being planned. We were simple viewers analyzing the various reactions as reflected by the television interviews. [Jimenez] Did the members of the Cabinet Council know of the content of the document that was going to be presented? [Alarcon] No Cabinet Council member knew about this document. This was a decision made solely by the president, and if you listen to the documentary introduction the president himself assumes the responsibility of the content of the document. [Jimenez] Politically speaking, who gains from this? [Alarcon] The people are the ones who benefit. The people are the ones who did not know the inside details of how the military government operated.
