Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] An Indonesian delegation led by Foreign Minister Mr. Ali Alatas, who were visiting Yangon [Rangoon] at the invitation of U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], left Yangon by special aircraft this afternoon. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw, Deputy Foreign Affairs Minister U Nyunt Swe, and responsible personnel, Indonesian Ambassador Mr. Mohamed Sanusi and embassy officials.
