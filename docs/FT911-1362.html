910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Construction Contracts: Building hotels in Moscow BAKER WILKINS & SMITH, British quantity surveyors and project managers, has signed a contract in Moscow involving a Pounds 70m deal for the construction and fitting out of two hotels, one of 300 rooms and the other of 750 rooms. The company says this is one of the first major construction contracts in Moscow involving a British company, and covers the contract management and financial control of the projects over the next four years. The contracts were signed with Kompleks, a Russian joint venture company, with finance in the form of a loan from the Italian government. Construction will be by Cogefar-Impresit, an Italian contractor. The management of the hotels will be by Ramada International Hotels.
