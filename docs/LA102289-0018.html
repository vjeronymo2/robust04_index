CRITICS' CHOICE: A HISTORY OF THE WORLD IN 10 1/2 CHAPTERS BY JULIAN BARNES (ALFRED A. KNOPF: $18.95; 309 PP.)

"We are tumbled amid the writer's humor, anger, irony, homely invention and a large lament for the cycle of human endeavor that dreams, corrupts what it dreams of, and dreams again." Richard Eder
