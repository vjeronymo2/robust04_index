BFN [Text] Beijing, June 6 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), met with a military delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) here today. The delegation, headed by Choi Gwang, chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, arrived here today at the invitation of the PLA Headquarters of the General Staff.
