941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury advances to Pounds 2.35m Shaftesbury, the specialist property company, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 2.35m for the year ended September 30. The improvement was achieved on operating income up from Pounds 5.87m to Pounds 6.25m and was struck after an exceptional debit of Pounds 433,000 on termination of an interest rate swap agreement and a reduced interest charge of Pounds 2.87m (Pounds 4.12m). The group's investment properties have been valued at Pounds 77.1m, showing a net surplus of Pounds 4.67m, while shareholders' funds grew by Pounds 6.99m to Pounds 48.7m at the year end - equivalent to net assets of 100p (86p) per share. After a tax refund of Pounds 465,000 (Pounds 49,000 charge), net profits advanced to Pounds 2.82m (Pounds 963,000). Earnings were 8p (3.5p) and there is a dividend of 1p (nil).
