921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / Bundesbank warns on EC money The Bundesbank, presenting a new set of bank notes, said yesterday the EC would be hard pressed for technical reasons to issue a common currency before the year 2000, Reuter reports from Berlin. In issuing the DM5, DM500 and DM1000 notes, board member Mr Gunter Storch, noted the bank had decided to replace old notes back in 1983 and had taken seven years.
