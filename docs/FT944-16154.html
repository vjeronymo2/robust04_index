941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Recovery at Tudor Improved gross margins at its core United Tile business and a reduced interest burden helped Tudor return to the black at the interim stage. On turnover of Pounds 9.05m (Pounds 8.63m), including Pounds 253,000 (Pounds 448,000) from the discontinued crystal glassware operation, the USM-traded ceramic tile distributor achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 39,000 for the six months to June 30, against losses of Pounds 240,000. Earnings per share were 0.36p (losses of 2.2p).
