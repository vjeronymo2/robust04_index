940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Business Travel: Qantas facelift Australia's Qantas Airways has unveiled plans for a facelift to improve services and make it more competitive with other leading Asian carriers before the company's flotation next year. Changes include new cabin interiors for the entire Qantas fleet of 120 aircraft, decorated with Australian designs and themes, as well as new staff uniforms. Qantas' managing director James Strong described the changes as the biggest since the airline introduced the jumbo jet to its fleet more than 20 years ago. Mr Strong said the changes would be introduced over the next year.
