BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 23 June (ITAR-TASS) -- Two attempts at armed infiltration into Tajikistan from Afghanistan by small groups of Tajik gunmen on sections of the Pyanj border detachment, were foiled during the night of 22-23 June by Russian border guards. The Directorate of the Group of Russian Border Guards in Tajikistan told the ITAR-TASS correspondent that artillery strikes were carried out near [border] crossings. It is not known if the infiltrators suffered losses. There were no losses among the Russian servicemen.
