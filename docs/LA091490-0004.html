September 14, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MANDATORY STATE QUAKE INSURANCE

So, Gov. George Deukmejian and the gang have decided that earthquake insurance is such a good idea that they have the right to force every homeowner to buy some whether he wants it or not.

I have a few questions, though.

Where does the government get the power to force people to insure their private property? Why does the government think it is proper to engage in a business that is best handled by private enterprise? Why is it proper for the government to seize a person's property because he refuses to insure it? When did we, the people, decide that government should do our thinking for us?

Thanks, Gov. Deukmejian. You have convinced me to vote Libertarian.

BROCK BACKLUND

Calabasas
