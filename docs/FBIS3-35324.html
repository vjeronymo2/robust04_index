Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The cabinet held an ordinary session today under Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali. During the session, Dr. al-Majali presented a briefing on Jordan's pan-Arab role in the Yemeni reconciliation, which was crowned with the meeting of Yemeni leaders in Amman and the signing of the accord document by Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, and all the leaders of the coalition and opposition parties. The document was drawn up by the Yemeni dialogue committee to end the political crisis in fraternal Yemen. Dr. al-Majali said that the Yemenis' implementation of the document is aimed at leading Yemen to the shores of safety. He stressed that the Yemeni people are aware of this critical historic stage they are facing and that they will overcome it with success, God willing. [passage omitted]
