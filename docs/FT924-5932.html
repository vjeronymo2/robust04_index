921124 FT 24 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Dwyer chief stands down Mr Desmond Bloom, chairman and chief executive of Dwyer, the loss-making property company, has resigned. The new chief executive is Mr Joey Esfandi, who is chief executive of Winglaw, a private property company which owns 15.5 per cent of Dwyer. Mr Peter Andrews, a director of Winglaw, will also join the board. Mr Anthony Kirwan, a board member of Dwyer, has been appointed non-executive chairman.
