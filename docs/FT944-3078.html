941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / County Hall sale to be probed The National Audit Office is to carry out a full investigation into the Pounds 60m sale last year of County Hall, the former headquarters of the Greater London Council, to the Shirayama Corporation, a Japanese company. Mr Alan Milburn, a Labour MP who is a member of the Public Accounts Committee, had complained that the contract for the sale by the London Residuary Body had included a confidentiality clause stressing that it should not be copied to the Department of the Environment. He said: 'It is outrageous that the disposal of national assets should be subject to this sort of secretive arrangement between an unaccountable quango and an overseas corporation.'
