941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / New charges in Indian spy case A suspect in India's biggest spy case has been charged under an anti-corruption law with illegally accumulating more than Dollars 100,000 in a sex, money and secrets scandal, court officials said yesterday. The Central Bureau of Investigation told a judge in the southern town of Trivandrum late on Tuesday that Mr D. Sasikumaran amassed wealth far beyond his regular income as a senior scientist with India's rocket programme.
