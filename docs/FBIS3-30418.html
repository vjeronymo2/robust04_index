Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Croatian Prime Minister Nikica Valentic today received Spanish Foreign Affairs Minister Javier Solana who said after the meeting that their talks related to the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Minister Solana stated that if the reports on the involvement of forces from the Republic of Croatia in the war in Bosnia-Herzegovina proved true, the international community would have to examine that question with utmost seriousness. Before returning to Madrid, the Spanish foreign minister will meet the UN secretary general's special envoy Yasushi Akashi.
