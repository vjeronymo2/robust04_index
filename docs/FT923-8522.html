920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Kabul comes under rocket fire A dissident mujahideen group yesterday launched its heaviest rocket bombardment on Kabul since the Afghan capital fell to the guerrillas in April, Reuter reports from Kabul. More than 650 rockets rained down on Kabul in the first 90 minutes of the assault, which began at first light from positions held by the radical Hezb-i-Islami party to the south of the city, one military expert said.
