October 31, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

LOOK-ALIKE VANNA WHITE DOLL

Vanna White is marketing a look-alike doll on cable TV and women's clothes that will allow viewers to coordinate their outfits with those of the dolls.

The Home Shopping Network said Tuesday that the Wheel of Fortune letter-turner's line of fashions also includes designer sheets. The merchandise is available only through cable TV.
