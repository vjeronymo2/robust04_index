Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Cuban Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea Juan Jose Leon Vega, who paid a farewell call on him. On hand were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and officials of the Cuban Embassy. President Kim Il-song had a talk with the ambassador. He received a gift from the ambassador.
