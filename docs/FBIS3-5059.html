Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A decree of the DPRK Central People's Committee [CPC] on dismissal and appointment of a minister of the DPRK State Administration Council: The DPRK CPC decides as follows: Comrade Kim Pil-hwan has been relieved from his post of minister of mining industry of the State Administration Council and Comrade Kim Pyong-kil has been appointed as minister of mining industry of the State Administration Council. DPRK President Kim Il-song 2 March 1994, Pyongyang
