930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / Colleges face lecturers' strike FURTHER education colleges in England and Wales face a strike on Thursday this week, following a ballot by lecturers. The National Association of Teachers in Further and Higher Education voted by a majority of 65 per cent in favour of a one-day strike, while the majority in favour of other forms of industrial action was 86 per cent. Natfhe is in dispute with the college employers' forum over new contracts following the transfer of the colleges from local authority to government funding last month. A total of 364 colleges were included in the ballot.
