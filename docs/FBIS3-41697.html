Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article by S. Kudryashov, journalist] [Text] Ufa -- For the first time in many years, a severe toxic form of diptheria has been recorded in Ufa. A 21-year-old woman has died. Despite physicians efforts, the patient could not be saved. In connection with the adverse diptheria epidemic situation, mass immunization of the adult population has begun in Ufa.
