930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Ex-Mirror editor goes to Today MR RICHARD Stott, former editor of the Daily Mirror, is to replace Mr Martin Dunn as editor of Today, News International, the newspaper group, announced last night. Mr Dunn is to edit the group's Boston Herald. Mr Stott was sacked as Mirror editor in November by Mr David Montgomery, the new chief executive of Mirror Group Newspapers. Mr Montgomery used to edit Today.
