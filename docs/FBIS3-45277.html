Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Heilongjiang Provincial United Front Work Conference was held in Harbin on 12 January. Ma Guoliang, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, presided over the conference. Sun Weiben, secretary of the provincial party committee, gave a speech on strengthening the party's leadership over united front work. He said: The party's leadership over the united front work during the new period should be mainly manifested in five aspects, namely, giving ideological guidance, organizational leadership, policy guidance, conducting democratic consultation, and having the full attention of the whole party. It is hoped that the united front work departments at all levels will further intensify the sense of responsibility and mission and give play to the advantage of the united front of having a galaxy of talents and the advantage of having extensive overseas contacts and extensive social basis in line with the three major tasks of reform, development, and stability. Provincial leaders, including Hou Wenhua, Tian Fengshan, Shan Rongfan, Chen Yunlin, An Zhendong, Qi Guiyuan, Zhou Tienong, Huang Feng, Tan Fangzhi, Fu Shiying, Guo Shouchang, Zhao Shijie, Chen Zhanyuan, Wang Zhitian, and Wu Dinghe attended the conference.
