920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / Management (In A Nutshell): 3 ayes have it Britain's small firms should be queuing up to raise venture capital. Their bankers are reluctant to lend and their balance sheets need a boost. In fact the venture capital industry appears to be struggling to attract new customers. 3i, the largest UK venture capital group and frequently a bellwether for the industry, yesterday announced plans to target companies in the London area with the launch of a Pounds 75m Greater London Growth Fund. It expects to help 150 companies with sums of between Pounds 100,000 and Pounds 5m over the next two years. Many growing companies are unaware that long-term investment finance is available because of the venture capital industry's emphasis on management buy-outs and the larger deals, 3i said.
