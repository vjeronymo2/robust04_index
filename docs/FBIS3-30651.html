Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpt] Sofia, February 6 (BTA) -- Finance Minister Stoyan Aleksandrov and Minister of Science and Education Marko Todorov will receive a formal invitation to take part in a televised dispute with representatives of the St. Kliment Okhridski University of Sofia students on strike, BTA was told by the Press Centre of the Strike Committee. The students propose to discuss the ways of financing science and education. The Sofia University students have been on strike since January 21, their lecturers joined the strike on February 3. The strike will go on until all students' demands are satisfied, including their much disputed demand for allocating 2 percent of the national budget to science and education, the strikers' Press Centre said.
