940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / International Capital Markets: Bank Bali interim profit shows marginal increase Bank Bali, Indonesia's seventh largest bank in terms of private assets, said its pre-tax profit for the first six months of this year rose by 2 per cent to Rp43.44bn (Dollars 20m) compared with Rp42.50bn in last year's first half. Total income rose to Rp274.79bn from Rp253.27bn in the year-earlier period. Total expenses climbed to Rp231.35bn from Rp210.81bn. Revenues from interest rose by 11 per cent to Rp232.04bn from Rp208.81bn, reflecting the bank's more aggressive attempts to to capture a share of Indonesia's loan market.
