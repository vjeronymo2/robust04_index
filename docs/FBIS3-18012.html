Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 30, BNS -- The Latvian Cabinet adopted a resolution Tuesday [29 March] on the foundation of a Privatization Agency and confirmed its statutes. The government also commissioned State Reforms Minister to organize Latvia's entrance to the International Institute of Administrative Sciences. In addition, the ministers passed a proposal to the Saeima (parliament) on the ratification of the 1963 Vienna convention on civil responsibility for nuclear damages and the 1988 common protocol on the use of Vienna and Paris conventions. The government also passed for Saeima consideration a bill on radiation and nuclear safety, and approved a Latvian-Indian memorandum on cooperation in culture, science, education, information and sports.
