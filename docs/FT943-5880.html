940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / International Company News: Asian deal for Goodman Fielder Goodman Fielder, the Australian food group whose dissident shareholders are trying to push through board changes, yesterday announced a joint venture deal with Indonesia's Sinar Mas and Risjadson groups, targeted at the country's snacks market, writes Nikki Tait. Goodman said it would invest ADollars 20m (USDollars 15m) in the venture over a five-year period via its Bluebird Foods subsidiary in New Zealand, and take a 50 per cent stake in Smartindo Kencana, a snacks manufacturer. Initially, Bluebird will 'part-manufacture' snacks products for export to the joint venture.
