BFN [Text] Ankara, May 6 (A.A) -- The High Election Board released the official final results of the March 27 local elections. According to the official results, the senior coalition partner True Path Party [DYP] topped all other parties in city councils while the pro-Islamic Prosperity Party (RP) took the most mayorships, and the main opposition Motherland Party (ANAP) topped the elections for borough selectmen. Voter turn out was 29,456,498 out of 31,960,555 registered voters. The DYP received 21.44 percent of the vote for city council races (1,085 city councilmen), followed by ANAP with 20.99 percent (744), RP with 19.09 percent (686) and SHP [Social Democratic Populist Party] with 13.56 percent (241). In the mayoral elections RP received 22.40 percent of the vote winning 28 mayorships, followed by ANAP with 14 mayorships, DYP 12, SHP 10, the Nationalist Movement Party (MHP) 7, and the Republican People's Party (CHP) 5. In the borough representatives elections ANAP received most of the votes with 22.75 percent but only 789 borough representative posts as compared to the DYP which received 18.96 percent of vote and 882 borough representative posts, RP with 18.94 percent and 324 borough representative posts, SHP with 16.83 percent and 432 borough representative posts. In the election for borough selectmen seats, ANAP finished with 22.71 percent of vote and 8,180 seats followed by RP with 19.13 percent and 4,282 seats, DYP with 18.89 percent and 8,538 seats, and SHP with 16.80 percent and 4,647. These results are expected to be published in Saturday's OFFICIAL GAZETTE.
