Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- MINJU CHOSON dedicates a by-lined article to the independence of Namibia. The article says: Since the proclamation of the Republic the Namibian people have striven to build a new life, valiantly overcoming trials and difficulties that cropped up in their way. Pursuing a non-aligned foreign policy, the Namibian Government is strengthening relations with many countries of the world and member states of the organization of African unity and fighting against imperialism and racism. The Korean and the Namibian people established friendly bonds on the road of the common struggle for independence against imperialism. Especially, the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries have further developed since the establishment of diplomatic relations at ambassadorial level on March 22, 1990. Believing that the relations between the two peoples will grow stronger and develop in different fields on the basis of the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the Korean people wish the Namibian people greater success in their strivings for the development and prosperity of the country.
