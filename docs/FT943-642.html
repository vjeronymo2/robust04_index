940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Goodhead back in the black Goodhead Group, the printing and publishing company, turned a Pounds 17m pre-tax loss into a profit of Pounds 40,000 for the year to May 31, struck after a Pounds 130,000 profit (Pounds 14m loss) on the disposal of discontinued operations and a loss of Pounds 43,000 (Pounds 309,000) on the sale of fixed assets. Turnover fell 25 per cent from Pounds 37.4m to Pounds 28.2m, with discontinued operations contributing Pounds 2.28m (Pounds 11.8m). Earnings per share, fully diluted, were 0.6p (85.9p losses) and the proposed final dividend is unchanged at 0.05p.
