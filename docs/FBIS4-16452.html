BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 16 TASS -- To execute Presidential Decree No. 784, Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev on Friday signed Order No. 116 on drafting Russian Federation citizens into military service in paril-June 1994 and transferring soldiers, sailors, sergeants, sergeants-major, petty officers and military builders who served the terms of military duty, to the reserve, the Defence Ministry's Directorate for Information and the press announced on Saturday. Under the order, subject to the draft are Russian Federation citizens born in 1967-1976 who are not entitled to deferment or exemption from the draft.
