June 17, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Paramount's press kit for "Another 48 HRS." boasts that composer James Horner "received Golden Glove nominations for 'Glory' and 'An American Tail.'

" Were those for boxing or fielding?

. . . "RoboCop 2" doesn't open until Friday, but already Orion Pictures is optimistic: They've registered the titles "RoboCop 3" and "4" with the MPAA. And Warner Bros. has registered "Beetlejuice Goes Hawaiian."
