BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow, 11 May -- The State Duma has refused a move by deputy Sergey Baburin to call into question the conduct of State Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin over his signing of the Treaty on Social Accord. At today's Duma plenary session, Sergey Baburin proposed adding to the agenda Ivan Rybkin's performance as chairman, but he failed to attract the necessary number of votes. In a brief speech from the floor, Sergey Baburin told his fellow deputies that the Duma chairman's act of signing the Treaty on Social Accord represents a distortion of the chamber's opinion.
