920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / International Company News: Proton share offer oversubscribed THE public offering for 30 per cent of the shares in Perusahaan Otomobil Nasional (Proton), Malaysia's national car maker, was oversubscribed 6.44 times, writes Lim Siong Hoon in Kuala Lumpur. The company - a joint venture between the Malaysian government and Mitsubishi of Japan - received MDollars 2.1bn (USDollars 808m) in bids for the 55.5m shares offered to the public at MDollars 5 each.
