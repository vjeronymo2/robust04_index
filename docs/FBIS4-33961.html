BFN [Excerpts] Mexico City, 13 Jun (EFE) -- Today, Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari said he is willing to mediate between Cuba and the United States to eliminate the economic embargo on the island, however, "no one has asked us to mediate, and we are not in the habit of going where we have not been invited." In a short news conference, shortly before leaving for Cartagena de Indias, Colombia, Salinas de Gortari said he is convinced the blockade solves nothing and added: "we believe it should be lifted and in this manner bridges for communication and dialogue would be built." [passage omitted] The Mexican president said the visit was "very positive and cordial and strengthened the traditional good relations that exist between our two countries." [passage omitted]
