BFN [Text] It has been announced in Islamabad that the prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, will leave for South Africa on Sunday to attend the ceremony at which Mr. Nelson Mandela will be installed as the first non-White president of the country. The prime minister during her three-day official visit will hold bilateral meetings with several heads of state and government who will be present at this ceremony. On her way back, the prime minister will make a brief stopover in Nairobi.
