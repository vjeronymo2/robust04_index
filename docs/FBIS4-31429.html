BFN [Text] We have just received two more reports on the activities of President Izetbegovic: As a part of his visit to the free territories of our country, President Alija Izetbegovic is visiting Tuzla today, where he will meet with political and public representatives, as well as members of the armed forces. President Izetbegovic spoke with Turkish President Suleyman Demirel over the phone today. They exchanged opinions on the current situation in our country. Demirel congratulated President Izetbegovic on the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation agreement and its realization. He also reiterated Turkey's support for Bosnia-Herzegovina.
