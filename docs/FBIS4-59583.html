BFN [Excerpt] On the third of Khordad [24 May], the anniversary of the epic of liberating Khorramshahr [from Iraqi occupation in 1982], victory bells will toll in the country's schools. Mr. Chamran, the cultural deputy of the armed forces general staff, while announcing the above, said: On the third of Khordad, named the day of resistance and victory, some units of the armed forces of the Islamic Republic of Iran will stage a maneuver in the regions of Khorramshahr and Jofeyr.
