940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Kier grows to Pounds 6.8m Kier, the housing and construction group bought by its employees from Hanson in July 1992, announced pre-tax profits up from Pounds 5.6m to Pounds 6.8m for the year to June 30. Turnover of continuing operations slipped to Pounds 500.2m (Pounds 512.2m). Earnings improved to 144p (120p) per share. During the year Hanson's remaining 10 per cent of ordinary and preference shares was bought out for Pounds 8m.
