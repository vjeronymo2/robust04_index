Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] "The most important thing is the structural collapse of the center, balanced by a shift to the right of the old power blocks," according to Massimo D'Alema, [deputy Leader of Democratic Party of the Left]. "Maybe we hoped that the old power block had fallen apart. However, it merged with the forces of the right," he added. "I think that in the end there will not be a majority capable of governing. I think that we will have to work for a constituent government."
