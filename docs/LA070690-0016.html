July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Like a Rolling Stone: Rolling Stones bassist Bill Wyman will record his first solo album in nine years when the Stones wind up their current world tour. The album is planned for release in March. Fellow Stones Mick Jagger, Keith Richards and Charlie Watts have all had outside projects in recent years that were met with no more than moderate popularity. GREG BRAXTON
