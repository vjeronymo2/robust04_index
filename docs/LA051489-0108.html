May 14, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ACETONE FLASH FIRE HOSPITALIZES 2 MEN

Two men were seriously burned Saturday morning in a flash fire at a Sun Valley company. Lee Reese, 46, and Enrique Perez, 39, were taken to Pacifica Community Hospital and later sent to Sherman Oaks Community Hospital after suffering second- and third-degree burns over 70% of their bodies, a Fire Department spokesman said.

The men were transferring acetone from one container to another when the chemical's vapors flashed.

Firefighters are not sure what caused the flash fire, which caused little damage.

The sprinklers at the company, Advanced Packaging Techniques, extinguished the fire.
