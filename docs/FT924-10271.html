921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / Gatt sets up dispute panel Gatt yesterday set up a dispute panel to investigate a Japanese complaint against duties imposed by the European Community on imported audio cassette tapes, trade sources said, Reuter reports from Geneva. Canada, the US and Hong Kong told the 25-nation committee that they had a 'trade interest' in the matter and would submit their own data to the panel. Five Japanese companies - Sony, Hitachi Maxell, TDK, Fuji and Denon Columbia -initiated the complaint.
