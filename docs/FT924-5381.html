921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / World Trade News: US-EC farm trade deal widens divisions in the Netherlands The US-EC farm trade deal has widened divisions within the Dutch farm sector, industry officials said yesterday, Reuter reports in Amsterdam. Farmers in the Netherlands - the world's third largest agricultural exporter after the US and France - are struggling in vain to find a common stand on an issue that has left them divided. 'Cereal producers are against Gatt, but market gardeners are strongly in favour,' said one farm representative. Grain farmers and the cheese industry rely on subsidies.
