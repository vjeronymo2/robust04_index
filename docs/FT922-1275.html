920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Westport losses down at Pounds 925,000 Westport Group, the USM-quoted exhibition, photographic and marketing services company, cut its pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.08m to Pounds 925,000 for the year ended April 30. Turnover was Pounds 14.2m, down from Pounds 18.2m, which included Pounds 1.4m from discontinued activities. The pre-tax figure was struck after exceptionals of Pounds 123,000 (Pounds 998,000) resulting from the reorganisation of its studios. Extraordinaries of Pounds 327,000 (Pounds 2.34m) resulted from the closure of an exhibition hire business. There were losses per share of 0.3p (0.7p) after a tax credit of Pounds 532,000 (Pounds 261,000).
