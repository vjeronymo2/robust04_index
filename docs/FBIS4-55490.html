BFN [Text] Kyril, the metropolitan of the Russian Orthodox Church, who is accompanying Patriarch Aleksey II on his visit to the Serbian Orthodox Church, said at the Patriarchy last night that Zagreb's Cardinal Franjo Kuharic and the Bosnian [Muslim] Reis ul-Ulema Mustafa Ceric had accepted the invitation by the Russian and Serbian patriarchs to meet in Sarajevo and talk about peace. According to Metropolitan Kyril, the meeting of the four religious leaders should take place at Sarajevo airport tomorrow.
