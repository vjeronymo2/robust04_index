August 9, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Jacques Poujadehas been named vice-president of Dynamic Group Securities Corp., a new affiliate of Newport Pacific Group, Newport Beach.

He will be responsible for finance, administration and investor relations, as well as overseeing day-to-day operations of the firm's brokers. Poujade was formerly vice- president of finance and administration for Zimmcor International Inc. in London. Before that, he was assistant to the president and vice-president of marketing for the Bank of Boston in Montreal.
