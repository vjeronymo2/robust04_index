BFN [Text] Baku (June 27) TURAN -- Over 15 Kurds-Yezidis were violently killed during the clashes on the ethnic base between the Kurd-yezids population of Garalar village of Araratskiy region in Armenia and group of armed Armenians, who came from Yerevan. TURAN was told by informed source that the clash took place in the middle of June, when some representatives of Kurd Working Party [PKK], functioning in the republic, were arrested by Armenian authorities. This event sharpened the complicated social and political situation in Armenia. The Kurds again remember the violent murder of Said Ibon, famous pediatrist, Kurd by nationality, killed by Armenians in 1991 in his office. According to information, Levon Ter-Petrosyan, Armenian president, ordered to deploy special forces and military hardware imto Garalar village and other regions where Kurd population live. The troops were ordered to open fire, if any attempts of counterraction on the part of local Kurd population take place.
