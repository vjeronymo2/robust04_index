940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Thornton Pan-Euro Net asset value per share of the Thornton Pan-European Investment Trust expanded over the 12 months to December 31 1993 from 32.29p to 40.79p. Earnings per share were just ahead at 0.92p, compared with 0.91p, while the single final dividend is again 1p.
