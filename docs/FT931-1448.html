930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Arthur Wood Pre-tax profits of Arthur Wood & Son (Longport), the Stoke-on-Trent maker of earthenware, beakers and terracotta teapots, fell from Pounds 261,431 to Pounds 76,882 for 1992. The dividend for the year is cut from 4.1p to 1.5p. Earnings per share fell to 2.79p (9.28p). Turnover totalled Pounds 3.34m (Pounds 3.7m). The directors said there was a high element of fixed costs in pottery manufacture and that any reduction in turnover had a 'disproportionate effect on profits.'
