930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / Ex-Soviet debt accord DETAILS of an agreement dividing the foreign debts and assets of the former Soviet Union between Ukraine and Russia were yesterday released in Kiev, write Chrystia Freeland from Kiev and Leyla Boulton from Moscow. According to the deal, signed in Moscow on Saturday, Russia and Ukraine have agreed to divide up the former Soviet Union's assets and debts and make separate arrangements for servicing their respective shares of the debt. However, Mr Alexander Shokhin, Russia's deputy prime minister responsible for the debt negotiations, said any agreement would have to be acceptable to the ex-Soviet Union's creditors and until now they had been unhappy about separate repayments.
