941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / International Company News: More time sought for Qantas sale Legislation which could give more time for the flotation of the state's 75 per cent stake in Qantas, the Australian airline, was introduced yesterday, writes Nikki Tait. The amendment bill extends a deadline of June 30 next year for the completion of the share offering, to the end of August. Meanwhile, the Australian Trade Practices Commission is due to announce today whether Qantas and British Airways, which holds a 25 per cent stake in the Australian carrier, should be allow to 'co-operate' on London-Australia services.
