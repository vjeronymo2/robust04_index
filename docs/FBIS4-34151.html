BFN [Text] What is going on within the Revolutionary Front for the Promotion and Progress of Haiti [FRAPH]? In a press release, the front has forbidden Mr. Louis Jodel Chambelain from making any statements on behalf of FRAPH. The general director of the front, namely Paul Arthur Fleurival Lahens, was contacted by phone and he spoke of polarization within the administrative staff. [Begin Lahens recording, in Creole] In any political movement in gestation, there is something called polarization. As you know, the front includes Emmanuel Constant, secretary general of the organization; Louis Jodel Chamblain, national coordinator; and Paul Arthur Fleurival Lahens, general director of the front. There is no problem. As I told you, there is only a polarization problem. But once it is a question of cooperation, everybody can get together. Certainly there are some mistakes. As the saying goes: To err is human. People may make mistakes, but they must not be grave mistakes. There is always a way to reconcile. We are only waiting for the appropriate time so there can be rallying cry. Then you will see that there is really nothing wrong. [end recording]
