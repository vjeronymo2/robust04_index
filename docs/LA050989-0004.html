May 9, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"A lot of people down here are down, and you're down because of the way you live. But you gotta look pretty sometime."

-- Diane Dukes, one of 200 Skid Row women who received the free services of professional makeup artists, hair stylists and manicurists at the downtown Fred Jordan Mission shelter Monday in honor of Mother's Day.
