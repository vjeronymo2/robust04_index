940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / Observer: Scrap it Is the Institute of Directors suffering from delusions of grandeur? Not only has it given itself a fashionable facelift by shifting its initials about, it has justified its move by saying that the new logo will be harder to forge than the old one. Now, there are letterheads that Observer might like to have access to, the Queen's or the chancellor's, for example. The IOD's notepaper happens not to be one of them.
