940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Bemrose Dollars 5.65m buy builds US side Bemrose Corporation, the security and promotional printer, has completed its Dollars 5.65m (Pounds 3.66m) purchase of Ritepoint, a Missouri-based manufacturer of promotional products. Bemrose, which last year used the proceeds of two rights issues to buy Barnard and Jackson, the UK-based diary publishers, and acquire the outstanding 50 per cent of its US joint venture company, said net book value of the assets acquired was Dollars 6.2m. Operating losses for the year to January 29 are estimated at about Dollars 350,000.
