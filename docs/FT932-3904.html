930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Delyn shares rise on 90% advance Shares in Delyn Group rose 16 per cent to 77p yesterday as the paper, packaging and printing group announced a 90 per cent jump in pre-tax profits for the year to January 31. On turnover of Pounds 12.3m (Pounds 13.1m), the pre-tax result of Pounds 998,000 (Pounds 524,000) was achieved after an exceptional charge of Pounds 59,000 (Pounds 42,000). Earnings rose to 7.31p (3.32p) per share and the proposed final dividend is increased to 1.17p for a 1.67p (1.45p) total. Mr Paul Norman, chairman, said the board had considered various options to develop the company and it intended to expand into the housing market.
