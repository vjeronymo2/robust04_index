920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / No deal over HK airport An Anglo-Chinese meeting to discuss financial plans for Hong Kong's multi-billion dollar airport and related projects ended yesterday with no agreement being reached, Simon Holberton reports from Hong Kong. However, British and Chinese officials said they would meet again soon to discuss solutions to the impasse put forward by both sides. No date was given. For the past three months the two sides have made little progress in resolving Chinese concerns about the financing of the HKDollars 175.3bn (Pounds 11.7bn) project.
