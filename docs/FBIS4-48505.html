BFN [Text] The government yesterday demobilized 1,036 soldiers. This represents a record number of soldiers demobilized in a single day since the start of the operation. Yesterday Renamo [Mozambique National Resistance] demobilized 172 troops. A total of 16,879 government and Renamo soldiers have already been demobilized, of which 16,041 are from the government and the remainder from Renamo.
