BFN [Text] Cosponsored by the Shandong branch of the XINHUA News Agency and the Shandong Provincial Commission for Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, WAIXIANG JINGJI DAOBAO [EXPORT-ORIENTED ECONOMIC GUIDE] started publication in Jinan a few days ago. This is the first newspaper of the province that specially serves the development of the export-oriented economy. Leading Comrades Li Lanqing, Jiang Chunyun, Wu Yi, and Zhao Zhihao wrote inscriptions for this newspaper. On 29 April, a ceremony was held at Jinan's Nanjiao Guest House to mark the start of publication of this newspaper. Provincial leading Comrades Song Fatang and Dong Fengji attended the ceremony.
