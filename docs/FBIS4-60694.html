BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 May (MENA) -- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa today received the delegation from the American Association of Chief Editors of Newspapers and Magazines. The delegation includes 14 chief editors and publishers representing some of the major newspapers in the United States. The meeting dealt with Egyptian-U.S. relations, Egypt's leading role in the area, and the important role it is playing in the peace process to achieve stability and security in the area. It also dealt with the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement between the Palestinians and Israelis, which is considered a step toward achieving Palestinian-Israeli peace. The meeting also dealt with the recent developments in Yemen and the Egyptian and U.S. efforts to stop the fighting and restart the dialogue between the Yemeni parties.
