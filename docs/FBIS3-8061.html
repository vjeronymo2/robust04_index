Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Hungarian Government is to grant Hungarian Radio a special fund [celtamogatas] of 105 million forints, earmarked for wage increases and severance pay, [Government Spokeswoman] Judit Juhasz has confirmed. The spokeswoman said that the government had passed special decrees on the legal status of public employees at Hungarian Radio, Hungarian Television, and the Hungarian Telegraphic Agency [MTI]. People can be appointed to leading positions only for five years in all three institutions. [passage omitted]
