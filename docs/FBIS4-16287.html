BFN [Excerpt] Strasbourg, Apr 14, BNS -- The Council of Europe [CE] should respect its own decisions concerning the Baltic states, the head of the Estonian delegation, Tunne Kelam, told a meeting of the CE Parliamentary Assembly today. Kelam, who is also one of the vice presidents of the Parliamentary Assembly, was speaking at a meeting discussing how the council's fresh members had abided by their obligations. Kelam in his address spoke about Estonia's current situation and achievements, a spokesman for the Estonian delegation said. The Estonian delegation this evening also met with the president of the Parliamentary Assembly, Miguel Angel Martinez. Representatives from Estonia, Lithuania and Romania were elected members of the European Court of Human Rights. Estonian delegation member Karin Jaani was elected a co-chairman of the European People's Party group in the assembly. [passage omitted]
