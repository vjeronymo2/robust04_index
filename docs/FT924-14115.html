921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / Gunmen kill local ANC leader A regional leader of Nelson Mandela's ANC and three youths were killed when gunmen fired on a car in which they were travelling, police said yesterday, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Witnesses said Prince Mhlambi, a civic leader in Tokoza and its adjoining squatter settlement of Phola Park, was in the car with five others when ambushed by unknown attackers.
