BFN [Text] The proceedings of the third session of the Parliamentary Assembly of Black Sea Economic Cooperation, which have been held for two days at Bucharest Parliament Palace, concluded today. This afternoon, the new leadership was elected. Aleksandr Moroz, president of Ukraine's Rada was elected president of the assembly. Mr. Vladimir Kovalev, head of the Russian Federation delegation and deputy president of Russia's State Duma, and Afayedin Zhalilov [name as heard], head of the delegation from Azerbaijan, were elected deputy presidents. Mr. Osman Seif of Turkey, member of the Judicial and Political Commission of the assembly, was elected treasurer.
