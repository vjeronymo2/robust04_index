BFN [Text] The national radio has learned that four political parties -- the National Liberation Front [FLN], the Islamic Society Movement [HAMAS], the Islamic Nahda [Movement] Party, and the Movement for Democracy in Algeria -- are meeting today to coordinate their stances regarding the dialogue resumed by President Lamine Zeroual, with political parties, national figures, and social and economic organizations, especially after the presidency official spokesman's meeting with the national press.
