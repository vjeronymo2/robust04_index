BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 23 KYODO -- NEC Corp. said Monday [23 May] it has signed an agreement with the Chinese Academy of Sciences [CAS] to jointly set up a computer software company in Beijing. NEC-CAS Software Laboratories Co. will be established June 15, with capital of about 150 million yen, NEC said. NEC will hold 90 percent of the shares, while the rest will be owned by the Software Development Division of the Chinese Academy of Sciences -- the Institute of Software of the Academia Sinica. The company will develop software technology for personal computers and workstations to meet the growing market demand in China. The company projected sales in the first year will hit 60 million yen and reach 200 million yen by 1999, NEC officials said.
