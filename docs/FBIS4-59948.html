BFN [Text] Ankara, May 17, IRNA -- Iran's Deputy Foreign Minister for Asia-Pacific 'Ala'eddin Borujerdi conveyed President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani's message to his Turkish counterpart Suleyman Demirel in a meeting here Monday. During the meeting the two sides discussed Tehran-Ankara bilateral ties, situation in northern Iraq and the forthcoming visit to Iran of the Turkish president. As Iraq's neighbors, Iran, Turkey and Syria are holding identical views on the preservation of Iraq's territorial integrity. Also, in a meeting with Turkish Minister of Foreign Affairs Hikmet Cetin, the Iranian official discussed promotion of bilateral relations. Borujerdi concluded his one-day visit to Turkey and returned home Tuesday night.
