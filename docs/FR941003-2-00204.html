Internal Revenue Service (IRS) OMB Number: 1545&hyph;0028. Form Number: IRS Forms 940 and 940&hyph;PR. Type of Review: Revision. Title: Employer's Annual Federal Unemployment (FUTA) Tax Return (940) Planilla Para La Declaracion Anual Del Patrono-La Contribucion Para El Desempleo (FUTA) (940&hyph;PR). Description: Internal Revenue Code (IRC) section 3301 imposes a tax on employers based on the first $7,000 of taxable annual wages paid to each employee. IRS uses the information reported on Forms 940 and 940&hyph;PR (Puerto Rico) to ensure that employers have reported and figured the correct FUTA wages and tax.
