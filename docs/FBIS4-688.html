BFN [Report by G. van Oudtshoorn] [Text] Bisho April 13 SAPA -- Four prominent members of former Ciskei military ruler Brig Oupa Gqozo's African Democratic Movement [ADM] have withdrawn as candidates for the elections, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reoported on Wednesday. ADM Publicity Secretary Vuyani Mati confirmed the resignations from the movement, naming the men as: Ciskei Department of Information Assistant Director Namba Sebe; ADM's former Publicity Secretary Vuyo Magodla; Ciskei consul general in Johannesburg Mthetheleli Taba; and Department of Information Acting Director Nolwandle Ngxizela. Earlier this week Ciskei's joint administrators announced that employed political candidates would have to take unpaid leave during the election period.
