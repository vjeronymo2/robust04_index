930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: A&C Black rises 38% to Pounds 0.54m A&C BLACK, the publishing group, lifted its pre-tax profit by 38 per cent to Pounds 535,000 for the year to end-December. Turnover was trimmed from Pounds 6.84m to Pounds 6.78m. Operating profit increased by Pounds 67,000 to Pounds 590,000 and interest payable showed a reduction from Pounds 133,000 to Pounds 61,000. Earnings per share worked through at 24.4p (16.9p) and the final dividend is 9.25p for a total of 13.5p (13p).
