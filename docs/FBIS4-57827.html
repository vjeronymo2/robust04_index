BFN [Text] Today in Rome, Cuba's new ambassador to Italy Mario Rodriguez Martinez asserted that he is impressed by Italian President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro, to whom Rodriguez presented his credentials on 23 May. Rodriguez told journalists that Scalfaro turned out to be very much aware of the problems of Cuba and Latin America. Rodriguez replaces Javier Ardizones, who was Cuba's ambassador to Italy for 10 years. Rodriguez added that among his goals are that of maintaining and further developing the already good political, economic, and trade relations with Italy and that of spreading the truth about Cuba. Rodriguez was very much interested in getting to know the new Italian Government, which defines itself as centrist, and he reiterated that he is very pleased to have met with Scalfaro.
