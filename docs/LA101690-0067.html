October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The Los Angeles-based investor group of Leonard Green & Partners said it has completed its $300-million acquisition of Carr-Gottstein, Alaska's largest supermarket chain . . . Maytag Co. said production and some support operations at its manufacturing facilities in Newton, Iowa, will be suspended next week. The layoffs affect 2,000 employees . . . The Federal Reserve reported that the average yield for one-year T-bills, the most popular index for making changes in adjustable-rate mortgages, rose to 7.62% last week, up from 7.58% the previous week.
