BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Presidential Council, returned to Sanaa this afternoon, winding up a working visit to the sisterly Sultanate of Oman. During the visit, brother 'Abd-al-Ghani conveyed a message from brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council and supreme commander of the Armed Forces, to his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman. The message deals with the latest Yemeni developments and bilateral issues of common interest. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the Presidential Council member explained that he was carrying a reply message to brother Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih from his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman. Brother 'Abd-al-Ghani was seen off at the Omani airport by brother Sayf al-Zawawi, Omani deputy prime minister; Omani officials; and the staff of the Yemeni Embassy in Oman.
