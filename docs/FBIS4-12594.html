BFN [Text] A 16-year-old was killed by soldiers' gunfire in the village of Bal'a in Samaria tonight. He was shot while hurling stones at an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] patrol. A 17-year-old youth sustained moderate injuries when soldiers fired at stone throwers in the village of Qabatiyah. A 35-year-old woman was slightly wounded in the 'Ayn Bayt al-Ma' refugee camp when a stun grenade exploded near her in the course of riots. IDF soldiers dispersed the rioters with stun grenades and rubber bullets. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that two Israeli civilians were slightly wounded from glass splinters when their cars were pelted with stones at the Yaqir Junction and in Ma'ale Levona tonight.
