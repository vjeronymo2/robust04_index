BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Apr (MENA)--Asyut Governor Samih al-Sa'id has said that the situation in his Governorate is stable and that the public is cooperating sincerely and fruitfully with the authorities. Everyone is aware that we are all in the same boat and we have to bring it safely to shore, he stated. The governor called on foreign media not to exaggerate what he describes as isolated incidents in Asyut. Interviewed on "Good Morning, Egypt," a program carried on Cairo Channel 1 Television Network, al-Sa'id said that as of Wednesday [27 April] 73 repentant terrorists had surrendered to Asyut authorities. The governor said that Asyut has several economic, social, and environmental problems, but "everyone is pitching in and we have come a long way this year toward resolving these problems."
