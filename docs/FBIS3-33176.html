Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro has received, in Havana, Dr. Jorge Allende, deputy director of the Pan-American Health Organization, PAHO, with whom he analyzed the new prospects for cooperation between PAHO and our country. The meeting agenda highlighted the close and useful collaboration [between PAHO and Cuba] and the support PAHO is giving Cuba in the establishment of an epidemiological focus in all of the national health system services. Cuban President Fidel Castro also received the Chilean legislators from the Christian Democratic Party, Senator Carmen Frei and Deputy Eugenio Ortega, who are on a working visit to Cuba. During the talks, aspects of the relations between the two countries were discussed and the progress made in areas of common interest was noted.
