October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'Roseanne' Stays Put: Cancel the moving vans. Roseanne Barr and company will be staying in Tinseltown for a while, instead of moving to Minneapolis next year.

Last week, Barr and her husband, Tom Arnold, announced they wanted out of tabloid-infested Los Angeles, and would move production of ABC's "Roseanne" to Minneapolis. But a statement released by the Carsey-Werner Co. with Barr and Arnold said that after exploring the move the couple found the costs "too prohibitive for the 1991-92 season." They said they plan to look into moving in the future.
