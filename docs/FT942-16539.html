940409 FT 09 APR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Officials from the West Indian Grenada Nutmeg Association met reporesentatives of the Indonesian industry in the Netherlands this week, reports Man Producten. They agreed to join efforts to promote a healthier market for nutmegs and mace. The local market in Indonesia was steady this week, thanks to smaller crop figures on the east Indian islands, where nutmeg trees are suffering heavily from insects. The joint effort aims gradually to increase prices to the following levels: abcd/suns USDollars 2,500 a tonne, shrivels/guns Dollars 2,200, bwp Dollars 1,500 and mace Dollars 4,000, all cost and freight Europe. Stocks in Europe remain small.
