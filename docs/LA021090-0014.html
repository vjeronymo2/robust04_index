February 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ETHNIC CONFLICT IN EAST BLOC

Zbigniew Brzezinski may be perilously correct in declaring that ". . . the national and ethnic animosities in Eastern Europe and the Soviet Union . . . could result in . . . geopolitical anarchy." Which is why we should be wary of naming Mikhail Gorbachev savior before the facts are all in.

It could be that the turmoil his reforms are causing within the Communist world will result in civil war within the Soviet Union and -- who knows? -- nuclear war with others. When there are too many fingers on too many buttons . . .

JIM REAGAN

Norwalk
