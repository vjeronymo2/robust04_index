940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Taipei unveils parts deal for F-16 Taiwan will begin production this year of parts for US F-16 fighters under a Dollars 40m contract with Lockheed, with more contracts expected to follow, Reuter reports from Taipei. The US government had for the first time approved technology transfers by Lockheed for eight different parts for the F-16, said Mr Jack Tang, deputy director of the cabinet's Committee for Aviation and Space Industry Development.
