920407 FT 07 APR 92 / International Company News: Nestle takes over at Perrier NESTLE, the Swiss food group, yesterday announced it had formally won control of Perrier, the French mineral water company for which it has fought a fiercely contested FFr15.46bn (Dollars 2.76bn) takeover bid. After months of corporate wrangling the battle came to an amicable conclusion two weeks ago.
