May 6, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DANNEMEYER PROTEST

Re: Gay Activists Protest Dannemeyer Event (April 30): "Dannemeyer Event?"

The other 49 authors (I included) who were honored at the 25th annual UCI Friends of the Library dinner will be astonished and insulted to see it so described.

Did your reporter stay for the event? She -- and the demonstrators -- might have been reminded that libraries exist to house everybody's beliefs and ideas -- including homosexuals and their enemies.

Freedom of speech is our lifeblood, and yours. The hot-eyed censors among us can only be encouraged by this kind of truth-bashing.

JEAN FEMLING

Costa Mesa
