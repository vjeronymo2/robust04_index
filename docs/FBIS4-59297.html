BFN [Text] Belarusian Deputy Prime Minister Mikalay Kostikaw at a meeting in Tehran today with Foreign Minister Velayati gave some explanations about the industrial situation and the commercial ties between Belarus and the rest of the world. He also called for further expansion of diplomatic and economic ties between the two countries. Also at a meeting today with Commerce Minister Ol-Eshaq, Kostikaw said: There are no problems in the way of any economic activities, investment, and private and government sector experience between us and Iran. At the meeting the commerce minister said: In terms of specialist manpower and industrial and agricultural products and political security, your country possesses a combination of growth factors. We shall try so that mutual ties are expanded in favour of both countries. The Belarus deputy prime minister also met and conferred with Mr. Akbar Torkan, minister of roads and transport, this afternoon.
