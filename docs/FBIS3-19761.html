Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar 21 (AA) -- The Constitutional Court on Monday [21 March] reversed parliament's decision to remove Democracy Party (DEP) Deputy Selim Sadak's parliamentary immunity. The court refused to reverse parliament's decision concerning the immunity of the other five DEP deputies and an independent deputy. Parliament on March 2, removed the immunity of six DEP members, a former party member, an anti-Kemalist and an independent. The DEP deputies, who were arrested last week by the court, are charged with crimes against the state.
