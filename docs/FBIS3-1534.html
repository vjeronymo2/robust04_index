Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Yoweri Museveni has received a special envoy from Libya, who delivered to him a special message from the Libyan head of state, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. The envoy Dr. ('Abd-al-Hafiz Zubayd) met the president at his home in (Rwakitura) Mbarara District. He was accompanied by the Libyan ambassador to Uganda Mr. 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Mawlah Abu-Jildayn.
