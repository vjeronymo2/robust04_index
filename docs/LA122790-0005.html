December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR

Henry N' Harry's Goat Hill Tavern, 1830 Newport Blvd., Costa Mesa.

Open daily from 11 a.m. to 2 a.m. (714) 548-8428. This place serves no fewer than 96 brands of tap beer; the keg room looks like Jerry Lee Lewis' idea of a well-stocked bomb shelter. The tavern proper consists of several rooms, packed with games and decorated with everything from buffalo heads to Elvis dolls. Best of all, there are, to quote a regular, "people from all over the world here, all kinds, slobs and business people, and they let it all down." JIM WASHBURN
