921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: AB sells offshoot for Pounds 2.74m AB ELECTRONIC Products is selling the capital of Swansea Industrial Components and land in Swansea to Mr Thomas Roberts, an AB director. Total consideration is Pounds 2.74m which will be used to reduce indebtedness. It consists of cancelling Mr Roberts' holding of 2.09m ordinary shares in AB, valued at Pounds 1.24m on October 22, and the issue of Pounds 1m secured loan stock at par by SIC to AB. Mr Roberts will also pay Pounds 500,000 cash for the land, and leave the board.
