920415 FT 15 APR 92 / UK Company News: West Industries net loss at Pounds 7.5m Net attributable losses at West Industries, the mechanical engineer, more than doubled in the year to March 31 1991. They were up from Pounds 3.34m to Pounds 7.55m, equal to 13.11p losses per share against 2.98p. Sales improved from Pounds 22.8m to Pounds 24.5m, but pre-tax losses rose six-fold to Pounds 6.3m (Pounds 1.08m). Extraordinary charges came to Pounds 1.97m (Pounds 2.23m).
