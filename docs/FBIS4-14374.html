BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] has received a letter from British Prime Minister John Major, in response to a letter from 'Arafat on the obstacles preventing the implementation of the PLO-Israeli agreement and the withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho. In his letter, Mr. Major stressed that the United Kingdom would continue to give its full support for the peace process. He said: We and our partners within the European Union are prepared use our means to help politically and financially.
