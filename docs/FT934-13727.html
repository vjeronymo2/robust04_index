931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Unisys up as revenues slip UNISYS, the US computer and electronics group, lifted earnings in its third quarter in spite of a decline in revenues blamed on currency exchange rates and weak sales in Europe and Japan. Net income for the quarter was Dollars 84.1m, or 29 cents a share, compared with Dollars 68.3m, or 23 cents last time. Excluding the positive effects of tax items in each year, earnings per share in the quarter were 20 cents, against 17 cents a year ago. Revenue dipped to Dollars 1.81bn from Dollars 2.07bn in the third quarter last year. For the nine-month period, net income was Dollars 447.7m, or Dollars 1.83, including a net gain of Dollars 203.8m, or 83 cents per share. Last year net income was Dollars 222m, or 79 cents, including Dollars 27m, or 16 cents, from tax benefits. Revenue was Dollars 5.64bn, compared with Dollars 6.17bn.
