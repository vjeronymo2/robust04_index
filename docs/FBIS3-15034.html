Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 01, BNS -- The most recent public opinion poll in Lithuania indicates that President Algirdas Brazauskas continues to top the list of most popular politicians after a year spent in office. The principal tendency remains the same -- political leaders are gradually losing popularity. Aloyzas Sakalas, leader of the Social Democratic Party, was the only exception during the period between mid-January and mid-February.
