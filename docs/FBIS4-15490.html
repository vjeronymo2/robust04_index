BFN [Text] "The Belarusian Government has not announced any official position regarding Russia's desire to set up its military bases in the CIS states," head of the governmental board of national security, Major-General Valeriy Pavlov told INTERFAX. At the same time, he noted that "alien strategic units cannot be stationed in Belarus, because that would run contrary to Belarus' aspiration to neutrality and non-nuclear status." In the opinion of Valeriy Pavlov, "the present of Russian aviation bases or air-defense units in Belarus would help Belarussian colleagues to maintain "good condition," to get familiarized with the latest examples of equipment and arms and to resolve social problems." Valeriy Pavlov noted that "all options of the presence of foreign military bases in Belarus will be thoroughly studied."
