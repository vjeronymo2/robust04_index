Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents] [Text] Paris/Rome/Jakarta February 28 TASS -- Bosnian Serbs, backed by helicopters and armoured personnel carriers, launched an attack Monday morning against the Bosnian Muslim town of Maglaj. The town besieged by Serbian and Croatian units is under heavy gun fire, the FRANCE-PRESSE news agency reported today. The American aircraft carrier "Saratoga" with 70 war planes aboard has cut short her visit to Trieste in Italy and is heading towards the cost of former Yugoslavia, the Italian news agency AGI reported from Rome today. The agency said it was connected with the morning shooting down of four Bosnian Serbs' aircraft by NATO. Indonesia, currently chairing the Non-Alignment Movement, demanded an urgent session of the UN Security Council to lift the embargo on weapons deliveries to Bosnian Muslims, TASS reported from Jakarta today.
