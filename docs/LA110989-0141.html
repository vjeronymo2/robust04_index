November 9, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BEATLES: The popular demand for obscure Fab Four material never has dried up, says Mike Lefebvre, whose Pepperland Records in Anaheim specializes in Beatles music. . . . And now, with the settlement of various lawsuits surrounding the group (F4), a potential bonanza awaits. . . . "We may finally see the light of day for unreleased tracks," Lefebvre said. "There's some really incredible things out there." Dana Parsons and Rick VanderKnyff
