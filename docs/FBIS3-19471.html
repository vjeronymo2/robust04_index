Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn -- Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' met with Chancellor Helmut Kohl for an exchange of views in Bonn this evening. The main topics were probably the peace process in the Middle East and the further development of German-Syrian relations. The Chancellor's Office did not reveal any details. On Thursday [17 March], the Syrian foreign minister will hold talks with Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany). Kinkel will host a dinner for his counterpart at the government's guest house in Petersberg near Bonn tomorrow evening.
