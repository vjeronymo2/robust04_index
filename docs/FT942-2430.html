940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Bomb explodes at Turkish rally Five Turks were wounded yesterday when a bomb exploded in an Istanbul square shortly before the start of a social democrat rally. The explosion occurred in Fatih district as Mr Bulent Ecevit, leader of the Democratic Left party and a former prime minister, was due to address thousands of people gathered for a local election campaign. No group has claimed responsibility for the bomb attack. Mr Ecevit, a leading anti-fundamentalist figure in Turkey, has recently stepped up criticism of radical Islamist movements.
