BFN [Text] Employees of Ukraine's Security Service have conducted an operation in Mariupol to seize and expose a group of foreign citizens, who had intended to commit a terrorist act in Moscow. For this they had made powerful explosive devices with a long fragmentation range. Many of the details of the operation are for the moment not subject to publication. But, as Colonel Dmytro Chornousenko, deputy chief of Ukraine's Security Service directorate in Donetsk Oblast, stated, it has been established that the detainees had intended to carry out a terrorist act in one of the embassies located in Russia's capital.
