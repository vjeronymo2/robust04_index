Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] YALA -- A camp of the Barisan Revolusi Nasional (BRN) separatists in Kabang sub-district was seized by a government patrol after a clash yesterday morning. The clash occurred around 7.50 a.m. when the army patrol from the Civilian-Police-Military Command 43 under Capt Thara Panyachan launched a surprise attack on a hilltop camp near Ban Cho Ludi in Tambon Bala. About five bandits, believed to be led by Suwa Khao of the BRN, fled the camp after the 20-minute battle. The government force, on the seventh day of its patrol, seized five huts and some food.
