BFN [Text] This morning, in an attempt to widen the corridor through Bosanska Posavina, the Serbs again launched a heavy artillery and infantry attack on the free territory of Brcko municipality. Over 400 tank, howitzer, and mortar shells have landed on the Croatian village of (Bodariste). All the lines jointly held by members of the Croat Defense Council [HVO] and the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army were also pounded. Three infantry forays were attempted during these attacks, during which two HVO soldiers were killed and six wounded.
