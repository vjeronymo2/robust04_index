Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] AL-RA'Y has learned that the Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Amman intends to reduce the number of diplomats working in the embassy. By the end of the month, 21 Iranian diplomats working in the Iranian Embassy are expected to leave Amman in response to a Jordanian Government request that the Iranian Government reduce the number of diplomats working in its mission in Jordan to a number equal to that of the staff of the Jordanian diplomatic mission in Tehran. The Iranian Government responded favorably to the Jordanian Government's request to reduce the number of staff at its diplomatic mission in Jordan.
