930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Swedes turning against EC A growing number of Swedes are saying 'no' to joining the European Community, according to an opinion poll yesterday, Reuter reports from Stockholm. A survey by the daily Dagens Nyheter and polling institute Temo showed 45 per cent against membership, with 31 per cent in favour. The figures a year ago were 38 and 39 per cent.
