Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, (A.A) -- Muhyettin Ayata, the Republican People's Party mayorship candidate for the Kayapinar township in Diyarbakir, has been killed following an armed attack. Ayata's wife and brother-in-law were also seriously wounded in the attack. According to information received from authorities, Ayata, his wife, and his brother-in-law were attacked by unidentified persons near the Huzurevi neighborhood last night around 2300. Muhyettin Ayata (50) was killed instantly. Rahime Ayata (38) and her brother-in-law Abdurrahman Bulbul (29) were taken to the Dicle University Faculty of Medicine Hospital for treatment. Efforts are under way to catch the assailants.
