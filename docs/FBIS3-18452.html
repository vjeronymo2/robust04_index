Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Now that he has concluded his official visit to Madrid the Argentine President Carlos Menem this afternoon begins a private visit in Santiago de Compostela in northwest Spain. Carlos Menem will also visit Catalonia. This morning President Menem attended a breakfast at the headquarters of the Spanish Confederation of Business Organizations with Spanish employers and presidents of Spanish banks and large companies. During the breakfast he urged the Spanish businessmen to continue to have confidence in Argentina and invited them to invest in his country. Carlos Menem stressed the important political transformation of his country during the past few years, in particular concerning economic policy, which has enabled Argentina to reduce inflation from 22,000 [as heard] percent in 1990 to 7.4 percent last year.
