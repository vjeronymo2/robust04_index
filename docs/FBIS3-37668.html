Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 20 Feb -- There will be no air strike by NATO air forces on Bosnian Serb positions tonight, as envisaged by the North Atlantic bloc's ultimatum. Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev said this in an interview today on NTV television's "Itogi" program. He noted that that "this had become quite clear" after his telephone conversation today with U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry.
