National Small Business Development Center Advisory Board; Public Meeting The National Small Business Development Center Advisory Board will hold a public meeting on September 18, 1994, through noon September 20, 1994, at the University of Hawaii Small Business Development Center, 523 West Lanikaula Street, Hilo, Hawaii. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss such matters as may be presented by Advisory Board members, staff of the SBA, or others present.
