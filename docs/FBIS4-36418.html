BFN [Text] Our informed military sources in Sanaa inform that there is an obscure difference between the ranks of the commanders and officers in north Yemen on the issue of the failed war which was led by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih against the people in the Democratic Republic of Yemen. The information says that secret meetings were held during the last three days between the groups of the non-Sanhanian [not further identified] commanders, which made 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih issue orders to collect all the forces and means that remain with him and to raise his photographs [as heard] on them in 48 camps in an attempt to delude the others that he is still strong after it had become certain to him that his recent directives to enter Aden had failed. The sources confirmed that time for 'Ali Salih is no more gold [as heard] and he is now thinking of what will secure the establishment of the regime of the iron fist in Sanaa.
