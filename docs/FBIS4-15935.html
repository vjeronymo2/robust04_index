BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] The Committee on Security and Defense of the Federation Council of the Russian Federal Assembly has held closed hearings on the problems of space and air defenses. Servicemen and those representing military sciences and industries said they are concerned about the state of space and air defenses. There is also concern over the implementation of the Russian president's decree setting up these defenses. The Government of Russia will be appraised of the conclusions reached by the hearings.
