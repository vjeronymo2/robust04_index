September 15, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

CYPRESS COLLEGE WINS IN WATER POLO, 10-6

Eric Coolbaugh scored three goals and Craig Garcia had two as Cypress College defeated De Anza, 10-6, in a nonconference water polo match Thursday at Cypress.

Pat Ruzgerian had 12 saves for Cypress (2-0).

De Anza is 6-1.
