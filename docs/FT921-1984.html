920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / UK Company News: James Halstead improves to Pounds 3.44m In 'very tough trading conditions' James Halstead, the floor coverings, weatherproof wear and trailers group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.44m for the half year to December 31. Profits last time were Pounds 3.34m. man, said the core floor coverings business had maintained its share of UK and export markets and had increased its profits. As already indicated, Belstaff International continued to incur a loss and Mr Clare said its closure should be completed by the year-end. An extraordinary Pounds 3m will be included in the full-year accounts to cover the estimated closure costs. Earnings per share worked through at 15.56p (14.9p) and the interim dividend is lifted from 4.25p to 4.5p. Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 28.9m (Pounds 28.2m).
