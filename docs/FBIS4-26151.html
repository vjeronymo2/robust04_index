BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 19 (XINHUA) -- The Hangzhou City Intermediate People's Court [IPC] convened a sentencing rally in Hangzhou this morning to announce the judgment of the Zhejiang Provincial Higher People's Court approving the death sentence for Wu Lihong, Hu Zhihan, and Yu Aijun, the three criminals guilty of the heinous crime of robbery and murder in Qiandao Lake, Chunan County, on 31 March. Leaders of Hangzhou city and representatives from all walks of life and relevant departments of the province and city -- 1,200 in all -- were present at the sentencing rally. Yang Gang, president of the Hangzhou City IPC, presided over the rally. Wang Zhijian, chief judge of the First Criminal Division of the Hangzhou City IPC, read the adjudication. After reviewing the death sentences, the Zhejiang Provincial Higher People's Court decided to approve the sentences -- death and deprivation of political rights for life -- for Wu Lihong, Hu Zhihan, and Yu Aijun for the crime of robbery and murder. The court decided the cumulative punishment of death and deprivation of political rights for life be carried out. Wang Yongming, mayor of Hangzhou, spoke at the rally. He pointed out that the sentencing rally fully reflects the purpose of judicial organs in upholding justice and being loyal to the law and people. It fully reflects the party and government's firm determination to safeguard public order, to severely punish criminals, and to protect the safety of life and property of the broad masses of the people as well as compatriots from Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, and overseas Chinese, and foreign nationals within Chinese territory. The severe punishment for the three criminals in accordance with law not only defends the dignity of law but also serves as the best consolation for the deceased compatriots. After the sentencing rally, the three criminals Wu Lihong, Hu Zhihan, and Yu Aijun were, after verification of their identity, escorted to the execution ground and shot in pursuance of an order of execution issued by the president of the Zhejiang Provincial Higher People's Court.
