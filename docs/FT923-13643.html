920711 FT 11 JUL 92 / Finance & The Family: A Wellcome reminder Time is running out for those who want to take advantage of the Wellcome share flotation. The offer closes on Tuesday July 21, but Wednesday July 15 is the effective deadline if you want to register with the Share Information Office (tel: 081-944-1242). On registration, the SIO will send a prospectus and application form. Anyone who does not register by then will need to collect a form from 'key' branches of Lloyds Bank. If the offer is oversubscribed, priority will be given to early applicants.
