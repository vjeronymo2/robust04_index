930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Great Portland GREAT PORTLAND Estates has paid Pounds 13.77m for three properties in Milton Keynes, West Bromwich and Bradford, being the entire holdings of two private companies, Thorntop and its associate, Oakport Properties. Consideration to be met as to Pounds 462,500 in cash and issue of 8.5m new ordinary GPE shares.
