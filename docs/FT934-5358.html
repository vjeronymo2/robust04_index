931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Stakis in Pounds 6m airport venture Stakis, the hotel and casino group which returned to profit last year, is to spend Pounds 6m on building a 150-room hotel at Edinburgh airport, its first construction for 3 1/2 years and the first to be built at the airport. It will be built in partnership with BAA, the airport's operator, which will lease the land to Stakis and take a percentage of turnover in rent.
