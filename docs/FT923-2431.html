920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Helical Bar returns to black with Pounds 0.5m Helical Bar, the property investment, development and trading company, returned to profit in the first half to July 31 with Pounds 536,000 pre-tax, against losses of Pounds 694,000. For the year to January there were losses of Pounds 6.49m. Mr John Southwell, chairman, said that in spite of poor conditions, Helical sold more than Pounds 14m of property in the first half at above book cost, reducing net borrowing to Pounds 87m (Pounds 120m) at July 31. Turnover fell to Pounds 15m (Pounds 21m). Earnings per share improved to 6.1p (0.2p) but the interim dividend is held at 2.4p.
