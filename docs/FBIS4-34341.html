BFN [Report by Jose Antonio Sarantes] [Text] The installation of emergency electric plants at the television, Radio Rebelde, and Radio Progreso transmitter facilities is one of the tasks that Pinar del Rio communicators are curently engaged in to salute the 26 July anniversary. An enormous effort is being exerted by technicians and specialists in this area at Vuelta Abajo to leave the so-called emergency plants ready and able to keep equipment operating in case of a blackout.
