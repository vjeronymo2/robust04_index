BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 Apr (MENA)--Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif and United Arab Emirates [UAE] Foreign Minister Rashid 'Abdallah al-Na'imi returned to Cairo this evening after conveying messages from President Husni Mubarak and UAE President Shaykh Zayid Ibn-Sultan to Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Al-Sharif said on his arrival that he will brief President Mubarak on the results of the visit.
