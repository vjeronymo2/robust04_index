BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 29 Apr (NA) -- UCR [Radical Civic Union] President Raul Alfonsin was elected on 29 April as president of the UCR bloc in the Constituent Assembly and Cordoba UCR leader Ramon Mestre was appointed first vice presidential candidate for the Constituent Assembly that will meet in the cities of Santa Fe and Parana. Both decisions were made during the first 10 minutes of a meeting the UCR Constituent Assembly members are holding at the party's National Committee headquarters in Buenos Aires' Congreso neighborhood. The motion for Alfonsin to preside over the UCR bloc was made by Antonio Hernandez, a pro-[Cordoba Governor Eduardo] Angeloz Constituent Assembly member from Cordoba, while Cesar Jaroslavsky, a pro-Alfonsin member from Buenos Aires, moved that Mestre be nominated first vice president of the Constituent Assembly.
