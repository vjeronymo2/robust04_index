BFN [Text] Ambassador 'Umar Yusuf Baridu, the first under secretary of the Foreign Ministry, has denied reports carried by some news agencies and media organs on Sudanese troops taking part in the ongoing war in Yemen. He added that the propagation of such allegations may be aimed at widening the scope of the war using it as a pretext for foreign intervention in the ongoing conflict in Yemen. Ambassador Baridu reaffirmed Sudan's principled attitude toward what was taking place in fraternal Yemen as exemplified by Sudan's eagerness to see the crisis approached within a Yemeni context. He said Sudan's position was based on clear and specific principles, including noninterference in the internal affairs of states and commitment to the charters of the United Nations, OAU, and the Arab League.
