Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Minister Tharin Nimmanhemin says it has been agreed that the meeting of APEC [Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation] finance ministers, to be held during 18-19 March in Hawaii, will discuss economic growth strategies, the role of revolving funds in national development, and the development of local financial markets. It has also been agreed that the meeting will not be used for forced access to financial markets or examination of a country's economic policies. He said that he would head the seminar on the topic of the role of revolving funds in national development. The finance minister disclosed that his delegation has been changed to include a representative of the office of the securities exchange management committee to cover the meeting's agenda. The delegation originally included only representatives of the Finance Ministry and the Bank of Thailand.
