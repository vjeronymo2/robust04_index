May 17, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

BRITAIN;

BOMB KILLS SOLDIER; SECOND IS WOUNDED

A bomb blast outside an army recruiting office in London killed a British sergeant and wounded another, and the armed forces minister said the attack was part of a "campaign of terror" by Irish nationalists. The rush-hour explosion in north London came two days after a bomb planted by the Irish Republican Army injured seven people at an army education center in the southern part of the capital.
