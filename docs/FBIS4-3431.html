BFN [Text] Beijing, April 20 (XINHUA) -- Liu Shuqing, president of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs (CPIFA) met with and hosted a dinner for a delegation from Germany's Baden- Weuttemberg State here today. The 9-member delegation, headed by the state's minister-president Erwin Teufel, is here as guest of CPIFA. It is accompanied by more than 100 entrepreneurs from Baden-Weuttemberg.
