930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Growth at Capital Capital and Regional Properties lifted pre-tax profit from Pounds 367,000 to Pounds 422,000 for the year to December 25, without any property sales. Net rental and fee income rose from Pounds 4.66m to Pounds 6.03m but net interest payable increased from Pounds 3.09m to Pounds 3.84m. In 1991 property sales brought in Pounds 666,000 and there were exceptional costs of Pounds 85,000. Earnings came out at 0.41p (1.26p) and the final dividend is 0.8p for a total of 1.1p (1p).
