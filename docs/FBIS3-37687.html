Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Georgiy Shmelev and Aleksandr Yakovlev] [Text] Prague, 20 Feb -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev believes that the views of the Russian and Czech sides coincide: Both believe that the efforts of the world community should now be concentrated on consolidating the positive elements that have emerged in the settlement of the crisis in Bosnia, especially after the message from Russian President Boris Yeltsin. Kozyrev said this at a news conference for Czech and foreign journalists at the end of his talks with Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus. In addition to current international problems, questions pertaining to the further development of bilateral relations were also discussed at the talks.
