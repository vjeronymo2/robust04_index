Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jan (MENA)--The areas of al-Ma'sarah, al-Huda, and Wadi Hawf in south Cairo were the scene of intensive security sweeps early this morning involving units from the State Intelligence Department, the Traffic Department, the Central Security Forces, and regular officers. The forces imposed a security cordon around the three areas while other forces carried out sweeps to arrest fugitive terrorist and criminal elements. A police unit manning a highway roadblock arrested one terrorist and wounded another. Two more escaped. The four terrorists were riding in a pickup truck. Police fired on the truck after the four refused to stop at the roadblock. The sweeps resulted in the arrest of 60 fugitive criminals. Two criminals were caught redhanded with a load of stolen iron in their car.
