Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 6 (KCNA) -- U.S. Secretary of state Warren Christopher was reported Thursday [3 March] to formally announce that the third round of DPRK-U.S. talks will be held in Geneva on March 21 as agreed between the two countries. Robert Gallucci, U.S. assistant secretary of state, will head the U.S. delegation to the third round negotiation, the State Department spokesman said in a statement on March 3.
