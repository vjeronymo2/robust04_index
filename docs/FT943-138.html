940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Linton higher A recovery in its Malawi operations and the inclusion of results from British African Tea Estates, acquired in January, enabled Linton Park to report pre-tax profits up from a restated Pounds 5.49m to Pounds 6.06m in the half year to end-June. The tea and coffee producer, importer and exporter, which also has interests in Scottish fishing, lifted turnover to Pounds 78.2m (Pounds 64.9m) including Pounds 6.99m from acquisitions. The interim dividend is doubled to 5p, payable from earnings per share of 18.3p (20.3p). Linton is ultimately owned by Lawrie Group.
