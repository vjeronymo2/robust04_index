920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG closed off the day's high as an early upswing lost momentum. The all-gold index ended up 11 at 1,325, off an early high of 1,331. The industrial index eased 2 to 4,512 after touching 4,526 earlier. The all-share index added 7 to 3,697.
