June 2, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

O.C. RELIGION NOTES: WORLD INVOCATION DAY TO BE HELD

World Invocation Day is the theme of a Festival of Goodwill to be held Fridayat the Unitarian Church of Orange County, 1120 W. Santa Ana St., Anaheim.

The event includes music, meditation and a presentation by educator Marilyn Mueller on the theme of World Invocation Day.

World Invocation Day, which was brought to the West through the writings of Alice Bailey, is an annual event of inter-faith cooperation and goodwill. On this day, members of meditation and prayer groups throughout the world unite in the use of a world prayer, the Great Invocation, to focus their massed invocation and to channel spiritual light and strength into human consciousness. The Great Invocation is based on truths common to all major religions and is used daily by millions of people all over the world. For information, call Dana Drake at (213) 435-9159.
