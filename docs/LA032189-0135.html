March 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Kenneth W. Bray has been appointed to the newly created position of vice president, direct operations, for Zee Medical Inc., a subsidiary of McKesson Corp. He will supervise the operation of the company's eight regional sales centers in the United States and Canada. Previously, Bray was vice president, strategic planning, for several McKesson operations, including Zee Medical. Based in Irvine, Zee Medical supplies first-aid and safety services and products to businesses.
