BFN [Text] His Excellency [H.E.] Vo Van Kiet, prime minister of the SRV, and his colleagues arrived in Phnom Penh, capital of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at 0910 this morning for an official two-day visit from 2 to 3 April 1994, at the invitation of the heads of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen. Upon its arrival at Pochentong international airport, the high-ranking SRV delegation was highly and cordially welcomed by Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister, and Samdech Hun Sen, second prime minister of the RGC. Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation; H.E. Sar Kheng, deputy prime minister and co-minister of interior and security; and many officials, and youth scouts were also there. Diplomats from various countries accredited to the Kingdom of Cambodia were also present. It is expected that during this visit H.E. Vo Van Kiet will meet with Samdech Chea Sim, acting head of state and chairman of the National Assembly, and hold bilateral talks with Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen.
