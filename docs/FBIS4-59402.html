BFN [Excerpts] President Hafiz al-Asad received Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah at al-Rawdah Palace at 1230 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the audience. Basindwah arrived in Damascus yesterday evening. [passage omitted] Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, Yemeni minister of oil and mineral wealth, has left Damascus for Amman at the conclusion of a two-day visit to Syria. He was seen off at the airport by Dr. Nadir Nabulsi, minister of oil and mineral resources.
