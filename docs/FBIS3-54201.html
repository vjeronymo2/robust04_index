Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Hamid al-Ghabid, secretary general of the Islamic Conference Organization [ICO], has confirmed the organization's backing and support for the efforts of the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development committee to achieve peace in southern Sudan. He said ICO is in constant contact with the committee. Dr. al-Ghabid added that the organization is working to strengthen Sudan's integrity and the safety of its territory. He referred to the ICO resolutions in its last session supporting this trend. Yesterday, Foreign Minister Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih discussed with Dr. Hamid al-Ghabid issues facing the Muslim nation and relations between Sudan and ICO. Dr. Abu-Salih stressed the role of the organization in achieving Islamic solidarity.
