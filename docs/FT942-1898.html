940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / People: Lloyds Bank Lloyds Bank has appointed Kent Atkinson, 49, as its chief financial officer from next January. He will succeed Colin Wilks, who retires at the end of this year after completing 37 years' service with the bank. Atkinson, who joined the Bank of London and South America in 1964 before it was acquired by Lloyds, worked in South America and the Middle East. He moved to the UK in 1989 and is general manager for UK banking retail operations.
