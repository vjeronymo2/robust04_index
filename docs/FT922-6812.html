920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Essex Furniture Essex Furniture is raising Pounds 1.3m net by way of a rights issue mainly to fund expansion prospects. The issue is on a 1-for-3 basis at 43p. This compares with yesterday's closing price of 52p, down 4p. The directors and families own 79 per cent of the capital and have renounced rights to the issue in favour of clients of Williams de Broe.
