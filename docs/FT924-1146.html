921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Sycamore improves 13% to Pounds 620,000 Sycamore Holdings, the maker of foundry products, moisture testing equipment, and kitchen and garden furniture, lifted pre-tax profits by 13 per cent, from Pounds 549,000 to Pounds 620,000, in the year to September 30. However, Mr Bill Rhodes, chairman, said the results were below directors' expectations and again no dividend is being recommended. Earnings came through at 0.61p (1p) per share. Turnover jumped to Pounds 20.9m (Pounds 9.5m) generating operating profits of Pounds 1.25m (Pounds 654,000). Interest charges took more at Pounds 635,000 (Pounds 189,000).
