Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Indonesia has asked the UN Security Council to pay serious attention and not neglect its responsibility in Israeli-occupied Palestine territory following the recent massacre of more than 50 Palestinians. (Isa Slamet Sudarga), deputy permanent representative to the United Nations, said Indonesia hopes the Security Council will not only end such atrocities against the Palestinians, but also make efforts to find a peaceful and comprehensive solution to the Middle East conflict. Therefore, along with Palestine, Indonesia called on the Security Council to take concrete actions to ensure that Israel performs its duty to protect Palestinian citizens in accordance with the Geneva Convention. Indonesia also fully supported the resolution adopted by the Organization of Islamic Conference which called on the UN Security Council to discuss the massacre so as to end the Israeli oppression and safeguard the safety of Palestinians through the presence of international observers.
