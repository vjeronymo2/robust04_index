920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Building products side mars Folkes A deterioration in the performance of its engineering and building products divisions resulted in a 24 per cent decline at Folkes Group in the first half of 1992. Pre-tax profits fell from Pounds 850,000 to Pounds 650,000 and sales contracted to Pounds 20.4m (Pounds 23.4m). Interest paid rose to Pounds 73,000 (Pounds 3,000). Directors said that the property division maintained profits at the same level as last year. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.575p on earnings per share of 1.25p (1.57p).
