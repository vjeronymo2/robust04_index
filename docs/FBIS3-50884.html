Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The 24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Cuauhtemoc Cardenas Solorzano, presidential candidate for the Party of the Democratic Revolution [PRD], expanded his position regarding the situation in Chiapas State and demanded new mechanisms to open up dialogue. [Begin Cardenas recording] This conflict has already adversely affected the electoral process, regardless of its outcome. [end recording] During a ceremony at a hotel in downtown Mexico City, Mr. Cardenas Solorzano accepted the support of the Broad Socialist Left Party [PAIS] for his presidential candidacy. The PRD candidate said the road leading to the solution of the Chiapas conflict is a negotiation and an immediate cease-fire.
