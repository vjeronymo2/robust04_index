940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / International Company News: Accor calls for SGB investment Accor would like Societe Generale de Belgique, a unit of Compagnie de Suez, to boost its stake in the hotel group to become its main shareholder, Mr Paul Dubrule, Accor co-chairman said, Reuter reports from Paris. 'We would like SGB to have this position but if they do not want to increase their stake, we will seek another partner,' he said. SGB has 12.4 per cent of Accor.
