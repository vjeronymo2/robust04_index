930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / International Capital Markets: S&P rates Dollars 1bn of Disney debt STANDARD & Poor's, the US credit rating agency, has assigned its double-A-minus rating to Walt Disney's shelf registration of Dollars 1bn of senior unsecured debt securities, Reuter reports. It also affirmed its ratings on other Disney debt. It said the ratings 'reflect Disney's well-diversified business mix and distinctive product franchises'.
