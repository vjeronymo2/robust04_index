941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Ewart improves Ewart announced a 46 per cent upturn in interim profits, as the Belfast-based property group continued to concentrate on combining steady rental growth with a 'low-risk approach' to development. Turnover in the half year to October 31 amounted to Pounds 2.66m, compared with Pounds 3.32m. After a reduced interest burden, the pre-tax line emerged at Pounds 511,000 (Pounds 351,000). The interim dividend goes up to 0.5p (0.4p), payable from earnings of 1.66p (1.86p) per share. This was on capital increased by the placing and open offer in the summer.
