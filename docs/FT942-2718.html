940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto equities followed the trend in Canadian bonds, recovering from an early fresh decline to leave the TSE 300 composite index just 2.8 off at 4,163.4. Volume was moderate but rose to 64m shares from Wednesday's 54.7m. Eleven of the 14 stock groups were lower, with real estate and golds recording the largest losses of 1.37 and 1.17 per cent respectively. Banking issues rebounded from the previous day's fall, rising 2.33 per cent.
