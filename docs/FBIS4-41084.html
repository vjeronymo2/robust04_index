BFN ["Decree of the Georgian Parliament on Ratification of the Convention on Cooperation Between Black Sea Countries in the Cultural, Educational, Scientific, and Information Sphere"] [Text] The Georgian Parliament decrees: To ratify the Convention on Cooperation Between Black Sea Countries in the Cultural, Educational, Scientific, and Information Sphere. [Signed] Vakhtang Goguadze, speaker of the Georgian Parliament. Tbilisi, 14 June 1994.
