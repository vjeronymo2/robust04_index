General Summary of Comments The proposed rule was published in the Federal Register (59 FR 26146) on May 19, 1994. Comments from interested parties were due on or before the close of business on June 3, 1994. Twenty-two letters were received on time from entities concerned with issues regarding the cotton and grain USWA changes. Because of the definite division of the comments between cotton and grain, our ``Summary of Comments'' will be separated accordingly.
