BFN [Text] Since early this year, the Tuyen Quang Province Electricity Authority has made considerable investment in building many rural electrification projects to support production and the daily activities of the local multiethnic compatriots. Thanks to its good preparations in material, means, and manpower, the electricity authority has completed a 35-kilovolt transformer station and the Tuyen Quang-Ham Yen powerline ahead of schedule in order to channel electricity promptly from the national power grid to serve the multiethnic compatriots of Ham Yen. At present, the provincial electricity authority is concentrating its capital, cadres, engineers, and means on completing the construction of the Tuyen Quang-Son Duong and Ham Yen-Chien Hoa powerlines and their supporting 35-kilovolt transformer stations in time to celebrate this year's National Day.
