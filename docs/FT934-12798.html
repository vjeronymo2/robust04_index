931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / Observer: Run out Well done the Toronto Blue Jays. Last year, Canada's constitutional referendum coincided with the Blue Jays' victory parade, after the baseball players became the first non-US team to win the World Series. In an encore this weekend, they comfortably retained the title ahead of today's general election. Sadly, Blue Jay-induced euphoria bodes ill for the ruling Progressive Conservative party. Last year, Brian Mulroney's constitutional proposal was firmly rejected, and now it looks as if Mulroney's successor, Kim Campbell, is poised for a resounding defeat. She could always take up baseball.
