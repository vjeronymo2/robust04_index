930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Exceptional charge leaves Hawtin lower Hawtin, the leisure and fitness group which also has interests in textiles and property, was hit by an exceptional charge of Pounds 492,000 which reduced pre-tax profits to Pounds 1.04m for the year ended September 30, against Pounds 1.4m previously. Before the item - relating to a liability on a London property lease - profits were 9 per cent higher at Pounds 1.53m. Earnings per share were 1.04p (1.71p) and the dividend has been increased from 0.86p to 0.89p.
