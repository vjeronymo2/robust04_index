920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / UK Company News: United Forgemasters management buy-out Wheway, the West Midlands-based engineering group, and Chillington, the conglomerate which was formerly a plantation company, have respectively sold United Forgemasters and Thomas Smith, both forging companies, to Woodcote Industries, a new group, for a total of Pounds 2m. Woodcote is a management buy-out company set up by the senior management of United Forgemasters led by Mr Jim Mundell. Woodcote paid Pounds 1.5m for United Forgemasters and Pounds 500,000 for Smith.
