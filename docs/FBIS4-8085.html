BFN [Text] Belgrade, 11 Apr (TANJUG) -- Radovan Karadzic, president of the Bosnian Serb Republic has cancelled his meeting with U.S. special envoy Charles Redman, scheduled for today, explaining the cancellation with the newly arisen situation. Jovan Zametica, the Serb Republic president's adviser, tonight sent a letter to Viktor Andreyev, the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] spokesman in Sarajevo, informing him about the cancellation. I am informing you with regret that, in the light of the newly arisen situation and the analysis of the situation on the ground, President Karadzic is unable to meet Charles Redman today, a letter, a copy of which was forwarded to TANJUG, says.
