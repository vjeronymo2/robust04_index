Tuesday September 13, 1994 Part IV Department of the Interior Bureau of Indian Affairs 25 CFR Part 5 Reference in Employment; Proposed Rule Federal Register ␣/␣Vol. 59, No. 176␣/␣Tuesday, September 13, 1994␣/␣Proposed Rules DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR Bureau of Indian Affairs 25 CFR Part 5 RIN 1076&hyph;AD05 Preference in Employment AGENCY: Bureau of Indian Affairs, Interior. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Bureau of Indian Affairs proposes to amend its Preference in Employment Regulations, 25 CFR part 5, by the removal of Paragraph (e) in §5.1 Definitions , which extended the application of Indian preference to persons of the Osage Tribe of Oklahoma, who were at least one-quarter degree Indian ancestry and whose rolls were closed by an act of Congress. On January 5, 1989, paragraph (e) was extended for one additional year to give the Osage Tribe the opportunity to organize and establish current membership standards.
