June 30, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

A TELEVISION 'RING'

I watched that mess, even "Gotterdammerung," which lasted almost five hours.

I was waiting for the Rhine to rise and wash away Valhalla -- and those stupid gods with it. That never happened.

And the ring itself -- it was supposed to give the owner power to rule the world. But when push comes to shove, it can't even hold off Brunnhilde's attacker. Some ring!

DALE RICHARDSON

Los Angeles
