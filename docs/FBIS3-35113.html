Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Beirut's first investigating judge, Sa'id Mirzah, today issued arrest warrants for the suspects charged with assassinating Na'ib 'Umran al-Ma'ayitah, first secretary at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut. They are Bassam 'Abdallah Muhammad 'Atiyah, Yusuf Muhammad Sha'ban, and Salim Qasim Mihyu. The warrants were issued according to Articles 549 and 72--weapons--of the Penal Code, on charges of premeditated murder.
