BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 24 May (WAKH) -- United Arab Emirates [UAE] President His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan has received a phone call [word indistinct] from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The Emirates News Agency said discussions dealt with current developments and the bloody confrontation between brothers in Yemen. Shaykh Zayid renewed his appeal to the Yemeni president to immediately stop fighting and avoid further shedding of Yemeni blood because what is going on is a disaster [words indistinct] Yemeni people who are suffering destruction to their property and national achievements. His highness said that he is pained and saddened for every drop of Arab blood shed anywhere in the Arab world. He called for resorting to reason and logic, and for giving priority to the interests of Yemen and the Yemeni people. His highness emphasized that the use of force will not lead to unity, and unity cannot be imposed by weapons but through understanding and agreement between the parties because the language of force and military means to resolve differences are rejected and do not serve sisterly Yemen.
