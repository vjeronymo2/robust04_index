BFN [Announcer-read report] [Excerpts] A Fatah member from Khan Yunus was killed this evening as he was assembling a bomb in the Khan Yunus refugee camp. The victim is Khalid Halabi, who spent eight years in an Israeli prison because of his Fatah membership. His body was snatched away from the hospital. Our correspondent Shlomi Eldar adds that shots were fired this evening at an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] lookout position in al-Shaykh Radwan. Nobody was hurt. The Fatah Hawks claimed responsibility for this attempted terrorist attack. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1900 GMT on 2 April adds: [passage omitted] "A firebomb was thrown this evening at an Israeli car in the 'Aqabat Jabr refugee camp on the outskirts of Jericho. Nobody was hurt and no damage was caused. The driver reported the incident and drove away. An IDF unit arrived on the scene and found the firebomb's remnants as well as empty cartridges. Shortly afterward, an Arab suffering serious wounds arrived in a hospital in the area. The security forces are checking whether the man was the one who threw the firebomb and was wounded by shots fired by passengers aboard the Israeli car."]
