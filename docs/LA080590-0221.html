August 5, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

EL TORO BICYCLIST KILLED BY CAR ON COAST HIGHWAY

An El Toro man was killed Saturday when he was struck by a passing car while riding his bicycle on Coast Highway, authorities said.

James Robert Bain, 42, was hit while riding on a stretch of open road north of Laguna Beach, according to the California Highway Patrol.

Details of the accident and the name of the driver were not available.
