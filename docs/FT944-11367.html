941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Pounds 45m car-ferry research planned A Pounds 45m research programme to prevent tragedies, such as the sinking of the Estonia car ferry in the Baltic, is to be carried out at the Kvaerner shipyard at Govan on the Clyde. The universities of Glasgow, Strathclyde and Newcastle-upon-Tyne will help in the three-year programme which, among other things, will look at the safety of roll-on roll-off ferries.
