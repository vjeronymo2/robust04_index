March 9, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: NEW GENERAL MANAGER FOR CRYSTAL COURT

Anton D. Segerstrom has been named general manager of Crystal Court retail center at South Coast Plaza in Costa Mesa.

He succeeds Jack H. Matthess, who will be responsible for special projects at South Coast Plaza. Segerstrom has been with the company since 1984, most recently as Crystal Court operations manager. He is the son of Henry T. Segerstrom, a managing partner of C.J. Segerstrom & Sons, manager of the shopping center. Succeeding Segerstrom as operations manager is Marc A. Hernandez, who joined C.J. Segerstrom in 1989 as property manager for Center Tower in Costa Mesa.
