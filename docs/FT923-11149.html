920727 FT 27 JUL 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 10m orders won by Trafalgar House The regional business of TRAFALGAR HOUSE CONSTRUCTION has won another Pounds 10m worth of contracts for civil engineering and building work. This includes a Pounds 2m contract for the construction of an industrial unit for Abbey Life Assurance Co to be built on a site adjacent to the Euroway Industrial Park near Swindon. The unit will have concrete foundations, a reinforced concrete floor, steel portal frame, profiled steel roof and wall cladding, brick external walls and internal offices. Road construction contracts worth almost Pounds 2m have been secured from the Cheshire County Council and the Leeds City Council. Work will begin on a 500 metre section of the Chester western bypass next month, while work on phase one of the Leeds city centre loop has already begun. In Scotland a Pounds 1.2m contract has been awarded for groundworks and site preparations associated with the Abbey Retail Park at Coatbridge. In Wales the company has a Pounds 1m design and construct contract for community housing.
