BFN [Text] Baghdad, 22 May (INA) -- Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf has condemned the announcement to secede from united Yemen made by 'Ali Salim al-Bid late Saturday night, 21 May. In a statement to INA, the foreign minister said Iraq emphasizes its full support for the territorial and popular unity of sisterly Yemen, and supports the legitimate and constitutional authority in Yemen, led by President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The Foreign minister said: We strongly condemn the ugly secessionist action carried out by 'Ali Salim al-Bid and his group. Concluding his statement, the foreign minister said: We wish success to our brothers in Yemen in safeguarding the Yemeni unity, security, safety, and the unity of the brotherly Yemeni people against the renegade and backward forces.
