BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 12 -- A delegation of the Control Commission of the Communist Party of Vietnam central Committee [CPVCC] led by Mr. Do Quang Thang, CPVCC secretary and chairman of the commission, is on a visit to China. On May 9, the Vietnamese delegation was received by Hu Chitao, permanent member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee. Speaking at the reception, Mr Hu welcomed the delegation's visit to China to exchange views on strengthening the party disciplines and fighting corruption in the current process of renovation in the two countries. He also expressed his pleasure at the development of cooperation between China and Vietnam since the normalization of relations between the two counties and the two parties.
