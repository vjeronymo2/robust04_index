Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 14 Feb -- Iberia reports that in the near future, the Russian Federation will have seven military bases in the Republic of Georgia, not three, as mentioned during the latest talks. The seven bases will be in Gudauta, Sukhumi, Ochamchire, Batumi, Akhalkalaki, Vaziani, and Tskhinvali.
