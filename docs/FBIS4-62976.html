BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku 18 May TASS -- Azerbaijan's Defence Ministry has released a statement explaining that Azerbaijan did not sign the protocol on ceasefire and the disengagement of the warring parties in the zone of conflict between Armenia and Azerbaijan, the main clauses of which had been agreed at the meeting of the defence ministers of Russia, Azerbaijan, and Armenia on 16 May, because of separate shortfalls in the document. The statement stresses that expert groups in every country, party to the agreement, continue to work on the document. The press service of the Azeri Defence Ministry does not rule out that a trilateral meeting of the defence ministers will meet in Moscow again after this work is completed.
