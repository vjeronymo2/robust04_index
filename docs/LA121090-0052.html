December 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

In a dismal finish to a profitable week overall, SURELOCK lost $200 Sunday when Lovelier Laura (fifth race) and Hot And Smoggy (the ninth) finished far back in their turf tests.

SURELOCK's Sun. Bankroll: $1,728

Sunday's Loss: $200

Current Bankroll: $1,528

Bob Mieszerski lost $50 when Avies' Sonata struck a sour chord in the second race.

Bob's Sun. Bankroll: $1,503.20

Sunday's Loss: $50

Current Bankroll: $1,453.20

Both started with $2,000 bankrolls.
