920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: F&C Enterprise net asset value up Over the six months ended June 30 1992 net asset value of Foreign & Colonial Enterprise Trust improved from 44.8p to 45.3p. At mid-1991 it stood at 44.4p. Mr John Sclater, chairman, said the trust took advantage of the weak market in unquoted companies to make a number of new management buy-out investments. Also, one investment, Business Advisory Services, was sold for a total of Pounds 2.5m against the cost in April 1991 of Pounds 1m. Attributable revenue for the half year came to Pounds 349,000 (Pounds 528,000) and earnings per share worked through at 0.37p (0.56p).
