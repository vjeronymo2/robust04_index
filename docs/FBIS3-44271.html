Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Parliament has expressed concern about the worsening relationship between Ghana and Togo. While some members called for stern measures against Togo, others urged the government to exercise restraint. This is because any war will result in the destruction of lives and property. This followed a statement by the member for Hoehoe-South, Mr. Koffi Kedem, at today's sitting of the house in Accra. The statement called for a quick and resolute response from the government for the crimes, unwarranted murders, and flagrant violation of the country's territory by Togo. They invited the minister of foreign affairs, Dr. Obed Asamoah, to brief parliament on the situation.
