BFN [Text] Tripoli, 5 Jun -- President Idriss Deby, president of the Republic of Chad, stressed that the Great Jamahiriyah is a country that works for peace and endeavors for dignified living. In a statement to JANA before leaving Tripoli this morning, President Idriss Deby said that the Great Jamahiriyah respects international law and its commitments toward the international community. President Deby said that "we will have the opportunity to say that. I am happy with that, and this is reality."
