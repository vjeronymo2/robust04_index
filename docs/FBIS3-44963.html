Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, January 8 (XINHUA) -- Bank of China Macao branch reported an increase of over 10 percent in terms of its realized profits in 1993, as compared with the figure for 1992. Bank savings rose by five percent over that of 1992 which was 23.52 billion Macao patacas (about 3.5 billion U.S. dollars). An increase of 30 percent was also reported in terms of bank loans in 1993, much higher than the 11.2 billion Macao patacas (about 1.4 billion U.S. dollars) in the previous year. According to an official with the bank, last year's bank loans were mainly used in industrial production and foreign trade, adding that the sharp increase in this regard had resulted from the huge demand of capital in Macao in the past year. Bank of China Macao branch is one of the leading commercial banks in Macao. Currently, it has 20 sub-branches all over Macao. In 1992, its realized profit stood at 34.5 billion Macao patacas (about 4.5 billion U.S. dollars)
