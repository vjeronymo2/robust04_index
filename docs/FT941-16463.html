940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): TSB Group - Other companies The market is likely to be cheered on the same day by the banking group's figures, which should have recovered on lower bad debt provisions. The City, though, is not holding its breath for substantial growth in operating income. Forecasts range from Pounds 260m to Pounds 280m pre-tax for the year to October, against Pounds 6m.
