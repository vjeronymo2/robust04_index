Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "Hold the Front Page!" rubric: "Military Ruse"] [Text] The Russian Defense Ministry's General Staff has decided to carry out plans for its own reduction in its own manner. It has become known to MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS from reliable sources that not a single officer will be discharged from the General Staff. There will be a reduction nevertheless. Several subunits will be detached from General Staff directorates and transformed into several sectors [napravleniya]. All the officers will remain in their positions and will not lose even a kopeck of their salaries. Defense Ministry chiefs will be able to report a 30-percent personnel cut. This is not the first time the military has resorted to such a ruse. At one time, the Central Command Post was withdrawn from the Main Operations Directorate of the General Staff. Those at the top were told about a 50-percent cut. Several months after, part of the subunits were taken back. The same will happen now too.
