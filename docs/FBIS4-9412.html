BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 Apr--Hanna Suchocka, on the Democratic Union [UD] ticket, and Janusz Lewandowski, from the Liberal Democratic Congress [KLD], have received the largest numbers of votes in the elections to the National Council of the Freedom Union [UW]. Such results were announced on Sunday at the first UW National Congress. Tadeusz Mazowiecki, the leader of the party, and Donald Tusk, deputy chairman, enter the Council automatically. Among the Council's members who have a UD pedigree, Jacek Taylor came second and Andrzej Potocki third. On the liberals' list were Michal Boni and Jan Krzysztof Bielecki, respectively.
