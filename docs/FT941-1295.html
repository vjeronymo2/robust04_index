940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands lower Lower profits from sales of stakes in subsidiaries left Ex-Lands' pre-tax profits for the six months to December 31 lower at Pounds 484,000, against Pounds 822,000. Turnover was higher at Pounds 1.23m (Pounds 731,000). Earnings per share came out at 0.68p (1.19p).
