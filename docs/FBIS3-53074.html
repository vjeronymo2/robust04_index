Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 Jan (INA)--Iraq has informed the Arab League that the Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers continue to provide facilities to the U.S., British, and French aircraft that violate Iraq's airspace. This poses a serious threat to Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter addressed to the Arab League secretary general yesterday, Iraq's permanent League representative said that on 4, 5, and 6 January these aircraft continued to violate Iraq's airspace after taking off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, carrying out 157 hostile air sorties. A list attached to the letter gave details on these violations.
