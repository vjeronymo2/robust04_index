921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / Wedgwood sheds up to 410 jobs WEDGWOOD, the fine china maker, will shed 160 jobs. It said another 250 were likely to follow. The group is to close the Johnson Brothers' Hanley Pottery in Stoke-on-Trent, employing 300 - of which 140 are expected to be redeployed within the group.
