April 14, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

NHL PLAYOFF DELAYS SOCCER GAME

The Wichita-Los Angeles Major Indoor Soccer League game scheduled Saturday night will be played Sunday because the Edmonton-Los Angeles NHL playoff series has gone the maximum seven games.

The Oilers and Kings will play the decider of the Smythe Division semifinal series at the Forum on Saturday night. The Wings-Lazers MISL game has been moved to 11:30 a.m. Sunday.
