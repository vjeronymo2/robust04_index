BFN [Text] The following report was received by the ELTA agency from the Embassy of the Russian Federation in the Republic of Lithuania: The administration of the Russian Federation Kaliningrad region announces that it has no intention to exploit the oil field that is in the Baltic Sea in the (?vicinity) of the town of Nida. Exploration work that had been carried out by the production association Kaliningrad Sea Oil and Gas was aimed at monitoring the above oil field and environmental protection. The platforms in this area used for exploration are now being moved to near Kaliningrad.
