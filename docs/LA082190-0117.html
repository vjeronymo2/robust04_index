August 21, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Wynonna Will Wed: Wynonna Judd, the daughter half of country music's mother-daughter tandem, the Judds, is engaged.

A spokesman says songwriter Tony King proposed to Wynonna before she left on her current tour. Mom Naomi Judd gave her blessing. King -- formerly in the group Memphis with Naomi's husband, Larry Strickland -- is currently playing guitar with Vince Gill's band. He's the co-writer of the No. 1 Ricky Van Shelton hit "I've Cried My Last Tear for You." No wedding date has been set. BETH KLEID
