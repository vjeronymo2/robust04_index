BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Golhan] [Text] Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan, who is on an official visit to France, has begun his contacts. He met with French Defense Minister Francois Leotard in Paris today. International issues such as Bosnia-Herzegovina and Azerbaijan, Turkey's relations with the European Union, and bilateral ties were discussed at the meeting. Golhan also briefed Leotard on terrorist activities in Turkey and the struggle being waged against terrorism. Leotard said that France will continue to support Turkey's struggle against terrorism. Golhan will visit Toulon and Marseilles today.
