January 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

IMPERFECTIONS

OK, there were a few gaffes in our Sneaks '89. It is Edward S. Feldman -- not Ed Pressman -- who is producing "Wired" with Charles Meeker. The Francis Ford Coppola-directed segment of "New York Stories" stars newcomer Heather McComb, not Coppola's daughter, Sofia. Sofia co-wrote the script with her father and also designed the costumes and titles. Merrin Holt and Ken Krauss are the writers of the comedy "Buy and Cell," not director Robert Boris. And the vampire picture "To Die For" -- which we labeled "distributor pending" -- will be distributed by Skouras Pictures in March.
