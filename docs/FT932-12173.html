930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Kier acquires Twigden Kier Group, the building contractor which emerged from Hanson in a Pounds 55m employee buy-out last July, has acquired Twigden, the holding company for Twigden Homes, an East Anglia based house-builder. Twigden owns some 1,000 plots with planning consent and has a substantial acreage under option. Mr Bob Stephens, former managing director of Beazer Homes, has been appointed head of Twigden Homes. Kier achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.3m in the six months ended December 1992 on turnover of Pounds 550m.
