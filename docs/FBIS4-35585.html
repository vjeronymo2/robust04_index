BFN [Text] Israel is expanding the scope of its acts of aggression against Lebanese territory in an attempt to change the course of the bilateral talks with Lebanon into security ones. At dawn today, Israeli warplanes raided the Ba'labakk area after numerous overflights. A security source made the following statement at 0600 today: The Israeli enemy has continued its acts of aggression against Lebanese territory. Israeli helicopters staged intensive overflights over Tannurin, Bsharri, and the al-Labwah areas in al-Biqa' between 2300 last night and 0200 today. The Israeli helicopters were confronted with Lebanese Army and Syrian Arab Forces defenses. At 0215 today the Israeli warplanes bombed a resistance training base in the Ba'labakk hills.
