940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Brooke Tool expands in US Brooke Tool Engineering, the Sheffield-based cutting tools and coil springs company, has, through its Cardinal Cutting Tools offshoot, acquired EG Saws of Chicago from receivers in the state of Illinois. Consideration for the current assets is Dollars 200,000 (Pounds 137,000). Cardinal will also pay Dollars 158,000 for plant and machinery plus Dollars 50,000 deferred for 12 months. EG, the sole US maker of cut-off saws, incurred pre-tax losses of Dollars 123,000 in the nine months to September 30 1993. Mr John Dashper, Brooke Tool chairman, said: 'The US is a major market for our products and having a manufacturing base will allow us to penetrate markets previously not available to us as an importer.'
