BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Ayhan Halit Acarkan, speaker of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus [TRNC] Assembly, has arrived in Ankara to participate in the 23 April National Sovereignty and Children's Holiday celebrations. Husamettin Cindoruk, the Turkish Grand National Assembly [TGNA] speaker, received the guest assembly speaker and the accompanying delegation. In a statement he issued, Acarkan said that their aim is to further the already close relations with the TGNA. Cindoruk, in turn, noted that the Turkish people and their parliament have always supported the TRNC. Indicating that the TGNA will hold a general discussion regarding the Cyprus issue on 3 May, Cindoruk noted that all details will be discussed at this meeting and a communique issued at its conclusion.
