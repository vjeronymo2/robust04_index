Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 23 (CNS) -- A taxation inspection of enterprises last year discovered various kinds of illegal tax- related practices involving RMB [Renminbi] 15.4 billion with RMB 10 billion being recovered by the state. Tax evasion has become rampant in the country with more than 50 percent of all enterprises and individually-owned business households found guilty of such practices. Some enterprises were found to have evaded taxes of more than RMB 1 million. Last year saw the industrial and commercial tax evasion being put at RMB 6.18 billion, evasion of income tax RMB 1.85 billion and evasion of payment for key items' construction as well as for budgetary regulation RMB 1.187 billion, adding up to RMB 9.22 billion and constituting 63.4 percent of all illegal funds. Another serious problem was the deduction of sums of state revenue. Rampant money-collecting by administrative departments and institutions was also serious. According to experts, there was an urgent need to establish a supervisory mechanism for handling such illegal matters in the country.
