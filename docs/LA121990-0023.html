December 19, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

MARTIN MARIETTA WINS HUD COMPUTER PACT

Martin Marietta Corp. has received a $525.9-million contract to modernize the information systems capabilities of the Department of Housing and Urban Development.

The contract, to be administered over 12 years, is one of five major awards that Martin Marietta has received since July. Total potential value of the contracts is more than $1 billion, the company said.

The newest contract involves consolidating HUD's automated data-processing operations.
