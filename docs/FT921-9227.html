920213 FT 13 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Castle Cairn asset value at 43.6p Net asset value of The Castle Cairn Investment Trust rose 14.4 per cent to 43.58p over the year to December 31. The figure, however, represented a reversal from the 44.5p reported at the interim stage. Net revenue in 1991 was down from Pounds 110,000 to Pounds 94,000 - the figures for 1990 were for an eight months period and had been adjusted due to an accountancy change - and earnings per share declined from 0.91p to 0.79p. But the proposed dividend is increased from 0.5p to 0.75p. Since the year-end, Castle Cairn Fund Managers has been acquired by Ivory & Sime.
