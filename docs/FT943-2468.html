940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Net assets advance at Primadona Net asset value for Primadona, the investment trust, advanced from 241p to 294p over the 12 months to June 30. Net revenue for the year to the end of June was Pounds 54,145 (Pounds 173,927) for earnings per share of 1.2p (3.9p). The proposed final dividend is held at 2.5p for an unchanged total of 4.5p.
