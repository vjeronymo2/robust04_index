Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- According to government spokesman Dieter Vogel, the German Government has "taken careful note" of the events in Moscow surrounding the resignation of Yegor Gaydar, the first deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers. Vogel told journalists in Bonn today that the Federal Government assumes that the policy of reform will be continued in Russia. The German Government does not have the slightest grounds for doubting this. It also wanted the policy of reform to be continued, Vogel is quoted as saying.
