Language: English Article Type:BFN [By AZERTAC correposndent Jamilya Kuliyeva for TASS] [Text] Baku March 19 TASS -- An explosion and an ensuing fire occurred in a front carriage of a metro train at the Baku underground railway station "20 January" at 14.00 on Saturday, competent sources told AZERTAC. A government commission is investigating the causes of the tragedy. Data on victims are being verified.
