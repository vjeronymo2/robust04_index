940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / The Liberal Democrats in Brighton: Party split on Europe Liberal Democrat voters are split over the party's strong European stance, says an opinion poll in today's Guardian newspaper. While the party pursues closer links with the rest of the European Union, the survey shows 45 per cent of Liberal Democrat voters do not want closer relations with the EU while 44 per cent do. ICM interviewed 1,437 adults of all political persuasions on September 9 and 10.
