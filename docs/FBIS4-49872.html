BFN [Text] Beijing, May 12 (XINHUA) -- Viktor Stepanovich Chernomyrdin, prime minister of the Government of Russian Federation, will pay an official visit to China from May 26 to 29, at the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Peng. A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman announced this at a weekly news briefing here this afternoon. Wu Jianmin, the spokesman, said this will be the first China visit made by a Russian prime minister, to which the Chinese side attaches great importance. In general, Wu noted, the Sino-Russian relations in various fields, with the joint efforts by both sides, have achieved positive progress since Russian President Boris Yeltsin visited China in December 1992. The purpose of the coming visit by the Russian prime minister is to "promote the steady and sustained development of bilateral relations on the basis of the achievements already made, especially to further promote the two countries' economic and trade cooperation", the spokesman said.
