910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Losses increase at Millicom Losses increased from Dollars 44.42m to Dollars 57.94m (Pounds 32.55m) in the year to December 31 at Millicom, the US-based telecommunications business quoted on the USM. Revenue from sales and services surged from Dollars 25.15m to Dollars 59.56m, but cost and expenses took Dollars 143,777 (Dollars 93,485). There was a gain of Dollars 7,700 (nil) representing the disposal of its cellular interests, but interest expense came to Dollars 11.54m (Dollars 4.34m). Losses per common share for the 12 months increased to Dollars 3.51 (Dollars 3.09).
