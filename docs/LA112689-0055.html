November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SYMONS TO HEAD REALTY BOARD IN BEVERLY HILLS

The Beverly Hills Board of Realtors will install its officers for 1990 at its 67th annual installation banquet Friday at the Beverly Hilton Hotel.

They are Frank Symons, president; Ron Lepp, first vice president; Layne Grasshoff, second vice president; Monty Gardner, treasurer, and Tricia Moore, executive vice president. Gertrude Key will receive the Realtor of the Year award.

For tickets, call the Beverly Hills Board of Realtors office at (213) 553-2171.
