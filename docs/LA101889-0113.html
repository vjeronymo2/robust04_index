October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The University of Tennessee spent more than $100,000 last spring to send running back Reggie Cobb to an out-of-state drug-rehabilitation clinic, according to a Nashville newspaper.

The Tennessean said that Cobb went through a six-week rehabilitation program after he was suspended from the team last February in the wake of the third positive drug test of his college career.
