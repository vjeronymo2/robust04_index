BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz have returned to Beirut from Damascus, where an expanded Lebanese-Syrian summit was held. Two rounds of talks were held, interrupted by a working luncheon. A meeting is now under way between President al-Hirawi and Prime Minister al-Hariri at the B'abda Palace. Speaker Birri left the palace a few minutes ago.
