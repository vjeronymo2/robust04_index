Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 8 (KNS-KCNA) -- The 13th general meeting of the Hokkaido People's Council for Japan-Korea Solidarity was held on February 25. The meeting discussed the action program for this year, including the questions of intensifying the movement for supporting and promoting an independent and peaceful reunification of Korea, stepping up the drive for the normalization of diplomatic relations between Japan and Korea and of grandly celebrating the 20th anniversary of the formation of the Hokkaido People's Council for Japan-Korea Solidarity. A "resolution on a peaceful settlement of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula, defence and expansion of the rights of Koreans in Japan" was adopted at the meeting.
