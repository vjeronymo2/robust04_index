940423 FT 23 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: Investors benefit The government's belated concession on capital gains tax indexation has some unexpected side effects. The change in the basis of calculating relief means that some investors will be better off than they would have been before the Budget rule change. Unusually, you can use up part of your annual CGT exemption in 1993-94, even if your indexation losses in that year exceed your gains.
