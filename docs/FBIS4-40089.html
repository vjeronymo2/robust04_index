BFN [Text] Today, Bulgarian President Zhelyu Zhelev received Vladimir Shumeyko, the chairman of the Federation Council, who is in Sofia now. ITAR-TASS reports that the sides discussed a wide range of issues regarding bilateral relations and topical international problems. Russian-Bulgarian relations should be developed harmoniously and to mutual benefit, the leader of the Russian delegation stressed. Incidentally, Vladimir Shumeyko noted that during the meetings held in the Bulgarian capital, some matters arose on which the sides need to clarify each others positions, in particular, the NATO program Partnership for Peace. But, according to Shumeyko, disputes are of an economic rather than political nature. During the conversation Vladimir Shumeyko conveyed to the Bulgarian president cordial greetings from Boris Yeltsin.
