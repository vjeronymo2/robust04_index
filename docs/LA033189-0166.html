March 31, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

PRINCESS DI HAS DATE WITH DENTIST

The Princess of Wales has entered a hospital for an operation to remove four impacted wisdom teeth, Buckingham Palace said today.

Princess Diana, 28, was expected to remain in the hospital for at least two days after today's surgery. Her husband, Prince Charles, left for a holiday with their two sons at the royal family's Scottish retreat of Balmoral as she checked into King Edward VII Hospital for Officers in London Thursday night.
