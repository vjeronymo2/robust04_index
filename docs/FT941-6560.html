940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: European Smaller European Smaller Companies, the investment trust, reported net asset value of 135.8p at December 31, compared with 116.7p at June 30 and 104.5p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to end-December showed losses of Pounds 31,000, against profits of Pounds 242,000. Earnings per share were nil, compared with 0.68p.
