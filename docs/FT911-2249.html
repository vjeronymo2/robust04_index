910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Improving trend for Addison Following a reorganisation, the Addison Consultancy Group, the market research company, finished the 1990 year with a strengthened balance sheet and nil gearing. It also returned to profit in the second half and for the full 12 months cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 368,000 to Pounds 95,000. Turnover fell to Pounds 20.54m (Pounds 45.89m) following disposals of non-core activities. Losses per share emerged at 0.2p (0.89p). There is no dividend for the year, against 0.6p last time.
