BFN [Text] Prime Minister Balladur supported the Baudis [Rally for the Republic/French Democratic Union] list. He spoke about the results this morning during his visit to Lille. The prime minister: [Begin Balladur recording] These elections in fact show a degree of dispersion in the representation of France in the European Parliament. At the same time, they show that the very great majority of French people are attached to Europe but dissatisfied at the way in which things work. [end recording] Mr. Balladur added that the votes for Mr. Philippe de Villiers belong to the majority and should be counted with those of the majority. He also announced this morning in a speech that he is at the moment preparing a new action plan for his government for the next six months.
