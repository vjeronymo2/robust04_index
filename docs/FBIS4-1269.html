BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon, ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] soldiers prevented a team of NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia] Legislative Assembly members in Kakata from entering Monrovia. Reports say ECOMOG is insisting that the officials had to be cleared at headquarters for official pass, a move condemned by the NPFL's TLA [Transitional Legislative Assembly] members. The NPFL Assembly members condemned ECOMOG for [words indistinct] in the way of free travel between Monrovia and Gbarnga, saying there is no need for a pass system in a reunified Liberia.
