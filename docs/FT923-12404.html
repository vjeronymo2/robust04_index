920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / Algerians clash Four demonstrators were killed and at least 15 were wounded, yesterday, in clashes between Moslem fundamentalists and armed riot police in Algiers, Reuter reports from Algiers. There were at least half-a dozen clashes across the capital after Friday prayers. Clashes were also reported in the eastern cities of Batna, Constantine and Setif, and Oran in the west.
