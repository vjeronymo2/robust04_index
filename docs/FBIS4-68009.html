BFN [Unattributed report: "Ex-RAF Member To Be Released in the Middle of May"] [Excerpts] The pardoning of former Red Army Faction [RAF] member Bernhard Roessner after 19 years in prison encountered a mainly positive reaction yesterday. Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel welcomed this decision, reached by President von Weizsaecker. Together with Justice Minister Sabine Leutheusser-Schnarrenberger, Kinkel had helped bring about Roessner's pardon. [passage omitted] Roessner is the third RAF terrorist serving a life sentence to be pardoned. Previously, Klaus Juenschke was pardoned in 1988, and Angelika Speitel in 1990. [passage omitted]
