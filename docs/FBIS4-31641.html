BFN [Text] This evening Minister Jerzy Milewski asked President Lech Walesa to release him from the duties of National Defense Committee secretary and the responsibility of heading the National Security Bureau attached to the president's chancellery, which is legally linked to them. Lech Walesa has accepted the resignation and appointed Henryk Goryszewski to these posts, and simultaneously appointed him also secretary of state in the Chancellery of the President of the Polish Republic.
