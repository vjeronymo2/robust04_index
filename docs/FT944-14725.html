941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Net assets fall at New Throgmorton The split-capital New Throgmorton Trust (1983) reported a net asset value of 144.5p per capital share as at September 30 - a year-on-year decline of 26 per cent and a fall of 19 per cent since the trust's March year end. Net revenue for the six months to end-September dipped to Pounds 828,000 (Pounds 871,000) for earnings of 2.12p (2.23p) per share. The second interim dividend is cut from 1.5p to 1p, making 2p to date.
