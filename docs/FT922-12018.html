920422 FT 22 APR 92 / Chamber sees signs of recovery THE DECLINE in manufacturing output is slowing and there are signs of recovery, according to a survey published yesterday by the London Chamber of Commerce and Industry. A total of 38 per cent of manufacturers said orders for the first quarter were up for the first time in two years, although 30 per cent reported a fall in orders. The balance of 8 per cent is the first positive figure for two years. A balance of 7 per cent of service-sector companies reported a fall in orders, while in the previous quarter a balance of 1 per cent expected a rise.
