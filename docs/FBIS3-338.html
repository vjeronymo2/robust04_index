Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Yoweri Museveni has received the Sudanese foreign minister, Dr. Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih. The meeting took place at the Uganda International Conference Center yesterday evening. The Sudanese foreign minister delivered a special message from the Sudanese President al-Bashir. He was accompanied by the Sudanese high commissioner to Kenya, Mr. Martin Malwal.
