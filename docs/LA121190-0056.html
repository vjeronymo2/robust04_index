December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Changes at ABC: Two ABC executives -- sports chief Dennis Swanson and Philip R. Beuth, vice president of the ABC Network Group -- will jointly take over the areas of daytime, children's and late-night programming formerly overseen by Michael Brockman. The executive left the job last week citing "philosophical differences" with ABC management. BETH KLEID
