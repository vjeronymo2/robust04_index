BFN [Text] With regard to the civil war in Yemen, the northern forces say they are about to take control of the former capital of the south, Aden. Most foreign nationals have now left the country. France led the operations from Djibouti. The [French] Defense Ministry announced in Paris this evening that the evacuation of foreign nationals by air or sea was now over. A total of 1,456 people left Yemen over the last few days, including 214 French citizens.
