June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A SOLUTION FOR KEEPING PENA OUT OF TROUBLE

I can't be the only one who sees it: If the Dodgers want to use Alejandro Pea, let him start.

That way, they can yank him if he gets into trouble, before the game gets out of hand.

Believe it or not, I used to say the same thing about Charlie Hough and Dave Stewart.

ALLYN M. SUFFENS

Irvine
