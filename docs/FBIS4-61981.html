BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] The Armenian forces resumed their attempts to fight their way through the Azerbaijani Armed Forces defences to Terter and Agdam in the morning. According to the Defence Ministry of Azerbaijan, 10 Armenian soldiers died and one tank became a casualty as a result of the fighting continued in the afternoon. The Azerbaijani Army forced the Armenian Armed Forces to retreat to their starting battle lines as a result of the heavy fighting taken place near the village of Kichik Akhmadagali in the district of Agdamskiy. Both the sides have suffered losses. More than 40 Armenian soldiers were killed, while the Azerbaijani side suffered 10 casualties. The enemy's three tanks and one vehicle gasoline tanker became casualties. Regiments of the Azerbaijani Army were subject to heavy artillery shelling in Geranboy and Kelbajar.
