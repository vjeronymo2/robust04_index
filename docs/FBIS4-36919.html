BFN [Text] A report from Pakistan says orders have been issued for the arrest of former prime minister, Mr. Nawaz Sharif. An agency quoting the governor of Punjab Province, Mr. Altaf Hussain, said that the permission of the Speaker of the National Assembly has been sought in this regard. Two members of the provincial assembly of Punjab belonging to Pakistan Muslim League -- Nawaz Group, have already been arrested in a case of allotment of plots scandal. Meanwhile, the Pakistan president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, has initiated legal proceedings against Mr. Nawaz Sharif for accusing him of making huge amounts of money by hoarding cotton bales. This was disclosed by Mr. Leghari in Rawalpindi yesterday. He said that he will sue Mr. Nawaz Sharif on another count also.
