941001 FT 01 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: Benefit advice line - At a glance Help the Aged says pensioners are missing out on state benefits worth about Pounds 400m a year. The charity says government statistics show that about 500,000 of the poorest pensioners are eligible for income support and other benefits, but do not claim them. Help the Aged has a free telephone advice line to help with this and other issues affecting the elderly. Call 0800-289 404 for advice.
