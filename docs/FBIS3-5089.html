Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 3 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- It is natural that now a requirement arises to expand and develop Mongolian-Vietnamese relations in accordance with the new tendencies and processes appeared in the world and the Asian-Pacific region, said the president of Mongolia in an interview to the Vietnamese newspaper "NHAN DAN". In an interview, which has been granted in connection with his official visit to the SRV, the president P. Ochirbat notes that the peoples of Mongolia and Vietnam have wonderful traditions of long-standing friendly relations. Mongolia and Vietnam are encountering today with objective difficulties in making active and developing cooperation between them because of the reason connected with economic reforms in the two countries, financial-economic difficulties caused by them. In addition to this, objective transport problems should be taken into consideration. That is why, said the president, the head of the Mongolian state is on the official visit to Vietnam. The purpose of the visit is to have talks, exchange views with the state and government leaders of Vietnam on the ways, forms and possibilities of making active and developing relations between the two states. We are confident in the availability of considerable opportunities for renewal and consolidation of bilateral relations and cooperation, leaning upon rich experience, traditions, of our relations and advantages of the two sides connected with their peculiarities. P. Ochirbat defined the rendering of support by the two sides for strengthening of business contacts between the representatives of the private sector as a new perspective form of relations, at the same time, of course, expansion of economic cooperation between the two countries on the state line.
