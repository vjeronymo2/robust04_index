941201 FT 01 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Mannesmann - Contracts and ventures Mannesmann, one of Germany's leading engineering groups, will begin a joint venture with Baoshan Iron and Steel to provide a full range of services for the construction of steel plants in China and south-eastern Asia. The new operation, in which Mannesmann Demag Huttentechnik (MDH) will hold a 51 per cent stake, will be called Demag Metal Plant Technology and is to be based in Shanghai. MDH has built a number of steel plants in China over the last 30 years. The joint venture would enable them to compete more effectively in other markets in the region, Mannesmann said.
