Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 24 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree today releasing Dmitriy Volkogonov from the post of adviser. He was an adviser on defense and security matters and chairman of the president's commission investigating the disappearance without trace of foreign citizens and of Russian citizens missing in unexplained circumstances outside the borders of the former Soviet Union. The Russian Federation president's decision was made in connection with D. Volkogonov's election to the State Duma of the Federal Assembly, the Russian Federation president's press service reports.
