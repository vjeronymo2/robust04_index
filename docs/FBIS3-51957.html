Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Nicaraguan press release issued by the Information and Press Directorate of the Presidency in Managua on 27 January -- read by announcer] [Text] The Information and Press Directorate of the Presidency informs citizens that the organizations, Canitran [Nicaraguan Chamber of Transportation], Fetracanic [Federation of Nicaraguan Cargo Carriers], Anatra [National Transportation Association], National Chamber of Transportation, and Fetracolnic [Federation of Nicaraguan Passenger Carriers], heeding a government summons, met with the negotiating commission at the Presidency at 2100. A meeting is scheduled with the National Transportation Commission for 0900 tomorrow. Government officials present at tonight's meeting were the finance, construction and transportation, economy and development, and labor ministers. The Government Ministry reports that transportation sector activities are increasing and the complete normalization of the sector is expected for tomorrow. [Issued] Managua, 27 January 1994
