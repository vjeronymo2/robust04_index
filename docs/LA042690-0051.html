April 26, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

SOUTH BAY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: LANDSCAPING CONTRACT IS APPROVED

The Palos Verdes Estates City Council on Tuesday approved a $48,206 contract with Bennett Enterprises to landscape the reconstructed portion of Palos Verdes Boulevard leading to its intersection with Palos Verdes Drives West and North. The landscaping plan was developed by the city's parklands committee, City Manager Jim Henrickson said, and should be completed by the middle of June.
