BFN [Text] We have just received the following: A short while ago, AFP cited an official in Sanaa as saying that a cease-fire will be announced in the next few hours. The northern Yemeni official, who asked to remain anonymous, said: The cease-fire announcement, which will be issued by the Presidential Council in Yemen, will come in harmony with the Sanaa government's acceptance of the Security Council resolution, calling for a cease-fire. The northern Yemeni official added: Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih is currently holding contacts with the Defense Ministry and the command of the Army units to take the necessary measures to achieve a cease-fire in Yemen.
