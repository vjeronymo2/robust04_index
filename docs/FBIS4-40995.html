BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Tamara Zamyatina, Vladimir Smelov, and Viktor Khrekov] [Text] Moscow June 21 TASS -- The Russian Foreign Ministry is ready and can help exports of Russian goods to the world market, however, the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations does not help the Foreign Ministry in this respect, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev told a meeting of editors-in-chief of Russian mass media bodies here today. That is why, the Foreign Ministry does not even know what state is the most efficient economic partner for Russia, he added. In the words of Andrey Kozyrev, a single state strategy for Russian entrance to the world market has not been elaborated since the time of Eduard Shevardnadze was Soviet foreign minister. "If we receive a corresponding concept from the minister of foreign economic relations, we could act in the Russian economic interests. Politics always influences the economy," said Kozyrev.
