BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk cancelled the resolution of Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov on military service of conscripts -- citizens of the Crimea dated March 15. Kravchuk's decree made public on Saturday said that Meshkov's order contradicts to Ukraine's Constitution and to the decrees on the division of authorities between the state power bodies of Ukraine and Crimea, on Ukraine's defense and military service. The document points out that "the determination of the arms of the service stationed on the republic's territory, training and distribution of military personnel "is the competence of Ukraine's Armed Forces." The same day Kravchuk appointed Nikolay Hlushko his envoy in Sevastopol, having released him from the post of the first deputy head of the Sevastopol city administration. Some time earlier Kravchuk submitted Hlushko's candidacy to the city council's session for consideration, however, half of the permanent commissions of the Sevastopol city council was against his appointment.
