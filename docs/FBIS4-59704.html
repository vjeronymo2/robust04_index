BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] is continuing to evacuate installations and turn them over to the Palestinians in the Gaza Strip. Early this morning, the IDF evacuated the police station in Jabalyah refugee camp's al-Madinah Square, the Civil Administration building there, and the last IDF outpost in the camp which also used to serve as a police station. This was reported by our Army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe.
