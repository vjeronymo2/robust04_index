BFN [Text] ITAR-TASS has circulated President Yeltsin's directive on an investigation of whether laws and other legal enactments adopted in the Republic of Mordovia conform with the Russian Constitution. To this end, the document envisages the establishment of a special commission made up of representatives of the Russian Justice Ministry, the president's State Legal Administration, and the president's Administration for Work with Territories. Sergey Filatov, head of the president's administration, has been given a week to endorse the composition of this commission.
