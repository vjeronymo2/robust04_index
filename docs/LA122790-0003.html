December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY

Ristorante Genovese, 214 N. Tustin Ave., Orange. Open Tuesday through Friday for lunch, 11 a.m. to 2 p.m.; Tuesday through Saturday for dinner, 5 to 10 p.m. (714) 633-5654.

A family-run restaurant that's intimate yet casual, classy yet reasonable, and serves deep red, rich, garlic-laden sauce straight from the soul of Sicily. Pasta dishes are popular, and the steak a la Sicilian is extraordinary. But no place is perfect. It's closed Sunday and Monday.JIM COLONNA
