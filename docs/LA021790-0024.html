February 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THEY HAVE POINTS TO MAKE AFTER 102-24 PREP GAME

Didn't anyone ever tell Mike Downey that quitters never win? Winning is a great feeling, but we often learn the most about ourselves when things don't go our way. If a young person is taught to give up when he or she is losing, then what do they take away from that experience?

PETE F. TEGAN

Manhattan Beach
