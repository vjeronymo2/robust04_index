910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Construction Contracts: Tarmac's road and warehouse orders TARMAC CONSTRUCTION has won orders worth over Pounds 23m. The largest is a Pounds 5.3m contract for roads, drainage and ancillary work at Branston housing development, Burton-on-Trent, for Branston Property Partnership. Work is scheduled for completion in the summer of next year. The company also has a Pounds 2.8m contract for warehousing, offices, car parking and landscaping at Stockport for Hilti (GB), and two contracts, each worth Pounds 1.3m, on Merseyside - building warehouses at Freeport, Liverpool, for Mersey Docks and Harbour Company, and factories at Wallasey for English Industrial Estates Corporation. A Pounds 2.2m contract for improving flats at Ballantay has been awarded by Glasgow District Council. Homes at Bootle worth Pounds 1.6m are to be built for Co-operative Development Services; and Pounds 1.5m improvements to homes at Farnborough are being carried out for Rushmoor Borough Council.
