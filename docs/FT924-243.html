921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / World Stock Markets: Brazil BRAZILIAN shares leapt ahead yesterday as the Sao Paulo Stock Exchange (Bovespa) index closed up 13.0 per cent at 67,351 yesterday. Trading was heavy at about Dollars 103m, up from a strong Dollars 100m last Wednesday. the last trading day before Christmas. The market began solidly yesterday and maintained its bullish trend after the announcement that the date of President Fernando Collor de Mello's impeachment trial would be maintained for today. Telebras, the state-owned telecommunications holding company, again led the upswing.
