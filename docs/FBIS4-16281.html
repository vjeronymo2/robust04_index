BFN [By AZERTAC correspondent Alla Baula for TASS] [Text] Baku April 14 TASS -- Armenian troops launched a fresh offensive in the Geranboi-Terter section of the Azerbaijani-Armenian front on Thursday, the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry said. It said Azerbaijani forces rebuffed the attack. The Armenians had to retreat after their attempt to seize the village of Gyullidzha in the Agdam district of Azerbaijan failed. They lost two tanks and several people were killed. Azerbaijani troops also destroyed Armenian positions which were used to shell Azerbaijani villages in the district, the report said. The ministry said that Armenian forces continue shelling border villages in the Kazakh district of Azerbaijan and the border outpost Kilid in the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic.
