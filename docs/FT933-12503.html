930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: British Syphon shares surge British Syphon Industries, the industrial group, said yesterday that it was to distribute a large part of its Pounds 33m cash reserves in a special dividend amounting to 62.35p per share. The shares responded via a 55p rise to 180p. For the past two years the group's strategy had been to make a substantial acquisition, a strategy that required the full support of Britannia, the main shareholder. However, in view of the difficulty in 'identifying' an appropriate acquisition the board had decided to distribute a 'large' part of the cash in the form of a special dividend.
