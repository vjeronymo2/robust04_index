BFN [Text] Johannesburg May 22 SAPA -- The Pan Africanist Congress [PAC] said on Sunday there was no reason to believe that King Goodwill Zwelithini would abuse his powers regarding the Ingonyama [King's] Land Trust. PAC economic director Sipho Shabalala said in a statement that "the land is to be held in trust for the born and unborn citizens" of South Africa. He said the transfer of land to the Ingonyama trust did not represent the privatisation of land, which the PAC rejected.
