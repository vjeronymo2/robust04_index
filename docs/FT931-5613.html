930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Kantor to meet auto leaders Mr Mickey Kantor, US trade representative, is to meet US car company and union leaders in Detroit 'to listen to their concerns and exchange ideas', Nancy Dunne reports. This comes as Mr Ron Brown, US commerce secretary, is said to be considering a government-sponsored anti-dumping action against Japanese car imports. US producers have rejected this, and are backed by Mr Kantor.
