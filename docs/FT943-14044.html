940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / Criminal reforms to miss assent The criminal justice bill, the subject of intense opposition in the House of Lords, will not receive its royal assent by the start of the Commons' summer recess on Thursday, Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, said yesterday. MPs yesterday approved amendments to the merchant shipping (salvage and pollution) bill which will enable the government to ensure that an international agreement to combat oil pollution comes into force next year.
