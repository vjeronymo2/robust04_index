Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text]Moscow, January 5 TASS -- Ex-President of Georgia Zviad Gamsakhurdia committed suicide in one of the districts of western Georgia on December 31, 1993. Gamsakhurdia's death was announced by his wife Manana. She said the place, where her husband happened to be at that time, was surrounded by unidentified men. According to her version, "the Georgian authorities are aware of Gamsakhurdia's death, but have carefully concealed the fact to this day."
