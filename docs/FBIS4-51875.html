BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 30 (KCNA) -- Solidarity messages came to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il from foreign party leaders supporting the Korean people's struggle to defend the dignity of their nation and the sovereignty of their country. Boris Petkov, chairman of the Executive Council of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party (Marxists), in his message said the Bulgarian communists resolutely denounced the United States and the South Korean authorities for hourly increasing the danger of war on the Korean peninsula, and urged a halt to military pressure and all hostile acts against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The Bulgarian communists will invariably stand firm on the side of the Korean people who are waging an indomitable struggle for justice and peace and for the triumphant advance of the socialist cause, he stressed. Dragomir Draskovic, president of the National Committee of the League of Communists-Movement for Yugoslavia, Ali Hassan, general secretary of the Supreme Council of the Nationalist Socialist Party of Syria in Lebanon, and Guzman Rivera Castaneda, general secretary of the Socialist Revolutionary Party of Peru, in their messages expressed the belief that the Korean people under the wise leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il would win a great victory in their struggle to accomplish the cause of building socialism of chuche and defend the security of their country. They stressed that they would stand for ever by the Workers' Party and People of Korea who are fighting the United States and its followers.
