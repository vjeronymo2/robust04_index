940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / International Company News: Savage Resources undersubscribed Savage Resources, the Australian company acquiring the US zinc mining assets of Belgium's Union Miniere for USDollars 200m, said its rights issue, designed to help fund the deal, was less 75 per cent subscribed. Some 135.8m shares, representing ADollars 169.7m, were committed to by shareholders, leaving 47.7m shares, or ADollars 58.9m-worth, to be taken up by the underwriters, Ord Minnett and Prudential-Bache. The issue price had been set at ADollars 1.25 two months ago, when the stock market was at significantly higher levels.
