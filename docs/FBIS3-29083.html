Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 15 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received gift from the Communist Party of Uruguay. The gift was handed to Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, by Pedro Balbi, executive secretary general of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay, on a visit to Korea.
