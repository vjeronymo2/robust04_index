April 3, 1989, Monday, Valley Edition

MISSING WOMAN, 77, FOUND IN SUNLAND

A 77-year-old woman missing from a senior citizen day-care center in Sunland since last Tuesday has been found, police said Sunday.

Lenore Jones, who suffers from memory loss, was found Saturday about 1:30 p.m. near Oro Vista Avenue and McGroarty Place in Sunland by two youths who were hiking, Los Angeles police Sgt. Ken Dionne said. The location is about three-fourths of a mile from the Canyon Senior Adult Day Care Center, from where Jones was reported missing.
