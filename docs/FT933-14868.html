930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / International Company News: Banner Homes to buy BES companies BANNER HOMES, the USM-quoted property trading and development group, is acquiring two BES housebuilding companies, Housebuilding Portfolio One and Housebuilding Portfolio Two, which it launched in 1990. Banner owns 10.7 per cent of their share capital and has managed them from inception. Banner has also announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 255,000 for the year to March 31, compared with losses of Pounds 688,000. Turnover was slightly lower at Pounds 6.63m (Pounds 6.83m). Had the two companies been part of the group as at March 31, they would have increased turnover by about 30 per cent. Their combined sales for the year were Pounds 1.81m and pre-tax profits Pounds 33,000. Net assets at March 31 amounted to Pounds 739,000. Earnings per share came through at 2p (losses 3.9p).
