930407 FT 07 APR 93 / World Trade News: EC machine import fears EUROPEAN construction equipment manufacturers are showing growing concern over the volume and price of machinery imports into the European Community, reports Andrew Baxter. At a meeting in Paris last week they voiced fears about the amount and price of earthmoving plant from South Korea and a possible surge in imports of cheap machinery from China and eastern Europe. According to an article intoday's Construction Weekly the construction talks were described as unofficial and at an early stage.
