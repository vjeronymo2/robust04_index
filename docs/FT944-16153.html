941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Delyn at Pounds 423,000 Increased expansion costs and pressure on gross margins checked pre-tax profits slightly at Delyn Group, the housebuilding and consumer packaging company. The pre-tax figure of Pounds 423,000 for the six months to July 31 compared with a loss of Pounds 142,000 for the corresponding period, which included losses totalling Pounds 591,000 in respect of the disposal of the former French associate. Operating profits were Pounds 476,000 (Pounds 494,000) on turnover of Pounds 7.95m (Pounds 6.71m). Earnings per share came out at 3.22p, compared with losses of 3.55p, and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
