Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Feb 6 (CTK) -- "The Sarajevo mortar massacre is an outrageous scandal. The action again confirms that every war leads to escalating cruelty," Czech President Vaclav Havel said today reacting to the shelling of a Sarajevo marketplace that left over 60 people dead. Havel told journalists before his departure on a visit to India and Thailand that "in his view, the international community should entervene much more energetically in Bosnia so that this fratricidal conflict be stopped."
