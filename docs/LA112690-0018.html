November 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: BY THE NUMBERS

Armand Hammer Museum of Art and Cultural Center in Westwood

1.

Opens Wednesday.

2. Hours: daily noon-7 p.m. (Closed Tuesday and holidays.)

3. Admission: $4.50 for adults. ($3 students, senior citizens.)

4. Parking fee: $3 for two hours.

5. Architect: Edward Larrabee Barnes.

6. Cost: $30 million.

7. Dimensions: 79,000 square feet.

8. Gallery space: 14,000 square feet.

9. Permanent collection value: $450 million.

10. Membership fees: $40-$1,000.

SOURCE: Armand Hammer Museum of Art and Cultural Center, (213) 443-7000
