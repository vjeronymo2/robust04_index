921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / International Company News: Fedex sees improvement FEDERAL Express, the Memphis-based delivery company, lifted second-quarter after-tax profits to Dollars 37.9m in the three months to end-November, against Dollars 26.5m a year ago, writes Nikki Tait. Revenues were virtually flat at Dollars 1.96bn, against Dollars 1.94bn. The group's troubled international operations made progress, although they still posted an operating loss of Dollars 37.6m on revenues of Dollars 564.2m, compared with a Dollars 69.6m loss on revenues of Dollars 651.6m a year earlier.
