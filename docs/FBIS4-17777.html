BFN [Text] Flight Control Center, 26 Apr -- The flight of the orbiting Mir station, manned by cosmonauts Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachev, and Valeriy Polyakov, is continuing. During the last two days the scientific section of the crew's program has included astrophysical and technical experiments, as well as medical check-ups. X-rays and gamma-radiation of extraterrestrial origin was measured by means of instruments mounted on the outside of the space station. Experiments to evaluate the condition of radioelectronic equipment, which has been exposed to the conditions of open space for a long time, were also carried out. The cosmonauts underwent routine medical checks, including clinical analysis of blood samples. In accordance with the timetable for routine preventive maintenance the crew will today replace coolant in the internal circuit of the Kvant-2 module's heat regulation system. According to reports from orbit and telemetric data the flight is proceeding normally.
