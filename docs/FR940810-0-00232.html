(vii) During all work covered by this section where employees are exposed above the TWA or excursion limit. (viii) In emergencies. (2) Respirator selection. (i) Where respirators are used, the employer shall select and provide, at no cost to the employee, the appropriate respirator as specified in Table 1, and shall ensure that the employee uses the respirator provided. (ii) The employer shall select respirators from among those jointly approved as being acceptable for protection by the Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) and the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) under the provisions of 30 CFR Part 11. (iii) The employer shall provide a tight fitting powered, air-purifying respirator in lieu of any negative-pressure respirator specified in Table 1 whenever: (A) An employee performing Class I, II or III work chooses to use this type of respirator; and (B) This respirator will provide adequate protection to the employee.
