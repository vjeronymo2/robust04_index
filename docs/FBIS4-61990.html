BFN [Statement issued by the Press Service of the Georgian Defense Ministry on 11 May] [Text] The Georgian Defense Ministry Press Service made the following statement: Russia's Ostankino television program, Novosti, based on Abkhaz sources, on 10 May, in effect, broadcast misinformation alleging the entry of Georgian armed groups into the Gali rayon. The same source asserts that 250 armed Georgian fighters are operating in the Gali rayon. The Press Service of the Georgian Defense Ministry is authorized to state that this news does not correspond to the truth. Similar information, based on Abkhaz sources, has been broadcast many times before. In this particular case, it aims at a specific target -- to separate or wreck Georgian-Abkhaz talks on peaceful settlement that started today in Moscow.
