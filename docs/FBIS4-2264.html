BFN [Text] The provincial government held a forum yesterday afternoon at the headquarters of the Fujian provincial Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Committee, in which non-CPC representatives from all walks of life participated by invitation to air their opinions on the government work report. Provincial CPC Secretary and Governor Jia Qinglin and leaders You Dexin, Wang Jianshuang, Liu Yongye, Zhang Jiakun, and Cao Jiufu attended the forum. Also present, were people in charge of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Democratic National Construction Association, the China Zhi Gong Dang, the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Jiusan Society, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and other public organizations concerned. During the forum, participants fully expressed their opinions on the various ideas for the development of the province in all aspects presented by the government work report. They particularly contributed many very valuable opinions and suggestions on such issues as lightening the burden on the peasants, deepening the rural reform, transforming the operational mechanism of state-run enterprises, increasing investment in education and in scientific farming projects, establishing an effective supervision mechanism, and curbing corruption. Provincial party committee Secretary and Governor Jia Qinglin addressed the forum, after hearing the opinions of non-CPC representatives from all walks of life, endorsing the opinions and suggestions presented by the forum participants and stating that their ideas would serve as a very useful reference for the government in policy making. Comrade Jia Qinglin briefed the participants on the economic development and progress in reform in the province this year. He pointed out that this year will be a crucial one for the province in achieving sustained, rapid, and healthy development. The province's task this year is to carry on the reform, keep up development, and ensure stability, while maintaining a balance between these three aspects. Jia Qinglin said: This province has initiated five reforms this year, specifically: Reform of the financial and taxation systems; the operation to introduce uniform foreign exchange rates; reform of the fundraising and financing systems; foreign trade reform; and reform of the banking system. As shown by the development over the past two months, smooth progress has been made in these reforms but more meticulous work must be done in the future. Jia hoped that everybody will make concerted efforts and that all the democratic parties will play an active part in political involvement and work together to do a good job in building Fujian Province.
