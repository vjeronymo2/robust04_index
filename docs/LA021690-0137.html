February 16, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS: DISTRIBUTED LOGIC POSTS A PROFIT

Distributed Logic Corp., the Anaheim computer products company, posted a net income of $18,641 for fiscal 1989, contrasted with a loss of $2.2 million for the year before.

Revenue for the year ended Oct. 31 rose slightly, to $51.5 million from $51.2 million.

For the fourth quarter of 1989, however, the company reported a net loss of $15,675, contrasted with a loss of $1.9 million for the period in 1988. Revenue increased 19%, to $13.5 million from $11.4 million.

Glenn C. Salley, Distributed chairman and chief executive, said net income was adversely affected by interest expense, foreign currency translation losses and unfavorable international tax charges.

"Although this resulted in a net loss for the fourth quarter, we were profitable for the full year, which is a significant improvement over the prior two fiscal years," he said.
