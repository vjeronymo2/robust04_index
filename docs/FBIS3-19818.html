Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Privatization Earns National Hydrocarbons Corporation 2.7 Billion Lire"] [Text] Rome, 17 Mar (ANSA) -- The privatization campaign carried out by the Italian state fuels group ENI [National Hydrocarbons Corporation] has already earned for the group some 2.67 trillion lire, just over some 1.6 billion dollars. The number of privatization operations carried out by ENI on Thursday [17 March] reached 48 with the sale of its interest in Liquipibigas to Novogas, for 245 billion lire, and Enichem synthesis interests to Great Lakes chemicals of the United States for 150 billion lire. Overall, ENI so far has sold off is participation in companies, plants and real estate for an overall earning of 4 trillion lire and involving some 11 thousand employees. ENI has carried out its privatization operations based on competitive auctions, publicized both at home and abroad with appraisals given by the world's leading specialized institutions.
