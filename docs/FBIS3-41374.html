Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cape Town Feb 17 SAPA -- Narcotics detectives seized 91,500 mandrax tablets with a street value of about R[rand]1.3 million and arrested two women and a man in Nyanga, outside Cape Town, on Wednesday afternoon. Police said on Thursday 1,500 tablets were found in a house in the area and two women were arrested. Detectives found a further 90,000 mandrax tablets hidden in a minibus parked outside the house and arrested a 42-year-old man. [passage omitted]
