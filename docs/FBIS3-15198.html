Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 1) TURAN--This noon President Aliyev met with Deputy Defense Minister of Russian Federation, Colonel-General, Kondratyev and Special Envoy of Russian President, Kazimirov. Journalists were not permitted to cover the meeting. The President's staff told TURAN that, "Russia has brought a new version of a settlement plan for the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict which contradicts the interests of Azerbaijan. Moreover, this version does not coincide with the programme of the settlement of the conflict that was proposed by the CSCE". The same source reports that "President Aliyev expressed his opinions and suggested that Kondratyev and Kazimirov perfect the Russian version". Kazimirov and Kondratyev are expected to return to Baku with a final version for a settlement plan.
