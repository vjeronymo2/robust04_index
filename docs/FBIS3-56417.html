Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow January 18 TASS -- President Boris Yeltsin and Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has not agreed on the new composition of the government so far and their meeting continues, TASS learned from well-informed sources in the Kremlin. It was initially reported that the meeting which began at noon [0900 GMT] would last two hours, but the source said Yeltsin and Chernomyrdin continued consultations after lunch break.
