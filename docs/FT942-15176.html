940416 FT 16 APR 94 / UK Company News: Johnson Matthey In yesterday's Financial Times it was stated that Johnson Matthey was 'ultimately controlled' by the Anglo American Corporation of South Africa. JM points out that Minorco and Johannesburg Consolidated Investment between them directly own 20 per cent of JM. Anglo in turn owns 43.1 per cent of Minorco and 39.6 per cent of JCI.
