930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / Iraqi ruling on money changers Iraq yesterday announced measures to legalise private money changers in an attempt to combat the country's thriving black market, and cure the economy's hyperinflation, writes James Whittington in Amman. A statement from Central Bank Governor Tareq al Tukmaji, published in the ruling Ba'ath party newspaper Al Thawra, said that the central bank 'has decided to process applications from people wishing to continue dealing with foreign currency.'
