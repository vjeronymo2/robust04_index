Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Investigating Judge Joseph Frayhah has issued official arrest warrants against the suspects Jirjis Tawfiq al-Khuri, Antoinette Yusuf Shahin, and Simon Edmund al-Kharyati -- all officials in the Lebanese Forces and who are in already in custody. Warrants were also issued for the arrest of Makram al-Mursi 'Ali and Jerome Yusuf Diya. Judge Frayhah also issued arrest warrants against the suspects Tony Ilyas 'Ubayd, the head of the intervention and protection branch of the Lebanese Forces security apparatus; Rushdi Tawfiq Ra'd, alias Nabil Fawzi alias Nabil Munassa, official of the same security apparatus; John Yusuf Shahin, alias Asad, official of the same security apparatus; and Vera Munassa, an official of the same Lebanese Forces security apparatus. They must be taken into custody. The investigating judge will continue interrogating other people who are already in custody to issue the necessary remand orders against them. Sources close to the investigation have revealed that several individuals now in custody have residence permits abroad and regularly moved between Lebanon and these other countries under various aliases, using forged passports of several nationalities. They used to enter Lebanon avoiding the official entry points, in fishing boats, via tourist areas, or by boarding Lebanese vessels on the high seas.
