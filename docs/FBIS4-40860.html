BFN ["Russian Specialists Began Clearing of Mines on the Georgian- Abkhazian Border"-- SNARK headline] [Text] Moscow, June 20 (SNARK) -- Moscow field-engineer battalion started clear of mine works of the observers' stationing sites at the Georgian-Abkhazian border. Abkhazian Representation to Moscow informed of it SNARK today. The complete entrance of the peace-keeping forces can begin only after the positive decision of this question in the Russia's Council of Federation on June 21. Plenipotentiary Representative of Abkhazia to Moscow Igor Akhba told SNARK that "Abkhazia, as well as Georgia expect the decision of the Council of Federation and hope that the deployment of peace- keeping forces will positively influence the peace and stability in the region". Moscow military specialists forecast the Council of Federation will adopt the decision on sending peace-keeping forces to the zone of Georgian-Abkhazian conflict.
