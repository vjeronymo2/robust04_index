BFN [Text] Beijing, May 2 (XINHUA) -- Ren Jianxin, president of the Chinese Supreme People's Court (SPC), met here this evening with Purwoto Gandasubrata, president of Indonesian Supreme Court, and his party. Gandasubrata and his ten-member entourage arrived here yesterday at the invitation of SPC. Besides Beijing they are also scheduled to visit Shanghai, Guangzhou and Shenzhen.
