July 30, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: COUNTYWIDE;

TRANSIT PANEL OFFERS BIKE ROUTE MAPS

A free map showing Ventura County bicycle routes is being distributed by the county Transportation Commission.

"Ventura County Bikeways" is available at libraries, police stations, bicycle shops and locations where bicycle licenses are issued, said Donna Cole, a commission spokeswoman. They are also available at the Ventura and Oxnard city halls and at commission headquarters.

Cole said 15,000 copies of the map were printed with help from the Air Pollution Control District and Commuter Transportation Services, which operates Commuter Computer. JACK SEARLES
