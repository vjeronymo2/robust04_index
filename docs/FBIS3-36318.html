Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Dniester Republic will not take part in the Moldovan Parliament elections, planned at the end of February, Dniester Moldavian Republic President Igor Smirnov said when meeting Bendery inhabitants. Igor Smirnov said the talks on a settlement to the Dniester conflict, in which Russia has mediated, have produced no practical results. According to Smirnov, Chisinau has rejected Tiraspols's proposal for the setting up of a confederation and ignored the proposal to set up a demilitarized zone in the area.
