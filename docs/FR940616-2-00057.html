DATES: The committee will meet on July 26, 1994, from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m., and on July 27, 1994, from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENCY [FRL 5000&hyph;6] Notice and Open Meeting of the Negotiated Rulemaking Advisory Committee for Small Nonroad Engine Regulations AGENCY: Environmental Protection Agency. ACTION: FACA Committee Meeting_Negotiated Rulemaking on Small Nonroad Engine Regulations.

SUMMARY: As required by section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463), EPA is giving notice of the next meeting of the Advisory Committee to negotiate a rule to reduce air emissions from small nonroad engines. The meeting is open to the public without advance registration. Agenda items for the meeting include reports from the 3 task groups and discussions of engine classification, air toxics data, and the small engine industry, and review of a draft ``single text'' strawman. DATES: The committee will meet on July 26, 1994, from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m., and on July 27, 1994, from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
ADDRESSES: The location of the meeting will be the South Wing Conference Center, Kentucky Fair and Exposition Center, 937 Phillips Lane, Louisville, KY, 40209, (502) 367&hyph;5000.
