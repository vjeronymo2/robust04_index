941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Syltone 14% ahead Syltone, the designer and manufacturer of transportation industry accessories, reported a 14 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.23m to Pounds 1.41m for the six months to September 30. Turnover was up 11 per cent at Pounds 21m (Pounds 18.9m). Earnings per share were 5.01p (4.24p), or 4.89p (4.15p) fully diluted. The interim dividend rises to 1.71p (1.625p). Mr John Clegg, chairman, said the group's only disappointing performance came from Rotocold, its air-conditioning subsidiary, where action was being taken to 'finally stem losses'.
