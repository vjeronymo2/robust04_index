940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Green Property Profits of Green Property rose to IPounds 2.14m (Pounds 2.06m) pre-tax for 1993, an improvement of 17 per cent over last time's IPounds 1.83m. Net rents advanced to IPounds 4.75m (IPounds 4.65m). Earnings emerged at 9.57p (7.68p) and a final dividend of 2.8p makes a 4p (3.8p) total. At the period-end net assets per share had risen to 197p (167p) while the balance sheet was strengthened by early conversion of the 1995 loan stock.
