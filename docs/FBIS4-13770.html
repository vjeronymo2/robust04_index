BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, 12 Indian troops were killed when mojahedin launched a retaliatory attack on military camp near (Dandalgam). Several soldiers were also injured in the attack. Meanwhile, Indian troops martyred another six persons during siege and search operations at various places in the valley. Two persons were killed in an indiscriminate firing by the troops in Sopor District, while two mojahedin fell in a clash at (Gulzar Tangmarg). A brutal crackdown was carried out in the suburban areas of Srinagar. People were forced to come out of their houses for searches and were made to stand in the open for hours. Over 50 people, mostly youths, were rounded up during the operation. Three people were seriously injured when a bomb exploded in Jammu region.
