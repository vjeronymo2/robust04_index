BFN [From MAINICHI SHIMBUN 26 April Morning Edition] [Text] Michio Watanabe, Liberal Democratic Party member and former deputy prime minister and foreign minister, commented on the new administration led by Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata on 25 April. He said: "I would like (Hata) to carry out Shinseito's original policies. I will cooperate in implementing policies that are beneficial for Japan, and will not cooperate with those that are not."
