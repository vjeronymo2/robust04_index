BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on developments related to the elections scheduled for 8 May. Yesterday, Juan Francisco de la Guardia, chairman of the Electoral Delegates Commission, told LA ESTRELLA DE PANAMA that on 8 May, 361 electoral delegates will montior Election Day developments and represent the Electoral Tribunal magistrates before governors, mayors, corregidores, and members of the public force. [Panama City LA ESTRELLA DE PANAMA in Spanish 23 Apr 94 p A1] The presidential candidate of the United People alliance, Ernesto Perez Balladares, has visited communities in Cocle Province, where he reiterated his commitment to a government that will promote "stability, reconciliation, security, and integrated development." [Panama City Circuito RPC Television in Spanish 2300 GMT 27 Apr 94]
