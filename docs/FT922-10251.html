920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Mortgage net assets at 173.7p Net asset value per share of Scottish Mortgage & Trust were 173.7p at the March 31 year end, 0.8 per cent up on the 172.3p of a year earlier. Earnings dipped from 4.42p to 4.25p. The dividend is increased from 3.7p to 3.9p with a final of 2.6p.
