BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry's Information and Press Department has reported that ordinary, diplomatic, and service passports will change. So that passports are in line with the objectives and values of the Islamic Republic of Iran's system, as of 1 April, the anniversary of the establishment of the Islamic Republic of Iran, new passports will replace the current ones. The new passports will be different in size, shape, and in their security aspect from the existing documents and will be compiled by a computer in a way that will facilitate access to information and to references.
