Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The European Union's [EU] Ministerial Council tonight unanimously approved the appointment of Frenchman Serge Abou as EU observer at the talks on the Cyprus issue. He will be reporting on the course of the talks and whether they are proceeding in accordance with Community norms. Ioannis Kranidhiotis, Greek Foreign Ministry secretary general, said that the observer's appointment was accepted by the 12 EU ministers with great satisfaction. The proposal for Serge Abou's appointment was submitted by Hans van den Broek, EU Commissioner in charge of foreign relations. Hans van den Broek made it clear that the observer will be following the talks on the Cyprus issue as an EU representative, but in his capacity as employee of the European Commission, he will submit his reports to the competent Commission members. According to our correspondent, the Council of Ministers will be free to summon the observer for direct briefings or it will be briefed by Hans van den Broek.
