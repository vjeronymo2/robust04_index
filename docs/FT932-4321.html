930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / World News in Brief: Second blast rocks Tyneside Tyneside was last night rocked by its second explosion in under 24 hours, this time at the North Shields Esso oil terminal where the IRA struck in April. Initial reports said their were no casualties or fire. The blast came as experts searched for clues to the explosion at Gateshead gas depot early yesterday. Earlier picture, Page 10
