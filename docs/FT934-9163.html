931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICO finished 4.2 per cent stronger on optimism over the progress of Nafta. The IPC index advanced 81.49 to 2,035.33 and volume was a heavy 161m shares. In Brazil, equities rose 6.9 per cent on hopes that the government would launch anti-inflationary measures before the year-end. The Bovespa index rose 1,485 to 22,829.
