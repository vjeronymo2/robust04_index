BFN [Text] Ibrahim Rugova, president of the republic of Kosova, is holding meetings with foreign diplomats. Mehmet Haziri: [Haziri] The Kosova Information Center reports that today, President Ibrahim Rugova received three countries' CSCE representatives -- (Laura Mehatian), charge d'affaires at the Italian Embassy; (Goran Jacobson) and (Thorsten Petren), charge d'affaires at the Swedish Embassy; as well as (Andreas Vinzel), charge d'affaires at the Hungarian Embassy in Belgrade. President Rugova informed the CSCE diplomats of recent developments and the grave situation in Kosova. Also, in a meeting with Mrs. (Ann Brulant), charge d'affaires of the Norwegian Embassy in Belgrade, and (Siri Ovre), they talked about the present situation in Kosova and recent developments in the region. In both of these meetings, President Rugova spoke of the continuing aggravation of the situation in Kosova due to the constant increase in Serbian repression and violence against the Albanian population. The CSCE diplomats and the Norwegians stayed in Kosova to complete their fact-finding mission on the present trends and efforts of Albanian political parties and individuals in attempting to resolve all problems peacefully and through dialogue.
