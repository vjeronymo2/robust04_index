Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Judge Joseph Furayhah, the judicial investigator in the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najat Church, has received the results of the investigation into the case. After questioning the suspects who are in custody, Judge Furayhah issued detention warrants against some and arrest warrants against fugitives on charges of participating in the operation. The security agencies have been instructed to pursue and arrest the fugitives. Security and judicial sources have declined to reveal the names and number of the detained and wanted suspects to preserve the confidentiality of the investigation and the arrests.
