BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Boris Nikolayevich Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation, on the national day of Russia. President Kim Il-song in his message expressed the belief that the relations between the two peoples will develop on the principle of independence, equality and non-interference in other's internal affairs.
