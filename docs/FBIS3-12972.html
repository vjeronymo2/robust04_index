Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Within the framework of the political forces' efforts to practically implement the document of pledge and accord signed in Amman on 10 Ramadan [20 February], the Political Forces' Dialogue Committee met at the Prime Ministry at noon today under the chairmanship of brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Presidential Council. The meeting was devoted to discussing the role of the Political Forces' Dialogue Committee in following and supervising the implementation of all aspects of the document in the light of the topics listed on the Committee's agenda. Following deliberations, the Dialogue Committee (?asked) the committees it formed to expedite the drafting of the required concepts in implementation of the relevant decisions. These committees are: The Security Committee, the Political and Military Committee, the Information Committee, and the committee in charge of drawing up a working program to follow up and supervise the implementation of the accord. These committees will present their plans to the Political Forces' Dialogue Committee meeting, which will be held the day after tomorrow, Wednesday.
