940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Exceptional lifts CRP Leisure After an exceptional credit of Pounds 529,655 following a loan waiver, CRP Leisure produced pre-tax profits of Pounds 245,726 for the year to October 31 from turnover of Pounds 644,732. This compared with FRS 3 adjusted losses of Pounds 1.21m last time, from turnover on continuing operations of Pounds 993,150. Earnings per share were 1.5p (losses 11.4p).
