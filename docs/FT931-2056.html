930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / International Company News: KPI seeks listing on Hong Kong Stock Exchange KPI, the Chinese fertiliser and agricultural products trader, is seeking a listing on the Hong Kong stock exchange through an issue of 70m shares to raise HKDollars 72.1m (USDollars 9.3m). The new shares represent 25 per cent of the company's enlarged issued share capital. 'Over 85 per cent of the group's sales are made to the China market,' said Mr Cheung Siulam, managing director.
