BFN [Unattributed report: "Crvenkovski Received by Berlusconi"] [Excerpt] The newly appointed Italian Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi received his Macedonian [[The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] counterpart, Branko Crvenkovski, yesterday. This is Berlusconi's first meeting with a foreign prime minister visiting Italy. He was informed on the situation in Macedonia and previous bilateral relations between the two countries. They also talked about the realization of the promised aid by the EU [European Union] and Berlusconi assured his country will do everything in its powers to accelerate it. Mr. Berlusconi greeted the idea of an East-West corridor, supposed to establish traffic and telecommunication links between Italy, Albania, Macedonia, Bulgaria, and Turkey. [passage omitted]
