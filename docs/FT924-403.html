921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / The shows go on A STRIKE by theatre technicians that threatened to close theatres in London's West End over the Christmas holiday was averted after a meeting at the conciliation service Acas last night. The Society of West End Theatre Producers agreed to raise its pay offer from the previous sticking point of 2.5 per cent to an immediate 3 per cent and then up to 4 per cent in six months' time.
