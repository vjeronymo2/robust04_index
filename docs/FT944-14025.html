941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / International Company News: Clayhithe CLAYHITHE: Offer for minority of Horstmann accepted in respect of 669,315 shares, representing 96.32 per cent of minority interests. Offer declared unconditional in all respects. EMBASSY PROPERTY Group said talks resulting from the approach on September 16 by John I Jacobs regarding a possible offer were continuing. Further announcement will be made as soon as possible.
