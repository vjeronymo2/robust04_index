921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / MMI sells its computer arm MUNICIPAL Mutual Insurance, the stricken local-authority insurer, sold its computer services division, MMC, to CFM Group, a Marlow-based facilities management company, yesterday. The deal was originally agreed in July. CFM will pay more than Pounds 1m to acquire MMC, employing about 60 of the company's 70 staff. MMC managed computer facilities for five local authorities.
