BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] The May actions of the irreconcilable opposition in Moscow, which ended with the Russian flag being burned and Rutskoy and his comrades-in-arms appealing for the overthrow of the present regime, have become the subject of a discussion in the Kremlin. On this occasion Boris Yeltsin was visited by the speaker of the upper chamber of parliament Vladimir Shumeyko and Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. Shumeyko reported that, when discussing the problem of ensuring stability in society, there was an unanimous opinion that any actions of the irreconcilables, which violate laws and the Constitution, should be stopped. In the meantime, the Moscow procuracy was instructed to determine within ten days whether there are grounds for instituting criminal proceedings on the basis of the information it possesses.
