BFN [Text] Brother President leader Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received the charge d'affaires of the U.S. Embassy in Cairo this afternoon. During the meeting, Brother Abu-'Ammar entrusted the charge d'affaires with a verbal message for President Bill Clinton on the talks currently under way on the implementation of the declaration of principles agreement, signed in Washington, and the agreements signed in Cairo. The message also concerns the attempts and obstacles trumped up by the Israeli Government to enable it to avoid implementing the agreements, a matter which exposes the peace process to danger.
