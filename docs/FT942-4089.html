940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Capital Gearing net assets ahead Capital Gearing Trust raised net asset value per share by 18 per cent from 443.5p to 522.5p at April 5 1994. Six months earlier, the figure was 505.4p. After-tax revenue moved ahead by Pounds 10,000 to Pounds 36,000 in the year and earnings per share came to 0.58p (0.49p). A dividend of 0.45p (0.43p) has already been declared.
