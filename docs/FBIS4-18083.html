BFN [Text] Munich -- The Landshut Public Prosecutor's Office has initiated preliminary investigations against Republikaner Chairman Franz Schoenhuber for slandering Ignatz Bubis, chairman of the Central Council of Jews in Germany. A decision as to whether there is sufficient reason for initiating investigations cannot be expected before Easter, Senior Public Prosecutor Bernhard Brenneis told DPA on Tuesday [29 March]. Before investigations are started, the European Parliament will have to lift Schoenhuber's immunity. Schoenhuber's accusation of "instigation" against Bubis has been met with dismay from all parties.
