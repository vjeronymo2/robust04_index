BFN [Text] Damascus -- At the invitation of the National Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, a delegation from the Chinese Communist Party, led by Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and head of the Chinese Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Damascus yesterday evening. The delegation was received at Damascus International Airport by comrade Mut'ib Shinan, member of the Ba'th Party's National Command; comrade 'Abdallah Abu-al-Rish, member of the Party Committee for Supervision and Inspection; comrade Mustafa 'Isa, member of the Executive Bureau of the General Federation of Trade Unions and secretary of the Arab and International Relations Office; several comrades in the National Command; and the Chinese ambassador in Damascus. The delegation will hold party talks with the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, in addition to political meetings with party and state officials.
