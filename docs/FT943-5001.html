940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Mithras Investment above benchmark Net asset value per share at Mithras Investment Trust improved from 54.2p at February 21, when it came to the market, to 61.5p at June 30. This marked a 13.5 per cent increase compared with a 11.7 per cent fall in the benchmark FT-SE-A All-Share Index. Net revenue for the period was Pounds 65,000 for earnings per share of 0.16p.
