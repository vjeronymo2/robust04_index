BFN [Report by Salwa al-Ustuwani] [Text] Damascus -- Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad has received a message from Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani stressing Iran's support for Syria in the event of any aggression on it following recent Israeli raids on Lebanon and the escalation in the military situation. Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati delivered the message to President al-Asad during their meeting in Damascus yesterday. The objective of his visit is to hold talks with senior Syrian officials on conditions in the region, especially the military deterioration in Lebanon and Israel's recent aggression on Hizballah's base in the Lebanese al-Biqa'. Upon his arrival in the Iranian Embassy in Damascus Velayati met with a Hizballah delegation led by Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah. They discussed developments in the situation in Lebanon in the light of the continued Israeli attacks. Velayati stressed Iran's absolute support for the Lebanese resistance against the Israeli occupation. Iranian sources in Damascus have told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT that the Hizballah secretary general held talks with some senior Syrian officials on conditions and coordinating positions with Syria.
