930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Thompson Clive net asset value ahead Over the 1992 year fully diluted net asset value at Thompson Clive Investments rose more than 14 per cent, from 165p to 188.6p. By March 31 it had further increased to 196p, after the flotation of Holliday Chemical Holdings in which Thompson Clive has a holding valued at Pounds 2.4m. Net revenue was Pounds 451,000 (Pounds 539,000) for earnings per share of 3.5p (4.2p). The dividend is held at 3.8p.
