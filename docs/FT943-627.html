940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: S Lyles picks up A generally higher level of activity, particularly in the second six months, helped S Lyles, the yarn spinner and dyer, turn in a pre-tax profit of Pounds 566,000 for the year ended June 30, against an Pounds 89,000 loss previously. Turnover rose 16 per cent to Pounds 16.9m (Pounds 14.5m) which reflected changes in product mix and pressure on selling prices. Earnings per share came to 10.37p (1.39p) while a final dividend of 2.5p makes a total of 3.5p (3.3p).
