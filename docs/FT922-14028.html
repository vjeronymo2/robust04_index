920408 FT 08 APR 92 / International Company News: Wheelabrator to build Dollars 240m mill WHEELABRATOR Techno-logies, a unit of Waste Management, plans to build a Dollars 240m linerboard recycling mill in Muskogee, Oklahoma, AP-DJ reports. The company said the mill will be able to convert 320,000 tonnes a year of corrugated boxes into more than 272,400 tonnes of linerboard. Construction of the mill, which will employ a permanent workforce of about 120, is expected to begin this summer.
