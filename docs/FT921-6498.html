920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / Nagorno-Karabakh fighting flares The dispute over Nagorno-Karabakh exploded in a fresh wave of fighting yesterday when Armenian forces backed by heavy weapons overran the Azeri-populated town of Khojaly, Reuter reports. Local news agencies said Armenian forces chased Azeri army units out of Khojaly, leaving the city of Shusha as the only Azeri stronghold inside the remote Transcaucasian region.
