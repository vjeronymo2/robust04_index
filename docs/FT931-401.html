930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Malaya deeper in loss with Pounds 981,725 Malaya Group, the USM-quoted garage operator, saw pre-tax losses nearly doubled from Pounds 499,705 to Pounds 981,725 over the 1992 year. Turnover improved to Pounds 8.35m (Pounds 7.74m). However, the pre-tax result was after increased operating and administrative costs and a property write-down of Pounds 396,592. Losses per share came through at 4.93p (5.84p). The group undertook a recapitalisation during the year and the acquisition of Western Motor Works (Chislehurst).
