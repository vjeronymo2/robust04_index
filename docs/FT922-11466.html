920425 FT 25 APR 92 / UK Company News: Joseph Holt shows all-round growth Joseph Holt, the Manchester-based brewer and hotelier, lifted turnover 26 per cent and pre-tax profit 11 per cent in 1991, and is raising the dividend. Turnover came to Pounds 22.5m (Pounds 17.8m) while the profit worked through at Pounds 6.37m (Pounds 5.72m). Earnings per share came out at 135.99p (123.99p) and a final dividend of 26p is proposed for a total of 35p, against 31p.
