810-nm High-Power Quantum Well Laser

94P60277C Beijing ZHONGGUO KEXUE BAO [CHINESE SCIENCE NEWS] in Chinese 29 Apr 94 p 4 94P60277C Beijing ZHONGGUO KEXUE BAO [CHINESE SCIENCE NEWS] in Chinese 29 Apr 94 p 4 Chinese CSO [Article by Zheng Huaide: ``810-nm High-Power Quantum Well Laser''] [Summary] This 810-nm high-power quantum well laser diode, which can replace a Xe lamp as a pump source for Nd:YAG, NYAB and other solid state lasers and for self-frequency-doubling crystals, is grown via MBE and MOVPE techniques. Performance indicators are as follows: output power = 1 W (room-temperature, CW), wavelength = 810 +/- 4 nm, lifetime = 3000 h (1 W, CW, 25 deg C), and optical-electronic conversion efficiency exceeds 30 percent.
