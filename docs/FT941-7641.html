940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / Woman shot at de Klerk rally A woman was shot dead yesterday at a chaotic campaign rally of President F W de Klerk's National Party in which a black gunman opened fire on African National Congress members, Reuter reports from Roodepan. A man was arrested and charged with murder. Mr De Klerk cancelled a planned appearance at the rally after being struck by a stone during an earlier campaign stop during his swing through the northern Cape. He was unhurt.
