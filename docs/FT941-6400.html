940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / Midlands economic forum announced Sandwell borough in the West Midlands is setting up an economic forum to develop and co-ordinate regeneration of the area. The forum is based on a joint approach of the borough council and the local training and enterprise council. It is an early sign of the way in which permanent partnerships are being created between the public and private sectors in response to the government's unification of both its regeneration budget and its departmental activities in the regions.
