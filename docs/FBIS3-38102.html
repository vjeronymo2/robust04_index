Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Qiao: `Tibet Will Continue To Be Part of China'"] [Text] Vienna -- On Friday [28 January] President Thomas Klestil broached the human rights situation in China and the situation in Tibet in a talk with Qiao Shi, president of the Chinese parliament. This was stated in a handout of the President's Office. Reportedly, Qiao reaffirmed China's point of view that Tibet has always been part of Chinese territory. In the parliament Qiao Shi had also affirmed that Tibet belongs to his country and declared that the Catholic Church is protected fully in the PRC. In a talk with members of the Bundesrat Presidium, the member of the CPC Political Bureau stated that the only condition for the Dalai Lama's return is his renunciation of Tibet's independence.
