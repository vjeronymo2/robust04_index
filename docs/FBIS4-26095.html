BFN [Text] Beijing, June 17 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese military medical delegation returned here today after attending the 30th International Congress of Military Medicine in Germany. During the meeting, its bid to host the 31st International Congress of Military Medicine in Beijing in 1996 won majority approval. The group was led by Major General Lu Zengqi, Chinese Government representative and director of the Health Department of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The International Committee of Military Medicine, recognized by the United Nations, is an inter-governmental organization which now has some 100 member countries. The successful bidding for the 1996 venue will facilitate military medicine exchanges between China and other member countries, sources here said.
