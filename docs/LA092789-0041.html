September 27, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"Roe vs. Wade," the TV movie about the events that culminated in the 1973 Supreme Court decision that legalized abortion in this country, will be released on home video early next year. The NBC-produced movie, which won Emmys for outstanding special and for Holly Hunter's lead performance just last week, first aired on the network last May. Generally, television movies air twice on the network before moving on to home video, but since NBC produced this project in house, the network can earn extra revenue from home video sales while retaining the rights to broadcast it again. Shauna Snow
