BFN [Text] Yemen's Minister of Justice 'Abdallah Ghanim left Damascus this morning. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Syria's Justice Minister Dr. 'Abdallah Tulbah and the two deputies of the president of the Cassation Court. In a predeparture statement to SANA, Ghanim noted that he delivered a message from Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in the Republic of Yemen, to His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad about current developments in Yemen. President al-Asad showed full understanding of Yemeni developments and expressed his support for the preservation of its unity, security, and stability.
