BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] The Governments of Panama and Peru mutually agreed to resume diplomatic relations, according to a Panamanian Foreign Ministry communique. The communique states that the decision was made following Foreign Minister Jose Mulino's visit to Peru. Both nations made the decision following a discussion during Mulino's visit of the historical ties uniting them, their traditional ties of friendship, and the noble goal of Latin American integration.
