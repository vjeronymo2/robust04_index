930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / Agency moves for payment change THE FORENSIC Science Service, the criminal investigation agency, is seeking to replace the case-by-case payment system with trying to negotiate fixed-term contracts with police forces. The agency said fixed contracts for police work, which accounts for more than 90 per cent of its annual Pounds 24.2m turnover, would simplify the process by which it recovers the costs of investigations.
