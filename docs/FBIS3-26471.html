Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese workers will get an extra half-day holiday a week starting March 1 this year, according to regulations issued by the State Council. The regulations said that workers in Chinese Government organs, institutions, enterprises and other organizations will work eight hours a day and 44 hours a week from March 1. Units that claim special difficulties in adopting the measure will be given a two-month grace period to institute the new system. Units may shorten working hours if special conditions are involved. No one shall extend working hours without authorization. When special conditions or urgent tasks call for extension of working hours, the relevant state stipulations shall be followed, the regulations said.
