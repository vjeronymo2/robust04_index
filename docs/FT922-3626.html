920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / ICI fund for long-term R&D IMPERIAL Chemical Industries, the UK's biggest corporate spender on research and development, is to set up a strategic research fund to support long-term projects. The fund will start with Pounds 5m next year and is likely to rise to Pounds 20m a year by 1995. ICI's total expenditure on R&D is about Pounds 600m a year.
