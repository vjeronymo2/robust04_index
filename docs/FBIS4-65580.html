BFN [Text] European Parliament [EP] President Egon Klepsch has stated that the EP supports the European Union [EU] expansion through the accession of Cyprus and Malta, and that he will also support this position at the Corfu Summit conference to be held in June. Klepsch stressed: We must exert every effort toward finding a peaceful Cyprus solution. It would be an honor for whomever achieves this goal. Klepsch, who is on a two-day official visit to Cyprus, made this statement after meeting with House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos this morning. The House of Representatives president said Klepsch's visit stresses the EP decision and collective desire to support Cyprus' efforts for its accession to the EU. Galanos noted: The most substantive message to the Turkish side so that it may sit at the talks with the political will to find a solution is for the talks on Cyprus' accession to the EU to begin in January 1995. Galanos said he was satisfied with Klepsch's moral support in these difficult times. The EP president also met with party leaders and representatives today. Now he is in a meeting with President Glavkos Kliridhis.
