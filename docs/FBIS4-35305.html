BFN [Text] Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the House of Representatives, and his delegation returned to Sanaa last night following a two-day visit to the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar made a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', in which he said: During our visit to the sisterly UAE, we met with His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan, president of the UAE, and a number of officials. We explained to them the reality of the situation in our country. We put them in the picture of the recent developments. We have sensed from His Highness Shaykh Zayid an understanding and a concern for Yemen and its unity and safety.
