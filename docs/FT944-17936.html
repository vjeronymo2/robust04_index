941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / International flight lands in Belgrade A Russian Aeroflot airliner landed in Belgrade yesterday, formally ending a 28-month UN ban on international commercial flights to and from Yugoslavia, writes James Whittington in Belgrade. The delayed flight carrying Mr Vitaly Churkin, Russian deputy foreign minister and peace envoy, finally landed to cheers. 'It is a very significant day today,' Mr Churkin said. 'Yugoslavia has firmly chosen the path of peace and political settlement'.
