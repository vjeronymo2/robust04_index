BFN [Article signed JWI: "Palestine: European Money for the Palestinian Police Force"] [Text] Luxembourg, 19 Apr (BELGA) -- The European Union [EU] will contribute ECU10 million to the establishment of the Palestinian police force in Gaza and Jericho following the decision of the European foreign ministers meeting in Luxembourg today. Belgian Foreign Minister Willy Claes briefly reported on his meeting last Wednesday [13 April] with PLO leader 'Arafat at the Council of Europe in Strasbourg. During that meeting Mr. 'Arafat had emphasized the need for quick and specific assistance for Gaza and Jericho, which will shortly have an independent Palestinian administration. According to a spokesman, 'Arafat asked for special assistance for the creation and the outfitting of the Palestinian police force. The EU is ready to meet the Palestinian request by pledging ECU10 million. The ministers have agreed that this amount will be taken from the European budget.
