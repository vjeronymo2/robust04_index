April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BELL HIGH 50-YEAR REUNION

The Bell High School winter and summer classes of 1940 will hold a 50-year reunion May 5. The reunion is scheduled to take place at the Embassy Suites Hotel, 8425 Firestone Blvd., Downey. For more information, call Richard Lawrence at 928-2491 or John Kinkade at 560-3593.
