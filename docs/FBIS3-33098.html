Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Friends, as we said in the headlines, OAS Secretary General Joao Baena Soares today reiterated that Cuba should return to the OAS. After the 25th special session [as heard] of the continent-wide organization opened, Baena Soares said that Cuba is a country of the American hemisphere and should for that reason be part of the OAS. Baena Soares announced that he will continue insisting that this matter be again put on the OAS agenda for analysis.
