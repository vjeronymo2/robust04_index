April 22, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Daniel W. Loyer has joined UniStructure Inc. in the new position of vice president of marketing and sales. Previously, he was vice president of sales and marketing at Teledyne Philbrick in Boston. Before that, he was founder and president of Alternative Technologies in San Jose and a sales manager for the Zeltex division of Silicon General. UniStructure, based in Irvine, is a military and commercial electronics company.
