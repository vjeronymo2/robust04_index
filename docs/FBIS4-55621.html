BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpt] Sofia, May 14 (BTA) -- BSP [Bulgarian Socialist Party] leader Zhan Videnov met today a delegation of the Socialist Party of Serbia, visiting Bulgaria at the invitation of the Sofia organization of the Bulgarian Socialists to attend its conference. "We expect the left-wing parties in the Balkans to support the efforts of the Socialist Party of Serbia with initiatives for lifting the sanctions", Mr. Zarko Obralovic, leader of the Serbian delegation, said after the meeting. The leadership of the BSP explained that the meeting was a protocol one. [passage omitted]
