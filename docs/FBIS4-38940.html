BFN [Text] The Daugavpils [town] council has presented a protest to the command of the Northwestern Group of Forces of the Russian Army concerning the fact that it is not observing the schedule agreed for the withdrawal of the army from the town and the provision of residences belonging to the special municipal services of the town, who cannot take over the unsupervised conditions and the ransacked apartments, depots, and storehouses. Restoration work is, in practice, carried out by workers paid by the municipal housing administration, but compensation for them has not reached the town, nor does it intend to place the burden of the expense on the people of Daugavpils.
