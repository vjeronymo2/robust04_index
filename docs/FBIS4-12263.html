BFN [Text] An Israeli resident of the Qatif Bloc was stabbed and slightly wounded this morning by a terrorist near the Kissufim checkpoint in the Gaza Strip. He is currently being evacuated to a hospital in Beersheba. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the Israeli came to the checkpoint to pick up workers. On his way back to the Qatif Bloc, he was stabbed in the chest and shoulder by one of the workers. The terrorist dropped the knife and fled. Other workers called soldiers from the checkpoint who found the wounded man in his car. The security forces have launched a manhunt after the assailant. In another incident, an Israeli citizen was injured in Qalqilyah when his car was pelted with stones. He lost control of the vehicle, which overturned. The man sustained moderate injuries and was taken to a hospital in Kefar Sava. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] tightened the closure on the territories this morning and increased security at its checkpoints, especially in Samaria. Soldiers are conducting very thorough inspections and hardly any Samaria Arabs are being permitted to enter the Green Line.
