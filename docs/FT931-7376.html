930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / ICI board meets on split Ronnie Hampel, chief operating officer of ICI, about to enter the group's Millbank headquarters for the historic board meeting to decide whether to split the UK's biggest manufacturer in two. Mr Hampel is designated deputy chairman and chief executive of ICI after the split. David Barnes is designated chief executive of Zeneca, comprising ICI's drugs, agrochemicals and speciality chemicals businesses.
