931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: AG Holdings beats forecast with Pounds 2.91m In its first results since coming to the market in June, AG Holdings, which makes rope and cable reels, beat its prospectus forecast of Pounds 2.7m with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.91m for the 12 months to July 31. Turnover of Pounds 15.8m compared with Pounds 10.7m at March 31. Earnings per share came out at 11.4p and there is a special dividend of 2p as forecast in the listing.
