940427 FT 27 APR 94 / International Company News: Asarco returns to the black Asarco, one of the world's leading integrated non-ferrous metals producers, reported first-quarter net earnings of Dollars 26.6m, or 64 cents a share, against losses of Dollars 30.9m, or 74 cents, in the same period of 1993, writes Kenneth Gooding. There was also a Dollars 31.9m net gain on the sale of the group's remaining interest in Asarco Australia. Net earnings fell by Dollars 10m, and sales were down to Dollars 443m from Dollars 457.1m.
