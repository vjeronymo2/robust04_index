941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Indonesia gets Dollars 350m loans The Asian Development Bank has approved loans to Indonesia totalling more than Dollars 350m (Pounds 216m) for a power project and construction of vocational schools. A Dollars 272m loan would finance 1,800km of power lines, while Dollars 85m would be used to build 27 vocational schools and repair 12. Mr Ermansyah Jamin, head of a negotiating team on private power plants, said the country was expected to sign agreements with several foreign and local companies by the end of this year for construction of four major power plants.
