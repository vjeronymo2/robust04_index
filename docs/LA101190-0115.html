October 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH JANICE;

THE REMATCH

I was saved from myself on Wednesday when I offered a $10 exacta box on a race in which there was no exacta wagering. I thought only computers made those kinds of mistakes.

Anyway, I still lost $16 when Queen Emissary finished second paying $3.20 to place. Today's selection is $50 to win and $100 to place on Mysterious Pirate in the sixth.

Janice Goldinger's Wed. Bankroll: $1,965

Wednesday's Loss: $16

Current Bankroll: $1,949

SURELOCK lost $60 Wednesday when his exacta horses Port Washington and Intelligently failed to finish one-two. Today's selections are $100 to win on Past Remembered and a $10 exacta box, Past Remembered-Bequest-Girl of France, in the eighth race.

SURELOCK's Wed. Bankroll: $1,580

Wednesday's Loss: $60

Current Bankroll: $1,520

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.

Both players started with a $2,000 bankroll.
