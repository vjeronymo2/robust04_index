March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

SCHABARUM WON'T RUN

County supervisors claim they can't afford to build roads or parks, but are going to be spending more than $5 million to defend a plan to keep themselves in office at the expense of Latinos and other county residents ("Latinos Urge Board to Settle Remap Suit With New District," Part B, March 14). When will the voters realize that their five elected representatives don't belong in office, they belong in jail.

JIM JAMES

Castaic
