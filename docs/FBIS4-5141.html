BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 6 -- Palestinian Foreign Minister Faruq Qaddumi and his party left Hanoi today, concluding their three-day official friendship visit to Vietnam. They were seen off at the government guest house by Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, Deputy Foreign Minister Nguyen Dy Nien, Deputy Head of the External Relations Commission of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee Do Van Tai and other Vietnamese officials. Palestinian Ambassador to Vietnam Sa'id Khalil al-Masri was also present. Before leaving here, Minister Faruq Qaddumi held a press conference on the results of this Vietnam visit.
