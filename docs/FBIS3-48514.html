Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] U Nyunt Swe, deputy minister of foreign affairs, returned to Yangon [Rangoon] by air last night after visiting India at the invitation of the Indian Government's External Affairs Ministry. The deputy foreign minister was welcomed at the airport by directors general and officials concerned from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Mr. Gopalaswani Parthasarathy, Indian ambassador to Myanmar [Burma]; and Indian Embassy officials.
