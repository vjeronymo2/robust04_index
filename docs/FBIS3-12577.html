Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The military committee, composed of a Yemeni military team, a Jordanian military team, an Omani military team, and the U.S. and French military attaches, this afternoon began to pay field visits to Abyan Governorate. After arriving in Abyan Governorate, the committee visited the Madram Brigade Command and explained the measures taken and the decisions that the committee has made in accordance with the powers authorized by the political leadership. Afterward, the committee visited the al-'Amaliqah Brigade Command and explained the measures agreed by the joint military committee as well as the tasks that will be carried out through direct supervision and monitoring in order to make sure that the forces have returned to their previous positions and that the checkpoints and recently erected roadblocks have been removed. The joint committee has set up a field subcommittee from its personnel. The subcommittee is charged with staying in Abyan in order to follow up the implementation of instructions and make sure that all parties comply with them. The committee voiced satisfaction with the commitment to the instructions, issued by both the defense minister and the chief of staff, to all units to return to their positions in accordance with the directives of the political leadership.
