940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Istanbul drops 6.9% on bonds Turkish equities remained under pressure from the bond market on news of another forthcoming treasury bill auction. The composite index fell 1,087.75, or 6.9 per cent, to 14,737.37, with most shares finishing limit down. Among the actives, Eregli weakened TL300 to TL4,050 as overall turnover remained static at TL894.6bn. Tel Aviv lost some of its gains on profit-taking after two sessions of rising share prices. The Mishtanim index closed 4.52, or 2.3 per cent, down at 194.82 following an advance of 3.5 per cent on Sunday, when dealers were already becoming pessimistic again. Traders said that portfolio managers, anxious to realise profits in a nervous market, dumped shares selectively.
