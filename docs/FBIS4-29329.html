BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il on June 15 sent a benevolent centenary birthday table to Yun Chin-ok residing in Kim Chaek City, North Hamgyong Province. Born into a fisherman's family, she was subjected to all manner of maltreatment and humiliation in the period of the feudal rule and during the harsh Japanese imperialist rule. She could enjoy a happy life after the liberation of the country and, now, has attained the age of 100 years thanks to the popular health policy of the party and the state. She is still in good health with a family of more than 50.
