November 1, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES;

'HORSE' IN AUTRY'S MUSEUM:

John Ford's 1924 silent classic "The Iron Horse" will be screened Sunday at 2 p.m. at the Gene Autry Western Heritage Museum Theater. On hand for the screening will be Madge Bellamy, who starred with George O'Brien in the film, which tells of the building of the transcontinental railroad. ALEENE MacMINN
