Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, March 7 (XINHUA) -- Croatian President Franjo Tudjman insisted today that the Muslim-Croat Federation- Confederation Agreement was not a result of instigation by the United States and its offer of 500 million U.S. dollars in aid. Tudjman said that Croatia's policy was consistent and that its constitution demands a referendum amongst the people to finally accept the confederation plan of the accord which was signed by Croatia, Bosnia's Croats and Muslims in Washington on March 1. Tudjman told a news conference in Zagreb that he and Bosnian Muslim leader Alija Izetbegovic had reached agreements, both open and secret, on the confederation plan. Izetbegovic had repeatedly voiced his support for the Croatian-Muslim alliance, he said.
