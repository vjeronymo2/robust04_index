BFN [Unattributed report: "Shipping Season on River"] [Text] The first ships traveled down the Vyatka River 28 April, starting timber logging. Shipment of grain and construction materials is next in line. In the whole shipping season, the crews of the Vyatka River Steamship Company have to ship around 2 million tonnes of freight. That is considerably less than in past years. Customers' inability to pay has robbed the enterprise of a chance of procuring the full amount of fuel. Some of the fleet will therefore be standing idle. However, passenger lines will open in time as planned and in a day or two the first motor ships will set out.
