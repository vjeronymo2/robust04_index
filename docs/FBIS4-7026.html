BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The North side has recently sent a letter, which contains the proposal to convene a national conference in Pyongyang or Seoul around the forthcoming August 15 to seek a way for great unity of the whole Korean nation, by international mail to the South Korean authorities, political parties, organizations, individual figures and overseas Koreans. The proposal was adopted at a joint meeting of the DPRK Government, political parties and public organizations on April 11. The letter was sent to the South Korean authorities, representatives of the "Democratic Liberal Party", the Democratic Party, the United People's Party, the "New Korea Party" and the New Political Reform Party, representatives of workers', peasants' and students' organizations, representatives of educational, cultural, press, religious and reunification movement organisations and other organisations, and figures of business and other circles. It was also mailed to the chairman, the vice-chairmen and the secretary general of the overseas headquarters of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification (Pomminnyon), chairmen of Pomminnyon regional headquarters, the co-chairman and the co-secretary general of the Alliance of Youths and Students for the Country's Reunification, the chairman and the advisor of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification, the chairman of the Committee of Korean Veterans in Moscow, the president of the International Taekwon-do Federation and other overseas Koreans.
