April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE INTIMATE EXPERIMENT

It is interesting that there is no mention at all of any of the Anglo fathers in this article. Could it be that they are so uncomfortable with the servant/nanny/ housekeeper/ baby-sitter that they have chosen to distance themselves from the whole thing and left their overburdened wives to deal with la situacion? If this is indeed an experiment in child rearing, when, how and by whom will the results be judged?

LELDE GILMAN

Santa Monica
