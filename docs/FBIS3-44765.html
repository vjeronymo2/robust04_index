Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 30 Dec (XINHUA) -- Presidential Decree No. 16 of the People's Republic of China The "Company Law of the People's Republic of China" has been adopted by the Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee of the People's Republic of China on 29 December 1993, and is hereby promulgated. The law will come into force on 1 July 1994. [Signed] Jiang Zemin, president of the People's Republic of China Dated] 29 December 1993
