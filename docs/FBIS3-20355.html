Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] "The Sudtirol Volks Partei [South Tirol Peoples Party, SVP] will not support any government that includes the National Alliance." The statement was released by the executive committee of the SVP, which met in Bolzano to examine the results of the election. According to the SVP, the success of the right on a national level represented a "heavy burden, whose dimensions are not yet clear." SVP President Brugger, who was reelected to the Chamber of Deputies, said : "As an ethnic minority, we are expecting to face hard times in Rome."
