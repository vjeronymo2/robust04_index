940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / World Trade News: Uruguay Round goes to US court Five environmental and public citizen groups opposing the Uruguay Round deal yesterday went to court in San Francisco to force the Office of the US Trade Representative to prepare an environmental impact statement for the Round, Nancy Dunne writes in Washington. A similar suit was filed last year in Washington against the North American Free Trade Agreement, linking the US, Canada and Mexico, to delay passage of the pact through Congress, but an appeals court upheld the government.
