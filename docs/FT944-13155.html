941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: Dofasco doubles its quarterly income Dofasco, one of Canada's two biggest steel makers, nearly doubled pre-tax profits in the third quarter with higher prices and surging demand for flat rolled products for the car, appliance and pipeline industries, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Sales rose 12 per cent to CDollars 565m and pre-tax profit was CDollars 57.2m (USDollars 42.5m), against CDollars 29.5m. But after special tax factors, final net profit was CDollars 53.8m or 56 cents against Dollars 62m or 70 cents a share a year earlier. At the nine-month stage, net profits rose to CDollars 157.5m or CDollars 1.66, up from 56 per cent or 46 cents a share on sales of CDollars 1.65bn, against CDollars 1.57bn.
