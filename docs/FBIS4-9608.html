BFN [Text] Konin, 29 Apr--Talks in Konin between Industry and Trade Minister Marek Pol and representatives of the striking brown coal miners did not resume at 0600 on 29 April as announced. According to unofficial reports, the trade unionists think that the government broke off Thursday's negotiations. On 28 April, during negotiations lasting approximately 12 hours, the trade unionists decided to extend the deadline set for the government to present a timetable for the implementation of mining and Solidarity demands until 1400 on Friday.
