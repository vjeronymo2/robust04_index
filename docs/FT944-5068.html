941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Whirpool signs agreement with Snowflake Electrical Appliance Group - Contracts & ventures Whirlpool of the US signed an agreement with China's Snowflake Electrical Appliance Group to make refrigerators at a plant near Beijing. The Dollars 30m joint venture plans output of 500,000 refrigerators a year and hopes to boost that to 1m. Snowflake, China's oldest refrigerator factory founded in 1956, is building a new plant on the outskirts of Beijing.
