Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn, Jan 9 (AFP) -- NATO Secretary General Manfred Woerner has won his battle against cancer after three operations and said he had a good chance to live, the Sunday weekly BILD AM SONNTAG reported. "I have beaten cancer," Woerner told the paper. "Doctors say I have an excellent chance" to live. The interview was published on the eve of a two-day summit meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization in Brussels. Woerner, 59, who was West German defense minister from 1982 to 1988, said he was operated on for the third time in mid-December in Aachen, western Germany. He underwent surgery for colon cancer in April 1992 and June 1993.
