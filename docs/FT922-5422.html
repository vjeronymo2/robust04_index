920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / Recovery in air traffic World airline passenger traffic will recover quickly over the next three years after a setback in 1991, the International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO) said yesterday, writes Daniel Green. ICAO predicted annual growth of more than 10 per cent in the Asia-Pacific region, by 1994, while growth in Europe was likely to remain below 2 per cent a year. The agency estimated that average world growth in 1992 would be 4.2 per cent, rising to 5.3 per cent in 1993 and 6.4 per cent in 1994.
