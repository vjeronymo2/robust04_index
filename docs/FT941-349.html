940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: FW Thorpe Pre-tax profits more than doubled, from Pounds 402,000 to Pounds 869,000, at FW Thorpe, the lighting equipment maker, in the half year to December 31. On turnover ahead from Pounds 7.54m to Pounds 9.5m operating profits on continuing operations were Pounds 786,000 (Pounds 279,000). Interest receivable fell to Pounds 83,000 (Pounds 123,000). Earnings per share rose to 4.7p (2.11p) and the interim dividend is lifted from 0.8p to 1p.
