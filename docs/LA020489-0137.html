February 4, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BUYING FIREWOOD -- A CONSUMER GUIDE

There are a number of Orange County businesses that sell firewood in quantity. For some, selling wood is the main order of business; for others, including some tree services, it is a sideline. Don Sayles, owner of one of the county's largest firewood sellers, Woodshed Firewood in Orange, has some tips for buyers: Make sure the wood is already seasoned, a process that takes months. Ask the seller exactly how much wood is in a cord; some businesses, Sayles said, sell less than the standard 128 cubic feet (a stack of wood 4 feet by 4 feet by 8 feet).

Softwoods, he said, light easier than hardwoods but do not burn as long; Sayles recommends getting a mixture, with softwoods for starting the fire and hardwoods for sustaining it.
