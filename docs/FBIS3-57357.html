Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] Vice President Yerik Asanbayev received the ambassador of the Republic of Korea to Kazakhstan at his request today. Under examination were prospects for furthering economic and cultural relations between our countries. Bilateral relations between the two countries are very effective, said Yerik Asanbayev. South Korean businessmen have began the production of TV sets in Almaty and fridges in Karaganda. However, cooperation has to be boosted especially in the sphere of granting Kazakhstan credits. He [Asanbayev] expressed certainty that the South Korean side, which has promised to grant Kazakhstan with a credit of $350 million, will keep its word. The ambassador was thanked for pledging $150,000 in humanitarian aid that arrived from Seoul today. The ambassador said that he will do everything so as to strengthen friendly ties between the two countries, facilitating economic ties, and attracting more investments from South Korea to Kazakhstan.
