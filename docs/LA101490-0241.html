October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Campaign funds -- A headline in Thursday's San Gabriel Valley section indicated that Diamond Bar City Councilman Paul Horcher, the Republican nominee in the 52nd Assembly District, had raised substantially more campaign funds than his Democratic opponent because of loans he had made to himself. Actually, Horcher raised $104,998 without the loans, and his Democratic opponent, Gary Neely, raised $22,586.

Also, the article should have stated that Horcher is paying consultant Harvey Englander $15,000 for his work since the June 5 primary.
