920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Nadir investment concern wound up SOUTH AUDLEY Management, the private investment vehicle owned by family trusts of Mr Asil Nadir, chairman of Polly Peck International, was compulsorily wound up in the High Court in London yesterday. The order was made on a petition presented by Kansallis-Osake-Pankki, a trade creditor for Pounds 300,000.
