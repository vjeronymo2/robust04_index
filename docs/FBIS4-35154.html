BFN [Report by Luiz Augusto Mendonca in Brasilia] [Text] The minimum wage will be $70 on 1 September. President Itamar Franco made this decision following a meeting with the finance, labor, and social security ministers, and the federal administration secretary. Labor Minister Marcelo Pimentel later said that by the end of October the government will send Congress a draft bill outlining a new minimum wage policy. President Itamar said military and public servants will receive a salary adjustment soon after the implementation of the real. The president said the date for the wage increase has yet to be established, but it will be implemented even if he has to fight with his economic ministers.
