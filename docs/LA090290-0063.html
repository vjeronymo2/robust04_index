September 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

SURELOCK lost $230 Saturday when Meadow's Interco finished fifth in the fifth race.

However, the loss didn't short circuit the computer's electronic enthusiasm for today's card, which features a turf triple header.

SURELOCK's bets are $20 to win and place on Freya Stark in the third race, $40 to win and place on Final Forecast in the fifth race and $100 to win and place on Classic Fame in the simulcast Arlington Million.

SURELOCK's Sat. Bankroll: $3,599

Saturday's Loss: $230

Current Bankroll: $3,369

Janice Goldinger lost $90 when Flying Elsie came to earth in the second race and her $50 exacta of Fantastic Look to Bayakoa finished in the opposite order in the eighth.

Janice will join SURELOCK on the turf for her bet today, a $25 exacta box of Final Forecast-Gift of Gold-Star Day.

Janice's Fri. Bankroll: $1,416.75

Saturday's Loss: $90

Current Bankroll: $1,326.75

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.
