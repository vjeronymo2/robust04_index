910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / International Capital Markets: New M&A chief at Credit Lyonnais CREDIT Lyonnais has appointed a new head of cross-border merger and acquisition activity, writes David Lascelles, Banking Editor. He is Mr Jean-Luc Biamonti. Mr Biamonti was previously responsible for European development at Wasserstein Perella, the US corporate finance house. Before that he worked on international M&A at First Boston, the Wall Street firm whose executives founded Wasserstein Perella.
