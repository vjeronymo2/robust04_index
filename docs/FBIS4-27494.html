BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 30 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists staged an air war exercise, deploying scores of overseas-based nuclear-capable F-16 and F-15 fighter-bombers, assault planes, pursuit-assault planes, VTOL [vertical takeoff and landing] assault planes and anti-submarine planes in South Korea on May 27 and 28, military sources said. This war gamble was part of their preparations for a war of aggression to stifle the DPRK by force of arms.
