Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 18 (YONHAP) -- A public funeral (sahoejang) will be accorded to the late Chong Il-kwon, one-time prime minister and National Assembly speaker, at the National Assembly Plaza on Yoi-do on the morning of Jan. 22. His remains will be flown home on Wednesday afternoon aboard a Korean Air plane and will be laid in state at the Seoul Chung-ang hospital at Pungnap-tong. The remains will be buried in the generals' grave yard in the national cemetery at Tongjak-tong in southern Seoul. Chong died of a lymph cancer at a Hawaii hospital late Monday.
