September 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Guard Alvin Robertson of the Milwaukee Bucks was ordered by a San Antonio judge to attend a program on spousal abuse and to perform 50 hours of community service in connection with a misdemeanor assault charge brought against him by his wife.

Robertson, 28, was charged after he allegedly grabbed the hand of his wife, Jacquelin, and struck it against a concrete floor last month.
