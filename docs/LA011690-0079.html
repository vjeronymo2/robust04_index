January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DISCONNECTED: The major disruption of AT&T long-distance service Monday (A3) caught many companies off guard, but not Amplicon Inc. of Santa Ana, a computer-leasing firm that routes long distance calls through two other carriers. . . . A loss of phone service "would have shut down 85% of all of my business," said the company's Randy Murphy. Steve Emmons and Mark I. Pinsky
