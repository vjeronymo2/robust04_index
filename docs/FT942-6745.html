940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: William Cook William Cook, the Sheffield-based steel castings maker, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 6.71m for the year to March 26 against restated losses of Pounds 4.04m. Turnover of continuing operations rose from Pounds 95.9m to Pounds 98.7m. Earnings per share came through at 20.73p (24.36p) and a final dividend of 5p (3.5p) makes a 7.5p (5p) total.
