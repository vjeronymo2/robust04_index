940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / Water cost found to be sixth-highest The cost of water in the UK is the sixth-highest among leading industrial countries, a new survey by National Utility Services has found. British consumers pay 58.67p per cubic metre compared with 109.93p in Germany, the most expensive country surveyed. The UK's water price increase in the year to July of 4.7 per cent was the fourth largest after Germany (8.5 per cent), the Republic of Ireland (6.7 per cent) and the Netherlands (5.8 per cent).
