January 19, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ABORTION: Activists on both sides of the abortion issue will mark Monday's anniversary of the Supreme Court's Roe vs. Wade decision in the customary fashion. . . . Anti-abortion demonstrators plan a "Rescue Day" Saturday at area clinics, while pro-choice supporters expect to trail them from their rallying point in Anaheim and confront them. . . . Few minds are changed at such meetings, says Operation Rescue's Sue Finn: "We see a lot of familiar faces." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
