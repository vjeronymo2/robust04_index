910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Appointments: Finance director for Medway Ports MEDWAY PORTS AUTHORITY has appointed Ms Philippa Gilham (pictured) as director of finance - a new post created in anticipation of privatisation. She was financial controller and southern director with the EPS Group. ***** CAWOOD, SMITHIE & CO, stockbrokers, has appointed as partners Mr Ashley Alger, Mr Richard Whiteley, Mr John Newsome, Mr Bill Sutton, Mr Carl Bushby, and Mr Robert Ash. ***** Following the move of MATTHEW CLARK's head office from London to Guildford, Mr Sam Gordon Clark, director fine wines, has resigned to pursue other interests in the wine trade. ***** Mr James Wheeler has been appointed managing director of HEWITSON-WALKER, subsidiary of Nestor-BNA. He replaces founder-director Mr John Walker who has retired to pursue personal interests. ***** Joining the board of NEW SCOTLAND INSURANCE GROUP, holding company of Independent Insurance, are: Mr Philip Condon, Mr Robert McCracken and Mr Alan Clarke - all are directors and assistant general managers of Independent Insurance. Mr Alexander Rankin Muir (pictured) has been appointed chairman of New Scotland Investments, a newly-formed subsidiary. He is also a director of Swinton CIB, Sceptre Insurance Services, and Cattle's Holdings.
