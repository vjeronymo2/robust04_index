940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Merivale Moore Pounds 12.4m purchase Merivale Moore, the property investment company, has acquired 16 commercial properties from Sun Life Assurance for Pounds 12.4m cash. The company said that the portfolio had an initial yield of 11.5 per cent and was split between office and industrial investments. The purchase follows Merivale's acquisition in July of a Pounds 6m portfolio from Warner Estate Holdings, which took a 20.5 per cent stake in Merivale in part payment.
