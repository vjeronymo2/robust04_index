BFN [Text] Banja Luka, 28 Jun (TANJUG) -- On several occasions today, Muslim forces opened heavy artillery fire from 122-mm howitzers on Serb settlements on Mount Vlasic, killing six persons, the Information Service of the First Krajina Corps of the army of the Serb Republic reported in Banja Luka tonight. In addition to these casualties another 20 people were wounded and a great deal of material damage was also inflicted. According to military sources, about 30 shells were fired. Units of the Serb Republic army were forced to return heavy fire in reply to these activities. There has also been sporadic fire in the Srbobran [Gornji Vakuf] battlefield from the direction of Prusac. There were no casualties in these operations. There was no return of fire to these activities.
