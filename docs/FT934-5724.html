931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Air France losses Air France, the state-owned airline, will this year lose FFr7.5bn (Pounds 857m) and see a further decline in its world market share, Mr Bernard Bosson, the transport minister, said at the weekend, writes David Buchan in Paris. With estimates of its 1993 losses steadily worsening from the FFr3.5bn deficit forecast in April, Mr Bosson told the Senate 'this shows how quickly the company has been sinking'.
