BFN [Text] The Sudanese ambassador to Somalia, 'Abd-al-Baqi Muhammad Hasan Ahmad, who was abducted on 19 April by an armed group belonging to the Hawadle clan, was released today in Mogadishu. Reports confirmed that the ambassador was in good health. The ambassador, in a statement to a SUDAN NEWS AGENCY correspondent, expressed his thanks and appreciation to all those concerned with his case throughout his abduction. He stated that he would soon leave Mogadishu for Khartoum. Sudan's ambassador to Somalia is the only Arab ambassador who has been present in Mogadishu throughout the developments in Somalia.
