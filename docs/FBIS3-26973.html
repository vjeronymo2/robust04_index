Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, February 16 (XINHUA) -- Tajikistan has expressed strong opposition to any use of force in Bosnia. The ITAR-TASS NEWS SERVICE quoted Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Alimov as saying today that any "means of violence" to be used to solve the conflict in former Yugoslavia is doomed to fail. "Violence will be answered by violence," he said, adding that no side involved in the Bosnian conflict should cross the "risk line" any more.
