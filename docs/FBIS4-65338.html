BFN [Report signed "jan": "Interest Rates Are Going Down: National Bank Cuts Key Rates"] [Excerpt] The downward trend of interest rates is continuing: As of next Friday [13 May], the Austrian National Bank will cut both the discount rate and the Lombard rate by 0.25 percentage points to 5.5 and 4.5 percent respectively. The interest rate that is decisive for open market transactions (Gomex) will be reduced from 5.2 to 5.1 percent. [passage omitted]
