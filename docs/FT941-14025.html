940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / Holiday price war set to end today The holiday price war is due to end today when Lunn Poly, the largest travel retail chain, ends its offer of 11 per cent discounts on summer bookings. Going Places, the second largest chain, ended its 11 per cent discounts last Saturday. Thomas Cook, the third biggest chain, had been promising customers that it would match Lunn Poly's prices.
