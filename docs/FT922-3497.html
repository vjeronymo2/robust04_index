920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Moorgate Smaller net assets up 8.9% Moorgate Smaller Companies Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 112.03p as at April 30 1992, a rise of 8.9 per cent since its inception in May last year. Net revenue for the period amounted to Pounds 2.34m for earnings of 4.83p per share. A proposed final dividend of 2.325p brings the total to the anticipated 4.125p.
