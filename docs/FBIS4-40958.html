BFN [Unattributed report: "Price Rises Continue To Slow"] [Text] The Russian State Committee for Statistics reports that the increase in prices of consumer goods and services during the period from 8 through 14 June was the smallest since the beginning of the year: 0.8 percent. During that week food prices rose by 0.7 percent, nonfood prices rose by 0.9 percent, and prices of consumer services rose by one percent. The cost of the basket of 19 basic foodstuffs was 56,000 rubles [R] for one month's consumption. The maximum cost of the basket was R150,400 in Magadan, and the minimum was R35,800 in Ulyanovsk. Since the beginning of the year the cost of individual types of housing and municipal services has increased six to eight times, the cost of travel on trains and city buses has increased four to five times, the cost of places in children's preschool establishments has trebled, and it is now twice as expensive to stay in sanatoriums and recreation centers.
