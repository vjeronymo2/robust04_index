Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 3 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor Li Tieying met with Sir Evelyn de Rothschild, chairman of the Rothschild & Sons Limited of Britain, here this afternoon. Li, also minister in charge of the State Commission for Economic Restructuring, exchanged views with Rothschild on topics of common interest. Rothschild arrived here this morning as the commission's guest.
