BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] has assured all public servants that their future is safe after the elections. In a statement the TEC asks public servants to give their full support to assure good administration and security during the elections. They are not to take part in disruptive activities such as strikes. The statement referred public servants to the provisions of the new constitution that provides for any person employed in any government department on 27 April to be retained in service after elections.
