940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: European sugar cut predicted European beet sugar production would fall to 29.18m tonnes in 1994-95 from 31.91m in 1993-94, German statistical agency FO Licht forecast yesterday, reports Reuters from Ratzeburg. Western Europe would produce 19.24m tonnes, down from 21.02m, and eastern Europe 9.94m tonnes, against 10.89m.
