BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 25 (KCNA) -- The Seoul Subway Workers Union entered a general strike at 4 on the morning of June 24, according to radio reports from Seoul. Though the trade union had final negotiations with the company on their wages, the latter persistently refused to accept its demand. The unionists are now on a sit-in strike at Korea University, the Myongdong Cathedral in Seoul and two other places of the city. The sympathy strike of subway workers in Seoul and Pusan in the wake of the start of the general strike by locomotive engineers throughout South Korea will tie up the traffic at the worst, said the reports.
