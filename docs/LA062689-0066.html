June 26, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: EXIT LINES

Actress Sally Kirkland revealed an interesting bit of personal trivia Friday on the "Today" show. She got her first kiss from Ted Koppel, who took her to her high school prom. Said Kirkland: "Ted, wherever you are, I want to be on 'Nightline.' Let's talk about old times." Claudia Puig
