930406 FT 06 APR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good but selective landed demand reports the Tea Brokers Association. The few brightest East African teas sold well at firm to dearer rates. All other descriptions were irregularly 5 to 10p lower. Offshore fairly widespread demand although prices were 3 to 5p easier. The highest price realised this week was 181p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 155p, nom good medium 125p, medium 120p low medium 92p.
