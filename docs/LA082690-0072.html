August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

USING THE U.N. TO SETTLE MIDEAST CRISIS

Why is it every time we have a crisis we are told to send our sons and daughters in harm's way, and yet others are allowed to profit from it?

I am a stockholder in an oil company, but I do not want to make profit on someone's blood.

A freeze should be put on everything until it's over; otherwise we are saying, use our children for cannon fodder for profit. I agree with President Bush's actions in the Persian Gulf, but not his follow-through.

BRUNO BIAVA

Dana Point
