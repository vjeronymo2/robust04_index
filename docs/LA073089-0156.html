July 30, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GOOD SAMARITAN

My deepest gratitude to the good Samaritan, who, early July 11th, observed my plight when I broke my ankle at the entrance to Crown Valley Community Park. Thanks to your prompt action in calling the paramedics, I had help within 12 minutes of my fall.

EVA M. HOLEMAN

Laguna Niguel
