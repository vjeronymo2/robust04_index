931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Harmony Leisure placing and purchase Harmony Leisure, the USM-quoted operator of restaurants, pubs and hotels, has agreed to acquire certain properties from British Land subsidiaries for an aggregate consideration of Pounds 5.35m. Harmony is also proposing to raise Pounds 5.8m net by way of a placing of 66m shares at 9 1/4 p each underwritten by Guinness Mahon. The group also proposes a change of name to Harmony Property Group. An extraordinary meeting has been called for December 6.
