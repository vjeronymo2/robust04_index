940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Burnfield tumbles to Pounds 0.78m Burnfield incurred a loss in the second half and for the whole of 1993 suffered a profits fall from Pounds 2.68m to Pounds 776,000 pre-tax. The total dividend year is being cut to 2.5p (5.75p) via a reduced final of 0.85p. Earnings per share were 1.4p (6.5p). The second half deficit of Pounds 493,000 compared with previous profits of Pounds 1.57m. The directors said, however, that 1994 had started well and that actions taken would bring an early return to earnings and dividend growth. The shares rose 5p to 77p. Turnover rose from Pounds 29.82m to Pounds 38.53m. The West Midlands-based group manufactures industrial controls and measurement equipment. Restructuring costs of Pounds 1m at the Isopad and Budenberg offshoots were charged against profits as were aborted acquisition costs and head office restructuring expenses of Pounds 486,000.
