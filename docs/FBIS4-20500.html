Certified 94P60138A Beijing ZHONGGUO KEXUE BAO [CHINESE SCIENCE NEWS] in Chinese 12 Jan 94 p 2 94P60138A Beijing ZHONGGUO KEXUE BAO [CHINESE SCIENCE NEWS] in Chinese 12 Jan 94 p 2 Chinese CSO [Article by Lu Jingben [4151 2417 3962] and Guan Jiabin [7070 1367 2430]: ``Superconducting Josephson-Junction-Array 1-Volt Voltage Standard Certified''] [Summary] The ``superconducting Josephson-junction-array 1-volt voltage standard'' jointly developed by the State Bureau of Technology Supervision, the China Metrology Research Institute, and the China Testing Technology Research Institute recently passed formal appraisal in Beijing. The new standard has a measurement uncertainty of a few parts per billion, meeting 1990s international levels for a quantum voltage standard.
