931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Venezuela Venezuelan equities regained positive territory after a steep fall on Tuesday following the election of a new government at the weekend. The Merinvest composite index closed up 1 per cent at 112.85. On Sunday, the country voted in the administration of Mr Rafael Caldera, which may revise the free trade policy of former President Carlos Andres Perez.
