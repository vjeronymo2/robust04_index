921222 FT 22 DEC 92 / Italian inflation still falling Italy's rate of inflation is still falling, despite the devaluation of the lira in September, writes Robert Graham in Rome. According to preliminary figures for December based on evidence from the country's main cities, inflation is running at an annual rate of 4.7 per cent, the lowest level since October 1988. Prices this month have risen 0.1 per cent, forcing the annual average down from November's 4.9 per cent.
