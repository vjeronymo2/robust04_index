930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Princedale to acquire Hillsdown companies for maximum Pounds 9.2m PRINCEDALE Group, the USM-quoted marketing and design consultancy, plans to form a new industrial division through the acquisition of Colloids, Hallam Plastics and Hallmark Contract Hire from Hillsdown Holdings and other minority holders. The initial cost is Pounds 7.65m, which may increase to a maximum Pounds 9.15m. At the same time, Princedale announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 71,000 (Pounds 326,000 profits) for the six months to March 31. Turnover fell to Pounds 4.47m (Pounds 6.37m) and there were losses per share of 0.01p (0.06p earnings). The acquisitions are being funded as to Pounds 3.49m by the issue to the vendors of new consolidated ordinary shares at 25p per share, Pounds 3.45m by the issue of 7 per cent fixed secured loan notes, and Pounds 700,000 in cash. A placing and open offer of 16m new consolidated ordinary shares, underwritten by Guinness Mahon, will raise about Pounds 4m, of which Pounds 1.6m is for Princedale. Princedale's existing ordinary shares will be consolidated on the basis of one consolidated ordinary share for every 20 existing ordinary shares. Shareholders may apply under the open offer on the basis of five offer shares for every eight consolidated ordinary shares resulting from the capital reorganisation. Princedale's current account period will run for 15 months.
