November 26, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: YOUTH, 17, HITS CAR; ELDERLY WOMAN DIES

A 68-year-old Lancaster woman died Saturday after her car was hit while she attempted a U-turn on L Street just west of the Sierra Highway in the Antelope Valley, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department said.

Her car was hit on the driver's side by a vehicle driven by a 17-year-old Lancaster youth. The victim's name was withheld pending notification of relatives, authorities said.

The woman was taken to Antelope Valley Medical Center where she was pronounced dead from internal injuries, authorities said.
