BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow, 23 May -- Acting Finance Minister Sergey Dubinin has received a reprimand from the Russian president for failing to carry out the head of state's instructions. He failed to provide financing to scientific and educational institutions on time. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from Sergey Filatov, head of the presidential administration, today.
