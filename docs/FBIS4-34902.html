BFN [From the "AM/PM" newscast] [Text] The Army captured (Carlos Arturo Velandia Jagua) in Santa Fe de Bogota on 25 June. He is considered the fourth in command within the Army of National Liberation, ELN. The guerrilla was at a downtown cafeteria extorting a citizen. Military intelligence files show that he was responsible for a dynamite attack on Finance Minister Rudolf Hommes and was involved in operations in which 56 soldiers and policemen died. He is considered to be an expert booby trap manufacturer due to training he received in Vietnam.
