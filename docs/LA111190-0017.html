November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LOWDOWN ON MOMA

I saw the "High & Low" exhibition on Oct. 13, and contrary to Knight's declaration that the exhibit is a "flop," I thoroughly enjoyed it, as did the huge crowd.

But, then, we only represent about 99% of the people. We are not educated or sophisticated in art history or theory.

What a treat it was to see Warhol's Campbell Soup series, Lichtenstein's vibrant "As I Opened Fire," Picasso's "Gertrude Stein" and Jenny Holzer's dramatic LED signs and tiles.

I have been to the major museums around the world. Although I am not artistically knowledgeable by Mr. Knight's definition, I know what I like, and you know what? That's all that matters.

MARLENE G. STEIN

Glendale
