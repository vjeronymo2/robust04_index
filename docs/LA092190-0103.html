September 21, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

FOOTBALL GAME RESCHEDULED

The high school football game between Beverly Hills and Harvard at Harvard has been rescheduled for 3:30 p.m. Saturday in observance of Rosh Hashanah.

Harvard Coach Gary Thran, who rescheduled the game, attempted to make the contest a night game on Saturday but was unable to locate an available field at short notice.

"Neither one of us have (lighted fields)," Thran said. "We also have a problem with the game being on Saturday because it's open house at Harvard. Our campus is really going to be crowded."
