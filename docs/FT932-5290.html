930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / Observer: Invisible man Hans van den Broek, the EC commissioner for external political relations, failed to show much sign of the Community's much-vaunted transparency yesterday. He turned up at the Commission's offices in London in the back of a large limo with heavily blacked-out windows. When questioned about his lack of visibility, the ever-cheerful Van den Broek quipped that this was just the car he used for shopping.
