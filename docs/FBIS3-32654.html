Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The new scenarios encouraged by some sectors of national life are simply exacerbating the crisis. This statement was made by Christian Democratic Assembly [RDC] leader Eddy Volel. The RDC leader takes this opportunity to denounce the behavior of Ambassador William Lacy Swing who is pressuring Aristide to choose a new prime minister. [Begin recording, in Creole] Given the way the nation is going and acting, the reactionaries are trying to cause trouble for Aristide so he cannot appoint... [pauses] so he cannot have a third prime minister ratified. Thus, these criminals are trying either to dismiss Aristide as president or stall long enough for Aristide's mandate to expire. This crisis has caused the Haitian people to lose confidence in numerous entities: politicians, friendly countries, the United Nations, the OAS, the international community, deputies and senators, and businessmen. What is the U.S. ambassador to Haiti doing? Why is William Lacy Swing so interested in the matter of appointing a Haitian prime minister in violation of Article 1 of our Constitution? [end recording]
