930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / Perez threatens to resign Venezuela's president, Mr Carlos Andres Perez, has said he will resign if the country's Supreme Court rules that he should be impeached for allegedly mishandling Dollars 17m in government funds, writes Joseph Mann in Caracas. The president's decision came after an unfavourable opinion on his case written by the chief justice of the Supreme Court was leaked to the press on May 5.
