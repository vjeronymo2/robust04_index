January 9, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

SEX SHOW COSTS HOTEL'S KOSHER LICENSE

The chief rabbi of Tiberias has revoked the kosher food license of a hotel for allowing a naked couple to have sex in a helicopter hovering above its pool during a New Year's party, the Jerusalem Post said Sunday.

The newspaper said the 20-minute live aerial sex act, the main attraction of the sold-out party at the Tiberias Club Hotel, was watched by guests from their bedrooms.

Chief Rabbi David Peretz said the hotel had sinned and did not deserve a license to serve kosher food.

"The directors of the hotel are completely nuts," Tiberias Mayor Yigal Bibi was quoted by the newspaper as saying.
