October 19, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OXNARD;

BOY SHOT IN FIGHT, NOT IN DRIVE-BY

An investigation into the reported drive-by shooting of a 15-year-old Wednesday has revealed that the shooting actually occurred during a fight at the same location, Oxnard police said.

The victim, whose name was not released, was struck in the arm by a bullet. He reported that he did not know the assailants. The police have no suspects and are still investigating the case. GERRY BRAILO SPENCER
