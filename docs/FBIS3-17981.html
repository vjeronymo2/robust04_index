Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Crimean Supreme Soviet Chairman Mykola Bahrov met today with U.S. Ambassador to Ukraine William Miller. The Crimean legislative speaker briefed the U.S. diplomat on results of the elections and the referendum conducted on the peninsula. Also discussed were prospects for economic cooperation, in particular, the possibility for investments in the Crimean economy. Ambassador Miller is also scheduled to meet with Crimean Deputy Prime Minister Yevhen Saburov and Black Sea Fleet Commander Eduard Baltin.
