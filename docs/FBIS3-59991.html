Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: ``Shabqadar Police Recover 13 Kilograms of Heroin''] [Text] Peshawar (MASHRIQ News) -- The Shabqadar police have recovered 13 kilograms of heroin from an unnamed person; however, he succeeded in running away. According to details, the S.H.O. [Station House Officer] of the Shabqadar police station was making his rounds with his assistants. They saw a man walking with a box in his hand. When he saw the police, he threw the box and ran away. When the box was searched and 13 kilograms of heroin were recovered.
