September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RAIDERS' PERFORMANCE A SAD SHOW OF SATIRE

On the TV screen and in theaters, I have seen put-downs and parodies of big business, medicine, government, the military and grand opera.

But, unless I miss my guess, last Sunday when I watched the L.A. Raiders play the Denver Broncos, I saw the Raiders satirizing the NFL game.

STAN GORDON, Encino
