June 18, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

SMALL QUAKE SHAKES SAN BERNARDINO AREA

A 3.7-magnitude earthquake rattled the San Bernardino-Riverside area late Saturday night, but there were no reports of injuries, authorities said.

The 11:08 p.m. temblor was centered six miles southeast of San Bernardino, said Doug Smith, spokesman for the Caltech Seismology Laboratory in Pasadena.
