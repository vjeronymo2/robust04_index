BFN [From Tokyo NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 14 Jun Morning Edition] [Text] According to a report by delegation members, a group of Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ] members met Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [CCWPK] and concurrently chairman of the Committee on Foreign Affairs, at the Mansudae Assembly Hall on the morning of 13 June. The SDPJ delegation, led by Kazo Watanabe, director of the general affairs department of the SDPJ Central Executive Committee, is currently visiting the DPRK. Watanabe and other delegation members stressed that the issue of North Korea's suspected development of nuclear weapons should be resolved through dialogue. In response, Hwang reiterated North Korea's stand, saying: "We do not plan to possess nuclear weapons. We do not have the technology nor the funds necessary to develop such weapons."
