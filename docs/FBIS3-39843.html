Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Trucks carrying food and medicine to Skopje proceed through the port of Thessaloniki or northern Greece's customs checkpoints without problems. However, about 50 trucks moving to and from Skopje remain stranded at Evzonoi customs checkpoint because the term "Republic of Macedonia" [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] is written on the cargo's accompanying invoices as their place of origin or destination.
