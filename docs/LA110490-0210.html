November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: $205,195 GRANT TO FUND VIDEO ON DRUG ABUSE

Charles Frost, chairman of the social work department at Cal Poly Pomona, has been awarded a $205,195 grant by the U.S. Department of Education to produce an educational video on drug abuse.

The video will be broadcast to 30 high schools over PolyNet, the college's cable broadcasting network.
