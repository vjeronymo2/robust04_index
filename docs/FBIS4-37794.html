BFN [Text] Tehran, June 23, IRNA -- A scheduled visit to Baghdad this Saturday [25 June] of an Iranian foreign ministry delegation has been postponed without indefinitely. [sentence as received] The postponement, according to a foreign ministry spokesman here tonight, is due to the terrorist bomb blast at the holy shrine of Imam Reza (as) in Mashhad on Monday last that took the lives of 26 mourners on the day of Ashura (martyrdom anniversary of Imam Hussein) and injured about 170 others. The terrorist Mujahidden Khalq outfit (MKO) has been held responsible for the blast. The visit to Baghdad was within the framework of pending matters between Tehran and Baghdad.
