BFN [Report by Zeynel Lule: "A PKK Office in Spain"] [Excerpt] Strasbourg--The Workers Party of Kurdistan [PKK] has opened an office in Madrid under the guise of a Kurdish Information Center. The opening ceremony on 27 April was attended by many members of parliament and several representatives of ERNK [Kurdish People's Liberation Front], which is the PKK's armed wing. The daily EL PAIS has reported that Faruk Dogru, a representative of ERNK, addressed the ceremony and urged the Spanish Government to direct pressure on Turkey. He also said that Spain's support would be sought in the struggle through the center in Madrid and initiatives launched with the Spanish Government for a military embargo against Turkey.
