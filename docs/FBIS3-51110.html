Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The Awakening" newscast] [Text] Military spokesmen in Managua who asked not to be identified said on 11 January that a group of 300 Nicaraguans are being trained in Honduran territory by U.S. instructors. The soldiers, who demanded that their names not be revealed, said the rebels, presumably anti-Sandinists, are being trained by two U.S. instructors. According to the sources, the rebels receiving military training are based in Yamales, near the Nicaraguan-Honduran border. They added that the rebels receive supplies every two days in a helicopter identified as an HLH [expansion unknown].
