BFN [Excerpt] Tallinn, June 27, BNS -- Estonian President Lennart Meri today approved the nominations of Andres Lipstok as finance minister and Peeter Olesk as the minister of culture and education. But the president still left it open whether or not he will approve of the prime minister's nomination of Enn Tupp for the post of the defense minister. Officials at the presidential chancellery were unable to say why the president had postponed his decision about Tupp's nomination. According to the Estonian laws, the president must announce his decision Tuesday at the latest. [passage omitted]
