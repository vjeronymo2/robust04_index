Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, mojahedin carried out a rocket attack on Brigade Headquarters at Kashmir Ghati in Punch area, causing heavy losses to the Indian forces. Reports say mojahedin also attacked military targets in various localities of Srinagar. Two Indian soldiers were killed in a grenade attack in Bata Kadal. Meanwhile, another 16 Kashmiris were martyred in clashes and due to indiscriminate firing by Indian troops. A shootout between mojahedin and the Indian troops was reported from Wirk in Doda area. Mojahedin claimed that a number of Indian troops were killed and wounded in the clash.
