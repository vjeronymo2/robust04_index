BFN [Article by "SD-r": "We Are Ready To Negotiate"] [Text] Bratislava--On 27 April 1994 the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Slovak Republic handed a note to the Hungarian side, through the Embassy of the Hungarian Republic in Bratislava, in reply to the Hungarian Republic's note of 14 April 1994. The note reaffirms the Slovak Republic's readiness to negotiate with the Hungarian side on establishing a temporary water regime on the Danube. The Ministry stresses that the talks should start as soon as possible and that no preliminary conditions should be set before them.
