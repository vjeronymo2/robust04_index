930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / Argentina and Brazil say they support Japan's UN bid Foreign ministers of Argentina and Brazil said yesterday they supported Japan's bid for a permanent seat on the United Nations Security Council as part of overall reform of the council, AP reports from Tokyo. 'We told Japan that our countries support its membership. . ,' said Mr Fernando Henrique Cardoso, Brazil's foreign minister. 'We also believe it is important to include representation from developing countries, including Latin America.'
