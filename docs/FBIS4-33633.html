BFN [Report by Aide Cedeno in Michoacan from the "Panorama Newscast" -- live] [Text] Federal deputy candidate Margarito Manrique Saucedo, of the Cardenist Front for National Reconstruction Party [PFCRN] in Tacambaro district, died in a traffic accident earlier today, just 72 days before the federal election, announced Luis Martinez Cetina, president of the PFCRN National Executive Committee, charging Manrique, 36, had been murdered, as everything indicated he had been intentionally run down. Luis Martinez said the alleged accident occurred in (Huatano), Michoacan, where the deceased was campaigning. As the candidate crossed a road, an unidentified pickup truck ran him down, killing him. Luis Martinez announced his party expects a thorough investigation of the facts and will demand justice. So far, no one has been identified as the alleged perpetrator or perpetrators of this crime, which occurred just hours ago.
