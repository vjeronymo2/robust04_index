920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / World News in Brief: PM's island choice Prime minister John Major, was guest on the 50th anniversary programme of the Desert Island Discs radio programme. Among the records he chose were Gershwin's Rhapsody in Blue, The Happening by Diana Ross and The Supremes and a John Arlott cricket commentary. His 'luxury' request was for a full-size replica of The Oval cricket ground.
