Allocation of NDA Act Power Western will use the following procedures to allocate NDA Act Power to a qualified applicant: (i) Western will determine whether sufficient Unutilized NDA Act Power is available to fulfill the applicant's request. (ii) If sufficient Unutilized NDA Act Power is available to fulfill the applicant's request, Western shall allocate the amount needed. (iii) If sufficient Unutilized NDA Act Power is not available to fulfill the applicant's request, Western shall allocate all Unutilized NDA Act Power and recall and reallocate NDA Act Power CROD to fulfill the request by following the reallocation procedures stated in G.3. below.
