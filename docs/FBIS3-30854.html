Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Feb 8 (CTK) -- Czech nationals applying for visa to the Yugoslav Federal Republic (Serbia and Montenegro) must present a valid passport, a filled-in form, and two photographs, Zita Senkova, a Foreign Ministry press official, told CTK today. The applicant must also submit a verified invitation letter and, in case of a tourist travel, a voucher. In case of a transit tour, the visa of the country of destination along with air or railway tickets must be shown, Senkova reported. The fee for one entrance or one-way transit is $20. Two entrances or a double transit cost $30. Several trips or a multiple transit for less than three months costs $50 and under six months $90, Senkova said, adding that the fees are paid exclusively in dollars. The regulation is in force starting today.
