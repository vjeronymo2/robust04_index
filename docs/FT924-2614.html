921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / UK Company News: LMS almost unchanged at Pounds 11.26m Pre-tax profits at London Merchant Securities, the property and investment company, were almost unchanged at Pounds 11.26m in the six months to end-September. This was in spite of a 10 per cent increased in net rental income from Pounds 13.7m to Pounds 15m. London Merchant's earnings per share fell from 2.85p to 2.68p and the company declared an unchanged interim distribution of 0.8p. During the period, First Leisure, the UK's largest leisure group, added only Pounds 1.8m to London Merchant's profits after the reduction of the company's stake to 15.2 per cent since April 1991. Lord Rayne, chairman, said that in spite of the fall in central London office values there were encouraging signs of increasing interest in the group's recently completed buildings.
