Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federation Council, the upper house of the Russian parliament, on Monday elected retired general Petr Shirshov, 48, Chairman of the parliamentary Defense and Security Committee. Vasily Tarasenko, a 43-year-old lawyer from Samara, was put at the head of the Committee for Federation Affairs. The election of a third deputy chairman of the Federation Council was postponed until the end of February, Federation Council Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko said. The house will meet again at 16:00 on Tuesday to elect chairpersons for the Budget and Finance Committee and the Committee for Agrarian Policy. The Federation Council also decided to issue a resolution approving the presidential decree extending the duration of the state of emergency in parts of North Ossetia and Ingushetia.
