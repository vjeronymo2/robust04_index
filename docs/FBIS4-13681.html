BFN [Text] Today in his Prime Ministry office, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received Iraqi Minister of Awqaf 'Abd-al-Mun'im Salih. The prime minister also received Ghalib al-Qirshi, Yemeni minister of awqaf and guidance. The two meetings were attended by Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-'Abbadi, minister of awqaf and Islamic affairs. During the two meetings views were exchanged on various issues of concern to Arab and Islamic countries. The Iraqi Awqaf minister expressed his thanks to the king, people and Government of Jordan for their pan-Arab stands and their support for various Arab issues that reinforce Arab solidarity. He said the Arab and Islamic delegations were eager to take part in the Kingdom's celebrations of the third Hashemite-sponsored renovation of the holy Dome of the Rock. He said the celebration has a deep meaning in terms of everyone's concern about the Arabism of Jerusalem. This is important to all. Jordan had the privilege of renovating it, despite the difficult circumstances it is facing. He said the issue of Jerusalem should unite Arabs and Muslims during the current circumstances facing the nation. He noted the importance of holding meetings among Arabs and Muslims in order to strengthen ties and prevent differences. For his part, the Yemeni minister made statements to the Jordanian News Agency PETRA noting the importance of exchanging views and ideas among the brothers to reach positive results that should unite the nation's ranks.
