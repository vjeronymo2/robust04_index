BFN [Text] Seoul, June 22 (YONHAP) -- The 169th extraordinary session of the National Assembly is set to open on June 25 for a 20- day run through July 14. The National Assembly speaker announced the convocation of the new assembly session Wednesday afternoon immediately after the floor leaders of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party [DLP] and opposition Democratic Party [DP] agreed on the new sitting. Yi Han-tong of the DLP and Sin Ki-ha of the DP agreed that the session would act on an amendment to the National Assembly law on June 25 and select the new assembly speaker, vice speakers and committee chairmen on June 28 before it would go into committee-level activities. The floor leaders also agreed to create an ad hoc committee on woman affairs at the assembly.
