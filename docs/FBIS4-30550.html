BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 27 (KCNA) -- A solidarity meeting supporting the just cause of the Korean people was held in Kathmandu on June 18. Chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party Narayan Man Bijukchhe declared at the meeting: "We will extend invariable support to the government and the people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in all their efforts for the peace and security of the Korean peninsula and its denuclearisation." Manik Lal Shrestha, chairman of the Nepal-Korea Friendship Association, noted that the "resolution" adopted at the board of governors of the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] is a prelude to the "sanctions" of the United Nations against the DPRK and virtually a declaration of war. He fully supported the Korean people who gave a decisive answer to the unjustifiable "step" of the IAEA. Member of parliament Shibash Nembag, said: "It is our duty, morality and conscience to extend militant support and solidarity to the fraternal Korean people. The Korean people and the world's peaceloving people will surely frustrate the moves of the imperialists and defend their just cause". A letter to the great leader President Kim Il-song was adopted at the meeting.
