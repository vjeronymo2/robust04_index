BFN [Excerpt] Santa Fe de Bogota, 28 Jun (EFE) -- According to SEMANA magazine, Gilberto Rodriguez Orejuela, the paramount leader of the "Cali Cartel," telephoned a subdirector of EL TIEMPO daily to swear to him on lives of his children that he did not make any contributions to any of the presidential candidates. The magazine assures that last Thursday [23 June] "amid the scandal caused by the alleged injection of hot money into the electoral contest," Rodrigo Orejuela called reporter Enrique Santos Castillo to explicitly tell him that his organization did not make any contributions to any of the candidates. The scandal, which resulted from the disclosure of audiocassette recordings of conversations between reporter Alberto Giraldo and the brothers Gilberto and Miguel Rodriguez Orejuela, has tainted Liberal candidate Ernesto Samper's 19 June election victory.
