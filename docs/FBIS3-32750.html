Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Azuzena Lievano from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] After a nine-hour session, the State Council issued a six-line communique this afternoon stating their decision not to comment on what they described as an affront by the president. They announced their intention to leave the investigation of the military troops in Juanchaco in the hands of the appropriate authorities and to let the public make up its own mind about the president's reaction to the Council's decision on the presence of foreign troops in Colombia.
