BFN [Text] Tirana, April 26 (ATA) -- Premier of the republic of Kosova, Dr. Bujar Bukoshi, who attended the proceedings of the pan-European congress between April 22-24 in Ulm of Germany, spoke of the situation in Kosova and around it, newspaper BUJKU of Prishtina reported on April 25. In order to avoid an outbreak of war in Kosova, which would be a Balkan war of unprecedented consequences, Premier Bukoshi proposed four measures. First, Kosova be made an international protectorate, secondly, Kosova be demilitarized and its democratic institutions be enabled to operate, thirdly, Kosova be included in the peace process on former Yugoslavia, and fourthly, the lifting of economic sanctions on Serbia also be conditioned by the resolution of the Kosova problem and the political will of its people be recognised under the right of the peoples' self-determination in the territory of ruined Yugoslavia, Premier Bukoshi said.
