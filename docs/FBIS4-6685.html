BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 20 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- For the incumbent cabinet of ministers to be remained, the President P. Ochirbat to resign and the meeting and hunger strike at the Suhbaatar Square to be ceased demanded the participants of the mass meeting held at the Erh Choloonii Square of the Mongolian capital called by the Union of 281 for Protection of Independence of the Mongolian People. The deepening of a political crisis all over the country would lead us to the edge of a confrontation said speaking at the meeting Z. Dorj, a writer and leader of the union he proclaimed his support of the actions of the government of Mongolia and the Prime Minister P. Jasray. Such an idea was penetrating through all the speeches of the people speaking at the meeting.
