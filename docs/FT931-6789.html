930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Tor Investment net asset value up 14% Net asset value per capital share at Tor Investment Trust increased 14 per cent from 1053.6p to 1204.4p over the 12 months to the end of January. The income share figures improved from 141p to 144.4p. Net revenue for the six months to January 31 improved to Pounds 593,000 (Pounds 579,000) for earnings per income share of 14.67p (14.3p). A second interim dividend of 10p is being declared making an unchanged 20p so far.
