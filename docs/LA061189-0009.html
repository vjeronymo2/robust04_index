June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TED TURNER

Regarding "The Man Hollywood Loves to Hate," by Stephen Farber (April 30): As an avid film buff, I heartily support Ted Turner for colorizing the film greats. Watching their revitalized images is like seeing the films for the first time. Turner should be applauded for preserving the old films that otherwise we may not have gotten a chance to see again.

STEVE BELK

Sun Valley
