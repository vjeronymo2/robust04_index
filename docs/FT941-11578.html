940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / International Company News: Australian profits up for BankAmerica BankAmerica's Australian unit has reported a sharp rise in net profit after tax to ADollars 58.2m (USDollars 41.6m) in calendar 1993 from ADollars 10.9m in 1992, Reuter reports from Sydney. Mr Barry Brownjohn, managing director, said the bank had improved its performance in all areas, with increased corporate banking and capital markets activity.
