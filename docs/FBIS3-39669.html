Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has stressed that German soldiers' missions on AWACS flights will not be affected by possible NATO air strikes on Serbian positions. Bundeswehr soldiers staying on board NATO ships in the Adriatic are also covered by the Basic Law, Kinkel told the private television station Vox today. The former Bundeswehr general Elmar Schmaehling warned earlier today that the participation of German soldiers on AWACS reconnaissance flights during a NATO air strike against Serbian positions in Bosnia would contravene the Basic Law.
