940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Ticketing Pounds 614,000 in the black Ticketing Group, which provides ticket sales and event management services and computerised ticketing systems, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 614,000 for the year to December 31. In 1992 there were losses of Pounds 13.3m after exceptional charges of Pounds 11.3m. The USM-quoted company had operated as a joint venture between Expedier and Wembley and was only constituted in its present form in February 1993 when its refinancing and reorganisation was completed. Turnover amounted to Pounds 47.9m (Pounds 7.1m) with Pounds 44.7m from acquisitions. Earnings per share came through at 0.09p against losses of 14.57p.
