Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report by "dr": "Move Must Not Cost More Than 20 Billion"] [Excerpt] Bonn -- The Berlin-Bonn Law, which settles the move of the Bundestag and the Federal Government to Berlin, was adopted with a large majority on Thursday [10 March]. According to this law, the operation is to be concluded by the year 2000. Speakers of the Bundestag groups of the Christian Democratic Union, Christian Social Union, and Free Democratic Party of Germany stressed that the cost ceiling set by the FRG finance minister at 20 billion marks must be observed. This also includes financial compensation measures for the affected cities of Bonn and Berlin. The decision envisages that the "central part" of the government functions will be moved to Berlin but that certain "political fields" will remain in Bonn. [passage omitted]
