Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta, currently rotating chairman of the CSCE in Europe, thinks it positive that "the international community is using every means to obtain the lifting of the seige of Sarajevo." The minister emphasized that the CSCE will make "all necessary contributions and efforts to speed up the peace process." Andreatta has asked for the CSCE missions blocked by the Serbs to be resumed in Vojvodina, Kosovo, and Sandzak [areas in Serbia with significant non-Serbian minorities].
