930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: CI falls 55% to Pounds 1.5m based steel and engineering company, reported pre - tax profits down by 55 per cent from Pounds 3.33m to Pounds 1.51m in the year to end-January, writes Paul Cheeseright. Earnings per share, before extraordinary items, were cut to 1.04p against 2.65p, and the final dividend is 0.175p, making a total of 1p (2.05p). Restructuring resulted in an Pounds 1.3m extraordinary debit but above the line there were exceptional items of Pounds 1m relating to redundancy costs. The payroll is now 19 per cent smaller than a year ago at 1,060. Recession hit the group hard last year, reducing turnover on continuing operations from Pounds 69.4m to Pounds 64.7m.
