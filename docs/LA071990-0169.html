"The Third Man" (1949), directed by Carol Reed. 100 minutes. No rating. An American comes to post-war Vienna to discover that a friend has been murdered and winds up learning a little about love and loyalty -- too late, alas, to help himself. With Joseph Cotten, Orson Welles, a script by Graham Greene that is a marvel of wit and intelligence, and some great zither music. M. FLAGG
