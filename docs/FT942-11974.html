940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Lendu loss Lendu Holdings, the Kent-based group involved in irrigated and dryland cotton, cereal and beef cattle production in Queensland, incurred pre-losses of Pounds 126,000 for the six months ended December 31. Exceptional gains of Pounds 898,000 together with contributions from a now discontinued activity lifted profits in the corresponding half year to Pounds 953,000.
