BFN [Text] Beijing, 13 May (XINHUA) -- Sheng Weitong (4141 3555 1749), deputy director and researcher of the China Forestry Institute under the Ministry of Forestry, and Wang Bingchen (3769 4426 1820), chief engineer and researcher of the department of hydrology of the Comprehensive Survey Institute under the Ministry of Construction, were recently hired by the State Council to serve as counsellors.
