Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- Russian Interior Minister Viktor Yerin today left for Kazan, the capital of Tatarstan, a constituent republic of the Russian Federation, to attend a summing up conference of local law enforcement bodies. Yerin, who is member of the Russian Security Council, was absent from today's meeting of the council. In the course of the visit, the Interior Ministry's public relations centre reports, Yerin will meet leaders of the republic, will visit law enforcement establishments and units of interior troops, as well as local penitentiaries. The minister arrived in the capital of Tatarstan on the same day.
