931124 FT 24 NOV 93 / International Company News: Macquarie Bank Macquarie Bank, the Sydney-based institution in which Hill Samuel holds a minority stake, yesterday reported profits after tax of ADollars 31.2m (USDollars 20.6m) in the six months to end-September, a 17.4 per cent improvement over the same period of 1992, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney.
