Language: English Article Type:BFN [By AZERTAC for TASS] [Text] Baku January 13 TASS -- Azerbaijan succeeded to repell two Armenian attacks and said that the enemy had sustained major losses. The Republican Defence Ministry said on Thursday that an Armenian tank and an armoured personnel carrier were destroyed in fighting in Fizuli region and another tank, a mortar and nearly one hundred mines were seized from retreating Armenians. A major Armenian unit got onto a mine field while approaching the city of Agdam. The Azerbaijani troops delivered flank strikes on Armenians who fled the battle scene leaving major losses behind them, according to the source. The ministry also said that casualties and damage were reported in Azeri border settlements in the north-west of the country in two days of intensive Armenian shelling from long-range artillery.
