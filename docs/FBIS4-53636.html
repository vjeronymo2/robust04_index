BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 21 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists on May 18 and 19 mobilized fighters of the marines from overseas bases to stage an air war exercise in coordination with the flying corps of the U.S. Air Force in South Korea, according to military sources. Participating in the exercise were formations of Japan-based pursuit-assault planes and F-16 and F-15 fighter-bombers, A-10 assault planes and OA-10 observation planes of the U.S. Air Force stationed in South Korea. Meanwhile, on the 19th the South Korean puppets deployed armed forces and large-calibre artillery groups in Inje, Paju and Yonchon counties adjacent to the demilitarized zone of the military demarcation line, and dropped commandos by formations of transport planes and helicopters. And they fired hundreds of shells and large-calibre machinegun bullets.
