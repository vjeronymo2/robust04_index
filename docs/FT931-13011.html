930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / World Trade News: GEC Alsthom in Mexican deal GEC Alsthom, the Anglo-French power engineering group, has won a Pounds 400m turnkey contract to build a 2 x 350MW oil-fired power station near Tuxpan in Veracruz state, Mexico, Andrew Baxter reports. GEC Alsthom was selected by the Mexican Federal Electricity Commission for the job, and is project leader in a consortium which includes two of its subsidiaries.
