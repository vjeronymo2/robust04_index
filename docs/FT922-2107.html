920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / Supervisors are 'underestimated' MANAGERS are confused about what supervisors do and frequently underestimate their competence, the UK body responsible for setting standards in management training said yesterday The Management Charter Initiative disclosed the finding at the launch of the first national standards outlining best practice for supervisors. Earlier this month, the Confederation of British Industry published research showing that supervisors themselves are confused about their jobs.
