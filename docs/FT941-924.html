940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Business Travel: Fare cuts Virgin Atlantic is this week expected to announce plans to cut its fares in response to price cuts by British Airways at the end of last week. Under BA's World Offers scheme, savings of more than Pounds 300 can be made on trips to the Far East or Mexico, Pounds 200 to the Caribbean and Pounds 100 to the US, South Africa and Europe. Trips to Europe or North America must include a Saturday night, and those elsewhere must be for at least a week.
