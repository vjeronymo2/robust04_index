930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea broker's Association reports, Landed strong and active demand. New season's Assams attracted good competition with better quality teas a very strong feature. East African teas were also well supported at fully firm rates. Bright liquoring kinds and good mediums showed a dearer tendency. Ceylons met ciably dearer, particularly for brighter descriptions. Offshore good demand at firm to dearer rates. Quotations quality 200p/kg nom, good medium 125p/kg, medium 109p/kg, low medium 93p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 220p for two lines of Assam pf.
