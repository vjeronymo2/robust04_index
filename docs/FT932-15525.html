930413 FT 13 APR 93 / Construction Contracts: Landfill treatment in Wales NUTTALL has won the Pounds 8.3m Penarth Haven landfill treatment and phase one infrastructure works contract for the Cardiff Bay Development Corporation. The site is located at Penarth Dock, South Glamorgan and represents the largest single contract awarded by the corporation to date. The works comprise the containment and encapsulation of approximately 750,000 cu metres of domestic landfill within the old dock. The scheme includes the application of substantial landfill gas abstraction and leachate collection systems on the site, together with associated pipework. Roadworks improvements and associated structures will include the construction of a new roundabout junction to replace an existing priority junction between Windsor Road and Penarth Dock Road, as well the provision of a new roundabout junction at Penarth Dock Road and some 250 metres of associated carriageways.
