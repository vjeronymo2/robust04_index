September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

SIDEWALK REPAIR PLANNED

A $5.5-million, two-year residential sidewalk repair program will begin in October in Northwest Pasadena as part of the city's six-year Infrastructure Repair Program.

Slated for repairs are sections of broken sidewalks and driveways with gaps or rises of an inch or more. About 250,000 square feet of pavement in front of homes and apartments of less than four units will be repaired.
