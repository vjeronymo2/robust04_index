BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received former Soviet labour heroine and people's actress Zykina Lyudmila Georgievna, president of the Academy of Russian Folk Culture and head of a Russian folk song and dance troupe, participating in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival, and her party. Present on the occasion were Chang-Chol, vice-premier and minister of Culture and Art, and officials concerned. The guests warmly congratulated President Kim Il-song on his birthday and wished him good health and long life. After expressing thanks for this, President Kim Il-song had a talk with them in a cordial and friendly atmosphere and gave a luncheon for them. He was presented with a gift by the head of the Russian folk song and dance troupe.
