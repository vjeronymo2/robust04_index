January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

GOOD GRIEF! DOG'S NAME SHOULD HAVE BEEN GREIF

Re, "He Really Took a Bite Out of Crime" (The Times, Jan. 18).

A nice story about a beautiful dog. His name should be Greif, not Grief. It is a popular dog name in Germany for centuries.

BERND MULLER AHRENS

Pasadena
