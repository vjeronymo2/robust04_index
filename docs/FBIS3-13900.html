Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, has said Pakistan cannot ignore its defense requirements and will continue to strengthen its defense forces within its limited resources. Addressing the Punjab regiment darbar [meeting] in Mardan today, she said Pakistan does not want confrontation with any country. However, the issue of Jammu and Kashmir has threatened the security of the region. She said Pakistan wants a peaceful and an early solution to the Kashmir issue. Ms. Benazir Bhutto stressed the need for a united strategy on national issues, including Jammu and Kashmir. She called on political parties to get together and draw up a joint strategy on the issue. The prime minister said the strength of the nation depends on the economy and therefore economic development of the country is top priority of her government. Referring to the law and order situation, she said the government is utilizing all its resources to eradicate social evils, including drug trafficking. She announced a grant of Rupees 80 lakhs for the Punjab regiment.
