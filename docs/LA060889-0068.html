June 8, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

AGOURA LEADS SOUTHERN SECTION WITH 5 TITLES

Led by the strength of its girls' sports program, Agoura High collected a total of five Southern Section divisional team championships in 1988-89, tops among the organization's 480-school membership.

Representing the Frontier League, Agoura collected championships in girls' 2-A Division cross-country, girls' 3-A soccer, girls' 1-A softball, girls' 1-A track and boys' 1-A track.

Ten Southern Section schools were multiple winners. Corona del Mar won four titles and five schools -- Capistrano Valley, Mission Prep of San Luis Obispo, San Marino, Trabuco Hills and Irvine Woodbridge -- each won three.

Six schools -- Harvard, Santa Clara, Arroyo Grande, El Dorado, Palos Verdes and Santa Maria St. Joseph -- each won two titles.
