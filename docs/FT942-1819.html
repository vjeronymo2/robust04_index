940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Strong second half boosts Sterling Industries to Pounds 4.74m Sterling Industries, the engineering group which has a 9.7 per cent stake in Caledonia Investments, reported a 26 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.74m to Pounds 4.71m for the year ended March 31. Turnover for the company, of which Cayzer Trust holds 48.8 per cent and Caledonia has a stake of 21.05 per cent, expanded to Pounds 47m, compared with Pounds 38.9m, with Pounds 2.23m coming from acquisitions, directors stated. Mr Peter Buckley, chairman, said that after a slow start to the year the second half had improved with a particularly strong performance from the thermal process division with GCD in Australia being the main contributor. The thermal process division contributed Pounds 1.93m to operating profits, against a previous Pounds 881,000, from turnover ahead from Pounds 27.1m to Pounds 34.1m. Hydraulics achieved Pounds 1.03m (Pounds 995,000) from turnover of Pounds 13m (Pounds 11.8m). Earnings per share rose from 8.6p to 12.06p while a recommended final dividend of 4.5p is proposed for a total distribution of 6.3p (5.6p).
