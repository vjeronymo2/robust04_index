October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

EXPRESSING SOME ROQ-SOLID POSITIONS

Reeb speaks of wanting to maintain KROQ's "nutty, offbeat and unpredictable" qualities while changing station format and marketing strategies. Is there not a contradiction in trying to research, format and market unpredictability?

He then goes on to state that he fired deejay Dusty Street because she was a "renegade." Well, it was such deejays who made KROQ the nutty, offbeat and unpredictable station it once was. Obviously, there's no place for them in Mr. Reeb's new scheme of things.

JEFF DiPERNA

Sherman Oaks
