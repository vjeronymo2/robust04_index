Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian Foreign Ministry is preparing for consultations with NATO leaders on Russia's possibly joining the Partnership for Peace program, Director of the Department for Cooperation with Europe Yuriy Ushakov told Interfax on Tuesday [1 February]. He said that Russia intended to find out the NATO leaders' position in relation to Russia and determine the parameters of the agreements to be concluded. The date of consultations has not been fixed yet. "However, they will take place in the near future," said the Russian diplomat.
