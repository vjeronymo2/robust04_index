940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / International Company News: Suez decision on Victoire in June Suez will announce its decision on Victoire, its insurance unit, at its shareholders' meeting on June 15, Mr Gerard Worms, chairman, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Paris. Mr Worms said Suez did not need to sell Victoire to raise money for its ongoing operations but if it did sell, it could use the funds for further corporate moves.
