Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States and Brazil have reached an agreement on the preservation of intellectual property rights. Special U.S. trade representative Mickey Kantor today informed Brazilian Ambassador to Washington Paulo Tarso Flecha de Lima that the United States has decided not to impose sanctions against Brazil. The Brazilian Government has announced that it intends to sign, in April, the international patent preservation agreement approved by GATT, the General Agreement on Trade and Tariffs.
