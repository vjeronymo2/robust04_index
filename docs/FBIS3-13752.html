Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] According to reports from Afghanistan, there has been sporadic rocket firing on the capital, Kabul, from this morning. The Afghan Defense Ministry has recently warned that if the Dostam militia resorts to shelling Kabul, the government air force will take retaliatory action and the militia bases in and around Mazar-e Sharif will come under air strikes. However, there has been no report of any aerial action so far. Reports also say that there has been continuous exchange of artillery and rocket fire between rival factions in the northern province of Baghlan.
