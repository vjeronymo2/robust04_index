920117 FT 17 JAN 92 / German retail sales slip Retail sales in west Germany provisionally fell 0.5 per cent in real terms in November against November 1990, the Federal Statistics office said, Reuter reports from Wiesbaden. Sales of new vehicles, car parts and tyres dropped 6 per cent while electronic and music equipment sales fell 4 per cent. Sales of pharmaceutical products rose 5 per cent and textile, clothing and shoe sales increased 3 per cent. Paper goods and office equipment sales rose 1 per cent. In the first 11 months of 1991, retail sales rose 6 per cent.
