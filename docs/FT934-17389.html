931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Politcal party funding A LABOUR government should take action to bring the funding of political parties out into the open, party supporters were told today. Delegates debated a motion calling for a ban on all donations from foreign nationals, and urging the recording of all donations above a minimum level and the compulsory publication of independently audited accounts for parties' income and expenditure. Mr Andy Prodger of the National Communications Union said: 'It should not be possible for company directors to hide their Tory handouts from their shareholders and from their customers. 'All company donations should be subject to a vote by their shareholders,' he said.
