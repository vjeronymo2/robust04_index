August 27, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE DEFENDER

It is with an extreme sense of ironic incredulity that I view Abramson. She makes the statement about being "bathed in the backwash of the Holocaust," yet she defends those whose crimes are no less heinous than the vile, Nazi thugs she deplores. One wonders if she would have been so charitable at Nuremburg.

MATTHEW L. DADAY

Torrance
