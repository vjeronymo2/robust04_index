April 27, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SAIGON SUNDAY: NBC's "Sunday Today" visits Garden Grove's Little Saigon, with an interview with Do Ngoc Yen, editor of the Westminster-based Nguoi Viet Daily News, the largest Vietnamese newspaper in the United States. . . . The subject: the conflict between communist and non-communist sympathizers that has escalated into violence. Do received numerous death threats, the most recent supposedly to be carried out April 30, 15th anniversary of the fall of Saigon, but says that this Monday "will be a normal day for me." Mark I. Pinsky and Jeffrey A. Perlman
