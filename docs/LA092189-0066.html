September 21, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

In the process of preparing a segment on the homeless, CBS' "60 Minutes" followed Martin Sheen to Skid Row this week, where the actor visited Chrysalis, a nonprofit homeless assistance agency that is one of his favorite causes. Sheen and his family will also participate in the third annual 5K/10K "Run for the Homeless" on Oct. 14 that will benefit the agency. ALEENE MacMINN
