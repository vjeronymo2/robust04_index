930422 FT 22 APR 93 / World Trade News: Kvaerner wins its biggest order The Norwegian group Kvaerner Engineering has won a Nkr1bn (Pounds 96m) contract - its biggest single order - for engineering and construction work on a new gas platform in the Norwegian sector of the North Sea, Deborah Hargreaves reports. The contract was placed by Statoil, the Norwegian state oil company, for its Sleipner 'T' gas treatment platform. The contract extends over four years as part of the Sleipner West development.
