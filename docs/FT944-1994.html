941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / Credit Suisse proceedings Swiss authorities have begun extradition proceedings against an Italian financier based in London in connection with an alleged Dollars 70m fraud against Credit Suisse bank. Mr Sergio Cuoghi, the head of the Ehrofin financial group of Geneva, faces a charge of obtaining Dollars 70m by deception from Credit Suisse between January 1 1992 and November 18 this year. As part of an investigation being carried out by the Swiss authorities, a former assistant manager with Fides, an investment trust company wholly owned by Credit Suisse, is currently being held in prison in Geneva. At an interim hearing at London's Bow St magistrates court yesterday, Mr Cuoghi's case was adjourned to January 9 next year. Mr Cuoghi, of Montpelier Square, London, has been remanded on bail until then.
