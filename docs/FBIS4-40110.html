BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev June 14 TASS -- President of Ukraine Leonid Kravchuk left for Luxembourg today. His working visit is aimed at signing an agreement on partnership and cooperation between Ukraine and European communities. [as received] Head of the Ukrainian presidential press service Viktor Stelmakh told ITAR-TASS that during his visit President Kravchuk will meet European Commission Chairman Jacque Delors, Grand Duke of Luxembourg Jean, Prime Minister Jacques Santer, speaker of the parliament Erna Hennicot-Schoepges and Vice-Premier Jacques Poos. Ukrainian foreign minister and his Luxembourg counterpart are also expected to sign an agreement on air service between the two states.
