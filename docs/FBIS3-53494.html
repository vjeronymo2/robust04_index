Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fayil Jibril, official spokesman of the Libyan opposition in Cairo, says he has information, the source of which he has not revealed, that Mansur al-Kikhya, former Libyan foreign minister and head of the opposition Libyan Democratic Grouping, was abducted and killed the day after he arrived in Libya. But Egyptian Interior Minister Major General Hasan al-Alfi says he has information that al-Kikhya is still at an unknown location in Egypt and has not been moved abroad.
