CSO [Unattributed article: "Tatarstan Delegation in Republic of Komi"] [Text] A delegation of the Republic of Tatarstan was in the Republic of Komi on 6-8 June. Headed by Vice President of the Republic of Tatarstan V. Likhachev, it came for the final stage of preparations of a Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the two republics. The delegation took part in a festive ceremony for the installation of Yu. Spiridonov in the post of first head of the Republic of Komi, which was held in the state republic opera and ballet theater. The signing of a treaty on cultural cooperation between the Republic of Tatarstan and the Republic of Komi was held yesterday in the small hall of the Supreme Soviet of the republic. It was signed by Minister of Culture of the Republic of Tatarstan M. Taishev and Minister of Culture of the Republic of Komi A. Beznosikov. A news conference was held afterwards. Taking part in the ceremony in the signing of the treaty and the news conference were: From the Tatar side--Vice President of the Republic of Tatarstan V. Likhachev, and I. Terentyev, manager of the department of international relations of the staff of the president of the Republic of Tatarstan; from the Komi side--First Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Komi I. Kulakov, and the deputy head of the Republic of Komi, V. Torlopov. Yesterday, the Tatarstan delegation departed Syktyvkar for home.
