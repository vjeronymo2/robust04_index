Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Global TV News" newscast] [Text] The government palace has maintained total silence since the promulgation of the law on venue dispute and amid rumors of the resignations of two ministers. President Alberto Fujimori has continued to work privately without making any public appearances for the past three days. The government palace press secretariat also declined to provide information on the rumored resignations of Council of Ministers President Alfonso Bustamante y Bustamante, who did not sign the law, and Economy Minister Jorge Camet.
