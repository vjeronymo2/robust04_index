Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The third test flight of the country's intermediate range ballistic missile, Agni [fire], will take place on Saturday [19 February] at Chandipur on sea in Orissa. According to official sources at Balasore, the decision to this effect was taken at a high-level meeting of defense authorities and the district administration yesterday. It may be recalled that the third launch of the missile slated for seventh of last month was postponed at the last minute following the detection of a technical snag in one of the onboard systems.
