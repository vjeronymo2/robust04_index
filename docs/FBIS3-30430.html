Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "UNPROFOR Soldiers Fight Against Police"] [Text] An incident took place at the Skopje city square, during the night between Friday and Saturday, last week, involving UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] soldiers and Macedonia police officers. Unofficially, about 20 UNPROFOR soldiers and 9 policemen started a street fight. Neither the Macedonian Police Department, nor the UNPROFOR Headquarters have so far issued any official announcement.
