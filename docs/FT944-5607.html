941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Asset Management Asset Management Investment Company is to seek a listing on the London Stock Exchange via a placing of 5.04m 25p shares at 100p apiece, with warrants attached on a 1-for-5 basis, valuing the company at Pounds 5.04m. The objective of Asset Management is to achieve long-term capital appreciation and growth of revenue by investing in the investment management industry.
