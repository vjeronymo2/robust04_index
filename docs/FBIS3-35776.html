Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] As a RIA correspondent was told at the CIS headquarters, the meeting of CIS ministers of internal affairs which was due to tackle questions of creating a single coordinating body to combat organized crime has been put off from 1 to 16 February and it will take place not in Minsk, but in Ashgabat. One of the reasons for the transfer is the absence of a minister of internal affairs of the Republic of Belarus, who was removed from his post by decision of the Supreme Soviet on 25 January because of the Lithuanian incident.
