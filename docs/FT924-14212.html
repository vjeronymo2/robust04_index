921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / World News in Brief: London stocks muted after Major speech The London stock market gave a muted response to the keynote speech by John Major at the Conservative party's conference, with share prices sliding back to close only marginally higher on the day. The FT-SE 100 index closed 2.4 firmer at 2,541.2, down 8.5 on the week. London stocks, Page 15; Snapshot, Page 9
