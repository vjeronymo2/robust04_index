940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Applied Distribution Applied Distribution, the contract distribution group which came to the market in March, has paid Pounds 3.3m cash for Bernard Brook Transport (Elland), including about Pounds 800,000 borrowings assumed. Bernard Brook is a family-owned company which turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 200,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.7m in the 53 weeks to January.
