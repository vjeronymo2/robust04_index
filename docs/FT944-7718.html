941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / International Company News: Communications investment fund to be launched Montgomery Asset Management, the San Francisco-based investment manager, and Barings Securities, part of the UK investment banking group, yesterday announced a Dollars 100m investment fund which will focus on communications and services in the emerging markets. The Montgomery Emerging Communications Fund will place up to 10m shares. Mr Oscar Castro, who currently manages the Montgomery Global Communications Fund, will act as principal portfolio manager for the fund, which will be registered as a closed end investment company in Bermuda. Baring Brothers is sponsor to the issue and Baring Securities is the placing agent. The placing is not underwritten. 'New technologies can offer emerging markets countries cheaper and faster solutions to their communications needs,' said Barings. 'For example, in some parts of Thailand and the Philippines, cellular telephone networks are being installed instead of standard land lines.' Montgomery will also target opportunities in broadcasting, media and cable TV.
