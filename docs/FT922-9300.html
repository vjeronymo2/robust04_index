920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: BMSS sharply down at Pounds 515,000 Profits of BMSS, the USM-quoted timber and building materials merchant, fell from Pounds 1.02m to Pounds 515,000 pre-tax over the 12 months ended January 31. Sales showed a 23 per cent downturn at Pounds 17.3m. However, gross margins improved to 31.23 (28.91) per cent reflecting the policy of rejecting very low margin business. Earnings emerged at 5.1p (11.3p). A final dividend of 2.7p makes a same-again 4.7p total.
