940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Culver rises Culver Holdings, the motor dealership and insurance broking group, achieved a near doubling of pre-tax profits from Pounds 183,000 to Pounds 350,000, in the half year to June 30. Turnover jumped to Pounds 22.9m (Pounds 11.1m) mainly as a result of acquisitions made in the second half last year. The interim dividend is lifted to 0.13p (0.11p), payable from earnings of 0.34p (0.3p) per share.
