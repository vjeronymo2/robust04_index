921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Barlows Difficulty in letting a joint venture office development contributed to increased interim pre-tax losses at Barlows, the property investor and developer. Overall losses from joint ventures in the six months to June 30 were Pounds 342,000 (Pounds 187,000) and pre-tax losses came out at Pounds 280,000, against Pounds 111,000. Lower turnover of Pounds 843,000 (Pounds 945,000) was offset by lower net interest charges of Pounds 282,000 (Pounds 394,000). Losses per share were 1.34p (0.72p) but the interim dividend is being maintained at 0.825p.
