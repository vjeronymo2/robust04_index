Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, today performed the noon prayers with their eminences the 'ulema in the historic al-Janad Mosque in Ta'izz. The brother Presidential Council chairman attended part of their eminences the 'ulema's meeting in al-Janad. He delivered a speech in which he said he was pleased to attend this meeting, which encompasses a group of their eminences the 'ulema, in response to the invitation they extended to him and to brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid to meet on this great day in which the Muslim world celebrates a glorious Muslim occasion [Prophet Muhammad's night journey from Mecca to Jerusalem]. He said: How I wished that brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid was here in order to work together to preserve the great achievement of unity that reunited the Yemeni family after its estrangement and ended our people's nightmare of division. The brother Presidential Council chairman said: We have come here to protect our homeland and spare our people the sedition hatched by those who do not wish to see them enjoy [word indistinct], security, and stability. Our responsibility toward God and our people dictates that we should rise above trivial things and give priority to the supreme national interest. The Presidential Council chairman urged the brother 'ulema to say what is right and to stand against evil and (?those who fabricate seditions), intensify them, and benefit from their continuation.
