920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Real Time Control static at Pounds 1.19m Real Time Control reported slightly lower pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.19m for the year to March 31, against Pounds 1.2m. Higher operating profits of Pounds 780,000 (Pounds 720,000) were offset by lower interest received of Pounds 412,000 (Pounds 479,000) resulting from lower interest rates. Turnover for the USM-quoted company, which services electronic point of sale systems, fell from Pounds 7.57m to Pounds 6.84m. Earnings per share were 11.1p (10.9p) after a lower tax charge of Pounds 415,000 (Pounds 439,000). A maintained single final dividend of 3p is proposed.
