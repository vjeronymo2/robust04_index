931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / Ford recalls 57,000 Mondeos FORD is recalling 57,000 Mondeo family saloon cars in the UK because of a minor defect to the handbrake lever. The recalled cars were made between March and June this year at Ford's Belgian factory in Genk. The defect is only in right-hand-drive vehicles. The handbrake design has been adjusted and Ford said that dealers would fit a new handgrip to the brake lever of affected cars.
