BFN [Text] President Geydar Aliyev received Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin and his delegation today. After welcoming the visiting delegation, President Aliyev briefed the Turkish diplomats on the social and political developments in Azerbaijan. Referring to the fighting in Azerbaijan, Aliyev said that while Armenia's aggression against the republic has intensified, Azerbaijan's people hoped that the countries around the world, particularly Turkey, would take resolute steps to end the aggression. However, Aliyev said: Regrettably, regarding the UN resolutions and the CSCE effort, a satisfactory outcome is impossible. This is because Armenia's aggression, which has continued for six years, [words indistinct]. An effective solution cannot be [words indistinct]. Foreign Minister Cetin said that Turkey cannot be reconciled with a country that occupies Azerbaijan's territory. He also noted that Turkey will always support Azerbaijan's efforts to defend its territory. Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, First Deputy Prime Minister Fuad Sharipov, State Advisers [name indistinct] and Vefa Gulizade, and Altan Karamanoglu, Turkey's ambassador to Azerbaijan, attended the meeting.
