920408 FT 08 APR 92 / Election 1992: Curtains up - Ballot Box The spirits of some of Labour's infantry out knocking on London doors yesterday were lifted by rumours that the Kinnocks had been invited to No 10 to measure up the curtains. The rumours spread fast enough to produce a summary denial from Downing Street. 'There will be plenty of time for that should the eventuality come to pass,' said a press officer.
