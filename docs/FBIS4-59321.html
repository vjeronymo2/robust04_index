BFN [Excerpts] Aden, Yemen, May 11 (AFP)--Southern Yemeni jets and artillery pounded rival northern forces Wednesday east of the strategic southern port city of Aden, a correspondent reported. The heaviest fire was concentrated on the Zinjibar area, about 60 kilometres (36 miles) east of Aden, as well as Ja'ar and Bakis, where the northern al-'Amaliqah brigade is based. Meanwhile, northern forces were also trying to open up a new front in the Bab al-Mandeb area, about 400 kilometres (248 miles) west of Aden, said Culture Minister Jarallah 'Umar, a southerner. An AFP correspondent reported that southern planes made numerous sorties over Aden. The southern air force is considered stronger than that of the north. [passage omitted] Some 12,000 northern troops are stationed in Abyan province, east of Aden, analysts say.
