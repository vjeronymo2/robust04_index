940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Coopers set to shed 90 partners Coopers & Lybrand, Britain's biggest accountancy firm, is to shed about 90 of its 670 partners in the coming year, an official confirmed yesterday. Last year Coopers cut 420 jobs, mainly through natural wastage, at a time when its fee income rose 1.3 per cent to Pounds 560m.
