930626 FT 26 JUN 93 / International Company News: Matra expects strong advance for full year MATRA-HACHETTE, the diversified missiles-to-magazines group, will show a much better profit this year, Mr Jean-Luc Lagardere, president, told shareholders yesterday. For 1993, the first full operating year after he merged the Matra aerospace, transport and telecommunications group with the Hachette publishing business, Mr Lagardere forecast that group turnover would remain stable at FFr51bn (Dollars 9.15bn). Improved profits would come from declining financial costs.
