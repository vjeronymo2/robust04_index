March 12, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

BEAUTY IS IN THE EYE OF THE BEHOLDER

Coach Pam Walker of Hart High should apologize for her insensitive comment made in The Times on March 3. She refers to her team as "tall, blonde and beautiful." Is being tall and blonde her standard of beauty?

What message does that give the black girl on the team?

RAFER and BETSY JOHNSON

Sherman Oaks
