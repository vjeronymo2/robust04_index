July 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Seidenbaum Tribute: Los Angeles Times columnist, editor and writer Art Seidenbaum, who died last Tuesday of cancer, will be remembered in a special program Tuesday on KCRW-FM (89.9) at 12:30 p.m. KCRW General Manager Ruth Hirschman will be joined by Times Arts Editor Charles Champlin and literary critic Jonathan Kirsch. GREG BRAXTON
