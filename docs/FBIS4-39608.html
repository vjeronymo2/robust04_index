BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov, Nikita Yermakov and Igor Shchegolev] [Text] Paris June 8 -- Russia will send a peace-keeping contingent to Abkhazia within the next few days, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said. Meeting journalists at the Russian embassy in Paris on Wednesday, Kozyrev said that no other country is ready to follow Russia's suit so far. The decision to send peace-keepers to the war zone has nothing to do with the imperial policy or irresponsibility, he added. This is a normal policy of a responsible country which closely cooperates with the United Nations and the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe, he said.
