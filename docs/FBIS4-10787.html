BFN [Text] The official visit to the sultanate of Oman by Cuban Vice Foreign Minister Nicolas Rodriguez [name and title as heard] has concluded. In Oman, Rodriguez met with Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin- 'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs. The two officials discussed political ties between the two nations, as well as possibilities for establishing economic ties. Rodriguez also met with other Omani foreign ministry officials and visited sites of economic and historic interest.
