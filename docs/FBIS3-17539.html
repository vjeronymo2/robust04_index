Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Vasiliy Belousov and Dmitriy Gorokhov] [Text] Murmansk, 26 Mar -- "One of the most promising regions of Russia and the nearest foreign parts in relation to Norway." This is how Andrey Kozyrev defined the choice of Murmansk as the venue for the meeting of the heads of the foreign policy departments of Russia and Norway. His talks with his Norwegian colleague Bjorn Tore Godal have only just begun in the modern hotel outside town called "Ogni Murmanska." The ministers are exchanging opinions on international issues and have touched upon a broad circle of subjects of regional partnership.
