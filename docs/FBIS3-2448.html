Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 8 Mar (XINHUA) -- The Second Session of the Eighth Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] National Committee opened at the Great Hall of the People this afternoon. Li Ruihuan, chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, presided over the opening. He declared that of the total 2,097 members of the CPPCC National Committee, 1,917 had already report to the session and 1,860 were attending the opening, thus fulfilling the quorum. Vice chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee who were seated in the front row of the rostrum were Ye Xuanping, Wu Xueqian, Yang Rudai, Wang Zhaoguo, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Seypidin Aze, Hong Xuezhi, Zhao Puchu, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Qian Zhengying, Su Buqing, Ding Guangxun, Dong Yinchu, Sun Fuling, Ann Tse-kai, Henry Fok Ying-tung, and Ma Man-kai. Party and state leaders Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Zhu Rongji, Liu Huaqing, Hu Jintao, and Rong Yiren were seated on the rostrum. They offered congratulations on the opening of the session. At 1500 [0700 GMT], Chairman Li Ruihuan declared the session open. As all member stood at attention, a military band played the national anthem. The session first examined and adopted an agenda for the current session of the CPPCC National Committee. According to the agenda, members of the CPPCC National Committee will: examine a work report of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee; attend the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress as nonvoting delegates; deliberate and approve the "CPPCC Constitution (amended);" deliberate and approve a report by the Motions Committee of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee on examining motions having been submitted to the Second Session of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee; deliberate and approve various resolutions to be adopted by the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress; and elect new vice-chairmen, Standing Committee members, and secretary- general of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee. Entrusted by the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, Ye Xuanping, vice chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, delivered a report on the work of the Standing Committee. In the report, the vice chairman first reviewed the work of the Standing Committee over the past year. He said: Since the First Session of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee, the Standing Committee, guided by Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, has worked hard to explore new ways to engage in political consultation and exercise democratic supervision, paying attention to selecting issues, bringing into play the favorable factors, and ensuring effective participation in the administration and discussion of state affairs focused on the central task of the nation. The Standing Committee has given full play to the role of the special committees and organized members to take part in various activities on a regular basis; has stepped up information service and reflected popular feelings in a timely and accurate manner; and has actively conducted CPPCC's exchanges with foreign countries in line with the overall arrangements of national diplomacy; thereby making advances in all fields of endeavor. He said: The party and the government have already decided on the basic policy for 1994, a year of vital importance to China's reform, opening up, and socialist modernization drive. This year will be the second year for the Eighth CPPCC National Committee to carry out its work. The main points of the Standing Committee's work are: studying in depth the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics to continuously raise the consensus; selecting major topics in economic construction, reform and opening up, spiritual civilization construction, and the people's life for investigation and study to ensure effective participation in the administration and discussion of state affairs; carrying forward the fine tradition and bringing into play the role of the democratic parties and the people's mass organizations in participating in the administration and discussion of state affairs; forging close ties with the people of all nationalities and in various circles, and harmonizing their relations and transmitting viewpoints of higher authorities to lower levels to safeguard political and social stability; improving and strengthening inspection and the work of handling motions to promote democratic supervision; broadening channels of communication with overseas Chinese to promote the motherland's peaceful reunification; utilizing the CPPCC's advantages to continue to develop friendly exchanges with parliaments and nongovernmental organizations of other countries; and improving the performance of offices in an all-round way by raising the quality of officers and raising office work efficiency. Ye Xuanping's report received thunderous applause from members. The "CPPCC Constitution (amended)" was submitted to members for deliberation at the meeting today. Leading comrades who were seated on the rostrum were also Tian Jiyun, Li Lanqing, Li Tieying, Yang Baibing, Zou Jiahua, Jiang Chunyun, Qian Qichen, Wei Jianxing, Wen Jiabao, Wang Hanbin, Zhang Zhen, Ren Jianxin, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muhua, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, Li Ximing, Wang Bingqian, Wang Guangying, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiaxi, Buhe, Tomur Dawamat, Li Peiyao, Wu Jieping, Chi Haotian, Song Jian, Li Guixian, Chen Junsheng, Ismail Amat, Pen Peiyuan, Luo Gan, and Zhang Siqing. After the meeting today, Chairman Li Ruihu and Vice Chairmen Ye Xuanping, Wu Xueqian, Yang Rudai, and Wang Zhaoguo met with new members of the Eighth CPPCC National Committee, who had been elected through by-elections.
