Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Jose Ignacio Penagos in Medellin from the "NTC Newscast"] [Text] The Mexican Government delegation--which is trying to resolve the electric power emergency in southern Mexico as a result of Zapatista National Liberation Army [EZLN] attacks--is in Medellin. Helped by executives of Electrical Inter-Connection, Inc. [ISA], they are seeking to establish contacts with private companies that can provide parts to the Mexican Federal Electricity Commission and to purchase an undetermined number of high-tension pylons. They have reached agreements with public enterprises of Medellin, the electric power companies of Santa Fe de Bogota and Cali, and with ISA. [Begin Victor Manuel Ibarra, Mexican delegation member, recording] I am only coordinating certain administrative matters. [end recording] Several members of the Mexican delegation have traveled to Cali. Meanwhile, other members are in Medellin with ISA Manager Diego Landizabal to meet with executives of the enterprises that can help resolve the Mexican electric power problem.
