Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Two unidentified armed gunmen on a motorcycle fired with a Kalashnikov rifle and hurled a grenade on people offering prayers during the Friday congregation at a mosque in Shahar Sultan in Muzaffargarh District yesterday. Five were killed and 25 others injured. According to police sources, the assailants fled the scene soon after the incident. The police have barricaded Shahar Sultan to arrest the assailants. It is reported that additional police reinforcements have been rushed to Shahar Sultan from Dera Ghazi Khan, Multan, and other places to maintain law and order there.
