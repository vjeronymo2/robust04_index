BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Proceeding from the recommendations made by the Council for Ensuring the National Security and based upon the political concord reached yesterday between the general secretary of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the leader of the Mongolian National Democratic Party and the head of the Mongolian Social-Democratic Party, the Great State Hural of Mongolia has passed late in night a decision aimed to regulate the complicated political situation arisen over the last days in Mongolia and settle down the burning questions. The Mongolian parliament has decided to consider by its spring session the draft laws on the procedure of carrying out demonstrations and meetings, the struggle against corruption as well as the draft law on freedom of press and information which is to be submitted for consideration on the initiative of the president of Mongolia. The parliament has charged the government to present for consideration at the spring session of 1994 the report on the issues concerning the social economic situation of the country. The government has been charged to work out and submit for discussion by the parliament within the year 1994 the drafts of laws on ethic standards for highest political holders of position and the nationwide referendum to be carried out.
