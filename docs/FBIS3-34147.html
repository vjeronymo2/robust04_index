Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Jammu and Kashmir, a government spokesman questioned the moral right of Pakistan Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto to take up the issue of human rights in Kashmir. He said Pakistan sponsored militants are the main violators of human rights. He pointed out that since January 1990, these militants have killed as many as 2,300 innocent civilians -- mostly Kashmiri Muslims -- and abducted more than 1,000 people.
