930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / Business and the Law: Control Securities - Legal Briefs In our issue of December 22, we reported that Mr Nazmu Virani, currently being prosecuted in connection with the Bank of Credit and Commerce International investigation, was the head of Control Securities plc. Control Securities has asked us to point out that he is no longer with the company and is not being prosecuted in connection with his former role there.
