940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / International Company News: Silvermines' IPounds 5.2m offer for Molynx Silvermines, the Dublin-based electrical and property group, has made an agreed offer for Molynx Holdings which values the closed circuit television, security and building energy management systems maker at IPounds 5.17m (Pounds 5.11m). The offer is of 55 Silvermines' shares for every 100 Molynx shares, valuing each Molynx share at 25.6p, or 25.3p sterling. Silvermines will also be raising about IPounds 7.4m net through a placing and open offer of 20.35m new shares at 43p on a 5-for-8 basis. The placing and open offer have been fully underwritten by Williams de Broe and Riada Corporate Finance. The proceeds will reduce Molynx's borrowings and provide capital for the enlarged group. Silvermines forecasts pre-tax profits of IPounds 1.4m and a dividend of 0.5p for 1993, while Molynx estimates pre-tax losses of Pounds 3.8m.
