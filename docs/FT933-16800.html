930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: British Polythene British Polythene Industries has purchased certain assets from the receiver of Punchcraft and its subsidiaries PC (Plastics) and Porter Hopkins, which are located at Workington, Cumbria, for Pounds 625,000 cash. In the years ended March 31 1991 and 1992 turnover of the two companies was Pounds 5.51m and Pounds 5.36m respectively. Last month Polythene purchased the polythene sack business of Crown Sacks & Systems for Pounds 4.4m cash.
