BFN [Text] A visiting delegation led by Datuk Sri Dr. Lim Keng Yaik, Malaysian primary industries minister, and including Malaysian private entrepreneurs left Yangon [Rangoon] by air at 0800 today. The Malaysian delegation was seen off at Yangon's airport by Lieutenant General Tun Kyi, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of trade; Deputy Trade Minister U Aung Thaung; responsible personnel from the trade organizations; and Malaysian Ambassador Mr. John Tenewi Nuek.
