February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

WASHINGTON STATE;

TEACHERS WALK OUT TO AIR PAY DEMANDS

Chanting that they refused to "work for peanuts anymore," at least a thousand teachers marched through downtown Seattle as more than 13,000 instructors across Washington state staged a one-day walkout to draw attention to their demands for a pay raise. The walkout by a quarter of the state's 43,000 teachers gave about 200,000 public school students a day off that will have to be made up.
