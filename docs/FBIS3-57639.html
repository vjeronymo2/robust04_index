Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A two-day meeting of the defense ministers of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and Uzbekistan ended in Bishkek today. As ITAR-TASS reports, the coordination of air defense systems, mutual supplies of technology, and scientific research work were among the questions discussed. During the meeting between Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan [as heard], a protocol on military cooperation in 1994 was signed.
