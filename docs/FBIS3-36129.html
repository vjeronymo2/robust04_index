Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Foreign Ministry Press and Public Relations Service has reported that President Levon Ter-Petrosyan has sent a reply message to Juan Antonio Samaranch, the president of the International Olympics Committee [IOC], expressing his and his people's determination to help achieve the Olympics cease-fire between 5 February and 6 March 1994 as declared by the 25 October 1993 resolution of the 48th session of the UN General Assembly at the initiative of the IOC. The message also notes that the Armenian Republic and Nagorno-Karabakh accepted the UN Security Council resolutions on the peaceful resolution to the Karabakh problem and the peace plan of the CSCE Minsk Group, and has given a positive reply to all the proposals of the international community, particularly that of Russia, including Russia's latest proposal to establish a cease-fire as of 1 February 1994.
