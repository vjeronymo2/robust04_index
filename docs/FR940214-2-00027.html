The application requests authorization under Section 3 of the Natural Gas Act, and the issuance of a Presidential Permit under Executive Order 10485, as amended by Executive Order 12038. SoCalGas seeks the Section 3 authorization and the Presidential Permit so that it may construct, connect, operate, and maintain certain natural gas pipeline and metering export facilities. SoCalGas proposes to construct and operate the following export facilities: (1) One metering station consisting of one 8-inch turbine meter run and one 12-inch turbine meter run; and, (2) Either approximately: (a) 900 feet of 16-inch diameter pipeline, with appurtenances, connecting the meter station to the border, (Bowker Road Alternative); or, (b) 4,800 feet of 16-inch diameter pipeline, with appurtenances, connecting the meter station to the border, (Calzada Justo Sierra Alternative).
