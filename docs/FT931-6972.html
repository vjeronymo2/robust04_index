930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Colorgen slips back to Dollars 9,000 Colorgen, the US-based colour matching specialist traded on the USM, reported a fall in pre-tax profits from Dollars 83,000 to Dollars 9,000 (Pounds 6,300) in the half year to December 31. The result was affected by expansion costs. Sales were up 50 per cent at Dollars 5.91m (Dollars 3.93m). Net income per share was 0.1 cents (0.5 cents).
