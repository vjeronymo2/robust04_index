Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 22 (KNS-KCNA) -- The Japanese people are protesting Kim Yong-sam's tour of their country. Genzu Watanabe, secretary of the Japan-South Korea Council of People's Solidarity, made a report and public figures speeches at a meeting held in Tokyo on March 17. They pointed out that the criminal purpose of the tour of Japan by the traitor Kim Yong-sam is to strengthen the Japanese-South Korean joint step against the DPRK, promote political conspiracy regarding the past Japan's aggression on Korea and colonial rule and build new relations between Japan and South Korea. They called upon the Japanese people to obtain a clear understanding of the essence of Kim Yong-sam's tour of Japan, strengthen solidarity with the South Korean people struggling for independence, democracy and peaceful reunification and wage a vigorous struggle in demand of a fundamental switchover of the Japan government's policy towards Korea.
