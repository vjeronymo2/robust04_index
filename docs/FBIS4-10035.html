BFN [Editorial Report] Havana PRENSA LATINA in Spanish at 1509 GMT on 1 April 1994 reports that Mexican Cubamor Travel Agency has announced it will send approximately 6,000 tourists to Cuba this year. According to Company Manager Bernardo Uriostegue Najera, this figure represents a hearty 10 percent of the total number of Mexican vacationers traveling to Cuba. The agency expects to begin moving cargo to and from Cuba soon.
