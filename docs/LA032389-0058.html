March 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Look for a parade of real-life TV journalists to shmooze with CBS' "Murphy Brown" soon. Linda Ellerbee comes to town Monday to tape an episode for this season. "And Walter Cronkite is committed to doing a show next season," promised show creator Diane English at a Museum of Broadcasting salute to the new hit series Tuesday night at the County Museum of Art. Murphy alter ego Candice Bergen also will be visiting with Kathleen Sullivan and Phil Donohue next season.ALEENE MacMINN
