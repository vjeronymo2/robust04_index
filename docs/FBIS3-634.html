Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The political and military situation is once again shaky in the besieged city of Cuito. The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola's [UNITA] rebels continue to prepare a large-scale attack on the city of Cuito. The local press learned this from Captain Jorge Regresso, spokesman of the Angolan Armed Forces command, in the region. According to the source, over the past few weeks, Savimbi's men have been carrying out constant movements of troops and war materiel from Andulo District and Huambo Province. They have also been planting mines, and kidnapping and assaulting civilians. Jorge Regresso also said that more than 16 night flights, carrying large quantities of war materiel to Andulo, were registered from January to March.
