Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 27 Dec (MENA)--Sudanese diplomatic sources have criticized what they called provocation by the U.S. Embassy in Khartoum, which presented awards to the families of Sudanese citizens in appreciation of their efforts during the U.S. humanitarian aid operations, despite a Sudanese criminal court condemning these activities. The official newspaper AL-INQADH AL-WATANI cited the sources as saying that the Sudanese Government does not want to respond to the U.S. provocation and has agreed to form a committee to discuss the trial's legal procedures.
