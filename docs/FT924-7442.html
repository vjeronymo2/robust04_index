921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Honecker trial delayed again The trial of former East German leader Erich Honecker was adjourned again yesterday as a second defendant, former Stasi security chief Erich Mielke, 84, was excused from appearing because of ill health, Reuter reports from Berlin. The trial was postponed last week after another defendant, ex-Prime Minister Willi Stoph, 78, suffered a heart attack.
