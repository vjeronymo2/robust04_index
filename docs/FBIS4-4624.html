BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 2 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet's arrival in Phnom Penh expected this morning for an official visit to Cambodia affirmed the determination of the two countries' leadership to improve the close neighbours' relations, consolidate their traditional friendship and promote their cooperation and mutual asistance, says the national paper NHAN DAN in an editorial today. The editorial further says: `The visits to Vietnam of the two Cambodian prime ministers, Mr Krom Preah Norodom Ranarridh and Mr Hun Sen, and of Cambodia's National Assembly chairman and acting head of state, Mr Chea Sim, earlier this year ushered in a new period of the two countries' relations'. `The visit of Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet indicates the consistent policy of the Vietnamese Government to solve the problems left behind by the history and accelerate the all-sided cooperation between the two countries in the new situation', notes the editorial. It also expersses the belief that his visit will bring in fine results contributing to the promotion, consolidation and development of the neighbourliness, the traditional friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
