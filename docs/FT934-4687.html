931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / International Company News: Mixed results for Indian group Grasim Industries, India's largest producer of viscose staple fibre, blamed a bumper cotton crop for a 17 per cent decline in net profits to Rs598m (Dollars 19m) in the six months to December, writes RC Murty in Bombay. Another company in the Adita Birla group, Hindalco Indutrie, a primary aluminium producer, saw profits slip 10 per cent to Rs545.7m, but a third, Indian Rayon and Industries, posted a 30 per cent rise to Rs340m.
