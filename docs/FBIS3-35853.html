Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukraine strives for equal rights and the peaceful coexistence of the nationalities living in the country, the Ukrainian foreign minister [Anatoliy Zlenko] told his Hungarian counterpart [Geza Jeszenszky] in Uzhgorod. Zlenko was of the view that parallel with the settlement of economic problems, the problems of minorities--including Hungarians--will also be settled in Ukraine. Regarding the talks, Jeszenszky said that they had discussed the extension of the two countries' economic relations and the speeding up of border traffic.
