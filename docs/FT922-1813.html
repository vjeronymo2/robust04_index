920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / French output up in April French industry increased its output by 1.5 per cent from March to April, showing an acceleration from the 1.3 per cent growth in the first quarter, William Dawkins writes from Paris. Two thirds of the rise, which came after a 0.4 per cent decline in March, was due to growth in energy output, said Insee, the state statistic institute.
