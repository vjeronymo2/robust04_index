BFN [Press release from the Scottish Office: "Latest AIDS Figures for Scotland] [Excerpt] An additional three AIDS cases were reported in Scotland during February making the cumulative total 487. The figures issued by The Scottish Office show that two further deaths from the disease were reported during February, the cumulative total therefore rises to 327. In the UK the number of reported cases of AIDS has risen to 8842 - an increase of 173 over the January 1994 figures. The total number of deaths from the disease reached 5,907 last month compared with 5,818 in January. Of the 487 reported cases in Scotland 44 per cent are related to sexual intercourse between men and 34 per cent to injecting drug use. The comparable figures for the rest of the UK are 76 per cent and three per cent, respectively. [passage on editorial notes omitted]
