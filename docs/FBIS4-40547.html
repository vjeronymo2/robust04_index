BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Borisenko] [Text] Washington, 17 June -- On Thursday the World Bank approved the granting of two loans to the Russian Federation for the development of agriculture. One of them -- for $240 million -- is intended for accelerating the process of reforms in the agricultural sector, for creating a network of information and consultation services for farmers, for help in organizing individual, highly efficient enterprises. The other -- for 80 million dollars -- should assist in the process of land reform and privatization. The loans are being granted on what are customary conditions for the World Bank, the repayment term is 17 years.
