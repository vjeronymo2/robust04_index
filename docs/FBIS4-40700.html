BFN [Text] Oleksandr Moroz, chairman of the Ukrainian Supreme Council, has spoken out against the division of the Black Sea Fleet. At a meeting with the Crimean Supreme Council deputies he proposed that the Black Sea Fleet should not be regarded as an object to be divided. According to Moroz, the Black Sea Fleet should be used jointly by Ukraine and Russia with the fleet remaining under a unified joint command, and with Russia giving orders to one part of the fleet and Ukraine to the other. There must be separate commands for parts the parts, but a joint command as well, so that the fleet should not be demolished as a fighting unit. The Black Sea is a special interest zone for Ukraine and Russia.
