BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Biryukov] [Text] Kaliningrad, 23 May -- Warships from the fleets of the Baltic countries have been invited to the first phase of the NATO "Baltops-94" joint naval maneuvers. Russia's Baltic fleet will be represented by the guided missile destroyer Neustrashimyy, whose crew will take part in working out combined sailing tasks, in demonstration submarine-hunting operations, helicopter landings of inspection groups, and search and rescue at sea. This stage will be under way from 7-10 June. More than 40 ships and combat craft, three submarines, and naval aviation from 12 countries will be taking part in combat tasks in the southern Baltic.
