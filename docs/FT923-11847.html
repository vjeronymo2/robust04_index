920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Overseas net assets dip 10% The Fleming Overseas Investment Trust saw its net asset value fall by some 10 per cent - from 239.6p to 214.8p - over the 12 months to June 30. Available revenue totalled Pounds 5.31m (Pounds 5.15m), equivalent to 4p (3.87p) per share. A same-again final dividend of 2.5p maintains the total for the year at 4p.
