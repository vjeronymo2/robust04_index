931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Bell Atlantic down slightly BELL Atlantic, the 'Baby Bell' regional telephone company, yesterday reported third-quarter net income of Dollars 378.5m, or 87 cents a share, compared with Dollars 386.6m, or 90 cents, writes Martin Dickson in New York. However, the group said with special items in the two periods stripped out, its earnings per share increased by 9.8 per cent, and net income was up 10.1 per cent. Operating revenues in the period totalled Dollars 3.18bn, up from Dollars 3.06bn. Bell Atlantic is currently making a bid for cable group Tele-Communications Inc, which could be worth more than Dollars 20bn.
