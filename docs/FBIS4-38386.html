BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, 27 May -- Principles of Russia's border policy will be set out today by Boris Yeltsin at a conference of top commanders of the Russian border troops. On the eve of the president's address the deputy commanders-in-chief of the Russian border troops and commanders of border districts and groups were presented to him. On arrival at border troops headquarters, Yeltsin said he intended to discuss with border troops the complex tasks facing the defenders of the Russian border. The president spoke of the need to organize a new system for the protection of borders with the Baltic republics, the Transcaucasus, and other states of the CIS. Yeltsin stressed that, in accordance with Russia's new constitution, it was necessary to improve the legislative basis for the activities of border forces and departments that cooperate with them.
