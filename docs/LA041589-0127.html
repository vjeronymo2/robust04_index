April 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

An article in the April 14 Business section incorrectly reported that Marukin Shoji Ltd. of Japan has completed the purchase of the Riviera Country Club. Marukin Shoji, which holds a 49% stake in a partnership that owns the Riviera Country Club and Riviera Tennis Club, intends to exercise an option to purchase the remaining 51% from Los Angeles-based LAACO Ltd. Completion of the deal is expected by Aug. 31.
