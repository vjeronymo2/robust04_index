921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Albrighton cuts losses to Pounds 1.2m The year ended March 31 1992 at Albrighton saw the completion of the business disposal programme, and the group now specialises in the quarrying, manufacture and supply of natural dimensional stone and waste disposal landfill. Turnover came to Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 23.3m) and the loss before tax to Pounds 1.21m, compared with Pounds 1.39m. Continuing activities provided Pounds 2.53m (Pounds 2.76m) of sales and Pounds 204,000 (Pounds 440,000) out of an operating profit of Pounds 399,000 (Pounds 897,000). Mr Peter Woodman, chairman, said the group was currently trading above break even, but he remained cautious about the year's results.
