920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / Management (The Growing Business): New venture for Foreign & Colonial - In a nutshell Foreign & Colonial Ventures, the venture capital arm of the Foreign & Colonial investment management group, is to take over the management of Hill Samuel Bank's development capital portfolio following the bank's decision to concentrate on its core merchant banking activities. The Hill Samuel portfolio is valued at under Pounds 20m. As part of the deal, Hill Samuel will invest in F&C's latest management buy-out and development capital fund, F&C Ventures Limited Partnership II, while F&C will gain access to investment opportunities from the bank's merchant banking operations and its branch network.
