BFN [From the "Business SNARK" file: "Transcaucasia Economic News"] [Text] YEREVAN [Dateline as received]-- On 2 May, Armenian Minister for Economy Armen Yegiazaryan signed Armenian-Austrian trade and economic treaty. Press-service of the Armenian Government told SNARK, the sides were preliminary signing the treaty on mutual foreign investments incentives and defence. The members of the Armenian delegation visited some enterprises in the capital of Austria, Vienna.
