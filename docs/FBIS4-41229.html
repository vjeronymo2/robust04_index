BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin has signed a decree on Russian servicemen as border troops taking part in work based on the international legal demarcation of the state border of the Russian Federation. This has been reported by the president's press service. In accordance with the document, border troops engaged in this work are being sent to carry out their military service to a department of the international legal demarcation of the state border, which is being set up within the Main Command of Border Troops. The decree provides for measures for the social protection of the servicemen during their transfer to the new department.
