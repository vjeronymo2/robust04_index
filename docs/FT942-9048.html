940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / Christopher first to visit Jericho US Secretary of State Warren Christopher (left), pictured yesterday with Palestinian leader Faisal Husseini, was the first world statesman to visit Jericho - seat of the new Palestinian autonomous area evacuated by Israel last week, Julian Ozanne writes. Mr Christopher's visit was clearly made to demonstrate US support for the Palestinian self-rule deal: 'One of the reasons I came here is to make it clear to our Palestinian friends that they are not alone in this endeavour and that we in the international community will stand with them as they make their historic transition.'
