Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 20 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa and Mrs. Hosokawa laid a wreath to the monument to the people's heroes on the Tiananmen Square here this morning. Among those accompanying Hosokawa on the occasion was Chen Jinhua, chairman of the Chinese Governmental Reception Committee and minister in charge of the State Planning Commission.
