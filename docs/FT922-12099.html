920422 FT 22 APR 92 / UK Company News: Keystone net asset value well down Net asset value per ordinary share of the Keystone Investment Company stood at 421.32p at March 31 1992. That compared with 461p a year earlier and with 489.13p at the end-September 1991 year-end. Net revenue for the half year to March 31 amounted to Pounds 721,513 (Pounds 873,294), equal to earnings of 5.05p (6.12p). The interim dividend is lifted to 5p (4p) and the board expects to recommend a same-again final of 10p for a 15p (14p) total.
