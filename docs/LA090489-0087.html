September 4, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

BIEDLER'S TWO GOALS LIFT TITANS IN SOCCER

Rick Biedler scored a a pair of goals in overtime to lift Cal State Fullerton to a 6-5 victory over the University of New Mexico Sunday in a nonconference soccer match at Fullerton.

Don Crevoiserat scored in the second half to put Fullerton in a 4-4 tie and force the two 10-minute overtime periods. Steve George, Travis England and Adam Lehner scored in the first half as Fullerton took a 3-2 lead. Fullerton is 2-0.

UC San Diego defeated UC Santa Barbara, 3-2, in another match Sunday at Fullerton.
