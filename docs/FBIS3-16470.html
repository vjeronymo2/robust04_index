Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 17 TASS -- Russia can join the Partnership for Peace programme at the end of March, Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev told journalists here today after his meeting with U.S. Defence Secretary William Perry. "Groups of experts are already completing work on our approach to Russia's membership of the Partnership for Peace project," the Russian minister said. "Proceeding from Russian specifics, we shall be able to join the concept by the end of this month," he explained.
