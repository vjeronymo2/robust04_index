Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] The Standing Committee of the Congress of the Union on 9 February condemned the new immigration measures adopted by the U.S. Government as well as the intensification of military surveillance along the Mexican-U.S. border. The measures adopted by the U.S. Government do not solve the illegal alien immigration problem. An agreement approved by all political parties called for, through the Human Rights and Border Issues Committees, public audiences to discuss mechanisms to promote the defense and protection of the rights of our fellow immigrant citizens.
