Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 14 (KCNA) -- Chairman of the State Educational Commission Choe Ki-yong, a special envoy of the great leader President Kim Il-song left here today to visit Egypt and Syria. He was seen off at Pyongyang Railway Station by Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Yi Won-kuk, Egyptian Ambassador to Korea Ali Hijazi and Syrian Ambassador to Korea Yasir Farah.
