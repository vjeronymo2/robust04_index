930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / UK Company News: GT Chile Growth Fund shows rise in asset value Basic net asset value per share of GT Chile Growth Fund stood at Dollars 25.39 (Dollars 30.79 or Dollars 27.54 diluted) at June 30. At August 10 the basic figure had risen to Dollars 26.33. Net income for the half year to end-June totalled Dollars 6.98m (Pounds 4.68m) compared with Dollars 4.47m. Basic earnings per share emerged at 58 cents (44 cents) and an interim dividend is 60 cents is declared. For the period of inception of the company, February 1990, to December 31 1992, a dividend of 25 cents was paid.
