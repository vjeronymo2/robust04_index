940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: British Mohair rises by 51% to Pounds 1.4m Textiles group British Mohair Holdings increased pre-tax profits by 51 per cent from Pounds 907,000 to Pounds 1.37m in the half year to June 30. Turnover rose 14 per cent to Pounds 22.6m (Pounds 19.8m) including a UK contribution of Pounds 17.2m (Pounds 14.8m). Mr Charles Fenton, chairman, foresaw a similar performance in the second half as 'interest in mohair as a unique fashion fibre' continued to increase. Earnings per share increased to 6.81p (4.48p) while the interim dividend remained at 1.4p.
