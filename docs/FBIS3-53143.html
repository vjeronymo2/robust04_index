Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of the Palestinian delegation to the Taba talks, Nabil Sha'th, says there was substantial progress on the transfer of civil powers to the autonomous authority to be set up in Gaza and Jericho. Sha'th told reporters that the two sides formulated proposals to transfer powers of 12 Israeli Civil Administration departments to the Palestinians. He felt that within two days the entire issue could be finalized. The Palestinian delegation head said also there had been some progress on security issues, but nothing was final on the question of the Jericho enclave area, and on the border crossing points. He said that the negotiations were taking place in a positive atmosphere.
