BFN [Text] Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet continues his visit to Ukraine. This morning he met Oleksandr Tkachenko, first deputy chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council. During the conversation, Tkachenko told Vo Van Kiet about the newly elected Supreme Council and its activities. They exchanged views on the future development of relations between the two countries. Also today the Vietnamese prime minister is planning to meet Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk. The high-ranking Vietnamese guest will visit the Kiev-Pechersk state historic and cultural preserve and Leninska Kuznya production association. He will also make a tour of the city.
