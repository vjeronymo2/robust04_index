October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH JANICE;

THE REMATCH

Mysterious Pirate, a chalk starter and a show finisher in the sixth, cost me my largest loss of the meet. I will try to rebound today, betting $50 to win and place on Franny Frizzle in the seventh.

Janice Goldinger's Thu. Bankroll: $1,949

Thursday's Loss: $150

Current Bankroll: $1,799

SURELOCK lost $100 when Past Remembered failed to win the eighth. However, his other two exacta horses in the race, Bequest and Girl Of France netted him $60 to cut his losses to $40 for the day. His selection today is $200 to win on Formidable Lady in the eighth.

SURELOCK's Thu. Bankroll: $1,520

Thursday's Loss: $40

Current Bankroll: $1,480

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.

Both players started with a $2,000 bankroll.
