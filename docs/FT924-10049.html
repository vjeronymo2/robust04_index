921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / World Trade News: French double-decker train deal A consortium led by GEC Alsthom, the Anglo-French engineering group, has been awarded a Ecu660m (Pounds 528.6m) order from French Railways (SNCF) and the Paris Transport Authority to supply 70 double-decker trains, GEC Alsthom said yesterday. The consortium includes ANf Industrie, the French rail rolling stock manufacturer. The order includes an option for 73 extra trains, bringing the total contract value to around Ecu1.3bn.
