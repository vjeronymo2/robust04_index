BFN [Text] Seoul, April 12 (YONHAP) -- U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry is scheduled to arrive in Seoul on the afternoon of April 17 for an official visit, the Defense Ministry reported Tuesday. During his three-day stay in Seoul, Secretary Perry will pay a courtesy call on President Kim Yong-sam and confer with Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu and Defense Minister Yi Pyong-tae on steps the two countries could take jointly to cope with the North Korean nuclear question. Perry, who is coming to Korea via Hawaii, will also visit U.S. and South Korean military installations before leaving the country on the afternoon of next Tuesday.
