940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / International Company News: Santos blames weak prices for decline Santos, the Adelaide-based energy group which recently bought a one-third interest in the Anglia gas field in the North Sea, reported an 18.2 per cent fall in operating profits after tax and abnormals in the six months to the end of June. Santos made ADollars 101.3m (USDollars 75.6m), compared with ADollars 123.9m previously, after an abnormal surplus of ADollars 37.6m, against ADollars 33.7m. Sales were ADollars 312.8m, against ADollars 353.3m. It blamed the downturn in profits on lower world liquids prices and the strengthening of the Australian dollar.
