930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Marshalls disposal for at least Pounds 5m Marshalls, the building materials and engineering group, has sold a fully-worked part of its Howley Park quarry to Biffa Waste Services, a wholly owned subsidiary of Severn Trent, for a minimum Pounds 5m. An initial Pounds 600,000 has already been paid and further payments of Pounds 500,000 are due in stages with a substantial final instalment in May 1997. The disposal will result in an exceptional profit to Marshalls of at least Pounds 2.9m for the year to March 31 1993, when the company expects profits to approach last year's Pounds 12.5m.
