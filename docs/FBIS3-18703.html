Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chile is to buy 20 Mirage fighter planes from Belgium. This was confirmed by the Chilean defense minister. The 20 planes will replace the Chilean British planes which date from the fifties. It's not known exactly how much the Chileans are paying for the planes, but an amount of three billion francs was mentioned earlier. The planes are up for sale as a result of the cutbacks in the Belgian Army. The planes were previously refurbished.
