931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Helene advances 20% to Pounds 975,000 Helene, the fashion wear company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 975,000 for the six months to June 30, a 20 per cent rise on the comparable Pounds 812,000. Turnover advanced 25 per cent from Pounds 36.7m to Pounds 45.9m. A satisfactory result for the year was seen with sales for July and August having been above the same period last year. Earnings per share were 0.56p (0.68p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.65p.
