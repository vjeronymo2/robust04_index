Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] With the aim of simplifying foreign travel procedures for Republic of Uzbekistan citizens, and in line with the decree by Islam Karimov, the president of Uzbekistan, on measures for further deepening economic reforms, providing for the protection of private property, and the development of entrepreneurship from 21 January of the current year, the Cabinet of Ministers adopted a decision on procedures for foreign travel by Republic of Uzbekistan citizens. The temporary procedures for foreign travel by Uzbekistan citizens will be published in the press.
