Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Eight members of Equatorial Guinea's opposition party, the Popular Union, have been detained for a week now in (Edipgin), not far from the Gabonese border. According to a communique issued by the party this morning in Libreville, the arrests are part of a strategy established by the Government of Equatorial Guinea to close down all the offices of opposition parties which boycotted the recent 21 December legislative elections.
