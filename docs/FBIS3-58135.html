Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed GGD (SVD): "Last Blue Helmets Return From Somalia"; as released by Brussels BELGA Database] [Excerpts] Melsbroek, 5 Jan (BELGA) -- On Wednesday [5 January], the last Belgian blue helmets arrived home from Somalia, whence they departed on Monday. [passage omitted] The 50 soldiers who returned on Wednesday constituted the rear guard of the Belgian contingent of the UN Operation in Somalia (UNOSOM II). They left Kismaayo (in the south of Somalia) on Monday, ending more than a year of Belgian military presence in the country. [passage omitted] The 20 Belgian soldiers still at UNOSOM headquarters in the Somali capital of Mogadishu are to return to Belgium by the end of January, a military source indicated.
