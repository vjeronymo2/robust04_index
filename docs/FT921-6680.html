920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: TR High Income net assets up 9.5% The net asset value of TR High Income Trust rose 9.5 per cent - from 86.5p to 94.7p - over the 12 months to December. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 1.28m (Pounds 957,000) for earnings of 6.21p (6.23p) per share. A fifth interim dividend of 0.8p maintains the total for the year at 6p on the increased capital following the rights issue last June.
