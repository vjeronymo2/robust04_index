920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Essex Furniture Despite the recessionary climate, USM-quoted Essex Furniture achieved a 17 per cent advance in pre-tax profit for the half-year ended December 31 1991. The profit was up from Pounds 320,000 to Pounds 375,000, on turnover nearly 48 per cent higher at Pounds 3.23m. Earnings came to 2.63p (2.32p) and the interim dividend is raised to 1.25p (1p).
