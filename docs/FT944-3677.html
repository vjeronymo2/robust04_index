941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Scudder Lat Amer Scudder Latin American Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 99.66p at October 31. The trust, which was incorporated in January and began trading in June, had net revenue of Pounds 50,744 for the period. Earnings per share emerged at 0.1p. The trust is marketed only to institutions. It is the first UK trust of Scudder Stevens Clark, the US fund management company.
