BFN [Text] In a news conference today in Havana, (Reibor Itohuaine), charge d'affaires of the Congolese Embassy in our country, stressed the 30 years of diplomatic relations between Cuba and Congo. The Congolese diplomat said Cuba and Congo have developed excellent relations of friendship and cooperation over a period of 30 years, and that they are united by history and blood. He also referred to cooperation between the two countries, which encompasses almost all areas of political, social, and economic life. He added that the next visit to Cuba by Congolese President Pascal Lissouba will enhance the development of relations for the next few years.
