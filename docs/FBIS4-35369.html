BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin revealed today that the movements of Jibril al-Rujub, head of the PLO's security apparatus in Jericho, have been restricted and that he has been barred from leaving the city in the wake of his recent pronouncements. During a visit to the Bedouin town of Lagiya in the Negev last Friday, al-Rujub stated that the autonomy was the first phase on the road to the establishment of a Palestinian state with Jerusalem as its capital, and that the Palestinians would not cease their struggle until Jerusalem has been liberated. Al-Rujub also called for the continuation of the armed struggle from within the boundaries of the autonomy.
