930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / International Company News: State Bank of NSW in loss STATE Bank of New South Wales, the Australian public sector regional bank, has fallen into the red. After abnormals, the bank turned a first-half ADollars 10.6m profit into an ADollars 89.6m (USDollars 62.4m) loss. This reflected a ADollars 95.9m abnormal loss mainly due to tax changes. Before abnormals and tax, the bank turned an ADollars 15.5m profit into a ADollars 20.3m loss following a 46 per cent rise in doubtful debt charges to ADollars 109.8m. A tax credit of ADollars 26.6m put the bank ADollars 6.3m in the black before abnormals.
