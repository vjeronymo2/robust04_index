BFN [Text] The 14th session of the Kyrgyz parliament's (?12th) convocation opened today at the government house in Bishkek. It was chaired by Sherimkulov. First, the deputies paid their tribute to the victims of natural calamities. The deputies elected a secretariat consisting of 11 people. The term of office of deputies Fomenko, Yezhov, Sergeyev, and others has been stopped because they have left the Republic. Altogether 323 deputies attended the work of the session, and it intends to examine 20 issues. Deputies Kasymbekov, Saparov, Prime Minister Dzhumagulov, and others spoke in favor of the Euroasian Union and further expansion of economic and political cooperation between the republics of Central Asia. Also under discussion was the need for further deepening agrarian reforms.
