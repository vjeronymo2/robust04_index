BFN [Text] The Cuban congressional delegation that is visiting our country met on 22 June with Chamber of Deputies President Francisco Jose De Vargas and congressional Public Health Committee members. The delegation of representatives from the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP] is made up of Carlos Dotres Martinez and Sergio Pastrana, chairman and secretary of the ANPP Health, Sport, and Environment Committee, respectively. They are being accompanied by Manuel Samora, a Central Committee International Department member. During the meeting the legislators discussed strengthening links between legislators from the two nations through an exchange of experiences. The Cuban legislators recalled that last year a delegation of National Congress Health Committee members visited Havana, adding that they had an opportunity to personally get acquainted with the progress achieved in this field by Fidel Castro's administration. At the end of the meeting with Deputy De Vargas the Cuban delegation visited the Clinic Hospital and then met Vice President Angel Roberto Seifart. The Cuban delegation also will visit historic and tourist attractions. The delegation will stay in the country until 26 June.
