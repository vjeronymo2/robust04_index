Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, January 5 (SNARK)--Abkhazia surely will return to the body of Georgia said Eduard Shevardnadze during a constituent meeting of the regional organization the Union of Georgia's citizens. On his words it is must take place by political way. However, if it doesn't happen he, the head of the state, personally, will head the "march of peace" and cross Abkhazia from Inguri river to Psoe river. As SNARK reporter informs seemingly under "march of peace" Shevarnadze means force methods. It is not accidental he also appeal for pressing formation of Georgian Army.
