BFN [Text] Being guided by the Lithuanian Constitution and taking into consideration the presentation by Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius, President Algirdas Brazauskas appointed in his edicts six ministers today: Mindaugas Laurynas Stankevicius was appointed minister of administration reforms and municipal affairs, releasing him of his duties as minister of social insurance. The edict comes into force on 1 July. Bronius Brazauskas was appointed minister of the protection of environment. The edict comes into force on 15 June. Vladislovas Domarkas was appointed minister of education and science. Dainius Trinkunas -- minister of culture; Albertas Vasiliauskas -- minister of forestry; Aleksandras Vasiliauskas -- minister of economy. The edicts as regards these ministers come into force today [10 June].
