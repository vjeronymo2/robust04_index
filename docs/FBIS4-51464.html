BFN [Text] Macao, May 24 (XINHUA) -- The Macao Tourism Department and 16 local enterprises including 13 hotels and three travel agencies are going to take part in the '94 International Tourism Fair to be held in Hong Kong from May 26 to 29. There will be representatives from 52 countries and regions worldwide present at the fair. Macao's local band "Tuna Macaense" will perform at the fair on May 26 and 27, while representatives from the Macao Tourism Department, media organizations, travel agencies and those from other countries and regions will attend an evening party -- the night of Macao, on the May 27 evening. It will be the ninth time that Macao participates in this international tourism fair and it won a best performance award in 1993. In recent years, with the fast development of local economy, Macao's tourism industry has also developed rapidly. In 1993, Macao registered a total of 7.8 million visits by tourists.
