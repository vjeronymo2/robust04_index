930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / International Company News: Robert Fleming teams up with SA broker MARTIN & Co, South Africa's top stockbroking firm, is teaming up with Robert Fleming, the UK merchant bank, in a partnership aimed at developing their international mining brokerage business. Robert Fleming will buy from McIntosh Securities, a listed Australian broker, its half-stake in McIntosh Martin, a joint venture with Martin & Co. When the deal is completed, McIntosh Martin, which trades in South African securities held by non-residents, will be renamed Copthall Martin. Robert Fleming intends supporting Martin & Co in widening the scope of the joint venture, particularly in the corporate finance area. Mr Winston Floquet, managing director of Martin & Co, said: 'We are delighted to be involved in a firm of Flemings' standing. 'What particularly attracted us was its global network, its roots in the investment business, and an affinity with its people and culture.'
