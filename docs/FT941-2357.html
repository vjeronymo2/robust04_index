940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): IBM - Other companies IBM chairman and chief executive for the past year Mr Lou Gerstner, is expected finally to reveal his strategies for IBM's recovery during an meeting with analysts in New York on Thursday. Industry watchers hope to hear Gerstner map out IBM's recovery, including details of how the company aims to halt the decline of its mainframe computer and data storage products revenues, boost personal computer profits and address new markets.
