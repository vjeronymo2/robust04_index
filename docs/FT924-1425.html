921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Russian space deals for US Russia's main space launcher maker has won permission to sign commercial contracts with Motorola and Lockheed of the US, writes Daniel Green. The Interfax news agency said Mr Viktor Chernomyrdin, Russia's new prime minister, had agreed that the Khrunichev factory sign the contracts. The deal with Motorola would be for the launch of three communications satellites, while a joint venture would be set up with Lockheed. Lockheed said yesterday it was continuing talks with several Russian companies.
