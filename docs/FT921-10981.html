920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / Attali suggests G8 THE Group of Seven leading industrial countries should become a Group of Eight, with Russia as a fully fledged member, Mr Jacques Attali, president of the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, said last night, writes Peter Norman, Economics Correspondent. Mr Attali said in London that it was essential for Russia to become directly involved in the G7 as its future was crucial to the world economy. Looking further ahead, Mr Attali said the G8 should merge with the UN Security Council to take better account of third world needs.
