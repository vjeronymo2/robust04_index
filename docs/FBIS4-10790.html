BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro has expressed the willingness of Cuba's people and government to expand and strengthen their ties of friendship and cooperation with Zimbabwe. On 15 April, Castro, who is president of the Councils of State and of Ministers, sent a letter of congratulations to Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe on the occasion of the 14th anniversary of Zimbabwe's independence, which is observed on 18 April. The letter points out that the Cuban people admire the enormous efforts made by the fraternal nation of Zimbabwe to promote the well-being of its people, who face resolutely every difficulty obstructing their economic and social development.
