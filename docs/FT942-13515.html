940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Start agreed for Pounds 200m retail site Work on a retail village in Southampton, the first phase of a Pounds 200m project, is to begin later this year. The city council said it and Imry Holdings had reached legal, financial and planning agreements for the beginning of the development. A detailed planning application is to be made for the 150,000 sq ft village on the Esplanade site. Work on a main shopping centre is expected to begin in 1996. The development is designed to include a 500,000 sq ft covered shopping centre, a 92,500 sq ft business park, and a concert arena.
