Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation in south Lebanon is worrying. We are receiving reports every five minutes of Israeli warplanes overflying the region. We received a report 10 minutes ago that Israeli warplanes are raiding the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights after intensive reconnaissance sorties throughout the night and into the morning over al-Nabatiyah, Iqlim al-Tuffah, western al-Biqa', al-Luwayzah, Jarju', and the central sector. We received a report two hours ago that the Israeli bombardment of villages in the south intensified and continued until 1300, targeting the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights, Jarju', 'Ayn Bu Siwar, and Arab Salim. At about 1330, Israeli warplanes flew intensively at low altitude over Iqlim al-Tuffah and al-Nabatiyah, staging mock raids. The Lebanese Army confronted the aircraft with anti-aircraft guns. Iqlim al-Tuffah areas and al-Luwayzah came under artillery barrage at 1100 from the South Lebanese Army [SLA] and Israeli positions in al-Zaffatah. Resistance men fired at the sources. Israeli aircraft reconnoitered the region up to east Sidon and al-Nabatiyah, breaking the sound barrier. Israeli warplanes flew low over western al-Biqa' at about 1100, again breaking the sound barrier over al-Nabatiyah. The resistance men this morning attacked an SLA position in Sujud with rockets and mortar and clashes ensued in which all types of weapons were used. At 1350 today, Israeli warplanes raided the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights, concentrating on al-Luwayzah, for the second time today. Just in from the south, Israeli military aircraft are flying low over the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights and Lebanese Army ground anti- aircraft guns are confronting them. The resistance fired surface- to-air Strella missiles at the Israeli aircraft, causing panic in the region and making the people believe the Israeli aircraft had launched a new raid.
