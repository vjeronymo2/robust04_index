Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Knesset Member Yosi Katz today received a letter from Yasir 'Arafat in which he expresses the hope that all the forces working for peace in Israel will participate in adopting practical measures to block the activities of the settlers, something that will make it possible to quickly implement the Declaration of Principles agreement. Our correspondent Yo'el Nir reports that 'Arafat sent the letter in response to a cable from Knesset Member Katz in which he called on him to demonstrate leadership and overcome those who are trying to torpedo the peace process.
