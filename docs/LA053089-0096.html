May 30, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA;

POLICE SEEK IDENTITY OF DEAD MAN LEFT IN ALLEY

Police said Monday that they still have not identified a man who was apparently shot to death, then dumped in an alley in the 1100 block of S. Raitt Street.

Officers said they had received a report of a man down in the alley about 7:15 p.m. The victim's only apparent wound was a single gunshot to the head, police said.

Police said the victim appears to be a male Caucasian, 16 to 19 years old, 5 feet, 7 inches, about 135 pounds, with short brown hair and light brown-hazel eyes.

Anyone who can help identify the victim is asked to contact Santa Ana police.
