Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Twelve Palestinians were wounded today in violent clashes with Israel Defense Forces soldiers throughout the territories. In the Jabalyah refugee camp, dozens of youngsters threw stones at soldiers. Palestinian sources in the Gaza Strip reported that three demonstrators were wounded by the soldiers' gunfire. A general strike, called by Islamic Jihad, was observed today in the territories. A quiet demonstration was staged today in the Christian town of Bayt Sahur, near Bethlehem, to protest the massacre at the Cave of the Patriarchs. When the demonstration ended, several youngsters set tires on fire and threw stones at soldiers.
