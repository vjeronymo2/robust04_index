921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / Chinese Prime Minster talks to Vietnamese counterpart in Hanoi Li Peng (left), the first Chinese prime minister to visit Hanoi in 21 years, talks with Vo Van Kiet, his Vietnamese counterpart in Hanoi yesterday. Li tried to calm Vietnamese fears by insisting Beijing had no expansionist plans in the region. The two countries normalised relations in November 1991 after more than a decade of hostility.
