BFN [By BUSINESS-TASS correspondent Oleg Deyneko] [Text] Moscow May 11 TASS -- The number of officially registered unemployed has gone up in Russia by 250,000 people since the beginning of the year, BUSINESS-TASS learned from the Russian Economics Ministry on Wednesday. A total of 1.4 million jobless people have applied for help at the employment service. 1.1 million of them were registered as unemployed and 80 per cent of them receive the dole. The number of people who have been unemployed for more than four months running comprises 56 per cent of the total, according to the ministry. The figure was 42 per cent in the first quarter of 1993.
