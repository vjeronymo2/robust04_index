BFN [Text] The brother leader of the revolution met this evening with Burkina Faso President Blaise Campaore, Malian President Alpha Oumar Konare, and the Niger President Mahamane Ousmane. The meeting was attended by the brother assistant secretary of the General People's Congress and the brother secretary of the general People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation and the foreign ministers of Burkina, Mali, and Niger. Among the issues of common concern raised in tonight's talks were aspects of cooperation in various fields and developments in Africa.
