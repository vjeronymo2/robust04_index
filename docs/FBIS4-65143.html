BFN [Text] Istanbul, 11 May (EFE) -- In a brief visit on his way to Turkmenistan, Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella today termed Turkey as a "bridge that leads to the Turkish countries of the old USSR." Di Tella arrived at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport from South Africa. On 12 May he will travel to Turkmenistan as part of a tour through the old USSR that will include a visit to Moscow between 14 and 17 May. After reminding reporters waiting for him that he had already visited Turkey with Argentine President Carlos Menem, Di Tella stressed that Buenos Aires is closely following events in this country.
