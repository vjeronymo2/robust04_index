BFN [Text] Iran says it fully supports India's stand that social issues like labor should not be used to dilute the multilateral GATT treaty. The Iranian labor minister, Mr. Hoseyn Kamali, conveyed this to the labor minister, Mr. P.A. Sangma, during their meeting in New Delhi. They also discussed the forthcoming labor ministers conference of nonaligned countries where this issue will be taken up for discussion. The two sides reviewed issues connected with the employment of workers in each other's countries.
