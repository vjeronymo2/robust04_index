BFN [Text] Minister to the Prime Minister's Office Sawit Phothiwihok is leading a 20-member delegation to Myanmar [Burma] during 5 and 8 May for talks on energy cooperation. Minister Sawit will negotiate the purchasing price of Myanmar natural gas to be supplied to the Petroleum Authority of Thailand from Myanmar's (Yedana) field in the Gulf of Martaban. Discussion with the Myanmar side will also touch on bilateral cooperation in tourism, investment, and water source development. The Thai delegation will call on Myanmar leaders and meet Thai investors there. Meanwhile, the Myanmar Government this week released 30 Thais mainly fishermen jailed in that country. The ceremony to free the Thais was held at the Victoria Point and in the presence of the Thai delegation. The release was made with the good relations between Thailand and Myanmar taken into account.
