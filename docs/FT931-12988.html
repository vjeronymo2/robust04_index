930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / Engineering plant to shed 50 workers PALLION ENGINEERING, which took over Sunderland's last shipyard as a base for heavy fabrication, is making half its 100 employees redundant, blaming the recession. The company also confirmed that Mr Bob Clay, the chief executive, had resigned. Mr Clay was Labour MP for Sunderland North when he led the campaign to save Sunderland's last shipyards. He resigned his seat before the last election.
