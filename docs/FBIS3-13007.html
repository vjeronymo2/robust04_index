Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Within the framework of national reconciliation plan in Tajikistan, the first round of talks between the leaders of Tajikistan's Islamic and national groups opposed to the present government in Tajikistan and the first deputy foreign minister of Russia, Anatoliy Adamishin, began in Tehran today. During these talks the leaders of the opposition groups explained their views on ways of restoring tranquillity and security in that country and stressed the need for establishment of democracy by involving all the forces of Tajikistan in running the country's affairs. These talks are taking place with approval of the present government in Tajikistan, UN support, and cooperation of the Islamic Republic of Iran [IRI]. The IRI, which is striving to restore peace in combination with tranquillity and stability in Tajikistan, stressed from the beginning that the military option cannot solve this country's political and economic problems; and the only way of resolving Tajikistan's difficulties is establishment of national reconciliation government.
