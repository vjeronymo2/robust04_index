Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tandil, 17 Jan (NA) -- Juan Carlos Pugliese, a former Chamber of Deputies president, died today at 0900 in Tandil, his home town, where he lived since giving up public functions. Pugliese was admitted to a local hospital by mid-week last week. He was the president of the Chamber of Deputies from the reestablishment of democracy in 1983 to 1989 when he left to become economy minister during the final stretch of the Raul Alfonsin administration.
