941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Orbis disposals Orbis has announced a return to profits and the sale of the manufacturing businesses of Hilclare. First Security (Guards), acquired in June, contributed Pounds 2.9m to turnover of Pounds 4m (Pounds 1.1m) and Pounds 296,000 to operating profits, helping to offset the Pounds 79,000 loss from the Hilclare businesses. Group pre-tax profits were Pounds 182,000 (Pounds 45,000 losses) for the six months to September 30. Orbis is building its security and related services and withdrawing from the manufacture of warning beacons, loop alarms and lighting products. These are being sold to their management for Pounds 837,000. An interim dividend of 0.125p is being paid from earnings per share of 0.62p (0.9p losses).
