BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Text] The CPC delegation headed by Wu Banggou, Politburo member and CPC secretary in Shanghai, has visited Cienfuegos Province. The delegation was greeted at the local government's headquarters by Manuel Menendez Castellano, member of the Central Committee and first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] in the province, and other provincial authorities. The delegation toured the Camilo Cienfuegos Refinery during a visit to the industrial park. The refinery is nearly completed and has already been producing for the last three years. The plant could benefit from an investment that would increase its efficiency. The delegation also visited the Juragua Nuclear Plant, where they were told that construction is temporarily halted but that the facility has been preserved. The delegation was also informed that negotiations recently got underway to form an economic association that will make it possible to complete the plant and start operations. The delegation also met higher education teachers and students in the province. The delegation will visit Matanzas Province on 11 June and is scheduled to depart Cuba on 12 June. This is the highest ranking PRC delegation to visit Cuba since Chinese President Jiang Zemin's visit.
