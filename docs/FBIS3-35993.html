Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ratification of the Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of Nuclear Weapons [NPT] will be the responsibility of the new Ukrainian parliament, Parliamentary Chairman Ivan Plyushch said. First of all, "we will have to see how the trilateral statement by the presidents of Ukraine, Russia and the United States and the supplement to it are implemented," Plyushch told reporters in Kiev on Tuesday.
