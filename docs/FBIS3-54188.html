Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] On the second day of his visit to Washington, His Majesty King Husayn met with U.S. Vice President Al Gore. During the meeting, they discussed the friendly bilateral relations and the need to enhance them to reach the highest levels of cooperation in all fields. The U.S. vice president welcomed His Majesty King Husayn to the United States, stressing the deep historical relations between the two countries. He also praised his majesty's ceaseless efforts to achieve peace in the Middle East. For his part, U.S. President Clinton praised Jordan's democratic march and appreciated his majesty's efforts to support and entrench this march. The U.S. vice president and his wife hosted a dinner banquet yesterday evening in the honor of His Majesty King Husayn and Her Majesty Queen Nur.
