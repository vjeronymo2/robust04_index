920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Barlo moves back into the black INCREASED turnover and a cut in operating costs produced a turnround at Barlo Group, the Dublin-based radiator manufacturer, following two years of losses. For the year ended March 31 1992 the company made a pre-tax profit of IPounds 1.88m, or Pounds 1.72m, compared with a deficit of IPounds 1.89m in 1991. Turnover was IPounds 14.7m (IPounds 13m) while operating costs were reduced to IPounds 13.2m (IPounds 13.4m). The profit was boosted by an exceptional credit of IPounds 454,000 (loss IPounds 981,000) and a Pounds 454,000 cut in interest charges. Earnings per share were 3p (losses 3.73p).
