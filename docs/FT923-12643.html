920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / Israel-Efta free trade pact Israel and the seven members of the European Free Trade Association (Efta) yesterday reached a trade agreement after 13 months of negotiation, writes Frances Williams in Geneva. The agreement will be signed later this year and is expected to come into effect on January 1, 1993. Customs duties and other trade barriers will be abolished for industrial and processed agricultural goods, fish and other marine products. Israel has also negotiated better terms for trade in farm products.
