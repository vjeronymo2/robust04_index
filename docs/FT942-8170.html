940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Long-term with-profits values rise - At a glance Money Marketing's annual survey of with-profits products shows that maturity values of long-term policies are moving upwards, while short-term returns continue to plummet. The highest payout on a 10 year policy (for a man aged 29 paying Pounds 35 a month) was from Royal London at Pounds 8,427, with the Tunbridge Wells in second place, followed by Equitable Life. The lowest payout was from Guardian, with a return of Pounds 5,786.
