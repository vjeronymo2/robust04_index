Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 27 (XINHUA) -- Secretary General of the Government of the Republic of Chile, Enrique Correa Rios, will pay a goodwill visit to China from January 31 to February 5, at the invitation of the government of People's Republic of China. Wu Jianmin, spokesman for the Chinese Foreign Ministry, made the announcement at a weekly press conference here this afternoon.
