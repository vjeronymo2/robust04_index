930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: Bundesbank blow to optimists - At a glance The Bundesbank kept its Lombard and discount rates unchanged this week, disappointing those optimists hoping for a cut. The French franc came under downward pressure after the Bundesbank decision; no doubt a matter for discussion between German Chancellor Helmut Kohl and French premier Edouard Balladur, who were meeting this week. On Monday, France cut its overnight interest rates.
