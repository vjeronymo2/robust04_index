940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves Among computer services groups, Micro Focus jumped 42 to 1025p ahead of preliminary figures expected on Thursday. Admiral, scheduled to report this morning, raced up 22 to a record 515p. The market's two debut stocks performed well, Radstone closing at 131p compared with the 125p placing price, and Finelist ending the session at 150p, against a placing level of 130p.
