Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. Ambassador to Lebanon Mark Hambley said after a meeting with Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri today that the U.S.-Syrian summit in Geneva will deal with the peace process, including matters of concern to Lebanon and Israel and Syria and Israel. Key issues affecting developments in the region, as well as American-Syrian relations will also be discussed in the summit, he said. He said he cannot definitely say that Lebanon will be discussed in the summit as an independent topic.
