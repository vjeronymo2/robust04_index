April 9, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST : SAN DIEGO

A 17-year-old boy was stabbed three times Sunday morning on the beach near the Bahia Resort Hotel on Mission Bay, police said.

The victim apparently was in a crowd when he was stabbed, and there are no suspects, police said. The victim, who lives in San Diego, was taken to Sharp Memorial Hospital, where he was listed in good condition.
