BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 13 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- According to the newspaper "EXPRESSTIME" citing the reliable sources, the other day two officials of the foreign ties department of the Communist Party of China have visited Mongolia at the invitation of the ruling Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party (MPRP). The MPRP has not so far informed about their arrival and stay here. However one of the independent newspapers of Mongolia is planning to make public the stay of party functioners from the Communist Party of China in the country, publicating their names in one of its nearest issues.
