931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Yorklyde rises 9% to Pounds 1.49m Yorklyde, the Huddersfield-based maker of fine cloths, scarves and rugs, lifted pre-tax profits by 9 per cent, from Pounds 1.37m to Pounds 1.49m, in the half year to July 31. The shares rose 29p to 209p. Turnover moved ahead to Pounds 7.87m (Pounds 7.37m) and operating profits rose from Pounds 1.29m to Pounds 1.44m. Other income dropped to Pounds 49,000 (Pounds 78,000). Earnings advanced to 10.2p (9.4p) and partly to reduce disparity the interim dividend is lifted to 2.2p (2p adjusted).
