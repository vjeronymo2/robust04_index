BFN [Text] Radomir Bogdanovic, deputy foreign minister of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia [FRY], arrived in Ukraine today on a working visit. He said that during the talks the sides exchanged views on the development of Ukrainian-Yugoslav relations and priorities in the two state's foreign policy efforts. They also discussed topical issues concerning the Yugoslav crisis. He added that Yugoslav people highly appreciated Ukraine's efforts to alleviate the situation in Yugoslavia.
