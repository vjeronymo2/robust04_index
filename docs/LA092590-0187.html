September 25, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

SAJAK, BROWN BECOME PARENTS

Fashion model Lesly Brown, the wife of "Wheel of Fortune" host Pat Sajak, has given birth to a boy.

Patrick Michael James Sajak weighed 7 pounds at birth Saturday at an undisclosed Los Angeles-area hospital, Sajak spokesman Bob Burton said Monday.

It was the couple's first child. Sajak, 43, and Brown, 25, were married in January.
