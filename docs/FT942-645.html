940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Clayhithe recovers to Pounds 1.06m Despite a Pounds 1.2m provision to cover projected costs of restructuring, Clayhithe, the finance and management provider, returned to profits with Pounds 1.06m pre-tax for the year ended March 31, against losses of Pounds 366,000. The provision, for engineering activities at Aylesbury, Bucks, compared with Pounds 500,000 last time. Turnover eased slightly to Pounds 35.5m (Pounds 35.9m) with continuing operations contributing Pounds 28.3m (Pounds 27.8m). Losses per share were 0.8p (4.4p) basic; fully diluted, earnings improved from 0.2p to 1.6p. A final dividend of 1.875p lifts the total to 2.625p.
