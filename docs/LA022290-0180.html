February 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The Orange County International Auto Show Grand Opening held at Anaheim Stadium on Feb. 16 raised an estimated $41,000 for the Cinderella Guild of Childrens Hospital of Orange County.

Sonia Ike was chairwoman.

The "Gala Mardi Gras" dinner held at the Santa Ana Country Club on Feb. 17 raised an estimated $7,000 for the Con Brio chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performing Arts Center. Shirley Hoesterey was chairman.
