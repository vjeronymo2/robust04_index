940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Hint of recovery in construction sector - Other companies The interim results season for construction and building materials companies kicks off in earnest on Wednesday when half-year figures are due from housebuilder Persimmon and Rugby Group, the cement company. The gloss has gone off construction sector shares during the past 18 months. But Persimmon is expected to have increased first-half profits to about Pounds 14m (Pounds 9.7m), while some analysts reckon Rugby will have raised profits from Pounds 30.2m to about Pounds 35m.
