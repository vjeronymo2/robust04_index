BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and chief of the Palestinian delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations to implement the Gaza-Jericho First agreement, has stated that today, the Phrasing Committee virtually completed the timetable for the entry of the police force to Gaza and Jericho and the details concerning its number, its jurisdiction, and powers. Speaking to reporters following the working sessions that ended at 1930, Sha'th said: We are awaiting the Palestinian and Israeli leaderships' approval of some articles. Commenting on reports of disagreements between the Israelis and Palestinians concerning the Palestinian police force, he said: I do not believe there are problems. We are talking about working out some details later on. Sha'th said that no final agreement has been reached yet and that the two sides are awaiting the Palestinian and Israeli leaderships' approval of what has been reached. He added that negotiations will continue on the timetable for the entry of the police force, the nature of the force, and its armament. The Palestinian and Israeli negotiators will resume their meetings in an hour.
