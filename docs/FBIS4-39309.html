BFN [Leonid Pozdeyev report: "Americans Buy Rockets in the Urals"] [Text] Following an exhibition of Russian technologies in the United States, the U.S. firm Douglas has concluded a contract with Yekaterinburg's "Novator" design bureau. The Urals firm has pledged to ship 2,000 rockets to the United States. These rockets are not going to join the U.S. Army's arsenal but will indirectly help enhance its combat might. The thing is that specialists from Douglas intend to use the Russian rockets as targets when testing new arms.
