930420 FT 20 APR 93 / EC urges Baltic withdrawal The European Community said yesterday that Russian charges of human rights abuses in the Baltic states were so far unproven and urged Moscow to resume withdrawal of an estimated 43,000 of its troops, Reuter reports from Copenhagen. President Yeltsin said two weeks ago that the Russian minorities in Estonia and Latvia were being persecuted and linked the troop withdrawal to an end to human rights abuses.
