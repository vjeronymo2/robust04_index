April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PRO-CHOICE MARCHERS

As a participant in the march in Washington for women's equality and reproductive rights, I would like to commend The Times for its coverage and editorial position.

However, the article on lobbying members of Congress omitted much of what actually occurred that day.

I was a member of the delegation from Los Angeles that had a scheduled and confirmed appointment with both Sens. Cranston and Wilson. Wilson did not show up for this meeting, but sent an aide to represent his position on the issues. Cranston not only met with the entire 200-plus members of the delegation, he stayed for half an hour to answer all questions.

LYNDA PALEVSKY

Santa Monica
