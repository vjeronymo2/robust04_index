930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: Pounds 53.89m for investment trusts -At a glance A record Pounds 53.89m was invested in investment trust savings schemes during the second quarter of 1993. The total invested in the first half of 1993 was Pounds 102m, compared to Pounds 58.6m in the first half of last year, and Pounds 115.5m in the whole of last year. There are now 62,831 regular savers. The average monthly investment is Pounds 75, and the average lump sum investment is Pounds 1,809.
