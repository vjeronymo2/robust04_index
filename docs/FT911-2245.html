910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK Company News: S&U improves 21% to Pounds 3.43m Shares of S&U Stores, the consumer credit, TV rental and hosiery maker, yesterday rose 12p to 138p after Mr Derek Coombs, chairman, unveiled a 21 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits to Pounds 3.43m for the year to end-January. The advance, from the previous year's Pounds 2.83m which included Pounds 343,000 from property sales compared with only Pounds 24,000 this time, was on turnover of Pounds 46.3m (Pounds 43.6m). A proposed final dividend of 6p raises the total by 2p to 8p. Earnings emerged at 21.53p (17.8p) per share.
