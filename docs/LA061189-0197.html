June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: SING SOFT, SING LOUD SCENES FROM TWO LIVES BY PATRICIA MCCONNEL (ATHENEUM: $18.95; 246 PP.)

"This is a book about jail, the women who live there, how they got there, how time passes for them, and how they survive. Extraordinary, heart- breaking, and yes, everybody should read it." Carolyn See
