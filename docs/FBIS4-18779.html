BFN [Text] Salvadoran Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Salaverria arrived at midday today at Madrid-Barajas international airport and will be received in the next few hours by his Spanish counterpart number, Javier Solana. In these talks they will deal with questions relating to the peace process and the holding of the second part of the general elections in El Salvador on 27 April. However, it is possible that in the talks they will discuss the documents published by the daily THE WASHINGTON POST indicating that the U.S. Administration knew of the involvement of the Salvadoran Army in the murder of Spanish Jesuits in November 1989.
