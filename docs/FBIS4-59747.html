BFN [Excerpts] This evening Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz returned to Beirut from South Africa where he was attending the inauguration of President Nelson Mandela. In his arrival statement, Buwayz said his participation in Mandela's inauguration reflects Lebanon's support for the return of democracy and genuine representation in South Africa. Here is a recording of Minister Buwayz' statement to colleague Darwish 'Ammar: [Begin recording] [passage omitted] ['Ammar] Mr. Minister, it is known that U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher began a new tour of the region. Do you know if he will visit Lebanon? What does Lebanon expect from Christopher's tour? [Buwayz] So far, we have not been informed of a possible visit to Lebanon by Christopher. We do not expect such a visit. I believe that Secretary Christopher is trying to address problems related to the Syrian-Israeli track of negotiations. As far as I know, his proposals are still somewhat far from constituting a genuine and constructive common ground. [end recording]
