BFN [Text] Prishtina, June 16 (ATA) -- The chairman of the regional council of the Serb Democratic Party in Kosova, Dragolub Popovic, declared that now the situation is favourable for a dialogue between Albanians and Serbs, but his party cannot accept the declaration of Fehmi Agani, vice chairman of LDK [Kosova Democratic Alliance], that the Serbs should discuss with those "elected by the people," reports the Serbian newspaper JEDINSTVO of Prishtina.
