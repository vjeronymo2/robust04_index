921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / Consumers target debt repayment CONSUMERS continued to make the repayment of debt a priority in the third quarter, and the number of people behind with repayments on consumer-credit agreements has fallen compared with a year ago. Infolink, a credit-information group, said the proportion of instalment credit accounts with payments fully up to date had increased to 90.4 per cent in the third quarter compared with 87.4 per cent in the same quarter last year.
