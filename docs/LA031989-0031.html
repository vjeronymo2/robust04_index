March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BROWN

Contrary to the statement in the section titled "When Reality Intrudes," "Electricity-generating windmills have become symbols of flakiness," the California wind-farm developments have become a major worldwide symbol of dramatic, efficient use of alternative energy. The wind turbine industry is a multibillion-dollar industry that is no longer esoteric, nor has anything to do with tax shelters. It creates jobs, pays taxes and is part of the solution to the growing environmental problems.

GEORGE WAGNER

WIND HARVEST CO.

Ventura
