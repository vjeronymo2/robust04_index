Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Telepress" newscast] [Text] This morning, some 4,000 public employees, members of the organization that groups state workers together, took part in a demonstration in western Guatemala. Public employees from Huehuetenango, Quezaltenango, Totonicapan, San Marcos, and Solola also took part in the demonstration, which concluded with a meeting at a place called Cuatro Caminos. In this way, these state employees joined demonstrations staged in the capital yesterday to protest the government's refusal to grant a 40 percent salary increase. According to local reports, traffic was interrupted on the Inter-American Highway for about two hours. The National Police dispatched officers to the site of the demonstration to guarantee public order along the highway.
