921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / International Company News: SA group quits Australian deal SENTRACHEM, the South African chemicals group, will not proceed with its proposed acquisition of Chemplex of Australia, writes Philip Gawith in Johannesburg. Mr John Job, Sentrachem chief executive, said it was unable to agree with the vendor, Mr Kerry Packer's Consolidated Press Holdings, on the price and other aspects. Sentrachem will not now proceed with its R240m (Dollars 49.5m) rights issue.
