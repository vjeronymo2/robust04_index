Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States has announced that it will send a diplomatic note to Poland, asking that no assistance be given to the regime in Burma. The Americans maintain that the Poles are helping the local junta by providing military training and maintenance of military equipment. Here is Wladyslaw Klaczynski, the director of the Foreign Affairs Ministry Information Department: [Begin recording] [Klaczynski] So far, the Foreign Affairs Ministry has received nothing from the Americans. We have not sold assault helicopters. Two years ago we sold transport helicopters from Swidnik. Allegedly, the Burmese have installed some weapons in them. This could happen. I would like to recall that Burma is not subject to any ban by the United Nations Organization. [Unidentified correspondent] Precisely, are we bound by UN bans or by U.S. bans? [Klaczynski] We are bound only by UN bans and by our own morality. [end recording]
