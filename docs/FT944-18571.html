941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Darby advances 49% Darby, the USM-quoted maker of specialist glass products, lifted pre-tax profits by 49 per cent from Pounds 379,000 to Pounds 565,000 in the six months to August 31. Turnover improved by 6.7 per cent to Pounds 8.29m (Pounds 7.77m). The interim dividend is lifted to 0.9p (0.5p), payable from earnings of 2.67p (2.2p) per share. Mr Michael Darby, chairman, said the group's concentration on improving the overall quality and mix of the business had proved successful, evidenced by the continued development of niche markets.
