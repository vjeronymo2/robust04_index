BFN [Text] Yohei Kono, president of the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP], gave a speech in Niigata City on 29 May, in which he said, "What can (Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata's) minority government promise when it goes to the meeting of leaders of the advanced nations (the Naples Summit)? If something is agreed upon at the summit, will it be able to implement it? We have to think about whom we should send out as a responsible representative to the international community." He indicated that the Hata cabinet should resign before the summit is held on 8 July.
