Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran described the recent claims of the head of the CIA about Tehran's support for terrorist groups and Iran's attempts to obtain nuclear weapons as the futile repetition of Washington's previous policies. The Islamic Republic of Iran's mission at the United Nations issued a communique last night describing the repetition of such allegations as the continuation of Washington's efforts to exert pressure on Iran and achieve the U.S. Government's political motives. The head of the CIA has said at a session of the U.S. Senate Select Committee on Intelligence that the Islamic Republic of Iran supports terrorism and endeavors to obtain nuclear weapons. The communique issued by our country's office at the United Nations explains that the Islamic Republic of Iran is itself a victim of terrorism and that during their inspctions of our country's nuclear sites, the International Atomic Energy Agency experts have testified that the U.S. claims are false.
