BFN [Text] Managua, 23 May (AFP) -- Official sources have reported that today, at the conclusion of the Sandinist National Liberation Front [FSLN] Special Congress, former Nicaraguan President Daniel Ortega was reelected secretary general of his party for a new three-year period. Ortega was able to secure the support of the majority of the "orthodox" sector of the FSLN for his proposal to maintain the party's traditional struggle politicies in opposition to the current endorsed by his election adversary, Henry Ruiz, a legendary Sandinist leader who represented the reformist sector of this political organization.
