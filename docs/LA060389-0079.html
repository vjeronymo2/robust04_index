June 3, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"We are eight to nine years into the epidemic, and we are still trying to discover why monies are not being spent in one of the most heavily impacted regions."

-- Assemblyman Richard Polanco (D-Los Angeles), who asked the state auditor general to look into reports that Los Angeles County may not spend several million dollars allocated for AIDS programs for the current fiscal year.
