BFN [By reporter Zhang Hao (1728 3185)] [Text] Vienna, 21 Apr (XINHUA) -- The 37th conference of the UN Commission on Narcotic Drugs concluded in Vienna on 21 April. The conference deliberated the work of the UN Drug Prohibition Agency and International Narcotics Control Bureau, emphatically studied and drew up new measures against drug-related crimes, and passed a series of resolutions on issues such as prohibiting the illegal production, trafficking, and consumption of drugs. Delegates to the conference also deliberated the resolution passed by the 48th UN General Assembly last year on drug prohibition and exchanged views on the present situation of drug dealing in the world, and the implementation of drug prohibition conventions by different countries. Chen Shiqiu, head of the Chinese delegation and Chinese permanent representative to the UN office and other international organizations in Vienna, spoke at the conference. He expounded the important measures and achievements in China's crackdown on drug-related crimes over recent years. The content of his speech won the attention and affirmation of fellow delegates at the conference. The conference opened on 13 April.
