BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The central Mongolian press give a wide coverage to the cessation of the political hunger strike called by the Mongolian Democratic Association and the Union of Mongolian Students demanding the resignation of the government as having failed to fulfill its obligations referring to the preelection programme. Prior to this the supporters of the government had decided to stop the hunger strike as well according to the newspaper, the Great State Hural of the country has considered the issue regarding the above mentioned events and took certain decisions relating to the regulation of the current situation arisen. The newspaper "ARDIYN ERH" informs that the parliament has passed a decision in association with the demands of the participants of the recent political actions and charging the government to work out and submit for consideration by the Great State Hural the draft laws concerning the ethical standards of activities for the top-ranking persons of position, the freedom of press, the procedure of carrying out mass meetings and demonstrations. The newspaper reports that the spring session holding currently of the Great State Hural has discussed in first reading the draft Law on Civil Defence. The newspaper points out that the members of the parliament have agreed with the necessity for the Civil Defence Service to be headed by the prime minister. The newspaper publishes an interview of its correspondent with the deputy head of the National Development Department L. Demberel. He said in his interview about the assistance and credits from international financial and economic organizations and foreign countries granted to the country for the purpose of overcoming the economic difficulties related to the transition to market relations. The newspaper provides the specified data concerning the economic assistance and credits of the international organizations and foreign countries. The governmental newspaper "DZASGIYN GADZRYN MEUEE" publishes the full text of the political agreement arrived at between the parties holding seats in the parliament. The agreement was reached with the participation of the president of the country P. Ochirbat. The Great State Hural has called upon the participants of the political hunger strike demanding the resignation of the government to stop their action. The newspaper also provides the information of the governmental press office about the statement by the Prime Minister P. Jasray concerning the demands by the participants of the rallies and hunger strikes held recently. The prime minister stressed that the people should not doubt that the government would resign in accordance with the provisions of the law. He charged the citizens to exert all their powers in creative labour. In connection with the forthcoming official visit of the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China (PRC) Li Peng to Mongolia the newspaper carries a political portrait of the Chinese premier. The newspaper publishes the decisions of the government approving the provision on the discipline of those working in the fuel and power industry. The newspaper carries the reference about the republic with parliamentary power. The newspaper "HUMUUN BICHIG" issued in the Mongolian script informs about the completion of the preparations for the spring sowing in the Uburhangay Aimag. In this aimag it is supposed to sow about 20 thousand hectares and much attention, the newspaper notes is to be paid to the horticulture. The observer of the newspaper is standing against the opinion by a number of scholars who are against the introduction of the Mongolian traditional script into the official documentary and business keeping. He notes in his respect that during the last years in the country has been arranged immense preparatory work for teaching the Mongolian script to the population. The newspaper also carries some excerpts from the governmental newspaper "DZASGIYN GADZRYN MEUEE" which stresses that the Mongolian script was proclaimed to be the official script of the state as early as the days of the Ghengis Khan. The newspaper also informs about the course of implementation of the decision of the government on intensifying the work on preparations for keeping all the Mongolian official documentary and business on the Mongolian script.
