940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Farringford looking for acquisition as losses rise Pre-tax losses at Farringford increased from Pounds 655,000 to Pounds 896,000 in the year to February 28. Directors said current operations were inadequate to enable the company to trade profitably. A recapitalisation and acquisition were required to secure its future. A number of potential acquisitions had been reviewed and an announcement to shareholders was likely in the near future. Sales fell to Pounds 636,000 (Pounds 654,000). An exceptional charge of Pounds 563,000 (Pounds 317,000) related mainly to a reduction in the value of the Farringford Hotel on the Isle of Wight, the company's main asset. Losses per share widened to 3.6p (2.8p).
