BFN [By Kim Pong-son] [Text] On the morning of 23 June, the government will hold an emergency meeting of the Unification-National Security Policy Coordination Council headed by Chairman Yi Hong-ku, deputy prime minister and minister of the National Unification Board, to fine- tune its final position before it takes future countermeasures in the wake of North Korea's acceptance of our proposal for a preliminary contact for the summit talks. In particular, the government will decide on which three delegates will represent our side at the preliminary contact and notify the North Korean side of the list. The government has temporarily designated Deputy Prime Minister Yi as the head delegate. It has been learned that the government will select two vice ministerial-level officials as other delegates. It has also been learned: President Kim Yong-sam gave directives that the government see to it that the summit meeting is realized as soon as possible, regardless of time or place. In this connection, the government will work towards finalizing its policy to realize summit talks in July.
