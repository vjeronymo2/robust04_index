Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Carlos Lage, vice president of the executive committee of the Council of Ministers [title as heard], says he encountered a very firm position on the part of the presidents of Brazil, Venezuela, and Colombia against the economic, financial, and trade blockade imposed on Cuba by the United States. Regarding his talks with Colombian President Cesar Gaviria yesterday, Lage stressed that they were on increasing trade relations through increasing fuel purchases by Cuba directly from Colombia, and on exporting Cuban medicines to Colombia.
