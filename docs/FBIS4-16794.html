BFN [INTERFAX report under the "In Brief" rubric: "They Are Getting Ready for May Day"] [Text] The Russian Federation internal troops "will be on duty on a reinforced basis; however, there will be no order to put them in full combat readiness" during the May Day celebrations, stated Colonel General Anatoliy Kulikov, internal troops commander, in a news briefing in Moscow yesterday. He pointed out that being on reinforced duty means reinforced security at a number of troop facilities to prevent seizures of weapons and hardware. "No concentration of forces from the other regions in Moscow is contemplated," the commander underlined. In his words, internal troops would be enlisted during the May Day celebrations to help the militia "as a reserve at the point of permanent deployment, as well as in separate regions in Moscow."
