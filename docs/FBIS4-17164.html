BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Grachev] [Text] Vladivostok April 22 TASS -- An ammunition depot belonging to the Pacific Fleet and located in the area of Vladivostok was attacked last night by unidentified assailants. The criminals attempted to break into the building from two sides. Two of them were spotted by a officer on guard who fired a warning shot. However, the intruders ignored the warning and then the guard shot to kill. One of the assailants was wounded, but the other one came to his rescue and both escaped. Two other assailants made an attack on the building from the other side. They were detected by a guard patrolling the area. The criminals noticed the guard and fled to a nearby forest. Local police have begun an investigation into the incident.
