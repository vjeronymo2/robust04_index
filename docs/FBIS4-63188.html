BFN [Text] The "Tsentr" TV company starts broadcasting early on Saturday morning [May 21] on Ostankino's First Channel. This is the so called channel for late night viewers. It will start operating at 0145 hours [0945 GMT]. In the first broadcast you can watch the French film "The Snatch Squad." In the long-term, the organizers are planning various entertaining topics, together with reportage on the life of nocturnal Moscow.
