Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] According to the Ecuadoran Central Bank, the country's leading exports registered a 3.4-percent drop at the end of 1993. Revenues from the sale of oil, bananas, shrimp, cacao, and seafood amounted to $2.9 billion compared to $3 billion earned in 1992. Oil exports yielded $1.149 billion; bananas, $503 million; and shrimp, $451 million. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 15 Mar 94)
