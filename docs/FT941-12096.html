940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: New London loss For the six months to September 30, pre-tax losses of New London, the oil field services concern, fell from Dollars 4.26m to Dollars 1.38m (Pounds 920,000), from turnover lower at Dollars 58.4m against Dollars 69.7m. Losses per share were 1.8 cents, compared with 4.7 cents.
