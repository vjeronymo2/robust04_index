940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / Housing market sales up 25% The total number of transactions in the housing market increased by 25 per cent in the six months to March compared with the same period in the previous year, says a report by the Valuation Office, an agency of the Inland Revenue. House prices rose by an average of 0.66 per cent over the six months, according to district valuers. The performance of different regions ranged from no movement in the north-west, south-west and west Midlands to a 2 per cent rise in the east Midlands. Residential building land values increased sharply in areas such as East Anglia, where they rose by 15 per cent.
