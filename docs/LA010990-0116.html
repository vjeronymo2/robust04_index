January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

TRICE LEADS CHAPMAN WOMEN TO VICTORY

Bethel Trice scored 24 points to lead Chapman College to a 92-77 victory over Millersville (Pa.) in a nonconference women's basketball game Monday night at Chapman College.

Julie King added 18 points for the Panthers, who led, 43-32, at halftime. Chapman is 12-3.
