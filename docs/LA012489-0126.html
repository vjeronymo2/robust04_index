January 24, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

SADDLEBACK CONTINUES TO TOP BASKETBALL POLL

Saddleback High School, scoring an average of 62.1 points per game and allowing an average of 49.2 points, remains the top-ranked team in the Orange County Sportswriters' Assn. prep basketball poll for the fourth week in a row.

Saddleback has won 13 consecutive games, the longest winning streak in the county. Sixth-ranked Savanna (18-1) has won 9 games in a row since losing to Irvine in the Irvine World News tournament.

El Dorado (15-4) makes its debut in the top 10 in the 10th position, pushing Orange Lutheran out of the poll.
