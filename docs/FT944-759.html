941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: Johannesburg slips back South African shares finished easier but the undertone was still firm, supported by a strong bullion price and gains on world markets. Futures were higher at the opening following the rise on Wall Street and further US interest in South African ADRs, but shed gains later. The overall index declined 5.8 to 5,798.7, the industrial index was 15.1 lower at 6,906.2 and the golds index moved 6.9 down to 1,964.6. Anglos fell 50 cents to R93.75 and De Beers lost 25 cents at R93.75, both shedding mild gains on the day. Gencor rose 10 cents to R14.50.
