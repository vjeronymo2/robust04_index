Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS string correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow March 17 TASS -- The Russian military space forces launched "Cosmos-2274" satellite with "Soyuz-5" carrier rocket from the Russian cosmodrome in Plesetsk at 19:30 Moscow time on Thursday. According to the press centre of the military space forces, the launch was successful. The space apparatus is moving at an orbit close to the estimated [sentence as received]. Its movements are monitored by the main centre for testing and controlling space apparatuses based in Golitsyno-2, a town in the Moscow region. The centre is a structural unit of the Russian military space forces. "Cosmos-2274" was the 1086th satellite to be launched from the Plesetsk cosmodrome inaugurated 28 years ago.
