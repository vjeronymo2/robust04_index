December 1, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Huffington Up For Sale: Huffington Corp., a privately held, Houston-based international oil and gas company, has put itself up for sale. Analysts said it could fetch about $1 billion. The company also owns a substantial amount of unimproved real estate, including the equivalent of 11 city blocks in Houston's central business district. The company's principal subsidiary is Roy M. Huffington Inc., which is best known for its Indonesian oil production operations.
