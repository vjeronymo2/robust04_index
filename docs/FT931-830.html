930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / Finance and the Family: Halifax increases profits and bad debt provisions - At a Glance HALIFAX Building Society this week announced an 8 per cent rise in annual pre-tax profits, from Pounds 628m to Pounds 680m. This was in spite of a 63 per cent increase to Pounds 374m in provisions against bad and doubtful debts; most of which related to residential mortgages. There were 22,486 mortgages in arrears at January 31 compared to 18,061 a year earlier, and total arrears were Pounds 268.7m. Provisions have grown steadily in the past five years from Pounds 8.8m in 1989. Total assets rose 7 per cent to Pounds 62.8bn. Jon Foulds, chairman, said the society had performed well in 'the worst year in the housing market since the war.'
