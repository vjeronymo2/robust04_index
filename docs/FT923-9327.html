920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Olympic News in Brief: Unified in basketball final The Unified Team women's basketball team staged a stunning upset of the US with a 79-73 semi-final victory that dashed American hopes for a third straight gold medal. The Unified Team will play for gold tomorrow against the winner of the Cuba-China semi-final. The Americans, considered the best women's team ever put together by the US, can do no better than the bronze in a game against the Cuba-China loser.
