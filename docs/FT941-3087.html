940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / International Company News: Caterpillar in China deal Caterpillar, the US heavy equipment manufacturer, has signed a joint venture agreement with China's leading diesel engine maker to build engines in China, writes Martin Dickson. Caterpillar will be the majority partner in the venture with Shanghai Diesel Engine. Under a previous agreement between the two companies, engines were built in China with Caterpillar technology, but sold under a different trademark. They will now be marketed in China as Caterpillar engines. ITT of the US said its ITT Flygt unit has signed a joint venture agreement with Dynapac Comercial for the manufacture and sale of submersible pumps and mixers in Brazil.
