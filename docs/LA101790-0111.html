October 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Rice Again: Donna Rice, a model and aspiring actress when her relationship with Gary Hart torpedoed his presidential campaign, is appearing in a production of "Largo Desolato," a play by Czech President Vaclav Havel in Alexandria, Va. Rice, who has been living quietly in the Washington area, is listed on the playbill simply as D.L. Rice. She plays Marguerite, a student out to seduce her professor. CLAUDIA PUIG
