June 4, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LOTTO TICKET BOUGHT IN MISSION VIEJO IS WORTH $9.67 MILLION

A ticket bought in Mission Viejo had all six winning numbers for the California Lottery's weekend "Lotto 6-49" game and is worth $9.67 million, lottery officials said Sunday.

The winning numbers, picked Saturday night, were: 31, 24, 43, 49, 3, 16, and the bonus number, 13.

Thirteen tickets had five numbers plus the bonus number, and each is worth $117,441. Lottery officials said those tickets were bought in Oxnard, Huntington Beach, San Clemente, Westminster, Santa Barbara, San Fernando, Sylmar, Reseda, Baker, San Leandro, Modesto, Chico and Watsonville.
