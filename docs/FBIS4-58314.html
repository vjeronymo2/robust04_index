BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 May (MENA)--The Egyptian delegation participating in the Egyptian-Jordanian preparatory meetings to be held in Amman tomorrow left Cairo this evening to prepare the agenda of the Higher Joint Committee between the two countries scheduled to begin meeting on Wednesday under the Jordanian and Egyptian prime ministers. Dr. Yusuf Butrus-Ghali, minister of state for international cooperation affairs, will leave Cairo for Amman Tuesday to attend the final meeting of the preparatory committee, which will forward the minutes of the meeting to the Higher Committee.
