BFN [Text] Gaza, 24 Jun (MENA) -- Counselor Khalid al-Qidrah, public prosecutor of the Palestinian National Authority, has said that suspects in the murder of Nasir Salluhah, an activist in the Hamas Movement who was kidnapped two days ago and whose body was found in Bayt Lahiyah in northern Gaza, are currently under investigation. In a statement to MENA, he affirmed that if the involvement of one or all the suspects in murdering Salluhah is proved, they will be tried under the standing Palestinian laws. He said that investigation and speedy trial procedures will be carried out without violating legal requirements. Salluhah's funeral is scheduled to take place after today's Friday prayer. In addition to Hamas leaders, representatives of all Palestinian organizations and the Palestinian National Authority will take part in the procession.
