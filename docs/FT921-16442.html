920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Nigerian cabinet dissolved President Ibrahim Babangida dissolved Nigeria's cabinet on Thursday before a reshuffle. This followed a decision to reduce the number of ministries from 25 to 16, Reuter reports from Lagos. The president said in his 1992 budget address on Wednesday that the cabinet would be pruned by a third to redesign government machinery 'on the lines of the best business practice'.
