BFN [Text] Seoul, April 22 (YONHAP) -- The director of the Hong Kong branch of China's XINHUA News Agency, Zhou Nan, arrived here Friday afternoon at the invitation of the Foreign Ministry. Zhou meets with Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu later in the day and will call on Prime Minister Yi Hoe-chang and Trade, Industry and Energy Minister Kim Chol-su during his six-day stay. He will also visit Samsung Electronics Co. and Daewoo Motor Co. as well as Songkyunkwan, a center for Confucian studies, and Kyongju, in North Kyongsang Province.
