Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 4 (XINHUA) -- XINHUA A and B stock indices, indicators of operation for China's securities market, continued to rise substantially today. Today's XINHUA A stock index closed at 56.91 points, 1.85 points higher than yesterday; and XINHUA B stock index rose by 4.14 points to close at 82.99. The XINHUA stock index is the country's first national unitary stock exponent, which is jointly designed and issued by the economic information department of the XINHUA NEWS AGENCY and Beijing University.
