BFN [Text] Guangzhou, May 21 (XINHUA) -- A new periodical titled "Economy of Guangdong and Hong Kong" has recently come off press in south China's Guangdong Province. With six issues per year, the periodical is published for industrial and commercial circles in Guangdong and Hong Kong. It will carry the market-oriented policies formulated by the party and state as well as targets and measures adopted by Guangdong Province in its economic reform and economic development. The periodical is also aimed at promoting the exchange of economic information between Guangdong and Hong Kong and Macao, and analyzing development trends and consumption tendencies in the three places. Every issue of the periodical, which comprises 80 to 100 pages, will be printed in the form of picture album. Two deputy governors of Guangdong Province are advisors to the periodicals. In the first issue of the periodical, there are columns such as economic forum, global trade, economic forecasts, commercial and business situation in Guangdong, and Hong Kong and Taiwan. In the foreward to the periodical, Peng Shubao, director of the Guangdong Provincial Economic Commission, noted that at present, the relations between economic development trends in Guangdong and in Hong Kong and Macao are becoming increasingly closer. The economic relations between guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao will produce an important impact on the economic expansion of the Asian-Pacific region in the next one or two decades, Peng said.
