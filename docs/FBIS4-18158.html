CSO [Article by Unto Hamalainen: "North Korea's Leader Sends Son as Ambassador to Finland"] [Excerpt] North Korean leader Kim Il-song's second son, Kim Pyong-il, arrived Friday [11 March] in Finland. Kim Pyong-il is his country's new ambassador in the Helsinki embassy. North Korea's embassy confirmed the envoy's arrival on Saturday. The new ambassador will submit his credentials to President Martti Ahtisaari next Friday. [passage omitted]
