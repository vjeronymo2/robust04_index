BFN [Text] Rights of persons belonging to national or ethnic minorities were discussed by experts in the Minsk-based CIS headquarters. The aim of the session was to continue drafting a convention regulating the rights of minorities living on the CIS territory. The subject regards the recognition of their rights to set up various organizations, associations, or national groups, etc. of an educational, religious, and law protecting nature. This document is supposed to ensure the political and social stability in the CIS and avoid any national disagreements.
