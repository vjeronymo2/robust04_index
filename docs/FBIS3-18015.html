Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "NATO/Bosnia: Fabbri on Jet Downings"] [Text] Rome, 28 Feb (ANSA) -- NATO pilots were acting in compliance with the no-fly zone resolution 816 of the United Nations when they shot down four fighter jets over Bosnia this morning, Italian Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri said. "We are faced with a painful episode," Fabbri said, adding: "The fact remains that the international community and the United Nations want to make credible and coherent their willingness to guarantee global order and security, they cannot avoid the use of force with the aim of peace-making and providing humanitarian aid." This, Fabbri said, is "the only way" the UN can maintain the authority and prestige necessary to keep the growing number of crisis areas around the world under control.
