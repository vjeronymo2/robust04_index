Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn, 25 Jan (DDP/ADN) -- Federal Finance Minister Theo Waigel is ill. A spokesperson for the ministry announced in Bonn today that Waigel is confined to bed because of severe bronchitis. Therefore the CSU [Christian Social Union] chairman is unable to take part in a meeting of leading politicians in the Bonn coalition with Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl today. A planned trip to South Africa has been cancelled.
