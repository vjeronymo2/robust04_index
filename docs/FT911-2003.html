910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / International Company News: Sanford lifts net earnings SANFORD, New Zealand's largest fishing company, lifted net earnings for the half-year by 22 per cent to NZDollars 6.45m (USDollars 3.79m) in the six months to February 28, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. The company has recently acquired the deep-sea fishing interests of Skeggs Seafoods, which, absorbed Fletcher Challenge's interests last year. The latest results do not include earnings from these interests. Sales and gross revenue rose 6 per cent to NZDollars 82.06m from NZDollars 77.77m, while pre-tax profit rose 13 per cent to NZDollars 10.21m (NZDollars 9.05m). Total assets rose 14 per cent to NZDollars 105.65m.
