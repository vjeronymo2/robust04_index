930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / Railways safety continues to improve RAIL safety continued its long-term improvement last year in spite of some well-publicised accidents such as the one at London's Cannon Street station, the Health and Safety Executive said yesterday, Richard Tomkins writes. Although the number of fatalities in train crashes rose from four to 11, the total of so-called significant train accidents - potentially the most dangerous to passengers - fell from 161 to 131.
