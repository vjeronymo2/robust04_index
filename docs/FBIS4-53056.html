BFN [Text] The Singapore economy expanded by 9.9 percent last year compared with 6 percent in 1992. Financial and business services was the fastest growing sector, expanding by 13 percent. The Department of Statistics also revealed that, compared to the past 10 years, prices of private property in 1993 rose by a record 24 percent. Prices of land and houses alone shot up 41 percent compared with 15 percent in 1992. The department also found that more middle-aged people are in the labor force compared with 10 years ago and a proportion of the elderly aged 60 years and above was also greater compared with 10 years ago. The number of elderly also increased at a faster rate than the general population.
