INTERSTATE COMMERCE COMMISSION [Finance Docket No. 32622] City of Los Angeles and City of Long Beach_Purchase Exemption_Union Pacific Railroad Company's San Pedro Branch AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission. ACTION: Notice of Exemption.

SUMMARY: Pursuant to 49 U.S.C. 10505, the Interstate Commerce Commission exempts from the prior approval requirements of 49 U.S.C. 11343&hyph;11345 the purchase by the City of Los Angeles, CA, and the City of Long Beach, CA, from Union Pacific Railroad Company (UP) of 18.65 miles of rail line known as the San Pedro Branch, between mileposts 3.06 and 21.71 in Los Angeles County, CA, subject to employee protective conditions. DATES: The exemption is effective on December 22, 1994. Petitions to reopen must be filed by January 11, 1995.
