Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] National Assembly President Ricardo Alarcon has expressed his conviction that Cuba's relations with the European Union [EU] are on the right track; he is hoping to conclude a general cooperation agreement with the EU soon. Alarcon is currently visiting Germany. On 2 February, in Bonn, he met with (Hans Klein), vice president of the German Parliament; deputies in charge of relations with Central America and the Caribbean; and representatives of the Christian Democratic Union, headed by Chancellor Helmut Kohl. In statements to the press, Alarcon stressed: Cuba has to make a series of changes in its economy, but it is not seeking to transform the basic elements of its political design, which is socialism.
