940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Reports of skirmishes throws shadow over renewed initiative Bosnian women carrying bags of UNHCR-donated flour the 10km to their homes in the village of Dubrave near Tuzla yesterday. Reports of skirmishes in northern Bosnia threw a shadow over a new international drive to end the war in former Yugoslavia, Reuter reports from Sarajevo. Bosnian government army sources said their Moslem-led troops, normally outgunned by Bosnian Serb foes, had pushed Serb forces back in three separate attacks near Tuzla in recent days.
