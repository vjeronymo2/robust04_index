Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Demirel] [Text] President Suleyman Demirel has sent messages to Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic and Croatian President Franjo Tudjman expressing his pleasure over the agreements signed in Washington. In his message, Demirel said that with these agreements a step in the right direction has been taken toward reaching a comprehensive solution in Bosnia-Herzegovina. He further expressed the wish that activities aimed at achieving peace and stability in all parts of the region will continue. Demirel also stressed Turkey's determination -- which includes the stationing of Turkish troops in Bosnia-Herzegovina -- to contribute to the process of establishing lasting peace and stability in the region.
