940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Cambridge Water Pre-tax profits of the Cambridge Water Company expanded from Pounds 2.94m to Pounds 4.14m for the year to March 31, from turnover little changed at Pounds 14.5m, against Pounds 14.3m. Earnings per share were 944p (726p) while the final dividend is 125p, lifting the total to 190p (170p).
