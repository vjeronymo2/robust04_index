(4) Brake Energy Limits_Section 4.7 of the Airplane Flight Manual: Reduce the maximum brake energy speed allowed with all brakes operative by 30 knots. Verify the scheduled V 1 is less than the reduced V MBE . If not, then takeoff weight must be reduced. (6) Maximum Quick Turnaround Weight_Section 4.13 of the Airplane Flight Manual: No change from the `all brakes operative' value.
