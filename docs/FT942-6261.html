940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / Cuban pledge to asylum seekers Cuba has given a verbal guarantee that 124 asylum seekers in the Belgian ambassador's residence in Havana will not be punished if they leave the embassy, a Cuban diplomat said yesterday, agencies report from Brussels and Havana. The asylum-seekers had raced through an open door or hopped over a wrought-iron fence to get into the embassy on Saturday.
