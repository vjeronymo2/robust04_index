June 10, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ON THE BEACHES;

ORANGE COUNTY BEACH GUIDE;

RULES, RULES, RULES

Some rules and regulations may vary from beach to beach, but the following apply everywhere:

NO GLASS CONTAINERS. Broken glass may be the beach-goer's biggest enemy.

NO PETS. While dogs are allowed on leash on some pedestrian paths and picnic areas, none are allowed on the sands.
