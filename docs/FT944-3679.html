941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / International Company News: Brazilian Investment Brazilian Investment Trust raised net assets per share by 36 per cent to 187 cents at the end of September, against 137.3 cents six months earlier. The trust incurred a net deficit of Dollars 397,000 (Pounds 242,000) for the half year, against Dollars 406,000 earnings. First-half losses per share were 0.64 cents (earnings of 0.65 cents).
