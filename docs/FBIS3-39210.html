Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Belgian Foreign Affairs Minister Willy Claes is very happy with the NATO ultimatum reached last Wednesday [9 February]. NATO has given the Bosnian Serbs 10 days to lift the siege of Sarajevo. According to the minister, the Serbs now have no other choice than to retreat from their positions around the city by 21 February. Also Belgium is prepared to cooperate with possible air strikes, but Minister Claes says he thinks that our country will in the first place be asked to offer logistics support. [passage omitted] Belgian Foreign Affairs Minister Willy Claes says he is not worried about the strong opposition of the Russians. He says that it is Moscow that is making a mistake, not NATO.
