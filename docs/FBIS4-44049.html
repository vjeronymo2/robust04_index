BFN [Excerpt] The House of Representatives delegation headed by House President Alexis Galanos concluded its talks in Moscow with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and officials of the Russian Parliament. The delegation also met Patriarch Aleksiy. Galanos will leave Moscow this afternoon for Amsterdam, where he will participate in the conference of the presidents of European parliaments. On Sunday [26 June], Galanos will go to Strasbourg to participate in the 3d section of the 1994 conference of the Council of Europe's Parliamentary Assembly. Then Galanos will go to Vienna to participate in the meeting of the permanent committee of the CSCE Parliamentary Assembly and the third annual meeting of the CSCE Central Committee. [passage omitted]
