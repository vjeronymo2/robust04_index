BFN [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau], May 17, RIA -- The Christian Democratic Popular Front of Moldavia [Moldova] views the current dialogue between the leaders of Moldavia and Transdniestria [Dniester] as a blow at national interests. The Front's current statement notes that the negotiating process signifies the admission of equality of the Transdniestrian Moldavian Republic. A RIA correspondent was told by sources close to the Front's leadership that it also has a negative vision of the CSCE mission's participation in the negotiating process because it becomes a supporter of Russia's imperial policy.
