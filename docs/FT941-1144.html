940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Atlas Converting falls to Pounds 4.07m Atlas Converting Equipment, the manufacturer of slitting and rewinding machines, yesterday announced a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 5.03m to Pounds 4.07m for 1993 on turnover down slightly at Pounds 44.6m against Pounds 46.4m. Earnings per share were 28.8p, compared with 41p, but the dividend is held at 22p with a same-again final of 15p.
