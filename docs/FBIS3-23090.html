Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Feb (MENA)--The security forces in Asyut killed a dangerous fugitive terrorist during a sweep they carried out on al-Ma'abidah Island, in the Nile, between Abnub and Manfalut, early this morning after receiving information that a number of terrorists are hiding there. The dead terrorist is Zayn Fawzi Mahran, 27, from al-'Awamir village. He has been on the wanted list in connection with numerous cases. The body of the dead terrorist was taken to the central hospital in Abnub. The security sweep on the island is continuing to clean up the remaining fugitive terrorists.
