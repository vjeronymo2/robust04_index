921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Acal Acal, the electronics and industrial controls distributor, reported lower interim profits on turnover increased by recent acquisitions. Pre-tax profits for the six months to September 30 fell by 12 per cent from Pounds 1.52m to Pounds 1.34m on turnover which grew from Pounds 29.1m to Pounds 30.9m, including Pounds 3m attributed to recent acquisitions. Earnings per share dropped to 6.3p (7.1p). The interim dividend is increased by 7.7 per to 2.1p (1.95p) per share.
