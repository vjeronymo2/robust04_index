Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- On Friday [25 February], FRG Chancellor Helmut Kohl received Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Mahmud Va'ezi, who has been in Bonn since Monday, for a talk. Va'ezi wanted to deliver a message to the chancellor from President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. According to official information, the Iranian politician came to Bonn within the framework of normal political consultations. He has already discussed with the Foreign Ministry the development of relations, especially in the field of culture, among other things.
