Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [NAEWOE News Agency report] [Text] It has been learned that North Korea established a special military unit called "commando unit" [tukkong yukchondae] and secretly trains it. Recently acquired documents on North Korea show that North Korea selected members of the commando unit from the "Nampo Military and Political School" and gives them ideological education. The commando unit is composed of orphans who have no close relatives. North Korea divided the 39 members of the commando unit (30 men and nine women) in two platoons and gives them spy training to send them to South Korea.
