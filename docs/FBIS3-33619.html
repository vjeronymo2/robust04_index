Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Montevideo LA MANANA in Spanish on 17 February publishes on page 6 a 500-word unattributed article on a cooperation agreement signed by the Andean Development Corporation, CAF, and the Latin American Integration Association, Aladi, which will enable CAF "to identify investment opportunities" in 11 Aladi member countries. CAF Chairman Enrique Garcia Rodriguez told LA MANANA that the agreement will be instrumental in helping Aladi member countries in their integration drive. The article quotes Garcia Rodriguez as saying: "We believe we can play a support role in trade exchanges between the different integration areas like the Andean Group; the Common Market of the South; the group of three -- Mexico, Colombia, and Venezuela; the Central American Common Market; and Chile, which even though it is not a member of these groups, furthers an open policy toward integration." Garcia Rodriguez noted: "Seventy percent of CAF credits are earmarked for bolstering private initiatives."
