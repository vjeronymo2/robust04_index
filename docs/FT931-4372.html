930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Observer: Balanced Accused of having sinister lunches with noted opponents of Maastricht such as Margaret Thatcher and Labour's Bryan Gould, the Treasury chief secretary Michael Portillo is on the counterattack. He's letting it be known he has also lunched not only with strongly pro-European ex-premier Edward Heath, but - as recently as Tuesday - with Hugh Dykes, Europhile Tory MP.
