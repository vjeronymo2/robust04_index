BFN [Text] Rabat, 22 Apr (INA) -- Barzan al-Tikriti, President Saddam Husayn's personal envoy, arrived here today. In a statement upon his arrival at Muhammad V Airport, al-Tikriti said he was carrying a special message from President Saddam Husayn to his brother King Hassan II about Arab and world issues, as well as the sanctions that have lost any justification. He emphasized that Iraq has an abiding interest in coordinating with brother Moroccans on issues of bilateral concern. On hand to welcome him at the airport were Abdelhadi Boutaleb, the king's adviser; the Iraqi ambassador in Rabat; and Moroccan Foreign Ministry officials.
