940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / Leaders call on 'silent majority' Business leaders call in a letter to the Financial Times today for British industry to 'stand up and be counted' in the forthcoming European elections. Lord Chalfont, Radio Authority chairman, along with chairmen and chief executives from across British industry, cite four key issues if the UK is to play its full part in Europe: 'The silent industrial majority must make its voice heard above the political bickering,' they write.
