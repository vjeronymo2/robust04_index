940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Select Industries SELECT INDUSTRIES: Call of 2 1/2 p per partly paid 7 1/2 p share due by January 31 1994 in respect of 142.94m partly paid shares in issue has resulted in Select receiving a total amount of Pounds 3.53m. The call will raise Pounds 3.57m and thus some 98.8 per cent of total due has been received to date.
