Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Vietnamese delegation led by Mr. Hoang Duc Nghi, minister and chairman of State Commission for Ethnic Minorities and Mountain Regions of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, called on Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] and deputy prime minister, at the latter's office at 1100. Cordial and frank talks were held on matters relating to economic cooperation, improvement of bilateral goodwill relations, and foreign investment.
