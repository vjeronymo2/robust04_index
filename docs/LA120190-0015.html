December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BUSH VISITS U.S. TROOPS

Charles Krauthammer's pungent column "Why Go After Hussein? It's as Easy as A-B-C" (Column Right, Commentary, Nov. 26) is the most incisive and perspicacious argument yet for why Hussein must be harshly dealt with and removed from Kuwait, even if it means an all-out attack by military forces. It is fervently hoped that President Bush and his advisers read this extremely important article.

LANNY R. MIDDINGS

San Ramon
