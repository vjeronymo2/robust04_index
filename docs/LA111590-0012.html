November 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Deep Regrets: "This is really getting kind of embarrassing," said Harry Thomason, co-executive producer of CBS' "Designing Women," in reference to Wednesday night's refueling of a feud with co-star Delta Burke during an interview on ABC's "The Barbara Walters Special." Burke grew teary when discussing how her complaints about her treatment risked "losing my career and my good name and the money." Thomason repeated an earlier admission that "I did yell and scream at her once two years ago -- and believe me, for that I am deeply, deeply sorry . . . I really don't know what else to say." CLAUDIA PUIG
