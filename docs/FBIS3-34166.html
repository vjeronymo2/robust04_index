Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Zionist enemy escalated its land, sea, and air aggression against Lebanon today. Enemy forces bombarded the western and central sectors of south Lebanon this morning. Israeli gunboats cruised off the Lebanese coast in south Lebanon and enemy helicopters strafed several areas. At 1115 today a resistance group attacked Bra'shit and Bayt Yahun posts and reports say they scored direct hits on both posts. Zionist enemy forces bombarded Bra'shit, Tibnin, and Haddatha. More than 10 shells landed in Bra'shit. The townships of al-Mansuri, Majdal Zun, Buyut al-Sayyid, and Zibqin came under sporadic artillery barrage that continued until noon and caused heavy property damage. An Amal resistance group retaliated by attacking the Jisr al-Hamra' and claimed the destruction of a 12.7-mm machine-gun post. According to later reports from Tyre, the Islamic Resistance attacked two enemy posts in al-Hamra' and Radar Hill this morning. During the attack, resistance men detonated explosive charges near enemy patrols in the two villages. Hostile helicopters strafed the towns of a-Mansuri and Buyut al-Sayyid along with hostile gunboats. Hostile warplanes flew several sorties over the south this morning.
