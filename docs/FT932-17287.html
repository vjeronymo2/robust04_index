930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: YWE Yorkshire Water Enterprises, the unregulated arm of the Leeds-based utility, is making its first move into continental Europe with the purchase of ALcontrol, which provides environmental testing services. YWE is paying Fl 38m (Pounds 13.9m) for the company, which is owned by Saybolt International and has laboratories in the Netherlands and Germany. There are possible further payments of up to Pounds 2.6m if ALcontrol's pre-tax profits exceed Fl 4.6m this year. Last year the company reported turnover of about Fl 20m.
