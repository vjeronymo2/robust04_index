940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / International Company News: Unisys creates business units Unisys, the US information systems group, has created two business units to focus on the worldwide transportation market and the US health information management market, agencies report. Mr Dennis Christ has been named president of the transportation unit and Mr Bill Sturm is president of the health information management area. Unisys said that in transportation, areas of market expansion would include airport modernisation, the travel and hospitality industry, railroads, and freight forwarders. In health information management, Unisys said that it planned to form alliances with health maintenance organisations and insurance companies.
