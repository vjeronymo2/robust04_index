940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Fixed mortgages rise again - At a glance Fixed rate mortgage issues from Nationwide building society and Barclays bank confirm an upward trend. Two, three, five and 10 years rates at Barclays are 6.99 per cent, 7.99 per cent, 8.79 per cent and 9.49 per cent. The fees are Pounds 400 for the 10-year rate, Pounds 300 for the rest. Nationwide offers those borrowing up to 95 per cent, 8.25 per cent fixed for three years; 9.35 per cent for five years and 9.79 per cent for seven years. The fees are Pounds 295. The two year rate is 7.75 per cent with a Pounds 250 fee.
