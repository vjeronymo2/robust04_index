930118 FT 18 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Motorway repairs COSTAIN BUILDING & CIVIL ENGINEERING has been awarded a Pounds 5.3m general maintenance contract for work on the M3, for a 14km section between junctions six and eight, by Hampshire County Council, which is acting as agent for the Department of Transport. Work on the contract will include 16 sq kms of resurfacing, crack sealing, hardening of the central reservation, providing new communication and drainage ducts and new safety fencing.
