940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / Pakistan expels Indian diplomat Pakistan yesterday ordered an Indian diplomat to leave the country within seven days after detaining him for several hours in a new spying row between the two old enemies, Reuter reports from Islamabad. The action against Mr VS Chauhan, an Indian High Commission attache, followed the Indian authorities' detention in New Delhi on Monday of Mr Afzal Khan Bajwa, a Pakistan High Commission official, for alleged spying.
