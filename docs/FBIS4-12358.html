BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana left Cairo today after a one-day visit during which he was received by President Husni Mubarak. He also chaired the annual meeting of the Joint Spanish-Egyptian Committee, which has been meeting annually since it was formed in 1988. During the visit Solana signed one agreement on protecting investments and another on avoiding double taxation. Ambassador Hasan al-'Ujayzi, chief of the Foreign Ministry's Protocol Department, saw him off at the airport.
