930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Mongolian president wins MONGOLIA'S President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat was heading yesterday for an easy re-election victory which was also a defeat for the ruling ex-communist party that dumped him as its candidate, Reuter reports from Ulan Bator. Preliminary official results from the election on Sunday, Mongolia's first direct presidential poll, showed Mr Ochirbat with a 20-point lead over his challenger, conservative editor Mr Lodongiyn Tudev.
