Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Toulouse, France, Jan 20 (AFP) -- French police said Thursday they had seized a record 1.2 tonnes of cocaine with a street value of 170 million dollars after carrying out two raids in this south-western region of France. Two-thirds of the drug was found late Wednesday [19 January] in an isolated house at Villefranche-de-Lauragais, near here and the remainder in the Narbonne area, police said. Three Italian nationals were arrested. The cocaine, of Colombian origin, appeared to be headed for the Italian market, police said.
