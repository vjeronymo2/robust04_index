December 6, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

ONE LOTTO JACKPOT TICKET SOLD

There was a single winning ticket sold for the $44.4-million jackpot in Wednesday's California Lottery Lotto 6/53 game, lottery officials said today.

The ticket, sold in Ontario, entitles the holder or holders to the ninth-largest prize in the history of the Lotto game.

Seven tickets picked five of six numbers, plus the bonus number, and each of those is worth $206,356. Two of those were sold in Oakland; the others were sold in Riverside, La Puente, Ontario, Santa Monica and North Hollywood.
