Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin goes out on car ride or takes walks when he is not working, officials in his entourage told Interfax. In particular, he took a walk in the vicinity of Elbrus last week-end. The sources said that Yeltsin's schedule was "fairly tight." Several senior administration officials, as well as Prime Minister Chernomyrdin, are expected Monday. The exact date of Yeltsin's return to Moscow has not been set but his vacation will not exceed the originally planned two weeks, Interfax was told. Yeltsin left Moscow last Monday.
