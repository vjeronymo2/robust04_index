Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Alfredo Ramirez was reelected president of the Nationalist Liberal Republican Movement (Molirena) during the party's 23 January convention at the Atlapa Convention Center. Ramirez has served in various ministries and was once a presidential hopeful. He is currently a member of the Canal Commission Board of Directors. Alfredo Ramirez has headed Molirena for the past 10 years and has been unanimously reelected to another five-year term.
