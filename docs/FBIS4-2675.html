BFN [Text] Beijing, April 12 (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng met a delegation of friendship from Tokyo, Japan's capital, led by its mayor Suzuki Shunichi, here this afternoon. Li offered congratulations on the fact that cooperation and friendly exchanges have developed in all fields between Beijing and metropolitan Tokyo since they cemented sister-ties 15 years ago. He also praised Suzuki's contribution to the development of Sino- Japanese relations, especially Beijing-Tokyo relations. He said the two countries' bilateral ties have a good growth momentum, with busy personnel exchanges and expanded exchanges and cooperation in the fields of trade and economy, culture and education, and science and technology. Li said it was worth noting that many cities in China and Japan have established friendly relations. Suzuki and his party, who arrived here today as guests of the Beijing municipality, will take part in a series of events organized by Beijing to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the establishment of its sister-relations with Tokyo.
