BFN [Konstantin Parishkura report: CIS Heads To Discuss Black Sea Fleet Problems"] [Text] Yuriy Dubinin, Russian Federation ambassador at large, and Feliks Gromov, commander in chief of the Russian Navy, announced on 13 April, following a meeting with senior officers of the Black Sea Fleet in Sevastopol, that their meeting with Ukraine President Leonid Kravchuk on 12 April "was constructive," and expressed the hope for resolving the Black Sea Fleet problems during the 15 April meeting of CIS leaders. An officers' meeting in Sevastopol passed on 13 April an appeal to the heads of Russia and Ukraine that voices indignation at the Ukrainian side's actions.
