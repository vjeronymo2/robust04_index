BP by X- Ray Spectroscopy [.sub]13[/][.sub]2[/] 43070061W Tokyo ISBB'93 in English Aug 93 p 104--FOR OFFICIAL USE ONLY 43070061W Tokyo ISBB'93 English CSO [English abstract of article by Kuniko Maeda and Tomoyuki Isomura, Institute of Physical and Chemical Research (RIKEN); and Jun Kuwai and Hirohiko Adachi, Department of Metallurgy, Kyoto University] [Text] The crystal structure of B[.sub]13[/]P[.sub]2[/] has been determined to be the _a_-rhombohedral boron structure with two phosphorous atoms (partly replaced by boron) in a unit cell from X-ray diffraction.[.sup]1[/] We measured P 3p electron density of states (DOS) of B[.sub]13[/]P[.sub]2[/] by X-ray emission spectroscopy.[.sup]2[/] We calculate the P 3p DOS of three structures of B[.sub]13[/]P[.sub]2[/]: (a) the two P sites are fully occupied by P, (b) one of the P sites is replaced by B and (c) one of the P sites is vacant. In Figure 1, the calculated DOS are compared with the measured P 3p DOS. It is found from Figure 1 that the experiment remarkably agrees with model (c), but does not agree with model (a) nor (b). Consequently one of the P sites is vacant and is not replaced by B. We conclude that the structure of B[.sub]13[/]P[.sub]2[/] is that shown in Figure 2. Figure 1.
