921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 17,356 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. The strong demand continued. Landed Assams met keen competition and the majority ruled firm to 10p dearer. Bangladesh teas again sold well, advancing 2-4p. Bright east Africans were strong and considerably dearer. Mediums, which had opened 6-8p higher, closed irregular but about steady on balance. Offshore: strong competition with prices up by 3-6p. The highest price realised this week was 203p for a Kenya pd. Quotations: quality 180p, good medium 160p, medium 137p, low medium 100p.
