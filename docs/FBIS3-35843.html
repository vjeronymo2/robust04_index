Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Milov] [Text] Ulyanovsk, 31 Jan -- The working visit to the Volga military district by Russian Defense Minister Army General Pavel Grachev, planned for this week, has been postponed until around 14 February. This was reported to an ITAR-TASS correspondent at the district's press center. According to the latter's information, the aims of the visit have not as yet undergone any changes. These are: to inspect the guards tank division in Perm and a number of other units of the district. It is not ruled out that Pavel Grachev will also visit leading enterprises of the defense complexes in Perm, Samara, and Ulyanovsk Oblasts.
