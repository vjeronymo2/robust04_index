Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the press review] [Text] On Cameroon's expansionist move, THE VANGUARD reports that it has now engaged in a fresh border dispute with its northern neighbor, the Chad Republic, despite Cameroon's claim to Nigeria's territory, that is the Bakassi Peninsula. The paper also adds that border skirmishes were reported between Cameroon and Chadian forces. Based on this, it reports that 10 Cameroonians have been arrested by the Chadians, but THE VANGUARD did not say whether they were soldiers or civilians.
