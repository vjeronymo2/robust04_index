930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Clayhithe Clayhithe, the investment company, achieved profits of Pounds 724,000 in the back half of the year to March 31, which enabled it to partly offset the first-half loss and report a reduced deficit of Pounds 366,000 for the year. The previous year's loss amounted to Pounds 963.000. The dividend is maintained at 2.5p via an unchanged final of 1.75p.
