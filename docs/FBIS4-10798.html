BFN [Text] On 17 April, militiamen and reserve troops promotion and award presentation ceremonies, as well as regional defense system exercises, were held as part of Havana's defense day in tribute to the Giron victory. The ceremonies were held in 10 de Octubre, Guanabacoa, and El Cotorro Municipalities. Jorge Lezcano, member of the Politburo and president of the provincial defense council, and Brigadier General Samuel Rodiles, a member of the Central Committee and deputy chief of the Western Army, were in attendance. At the ceremony in Hermanos Gomez Polytechnical School, 10 de Octubre Municipality, Lezcano stressed the praiseworthiness of the thousands of Havana residents who are, in the midst of severe economic limitations, preparing militarily to save the fatherland and the Revolution.
