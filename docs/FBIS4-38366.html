BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Paris, 27 May -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who has taken part in the two-day international conference here to work out a pact on stability in Europe, has left Paris's Orly airport for Moscow. In a conversation with an ITAR-TASS correspondent not long before his departure, he said that "Russia supported the French initiative for the conclusion of this pact, helped to implement and formulate the initiative, and assisted the success of the Paris conference." Delegations from 57 states and international organizations took part in the conference.
