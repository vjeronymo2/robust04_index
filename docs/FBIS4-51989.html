BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- Rice-transplanting has begun in the countryside of Korea. Co-operative farmers have brought up sturdy rice seedlings despite the unfavorable weather in spring this year. They do farming on a scientific and technical basis as required by the chuche method. This year, too, they have started rice-transplanting when the right time came. Rice-transplanting, one of the most important farming processes, begins in early May and is carried out in 20 odd days by intensive efforts.
