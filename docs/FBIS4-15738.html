BFN [Text] In Madrid today, Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez signed a treaty of friendship and cooperation between the two countries. This morning the Russian president laid a wreath at the memorial to those who sacrificed their lives for Spain. Then he visited Madrid city hall. In his speech Boris Yeltsin stressed that Russia strives for the development of diverse ties with Spain, on the basis of cordiality, mutual liking, and warmth between the peoples of the two countries. The Russian president also held a meeting with members of the presidium and leaders of parliamentary groups in the Spanish Congress of Deputies. He also addressed representatives of both chambers of the General Cortes. This evening Boris Yeltsin will meet with Spanish businessmen, RIA reports.
