Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] this morning met with Viktor Posuvalyuk, the special envoy of Russian President Yeltsin, for the fourth time in two days. At the meeting, they examined the latest developments and contacts on the implementation of the [UN] Security Council Resolution 904 on the massacre at the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron, as well as the forthcoming meeting of the volunteers [as heard] due to take place in Cairo on 24 March on [Palestinian] security.
