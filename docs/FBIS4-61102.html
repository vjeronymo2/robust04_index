BFN [Report by correspondent ITAR-TASS Anatoliy Gordeyev] [Text] Makeyevka (Donetsk Oblast), 2 May--According to preliminary reports, 12 miners died today at the Sovetskaya mine of the Makeyevugol production association, when a methane explosion occurred during the first shift. The cause of the accident is being investigated. A government commission headed by Deputy Prime Minister Vasyl Yevtukhov has arrived at the site of the tragedy. Acting Ukrainian Prime Minister Yefim Zvyahilskyy has also arrived in Makeyevka.
