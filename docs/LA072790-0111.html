July 27, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

HIGHLAND TALK WINS LAMB CHOP

Highland Talk, ridden by John Velasquez, took the lead entering the stretch and then drew away to beat Etoile Eternelle by three lengths in Thursday's Lamb Chop Purse for fillies and mares at Belmont Park.

Etoile Eternelle finished two lengths ahead of Gottagetitdone in the field of six.
