BFN [Editorial Report] Havana PRENSA LATINA in Spanish at 0122 GMT on 13 May reports that Cesar Gonzalez, manager of Veracuba Company, which is a branch of the Cubanacan, S.A. tourism group, says Veracuba's income is expected to increase by 20 percent from over $13 million in 1993 to $17 million in 1994. Veracuba, he explained, is involved in three activities: bus services, vehicle rentals and taxi services; and its purpose is to broaden Cubanacan's commercial activities.
