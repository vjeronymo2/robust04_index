October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COMMENTS ON THE TIMES' NEW FORMAT

The Sunday edition (Oct. 15) was a special surprise. The new arrangement of the summaries of news on Page 2 is a masterpiece of professional reporting and proof of your specific knowledge and understanding of your readers' interests.

Let this be an opportunity for expressing sincere congratulations to your valuable achievement. I have been in active contact with the press since 1926 and I have been your subscriber since 1957.

L.A. KRATKY

Laguna Hills
