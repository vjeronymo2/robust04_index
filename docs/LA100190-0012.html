October 1, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

A Novel Drama: Put aside your books on tape: Starting today you can listen to a book over the airwaves. KCRW-FM (89.9) teams with L.A. Theatre Works to present a dramatization of Frank Norris' 1899 novel "McTeague." The serial drama about turn-of-the-century San Francisco will air on "Everyday Playhouse" Monday through Thursday from 2:30 to 3 p.m. Starting Saturday, the play can be heard on Saturdays at 10 p.m. Some famous voices will grace the reading: Stacy Keach, Carol Kane, JoBeth Williams, Michael York, Amy Irving, Rue McClanahan, Marsha Mason and others will bring the stormy romance to life. BETH KLEID
