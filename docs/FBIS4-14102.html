BFN [Aharon Barne'a video report from Cairo] [Excerpts] There will be no agreement between the PLO and Israel unless a timetable is determined for the release of all Palestinian prisoners, including Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] members. This was said today by Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, 'Arafat's adviser. It was reported in Cairo today that the delegations will continue their meetings next week. The date for the signing of the agreement has still not been determined. [Begin recording] [passage omitted] [Al-Tibi] Without an agreed-upon timetable for the release of all the Palestinian prisoners there will be no agreement. [Barne'a] Are you referring to all the prisoners, including Hamas people? [Al-Tibi] All the prisoners are Palestinians, and the PLO is the legitimate leadership of the entire Palestinian people, therefore it is responsible for all prisoners, regardless of their affiliation. [end recording]
