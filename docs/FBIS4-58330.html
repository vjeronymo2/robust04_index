BFN [Text] In a banquet given in honor of the Vietnamese president on Saturday night, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani delivered a speech in which he described Vietnam as a country which enjoys a very good position in the minds of the Iranian people. He added: The period during which you defended your country and the acts of heroism undertaken by the Vietnamese people in the face of Western invaders have reached epic status in Iran. Your successes warmed the hearts of independence-seeking peoples throughout the world and you gave valuable historic lessons to humanity. Our country's president expressed the hope that, during the period of reconstruction, too, the Vietnamese nation would continue to pursue the correct and independent path it has embarked on towards reforming its economic structure and that it would serve as a good model for the world's other countries with the cooperation of the Islamic Republic of Iran and through the exchange of experiences between the two revolutionary nations. The Vietnamese president, for his part, delivered a speech in which he praised the Islamic Republic of Iran and the gains made by our country.
