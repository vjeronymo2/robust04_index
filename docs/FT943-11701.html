940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Toyota UK engines for Turkey Toyota said its British unit Toyota Motor Manufacturing (UK) had begun exporting TMUK-made engines to Turkey, Reuter reports from Tokyo. TMUK will export 2,000 1.6-litre engines this year to Toyota's Turkish venture Toyotasa in Adapazari for use in Corolla cars. The number will rise to 10,000 engines in 1995. Toyotasa will assemble 2,000 Corollas in 1994 and 10,000 1.6-litre Corollas and 10,000 1.3-litre Corollas in 1995. Toyotasa is owned 50 per cent by Sabanci Group, 40 per cent by Toyota and 10 per cent by Mitsui.
