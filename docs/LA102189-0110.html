October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COMMENTS ON THE TIMES' NEW FORMAT

Congratulations on the new format. The little boxed background are especially helpful in recalling material once digested but since misplaced. This comes close to fulfilling an old dream of mine -- a newspaper so presented that the reader can see the relationship among stories printed from all parts of the world, including home. And while I usually "study" my morning newspaper, those days do come along when one has to glance and run.

One problem (isn't there always?) the bright orange flier announcing the "Good News" was slicker than a banana peel.

Thanks for all the good thought that went into these changes.

WILLA SIBBS

Palm Desert
