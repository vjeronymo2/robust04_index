BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, Presidential Council member, this afternoon received brother Ambassador Badr Hammam, the Egyptian assistant foreign minister, who is visiting our country. The ambassador reported on his talks in the economic and commercial capital Aden, within the framework of Egypt's brotherly efforts to contain the current crisis. His talks dealt with the latest developments in the crisis and how further complications can be averted. Brother 'Abd-al-Ghani told Hammam that Yemen appreciates brother President Mubarak's sincere efforts to contain and resolve the crisis, emphasizing that [words indistinct] with these efforts so that Yemen's unity, security, and safety are assured. The meeting was attended by 'Ata Harun, Egypt's ambassador to our country.
