Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just received a report by the LUR agency that the Azerbaijani air force is bombing towns and villages in Nagorno-Karabakh. At 1100 hours, the Azerbaijani air force violated the airspace of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic and carried out an air strike against several populated localities in Askeranskiy rayon. Casualty figures are being clarified.
