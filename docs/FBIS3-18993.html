Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Euro-Arab Delegates Acknowledge Importance of Continuing Peace Process"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] The third Euro-Arab meeting, held on Tuesday [8 March], brought together 29 delegates from 14 Arab countries and several Members of the European Parliament [EP], who discussed political and economic support for the Middle East peace process, stability and security in the Arab world and Europe, and Euro-Arab economic cooperation. Recent events, especially the Hebron massacre, were in the forefront at this time, as participants observed a minute of silence in tribute to the victims at the opening of the session. All the delegations acknowledged the importance of continuing the peace process, noted Mr. Claude Cheysson (Socialist, French) at the press conference following the conclusion of the meeting. Participants also insisted on the importance of providing protection for the Palestinian population under United Nations auspices.
