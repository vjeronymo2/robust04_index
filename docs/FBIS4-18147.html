BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Mar. 31 (A.A.) -- Social Democratic Populist Party (SHP) mayoral candidate Vahit Yigit and independent candidate Esref Tanriverdi filed a formal protest to the election board as great numbers of stamped ballots were found in a dump. Yigit and Tanriverdi requested the nullification of the election. Officials of the Motherland Party (ANAP) filed an official complaint because 6,000 ballots in the Black Sea city of Trabzon were found. As the local election board rejected the objection, ANAP officials said that they will apply to a higher board. The candidate of fundamentalist Prosperity Party (RP) had won over the ANAP candidate with by narrow margin of 2,130 votes. The former president of the high election board Orhan Aydin said, after reports on huge amounts of ballots in dumps and schools that the March 27 local elections might be canceled if the ballots found would change the result.
