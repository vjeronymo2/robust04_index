921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Industrial boost for Sweden Swedish industry can expect its competitiveness to improve by 15 per cent next year as a result of the floating of the krona, according to forecasts from the ministry of finance, writes Robert Taylor in Stockholm. At the same time, unemployment will rise from 4.8 per cent this year to 6.2 per cent in 1993 and 7.0 per cent in 1994 while disposable income will fall by 3.8 per cent next year after a 2.6 per cent improvement in 1992.
