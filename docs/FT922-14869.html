920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: B Wehmiller recovers strongly to Pounds 2.25m BARRY WEHMILLER Inter-national, the Cheshire-based packaging and process machinery manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.25m for the six months to January 31. The outcome showed a 48 per cent decline on the comparable period, but represented a sharp improvement on the second half of last year when profits dived to just Pounds 715,000. Mr Nigel McLean, chairman, said that the success of last October's Pounds 12.9m rights issue and cash generated from operations left the group with nil gearing. All units were now operating profitably and a modest increase in order backlog showed a slight improvement in demand, he said. Turnover dipped 9 per cent to Pounds 35.7m. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.4p on the increased capital, from earnings of 3.9p (8.4p)
