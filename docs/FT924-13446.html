921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / MI6 link with toolmaker alleged A FORMER executive with Matrix Churchill, the Coventry-based machine tool makers, was working for MI6, the secret intelligence service while promoting the company's exports, the Old Bailey heard. Mr Mark Gutteridge, a former export sales manager with the Iraqi-owned company, worked for MI6 with the approval of Mr Paul Henderson, the company's managing director, Mr Geoffrey Robertson QC, for Mr Henderson, told the court. He was cross-examining Mr Ronald Ash, a former defence project executive with Matrix Churchill who worked with Mr Gutteridge in building the company's trading links with Iraq. Mr Henderson, Mr Trevor Abraham, a former commercial director with the company and Mr Peter Allen, a former sales director, all deny breaking export regulations to sell Iraq machine tools with a military application. The trial continues today.
