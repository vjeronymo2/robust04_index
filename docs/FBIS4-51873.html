BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 4 (KCNA) -- A Malian joint seminar on the chuche idea and education was held in Bamako of Mali on the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Modibo Kane Cisse, chairman of the Malian National Committee for the Study of the Chuche Idea, in an opening address paid greatest regards to President Kim Il-song who founded the immortal chuche idea to greatly contribute to the development of the human thought and led the national liberation and the socialist revolution and construction to victory to perform immortal feats in human history and wished him a long life in good health. In his report on the subject "The Chuche Idea and Education" Secretary General of the Committee N. Coulibaly elaborated on the founding of the chuche idea and its historical significance, the brilliant application of the chuche idea in Korea, the achievements in education in particular, adding the road to be followed by the African people is the way indicated by the chuche idea. Messages of greetings to President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the seminar. A national seminar on President Kim Il-song's famous work "For the Ultimate Solution of the Rural Question Under the Banner of the Socialist Rural Theses" was sponsored by the Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Resources of Guinea and the Society for the Study of the Chuche Methods of Farming at the Kim Il-song Institute of Agricultural Science in Guinea on the occasion of the April 15 fete. Marcel, director of the general bureau of technology dissemination of the Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Resources, in his speech said President Kim Il-song in the work reviewed the brilliant achievements made by the Korean people in implementing the socialist rural theses and indicated the tasks and ways for the final solution of the rural question. His work is a highly important work by which the Korean people as well as the developing countries should be guided in settling the rural question, he noted. Alpha Toure, director of the Kim Il-song Institute of Agricultural Science, said in his speech: "His excellency the great President Kim Il-song in his classical work expounded the tasks and ways for the final solution of the rural question so as to elevate the agriculture of Guinea and the work of the institute to a high stage. "His excellency President Kim Il-song is a father of the Korean people as well as the Guinean people and the great sun of the world's people." A message of greetings to President Kim Il-song was adopted at the seminar. Seminars on "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country", a famous work of President Kim Il-song, were held in Uganda, Nigeria and Togo and at the Nepal Institute for Chuche Studies and the Marsaxlokk Club of the Malta Labour Party. Seminars on the part of national reunification from his work "Let Us Bring the Advantages of Socialism in Our Country Into Full Play" and his work "Non-Aligned Information Services Must Contribute to the People's Cause of Independence" were held by the Guyana torchlight Taekwondo Group for the Study of the Chuche Idea of Comrade Kim Il-song and the Society for the Study of the Chuche Idea of the University of Madagascar and a seminar on his work "Enhancing the Role of the Popular Masses is the Guarantee for Victory of the Cause of Independence" by the study group on the chuche idea and works of Comrade Kim Il-song of the Lagos State Wing of the Nigeria Union of Teachers.
