941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / Weekend Money: CGT indexation allowances - November --------------------------------------------------------------------- The table shows capital gains tax indexation allowances for assets sold in November. Multiply the original cost of the asset by the figure for the month in which you bought it. Subtract the result from the proceeds of your sale: the balance will be your taxable gain or loss. Suppose you bought shares for Pounds 6,000 in November 1985 and sold them in November 1994 for Pounds 13,000. Multiplying the original cost by the November 1985 figure of 1.515 gives a total of Pounds 9,090. Subtracting that from Pounds 13,000 gives a capital gain of Pounds 3,910 which is within the CGT allowance of Pounds 5,800. If selling shares bought before April 6 1982, you should use the March 1982 figure.
