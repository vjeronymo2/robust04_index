941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Loyalist leaders in Maze prison talks Paramilitary leaders held talks with jailed members of the loyalist Ulster Freedom Fighters and the Ulster Volunteer Force in Ulster's top security Maze prison, the Northern Ireland Office said. The discussions concerned a possible ceasefire in response to the IRA's 40-day-old truce. Scaled-down response considered, Page 11
