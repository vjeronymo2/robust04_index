931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Clyde Blowers back in black at Pounds 205,000 Clyde Blowers, the producer of cleaning equipment for boilers, returned to the black in the year to August 31. Profits were Pounds 205,000 pre-tax, against losses of Pounds 141,000. Turnover grew to Pounds 7.41m (Pounds 4.63m). At the operating level, however, there were losses of Pounds 269,000 (Pounds 253,000), more than offset by income from investments of Pounds 474,000 (Pounds 264,000). Earnings were 14.73p (losses 4.3p) and the final dividend is 8p to make 12p (7.9p).
