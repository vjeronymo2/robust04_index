920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / World News In Brief: Mansell signs for Indy race team Formula One Motor Racing World champion Nigel Mansell will join film star Paul Newman's Newman-Haas racing team in the 1993 Indy Car World Series and the Indianapolis 500. Mansell, who on Sunday said he would retire from Formula One racing at the end of the season, will team up with American Mario Andretti, 52. Mansell's deal is believed to be worth about 3m pounds.
