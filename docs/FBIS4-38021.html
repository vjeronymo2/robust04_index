BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, today received brothers 'Ali al-Qutaybi, ambassador of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia; Sayf al-Mansuri, ambassador of the United Arab Emirates [UAE]; 'Awad Mahfuz Bakathir, ambassador of the Sultanate of Oman; and Mansur al-'Awadi, charge d'affaires of the State of Kuwait to our country who conveyed to the brother president verbal messages from the leaders of their countries in response to the verbal messages the brother president addressed to his brothers Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques Fahd Bin-'Abd al-'Aziz, monarch of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia; His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan, UAE president; His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id, Sultan of Oman; and His Highness Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, amir of the State of Kuwait during his meeting with the ambassadors of the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC] states to our country last week. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', learned that the messages deal with developments in our country and issues of common interest between our country and the GCC states.
