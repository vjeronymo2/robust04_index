BFN [Text] According to information provided by the National Security Ministry, a high-powered explosive device in a cardboard box was found and defused on the No. 5 Baku-to-Moscow passenger train during a joint search by the employees of the republic's National Security Ministry and the Interior Ministry at Baku train station some days ago. Two citizens suspected of the sabotage act were detained. The National Security Ministry's investigation department has instituted criminal proceedings and launched an investigation.
