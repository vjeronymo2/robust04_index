930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / International Company News: Golden Vale buys in the Netherlands GOLDEN VALE, the Irish dairy group, is acquiring Vonk Food Holland, a producer of processed cheese, for a maximum Fl 23m (Pounds 8m). Golden Vale is also taking on Vonk's debts of Fl 54m. In a separate deal the Irish group is to acquire a 61 per cent interest in Vonkpol, a processed cheese manufacturer established in Poland, for a maximum Fl 13m.
