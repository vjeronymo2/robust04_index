930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: Home secretary defends exclusion order Home secretary Michael Howard was satisfied that John Matthews, 22, was involved in terrorism, the Home Office announced last night. Mr Matthews was yesterday escorted to Belfast by two officials under an exclusion order, despite earlier being cleared by magistrates of an attempted taxi bomb attack on Downing Street. Material inadmissible as court evidence had been studied by the Home Office, a statement said.
