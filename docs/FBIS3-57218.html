Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Brussels, 21 Jan -- Volodymyr Vasylenko, Ukraine's ambassador to the Benelux countries, met Ambassador Napolitano [name as received], coordinator of EC/CSCE sanctions against the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. The sides discussed a wide range of issues connected to Ukraine's participation in implementing UN sanctions against Yugoslavia. Individual attention was paid to cooperation by the customs and border services of Ukraine with the authorities of Danube countries and with the missions for facilitating sanctions in these countries, and to the possibilities of rendering financial and technical assistance to Ukraine, which is needed for efficiently securing the sanctions regime. The Ukrainian ambassador drew Napolitano's attention to the necessity of stepping up the search for practical ways of compensating losses experienced by third countries, including our state, as a result of introducing sanctions against Yugoslavia.
