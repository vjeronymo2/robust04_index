Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Muhammad Husni Mubarak received the DPRK's education minister today. Education Minister Dr. Husayn Kamil Baha'-al-Din attended the meeting. Following the session, the Korean minister said that he conveyed to President Mubarak an invitation from Korean President Kim Il-song to visit the DPRK. He added that he discussed with President Mubarak various regional and international issues, ways of developing ties of friendship and cooperation between the two countries, and ways of bolstering the Nonaligned Movement. Meanwhile, Dr. Baha'-al-Din said that the Korean minister delivered a message to President Mubarak from President Kim Il-song. He added that President Mubarak accepted the invitation to visit the DPRK at a date that will be set later.
