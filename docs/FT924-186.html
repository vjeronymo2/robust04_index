921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / Scotland promised a greater say MR Ian Lang, Scottish secretary, yesterday promised that Scotland would have a greater say in its own affairs as a result of Mr John Major's 'taking stock' exercise on its constitutional position - results of which will be unveiled early next year - but stopped short of offering any home rule. Mr Lang said: 'New ways must be found of enhancing Scotland's distinctive contribution to the United Kingdom. As a result, Scots should have more say than before over their own lives.'
