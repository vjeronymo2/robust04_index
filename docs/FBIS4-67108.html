Tretyakov, in his meeting with Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammed Besharati, expressed hope that grounds would be paved for bilateral especially in the campaign against traffickers. [sentence as received] He added that exchange of visits as well as sharing of experience and views by Iranian and Russian officials would help strengthen bilateral relations. Besharati in return said Russia can play a "positive" role in solving regional disputes. He expressed satisfaction with the two countries political and economic relations and called for their consolidation and deepening. The Iranian official hailed the importance of Irano-Russian "historical" ties. Besharati urged Russia to deal more actively with regional issues and said Russia as a permanent member of the United Nations Security Council can play an "important and effective" role on international scenes.
