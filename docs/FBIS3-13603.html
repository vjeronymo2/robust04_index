Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The United Nations, 17 Mar (INA)--Responsible diplomatic sources have denied the reports the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY carried, quoting UN sources, on the French position toward Ambassador Rolf Ekeus, chairman of the UN Special Commission. These sources added that the statements attributed to the French ambassador that were made to the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY by the UN sources were not accurate. The reported statements are groundless, the sources noted.
