940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / Fraud civil servant sued by government The government last night issued a writ against Mr Gordon Foxley - the former Ministry of Defence civil servant jailed this week for taking bribes -'and others' in an attempt to recover millions of pounds. Police have said that only Pounds 500,000 of the bribes from foreign arms companies that Mr Foxley channelled into Swiss bank accounts has been traced.
