BFN [Text] Beijing, April 10 (XINHUA) -- Ren Jianxin, president of the Supreme People's Court of China, met with G.P.S. de Silva, chief justice of Sri Lanka's Supreme Court, here today. The two sides exchanged views on the friendly contacts and cooperation between the two supreme courts. Silva and his party arrived here yesterday at Ren's invitation. Apart from Beijing, the Sri Lanka guests are scheduled to visit Shenyang and Dalian, two cities in Northeast China.
