BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- Delegates of Japan-Korea friendship organisations denounced the Japanese authorities' hostile acts against the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) on May 26. Akira Iwai, chairman of the Japan Committee for Supporting the Independent and Peaceful Reunification of Korea, Shigeru Tokoi, chairman of the Executive Committee of the Forum on the Human Rights of Korean Residents in Japan, Masako Yamashita, advisor to the Japan Women's Society and other delegates formed a fact-finding team to investigate the suppression of the Osaka organisation of Chongnyon and called for an immediate return of the documents and materials seized by the Osaka prefectural police to the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon. They demanded that the Japanese authorities apologize to Chongnyon for the suppression and immediately stop search of Chongnyon and its affiliated organisations, which is aimed at political crackdown.
