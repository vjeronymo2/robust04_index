May 17, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

BRITAIN;

OFFICIAL PROBE OF 747 BOMBING URGED

The opposition Labor Party issued a fresh call for an official British inquiry into the 1988 bombing of a Pan Am 747 airliner over Scotland that killed 270. The call, backed by families of British victims, follows a U.S. report that suggested the bombing could have been prevented by better security. The British government has said that an independent inquiry cannot take place until criminal investigations are complete.
