Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Salalah, 23 Mar (WAKH)--King Husayn of Jordan left Salalah today at the end of a private three-day visit to the Sultanate of Oman. The Jordanian monarch held several meetings with His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id in which bilateral relations and means to bolster them were reviewed, in addition to current developments in the Arab and international arenas and issues of mutual concern.
