BFN [Text] A media spokesman for the unified Central Command of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] and the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP] has said that intense pressure is being applied on their detainees inside the prisons of the Israeli occupation authorities to secure their approval of the Gaza-Jericho accord. The spokesman said in Damascus today that Israeli authorities have been seeking to strike a demeaning and cheap bargain with the detainees requiring them to undertake in writing that they support the Gaza-Jericho accord. The detainees, noted the spokesman, rejected the deal that seeks to deprive them of their legitimate right to pursue the fight against occupation and settlement activity. He urged human rights organizations to move to obtain freedom for the detainees and guarantee their right to free self-expression.
