Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Summary] According to experts, in the last two years there has been a significant increase in the availability of drugs in Georgia, mainly from Azerbaijan and the Tskhinval region. On 22 January, Georgian law enforcement bodies started a massive operation against drug dealers in Georgia. The republic was divided into nine sectors. In each the operation was headed by deputy security and internal affairs ministers. It involved 1,500 people. As a result of the operation, 20 machine guns and carbines and about 50 kilos of drugs were confiscated, and 12 stolen cars were recovered by 24 January. About 20 people were arrested. According to the spokesman of the Internal Affairs Ministry, some high ranking officials have been implicated in drug trafficking.
