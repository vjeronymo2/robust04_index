Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, March 9 (BTA) -- Deputy Prime Minister Evgeni Matinchev today received a government delegation of the Russian Federation, led by Chairman of the State Machine-Building Committee Anatoliy Ogurtsov. The meeting was attended by Deputy Prime Minister Valentin Karabashev, Chairman of the Power Engineering Committee Nikita Shervashidze, and Deputy Minister of Industry Racho Petrov. The prospects of Bulgarian-Russian relations in industry were on the agenda. Mr. Ogurtsov declared that Russian enterprises are now ready for wide-scale cooperation with Bulgaria and for all-round assistance. He stressed that this is the official stand of the Russian Government. The sides considered some specific aspects of cooperation in power and mechanical engineering, the debts which Bulgaria and Russia owe each other and some other problems. Mr. Karabashev familiarized the guests with the Bulgarian Government's decision on reciprocal settlement of obligations and asked Mr. Ogurtsov to assist for a prompt reply from the Russian Government, containing its specific proposals on the matter, the Government said in a press release.
