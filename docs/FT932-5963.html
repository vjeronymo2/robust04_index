930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: Building office complex in Kent Pharmaceutical companies Pfizer and Wellcome have placed contracts with BALLAST NEDAM CONSTRUCTION worth over Pounds 14m. At Sandwich, Kent, a 13,300 sq metres, four-storey office block and car park will be constructed for Pfizer at a cost of Pounds 11.4m. Work has begun and the project will be completed in August 1994. For the Wellcome Foundation a laboratory at Dartford, Kent, will be refurbished in a Pounds 2.7m contract running from June to May 1994. The award of these contracts brings new gains for the south east regional office to some Pounds 18.5m. Ballast Nedam Construction is a member company of British Aerospace and a part of the international contracting group Ballast Nedam BV of the Netherlands.
