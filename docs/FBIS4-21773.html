BFN [Excerpts] Police say the killing of 28-year-old Yosi Zandani in Bene 'Ayish near Gedera was apparently a terrorist attack. Zandani was found strangled and stabbed to death early yesterday morning. A police spokesman said a letter in Arabic claimed the murder had been carried out to avenge the death of a Gaza Arab. Yitzhaq Rottenberg, who died after being wounded in a terrorist attack on Tuesday, will be laid to rest this afternoon. [passage omitted]
