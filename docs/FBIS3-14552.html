Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] One Arab was killed and another 45 were wounded this morning in riots in the territories in response to the killing of six Fatah members in the Jabalyah refugee camp last night. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that 17-year-old 'Umar 'Awwad al-Qabbani was killed by soldiers' gunfire in the al-Burayj refugee camp. His body was snatched away and buried. Another 15 Arabs were wounded by soldiers' gunfire in Jabalyah, Bayt Hanun, and Gaza. Riots erupted this morning in most cities and refugee camps in Judaea and Samaria. The security forces clamped a curfew on Nabulus and its environs after 30 locals were wounded by soldiers' gunfire. A curfew was also imposed on Tulkarm and its adjacent refugee camps. Riots and disturbances also occurred in Qalqilyah, Bethlehem, Ramallah, and Janin.
