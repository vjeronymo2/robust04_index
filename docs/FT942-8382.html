940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Alexanders Hldgs advances to Pounds 0.4m Alexanders Holdings, the Glasgow-based Ford main dealer, showed further improvement in the half-year to March 31. On sales up by Pounds 14m to Pounds 52.5m, pre-tax profits advanced to Pounds 406,000. The figure compared with Pounds 32,000 before tax in the previous first half and Pounds 512,000 in the full year to end-September. An interim dividend of 0.3p is declared, payable from per share of 0.89p (0.06p). The company said it was in negotiations to make acquisitions within the sector. It had also made two non-board appointments - a director of dealer operations and a director of business development.
