Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 3 -- President Le Duc Anh today held talks with Mongolian President P. Ochirbat on a visit to Vietnam. Speaking at the talks, President Le Duc Anh and President Ochirbat informed each other of the situation of each country, and exchanged views on measures aimed at developing the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Mongolia and the international and regional issues of common concern. The two sides expressed their satisfaction at the consolidation and development of the traditional friendship and cooperation between the two countries and discussed measures to further promote these relations in the new situation. The two sides agreed that an agreement on cooperation in economy, science and trade would be signed soon and will encourage and that the two sides would encourage and create favourable conditions for businessmen of the two countries to accelerate their co-operation in the forms of direct investment and joint venture. Talking about international and regional issues, President Le Duc Anh and President Ochirbat paid much attention to the new steps of development in the Pacific-Asian region, and expressed their desire to broaden the friendly and cooperative relations of mutual benefit with the countries in the region aimed at building the Asian-Pacific region into a region of peace stability, friendship and cooperation in conformity with the aspiration and fundamental and lasting interests of the peoples in the region. The Mongolian president invited President Le Duc Anh to visit Mongolia. President Anh accepted the invitation with pleasure. The talks took place in an atmosphere of friendship and mutual understanding.
