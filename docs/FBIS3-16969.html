Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Antisubmarine Exercise in the Black Sea Fleet"] [Text] On the evening on 17 March, near the Crimean shores, a large-scale Black Sea Fleet exercise started. It was meant to develop methods of conducting combat operations against submarines of an imaginary adversary with an active air force counteraction. On the morning of 18 March, as many as 25 ships, two submarines, and five auxiliary ships were already involved in the exercise. They occupied the area from Donuzlav (western shore) to Kerch (in the east). The exercise had been coordinated with Russia's Ministry of Defense. As our correspondent was told at the Press Center of Ukraine's Ministry of Defense, the Black Sea Fleet Command had not informed Ukraine's Ministry of Defense of this exercise.
