940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: FW Thorpe rises 51% to Pounds 2.51m FW Thorpe's pre-tax profits for the year ended June 30 rose from Pounds 1.66m to Pounds 2.51m, an increase of 51 per cent. Turnover at the electronic and electrical equipment company was 21 per cent ahead at Pounds 19.5m against Pounds 16.1m. Earnings per share jumped to 13.7p (8.6p). The proposed final dividend is 2.2p (1.87p) for a 3.2p (2.67p) total.
