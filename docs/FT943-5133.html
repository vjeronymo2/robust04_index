940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / World Trade News: EU starts probe into microdisk prices The European Commission said it was investigating charges that imports of micro-disks from the US, Mexico and Malaysia were sold at unfairly low prices in the European Union, Reuter reports from Brussels. The inquiry followed a complaint by the Committee of European Diskette Manufacturers that prices at which 3.5in microdisks were sold in the US and Mexico did not permit recovery of costs plus a reasonable profit margin.
