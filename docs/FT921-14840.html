920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Belfox options trading delayed TRADING of share options on Belfox, Belgium's futures and options exchange, has been postponed, says Belfox general manager Mr Jos Schmitt, Reuter reports from Brussels. It had been due to start in late January or early February. Mr Schmitt said the launch had been delayed because trading simulation had not started yet in Belgium. A simulation was currently running in London, he added.
