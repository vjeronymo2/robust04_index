BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 8 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat has placed a veto on the Law on Currency Regulation which was approved recently by the Great State Hural. The press office of the president explained in this connection that the President P. Ochirbat proceeded from the fact that the new law was not keeping with the current state of economy in the country as well as the international practice.
