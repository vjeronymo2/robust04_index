940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / Developer chosen for Welsh village Redrow Homes has been chosen to build a Pounds 10m centre for Victoria, an urban village being developed on the site of the former garden festival at Ebbw Vale in south Wales. The 20-acre centre will include shops, offices, community facilities and about 200 houses. The project is the second phase of the development of the garden festival site by Victoria Partnership.
