BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 12 (XINHUA) -- The Shanghai office of the First National Bank of Boston, a large bank of the United States, was open here today. This is the 64th office of foreign financial institutions that have opened in this commercial and financial center of China. According to administrative regulations issued by the Chinese Government regarding foreign financial institutions, such offices can be upgraded into branches after two years in the country. Local sources said the majority of the offices have plans to become branches. Ira Stepanian, president of First National of Boston, who attended the opening of the bank's office. Said that his bank is confident in China's financial market. He expressed his hope that the bank would soon set up at least five such offices in China, which would be soon upgraded into branches. He disclosed that the Beijing office of this world-known bank will also be opened soon. First National of Boston, founded in 1784, has over 100 branches and offices worldwide, and it now has branches in Hong Kong, Taipei, Tokyo, Singapore, Seoul and Manila.
