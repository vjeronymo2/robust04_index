910416 FT 16 APR 91 / World Trade News: Germans order giant press Deutsche-Airbus, the German aerospace group, has ordered the largest metal forming press of its kind from GEC-Alsthom, the Anglo-French power equipment and engineering joint venture, writes Charles Leadbeater, Industrial Editor. The Pounds 3m order is for a 1,500 tonne press, to form 12 metre by 3 metre aluminium titanium alloy sheets used to make aircraft fuselages and wings. The investment in the press, which will be manufactured at ACB, the GEC-Alsthom subsidiary based at Nantes in France, is a signal of Deutsche Airbus' growing determination to expand its activities.
