BFN [Text] Unal Erkan, state of emergency region governor, has announced that 150 terrorists were killed and 50 terrorists were captured in operations conducted in the state of emergency region in the past week. Addressing the inauguration of the communications satellite ground station in Batman's Sason District, Erkan declared that the terrorist organization has reached the point of collapse as a result of the assistance the regional population has extended to the security forces in their joint struggle against terrorism. Pointing out that the number of terrorists surrendering to the security forces is increasing, despite the groundless propaganda that terrorists who surrender are mistreated, Erkan explained that the organization's sources of income from narcotics smuggling have also been wiped out.
