940718 FT 18 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): First Technology - Other companies On Thursday, the designer and maker of crash dummies and safety sensors for the car industry, is expected to report full-year profits of about Pounds 4m, up from Pounds 2.34m the previous year, but still below the Pounds 6.7m of 1989-90. The dividend is expected to treble to 3p, payable from earnings per share of 16.7p (10.8p).
