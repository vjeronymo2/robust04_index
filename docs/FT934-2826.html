931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Drayton Recovery Drayton Recovery Trust reported net assets per share up from 77p to 132p over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 725,000, against Pounds 796,000 for the previous 12 1/2 months. Earnings per share were 8.77p (9.63p) and a proposed final dividend of 4p (3.95p) makes a total of 8p, compared with 7.95p, which included a special of 2p.
