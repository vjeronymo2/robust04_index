940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / Plastic-card fraud costs down 21% Plastic-card fraud cost the banking industry Pounds 129.8m last year - a 21 per cent drop on the Pounds 165m losses during 1992. Nearly three-quarters of this went through shops and stores while the amount taken from cash-point machines, perhaps the greatest source of public unease, was only Pounds 2.5m.
