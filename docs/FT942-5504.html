940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / Boost for Swedish vehicle sector The Swedish government yesterday agreed to help fund a joint research programme with Volvo, Saab Automobile, Scania and the country's component suppliers to bolster the competitiveness of the Swedish motor industry, writes Hugh Carnegy in Stockholm. The state will provide half of the SKr180m (Pounds 15.5m) to be spent on the project over the next three years, adding to the SKr600m already spent annually by the government on backing industrial research and development.
