930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Accountants' TV services clash THE BATTLE for the television screens of Britain's accountants has hotted up with a solicitor's letter from one of the two competitors demanding that remarks allegedly made about its financial status be withdrawn. Accountancy Television, part of the BBC Select late night TV broadcasts, has sent a request to Television Education Network (TEN) that it dissociate itself from statements purportedly made by sales staff. No response has been received. A potential customer has written to Accountancy Television about remarks they claim were made to them by a TEN representative. Mr Chris Bunyon, marketing officer for TEN, confirmed the letter had been received but denied the allegations. 'It is not our policy to defame the opposition. We are not aware it has been happening.'
