920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / Kouchner accuses French doctors of prolonging the prison strike DR Bernard Kouchner, above, the French health and humanitarian affairs minister, yesterday accused French doctors of writing bogus sick notes for prison guards, enabling them to continue the strike which has paralysed French jails, Reuter reports from Paris. 'One doctor filled out 31 certificates and many wrote out a dozen certificates each,' said Dr Kouchner.
