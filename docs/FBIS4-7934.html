BFN [Text] Warsaw, April 6 -- The Sejm Committee for Constitutional Accountability on Wednesday decided to submit to parliament a motion to drop proceedings against architects of martial law Gen. Wojciech Jaruzelski and Gen. Czeslaw Kiszczak, charged with destroying the 1982-1989 protocols of the Polish United Workers Party (PZPR) Politburo meetings. The committee took the decision with 10 votes for, 4 against and no abstentions.
