BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] In a statement at the airport on his return from a visit to Turkey as the guest of Ankara businessmen and industrialists, Prime Minister Hakki Atun said he observed that Turkish businessmen and industrialists want to invest in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus [TRNC], given the important stage it is in. Atun said his visit reached its goal in this respect. The prime minister pointed out that he discussed the work of the Turkish Cypriot coalition government during his talks with the officials of the Turkish Government and state, and that they jointly assessed the latest political situation. He said: I was reassured that Turkey will try to continue to extend its contributions to the TRNC as before. Atun added that his coalition government will continue to fulfill its duties in peace of mind.
