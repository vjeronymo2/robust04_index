BFN [Excerpts] An offensive by Golboddin-Dostam's militia was aborted this morning by Islamic Army forces. A source from the General Command of Kabul Garrison told a BIA military correspondent that the infamous militia of Golboddin-Dostam launched a failed offensive at about 0500 this morning from an easterly direction. It was aborted in the first few hours as a result of the resistance and defense of mojahedin and the Islamic Army forces. The rebellious militia suffered 40 casualties and dozens of injuries and a number of their light and heavy weapons were destroyed. According to another report, following their defeat, the failed militia subjected residential areas and public utility to blind rocket attacks. As a result of those savage attacks five compatriots were injured. Similarly, as a result of a rocket landing in the contagious diseases hospital two of our compatriots were slightly injured and the hospital building was damaged. [passage omitted] After a failed attack in Samangan Province, the infamous militia faced a counteroffensive by forces of the Islamic Army of Afghanistan, and three enemy positions were conquered. A DShK machine-gun, 10 Kalashnikovs, a PK machine-gun, along with a quantity of the militia's ammunitions have been captured by the Islamic Army Mojahedin in the operation. The enemy's offensive started from the east and west of (Shorotyar) in Dehkoh where it was heavily dislodged as a result of joint operations of the Islamic Army forces under the command of the great jehadi commanders esteemed Mawlawi Din Mohammad and esteemed Khoshbakht. The enemy suffered heavy losses.
