DATES: Comments must be filed on or before February 25, 1994, and reply comments on or before March 14, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 93&hyph;316, RM&hyph;8403] Radio Broadcasting Services; Douglas and Unionville, GA AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: This document requests comments on a petition by Orchon Media, Inc., permittee of Station WKZZ(FM), Channel 223A, Douglas, Georgia, requesting the substitution of Channel 223C3 for Channel 223A at Douglas, Georgia, and the reallotment of Channel 223C3 from Douglas to Unionville, Georgia, and the modification of the construction permit for Station WKZZ(FM) to specify Unionville as its community of license, in accordance with Section 1.420(i) of the Commission's rules. The coordinates for Channel 223C3 at Unionville are North Latitude 31&hyph;31&hyph;05 and West Longitude 83&hyph;20&hyph;43. DATES: Comments must be filed on or before February 25, 1994, and reply comments on or before March 14, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Federal Communications Commission, Washington, DC 20554. In addition to filing comments with the FCC, interested parties should serve the petitioner, or its counsel or consultant, as follows: Robert S. Stone, McCampbell & Young, 2021 Plaza Tower, P.O.
