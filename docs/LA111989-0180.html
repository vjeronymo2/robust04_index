November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski recorded a $79 profit Saturday when one of his $10 exacta boxes, Silver Hatchet and Best Picture in the third race hit for a $21.80 payoff for $2. The profit pushed Mieszerski back into the profit column. He started the meeting Wednesday with a mythical bankroll of $1,000 which now stands at $1,077.

Each racing day Bob will make at least one bet. He could make a straight win bet, he could make a daily double bet, he could make an exacta bet, he could make other exotic bets, or any combination of bets. But, he will make at least one bet.

For today, Bob's bets are $10 daily triples from Defend Your Man in the fourth to Keen Night, Naturally Mitch, Rusty Attitude and His Legacy in the fifth to Sun Streak in the sixth.

Beginning Bankroll: $1,000

Saturday's Profit: $79

Current Bankroll: $1,077
