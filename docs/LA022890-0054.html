February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Fraud Seen at 60% of Failed S&Ls: L. William Seidman, chairman of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. and the Resolution Trust Corp., estimated that "serious criminal activity" took place at 60% of the more than 300 seized S&Ls. He told a House Ways and Means oversight subcommittee hearing that a special "fraud squad" has been formed to prosecute culprits.
