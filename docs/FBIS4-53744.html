BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 21 -- Deputy Premier Nguyen Khanh visited Tunisia from 16 to 19 of May. During his stay in Tunis, Mr Nguyen Khanh held talks with a Tunisian delegation led by Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia in which the two sides agreed to promote cooperative relations to suit good long standing political ties between the two countries. Trade Minister Le Van Triet, who accompanied the deputy premier, signed a trade agreement with his counterpart Mr Sadok Rabah. The Vietnamese deputy premier and his delegation were received by Tunisian Premier Hamed Karoui, who said, among other things, that Dien Bien Phu victory won by Vietnam had inspired strongly the struggle for national independence in North Africa as a whole and in Tunisia in particular. Mr Nguyen Khanh also paid a courtesy visit to Palestinian President Y. 'Arafat at PLO headquarters in Tunis.
