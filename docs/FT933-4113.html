930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / Labour sets up 'quango-watch' LABOUR has called on all local authorities to monitor the activities of non-elected public bodies in their areas as a response to the shift of power away from elected councillors to quangos. Mr Jack Straw, shadow environment secretary, said that since 1979 non-elected bodies had taken over direct control from local authorities of more than Pounds 4.5bn of spending per year.
