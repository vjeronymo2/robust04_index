BFN [Text] In implementation of the directives of leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve and protect him, to expand on the cultivation of rice, the Baghdad Department of Agriculture has prepared all the arrangements for the rice growing campaign, including the supply of seeds and fertilizers. Announcing this, agricultural engineer 'Imad Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman, director of the department, said: Efforts are currently being concentrated on expanding the area to be cultivated with rice in order to ensure our people's needs in the present conditions experienced by our country due to the continuation of the unfair blockade. Engineer 'Abd-al-Rahman explained that the Baghdad Department of Agriculture is redoubling its efforts to complete the wheat and barley harvest campaign on time. He said that as of 12 June, percentage mechanized harvesting of wheat reached 61 percent and of barley 87 percent.
