BFN [Text] Havana, 25 Apr (PL) -- President Fidel Castro welcomed the 225 participants in the "The Nation and the Emigrants" conference, greeting them personally one by one. The weekly TRABAJADORES magazine reports today that the Cuban leader thanked the emigres during the meeting last night for their "solidarity and presence in the fatherland, for their expressions of support for Cuba, for preserving the achievements of the revolution, and for our people and their continuous, tremendous struggle against the U.S. Government's blockade." The weekly notes in a short article that Carlos Lage, secretary of the executive committee and vice president of the Council of State; Foreign Affairs Minister Roberto Robaina; Ricardo Alarcon, president of the National Assembly of the People's Government; Abel Prieto, president of the National Union of Cuban Writers and Artists; and Jose Ramon Balaguer, all of whom are members of the Politburo of the Communist Party of Cuba, participated in the meeting with Fidel Castro.
