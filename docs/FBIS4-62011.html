BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kulagin] [Text] Almaty May 12 TASS -- The Kazakh State Committee for National Security, the Uzbek Security Service and the Kyrgyz State Committee for National Security signed a tripartite agreement on cooperation and interaction on technical provision of the security services of the three countries on principles of mutual payments. The sides outlined concrete ways to carry out their plans, develop coordination and interaction and exercise manoeuvres by means of forces and technical facilities available. Apart from this agreement, the Kazakh and Kyrgyz security departments signed and agreement on cooperation in military counter-intelligence.
