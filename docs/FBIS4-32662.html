BFN [Text] Delegations from the Croatian and Turkish Defense Ministries have held talks in Zagreb on promoting bilateral cooperation. Croatian Deputy Defense Minister Josip Juras received the Turkish Defense Ministry delegation. The Turkish delegation, led by Colonel General Teoman Ekran, held talks with Brigadier General Janko Bobetko, the chief of the General Staff of the Croatian Army; and Brig. Gen. Antun Tus, the main military adviser to the Croatian president. After the talks Josip Juras said that the two delegations had discussed military and technical cooperation and aid, as well as cooperation at experts' level. He added that some very concrete agreements had been reached, and he believed they would materialize in the near future, in the interest of both countries. The aim of our visit is to strengthen relations between Croatia and Turkey and discuss possibilities for cooperation between the two ministries, Ekran said.
