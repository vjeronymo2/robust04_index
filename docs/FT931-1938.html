930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / Letter: Disharmony in the garden Sir, Your gardening correspondent, Robin Lane Fox, recommends spraying every pest in sight and following up with concentrated plant food (March 13/14). How is this advice to be harmonised with Bronwen Maddox's warning (Business and the Environment: 'The world's tap seizes up', March 17) of the threat to the world's water supply of contamination by agricultural chemicals? Michael Singer, Quinta da Lagoa Funda, Azoia, 2710 Sintra, Portugal
