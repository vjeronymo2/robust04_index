Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today said that the Greek diplomatic service, including representatives at the United Nations and with the governments of Balkan countries as well as with those that play a role in the Bosnia-Hercegovina crisis, are fully mobilized to prevent the participation of Turkish soldiers in the international peacekeeping force in the former Yugoslavia. The spokesman added that the United Nations should keep in mind that the region does not need new tensions. Anyway, Venizelos said, Turkey is not the most ideal country when it comes to the implementation of UN regulations. The spokesman also repeated Greece's firm position against the participation in the peacekeeping operations of countries that are historically or geographically involved in the problem. The spokesman concluded: The framework of Greek foreign policy is formulated by the political party leaders' conference under the president.
