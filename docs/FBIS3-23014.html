Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow (February 12) TURAN -- A well-known Russian rights defender, one of the leaders of the Democratic Russia Party, Eugenia Novodvorskaya, told journalists in Moscow yesterday that according to the information she has, an attempt upon the ex-president of Azerbaijan, Abulfaz Elchibey, is being planned. "Elchibey's fate will become that of Gamsakhurdia's, since he possesses a serious threat to Russian influence in Transcaucasus," she said.
