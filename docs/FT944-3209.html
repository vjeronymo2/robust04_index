941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Samuel Heath Despite an increase in turnover from Pounds 3.59m to Pounds 3.82m, pre-tax profits of Samuel Heath & Sons, the hardware and giftware company, fell from Pounds 136,000 to Pounds 103,000 in the half year to end-September. Earnings emerged at 2.3p (3p) per share and the interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
