Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report under the "Yesterday" rubric: "Defense Ministers Will Meet"] [Text] At the invitation of Army General Chi Haotian, PRC defense minister, Army General Sagadat Nurmagambetov, defense minister of the Republic of Kazakhstan, will visit China 11-14 January. While in Beijing, the head of Kazakhstan's military department will also meet with Zhang Zhen, deputy chairman of the PRC Central Military Commission. The talks will cover an exchange of experience in the education and instruction of personnel, the training of cadres, and other problems of military cooperation between the two states, the press service of the defense minister of Kazakhstan has reported.
