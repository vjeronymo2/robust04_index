BFN [Text] More than 30 billion rials has been allocated for the purchase of wheat from farmers in the current season. This was announced by the deputy agriculture minister for the regulation of agricultural produce at a news conference. He said: About 5,350 tonnes of wheat were purchased from farmers last year. Over 5,800 tonnes will be purchased, at a guaranteed price of 260 rials per kilogram, this year. He added: Other produce will also be bought at guaranteed prices. He added: More than $800 million worth of assorted agricultural produce was exported last year. By the end of this year, more than $850 million worth of agricultural produce will be exported.
