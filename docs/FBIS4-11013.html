BFN [Report by Francisco Valeriano Giron on interview with Honduran Foreign Minister Ernesto Paz Aguilar by unidentified reporters at the Presidential Palace in Tegucigalpa; date not given] [Excerpts] Voz de Honduras listeners, we approached Foreign Minister Ernesto Paz Aguilar to ask him about changes in the foreign service. He said that no decision has been made and that the government will allow those ambassadors whose children are studying in the country where they are accredited to remain in office until the school year is over. At that time, changes will be made. Let us hear what the foreign minister has to say about changes in the foreign service. [Begin recording] [Paz Aguilar] No changes yet. The president will announce these changes at the appropriate time. On several occasions I have said the changes suggested have been on the president's desk for the past several weeks.
