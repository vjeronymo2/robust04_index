920302 FT 02 MAR 92 / Arts: 1992 Camden Jazz Festival This year's Camden Jazz Festival takes place from March 15 to 28. Most of the concerts will be held in the Bloomsbury Theatre, Gordon Street, WC1, but the Royal Festival Hall will be the venue for a concert by the Chick Corea Elektric Band on March 23 and the Camden Parkway cinemas will be the setting for the opening events on Sunday March 15. These will comprise a showing of the film Story Weather (3.00pm) and an evening concert by Billy Jenkins and the Voice of God Collective who will perform live to classic cartoons. Among the other artists who will be appearing in the course of the festival are saxophonist John Harle, Andy Sheppard and Steve Lodder, the Stan Tracey octet, Brazilian percussionist Nana Vasconcelos, pianist Keith Tippett in a solo concert, guitarist Allan Holdsworth, the European Jazz Quintet and the Royal Academy Big Band.
