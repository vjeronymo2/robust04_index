BFN [Text] Sidon, Lebanon, April 11 (AFP) -- Palestinian gunmen shot dead an official of PLO chief Yasir 'Arafat's Fatah movement on Monday [11 April] in southern Lebanon, Palestinian sources said. Husayn Abu-Zayd, 47, a deputy to Fatah's former intelligence chief in Lebanon Kamal Midhat, was killed with automatic weapons in the 'Ayn al-Hulwah Palestinian refugee camp on the eastern outskirts of this port city, they said. His killers, two brothers who also belong to Fatah, were arrested by Palestinian military police and handed over to the Lebanese authorities, they said. An informed Palestinian sources said Abu-Zayd had had a financial and personal dispute with the two men.
