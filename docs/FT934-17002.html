931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / International Company News: Murdoch completes NY Post acquisition MR RUPERT Murdoch has completed his purchase of the New York Post newspaper, AP-DJ reports from New York. The purchase, by News Corp subsidiary New America Publishing, follows ratification of new agreements by production workers. News Corp bought the Post from the estate of Mr Peter Kalikow, the paper's former owner, for around Dollars 25m.
