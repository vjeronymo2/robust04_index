930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Hambro Ins beats listing forecast HAMBRO Insurance Services Group, announced pro forma pre-tax profits of Pounds 9.52m for the year ended March 31, just beating its listing forecast of Pounds 9.4m. The Hambros merchant bank subsidiary was floated in March, valued at Pounds 86.7m. Cunningham Group, the loss adjuster division, contributed profits of Pounds 5m and sales of Pounds 41.6m to overall turnover of Pounds 67m. Hambro Legal Protection increased sales to Pounds 9.9m and profits to Pounds 3m and Beale Dobie achieved sales of Pounds 14.9m and made Pounds 1.2m. Berkeley Insurance raised profits to Pounds 300,000. Earnings came to 10.46p. The group also announced yesterday that Beale Dobie had reached preliminary agreement with BZW to launch an offshore investment vehicle involved in second hand endowment policies. On a consolidated basis for the period from July 17 to March 31 1993 group pre-tax profit amounted to Pounds 4.63m on turnover of Pounds 25.8m.
