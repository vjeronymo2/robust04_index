BFN [Text] Tokyo April 26 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- A Japan-Korea emergency round-table conversation on the denuclearisation of the Korean peninsula and the nuclear issue was held in Nagoya city on April 13 under the sponsorship of the Aichi Prefectural People's Council for Japan-Korea Friendship. Mitsudoshi Omatsuzawa, secretary general of the Aichi prefectural headquarters of the Japan Social-Democratic Party, addressing the round-table conversation, said the joint declaration on the conversion of Northeast Asia into a nuclear-free, peace zone, which was issued between the Japan Social-Democratic Party and the Workers' Party of Korea in March 1981, is of weighty significance at present. Noting that the Japan Social-Democratic Party assumes a heavy responsibility in making the Japanese Government approach the settlement of the problem with a right stand under the overflow of the informations slandering the Democratic People's Republic of Korea over the nuclear issue, he stressed the Japan Social-Democratic Party would support the independent, peaceful reunification of Korea.
