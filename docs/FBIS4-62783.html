BFN [Unattributed report: "Official"] [Text] With a view to boosting the effectiveness of the Russian Federation [RF] Presidential Council for Cadre Policy, by edict of the RF president the following words are to be added to Point 3 Paragraph 3 of RF presidential edict No. 848 of 3 June 1993 "On Immediate Measures To Organize the System of State Service in the RF" (SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA POSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, 1993, No. 23, p 2108) after the word "includes": "the RF minister for nationalities' affairs and regional policy." S.M. Shakhray, RF minister for nationalities' affairs and regional policy, is included in the membership of the RF Presidential Council for Cadre Policy. By RF Government directive, RF Education Minister Yevgeniy Viktorovich Tkachenko and Vladimir Georgiyevich Kinelev, chairman of the RF State Committee for Higher Education, have been appointed official RF Government representatives during the Federal Assembly chambers' consideration of the draft RF Law "On the Federal Program for the Development of Education in Russia."
