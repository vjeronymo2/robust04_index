October 7, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

NONLEAGUE

Oak Park 27, Paraclete 12 -- Oak Park rebounded from a 12-0 halftime deficit to win at home. Kent Richter rushed for 217 yards in 27 carries and scored three touchdowns in the second half.

After scoring on a three-yard run late in the third quarter, Richter exploded for a 60-yard run early in the fourth quarter to put Oak Park ahead, 13-12. Mike Stern scored on a two-yard run with 7:21 left and his two-point conversion pass to Richter gave Oak Park (4-1) a 21-12 lead. Richter capped the Oak Park scoring with a 14-yard run.

Chuck Lang scored two first-half touchdowns for Paraclete (2-3). NAOMI TWARD
