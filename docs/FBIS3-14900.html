Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] People's Deputy Stanislav Shushkevich held two meetings with workers in Mogilev this past Sunday [27 February]. The former parliamentary speaker sees early parliamentary elections as the way out of the current difficult situation. Touching upon the unification of monetary systems of Russia and Belarus, Stanislav Shushkevich spoke in favor of this agreement concluded on equal conditions without infringing upon the interests of our Republic. The leaders of the local "Spring 94" bloc of democratic forces publicized an appeal to Stanislav Shushkevich, in which they requested that he put forward his candidacy for the post of Belarus' first president. This appeal was approved by the supporters of democratic parties.
