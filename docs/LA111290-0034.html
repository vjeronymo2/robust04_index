November 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LUTHER CAMPBELL'S LYRICS

Luther Campbell self-righteously puts an "adult" sticker on the album and hides behind his claim of comedy ("Today They're Trying to Censor Rap, Tomorrow. . . ," Nov. 5).

Since when is it entertainment to tear open women's vaginas, bust the walls, break their backbones?

They call this comedy? I call it rape, and rape and sexual violence aren't funny. Ask the runner in Central Park.

KATHY SILVERI

Channel Islands Beach
