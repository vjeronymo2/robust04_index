930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / International Company News: Advance Bank ahead 25% to ADollars 56m for year ADVANCE BANK, the Australian bank, reports a 25 per cent earnings gain to ADollars 55.9m (USDollars 38m) for the year ended May. The dividend is going up from 38 cents to 50 cents a share. Bad and doubtful debt expense was reduced from ADollars 36m to ADollars 27.5m, and net non-performing loans fell from ADollars 116.0m to ADollars 83.2m. Gross assets grew from ADollars 8bn to ADollars 9.2bn. Mr Jim Service, chairman, described the result as pleasing in difficult and competitive conditions.
