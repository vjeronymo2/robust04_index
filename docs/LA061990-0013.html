June 19, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Board o' Yuks: The International Comedy Film Festival and Competition, a branch of the Cinetex '90 Film Market and Production Exposition, has named a comedy advisory board. Among those participating are Danny DeVito, Blake Edwards, Whoopi Goldberg, Spike Lee, Jerry Lewis, Paul Mazursky, Carl and Rob Reiner, Joan Rivers, George Segal and Gene Wilder. Now in its third year, Cinetex is scheduled for Sept. 6-10 in Las Vegas, where awards will be given to movie and TV comedy productions and for best comedic performers, director, screenplay and cinematography. GREG BRAXTON
