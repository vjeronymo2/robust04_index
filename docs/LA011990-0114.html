January 19, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ART NEWS: Irvine Co. Chairman Donald L. Bren is one of the world's top 200 art collectors, says ArtNews magazine. . . . The Newport Beach real estate developer, who collects contemporary art, is one of 24 Southern Californians on the magazine's annual ranking. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
