941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / Observer: Speeding bullet You've heard of Japan's bullet-train, now meet Brazil's bullet-proof train. Next month a private company plans to resume train services between Sao Paulo and Rio de Janeiro. It's got an unusual marketing gimmick. In a bid to prevent passengers being caught in the crossfire between Rio's police and drug traffickers all the windows are to be fitted with bullet-proof glass.
