BFN [Text] Parliament May 20 SAPA -- South Africa's new 90-member Senate met for the first time on Friday at 10AM in the historic old senate chamber at Parliament. Chief Justice Michael Corbett constituted it and started swearing in its members. One of the Senate's main functions will be to form the Constitutional Assembly with the 400 members of the National Assembly to write South Africa's final constitution. It will also be able to initiate legislation.
