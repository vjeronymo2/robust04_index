930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / UK Company News: BMSS falls into the red BMSS, the USM-quoted timber and builders' merchant, reported a loss of Pounds 95,498 in the year to January 31, against profits of Pounds 515,309. Turnover was down 12 per cent to Pounds 15.2m (Pounds 17.3m). Gross margins were maintained at 31.94 per cent (31.23 per cent). There was a trading profit of Pounds 210,862 (Pounds 795,673) but this was wiped out by net interest charges of Pounds 306,360 (Pounds 280,364). A final dividend of 3p is proposed for the year; the interim was passed. Last time shareholders received a total of 4.7p including a final of 2.7p. Losses were 0.7p (5.1p earnings).
