940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / Pollution link to asthma 'likely' Traffic pollution is a likely cause of rising levels of respiratory disease including asthma, the government says today. It says in a report that trends in road traffic are 'consistent with trends in allergic conditions', although the evidence is not conclusive. The question of the link between traffic fumes and health is one of the most sensitive environmental questions facing the government.
