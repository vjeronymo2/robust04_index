920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / International Company News: PFC reports sharp rebound POWER Financial (PFC), Mr Paul Desmarais, the Montreal financier's financial services group, said earnings rebounded sharply in the second quarter and first half, writes Robert Gibbens. Subsidiaries Great-West Life and Investors Group in Canada made higher contributions. PFC's 26 per cent holding in Pargesa, the big European holding company, also contributed more. Second-quarter profit was CDollars 90.7m (USDollars 76.8m) or CDollars 1.05 a share, up from CDollars 55.3m or 62 cents a year earlier and in the first half CDollars 135.7m or Dollars 1.56 a share, compared with CDollars 91.9m or CDollars 1.02 a share a year earlier.
