BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow May 18 TASS -- Chairman of the upper chamber of Russia's legislature Vladimir Shumeyko told journalists here today that the resignation of Viktor Chernomyrdin as prime minister "is extremely undesirable in the present situation". He stressed that "Russia was lucky that Chernomyrdin became the head of government", and added it was doubtful that Chernomyrdin might tender his resignation. "My experience of work with the premier has shown that Chernomyrdin is a fighter," Shumeyko said.
