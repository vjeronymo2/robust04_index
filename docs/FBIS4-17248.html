BFN [Text] Baku, 20 April (TURAN)--Late last night President Aliyev received the head of the National Independence Party of Azerbaijan, Etibar Mamedov. During a discussion about the internal political situation was mentioned as well as urgent measures needed to be taking in order to stabilize the situation. Connected with this question, Mamedov offered to hold a dialogue with some of the political parties. President Aliyev agreed with the proposal. TURAN was informed that confidential meetings have already taken place between the opposition and the President. The results of meetings are expected to be optimistic.
