921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Borthwicks advances to Pounds 1.28m PRE-TAX profits of Borthwicks, the natural flavours and food products group, increased by Pounds 344,000 to Pounds 1.28m in the six months to September 26. The pre-tax figure was boosted by cash received and the release of a provision amounting to Pounds 442,000 from a discontinued operation. Profits for the comparable period last time were depressed by reorganisation costs totalling Pounds 346,000. Turnover from continuing operations increased from Pounds 22.8m to Pounds 23.2m. The interest charge fell slightly to Pounds 324,000 (Pounds 356,000). The interim dividend is being maintained at 0.5p, payable from earnings per share of 1.9p (1.2p).
