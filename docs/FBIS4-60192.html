BFN [Text] In Yemen, Sanaa radio announced this evening that the presidential council, the parliament, and the premiership council had decided on a three-day ceasefire. The same source added that all military activities will be suspended throughout the country beginning midnight on the occasion of the blessed Adha 'Id and the fourth anniversary of the unification of Yemen. In Aden, very informed sources said that the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], after meetings that lasted 24 hours, decided to secede from Yemen and establish an independent state in the south. The same sources added that an official announcement and more details were expected any time now. This announcement will be made as soon as the leader of the YSP, Ali Salim al-Bidh, returns to Aden from Hadramawt's eastern region.
