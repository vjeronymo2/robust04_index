September 26, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY PEOPLE

Electro Rent Corp. of Van Nuys announced that Will Richeson was elected to the company's board, replacing John M. Albertine, who resigned.

Richeson, 65, was a general partner with Riordan Freeman & Spogli, a merchant banking firm, from 1985 to 1988. Electro Rent leases and rents electronic test equipment.
