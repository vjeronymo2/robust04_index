January 10, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Randall White has been appointed regional director of Mutual of New York's (MONY) new Western regional pension office in Irvine.

He was formerly a senior account executive at MONY in San Francisco. Before that, he was account representative with Travelers Asset Management. MONY's pension operations offer retirement savings plans, along with asset management and allocation services.
