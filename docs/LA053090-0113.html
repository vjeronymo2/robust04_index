May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

WOMAN SHOT BY GUNMAN DRESSED AS A CLOWN DIES

A woman shot at point-blank range by a gunman dressed as a clown died of her injuries, Palm Beach County deputies said Tuesday.

Marlene Warren, 40, died Monday night. She had been in critical condition since the shooting Saturday. Sheriff's deputies said they were investigating several leads but would not elaborate.
