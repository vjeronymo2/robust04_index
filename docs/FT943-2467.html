940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Elys (Wimbledon) up 45% to Pounds 107,000 Pre-tax profits rose 45 per cent at Elys (Wimbledon), the department store, from Pounds 74,000 to Pounds 107,000, in the half year to July 30. Turnover increased slightly from Pounds 4.35m to Pounds 4.54m. As confidence returned to the housing market, the household departments, particularly linens, soft furnishings and electrical goods, showed strong improvement. Earnings per share rose to 6p (4.1p) and the interim dividend goes up by 0.5p to 2p.
