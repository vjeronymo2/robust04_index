930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Samuel Heath 17% ahead to Pounds 361,000 Samuel Heath & Sons, the hardware and gift maker, lifted pre-tax profits by 17 per cent from Pounds 308,000 to Pounds 361,000 in the year ended March 31. Turnover slipped to Pounds 7.06m (Pounds 7.1m). Earnings advanced to 8.5p (7.3p) per share and the dividend is maintained at 5.5p via an unchanged final of 4p.
