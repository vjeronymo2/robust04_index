BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Brother 'Abd-al-Qadir Ba Jammal today received Italian Ambassador (Peter Mordoni). They discussed ties between the two friendly countries and prospects for expanding and developing them. They also reviewed developments in the Yemeni arena and exchanged views on several issues of mutual concern. The brother deputy prime minister voiced appreciation for the principled positions adopted by the Italian Government toward the current conditions in our country and its support for the Republic of Yemen on the basis of the strong historical ties between the Yemeni and Italian peoples. The meeting was attended by (Giovani Etelling), the Italian charge d'affaires in Sanaa.
