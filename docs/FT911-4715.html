910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Eidos loss lower than forecast Eidos, engaged in electronic data processing equipment, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 61,000 for 1990. The company joined the USM in December with forecast losses of Pounds 153,000. The company said the reduced loss was a result of being able to delay a proportion of anticipated overheads. The company is also writing off goodwill of Pounds 116,400 that arose on the purchase of Desk Top One. Losses per share worked through at 2.76p.
