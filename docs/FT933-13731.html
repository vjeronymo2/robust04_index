930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Life Sciences in Dollars 6.5m acquisition Life Sciences International has acquired the business and trading assets of ALKO Diagnostic for Dollars 6.5m cash (Pounds 4.4m). ALKO makes reagents and other consumables for use in analytical instruments in hospitals and laboratories. In 1992 it made trading profits of some Dollars 1m (Pounds 700,000) on sales of Pounds 2.4m. With access to increased telemarketing effort, ALKO is expected to lift sales and profits significantly in the future.
