Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 14 (YONHAP) -- Former lawmaker So Sok-chae returned from London on Thursday [13 January] evening and went to Pusan. So met his daughter in London and will officiate the wedding Saturday afternoon at Dong-a University in Pusan of a man who belonged to his local chapter. He will depart for Japan on Sunday from Pusan, and study at the East-West Cultural Center in Hawaii University in February. So, a close aide of President Kim Yong-sam, had his civil rights restored in December and many political analysts have a deep interest in his political future.
