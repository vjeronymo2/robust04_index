Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received Yusuf Bin-'Alawi 'Abdallah, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs, at the Royal Hashemite Court last night. The minister conveyed to his majesty a verbal message from his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman dealing with Jordanian-Omani efforts and coordination to bring the views of the various Yemeni parties closer, especially after the success of Jordanian efforts that culminated in the signing of the Yemeni document of pledge and accord, which safeguards the unity of sisterly Yemen and helps build its modern state. The talks also dealt with the distinguished relations between the two sisterly countries as well as regional and international issues of common concern. His Majesty King Husayn asked the guest minister to convey his greetings to his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id. Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's political adviser; Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; and the Omani ambassador in Amman attended the audience. Bin-'Alawi arrived in Amman yesterday evening and was received upon arrival by Marwan al-Qasim and Talal Sat'an al-Hasan.
