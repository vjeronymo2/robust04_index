December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

DISABLED TO SEE PARADE FREE

Two free viewing areas along the Tournament of Roses Parade route have been set aside for the disabled.

One viewing section, provided by the city of Pasadena, will be along the south side of Colorado Boulevard between Marengo and Garfield avenues. Each disabled person can receive two tickets and more if space permits.

The tickets are available from the city's Affirmative Action Department, (818) 405-4216. Also available is transportation to the site via Dial-a-Ride, or parking permits for a garage one block from the viewing area.

A second viewing area for the disabled will be at a lot on the corner of Sierra Madre Boulevard and Villa Street. It is provided by Wheelchair Haven, a volunteer group, which will send two free tickets to each person writing to Wheelchair Haven, 750 E. Green St., Room 340, Pasadena, Calif. 91101.
