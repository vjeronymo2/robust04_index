Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Matanzas engineering regiment has been once again rewarded for its outstanding work. Making a sustained effort in refurbishing the theater of military operations and in maintaining techniques, the Central Army engineering regiment obtained the special Victoria-25 Emulation award. Engineering Unit 3170 received the award of being the first group of its type in the country, and the banner that raises it to the level of National Vanguard of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, an award obtained for the sixth consecutive year. During 1993 this unit made laudable efforts to build tunnels, silos, and firing positions, and achieved considerable progress in conserving resources and improving the troops' living conditions. On evaluating the fighting capacity of the regiment, Division General Joaquin Quintas Solas said: [Begin recording] The fighting readiness of this regiment, as well as that of the other regular units, the militia, and the production and defense units throughout the Army territory, is high and is equal to the times in which we are living today. All warriors clearly understand that only by fighting does one enter this country, and one leaves -- well, we all know how, if one manages to leave. I was told that the readiness of this unit is very satisfactory, an outstanding grade within the Revolutionary Armed Forces. [end recording]
