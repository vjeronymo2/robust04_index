920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / Petition on Sunday trading presented THE SHOPPING Hours Reform Council, the organisation that campaigns in favour of Sunday shopping, yesterday presented MPs with a petition signed by 1m people demanding abolition of the Sunday trading laws, John Thornhill writes. The move followed the launch of a manifesto earlier this week by the Keep Sunday Special Campaign. Both sides are vigorously lobbying MPs to try to turn Sunday trading into an election issue.
