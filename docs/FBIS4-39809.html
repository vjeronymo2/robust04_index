BFN [Text] A RID reporter has learned that the Republican and Minsk City Councils [as heard] and the Movement for Democracy, Social Progress, and Justice have issued appeals to presidential candidates Dubko, Kebich, Lukashenka, and Novikaw. The statements, in particular, urge the candidates to annul the Viskuli agreements [leading to the dissolution of USSR], to halt the (?capitalist) path of development of the Republic, to strengthen and develop the soviets as organs of genuine power of the people, to abolish the presidency, to terminate the privatization of the all-people ownership, to liquidate the existing state symbols by 3 July 1994, to recognize the equal rights of the Belarusian and Russian languages, and to restore the holiday on 7 November [the day when the socialist revolution was held] in their election programs.
