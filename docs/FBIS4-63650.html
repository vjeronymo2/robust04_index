BFN [Text] Ukraine has not suggested a UN discussion of the Crimean question. This was reported on Monday [23 May] to INTERFAX-Ukraine by the head of the information department of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry Yuriy Sergeyev. Commenting on the statement of a political advisor of the Russian foreign minister Galina Sidorova that Ukraine wishes the Crimean question to be discussed at the UN, Sergeyev expressed surprise at how she could have come to such a conclusion. He stressed that the Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko in a letter to the heads of state and leaders of international organizations only drew the attention of the world public to the complexity of the situation in Crimea.
