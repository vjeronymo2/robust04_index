941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Faberge Imperial egg to be sold The Winter Egg, one of Carl Faberge's Imperial Easter eggs, is to be offered at auction at Christie's in Geneva on November 16. The egg, a gift from Tsar Nicholas II to his mother, in 1913, is made of rock crystal with a 'surprise' of a platinum basket and quartz anenomes, and has more than 3,000 diamonds. It is expected to realise between Pounds 1.6m and Pounds 3m.
