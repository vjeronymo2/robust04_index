June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FAIR HOUSING AGENCY SELECTS NEW DIRECTOR

Stephanie Knapik, assistant director of the Westside Fair Housing Council, has been named executive director of the nonprofit group.

Knapik, 38, has worked at the council since 1985. She was previously center director of the Los Angeles Chapter of the National Organization for Women, and a survey analyst at RAND Corp.

The Westside Fair Housing Council oversees the implementation of local, state and federal fair housing laws in West Los Angeles.
