BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Temnenkov] [Text] Bonn, 5 Jun -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, who is expected in Germany today, will be staying here on a private visit. An official representative of the German Foreign Ministry told ITAR-TASS that no meetings or talks between V. Chernomyrdin and German leaders were envisaged. "It is purely a private visit."
