940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / Receivers voice doubts on Swans The receivers to Swan Hunter, the Tyneside shipbuilder facing closure, last night voiced grave doubts about a proposal from the sole prospective bidder for the company. Joint receiver Mr Ed James of Price Waterhouse said outline proposals from French-owned Soffia/CMN would need to be 'significantly improved', both in terms of the price offered and Swans' substantial continuing operating costs pending a sale, for there to be any chance of agreement.
