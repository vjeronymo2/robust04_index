BFN [Text] Continuing their meetings with the leaders of the rival Afghan groups, the UN delegation left Mazar-e Sharif for Herat yesterday. The members of this delegation are to hold talks with the provincial governor of Herat, who is a member of Jamiat-e Eslami. Before leaving Mazar-e Sharif, Mahmud Mestiri, the leader of the UN delegation, had another meeting with Abdol Rashid Dostum, the leader of the National-Islamic Movement of Afghanistan. According to the Central News Unit from Islamabad, Dostum said the only way to establish peace in Afghanistan is for Rabbani to resign as president.
