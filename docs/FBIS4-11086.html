BFN [Text] During the provincial meeting to summarize the workers parliaments in Matanzas Province, Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada, president of the National Assembly of the People's Government, stated that only the people can work economic miracles. Alarcon stated that no law can accomplish the miracles that the people can with their resistance and popular struggle toward the established objectives, and predicted that practice will show how the Cuban people are capable of overcoming this difficult period. The attendants at the meeting learned about the main measures presented by the workers of Matanzas Province, which include the reorganization of the domestic economy, profitability of the industrial sector, elimination of inappropriate subsidies, and generalized efficiency.
