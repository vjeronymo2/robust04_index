Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 26 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the day of the Republic of India. The Indian people have made great strides in their efforts to consolidate national independence and build a prosperous new society since the foundation of the republic, MINJU CHOSON in a by-lined article says, and continues: The Republic of India, one of the founders of the Non-Aligned Movement, is pursuing the non-aligned foreign policy. India is developing the friendly and cooperative relations with different countries including neighbouring countries and striving for peace in Asia and the rest of the world. The Governments and peoples of Korea and India, non-aligned countries in Asia, established the bonds of friendship and cooperation long ago and have developed it. The Korean people, believing that the ties between the two peoples will further strengthen in future, sincerely hope that the Indian people will make greater progress in the endeavours for the independent development and prosperity of the country.
