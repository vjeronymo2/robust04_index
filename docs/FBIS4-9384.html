BFN [Report by Enes Musovic] [Excerpt] Gorazde -- The chetniks have not pulled back to a distance of 3 km from the town center, as stipulated by the NATO ultimatum. The UN forces still have not occupied a single position in the region as envisaged by the NATO ultimatum. The chetniks are settling Kopaci and Zubcici with civilian populations. These villages are in the direct vicinity of Gorazde. At the same time the chetniks are concentrating major forces on the territory around the free part of the Gorazde district. [passage omitted]
