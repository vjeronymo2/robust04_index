930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Highbury screen contract for Avesco Avesco, through its giant screen rental company Screenco, has been awarded a five-year exclusive contract to manage Arsenal Football Club's two new Sony Jumbotron video displays. The installation of the screens at each end of the Highbury ground to show pre and post match entertainment is planned for the autumn. Arsenal has invested Pounds 2.5m and will be the first soccer club in the UK to introduce them on a permanent basis. Apart from 30 match days, Avesco will have access to remove the screens for use at other events.
