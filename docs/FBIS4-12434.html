BFN [Text] Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, who is on an official visit to Egypt, was received yesterday by Egyptian President Husni Mubarak. The minister presented the Azerbaijani president's letter to Husni Mubarak. The question of expanding bilateral ties were discussed at the meeting. Gasan Gasanov briefed Husni Mubarak in detail on the Armenian aggression. Husni Mubarak stated that Egypt resolutely condemns this aggression and wants the ouster of the occupationist forces from the Azerbaijani lands as soon as possible and the peaceful settlement of the conflict. He noted that Egypt, and he personally, opposes the forcible change of borders. President Husni Mubarak said that the Egyptian people regard the Azerbaijani people as friendly and fraternal. He hoped that Azerbaijan President Geydar Aliyev's official visit to Egypt will help further improve relations between our countries. Gasan Gasanov also visited the Arab League, met Secretary General 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, and provided detailed information to him on the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict and gave him an information package to be sent to the members of the Arab League. Gasan Gasanov addressed the Arab League's extraordinary session on 7 April.
