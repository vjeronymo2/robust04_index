BFN [Text] Valletta, May 31 (XINHUA) -- Maltese President Dr. Ugo Mifsud Bonnici met here today with a delegation from the National People's Congress of China (NPC) led by vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the NPC, Wang Guangying. During the meeting Bonnici spoke highly of China's great achievements in many areas since the adoption of the reform and opening policy in the late 1970s. Bonnici and Wang both stressed that further development of friendly relations between Malta and China would promote the two nations' economic development and world peace and stability. They also discussed bilateral cooperation in the fields of politics, the economy and culture as well as international issues of mutual concern. Earlier, the speaker of the Maltese parliament also met with Wang and his party. The Chinese delegation arrived here on Monday after visiting Portugal.
