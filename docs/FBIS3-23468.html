Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Erzurum, March 19 (A.A.) -- In a land scanning in the rural section of Erzurum, security forces came across a terrorist, of Armenian origin, who was on the brink of death, of freezing. The security forces hospitalized the man for treatment. Other terrorists in the same hospital recognized him as a member of their organization and an Armenian living in Syria. The man , called as 'Cudi', was , according to the other terrorists, the trigger man of the separatist terror organization and was also a 'commander' in the organization.
