Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Homes of Three Drug Smugglers Demolished"] [Text] Mir Insha (Special correspondent) -- Homes belonging to three drug smugglers in Ditta Khel village in Mir Insha area were blown up using dynamite. The action was taken on the instruction of Mohammed Amin Khatak, the political agent there. A.P.A. Shafir Allah, commander of Tochi Scouts; Major Liaqat Ali, tehsildar; Mir Inshah Ghazi Mirjan; and Subedar Taj Mohammed also took part in this operation. This action was part of the clean up operation aimed at the antisocial elements. The tribal representatives in the region have expressed satisfaction at this action.
