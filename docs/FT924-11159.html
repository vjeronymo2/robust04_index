921027 FT 27 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 21,241 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed Assams were again well supported with medium and better liquoring kinds 5-10p dearer. Bangladesh teas attracted improved competition mostly at dearer rates. East Africans came to a strong and active market particularly for Pekoe Dusts. Price levels advanced by up to 10p. Offshore: fair demand with coloury mediums selling readily at dearer rates. The highest price realised was 272p for an Assam pf. Quotations: quality 200p, good medium 148p, medium 135p, low medium 88p.
