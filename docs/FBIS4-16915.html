BFN [Text] During the intervals of the sessions of the CIS council of the heads of states and governments in Moscow, Emomali Rakhmonov, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan, held talks with Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin. In a meeting which was held in an atmosphere of mutual understanding they discussed strengthening and expanding mutually beneficial cooperation between Tajikistan and Russia. In addition, Emomali Rakhmonov held talks with Viktor Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation; Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Russian Federation Council; Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev; and Islam Karimov, president of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
