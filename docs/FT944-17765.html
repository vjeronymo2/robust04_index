941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / International Company News: Orkla ahead and buys EniChem arm Orkla, the Norwegian group with interests ranging from branded consumer goods to chemical processing, yesterday announced an improvement in eight-month pre-tax profits and the acquisition of the fine chemicals activities of EniChem of Italy for an undisclosed amount. Group pre-tax profits rose to NKr977m (Dollars 138m) from NKr907m of which NKr274m was derived from the investment division, against NKr202m in the same period last year. Group sales advanced to NKr13.66bn from NKr11.28bn as operating profit increased to NKr918m from NKr852m, helped mainly by good progress in Orkla's chemicals business which lifted operating profit 62 per cent to NKr203m. Orkla's industrial activities, in which chemicals is grouped, lifted operating profit to NKr897m from NKr835m. Branded consumer goods, part of Orkla's industrial activities, saw operating profit little changed at NKr657m against NKr659m last year.
