Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Chilean Foreign Ministry has announced that it is ready to advise the government on an eventual decision to reestablish diplomatic ties with Cuba. Chilean Foreign Minister Enrique Silva Simma explained that no deadline has been set for a final decision but affirmed that all aspects of relations with Cuba are being analyzed. The Chilean minister added that the pace at which his ministry is working complies with the opinion expressed by President-Elect Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle to the effect that this type of matter must be undertaken slowly.
