Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Highness Prince Muhammad Bin-Talal, the regent, received at the Royal Hashemite Court today General Joseph Hoar, commander of the U.S. Central Command. They reviewed bilateral relations and regional and international issues of common interest. Attending the meeting were His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin-al-Husayn, and Staff General 'Abd-al-Hafiz Mar'i al-Ka'abinah, chairman of the joint chiefs of staff, and the U.S. charge d'affaires in Jordan. In the presence of His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin-al-Husayn, His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court, also received General Joseph Hoar. They exchanged views on various regional and international issues and bilateral relations. Staff General 'Abd-al-Hafiz Mar'i al-Ka'abinah, chairman of the joint chiefs of staff, received General Hoar at the General Command today. Al-Ka'abinah reviewed the regional situation in general, the region's strategic importance, and the humanitarian role the Armed Forces are carrying out to maintain security and peace in various world regions. They also reviewed aspects of bilateral cooperation and coordination and issues of common interest.
