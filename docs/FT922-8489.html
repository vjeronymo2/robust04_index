920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / International Company News: Whirlpool deal valued at Dollars 14m WHIRLPOOL, the largest US white goods producer, is consolidating its presence in eastern Europe with the establishment of a joint venture with Tatramat, the leading washing machine producer in Czechoslovakia. The joint venture is valued at Dollars 13.7m of which Tatramat contributed Dollars 10.7m in fixed assets and Whirlpool Dollars 3m in cash. A further Dollars 3m was paid by Whirlpool for a second purchase of shares which brought the group's stake to 43.8 per cent. The venture intends a further Dollars 28m in capital expenditures over the next three or four years. The venture will produce 400,000 washing machines by 1993 and export them to Hungary, Poland and the former Soviet Union. Tatramat, located in Slovakia, makes about 70 per cent of washing machines in Czechoslovakia. Its only rival is a small Moravian company, Romo Fulnek.
