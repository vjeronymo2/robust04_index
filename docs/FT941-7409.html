940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Nordics in EU entry strategy meeting The prime ministers of Finland, Norway and Sweden meet today in Helsinki to co-ordinate their positions before sending negotiators to Brussels for the weekend's make-or-break talks on their terms for entry to the EU, writes Hugh Carnegy in Stockholm. Stockholm has hinted it would consider moving ahead to signing an accession agreement even if one or all of its fellow applicants - including Austria - were not ready to do so next week. But Mr Carl Bildt, the prime minister, said he was anxious the Nordic countries should reach agreement simultaneously.
