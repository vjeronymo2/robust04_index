BFN [Text] Army General Pavel Grachev, Russian minister of defense, ended his official visit to Romania today with a joint news conference together with his Romanian counterpart Gheorghe Tinca. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the results of the visit. The Russian guest and his retinue were received during their stay in Romania by President Ion Iliescu, Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu, and Teodor Melescanu, minister of state and minister of foreign affairs. Likewise, the Russian defense minister and the Romanian minister of national defense, Gheorghe Tinca, signed an accord between the Ministry of National Defense of Romania and the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation on the military cooperation between the two countries, which was considered by both sides as a start for relaunching the overall bilateral relations.
