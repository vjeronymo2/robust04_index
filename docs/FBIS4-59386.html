BFN [Text] Cairo, May 12 (AFP) -- The partial list of Palestinian authority members announced on Thursday [12 May] by chief PLO negotiator Nabil Sha'th is as follows: - Yasir 'Arafat, PLO chairman - Zakariya al-Agha, head of Arafat's mainstream Fatah faction in Gaza - Frayh Abu-Midyan, former president of the Gaza Bar Association - Faysal al-Husayni, the leading PLO man in the occupied territories - Hanan 'Ashrawi, former spokeswoman of the negotiating team - Sa'ib 'Urayqat, head of the Palestinian team to the bilateral talks - Riyad al-Za'nun, head of the higher health council in the Gaza Strip - Nabil Sha'th, PLO chief autonomy negotiator - Yasir 'Amr, PLO executive committee member - Intisar al-Wazir (Umm Jihad), widow of Arafat's second-in-command Khalil al-Wazir, who was killed in 1988 - 'Azmi al-Shu'aybi, head of the Palestinian Democratic Union in the occupied territories - Ilyas Furayj, mayor of Bethlehem in the occupied West Bank - 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Haj Ahmad, a former deportee - Ahmad Quray' (Abu-'Ala'), PLO economic departement chief - Samir Ghawshah, Popular Struggle Front leader, PLO executive committee member The authority is to take over on May 25, and 10 other members are to be announced.
