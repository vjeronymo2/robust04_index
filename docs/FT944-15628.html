941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / Iraq to approve border Iraq's People's Assembly yesterday gathered for an extraordinary, secret session to approve the recognition of United Nations-demarcated borders with Kuwait and the Gulf state's sovereignty - a fundamental UN Security Council precondition for any moves towards the easing of sanctions. The hastily-called meeting was expected to deliver an official recognition of Kuwait's sovereignty and borders in the terms required by this weekend's Security Council resolution, which called on Iraq to make the recognition through 'full and formal constitutional procedures'.
