BFN [Text] Cairo, April 25 (AFP) -- Police on Monday shot dead the leader of the underground al-Jama'ah al-Islamiyah which has been waging a two-year-long campaign aimed at toppling the government, police said. They said Tal'at Yasin Hammam was killed in a clash with police at his apartment in northeast Cairo. He had been condemned to death by a military court in January 1993.
