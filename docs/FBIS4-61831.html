BFN [Text] INTERFAX has been informed by the press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh defense army that from 0000 on 9 May the armed forces of the self-proclaimed republic have been strictly observing the unilateral cease-fire that was declared yesterday in Stepanakert. Despite this, according to the same source, last night and again today, the Azeri forces shelled Karabakh positions in several sections of the front, particularly in the Mardakert region. It was stressed in Stepanakert that there had been no return fire. Meanwhile, according to information from Azerbaijan's Ministry of Defense, at about 0300, i.e., after cease-fire was declared, Armenian forces attacked Azeri positions in the south of the Fizulinskiy rayon. The attack was beaten off, a military report from Baku states.
