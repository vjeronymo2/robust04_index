Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Samsun, Feb 17 (AA) -- Security teams caught five members of the THKP-C [Turkish People's Liberation Party Front] Dev-Sol [Revolutionary Left] terror organization in this Black Sea city on Wednesday. AA learned that acting on a tip-off, anti-terror teams raided a house in the city, capturing Celal Ozkul, the Black Sea region official of the organization and four others. A revolver, shells, a copy machine, material used in explosives, and organizational documents were found in the house. Officials of the security directorate said that the five people caught were a bombing team and were preparing for terror actions in the Black Sea region.
