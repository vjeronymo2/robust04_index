Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fadl Shururu [secretary of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command's Political Bureau and official in charge of the front's Information and Central Guidance Department] has stated the following: A central meeting of the Palestinian forces' alliance was held last night, Thursday, 30 December 1993 in the presence of all the alliance's forces. The conferees made the first reading of all the proposals pertaining to the Palestinian alliance's organizational formula. Shururu also said that the conferees agreed to consider the meetings open in order to monitor developments and to act jointly to support the glorious Palestinian intifadah against the Zionist occupation and the agreement of humiliation.
