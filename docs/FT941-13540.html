940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Lex sells rest of Arrow shares Lex Service has completed the sale of the 6.8m Arrow Electronics shares it received in partial payment for the disposal of its North American electronic component distribution businesses to Arrow in September 1991. The remaining 384,941 shares were sold in the market by Goldman Sachs at an average of Dollars 41 net of costs. Total proceeds to Lex will be Dollars 15.8m (Pounds 10.5m).
