February 9, 1989, Thursday, Orange County P.M. Final

SOVIETS WIN 2ND GOLD IN BIATHLON

The Soviet Union won the second gold medal at the World Biathlon Championships, taking the women's 15-kilometer today in 1 hour, 5 minutes, 38.8 seconds.

Earlier, the Soviet Union won the gold medal in the 20-kilometer team event in 59:36.9, beating West Germany by 7.3 seconds.
