BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] arranged a reception Sunday evening for the delegation of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan [SPK] headed by its Co-Chairman P.V. Svoik on a visit to Korea. Kye Ung-tae, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the WPK Central Committee, said in his address that leaders of Socialist Party of Kazakhstan visited Korea in April last year and again this time. We are thankful for your sincerity, considering it an expression of deep trust and solidarity of your party with the great leader of our party and people Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and with our party and our cause of socialism, he noted. He highly estimated the efforts of the SPK to achieve national reconciliation and unity, defending faith in socialism and build a society where all people live an equal life and sincerely wished it new greater success in the future work to build an independent and democratic society. Co-Chairman P.V. Svoik said in his address that the delegation of his party visited Korea for the second time, which means that his party is continuously developing friendly relations with the WPK. He said it was happy to visit Korea at the significant time of greeting the spring holiday of April when Comrade Kim Il-song was born. The delegation of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan arrived here Sunday.
