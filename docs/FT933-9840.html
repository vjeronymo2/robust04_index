930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Major to lead Olympic delegation MR JOHN MAJOR is to lead the British Olympic delegation to Monaco when it makes its final presentation in its bid to bring the games to Manchester in the year 2000. The prime minister will be stopping off in Monaco on September 23 for the International Olympic Committee meeting on his way back from the Far East. Mr Major has already had several meetings with Mr Juan Antonio Samaranch, the IOC president, and other committee members in an attempt to further Manchester's cause. Mr Bob Scott, chairman of Manchester 2000, said last night that he was 'obviously delighted' the prime minister would lead the British team in Monaco.
