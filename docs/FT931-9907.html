930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / Move to win gene research centre THE UK submitted an Pounds 8m bid yesterday to host a new international science centre near Cambridge. The government's Medical Research Council and the Wellcome Trust, the largest UK charity, would fund the building of the European Bioinformatics Institute, which will provide information on human, animal and plant genes. Germany is competing for the institute. It wants to put it in a new building at the European Molecular Biology Laboratory in Heidelberg.
