February 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NUNN-STARLING BOUT RESET FOR APRIL 14

The Michael Nunn-Marlon Starling bout for the International Boxing Federation middleweight championship, postponed after Nunn suffered a back injury, has been rescheduled for April 14.

The fight was originally scheduled for last Saturday.

It will be the second boxing event at The Mirage Resort, the first in the hotel's 4,600-seat arena.

Nunn, 34-0 with 23 knockouts, will defend his title. Starling, 45-5-1 with 27 knockouts, is the World Boxing Council welterweight champion.
