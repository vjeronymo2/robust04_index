Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow February 10 TASS -- No plans have been made for a meeting between President Boris Yeltsin and newly elected President Yuriy Meshkov of Crimea in the near future, Russian presidential spokesman Anatoliy Krasikov told ITAR-TASS on Thursday [10 February]. The statement followed mass media reports that Meshkov was planning to visit Moscow for talks with Yeltsin. Krasikov said that the Russian leader has a cold and is working at his countryside house.
