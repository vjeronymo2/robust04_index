940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / International Company News: Italian bank plans capital increase Cassa di Risparmio delle Provincie Lombarde (Cariplo), Italy's largest savings bank, plans a capital increase and part floatation, Reuter reports from Rome. Mr Sandro Molinari, director general, said the flotation would be aimed at institutional investors, clients and bank employees. 'By the end of 1994, Cariplo will be on the bourse with a capital increase that will be close to 20 per cent and worth not much less than L2,000bn (Dollars 1.19bn),' Mr Molinari said.
