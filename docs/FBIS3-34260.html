Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 5 Feb (KUNA) -- The State Security Court resumed here today hearings in the case of the terrorist network, which attempted to assassinate former U.S. President George Bush during his visit to Kuwait last year, instigated and planned by the Iraqi intelligence. On 25 December 1993, the court postponed issuing sentences in this case in which 11 Iraqi and three Kuwaiti defendants are involved and decided to retry the case and hear the key witness in this case.
