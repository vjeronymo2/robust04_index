BFN [Letter from Colombian Liberal Party presidential candidate Ernesto Samper Pizano to Dario Arizmendi, news director of Caracol Network on the cancellation of the scheduled presidential debate; date of letter not given] [Text] Dear Dario: Last week, Conservative Party presidential candidate Andres Pastrana sent a message to the media in which he tried to schedule a program of debates specifying the topics, participants, and hours of the debates among the presidential and vice presidential candidates. Within this timeframe, a debate was scheduled for tomorrow in Cadena Caracol studios on the topic of farming. Subsequently, Dr. Pastrana sent a message expressing his refusal to participate in the debate proposed by himself. I deplore the situation created by this inconsistency, which has occurred previously in responses by Dr. Pastrana to other media proposals. Consequently, I believe that none of the meetings proposed for tomorrow in Caracol studios will be held. This, however, does not preclude their being rescheduled in the future. Please receive a cordial greeting. [signed] Ernesto Samper Pizano.
