930417 FT 17 APR 93 / UK Company News: CSC Investment net asset value lower CSC Investment Trust reported net asset value of 101.63p at December 31 down from 108.42p a year earlier. For the 1992 year net revenue came out at Pounds 45,000 (Pounds 85,000) for earnings per share of 2.75p (5.15p). The final dividend is a proposed 2.5p for a total of 4p (5p).
