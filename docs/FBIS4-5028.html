BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 6 -- Documents on cooperation between Vietnam and Bulgaria were signed here this evening. Signatories were Ngo The Dan deputy minister of agriculture and food industry, and head of the Vietnamese sub-commission and of the Vietnamese Government's economic delegation, and Rumen Khristov, head of the Bulgarian sub-commussion and of the Bulgarian Government's economic delegation. The documents include minutes of the 15th session of the Vietnam- Bulgaria Inter-Governmental Commission for Economic, Scientific and Technological Cooperation, a document on amendment of regulations on the organization of the commission, and, an agreement on veterinary hygiene between Vietnam and Bulgaria.
