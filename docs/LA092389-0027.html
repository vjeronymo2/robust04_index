September 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RAIDER MOTTO: HAVE BALL, WILL TRAVEL

For all the business savvy that Al Davis has, he has failed to seize upon the sports entertainment idea of the future: Port-a-Ball.

The Oakramento (or Sacland, if you wish) Raiders will play four games in Oakland and four home games in Sacramento with any playoff games to be played in Irwindale.

In addition, the Raiders will pack their bags every five years, move to a new locale, and adopt the slogan, "Just move, baby."

DAVID PAGE, Ventura
