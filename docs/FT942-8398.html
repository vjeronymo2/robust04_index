940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Healthcall Group HEALTHCALL GROUP: under the recent placing and intermediaries offer of 29.7m shares, 22.2m shares were placed firm. Of the 7.4m shares made available under the intermediaries offer eligible employees applied for 230,600 shares which will be satisfied in full. Applications from intermediaries in respect of the outstanding 7.18m shares were received in respect of 2.65m shares, also to be satisfied in full. The 4.53m balance of shares will be subscribed for by placees.
