Language: Swedish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] It is finally clear, Sunday,13 November will be the day of the national referendum on the European Union [EU], the party leaders agreed today. This means that Prime Minister Carl Bildt, who wanted to have the national referendum in June, has given in to the Social Democrats who wanted to have the national referendum in November. The most important thing is that in this way Sweden can become a member on 1 January next year, Carl Bildt says. [passage omitted]
