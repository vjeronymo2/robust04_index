Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- Political party leaders of Peru voiced full support and solidarity for the New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song, when they met the Korean ambassador to Peru on January 6. General Secretary of the Revolutionary Socialist Party of Peru Guzman Rivera Castaneda said the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in his New Year address analysed the last year's situation on a scientific basis and clearly indicated the way ahead of the Korean people this year. He said his party fully hailed his New Year address. Under the wise guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il the Workers' Party and the people of Korea resolutely smashed all sorts of the imperialists' manoeuvrings and made brilliant success in the socialist construction last year, inspiring the progressive people of the world struggling for socialism with confidence in victory of socialism, he said. He wished the Workers' Party and the people of Korea greater success in the new year in their endeavours for the socialist construction and national reunification in hearty response to the New Year address. Genaro Ledesma Izquieta, chairman of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students, said: The world is now vigorously advancing along the road of the chuche idea, the road of Korean-style socialism. The reality of socialist Korea, embodiment of the chuche idea, is, indeed, the ideal and hope of mankind. We will always stand by the Workers' Party of Korea in the struggle for socialism.
