November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

UCLA earned an automatic berth in the NCAA women's volleyball tournament by beating Washington State, 12-15, 15-11, 15-6, 15-7, in Pullman, Wash.

The Bruins qualified by winning the Pacific 10 Conference regular-season title. They are 16-0 in conference play and 27-1 overall.
