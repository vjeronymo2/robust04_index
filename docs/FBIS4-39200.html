BFN [Karen Topchyan report: "Armenia-Iran: Ties Broaden"] [Text] Armenia and Iran have signed an agreement on border trade between Armenia's Megrinskiy Rayon and Iran's Marand Province. This document envisages opening in the next few days a permanent market on the Armenian-Iranian border which will sell mainly clothing and foodstuffs duty-free. This agreement is by no means the first joint document. Today over a dozen treaties are being implemented between the two neighboring countries in the sphere of politics, the economy, culture, and sport. Iran is Armenia's second most important economic partner after Russia.
