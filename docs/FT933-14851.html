930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / International Company News: Saudi American Bank Saudi American Bank, which is 30 per cent owned by Citibank of the US, improved first-half net profits by 5.3 per cent to SR470.6m (Dollars 127.2m), compared with the year-earlier period, AP-DJ reports. Operating income was little changed at SR465.5m. However, the bank recorded a SR5.1m surplus in debt recovery compared with a SR17m provision against bad debts a year earlier.
