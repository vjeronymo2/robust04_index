BFN [Report by Vera Lima from Manaus, Amazonas State] [Text] The Amazonia Military Command [CMA] is monitoring a Venezuelan operation against gold prospectors at the Venezuelan-Brazilian border. The operation seeks to destroy clandestine airfields. In an official communique, the CMA reports that it was informed in advance of the operation by the Venezuelan Army. The communique notes that the Venezuelan Air Force is trying to destroy clandestine airfields in the Alto Quieto and Alto Ocamo townships, adjacent to the Brazilian border. The communique adds that this operation has been planned since late March. At that time, 36 gold prospectors who had been illegally prospecting in Venezuelan territory were turned over to Brazilian authorities. The CMA states that there are no reports of damage in Brazilian territory as a result of an air strike that took place in Venezuelan territory on 8 April. The CMA believes the Venezuelan authorities are just exercising their legitimate sovereign rights.
