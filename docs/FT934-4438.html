931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Inchcape in Far Eastern venture Inchcape, the international services and marketing group, has formed a joint venture with LA Gear of the US to market, distribute and sell LA Gear branded footwear, apparel and accessories in selected south-east Asian markets. Operations of the joint venture will begin in Singapore, Malaysia and Indonesia and will subsequently extend into other markets, including China.
