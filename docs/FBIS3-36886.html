Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" feature No. 27] [Text] In the course of his official visit to the U.S. on February 13-17 President Nursultan Nazarbayev will sign a long-term comprehensive cooperation treaty with that country, Kazakh Foreign Minister Tuleutay Suleymenov said at a news conference in Almaty. Nazarbayev will also brief the U.S. president on Kazakhstan's joining the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty. Suleymenov added that Britain and Russia would be notified later. According to the Minister, Nazarbayev is scheduled to meet with U.S. administration members, World Bank and International Monetary Fund managers and UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali.
