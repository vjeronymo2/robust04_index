Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 28/2/1994 -- Old parliament's leadership and members of Parliamentary Presidium, total 26 persons, will keep their status and will activate until the day of first session of the newly elected parliament. This first session is planned for March 15th, stated to BASA Pavel Rotaru, from the Parliamentary Press Service. Before February 27th, former MP's should have abandoned their ID's and diplomatic passports. Parliament commissions have formally finished their existence on the eve of elections, but most deputies engaged in commissions are still in offices in the parliament building, said Rotaru. He noted that the future MP's will enjoy the parliament's rooms and services only after the Central Electoral Commission confirm their status and their mandates are validated during first sessions of the new parliament.
