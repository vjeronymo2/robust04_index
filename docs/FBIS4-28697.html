BFN [Text] Vietnam was adjusting its foreign policy in order to serve the country's top priorities to meet its economic development. This was said by Deputy Foreign Minister Le Mai in an international investment seminar in Hanoi. He said: Vietnam has no time for getting involve in hostilities in neighboring Cambodia. On military aid to Cambodia, Mr. Le Mai said: It was no longer Vietnam's duty to do that. Vietnam did once and that was enough. Now it's turn for other countries if they want to help the Cambodian Government fight against the Khmer Rouge. Mr. Le Mai also said the United States will soon open a diplomatic mission in Vietnam and that the only remaining obstacle was the difficulty in finding suitable accommodation in Hanoi. He said economic development was a top priority for Vietnam.
