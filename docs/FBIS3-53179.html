Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on the U.S. Congress team's visit to Lebanon, Ja'farite Mufti Shaykh 'Abd-al-Amir Qabalan said it demonstrates the continuing U.S. partiality toward the Israeli enemy. He said the team's principal concerns are the remains of the Israelis and the whereabouts of the Israeli pilot. Mufti Qabalan added that the Lebanese and Arab detainees who are suffering in Israeli prisons are of no value in the American view. Shaykh Qabalan called for releasing the Lebanese detainees if the Americans wish to show signs of goodwill.
