BFN [Text] Interfax learned Friday at the press center of the Georgian Defense Ministry that more than 500 Abkhaz fighters were concentrated in the Gali region. The number is constantly increasing. There is also a high concentration of military technology. The center reported it had evidence to show that in the night of May 20-21 Abkhazia was planning wide scale operations to put an end to the peace talks and provoke a new escalation of the conflict. The Georgian Defense Ministry calls on the leaders of Abkhaz armed formations to withhold from any military actions which could lead to renewed bloodshed.
