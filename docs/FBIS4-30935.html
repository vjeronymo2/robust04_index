BFN ["A Document Which Testifies to the Involvement of Janullatos Subordinates in the Anti-Albanian Policy" -- ATA headline] [Text] Tirana, May 27 (ATA) -- The chairman of the Managing Council of Saranda Religious Commission, Mr. Niko Papanikolau, has issued a document in Greek language, which serves as a legal document in Greece, for an Albanian citizen from a village near Vlore. On the document is written "Villages Narte and Zvernec of Vlore are Greek villages and their inhabitants are of Greek origin, speak Greek language, and belong to Orthodox religion. In the document is also written "Albanian state has registered as Albanian citizens the people of Narte and Zvernec villages, the same thing has happened with Himare region. Saranda Council, Omonia, and Sillogu of northern Epirus in Corfu are the three institutions of Greek state, which are authorized to issue different certificates for the Albanians of southern part of the country, reports newspaper REPUBLIKA on May 26.
