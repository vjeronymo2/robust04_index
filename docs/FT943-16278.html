940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / International Company News: Qantas flotation early next year Qantas, the government-owned Australian airline in which British Airways holds a minority stake, is to be privatised in the first half of 1995, writes Nikki Tait. Mr Kim Beazley, finance minister, said yesterday that the timing was 'likely to maximise proceeds' for the federal government. In its recent budget, the government had confirmed the sale would go ahead in the current fiscal year, but gave no specific timing. See Lex
