940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / Trains to run to Brighton for first time in strikes A third of the normal number of trains will run tomorrow and on Monday and Tuesday next week if the expected signal workers' strike takes place, British Rail said yesterday. It hoped to offer a 'nearly normal' service over the weekend. Trains will run from London to Dover, Folkestone, Brighton and Worthing for the first time on strike days.
