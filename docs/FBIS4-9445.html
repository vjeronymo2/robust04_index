BFN [Excerpts] Sofia, April 25 (BTA) -- The Council of Ministers today relieved of his post Todor Borilkov, a member of the Privatization Agency's Supervisory Board who had tendered his resignation. [passage omitted] Behind closed doors the cabinet today approved a project for the construction of the Bulgarian section of a railway linking Bulgaria and Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] in the area of Guesevo and Kumanovo. The existing railway service is through Serbia. The cabinet approved a draft agreement that will serve as a basis for further talks with Macedonia on the construction of the railway, the government spokesman told the press.
