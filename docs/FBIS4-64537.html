BFN [Text] Istanbul, May 3 (A.A.) -- Energy Minister Veysel Atasoy left for Egypt on Tuesday [3 May] as the official guest of Egyptian Electric and Energy Minister Mahir Abazah. He told reporters at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport that during his visit talks will be held on the possibilities of increasing present cooperation in the field of energy. Atasoy said discussions will also take place on the setting up of an inter-connection system (United Energy Distribution System) between Turkey, Egypt, Jordan, Syria, and Iraq.
