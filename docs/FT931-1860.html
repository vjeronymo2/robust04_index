930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / Nynex buys cable franchises NYNEX CableComms, part of the US-based Nynex Corporation, announced said that it had bought three cable franchises in the UK from PacTel Cable UK, a subsidiary of the US Pacific Telesis Group, for an undisclosed sum. The three cable franchises - in greater Manchester, Bolton and Derby - give Nynex a total of 14 cable television and telephony franchises in the UK, reaching about 2m homes and businesses.
