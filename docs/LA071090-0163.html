July 10, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

CALVIN KLEIN OUT OF HOSPITAL

Calvin Klein was released from New York Hospital after a six-day stay for treatment of injuries he suffered while horseback riding.

Klein, 47, suffered two broken ribs and other injuries when he was thrown from a spooked horse July 3 in Bridgehampton, on Long Island. He was released Monday.
