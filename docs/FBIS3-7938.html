Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] An exchange of Croatian and Bosnian prisoners was carried out in Bosnia-Herzegovina today under the auspices of the International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC], the headquarters of this organization in Zagreb has reported. The Bosnia-Herzegovina authorities released 61 Croats from a prison in Jablanica, while the Bosnian Croats released 128 Bosniaks from Rodoc. The prisoners will be transported to a destination of their choice. At the same time, 200 Bosnian Croats left Jablanica with the help of the Red Cross. The majority of them were women and children.
