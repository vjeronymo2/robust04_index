941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Company News (this Week): Sharp recovery for insurance sector Burton Group - Other Companies The clothing retail group is forecast to announce pre-tax profits of between Pounds 40m and Pounds 43m for the year, compared to Pounds 18.5m last year, when it reports on Thursday. Margins at the multiples, including Burtons, Dorothy Perkins and Top Shop, are thought to have been squeezed.
