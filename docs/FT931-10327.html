930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Road funds shortfall forecast ADDITIONAL SOURCES of finance to fund Britain's road-building programme may be needed by the end of the century if congestion on motorways and trunk roads is to be avoided, Mr John MacGregor told MPs yesterday. 'We have to see whether private finance can augment public finance,' he said, responding to opposition calls to clarify whether the government was considering introducing road tolls as an environmental measure or to raise Treasury revenues.
