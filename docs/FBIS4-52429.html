BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 8 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the 25th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the DPRK and Chad. A bylined article of NODONG SINMUN says the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries at the ambassadorial level was a reflection of the common desire of the two peoples for mutual support and cooperation in the building of a new society. Over the past 25 years the friendship between the two countries has developed and the understanding between the two peoples deepened, adds the paper. The author of an article of MINJU CHOSON says: Our people will strive to strengthen their relations with the Chadian people on the basis of the idea of independence, peace and friendship. Our people wish the Chadian people greater success in their work, believing that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will grow stronger and develop.
