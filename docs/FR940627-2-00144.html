DATE: Membership is effective on the date of this notice. DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY Senior Executive Service; Combined Performance Review Board (PRB) AGENCY: Treasury Department. ACTION: Notice of Members of Combined PRB.

SUMMARY: Pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), this notice announces the appointment of members of the Combined PRB for the Bureau of Engraving and Printing, the Financial Management Service, the U.S. Mint, and the Bureau of the Public Debt. The Board reviews the performance appraisals of career senior executives below the level of bureau head and principal deputy in the four bureaus, except for executives below the Assistant Commissioner level in the Financial Management Service. The Board makes recommendations regarding ratings, bonuses, and other personnel actions. Three voting members constitute a quorum.
