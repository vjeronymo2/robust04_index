BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] The 10th meeting of the 10th Shanghai Municipal People's Congress Standing Committee, which concluded yesterday, deliberated and adopted resolutions on amending the Shanghai Municipality Regulations on Protecting Youngsters and the Shanghai Municipality Provisions on Protecting and Developing Postal and Telecommunications Services. The meeting also examined and approved reports by the municipal higher people's court and people's procuratorate on the status of processing written suggestions, as well as personnel appointments and removals.
