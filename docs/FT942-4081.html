940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Carlisle in loss but optimistic As indicated in January at the time of its proposed rights issue, share subscription and capital reorganisation, Carlisle Group, the property services company, incurred increased pre-tax losses for 1993. On turnover of Pounds 4.89m (Pounds 5.15m), including Pounds 1.47m (Pounds 1.66m) from discontinued operations, losses amounted to Pounds 2.86m against Pounds 947,000 in 1992. Losses per share deepened from 2.8p to 10.5p. The directors said it was a priority to restore dividend payments as soon as possible. Mr Nigel Wray, chairman, said the group now had a solid balance sheet with substantial cash resources and was 'well placed to take advantage of some very interesting opportunities'. He was optimistic about the next few years.
