920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Fall in income for poorest reported THE BOTTOM 10 per cent of households saw a fall in their real disposable income of 6 per cent between 1979 and 1989, according to a survey published yesterday. This compares with a 30 per cent rise in the average income of all households. Households below average income 1979-1988/89. HMSO. Pounds 9.95.
