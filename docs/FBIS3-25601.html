Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A buffer force is today being deployed in the southern suburbs of Brazzaville in Congo, which have been the scene of bloody ethnic clashes in recent months. All sides agreed to the deployment of the force after the cease-fire agreement signed on 30 January by deputies from the four regions of the south of the country. The force is being sent to keep the belligerents apart and to contain the various militias in their zones. This force of 400 men is due to be deployed today. It already began to take up its positions on the night of Friday to Saturday [18-19 February]. [passage omitted]
