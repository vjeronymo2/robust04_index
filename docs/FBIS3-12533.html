Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Sa'ib 'Urayqat, head of the Palestinian delegation to the Washington talks, was injured during a confrontation between residents and the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] in Jericho at midday today. A shock grenade hurled to disperse demonstrators exploded near the place where he was standing, slightly injuring him in the leg. He was treated on the spot and is now back home. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that a homemade bomb was thrown at an IDF patrol in the Jabalyah refugee camp. The bomb did not explode, and a Border Police bomb disposal expert defused it safely.
