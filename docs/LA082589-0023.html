August 25, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Nikko Securities Co. International has appointed Hideo Suzuki chairman and CEO. Suzuki, formerly the president of Nikko International, will also serve as resident managing director of the Americas operation for the parent company, Nikko Securities Co. He succeeds Toshio Mori, who will return to Japan in a senior management position. The new president of Nikko International is Tsuneo Iida, previously executive vice president. Nikko International has offices in New York, Chicago, Los Angeles and San Francisco.
