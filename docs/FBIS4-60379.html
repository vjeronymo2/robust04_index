BFN [Text] Baghdad, 22 May (INA)--Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz returned to Baghdad from New York this evening. During his visit to the United Nations, Tariq 'Aziz held extensive consultations with the ambassadors of the Security Council's member states, particularly those of the permanent member states, on the economic blockade imposed on Iraq. Tariq 'Aziz also met with the Austrian foreign minister in Vienna on his way to New York.
