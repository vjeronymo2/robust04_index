July 25, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"It's sad to see violence and murder at any level, but when it gets so bold as to take place in a (gathering) of Christians, well, the lack of reverence emerging in the community is really frightening."

-- Rev. Dumas Harshaw, pastor of Trinity Baptist Church, on the shooting Friday at Mt. Olive Church of God in Christ, in which two women were killed and a man wounded.
