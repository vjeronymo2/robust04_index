BFN [Text] Economy and Planning Minister Shim'on Shetrit met yesterday in Bucharest with Tunisian Economic Minister Mohamed Ghannouchi [title as heard]. In their meeting, the two men agreed that informal business ties should be established between Israel and Tunisia as an introductory step toward the tightening of trade and tourism relations between the two countries. Shetrit and Ghannouchi agreed that some of these ties should be conducted through a third country. This was reported by our correspondent Gid'on Kouts.
