940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / Delors to speak to TUC gathering Mr Jacques Delors, outgoing European Commission president, has agreed to address the Trades Union Congress in Blackpool this autumn, in a move that will be seen as a provocative act by the government and Conservative Euro-sceptics. He is expected to give a vigorous defence of the welfare state and full employment and spell out his enthusiasm for a social dimension in the European Union and the development of workers' rights.
