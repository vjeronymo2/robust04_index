BFN [Text] Jeddah, April 15, SPA -- The Ministry of the Interior issued a statement saying that Bashir Ahmad Bashir Karkahsah, a Syrian national, was beheaded here on Friday for smuggling hashish into the kingdom. The statement said the execution was carried out following the issuance of a court order approved by the High Judicial Council and a royal decree to this effect. The ministry warned that the same fate awaits those who commit the same crime.
