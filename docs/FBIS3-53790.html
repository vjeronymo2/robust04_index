Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The secretariat of the General People's Congress [GPC] today issued the following decision on the convening of a GPC session: The GPC is being convened to draft the resolutions of the basic people's congresses at their second regular session of the year 1403 after the Prophet's death [1994]. The GPC session will start on Saturday, 10 Sha'ban 1403 after the Prophet's death, corresponding to 22 January 1994, in the Hall of Loyalty in the city of Surt, at exactly 0900 [0700 GMT].
