930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / International Company News: Cascades shortfall CASCADES, the international packaging and paper group, reported a loss of CDollars 1.95m (USDollars 1.47m) in the second quarter against a loss net profit of CDollars 6.9m a year ago on sales of CDollars 415m against Dollars 212m. For the first half, the company had a loss of CDollars 1.1m against a profit of CDollars 12.3m.
