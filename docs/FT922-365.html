920627 FT 27 JUN 92 / Finance & The Family (At a glance): Low-cost share service Allied-Lyons is launching a low-cost share dealing service with commissions of just 1 per cent on its own shares and no minimum. Purchases can be made on a lump sum basis or via monthly savings of Pounds 50 or more. The company is also launching two personal equity plans - a corporate Pep, with an annual Pounds 6,000 limit, and a single company Pep, with a ceiling of Pounds 3,000. There will be an initial fee of Pounds 10 plus VAT and an annual management charge of 0.5 per cent, plus VAT. Brokerage fees within the Pep will be 0.25 per cent, plus stamp duty. The Pep is being administered by Bradford & Bingley (0274-555677) and details of the share dealing service can be obtained on 071-606-1768.
