By formal agreement, the CDC Procurement and Grants Office will act on behalf of and for ATSDR on this matter. A. Deadline Applications shall be considered as meeting the deadline if they are either: 1. Received on or before the deadline date; or 2. Sent on or before the deadline date and received in time for submission to the independent review group. (Applicants must request a legibly dated U.S. Postal Service postmark or obtain a legibly dated receipt from a commercial carrier or the U.S. Postal Service. Private metered postmarks shall not be acceptable as proof of timely mailing.) B. Late Applications Applications that do not meet the criteria in A.1. or A.2. above are considered late applications. Late applications will not be considered in the current competition and will be returned to the applicant.
