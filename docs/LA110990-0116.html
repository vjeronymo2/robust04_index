November 9, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NFL;

FALCONS' CASILLAS OFF SUSPENDED LIST

Atlanta Falcon nose tackle Tony Casillas was activated from the suspended list. Casillas had been suspended by Coach Jerry Glanville on Oct. 23 for two games after missing a plane to Los Angeles to play the Rams.

The Falcons also placed safety Eric Bergeson on the practice squad after he cleared waivers.
