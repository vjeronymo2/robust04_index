920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Aberforth Smaller net assets growth Net asset value of ordinary shares in Aberforth Smaller Companies Trust rose from 96.7p to 126.2p over the period from December 10 1990, its launch date, to end-1991. Total income came to Pounds 1.36m and earnings per share were 5.27p. The final dividend of 2.5p makes a total of 4.5p. In November Pounds 53m net was raised through a placing and open offer of C shares, and at December 31 62.5 per cent of that capital was invested and the net asset value per C share was 96.3p.
