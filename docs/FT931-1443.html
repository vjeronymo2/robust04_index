930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Vizcaya Vizcaya Holdings, the USM-quoted mining and exploration group, incurred a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.46m for the year to end-December. The outcome compared with a deficit of Pounds 3.58m last time, and was struck after an exceptional write-down of Pounds 1.38m against the group's concession in the non-operational Coto Chomin mine. The previous year saw an exceptional debit of Pounds 3.41m. Losses at the operating level amounted to Pounds 80,611 (Pounds 171,093). Losses per share worked through at 2.68p (6.7p). Mr Desmond Bloom, chairman, said the group intended to reorganise and focus its activities on property investment.
