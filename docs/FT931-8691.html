930218 FT 18 FEB 93 / Secretaries beat inflation SECRETARIES of chief executives received pay rises of 5.7 per cent last year compared with of 3.2 per cent for departmental secretaries and 2.8 per cent for clerk/typists. Average basic pay for a chief executive's secretary is Pounds 13,800 - ranging between Pounds 19,000 in central London and Pounds 12,455 in East Anglia. Average basic pay is Pounds 12,400 for a director's secretary and Pounds 8,100 for a clerk/typist.
