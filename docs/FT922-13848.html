920409 FT 09 APR 92 / Election 1992: Still friends - Ballot Box Yesterday's note about the Conservative party advertising campaign brought a forthright response from Saatchi & Saatchi Advertising. Bill Muirhead, the chairman and chief executive, says that the firm's relationship with Conservative Central Office is excellent, and that there is absolutely no question of the account being 'as good as lost.' Meanwhile, it's back to nail-biting.
