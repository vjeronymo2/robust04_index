BFN [Excerpt] His Highness Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, the amir of the fraternal state of Kuwait, this afternoon received brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, who briefed him on the developments of the dispute in our country, the progress of the battles there, and regional and Arab reactions. During the meeting, Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah expressed fraternal Kuwait's firm and consistent stand which calls for the settling of disputes with the logic of national interest and fraternal dialogue in isolation of the use of force. His highness stressed during his meeting with brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas that the use of force and arms would only exacerbate the differences and problems. Wounds would be deepened and strength would be lost, while destruction would prevail. He said Kuwait would support any sincere and useful effort to stem the bloodshed and restore understanding and amity between the two parts of Yemen. The amir of Kuwait expressed fear that the Yemeni conflict would have adverse effects on the overall Arab situation. [passage omitted]
