930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Receivers appointed at Novalal Novalal, the USM-quoted biotechnology and plant propagation company which was suspended last week pending clarification of its financial position, has been placed in administrative receivership. Novalal had been in trouble for some time and its last published results, for the 17 months to end-December 1991, showed a Pounds 3.5m pre-tax loss. Mr Peter Lawrence and Mr Brian Mills, of accountants Booth White, have been appointed administrative receivers.
