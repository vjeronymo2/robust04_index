930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Foreign & Colonial High assets rise Net asset value per ordinary share of Foreign & Colonial High Income Trust stood at 70.7p at end-June, an improvement of 26 per cent over the 56p standing 12 months earlier. Available revenue for the year worked through at Pounds 825,000 (Pounds 930,000) and earnings per share slipped to 2.75p (3.1p). A final dividend of 1.42p makes a 2.62p (2.52p) total.
