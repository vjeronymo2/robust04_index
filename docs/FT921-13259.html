920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK News (Employment): FT strike vote majority increases A SECOND strike ballot of Financial Times journalists has produced a larger majority in favour of strike action if the management goes ahead with plans for compulsory retirement on health grounds of nine people suffering from repetitive strain injury. In the latest poll, 83 per cent supported strike action, against 71 per cent in the last ballot. There were 93 per cent in favour of action short of a strike (91 per cent).
