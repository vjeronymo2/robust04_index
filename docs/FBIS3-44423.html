Tsai] [Text] Taipei, Jan. 4 (CNA) -- Insurance experts from Taiwan and Mainland China will meet in Taipei January 22-25 in a bid to strengthen cross-strait insurance cooperation. The three-day forum will be the second of its kind, the first being held in Beijing and Shanghai in September 1992. A 20-member mainland delegation is expected. It will be the first time in more than four decades that mainland Chinese insurance experts have visited Taiwan. Sponsored by the National Insurance Institute, the forum will focus on insurance rules and regulations, future trends, and the division of social and business insurance.
