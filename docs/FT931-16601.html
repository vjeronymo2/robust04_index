930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / Australia rules out rate cut The Australian government yesterday ruled out a reduction in interest rates after the Australian dollar touched a five-year low of 67.70 US cents in nervous trading before the release of the November balance of payments data, writes Kevin Brown in Sydney. Interest rates have fallen 12 times to 5.75 per cent since peaking at 18 per cent in January 1989.
