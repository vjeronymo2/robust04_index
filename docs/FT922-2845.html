920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / International Company News: Princedale Princedale Group, the marketing services company formerly known as Craton Lodge and Knight, returned to profit in the six months to March 31. The pre-tax line amounted to Pounds 326,000 against a deficit last time of Pounds 196,000. Turnover, reflecting a disposal, declined to Pounds 6.37m (Pounds 6.99m). Earnings were 0.06p (losses 0.05p).
