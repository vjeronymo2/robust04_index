Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, Feb 13 (AFP) -- Three Chinese technicians helping to build a road in northeast Yemen have been kidnapped, Chinese diplomats said Sunday. The three were abducted in a mountainous area while building a road between Hija, 127 kilometers (75 miles) north of Sanaa and Ma'rib, 173 kilometers (100 miles) east of the capital, they said. No date was given for the abduction.
