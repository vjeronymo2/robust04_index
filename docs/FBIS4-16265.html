BFN [Text] One of the leaders of the Russian community in northern Kazakhstan, (Boris Suprunyuk) was arrested at his apartment in Petropavlovsk, Kazakhstan, on 12 April. This is reported by the Congress of Russian Societies. The authorities claim that (Suprunyuk) was arrested because he ignored a summons to attend the oblast prosecutor's office. In July 1993, a criminal case was instituted against (Suprunyuk) on charges of inciting interethnic discord. (Suprunyuk) has declared a hunger strike. Boris (Suprunyuk) has repeatedly opposed infringement of the rights of the Russian-speaking community in Kazakhstan.
