931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Olives acquires Fine's Properties Olives Property is acquiring Fine's Properties for Pounds 2.53m. The consideration will be satisfied by the allotment of 1.39m new shares, Pounds 1.28m in redeemable loan stock to be issued at par, and Pounds 750,000 in convertible loan stock at par. Fine's has a portfolio of 11 properties, mainly freehold secondary retail shopping parades in the south of England, producing a net rental income of Pounds 533,000 per annum.
