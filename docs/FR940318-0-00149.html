(11) R&hyph;400(60/40) and CFC&hyph;114 Industrial Process Air Conditioning, Retrofit. (a) HCFC&hyph;124. HCFC&hyph;124 is acceptable as a substitute for R&hyph;400 (60/40) and CFC&hyph;114 in industrial process air conditioning. HCFC&hyph;124 has a very low ODP and GWP. HCFC&hyph;124 is the only refrigerant that has been submitted for this end-use, and EPA invites more submissions and information related to substitutes.
