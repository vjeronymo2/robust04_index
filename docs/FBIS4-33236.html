BFN [Text] Defense Minister General Jose Gallardo has announced that Red Sun--Puka Inti, in Quechua--an Ecuadoran terrorist group similar to Peru's Shining Path Maoist movement, will surrender its weapons. This evening we will have very pleasant news for the Ecuadoran people, Gallardo said, although he would not provide details. Red Sun began operating about three years ago and carried out dynamite attacks on public installations and on the headquarters of political parties. None of those attacks produced casualties. [Mexico City NOTIMEX in Spanish at 2105 GMT on 2 June, in a similar 150-word, Quito-datelined report, adds: "Gallardo reported that the subversive Ecuadoran group will surrender its weapons to military authorities within the next few hours and that its members will join the legal democratic process."]
