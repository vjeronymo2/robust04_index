December 8, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SDSU HAS ITS HANDS, NOT ITS STANDS, FULL

What a shame it was to attend the San Diego State-Miami game and have only 34,200 other "real fans" attend.

I'm sure next season the typical San Diego sports fans (also called "front-runners") will begin to show up and support the Aztec football program.

Al Luginbill has turned the program around and is a top-notch coach. Look for San Diego State in the Top 20 for years to come. Maybe then, even the front-runners will regularly attend games.

JOEL H. BRYDEN

San Diego
