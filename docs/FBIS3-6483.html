Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A national meeting of foreign trade officials took place at the People's Palace of Culture on 15 and 16 March. The meeting summed up the achievements and experiences gained in foreign trade under the wise guidance of the party and the leader [suryong], and discussed tasks to effect a new change in the implementation of the foreign trade-first policy in hearty response to the party's revolutionary economic strategy. Portraits of Comrade Kim Il-song, the great leader of our party and our people, and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were placed at the front of the meeting place. Slogans reading "Long live the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song!" and "Long live the glorious Workers Party of Korea [WPK]!" were put up. Present at the meeting were Comrade Kang Song-san, member of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and premier of the State Administration Council [SAC]; Comrade Han Song-yong, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the WPK Central Committee; Comrade Choe Yong-nim, candidate member of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and vice premier of the SAC; Comrade Hong Sok-hyong, candidate member of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and chairman of the State Planning Committee; Comrades Kim Pok-sin and Kim Hwan, vice premiers of the SAC; Comrade Pak Nam-ki, chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Administrative and Economic Committee, other officials concerned; and officials of the trade sector. The meeting began with the chorus of the song of General Kim Il-song and the song of the dear Comrade Kim Chong-il. At the meeting, a report by Yi Song-tae, chairman of the State External Economic Committee, was followed by speeches. The reporter and speakers said our country's foreign trade has traversed the road of victory under the tested guidance of the great leader [suryong] and the party. They said the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, after the liberation, has set forth ideas and theories on developing foreign trade on the principle of mutual benefit, equality, and filling each other's need on the basis of building the self-reliant national economy, and has wisely led the foreign trade work. They noted that the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il has set up steps to strengthen and develop foreign trade in conformity with the demands of developing realities, and has led to endlessly promote the foreign trade work in accordance with the trend of the country's economic and trade development. In the past period, they noted, solid export goods production bases have been built, exports increased and progress has been made in the efforts for multi-faceted and diversified foreign trade under the leadership of the party. Noting that our country is now developing economic relations with other countries on the principles of complete equality and mutual benefit, observing the principles of chuche and independence, they said the nation's foreign trade has made an active contribution to the defense of the dignity and honor of the country, the economic construction of the country and the improvement of the people's living. The reporter and speakers said they, with the meeting participants' hearts of invariable loyalty, extend the highest honor and the most heartfelt thank you to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, who have wisely led the foreign trade work and given warm love and trust to the officials of the foreign trade sector. They pointed out that heavy, yet glorious, tasks of effecting upturns in foreign trade and upholding the party's revolutionary economic strategy lie ahead the officials of the foreign trade sector. The reporter and speakers spoke on being dedicated to the implementation of the party's revolutionary economic strategy by accomplishing bigger results in foreign trade, holding aloft the great leader's [suryong] teaching. They called for actively cultivating foreign markets and building up an unquestionable reputation in foreign trade in keeping with the changed circumstances. They also spoke on ways to strengthen the bases for the production of export goods in many sectors of the national economy, to increase their production, and to improve their qualities to the highest level. They called on the managerial officials of economy to give precedence to foreign trade in conformity with the demands of the foreign trade-first policy and urged plants and enterprises to fulfill their export plan by the day, by the month, and by the indicators. They also called for properly carrying out export freight transportation. They pointed out that foreign trade should be multi-faceted and diversified in order to bring about a decisive change in foreign trade. The reporter and speakers stressed on making contributions to defending and adhering to the socialist cause and achieving bigger results in the foreign trade work by actively waging the struggle to implement the party's foreign trade-first policy with the conviction of sure victory and the revolutionary zeal. A resolution was adopted at the meeting.
