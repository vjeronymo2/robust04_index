940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Bronze for Torvill and Dean Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean failed to repeat their 1984 Winter Olympic gold performance at Sarajevo and had to settle for bronze in the ice dance competition at Hamar, Norway, behind Russians Oksana Gritschuk and Evgeny Platov in first place and Maia Usova and Alexander Zhulin.
