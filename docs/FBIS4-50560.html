BFN [Text] Budapest, May 17 (XINHUA) -- Hungary is willing to expand cooperation with China in economy and military projects, Hungarian President Arpad Goncz told Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, here today. During a meeting with General Zhang, Goncz said Hungary is ready to deepen its cooperation with China, which has witnessed phenomenal economic growth in recent years. The president said Zhang's visit will contribute to the further development of cooperative ties between the two countries and between their armed forces. The Chinese general, who arrived here last Friday [13 May] for a five-day visit to Hungary, said he is confident that the relations of cooperation between the armed forces of the two countries will grow to a new level. Prior to Hungary, Zhang visited Romania and Poland.
