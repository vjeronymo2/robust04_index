Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Another 200 Italian soldiers have left Somalia. As the conclusion of the UN mandate approaches, all the international contingents are reducing their presence. Some 1,000 Italian troops remain in Mogadishu. The demobilization of the UNOSOM [United Nations Operation in Somalia] troops is taking place in a climate of intense fighting between warring factions that are still a long way from reaching any peace settlement.
