BFN [Text] Zagreb, April 10 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serb troops on Sunday began crossing the Drina river just south of the besieged Moslem town of Gorazde following a massive artillery offensive, the UN High Commissioner for Refugees announced. The soldiers began crossing the river shortly after 3:00 p.m. (1300 GMT), the UNHCR spokesman in Zagreb, Peter Kessler said. The river had marked the front line between the warring forces.
