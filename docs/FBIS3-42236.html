Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni Minister of Interior Brigadier General Yahya al-Mutawakkil has denied that the kidnapping of three French nationals northwest of Sanaa last week was politically motivated. In press statements today, he added that the kidnappers motive in taking the three hostages, one man and two women, is to provoke the government and make unrealistic demands. He went on to say that his ministry has taken practical measures to besiege the kidnappers in case they do not surrender and free the hostages.
