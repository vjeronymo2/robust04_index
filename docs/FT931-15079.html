930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICAN stocks lost ground on nervousness picked up from foreign markets following the US and allied military attack on Iraq, but saw some recovery by the close. The BMV index of 40 active stocks lost 18.3 at 1,763.8, after falling to 1,732.6. Telmex shed 0.57 per cent on conservative assumptions about its performance in the fourth quarter of 1992.
