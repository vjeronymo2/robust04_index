Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The PLO and Israeli delegations in Davos last night issued a joint communique stating that the first meeting between the two parties was conducted in a very positive atmosphere which indicated good chances for future progress. Both delegations established a joint formulations committee which held its first meeting last night. This committee will continue work this morning. The formulations committee will deal with the major issues currently on the agenda: the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho First agreement and the issue of the border crossings. This morning, the committee will report to the plenum on the results of the discussions. The members of the formulations committee are Nabil Sha'th, Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'], and Akram Haniyah, on behalf of the PLO, and its Israeli members are Uri Savir, Major General 'Uzi Dayan, and Yo'el Singer. Minister Yosi Sarid warned against excessive optimism and explained that there were still gaps between the parties' positions. Sarid said that it would only be possible this evening to know whether an agreement had been reached. Last night, Yasir 'Arafat and Shim'on Peres held a private meeting which lasted approximately two hours.
