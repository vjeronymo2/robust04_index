920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Reduced interest boosts Honeysuckle As well as showing a significant increase in results for 1991-92, the Honeysuckle Group, USM-quoted ladies' fashion manufacturing group is forecasting improved turnover and profits in the current year. In the year ended May 31 1992 turnover fell to Pounds 13.8m (Pounds 18.7m). After lower costs the operating surplus came to Pounds 684,000 (Pounds 666,000), and a reduction from Pounds 635,000 to Pounds 210,000 in interest payable pushed up the pre-tax profit from Pounds 275,000 to Pounds 720,000. Earnings per share were 6.3p (2p) and the dividend is held at 1p. Subsequent to the year-end Level 2, the woven garment subsidiary, had been closed and losses of Pounds 339,000 had been provided via an extraordinary charge.
