930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: M&G Second Dual net assets ahead Net asset value of the split capital M&G Second Dual Trust improved from 474.02p to 549.37p per capital share over the 12 months to May 31. Attributable revenue was virtually static at Pounds 2.39m for earnings of 23.87p (23.86p) per income share. A proposed final distribution of 10.67p maintains the total for the year at 23.86p.
