BFN [Text] A group of approximately 40 Council of Jewish Settlements leaders and yeshiva students, headed by Knesset Member Hanan Porat, arrived in Jericho's old synagogue a short time ago. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports they arrived in a convoy of cars hoisting Israeli flags. The commander of the Palestinian police force in the area opened the synagogue gates and they entered accompanied by many media crews. Our correspondent notes that there is great tension in the place.
