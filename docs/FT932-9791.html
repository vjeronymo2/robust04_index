930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: BM banking facilities extended BM Group, the construction equipment concern, has successfully negotiated continued financing facilities with its 10 banks for an initial period to June 6. Talks on securing longer term funding are proceeding but it is thought unlikely there will be an agreement by the end of the period, requiring an extension of the arrangements. The company is working on a survival plan expected to cost about Pounds 42m in redundancies, closures, disposals and write-offs.
