921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Pacific Horizon shows recovery At July 31 1992 net asset value of Pacific Horizon Investment Trust amounted to 26.26p, against 35.52p a year earlier. However, on October 26 it had risen to 33.2p. In the 12 months to end-July, net revenue rose to Pounds 97,000 (Pounds 29,000) for earnings per share of 0.24p (0.07p). The dividend, however, is cut from 0.729p to 0.14p.
