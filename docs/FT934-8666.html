931113 FT 13 NOV 93 / Caterpillar workers walk out A long-running dispute over work practises between Caterpillar nd the United Auto Workers escalated yesterday when a walk-out by union members in Illinois spread to 11 other facilities in three states, Frank McGurty reports from New York. The UAW, which represents 14,500 Caterpillar workers, said the walk-out was called after the US heavy equipment manufacturer took disciplinary action against a UAW official.
