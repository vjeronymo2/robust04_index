BFN [Text] The Democratic Liberal Party [DLP] held a meeting of senior party post holders today and discussed measures the party should take as the national security is driven into a tense situation while international sanctions against the North is impending because of the North Korean rejection of nuclear inspections. In the meeting, the DLP decided to make efforts to have the people become aware of the present security situation. At today's meeting, Yi Se-ki, chairman of the Policy Committee, said that the crisis situation resulting from the North Korean nuclear issue is heightened because North Korea claimed that it would regard any form of sanctions against it as an act of war declaration against North Korea. Yi also said that in case the UN sanctions turn out to be difficult because of the Chinese opposition, despite the fact that measures for sanctions are pushed ahead on the part of the United Nations, the government is grouping for multinational sanctions among the ROK, Japan, and the United States. DLP Chairman Kim Chong-pil noted this is the time for the people to be precisely aware of the national security situation, and stressed the need for the authorities to explain the present situation to the people and have them share the correct awareness. Chairman Kim Chong-pil stressed that the DLP should play a leading role in this.
