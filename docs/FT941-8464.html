940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Anglo & Overseas lifts net assets 34% Anglo & Overseas Trust, managed by Morgan Grenfell, had a net asset value of 506.6p per share at December 31, a rise of some 34 per cent on the comparable 1992 figure of 379.6p. After the preference dividend, available revenue for the 1993 year amounted to Pounds 8.92m (Pounds 8.89m) for earnings of 7.81p (7.78p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 5.4p brings the total for the year to 7.15p (6.85p).
