Driving Pattern Factor Section 503(c)(3)(A)(iv) of the Motor Vehicle Information and Cost Savings Act requires that DOE take into account ``the specific driving patterns of electric vehicles as compared with those of petroleum-fueled vehicles.'' The driving pattern factor is the ratio of annual vehicle miles travelled for an electric vehicle to that of a petroleum-fueled vehicle. Since there is an insufficient number of electric vehicles in service for use as a sample, a factor of 100 percent (1.00) will be used until such time DOE has collected sufficient data to show otherwise.
