BFN [Unattributed SEGODNYA report: "Bankers Will Meet Regularly"] [Text] According to informed sources, a decision has been made to hold regular meetings between the leadership of the Russian Federation Central Bank and Russia's largest banks. The first meeting took place at the end of last week at the Center of Liberal-Conservative Policy. It was attended by Central Bank Chairman Viktor Gerashchenko and by directors of Imperial, Menatep, Alfa- bank, National Credit, Sberbank, Stolichnyy, Promstroybank, and other banks. The bankers declared their positions with respect to current financial processes.
