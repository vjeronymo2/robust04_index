921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / Chief justice warns of unrest LORD Taylor, the Lord Chief Justice, warned last night that civil unrest could break out unless more money was put into the justice system. Cost and delay were two 'blots' on the system, and to deprive it of adequate funds 'endangers the very framework of our society,' he said when delivering the BBC's Richard Dimbleby Lecture. The backlog of court cases threatened to become a national disgrace.
