June 28, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

STUDENT LOSES ON ILLEGAL GAMBLING

A college student has lost a bid to collect a $1.1-million casino jackpot he hit while gambling illegally as a 19-year-old.

State District Judge Donald Mosley Tuesday upheld a ruling by state gaming regulators allowing Caesars Palace to refuse to pay Kirk Erickson, now 21, of Royal, Ark., the jackpot he won on a $1 slot machine in 1987.

Noting that Nevada law bars those under 21 from gambling, Mosley said his ruling centered on whether a person should be allowed to profit from an illegal act.

Erickson said he will appeal.
