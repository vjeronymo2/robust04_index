August 11, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: VILLA PARK;

WOMAN'S BODY FOUND IN SWIMMING POOL

The body of a young woman was found in a Villa Park swimming pool Thursday and police said an autopsy was planned to determine the cause of death.

Sheriff's Department Lt. William Francis said the woman, whose name was not released, could not swim, but it was not known how or why she got into the pool.

The woman was visiting a family in the 19000 block of Valley Drive, and no one saw her enter the pool, he said.
