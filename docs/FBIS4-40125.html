BFN [Decree No. 123-I SF of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly "On Preparations for the 50th Anniversary of Victory in the Great Patriotic War," dated Moscow, 1 June 1994 and signed by V.N. Viktorov, deputy chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly] [Text] Having listened to information submitted by the Federation Council Committee for Security and Defense Questions regarding preparations for the 50th anniversary of victory in the Great Patriotic War, the Federation Council decrees that: 1. The following proposals shall be submitted to the Russian Federation Government: --funds shall be allocated from the federal budget to prepare and hold events in connection with celebrations to mark the memorial dates of the Great Patriotic War of 1941-1945; --a set of measures to be organized and held in 1994-1995 involving searching for and ceremonially reinterring the remains of soldiers who died during the Great Patriotic War on Russian territory and who were not given a proper burial is to be included in the work plan of the Russian Organizational Committee for Preparing and Holding Events in Connection With the Memorial Dates of the Great Patriotic War of 1941-1945. 2. Russian Federation representatives in the Russian Federation parliamentary delegation to the Interparliamentary Assembly of CIS Member States are to be tasked during 1994-1995 with putting the issue of organizing and carrying out searches and ceremonial reinterments of the remains of soldiers who died during the Great Patriotic War on the territory of CIS member states and who were not given a proper burial onto the agenda of the next session of the Interparliamentary Assembly. [Signed] V.N. Viktorov, deputy chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Moscow 1 June 1994 No. 123-I SF
