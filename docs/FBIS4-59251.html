BFN [Text] President Yasir 'Arafat met the Cuban president, comrade Fidel Castro, in Pretoria yesterday evening. During the meeting, recent developments with regard to the Palestinian cause were discussed. President 'Arafat gave a detailed explanation of the developments in the peace process. President Castro expressed his full support for the great efforts made by President 'Arafat and his brothers at the PLO Executive Committee to recover the Palestinian people's rights under these conditions. He stressed that peace, like war, required courage, pointing out that the Palestinian people's great sacrifices have been crowned with this victory, which is the way to full independence. The meeting was attended by the members of the delegation accompanying President 'Arafat and by the Cuban foreign minister.
