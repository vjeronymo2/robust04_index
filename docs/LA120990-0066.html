December 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

INN ADVICE

Several years ago, you wrote an article about The Governor's Inn, 86 Main St., Ludlow, Vt. 05149.

I saved the article because of your favorable review. My wife and I have just returned from a four-day stay at the inn. It was all that you said and more. We were pampered and spoiled by the staff. And the food was outstanding.

THOMAS R. FLYNN

Santa Ana
