920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / Skin specialist treats firemen A SKIN specialist from Guy's Hospital in London was last night called in to examine firemen who tackled a fatal explosion at the Hickson and Welch chemical plant in Castleford, West Yorkshire, on Monday. Three people were still in a critical condition yesterday following the explosion, which killed two workers and injured 15 others. Nine firemen were still in hospital last night. Mr Kevin Osborne, chairman of the West Yorkshire Fire Brigades Union, said: 'We are led to believe some are suffering from 'glowing fingertips'.'
