BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Petrovac, April 10 (TANJUG) -- Inter-Moslem clashes on Sunday continued at unabated intensity along all demarcation lines in the Cazin Krajina, in western Bosnia-Herzegovina, Moslem media in the Bihac region reported. Forces of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia (APWB), proclaimed last September, are successfully repelling a fresh offensive by members of the 5th Corps of the Moslem Army, loyal to hardline leader Alija Izetbegovic, the Western Bosnian News Agency ZBIA said. Moslem-controlled radio in Bihac said that both sides reported casualties and the missing during Sunday's fightings in the Cazin Krajina. This was also confirmed by the Moslem Radio Sarajevo. The Moslem Army command, loyal to Izetbegovic, has admitted that 500 people were killed and over 2,000 wounded during eight months of fighting with the APWB forces, loyal to Izetbegovic's rival Fikret Abdic who opposes Izetbegovic policy of war.
