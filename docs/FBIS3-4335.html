Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "Hookup" program] [Text] The Mainland Affairs Council [MAC] held a plenary meeting on 28 March. The meeting adopted the division of work in handling the unauthorized entry of mainland vessels into [words indistinct] Tung-sha and Nan-sha waters. The MAC decided in principle that it is necessary to fire warning shots to drive away any mainland vessels which enter without permission our country's restricted water areas. If they enter prohibited water areas, they shall be driven away with gunfire without warning. However, if they refuse to leave, both the vessels and the personnel on board shall be taken into custody. If a vessel is taken into custody twice, it shall be confiscated. Kao Kung-lien, vice chairman of the MAC, said: [Begin recording] The detention shall be recorded into the ship's data. According Article 32 of the (?Cross-Strait) Regulations [liang an tiao li], a vessel shall be confiscated if it is detained twice. [end recording] On the handling of smuggled goods, the MAC meeting decided: Smuggled tobacco, wine, and other ordinary goods shall be confiscated. Smuggled farm products shall be handled by the local county government on behalf of the Council of Agriculture.
