931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: 15% growth at MMT Computing MMT COMPUTING, the USM-quoted computer systems consultant, lifted pre-tax profits by 15 per cent, from Pounds 1.5m to Pounds 1.73m, in the year to August 31. Mr Mike Tilbrook, the chairman, said that trading conditions were slowly improving, and that although margins remained under considerable pressure, the current order book was 'excellent'. He added that with cash balances of about Pounds 4.5m, the board was recommending a final dividend of 2.75p (2.4p), making 4p (3.5p) for the year. Turnover on continuing operations improved from Pounds 6.22m to Pounds 7.06m. Earnings per share worked through at 9p (7.9p).
