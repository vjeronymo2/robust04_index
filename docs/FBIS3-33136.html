Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Bolivia through 17 February: The Inter-American Development Bank, IDB, will grant Bolivia a $100 million loan to build houses this year. (Santa Cruz EL MUNDO in Spanish 9 Feb 94 p 1) Bolivian Government Oil Deposits President Mauricio Gonzales on 8 February reported that the company's revenues in 1993 totaled 2.882 billion bolivianos and had a deficit of 82 million bolivianos. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 9 Feb 94 Economic and Business Section p 1) On 8 February, IDB President Enrique Iglesias and Bolivian Ambassador Andres Petricevic signed in Washington a $64 million loan agreement that will be designed to implement urban development and cleanup projects. The World Banks also approved a $42 million credit to finance municipal development projects. (La Paz HOY in Spanish 9 Feb 94 p 6)
