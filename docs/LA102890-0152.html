October 28, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

ALPHA LEAGUE

Village Christian 27, Maranatha 12 -- Myles Brown rushed for 97 yards in 12 carries and three touchdowns to lead Village Christian (7-1-1, 1-1) at Kennedy High. Village Christian quarterback Mark Vail completed seven of 11 for 107 yards. The Crusaders rolled up 349 yards while limiting Maranatha (3-4, 1-1) to 93. SCOTT KATCHER
