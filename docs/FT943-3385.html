940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Owen & Robinson Owen & Robinson, the jewellery and sports footwear retailer, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.67m to Pounds 1.49m in the half year to July 31, on slightly lower turnover of Pounds 8.09m, against Pounds 8.21m. Losses per share were cut from 13.94p to 6.06p.
