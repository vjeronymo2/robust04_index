Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 19 (SNARK) -- The Presidium of Karabakh Supreme Soviet expressed condolence to relatives of the people who died as result of air crash of Iranian plane near Stepanakert. "The disaster overtook Iranian brothers and sisters has become a great tragedy for Karabakh people, who have seen a lot grief and suffering," it is said in mourning message. The Presidium of Karabakh Parliament stated about the readiness of Karabakh authorities to provide all necessary conditions for detail investigation of the air crash.
