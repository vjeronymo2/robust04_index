920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Personal Assets net assets up 18% Personal Assets Trust reported a net asset value of 70.92p per share as at April 30 - an improvement of some 18 per cent over the year. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 250,000, up from a restated Pounds 216,000, for earnings of 1.67p (1.45p) per share. A second interim dividend of 0.85p, making a total of 1.6p for the year, was announced last month.
