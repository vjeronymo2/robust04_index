October 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

FLORIDA;

DISCOVERY IS MOVED TO LAUNCHING PAD

The space shuttle Discovery was moved to the launching pad at the Kennedy Space Center in Florida for the start of a secret military mission on Nov. 20. Running two days behind schedule because of thunderstorms, Discovery and its mobile launching platform were mounted atop launching pad 39B. Engineers began preparing for a dress-rehearsal countdown.
