920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Cadbury Schweppes buys Alma brands Cadbury Schweppes has acquired the Hacks and Victory V brands from the receivers of Alma Holdings for Pounds 3.08m cash. The purchase price included the production equipment and the Angus manufacturing facility in Dundee. Hacks and Victory V are well-known brands in the UK medicated confectionery market, estimated to be worth some Pounds 68m a year.
