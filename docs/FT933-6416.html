930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Burlington Group BURLINGTON GROUP reported profits before tax of Pounds 64,000, against Pounds 54,000, for the half year to June 30. Earnings per share improved from 0.28p to 0.39p. Net asset value was 18.78p, up from 18.13p at the December year-end and 17.52p at the end of June 1992.
