BFN [Text] Geneva, June 3 (AFP) -- Serb forces resumed Friday their withdrawal from the UN-declared exclusion zone around Gorazde in eastern Bosnia, raising hopes that truce talks stalled since Thursday would be able to get under way, UN special envoy Yasushi Akashi said Friday. Speaking after a meeting with Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic, Akashi said he hoped the Geneva talks, aimed at securing an overall ceasefire in Bosnia-Herzegovina, could start on Saturday morning.
