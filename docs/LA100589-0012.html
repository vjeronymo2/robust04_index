October 5, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DUMPING L.A. TRASH IN DESERT

What will the little boys with the train fetish think of next? Two years ago it was "missiles-by-rail" and now it is "trash-by-train." We wonder if Christmas gifts of toy train sets for these wackos will end their frustrations and nutty ideas for our Riverside County rights-of-way and leave us to the serious business of promoting needed rail commuter service between Coachella Valley and the coast through the pass and Inland Empire.

DOLLIE IRWIN

Banning

CAROLYN TOENJES

Palm Springs
