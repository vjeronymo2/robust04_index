Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak and Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat continued their talks over breakfast this morning. Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif stated that the talks between the Egyptian and Palestinian delegations lasted 90 minutes. He said that they discussed the results of the talks held in Oslo yesterday between 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres and future steps in the Middle East peace process.
