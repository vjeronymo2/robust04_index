BFN [Text] Suhaj, 7 Apr (MENA)--Soldier Nadi Muhammad, 20, from the Central Security Forces, was martyred today during a shoot-out in Markaz Tima in the Suhaj Governorate between a group from the police force and some fugitive terrorist elements. Terrorist 'Abd-al-Hadi Haqq was killed in the accident. During the shoot-out, Captain Mustafa Muhammad Ahmad, assistant at the Markaz Tima investigations department, and soldier Muhammad Mutawi' received light injuries. A group from the department for executing verdicts in Markaz Tima was executing verdicts against defendants sentenced in various cases. While performing its duty in Tima, the group was surprised by bullets directed on it by terrorists. It dealt with the situation immediately, killing one terrorist. The soldier was martyred and the Markaz Tima investigations assistant and another soldier were slightly injured. The Suhaj Security Department command and the various investigation organs went to the scene to inspect it. The prosecution has been informed to proceed with investigations.
