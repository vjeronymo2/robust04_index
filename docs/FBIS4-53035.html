BFN [Text] A seven-member delegation from the People's Republic of China led by Mr. He Zhiqiang, governor of Yunnan Province, arrived in Yangon [Rangoon] by air from Bangkok this afternoon. The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Brigadier General Abel, minister for national planning and economic development; responsible officials; Mr. Liang Feng, PRC ambassador to Myanmar [Burma]; and embassy staff. The delegation led by Yunnan provincial governor is accompanied by an 18-member economic cooperation mission comprising members of economic enterprises in Yunnan Province. The delegation and the economic cooperation mission will visit Myanmar from 16 to 24 May and hold talks on economic cooperation between Myanmar and Yunnan Province. Minister Brig. Gen. Abel hosted a dinner in honor of the Yunnan Province governor's delegation and economic cooperation mission at the People's Park Restaurant.
