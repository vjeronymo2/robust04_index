October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT WEEKDAY UPDATE;

UCLA;

ERROR COULD HAVE COST GAME

UCLA had to settle for a field goal against Washington State Saturday night in the third quarter after driving to the one-yard line.

The Bruins were assessed a 15-yard penalty for having 12 men on the field.

"It was a good call," Coach Terry Donahue said. "It was a game-administration mistake and it shouldn't happen. It could have cost us the game."

Instead of scoring a touchdown, UCLA trailed, 20-13. But the Bruins went on to score 17 points in the fourth quarter. MAL FLORENCE
