920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Treuhand reports fall in orders East German companies controlled by the Treuhand agency have suffered a steep fall in orders from both east and west, which will make the agency's privatisation programme more difficult, writes Leslie Colitt in Berlin. Mrs Birgit Breuel, the Treuhand president, blamed west German companies for spreading rumours of the imminent collapse of their east German competitors to prevent them getting orders.
