Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Senate today held a session under speaker Ahmad al-Lawzi to discuss a number of subjects on its agenda. Details in the following report by Jamal al-'Abbadi: [Begin al-'Abbadi recording] The Senate denounced the Serb aggression on the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the ongoing ethnic cleansing drive targeting the republic's peaceful people. A statement issued by the Senate today appealed to the international community to take military action to stop the aggression and lift the arms embargo on the republic to enable the Bosnians to defend themselves. [passage omitted] Responding to a request by Senator Hammad al-Ma'ayitah, Deputy Prime Minister Dr. Ma'n Abu-Nuwar said investigations are still continuing into the martyrdom of Na'ib 'Imran al-Ma'ayitah, counselor at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut, and the explosive devices that went off in Cinema Rivoli in Amman and Cinema Salwa in al-Zarqa'. The government, he said, can not, for the time being, go further than the facts revealed by the interior minister. The deputy prime minister praised the watchful eye of Jordan's public security agencies. [end recording]
