Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Affairs Ministry Press Center has reported that at the invitation of China's Internal Trade Ministry, a delegation from Ukraine's Agriculture and Food Ministry is over there. Employees from this department are in the delegation, and so are representatives from our state's business circles. The delegation has already held a number of meetings at the Ministries of Internal Trade and Agriculture of the People's Republic of China. The sides determined specific directions of joint work in the sphere of production and scientific-technical cooperation -- setting up joint enterprises, introducing new agrarian technologies, and the exchange of information. During the course of talks a protocol was signed on economic, scientific-technical, and production cooperation.
