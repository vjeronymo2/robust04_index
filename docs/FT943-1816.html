940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / Letters to the Editor: Not all can have a choice Sir, I enjoyed Giles MacDonogh's article, 'Vegetarian food terrorism' (September 17/18). Not long ago I was introduced to a couple of ladies who promptly informed me they were vegetarians. Their faces conveyed self-righteousness and even seemed to challenge me. My comment was that they were very lucky to have a choice. Having lived most of my life in what is known as the third world, I ate what was available or what I could afford. There followed a dead silence and the subject was then dropped] Lydia Nijhof, Albany Court, Robertson Terrace, Hastings, TN34 1JH
