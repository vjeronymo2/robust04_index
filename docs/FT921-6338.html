920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / South Korean farm deficit South Korea's customs-cleared trade deficit in agriculture, forestry and fisheries rose 16 per cent to Dollars 6.76bn last year from Dollars 5.82bn in 1990, provisional trade ministry figures showed, Reuter reports from Seoul. The total deficit was Dollars 9.65bn last year. Exports of agricultural, forestry and fisheries goods were Dollars 3.06bn in 1991 while imports stood at Dollars 9.82bn, against Dollars 3bn exports and Dollars 8.82bn in imports in 1990. The rise in the deficit was attributed partly to a sharp increase in imports from China.
