June 4, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

TICKET SOLD IN CITY WORTH $9.6 MILLION

A ticket buyer in Mission Viejo won the $9.6-million jackpot in Saturday night's Lotto 6/49 game, it was announced Sunday.

The winning numbers were 3, 16, 24, 31, 43 and 49, with the bonus number 13. Ticket sales were $14.3 million.

There were 13 winners in the 5-of-6 plus the bonus category. They bought their tickets in San Leandro, Watsonville, Chico, Modesto, Sylmar, Baker, San Fernando, Santa Barbara, Reseda, Westminster, San Clemente, Huntington Beach and Oxnard.

The estimated prize pool for Wednesday's game is $4 million.
