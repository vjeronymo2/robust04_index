February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME BUILDERS COUNCIL INSTALLS JAN WHITMAN AS 1989 PRESIDENT

Jan Whitman, director of land acquisition at Premier Homes of Newport Beach, has been installed as 1989 president of the Home Builders Council, educational arm of the Building Industry Assn. of Southern California.

Other new officers are Jay Blakslee of Chicago Title, first vice president; Paul Johnson of The Fieldstone Co., second vice president; Scott Allen, Citation Builders, treasurer; Ann Romano, Ann Romano Associates, secretary, and Greg Currens, Mission Viejo Co., immediate past president.

Members of the board of directors are JoAnn Borg, Sue Case, Pegi DiRienzo, Charleen Hannah, Mark Hoover, Debbie Johnson, Kathy Johnson, Chris Johnston, Darlene Lavender, Larry Lewis, Howard Mitzman, Julie Oberg, Barbara Johnson Parish, Debra Pellerino, Rick Peters, Dave Placek, David Salene, Donna Silliphant, and Paul Ware.
