Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry press service reported that the situation remains tense in our republic's northwestern regions bordering on Armenia. The border settlements in Kazakhskiy Rayon came under intensive shelling from armored cars and artillery throughout 24 March. (?Seven) residents were injured. The enemy shelled our positions around Goradiz with various types of weapons on the night of 24-25 March. Our fighters are dealing a appropriate reply to the enemy. A relative calm is prevailing on the other sectors of the front.
