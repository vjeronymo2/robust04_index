BFN [Royal decree read by the Secretary General of the Parliament Salih al-Zu'bi during a Senate session on 17 May] [Text] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. We, Husayn I, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, taking cognizance of Article 36 of the Constitution, decree the following: His Excellency Ahmad al-'Aqayilah shall be appointed a member of the Senate as of 16 May 1994.
