940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: BBA settles US litigation for Dollars 19.8m BBA, the engineering and motor components group, has settled litigation between its Automotive Products subsidiary and Tilton Engineering out of court for a total of Dollars 19.8m (Pounds 12.8m). The amount, payable in two instalments, will be taken as an exceptional item in BBA's current year results. AP will be granted a licence to manufacture Tilton's carbon racing clutch, the subject of the dispute, and all outstanding claims against AP will be dropped. BBA also announced that trading for the first five months of the year had been encouraging with the benefits of the restructuring and subsequent action on the cost base beginning to come through. The company's shares fell 5p to 183p.
