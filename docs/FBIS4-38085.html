BFN [Text] During the course of successful operations, the areas of Arzan-Qimat, Shina, and an important hill of the rocket brigade have been completely cleared of the Golboddin-Dostam militia. Reports received by the Bakhtar Information Agency say that these areas were conquered by the (?Islamic army) during the course of today's battles. Similarly, in the west of Kabul, the areas of Dasht-e Khairabad, the Hassan-e Gosala bridge, and Security Zone 7 have been captured by the forces of the Islamic State. During the course of the clashes, three armored vehicles and many militia members were taken prisoner by the mojahedin, including seven foreign citizens. The prisoners were captured in the Karta-e Naw and Bini Hesar areas.
