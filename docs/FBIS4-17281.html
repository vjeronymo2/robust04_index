BFN [Unattributed report under the "Around the Clock Non-Stop" rubric: "State Duma Jeered At"] [Text] Yesterday the Judicial Chamber on Information Disputes proposed that the founders of EKSPRESS GAZETA -- KOMSOMOLSKAYA PRAVDA and the SEGODNYA group -- fire the weekly's editor-in-chief, Aleksandr Kupriyanov. The reason for this disgrace was a March article about all kinds of perks enjoyed by State Duma deputies. The chamber, in the person of its chairman, Anatoliy Vengerov, deemed this publication to be "a jeer" at people's deputies.
