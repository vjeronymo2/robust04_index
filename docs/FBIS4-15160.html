BFN [Unattributed report: "Drop in Production Continues"] [Text] According to the Dnipropetrovsk Oblast Statistics Department, the volume of production in January-March 1994 decreased by 45 percent compared with the corresponding period last year. The decline in production particularly affected construction materials, black metallurgy, and machine-building. More than half of enterprises in the oblast reduced the production of consumer goods. There are, however, certain positive aspects of the production slump, as ATN admits. Industrial enterprises emitted to atmosphere 18 percent less harmful substances compared with the parallel period last year.
