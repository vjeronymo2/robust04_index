May 18, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Robert J. Stetson, Burger King Corp.'s chief financial officer since February, 1989, has been named the fast-food company's chief operating officer. Stetson, 39, will be responsible for the operation of the more than 5,400 Burger King restaurants in the United States. No one has been named to replace Stetson as CFO.
