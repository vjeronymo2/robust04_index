940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Mercury purchase multimedia stake Mercury Communications, whose parent company is Cable and Wireless, has made its first investment in a multimedia group with the acquisition of 36.8 per cent of M33, for Pounds 3.75m. M33 produces reference books and multimedia products under the Andromeda Oxford and Andromeda Interactive names. Mercury has electronic on-line distributive rights, allowing it to market Andro-meda interactive products directly to consumers.
