Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] In a successful operation the combatants of the 41st Sarallah Division of the Guards Corps Ground Forces smashed six convoys carrying narcotics in the general vicinity of Andujerd in Kerman and impassable heights. According to a report from the Guards Corps, in the operation codenamed the Birth Anniversary of Mahdi, God speed his advent, brave personnel of the forces of Islam, in a clash lasting for several hours, surrounded the convoys of 'Isa, Javad, and Shamshir Shahbakhsh and three other convoys of the Shahbakhsh group and dealt them heavy blows. According to this report, in the clash, which was executed using the element of surprise, the combatants of the 41st Sarallah Division of the Guards Corps Ground Forces, with the cooperation of the brave personnel of Kerman Army Aviation personnel, killed six merchants of death and wounded 10 others. The forces of Islam seized 32 sacks of narcotics weighing 241 kg, five vehicles, 30 motorcycles, and 40 light weapons with ammunition in the operation.
