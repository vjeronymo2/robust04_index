910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Soldier dies following attack in Armenia A Soviet officer confronts an Armenian woman during an argument in the Armenian town of Artzvashan, surrounded by tanks as residents handed over their weapons. Armenian militants attacked interior ministry troops in Azerbaijan with machine guns and grenades yesterday, killing one soldier and wounding eight, the Azerbaijani press agency Assa-Irada said. It said the attack took place at dawn on a road between the villages of Kehne Gishlak and Tatli in Akstafa province, 1 1/2 miles from the Armenian border.
