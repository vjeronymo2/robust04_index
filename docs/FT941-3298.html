940316 FT 16 MAR 94 / Survey of Information and Communications Technology (33): Digital's investment in Scotland Digital Equipment Corporation, a leading supplier of networked computer systems, has announced an investment package of more than Pounds 90m for its Scottish manufacturing sites in Ayr and South Queensferry. Mr Chris Conway, managing director of Digital UK says this investment confirms the company's commitment to manufacturing in Scotland where it makes a range of products from silicon chips to implemented computer systems -'half of Digital's revenues come from Europe and the UK leads the field in both sales and manufacturing,' he adds. Digital's total investment in Scotland stands at Pounds 300m. The latest investment package involves more than Pounds 65m for the South Queensferry facility. This will increase production capacity for the Alpha AXP silicon chip.
