November 17, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

EAST VALLEY LEAGUE

Poly 56, North Hollywood 19 -- Running back Jonathan Campbell rushed for 175 yards in 19 carries and one touchdown at North Hollywood as the Parrots clinched a berth in the 3-A Division playoffs.

Poly (7-3, 5-2) quarterback Lance Garcia gained 59 yards in seven carries and scored three touchdowns. Leon Gable gained 138 yards in 23 carries and scored two touchdowns for North Hollywood (5-4, 4-3). TODD JACOBSON
