940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Low & Bonar makes disposal in US Low & Bonar, the international packaging and materials group, has sold Bonar Fabrics, its non-wovens business in South Carolina, to HDK Industries of Tennessee for Dollars 2.7m (Pounds 1.84m) cash. The sale completes the group's exit from this business sector following the closure of its Scottish plant in 1993.
