940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Smith New Court award upheld The Court of Appeal yesterday upheld a High Court ruling that Citibank NA should pay compensation to Smith New Court, the securities house, over losses incurred in SNC's purchase of Ferranti shares worth Pounds 23m. The Appeal Court reduced the amount of damages to be paid to SNC from Pounds 10,764,005 to Pounds 1,196,010.
