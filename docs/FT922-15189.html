920402 FT 02 APR 92 / Palestinians die in Gaza clash Four Palestinians were killed and as many as 80 wounded in a clash involving Israeli paramilitary police in the occupied Gaza Strip yesterday, Agencies report. There were conflicting accounts of the violence. Palestinians claimed police in pursuit of armed activists opened fire on bystanders, whereas the Israeli army claimed Palestinians who had attacked soldiers fled in a car that ran over three people. Hospital officials and witnesses said four people were shot dead and at least five others had gunshot wounds.
