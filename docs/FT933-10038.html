930806 FT 06 AUG 93 / IoD favours hard Ecu option THE HARD Ecu represents the only option left for business to gain full advantage from the single European market, the Institute of Directors said yesterday. It said that, with the timetable for a single currency 'inevitably delayed', the early introduction of a common currency was of paramount importance for the European economy. Mr Peter Morgan, IoD director-general, said the present method envisaged for creating monetary union was unrealistic and that the hard Ecu 'currently represents the best alternative route'.
