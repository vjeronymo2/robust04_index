BFN [Text] At 1230 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received at the al-Rawdah Palace a Yemeni delegation comprising Salim Salih Muhammad, assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, and Muhsin Muhammad Abu-Bakr. The meeting was attended by Deputy Prime Minister Rashid Akhtarini and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'.
