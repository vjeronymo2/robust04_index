October 6, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

Actor Lance Kerwin, 28, was arrested Wednesday on charges of drug possession as he left a motel room near Candlestick Park in San Francisco. A woman identified as Desiree Posey, 33, of San Francisco, walked out of the motel room with Kerwin and was also arrested on drug possession charges, police said. Kerwin was seen last Sunday as a guest star on "Murder She Wrote" and as a child star was in "The Family Holvak" 1975-77. ALEENE MacMINN
