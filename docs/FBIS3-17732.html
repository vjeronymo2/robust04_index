Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Spies are stepping up their activities in Russia and in Bryansk Oblast, however strange that might seem. The increased activities of foreign special services was noted by Maj-Gen. Vladimir Bobrovskiy, head of the Federal Counterintelligence Service directorate in Bryansk Oblast. Military and defense installations, as well as the region's military potential, are of special interest to them. For example, a hotel room was recently equipped as a center capable of monitoring the work of the technical services of a military aerodrome situated nearby. However, Bryansk's counterintelligence agents detained the visitors to the town in time.
