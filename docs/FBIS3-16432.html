Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow March 15 TASS -- Moscow Welcomes the March 12 meeting of Azeri Parliament Speaker Rasul Guliyev and his Armenian counterpart Babken Ararktsyan, their essential agreements and striving to continue personal contacts, head of the Russian Foreign Ministry's Information and Press Department Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing here today. Russia has always supported and encouraged direct conflicts between the warring sides on a realistic basis, he said [sentence as received]. In the opinion of the diplomat, vague wordings of the cease-fire protocol resulted in close contact of the warring units and resumption of fighting. Russia insistently continues mediating efforts and interacts with all states and international organizations interested in settlement of the Karabakh conflict, stressed the Foreign Ministry's spokesman.
