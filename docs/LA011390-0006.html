January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DISNEY EMPLOYEES AND BEARDS

Mickey Mouse, Abe Lincoln, Captain Hook, Pluto, Goofy, Walt Disney and the Seven Dwarfs: What do they have in common? They have facial hair and they are employed in one way or another by Disney. If the amusement industry giant would be consistent in its rules, all of these characters should be laid off. What's next -- barring admission to their parks and hotels of customers with mustaches? What a Goofy, Dopey, Mickey Mouse rule! Where is the ACLU when we need it most?

TOM DEITERICH

Ontario
