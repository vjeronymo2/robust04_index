941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / International Company News: Adare at IPounds 798,000 Adare Printing Group, the Dublin-based USM concern, almost doubled interim pre-tax profits to IPounds 798,000 (Pounds 787,000) on turnover 59 per cent ahead at IPounds 20m. Last year's figures for the period to October 31 were IPounds 419,000 and IPounds 12.6m respectively. Mr Nelson Loane, chief executive, said Alexander Pettigrew and Label Converters, acquired in the first quarter, had been 'assimilated smoothly' and were performing to expectations. Earnings per share came out at 8.77p (5.87p). The interim dividend rises to 1.0395p (0.945p).
