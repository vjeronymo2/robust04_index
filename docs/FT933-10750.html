930802 FT 02 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Demjanjuk's deportation blocked Israel's Supreme Court, which last week cleared John Demjanjuk (left) last week of being 'Ivan the Terrible', the gas chamber operator at Treblinka death camp in world war two, blocked his deportation to his native Ukraine while it considers whether he can stand trial for other alleged war crimes. His departure was challenged on the basis that he was a guard at other camps.
