Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemen's ulema held a closed meeting at the Mu'adh Bin-Jabal Mosque in al-Janad today to discuss the political crisis in the country and what the ulema can do as part of their duty and responsibilities to tackle the crisis. Earlier, a speech rally was held at the Mu'adh Bin-Jabal Mosque. It was attended by brothers Field Marshal 'Abdallah al-Sallal; Shaykh 'Abd-al-Wahib al-Zandani, member of the Presidential Council and member of the Yemeni Ulema Society; Judge Muhammad Isma'il al-Hajji, head of the Yemeni Ulema Society; and a large number of senior ulema.
