940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / Electricity price cuts announced Norweb, the Manchester-based electricity company, announced a series of price cuts and other improvements for domestic and business customers following the recent price capping agreement by electricity generators. It said the cuts would reduce power costs for 1.8m standard household customers by 6 per cent. Seeboard, based in Hove, is extending recent price cuts for businesses to domestic customers. Tariffs would be reduced by an average of 2 per cent, saving households an average of Pounds 14 a year including an Pounds 8 rebate already announced.
