Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A convoy of seven trucks carrying 1,250 tents left Peshawar this morning for Jalalalbad. These tents have been sent by the World Food Program with the cooperation of Pakistan for distribution among the Afghan war affectees. Reports from Afghanistan speak of fresh fierce fighting between the rival warring factions in (Chehalstun) and Gazergah area of Kabul. The AFGHAN ISLAMIC PRESS reported from Kabul that the antigovernment forces used heavy shelling and rocket fire against government strongholds in southern parts of the city. The television mountain also came under heavy fire. In retaliation, the air force of the state Defense Ministry bombed the Dostam militia positions in Bala Hesar and (Tappa Maranjan). Afghan sources told Radio Pakistan's Peshawar representative that this is for the first time in the month-long war that the fighting has spread south of Kabul.
