BFN [Text] Tallinn, May 17, BNS -- The CSCE High Commissioner for Ethnic Minorities, Max Van der Stoel, began a two-day visit to Estonia today. Van der Stoel had meetings today with President Lennart Meri, members of the parliament's foreign affairs commission, and officials from the Estonian Institute of Human Rights, the Foreign Ministry press office reports. The CSCE high commissioner is scheduled to meet Prime Minister Mart Laar, representatives of the parliament's law commission and foreign ministry officials Wednesday [18 May]. He will also meet with Russian Ambassador Aleksandr Trofimov.
