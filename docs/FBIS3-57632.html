Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Valentin Sergeyev, head of the government press service, refuted an expression attributed to Viktor Chernomyrdin by the Italian newspaper CORRIERE DELLA SERA: I am in charge in Moscow. In his interview to INTERFAX, Sergeyev, who is accompanying the Russian prime minister on his visit to Italy visit, described the quote which was published in the Italian newspaper as a rather annoying misunderstanding. Such methods can play into the hands of those who are not happy about Yeltsin and Chernomyrdin working together on forming a government capable of continuing the course of reforms, the head of the Russian government press service noted.
