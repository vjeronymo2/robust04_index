940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: TSB chief's pay lifted to Pounds 0.5m Mr Peter Ellwood, chief executive of TSB Group, was paid Pounds 516,819 last year, against Pounds 373,407, as a result of the bank's sharply improved performance, it disclosed yesterday, writes John Gapper. TSB, which made pre-tax profits of Pounds 301m (Pounds 5m) in the year to October 31, said in its annual report that Mr Ellwood had received a bonus of Pounds 213,750 for exceeding an earnings per share target. Sir Nicholas Goodison, the TSB chairman, received total emoluments of Pounds 274,000, against Pounds 271,105.
