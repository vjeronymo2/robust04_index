BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Shulman] [Text] Moscow April 28 TASS -- Russian Federation Council Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko and visiting Azerbaijani Parliament Chairman Rasul Guliyev signed an agreement on inter-parliamentary cooperation between the Russian Federal Assembly and the Azerbaijani Supreme Soviet in Moscow on Thursday. The agreement comes into force as of the day it is signed. The sides pledged to promote all forms of inter-parliamentary cooperation in order to establish a legal basis for the development of relations in all spheres of mutual interest. They also agreed to set up a standing inter-parliamentary commission to coordinate interaction. The commission will aim at promoting the development of state relations between the Russian Federation and Azerbaijan. It will hold sessions at least twice a year in Moscow and Baku.
