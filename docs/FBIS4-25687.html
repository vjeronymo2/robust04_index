BFN [Text] Beijing, June 14 (XINHUA) -- Wang Bingqian, vice- chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), met with a delegation from the Danish parliament's Trade and Industry Committee, here today. The 24-member delegation, led by Chairman Lissa Mathlasen of the committee, arrived here on June 12 for a visit to China at the invitation of the NPC's Financial and Economic Committee. The delegation is expected to travel to Shanghai, in addition to its stay in Beijing.
