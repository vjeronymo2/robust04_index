920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Davy wins Utah smelter contract Davy McKee, part of the engineering division of Trafalgar House, and Fluor Daniel of the US have been awarded a contract for the engineering and construction management of a new Dollars 600m (Pounds 324.3m) copper smelter in Utah, writes Andrew Baxter. The client is Kennecott, owned by RTZ of the UK, which is spending Dollars 880m on the new environmentally friendly smelter and refinery at its Utah Copper operations near Salt Lake City. Although Davy would not disclose its share of the 50/50 contract, the deal is one of its biggest since the takeover last year by Trafalgar House and one of Davy's biggest US contracts for some years.
