920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Buoyant exports aid Dale Electric Robust export markets helped Dale Electric International, the Yorkshire-based power and lighting company, report a 50 per cent rise to Pounds 1.9m in 1991-92 profits before tax and exceptional items, despite a small sales fall from Pounds 56.6m to Pounds 55.9m. The final dividend is again 3.1p, for a same-again 5.1p total.
