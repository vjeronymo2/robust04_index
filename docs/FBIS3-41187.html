Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Karam Agency Administration Carries Out Successful Raid on Drug Smugglers; 1399 Kilograms of Heroin and the Pick-Up Truck Used for Smuggling Were Seized."] [Text] Peshawar (MASHRIQ News): The political administration of Karam Agency has seized 1,399 kilograms of heroin worth millions of rupees, as well as the pickup truck used to smuggle the narcotics. According to details received, the Agency's political agent was informed that certain international smugglers would smuggle a large quantity of heroin from Ilaqa Ghair to Bando Basti Ilaqa and from there to foreign countries. A raiding party was formed under the supervision of assistant political agent, Abdullah Khan. In the Khoidad Kheel police station area, the raiding party signalled a pickup truck, number KAW8503, to stop. At that time the smugglers riding in the truck started firing on the levy force. When the levy force fired back, the smugglers left the truck and ran away. A search of the truck produced 1,399 kilograms of heroin. The political administration has seized the pickup and its cargo and started an investigation.
