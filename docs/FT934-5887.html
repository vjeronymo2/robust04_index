931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Badgerline shares end day unchanged Shares in Badgerline, the Avon-based bus company, saw modest turnover of 2.9m shares on its first day of trading yesterday and closed unchanged on the day at 115p, after fluctuating between 112p and 117p. The flotation price valued the group at Pounds 99.3m. Lazard Brothers, Badgerline's merchant bank, placed 23.4m shares with institutions and other investors. The offer of a further 14.2m shares to the public had been 1.7 times subscribed.
