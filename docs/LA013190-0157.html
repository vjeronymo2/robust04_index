January 31, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

GLIMPSES

The Grammy ceremonies on Feb. 21 will feature Meryl Streep presenting a lifetime achievement award to Paul McCartney while Miles Davis is similarly honored. The live entertainment will include Grammy nominees Rodney Crowell, Fine Young Cannibals, Don Henley, Billy Joel, k.d. lang, Bonnie Raitt and the team of Linda Ronstandt and Aaron Neville. Lined up to present the awards are Paula Abdul, Natalie Cole, Richard Marx, Motley Crue, Olivia Newton-John, Bob Seger, Milli Vanilli and Dwight Yoakum.
