BFN [Text] The Albanian Government at noon today declared Khristos Iakovou, Greek general consul in Gjirokaster, persona non grata. The decision was officially stated in a note to Greek Ambassador in Tirana Tsalikis. The note from the Albanian Foreign Ministry states that the decision to expel the Greek consul was based on activities not in accordance with his duties in our country. He is obliged to leave the Republic of Albania within 48 hours from the time of the official note's delivery.
