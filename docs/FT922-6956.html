920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Monument to sell stake in gas field Monument Oil and Gas, the independent exploration and production company, has agreed to sell its 3 per cent interest in the Markham gas field in line with its strategy of disposing of licences in which its interest is relatively small. Wintershall (UK) is paying some Pounds 10.4m cash for UKCS License P701. This includes the reimbursement of certain development costs incurred by Monument. The agreement provides for adjustment of the settlement subject to a redetermination of field interest between the participants.
