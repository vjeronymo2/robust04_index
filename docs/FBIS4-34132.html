BFN [From the "Seven O'Clock National Newscast"] [Excerpt] Acting President Guillermo Ford Boyd chaired the scheduled cabinet meeting at the Presidential Palace. Deputy Foreign Minister Carlos Cordero announced that the government will take steps against Haiti. [Begin recording] [Cordero] In accordance with recommendations made at the recent meeting of foreign ministers in Belen de Para, Brazil, within the context of the OAS General Assembly meeting, Panama issued a decree prohibiting all commercial flights between Panama and Haiti. Acting President Ford signed the decree that also prohibits the issuance of takeoff permits for any flights heading for Haiti. [Unidentified reporter] What criteria were taken into account? [Cordero] The OAS recommendations. This is another diplomatic effort directed at solving the Haitian problem. [end recording] [passage omitted]
