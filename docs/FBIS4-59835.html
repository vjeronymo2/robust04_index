BFN [Text] In a reciprocal artillery attack by the mojahedin and the Islamic Army forces in Kabul, 16 militia were killed and 17 others were injured, including Mir Yaqub, a commander of Golboddin's militia. According to the reports from the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY, the artillery attack took place two nights ago in response to the missile attacks carried out by the militia's of Golboddin-Dostam, in which the militia's had targeted the residential areas of Kabul city. In the course of these attacks, in addition to the aforementioned losses, two rocket launchers, a DShKS, two PK machine-guns, and several militia strongholds were destroyed in the southeast of Kabul. Recently, according to another report, 15 members of Dostam's militia sold their weapons in Charasiab in Kabul and fled to Pakistan via Kandahari bridge located in Mohammad Aqha District, Logar Province.
