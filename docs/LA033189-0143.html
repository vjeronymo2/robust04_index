March 31, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST : LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

FOUNTAIN VALLEY;

THIEF, CLAIMING TO HAVE GUN, ROBS BANK, FLEES

A Fountain Valley bank was robbed just before noon Thursday by a "baby-faced" thief who escaped, police said.

The Security Pacific Bank branch at 17970 Magnolia St. was robbed at 11:56 a.m. by a blond about 21 years old, 5-feet-10 and 120 pounds. The robber was wearing a black leather jacket and white T-shirt, Police Lt. Rod Gillman said.

Gillman said the robber was given an undetermined amount of money after he handed a teller a demand note that said he had a gun. Officers were unsure if he fled in a car or on foot.
