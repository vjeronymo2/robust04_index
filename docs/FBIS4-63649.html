BFN [Text] On 24 May, Ukrainian Ambassador to Moldova Vitaliy Boyko met Moldova's Deputy Foreign Minister (Osmochescu) and handed him a letter from the Ukrainian foreign minister on Ukraine's stance regarding the Crimean issue. The minister pointed out that Moldova, which has suffered from the policy of separatism, fully realizes the threat posed by the actions of Crimean power structures; entirely supports the Ukrainian leadership, denounces separatism, and supports Ukraine's unconditional territorial integrity. Mr. (Osmochescu) assured the ambassador that the Moldovan leadership would support Ukraine's position with regard to denouncing separatism at all levels, both within the CIS borders and in the international arena.
