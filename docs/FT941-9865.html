940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / Social affairs minister sacked The Danish prime minister, Mr Poul Nyrup Rasmussen, yesterday dismissed his social affairs minister, Mrs Bente Juncker, after only 14 days, writes Hilary Barnes from Copenhagen. Mrs Juncker, of the coalition government's Centre Democratic party (CD), was sacked after relaying unfounded rumours to journalists that one of her severest political critics, the head of a home for mentally retarded adults, had once been under investigation for sexually abusing his clients.
