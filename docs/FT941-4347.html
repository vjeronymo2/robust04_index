940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Singapore Para Turnover of the Singapore Para Rubber Estates fell from Pounds 781,113 to Pounds 739,595 for 1993, but pre-tax profits increased to Pounds 465,930, compared with Pounds 363,124. Earnings per share of this producer of oil palm fresh fruit bunches and natural rubber, rose from 2.41p to 2.99p, while the dividend is lifted to 1.9p (1.7p).
