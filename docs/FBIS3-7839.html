Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 28 Feb--The first congress of the Self-Defense National Front [FNS] will be held in Warsaw on 12 March, the FNS Political Council decided at its extraordinary meeting. Twenty-two regions were represented at the meeting. Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party, will be a guest of the congress. The date of Zhirinovskiy's arrival in Poland and the program of his visit have not been disclosed so far.
