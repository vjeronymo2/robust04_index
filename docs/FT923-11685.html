920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / Argentina in Paris Club debt deal ARGENTINA has signed a deal to restructure part of its Dollars 8.73bn (Pounds 4.57bn) Paris Club debt, Mr Domingo Cavallo, economy minister, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Buenos Aires. 'An agreement has just been signed in Paris with the governments of the (Paris Club) to restructure payments due over the next 10 years,' he told a news conference. Under the deal, Dollars 2.8bn due this decade was rescheduled to 16 years with a four-year grace period. During the fifth and sixth year of the deal, starting in March 1995, amortisation payments would be limited to 3 per cent of the whole debt.
