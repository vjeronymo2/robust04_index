BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text]Armed military detachments have been introduced in Alma-Ata [Almaty] in a move to combat the rising wave of crime in the city. In four days of what our correspondent describes as essentially a state of emergency, more than 200 kg of hashish, marijuana and opium, and hundreds of weapons have been seized, 85 persons under investigation have been detained, 336 crimes have been solved soon after being committed, and 11 criminal groups have been made harmless. [Video shows armed detachments in streets; uniformed men on parade; guns, knives, ammunition laid out on a table]
