Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA/ITAR-TASS report under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "Supplies to Moscow Threatened With Disruption"] [Text] The tense situation in the capital's economy has forced Moscow Mayor Yuriy Luzhkov to cut short his vacation. He had flown to Orel together with Viktor Chernomyrdin. The reason for the economic problems is that the Russian Government has built up a debt of approximately 250 billion rubles with various Moscow organizations and enterprises over the past year. The supply of vital foodstuffs, goods, and services to Muscovites is threatened with disruption.
