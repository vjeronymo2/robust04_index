920409 FT 09 APR 92 / World Trade News: BA wins Kuwait Airways deal BRITISH Airways has agreed to help relaunch Kuwait Airways as a leading Middle Eastern and international airline by seconding BA managers and other staff to assist in the reconstruction of the Kuwaiti carrier, reports Paul Betts, Aerospace Correspondent. An agreement has been finalised between BA and its Speedwing Consulting subsidiary with Kuwait Airways to provide the Middle East carrier with a two-phase programme to relaunch the airline on the international market following last year's Gulf conflict. The UK carrier will assist Kuwait Airways on product, brand and marketing development. Detailed proposals have already been drawn up and the recovery programme is expected to be completed by the middle of next year.
