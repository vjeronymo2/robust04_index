920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / UK Company News: A Cohen shares fall by 25p Shares of A Cohen, maker of non-ferrous metal ingots, fell 25p to 375p yesterday as profits continued to decline in the second half and the dividend was cut. The pre-tax profit for 1991 dropped from Pounds 2.03m to Pounds 783,000, on turnover of Pounds 83.7m (Pounds 88.8m). After tax of Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 1.33m) there were losses per share of 21.2p (earnings 43.5p). The final is cut to 3.4p leaving the total at 10p, against 23.1p. The severity of the recession was exacerbated by big exports of secondary aluminium from the former Soviet Union.
