Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Citing sources of the Supreme Assembly of the Islamic Revolution in Iraq, AL-MU'TAMAR newspaper reports today that two companies [sariyah] with six officers defected to the liberated Kurdistan region on 17 March. The paper says that the two companies were among the Iraqi regime forces stationed in the Karkuk sector adjacent to the liberated Kurdistan region. Although no information is available about the military unit to which the two companies belong or the ranks of the officers, the sources believe that they belonged to the first army corps of the Iraqi regime.
