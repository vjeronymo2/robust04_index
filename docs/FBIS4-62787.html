BFN [Text] Moscow, 16 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The press service of the president of the Russian Federation reports that today President Boris Yeltsin has by his edict ordered territories in two rayons to be regarded as rayons of the Far North, and the territories of 14 rayons and the town of Kyzyl of the Republic of Tyva to be regarded as localities that are on the same footing as Far North rayons. This is because of the combination of extreme environmental and climatic conditions and social and economic conditions in which the population of the Republic of Tyva live.
