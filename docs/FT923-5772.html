920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / Patten to visit China Mr Chris Patten, governor of Hong Kong, is to visit China in October. The trip, his first since taking up his post, is viewed as part of consultations following his key policy speech to the Hong Kong legislative council on October 7, writes Simon Davies in Hong Kong. He is expected to see Chinese Premier Li Peng and Lu Ping, the director of the Chinese State Council's Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office.
