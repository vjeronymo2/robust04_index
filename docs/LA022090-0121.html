February 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

SEAL BEACH CRASH KILLS WOMAN

A Huntington Beach woman died today after she drove through a lane closure and crashed into a portable oil tank, police said.

Marie McGurr, 26, was eastbound on Westminster Avenue, a mile east of Seal Beach Boulevard about 2:20 a.m. today in a 1986 Dodge sedan when she apparently drove through several lighted barriers and struck an oil tank where road work was being performed, Seal Beach police dispatcher Bonnie Tyler said.
