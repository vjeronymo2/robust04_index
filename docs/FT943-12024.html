940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: PSIT buys industrial estate for Pounds 12.3m PSIT, the property company, has acquired Houndmills industrial estate, Basingstoke, Hampshire, from British Rail Pension Fund for Pounds 12.3m. The site comprises a substantial office and industrial estate of about 420,000 sq ft let to 13 different tenants on leases with unexpired terms of between five and 20 years.
