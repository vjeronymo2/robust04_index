930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Wolseley in Pounds 3m expansion Wolseley, the heating and plumbing distributor, has paid Pounds 3.1m in shares for Form Fittings, a private Rotherham-based maker of kitchen light fittings, power modules and cable management systems. For the year to end-April 1993 Form Fittings achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 713,000. Net assets amounted to Pounds 1.02m after deducting net debt of Pounds 534,300. Initial consideration will be met via the issue of 484,640 new shares. Up to an additional 24,685 shares will be issued to satisfy the deferred consideration.
