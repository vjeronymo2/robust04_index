940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / Record price paid for Victorian art A portrait by the Victorian painter William Holman Hunt of his son Hilary sold for Pounds 969,500 at Christie's yesterday, a record for this Pre-Raphaelite artist. The previous best was Pounds 48,000 paid in 1973. At Christie's South Kensington 'African Children' by David Sheppard, depicting two young zebra, sold for Pounds 110,000 - a record for this artist - at Christie's first sale devoted exclusively to wildlife art.
