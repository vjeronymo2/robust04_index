October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

J. MURRAY MITCHELL; CLIMATOLOGIST WARNED OF GLOBAL WARMING

J. Murray Mitchell, 62, retired climatologist who warned for years of impending global warming.

Mitchell had retired from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. He was a leading authority on long-term weather trends and the impact of climate change on populations. On Oct. 5 in Washington, of cancer.
