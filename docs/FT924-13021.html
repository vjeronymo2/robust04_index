921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Asset fall at Abtrust New European Tst Over the six months ended August 31 1992 net asset value of Abtrust New European Investment Trust fell from 77.92p to 67.19p. A year earlier it stood at 79.75p. The portfolio exposure to the larger markets of Germany and France had been increased at the expense of Austria and Scandinavia, with more modest reductions in Italy, Spain and the Netherlands. Total income for the half year was Pounds 561,000 (Pounds 537,000) and net revenue worked through at Pounds 193,000 (Pounds 192,000) for unchanged earnings per share of 0.56p.
