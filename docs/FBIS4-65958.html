BFN [Text] Russian parliamentarians reject the idea of returning Karelia to Finland. The Diet Foreign Affairs Committee has had talks in Moscow with representatives of the Russian Duma on this issue. Diet Speaker Pertti Paasio says that discussions on changing the borders are perceived as negative and there is no willingness to hold such talks. According to the Duma representatives, however, there can be discussions on cooperation in developing Karelia. Foreign Ministry official Joern Donner discussed the Karelia question in Moscow.
