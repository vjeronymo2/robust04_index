BFN [Text] Paris, March 30 (AFP) -- Major French insurance group AGF reported Wednesday [30 March] that it made a group-share consolidated net profit of 977 million francs (170 million dollars) last year, down 35 percent from 1.49 billion francs in 1992. New chairman Antoine Jeancourt-Galignani said that banking activities (losses of Comptoir des Entrepreneurs and of Banque du Phenix) had depressed the group result by 2.5 billion francs. He said the goup's over-all result should improve this year.
