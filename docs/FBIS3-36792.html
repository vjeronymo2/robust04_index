Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By correspondent Ukrinform Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol, 8 Feb -- Yuriy Meshkov, recently elected as the first president of Crimea, has assessed very positively his meeting with Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk. According to the press service of the Crimean president, "it gave him confidence that the socioeconomic problems of the republic can be overcome." Yuriy Meshkov reiterated his confidence at today's meeting with French Ambassador to Ukraine Michel Yves Peissik. The guest was interested in the situation in the republic, the program of its economic revival, and the investment policy of the Crimean leadership. They talked about cooperation in culture and tourism.
