Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Some 300 kg of heroin was seized in a Turkish truck in Trieste in northern Italy. Its market price is $294 million. It was reported that this record amount of narcotic material was destined for Austria, Italy, Germany, and the Netherlands, and that two people have been detained in connection with the incident. It was found out that the truck was brought to the Trieste port on the "Kaptan Abidin Doran" ferry. It was learned that the ferry belongs to Maritime Transportation.
