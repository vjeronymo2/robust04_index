December 5, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

Tyson-Givens Rematch: Boxing champ Mike Tyson and ex-wife Robin Givens are still exchanging jabs six months after their divorce. The problem this time is the 10 acres of land adjacent to the Bernardsville, N.J., mansion they bought as newlyweds. Tyson is being sued by real estate developer Vincent Rasa Jr., who claims he is owed $95,000 in commissions for the sale of the $1.9 million property. In return, Tyson filed a counterclaim, saying Rasa, Givens, her mother Ruth Roper, the previous owner of the land and a real estate company all engaged in conspiracy. Tyson claims the group worked together to inflate the cost of the property, which he says should have cost about $160,000. DANIEL CERONE and SHAUNA SNOW
