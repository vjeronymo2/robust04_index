931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons and Pernod in swap deal Pernod-Ricard and Allied-Lyons have concluded a whisky-swap agreement, the French drinks group said yesterday. No financial details were disclosed. The deal involves Pernod, through its Irish Distillers offshoot, ceding the Tulamore Dew brand to Allied-Lyons' Cantrell and Cochrane operation in exchange for the latter's Royal Canadian brand.
