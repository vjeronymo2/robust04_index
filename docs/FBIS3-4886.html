Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Within the framework of the official visit of the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat in Bangkok signed are the bilateral documents on non-visa exchange of citizens, having diplomatic and official passports, and on establishment of air communications between the two countries. After signing the agreements by the heads of corresponding departments of the two countries the President P. Ochirbat and the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand Chuan Likphai have exchanged speeches. The two leaders have pointed out to the importance of these documents in development of relations between the two states in different spheres.
