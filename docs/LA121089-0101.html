December 10, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ONE WOMAN'S CHOICE -- PREGNANCY SERVES AS TEST

I'm getting sick and tired of hearing about pro-choice and all these women who are for abortion. Today's article (Dec. 3) by Dianne Klein was such a warm, touching, positive story, it left me with tears on my cheeks.

Thank God this lady had the courage and faith in Jesus to keep this child, knowing that the odds of abnormality were high.

Thank God she went by the hospital to visit the baby and felt that tugging in her heart to take her baby home instead of put him up for adoption.

Thank God the father was a mature, kind and loving man to marry this woman and to care for this child when it would have been easier to run the other way.

Thank God this mother was brave enough to share her story that will make a difference in this world.

Thank God there are people who value life even when it starts at conception.

SHELLEY E. HULSIZER

San Clemente
