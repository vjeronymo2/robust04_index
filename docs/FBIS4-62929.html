BFN [Report by Yuriy Horban] [Excerpts] The Central Electoral Commission yesterday continued its verification of the results of the signature campaign in support of presidential candidates, and it finally began the registration process. The two candidates registered on the previous day -- [President] Leonid Kravchuk and [former Supreme Council Chairman] Ivan Plyushch, whom I do not have to introduce -- were joined by another five people. These are People's Deputy Valeriy Babych, president of the Ukrainian Financial Group; People's Deputy Leonid Kuchma, president of the Ukrainian Union of Entrepreneurs and Industrialists; newly elected Supreme Council Chairman Oleksandr Moroz, chairman of the Socialist Party of Ukraine; People's Deputy Volodymyr Lanovyy, president of the Market Reforms Center; and Ukrainian Education Minister Petro Talanchuk. All these candidacies have been approved. [passage omitted] Finally, the Central Electoral Commission confirmed the fact and date for holding the presidential elections. The registration of candidates for the office of Ukrainian president was concluded.
