Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Liberal Democratic Party leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy intends to sue St. Petersburg Mayor Anatoliy Sobchak, leader of the State Duma faction Russia's Choice Yegor Gaydar, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and retired KGB Major-General Oleg Kalugin for defamation, Zhirinovskiy's lawyer Sergey Belyak told Interfax Monday. He said the move was caused by statements of these political figures addressed at Zhirinovskiy in which he "was called an agent of special services and a product of the KGB, compared to Hitler and accused of spreading fascist ideology." Besides, Belyak said that Zhirinovskiy would also sue the mass media which reported these statements. The amount of reimbursement is still being kept secret. Belyak said that the documents had already been prepared and would be presented to court within the next few days.
