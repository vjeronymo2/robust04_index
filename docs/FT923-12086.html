920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Building groups appeal to PM LEADERS of recession-hit construction companies appealed to the prime minister yesterday to introduce measures to stimulate the industry. The presidents of the industry's three biggest trade associations, the Building Employers Confederation, the National Council of Building Material Producers and the Federation of Civil Engineering Contractors, called on Mr John Major to increase public spending on construction, to stimulate the housing market and to seek greater private investment in schemes such toll roads.
